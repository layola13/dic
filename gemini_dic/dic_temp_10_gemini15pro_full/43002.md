cowardices:/ˈkaʊərdɪsɪz/| n. |怯懦，胆怯|The soldier's display of cowardices shocked his comrades.  这个士兵表现出的怯懦震惊了他的战友们。|近义词: timidity, pusillanimity, faintheartedness|反义词: bravery, courage, valor|词性:noun

Etymology: From Middle English *cowardyse, from Old French coardise, from coward + -ise. Ultimately from Latin cauda (“tail”).  The original sense was "the state of having one's tail between one's legs," like a frightened dog.
