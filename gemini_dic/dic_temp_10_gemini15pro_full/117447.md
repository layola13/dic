meditating:/ˈmedɪteɪtɪŋ/
v.
冥想，沉思
Engaging in contemplation or reflection, often in a spiritual context.
She is meditating in her room. 她正在房间里冥想。
He was meditating on the meaning of life. 他在沉思人生的意义。
近义词：contemplating, pondering, reflecting
反义词：rushing, hurrying
词性：动词(present participle)
