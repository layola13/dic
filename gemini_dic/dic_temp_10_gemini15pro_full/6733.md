analyse:/ˈænəlaɪz/ (UK) /ˈænəˌlaɪz/ (US)| v. |分析，解析|To examine something methodically and in detail in order to explain and interpret it. |The scientist analysed the data from the experiment. 科学家分析了实验数据。

The company hired a consultant to analyse their sales figures. 公司聘请了一位顾问来分析他们的销售数据。

近义词：examine, investigate, study, scrutinize, assess, evaluate
反义词：synthesize, combine, ignore
词性：动词
