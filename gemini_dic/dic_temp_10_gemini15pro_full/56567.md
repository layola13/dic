dominion:/dəˈmɪnjən/ |n.|统治；支配；领土；领域|Supreme authority; control.  A territory or sphere of influence or control. |The king held dominion over the entire kingdom. 国王统治着整个王国。

The company has dominion over a vast share of the market. 这家公司控制着市场的很大份额。|近义词：rule, control, sway, authority, sovereignty, territory, realm, domain|反义词：subjection, servitude, dependence|词性: 名词

词源: From Old French *dominion*  "lordship, rule, power," from Latin *dominium* "property, right of ownership," from *dominus* "lord, master" (from *domus* "house").
