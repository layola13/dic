opusculum:/ˌɒpəˈskjʊləm/ | n. | 小作品，短篇著作，小册子 | A small literary or artistic work, especially one that is not considered to be of major importance. |  He published several opuscula on local history. 他出版了几本关于当地历史的小册子。 |  The artist's early opuscula showed promise of his later greatness.  这位艺术家早期的短篇作品展现了他日后伟大的潜力。 |  work, booklet, pamphlet, treatise, monograph |  tome, magnum opus | noun
