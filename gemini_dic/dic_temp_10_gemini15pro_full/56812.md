dorism: /ˈdɔːrɪzəm/
n.
多利斯主义, 古希腊多利安人的文化和艺术风格
A style of art, especially sculpture, characteristic of the ancient Dorians, noted for its severity and formality.
The museum's collection includes several fine examples of Dorism.  博物馆的藏品包括几个多利斯主义的精美例子。
近义词:  Doric style, Spartan style
反义词:  Ionian style, Attic style
词性: noun

词源: From Doric + -ism.  源自Doric（多利安） + -ism（主义）。
