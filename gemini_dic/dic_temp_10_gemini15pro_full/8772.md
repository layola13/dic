antidynastical:/ˌænti daɪˈnæstɪkəl/
|adj.|
反王朝的，反对王朝统治的|
Opposed to dynasties or dynastic rule; against the rule of a succession of rulers from the same family.|
例句：The antidynastical movement gained momentum after the king's controversial decision. 反王朝运动在国王做出有争议的决定后势头增强。

例句：His antidynastical views were not popular with the aristocracy. 他的反王朝观点不受贵族欢迎。

|近义词：anti-monarchical, revolutionary|
|反义词：dynastic, monarchist, pro-monarchy|
|词性：adjective|
