O-levels:/ˈoʊ lɛvəlz/ | n. | 普通中等教育证书 | Examinations taken in various subjects, usually at the age of 16, in the UK and other countries, formerly called the General Certificate of Education (GCE) Ordinary Level. |  She passed her O-levels with flying colors. 她以优异的成绩通过了普通中等教育证书考试。 |  GCSE, secondary school exams |  A-levels | noun
Etymology:  Abbreviation of Ordinary Levels, from General Certificate of Education Ordinary Level.
