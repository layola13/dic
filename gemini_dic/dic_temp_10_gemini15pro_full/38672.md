condemnatory:/kənˈdɛmnəˌtɔːri/ /kənˈdɛmnəˌtɔːri/
adjective
谴责的；批判的
Expressing strong disapproval; tending to condemn.
The judge made some condemnatory remarks about the defendant's behavior. 法官对被告的行为发表了一些谴责性言论。

His condemnatory tone made it clear he disapproved. 他谴责的语气清楚地表明他不赞成。

近义词:critical, disapproving, censorious, accusatory
反义词:laudatory, complimentary, approving
词性:adjective
