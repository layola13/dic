impediment:/ɪmˈpɛdɪmənt/| n.|障碍，阻碍，妨碍|something that makes progress, movement, or achieving something difficult or impossible|His stammer was a serious impediment to his career as a lawyer. 他的口吃是他律师生涯的严重障碍。|Synonyms: obstacle, hindrance, obstruction, barrier, hurdle|Antonyms: advantage, aid, assistance, benefit, help|noun

impediment:/ɪmˈpɛdɪmənt/| n.|言语障碍，口吃|a defect in a person's speech, such as a stammer|She overcame her speech impediment with years of therapy. 她通过多年的治疗克服了言语障碍。|Synonyms: stammer, stutter, lisp|Antonyms: fluency|noun
