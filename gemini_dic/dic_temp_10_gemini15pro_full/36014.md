codifier:/ˈkɒdɪfaɪə(r)/ /ˈkoʊdəˌfaɪər/| n. |编纂者；整理者；法律汇编者|A person who arranges laws or rules into a systematic code.|The codifier organized the scattered rules into a comprehensive legal document.  编纂者将分散的规则整理成一份全面的法律文件。|Example: Justinian is remembered as the great codifier of Roman law.  查士丁尼被誉为罗马法伟大的编纂者。|Synonyms: compiler, systematizer, organizer|Antonyms: disorganizer|noun


codify:/ˈkɒdɪfaɪ/  /ˈkoʊdəˌfaɪ/| vt. |编纂；整理；将…编成法典|To arrange laws or rules into a systematic code.|The government codified the new regulations. 政府将新规章编纂成法典。|Example:  The committee codified the rules of the game. 委员会将游戏规则编纂成册。 |Synonyms: systematize, organize, formulate|Antonyms: disorganize, disorder|verb
