neglecters:/nɪˈɡlɛktərz/| n.|疏忽者，忽略者|People who fail to care for someone or something properly.  Those who omit to do something.|The neglecters of the garden allowed the weeds to grow rampant.  花园的疏忽者让杂草疯长。|synonyms:  slackers, shirkers, delinquents|antonyms: carers, guardians, custodians|noun
