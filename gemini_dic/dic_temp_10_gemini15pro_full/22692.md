bore-off:/bɔːr ɒf/ | phrasal verb | 滚开；走开；别烦我 | To tell someone to go away because they are boring or annoying. | "Oh, bore off, will you?" 哦，走开，好吗？| "You're talking nonsense, just bore off." 你在胡说八道，走开。|  Synonyms: get lost, go away, clear off, beat it, scram | Antonyms: come here, stay, welcome | phrasal verb

bore-off:/bɔːr ɒf/ | n. | (n.) an act of telling someone to go away because they are boring. This usage is less common than the phrasal verb usage.  | "His constant complaints led to a swift bore-off from his friends." 他不断的抱怨导致他的朋友们迅速地让他走开。 | "She gave him the bore-off he deserved." 她给了他应得的滚蛋。 | Synonyms: dismissal, rejection, brush-off | Antonyms: invitation, welcome | noun
