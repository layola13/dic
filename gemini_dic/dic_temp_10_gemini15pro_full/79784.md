glutton:/ˈɡlʌtn/ | n. | 贪吃的人；暴食者 |  A person who eats and drinks excessively or has a great capacity for something. | He's such a glutton when it comes to desserts.  他吃甜点真是个贪吃鬼。 | synonyms: gourmand, overeater, gormandizer, pig, hog | antonyms: ascetic, abstainer | noun
