gloved:/ɡlʌvd/
adjective
戴手套的
Wearing gloves; having gloves on.
The surgeon's gloved hands moved with precision.  外科医生戴着手套的手精准地移动着。
The gloved waiter poured the wine. 戴着手套的服务员倒了酒。
近义词: mittened
反义词: barehanded, ungloved
词性: adjective


词源: From glove + -ed.  由glove(手套) + -ed(形容词后缀)构成。
