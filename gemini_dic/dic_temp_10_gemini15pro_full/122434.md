moatlike: /ˈmoʊtˌlaɪk/| adj.|像护城河一样的|Resembling a moat, especially in providing a protective barrier.|The house was surrounded by a moatlike ditch. 这栋房子被一条像护城河一样的沟渠包围着。|近义词:protective, safeguarding, ditchlike, canal-like|反义词:exposed, vulnerable|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From *moat* + *-like*.  *Moat* comes from Old French *mote* "mound, embankment," from a Vulgar Latin word derived from Latin *movere* "to move."  *-like* is a common suffix forming adjectives and meaning "resembling, characteristic of."
