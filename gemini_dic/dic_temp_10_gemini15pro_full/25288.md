bug-off:/bʌɡ ˈɒf/ | phrasal verb | 走开；滚开；别烦我 | Used to tell someone to go away, especially because they are annoying you. | "Bug off!" he shouted at the persistent salesman. “滚开！”他对着纠缠不休的推销员喊道。 | Example: The kids were teasing him, so he told them to bug off. 孩子们一直在戏弄他，所以他让他们走开。 | Synonyms: go away, get lost, leave me alone, clear off | Antonyms: come here, stay | Verb phrase

bug-off:/bʌɡ ˈɒf/ | interjection | 走开；滚开；别烦我 | Used as an exclamation to tell someone to go away. | "Bug off!" she yelled, slamming the door. “滚开！”她喊道，砰地一声关上了门。 | Example:  He was getting annoyed, so he simply said "Bug off!" 他开始生气了，所以他只是说了句“滚开！” | Synonyms: get lost, scram, beat it | Antonyms: welcome | Interjection
