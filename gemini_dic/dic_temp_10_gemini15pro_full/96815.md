inerts:/ɪˈnɜːrts/ | n. |惰性物质; 惰性气体|Substances that are chemically inactive, especially noble gases.| Argon is one of the inert gases. 氩气是一种惰性气体。| synonyms: inactive substances, noble gases | antonyms: reactive substances | noun

inerts:/ɪˈnɜːrts/ | adj. |惰性的; 不活泼的|Chemically inactive.|Inert gases rarely react with other elements. 惰性气体很少与其他元素发生反应。| synonyms: inactive, unreactive, passive | antonyms: active, reactive | adjective
