celsius's:/ˈselsiəsəz/| n.|摄氏度|The possessive form of Celsius, referring to the temperature scale.  It indicates something belonging to or associated with the Celsius scale.|例句：The celsius's scale is used worldwide. 摄氏温标在世界范围内使用。

例句：Water boils at 100 degrees celsius's. 水在100摄氏度沸腾。|近义词：centigrade|反义词:Fahrenheit|词性:noun
