busbars:/ˈbʌsbɑːrz/ (US), /ˈbʌsbɑːz/ (UK)| n.|汇流排，母线|A busbar (also bus bar) is a metallic strip or bar, typically housed inside switchgear, panel boards, and busway enclosures for local high current power distribution. It is a type of conductor used to connect high-voltage equipment in a power system or to connect related equipment in an electrical switchboard or panelboard.|The busbars were carefully inspected for any signs of corrosion.|近义词：conductor, power rail|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps insulator in a broader context)|noun
