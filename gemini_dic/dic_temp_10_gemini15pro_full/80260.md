goggled:/ˈɡɒɡəld/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːɡəld/ (US)| adj.|戴着护目镜的；瞪大眼睛的|Wearing goggles; having eyes wide open in surprise, fear, or excitement.|例句：The goggled scientist peered into the microscope. 戴着护目镜的科学家凝视着显微镜。|近义词：wide-eyed, spectacled|反义词：ungoggled|词性:形容词
