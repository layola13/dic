muddily:/ˈmʌdɪli/| adv.|浑浊地；模糊地；混乱地|In a muddy or unclear manner; with confusion or lack of clarity.|The writer expressed his ideas muddily, making it difficult to understand his point. 作者表达思想很混乱，让人难以理解他的观点。|近义词：confusedly, vaguely, unclearly|反义词：clearly, distinctly, lucidly|词性:副词
词源: From Middle English *muddeli, derived from muddy + -ly. Ultimately from Old English muddig.
