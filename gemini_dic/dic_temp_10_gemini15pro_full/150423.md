pansophical:/ˌpæn.səˈfɪk.əl/ | adj. | 博学的，无所不知的 |  Relating to or characterized by pansophy; having or showing knowledge of all things. | He had a pansophical view of the world, believing he understood all its complexities. 他拥有无所不知的世界观，相信自己理解世界上所有的复杂性。 |  The ancient library aimed to be a pansophical collection of all human knowledge. 这座古老的图书馆旨在成为一个人类所有知识的总汇。 | 近义词: encyclopedic, omniscient, all-knowing | 反义词: ignorant, unknowing | 词性: adjective

词源: From Greek πᾶν (pân, “all”) + σοφία (sophía, “wisdom”).
