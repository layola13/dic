incapsulated:/ɪnˈkæpsjʊleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 被封装的，被包住的 | Enclosed in a capsule or other protective covering. incapsulate:/ɪnˈkæpsjʊleɪt/ | v. | 封装，包住；概括 | To enclose something in a capsule or other protective covering.
