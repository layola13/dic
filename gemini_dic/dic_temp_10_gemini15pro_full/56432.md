dollishness:/ˈdɑːlɪʃnɪs/ | n. | 娃娃气，幼稚，像娃娃一样 | The quality of resembling or behaving like a doll; innocent, childlike, and sometimes slightly artificial charm or appearance.  | Her dollishness, though charming at first, eventually became irritating. 她那种娃娃气，虽然起初很迷人，但最终变得令人恼火。 | 近义词: childishness, naivety, innocence, coquettishness | 反义词: maturity, sophistication, worldliness | 词性: noun

词源:
doll + -ishness
doll (n.) from Middle Dutch dolle "doll, bundle of rags," of unknown origin.
-ishness (suffix) forming abstract nouns from adjectives, usually of depreciative or pejorative force, from Old English -iscnys.
