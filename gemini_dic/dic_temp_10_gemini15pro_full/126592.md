naggingly:/ˈnæɡɪŋli/| adv.|唠叨地，不断地|In a persistently annoying or bothersome way.  It describes an action or feeling that continues to irritate or bother someone over an extended period.|The thought nagged naggingly at the back of her mind.|近义词：persistently, constantly, incessantly, repeatedly|反义词：occasionally, sporadically, rarely|词性: 副词

Etymology: From "nag" + "-ing" + "-ly". "Nag" comes from Old Norse *nagga "to gnaw, bite, grumble".
