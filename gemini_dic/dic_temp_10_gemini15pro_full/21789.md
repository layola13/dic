bobble:/ˈbɑːbl/ (US) /ˈbɒbl/ (UK)| n. v. |小球, 绒球;  失误, 拙劣的尝试; |A small ball of wool or other material used as a decoration. ETYMOLOGY->
From the earlier bob, meaning "to move up and down quickly," probably of imitative origin.  The noun sense developed from the verb, referring to the movement of the bobble.
