interparietal:/ˌɪntərˈpəˈraɪɪtəl/| adj. n.|顶间的，顶间骨|Located between parietal bones; pertaining to the interparietal bone.|The interparietal bone is a small bone located at the back of the skull. 顶间骨是位于颅骨后部的一块小骨。|The interparietal suture is a fibrous joint that unites the two parietal bones. 顶间缝是连接两块顶骨的纤维连接。|Synonyms: interparietal bone, os interparietale|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for an anatomical term)|adjective, noun
