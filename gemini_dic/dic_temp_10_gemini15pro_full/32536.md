chipangu:/tʃɪˈpæŋɡuː/
n.|日本（古称）|An archaic name for Japan, derived from Marco Polo's transcription of the Japanese word "Cipango".  It represents a romanticized and somewhat inaccurate portrayal of Japan, based on secondhand accounts and legends. |例句：The tales of Chipangu, filled with gold and riches, fueled European exploration.  奇帕古充满黄金和财富的故事，激发了欧洲的探索。

例句：In the Middle Ages, Chipangu was believed to be a land of immense wealth. 在中世纪，奇帕古被认为是一个拥有巨大财富的土地。
近义词：Japan, Nippon|
