decagonal: /dɪˈkæɡənəl/
adjective
十邊形的；十角形的
Having ten sides and ten angles.
A decagonal prism has ten rectangular faces and two decagonal bases.  一个十边形棱柱有十个矩形面和两个十边形底面。

The architect designed a decagonal pavilion for the park.  这位建筑师为公园设计了一个十边形的亭子。

Synonym: ten-sided
Antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider shapes with different numbers of sides, e.g., triangular, square, pentagonal)
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *deka- "ten" + -gōnos "angled"
