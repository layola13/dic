blockchain: /ˈblɒkˌtʃeɪn/ (UK) /ˈblɑːkˌtʃeɪn/ (US)| n. |区块链|A system in which a record of transactions made in bitcoin or another cryptocurrency are maintained across several computers that are linked in a peer-to-peer network.|Bitcoin uses blockchain technology to record transactions. 比特币使用区块链技术来记录交易。|synonyms: distributed ledger, shared ledger|antonyms: centralized database|noun

blockchain: /ˈblɒkˌtʃeɪn/ (UK) /ˈblɑːkˌtʃeɪn/ (US)| adj. |区块链的|Relating to or involving blockchain technology.|Blockchain security is a major concern. 区块链安全是一个主要问题。|synonyms: distributed ledger, shared ledger|antonyms: centralized database|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
block + chain.  "Block" referring to a group of transactions, and "chain" referring to the chronological linking of these blocks.
