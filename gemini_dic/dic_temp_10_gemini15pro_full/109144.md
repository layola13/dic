legislatively:/ˌlɛdʒɪsˈleɪtɪvli/
adverb
立法地；通过立法
In a legislative manner; by legislation.
The issue was handled legislatively.  这个问题是通过立法处理的。
Congress acted legislatively to pass the bill. 国会通过立法程序通过了该法案。
Synonym: by law, by statute, legally
Antonym: executively, judicially


词性: adverb
