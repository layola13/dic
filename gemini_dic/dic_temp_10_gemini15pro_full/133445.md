nonfatalistic:/ˌnɒnˌfeɪtəˈlɪstɪk/
adj.
非宿命论的
Not believing in fate; believing that people can control their own lives.
He has a nonfatalistic approach to life and believes hard work will bring success. 他对人生持有非宿命论的观点，相信努力工作会带来成功。

She displayed a nonfatalistic attitude, choosing to fight her illness rather than accept it passively. 她表现出一种非宿命论的态度，选择与疾病抗争而不是被动接受。

optimistic, hopeful, positive
fatalistic, pessimistic, resigned
adjective
