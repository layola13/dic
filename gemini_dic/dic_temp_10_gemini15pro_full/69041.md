fatwa:/ˈfæt.wɑː/ /ˈfɑːt.wɑː/| n. |宗教裁决，教令|A legal opinion or decree handed down by an Islamic religious leader.|The ayatollah issued a fatwa against the author. 阿亚图拉对这位作家发布了宗教裁决。|近义词：decree, edict, ruling, pronouncement|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "secular law")|noun
