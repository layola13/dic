noncommendablenesses:/ˌnɒnkəmɛndəbəlnəsɪz/ | n. | 不可称道之处；不值得推荐之处 |  The numerous noncommendablenesses of the policy led to its eventual repeal.  This refers to the multiple aspects of the policy that were not worthy of praise or recommendation. | 例句：The noncommendablenesses of his behavior were obvious to everyone. 他的行为的不可取之处对每个人都很明显。

The report highlighted the noncommendablenesses of the project's implementation.  这份报告强调了该项目实施过程中不值得称道的地方。


近义词：faults, shortcomings, flaws, defects | 反义词: virtues, merits, strengths | 词性: 名词
