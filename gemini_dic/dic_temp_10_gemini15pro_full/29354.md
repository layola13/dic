castorite:/ˈkæstəˌraɪt/ | n. | 海狸香 | A reddish-brown, unctuous substance with a strong odor, secreted by beavers and used in perfumery.  It is obtained from the castor sacs of both male and female beavers. |  Castorite is a key ingredient in some classic perfumes. 海狸香是一些经典香水的关键成分。 |  The perfumer carefully measured out the castorite. 调香师仔细地量取海狸香。 | synonyms: castoreum | antonyms: None | noun
