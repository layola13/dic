distributively:/dɪˈstrɪbjətɪvli/
adv. | 分配地；分别地 |  In a distributive manner; each individual member of a group receiving a share. | The bonus was awarded distributively to each employee. 奖金分别授予每位员工。 | separately, individually, respectively | collectively, jointly | adverb

distributively:/dɪˈstrɪbjətɪvli/
adv. | 分配地；分别地 |  Relating to or denoting an operation or property which is applied to each element of a collection, set, etc., and then combined. (Mathematics) | The distributive property of multiplication over addition states that a(b + c) = ab + ac.  乘法对加法的分配律表明a(b + c) = ab + ac。 | individually, element-wise, one by one | collectively, as a whole | adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *distributivus, from Latin distribut- “distributed,” past participle stem of distribuere “to distribute.”  From dis- "apart" + tribuere "to allot, assign."
