canonicate:/kəˈnɒnɪkɪt/| n.|教士职位；法政牧师职位|The position of a canon in a cathedral or collegiate church.  A canonicate is an ecclesiastical office, especially in a cathedral or collegiate church.|He was awarded a canonicate at St. Paul's Cathedral.|近义词：prebend, canonry|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: Late Middle English: from medieval Latin canonicatus, from canonicus ‘canon’.
