auctioneered:/ˈɔːkʃəˌnɪərd/
verb
拍卖；主持拍卖
acted as auctioneer; sold at auction.
例句：He auctioneered the antique furniture. 他拍卖了古董家具。

例句：The painting was auctioneered off for a record price. 这幅画以创纪录的价格被拍卖掉。

近义词：sold, auctioned, hawked
反义词：bought, purchased
词性：v.
