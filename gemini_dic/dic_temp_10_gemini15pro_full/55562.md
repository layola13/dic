distrusts:/dɪsˈtrʌsts/
verb
不信任，怀疑
lacks trust or confidence in
He distrusts politicians and their promises. 他不信任政客和他们的承诺。
近义词:doubts, suspects, mistrusts
反义词:trusts, believes, has faith in
词性:verb (third-person singular present tense)

distrust:/dɪsˈtrʌst/
noun
不信任，怀疑
lack of trust or confidence
There is a deep distrust between the two communities. 这两个社群之间存在着深深的不信任。
近义词:suspicion, mistrust, doubt
反义词:trust, confidence, belief, faith
词性:noun

distrust:/dɪsˈtrʌst/
verb
不信任，怀疑
regard with doubt or suspicion
She distrusted her own judgment after the accident. 事故之后她不相信自己的判断。
近义词:doubt, suspect, mistrust
反义词:trust, believe, confide in
词性:verb

词源:
From Middle English *dis trusten*, from dis- + trusten (“to trust”). Equivalent to dis- +‎ trust.
