matin: /ˈmætɪn/ (US), /ˈmætɪn/ (UK)| n.|晨祷，晨歌; 早晨; 早课|A religious service held in the morning.  Also refers to a poem or song appropriate for morning.|例句：The matin bells rang out across the valley. 晨祷的钟声响彻山谷。

例句：He awoke early to attend matin. 他早起去参加晨祷。

近义词：morning prayer, morning service, dawn service, lauds|反义词:vespers, evensong|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *matin*, from Late Latin *matutinum* (“of the morning”), neuter of *matutinus*, from *Matuta*, goddess of the dawn.
