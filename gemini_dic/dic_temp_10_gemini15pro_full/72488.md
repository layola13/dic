fluorene:/ˈflʊəriːn/ | n. |芴|A colorless crystalline hydrocarbon, C₁₃H₁₀, extracted from coal tar or obtained by the distillation of diphenylmethane. It is used in the manufacture of dyes, plastics, and insecticides. |Fluorene is a common component of coal tar. 芴是煤焦油的常见成分。| Fluorene derivatives are used in organic electronics. 芴的衍生物用于有机电子学。 |  synonym: no single perfect synonym, but related compounds include anthracene, phenanthrene | antonym: N/A | noun
