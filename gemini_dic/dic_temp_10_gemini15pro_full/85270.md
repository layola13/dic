harmonies:/ˈhɑːrməniz/ /ˈhɑːrmoʊniz/| n.|和谐，协调，和声|A pleasing arrangement of parts, especially musical notes.|The harmonies of the choir were beautiful. 合唱团的和声很优美。|近义词：chords, melodies, agreements, consonances|反义词：discords, dissonances, conflicts|词性:Noun (plural)

Etymology:
From Middle English *harmonie*, from Old French *harmonie*, from Latin *harmonia*, from Ancient Greek *ἁρμονία (harmonía)*, from *ἁρμόζω (harmozō)* "to fit, join".
