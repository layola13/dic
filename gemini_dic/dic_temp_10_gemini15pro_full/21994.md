boil-out:/ˈbɔɪl aʊt/| n.| 煮沸，清洗，排污|The boil-out procedure removes impurities from the system. 煮沸程序去除系统中的杂质。|例句：The boiler underwent a thorough boil-out before commissioning. 锅炉在调试前进行了彻底的煮沸清洗。

例句：A boil-out is necessary to remove any residual oils from the new pipes.  对于新管道来说，煮沸清洗是必要的，以去除任何残留的油污。

近义词：cleaning, flushing, purging|反义词:contamination|词性:noun

boil-out:/ˈbɔɪl aʊt/| v.| 煮沸清洗，排污|They boiled-out the reactor to remove contaminants. 他们煮沸清洗反应堆以去除污染物。|例句：We need to boil-out the tank before we can use it. 在使用储罐之前，我们需要对其进行煮沸清洗。

例句：They boiled-out the system to prepare it for operation. 他们煮沸清洗系统以使其做好运行准备。
