diviningly:/dɪˈvaɪnɪŋli/
adv.
神圣地，超自然地，不可思议地
By some seemingly diviningly power, she knew exactly what he was thinking.  她似乎拥有一种不可思议的力量，能准确地知道他在想什么。
He spoke diviningly about the future. 他神奇地谈论着未来。
近义词: prophetically, mystically, supernaturally, miraculously
反义词: ordinarily, normally, naturally
词性: 副词

Etymology:
From divine (adj.) + -ly (adv. suffix).  "Divine" comes from Old French "devin" and ultimately from Latin "divinus" meaning "of or belonging to a god, godlike," related to "divus" meaning "god."  The suffix "-ly" forms adverbs from adjectives.
