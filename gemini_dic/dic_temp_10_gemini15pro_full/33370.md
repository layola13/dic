chundra: /ˈtʃʌndrə/ | n. |  月神;月亮 (主要用于印度教和佛教语境) | A chundra is a term, primarily used in Hindu and Buddhist contexts, referring to the moon or a lunar deity.  Specifically, it can refer to Chandra, the Hindu moon god. |  The temple was dedicated to the chundra.  这座寺庙是献给月神的。|  The chundra shone brightly in the night sky. 月亮在夜空中闪耀着光芒。 | synonyms: moon, lunar deity | antonyms: sun, solar deity | noun
