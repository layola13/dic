overpackage:/ˌoʊvərˈpækɪdʒ/
verb
过度包装
to wrap or package (something) with excessive amounts of material
The company was criticized for overpackaging its products. 该公司因产品过度包装而受到批评。

overpackage:/ˌoʊvərˈpækɪdʒ/
noun
过度包装
the act or process of wrapping or packaging (something) with excessive amounts of material
The overpackaging of the product was unnecessary and wasteful.  产品的过度包装是不必要且浪费的。


overpackaging:/ˌoʊvərˈpækɪdʒɪŋ/

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + package.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much". "Package" refers to the act of wrapping or enclosing something.


SYNONYMS->
overwrap, excessively package


ANTONYMS->
