forayers:/ˈfɒrɪərz/ (US) /ˈfɒr.eɪ.ərz/ (UK)| n. | 觅食者；突袭者；抢劫者 | People or animals who go into an area to find food or other valuable things, often by taking it from other people or places by force. |  The Roman legions were formidable foragers, stripping the countryside bare as they marched. 罗马军团是可怕的觅食者，他们在行军过程中将乡村洗劫一空。

The foragers returned to the camp with baskets full of berries and mushrooms. 觅食者带着装满浆果和蘑菇的篮子回到了营地。 | 近义词：scavengers, raiders, marauders | 反义词: settlers, cultivators | 词性: 名词
