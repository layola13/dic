fizzle:/ˈfɪzəl/| v. n. |发出嘶嘶声；失败；虎头蛇尾|To gradually end, often in a weak or disappointing way.  To make a hissing or sputtering sound.|The fireworks fizzled out after a few seconds.  烟花燃放了几秒钟就熄灭了。|近义词：fail, flop, peter out, sputter|反义词：succeed, thrive|词性：动词, 名词

fizzle:/ˈfɪzəl/| n. |嘶嘶声；失败|A hissing or sputtering sound. A failure.|The project ended in a fizzle.  这个项目以失败告终。|近义词：failure, flop|反义词：success|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From imitative base *fit-, source of fit (v.).  The noun is from the verb.  Meaning "failure" is from 1832.
