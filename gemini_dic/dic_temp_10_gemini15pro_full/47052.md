dataria: /dɑːˈtɑːriə/ /deɪˈtɛəriə/
noun
教廷新闻处；教廷新闻办公室
The office of the Papal Dataria, responsible for issuing certain papal documents.
The Dataria issued a statement regarding the Pope's health. 教廷新闻处就教皇的健康状况发表了一份声明。
synonyms:  Papal Chancellery, Apostolic Chancery
antonyms: N/A (lacks a direct opposite)
词性: noun
