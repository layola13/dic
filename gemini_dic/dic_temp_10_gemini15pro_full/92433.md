hyponome:/ˈhaɪpəˌnoʊm/| n. |下层; 下位; 受支配者; 下位概念|A subordinate or specific term; a word of more specific meaning than a general or superordinate term applicable to it.  A word that is a hyponym of another shares a type-of relationship with it. For example, *spoon* is a hyponym of *cutlery*.|The hyponyms of "color" include "red," "blue," "green," etc.|近义词:subordinate term, specific term|反义词:hypernym, superordinate term|词性:noun
