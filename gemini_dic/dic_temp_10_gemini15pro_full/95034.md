inaccuratenesses: /ɪnˈækjʊrətnəsɪz/ | n. | 不准确，误差，错误 |  Plural form of inaccuracy, referring to multiple instances of being not accurate or correct. | The report was riddled with inaccuracies, undermining its credibility.  这份报告充满了错误，损害了它的可信度。| Synonyms: errors, mistakes, fallacies, discrepancies | Antonyms: accuracies, precisions, exactnesses | noun

Etymology:
From in- (not) + accurate + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  "Accurate" comes from Latin "accuratus," past participle of "accurare" (to take care of), from "ad-" (to) + "cura" (care).
