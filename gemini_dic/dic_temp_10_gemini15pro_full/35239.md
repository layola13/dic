clumpy:/ˈklʌmpi/ | adj. | 块状的，结块的 | Forming or consisting of clumps; lumpy. | The sauce was thick and clumpy. 酱汁又浓又结块。| The batter was too clumpy to pour. 面糊太稠了，倒不出来。| lumpy, bumpy, chunky | smooth, creamy | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From clump + -y.  "Clump" originates from a Scandinavian source, possibly Norwegian *klumpr or Swedish klump, meaning "lump, mass." The suffix "-y" is commonly used to form adjectives indicating "full of" or "characterized by."
