hydrant:/ˈhaɪdrənt/ (US) /ˈhaɪdrənt/ (UK)| n. |消防栓；消防龙头|A hydrant is a discharge pipe with a valve and spout at which water may be drawn from a water main, used especially for fighting fires.|The firefighters connected their hose to the hydrant. 消防队员把水管连接到消防栓上。|Synonyms: fireplug, fire hydrant|Antonyms: None|Noun
