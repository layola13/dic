malarkies: /məˈlɑːkiːz/
noun
胡闹；恶作剧；无聊的活动
Foolish or mischievous behavior; activities that are a waste of time.
He's always up to some malarkies. 他总是搞些恶作剧。
synonyms: antics, shenanigans, pranks, mischief, tomfoolery
antonyms: seriousness, diligence, work, responsibility
n.

malarky: /məˈlɑːrki/
noun
胡闹；恶作剧；无聊的活动
Foolish or mischievous behavior; activities that are a waste of time.
Stop all this malarky and get back to work! 别再胡闹了，回去工作！
synonyms: nonsense, rubbish, baloney, bunkum
antonyms: truth, fact, reality
n.
