certain:/ˈsɜːrtn/ (US) /ˈsɜːtən/ (UK)| adj. pron.|确定的，肯定的；某一，某些|Fixed, settled; without doubt; specific but not named.|例句：I'm certain that I locked the door. 我确定我锁了门。|近义词：sure, definite, positive|反义词：uncertain, unsure, doubtful|词性:形容词

certain:/ˈsɜːrtn/ (US) /ˈsɜːtən/ (UK)| pron.|某些，一些|Some; particular but not specified.|例句：Certain people are more susceptible to the flu. 某些人更容易感染流感。|近义词：some, particular|反义词：none, all|词性:代词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *certein, from Old French *certain, from Latin *certus (“settled, determined, sure”), past participle of *cernō (“I separate, distinguish, decide”).
