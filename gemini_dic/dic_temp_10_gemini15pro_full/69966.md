festiveness:/ˈfɛstɪvnəs/| n.| 节日气氛；欢乐气氛；喜庆气氛 |The air was filled with festiveness.  空气中充满了节日气氛。| The festiveness of the occasion was infectious.  这种场合的喜庆气氛很有感染力。 | 近义词：celebration, gaiety, merriment, jollity | 反义词：somberness, gloom, dreariness | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From festive + -ness.  "Festive" comes from the Latin "festivus" meaning "festive, joyous," related to "festum" meaning "festival."  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns expressing a state, quality, or condition.
