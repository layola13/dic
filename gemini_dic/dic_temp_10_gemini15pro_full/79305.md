glazing:/ˈɡleɪzɪŋ/| n.|上釉，釉料，(糕点上的)糖衣，窗玻璃|The glazing on the cake made it look delicious. 蛋糕上的糖衣使它看起来很美味。|近义词：enamel, varnish, frosting, icing, pane|反义词：-|n.

glaze:/ɡleɪz/| vt. & vi. |给…上釉，装玻璃，(目光)变得呆滞无神|He glazed the doughnuts with sugar. 他给甜甜圈裹上了糖衣。  She glazed over when he started talking about his stamp collection. 当他开始谈论他的邮票收藏时，她的目光变得呆滞无神。|近义词：polish, varnish, coat, cover, dull|反义词：-|v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *glas*, from Late Latin *glacia* "ice," from Latin *glacies* "ice."  The connection is likely due to the smooth, glassy appearance of ice.
