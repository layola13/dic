doubleminded:/ˌdʌblˈmaɪndɪd/| adj.|优柔寡断的，三心二意的|Having two minds; undecided; wavering; unstable in opinion or purpose.|She was doubleminded about accepting the job offer. 她对于是否接受这份工作犹豫不决。|Synonyms: ambivalent, irresolute, uncertain, wavering, hesitant|Antonyms: resolute, decisive, determined, steadfast|adjective
