gilets:/ˈdʒɪleɪz/ (UK), /ʒɪˈleɪz/ (US)| n. | 马甲；防弹背心 | A sleeveless padded jacket, often worn over other clothes for warmth. Also, a bulletproof vest.| He wore a bright yellow gilet for visibility on his bike. 他骑自行车时穿了一件亮黄色的马甲以提高可见度。|  The police officer's gilet protected him from the gunfire.  警察的防弹背心保护他免受枪击。| synonyms: vest, body warmer | antonyms: coat, jacket | noun
