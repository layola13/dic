mantispid: /ˈmæntɪspɪd/ | n. | 螳蛉 | An insect of the order Mantispida, which has a long prothorax and raptorial forelegs similar to those of a praying mantis. |  Mantispids are fascinating insects that mimic praying mantises. 螳蛉是一种迷人的昆虫，模仿螳螂。

Mantispids are often found near woodpiles where their prey resides. 螳蛉经常在它们的猎物居住的木堆附近被发现。 | 近义词: mantisfly | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific insect) | 词性: noun
