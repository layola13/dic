fritterer:/ˈfrɪtərər/| n.|挥霍者，浪费者|Someone who fritters something away, especially time or money.  A person who spends small amounts of money or time on unnecessary things.|He was a fritterer of time and talent. 他是个浪费时间和才能的人。|近义词：spendthrift, squanderer, waster|反义词：saver, economizer|noun
