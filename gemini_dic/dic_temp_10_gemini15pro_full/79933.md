gnomology:/nɒˈmɒlədʒi/ | n. | 格言学；箴言集 | The study of maxims, proverbs, or aphorisms; a collection of maxims. |  He was a student of gnomology, fascinated by the wisdom contained in short, pithy sayings. 他是格言学的学生，着迷于简短精辟的谚语中蕴含的智慧。| synonyms:  proverbiology, paremiology | antonyms:  None | noun

gnomologic:/ˌnɒməˈlɒdʒɪk/ | adj. | 格言的；箴言体的 | Relating to gnomology or maxims. |  His gnomologic approach to writing made his essays both concise and insightful. 他写文章的方式很精炼，使他的文章既简洁又有见地。| synonyms:  aphoristic, proverbial, sententious | antonyms:  prolix, verbose | adjective
