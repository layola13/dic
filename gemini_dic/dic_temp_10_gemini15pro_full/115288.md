manlihood:/ˈmænˌlɪˌhʊd/| n.|男子气概，成年|The state of being a man; manly qualities.  The qualities traditionally associated with men, such as courage, strength, and independence.|He finally proved his manhood by facing his fears.|Synonyms: masculinity, virility, maturity|Antonyms: boyhood, childhood, immaturity|noun
