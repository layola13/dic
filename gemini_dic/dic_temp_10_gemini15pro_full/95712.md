incongruousness:/ˌɪnkɒŋˈɡruːəsnəs/ /ˌɪnkɑːŋˈɡruːəsnəs/
n.
不协调，不一致，不相称
the state or quality of being incongruous; lack of harmony or appropriateness.
The incongruousness of his formal attire at the beach party was noticeable. 他在海滩派对上穿着正式服装，显得格格不入。
incongruity, disparity, incompatibility, inconsistency, inappropriateness
harmony, appropriateness, congruity, suitability, consistency
noun
