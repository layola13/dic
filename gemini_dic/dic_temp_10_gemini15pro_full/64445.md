eridanus:/ɪˈrɪdənəs/ /ɛˈrɪdənəs/| n.|波江座; 天河座|A constellation in the southern hemisphere, said to represent the river Eridanus into which Phaethon fell after his disastrous attempt to drive the chariot of the sun.|The faint constellation Eridanus meanders across the southern sky. 波江座蜿蜒穿过南方的天空。|Near Antonyms: Ursa Major, Ursa Minor|Far Antonyms:  north star, pole star|noun
