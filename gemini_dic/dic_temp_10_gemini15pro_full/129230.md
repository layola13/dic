noctiferous:/nɒkˈtɪfərəs/ | adj. |夜行性的；在夜间活动的|Carrying or bringing night; night-producing.  Active in the night.|Nocturnal animals, such as bats and owls, are noctiferous, preferring the dark hours for hunting and foraging. 夜行动物，如蝙蝠和猫头鹰，都是夜行性的，它们喜欢在黑暗中狩猎和觅食。

The noctiferous insects filled the night air with their buzzing. 夜行性昆虫的嗡嗡声充满了夜空。|近义词：nocturnal, crepuscular |反义词:diurnal |词性:adjective
