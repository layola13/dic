equivote:/ˈiːkwɪˌvoʊt/
noun
同票；相等的投票数
A situation in which two or more candidates or options receive the same number of votes.
The election resulted in an equivote, requiring a recount.  选举结果是票数相同，需要重新计票。
tie, draw, deadlock
majority, landslide, clear win
n.
