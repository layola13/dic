excusal: /ɪkˈskjuːzəl/ | n. | 借口；辩解；免除 | An explanation offered to justify or obtain forgiveness for a fault or offense; release from an obligation. |  He offered a flimsy excuse for his tardiness. 他为自己的迟到找了个站不住脚的借口。| synonyms: apology, justification, alibi, plea, pretext | antonyms: accusation, blame, condemnation | noun

excusals: /ɪkˈskjuːzəlz/ | n. | 借口（复数）；辩解（复数）；免除（复数） |  Multiple explanations offered to justify or obtain forgiveness for faults or offenses; releases from obligations. | They offered various excusals for their absences. 他们为缺席提供了各种各样的借口。| synonyms: apologies, justifications, alibis, pleas, pretexts | antonyms: accusations, blames, condemnations | noun
