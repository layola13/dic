bucker:/ˈbʌkər/| n.|桶; 一桶的量; (尤指马术运动中的)障碍物;  (非正式)美元|A bucket is a round, open container with a handle, used for carrying liquids. It can also refer to the amount contained in a bucket, a jump in equestrian sports (especially one using a water jump), or (informally) a dollar.|He filled the bucket with water. 他把桶装满了水。|Synonyms: pail, container, vessel|Antonyms:  (For container meaning) lid, cover|noun

bucker:/ˈbʌkər/| v.| (马)猛然弓背跃起; 猛烈震动; 颠簸|If a horse bucks, it jumps into the air and brings its front legs down hard.|The horse bucked and threw the rider. 马猛然弓背跃起，把骑手摔了下来。|Synonyms:  rear, jump, leap|Antonyms: settle, calm|verb
