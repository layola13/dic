industrialize:/ɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪz/ (US), /ɪnˈdʌstrɪəlaɪz/ (UK)| v. |工业化|To develop industries in (a country or region) on a wide scale.|例句：The government implemented policies to industrialize the nation. 政府实施了工业化国家的政策。|例句：Many developing countries are trying to industrialize their economies. 许多发展中国家正在努力实现经济工业化。|近义词：modernize, develop, mechanize|反义词:ruralize, deindustrialize|词性:动词

词源:
From industry + -ize.  The suffix "-ize" comes from Greek "-ίζειν" (-izein), and forms verbs meaning "to make or become like," "to subject to," "to engage in," "to become," "to treat with," "to cause to resemble."
