giblets:/ˈdʒɪblɪts/| n. |内脏，杂碎|The giblets of a chicken include the heart, liver, and gizzard.  鸡的内脏包括心脏、肝脏和胗。|例句：She used the giblets to make a rich gravy. 她用内脏做了一份浓郁的肉汁。

例句：The giblets were removed before the chicken was roasted.  在烤鸡之前，内脏被去除了。|近义词：offal, innards|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

giblet:/ˈdʒɪblɪt/| adj. |微不足道的，琐碎的 (rarely used)|  A giblet of information is a tiny, often insignificant piece. 一点点信息是微不足道的，通常无关紧要的。|例句：He offered a few giblet details about the project. 他提供了一些关于该项目的零星细节。

例句：The report contained only a few giblet facts. 这份报告只包含了一些零碎的事实。|近义词：trivial, insignificant|反义词:significant, important|词性:adjective
