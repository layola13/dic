inalienable:/ɪnˈeɪliənəbl/ /ɪnˈeɪljənəbl/
adj.
不可剥夺的；不可分割的
not able to be taken away or given away by the possessor.
例句：Everyone has the inalienable right to freedom of speech. 每个人都拥有不可剥夺的言论自由权利。

例句：The right to life, liberty, and the pursuit of happiness are considered inalienable rights. 生命权、自由权和追求幸福的权利被认为是不可剥夺的权利。

近义词：inherent, absolute, inviolable, unassailable
反义词：alienable, transferable, revocable
词性：adjective
