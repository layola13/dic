musa:/ˈmjuːsə/ | n. |香蕉；芭蕉属植物；缪斯（希腊神话中掌管艺术和科学的九位女神）|A genus of large herbaceous flowering plants in the family Musaceae, native to tropical Asia and Australia, some species of which are cultivated worldwide for their nutritious fruit. Also: the fruit of these plants, especially the banana.  In Greek mythology, each of the nine goddesses, the daughters of Zeus and Mnemosyne, who preside over the arts and sciences.|例句：The musa basjoo is a hardy banana plant.  芭蕉是一种耐寒的香蕉植物。

例句：I enjoy eating musa acuminata, commonly known as the Cavendish banana. 我喜欢吃尖叶蕉，也就是我们常说的卡文迪什香蕉。|近义词：banana, plantain|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
