almsgiving:/ˈɑːmzˌɡɪvɪŋ/
n.
施舍，赈济
the act of giving money or food to poor people
例句：Almsgiving is a common practice in many religions.  在许多宗教中，施舍是一种常见的做法。

例句：The organization relies on public almsgiving to fund its charitable work.  该组织依靠公众施舍来资助其慈善工作。

近义词：charity, donation, philanthropy
反义词：stinginess, miserliness
词性：名词

词源：From Old English ælmessegyfu, from ælmesse "alms" + gyfu "gift."  来自古英语 ælmessegyfu，由 ælmesse “施舍” + gyfu “礼物” 组成。
