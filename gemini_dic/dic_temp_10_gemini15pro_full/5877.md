ambassadresses:/æmˈbæsədrɪsɪz/
n.
女大使；女使节
A female ambassador; a woman who acts as an official representative of her country in a foreign country.
The ambassadresses from several countries met to discuss the trade agreement.
几位女大使会面讨论贸易协定。

近义词:female ambassadors, envoys
反义词:(No direct antonym,  but perhaps "citizen" in a general sense)
词性:Noun (plural)
