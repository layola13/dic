counterlath: /ˈkaʊntərˌlæθ/ /ˈkaʊntərˌlæθ/ | n. |反向板条，挂瓦条 |A thin strip of wood nailed to rafters, across which shingles or tiles are laid. | The roofer installed the counterlath before laying the tiles. 屋顶工人在铺瓦之前安装了反向板条。

The counterlath provides an air space beneath the tiles for ventilation.  反向板条在瓦片下方提供了一个用于通风的空气空间。|近义词: furring strip, batten |反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
