fictitiousness:/fɪkˈtɪʃəsnəs/
n.
虚构性；编造；捏造
The fictitiousness of the story was obvious.  这个故事的虚构性显而易见。
The author acknowledged the fictitiousness of the characters. 作者承认了角色的虚构性。
Synonyms: fabrication, invention, fictionality, artificiality
Antonyms: reality, truth, factuality
ETYMOLOGY->
From fictitious + -ness.  Fictitious comes from Late Latin fictitius "artificial, feigned," from Latin fictus "formed, shaped, made, invented, devised, feigned, false," past participle of fingere "to shape, form, devise, invent," from PIE root *dheigh- "to form, build."
