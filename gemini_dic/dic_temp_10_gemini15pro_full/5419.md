almighty:/ɔːlˈmaɪti/ | adj. | 全能的，万能的；非常强大的 | Having unlimited power; all-powerful. |  God is often referred to as almighty. 上帝常被称为全能的。

The almighty dollar seems to rule everything these days.  如今似乎万能的金钱主宰一切。 | 近义词: omnipotent, all-powerful | 反义词: powerless, weak | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Old English *eallmihtig (unattested), from eall (“all”) + mihtig (“mighty”). Cognate with German allmächtig, Dutch almachtig.
