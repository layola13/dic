boisterous:/ˈbɔɪstərəs/ | adj. |喧闹的；狂暴的；吵闹的|Full of noisy, rough, and energetic activity.| The children were boisterous and full of energy. 孩子们喧闹活泼，精力充沛。|  The party was a boisterous affair. 派对很热闹。 | synonyms:  noisy, rowdy, rambunctious, clamorous, uproarious | antonyms: quiet, calm, peaceful, tranquil, serene | adjective

Etymology:
From Middle English *boistous, borrowed from Anglo-Norman boistous (“rough, clumsy, violent”), perhaps ultimately from Old French boiste (“box”), but the connection is uncertain.
