counterassault:/ˈkaʊntərəˌsɔːlt/| n. |反突击，反攻|An attack made in response to an enemy attack.  A counterassault aims to regain lost ground or disrupt the enemy's advance.|The general ordered a swift counterassault to push back the enemy forces. 将军下令迅速反攻，击退敌军。|
The troops launched a daring counterassault under heavy fire.  部队冒着猛烈的炮火发动了大胆的反攻。|
Synonyms: counterattack, offensive, riposte|
Antonyms: retreat, withdrawal, defense|
noun


counterassault:/ˈkaʊntərəˌsɔːlt/| v. |反突击，反攻|To attack in response to an enemy attack.  To launch a counteroffensive.|The commander decided to counterassault the enemy's flank. 指挥官决定反攻敌人的侧翼。|
The soldiers counterassaulted with surprising ferocity. 士兵们以惊人的凶猛进行反击。|
Synonyms: counterattack, strike back, retaliate|
Antonyms: retreat, withdraw, defend|
