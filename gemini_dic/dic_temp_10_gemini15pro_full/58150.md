drought:/draʊt/| n.|干旱，旱灾|A prolonged period of abnormally low rainfall, leading to a shortage of water.|The drought caused severe crop failures.  干旱导致庄稼严重歉收。|Synonyms: aridity, dryness|Antonyms: flood, deluge|noun
