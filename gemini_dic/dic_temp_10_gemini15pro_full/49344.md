delineating:/dɪˈlɪnɪeɪtɪŋ/
verb
描绘，勾勒，描述
representing accurately or precisely
The artist delineated the scene with precise strokes.  这位艺术家用精确的笔触描绘了这个场景。
describing or portraying something precisely
The report clearly delineates the steps involved in the process. 报告清晰地描述了该过程涉及的步骤。

近义词: outlining, sketching, describing, portraying, defining, explaining
反义词: obscuring, confusing, blurring

词性: verb (present participle)
词源: From Latin dēlineāre (“to sketch, trace”), from dē- (“down, completely”) + lineāre (“to make a line”), from līnea (“line”).
