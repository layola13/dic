joshing:/ˈdʒoʊʃɪŋ/
verb
开玩笑；戏弄
Engaging in playful teasing or joking.
例句：They were just joshing around, don't take it seriously. 他们只是在开玩笑，别当真。

例句：He's always joshing with his friends. 他总是和他的朋友们开玩笑。


近义词：teasing, kidding, joking, bantering
反义词：serious, earnest
词性：v.
