parakeet:/ˈpærəkiːt/| n. |长尾小鹦鹉|A small, slender parrot with a long, graduated tail.|例句：The parakeet perched on her finger. 长尾小鹦鹉栖息在她的手指上。

例句：My parakeet mimics everything I say. 我的鹦鹉会模仿我说的一切。|近义词：parrot, budgie |反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:
From French *perroquet*, ultimately from Spanish *periquito* (small parrot), diminutive of *perico* (parrot).
