dethroner:/diːˈθroʊnər/
n.|废黜者， dethroner|Someone who removes a monarch from power. |The dethroner seized the crown and proclaimed himself the new king.|近义词：deposer, ouster, usurper|反义词:supporter, upholder|词性:noun
dethrone:/diːˈθroʊn/
vt.|废黜，罢免|To remove a monarch from power; to remove someone from a position of authority or dominance.|The rebels dethroned the king and established a republic.|近义词：depose, oust, unseat|反义词:install, enthrone|词性:verb

词源:
From de- + throne.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "down" or "away from."  "Throne" refers to the seat of a monarch.
