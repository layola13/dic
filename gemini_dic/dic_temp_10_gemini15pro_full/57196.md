down-payment:/ˌdaʊnˈpeɪmənt/ |n.| 预付款，首付款 |A portion of the total purchase price paid upfront, usually for a large purchase like a house or car.  It reduces the remaining balance that needs to be financed.|  We made a 20% down payment on our new house. 我们新房子付了20%的首付。| synonyms: deposit, initial payment | antonyms: final payment | noun

down-payment:/ˌdaʊnˈpeɪmənt/ |n.| 定金 | An initial amount of money paid as a promise to fulfill a contract. | The down payment secures the purchase and shows the seller you're serious. 这笔定金确保了购买，并向卖家表明你是认真的。| synonyms: earnest money | antonyms: balance due | noun
