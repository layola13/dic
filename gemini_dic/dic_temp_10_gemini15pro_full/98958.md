inter-european:/ˌɪntərjʊərəˈpiːən/| adj.|欧洲内部的，欧洲之间的|Relating to or involving relations between European countries.|例句：Inter-European trade has increased significantly after the establishment of the European Union. 欧洲内部贸易在欧盟成立后显著增加。

例句：The inter-European railway network facilitates travel across the continent. 欧洲之间的铁路网络方便了整个大陆的旅行。|近义词：intra-European, transnational|反义词：extra-European, intercontinental|词性:形容词

词源:
"inter-" (prefix): Latin, meaning "between," "among," "mutually," "reciprocally."
"European":  Derived from "Europa," a figure in Greek mythology and the name of the continent.
