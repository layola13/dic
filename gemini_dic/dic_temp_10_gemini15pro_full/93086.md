/wɒt/ (UK) /wʌt/ (US)| pron. det. adv. int. | 什么，多么 | Used to ask for information about something or someone. | What is your name? 你叫什么名字？| Which, who | Nothing (as a direct antonym.  The absence of an answer would be silence or "I don't know") | pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection
