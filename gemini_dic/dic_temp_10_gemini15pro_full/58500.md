duelings:/ˈduːəlɪŋz/
noun
决斗（duel的名词复数）
fights between two people, often according to rules or a code of conduct.
The duelings in the old West were often over matters of honor.  旧西部的决斗通常是为了荣誉而进行的。
The novel depicted several dramatic duelings with swords. 小说描绘了几场戏剧性的剑术决斗。
近义词: combats, fights, battles
反义词: truces, peace, agreements
词性:n.
