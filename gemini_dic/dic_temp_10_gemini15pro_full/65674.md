even-tempered:/ˈiːvən ˈtempərd/| adj.|性情平和的；沉稳的；不易激动的|Having a calm and stable temperament; not easily angered or upset.|She is known for her even-tempered personality, rarely getting ruffled even under pressure. 她以性情平和著称，即使在压力下也很少慌乱。|例句：He remained even-tempered despite the provocation. 尽管受到了挑衅，他仍然保持冷静。|近义词：calm, composed, placid, serene, tranquil|反义词：irascible, irritable, short-tempered, volatile|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "even" (meaning steady, uniform) + "tempered" (meaning having a particular temperament). "Temper" ultimately comes from Latin "temperare" (to mix, blend, moderate).
