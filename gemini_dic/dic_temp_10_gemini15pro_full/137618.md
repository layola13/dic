nontrusts:/ˈnɒnˌtrʌsts/ | n. | 非信托财产/非信托机构 |  Assets or organizations that are not held or managed within a trust structure.  Generally referring to assets or entities not subject to the legal framework and fiduciary responsibilities of a trust. |  Example:  His nontrusts were subject to estate tax. 他的非信托财产需要缴纳遗产税。| 近义词: non-trust assets | 反义词: trust assets/trusts | 词性: 名词
