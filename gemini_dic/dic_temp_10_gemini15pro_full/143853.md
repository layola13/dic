outplaying:/ˌaʊtˈpleɪɪŋ/
verb
胜过，以技巧取胜
To perform better than another player or team in a game or sport, especially through greater skill or strategy.
例句：He outplayed his opponent in the chess match. 他在棋赛中技高一筹，胜过了对手。

例句：The team outplayed their rivals and secured a place in the finals.  该队技高一筹，战胜了对手，获得了决赛资格。

近义词：outmaneuver, surpass, defeat, beat
反义词：lose to, be defeated by
词性：v.
