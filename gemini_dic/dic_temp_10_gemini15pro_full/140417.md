ok's:/oʊˈkeɪz/ |n.| 同意；许可；批准 |Indicates agreement or approval, often used as a plural of "OK" when referring to multiple instances of approval or permission. | The ok's from the board finally came through. 董事会的批准终于通过了。|Synonyms: approvals, permissions, consents| Antonyms: disapprovals, denials, rejections | noun

ok's:/oʊˈkeɪz/ |v.| 批准；认可 (Third-person singular simple present tense of "OK")| To give approval or acceptance to something. | He ok's the project budget. 他批准了项目预算。|Synonyms: approves, authorizes, sanctions| Antonyms: disapproves, rejects, vetoes | verb

词源：OK 的复数形式，表示多项许可或批准。 OK 的词源有多种说法，其中最广为接受的说法是来源于19世纪初美国波士顿地区的一种幽默拼写法，将 "all correct" 缩写为 "oll korrect"，再简写为 "OK"。
