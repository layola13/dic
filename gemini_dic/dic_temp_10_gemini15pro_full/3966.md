agrostological:/ˌæɡrɒˈstɒlədʒɪkəl/
adjective

Relating to agrostology; the study of grasses.
Agrostological research is crucial for developing sustainable pasture management.  草本学研究对于发展可持续的牧场管理至关重要。
The botanist specialized in agrostological studies, focusing on the identification and classification of various grass species.  这位植物学家专门从事草本学研究，专注于各种草种的鉴定和分类。
