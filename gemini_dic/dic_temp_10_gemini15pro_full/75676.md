fulfilling:/fʊlˈfɪlɪŋ/
adjective
令人满足的；使人有成就感的
making someone satisfied or happy because of fully developing their character or abilities
例句：She finds her work as a teacher very fulfilling. 她觉得她作为一名教师的工作非常有成就感。

例句：Volunteering at the animal shelter is a fulfilling experience. 在动物收容所做志愿者是一项令人满足的经历。

近义词：satisfying, rewarding, gratifying
反义词：disappointing, frustrating, unfulfilling
词性: adjective
