pastises:/pɑːˈstiːz/ /pæˈstiːz/
n.
模仿作品；戏仿作品；（尤指）拙劣的模仿作品
An artistic work that imitates the style of another work or artist, often in a humorous or satirical way, especially a rather poor imitation.


The play was a weak pastiche of Shakespearean comedy.
这出戏是对莎士比亚喜剧的拙劣模仿。


This film is a pastiche of several classic horror movies.
这部电影是几部经典恐怖电影的模仿之作。


Synonyms: imitation, parody, travesty, spoof, takeoff
Antonyms: original, authentic, genuine


词性: noun
中文翻译: 模仿作品; 戏仿作品
