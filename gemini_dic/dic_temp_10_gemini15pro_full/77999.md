gentlemanhood:/ˈdʒɛntlmənhʊd/| n. |绅士风度，绅士身份|The state or quality of being a gentleman; gentlemanly conduct or behavior.  It also refers to the collective body of gentlemen.|He displayed true gentlemanhood by offering his seat to the elderly woman. 他展现了真正的绅士风度，把座位让给了那位老妇人。|近义词：chivalry, courtesy, gentility|反义词:rudeness, boorishness, incivility|词性:noun

Etymology:
From *gentleman* + *-hood*.  *Gentleman* comes from *gentle* (meaning 'noble, of good family') + *man*.  *-hood* is a suffix denoting a state, condition, or quality of being.
