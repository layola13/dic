overloup:/ˌoʊvərˈluːp/| n. v.|重叠，重复；交叠，部分一致|An overlap is a situation in which two or more things cover part of the same area.  重叠是指两个或多个事物覆盖同一区域的一部分的情况。|The shingles on the roof should overlap. 屋顶上的瓦片应该重叠。|近义词：intersection, convergence, coinciding, concurrence |反义词:divergence, separation|noun
overloup:/ˌoʊvərˈluːp/| v.|（与...）部分重叠；（与...）部分同时发生|If two or more activities or periods of time overlap, they have some parts that are the same. 如果两个或多个活动或时间段重叠，则它们有一些部分相同。|My musical tastes overlap with my brother's. 我的音乐品味和我哥哥的重叠。|近义词：coincide, intersect, |反义词:diverge, separate|verb
