oh-so:/ˌəʊ ˈsəʊ/| adj.|非常；极其；太…|Used to emphasize how great or extreme a quality or feeling is.  It is often used ironically or humorously.  It combines "oh" (an exclamation) with "so" (an intensifier).|"She gave him an oh-so-sweet smile." 她给了他一个非常甜美的微笑。|近义词：very, extremely, exceedingly|反义词：not very, slightly|adjective

oh-so:/ˌəʊ ˈsəʊ/| adv.|非常地；极其地；太…地|Used as an intensifier, often ironically or humorously.|"He was oh-so-charming." 他真是太迷人了。|近义词：very, extremely, exceedingly|反义词：not very, slightly|adverb
