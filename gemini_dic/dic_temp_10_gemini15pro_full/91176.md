hydrargochloride:/ˌhaɪdrɑːrɡoʊˈklɔːraɪd/ | n. | 氯化亚汞; 甘汞 |A white, odorless powder that is a compound of mercury and chlorine (Hg₂Cl₂).  It was formerly used as a purgative and disinfectant, also known as calomel.|  Hydrargochloride, or calomel, was once a common treatment for syphilis. 氯化亚汞，或甘汞，曾经是治疗梅毒的常用药物。|Synonym: calomel, mercurous chloride | Antonym: None (as it's a specific chemical compound) | noun
