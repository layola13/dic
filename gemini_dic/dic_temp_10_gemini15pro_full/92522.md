hypotrichosis:/ˌhaɪpoʊtraɪˈkoʊsɪs/ /ˌhaɪpəʊtraɪˈkəʊsɪs/
n.
hypotrichosis (少毛症)
A condition characterized by a deficiency or absence of hair where it normally grows.  It can affect the scalp, eyebrows, eyelashes, or other areas of the body. 指头发在通常生长的地方缺乏或没有毛发的疾病。它可以影响头皮、眉毛、睫毛或身体的其他部位。
Hypotrichosis can be inherited or acquired due to various factors. 少毛症可以是遗传的，也可以是由于各种因素后天获得的。
Synonym: atrichia, hairlessness
Antonym: hypertrichosis
词性: noun
