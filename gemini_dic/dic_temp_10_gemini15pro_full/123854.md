moralizers:/ˈmɔːrəlaɪzərz/
n.|说教者，道德家|People who preach or give lessons on morality, often in a self-righteous or tedious way.  Those who habitually moralize.|The moralizers were quick to condemn his actions. 这些说教者迅速谴责了他的行为。|preachers, sermonizers, didactic people| amoralists, libertines|noun
