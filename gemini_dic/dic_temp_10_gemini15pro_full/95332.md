incense-breathing:/ˈɪnsɛns briːðɪŋ/
adjective
焚香呼吸的；弥漫着香气的
The act of breathing in incense smoke, often as part of a religious or spiritual practice. It can also refer to an atmosphere filled with the scent of incense.
The incense-breathing ceremony filled the temple with a mystical aroma. 寺庙里焚香呼吸的仪式弥漫着神秘的香味。
Near synonyms: aromatic, fragrant, perfumed, scented
Opposite: odorless, unscented
词性: adjective
