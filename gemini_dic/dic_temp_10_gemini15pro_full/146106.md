overelliptically:/ˌoʊvərɪˈlɪptɪkli/
adverb|过于省略地|In an excessively elliptical or concise manner, omitting more words or information than is considered standard or clear.|The instructions were written overelliptically, leaving many users confused.|近义词: concisely, tersely, succinctly, compactly|反义词: verbosely, prolixly, lengthily|词性:副词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "elliptical" (adjective meaning "containing an ellipsis; expressed with extreme or excessive economy") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)  The word "elliptical" ultimately derives from the Greek word "elleipsis" meaning "a falling short" or "omission."
