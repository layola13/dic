gregariousnesses:/ˌɡrɛɡˈɛəriəsnəsɪz/
n.
群居性，合群性
The state or quality of being gregarious; sociability.
Her gregariousness made her the life of the party. 她的合群使她成为派对的灵魂人物。
sociability, conviviality, friendliness
aloofness, unsociability, reclusiveness
