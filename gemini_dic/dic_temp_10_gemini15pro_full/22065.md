bolide:/ˈboʊlaɪd/ /ˈbɒlaɪd/| n.|火流星；陨石；非常明亮的流星|A bolide is an extremely bright meteor, especially one that explodes in the atmosphere.|例句：The bolide streaked across the sky, leaving a fiery trail. 火流星划过天空，留下了一道火痕。

例句：Scientists believe the crater was created by a large bolide impact millions of years ago. 科学家们认为，这个陨石坑是由数百万年前一颗大型火流星撞击造成的。|近义词：meteor, fireball, meteoroid|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
