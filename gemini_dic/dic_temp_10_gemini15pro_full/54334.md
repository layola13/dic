disinherit:/ˌdɪsɪnˈherɪt/ | vt. |剥夺继承权，取消继承资格|To prevent (someone) from inheriting property, especially one's child.|He disinherited his son for marrying against his wishes. 他因为儿子违背他的意愿结婚而剥夺了他的继承权。|近义词：cut off, deprive |反义词: bequeath, endow|Verb
