outpeep:/ˌaʊtˈpiːp/
verb
窥视，胜过，比...看得更远
To look out further or more effectively than someone or something else.
From a hidden vantage point, she outpeeped the guards and signaled to her allies. 她从一个隐蔽的有利位置窥视守卫，并向她的盟友发出信号。
The tall sunflower outpeeped all the other plants in the garden.  这株高大的向日葵比花园里所有其他植物都看得更远。
synonyms: outview, overlook, ogle, peer, spy
antonyms: hide, conceal
词性：动词
