anorthosite:/ænˈɔːrθəˌsaɪt/
n.
斜长岩
An igneous rock composed primarily of plagioclase feldspar.
Anorthosite is the primary rock type of the lunar highlands. 斜长岩是月球高地的主要岩石类型。
The Adirondack Mountains are largely composed of anorthosite. 阿迪朗达克山脉主要由斜长岩构成。
