nondeterministic:/ˌnɒndɪˌtɜːmɪˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非确定性的 | Not determined in advance by the state of the system or by the input.  Unable to predict the next state or output based on the current state and input. |  A nondeterministic algorithm may produce different results on different runs with the same input.  一个非确定性算法在使用相同输入的不同运行中可能会产生不同的结果。

The behavior of the system is nondeterministic. 该系统的行为是非确定性的。 | 近义词: unpredictable, random, stochastic | 反义词: deterministic, predictable | 词性: adjective
