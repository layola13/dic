enclosable:/ɪnˈkloʊzəbl/
adjective
可封闭的; 可附上的
That can be enclosed.  Something that is capable of being enclosed, typically within a container or envelope.
The documents are enclosable in this padded envelope.  这些文件可以装在这个衬垫信封里。
The garden is enclosable with a fence. 这个花园可以用栅栏围起来。
Synonyms: containable, insertable
Antonyms: open, unenclosed
词性: adjective
