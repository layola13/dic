parallel's:/ˈpærəˌlɛlz/
noun
平行线；相似之处；比较
lines, planes, or surfaces side by side and having the same distance continuously between them; something having parts or aspects that correspond closely to something else.
The parallels between the two cases are striking. 这两个案例之间的相似之处是惊人的。
synonyms: similarity, resemblance, likeness, analogy, correspondence
antonyms: difference, dissimilarity, divergence, contrast

He drew a parallel between the two situations. 他比较了两种情况，指出它们的相似之处。

antonyms: difference, contrast, dissimilarity
