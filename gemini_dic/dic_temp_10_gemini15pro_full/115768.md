marination:/ˌmærɪˈneɪʃən/| n.|腌制，浸泡|The process of soaking foods in a seasoned liquid before cooking.  This process tenderizes and flavors the food.|The marination of the chicken made it very tender.  鸡肉的腌制使它非常嫩。|近义词：soaking, steeping, maceration|反义词:|noun
