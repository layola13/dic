flysheet:/ˈflaɪʃiːt/| n.|防雨布，帐篷外罩|A waterproof sheet used as a secondary outer covering for a tent, providing additional protection from rain and wind.|We used a flysheet to protect our tent from the heavy rain. 我们用防雨布保护我们的帐篷免受暴雨的侵袭。|Synonyms: outer tent, rain fly, tarp|Antonyms: inner tent|noun
