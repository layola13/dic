depletable:/dɪˈpliːtəbl/
adjective
可耗尽的
able to be depleted, or used up.
The Earth's depletable resources must be managed carefully.  地球上可耗尽的资源必须谨慎管理。
近义词: exhaustible, consumable
反义词: inexhaustible, renewable
词性: adjective

deplete:/dɪˈpliːt/
verb
耗尽，用尽
reduce the amount of something.
Overfishing has severely depleted fish stocks in the area. 过度捕捞严重耗尽了该地区的鱼类资源。
近义词: exhaust, drain, consume
反义词: replenish, augment, increase
词性: verb
