erodent:/ɪˈroʊdənt/| adj. n.|腐蚀的，侵蚀的；腐蚀剂|Causing erosion; an agent of erosion.|Acid rain is an erodent that can damage buildings and statues. 酸雨是一种腐蚀剂，会损坏建筑物和雕像。|The harsh winds and blowing sand acted as an erodent on the exposed rock formations.  狂风和飞沙对裸露的岩层起到了侵蚀作用。|近义词：corrosive, abrasive|反义词：protective, preservative|词性: adjective, noun|词源: From Latin erodent- ‘gnawing away’, from the verb erodere, from e- (variant of ex- ) ‘out’ + rodere ‘to gnaw’.
