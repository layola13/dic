eventual:/ɪˈventʃuəl/ | adj. | 最终的，最后的 | Happening at the end of a process or period of time. |  The eventual cost of the project was much higher than expected.  这个项目的最终成本比预期的要高得多。

The eventual winner of the competition was a dark horse. 比赛最后的赢家是一匹黑马。

近义词: final, ultimate, last, resulting, consequent | 反义词: initial, immediate, first, original | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin eventualis, from eventus ‘outcome’ (see event).
