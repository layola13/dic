odes:/əʊdz/ | n. | 颂诗；颂歌 | A lyric poem typically of elaborate or irregular metrical form and expressive of exalted or enthusiastic emotion. |  Shelley's "Ode to the West Wind" is a powerful meditation on nature and the human spirit.  雪莱的《西风颂》是对自然和人类精神的深刻沉思。| synonyms: poem, lyric, hymn, song, anthem | antonyms: prose, story, narrative | noun
