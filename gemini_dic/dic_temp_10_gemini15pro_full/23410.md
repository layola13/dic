bracingness:/ˈbreɪsɪŋnɪs/| n. |令人振奋；鼓舞人心|The quality of being bracing; invigorating or stimulating.|The bracingness of the mountain air refreshed her. 山上清新的空气让她精神焕发。|近义词：invigoration, stimulation, refreshment|反义词: lethargy, dullness|词性:名词

词源:  From brace (v.) + -ing + -ness.  The verb "brace" originally meant "to make ready, prepare," and later acquired the sense of "to strengthen, invigorate."  The suffix "-ing" forms a present participle or gerund, and "-ness" turns it into a noun denoting a state or quality.
