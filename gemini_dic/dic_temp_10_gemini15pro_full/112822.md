lucidly:/ˈluːsɪdli/ | adv. | 清晰地，明白地 | In a clear and easily understood manner. | She explained the complex issue lucidly, making it easy for everyone to grasp. 她清晰地解释了这个复杂的问题，使每个人都容易理解。| clearly, cogently, intelligibly | vaguely, obscurely, unclearly | adverb

Etymology:
From Latin "lucidus" (clear, bright, shining) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).
