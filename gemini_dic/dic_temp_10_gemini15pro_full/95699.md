inconformable:/ˌɪnkənˈfɔːrməbl/ | adj. | 不整合的，不一致的 | Not conforming to an established rule, type, or pattern; inconsistent.  Specifically in geology, referring to rock layers that are not deposited in a continuous sequence, indicating a gap in time. | The unconformable contact between the two rock strata suggested a period of erosion or uplift. 这两个岩层之间的不整合接触表明存在一段时间的侵蚀或抬升。|  discordant, incompatible, inconsistent, disparate | conformable, consistent, harmonious | adjective
