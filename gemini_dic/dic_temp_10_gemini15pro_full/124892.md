muddledly:/ˈmʌdldli/ | adv. | 混乱地，糊涂地 | In a confused or muddled manner; unclearly or vaguely. | She explained the situation muddledly, leaving everyone even more confused. 她含糊不清地解释了情况，让每个人都更加困惑了。| Synonyms: confusedly, vaguely, unclearly, incoherently | Antonyms: clearly, distinctly, coherently, lucidly |词性: 副词

muddled:/ˈmʌdld/ | adj. | 混乱的，糊涂的 | In a state of confusion or disorder. | His thinking was muddled after the long journey. 长途旅行后，他的思维混乱了。| Synonyms: confused, bewildered, perplexed, disoriented | Antonyms: clear, lucid, coherent, organized |词性: 形容词

muddle:/ˈmʌdl/ | v. | 使混乱，弄糟 | To bring into a disordered or confusing state. | Don't muddle the papers on my desk. 不要弄乱我桌子上的文件。| Synonyms: confuse, disarrange, disorder, jumble | Antonyms: organize, arrange, tidy, clarify |词性: 动词

词源: From the verb "muddle," which likely originates from the Middle Low German word "muddelen," meaning "to make muddy or turbid."  This meaning extended metaphorically to describe mental confusion.
