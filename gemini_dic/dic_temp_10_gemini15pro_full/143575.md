outhouses:/ˈaʊthaʊzɪz/
n.
户外厕所；茅厕
A small building outside a house, used as a toilet.
The outhouse smelled terrible.  户外厕所的气味很难闻。

The old farmhouse had an outhouse instead of indoor plumbing.  那座旧农舍没有室内管道系统，只有一个户外厕所。

近义词: privy, latrine, lavatory
反义词: bathroom, restroom, toilet (indoor)
词性: 名词 (plural)
