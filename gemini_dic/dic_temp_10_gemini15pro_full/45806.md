custodier:/kʌˈstoʊdiən/ | n. | 保管人，监护人，管理人 | A person who has responsibility for or looks after something. | The custodian of the museum is responsible for its security. 博物馆的管理员负责其安全。 | synonyms: guardian, keeper, caretaker, superintendent | antonyms: owner, possessor | noun

custodier:/kʌˈstoʊdiən/ | n. | 托管人 | A person or institution that holds and protects assets on behalf of the beneficiary. | The bank acted as custodian of the client's funds.  银行作为客户资金的托管人。| synonyms: trustee, guardian, keeper | antonyms: beneficiary, owner | noun
