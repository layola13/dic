disorientating: /dɪsˈɔːriənˌteɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 使人迷失方向的，使人迷惑的 | Causing someone to lose their sense of direction or confusion. | The flashing lights were disorientating. 闪爍的灯光令人迷失方向。 |  The complex maze was disorientating, and many people got lost. 复杂的迷宫令人迷惑，许多人迷路了。| 近义词: confusing, bewildering, perplexing | 反义词: orientating, guiding, clarifying | 词性: adjective
