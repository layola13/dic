osmiamate:/ɒzˈmiːəmeɪt/ | n. |锇酸盐|A salt of osmic acid (OsO4). Osmium tetroxide reacts with a base to form an osmiamate salt.|Potassium osmiamate is a useful oxidant in organic synthesis.|Synonyms:  osmate (although technically different, often used interchangeably in informal contexts)|Antonyms: None (lack of a direct chemical opposite)|Noun
