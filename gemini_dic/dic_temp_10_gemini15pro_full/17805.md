bedroom: /ˈbedruːm/ (US) /ˈbedrʊm/ (UK)| n. |卧室|A room furnished and used for sleeping.|例句：I like to read in my bedroom before going to sleep. 我喜欢在睡觉前在我的卧室里看书。

例句：The children are playing in their bedroom. 孩子们正在他们的卧室里玩耍。


近义词：chamber, sleeping room|反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider spaces not used for sleeping, like living room, kitchen) |词性: noun
