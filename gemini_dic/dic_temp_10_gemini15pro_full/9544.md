antisepalous:/ˌæntiˈsɛpələs/
adjective
对萼的；与萼片对生的
Positioned opposite the sepals.
The petals of the flower are antisepalous, arising directly across from the sepals.  这朵花的花瓣是对萼的，直接从萼片对面长出来。
Antisepalous stamens are a characteristic feature of this plant family.  对萼雄蕊是这个植物科的特征。
近义词: opposite the sepals
反义词: antepetalous
词性: adjective

Etymology:
anti- (Greek) : against, opposite
sepalous (from sepal): relating to sepals.  Sepal itself comes from New Latin "sepalum," coined by Necker from Greek 'skepe', meaning 'covering.'
