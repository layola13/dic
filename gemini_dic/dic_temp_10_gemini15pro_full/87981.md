high-minded:/ˌhaɪˈmaɪndɪd/
adjective
高尚的；高洁的
Having noble or elevated principles; of high moral character.
He was a high-minded man, always striving to do what was right. 他是一个高尚的人，总是努力做正确的事。
She made a high-minded decision to donate her inheritance to charity. 她做了一个高尚的决定，把她的遗产捐给了慈善机构。
近义词:noble, virtuous, principled, ethical, moral
反义词:base, ignoble, mean, petty, selfish
词性:adjective

词源:
From high + minded.  "Minded" acts as a suffix here, meaning "having a specified kind of mind or outlook."  The concept of "high" refers to elevated moral or intellectual standards.
