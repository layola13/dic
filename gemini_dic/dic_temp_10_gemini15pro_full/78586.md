giantism: /ˈdʒaɪɡənˌtɪzəm/ | n. |巨人症；巨大发育；巨大化 | A condition characterized by excessive growth and height significantly above average, usually caused by overproduction of growth hormone by the pituitary gland. |  Gigantism is a rare disorder. 巨人症是一种罕见的疾病。|  His extreme height is due to gigantism. 他的身高异常是由于巨人症。 | 近义词: acromegaly (肢端肥大症, 更特指成年后发病) | 反义词: dwarfism (侏儒症) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *gigantismus, from Latin gigant-, gigas "giant" + -ismus -ism.
