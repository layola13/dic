diss:/dɪs/
verb|
侮辱，轻视，贬低|To speak disrespectfully to or criticize someone or something.|
He dissed his opponent's skills during the rap battle. 他在说唱比赛中贬低了对手的技巧。
The movie critic dissed the film as predictable and boring.  影评人批评这部电影老套又无聊。

|近义词:criticize, belittle, insult, put down, slam|
反义词:praise, compliment, commend|
词性:v.


diss:/dɪs/
noun|
侮辱，轻蔑|An expression of disrespect or criticism.|
The rapper's diss track sparked a feud between the two artists.  这位说唱歌手的diss曲目引发了两位艺术家之间的不和。
She took his comment as a personal diss. 她把他的评论视为对个人的侮辱。
|近义词:insult, slight, put-down|
反义词:compliment, praise|
词性:n.
