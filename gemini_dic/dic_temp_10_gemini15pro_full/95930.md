incrustation:/ɪnˌkrʌˈsteɪʃən/ | n. | 积垢；外壳；镶嵌装饰 |A hard coating or deposit of something, such as minerals or plaque.  A decorative covering or crust. | The pipes were clogged with incrustation. 管道被积垢堵塞了。| The ancient vase was covered in an incrustation of jewels.  这古老的花瓶上镶嵌着珠宝。| synonyms: coating, deposit, covering, crust, scale, film, layer, patina | antonyms: erosion, wearing away | noun


incrust:/ɪnˈkrʌst/ | vt. | 包上硬壳；镶嵌 | To cover with a hard crust or coating; to decorate by embedding something in a surface. | The artist incrusted the box with semiprecious stones.  艺术家用半宝石装饰盒子。| Barnacles incrusted the hull of the ship. 藤壶附着在船体上，形成了硬壳。 | synonyms: coat, cover, encrust, inlay, embed | antonyms: strip, uncover, expose | verb
