diaphemetric:/ˌdaɪəˈfɛmɪtrɪk/
adjective
透射测量的，透射计的
Relating to or measured by a diaphemeter.  A diaphemeter is an instrument used to measure transparency or translucence.
The diaphemetric properties of the liquid were carefully analyzed.  这种液体的透射测量特性经过了仔细的分析。
近义词:transmissive, transparent
反义词:opaque
词性:adjective


Etymology:
From dia- (through) + -phemetric (measuring), from Greek *diaphainein (to show through) + -metric (measuring).
