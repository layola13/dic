adaptitude:/ˈæptɪtjuːd/ (US) /ˈæptɪtjuːd/ (UK)| n.| 才能，天资，适应性|The natural ability or skill to do something well; suitability or appropriateness.|She showed a natural aptitude for music. 她展现出天生的音乐才能。|近义词：talent, gift, ability, skill, suitability, fitness|反义词：inability, incompetence, unsuitability|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *aptitūdō*, from Latin *aptus* ("fit, suitable") + *-tūdō* (suffix forming abstract nouns).
