overferventnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrvəntnəsɪz/
noun
过度热情，过分狂热
The state of being excessively fervent or zealous.
His overferventnesses in religious matters alienated some of his friends. 他在宗教问题上的过度热情疏远了他的一些朋友。
Synonym:  excessiveness,  zealotry, fanaticism
Antonym: apathy, indifference, coolness
词性: 名词


overfervent:/ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrvənt/
adjective
过度热情的，过分狂热的
Excessively fervent or zealous.
Her overfervent support for the candidate bordered on obsession. 她对这位候选人的过度热情的支持近乎痴迷。
Synonym:  excessive, zealous, fanatical
Antonym: apathetic, indifferent, cool
词性: 形容词


fervent:/ˈfɜːrvənt/
adjective
热情的，热烈的
Having or displaying a passionate intensity.
She offered a fervent prayer for their safety. 她为他们的安全献上热切的祈祷。
Synonym: ardent, passionate, zealous
Antonym: apathetic, indifferent, cool
词性: 形容词
