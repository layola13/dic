houndiest:/ˈhaʊndɪɪst/
adjective
最猎犬般的，最热衷追捕的
Most like a hound, especially in persistence or keen scent.
The houndiest detective on the force wouldn't give up until he'd cracked the case.  这个警队里最像猎犬的侦探不破案不罢休。
近义词: most persistent, most relentless, most tenacious
反义词: least persistent, most easily dissuaded

词性: adjective
