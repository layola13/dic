cravings: /ˈkreɪvɪŋz/ | n. | 渴望，热望，瘾 | An intense desire for something, often food or a substance. |  I have been having cravings for chocolate all day. 我整天都渴望吃巧克力。

She had a sudden craving for ice cream. 她突然想吃冰淇淋。

| 近义词：yearnings, desires, hankerings, longings | 反义词: aversions, dislikes | 词性: noun (plural)
