dimyarian:/ˌdaɪmaɪˈɛəriən/| adj.| 双肌的，具有两束闭壳肌的| Having two adductor muscles (in bivalve mollusks).  These muscles are used to close the shell. | Example 1: The oyster is a dimyarian mollusk. 牡蛎是一种双肌的软体动物。

Example 2: Dimyarian bivalves are more common than monomyarian ones. 双肌瓣鳃纲动物比单肌瓣鳃纲动物更常见。| Near synonyms: bivalve, | Antonym: monomyarian | Adjective
