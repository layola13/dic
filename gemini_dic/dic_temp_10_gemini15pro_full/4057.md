aids:/eɪdz/ | n. |  艾滋病; 辅助器具; 帮助; | Acquired Immune Deficiency Syndrome, a disease in which there is a severe loss of the body's cellular immunity, greatly lowering the resistance to infection and malignancy. |  AIDS is a serious global health issue. 艾滋病是一个严重的全球健康问题。 | synonym: HIV, immunodeficiency | antonym: health, immunity | noun
