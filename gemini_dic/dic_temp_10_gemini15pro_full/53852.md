discontentful:/ˌdɪskənˈtɛntfʊl/
adjective
不满的；不满足的
Filled with or expressing discontent; dissatisfied.
The discontentful workers went on strike.  不满的工人举行罢工。
The king grew discontentful with his advisors. 国王对他的顾问越来越不满意。
近义词:discontented, dissatisfied, malcontent, disgruntled
反义词:content, satisfied, happy, pleased
词性:adjective

词源:
From discontent + -ful.  Discontent itself comes from the Old French descontentement, from des- (expressing negation) + contentement (contentment).
