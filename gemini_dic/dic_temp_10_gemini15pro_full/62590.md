enfeeblement:/ɪnˈfiːblmənt/| n. | 衰弱，虚弱 |The state of being weakened or debilitated, either physically or mentally. |  His long illness led to the enfeeblement of his body.  他的长期疾病导致他身体衰弱。| 近义词：weakening, debilitating, impairment | 反义词：strengthening, invigoration | noun


enfeeble:/ɪnˈfiːbl/ | vt. | 使衰弱，使无力 |To make weak or feeble. | The disease enfeebles the immune system.  这种疾病会削弱免疫系统。| 近义词：weaken, debilitate, undermine | 反义词：strengthen, invigorate | verb
