conceiver:/kənˈsiːvər/| n.|构思者，设想者，发明者|A conceiver is someone who forms an idea or plan in their mind. |The conceiver of the project was awarded a prize.  这个项目的构思者获得了奖项。|Synonyms: creator, inventor, originator, designer, architect, author, mastermind|Antonyms: imitator, copier, follower|noun
