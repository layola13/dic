mitigators:/ˈmɪtɪɡeɪtərz/| n. |缓和因素，减轻者|People or things that mitigate something; that make something less serious, severe, or painful. |The company has implemented several mitigators to reduce the risk of data breaches.  该公司已经实施了几项缓解措施，以降低数据泄露的风险。|synonyms: reducers, alleviators, moderators, diminishers |antonyms: aggravators, exacerbators, intensifiers|noun
