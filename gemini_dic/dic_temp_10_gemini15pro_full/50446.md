depreciated:/dɪˈpriːʃieɪtɪd/
adjective
贬值的；折旧的
Reduced in value.
例句：The car depreciated quickly after I bought it. 我的车在我买后很快就贬值了。

例句：Depreciated assets can be a liability for a company.  贬值的资产对公司来说可能是一种负债。

近义词：devalued, reduced, lessened, cheapened
反义词：appreciated, increased, enhanced
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *depretiatus*, past participle of *depretiare* "to lower the price of," from *de-* "down" + *pretium* "price."
