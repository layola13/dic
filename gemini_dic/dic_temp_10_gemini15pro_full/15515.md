baffle:/ˈbæfl/ (US), /ˈbæfəl/ (UK)| v. n.|使困惑，难倒；挡板，隔板|To confuse or frustrate someone completely; a device used to control the flow of air, light, or sound.|The mystery baffled the detectives for months.  这个谜题让侦探们困惑了几个月。|近义词：confuse, perplex, bewilder, frustrate, foil, thwart|反义词：clarify, enlighten, explain|词性: verb, noun
