heterozygote:/ˌhɛtərəˈzaɪɡoʊt/ | n. | 杂合子 | An individual having two different alleles of a particular gene or genes, and so giving rise to varying offspring. |  Example 1: The heterozygote for eye color had one allele for brown eyes and one for blue eyes.  杂合子的眼睛颜色有一个棕色眼睛的等位基因和一个蓝色眼睛的等位基因。

Example 2:  A heterozygote advantage can maintain genetic diversity in a population. 杂合子优势可以在种群中维持遗传多样性。 | synonym: hybrid | antonym: homozygote | noun
