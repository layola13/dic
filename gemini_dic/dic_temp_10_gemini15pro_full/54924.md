disposing:/dɪˈspəʊzɪŋ/
verb
处理，处置
the act of getting rid of something, especially by throwing it away
例句：The disposing of hazardous waste is regulated by law.  处置有害废物受法律管制。

例句：They are disposing of their old furniture. 他们正在处理他们的旧家具。

近义词：discarding, eliminating, getting rid of
反义词：keeping, retaining, storing
词性：动词 (present participle of dispose)
