legitimateness:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmɪtnəs/| n. |合法性；正统性|The quality or state of being legitimate; lawfulness. |The legitimacy of the election results was questioned. 选举结果的合法性受到了质疑。|Synonyms: validity, lawfulness, legality |Antonyms: illegality, illegitimacy, invalidity|noun


legitimate:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmət/| adj. |合法的；正当的；合理的|Conforming to the law or to rules. |His claim to the throne is legitimate. 他对王位的要求是合法的。|Synonyms: legal, lawful, valid |Antonyms: illegal, unlawful, invalid|adjective
