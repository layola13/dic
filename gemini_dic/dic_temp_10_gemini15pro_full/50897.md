desertification:/dɪˌzɜːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/
n.
荒漠化，沙漠化
The process by which fertile land becomes desert, typically as a result of drought, deforestation, or inappropriate agriculture.
例句：Desertification is a serious environmental problem in many parts of the world.  世界许多地区的荒漠化是一个严重的环境问题。

例句：The ongoing desertification is threatening the livelihoods of millions.  持续的荒漠化正在威胁着数百万人的生计。

近义词：aridification, degradation
反义词：reforestation, afforestation
词性：noun

词源: From desert + -ification.  来自desert（沙漠）+ -ification（名词后缀，表示过程或结果）。
