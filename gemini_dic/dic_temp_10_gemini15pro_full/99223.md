intercolonized:/ˌɪntərˈkɑːlənaɪzd/ | /ˌɪntəˈkɒlənaɪzd/ | v. |相互殖民，互相殖民|The act of two or more colonies establishing settlements within each other's territories.  The two powers intercolonized each other's territories throughout the 18th century.  两个强国在整个 18世纪都在互相殖民对方的领土。|例句：The two empires intercolonized each other's territories, leading to a complex mix of cultures.  两个帝国互相殖民对方的领土，导致了复杂的文化融合。

例句：The intercolonized populations developed a unique pidgin language. 相互殖民的人群发展出了一种独特的皮钦语。|近义词：cross-colonize, mutually colonize|反义词:decolonize|词性:动词
