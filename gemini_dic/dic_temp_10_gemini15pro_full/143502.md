outglowed: /ˌaʊtˈɡloʊd/
verb
胜过…的光辉；比…更明亮
shone more brightly than; surpassed in brightness
The setting sun outglowed the city lights. 落日比城市灯光更明亮。
Synonyms: outshone, eclipsed
Antonyms: dimmed, overshadowed
词性: 动词
