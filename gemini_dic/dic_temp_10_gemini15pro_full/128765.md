nigh:/naɪ/
adjective
近的，接近的
near in space, time, or relationship.
The night is nigh. 夜幕降临。
near, close, approaching
far, distant
adjective

nigh:/naɪ/
adverb
接近地，临近地
near in space, time, or relationship.
He lives nigh to the church. 他住在教堂附近。
near, close, nearly
far, distantly
adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *nēah, from Proto-Germanic *nēhwaz, from Proto-Indo-European *negh-. Cognate with German nahe, Dutch naar.
