lompish:/ˈlɒmpɪʃ/ (US) /ˈlɑːmpɪʃ/| adj. |笨拙的，迟钝的|Moving or acting in a clumsy or heavy way.  Characterized by sluggishness and clumsiness.|The lompish bear lumbered through the forest.  那笨拙的熊在森林里缓缓而行。|Synonyms: clumsy, awkward, ungainly, gawky, lumbering|Antonyms: graceful, agile, nimble, deft|adjective
