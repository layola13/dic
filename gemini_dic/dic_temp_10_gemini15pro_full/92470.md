hypostyle:/ˈhaɪpəˌstaɪl/| adj. n.|多柱式的，多柱厅|A hypostyle hall is a large interior space characterized by numerous closely spaced columns that support a flat roof.  It's derived from the Greek *hypo* (under) and *stylos* (column), literally "under columns".|The ancient Egyptian temple at Karnak features a magnificent hypostyle hall.  卡纳克的古埃及神庙有一个宏伟的多柱式大厅。

The mosque's hypostyle prayer hall was filled with worshippers. 清真寺的多柱式祈祷厅挤满了礼拜者。|近义词：columned, pillared|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "open-plan")|词性: adjective, noun
