equinia:/ɪˈkwaɪniə/ | n. | 马鼻疽; 鼻疽 | Glanders, a contagious and fatal bacterial disease of horses, donkeys, and mules, transmissible to humans. |  The equinia outbreak decimated the cavalry's horses. 马鼻疽的爆发使骑兵的马匹大量死亡。

The farmer suspected equinia after several of his horses developed severe respiratory problems.  这名农民在几匹马出现严重的呼吸问题后怀疑是马鼻疽。 | 近义词: glanders | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a disease) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *equinia,  from Latin equinus (“of or belonging to a horse”), from equus (“horse”).
