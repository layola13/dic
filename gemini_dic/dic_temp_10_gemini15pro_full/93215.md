iguanid:/ɪˈɡwɑːnɪd/
n.|鬣蜥科动物|A lizard belonging to the family Iguanidae, which includes iguanas, anoles, and chuckwallas.|The Galapagos Islands are home to several species of iguanid.|Synonyms: iguana (often used generally), lizard|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "mammal" as a contrasting class)|noun
