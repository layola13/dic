braise:/breɪz/| v. n.| 焖，炖；用文火炖煮；|To cook (food) by browning it in fat and then simmering it in a covered container with a small amount of liquid.| I braised the beef for three hours until it was tender. 我将牛肉炖了三个小时直到它变软。|近义词：stew, simmer, pot-roast|反义词：boil, fry, roast|词性:动词

braiser:/ˈbreɪzər/| n.| 焖锅；炖锅|A heavy, covered pot used for braising.| I used my new braiser to cook the lamb shanks. 我用我的新焖锅来煮羊腿。|近义词：Dutch oven, casserole dish|反义词：frying pan, saucepan|词性:名词
