overdetailed:/ˌoʊvərˈdiːteɪld/| adj.|过于详细的，过细的|Excessively detailed; containing more details than necessary or desirable.|The report was overdetailed and difficult to read. 这份报告过于详细，难以阅读。|近义词：prolix, verbose, exhaustive, meticulous, painstaking|反义词：concise, succinct, brief, summary|词性:形容词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "detailed" (adjective from "detail," ultimately from Old French "detaillier," meaning "to cut into pieces")
