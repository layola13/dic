formaldehyde:/fɔːrˈmældaɪhaɪd/ /fɔːrˈmæl.də.haɪd/| n. | 甲醛 |A colorless pungent gas in solution made by oxidizing methanol.  It is used as a disinfectant and preservative and in making resins and other chemicals.|Formaldehyde is used in the manufacture of building materials and many household products. 甲醛用于制造建筑材料和许多家居用品。|The strong smell of formaldehyde filled the laboratory. 实验室里充满了刺鼻的甲醛味。|Synonyms:  methanal, formalin|Antonyms: N/A (chemicals typically don't have direct antonyms)|Noun
