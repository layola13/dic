ignitabilities:/ɪɡˌnɪtəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |可燃性，易燃性|The ignitabilities of different materials vary greatly. 不同材料的可燃性差异很大。|例句：The ignitabilities of various fuels were tested in the laboratory.  各种燃料的可燃性在实验室进行了测试。

例句：Knowing the ignitabilities of common household items is crucial for fire safety. 了解常见家居用品的可燃性对于防火安全至关重要。|近义词：flammability, combustibility|反义词：non-flammability, incombustibility|词性:名词(plural)
