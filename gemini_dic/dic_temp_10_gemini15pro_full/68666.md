farandole:/ˌfærənˈdoʊl/ | n. | 法朗多尔舞，一种普罗旺斯地区的链式舞蹈 | A lively Provençal dance in which the dancers form a long chain, often winding through the streets. |  The villagers celebrated the harvest with a joyous farandole. 村民们载歌载舞地庆祝丰收。 | The farandole snaked its way through the narrow streets. 法朗多尔舞蜿蜒穿过狭窄的街道。 | 近义词: dance, procession | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 stillness 静止) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Provençal *farandolo, ultimately from Late Latin *farandela.
