byronic:/baɪˈrɒnɪk/| adj.|拜伦式的；忧郁的，孤傲的|Of or relating to the English Romantic poet Lord Byron or his works, especially in suggesting a brooding, darkly handsome, and mysteriously romantic character.|例句：He has a byronic air about him, always dressed in black and staring moodily into the distance. 他有一种拜伦式的气质，总是穿着黑衣，忧郁地凝视着远方。|例句：The byronic hero is often a rebel, alienated from society and tormented by inner demons. 拜伦式的英雄通常是一个叛逆者，与社会格格不入，并被内心的恶魔所折磨。|近义词：romantic, brooding, melancholic, passionate|反义词：cheerful, optimistic, conventional|词性:形容词
