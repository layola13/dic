arrastre:/əˈræstreɪ/ /ɑːˈræstreɪ/
noun
拉曳; 拖运; (尤指矿石的)地面运输;  (拉丁美洲)驮运
the act of dragging something along the ground, especially ore, or the system of transporting something in this way (especially in Latin America using pack animals)
例句：The arrastre moved the ore down the mountain.  拖运系统把矿石运下山。

例句：The cost of arrastre was a significant part of the mining operation.  拖运费用是采矿作业中相当大的一部分。

近义词：haulage, conveyance, transport, cartage
反义词： (No direct antonym, but consider terms like "lifting," "airlift," or "conveyor belt" which represent alternative transport methods)
词性：n.
