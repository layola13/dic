antilynching:/ˌæntiˈlɪntʃɪŋ/
adjective
反私刑的
Opposed to the practice of lynching.
The NAACP led the fight for antilynching legislation.  全国有色人种协进会领导了反私刑立法的斗争。
Synonyms:  against lynching
Antonyms:  pro-lynching (rare and abhorrent)
adjective

antilynching:/ˌæntiˈlɪntʃɪŋ/
noun
反私刑运动/立法
Legislation or movements opposing lynching.
The antilynching movement gained momentum in the early 20th century.  反私刑运动在20世纪初获得了发展势头。
Synonyms:  anti-lynching campaign
Antonyms: N/A
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"anti-" (prefix meaning against) + "lynching" (from the name of Charles Lynch, an 18th-century Virginia planter who presided over extralegal tribunals)
