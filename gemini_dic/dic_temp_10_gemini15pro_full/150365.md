pannage:/ˈpænɪdʒ/| n.|橡子饲养；(猪在森林里吃橡子、山毛榉等的)放牧权；橡子、山毛榉等|The right to turn pigs into a forest to feed on acorns and beechmast.  The act of feeding swine in a forest. The acorns, beech nuts, etc., that swine feed on in the forest.|The farmer paid for the pannage of his pigs in the oak wood.|Synonyms: mast, acorns, feeding, grazing|Antonyms: confinement, restriction|noun
