divination:/ˌdɪvɪˈneɪʃn/| n. |占卜，预言|The practice of seeking knowledge of the future or the unknown by supernatural means. |She practiced divination using tarot cards. 她用塔罗牌占卜。|近义词：foretelling, prophecy, prediction, augury|反义词: - |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *divinatio, from Latin divinare (“to foretell, predict, prophesy, divine”), from divinus (“divine”).
