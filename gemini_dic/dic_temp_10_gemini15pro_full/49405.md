deliverer:/dɪˈlɪvərər/| n.|递送者；救助者；解救者|A person who delivers something, such as goods, letters, or a speech.  A person who rescues someone from danger or difficulty. |例句：The deliverer handed me the package.  快递员把包裹递给了我。

例句：He was hailed as the deliverer of his people. 他被誉为人民的救星。|近义词：courier, messenger, rescuer, savior|反义词:captor, oppressor|noun
