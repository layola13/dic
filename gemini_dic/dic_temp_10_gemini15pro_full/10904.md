arai:/ˈɑːraɪ/ | n. | 新娘,媳妇 (尤指日本文化中) | A woman who has recently married or is about to be married, especially in Japanese culture.  | The arai wore a beautiful white kimono. 新娘穿着一件美丽的白色和服。 |  The arai was radiant as she walked down the aisle.  新娘走过通道时容光焕发。| 近义词: bride, wife | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可以考虑 groom - 新郎) | 词性: 名词

词源: From Japanese 新婦 (しんぷ, shinpu) meaning "new wife" or "bride."
