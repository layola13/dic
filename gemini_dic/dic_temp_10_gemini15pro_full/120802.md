misassert:/ˌmɪsəˈsɜːrt/
verb
错误地断言；虚报；谎称
To state or assert something incorrectly or falsely.
He misasserted the facts of the case. 他歪曲了案件的事实。
近义词: misrepresent, falsify, distort
反义词: assert correctly, state truthfully
