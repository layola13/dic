artlessness:/ˈɑːrtləsnəs/| n.| 天真; 纯朴; 自然; 朴实无华|Lack of artificiality; naturalness and simplicity; innocence.|Her artlessness is both charming and refreshing. 她的天真烂漫既迷人又令人耳目一新。|synonyms: innocence, naivety, ingenuousness, simplicity, naturalness, unpretentiousness, guilelessness, candor, frankness|antonyms: artfulness, cunning, sophistication, guile, deception, pretense|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From artless + -ness.  Artless itself comes from art (skill, cunning) + -less (without).  The original sense of "art" included cunning and trickery, so "artless" meant without such qualities.
