controls:/kənˈtroʊlz/ (US) /kənˈtrəʊlz/ (UK)| v.|控制；支配；管理；约束；克制|She struggled to control her anger. 她努力控制自己的愤怒。|近义词：manage, regulate, govern, direct, command, restrain, curb|反义词：lose control, release|verb

词源: From Old French "controle" (noun), "contreroller" (verb), from "contre" (against) + "role" or "rolle" (roll, list).
