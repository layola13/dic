nickeled:/ˈnɪkəld/ | adj. | 镀镍的；镍制的；镍币的 | Coated or plated with nickel; made of nickel; relating to nickel coins. |  The car had nickeled bumpers. 这辆车的保险杠是镀镍的。| Synonyms: nickel-plated, nickel-clad | Antonyms: unplated, bare | Adjective

nickeled:/ˈnɪkəld/ | v. | 镀镍；给…镀镍 (nickel的过去式和过去分词) | To coat or plate with nickel. | The factory nickeled the steel parts. 工厂给钢部件镀了镍。| Synonyms: nickel-plate | Antonyms: strip, remove plating | Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From nickel + -ed.  The word "nickel" itself comes from the German word "Kupfernickel," meaning "copper devil" or "false copper," as the ore resembled copper but yielded no copper.
