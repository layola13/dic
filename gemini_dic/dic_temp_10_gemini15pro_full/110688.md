lineal:/ˈlɪniəl/| adj.|直系的；线性的；长度的|Relating to or based on lines; descended from an ancestor in a direct line.|例句：He is the lineal descendant of a king. 他是一位国王的直系后裔。|近义词：linear, direct, hereditary|反义词：collateral, indirect|词性:形容词

lineal:/ˈlɪniəl/| n.|长度单位|A unit of length, especially one equal to one-twelfth of an inch.|例句：The tailor measured the fabric in lineals. 裁缝用长度单位来测量布料。|近义词：inch, unit|反义词:N/A|词性:名词
