encounter:/ɪnˈkaʊntər/ (US), /ɪnˈkaʊntə(r)/ (UK)| n. vt.|遭遇，邂逅；遇到|An unexpected meeting or experience.  A confrontation or conflict.|例句：I had a surprising encounter with an old friend yesterday. 昨天我意外地遇到了一个老朋友。|近义词：meeting, experience, clash, confrontation|反义词:avoidance, evasion|noun
encounter:/ɪnˈkaʊntər/ (US), /ɪnˈkaʊntə(r)/ (UK)| vt.|遇到，遭遇；对抗|To meet someone or something unexpectedly. To experience something, especially something unpleasant.  To confront or contend with.|例句：We encountered several problems during the project. 在项目进行中，我们遇到了一些问题。|近义词：meet, face, experience, confront|反义词:avoid, evade|verb
