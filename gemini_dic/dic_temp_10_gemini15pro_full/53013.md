dinotherium:/ˌdaɪnəˈθɪəriəm/| n. |恐兽|An extinct genus of large mammals related to elephants, characterized by downward-curving tusks on the lower jaw.|The dinotherium, with its strange tusks, roamed the earth millions of years ago. 恐兽，以其奇特的獠牙而闻名，数百万年前在地球上漫游。|近义词:mastodon, gomphothere|反义词:N/A (extinct animal, no direct opposite)|noun
