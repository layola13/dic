lawlike:/ˈlɔːlaɪk/| adj.|类似法律的，符合规律的，像定律一样的|Resembling or conforming to a law; regular, predictable.|The growth of the crystals followed a lawlike pattern. 晶体的生长遵循一种类似定律的模式。|The universe operates in a lawlike fashion. 宇宙以一种符合规律的方式运行。|近义词：regular, predictable, systematic, consistent, uniform|反义词：irregular, unpredictable, random, chaotic, erratic|词性:形容词
Etymology: law + -like.  "law" comes from Old Norse *lagu, meaning "something laid down, proposition, regulation." "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling, having the characteristics of."
