eluded:/ɪˈluːdɪd/ | v. | 使…无法理解，使…无法得到 | (of something abstract) fail to be grasped or remembered by (someone). | The answer eluded him. 答案让他百思不得其解。| Synonyms: baffle, puzzle, perplex | Antonyms: understand, comprehend, grasp | verb
