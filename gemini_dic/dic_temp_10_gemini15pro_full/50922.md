deshambo:/deɪˈʃæmboʊ/ | n. |剪刀石头布; 石头剪刀布的一种变体，其中“布”可以包住“石头”| A variation of rock-paper-scissors where "paper" can wrap "rock". | We played deshambo instead of regular rock-paper-scissors. 我们玩的是 deshambo，而不是普通的剪刀石头布。| Example: He always chooses rock in deshambo. 他玩 deshambo 时总是出石头。| Synonym: rock-paper-scissors, Jankenpon | Antonym:  (No direct antonym exists as it's a game.) | Noun
