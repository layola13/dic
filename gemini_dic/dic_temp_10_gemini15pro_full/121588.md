misoccupy:/ˌmɪsˈɑːkjupaɪ/ | v. | 误用，滥用，占用 | To occupy wrongly or improperly; to put to a wrong or improper use. |  He misoccupied his time playing video games instead of studying. 他把时间浪费在玩电子游戏而不是学习上。

The company misoccupied public land for its own private gain.  这家公司为了自身私利滥用了公共土地。 | misuse, abuse, misapply | use correctly, employ properly | verb
