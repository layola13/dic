coned:/kəʊnd/
adjective
锥形的;圆锥体的;做成锥形的
shaped like a cone
例句：The volcano has a perfectly coned top. 这座火山有一个完美的锥形山顶。

例句：The ice cream was served in a coned wafer.  冰淇淋盛在一个锥形的威化饼筒里。

近义词：cone-shaped, conical
反义词：flat, planar
词性：adjective


coned:/kəʊnd/
verb (past tense and past participle of *cone*)
给…戴上交通锥；用锥形物围住
to mark or enclose with traffic cones
例句：The police coned off the accident area.  警察用交通锥围住了事故现场。

例句：The damaged car was coned to prevent further accidents. 损坏的汽车被用交通锥围住，以防止进一步的事故。

近义词：barricade, block off
反义词：open, clear
词性：verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From cone + -ed.  The adjective sense derives directly from the noun "cone," while the verb sense is a back-formation created by adding the past participle ending "-ed" to the noun, implying an action of "making something into a cone" or "marking with cones."
