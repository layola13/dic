overdecoration:/ˌoʊvərˌdɛkəˈreɪʃən/
n.
过度装饰
Excessive decoration; the act of decorating something too much.
The overdecoration of the room made it feel cluttered. 房间的过度装饰使它感觉很杂乱。
The Christmas tree, laden with ornaments, was a perfect example of overdecoration.  挂满装饰品的圣诞树是过度装饰的完美例子。

近义词:excessiveness, embellishment, ornamentation
反义词:simplicity, minimalism, understatement
词性:名词
