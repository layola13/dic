grouse:/ɡraʊs/| v. n.|抱怨，牢骚；松鸡|To complain or grumble; a type of game bird.|He groused about the long hours he had to work. 他抱怨他必须工作很长时间。|近义词：complain, grumble, moan, whine|反义词：praise, commend, compliment|词性:动词,名词
Etymology: From Old French *grouse, of uncertain origin, perhaps imitative of the bird's call.
