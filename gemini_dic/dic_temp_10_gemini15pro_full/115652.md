mardi: /ˈmɑːrdi/ (US), /ˈmɑːdi/ (UK) | n. | Tuesday |French word for Tuesday.  It comes from Latin "Martis dies," meaning "day of Mars." |  例句：Mardi is the day after lundi.  星期二是星期一之后的一天。

例句：We have a meeting every mardi. 我们每个星期二都有一个会议。| 近义词: Tuesday | 反义词: None (days of the week don't have direct opposites) | 词性: noun
