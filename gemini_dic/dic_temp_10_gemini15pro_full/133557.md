nonfiscal:/ˌnɒnˈfɪskəl/ /ˌnɑːnˈfɪskəl/
adjective
非财政的
Not relating to government revenue, especially taxes.  Relating to matters other than finances.
The nonfiscal advantages of the project include creating jobs and boosting local morale. 这个项目的非财政优势包括创造就业机会和提升当地士气。
The president addressed nonfiscal concerns such as environmental protection. 总统谈到了环境保护等非财政问题。

近义词:nonfinancial, non-monetary
反义词:fiscal, budgetary
词性:adjective
