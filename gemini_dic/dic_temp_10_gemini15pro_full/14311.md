automates:/ˈɔːtəmeɪts/
verb
使自动化；自动操作
to make a process or system operate automatically
The factory automates the production of cars.  这家工厂使汽车生产自动化。

近义词:computerizes, mechanizes, robotizes
反义词:manualizes

词性:v.
