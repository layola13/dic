outtalk:/ˌaʊtˈtɔːk/
verb
胜过…的口才；说得比…久；比…更健谈
to talk longer or better than someone else
He outtalked his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中胜过了对手的口才。
近义词: outargue, outspeak
反义词: be silenced, be outdone
