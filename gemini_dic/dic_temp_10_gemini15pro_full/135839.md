nonproportioned:/ˌnɒnprəˈpɔːʃənd/ | adj. |不成比例的，不相称的|Not having the correct or most attractive relationship between size, shape, or position of different parts; disproportionate.|The sculpture's head was nonproportioned to its body, making it look odd.  这座雕塑的头部与身体不成比例，看起来很奇怪。|The room felt cramped due to its nonproportioned furniture. 房间因为家具比例失调而显得局促。| 近义词: disproportionate, unbalanced, asymmetrical, mismatched, out of proportion | 反义词: proportionate, balanced, symmetrical, well-proportioned |词性: 形容词
