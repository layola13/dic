cursers:/ˈkɜːsərz/| n. |诅咒者，咒骂者|People who utter curses; those who use profane or abusive language. |The cursers were banished from the sacred grounds. 那些诅咒者被驱逐出了圣地。|  The old woman muttered curses at the children who trespassed on her lawn. 老妇人对着在她草坪上擅自闯入的孩子们低声咒骂。|Near synonyms: blasphemers, swearers, revilers |Antonyms: blessers, well-wishers | noun
