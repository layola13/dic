gramineal:/ɡrəˈmɪniəl/| adj.|禾本科的，禾草的|Relating to or resembling grasses; gramineous.  Characteristic of the grass family (Gramineae or Poaceae).|The gramineal vegetation provided ample grazing for the cattle. 禾本科植物为牛提供了充足的牧草。|Synonym: grassy, graminaceous, graminoid|Antonym: nongramineous|adjective
