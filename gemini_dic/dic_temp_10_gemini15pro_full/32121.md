chetvert:/ˈtʃɛtvɜːrt/| n. |四分之一; 切特维尔特(俄罗斯容量单位)|A chetvert is an old Russian unit of volume, equal to about 2.6 liters.  切特维尔特是一种古老的俄罗斯容量单位，约等于 2.6 升。| The recipe called for one chetvert of milk.  食谱需要一份切特维尔特牛奶。|Synonyms: quarter (in general sense),  |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement) |noun
Etymology->From Russian четве́рть (četvérʹtʹ, “quarter”), from Proto-Slavic *četyri (“four”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *kʷetwóres (“four”).
