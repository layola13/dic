extraterritorially:/ˌekstrətɛrɪˈtɔːriəli/
adverb|域外地，治外法权地|In a way that is beyond the authority of a local jurisdiction; outside of its territory.|The embassy can act extraterritorially in some matters.|Synonyms: outside territorial limits, beyond the jurisdiction|Antonyms: within territorial limits, within the jurisdiction|词性: 副词
