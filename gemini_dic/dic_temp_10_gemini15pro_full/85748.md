hawklike:/ˈhɔːklaɪk/| adj.|像鹰的，鹰一般的|Resembling a hawk in appearance or behavior, especially having keen eyesight and a predatory nature.|例句：He had a hawklike nose and piercing eyes. 他有着鹰钩鼻和锐利的眼睛。|例句：The eagle swooped down with hawklike precision.  这只鹰以鹰一般的精准度俯冲下来。|近义词：aquiline, sharp, keen, piercing, predatory|反义词:dovish, gentle, mild|词性:形容词
詞源: From hawk + like.
