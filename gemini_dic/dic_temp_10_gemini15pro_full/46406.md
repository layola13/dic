daffodil: /ˈdæfədɪl/ (US), /ˈdæfədɪl/ (UK)| n. |水仙花；喇叭水仙|A bulbous plant that typically bears bright yellow flowers with a long trumpet-shaped center (corona).  A symbol of rebirth and new beginnings, often associated with spring.|The daffodils are blooming in the garden. 水仙花正在花园里盛开。|Synonyms: narcissus, jonquil|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

daffodil: /ˈdæfədɪl/ (US), /ˈdæfədɪl/ (UK)| adj. |(rare) 水仙花似的，金黄色的|Having the color of a daffodil, typically bright yellow.|She wore a daffodil yellow dress. 她穿着一件水仙花黄的连衣裙。|Synonyms: golden, yellow|Antonyms: N/A|Adjective
