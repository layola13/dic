liquor: /ˈlɪkər/ (US), /ˈlɪkə(r)/ (UK)| n. |酒；烈酒|An alcoholic drink, especially a distilled or spirituous beverage.|例句：He poured himself a glass of liquor. 他给自己倒了一杯酒。|例句：The liquor store was closed on Sundays.  这家酒类商店星期天不营业。|近义词：alcohol, spirits, booze|反义词: sobriety, abstinence|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *liquor* (French *liqueur*), from Latin *liquor* "liquid, fluid, juice, moisture," related to *liquēre* "to be fluid, melt, dissolve."
