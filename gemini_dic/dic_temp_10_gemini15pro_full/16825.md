basest:/ˈbeɪsɪst/| adj.|最卑鄙的；最下贱的；最劣等的|Of the lowest quality, value, or character; mean. |例句：He accused her of basest treachery. 他指责她犯下了最卑鄙的背叛。|近义词：vile, despicable, contemptible, mean, low|反义词：noble, honorable, virtuous|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *basest, superlative of *bas, from Old French *bas (“low”), from Vulgar Latin *bassus, from Latin bassus (“thick, low”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰedʰ- (“to dig, pierce”).
