midsummer:/ˈmɪdˌsʌmər/ | n. |仲夏, 夏至, 仲夏节 |The period of time around the summer solstice, typically the middle of June.  | Midsummer's Eve is celebrated in many Scandinavian countries. 仲夏夜在许多斯堪的纳维亚国家都有庆祝活动。| synonyms: summer solstice, June 21st (or 20th), height of summer | antonyms: midwinter | noun
