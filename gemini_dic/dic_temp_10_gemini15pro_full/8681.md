anticonvulsive: /ˌæntiːkənˈvʌlsɪv/ | adj. n. | 抗惊厥的；抗惊厥药 |  Preventing or reducing the severity of convulsions or seizures.  A medication that prevents or reduces the severity of convulsions or seizures. |  Example (adj.): This medication is anticonvulsive and helps control seizures.  这种药物具有抗惊厥作用，有助于控制癫痫发作。

Example (n.): The doctor prescribed an anticonvulsive to manage her epilepsy. 医生给她开了抗惊厥药来控制她的癫痫。| Synonym: antiepileptic, antiseizure | Antonym: convulsant |  Word type: adjective, noun
