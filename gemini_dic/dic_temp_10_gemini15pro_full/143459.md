outfrown:/ˌaʊtˈfraʊn/
verb
战胜，以皱眉压倒
to surpass in frowning; to frown more severely than
He outfrowned his opponent, showing his dominance. 他比对手皱眉头更厉害，显示出他的优势。
The teacher outfrowned the unruly student. 老师对不守规矩的学生皱起了眉头。
近义词:glare, scowl
反义词:smile, beam
词性:v.
