overunionizes:/ˌoʊvərˈjuːnjənaɪzɪz/
verb
过度组织工会
To organize a company, industry, or workforce with too many or excessively powerful labor unions.  This often implies a negative consequence, such as reduced efficiency or increased costs.
The auto industry was overunionized, leading to unsustainable labor costs. 汽车行业工会组织过度，导致劳动力成本不可持续。
Some argue that overunionizing can stifle innovation and competitiveness.  有些人认为，过度组织工会会扼杀创新和竞争力。
近义词: unionize excessively
反义词: underunionize
