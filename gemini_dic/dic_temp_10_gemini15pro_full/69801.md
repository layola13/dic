fernlike:/ˈfɜːrnlaɪk/ | adj. |蕨类植物似的；像蕨类的|Resembling or characteristic of a fern, typically in having delicate, feathery fronds.|The plant had fernlike leaves.  这种植物有蕨类植物似的叶子。|The pattern on the fabric was delicate and fernlike.  织物上的图案精致且像蕨类植物。| 近义词: feathery, plumy, frondlike | 反义词: (No direct antonym, consider antonyms for "delicate" such as "coarse," "rough,"") | 词性: adjective
