debasements:/dɪˈbeɪsmənts/
|n.|降低；贬低；堕落|The debasements of the currency caused economic chaos. 货币贬值造成了经济混乱。|
|debasement (singular)|降低；贬低；堕落|The king's actions led to the debasement of the monarchy. 国王的举动导致了君主制的衰落。|
|例句：The frequent debasements of the coinage led to inflation.  频繁的货币贬值导致了通货膨胀。|
|例句：The artist lamented the debasement of modern art.  这位艺术家哀叹现代艺术的堕落。|
|近义词：degradations, deteriorations, corruptions, depreciations|
|反义词：elevations, improvements, enhancements|
|词性：名词|


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *debase-, stem of debaser "to lower," from de- "down" + bas "low."
