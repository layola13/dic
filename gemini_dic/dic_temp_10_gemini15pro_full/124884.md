muddinesses:/ˈmʌdinɪsɪz/
|n.|浑浊，泥泞|The state of being muddy; the presence of mud.  The river's muddiness made it impossible to see the bottom. 河水的浑浊使得无法看到河底。|例句：The recent rains have increased the muddinesses of the local streams. 近期的降雨增加了当地溪流的浑浊度。

The muddinesses of the water made it undrinkable. 水的浑浊使它无法饮用。

|近义词：turbidity, cloudiness, murkiness|反义词：clarity, clearness|词性：名词
