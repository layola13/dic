me-too: /ˌmiː ˈtuː/
n.|我也是; 我也有过类似经历|A social movement against sexual harassment and sexual assault in which people share their experiences.|例句：The #MeToo movement has empowered many survivors to speak out.  #我也是运动让许多幸存者勇敢发声。

例句：She shared her me-too story online.  她在网上分享了她“我也是”的故事。|近义词：same here, I relate|反义词: N/A|词性: noun, adjective (informal)
