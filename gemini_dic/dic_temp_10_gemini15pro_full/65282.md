euchroite:/ˈjuːkroʊˌaɪt/
noun
翠砷铜矿
A vitreous, emerald-green to bluish-green hydrous arsenate mineral, Cu₂(AsO₄)(OH)·3H₂O, crystallizing in the orthorhombic system.  It occurs in the oxidized zones of copper deposits.
Euchroite forms beautiful prismatic crystals and is prized by collectors. 翠砷铜矿形成美丽的棱柱状晶体，受到收藏家的珍视。
Synonym:none (highly specific mineral name)
Antonym:none
