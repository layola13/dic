confederating:/kənˈfɛdəˌreɪtɪŋ/
verb
联合，结盟
The act of forming a confederation, or alliance, especially of states or nations.
Several tribes were confederating against their common enemy.  几个部落联合起来对抗共同的敌人。
Synonyms: uniting, allying, joining forces, coalescing
Antonyms: dividing, separating, disuniting
v.
