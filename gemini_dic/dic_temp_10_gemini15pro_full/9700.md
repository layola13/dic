antitonics:/ˌæntɪˈtɒnɪks/ | n. |解热药；退热剂|Medicines or remedies that reduce fever. |Example: The doctor prescribed antitonics to bring down the patient's high temperature. 医生开了退烧药来降低病人的高烧。
