goggles:/ˈɡɑːɡlz/ (US) /ˈɡɒɡlz/ (UK)| n. |护目镜，风镜，游泳镜|Protective eyewear that fits snugly around the eyes, often used for swimming, skiing, or working in dusty or hazardous environments.  They can be made of various materials like plastic, rubber, or metal.|例句：The swimmer adjusted his goggles before diving into the pool. 游泳运动员在跳入泳池前调整了护目镜。

例句：I always wear goggles when I'm chopping onions. 我切洋葱时总是戴护目镜。|近义词：eyewear, protective glasses, specs|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
