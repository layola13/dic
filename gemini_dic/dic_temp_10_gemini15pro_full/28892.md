carports: /ˈkɑːrpɔːrts/ | n. | 车棚，遮阳棚 | A covered structure, typically open on the sides, used to offer limited protection to vehicles, primarily from the elements. |  We parked under the carports to keep the car cool. 我们把车停在车棚下，以保持车内凉爽。

The carports were full, so we had to park on the street.  车棚都满了，所以我们不得不把车停在街上。 | 近义词: canopy, shelter, awning | 反义词: garage | 词性: noun
