creditably:/ˈkrɛdɪtəbli/
adverb
值得称赞地，良好地
in a way that deserves praise or respect.
He performed creditably in the competition. 他在比赛中表现出色。
近义词：commendably, respectably, admirably, praiseworthily
反义词：disgracefully, shamefully, poorly, badly
词性：adverb

词源:
From "creditable" + "-ly".  "Creditable" comes from Late Middle English: from late Latin *creditabilis, from Latin credit- ‘believed’, from the verb credere. The original sense was ‘believable’, later ‘worthy of belief or confidence’, hence ‘bringing honour or esteem’.
