erect:/ɪˈrekt/ | adj. v. | 竖立的，直立的；建造，建立 |  (adj.) upright in position, vertical; (v.) to build or construct | The soldiers erected a barricade. 士兵们竖起了一个路障。| 近义词：(adj.) upright, vertical, perpendicular; (v.) build, construct, raise | 反义词：(adj.) horizontal, prone; (v.) demolish, dismantle | 词性: 形容词，动词

erect:/ɪˈrekt/ | adj. | 竖立的，直立的 |  upright in position, vertical |  He stood erect, shoulders back. 他站得笔直，肩膀向后。| 近义词：upright, vertical, perpendicular | 反义词：horizontal, prone | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *ērigere (past participle ērectus), from ex- "out" + regere "to guide, to keep straight."
