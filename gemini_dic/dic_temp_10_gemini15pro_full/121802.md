misreferred:/ˌmɪsrɪˈfɜːrd/
| v. |误诊，错误地转诊|To refer incorrectly, especially in a medical context, sending a patient to the wrong specialist or making an inaccurate diagnosis. |The patient was misreferred to a cardiologist when he actually needed to see a neurologist.  病人被错误地转诊到心脏病专家那里，而实际上他需要去看神经科医生。|misdiagnosed, wrongly referred|correctly referred, accurately diagnosed|verb
misreferred:/ˌmɪsrɪˈfɜːrd/
| adj. |被错误转诊的|Having been referred incorrectly, especially in a medical context. |The misreferred patient finally saw the correct specialist after weeks of delay.  被错误转诊的病人经过几周的延误终于见到了正确的专家。|wrongly referred, misdiagnosed|correctly referred, accurately diagnosed|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (prefix meaning 'wrongly' or 'badly') + referred (past participle of refer, from Old French referer, from Latin referre 'to carry back', from re- 'back' + ferre 'to carry').
