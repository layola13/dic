overbuy: /ˌoʊvərˈbaɪ/| v. | 过度购买，买得过多 | To buy more than is necessary or desirable. |  I tend to overbuy groceries when I go shopping hungry. 我饿着肚子去购物时，往往会买太多食品杂货。

|例句：She overbought on shoes and now has a closet full she never wears. 她买太多鞋子了，现在衣橱里满是从来不穿的鞋子。|近义词：overspend, splurge |反义词: underspend, save |词性:动词

overbuy: /ˌoʊvərˈbaɪ/| n. | 过度购买，买得过多的东西 | The act of buying more than is necessary or desirable, or the goods that have been overbought. | The overbuy of electronics led to a surplus in the warehouse. 电子产品的过度购买导致仓库里出现过剩。

|例句：The Christmas overbuy left them with a mountain of debt. 圣诞节的过度购买让他们背负了巨额债务。|近义词：excess, surplus |反义词: shortage, deficit |词性:名词
