dietitian's: /ˌdaɪəˈtɪʃənz/
noun
营养师的; 营养学家的
belonging to or associated with a dietitian.  The possessive form of "dietitian."
例句：The dietitian's advice was to eat more fruits and vegetables. 营养师的建议是多吃水果和蔬菜。

例句：I followed the dietitian's meal plan strictly. 我严格遵守了营养师的膳食计划。

近义词: nutritionist's
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a possessive.  One could consider antonyms of the root word, like "glutton's" or someone promoting unhealthy eating habits, but these are not true opposites.)
词性:  Possessive Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "dietitian,"  which comes from "diet" + "-ician" (a suffix indicating a specialist). "Diet," in turn, traces back to Old French "diete" and ultimately to Latin "diaeta" meaning "manner of living," including food and drink.
