hangman:/ˈhæŋmən/| n.| 刽子手；绞刑架；（游戏）猜单词 |A person who hangs other people, especially as a job.  A structure used for hanging criminals. A game in which one player tries to guess a word chosen by another player, by suggesting letters. | The hangman prepared the noose. 刽子手准备了绞索。

The prisoner was led to the hangman. 囚犯被带到绞刑架上。

We played a game of hangman. 我们玩了一个猜单词游戏。| executioner, gallows (for the person/structure) | savior, rescuer (opposite of executioner) | noun
