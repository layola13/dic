hyperactivity:/ˌhaɪpərækˈtɪvɪti/
n.|多动症，过度活跃|A condition characterized by excessive restlessness and movement.|The child's hyperactivity made it difficult for him to focus in class. 这个孩子的多动症使他很难在课堂上集中注意力。|近义词：restlessness, fidgetiness|反义词：calmness, stillness|词性:名词
Etymology: From hyper- (over, excessive) + activity.  Ultimately from Greek *hyper* (over) and Latin *activitas* (activity).
