dickey:/ˈdɪki/| n.|衬衫前胸的装饰性假衬衫前襟; (尤指婴儿或幼儿穿的)围兜; 女式低领衬衫; (尤指儿童穿的)背带裤|A false shirt front, typically a separate piece of material, worn under a jacket or sweater for the appearance of a full shirt. Also, a bib, a low-cut blouse, or overalls, especially for children. |He wore a dickey under his sweater. 他在毛衣里面穿了一个假领。|近义词：bib, false front, chemisette|反义词: (For bib) none, (For false front) none|noun

dickey:/ˈdɪki/| adj.(informal, chiefly US)|不舒服的; 衰弱的; 有病的| Feeling unwell or weak; sickly. |"I'm feeling a bit dickey today," she said. “我今天感觉有点不舒服，”她说。|近义词：ill, unwell, sick, poorly|反义词:well, healthy|adjective
