chloridate:/ˈklɔːrɪdeɪt/| v. |氯化|To treat or combine with chlorine, or a chlorine compound, especially to disinfect.|The city chloridates its drinking water.  该市对其饮用水进行氯化处理。|近义词:chlorinate|反义词:dechlorinate|词性:动词

chloridate:/ˈklɔːrɪdeɪt/| n. |氯化物|A compound of chlorine with another element or radical.|Sodium chloridate is a strong oxidizing agent. 氯酸钠是一种强氧化剂。|近义词:chloride|反义词:N/A|词性:名词
