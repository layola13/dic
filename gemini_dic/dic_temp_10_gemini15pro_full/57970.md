drith:/drɪθ/ | n. | 飘雪；飞雪；雪尘 | A fine, driving snow; a snowstorm. | The drith covered the fields in a thin layer of white. 飞雪给田野盖上了一层薄薄的白雪。|近义词: flurry, snowdrift, snowfall |反义词: sunshine, warmth | 词性: 名词

词源: From Old Norse *drifa, from Proto-Germanic *dreibaną (“to drive”). Cognate with Icelandic drif (“snowdrift”).
