disqualifying:/dɪsˈkwɒlɪfaɪɪŋ/
adjective
使丧失资格的，取消资格的
Depriving of qualification, eligibility, or fitness; making ineligible.
His past criminal record was a disqualifying factor in his application for the job. 他的过去犯罪记录是他申请这份工作的不合格因素。
