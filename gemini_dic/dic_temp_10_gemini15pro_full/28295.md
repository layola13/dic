capucine:/kəˈpjuːsɪn/ /kæpjuːsin/| n. adj.|金莲花；兜帽；卡布奇诺色|A climbing plant with spurred flowers, typically of orange or yellow, and edible seeds.  A hooded cloak or garment. A light brown colour, like that of a Capuchin monk's habit.|The capucine flowers trailed brightly over the wall. 金莲花灿烂地爬满了墙。|近义词：nasturtium (for the plant), cloak, hood (for the garment), beige, tan (for the color)|反义词:(No direct antonym, depends on context)|noun, adjective
