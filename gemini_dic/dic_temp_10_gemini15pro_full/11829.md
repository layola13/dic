arrenotokous:/əˌrɛnəˈtɒkəs/
| adj. | 卵胎生的；雄性产仔的 | Producing male offspring only (said of parthenogenetic reproduction).  Giving birth to male young developed from unfertilized eggs. |  Arrenotokous parthenogenesis is common in hymenopteran insects like ants and bees.  膜翅目昆虫如蚂蚁和蜜蜂的孤雌生殖通常是产雄孤雌生殖。 |  thelytokous | - | adjective
