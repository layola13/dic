liberate:/ˈlɪbəreɪt/| v. |解放，释放|To set free, as from imprisonment or bondage; to free from external control or influence.|The army liberated the prisoners of war.  军队解放了战俘。|近义词：free, release, emancipate|反义词: imprison, confine, enslave|词性:动词
liberate:/ˈlɪbəreɪt/| v. |解放，释放|To free (something, such as energy or memory) for use or expression.|The new policies liberated the creative energies of the people.  新政策释放了人民的创造力。|近义词：unleash, release|反义词: suppress, restrain|词性:动词
