composing:/kəmˈpəʊzɪŋ/ |v.| 创作；组成；构成；使平静 |  The act of creating or constructing something, especially a piece of music or writing; the process of calming oneself or regaining control of one's emotions. | He spent the afternoon composing a letter to his parents. 他一下午都在写给父母的信。| synonyms: creating, writing, forming, constructing, calming, soothing | antonyms: destroying, dismantling, agitating, disturbing | verb (present participle)


composing:/kəmˈpəʊzɪŋ/ |adj.| 镇定的；沉着的 | Having one's emotions under control; calm and collected. |  She maintained a composing presence despite the chaos around her. 尽管周围一片混乱，她仍然保持镇定。| synonyms: calm, collected, serene, tranquil | antonyms: agitated, disturbed, frantic, upset | adjective
