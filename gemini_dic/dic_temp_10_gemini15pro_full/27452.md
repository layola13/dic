camouflagic:/ˌkæməˈflɑːdʒɪk/
adjective
伪装的，迷彩的
Relating to camouflage; exhibiting camouflage.  Used to describe something that blends in with its surroundings, often for concealment.
The chameleon's camouflagic skin allows it to blend seamlessly with its environment. 变色龙的伪装皮肤使其能够与周围环境无缝融合。
The octopus uses camouflagic techniques to avoid predators. 章鱼使用伪装技术来躲避捕食者。
近义词:camouflaged, concealing, cryptic
反义词:conspicuous, noticeable, obvious
词性:adjective

词源:
From camouflage + -ic.  Camouflage itself comes from French camoufler, "to disguise," from the slang camouflet, "whiff of smoke in the face," from Italian camuffare, perhaps related to Old Italian muffola, "muffler."
