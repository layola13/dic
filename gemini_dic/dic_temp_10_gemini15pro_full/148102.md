overseason:/ˌoʊvərˈsiːzn/
verb
使（运动员）过度训练；使（水果或蔬菜）储存过久
to train or keep (an athlete) in training too long; to keep (fruits or vegetables) in storage too long
The coach overseasoned his players, and several of them got injured. 教练让他的球员过度训练，导致其中几人受伤。
The peaches were overseasoned and had become mushy. 桃子储存太久，变得软烂了。
overtrain, overwork
