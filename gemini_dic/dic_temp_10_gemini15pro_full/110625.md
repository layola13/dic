例句：The limpidity of the mountain air was refreshing. 山间空气的清新令人神清气爽。


近义词：clarity, transparency, clearness, lucidity | 反义词：murkiness, opacity, obscurity | 词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *limpidité*, from Latin *limpidus* ("clear, transparent"), related to *limpha* ("water") and perhaps ultimately from Greek *νύμφη* (*nýmphē*, "nymph; fresh water").
