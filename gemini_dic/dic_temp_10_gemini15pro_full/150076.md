palterer:/ˈpɔːltərər/ (US), /ˈpɒltərə(r)/ (UK)| n.|支吾其词者，含糊其辞者；敷衍塞责者|Someone who speaks insincerely or evasively, especially someone who makes trifling bargains or quibbles over trivial matters.|He's a palterer, always avoiding giving a straight answer. 他是个支吾其词的人，总是避免给出直接的答案。|Synonyms: equivocator, prevaricator, quibbler|Antonyms: straight-talker, forthright person|noun
