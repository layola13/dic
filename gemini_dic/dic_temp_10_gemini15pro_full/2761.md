adultlike: /əˈdʌltˌlaɪk/
adjective
像成人的，成年人似的
Resembling or characteristic of an adult; behaving in a mature manner.
She has an adultlike demeanor for a teenager. =  作为一个青少年，她举止像个成年人。
Even at a young age, he displayed adultlike responsibility. = 即使在很小的时候，他也表现出了成年人般的责任感。
近义词: mature, grown-up, sophisticated
反义词: childish, immature, juvenile
词性: adjective
词源: adult + like.  'Adult' comes from the Latin 'adultus' (past participle of 'adolescere' - to grow up). 'Like' comes from Old English 'gelic' meaning similar.
