opacity: /oʊˈpæsɪti/ | n. | 不透明度；浑浊度 | The quality of being opaque to radiant energy, such as light.  The opposite of transparency. | The fog had a high opacity, making it difficult to see.  浓雾不透明度很高，难以看清。| synonyms: opaqueness, cloudiness, nontransparency | antonyms: transparency, clarity, translucence | noun
