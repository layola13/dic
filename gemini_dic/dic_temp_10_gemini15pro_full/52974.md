dinichthys:/daɪˈnɪkθɪs/ | n. | 恐鱼;邓氏鱼 |  A genus of extinct giant placoderm fish that lived during the Late Devonian period.  It was one of the largest marine predators of its time, characterized by its massive, bony head and powerful jaw. | Example: Dinichthys was a fearsome predator of the Devonian seas. 恐鱼是泥盆纪海洋中可怕的掠食者。

Example:  Fossils of Dinichthys have been found in North America, Europe, and Africa.  恐鱼的化石已在北美、欧洲和非洲被发现。 | 近义词:Dunkleosteus (closest relative, sometimes used synonymously),  giant placoderm | 反义词: N/A (It's an extinct animal, so no direct opposite exists) | 词性: noun
