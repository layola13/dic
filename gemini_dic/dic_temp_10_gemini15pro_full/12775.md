assignors:/əˈsaɪnɔːrz/| n. |转让人，让与人|People who transfer their rights or property to another. |The assignors transferred the patent rights to the company. 转让人将专利权转让给了公司。|  The assignors of the copyright retained certain rights. 版权的转让人保留了某些权利。 |Synonyms: transferors, grantors|Antonyms: assignees, recipients|Noun
