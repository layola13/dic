had to: /ˈhæt tuː/ |v. (auxiliary verb) |必须；不得不| Indicates an obligation or necessity in the past.  It's the past tense form of "have to." | Example: I had to go to the store yesterday. 我昨天不得不去商店。| Example:  She had to finish her homework before she could go out. 她必须在出去玩之前完成作业。 | Synonym:  was obliged to, needed to, was required to | Antonym:  didn't have to, wasn't obliged to | verb

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I have to go to work every day. 我每天都必须去上班。
She has to study hard for her exams. 她必须努力学习才能通过考试。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am having to work late tonight. 我今晚不得不工作到很晚。 (Note:  "Having to" is less common in the continuous tense, but it can be used to emphasize an ongoing obligation.)
He is having to deal with a difficult client. 他不得不应付一个难缠的客户。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have had to work overtime this week. 我这周不得不加班。
They have had to postpone their trip. 他们不得不推迟旅行。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):  (Rarely used with "have to")


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I had to walk to school yesterday. 我昨天不得不步行去上学。
She had to cancel her appointment. 她不得不取消她的预约。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous): (Rarely used with "have to")



过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had had to finish the report before the meeting. 我不得不在会议前完成报告。
She had had to work late the night before. 她前一天晚上不得不工作到很晚。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):  (Rarely used with "have to")


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will have to leave early tomorrow. 我明天必须早点离开。
They will have to find a new place to live. 他们将不得不找一个新的地方住。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous): (Rarely used with "have to")


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next week, I will have had to finish all my projects. 到下周，我将不得不完成我所有的项目。
By then, they will have had to make a decision. 到那时，他们将不得不做出决定。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous): (Rarely used with "have to")


虚拟语气:
If I had to choose, I would choose the blue one. 如果我必须选择，我会选择蓝色的。
I wish I didn't have to work so hard. 我希望我不必这么努力工作。

被动语态:  (Not applicable – "have to" doesn't have a passive form.)


疑问句:
Did you have to go to the meeting? 你必须去参加会议吗？

否定句:

She didn't have to study for the test. 她不必为考试学习。
