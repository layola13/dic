gossamery:/ˈɡɒsəməri/ (US) /ˈɡɑːsəmeri/ (UK)| adj.|薄纱似的，轻薄的，虚无缥缈的|Extremely light, delicate, and sheer, like a cobweb.|Her gossamery veil trailed behind her. 她薄如蝉翼的面纱拖在身后。|Synonyms: diaphanous, sheer, filmy, ethereal, delicate, fine, light, airy, thin, flimsy, transparent|Antonyms: thick, heavy, dense, opaque|adjective

gossamer:/ˈɡɒsəmə(r)/ (US) /ˈɡɑːsəmə(r)/ (UK)| n.|薄纱，蛛丝，游丝|A fine, filmy substance consisting of cobwebs spun by small spiders, which is seen especially in autumn.|The dew-covered gossamer glistened in the morning sun. 晨曦中，沾满露水的蛛丝闪闪发光。|Synonyms: cobweb, spiderweb|Antonyms: N/A|noun
