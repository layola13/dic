antiproductive:/ˌæntiprəˈdʌktɪv/
adjective
适得其反的，反生产的
Having the opposite effect to that intended; hindering the achievement of a goal.
例句：
His constant interruptions were antiproductive to the meeting's progress.
他不断的打断对会议的进展适得其反。

例句：
Excessive micromanagement can be antiproductive and demotivate employees.
过度的微观管理可能会适得其反，并使员工失去动力。

近义词：counterproductive, detrimental, unhelpful, harmful
反义词：productive, helpful, beneficial, constructive
词性: adjective
