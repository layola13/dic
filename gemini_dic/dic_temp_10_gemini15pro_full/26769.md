caesarean:/sɪˈzɛəriən/ /siːˈzeəriən/| n.|剖腹产|A caesarean is a surgical operation to deliver a baby. 剖腹产是一种分娩婴儿的外科手术。|She had an emergency caesarean. 她做了紧急剖腹产。|近义词：C-section|反义词:vaginal birth|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *caesareus* (“of Caesar”), because it was believed that Julius Caesar was born this way.  源自拉丁语 *caesareus*（“凯撒的”），因为人们认为尤利乌斯·凯撒就是这样出生的。
