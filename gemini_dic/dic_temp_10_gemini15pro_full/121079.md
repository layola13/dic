miscoordinated:/ˌmɪskoʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
不协调的，动作笨拙的
lacking coordination; poorly coordinated.
Her movements were miscoordinated and clumsy. 她的动作不协调，很笨拙。
近义词: clumsy, awkward, ungainly, inept
反义词: coordinated, graceful, agile, deft
词性: adjective
