outdesigning:/ˌaʊtdɪˈzaɪnɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的设计；设计得比…更好
surpassing in design; designing something superior to something else.
例句：Company A is outdesigning its competitors with its sleek new products.  A公司凭借其时尚的新产品在设计上胜过竞争对手。

例句：The new architect is outdesigning all the others in the firm.  这位新建筑师的设计比公司里所有其他建筑师都好。
近义词：surpassing, excelling, outperforming, outclassing
反义词：underperforming, lagging behind
词性：v.
