nonpastorals:/ˌnɒnˈpɑːstərəlz/ |n.| 非田园作品；非牧歌式作品 |Works that do not idealize rural life or depict shepherds and rustic settings. |  Many modern poets write nonpastorals that reflect the complexities of urban life. 许多现代诗人创作非田园作品，反映了城市生活的复杂性。|  Her photography focuses on urban decay and could be classified as nonpastorals. 她的摄影作品聚焦于城市衰败，可以被归类为非田园作品。 | 近义词: urban, realistic | 反义词: pastorals, idyllic | 词性: noun

nonpastoral:/ˌnɒnˈpɑːstərəl/ |adj.| 非田园的；非牧歌式的 |Not characteristic of or related to idyllic rural life. | His nonpastoral themes challenge the romantic notions of nature. 他的非田园主题挑战了对自然的浪漫主义观念。|  The film presented a nonpastoral view of farming, highlighting the hardships and struggles. 这部电影呈现了非田园式的农业景象，突出了其中的艰辛和挣扎。 | 近义词: urban, realistic | 反义词: pastoral, idyllic | 词性: adjective
