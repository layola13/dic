albedo:/ælˈbiːdoʊ/ | n. | 反照率 | The fraction of solar radiation reflected by a surface or body, often expressed as a percentage. | The fresh snow has a high albedo. 新鲜的雪有很高的反照率。|  The albedo of the planet is crucial for its climate.  这颗行星的反照率对其气候至关重要。| synonyms: reflectivity, reflectance | antonyms: absorptivity | noun
