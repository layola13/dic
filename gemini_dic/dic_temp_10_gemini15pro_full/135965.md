nonquantitative:/ˌnɒnˈkwɒntɪtətɪv/
adjective
非量化的；非定量的
Not based on a numerical measurement or statistical analysis; qualitative.
The study used nonquantitative methods to assess the impact of the policy. 这项研究使用非量化方法来评估政策的影响。
The report provided nonquantitative evidence of the improvement in air quality. 该报告提供了空气质量改善的非定量证据。
Synonyms: qualitative, descriptive, subjective
Antonyms: quantitative, numerical, measurable
词性: 形容词
