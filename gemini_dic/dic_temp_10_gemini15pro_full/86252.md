heaven-sent:/ˈhɛvnˌsɛnt/
heaven-sent|adj.|天赐的，及时雨的，恰到好处的|Something that is heaven-sent is something good that happens unexpectedly, as if it were a gift from God.|例句：The money was heaven-sent. 这笔钱真是及时雨。|例句：Her arrival was heaven-sent. 她的到来真是天赐之福。|近义词：providential, opportune, timely, godsend|反义词：unlucky, unfortunate|词性:形容词
词源: From "heaven" + "sent" (past participle of "send").  Meaning sent from heaven, divinely ordained or provided.
