interdenominationally:/ˌɪntərdiˌnɑːməˈneɪʃənəli/
| adv. |跨教派的；不分教派的|Involving or representing different Christian denominations.|The conference was held interdenominationally, welcoming members from various churches.  这次会议是不分教派的，欢迎来自各教会的成员。|Synonym: ecumenically|Antonym: denominationally|word property:adverb

Etymology:
inter- (prefix): between, among  (Latin)
denomination (noun): a religious group or sect  (Late Middle English, from Old French)
-al (suffix): relating to  (Latin)
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs  (Old English)
