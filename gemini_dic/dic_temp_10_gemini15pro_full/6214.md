ammoniums: /əˈmoʊniəmz/
| n. |铵盐（复数）|Plural form of ammonium, referring to more than one type or instance of the positively charged ion (NH4+) or its salts.|Ammonium chloride and ammonium nitrate are common ammoniums used in fertilizers. 氯化铵和硝酸铵是肥料中常用的铵盐。|近义词：ammonium salts|反义词: N/A|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From ammonia + -ium.  Ammonia ultimately derives from the name of the ancient Egyptian god Amun, near whose temple the substance was originally prepared.
