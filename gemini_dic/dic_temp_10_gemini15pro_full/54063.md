disdiapason:/ˌdɪsdʌɪəˈpeɪzən/ | n. | 全音域,八度,和谐 | A full octave; an interval of an octave.  It also can refer to harmony or concord. | The organ's disdiapason filled the cathedral.  教堂的八度音充满了整个大教堂。 | Synonym: octave, concord, harmony | Antonym: dissonance, discord | noun

disdiapason:/ˌdɪsdʌɪəˈpeɪzən/ | adj. | 八度音程的，和谐的 |  Relating to or having a range of an octave; harmonious.  This usage is less common. |  The disdiapason chords resonated beautifully. 八度和弦产生了优美的共鸣。 | Synonym: octave, harmonious, concordant | Antonym: dissonant, discordant | adjective
