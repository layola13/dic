overgrade:/ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪd/| vt.|过高评价，高估；评级过高；给…过高的等级|To assign a higher grade or value to something than it deserves.|The teacher overgraded the student's essay, giving it an A when it really deserved a B. 老师对学生的文章评价过高，给了A，而实际上应该给B。|Overestimate, overvalue, overrate|Underestimate, undervalue, underrate|Verb

overgrade:/ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪd/| n.|过高的等级|A grade that is too high.|The student received an overgrade on the project. 学生在这个项目上得到了过高的等级。|Overestimation, overvaluation|Underestimation, undervaluation|Noun
