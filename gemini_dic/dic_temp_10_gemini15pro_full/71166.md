fissiparism:/ˈfɪsɪˌpɑːrɪzəm/
n.
分裂生殖；分裂；裂殖
Reproduction by spontaneous division of the body into two or more parts, each of which grows into a complete organism.  The tendency of a group or organization to split into factions.
例句：
The amoeba reproduces by fissiparism.
变形虫通过分裂生殖繁殖。

The fissiparism within the political party led to its eventual downfall.
该政党内部的分裂导致其最终垮台。

近义词：division, fragmentation, schism, splitting
反义词：unity, unification, coalescence
词性: noun
