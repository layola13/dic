grubs:/ɡrʌbz/| n.|幼虫，蛆，食物|The chickens pecked at the grubs in the dirt. 小鸡啄食地上的幼虫。| larva, maggots, food |worms, insects|
Etymology: From Middle English *grubben*, to dig, delve, related to Old English *græf*, ditch, grave.  The sense of "food" arose from the notion of "digging for food".
