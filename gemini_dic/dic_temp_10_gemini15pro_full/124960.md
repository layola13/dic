muffy:/ˈmʌfi/| adj.|闷热的，不通风的；(非正式) 恼怒的，生气的|A stuffy room is one that is hot and has no fresh air.  (闷热的房间指一个炎热且不通风的房间。)  Muffy can also informally describe someone as annoyed or irritated. (Muffy也可以非正式地描述某人生气或恼怒。) |The room was muffy and smelled of stale cigarettes. (房间里很闷，弥漫着陈腐的香烟味。)  She gave me a muffy look when I interrupted her. (我打断她时，她给了我一个恼怒的眼神。)|近义词:stuffy, airless, close, stifling, irritated, annoyed|反义词:airy, ventilated, pleased, happy|词性:形容词

词源:
Likely a variant of "stuffy," with the initial "st-" replaced by "m-." The exact origin of this alteration is uncertain.
