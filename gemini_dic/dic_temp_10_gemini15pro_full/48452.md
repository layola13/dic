degree: /dɪˈɡriː/ /dɪˈɡri/ | n. |学位；程度；等级；度数| a unit for measuring temperature or the size of an angle  A qualification awarded to someone who has successfully completed a university course.  The amount or level of something.|
例句：She has a degree in physics. 她拥有物理学学位。

例句：The temperature reached 30 degrees Celsius. 温度达到了30摄氏度。

近义词：level, extent, qualification, diploma
反义词:lack, absence
词性:noun
