buzzard:/ˈbʌzərd/ (US) /ˈbʌzəd/ (UK)| n.|秃鹫；（比喻）贪婪的人|A large bird of prey that feeds on carrion (dead animals). Also used figuratively to describe a greedy or opportunistic person.|例句：The buzzard circled overhead, waiting for the dying animal below. 秃鹫在头顶盘旋，等待着下面垂死的动物。

例句：He's a real buzzard when it comes to business deals.  在生意上他真是个贪婪的人。|近义词：vulture, hawk, condor|反义词: dove (象征和平的鸟)|词性:noun
