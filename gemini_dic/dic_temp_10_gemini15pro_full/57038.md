doubling:/ˈdʌblɪŋ/ | n. | 加倍，翻倍 | The doubling of the population in the last fifty years has put a strain on resources.  过去五十年人口翻了一番，给资源带来了压力。 | Example: The company saw a doubling of profits this year.  今年公司利润翻了一番。 | Synonyms: duplication, increasing, multiplication | Antonyms: halving, dividing, reduction | noun

doubling:/ˈdʌblɪŋ/ | adj. | 双重的，加倍的 |  A doubling effect was observed in the experiment. 在实验中观察到了加倍的效果。 | Example:  He's taking a doubling dose of vitamins. 他服用了双倍剂量的维生素。 | Synonyms: dual, twofold, double | Antonyms: single, half | adjective
