allspice:/ˈɔːlˌspaɪs/ | n. |多香果，牙买加胡椒|A spice made from the dried unripe berries of the pimento tree, having a flavor suggestive of a combination of cinnamon, cloves, and nutmeg.|例句：I added a pinch of allspice to the pumpkin pie. 我在南瓜派里加了一撮多香果。|近义词：pimento|反义词:  (No direct antonym for a spice) |词性:noun


allspice:/ˈɔːlˌspaɪs/ | adj. |用多香果调味的|Flavored with allspice.|例句：The allspice cookies were delicious. 多香果饼干很好吃。|近义词：(No direct synonym for this adjectival use) |反义词: (No direct antonym for this adjectival use) |词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From all + spice,  because its flavor was thought to resemble a blend of several spices. The word dates back to the early 17th century.
