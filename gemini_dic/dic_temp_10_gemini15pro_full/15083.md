baccalaureate: /ˌbækəˈlɔːriət/ (US) /bəˌkæləˈriːət/ (UK)| n.|学士学位；中学毕业会考；(尤指天主教的)学士学位授予仪式|A university degree awarded after three or four years of study, or a ceremony at which such degrees are awarded.  The baccalaureate sermon was delivered by the university president.|例句：She received her baccalaureate degree in English literature. 她获得了英语文学学士学位。

例句：The baccalaureate service was held in the university chapel. 学士学位授予仪式在大学礼拜堂举行。|近义词：degree, diploma|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
