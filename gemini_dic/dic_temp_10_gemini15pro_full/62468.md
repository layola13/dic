endoscopists:/ɛnˈdɒskəpɪsts/
n.
内窥镜医师，内窥镜专家
Medical professionals who are skilled in using endoscopes to examine the interior of the body.
The endoscopists performed a colonoscopy to examine the patient's large intestine. 内窥镜医师对病人进行了结肠镜检查以检查其大肠。
The experienced endoscopists skillfully navigated the endoscope through the patient's digestive tract. 经验丰富的内窥镜医师熟练地将内窥镜穿过患者的消化道。
近义词:endoscopy specialists
反义词:None
