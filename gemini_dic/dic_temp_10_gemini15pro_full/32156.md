chewiest:/ˈtʃuːiɪst/| adj.|最难嚼的，最有嚼劲的|Superlative form of "chewy," meaning requiring much chewing or having a texture that resists biting or chewing.|This is the chewiest steak I've ever had. 这是我吃过最难嚼的牛排。|The taffy is incredibly chewiest. 这太妃糖非常有嚼劲。|近义词：toughest, firmest, rubbery|反义词：tender, soft, melt-in-your-mouth|词性:形容词

词源: From "chew" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives meaning 'having the quality of') + "-est" (superlative suffix).  "Chew" comes from Old English "cēowan."
