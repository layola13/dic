composure:/kəmˈpəʊʒər/ (British) /kəmˈpoʊʒər/ (American)| n. |沉着；镇静；泰然自若|The state of being calm, cool, and collected; self-possession.|She maintained her composure despite the chaos around her. 她尽管周围一片混乱，依然保持镇静。|Synonyms: calmness, equanimity, poise, self-control|Antonyms: agitation, anxiety, panic, distress|noun
