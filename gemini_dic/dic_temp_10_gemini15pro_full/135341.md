nonpenetrating:/ˌnɒnˈpɛnɪtreɪtɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnˈpɛnɪtreɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非穿透的；非渗透的
Not penetrating; unable to penetrate or pass through something.
The doctor confirmed the wound was nonpenetrating. 医生确认伤口没有穿透。
The coating provides a nonpenetrating barrier against moisture.  这层涂料提供了一个防潮的非渗透屏障。
近义词：impermeable, noninvasive, superficial
反义词：penetrating, invasive, piercing
词性：adjective
词源：non- (not) + penetrating (from Latin penetrare, "to penetrate")
