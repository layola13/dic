monometer:/məˈnɒmɪtər/ |/məˈnɑːmɪtər/ | n. |单音步，单音节诗行|A metrical line of verse consisting of one metrical foot, or one stressed syllable. |The poem was written entirely in monometers, creating a stark and rhythmic effect. 这首诗完全以单音步写成，营造出一种鲜明而有节奏的效果。|  The child's first poem was a simple monometer.  孩子的第一首诗是一个简单的单音节诗行。 |近义词: single-foot verse |反义词:  (No direct antonym, but could contrast with)  dimeter, trimeter, tetrameter, pentameter, hexameter |词性: noun
