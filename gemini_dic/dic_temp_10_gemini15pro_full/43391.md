cranker:/ˈkræŋkər/| n.|怪人，脾气坏的人；手摇曲柄；<非正式>汽车|A cranker is an informal term for a person who is bad-tempered or eccentric, especially one who has strong, fixed, and often eccentric beliefs. It can also refer to a hand crank or, informally, a car.|He's a bit of a cranker, always complaining about something. 他有点怪，总是抱怨个不停。|Synonyms: eccentric, grouch, curmudgeon, crank, oddball|Antonyms: agreeable person, pleasant person|noun


cranker:/ˈkræŋkər/| n.|启动装置，起动机|A device, especially a handle, that is turned to start an engine or other machinery.|The old car needed a few cranks to get started.  这辆老爷车需要摇几下手柄才能启动。|Synonyms: starter, handle, winder|Antonyms: N/A|noun
