autostylic:/ˌɔːtəˈstaɪlɪk/
adjective
自身支撑的；自柱的
(of a jaw suspension in vertebrates) in which the upper jaw is connected directly to the cranium, without intervening support from the hyomandibular arch, as in mammals and chimaeras.  (脊椎动物的颌骨悬挂方式)上颌骨直接与颅骨相连，没有来自舌颌弓的中间支撑，如哺乳动物和银鲛。
Autostylic jaw suspension is a derived condition found in mammals.  自身支撑的颌骨悬挂是哺乳动物的一种衍生特征。
synonym:  holostylic (somewhat archaic, more commonly used for fish)
antonym:  hyostylic, amphistylic
词性: adjective
