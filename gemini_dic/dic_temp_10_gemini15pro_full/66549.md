exhibitable:/ɪɡˈzɪbɪtəbl/| adj.|可展览的，可展出的|Suitable or fit to be exhibited; capable of being displayed or shown publicly. |The artist's paintings were deemed exhibitable and were displayed in a local gallery.  这位艺术家的画被认为是可以展出的，并在当地一家画廊展出。|Synonyms: displayable, showable, presentable, demonstrable|Antonyms: unpresentable, unsuitable, unfit|adjective
