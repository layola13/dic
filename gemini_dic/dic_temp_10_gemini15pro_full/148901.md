overwhips:/ˌoʊvərˈhwɪps/ | v. | 过度搅打 | To beat (cream, eggs, etc.) excessively, resulting in a stiff or grainy texture. |  The chef overwhipped the cream, and it turned into butter.  厨师过度搅打了奶油，结果变成了黄油。| overbeat, overmix | underwhip | verb
overwhips:/ˌoʊvərˈhwɪps/ | n. | 过度搅打的结果(通常指奶油或蛋白)| The stiff, grainy result of overwhipping cream or eggs. | The overwhip was unusable for the cake frosting.  过度搅打后的奶油无法用于蛋糕糖霜。| - | - | noun
