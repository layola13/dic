overrepresentative:/ˌoʊvərˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪv/
adjective
代表过多的，比例过高的
having more members of a particular group than is justified or usual, given the proportion of that group in the general population.
例句：The study showed that women are overrepresented in administrative roles. 这项研究表明，女性在行政职位上的比例过高。

例句：Certain ethnic groups are overrepresented in the prison population.  某些种族群体在监狱人口中比例过高。

近义词：disproportionate, disproportionately represented
反义词：underrepresented
词性：adjective
