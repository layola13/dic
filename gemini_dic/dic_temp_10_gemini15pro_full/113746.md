macrology:/məˈkrɒlədʒi/| n.|冗词赘述，长篇大论|The professor's lectures were often criticized for their macrology, spending too much time on minor details.|例句：His macrology bored the audience. 他的长篇大论让听众感到厌烦。

例句：The book was filled with macrology and could have been half as long. 这本书充满了冗词赘述，篇幅可以缩短一半。|近义词：prolixity, verbosity, wordiness|反义词:conciseness, brevity|词性:noun

Etymology->
From Ancient Greek  μακρολογία (makrología), from μακρός (makrós, “long”) + -λογία (-logía, “-logy”).
