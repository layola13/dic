nonabstemiousnesses:/ˌnɒnæbˈstiːmiəsnɪsɪz/
noun
不节制,放纵
Lack of moderation or restraint, especially in eating or drinking.
His nonabstemiousnesses led to health problems. 他的不节制导致了健康问题。
Synonyms:intemperance, indulgence, excess, gluttony
Antonyms:abstinence, temperance, moderation, sobriety
