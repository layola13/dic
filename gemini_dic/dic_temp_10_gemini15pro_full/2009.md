actualizes: /ˈæktʃuəlaɪzɪz/
verb
实现，实行
To make something real or actual; to put into effect; to fulfill.
The company finally actualized its plans for expansion. = 公司最终实现了其扩张计划。

He actualized his dream of becoming a doctor. = 他实现了成为一名医生的梦想。

近义词: realize, achieve, fulfill, complete
反义词: neglect, abandon, fail

词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin actualis "active, practical" (from Latin actus, past participle of agere "to do, drive, lead, act"; see act (n.)) + -ize.
