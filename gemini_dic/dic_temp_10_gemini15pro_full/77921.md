genitocrural:/ˌdʒɛnɪtoʊˈkruːrəl/
adjective
生殖器与大腿的；腹股沟的
Relating to the genitalia and the thigh or groin.
The genitocrural nerve supplies sensation to the skin of the groin and upper thigh. 腹股沟神经为腹股沟和大腿上部皮肤提供感觉。
The patient presented with genitocrural pain. 病人表现出腹股沟疼痛。
近义词: inguinofemoral
反义词: (No direct antonym, could be considered contextually as areas other than the groin and thigh, e.g., cephalic, caudal)
词性: adjective
