ectosteal:/ˌɛk.toʊˈstiː.əl/
adjective
外骨骼的，膜骨的
Relating to or derived from ectosteal bone; formed from or by a membrane bone.
The ectosteal layer of the skull forms the outer surface.  颅骨的外骨骼层形成外表面。
The fish's scales are of ectosteal origin.  这鱼的鳞片起源于外骨骼。
synonym: periosteal, membranous
antonym: endochondral
adjective
