contraplex:/ˌkɒntrəˈpleks/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈpleks/
n.
交织物，复杂结构
A complex or interwoven structure or system.
This city is a contraplex of old and new buildings.  这座城市是新旧建筑的交织体。
The human brain is an amazing contraplex of neurons.  人脑是一个令人惊叹的神经元复杂结构。
近义词:complex, network, web, nexus
反义词:simplicity, basic, elementary
词性:名词


Etymology:  "Contra-" (against, opposite) + "-plex" (fold, weave, braid)  from Latin plicāre (to fold). The prefix suggests a folding or weaving together of different elements, often in a complex or intricate way.
