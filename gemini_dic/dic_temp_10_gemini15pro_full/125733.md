munjistin:/mʌnˈdʒɪstɪn/ | n. |芒吉斯汀; 红树皮提取物; |A natural red dye extracted from the root bark of the Indian mulberry (Morinda citrifolia).  It is used for dyeing fabrics and as a food coloring.|The traditional batik uses munjistin as a vibrant red dye.  传统的蜡染使用芒吉斯汀作为鲜艳的红色染料。|Synonyms:  Indian mulberry dye, Morinda dye|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
