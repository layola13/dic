non-prejudiced:/ˌnɒnˈprɛdʒʊdɪst/ | adj. | 没有偏见的；公正的 | Not having or showing prejudice; impartial. |  The jury was instructed to be non-prejudiced and consider only the facts presented. 陪审团被指示要保持公正，只考虑提出的事实。

The organization is committed to non-prejudiced hiring practices. 该组织致力于无偏见的招聘实践。| 近义词: unbiased, impartial, fair, objective, unbigoted, tolerant | 反义词: prejudiced, biased, bigoted, intolerant, discriminatory | 词性: 形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non, meaning "not"
prejudiced (adjective): from Late Middle English, from Old French *prejudicier (“to prejudice”), from Latin praejudicium, from prae- ("before") + judicium ("judgment").
