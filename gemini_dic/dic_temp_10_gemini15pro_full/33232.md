chronographically:/ˌkrɒnəˈɡræfɪkli/
adv.|按时间顺序地|in a way that arranges events in the order of their occurrence.|The events were described chronographically in the documentary.|近义词：sequentially, chronologically|反义词:randomly, haphazardly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From chrono- (time) + -graphically (in a descriptive manner).  "Chrono-" ultimately derives from the Greek word "khronos" (time). "-graphically" comes from "-graphic" (relating to writing or recording) + "-ally" (in a manner).
