overoptimistic:/ˌoʊvərˌɑːptɪˈmɪstɪk/ | adj. | 过分乐观的 | Excessively or unrealistically optimistic; too hopeful or sanguine.  Characterized by excessive optimism, often to the point of being unrealistic or impractical. | He was overoptimistic about his chances of winning the lottery. 他对赢得彩票的机会过于乐观。|  She had an overoptimistic view of the housing market. 她对房地产市场抱有过分乐观的态度。| 近义词:  excessively optimistic, unrealistically optimistic, sanguine, Pollyannaish | 反义词: pessimistic, realistic, cynical, pragmatic | 词性: 形容词
