lithocyst: /ˈlɪθəˌsɪst/
n.
石细胞囊,石细胞
A small, hard, calcareous body found in the tissues of certain invertebrates, especially the statocysts of some mollusks.
The lithocyst helps the mollusk maintain balance and orientation.
例句：The mollusk uses its lithocyst to sense gravity.  软体动物利用它的石细胞囊来感知重力。

例句：Researchers are studying the lithocyst to understand its function in molluscan balance.  研究人员正在研究石细胞囊以了解其在软体动物平衡中的作用。

近义词: statolith, otolith
