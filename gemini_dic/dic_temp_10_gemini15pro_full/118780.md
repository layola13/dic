metalloid:/ˈmɛt(ə)lɔɪd/ | n. | 类金属 | An element (e.g. arsenic, antimony, or tin) whose properties are intermediate between those of metals and solid nonmetals. They are electrical semiconductors. | Silicon is a metalloid used in computer chips.  硅是一种用于计算机芯片的类金属。 |  semiconductor, semimetal | metal, nonmetal | noun
