microgyria:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈdʒaɪriə/| n. |小脑回|A condition characterized by small, abnormally formed convolutions (gyri) of the cerebral cortex.|Microgyria can be a cause of epilepsy. 小脑回可能是癫痫的病因。|Synonyms: polymicrogyria (more severe form)|Antonyms: None (absence of gyri is called lissencephaly)|noun


Etymology->
From micro- + gyria, from Ancient Greek γῦρος (gûros, “ring, circle”).
