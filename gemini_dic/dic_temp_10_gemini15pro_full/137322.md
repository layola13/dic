nontangential:/ˌnɑːn tænˈdʒɛnʃəl/ | adj. |  直接相关的，切题的 |  Relating directly to the matter at hand; pertinent.  Staying focused on the main topic without digressing or going off on tangents. |  Her questions were nontangential and helped to clarify the situation. 她的问题切中要害，有助于澄清情况。 |  The discussion remained nontangential, focusing solely on the project's goals.  讨论始终紧扣主题，只关注项目目标。 | Synonyms: relevant, pertinent, germane, to the point | Antonyms: tangential, irrelevant, digressive, unrelated | adjective
