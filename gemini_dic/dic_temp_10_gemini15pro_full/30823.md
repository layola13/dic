cha-cha:/ˈtʃɑː tʃɑː/| n. vi.|恰恰舞，跳恰恰舞|A fast rhythmic ballroom dance of Cuban origin, consisting of a sequence of three steps and a shuffle.|They danced the cha-cha all night. 他们整晚都在跳恰恰舞。|近义词：rumba, mambo|反义词:None|名词/动词
