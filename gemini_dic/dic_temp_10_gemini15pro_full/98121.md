insalubrity:/ˌɪnsəˈlubrɪti/
n.
不健康，不卫生；有害健康
The state of being unhealthy; unwholesomeness.
The insalubrity of the swamp made it a breeding ground for mosquitoes.  沼泽地不卫生，成了蚊子的滋生地。
近义词：unhealthiness, noxiousness, pestilence
反义词：healthiness, salubrity, wholesomeness
词性: 名词

insalubrious:/ˌɪnsəˈlubriəs/
adj.
不健康的，不卫生的；有害健康的
Not conducive to health; unhealthy.
The insalubrious living conditions in the slums led to a high rate of disease. 贫民窟不卫生的生活条件导致了高发病率。
近义词：unhealthy, unwholesome, noxious
反义词：healthy, wholesome, salubrious
词性: 形容词


词源:
From French *insalubre*, from Latin *insalubris* (“unwholesome, unhealthy”), from *in-* (“not”) + *salubris* (“healthful”).
