let-in:/ˌlɛt ˈɪn/ | adj. |嵌入的，镶嵌的；允许进入的| Built-in, inserted, or incorporated as part of something larger; allowed to enter. |  The windows were let-in to the roof. 窗户被嵌入屋顶。| synonyms: built-in, incorporated, inserted, embedded, admitted | antonyms: freestanding, separate, excluded, barred | adjective

let-in:/ˌlɛt ˈɪn/ | n. |嵌入物；允许进入| Something that is let in, such as a panel or section; the act of allowing someone or something to enter. | The let-in for the air conditioning unit was too small.  空调装置的嵌入部分太小了。 | synonyms: insertion, inlet, intake, admittance | antonyms: outlet, outake, exclusion | noun
