industrialism: /ɪnˈdʌstriəlɪzəm/
n.
工业主义；产业主义
A social system in which industry and factories are the most important parts of the economy.  A period of social and economic change that transforms a human group from an agrarian society into an industrial one.
The Industrial Revolution marked a major turning point in human history; almost every aspect of daily life was eventually influenced in some way.
工业革命标志着人类历史上的一个重要转折点；几乎日常生活的方方面面最终都受到了某种程度的影响。

industrialism: /ɪnˈdʌstriəlɪzəm/
n.
工业主义；产业主义
The development of industries in a country or region on a wide scale.
The rapid industrialism of the 19th century brought about significant social and economic changes.
19世纪的快速工业化带来了重大的社会和经济变革。


近义词: industrialization, manufacturing
反义词: agrarianism
