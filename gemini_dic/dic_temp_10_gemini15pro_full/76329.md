gablet:/ˈɡeɪblɪt/| n.|三角墙，山墙|A small gable, especially a decorative one over a window or door.|The gablet above the window added a touch of charm to the cottage.|Synonym: gable, pediment|Antonym: (No direct antonym)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old North French *gablel, diminutive of gable. Ultimately from Old Norse gafl.
