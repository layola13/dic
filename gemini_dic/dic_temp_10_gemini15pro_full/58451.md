duct:/dʌkt/ | n. | 管道，导管 | A channel or tube for conveying something, such as air, liquid, cables, etc. |  The air conditioning duct is clogged with dust. 空调管道被灰尘堵塞了。

The tear duct was blocked. 泪管堵塞了。

| 近义词：channel, tube, conduit, pipe | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 blockage 阻塞) | 词性: 名词

duct:/ˈdʌktɪl/ | adj. | 可延展的，易延展的 | Easily drawn out into wire or hammered thin. | Copper is a ductile metal, easily shaped into wires. 铜是一种延展性很好的金属，很容易塑造成电线。

The clay was still ductile and easy to mold.  黏土仍然具有延展性，很容易塑形。

| 近义词：malleable, pliable, flexible | 反义词: brittle, inflexible, rigid | 词性: 形容词
