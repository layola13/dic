allosaurus:/ˌæləˈsɔːrəs/
n.
异特龙（一种大型肉食性恐龙）
A large carnivorous dinosaur of the Late Jurassic period.
The Allosaurus was a fearsome predator. 异特龙是一种可怕的掠食者。
The museum's main exhibit featured a complete Allosaurus skeleton.  博物馆的主要展品以完整的异特龙骨架为特色。
近义词:carnosaur, theropod
反义词:herbivore (广义上的反义词，指食草动物)
词性:noun


Etymology:  From Greek *allos* "different" + *sauros* "lizard".  The name refers to the different structure of its vertebrae compared to other dinosaurs known at the time of its discovery.
