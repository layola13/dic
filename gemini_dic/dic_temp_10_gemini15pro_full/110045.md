licenser:/ˈlaɪsənsər/ | n. |许可人，授权人|Someone who grants a license.  The person or entity that owns the rights to something and allows another party to use it under agreed-upon terms.| The software licenser requires users to register their product online.  该软件许可方要求用户在线注册其产品。 | synonyms: grantor, authorizer | antonyms: licensee | noun
