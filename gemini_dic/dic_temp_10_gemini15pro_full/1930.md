actinometric:/ˌæktɪnəˈmɛtrɪk/
adjective
光化测量的，光能测量的
Relating to or involving the measurement of the intensity of electromagnetic radiation, especially sunlight.
Actinometric measurements are crucial for understanding the energy balance of the Earth.  光化测量对于理解地球的能量平衡至关重要。

The researchers used an actinometric method to determine the amount of UV radiation reaching the ground. 研究人员使用光化测量方法来确定到达地面的紫外线辐射量。

近义词:radiometric
反义词:None
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From actino- (relating to radiation) + -metric (relating to measurement).  The prefix "actino-" comes from the Greek "aktīn" meaning "ray".
