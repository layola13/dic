akinesia: /ˌeɪkaɪˈniːʒə/ /ˌeɪkɪˈniːziə/ | n. |运动不能，运动迟缓|Loss or impairment of the power of voluntary movement.| Parkinson's disease can cause akinesia, making it difficult for sufferers to initiate movement.  帕金森氏病会导致运动不能，使患者难以启动运动。

The akinesia made it difficult for him to even lift a spoon. 运动不能让他连勺子都举不起来。|近义词：hypokinesia, bradykinesia |反义词: hyperkinesia |词性: noun
