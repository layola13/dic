inflexiblenesses:/ɪnˌflɛksɪˈbɪlnɪsɪz/| n. |不灵活性，僵化，顽固|The inflexiblenesses of the company's policies made it difficult to adapt to changing market conditions. 公司政策的不灵活性使其难以适应不断变化的市场环境。|例句：The inflexiblenesses of his attitude frustrated his colleagues. 他态度的僵化使他的同事们感到沮丧。

The inflexiblenesses of the old system hindered progress. 旧系统的不灵活性阻碍了进步。


|近义词：rigidities, stiffness, intransigence|反义词: flexibility, adaptability, pliability |词性: 名词
