boustrophedonic:/ˌbuːstrəˈfɛdənɪk/
adjective
回转形的，来回书写的
Relating to a writing system in which alternate lines are written in opposite directions (as from right to left and then from left to right).
The ancient inscription was written in a boustrophedonic style, alternating directions with each line. 这段古代铭文以回转形书写，每一行的方向都交替变化。
The ox plowed the field in a boustrophedonic pattern.  牛以回转形的方式犁地。

近义词: bidirectional, alternating
反义词: unidirectional, linear
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Greek βουστροφηδόν (boustrophēdón), from βοῦς (boûs, “ox”) + στροφή (strophḗ, “turn”) + -δόν (-dón, adverbial suffix).  Literally "ox-turning-wise", referring to the way an ox plows a field, turning back and forth at the end of each row.
