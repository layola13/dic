microchipping: /ˈmaɪkroʊˌtʃɪpɪŋ/ | n. | 微芯片植入；芯片植入 | The act of implanting a microchip, especially in an animal for identification. |  Microchipping your pet is a responsible way to ensure its safe return if it gets lost. 给你的宠物植入芯片是一个负责任的做法，可以确保它走失后安全返回。 | Synonyms: chip implantation, tagging | Antonyms:  N/A | noun

microchip: /ˈmaɪkroʊtʃɪp/ | n. vt. | 微芯片；芯片；给…植入微芯片 | A tiny integrated circuit; to implant a microchip. | The dog was microchipped for identification. 这只狗被植入了微芯片以便识别。 | Synonyms: integrated circuit, chip | Antonyms: N/A | noun, verb
