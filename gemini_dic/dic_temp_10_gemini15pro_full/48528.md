deev: /diːv/
noun
精灵，妖怪 (Scottish folklore)
A supernatural being in Scottish folklore, often mischievous or malevolent.
例句：The old woman told stories of deevs that lived in the forest. 老妇人讲述了住在森林里的妖怪的故事。

例句：Legend has it that deevs would steal children who wandered too far from home.  传说妖怪会偷走离家太远的孩子。

近义词：demon, imp, sprite, fairy, elf
反义词: angel
词性: noun
