flippantness:/ˈflɪpəntnəs/
n.|轻率;轻浮;无礼|The flippantness of his response angered her. 他轻率的回答激怒了她。|
Etymology: From flippant + -ness.  Flippant (adj.) from flip (v.)  "to flick, toss" (1590s) + -ant.  Ultimately from Middle Low German or Middle Dutch flippen "to fly quickly."|
Synonyms: frivolity, levity, impertinence, sauciness|
Antonyms: seriousness, gravity, respectfulness, solemnity|
