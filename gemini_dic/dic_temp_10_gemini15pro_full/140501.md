ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ealdmann* (attested only as a surname), from *eald* ("old") + *mann* ("man"). Cognate with Old Frisian *aldman*, Old Saxon *aldman*, Old High German *altman*, Old Norse *aldmaðr*.
