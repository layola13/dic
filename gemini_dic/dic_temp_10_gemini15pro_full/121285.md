misfeeling:/ˌmɪsˈfiːlɪŋ/
noun
不和；嫌隙；恶感
a feeling of distrust or antipathy between people.
There was some misfeeling between the two families. 这两个家庭之间有些不和。

There's been some misfeeling between the brothers ever since the argument. 自从争吵之后，兄弟俩就一直有些不和。

misunderstanding, bad blood, animosity, friction, tension, resentment, hostility, ill will, acrimony, antagonism
goodwill, amity, friendship, rapport
