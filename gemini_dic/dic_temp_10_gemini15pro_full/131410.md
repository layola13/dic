nonclassified:/ˌnɒnˈklæsɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 非机密的，未分类的 | Not formally designated as secret, confidential, or restricted. | The information was nonclassified and available to the public.  这个信息是非机密的，公众可以获取。 |  public, open, unrestricted | classified, confidential, secret | adjective

例句：

The document is nonclassified and can be shared with anyone.
这份文件是非机密的，可以与任何人分享。

He released nonclassified information to the press.
他向媒体发布了非机密信息。

反义词:

词源:

Prefix "non-" (not) + "classified" (arranged in classes or categories, especially relating to security).  由前缀“non-”（不）和“classified”（分为类别或等级，尤指与安全相关）组成。
