determinisms: /dɪˈtɜːrmɪnɪzəmz/
|n.|决定论|The doctrine that all events, including human action, are ultimately determined by causes external to the will.  一切事件，包括人类行为，最终都是由意志以外的原因决定的学说。|Some philosophers believe in strict determinisms, arguing that free will is an illusion. 一些哲学家相信严格的决定论，认为自由意志是一种幻觉。|近义词: fatalism, predestination|反义词: indeterminism, free will|词性: noun


Etymology:
From determine + -ism.  Determine comes from Old French *determiner "to limit, fix, decide," from Latin determinare "to mark off, limit, fix," from de- "completely" (see de-) + terminare "to set bounds to," from terminus "boundary mark."
