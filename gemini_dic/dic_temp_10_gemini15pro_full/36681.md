collies:/ˈkɒliz/ (UK), /ˈkɑːliz/ (US)| n.|牧羊犬（尤指苏格兰牧羊犬）|A breed of large, long-haired dog, originally bred in Scotland for herding sheep.|例句：The collies herded the sheep into the pen. 牧羊犬把羊群赶进了羊圈。|例句：Two collies were playing in the field. 两只牧羊犬正在田野里玩耍。|近义词：sheepdogs, herding dogs|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
