cuirassier:/ˌkwɪrəˈsɪər/| n.|胸甲骑兵|A heavily armed cavalry soldier, especially one equipped with a cuirass (breastplate and backplate).|例句：The cuirassiers charged into the enemy lines. 胸甲骑兵冲入敌阵。

例句：Napoleon's cuirassiers were a formidable force on the battlefield. 拿破仑的胸甲骑兵在战场上是一支强大的力量。|近义词：cavalryman, trooper|反义词: infantryman|词性:noun
