chartreux:/ʃɑːrˈtrɜːz/ (US) /ʃɑːˈtrɜː/ (UK)| n. |沙特尔猫; 灰蓝色|A breed of domestic cat known for its bluish-gray coat and orange eyes. Originating in France, it is known for its quiet nature.|例句：My Chartreux is a very affectionate cat. 我的沙特尔猫非常 affectionate.|近义词：cat, feline|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

chartreuse:/ʃɑːrˈtrɜːz/ (US) /ʃɑːˈtrɜː/ (UK)| adj. |灰蓝色的|A color described as a grayish or muted yellowish-green, similar to the color of a Chartreux cat's fur.|例句：She wore a chartreuse dress to the party. 她穿了一件灰蓝色的连衣裙去参加派对。|近义词：bluish-gray, yellowish-green|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective
