caudal:/ˈkɔːdəl/| adj.|尾部的，尾状的|Of, relating to, or situated near the tail or posterior part of the body.|The caudal fin of the fish propelled it forward.  鱼的尾鳍推动它前进。|近义词：posterior, hind, rear|反义词：anterior, cranial, rostral|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *caudālis*, from cauda (“tail”).
