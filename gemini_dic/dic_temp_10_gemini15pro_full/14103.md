authentic:/ɔːˈθɛntɪk/ | adj. | 真实的，真正的；可信的；可靠的 | genuine; real; true | The painting was examined by experts and found to be authentic.  这幅画经专家鉴定是真品。 | 近义词: genuine, real, true, bona fide | 反义词: fake, counterfeit, spurious, bogus | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Late Latin *authenticus, from Latin authenticus (“authoritative, genuine”), from auctor (“originator, author”).  源自晚期拉丁语 *authenticus，来自拉丁语 authenticus（“权威的，真实的”），来自 auctor（“创始人，作者”）。
