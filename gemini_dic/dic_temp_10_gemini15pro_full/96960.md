infamously:/ˈɪnfəməsli/ | adv. | 声名狼藉地，臭名昭著地 | in a way that is well known for being bad |例句：The politician is infamously known for his corruption.  这位政客因腐败而臭名昭著。| 近义词：notoriously, disreputably | 反义词：famously, honorably | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *infamosus + -ly.  *Infamosus from in- "not" + fama "fame, reputation" + -osus "-ous."
