cautioned:/ˈkɔːʃənd/ | v. | 警告，告诫 | To warn someone about a possible danger or problem. | The doctor cautioned her against smoking. 医生告诫她不要吸烟。|  warned, advised, alerted |  encouraged, urged | verb
Etymology:  From the Old French "caucioner" and ultimately from the Latin "cautio" (carefulness, precaution).
