faithfulnesses:/ˈfeɪθfəlnəsɪz/
n.|忠诚；忠实（复数）|The faithfulnesses of dogs are well known. 狗的忠诚是众所周知的。|例句：His faithfulnesses to his wife and family were unwavering. 他对妻子和家人的忠诚坚定不移。

例句：The faithfulnesses of the disciples were tested during Jesus's crucifixion. 耶稣受难期间，门徒们的忠诚受到了考验。


近义词：loyalties, fidelities, constancies
反义词：treacheries, betrayals, infidelities
词性：名词


词源：From Middle English *feithfulnes, from feithful +‎ -nes. Equivalent to faithful +‎ -ness.
