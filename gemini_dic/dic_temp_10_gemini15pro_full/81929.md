grilses: /ˈɡrɪlsɪz/ | n. | 幼鲑; 灰鲑;| A young salmon on its first return from the sea to fresh water.|  例句：The river was teeming with grilses returning to spawn.  这条河里充满了返回产卵的幼鲑。

例句：The grilses leaped out of the water, flashing silver in the sunlight.  幼鲑跃出水面，在阳光下闪着银光。 | 近义词：salmon, parr, smolt | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用成年鲑 adult salmon) | 词性: 名词


Etymology: From Scottish Gaelic *greusaich*, diminutive of *greus*, "salmon".
