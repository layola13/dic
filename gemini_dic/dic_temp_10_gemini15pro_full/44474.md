crossbones:/ˈkrɒsbəʊnz/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsbəʊnz/ (US)| n.|交叉骨|Two bones, especially from a human skull, laid across each other to form an X.  Often used as a symbol of danger or death.|The poison bottle was marked with a skull and crossbones.  毒药瓶上标有骷髅头和交叉骨。|近义词:skull and crossbones|反义词:None|词性:noun
