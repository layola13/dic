过度缓慢，极端缓慢
The overslownesses of the bureaucratic process frustrated everyone involved.  官僚程序的过度缓慢让所有参与的人都感到沮丧。
Extreme slowness, excessive slowness
The overslownesses in the system caused significant delays. 系统的极端缓慢造成了严重的延误。
Synonyms: sluggishness, tardiness, dilatoriness
Antonyms: speed, rapidity, swiftness
