empiric:/ɪmˈpɪrɪk/ | adj. n.| 经验主义的; 经验主义者 |Based on observation or experience rather than theory or pure logic. |  Empiric evidence suggests that this treatment is effective. 经验表明这种疗法是有效的。| synonyms: experiential, observed, practical, experimental | antonyms: theoretical, hypothetical, conjectural | adjective
empiric:/ɪmˈpɪrɪk/ | n.| 经验主义者 | A person who relies on observation and experiment. | The empiric questioned the traditional remedies.  这位经验主义者对传统疗法提出了质疑。 | synonyms: pragmatist, experimentalist | antonyms: theorist, doctrinaire | noun
