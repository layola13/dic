inlaying:/ɪnˈleɪɪŋ/
n.|镶嵌; 嵌入; 镶嵌物|The process of decorating a surface by inserting pieces of a different material into it, flush with the surface.  |The inlaying on the wooden box was exquisite.  木盒上的镶嵌工艺精美绝伦。|近义词: embedding, insertion, setting |反义词: removal, extraction
词性: 名词

inlay:/ˈɪnleɪ/
v. |镶嵌; 嵌入|To decorate a surface by inserting pieces of a different material into it, flush with the surface. |He inlaid the table with mother-of-pearl. 他在桌子上镶嵌了珍珠母。|近义词: embed, insert, set |反义词: remove, extract
词性: 动词
