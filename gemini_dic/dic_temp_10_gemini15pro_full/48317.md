decree: /dɪˈkriː/
n.|法令，命令|An official order that has the force of law.|The king issued a decree banning smoking in public places. 国王颁布了一项法令，禁止在公共场所吸烟。| edict, order, proclamation| repeal, countermand|noun
decree: /dɪˈkriː/
vt.|颁布（法令），裁定|To issue a decree; to order something officially.|The court decreed that the company must pay compensation. 法院裁定该公司必须支付赔偿金。| order, command, rule| rescind, revoke|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *decreer*, from Latin dēcrēscere "to decide, determine," from dē- "down, completely" + crēscere "to grow, increase."  The sense shifted from "decide" to "ordain, decree."
