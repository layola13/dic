misdescribed:/ˌmɪsdɪˈskraɪbd/
verb
错误描述的，描述不当的
incorrectly or misleadingly described
The product was misdescribed in the advertisement.  该产品在广告中描述不当。
The witness misdescribed the suspect's car. 目击者对嫌疑人的车辆描述有误。
Synonyms: misrepresented, distorted, misstated
Antonyms: accurately described, correctly portrayed
词性: 动词
