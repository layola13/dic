nonrationally:/ˌnɒnˈræʃənəli/
| adv. |非理性地|Not based on reason or logic; irrationally.| He acted nonrationally, driven by pure emotion. 他非理性地行事，完全受情绪驱使。|
|例句：She nonrationally clung to the belief that he would return. 她非理性地坚信他会回来。|
|例句：He spent his money nonrationally, buying things he didn't need. 他非理性地花钱，买了很多不需要的东西。|
|近义词：irrationally, unreasonably, illogically|
|反义词：rationally, reasonably, logically|
|词性：副词|
