garbler:/ˈɡɑːrblər/ | n. | 模糊不清说话的人；扰乱者；加密器 | Someone who garbles speech or other communication; a device that distorts or scrambles signals to make them unintelligible. | The garbler made it difficult to understand the message. 这个含糊其辞的人使得信息难以理解。| synonyms: distorter, scrambler, mangler, muddler | antonyms: clarifier, interpreter, elucidator | noun

garble:/ˈɡɑːrbl(ə)l/ | vt. |  断章取义；歪曲；混淆；（使）变得模糊不清 | to reproduce (a message, sound, or transmission) in a confused and distorted way. |  He garbled the message so badly that no one could understand it. 他把信息歪曲得面目全非，以至于没有人能理解。| synonyms: distort, twist, misrepresent, mangle, muddle, confuse | antonyms: clarify, explain, elucidate | verb
