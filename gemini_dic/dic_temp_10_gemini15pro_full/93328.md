ill-treated: /ɪlˈtriːtɪd/
adjective
受虐待的，被虐待的
Having been treated badly or cruelly.
The ill-treated child was finally rescued from the abusive home.  这个受虐待的孩子终于从虐待的家中被救了出来。
The ill-treated dog was emaciated and covered in fleas.  这只受虐待的狗骨瘦如柴，身上爬满了跳蚤。

近义词: abused, mistreated, maltreated
反义词: well-treated, cherished, nurtured
词性: adjective
