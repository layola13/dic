incorrigibly:/ɪnˈkɔːrɪdʒəbli/
adjective
无可救药地，积习难改地
in a way that is impossible to correct or improve
He is incorrigibly lazy. 他懒得无可救药。
Synonyms: hopelessly, irredeemably, inveterately, unchangeably
Antonyms: corrigibly, remediably
词性: 副词
