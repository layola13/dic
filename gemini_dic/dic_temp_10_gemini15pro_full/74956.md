friarly:/ˈfraɪərli/
adjective
修士般的; 似修士的;
Like or characteristic of a friar.
He had a friarly appearance, with his simple robe and tonsured head.  他有着修士般的外表，穿着简单的长袍，剃着光头。
The old library had a friarly atmosphere of quiet study.  古老的图书馆有一种修士般的宁静学习氛围。
近义词: monastic, ascetic
反义词: worldly, secular
词性: adjective
词源: From "friar" (monk) + "-ly" (suffix forming adjectives).  来自“friar”（修士）+“-ly”（构成形容词的后缀）。
