macchiato:/ˌmɑːkiˈɑːtoʊ/ (US), /ˌmækɪˈɑːtəʊ/ (UK)| n. |玛奇朵|An espresso coffee drink topped with a small amount of foamed milk.|I ordered a macchiato with an extra shot of espresso. 我点了一杯加浓的玛奇朵。|近义词:cortado, espresso|反义词:latte, cappuccino|noun

macchiato:/ˌmɑːkiˈɑːtoʊ/ (US), /ˌmækɪˈɑːtəʊ/ (UK)| adj. |(咖啡)加少量奶泡的|Stained or marked with small spots.|She wore a macchiato-colored scarf. 她戴着一条玛奇朵色的围巾。|近义词:spotted, speckled|反义词:plain, solid|adjective
