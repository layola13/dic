alphamerically:/ˌælfəˈmɛrɪkli/ | adv. | 按字母顺序地 | Arranged in alphabetical order. |  The list of names was organized alphamerically. 名单上的名字是按字母顺序排列的。| The words are listed alphamerically. 这些单词按字母顺序排列。 | alphabetically | non-alphabetically | adverb

词源:  From alphabetic + -ally.  "Alphabetic" comes from "alphabet," which ultimately derives from the names of the first two letters of the Greek alphabet, "alpha" and "beta."
