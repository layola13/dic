outscores:/ˌaʊtˈskɔːrz/
verb
超过；胜过；得分高于
To score more points than (an opponent or another team).
The Lakers outscored the Celtics in the fourth quarter. 湖人队在第四节得分超过了凯尔特人队。
synonyms: surpasses, beats, defeats, triumphs over
antonyms: loses to, is defeated by
v.
