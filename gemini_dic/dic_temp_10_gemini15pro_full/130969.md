nonasthmatic: /ˌnɒnæsˈmætɪk/ (British) /ˌnɑːnæsˈmætɪk/ (American)| adj.|非哮喘的|Not relating to or suffering from asthma; not asthmatic.|She was diagnosed with a nonasthmatic respiratory condition.|近义词: non-asthmatic, healthy (in this context)|反义词: asthmatic|词性: adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix): Latin origin, meaning "not"
asthmatic: from Greek *āsthmatikós, from asthma, meaning "panting, short-drawn breath"
