imperforata:/ɪmˌpɜːrfəˈreɪtə/
adjective
无孔的，闭锁的
not open, closed, specifically referring to a natural opening that is abnormally closed.
The baby was born with an imperforate anus.  这个婴儿出生时肛门闭锁。
The hymen was imperforate. 处女膜是闭锁的。
Synonyms: closed, atretic, occluded
Antonyms: open, patent, perforated
词性: adjective
