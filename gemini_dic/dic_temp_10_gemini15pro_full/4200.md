airfreighted:/ˈɛərˌfreɪtɪd/| v. |空运的|Shipped or transported by airplane.  The goods were airfreighted to the customer. |
例句：The package was airfreighted from China. 包裹是从中国空运过来的。

例句：The flowers were airfreighted to ensure their freshness. 这些花是空运的，以确保它们的新鲜度。

近义词：air-shipped, flown, transported by air|反义词:sea-freighted, surface-mailed|词性:动词
