lithontriptic:/ˌlɪθənˈtrɪptɪk/
adjective
碎石的，溶石的
Capable of dissolving or breaking down stones, especially calculi in the kidneys or gallbladder.
The doctor prescribed a lithontriptic medication to help dissolve the kidney stones. 医生开了碎石药来帮助溶解肾结石。
Synonyms:antilithic
Antonyms:N/A
词性:adjective


lithontriptic:/ˌlɪθənˈtrɪptɪk/
noun
碎石剂
A medicine or agent that dissolves or breaks down stones, especially calculi in the kidneys or gallbladder.
The patient was given a lithontriptic to break up the stones in his bladder. 病人被给予碎石剂来分解膀胱里的结石。
Synonyms:antilithic
Antonyms:N/A
词性:noun
