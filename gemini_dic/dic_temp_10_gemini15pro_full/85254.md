harmlessly:/ˈhɑːrmləsli/| adv.|无害地，安全地|without causing any harm|The spider is large but harmlessly. 这只蜘蛛很大，但无害。|近义词：safely, innocuously|反义词：dangerously, harmfully|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From harm + -less + -ly.  "Harm" comes from Old English "hearm" meaning injury or grief.  The suffix "-less" indicates a lack of something, and "-ly" transforms the adjective "harmless" into an adverb.
