countertops:/ˈkaʊntərˌtɑːps/ | n. |檯面; 工作檯面 |A flat surface, typically found in kitchens and bathrooms, used for preparing food, placing items, or other activities. |The new countertops in the kitchen are made of granite. 厨房裡新的檯面是大理石做的。

|Synonyms: worktops, surfaces, benchtops|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "floor" in a contrasting sense) |noun|


countertop:/ˈkaʊntərˌtɑːp/ | adj. |檯面的; 放在檯面上的 |Designed to be placed on or used on a countertop. |We bought a countertop microwave oven. 我們買了一個檯面式微波爐。|Synonyms: table-top, benchtop|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "under-counter" in a contrasting sense) |adjective|

ETYMOLOGY->
From "counter" (meaning a table or surface used for transactions or work) + "top" (the uppermost surface of something).
