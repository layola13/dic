outcaroling: /ˌaʊtˈkærəlɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的颂歌; 以更优美的颂歌压倒
surpassing in caroling; excelling in singing carols
例句：The children were outcaroling each other in the town square, each trying to sing the loudest. 孩子们在市镇广场上互相以更响亮的颂歌压倒对方，每个人都试图唱得最大声。

例句：The choir from St. Michael's Church was renowned for outcaroling every other choir in the Christmas competition.  来自圣迈克尔教堂的唱诗班以在圣诞节比赛中以更优美的颂歌压倒其他所有唱诗班而闻名。

近义词：outsinging
反义词：being outsung

词性:v.
