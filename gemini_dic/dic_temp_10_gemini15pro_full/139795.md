octavos:/ɒkˈtɑːvəʊz/ /ɑːkˈteɪvoʊz/
noun
八开本（复数）
A size of book page resulting from folding a sheet of paper three times to create eight leaves.
The library has several rare books in octavos.  图书馆里有一些罕见的八开本书。
Octavos were a popular format for books in the 16th century. 16世纪，八开本是书籍的流行格式。
synonyms:  small books, pocket books
antonyms: folios, quartos
