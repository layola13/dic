assume:/əˈsjuːm/ (US) /əˈsjuːm/ (UK)| v. |假设；承担；呈现|To suppose to be the case, without proof; to take or begin to have (power or responsibility).|She assumed he was guilty. 她假定他有罪。|presume, suppose, posit|deny, reject|verb
assumer:/əˈsjuːmər/ (US) /əˈsjuːmər/ (UK)| n. |假定者；承担者|A person who assumes something.|The assumer of the risk will be compensated. 承担风险的人将得到补偿。|presumer, supposer|denier, rejector|noun
