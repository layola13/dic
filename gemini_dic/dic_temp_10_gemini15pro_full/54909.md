disposables:/dɪˈspoʊzəblz/
n.|一次性用品|Items designed to be thrown away after one use.
|We use a lot of disposables, like paper plates and plastic cutlery, for picnics.|一次性用品，例如纸盘和塑料餐具，我们野餐时经常使用。
|近义词：throwaways, expendables|反义词:reusables, durables
词性:名词

disposable:/dɪˈspoʊzəbl/
adj.|一次性的，可任意处理的|Designed to be thrown away after one use; available for use.|Disposable diapers are convenient but not environmentally friendly.|一次性尿布很方便，但不环保。
|近义词：expendable, single-use|反义词:reusable, permanent
词性:形容词
