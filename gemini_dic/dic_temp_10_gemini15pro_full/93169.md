ignition:/ɪɡˈnɪʃən/ | n. | 点火；点火装置；着火；燃烧；启动 | the process of something starting to burn or of making something start to burn | The car's ignition failed, leaving us stranded. 汽车的点火装置失灵了，让我们束手无策。| synonyms: firing, kindling, combustion | antonyms: extinguishing, quenching | noun

ignition:/ɪɡˈnɪʃən/ | n. | (figurative) the start of a process or activity | The discovery ignited a debate among scientists.  这个发现引发了科学家们的争论。 | synonyms: commencement, initiation, onset | antonyms: conclusion, termination, end | noun
