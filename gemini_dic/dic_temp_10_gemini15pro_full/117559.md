megacoulomb:/ˈmɛɡəkuːlɒm/ |n.| 兆库仑 |A unit of electric charge equal to one million coulombs.  It is a measure of the quantity of electricity.| A lightning strike can transfer several megacoulombs of charge.  一次闪电可以转移几兆库仑的电荷。|Synonyms: None (as it's a specific unit)|Antonyms: None (as it's a specific unit)|noun
Etymology: mega- (from Greek μέγας megas, meaning "great") + coulomb (named after French physicist Charles-Augustin de Coulomb)
