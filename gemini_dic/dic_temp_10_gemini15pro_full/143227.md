outcutting:/ˌaʊtˈkʌtɪŋ/
|n.| 外切; 超越切割; 开凿; 挖掘|
|中文翻译：外切，超越切割，开凿，挖掘（主要指比周围部分更深或更低的切割或挖掘）|
|英文解析：The act of cutting something out, especially a deeper or lower cut than the surrounding area.  Often refers to excavation or the creation of a channel.|
|例句：The outcutting of the new drainage ditch prevented flooding in the field. 开凿新的排水沟防止了田地被淹。|

|例句：The archaeologist carefully examined the outcutting in the rock face, believing it to be an ancient inscription.  考古学家仔细检查了岩壁上的凿痕，认为这是一个古老的铭文。|
|近义词：excavation, quarrying, channeling, digging, cutting|
|反义词：filling, depositing, backfilling|
|词性：名词|
