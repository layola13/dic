autos:/ˈɔːtoʊz/
n.|汽车（尤指旧式汽车）|A car, especially one that is old-fashioned.|He collects vintage autos.|近义词: cars, automobiles|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 transportation alternatives like bikes, buses)|词性: 名词

Etymology: Shortening of automobile, from French, from auto- + mobile 'movable'.
