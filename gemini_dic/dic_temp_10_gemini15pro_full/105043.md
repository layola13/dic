kerana:/kəˈrɑːnə/
conj.
because, because of, since, due to, as
This word is primarily used in Malay and Indonesian, meaning "because".
Saya tidak pergi ke sekolah kerana saya sakit. (I didn't go to school because I was sick.)
近义词: sebab, oleh sebab
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but contrasting conjunctions like "although" or "but" could be used)
词性: conjunction
