deep-fry: /ˌdiːpˈfraɪ/ | v. |油炸 | To cook food by submerging it in hot oil. | Example: I decided to deep-fry the chicken for dinner. 我决定晚餐炸鸡。

Example:  They deep-fried the fish until it was golden brown. 他们把鱼炸至金黄色。 | 近义词: fry, French fry | 反义词: bake, boil, steam | 词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From *deep* + *fry*.  *Deep* meaning fully submerged and *fry* meaning to cook in hot fat.  First recorded use in the early 20th century.
