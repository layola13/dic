exerciser:/ˈɛksərˌsaɪzər/| n.|锻炼器; 运动器; 练习者; 锻炼者|A person or device that exercises something, typically a part of the body.|She uses an exerciser to strengthen her leg muscles. 她使用锻炼器来增强腿部肌肉。|近义词：trainer, workout machine, apparatus|反义词:couch potato, idler|词性:noun
Etymology: From exercise + -er (agent suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *exercēre*.
