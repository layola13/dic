fritter:/ˈfrɪtər/ | v. | 浪费，挥霍 | to waste time, money, or effort on unimportant things | He frittered away his inheritance on fast cars and gambling. 他把遗产浪费在跑车和赌博上。|
fritter:/ˈfrɪtər/ | v. | 切碎，磨碎 | to cut or break something into small pieces |  She frittered the onion for the omelet. 她把洋葱切碎做煎蛋卷。|
fritter:/ˈfrɪtər/ | n. | 油炸馅饼 | a small quantity of fried batter often containing fruit or meat | Apple fritters are a popular dessert. 苹果油炸饼是一种很受欢迎的甜点。 |
fritter:/ˈfrɪtər/ | n. | 碎屑，碎片 | a small piece or amount of something |  The glass shattered into fritters. 玻璃碎成了碎片。 |

近义词: squander, waste, dissipate, fragment (v.); scrap, piece, crumb (n.)
反义词: save, conserve, hoard (v.); whole (n.)
词性: verb, noun

词源: From Old French *fricter "to rub, chafe," from Vulgar Latin *fricticare, frequentative of Latin fricare "to rub."
