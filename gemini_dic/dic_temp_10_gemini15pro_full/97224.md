inflation: /ɪnˈfleɪʃən/ (US), /ɪnˈfleɪʃn/ (UK)| n. |通貨膨脹|A general increase in prices and fall in the purchasing value of money.| Example 1: The country is struggling with high inflation.  这个国家正在与高通胀作斗争。

Example 2: Inflation is eroding the value of our savings. 通货膨胀正在侵蚀我们储蓄的价值。|Synonyms: price rise, price hike, escalation, increase|Antonyms: deflation, disinflation|noun
