historicist: /hɪˈstɔːrɪsɪst/ | n. adj. | 历史主义者；历史相对主义者 |  Someone who believes that all phenomena, including knowledge and values, are historically conditioned and that history follows certain laws or patterns.  As an adjective, it describes something related to this belief. |  He's a historicist who believes that all truth is relative to its time. 他是一位历史主义者，认为所有真理都是与其时代相关的。| synonyms: relativist, contextualist | antonyms: absolutist, universalist | noun, adjective

historicist: /hɪˈstɔːrɪsɪst/ | adj. | 历史主义的；历史相对主义的 | Relating to the belief that all phenomena are historically conditioned. | The historicist approach to literature considers the social and political context of the work. 历史主义的文学研究方法会考虑作品的社会和政治背景。| synonyms: contextual, historical | antonyms: ahistorical, universal | adjective
