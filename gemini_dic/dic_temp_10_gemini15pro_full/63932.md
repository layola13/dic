epipodite:/ˈɛpɪpəˌdaɪt/| n. | 外肢 |A lateral process or appendage on the protopodite of a crustacean limb, often functioning as a gill. |The epipodite of the crab is feathery and aids in respiration. 蟹的外肢呈羽毛状，有助于呼吸。|Near synonyms: exopodite, branchia|Antonym: endopodite|noun
