iacocca:/ˌaɪəˈkoʊkə/
n.
李·艾柯卡 (人名)
Lee Iacocca, an American automobile executive known for his role in saving Chrysler Corporation from bankruptcy in the late 1970s. He also led the development of the Ford Mustang and the Ford Pinto.
