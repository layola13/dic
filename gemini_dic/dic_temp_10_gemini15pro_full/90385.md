howel:/ˈhaʊəl/| n.|哀号，号叫|A long, mournful cry or wail, typically of a dog or wolf.|The mournful howel of a distant dog echoed through the night. 远处传来一阵狗的哀号，在夜里回荡。|近义词：wail, howl, cry, moan|反义词:whisper, murmur|词性:名词
