homewards:/ˈhəʊmwədz/ (British), /ˈhoʊmwərdz/ (American)| adv.|回家地，向家地|Towards home.  Indicates movement in the direction of home.|She started homewards as the sun began to set. 她开始回家了，因为太阳开始落山了。|synonyms: homeward, toward home, for home|antonyms: away from home|adverb
