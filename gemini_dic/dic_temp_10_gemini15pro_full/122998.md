monarchian:/məˈnɑːrkiən/
| n. adj.|君主主义者；君主制的|A believer in monarchianism; of or relating to monarchianism.|例句：The monarchian believed in the sole rule of God the Father.  这位君主主义者相信圣父的唯一统治。|例句：He held a monarchian view of the early church. 他对早期教会持君主主义的观点。|近义词：royalist, monarchist|反义词：republican, democrat|词性:名词, 形容词
monarchianism:/məˈnɑːrkiənɪzəm/
| n.|君主主义|A theological doctrine that emphasizes the sole sovereignty of God the Father, rejecting the doctrine of the Trinity or subordination of the Son to the Father.|例句：Monarchianism was a controversial doctrine in the early church. 君主主义在早期教会是一个有争议的教义。|例句：Some scholars argue that early Christianity had monarchian tendencies. 一些学者认为早期基督教有君主主义的倾向。|近义词：unitarianism|反义词：trinitarianism|词性:名词
