consignification:/kənˌsɪɡnɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 共同意义，共同指称 | The consignification of these symbols creates a deeper meaning within the artwork. 这些符号的共同意义在艺术作品中创造了更深层的含义。 |  The artist used color and shape in consignification to represent life and death. 艺术家运用颜色和形状的共同指称来表现生与死。 |近义词：connotation, implication, significance |反义词：denotation |词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin consignificatio, from consignificare "to signify together," from com- "together" + significare "to signify."
