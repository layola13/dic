clinic:/ˈklɪnɪk/| n.|诊所，门诊部|A place where people go to receive medical treatment, especially one that is not part of a hospital.| I went to the clinic to get a flu shot. 我去诊所打了流感疫苗。| synonyms: medical center, health center, doctor's office | antonyms: hospital (although sometimes used interchangeably in casual conversation) | noun


clinic:/ˈklɪnɪk/| n.|（尤指大学的）临床教学，临床实习|A session or class for practical instruction and examination of patients.| The medical students attended a clinic on dermatology. 医学生参加了皮肤病学的临床实习。| synonyms: practicum, workshop| antonyms: theory, lecture | noun
