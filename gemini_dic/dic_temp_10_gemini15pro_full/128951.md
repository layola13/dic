ninjas:/ˈnɪndʒəz/ | n. |忍者|A member of a feudal Japanese society of mercenary agents highly skilled in martial arts and stealth, who were hired for covert purposes ranging from espionage to sabotage and assassination.|The ninjas silently infiltrated the enemy compound. 忍者悄无声息地潜入了敌人的营地。|近义词:assassin, spy, mercenary, warrior|反义词:knight, samurai (sometimes), law enforcement|词性:noun
