disorderliness:/dɪsˈɔːrdərlɪnɪs/
n.
混乱，无秩序
lack of order or tidiness; untidiness.
The disorderliness of his room reflected his state of mind. 他房间的凌乱反映了他的精神状态。
近义词: chaos, mess, disarray, confusion, untidiness
反义词: order, tidiness, neatness, organization
词性: 名词

disorderly:/dɪsˈɔːrdərli/
adj.
混乱的，无秩序的
lacking order or tidiness; untidy.
The police arrested the disorderly fans after the game. 警方在比赛结束后逮捕了那些行为不检点的球迷。
近义词: chaotic, messy, disarrayed, confused, untidy
反义词: orderly, tidy, neat, organized
词性: 形容词
