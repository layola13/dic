meatily:/ˈmiːtɪli/| adv.|adv. 肉感地; 丰满地; 实质性地|In a meaty way; substantially; dealing with issues in a thorough and satisfying way.  Having substance or weight; like meat.|He wrote a meatily argued essay. 他写了一篇论证充分的论文。|近义词：substantially, solidly, heartily|反义词：superficially, slightly, insubstantially|词性:副词

词源: From meat + -ily.  The suffix "-ily" forms adverbs from adjectives, often implying "in a ... manner".  "Meat" itself comes from Old English *mete, meaning "food" generally.
