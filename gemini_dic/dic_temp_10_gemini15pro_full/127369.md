ne'er-do-well:/ˌnerˈduːˌwel/
noun
游手好闲的人；败家子；没出息的人
a person who is lazy and irresponsible and will never be successful
例句：He was known as the town ne'er-do-well, always getting into trouble. 他是镇上出了名的游手好闲的人，总是惹麻烦。

例句：Her family had always considered him a ne'er-do-well, but he proved them wrong by starting a successful business. 她的家人一直认为他是个没出息的人，但他创办了一家成功的企业，证明他们错了。

近义词：good-for-nothing, wastrel, loafer, idler, slacker
反义词：go-getter, achiever, hard worker


词性: noun
