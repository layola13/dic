engraving's:/ɪnˈɡreɪvɪŋz/
n.
雕刻品；雕刻艺术
The process or art of cutting or carving a design on a hard surface, especially so as to make a print.  The design or figure thus cut.  A print made from an engraved plate.
I admired the delicate engraving's on the silver locket. 我欣赏银质小盒子上精致的雕刻。
近义词：carving, etching, inscription
反义词：-
词性: 名词
