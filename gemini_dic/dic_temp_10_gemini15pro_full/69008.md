fatted:/ˈfætɪd/ | adj. | 肥育的，养肥的 | Made fat by special feeding, typically for slaughter. | The farmer brought a fatted calf to the feast. 农民带了一头肥育的小牛来赴宴。| The fatted goose was ready for Christmas dinner.  养肥的鹅已准备好圣诞晚餐。 | 近义词：plump, fleshy, well-fed | 反义词：lean, thin, emaciated | 词性: 形容词

词源: From the verb "fatten," which comes from Old English "fættan," meaning "to make fat," from "fæt" (fat).
