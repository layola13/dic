nematode:/ˈnɛməˌtoʊd/ (US), /ˈnɛmətəʊd/ (UK)| n. |线虫，蛔虫|A worm of the large phylum Nematoda, such as a roundworm or threadworm.|例句：The soil was infested with nematodes. 土壤里充满了线虫。

例句：Many nematodes are parasites of plants and animals. 许多线虫是动植物的寄生虫。|近义词：roundworm, threadworm|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek νῆμα (nêma, “thread”) + -ode, equivalent to nema- +‎ -tode.  The suffix -ode means "resembling" or "having the form of".
