endosymbiosis:/ˌɛndoʊˌsɪmbaɪˈoʊsɪs/| n. |内共生|A symbiotic relationship where one organism lives inside the other, typically to the advantage of both.|Example: The mitochondria in our cells are thought to have originated through endosymbiosis.  我们的细胞中的线粒体被认为是起源于内共生。|Example:  Endosymbiosis is a key concept in understanding the evolution of eukaryotic cells. 内共生是理解真核细胞进化的关键概念。|近义词：symbiosis|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "exosymbiosis" though not commonly used)|词性:noun
