ingrainedly:/ɪnˈɡreɪndɪdli/
adverb
根深蒂固地，牢固地
firmly fixed or established; deeply rooted
His distrust of authority was ingrainedly. 他对权威的不信任根深蒂固。
近义词: deeply, firmly, fixedly, ineradicably
反义词: superficially, shallowly
词性: adverb
词源: From "ingrained" + "-ly". "Ingrained" comes from "ingrain," which means to dye in the grain, thus making the color permanent.  It's derived from "in-" (in) + "grain" (the texture or fiber of something).
