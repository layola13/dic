humoral:/ˈhjuːmərəl/ |adj.|体液的；由体液引起的；幽默的|Relating to the body fluids or humors.  Pertaining to the theory of humors (historical medical theory).|例句：The doctor explained that the disease was caused by a humoral imbalance. 医生解释说这种疾病是由体液失衡引起的。

例句：Humoral immunity is an important part of the body's defense system. 体液免疫是人体防御系统的重要组成部分。|近义词：fluid, liquid,  humorous (in the archaic sense)|反义词: (No direct antonym, depends on context) cellular (when referring to immunity)|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *humōrālis, from Latin humor (“moisture, liquid”), from PIE *sekʷ- (“to pour out, run”). Cognate with humor.  The sense relating to the historical theory of the four humors comes from the belief that these fluids determined a person's temperament.
