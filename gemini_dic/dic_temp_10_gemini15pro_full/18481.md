benching:/ˈbentʃɪŋ/| n.|把…列入后备队员名单; 冷落; 搁置; 替补席|The act of putting someone on a bench, either literally (as in sports) or figuratively (as in a relationship).  It can also refer to postponing or setting aside a task or project.|He was benched for the entire game. 他整场比赛都被列为后备队员。|Synonyms: sidelining, shelving, postponing, freezing out|Antonyms: activating, starting, prioritizing|Noun
