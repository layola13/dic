mewling:/ˈmjuːlɪŋ/ | adj.  n. |  发出微弱的呜咽声；呜咽；（尤指婴儿）哭闹 | making a feeble, wailing cry; whimpering; (especially of a baby) crying weakly. |  The mewling kittens huddled together for warmth.  发出微弱呜咽声的小猫挤在一起取暖。| 近义词: whimpering, crying, wailing | 反义词:  silent, quiet | 词性: adjective, noun
Etymology: From Middle English *meulen, from Old French miauler, from Late Latin miulāre, from Latin miulāre, from the sound cats make.
