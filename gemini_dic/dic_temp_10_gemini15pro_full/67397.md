exterminate: /ɪkˈstɜːmɪneɪt/ /ɪkˈstɝːmɪneɪt/
verb
消灭，根除
To destroy or kill completely, especially a group of people or animals.
The Nazis tried to exterminate the Jews during World War II.  纳粹在二战期间试图灭绝犹太人。

Pesticides are used to exterminate insects.  杀虫剂被用来消灭昆虫。

近义词: annihilate, eradicate, wipe out, destroy
反义词: preserve, protect, conserve
词性: verb
