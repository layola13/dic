inflictive: /ɪnˈflɪktɪv/
| adj. |施加的，导致的，带来…的 |Tending to inflict; inflicting.  Relating to or characterized by infliction. | The inflictive nature of the punishment caused outrage. 这种惩罚的施加性质引起了公愤。|近义词：imposing, inflicting, causing |反义词: soothing, alleviating |词性:形容词
词源: From Latin inflictus, past participle of infligere "to strike or dash against, inflict," from in- "into, in, on, upon" + fligere "to strike."
