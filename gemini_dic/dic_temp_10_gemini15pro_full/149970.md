palmated:/ˈpælmeɪtɪd/| adj.|掌状的，蹼状的|Resembling a hand with the fingers spread; having lobes radiating from a common point.|例句：The maple leaf is a well-known example of a palmated leaf. 枫叶是掌状叶的一个著名例子。

例句：The duck has palmated feet, which help it swim. 鸭子有蹼状的脚，这有助于它游泳。|近义词：lobed, webbed|反义词:pinnate, entire|词性:adjective
词源:From Latin *palmatus, from palma "palm of the hand."
