inflated: /ɪnˈfleɪtɪd/
adjective
膨胀的；夸大的；通货膨胀的
Filled with air or gas; Exaggerated; Subject to or affected by economic inflation.
例句：The inflated balloon floated to the ceiling. 膨胀的气球飘到了天花板上。

例句：He has an inflated sense of his own importance. 他自视过高。

近义词：expanded, swollen, exaggerated, overblown, pompous
反义词：deflated, reduced, modest, understated

词性: adjective
