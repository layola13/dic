anythingarian:/ˌɛnɪθɪŋˈɛəriən/ | n. |  什么都吃的人;不拘泥于任何特定饮食习惯的人 | Someone who eats anything; a person who is not restricted to any particular diet. |  He's an anythingarian; he'll eat meat, vegetables, anything you put in front of him. 他是个什么都吃的人；他会吃肉、蔬菜，任何你放在他面前的东西。|  She's not a vegetarian or a vegan, she's an anythingarian. 她不是素食主义者也不是纯素食主义者，她是什么都吃的人。| synonym: omnivore | antonym: picky eater | noun
