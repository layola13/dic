bishoping:/ˈbɪʃəpɪŋ/
noun
主教任命；主教职务；主教的职能
The act of appointing or acting as a bishop; the office or function of a bishop.
The bishoping of new priests is a solemn occasion.  新神父的主教任命是一个庄严的时刻。
ordination, consecration, appointment
removal, dismissal
