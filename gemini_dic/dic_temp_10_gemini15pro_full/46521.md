dacoity: /dəˈkɔɪti/ | n. | 抢劫；盗匪行为 | An act of robbery; banditry.  Specifically, in India, organized robbery, usually committed by a band of armed dacoits. |  The dacoity occurred in broad daylight, shocking the villagers.  光天化日之下发生的抢劫事件震惊了村民们。|  The gang was notorious for its dacoities throughout the region.  该团伙因在该地区的抢劫行为而臭名昭著。 | synonyms: robbery, banditry, brigandage, plundering | antonyms:  donation, charity | noun
