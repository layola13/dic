critically:/ˈkrɪtɪkli/| adv.|批判地；严重地；危急地|In a way that involves careful analysis and judgment; seriously; in a dangerous condition.|He looked critically at the report. 他批判性地看了这份报告。 The patient is critically ill. 病人病危。|近义词:analytically, seriously, gravely|反义词:uncritically, mildly, superficially|词性:副词
词源: From Late Latin *criticalis, from Latin criticus (“critical, able to judge”), from Ancient Greek κριτικός (kritikós, “of or for judging”), from κριτής (kritḗs, “judge”), from κρίνω (krínō, “I separate, judge, decide”).
