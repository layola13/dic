heliostat:/ˈhiːliəˌstæt/ | n. |日光反射镜，定日镜 | A device, usually consisting of a movable mirror, that reflects sunlight in a fixed direction, typically onto a target such as a solar furnace or a power tower. | The heliostat field focused the sun's rays onto the central receiver. 定日镜场将太阳光聚焦到中央接收器上。

The heliostat tracked the sun's movement across the sky. 定日镜跟踪太阳在天空中移动的轨迹。 | synonyms: solar reflector, mirror, | antonyms: N/A | noun
