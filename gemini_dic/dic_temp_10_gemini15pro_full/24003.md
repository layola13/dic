breathlessly:/ˈbreθləsli/ | adv. | 气喘吁吁地，屏息地 | In a way that involves difficulty breathing, typically after exertion or excitement. | She ran breathlessly to the finish line. 她气喘吁吁地跑到终点线。|  He waited breathlessly for her response. 他屏息以待她的回复。 | 近义词: pantingly, gaspingly | 反义词: calmly, quietly | 词性: 副词

词源: From breath + -less + -ly.  "Breath" comes from Old English "bræth," related to Old High German "brādam." "-less" is a suffix meaning "without," from Old English "-lēas." "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs, from Old English "-lice."
