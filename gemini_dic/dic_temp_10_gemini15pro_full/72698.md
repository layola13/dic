focalizes:/ˈfoʊkəlaɪzɪz/
verb
集中；聚焦
To concentrate or focus something, often a narrative or point of view, on a particular subject or perspective.
The film focalizes the story on the experiences of a single soldier. 这部电影将故事集中在一个士兵的经历上。
The author focalizes the narrative through the eyes of the child. 作者通过孩子的视角来聚焦叙事。
focuses, concentrates, centers
scatters, diffuses
v.
