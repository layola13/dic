blitzkrieged:/ˈblɪtskriːɡd/
verb
闪电战;以闪电战攻击
attacked suddenly and violently, like a blitzkrieg.
Poland was blitzkrieged by Germany in 1939.  波兰在1939年遭到了德国的闪电战。
The small country was blitzkrieged by its larger neighbor.  这个小国被其强大的邻国以闪电战攻击。
overwhelmed, overrun
defended, protected
v.
