paralympics:/ˌpærəˈlɪmpɪks/ | n. | 残奥会 |  An international sporting event for athletes with disabilities, held after the Olympic Games in the same host city. | The Paralympics showcase the incredible strength and determination of athletes with disabilities. 残奥会展示了残疾运动员令人难以置信的力量和决心。 |  The 2020 Tokyo Paralympics were postponed due to the pandemic. 2020年东京残奥会因疫情而推迟。 | 近义词: Paralympic Games | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From "para" (beside or alongside) + "Olympics."  The original name, "Paraplegic Olympics," referred specifically to athletes with spinal cord injuries.  Later, the scope broadened to include other disabilities, and the name was shortened to "Paralympics."
