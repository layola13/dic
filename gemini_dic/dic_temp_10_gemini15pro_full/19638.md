bikes:/baɪks/ | n. |自行车；摩托车|A vehicle with two wheels, powered by pedals or a motor. |例句：I ride my bike to work every day. 我每天骑自行车上班。

例句：He bought a new mountain bike. 他买了一辆新的山地自行车。|近义词：cycles, bicycles, motorcycles (if motorized) |反义词: N/A |词性:noun


词源: From bike, a shortened form of bicycle, from bi- "two" + cycle "wheel."
