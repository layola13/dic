oversmoked:/ˌoʊvərˈsmoʊkt/
adjective
熏过头的，烟熏味过重的
exposed to too much smoke, especially in cooking, resulting in an unpleasant taste or smell.
The salmon was oversmoked and tasted bitter.  这条鲑鱼烟熏过量，味道发苦。
oversmoked ribs  烟熏过头的肋排
synonyms:burnt, charred, overcooked
antonyms:undersmoked, raw
词性:形容词
