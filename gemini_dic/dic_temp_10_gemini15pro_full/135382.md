nonperiodical:/ˌnɒnˌpɪəˈrɪɒdɪkəl/ | adj. | 非期刊的；不定期出版的 | Not appearing or published at regular intervals. | This is a nonperiodical publication, released only when new research is available. 这是一份非期刊出版物，只有在新研究成果出来时才会发行。| The conference proceedings are nonperiodical. 会议记录不是定期出版的。 |  近义词：irregular, sporadic, occasional | 反义词：periodical, regular | 词性：形容词

Etymology:
non- + periodical
non-: prefix meaning "not"
periodical: from Late Latin periodicus, from Latin periodus "period"
