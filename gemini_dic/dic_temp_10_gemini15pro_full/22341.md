bonnyclabber:/ˈbɑːniˌklæbər/
n.|凝乳，酸牛奶|Thick, sour milk; clabber.|The bonnyclabber was thick enough to cut with a knife. 凝乳很稠，可以用刀切开。|Synonyms: clabber, curdled milk, sour milk|Antonyms: fresh milk|Noun


Etymology:
From Scottish Gaelic *bainne clabair*, from bainne ("milk") + clabar ("mud, mire").  The "mire" part refers to the thickness of the clabbered milk.
