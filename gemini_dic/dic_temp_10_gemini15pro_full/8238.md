anti-ideological:/ˌæntiˌaɪdiəˈlɒdʒɪkl/ /ˌæntiˌaɪdiəˈlɑːdʒɪkl/
adjective
反意识形态的; 非意识形态的
Opposed to or lacking a strong ideology.
He took a rather anti-ideological stance, preferring pragmatism. 他采取了一种相当反意识形态的立场，更喜欢实用主义。
The organization promotes an anti-ideological approach to problem-solving. 该组织提倡用非意识形态的方法来解决问题。
近义词: pragmatic, non-ideological, practical
反义词: ideological, doctrinaire, dogmatic
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to. From Greek _anti_.
ideological (adjective): relating to or concerned with ideology. From _ideology_ (from French _idéologie_).
