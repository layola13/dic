finger-like:/ˈfɪŋɡərˌlaɪk/| adj.|手指状的，指状的|Resembling a finger in shape, often long and slender.|The plant has finger-like leaves.  这种植物有指状的叶子。|The robot has a finger-like gripper.  这个机器人有一个指状的抓取器。|similar, digit-like, tentacle-like|dissimilar, unlike, shapeless|adjective

Etymology:
From "finger" + "-like". "Finger" comes from Old English "finger", from Proto-Germanic "*fingraz". "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of", from Old English "-lic".
