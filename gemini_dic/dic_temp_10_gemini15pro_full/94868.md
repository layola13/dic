ETYMOLOGY->
From the phrase "in any event," meaning "in any case" or "in any circumstance."  The phrase itself is straightforward, combining "in" (preposition indicating location or condition), "any" (determiner indicating one or some indiscriminately of whatever kind), and "event" (noun referring to something that happens or is regarded as happening).
