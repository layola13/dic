noninoculative:/ˌnɒnɪˈnɒkjʊlətɪv/
adjective
非接种的
Not involving or requiring inoculation; not introduced by inoculation.
Noninoculative methods of disease transmission include direct contact and airborne spread. 非接种性疾病传播方式包括直接接触和空气传播。
Noninoculative transmission of plant viruses can occur through mechanical means like pruning shears. 植物病毒的非接种性传播可以通过机械方式发生，例如修枝剪。
近义词: noninfectious, abiotic
反义词: inoculative, infectious
词性: adjective

词源:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "inoculative" (from "inoculate," from Latin "inoculatus," past participle of "inoculare" meaning "to engraft," from "in-" (meaning "in, on") + "oculus" (meaning "eye, bud"))
