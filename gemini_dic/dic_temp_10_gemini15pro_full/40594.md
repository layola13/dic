convalescents:/ˌkɒnvəˈlesənts/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈlesənts/| n.| 恢复期病人；康复者|People recovering from illness or injury are called convalescents.  那些正在从疾病或受伤中恢复的人被称为康复期病人。|The hospital has a special wing for convalescents. 这家医院有一个专门收治康复期病人的病房。|近义词：recovering patients, recuperating individuals|反义词: sick people, patients|词性:noun


convalescent:/ˌkɒnvəˈlesənt/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈlesənts/| adj.| 恢复期的；康复中的|She is convalescent after a long illness. 她久病之后正在康复中。|He spent a month in a convalescent home. 他在一个康复疗养院待了一个月。|近义词：recovering, recuperating|反义词: sick, ill|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *convalescentem, present participle of convalescere ("to regain health"), from com- ("with, together") + valescere ("to grow strong").
