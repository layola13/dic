anthracomancy:/ˈænθrəkəmænsi/
n.
占卜(通过检查燃烧的煤炭)
divination by examining burning coals.
The ancient Greeks sometimes practiced anthracomancy to predict the future. 古希腊人有时会练习炭卜来预测未来。
Anthracomancy was a popular form of divination in some cultures. 在某些文化中，炭卜是一种流行的占卜形式。
近义词：divination, fortune-telling
反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially "scientific prediction")
词性: noun
