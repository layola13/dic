nonsupporters:/ˌnɒn.səˈpɔː.təz/ | n. | 非支持者，不支持者 | People who do not support a particular person, group, or cause. | Example: The nonsupporters of the new policy voiced their concerns at the meeting.  新的政策的非支持者在会议上表达了他们的担忧。| Example: The politician's nonsupporters launched a campaign against him.  这位政治家的非支持者发起了一场反对他的运动。| Synonym: opponents, detractors, dissenters | Antonym: supporters, backers, advocates | noun
