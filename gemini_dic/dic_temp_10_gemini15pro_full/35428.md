co-owners:/ˈkəʊˌəʊnərz/ (UK) /ˈkoʊˌoʊnərz/ (US)| n. |共同所有者，共同业主|People or entities who own something together. |例句：The co-owners of the property decided to sell it. 这处房产的共同所有者决定将其出售。

例句：My brother and I are co-owners of a small business. 我和我哥哥是一家小企业的共同所有者。

近义词：joint owners, shared owners|反义词:sole owner, sole proprietor|词性:noun


co-owner:/ˈkəʊˌəʊnə(r)/ (UK) /ˈkoʊˌoʊnər/ (US)| n. |共同所有者，共同业主|One of two or more people who own something together. |例句：Each co-owner is responsible for their share of the expenses.  每位共同所有者都有责任支付他们那部分的费用。

例句：She became a co-owner of the restaurant after investing a substantial amount of money. 在投资了一大笔钱后，她成为了这家餐厅的共同所有者。
