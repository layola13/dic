groundward:/ˈɡraʊndwərd/ | adj. adv.| 向地面；朝下 | Towards the ground; downwards. | The apple fell groundward from the tree. 苹果从树上掉落到地面。| The kite spiralled groundward after the string broke.  线断了之后，风筝盘旋着落向地面。 | 近义词: downward, earthward | 反义词: upward, skyward | 词性: adjective, adverb

词源: From "ground" + "-ward" (suffix indicating direction).  "-ward" 源自古英语 "-weard"，表示“朝向”。
