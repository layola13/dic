individuality:/ˌɪndɪˌvɪdʒuˈælɪti/| n. |个性，特质，独特|The quality or character of a particular person or thing that distinguishes them from others of the same kind, especially when strongly marked.|She expresses her individuality through her unique style of dress. 她通过独特的着装风格展现自己的个性。|synonyms: uniqueness, distinctiveness, originality, singularity, peculiarity |antonyms: conformity, sameness, uniformity |noun
