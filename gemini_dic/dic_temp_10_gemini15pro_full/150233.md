pandemonic:/ˌpændɪˈmɒnɪk/ (US) /ˌpænˈdɛmənɪk/ (UK)| adj.|Pandemonic; 混乱的；极其吵闹的；狂暴的|Relating to or resembling pandemonium; wild and noisy. Characterized by widespread chaos and disorder, often suggestive of demonic activity.|The pandemonic scene at the concert was overwhelming. 音乐会现场一片混乱，让人不知所措。|The Black Friday sales created a pandemonic rush at the store entrance. 黑色星期五的促销活动在商店入口处引发了一场混乱的抢购。|Synonyms: chaotic, tumultuous, frenzied, unruly, riotous, boisterous|Antonyms: peaceful, calm, tranquil, orderly, serene|adjective

pandemonium:/ˌpændɪˈməʊniəm/ (UK) /ˌpændəˈmoʊniəm/ (US)| n.|Pandemonium; 混乱；喧嚣；骚动|Wild and noisy disorder or confusion; uproar.|Pandemonium broke out when the fire alarm sounded. 火警响起时，现场一片混乱。|The sudden power outage caused pandemonium in the city. 突然停电导致城市陷入混乱。|Synonyms: chaos, bedlam, mayhem, uproar, tumult, anarchy|Antonyms: order, peace, tranquility, calm, quiet|noun
