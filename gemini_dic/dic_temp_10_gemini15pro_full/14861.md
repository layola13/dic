axonometry:/ækˌsɒˈnɒmɪtri/
n.
轴测图；轴测法
A method of projection in which a three-dimensional object is represented by a drawing having all lines parallel to three axes intersecting at oblique angles.
The architect used axonometry to create a clear representation of the building's complex structure.  这位建筑师使用轴测法清晰地展现了建筑复杂的结构。
isometric projection, orthographic projection
perspective projection
noun
