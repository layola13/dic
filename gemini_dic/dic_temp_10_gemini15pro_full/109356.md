lenin: /ˈlɛnɪn/ | n. | 列宁 | Vladimir Ilyich Ulyanov, better known by his alias Lenin, was a Russian revolutionary, politician, and political theorist. He served as the first and founding head of government of Soviet Russia from 1917 to 1924 and of the Soviet Union from 1922 to 1924. | Lenin led the Bolshevik Revolution in October 1917. 列宁领导了1917年10月的布尔什维克革命。| Lenin's ideas formed the basis of Leninism. 列宁的思想构成了列宁主义的基础。 | synonym:  leader, revolutionary | antonym:  tsar, monarch | proper noun
