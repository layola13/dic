nonfatty:/nɒnˈfæti/ (US) /nɒnˈfætɪ/ (UK)| adj.|无脂的，脱脂的|Containing no fat or very little fat.  Describes food products that have had the fat removed or significantly reduced. |I prefer nonfatty yogurt for breakfast. 我早餐喜欢吃脱脂酸奶。|Synonyms: fat-free, low-fat, lean|Antonyms: fatty, full-fat|adjective
