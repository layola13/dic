dialects:/ˈdaɪəlekts/ (US), /ˈdaɪəlekts/ (UK)| n.|方言，土语|A particular form of a language which is peculiar to a specific region or social group.|She spoke in a broad Yorkshire dialect. 她操着浓重的约克郡方言。|synonyms: vernacular, patois, idiom, accent|antonyms: standard language, official language|noun
