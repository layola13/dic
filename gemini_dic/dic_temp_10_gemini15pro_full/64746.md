escot:/ˈɛskɒt/| n.| 税金，征税，账单；尤其指苏格兰法律中房客缴纳的地租或税金|A payment, especially a tenant's rent or tax under Scottish law.|The escot was due at the end of the month. 月底要缴纳地租。|近义词：tax, levy, assessment, rent|反义词: exemption|词性:名词

Etymology: From Old North French *escot, from Frankish *skot "tax, contribution," from *skot- "to shoot, throw," of uncertain origin. The word is related to Old English sceot "payment, contribution," and "shot" (as in "paying one's shot").
