feudist:/ˈfjuːdɪst/| n. |封建领主; 世仇者; 宿敌; |A person involved in a feud, especially one engaged in a long-standing and bitter quarrel or rivalry, often involving violence between families or clans. |The two families were bitter feudist, their animosity stretching back generations. 这两个家族是宿敌，他们的仇恨可以追溯到几代人以前。

The feudist refused to reconcile with his rivals.  这个世仇者拒绝与他的对手和解。|近义词：rival, enemy, antagonist, adversary |反义词:ally, friend, supporter|noun
