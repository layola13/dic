mutt:/mʌt/| n. |杂种狗，笨蛋，傻瓜|A dog of mixed breed, especially a mongrel.  Informally, can also refer to a foolish or inept person.|例句：That mutt of mine ate my homework! 我那只笨狗吃了我的作业！

例句：He called me a mutt, but I didn't care. 他叫我杂种，但我不在乎。|近义词：mongrel, cur, crossbreed, fool, idiot |反义词:purebred (for dog), genius (for person) |词性:noun
