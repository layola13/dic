antipyretic:/ˌæntipaɪˈrɛtɪk/
adjective
退热的；解热的
Reduces fever; antifebrile.
Example: Aspirin is an antipyretic medication. 阿司匹林是一种退烧药。
Synonym: antifebrile, febrifuge
Antonym: pyrogenic
adjective

antipyretic:/ˌæntipaɪˈrɛtɪk/
noun
退热药；解热剂
A medicine that reduces fever.
Example: The doctor prescribed an antipyretic for the child's high fever. 医生给孩子开了退烧药。
Synonym: antifebrile, febrifuge
Antonym: None
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀντιπυρετικός (antipyretikós, “against fever”), from ἀντί (antí, “against”) + πυρετικός (pyretikós, “febrile, feverish”), from πυρετός (pyretós, “fever”).
