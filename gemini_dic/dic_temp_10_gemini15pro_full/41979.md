cost-accounting:/ˌkɒst əˈkaʊntɪŋ/ /ˌkɑːst əˈkaʊntɪŋ/| n.|成本会计|A system of recording and analyzing the costs of producing goods or services.|Example: The company implemented a new cost-accounting system to track expenses more effectively.  公司实施了新的成本会计制度，以更有效地跟踪支出。|Synonym: cost analysis, cost control|Antonym: N/A|noun

cost-accountings:/ˌkɒst əˈkaʊntɪŋz/ /ˌkɑːst əˈkaʊntɪŋz/| n.|成本会计（复数，较少使用）|Multiple systems of recording and analyzing the costs of producing goods or services. This plural form is less common.|Example:  Comparing different cost-accountings across industries can reveal best practices. 比较不同行业的不同成本核算可以揭示最佳实践。|Synonym: cost analyses, cost controls|Antonym: N/A|noun
