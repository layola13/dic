biologic:/ˌbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪk/ /ˌbaɪəˈlɑːdʒɪk/| adj. n.|生物的，生物制剂|A substance, such as a serum or vaccine, derived from biological sources or synthesized by mimicking a biological process and used to treat or prevent disease.  A therapeutic or prophylactic agent, such as an antitoxin, serum, vaccine, or antibiotic, or a derivative of such an agent, that is prepared from or with living organisms.|The doctor prescribed a biologic to treat her rheumatoid arthritis.|近义词：biological, organic|反义词：inorganic|词性：adjective, noun

biologics:/ˌbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪks/ /ˌbaɪəˈlɑːdʒɪks/| n.|生物制剂|Medicinal products derived from living organisms. | Biologics are becoming increasingly important in the treatment of various diseases.|近义词：biologicals, biopharmaceuticals|反义词：N/A|词性：noun


词源: From bio- + -logic,  from Greek bios "life" + -logos "study".
