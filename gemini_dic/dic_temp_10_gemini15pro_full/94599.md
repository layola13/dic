imprecise:/ˌɪmprɪˈsaɪs/| adj.|不精确的，不准确的，不明确的|not exact or precise.|His instructions were imprecise, leading to confusion. 他的指示不明确，导致了混乱。|inexact, inaccurate, vague|precise, accurate, exact|adjective
