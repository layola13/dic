catholicos:/kəˈθɒlɪkɒs/ | n. |大主教，宗主教|A title used for the head of certain Eastern churches, such as the Armenian Apostolic Church.  A chief bishop or primate especially of the Eastern Orthodox churches. |例句：The Catholicos presided over the important religious ceremony.  大主教主持了重要的宗教仪式。

例句：The election of a new Catholicos is a significant event. 选出一位新的大主教是一件意义重大的事件。 |近义词：patriarch, primate, archbishop |反义词:N/A |词性:noun
