dezincification:/diːˌzɪŋkɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
脱锌，去锌
The selective corrosion of zinc from brass, leaving a porous copper residue.
Brass fittings in plumbing systems can suffer from dezincification.  黄铜配件在管道系统中会遭受脱锌腐蚀。
corrosion, leaching
