holostei:/ˈhɒləstiˌaɪ/ /ˈhoʊləstiˌaɪ/
n.
硬骨鱼纲的雀鳝目
a primitive bony fish, such as a gar or bowfin, of a subclass (Holostei) formerly considered distinct from the teleosts, now usually considered a group within the teleosts.
The holostei include gars and bowfins. 硬骨鱼包括雀鳝和弓鳍鱼。
近义词:ganoid fishes,  garpikes, bowfin
词性:noun
