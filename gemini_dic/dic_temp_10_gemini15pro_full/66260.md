exclusions:/ɪksˈkluːʒənz/| n.|排除；除外；被排除在外的事物|The price includes everything, with no exclusions. 价格包含一切，没有任何例外。|例句：There are some exclusions to this rule. 这条规则有一些例外。|例句：Membership exclusions apply during peak season.  会员资格在旺季不适用。|近义词：exceptions, omissions, restrictions|反义词：inclusions, additions|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *exclusioun*, from Late Latin *exclusiō*, from Latin *exclūdō* (“I shut out”), from *ex* (“out”) + *claudō* (“I shut, close”).
