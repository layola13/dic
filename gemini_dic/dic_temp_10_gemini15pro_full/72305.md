flouriness:/ˈflaʊərinɪs/
n.
粉状；面粉状态
The state or quality of being like flour; a soft, powdery texture.
The cake had an unpleasant flouriness to it.  这个蛋糕有一种令人不快的粉状口感。
近义词: powderiness, dustiness
反义词: moistness, dampness
词性: 名词
