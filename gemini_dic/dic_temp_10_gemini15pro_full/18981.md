besting:/ˈbɛstɪŋ/
verb
战胜，击败
defeating someone in a competition or fight.
He bested his rival in the final round. 他在最后一轮击败了对手。
近义词: defeating, conquering, overcoming, triumphing over, outdoing, surpassing, exceeding
反义词: losing to, being defeated by, succumbing to
词性: 动词(verb)

besting:/ˈbɛstɪŋ/
noun
击败，战胜 (较少用作名词)
The act of defeating someone.
His besting of the champion was a surprise to everyone.  他战胜冠军让每个人都感到惊讶。
近义词: defeat, victory, triumph
反义词: loss, defeat
词性: 名词 (noun)


词源:
From the verb "best," which comes from Old English "betst," the superlative of "bet" (good).  源自动词 "best"，它来自古英语 "betst"，是 "bet" (好的) 的最高级。
