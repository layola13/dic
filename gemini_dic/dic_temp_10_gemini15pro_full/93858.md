immobilizes:/ɪˈmɒbəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使不动；固定；使不能活动
To prevent something or someone from moving or operating.
The doctor immobilized his broken leg with a cast.  医生用石膏固定了他断掉的腿。
The police immobilized the vehicle with a tire boot. 警察用车轮锁锁住了车辆。
Synonyms: restrict, disable, paralyze
Antonyms: mobilize, activate, free
词性: 动词
