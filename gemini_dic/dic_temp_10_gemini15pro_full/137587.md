nontreaties:/nɒnˈtriːtiz/| n.|非条约；未经条约规定的|Agreements or practices that are not formalized by treaties.  指未通过正式条约确立的协议或惯例。|The two countries maintained certain nontreaties arrangements regarding trade. 这两个国家在贸易方面保持着某些非条约安排。|近义词：agreements, understandings|反义词：treaties|词性：名词


词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non.  表示“不”的意思，源自拉丁语 non。
treaty (noun): From Old French traité, past participle of traiter "to handle, treat," from Latin tractare "to drag, handle, deal with." 源自古法语 traité，traiter 的过去分词，意为“处理，对待”，源自拉丁语 tractare “拖，处理，对付”。
