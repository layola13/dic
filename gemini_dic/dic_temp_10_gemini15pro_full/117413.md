medicining:/ˈmedɪsɪnɪŋ/
|n.|用药，治疗|The act of giving or taking medicine.  The process of treating with medicine.|He's been medicining himself with over-the-counter drugs. 他一直在用非处方药给自己治疗。| 近义词: treating, dosing, medicating | 反义词: None (The act of *not* medicating doesn't have a single concise word.) |词性:名词 (gerund)


Etymology: From the verb "medicine" + "-ing" (present participle/gerund suffix).  "Medicine" comes from Middle English *medicinen*, from Old French *medicine*, and ultimately from Latin *medicīna*, meaning "the healing art," from *medicus* "physician."
