flawlessly:/ˈflɔːləsli/ | adv. |完美地，无瑕疵地 | In a manner that is free from flaws or imperfections; perfectly. | She danced flawlessly, not missing a single step. 她跳舞完美无瑕，没有错过任何一个舞步。|  She executed the dive flawlessly. 她完美地完成了跳水。 |  perfectly, impeccably, faultlessly | imperfectly, defectively | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From flaw + -less + -ly.  "Flaw" comes from Old Norse *flaga ("flake, fragment").
