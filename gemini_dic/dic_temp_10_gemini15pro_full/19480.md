bidder:/ˈbɪdər/| n.|投标人，竞标者|Someone who offers a particular amount of money for something that is being sold.|The highest bidder won the auction. 最高出价者赢得了拍卖。|Synonyms: tenderer, offerer, applicant|Antonyms: seller, vendor|noun

bidder:/ˈbɪdər/| n.|出价者|Someone who makes a bid, especially at an auction.|Several bidders competed for the antique vase.  几位竞标者竞争那只古董花瓶。|Synonyms: offerer, tenderer|Antonyms: recipient, receiver|noun
