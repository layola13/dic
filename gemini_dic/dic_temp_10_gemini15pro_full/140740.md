omnipresently:/ˌɒm.nɪˈprez.ənt.li/ /ˌɑːm.nɪˈprez.ənt.li/
adv.
无所不在，普遍存在
Being or seeming to be everywhere at the same time.
例句：God is believed to be omnipresently watching over us. 上帝被认为无所不在地守护着我们。

例句：Social media has become an omnipresently influential force in modern society. 社交媒体已经成为现代社会中无所不在的影响力。

近义词：everywhere, ubiquitously
反义词：nowhere, locally
词性：副词
