hydrographical:/ˌhaɪdrəˈɡræfɪk(ə)l/
adjective
水文的；水道测量的
relating to hydrography; the science of surveying and charting bodies of water
例句：Hydrographical surveys are essential for safe navigation. 水文测量对于安全航行至关重要。

例句：The hydrographical data is used to create nautical charts.  水文数据用于创建航海图。

近义词：hydrographic, bathymetric
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps consider "terrestrial" or "geological" as pertaining to land rather than water)
词性: adjective
