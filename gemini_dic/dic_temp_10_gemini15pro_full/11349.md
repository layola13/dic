arenicolite:/ˌærɪˈnɪkəˌlaɪt/ | n. | 沙栖生物；海沙虫 | A worm or other organism that lives in sand. |  Arenicolites are commonly found in intertidal zones.  沙栖生物常见于潮间带。 | Synonyms: sand-dweller,  infaunal organism | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider pelagic organism -  an organism living in open water) | Noun
