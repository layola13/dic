counter-book: /ˈkaʊntərˌbʊk/ | n. |对账簿，流水账簿 | A book used to record transactions, especially in banking or accounting, providing a detailed record of deposits, withdrawals, and other financial activities.  It serves as a check against errors and discrepancies. | The bank teller meticulously updated the counter-book after each transaction.  银行出纳员在每次交易后都一丝不苟地更新对账簿。

The auditor compared the entries in the counter-book with the bank statements. 审计员将对账簿中的条目与银行对账单进行了比较。 | synonym: ledger, account book, journal | antonym: N/A | noun
