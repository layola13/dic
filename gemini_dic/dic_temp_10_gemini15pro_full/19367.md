bibliothec: /ˈbɪbliəˌθɛk/ (often pronounced /ˈlaɪbrəri/ in modern usage) | n. | 图书馆，藏书室 | A room or building containing a collection of books and other materials for reading, study, or reference.  This is an archaic form, and "library" is the commonly used word today. |  The bibliothec held many ancient tomes. (Archaic - 藏书室里收藏着许多古代巨著。) | Synonym: library, archive | Antonym: None (The opposite concept might be "empty room" or "nothingness", but there isn't a direct antonym.) | Noun
