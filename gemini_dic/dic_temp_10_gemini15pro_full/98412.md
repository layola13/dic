insonorous:/ɪnˈsɒnərəs/ /ɪnˈsɑːnərəs/| adj.|声音不清的；沉闷的；无音的|Not producing or having a full, rich, or pleasing sound; lacking resonance.|The insonorous voice of the speaker made it difficult to hear him in the large hall. 演讲者沉闷的声音使他在大厅里很难被听到.|近义词：dull, muffled, muted, hushed, quiet|反义词：sonorous, resonant, loud, clear|词性:形容词

词源:  Late 16th century: from Latin in- ‘not’ + sonorus ‘sounding’ (from sonor ‘sound’).
