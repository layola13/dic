confit:/ˈkɒnfiː/ (UK) /kɔːnˈfiː/ (US)| n.| 油封，蜜饯 |A dish, especially of meat, cooked slowly in its own fat; fruit preserved in sugar.|例句：The duck confit was rich and flavorful. 这道鸭油封浓郁而美味。

例句：She made a delicious apricot confit. 她做了一份美味的杏蜜饯。|近义词：preserve, conserve |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


confit:/ˈkɒnfiː/ (UK) /kɔːnˈfiː/ (US)| v.| 油封，蜜饯（动词形式，较少用） |To cook something, especially meat, slowly in its own fat; to preserve fruit in sugar.|例句：The chef confited the duck legs for hours. 厨师将鸭腿油封了几个小时。

例句：They confited the oranges in a sugar syrup. 他们用糖浆蜜饯了橙子。|近义词：preserve, conserve |反义词: N/A |词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *confit*, past participle of *confire* "to preserve, pickle, jam," from Latin *conficere* "to accomplish, produce, make, prepare," from *com-* "together" (see com-) + *facere* "to do, make" (see fac-)
