frowningly:/ˈfraʊnɪŋli/| adv.|皱眉地，不悦地|In a manner that expresses disapproval or displeasure by frowning.  It describes the action of frowning as an adverb.|She looked at him frowningly, clearly disapproving of his suggestion. 她皱着眉头看着他，显然不赞成他的建议。|Synonyms: disapprovingly, sullenly, sternly, crossly|Antonyms: smilingly, cheerfully, approvingly|adverb
