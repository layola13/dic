ibexes:/ˈaɪbɛksɪz/
n.
野山羊（复数）
Wild goats, especially of mountainous regions; plural of ibex.
The herd of ibexes nimbly navigated the steep cliffs. 这群野山羊灵活地穿梭于陡峭的悬崖上。
Near synonyms: wild goats, mountain goats
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider domesticated goats)
Noun


ibex:/ˈaɪbɛks/
n.
野山羊（单数）
A wild goat, Capra ibex, with long, ridged horns, that lives in mountainous regions of Eurasia and North Africa.
The ibex stood majestically on the mountain peak. 那只野山羊雄赳赳地站在山峰上。
Near synonyms: wild goat, mountain goat
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider domesticated goat)
Noun
