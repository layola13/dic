intruding: /ɪnˈtruːdɪŋ/
adjective
侵入的，打扰的
pushing oneself into a place or situation where one is unwelcome or uninvited.
例句：The intruding noise from the street made it hard to concentrate. 街上传来的噪音让人难以集中注意力。

例句：She felt like an intruding presence in their close-knit family. 她觉得自己像个闯入者，打扰了他们紧密的家庭。

近义词：interfering, invasive, encroaching, trespassing
反义词：welcomed, invited, permitted

词性: adjective (形容词)

词源: From the verb "intrude," which comes from the Latin "intrudere" (to thrust in), from "in-" (in) + "trudere" (to thrust).
