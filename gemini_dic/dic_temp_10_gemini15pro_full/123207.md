monochromy: /məˈnɑːkrəmi/ /məˈnɒkrəmi/ | n. | 单色; 单色画法 | The state or quality of being monochromatic; the use of only one color or shades of one color, especially in works of art. | The artist chose monochromy to create a sense of starkness and simplicity. 艺术家选择单色来营造一种鲜明和简洁的感觉。 | The photograph was printed in monochromy. 这张照片是用单色印刷的。 | synonyms: monochrome, one-color, single-color | antonyms: polychromy, multicolor | noun

monochromatic: /ˌmɑːnəkrəˈmætɪk/ /ˌmɒnəkrəˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 单色的; 单频的 | Having or consisting of one color or shades of one color. | The room was decorated in a monochromatic scheme of gray. 房间以灰色的单色方案装饰。| The laser emitted monochromatic light. 激光器发出单色光。 | synonyms: one-color, single-color, monotone | antonyms: polychromatic, multicolor | adjective
