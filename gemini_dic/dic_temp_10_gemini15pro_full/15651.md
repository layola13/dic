bajillion:/bəˈdʒɪljən/ | n. |无数，极大数量|An unspecified, extremely large number.  It is a humorous, informal word and not a precise quantity. |  I've told you a bajillion times to clean your room! 我已经告诉过你无数次要打扫你的房间了！ | Example: There were a bajillion mosquitoes at the campsite.  露营地有无数的蚊子。|  Near Synonyms: zillion, gazillion, jillion, a lot, tons, heaps| Antonyms: few, little | Noun
