myriapod:/ˌmɪriəˈpɒd/ (US) /ˌmɪriəˈpɑːd/| n. |多足类动物|An arthropod with many body segments, each bearing one or two pairs of legs, e.g., a centipede or millipede.|例句：Myriapods, such as centipedes and millipedes, thrive in damp environments. 多足类动物，如蜈蚣和千足虫，在潮湿的环境中 thrive。

例句：The myriapod scurried under a rock to escape the sunlight. 这只多足类动物 scurried 到岩石下躲避阳光。|近义词：arthropod 节肢动物|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps vertebrate 脊椎动物 as a contrasting group) |词性:noun
