naturalization:/ˌnætʃrələˈzeɪʃn/ |n.|入籍，归化；驯化|The process by which a foreign citizen becomes a citizen of a new country.  一个外国公民成为一个新国家的公民的过程。|Example: After living in the US for five years, she applied for naturalization.  在美国生活了五年后，她申请了入籍。

Example: The naturalization ceremony was a moving experience for the new citizens.  入籍仪式对新公民来说是一次感人的经历。|Synonyms: citizenship, incorporation |Antonyms: denaturalization, expatriation|noun
