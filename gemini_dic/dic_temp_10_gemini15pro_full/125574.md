multitool:/ˈmʌltɪtuːl/| n.|多功能工具|A hand tool that combines several individual functions in a single unit, such as pliers, a screwdriver, a knife, and a saw.|I always carry a multitool when I go camping; it's so useful.|Synonyms: multi-tool, Swiss Army knife, pocket tool|Antonyms: single-purpose tool|noun

multitools:/ˈmʌltɪtuːlz/| n.|多功能工具（复数）|Plural form of multitool, referring to more than one multitool.|He collects multitools and has a wide variety of different models.|Synonyms: multi-tools, Swiss Army knives, pocket tools|Antonyms: single-purpose tools|noun
