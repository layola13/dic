overconcentration:/ˌoʊvərˌkɑːnsənˈtreɪʃən/ | n. | 过度集中，过度关注 | Excessive concentration on one aspect or area, often to the detriment of others. | The overconcentration of power in the hands of a few can lead to corruption.  权力过度集中在少数人手中会导致腐败。|  His overconcentration on work led to him neglecting his family. 他过度专注于工作导致他忽略了家人。 | 近义词：fixation, obsession, preoccupation | 反义词：distraction, inattention | 词性: 名词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "concentration" (from Latin "concentrare," meaning "to bring to a center").
