divulge:/daɪˈvʌldʒ/ /dɪˈvʌldʒ/| vt.|泄露，透露|to make known (private or sensitive information)|She refused to divulge the source of her information. 她拒绝透露消息来源。|synonyms: reveal, disclose, unveil, impart|antonyms: conceal, hide, suppress|verb

divulge:/daɪˈvʌldʒ/ /dɪˈvʌldʒ/| n.|泄露，透露（的行为） (less common usage)|The divulgence of the secret caused a scandal.  这个秘密的泄露引起了一场丑闻。|synonyms: revelation, disclosure|antonyms: concealment, suppression|noun
