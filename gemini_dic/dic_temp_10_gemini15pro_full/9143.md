antinationalistic:/ˌæntiˌnæʃənəˈlɪstɪk/| adj.|反民族主义的，反对民族主义的|Opposed to nationalism; showing opposition to nationalism.  It describes sentiments, policies, or actions that reject or oppose the ideology of nationalism. |His antinationalistic views made him unpopular in his home country. 他的反民族主义观点使他在自己的国家不受欢迎。|Antinationalist sentiment is growing among the youth. 年轻人中的反民族主义情绪正在增长。|近义词：anti-national, cosmopolitan, globalist, internationalist|反义词：nationalistic, patriotic, jingoistic|词性:adjective
