lententide: /ˈlɛntɪntaɪd/ | n. | 四旬斋期; 大斋期 | Lententide is a period of fasting and penitence observed by many Christians, especially in the Anglican and Roman Catholic traditions.  It begins on Ash Wednesday and ends on Easter Sunday. |  Lententide is a time for reflection and prayer. 四旬期是反思和祈祷的时期。 |  Many Christians give up something for Lententide, such as chocolate or alcohol. 许多基督徒在四旬斋期间放弃一些东西，例如巧克力或酒精。 | synonyms: Lent, Quadragesima | antonyms: Eastertide | noun
