copycats:/ˈkɒpiˌkæts/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpiˌkæts/ (US)| n. |抄袭者，模仿者|A person who copies another's work, behavior, or style.|例句：He's such a copycat, always wearing the same clothes as me.  他真是个爱模仿的人，总是穿跟我一样的衣服。

例句：Many startups are just copycats of successful Silicon Valley companies. 许多初创公司只是硅谷成功企业的模仿者。|近义词：imitator, emulator, mimic|反义词：innovator, originator, pioneer|词性：名词
