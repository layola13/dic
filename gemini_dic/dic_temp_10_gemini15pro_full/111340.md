liveliest:/ˈlaɪvliɪst/| adj.|最活泼的，最有生气的|Superlative form of lively: most lively.  It means full of life and energy; active and enthusiastic.|The liveliest party of the year was held in the town square.  一年中最热闹的派对在城镇广场举行。|近义词：most energetic, most vibrant, most animated|反义词:dullest, quietest, least active|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *livelyest, *lyflyest, from Old English līflīcost, superlative of līflīc (“living, lively”).
