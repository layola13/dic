outlive:/ˌaʊtˈlɪv/ | v. | 比...活得长; 比...持久; 经受住;  | To live or exist longer than someone or something. |  She outlived her husband by twenty years. 她比她丈夫多活了二十年。

She outlived all her children. 她比她所有的孩子都活得长。


近义词: survive, outlast | 反义词: predecease | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ūtlifian, from ūt- "out" + libban "to live".
