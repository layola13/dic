nonsequestered:/nɒnˈsiːkwɛstəd/ | adj. | 未隔离的，未扣押的 | Not sequestered; not separated or isolated. |  The jury was nonsequestered, allowing them to go home each night. 陪审团没有被隔离，允许他们每晚回家。

The nonsequestered funds were immediately available for use. 未被扣押的资金可立即使用。 | 近义词: unsequestered, free, available, released | 反义词: sequestered, isolated, confined, seized | 词性: 形容词
