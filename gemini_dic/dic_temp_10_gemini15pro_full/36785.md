colocynth:/ˈkɒləsɪnθ/ (US) /ˈkɑːləsɪnθ/| n.|药葫芦；药葫芦的果实|A Mediterranean climbing plant (Citrullus colocynthis) related to the cucumber, whose dried, spongy pulp has been used since ancient times as a powerful purgative.|The colocynth is a potent medicinal plant, but should be used with caution. 药葫芦是一种强效药用植物，但应谨慎使用。|Synonyms: bitter apple, bitter cucumber, egusi|Antonyms: N/A|Noun


colocynth:/ˈkɒləsɪnθ/ (US) /ˈkɑːləsɪnθ/| n.|药葫芦的提取物|The extract obtained from the colocynth plant, used medicinally.|Colocynth extract can be used to treat constipation. 药葫芦提取物可用于治疗便秘。|Synonyms: colocynth pulp, colocynthine|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
