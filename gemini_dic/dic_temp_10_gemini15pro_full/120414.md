minisummit:/ˈmɪniˌsʌmɪt/
noun
小型峰会，小型首脑会议
A small summit, typically involving a limited number of participants or focusing on a specific issue.
The two leaders held a minisummit to discuss the trade dispute.  两国领导人举行了一次小型峰会，讨论贸易争端。
The minisummit focused on the ongoing humanitarian crisis. 小型峰会重点关注正在进行的人道主义危机。
Synonym: small summit, meeting
Antonym: major summit, large conference
n.
