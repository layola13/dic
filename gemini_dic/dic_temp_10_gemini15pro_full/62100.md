enchantedly:/ɪnˈtʃæntɪdli/
adverb
着迷地，欣喜若狂地
In a manner filled with delight and wonder; magically.
She listened to the story enchantedly, her eyes wide with wonder. 她着迷地听着故事，眼睛睁得大大的，充满了好奇。
The children gazed enchantedly at the fireworks. 孩子们欣喜若狂地凝视着烟火。
近义词: rapturously, magically, delightfully, blissfully
反义词: indifferently, apathetically, boredly
词性: adverb

enchant:/ɪnˈtʃænt/
verb
使着迷，施魔法
To fill with delight and wonder; to put under a spell.
The magician enchanted the audience with his tricks. 魔术师用他的戏法使观众着迷。
The beautiful scenery enchanted us. 美丽的景色使我们着迷。
近义词: charm, captivate, fascinate, bewitch
反义词: repel, disgust, bore
词性: verb

词源:
From Old French enchanter, from Late Latin incantare “to sing a magic formula over,” from in- “in, on” + cantare “to sing.”  The original sense was thus "to put a spell on (someone) by singing."
