digitalization:/ˌdɪdʒɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |数字化，数位化|The process of converting information into a digital format.  The conversion of analog information into digital form. |例句：The digitalization of the library has made it easier to access books. 图书馆的数字化使人们更容易获得书籍。

例句：The company invested heavily in the digitalization of its operations.  这家公司在其运营的数字化方面投入了巨资。|近义词：digitization, computerization, automation|反义词:analogization|词性:noun
