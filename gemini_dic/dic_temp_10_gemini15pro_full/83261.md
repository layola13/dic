gymnoglossa:/ˌdʒɪmnoʊˈɡlɒsə/ | n. | 裸舌类 | A suborder of frogs characterized by having no tongue. |  These tiny frogs, belonging to the gymnoglossa suborder,  catch their prey with a sticky secretion on their palate. 这些小青蛙属于裸舌亚目，用上颚的粘性分泌物捕捉猎物。 |  近义词: (No direct synonym, as it's a taxonomic classification. Could list related families like Pipidae) | 反义词: (No antonym for a taxonomic group) | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek γυμνός (gumnós, “naked, bare”) + γλῶσσα (glôssa, “tongue”).
