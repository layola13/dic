amenities:/əˈmɛnɪtiz/
n.
便利设施；舒适设施；礼仪；宜人之处
things that make a place comfortable or easy to live in, such as hot water, bathrooms, and shops
The hotel offers a wide range of amenities, including a swimming pool, spa, and fitness center.  这家酒店提供各种便利设施，包括游泳池、水疗中心和健身中心。
facilities, conveniences, comforts
inconveniences, disadvantages
noun

amenity:/əˈmɛnɪti/
n.
舒适；礼仪；宜人
the pleasantness or agreeableness of something
We appreciate the amenity of the climate here. 我们喜欢这里宜人的气候。
pleasantness, agreeableness
unpleasantness, disagreeableness
noun
