conch:/kɒŋk/ (UK), /kɑːŋk/ (US)| n. |海螺; 海螺壳;  |A large spiral shell of a marine mollusk, especially of the genera Strombus and Cassis. The shell is often used as a horn, or for decoration. |We found a beautiful conch on the beach. 我们在海滩上发现了一个漂亮的海螺。|Synonyms: seashell, shell, whelk|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

conchs:/kɒŋks/ (UK), /kɑːŋks/ (US)| n. |海螺（复数） |Plural form of "conch".  Multiple large spiral shells of marine mollusks. |The children collected conchs and other seashells. 孩子们收集了海螺和其他贝壳。|Synonyms: seashells, shells|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
