ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *lātheus, from Latin lāthyrus (“vetchling, chickling vetch”), from Ancient Greek λάθυρος (láthuros, “a kind of pulse”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *lē- (“to bend, twist”).
