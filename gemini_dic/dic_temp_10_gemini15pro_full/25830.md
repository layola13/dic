burglariously: /bɜːrˈɡlɛəriəsli/
| adv. |
| 以盗窃的方式；偷偷地 |
| In a manner suggestive of burglary; stealthily and illegally. |
| He entered the house burglariously, hoping to find valuables. 他偷偷地潜入房子，希望找到值钱的东西。 |

| The cat burglariously crept into the kitchen, hoping to steal some food.  这只猫偷偷摸摸地溜进厨房，希望能偷到一些食物。|
| stealthily, secretly, furtively, surreptitiously, covertly |
| openly, publicly, honestly |
| adverb |

Etymology:
From "burglar" + "-ious" (forming adjectives meaning "having the quality of") + "-ly" (forming adverbs). "Burglar" comes from Old French "burgler" meaning "housebreaker," ultimately from Germanic roots.
