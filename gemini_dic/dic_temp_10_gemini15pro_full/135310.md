nonpathologic:/ˌnɒnpæθəˈlɒdʒɪk/ | adj. | 非病理性的 | Not due to disease; not indicative of disease; normal. |  Example: The doctor assured her that the lump was nonpathologic. 医生向她保证肿块是非病理性的。

Example:  Nonpathologic changes in breast tissue can occur due to hormonal fluctuations.  乳腺组织的非病理性变化可能是由于荷尔蒙波动引起的。| 近义词: normal, healthy, benign, innocuous | 反义词: pathologic, diseased, abnormal, malignant | 词性: adjective

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + pathologic (adjective meaning "relating to or caused by disease")
