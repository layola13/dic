barometer:/bəˈrɒmɪtər/ (UK) /bəˈrɑːmɪtər/ (US)| n. |气压计；晴雨表；指标；标尺|An instrument measuring atmospheric pressure, used especially in forecasting the weather and determining altitude.|The falling barometer indicated that a storm was coming. 气压下降表明暴风雨即将来临。|Synonyms: gauge, indicator, measure, index|Antonyms: N/A|noun

barometric:/ˌbærəˈmɛtrɪk/ (UK) /ˌbærəˈmɛtrɪk/ (US) | adj. |气压的|Of or relating to a barometer or atmospheric pressure. |Barometric pressure is a key factor in weather forecasting. 气压是天气预报的关键因素。|Synonyms: atmospheric|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
