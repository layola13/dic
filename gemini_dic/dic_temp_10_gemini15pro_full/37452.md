commissarial: /ˌkɒmɪˈsɛəriəl/ (UK) /ˌkɑːmɪˈsɛriəl/ (US)| adj.| 军需的；补给的；粮食的| relating to the supply of food and other provisions, especially for an army or other large group of people| The commissarial arrangements for the festival were excellent.  节日食品供应安排非常出色。| Synonyms: supply, provisioning, catering| Antonyms:  lacking, deficient| adjective

commissariat: /ˌkɒmɪˈsɛəriət/ (UK) /ˌkɑːmɪˈsɛriət/ (US)| n.| 军需部门；补给；粮食| a department or organization responsible for supplying food and other provisions, especially for an army or other large group of people; the food and provisions themselves| The army's commissariat was stretched thin during the long campaign.  在漫长的战役中，军队的粮食供应捉襟见肘。| Synonyms: supply department, provisions, victuals| Antonyms:  lack, scarcity| noun
