pasan:/ˈpɑːsɑːn/| n.|通行证，护照；执照；批准；许可；及格；通行；山隘，关口|A document or other object that authorizes a person to pass or go somewhere.  A narrow passage, especially through mountains.|He showed his pasan to the guard. 他向警卫出示了他的通行证。|近义词：permit, license, pass, passport, authorization, clearance, document, safe-conduct, warrant|反义词: denial, refusal|词性:名词

词源: From Spanish *pasante* "one who passes," from *pasar* "to pass," from Late Latin *passare*.
