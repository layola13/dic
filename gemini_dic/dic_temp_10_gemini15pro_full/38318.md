conceals:/kənˈsiːlz/
verb
隐藏，隐瞒
to prevent something from being seen or known about
The trees concealed the house from view.  树木遮蔽了房子，使之无法看到。
He concealed his disappointment from his friends. 他向朋友们隐瞒了他的失望。
hide, disguise, mask, cover
reveal, expose, uncover
v.
