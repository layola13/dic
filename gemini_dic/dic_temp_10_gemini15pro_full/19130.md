beveled:/ˈbɛvəld/ (US), /ˈbɛvld/ (UK)| adj. |斜面的，斜角的|Having a sloping edge or surface.  Cut at an angle, not square.|The mirror had a beveled edge.  镜子有斜边。|近义词：sloped, angled, slanted, chamfered|反义词：square, flat|adjective

bevel:/ˈbɛvəl/ (US), /ˈbɛvld/ (UK)| n. |斜面，斜角|A sloping surface or edge.|The carpenter used a plane to create a bevel on the wood. 木匠用刨子在木头上做了一个斜面。|近义词：slope, angle, slant, chamfer|反义词：squareness, flatness|noun

bevel:/ˈbɛvəl/ (US), /ˈbɛvld/ (UK)| vt. |将…切成斜角，使成斜面|To cut or shape an object so that it has a sloping edge or surface.|He beveled the edges of the table to give it a more elegant look. 他把桌子的边缘削成斜角，让它看起来更优雅。|近义词：slope, angle, slant, chamfer|反义词：square|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *bivel, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old French beivre "to drink," referring to the slanted edge of a drinking glass.
