decime:/ˈdaɪsiːm/ /ˈdɛsɪm/ | n. |十分之一，十分钱|A coin of the United States or Canada worth one tenth of a dollar or of the basic monetary unit.  It is made of an alloy of copper and nickel. |例句：He had only a few dimes in his pocket. 他口袋里只有几枚十分钱硬币。

例句：The price was raised by a dime. 价格上涨了十分钱。|近义词：tenth, bit (informal) |反义词: dollar |词性:noun
