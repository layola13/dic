consigns:/kənˈsaɪnz/
verb
交付；托运；寄售
to give, transfer, or deliver into the care or charge of another
The artist consigned his paintings to the gallery.  这位艺术家把他的画寄售给了画廊。
commit; entrust; deliver; transfer
retain; keep; withhold
v.
