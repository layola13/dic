implementers:/ˈɪmplɪmentərz/
n.
实施者，执行者
People who put plans or ideas into action.
The implementers of the new policy faced considerable resistance. 新政策的执行者面临相当大的阻力。
implementors, executors, administrators
non-implementers
noun
