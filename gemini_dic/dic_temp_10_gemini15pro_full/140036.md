oenologies:/ˌiːnɒlədʒiz/
n.
葡萄酒学，酿酒学
The study of wines and winemaking.
He took a course in oenologies to learn more about the winemaking process. 他参加了一个葡萄酒学课程，以了解更多关于酿酒过程的知识。
The university offers a degree program in oenologies. 这所大学提供葡萄酒学学位课程。
近义词: viticulture, viniculture
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

Etymology: From French *œnologie*, from Ancient Greek *οἶνος* (oînos, “wine”) + *-λογία* (-logía, “-logy”).
