battlefronts: /ˈbætlfʌnts/ | n. | 战线 |  The areas or spheres of activity where a struggle or conflict is being waged.  | The political battlefronts are heating up as the election approaches.  政治战线随着选举的临近而升温。

The company is facing battlefronts on several legal issues.  公司在几个法律问题上都面临着挑战。 | 近义词: front lines, theaters (of war), arenas | 反义词: rear, home front | 词性: 名词

词源:  From "battle" (Old French bataille) + "front" (Old French front).
