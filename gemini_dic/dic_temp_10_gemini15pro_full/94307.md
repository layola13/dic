imperviously:/ɪmˈpɜːrviəsli/
adverb
不能渗透地；无动于衷地
not allowing fluid to pass through.  Not affected or influenced by something.
The fortress walls were built so imperviously that even the heaviest rains could not penetrate them.  堡垒的城墙建得非常坚固，即使是最猛烈的暴雨也无法渗透。

He listened imperviously to her pleas, his face a mask of stone. 他无动于衷地听着她的恳求，脸上毫无表情。
近义词:impenetrably, impermeably, insensitively, unfeelingly
反义词:permeably, sensitively, responsively
词性:adverb
