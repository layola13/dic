malinger:/məˈlɪŋɡər/| vi.|装病；逃避责任|to pretend to be ill in order to avoid work or duty|He was suspected of malingering to avoid military service. 他被怀疑装病以逃避兵役。|Synonyms: feign illness, shirk, dodge|Antonyms: work diligently, fulfill obligations|Verb

malingerer:/məˈlɪŋɡərər/| n.|装病者；逃避责任者|a person who malingers|The malingerer was eventually caught and dismissed from his job.  这个装病者最终被发现并被解雇了。|Synonyms: shirker, dodger, slacker|Antonyms: hard worker, diligent person|Noun
