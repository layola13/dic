hyperlustrousnesses:/ˌhaɪpərˈlʌstrəsnəsɪz/ | n. | 极度光亮；极度光泽 |  A state of extreme or excessive lustrousness.  This word emphasizes an extreme degree of shine or brilliance. | The hyperlustrousnesses of the newly polished car blinded me momentarily. 新抛光汽车的极度光泽让我一时眼花缭乱。|  The hyperlustrousnesses of the gemstones captivated everyone in the room. 宝石的极度光泽吸引了房间里所有人的目光。 | 近义词： brilliance, radiance, sheen, glitter | 反义词： dullness, opacity | 词性: 名词

词源:  "hyper-" (Greek, meaning "over, excessive") + "lustrous" (from Old French "lustros," meaning "shining") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).
