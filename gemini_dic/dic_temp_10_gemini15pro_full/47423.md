noun

The state or quality of being dead; lack of vitality or activity.
The deadness's of the winter landscape was oppressive. 冬日景象的死气沉沉令人压抑。
近义词: lifelessness, inertia, stagnation
反义词: liveliness, vibrancy, activity
词性: 名词


词源:
From deadness + -'s (possessive suffix).  Deadness itself comes from dead (adjective) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality).  The ultimate root is the Old English word "dēad."
