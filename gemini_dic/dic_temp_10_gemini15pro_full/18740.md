bergschrund:/ˈbɛrɡʃrʊnd/ |n.|冰裂缝，冰斗裂缝|A crevasse or wide crack that forms where moving glacier ice separates from stagnant ice clinging to the headwall of a cirque or valley.|The climbers had to navigate a treacherous bergschrund. 登山者不得不穿过一条危险的冰裂缝。|Synonyms: crevasse, fissure, rift|Antonyms: closure, seal|noun
