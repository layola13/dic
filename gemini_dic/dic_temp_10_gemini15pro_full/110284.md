ligates:/ˈlaɪɡeɪts/
verb
连接；结合；捆绑
acts to bind or connect something
例句：The enzyme ligates the DNA fragments together. 这种酶将DNA片段连接在一起。

例句：The surgeon ligated the bleeding artery.  外科医生结扎了出血的动脉。
近义词：binds, joins, connects, combines, fastens, attaches
反义词：separates, disconnects, detaches
词性：v.


ligate:/ˈlaɪɡeɪt/
noun
连接物；系带
something that binds or ties
例句：The surgeon used a ligate to tie off the blood vessel.  外科医生用系带绑住了血管。

例句：The ligate held the two pieces of wood together.  连接物将两块木头固定在一起。
近义词：bond, tie, fastener, connector
反义词：N/A
词性：n.
