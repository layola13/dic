outbragged: /ˌaʊtˈbræɡd/
verb
吹嘘胜过，夸口超过
boasted more than; surpassed in bragging
He outbragged all his friends about his new car. 他吹嘘他的新车超过了他所有的朋友。
近义词: outboasted, outsold
反义词: understated, downplayed
词性: verb (past tense)
