forbearingly:/fɔːrˈbɛərɪŋli/
adverb
宽容地；忍耐地；有耐心地
in a patient and forgiving way, showing tolerance or self-control.
She forbearingly listened to his long, rambling explanation. 她耐心地听了他冗长而散漫的解释。
Near Synonyms: patiently, tolerantly, leniently, indulgently
Antonyms: impatiently, intolerantly, harshly, critically
词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From forbear + -ing + -ly.  Forbear comes from Old English forberan, meaning "to refrain from, endure."
