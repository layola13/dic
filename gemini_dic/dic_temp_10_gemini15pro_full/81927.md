grilly:/ˈɡrɪli/| adj.|烤的，烧烤味的|Having the taste or appearance of grilled food; suitable for grilling.|The air smelled grilly from the barbecue next door. 隔壁烧烤散发出的空气闻起来有烧烤味。|近义词：barbecued, roasted, charred|反义词:boiled, steamed|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From "grill" + "-y" (adjective-forming suffix). "Grill" originates from the Old French "griller," meaning "to broil."
