overhomely:/ˌoʊvərˈhoʊmli/| adj.|过于朴素的，过于简单的，过于家常的|Excessively plain or simple, lacking refinement or elegance; too suggestive of home life in a negative way.  It implies a lack of sophistication or worldliness.|例句：Her overhomely dress was inappropriate for the gala. 她的过于朴素的裙子不适合参加晚会。|近义词：plain, simple, unpretentious, dowdy, frumpy|反义词：stylish, elegant, sophisticated, chic|词性:形容词
