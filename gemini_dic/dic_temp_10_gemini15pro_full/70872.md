finnish:/ˈfɪnɪʃ/ | adj. n. | 芬兰的；芬兰语；芬兰人 |  Relating to Finland, its people, or their language.  Also a noun referring to the Finnish language or the Finnish people (plural: Finns). |  例句：She speaks Finnish fluently. 她芬兰语说得非常流利。

例句：The Finnish landscape is beautiful. 芬兰的风景很美。| 近义词：(for the language) Suomi | 反义词: (No direct antonym, could use non-Finnish) | 词性: adjective, noun


finnish:/ˈfɪnɪʃ/ | v. | 完成；结束；用...结束 | To bring (something) to an end; complete. |  例句：I need to finish my homework before I can go out. 我需要完成我的作业才能出去。

例句：The race finished with a sprint to the finish line. 比赛以冲刺到终点线结束。 | 近义词：complete, conclude, end, terminate | 反义词: start, begin, commence | 词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *finisshen, *fenisshen, from Old French fenir, finir (“to finish, end, conclude”), from Latin fīnīre (“to limit, bound, put an end to, finish”), from fīnis (“boundary, limit, end”). Doublet of fine.
