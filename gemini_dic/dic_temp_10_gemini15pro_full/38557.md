conclusivenesses:/kənˈkluːsɪvnəsɪz/
|n.| 结论性；确定性；决定性
|The state or quality of being conclusive; the ability of something to settle an issue or prove a point beyond doubt.|
|The conclusivenesses of the evidence led to a guilty verdict.  这些证据的决定性导致了有罪判决。|
|The various conclusivenesses of the different studies made it hard to draw a firm conclusion.  不同研究的不同结论性使得难以得出确定的结论。|
|conclusivity, decisiveness, finality|
|inconclusiveness, ambiguity, uncertainty|
|noun|
