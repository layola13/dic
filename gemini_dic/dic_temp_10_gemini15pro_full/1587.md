achronic: /eɪˈkrɒnɪk/
adjective
非时间性的; 与时间无关的
Not existing in time or historical time; not subject to the limitations of time.
His art can be described as achronic, existing outside of any specific time period. 他的艺术可以被描述为非时间性的，存在于任何特定时期之外。

The novel presents an achronic world, where past, present, and future blur together.  这部小说呈现了一个非时间性的世界，过去、现在和未来交织在一起。

近义词: timeless, eternal, nontemporal
反义词: temporal, historical, chronological
词性: adjective

From a- (not) + chron- (time) + -ic (adjective suffix).  The prefix "a-" signifies negation, "chron-" relates to time, and "-ic" forms an adjective.  This construction signifies "not related to time."
