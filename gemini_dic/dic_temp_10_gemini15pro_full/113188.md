lurg:/lɜːrɡ/ | n. | (非正式)一种不明原因的疾病，通常指感冒或类似流感 | An unspecified illness, usually a cold or flu-like illness (informal). |  I've caught a terrible lurg. 我得了重感冒。| 近义词: cold, flu, bug, virus | 反义词: health, wellness | 词性: 名词

词源:  The origin of "lurg" is uncertain. It likely emerged in British slang in the mid-20th century, possibly influenced by words like "allergy" or the German word "Lunge" (lung).
