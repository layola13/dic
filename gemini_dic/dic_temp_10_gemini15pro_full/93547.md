imaginative:/ɪˈmædʒɪnətɪv/ (US), /ɪˈmædʒɪnətɪv/ (UK)| adj.|富有想象力的；爱想象的|Having or showing a good imagination; creative.|She's a very imaginative writer. 她是一位非常有想象力的作家。|近义词：creative, inventive, original, visionary|反义词：unimaginative, unimaginative, dull, pedestrian|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from late Latin imaginativus, from imaginari ‘to imagine.’
