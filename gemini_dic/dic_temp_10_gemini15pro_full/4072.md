aiguillette:/ˌeɪɡwiˈlɛt/ | n. | aiguillette (US), aiguillettes (UK) |  A braided cord or string with metal tips, worn as an ornament on uniforms, especially military uniforms. Also, a thin strip of meat, especially poultry. | The general's uniform was adorned with gold aiguillettes. 将军的制服上装饰着金色的肩章。|  The chef prepared chicken aiguillettes with a creamy mushroom sauce.  厨师准备了奶油蘑菇酱鸡柳。| 近义词: cord, braid, frog (for the uniform ornament); strip, fillet (for the meat) | 反义词: None | noun
