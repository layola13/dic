mariput:/ˈmærɪpʌt/ | n. |印尼语，椰子油渣 | Residue from coconut milk after the oil has been extracted, often used as animal feed or fertilizer in Indonesia. | The farmer used the mariput as fertilizer for his crops. 农民用椰子油渣作为作物肥料。 | 近义词:  copra meal, coconut pomace | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could be considered the opposite of coconut oil itself) | 词性: noun
