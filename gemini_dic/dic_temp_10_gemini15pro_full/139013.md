o'clock: /əˈklɒk/ (UK) /əˈklɑːk/ (US)| adv.|点钟|Contraction of "of the clock," used to specify the exact hour. It's primarily used with whole numbers representing hours.|It's three o'clock. 现在是三点钟。|近义词: sharp (when emphasizing punctuality)|反义词: N/A|词性: adverb

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The clock strikes twelve o'clock. 时钟敲响十二点。
The train leaves at six o'clock every morning. 火车每天早上六点出发。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
It's getting close to five o'clock. 快到五点了。
The meeting is starting at two o'clock sharp. 会议两点准时开始。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
It has just turned ten o'clock. 刚到十点。
The bell has rung for eleven o'clock. 十一点的钟声已经响了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been waiting since eight o'clock. 我从八点开始就一直在等。
She has been working since seven o'clock this morning. 她从今天早上七点就开始工作了.


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
The alarm went off at six o'clock. 闹钟在六点响了。
The film started at eight o'clock. 电影八点开始。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was sleeping at seven o'clock this morning. 今天早上七点我正在睡觉。
She was cooking dinner at six o'clock last night. 昨晚六点她在做饭。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
By nine o'clock, I had finished my work. 到九点，我已经完成了我的工作。
They had left by ten o'clock. 他们在十点前离开了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been studying since six o'clock when he called. 他打电话的时候，我从六点就开始学习了。
She had been waiting for two hours by the time he arrived at four o'clock. 他四点到达时，她已经等了两个小时。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will be there by five o'clock. 我五点前会到那里。
The shop will close at six o'clock. 商店六点关门。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be working at nine o'clock tomorrow. 我明天九点钟将会在工作。
She will be traveling at this time next week, at around ten o'clock.  下周这个时候，大约十点左右，她将会在旅行。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By six o'clock, I will have finished the project.  到六点，我将完成这个项目。
By eight o'clock, we will have eaten dinner. 到八点，我们将吃完晚饭。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
