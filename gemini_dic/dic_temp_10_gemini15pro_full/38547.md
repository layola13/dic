concluding:/kənˈkluːdɪŋ/
adjective
结束的，结论性的
serving to bring to a close or to a final settlement
The concluding chapter summarized the main points of the book.  结束章节总结了这本书的要点。
近义词: final, closing, last, terminal
反义词: opening, beginning, initial, introductory
词性: adjective


conclude:/kənˈkluːd/
verb
结束；推断出
1. bring or come to an end
2. arrive at a judgment or opinion by reasoning
The meeting concluded with a vote of thanks. 会议以表决感谢结束。
From the evidence, we can conclude that he is guilty. 从证据来看，我们可以断定他有罪。
近义词: finish, end, terminate, deduce, infer
反义词: begin, start, commence
词性: verb

词源: From Late Latin *concludere, from Latin con- + claudere "to close."
