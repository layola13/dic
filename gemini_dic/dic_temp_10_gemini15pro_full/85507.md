hastiest:/ˈheɪstiɪst/| adj.|最仓促的，最匆忙的|Characterized by undue haste and speed; done too quickly.  Showing excessive or undue haste or urgency.|He made the hastiest decision of his life and regretted it immediately. 他做了他人生中最仓促的决定，并立刻后悔了。|近义词：quickest, most hurried, most rushed|反义词：slowest, most deliberate, most leisurely|词性:形容词
