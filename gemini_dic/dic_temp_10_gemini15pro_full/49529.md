demarcated:/ˌdiːˈmɑːrkeɪtɪd/
demarcated|adj.|划定的，界定的|Clearly marked or defined, often by boundaries.|The playing field was clearly demarcated by white lines.  比赛场地用白线清楚地划定了界限。|Synonyms: defined, delineated, bounded, separated, delimited|Antonyms: undefined, unbounded, limitless, vague, blurred
demarcate:/ˈdiːmɑːrkeɪt/
demarcate|v.|划定界限，标明|To set the boundaries or limits of something.|The border between the two countries is demarcated by a river.  两国之间的边界以一条河流为界。|Synonyms: define, delineate, bound, separate, delimit|Antonyms: merge, blend, confuse, obscure
demarcation:/ˌdiːmɑːrˈkeɪʃn/
demarcation|n.|划界，界限|The action of fixing the boundary or limits of something.|The demarcation of the new territories led to disputes. 新领土的划分导致了争端。|Synonyms: definition, delineation, boundary, separation, delimitation|Antonyms:  integration, fusion, blurring
