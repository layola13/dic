micropyle: /ˈmaɪkrəˌpaɪl/ | n. | 珠孔 | A small opening in the surface of an ovule of a seed plant, through which the pollen tube penetrates, often visible as a small pore in the ripe seed. | The pollen tube enters the ovule through the micropyle. 花粉管通过珠孔进入胚珠。| The micropyle is essential for fertilization in plants. 珠孔对植物的受精至关重要。 |  aperture, pore, opening | N/A | noun

micropyle: /ˈmaɪkrəˌpaɪl/ | n. | 卵孔（动物）| A small opening in the membrane of some animal eggs, through which sperm can enter. |  The sperm enters the egg through the micropyle. 精子通过卵孔进入卵子。| The micropyle allows for fertilization in certain animal species.  卵孔使得某些动物物种能够受精。| pore, opening, aperture | N/A | noun
