collusively:/kəˈluːsɪvli/
adverb
串通地，共谋地
in a collusive manner; in secret agreement or cooperation especially for an illegal or deceitful purpose
The two companies collusively fixed prices to eliminate competition.  这两家公司串通一气操纵价格以消除竞争。
近义词: secretly, conspiratorially, fraudulently
反义词: openly, honestly, transparently
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From Latin *collusus, past participle of colludere (“to play together, conspire”), from com- (“together”) + ludere (“to play”).
