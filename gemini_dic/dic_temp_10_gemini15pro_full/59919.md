edentate: /iːˈdɛnteɪt/ | adj. n. | 无齿的，贫齿目动物 | Lacking teeth; belonging to the order Edentata (now Xenarthra), a group of mammals including sloths, anteaters, and armadillos. | The giant anteater is an edentate mammal. 巨食蚁兽是一种无齿的哺乳动物。 |  The museum's collection includes the skeleton of an edentate.  博物馆的藏品包括一个贫齿目动物的骨骼。 | 近义词: toothless | 反义词: toothed | 词性: 形容词, 名词

Etymology: From Latin *edentatus*, past participle of *edentare* "to make toothless," from *e-* "out" + *dentatus* "toothed," from *dens* (genitive *dentis*) "tooth."
