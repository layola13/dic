interpreting:/ɪnˈtɜːrprɪtɪŋ/ |n./ɪnˈtɜːrprɪtɪŋ/ |gerund|解释，翻译，理解|the act of explaining or understanding the meaning of something|例句：Interpreting the data requires specialized knowledge. 解读这些数据需要专业知识。

Interpreting his silence as agreement was a mistake. 把他的沉默理解为同意是个错误。|近义词：explaining, understanding, translating, deciphering|反义词：misinterpreting, misunderstanding|词性: 动名词 (gerund)

interpret:/ɪnˈtɜːrprɪt/ |v. /ɪnˈtɜːrprɪt/|解释，翻译，理解|to explain the meaning of something|例句：How do you interpret this poem? 你是如何解读这首诗的？

She interpreted his silence as a sign of disapproval. 她把他的沉默理解为不赞成。|近义词：explain, understand, translate, decipher|反义词：misinterpret, misunderstand|词性: 动词 (verb)

interpreter:/ɪnˈtɜːrprɪtər/ |n. /ɪnˈtɜːrprɪtər/|口译员，翻译|a person who interprets, especially one who translates orally|例句：The interpreter translated the speech into English. 口译员将演讲翻译成英语。

We needed an interpreter for our meeting with the Chinese delegation. 我们与中国代表团会晤时需要一名口译员。|近义词：translator|反义词：N/A|词性: 名词 (noun)
