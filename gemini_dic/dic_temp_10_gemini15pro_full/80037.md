go viral:/ɡoʊ ˈvaɪrəl/| adj. vi.| 走红，病毒式传播 |To spread rapidly and widely, especially through the internet or social media.  Something goes viral when it's shared by a large number of people online, often quickly and unexpectedly.|The video of the cat playing the piano went viral. 这只猫弹钢琴的视频在网上疯传了。|近义词：spread rapidly, become popular, trend, catch on|反义词: flop, fall flat|verb phrase
