aswing:/əˈswɪŋ/
adj.
摇摆的，摆动的
Moving back and forth or from side to side while suspended or hung.
The pendulum was aswing. 钟摆在摆动。
近义词:swinging, oscillating, swaying
反义词:still, motionless
词性:形容词

aswing:/əˈswɪŋ/
adv.
摇摆地
In a swinging motion.
The gate was aswing. 大门在摇摆。
近义词:swingingly
反义词:motionlessly
词性:副词
