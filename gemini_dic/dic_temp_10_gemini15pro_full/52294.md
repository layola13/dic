dichroous:/ˈdaɪkrəʊəs/ | adj. | 二色的，双色的 | Exhibiting two colors, especially two complementary colors when viewed by transmitted and reflected light. | The gemstone displayed a dichroous effect, appearing blue from one angle and green from another. 宝石呈现出二色性效果，从一个角度看是蓝色，从另一个角度看是绿色。 | dichromatic, birefringent | monochromatic | adjective
