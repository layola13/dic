catechisation:/ˌkætɪkɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 教理问答，教导 | The process of instructing someone in the principles of a Christian religion by means of questions and answers. |  The catechisation of young children is important in many churches.  教堂里的儿童教理问答很重要。 | synonyms: instruction, teaching, indoctrination, education | antonyms:  misinformation, misteaching, neglect | noun

catechize: /ˈkætɪkaɪz/ | v. | 教导，盘问 | To instruct someone in the principles of a Christian religion by means of questions and answers; to question closely or interrogate. | The priest catechized the children before their first communion.  神父在孩子们初领圣餐前对他们进行了教导。| synonyms: instruct, teach, question, interrogate, examine, quiz | antonyms: confuse, mislead, misinform | verb
