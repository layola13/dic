orderliness:/ˈɔːrdərlɪnɪs/
n.
整洁；秩序井然
the quality or state of being orderly
The orderliness of her desk impressed her colleagues. 她的办公桌的整洁给她的同事留下了深刻的印象。

近义词:tidiness, neatness, organization, system
反义词:disorder, mess, chaos, untidiness
