metallic:/məˈtælɪk/| adj.|金属的，金属般的|Made of metal, containing metal, or resembling metal in color, sound, or strength. |例句：The car has a metallic blue paint. 这辆车是金属蓝色漆。|近义词：metal, silvery, steely|反义词:nonmetallic, dull|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *métallique*, from Late Latin *metallicus*, from Latin *metallum* (metal) + *-icus* (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Greek *μέταλλον* (metallon, “mine, quarry, metal”).
