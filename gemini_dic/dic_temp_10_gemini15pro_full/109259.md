lemman:/ˈlɛmən/| n.|引理，辅助定理|A subsidiary proposition assumed to be valid and used to demonstrate a principal proposition.  A lemma is a proven statement used as a stepping-stone toward the proof of a larger result. |Example 1: The mathematician used a lemma to simplify the proof of the theorem.  这位数学家使用了一个引理来简化定理的证明。

Example 2:  This lemma is crucial for proving the main theorem.  这个引理对于证明主要定理至关重要。|Synonym: theorem, proposition, corollary|Antonym: N/A|noun
