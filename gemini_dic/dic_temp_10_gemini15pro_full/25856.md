burinist:/ˈbɜːrɪnɪst/
n.
雕刻师，雕刻匠，(尤指)宝石雕刻匠
A person who engraves, especially one who engraves gems.
The burinist skillfully carved the intricate design into the diamond.  雕刻师巧妙地将复杂的图案雕刻在钻石上。

近义词: engraver, sculptor, carver
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "destroyer" in a figurative sense)
词性: noun
