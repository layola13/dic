dorsiparous: /dɔːrˈsɪpərəs/
adjective
背部产卵的；在背部孵卵的
Producing or carrying young on the back.  Specifically, used to describe certain frogs and toads that carry eggs and/or developing young on their backs.
The Surinam toad (Pipa pipa) is a dorsiparous amphibian, with the female embedding fertilized eggs into her back where they develop into fully formed toadlets.  苏里南蟾蜍（负子蟾）是一种背部产卵的两栖动物，雌性将受精卵嵌入背部，在那里它们发育成完全成形的幼蟾。
The male midwife toad (Alytes obstetricans) carries the fertilized eggs wrapped around his hind legs, not truly dorsiparous but exhibiting a similar form of parental care.  雄性产婆蟾蜍（Alytes obstetricans）将受精卵缠绕在后腿上，并不是真正的背部产卵，但表现出类似的父母照顾形式。
近义词: oviparous (for egg-laying in general)
反义词: viviparous
