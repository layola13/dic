all-of-heap:/ɔːl-əv-hiːp/ | adj. | 倾斜的，摇摇欲坠的；(非正式)筋疲力尽的 |  (informal) In a state of exhaustion or near collapse; also, dilapidated or rickety.  From the nautical term "all of a heap," referring to a ship listing heavily to one side.  | The old barn was all-of-heap and looked ready to fall down.  那个旧谷仓摇摇欲坠，看起来快要倒塌了。 | 近义词：exhausted, worn-out, dilapidated, rickety | 反义词：energetic, robust, sturdy | 词性: 形容词

all-of-heap:/ɔːl-əv-hiːp/ | adj. | (非正式)筋疲力尽的 | (informal) Extremely tired or exhausted. | After a long day of hiking, I was all-of-heap.  经过一整天的徒步旅行，我筋疲力尽。 | 近义词：exhausted, worn-out, spent | 反义词：energetic, refreshed, invigorated | 词性: 形容词
