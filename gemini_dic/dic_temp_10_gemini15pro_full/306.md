aberrationally: /ˌæbəˈreɪʃənəli/
adverb
异常地，反常地
in a way that is not typical or normal; deviating from the usual or natural type
His heart was beating aberrationally. 他的心跳异常。
The system has been behaving aberrationally. 系统一直表现异常。
近义词: abnormally, anomalously, atypically, irregularly, unusually
反义词: normally, typically, usually
词性: adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From aberration + -ally.  Aberration comes from Late Latin aberrātiōn- (stem of aberrātiō ), from aberrāre  "to stray," from ab- "off, away from" + errāre "to wander, err."

USAGE->
This word is less common than its synonyms "abnormally," "anomalously," and "atypically."  It's typically used in more formal or technical contexts.
