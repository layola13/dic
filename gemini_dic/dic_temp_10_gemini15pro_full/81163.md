granddaddy:/ˈɡrændædi/ (US), /ˈɡrændædi/ (UK)| n. |祖父；鼻祖；元老|A grandfather. Also used to refer to something old or original from which other things have developed.|My granddaddy used to tell me stories about his childhood. 我的祖父过去常常给我讲他童年的故事。| The Model T Ford is the granddaddy of all modern cars.  T型福特是所有现代汽车的鼻祖。 |Synonyms: grandfather, forefather, ancestor, originator, progenitor|Antonyms: descendant, offspring|noun
