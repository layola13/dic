amass:/əˈmæs/| v. |积聚，积累|to gather together or accumulate (something, especially a large amount) over a period of time.|He amassed a fortune in real estate. 他在房地产上积累了一笔财富。|synonyms: accumulate, gather, collect, stockpile, hoard|antonyms: disperse, scatter, dissipate, squander|verb

amass:/əˈmæs/| v. |收集，聚集|to collect something, especially in large quantities.|She has amassed a huge collection of antique dolls. 她收集了大量的古董娃娃。|synonyms:  garner, compile, assemble|antonyms: distribute, divide|verb
