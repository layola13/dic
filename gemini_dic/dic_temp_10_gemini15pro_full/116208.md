maslin:/ˈmæslɪn/| n.|混播谷物; 混播作物（尤指小麦和黑麦）; 由混播谷物磨成的面粉; 由这种面粉制成的面包|A mixture of grains, especially wheat and rye, sown and harvested together; flour made from such a mixture; bread made from this flour.|The farmer sowed the field with maslin. 农民在地里播种了混播谷物。|Synonyms: mixed grain, mixed crop|Antonyms: pure wheat, pure rye|noun
