encrypt:/ɪnˈkrɪpt/ /ɛnˈkrɪpt/| vt. |加密，编码|To convert (information or data) into a code, especially to prevent unauthorized access.|She encrypted the file before sending it. 她发送文件前先加密了。|近义词：encode, cipher, code|反义词:decrypt, decode|动词

encrypt:/ɪnˈkrɪpt/ /ɛnˈkrɪpt/| n. |加密，编码（名词形式较少用）|The act of converting information or data into a code.|The encrypt was successful. 加密成功了。（这种用法比较少见）|近义词：encoding, ciphering, coding|反义词:decryption, decoding|名词
