counterirritation:/ˌkaʊntərˌɪrɪˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 对抗刺激，反刺激 | The application of a mild irritant to relieve a more serious irritation in another part of the body.  Applying a mustard plaster to the chest to relieve congestion in the lungs is an example of counterirritation. |  He used a mustard plaster as a counterirritation for his chest cold. 他用芥末膏来对抗胸部感冒引起的刺激。 | synonyms: counter-stimulant, revulsive | antonyms:  irritation, stimulant | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- + irritation.  "Counter-" signifies "against" or "opposed to." "Irritation" refers to inflammation or other discomfort.
