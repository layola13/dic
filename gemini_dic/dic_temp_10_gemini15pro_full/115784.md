词源:
From French marionnette, diminutive of Marion, a pet form of Marie (Mary), applied to the Virgin Mary in medieval miracle plays in which she was frequently represented by a puppet.
来自法语 marionnette，是 Marion 的缩小词，Marion 是 Marie (Mary) 的昵称，用于中世纪的神迹剧中，剧中圣母玛利亚经常由木偶扮演。
