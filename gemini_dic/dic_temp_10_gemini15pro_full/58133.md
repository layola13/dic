dropwort: /ˈdrɒpwɜːt/ (UK), /ˈdrɑːpwɜːrt/ (US) | n. |  假香芹 |  A highly poisonous plant (Oenanthe crocata) of the parsley family, resembling parsnip and having small white flowers. |  Ingestion of dropwort can be fatal.  摄入假香芹可能是致命的。 | The dropwort is also known as hemlock water dropwort. 假香芹也被称为毒芹水落新妇。 | Synonyms: hemlock water dropwort, dead man's fingers | Antonyms: N/A | noun
