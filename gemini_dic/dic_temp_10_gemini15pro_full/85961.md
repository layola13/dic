headquartered:/ˈhedˌkwɔːrtərd/| adj.|总部设在…的；将总部设于…|Having its headquarters in a specified place.  The company is headquartered in New York.  这家公司的总部设在纽约。|例句：The multinational corporation is headquartered in London. 这家跨国公司的总部设在伦敦。|例句：The company, headquartered in Beijing, has branches all over the world.  这家总部位于北京的公司在世界各地都有分公司。|近义词：based, located, situated|反义词: N/A|词性:形容词

Etymology:
headquarter (n.) (1814) from head (n.) + quarter (n.) "assigned station, lodging."  The verb headquarter (1865) is from the noun.
