earmarking: /ˈɪəmɑːkɪŋ/ | n. | 指定用途；专用拨款；预留 | The act of setting aside or reserving something, typically money, for a specific purpose. | The government's earmarking of funds for education has been welcomed by teachers. 政府将资金指定用于教育，受到了教师们的欢迎。|  The company's earmarking of profits for research and development. 公司将利润指定用于研发。 | Synonyms: allocation, appropriation, designation, reservation | Antonyms:  misappropriation, misuse, squandering | noun
