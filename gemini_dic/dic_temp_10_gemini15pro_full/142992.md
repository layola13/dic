outbawl: /ˌaʊtˈbɔːl/
verb
大声哭喊，胜过…的哭喊声
to cry louder than someone else
The baby outbawled his older brother.  这个婴儿比他哥哥哭得更大声。
近义词: outshout, outcry
反义词: whimper, whisper
词性: verb

outbawl: /ˌaʊtˈbɔːl/
noun (rare)
大声哭喊
a loud cry or shout
The outbawl of the child startled the birds.  孩子的哭喊声吓到了鸟儿。
近义词: yell, scream
反义词: murmur, whisper
词性: noun
