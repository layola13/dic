词源:

"Nonrespectably" comes from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") added to the adverb "respectably."  "Respectably" is derived from the adjective "respectable," which in turn comes from the verb "respect." The ultimate origin is the Latin word "respectus," meaning "regard" or "consideration."
