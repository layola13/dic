fracture:/ˈfræktʃər/ (US) /ˈfræktʃə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|骨折；断裂；破裂|The act or process of breaking or the state of being broken.  A break, rupture, or crack, especially in bone or cartilage.|
He suffered a fracture of the skull. 他颅骨骨折了。|
The earthquake caused fractures in the earth's crust. 地震导致地壳断裂。|
synonyms: break, crack, rupture, split, shatter|
antonyms: mend, repair, heal, join|
