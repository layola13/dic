outreach:/ˈaʊtriːtʃ/
outreach|n.|外展，推广；延伸，覆盖范围|The church has an outreach program for the homeless. 教会为无家可归者设立了一个外展项目。|近义词: extension, expansion, service|反义词: withdrawal, retreat|词性:名词

outreach:/ˌaʊtˈriːtʃ/
outreach|v.|接触，联系；超过，胜过|The organization outreached to the community for support. 该组织与社区联系以寻求支持。|近义词: reach out, connect, exceed|反义词: fall short|词性:动词

outreached:/ˌaʊtˈriːtʃt/
outreached|v.|（outreach的过去式和过去分词）接触，联系；超过，胜过|The program outreached to hundreds of families last year. 该项目去年联系了数百个家庭。|近义词: reached out to, connected with, exceeded|反义词: fell short of|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + reach.  "Out-" signifies extending beyond or surpassing. "Reach" refers to the act of extending or stretching. Thus, "outreach" literally means to extend beyond a normal limit or to surpass in reach.
