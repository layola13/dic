hyperphoria:/ˌhaɪpərˈfɔːriə/ | n. | 上隐斜视 | A type of vertical strabismus where one eye tends to deviate upwards when the other eye is covered.  |  The patient's hyperphoria was corrected with prism lenses. 病人的上隐斜视用棱镜镜片矫正了。 | Near synonyms: vertical strabismus, hypertropia | Antonyms: hypotropia | Noun

hyperphoria:/ˌhaɪpərˈfɔːriə/ | n. | 上斜视 | A condition in which one eye looks upward relative to the other eye, often only noticeable when binocular vision is disrupted. | She experienced double vision due to her hyperphoria. 由于上斜视，她出现了复视。 | Near synonyms: vertical deviation, eye misalignment | Antonyms: hypotropia | Noun
