hydrates:/ˈhaɪdreɪts/ /ˈhaɪdrɪts/ | n. |水合物；含水化合物 |A compound containing water molecules combined in a definite ratio as an integral part of the crystal.|Copper sulfate pentahydrate is a common blue crystal used in chemistry demonstrations. 五水合硫酸铜是化学演示中常用的蓝色晶体。|The athlete drank electrolyte hydrates to replenish lost fluids. 运动员喝了电解质水合物来补充流失的体液。|synonyms: compounds, complexes |antonyms: anhydrates| noun
