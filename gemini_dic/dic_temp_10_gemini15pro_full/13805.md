audiphone: /ˈɔːdiˌfoʊn/ (US) /ˈɔːdiəʊfəʊn/ (UK)| n.|助听器|A device worn in or behind the ear to amplify sound for someone who is hard of hearing.|She wears an audiphone because she's partially deaf. 她戴助听器，因为她部分失聪。|Synonym: hearing aid|Antonym: N/A|noun
