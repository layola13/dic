enduringness:/ɪnˈdjʊərɪŋnəs/ /ɪnˈdʊrɪŋnəs/| n. |持久性；耐久性|The enduringness of their love was a testament to their commitment. |例句：The enduringness of the ancient pyramids is remarkable.  古代金字塔的持久性非常显著。

例句：The artist was known for the enduringness of his paintings.  这位艺术家以其画作的耐久性而闻名。|近义词：durability, permanence, longevity, lastingness|反义词:transience, ephemerality, brevity|词性:noun

Etymology->
From endure (v.) + -ing + -ness.  Endure comes from Old French endurer, from Latin indurare "to harden," from in- "in" + durare "to harden, last," from durus "hard."
