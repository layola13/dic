metaldehyde: /ˌmɛtˈæl.dɪ.haɪd/ | n. | 聚乙醛；四聚乙醛 | A cyclic tetramer of acetaldehyde, used as a solid fuel and as a molluscicide. | Slugs and snails can be controlled by the use of metaldehyde.  用聚乙醛可以控制蛞蝓和蜗牛。| Metaldehyde pellets are commonly used in gardens. 聚乙醛颗粒常用于花园。 | synonyms:  molluscicide, slug pellets | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider terms like "snail attractant") | noun
