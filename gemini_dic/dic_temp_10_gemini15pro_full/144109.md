outscolded:/ˌaʊtˈskoʊldɪd/
verb
责骂得比...厉害, 斥责得超过
outdo someone in scolding; scold more vehemently or effectively than another.
She outscolded her neighbor in the argument about the fence. 她在那场关于栅栏的争论中把邻居骂得狗血淋头。
近义词: outargue, outdo, surpass
反义词: lose to, be outdone by
词性: verb (past tense)
