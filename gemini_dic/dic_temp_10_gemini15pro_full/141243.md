op-ed:/ˈɑːp ɛd/ |n.|评论文章；专栏文章|An article that expresses the opinions of a named writer usually not affiliated with the publication's editorial board.|The newspaper published an op-ed by a leading economist on the current financial crisis. 该报发表了一篇由一位著名经济学家撰写的关于当前金融危机的评论文章。|Synonym: opinion piece, commentary, column|Antonym: news report|noun
