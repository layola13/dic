nonchafing:/ˌnɒnˈtʃeɪfɪŋ/
adjective
防磨损的；不磨损的
Designed to prevent chafing; not causing chafing.
This shirt is made of nonchafing material, perfect for long runs. 这件衬衫由防磨损材料制成，非常适合长跑。
These socks are nonchafing and will keep your feet comfortable all day. 这些袜子不磨脚，可以让你的脚一整天都保持舒适。
近义词: chafe-free, anti-chafing
反义词: chafing, abrasive
