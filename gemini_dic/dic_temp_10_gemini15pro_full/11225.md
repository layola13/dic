archivers:/ˈɑːrkaɪvərz/
n.
档案管理员，案卷保管员
People who maintain and manage archives.  Those responsible for preserving historical documents and records.
The archivists carefully cataloged the newly discovered letters. 档案管理员仔细地将新发现的信件编入目录。
The university archivists are responsible for preserving important historical documents. 大学档案管理员负责保存重要的历史文献。
curator, recorder, record keeper
destroyer
