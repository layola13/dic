declaimer:/dɪˈkleɪmər/ /diˈkleɪmər/
noun
朗诵者；演讲者；反对者
a person who recites something, especially poetry, in a dramatic or theatrical way; a person who speaks formally and publicly against something
The declaimer captivated the audience with his powerful voice.  这位朗诵者用他洪亮的声音吸引了观众。
He was a fierce declaimer against the new law. 他是这项新法律的强烈反对者。
synonym: reciter, orator, speaker, opponent, objector
antonym: supporter, proponent
n.
