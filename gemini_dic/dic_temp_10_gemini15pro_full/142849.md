out-glooms:/ˌaʊtˈɡluːmz/
verb
使…更显阴暗；使…黯然失色
to be gloomier than; to surpass in gloominess.
The storm clouds out-gloomed the already gray sky.  暴风云使已经灰蒙蒙的天空更加阴暗。
The actor's performance out-gloomed all the others. 这位演员的表演使所有其他演员黯然失色。
近义词: overshadow, eclipse, darken
反义词: brighten, outshine, illuminate
词性: verb
