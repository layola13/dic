atrous: /ˈeɪtrəs/ | adj. |多孔的，空洞的; (信号处理)带孔的 | Having holes or cavities; (in signal processing) with holes or gaps, typically referring to a filter or convolution operation where the kernel elements are spaced apart. |  Atrous convolution is used in deep learning to increase the receptive field of filters without increasing the number of parameters.  带孔卷积用于深度学习中，在不增加参数数量的情况下增加过滤器的感受野。|  The atrous algorithm helps preserve details in upsampled images.  带孔算法有助于保留上采样图像中的细节。 | porous, perforated, holey | dense, solid | adjective
