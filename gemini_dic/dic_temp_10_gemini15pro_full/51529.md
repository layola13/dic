detruncate:/diːˈtrʌŋkeɪt/
verb
去截断，恢复原状
To restore something to its original length or form, especially after it has been shortened or truncated.
The historian detruncated the ancient manuscript, revealing the missing verses. 历史学家复原了古代手稿，揭示了缺失的诗句。
近义词:restore, lengthen, extend, expand
反义词:truncate, shorten, abbreviate, curtail
词性:v.
