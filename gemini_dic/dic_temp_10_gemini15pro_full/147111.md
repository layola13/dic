overliteral:/ˌoʊvərˈlɪtərəl/| adj.|过于字面的，过于拘泥字面的|Adhering too closely to the literal meaning of something; lacking flexibility in interpretation.|He took the joke too overliterally and got offended. 他对这个笑话理解得太字面化了，所以生气了。|近义词：literal-minded, inflexible, rigid|反义词：flexible, nuanced, figurative|词性:形容词

Etymology: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "literal" (from Latin "litteralis" meaning "of or belonging to letters")
