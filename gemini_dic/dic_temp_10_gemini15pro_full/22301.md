bonhomie:/ˌbɒnəˈmiː/ (US) /ˌbɑːnəˈmiː/| n.|友好；和蔼；温厚|Good-natured, easy friendliness or conviviality.|He exuded an air of bonhomie.|近义词：geniality, affability, cordiality, amiability|反义词：hostility, coldness, unfriendliness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From French bonhomie, from bonhomme "good fellow," from bon "good" + homme "man." Ultimately from Latin bonus "good" and homō "man."
