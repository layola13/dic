iterating:/ˈɪtəreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 迭代的，重复的 |  The act of repeating a process, often with slight changes each time to improve or refine the outcome. |  The program is iterating through the data to find patterns. 程序正在迭代数据以查找模式。|  repeating, cyclical, recursive | non-iterative, single, one-time | adjective


iterating:/ˈɪtəreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 迭代，重复 |  To perform or utter repeatedly. Often used in computing contexts to describe a process that repeats a set of instructions. |  We are iterating on the design based on user feedback. 我们正在根据用户反馈迭代设计。 |  repeating, cycling, reiterating | stopping, halting, concluding | verb
