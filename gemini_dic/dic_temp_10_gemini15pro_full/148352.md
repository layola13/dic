overspeculating: /ˌoʊvərˈspɛkjəˌleɪtɪŋ/
verb
过度推测，过分臆测
Engaging in excessive speculation; conjecturing or theorizing too much, often based on insufficient evidence.
He was overspeculating about the company's future and lost a lot of money. 他对公司的未来过度推测，损失了很多钱。
The media is overspeculating on the cause of the accident. 媒体对事故原因进行了过多的猜测。
近义词: overthinking, conjecturing, surmising
反义词: underestimating, downplaying
词性: verb (present participle)
