floppily:/ˈflɒpɪli/ (US) /ˈflɒpɪli/ (UK)| adv.| 松散地，软弱无力地 |In a loose and flexible manner; limply. |The dog's ears hung floppily. 狗的耳朵耷拉着。| The flag hung floppily from the pole. 旗帜无力地挂在旗杆上。| 近义词：limply, loosely, slackly | 反义词：firmly, tightly, rigidly | 词性: 副词

词源: From the adjective "floppy" + the adverbial suffix "-ly."  "Floppy" originates from the verb "flop," which imitates the sound and movement of something falling loosely.
