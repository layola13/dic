hatlessnesses:/ˈhæt.ləs.nəs.ɪz/
n.
无帽状态；未戴帽子的情况
The state or quality of being without a hat or hats.
The hatlessnesses of the attendees at the royal wedding caused a stir.  与会者在皇家婚礼上未戴帽子的情况引起了轰动。
Near Synonyms:  uncovered heads, bareheadedness
Antonyms: hattedness (rarely used), wearing hats
词性: noun
