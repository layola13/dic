epitaphical:/ˌɛpɪˈtæfɪk(ə)l/
adjective
墓志铭的；适合做墓志铭的
Of, relating to, or resembling an epitaph.  Suitable for an epitaph; commemorative.
The poem had an epitaphical tone, reflecting on mortality and loss. 这首诗有一种墓志铭的基调，反思着死亡和失去。
His speech was strangely epitaphical, as if he were summing up his life's work. 他的演讲出奇地像一篇墓志铭，仿佛在总结他一生的工作。
synonyms: commemorative, memorial, elegiac, funereal
antonyms: celebratory, joyous, lively
