overcluttered:/ˌoʊvərˈklʌtərd/
adjective
过于杂乱的，堆满东西的
Excessively filled or covered with clutter; too full of objects.
The room was so overcluttered that it was difficult to move around. 这个房间太乱了，难以走动。
The attic was overcluttered with old furniture and boxes. 阁楼里堆满了旧家具和箱子。
近义词: cluttered, crammed, overcrowded, overflowing
反义词: tidy, organized, neat, minimalist
词性: adjective
词源: From "over-" (excessively) + "cluttered" (filled with clutter).  "Clutter" ultimately derives from Middle English "clotteren" (to form clots or lumps).
