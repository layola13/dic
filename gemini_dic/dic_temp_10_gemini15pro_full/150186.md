pancaking:/ˈpænˌkeɪkɪŋ/| n.| 煎饼状; 层叠; (直升机)下降时旋转|The helicopter experienced pancaking during the emergency landing. 直升机在紧急着陆时出现了煎饼状下降。|近义词：stacking, layering|反义词: (For the helicopter meaning) controlled descent|词性:名词

pancaking:/ˈpænˌkeɪkɪŋ/| v.|(使)成煎饼状; (使)层叠; (指直升机)下降时旋转|The soil pancaked after the heavy rain.  大雨过后，土壤变得像煎饼一样扁平。|近义词：flattening, collapsing|反义词: (For the helicopter meaning) ascending|词性:动词

词源: From "pancake" + "-ing," denoting the action or result of becoming like a pancake.
