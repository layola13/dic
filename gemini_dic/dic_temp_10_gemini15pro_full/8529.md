anticensoriousnesses:/ˌæntisɛnsɔːriəsnəsəz/
n.
不爱挑剔；宽容
lack of censoriousness; tolerance.
Their anticensoriousnesses allowed for a diverse range of opinions within the group.  他们的宽容使得团体内部可以容纳各种不同的观点。
The teacher's anticensoriousness created a relaxed classroom atmosphere. 老师的不爱挑剔营造了轻松的课堂氛围。
近义词: tolerance, forbearance, leniency, indulgence
反义词: censoriousness, criticalness, fault-finding,苛刻, 吹毛求疵
词性: noun
