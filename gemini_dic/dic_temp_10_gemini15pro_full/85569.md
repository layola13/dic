hater:/ˈheɪtər/| n.|憎恨者，仇恨者|A person who greatly dislikes a specified person or thing.|She's a hater of anything sweet. 她讨厌任何甜食。|Synonyms: enemy, antagonist, foe|Antonyms: admirer, supporter, lover|noun

hater:/ˈheɪtər/| n.|讨厌的人，憎恶者|Someone who expresses or feels extreme dislike.|Online haters can be very cruel. 网上的喷子可以非常残忍。|Synonyms: critic, detractor, faultfinder|Antonyms: fan, devotee, enthusiast|noun
