arrowworm:/ˈæroʊwɜːrm/
n.
箭虫
A small, transparent, predatory marine worm with horizontal fins and grasping spines around the mouth, belonging to the phylum Chaetognatha.
Arrowworms are important members of the zooplankton. 箭虫是浮游动物的重要成员。
Arrowworms prey on copepods and other small crustaceans. 箭虫捕食桡足类和其他小型甲壳类动物。
近义词:chaetognath
反义词:None
词性:noun
