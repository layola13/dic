inermis:/ɪˈnɜːrmɪs/
adjective
无刺的， unarmed, without spines/thorns
Latin origin: in- (not) + arma (arms) meaning "without arms/weapons",  referring to plants lacking thorns or spines.
This rose cultivar is described as inermis, making it safe for handling.  这种玫瑰品种被描述为无刺的，因此可以安全地进行处理。
The inermis variety of the acacia tree is popular in landscaping.  这种无刺的金合欢树品种在园林绿化中很受欢迎。
近义词:unarmed, spineless, thornless
反义词:armed, spiny, thorny
词性:adjective
