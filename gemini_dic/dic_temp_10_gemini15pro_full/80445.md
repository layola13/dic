goners:/ˈɡɒnəz/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːnərz/ (US)| n.|注定要失败的人；注定要完蛋的人；没救的人|People who are goners are certain to die or to fail.|The wounded soldiers were all goners.  那些受伤的士兵都没救了。|
The company's a goner if they don't get new investment. 如果他们得不到新的投资，这家公司就完蛋了。|synonyms: doomed, finished, lost cause|antonyms: safe, secure|noun
