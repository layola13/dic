aristotelianism:/ˌærɪstəˈtiːliənɪzəm/
n.
亚里士多德主义
The philosophy of Aristotle and his followers.  It emphasizes logic, observation, and classification, and encompasses a wide range of subjects, including metaphysics, physics, ethics, politics, and aesthetics.
例句：The professor's lecture focused on the core tenets of Aristotelianism. 教授的讲座集中讨论了亚里士多德主义的核心原则。

例句：Medieval scholasticism was heavily influenced by Aristotelianism. 中世纪的经院哲学深受亚里士多德主义的影响。

近义词：peripateticism
反义词:  platonism (often considered a contrasting philosophy)
词性: noun
