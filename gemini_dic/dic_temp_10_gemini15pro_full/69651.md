fence:/fɛns/ | n. vt. |围栏，栅栏；击剑|A barrier enclosing or bordering a field, yard, etc., usually made of posts and wire or wood; the art or sport of fighting with swords. |例句：The farmer built a fence around his field. 农民在他的田地周围建了一道栅栏。|近义词：barrier, railing, enclosure, hedge, wall |反义词：opening, gap |词性: noun, verb

fence:/fɛns/ | vt. | ограждать забором; заниматься фехтованием|To enclose with a fence; to practice the art of fencing. |例句：They fenced the yard to keep the dog in. 他们用栅栏围住院子以防狗跑出去。|近义词：enclose, surround, protect |反义词：open, expose |词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fens*, from Old French *defens*, from *defendre* ("to defend"), from Latin *dēfendere* (“to ward off, defend”).  Ultimately from *dē* (“down, away”) + *fendere* (“to strike, push”).
