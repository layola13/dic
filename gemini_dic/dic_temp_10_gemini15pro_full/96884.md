inexpectant:/ˌɪnɪkˈspɛktənt/
adjective
未料到的，没指望的，想不到的
Not expecting something to happen; not aware of something that will happen.
She gave an inexpectant shrug. 她漫不经心地耸了耸肩。
Near Synonyms:  unaware, unconscious, oblivious, unsuspecting
Antonyms: expectant, anticipating, waiting for
词性: 形容词
