hooves:/huːvz/| n. |蹄子|The hard covering on the feet of certain animals, such as horses, cows, and deer.  It is the plural form of "hoof".|The horse's hooves clattered on the cobblestones. 马的蹄子在鹅卵石路上发出嗒嗒的响声。|synonyms: feet (in this context),  ungulate feet|antonyms:  (no direct antonym, perhaps paw/claw for contrasting animal feet)|noun
