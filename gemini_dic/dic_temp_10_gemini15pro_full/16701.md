barrier:/ˈbæriər/ (US), /ˈbæriə(r)/ (UK)| n.|障碍; 屏障; 阻碍; 隔阂|Something that prevents or blocks movement, progress, or access.  A physical object or a non-physical obstacle such as a rule or policy.|The language barrier made it difficult to communicate.  语言障碍使得交流变得困难。|Synonyms: obstacle, obstruction, impediment, hurdle|Antonyms: opening, access, passage|noun

barrier:/ˈbæriər/ (US), /ˈbæriə(r)/ (UK)| v.|设置障碍; 封锁|To block or prevent access to something.|Police barriers were erected around the crime scene.  警方在犯罪现场周围设置了路障。|Synonyms: block, obstruct, barricade|Antonyms: open, clear, free|verb
