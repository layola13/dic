heterogenous:/ˌhɛtərəˈdʒiːniəs/ | adj. |异质的，异种的，由不同成分组成的|Diverse in character or content; composed of different elements.|The city is a heterogenous mix of cultures and ethnicities. 这个城市是文化和种族的异质混合体。|例句：The study sample was heterogenous, including people of different ages, backgrounds, and health conditions. 研究样本是异质的，包括不同年龄、背景和健康状况的人。

例句：The heterogenous nature of the group made it difficult to reach a consensus.  该小组异质的性质使得难以达成共识。

|近义词：diverse, varied, miscellaneous, assorted, mixed|反义词：homogeneous, uniform, similar|词性:形容词
