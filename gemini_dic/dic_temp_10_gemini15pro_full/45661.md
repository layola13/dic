curtail:/kərˈteɪl/
verb
缩减，削减
reduce in extent or quantity; impose a restriction on.
例句：Budget cuts have drastically curtailed library services. 预算削减严重缩减了图书馆服务。

例句：She had to curtail her visit because of the emergency. 由于紧急情况，她不得不缩短访问时间。

近义词：reduce, shorten, decrease, diminish, restrict, limit
反义词：increase, expand, extend, lengthen, prolong
词性：verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *curtailler, from curt "short" (from Latin curtus) + -ailler (suffix forming verbs).
