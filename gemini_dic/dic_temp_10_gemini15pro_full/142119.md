orig:/wɒt/ /hwɑːt/ |pron.|什么，多么| Used to ask for information about something. | What is your name? 你叫什么名字？| What color is the sky? 天空是什么颜色？ |which, who | none | pronoun


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she think about the new policy? 她对新政策有什么看法？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done to deserve this award? 她做了什么值得这个奖项？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading this week? 他这周一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last night? 你昨晚做了什么？
What happened at the party? 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting? 会议前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the accident? 事故发生前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen next? 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你会完成什么？
What will have changed by then? 到那时会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2030? 到2030年你将已经做了什么十年了？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
