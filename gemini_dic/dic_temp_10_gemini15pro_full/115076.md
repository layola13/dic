maneuverable:/məˈnuːvərəb(ə)l/ /məˈnuːvrəbəl/
adjective
易操纵的；灵活的
Easy to steer or manipulate; readily handled or controlled.
The car was small and maneuverable, perfect for city driving. 这辆车小巧灵活，非常适合在城市里驾驶。
The new fighter jet is highly maneuverable.  这架新型战斗机非常灵活。
近义词:agile, nimble, controllable, steerable, flexible
反义词:clumsy, awkward, unwieldy, inflexible, rigid
词性:adjective
词源:From French *manœuvrable*, from *manœuvrer*  "to maneuver,"  from *manœuvre* "maneuver."
