ectropium:/ɛkˈtroʊpiəm/
n.
外翻，外翻症
the turning outward of an edge or margin, as of an eyelid or lip.
Ectropium of the eyelid can cause dryness and irritation. 眼睑外翻会导致干燥和刺激。
近义词:eversion
反义词:entropion
词性:noun

Etymology: From Greek *ektrope*,  "a turning out" + -ium.  *ek* "out" + *trepein* "to turn."
