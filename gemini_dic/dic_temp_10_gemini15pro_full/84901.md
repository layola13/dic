hankers:/ˈhæŋkərz/| v.|渴望，热切希望|To have a strong desire for something.|She hankers for a life of adventure. 她渴望冒险的生活。|近义词：yearns, longs, craves|反义词:dislikes, hates|verb (third-person singular simple present hankers, present participle hankering, simple past hankered, past participle hankered)

hankering:/ˈhæŋkərɪŋ/| n.|渴望，热切的愿望|A strong desire for something.|He had a hankering for a big slice of chocolate cake. 他渴望吃一大块巧克力蛋糕。|近义词：yearning, longing, craving|反义词:aversion, dislike|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hankeren, frequentative of hanken (“to hang”), of uncertain origin. Perhaps related to hang, in the sense of "linger, dwell on."
