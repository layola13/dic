misshipment:/ˌmɪsˈʃɪpmənt/
n.
错误装运；货物误运
the act of shipping something to the wrong place or to the wrong person.
The misshipment of the goods caused a delay in production. 货物误运导致生产延误。
近义词：misdelivery, wrong delivery
反义词：correct shipment, proper delivery
词性: 名词

Etymology:
"mis-" (prefix meaning "wrong" or "bad") + "shipment" (noun meaning "the act of shipping goods")
