improvabilities:/ɪmˌpruːvəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |可改进性；改进的可能性|The improvabilities of the system are numerous, offering many opportunities for optimization.  该系统的可改进性有很多，提供了许多优化的机会。|例句：The report highlighted the improvabilities of the current manufacturing process.  报告强调了当前制造工艺的可改进性。

The improvabilities of the education system are being explored by the government. 政府正在探索教育系统的可改进性。|近义词：potentialities, possibilities,  capabilities|反义词: limitations, weaknesses,  incapabilities|词性:名词

词源: improvability (可改进性) + -s (复数后缀)  来自 improve (改进) + -able (可...的)  最终源自拉丁语 probus (好的, 诚实的).
