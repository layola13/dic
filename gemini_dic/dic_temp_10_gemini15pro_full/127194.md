naturism:/ˈneɪtʃərɪzəm/
n.
裸体主义，自然主义
The practice of going without clothes, especially in designated areas, for reasons of health or personal freedom.  /  出于健康或个人自由的原因不穿衣服，尤其是在指定区域。
Some people find naturism liberating.  /  有些人觉得裸体主义很解放。
Naturism is practiced at several beaches along this coast. /  沿着这条海岸的几个海滩都有人在实行裸体主义。
近义词:nudism
反义词:prudery
词性:noun
