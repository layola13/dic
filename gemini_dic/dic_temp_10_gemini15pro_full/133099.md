nonerotic:/ˌnɒnɪˈrɒtɪk/ | adj. | 非色情的 | Not of, relating to, or intended to arouse sexual desire. |  The museum exhibit featured nonerotic nude paintings focusing on the human form.  博物馆展览的特色是非色情裸体画，专注于人体形态。 | The movie was a nonerotic portrayal of love and loss.  这部电影是对爱与失去的非色情描写。 | 近义词: nonsexual, asexual, platonic | 反义词: erotic, sexual | 词性: adjective
词源:  From non- + erotic.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "erotic" comes from the Greek "erōtikos," meaning "of or pertaining to love, especially sexual love."
