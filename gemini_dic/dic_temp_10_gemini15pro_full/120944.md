miscensured: /ˌmɪsˈsɛnʃərd/
词性: adjective
中文翻译: 错误地谴责的；不应受谴责的
英文解析: wrongly censured; undeserving of censure.  Censure implies strong disapproval and criticism.  "Miscensured" suggests that this criticism was unjustified or misplaced.
例句：The miscensured employee was eventually vindicated when the true culprit was found.  被错误谴责的员工最终被证明是清白的，因为真正的罪魁祸首被找到了。

例句：Despite being miscensured by the media, the politician continued to stand by his principles. 尽管被媒体错误地谴责，这位政治家仍然坚持自己的原则。

近义词：wrongfully accused, unjustly criticized, unfairly blamed
反义词：rightly censured, justly criticized, deservedly blamed
