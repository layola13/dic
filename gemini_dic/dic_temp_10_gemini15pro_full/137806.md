nonveritably:/ˌnɒnvəˈrɪtəbli/
adv. |非真实地，不确凿地|Not truly; not in a way that corresponds to reality or truth.|His claims, while presented confidently, were nonveritably portrayed, omitting key details that contradicted his narrative. 他虽然自信地提出了自己的主张，但却并非真实地描绘了情况，省略了与其叙述相矛盾的关键细节。| The politician nonveritably described the economic situation, painting a rosy picture that ignored the rising unemployment rate.  这位政客没有如实地描述经济形势，描绘了一幅忽略了不断上升的失业率的乐观景象。 | 近义词:falsely, untruly, spuriously, inaccurately | 反义词: truly, veritably, accurately, genuinely|词性:副词
