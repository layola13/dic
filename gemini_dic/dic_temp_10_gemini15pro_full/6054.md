amended:/əˈmɛndɪd/
adjective
修改的，修正的
Corrected or revised.
The contract was amended to include the new clause.  合同被修改以包含新的条款。
amended tax returns  修改过的纳税申报表
近义词: revised, corrected, altered, modified
反义词: original, unchanged, unaltered
词性: adjective

词源: From the Latin "emendare" meaning "to free from fault, correct."  来自拉丁语 "emendare"，意思是“免于错误，改正”。
