borings:/ˈbɔːrɪŋz/ | n. |  (通常指金属或木头上的)屑，碎渣 |  Metal borings littered the floor of the workshop.  金属碎屑散落在车间的地板上。| 近义词：shavings, filings, chips, debris | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词


词源:  From the verb "bore" (钻孔) which comes from Old English *borian, of Germanic origin, related to Dutch boren and German bohren.
