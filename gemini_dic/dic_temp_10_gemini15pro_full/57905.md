driftway:/ˈdrɪftweɪ/ | n. | 小路，羊肠小道 | A narrow path or road, especially one made by the passage of animals or people. |  The sheep followed the driftway across the moor. 羊群沿着小路穿过荒野。| Synonyms: path, track, trail, byway | Antonyms: highway, main road | noun
