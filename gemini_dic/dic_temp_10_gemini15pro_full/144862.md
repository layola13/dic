over-practices:/ˌoʊvərˈpræktɪsɪz/ | v. | 过度练习；练习过多 | To practice something excessively, often to the point of detriment. | She over-practices the piano and now has tendinitis in her wrist. 她过度练习钢琴，现在手腕得了腱鞘炎。| He over-practices his golf swing, neglecting other aspects of his game. 他过度练习高尔夫挥杆，而忽略了比赛的其他方面。|近义词：overtrain, overdo | 反义词: under-practice |词性:动词

over-practices:/ˌoʊvərˈpræktɪsɪz/ | n. | 过度练习；练习过多的行为 | The act of practicing something excessively. | Her over-practices led to injury. 她的过度练习导致了受伤。| The coach warned the team against over-practices. 教练警告队员不要过度练习。|近义词：overtraining |反义词: under-practicing |词性:名词
