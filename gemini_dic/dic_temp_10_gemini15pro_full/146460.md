overfrightens:/ˌoʊvərˈfraɪtnz/| v. |过度惊吓|To frighten someone excessively.|The loud noise overfrightened the children. 巨大的噪音过度惊吓了孩子们。|The sudden appearance of the dog overfrightened her. 狗的突然出现把她吓坏了。|Synonyms: terrify, scare, alarm, startle|Antonyms: comfort, soothe, calm|verb
