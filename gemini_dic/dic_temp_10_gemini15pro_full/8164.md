anti-cruelty:/ˌæntiˈkruːəlti/
|adj.|
反对虐待的；防止虐待的|
Opposing or intended to prevent cruelty to animals or people.|
The anti-cruelty society works to protect animals from abuse.  这个反虐待协会致力于保护动物免受虐待。
The organization promotes anti-cruelty legislation.  该组织推动反虐待立法。|
近义词:humane, compassionate|
反义词:cruel, inhumane|
词性:adjective


anti-cruelty:/ˌæntiˈkruːəlti/
|n.|
反对虐待；防止虐待|
The act or practice of opposing cruelty.|
Anti-cruelty towards animals is gaining momentum.  反对虐待动物的运动正在 gaining momentum。
Her lifelong dedication to anti-cruelty earned her numerous awards. 她毕生致力于反虐待事业，赢得了无数奖项。|
近义词:humaneness, compassion|
反义词:cruelty, inhumanity|
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- "against" + cruelty.  "Cruelty" comes from Old French "crualte" derived from Latin "crudus" meaning "raw, unripe, harsh".
