backlash:/ˈbæklæʃ/| n. vi.|强烈抵制；反冲；后座力；激烈反应|A strong negative reaction by a large number of people, especially to a social or political change.|The government faced a backlash from voters over the new tax. 政府面临选民对新税的强烈反对。|Synonyms: reaction, resistance, opposition, counterblast, recoil|Antonyms: support, approval|noun

backlash:/ˈbæklæʃ/| vi.|发生强烈抵制；产生反冲；后座；反抗|To react strongly against a social or political change.|The policy backlashed against the government. 这项政策引起了对政府的强烈反对。|Synonyms: react, resist, oppose|Antonyms: support, approve|verb
