antinaturalism:/ˌæntɪˈnætʃrəlɪzəm/
n.|反自然主义|A philosophical viewpoint, especially in art and literature, that rejects the idea that nature is the ultimate reality and source of value, often emphasizing the role of human constructs or the supernatural.|The play’s antinaturalism was evident in its stylized dialogue and unrealistic setting.|近义词:artificiality, constructivism|反义词:naturalism, realism|词性:noun

Etymology:
anti- (prefix meaning "against, opposite of") + naturalism
