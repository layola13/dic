ashore:/əˈʃɔːr/
adjective|在岸上的，向岸上|On the shore; on land after being at sea.|The sailors swam ashore after the shipwreck. 海员们在船失事后游上了岸。|近义词：aground, beached, landed|反义词：aboard, afloat, offshore
adverb|在岸上，向岸上|Towards or onto the shore; on land.|The lifeboat brought the survivors ashore. 救生艇把幸存者们带上了岸。|近义词：landward|反义词：seaward, overboard
