braze:/breɪz/| v. n.|铜焊，硬焊；使变得厚脸皮；|To join two pieces of metal together using a brass alloy as solder; To become or make someone hardened to unpleasantness.|例句：The pipes were brazed together. 这些管道是用铜焊接在一起的。

例句：Years of war had brazed the soldiers to violence. 多年的战争使士兵们对暴力麻木了。|近义词：solder, weld, fuse, join, harden, inure|反义词:separate, soften|词性:动词, 名词

braze:/breɪz/| n.|铜焊料|A brass alloy used as solder.|例句：The plumber used braze to join the pipes. 水管工用铜焊料连接管道。

例句：He bought a roll of braze for the repair job. 他买了一卷铜焊料来进行修理工作。|近义词：solder, filler metal|反义词:N/A|词性:名词
