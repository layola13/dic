e-tailing:/ˈiːteɪlɪŋ/
noun
电子零售
The selling of goods or services directly to consumers over the Internet.
Amazon is a giant in the world of e-tailing. 亚马逊是电子零售业的巨头。
E-tailing offers consumers the convenience of shopping from home. 电子零售为消费者提供了在家购物的便利。
online retailing, internet retailing
brick-and-mortar retailing
