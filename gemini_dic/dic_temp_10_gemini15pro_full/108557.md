lazying:/ˈleɪziɪŋ/ | v. | 懒惰，无所事事 |  The act of being lazy; spending time idly. | He was lazying around the house all day.  他整天在家无所事事。|Synonyms: lounging, idling, relaxing, loafing | Antonyms: working, striving, hustling | Verb (present participle)


Etymology -> From lazy + -ing.  "Lazy" comes from Middle English *lazi, from Old English *læsig.
