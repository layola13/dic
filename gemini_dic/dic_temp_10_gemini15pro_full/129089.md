nitroso:/naɪˈtroʊsoʊ/ /naɪˈtrɒsəʊ/
adjective
亚硝基的
Containing the NO group, where nitrogen is bonded to oxygen.
Nitroso compounds are organic compounds containing the nitroso functional group (-N=O).  They are often intensely colored and can be quite reactive.

Nitrosamines are formed from nitrites and secondary amines and are known to be carcinogenic. 亚硝胺由亚硝酸盐和仲胺形成，已知具有致癌性。

Sodium nitrite is used in curing meat to inhibit the growth of Clostridium botulinum, the bacterium that causes botulism. 亚硝酸钠用于腌制肉类，以抑制导致肉毒中毒的肉毒杆菌的生长。

Near nitroso compounds:  nitrite, nitrate, nitric oxide.
Opposite: No direct antonym, but consider "denitrosated" (having the nitroso group removed).
词性: adjective
