cul-de-sacs:/ˈkʌl də sæks/ /ˈkʊl də sæks/| n.|死胡同，尽头路|A street or passage closed at one end.|We live in a quiet cul-de-sac. 我们住在一条安静的死胡同里。|The police chased the suspect into a cul-de-sac. 警察把嫌疑人追到了一条死胡同里。|Synonyms: dead end, blind alley|Antonyms: through street, thoroughfare|Noun

cul-de-sac:/ˈkʌl də sæk/ /ˈkʊl də sæk/| n.|困境，僵局|A situation in which further progress is impossible.|The negotiations have reached a cul-de-sac. 谈判已陷入僵局。|His career was at a cul-de-sac. 他的事业陷入了困境。|Synonyms: impasse, stalemate|Antonyms: breakthrough, progress|Noun
