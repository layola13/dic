etymology: /ˌetɪˈmɒlədʒi/ | n. | 词源；词源学 | The study of the origin of words and the way in which their meanings have changed throughout history. | The etymology of the word "nice" traces back to the Latin word "nescius," meaning "ignorant."  “nice”这个词的词源可以追溯到拉丁语单词“nescius”，意思是“无知的”。 | synonyms: derivation, origin, root | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for etymology exist.) | noun
