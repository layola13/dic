outsmells:/ˌaʊtˈsmɛlz/
verb
胜过…的气味; 比…的气味更好闻
surpasses something else in smell, has a more pleasant smell than something else
例句：This new air freshener outsmells all the others I've tried.  这款新的空气清新剂比我试过的所有其他空气清新剂都好闻。

例句：The roses outsmell the lilies in the garden.  花园里玫瑰花的香味胜过百合花。
近义词:out-fragrances, smells better than
反义词:is outsmelled by, smells worse than
词性:verb
