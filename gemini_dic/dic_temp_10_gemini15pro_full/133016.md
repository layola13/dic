nonenticingly:/ˌnɑːnɛnˈtaɪsɪŋli/
adjective
没有吸引力地；引不起兴趣地
In a way that does not attract or interest.
The food was presented nonenticingly on the plate.  食物被毫无吸引力地摆放在盘子里。
The story was told nonenticingly, and I quickly lost interest.  这个故事讲得很枯燥，我很快就失去了兴趣。
Synonyms: unattractively, dully, boringly, tediously, uninterestingly
Antonyms: enticingly, attractively, interestingly, captivatingly
词性: 副词 (adverb)
