Hemostatics were quickly applied to the wound. 止血药迅速应用于伤口。|近义词：coagulants, styptics|反义词:|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From hemo- (blood) + -static (causing to stand still).  Ultimately from Greek haima (blood) and statikos (causing to stand).
