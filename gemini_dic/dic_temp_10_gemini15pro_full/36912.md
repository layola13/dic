colorizes:/ˈkʌləˌraɪzɪz/
verb
为...着色，给...上色
adds color to something, typically a black and white film or photograph.
The software colorizes old black and white photos. 该软件可以给黑白老照片上色。
tints, dyes, paints
bleaches, fades
