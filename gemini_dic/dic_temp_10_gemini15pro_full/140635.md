olympianism: /oʊˈlɪmpiənɪzəm/
n.
奥林匹克精神; 奥林匹克主义
The set of values associated with the Olympic Games, including sportsmanship, fair play, and the pursuit of excellence.  It also encompasses the educational and cultural aspects of the Games.
He embodies the true spirit of Olympianism.  他体现了真正的奥林匹克精神。
近义词: sportsmanship, fair play, athleticism
反义词: cheating, dishonesty, corruption
词性: noun

Etymology:  From "Olympian" + "-ism." "Olympian" refers to the Olympic Games, which were held in ancient Greece at Olympia.  "-ism" is a suffix forming nouns meaning a doctrine, belief, or practice.
