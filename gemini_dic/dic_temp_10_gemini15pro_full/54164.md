disinter:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtɜːr/ (US) /ˌdɪsɪnˈtɜː/ (UK)| vt.|掘出; 挖出;  |To dig up something that has been buried, especially a corpse. |例句：The bodies were disinterred for forensic examination. 尸体被挖出来进行法医检查。

例句：Archaeologists disinterred an ancient city buried by volcanic ash. 考古学家挖出了一座被火山灰掩埋的古城。

近义词：exhume, unearth, uncover|反义词: bury, inter|词性:动词


disinter:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtɜːr/ (US) /ˌdɪsɪnˈtɜː/ (UK)| vt.|使(被遗忘或被掩藏的东西)重现;  |To bring something back into notice or use after it has been forgotten or ignored. |例句：He disinterred a forgotten law from the archives. 他从档案中挖掘出一条被遗忘的法律。

例句：She disinterred old family photos from the attic. 她从阁楼里翻出了旧的家庭照片。

近义词：unearth, resurrect, revive|反义词: conceal, hide|词性:动词
