drag-around:/ˈdræɡ əˈraʊnd/ | adj. n.| 拖来拖去的，累赘的；拖油瓶 |Something that is a drag-around is inconvenient to carry or deal with, often because it is heavy or cumbersome. It can also refer to a person, especially a child, who is considered a burden or inconvenience. |  That old suitcase is a real drag-around.  那个旧手提箱真是个累赘。 | Synonyms: burden, encumbrance, nuisance, inconvenience | Antonyms: asset, help, advantage | adjective, noun
