etch:/etʃ/ | v. |蚀刻；铭刻；留下深刻印象|To cut into a surface, especially metal or glass, by means of acid or a sharp instrument; to impress deeply on the memory. |The artist etched the design onto the copper plate. 艺术家把设计蚀刻在铜板上。

The experience was etched in her memory. 这段经历深深地印在了她的记忆里。| 近义词：engrave, carve, inscribe | 反义词: erase, obliterate |词性：动词

词源：From Middle Dutch etsen, frequentative of eten "to eat."
