aerobiologist:/ˌɛəroʊbaɪˈɑːlədʒɪst/ /ˌeərəʊbaɪˈɒlədʒɪst/| n.| 空气生物学家|A scientist who studies airborne organisms, such as pollen, spores, and microorganisms, and their effects on the environment and living things.|The aerobiologist collected air samples to analyze the pollen count.  空气生物学家收集空气样本以分析花粉数量。

The aerobiologist studied the spread of fungal spores in the atmosphere.  空气生物学家研究了真菌孢子在大气中的传播。|近义词:biological scientist, environmental scientist|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From aero- (relating to air) + bio- (relating to life) + -logist (one who studies).  "Aero-" comes from Greek "aer" (air). "Bio-" comes from Greek "bios" (life). "-logist" comes from Greek "-logos" (study).
