choose:/tʃuːz/
| v. |选择，挑选|To select from a number of possibilities; to decide on a course of action.|I chose the red dress. 我选择了那件红色的裙子。|select, pick, opt for|reject, decline|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *chosen*, from Old English *cēosan* (to choose, test, taste, seek out), from Proto-Germanic *keusaną* (to choose, taste, test), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵews-*. Cognate with Scots *chose* (to choose), West Frisian *kieze* (to choose), Dutch *kiezen* (to choose), German *kiesen* (to choose), Danish *kyse* (to pick, choose), Swedish *kösa* (to choose), Icelandic *kjósa* (to choose, elect, vote).
