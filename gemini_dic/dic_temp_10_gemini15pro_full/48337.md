decriminalized:/diːˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪzd/
v.|非刑事化的，合法化的|To make something that was previously a crime no longer a crime.  The act of removing or reducing the criminal penalties associated with a particular action or behavior.|Marijuana has been decriminalized in several states.  大麻在几个州已被非刑事化。|legalized, permitted|criminalized, prohibited|verb

decriminalization:/diːˌkrɪmɪnəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.|非刑事化，合法化|The action or process of making something that was previously a crime no longer a crime.|The decriminalization of marijuana is a controversial topic.  大麻合法化是一个有争议的话题。|legalization, legitimization|criminalization, prohibition|noun
