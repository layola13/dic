imitableness:/ˈɪmɪtəbəlnəs/
n.
可模仿性, 可效仿性
The quality of being imitable; capable of being imitated or copied.
Her imitableness made her a popular subject for impersonators. 她的可模仿性使她成为模仿者的热门对象。
imitability, copyability
inimitability, originality
noun
