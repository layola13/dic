Instacart: /ˈɪnstəkɑːrt/ | n. |  Instacart（一款杂货配送应用程序） | A grocery delivery and pick-up service in the U.S. and Canada. Customers shop for groceries online and have them delivered by a personal shopper. | I ordered groceries on Instacart and they arrived within an hour. 我在Instacart上订购了杂货，它们在一小时内就送到了。| 近义词: grocery delivery service,  online grocery platform,  Shipt,  Amazon Fresh,  Walmart Grocery | 反义词: brick-and-mortar grocery store | 词性: noun


Instacart: /ˈɪnstəkɑːrt/ | n. |  Instacart送货员 | A personal shopper who works for the Instacart platform, picking up and delivering groceries to customers. | The Instacart shopper carefully selected the ripest avocados.
