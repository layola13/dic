mustacho:/məˈstɑːʃoʊ/ /məˈstɑːʃ/
|n.|胡子，髭|A mustache is the hair that grows above the upper lip.|He waxed his mustache. 他给他的胡子打了蜡。|近义词：moustache, whiskers|反义词: clean-shaven|noun
