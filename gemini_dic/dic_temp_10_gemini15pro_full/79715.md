glucosuria: /ˌɡluːkəˈsjʊəriə/ /ˌɡluːkoʊˈsjʊəriə/
| n. | 糖尿 | The presence of glucose in the urine, typically a symptom of diabetes. |  Glucosuria is a common sign of diabetes. 糖尿是糖尿病的常见症状。|  The doctor tested her urine and found glucosuria. 医生检查了她的尿液，发现了糖尿。 | synonyms: glycosuria | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but absence of glucose in urine is normal) | noun

Etymology:
From gluco- (glucose) + -suria (urine).  Ultimately from Greek *gleûkos "sweet wine, must" +  ouron "urine".
