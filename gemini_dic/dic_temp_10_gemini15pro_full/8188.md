anti-dilutive:/ˌænti daɪˈluːtɪv/
adjective
反稀释的
Relating to a financial instrument or action that does not decrease earnings per share or ownership percentage.  It often refers to situations where the exercise of warrants or stock options would increase, rather than decrease, earnings per share.
The company's recent acquisition proved to be anti-dilutive, boosting earnings per share.  公司最近的收购被证明是反稀释的，提高了每股收益。
The issuance of these new shares will be anti-dilutive to existing shareholders. 发行这些新股将对现有股东反稀释。
近义词: accretive
反义词: dilutive
词性: adjective
