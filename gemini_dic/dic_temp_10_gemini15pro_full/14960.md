broad:/brɔːd/| n.|俚语，女人|Slang term for a woman|He referred to his wife as "the broad." 他把妻子称为“那个女人”。|近义词：woman, lady, female (though these are not slang)|反义词：man (in the slang context, no direct opposite)|noun
