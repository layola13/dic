frigidness:/ˈfrɪdʒɪdnəs/| n.|冷漠，冷淡|The frigidness of her response surprised him.  Her demeanor was marked by a distinct frigidness.|例句：The frigidness of the room made me shiver.  房间的寒冷让我打了个寒颤。

The frigidness in her voice made him uneasy. 她语气中的冷漠让他感到不安。|近义词：coldness, iciness, aloofness, indifference|反义词:warmth, cordiality, affection|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From frigid + -ness.  Frigid itself comes from Latin frigidus, meaning "cold," from frigere "to be cold."
