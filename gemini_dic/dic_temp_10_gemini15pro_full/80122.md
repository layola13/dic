goblinesque:/ˌɡɒblɪnˈesk/ | adj. |妖精似的，丑陋怪诞的 | Resembling or suggestive of a goblin; grotesque and malicious. |  The old house had a goblinesque appearance, with its crooked chimney and dark, empty windows. 这栋老房子有一种妖精般的外观，有着歪斜的烟囱和黑暗、空洞的窗户。

The twisted, gnarled branches of the tree gave it a goblinesque quality.  这棵树扭曲、粗糙的树枝赋予了它一种妖精般的气质。 | 近义词：grotesque, monstrous, hideous, gruesome | 反义词：beautiful, attractive, pleasant | 词性: adjective


词源: From goblin + -esque (a suffix meaning "in the style of; resembling").  来自goblin（妖精）+ -esque（后缀，意为“风格的；类似于”）。
