herbivores:/ˈhɜːrbɪvɔːrz/ (US) /ˈhɜːbɪvɔːz/ (UK)| n.|食草动物|Animals that feed exclusively on plants.|Cows and deer are herbivores. 牛和鹿是食草动物。|Rabbits are herbivores and eat grass, leaves, and vegetables. 兔子是食草动物，吃草、树叶和蔬菜。|近义词：vegetarian, plant-eater|反义词:carnivore, omnivore|词性:noun
