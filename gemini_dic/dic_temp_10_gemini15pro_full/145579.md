overcompressed:/ˌoʊvər.kəmˈprest/
adjective
过度压缩的
Compressed beyond what is optimal or desirable, resulting in a loss of quality or detail.
The image was overcompressed, making it look pixelated.  这张图片过度压缩，看起来像素化了。
The audio file was overcompressed, and the music sounded distorted.  音频文件过度压缩了，音乐听起来失真。
Synonyms: over-compressed, excessively compressed
Antonyms: undercompressed, lightly compressed
词性: 形容词
