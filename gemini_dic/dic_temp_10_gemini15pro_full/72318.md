floury:/ˈflaʊəri/ (US), /ˈflaʊri/ (UK)| adj.| 面粉的；覆盖着面粉的；像面粉的| Covered with or resembling flour; containing flour. |The baker's hands were floury.  面包师的手上沾满了面粉。|近义词：powdery, dusty|反义词: clean, unfloured|词性:形容词
词源: From flour + -y. Ultimately from Latin *flor-, from flos "flower".
