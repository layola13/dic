classicalist:/ˈklæsɪkəlɪst/| n.|古典主义者；古典学者|A person who studies or is an expert in the classics, especially ancient Greek and Roman literature, art, and culture.|例句：The classicalist gave a lecture on the influence of Homer.  这位古典学者做了一个关于荷马影响的讲座。

例句：She's a renowned classicalist and has published several books on ancient Greek philosophy. 她是一位著名的古典学者，出版了几本关于古希腊哲学的书籍。|近义词：scholar, humanist, classicist|反义词：modernist, contemporary|词性: noun
