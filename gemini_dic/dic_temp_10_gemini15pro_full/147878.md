overreaction:/ˌoʊvərriˈækʃən/| n. |过度反应，反应过激|An excessive or exaggerated reaction to something.|例句：His anger was a complete overreaction to the situation. 他的愤怒是对这种情况的完全过度反应。

例句：The stock market's overreaction to the news caused a sharp drop in prices. 股市对这一消息的过度反应导致价格暴跌。|近义词：overkill, exaggeration, hysteria|反义词:underreaction, calm, composure|词性:名词
