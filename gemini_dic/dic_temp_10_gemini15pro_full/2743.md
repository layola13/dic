adulterate: /əˈdʌltəreɪt/
verb
掺假，劣化的；通奸的
to make something impure or weaker by adding something of inferior quality.
The company was accused of adulterating its products with cheaper ingredients. 这家公司被指控在产品中掺入廉价成分以次充好。

The milk was adulterated with water. 牛奶里掺了水。


近义词: contaminate, debase, pollute, corrupt
反义词: purify, refine
词性: verb
