impugnable:/ɪmˈpʌɡnəbl/ | adj. | 可非难的，可责难的，可置疑的 | Open to attack, criticism, or question; vulnerable. |  Her testimony was easily impugnable due to her previous lies. 她的证词很容易被质疑，因为她以前撒过谎。

The politician's character was impugnable after the scandal.  这位政治家的品格在丑闻曝光后受到了质疑。 | 近义词: challengeable, questionable, disputable, contestable, assailable | 反义词: unimpeachable, irrefutable, unquestionable, inarguable, indisputable | 词性: adjective
