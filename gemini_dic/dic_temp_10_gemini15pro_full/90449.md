hu:/huː/ |interj.| 呸; 表示轻蔑或不耐烦| An exclamation of disgust or impatience. | "Hu!" she snorted, turning away in disgust. 她轻蔑地哼了一声“呸”，厌恶地转过身去。 | 近义词: pshaw, pooh, tut | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's an exclamation) | 词性:感叹词

词源:  Imitative origin.  Many languages have similar interjections representing this sound.


注意事项:  "Hu" is very informal and can be considered rude.  Use with caution.
