deflux:/diːˈflʌks/ | n. | 下流；向下流动；泻流 | A flowing down; a downward flow, especially of humors or fluids.  A copious discharge of fluid matter from a body, especially a mucous discharge from the bowels or nose. | The deflux of the river caused significant erosion. 河水的向下流动造成了严重的侵蚀。| 近义词：discharge, flow, flux, effusion | 反义词：influx | 词性：名词

deflux:/diːˈflʌks/ | v. | 流下；泻下 | To flow down; to cause to flow down. |  Ancient physicians believed illness could be cured by defluxing the harmful humors. 古代医生认为疾病可以通过排出有害的体液来治愈。| 近义词：discharge, drain, empty | 反义词：fill, accumulate | 词性：动词
