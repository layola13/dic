engraftment:/ɪnˈɡræftmənt/
noun
移植；植入；嫁接
The process in which transplanted tissue becomes established in its new site.
The engraftment of the new bone marrow was successful.  新的骨髓移植成功了。
近义词: implantation, transplantation
反义词: rejection
