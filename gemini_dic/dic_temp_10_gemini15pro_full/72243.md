floridness:/ˈflɔrɪdnəs/ | n. | 华丽；绚丽；花哨；（不健康的）红润 |  The quality of being florid, especially excessively ornate or embellished in style or appearance.  Also, a redness of complexion. |  The floridness of his prose was a bit much for my taste. 他的散文过于华丽，我不太喜欢。 |  ornateness, elaborateness, flamboyance, extravagance | plainness, simplicity, austerity | noun

florid:/ˈflɔrɪd/ | adj. | 华丽的；绚丽的；花哨的；（不健康的）红润的 | Having a red or flushed complexion. Also, excessively decorated or embellished. | He gave a florid speech, full of elaborate metaphors. 他发表了一篇华丽的演讲，充满了精美的比喻。 | ornate, elaborate, flamboyant, extravagant | plain, simple, austere | adjective
