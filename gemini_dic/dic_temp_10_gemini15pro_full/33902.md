circumventable:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈvɛntəbl/ | adj. | 可规避的，可绕过的 |  Able to be circumvented, bypassed, or avoided.  It describes something that can be overcome or outsmarted, often through indirect or clever means. | The security system, though advanced, proved circumventable by a skilled hacker.  这个安全系统虽然先进，但还是被一个熟练的黑客绕过了。

The company's strict regulations were circumventable by employees who knew the loopholes. 公司的严格规定被知道漏洞的员工规避了。 | 近义词: avoidable, bypassable, escapable | 反义词: unavoidable, inescapable, inevitable | 词性: adjective

词源: From circum-(around) + vent-(come) + -able (capable of being).  Ultimately from Latin circumvenire (to come around, surround).
