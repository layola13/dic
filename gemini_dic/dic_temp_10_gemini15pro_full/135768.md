nonprofit:/nɒnˈprɒfɪt/ /nɑːnˈprɑːfɪt/| n.|非营利组织|An organization that does not operate for profit.|例句：Many nonprofits rely on donations and grants. 许多非营利组织依靠捐款和拨款。|例句：The nonprofit provides services to the homeless. 这个非营利组织为无家可归者提供服务。|近义词：charity, foundation, not-for-profit organization|反义词：for-profit business, corporation|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + profit.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "profit" refers to financial gain.
