dilatoriness:/ˈdɪləˌtɔːrɪnəs/
n.|拖延；迟缓|The dilatoriness of the committee in reaching a decision frustrated everyone involved. 委员会迟迟未能作出决定，让所有相关人员都感到沮丧。|例句：The dilatoriness in responding to the email caused further delays. 回复邮件的拖延导致了进一步的延误。|近义词：slowness, tardiness, procrastination, delay|反义词：promptness, speed, alacrity, expedition|词性:名词

Etymology:  From Late Latin *dilatorius, from Latin dilator "delayer, procrastinator," from dilatus, past participle of differre "to postpone, defer," from dis- "apart" + ferre "to carry, bear."
