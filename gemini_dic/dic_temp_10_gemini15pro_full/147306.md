overmoisten:/ˌoʊvərˈmɔɪsən/| v. |过度润湿，过分湿润|To moisten excessively; to make too damp.|The gardener overmoistened the soil, causing the roots to rot. 园丁过度湿润了土壤，导致根部腐烂。|The baker overmoistened the dough, making it sticky and difficult to work with.  面包师把面团弄得太湿了，使它很粘，难以操作。|overwater, oversaturate|dry out, dehydrate|verb
