epidermatoid:/ˌɛpɪdɜːrˈmætɔɪd/ | adj. n.|表皮样的，类表皮的；表皮样瘤|Resembling or derived from the epidermis; a tumor originating from epidermal cells.|
The doctor diagnosed an epidermatoid cyst. 医生诊断出这是一个表皮样囊肿。|
Epidermatoid carcinomas are a type of skin cancer. 表皮样癌是一种皮肤癌。|
近义词:epithelial, epidermal |反义词: None|词性:adjective, noun


Etymology:
From epi- + derm(a) + -at + -oid

epi-:  Greek prefix meaning "upon," "on," "over," "near," "at," "before," "after,"  "besides," "in addition to."
derm(a):  Greek "derma," meaning "skin."
-at:  Suffix forming nouns and adjectives.
-oid:  Suffix meaning "resembling," "like," or "having the form of."
