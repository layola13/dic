desexualization:/diːˌsɛkʃuəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |去性化，非性化|The process of removing sexual characteristics or connotations from something or someone.  The act of divesting of sexual character or quality.|The desexualization of women in advertising is a common feminist critique.  广告中女性的去性化是女权主义者的常见批评。|Near synonyms: neutralization,  de-eroticization|Antonyms: sexualization, eroticization|noun
