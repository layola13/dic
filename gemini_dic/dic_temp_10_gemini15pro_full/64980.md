estivate:/ˈɛstɪveɪt/| v.|夏眠；过夏天|To spend the summer in a state of dormancy or inactivity, similar to hibernation in winter.|Example: Desert tortoises estivate during the hottest months. 沙漠陆龟在最热的几个月里夏眠。

Example:  Some snails estivate to avoid drying out in the summer heat.  一些蜗牛夏眠以避免在夏季炎热中脱水。


|近义词：dormate, sleep, hibernate|反义词: be active, awaken|词性: verb
