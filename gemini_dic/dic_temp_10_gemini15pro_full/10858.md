arabinose:/əˈræbɪnoʊs/| n. |阿拉伯糖|A five-carbon sugar (pentose) found in certain plant gums and other substances. It is a component of hemicellulose and pectin.|Arabinose is a common sugar in the plant kingdom. 阿拉伯糖是植物界中常见的糖。| The scientist isolated arabinose from gum arabic. 科学家从阿拉伯树胶中分离出阿拉伯糖。| synonyms: pectinose, gum sugar | antonyms: None | noun
