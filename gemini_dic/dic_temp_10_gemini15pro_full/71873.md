He was known as a fleshmonger in the red-light district. 他在红灯区被称为皮条客。
The king dismissed the corrupt general, calling him a fleshmonger who traded lives for power. 国王斥责了腐败的将军，称他是一个为了权力而出卖生命的皮条客。
