orphans:/ˈɔːrfənz/ (US), /ˈɔːfənz/ (UK)| n.|孤儿，失去双亲的孩子|Children who have lost both parents are called orphans. 失去双亲的孩子被称为孤儿。|The earthquake left many children as orphans. 地震使许多孩子成了孤儿。|近义词：fatherless, motherless, bereaved children|反义词: N/A| noun

词源: From Old French *orfan, from Late Latin orphanus, from Greek orphanós.
