audiometer:/ˌɔːdiˈɒmɪtər/ (US) /ˌɔːdiˈɒmɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |测听计；听力计|An instrument used to measure hearing acuity.  It presents sounds of different frequencies and intensities to determine the threshold of hearing for each frequency.|The audiologist used an audiometer to test the patient's hearing. 听力学家用测听计测试病人的听力。|Synonyms: hearing tester|Antonyms: N/A|noun
