familiarised:/fəˈmɪliəraɪzd/ |v. |使熟悉；使了解|To make someone familiar with something; to accustom someone to something. |  She familiarised herself with the company's procedures before her first day. 她第一天上班前熟悉了公司的规章制度。

| The guide familiarised the tourists with the local customs. 导游让游客们熟悉了当地的风俗习惯。 |  accustomed, acquainted, versed | unfamiliar, unacquainted |verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From familiar + -ise/-ize.  "Familiar" comes from the Latin "familiaris" meaning "of the household, domestic," from "familia" meaning "household, family." The suffix "-ise/-ize" is used to form verbs meaning "to make or become."
