convective:/kənˈvektɪv/| adj.|对流的，传送的|Relating to, or operating by convection.  Characterized by the upward movement of heated air or fluid.|例句：Convective clouds form when warm, moist air rises. 对流云在温暖潮湿的空气上升时形成。

例句：Convective heat transfer is important in many industrial processes. 对流传热在许多工业过程中都很重要。|近义词：thermal, circulatory|反义词：conductive, radiative|词性:形容词
convection:/kənˈvekʃn/| n.|对流，传送|The movement caused within a fluid by the tendency of hotter, less dense material to rise, and colder, denser material to sink under the influence of gravity, which consequently results in transfer of heat.|例句：Convection currents in the mantle are thought to be responsible for plate tectonics. 地幔中的对流被认为是板块构造的原因。

例句：The pot of water was heated by convection. 这壶水是通过对流加热的。|近义词：circulation, transmission|反义词：conduction, radiation|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *convectivus*, from Latin convectus (“carried together”), past participle of convehere (“to carry or bring together”), from con- (“together”) + vehere (“to carry”).
