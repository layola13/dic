flashings:/ˈflæʃɪŋz/
n.
防水板，遮雨板；（屋顶、烟囱周围的）金属盖板
Pieces of metal or other material used to cover joins in a roof or wall to prevent water from getting in.
The flashings around the chimney need to be replaced. 烟囱周围的金属盖板需要更换。

近义词: flashing, weatherproofing, cladding
反义词: None

词性: noun
