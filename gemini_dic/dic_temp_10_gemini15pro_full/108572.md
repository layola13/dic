le:/lə/ | pron. | 他，她，它（宾格）| Used as the object of a verb or preposition, referring to a person or thing previously mentioned. | Example: I saw him and told him the news. 我看到他，并告诉了他这个消息。| Example:  The book is interesting; I enjoyed reading it. 这本书很有趣；我很喜欢读它。| Synonym: him, her, it | Antonym: (No direct antonym for a pronoun) | Pronoun


/lə/ | art. | 定冠词（法语）| The definite article in French, equivalent to "the" in English.  It precedes nouns, both masculine and feminine, singular and plural. | Example: Le livre est sur la table. The book is on the table. | Example: Les enfants jouent dans le parc. The children are playing in the park. | Synonym: (No English synonym, it's a grammatical function word) | Antonym: (No antonym for a definite article) | Article

Simple Present:
What does he want? = 他想要什么？
She gives him the book. = 她把书给了他。

Present Continuous:
She is giving it to him now. = 她现在正在把它给他。
They are buying it for her. = 他们正在为她买它。

Present Perfect:
I have given it to him already. = 我已经把它给他了。
She has told him the news. = 她已经告诉他这个消息了。

Present Perfect Continuous:
They have been looking for it all day. = 他们找了它一整天。
He has been waiting for her for an hour.  = 他已经等了她一个小时了。

Simple Past:
I saw her yesterday. = 我昨天看到她了。
He gave it to me last week. = 他上周把它给了我。

Past Continuous:
I was talking to him when the phone rang. = 电话响的时候我正在和他说话。
She was reading it when I came in. = 我进来的时候她正在读它。

Past Perfect:
I had already seen him before the party. =  在聚会之前我已经见过他了。
She had given it to him before I asked. = 在我问之前她已经把它给他了。

Past Perfect Continuous:
I had been waiting for him for an hour before he arrived. = 在他到达之前我已经等了他一个小时了。
They had been looking for it all morning. = 他们整个上午都在找它。

Simple Future:
I will give it to him tomorrow. = 我明天把它给他。
She will see him next week. = 她下周会见到他。

Future Continuous:
I will be seeing him tomorrow afternoon. = 明天下午我会见到他。
She will be reading it on the train. = 她会在火车上读它。

Future Perfect:
I will have given it to him by next week. = 到下周我就会把它给他了。
She will have finished reading it by then. = 到那时她就会读完了。

Future Perfect Continuous:
By next year, I will have been living here for five years. = 到明年，我将已经在这里住了五年了。（例句中用“here”代替了“le/him/her/it”以更好地演示用法）
By the time she graduates, she will have been studying French for four years. = 到她毕业的时候，她将已经学习法语四年了。（例句中用法语代替了“le/him/her/it”以更好地演示用法）



PHRASE->
(As "le" is a pronoun or article, it doesn't form phrases in the same way as other words.  Here are some examples of "le" used in French phrases):
Le matin = in the morning
Le soir = in the evening
Le lundi = on Monday
Le week-end = on the weekend
Sur le bureau = on the desk
