nonexchangeable:/ˌnɒnɪksˈtʃeɪndʒəbl/
adjective
不可交换的
not able to be exchanged
例句：These tokens are nonexchangeable and cannot be redeemed for cash. 这些代币不可兑换，也不能兑换现金。

例句：The contract stipulates that the goods are nonexchangeable. 合同规定货物不可更换。

近义词：non-interchangeable, irreplaceable, nonsubstitutable
反义词：exchangeable, interchangeable, replaceable, substitutable
词性: adjective
