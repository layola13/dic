aflame:/əˈfleɪm/| adj.|燃烧的，着火的；激动的，兴奋的|Burning; lighted up with flames; ablaze; figuratively, excited or passionate.|例句：The building was aflame. 这栋楼着火了。

例句：Her face was aflame with excitement. 她的脸因兴奋而通红。|近义词：ablaze, alight, burning, fiery, ignited, inflamed|反义词：extinguished, unlit, calm, peaceful|词性:形容词

词源: a- (prefix meaning "on" or "in") + flame (noun)  From Middle English *aflame, *aflaumbe, equivalent to a- +‎ flame.
