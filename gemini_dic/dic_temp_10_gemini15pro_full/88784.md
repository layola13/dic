hodman:/ˈhɒdmən/ (UK) /ˈhɑːdmən/ (US)| n. | 苦力；泥瓦匠助手；搬运工 | A hodman is a laborer who carries bricks, mortar, and other building materials for a bricklayer. | The hodman struggled under the weight of the bricks.  泥瓦匠助手在砖块的重压下挣扎。|  The hodman quickly ascended the ladder with a full hod.  搬运工扛着一满桶的灰泥迅速爬上梯子。|Synonyms: laborer, builder's helper, construction worker | Antonyms: foreman, supervisor | noun
