blanchimeter:/blænˈtʃɪmɪtər/ | n. | 漂白计，白度计 | An instrument used to measure the degree of whiteness of a substance, especially paper or textiles. | Example 1: The blanchimeter showed that the paper had reached the desired level of brightness. 漂白计显示纸张已达到所需的亮度。

Example 2:  Textile manufacturers use blanchimeters to ensure consistent color quality in their products.  纺织品制造商使用白度计来确保产品颜色质量的一致性。 | Synonyms: whiteness meter, reflectometer | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
