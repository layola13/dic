fraudulencies:/ˈfrɔːdjələnsis/
n.
欺诈行为，诈骗行为
Acts of fraud or deception.
The company uncovered a series of  fraudulencies in their accounting department. 该公司在其会计部门发现了一系列欺诈行为。
近义词:frauds, scams, deceptions, cheats
反义词:honesty, integrity
词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *fraudulencie*, from Old French *fraudulence*, from Latin *fraudulentia*, from *fraudulentus* ("fraudulent"), from *fraus* ("fraud, deceit").
