glad:/ɡlæd/| adj.|高兴的，乐意的|Feeling pleasure or happiness.| I'm glad to hear that. 我很高兴听到这个消息。|近义词：happy, pleased, cheerful, delighted|反义词：sad, unhappy, sorrowful|adjective

glad:/ɡlæd/| adj.|乐于(做某事)的;情愿(做某事)的|Willing or happy to do something.|I'd be glad to help. 我很乐意帮忙。|近义词：willing, happy|反义词：unwilling, reluctant|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *glæd, from Proto-Germanic *gladiz, from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰlad- (“bright, shining”). Cognate with Dutch glad (“smooth, slippery, joyful”), German glatt (“smooth, slippery”), dialectal German glat (“joyful”), Old Norse glaðr (“smooth, joyful”), Gothic 𐌲𐌻𐌰𐌳𐌿𐍃 (glaþus, “joyful”). Related to glad (verb).
