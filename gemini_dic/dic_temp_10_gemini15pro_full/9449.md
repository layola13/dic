antireductive:/ˌæntiɹɪˈdʌktɪv/
| adj. | 反还原的，反对还原论的 | Characterized by opposition to reductionism; resisting simplification to a more basic level of explanation. |  Her antireductive approach to the mind considered the whole greater than the sum of its parts. 她对心灵的反还原论方法认为，整体大于部分之和。|近义词：holistic, integrative |反义词：reductionist, simplistic |词性: adjective

antireductive:/ˌæntiɹɪˈdʌktɪv/
| n. | 反还原论者，反对还原论的事物 |  A person who opposes reductionism; something that resists simplification. | His work as an antireductive challenged the prevailing scientific paradigm. 他作为一名反还原论者的工作挑战了盛行的科学范式。 | 近义词：holist | 反义词：reductionist | 词性: noun
