dunder:/ˈdʌndər/| n. |双下划线|Double underscore, a slang term used in Python programming to refer to names that are prefixed and suffixed with two underscores, such as __init__.  These names have special meaning in Python.|Example:  The __init__ dunder method is used to initialize a class.  双下划线方法 __init__ 用于初始化一个类。|近义词: magic method, special method|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
