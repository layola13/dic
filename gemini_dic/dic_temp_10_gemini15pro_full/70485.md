filamentoid:/ˌfɪləˈmɛntɔɪd/
adj.
丝状的; filament-shaped
Resembling a filament; threadlike.
The bacteria formed filamentoid colonies.  这些细菌形成了丝状菌落。

近义词:filamentous, threadlike, filiform, stringy
反义词:amorphous, shapeless

词性: adjective

词源: From filament + -oid.
