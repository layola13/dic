From French *envisager*, from en- (“in, into”) + visage (“face, appearance”).

PHRASE->
envisage doing something: 设想做某事  I can't envisage ever leaving this town. 我无法设想离开这个小镇。
envisage something happening: 设想某事发生  Do you envisage any problems occurring? 你预见到会发生什么问题吗？
envisage that…: 设想… I envisage that this project will take another six months to complete. 我估计这个项目还需要六个月才能完成。

envisage as: 设想为 I don't envisage him as a leader. 我不认为他是一个领导者。
