glacier:/ˈɡleɪʃər/ (US), /ˈɡlæsiə(r)/ (UK)| n.|冰川；冰河|A large mass of ice that moves slowly over land.|The glacier carved a deep valley through the mountains. 冰川在群山中刻出了一条深谷。|Synonyms: ice sheet, ice cap, ice field|Antonyms: desert, volcano|noun

glacier:/ˈɡleɪʃər/ (US), /ˈɡlæsiə(r)/ (UK)| n.|冰川；冰河|A large, slow-moving river of ice.|Glaciers are formed from compacted snow over many years.  冰川是由多年的积雪压缩而成的。|Synonyms: ice flow, ice stream|Antonyms: lava flow|noun
