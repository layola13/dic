co-operative:/kəʊˈɒpərətɪv/| adj. n.|合作的，协作的；合作社|A co-operative enterprise is one in which the members share the profits. 合作企业是指成员共享利润的企业。|例句：
The project was a co-operative effort between several organizations.
这个项目是几个组织合作的成果。

They decided to set up a co-operative to sell their produce.
他们决定建立一个合作社来销售他们的产品。
|近义词：collaborative, joint, shared|反义词：competitive, individualistic|词性：形容词/名词

co-operate:/kəʊˈɒpəreɪt/| v.|合作，协作|To co-operate is to work together towards a common goal. 合作是为了共同的目标而一起工作。|例句：

We need to co-operate if we want to finish this project on time.
如果我们想按时完成这个项目，我们需要合作。

The two companies agreed to co-operate on the development of new software.
两家公司同意合作开发新软件。
|近义词：collaborate, work together, team up|反义词：compete, obstruct, hinder|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Latin "co-" (together) and "operari" (to work).  Comes from the Latin word "cooperativus," meaning "working together."
