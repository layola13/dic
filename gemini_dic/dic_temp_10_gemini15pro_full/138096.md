normativenesses:/ˈnɔːrmətɪvnəsɪz/
n.
规范性（复数）

The normativenesses of societal expectations can be stifling to individual expression.  社会期望的规范性可能会扼杀个人的表达。
The various normativenesses surrounding gender roles have changed significantly over time.  围绕性别角色的各种规范性随着时间的推移发生了显著变化。

abnormalities, deviations, irregularities
