anticoagulation:/ˌæntikoʊˌæɡjʊˈleɪʃən/| n.|抗凝|The prevention of blood clotting, typically by administering an anticoagulant.|The doctor prescribed anticoagulation therapy after his surgery to prevent blood clots. 医生在他的手术后开了抗凝治疗以防止血栓形成。|Synonyms: blood thinning,  thromboprophylaxis|Antonyms: coagulation, clotting|noun

anticoagulant:/ˌæntikoʊˈæɡjʊlənt/| n. adj.|抗凝剂; 抗凝的|A substance that prevents blood clotting.|Warfarin is a commonly used anticoagulant.  华法林是一种常用的抗凝剂。  He is on anticoagulant medication. 他正在服用抗凝药物。|Synonyms: blood thinner, antithrombotic|Antonyms: coagulant, procoagulant|noun, adjective
