lectureship: /ˈlɛktʃəʃɪp/ (UK) /ˈlɛktʃərʃɪp/ (US)
n.
讲师职位；讲师的职务
the position or rank of a lecturer
She was awarded a lectureship in the Department of History. 她被授予历史系的讲师职位。
He holds a lectureship at the university. 他在大学担任讲师一职。
Synonyms:  lecturership, teaching post, faculty position
Antonyms:  professorship
