evangelizers:/ɪˈvændʒəlaɪzərz/| n. |福音传道者；传福音的人|People who spread the Christian gospel and try to convert others to Christianity.  Those who actively promote or spread a particular doctrine, cause, or belief.|例句：The evangelizers traveled to remote villages to share their faith. 福音传道者前往偏远村庄分享他们的信仰。

例句：The company's CEO is a passionate evangelizer for sustainable business practices.  这家公司的首席执行官是可持续商业实践的热情倡导者。|近义词：missionaries, preachers, apostles, advocates, proponents|反义词: atheists, skeptics|词性:noun
