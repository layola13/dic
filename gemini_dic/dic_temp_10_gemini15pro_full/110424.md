likeably:/ˈlaɪkəbli/| adv.|讨人喜欢地，可爱地|in a likeable or pleasant manner|She spoke likeably about her childhood. 她愉快地谈起了她的童年。|近义词：pleasantly, charmingly, agreeably|反义词：disagreeably, unpleasantly|词性:副词
词源: From likeable + -ly.  Likeable is from like (verb) + -able.  The verb "like" ultimately comes from Old English *līcian "be pleasing," from Proto-Germanic *likon "have form, body; be agreeable," from PIE root *leig- "to lick, to taste."
