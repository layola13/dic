basins:/ˈbeɪsɪnz/
noun
盆地；流域；盆；脸盆
A basin is a bowl-shaped container used for holding liquids, typically water, for washing.  A geographical basin is a large, bowl-shaped area of land where water collects and drains into a river or lake.
The river flows into the Amazon basin. 这条河流入亚马逊盆地。
She filled the basin with warm water. 她把盆里装满了温水。
Synonyms: bowl, container, sink, valley
Antonyms: peak, summit
