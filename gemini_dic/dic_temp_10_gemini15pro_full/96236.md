indican:/ˈɪndɪkæn/ | n. |靛苷，吲哚酚硫酸钾|A colorless organic compound, C14H17NO6S·3H2O, found in the urine of herbivores, especially horses, and formed by bacterial decomposition of indole in the intestine. It is also present in certain plants, such as indigo, woad, and isatis.|例句：Indican is a precursor to indigo dye. 靛苷是靛蓝染料的前体。

例句：The presence of indican in urine can indicate bacterial overgrowth in the gut.  尿液中靛苷的存在可能表明肠道细菌过度生长。|近义词：indoxyl sulfate (in some contexts)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
