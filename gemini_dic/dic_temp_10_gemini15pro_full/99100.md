intercalibrations:/ˌɪntərˌkælɪˈbreɪʃənz/ | n. | 校准，比对 | The process of comparing and adjusting the measurements or standards of different instruments or systems to ensure consistency and accuracy. |  Intercalibrations of the instruments were performed regularly to ensure consistent data.  仪器定期进行比对，以确保数据一致。| synonyms: comparisons, standardizations, adjustments | antonyms: discrepancies, inconsistencies | noun

intercalibration:/ˌɪntərˌkælɪˈbreɪʃən/ | n. | 校准，比对 | The process of comparing and adjusting the measurements or standards of a single instrument or system over time to ensure continued accuracy. | Regular intercalibration is essential to maintain the precision of the equipment. 定期校准对于维持设备的精度至关重要。 | synonyms: validation, verification, checking | antonyms: deviation, error | noun
