outrivaling: /ˌaʊtˈraɪvəlɪŋ/
verb
胜过，超过
surpassing or exceeding in rivalry; defeating in competition.
The new company is outrivaling all its competitors in sales. 这家新公司在销售方面胜过所有竞争对手。
Synonyms: surpassing, exceeding, outdoing, outstripping, eclipsing, besting, beating, defeating, triumphing over, winning against
Antonyms: losing to, being defeated by, being inferior to
