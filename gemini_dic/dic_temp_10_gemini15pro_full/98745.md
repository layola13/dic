insurrections: /ˌɪnsəˈrekʃənz/
n.
起义，暴动，叛乱
An act of rising in revolt, rebellion, or resistance against civil authority or an established government.
The government crushed the insurrections in the countryside. 政府镇压了农村的起义。

The museum has a fascinating exhibit on the insurrections of the 18th century.  博物馆有一个关于18世纪起义的引人入胜的展览。

近义词: rebellions, revolts, uprisings
反义词: obedience, submission
词性: 名词 (plural)

词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French insurrection or late Latin insurrectio(n-), from insurgere ‘rise up’.
