nurse:/nɜːrs/| n. vt.|护士；护理，照料|A nurse is a person who cares for the sick or infirm, especially in a hospital.  As a verb, to nurse means to care for someone who is ill or injured.|The nurse monitored the patient's vital signs.  护士监测了病人的生命体征。 She nursed her sick mother back to health. 她护理她生病的母亲直到康复。|近义词：caregiver, attendant (n.); tend, care for (v.)|反义词: patient (as in someone receiving care) |Noun, Verb
