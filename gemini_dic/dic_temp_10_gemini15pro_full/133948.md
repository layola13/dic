nonhectic:/nɑːnˈhɛktɪk/
adjective
平静的；不忙乱的
Not hectic; calm and relaxed.
例句：We had a nonhectic weekend at home. 我们在家度过了一个平静的周末。

例句：She prefers a nonhectic lifestyle. 她更喜欢一种平静的生活方式。

近义词：calm, peaceful, relaxed, tranquil, serene, quiet
反义词：hectic, busy, frantic, chaotic

词性: adjective
