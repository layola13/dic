immobilizing: /ɪˈmoʊbəˌlaɪzɪŋ/ /ɪˈmɒbɪlaɪzɪŋ/
| adj. | 使不能动的，使固定的 |  Making something or someone unable to move. | The doctor immobilized her broken leg with a cast. 医生用石膏固定了她断腿。 |  restricting, confining, crippling | freeing, mobilizing | adjective

immobilize: /ɪˈmoʊbəˌlaɪz/ /ɪˈmɒbɪlaɪz/
| vt. | 使不动，使固定 |  To prevent something or someone from moving. | The police immobilized the suspect's car with a tire boot. 警察用轮胎锁固定了嫌疑人的车。 | restrict, confine, cripple | free, mobilize | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *immobiliser*, from *im-* (not) + *mobiliser* (to mobilize), ultimately from Latin *mobilis* (movable).
