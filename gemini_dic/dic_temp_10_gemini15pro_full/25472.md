bulleting:/ˈbʊlɪtɪŋ/
n.
项目符号，列表; 公告，布告;  弹痕
The bulleted list made the information easier to read. 项目符号列表使信息更容易阅读。
The sheriff posted a bulleting about the escaped convict. 警长张贴了关于逃犯的公告。
The wall was riddled with bulletings from the shootout.  墙上布满了枪战留下的弹痕。
Synonyms: listing, itemization, posting, notice, bullet points, indentation
Antonyms: paragraph, essay, narrative
noun

Etymology: From "bullet" (referring to the small round mark used in lists) + "-ing" (forming a noun from a verb, indicating an action or process). Ultimately from Middle French "boulette" meaning "small ball."
