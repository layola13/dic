despisers:/dɪˈspaɪzərz/
|n.|蔑视者，鄙视者|People who despise others; those who look down on or feel contempt for something or someone.|The despisers of tradition often fail to see its value.  那些蔑视传统的人往往看不到它的价值。|近义词：scorners, detractors, critics, mockers|反义词：admirers, supporters, advocates|词性:noun

despisers:/dɪˈspaɪzərz/
|n.|蔑视者，鄙视者|Those who express contempt or disdain for something or someone.|The despisers of the new law protested vehemently.  鄙视新法律的人强烈抗议。|近义词：contemners, disdainers|反义词：respecters, venerators|词性:noun
