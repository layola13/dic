impingent:/ɪmˈpɪndʒənt/
adj.
冲击的，撞击的；侵犯的， impinge 的现在分词形式
Having an effect or impact, especially a negative one; encroaching.
The loud music from the concert was impingent on the quiet neighborhood.  音乐会喧闹的音乐影响了宁静的街区。
近义词: intrusive, impacting, affecting, influencing
反义词: negligible, insignificant, unobtrusive
