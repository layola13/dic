disrober: /dɪsˈroʊbər/
n. |脱衣者；使人脱衣的人或物|A person or thing that disrobes someone or something.
|The nurse acted as a disrober for the patient before the examination.|护士在检查前为病人脱衣服。
|The cold wind acted as a disrober, stripping the trees of their leaves.|寒风像个脱衣者，剥光了树上的叶子。
|Synonym: stripper, undresser|
|Antonym: dresser, clother|
noun
