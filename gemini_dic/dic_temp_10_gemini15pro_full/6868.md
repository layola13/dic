anatropal:/əˈnætrəpəl/
adjective
倒生的，反向的
(of an ovule) inverted so that the micropyle is near the point of attachment of the funiculus.  (植物学) 指胚珠倒转，以使珠孔靠近珠柄的附着点。
The anatropal ovule is the most common type found in flowering plants.  倒生胚珠是被子植物中最常见的类型。
synonym:inverted, resupinate
antonym:orthotropous
词性:adjective
