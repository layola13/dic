outsteam:/aʊtˈstiːm/
verb
喷出蒸汽; 以蒸汽方式排出
to emit steam; to discharge in the form of steam
The old locomotive outsteamed a great cloud of smoke and steam.  这辆老式机车喷出一大团烟雾和蒸汽。
The factory outsteamed pollutants into the air. 这家工厂向空气中排放污染物。
synonym: vent, exhaust, emit, discharge
antonym: inhale, absorb, contain
