cross-questioned:/ˈkrɒs kwɛstʃənd/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːs kwɛstʃənd/ (US)| v. |盘问，反复询问|To question (someone) closely, often aggressively, on a particular subject, especially in order to expose inconsistencies or falsehoods.|The police cross-questioned the suspect for hours. 警方盘问了嫌疑人几个小时。|Synonyms: interrogate, grill, examine, quiz|Antonyms:  evade, avoid, skirt|verb

cross-questioned:/ˈkrɒs kwɛstʃənd/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːs kwɛstʃənd/ (US)| adj. |被盘问的|Having been subjected to close questioning.|The cross-questioned witness became flustered.  被盘问的证人变得慌乱起来。|Synonyms: interrogated, examined, quizzed|Antonyms: unchallenged, unquestioned|adjective
