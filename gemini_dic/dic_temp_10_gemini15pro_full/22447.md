booking-out:/ˈbʊkɪŋ aʊt/ | n. | 退房；结账离开；签退 | The act of officially recording one's departure from a hotel, hospital, military base, etc. |  Example: My booking-out time is 11 am tomorrow.  我的退房时间是明天上午11点。| Synonyms: checking out, signing out | Antonyms: checking in, booking in | noun

booking-out:/ˈbʊkɪŋ aʊt/ | n. | 登记外出 | The act of recording one's departure from a place, especially in a formal context like a military base or workplace. | Example:  All personnel must complete booking-out procedures before leaving the base. 所有人员离开基地前必须完成签退手续。 | Synonyms: signing out, logging out | Antonyms: signing in, logging in | noun
