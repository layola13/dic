destitute:/ˈdestɪtjuːt/ /ˈdɛstɪtut/
adjective
赤贫的，穷困潦倒的；缺乏的
Without the basic necessities of life; lacking something needed or desirable.
例句：The earthquake left thousands of people destitute. 地震使数千人一贫如洗。

例句：They were destitute of any hope. 他们完全绝望了。

synonym
impoverished, indigent, penniless, poverty-stricken, needy, deprived
antonym
wealthy, affluent, rich, prosperous
词性
形容词


destitutes:/ˈdestɪtjuːts/ /ˈdɛstɪtuts/
noun
穷人，贫民
People who are without the basic necessities of life.
例句：The charity provides food and shelter for destitutes.  这个慈善机构为穷人提供食物和住所。

例句：The government is working to address the needs of the destitutes in the city. 政府正在努力解决城市贫民的需求。
synonym
the poor, the needy, the impoverished, the indigent
antonym
the rich, the wealthy, the affluent
