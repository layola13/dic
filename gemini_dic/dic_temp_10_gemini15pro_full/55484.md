This is an incorrect option in a multiple-choice question designed to distract the test-taker from the correct answer. |  The distracter in question 3 was so convincing that many students chose it. 第三题中的干扰项非常具有迷惑性，以至于很多学生都选择了它。

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "distract" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Distract" comes from the Latin "distractus," past participle of "distrahere," meaning "to draw apart, divide, distract," from "dis-" (apart) + "trahere" (to draw).
