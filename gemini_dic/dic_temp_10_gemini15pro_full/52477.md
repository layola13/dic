dietetical:/ˌdaɪəˈtɛtɪkəl/
adjective
饮食的；营养的
Relating to diet or to the rules of diet.
She is following dietetical advice from her doctor. 她正遵照医生的饮食建议。
The dietetical properties of certain foods are beneficial for health. 某些食物的营养特性对健康有益。


近义词:nutritional, dietary
反义词: unhealthy


词性: adjective
词源: From Greek diaita "manner of living," later "diet"
