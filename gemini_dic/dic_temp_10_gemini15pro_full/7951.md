anthemic:/ænˈθɛmɪk/
adj.
像赞歌的；雄壮的；激昂的；气势磅礴的
Resembling or suggestive of an anthem; inspiring or uplifting.  Characterized by qualities of grandeur, exaltation, and powerful emotion.
The concert ended with an anthemic performance of their most popular song.  音乐会以一首气势磅礴的热门歌曲的演出结束。
His latest single is an anthemic rock ballad about overcoming adversity. 他最新的单曲是一首关于克服逆境的雄壮的摇滚民谣。
近义词:inspiring, uplifting, rousing, majestic, grand, epic
反义词:unemotional, subdued, quiet, low-key
词性:adjective

词源: From anthem + -ic.  "Anthem" comes from the Old English "antefn," ultimately from Greek "antiphōnos" meaning "responsive."
