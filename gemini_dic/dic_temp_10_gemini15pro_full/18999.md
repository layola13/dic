bestraddle: /bɪˈstrædəl/
verb
跨坐，骑，横跨，控制
to sit or stand with one leg on each side of something; to extend across or over
The Colossus of Rhodes bestraddled the harbor entrance.  罗德岛巨像跨立在港口入口处。

The lawyer bestraddles two worlds - corporate law and human rights.  这位律师横跨两个领域——公司法和人权。
近义词: straddle, span, cover, dominate
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 be contained within 被...包含)
词性:  v.
