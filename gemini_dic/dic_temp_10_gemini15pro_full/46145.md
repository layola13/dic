cymophanous:/saɪˈmɒfənəs/| adj.|猫眼状的，闪光的|Exhibiting a changeable silky luster or opalescence, like that of a cat's eye.  This optical phenomenon arises from the reflection of light from parallel fibrous inclusions within the gemstone.|The cymophanous chrysoberyl is highly prized. 这颗猫眼状的金绿宝石价值连城。|Near Synonyms: chatoyant, opalescent, iridescent, shimmering|Antonyms: dull, matte|Adjective
