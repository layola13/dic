devotionals:/dɪˈvəʊʃənəlz/| n.|灵修书籍；灵修材料；灵修节目|Short writings or recordings offering spiritual guidance or reflection.|She listens to devotionals every morning. 她每天早上听灵修节目。|近义词：prayers, meditations, reflections|反义词：blasphemies, profanities|词性:名词

词源: From "devotional," ultimately from Late Latin "devotionalis," from Latin "devotio" (devotion).
