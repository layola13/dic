dirl:/dɜːrl/ /dəːrl/| n.|颤音; 震颤声|A vibrant, tremulous sound.|The dirl of the pipes filled the air. 风笛的颤音充满了空气。|近义词：vibration, trill, resonance|反义词:silence|词性:名词
