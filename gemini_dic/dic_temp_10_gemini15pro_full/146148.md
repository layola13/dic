overengineer:/ˌoʊvərˌɛndʒɪˈnɪr/
verb
过度工程化，过度设计
To design or build something to be more complex or elaborate than is necessary.
The software was overengineered, making it difficult to use and maintain.  这个软件被过度设计了，导致它难以使用和维护。
They overengineered the bridge, spending far more money than necessary.  他们过度设计了这座桥，花费了比所需多得多的钱。
Synonyms: overdesign, overcomplicate, overbuild
Antonyms: simplify, streamline
词性: 动词


overengineer:/ˌoʊvərˌɛndʒɪˈnɪr/
noun
过度工程化的产物
Something that has been overengineered.
The new phone was an overengineer, packed with features no one really needed.  这款新手机是一个过度工程化的产物，塞满了没有人真正需要的功能。
The project became an overengineer due to constant changes in requirements.  由于需求不断变化，该项目变成了一个过度工程化的产物。
Synonyms: overdesign, overcomplication
Antonyms: simplification, streamlining
词性: 名词
