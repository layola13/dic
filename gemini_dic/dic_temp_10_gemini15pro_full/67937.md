facetiousnesses:/ˌfeɪʃiˈɑːʃəsnɪsɪz/
n.
滑稽；戏谑；诙谐
The tendency to make inappropriate jokes or say witty things at inappropriate times.
His facetiousnesses were not appreciated during the serious meeting.  他的戏谑在严肃的会议上并不受欢迎。
facetiousness is the plural form of facetiousness.  facetiousnesses 是 facetiousness 的复数形式。
近义词: levity, flippancy, wittiness, jocularity
反义词: seriousness, solemnity, gravity
词性: 名词

facetious:/fəˈsiːʃəs/
adj.
爱开玩笑的；滑稽的；诙谐的
Treating serious issues with deliberately inappropriate humor; flippant.
He made a facetious remark about the boss's new haircut. 他对老板的新发型开了个轻佻的玩笑。
近义词: humorous, witty, jocular, playful, frivolous
反义词: serious, solemn, earnest, sincere
词性: 形容词
