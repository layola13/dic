daybreak:/ˈdeɪbreɪk/| n.|黎明，破晓|The birds started singing at daybreak. 鸟儿们在黎明时分开始歌唱。|Daybreak found them miles from home. 破晓时分，他们发现自己离家数英里。|近义词：dawn, sunrise, first light|反义词：sunset, nightfall, dusk|词性:名词

Etymology->
From Old English *dægbryce "daybreak," from dæg "day" + bryce "a breaking."  The word "break" here refers to the breaking of daylight upon the darkness of night.
