epigrammatist:/ɪˈpɪɡræmətɪst/ | n. | 警句家；善作隽语的人 | A person who composes or uses epigrams. |  He was a renowned epigrammatist, known for his witty and concise sayings. 他是一位著名的警句家，以其机智简洁的格言而闻名。

Oscar Wilde was a master epigrammatist. 奥斯卡·王尔德是一位警句大师。 | 近义词: wit, pundit, quipster | 反义词:  None (No direct antonym for a person who writes epigrams) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *épigrammatiste*, from Latin *epigramma*, from Greek *epigramma*  "inscription," from *epi* "upon" + *graphein* "to write."
