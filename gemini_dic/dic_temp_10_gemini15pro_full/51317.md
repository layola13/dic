detainer:/dɪˈteɪnər/| n.|扣留令，拘留状；扣留物|A writ authorizing the continued detention of someone already in custody, especially to face trial on another charge.  A thing that detains someone.|The police issued a detainer for the suspect. 警方对嫌疑人发出了拘留令。|近义词：warrant, hold, custody|反义词:release|noun


detain:/dɪˈteɪn/| vt.|拘留，扣留；耽搁|Keep (someone) in official custody, typically for questioning about a crime or in politically sensitive situations.  Keep (someone) from proceeding; hold back.|The police detained the suspect for questioning. 警方拘留了嫌疑人进行讯问。|近义词：hold, arrest, confine|反义词:release|verb
