lucimeter:/luːˈsɪmɪtər/
noun
光度计
An instrument for measuring the intensity of light.
The lucimeter showed a significant decrease in light levels after the storm.  暴风雨过后，光度计显示光照水平显著下降。

The scientist used a lucimeter to measure the brightness of the newly discovered star.  科学家使用光度计测量新发现的恒星的亮度。

近义词:photometer, light meter
反义词:N/A
词性:n.

Etymology:
From Latin *lūci- (stem of lūx, "light") + -meter ("measuring instrument").
