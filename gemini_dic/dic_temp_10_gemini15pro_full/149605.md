The painfulness of the treatment was unbearable. 治疗过程的痛苦难以忍受。|近义词：agony, suffering, torment, torture|反义词:pleasure, comfort, ease|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From painful + -ness.  Painful traces back to Old French *painful (Modern French pénible), from peine "pain, penalty" + -ful.  Ultimately from Latin poena "punishment, penalty."
