eremitage:/ˈɛrəmɪtɪdʒ/ |n.|隐居处，遁世处，修道院|A secluded residence, typically where a hermit lives; a place of solitude or retreat, sometimes a monastery.|He sought solace in his eremitage high in the mountains. 他在高山上的隐居处寻求慰藉。|Synonyms: hermitage, retreat, sanctuary, seclusion, solitude|Antonyms: city, society, community|noun
