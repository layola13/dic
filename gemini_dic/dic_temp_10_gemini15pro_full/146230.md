overexpose:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspoʊz/ /ˌəʊvərɪkˈspəʊz/ | vt. |过度曝光；过分暴露|To expose something, especially film, to too much light or for too long, or to publicize someone or something too much. |例句：The film was overexposed and came out too light. 这部电影曝光过度，出来的画面太亮了。

例句：The celebrity felt overexposed by the media.  这位名人感到被媒体过度曝光了。|近义词：over publicize, saturate|反义词：underexpose, conceal|词性: 动词
