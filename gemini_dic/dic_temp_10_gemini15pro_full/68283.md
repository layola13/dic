falafel: /fəˈlɑːfəl/ (US), /fəˈlæfəl/ (UK) | n. | 炸鹰嘴豆丸子，沙拉三明治 | Deep-fried balls or patties made from ground chickpeas or fava beans, often served in pita bread with various toppings and sauces.  | Example: I had a delicious falafel sandwich for lunch. 我午餐吃了一个美味的炸鹰嘴豆丸子三明治。

| Example:  Falafel is a popular street food in the Middle East. 炸鹰嘴豆丸子是中东地区流行的街头小吃。 | Synonym: chickpea fritters, fava bean fritters | Antonym: N/A | noun
