nonfluids:/nɒnˈfluːɪdz/
n.
非流体
Substances that do not flow easily like liquids or gases.
Nonfluids, like sand or gravel, behave differently under pressure than fluids.  非流体，例如沙子或砾石，在压力下的表现与流体不同。
Solids are typically considered nonfluids. 固体通常被认为是非流体。
Synonyms: solids, non-liquids
Antonyms: fluids, liquids, gases
noun
