count-in:/ˈkaʊntɪn/| n.|计数，读秒；(尤指音乐的)预备拍|The band waited for the drummer's count-in before starting the song. 乐队等待鼓手的预备拍后再开始演奏歌曲。|English explanation: A count-in is a short introductory passage, often a series of beats or numbers spoken aloud, that signals the start of a musical performance or recording. It helps musicians synchronize their playing. It can also refer to a countdown before an event.|例句：The conductor gave a four-beat count-in. 指挥给了一个四拍的预备拍。|例句：They started recording after a brief count-in.  他们在简短的倒计时后开始录音。|近义词：lead-in, intro, cue|反义词: outro, coda|词性:noun
