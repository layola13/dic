disputablenesses:/dɪˈspjuːtəbəlnəsɪz/

可争议性；争议点
The quality or state of being disputable; the fact of being open to question or debate.
The disputableness of the election results led to widespread protests.  选举结果的可争议性导致了广泛的抗议。

Antonyms: certainty,  indisputability
