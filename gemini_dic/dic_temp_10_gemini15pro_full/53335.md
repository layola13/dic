disacryl:/daɪˈsækrɪl/
n.
diacrylate|二丙烯酸酯;双丙烯酸酯|Disacryl is a type of monomer used in the production of various polymers. 二丙烯酸酯是一种用于生产各种聚合物的单体。|Disacryl resins are commonly used in dental fillings.  二丙烯酸酯树脂常用于牙科填充物。|近义词: diacrylate, crosslinking agent |反义词:  (No direct antonym. Could consider monomers that don't crosslink.)
词性: noun
