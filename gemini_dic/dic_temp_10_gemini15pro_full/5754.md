amadavat:/ˌæməˈdɑːvət/ | n. | 红梅花雀，红雀 | A small, brightly colored finch (Amandava amandava) native to India and Southeast Asia, popular as a cage bird. |  The amadavat is a popular cage bird due to its vibrant plumage.  红梅花雀因其鲜艳的羽毛而成为一种受欢迎的笼鸟。

 The male amadavat displays brilliant red plumage during the breeding season.  雄性红梅花雀在繁殖季节会展现出鲜红色的羽毛。 | 近义词: red avadavat, strawberry finch | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
