paperbacked:/ˈpeɪpərˌbækt/| adj.|平装本的，纸皮书的|A book that is bound in a flexible paper cover, typically less expensive than a hardcover edition.  Bound with a paper cover instead of a hard one. | I prefer paperbacked books because they are lighter and cheaper.| 近义词:softcover, softback| 反义词:hardcover, hardback|词性:形容词
