listerial:/lɪˈstɪəriəl/
adjective|李斯特菌的；李斯特菌病的|Relating to or caused by Listeria, a type of bacteria that can cause food poisoning.|The listerial outbreak was traced back to contaminated cantaloupe. 李斯特菌的爆发可以追溯到被污染的哈密瓜。|
近义词: bacterial (细菌的) |反义词: None (无)|词性: adjective


listeriosis:/ˌlɪstɪəˈriːoʊsɪs/
noun|李斯特菌病|A type of food poisoning caused by the bacterium Listeria monocytogenes.|Pregnant women are particularly susceptible to listeriosis. 孕妇特别容易感染李斯特菌病。|近义词: food poisoning (食物中毒) |反义词: None (无)|词性: noun
