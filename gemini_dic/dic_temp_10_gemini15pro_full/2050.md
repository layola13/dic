acupressure: /ˈækjʊprɛʃər/| n.|指压，穴位按摩|A form of massage, developed in Asia, in which pressure is applied to specific points on the body, believed to stimulate the flow of energy and promote healing.|She tried acupressure to relieve her back pain. 她尝试用指压来缓解背痛。|近义词: massage, shiatsu, reflexology|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps medication or surgery for pain relief)|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From acu- (needle, sharp) + pressure.  Ultimately from Latin *acus (needle).
