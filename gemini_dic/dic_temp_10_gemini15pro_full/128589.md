niceling:/ˈnaɪslɪŋ/ |n.|好人，讨人喜欢的人|A person who is exceptionally nice, often to a fault; someone excessively agreeable and eager to please.| He's such a niceling, he agrees with everything everyone says. 他真是个老好人，别人说什么他都同意。|近义词:people-pleaser, goody-goody, yes-man|反义词:jerk, curmudgeon|词性:名词

词源: "nice" + "-ling" (a suffix denoting a diminutive person or thing, often with a negative connotation). While "nice" has a positive meaning, the suffix "-ling" can add a sense of weakness or excessive agreeableness.
