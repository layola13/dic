fascicularly:/fəˈsɪkjʊləli/
| adv. |束状地；簇生地|In botany, arranged in or forming small bundles or fascicles.  This describes how parts of a plant, like leaves or flowers, grow together in a close bundle or cluster.|The needles of the pine tree are arranged fascicularly.|近义词：in bundles, in clusters, in groups|反义词：singly, individually, separately|词性:副词
fascicle:/ˈfæsɪkəl/
| n. |小束，簇；分册|A small bundle, cluster, or collection; a separately published installment of a book.|The fascicle of nerves controlled the movement of his hand.  The publisher released the encyclopedia in fascicles.|近义词：bundle, cluster, sheaf, part, installment|反义词：none (lacks a direct antonym)|词性:名词
