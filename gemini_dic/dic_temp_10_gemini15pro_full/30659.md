cerograph: /ˈsɪərəɡræf/ /ˈsɪrəɡræf/ | n. |蜡版；用蜡板记录的东西；蜡版记录|An instrument for recording variations in the thickness of a wax coating, especially as a method of measuring variations in the growth of plants.| The botanist used a cerograph to track the minute changes in the tree's diameter. 植物学家使用蜡版仪来追踪树木直径的微小变化。 |Near Synonyms:  recording device, measuring instrument | Antonyms: None | Noun
