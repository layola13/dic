furl:/fɜːrl/ | n. | furlong的缩写，浪高；收起，卷起 | A measure of distance, equal to one-eighth of a mile (201.17 meters).  Also, the act of rolling or folding something up, such as a sail or a flag. | The yacht was making good time, with all sails unfurled.  游艇全速前进，所有的帆都张开了。 | Synonyms: roll up, fold, stow, furlong | Antonyms: unfurl, unfold, spread | noun
furl:/fɜːrl/ | v. | 收帆；卷起；折叠 | To roll or fold up and secure, as a sail or a flag. | He furled the sails before the storm hit. 他在暴风雨来临前收起了帆。 | Synonyms: roll up, fold, stow  | Antonyms: unfurl, unfold, spread | verb
