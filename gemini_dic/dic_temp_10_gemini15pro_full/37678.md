compadre:/kəmˈpɑːdreɪ/ /kəmˈpɑːdri/ | n. |朋友；伙伴；老兄；(尤指拉丁美洲国家的)教父 |A close friend or companion, especially in Spanish-speaking cultures. Often used to refer to a godfather. |例句：He greeted his compadre with a warm embrace. 他热情地拥抱了他的朋友。

例句：They've been compadres since childhood.  他们从小就是朋友。|近义词：friend, pal, buddy, chum, mate, amigo |反义词:enemy, foe |词性:noun

compadre:/kəmˈpɑːdreɪ/ /kəmˈpɑːdri/ | n. |教父|The godfather of one's child. |例句：He asked his closest friend to be his son's compadre. 他请他最亲密的朋友做他儿子的教父。

例句：The compadre held the baby during the baptism. 教父在洗礼仪式上抱着婴儿。|近义词：godfather |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
