esteeming:/ɪˈstiːmɪŋ/
verb
尊重; 尊敬; 认为
regarding someone or something as having value or worth.
He esteemed his colleagues highly. 他非常尊重他的同事。
Synonyms: respecting, valuing, appreciating, admiring, honoring, revering, prizing
Antonyms: despising, scorning, disdaining
词性: 动词 (present participle of esteem)
