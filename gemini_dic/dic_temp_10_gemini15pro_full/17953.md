befitting:/bɪˈfɪtɪŋ/
adjective
适当的，相称的
Suitable or appropriate; proper.
She wore a gown befitting a queen. 她穿着一件适合女王的礼服。
近义词:appropriate, suitable, fitting, apt, proper, seemly, decorous
反义词:inappropriate, unsuitable, unfitting, improper, unbecoming, unseemly, indecorous
词性:adjective

词源:From befit + -ing.  From be- + fit (suitable).
