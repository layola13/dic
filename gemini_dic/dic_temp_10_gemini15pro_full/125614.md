multivocal:/ˌmʌltiˈvoʊkəl/ | adj. | 多音的；多义的；有多种解释的 | Having many voices or interpretations; capable of being understood in multiple ways. | The poem is multivocal, allowing for a range of interpretations. 这首诗是多义的，允许多种解读。| The artist's work is intentionally multivocal, inviting viewers to draw their own conclusions. 这位艺术家的作品有意地采用了多义的表现手法，邀请观赏者得出自己的结论。 | 近义词: ambiguous, polysemous, multifaceted | 反义词: univocal, unambiguous, singular | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *multivocalis*, from *multi-* (many) + *vocalis* (vocal, relating to voice).  The word entered English in the mid-19th century.
