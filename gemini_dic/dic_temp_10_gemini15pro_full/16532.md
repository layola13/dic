barking:/ˈbɑːrkɪŋ/ (US), /ˈbɑːkɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. n.|吠叫的，狗叫声；狂吠的；十足的，非常的|The continuous, sharp sound made by a dog; also used figuratively to describe something loud, harsh, or insistent.  Derived from the verb "bark."  Old English *beorcan "to bark," of imitative origin.|

例句：
The barking dog woke the entire neighborhood.
那条狂吠的狗吵醒了整个街区。

The barking of the seals was deafening.
海豹的叫声震耳欲聋。

He has a barking cough.
他咳嗽得厉害。

It's barking mad to go out in this weather.
在这种天气出门简直是疯了。


近义词：yelping, howling, baying (for dogs); loud, harsh, insistent (figurative)|
反义词:quiet, silent|
词性:adjective, noun|


barking:/ˈbɑːrkɪŋ/ (US), /ˈbɑːkɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|吠叫，狗吠声|The act of a dog making a sharp, loud sound.|

例句：
The constant barking kept me awake all night.
不断的狗吠声让我整夜没睡。

The barking grew louder as we approached the house.
随着我们靠近房子，狗吠声越来越大。


近义词：yelping, howling, baying|
反义词:whimpering, silence|
词性:noun|
