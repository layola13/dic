butterinesses:/ˈbʌtərɪnɪsɪz/
n.
油腻感；奶油味；黄油味
The quality of being buttery; resembling or containing butter.
The butterinesses of the croissants were delightful.  羊角面包的奶油味很令人愉悦。
近义词: richness, creaminess, fattiness
反义词: dryness, staleness
