parched:/pɑːrtʃt/ /pɑːrʧt/
adjective
干涸的；焦渴的
dried out with heat; extremely thirsty
The parched earth cracked under the intense sun.  在烈日下，干涸的土地裂开了。
The travelers were parched after walking through the desert.  旅行者在穿过沙漠后感到口渴难耐。
近义词:arid, dry, dehydrated, thirsty
反义词:moist, wet, hydrated
词性:adjective
词源:From Middle English *perchen, from Old French perchier, percher (“to become dry, wither”), of uncertain origin.
