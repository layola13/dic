interclouded:/ˌɪntərˈklaʊdɪd/ | adj. |  间有云的；时阴时晴的 | Partially or intermittently covered with clouds; alternately clear and cloudy. | The sky was interclouded, with brief bursts of sunshine. 天空间有云，阳光时而照射下来。 |  The weather forecast predicted an interclouded day with occasional showers. 天气预报预测今天是间有云的天气，偶尔会有阵雨。 | 近义词: partly cloudy, variably cloudy | 反义词: clear, cloudless | 词性: 形容词
