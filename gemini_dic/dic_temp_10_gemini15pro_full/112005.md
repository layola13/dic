lonesomenesses:/ˈloʊnsəmnɪsɪz/ | n. | 孤独，寂寞 | The state of being lonesome; feelings of sadness and isolation. |  She experienced deep lonesomenesses after her husband died. 她丈夫去世后，她感到非常孤独。| synonyms: loneliness, isolation, solitude | antonyms: togetherness, companionship | noun

lonesome:/ˈloʊnsəm/ | adj. | 孤独的，寂寞的 | Sad and lonely because you are not with other people. | The lonesome wail of a train whistle echoed through the valley. 火车汽笛的孤独呜咽声在山谷中回荡。| synonyms: lonely, solitary, isolated | antonyms: accompanied, sociable | adjective
