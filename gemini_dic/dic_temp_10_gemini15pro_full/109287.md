lemur:/ˈliːmə(r)/| n. |狐猴|A small, arboreal primate with a pointed snout, large eyes, and a long tail, native to Madagascar.|The lemur leaped from branch to branch. 狐猴在树枝间跳跃。|近义词：primate, monkey (loosely)|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider predator like fossa)|noun
