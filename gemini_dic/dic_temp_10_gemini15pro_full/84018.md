half-drowned:/ˌhæf ˈdraʊnd/ | adj. | 半淹死的，快要淹死的 | Almost drowned, but not completely.  Having nearly drowned and still suffering the effects. |  The half-drowned sailor was pulled aboard the lifeboat.  半淹死的海员被拉上了救生艇。|  Near-drowned, partially drowned | Alive, dry | adjective

half-drowned:/ˌhæf ˈdraʊnd/ | adj. | 奄奄一息的，精疲力竭的（比喻） | Exhausted and weakened, as if having nearly drowned. Figurative use to describe extreme fatigue or distress. |  After running the marathon, I felt half-drowned.  跑完马拉松后，我感觉快要累死了。|  Exhausted, drained, spent | Energetic, refreshed | adjective
