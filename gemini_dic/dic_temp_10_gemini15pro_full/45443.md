curacy: /ˈkjʊərəsi/ | n. | 准确性，精确性 | The accuracy of the measurements is crucial to the success of the experiment.  测量结果的准确性对实验的成功至关重要。| Example: The archer was known for his incredible accuracy.  这位弓箭手以其惊人的准确性而闻名。| Synonyms: precision, exactness, correctness | Antonyms: inaccuracy, imprecision, error | noun

Etymology:  From Late Latin *accuratia, from Latin accūrātus (“prepared with care, exact, elaborate”), past participle of accūrō (“I take care of, attend to”), from ad- (“to”) + cūra (“care”).
