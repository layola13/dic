aurality:/ɔːˈrælɪti/
n.
听觉性；听觉特性
The quality of being perceived through the sense of hearing.  Relating to or experienced through hearing.
The composer explored the aurality of the piece, focusing on the interplay of different sounds. 作曲家探索了这首作品的听觉性，重点关注不同声音的相互作用。

The aurality of the rainforest is overwhelming, with the constant buzzing of insects and calls of birds.  雨林的听觉环境令人震撼，昆虫不断嗡嗡作响，鸟儿不停地鸣叫。
近义词：audibility, sonority
反义词：inaudibility, silence
词性：noun
