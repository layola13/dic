geodephagous:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈdɛfəɡəs/
adjective
食土的；食泥的
Feeding on earthy matter, especially clay.
例句：Certain species of parrots are known for their geodephagous behavior, consuming clay to neutralize toxins in their diet.  某些种类的鹦鹉以其食土行为而闻名，它们食用黏土来中和饮食中的毒素。

例句：The scientist observed the geodephagous habits of the pregnant chimpanzee.  科学家观察了怀孕黑猩猩的食土习惯。

近义词：clay-eating, earth-eating
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *gē* (earth) + *-ēd-* (to eat) + *-phagos* (eater).
