outvoting:/ˌaʊtˈvoʊtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
以多数票击败
defeating by a higher number of votes
The chairman was outvoted by the board members. 董事长被董事会成员以多数票否决。
The proposal was outvoted by a narrow margin.  这项提议以微弱的差距被否决了。
近义词: defeating, overruling
反义词: losing the vote, being defeated
