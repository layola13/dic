ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hēafian, from Proto-Germanic *hafjaną (“to lift, raise”), from Proto-Indo-European *keh₂p- (“to seize, grasp”).  Cognate with Old Frisian heva (“to lift”), Old Saxon hebbian (“to lift”), Dutch heffen (“to lift”), German heben (“to lift”), Old Norse hefja (“to lift”), Gothic 𐌷𐌰𐍆𐌾𐌰𐌽 (hafjan, “to lift”).
