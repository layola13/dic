noncrime:/ˈnɑːnkraɪm/ | n. | 非犯罪行为 | An act that is not a crime, often referring to behaviors that were previously considered criminal but have been decriminalized, or actions that are not legally defined as crimes despite causing harm or being morally questionable. |  Many argue that drug use should be treated as a public health issue, a noncrime, rather than a criminal offense. 许多人认为，吸毒应该被视为一个公共卫生问题，一种非犯罪行为，而不是刑事犯罪。 | Near synonyms:  non-offense, lawful act | Antonyms: crime, offense | noun

noncrime:/ˈnɑːnkraɪm/ | adj. | 非犯罪的 |  Relating to or characteristic of something that is not a crime. | The report categorized the incident as a noncrime disturbance. 该报告将这起事件归类为非犯罪骚乱。 | Near synonyms: non-criminal, lawful | Antonyms: criminal, illegal | adjective
