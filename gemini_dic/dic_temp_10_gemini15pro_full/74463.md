frankenfoods:/ˈfræŋkənˌfuːdz/
n.
转基因食品，基因改造食品
Genetically modified (GM) foods, often viewed negatively due to perceived unnatural manipulation.
Frankenfoods are a controversial topic, with some people concerned about potential health risks.  转基因食品是一个有争议的话题，有些人担心潜在的健康风险。

Many consumers avoid frankenfoods due to ethical concerns. 许多消费者出于伦理方面的考虑而避免食用转基因食品。

近义词: genetically modified foods, GM foods
反义词: organic foods, natural foods
词性: 名词 (Noun)

词源:
A blend of "Frankenstein" (from Mary Shelley's novel) and "foods."  The term evokes the image of something monstrous or unnatural created through scientific manipulation, reflecting public anxieties about genetic engineering.  由“弗兰肯斯坦”（来自玛丽·雪莱的小说）和“食物”混合而成。该词唤起了一种通过科学操作创造出的怪异或非自然事物的形象，反映了公众对基因工程的焦虑。
