centigram: /ˈsɛntɪɡræm/ | n. | 厘克 | A unit of mass equal to one hundredth of a gram. | A centigram is a small unit of mass. 厘克是一个很小的质量单位。

The chemist measured out 2 centigrams of the powder.  化学家量取了2厘克的粉末。

A ladybug weighs about one centigram. 一只瓢虫的重量大约为一厘克。


|近义词: cg (abbreviation) |反义词: kilogram, gram |词性: noun
