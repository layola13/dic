limbec:/lɪmˈbɛk/| n.|蒸馏器，蒸馏釜|An apparatus used for distilling, especially alcohol; an alembic.|The alchemist used a limbec to purify his concoctions.  炼金术士用蒸馏器来提纯他的混合物。|例句：The old limbec sat unused in the corner of the distillery.  旧的蒸馏器闲置在酿酒厂的角落里。

例句：He carefully heated the limbec, watching the condensation form. 他小心翼翼地加热蒸馏器，观察冷凝物的形成。|近义词：alembic, still, retort|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
