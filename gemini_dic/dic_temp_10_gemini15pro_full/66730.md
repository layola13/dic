exorcise:/ˈɛksɔːrsaɪz/ /ˈɛksərˌsaɪz/| vt.|驱魔；去除(坏念头等)；|To drive out an evil spirit from a person, place, or thing; to free from bad thoughts or feelings.|例句：The priest exorcised the demons from the possessed man. 神父从被附身的人身上驱走了恶魔。|例句：She tried to exorcise the painful memories from her mind. 她试图从脑海中抹去痛苦的回忆。|近义词：expel, banish, purge|反义词:summon, invite|词性:动词

exorcise:/ˈɛksɔːrsaɪz/ /ˈɛksərˌsaɪz/| n.|驱魔；驱邪|The act or practice of exorcising.|例句：The exorcism of the house was performed by a local shaman.  这栋房子的驱魔仪式是由当地一位萨满进行的。|例句：The film depicted a terrifying exorcism. 这部电影描述了一场可怕的驱魔仪式。|近义词：purification, cleansing|反义词: - |词性:名词
