coaptation:/ˌkoʊæpˈteɪʃən/
n.
适应；接合；复位
The adaptation and fitting of things together.  The process of setting a broken bone.
The coaptation of the broken bone was successful. 断骨的复位很成功。
近义词：adaptation, adjustment, accommodation, fitting, setting, reduction
反义词：dislocation, separation
词性：名词

coaptation:/ˌkoʊæpˈteɪʃən/
n.
接合；配合
The act or process of fitting together or adapting.
The surgeon performed a coaptation of the fractured bone.  外科医生对骨折的骨头进行了接合。
近义词：junction, union, connection, joining
反义词：disunion, separation
词性：名词


词源: From Late Latin coaptātiōn- (stem of coaptātiō ), from coaptāre ‘fit together,’ from co- ‘together’ + aptāre ‘fit.’
