baksheesh:/bækˈʃiːʃ/ /ˈbɑːkˌʃiːʃ/
Noun
小费，赏钱，贿赂
A tip or gratuity, especially one given in Middle Eastern countries; also, a bribe.
He offered the border guard a small baksheesh to expedite his passage.  他给边防警卫一点小费，以加快通关。
Synonym: tip, gratuity, bribe, kickback
Antonym:  penalty, fine
词性: 名词


baksheesh:/bækˈʃiːʃ/ /ˈbɑːkˌʃiːʃ/
Verb
给小费，行贿
To give a tip or bribe.
He baksheeshed the waiter generously. 他慷慨地给了服务员小费。
Synonym: tip, bribe
Antonym: penalize, fine
词性: 动词
