go-missing:/ɡəʊ ˈmɪsɪŋ/| v. |失踪，不见|To become lost or disappear; to be unaccounted for.|The hiker went missing in the mountains.  那个徒步旅行者在山中失踪了。|My keys have gone missing again. 我的钥匙又不见了。|近义词：disappear, vanish, get lost|反义词:appear, be found|词性:动词
词源: "go" (Old English *gan, "to go") + "missing" (present participle of "miss," from Old English missan, "to fail to hit, miss").  The phrase "go missing" emerged in the late 19th century.
