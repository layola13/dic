micronuclear: /ˌmaɪkroʊˈnjuːkliər/ | adj. | 微核的 | Relating to or constituting a micronucleus. |  Micronuclear tests can detect chromosomal damage. 微核测试可以检测染色体损伤。|  Micronuclear abnormalities were observed in the cells. 在细胞中观察到微核异常。| 近义词:  karyorrhectic (describing nuclear fragmentation) | 反义词:  macronuclear (referring to the large nucleus in ciliates)|词性: 形容词

micronucleus: /ˌmaɪkroʊˈnjuːkliəs/ | n. | 微核 | A small, extra nucleus in a cell, typically formed from chromosome fragments or whole chromosomes that were not incorporated into the main nucleus during cell division. It is a marker of genomic instability and can be used in toxicology studies. | The presence of micronuclei indicates DNA damage. 微核的存在表明DNA损伤。|  The researchers counted the micronuclei in the blood cells. 研究人员计算了血细胞中的微核数量。 | 近义词: Howell-Jolly body (a similar nuclear remnant, but with different origin) | 反义词: macronucleus |词性: 名词
