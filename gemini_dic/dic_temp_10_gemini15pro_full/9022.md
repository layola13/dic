She was prescribed antimalarial tablets before travelling to Africa. 她去非洲旅行前开了些抗疟疾药片。|
The clinic distributes antimalarial medication to the local population.  诊所向当地居民分发抗疟药物。|
近义词: antimalarial drug, anti-malaria medicine |反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider:  malaria-causing,  malaria-promoting) |词性: adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- (prefix meaning "against") + malarial (relating to malaria).  Malaria itself comes from Italian mala aria ("bad air"), reflecting the former belief that the disease was caused by bad air.
