firestorms:/ˈfaɪərstɔːrmz/| n. |火风暴；火灾风暴|A large-scale conflagration producing strong, inward-spiraling winds. A firestorm is characterized by intense heat and rapid spread, often consuming everything in its path.|The firestorms after the bombing of Dresden were devastating. 德累斯顿轰炸后的火风暴是毁灭性的。| The firestorms raged for days, leaving a trail of destruction. 火风暴肆虐了数日，留下一片破坏的痕迹。 |近义词：conflagration, inferno|反义词: drizzle, shower|名词
