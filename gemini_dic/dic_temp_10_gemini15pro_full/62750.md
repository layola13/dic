engorge:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒ/ /ɛnˈɡɔːrdʒ/| v. |使充血；使充盈；狼吞虎咽|to cause to swell with blood, fluid, or food; to devour greedily|The mosquito engorged itself with blood.  蚊子吸饱了血。|近义词：swell, bloat, distend, gorge, stuff|反义词:shrink, contract|词性:动词

engorged:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒd/ /ɛnˈɡɔːrdʒd/| adj. |充血的；充盈的|swollen with blood, fluid, or food|The tick was engorged after feeding.  蜱虫吃饱后鼓起来了。|近义词：swollen, bloated, distended, full|反义词:empty, deflated|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French engorger "to swallow up, devour," from en- "in" + gorge "throat."
