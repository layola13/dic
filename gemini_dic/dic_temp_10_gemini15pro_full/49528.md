demarcate:/ˈdiːmɑːrkeɪt/ (US), /dɪˈmɑːkeɪt/ (UK)| v. |划定界限，标明界限|To set the boundaries or limits of something, often clearly or precisely. |例句：The new fence demarcates the boundary between the two properties. 新的栅栏划定了这两处房产之间的界限。

例句：The river demarcates the border between the two countries. 这条河流标明了两国之间的边界。|近义词：define, delimit, distinguish, separate, mark off|反义词：merge, blend, confuse, obscure|词性:动词
