gouland:/ˈɡuːlənd/| n.|贪吃的人，贪婪的人|A person who eats greedily or is excessively fond of food; a glutton.|He's such a gouland, he ate the entire cake by himself. 他真是个贪吃鬼，把整个蛋糕都自己吃了。|近义词：glutton, gourmand, gormandizer|反义词: ascetic|词性:名词
