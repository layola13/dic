gloxinia: /ɡlɒkˈsɪniə/ | n. |大岩桐，落雪泥|A tropical South American plant of the gesneria family, widely cultivated as a houseplant for its velvety leaves and bell-shaped flowers.|例句：The gloxinia on the windowsill is blooming beautifully. 窗台上的大岩桐开得很漂亮。

例句：She received a gloxinia as a birthday gift. 她收到了一盆大岩桐作为生日礼物。|近义词：sinningia (Technically, Sinningia is the correct genus name, but gloxinia is still commonly used.)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
