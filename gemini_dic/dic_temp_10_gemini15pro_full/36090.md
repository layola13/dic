coelospermous:/ˌsiːləˈspɜːrməs/
adj.
中空种子的；腹缝线的
Having seeds that are hollow on one side, as in the Umbelliferae.
The coelospermous fruits are characteristic of the Apiaceae family.  这种中空种子的果实是伞形科植物的特征。
Coelospermous seeds are adapted for wind dispersal. 中空种子适合风力传播。
近义词: hollow-seeded
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "solid-seeded" though not a standard term)
词性: adjective
