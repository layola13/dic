ayn:/aɪn/ | determiner, pronoun | which, what |  An archaic or dialect form of "which" or "what," particularly common in Scottish and Northern English dialects.  It can refer to one of a group, or inquire about something. | Ayn o' ye is comin' wi' me? (Which of you is coming with me?) |  which, what | such, this | determiner, pronoun
