hyperimmunized:/ˌhaɪpərˈɪmjənaɪzd/
adjective
高度免疫的
Having been made highly resistant to a specific disease or toxin through repeated injections of an antigen.
The hyperimmunized horses were used to produce antivenom.  这些高度免疫的马被用来生产抗蛇毒血清。
The researchers used hyperimmunized mice in their study.  研究人员在他们的研究中使用了高度免疫的小鼠。
Synonyms: highly immunized, highly resistant
Antonyms: non-immunized, susceptible
