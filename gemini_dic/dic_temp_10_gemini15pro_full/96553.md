indorsed:/ɪnˈdɔːrst/ /ɪnˈdɔːrsd/
verb (past tense)|认可；签署；背书；赞同| To give one's public approval or support to.|The proposal was endorsed by the committee.| 近义词: supported, approved, sanctioned, signed, countersigned |反义词: opposed, rejected, disapproved
词性:动词

indorsed:/ɪnˈdɔːrst/ /ɪnˈdɔːrsd/
adjective|（支票等）背书的；经过签字同意的|Having been signed on the back, typically a check or other financial document.|The endorsed check was deposited into the account.|近义词: signed, countersigned, approved |反义词: unsigned, unapproved
词性:形容词
