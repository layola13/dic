allusiveness:/əˈluːsɪvnəs/| n.|暗示性；含蓄；隐晦|The poem is characterized by its allusiveness and ambiguity. 这首诗的特点是它的暗示性和歧义性。|例句：The allusiveness of her remarks made it difficult to understand her true meaning. 她话语的含蓄性让人难以理解她的真实意思。

例句：His writing style is known for its allusiveness, drawing upon a vast range of literary and historical references. 他的写作风格以其暗示性而闻名，借鉴了大量的文学和历史典故。|近义词：indirectness, suggestiveness, implication|反义词：directness, explicitness, clarity|词性:名词
