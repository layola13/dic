acinesia: /ˌeɪsaɪˈniːʒə/ /ˌeɪsaɪˈniːziə/
noun
运动不能，运动迟缓
Loss or impairment of the power of voluntary movement.
The patient exhibited acinesia of the left arm after the stroke.  病人中风后左臂出现运动不能。
Acinesia can be a symptom of Parkinson's disease. 运动不能可能是帕金森病的一种症状。

近义词: akinesia, hypokinesia, bradykinesia
反义词: hyperkinesia
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *a* (without) + *kinesis* (movement)
