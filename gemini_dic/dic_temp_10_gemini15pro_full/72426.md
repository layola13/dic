flugelman:/ˈfluːɡəlmən/| n.|领队，指挥，带头人|A soldier who formerly acted as a guide to others in drill or other exercises; a leader or director (especially of a political group).|The flugelman signaled the start of the march. 领队发出开始游行的信号。|Synonyms: leader, director, conductor, frontman, bellwether|Antonyms: follower, subordinate|noun

flugelman:/ˈfluːɡəlmən/| n.|领队，指挥，带头人|He acted as the flugelman for the entire political movement. 他担任了整个政治运动的领队。|Synonyms: leader, director, conductor, frontman, bellwether|Antonyms: follower, subordinate|noun
