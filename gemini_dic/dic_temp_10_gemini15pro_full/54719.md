disoxidation:/ˌdɪsˌɒksɪˈdeɪʃən/
noun
脱氧，去氧化
The process of removing oxygen from a substance.
The disoxidation of iron ore is a crucial step in steel production. 钢铁生产中，铁矿石的脱氧是至关重要的一个步骤。
