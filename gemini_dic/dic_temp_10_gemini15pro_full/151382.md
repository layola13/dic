parliament:/ˈpɑːrləmənt/ (US) /ˈpɑːləmənt/ (UK)| n. |议会，国会|A national legislature, especially that of Great Britain or a similar one in another country.|The parliament voted in favor of the new law. 国会投票赞成新法律。|近义词：congress, legislature, assembly|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "dictatorship")|noun
