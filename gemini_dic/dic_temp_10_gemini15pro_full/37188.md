comedietta:/ˌkɒmɪdiˈɛtə/ /ˌkɑːmədiˈɛtə/| n. |小喜剧，短喜剧|A short, light comedy; a short comic play or dramatic piece, often light and amusing. |例句：The theater group performed a charming comedietta about mistaken identities.  剧团表演了一出关于身份错乱的迷人小喜剧。

例句：She wrote a witty comedietta for the school's drama festival. 她为学校戏剧节写了一部诙谐的小喜剧。|近义词：farce, skit, playlet|反义词：tragedy, drama|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *comedietta*, diminutive of *commedia* "comedy," from Latin *comoedia*, from Greek *kōmōidia*.
