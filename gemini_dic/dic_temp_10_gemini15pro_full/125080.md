mulled:/mʌld/| adj. |加热的（通常指葡萄酒），深思熟虑的|Heated and spiced (typically of wine); thought over carefully|例句：We drank mulled wine at the Christmas market. 我们在圣诞市场喝了热红酒。|近义词：heated, warmed, spiced, considered, contemplated|反义词：chilled, cooled, unconsidered|词性:形容词

mulled:/mʌld/| v. |加热（通常指葡萄酒），深思熟虑|To heat and spice (typically wine); to think over carefully|例句：She mulled over the offer for a few days before accepting. 她仔细考虑了几天才接受这个提议。|近义词：heat, warm, spice, consider, contemplate|反义词：cool, chill, ignore|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mullen*, likely from Old French *moeler* (to soften), from Latin *mollire* (to soften), from *mollis* (soft).  The sense of "to ponder" developed later, perhaps influenced by the idea of warming something up in the mind.
