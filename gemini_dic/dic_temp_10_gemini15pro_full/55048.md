disquisitional:/ˌdɪskwɪˈzɪʃənəl/
adjective
探讨性的；论述的；论文体的
Characterized by or relating to formal systematic inquiry; resembling or suitable for a dissertation or treatise.
His writing style is dense and disquisitional. 他的写作风格浓厚且充满论述性。

The professor delivered a disquisitional lecture on the history of philosophy.  教授做了一个关于哲学史的专题演讲。

近义词: analytical, argumentative, critical,  expository,  scholarly,  systematic,  theoretical
反义词:  superficial,  uncritical,  imprecise,  vague


词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *disquisition- (stem of disquisitio ), from disquirere 'seek out, examine,' from dis- 'apart' + quaerere 'seek.'
