overdrive:/ˈoʊvərdraɪv/ (US) /ˈəʊvədraɪv/ (UK)| n. v.|超速运转；加倍努力； overdrive (n.) 最高档； overdrive (v.) 使过度运转|A state of increased or excessive activity, effort, or productivity.  (n.) The highest gear in a motor vehicle. (v.) To work or use something too hard or too much.|

例句：
The factory is in overdrive to meet the Christmas orders. = 工厂正加紧生产以完成圣诞订单。

The media went into overdrive when the scandal broke. =  丑闻曝光后，媒体大肆报道。


He put the car into overdrive. = 他把车挂到最高档。

She's been overdriving herself and needs a break. = 她一直把自己逼得太紧，需要休息一下。


近义词：overwork, strain, push, accelerate, high gear|
反义词：underdrive, relax, slow down|
词性: noun, verb
