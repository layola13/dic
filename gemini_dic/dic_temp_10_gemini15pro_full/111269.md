litotes:/ˈlaɪtəˌtiːz/ /ˈlɪtəˌtiːz/| n.|低调陈述; 反语|A figure of speech consisting of an understatement in which an affirmative is expressed by negating its opposite.|He's not the brightest crayon in the box. (Meaning: He's not very intelligent.) 他并不是盒子里最亮的那支蜡笔。（意思是：他不怎么聪明。）

She's not unlike her mother. (Meaning: She's similar to her mother.) 她和她母亲并非不像。（意思是：她和她母亲很像。）|近义词:understatement, meiosis|反义词:hyperbole, overstatement|词性:noun
