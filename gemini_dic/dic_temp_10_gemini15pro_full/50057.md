dens:/dɛnz/| n.|窝，穴|A wild animal's den is the place where it lives and sleeps. 野生动物的窝是它居住和睡觉的地方。|The bear retreated to its den for the winter. 熊回到它的洞穴过冬。|lair, burrow, nest|cave, house|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dēns* (tooth), related to Proto-Indo-European *h₂dónts*. The shift in meaning from "tooth" to "den" likely occurred due to the association of animal dens with teeth, perhaps because of bones found within them.
