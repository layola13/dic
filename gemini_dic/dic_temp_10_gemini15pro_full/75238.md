frolicness:/ˈfrɑːlɪknəs/ | n. |嬉戏; 欢乐; |The quality or state of being frolicsome; gaiety; merriment. |  The children's frolicness filled the park with laughter. 孩子们的嬉闹让公园充满了笑声。 | synonyms: gaiety, merriment, playfulness, jollity, mirth, cheerfulness, lightheartedness | antonyms: seriousness, solemnity, gloominess | noun

Etymology: From frolic + -ness.  Frolic itself comes from the Middle Dutch "vrolyc" meaning joyful, merry.
