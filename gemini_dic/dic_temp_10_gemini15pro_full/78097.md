geographer:/dʒiˈɒɡrəfər/ (UK) /dʒiˈɑːɡrəfər/ (US)| n. |地理学家|A person who studies geography, the earth's physical features, climates, populations, resources, etc.|例句：The geographer studied the effects of climate change on coastal communities.  这位地理学家研究了气候变化对沿海社区的影响。

geographer:| n. |地理学家|A scholar whose area of study is geography.|例句：The geographer presented his findings on the migration patterns of birds.  这位地理学家介绍了他对鸟类迁徙模式的研究结果。|近义词：cartographer, topographer|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
