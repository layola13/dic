hematoma:/ˌhiːməˈtoʊmə/ (US), /ˌhiːməˈtəʊmə/ (UK)| n. |血肿|A localized swelling filled with blood, caused by a break in a blood vessel.|The boxer suffered a severe hematoma above his left eye.|近义词：bruise, contusion|反义词: (No direct antonym for a physical condition like hematoma)|noun
