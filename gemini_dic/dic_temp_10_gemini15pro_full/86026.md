healingly:/ˈhiːlɪŋli/
adverb
治愈地，有益健康地
in a way that promotes healing or well-being
Her touch was healingly gentle. 她的触摸轻柔地治愈着我。

近义词:therapeutically, restoratively, soothingly
反义词:harmfully, destructively

词性:adverb
词源:From heal + -ing + -ly.  Ultimately from the Proto-Germanic *hailjaną (“to heal”).
