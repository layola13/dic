panoramically:/ˌpænərˈæmɪkli/
| adv. | 全景地；全方位地 | In a way that relates to or resembles a panorama; so as to afford a wide or complete view.  |  We viewed the city panoramically from the top of the hill. 我们从山顶上全景式地欣赏了这座城市。 | 近义词：comprehensively, broadly, widely | 反义词：narrowly, restrictedly | 词性: 副词

panoramically:/ˌpænərˈæmɪkli/
|  |  | Etymology: From panorama + -ical + -ly.  "Panorama" comes from Greek "pan" (all) and "horama" (sight). |  |  |
