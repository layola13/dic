controllability:/kənˌtroʊləˈbɪləti/ | n. |可控性；可控制性|The quality of being controllable; the extent to which something can be controlled. |The controllability of the aircraft was compromised due to the strong winds. 由于强风，飞机的可控性受到了影响。|Example 2: The government is trying to improve the controllability of inflation. 政府正在努力提高通货膨胀的可控性。|synonyms: manageability, governability, maneuverability |antonyms: uncontrollability, unmanageability, intractability |noun

controllable:/kənˈtroʊləbl/ | adj. |可控制的；可支配的|Capable of being controlled. |The fire was quickly controllable. 火势很快得到了控制。|Example 2:  Is your anger controllable? 你的愤怒可以控制吗？|synonyms: manageable, governable, restrainable |antonyms: uncontrollable, unmanageable, irrepressible |adjective
