europop:/ˈjʊərəʊpɒp/ (UK) /ˈjʊroʊpɑːp/ (US)| n. |欧洲流行音乐|A genre of popular music originating in Europe, typically featuring catchy melodies, electronic instrumentation, and often multilingual lyrics. |Example:  Europop was incredibly popular in the 1990s. 欧洲流行音乐在20世纪90年代非常流行。

Example: Many consider ABBA to be pioneers of the europop sound. 许多人认为ABBA是欧洲流行音乐的先驱。|近义词：pop, dance-pop|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "traditional folk music")|词性:noun
