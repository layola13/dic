multiplatform:/ˌmʌltiˈplætˌfɔːrm/ | adj. | 多平台的，跨平台的 | Designed to run on multiple computing platforms. |  This new software is multiplatform, working on both Windows and macOS.  这款新软件是多平台的，可以在Windows和macOS上运行。| Example: The game developer released a multiplatform version of their hit title.  游戏开发者发布了他们热门游戏的跨平台版本。| Synonyms: cross-platform, portable | Antonyms: platform-specific, single-platform | Adjective
