disport:/dɪˈspɔːrt/| v. n.|娱乐，玩耍；炫耀|To amuse oneself in a light or lively fashion; frolic.  To display ostentatiously.|例句：The children disported themselves in the park. 孩子们在公园里玩耍。|近义词：frolic, play, sport, amuse, entertain, divert, revel, rejoice|反义词：work, toil, labor|词性：verb, noun

disport:/dɪˈspɔːrt/| v. n.|炫耀，卖弄|To display ostentatiously; flaunt.|例句：The peacock disported its magnificent plumage.  孔雀炫耀着它华丽的羽毛。|近义词：flaunt, show off, parade, exhibit, display|反义词：hide, conceal|词性：verb, noun
