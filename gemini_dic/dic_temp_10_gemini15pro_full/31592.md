chastely:/ˈtʃeɪstli/
adverb
贞洁地，纯洁地
in a pure and virtuous manner; without any sexual activity or intention.
She dressed chastely in a simple white gown. 她穿着朴素的白色长袍，显得贞洁端庄。
近义词: purely, virtuously, modestly, decently
反义词: impurely, lewdly, indecently
词性: adverb

chaste:/tʃeɪst/
adjective
贞洁的，纯洁的
abstaining from sexual intercourse; not having any sexual nature or intention.
Their relationship remained chaste. 他们的关系依然纯洁。
近义词: pure, virtuous, modest, decent
反义词: impure, lewd, indecent
词性: adjective

chastity:/ˈtʃæstəti/
noun
贞洁，纯洁
the state or practice of refraining from extramarital, or especially from all, sexual intercourse.
She vowed a life of chastity. 她发誓要过贞洁的生活。
近义词: purity, virtue, modesty, decency
反义词: impurity, lewdness, indecency
词性: noun
