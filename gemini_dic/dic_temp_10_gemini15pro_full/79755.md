gluons:/ˈɡluːɒnz/ (UK), /ˈɡluːɑːnz/ (US)| n. | 胶子 |Elementary particles that act as the exchange particles (or gauge bosons) for the strong force between quarks, analogous to the exchange of photons in the electromagnetic force between two charged particles.  Gluons bind quarks together, forming hadrons such as protons and neutrons.|

例句：
Gluons are responsible for holding quarks together.
胶子负责将夸克结合在一起。

Gluons themselves carry color charge and hence interact with each other, making quantum chromodynamics much more complicated than quantum electrodynamics.
胶子自身带有色荷，因此彼此相互作用，使得量子色动力学比量子电动力学复杂得多。


近义词: gauge boson (广义上的规范玻色子)
反义词:  None (胶子没有直接的反义词)
词性: noun
