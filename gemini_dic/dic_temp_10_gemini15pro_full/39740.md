consociation:/kənˌsōʃiˈeɪʃən/ |n.|联合，联盟，结合；合伙；协会；会社|The consociation of several groups led to the formation of a new political party.  几个团体的联合导致了一个新政党的成立。|The consociation agreed on a joint strategy. 该协会就一项联合战略达成一致。|alliance, association, federation, union, partnership|dissociation, separation|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin consociatio ("association, union, partnership"), from consociare ("to associate, unite, join together"), from con- ("together, with") + sociare ("to join, unite, associate"), from socius ("companion, ally, partner").
