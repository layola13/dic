massasoit:/ˈmæsəsɔɪt/| n. |马萨索伊特|A great sachem of the Wampanoag who befriended the Pilgrims at Plymouth Colony.| Massasoit welcomed the Pilgrims and helped them survive their first winter. 马萨索伊特欢迎清教徒的到来，并帮助他们度过了第一个冬天。|近义词：chieftain, leader|反义词:follower, subordinate|词性:noun

Etymology: From the Wampanoag language, meaning "great leader".
