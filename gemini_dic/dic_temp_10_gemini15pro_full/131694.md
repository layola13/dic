noncondensing:/ˌnɒnkənˈdensɪŋ/ | adj. | 非冷凝的 | Not condensing; specifically, (of a gas appliance, such as a boiler or furnace) designed to operate without condensing the water vapor produced during combustion. |  The noncondensing boiler was less efficient than the newer model.  非冷凝锅炉的效率比新型号低。| synonyms: non-condensable | antonyms: condensing | word type: adjective
