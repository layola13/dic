lingle: /ˈlɪŋɡəl/ | n. | 线，绳，细绳；（尤指）钓鱼线 | A length of cord, thread, or especially fishing line. |  He cast his lingle into the river. 他把他的钓鱼线抛进了河里。 | 近义词: line, cord, string, thread | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *lyngel, from Old English *lyngell, diminutive of *lynge (“line, string”). Cognate with Middle Low German *linge (“line, string”).
