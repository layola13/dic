contemporaneousness:/kənˌtɛmpəˈreɪniəsnəs/
n.
同时代；同时期性
The state of existing or occurring at the same time.
The contemporaneousness of the two events was remarkable.  这两个事件的同时发生非常引人注目。
contemporaneity, simultaneity
asynchrony
noun
