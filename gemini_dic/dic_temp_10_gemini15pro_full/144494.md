outthwacking: /ˌaʊtˈθwækɪŋ/
verb
痛击，猛击
Hitting or striking with great force, surpassing in the act of thwacking.
The knight was outthwacking his opponent with a mighty blow. 骑士猛力一击，痛击了他的对手。
The baseball player was outthwacking home runs all season.  这位棒球运动员整个赛季都在猛击本垒打。
synonyms:  bashing, clobbering,  pummeling, thrashing, walloping
antonyms:  caressing,  tapping
