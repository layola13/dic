dichroic:/daɪˈkroʊɪk/| adj.|二色的，双色的|Exhibiting dichroism; having two colors, appearing of two different colors, especially when viewed by transmitted light under different angles or polarizations.|The gemstone displayed a dichroic effect, appearing blue from one angle and green from another. 这颗宝石呈现出二色性效果，从一个角度看是蓝色的，从另一个角度看是绿色的。|近义词:two-colored, birefringent|反义词:monochromatic|词性:形容词

dichroism:/ˈdaɪkroʊɪzəm/| n.|二色性，双色性|The property of exhibiting different colors by reflected or transmitted light.|Dichroism is a common phenomenon in certain minerals and crystals. 二色性是某些矿物和晶体中常见的现象。|近义词:pleochroism|反义词:monochromatism|词性:名词
