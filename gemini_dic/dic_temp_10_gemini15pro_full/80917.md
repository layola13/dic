gowning:/ˈɡaʊnɪŋ/| n.|穿隔离衣，穿手术衣|The process of putting on protective clothing, such as gowns, masks, and gloves, typically in a clean or sterile environment.  This is common practice in healthcare settings, cleanrooms, and laboratories.|例句：Gowning is a crucial step before entering the operating room.  穿隔离衣是进入手术室前的关键步骤。

例句：Proper gowning procedures are essential to prevent contamination. 正确的穿隔离衣程序对于防止污染至关重要。|近义词：garbing, dressing|反义词:disrobing|词性:名词
