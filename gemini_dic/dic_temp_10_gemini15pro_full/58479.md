dudes:/duːdz/| n.|伙计们；哥们儿；老兄（guy的复数）|A group of men, typically friends.  Refers to males, often used informally.|例句：Hey dudes, what's up? 嘿，伙计们，怎么了？

例句：Those dudes are always causing trouble.  那些家伙总是惹麻烦。

近义词：guys, fellas, pals, buddies|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly ladies if referring specifically to a mixed-gender group) |词性: Noun


dudes:/duːdz/|int.|（表示惊奇或兴奋）|Used to express surprise or excitement.|例句：Dudes! That concert was awesome!  哇！那场音乐会太棒了！

例句：Dudes, I can't believe we won! 哥们儿，真不敢相信我们赢了！

近义词：wow, cool, awesome|反义词: (No direct antonym - depends on the emotion being expressed) |词性: Interjection
