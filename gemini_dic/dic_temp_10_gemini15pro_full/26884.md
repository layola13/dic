ETYMOLOGY->
From the late 19th century.  Originally referred to a dance contest performed by African American couples in the Southern United States.  The prize for the best performance was often a cake, hence the name.  Over time, the term evolved to mean anything easily accomplished.  The dance itself was often a stylized, high-stepping strut, reflecting a sense of pride and dignity in the face of adversity.
