chman:/ˈtʃmæn/ | n. |  (slang, often derogatory) Man; fellow; guy |  This word is considered slang and can be derogatory, especially when used to refer to someone of a different ethnic or social group.  It's shortened from "chapman," an archaic term for a merchant or trader, which itself comes from Old English *cēapmann (merchant).  | He's a tricky chman. 他是个狡猾的家伙。| 近义词: guy, fellow, bloke (UK) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, depends on the context. Could be woman, lady, etc. if referring to gender.) | noun
