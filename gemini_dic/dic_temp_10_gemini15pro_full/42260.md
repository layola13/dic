|The politician skillfully deflected the journalist's question with a clever counter-argument.  这位政治家巧妙地用一个聪明的反驳挡开了记者的提问。| 近义词: rebuttal, rejoinder, response | 反义词: argument, claim, proposition | 词性: noun
