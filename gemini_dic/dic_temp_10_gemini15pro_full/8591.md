anticlericalism:/ˌæntiˈklɛrɪkəlɪzəm/
n.
反教权主义
Opposition to the power of the clergy or churches in political or public affairs.
The French Revolution was a period of strong anticlericalism.  法国大革命时期是反教权主义盛行的时期。
The government's policies fueled anticlericalism in the country. 政府的政策激起了国内的反教权主义情绪。
