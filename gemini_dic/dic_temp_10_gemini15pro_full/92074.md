hyperpurities:/ˌhaɪpərˈpjʊrɪtiz/
n.|超高纯度；极高纯度|The process yields materials of exceptional hyperpurities.  这个过程产生了具有极高纯度的材料。|
近义词: ultra-purities, extreme purities
反义词: impurities, contaminants
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

hyperpurity:/ˌhaɪpərˈpjʊrɪti/
n.|超高纯度；极高纯度|The hyperpurity of the silicon is crucial for semiconductor manufacturing. 硅的超高纯度对于半导体制造至关重要。|
近义词: ultra-purity, extreme purity
反义词: impurity, contamination
词性: 名词

词源:  "hyper-" (prefix) from Greek meaning "over, above, beyond" + "purity" from Old French "purete" ultimately from Latin "puritas" meaning "cleanness, clearness".
