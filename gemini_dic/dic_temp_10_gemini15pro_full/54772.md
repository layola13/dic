dispelled:/dɪˈspɛld/
verb
驱散，消除
to make something go away, especially a feeling, idea, or belief
The sun dispelled the fog. 太阳驱散了雾。
近义词：banish, disperse, dissipate, scatter
反义词：gather, collect, accumulate
词性：v.

Etymology:
From Late Middle English dispellen, from Old French dispeller, from Latin dispellere (“to drive away, scatter”), from dis- (“away, apart”) + pellere (“to drive, push”).
