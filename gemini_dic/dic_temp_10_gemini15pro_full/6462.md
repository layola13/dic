amply:/ˈæmpli/| adv.|充足地，充分地|More than adequate; sufficiently.|例句：The evidence amply demonstrates her guilt. 证据充分证明她有罪。|例句：They were amply rewarded for their efforts. 他们的努力得到了丰厚的回报。|近义词：abundantly, copiously, fully, generously, sufficiently|反义词：inadequately, insufficiently, meagerly, sparsely|词性:副词
词源:From Late Latin *ample, from Latin amplus (“large, spacious, great, strong, full”)
