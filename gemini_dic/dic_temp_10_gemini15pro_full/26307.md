buxom:/ˈbʌksəm/| adj.|丰满的，健康的|Full-figured and healthy-looking; (of a woman) attractively plump.|例句：The buxom waitress served us our drinks.  丰满的女服务员给我们上了饮料。

例句：The actress playing the farm wife was a buxom blonde.  扮演农妇的女演员是一位丰满的金发女郎。|近义词：plump, curvy, full-figured, ample, voluptuous|反义词：slim, thin, slender|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *buhsom*, from Old English *būhsum* (“pliant, obedient, yielding”), from *būgan* (“to bend, bow, stoop”) + *-sum*.  The meaning shifted from "obedient, yielding" to "healthy, vigorous" and then to "full-figured" over time.
