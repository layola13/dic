alectoromancy:/ˌælɪktəˈrɒmənsi/ |n.| 鸡卜；雄鸡占卜 |Divination by means of a rooster pecking grain.  A form of divination in which a rooster is allowed to pick letters or grains of corn, and the resulting pattern is interpreted to reveal hidden knowledge or future events.|  The villagers practiced alectoromancy to determine the next harvest. 村民们用鸡卜来预测下一次的收成. |  Synonym:  augury, divination | Antonym:  N/A | Noun
