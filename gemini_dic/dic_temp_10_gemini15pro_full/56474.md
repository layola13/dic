doltishly:/ˈdoʊltɪʃli/| adv.|愚蠢地，笨拙地|In a stupid or foolish manner; like a dolt.|He doltishly walked into the wrong room. 他愚蠢地走进了错误的房间。|例句：She doltishly left her keys in the car. 她笨手笨脚地把钥匙忘在了车里。|近义词：stupidly, foolishly, idiotically, obtusely|反义词：intelligently, wisely, cleverly, astutely|词性:副词
词源:From dolt + -ish + -ly.  Dolt comes from Middle English *dolte, of uncertain origin.
