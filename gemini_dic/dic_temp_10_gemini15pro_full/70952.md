fireflies:/ˈfaɪərˌflaɪz/ (US), /ˈfaɪəflaɪz/ (UK)| n. |萤火虫|Nocturnal beetles that produce light through bioluminescence, typically having a soft yellow or green glow.|The children chased fireflies in the summer evening. 孩子们在夏夜追逐萤火虫。|Synonyms: lightning bugs, glowworms|Antonyms: None|Noun

firefly:/ˈfaɪərˌflaɪ/ (US), /ˈfaɪəflaɪ/ (UK)| n. |萤火虫 (单数)|A single nocturnal beetle that produces light through bioluminescence.|A lone firefly blinked in the darkness. 一只孤独的萤火虫在黑暗中闪烁。|Synonyms: lightning bug, glowworm|Antonyms: None|Noun
