breastbeam: /ˈbrestˌbiːm/ | n. | 胸梁，托梁 | A horizontal beam supporting the front of a building, especially in timber framing, often at the level of the first floor. It may also be called a bressummer. | The old house had a beautifully carved breastbeam.  这栋老房子有一根雕刻精美的胸梁。| Synonym: bressummer, summer, lintel | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps foundation as it supports from below) | Noun
