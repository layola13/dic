lifecycle: /ˈlaɪfˌsaɪkəl/ | n. | 生命周期，生存期 | The series of changes in the life of an organism or thing. |  The lifecycle of a butterfly is fascinating. 蝴蝶的生命周期很迷人。| synonyms: lifespan, duration, existence | antonyms: death, extinction | noun

lifecycle: /ˈlaɪfˌsaɪkəl/ | n. |  生命周期，生存期 | The series of stages through which something (such as a product or concept) passes during its existence.  | The product lifecycle is key to marketing strategy. 产品生命周期是市场营销策略的关键。| synonyms: progression, development, phases | antonyms: stagnation, cessation | noun
