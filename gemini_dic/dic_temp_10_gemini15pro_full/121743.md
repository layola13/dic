misproposed:/ˌmɪsprəˈpoʊzd/
verb
错误地提议；提议不当
incorrectly proposed; proposed something unsuitable or inappropriate
The committee misproposed a solution that would have actually worsened the problem. 委员会错误地提出了一个实际上会使问题恶化的解决方案。
The architect misproposed a design that was structurally unsound.  建筑师提出了一个结构不稳固的设计。
近义词:misstated, misrepresented, bungled, botched
反义词:correctly proposed, aptly proposed, suggested appropriately
词性:verb
