enguard:/ɛnˈɡɑːrd/ | adj. | 警戒的，守卫的 | On guard; watchful; vigilant.  | The soldiers stood enguard, protecting the entrance to the castle. 士兵们站岗守卫着城堡的入口。 | 近义词: watchful, vigilant, alert, on guard | 反义词: unguarded, careless, inattentive | 词性: 形容词

词源: From en- + guard. "En-" is a prefix meaning "in, into, or on." "Guard" comes from Old French "garde" meaning "watch, care, protection."
