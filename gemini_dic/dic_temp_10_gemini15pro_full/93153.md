ignify:/ɪɡˈnaɪt/ | v. | 点燃，使燃烧；激起，煽动 | To set on fire; to arouse or excite feelings or passions. | The match ignified the kindling. 火柴点燃了引火物。

The speaker's words ignified the crowd's anger. 演讲者的话激起了人群的愤怒。 | 近义词: kindle, inflame, ignite, arouse, provoke | 反义词: extinguish, quench, suppress, calm, soothe | 词性: 动词

词源: From Late Latin *ignificāre, from Latin ignis ("fire") + -ficāre,  combining form of facere ("to make, to do").  Essentially meaning "to make into fire."
