coafforest:/ˌkoʊəˈfɔːrɪst/ | vt. | 共同造林，合作造林 | To establish a forest in cooperation with others, especially on land that has not been forested recently. |  The community coafforested the abandoned mine site to restore the natural habitat. 社区合作在废弃的矿区造林，以恢复自然栖息地。 |  The two countries agreed to coafforest a large area along their shared border. 两国同意在其共同边界沿线大面积合作造林。 | synonyms: afforest, reforest, plant | antonyms: deforest, clear-cut | verb
