lubricating:/ˈluːbrɪkeɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 润滑的，起润滑作用的 | Applying a lubricating substance to reduce friction between moving parts. |  The mechanic applied lubricating oil to the engine. 机械师给发动机加了润滑油。| synonyms: oiling, greasing, slicking | antonyms: drying, roughening, abrasive | adjective
