battle-scarred: /ˈbætəl skɑːrd/ | adj. | 饱经战火的，伤痕累累的 | Showing the effects of having been in battles or other difficult situations. Marked or damaged by experience. | The battle-scarred veteran limped through the streets. 这位饱经战火的退伍军人一瘸一拐地走在街上。 | The battle-scarred walls of the castle told tales of centuries of conflict. 城堡饱经战火的墙壁讲述着几个世纪的冲突故事。 | 近义词：war-torn, veteran, hardened, experienced | 反义词：pristine, untouched, unscarred, new | 词性：形容词
