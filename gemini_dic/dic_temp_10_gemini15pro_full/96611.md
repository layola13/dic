indulge:/ɪnˈdʌldʒ/ | v. | 纵容；沉溺；满足；享用 | to allow oneself or another person to enjoy the pleasure of something, especially something considered bad for you |  She indulged in a long hot bath. 她泡了个长时间的热水澡。| synonyms: pamper, spoil, gratify, humor, cater to, accommodate, oblige, pander to | antonyms: deny, abstain, refrain, resist | verb

indulge:/ɪnˈdʌldʒ/ | v. | 迁就；放任 | to allow someone to have or do what they want, even if it is bad for them |  His mother indulged him in every whim. 他母亲对他百依百顺。| synonyms: pamper, spoil, coddle | antonyms: discipline, restrain | verb
