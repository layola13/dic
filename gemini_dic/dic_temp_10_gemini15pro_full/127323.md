naysayers:/ˈneɪˌseɪərz/ | n. |反对者，唱反调的人|A person who criticizes, objects to, or opposes something.|例句：The naysayers predicted the project would fail, but they were proven wrong. 反对者预测该项目会失败，但事实证明他们错了。

例句：Despite the naysayers, she persevered and achieved her goal. 尽管有人反对，她还是坚持不懈，最终实现了目标。|近义词：critics, dissenters, objectors, opponents|反义词：supporters, proponents, advocates|Noun
