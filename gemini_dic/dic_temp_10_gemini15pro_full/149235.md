oxysalt:/ˈɒksɪsɔːlt/ | n. |含氧盐|A salt of an oxyacid.  A salt formed from an acid containing oxygen.  |Sodium sulfate (Na₂SO₄) is an oxysalt formed from sulfuric acid (H₂SO₄). 硫酸钠 (Na₂SO₄) 是一种由硫酸 (H₂SO₄) 形成的含氧盐。| Potassium nitrate is a common oxysalt used in fertilizers. 硝酸钾是一种常用的用于肥料的含氧盐。 |salt, oxyacid salt |  |noun
