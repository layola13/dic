gastritic:/ɡæˈstrɪtɪk/ | adj. | 胃炎的，患胃炎的 | Relating to, affected by, or causing gastritis (inflammation of the lining of the stomach). |  He was diagnosed with gastritic inflammation. 他被诊断出患有胃炎。|  The doctor prescribed medication for his gastritic pain. 医生给他开了治疗胃痛的药。| 近义词: inflamed, irritated | 反义词: healthy | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *gastrīticus*, from Greek gastēr (stomach) + -itis (inflammation) + -ic (relating to).
