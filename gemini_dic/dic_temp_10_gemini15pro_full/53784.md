discompliance:/ˌdɪskəmˈplaɪəns/
n.
不服从，不顺从
failure or refusal to comply with a rule or request.
The patient's discompliance with the prescribed medication regimen led to a worsening of his condition. 病人未遵守规定的药物治疗方案，导致病情恶化。
近义词：noncompliance, disobedience, insubordination
反义词：compliance, obedience, conformity
词性：名词
