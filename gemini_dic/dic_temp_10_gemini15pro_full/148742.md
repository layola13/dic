overtroubles:/ˌoʊvərˈtrʌbəlz/| v. |过分担忧，过分困扰|To worry or trouble excessively; to cause undue anxiety or distress.|She overtroubles herself about her children's future. 她过分担忧孩子们的未来。|近义词：fret, worry, agonize, overthink|反义词: relax, disregard, neglect|词性:动词

overtroubles:/ˌoʊvərˈtrʌbəlz/| n. |过分的烦恼，过度的忧虑|Excessive worries or troubles.|Her overtroubles are affecting her health. 她过度的忧虑正在影响她的健康。|近义词： anxieties, worries, concerns|反义词: peace, tranquility, calmness|词性:名词


词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning excessively) + "troubles" (noun meaning difficulties or worries).  This word is a relatively modern formation and isn't found in older dictionaries.  It's created by adding the intensifying prefix "over-" to the existing noun "troubles."
