devilize:/ˈdɛvɪlaɪz/ /ˈdiːvɪlaɪz/
verb
使邪恶化；使堕落；诽谤；中伤
To make evil or wicked; to corrupt the morals of; to represent as wicked.
The propaganda sought to devilize the enemy. 这则宣传试图将敌人妖魔化。
近义词:corrupt, deprave, pervert, demoralize
反义词:purify, sanctify, elevate
词性:v.
