comatulid:/ˈkɒməˌtjuːlɪd/ /ˈkɑːməˌtuːlɪd/ | n. | 海羊齿，羽星 | A crinoid of the order Comatulida, typically having feathery arms but lacking a stalk.  These are free-moving crinoids, unlike their stalked relatives. |例句：The comatulid gracefully extended its arms, filtering plankton from the water.  海羊齿优雅地伸展着它的腕，从水中过滤浮游生物。

例句：Divers marveled at the vibrant colors of the comatulids on the reef.  潜水员惊叹于珊瑚礁上海羊齿鲜艳的色彩。| 近义词：feather star, crinoid | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific type of animal) | 词性: noun
