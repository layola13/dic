confrontationalist:/ˌkɒnfrʌnˈteɪʃənəlɪst/ /ˌkɑːnfrʌnˈteɪʃənəlɪst/
noun
对抗主义者; 好争论者
A person who favors confrontation.
He's known as a bit of a confrontationalist. 他被认为是一个有点好争论的人。
synonyms: aggressor, antagonist, fighter, disputant, challenger
antonyms: pacifist, mediator, conciliator
词性: noun


confrontational:/ˌkɒnfrʌnˈteɪʃənəl/ /ˌkɑːnfrʌnˈteɪʃənəl/
adjective
对抗性的; 对抗的
Tending to deal with situations in an aggressive way; likely to cause argument or make people angry.
She has a very confrontational attitude. 她有一种非常对抗的态度。
synonyms: aggressive, antagonistic, belligerent, hostile, provocative
antonyms: conciliatory, cooperative, peaceful, amicable
词性: adjective
