consignation:/ˌkɒnsaɪˈneɪʃn/ /ˌkɑːnsaɪˈneɪʃn/
n.
寄售；交付；委托；托运
The act of consigning something to another's care, typically for sale or safekeeping.  The goods were held in consignation until payment was received.
The act of giving or delivering something to another person or agent, especially for sale. The paintings are on consignation at the gallery.
近义词：deposit, commitment, entrustment
反义词：retrieval, reclamation
词性: noun


consignment:/kənˈsaɪnmənt/ /kənˈsaɪnmənt/
n.
寄售；交付；委托；托运
A batch of goods destined for or delivered to someone.  The latest consignment of clothes has arrived.
The act of consigning goods.  The consignment of the goods took place on Monday.
近义词：shipment, delivery, batch
反义词：receipt


词源:
From Late Latin consignatio, from consignare  "to sign, seal, mark," from Latin con- "together" + signare "to sign, seal, mark," from signum "a mark, sign."
