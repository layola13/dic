cancellations: /ˌkænsəˈleɪʃənz/
n.
取消，撤销
The act of cancelling something; the state of being cancelled.
Due to the storm, there were numerous flight cancellations. 由于暴风雨，很多航班被取消了。
The cancellation of the concert disappointed many fans.  演唱会的取消让很多歌迷失望。
synonyms: abolishments, revocations, withdrawals, rescissions
antonyms: confirmations, approvals, authorizations
noun
