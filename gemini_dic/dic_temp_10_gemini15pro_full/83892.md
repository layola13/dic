half-aristotelian:/ˌhæf ˌærɪstəˈtiːliən/| adj.| 半亚里士多德式的|Partially adhering to the philosophy of Aristotle, often implying a blend of Aristotelian and other (usually more modern) philosophical ideas.|His theory presents a half-Aristotelian, half-modern approach to metaphysics.|Near Synonyms: semi-Aristotelian, partially Aristotelian|Antonyms: non-Aristotelian, anti-Aristotelian|adjective
