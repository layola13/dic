malpighian: /mælˈpɪɡiən/ | adj. | 马尔皮基氏的; 排泄的 | Relating to or denoting the excretory organs of insects and other arthropods, consisting of fine tubules that extract waste products from the blood and discharge them into the gut. |  Malpighian tubules are important for insect osmoregulation.  马尔皮基小管对昆虫的渗透调节很重要。 |  The malpighian layer of the skin is responsible for producing melanin. 皮肤的马尔皮基层负责产生黑色素。 | 近义词: excretory | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps "absorptive" in a contrasting context) | 词性: adjective

malpighian tubule: /mælˈpɪɡiən ˈtjuːbjuːl/ | n. | 马尔皮基小管 |  A type of excretory tubule found in insects and other arthropods.  | Insects excrete waste through their malpighian tubules. 昆虫通过马尔皮基小管排出废物。 | The malpighian tubules filter metabolic wastes from the hemolymph. 马尔皮基小管过滤来自血淋巴的代谢废物。 | 近义词: excretory tubule | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
