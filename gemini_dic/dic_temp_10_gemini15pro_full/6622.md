anaemic:/əˈniːmɪk/ (US) /əˈnemɪk/ (UK)| adj.|贫血的；苍白的；缺乏活力的；(比喻)没有生气的|Lacking vigor or vitality; listless and pale.  Figuratively, lacking in substance or effectiveness.|The play was an anaemic production, lacking energy and passion. 这部戏剧制作得很苍白无力，缺乏活力和激情。|Synonyms: pale, wan, pallid, ashen, colorless, bloodless, feeble, weak, debilitated, listless, languid, spiritless, lifeless, vapid, insipid, uninspired, lackluster, ineffective|Antonyms: robust, healthy, vigorous, vibrant, colorful, lively, energetic|adjective

anaemia:/əˈniːmiə/ (US) /əˈniːmiə/ (UK)| n.|贫血；缺乏活力|A condition in which the blood doesn't have enough healthy red blood cells.|The doctor diagnosed her with anaemia after noticing her pale skin and fatigue. 医生注意到她脸色苍白、疲惫不堪，于是诊断她患有贫血。|Synonyms: bloodlessness|Antonyms: robustness, health|noun
