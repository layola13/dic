curies:/ˈkjʊəriz/ (US), /ˈkjuːriz/ (UK)| n.|居里（复数）|Plural form of curie, a unit of radioactivity.|The combined activity of the samples was several hundred curies.|Synonyms: becquerels (Bq) (SI unit, plural)|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)|noun
