bostryx:/ˈbɒstrɪks/| n. |螺状物，螺旋形，蜷曲|A helix; a spiral form, especially of a curl of hair.  The arrangement of a cluster of grapes. |The tendrils of the vine formed bostryxes around the trellis.  葡萄藤的卷须在格子上形成了螺旋状。|近义词：helix, spiral, curl, whorl|反义词:straight|词性:noun

Etymology: From Latin *bostryx*, from Ancient Greek βόστρυχος (bóstrukhos, “curl of hair, tendril of a vine, cluster of grapes”).
