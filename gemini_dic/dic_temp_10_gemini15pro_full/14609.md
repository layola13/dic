aviso:/əˈviːsoʊ/ (US), /əˈviːzəʊ/ (UK)| n.|通知，公告，警告|A formal or official notice or warning. |例句：The aviso warned of approaching storms.  公告警告风暴即将来临。

例句：We received an aviso about the road closure. 我们收到了关于道路封闭的通知。|近义词：notice, warning, announcement, alert|反义词: None (lack of notice/warning implied)|noun
