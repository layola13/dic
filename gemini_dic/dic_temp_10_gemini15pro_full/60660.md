electo:/ɪˈlɛktoʊ/
n.
被选者;当选人
A person who has been elected to an office or position but has not yet been formally installed.
The electo will be sworn in next month.  这位当选人将在下个月宣誓就职。
近义词: elect, nominee
反义词: none
词性: 名词

electo:/ɪˈlɛktoʊ/
adj.
当选的
Having been elected but not yet installed in office.
The electo-mayor addressed the crowd. 当选市长向人群发表了讲话。
近义词: elected
反义词: unelected
词性:形容词
