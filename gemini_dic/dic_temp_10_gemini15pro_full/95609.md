incommunicating:/ˌɪnkəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
不交流的，不沟通的
Not communicating; not able or willing to share thoughts or feelings.
例句：He became increasingly incommunicating in his later years.  他晚年变得越来越不爱与人交流。

例句：The family was incommunicating after the argument.  争吵过后，这家人互不理睬。

近义词：uncommunicative, withdrawn, reserved, silent, taciturn
反义词：communicative, outgoing, sociable, talkative
词性：adjective
