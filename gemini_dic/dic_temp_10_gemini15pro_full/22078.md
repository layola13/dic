bollard:/ˈbɒləd/ (UK) /ˈbɑːlərd/ (US)| n. |短柱; 系船柱; |A short, thick post on a wharf or ship used to secure mooring lines. Also used on roads or pavements as a protective or obstructive device. |例句：The ship was tied to the bollard. 船被系在系船柱上。

例句：Bollards were installed to prevent cars from parking on the sidewalk.  安装了短柱以防止汽车停在人行道上。|近义词：post, pillar, barrier |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "opening" in a contextual sense) |词性:noun

bollards:/ˈbɒlədz/ (UK) /ˈbɑːlərdz/ (US)| n. |短柱; 系船柱; (bollard的复数形式)|Multiple short, thick posts. |例句：The bollards lining the harbor were all painted white. 港口边排列的系船柱都被漆成了白色。

例句：Several bollards were damaged in the collision.  几根短柱在碰撞中受损。|近义词：posts, pillars, barriers |反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:noun
