centime: /sɑ̃ˈtim/ (French pronunciation)| n. | 生丁 | A former monetary unit of France and several other countries, equal to one hundredth of a franc (and now, one hundredth of a euro in those countries that use the euro).  It is no longer in physical circulation. | I found a few old centimes in my grandfather's coin collection. 我在我祖父的硬币收藏中发现了一些旧生丁。|  Synonym: cent (in other contexts), penny | Antonym: N/A (unit of currency; no direct opposite) | noun
