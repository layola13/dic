outshrieked:/ˌaʊtˈʃriːkt/
verb
胜过…的喊叫，比…叫得更响亮
outdo in shrieking; shriek louder than.
She outshrieked the other fans at the concert. 她胜过了音乐会上其他粉丝的喊叫声。
Synonyms: outyelled, outcried
Antonyms: whispered, murmured
词性:动词
