grottiest:/ˈɡrɒtɪɪst/ | adj. | 最肮脏的，最破烂的 | Superlative form of grotty: most grotty.  It means extremely unpleasant, dirty, or of poor quality. |  The grottiest apartment I've ever seen was infested with cockroaches. 我见过的最肮脏的公寓里到处都是蟑螂。|  The bathroom was in the grottiest condition imaginable.  洗手间脏得难以想象。 | 近义词: dirtiest, filthiest, shabbiest, nastiest | 反义词: cleanest, tidiest, nicest, most beautiful | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From 'grotty,' which is British slang, possibly originating from "grotesque" or related to "grubby."
