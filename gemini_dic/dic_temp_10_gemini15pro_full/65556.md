eutizing: /juːtɪzaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 使安乐死; 使无痛死亡 |  The act of euthanizing, or putting to death painlessly. It is often used in the context of ending the suffering of a terminally ill animal or person. | The veterinarian eutized the suffering dog. 兽医对那只受苦的狗实施了安乐死。| The family made the difficult decision to eutize their beloved pet. 家人做出了艰难的决定，对他们心爱的宠物实施安乐死。 | synonyms: euthanasing, mercy killing | antonyms: resuscitating, reviving | verb
