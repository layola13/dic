|近义词：concurs, consents, assents|
|反义词：disagrees, dissents, objects|

agrees: /əˈɡriːz/
| v. |（与…）相符，一致|
|Her story agrees with his. 她的说法和他的说法一致。|
|The climate here doesn't agree with me. 我不适应这里的气候。|
|近义词：corresponds, matches, tallies|
|反义词：clashes, conflicts, differs|
|词性: 动词|


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French agreer "to please, consent to, accept," from a- "to" (from Latin ad) + gre "pleasure, satisfaction, liking, thanks," from Latin gratum "pleasing, agreeable, welcome, grateful," neuter of gratus.
