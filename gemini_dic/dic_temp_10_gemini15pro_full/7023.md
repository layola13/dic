androspore:/ˈændrəˌspɔːr/
n.
雄孢子
A motile spore produced by certain algae, fungi, and lower plants, typically smaller than the female spore and regarded as male.
Androspores are released into the water and swim to the oogonia. 雄孢子被释放到水中并游向卵囊。
The androspores fertilize the egg within the oogonium. 雄孢子使卵囊内的卵子受精。
Synonym: spermatozoid, antherozoid
Antonym: oospore
