noncausable:/ˌnɒnˈkɔːzəbl/ | adj. | 非使役的，不能被引起的 | Not able to be caused; not having a causative form.  A noncausative verb describes a situation or an action that happens without an external agent. | The window broke. (Noncausable - implies it broke on its own or by accident) | 近义词: uncaused, accidental, spontaneous | 反义词: causative, induced, triggered | 词性: adjective
