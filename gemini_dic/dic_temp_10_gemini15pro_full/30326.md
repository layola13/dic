censurable:/ˈsɛnʃərəbl/| adj.|应受谴责的，可非难的|Deserving of censure; blameworthy.|His censurable behavior led to his dismissal from the company. 他应受谴责的行为导致他被公司解雇。|The film was considered censurable for its excessive violence. 这部电影因其过度的暴力而被认为应受谴责。|近义词：blameworthy, reprehensible, culpable, reproachable|反义词：laudable, commendable, praiseworthy|词性:adjective
词源:Late Middle English: from late Latin censurabilis, from censurare ‘to assess’ (see censure).
