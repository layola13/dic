inharmonic:/ˌɪnhɑːrˈmɒnɪk/ |adj.|不和谐的，非谐和的|The inharmonic sounds of the construction site made it difficult to concentrate. 建筑工地不和谐的声音使人难以集中注意力。|The inharmonic overtones of the bell created a unique timbre. 钟声不和谐的泛音创造了独特的音色。|近义词:discordant, dissonant, jarring|反义词:harmonic, consonant, melodious|词性:形容词
