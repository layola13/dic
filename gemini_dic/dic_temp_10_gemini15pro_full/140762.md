omnivorism:/ˈɒmnɪvɔːrɪzəm/ | n. |杂食性|The practice of consuming both animal and plant foods. |Humans are a classic example of omnivorism. 人类是杂食性的典型例子。| Many cultures embrace omnivorism as a dietary norm. 许多文化将杂食性作为一种饮食规范。 |近义词: polyphagy |反义词: herbivorism, carnivorism | 词性: noun
