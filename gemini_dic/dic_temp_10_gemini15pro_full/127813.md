nelsons:/ˈnɛlsənz/| n.|纳尔逊（姓氏）; 纳尔逊战役（特指1805年特拉法加海战）|A surname, most famously associated with Admiral Horatio Nelson, who won the Battle of Trafalgar.  It can also refer to the battle itself.|Nelson's Column stands tall in Trafalgar Square. 纳尔逊纪念柱高高耸立在特拉法加广场。|近义词: (For the battle) Trafalgar|反义词:  None|noun

nelsons:/ˈnɛlsənz/| n.|纳尔逊（摔跤或柔道中的一种固定技）|A hold in wrestling or judo.|He used a nelson to pin his opponent. 他用纳尔逊固定技压制住了他的对手。|近义词: hold, pin|反义词:  None|noun
