compliance:/kəmˈplaɪəns/ | n. | 服从，遵守，依从 |The act or process of complying to a desire, demand, proposal, or regimen or to coercion. |  The company is in compliance with all safety regulations.  这家公司遵守所有安全规定。 | synonyms: obedience, conformity, acquiescence | antonyms: defiance, disobedience, noncompliance | noun

compliances:/kəmˈplaɪənsiz/ | n. | 服从，遵守，依从（复数） | Multiple acts or processes of complying. | The company demonstrated several compliances with industry standards. 这家公司展现了对行业标准的多项遵守。 | synonyms: obediences, conformities | antonyms: defiances, disobediences | noun
