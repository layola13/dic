misdefine:/ˌmɪsdɪˈfaɪn/
verb
错误地定义；定义不当
To define incorrectly; to give a wrong or inaccurate definition.
The word "love" is often misdefined as simply a strong feeling of affection.  “爱”这个词经常被错误地定义为仅仅是一种强烈的喜爱之情。
Some people misdefine success as accumulating wealth.  有些人错误地将成功定义为积累财富。
Synonyms: misinterpret, misconstrue, misrepresent
Antonyms: define correctly, interpret correctly, explain accurately
词性: 动词
