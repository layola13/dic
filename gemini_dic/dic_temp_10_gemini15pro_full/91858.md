hyperimmunize:/ˌhaɪpərˈɪmjʊnaɪz/ | v. | 超免疫，高度免疫 | To induce an abnormally high level of immunity; to immunize repeatedly or with large doses of antigen. | The horses were hyperimmunized with the venom to produce antivenom.  这些马被注射了大量毒液以产生抗毒血清。


The researchers hyperimmunized the mice with a modified protein. 研究人员用一种改良的蛋白质对小鼠进行了超免疫。 | Near synonyms:  immunize, vaccinate | Antonyms: N/A | Verb
