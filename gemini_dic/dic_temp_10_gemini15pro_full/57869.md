drgs:/diːɑːrˈdʒiːz/ | n. | 诊断相关组 | Diagnosis-Related Groups (DRGs) is a system to classify hospital cases into one of approximately 500 groups, expected to require similar hospital resources. |  The hospital used DRGs to categorize patient stays for billing purposes. 这家医院使用DRG对病人住院进行分类以用于计费。|  The new DRG system has significantly impacted hospital reimbursement. 新的DRG系统对医院的报销产生了重大影响。 | synonyms: case mix groups, payment groups | antonyms: N/A | noun
