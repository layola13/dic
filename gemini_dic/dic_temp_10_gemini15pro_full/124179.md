mosaiced:/moʊˈzeɪkt/| adj.|镶嵌的，用马赛克装饰的|Decorated with a mosaic; resembling a mosaic.|The floor was mosaiced with colorful tiles. 地板用彩色瓷砖镶嵌而成。|近义词：inlaid, tessellated|反义词: plain, simple|词性:形容词
词源: From French *mosaïque*, from Italian *mosaico*, ultimately from Late Latin *musaicum (opus)* "mosaic (work)," neuter of *musaicus* "of the Muses," from Greek *Mouseios* "of the Muses."  The original sense referred to works of art considered fit to be placed in a shrine of the Muses.
