deconcentration:/diːkɒnsənˈtreɪʃn/
n.
权力下放；分散
The redistribution of administrative functions to lower levels of government.
The government implemented a policy of deconcentration to give more power to local authorities. 政府实施了一项权力下放政策，赋予地方当局更多权力。
Decentralization, devolution
Centralization, concentration
noun

deconcentration:/diːkɒnsənˈtreɪʃn/
n.
疏散；分散
The act of dispersing or spreading out something that was previously concentrated.
The deconcentration of industrial activity away from major cities is a key environmental goal.  将工业活动从主要城市疏散是重要的环境目标。
Dispersal, diffusion
Concentration, centralization
noun
