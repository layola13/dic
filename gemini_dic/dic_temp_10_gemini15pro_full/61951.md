emulsifier:/ɪˈmʌlsɪfaɪər/
noun
乳化剂
A substance that stabilizes an emulsion, preventing liquids that usually do not mix from separating.
Lecithin is a common emulsifier used in food production. 卵磷脂是食品生产中常用的乳化剂。
Synonym: emulsifying agent
Antonym: (No direct antonym, but consider "separator")
词性: noun (名词)
