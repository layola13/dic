co-operativeness:/kəʊˈɒpərətɪvnəs/
n.
合作性，协作性
The willingness and ability of people to work together effectively.
The team's co-operativeness was key to their success.  团队的合作精神是他们成功的关键。
co-operation, collaboration, teamwork
uncooperativeness, antagonism, obstructiveness
noun
