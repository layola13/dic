oneiromancy: /ˌoʊnaɪˈrɒmənsi/ /ˌoʊnaɪˈrɑːmənsi/
n.|梦占，解梦|The practice of interpreting dreams in order to foretell the future.|She practiced oneiromancy, believing her dreams held clues to the future. 她相信梦境蕴藏着未来的线索，于是进行解梦。|近义词：dream interpretation, dream divination|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
Etymology: From Greek oneiros (dream) + manteia (divination).
