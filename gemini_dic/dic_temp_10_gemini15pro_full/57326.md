downtroddenness:/ˌdaʊnˈtrɒdn̩nəs/
noun
受压迫；被蹂躏的状态
The state of being downtrodden; oppression.
The downtroddenness of the working class was evident in their poor living conditions. 工人阶级的受压迫状态体现在他们恶劣的生活条件中。
Near synonyms: oppression, suppression, subjugation, repression
Antonyms: empowerment, liberation, freedom
词性: 名词


downtrodden:/ˌdaʊnˈtrɒdn/
adjective
受压迫的；被蹂躏的
Oppressed; treated badly and unfairly.
The downtrodden peasants finally revolted against the tyrannical king. 受压迫的农民最终反抗了暴君国王。
Near synonyms: oppressed, suppressed, subjugated, repressed
Antonyms: empowered, liberated, free
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From down + trodden, past participle of tread.  "Tread down" means to crush or oppress.
