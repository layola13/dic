equips:/ɪˈkwɪps/ | vt. | 装备，配备 | To provide with the necessary tools, supplies, or equipment. | The company equips its employees with the latest technology.  这家公司为员工配备了最新的技术。 | equips its workers with protective gear.  公司为工人配备了防护装备。 | synonyms: furnishes, provides, supplies, outfits | antonyms: deprives, strips, divests | verb

equips:/ɪˈkwɪps/ | v. | 使…有所准备；使…有能力 | To prepare someone or something for a particular task or situation. |  A good education equips children for life. 良好的教育使孩子们为生活做好准备。 |  The course equips students with the skills they need for a career in journalism.  该课程使学生具备新闻业职业所需的技能。| synonyms: prepares, enables, empowers, qualifies | antonyms: disqualifies, hinders, weakens | verb
