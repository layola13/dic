entocuneiform:/ˌɛntoʊkjuˈniːɪfɔːrm/
| adj.|内楔形的|Relating to or denoting a type of cuneiform writing in which the wedge-shaped characters are made by pressing the stylus into the clay at an oblique angle rather than vertically.|The entocuneiform script was used to write several ancient Mesopotamian languages.|近义词:cuneiform|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "non-cuneiform")|词性:adjective
etymology->From Ancient Greek ἐντός (entós, “within”) + Latin cuneus (“wedge”) + -form.
