demonist: /ˈdiːmənɪst/ | n. | 恶魔崇拜者；信奉魔鬼的人 | A person who believes in or worships demons. | He was accused of being a demonist and practicing dark magic.  他被指控为恶魔崇拜者并施行黑魔法。 | Synonyms: devil-worshipper, Satanist | Antonyms: theist, believer (in God) | noun

Etymology:  Late 16th century: from French démoniste, from medieval Latin daemonista, from Greek daimōn ‘demon’.
