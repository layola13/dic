gobang:/ˈɡoʊbæŋ/| n.|五子棋|A board game of Japanese origin, played on a grid of 15x15 or 19x19 intersecting lines, where two players take turns placing black and white stones. The first player to get five stones in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins.|We played gobang all afternoon. 我们整个下午都在玩五子棋。|近义词：Five in a Row|反义词:  (No direct antonym for a game)
詞性: noun
