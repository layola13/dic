caressable:/kəˈrɛsəbl/
adjective
可抚摸的; 令人想拥抱的; 可爱的
pleasant or attractive to touch; inviting affection or cuddling.
Her newborn baby's skin was so soft and caressable. 她新生婴儿的皮肤如此柔软，令人想抚摸。
The kitten was small and caressable.  这只小猫又小又可爱，让人想抱抱。
近义词:touchable, huggable, cuddly, soft, smooth
反义词:rough, prickly, coarse, uninviting
词性:adjective
