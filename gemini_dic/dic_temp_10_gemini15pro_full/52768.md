dilatometer:/ˌdaɪləˈtɒmɪtər/ (US: /ˌdaɪləˈtɑːmɪtər/) | n. | 膨胀计，膨胀仪 | An instrument used to measure changes in volume, especially as a result of temperature changes. |  The dilatometer recorded a significant expansion of the material when heated. 膨胀计记录到材料受热时显著膨胀。

The researchers used a dilatometer to study the thermal expansion of different metals. 研究人员使用膨胀计研究不同金属的热膨胀。 | 近义词: expansion meter | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "contraction meter" if such a device exists) | 词性: noun
