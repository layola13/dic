immodesty:/ˌɪˈmɒdɪsti/ | n. | 不谦虚，不端庄，轻浮 | Lack of modesty; behavior or appearance that is considered inappropriate or sexually suggestive. | Her immodesty made some people uncomfortable. 她的不端庄让一些人感到不舒服。 |  She dressed with a certain immodesty that attracted attention.  她穿得有点轻浮，吸引了人们的注意。 | 近义词：indecency, impropriety, brazenness, audacity | 反义词：modesty, decency, humility | 词性: noun

immodest:/ɪˈmɒdɪst/ | adj. | 不谦虚的，不端庄的，轻浮的 | Not showing modesty; having or showing a tendency to boast about one's accomplishments or to behave in a way that is considered inappropriate or sexually suggestive. | He was immodest about his achievements. 他对自己的成就毫不谦虚。|  She wore an immodest dress to the party. 她穿着一件不端庄的裙子去参加了聚会。 | 近义词：indecent, improper, brazen, audacious | 反义词：modest, decent, humble | 词性: adjective
