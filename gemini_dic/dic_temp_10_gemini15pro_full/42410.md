countercomplaint:/ˈkaʊntər.kəmˈpleɪnt/| n.|反诉，反抱怨|A complaint made in response to an initial complaint.|例句：She filed a countercomplaint against her former employer. 她对她的前雇主提出了反诉。

例句：He responded to the lawsuit with a countercomplaint. 他以反诉回应了这场官司。


|近义词：countersuit, counterclaim|反义词:complaint|词性:noun
