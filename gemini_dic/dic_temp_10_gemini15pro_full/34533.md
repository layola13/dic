cleansable:/ˈklɛnzəbl/
adjective
可清洗的; 可清洁的
Able to be cleaned.
This sofa cover is removable and cleansable.  这款沙发套可拆卸可清洗。

近义词:washable, cleanable
反义词:uncleanable, non-washable
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From clean + -able.  Clean comes from Old English *clæne (akin to Old High German kleini "fine, delicate, pure," from Proto-Germanic *klainja- "bright, shining, pure").  The suffix -able comes from Old French -able and ultimately from Latin -abilis.
