dyslexias:/dɪsˈleksiəz/
noun
阅读障碍（复数）
A learning disorder that involves difficulty reading due to problems identifying speech sounds and learning how they relate to letters and words.  It is a common condition that affects the way the brain processes written and spoken language.
Many students with dyslexias struggle in traditional classroom settings. 许多患有阅读障碍的学生在传统的课堂环境中苦苦挣扎。
Dyslexias can manifest in different ways, affecting reading fluency, spelling, and writing. 阅读障碍可以以不同的方式表现出来，影响阅读流畅性、拼写和写作。
synonyms: reading disorders, learning disabilities
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a medical condition. One could consider strong reading abilities as a contrasting concept.)
