milkier:/ˈmɪlkiər/ | adj. | 更乳白色的；更像牛奶的；更柔和的 | More milky; resembling milk more closely; more gentle or mild. | The sauce was milkier than usual.  酱汁比平时更乳白色。| The fog made the air milkier.  雾使空气更加乳白。 | 近义词: creamier, whiter, paler | 反义词: clearer, darker | 词性:形容词

词源: From milk (n.) + -y (adj. suffix) + -er (comparative suffix).  源于名词 milk，加上形容词后缀 -y 和比较级后缀 -er。
