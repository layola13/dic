measuredly:/ˈmeʒərdli/ | adv. | 慎重地，缓慢而谨慎地 | In a measured and deliberate way; carefully and slowly. | She spoke measuredly, choosing her words with care. 她谨慎地说着，字斟句酌。|  He walked measuredly across the room. 他缓慢而谨慎地走过房间。| 近义词: cautiously, deliberately, slowly, carefully | 反义词: hastily, rashly, impulsively | 词性: 副词
