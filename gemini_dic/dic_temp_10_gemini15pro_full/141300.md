open-mindedness:/ˌoʊpənˈmaɪndɪdnəs/| n. |开放思想；心胸开阔|The state of being receptive to new ideas and different perspectives.|例句：Her open-mindedness allowed her to consider all sides of the argument. 她的心胸开阔使她能够考虑论证的各个方面。

例句：Open-mindedness is essential for personal growth and understanding. 开放的思想对于个人成长和理解至关重要。|近义词：receptiveness, tolerance, broad-mindedness|反义词：close-mindedness, intolerance, prejudice|词性:名词

open-minded:/ˌoʊpənˈmaɪndɪd/| adj. |虚心的；思想开明的|Willing to consider new ideas and different perspectives.|例句：He was open-minded enough to listen to her unconventional ideas. 他思想开明，愿意倾听她非传统的观点。

例句：An open-minded approach is crucial for scientific progress.  开放的思想对科学进步至关重要。|近义词：receptive, tolerant, broad-minded|反义词：close-minded, intolerant, prejudiced|词性:形容词
