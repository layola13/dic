objectival:/ˌɒbdʒɛkˈtaɪvəl/
adjective
客观的；目标的
Relating to external reality; unbiased.  Also, relating to or expressing the grammatical object.
The scientist conducted an objectival analysis of the data. 科学家对数据进行了客观的分析。
The objectival case marks the direct object of a verb.  宾格标记动词的直接宾语。
synonyms: objective, unbiased, impartial, factual, external, real
antonyms: subjective, biased, prejudiced, personal, internal
词性: adjective
