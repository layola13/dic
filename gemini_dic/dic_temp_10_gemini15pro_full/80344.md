goldilocks: /ˈɡoʊldɪlɒks/ (US) /ˈɡəʊldɪlɒks/ (UK)| n. adj.|金发姑娘; 恰到好处的事物|A girl with golden hair, from the fairy tale "Goldilocks and the Three Bears".
