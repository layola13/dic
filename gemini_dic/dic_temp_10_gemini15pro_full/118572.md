mesoseme:/ˈmɛsoʊˌsiːm/
n.|中间义位|A word or phrase whose meaning is intermediate between a hyponym and a superordinate term.  It represents a level of semantic categorization that is neither too specific nor too general.|The word "dog" is a mesoseme, broader than "poodle" but narrower than "animal".|近义词:mid-level term, intermediate category|反义词:hyponym, superordinate|词性:noun

词源: From Greek *mesos* "middle" + *sēma* "sign, mark".
