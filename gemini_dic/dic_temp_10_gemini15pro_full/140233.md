offing:/ˈɒfɪŋ/| n.| 近海, 即将来临 |The ship was sighted in the offing.  那艘船在近海被看到了。|There are big changes in the offing. 重大变革即将发生。|近义词：approaching, imminent, forthcoming|反义词: distant, remote|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *offinge, from off + -ing.  Originally a nautical term referring to the part of the sea visible from the shore.  The figurative sense of "in the near future" developed later.
