indiscriminateness:/ˌɪndɪˈskrɪmɪnətnəs/
n.|不加区分；任意性；杂乱无章|The state or quality of not discriminating; lack of careful distinction or choice.|His indiscriminateness in choosing friends led him into trouble. 他在择友方面的不加区分使他陷入了困境。|近义词：randomness, haphazardness, promiscuity, impartiality|反义词：selectivity, discrimination, discernment|词性:名词

indiscriminate:/ˌɪndɪˈskrɪmɪnət/
adj.|不加选择的；不加区分的；任意的；杂乱的|Not discriminating; lacking in selectivity or discernment.|The indiscriminate use of pesticides can harm the environment.  滥用杀虫剂会危害环境。|近义词：random, haphazard, promiscuous, unselective|反义词：selective, discriminating, discerning|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin indiscriminatus, from in- "not" + discriminatus, past participle of discriminare "to distinguish, separate," from discrimen "distinction, difference."
