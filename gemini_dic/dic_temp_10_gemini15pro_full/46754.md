dandyism:/ˈdændiɪzəm/| n.|纨绔主义；讲究衣着打扮；花花公子作风|A style of dress and behavior characterized by excessive refinement and attention to one's appearance.|He was known for his dandyism, always impeccably dressed and groomed. 他以其纨绔主义而闻名，总是衣着 impeccable 并精心打扮。|Synonyms: foppery, flamboyance, stylishness|Antonyms: untidiness, slovenliness|noun

dandy:/ˈdændi/| n.|花花公子；衣着讲究的男子|A man who is excessively concerned with his clothes and appearance.|He was a dandy, always dressed in the latest fashions. 他是个花花公子，总是穿着最新的时装。|Synonyms: fop, coxcomb, beau|Antonyms: slob|noun
