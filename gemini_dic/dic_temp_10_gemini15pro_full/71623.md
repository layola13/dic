flative:/ˈfleɪtɪv/| adj.|气音的；吹气音的；摩擦音的|Relating to or characterized by a speech sound produced by forcing breath through a narrow channel, such as /f/, /θ/, /s/, or /h/.|例句：The sound /f/ is a flative consonant. /f/音是一个摩擦辅音。|例句：Flative sounds are produced with friction. 摩擦音是通过摩擦产生的。|近义词：fricative|反义词：plosive (爆破音)|词性：adjective
