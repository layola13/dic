bathing:/ˈbeɪðɪŋ/ | adj. | 洗澡的，沐浴的 | Related to or used for bathing. |  bathing suit 泳衣 | swimming, showering, washing | dressing | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *baþung, from baþian "to bathe."  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *baþōną.  Related to Dutch baden, German baden.
