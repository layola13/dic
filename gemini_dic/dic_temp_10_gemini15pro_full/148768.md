overunionize:/ˌōvərˈyūnyənīz/ | v. |过度组织|To organize a workforce with excessive or unnecessarily powerful labor unions, potentially leading to inflexibility and inefficiency. | The company became overunionized, leading to frequent strikes and a decline in productivity.  这家公司工会力量过大，导致频繁罢工，生产力下降。| oversyndicalize | underunionize | verb
overunionized:/ˌōvərˈyūnyənīzd/ | adj. |过度组织的|Characterized by having too many or overly powerful labor unions. | The overunionized factory struggled to compete with more flexible competitors.  这个工会力量过大的工厂难以与更灵活的竞争对手竞争。| excessively unionized | underunionized | adjective
