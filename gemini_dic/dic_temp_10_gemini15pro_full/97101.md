infestivity:/ɪnˌfestɪvɪti/| n. |感染力，侵染力|The ability of a pathogen to establish an infection in a host.  The degree to which something is able to infest or spread.|The infestivity of the new virus strain is alarmingly high.  这种新病毒株的感染力惊人地高。|近义词：contagiousness, transmissibility, virulence|反义词: resistance, immunity|词性:noun
