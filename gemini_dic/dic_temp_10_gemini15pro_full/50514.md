depriver:/dɪˈpraɪvər/| n. |剥夺者，使丧失者|Someone who deprives another person or thing of something.|The depriver of the child's inheritance was brought to justice.  剥夺孩子继承权的人被绳之以法。|近义词：thief, robber, despoiler|反义词:giver, provider|词性:noun
deprive:/dɪˈpraɪv/| vt. |剥夺，使丧失|To take something away from someone or something; to prevent someone or something from having or using something.|The drought deprived the region of its vital water supply. 干旱使该地区失去了重要的水源。|近义词：divest, strip, bereave|反义词:give, provide, endow|词性:verb

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French depriver, based on Latin privus ‘private’.
