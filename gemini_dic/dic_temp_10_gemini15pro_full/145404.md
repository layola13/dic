overcensor:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsər/| v. |过度审查|To censor something excessively, often to the point of suppressing important information or stifling free expression.|The government overcensored the media, restricting access to crucial news. 政府过度审查媒体，限制了关键新闻的获取。|The film was so overcensored that it lost its artistic integrity.  这部电影被过度审查，以至于失去了艺术完整性。|近义词：overscrutinize, overregulate|反义词: undercensor, permit|词性:动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "censor" (from Latin "censere," meaning "to assess, judge, or rate")
