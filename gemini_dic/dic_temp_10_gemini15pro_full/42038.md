costrel:/ˈkɒstrəl/ | n. |  旅行水壶；酒壶；猎鹰用的小袋 | A small, portable container for carrying liquids, especially water or wine, often used by travelers or hunters in the past. Also, a small bag used in falconry. |  The knight carried a costrel filled with water on his journey. 骑士在旅途中带着一个装满水的旅行水壶。| Synonyms: flask, canteen, water bottle | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "tank" or "reservoir" for contrasting size) | Noun
