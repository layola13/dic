detachability:/dɪˌtætʃəˈbɪləti/| n. |可拆卸性，可分离性|The quality of being able to be detached or separated. |The detachability of the keyboard makes the tablet more versatile.  这款平板电脑的键盘可拆卸，使其更加通用。|近义词：removability, separability|反义词：permanence, fixity|词性:名词

词源:  From detach + -ability.  Detach comes from Old French "destachier," from "des-" (apart) + "atachier" (to attach).
