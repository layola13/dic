fluoric:/fluːˈɒrɪk/ /flʊˈɔːrɪk/| adj.| 氟的，含氟的|Pertaining to or derived from fluorine.  Relating to or containing hydrofluoric acid.|Hydrofluoric acid is extremely corrosive and dangerous, so handle all fluoric compounds with extreme caution. 氢氟酸具有极强的腐蚀性和危险性，因此处理所有含氟化合物时都必须格外小心。|近义词:fluorinated|反义词: non-fluoric|词性:形容词
