-alogy:/ˈælədʒi/| n.|类比；类似；相似|A resemblance in some particulars between things otherwise unlike; similarity:  The analogy between the heart and a pump. |The analogy between the heart and a pump is often used to explain its function. 心脏和泵之间的类比常用来解释心脏的功能。|

|Analogy helps us understand complex concepts by comparing them to familiar things.  类比帮助我们通过与熟悉的事物进行比较来理解复杂的概念。|
近义词：similarity, resemblance, likeness, parallel|反义词：difference, dissimilarity, contrast|词性:noun
