intemperant:/ɪnˈtɛmpərət/| adj.|无节制的，放纵的，酗酒的|Lacking moderation or self-restraint, especially in consuming alcohol.|He was known for his intemperant outbursts of anger. 他以其无节制的愤怒爆发而闻名。|Synonyms: excessive, immoderate, unrestrained, indulgent, dissipated|Antonyms: temperate, moderate, restrained, abstemious|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *intemperans*, from Latin in- (“not”) + temperans (“moderating”), present participle of temperare (“to moderate, regulate”).
