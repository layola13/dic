clammer:/ˈklæmər/| n.|攀登者；努力追求的人|A person who climbs, especially one who climbs with difficulty or effort. |The clammera ascended the steep cliff face. 攀登者登上了陡峭的悬崖面。|近义词：climber, scaler, mountaineer|反义词:descender|词性:noun

clammer:/ˈklæmər/| v.|爬；努力争取|To climb, especially with difficulty or effort; to strive or struggle for something. |He clammered over the rocks to reach the summit. 他攀过岩石到达山顶。|近义词：climb, scramble, strive|反义词:descend|词性:verb
