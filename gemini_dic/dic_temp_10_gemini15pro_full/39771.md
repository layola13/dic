consoling:/kənˈsoʊlɪŋ/
adjective
安慰的，慰藉的
reducing or eliminating grief or disappointment
例句：Her consoling words brought me some comfort. 她的安慰话语给我带来了一些安慰。

例句：It was consoling to know that I wasn't alone. 知道我不是一个人，这让我感到安慰。

近义词：comforting, soothing, reassuring
反义词：distressing, upsetting, disheartening
词性：adjective

consoling:/kənˈsoʊlɪŋ/
verb
安慰，慰问 (present participle of console)
to comfort someone at a time of grief or disappointment
例句：She was consoling her friend after the loss of her job.  她在朋友失业后安慰她。

例句：He spent the afternoon consoling his grieving sister. 他用了一下午的时间安慰他悲伤的妹妹。

近义词：comforting, soothing
反义词：distressing, upsetting
词性：verb
