enzymatic:/ˌɛnzaɪˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 酶的；酶促的 | Relating to or involving enzymes; catalyzed by an enzyme. |  The enzymatic breakdown of starch into sugars. 淀粉酶促分解成糖。| enzymatic activity is essential for digestion. 酶活性对消化至关重要。 | 近义词: catalytic, fermentative | 反义词: non-enzymatic | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From enzyme + -atic.  Enzyme comes from Greek "enzymos" meaning "leavened," from "en-" (in) + "zyme" (leaven).
