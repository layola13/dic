anchorless:/ˈæŋkərˌləs/| adj.|无锚的，无着落的，没有方向的|Lacking an anchor; also, lacking stability or direction.  Figuratively, feeling lost or adrift.|例句：He felt anchorless and adrift after losing his job.  他失掉工作后感到茫然无措。

例句：The anchorless boat drifted out to sea.  没有锚的船漂到了海上。|近义词：adrift, rootless, unstable, groundless, directionless|反义词:grounded, rooted, secure, stable, directed|词性:形容词
