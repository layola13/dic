guiding:/ˈɡaɪdɪŋ/| adj.|指导的；引导的；主导的|serving as a guide; directing or influencing someone or something.|例句：The guiding principles of the organization are transparency and accountability. 该组织的指导原则是透明度和问责制。|近义词：leading, directing, piloting, shepherding|反义词：misleading, following|词性:形容词

guiding:/ˈɡaɪdɪŋ/| n.|指导；引导|the act of guiding or leading someone or something.|例句：The museum offers guiding tours for visitors.  博物馆为游客提供导游服务。|近义词：leadership, direction, guidance|反义词：misguidance|词性:名词


词源: From Middle English *guiden*, from Anglo-Norman guider, Old Northern French *guider* (“to guide, lead”), of Germanic origin; related to Old High German witan (“to watch, guard”), from Proto-Germanic *witaną (“to watch, observe, know”).
