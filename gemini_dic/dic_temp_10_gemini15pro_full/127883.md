neocortices:/ˌniːoʊˈkɔːrtɪsiːz/ | n. | 新皮质 | The plural form of neocortex, referring to the evolutionarily newest part of the cerebral cortex, responsible for higher-order brain functions such as sensory perception, motor commands, spatial reasoning, conscious thought, and language. | The neocortices of mammals are much larger than those of reptiles. 哺乳动物的新皮质比爬行动物的大得多。 | Synonym: neocortex (singular) | Antonym: allocortex | Noun
