monographer:/məˈnɒɡrəfər/
n.
专著作者；专题论文作者
A person who writes a monograph (a detailed written study of a single specialized subject or an aspect of it).
Alan Macfarlane, the Cambridge social anthropologist and monographer of the English family, has turned his attention to Japan.  艾伦·麦克法兰，剑桥社会人类学家和英国家庭专题论文作者，已将注意力转向日本。
近义词:specialist, expert, scholar, writer
反义词:generalist, amateur
词性:noun

monograph:/ˈmɒnəɡrɑːf/
n.
专著；专题论文
A detailed written study of a single specialized subject or an aspect of it.
He is preparing a monograph on the history of jazz. 他正在准备写一本关于爵士乐历史的专著。
近义词:treatise, dissertation, study, paper
反义词:overview, summary
词性:noun
