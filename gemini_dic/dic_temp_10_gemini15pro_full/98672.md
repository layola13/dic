insulating:/ˈɪnsəˌleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 绝缘的，隔热的 | Preventing the passage of heat, electricity, or sound. | The house has good insulating walls, so it stays warm in winter.  这房子墙壁的隔热效果很好，所以冬天很暖和。| 近义词: non-conducting, isolating, protective | 反义词: conducting, transmitting | 词性: adjective

insulate:/ˈɪnsəˌleɪt/ | v. | 隔离，使绝缘，使隔热 | To cover or protect something with a material that prevents the passage of heat, electricity, or sound. |  They insulated the attic to save energy. 他们把阁楼隔热以节省能源。| 近义词: protect, shield, isolate | 反义词: expose | 词性: verb

insulation:/ˌɪnsəˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 绝缘，隔热，隔离材料 | Material used to prevent the passage of heat, electricity, or sound. | The insulation in the walls keeps the house warm. 墙壁中的隔热材料使房子保持温暖。| 近义词: padding, lagging, isolation | 反义词: conduction | 词性: noun

词源:  From Latin *insulatus*, past participle of *insulare* "to make into an island, isolate," from *insula* "island."
