overexpressively:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspresivli/
adverb
过度表达地，表情夸张地
In a way that expresses feelings too strongly or obviously.
She reacted overexpressively to the news, bursting into tears and then into laughter. 她对这个消息反应过度，先是放声大哭，然后又大笑起来。
He overexpressively gestured with his hands while telling the story. 他讲故事的时候手势夸张。
Synonym: dramatically, theatrically, exaggeratedly
Antonym: subtly, understatedly, reservedly
词性: 副词
