ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *exaltacioun*, from Old French *exaltacion*, from Latin *exaltātiō*, from *exaltātus*, past participle of *exaltāre* (“to raise, elevate”), from *ex-* (“out, up”) + *altus* (“high”).
