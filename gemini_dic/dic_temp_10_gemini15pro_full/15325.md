backslappings:/ˈbækˌslæpɪŋz/
n.
互相拍背；庆功会；过分的恭维；(复数)庆祝活动
the act of hitting someone on the back in a friendly way, especially to show congratulations
例句：There was a lot of backslapping and cheering after the team won the championship.  球队赢得冠军后，大家互相拍背欢呼。

例句：The CEO's speech was full of self-congratulatory backslappings. 首席执行官的演讲充满了自吹自擂。
近义词：congratulations, praise, celebration, felicitation
反义词：criticism, condemnation
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From back + slapping (present participle of slap).  由 back（背部）+ slapping（slap 的现在分词）构成。
