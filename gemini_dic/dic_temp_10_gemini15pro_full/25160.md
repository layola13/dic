buckshot:/ˈbʌkʃɒt/ (UK) /ˈbʌkʃɑːt/ (US)| n. |鹿弹，霰弹|A type of large lead shot used in shotgun shells, often for hunting deer or other large game. It consists of many small pellets fired simultaneously.|The hunter loaded his shotgun with buckshot. 猎人用鹿弹装填了他的猎枪。|He bagged a deer with a single buckshot. 他用一颗鹿弹打到了一只鹿。|近义词：shot, pellets|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

buckshots:/ˈbʌkʃɒts/ (UK) /ˈbʌkʃɑːts/ (US)| n. |鹿弹（复数）|Multiple pellets of buckshot.|The buckshots spread wide upon firing. 鹿弹发射后散布得很广。|He found several buckshots lodged in the tree. 他发现几颗鹿弹卡在了树上。|近义词：shots, pellets|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
