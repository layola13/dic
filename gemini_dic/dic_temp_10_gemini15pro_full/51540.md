deturpate: /dɪˈtɜːrpeɪt/ /dɪˈtɝːpeɪt/
verb
玷污，败坏，损毁
to make impure or unclean; to corrupt morally; to debase; to defile.
The scandal deturpated the reputation of the politician.  这桩丑闻败坏了这位政治家的名声。
The graffiti deturpated the beauty of the ancient building.  涂鸦损毁了这座古老建筑的美观。
近义词: defile, corrupt, debase, taint, sully, mar, spoil
反义词: purify, cleanse, elevate, enhance, improve
词性: verb

Etymology:
From Latin *dēturpāre*, from *dē-* (down, away from) + *turpis* (ugly, shameful, base).
