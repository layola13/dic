encampment:/ɪnˈkæmpmənt/| n. |营地，宿营地|A place where people set up camp, especially a military camp or a temporary settlement of refugees or travelers.|The refugees set up an encampment near the border.  难民们在边境附近建立了一个营地。|The army established an encampment in the valley.  军队在山谷中建立了营地。|近义词：camp, bivouac, settlement|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "encampement" (act of encamping), from "encamper" (to encamp), from "en-" (in) + "camp" (field). Ultimately from Latin "campus" (field).
