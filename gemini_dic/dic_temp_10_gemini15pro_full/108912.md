ledgy:/ˈlɛdʒi/
adjective
多岩架的；峭壁的；险峻的
Having or abounding in ledges. Characterized by narrow horizontal shelves or ledges.
The climbers scaled the ledgy cliffs. 攀岩者攀登了多岩架的悬崖。
近义词:cliff-like, craggy, precipitous, rocky
反义词:smooth, flat, even
词性:adjective
