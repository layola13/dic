desks: /desks/
n.
桌子(复数)
Flat surfaces, often rectangular, supported by legs, used for working, writing, or placing things on.  A piece of furniture with a flat top and one or more legs, providing a level surface for working at, writing on, or displaying objects.
The students sat at their desks, diligently taking notes. 学生们坐在自己的桌子旁，认真地做笔记。
Many modern offices have standing desks. 许多现代办公室都配备了站立式办公桌。
synonyms: tables, work surfaces, countertops
antonyms: floor, ground
noun
