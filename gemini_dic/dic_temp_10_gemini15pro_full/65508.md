eurosceptics:/ˈjʊrəʊˈskɛptɪks/ | n. | 欧洲怀疑论者 | People who are opposed to increasing the powers of the European Union or to further European integration. |  Many eurosceptics believe that national sovereignty is being eroded by the EU. 许多欧洲怀疑论者认为国家主权正被欧盟侵蚀。

The party attracted a large number of eurosceptics. 该党吸引了大量的欧洲怀疑论者。| 近义词：anti-federalists, EU-critics | 反义词：europhiles, pro-Europeans | 词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "Euro-" (short for "European") + "sceptic" (from Greek "skeptikos," meaning "inquiring" or "thoughtful," later evolving to mean "doubtful").
