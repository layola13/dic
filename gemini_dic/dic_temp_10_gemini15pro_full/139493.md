obstruct:/əbˈstrʌkt/
verb
阻碍，妨碍
To block or hinder progress or movement.
The fallen tree obstructed the road.  倒下的树挡住了道路。
Bureaucracy often obstructs progress. 官僚主义常常阻碍进步。
近义词: block, hinder, impede
反义词: aid, assist, facilitate
词性: verb

obstruction:/əbˈstrʌkʃən/
noun
阻碍，障碍物
Something that blocks or hinders progress or movement.
The road was closed due to an obstruction. 由于障碍物，道路封闭了。
His presence was an obstruction to our plans. 他的出现对我们的计划是一个阻碍。
近义词: obstacle, barrier, impediment
反义词: aid, assistance, help
词性: noun
