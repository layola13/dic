ianthina:/ˌaɪænˈθaɪnə/ /ˌaɪænˈθiːnə/
noun
紫螺，帆螺
A genus of small pelagic marine snails, also known as violet snails or ocean snails, that float on the ocean surface by means of a raft of air bubbles. They feed on jellyfish and other pelagic organisms.
The ianthina captured a small jellyfish with its sticky mucus. 紫螺用它的粘液捕获了一只小水母。
近义词: violet snail, ocean snail
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific genus)
词性: n.

ianthine:/ˈaɪænθaɪn/ /ˈaɪænθiːn/
adjective
紫罗兰色的，紫色的
Of a violet or purple color, like that of the ianthina snail.
The sunset painted the sky in ianthine hues. 日落把天空染成了紫罗兰色。
近义词: violet, purple, lavender
反义词: yellow, green, orange
词性: adj.
