countableness:/ˈkaʊntəb(ə)lnəs/
n.
可数性
The quality of being countable; the fact of being able to be counted.
The countableness of nouns is an important concept in English grammar.  名词的可数性是英语语法中的一个重要概念。
近义词: numerability
反义词: uncountableness
词性: noun
