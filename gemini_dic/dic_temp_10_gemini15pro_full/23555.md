brainstem:/ˈbreɪnstem/| n.|脑干|The part of the brain connecting the cerebrum with the spinal cord, comprising the midbrain, pons, and medulla oblongata. It controls fundamental body functions such as breathing, heart rate, and consciousness.|Damage to the brainstem can be life-threatening.  脑干损伤可能会危及生命。

The brainstem regulates vital functions. 脑干调节重要的生命功能。|近义词:None (It's a specific anatomical term)|反义词:None|词性:noun
