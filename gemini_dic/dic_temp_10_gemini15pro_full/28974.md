cars:/kɑːrz/| n.|汽车；轿车|A road vehicle, typically with four wheels, powered by an internal combustion engine and able to carry several passengers.|There are too many cars on the road. 路上的汽车太多了。|automobiles, vehicles|trucks, bicycles|noun
Etymology: From Middle English *cares, from Old Northern French *cars (plural of *car), from Vulgar Latin *carra (plural of *carrum), from Gaulish *carros.
