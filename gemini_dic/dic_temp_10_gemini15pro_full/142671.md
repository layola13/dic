ostriferous:/ɒˈstrɪfərəs/
adjective
产牡蛎的；盛产牡蛎的
Producing or yielding oysters; abounding in oysters.
The Chesapeake Bay is an ostriferous estuary. 切萨皮克湾是一个盛产牡蛎的河口。
Synonyms: oyster-bearing, oyster-rich
Antonyms: barren, oysterless
词性: 形容词
