hastinesses:/ˈheɪstinɪsɪz/
noun
仓促，草率，轻率
The state of being hasty; undue speed or rashness.
His hastinesses in making decisions often led to mistakes. 他做决定时的草率常常导致错误。
The project was marred by hastinesses and a lack of planning.  这个项目因仓促和缺乏规划而受到损害。

近义词:rush, hurry, rashness, impulsiveness, precipitateness
反义词:carefulness, deliberation, caution, patience, thoroughness
词性:n. (plural noun)
