atmolysis:/ætˈmɒlɪsɪs/
noun
渗透分气法; 气体扩散分离法
The separation of gases by using their different rates of diffusion through a porous barrier.
Atmolysis is used to separate isotopes of uranium. 渗透分气法用于分离铀的同位素。
近义词: gaseous diffusion
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "gas mixing")
词性: n.
