demilitarise:/diːˈmɪlɪtəraɪz/| v.|使非军事化，解除武装|To remove military forces, weapons, or fortifications from a place.|The treaty required both countries to demilitarise the border zone.  该条约要求两国将边界地区非军事化。|The government decided to demilitarise the island. 政府决定将该岛非军事化。|近义词：disarm, neutralise|反义词：militarise, arm|词性:动词


demilitarisation:/diːˌmɪlɪtərəˈzeɪʃən/| n.|非军事化，解除武装|The act or process of demilitarising a place or thing.|The demilitarisation of the area was a key part of the peace agreement.  该地区的非军事化是和平协议的关键部分。|The successful demilitarisation of the zone reduced tensions significantly.  该区域的成功非军事化大大缓和了紧张局势。|近义词：disarmament, neutralisation|反义词：militarisation, armament|词性:名词


词源:  de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + military + -ise/-ize (suffix forming verbs)  来自"de-"（前缀，意为“移除”或“反转”）+ military（军事的）+ -ise/-ize（后缀，构成动词）
