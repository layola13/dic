belly:/ˈbeli/ (US) /ˈbɛli/ (UK)| v. |鼓起，膨胀|To swell or bulge outwards.|The sails bellied in the wind. 帆在风中鼓了起来。|synonyms: swell, bulge, expand, distend|antonyms: deflate, shrink, contract|verb
