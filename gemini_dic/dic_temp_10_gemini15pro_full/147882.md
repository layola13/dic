overreacts:/ˌoʊvərriˈækts/| v.|反应过度|To react or respond more strongly than is necessary or appropriate.|例句：He tends to overreact to criticism. 他容易对批评反应过度。

例句：She overreacted when I told her the news. 当我告诉她这个消息时，她反应过度了。|近义词：overdo, exaggerate, overplay|反义词: underreact|词性:动词
