colossality:/ˌkɑːləˈsælɪti/ /ˌkɒləˈsælɪti/| n.|巨大；庞大；巨大性|The colossality of the project intimidated the team.  这个项目的庞大性吓倒了团队。|The sheer colossality of the ancient pyramids is awe-inspiring. 古老金字塔的巨大规模令人敬畏。|近义词：immensity, vastness, enormity, hugeness|反义词: smallness, tininess, minuteness|词性:noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *colossalitas, from Latin colossus (“gigantic statue”) + -itas (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality). Ultimately from Ancient Greek κολοσσός (kolossós, “gigantic statue”).
