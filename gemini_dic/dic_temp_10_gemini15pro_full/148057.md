oversales:/ˌoʊvərˈseɪlz/ | n. | 超额销售；过量销售 | The act of selling more of something, such as tickets or reservations, than is actually available.  | The airline oversold the flight and had to bump several passengers. 航空公司超售了航班，不得不拒绝几名乘客登机。 |
