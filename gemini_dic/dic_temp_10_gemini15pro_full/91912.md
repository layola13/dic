hyperlustrousness:/ˌhaɪpərˈlʌstrəsnəs/
| n. |极度光亮，极度光泽 |Extreme shininess or glossiness; excessive luster.|The hyperlustrousness of the newly polished car was blinding in the sunlight. 新抛光汽车的极度光泽在阳光下令人目眩。|近义词：glossiness, sheen, brilliance, radiance|反义词：dullness, matteness|词性:名词

词源:
hyper- (prefix):  from Greek, meaning "over, beyond, excessive"
lustrous (adjective): from Old French *lustros, from Latin lustrosus "bright, shining," from lustrare "to purify, make bright," related to lux "light"
-ness (suffix): forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality
