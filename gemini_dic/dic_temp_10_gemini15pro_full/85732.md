hawkbill:/ˈhɔːkbɪl/| n.|玳瑁，鹰嘴龟，玳瑁甲|A marine turtle with a sharply curved beak-like mouth, prized for its shell, which is used to make jewelry and other decorative items.|The hawkbill turtle is critically endangered due to habitat loss and poaching for its beautiful shell. 玳瑁由于栖息地丧失和因其美丽的壳而被偷猎，正处于极度濒危状态。|Synonyms: hawksbill turtle, tortoise shell turtle|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a specific animal species)|noun

hawkbill:/ˈhɔːkbɪl/| adj.|钩状的，鹰嘴状的|Shaped like the beak of a hawk.|The hawkbill knife has a distinctive curved blade. 这把鹰嘴刀有一个独特的弯曲刀刃。|Synonyms: curved, hooked, beak-like|Antonyms: straight|adjective
