micronutrients:/ˈmaɪkrəˌnjuːtriənts/ | n. |微量营养素|Nutrients required by the body in small amounts, such as vitamins and minerals. | A balanced diet should include sufficient micronutrients for optimal health. 均衡的饮食应包含足够的微量营养素以达到最佳健康状态。|  Micronutrients are essential for various bodily functions. 微量营养素对各种身体机能至关重要。|synonyms: trace elements, vitamins, minerals | antonyms: macronutrients | noun
