blog:/blɒɡ/ (British) /blɑːɡ/ (American)| n. v.|博客；网络日志|A regularly updated website or web page, typically one run by an individual or small group, that is written in an informal or conversational style.|She writes a blog about her travels around the world. 她写了一个关于她环游世界旅行的博客。|He blogs about his daily life. 他在他的博客上记录他的日常生活。|近义词：online journal, weblog|反义词: None|词性: noun, verb

Etymology: Shortening of *weblog*, a blend of *web* and *log*.  Originally, a log was a record of a ship's speed and progress, later extended to any kind of record.
