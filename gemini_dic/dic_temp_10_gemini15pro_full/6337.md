amperage:/ˈæmpərɪdʒ/| n.|电流强度；安培数|The amperage of the circuit is too high. 电路的电流强度太高。|例句：The amperage of the house wiring needs to be checked.  房屋布线的安培数需要检查。

例句：High amperage can cause overheating. 高电流会导致过热。|近义词：current, current strength|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "low amperage" or "resistance")|词性: noun
