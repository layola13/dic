nonsmoking:/ˌnɑːnˈsmoʊkɪŋ/ /ˌnɒnˈsməʊkɪŋ/| adj.|不吸烟的；禁止吸烟的|Not smoking; designated for people who do not smoke.|This is a nonsmoking restaurant. 这是一家禁烟餐厅.|
Synonyms: smoke-free, tobacco-free|
Antonyms: smoking|
adjective


nonsmoking:/ˌnɑːnˈsmoʊkɪŋ/ /ˌnɒnˈsməʊkɪŋ/| n.|不吸烟者；禁止吸烟|The practice or state of not smoking; a person who does not smoke (less common as a noun).|The nonsmoking section was full. 禁烟区已满。|
Synonyms: nonsmoker|
Antonyms: smoker|
noun
