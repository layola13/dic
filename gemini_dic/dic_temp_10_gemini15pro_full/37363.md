compensation:/ˌkɒmpənˈseɪʃn/ | n. | 补偿，赔偿，报酬 | something, typically money, awarded to someone to make up for loss, injury, or suffering. |例句：He received compensation for his injuries. 他因受伤获得了赔偿。

例句：She demanded compensation for the damage to her car. 她要求赔偿她的汽车损失。

近义词：remuneration, recompense, indemnity, restitution |反义词：penalty, forfeiture | 词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *compensationem, from compensare "to weigh against, counterbalance," from com- "together" + pensare "to weigh."
