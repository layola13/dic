evoked: /ɪˈvəʊkt/ /ɪˈvoʊkt/ | adj. | 唤起的，激起的 | Brought to mind or recollection; called forth. |  The song evoked memories of my childhood. 这首歌唤起了我对童年的回忆。 |近义词：elicited, aroused, stimulated, prompted, inspired | 反义词：suppressed, quelled, stifled | 词性：形容词

evoke: /ɪˈvəʊk/ /ɪˈvoʊkt/ | vt. | 唤起，引起 | To call forth (a feeling, response, etc.); elicit. | Her performance evoked enthusiastic applause. 她的表演引起了热烈的掌声。 |近义词：elicit, arouse, stimulate, prompt, inspire | 反义词：suppress, quell, stifle | 词性：动词
