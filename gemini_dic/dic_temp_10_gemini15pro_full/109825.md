lexicographer:/ˌlɛksɪˈkɒɡrəfər/ /ˌlɛksɪˈkɑːɡrəfər/ | n. | 词典编纂者 | A person who compiles dictionaries. | Samuel Johnson is perhaps the most famous lexicographer in the English language.  塞缪尔·约翰逊也许是英语中最著名的词典编纂者。

| Example: The lexicographer spent years researching the etymology of words.  这位词典编纂者花了数年时间研究词源。| Synonym: dictionary compiler, word expert | Antonym: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *lexicographe*, from Greek *lexikographos* "a writer of dictionaries," from *lexikon* "dictionary" + *-graphos* "writer."
