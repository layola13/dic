anthologizing:/ænˈθɑːləˌdʒaɪzɪŋ/
v.
选集编纂；将…收入选集
The process of compiling an anthology, or including a piece of writing in an anthology.
He is anthologizing the best poems of the 20th century. 他正在编纂20世纪最佳诗歌选集。
近义词: compiling, collecting, selecting
反义词: excluding, omitting
词性: 动词 (verb)

Etymology:  From anthology + -ize.  Anthology comes from the Greek "anthologia" meaning "collection of flowers" (anthos = flower, logia = collection).  The "-ize" suffix turns a noun into a verb meaning "to make or become".
