fengite:/ˈfɛnˌaɪt/
n.
透光石膏，透石膏
A variety of gypsum that is transparent or translucent and sometimes used for windows.
The medieval castle's narrow windows were made of fengite, allowing some light to filter through. 中世纪城堡的窄窗是用透光石膏制成的，允许一些光线透过。
synonyms:selenite, alabaster
antonyms: N/A
