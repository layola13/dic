hi:/haɪ/
interjection
你好，嗨
An informal greeting.
Hi, there! 嗨，你好！
Hi, how are you doing? 嗨，你好吗？
Synonyms: hello, hey
Antonyms: goodbye, farewell
词性:感叹词

被动语态 (Passive Voice):  Hi is not typically used in the passive voice as it is an action of greeting.  打招呼这个词本身通常不用于被动语态。
