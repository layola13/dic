antitheoretical:/ˌæntiθiːəˈretɪkl/
adjective
反理论的；反对理论的
opposed to or distrustful of theory, especially when it is considered impractical
His antitheoretical stance made him resistant to new ideas in the field.  他反理论的立场使他不接受该领域的新思想。
The CEO, a self-made man, had an antitheoretical approach to business.  这位白手起家的首席执行官对商业采取了一种反理论的方法。
近义词:empirical, practical, pragmatic
反义词:theoretical, academic, speculative
词性:adjective
