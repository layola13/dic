considerably:/kənˈsɪdərəbli/| adv.|相当地，显著地|To a great extent or degree; significantly.|The price has increased considerably. 价格上涨了很多。|近义词：significantly, substantially, markedly, noticeably, appreciably|反义词：slightly, marginally, insignificantly|词性:副词
词源: From the adjective "considerable" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Considerable" comes from the Late Latin "considerabilis," from "considerare" (to consider), from "com-" (together) + "sidus" (star, constellation). Originally, the word meant "worthy of consideration," later evolving to mean "large" or "important."
