gapless:/ˈɡæpləs/| adj.|无缝的，连续的，无间隙的|Without any gaps or interruptions; continuous.|The album features a gapless playback experience, flowing seamlessly from one track to the next. 这张专辑的特点是无缝播放体验，可以从一首曲目无缝过渡到下一首曲目。|例句：The film is a gapless narrative, unfolding in real time.  这部电影是一个无缝的叙事，实时展开。|近义词：continuous, seamless, unbroken, uninterrupted|反义词:broken, interrupted, discontinuous, gapped|词性:形容词
Etymology:  "gap" (Old Norse "gap" meaning opening) + "-less" (Old English "-lēas" meaning without).  Essentially means "without gaps".
