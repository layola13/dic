civilising:/ˈsɪvɪlaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
使文明的，教化的
bringing culture and education to a society or group of people, making them more refined and polite
The civilising influence of education is undeniable. 教育的教化作用是不可否认的。
近义词:  refining, cultivating, enlightening, educating
反义词:  barbarising, brutalising, coarsening
词性: adjective
