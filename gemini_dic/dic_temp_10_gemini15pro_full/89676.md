hoopoes:/ˈhuːpuːz/| n. |戴胜|A brightly colored bird with a long, downcurved bill and an erectile crest.|例句：We saw a hoopoe foraging for insects in the grass. 我们看到一只戴胜在草地上觅食昆虫。

例句：Hoopoes are known for their distinctive call. 戴胜以其独特的叫声而闻名。|近义词：bird, fowl |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

Etymology: From Old French *hupe, from Latin *upupa, imitative of the bird's cry.
