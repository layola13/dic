nonvirtuous:/ˌnɑːnˈvɜːrtʃuəs/| adj.|不道德的，邪恶的，没有德行的|Lacking virtue; wicked or immoral.|例句：His nonvirtuous behavior led to his downfall. 他的不道德行为导致了他的垮台。|例句：The nonvirtuous leader was eventually overthrown.  那个邪恶的领导人最终被推翻了。|近义词：wicked, immoral, unethical, sinful, corrupt|反义词：virtuous, righteous, moral, ethical|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From non- + virtuous.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "virtuous" comes from the Latin "virtus," meaning "strength, excellence, goodness, virtue."
