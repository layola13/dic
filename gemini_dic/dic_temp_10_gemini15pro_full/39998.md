consultantships:/kənˈsʌltənʃɪps/ | n. | 咨询工作；顾问职位；咨询服务 | Many graduates pursue consultantships before settling into permanent roles. 许多毕业生在找到固定工作之前会从事咨询工作。| Synonyms: consultancies, advisory positions, advisory services | Antonyms:  permanent positions, full-time employment | noun

Etymology: From "consultant" + "-ship," a suffix denoting a state, condition, or office.  "Consultant" ultimately derives from the Latin "consultare," meaning "to consult."
