coaches:/ˈkoʊtʃɪz/| n.|教练；长途公共汽车；四轮大马车；经济舱|A coach is a person who trains athletes or other performers. It can also refer to a bus or carriage used for long-distance travel, or the cheapest class of air travel.|The football coaches are working hard to prepare the team for the championship game. 足球队教练们正在努力备战冠军赛。|近义词：trainer, instructor, bus, carriage, economy class|反义词:student, player, first class|noun


coaches:/ˈkoʊtʃɪz/| v.|训练；指导；辅导|To coach is to train or instruct someone, typically in sport or a particular skill.|She coaches the local swim team. 她执教当地的游泳队。|近义词：train, instruct, tutor, guide, mentor|反义词:learn|verb


Etymology:
From the Hungarian town of Kocs, where fast, horse-drawn carriages were made. The word "coach" originally referred to these carriages and later expanded to include other meanings related to transportation and instruction.
