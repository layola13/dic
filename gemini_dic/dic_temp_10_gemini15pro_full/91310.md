hydrometric:/ˌhaɪdrəˈmɛtrɪk/
adjective
水文的；测水的
Relating to the measurement of water, especially the flow and level of rivers and streams.
Hydrometric measurements are crucial for managing water resources.  水文测量对于管理水资源至关重要。
The hydrometric station collected data on river discharge. 水文站收集了有关河流流量的数据。
近义词: water-measuring
反义词: None
