caffetannic:/ˌkæfiˈtænɪk/| adj.|咖啡鞣酸的，咖啡单宁酸的|An adjective describing substances related to or derived from caffetannic acid, a type of tannin found in coffee.|Coffee's bitter taste is partially due to its caffetannic acid content. 咖啡的苦味部分源于其咖啡鞣酸含量。|Synonyms: chlorogenic, caffeic|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "sweet" in the context of taste)|adjective
