meetings:/ˈmiːtɪŋz/ (US), /ˈmiːtɪŋz/ (UK)| n. |会议，会面|A gathering of people for a particular purpose, especially for discussion or decision-making.|We have weekly meetings every Monday. 我们每周一开会。|
近义词：gatherings, assemblies, conferences, sessions|
反义词：separations, dispersals|
词性：名词(plural)

meetings:/ˈmiːtɪŋz/| n. |会面|An occasion when people come together.|I had a brief meeting with the CEO. 我和首席执行官进行了一次简短的会面。|
近义词：encounters, rendezvous|
反义词：partings|
词性：名词(plural)


词源: From Middle English *metynge, from Old English *mēting, from Proto-Germanic *mōtijaną (“to meet”), from Proto-Indo-European *mot- (“to assemble”).
