muskiness:/ˈmʌskɪnɪs/ | n. |麝香味，麝香的气味 |A faint but pervasive smell, resembling that of musk. | The room was filled with the muskiness of old books and dried flowers.  房间里充满了旧书和干花的麝香味。| 近义词: aroma, scent, fragrance, redolence | 反义词: odorlessness, inodorousness |词性:名词

musky:/ˈmʌski/ | adj. | 麝香的，有麝香气味的 | Having a smell or taste of musk; smelling strongly of musk. | The old wooden chest had a musky aroma.  那个旧木箱有一股麝香的气味。| 近义词: aromatic, fragrant, scented | 反义词: unscented, odorless |词性:形容词
