outflashing:/ˌaʊtˈflæʃɪŋ/
verb
胜过；比…更闪亮
surpassing in flashing; emitting a brighter flash than.
The fireworks were outflashing each other in the night sky. 焰火在夜空中交相辉映，一个比一个闪亮。
synonyms: outshining, outdazzling, outglittering
antonyms: dimming, darkening
词性: 动词
