cubits:/ˈkjuːbɪts/| n.|腕尺，肘|An ancient unit of length, approximately equal to the length of a forearm from the elbow to the tip of the middle finger, typically about 18 inches (46 centimeters).|The ark was 300 cubits long, 50 cubits wide and 30 cubits high. 方舟长300肘，宽50肘，高30肘。|近义词：forearm, ell|反义词:|名词
