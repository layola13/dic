estimableness:/ˈɛstɪməbəlnəs/
|n.|可估量性；值得尊敬；可敬|The estimableness of a person's character is often revealed through their actions. 一个人的品格是否值得尊敬通常通过他们的行为来体现。|
|英文解析: The quality of being estimable; worthiness of esteem; the state of deserving respect and admiration.|例句：Her estimableness was evident in her dedication to helping others. 她的可敬之处体现在她致力于帮助他人。|例句: The estimableness of the scientific method lies in its objectivity and reproducibility. 科学方法的可贵之处在于它的客观性和可重复性。|近义词：respectability, worthiness, merit, value|反义词：unworthiness, disreputability, contemptibility|词性: noun

Etymology:
From estimable + -ness.  Estimable comes from Late Middle English: from Old French estimable, from Latin aestimabilis, from aestimare ‘to estimate’.
