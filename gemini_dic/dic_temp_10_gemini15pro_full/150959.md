paramaleic:/ˌpærəˈmeɪlɪk/| adj.| 副顺丁烯二酸的，顺酐的 | relating to or denoting the isomer of maleic acid in which the carboxyl groups are on opposite sides of the molecule |  Paramaleic acid is less stable than maleic acid. 副顺丁烯二酸不如顺丁烯二酸稳定。| synonym: fumaric | antonym: maleic | adjective
