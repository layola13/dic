flattest:/ˈflætɪst/ | adj. | 最平的，最平坦的 |  Superlative form of "flat," meaning having a level surface without raised or indented areas.  It indicates the highest degree of flatness among a group of things being compared. | This is the flattest pancake I've ever seen. 这是我见过最平的煎饼。 | The Netherlands is one of the flattest countries in the world. 荷兰是世界上最平坦的国家之一。 | level, even, smooth, plane |  hilly, bumpy, uneven, rugged | adjective
