extrapolatable:/ɪkˈstræpəleɪtəbl/
adjective
可外推的，可推断的
able to be extrapolated.  To infer (an unknown) from something that is known; conjecture.
The results of the study are extrapolatable to the general population. 这项研究的结果可以推断到一般人群。
extensible, predictable, projectable
limited, restricted
adjective
