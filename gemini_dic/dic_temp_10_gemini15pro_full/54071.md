diseasement:/dɪˈziːzmənt/ | n. | 疾病，病害 | The state of being diseased or afflicted by illness. |  The diseasement of the crops caused a widespread famine. 庄稼的病害导致了大范围的饥荒。| 近义词: sickness, illness, ailment | 反义词: health, wellness | 词性: 名词

词源: From disease + -ment.  "Disease" comes from Old French "desaise" meaning "discomfort, lack of ease," ultimately from Latin "dis-" (not) + "aise" (ease). The suffix "-ment" forms nouns from verbs or other nouns, indicating a state, condition, or result.
