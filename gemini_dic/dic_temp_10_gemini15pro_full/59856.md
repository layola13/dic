ectozoon:/ˈektəʊˌzuːɒn/ (US: /ˈektəˌzoʊɑːn/)
n.
外寄生虫
An animal that lives on the outside of another animal.
Fleas and lice are ectozoons. 跳蚤和虱子都是外寄生虫。
