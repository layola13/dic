dehumanises:/ˌdiːhjuːmənaɪzɪz/
verb
使失去人性
Deprives of human qualities, personality, or spirit; makes impersonal or mechanical.
The propaganda dehumanises the enemy, portraying them as monsters.  这宣传使敌人失去人性，把他们描绘成怪物。
The factory work dehumanises the workers, turning them into cogs in a machine. 工厂的工作使工人失去人性，把他们变成机器上的齿轮。
近义词:degrades, brutalizes, depersonalizes
反义词:humanizes, personalizes, dignifies
词性:v.


dehumanise:/ˌdiːhjuːmənaɪz/
verb
使失去人性
Deprives of human qualities, personality, or spirit; makes impersonal or mechanical.
The prison system can dehumanise inmates. 监狱制度会使囚犯失去人性。
War often dehumanises the enemy. 战争经常使敌人失去人性。
近义词:degrades, brutalizes, depersonalizes
反义词:humanizes, personalizes, dignifies
词性:v.


词源:  From de- + humanize, from human + -ize.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse." "Humanize" means "to make human." Therefore, "dehumanize" means to remove human qualities.
