noninflations:/ˌnɒnɪnˈfleɪʃənz/ | n. | 非通货膨胀；非通货膨胀时期 |A period of little or no inflation.  A state where the general price level of goods and services is stable or experiencing a very slight increase over a period of time, as opposed to a significant and sustained rise.|  The government's policies aimed to create an era of noninflations. 政府的政策旨在创造一个非通货膨胀的时代。|Synonyms: price stability, disinflation, low inflation | Antonyms: inflation, hyperinflation | noun
