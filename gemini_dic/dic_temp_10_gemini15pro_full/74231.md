fowl:/faʊl/
n.|家禽；禽肉|Any bird kept for its eggs or flesh, especially chickens.|We raise chickens and other fowl. 我们饲养鸡和其他家禽。|近义词:bird, poultry|反义词:game (referring to wild birds hunted for food)|词性:名词

fowl:/faʊl/
adj.(古语，罕用)|污秽的；恶劣的；犯规的|Offensive, disgusting, or unfair.|The fowl weather kept us indoors. 恶劣的天气让我们待在室内。|近义词:unpleasant, disgusting, unfair|反义词:pleasant, fair|词性:形容词

fowl:/faʊl/
v.(古语，罕用)|捕猎野禽|To hunt wildfowl.|He went fowling in the marshes. 他到沼泽地去捕猎野禽。|近义词:hunt|反义词:release|词性:动词


词源:
From Old English *fugol, from Proto-Germanic *fuglaz, from Proto-Indo-European *plew- (“to fly”). Cognate with Dutch vogel, German Vogel, Gothic fugls.
