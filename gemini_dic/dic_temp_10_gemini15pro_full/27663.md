candidest:/ˈkændɪdɪst/
adjective
最坦率的，最直白的
most honest and straightforward; frank
例句：That was the candidest answer I've ever heard. 那是我听过的最坦率的回答。

例句：She gave the candidest assessment of the situation. 她对形势作出了最坦率的评估。

近义词：frankest, most honest, most straightforward, bluntest, plainest
反义词：most evasive, most secretive, most guarded, least honest

词性: adjective
