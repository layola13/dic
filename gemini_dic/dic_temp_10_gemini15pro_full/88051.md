highfalutin:/ˌhaɪfəˈluːtɪn/| adj.|浮夸的，高傲的，装腔作势的|Pompously inflated or pretentious, especially in language.|He delivered a highfalutin speech that no one understood. 他发表了一篇没人能懂的浮夸演讲。|近义词：pompous, pretentious, bombastic, grandiose|反义词：simple, unpretentious, modest, down-to-earth|adjective
