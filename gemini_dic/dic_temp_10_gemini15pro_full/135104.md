nonobsessions:/ˌnɒnəbˈseʃənz/
n.
非痴迷；不痴迷的事物
Lack of obsessions; things that one is not obsessed with.
Her nonobsessions include celebrity gossip and reality TV. 她不痴迷的事情包括名人八卦和真人秀电视。
近义词:disinterests, apathy
反义词:obsessions, fixations
词性:名词

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "obsession" (from Late Latin obsessionem, "siege," from Latin obsidere, "besiege," from ob- "against" + sedere "to sit").  Thus, literally "not a siege (of the mind)."
