fortifies:/ˈfɔːrtɪfaɪz/
verb
加强，增强，巩固
to make something stronger, especially in order to protect it against attack
例句：The troops fortified the city against the invaders. 部队加固了城市以抵御入侵者。

例句：He fortified himself with a hot drink before going out in the blizzard. 他在暴风雪中外出前喝了一杯热饮来增强体力。
近义词：strengthen, reinforce, bolster
反义词：weaken, undermine
词性：v.


fortification:/ˌfɔːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/
noun
防御工事，强化，增强
the act of fortifying something, or something that has been built to protect a place
例句：The castle’s fortifications were impressive. 城堡的防御工事令人印象深刻。

例句：The addition of vitamins is a fortification for your health. 添加维生素可以增强你的健康。
近义词：defense, reinforcement, stronghold
反义词：weakness, vulnerability
词性：n.
