demobilizing:/diːˈmoʊbəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
|v.|复员；遣散（尤指战后）|The process of discharging personnel from military service, especially after a war.  The government is demobilizing thousands of soldiers. 政府正在遣散数千名士兵。|
|adj.|复员的；遣散的|Relating to or involved in demobilization.  The demobilizing troops faced an uncertain future.  正在复员的部队面临着不确定的未来。|
|近义词:|discharging, releasing, dismissing|
|反义词:|mobilizing, recruiting, enlisting|
|词性:|Verb, adjective|
|词源:|From de- (meaning "reverse" or "remove") + mobilize (meaning "to make ready for war or other emergency").  Ultimately from Latin mobilis ("movable").|
