debar:/dɪˈbɑːr/
verb
禁止，阻止
to officially prevent someone from doing something or going somewhere
例句：He was debarred from holding public office. 他被禁止担任公职。

例句：She was debarred from the competition for using illegal drugs. 她因使用违禁药物而被禁止参赛。

近义词：prohibit, forbid, preclude, prevent, exclude
反义词：permit, allow, authorize
词性：v.
