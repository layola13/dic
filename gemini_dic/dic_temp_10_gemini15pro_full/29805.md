caudicle:/ˈkɔːdɪkəl/
n.
小柄，花柄，花粉块柄
A small stalk, stem or support, especially the stalk attaching a pollen mass to the retinaculum in orchids.
The caudicle of the orchid is delicate and easily broken.  兰花的花粉块柄很脆弱，容易断裂。
