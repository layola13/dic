factorizing:/ˈfæktəˌraɪzɪŋ/
n./因式分解，分解因数/
The process of finding the factors of a number or expression.
例句：Factorizing large numbers can be challenging.  分解大数可能很有挑战性。

例句：The factorizing of the polynomial revealed its roots. 多项式的因式分解揭示了它的根。

近义词：factoring, decomposition
反义词：multiplying, expanding
词性：动词的现在分词形式，用作名词


factorize:/ˈfæktəˌraɪz/
v./分解因数，因式分解/
To express a number or algebraic expression as a product of its factors.
例句：Can you factorize this equation? 你能分解这个方程式吗？

例句：We learned how to factorize quadratic equations in algebra class. 我们在代数课上学了如何分解二次方程。

近义词：factor, decompose
反义词：multiply, expand
词性：动词
