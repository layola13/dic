blazonment:/ˈbleɪzənmənt/
n.
纹章的描绘；炫耀；公开宣扬
the act of describing a coat of arms in technical terms; a showy display; a public proclamation
The blazonment of the royal arms was intricate and detailed. 皇家纹章的描绘错综复杂，非常详细。
The company's success was a blazonment of their hard work and dedication. 公司的成功是他们辛勤工作和奉献精神的炫耀。

近义词:display, exhibition, proclamation, announcement
反义词:concealment, hiding, suppression

词性:noun

词源: From Old French *blasonnement, from blasoner "to describe a coat of arms," from blason "coat of arms."
