booth:/buːθ/
n.
小隔间，摊位，电话亭
a small enclosed space, especially one where you can do something privately, such as voting or making a phone call.
I went into the phone booth to call my mom. 我到电话亭里给妈妈打电话。

The party had a booth at the county fair. 该党在县集市上有一个摊位。

stall, kiosk, cubicle
shop, store
noun
