motherliness:/ˈmʌðərlɪnɪs/| n.|母性，慈母般|The quality of having or showing the tenderness, care, and protectiveness of a mother.|She radiated motherliness, comforting the crying child.|近义词：maternality, maternalism|反义词：coldness, indifference|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From mother + -li + -ness.  "Mother" comes from Old English "mōdor," from Proto-Germanic "*mōdēr," ultimately from Proto-Indo-European "*méh₂tēr."  "-li" is a suffix forming adjectives, and "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
