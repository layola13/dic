minaret:/ˌmɪnəˈrɛt/ /ˈmɪnəˌrɛt/ | n. |尖塔，光塔|A tall slender tower, typically part of a mosque, with a balcony from which a muezzin calls Muslims to prayer.|The minaret stood tall against the evening sky.  尖塔高耸于夜空之下。|The mosque's minaret is visible from miles away. 从几英里外就能看到清真寺的尖塔。|近义词：spire, tower|反义词:base, foundation|词性:noun

minaret: |n.|叫拜楼|A slender tower attached to a mosque, from which Muslims are called to prayer.|We could hear the call to prayer from the minaret. 我们可以听到从尖塔传来的祈祷的召唤。|From the top of the minaret, you could see the whole city. 从尖塔的顶部，你可以看到整个城市。|近义词：bell tower (functionally similar in Christianity)|反义词: N/A (No direct architectural opposite)|词性:noun

Etymology: From French *minaret*, from Turkish *minare*, from Arabic *manāra* "lighthouse," from *nāra* "fire".
