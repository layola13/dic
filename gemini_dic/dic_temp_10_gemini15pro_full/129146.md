no-frills:/ˌnoʊˈfrɪlz/| adj.|朴实的，简单的，廉价的|Without unnecessary extras or features; basic.  Often implies low cost.  Derived from "frills" meaning ornamental additions, suggesting the absence of these makes something simpler and cheaper.|We stayed in a no-frills hotel near the airport. 我们住在机场附近一家朴实无华的酒店。|Synonyms: basic, simple, budget, economical, inexpensive|Antonyms: luxurious, extravagant, premium, deluxe|adjective

no-frills:/ˌnoʊˈfrɪlz/| n.|朴实无华，简单|The state of being without unnecessary extras or features. |The no-frills approach to travel appealed to him. 这种朴实无华的旅行方式吸引了他。|Synonyms: simplicity, basicness, economy|Antonyms: luxury, extravagance, premium|noun
