autoclave:/ˈɔːtəˌkleɪv/ (US), /ˈɔːtəˌkleɪv/ (UK)| n. v.|高压灭菌器；用高压灭菌器消毒|A strong, heated container used for chemical reactions and other processes using high pressures and temperatures, especially for sterilizing medical equipment.|The dentist's office uses an autoclave to sterilize their instruments. 牙医诊所使用高压灭菌器对器械进行消毒。|We autoclaved the surgical tools before the operation. 我们在手术前用高压灭菌器对外科工具进行了消毒。|近义词：sterilizer|反义词:|n., v.
