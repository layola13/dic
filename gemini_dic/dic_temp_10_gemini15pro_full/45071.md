cubicles:/ˈkjuːbɪkəlz/
n.
小隔间，办公隔间
A small partitioned-off area of a room, for example one containing a bed or desk.
Many office workers spend their days in cubicles. 许多办公室职员整天都在小隔间里工作。

cubicles:/ˈkjuːbɪkəlz/
n.
小房间，小卧室
A small room or compartment.
The hospital's intensive care unit is divided into individual cubicles. 医院的重症监护室被分成独立的小房间。


Etymology: From Latin *cubiculum* (a bedroom, sleeping chamber), diminutive of *cubus* (a cube).

Synonyms: compartments, booths, partitions, stalls, workstations

Antonyms: open space, large room, hall
