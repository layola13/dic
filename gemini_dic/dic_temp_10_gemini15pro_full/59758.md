economicus:/ˌiːkəˈnɒmɪkəs/ | adj. | 经济人的；理性经济人的 |  Relating to or characteristic of homo economicus, a hypothetical person who behaves rationally and is primarily motivated by self-interest. | Example:  The "economicus" model assumes individuals always make optimal choices. “经济人”模型假设个人总是做出最优选择。| Synonym: rational, self-interested | Antonym: altruistic, irrational | adjective
