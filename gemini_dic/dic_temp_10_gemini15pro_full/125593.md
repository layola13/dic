multiunion:/ˌmʌltiˈjuːnjən/ | n. |多工会|A situation where multiple labor unions represent workers within the same organization or industry.|The multiunion environment made negotiations complex. 多工会环境使谈判变得复杂。|Example: The company had to navigate a complex multiunion environment. 这家公司不得不应对复杂的多工会环境。|Synonyms: multi-unionism|Antonyms: single-union, sole-union|Noun
