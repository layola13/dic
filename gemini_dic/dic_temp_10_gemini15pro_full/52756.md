dilate:/daɪˈleɪt/
|v.|
|扩张，膨胀|
|To make or become wider, larger, or more open.|
|Her pupils dilated in the dark. 她的瞳孔在黑暗中扩大了。|
|The blood vessels dilate to increase blood flow. 血管扩张以增加血流量。|
|近义词: expand, widen, enlarge|
|反义词: contract, constrict, shrink|
|词性: verb|
|词源: From Latin "dilatare" (to spread out).|
