metacryst:/ˈmɛtəkrɪst/ | n. |变晶，偏晶|A relatively large crystal embedded in a finer-grained matrix of the same mineral. A metacryst forms by recrystallization within a metamorphic rock.|例句：The metacryst of garnet stood out against the finer-grained schist.  石榴石变晶在细粒片岩中很突出。

例句：The geologist identified a large metacryst of kyanite in the metamorphic rock sample. 地质学家在变质岩样品中发现了一块大的蓝晶石变晶。|近义词：porphyroblast, porphyroclast|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
