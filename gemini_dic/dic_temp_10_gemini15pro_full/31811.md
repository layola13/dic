checkroom: /ˈtʃɛkˌruːm/ (US), /ˈtʃekˌrʊm/ (UK)| n. |衣帽间，行李寄存处|A room, as in a hotel, restaurant, or theater, where coats, baggage, and other articles may be left for safekeeping.|I left my coat in the checkroom. 我把我的外套放在衣帽间了。|近义词：cloakroom, baggage room|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "check" (meaning to leave or deposit something) + "room." "Check" in this sense comes from the practice of giving a token or check as a receipt for the item left.
