gulleting:/ˈɡʌlɪtɪŋ/
verb
狼吞虎咽，吞食
the action of swallowing something greedily or rapidly.
He was gulleting down his food as if he hadn't eaten in days. 他狼吞虎咽地吃着东西，好像好几天没吃东西一样。
近义词: devouring, gobbling, gorging
反义词: nibbling, sipping
词性: verb (动名词)


gullet:/ˈɡʌlɪt/
noun
食道，咽喉
the esophagus; the passage by which food passes from the mouth to the stomach.
The fish bone got stuck in his gullet. 鱼刺卡在他的食道里了。
近义词: esophagus, throat
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a body part)
词性: noun
