committer:/kəˈmɪtər/| n.|提交者，犯事者，委托人|A person who commits something, especially a crime or a mistake, or who makes a promise to do something.|The committer of the crime was never caught. 犯罪者从未被抓获。|Synonyms: perpetrator, offender, culprit|Antonyms: victim, innocent|noun


committer:/kəˈmɪtər/| n.|承诺者，保证人|A person who pledges or commits themselves to a particular cause or course of action.|She is a committer to environmental protection. 她是一个致力于环境保护的人。|Synonyms: supporter, advocate, devotee|Antonyms: opponent, adversary|noun
