melodyless:/ˈmɛlədilɪs/| adj.|无旋律的，不悦耳的|Lacking melody; tuneless.  It describes something that doesn't have a pleasant or recognizable tune.|The melodyless song was hard to enjoy. 这首没有旋律的歌很难欣赏。|Synonyms: tuneless, unmelodious, unmusical|Antonyms: melodic, melodious, musical|adjective
