matoke:/məˈtoʊkeɪ/
n.
煮熟的香蕉，尤其指东非的一种主食
A starchy variety of banana, especially one used as a staple food in East Africa.
Matoke is a popular dish in Uganda.  马托克是乌干达的一道流行菜。
We had matoke and beef stew for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了马托克和炖牛肉。
近义词:plantain, green banana
反义词:(无明确反义词)
词性:名词
