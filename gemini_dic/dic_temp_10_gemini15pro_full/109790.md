levorotatory:/ˌliːvəˈrəʊtətəri/ /ˌlevəˈroʊtətɔːri/| adj.|左旋的|Rotating the plane of polarized light to the left.  A levorotatory substance rotates polarized light counterclockwise.|例句：The compound was found to be levorotatory.  该化合物被发现是左旋的。

例句：Levorotatory isomers are designated with a minus sign (-). 左旋异构体用负号 (-) 表示。|近义词：left-handed, counterclockwise|反义词:dextrorotatory, clockwise|词性:adjective
