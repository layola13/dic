ablaze:/əˈbleɪz/| adj.|燃烧的，着火的；闪耀的，激动的|Burning fiercely; very brightly lit or colored; filled with anger or another strong emotion.|例句：The building was ablaze. = 这栋楼着火了。

The sky was ablaze with stars. = 天空布满了星星。|近义词：aflame, alight, burning, fiery, ignited, blazing, radiant, glowing|反义词:extinguished, unlit, dull|词性:形容词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The forest is ablaze after the lightning strike. = 闪电过后，森林燃烧起来了。
Her face is ablaze with excitement. = 她的脸上洋溢着兴奋的光芒。


一般过去时:
The car was ablaze after the crash. = 撞车后，汽车燃烧了起来。
His eyes were ablaze with anger. = 他的双眼充满了愤怒。


现在完成时:
The house has been ablaze for hours. = 这房子已经燃烧了好几个小时了。
The city has been ablaze with lights for the festival. = 为了节日，城市灯火通明。


过去完成时:
The warehouse had been ablaze for some time before the firefighters arrived. = 在消防员到达之前，仓库已经燃烧了一段时间了。
Her cheeks had been ablaze with embarrassment after the incident. = 事件发生后，她的脸颊因为尴尬而涨得通红。


将来时:
If the fire spreads, the whole mountain will be ablaze. = 如果火势蔓延，整座山都会着火。
The stadium will be ablaze with lights for the concert. = 音乐会期间，体育场将灯火辉煌。


虚拟语气:
If the house were ablaze, I would call the fire department. = 如果房子着火了，我会打电话给消防部门。
I wish the sky were ablaze with northern lights tonight. = 我希望今晚的天空能闪耀着北极光。


被动语态:
(Ablaze is rarely used in the passive voice as it describes a state.)


疑问句:
Is the building ablaze? = 这栋楼着火了吗？
Was her face ablaze with anger? = 她的脸上是充满了愤怒吗？


否定句:
The building is not ablaze. = 这栋楼没有着火。
Her eyes were not ablaze with anger, but with sadness. = 她的眼里没有燃烧着怒火，而是充满了悲伤。




PHRASE->
set ablaze = 点燃，使燃烧
The arsonist set the building ablaze. = 纵火犯点燃了大楼。

be ablaze with = 燃烧着...；闪耀着...
The sky was ablaze with stars. = 天空布满了星星。

become ablaze = 开始燃烧
The dry leaves quickly became ablaze. = 干枯的叶子迅速燃烧起来。

keep ablaze = 保持燃烧
They kept the bonfire ablaze all night. = 他们让篝火整夜燃烧着。

burst into ablaze = 突然燃烧起来
The oil-soaked rags suddenly burst into ablaze. = 浸油的破布突然燃烧起来。
