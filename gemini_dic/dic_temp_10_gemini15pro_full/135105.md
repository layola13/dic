nonobsessive:/ˌnɒnəbˈsesɪv/ | adj. | 非强迫性的；不沉迷的 | Not characterized by or exhibiting obsession; not excessively preoccupied with something. | She has a nonobsessive interest in healthy eating. 她对健康饮食有着非强迫性的兴趣。|  He took a nonobsessive approach to collecting stamps. 他以一种不沉迷的方式收集邮票。 | 近义词: casual, moderate, balanced, healthy | 反义词: obsessive, compulsive, fixated | 词性: 形容词

词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + obsessive (from Latin *obsessus, past participle of obsidere "to besiege, blockade," from ob "against" + sedere "to sit").
