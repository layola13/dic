mismates:/ˌmɪsˈmeɪts/
noun
不匹配的人或物；错误搭配；配错对
people or things that are not well suited to each other; a bad match or pairing.
The dating app was full of mismates, leading to many awkward first dates.  这个约会应用程序充满了不匹配的人，导致许多尴尬的第一次约会。
近义词: mismatches, incompatibles
反义词: matches, compatibles
词性:n.

mismate:/ˌmɪsˈmeɪt/
verb
错误地匹配；配错对
to match incorrectly or pair wrongly.
The scientist mismated the chemicals, causing a small explosion. 科学家错误地搭配了化学品，导致了小爆炸。
近义词: mismatch
反义词: match
词性:v.
