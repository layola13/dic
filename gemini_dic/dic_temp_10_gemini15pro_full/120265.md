mini-nation:/ˈmɪni ˈneɪʃən/ |n.|微型国家，小型民族|A very small nation, often referring to a micronation or a nation with a very small population or land area.|  The concept of a mini-nation, while intriguing, often lacks international recognition. 微型国家的概念虽然有趣，但往往缺乏国际认可。

The island declared itself a mini-nation, much to the amusement of its larger neighbors.  这个岛屿宣布自己是一个微型国家，这让它的邻国感到很有趣。|近义词:micronation, small nation, microstate|反义词:large nation, superpower|词性:noun
