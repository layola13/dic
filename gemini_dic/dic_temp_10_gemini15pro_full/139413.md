observes:/əbˈzɜːrvz/
verb
观察；遵守；庆祝；评论
watches carefully; follows rules or customs; commemorates; remarks
The scientist observes the behavior of the animals. 科学家观察动物的行为。
She observes all the religious holidays. 她遵守所有的宗教节日。
近义词: watches, monitors, follows, celebrates, comments
反义词: ignores, disregards, violates, neglects
词性: verb
