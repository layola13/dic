oblivionize: /əˈblɪviəˌnaɪz/ | v. | 使遗忘；消除记忆 | To cause someone to forget something, often magically or through other extraordinary means. |  The wizard oblivionized the witnesses to ensure his secret remained safe.  巫师消除了目击者的记忆，以确保他的秘密安全。 |  The Aurors oblivionized the Muggle who accidentally saw a dragon.  傲罗们消除了意外看到龙的麻瓜的记忆。 | 近义词: erase, wipe out, expunge | 反义词: remember, recall | 词性: 动词

Etymology:  From Latin *oblīviō* (“forgetfulness”) + *-ize*, a suffix used to form verbs meaning “to make or become”.
