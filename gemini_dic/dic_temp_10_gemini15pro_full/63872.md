epileptiform:/ˌɛpɪˈlɛptɪfɔːrm/ | adj. |癫痫样的，癫痫形式的 |Resembling epilepsy or its manifestations; characteristic of epilepsy. |The EEG showed epileptiform activity. 脑电图显示癫痫样放电。| The patient experienced epileptiform seizures. 病人经历了癫痫样发作。|Synonyms: epileptic, convulsive | Antonyms: non-epileptic | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From epi- (upon, over) + Greek *lēptikós (able to seize) + -form (having the form of).  Ultimately from the Greek verb *lambánein (to take, seize).
