archeologies: /ˌɑːrkiˈɒlədʒiz/ | n. |考古学（archaeology的复数形式）| the study of human history and prehistory through the excavation of sites and the analysis of artifacts and other physical remains. |例句：New archeologies suggest the site is much older than previously thought. 新的考古学研究表明，该遗址比以前认为的要古老得多。

例句：The various archeologies of the region reveal a complex and fascinating past. 该地区的各种考古研究揭示了一个复杂而迷人的过去。| 近义词：archaeology | 反义词:  (archaeology lacks a direct antonym; consider terms like "futurology" or "modern studies" for contrasting concepts) | 词性: noun
