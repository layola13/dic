outgleamed:/ˌaʊtˈɡliːmd/
verb
胜过…的光辉；比…更闪亮
surpassed in brightness or splendor.
Her diamond outgleamed all the others at the gala. 她的钻石在晚会上比所有其他钻石都闪亮。
近义词:outshone, eclipsed, surpassed
反义词:dimmed, overshadowed
词性:动词

词源:
From out- + gleam.  "Out-" acts as a prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing. "Gleam" comes from Old English *glǣm, of Germanic origin.
