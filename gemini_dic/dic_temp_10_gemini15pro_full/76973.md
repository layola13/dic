garboesque:/ˌɡɑːrboʊˈɛsk/| adj.|神秘的，孤僻的，有格调的|Evoking the mysterious, aloof, and glamorous style of Greta Garbo, a Swedish-American film actress known for her enigmatic persona.|Her garboesque demeanor added to her mystique. 她那神秘孤傲的举止增添了她的神秘感。|Synonyms: aloof, enigmatic, glamorous, mysterious, reserved, reticent, stylish, sophisticated|Antonyms: approachable, communicative, extroverted, outgoing, sociable|adjective
