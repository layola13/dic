babelic:/ˌbæˈbɛlɪk/ /ˌbeɪˈbɛlɪk/
adjective
混乱的，嘈杂的，像巴别塔一样的
Resembling the Tower of Babel; characterized by a confusion of voices and languages.  A scene of utter chaos and noise, reminiscent of the biblical Tower of Babel.
The marketplace was a babelic scene, with vendors shouting in a dozen different languages.  市场像巴别塔一样嘈杂，小贩们用十几种不同的语言叫卖。

The construction site was a babelic mix of noise, dust, and activity.  建筑工地一片混乱，到处是噪音、灰尘和各种活动。

近义词: chaotic, tumultuous, noisy, clamorous
反义词: quiet, peaceful, orderly, serene
词性: adjective
词源:  From Babel, the biblical city where the confusion of languages originated (Genesis 11:9).
