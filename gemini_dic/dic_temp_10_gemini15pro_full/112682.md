lowkey:/ˌloʊˈkiː/| adv. adj.|低调地，低调的|Quietly or subtly; not elaborate, showy, or intensive.  Origin: African American Vernacular English (AAVE), from "low key" meaning literally a lower musical key or volume, figuratively meaning subdued or restrained.|例句：She had a lowkey birthday celebration this year. 今年她低调地庆祝了生日。|近义词：subtle, understated, discreet, quiet, chill|反义词：flashy, ostentatious, loud, public|词性:副词/形容词
