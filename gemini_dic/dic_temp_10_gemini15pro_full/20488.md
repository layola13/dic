bituminizing:/bɪˈtʃuːmɪnaɪzɪŋ/
| v. |沥青化; 柏油化|The process of treating something with bitumen or asphalt.  This involves applying bitumen to a surface, such as a road, to create a waterproof and durable layer.|They are bituminizing the road outside my house. 他们正在我家外面的道路上铺沥青。|近义词：asphalting, tarring|反义词: N/A|动词

bituminized:/bɪˈtʃuːmɪnaɪzd/
| adj. |沥青化的; 柏油化的|Treated or coated with bitumen or asphalt.|The newly bituminized road was smooth and black. 新铺的柏油路又平又黑。|近义词：asphalted, tarred|反义词: N/A|形容词
