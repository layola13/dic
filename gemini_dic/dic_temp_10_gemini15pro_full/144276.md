outsource: /ˈaʊtsɔːrs/ (UK), /ˈaʊtsɔːrs/ (US) | v. | 外包，外购 | To obtain (goods or a service) from an outside or foreign supplier, especially in place of an internal source. |  Many companies outsource their IT operations to specialized firms. 许多公司将他们的IT运营外包给专业公司。

The company outsources its customer service to a call center in India.  这家公司将其客户服务外包给印度的一个呼叫中心。

|Synonyms: contract out, subcontract, farm out| Antonyms: insource| verb
