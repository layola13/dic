malted:/ˈmɔːltɪd/ | adj. |麦芽的；用麦芽制成的 | Made with malt. |  She enjoys malted milk balls. 她喜欢吃麦芽牛奶球。| Synonyms: malty | Antonyms: None | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From malt + -ed.  Malt itself comes from Old English *mealt (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *maltą (“malt”), from Proto-Indo-European *mel- (“to grind, rub”).
