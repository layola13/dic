counterhypotheses:/ˌkaʊntərhaɪˈpɑːθəsiːz/
|n.|反假设，对立假设|A proposition that is the opposite of a hypothesis being tested. It offers an alternative explanation for the phenomenon under investigation.|Researchers tested several counterhypotheses to ensure the robustness of their findings. 研究人员测试了几个反假设，以确保他们研究结果的稳健性。|The defense attorney presented a counterhypothesis to explain the evidence. 辩护律师提出了一个反假设来解释证据。|alternatives, competing theories|null hypothesis|noun
