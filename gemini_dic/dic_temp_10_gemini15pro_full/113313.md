lutz:/lʌts/| n.|卢茨跳|A jump in figure skating in which the skater takes off from the back outside edge of one skate, rotates once in the air, and lands on the back outside edge of the other skate. |She landed the lutz perfectly. 她完美地完成了卢茨跳。|近义词:toe loop, flip, axel (all figure skating jumps)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
