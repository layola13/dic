impluvium:/ɪmˈpluːviəm/| n.|蓄水池，天井|A basin or cistern in the floor of a Roman atrium to receive rainwater from the compluvium.|The impluvium collected rainwater, which was then stored for household use. 天井收集雨水，然后储存起来供家庭使用。|Synonyms: cistern, basin, reservoir|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "roof" or "drainage system" in a functional sense)|noun
