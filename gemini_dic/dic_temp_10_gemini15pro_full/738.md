absinthic: /æbˈsɪnθɪk/
adjective
苦艾酒的；像苦艾酒的
Of or relating to absinthe; containing or resembling absinthe in flavor, color, or intoxicating effect.
His breath had an absinthic tang. 他的呼吸带有一股苦艾酒的味道。
The painting evoked an absinthic haze, a swirling green mist of memory and desire. 这幅画唤起了一种苦艾酒般的朦胧感，一种记忆和欲望的绿色漩涡。
近义词: absinthe-flavored, absinthe-like
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) sober, clear-headed
词性: adjective

absinthic: /æbˈsɪnθɪk/
noun
苦艾酒
Absinthe. (Less common as a noun)
