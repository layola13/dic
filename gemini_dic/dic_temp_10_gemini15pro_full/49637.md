demilancer:/ˌdemɪˈlænsər/ | n. | 半枪骑兵；轻骑兵 | A soldier, originally one of a class of light cavalry armed with a lance. |  Example 1: The demilancer charged across the field, his lance lowered. 轻骑兵冲过战场，他的长矛放低了。

Example 2:  Demilancers were often used for scouting and raiding. 轻骑兵经常被用于侦察和突袭。| 近义词: light horseman, lancer, cavalryman | 反义词: infantryman, foot soldier | 词性: noun

词源: From French "demi-lancier," meaning "half-lancer," from "demi-" (half) + "lancier" (lancer).  The term originally referred to a light cavalryman armed with a lance, lighter than a fully armored lancer.
