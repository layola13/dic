herringbone:/ˈherɪŋˌboʊn/| n. adj.|人字形，人字纹|A pattern consisting of rows of short slanted parallel lines, with the direction of the slant alternating row by row, resembling the bones of a herring.|The floor was laid in a herringbone pattern.|近义词：zigzag, chevron|反义词:|noun, adjective
