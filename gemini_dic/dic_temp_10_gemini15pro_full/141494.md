opisthobranchiate: /əˌpɪsθəˈbræŋkiət/ | adj. n. | 后鳃类的，后鳃动物 |  Relating to or denoting a large subclass (Opisthobranchia) of marine gastropod mollusks that includes the sea slugs, sea hares, bubble shells, and sea butterflies. They are characterized by having the gills, when present, posterior to the heart and often exposed or partially exposed.  | Example: The opisthobranchiate sea slug is a fascinating creature.  后鳃类海蛞蝓是一种迷人的生物。

Example:  Opisthobranchiates are found in various marine environments.  后鳃动物存在于各种海洋环境中。| 近义词: gastropod (更广义), sea slug (部分后鳃类) | 反义词:  N/A (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑其他类型的软体动物) | 词性:  adjective, noun


Etymology: From Greek *opistho- "behind" + *branchia "gill"
