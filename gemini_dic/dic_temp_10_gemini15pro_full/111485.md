loadstar:/ˈləʊdstɑːr/ (US) /ˈloʊdstɑːr/| n. |指路明灯；指导原则；北极星|Something that serves as a guide or inspiration.  It originally referred to Polaris, the North Star, used for navigation.|例句：The company's mission statement serves as a loadstar for all its employees.  公司使命宣言是所有员工的指路明灯。

例句：Her unwavering faith was her loadstar in difficult times. 她坚定的信念是她度过难关的指路明灯。|近义词：guiding light, polestar, north star, beacon, lodestar|反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider phrases like "source of confusion," "misleading influence")|词性: noun
