nonedibility:/ˌnɒnˌedɪˈbɪləti/
n.
不可食性；不能食用
The nonedibility of certain mushrooms can have serious consequences.
某些蘑菇的不可食性可能会造成严重后果。
nonedibility stems from various factors, including toxins, tough texture, or unpleasant taste.
不可食用性源于多种因素，包括毒素、坚韧的质地或令人不快的味道。
近义词:inedibility, unsuitability for consumption
反义词:edibility
词性:名词

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + edible + -ity (suffix forming nouns denoting quality or state)
