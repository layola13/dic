outglooming:/ˌaʊtˈɡluːmɪŋ/
|v.|使黯然失色，胜过|To surpass in gloominess; to exceed in depressing or disheartening character.|The dark clouds outglooming the sun portended a storm. 乌云遮蔽了太阳，预示着暴风雨即将来临。| Outglooming the previous year's poor performance, this year's sales figures were truly dismal.  今年的销售数据比去年糟糕的表现还要惨淡，实在令人沮丧。|近义词:overshadowing, eclipsing, surpassing|反义词:brightening, cheering, encouraging|词性:动词

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + "gloom" (noun meaning darkness, sadness) + "-ing" (present participle suffix)
