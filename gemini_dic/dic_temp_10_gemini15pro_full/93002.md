idiopathy:/ˌɪdiəˈpæθi/ | n. | 特发症，原因不明的疾病 |A disease or condition that arises spontaneously or for which the cause is unknown. | Example: The doctor diagnosed the patient with idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis. 医生诊断该病人患有特发性肺纤维化。

Example:  Idiopathic hypersomnia can be a debilitating condition. 特发性嗜睡症可能是一种使人衰弱的疾病。 | Synonym: essential, primary, spontaneous | Antonym: secondary, acquired | noun
