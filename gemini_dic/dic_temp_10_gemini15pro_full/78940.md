girder:/ˈɡɜːrdər/ (US), /ˈɡɜːdə/ (UK)| n. |大梁，桁梁|A large horizontal beam, typically of steel or concrete, that supports a structure such as a bridge or building.|The bridge was supported by massive steel girders. 这座桥由巨大的钢梁支撑。|The engineer calculated the load-bearing capacity of the girder. 工程师计算了大梁的承重能力。|近义词:beam, joist|反义词:N/A|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *gyrdere, from gyrdan "to gird," related to yard.  The original sense was "something that girds or encircles," hence a horizontal supporting timber.
