cites:/saɪts/| v.|引用，引证，提及|mentions as an example or to support an argument or point.|The author cites several experts in the field. 作者引用了该领域的几位专家。|quotes, references, mentions|omits, ignores|verb
cites:/saɪts/| n.| 召唤，传唤（古语，罕用）|a summons to appear in court (archaic)|He received a cites to appear before the judge. 他收到了出庭的传唤。(罕用)|summons, subpoena|release, dismissal|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *citen*, from Anglo-Norman *citer*, from Latin *citāre* (“to summon, urge, call, rouse”), frequentative of *ciēre* (“to move, stir, rouse, call”).
