logging:/ˈlɒɡɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈlɑːɡɪŋ/ (US)| n.|伐木；日志记录|The act of cutting down trees for timber.  The act of recording events, processes, or data.|例句：Logging is a major industry in this region.  伐木是这个地区的主要产业。

例句：The system has automatic error logging. 系统具有自动错误记录功能。|近义词：lumbering, timbering (for cutting trees); recording, documenting (for recording data)|反义词:reforestation, afforestation (for cutting trees)|noun
