aryanize:/ˈɛəriəˌnaɪz/ | v. | 雅利安化；使具有雅利安特征 | To give something or someone characteristics of Aryans, often by purging elements considered non-Aryan.  This often involved promoting a racist ideology and persecuting minority groups. | The Nazis tried to Aryanize German culture by eliminating Jewish influences.  纳粹试图通过消除犹太人的影响来使德国文化雅利安化。 |  The regime sought to Aryanize the education system. 该政权试图将教育系统雅利安化。 | 近义词: racialize, purify (in a racist context) | 反义词: diversify, integrate | 词性: 动词
