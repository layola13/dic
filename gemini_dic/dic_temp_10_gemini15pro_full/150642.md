papistlike:/ˈpeɪpɪstlaɪk/
adjective
像天主教徒的；像教皇的；具有天主教徒特征的
Resembling or characteristic of a papist (a historical term for a Roman Catholic, often used disparagingly).
His papistlike reverence for tradition irritated his more progressive colleagues. 他对传统的类似天主教徒的崇敬激怒了他那些更进步的同事。
近义词: popish, Roman Catholic (in older, often derogatory usage)
反义词: Protestant, non-Catholic
