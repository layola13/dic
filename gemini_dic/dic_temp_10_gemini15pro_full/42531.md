countermand:/ˌkaʊntərˈmænd/ | v. | 撤销，取消（命令）；收回（成命） | To revoke or cancel (a command or order); to recall or rescind (a previous order). | The general countermanded the attack after realizing the enemy was stronger than anticipated. 将军意识到敌人比预期的要强大后，取消了进攻。|  The CEO countermanded the previous instructions regarding the merger. 首席执行官撤销了先前关于合并的指示。 | synonyms: revoke, rescind, cancel, annul, override, repeal | antonyms: order, command, authorize, approve, ratify | verb

countermand:/ˈkaʊntərˌmænd/ | n. | 取消（命令）；撤回（成命） | An order revoking a previous command. | The countermand arrived just in time to prevent the disastrous mission. 取消命令及时到达，阻止了灾难性的任务。 | The captain received a countermand from headquarters. 船长收到了总部的取消命令。 | synonyms: revocation, cancellation, annulment, repeal | antonyms: order, command, authorization, approval, ratification | noun
