coincidentally: /ˌkoʊˌɪnsɪˈdɛntəli/ /ˌkəʊɪnsɪˈdentəli/ | adv. | 巧合地，碰巧地 |  happening by chance or at the same time as something else | I coincidentally ran into my old friend at the airport. 我在机场碰巧遇到了我的老朋友。| synonym: accidentally, fortuitously, by chance | antonym: deliberately, intentionally, purposely | adverb

coincidence: /koʊˈɪnsɪdəns/ /kəʊˈɪnsɪdəns/ | n. | 巧合，巧合的事 | a situation in which two or more things happen at the same time by chance | It was a remarkable coincidence that we both chose the same hotel. 我们都选择了同一家酒店，真是个非凡的巧合。| synonym: accident, chance, fluke | antonym: intention, plan, design | noun

coincide: /ˌkoʊɪnˈsaɪd/ /ˌkəʊɪnˈsaɪd/ | v. | 巧合，同时发生 | to happen at the same time |  My birthday coincides with my parents' anniversary. 我的生日与我父母的结婚纪念日是同一天。| synonym: concur, synchronize, correspond | antonym: differ, diverge, clash | verb
