antidogmatical:/ˌæntiˌdɒɡˈmætɪkl/ /ˌæntiˌdɑːɡˈmætɪkl/ | adj. |反教条的，非教条的|Opposed to or characterized by the rejection of dogma, especially in religion or philosophy.  It describes someone or something that avoids strict adherence to established doctrines or beliefs.| He took an antidogmatical approach to religious education, encouraging students to question and explore. 他对宗教教育采取了反教条主义的方法，鼓励学生质疑和探索。| synonyms: undogmatic, open-minded, liberal, tolerant, flexible | antonyms: dogmatic, doctrinaire, rigid, inflexible, authoritarian | adjective
