corrugated:/ˈkɔːrəɡeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 波纹状的，有瓦楞的 | Having a wavy surface or formed into folds or grooves. | Example: Corrugated cardboard is commonly used for packaging.  波纹状纸板通常用于包装。 | Example: The roof was made of corrugated iron. 屋顶是用波纹铁皮做的。 | Synonyms: ridged, grooved, furrowed, fluted | Antonyms: smooth, flat | adjective

corrugate:/ˈkɔːrəɡeɪt/ | v. | 使起波纹，使起皱褶 | To contract or cause to contract into wrinkles or folds; to make wavy. | Example: The metal sheet was corrugated to increase its strength.  金属板被弄皱以增加其强度。 | Example:  Heat can corrugate plastic.  热量会使塑料起皱。 | Synonyms: wrinkle, crinkle, furrow, crimp | Antonyms: smooth, flatten | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "corrugatus," past participle of "corrugare" (to wrinkle), from "com-" (together) + "ruga" (wrinkle).
