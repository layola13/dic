masoner:/ˈmeɪsənər/| n.|石匠，泥瓦匠|A person skilled in building with stone or brick.|The masoner carefully placed each brick, creating a sturdy wall. 石匠小心翼翼地放置每一块砖，砌成了一堵坚固的墙。|近义词：bricklayer, stonemason|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑 amateur, novice)|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *masçon, from Frankish *makjo, from Latin macio (“hammer”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *mag- (“to knead, fit”).
