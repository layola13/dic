clickjacking:/ˈklɪkˌdʒækɪŋ/
n.
点击劫持
A malicious technique in which an attacker tricks a user into clicking on something different than what the user perceives they are clicking on, often by placing a transparent layer over a legitimate website.
A user believes they are clicking on a harmless button, but they are actually clicking on a hidden malicious link.  用户认为他们点击的是一个无害的按钮，但实际上他们点击的是一个隐藏的恶意链接。
Clickjacking can be used to steal login credentials or spread malware. 点击劫持可以用来窃取登录凭证或传播恶意软件。
近义词:UI redress attack, interface injection
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "secure browsing")
词性:noun
