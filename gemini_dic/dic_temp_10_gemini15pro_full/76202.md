fustiest:/ˈfʌstiɪst/| adj.|最陈腐的；最霉臭的；最古板的|Smelling stale, damp, and stuffy, as from having been closed up for a long time; old-fashioned or out-of-date.|The library was filled with the fustiest books imaginable.  这个图书馆里满是能想象得到的最陈腐的书籍。| The attic held a fustiest odor. 阁楼里散发着一股霉味。|Synonyms: stale, musty, stuffy, old-fashioned, outdated, antiquated|Antonyms: fresh, airy, modern, contemporary|adjective
