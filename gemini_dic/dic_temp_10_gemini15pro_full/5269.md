allomorph:/ˈæləmɔːrf/ (US) /ˈæləmɔːf/ (UK)| n. | 同质异形体 | One of two or more variants of a morpheme.  Allomorphs are different forms of the same morpheme, meaning they have the same meaning but different sounds or spellings. | The plural morpheme in English has several allomorphs, including /-s/ (cats), /-z/ (dogs), and /-ɪz/ (dishes).  英语中的复数词素有几个同质异形体，包括 /-s/ (cats)、/-z/ (dogs) 和 /-ɪz/ (dishes)。| synonyms: variant, variation | antonyms: invariant | noun
