graniferous:/ɡrəˈnɪfərəs/
adj.
含颗粒的，多颗粒的
Containing granules; granular.
The soil sample was graniferous, full of tiny particles. 土壤样本含有很多颗粒，充满了微小的粒子。
The bread had a pleasantly graniferous texture.  这种面包有着令人愉悦的颗粒状质地。
Synonyms: granular, grainy
Antonyms: smooth, fine
adjective
