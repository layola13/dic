ostitis:/ˌɒstiˈaɪtɪs/ /ˌɑːstiˈaɪtɪs/
noun
骨炎
Inflammation of bone tissue.
The doctor diagnosed him with ostitis after examining the X-ray. 医生检查了X光片后诊断他患有骨炎。
Synonyms: osteomyelitis, periostitis
Antonyms: bone health
