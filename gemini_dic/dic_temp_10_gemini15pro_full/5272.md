allonym:/ˈælənɪm/| n. |同名作品；公开姓名作品|A work published under the real name of the author, as opposed to a pseudonym. |Example 1:  Mark Twain is an allonym for Samuel Clemens. 马克·吐温是塞缪尔·克莱门斯的公开姓名。

Example 2:  Unlike her pen name, her allonym was known only to a small circle. 与她的笔名不同，她的真名只有少数人知道。|Synonym: real name, true name, actual name|Antonym: pseudonym, pen name, nom de plume|noun
