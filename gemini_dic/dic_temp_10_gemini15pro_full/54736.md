disparagingly:/dɪˈspærɪdʒɪŋli/
adverb
轻蔑地，贬低地
in a way that expresses a low opinion of someone or something.
He spoke disparagingly of his colleagues. 他轻蔑地谈论他的同事。
近义词:contemptuously, scornfully, disdainfully
反义词:respectfully, admiringly
词性:adverb

disparage:/dɪˈspærɪdʒ/
verb
贬低，轻视
to criticize someone or something in a way that shows you do not respect or value him, her, or it
The actor's work for charity has recently been disparaged in the press. 这位演员的慈善工作最近受到了媒体的贬低。
近义词:belittle, denigrate, deprecate
反义词:praise, commend
词性:verb


词源:
From Late Latin *disparāre  "to separate from its pair, make unequal," from dis- "apart" + par "equal"
