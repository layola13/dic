fakie:/ˈfeɪki/| adv. n.|反向的，倒滑的；倒滑动作|Riding fakie means riding backwards, usually on a skateboard, snowboard, or BMX bike.  It involves riding in your normal stance but moving in the opposite direction you normally would.|He landed the trick fakie. 他反向完成了这个技巧。|近义词：switch, backward|反义词：forward|词性: 副词，名词
