biodegradable:/ˌbaɪ.oʊ.dɪˈɡreɪ.də.bəl/
adjective
可生物降解的
Capable of being decomposed by bacteria or other living organisms.
This carton is made of biodegradable material.  这个纸盒是由可生物降解的材料制成的。
Packaging should be made from biodegradable materials. 包装应该由可生物降解材料制成。

近义词:compostable, decomposable
反义词:non-biodegradable, persistent

词性:adjective
