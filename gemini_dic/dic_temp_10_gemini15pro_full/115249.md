manipulatable:/məˈnɪpjələtəbl/
adjective
可操纵的；易受摆布的
able to be manipulated or controlled by someone.
The system is designed to be manipulatable by the user.  这个系统设计成可由用户操纵的。
The software is highly manipulatable, allowing for a wide range of customizations. 这个软件具有很高的可操作性，允许进行各种定制。
近义词: controllable, manageable, exploitable
反义词: unmanageable, intractable, resistant
词性: adjective

manipulate: /məˈnɪpjuleɪt/
verb
操纵，控制；(熟练地)操作，使用
1.(often disapproving) to control something or someone to your advantage, often unfairly or dishonestly
2.to control something using your hands
The puppet was manipulated by strings. 木偶是用绳子操纵的。
He manipulated the data to make it look like the company was more profitable than it actually was. 他操纵数据，使公司看起来比实际盈利更多。
近义词: control, influence, exploit
反义词: leave alone, neglect
