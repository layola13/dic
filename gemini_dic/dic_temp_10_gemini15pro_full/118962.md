meterage:/ˈmiːtərɪdʒ/
n.
计量；测量；尺寸
the measurement of something in meters
例句：The meterage of the land was disputed. 这块土地的测量结果存在争议。

例句：Accurate meterage is essential for construction projects.  精确的测量对于建筑项目至关重要。

近义词：measurement, mensuration
反义词：(No direct antonym, could consider "estimation" as a loose opposite)
词性：noun
