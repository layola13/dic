egocentric:/ˌiːɡoʊˈsɛntrɪk/ /ˌɛɡəʊˈsɛntrɪk/| adj.|自我中心的；以自我为中心的|Thinking only of oneself, without regard for the feelings or desires of others; self-centered.|例句：He's so egocentric, he never considers anyone else's feelings. 他太以自我为中心了，从不考虑别人的感受。

例句：The egocentric child demanded all the toys for himself.  这个以自我为中心的孩子要求把所有的玩具都给他自己。|近义词：self-centered, narcissistic, selfish|反义词：altruistic, selfless|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From ego + centric.  Ego, from Latin, meaning "I". Centric, meaning "relating to a center", ultimately from Greek kentron "center".
