nonhubristic:/ˌnɒnˈhjuːbrɪstɪk/
adjective
非傲慢的；谦逊的
Not characterized by hubris; lacking excessive pride or arrogance.
Her nonhubristic acceptance of the award was refreshing. 她谦逊地接受了奖项，令人耳目一新。
Synonyms: humble, modest, unassuming, unpretentious
Antonyms: arrogant, haughty, hubristic, proud
词性: 形容词
