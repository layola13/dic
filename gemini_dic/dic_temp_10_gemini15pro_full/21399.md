blousing:/ˈblaʊzɪŋ/ | n. | 宽松，打褶；（尤指军装衬衫）塞进裤子里的部分 | The blousing of his uniform was impeccable. 他的军装塞得一丝不苟。 |  The extra fabric allows for blousing. 多余的布料可以打褶。 | 近义词：gathering, fullness, draping | 反义词：tightness, flatness | 词性：名词

blousing:/ˈblaʊzɪŋ/ | v. | （使）宽松下垂；（尤指军装衬衫）塞进裤子里 | He bloused his boots over his trousers. 他把靴筒套在裤子外面。| She bloused her shirt at the waist. 她把衬衫塞进腰带里。 | 近义词：tuck, gather, drape | 反义词：tighten, flatten | 词性：动词

词源:  From blouse + -ing,  ultimately from Old French *bouse, of uncertain origin.
