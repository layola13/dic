nodding-off:/ˈnɒdɪŋ ɒf/ (UK) /ˈnɑːdɪŋ ɔːf/ (US)| n./v. (present participle)|打瞌睡，点头睡着|The act of falling asleep briefly, often unintentionally, characterized by a drooping head.|例句：I caught myself nodding-off during the meeting. 我发现自己在会议期间打瞌睡了。

例句：He kept nodding-off in front of the television. 他一直在电视机前打瞌睡。

近义词：dozing off, drifting off, falling asleep,  drowsing|反义词:waking up, staying awake|词性:verb (present participle)
