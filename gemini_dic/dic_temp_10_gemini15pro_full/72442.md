flukan:/ˈfluːkæn/| n.|氟康唑 (Fluconazole)|Antifungal medication used to treat a variety of fungal infections.|例句：The doctor prescribed flukan for her yeast infection. 医生给她开了氟康唑治疗酵母菌感染。

例句：Flukan is effective against many types of fungi. 氟康唑对多种真菌有效。|近义词：Diflucan (brand name), antifungals|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


词源:  "Flukan" is a generic name for the drug fluconazole.  It's a triazole antifungal, meaning its chemical structure contains a triazole ring. The name likely derives from a combination of "fluoro" (referring to the fluorine atom in its structure) and a shortened form of "triazole" or a similar chemical term.
