brazier:/ˈbreɪʒər/ (US), /ˈbreɪziər/ (UK)| n.|火盆，炭盆；烤肉架|A metal container that holds burning coals, used to provide heat or to cook food.|The blacksmith used a brazier to heat the metal. 铁匠用火盆加热金属。

The aroma of grilled meat wafted from the brazier. 烤肉的香味从烤肉架飘来。|Synonyms: grill, barbecue, hibachi, fire pit|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider:  refrigerator, freezer)|noun
