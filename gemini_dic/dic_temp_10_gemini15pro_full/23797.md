brayer:/ˈbreɪər/ (US), /ˈbreɪə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|研磨机，碾磨机；（印刷）墨辊；磨碎者；|A tool used for grinding or spreading ink, paint, etc.  A person or thing that brays.|The artist used a brayer to spread the ink evenly on the block. 艺术家用墨辊将墨水均匀地涂在木板上。 He brayed the spices in a mortar and pestle. 他用研钵和研杵研磨香料。|近义词：roller, grinder, crusher|反义词: N/A|词性:名词, 动词
