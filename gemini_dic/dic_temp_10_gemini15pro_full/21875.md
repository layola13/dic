body-popping:/ˈbɒdiˌpɒpɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbɒdiˌpɒpɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|机械舞，震感舞|A street dance characterized by abrupt, jerky movements of the body, as if the dancer were being hit by electric shocks.|He's incredibly talented at body-popping. 他非常擅长跳机械舞。|近义词：popping, electric boogaloo|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "smooth dancing")|词性:noun
