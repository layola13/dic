notopodium:/ˌnɒtəˈpəʊdiəm/| n. |疣足的背枝|The dorsal lobe or branch of a parapodium in polychaete worms. 多毛纲环节动物疣足的背枝。|The notopodium bears bristles called notochaetae. 疣足的背枝上长着名为背刚毛的鬃毛。|The polychaete uses its notopodia for respiration and locomotion.  多毛类动物用它的背足进行呼吸和运动。|近义词: dorsal parapodium|反义词: neuropodium|词性: noun
