overgoverning:/ˌoʊvərˈɡʌvərnɪŋ/
adjective
过度管理的；管制过多的
governing too much; excessively controlling
The overgoverning regulations stifle innovation and economic growth. 过度管理的法规扼杀了创新和经济增长。
The company's overgoverning policies led to employee dissatisfaction. 该公司过度管理的政策导致员工不满。
近义词: overregulating, micromanaging
反义词:  deregulated, laissez-faire
词性: adjective
