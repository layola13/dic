linguals:/ˈlɪŋɡwəlz/
noun
舌音; 舌面音; 舌尖音; 舌根音
sounds articulated with the tongue
The "t" and "d" sounds are examples of linguals. / "t" 和 "d" 音是舌面音的例子。
synonyms: tongue sounds, apical sounds, dorsal sounds
antonyms: N/A
