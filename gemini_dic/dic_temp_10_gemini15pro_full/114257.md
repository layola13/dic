maimedness:/ˈmeɪmdnəs/| n. |残废，伤残|The state of being maimed; the condition of having a limb or other body part seriously injured or disabled.|His maimedness prevented him from pursuing a career in professional sports. 他的残疾使他无法从事职业体育事业。|近义词：disability, impairment, mutilation, crippling|反义词：wholeness, soundness, health|词性: noun

maim:/meɪm/| vt. |使残废，使伤残|To wound or injure someone so severely that a part of their body is permanently damaged.|The accident maimed him for life.  这起事故使他终身残疾。|近义词：cripple, disable, mutilate, injure|反义词：heal, cure, mend|词性: verb
