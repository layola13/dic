hornbeam:/ˈhɔːrnbiːm/| n. |角树，鹅耳枥|A tree of the birch family, with smooth grey bark, toothed leaves and hop-like fruit.  The wood is very hard and is used for making tools and other implements.|例句：The hornbeam is a common tree in European forests. 角树是欧洲森林中常见的树木。

例句：The carpenter used hornbeam to make the mallet. 木匠用角树木制作木槌。|近义词：ironwood|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑一些质地较软的树木，例如pine 松树) |词性:noun
