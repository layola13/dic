inebriant:/ɪnˈiːbriənt/| n. adj.| 醉人的东西；令人陶醉的 | Something that causes intoxication; intoxicating. | The sweet scent of the flowers was an inebriant. 花的甜香令人陶醉。| 近义词：intoxicant, stimulant, narcotic | 反义词：sobering, depressant | 词性: 名词, 形容词
Etymology:  From Late Latin *inebriant-, present participle of inebriare "to intoxicate," from Latin in- "in" + ebriare "to make drunk," from ebrius "drunk."
