brominate:/ˈbroʊmɪneɪt/| v. |溴化|To treat or combine with bromine.  To introduce bromine into a compound.|例句：The researchers brominated the alkene to form a dibromoalkane. 研究人员将烯烃溴化形成二溴烷烃。

例句：Unsaturated fats can be brominated to increase their stability. 不饱和脂肪可以被溴化以增加其稳定性。|近义词：halogenate|反义词: debrominate|词性:动词
