fluctuate:/ˈflʌktʃueɪt/ (US), /ˈflʌktʃʊeɪt/ (UK)| v. |波动，起伏，变动|To change continually; to rise and fall.|The price of gold has been fluctuating wildly this year. 今年黄金价格波动剧烈。|Synonyms: vary, oscillate, waver, change, shift|Antonyms: stabilize, remain constant, stay fixed|verb

fluctuation:/ˌflʌktʃuˈeɪʃn/ (US), /ˌflʌktʃʊˈeɪʃn/ (UK)| n. |波动，起伏，变动|A continual change; a rise and fall.|There have been significant fluctuations in the stock market recently. 最近股市出现了显著的波动。|Synonyms: variation, oscillation, wavering, change, shift|Antonyms: stability, constancy|noun
