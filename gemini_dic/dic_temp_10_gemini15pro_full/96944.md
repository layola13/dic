inextricableness:/ˌɪnɪkˈstrɪkəbəlnəs/
n.
不可分割性；错综复杂
the state of being inextricable; inability to be disentangled or separated.
The inextricableness of the vines made it impossible to pass through. 这些藤蔓错综复杂，无法穿过。
近义词: inseparability, indivisibility, entanglement, complexity
反义词: separability, simplicity, clarity
词性: 名词

inextricableness:/ˌɪnɪkˈstrɪkəbəlnəs/
n.
解不开；逃不掉
the state of being impossible to escape from.
The inextricableness of the situation left them feeling hopeless.  这种无法摆脱的困境让他们感到绝望。
近义词: inescapability,  hopelessness,  dilemma
反义词: escapability, freedom,  solution
词性: 名词


词源:  From inextricable + -ness.  Inextricable comes from Late Latin inextricabilis, from in- 'not' + extricabilis (from extricare 'disentangle').
