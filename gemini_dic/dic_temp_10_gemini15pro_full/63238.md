enterorrhaphy: /ˌɛntəˈrɔːrəfi/ | n. |肠缝合术|The surgical suturing of a wound or tear in the intestines.|例句：The surgeon performed an enterorrhaphy after removing the damaged section of bowel.  外科医生在切除受损的肠段后进行了肠缝合术。

例句：Enterorrhaphy is a common procedure following intestinal surgery.  肠缝合术是肠道手术后的常见步骤。|近义词：intestinal repair, bowel repair|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology:  From entero- (intestine) + -rrhaphy (suturing).  Entero- comes from the Greek "enteron" meaning intestine. -rrhaphy comes from the Greek "rhaphe" meaning suture.
