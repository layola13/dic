overburns:/ˌoʊvərˈbɜːrnz/
verb
过度燃烧；烧过头
To burn something excessively, beyond what is necessary or desirable.
The toast overburns easily in this toaster.  这台烤面包机很容易把面包烤焦。
The chef overburned the steak.  厨师把牛排烤糊了。
Synonyms: scorch, char, sear
Antonyms: undercook
词性: 动词
