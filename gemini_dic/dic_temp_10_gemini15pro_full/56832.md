dormy:/ˈdɔːrmi/| adj.|领先一洞（高尔夫球术语）|Being in a position in match play where one is as many holes up as there are holes left to play, thus guaranteeing victory.|例句：She went dormy two on the 16th hole. 她在第16洞领先两洞，只差两洞就赢了。|近义词: winning, advantageous|反义词: losing, behind|词性:形容词

词源:  Shortened from "dormant," referring to the idea that the opponent's chances of winning are effectively dormant or inactive.
