disembarkations:/ˌdɪsɪmbɑːrˈkeɪʃənz/
noun
下船; 登陆; 卸客
The process of leaving a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle.  The act of passengers and crew getting off a ship or aircraft.
The disembarkations were delayed due to bad weather. 由于天气恶劣，下船被推迟了。
synonyms: landings, departures, exits
antonyms: embarkations, boardings
n.
