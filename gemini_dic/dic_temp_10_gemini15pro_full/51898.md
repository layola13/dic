dharna:/ˈdɑːrnə/| n. |静坐示威，静坐抗议|A form of non-violent protest in which a group of people occupy a place and refuse to leave until their demands are met. It originated in India.|例句：The farmers organized a dharna to protest against the new agricultural laws. 农民们组织静坐示威抗议新的农业法。

例句：The workers staged a dharna outside the factory gates. 工人们在工厂门口举行静坐示威。|近义词：sit-in, protest, demonstration, picket|反义词: acquiescence, compliance|词性:noun
