cataphracted:/kəˈtæfræktɪd/
adjective
披甲的，重甲的
Covered with protective armor, especially of overlapping metal plates.  Relating to or characteristic of cataphracts (heavily armored cavalry).
The cataphracted knight charged into battle. 披甲骑士冲入战场。
Synonyms: armored, mail-clad, shielded, protected
Antonyms: unarmored, unprotected, vulnerable
词性: adjective
