endodermic:/ˌɛndəˈdɜːrmɪk/ | adj. | 内胚层的，内皮的 | Relating to or derived from the endoderm (the innermost layer of cells or tissue of an embryo in early development). |  The endodermic layer gives rise to the lining of the digestive tract. 内胚层形成消化道的内壁。| The doctor explained the endodermic origin of the tumor. 医生解释了肿瘤的内胚层起源。 |  inner, internal, interior | outer, external, ectodermic | adjective

endoderm:/ˈɛndəˌdɜːrm/ | n. | 内胚层 | The innermost of the three primary germ layers of an embryo, developing into the lining of the digestive tract and its derivative organs. | The endoderm forms the lining of the gut. 内胚层形成肠道内壁。 | The development of the endoderm is crucial for the formation of vital organs. 内胚层的发育对重要器官的形成至关重要。|  hypoblast | ectoderm, mesoderm | noun
