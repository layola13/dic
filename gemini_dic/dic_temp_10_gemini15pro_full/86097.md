heart-searching:/ˈhɑːrtˌsɜːrtʃɪŋ/ | n. | 深思；反省；扪心自问 |  The process of examining one's own thoughts and feelings, especially with regard to motives and morality. | After a period of intense heart-searching, she decided to quit her job.  经过一番深刻的反省之后，她决定辞职。 | soul-searching, introspection, self-examination | superficiality, thoughtlessness | noun

heart-searching:/ˈhɑːrtˌsɜːrtʃɪŋ/ | adj. |  令人深思的；发人深省的 | Causing or involving deep and often painful thought about one's own thoughts and feelings. | The film posed some heart-searching questions about the nature of love and loss. 这部电影提出了一些关于爱与失去的本质，令人深思的问题。 | thought-provoking, introspective, reflective | superficial, shallow | adjective
