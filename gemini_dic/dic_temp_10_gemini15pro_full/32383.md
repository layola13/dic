chiliast:/ˈkɪliæst/| n.|千禧年主义者；相信千年至福说的人|A person who believes in the doctrine of chiliasm, especially in a form of millenarianism.|例句：The chiliast predicted the world would end in the year 2000.  千禧年主义者预言世界将在2000年终结。

例句：Many chiliasts throughout history have anticipated a golden age of peace and prosperity. 历史上许多千禧年主义者都预料到一个和平与繁荣的黄金时代。|近义词：millenarian, millennialist|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly secularist or non-believer)|词性:noun
