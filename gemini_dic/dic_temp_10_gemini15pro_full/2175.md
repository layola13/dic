addice: /ˈædɪs/| n.|手斧，冰斧|A tool similar to an axe, but with a curved blade, used for shaping wood or ice.|He used an addice to shape the wooden beam. 他用一把手斧来 shaping 木梁。

The climber used an addice to secure his footing on the icy slope. 登山者用冰斧在结冰的斜坡上固定自己的位置。|近义词：adze, axe, hatchet|反义词: None|词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ădēse, from Proto-Germanic *adīsō, related to Old High German dehsala. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ed- (“to eat; sharp”).

USAGE->
The addice is an essential tool for woodcarvers.
手斧是木雕工匠的必备工具。

The museum displayed several ancient addices used by early settlers.
博物馆展出了早期定居者使用的几把古代手斧。
