palaeolithic:/ˌpeɪlɪəˈlɪθɪk/ | adj. n. | 旧石器时代的；旧石器时代 | Relating to or denoting the early phase of the Stone Age, lasting about 2.5 million years, when primitive stone implements were used. |  The palaeolithic era saw the development of the first tools. 旧石器时代见证了第一批工具的发展。|  Paleolithic man hunted and gathered for survival.  旧石器时代的人类靠狩猎和采集为生。 |  Synonyms: Stone Age, prehistoric | Antonyms: modern, contemporary | adjective, noun
