descanter:/dɪˈkæntər/ | n. | 倾析器；醒酒器 | A container, typically of glass, into which wine is decanted. | He carefully decanted the red wine into the crystal decanter. 他小心翼翼地将红酒倒入水晶醒酒器中。|  synonyms: carafe, bottle, flagon | antonyms: N/A | noun


descanter:/dɪˈkæntər/ | v. | 倾析；醒酒  | To pour (liquid, especially wine) from one container into another, typically in order to separate out sediment. |  She decanted the wine before serving it.  她在上酒前先将酒醒了一下。| synonyms: pour, transfer, siphon | antonyms: N/A | verb
