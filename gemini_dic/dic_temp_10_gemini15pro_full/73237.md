forcut: /ˈfɔːrkʌt/ | n. | 分叉路口；岔路；分岔点 | A place where a road or path divides into two or more branches. |  The hikers reached a forcut in the trail and had to decide which way to go.  徒步旅行者到达了小路上一个岔路口，不得不决定走哪条路。| synonyms: fork, junction, crossroads, intersection | antonyms: straight path, single track | noun

forcut: /ˈfɔːrkʌt/ | v. | 分叉；岔开 | To divide into two or more branches. | The river forcuted several miles downstream.  河流在下游几英里处分叉了。| synonyms: branch, diverge, split | antonyms: converge, join, merge | verb
