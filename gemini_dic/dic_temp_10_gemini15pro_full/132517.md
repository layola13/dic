nondetermination:/ˌnɒndɪˌtɜːmɪˈneɪʃn/ |n.| 非决定性；不确定性 |The principle of nondetermination asserts that mental events are not causally determined by physical events. 非决定性原则断言，心理事件并非由物理事件因果决定。| Nondetermination makes it difficult to predict the future.  不确定性使得预测未来变得困难。 |Near Synonyms: indeterminacy, uncertainty, unpredictability |Antonyms: determinism, certainty, predictability|noun
