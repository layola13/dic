astrophotometer:/ˌæstroʊfoʊˈtɑːmɪtər/ | n. | 天体光度计 | An instrument used to measure the brightness of celestial objects, such as stars or galaxies. | Example 1: The astronomer used an astrophotometer to measure the star's magnitude. 天文学家用天体光度计测量了这颗恒星的星等。

Example 2: The new astrophotometer allowed for more precise measurements of the faintest objects in the night sky. 新的天体光度计可以更精确地测量夜空中最微弱的天体。 | Synonym: photometer | Antonym: N/A | noun
