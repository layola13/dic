heteropathy:/ˌhetərˈɒpəθi/
n.
异疗法；异种疗法
A system of medical treatment using remedies different from those usually employed in the treatment of a particular disease, especially the treatment of one disease by exciting another.
Heteropathy, unlike homeopathy, uses dissimilar remedies to treat ailments. 异疗法不像顺势疗法，它使用不同的疗法来治疗疾病。

Heteropathy is sometimes used in alternative medicine. 异疗法有时用于替代医学。

近义词: allopathy, conventional medicine
反义词: homeopathy
词性: noun
