ihram:/ɪˈhrɑːm/ | n. | 伊赫拉姆，戒衣 | The state of ritual purity that Muslims must enter before performing the Hajj or Umrah pilgrimage.  This involves wearing specific clothing and abstaining from certain actions. |  Pilgrims must enter ihram before arriving in Mecca. 朝觐者必须在抵达麦加之前进入戒衣状态。

He purchased his ihram garments in Medina. 他在麦地那购买了戒衣。 | 近义词: None (Highly specific term) | 反义词: None (Highly specific term) | 词性: noun
