aggregator:/ˈæɡrɪɡeɪtər/| n.|聚合器；集成器；汇集者|A website or program that collects related items of content and displays them or links to them.|Example: Google News is a news aggregator. 谷歌新闻是一个新闻聚合器。|Example: The website serves as an aggregator for various online retailers.  这个网站充当了各种在线零售商的聚合器。|Synonyms: collector, compiler, assembler, consolidator|Antonyms: distributor, disseminator|noun
