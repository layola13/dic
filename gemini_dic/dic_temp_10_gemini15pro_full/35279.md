clutchiest:/ˈklʌtʃiɪst/| adj.|最关键的，最紧要的；最可靠的|Superlative form of "clutch," meaning performing well in crucial situations.  It describes something or someone that is most reliable or performs best under pressure.|Example: He made the clutchiest free throws of his career, winning the game for his team. 他投进了职业生涯中最关键的罚球，为他的球队赢得了比赛。|Example:  She's the clutchiest player on the team; we always rely on her in tight situations. 她是队里最可靠的球员；在紧张的情况下，我们总是依靠她。|近义词：reliable, dependable, crucial, decisive|反义词: unreliable, undependable, insignificant|词性:形容词

词源: From "clutch" (n.), from Old English *clyccan "to bend, hook," related to Middle Low German kluken "to clutch, seize." The adjectival form developed later, with "-y" and then the superlative "-est."
