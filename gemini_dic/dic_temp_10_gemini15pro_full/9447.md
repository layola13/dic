antireductionism:/ˌænti rɪˈdʌkʃənɪzəm/
|n.|反还原论|The philosophy that opposes reductionism, arguing that complex systems cannot be fully explained by their simpler constituent parts.|His antireductionism led him to believe that consciousness cannot be explained solely by brain activity.|近义词：holism, organicism|反义词：reductionism|词性:名词

antireductionisms:/ˌænti rɪˈdʌkʃənɪzəmz/
|n.|反还原论（复数）|Plural form of antireductionism, referring to multiple instances or types of antireductionist thought.|The various antireductionisms in the field of psychology offer alternative perspectives on human behavior.|近义词：holisms, organicisms|反义词：reductionisms|词性:名词
