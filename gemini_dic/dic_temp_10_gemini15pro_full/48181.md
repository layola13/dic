decomposed:/ˌdiːkəmˈpoʊzd/ /ˌdiːkəmˈpəʊzd/| adj.|分解的，腐烂的，腐败的|Broken down into simpler chemical parts by natural processes; rotted. |例句：The decomposed body of the animal was found in the woods. 动物腐烂的尸体在树林里被发现了。

例句：The leaves decomposed quickly in the compost bin. 叶子在堆肥箱里很快就腐烂了。|近义词：decayed, rotted, putrefied, broken down|反义词：intact, whole, preserved|词性：形容词
