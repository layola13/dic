apagogical:/ˌæpəˈɡɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 反证法的，间接论证的 | Relating to or involving apagogy; demonstrating the truth of a proposition by proving the absurdity of the contradictory proposition. |  The lawyer used an apagogical argument to demonstrate his client's innocence. 律师用反证法证明了他的委托人的清白。| The professor presented an apagogical proof of the theorem. 教授用反证法证明了这个定理。 | 近义词: indirect, reductio ad absurdum | 反义词: direct | 词性: adjective
