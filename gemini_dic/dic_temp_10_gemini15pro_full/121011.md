miscoinage:/ˌmɪsˈkɔɪnɪdʒ/
n.
伪造的货币;杜撰的词语
A word or phrase that has been incorrectly or inappropriately created.
The word "irregardless" is considered a miscoinage.
“irregardless”这个词被认为是一个杜撰词。
miscreation, neologism, solecism
correct usage, proper word
noun
