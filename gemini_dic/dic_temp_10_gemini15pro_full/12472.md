aspartame:/ˈæspərteɪm; əˈspɑːrteɪm/
n.
阿斯巴甜; 阿斯巴坦
A very sweet, low-calorie artificial sweetener that is used in foods and drinks instead of sugar.
Example: Many diet sodas are sweetened with aspartame. 许多无糖汽水是用阿斯巴甜增甜的。
Example:  He avoids food and drinks containing aspartame. 他不吃含阿斯巴甜的食物和饮料。
Synonym:  artificial sweetener, NutraSweet, Equal
Antonym: sugar, sucrose, natural sweetener
noun
