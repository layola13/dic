infertility:/ˌɪn.fɚˈtɪl.ə.ti/ | n. |不孕，不育 |The inability to conceive children or young. |例句：The couple struggled with infertility for years. 这对夫妇多年来一直在与不孕症作斗争。

例句：Infertility can be caused by a variety of factors. 不孕症可能是由多种因素引起的。|近义词：sterility, barrenness |反义词：fertility, fecundity |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + fertile + -ity (noun suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *infertilitatem, from infertilis "unfruitful, barren," from in- "not" + fertilis "fertile."
