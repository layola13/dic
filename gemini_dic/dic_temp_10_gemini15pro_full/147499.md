overoxidizing:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːksɪdaɪzɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度氧化
Oxidizing something to an excessive degree.
The overoxidizing of the compound resulted in an explosion. 该化合物的过度氧化导致了爆炸。
Overoxidizing the hair can make it brittle and dry.  过度氧化头发会使头发变得脆弱干燥。
近义词: overburning
反义词: underoxidizing
词性: verb (present participle)
