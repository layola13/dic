dissembles:/dɪˈsɛmblz/
verb
掩饰，假装；隐藏（真实感情或意图）
conceals or disguises one's true feelings or intentions.
He dissembled his fear with a smile. 他用微笑掩饰他的恐惧。
synonyms: disguise, conceal, mask, feign, pretend, affect, simulate, fake
antonyms: reveal, expose, disclose, divulge
词性：动词
