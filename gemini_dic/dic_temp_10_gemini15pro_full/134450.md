nonissues:/ˈnɑːnˌɪʃuːz/ (US), /ˈnɒnˌɪʃuːz/ (UK)| n. | 无关紧要的问题；非问题 | Matters of little or no importance; things that are not real problems.  They might be perceived as problems by some, but are generally considered trivial or irrelevant. |  The dress code was a nonissue; everyone wore jeans. 着装规范根本不是问题；每个人都穿着牛仔裤。| Synonyms: trivialities, insignificant matters, non-problems | Antonyms:  issues, problems, important matters | Noun
