nonsurrealistically:/ˌnɒnsərɪəˈlɪstɪkli/ (US) /ˌnɑːnsərɪəˈlɪstɪkli/
nonsurrealistically| adv. |非超现实主义地|In a way that is not surreal or dreamlike; realistically.  Not in a bizarre, illogical, or hallucinatory manner, as seen in surrealist art.|The author described the events nonsurrealistically, focusing on the mundane details.|近义词: realistically, practically, pragmatically|反义词: surrealistically, fantastically, dreamily|词性:副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin *non, from Old Latin noenum "not one," from ne "not" + oinos "one."
surrealistic (adjective): From French surréaliste, from sur- "super-" + réalisme "realism."
-ally (suffix):  Forms adverbs from adjectives, meaning "in a ... manner," from Old English -lice, from Germanic *-liko-, cognate with German -lich.
