nip-in-the-bud:/nɪp ɪn ðə bʌd/
|idiomatic expression|
|防患于未然；扼杀在萌芽状态|
|To stop something at an early stage, before it can develop into something larger or more serious.|
|We need to nip this problem in the bud before it gets out of hand. 我们需要把这个问题扼杀在萌芽状态，以免它失控。|

|The government is trying to nip the rebellion in the bud. 政府正试图将叛乱扼杀在摇篮里。|

|近义词：stop, prevent, thwart, suppress, quell|
|反义词：encourage, promote, foster, cultivate|
|phrase|
