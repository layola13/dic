gordy:/ˈɡɔːrdi/| adj.|肥胖的，矮胖的；丰盛的|A gordy person is rather fat and heavy, often in a pleasant-looking way.  A gordy meal is large and satisfying. |例句：He's gotten a bit gordy since he stopped playing football.  自从他不踢足球后，他变得有点胖了。

例句：We had a right gordy breakfast before setting off on our hike. 我们出发远足前吃了一顿非常丰盛的早餐。|近义词：chubby, plump, stout, hearty (for meal)|反义词：thin, slim, slender, meager (for meal)|词性:形容词
