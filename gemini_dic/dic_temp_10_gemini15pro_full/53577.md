disbodied:/dɪsˈbɒdid/ /dɪsˈbɑːdid/| adj.|无实体的，脱离肉体的，灵魂出窍的|Separated from or existing without the body.  Relating to something that lacks physical form.|Her disbodied spirit floated above the scene. 她的灵魂飘荡在现场上方。|近义词：incorporeal, disembodied, spiritual, ethereal|反义词：corporeal, embodied, physical|词性:形容词

词源: From dis- + embodied, from embody + -ed.  "Dis-" prefix meaning "apart," "asunder," "away," expressing negation, reversal, or deprivation.  "Embody" means to give a body to; incarnate.
