farsightedness:/ˌfɑːrˈsaɪtɪdnəs/ | n. |远视 |The condition of being able to see distant objects more clearly than near ones.  Being able to see things that are far away but not things that are close. | Example 1:  His farsightedness made it difficult for him to read. 他的远视让他阅读困难。

Example 2:  She wears glasses to correct her farsightedness. 她戴眼镜来矫正她的远视。 | 近义词: hyperopia, longsightedness | 反义词: nearsightedness, myopia | 词性: noun

farsighted:/ˌfɑːrˈsaɪtɪd/ | adj. |有远见的 |Able to see distant objects clearly; having foresight. | Example 1:  A farsighted leader anticipates future challenges. 一位有远见的领导者会预见到未来的挑战。

Example 2:  It was a farsighted decision to invest in renewable energy. 投资可再生能源是一个有远见的决定。 | 近义词: prescient, visionary | 反义词: shortsighted, myopic | 词性: adjective
