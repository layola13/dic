ovism:/ˈoʊvɪzəm/| n.|卵细胞主义，卵子主义|The theory that the female reproductive cell, or ovum, contains within itself the entire organism and that the male semen merely stimulates its development.  The belief that the female ovum contains the entire future organism and the male sperm only activates its growth.|  Her research challenged the then-dominant theory of ovism. 她的研究挑战了当时占主导地位的卵细胞主义理论。|Synonym:  preformationism (a more general term)|Antonym:  spermism, animalculism|Noun
