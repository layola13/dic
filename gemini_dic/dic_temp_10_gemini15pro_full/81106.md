grammarianism: /ˌɡræˈmɛəriənɪzəm/ | n. | 语法学究主义；语法癖；拘泥语法 | Excessive concern with minor details of grammar or style. |  He was known for his grammarianism, correcting everyone's speech. 他以语法癖著称，总是纠正每个人的讲话。

He dismissed her concerns as mere grammarianism. 他认为她所担心的不过是语法学究主义。| 近义词：pedantry, purism | 反义词:  laxity, informality | 词性: noun
