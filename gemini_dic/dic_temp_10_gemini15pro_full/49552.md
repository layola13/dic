demean:/dɪˈmiːn/| vt.|贬低，降低…的身份；使卑屈|to cause a lowering of self-esteem; to lower in dignity or standing|Don't demean yourself by lying. 不要用谎言贬低自己。|synonyms: degrade, humble, abase|antonyms: exalt, elevate, ennoble|verb

demean:/dɪˈmiːn/| vt.|举止，表现|to behave or conduct oneself (usually followed by *oneself*)|He demeaned himself like a true gentleman. 他表现得像个真正的绅士。|synonyms: behave, conduct, comport|antonyms: misbehave|verb
