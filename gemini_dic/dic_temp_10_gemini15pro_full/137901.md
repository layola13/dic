nonviviparity:/ˌnɒnˌvɪvɪˈpærɪti/
n.
非胎生
The absence of viviparity; reproduction by laying eggs that develop and hatch outside the mother's body.
Most reptiles exhibit nonviviparity. 大多数爬行动物都表现出非胎生。
oviparity, ovoviviparity
