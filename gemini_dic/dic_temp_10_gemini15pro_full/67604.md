extravagate:/ɪkˈstrævəɡeɪt/| v. |溢出；漫出；扩散|To go beyond proper limits; to wander beyond the usual confines.|The river extravagated its banks after the heavy rain. 河水在大雨过后漫过了河岸。|近义词：overflow, spill, spread, diffuse|反义词：contain, restrict|词性:动词
词源: From Latin extravāgātus, past participle of extravāgārī ("to wander outside or beyond"), from extra ("outside") + vāgārī ("to wander, roam, stray").
