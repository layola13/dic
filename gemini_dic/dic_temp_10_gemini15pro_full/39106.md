confrontative:/kənˌfrʌnˈteɪtɪv/ | adj. | 对抗性的，挑衅的 | Inclined to confront or challenge; tending to deal with situations in an aggressive or hostile way. |  He had a confrontative attitude, always ready for an argument. 他有一种对抗性的态度，总是准备好争论。|  She adopted a confrontative stance during the negotiation, which ultimately proved counterproductive.  她在谈判中采取了对抗性的立场，最终适得其反。 |  aggressive, antagonistic, belligerent, combative, hostile, provocative |  cooperative, conciliatory, passive, diplomatic, accommodating | adjective


confront:/kənˈfrʌnt/ | vt. | 面对，对抗 |  Meet (someone) face to face with hostile or argumentative intent. |  He confronted his bully after school. 放学后，他与欺负他的人对峙。|  She was confronted by a man demanding money. 她被一个索要钱财的男人拦住对峙。 | face, encounter, tackle, address, challenge | avoid, evade, dodge, shirk, ignore | verb
