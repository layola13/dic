nonconnivance:/ˌnɑːnkəˈnaɪvəns/
n.
不串通；不默许；不知情
lack of collusion or involvement, especially in wrongdoing.
The lawyer argued his client's nonconnivance in the crime.  律师辩称他的客户没有参与犯罪。
The insurance company required proof of nonconnivance in the fire. 保险公司要求提供没有纵火的证据。
近义词: noncomplicity, noninvolvement, innocence
反义词: connivance, complicity, collusion
词性: 名词
