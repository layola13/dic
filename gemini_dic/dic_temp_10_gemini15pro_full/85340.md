harpsichordists:/ˈhɑːrpsɪˌkɔːrdɪsts/ | n. | 大键琴演奏家 | A person who plays the harpsichord. |  Johann Sebastian Bach wrote many pieces for harpsichordists. 约翰·塞巴斯蒂安·巴赫为大键琴演奏家创作了许多作品。|  keyboardists, pianists, musicians | N/A | noun

Etymology:  From harpsichord + -ist.  "Harpsichord" comes from the Italian "arpicordo," itself derived from "arpa" (harp) and "corda" (string).
