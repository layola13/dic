contemplatingly:/ˈkɒntɛmpleɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 沉思地，若有所思地 | in a contemplating manner; thoughtfully | She gazed contemplatingly out the window. 她若有所思地凝视着窗外。| He stroked his beard contemplatingly as he listened to the proposal. 他一边听着提议，一边若有所思地捋着胡须。 | 近义词: thoughtfully, pensively, meditatively | 反义词: rashly, impulsively | 词性: 副词

词源: From contemplate + -ing + -ly.  "Contemplate" comes from the Latin contemplatus, the past participle of contemplari "to gaze attentively, observe, contemplate," originally "to mark out a space, survey," from con- "together" (see con-) + templum "temple, consecrated place" (see temple (n.)).
