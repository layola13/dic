nosh:/nɒʃ/ (British), /nɑːʃ/ (American)| n. vi.|零食，小吃；吃零食|A small meal or snack.  It can also be used as a verb meaning "to eat a snack or small meal".|
例句：We had a quick nosh before the movie.  我们电影前吃了一点零食。

例句：I'm going to nosh on some popcorn. 我要吃点爆米花。


近义词：snack, bite, nibble|反义词:feast, banquet|词性:noun, verb

nosh:/nɒʃ/ (British), /nɑːʃ/ (American)| vi.|吃零食|To eat a snack or small meal.|
