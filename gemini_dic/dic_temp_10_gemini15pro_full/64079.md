epizootic:/ˌɛpɪzuːˈɒtɪk/| adj. n.|动物流行性的，动物流行病|An epizootic is a disease event in a nonhuman animal population analogous to an epidemic in humans.|例句：The epizootic outbreak decimated the local deer population. 动物流行病的爆发导致当地鹿群大量死亡。

例句：The study of epizootics helps us understand disease transmission in animal populations. 对动物流行病的研究有助于我们了解动物种群中的疾病传播。|近义词：epidemic (in animals), enzootic (if endemic), pandemic (if widespread)|反义词: non-epidemic, sporadic|词性: adjective, noun
