oik:/ɔɪk/ | n. | 家伙，老兄；（尤指）自己人；支持者；信徒 | A fellow, especially a member of one's own group; a supporter or adherent. | He's one of our oiks. 他是我们自己人。| 近义词: chap, fellow, mate, supporter, follower | 反义词: opponent, enemy | 词性: 名词

词源:  From British dialect, perhaps a variant of *ike* "familiar person," related to Dutch *eik* "oak tree" (a traditional meeting place).
