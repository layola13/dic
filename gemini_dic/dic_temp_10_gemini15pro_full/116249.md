mass-producer:/ˈmæs prəˈd(j)uːsər/ | n. | 大量生产者，批量生产商 | Someone or a company that produces large quantities of goods using machinery. |  Henry Ford was a pioneer mass-producer of automobiles.  亨利·福特是汽车批量生产的先驱。

Ford Motor Company became a mass-producer of cars. 福特汽车公司成为了汽车的大规模生产商。 | synonyms: manufacturer, maker, producer | antonyms: artisan, craftsman | noun
