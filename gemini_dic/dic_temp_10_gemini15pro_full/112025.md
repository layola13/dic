long-sightedly:/ˌlɒŋˈsaɪtɪdli/ /ˌlɔːŋˈsaɪtɪdli/
| adv. |远见卓识地；目光长远地|Showing foresight and planning for the future.|He invested his money long-sightedly, ensuring a comfortable retirement. 他很有远见地投资了他的钱，确保了舒适的退休生活。|例句：She long-sightedly anticipated the changes in the market and adjusted her strategy accordingly. 她很有远见地预料到市场变化，并相应地调整了她的策略。|近义词：prudently, wisely, farsightedly|反义词：short-sightedly, myopically|词性:副词
