dispossessions:/ˌdɪspəˈzɛʃənz/
|n.| 剥夺，驱逐，没收 |The act of dispossessing someone of something, especially land or property.  The state of being dispossessed.|
例句：The dispossessions of farmers from their land caused widespread unrest. 农民被剥夺土地引起了广泛的社会动荡。

例句：The history of colonialism is filled with stories of dispossessions and exploitation.  殖民主义的历史充满了剥夺和剥削的故事。
|近义词：evictions, expropriations, seizures, confiscations|
|反义词：provisions, endowments, grants|
|词性：名词|
