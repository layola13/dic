back-away:/ˈbæk əˈweɪ/ | phrasal verb | 后退，退缩 | To move backwards, especially as a sign of fear or reluctance. | The dog growled and I backed away slowly. 狗咆哮着，我慢慢地后退。| synonyms: retreat, withdraw, recoil | antonyms: advance, approach | verb phrase

back-away:/ˈbæk əˈweɪ/ | phrasal verb | 放弃，退出 | To withdraw from a commitment, agreement, or difficult situation. |  He backed away from the deal at the last minute. 他在最后一刻退出了这笔交易。| synonyms: abandon, renounce, relinquish | antonyms: pursue, persist | verb phrase
