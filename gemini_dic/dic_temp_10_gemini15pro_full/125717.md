municipally:/mjuːˈnɪsɪpəli/
| adv. |市政地，由市政当局地| Operated or controlled by a municipality. | The library is municipally funded.  图书馆是由市政出资的。| 近义词: publicly, civically | 反义词: privately | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *municipalis, from Latin municipium (“free town, municipality”), from municeps (“citizen with full rights, freeman, inhabitant of a free town”), from munia (“duties, functions, offices”) + capere (“to take, seize”).
