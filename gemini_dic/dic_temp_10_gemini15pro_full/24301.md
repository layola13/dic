brightens:/ˈbraɪtnz/| v. |(使)变亮，(使)更有光泽，(使)更快乐，(使)更有希望| Makes something lighter, shinier, happier, or more hopeful. |例句：The sun brightens the sky. 太阳照亮了天空。|近义词：illuminates, lightens, cheers, encourages|反义词：darkens, dims, saddens, discourages|词性:动词

Etymology: From Middle English *brightnen, from Old English *beorhtnian, from beorht (bright).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *berhtaz ("bright"), from Proto-Indo-European *bhereg- ("to shine").
