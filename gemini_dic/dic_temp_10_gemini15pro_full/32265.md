Chicxulub: /tʃiːkʃʊˈluːb/ | n. |  希克苏鲁伯（陨石坑） |  A large impact crater buried underneath the Yucatán Peninsula in Mexico. It is believed to be the impact site of the asteroid that caused the Cretaceous–Paleogene extinction event, which led to the extinction of the dinosaurs. | The Chicxulub impact crater is one of the largest known impact structures on Earth. 希克苏鲁伯撞击坑是地球上已知的最大的撞击构造之一。

Chicxulub crater is evidence of a catastrophic event that changed the course of life on Earth. 希克苏鲁伯陨石坑是一个灾难性事件的证据，它改变了地球上生命的进程。
