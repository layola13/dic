mawawal:/məˈwɑːwɑːl/ | n. | 颤音，颤声 | A quavering or tremulous sound, often in singing. |  The singer's voice had a beautiful mawawal. 歌手的嗓音有一种美丽的颤音。| synonyms: trill, vibrato, quaver | antonyms: monotone | noun

mawawal:/məˈwɑːwɑːl/ | adj. | 颤音的，颤声的 | Characterized by a quavering or tremulous sound. | The mawawal quality of her voice added depth to the song. 她颤音的嗓音为歌曲增添了深度。| synonyms: tremulous, quavering, vibrating | antonyms: steady, firm | adjective
