araceous:/əˈreɪʃəs/
adj.
天南星科的;类似天南星科植物的
belonging to or resembling the Araceae family of plants (which includes arums, callas, and philodendrons).
The spadix is a characteristic inflorescence of araceous plants.  肉穗花序是天南星科植物的特征花序。
The large, glossy leaves are typical of many araceous species. 这些宽大有光泽的叶子是许多天南星科植物的典型特征。
近义词:arum-like
反义词: (No specific antonym, perhaps non-araceous)
词性:adjective
