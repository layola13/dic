deterministically:/dɪˌtɜːrmɪˈnɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 确定地；必然地 | In a deterministic manner; predictably; without randomness.  It describes actions or processes that are predetermined and predictable, without any element of chance or randomness. | The computer program deterministically generated the same output every time.  计算机程序每次都能确定地生成相同的输出。

The outcome of the experiment was deterministically controlled by the initial conditions. 实验结果是由初始条件确定性地控制的。| 近义词：predictably, invariably, necessarily | 反义词：randomly, stochastically, probabilistically | 词性: 副词
