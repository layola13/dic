archery: /ˈɑːrtʃəri/ (US) /ˈɑːtʃəri/ (UK)| n.|射箭, 弓箭术|The sport of shooting arrows with a bow.  It requires skill, precision, and focus.|Robin Hood is famous for his skill in archery. 罗宾汉以其精湛的箭术而闻名。|Archery is an Olympic sport. 射箭是一项奥林匹克运动。|近义词: toxophily|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology: From Old French *archerie*, from archer "archer," from Latin *arcuarius* "bowman," from arcus "bow."
