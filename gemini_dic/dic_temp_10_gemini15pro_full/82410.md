grudging:/ˈɡrʌdʒɪŋ/| adj.|勉强的，不情愿的，吝啬的|Given, granted, or allowed reluctantly or resentfully.|He gave a grudging apology. 他很不情愿地道歉了。|近义词：reluctant, unwilling, resentful|反义词：willing, eager, enthusiastic|词性:形容词
grudge:/ɡrʌdʒ/ |n. vt.|怨恨，妒忌；吝惜，勉强给|A persistent feeling of ill will or resentment resulting from a past offense or injury.|She held a grudge against him for years. 她对他怀恨在心多年。|近义词：resentment, grievance|反义词：forgiveness, acceptance|词性:名词,动词
