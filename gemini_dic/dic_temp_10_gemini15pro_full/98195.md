insecta:/ɪnˈsɛktə/
n.
昆虫纲; 昆虫
A large class of arthropods with three body sections (head, thorax, and abdomen), six legs, one pair of antennae, and usually one or two pairs of wings.
Insects play a vital role in the ecosystem. 昆虫在生态系统中起着至关重要的作用。
近义词: bug, creepy-crawly
反义词:  (无明显的反义词，可以考虑其他动物类别，例如mammal 哺乳动物)
词性: 名词
