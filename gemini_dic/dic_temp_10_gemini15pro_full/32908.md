chook:/tʃʊk/ | n. | 小鸡; 母鸡 | A chicken, especially a hen. |例句：The farmer went out to feed the chooks. 农民出去喂鸡。|近义词：chicken, hen |反义词:rooster (限于指母鸡时) | noun

chook:/tʃʊk/ | v. | 驱赶; 扔; 离开 | To throw something casually; to leave or depart, often quickly. | 例句：He chooked the ball to his friend. 他把球扔给他的朋友。 | 近义词：throw, toss, chuck, leave, depart | 反义词:catch, stay, arrive | verb
