galimatias:/ˌɡælɪˈmeɪʃiəs/ /ˌɡælɪˈmɑːtiəs/
n.
胡言乱语，混乱，废话
Nonsense; confused and unintelligible talk.
His speech was pure galimatias, a nonsensical jumble of words that left the audience bewildered. 他的演讲完全是胡言乱语，一堆毫无意义的词汇让听众感到困惑。

The contract was filled with legal galimatias that no one could understand.  合同里充斥着没人能懂的法律术语。
近义词：gibberish, jargon, nonsense, babble
反义词：sense, clarity, lucidity
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French galimatias, of unknown origin. Possibly related to Old French galimafrée "hodgepodge," itself of uncertain origin.
