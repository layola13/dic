gollock: /ˈɡɒlək/ | n. | 笨蛋，傻瓜；阴茎 | A foolish or stupid person; penis (vulgar slang). | He's such a gollock, he locked his keys in the car. 他真是个笨蛋，把钥匙锁在车里了。|  近义词: idiot, fool, moron, dickhead (vulgar) | 反义词: genius, smart person | 词性: 名词


词源:  The origin of "gollock" is uncertain.  It is likely a dialectal or slang term, possibly related to "gallock," meaning "testicle," which itself is of obscure origin.  Its use as an insult meaning "idiot" probably arose from the association of stupidity with the genitals.
