mechitarist:/ˌmɛkɪˈtɑːrɪst/
n.
Mechitarist; Mechitarists (plural)
梅希塔里斯特会修士；梅希塔里斯特会(复数)
A member of a congregation of Armenian Catholic monks founded in 1701 by Mechitar of Sebaste.  They are dedicated to scholarship and the preservation of Armenian culture.
例句：The Mechitarist monks are known for their scholarly work.  梅希塔里斯特会的修士以他们的学术工作而闻名。

例句：Many ancient Armenian texts were preserved by the Mechitarists. 许多古代亚美尼亚文献是由梅希塔里斯特会保存下来的。
近义词:Armenian Catholic monk, scholar
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps secularist or anti-clerical)
