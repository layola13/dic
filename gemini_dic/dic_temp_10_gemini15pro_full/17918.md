beestings:/ˈbiːstɪŋz/
noun
蜂刺；初乳
the first milk produced by a cow after giving birth, rich in antibodies and nutrients
例句：The calf eagerly drank the beestings. 小牛贪婪地喝着初乳。

例句：Beestings are considered a delicacy in some cultures. 在某些文化中，初乳被认为是一种美味佳肴。
近义词：colostrum, first milk
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
