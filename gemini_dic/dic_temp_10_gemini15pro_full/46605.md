dambo:/ˈdæmboʊ/| n. |草原洼地，季节性沼泽|A dambo is a shallow, seasonally flooded, grass-covered depression or valley bottom which occurs in the headwater zone of rivers in central, southern and eastern Africa.|The farmers planted crops in the dambo after the rains.|近义词：grassland, marsh, swamp, wetland|反义词:desert, upland|Noun
