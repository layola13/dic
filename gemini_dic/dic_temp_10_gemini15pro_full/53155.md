diplostemonous:/dɪˌplɒˈstɛmənəs/| adj.|双雄蕊的|Having twice as many stamens as petals, with the stamens arranged in two whorls, the outer whorl alternating with the petals.|Example:  The flower is diplostemonous, with ten stamens arranged in two rings of five.  这朵花是双雄蕊的，有十个雄蕊排列成两个五环。

Example: Diplostemonous flowers are common in the mustard family. 双雄蕊的花在十字花科中很常见。|近义词:polyandrous (多雄蕊的 -  a broader term)|反义词:haplostemonous (单雄蕊的), obdiplostemonous (外轮对萼的)|词性:adjective
