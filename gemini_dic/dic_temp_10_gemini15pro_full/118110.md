mennonite:/ˈmɛnənaɪt/| n. |门诺派教徒；门诺派信徒|A member of a Protestant sect originating in the 16th century from the Anabaptist movement. Mennonites are known for their pacifism, simple living, and emphasis on community.|例句：The Mennonites in this community are known for their beautiful quilts.  这个社区的门诺派教徒以他们美丽的被子而闻名。

例句：Many Mennonites are farmers and live a rural lifestyle. 许多门诺派教徒是农民，过着乡村生活。|近义词：Amish, Anabaptist|反义词: None (lacks a direct opposite)|词性: Noun
