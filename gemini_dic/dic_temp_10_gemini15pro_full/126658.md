nakedness:/ˈneɪkɪdnəs/
n.
裸体；赤裸；无遮蔽；缺乏掩饰
The state of being without clothes.  A lack of covering or protection.
The painting celebrates the nakedness of the human form.  这幅画颂扬了人体的裸露。
The nakedness of the land after the fire was shocking.  火灾后土地的裸露令人震惊。
nudity, bareness, exposure
concealment, covering, clothedness
noun
