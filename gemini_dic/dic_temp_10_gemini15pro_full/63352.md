entombment:/ɪnˈtuːmment/
n.
埋葬，安葬
The act of placing a body in a tomb.
The entombment of the pharaoh took place with great ceremony.  法老的安葬仪式非常隆重。
burial, interment
exhumation
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English entombing, from Old French entomber, from en- (in) + tombe (tomb).
