hypochondriacs: /ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndrɪæks/ (UK) /ˌhaɪpəˈkɑːndrɪæks/ (US)| n. |疑病症患者; 臆想症患者|A person who is abnormally anxious about their health.  A person who continually worries about their health without having any reason to do so. |例句：He's a terrible hypochondriac and always thinks he's dying. 他是个严重的疑病症患者，总是觉得自己快要死了。

例句：She's become a real hypochondriac since reading that article about rare diseases.  自从读了那篇关于罕见疾病的文章后，她就变成了一个真正的疑病症患者。|近义词：valetudinarian, worrier|反义词: stoic|词性: noun


hypochondriac: /ˌhaɪpəˈkɒndrɪæk/ (UK) /ˌhaɪpəˈkɑːndrɪæk/ (US) | adj. |疑病的; 臆想病的|Suffering from or relating to hypochondria. Excessively worried about one's health, often without any real cause.|例句：He was always complaining of some new ailment – he was very hypochondriac. 他总是抱怨一些新的病痛——他非常疑病。

例句：She dismissed his complaints as hypochondriac. 她认为他的抱怨是疑病症的表现而不予理会。 |近义词：valetudinarian, neurotic|反义词: healthy, well|词性: adjective
