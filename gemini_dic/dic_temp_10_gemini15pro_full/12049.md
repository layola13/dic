articulator:/ɑːrˈtɪkjʊleɪtər/ /ɑːrˈtɪkjəleɪtər/| n.|发音器官；清晰吐字的人|A structure in the mouth used to produce speech sounds, such as the tongue, lips, teeth, and palate.  A person who speaks clearly and distinctly.|例句：The tongue is a vital articulator in speech production. 舌头是言语生成中至关重要的发音器官。

例句：She is a skilled articulator, able to pronounce even the most complex words with ease. 她是一个吐字清晰的人，即使是最复杂的单词也能轻松发音。|近义词：speaker, orator|反义词:mumbler|noun
