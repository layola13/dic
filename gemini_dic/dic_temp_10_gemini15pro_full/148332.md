overspaciously:/ˌoʊvərˈspeɪʃəsli/
adjective
过于宽敞地
in a way that uses or provides too much space
The room was overspaciously furnished, with only a few pieces of furniture scattered around.  这个房间布置得过于宽敞，只有几件家具散落在周围。
The architect designed the building overspaciously, resulting in wasted space.  建筑师把建筑设计得过于宽敞，导致空间浪费。
Synonyms: excessively spacious, too roomy, unnecessarily large
Antonyms: cramped, crowded, compact
词性: 形容词
