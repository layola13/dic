assureds:/əˈʃʊrdz/
n.
被保险人，受保人
People or organizations covered by an insurance policy.
The policy clearly states the rights of the assureds.  该保单明确规定了被保险人的权利。
The company promptly paid the claim to its assureds.  该公司迅速向其被保险人支付了索赔。
Synonyms: insured, policyholder, beneficiary
Antonyms: insurer, underwriter
noun
