friseur:/frɪˈzɜːr/ (UK), /frɪˈzʊər/ (US)| n.|理发师，美发师|A hairdresser, especially a fashionable one.|例句：I went to the friseur to get my hair cut. 我去理发店剪头发了。

例句：The friseur styled her hair beautifully for the wedding.  这位美发师为婚礼漂亮地给她做了头发。

近义词：hairdresser, stylist, barber|反义词: None|词性:Noun

Etymology:
From French *friseur*, from *friser* "to curl," from Frankish *frisan*, from Proto-Germanic *frisōną* "to curl, crisp," from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kreis-* "to turn, bend, curve."
