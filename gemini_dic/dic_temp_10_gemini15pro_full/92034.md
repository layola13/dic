hyperpatriotisms:/ˌhaɪpərˈpeɪtriətɪzəmz/
|n.|
|极端爱国主义；过度的爱国主义|
|Excessive or exaggerated patriotism, often characterized by aggressive nationalism and intolerance of dissent.|
|The rise of hyperpatriotisms in the country led to increased tensions with its neighbors. 这个国家极端爱国主义的兴起导致与其邻国的关系日益紧张。|

|His speeches were filled with hyperpatriotisms, alienating many moderate voters. 他的演讲充满了极端爱国主义，疏远了许多温和的选民。|
|近义词：ultranationalism, jingoism, chauvinism|
|反义词：internationalism, cosmopolitanism|
|词性：名词（plural）|


词源：
hyper- (Greek: over, excessive) + patriotism
