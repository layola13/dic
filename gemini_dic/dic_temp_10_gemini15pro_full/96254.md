indicatrix:/ɪndɪˈkeɪtrɪks/ | n. |指示函数；指标 |A curve or other figure that indicates, by its direction or magnitude at any point, the direction and magnitude of a vector quantity at that point. | The indicatrix of refraction is a useful tool in crystal optics.  折射率指示函数是晶体光学中的一个有用工具。

The indicatrix shows the variation in refractive index with direction in a crystal. 指示函数显示了晶体中折射率随方向的变化。 | synonyms: indicator, gauge, measure | antonyms: N/A | noun
