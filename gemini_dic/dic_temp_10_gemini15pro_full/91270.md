hydrogenating:/ˈhaɪdrədʒəneɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 氢化 | The process of combining something with hydrogen, typically by causing hydrogen to react with an unsaturated organic compound. |  Margarine is made by hydrogenating vegetable oil. 人造黄油是通过氢化植物油制成的。| synonyms: reducing, saturating | antonyms: dehydrogenating | verb

hydrogenating:/ˈhaɪdrədʒəneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 氢化的 | Relating to the process of hydrogenation. | The hydrogenating process increases the shelf life of the oil.  氢化过程增加了油的保质期。 | synonyms: reduced, saturated | antonyms: dehydrogenated | adjective
