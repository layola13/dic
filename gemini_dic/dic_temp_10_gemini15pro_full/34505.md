clean-out:/ˈkliːnaʊt/| n.| 清理，清除；清仓大甩卖|A thorough cleaning or emptying of something, often a room, cupboard, or container. clean-out:/ˈkliːnaʊt/| n.| 洗劫，彻底击败|An act of emptying something completely, often by stealing.|The burglars made a clean-out of the jewelry store. 盗贼洗劫了珠宝店。|近义词：robbery, theft, heist|反义词: - |词性:noun
