breakable:/ˈbreɪkəbl/| adj.|易碎的，脆弱的|Something that is breakable is easy to break. |The plates are breakable, so please handle them with care.  盘子易碎，请小心轻放。|fragile, delicate, frail|robust, sturdy, strong|adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From break (verb) + -able (suffix). The suffix "-able" forms adjectives meaning "capable of being," "suitable for being," or "tending to."  "Break" comes from Old English "brecan."
