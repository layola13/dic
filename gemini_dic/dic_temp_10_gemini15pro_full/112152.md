look-down-up-on:/ˌlʊk ˈdaʊn əˈpɒn/ (UK), /ˌlʊk ˈdaʊn əˈpɑːn/ (US)| phrasal verb |轻视，瞧不起|To regard (someone) with a feeling of superiority.|He looks down upon anyone who hasn't had a university education. 他瞧不起任何没有受过大学教育的人。|近义词：despise, disdain, scorn|反义词: admire, respect|词性:动词短语

look down upon:/ˌlʊk ˈdaʊn əˈpɒn/ (UK), /ˌlʊk ˈdaʊn əˈpɑːn/ (US)| phrasal verb |轻视，瞧不起|To consider someone or something inferior; to have contempt for.|She looks down upon people who are less fortunate than her. 她瞧不起那些不如她幸运的人。|近义词：condescend to, patronize, sneer at|反义词: look up to, admire|词性:动词短语
