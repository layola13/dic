eminently:/ˈemɪnəntli/| adv.|非常地，显著地，突出地|in a highly noticeable or prominent manner; to a notable degree; very.|She is eminently qualified for the job. 她非常胜任这份工作。|近义词：highly, exceptionally, notably, remarkably, particularly, supremely|反义词：slightly, hardly, marginally|词性:副词
Etymology: From Latin *eminenter*, from *eminens*, present participle of *eminēre* "to stand out, project," from *ex- "out" + *minēre* "to project."
