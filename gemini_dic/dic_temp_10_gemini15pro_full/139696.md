oceanic: /ˌoʊʃiˈænɪk/ | adj. | 海洋的；广阔无垠的 | Relating to the ocean; vast and boundless. |例句：Oceanic currents influence global climate. 洋流影响全球气候。

例句：The oceanic depths remain largely unexplored. 海洋深处很大程度上仍未被探索。

近义词：marine, maritime, pelagic | 反义词：terrestrial, inland | 词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *océanique*, from Latin *oceanicus*, from *oceanus* ("ocean"), from Ancient Greek *ōkeanós* (literally "great stream believed to encircle the earth's disc"). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ṓḱeanos* (“outer waters”).
