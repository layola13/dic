overdocument:/ˌoʊvərˈdɑːkjəment/ | vt. | 过度记录；提供过多文件 | To provide excessive documentation for something; to document something to an unnecessary degree. | The lawyer overdocumented the case, burying the jury in paperwork. 律师为这个案件提供了过多的文件，使陪审团淹没在文书工作中。| The company overdocumented the process, creating a mountain of unnecessary paperwork.  这家公司过度记录了这个流程，制造了大量的无用文件。 | 近义词: overrecord | 反义词: underdocument | 词性: 动词
