hypo:/ˈhaɪpoʊ/| prefix|低于，次于，在…之下|A prefix meaning "under," "beneath," "less than normal," or "deficient."|例句：Hypothermia is a dangerous condition where the body loses heat faster than it can produce it. 低体温是一种危险的状况，身体散热的速度快于产生热量的速度。

例句：He suffers from hypoglycemia, which means his blood sugar levels are often too low. 他患有低血糖症，这意味着他的血糖水平通常过低。|近义词：sub-|反义词：hyper-|词性：前缀
