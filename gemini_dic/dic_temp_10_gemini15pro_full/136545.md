nonsaccharine:/nɒnˈsækərɪn/ /nɑːnˈsækərɪn/
adjective
不甜的；不含糖的；不讨好人的；不感情用事的
Not sweet; not sugary; lacking in sentimentality or emotional appeal.
Her nonsaccharine portrayal of the character was refreshing. 她对这个角色不加糖饰的刻画令人耳目一新。
近义词: unsweetened, sugarless, dry, austere, unemotional
反义词: sweet, sugary, saccharine, sentimental, emotional
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + saccharine (derived from Latin "saccharum" meaning "sugar")
