hypothenuses:/haɪˈpɒtənjuːziz/
|n.|斜边（复数）
|The length of the hypotenuses of these right triangles are equal. 这些直角三角形的斜边长度相等。
|The hypotenuses of these triangles are all different lengths. 这些三角形的斜边长度各不相同。
|近义词: longest side (in a right triangle)
|反义词: leg (of a right triangle),  cathetus
|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ὑποτείνουσα (hupoteínousa, “subtending line, hypotenuse”), feminine present participle of  ὑποτείνω (hupoteínō, “I stretch or extend under; I subtend”).  It refers to the line segment "stretched under" the right angle in a right triangle.
