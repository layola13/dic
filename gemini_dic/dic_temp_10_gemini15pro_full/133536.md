nonfigurativenesses:/ˌnɒnfɪɡjərətɪvnəsnəsɪz/ /ˌnɑːnfɪɡjərətɪvnəsnəsɪz/
noun
非具象性；非象征性
The state or quality of being nonfigurative; lack of representation of recognizable objects or figures.
The artist explored nonfigurativenesses in his later works, abandoning his earlier realistic style.  艺术家在他后期作品中探索了非具象性，放弃了他早期的现实主义风格。
Near synonyms: abstraction, nonrepresentation, nonobjectivity
Antonyms: figurativeness, representationalism
词性: 名词
