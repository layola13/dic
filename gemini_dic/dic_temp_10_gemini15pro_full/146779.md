overidentification:/ˌoʊvərɪˌdentɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度认同，过度认同感 | The act of identifying too strongly with someone or something, often to the point of losing one's own separate identity or objectivity. |  He showed signs of overidentification with his favorite superhero. 他表现出对他最喜欢的超级英雄过度认同的迹象。

Overidentification with a patient can lead to burnout in therapists. 对病人过度认同会导致治疗师倦怠。 | 近义词: excessive identification, empathizing too much | 反义词: detachment, disinterest | 词性: noun


词源:
From over- + identification.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much."  "Identification" comes from the Latin "identificare," meaning "to identify."
