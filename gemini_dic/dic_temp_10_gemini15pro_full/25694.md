bunkered:/ˈbʌŋkərd/| adj.|被困住的，陷入困境的；(高尔夫球)进入沙坑的|Being in a difficult situation, trapped, or in a sand trap (in golf).|He was bunkered down in his apartment during the blizzard. 他在暴风雪期间被困在他的公寓里。|The golfer's ball was bunkered.  高尔夫球手的球进了沙坑。|近义词：trapped, stuck, stranded, cornered (in a difficult situation); in a sand trap (in golf)|反义词：free, liberated|词性:形容词

bunkered:/ˈbʌŋkərd/| v.|躲藏，隐蔽；储存|To hide or take shelter; to store.|The soldiers bunkered down during the bombardment. 士兵们在轰炸期间躲了起来。|The squirrels bunkered nuts for the winter. 松鼠们为冬天储存坚果。|近义词：hide, shelter, conceal, store, stockpile|反义词：expose, reveal|词性:动词
