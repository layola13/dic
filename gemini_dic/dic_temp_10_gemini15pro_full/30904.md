chagrin:/ʃəˈɡrɪn/ /ʃəˈɡrɪn/
n.|懊恼，失望，悔恨|A feeling of annoyance or disappointment at having failed or been humiliated.|His chagrin was evident when he lost the game. 他的懊恼在输掉比赛时显而易见。|
chagrin:/ʃəˈɡrɪn/ /ʃəˈɡrɪn/
vt.|使懊恼，使失望，使悔恨|To cause (someone) to feel annoyed or disappointed.|It chagrined her that she hadn't been invited to the party. 没有被邀请参加聚会让她很懊恼。|
chagrin:/ʃəˈɡrɪn/ /ʃəˈɡrɪn/
