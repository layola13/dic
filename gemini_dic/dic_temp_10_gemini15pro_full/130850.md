nonapproachable:/ˌnɒnəˈprəʊtʃəbl/ (US) /ˌnɑːnəˈproʊtʃəbl/| adj.|难以接近的，冷漠的|Not easy to talk to; unfriendly.|She seemed cold and nonapproachable, so I didn't try to talk to her. 她看起来很冷漠，难以接近，所以我没有尝试和她说话。|The CEO has a reputation for being nonapproachable, making employees hesitant to share their concerns.  这位CEO以难以接近而闻名，这让员工们不敢分享他们的担忧。|近义词：unfriendly, aloof, distant, standoffish, inaccessible|反义词：approachable, friendly, warm, welcoming|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From non- + approachable.  "Approachable" comes from the verb "approach," which ultimately derives from the Old French "aprochier," meaning "to draw near." The prefix "non-" simply negates the meaning.
