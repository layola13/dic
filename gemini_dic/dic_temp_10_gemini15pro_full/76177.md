fusser:/ˈfʌsər/| n. |大惊小怪的人；小题大做的人；过于讲究细节的人|Someone who fusses, worries, or makes a great deal of unnecessary preparation over something, especially minor details. |例句：He's such a fusser about his food, always sending things back to the kitchen. 他对食物太挑剔了，总是把东西退回厨房。

例句：She's a real fusser when it comes to planning parties.  她在筹备派对时真是个事无巨细的人。|近义词：worrier, nitpicker, perfectionist|反义词:easygoing person, relaxed person|Noun
