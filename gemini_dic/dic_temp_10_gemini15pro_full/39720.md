consignor:/kənˈsaɪnər/ | n. |发货人；委托人；寄售人|A person or company that sends goods to another party for sale or delivery.  A consignor retains ownership of the goods until they are sold.|The consignor shipped the goods by rail. 发货人通过铁路运输货物。|synonyms: shipper, sender, exporter|antonyms: consignee, receiver, importer|noun

consignor:/kənˈsaɪnər/ | n. |发货人；委托人；寄售人|A person or company that entrusts goods to another party for sale or delivery, retaining ownership until the goods are sold.  This often occurs in consignment shops or art galleries.|The artist acted as consignor for her paintings at the gallery.  这位艺术家在画廊里充当了她作品的委托人。|synonyms:  vendor, supplier, principal|antonyms: agent, broker, representative|noun
