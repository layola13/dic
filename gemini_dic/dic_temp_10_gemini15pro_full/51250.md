destructured:/diːˈstrʌktʃəd/| adj.|解构的，结构化的|Broken down into constituent parts, especially of data structures.  Often used in the context of programming, where it refers to extracting values from objects or arrays.|

例句：
The destructured data was easier to process.
解构后的数据更容易处理。

例句：
Destructuring assignment syntax makes it easy to extract data from objects.
解构赋值语法使从对象中提取数据变得容易。

近义词：decomposed, disassembled, broken down|
反义词：structured, composed, assembled|
词性: adjective
