eldritch:/ˈeldrɪtʃ/| adj.|怪异的，奇异的，恐怖的|Strange and eerie; suggestive of supernatural powers.  Related to elves and other supernatural beings.|The house had an eldritch atmosphere, with strange shadows and unexplained noises. 这房子有一种怪异的气氛，到处是奇怪的阴影和无法解释的声音。|近义词：weird, uncanny, eerie, otherworldly, supernatural|反义词：normal, ordinary, mundane|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *elrische, from Old English *ylfiþe ("elf-threat," "nightmare").  The modern form is influenced by Scottish usage.
