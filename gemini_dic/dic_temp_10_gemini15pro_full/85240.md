词源:
From Middle English *harmen, *hermen, from Old English hearmian (“to harm, hurt, damage”), from Proto-Germanic *harmijaną (“to harm”), from Proto-Indo-European *korm-  (“to cut, shear, crop”).  Cognate with Dutch ermen (“to grieve”), German ermarmen (“to move to pity”). Related to harm (n.).
