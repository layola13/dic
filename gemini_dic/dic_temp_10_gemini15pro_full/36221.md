cognately:/ˈkɒɡnɪtli/ /ˈkɑːɡnɪtli/| adv.|同源地，同族地|related by descent from the same ancestor|The words "brother" and "bruder" are cognate. “brother”和“bruder”这两个词是同源词。|近义词：relatedly, similarly|反义词：differently, unrelatedly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *cognatus, from Latin cognatus (“related by birth”), from co- (“together”) + natus (“born”), perfect passive participle of nasci.
