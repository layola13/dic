asbo: /ˈæzbəʊ/ (UK)
noun
反社会行为令 (Anti-Social Behaviour Order)
A civil order made against a person who has been shown, on the balance of probabilities, to have engaged in anti-social behaviour.
例句：He was given an ASBO for playing loud music late at night.  他因深夜播放 loud music late at night.
近义词：injunction, order, restriction
反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly "commendation" or "award" in a loose sense)
词性: noun
