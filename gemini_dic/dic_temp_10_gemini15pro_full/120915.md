miscasts:/ˌmɪsˈkɑːsts/
|n.|错误的选角|An instance of casting an actor in a role for which they are unsuited.|The play suffered from several miscasts, with actors clearly uncomfortable in their roles.  这部戏有几个选角错误，演员们显然对自己的角色感到不适。|近义词: mismatches, bad casting|反义词: perfect casting, appropriate casting|词性:名词

miscast:/ˌmɪsˈkɑːst/
|vt.|选错角色|To cast an actor in a role for which they are not suited.|The director miscast the lead role, choosing an actor who lacked the necessary experience. 导演选错了主角，选了一个缺乏必要经验的演员。|近义词: misassign, misallocate|反义词: cast perfectly, cast appropriately|词性:动词
