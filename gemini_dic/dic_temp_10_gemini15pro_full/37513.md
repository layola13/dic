commodore:/ˈkɒmədɔːr/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmədɔːr/ (US)| n.|海军准将；船队队长；（某些组织的）会长，主席|A naval officer of high rank, above a captain and below a rear admiral.  Also, the title of the senior captain in a fleet of merchant ships or the president of a yacht club or similar organization.|The commodore issued orders to the fleet. 船队队长向舰队下达了命令。|Near synonyms: captain, admiral, commander, leader, chief|Antonyms: subordinate, follower|Noun
