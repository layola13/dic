acolyte: /ˈækəlaɪt/ | n. |侍僧，助手，追随者 |  A person assisting a priest in a religious service; a follower or assistant. | The acolyte lit the candles on the altar.  侍僧点燃了祭坛上的蜡烛。

The CEO's acolytes quickly carried out his orders. 首席执行官的助手们迅速执行了他的命令。 | 近义词: follower, assistant, supporter, disciple, attendant | 反义词: leader, opponent, antagonist | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *acolythus*, from Greek *akolouthos* "follower, attendant," from *akolouthein* "to follow, attend," from *a- "together"* + *keleuthos* "path, way."
