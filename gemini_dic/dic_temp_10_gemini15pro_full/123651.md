monumentality:/ˌmɒnjʊmɛnˈtælɪti/ /ˌmɑːnjʊmɛnˈtælɪti/| n. |不朽性；巨大；雄伟|The quality of being monumental, especially in terms of size, permanence, or impact.  The monumentality of the pyramids is awe-inspiring.|例句：The monumentality of the ancient ruins impressed all the visitors. 古代遗址的雄伟气势给所有游客留下了深刻的印象。

例句：The sheer monumentality of the project overwhelmed the team. 项目的庞大规模让团队不堪重负。|近义词：immensity, vastness, grandeur, majesty|反义词：insignificance, smallness, triviality|词性:noun
