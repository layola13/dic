anticlericals:/ˌæntiˈklɛrɪkəlz/
| n. |反教权主义者；反对神职人员者|People who oppose the power or influence of the clergy in political and social affairs.  They may or may not oppose religion itself.|The rise of anticlericals in the 19th century threatened the established power of the Church. 19世纪反教权主义者的兴起威胁到了教会的既定权力。|近义词：secularists, freethinkers|反义词:clericals, devout|noun

anticlerical:/ˌæntiˈklɛrɪkəl/
| adj. |反教权的；反对神职人员的|Opposed to the power or influence of the clergy.|The government adopted several anticlerical measures. 政府采取了几项反教权措施。|近义词：secular, irreligious|反义词:clerical, religious|adjective
