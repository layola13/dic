hubub:/ˈhʌbʌb/ (US), /ˈhʌbʌb/ (UK)| n. |喧闹声，嘈杂声，混乱|A loud confused noise of many voices or sounds.|The hubub of the marketplace made it difficult to hear him.  市场上的喧闹声让他很难被听到。|Synonyms: clamor, din, uproar, racket|Antonyms: quiet, silence, stillness|Noun
