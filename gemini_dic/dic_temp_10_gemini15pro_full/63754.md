epicentral: /ˌɛpɪˈsɛntrəl/ | adj. |震中的；中心的；焦点的|Of or relating to the epicenter of an earthquake; situated at or being the epicenter. Also used figuratively to describe something as the center of activity or attention. |The epicentral area suffered the greatest damage. 震中区域遭受了最大的破坏。|The political debate was epicentral to the news coverage that week. 那场政治辩论是那周新闻报道的焦点。| 近义词: central, focal, nuclear | 反义词: peripheral, outlying | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  From epi- + center + -al.  Epi- is a Greek prefix meaning "upon," "at," "over," or "near."  Center comes from the Latin centrum, meaning "center point."  The suffix -al forms adjectives meaning "relating to" or "characterized by."
