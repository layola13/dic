cookbooks:/ˈkʊkbʊks/ | n. |食谱；烹饪书籍|A book containing recipes and other information about cooking. |例句：I have a collection of cookbooks from around the world. 我收藏了来自世界各地的食谱。

例句：She learned to cook from her grandmother's old cookbooks. 她从她祖母的旧食谱中学做饭。|近义词：recipe books, culinary books|反义词: None (No single antonym exists for a collection of recipes.)|词性:noun
