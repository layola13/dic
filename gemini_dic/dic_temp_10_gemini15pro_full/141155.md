ooephore:/ˈoʊəfɔːr/
noun
卵巢
One of the two female reproductive glands in which ova (eggs) are produced.
The surgeon removed the patient's ooephore due to ovarian cancer. 由于卵巢癌，外科医生切除了病人的卵巢。
Synonyms: ovary
Antonyms: N/A
