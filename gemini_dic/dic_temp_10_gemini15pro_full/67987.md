factful:/ˈfæktfʊl/
adjective
基于事实的；尊重事实的
Based on facts; concerned with what is actually the case.
Hans Rosling's book "Factfulness" encourages a fact-based worldview.  汉斯·罗斯林的著作《事实》鼓励人们基于事实的世界观。

Factfulness promotes a clearer understanding of the world.  尊重事实能促进对世界的更清晰的理解。

synonym:  factual, data-driven, evidence-based, objective, realistic
antonym:  misinformed, biased, subjective, prejudiced, fanciful
词性: 形容词
