dramatic:/drəˈmætɪk/| adj.|戏剧的，引人注目的，突然的|Relating to drama or the performance or study of drama; sudden and striking; exciting or impressive.| The meeting ended with a dramatic announcement. 会议以一项引人注目的公告结束。|  The storm caused a dramatic change in the weather.  暴风雨导致天气发生了巨大的变化。 |近义词：theatrical, striking, impressive, sudden, exciting|反义词：undistinguished, unexciting, ordinary, gradual|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dramaticus*, from Greek *dramatikos* "pertaining to plays," from *drama* (genitive *dramatos*) "a play, action, deed" (see drama).
