noncircularly:/ˌnɒnsɜːkjʊləli/
adverb|非循环地，非圆形地|Not in a circular manner; not forming a closed loop or circle.|The ribbon was wound noncircularly around the pole.|近义词：linearly, straightly|反义词：circularly, roundly|词性:副词
Etymology: non- + circular + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "circular" refers to a circle or loop, and "-ly" forms adverbs.
