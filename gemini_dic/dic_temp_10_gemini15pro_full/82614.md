Guatemala: /ˌɡwɑːtəˈmɑːlə/ (US), /ˌɡwætəˈmɑːlə/ (UK)| n. |危地马拉|A country in Central America bordered by Mexico, Belize, Honduras, and El Salvador. It is known for its Mayan ruins and volcanic landscapes.|My friend just returned from a trip to Guatemala. 我的朋友刚从危地马拉旅行回来。| Guatemala is famous for its coffee. 危地马拉以其咖啡而闻名。|近义词: (无，专有名词)|反义词: (无，专有名词)|词性: noun

Etymology: From Nahuatl *Cuauhtēmallān (meaning "place of many trees," likely referring to the Cuate/Cuatli tree).
