desudation:/ˌdɛsjuːˈdeɪʃən/ /ˌdɛsuːˈdeɪʃən/
n.
出汗; 排汗; 发汗
The process of sweating.  Profuse desudation is a common symptom of heatstroke.
The patient experienced profuse desudation during the fever. 病人在发烧期间大量出汗。

近义词: sweating, perspiration, diaphoresis
反义词: anhidrosis

词性: noun

词源: From Latin *desudatio*, from desudare "to sweat greatly," from de- "from, out of" + sudare "to sweat."
