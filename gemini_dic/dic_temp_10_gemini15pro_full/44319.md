cropless:/ˈkrɑːplɪs/| adj.|无裁剪的，未经裁剪的|Refers to an image or video that has not been cropped, preserving its original aspect ratio and full content.  It often refers specifically to images shared on social media platforms where cropping is common.|She posted a cropless photo on Instagram to showcase the entire outfit.|近义词：uncropped, full-frame, complete|反义词:cropped, trimmed, cut|词性:adjective
