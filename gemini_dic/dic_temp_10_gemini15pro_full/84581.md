hammock:/ˈhæmək/ (US), /ˈhæmək/ (UK)| n. |吊床|A hanging bed or couch, typically made of canvas or rope netting, suspended between two points.|She relaxed in the hammock, reading a book. 她躺在吊床上看书。|He strung his hammock between two palm trees. 他把吊床系在两棵棕榈树之间。|Synonyms: swing bed, hanging bed|Antonyms: bed, couch|noun
