hellenistically:/ˌhɛlɪˈnɪstɪkəli/
adv.
希腊化地，受希腊文化影响地
in the manner of Hellenistic Greece, or showing the influence of Hellenistic Greek culture.
The sculptures were hellenistically inspired, blending Eastern and Western artistic traditions. 这些雕塑受到了希腊化风格的启发，融合了东西方艺术传统。
Near synonyms:  Greek-influenced, Hellenic, Alexandrian
Antonyms:  non-Hellenistic, barbarian (in the ancient Greek sense)
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Hellenistic + -ally.  Hellenistic derives from Hellenist (a person who adopted Greek language and culture) + -ic.  Ultimately from Greek Hellēn (Greek).
