growbag: /ˈɡroʊbæɡ/ | n. | 种植袋；育苗袋 | A bag filled with compost, used for growing plants. | I bought some growbags to grow tomatoes on my balcony. 我在阳台上种西红柿，买了一些种植袋。|  She planted potatoes in growbags. 她把土豆种在种植袋里。 | synonyms: planting bag, growing bag | antonyms: pot, planter | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *grow* + *bag*.  *Grow* comes from Old English *grōwan*, meaning "to grow." *Bag* comes from Old Norse *baggi*, meaning "bag, bundle."
