passport:/ˈpæspɔːrt/ (US), /ˈpɑːspɔːrt/ (UK)| n.|护照|An official document issued by a government, certifying the holder's identity and citizenship and entitling them to travel under its protection to and from foreign countries.|She needed to renew her passport before her trip. 她需要在旅行前更新护照。|Synonyms: travel document, identification, ID|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
