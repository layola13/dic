abolitionize:/ˌæbəˈlɪʃənaɪz/
verb
废除; 倡导废除
To advocate for the abolition of something, especially slavery.  To work towards abolishing something.
The group sought to abolitionize the death penalty in their state.  该组织试图在他们所在州废除死刑。

Abolitionists worked tirelessly to abolitionize slavery in the United States.  废奴主义者不知疲倦地致力于在美国废除奴隶制。
近义词: abolish, eradicate, eliminate, extirpate
反义词: establish, institute, uphold, maintain
词性: verb
