collapsars:/kəˈlæpsɑːrz/| n. |坍缩星；塌缩星|A massive star that undergoes gravitational collapse, especially one that forms a black hole.  A hypothetical type of supernova explosion thought to be produced by a very massive star at the end of its life.|Astronomers believe some gamma-ray bursts are produced by collapsars. 天文学家认为一些伽马射线暴是由坍缩星产生的。|
The collapsar model explains the origin of long-duration gamma-ray bursts. 坍缩星模型解释了长时间伽玛射线暴的起源。|Synonyms: hypernova, core-collapse supernova|Antonyms: N/A (no direct opposite for a celestial event)|noun
