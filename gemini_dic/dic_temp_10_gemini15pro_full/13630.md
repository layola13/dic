attirer:/atɪʁe/ (French)| v. |引诱，诱惑|To tempt or entice someone to do something. |The smell of freshly baked bread attracted me into the bakery.  新鲜出炉面包的香味把我吸引进了面包店。|Synonyms: tempt, entice, lure, seduce|Antonyms: deter, discourage|Verb
