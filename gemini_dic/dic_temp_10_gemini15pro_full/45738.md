curving:/ˈkɜːrvɪŋ/| adj. v. (present participle of curve)|弯曲的，曲线形的；使弯曲|Forming a curve or moving along a curve. |The road was curving sharply to the left. 这条路向左急转弯。|The artist is curving the metal into an interesting shape.  这位艺术家正把金属弯成一个有趣的形状。|近义词：bending, winding, twisting|反义词：straightening, straightening out|词性: adjective, verb (present participle)
Etymology: From Middle English *curven, from Old French corber (“to bend, curve, crook”), from Late Latin *curvare, from Latin curvus (“bent, curved”).
