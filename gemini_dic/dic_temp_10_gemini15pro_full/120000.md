millilitre:/ˈmɪlɪˌliːtər/ (US) /ˈmɪlɪˌliːtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|毫升|A unit of volume equal to one thousandth of a litre.|例句：The recipe calls for 250 millilitres of milk.  食谱需要250毫升牛奶。

例句：Please give me 50 millilitres of that cough syrup. 请给我50毫升那种止咳糖浆。|近义词：cc (cubic centimetre), mL|反义词: litre, kilolitre|noun
