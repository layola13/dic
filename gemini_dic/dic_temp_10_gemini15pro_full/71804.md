fleeced:/flist/ | v. | 欺骗，诈取，敲竹杠 | to cheat someone, especially by charging them too much money for something | He was fleeced of his life savings by a con artist. 他被一个骗子骗走了他一生的积蓄。 |  The tourists were completely fleeced by the street vendors.  游客们完全被街头小贩敲了竹杠。 | synonyms: cheated, swindled, defrauded, ripped off | antonyms: honest, fair, equitable | verb

fleeced:/flist/ | adj. | 被骗的，被敲竹杠的 | having been cheated or swindled |  He felt fleeced after buying the overpriced car.  买了那辆价格过高的车后，他觉得自己被骗了。 | The fleeced customer complained to the manager. 被敲竹杠的顾客向经理投诉。| synonyms: cheated, swindled, defrauded, ripped off | antonyms: protected, safeguarded | adjective
