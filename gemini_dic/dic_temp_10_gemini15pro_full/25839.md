burgonet:/ˈbɜːrɡənɪt/ |n.|轻盔，勃艮第盔|A type of helmet, typically with a visor and a peak, popular in the 16th and 17th centuries.|The knight wore a gleaming burgonet into battle.  骑士戴着闪亮的轻盔上战场。|Synonyms: helmet, helm, casque|Antonyms: None|Noun
