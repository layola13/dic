nucumentaceous:/ˌnjuːkəmənˈteɪʃəs/
adjective
多核的; 多坚果的
Containing many nuts or nut-like parts.  Relating to or resembling a nucule.
The nucumentaceous fruit of the lotus is a unique structure. 莲的多核果实是一种独特的结构。
The botanist described the plant as having a nucumentaceous pod.  植物学家描述这种植物的荚果是多核的。

近义词: nut-bearing, multi-seeded
反义词: seedless

词性: adjective
