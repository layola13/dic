nondeciduousness:/ˌnɑːndɪˈsɪdʒuəsnəs/
n.
常绿性；不落叶性
The quality or state of being nondeciduous; evergreenness.
The nondeciduousness of the trees kept the garden green all year round. 这些树木的常绿性使花园一年四季都保持绿色。
Near Synonyms: evergreenness, persistence of foliage
Antonyms: deciduousness, leaflessness
noun
