beckoning:/ˈbɛkənɪŋ/| adj.| 召唤的，吸引人的 | Appealing or inviting; attractive.  Making you want to go somewhere or do something. | The beckoning lights of the city drew her in. 城市的灯光吸引着她。|Synonyms: alluring, enticing, tempting, inviting, attractive | Antonyms: repellent, repulsive, uninviting, off-putting | adjective

beckon:/ˈbɛkən/| v.| 召唤，示意 | To make a gesture with the hand, arm, or head to encourage someone to come nearer or follow. | He beckoned her over with a smile. 他笑着向她招手。| Synonyms: signal, gesture, summon, wave, invite | Antonyms: dismiss, repel, warn away | verb
