greenishness:/ˈɡriːnɪʃnəs/| n. |浅绿色，略带绿色，绿色程度|The greenishness of the water suggested the presence of algae. 水略带绿色表明其中存在藻类。|例句：The painting captured the greenishness of the early spring leaves. 这幅画捕捉到了早春树叶的浅绿色。

例句：There's a slight greenishness to his complexion today. 他今天的脸色有点发绿。|近义词：viridity, verdancy|反义词: paleness, yellowness|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From green + -ish + -ness.  "Green" comes from the Old English "grēne," related to "grow." The suffix "-ish" indicates a degree or quality of something, while "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives.
