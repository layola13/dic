disintegrative:/dɪsˈɪntɪˌɡreɪtɪv/
adjective
分裂的，瓦解的
Causing or tending to cause disintegration; breaking up into parts or components.
The disintegrative forces within the empire eventually led to its collapse.  帝国内部的分裂力量最终导致了它的崩溃。
The report highlighted the disintegrative effects of long-term unemployment on families.  报告强调了长期失业对家庭的瓦解性影响。
近义词: disruptive, divisive, destructive, fragmenting
反义词: unifying, cohesive, constructive, binding
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From disintegrate + -ive.  Disintegrate comes from the Latin prefix dis- meaning "apart, asunder" + integrate from Latin integrāre "to make whole".
