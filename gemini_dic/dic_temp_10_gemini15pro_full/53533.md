disavouch:/ˌdɪsəˈvaʊtʃ/ | vt. |否认，拒绝承认， repudiate|To deny responsibility for or connection with; to refuse to acknowledge or support.|He disavouched any knowledge of the plot. 他否认对该阴谋有任何了解。|例句：The government disavouched the actions of its ambassador. 政府否认对其大使行为的责任。

例句：She disavouched her former friend after he was convicted of the crime. 在她的前朋友被判有罪后，她与他断绝了关系。

近义词：deny, repudiate, disclaim, renounce|反义词：acknowledge, admit, avow, confess|词性：动词
