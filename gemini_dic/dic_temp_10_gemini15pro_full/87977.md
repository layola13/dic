high-hatters:/ˈhaɪˌhætərz/| n. |自命不凡的人；摆架子的人|People who are snobbish and look down on others.  They act superior and condescending.|例句：The high-hatters at the gala wouldn't even acknowledge us.  gala晚会上那些自命不凡的人甚至都不理睬我们。

|例句：He doesn't have many friends because he's such a high-hatter. 他没有很多朋友，因为他太自命不凡了。|近义词：snobs, elitists, snooty people |反义词:humble people, down-to-earth people|词性:noun
