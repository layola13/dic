overdeep: /ˌoʊvərˈdiːp/ | adj. | 过深的，太深的 | Excessively deep; deeper than is usual or desirable. | The overdeep excavation posed a safety hazard. 过深的挖掘造成了安全隐患。 | The lake was overdeep for safe swimming. 这个湖太深了，不适合安全游泳。 | 近义词: excessively deep, profound, bottomless | 反义词: shallow | 词性: 形容词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "deep" (adjective meaning "extending far down from the top or surface").  "Over-" comes from Old English "ofer," and "deep" comes from Old English "deop."
