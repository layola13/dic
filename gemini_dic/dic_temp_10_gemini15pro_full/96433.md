indistinctive:/ˌɪndɪˈstɪŋktɪv/| adj.|无特色的，不明显的，难以区分的|Lacking distinguishing features or characteristics; not easily noticed or recognized.|The music was pleasant but indistinctive.  这音乐悦耳但没什么特色。|The twins wore indistinctive clothing so their teachers couldn't tell them apart. 这对双胞胎穿着毫无特色的衣服，所以老师们分不清他们。|近义词：nondescript, unremarkable, ordinary, common, average, run-of-the-mill, homogenous, characterless, featureless|反义词：distinctive, noticeable, remarkable, unique, characteristic, individual|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + distinctive (serving to distinguish).  The word "distinctive" itself comes from the Latin "distinguere" meaning "to separate, distinguish".
