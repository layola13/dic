leperous:/ˈlɛprəs/| adj.|麻风的；似麻风的；被社会排斥的；道德败坏的|Affected with, resembling, or suggestive of leprosy; (figurative) morally corrupt or outcast.|例句：The leperous beggar was shunned by the villagers.  麻风乞丐被村民们避之不及。

例句：The company's leperous reputation made it difficult to attract investors.  该公司声名狼藉，难以吸引投资者。|近义词：leprous, diseased, contaminated, corrupt, immoral, outcast, pariah|反义词：healthy, clean, pure, virtuous, moral, accepted|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *leprus, from Old French leprus, from Latin leprosus, from lepra (“leprosy”) + -osus (suffix forming adjectives meaning "full of, prone to, characterized by").  Ultimately from Greek λέπρα (lépra, “leprosy”).
