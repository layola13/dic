chanterelle:/ˌʃæntəˈrɛl/ | n. |鸡油菌；鸡油菇|A wild, edible mushroom, Cantharellus cibarius, with a distinctive funnel shape and yellow-orange color, known for its delicate flavor.  |例句：The chef sautéed the chanterelles in butter and garlic.  厨师用黄油和大蒜煎了鸡油菌。

例句：We found a patch of chanterelles growing under the oak trees. 我们在橡树下发现了一片鸡油菌。|近义词：girolle, golden chanterelle|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific type of mushroom) |词性:noun
