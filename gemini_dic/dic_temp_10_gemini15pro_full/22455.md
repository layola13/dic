bookkeepers:/ˈbʊkkiːpərz/
n.
簿记员，记账员
A person who records a business's financial transactions.
The bookkeepers meticulously recorded every transaction in the ledger.  簿记员一丝不苟地在账本上记录每一笔交易。
The company hired two new bookkeepers to manage its growing accounts. 公司雇佣了两名新的簿记员来管理其日益增长的账户。
accountant, clerk
manager, executive
noun
