non-scientific:/ˌnɒnˌsaɪənˈtɪfɪk/ /ˌnɑːnˌsaɪənˈtɪfɪk/
adjective
非科学的
Not based on or characterized by the methods or principles of science.
His approach to the problem was entirely non-scientific. 他解决这个问题的方法完全不科学。
近义词:unscientific, nontechnical, informal
反义词:scientific, technical, formal
词性:adjective
