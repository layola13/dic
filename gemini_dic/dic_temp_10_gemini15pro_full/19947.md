biodiesel:/ˈbaɪoʊˌdiːzəl/| n.|生物柴油|A fuel made from vegetable oils or animal fats, intended as a substitute for diesel.|Biodiesel is a renewable fuel source. 生物柴油是一种可再生燃料。| Many European countries are promoting the use of biodiesel. 许多欧洲国家正在推广使用生物柴油。|近义词:biofuel, renewable fuel|反义词:petrodiesel, fossil fuel|词性:名词
