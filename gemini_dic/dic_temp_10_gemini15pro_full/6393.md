amphigoric:/ˌæmfɪˈɡɔːrɪk/ | adj. n.| 隐晦的，模棱两可的；谜语(般的) |obscure or ambiguous in meaning; a riddle | The politician's speech was so amphigoric that no one could understand his stance on the issue. 这位政客的演讲含糊不清，以至于没有人能理解他对这个问题的立场。 | The sphinx posed an amphigoric riddle to Oedipus. 斯芬克斯向俄狄浦斯提出了一个谜语。 |近义词: enigmatic, obscure, ambiguous, puzzling, riddling |反义词: clear, lucid, unambiguous, straightforward |词性: adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *amphigouri*, from Italian *anfìguri*, of uncertain origin. Possibly from Greek *amphi* (around, about) + *gyros* (circle, ring), suggesting something convoluted or roundabout.
