confetto:/kənˈfɛtoʊ/ (US), /kənˈfetti/ (UK)| n.|五彩纸屑（单数），糖果（单数）|A small piece of brightly colored paper, or a sugar-coated almond, traditionally thrown at celebrations, especially weddings.|例句：Guests showered the happy couple with confetti. 客人们向幸福的夫妇撒了五彩纸屑。

例句：In Italy, sugared almonds, or confetti, are often given as wedding favors. 在意大利，糖衣杏仁或糖果通常作为婚礼礼物赠送。|近义词：sprinkles, decoration, sweet, candy|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


confetti:/kənˈfɛti/| n.|五彩纸屑（复数）|Small pieces of brightly colored paper, traditionally thrown at celebrations, especially weddings.|例句：The confetti rained down on the parade. 五彩纸屑像雨点般落在游行队伍上。


例句：The streets were covered in confetti after the New Year's Eve celebration.  新年夜庆祝活动后，街道上到处都是五彩纸屑。|近义词：sprinkles, decorations|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *confetto*, diminutive of *confezione* "sweetmeat," from Latin *conficere* "to make, prepare, put together, accomplish."
