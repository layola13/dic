escrow:/ˈɛsˌkroʊ/ (US), /ˈɛsˌkrəʊ/ (UK)| n. |第三方托管，暂管;  |A third party holds something (usually money or property) until certain conditions are met.|The buyer's deposit is held in escrow until the sale is finalized. 买方的定金由第三方保管，直到交易完成。|近义词：safekeeping, trust, custody|反义词:release, distribution|noun


escrow:/ˈɛsˌkroʊ/ (US), /ˈɛsˌkrəʊ/ (UK)| vt. |将…置于第三方托管|To place something in the care of a third party until certain conditions are met.|The funds were escrowed until the building was completed.  资金被托管，直到建筑物完工。|近义词：hold, deposit, entrust|反义词:release, distribute|verb
