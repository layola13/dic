fleckless:/ˈflɛklɪs/| adj.|无瑕的，无斑点的|Free from flecks; spotless.|Her fleckless complexion was the envy of all her friends. 她无瑕的肤色令所有朋友羡慕。|近义词：spotless, immaculate, unblemished, flawless|反义词：spotted, blemished, flawed, stained|词性:形容词
Etymology: From fleck + -less.  "Fleck" comes from Middle English flek, from Old Norse flekkr, meaning "spot" or "patch".
