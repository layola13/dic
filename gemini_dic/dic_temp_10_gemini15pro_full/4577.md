alcoholics: /ˌælkəˈhɔːlɪks/ | n. | 酒鬼；酗酒者 | People who are addicted to alcohol and suffer from alcoholism. |  Alcoholics Anonymous is a support group for people struggling with alcohol addiction. 匿名戒酒会是一个为与酒精成瘾作斗争的人们提供的支持小组。| synonyms:  drunkards, inebriates | antonyms: teetotalers, abstainers | noun

alcoholics: /ˌælkəˈhɔːlɪks/ | adj. | 酗酒的；酒精的 | Relating to or caused by alcoholism. | The alcoholic beverages were locked away.  酒精饮料被锁起来了。| synonyms: intoxicating | antonyms: non-alcoholic | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From alcohol + -ic + -s (plural suffix).  Alcohol ultimately derives from Arabic "al-kuḥl" (الكحل), referring to a fine powder used as eye makeup.  The sense of the word shifted to refer to distilled spirits and eventually to the intoxicating ingredient itself.
