nonlaminated:/ˌnɒnˈlæmɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
未层压的，非叠层的
not having been covered with a thin protective layer of plastic or other material
The doctor requested a nonlaminated X-ray film. 医生要求提供未层压的X光片。
The book cover is nonlaminated and therefore prone to wear and tear. 这本书的封面没有覆膜，因此容易磨损。
uncoated, unlaminated
laminated, coated
adjective
