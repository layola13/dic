batrachomyomachy:/ˌbætrəkoʊmaɪˈɒməki/ | n. | 蛙鼠之争 | A mock-heroic poem, or a petty quarrel over trivial matters.  It alludes to the ancient Greek mock-heroic poem Batrachomyomachia, which depicts a war between frogs and mice. | The argument over who left the milk out of the fridge escalated into a full-blown batrachomyomachy.  牛奶是谁没放进冰箱的争论升级成了一场彻头彻尾的蛙鼠之争。 | Example: Their dispute over the parking space was a ridiculous batrachomyomachy. 他们的停车位之争简直就是一场可笑的蛙鼠之争。| Synonyms: squabble, altercation, tiff | Antonyms: agreement, harmony, concord | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek βατραχομυομαχία (batrakhomuomakhia), from βάτραχος (batrakhos, “frog”) + μῦς (mus, “mouse”) + μάχη (makhē, “battle”).
