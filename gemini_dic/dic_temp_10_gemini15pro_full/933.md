abyss:/əˈbɪs/| n.|深渊，无底洞; 混乱; 极大的痛苦|a deep or seemingly bottomless chasm. A state of extreme misfortune or misery.|She stared into the abyss. 她凝视着深渊。|近义词：chasm, gorge, void, gulf|反义词:summit, peak|noun
