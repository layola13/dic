bramah:/ˈbrɑːmə/ | n. | 婆罗贺摩；梵天；一种高级锁 | A type of high-security lock, originally invented by Joseph Bramah.  It is also a variant spelling of Brahma, the Hindu god of creation. |  The antique desk was fitted with a Bramah lock. 这张古董桌子装有婆罗门锁。 | The Bramah is one of the principal deities of Hinduism. 梵天是印度教的主要神灵之一。| synonyms: lock (for the physical lock), Brahma (for the deity) | antonyms:  (none for the lock);  Shiva, Vishnu (as contrasting deities, not strict opposites) | noun

brahmah:/ˈbrɑːmə/ | n. | 婆罗门鸡 | A breed of large domestic fowl. |  The farmer raised prize-winning Brahmah chickens.  这位农民饲养了获奖的婆罗门鸡。| synonyms: chicken, fowl, poultry | antonyms: none | noun
