aromatic:/ˌærəˈmætɪk/ | n. | 芳香植物；芳香物质 | A plant or substance with a pleasant smell. | Many herbs and spices are aromatics. 许多草药和香料都是芳香植物。| fragrance, perfume, spice | stench | noun
