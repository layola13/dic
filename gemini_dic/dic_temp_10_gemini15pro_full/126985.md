nary:/ˈnɛəri/| adj.|没有的；几乎没有的|Not any; hardly any.  Derived from "ne'er a," a contraction of "never a."|例句：There wasn't a nary cloud in the sky. 天空中一丝云彩也没有。|例句：He had nary a penny to his name. 他身无分文。|近义词：no, not a, hardly any, scarcely any|反义词:many, several, some|词性:形容词
