multiareolate:/ˌmʌltiˈæriəˌleɪt/
adjective
多孔的，多小室的
Having multiple areolae (small cavities or spaces).  These areolae can be in various contexts, such as in biology (describing a structure) or in geology (describing a rock).
The fossilized sponge exhibited a multiareolate structure. 这块石化海绵展现出多孔结构。
The geologist identified the rock sample as multiareolate due to its numerous small cavities. 地质学家将这块岩石样本鉴定为多孔岩石，因为它有许多小孔洞。
近义词: porous, cellular, alveolate, fenestrate
反义词: nonporous, solid, compact
词性: adjective
词源: multi- (many) + areola (small open space) + -ate (adjective suffix)
