bulletins:/ˈbʊlətɪnz/ | n. | 公告；简报；新闻快报 | A short, official statement or broadcast summary of news. | The company released a series of bulletins about the merger. 公司发布了一系列关于合并的公告。 |  The evening news bulletins reported the developing crisis. 晚间新闻快报报道了正在发展的危机。 | announcements, reports, dispatches |  -- | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *bulletin, diminutive of bulle (“papal bull, edict”), from Latin bulla (“bubble, knob, round seal”).  The sense transferred from a sealed document to a short official statement.
