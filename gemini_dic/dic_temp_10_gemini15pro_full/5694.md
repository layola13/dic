aluminiferous:/ˌæljuːmɪˈnɪfərəs/
adjective
含铝的；产铝的
Containing or yielding aluminum.
Bauxite is an aluminiferous rock. 铝土矿是一种含铝的岩石。
This region is known for its aluminiferous deposits.  这个地区以其铝矿床而闻名。
synonyms: aluminum-bearing, aluminum-rich
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, consider "barren" or "depleted" in specific contexts)
词性: adjective
