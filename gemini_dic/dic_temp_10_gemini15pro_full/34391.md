noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From classy + -ness + -es (plural suffix). Classy originates from class, meaning "social rank," with the suffix -y indicating "having the quality of."  The suffix -ness forms nouns indicating a state or quality. Finally, -es creates the plural form.
