counterattack:/ˈkaʊntərətæk/
| n. v. |反击，反攻|An attack made in response to an attack by an opponent.  The general ordered a counterattack.|例句：The counterattack was successful in pushing back the enemy.  反击成功地击退了敌人。

例句：The team launched a counterattack in the second half. 球队在下半场发动了反攻。|近义词：offensive, retaliation, riposte|反义词：defense, retreat, withdrawal|词性: noun, verb


词源: counter- (against) + attack (to set upon with force)
