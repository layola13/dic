conspiringly:/kənˈspaɪərɪŋli/
adverb
阴谋地，密谋地
in a way that suggests secret planning with someone to do something wrong or illegal
They whispered conspiringly in the corner. 他们在角落里密谋地低声说话。
Synonyms: secretly, furtively, stealthily, clandestinely
Antonyms: openly, publicly, frankly


ETYMOLOGY->
From conspire + -ing + -ly.  Conspire comes from the Latin word "conspirare," meaning "to breathe together," implying a shared secret or plot.
