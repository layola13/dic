/ˈkænɪbəlaɪz/ | v. | 同类相食；拆用配件；损害…的销售；调拨…的资金 | to take parts from one machine to repair another; to reduce the sales of one product by introducing a similar, newer product; to take (something) away from one thing (such as one part of a company or system) in order to use it for something else| The company cannibalized its older models to produce the new one.  公司拆用了旧型号的配件来生产新的型号。| synonyms: strip, dismantle, usurp, appropriate | antonyms: assemble, construct, supplement | verb

/ˈkænɪbəlaɪz/ | n. |  (名词形式较少用，通常指同类相食的行为) | the act of cannibalizing | The cannibalization of the older product line led to a drop in overall profits. 旧产品线的拆用导致了整体利润的下降。 | synonyms: appropriation, usurpation | antonyms: construction, assembly | noun
