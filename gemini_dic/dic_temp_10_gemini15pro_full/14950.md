azymic:/əˈzɪmɪk/
adjective
无酵的；不发酵的
Unleavened; not containing yeast or other leavening agent.
The Jewish Passover commemorates the Israelites' hasty escape from Egypt, when they had no time to let their bread rise, hence the tradition of eating azymic matzah. 犹太逾越节是为了纪念以色列人匆忙逃离埃及，当时他们没有时间让面包发酵，因此有了吃无酵薄饼的传统。
The recipe called for azymic wafers.  这个食谱需要无酵薄饼。
近义词:unleavened
反义词:leavened
词性:adjective
词源:From Greek *azumos*, from *a-* "without" + *zumē* "leaven."
