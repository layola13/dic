ochrier:/ˈoʊkəriər/ | adj. | 赭色的，赭石色的 | Of a yellowish-orange color, like ochre. | The walls were painted an ochrier hue than I expected.  墙被漆成了比我预期的更赭的颜色。 |  近义词: ochreous, yellowish-brown, brownish-yellow, tawny | 反义词: bluish, greenish | 词性: adjective

词源: From ochre + -ier (a comparative suffix).  Ochre itself comes from the Old French ocre, ultimately from the Greek ōkhra, meaning "pale yellow earth".
