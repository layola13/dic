illustratory:/ɪˈlʌstrətɔːri/ /ɪˈlʌstreɪtɔːri/
adjective
说明性的；解说性的；作为例证的
serving as an example or explanation
The speaker showed an illustrative video to explain the complex process. 演讲者播放了一个说明性视频来解释这个复杂的过程。
The museum displayed illustrative examples of ancient pottery. 博物馆展出了古代陶器的说明性例子。
synonyms: explanatory, exemplifying, demonstrative, representative
antonyms: unrepresentative, misleading, confusing
词性: 形容词
