antisocietally: /ˌæntiˈsoʊʃəli/ | adv. | 反社会地 | In a manner that is harmful or disruptive to society; against the norms and values of society. |  His antisocially reckless driving endangered everyone on the road. 他反社会地鲁莽驾驶危及了路上的每个人。|  His antisocially loud music disturbed the entire neighborhood. 他反社会地大声播放音乐打扰了整个社区。| 近义词: disruptively, harmfully, detrimentally, destructively | 反义词: prosocially, constructively, helpfully | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From anti- (against) + social + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Anti-" comes from Greek "anti" (against, opposite). "Social" comes from Latin "socialis" (companionable, allied). "-ly" is a common adverbial suffix.
