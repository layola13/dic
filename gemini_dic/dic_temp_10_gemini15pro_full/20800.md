blas:/blæs/ | v. |炸，爆破；发出响而刺耳的声音；枯萎；使…凋谢|To destroy or damage something with explosives; to make a loud, harsh sound; to cause a plant to wither and die. |They blasted the rock face to build the road. 他们炸开岩壁来修路。|近义词：explode, detonate, criticize|反义词:build, construct|verb
