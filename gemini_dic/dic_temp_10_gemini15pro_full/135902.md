nonpsychically:/ˌnɑːnˈsaɪkɪkli/ | adv. | 非心灵地，非超自然地 | Not involving psychic or supernatural powers; by ordinary, natural means. | She solved the mystery nonpsychically, through careful observation and deduction. 她通过仔细的观察和推理，以非超自然的方式解开了这个谜团。|  The detective approached the case nonpsychically, relying on evidence and logic.  侦探以非超自然的方式处理案件，依靠证据和逻辑。| Synonyms: naturally, normally, ordinarily | Antonyms: psychically, supernaturally | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
* non-:  Latin prefix meaning "not".
* psychic: From Greek *psykhikos* "of the soul," from *psykhē* "soul, mind, spirit."
* -ally: Adverbial suffix.
