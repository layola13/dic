looseness:/ˈluːsnəs/| n.| 松弛，宽松，不严密|The looseness of the soil made it easy to dig. 土壤的松弛使得挖掘很容易。|例句：The looseness of the regulations allowed for some flexibility.  规定的松散性允许一些灵活性。

例句：There's a looseness in the plot that makes the film less compelling.  情节的松散使得这部电影不那么引人入胜。|近义词：slackness, laxity, flexibility|反义词:tightness, firmness, rigidity|词性:noun
词源:From Middle English *losenesse, equivalent to loose +‎ -ness.
