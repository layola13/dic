excorticate:/ɛksˈkɔːrtɪkeɪt/| vt.| 剥皮；严厉批评；痛斥 | To strip off the bark or rind; to denounce or criticize severely. | The critic excoriated the new movie, calling it "pretentious and derivative." 评论家严厉批评了这部新电影，称其为“自命不凡且缺乏 originality.” | 近义词：denounce, condemn, criticize, lambaste, castigate | 反义词：praise, commend, laud | verb

excorticate:/ɛksˈkɔːrtɪkeɪt/| vt.| 剥(树)皮；(医学)剥落 | To remove the cortex or outer layer of something, especially skin or bark.  | The gardener carefully excorticated the damaged bark from the tree. 园丁小心翼翼地从树上剥下受损的树皮.  | 近义词：peel, flay, strip | 反义词：cover, protect | verb
