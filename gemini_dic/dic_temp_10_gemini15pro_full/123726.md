moonsail:/ˈmuːnseɪl/| n.|月帆；太阳帆|A large, reflective sail used to propel a spacecraft by means of the pressure exerted by sunlight or, theoretically, moonlight.|例句：The moonsail, unfurling in the void, caught the faint light of the distant star. 月帆在虚空中展开，捕捉到了遥远恒星的微弱光芒。

例句：Scientists are exploring the feasibility of using a moonsail for interstellar travel. 科学家们正在探索使用月帆进行星际旅行的可行性。|近义词：solar sail, lightsail|反义词: (no direct antonym)|词性:noun
