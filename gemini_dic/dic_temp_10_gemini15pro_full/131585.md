noncompensations:/ˌnɒnkɒmpenˈseɪʃənz/ | n. | 无赔偿；不补偿；未补偿 | Lack of compensation or recompense for loss, damage, or injury.  |  The noncompensations for the victims of the disaster were appalling.  这场灾难的受害者没有得到任何补偿，令人震惊。|  nonpayment,  no recompense | compensation, recompense | noun
