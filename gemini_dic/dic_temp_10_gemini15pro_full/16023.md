banc:/bæŋk/ | n. |法官席，长凳; (尤指证券交易所的)交易场所，营业所| A judge's seat or the place where judges sit in court. Also, a place where financial transactions are conducted, especially a stock exchange. | The lawyers approached the banc. 律师们走近法官席。| The investment banc handled the company's IPO. 这家投资银行处理了该公司的首次公开募股。| synonyms: bench, trading floor, brokerage | antonyms: N/A | noun

banc:/bæŋk/ | n. | (尤指赌博用的)钱，赌本| A sum of money, especially one used for gambling. | He lost his entire banc at the casino. 他在赌场输光了所有的赌本。 | The poker players each put $100 into the banc. 扑克玩家每人拿出100美元作为赌注。 | synonyms: stake, kitty, pot | antonyms: winnings, profit | noun
