crustless:/ˈkrʌstlɪs/| adj.|无壳的，去壳的|Without a crust.  Referring typically to food items like pies or quiches that are made without a bottom or edge crust.|例句：I prefer crustless quiche because it's lighter. 我更喜欢无壳的乳蛋饼，因为它更清淡。|例句：She made a crustless apple pie for the picnic. 她为野餐做了一个无壳的苹果派。|近义词：uncrusted|反义词:crusted|词性:形容词
