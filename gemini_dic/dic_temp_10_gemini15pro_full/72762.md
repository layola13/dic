foggy:/ˈfɒɡi/ (UK) /ˈfɔːɡi/ (US)| adj.|有雾的，模糊的|Characterized by fog; indistinct or vague.|

例句：
The morning was foggy, and visibility was poor.
早晨雾气弥漫，能见度很低。

The details of the agreement remained foggy.
协议的细节仍然模糊不清。

近义词：misty, hazy, cloudy, obscure, vague|
反义词：clear, sunny, distinct|
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From fog + -y.  "fog" comes from Old English *fogga, of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old Norse fūk "drifting snow."
