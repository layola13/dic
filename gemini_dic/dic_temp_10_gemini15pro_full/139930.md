odists:/ˈoʊdɪsts/| n. |颂诗作者；歌唱家；作诗者|A person who writes or sings odes (lyrics that express noble feelings).|The ancient Greek odists were highly respected for their lyrical abilities. 古希腊的颂诗作者因其抒情能力而备受尊敬。|The odist composed a moving tribute to the fallen hero.  这位歌唱家为牺牲的英雄谱写了一首感人的颂歌。|Synonyms: poet, lyricist, singer, bard|Antonyms: N/A|noun
