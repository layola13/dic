glutted:/ˈɡlʌtɪd/
adjective
充满的，过饱的
supplied or filled to excess.
The market is glutted with cheap imports. 市场充斥着廉价的进口商品。
近义词：sated, surfeited, gorged, overflowing
反义词：depleted, lacking, scarce
词性: adjective

glutted:/ˈɡlʌtɪd/
verb
充斥，过饱
supply or fill to excess.
The warehouses were glutted with unsold goods. 仓库里堆满了未售出的货物。
近义词：flood, inundate, saturate, overload
反义词：deplete, drain, empty
词性: past participle of glut


ETYMOLOGY->
From glut (v.) + -ed.  The verb "glut" comes from the Old French "glotir" (to swallow, gulp down), ultimately from the Latin "glutire" (to swallow).
