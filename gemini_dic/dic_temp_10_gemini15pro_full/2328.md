adherence:/ədˈhɪərəns/ | n. | 坚持；遵守；依附；粘附 |  The doctor emphasized the importance of adherence to the prescribed medication. 医生强调了坚持服用处方药的重要性。 |  例句：Strict adherence to the rules is essential. 严格遵守规则是必要的。

Her adherence to a vegan diet is impressive. 她对纯素食的坚持令人印象深刻。 | 近义词：compliance, conformity, obedience, attachment, adhesion | 反义词：noncompliance, disobedience, detachment | 词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adhaerentia, from Latin adhaerēns, present participle of adhaerēre "to stick to," from ad- "to" + haerēre "to stick."
