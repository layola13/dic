germinated: /ˈdʒɜːrmɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
发芽的
having put forth shoots from a seed or spore; begun to grow.
The germinated seeds were ready to be planted.  发芽的种子可以播种了。
近义词: sprouted, burgeoning, nascent
反义词: dormant, undeveloped, ungerminated
词性: adjective

germinate: /ˈdʒɜːrmɪneɪt/
verb
发芽
(of a seed or spore) begin to grow and put out shoots after a period of dormancy.
The seeds will germinate in a few days.  这些种子几天后就会发芽。
近义词: sprout, burgeon, develop
反义词: wither, die, stagnate
词性: verb


Etymology: From Latin germinatus, past participle of germinare "to sprout," from germen "bud, sprout."
