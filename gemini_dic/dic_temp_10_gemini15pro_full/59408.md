earthdrake:/ˈɜːrθˌdreɪk/| n. |地龙; 土龙; 大地之龙|A mythical creature, typically depicted as a large serpentine or draconic being associated with the earth element.  A creature of fantasy, often found in role-playing games and fantasy literature.|The earthdrake burrowed deep beneath the mountains. 地龙钻入群山深处。|The adventurers battled a fearsome earthdrake guarding a hoard of treasure.  冒险者们与守护着一堆宝藏的可怕地龙战斗。|近义词：wyrm, dragon, lindworm|反义词: (No direct antonym for a mythical creature.  Could consider celestial beings or creatures of air/fire as opposing elements.)|词性:noun
