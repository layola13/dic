mygale:/ˈmaɪɡeɪl/ | n. | 鸟蛛，一种大型多毛蜘蛛 | A large, hairy spider, especially of the family Theraphosidae. |  My uncle keeps a mygale as a pet, which frightens me. 我叔叔养了一只鸟蛛当宠物，这让我很害怕。| Mygale spiders are often kept as exotic pets. 鸟蛛经常被当作异国宠物饲养。 | 近义词: tarantula, bird-eating spider | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

词源: From French *mygale*, from Ancient Greek *μυγαλέη* (*mygaléē*) meaning "shrew" or "field mouse".  The term was originally applied to shrews and then later transferred to spiders due to the hairy appearance of some species.
