misprovokes:/ˌmɪsprəˈvoʊks/
verb
激怒；挑衅
To provoke wrongly or mistakenly; to incite to anger or resentment through error or misunderstanding.
His clumsy apology only misprovoked his already angry wife. 他笨拙的道歉反而激怒了他已经生气的妻子。
近义词: provoke, irritate, annoy, exasperate
反义词: soothe, calm, placate
词性: verb
