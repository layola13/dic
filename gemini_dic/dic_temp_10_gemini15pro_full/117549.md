megabyte:/ˈmɛɡəˌbaɪt/ /ˈmeɡəˌbaɪt/| n.|兆字节，百万字节|A megabyte is a unit of information equal to 2^20 bytes or, loosely, one million bytes. |例句：This file is about 5 megabytes.  这个文件大约5兆字节。

例句：My hard drive has a capacity of one terabyte, which is equivalent to 1024 gigabytes or roughly a million megabytes. 我的硬盘容量为1TB，相当于1024GB或大约一百万MB。|近义词：MB|反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement)|词性:noun
