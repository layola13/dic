antiquarian:/ˌæn.tɪˈkweə.ri.ən/
n.|古董收藏家；古物研究者|A person who studies or collects antiques or antiquities.|He was a renowned antiquarian with a vast collection of Roman artifacts. 他是一位著名的古董收藏家，收藏了大量的罗马文物。|Synonyms: collector, connoisseur, archaeologist|Antonyms: modernist, contemporary|Noun

antiquarian:/ˌæn.tɪˈkweə.ri.ən/
adj.|古董的；古物研究的|Relating to or dealing in antiques or antiquities.|She inherited her grandmother's antiquarian books. 她继承了祖母的古董书籍。|Synonyms: ancient, antique, archaic|Antonyms: modern, contemporary|Adjective
