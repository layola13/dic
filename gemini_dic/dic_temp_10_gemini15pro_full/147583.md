overphilosophize: /ˌoʊvərˈfɪləˌsɑːfaɪz/ | v. | 过度哲学化；过度思辨 | To think about or discuss something in a way that is too abstract, complex, or elaborate, often to the point of inaction or confusion. | He tends to overphilosophize simple problems, making them seem more complicated than they are. 他倾向于过度思辨简单的问题，使它们看起来比实际更复杂。 |  She overphilosophized her breakup, analyzing every detail until she was exhausted. 她过度分析了她的分手，分析每一个细节直到筋疲力尽。 | 近义词: overthink, overanalyze, intellectualize | 反义词: simplify,  underthink | 词性: 动词
