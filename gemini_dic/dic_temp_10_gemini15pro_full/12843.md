associative:/əˈsoʊʃiətɪv/ /əˈsəʊʃɪətɪv/| adj.|联想的，关联的|Relating to or involving association or the power of association.|例句：Associative learning is a powerful tool. 联想学习是一个强大的工具。|例句：The brain's associative areas are responsible for higher-level thinking. 大脑的联想区域负责高级思维。|近义词：related, connected, linked|反义词：unrelated, disconnected, separate|词性:形容词

associative:/əˈsoʊʃiətɪv/ /əˈsəʊʃɪətɪv/| n.|结合律|A property in mathematics, especially in the study of binary operations, where the order of operations does not affect the result. (Specifically, for an operation *,  (a * b) * c = a * (b * c)).|例句：Addition is an associative operation. 加法是一个结合律运算。|例句：Matrix multiplication is associative but not commutative. 矩阵乘法满足结合律但不满足交换律。|近义词：commutative (sometimes)|反义词：non-associative|词性:名词
