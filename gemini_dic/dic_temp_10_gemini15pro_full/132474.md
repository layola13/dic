nonderivatives:/ˌnɒndɪˈrɪvətɪvz/ | n. | 非衍生品；非衍生词 |  A nonderivative is a financial security whose value isn't derived from another asset or security. In linguistics, a nonderivative word is a root word, not formed from another word. | Example 1: Gold is considered a nonderivative asset. 黄金被认为是一种非衍生资产。

Example 2: The word "book" is a nonderivative. “book”这个词是一个非衍生词。 | Synonyms: (Finance) underlying asset, primary asset; (Linguistics) root word, base word | Antonyms: (Finance) derivative, secondary asset; (Linguistics) derivative word, derived word | noun
