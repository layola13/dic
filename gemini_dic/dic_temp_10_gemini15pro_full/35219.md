clued:/kluːd/ | adj. | 知情的，了解情况的；线索的 | Having information or knowledge about something, especially something secret or hidden.  Informed about something. Often used in the passive form. | He was clued in on the latest developments. 他了解最新的进展。| The detective was clued into the suspect's alibi.  侦探了解了嫌疑人的不在场证明。 | synonyms: informed, aware, in the know, knowledgeable, apprised | antonyms: unaware, ignorant, uninformed | adjective
