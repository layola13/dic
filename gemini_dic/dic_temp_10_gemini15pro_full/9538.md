antisensuous:/ˌæntiˈsɛnʃuəs/
adjective
反感官的，禁欲的
opposed or hostile to the gratification of the senses; ascetic.
He led an antisensuous life, rejecting all forms of luxury and pleasure. 他过着禁欲的生活，拒绝一切形式的奢华和享乐。

近义词:ascetic, abstinent, austere
反义词:sensual, hedonistic, indulgent
词性:adjective
