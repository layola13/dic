biquadratic:/ˌbaɪkwɒˈdrætɪk/ /ˌbaɪkwɑːˈdrætɪk/| adj. n.|双二次的，四次的；双二次方程式|Of or relating to the fourth degree.  A biquadratic equation is one in which the highest power of the unknown quantity is the fourth power.|This equation is biquadratic. 这个方程式是四次的。|近义词：quartic|反义词:|adjective, noun

词性
adjective

词性
noun

词源
From bi- + quadratic.
