bipinnate:/baɪˈpɪneɪt/| adj.|二回羽状的|Twice pinnate; having leaflets arranged along each side of a secondary axis which itself branches from the main petiole.  It refers to a leaf structure where the leaflets are themselves pinnately divided.  Think of a fern frond where the divisions are divided again.|The bipinnate leaves of the Jacaranda tree offer dappled shade. 蓝花楹的二回羽状叶子提供了斑驳的树荫。|The sensitive fern exhibits beautiful, bipinnate fronds.  敏感蕨展示出美丽的二回羽状叶子。|近义词:bipinnatifid|反义词:simple|词性:形容词
