词源:  From the Middle English *as a reule*, meaning "according to a regulation."  The word "rule" ultimately comes from the Latin *regula* meaning "straight stick, bar, ruler."


注意事项:  While "as a rule" implies a common practice, it allows for exceptions.  It does not mean "always" or "without exception."
