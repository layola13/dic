misinform:/ˌmɪsɪnˈfɔːrm/ /ˌmɪsɪnˈfɔːrm/ | vt. | 误报；误导；提供错误信息 | To give someone incorrect information. | The public was misinformed about the true nature of the event. 公众被误导了关于事件的真实性质。

| The article misinformed readers about the health benefits of the product. 这篇文章向读者提供了关于该产品健康益处的错误信息。

| 近义词：deceive, mislead | 反义词：inform, enlighten | 词性：动词
