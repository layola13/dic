handwrite:/ˈhændraɪt/ | v. |手写|to write something by hand instead of using a typewriter or computer|She handwrote a letter to her grandmother. 她亲手给祖母写了一封信。|近义词: pen, script, inscribe |反义词: type, print |动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From hand + write.  "hand" comes from Old English "hand," and "write" comes from Old English "wrītan."
