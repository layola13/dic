implicitly:/ɪmˈplɪsɪtli/| adv.|含蓄地，暗中地|Suggested but not directly expressed; understood though not plainly stated.|She implicitly criticized the director by refusing to follow his instructions. 她拒绝遵从导演的指示，含蓄地批评了他。|implicitly acknowledges the limitations of the study. 该研究含蓄地承认了其局限性。|Synonym: tacitly, indirectly, by implication|Antonym: explicitly, directly, overtly|adverb
