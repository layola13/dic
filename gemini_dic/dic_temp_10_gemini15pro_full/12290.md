ascidioidea:/æsɪdiˈɔɪdiə/ | n. |海鞘纲|A class of marine invertebrate animals, commonly known as sea squirts, belonging to the subphylum Tunicata. They are characterized by a sac-like body and two siphons used for filter feeding.|Sea squirts, members of the Ascidioidea class, are fascinating creatures. 海鞘，海鞘纲的成员，是迷人的生物。|近义词: tunicates|反义词: None|noun

ascidioidean:/æsɪdiˈɔɪdiən/ | adj. |海鞘纲的|Relating to or belonging to the class Ascidioidea.|The ascidioidean larva undergoes a dramatic metamorphosis. 海鞘幼虫经历了剧烈的变态。|近义词: tunicate|反义词: None|adjective
