bereaved: /bɪˈriːvd/ | adj. n. | 丧失亲友的，失去亲人的 | Deprived of a close relation or friend through their death.  Someone who is bereaved has recently lost a loved one. |  She was still bereaved after the death of her husband. 她仍然沉浸在丧夫之痛中。| The bereaved family gathered for the funeral.  这个失去亲人的家庭聚集在一起参加葬礼。 | heartbroken, grieving, mourning |  joyful, celebrating | adjective, noun
