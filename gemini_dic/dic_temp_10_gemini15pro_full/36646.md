collects:/kəˈlekts/
verb
收集，采集
gathers or accumulates something.
He collects stamps. 他收集邮票。
The museum collects antique toys.  这家博物馆收集古董玩具。
synonyms: gathers, accumulates, amasses, assembles
antonyms: disperses, scatters, distributes
词性: 动词(third-person singular present)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *collectus*, past participle of *colligere* "to gather together," from *com-* "together" + *legere* "to gather, select."
