gadder:/ˈɡædər/| n.|闲逛者，社交爱好者|A person who goes to a lot of social events, especially ones where they can meet important or fashionable people.  Someone who spends much time gadding about.|例句：She's such a gadder; she's always out at parties and events. 她真是个社交迷，总是参加各种派对和活动。
