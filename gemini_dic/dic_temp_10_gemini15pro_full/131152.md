nonbookishly:/ˌnɒnˈbʊkɪʃli/ | adv. | 非书本式地，不学究地 |  In a manner that is not characteristic of someone who spends a lot of time reading or studying; in a practical or worldly way. | He approached the problem nonbookishly, relying on his experience rather than theory.  他以非书本的方式处理这个问题，依靠的是他的经验而不是理论。 | Synonyms: practically, pragmatically, realistically | Antonyms: academically, theoretically, scholastically | 词性: 副词

nonbookishly:/ˌnɒnˈbʊkɪʃli/ | adv. | 非书本式地，不学究地 | She explained the complex concept nonbookishly, making it easy for everyone to understand. 她用非书本的方式解释了这个复杂的概念，使每个人都容易理解。 | Synonyms: simply, plainly, straightforwardly | Antonyms: eruditely, pedantically, abstrusely | 词性: 副词
