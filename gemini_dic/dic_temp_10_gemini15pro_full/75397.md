forth:/fɔːrθ/
|adv.|向前；向外；往外；出现；涌现|
|Meaning: Out into view; forward in place, time, or order.|
|Example 1:  She stepped forth from the shadows. 她从阴影中走了出来。|

|Example 2: From that day forth, he never spoke of it again. 从那天起，他再也没有提起过这件事。|
|Synonyms: forward, onward, ahead, out, into view|
|Antonyms: back, backward, inwards|
|Word Type: Adverb|
