brocatello:/ˌbrɒkəˈtɛloʊ/| n.|织锦大理石；金线大理石|A richly patterned marble, typically reddish-brown with white or yellow veining, resembling brocade.|The palace was adorned with rare brocatello columns. 宫殿装饰着稀有的织锦大理石柱。|近义词：marble, limestone|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑一些质地完全不同的石头，例如 granite 花岗岩)|词性:noun
