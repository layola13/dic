entomere:/ˈentəˌmɪər/| n. |体节，昆虫体节|A body segment of an insect or other arthropod. |The thorax of an insect is composed of three entomeres.  昆虫的胸部由三个体节组成。|The abdomen of this insect has ten distinct entomeres.  这只昆虫的腹部有十个明显的体节。|近义词：segment, somite, metamere|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
