hairier:/ˈheəriər/ (US), /ˈhɛːrɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|毛发更多的；更毛茸茸的|Covered with more hair; more hairy than something/someone else.|例句：The dog is hairier than the cat. 这只狗比猫毛多。

例句：My legs are hairier in the winter. 我的腿在冬天毛发更多。

近义词：shaggier, furrier, woollier|反义词:smoother, balder, less hairy|词性:形容词
