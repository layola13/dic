inflaming:/ɪnˈfleɪmɪŋ/ | v. | 激怒；使加剧；使发炎 |  Arousing or intensifying strong feelings, especially anger or excitement; causing inflammation in a part of the body. |  The politician's speech was inflaming tensions between the two countries.  这位政治家的演讲加剧了两国间的紧张局势。

His words were inflaming the already tense situation. 他的话使原本就紧张的局势火上浇油。 | 近义词：aggravating, exacerbating, inciting, irritating | 反义词：soothing, calming, alleviating | 词性: 动词

inflaming:/ɪnˈfleɪmɪŋ/ | adj. |  煽动性的；激怒人的；炎症性的 | Tending to arouse strong feelings, especially anger or excitement; causing inflammation. | The inflaming rhetoric of the speaker caused a riot. 演讲者煽动性的言辞引发了一场骚乱。

The inflaming article caused a lot of controversy. 这篇煽动性的文章引起了很多争议。 | 近义词：inflammatory, provocative, incendiary | 反义词：soothing, calming, placatory | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Middle English *inflamen, from Latin inflammare, from in- ‘in, into’ + flamma ‘flame’.
