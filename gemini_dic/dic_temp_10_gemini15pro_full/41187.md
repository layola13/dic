词源：
From copy + right.  The original sense was the right to copy a book (granted by the Stationers' Company).  The modern sense of the author's right to control the reproduction of their work developed later.  由 copy（复制）+ right（权利）组成。最初的含义是由（Stationers' Company）授予的复制书籍的权利。后来发展为作者控制其作品复制的权利的现代含义。
