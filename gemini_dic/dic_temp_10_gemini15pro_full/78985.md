girth:/ɡɜːrθ/ /ɡɝːθ/
n.
周长，围长；腰围
The distance around something; circumference. Specifically refers to the measurement around the middle of a person or object.
The tailor measured the girth of his customer's waist.  裁缝量了顾客的腰围。

The girth of the tree was so large that it took three people to hug it. 这棵树的周长如此之大，以至于需要三个人才能合抱它。

近义词: circumference, perimeter, size
反义词: length, height


词性: noun
词源: From Old Norse *gjǫrð, meaning "girdle, belt." Related to Old English gyrdan "to gird."
