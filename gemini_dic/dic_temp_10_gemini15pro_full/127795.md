neighbouring:/ˈneɪbəriŋ/ | adj. | 邻近的，附近的 | Located near or next to something else. |  Example: We visited the neighbouring town. 我们参观了邻近的城镇。

Example:  Neighbouring countries often share cultural similarities.  邻国通常有相似的文化。

| 近义词: adjacent, adjoining, nearby, surrounding | 反义词: distant, remote | 词性: 形容词
