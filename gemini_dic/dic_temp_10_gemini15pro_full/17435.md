beanfests:/ˈbiːnfɛsts/
n.
豆宴；聚会，欢乐的集会
A large, usually informal, gathering or celebration, often characterized by plentiful food and drink.
例句：The annual family reunion is always a big beanfest.  每年的家庭聚会总是一场盛大的聚餐。

例句：The office beanfest was a great way to boost morale. 办公室的聚会是提高士气的好方法。

近义词：feast, party, celebration, get-together, bash
反义词：wake, funeral

词性: noun
