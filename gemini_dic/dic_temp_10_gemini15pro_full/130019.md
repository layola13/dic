non-operational:/ˌnɒnˌɒpəˈreɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 非运行的；不能运转的 | Not in operation; not working or functioning. | The factory was rendered non-operational after the fire. 工厂在大火之后无法运转了。

The old computer system is now non-operational. 旧的计算机系统现在不能运行了。


| 近义词: inoperative, defunct, out of service, disabled | 反义词: operational, working, functioning | 词性: adjective
