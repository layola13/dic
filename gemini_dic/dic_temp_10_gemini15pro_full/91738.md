hyperdeveloped:/ˌhaɪpərdiˈvɛləpt/ | adj. | 过度发达的；高度发展的 | Excessively or extremely developed, often beyond a point of sustainability or with negative consequences. |  The city's infrastructure is hyperdeveloped, but its social services are lacking.  这个城市的基础设施过度发达，但社会服务却很缺乏。| Synonyms: overdeveloped, excessively developed | Antonyms: underdeveloped, undeveloped | Adjective


Etymology:
hyper- (prefix): from Greek *hupér "over, above, beyond"
developed (past participle of develop): from Old French desveloper "to unfold, unwrap," from des- "un-, out" + voloper "to wrap"
