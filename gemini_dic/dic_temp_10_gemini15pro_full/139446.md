obsolete:/ˌɒbsəˈliːt/ (UK) /ˌɑːbsəˈliːt/ (US)| adj.|过时的，废弃的|No longer produced or used; out of date.|例句：Typewriters are now largely obsolete. 打字机现在基本上已经过时了。|近义词：outdated, archaic, antiquated, old-fashioned, disused, outmoded|反义词: modern, current, contemporary, up-to-date|词性:形容词

obsolete:/ˌɒbsəˈliːt/ (UK) /ˌɑːbsəˈliːt/ (US)| vt.|使过时，淘汰|To make something obsolete by introducing something new.|例句：New technology is rapidly obsoleting existing equipment. 新技术正在迅速淘汰现有设备。|近义词：replace, supersede, displace|反义词: update, modernize|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin obsoletus (“worn out, grown old, decayed”), past participle of obsolescere (“to fall into disuse, decay”), from ob- (“away, down”) + solescere (“to become accustomed”), inchoative of solere (“to be accustomed”).
