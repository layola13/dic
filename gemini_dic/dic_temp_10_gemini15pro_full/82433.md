gruffly:/ˈɡrʌfli/| adv.|粗暴地，生硬地|In a rough, harsh, or surly manner.|"He answered gruffly, clearly annoyed by the interruption." 他粗暴地回答，显然是被打扰了。|例句：The guard gruffly told them to leave. 警卫粗暴地叫他们离开。|近义词：curtly, abruptly, roughly, harshly, sternly|反义词：gently, kindly, politely|词性:副词
Etymology: From gruff + -ly.  Gruff comes from Middle Dutch *grof, related to Low German groff "coarse, rough."
