forgoes:/fɔːrˈɡoʊz/
verb
放弃，摒弃
To forgo something means to give it up or do without it.
She forgoed dessert because she was trying to lose weight. 她为了减肥而放弃了甜点。
synonyms: relinquish, abstain from, give up, waive, sacrifice
antonyms: indulge in, partake of, accept, embrace
词性:动词
