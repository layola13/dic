brittle:/ˈbrɪtl/| adj.|易碎的，脆弱的|Easily broken or cracked; fragile.  Likely to snap or break under pressure or stress.  Can also refer to a person's emotional state, meaning easily upset or offended.|The old vase was so brittle that it crumbled at the slightest touch.  这个旧花瓶太脆了，轻轻一碰就碎了。

The dry leaves were brittle and crumbled underfoot.  干燥的叶子很脆，在脚下嘎吱作响。|近义词：fragile, frail, delicate, breakable|反义词：flexible, resilient, tough, strong|词性:形容词
词源: From Old English *bryttian "to break, shatter," from Proto-Germanic *breutan (source also of Old Saxon brutian, Old Frisian britta, Middle Dutch broten, Old High German brestan, German brechen "to break").
