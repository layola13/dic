hypostatize:/haɪˈpɒstətaɪz/ /haɪˈpɑːstətaɪz/| v. |使实体化；使具体化；把…看作实体|To treat or regard (an abstract quality or concept) as if it were a concrete reality.|He tends to hypostatize the forces of history. 他倾向于将历史的力量实体化。|近义词：reify, embody, personify|反义词: abstract|词性:动词

hypostatization:/haɪˌpɒstətɪˈzeɪʃn/  /haɪˌpɑːstətɪˈzeɪʃn/| n. |实体化；具体化|The act of hypostatizing, or regarding something abstract as a material thing.|The hypostatization of abstract concepts can lead to misunderstandings. 将抽象概念实体化会导致误解。|近义词：reification, embodiment, personification|反义词: abstraction|词性:名词
