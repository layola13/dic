altercation:/ˌɔːltərˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |争吵，口角|A noisy argument or disagreement, especially in public.|例句：He got into an altercation with another driver over a parking space. 他因为停车位与另一名司机发生争吵。

例句：The bar was noisy, filled with the sounds of laughter and occasional altercations. 酒吧里很吵闹，充满了笑声和偶尔的争吵声。|近义词：argument, quarrel, dispute, squabble, fight, clash, brawl|反义词：agreement, harmony, concord, peace|词性：名词
