overtreat:/ˌōvərˈtrēt/| v. |过度治疗|To treat (a medical condition) excessively or more intensively than is necessary or beneficial.|The doctor warned against overtreating the patient with antibiotics. 医生警告不要过度使用抗生素治疗病人。|Overtreatment with antibiotics can lead to drug-resistant bacteria.  抗生素的过度使用会导致耐药菌。|近义词：overmedicate|反义词:undertreat|词性:动词
