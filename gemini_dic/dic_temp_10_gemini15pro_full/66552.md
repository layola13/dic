exhibited:/ɪɡˈzɪbɪtɪd/
verb
展出, 展示, 表现
past tense and past participle of exhibit  - showed something publicly
The museum exhibited a collection of ancient artifacts.  博物馆展出了一批古代文物。
Her paintings were exhibited in a local gallery. 她的画作在当地一家画廊展出。
近义词:displayed, shown, presented, demonstrated
反义词:concealed, hidden, withheld
词性:verb (past participle)

exhibit:/ɪɡˈzɪbɪt/
verb
展出, 展示, 表现
to show something publicly
The museum will exhibit the new sculptures next month. 博物馆下个月将展出新的雕塑作品。
He exhibited great courage in the face of danger. 面对危险，他表现出了极大的勇气。
近义词:display, show, present, demonstrate
反义词:conceal, hide, withhold
词性:verb

exhibit:/ɪɡˈzɪbɪt/
noun
展品, 展览
an object or collection of objects put on public display
The dinosaur exhibit at the museum is very popular. 博物馆里的恐龙展很受欢迎。
Her artwork was the main exhibit at the gallery. 她的艺术作品是画廊的主要展品。
近义词:display, exhibition, show
反义词: N/A
词性:noun
