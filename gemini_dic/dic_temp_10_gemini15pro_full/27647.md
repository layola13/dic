candicant:/ˈkændɪkənt/| adj.| 洁白的，耀眼的，白热化的| Glowing white with heat; incandescent.  Shining brilliantly; dazzling.| The candicant moon illuminated the night sky. 皎洁的月亮照亮了夜空。| 近义词：incandescent, luminous, brilliant, dazzling| 反义词：dim, dull, dark| 词性: 形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *candicant-, present participle of *candicare "to glow white," from candēre "to glow, to shine."
