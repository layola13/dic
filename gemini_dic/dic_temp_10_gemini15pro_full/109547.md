lessness:/ləsnəs/| n.| 无，缺乏，没有 | absence, lack of something; state of being without something |  Her fearlessness impressed everyone. 她的无畏给每个人都留下了深刻的印象。| synonyms: absence, lack, dearth, deficiency, deprivation | antonyms: presence, abundance, fullness, completeness | suffix

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *lēasnes, from lēas (“false, untrue; devoid of, lacking; wicked, evil”) + -nes, equivalent to less + -ness. Cognate with Scots lessness, lessnes (“lessness”).
