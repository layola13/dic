accidentals: /ˌæk.sɪˈden.təlz/
noun
临时记号;  意外事件;
Symbols used in musical notation to raise or lower the pitch of a note, or to cancel a previous accidental. Also, in a more general sense, notes that are not part of a key signature but occur within a piece of music.
The pianist played the piece flawlessly, even the complex accidentals.  这位钢琴家完美地演奏了这首曲子，即使是复杂的临时记号也演奏得很好。
Sharps, flats, and naturals are examples of accidentals. 升号、降号和还原号都是临时记号的例子。
近义词: sharps, flats, naturals
反义词: key signature
词性: noun
