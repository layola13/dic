outroad:/ˈaʊtroʊd/
n.
通路，出口，出路
A way out; an exit.
The hikers searched for an outroad from the dense forest.  徒步旅行者在茂密的森林中寻找出路。
exit, escape route, way out
dead end, impasse
noun
