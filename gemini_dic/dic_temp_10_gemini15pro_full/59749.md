ecology: /ɪˈkɒlədʒi/ (UK) /iˈkɑːlədʒi/ (US)| n. |生态学；生态环境|The study of the relationships between organisms and their environment.|例句：The ecology of the rainforest is incredibly diverse. 雨林的生态极其多样。|例句：Urban development is having a negative impact on the local ecology. 城市发展对当地生态环境产生了负面影响。|近义词：environment, ecosystem, biosphere|反义词: N/A | noun
