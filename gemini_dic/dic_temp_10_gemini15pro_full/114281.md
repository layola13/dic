mainland:/ˈmeɪnlənd/ | n. |大陆，本土|The bridge connects the island to the mainland. 这座桥连接着岛屿和大陆。|Etymology: From Middle English *maynland, equivalent to main +‎ land.  Originally meaning 'main land', distinguishing the principal part of a territory from outlying islands or other detached portions.|Examples: Many tourists come from the mainland. 许多游客来自大陆。  Goods are shipped to the island from the mainland. 货物从大陆运到岛上。|Synonyms: continent, mainland|Antonyms: island, isle|Noun
