mammonisms:/ˈmæmənɪzəmz/
n.
拜金主义；贪财；唯利是图
Excessive devotion to or pursuit of wealth, often at the expense of ethical considerations.  The worship of riches.
He railed against the mammonisms of modern society. 他痛斥现代社会的拜金主义。
materialism, avarice, greed, acquisitiveness
altruism, generosity, philanthropy
noun
