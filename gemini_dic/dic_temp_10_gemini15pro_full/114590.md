maldanian: /mælˈdeɪniən/ | adj. n. | 马尔丹蠕虫的；马尔丹蠕虫 | Relating to or denoting maldanid worms, a family of segmented marine worms.  A maldanid worm. |  例句：Maldanian worms are common in marine sediments. 马尔丹蠕虫在海洋沉积物中很常见。

例句：The study focused on the feeding habits of maldanian worms. 这项研究的重点是马尔丹蠕虫的摄食习性。| 近义词: maldanid | 反义词:  (无明确的反义词) | 词性:  adjective, noun

Etymology:  From Maldane, the genus name of a type of maldanid worm,  + -ian.  Maldane is from the name of a mythological figure, a sea nymph.
