aeriferous:/ˌɛərˈɪfərəs/
adjective
含气的；输送空气的
Containing air; conveying air.
The aeriferous chambers of the lungs allow for efficient gas exchange.  肺部充满空气的腔室允许有效的气体交换。
The ventilation system uses aeriferous ducts to circulate fresh air throughout the building.  通风系统使用输送空气的管道在整个建筑物内循环新鲜空气。
近义词: airy, pneumatic
反义词: airless, airtight
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *aerifer* (air-bearing), from *aer* (air) + *-fer* (bearing, carrying).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂éws- (to breathe, air).

USAGE->
The aeriferous qualities of the material make it ideal for insulation.  这种材料的透气性使其成为理想的绝缘材料。
Aeriferous balloons floated gracefully across the sky.  充满空气的气球优雅地飘过天空。
