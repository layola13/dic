come:/kʌm/ (US) /kʌm/ (UK)| v. | 来，来到，到达，出现，发生，开始，变成 | A basic verb indicating movement towards the speaker or a specified place or state.  |

例句：
I will come to your party tonight.
今晚我会来参加你的派对。

He came to the realization that he was wrong.
他意识到自己错了。


近义词：arrive, approach, appear | 反义词: go, leave, depart | 词性: verb
