mis-writes:/ˌmɪsˈraɪts/| v.|写错，误写|He often mis-writes common words due to carelessness. 他经常因为粗心而写错常见的单词。|misspells, misspells|correctly writes|verb
Etymology:  From mis- (prefix meaning 'wrongly' or 'badly') + write. The prefix "mis-" comes from Old English "mis-" and ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*missa-", meaning "wrong," "amiss," or "bad." "Write" comes from Old English "wrītan," from Proto-Germanic "*writan," meaning "to tear, scratch, draw, write."
