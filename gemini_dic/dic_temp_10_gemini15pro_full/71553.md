flash-out:/ˈflæʃ aʊt/ | v. | 冲洗；清除；（使）突然出现；详细阐述 | To remove something by flushing with liquid; to suddenly appear or become visible; to develop or explain something in more detail. | 例句：The police flashed out their badges. 警察亮出了他们的警徽。 |例句：He flashed out a wad of cash. 他突然拿出一叠现金。|近义词：flush, rinse, elaborate, develop | 反义词：conceal, hide |词性：动词

flash-out:/ˈflæʃ aʊt/ | n. | 冲洗；清除；（使）突然出现；详细阐述 | The act of flushing something out; a sudden appearance or outburst. | 例句：The system requires a regular flash-out to prevent blockages. 该系统需要定期冲洗以防止堵塞。|例句：There was a flash-out of anger from the crowd. 人群中爆发出一阵愤怒。| 近义词：flush, outburst, eruption | 反义词：suppression, concealment |词性：名词
