noddling: /ˈnɒdlɪŋ/ (British) /ˈnɑːdlɪŋ/ (American)| n.|摸鱼；浑水摸鱼(尤指捕鱼)；非法捕鱼|The act of catching fish with one's bare hands, typically catfish, by reaching into underwater holes and crevices. It is also sometimes used to refer to illegal fishing methods.|He went noddling for catfish in the river. 他到河里摸鱼去了。|近义词：fishing, poaching (when illegal)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "angling" as a more formal and legal method)|词性:noun
