discount:/ˈdɪskaʊnt/ (US), /ˈdɪskaʊnt/ (UK)| n. vt.|折扣，打折|A deduction from the usual cost of something.|The store offers a 10% discount on all purchases.  这家商店所有商品都打九折。|近义词：reduction, rebate, allowance|反义词：premium, surcharge|词性:名词,动词

discount:/ˈdɪskaʊnt/ (US), /ˈdɪskaʊnt/ (UK)| vt.|打折，低估|To reduce the price of something.|They discounted the price of the car because it had a small dent. 他们因为车上有一个小凹痕而降低了车的价格。|近义词：reduce, lower, mark down|反义词：increase, raise, mark up|词性:动词

词源: From Old French *desconter (Modern French décompter), from des- "off, away" + compter "to count."
