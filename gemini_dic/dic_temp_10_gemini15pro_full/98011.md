inopercular:/ɪnɒˈpɜːkjʊlə(r)/ /ɪnɑːˈpɜːrkjələr/
| adj. |无鳃盖的，没有厣的|Lacking an operculum; without a covering flap, specifically referring to the gill cover in fish or the lid-like structure in some snails.|The inopercular snail is vulnerable to predators.  这种无鳃盖的蜗牛很容易受到捕食者的攻击。|The scientist studied the inopercular features of the newly discovered fish species.  科学家研究了新发现的鱼类的无鳃盖特征。|近义词: uncovered, lidless|反义词: operculate|词性:形容词
