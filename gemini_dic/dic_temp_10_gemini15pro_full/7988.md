anthologization:/ˌænθəˈlɒdʒaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
选集化；编入选集
The process of including a piece of writing or art in an anthology.
Her short story's anthologization cemented her status as a rising literary star.  她的短篇小说被选入选集，巩固了她作为文坛新星的地位。
inclusion, compilation
exclusion, omission
noun
