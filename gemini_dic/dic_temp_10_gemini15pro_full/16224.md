banqueted:/ˈbæŋkwɪtɪd/
verb
宴请; 参加宴会
Having feasted and banqueted.  饱餐宴饮之后.
The king banqueted his guests in the great hall. 国王在大厅里宴请他的客人。
近义词:feasted, dined
反义词:fasted, starved
词性:v.
