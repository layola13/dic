gila:/ˈxiːlə/ | n. | 吉拉毒蜥 | A venomous lizard (Heloderma suspectum) native to the southwestern United States and northwestern Mexico. |  The gila monster is one of the few venomous lizards. 吉拉毒蜥是少数几种有毒蜥蜴之一。|  The gila monster's bite, while painful, is rarely fatal to humans. 虽然吉拉毒蜥的咬伤很疼，但很少对人类致命。 | synonyms: Gila monster | antonyms: N/A | noun
