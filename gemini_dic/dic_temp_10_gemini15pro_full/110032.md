licen:/ˈlaɪsən/| n. |许可，执照|A legal document giving official permission to do something.|She doesn't have a driver's licen. 她没有驾驶执照。|Synonyms: permit, authorization|Antonyms: prohibition, ban|noun

licen:/ˈlaɪsən/| v. |许可，批准|To grant official permission for someone to do something.|The government licened the company to operate the mine. 政府批准该公司运营该矿。|Synonyms: authorize, permit|Antonyms: forbid, prohibit|verb
