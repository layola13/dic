outbray:/aʊtˈbreɪ/| v. |大声喊叫，胜过(尤指驴叫)|To shout or roar louder than someone or something, especially a donkey. |The donkey outbrayed its rivals. 这头驴的叫声盖过了它的对手们。|近义词：outbellow, outscream, outshout|反义词:whisper, murmur|词性:动词

outbray:/aʊtˈbreɪ/| v. |大声斥责，胜过|To outdo or defeat someone by shouting or making a lot of noise.|He outbrayed his opponents in the debate. 他在辩论中以大声斥责压倒了对手。|近义词：defeat, overcome, conquer|反义词:lose, surrender|词性:动词
