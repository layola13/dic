metagraphic:/ˌmɛtəˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
元图的，超图的
Relating to or denoting a system of graphic notation that goes beyond the conventional representation of language, incorporating visual elements to convey meaning.
Metagraphic novels often use symbols and imagery to enhance the narrative. 元图小说经常使用符号和图像来增强叙事。
近义词: visual, symbolic, iconic
反义词: textual, linguistic
词性: adjective

Etymology:  From meta- (beyond, transcending) + graphic (relating to visual art, especially involving drawing, engraving, or lettering).
