chalkstone:/ˈtʃɔːkˌstoʊn/| n. |白垩岩，白垩石|A type of limestone formed largely from the calcareous remains of marine organisms, especially foraminifera.  It is a relatively soft, white, porous sedimentary rock.|The cliffs were composed primarily of chalkstone.  这些悬崖主要由白垩岩构成。|The ancient building was constructed from blocks of chalkstone. 这座古老的建筑是用白垩岩石块建造的。|近义词：limestone, chalk|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps granite or other hard rocks)|词性:noun
