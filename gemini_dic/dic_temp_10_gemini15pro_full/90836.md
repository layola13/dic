hundreds:/ˈhʌndrədz/| n.|数百;几百;许多|Several hundreds of people attended the concert. 数百人参加了音乐会。|Synonym: many, numerous, a lot of |Antonym: few, little|noun

Etymology->
From Old English "hundredu," from Proto-Germanic "*hundaraduz," meaning "a hundred." The "-s" ending marks the plural form.
