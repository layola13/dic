depilate:/ˈdɛpɪleɪt/ /ˈdiːpɪleɪt/| v. |脱毛，除毛|To remove hair from (a part of the body).|例句：She decided to depilate her legs before going to the beach. 她决定去海滩前脱毛腿。

例句：Many people choose to depilate their underarms for cosmetic reasons. 许多人出于美容原因选择去除腋毛。

|近义词：remove hair, shave, wax, epilate|反义词: grow hair|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dēpīlāre, from dē- (away from) + pilus (hair).
