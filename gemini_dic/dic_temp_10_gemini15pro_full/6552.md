amylometer: /ˌæmɪˈlɒmɪtər/ (US) /ˌæmɪˈlɒmɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 淀粉测定仪；糊化仪 | An instrument used to measure the viscosity of starch paste, indicating the enzyme activity of alpha-amylase.  It helps determine the quality of flour for baking. |  The baker used an amylometer to assess the flour's properties.  面包师用淀粉测定仪评估面粉的特性。| synonym: viscometer | antonym: N/A | noun
