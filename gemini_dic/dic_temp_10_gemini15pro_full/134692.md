nonmasculine:/ˌnɑːnˈmæskjəlɪn/
adjective
非男性的；非阳性的
Not of the male gender; not having qualities traditionally associated with men.

The store carries a wide selection of nonmasculine clothing.
这家商店出售种类繁多的非男性服装。

They challenged traditional nonmasculine roles in society.
他们挑战了社会中传统的非男性角色。

近义词:feminine, effeminate, unmanly
反义词:masculine

词性:adjective
