boundarylessness:/ˈbaʊndri.lɪs.nɪs/ /ˈbaʊndri.ləs.nəs/
n.|无边界性，无界限性|The state of being without boundaries, especially in a social or organizational context, often referring to a work environment where roles and responsibilities are fluid and collaborative.|The company embraced boundarylessness to foster innovation and teamwork.  这家公司拥抱无边界性以促进创新和团队合作。|近义词: fluidity, openness, permeability, limitlessness|反义词: boundedness, restriction, limitation|词性:名词

Etymology:
boundary (n.) + -less (suffix) + -ness (suffix)
boundary: from Old French bounde "boundary, limit"
-less: Old English -leas "free from, without"
-ness: Old English -nes(s), forming abstract nouns from adjectives and participles
