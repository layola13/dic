例句：He was struck by the ephemeralness of the morning mist.  他被晨雾的短暂性所震撼。|近义词：transience, brevity, fleetingness|反义词：permanence, eternity, durability|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From ephemeral + -ness.  Ephemeral comes from the Greek *ephēmeros*, meaning "lasting only a day."
