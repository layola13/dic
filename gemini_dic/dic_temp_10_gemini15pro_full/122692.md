moeve:/ˈmuːv/| v. |移动，搬动 (This word is archaic or dialectal and rarely used in modern English.)|To move something or someone from one place to another.  This word is an older form and is not commonly used in contemporary English.  The standard modern word is "move."|He moeved the chair closer to the fire. (Archaic) 他把椅子挪近火炉。|近义词：move, shift, displace|反义词:fix, stay|词性:verb
