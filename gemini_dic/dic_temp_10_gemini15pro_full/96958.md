infamize:/ˈɪnfəmaɪz/| v. |使声名狼藉，败坏名声|To make infamous; to give a bad reputation to.|The tabloid sought to infamize the politician with scandalous stories.|近义词：discredit, vilify, defame, slander, libel|反义词：extol, praise, celebrate, honor|词性:动词

Etymology: From Late Latin *infamizare, from Latin infamis (“infamous”).
