cayuga:/kəˈjuːɡə/ | n. |卡尤加; 卡尤加湖; 卡尤加族; 卡尤加鸭|A Native American tribe of the Iroquois Confederacy formerly inhabiting the region around Cayuga Lake, New York. Also: a member of this tribe.  A variety of black duck bred in the US. |例句：The Cayuga people lived around Cayuga Lake.  卡尤加人居住在卡尤加湖周围。

例句：The Cayuga duck is known for its dark plumage. 卡尤加鸭以其深色的羽毛而闻名。|近义词：Iroquois (for the tribe), black duck (for the duck) |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
