panteutonic:/ˌpænˌtjuːˈtɒnɪk/
adjective
泛日耳曼的
Relating to all the Teutonic peoples; common to the Teutonic languages.  Encompassing or pertaining to all Germanic peoples or their languages.
The panteutonic ideal of a unified Germanic empire never came to fruition.  泛日耳曼统一帝国的理想从未实现。
Near Synonyms: Germanic, Teutonic
Antonyms: Non-Germanic
词性: adjective
