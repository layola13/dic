dissuasory:/dɪˈswɔːsəri/ /dɪˈswɔːri/
adjective
劝退的，阻止的，威慑的
Tending to dissuade; deterring.
The high price of the tickets was dissuasory to many fans.  高昂的票价劝退了许多粉丝。
The government imposed dissuasory taxes on cigarettes. 政府对香烟征收劝退性税收。
近义词:deterrent, discouraging, preventative
反义词:encouraging, promoting, inciting
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin dissuasorius, from dissuadere (to dissuade).  Dissuadere is formed from dis- (apart, away) + suadere (to advise, persuade).
