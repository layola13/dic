decarburize:/diːˈkɑːrbəraɪz/
verb
脱碳，除去…的碳
To remove carbon from (iron or steel) by heating it in an oxidizing atmosphere.
The steel is decarburized in the furnace.  钢在炉子里脱碳。
decarbonize, decarbonise
carburize, carbonize
