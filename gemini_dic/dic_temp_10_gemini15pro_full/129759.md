non-exhausted:/nɒn-ɪɡˈzɔːstɪd/ | adj. | 未耗尽的，未用完的 | Not completely used up or depleted; still having some remaining. | The battery is non-exhausted, we still have some power left. 电池还没用完，我们还有一些电量剩余。 |  The list of potential benefits is non-exhausted.  潜在好处清单并未穷尽。 | Near Synonyms: remaining, unused, left over, surplus, unexpended | Antonyms: exhausted, depleted, used up, spent, consumed | adjective
