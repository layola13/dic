nonblending:/nɒnˈblɛndɪŋ/ | adj. | 非混合的，不混合的 | Not blending; incapable of being blended.  | This paint is nonblending, so be careful not to mix the colors.  这种颜料是不混合的，所以小心不要把颜色混在一起。 | The nonblending nature of the oil and water made it difficult to create a smooth emulsion. 油和水的不混合特性使得难以形成光滑的乳液。 | 近义词：immiscible, unmixable | 反义词：blending, miscible | 词性：形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "blending" (present participle of "blend," from Old Norse "blanda" - to mix).
