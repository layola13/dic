fetterer:/ˈfetərər/| n.|束缚者，限制者|Someone or something that restricts or restrains.|The bureaucracy is a fetterer of progress. 官僚主义是进步的束缚。|近义词：restrainer, inhibitor, hinderer|反义词:liberator, emancipator|noun

fetter:/ˈfetər/| n. vt.| 枷锁，束缚；束缚，限制|A chain or shackle placed on the feet; restrain with chains or manacles.|The prisoner's feet were fettered. 囚犯的脚被铐住了。  He felt fettered by his responsibilities. 他感到被自己的责任所束缚。|近义词：chains, shackles, restrain, restrict|反义词:liberate, free|noun, verb
