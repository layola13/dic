foals:/foʊlz/| n.|幼驹；小马；小驴|A foal is a young horse, or the young of other equine animals such as donkeys or zebras. |The mare protectively nuzzled her newborn foal. 母马爱怜地用鼻子轻触她刚出生的幼驹。

The farmer had two foals and three adult horses. 农夫有两匹幼驹和三匹成年马。|近义词：colt (male foal), filly (female foal), young horse, baby horse|反义词：stallion, mare, adult horse|词性:noun
