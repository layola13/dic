miser:/ˈmaɪzər/| n. |吝啬鬼，守财奴|A person who hoards wealth and spends as little money as possible.|Scrooge was a miser who hated spending money. 斯克鲁奇是个守财奴，讨厌花钱。|The old miser lived in a dilapidated house despite his vast fortune. 尽管拥有巨额财富，这位老吝啬鬼却住在破旧的房子里。|近义词：skinflint, tightwad, penny-pincher|反义词：spendthrift, philanthropist|词性:noun
