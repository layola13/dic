frangulin: /ˈfræŋɡjʊlɪn/ | n. |弗兰古林，鼠李糖苷|A glycoside obtained from the bark of the alder buckthorn (Frangula alnus), formerly used as a purgative.  It is an orange-yellow crystalline substance. |Buckthorn bark contains frangulin, a powerful laxative. 鼠李树皮含有弗兰古林，一种强力泻药。|Synonym: glucofrangulin|Antonym: N/A|Noun
