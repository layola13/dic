garnetiferous:/ˌɡɑːrnɪˈtɪfərəs/
adjective
含石榴石的；产石榴石的
Containing garnet; yielding garnet.
The geologist discovered a garnetiferous schist in the mountain range.  地质学家在山脉中发现了一种含石榴石的片岩。
The rock sample was identified as a garnetiferous gneiss.  岩石样本被鉴定为含石榴石的片麻岩。
Synonyms: garnet-bearing, garnet-rich
Antonyms: barren (in the context of minerals)
词性: adjective
