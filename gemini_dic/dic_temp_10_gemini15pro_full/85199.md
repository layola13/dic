hari-kari:/ˌhɑːriˈkɑːri/ /ˌhærɪˈkɛəri/ | n. | 切腹自杀；剖腹 | Ritual suicide by disembowelment, formerly practiced by the samurai in Japan. |  He committed hari-kari rather than face dishonor. 他为了避免蒙羞而切腹自杀。| Synonym: Seppuku | Antonym: Survival | noun

hari-kari:/ˌhɑːriˈkɑːri/ /ˌhærɪˈkɛəri/ | n. |  剖腹自杀；自杀 |  An act of self-destruction or deliberate self-sabotage. (Figurative) | The company committed financial hari-kari by investing in that failing project.  这家公司因投资那个失败的项目而自取灭亡。| Synonym: Suicide, self-destruction | Antonym: Self-preservation | noun
