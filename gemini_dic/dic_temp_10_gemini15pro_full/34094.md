civilizer:/ˈsɪvɪˌlaɪzər/
n.
文明化的人;教化者
A person or thing that civilizes a place or people.
The Romans were great civilizers, bringing their laws and customs to conquered territories.  罗马人是伟大的文明者，他们把自己的法律和习俗带到了被征服的领土。
The printing press was a powerful civilizer. 印刷机是一个强大的文明化工具。
近义词: enlightener, reformer, educator
反义词: barbarian, savage, destroyer
词性: noun


Etymology:
From civilize + -er.  From French civiliser, from civil + -iser.  Ultimately from Latin civilis.
