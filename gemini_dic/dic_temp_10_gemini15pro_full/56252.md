dogate:/ˈdoʊɡeɪt/ | n. |总督; 总督职位; 总督任期; 总督管辖区| A dogate is the office or term of office or jurisdiction of a doge.  A doge was the chief magistrate of Venice and Genoa. | The dogate of Venice lasted for over a thousand years. 威尼斯总督的统治持续了一千多年。|  The power of the doge was limited during the later years of the dogate. 在总督统治的后期，总督的权力受到了限制。 | synonyms: governorship, dominion, reign | antonyms: servitude, subjection | noun
