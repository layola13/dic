incorrection:/ˌɪnkəˈrɛkʃən/
n.
不正确，错误
the state or quality of being incorrect
The student's essay was filled with incorrections.  这个学生的文章充满了错误。

近义词:error, mistake, inaccuracy, fallacy
反义词:correction, accuracy, rightness, truth

词源:
Late Middle English: from Old French incorrection, from late Latin incorrectio(n-), from in- ‘not’ + correctio(n-) (see correction).
