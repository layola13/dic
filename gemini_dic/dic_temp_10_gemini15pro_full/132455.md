nondeprecatingly:/ˌnɒndɪˈprɛkətɪŋli/ | adv. | 不贬低地，不谦虚地，不自谦地 | In a manner that does not express disapproval or belittlement of oneself or one's abilities.  It implies a confident and straightforward presentation of oneself or one's work, without false modesty. | He spoke nondeprecatingly about his accomplishments, highlighting his key contributions to the project. 他毫不谦虚地谈论了自己的成就，强调了他对项目的关键贡献。

He nondeprecatingly accepted the award, acknowledging the hard work of his team. 他不谦虚地接受了奖项，肯定了团队的辛勤工作。 | 近义词: confidently, assertively, proudly, unreservedly | 反义词: modestly, humbly, self-deprecatingly | 词性: 副词

词源:

* non-:  prefix meaning "not"
* deprecating: from Latin "deprecatus," past participle of "deprecari," meaning "to pray against, avert by prayer," later meaning "to express disapproval of."
* -ly: adverbial suffix
