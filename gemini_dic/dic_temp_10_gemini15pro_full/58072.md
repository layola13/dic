dronish:/ˈdroʊnɪʃ/| adj.|单调乏味的，嗡嗡作响的|Resembling the monotonous droning sound of a drone; tedious and monotonous.|The professor's dronish lecture put half the class to sleep.  教授单调乏味的讲座让一半的学生都睡着了。|Synonyms: monotonous, tedious, dull, boring|Antonyms: exciting, interesting, stimulating|Adjective
Etymology: From *drone* (n.) + *-ish* (suffix forming adjectives). The word "drone" itself comes from Old English *drān*, meaning "male bee," referring to the sound it makes.
