flagworm:/ˈflæɡwɜːrm/
noun
旗杆蠕虫，又称管栖蠕虫，一种海洋多毛纲动物
A marine polychaete worm, often tube-dwelling, sometimes associated with shipwrecks or other submerged structures.
Flagworms are often found near shipwrecks.  旗杆蠕虫经常在沉船附近被发现。
Flagworms build protective tubes around their bodies. 旗杆蠕虫在它们的身体周围建造保护管。
Synonyms: tube worm, polychaete
