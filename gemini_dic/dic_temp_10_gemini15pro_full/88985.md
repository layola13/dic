holist:/ˈhəʊlɪst/| n. adj.|整体论者，整体的|A holist is someone who believes in holism.  A holistic approach considers all aspects of a person or system. |例句：She is a holist who believes in treating the whole person, not just the disease. 她是一位整体论者，相信治疗的是整个人，而不仅仅是疾病。

例句：Holistic medicine considers the physical, emotional, and spiritual well-being of the patient. 整体医学考虑病人的身体、情绪和精神健康。|近义词：comprehensive, integrated|反义词：reductionist, atomistic|词性：noun, adjective
