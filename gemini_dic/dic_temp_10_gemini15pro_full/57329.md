downturned:/ˌdaʊnˈtɜːrnd/| adj.|向下弯曲的，低迷的|Curving or facing downwards; pessimistic or depressed.|Her downturned mouth suggested she was unhappy. 她向下弯的嘴角表明她不高兴。|The economy is currently in a downturned state. 目前经济处于低迷状态。|近义词：downward, drooping, pessimistic, declining|反义词:upturned, optimistic, rising|词性:形容词
Etymology: down + turned (past participle of turn)  "down" indicating a downward direction and "turned" signifying the act of turning or curving.
