overfattening: /ˌoʊvərˈfætənɪŋ/ | adj. | 使过度肥胖的，容易使人发胖的 | Causing excessive fatness; tending to make one excessively fat. | The overfattening diet of modern society contributes to health problems. 现代社会容易使人发胖的饮食导致了健康问题。|  These overfattening snacks are bad for your health. 这些容易使人发胖的零食对你的健康有害。 | 近义词: fattening, obesogenic | 反义词: slimming, healthy | 词性: adjective
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "fatten" (verb meaning "to make or become fat")
