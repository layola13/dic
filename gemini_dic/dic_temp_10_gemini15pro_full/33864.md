circumscribe:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈskraɪb/ /ˌsɝːkəmˈskraɪb/| vt.|画…的外接圆|To draw a figure around another, touching it at points but not cutting it.|Circumscribe a circle around the triangle.  画一个三角形的外接圆。|Synonyms: encircle, surround|Antonyms: intersect|verb
