giantesses:/ˈdʒaɪəntɪsɪz/
noun
女巨人
Female giants; women of extraordinary size and strength.
The giantesses towered over the trees. 女巨人高耸入云，俯瞰着树木。

In folklore, giantesses often guarded sacred places. 在民间传说中，女巨人经常守护着圣地。

近义词:ogresses, titans
反义词:dwarfs, midgets
词性:n.
