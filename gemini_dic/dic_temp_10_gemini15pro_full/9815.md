anybody:/ˈɛnɪbɒdi/ (UK), /ˈɛnɪˌbɑːdi/ (US)|pron.|任何人；有人；随便哪个人|Someone or anyone; an unspecified or unknown person. |Is anybody home? 有人在家吗？|Anybody can learn to swim. 任何人都可以学会游泳。|近义词: anyone, someone, somebody|反义词: nobody|词性: 代词

词源: From Old English *ǣnig + bodig ("body").  Essentially meaning "any body," or any person.
