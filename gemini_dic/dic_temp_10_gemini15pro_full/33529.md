cicus: /ˈsɪrkəs/ | n. |马戏团，喧闹的场面|A traveling company of acrobats, clowns, and trained animals that gives performances, typically in a tent.  A chaotic or noisy situation. |例句：The children loved the circus. 孩子们喜欢马戏团。

例句：The meeting was a complete circus. 会议一片混乱。| 近义词：carnival, show, spectacle | 反义词: order, peace | 词性: 名词
