downsizing:/ˈdaʊnsaɪzɪŋ/
n.
缩小规模，裁员
The act of reducing the size of a company or organization by reducing the number of employees.
The company announced a major downsizing, resulting in hundreds of job losses. 公司宣布了大规模裁员，导致数百人失业。
reduction, streamlining, shrinking
expansion, growth
noun
