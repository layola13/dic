dissocialize:/dɪsˈsəʊʃəlaɪz/ | v. |  使脱离社会；使不善交际；使反社会 | To cause someone to become less social or to lose social skills; to adjust to being away from society. |  Years of solitary confinement dissocialized him.  多年的单独监禁使他脱离了社会。 | synonyms: isolate, alienate, withdraw | antonyms: socialize, integrate | verb

dissocialize:/dɪsˈsəʊʃəlaɪz/ | adj. |  反社会的；不善交际的 | Unsociable, antisocial. | He exhibited dissocialized behavior, preferring to be alone. 他表现出不善交际的行为，喜欢独处。 | synonyms: antisocial, unsociable, withdrawn | antonyms: sociable, gregarious | adjective
