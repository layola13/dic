outfits:/ˈaʊtfɪts/| n.|服装，装备，全套服装|A set of clothes worn together, especially for a particular occasion or activity.|She bought several new outfits for her vacation. 她为假期买了幾套新衣服。|近义词：ensemble, attire, clothing, costume, gear|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑"individual items" 或 "separate pieces")|noun

outfits:/ˈaʊtfɪts/| vt.|装备，配备|To provide someone or something with equipment or clothes.|The expedition was outfitted with the latest technology. 探险队配备了最新的科技设备。|近义词：equip, furnish, supply, rig, provide|反义词: strip, divest|verb
