hoisin:/ˈhɔɪsɪn/ | n. |海鲜酱；海皇酱 |A thick, dark, reddish-brown sauce made from soybeans, sugar, vinegar, garlic, and various spices, commonly used in Cantonese cuisine.|  I marinated the pork in hoisin sauce before grilling. 我在烤之前用海鲜酱腌制了猪肉。

| The hoisin sauce added a sweet and savory flavor to the stir-fry. 海鲜酱为这道炒菜增添了甜味和咸味。 | 近义词: Chinese barbecue sauce | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific sauce) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Cantonese 海鲜酱 (hoi2 sin1 jeung3), literally "seafood sauce," although it doesn't typically contain seafood.  The name is thought to be a misnomer.
