besetment:/bɪˈsɛtmənt/| n. |困境，围攻，困扰|The state of being beset; a beleaguerment.  A state of being troubled or harassed.|The small village suffered a besetment of locusts.|近义词：affliction, trouble, harassment, siege, plight|反义词:peace, tranquility, calm|noun

beset:/bɪˈsɛt/| v. |困扰，围攻|To trouble persistently; harass. To surround; hem in.|The hikers were beset by mosquitoes.|近义词：harass, plague, surround, besiege|反义词:aid, assist, help|verb
