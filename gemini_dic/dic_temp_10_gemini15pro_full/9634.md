antistrikers:/ˌæntiˈstraɪkərz/
|n.|反罢工者，反对罢工的人|People who oppose strikes or striking workers.  Those who are against workers going on strike.|The antistrikers held a rally downtown. 反罢工者在市中心举行集会。|
|The company hired antistrikers to replace the striking workers. 公司雇佣了反罢工者来取代罢工工人。|
|opponents of strikes, strikebreakers, scabs|
|strikers, union members, labor activists|
|noun|

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek anti-)
striker: one who strikes, especially a worker on strike
