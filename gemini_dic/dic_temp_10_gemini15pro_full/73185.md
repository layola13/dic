forbore:/fɔːrˈbɔːr/
verb
克制；忍住；避免
refrained from doing something; politely or patiently restrain oneself from doing something
She forbore to ask any questions. 她忍住没问任何问题。
近义词: refrain, abstain, desist, withhold
反义词: indulge, yield, succumb
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *forberen, from Old English forberan (“to bear with, endure, tolerate, refrain from, spare, avoid, escape”), from Proto-Germanic *furberanan (“to forbear, hold back”), equivalent to for- + bear. Cognate with Dutch verberen (“to conceal, suppress, contain”), German verbergen (“to hide, conceal”), Gothic 𐍆𐌰𐌿𐍂𐌱𐌰𐌹𐍂𐌰𐌽 (faurbairan, “to endure”).
