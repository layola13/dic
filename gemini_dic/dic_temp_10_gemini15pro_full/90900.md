hunters:/ˈhʌntərz/| n.|猎人，狩猎者|People or animals that hunt.  Those who pursue game or other wild animals for food or sport.|The hunters tracked the deer through the forest. 猎人追踪着鹿穿过森林。|近义词：sportsmen, huntresses, trappers|反义词:prey, quarry|noun

词源: From Old English hunta "hunter," agent noun from huntian "to hunt."
