botherer:/ˈbɒðərə(r)/ (US) /ˈbɑːðərər/| n. | 扰乱者；麻烦制造者；令人讨厌的人 |Someone who bothers or annoys others. |He's a real botherer, always causing trouble. 他真是个麻烦制造者，总是惹事生非。|Example: The constant botherer was asked to leave the library.  那个不断打扰别人的人被要求离开图书馆。|Synonyms: pest, nuisance, annoyance, troublemaker |Antonyms: helper, assistant, facilitator |noun

bother:/ˈbɒðə(r)/ (US) /ˈbɑːðərər/| v. | 打扰；烦扰；操心；费力 |To cause someone trouble or annoyance. |Don't bother me with trivial matters. 别拿琐碎的事情来烦我。|Example:  I didn't bother to call him back. 我懒得给他回电话。|Synonyms: annoy, disturb, irritate, pester |Antonyms: soothe, comfort, please, assist|verb
