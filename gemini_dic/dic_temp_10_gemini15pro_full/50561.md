deputized: /ˈdɛpjʊˌtaɪzd/ | v. | 代理的，委任的 |  Having been appointed to act as a substitute for another, especially in a temporary or subordinate capacity.  | The sheriff deputized several citizens to help search for the missing child. 警长委托了几位市民协助寻找失踪的孩子。 |  authorized, commissioned, delegated, appointed |  removed, dismissed, discharged | verb
