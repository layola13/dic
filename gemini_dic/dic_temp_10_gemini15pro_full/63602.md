envyful:/ˈɛnviːfʊl/| adj.|羡慕的，嫉妒的|Feeling or showing envy; covetous.  Characterized by or expressing envy.|She cast an envyful glance at her friend's new car. 她嫉妒地看了一眼她朋友的新车。|近义词：envious, jealous, covetous|反义词：generous, benevolent|词性:形容词

词源:  From envy + -ful.  Envy comes from Old French envie, from Latin invidia "envy, jealousy, ill will," from invidēre "regard maliciously, grudge, look askance at," from in- "upon, against" + vidēre "to see."
