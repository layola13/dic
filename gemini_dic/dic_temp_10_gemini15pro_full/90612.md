humanely:/hjuːˈmeɪnli/| adv.|人道地，仁慈地|In a way that shows compassion or consideration for the pain or suffering of other living things.|The animals were treated humanely and given proper medical care. 这些动物受到了人道的对待，并得到了适当的医疗照顾。|近义词：compassionately, mercifully, kindly|反义词：cruelly, inhumanely, brutally|词性:副词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: humane + -ly.  "Humane" comes from the Latin "humanus" meaning "human," ultimately from "homo" meaning "man."  The suffix "-ly" transforms the adjective "humane" into the adverb "humanely."
