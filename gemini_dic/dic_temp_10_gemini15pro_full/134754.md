nonmelodramatic:/ˌnɒnˌmɛlədrəˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 不煽情的；不夸张的 | Not melodramatic; avoiding exaggerated emotion or behavior. |  The film's nonmelodramatic portrayal of grief resonated with audiences. 这部电影对悲伤的非煽情刻画引起了观众的共鸣。

The news report was factual and nonmelodramatic. 这篇新闻报道是真实的、不煽情的。 | 近义词: understated, restrained, subtle, low-key | 反义词: melodramatic, dramatic, exaggerated, histrionic | 词性: adjective
