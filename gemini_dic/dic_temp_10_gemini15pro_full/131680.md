nonconclusion:/ˌnɒnkənˈkluːʒən/
noun
非结论，无结论
a result or outcome that does not provide a definite answer or solution
The investigation ended in nonconclusion due to lack of evidence. 由于缺乏证据，调查以无结论告终。
non-result, indecision, unresolved ending
conclusion, resolution, decision
n.
