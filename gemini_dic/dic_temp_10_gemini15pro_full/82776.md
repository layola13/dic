guilt:/ɡɪlt/ | n. |内疚，罪行 | The fact of having committed a specified or implied offense or crime. | He felt a pang of guilt. 他感到一阵内疚。| synonyms: remorse, shame, regret | antonyms: innocence, shamelessness | noun
