frisks:/frɪsks/ | v.  n. |搜身，嬉戏 |To pass the hands quickly over (someone) in search of hidden weapons, drugs, or other items. Also, to leap or skip playfully.| The police officer frisked the suspect. 警察搜查了嫌疑犯的身子。 |  The lambs frisked in the meadow. 羊羔在草地上嬉戏。 |近义词：search, pat down, frolic, gambol |反义词: examine thoroughly (for v.), stand still (for n.) | verb, noun
