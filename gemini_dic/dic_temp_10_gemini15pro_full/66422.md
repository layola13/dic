executrix:/ɪɡˈzekjətrɪks/| n. |女遗嘱执行人|A woman appointed by a testator to carry out the terms of their will.|例句：The executrix of the estate handled the sale of the property.  遗产的女遗嘱执行人处理了房产的出售。

例句：She was named executrix in her mother's will. 在她母亲的遗嘱中，她被指定为女遗嘱执行人。|近义词：executor, administrator|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps beneficiary)|词性:noun
