mayfly:/ˈmeɪflaɪ/| n. |蜉蝣|A short-lived insect with delicate wings, typically found near water.|例句：Mayflies have a very short lifespan. 蜉蝣的寿命很短。

例句：The mayflies were swarming around the lake. 蜉蝣成群地在湖边飞舞。|近义词：shadfly|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *maifly, equivalent to may + fly.  The "may" refers to the month of May, when these insects often emerge.
