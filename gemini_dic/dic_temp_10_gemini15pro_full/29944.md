caveating:/ˈkeɪviˌeɪtɪŋ/
v.
提出警告；附加说明；加限制条件
Adding a proviso or caveat to a statement or an agreement.
He accepted the offer, caveating that he would have to consult with his lawyer first. 他接受了这个提议，但附加说明他必须先咨询他的律师。
近义词: stipulating, qualifying, restricting, limiting
反义词: guaranteeing, promising, asserting, unconditionally
