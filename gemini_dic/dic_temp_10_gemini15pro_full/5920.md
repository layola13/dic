ambitioning:/æmˈbɪʃənɪŋ/
v.
有雄心壮志；追求；力求
having ambitious aims or plans.
He is ambitioning a career in politics. 他正追求政治生涯。
近义词: aspiring, striving, pursuing, aiming for
反义词: unambitious, complacent, resigned
词性: 动词 (verb)

词源:  From the noun "ambition" + "-ing" suffix to form a present participle, used here as a dynamic verb.
