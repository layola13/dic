criticisms:/ˈkrɪtɪsɪzəmz/
n.
批评，批判；指责，非难
The author responded to the criticisms of his new book.  作者回应了对他新书的批评。
synonyms: critiques, objections, censures, condemnations
antonyms: praise, compliments, accolades
noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *criticism, from Late Latin criticismus, from Ancient Greek κριτικισμός (kritikismós, “art of criticism”), from κριτικός (kritikós, “critical, able to discern”), from κρίνω (krínō, “I separate, distinguish, judge”).
