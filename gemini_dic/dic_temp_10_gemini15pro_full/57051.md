doubtful:/ˈdaʊtfəl/ (US), /ˈdaʊtfʊl/ (UK)| adj.|可疑的，怀疑的，不确定的|Feeling or showing uncertainty about something; questionable.  |例句：The future of the project is doubtful.  这个项目的未来是不确定的。|例句：I'm doubtful about his ability to finish the job. 我怀疑他是否有能力完成这项工作。|近义词：uncertain, questionable, dubious, unsure|反义词：certain, sure, confident|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *doutful, from Old French *douteus, from Late Latin *dubitōsus, from Latin dubitō (“I hesitate, doubt”), from dubius (“wavering, doubtful”).
