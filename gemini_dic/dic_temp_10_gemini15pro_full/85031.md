词源: From Middle English *harbingere, from Anglo-Norman herbergere, from Frankish *heriberga (“lodging, shelter”), from *heri (“army”) + *berga (“protection, shelter”). More at here, borough.  Influenced by harbor. Originally referred to a person sent ahead to arrange lodgings, later to any forerunner.
