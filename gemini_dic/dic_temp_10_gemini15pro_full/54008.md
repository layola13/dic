discriminatory:/dɪˈskrɪmɪnətɔːri/ |adj.|歧视性的，差别待遇的|Making distinctions based on prejudice or bias, typically resulting in unfair treatment of a particular group or individual.|例句：The company has been accused of discriminatory hiring practices.  该公司被指控招聘过程中存在歧视行为。

例句：Discriminatory laws against minorities are unacceptable.  针对少数族群的歧视性法律是不可接受的。|近义词：prejudiced, biased, unfair, unjust|反义词：fair, just, impartial, equitable|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin *discriminātōrius, from discrimināre (“to distinguish, separate”)  + -tōrius.  Ultimately from Latin discernere (“to separate, distinguish”), from dis- (“apart”) + cernere (“to sift, separate, distinguish”).
