nonexpansivenesses:/ˌnɒnɪkˈspænsɪvnɪsɪz/
noun
非扩张性（复数）
The quality or state of being nonexpansive; the property of not increasing in size or scope.
The nonexpansivenesses of the functions guarantee the stability of the system. 这些函数的非扩张性保证了系统的稳定性。
Near Synonyms:  constrictions, limitations, restrictions
Antonyms: expansions, growths, increases
词性: 名词
