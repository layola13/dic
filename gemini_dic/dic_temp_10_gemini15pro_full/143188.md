outcompete:/ˌaʊtkəmˈpiːt/
verb
胜过，在竞争中击败
to defeat someone in competition, especially in business, by offering better products, services, etc.
Large companies can easily outcompete smaller businesses.  大公司很容易在竞争中击败小企业。
The company outcompeted its rivals by offering lower prices.  该公司通过提供更低的价格胜过了竞争对手。
近义词: beat, surpass, defeat, outdo, outstrip, outmaneuver
反义词: lose to, be defeated by
词性: verb
