cushman:/ˈkʊʃmən/| n.|卡什曼（姓氏）；轻便马车；轻便摩托车|A light horse-drawn vehicle, especially one used for deliveries; a motorized scooter or light motorcycle.  Originally a surname.|例句：The cushman zipped through the narrow streets.  轻便摩托车在狭窄的街道上穿梭。

例句：Mr. Cushman was known for his collection of antique vehicles.  卡什曼先生以收藏古董车而闻名。|近义词：scooter, motorbike, buggy|反义词:truck, car|词性:noun
