loaders:/ˈloʊdərz/| n.|装载机; 加载器; 装填手|A machine or person that loads something, especially goods onto a vehicle or bullets into a gun.|The loaders were busy filling the trucks with gravel. 装载机正忙着往卡车上装砾石。|synonyms: loaders, excavators, front-end loaders, bucket loaders, loading shovels, bulldozers|antonyms: unloaders, dischargers|noun

loaders:/ˈloʊdərz/| n.|加载程序|A program that loads other programs into the computer's memory.|The operating system uses a loader to start applications. 操作系统使用加载程序来启动应用程序。|synonyms: boot loader, program loader, bootstrap loader|antonyms: unloaders, dischargers|noun
