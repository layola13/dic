facilitatory:/fəˈsɪlɪteɪtɔːri/
adjective
促进的，便利的
Making something easier or more likely to happen.
The teacher created a facilitatory environment for learning. 老师创造了一个有利于学习的环境。
The government introduced facilitatory measures to boost the economy. 政府出台了促进经济增长的措施。
近义词: helpful, conducive, enabling, supportive
反义词: hindering, obstructive, inhibiting
词性: adjective

词源: From the verb "facilitate," which comes from the Latin "facilis" meaning "easy."  The suffix "-ory" indicates that the word is an adjective.
