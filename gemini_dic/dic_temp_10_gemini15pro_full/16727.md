The hotel has two bars and a restaurant. 这家酒店有两个酒吧和一个餐厅。

The iron bars of the gate were rusty. 大门的铁条生锈了.

Poverty is a bar to education for many children. 贫困是许多孩子受教育的障碍。

He was called to the bar last year. 他去年获得了律师资格.
