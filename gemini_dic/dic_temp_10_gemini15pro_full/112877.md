lucubratory:/ˈluːkjʊˌbreɪtəri/| adj.|苦心研究的，勤奋刻苦的，在夜间工作的|Characterized by laborious or intensive study, especially at night.|例句：He spent many lucubratory hours in the library researching his dissertation. 他在图书馆里花了许多时间苦心研究他的论文。

例句：Her lucubratory efforts finally paid off when she received a grant for her research. 她勤奋刻苦的努力最终得到了回报，她获得了研究经费。|近义词：studious, scholarly, diligent, assiduous|反义词：lazy, idle, indolent|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *lucubratorius*, from *lucubrare* "to work by lamplight," from *lux* (genitive *lucis*) "light."
