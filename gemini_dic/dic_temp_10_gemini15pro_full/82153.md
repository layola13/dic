groomsman:/ˈɡruːmz.mən/| n. |男傧相，伴郎|A male friend or relative who attends the bridegroom at his wedding.| My brother was my groomsman at my wedding. 我的兄弟是我婚礼上的伴郎。|Synonym: best man (if the principal groomsman), usher|Antonym: bridesmaid|Noun
