boardwalks:/ˈbɔːrdwɔːks/ | n. | 木板路，栈道 | A walkway made of wooden boards, typically along a beach or waterfront. | We strolled along the boardwalk, enjoying the ocean breeze. 我们沿着木板路漫步，享受着海风。|Synonyms: promenade, pier, esplanade | Antonyms: path, trail | noun

Etymology:  From "board" (a plank of wood) + "walk" (a path for walking).
