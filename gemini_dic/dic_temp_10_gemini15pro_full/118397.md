meropodite: /ˈmɛrəpəˌdaɪt/| n. |节肢动物的肢节|A segment of a limb in arthropods, typically between the ischiopodite and the carpopodite.|The meropodite of the crab's claw is particularly strong. 螃蟹爪的肢节特别强壮。|Near synonyms: podomere, segment|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a body part)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *mēros* "part, portion" + *pous* (genitive *podos*) "foot"
