discontentments:/ˌdɪskənˈtɛntmənts/| n. |不满，不满足|a feeling of wanting something more or different from what one has; dissatisfaction|The survey revealed widespread discontentments among the workers regarding their wages and working conditions. 这项调查揭示了工人们普遍对工资和工作条件不满。|近义词：grievances, complaints, disaffections|反义词：contentment, satisfaction|词性:名词
Etymology: From dis- (expressing negation) + contentment (state of being satisfied). Ultimately from Latin contentus, past participle of continēre "to hold together, contain, restrain."
