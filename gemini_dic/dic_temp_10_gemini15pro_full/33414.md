churchman:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃmən/ | n. | 教会人士；教士；虔诚的教徒 | A man who is actively involved in the affairs of a church, especially a clergyman. | He was a devout churchman, attending services every Sunday. 他是一位虔诚的教徒，每个星期天都去做礼拜。 | Synonyms: clergyman, cleric, ecclesiastic, parishioner (if lay member) | Antonyms: atheist, nonbeliever, secularist | noun

churchman:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃmən/ | n. | 国教教徒 | A member of an established church, specifically the Church of England. |  As a staunch churchman, he supported the Church of England's role in society.  作为一名坚定的国教教徒，他支持英国国教在社会中的作用。 | Synonyms: Anglican (specifically for Church of England) | Antonyms: dissenter, nonconformist | noun
