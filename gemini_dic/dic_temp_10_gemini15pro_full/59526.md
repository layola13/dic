eat dirt:/iːt dɜːrt/
|idiomatic expression|
|忍辱负重；低声下气；承认错误|
|To be humiliated or forced to submit; to retract a statement or apologize abjectly.|
|He made me eat dirt after I challenged him in front of everyone.| 他让我在众人面前挑战他之后，让我颜面扫地。|
|Synonym:  grovel,  humble oneself,  back down,  apologize,  recant|
|Antonym:  stand one's ground,  assert oneself,  refuse to apologize|
|phrase|


eat dirt:/iːt dɜːrt/
|idiomatic expression|
|忍受屈辱；被迫让步|
|To endure humiliation or be forced to concede.|
|She had to eat dirt when her lies were exposed.|当她的谎言被揭穿时，她不得不忍受屈辱。|
|Synonym:  swallow one's pride, take it,  cope with humiliation|
|Antonym:  retaliate,  fight back|
|phrase|
