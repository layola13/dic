carin:/ˈkærɪn/| n. |龙骨，脊，隆起线|A projecting structure or ridge, especially one on the bottom of a ship's hull or the keel of a bird.|The carin on the bird's breastbone helps it fly more efficiently. 鸟类胸骨上的龙骨有助于它更有效地飞行。|近义词：keel, ridge, crest|反义词:groove, furrow|名词
