cohort:/ˈkoʊhɔːrt/ (US), /ˈkəʊhɔːt/ (UK)| n.|同龄人；一群人；支持者；步兵大队|A cohort is a group of people who share a common characteristic, typically age.  It can also refer to a companion or supporter, and historically, a cohort was a unit in the Roman army.|例句：The 1990 birth cohort is entering the workforce.  1990年出生的人群正在进入劳动力市场。

例句：The CEO has a cohort of loyal advisors. 首席执行官有一群忠实的顾问。|近义词：group, band, company, posse, following|反义词:opponent, enemy|词性:noun
