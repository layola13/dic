dewani:/dəˈvɑːniː/
|n.|
|德瓦尼(一种波斯语体的书法)|
|Dewani is a cursive style of Arabic calligraphy developed during the reign of the Ottoman Turks.|
|The official documents were written in elegant dewani script.|官方文件是用优雅的德瓦尼字体书写的。|
|naskh, thuluth, ruq'ah|
