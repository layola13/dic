calzoons: /kælˈzuːnz/
n.
calzones (plural)
意大利烤馅饼（通常为半月形，内馅有奶酪、肉、蔬菜等）
A type of folded pizza, often stuffed with ricotta, mozzarella, and various other ingredients.
例句：I ordered calzones for dinner last night. 我昨晚晚餐点了烤馅饼。

例句：The calzones at this restaurant are delicious.  这家餐厅的烤馅饼很好吃。
