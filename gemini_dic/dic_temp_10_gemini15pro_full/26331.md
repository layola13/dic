buzz-saws:/ˈbʌzˌsɔːz/ (US), /ˈbʌzˌsɔːz/ (UK)| n. |嗡嗡作响的锯子; 圆锯 (通常指复数)|A buzz-saw is a circular saw, typically used for cutting wood, that makes a loud buzzing sound. It often refers to multiple saws due to the plural form.|The carpenter used his buzz-saws to cut the planks for the deck.  木匠用他的圆锯切割甲板的木板。|
The buzz-saws whirred loudly in the lumber mill. 圆锯在木材厂里嗡嗡作响。|Synonyms: circular saws, power saws|Antonyms: hand saws, (arguably) quiet tools|noun

buzz-saw:/ˈbʌzˌsɔː/ (US), /ˈbʌzˌsɔː/ (UK)| v. |用圆锯锯;|To buzz-saw means to cut something using a buzz-saw.  It is less common than the noun form.|He buzz-sawed through the thick branch with ease. 他轻松地用圆锯锯开了粗树枝。|
She buzz-sawed the logs into smaller pieces for the fireplace. 她用圆锯把木头锯成小块，以便放入壁炉。|Synonyms: saw, cut|Antonyms: assemble, join|verb
