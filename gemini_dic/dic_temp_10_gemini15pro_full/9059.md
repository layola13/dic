antimetabole:/ˌænti məˈtæbəli/ | n. |逆反, 交错配列法 | A rhetorical device that involves repeating a phrase or sentence in reverse order.  It is a type of chiasmus. |  "Ask not what your country can do for you; ask what you can do for your country." |Synonyms: chiasmus, inversion, antimetathesis | Antonyms: N/A | noun
