chromatic:/krəˈmætɪk/| adj.|半音的，彩色的，着色的|Relating to or using notes not belonging to the diatonic scale of the key in which a passage is written.  Also, relating to color or color phenomena.|The chromatic scale includes all twelve notes within an octave.|近义词：colorful, hued, pigmented|反义词：achromatic, colorless|词性: adjective
