imax:/ˈaɪmæks/| n.|巨幕电影，巨幕影院|A movie format and a set of cinema projection standards creating an image of greater size and resolution than conventional film systems. |We saw the new movie in IMAX. 我们在巨幕影院看了这部新电影。|近义词:large-format film, giant screen|反义词:standard format|noun
