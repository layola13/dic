caramels:/ˈkærəməlz/ | n. |焦糖,太妃糖|(plural of caramel) A chewy candy made from sugar, butter, cream, and sometimes flavorings like vanilla or chocolate.  A type of sweet confectionery made by heating sugar. | I love salted caramels. 我喜欢咸焦糖。| The caramels were soft and chewy. 这些焦糖又软又嚼。|Synonyms: toffee, fudge | Antonyms: N/A | noun
