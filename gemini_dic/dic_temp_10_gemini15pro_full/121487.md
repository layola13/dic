mislaying:/mɪsˈleɪɪŋ/
verb
放错地方；忘了放在哪里
The act of putting something in a place and then being unable to find it.
I'm always mislaying my keys. 我总是忘记把钥匙放在哪里了。
近义词: losing, misplacing
反义词: finding, locating
词性: verb (present participle of mislay)
