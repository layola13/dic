passover:/ˈpæsˌoʊvər/ (US) /ˈpɑːsˌəʊvə/ (UK)| n.|逾越节， Passover (犹太教节日)|A Jewish festival commemorating the exodus of the Israelites from slavery in Egypt.  It is celebrated in the spring, and involves a special meal called the Seder.|例句：We celebrate Passover every year. 我们每年都庆祝逾越节。

例句：The Passover Seder is a very important ritual. 逾越节家宴是一个非常重要的仪式。|近义词：Pesach (希伯来语)|反义词: None|词性: noun
