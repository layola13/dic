backcombed: /ˈbækˌkəʊmd/ (UK) /ˈbækˌkoʊmd/ (US)
adjective
逆梳的，蓬松的
Combed backwards, towards the scalp, so as to create volume or a tangled appearance.
Her hair was backcombed into a huge beehive. 她的头发逆梳成一个巨大的蜂窝状。
Her backcombed hair made her look taller. 她逆梳的头发使她看起来更高。
synonyms: teased, ratted, bouffant
antonyms: smooth, flat, sleek
词性: adjective
