misvouch:/mɪsˈvaʊtʃ/
verb
错误地担保；作不实的推荐
to vouch for incorrectly; to give a false recommendation
He misvouched for his friend's character, leading to problems later on. 他错误地担保了他朋友的品格，后来导致了问题。
The company misvouched for the product's quality, and now faces a lawsuit. 这家公司对产品的质量作了不实的担保，现在面临着诉讼。
近义词: misrepresent, belie
反义词: endorse, guarantee
词性: verb
