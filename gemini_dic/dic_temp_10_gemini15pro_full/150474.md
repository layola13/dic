pantherish:/ˈpænθəriʃ/| adj.|像豹的，豹子似的；(指人)阴险的，凶残的|Resembling or suggestive of a panther, especially in being stealthy, cunning, or ferocious.|例句：He moved with a pantherish grace. 他行动起来像豹子一样优雅。|例句：She gave him a pantherish look. 她给了他一个凶狠的眼神。|近义词：feline, stealthy, cunning, ferocious|反义词：gentle, kind, harmless|词性:形容词
词源：From panther + -ish.
