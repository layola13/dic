cambial:/ˈkæmbiəl/| adj.|形成层的， câmbio的|Relating to a cambium (a layer of tissue in plants that produces new cells).|The cambial zone is where growth occurs. 形成层区域是生长发生的地方。|Synonyms: meristematic, generative|Antonyms: differentiated, mature|adjective

cambial:/ˈkæmbiəl/| n.|形成层|A layer of tissue in plants that produces new cells for growth, particularly in stems and roots.|The tree's cambial layer was clearly visible. 这棵树的形成层清晰可见。|Synonyms: cambium, growth layer|Antonyms:  N/A (lack of a direct antonym for a physical structure)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *cambialis, from Latin cambiare "to exchange," from PIE root *kamb- "to bend, crook, exchange."  The sense shift arose from the idea of the cambium "exchanging" old cells for new ones.
