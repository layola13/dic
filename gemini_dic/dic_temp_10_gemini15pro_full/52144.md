diaphaned:/daɪˈæfənd/| adj.|透明的，半透明的，精致的|Characterized by such fineness of texture as to permit seeing through; transparent or translucent.  Also, delicately wrought; ethereal.|Her diaphaned gown shimmered in the candlelight. 她的透明长袍在烛光下闪闪发光。|近义词：transparent, translucent, sheer, gossamer, ethereal|反义词：opaque, dense, thick|词性:形容词
词源: From Greek diaphanēs, from diaphainein (to show through), from dia- (through) + phainein (to show).
