brachystochrone:/ˌbrækɪˈstəʊkrəʊn/ /ˌbrækiˈstɑːkroʊn/| n. |最速降线；摆线|A curve between two points along which a body descending under gravity travels from the higher to the lower point in the least time.|The brachistochrone problem, the challenge of finding the fastest descent curve, fascinated mathematicians for centuries.  最速降线问题，即寻找最快下降曲线的挑战，几个世纪以来一直吸引着数学家们。

The shape of a hanging chain is actually a catenary, not a brachistochrone, although they appear similar.  悬挂链的形状实际上是悬链线，而不是最速降线，尽管它们看起来很相似。|近义词:cycloid (摆线，虽然技术上略有不同，但在重力作用下，它是最速降线)|反义词: N/A |词性:noun


Etymology:
From Ancient Greek βράχιστος (brákhistos, “shortest”) + χρόνος (khrónos, “time”).
