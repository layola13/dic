impardonable:/ɪmˈpɑːrdənəbl/
adjective
不可原谅的，不可饶恕的
not able to be forgiven; unforgivable.
His conduct was simply impardonable. 他的行为简直不可饶恕。
近义词: unforgivable, inexcusable, unjustifiable
反义词: forgivable, excusable, justifiable
词性: adjective
词源: From in- + pardonable, from Old French pardoner, from Latin perdonare.
