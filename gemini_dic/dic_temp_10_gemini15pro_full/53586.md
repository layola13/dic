disbursable:/dɪsˈbɜːrsəbl/
adjective
可支付的，可拨付的
able to be paid out or disbursed.
The funds are disbursable upon completion of the project.  项目完成后，资金即可支付。
The scholarship money is disbursable in two installments. 奖学金分两次发放。
近义词:payable, distributable
反义词:non-disbursable, withheld
词性:adjective

词源:
From disburse + -able.  Disburse comes from Old French desborser, from des- (apart, away) + bourse (purse).
