bedazzles:/bɪˈdæzəlz/
verb
使眼花缭乱，使着迷
to impress or excite someone very much
The fireworks bedazzled the children. 焰火使孩子们眼花缭乱。
Synonyms: dazzle, fascinate, enthrall
Antonyms: bore, tire, disappoint
词性:动词

bedazzled:/bɪˈdæzəld/
adjective
眼花缭乱的，着迷的
impressed or excited by someone very much
I was bedazzled by her beauty. 我被她的美貌所倾倒。
Synonyms: dazzled, fascinated, enthralled
Antonyms: bored, tired, disappointed
词性:形容词


词源:  From be- + dazzle, from obsolete dazzle "to grow dim or blind" (early 15c.), of uncertain origin.  The sense shifted to "overwhelm with light, confuse the sight of" (1590s), probably influenced by daze.
