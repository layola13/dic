amblygonal: /æmˈblɪɡənəl/
| adj. |钝角的，具有钝角的|Having obtuse angles. An amblygonal figure has at least one obtuse angle.|The architect designed an amblygonal room to create a unique and interesting space.|近义词: obtuse-angled|反义词: acute-angled|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Greek *ἀμβλύς (amblýs)  meaning "blunt, obtuse" + -gonal from Greek *γωνία (gōnía) meaning "angle."
