opinionately:/əˈpɪnjəneɪtli/
adjective
固执己见地，武断地
Holding stubbornly and often unreasonably to one's own opinions.
She argued her point opinionately, refusing to consider any other perspectives. 她固执己见地争论着自己的观点，拒绝考虑任何其他看法。
dogmatically, stubbornly, assertively, peremptorily
open-mindedly, receptively, flexibly
adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From opinion + -ate + -ly.  "Opinion" comes from the Latin "opinio," meaning "thought, conjecture, belief."  The suffix "-ate" forms adjectives and verbs, and "-ly" creates adverbs.
