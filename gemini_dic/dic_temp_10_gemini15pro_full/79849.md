glyptotheca:/ˌɡlɪptəˈθiːkə/
n.
雕塑博物馆，雕刻收藏馆
A museum or building housing a collection of sculptures, especially ancient ones.
The Glyptothek in Munich houses a renowned collection of Greek and Roman sculptures.  慕尼黑的雕刻陈列馆收藏了著名的希腊和罗马雕塑。
The city's glyptotheca boasted an impressive array of marble statues. 这座城市的雕塑博物馆拥有令人印象深刻的各种大理石雕像。
Synonym: sculpture gallery, museum
Antonym: N/A (lack of a direct opposite)
