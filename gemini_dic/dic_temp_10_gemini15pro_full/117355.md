median: /ˈmiːdiən/ (US) /ˈmiːdiən/ (UK) | n. adj.| 中位数，中间的 |The middle value in a list of numbers when they are arranged in order. Also, relating to or situated in the middle. |The median house price in this area is $500,000.  这个地区房屋价格的中位数是50万美元。|Synonyms: average, middle, central| Antonyms: extreme, edge, outer | noun, adjective
