hingelike:/ˈhɪndʒˌlaɪk/| adj.|像铰链的，像枢纽的|Resembling a hinge in form or function; pivotal.|The door moved with a hingelike motion. 这扇门以类似铰链的方式移动。|近义词：pivotal, crucial, central|反义词：unimportant, peripheral|词性:形容词
词源：From *hinge* + *-like*. *Hinge* comes from Middle English *heng*, from Old English *henge*; akin to Old High German *hanga* slope, declivity, Old Norse *hengja* to hang.  *-like* is a common suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of."
