indifuscin:/ɪndɪˈfʌsɪn/
noun
indifuscin(不溶性脂褐素)
A yellowish-brown pigment found in the heart muscle and nerve cells, associated with aging.  It is considered a type of lipofuscin, also known as "wear and tear" pigment.
Example:  Indifuscin accumulation is observed in the aging heart.  在老化的心脏中观察到不溶性脂褐素的积累。
Example:  The study examined the relationship between indifuscin deposition and cardiac function.  该研究调查了不溶性脂褐素沉积与心脏功能之间的关系。
Synonym: lipofuscin, age pigment
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, as it's a naturally occurring substance)
