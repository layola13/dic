availer:/əˈveɪlər/| n.|利用者，受益者|Someone who takes advantage of something, especially an opportunity or resource. |Many small businesses are now availers of government grants. 许多小企业现在都是政府拨款的受益者。|Example:  New mothers are encouraged to be availers of the free childcare services offered by the city.  新妈妈们被鼓励成为城市提供的免费儿童保育服务的受益者。|Synonyms: user, utilizer, beneficiary, exploiter|Antonyms: non-user, provider|noun
