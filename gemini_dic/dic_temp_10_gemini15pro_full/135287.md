nonparticulate:/ˌnɒnpɑːˈtɪkjʊlət/
adjective
非颗粒状的; 非微粒的
Not consisting of distinct particles.
Filters can remove particulate and nonparticulate matter from water. 过滤器可以去除水中的颗粒物和非颗粒物。
The nonparticulate pollutants include gases like nitrogen oxides and sulfur dioxide. 非颗粒污染物包括氮氧化物和二氧化硫等气体。
synonyms:dissolved, non-granular, homogenous
antonyms:particulate, granular, heterogeneous
词性: adjective
