firmware's:/ˈfɜːrmwɛərz/
noun
固件（的）
Permanent software embedded in a hardware device, such as a ROM chip.  It provides the low-level control for the device's specific hardware.
The router's firmware needs to be updated. 路由器的固件需要更新。
近义词:software, microcode
反义词:hardware
