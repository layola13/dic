hudibrastic:/ˌhjuːdɪˈbræstɪk/| adj.|滑稽模仿的，以讽刺诗体写的，诙谐的|Of, relating to, or resembling the style of Samuel Butler's Hudibras, a 17th-century mock-heroic poem characterized by  doggerel verse and satirical wit.|The play's hudibrastic humor delighted the audience.  这出戏滑稽模仿的幽默使观众很开心。|Synonyms: burlesque, comical, mock-heroic, satirical, witty|Antonyms: serious, solemn, earnest|adjective
