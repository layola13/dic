lusciousness:/ˈlʌʃəsnəs/| n. |美味；甘美；芬芳；华丽|The state or quality of being luscious; deliciousness, richness, or luxuriance.|The lusciousness of the ripe peaches was irresistible.  成熟桃子的美味让人无法抗拒。|近义词：deliciousness, richness, sweetness, luxuriance|反义词：blandness, insipidity, dullness|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From luscious + -ness.  Luscious from Middle English *luscious, alteration of licious (“delicious”), from Anglo-Norman licius, from Old French licious (“delicious, pleasant”), from Latin deliciosus (“delightful, charming, luxurious”), from deliciae (“delight, pleasure, charm”), from delicere (“to allure, entice”), from de- (“from”) + lacere (“to entice, allure”).
