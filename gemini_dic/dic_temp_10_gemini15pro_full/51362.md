detergent:/dɪˈtɜːrdʒənt/ /dɪˈtɜːrdʒənt/| n. |洗涤剂，清洁剂|A substance used for cleaning, especially washing clothes or dishes.|例句：She added detergent to the washing machine. 她往洗衣机里加了洗涤剂。|近义词：cleaner, soap, cleanser|反义词:stain, dirt|noun

detergent:/dɪˈtɜːrdʒənt/ /dɪˈtɜːrdʒənt/| adj. |去污的，清洁的|Having the properties of a detergent.|例句：The detergent action of the soap removed the grease. 肥皂的去污作用去除了油脂。|近义词：cleaning, cleansing, purifying|反义词:dirtying, soiling|adjective
