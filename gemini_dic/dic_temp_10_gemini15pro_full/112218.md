loop:/luːp/| n. v. |环，圈，循环，弯曲部分；使成环，以环状物套住|A loop is a shape produced when a line curves round and crosses itself. |She tied a loop of rope around a post. 她把绳子绕在柱子上打了个圈。|近义词:circle, ring, coil, spiral|反义词:line, straight|词性:名词,动词

loop:/luːp/| v. |使成环，以环状物套住|To loop something means to make it into a loop, or to put a loop of something around it. |Loop the wire around the post. 把金属丝绕在柱子上。|近义词:circle, ring, coil, spiral|反义词:unloop, straighten|词性:动词
