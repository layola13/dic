immenseness:/ɪˈmɛnsnəs/| n. |巨大；广大；无限|The vastness or enormity of something; immeasurable size or extent.|She was struck by the immenseness of the ocean. 她被大海的浩瀚所震撼。|近义词：vastness, enormity, hugeness, immensity|反义词：smallness, tininess, insignificance|词性: noun
词源: From immense + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin in- (not) + mensus (measured).
