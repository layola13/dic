fiacre:/ˈfiːækrə(r)/ /ˈfaɪˌækrər/| n.|出租马车；四轮马车|A horse-drawn carriage for hire, especially a hackney carriage.|例句：The fiacre rattled over the cobblestones. 四轮马车在鹅卵石路上嘎嘎作响。

例句：She hailed a fiacre to take her to the opera. 她叫了一辆出租马车去歌剧院。|近义词：cab, carriage, hackney|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *fiacre*, from the name of Saint Fiacre, whose image was displayed outside a Paris carriage stand.
