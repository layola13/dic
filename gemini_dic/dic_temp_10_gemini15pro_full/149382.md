pacifistically:/ˌpæsɪˈfɪstɪkli/
adverb
和平地；非暴力地
In a peaceful and non-violent manner; opposing war or violence.
He argued pacifistically against the war. 他以和平主义的方式反对战争。
Synonyms:peacefully, nonviolently, antimilitaristically
Antonyms:violently, aggressively, militaristically
词性:副词
