overbrightly: /ˌoʊvərˈbraɪtli/ | adv. | 过于明亮地，过分鲜艳地 | Excessively bright; in a way that is too bright or showy.  It combines "over" (meaning excessively) with "brightly" (the adverb form of "bright"). | The stage was lit overbrightly, making the actors squint.  舞台灯光太亮了，使演员们眯起了眼睛。|  The clown wore an overbrightly colored suit. 小丑穿着一套过于鲜艳的衣服。 | 近义词: garishly, gaudily | 反义词: dimly, subtly | 词性: 副词
