cobra:/ˈkoʊbrə/ (US), /ˈkəʊbrə/ (UK)| n. |眼镜蛇|A venomous snake of the family Elapidae, characterized by its ability to flatten its neck ribs to form a hood when threatened.|例句：The cobra raised its hood and hissed. 眼镜蛇竖起颈部的皮褶并发出嘶嘶声。

例句：Cobras are found in Africa and Asia. 眼镜蛇分布在非洲和亚洲。|近义词：serpent, viper|反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑prey猎物)|词性:noun
