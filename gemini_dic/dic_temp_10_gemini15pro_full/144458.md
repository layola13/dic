outta:/ˈaʊtə/| prep. adv.|从...出来，离开，|Non-standard spelling of "out of".  Indicates departure or origin from a place, situation, or condition.  Used informally, especially in spoken English and dialects.|Get outta here!  离开这里！|She's outta luck. 她运气不好。|近义词：out of|反义词:into|词性:介词, 副词
