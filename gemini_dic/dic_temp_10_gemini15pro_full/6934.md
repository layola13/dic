anconeal:/ˌæŋˈkoʊniəl/| adj.| 肘的，尺骨的|Relating to the elbow; specifically, relating to the region of the olecranon (the bony point of the elbow).|The anconeal fossa is a triangular depression on the posterior surface of the humerus that receives the olecranon of the ulna when the forearm is extended.|Synonyms:  cubital, olecranal|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider terms relating to other body parts, e.g., carpal - wrist)|adjective
