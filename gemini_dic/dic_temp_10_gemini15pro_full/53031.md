diodes:/ˈdaɪoʊdz/| n. |二极管|A semiconductor device with two terminals, typically allowing the flow of current in one direction only. |The radio uses diodes to rectify the alternating current. 这台收音机使用二极管来整流交流电。|近义词：rectifier, semiconductor|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps a broken or malfunctioning diode)|noun
