fulgurate:/ˈfʌlɡjʊˌreɪt/
verb
烧灼，电灼
to destroy or remove (as diseased tissue) by electric current
The surgeon fulgurated the abnormal tissue.  外科医生用电灼法切除了异常组织。

近义词:cauterize, electrocauterize
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "heal" or "repair")
词性: verb
