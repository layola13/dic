magniloquent:/mæɡˈnɪləkwənt/| adj.|夸夸其谈的，辞藻华丽的|Using high-flown or bombastic language; loftily or pompously eloquent.|例句：The mayor's magniloquent speech impressed no one. 市长夸夸其谈的演讲没有打动任何人。|例句：His magniloquent style of writing often obscured the meaning of his text. 他辞藻华丽的写作风格常常掩盖了他文本的意义。|近义词：bombastic, grandiloquent, pompous, lofty|反义词：simple, plain, unpretentious, humble|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *magniloquus*, from *magnus* (large, great) + *loquor* (to speak).
