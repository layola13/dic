callisthenics:/ˌkælɪsˈθɛnɪks/| n. |健美操，徒手体操|Systematic rhythmic bodily exercises performed usually without apparatus or with light hand weights, designed to develop strength and grace.|例句：She practices callisthenics every morning to stay in shape. 她每天早上做健美操以保持身材。|例句：Callisthenics is a great way to improve your fitness without needing a gym. 健美操是一种无需去健身房就能提高身体素质的好方法。|近义词：exercises, gymnastics, workout|反义词:inactivity, laziness|Noun
