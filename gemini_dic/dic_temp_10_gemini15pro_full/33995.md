cite's:/saɪts/| n.|引用; 引文出处|A location or source of information.  It often refers to the specific place in a book, article, or website where information is found.|"The author failed to provide cites for his claims." 作者未能提供其论断的出处。|synonyms: source, reference, citation|antonyms: N/A|noun
