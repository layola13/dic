countercommercials:/ˌkaʊntər kəˈmɜːrʃəlz/
noun
反广告；公益广告
Advertisements that respond to and challenge the claims made in commercial advertisements, often addressing social or environmental issues.
Many countercommercials aim to raise awareness about the dangers of smoking. 许多反广告旨在提高人们对吸烟危害的认识。
Synonyms: public service announcements, advocacy ads
Antonyms: commercials, advertisements
n.

countercommercials:/ˌkaʊntər kəˈmɜːrʃəlz/
noun, plural
反广告；公益广告
A message broadcast on radio or television that attempts to counter the effects of commercial advertisements, often for a social cause.
The group produced a series of countercommercials highlighting the environmental damage caused by plastic pollution.  该组织制作了一系列反广告，突出了塑料污染造成的环境破坏。
Synonyms: public service announcements, advocacy ads, counter-advertising
Antonyms: commercials, advertisements, marketing campaigns
n.
