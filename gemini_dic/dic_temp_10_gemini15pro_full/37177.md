comeback:/ˈkʌmbæk/| n.|回归，复出，反驳|a return to a former position of power, success, or fame; a quick, witty reply.|例句：The band made a comeback after a ten-year break.  这支乐队在十年休整后复出。

例句：She had a witty comeback for every insult.  对于每一次侮辱，她都有机智的反驳。|近义词：return, revival, recovery, retort, reply|反义词:decline, downfall, departure|词性:名词

come back:/kʌm ˈbæk/| v.|回来，恢复，重新流行|to return to a place or condition; to become popular or successful again|例句：He promised to come back soon. 他承诺很快回来。

例句：That style is coming back into fashion.  那种风格又重新流行起来了。|近义词：return, reappear, revive|反义词:leave, depart, go away|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "come" + "back".  "Come" traces back to Old English "cuman," and "back" to Old English "bæc."  The combined form as a noun emerged in the late 19th century.
