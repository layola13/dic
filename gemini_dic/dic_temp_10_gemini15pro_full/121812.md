misreforms:/ˌmɪsrɪˈfɔːrmz/
noun
错误的改革，不当的改革
Reforms that are badly conceived, implemented, or have unintended negative consequences.
The government's misreforms in the education sector led to a decline in standards. 政府在教育部门的错误改革导致了标准的下降。
Misguided reforms have exacerbated the problem. 被误导的改革加剧了这个问题。
misguided reforms, bad reforms, flawed reforms
reforms, successful reforms
n.
