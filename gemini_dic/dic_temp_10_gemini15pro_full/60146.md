effectuate: /ɪˈfɛktʃueɪt/
verb
实施，实现，完成
To put into effect; to bring about; to cause to happen; to carry out; to achieve; to accomplish.
The government effectuated the new policy. 政府实施了新政策。
The company effectuated a merger with its competitor.  该公司完成了与竞争对手的合并。
synonyms: accomplish, achieve, complete, execute, fulfill, implement, realize
antonyms: abandon, fail, neglect, prevent, stop
词性: 动词
