partialness:/ˈpɑːrʃəl.nɪs/
n.|偏袒，偏心，不公|The judge was accused of partialness towards the defendant.  法官被指控偏袒被告。|例句：His partialness to his youngest child was obvious. 他对最小的孩子明显的偏心。|近义词：bias, favoritism, prejudice, unfairness|反义词:impartiality, fairness, neutrality|词性:名词

partialness:/ˈpɑːrʃəl.nɪs/
n.|偏爱，偏好|She showed a marked partialness for classical music. 她表现出对古典音乐的明显偏爱。|例句：He has a partialness for spicy food. 他偏爱辛辣食物。|近义词：preference, fondness, liking|反义词:indifference, aversion|词性:名词
