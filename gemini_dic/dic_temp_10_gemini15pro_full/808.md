abstained:/əbˈsteɪnd/| v. |戒除，弃权|To restrain oneself from doing or enjoying something.|He abstained from alcohol for a month. 他戒酒一个月了。|She abstained from voting in the election. 她在选举中弃权了。|近义词：refrain, forgo, decline, avoid|反义词：indulge, partake, participate|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English abstenen, from Old French abstenir, from Latin abstinēre (to hold oneself from, refrain from), from abs- (from, away from) + tenēre (to hold).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He abstains from smoking. = 他戒烟。
She abstains from voting on controversial issues. = 她对有争议的问题弃权投票。

一般过去时:
They abstained from eating meat during Lent. = 他们在四旬期戒肉。
He abstained from commenting on the situation. = 他避免对情况发表评论。

现在完成时:
She has abstained from alcohol since she became pregnant. = 自从怀孕以来，她一直戒酒。
He has abstained from voting in every election. = 他每次选举都弃权。

过去完成时:
He had abstained from sugar for a year before he had a relapse. = 他戒糖一年后复发了。
She had abstained from expressing her opinion until she was asked directly. = 她一直弃权表达意见，直到被直接问到。

将来时:
I will abstain from caffeine before the race. = 我会在比赛前戒掉咖啡因。
He will abstain from voting if he doesn't understand the issue. = 如果他不理解这个问题，他将弃权投票。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would abstain from making any promises. = 如果我是你，我会避免做出任何承诺。
I wish I had abstained from eating that last piece of cake. = 我希望我没有吃最后一块蛋糕。

被动语态:
Further comment was abstained from. = 没有进一步的评论。(此处被动语态略显正式和古板，更常见的是主动表达)


疑问句:
Why did you abstain from voting? = 你为什么弃权投票？
Does he usually abstain from dessert? = 他通常不吃甜点吗？

否定句:
He did not abstain from expressing his disapproval. = 他没有避免表达他的反对意见。
She doesn't abstain from any kind of food. = 她什么食物都不忌口。

PHRASE->

abstain from sex = 禁欲
