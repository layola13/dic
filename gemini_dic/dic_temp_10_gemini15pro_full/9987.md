aphorists:/ˈæfərɪsts/
n.
格言家；警句作者
A person who writes or speaks aphorisms (short, witty sayings).
The book is a collection of writings by various aphorists. 这本书是 various 格言家作品的合集。
Writers, philosophers, thinkers
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps)  prolix writers, rambling speakers
noun
