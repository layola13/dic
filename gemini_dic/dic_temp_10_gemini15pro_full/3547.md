agalactous:/ˌeɪɡəˈlæktəs/| adj.|无乳的，不分泌乳汁的|Not producing milk; lacking milk.  Derived from Greek *a-* (without) + *galakt-* (milk) + *-ous* (adjective ending).|The agalactous cow was unable to feed her calf. 这头不分泌乳汁的母牛无法喂养她的小牛。|Synonyms: milkless, lactless|Antonyms: lactating, milk-producing|adjective
