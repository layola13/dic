daymaid:/ˈdeɪmeɪd/
n.
女佣，白天做家务的帮佣
A female servant who performs household duties during the day.
The daymaid came in every morning to clean the house.  女佣每天早上都来打扫房子。
近义词:  housemaid, maid, charwoman, cleaning lady
反义词:  employer, master
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From day + maid (Old English mægden "maiden, virgin, female servant").  由 day + maid（古英语 mægden “少女、处女、女仆”）组成。
