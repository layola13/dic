exhalation:/ˌɛks(h)əˈleɪʃən/ /ˌɛk.səˈleɪ.ʃən/
n.|呼气，呼出；散发，发出|The exhalation of breath forms a cloud in cold air.  呼出的气在冷空气中形成一团雾。|近义词：expiration, breath, emission|反义词：inhalation, inspiration|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin exhalatio, from Latin exhalare "to breathe out," from ex- "out" + halare "to breathe."
