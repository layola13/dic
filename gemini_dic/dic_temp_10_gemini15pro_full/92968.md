idil:/ˈɪdɪl/ | n. | 田园诗; 牧歌; | A short description of a peaceful or romantic scene, especially in the countryside. |  Example: The poem painted an idyll of rural life. 这首诗描绘了一幅乡村生活的田园诗。

Example:  Their honeymoon was an idyll of romantic walks and quiet evenings. 他们的蜜月是一段浪漫散步和宁静夜晚的田园诗。 | 近义词:pastoral, romance | 反义词: nightmare, ordeal | 词性:noun

词源: From French *idylle*, from Late Latin *idyllis*, from Greek *eidyllion* (“little picture, short poem”), diminutive of *eidos* (“form, shape”).
