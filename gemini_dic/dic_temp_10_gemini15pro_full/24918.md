brunched:/brʌntʃt/
verb
吃早午餐；
had brunch; ate a late morning meal combining breakfast and lunch.
We brunched at a cafe on Sunday. 我们周日在一个咖啡馆吃早午餐。
They brunched on pancakes and bacon.  他们早午餐吃了煎饼和培根。
近义词: had a late breakfast, had an early lunch
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑) skipped brunch, ate a separate breakfast and lunch
词性: verb (past tense of "brunch")
