melinite:/ˈmɛlɪnaɪt/
noun
苦味酸;黄色炸药
A high explosive made from picric acid.
Melinite was used as the main explosive filling for French artillery shells in World War I.  苦味酸在一战中被用作法国炮弹的主要爆炸填充物。
The French army adopted melinite as their standard high explosive. 法国军队采用苦味酸作为他们的标准高爆炸药。
Synonyms: lyddite, picric acid
Antonyms: N/A (No direct antonyms for explosives)
词性: n.
