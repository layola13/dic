anthropomorphously:/ˌænθrəpəˈmɔːrfɪkli/ | adv. | 拟人地，人格化地 |  In a way that gives human characteristics to non-human things, such as animals or objects. | The author anthropomorphously described the tree as waving its branches in greeting.  作者将树拟人化，描述它挥舞着树枝表示问候。 |  humanly, personally | impersonally | adverb
