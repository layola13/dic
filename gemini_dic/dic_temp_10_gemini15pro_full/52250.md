dibranchiata:/ˌdaɪbræŋkiˈɑːtə/ | n. |  双鳃类 |Cephalopods with two gills, such as squid, cuttlefish, and octopuses.  These mollusks are characterized by their internal shell (or lack thereof), advanced nervous system, and excellent eyesight. | Squid and octopuses are members of the Dibranchiata.  乌贼和章鱼是双鳃类的成员。| synonym:  Coleoidea | antonym: Tetrabranchiata (although this group is now considered paraphyletic) | noun
