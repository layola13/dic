mosaicking:/moʊˈzeɪɪkɪŋ/| n. |镶嵌；镶嵌工艺；镶嵌图案|The process of creating a mosaic, or a mosaic itself.  The act of assembling small pieces of colored glass, stone, or other materials to create a decorative image or pattern.|The mosaicking in the church was incredibly detailed. 教堂里的马赛克装饰非常精致。|Near synonyms:  tessellation, tiling, inlay, patchwork|Antonym:  fragmentation|Noun

mosaicking:/moʊˈzeɪɪkɪŋ/| adj. |用于镶嵌的|Used for or relating to making mosaics.|Mosaicking tiles were imported from Italy. 用于镶嵌的瓷砖是从意大利进口的。|Near synonyms:  tessellated, inlaid|Antonym:  N/A|Adjective
