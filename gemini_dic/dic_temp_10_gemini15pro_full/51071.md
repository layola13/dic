despairers:/dɪˈspɛərərz/
n.
绝望者，感到绝望的人
People who have lost all hope.
The despairers sat huddled together, their faces etched with grief.  绝望的人们挤在一起坐着，脸上写满了悲伤。
The refugees, many of them despairers, clung to the hope of a better future. 许多难民，他们中的许多人都感到绝望，仍然 clinging to the hope of a better future。
近义词:pessimists, cynics
反义词:optimists, hopers
词性:名词
