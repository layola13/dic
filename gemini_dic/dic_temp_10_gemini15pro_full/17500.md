bearward:/ˈbeərˌwɔːrd/ | n. | 熊管理员，养熊人 | A person who keeps or tends bears, especially for entertainment. |  The bearward led the chained bear through the streets. 养熊人牵着被锁链拴住的熊穿过街道。| Synonyms: bear keeper, bear handler | Antonyms: None | noun

bearward:/ˈbeərˌwɔːrd/ | adj. (obsolete) | 向着熊的；与熊有关的 |  Moving or facing towards a bear.  |  He took a bearward step, cautiously approaching the den. 他小心翼翼地向熊窝靠近，迈出了朝着熊的一步。 (This usage is archaic and rarely encountered.) | Synonyms: None | Antonyms: None | adjective
