cavilingly:/ˈkævɪlɪŋli/ | adv. | 吹毛求疵地，挑剔地 | In a manner that raises petty objections or makes trivial criticisms.  | He cavilingly criticized every aspect of the project, from the font choice to the paper quality. 他对项目的各个方面都吹毛求疵，从字体选择到纸张质量。|  He cavilingly pointed out a minor grammatical error in the otherwise perfect essay.  他在一篇本来很完美的文章中吹毛求疵地指出一个小小的语法错误。 | synonym: carpingly, nitpickingly, quibblingly | antonym: approvingly, generously, uncritically | adverb
