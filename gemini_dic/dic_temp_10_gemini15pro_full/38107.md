compounders:/kəmˈpaʊndərz/| n.|复利者，混合者，调配者|A person or thing that compounds something, especially a pharmacist who prepares medicines by combining ingredients.|The compounders carefully measured and mixed the ingredients for the medication. 调配师仔细地测量和混合药物的成分。|近义词：mixer, blender, combiner|反义词:separator, divider|noun

compounders:/kəmˈpaʊndərz/| n.|加重者，使恶化者|Something that makes a problem or difficult situation worse.|The lack of communication was a major compounder of the conflict. 缺乏沟通是冲突的主要加剧因素。|近义词：aggravator, intensifier, exacerbator|反义词:alleviator, mitigator|noun
