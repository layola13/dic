compendium:/kəmˈpɛndiəm/| n.|概要，纲要，简编|a collection of concise but detailed information about a particular subject, especially in a book or other publication. |例句：The book is a compendium of useful information for travelers. 这本书是旅行者实用信息的概要。

例句：She compiled a compendium of folklore and traditions. 她汇编了一本民间传说和传统的概要。|近义词：summary, abstract, synopsis, digest, compilation, anthology|反义词:expansion, enlargement|词性:noun
