instill:/ɪnˈstɪl/| vt.|灌输，逐渐灌输|to gradually introduce an idea or feeling into someone's mind, so that it has a strong influence on the way they think or behave.|例句：The coach instilled a sense of discipline in his players. 教练向他的队员灌输纪律意识。

例句：Her parents instilled in her a love of reading. 她的父母在她心中灌输了对阅读的热爱。|近义词：implant, inculcate, infuse, introduce|反义词：eradicate, remove, extract|词性：动词
