beaks:/biːks/
noun
喙; 鸟嘴
the hard pointed or curved outer part of a bird’s mouth
The parrot used its beak to crack open the nut.  鹦鹉用它的喙敲开了坚果。

近义词:bill, nib
反义词:None
词性:n. (noun)
