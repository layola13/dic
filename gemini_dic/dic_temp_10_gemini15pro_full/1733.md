acquaints:/əˈkweɪnts/ | vt. | 使熟悉，使认识 | To make someone aware of or familiar with something. |  She acquainted herself with the rules of the game before playing. 她玩游戏前先熟悉了游戏规则。

The guide acquainted the tourists with the local customs. 导游让游客们了解了当地的风俗习惯。 |  familiarize, introduce | alienate, estrange | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *acointer, from a- (to) + cointe (known, acquainted), from Latin cognitus (known).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He acquaints himself with the new regulations. = 他让自己熟悉新规章。
She acquaints her students with the basics of grammar. = 她让学生们了解语法基础知识。

一般过去时:
I acquainted him with my concerns. = 我让他了解了我的担忧。
The teacher acquainted the class with the new syllabus. = 老师让全班同学了解了新的教学大纲。

现在完成时:
The company has acquainted its employees with the new policies. = 公司已经让员工们了解了新的政策。
They have acquainted themselves with the local culture. = 他们已经熟悉了当地文化。

过去完成时:
She had acquainted herself with the material before the exam. = 她在考试前已经熟悉了这些材料。
He had acquainted his boss with the project's progress. = 他在开会前已经让他的老板了解了项目的进展。

将来时:
The manager will acquaint the team with the new strategy. = 经理将让团队熟悉新的策略。
I will acquaint myself with the software before starting the project. = 我会在开始项目之前熟悉这个软件。

虚拟语气:

I wish I had acquainted myself with the situation earlier. = 我希望我早点了解情况。
