dodger:/ˈdɑːdʒər/ (US) /ˈdɒdʒər/ (UK)| n.|躲避者，逃避者，骗子|A person who avoids something, especially a duty or responsibility.  Someone who is skillful at dodging.|He's a tax dodger. 他是个逃税者。|The dodger quickly moved out of the way of the oncoming ball. 这个躲避者迅速躲开了迎面而来的球。|synonyms: evader, shirker, avoider, cheat, swindler|antonyms: conformist, honest person|noun
