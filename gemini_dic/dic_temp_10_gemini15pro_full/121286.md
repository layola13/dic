misfeign:/ˌmɪsˈfeɪn/
verb
假装；伪装；装作
To feign something falsely; to pretend with a false show.
He misfeigned illness to avoid going to school. 他装病不去上学。
近义词: sham, simulate, pretend
反义词: be genuine, be sincere
词性: verb
