hyperenergetic:/ˌhaɪpərˌɛnərˈdʒɛtɪk/
adjective
极度活跃的；精力过剩的
Excessively energetic; extremely active.
例句：The hyperenergetic puppy bounced off the walls.  精力过剩的小狗在墙上蹦来蹦去。

例句：He's a hyperenergetic child who never seems to tire. 他是个精力过剩的孩子，好像永远不知疲倦。
近义词：overactive, frenetic, exuberant, dynamic
反义词：lethargic, sluggish, apathetic, listless
词性: adjective


词源:
From hyper- + energetic.  "Hyper-" is a prefix meaning "over, above, beyond," derived from Greek "hyper" (over, beyond).  "Energetic" comes from Late Latin "energeticus," from Greek "energetikos" (active, operative), from "energos" (active, working), from "en-" (in) + "ergon" (work).
