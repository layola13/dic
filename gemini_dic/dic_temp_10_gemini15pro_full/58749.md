dunghill:/ˈdʌŋhɪl/| n.|粪堆，垃圾堆，卑贱的地位|A pile of dung or refuse; a place of degradation or squalor. |The rooster strutted proudly atop the dunghill. 公鸡骄傲地在粪堆上昂首阔步。|Synonyms: muckheap, manure pile, compost heap|Antonyms:  palace, mansion|noun


dunghill:/ˈdʌŋhɪl/| adj.|卑贱的，可鄙的|Low, mean, or contemptible. |He called him a dunghill coward. 他称他是一个卑鄙的懦夫。|Synonyms: base, vile, despicable|Antonyms: noble, honorable|adjective
