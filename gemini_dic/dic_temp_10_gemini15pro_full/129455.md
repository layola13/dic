non-adherent:/ˌnɒn ədˈhɪərənt/ | adj. | 非粘附的，不依附的 | Not sticking to something; not adhering.  Relating to a substance or material that does not stick to another substance or material. Can also refer to someone who does not adhere to a particular belief, opinion, or cause. | The non-adherent bandage was easy to remove.  这种非粘连绷带很容易去除。

The doctor prescribed a non-adherent dressing for the wound. 医生给伤口开了不粘连的敷料。 | 近义词: non-adhesive, loose, detached | 反义词: adherent, adhesive, attached | 词性: adjective

non-adherent:/ˌnɒn ədˈhɪərənt/ | n. | 不依附者，不服从者 | A person who does not adhere to a particular belief, opinion, or cause. |  Non-adherents to the political party were often ostracized. 不依附该政党的人经常被排斥。

Many non-adherents to the religion chose to live outside the community. 许多不信奉该宗教的人选择住在社区外。 | 近义词: dissenter, rebel, nonconformist | 反义词: adherent, follower, supporter | 词性: noun
