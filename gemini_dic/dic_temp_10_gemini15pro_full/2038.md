aculeated:/əˈkjuːlieɪtɪd/
adjective
有刺的；尖的
Having a sharp point; prickly.
The aculeated leaves of the holly bush are a beautiful but painful decoration. 冬青灌木多刺的叶子是一种美丽却带刺的装饰。
The cactus is an aculeated plant, covered in sharp spines. 仙人掌是一种多刺的植物，全身覆盖着尖刺。
近义词: prickly, spiny, thorny, barbed
反义词: smooth, blunt
词性: adjective

USAGE->
The aculeated leaves of the thistle protected it from grazing animals. 刺蓟带刺的叶子保护它免受食草动物的啃食。

Roses are beautiful but aculeated. 玫瑰很美，但多刺。

词源: From Latin aculeatus, past participle of aculeare (“to furnish with prickles”), from aculeus (“a sting, prickle, thorn”).  源自拉丁语 aculeatus，aculeare（“用刺装备”）的过去分词，来自 aculeus（“刺、尖刺、荆棘”）。
