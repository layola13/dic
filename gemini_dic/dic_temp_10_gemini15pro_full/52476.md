dietetic:/ˌdaɪəˈtɛtɪk/ | adj. | 饮食的；营养学的 | Relating to diet or to the regulation of food and nutrition. |  Example 1:  Dietetic advice is crucial for managing diabetes.  饮食建议对控制糖尿病至关重要。

Example 2: She pursued a career in dietetic counseling. 她从事营养咨询的职业。|  Synonyms:  nutritional, dietary | Antonyms:  None | Adjective


dietetics:/ˌdaɪəˈtɛtɪks/ | n. | 营养学；饮食学 | The science of applying the principles of nutrition to the feeding of individuals or groups, especially in health and disease. | Example 1:  She studied dietetics in college. 她在大学学习营养学。

Example 2:  Dietetics plays a vital role in public health. 营养学在公共卫生中起着至关重要的作用。| Synonyms:  nutrition, nutri dietetics | Antonyms: None | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek diaita "manner of living," later "diet," from diaitasthai "lead one's life," from dia "through" + aitasthai "to ask, demand."
