light-footedly:/ˌlaɪtˈfʊtɪdli/
adv.|轻快地，轻盈地|Moving and stepping lightly and gracefully.|She danced light-footedly across the stage. 她轻盈地在舞台上跳舞。|lightly, nimbly, gracefully|heavily, clumsily|副词

light-footedly:/ˌlaɪtˈfʊtɪdli/
Etymology:  From "light" (referring to weight) + "foot" + "-ed" (forming an adjective) + "-ly" (forming an adverb).  The sense is of having light feet, thus moving nimbly and gracefully.
词源: 由“light”（指重量）+“foot”（脚）+“-ed”（构成形容词）+“-ly”（构成副词）组成。意思是脚步轻盈，从而移动轻快而优雅。
