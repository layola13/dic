liberal:/ˈlɪbərəl/ (US) /ˈlɪbrəl/ (UK)| adj. n.|自由主义的；自由主义者；慷慨的；丰富的|Favoring individual liberty, free trade, and moderate political and social reform.  A person with such beliefs. Generous or given freely.|
例句：He has liberal views on immigration. 他对移民持自由主义观点。

例句：She made a liberal donation to the charity. 她向慈善机构慷慨解囊。

近义词：progressive, open-minded, generous, bountiful|
反义词：conservative, restrictive, stingy, meager|
词性：adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "liberalis" (meaning "of freedom, pertaining to a freeman, worthy of a free man, generous"), derived from "liber" (meaning "free").
