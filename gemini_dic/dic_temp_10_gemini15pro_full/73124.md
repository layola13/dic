for-the-love-of:/fɔːr ðə lʌv əv/
|phrase|
|为了...的热爱; 看在...的份上|
|Used to express exasperation, annoyance, or disbelief, often as an appeal.  It emphasizes strong feelings related to the following noun.  It can also express deep affection or passion for something.|
|For the love of God, turn that music down!|看在上帝的份上，把音乐关小点！

|For the love of all that is holy, please stop talking!|看在所有神圣之物的份上，请停止说话！

|She paints for the love of art.|她为了对艺术的热爱而绘画。

|近义词: for God's sake, for heaven's sake, for Pete's sake|
|反义词: (No direct antonym, but the opposite sentiment might be expressed with phrases indicating apathy or hatred)|
|词性: phrase|
