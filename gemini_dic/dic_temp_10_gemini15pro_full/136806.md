nonshattering:/ˌnɒnˈʃætərɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnˈʃætərɪŋ/| adj.|非破碎的，防碎的|Not shattering; resistant to shattering.  This describes something that doesn't break into pieces or fragments easily upon impact.|The car's nonshattering windshield prevented serious injuries in the accident. 汽车的防碎挡风玻璃在事故中避免了严重伤害。|Near synonyms: shatterproof, shatter-resistant, unbreakable, toughened|Antonyms: shattering, brittle, fragile|adjective
