coverable:/ˈkʌvərəbl/| adj.|可覆盖的，可掩盖的|Something that is coverable can be covered.  This could be with a physical object like a blanket or lid, or it can be metaphorically covered, like an expense being coverable by insurance.|The damage to the car was coverable by my insurance policy. 汽车的损坏在我的保险范围内。|Synonyms: protectable, shieldable, insurable|Antonyms: uncoverable, exposed, unprotected|adjective
