paneer:/pəˈniːr/| n.| 印度奶酪，芝士 |A fresh cheese common in South Asian cuisine. It is an unaged, non-melting farmer cheese made by curdling heated milk with lemon juice, vinegar, or any other food acid.|  Paneer is a key ingredient in many Indian dishes. 印度奶酪是许多印度菜肴中的关键成分。|  I love paneer tikka masala. 我喜欢印度奶酪咖喱。 | 近义词: cheese, curd | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
