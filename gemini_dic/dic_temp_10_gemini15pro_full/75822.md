fumigate:/ˈfjuːmɪɡeɪt/| vt.|熏蒸，烟熏消毒|To expose to smoke or fumes, especially for disinfection or to destroy pests.|例句：They fumigated the house to get rid of the termites. 他们熏蒸房子以除掉白蚁。

例句：The warehouse was fumigated after the discovery of rats.  在发现老鼠后，仓库进行了熏蒸。|近义词：disinfect, sterilize, sanitize|反义词:contaminate, infect|词性：动词

fumigation:/ˌfjuːmɪˈɡeɪʃn/| n.|熏蒸，烟熏消毒|The act of fumigating something.|例句：The fumigation of the building took several hours. 这栋楼的熏蒸工作花了几个小时。

例句：Regular fumigation is necessary to prevent infestations. 定期熏蒸对于预防虫害是必要的。|近义词：disinfection, sterilization, sanitization|反义词:contamination, infection|词性：名词
