insipient:/ɪnˈsɪpiənt/| adj.|初期的，刚出现的|Beginning to exist or appear; in an early stage.|例句：The project is still in its incipient stages, but it shows great promise.  这个项目仍处于初期阶段，但它显示出巨大的潜力。

例句：We noticed the incipient signs of a rebellion brewing amongst the workers. 我们注意到工人们开始出现叛乱的迹象。|近义词：beginning, nascent, initial, developing, embryonic|反义词：developed, mature, advanced, established|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *incipientem (nominative incipiens), present participle of incipere "to begin," from in- "in, on" + capere "to take, seize."
