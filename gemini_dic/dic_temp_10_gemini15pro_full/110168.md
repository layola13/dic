lien:/ˈliːən/ | n. |留置权；扣押权|A right to keep possession of property belonging to another person until a debt owed by that person is discharged.|The bank had a lien on the property until the loan was repaid. 银行对该房产拥有留置权，直到贷款还清。|synonyms: charge, security, encumbrance|antonyms: release|noun

lien:/ˈliːən/ | v. |对…有留置权;扣押|To have a legal claim or right on someone's property as security for a debt.|The creditor liened the debtor's assets. 债权人扣押了债务人的资产。|synonyms: attach, seize|antonyms: release|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *lien (“a tie, bond”), from Latin ligamen (“band, tie”), from ligare (“to bind”).
