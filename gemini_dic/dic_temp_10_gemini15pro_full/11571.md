aristotelian:/ˌærɪstəˈtiːliən/
adjective
亚里士多德的；亚里士多德哲学的
Relating to the philosophy of Aristotle.
例句：His arguments were based on Aristotelian logic. 他的论证基于亚里士多德的逻辑。

例句：The Aristotelian view of the universe placed Earth at the center.  亚里士多德的宇宙观认为地球是宇宙的中心。

近义词：peripatetic
反义词：Platonic
词性：adjective


aristotelic:/ˌærɪstəˈtɛlɪk/
adjective
目的论的；具有内在目的的
Exhibiting or relating to a view of the universe in which everything has a purpose or final cause.  (Often contrasted with a mechanistic view).
例句：An Aristotelic explanation of a bird's wings would emphasize their role in enabling flight, their purpose. 对鸟翅膀的亚里士多德式解释会强调它们在使鸟类能够飞翔的作用，即它们的目的。

例句：An Aristotelic perspective sees human life as striving towards self-realization. 亚里士多德的观点认为，人类生活是为了追求自我实现。

近义词：teleological, purposive
反义词：mechanistic
词性：adjective
