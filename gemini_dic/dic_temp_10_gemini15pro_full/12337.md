asexualization:/ˌeɪˈsekʃuəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |无性化，去性化|The process of depriving someone of their sexuality or sexual characteristics, either physically or psychologically.  It can also refer to the societal or cultural suppression of sexuality.|例句：Asexualization of women in media often depicts them as passive and devoid of sexual agency.  媒体中女性的无性化通常将她们描绘成被动且缺乏性主体性的。

例句：The asexualization of children is crucial for their healthy development.  对儿童进行去性化对于他们的健康发展至关重要。|近义词：desexualization, neutralization of sexuality|反义词：sexualization|词性：名词
