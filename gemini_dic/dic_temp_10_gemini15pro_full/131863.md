nonconsumers:/ˌnɑːnkənˈsuːmərz/ |n.| 非消费者 |People who do not consume a particular product or service.  Those who abstain from purchasing or using certain goods or services, often for ethical, environmental, or economic reasons. |  Many nonconsumers choose to avoid fast fashion due to its environmental impact. 许多非消费者由于快时尚对环境的影响而选择避免购买。

Many nonconsumers of meat are motivated by animal welfare concerns. 许多不吃肉的非消费者是出于对动物福利的担忧。 | near synonyms: abstainers, boycotters, non-users | antonyms: consumers, users, customers | noun
