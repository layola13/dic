nonrhyming:/ˌnɒnˈraɪmɪŋ/ | adj. | 非押韵的，不押韵的 | Not rhyming; having no rhymes.  | The poem was deliberately nonrhyming, giving it a more modern feel.  这首诗特意不押韵，使其更具现代感。 | Example:  She prefers nonrhyming poetry. 她更喜欢非押韵的诗歌。 | Synonyms: unrhymed, blank | Antonyms: rhyming, rhymed | Adjective


Etymology->
From non- + rhyming, present participle of rhyme.
