| adj. n.| 反保护主义的；反保护主义者 | Opposed to protectionism (the theory or practice of shielding a country's domestic industries from foreign competition by taxing imports).|  The senator is a staunch antiprotectionist, advocating for free trade agreements.  这位参议员是一位坚定的反保护主义者，倡导自由贸易协定。|  He wrote an antiprotectionist article for the newspaper. 他为报纸写了一篇反保护主义的文章。 | synonyms: free trader, liberal (in economics), advocate of free trade | antonyms: protectionist, mercantilist | adjective
