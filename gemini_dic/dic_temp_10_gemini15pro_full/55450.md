ETYMOLOGY->
From distinguishable + -ness.
Distinguishable: from distinguish + -able.
Distinguish: from Old French distinguer (to distinguish, separate, divide), from Latin distinguere (to separate by pricking, mark, distinguish), from dis- (apart) + stinguere (to prick, extinguish).
