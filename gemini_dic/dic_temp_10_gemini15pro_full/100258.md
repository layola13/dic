intersales:/ˈɪntərseɪlz/
noun
相互销售；部门间销售；内部销售
The exchange of goods or services between different departments or divisions within the same company or organization.
Intersales agreements can help companies optimize resource allocation. 部门间销售协议可以帮助公司优化资源配置。
Intersales between departments can improve efficiency. 部门间的内部销售可以提高效率。
近义词: internal sales, cross-selling, interdepartmental sales
反义词: external sales
词性: noun
