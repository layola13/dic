nonidiomatical:/ˌnɒnɪdiəˈmætɪkl/ | adj. | 非惯用的，不合乎习惯用法的 | Not characteristic of the natural or fluent way in which a language is spoken or written by native speakers. |  His speech was formal and somewhat nonidiomatical. 他的讲话很正式，有些不合乎习惯用法。 |  unidiomatic, unnatural, awkward | idiomatic, natural, fluent | adjective
