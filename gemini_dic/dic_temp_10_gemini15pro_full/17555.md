beatnik: /ˈbiːtnɪk/ | n. | 垮掉的一代成员，颓废派艺术家 | A member of the Beat Generation, a group of American writers and artists popular in the 1950s and early 1960s, who rejected conventional social values and experimented with nonconformist lifestyles, often involving drugs, jazz music, and Eastern religions. |  Jack Kerouac's novel "On the Road" captured the essence of the beatnik lifestyle.  杰克·凯鲁亚克的小说《在路上》捕捉了垮掉一代生活方式的精髓。 | synonyms: hipster, nonconformist, bohemian | antonyms: conformist, traditionalist | noun

beatnik: /ˈbiːtnɪk/ | adj. | 垮掉的一代的，颓废派的 | Relating to or characteristic of the Beat Generation. |  She wore beatnik clothes and listened to bebop jazz. 她穿着垮掉一代的服装，听着比波普爵士乐。 | synonyms: hipster, bohemian | antonyms: conventional, traditional | adjective
