methodical:/məˈθɒdɪkəl/ (US) /məˈθɑːdɪkəl/| adj.|有条理的，有条不紊的，井井有条的|Done according to a systematic or established form of procedure.|She is a methodical worker and never misses a detail. 她工作很有条理，从不漏掉任何细节。|例句：He adopted a methodical approach to solving the problem. 他采用了一种有条不紊的方法来解决这个问题。|近义词：systematic, organized, orderly, structured|反义词：disorganized, chaotic, haphazard, random|词性:形容词
詞源: Late 16th century: from French méthodique or late Latin methodicus, from Greek methodos ‘pursuit of knowledge’.
