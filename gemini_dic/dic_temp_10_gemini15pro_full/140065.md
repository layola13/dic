ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek οἶστρος (oîstros, “gadfly; frenzy, passion”) + -gen, from Ancient Greek -γενής (-genḗs, “producing, produced by”).  The term reflects the association of these hormones with the estrous cycle in mammals.
