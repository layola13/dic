biorhythmists:/ˌbaɪoʊˈrɪðəmɪsts/
n.
生物节律学家
A person who believes in or studies biorhythms.
Biorhythmists claim that our lives are governed by predictable cycles. 生物节律学家声称，我们的生活是由可预测的周期支配的。
synonyms:  biorhythm believer, cycle theorist
antonyms: skeptic, doubter
noun

Etymology:
bio- + rhythm + -ist
bio-: from Greek bios "life"
rhythm: from Greek rhythmos "measured motion, time, proportion"
-ist: a suffix forming agent nouns, denoting a person who practices or is concerned with something specified.
