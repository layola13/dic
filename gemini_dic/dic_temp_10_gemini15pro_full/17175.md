battue:/bæˈtjuː/| n.|集体围猎，大规模捕杀，（尤指政治上的）清洗|An organized slaughter of animals, especially game driven towards waiting hunters; a vigorous attack or criticism of someone or something.|例句：The annual pheasant battue on the estate was a grand affair.  庄园一年一度的野鸡围猎是一件盛事。

例句：The CEO faced a battue of questions from angry investors. 首席执行官面临着愤怒的投资者的一连串质问。|近义词：slaughter, massacre, onslaught, barrage|反义词:conservation, preservation|词性:名词

battue:/bæˈtjuː/| v.|集体围猎，大规模捕杀|To hunt game by beating the woods and driving the animals towards waiting hunters.|例句：They planned to battue the area for wild boar. 他们计划在该地区围猎野猪。

例句：The opposition parties battened the government with criticism. 反对党对政府进行了猛烈的抨击。|近义词：hunt, kill, attack, criticize|反义词:protect, defend|词性:动词
