comfily:/ˈkʌmfɪli/| adv.|舒适地，安逸地|In a comfortable manner.  Derived from "comfortable" with the suffix "-ly" to form an adverb.|She sat comfily in the armchair by the fire. 她舒服地坐在火炉旁的扶手椅上。|comfortably, snugly, cozily|uncomfortably, awkwardly|adverb

Etymology:
From "comfortable" + "-ly". "Comfortable" comes from the late Middle English *comfortable*, from Old French confortable, from Late Latin confortabilis, from Latin confortare "to strengthen," from com- "with" + fortis "strong."  The suffix "-ly" is a common adverbial ending.
