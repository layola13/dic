leucine: /ˈluːsiːn/ (US), /ˈljuːsiːn/ (UK)| n.|亮氨酸|An essential amino acid, one of the branched-chain amino acids (BCAAs), not synthesized by the body and must be obtained through diet.|Leucine is important for muscle protein synthesis. 亮氨酸对肌肉蛋白质合成很重要。|Leucine is found in foods like meat, fish, and eggs. 亮氨酸存在于肉类、鱼类和鸡蛋等食物中。|Synonyms: amino acid, BCAA|Antonyms: None|Noun
