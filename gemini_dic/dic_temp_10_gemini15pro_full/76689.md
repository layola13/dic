galvanoscope:/ˌɡælvəˈnəskoʊp/ | n. | 电流计，检流计 | An instrument for detecting the presence and direction of a small electric current. |  The scientist used a galvanoscope to detect the weak current. 科学家用检流计探测微弱的电流。| synonyms: ammeter, current detector | antonyms: None | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *galvanoscope*, from *galvano-* (referring to Luigi Galvani, Italian physician and physicist who studied animal electricity) + *-scope* (from Greek *skopein* "to look at").
