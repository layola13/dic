haemal:/ˈhiːməl/| adj.|血液的；血管的|Relating to the blood or blood vessels.|The haemal arch of a fish vertebra surrounds the caudal artery and vein. 鱼椎骨的血管弓环绕着尾动脉和尾静脉。|Synonyms: sanguine, sanguinary, bloody|Antonyms: bloodless|adjective
