anticommercialness:/ˌæntiˌkəˈmɜːrʃəlnəs/
n.
反商业性，非商业化，不重商业利益
The quality or state of being opposed to or avoiding commercialism.
His art was defined by its anticommercialness, he refused to sell out. 他的艺术以其非商业性而闻名，他拒绝向商业利益妥协。
The band's anticommercialness made it difficult for them to get radio play.  这支乐队的非商业化使得他们很难在电台播放他们的音乐。
noncommercialism, uncommercialism
commercialism, mercantilism
