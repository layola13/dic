apprise /əˈpraɪz/
verb
通知，告知
to inform someone of something
The staff was apprised of the new regulations.  员工们被告知了新的规章制度。
I thought it right to apprise you of the situation. 我认为有必要让你了解一下情况。
inform, notify, advise
conceal, withhold
verb
