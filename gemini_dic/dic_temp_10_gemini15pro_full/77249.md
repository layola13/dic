gastronome:/ˈɡæstrənəʊm/ (UK) /ˈɡæstrənoʊm/ (US)| n. |美食家；讲究饮食的人|A gastronome is someone who enjoys good food and drink and knows a lot about them.  A person with a refined palate and a love of fine dining.|He considers himself a gastronome and frequents the city's finest restaurants. 他自认为是美食家，经常光顾城里最好的餐厅。|Synonyms: gourmand, epicure, connoisseur|Antonyms: glutton (贬义), philistine (对艺术、文化不感兴趣的人)|noun


gastronomic:/ˌɡæstrəˈnɒmɪk/ (UK) /ˌɡæstrəˈnɑːmɪk/ (US)| adj. |美食的；烹饪的|Relating to gastronomy; the art or science of good eating.|We had a gastronomic experience at that new French restaurant. 我们在那家新开的法国餐厅享受了一次美食体验。|Synonyms: culinary, epicurean|Antonyms: N/A|adjective
