choosiest:/ˈtʃuːziɪst/| adj.|最挑剔的|Superlative form of choosy: most choosy.  It describes someone who is very selective and particular about what they like or accept.|例句：She's the choosiest eater I know; she only likes a few specific foods. 她是我认识的最挑剔的食客；她只喜欢几种特定的食物。

例句：He's the choosiest dresser in the family, always taking hours to pick out an outfit. 他是家里最挑剔的穿着者，总是要花好几个小时来挑选一套衣服。|近义词：pickiest, fussiest, most selective, most discriminating|反义词：undiscriminating, unselective, easygoing, accepting|词性:形容词
