boneyard:/ˈboʊnjɑːrd/| n. |飞机坟场；墓地；化石蕴藏地|A place where old or unwanted aircraft are stored or dismantled; a cemetery; a place where fossils are found in abundance.|The Arizona desert is a boneyard for old airplanes.|近义词：graveyard, cemetery, junkyard, scrapheap|反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly "museum" in some contexts)|词性:noun


boneyard:/ˈboʊnjɑːrd/| n. |骨场；骨库|A place where bones are kept, often for study or display. |The museum's boneyard contains skeletons of various extinct animals. |近义词：ossuary, charnel house|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "bone" + "yard,"  meaning an enclosed area. The term gained its aviation-related meaning in the mid-20th century.
