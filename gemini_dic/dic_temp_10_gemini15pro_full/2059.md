acupuncturists: /ˈækjʊpʌŋktʃərɪsts/
n.
针灸师
A person who practices acupuncture.
例句：
Many acupuncturists believe that acupuncture can help with pain relief.
许多针灸师认为针灸可以帮助缓解疼痛。

The acupuncturists used thin needles to stimulate specific points on the patient's body.
针灸师用细针刺激病人身体上的特定穴位。

近义词：
practitioner of acupuncture, acupuncturist doctor
反义词:
(No direct antonym, but perhaps) conventional doctor, Western medicine practitioner
词性:
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From acupuncture + -ist.  Acupuncture itself comes from Latin acus "needle" + punctura "pricking," from pungere "to prick."
