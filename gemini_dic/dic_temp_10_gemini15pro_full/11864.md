arrivance: /əˈraɪvəns/
n.
到达，到来
The arrivance of the guests was delayed due to the storm.
客人们的到来由于暴风雨而推迟了。
The long-awaited arrivance of spring brought joy to everyone.
期待已久的春天到来给大家带来了欢乐。
arrival, advent, coming
departure, leaving
noun

Etymology:
From French arriver ("to arrive"), from Late Latin arripare ("to come to shore," literally "to come to the bank"), from ad- ("to") + ripa ("bank, shore"). The suffix -ance derives from Latin -antia.
