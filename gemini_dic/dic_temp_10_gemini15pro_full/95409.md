incidence:/ˈɪnsɪdəns/| n.|发生率，影响，入射|The incidence of the disease is increasing.  这种疾病的发病率正在上升。|近义词：occurrence, frequency, prevalence|反义词:rarity, infrequency|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *incidentia, from Latin incidens, present participle of incidere "to fall upon, happen," from in- "upon" + cadere "to fall."
