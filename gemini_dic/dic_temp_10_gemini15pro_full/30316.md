censorian:/senˈsɔːriən/
n.
审查员，监察官
an official who examines books, films, etc., and removes anything considered offensive or harmful
The censorian deemed the film unsuitable for children. 审查员认为这部电影不适合儿童观看。
近义词：censor, examiner, inspector
反义词：N/A
词性: 名词

censorian:/senˈsɔːriən/
adj.
审查的，监察的
relating to censorship
Censorian powers were used to suppress dissent. 审查权被用来压制异议。
近义词：censoring, supervisory, controlling
反义词：N/A
词性: 形容词


词源:  From Latin *cēnsōrius (adj.) < cēnsor (“censor”) + -ius (suffix forming adjectives).
