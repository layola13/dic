measuring:/ˈmeʒərɪŋ/| adj. n.|测量的，测量；尺寸|Present participle of measure.  The act of ascertaining the size, amount, or degree of something.|She was measuring the flour for the cake. 她正在称量做蛋糕用的面粉。|近义词：gauging, calculating, determining|反义词: estimating (as a less precise alternative)|v.
measuring:/ˈmeʒərɪŋ/| n.|测量；尺寸|The act of ascertaining the size, amount, or degree of something. |The measuring of the room was done carefully. 房间的测量做得非常仔细。|近义词：measurement, dimension, size|反义词: - |n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mesuren*, from Old French *mesurer*, from Latin *mēnsūra* (“measure, measuring”), from *mētior* (“I measure”).
