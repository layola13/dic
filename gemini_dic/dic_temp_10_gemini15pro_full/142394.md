orthotopically:/ˌɔːrθəˈtɒpɪkli/| adv.|原位地，同位地|Located in the normal or usual place; in the proper place.  Relating to or involving orthotopic transplantation, where the graft is placed in the same anatomical location as the organ it replaces.|The tumor was orthotopically transplanted into the mice.  该肿瘤被原位移植到小鼠体内。|近义词：in situ, autochthonous|反义词: heterotopic|词性:副词
