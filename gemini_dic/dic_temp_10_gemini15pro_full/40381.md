contradictor:/ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪktər/ | n. | 反驳者，否认者，矛盾者 | A person who contradicts or denies something. |  He is a known contradictor of the government's policies. 他是众所周知的政府政策反对者。

The lawyer acted as a contradictor to the witness's testimony. 律师反驳了证人的证词。
