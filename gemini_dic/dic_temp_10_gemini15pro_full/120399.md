ministerially:/ˌmɪnɪˈstɪəriəli/
adverb
部长地；官员地；照章办事地
In a ministerial way; as a minister; officially, but without real power or influence.
The president ministerially signed the document. 总统例行公事般地签署了文件。
Synonyms: officially, formally, routinely, perfunctorily
Antonyms:  informally, personally, unofficially
词性: 副词
