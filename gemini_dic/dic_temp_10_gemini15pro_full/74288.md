fractal:/ˈfræktəl/ | n. |分形，碎形|A geometric shape that is self-similar at different scales. A small part of a fractal looks similar to the whole shape.|例句：The coastline of Norway is a good example of a fractal. 挪威的海岸线是分形的一个很好的例子。

例句：Many natural forms, such as snowflakes and coastlines, exhibit fractal properties. 许多自然形态，如雪花和海岸线，都表现出分形特性。

近义词：self-similar, irregular, fragmented|反义词: smooth, regular|词性:noun
