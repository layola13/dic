anisodactyls:/ˌænɪsoʊˈdæktɪlz/
n.
不等趾类动物
Animals having unequal toes, especially those having three toes pointing forward and one pointing backward, characteristic of most perching birds.
The robin is an anisodactyl, as are most songbirds. 知更鸟是不等趾类动物，大多数鸣禽也是如此。
近义词:  passerine, perching bird
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider flightless birds or birds with different toe arrangements)
词性: noun

anisodactyl:/ˌænɪsoʊˈdæktɪl/
adj.
不等趾的
Having unequal toes, typically three forward and one back.
An anisodactyl foot is well-suited for perching. 不等趾的脚很适合栖息。
近义词:  (No clear single synonym, could use descriptive phrases like "having three toes forward and one back")
反义词:  zygodactyl, heterodactyl
词性: adjective

anisodactyly:/ˌænɪsoʊˈdæktɪli/
n.
不等趾
The condition of having unequal toes, specifically three pointing forward and one backward.
Anisodactyly is a common adaptation in birds. 不等趾是鸟类中常见的适应性特征。
近义词:  (No direct synonym, could use descriptive phrases)
反义词:  zygodactyly, heterodactyly
词性: noun
