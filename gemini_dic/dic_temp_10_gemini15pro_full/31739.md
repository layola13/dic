cheapos:/ˈtʃiːpoʊz/| n.|便宜货；廉价商品|Something cheap, often of low quality.  These items are usually considered inexpensive but may also be poorly made or of inferior quality. Refers to things, not people. |I bought some cheapos at the flea market.|近义词：bargains, knockoffs|反义词:luxury items, high-end goods|Noun
