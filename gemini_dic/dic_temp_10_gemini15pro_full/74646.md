free-floating:/ˌfriː ˈfloʊtɪŋ/
adjective
自由浮动的，无固定联系的
not attached to anything and moving freely
例句：Free-floating anxiety can be difficult to manage.  自由浮动的焦虑很难控制。

例句：The free-floating exchange rate is determined by market forces. 自由浮动的汇率由市场力量决定。

近义词：unattached, adrift, loose, untethered
反义词：fixed, attached, anchored
词性: adjective


free-floating:/ˌfriː ˈfloʊtɪŋ/
noun
自由浮动的事物
something that is not attached and moves freely
例句：Free-floatings in the stock market can affect prices. 股票市场的自由流通股会影响价格。

例句：The free-floatings in the harbor posed a danger to navigation. 港口中的自由漂浮物对航行构成危险。

近义词：flotsam, jetsam
反义词：fixture
词性: noun


词源:  "free" (Old English "freo," meaning "free, exempt, noble") + "floating" (present participle of "float," from Old English "floatian," meaning "to float, swim")
