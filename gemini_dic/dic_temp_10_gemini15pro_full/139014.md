o'er:/ɔːr/| prep.|在…之上；越过；遍及|A poetic contraction of "over". It means above or across something.|例句：The ship sailed o'er the bounding main. 船航行在大海上。|例句：O'er the hills and far away. 翻山越岭，走向远方。|近义词：over, above, across|反义词：under, below|介词
