exited:/ɪkˈsaɪtɪd/
adjective
兴奋的，激动的
Feeling or showing happiness and enthusiasm.
She was excited about the upcoming trip. 她对即将到来的旅行感到兴奋。
The children were excited to open their presents. 孩子们兴奋地打开礼物。
近义词:thrilled, enthusiastic, elated
反义词:bored, apathetic, indifferent
词性:adjective
