grimmest:/ˈɡrɪmɪst/| adj.|最冷酷的，最严峻的，最令人沮丧的|Superlative form of grim: most grim.  It describes something extremely serious, depressing, or threatening.|The grimmest news came just as we were about to celebrate.  最糟糕的消息恰好在我们即将庆祝的时候传来。|The family faced the grimmest Christmas of their lives.  这个家庭面临着他们一生中最凄凉的圣诞节。|近义词：bleakest, darkest, harshest, sternest, severest|反义词：brightest, happiest, cheeriest|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *grim(m), of Germanic origin; related to Dutch grim, German grimm, also to grime.
