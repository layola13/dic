eschew:/ɪsˈtʃuː/
verb
避免；避开；回避
deliberately avoid using; abstain from
He eschewed publicity and avoided the media. 他避免抛头露面，避开媒体。
Synonyms: avoid, shun, abstain from, refrain from, forgo, renounce, steer clear of, give a wide berth to, fight shy of
Antonyms: embrace, indulge in, welcome
词性: 动词
