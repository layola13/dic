deputyships:/ˈdepjutiʃɪps/
n.
副职，代理职务，副手职位
The position or office of a deputy.  The collective status or responsibilities associated with being deputies.
Several deputyships were left vacant after the resignations. 许多副职在辞职后空缺。
The conference addressed the challenges facing women in deputyships. 会议讨论了女性在担任副职时面临的挑战。
synonyms: deputies, vice-positions, subordinate positions, assistant positions
antonyms: leadership, headship, directorship
