concordance:/kənˈkɔːrdəns/| n.|一致，和谐，索引|an agreement or harmony between people or groups; an alphabetical list of the principal words used in a book or body of work, listing every instance of each word with its immediate context|The two sides reached a concordance on the trade agreement. 双方就贸易协定达成了一致。|近义词：agreement, harmony, unity, accord, consensus|反义词：discord, disagreement, conflict, disharmony|noun
