drollish:/ˈdroʊlɪʃ/| adj.|滑稽古怪的，离奇可笑的|Oddly amusing; whimsical and humorous.|Her drollish comments kept us entertained throughout the evening. 她滑稽古怪的评论让我们整个晚上都非常开心。|例句：He has a drollish way of telling stories. 他讲故事的方式滑稽古怪。|例句：The play was filled with drollish humor.  这部戏剧充满了滑稽古怪的幽默。|近义词：whimsical, comical, quirky, eccentric, amusing|反义词：serious, solemn, humorless|词性:形容词
词源: From droll + -ish.  Droll itself comes from the Middle French word *drolle*, meaning "funny, comical fellow, jester, buffoon."
