anticausodic:/ˌæntikaʊˈzɑːdɪk/
adjective
反因果的; 逆因果的
Relating to or involving anticausality, a hypothetical concept where an effect precedes its cause.  A system is anticausal if its output depends on future values of its input.
Anticausodic filtering can sometimes improve signal processing results in specific applications. 反因果滤波有时可以改善特定应用中的信号处理结果。
This anticausodic behavior is often observed in quantum mechanics. 这种逆因果行为在量子力学中经常被观察到。
Near Antonyms: causal, causative
Antonyms: None (Anticausality is a relatively rare concept, so direct antonyms are not commonly used.)
词性: adjective
