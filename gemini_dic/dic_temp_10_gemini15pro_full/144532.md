outvenomed: /ˌaʊtˈvɛnəmd/
Verb
以毒胜过，毒害更甚于
To inflict or administer venom more effectively or to a greater degree than another.
The scorpion outvenomed the spider in their deadly duel.  蝎子在与蜘蛛的致命决斗中以毒胜过对方。
The assassin outvenomed his target with a potent new toxin. 刺客用一种强效的新毒素毒害了他的目标。
近义词: outpoison, surpass in toxicity
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "neutralized," "cured," "detoxified" in context)
词性: verb
