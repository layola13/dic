antherless:/ˈænθərlɪs/| adj.|无花药的|Lacking anthers; without anthers. Anthers are the pollen-bearing parts of a flower's stamen.|The antherless flowers were unable to produce pollen.  这些无花药的花朵无法产生花粉。|Synonyms: stamenless, pollenless|Antonyms: antheriferous, staminate|adjective
