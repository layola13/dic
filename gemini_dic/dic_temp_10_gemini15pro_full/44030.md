crimpy:/ˈkrɪmpi/| adj.|卷曲的，波状的，有褶皱的|Having many small folds or waves; crinkled.  Characterized by tight curls or waves.|Her hair was naturally crimpy. 她的头发天生卷曲。The crimpy fabric was perfect for the dress. 这种有褶皱的面料非常适合做连衣裙。|近义词：curly, wavy, crinkled, frizzy|反义词:straight, smooth|词性:形容词
词源:From crimp + -y.  Crimp itself comes from  Middle Low German or Middle Dutch krimpen, meaning "to shrink" or "to curl."
