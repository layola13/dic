deducible:/dɪˈdjuːsəbl/ /dɪˈduːsəbl/
adjective|可推论的，可演绎的|able to be deduced; able to be inferred|例句：The conclusion is easily deducible from the premise.  这个结论很容易从前提推导出来。|例句：His guilt was deducible from the evidence.  他的罪行可以从证据中推断出来。|近义词：inferable, derivable, concludable|反义词：indeducible, uninferable|词性:形容词
词源:Late Middle English: from late Latin deducibilis, from deducere ‘lead down or away’ (see deduce).
