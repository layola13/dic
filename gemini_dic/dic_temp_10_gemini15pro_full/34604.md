cleavelandite:/ˈkliːvləndaɪt/
noun
钠长石，克利夫兰石
A platy, white variety of albite, a plagioclase feldspar mineral.  It is often found in granite pegmatites.
Cleavelandite is a common mineral in pegmatites. 钠长石是伟晶岩中常见的矿物。
Large crystals of cleavelandite were found in the mine.  在这个矿山中发现了大的钠长石晶体。
Synonyms: albite, plagioclase
