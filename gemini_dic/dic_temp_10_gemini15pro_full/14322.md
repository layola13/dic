automatism:/ɔːˈtɒmətɪzəm/ (US: /ɔːˈtɑːmətɪzəm/) | n. | 自动症；无意识行为 | The performance of actions without conscious thought or intention.  An act performed unconsciously, especially as a characteristic of some types of epilepsy and other neurological disorders, or a mechanism in Surrealist art and literature aiming at expressing the subconscious. |  He claimed the crime was committed during an episode of automatism. 他声称犯罪是在无意识状态下犯下的。

The artist used automatism to create the painting.  这位艺术家用自动主义创作了这幅画。|  近义词: unconsciousness, involuntary action, reflex | 反义词: conscious action, volition | 词性: noun
