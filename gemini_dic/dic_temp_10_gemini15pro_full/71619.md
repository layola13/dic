flatfish: /ˈflætˌfɪʃ/ | n. | 比目鱼，扁鱼 | A fish with a flattened body that swims on its side, with both eyes on the upper side. |  Example 1: The flounder is a type of flatfish. 比目鱼是一种扁鱼。

Example 2:  Flatfish are masters of camouflage on the ocean floor. 比目鱼是海底伪装大师。 | Synonyms:  fluke, sole, plaice, halibut | Antonyms:  roundfish | noun


flatfish: /ˈflætˌfɪʃ/ | adj. (rare) | 扁平的，像比目鱼的 | Resembling a flatfish; flat and broad.  | Example 1: The oddly shaped rock had a flatfish appearance. 这块形状奇特的岩石看起来像比目鱼一样扁平。

Example 2: He described the strange creature as having a flatfish body. 他形容这种奇怪的生物拥有像比目鱼一样扁平的身体。 | Synonyms:  flat, compressed | Antonyms:  rounded, spherical | adjective
