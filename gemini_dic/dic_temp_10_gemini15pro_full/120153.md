mincingly:/ˈmɪnsɪŋli/| adv.|装腔作势地，矫揉造作地|In an affectedly dainty or delicate manner, typically used to describe speech or movement.|She mincingly picked at her food, barely touching it. 她装腔作势地挑剔着食物，几乎没碰一下。|近义词：affectedly, pretentiously, artificially|反义词：naturally, genuinely, sincerely|词性:副词
词源: From mince (v.) + -ing + -ly.  "Mince" originally meant "to cut into small pieces," but later developed the sense of "to speak or behave in an affectedly refined way."
