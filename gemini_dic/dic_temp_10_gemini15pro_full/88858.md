hoicks:/hɔɪks/ | v. | 拉起；拽起；提起；（尤指）猛地拉起 |  to lift or pull something or someone upwards suddenly and with effort |  He hoicked the heavy suitcase onto the rack. 他猛地把沉重的行李箱拽到架子上。 | 近义词：jerk, yank, heave, pull | 反义词: lower, drop | 词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
Likely a variant of "hitch," meaning to pull or jerk something upwards.  Its origin can be traced back to Middle English.
