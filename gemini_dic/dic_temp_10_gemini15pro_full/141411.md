ophiomorphite:/ˌɒfiəˈmɔːrfaɪt/ | n. | 蛇形管化石 | A trace fossil consisting of a burrow or tunnel system, typically preserved in sedimentary rock, that resembles the shape of a snake.  It is formed by crustaceans, particularly ghost shrimps (thalassinidean decapods). | The geologist identified the ophiomorphite in the sandstone cliff. 地质学家在砂岩峭壁中发现了蛇形管化石。| The team discovered several well-preserved ophiomorphites in the Cretaceous sediments.  研究小组在白垩纪沉积物中发现了几处保存完好的蛇形管化石。 | synonym: trace fossil, burrow, Thalassinoides | antonym: body fossil | noun
