ineffectualnesses:/ˌɪnɪˈfɛktʃuəlnəsɪz/
词性:n.
中文翻译:无效；无能；徒劳无功
英文解析:The state or quality of being ineffectual; lack of effectiveness.  A plural form of ineffectualness.
例句：The ineffectualnesses of their attempts to solve the problem became increasingly frustrating. 他们试图解决这个问题的徒劳无功变得越来越令人沮丧。

例句：The many ineffectualnesses of the committee led to its disbandment. 委员会的多次无效导致其解散。

近义词:futility, impotence, inefficacy, uselessness
反义词:effectiveness, efficacy, potency, usefulness
