cloche:/klɒʃ/ (UK) /kloʊʃ/ (US)| n.|钟形罩，玻璃罩，女式钟形帽|A bell-shaped glass cover used to protect plants, especially seedlings, from the cold. Also, a woman's close-fitting, bell-shaped hat.|例句：The gardener placed cloches over the young tomato plants. 园丁在幼番茄植株上盖上了钟形罩。

例句：She wore a stylish cloche to the races. 她戴着一顶时髦的钟形帽去参加赛马会。|近义词：cover, dome, hat, bonnet|反义词:|noun
