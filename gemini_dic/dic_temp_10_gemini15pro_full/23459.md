braggadocios:/ˌbræɡəˈdoʊʃəs/ | adj. | 吹牛的，自夸的 | Given to or characterized by bragging; boastful.  | He was braggadocios about his accomplishments, constantly reminding everyone of his successes.  他总是吹嘘自己的成就，不断地提醒每个人他的成功。 | Synonyms: boastful, swaggering, vainglorious, pompous | Antonyms: humble, modest, unassuming | adjective

braggadocio:/ˌbræɡəˈdoʊʃioʊ/ | n. | 吹牛，自夸 | Boastful or arrogant behavior.  | His braggadocio was tiresome and alienated his colleagues. 他的自夸令人厌烦，使他的同事们疏远了他。 | Synonyms: boasting, bragging, swagger, bombast | Antonyms: humility, modesty | noun
