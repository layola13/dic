enceinte:/ɒ̃ˈsɛ̃t/  /ɑːnˈsæ̃t/ | adj. n.|怀孕的（主要用于指女性）；围起来的，被围住的|Pregnant (used chiefly of women); surrounded or encompassed, especially by fortifications.|例句：The queen is enceinte. 女王怀孕了。

例句：The enceinte castle was well protected. 城堡被围墙保护得很好。|近义词：(adj.) pregnant, expecting, gravid; (n.) enclosure, enclosure, perimeter |反义词:(adj.) nonpregnant, barren; (n.) open space|词性:形容词，名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French enceinte, from Old French enceint, past participle of enceindre (“to encircle, surround”), from Latin incincō (“to gird, encompass”), from in (“in, on”) + cingō (“to gird”).
