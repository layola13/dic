cannelure:/ˈkæn(ə)ljʊr/ |n.| 膛线，弹壳上的环形槽|A groove, especially a circumferential groove as in a column or the casing of a bullet or cartridge.|The cannelure on the bullet helps it grip the rifling of the barrel. 子弹上的环形槽有助于它抓住枪管的膛线。|Synonyms: groove, channel, furrow|Antonyms: ridge, protrusion|noun

cannelure:/ˈkæn(ə)ljʊr/ |n.| (建筑) 柱上的凹槽|A groove or channel, especially one cut into the shaft of a column.|The architect specified a decorative cannelure on each of the building's columns. 建筑师在建筑物的每一根柱子上都设计了装饰性的凹槽。|Synonyms: groove, fluting, channel|Antonyms: ridge, protrusion|noun
