disrespecting:/ˌdɪsrɪˈspektɪŋ/
v. |不尊重， disrespect的现在分词形式|Showing a lack of respect or courtesy.|He was disrespecting his elders by talking back. 他顶嘴，对长辈很不尊重。|近义词：insulting, slighting, offending|反义词：respecting, honoring, revering|词性:动词

disrespecting:/ˌdɪsrɪˈspektɪŋ/
adj. |失礼的，无礼的|Lacking respect; impolite.|His disrespecting behavior towards the teacher was unacceptable. 他对老师不尊重的行为是不可接受的。|近义词：rude, impolite, discourteous|反义词：respectful, polite, courteous|词性:形容词
