attorneyships:/əˈtɜːrniʃɪps/
n.
律师职位；检察官职位
the position of an attorney, especially a government attorney
She accepted an attorneyship with the Department of Justice. 她接受了司法部的一个律师职位。

近义词:attorney positions, legal positions
反义词: (No direct antonym for a position. Could consider unemployment, joblessness for the state of *not* having such a position.)
词性:noun
