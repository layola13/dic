nonaccelerations: /ˌnɒnəkˌsɛləˈreɪʃənz/ (US) /ˌnɒnəkˌsɛləˈreɪʃənz/ (UK)
noun
无加速
absence of accelerations (increase in fetal heart rate); typically used in the context of fetal monitoring during pregnancy.
The nonaccelerations on the fetal heart rate monitor concerned the doctor. 胎心监护仪上没有显示胎心加速让医生很担心。
Near Antonyms: accelerations
