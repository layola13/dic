hemuse:/hɪˈmjuːz/ | v. | 犹豫；踌躇；支吾 | To hesitate or be reluctant to speak or act, often due to shyness or uncertainty. | She hemmed and hawed for a few minutes before finally answering the question. 她犹豫了几分钟才最终回答了这个问题。|  He hemused over whether to accept the job offer. 他对是否接受这份工作犹豫不决。 | 近义词: hesitate, waver, vacillate, stall | 反义词: decide, resolve, determine | 词性: 动词

词源:  "Hemuse" likely comes from a combination of "hem" (to clear the throat, often a sign of hesitation) and "muse" (to think deeply), reflecting the act of pausing and pondering before speaking or acting.
