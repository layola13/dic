crocodile:/ˈkrɒkədaɪl/ (UK) /ˈkrɑːkədaɪl/ (US)| n.|鳄鱼|A large reptile with a long body, thick skin, and sharp teeth, that lives in rivers and lakes in tropical countries.|The crocodile lurked beneath the murky water. 鳄鱼潜伏在浑浊的水下。|Example 2:  We saw a crocodile basking in the sun on the riverbank. 我们看到一条鳄鱼在河岸上晒太阳。|Synonym: alligator, gavial|Antonym:  (No direct antonym for an animal)|noun
