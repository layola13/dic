carbamic:/kɑːrˈbæmɪk/| adj.|氨基甲酸的|Relating to or containing carbamic acid (NH2COOH).|例句：Carbamic acid is unstable and readily decomposes. 氨基甲酸不稳定，容易分解。

例句：Carbamic pesticides are widely used in agriculture. 氨基甲酸酯类农药广泛用于农业。|近义词：amino formate (rarely used)|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From carbamide (urea) + -ic.  Ultimately from Latin *carbo, *carbon- (“coal, charcoal”) + amide (“-amide”).
