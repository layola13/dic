contrabass:/ˈkɑːntrəˌbeɪs/ /ˈkɒntrəˌbeɪs/| n.|低音提琴，低音号|A large musical instrument of the violin family, the largest and lowest pitched of the bowed string instruments.|The contrabass provided a deep, resonant foundation for the orchestra. 低音提琴为管弦乐队提供了深沉、洪亮的基调。|近义词：double bass, string bass|反义词:|n.

contrabass:/ˈkɑːntrəˌbeɪs/ /ˈkɒntrəˌbeɪs/| adj.|低音的|Producing the lowest tones.|The contrabass section of the orchestra was particularly strong.  管弦乐队的低音提琴部分特别强。|近义词：bass, low-pitched|反义词：treble, high-pitched|adj.
