pantisocracy: /ˌpæn.tɪˈsɒk.rə.si/
n.
乌托邦式的平等主义；泛爱主义社会
A system of government where all rule equally.  A utopian community where all property is shared and all members are equal.
The small group of poets dreamed of establishing a pantisocracy on the banks of the Susquehanna River.  这群诗人梦想着在萨斯奎哈纳河畔建立一个泛爱主义社会。
近义词: utopia, egalitarianism, communalism
反义词: tyranny, dictatorship, autocracy
词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *pan* (all) + *isos* (equal) + *-cracy* (rule, government).  The term was coined by Samuel Taylor Coleridge and Robert Southey.
