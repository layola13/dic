narrow-mindedness:/ˌnæroʊˈmaɪndɪdnəs/| n.|心胸狭窄，狭隘|The narrow-mindedness of the committee prevented them from considering new ideas. 委员会的狭隘阻碍了他们考虑新的想法。|例句：His narrow-mindedness prevented him from appreciating other cultures. 他的狭隘使他无法欣赏其他文化。

|近义词：bigotry, intolerance, prejudice|反义词:open-mindedness, tolerance, broad-mindedness|词性:名词
