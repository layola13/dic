nonunionized:/ˌnɒnˈjuːnjənaɪzd/ /ˌnɑːnˈjuːnjənaɪzd/
adjective
非工会的
Not belonging to or represented by a labor union.  Lacking union representation or protection.
The nonunionized workers were paid less than their unionized counterparts.  非工会的工人比加入工会的工人收入低。

The company actively discouraged its employees from becoming nonunionized. 该公司积极劝阻其员工不要加入工会。(此处应为unionized，原句意思相反)

近义词：non-union, unorganized
反义词：unionized, organized
词性：adjective
