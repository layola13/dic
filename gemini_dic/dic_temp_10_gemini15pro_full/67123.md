exponentiation:/ˌɛkspoʊˌnɛnʃiˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 指数运算，乘方 | the mathematical operation of raising a quantity to a power. |  Example: Exponentiation is a fundamental concept in algebra. 指数运算是代数中的一个基本概念。
