goof-off: /ˈɡuːfˌɔːf/ (US), /ˈɡuːfˌɒf/ (UK)
noun| 懒鬼，游手好闲的人|A person who evades work or responsibility; an idler.|例句：He's such a goof-off, always finding ways to avoid work. 他真是个懒鬼，总是想方设法逃避工作。

例句：My boss called me a goof-off for taking so many breaks. 我的老板因为我休息太多而叫我懒鬼。
|近义词：slacker, loafer, idler|反义词：hard worker, diligent person|词性: 名词
