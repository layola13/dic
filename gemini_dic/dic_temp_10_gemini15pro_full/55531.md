distributing:/dɪˈstrɪbjʊtɪŋ/
verb
分发，分配
the act of spreading or supplying something
例句：The company is distributing free samples of its new product.  这家公司正在分发其新产品的免费样品。

例句：They are distributing leaflets to raise awareness about the issue. 他们正在散发传单以提高人们对这个问题的认识。

近义词：allocating, dispensing, sharing, apportioning
反义词：collecting, gathering, hoarding

词性：v.  (present participle of distribute)
词源：From Latin distribuere, from dis- "apart" + tribuere "to assign, allot".
