observantines:/əbˈzɜːrvəntɪnz/ | n. | 遵守者；奉行者；(尤指) 严守宗教仪式者 | People who observe a particular custom, rite, or law, especially in a strict or meticulous manner.  |  The observantines of the monastery adhered to a strict daily schedule of prayer and work. 修道院的奉行者严格遵守每天的祈祷和工作时间表。 | near synonyms: observers, adherents, followers, devotees | antonyms: nonconformists, rebels, dissenters | noun

observantine:/əbˈzɜːrvəntɪn/ | adj. | 善于观察的；机警的；敏锐的 | Quick to notice; attentive; perceptive. | He has an observantine eye for detail. 他对细节有敏锐的观察力。| near synonyms: observant, perceptive, astute, sharp-witted | antonyms: unobservant, inattentive, oblivious, unperceptive | adjective
