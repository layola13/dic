nonmainstream:/ˌnɒnˈmeɪnstriːm/ | adj. | 非主流的 | Not belonging to the mainstream; unconventional, alternative, or against the prevailing trend. |  Her fashion sense is decidedly nonmainstream. 她的时尚感绝对是非主流的。|  His nonmainstream views often sparked debate.  他的非主流观点经常引发争论。| 近义词: alternative, unconventional, indie, underground, counterculture | 反义词: mainstream, conventional, orthodox, traditional | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin *non, from Old Latin *noenum, contraction of *ne oinom "not one."
mainstream (n.): From main (adj.) + stream (n.).  Referencing the main current of a river, then figuratively the prevailing trend or current of thought.
