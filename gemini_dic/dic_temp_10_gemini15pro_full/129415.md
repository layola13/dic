nominately:/ˈnɑːmɪnətli/| adv.|名义上地，有名无实地|In a nominal way; by name or designation only, but not in reality. |He was nominally in charge, but his assistant did all the work. 他名义上是负责人，但实际上他的助手做了所有工作。|近义词:ostensibly, superficially, titularly|反义词:actually, really, truly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *nominatim (“by name”), from Latin nominatus, past participle of nominare (“to name”), from nomen (“name”).
