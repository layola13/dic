duxes:/ˈdʌksiːz/
n.
leaders, commanders
A plural form of "dux," referring to leaders, often in a scholastic context.
The duxes of the school led the graduation procession. 学校的领袖们带领着毕业游行队伍。
synonyms: leaders, chiefs, commanders, heads
antonyms: followers, subordinates
noun
