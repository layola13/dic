aluminated:/əˈluːmɪneɪtɪd/
adj.
镀铝的；用铝处理的；含铝的
Coated or treated with aluminum; containing aluminum.
The aluminated surface of the mirror reflected the light brightly.  镀铝的镜面将光线明亮地反射出来。
The telescope's mirror was aluminated for better reflectivity.  为了更好的反射率，望远镜的镜面镀了铝。
近义词: aluminum-coated, aluminum-plated, aluminized
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider: uncoated, untreated)
词性: adjective


aluminize:/əˈluːmɪnaɪz/
vt.
镀铝；用铝处理
To coat or treat with aluminum.
They aluminize the mirrors to improve their reflection.  他们给镜子镀铝以提高反射率。
The company specializes in aluminizing telescope mirrors.  这家公司专门从事望远镜镜面镀铝。
近义词: aluminate, aluminum-coat, aluminum-plate
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider: strip, remove coating)
词性: verb
