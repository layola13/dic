gripe:/ɡraɪp/| n. v. |抱怨，牢骚；腹痛，肠绞痛|A complaint, especially a minor or persistent one.  To complain naggingly or constantly.|例句：He's always griping about the weather.  他总是抱怨天气。|近义词：complain, grumble, moan, whine|反义词：praise, commend|词性:名词,动词
gripes:/ɡraɪps/| n. |抱怨，牢骚；腹痛，肠绞痛|Complaints, especially minor or persistent ones.  (Usually plural).|例句：My main gripe about the hotel was the poor service. 我对这家酒店的主要抱怨是服务差。|近义词：complaints, grievances|反义词：compliments, praises|词性:名词
