estheticizing:/ɪsˈθɛtɪsaɪzɪŋ/
verb
美化；审美化
The act of making something aesthetically pleasing, or focusing on the aesthetic qualities of something.  The process of representing something as being beautiful or artistically pleasing.
He was accused of estheticizing poverty in his photographs. 他被指责在他的照片中美化贫困。
beautifying, embellishing, glorifying, romanticizing
uglifying, vulgarizing
