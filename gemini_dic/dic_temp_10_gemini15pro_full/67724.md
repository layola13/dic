exuviae:/ɛksˈuːviːiː/
n.
蜕皮; (昆虫或其他节肢动物的)外壳，蜕壳
cast-off outer covering of an animal (especially insects and other arthropods)
The cicada left its exuviae clinging to the tree bark. 蝉将它的蜕皮留在树皮上。

exuviae:/ɛksˈuːviːiː/
n.
(蛇、蜥蜴等爬行动物的)蜕皮
sloughed-off skin of a reptile
The snake shed its exuviae, revealing a vibrant new skin. 蛇蜕掉了它的皮，露出了鲜艳的新皮。


近义词: shell, skin, molt, husk
反义词:  (无明确的反义词)
词性: 名词 (plural noun, always used in plural form)


词源:
From Latin *exuviae*, plural of *exuvia* "that which is stripped off, anything cast off," from *exuere* "to strip off, put off, take off (clothes), divest, peel off."
