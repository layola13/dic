argonautic:/ˌɑːrɡəˈnɔːtɪk/
adjective
阿尔戈英雄般的;  冒险的; 远征的
Relating to the Argonauts, the heroes who sailed with Jason in search of the Golden Fleece; adventurous, especially involving a long and difficult journey.
Jason's argonautic expedition for the Golden Fleece is one of the most famous quests in Greek mythology. 杰森寻找金羊毛的阿尔戈英雄般的远征是希腊神话中最著名的探险之一。
The team embarked on an argonautic journey to discover the lost city.  这个团队开始了一场冒险的旅程，去寻找失落的城市。

近义词:adventurous, epic, daring, perilous
反义词:cowardly, timid, safe, uneventful
词性:adjective

词源:From Greek *Ἀργοναυτικός (Argonautikos), from *Ἀργοναύτης (Argonautēs, “Argonaut”), from *Ἀργώ (Argō, “Argo (Jason's ship)”) + *ναύτης (nautēs, “sailor”).
