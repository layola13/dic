nonspeakers:/ˈnɒnˈspiːkəz/ (UK) /ˈnɑːnˈspiːkərz/ (US)
n.|非讲话者，不会说话的人|People who do not speak a particular language or who are unable to speak. |The conference provided sign language interpreters for nonspeakers of English.  会议为不懂英语的人提供了手语翻译。|Synonyms:  non-speakers, the mute, the silent|Antonyms: speakers, orators|Noun
