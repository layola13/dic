mutter:/ˈmʌtər/ (US) /ˈmʌtə(r)/ (UK)| v. n.|低语，咕哝；抱怨|To speak in a low, indistinct voice, often complainingly.  Also, a low, indistinct utterance.|He muttered something about the weather. 他低声抱怨着天气。|近义词：murmur, mumble, grumble|反义词：shout, yell|词性:动词,名词

mutter:/ˈmʌtər/ (US) /ˈmʌtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|低语，咕哝|A low, indistinct utterance.|I could hear a mutter of discontent from the crowd. 我能听到人群中不满的低语。|近义词：murmur, mumble, grumble|反义词：shout, yell|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *muteren*, probably of imitative origin.  Cognate with Dutch *motteren*, German *muttern*.
