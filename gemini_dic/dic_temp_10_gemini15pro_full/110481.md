lily-like:/ˈlɪliˌlaɪk/
adjective
像百合花的; 百合花似的
Resembling a lily in appearance or qualities.
The flowers were lily-like, white and elegant.  这些花像百合花一样，洁白而优雅。
She has lily-like hands, long and slender. 她有一双像百合花一样的手，纤长而白皙。
近义词:lily-white, floral, delicate
反义词:coarse, rough
词性:adjective
