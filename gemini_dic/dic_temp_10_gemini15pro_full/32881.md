chondrin:/ˈkɒndrɪn/ | n. | 软骨素 | A gelatinous protein substance that forms the matrix of cartilage. | Cartilage is made up primarily of chondrin and collagen. 软骨主要由软骨素和胶原蛋白组成。| Chondrin is essential for the flexibility and strength of cartilage.  软骨素对软骨的灵活性和强度至关重要。 | synonyms: cartilage matrix protein | antonyms: N/A | noun
