fairing-up:/ˈfeərɪŋ ʌp/
noun
改进; 修整;  (尤指飞机或船体)流线型部件的安装
the process of improving something, or fitting fairings (streamlined coverings) to something, especially an aircraft or boat.
The fairing-up of the aircraft's wings improved its aerodynamic performance.  飞机机翼的改进提高了它的空气动力性能。
improvement, refinement, streamlining
deterioration, worsening
n.
