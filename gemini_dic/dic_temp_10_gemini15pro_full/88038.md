highbrowisms:/ˈhaɪˌbraʊɪzəmz/
noun
高雅文化；自认为高雅的言行；知识分子式的言行
Pretentious displays of intellectualism or refined taste.
He sprinkled his conversation with highbrowisms, hoping to impress the professor. 他在谈话中穿插着一些高雅的言辞，希望能给教授留下深刻印象。
synonyms: intellectualism, pretentiousness, refinement, snobbishness
antonyms: simplicity, unpretentiousness, vulgarity
n.
