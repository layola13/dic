caoncito:/kaʊnˈsitoʊ/ (Spanish pronunciation may vary regionally)
n.
small canyon; gully; small ravine
A small, narrow canyon or ravine, often dry.  Diminutive of *cañón* (canyon).
The hikers followed the caoncito down to the river.  徒步旅行者沿着小峡谷下到河边。
近义词:gulch, ravine, arroyo
反义词:mesa, plateau
词性:noun
