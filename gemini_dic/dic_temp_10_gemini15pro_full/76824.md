ganch:/ɡæntʃ/ | n. |钩子；(尤指)肉钩；搭便车|A large hook, especially one used for hanging meat. Also, to hitchhike. | The butcher used a ganch to hang the carcass. 肉贩用钩子挂起了屠体。 He ganched a ride to the next town. 他搭便车去了下一个城镇。|近义词：hook, clasp, hitchhike |反义词: - |词性: 名词
