non-rhetorical:/ˌnɒn rɪˈtɒrɪkəl/ | adj. | 非修辞的；非反问的；真正的；不只是说说而已 | Not rhetorical; meant seriously; not merely for effect.  A non-rhetorical question genuinely seeks information. |  "It's a non-rhetorical question," she insisted, "I really want to know the answer." “这不是反问，”她坚持道，“我真的想知道答案。” | Synonyms: genuine, sincere, earnest, heartfelt, serious | Antonyms: rhetorical, insincere, feigned, artificial | adjective
