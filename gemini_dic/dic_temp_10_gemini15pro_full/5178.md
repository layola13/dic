alleviatory:/əˈliːviətɔːri/
adjective
减轻的，缓和的
Making something less severe or intense; relieving.
The doctor prescribed alleviatory medication for her back pain. 医生给她开了缓解背痛的药。
The government implemented alleviatory measures to address the economic crisis. 政府实施了缓解经济危机的措施。
近义词: palliative, soothing, mitigating, relieving
反义词: aggravating, exacerbating
词性: adjective

词源:  From Late Latin *alleviatorius, from Latin alleviatus (past participle of alleviare “to lighten, relieve”), from ad- “to” + levis “light”.
