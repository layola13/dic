device:/dɪˈvaɪs/ (US) /dɪˈvaɪs/ (UK)| n.|设备，装置，方法，策略|A thing made or adapted for a particular purpose, especially a piece of mechanical or electronic equipment.|She invented a device for peeling oranges quickly. 她发明了一种快速剥橙子的装置。|近义词：gadget, apparatus, instrument, tool, appliance, contrivance|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "malfunction" as a contrasting concept)|noun

device:/dɪˈvaɪs/ (US) /dɪˈvaɪs/ (UK)| v.|设计，发明，策划|Plan or invent (a complex procedure, system, or mechanism) by careful thought.|He devised a plan to escape from prison. 他设计了一个越狱计划。|近义词：contrive, invent, design, formulate, create, engineer|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "destroy" as a contrasting concept)|verb
