interdifferentiated:/ˌɪntərˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃɪeɪtɪd/
|adj.|相互分化的，相互区别的|Made or become different from each other.|The cells have interdifferentiated to form distinct tissues. 这些细胞已经相互分化形成不同的组织。|近义词：differentiated, distinct, separate|反义词：undifferentiated, homogeneous, similar|词性:形容词
词源:  inter- (between) + differentiate (to make or become different)
