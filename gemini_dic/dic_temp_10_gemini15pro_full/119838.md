milder:/ˈmaɪldər/| adj.|更温和的，更轻微的，更暖和的|Less harsh, severe, or intense; gentler or softer.|The weather is milder today than yesterday. 今天的天气比昨天更暖和。|近义词：gentler, softer, warmer, calmer|反义词：harsher, stronger, colder, severer|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *mylder, from Old English *mildre, comparative of milde (“mild”). Cognate with West Frisian milder (“milder”), Dutch milder (“milder”), German milder (“milder”).
