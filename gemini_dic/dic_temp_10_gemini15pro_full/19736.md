billiard:/ˈbɪljərd/| n. adj.|台球，台球的|A game played on a rectangular table covered with green cloth, in which cues are used to strike balls against each other and into pockets around the edge of the table. Also, relating to billiards.|例句：He plays billiard every weekend. 他每个周末都打台球。|近义词：pool, snooker|反义词:|n. adj.
billiard:/ˈbɪljərd/|n.|台球，弹子戏|A game played with cues on a rectangular, felt-covered table, in which the object is to use a cue ball to pocket numbered object balls.|例句：He enjoys a game of billiard after work. 他喜欢下班后打一局台球。|近义词：pool, snooker|反义词:|n.
