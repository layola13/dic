panicking:/ˈpænɪkɪŋ/| v.| 恐慌，惊慌失措 |Experiencing or showing sudden uncontrollable fear or anxiety, often causing wildly unthinking behavior.|  The crowd was panicking after the explosion. 人群在爆炸后惊慌失措。|近义词：freaking out, losing it, overreacting|反义词: calming down, staying calm |verb (present participle)
panic:/ˈpænɪk/| n. v.| 恐慌，惊慌 |A sudden uncontrollable fear or anxiety, often causing wildly unthinking behavior.| There was a panic when the fire alarm went off. 火警响起时，人们一阵恐慌。|近义词：fear, terror, alarm|反义词:calm, composure |noun, verb
