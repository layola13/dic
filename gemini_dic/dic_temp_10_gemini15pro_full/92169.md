hypersophistication:/ˌhaɪpərˌsəˌfɪstɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度复杂，过度精细，过度世故 | Extreme sophistication; excessive refinement or elaboration. | Her hypersophistication in fashion made her stand out in the crowd. 她在时尚方面的过度讲究让她在人群中脱颖而出。 | The restaurant's menu was a study in hypersophistication, with dishes featuring exotic ingredients and complex preparations. 这家餐厅的菜单堪称过度复杂的研究，菜肴以异国情调的食材和复杂的制作方法为特色。 |  近义词: overrefinement, overelaboration, complexity | 反义词: simplicity, artlessness, naturalness | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From hyper- + sophistication.  "Hyper-" is a prefix meaning "over, above, beyond," from Greek *uper. "Sophistication" comes from Late Latin sophisticationem, from sophisticatus, past participle of sophisticare "to adulterate, falsify, make artificial," from Greek sophistikos "clever, skillful," from sophistes "wise man, teacher."
