coralloidal:/ˌkɒrəˈlɔɪdəl/ /ˌkɔːrəˈlɔɪdəl/
adjective
珊瑚状的，珊瑚形的
Resembling coral in form or texture; having a branched or rounded structure like that of coral.
The mineral deposit had a coralloidal appearance.  这种矿物沉积物外观呈珊瑚状。
The bacteria formed coralloidal colonies on the agar plate.  细菌在琼脂平板上形成了珊瑚状菌落。
Synonyms: coral-like, branched, dendritic, botryoidal
Antonyms: smooth, flat, unbranched
