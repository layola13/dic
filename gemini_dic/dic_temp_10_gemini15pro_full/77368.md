gaul:/ɡɔːl/| n. |高卢人；高卢（古代西欧地区，相当于现在的法国和比利时）|An inhabitant of ancient Gaul.  A region of western Europe occupied by Celtic peoples, roughly corresponding to modern-day France, Belgium, and parts of Germany, Switzerland, and Italy.|Caesar conquered Gaul. 凯撒征服了高卢。|近义词：Celt, Frenchman (in a historical context)|反义词:Roman (in a historical context)|noun
