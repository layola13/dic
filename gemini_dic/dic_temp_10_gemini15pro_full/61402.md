emblemmed:/ɪmˈblɛmd/
adjective
饰有徽章的，印有标志的
Decorated or marked with an emblem.
The ambassador's car was emblemmed with the flag of his country. 大使的汽车上印有他的国旗。
emblazoned, marked, imprinted, decorated
plain, unmarked, undecorated
词性:形容词
