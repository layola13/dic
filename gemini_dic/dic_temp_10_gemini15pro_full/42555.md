counternatural:/ˌkaʊntərˈnætʃrəl/| adj.|违反自然的，反自然的|Going against the natural order or what is considered normal or expected. |例句：His counternatural calmness in the face of danger was unsettling.  面对危险，他反常的平静令人不安。

例句：The idea of a counternatural alliance between the two warring nations seemed impossible. 这两个交战国之间建立一个违反自然的联盟的想法似乎是不可能的。|近义词：unnatural, abnormal, perverse, aberrant|反义词：natural, normal, typical|词性:形容词
