multisystem:/ˌmʌltiˈsɪstəm/ | adj. | 多系统的，涉及多个系统的 | Affecting or involving many systems of the body or many different systems. |  The patient presented with multisystem failure.  病人出现了多系统衰竭。|  Multisystem trauma requires specialized care. 多系统创伤需要专门的护理。| 近义词：polysystemic, multiorgan | 反义词：unisystem, single-system | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
multi- (prefix): from Latin multus "much, many"
system: from Late Latin systema "organized whole, a whole compounded of parts," from Greek systema "organized whole, government, constitution, a body," from syn- "together" (see syn-) + histanai "cause to stand," from PIE root *sta- "to stand, make or be firm."
