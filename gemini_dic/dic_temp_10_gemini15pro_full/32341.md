childed:/ˈtʃaɪldɪd/
adjective
有孩子的；已生育的
Having children; having given birth to a child.
She was a childed woman with three young daughters. 她是一位有孩子的妇女，有三个年幼的女儿。
近义词: child-bearing, motherly, parental
反义词: childless
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From child + -ed.  The suffix "-ed" is commonly used to form adjectives from nouns, indicating possession or having the characteristics of the noun.  In this case, "childed" signifies "having children".
