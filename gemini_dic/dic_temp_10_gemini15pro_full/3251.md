affinal:/əˈfɪn(ə)l/
adjective
姻亲的
related by marriage
例句：My affinal relatives include my wife's parents and siblings. 我的姻亲包括我妻子的父母和兄弟姐妹。

例句：Affinal kinship ties are important in many cultures.  在许多文化中，姻亲关系很重要。

近义词：in-law, related by marriage
反义词：consanguineal (血亲的)
词性：adjective
