half-doors:/ˌhɑːf ˈdɔːrz/ | n. | 半门 | Doors that are only half the height of a standard door, often found on vehicles like Jeeps or certain types of trucks. They offer open-air driving while still providing some side protection. |  The Jeep Wrangler is known for its removable half-doors. 吉普牧马人以其可拆卸的半门而闻名。

|  Half-doors are a popular modification for off-road vehicles. 半门是越野车的热门改装。 | synonyms: half doors (no true synonym, it's a descriptive term), partial doors | antonyms: full doors, solid doors | noun
