dramatises:/ˈdræməˌtaɪzɪz/ | v. | 戏剧化，使戏剧化，夸张 |  To present or represent (something) in a dramatic or exaggerated way.  | The movie dramatises the events of the war.  这部电影戏剧化地展现了战争的事件。| synonyms: exaggerate, overstate, embellish, heighten, amplify | antonyms: downplay, understate, minimize | verb

dramatically:/drəˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 戏剧性地，显著地 | In a dramatic or striking manner. | The weather changed dramatically. 天气发生了戏剧性的变化。 | synonyms: strikingly, drastically, considerably, significantly, remarkably | antonyms: slightly, subtly, gradually | adverb
