freehold:/ˈfriːhoʊld/| n.|永久产权，自由保有权|The ownership of land or property without any time limit.|She owns the freehold of the house and the surrounding land. 她拥有这所房子及其周围土地的永久产权。|近义词：fee simple|反义词:leasehold|词性:名词

freehold:/ˈfriːhoʊld/| adj.|永久产权的|Relating to land or property that is owned outright.|They live in a freehold house. 他们住在一栋拥有永久产权的房子里。|近义词：owned outright|反义词:leasehold|词性:形容词
