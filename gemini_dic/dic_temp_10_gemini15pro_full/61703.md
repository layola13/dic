emongst: /əˈmʌŋst/ | prep. |在…之中；在…之间| Among; in the midst of.  A variant of "among," chiefly used in literary or archaic contexts. |  He found himself emongst a crowd of strangers. 他发现自己身处一群陌生人之中。|  She distributed the gifts emongst the children. 她把礼物分发给孩子们。| 近义词: among, amidst, amid | 反义词: outside, without | 词性: 介词

词源:
Middle English *emonges, from Old English amang, onmang (prep., adv.) "among," from a- (1) "on" + gemang "crowd, throng."  The -st ending is an analogical formation from amongst, against, etc.
