nattier:/ˈnætiər/| adj.|更优雅的，更时髦的，更漂亮的|More stylish, elegant, or chic than something else; more natty.|Her new dress is nattier than her old one. 她的新裙子比旧的更时髦。|近义词：smarter, more stylish, chicer, more fashionable|反义词：shabbier, dowdier, less stylish|词性:形容词

Etymology:
Comparative form of *natty*, from 18th century English slang, possibly related to *neat*.
