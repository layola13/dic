dusks:/dʌsks/
noun
黄昏，薄暮
The period of time at the end of the day, just before night.
As dusk settled, the streetlights flickered on.  随着黄昏降临，街灯闪烁着亮了起来。
The birds returned to their nests at dusk.  鸟儿在黄昏时分回到了它们的巢穴。

近义词:twilight, evening, nightfall
反义词:dawn, daybreak
词性:n. (noun)
