humpless:/ˈhʌmplɪs/| adj.|无驼峰的，无隆肉的|Lacking a hump or humps; not humped.  Specifically referring to animals that typically have humps, but this individual or species does not. |The humpless camel is native to South America.  这种无峰驼原产于南美洲。|近义词:smooth, level, flat|反义词:humped, hunched|词性:adjective
词源:From hump + -less.  "Hump" comes from Proto-Germanic *hump- (meaning "lump, hunch"). "-less" is a suffix meaning "without" or "lacking".
