decaying:/dɪˈkeɪɪŋ/
adjective
腐烂的，衰败的
undergoing the process of decay; rotting or decomposing.
The decaying leaves formed a thick carpet on the forest floor. 腐烂的叶子在地面上形成了厚厚的一层。
The smell of decaying wood filled the abandoned cabin.  废弃的小屋里充满了腐木的臭味。

近义词: rotting, decomposing, deteriorating, putrefying
反义词: growing, flourishing, thriving, improving
词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *decaien, from Anglo-Norman decair, dechaer, from Late Latin *cadēre (“to fall”), from Latin cadō (“I fall”).
