flockling:/ˈflɒklɪŋ/| n.|雏鸟；幼畜（尤指羊羔）；年轻人，新手|A young bird or animal, especially a lamb, or a young, inexperienced person.|The shepherd guided the flocklings to the pasture. 牧羊人带领着羊羔们去牧场。|Synonyms: chick, fledgling, newbie, beginner|Antonyms: adult, veteran, expert|Noun
