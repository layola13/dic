grokking:/ˈɡrɑːkɪŋ/ | v. | 理解；深刻理解；彻底领悟 | To understand something deeply and intuitively; to empathize with something so completely that it becomes part of oneself. |例句：After spending weeks with the code, I finally grokked how it worked.  在花了数周时间研究代码之后，我终于彻底理解了它的工作原理。

例句：She grokked the nuances of the poem after reading it several times.  她读了几遍这首诗后，领悟了其中的细微之处。 | 近义词：understand, comprehend, grasp, internalize | 反义词：misunderstand, misinterpret | 词性: 动词
