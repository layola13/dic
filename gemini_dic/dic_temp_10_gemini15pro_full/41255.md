corbett:/ˈkɔːrbɪt/ | n. | 科比特（姓氏）;  科比特国家公园（印度北部的一个国家公园）| A surname, also the name of a national park in northern India. |Jim Corbett is a famous hunter and conservationist. 吉姆·科比特是一位著名的猎人兼自然资源保护主义者。|  We visited Corbett National Park last year. 我们去年参观了科比特国家公园。 | 近义词: (For the park) reserve, sanctuary | 反义词:  (For the park) city, urban area | 词性:noun


corbett:/ˈkɔːrbɪt/ | n. |  (尤指大型食肉动物的)尸体，残骸 | (especially of a large carnivorous animal) a dead body, carcass | The tiger left the corbett of its prey. 老虎离开了它的猎物尸体。 | The vultures circled above the corbett. 秃鹫在尸体上方盘旋。| 近义词: carcass, remains | 反义词: living, alive | 词性:noun
