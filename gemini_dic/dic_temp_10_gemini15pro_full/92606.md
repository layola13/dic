iambus's:/aɪˈæmbəsɪz/
n.|抑扬格的|The possessive form of "iambus," referring to something belonging to or associated with an iambus (a metrical foot in poetry consisting of one unstressed syllable followed by one stressed syllable).|The poem's iambus's rhythm created a soothing effect. 这首诗的抑扬格节奏营造了一种舒缓的效果。|Synonyms: iamb's, iambic (adjective form)|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider trochaic, dactylic for contrasting metrical feet)|Noun
