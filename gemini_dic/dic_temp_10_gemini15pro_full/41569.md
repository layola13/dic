corporateness: /ˈkɔːrpərətnɪs/ /ˈkɔːrpərɪtnɪs/
n.
公司性质;法人资格;集体性;组织性
The quality or state of being corporate; the state of being united or combined into one.
The corporateness of the project ensured its success.  这个项目的集体性确保了它的成功。
近义词: collectivity, unity, solidarity
反义词: individuality, division, fragmentation
词性: 名词
