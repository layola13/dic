dippables:/ˈdɪpəbəlz/
noun
蘸酱食品；可以蘸着吃的食物
Small pieces of food that are designed to be dipped into a sauce.
We had a selection of dippables, including carrots, celery, and crackers. 我们准备了一些可以蘸着吃的食物，包括胡萝卜、芹菜和饼干。
synonyms: finger foods, appetizers, snacks
antonyms: main course, full meal
词性: 名词
