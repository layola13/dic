moringic: /mɔːˈrɪndʒɪk/ | adj. | 晨露般的，清晨的 | Relating to or resembling morning dew; characteristic of the morning. | The moringic air was crisp and cool. 清晨的空气清新凉爽。| The flowers were covered in moringic droplets. 花朵上覆盖着晨露般的水珠。 | 近义词：dewy, fresh, early | 反义词：nightly, evening | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Likely a modern coinage based on "morning" + "-ic" (a suffix forming adjectives).  No established etymology exists in major dictionaries. This word is not common and might be considered neologistic or poetic.
