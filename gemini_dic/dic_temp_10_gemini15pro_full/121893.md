missample: /mɪsˈsæmpl/ | v. | 错误取样，取样不当 | To take a sample that is not representative of the whole population or data set. | The survey missampled the population, leading to inaccurate conclusions. 这项调查的抽样不当，导致结论不准确。| The researchers missampled the soil, affecting the validity of their experiment. 研究人员对土壤进行了错误取样，影响了他们实验的有效性。 | 近义词: misrepresent, distort | 反义词: accurately sample | 词性: 动词
