parasocial: /ˌpærəˈsoʊʃəl/ | adj. |  准社会的；单方面的 |  Of or relating to a relationship in which one person extends emotional energy, interest and time, and the other party, the persona, is completely unaware of the other’s existence.  |  She developed a parasocial relationship with the main character of her favorite TV show. 她和她最喜欢的电视剧主角发展出了一种准社会关系。|  One-sided, imagined, pseudo |  Reciprocal, mutual | adjective

parasocial interaction: /ˌpærəˈsoʊʃəl ɪntərˈækʃən/ | n. | 准社会互动 | The seemingly interpersonal relationship between a media user and media persona. |  She felt a strong parasocial interaction with the vlogger, leaving comments on every video.  她感觉和这位视频博主有很强的准社会互动，在每个视频下都留言。 | one-sided communication, imagined relationship |  real-life interaction | noun
