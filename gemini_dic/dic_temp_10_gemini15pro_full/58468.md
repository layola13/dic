ductor:/ˈdʌktər/| n.| 导体，导管，售票员 |A substance or object that allows electricity, heat, or sound to pass through it.  A person who collects fares or tickets on a bus or train. |例句：Copper is a good conductor of electricity. 铜是良好的电导体。

例句：The conductor punched my ticket. 售票员在我的票上打了个孔。|近义词：leader, guide, director (for a person); conduit, channel (for a substance) |反义词:insulator (for a substance)|n.
