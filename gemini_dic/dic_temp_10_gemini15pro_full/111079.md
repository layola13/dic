listicle:/ˈlɪstɪkəl/ | n. | 列表文章；清单体文章 | An online article presented in the form of a numbered or bulleted list. | Example: BuzzFeed is known for its listicles. BuzzFeed以其列表文章而闻名。 | Example:  The listicle format is popular because it's easy to read. 列表文章的格式很受欢迎，因为它易于阅读。 | Synonyms: list article, numbered list, bulleted list | Antonyms: essay, long-form article | noun
