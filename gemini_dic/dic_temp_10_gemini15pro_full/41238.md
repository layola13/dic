coralwort:/ˈkɒrəlˌwɜːt/ (UK) /ˈkɔːrəlˌwɜːrt/ (US)| n. |延龄草|A small herbaceous plant, *Cardamine bulbifera* (formerly *Dentaria bulbifera*), of the mustard family (Brassicaceae), having white or pinkish flowers and bearing small bulbils in the axils of the upper leaves.  It is native to Europe and western Asia.|Coralwort is a shade-loving plant often found in damp woodland.|近义词:toothwort,  bulbiferous corydalis|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
