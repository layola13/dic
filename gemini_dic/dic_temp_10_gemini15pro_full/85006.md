harams:/həˈrɑːmz/ | adj. | 被禁止的，非法的 (尤指伊斯兰教法禁止的) | Forbidden or unlawful according to Islamic law. | Alcohol is considered haram. 酒被认为是非法的。 | Synonyms: forbidden, prohibited, unlawful | Antonyms: halal, permissible, lawful | adjective
