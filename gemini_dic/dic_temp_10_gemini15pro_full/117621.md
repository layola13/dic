Example 2: Megascans significantly speeds up the workflow for creating photorealistic scenes. Megascans 大大加快了创建逼真场景的工作流程。|近义词:textures, 3D assets, scanned data|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "hand-crafted assets")|词性:noun
