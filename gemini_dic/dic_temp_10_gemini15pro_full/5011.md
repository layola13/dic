all-better:/ɔːlˈbɛtər/
adjective
痊愈的，好了的
Fully recovered from an illness or injury, especially a minor one.
"My knee is all better now." 我的膝盖现在完全好了。
近义词:cured, healed, recovered, well
反义词:sick, ill, injured, hurt
词性:adjective

词源:  Compound of "all" and "better." "All" in this context emphasizes the completeness of the recovery. "Better" indicates an improvement in health or condition.
