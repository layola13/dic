megadyne: /ˈmɛɡədaɪn/ | n. | 百万达因 | A unit of force equal to one million dynes.  A dyne is the force required to accelerate a mass of one gram at a rate of one centimeter per second squared.  It is a CGS unit of force. | The force required to lift a small insect can be measured in megadynes.  | 近义词: meganewton (in the SI system) | 反义词: N/A | noun
