attributive: /əˈtrɪbjʊtɪv/ | n.| 定语 |A word or phrase that acts as an attributive.|  "Red" is the attributive in "red car". “红色”是“红色汽车”中的定语。| synonyms: modifier | antonyms:  None | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *attributīvus, from Latin attributus (past participle of attribuere ‘assign to’), from ad- ‘to’ + tribuere ‘assign’.
