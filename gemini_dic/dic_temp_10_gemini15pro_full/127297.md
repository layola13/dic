navigator:/ˈnævɪɡeɪtər/| n.|航海家，领航员，导航仪|A person who directs the route or course of a ship, aircraft, or other form of transportation, especially by using instruments and maps.  A device or system that uses signals to determine the position of something, such as a ship or aircraft, and to plan its route.|例句：The navigator plotted the ship's course across the ocean. 领航员绘制了船只穿越海洋的航线。

例句：The car's satellite navigator helped us find our way through the unfamiliar city. 汽车的卫星导航仪帮助我们在陌生的城市里找到了路。|近义词：pilot, guide, director, explorer|反义词:follower, passenger|noun
