escallop: /ɪˈskɒləp/ (UK), /ɪˈskɑːləp/ (US) | n. vt.|扇贝；扇贝壳；用扇贝壳装饰|A shellfish with a ribbed fan-shaped shell.  As a verb, it means to bake (food) in a sauce, often topped with breadcrumbs.|例句：
We had scallops for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了扇贝。

The dish was escalloped with potatoes. 这道菜是用土豆泥烤制的，上面点缀着面包屑。

近义词：scallop shell, clam, oyster|反义词: None (no direct opposite for a type of shellfish)|词性: Noun, Verb
