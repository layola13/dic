carriages:/ˈkærɪdʒɪz/
n.
马车；（火车）车厢；运输；姿态；举止
a vehicle with four wheels drawn by two or more horses, used for carrying passengers
The queen arrived in a horse-drawn carriage. 女王乘坐马车抵达。
coach, wagon, cart, chariot
automobile, airplane


carriages:/ˈkærɪdʒɪz/
n.
运输
the transporting of goods or people
The carriage of goods by road is expensive. 公路运输货物很昂贵。
transportation, conveyance, delivery, shipment



carriages:/ˈkærɪdʒɪz/
n.
姿态；举止
a person's way of standing or moving
She has an elegant carriage. 她的姿态很优雅。
posture, bearing, deportment, demeanor
