nonpurulent:/ˌnɒnˈpjʊərələnt/ /ˌnɑːnˈpjʊrələnt/| adj.|无脓的；非化脓性的|Not containing or producing pus; not purulent.|The wound was clean and nonpurulent.  伤口干净且无脓。|The doctor confirmed that the infection was nonpurulent. 医生确认感染是非化脓性的。|近义词：non-suppurative, sterile|反义词: purulent, suppurative|词性:形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin, meaning "not"
purulent (adjective): from Latin *purulentus*, from *pus, puris* "pus"
-ent (suffix): forming adjectives, often indicating a present participle
