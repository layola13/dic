illusively:/ɪˈluːsɪvli/
adv.
虚幻地；迷惑地
in a way that creates a false or misleading impression
The moon hung illusively in the sky, appearing closer than it actually was. 月亮虚幻地挂在天空，看起来比实际更近。
近义词: deceptively, misleadingly, elusively
反义词: clearly, distinctly, realistically
词性: 副词
