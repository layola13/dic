abortiveness:/əˈbɔːrtɪvnəs/| n.|失败，夭折，无结果|The abortiveness of the project was due to lack of funding. 该项目的失败是由于缺乏资金。|例句：The abortiveness of the peace talks led to renewed conflict. 和谈的失败导致了冲突重燃。

The abortiveness of her attempts to learn the piano frustrated her. 她学习钢琴的屡次失败让她感到沮丧。|近义词：failure, futility, fruitlessness|反义词：success, effectiveness, fruitfulness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From abortive + -ness.  Abortive comes from the Latin *abortivus*, from aboriri "to miscarry," from ab- "away, from" + oriri "to arise, be born."

USAGE->
The abortiveness of the attempt was evident. 尝试的失败显而易见。

The play was closed after a week due to the abortiveness of its run.  由于演出失败，该剧一周后停演。


PHRASE->
the abortiveness of (something)  (某事的)失败
