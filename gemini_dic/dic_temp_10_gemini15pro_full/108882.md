lectrices:/lɛkˈtriːsiːz/
n.
female readers; female lecturers
A female reader or lecturer, especially in a French-speaking context.
The lectrices gathered for their annual conference.  女讲师们聚集在一起参加年度会议。
The lectrices offered insightful commentary on the text. 女读者们对文本提供了富有洞察力的评论。
近义词: readers, lecturers, instructors (if teaching)
反义词:  listeners, audience (if referring to the act of reading aloud)
词性: Noun (plural)


lectrice:/lɛkˈtriːs/
n.
female reader; female lecturer
A female reader or lecturer, especially in a French-speaking context.
The lectrice captivated her audience with her expressive reading. 女朗读者以她富有表现力的朗读吸引了她的听众。
The university hired a new lectrice for the French department. 这所大学为法语系聘请了一位新的女讲师。
近义词: reader, lecturer, instructor (if teaching)
反义词: listener, audience (if referring to the act of reading aloud)
词性: Noun (singular)


词源：From French lectrice, feminine form of lecteur (“reader”), from Latin lēctor (“reader”), from legō (“I read”).
