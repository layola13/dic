asemia:/eɪˈsiːmiə/ /əˈsiːmiə/| n.|失语症; 无法理解或表达语言|A medical term referring to the inability to comprehend or formulate language due to damage to specific brain regions.  It can affect spoken and written language, as well as gestures.|He suffered from asemia after the stroke and could no longer speak or understand words. 他在中风后患上了失语症，再也无法说话或理解单词了。

|The asemia made it impossible for her to communicate her needs. 失语症使她无法表达自己的需求。|近义词：aphasia|反义词:|n.
