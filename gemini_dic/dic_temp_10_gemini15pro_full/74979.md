frictional:/ˈfrɪkʃənəl/| adj.|摩擦的；由摩擦引起的|Relating to or caused by friction; involving friction.|The frictional force slows down the moving object. 摩擦力使运动的物体减速。|Synonyms: rubbing, abrasive, rasping|Antonyms: smooth, slick, frictionless|adjective

friction:/ˈfrɪkʃən/| n.|摩擦；摩擦力；冲突，不和|The resistance that one surface or object encounters when moving over another.|The friction between the two countries increased.  两国之间的摩擦加剧了。|Synonyms: abrasion, attrition, rubbing, conflict, disagreement|Antonyms: harmony, agreement, peace|noun
