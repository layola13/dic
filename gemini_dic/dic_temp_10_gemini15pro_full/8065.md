anthropomorpha:/ˌænθrəpəˈmɔːrfə/ | n. |拟人化；被赋予人形的事物；|Anthropomorpha is the attribution of human traits, emotions, or intentions to non-human entities. It is considered to be an innate tendency of human psychology.| Many children's stories feature anthropomorpha, such as talking animals or toys. 许多儿童故事以拟人化为特色，例如会说话的动物或玩具。 | synonym: personification, humanization | antonym: objectification | noun
