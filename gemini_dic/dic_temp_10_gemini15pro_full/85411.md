harvestability:/ˌhɑːrvɪstəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可收获性；可采性 | The ease and efficiency with which a crop can be harvested. | The harvestability of the wheat crop was affected by the heavy rains. 小麦作物的可收获性受到了暴雨的影响。| synonyms: reap-ability, gather-ability | antonyms: unharvestability, non-harvestability | noun
