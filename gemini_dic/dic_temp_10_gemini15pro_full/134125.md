nonimpedimentary:/ˌnɒnɪmˌpɛdɪˈmɛntəri/
adjective
无障碍的，不阻碍的
Not presenting any impediment; not hindering or obstructing.
The council issued a nonimpedimentary certificate for the couple to marry.  市议会签发了这对夫妇结婚的无障碍证明。
The process was nonimpedimentary, allowing for a smooth transition.  这个过程是无障碍的，允许平稳过渡。
synonyms: unobstructed, unhindered, facilitating, permissive
antonyms: obstructive, hindering, impeding, restrictive
词性: adjective
