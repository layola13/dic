hydraulical:/haɪˈdrɔːlɪkəl/
adjective
水力的；液压的
operated by, moved by, or employing liquid (water or oil) under pressure.
例句：The hydraulical system failed, causing the brakes to malfunction. 液压系统失灵，导致刹车故障。

例句：Hydraulical engineering is a challenging but rewarding field. 水利工程是一个充满挑战但回报丰厚的领域。

近义词：hydraulic
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps manual or mechanical in certain contexts)
词性: adjective
