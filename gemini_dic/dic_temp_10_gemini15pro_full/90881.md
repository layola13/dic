hunkies:/ˈhʌŋkiz/| n.|体力劳动者，（尤指）外国来的体力劳动者；东欧移民（贬义）|A derogatory term referring to immigrants, particularly from Eastern Europe, often employed to suggest they are uneducated and perform menial labor.  This term carries strong negative connotations and is considered offensive.|Example 1:  The foreman yelled at the "hunkies" to work faster. 工头对那些“外国佬”大喊让他们工作快点。

Example 2:  The term "hunkies" was used to denigrate immigrant workers in the early 20th century.  “hunkies”一词在20世纪早期被用来贬低移民工人。|近义词：laborers, immigrants, foreigners (although these lack the pejorative connotation)|反义词:  None (due to the derogatory nature, a direct opposite is difficult to provide).|词性:名词
