drumble:/ˈdrʌmbəl/| v. |低沉地发出嗡嗡声；缓慢而笨拙地移动|To make a low, rumbling sound; to move slowly and awkwardly.|The old refrigerator drumbled in the corner of the kitchen.  旧冰箱在厨房的角落里发出低沉的嗡嗡声。

The tired hikers drumbled along the path.  疲惫的徒步旅行者沿着小路缓慢而笨拙地行走。|近义词：hum, drone, rumble, lumber|反义词：sprint, dash|词性：动词
