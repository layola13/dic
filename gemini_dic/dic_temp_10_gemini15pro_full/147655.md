overponderousnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈpɒndərəsnəsəz/ | n. | 过于沉重；过于繁琐 |  The state of being excessively ponderous; excessive weight or heaviness, especially in style or manner. |  His writing suffered from overponderousnesses, making it difficult to read. 他的写作过于沉重，难以阅读。| 近义词: heaviness, ponderosity, clumsiness, awkwardness | 反义词: lightness, agility, grace, conciseness | noun

overponderous:/ˌoʊvərˈpɒndərəs/ | adj. | 过于沉重的；过于繁琐的 | Too heavy or bulky; excessively weighty, especially in style or manner; clumsy or awkward. | His overponderous prose made the lecture tedious. 他过于沉重的散文使讲座变得冗长乏味。| 近义词: heavy, weighty, cumbersome, awkward | 反义词: light, agile, graceful, concise | adjective
