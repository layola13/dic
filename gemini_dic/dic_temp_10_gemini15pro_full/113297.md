lutheranism:/ˈluːθərənɪzəm/ | n. |路德教，路德宗|A major branch of Protestant Christianity based on the theological teachings of Martin Luther.| Example: Lutheranism is the largest Protestant denomination in Germany. 路德教是德国最大的新教教派。| Example:  He converted to Lutheranism after studying the writings of Martin Luther.  他研读了马丁·路德的著作后皈依了路德教。 |Synonym: Protestantism (broadly), Evangelicalism (in some contexts)|Antonym: Catholicism, Calvinism|noun
