discredit:/dɪˈskredɪt/
n.|不信任，怀疑；丢脸，耻辱|Lack of belief or trust; something that causes disgrace or dishonor.|The scandal brought discredit on the whole company.  这件丑闻给整个公司带来了耻辱。|disgrace, dishonor, shame, reproach|credit, honor, reputation|noun
discredit:/dɪˈskredɪt/
vt.|使不相信，使怀疑；败坏…的名誉，使丢脸|To cause someone or something to seem not to be trusted or respected; to damage the reputation of someone or something.|The lawyer discredited the witness by revealing his past criminal record.  律师通过揭露证人过去的犯罪记录来败坏他的名声。|disgrace, dishonor, shame, tarnish|credit, honor, praise, commend|verb
