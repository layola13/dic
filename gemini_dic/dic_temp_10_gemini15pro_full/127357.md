ncr:/ˌɛn siː ˈɑːr/ |abbr.| (mathematics)  n choose r; the binomial coefficient; the number of combinations of n things taken r at a time |NCR is used to represent "n choose r" in combinatorics. | The NCR of 5 and 2 is 10.  5个里取2个的组合数是10。|近义词:combination|反义词:permutation|abbr.
