affirmation:/ˌæfərˈmeɪʃən/| n.|肯定，断言；证实；批准|The president's affirmation of the treaty ensured its implementation. 总统批准了该条约，确保了它的执行。|例句：His nod was taken as an affirmation. 他的点头被认为是一种肯定。

例句：Daily affirmations can improve your self-esteem. 每日的肯定可以提升你的自尊。|近义词：confirmation, assertion, declaration|反义词：denial, negation|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *affirmātiō, from Latin affirmō (“I affirm”), from ad (“to”) + firmō (“I strengthen”).
