foresaid:/ˈfɔːrˌsed/| adj.|上述的，前述的|Mentioned or referred to earlier.|The aforesaid document outlines the terms of the agreement. 前述文件概述了协议的条款。|近义词：aforementioned, above-mentioned, previously mentioned|反义词：following, subsequent|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *forseid*, from Old English *foresecgan* (to predict, foretell), from *fore-* (before) + *secgan* (to say). The past participle *foresecgd* evolved into *forsaid*.
