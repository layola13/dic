ghosted:/ˈɡəʊstɪd/| v. | 忽視，突然不再联系，玩消失 |To abruptly end communication with someone (often a romantic interest) without explanation. |例句：He ghosted her after three dates. 他約會三次後就玩失蹤了。

例句：She was ghosted by her online friend. 她的網友突然不再联系她了。

近义词：ditched, ignored, dropped, jilted | 反义词：communicated, engaged | 词性: 动词
