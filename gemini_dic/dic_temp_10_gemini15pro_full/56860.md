dorsiventral:/ˌdɔːrsɪˈventrəl/
adjective
背腹的；上下不同的
Having differentiated dorsal and ventral surfaces.  Relating to or involving the dorsal and ventral sides.
The leaves of many plants show dorsiventral anatomy. 许多植物的叶子呈现背腹性解剖结构。
近义词: bifacial
反义词: radial, cylindrical
词性: adjective
