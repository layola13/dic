middle-age:/ˌmɪdlˈeɪdʒ/ | n.| 中年|The period between youth and old age, typically between the ages of 45 and 65. |例句：She's in middle age and enjoying it. 她正值中年，并且很享受。|近义词：midlife |反义词:youth, old age |词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *middel age*, from Old French *moien age* (whence also Modern French *moyen âge*), from Late Latin *media aetas*. Equivalent to *middle* +‎ *age*.
