exteriors:/ɪkˈstɪəriərz/
noun
外部，外观，外景
The exteriors of the houses were all painted white. 这些房子的外部都被漆成了白色。
例句：The film's exteriors were shot in Montana. 这部电影的外景是在蒙大拿州拍摄的。
近义词：outsides, facades, surfaces
反义词：interiors
词性：名词(plural)

exterior:/ɪkˈstɪəriər/
noun
外部，外观，外景
The exterior of the building was covered in scaffolding.  这栋建筑的外部被脚手架覆盖。
例句：The car's exterior was badly damaged in the accident. 这辆车的外部在事故中严重受损。
近义词：outside, facade, surface
反义词：interior
词性：名词

exterior:/ɪkˈstɪəriər/
adjective
外部的，外面的，外表的
The exterior walls of the house need repainting. 房子的外墙需要重新粉刷。
例句：The exterior doors were locked. 外面的门都锁上了。
近义词：outer, outside, external
反义词：interior, inner, internal
词性：形容词
