humiri:/hʊˈmɪri/ | n. |胡蜜树，胡蜜树脂|A tropical American tree of the genus Humiria, yielding a fragrant resin.|The humiri tree is native to South America. 胡蜜树原产于南美洲。|The resin of the humiri tree is used in perfumes. 胡蜜树的树脂用于香水。|Synonym: Humiria|Antonym: N/A|Noun
