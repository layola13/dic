codesigning:/ˈkoʊdˌsaɪnɪŋ/| n.|代码签名|The process of digitally signing software to confirm its authenticity and integrity and guarantee that it hasn't been tampered with.|Example 1: Codesigning is essential for distributing apps on iOS. 代码签名对于在 iOS 上分发应用程序至关重要。

Example 2:  Without proper codesigning, your application might be blocked by the operating system. 没有正确的代码签名，你的应用程序可能会被操作系统阻止。|近义词：digital signature, code signing certificate|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
