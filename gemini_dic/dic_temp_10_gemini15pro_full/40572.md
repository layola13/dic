contuses:/kənˈtjuːzɪz/
| n. | 挫伤；青肿 |  An injury to the body, typically one in which the skin is not broken but there is bruising underneath. | The boxer suffered several contuses to the head and body. 拳击手头部和身体多处挫伤。 |  bruises, injuries | cuts, lacerations | noun
contuses:/kənˈtjuːzɪz/
| v. | 挫伤；使…出现青肿 | To injure the body, typically one in which the skin is not broken but there is bruising underneath. |  The fall contused his knee. 摔倒挫伤了他的膝盖。 | bruise, injure | heal, mend | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin contusus, past participle of contundere "to bruise, crush," from com- "together" + tundere "to beat, pound."
