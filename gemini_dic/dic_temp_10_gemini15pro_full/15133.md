bacilliform: /bəˈsɪlɪfɔːrm/ /bəˈsɪləˌfɔrm/
adjective
杆状的；棒状的
Shaped like a bacillus; rod-shaped.
Bacilliform bacteria were observed under the microscope. 在显微镜下观察到了杆状细菌。
近义词: rod-shaped, cylindrical
反义词: coccoid, spherical
词性: adjective

词源:  From bacillus + -i- + -form.  "Bacillus" comes from Latin, meaning "little stick". "-form" comes from Latin "forma", meaning "shape".
