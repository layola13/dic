bookmonger:/ˈbʊkˌmʌŋɡər/| n.|书商；书痴；爱书的人|A person who deals in books, especially old books, or who has an excessive fondness for books.|He was a well-known bookmonger with a shop full of rare first editions. 他是一位著名的书商，他的店里满是珍稀的初版书。|近义词：bibliophile, book lover, book dealer, bookseller|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps someone who dislikes or avoids books) |noun
