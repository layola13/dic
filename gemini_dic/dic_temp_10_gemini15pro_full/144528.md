outvanished: /ˌaʊtˈvænɪʃt/
verb
彻底消失，比...消失得更早
to vanish or disappear more completely or for a longer time than something or someone else
The dinosaurs outvanished the trilobites. 恐龙比三叶虫消失得更彻底/更早。
The ancient civilization outvanished many other cultures of its time.  这个古老的文明比同时代的许多其他文化消失得更早。
近义词: outlasted, surpassed, exceeded
反义词: predeceased
