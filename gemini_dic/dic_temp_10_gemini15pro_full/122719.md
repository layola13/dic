mohawk:/ˈmoʊhɔːk/ (US) /ˈməʊhɔːk/ (UK)| n.|莫霍克人，莫霍克族发型|A member of a Native American people formerly living mainly in the Mohawk River valley of northern New York state. Also, a hairstyle with a shaved head except for a central strip of upright hair.|Many Mohawks live in Canada and the United States. 许多莫霍克人居住在加拿大和美国。|The punk rocker sported a bright red mohawk.  那个朋克摇滚歌手留着一个亮红色的莫霍克发型。|synonyms: Iroquois (for the people), Mohican (sometimes used interchangeably, but technically distinct), crew cut (for the hairstyle, loosely)|antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the people), long hair (for the hairstyle)|noun
