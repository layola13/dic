fulgurant:/ˈfʌlɡjʊrənt/| adj.|闪亮的，耀眼的；迅疾的，快速的|Coming on suddenly and intensely; dazzling or stunning.  Resembling or suggestive of lightning; flashing.|The fulgurant pain in his chest made him cry out. 他胸口的剧烈疼痛让他叫出声来。|Synonyms: dazzling, blinding, flashing, lightning-like, sudden, swift, rapid|Antonyms: dim, dull, gradual, slow|adjective


fulgurating:/ˈfʌlɡjʊreɪtɪŋ/| adj.|闪亮的，耀眼的；迅疾的，快速的|Present participle of fulgurate, meaning "to flash or dart like lightning".  Used to describe something that is happening suddenly and intensely.|The fulgurating fireworks lit up the night sky. 闪烁的烟花照亮了夜空。|Synonyms: flashing, dazzling, sparkling, glittering, gleaming|Antonyms: dim, dull, fading, waning|adjective
