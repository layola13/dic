anticompetitively:/ˌæntiˌkəmˈpɛtɪtɪvli/
adverb
反竞争地；限制竞争地
in a manner that reduces or eliminates competition
The company acted anticompetitively by buying out all of its rivals.  该公司通过收购所有竞争对手来进行反竞争行为。
They used their market dominance anticompetitively to stifle innovation. 他们利用其市场支配地位进行反竞争行为以扼杀创新。
近义词:monopolistically, restrictively
反义词:competitively, fairly
词性:adverb
