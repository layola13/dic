auriflamme: /ˌɔːrɪˈflæm/ | n. | 金焰旗；金黄色火焰；光辉灿烂的象征 | A gold or orange banner or flame, used as a symbol of royal power or brilliant achievement.  It specifically refers to the sacred banner of the French kings. |  The auriflamme of the French monarchy was lost in battle. 法国君主制的金焰旗在战斗中丢失了。| The setting sun cast an auriflamme across the sky. 落日将金色的火焰洒满天空。 | synonyms: banner, standard, pennant, emblem, symbol | antonyms: obscurity, insignificance | noun
