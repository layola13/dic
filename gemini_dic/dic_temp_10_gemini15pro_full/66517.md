exhalence:/ˈɛkshələns/ /ɛkˈseɪləns/| n.|呼气；散发物；蒸发；馨香|Something exhaled, breathed out, or given off as a vapor or aroma.  An emanation, especially a pleasant odor.|The exhalence of the flowers filled the room.  花朵的香气弥漫了整个房间。|近义词：aroma, fragrance, scent, perfume, effluvium|反义词: stench, odor, smell|词性: noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *exhalentia, from Latin exhalare "to breathe out," from ex- "out" + halare "to breathe."
