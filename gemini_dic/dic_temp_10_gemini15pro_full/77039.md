garlandlike:/ˈɡɑːrləndˌlaɪk/
adjective
像花环的; 花环状的
Resembling a garland; arranged in a garland-like fashion.  Like a wreath or festoon.
The bride wore a garlandlike headpiece of white flowers.  新娘戴着白色花朵编成的花环状头饰。
The Christmas lights were strung garlandlike across the street. 圣诞彩灯像花环一样挂在街道上。
近义词:festooned, wreathed, garlanded
反义词:plain, unadorned
词性:adjective
