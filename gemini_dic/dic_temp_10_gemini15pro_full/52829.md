diluvial:/dɪˈluːviəl/| adj.| 洪积的，由洪水造成的 | Relating to or brought about by a flood; specifically, relating to the Deluge (Noah's flood). | The diluvial deposits covered a vast area.  洪积沉积物覆盖了广阔的区域。| The museum displayed diluvial artifacts from the region.  博物馆展出了该地区的洪积文物。|近义词：alluvial, flood, deluge |反义词: n/a |词性: adjective

diluvium:/dɪˈluːviəm/| n.| 洪积层，洪积物 |A deposit of clay, sand, gravel, and boulders left by flowing water, especially a flood, or by the melting of glacial ice. | The diluvium contained fossils of extinct animals. 洪积层中包含已灭绝动物的化石。| The geologists studied the diluvium to understand past climate changes. 地质学家研究洪积层以了解过去的气候变化。| 近义词：alluvium, drift |反义词: n/a |词性: noun
