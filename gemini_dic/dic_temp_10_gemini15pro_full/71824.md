fleet's:/fliːts/
noun
舰队（的），车队（的）
Possessive form of "fleet," meaning belonging to or associated with a fleet.
The fleet's departure was delayed due to the storm. 由于暴风雨，舰队的出发被推迟了。
The company's fleet's maintenance costs were high this year.  今年公司车队的维护费用很高。
synonyms: navy's, flotilla's, squadron's
antonyms: individual's, single's
