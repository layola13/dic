metagenesis:/ˌmɛtəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
n.| 世代交替 |The alternation of generations, especially between sexual and asexual reproductive forms, as in ferns and certain jellyfish.|The jellyfish exhibits metagenesis, alternating between a polyp and medusa stage. 水母表现出世代交替，在水螅体和水母体阶段之间交替。|alternation of generations| - |noun
Etymology: From Greek *meta* (change) + *genesis* (origin, creation)
