blogger:/ˈblɒɡər/ (UK) /ˈblɑːɡər/ (US)| n. | 博主；写博客的人 | A person who writes or publishes a blog. |  She's a fashion blogger with thousands of followers. 她是一位拥有数千粉丝的时尚博主。

|  He started as a food blogger, reviewing local restaurants. 他最初是一名美食博主，评论当地餐馆。
| 近义词: vlogger, online journalist, web writer | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "traditional journalist") | 词性: noun
