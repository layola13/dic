collaboratively:/kəˈlæbərətɪvli/
adverb|合作地，协作地|in a way that involves working together with other people|例句：The project was completed collaboratively by a team of experts.  这个项目是由一个专家团队合作完成的。|例句：They worked collaboratively on the presentation. 他们在演示文稿上进行了合作。|近义词：jointly, together, cooperatively|反义词: individually, separately, independently|词性:副词

Etymology:
From collaborate + -ive + -ly.  Collaborate comes from Latin collaborare "to labor together," from com- "together" + laborare "to work."
