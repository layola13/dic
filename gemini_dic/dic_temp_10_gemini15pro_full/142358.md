orthopaedics: /ˌɔːθəˈpiːdɪks/ /ˌɔːrθəˈpiːdɪks/ | n. | 骨科，矫形外科 |  The branch of medicine dealing with the correction of deformities of bones or muscles.  It comes from the Greek words "orthos," meaning "straight," and "pais," meaning "child." Originally, orthopedics focused on correcting skeletal deformities in children. |  She specializes in orthopedics and treats a lot of sports injuries. 她专攻骨科，治疗很多运动损伤。 | synonyms:  orthopaedy, bone surgery | antonyms: N/A | noun


orthopedic: /ˌɔːθəˈpiːdɪk/ /ˌɔːrθəˈpiːdɪk/ | adj. | 骨科的，矫形的 | Relating to the branch of medicine dealing with the correction of deformities of bones or muscles. | He needs orthopedic shoes to correct his flat feet. 他需要矫形鞋来矫正他的扁平足。 | synonyms: orthopaedic, musculoskeletal | antonyms: N/A | adjective
