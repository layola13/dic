half-cured:/ˌhæf ˈkjʊərd/ | adj. | 半固化的，未完全固化的 | Partially cured; not completely processed or preserved. | The half-cured concrete was still soft enough to be molded.  半固化的混凝土仍然足够柔软，可以塑形。| The half-cured ham needed further smoking. 这块半熟的火腿需要进一步烟熏。 | 近义词: partially cured, undercured | 反义词: fully cured, completely cured | 词性: 形容词

词源: "half" (Old English healf) + "cured" (past participle of "cure" - from Old French curer, ultimately from Latin curare "to care for")
