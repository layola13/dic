at-worst:/æt wɜːrst/| adv.|最坏的情况下；往最坏里说|In an at-worst scenario, we might lose all our investment. 最坏的情况下，我们可能会损失所有的投资。|Synonyms: in the worst-case scenario, if things go badly, at the very least|Antonyms: at best, in the best-case scenario, ideally|adverb
at-worst:/æt wɜːrst/| adj.|最坏的|This is the at-worst outcome we could have imagined. 这是我们能想到的最坏的结果了。  (Note: While "at-worst" can function adjectivally, it's less common than its adverbial use.)|Synonyms: worst possible, least favorable|Antonyms: best possible, most favorable|adjective
