osmazome: /ˈɒzməˌzəʊm/ /ˈɑːzməˌzoʊm/ | n. | 肉味；肉香；鲜味 |  A water-soluble extract of meat containing the flavoring and aromatic constituents. It was formerly thought to be a distinct substance. | The rich osmazome of the roasted venison filled the air. 烤鹿肉浓郁的香味弥漫在空气中。 | 近义词: aroma, flavor, savor | 反义词: blandness | 词性: noun

词源: From French *osmazôme*, from Ancient Greek ὀσμή (*osmḗ*, “smell”) + ζωμός (*zōmós*, “broth”).
