baroque: /bəˈroʊk/ /bəˈrɒk/ | adj. n. | 巴洛克式的，巴洛克风格；巴洛克时期 | Relating to or denoting a style of European architecture, music, and art of the 17th and 18th centuries that followed Mannerism and is characterized by ornate detail.  As a noun, it refers to the Baroque period or the Baroque style. | The palace is a beautiful example of baroque architecture. 这座宫殿是巴洛克式建筑的典范。 |  The composer's music is clearly from the Baroque period.  这位作曲家的音乐显然是巴洛克时期的。 | 近义词: ornate, elaborate, flamboyant | 反义词: simple, plain, austere | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From French *baroque* "irregularly shaped pearl," from Portuguese *barroco,* Spanish *barrueco* with the same meaning, probably ultimately from Latin *verruca* "wart."
