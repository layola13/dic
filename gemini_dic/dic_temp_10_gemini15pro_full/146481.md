overfurnished:/ˌōvərˈfɜːrnɪʃt/
adjective
过度布置的；家具过多的
Containing too much furniture.
The room was dark and overfurnished.  这个房间光线昏暗，家具太多。
Her apartment is small and overfurnished. 她的公寓很小，但家具太多。
overstuffed, cluttered, crowded
sparse, minimalist
adjective
