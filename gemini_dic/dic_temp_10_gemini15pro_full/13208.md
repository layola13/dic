at-the-most:/æt ðə məʊst/ | adv. | 至多；最多 |  Meaning "no more than," indicating an upper limit.  It emphasizes that the quantity or number mentioned is the absolute maximum. |  We can accommodate at the most ten people in this room.  我们这个房间最多可以容纳十个人。| Synonyms: at most, maximum, not more than, tops | Antonyms: at least, minimum, at a minimum | adverb

at-the-most:/æt ðə məʊst/ | adj. (less common) | 最多的（较少用）| Used attributively to mean "maximum."  Less common than the adverbial use. | The at-the-most capacity of this elevator is 10 people.  这台电梯的最大容量是10人。  (This usage is less common and might sound awkward. "Maximum capacity" is preferred.) | Synonyms: maximum, greatest | Antonyms: minimum, least, smallest | adjective
