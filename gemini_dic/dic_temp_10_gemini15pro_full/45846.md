cut a deal:/kʌt ə diːl/
verb phrase
达成交易；做成买卖
To reach an agreement or make a bargain, often in business or politics.
The two companies cut a deal to share the profits.  两家公司达成协议分享利润。
The government cut a deal with the union to avoid a strike. 政府与工会达成协议以避免罢工。
近义词:make a deal, strike a bargain, reach an agreement, negotiate a settlement
反义词:break off negotiations
