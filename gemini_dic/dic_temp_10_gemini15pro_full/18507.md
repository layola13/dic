beneaped:/bɪˈniːpt/| adj.|搁浅的，陷入困境的|Left aground by the tide; stranded.  Figuratively, in difficulty.|The ship was beneaped on a sandbar during low tide. 这艘船在退潮时搁浅在沙洲上。 The travelers were beneaped by the sudden blizzard.  旅行者们因突如其来的暴风雪而受困。|近义词：stranded, grounded, marooned, stuck|反义词：afloat, free|词性:形容词
Etymology: From be- + neap + -ed.  "Neap" refers to a tide of minimum range.
