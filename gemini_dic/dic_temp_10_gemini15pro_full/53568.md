disbeliever's:/ˌdɪsbɪˈliːvərz/
noun
不信者（的）
Possession form of "disbeliever" - belonging to or associated with a person who does not believe.
The disbeliever's skepticism was evident in his questions. 不信者的怀疑在他的问题中显而易见。
synonyms: skeptic's, doubter's, cynic's
antonyms: believer's, faithful's
