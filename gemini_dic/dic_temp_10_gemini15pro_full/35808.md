cockeyednesses:/ˌkɒkiˈaɪdnɪsɪz/
noun
斜视，歪斜，古怪
The state of being cockeyed; askew or lopsided vision or appearance.  A general state of crookedness, awryness, or eccentricity.
Her cockeyednesses made it difficult for her to judge distances accurately. 她的斜视使她难以准确判断距离。
crookedness, askewness, lopsidedness, eccentricity
straightness, symmetry, normality
