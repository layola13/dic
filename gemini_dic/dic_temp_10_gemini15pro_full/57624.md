draughtily:/ˈdrɑːf.tɪ.li/ | adv. | 通风地，有穿堂风地 | In a draughty manner; so as to be exposed to draughts of air. | The old house was draughtily built, and the wind whistled through the gaps in the window frames.  这栋老房子建得很通风，风从窗框的缝隙中呼啸而过。 |例句：The windows were ill-fitting and the room was terribly draughty. 窗户安装得很差，房间里非常通风。|近义词：airily, breezily, coldly |反义词: snugly, warmly, tightly |词性: 副词

draught:/drɑːft/ | n. |  气流，通风，草稿 |A current of air (usually coming into a room or vehicle). Also: a preliminary version of a piece of writing. | There's a nasty draught coming from under the door. 门底下有一股讨厌的冷风。 |例句：She shivered in the cold draught. 她被冷风吹得瑟瑟发抖。|近义词：breeze, current, draft |反义词: stillness, warmth |词性: 名词
