overnobleness:/ˌoʊvərˈnoʊbəlnəs/
n.
过分的崇高；过度的贵族气
Excessive nobleness; exaggerated aristocratic behavior.
His overnobleness made him seem out of touch with the common people. 他过分的贵族气让他看起来与平民百姓脱节。
近义词：haughtiness, arrogance, pomposity
反义词：humility, modesty, humbleness
词性: noun

词源:
From "over-" (excessive) + "noble" (of high social rank) + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality).  Ultimately derived from Latin "nobilis" (well-known, famous, high-born).
