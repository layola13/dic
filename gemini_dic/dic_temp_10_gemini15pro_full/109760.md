levigation:/ˌlɛvɪˈɡeɪʃən/
n.
研磨，细磨，水磨
The process of grinding an insoluble substance to a fine powder while wet.
The apothecary used levigation to create a smooth ointment. 药剂师用水磨法制作了一种光滑的药膏。
trituration, grinding, milling, pulverization
