lentiscus:/lɛnˈtɪskəs/| n. |乳香黄连木，乳香树|An evergreen shrub or small tree of the Mediterranean region, yielding a fragrant resin called mastic.|The lentiscus provides the resin mastic. 乳香黄连木生产一种叫做乳香的树脂。| The mastic gum is harvested from the lentiscus tree.  乳香胶是从乳香树上采集的。|Synonym: mastic tree|Antonym: N/A|Noun
