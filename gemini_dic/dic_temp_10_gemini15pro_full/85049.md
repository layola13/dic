harbour:/ˈhɑːrbər/ (US) /ˈhɑːrbə(r)/ (UK)| n. v. | 港口，海港；庇护；窝藏；怀有（想法或感情）|A place on the coast where ships may moor in shelter, especially one protected from rough water by piers, jetties, and other artificial structures.  Can also be used figuratively to mean to give shelter or refuge to, or to hold (thoughts or feelings).|The ship sailed into the harbour at dawn.  这艘船在黎明时分驶入港口。|synonyms: port, haven, dock, marina, shelter, refuge | antonyms: expose, expel| noun
