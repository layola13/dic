granger:/ˈɡreɪndʒər/| n.|农场主，农夫；格蘭傑（姓氏）|A granger is a farmer, especially in the American Midwest.|The old granger worked his fields from sunrise to sunset. 老农场主从日出到日落都在田里劳作。|近义词：farmer, agriculturalist|反义词:city dweller|词性:noun
词源:From Anglo-French *graunger, from Late Latin granicare (“to manage a grange”), from granica (“granary”), from granum (“grain”).
