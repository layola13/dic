infiltrators:/ˈɪnfɪltreɪtərz/| n. |渗透者；潜入者|A person who enters or gains access to an organization or place surreptitiously, especially in order to acquire secret information or cause damage.|The infiltrators were caught trying to steal confidential documents. 渗透者在试图窃取机密文件时被抓获。|Synonyms: spies, moles, agents, plants, interlopers|Antonyms: guards, protectors, defenders|noun
