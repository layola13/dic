accommodativenesses: /əˌkɒməˈdeɪtɪvnəsɪz/
noun
乐于助人； accommodatingness的复数
The state of being willing to fit in with someone's wishes or needs.  Multiple instances of a willingness to be helpful and adaptable.
The hotel manager's accommodativenesses ensured every guest felt welcomed and catered to.  酒店经理的乐于助人确保每位客人都感到宾至如归。

His various accommodativenesses towards his colleagues earned him the respect of the entire team. 他对同事的各种照顾赢得了整个团队的尊重。


近义词: helpfulness, obligingness, kindness, courtesy, consideration
反义词: unhelpfulness, inconsiderateness, selfishness

词性: noun (plural)

词源:  From accommodate + -ive + -ness + -es. Ultimately from Latin accommodāre “to make fit, adapt.”
