harebell:/ˈheəˌbel/ (US), /ˈhɛəˌbɛl/ (UK)| n. |风铃草；圆叶风铃草|A delicate blue bell-shaped flower, Campanula rotundifolia, of the heath family, common in northern temperate regions.  It's named for its resemblance to the bells hares were believed to wear. |例句：The harebells swayed gently in the breeze. 风铃草在微风中轻轻摇曳。

例句：A field of harebells carpeted the hillside. 一片风铃草覆盖了山坡。|近义词：bluebell, bellflower|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
