gibibyte:/ˈɡɪbiˌbaɪt/| n.|吉字节|A unit of information equal to 2^30 bytes (1,073,741,824 bytes).  It is a member of the set of binary prefixes defined by the International Electrotechnical Commission (IEC) in 1998.|My new hard drive has a capacity of one gibibyte.|Synonym: gigabyte (often used interchangeably, though technically incorrect)|Antonym: N/A|noun
