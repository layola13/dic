nonclassroom:/ˌnɒnˈklɑːsrʊm/ | /ˌnɑːnˈklæsruːm/ | adj. | 非课堂的，课外的 | Not relating to or taking place in a classroom; outside of the regular school environment. |  Many students engage in nonclassroom activities like sports and clubs. 许多学生参加体育和俱乐部等课外活动。|  The museum offers nonclassroom learning experiences.  博物馆提供课堂外的学习体验。 | 近义词：extracurricular, out-of-class, informal | 反义词：classroom, academic, formal | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + classroom.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "classroom" refers to a room in which classes are held.
