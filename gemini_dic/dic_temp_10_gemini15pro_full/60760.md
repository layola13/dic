electrography:/ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒɡrəfi/ | n. |心电描记术；电描记法|A method of recording the electrical activity of an organ (especially the heart).|Electrography is used to detect heart abnormalities. 心电描记术用于检测心脏异常。|The doctor ordered an electrography to assess the patient's heart rhythm. 医生要求进行心电描记术以评估患者的心律。| 近义词: electrocardiography, ECG, EKG | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps physical examination) | 词性: noun
