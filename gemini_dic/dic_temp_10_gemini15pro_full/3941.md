agro-industrialists:/ˌæɡroʊɪnˈdʌstriəlɪsts/ | n. | 农业工业家，农工企业家 | People or companies involved in both agricultural production and industrial processing of agricultural products.  They often operate large-scale businesses that integrate farming with the manufacturing and distribution of food and other agricultural goods. | The agro-industrialists are investing heavily in new technologies to improve crop yields. 这些农业工业家正在大力投资新技术以提高作物产量。|  Agribusinesses, agricultural corporations |  Small farmers, subsistence farmers | noun
