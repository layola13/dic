edgeways:/ˈɛdʒweɪz/| adv.|侧着，斜着，勉强地|In a sideways direction; with the narrowest part foremost.  It means something is inserted or moved in a way that its thin edge goes first, or that something is done with difficulty or by managing to find a small amount of time or space.|I managed to get a word in edgeways. 我好不容易插了一句嘴。|Synonyms: sideways, sidelong, aslant|Antonyms: straight, directly|adverb
