assistive:/əˈsɪstɪv/| adj.|辅助的，帮助的|designed or intended to help somebody who has a disability|例句：Assistive technology can help people with disabilities live more independently.  辅助技术可以帮助残疾人更独立地生活。

例句：She uses assistive devices to help her with daily tasks. 她使用辅助设备来帮助她完成日常任务。|近义词：helpful, supportive, aiding, auxiliary|反义词：hindering, obstructive|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From assist + -ive.  "Assist" comes from the Latin "assistere" (to stand by, attend to), from "ad-" (to, towards) + "sistere" (to stand).
