barricado:/ˌbærɪˈkeɪd/ /ˌbærɪˈkɑːdoʊ/| n. vt.|路障，障碍物；街垒|A defensive barrier hastily constructed, as in a street, to obstruct the advance of an enemy or opponent.|The protesters erected a barricade to block the street. 抗议者设置路障堵住了街道。|Synonyms: barrier, obstacle, blockade, obstruction|Antonyms: opening, passage, access|noun

barricado:/ˌbærɪˈkeɪd/ /ˌbærɪˈkɑːdoʊ/| vt.|设路障；阻挡|To obstruct or fortify with a barricade.|They barricaded the doors and windows to protect themselves from the storm.  他们用障碍物堵住门窗以保护自己免受暴风雨的侵袭。|Synonyms: block, obstruct, fortify, defend|Antonyms: open, clear, expose|verb
