cohead:/ˈkəʊˌhed/| n.|共同领导，共同负责人|A person who shares a position of leadership with another person. |The company appointed two coheads to oversee the merger. 公司任命了两位共同负责人来监督合并。|The project has two coheads, both experienced in their fields.  这个项目有两个共同负责人，都在各自的领域经验丰富。|synonyms: joint head, co-leader, co-director|antonyms: subordinate, assistant|noun
