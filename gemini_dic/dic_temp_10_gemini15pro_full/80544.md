goodlyhood: /ˈɡʊdliˌhʊd/ | n. |  好处，利益；生计，生活 | A state of prosperity or well-being.  A means of livelihood. |  He earned a goodlyhood by selling handcrafted goods. 他靠卖手工艺品为生。 | 近义词: livelihood, living, sustenance | 反义词: poverty, destitution | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Middle English *godlyhed, from Old English *gōdlīchad (“goodness, virtue, kindness”). Equivalent to goodly +‎ -hood.
