bairnly:/ˈbɛərnli/| adj.|孩子气的，幼稚的|Characteristic of or suitable for a child; childish.  Having a childlike innocence or simplicity.|She had a bairnly sweetness about her that was endearing. 她身上有一种孩子气的甜美，很讨人喜欢。|近义词：childish, childlike, innocent, naive|反义词：mature, sophisticated, grown-up|词性:形容词
词源: From Scots bairn (“child”) +‎ -ly.  Ultimately from Old English bearn (“child”).
