lipidic:/lɪˈpɪdɪk/
adjective
脂质的；类脂的
Relating to or resembling lipids.
The cell membrane has a lipidic bilayer. 细胞膜有一个脂质双层。
Adipose tissue is primarily lipidic in nature. 脂肪组织本质上主要是脂质的。
近义词:fatty, lipoid
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-lipidic could be used in specific contexts)
词性:adjective
