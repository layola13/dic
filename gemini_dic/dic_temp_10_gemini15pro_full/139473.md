obstinate:/ˈɒbstɪnət/ (UK) /ˈɑːbstɪnət/ (US)| adj.|固执的，顽固的|Unreasonably determined to have one's own way, not yielding to reason or persuasion.|He was obstinate in his refusal to apologize. 他固执地拒绝道歉。|The obstinate donkey refused to move.  这头倔驴拒绝移动。|stubborn, headstrong, inflexible, unyielding|compliant, amenable, docile|adjective
