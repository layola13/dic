experimented:/ɪkˈspɛrɪməntɪd/
verb
做过实验，进行过试验
past tense and past participle of "experiment" -  having carried out tests or investigations.
They experimented with different types of fertilizer to see which worked best. 他们试验了不同类型的肥料，看看哪一种效果最好。
The chef experimented with a new recipe.  这位厨师尝试了一种新的食谱。
近义词: tested, tried, explored
反义词: abstained, avoided
词性: verb
