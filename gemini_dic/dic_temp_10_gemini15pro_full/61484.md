embrew:/ɪmˈbruː/ | vt. | 浸染；使沾染；使充满 | to stain or dye deeply; to permeate or imbue |  The artist embrews the canvas with vibrant colors. 艺术家用鲜艳的色彩浸染画布。 |  The battlefield was embrewn with blood. 战场血流成河。 | 近义词: saturate, imbue, drench, steep | 反义词: cleanse, purify | 词性: 动词

词源:  From en- + brew (v.), influenced by imbrue.  "En-" acts as an intensifier, while "brew" originally referred to the process of steeping or infusing, often in a liquid.
