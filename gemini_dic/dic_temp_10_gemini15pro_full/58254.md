drumlinoid:/ˈdrʌmlɪnɔɪd/
adjective
鼓丘状的，似鼓丘的
Resembling or suggesting a drumlin in form.  A landform that looks like a drumlin but is smaller or less perfectly formed.
Example: The landscape was dotted with drumlinoid hills.  这片景观点缀着鼓丘状的小山。
Example: The researchers identified several drumlinoid features in the valley. 研究人员在山谷中发现了几处鼓丘状的地貌。
Synonym: drumlin-like, elliptical, oval
Antonym: irregular, shapeless
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From drumlin + -oid.  "Drumlin" comes from the Irish word "droimnín," meaning "little ridge."  "-oid" is a suffix meaning "resembling," "like," or "having the form of."
