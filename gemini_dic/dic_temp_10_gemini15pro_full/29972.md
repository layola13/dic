cavicorn:/ˈkævɪkɔːrn/| n.|空心角；洞角；中空角|A hollow horn, or a horn-shaped cavity.|The fossilized cavicorn of the extinct animal was discovered in a cave.  这头灭绝动物的空心角化石是在一个洞穴中发现的。

The bull lowered its massive cavicorn as it charged. 公牛在冲锋时 lowered its massive cavicorn. |近义词:horn, antler|反义词:solid horn (实心角) (This is a somewhat contrived antonym, as there isn't a direct opposite for a hollow horn.)|词性:名词
