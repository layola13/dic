noneliminative:/nɒnɪˈlɪmɪnətɪv/
adjective|非消除性的，非排斥性的|Not involving or based on the elimination or rejection of something; tending to retain or include rather than exclude.|Cognitive science takes a noneliminative approach to folk psychology, suggesting that everyday mental state concepts are not entirely wrong and can be refined rather than discarded. 认知科学对俗民心理学采取非消除性方法，认为日常心理状态概念并非完全错误，可以被改进而不是被抛弃。|The philosopher argued for a noneliminative materialist view of the mind.  这位哲学家主张一种非消除性的唯物主义的思想观点。|近义词:inclusive, integrative, retentive|反义词:eliminative, reductive|词性:形容词
