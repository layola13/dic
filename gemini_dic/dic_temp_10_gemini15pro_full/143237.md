outdazzled: /ˌaʊtˈdæz(ə)ld/
verb
使...更目眩，胜过...的炫目
to surpass in dazzling or splendor.
The fireworks display outdazzled all previous ones.  这场烟花表演胜过了所有以前的烟花表演。
The beauty of the sunset outdazzled the city lights. 日落的美丽胜过了城市的灯光。
近义词: outshine, eclipse, surpass
反义词:  pale in comparison to, be overshadowed by
词性: verb
