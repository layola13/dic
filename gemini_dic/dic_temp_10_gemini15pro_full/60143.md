effectualness:/ɪˈfɛktʃuəlnəs/
n.
有效性；效力
the quality of being effective or producing the intended result.
Her effectualness as a teacher is evident in her students' high test scores. 她作为一名教师的效力体现在她学生的高考试成绩上。
efficacy, effectiveness, potency
ineffectualness, inefficacy, impotence
noun
