nitwit:/ˈnɪtwɪt/| n. |笨蛋，傻瓜|A foolish or stupid person.|例句：Those nitwits locked their keys in the car. 那些笨蛋把钥匙锁在车里了。

例句：Don't be such a nitwit!  别这么傻！|近义词：idiot, fool, moron, imbecile, dolt|反义词：genius, intellectual|词性：名词

词源：
From nit +‎ wit.  Nit originally referred to the egg of a louse, hence something small and insignificant.  Combined with wit (intelligence), it implies someone with very little wit.
来源于 nit + wit。Nit 最初指的是虱子的卵，因此是指一些细小而无关紧要的东西。与 wit（智力）结合，它暗示着智力低下的人。
