overload:/ˌoʊvərˈloʊd/ | /ˌəʊvəˈləʊd/ | n. v. | 超载，过载；负担过重 | An overload of information can be overwhelming. 信息过载可能会让人不知所措. | The circuit overloaded and caused a power outage. 电路过载导致停电. | 近义词: overburden, overwhelm, excess | 反义词: underload | 词性: 名词，动词

overload:/ˌoʊvərˈloʊd/ | /ˌəʊvəˈləʊd/ | n. v. | 超负荷，过载；功能重载 |  Trying to do too many things at once can overload your system. 同时做太多事情会使你的系统超负荷. |  The function is overloaded to handle different data types.  该函数被重载以处理不同的数据类型. | 近义词: overburden, strain, tax | 反义词: underutilize | 词性: 名词，动词
