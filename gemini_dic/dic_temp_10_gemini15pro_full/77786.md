generalissimo:/ˌdʒɛnərəˈlɪsɪmoʊ/| n.|最高统帅，大元帅|A supreme commander of a combined military force, especially one who holds dictatorial powers. |例句：He was appointed generalissimo of the armed forces. 他被任命为武装部队的最高统帅。

例句：The generalissimo ruled the country with an iron fist.  这位大元帅用铁腕统治着这个国家。

近义词：commander-in-chief, supreme commander|反义词:subordinate, follower|词性:noun
