algae:/ˈældʒiː/ | n. | 水藻，藻类 |A simple, non-flowering, and typically aquatic plant of a large group that includes the seaweeds and many single-celled forms. Algae contain chlorophyll but lack true stems, roots, leaves, and vascular tissue. |  Example 1: The pond was covered in green algae. 池塘里覆盖着绿色的藻类。

Example 2:  Algae are an important part of the aquatic food chain. 藻类是水生食物链的重要组成部分。| 近义词: seaweed | 反义词: None | Noun
