antisyndicalist:/ˌæntiˈsɪndɪkəlɪst/
n.|反工团主义者；反对工会的人|Someone who is opposed to syndicalism.  Syndicalism is a political ideology that advocates for worker control of the means of production and the organization of society through trade unions.|The newspaper editorial denounced the politician as an antisyndicalist.|Synonyms: anti-unionist, anti-labor|Antonyms: syndicalist, unionist, labor activist|noun

antisyndicalist:/ˌæntiˈsɪndɪkəlɪst/
adj.|反工团主义的|Opposed to syndicalism.|His antisyndicalist views made him unpopular with the workers.|Synonyms: anti-union, anti-labor|Antonyms: syndicalist, pro-union, pro-labor|adjective
