digitalis:/ˌdɪdʒɪˈteɪlɪs/| n. |洋地黄，毛地黄（提取物可做强心剂）|A drug prepared from the dried leaves of foxglove and containing substances (especially digitoxin and digoxin) that stimulate the heart muscle. It is used in the treatment of certain heart conditions.|The doctor prescribed digitalis for his heart condition.|近义词：foxglove|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology:
From Latin *digitalis*, from *digitus* ("finger, toe"). So called from the finger-like shape of the flowers.
