Nationalrat: /ˌnatsi̯oˈnaːlˌʁaːt/ | n. |国民议会（奥地利）| The lower house of the Austrian parliament.  It is the more powerful of the two chambers and is responsible for passing legislation. | Austria's Nationalrat passed a new budget yesterday. 昨天奥地利国民议会通过了一项新的预算。|Synonyms: lower house, chamber of deputies |Antonyms: upper house, senate | noun
