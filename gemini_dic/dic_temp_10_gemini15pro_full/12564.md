aspirement:/əˈspaɪərmənt/ | n. |抱负，渴望，志向 | A strong desire to achieve something high or great. |  Example 1: Her aspiration is to become a doctor. 她渴望成为一名医生。

Example 2: He has high aspirations for his children. 他对他的孩子们寄予厚望。 | Synonyms: ambition, goal, dream, desire, objective | Antonyms: apathy, indifference, lack of ambition | noun
