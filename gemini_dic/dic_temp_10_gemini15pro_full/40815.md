convolvuli:/ˌkɒnvəˈvjuːlaɪ/ | n. |旋花属植物；牵牛花 |The Convolvuli are a genus of about 200 to 250 species of flowering plants in the bindweed family Convolvulaceae, commonly known as bindweeds or morning glories.|  The garden was filled with colorful convolvuli. 花园里开满了五颜六色的旋花。 |Synonyms: bindweed, morning glory | Antonyms: None | Noun

convolvulus:/kənˈvɒlvjʊləs/ | n. |旋花属植物；牵牛花（单数） | A convolvulus is a climbing plant with trumpet-shaped flowers. | The convolvulus climbed up the trellis. 牵牛花爬上了棚架。| Synonyms: bindweed, morning glory | Antonyms: None | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *convolvulus*, from *convolvere* "to roll together, roll up," from *com-* "together" (see com-) + *volvere* "to roll" (see voluble).
