leerest:/ˈlɪərɪst/| adj.| 最斜视的; 眼神最淫邪的 |  archaic superlative of leer | He gave her the leerest look he could muster. 他向她投去他能做出的最淫邪的目光。 | 近义词: most leering, most lascivious | 反义词: chaste, modest | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Middle English *leren, from Old English lēran (“to teach, instruct, advise, counsel; learn”), from Proto-Germanic *laizijaną (“to teach”), from Proto-Indo-European *lois- (“furrow, track”).  The sense development is from "to look sideways" to "to look or glance obliquely or slyly." The superlative suffix "-est" is a standard English inflection.
