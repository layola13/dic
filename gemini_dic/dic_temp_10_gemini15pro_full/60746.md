electrodeposition:/ɪˌlɛktrəʊˌdɛpəˈzɪʃən/ | n. |电沉积，电镀| The process of depositing a substance on an electrode by electrolysis.  It involves the reduction of metal ions from a solution onto a conductive surface (the cathode) by applying an electric current. |Example 1: Electrodeposition is commonly used to create a protective coating on metal parts. 电沉积通常用于在金属部件上形成保护涂层。

Example 2: The thickness of the coating can be controlled precisely during electrodeposition.  在电沉积过程中，涂层的厚度可以被精确控制。| 近义词: electroplating, galvanization, electrochemical deposition | 反义词: electropolishing | 词性: 名词
