overimpressionability:/ˌoʊvərɪmˌprɛʃənəˈbɪləti/
n.
过度印象, 过于敏感
The state of being easily influenced or overly affected by impressions or suggestions.
Her overimpressionability made her susceptible to various scams.  她过度敏感使她容易受到各种骗局的影响。
He displayed overimpressionability in his youth, believing everything he read. 他年轻时表现出过度敏感，相信他读到的一切。
近义词:suggestibility, sensitivity, impressionability
反义词:imperviousness, resistance, insensitivity
词性:noun

Etymology:
From over- + impressionability.  "Over-" indicates an excess or too much, and "impressionability" refers to the quality of being easily impressed or influenced.
