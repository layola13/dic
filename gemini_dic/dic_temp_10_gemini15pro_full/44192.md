例句：The singer's croakinesses worsened as the tour went on. 歌手的嘶哑声随着巡演的进行而加重。

例句：He experienced recurring croakinesses throughout the winter. 他整个冬天都反复出现嘶哑的声音。

近义词：hoarseness, raspiness, roughness|反义词:smoothness, clarity|词性:noun
