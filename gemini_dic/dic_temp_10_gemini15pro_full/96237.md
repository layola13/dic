indicant:/ˈɪndɪkənt/ | n. | 指示物，标志，迹象 | Something that serves to indicate or point out; a sign or symptom. |  A fever is often an indicant of infection. 发烧通常是感染的迹象。| synonyms: indicator, sign, symptom, mark, signal | antonyms:  concealment,  camouflage | noun

indicant:/ˈɪndɪkənt/ | adj. | 指示的，表明的 | Serving to indicate; indicative. |  His flushed face was indicant of his embarrassment. 他涨红的脸表明他很尴尬。 | synonyms: indicative, suggestive, symptomatic | antonyms: concealing, masking | adjective
