indemnifies:/ɪnˈdɛmnɪfaɪz/
verb
赔偿；保障
compensate (someone) for harm or loss.
The insurance company indemnifies its clients against fire and theft.  这家保险公司为其客户提供火灾和盗窃的赔偿。
compensate, reimburse, repay, recompense
charge, fine, penalize
v.
