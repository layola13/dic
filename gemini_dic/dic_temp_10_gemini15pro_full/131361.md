nonchronically: /nɒnˈkrɒnɪkli/ | adv. | 非慢性地 | Not chronically; not happening over a long period of time.  | The pain was nonchronically present, only appearing after strenuous exercise. 疼痛并非慢性存在，只在剧烈运动后出现。|  The issue was addressed nonchronically, with a quick and effective solution.  这个问题得到了迅速有效的解决，而不是长期拖延。 | 近义词: acutely, briefly, temporarily | 反义词: chronically, persistently, long-term | 词性: 副词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "chronically" (from Greek "khronos" meaning "time").
