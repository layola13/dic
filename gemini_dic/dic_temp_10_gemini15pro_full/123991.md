morning:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmɔːrnɪŋ/ (US)| n.|早晨，上午|The period of time between midnight and noon, especially from sunrise to noon. |I woke up early this morning. 我今天早上醒得很早。|近义词:dawn, sunrise, forenoon|反义词:evening, night|词性:noun

morning:/ˈmɔːrnɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmɔːrnɪŋ/ (US)| int.|早安|Used as a greeting.|Good morning, everyone! 大家早安！|近义词:hi, hello|反义词:good night, good evening|词性:interjection
