horometrical: /ˌhɔːrəˈmɛtrɪk(ə)l/
adjective
测时的，时间测量的
Relating to the measurement of time.
This horometrical device can measure time with extreme precision.  这个测时装置可以极其精确地测量时间。
The horometrical data collected by the scientists helped them understand the phenomenon. 科学家收集的测时数据帮助他们理解了这一现象。
近义词: chronometric
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective

词源:
From Greek *hōra* "hour, time" + *-metros* "measurer," from *metron* "measure."
