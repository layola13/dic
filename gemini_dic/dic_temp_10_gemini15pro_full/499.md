abide by:/əˈbaɪd baɪ/| v.|遵守，遵循|To accept or follow a decision, rule, or agreement.|Both parties agreed to abide by the judge's decision. 双方都同意遵守法官的裁决。

The company must abide by the regulations set forth by the government.  公司必须遵守政府制定的规章制度。|近义词：comply with, adhere to, observe, follow, respect|反义词：disobey, violate, breach, ignore|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *abiden bi*, from Old English *ābīdan bī* ("to remain by, await by"), equivalent to *abide* + *by*.  The sense shifted from "waiting near something" to "staying with an agreement or decision".

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He abides by the rules of the game. = 他遵守游戏规则。
She always abides by her promises. = 她总是信守诺言。

一般过去时:
They abided by the terms of the contract. = 他们遵守了合同条款。
We abided by the decision of the court. = 我们遵守了法院的判决。

现在完成时:
The company has always abided by the law. = 这家公司一直遵守法律。
He has abided by the agreement since it was signed. = 自从协议签署以来，他一直遵守该协议。

过去完成时:
They had abided by the treaty for many years before the war broke out. = 在战争爆发前，他们遵守该条约已有多年。
She had abided by her parents' wishes until she turned 18. = 她一直遵守父母的意愿，直到18岁。

将来时:
We will abide by the results of the election. = 我们将遵守选举结果。
I will abide by your decision. = 我将遵从你的决定。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would abide by the doctor's orders. = 如果我是你，我会遵守医生的嘱咐。
I wish they would abide by the agreement. = 我希望他们能遵守协议。

被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
