papaphobia:/ˌpeɪpəˈfoʊbiə/ | n. | 教皇恐惧症；罗马天主教恐惧症 | An irrational and persistent fear of the Pope.  It is a specific phobia, characterized by excessive anxiety and avoidance of anything related to the Pope or the Catholic Church. | Example: His papaphobia prevented him from visiting Vatican City. 他的教皇恐惧症使他无法参观梵蒂冈城。
Example:  She experiences intense anxiety even thinking about seeing the Pope on television, a clear sign of papaphobia.  即使想到在电视上看到教皇，她也会感到极度焦虑，这显然是教皇恐惧症的迹象。 | 近义词: ecclesiophobia (对教堂的恐惧), hagiophobia (对圣人或神圣事物的恐惧) | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
