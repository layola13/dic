misplays:/ˌmɪsˈpleɪz/
|n.|失误，错误的打法|An instance of playing something badly or making a wrong move, especially in a game or sport. |He lost the game due to a series of misplays in the final set. 他在最后一局因为一系列的失误输掉了比赛。|Synonyms: blunders, errors, mistakes, goofs|Antonyms: masterstrokes, good plays, clever moves|Noun
misplay:/ˌmɪsˈpleɪ/
|v.|玩得不好，打错|To play something badly or make a wrong move, especially in a game or sport.|She misplayed the ball and it went out of bounds. 她打错了球，球出界了。|Synonyms: blunder, err, fumble, goof|Antonyms: excel, succeed, master|Verb
