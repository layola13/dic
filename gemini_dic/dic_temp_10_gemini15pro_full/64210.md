equifinality:/ˌiːkwɪfaɪˈnælɪti/ | n. | 均终性，等终性 | The principle that in open systems, a given end state can be reached by many potential means. | Many different paths can lead to the same outcome, demonstrating equifinality. 许多不同的路径可以导致相同的结果，这证明了均终性。 | The company reached its sales target through equifinality, utilizing both online and offline marketing strategies. 公司通过均终性达到了销售目标，同时利用了线上和线下营销策略。 | synonyms: convergence, multifinality (in some contexts, referring to the opposite concept) | antonyms: unilinearity, single pathway | noun
