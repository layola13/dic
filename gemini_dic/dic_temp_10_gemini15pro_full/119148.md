mew's:/mjuːz/
noun
喵喵声; 小猫的叫声
The soft mews of the kittens were barely audible. 小猫的轻柔喵喵声几乎听不见。
English Explanation: The plural form of "mew," referring to the characteristic crying sound made by a cat, especially a kitten.  It can also refer to the act of mewing itself.
Example: The mew's of the abandoned kittens were heartbreaking. 被遗弃的小猫的喵喵叫声令人心碎。
Synonyms: meows, cries, whimpers
Antonyms: roars, barks, growls
Part of Speech: Noun
