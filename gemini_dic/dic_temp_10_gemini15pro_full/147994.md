overridingly:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪdɪŋli/
adv.
压倒一切地，最重要的是
in a way that overrides everything else; predominantly.
Her desire to help others was overridingly strong. 她帮助他人的愿望非常强烈。
Synonyms: predominantly, primarily, chiefly, mainly, principally, above all, first and foremost
Antonyms: subordinately, secondarily
词性: 副词
