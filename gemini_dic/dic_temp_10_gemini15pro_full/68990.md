Fatimid: /ˈfætɪmɪd/ | adj. & n. | 法蒂玛王朝的；法蒂玛王朝的人 | Relating to the Fatimid caliphate, a Shiite Muslim dynasty that ruled over various parts of North Africa, Egypt, and the Levant from the 10th to the 12th centuries. | The Fatimid caliphate was known for its patronage of the arts and sciences. 法蒂玛王朝以其对艺术和科学的赞助而闻名。|  He studied Fatimid architecture. 他研究法蒂玛王朝的建筑。 | 近义词: Ismaili (related, but not synonymous - Ismaili refers to the broader religious sect)  | 反义词:  (No direct antonym; could consider Abbasid, a rival caliphate) | 词性: adjective, noun
