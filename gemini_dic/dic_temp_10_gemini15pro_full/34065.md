civil: /ˈsɪvl/ | adj. | 国民的，公民的；民事的；文明的，有礼貌的 | relating to the ordinary people of a country, rather than to the armed forces or the Church; connected with the legal matters that are not criminal; polite and formal |  The case will be heard in a civil court. 这个案件将在民事法庭审理。 | Synonyms: civic, domestic, polite, courteous | Antonyms: military, religious, rude, impolite | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *civile*, borrowed from Old French *civil*, from Latin *cīvīlis* ("relating to a citizen, relating to the state, courteous, affable, popular"), derived from *cīvis* ("citizen").
