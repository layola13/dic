comply:/kəmˈplaɪ/| v. |遵守，遵从，依从|act in accordance with a wish or command.|例句：The company complied with the regulations. 这家公司遵守了规定。|例句：We are unable to comply with your request at this time. 我们目前无法满足您的要求。|近义词：obey, conform, adhere, submit|反义词：disobey, defy, resist|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *complir*, from Latin complēre (“to fulfill”), from com- (“together”) + plēre (“to fill”).
