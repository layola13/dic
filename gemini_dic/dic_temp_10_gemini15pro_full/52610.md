digestibleness:/daɪˌdʒɛstəˈbɪlnəs/ /daɪˌdʒɛstɪˈbɪlnəs/| n. |易消化性；消化率|The digestibleness of food is an important factor in nutrition.  食物的易消化性是营养学中的一个重要因素。|The digestibleness of the new baby formula was clinically tested.  这种新型婴儿配方奶粉的消化率经过了临床测试。|近义词：digestibility|反义词：indigestibility|词性:noun

Etymology: From *digestible* + *-ness*. *Digestible* comes from Late Middle English *digestible*, from Late Latin *digestibilis*, from Latin *digerere* "to separate, distribute, digest," from *dis-* "apart" + *gerere* "to carry, bear."  *-ness* is a native English suffix forming nouns from adjectives, meaning "state or quality of being."
