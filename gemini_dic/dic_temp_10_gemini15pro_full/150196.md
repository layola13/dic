pancosmic:/pænˈkɒzmɪk/| adj.|泛宇宙的，遍及宇宙的|Relating to the entire cosmos; universal in scope.|例句：The pancosmic consciousness is a concept that suggests interconnectedness of all things in the universe. 泛宇宙意识是一个表明宇宙万物相互关联的概念。

例句：Her pancosmic vision encompassed everything from the smallest particle to the largest galaxy. 她的泛宇宙视野涵盖了从最小粒子到最大星系的一切。|近义词：universal, cosmic, global|反义词：limited, restricted|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From pan- + cosmic.  Pan- is a combining form meaning "all," "of everything," or "involving all members of a group," ultimately from Greek *pan*, neuter of *pas* "all." Cosmic relates to the cosmos or universe, from Greek *kosmikos*, from *kosmos* "order, the world."
