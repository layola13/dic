drive:/draɪv/| n. v.|驾驶，驱动，车道; 驱使，迫使|A drive is a trip in a car.  His ambition drives him to work hard. The long driveway led to a grand house.|She went for a drive in the country. 他去乡下开车兜风。|近义词：operate, steer, propel, force, compel|反义词:ride, follow, hinder|词性:noun, verb
Etymology: From Old English *drīfan (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *dreibaną (“to drive, push, move”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰreibʰ- (“to drive, push, shove, thrust”).
