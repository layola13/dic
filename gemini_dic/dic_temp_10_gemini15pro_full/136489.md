nonrioters:/ˌnɒnˈraɪətərz/ | n. | 非暴乱者，未参与暴乱的人 | People who are not involved in a riot; those who remain peaceful and orderly. |  The police worked to separate the nonrioters from the aggressive crowd. 警方努力将非暴乱者与激进的人群分开。 | The nonrioters watched the chaos unfold from a safe distance. 非暴乱者从安全距离观看混乱的场面。 | 近义词: peaceful protesters, bystanders, onlookers | 反义词: rioters, insurgents | 词性: 名词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin *non < PIE *ne "not".
riot (noun): From Old French riote "dispute, quarrel, uproar," from Gallo-Romance *riota, of uncertain origin.
-er (suffix): Forms agent nouns, from Old English -ere.
