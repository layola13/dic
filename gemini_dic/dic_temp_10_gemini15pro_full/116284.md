masseurs:/mæˈsɜːrz/ /məˈsɜːz/
n.
按摩师（尤指男性）
People who give massages, especially men.
The masseurs at the spa were highly skilled.  这家温泉浴场的按摩师技术娴熟。
近义词：massage therapists, masseuses
反义词: None (No direct opposite for a profession)
词性: Noun (Plural)

masseurs:/mæˈsɜːrz/ /məˈsɜːz/
Etymology: From French masseur, from masser "to massage," from Arabic massa "to touch, to handle."
