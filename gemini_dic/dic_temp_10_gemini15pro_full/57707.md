dray:/dreɪ/
v.|搬运，拖，拉|To transport (goods) by dray; haul.|The farmer drayed his harvest to the market. 农民用货车把收成运到市场。|近义词：haul, cart, transport|反义词:N/A
词性：动词

dray:/dreɪ/
n.|货车，运货马车|A low, strong cart for heavy loads.|The dray was pulled by two large horses. 这辆货车由两匹大马拉着。|近义词：cart, wagon|反义词:N/A
词性：名词

词源: From Old English *dræge  "act of drawing, dragging,"  from Proto-Germanic *draganą (source also of Old Saxon dragan, Old Frisian draga, Middle Dutch and Dutch dragen, Old High German tragan, German tragen "to carry," Old Norse draga "to draw, pull, drag," Gothic dragan "to draw, carry"). The noun is from the verb.
