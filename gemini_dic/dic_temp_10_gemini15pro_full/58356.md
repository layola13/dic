dua:/ˈduːɑː/| n.|祈祷，恳求，祝福|A dua is a prayer or invocation, especially one made by Muslims.  It is a personal supplication to God, expressing gratitude, seeking guidance, or asking for help.|Example: He offered a dua for the safety of his family. 他为家人的安全祈祷。

Example: Her dua was answered when she finally found a job. 当她终于找到工作时，她的祈祷应验了。|Synonym: prayer, supplication, invocation|Antonym: curse, malediction|noun
