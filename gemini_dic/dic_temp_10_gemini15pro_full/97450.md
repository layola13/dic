infusibleness:/ɪnˈfjuːzɪbəlnəs/| n. |不可熔性；不易动摇；顽固 |The quality of being infusible; inability to be melted or liquefied; also, figuratively, unyieldingness or stubbornness.|The infusibleness of his opinions made it difficult to reason with him. 他观点的顽固性使得很难和他讲道理。|Near synonyms:  inresistance, refractoriness, obstinacy, pertinacity|Near antonyms: fusibility, malleability, flexibility, fluidity|noun
