megalith: /ˈmɛɡəlɪθ/ | n. | 巨石; 巨石建造物 | A large prehistoric stone, often used in the construction of monuments or tombs. |  Example 1: Stonehenge is a famous example of a megalithic structure. 巨石阵是巨石建筑的著名例子。

Example 2:  Many megaliths are found in Western Europe. 许多巨石在西欧被发现。 | Synonym: monolith, giant stone | Antonym: pebble, small stone | noun
