heredities: /həˈrɛdɪtiz/
| n. |遗传特征，遗传性|The sum of the qualities and potentialities genetically derived from one's ancestors.|Heredities can influence a person's susceptibility to certain diseases. 遗传特征会影响一个人对某些疾病的易感性。|Synonyms: inheritance, genetics, ancestry|Antonyms: acquired characteristics|noun
