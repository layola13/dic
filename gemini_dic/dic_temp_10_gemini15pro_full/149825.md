paleontographical:/ˌpeɪliˌɒntəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
古生物学文献的；古生物学著作的
Relating to the description of fossils.
The paleontographical society publishes monographs on fossils.  古生物学会出版关于化石的专著。
The museum library has a vast collection of paleontographical literature.  博物馆图书馆收藏了大量的古生物文献。
近义词: paleontological
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, perhaps "non-paleontographical")
词性: adjective
