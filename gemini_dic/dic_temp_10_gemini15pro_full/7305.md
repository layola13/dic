anguish:/ˈæŋɡwɪʃ/| n. |极度痛苦，悲痛|Extreme mental or physical suffering.|She cried out in anguish when she heard the news. 她听到这个消息后痛苦地哭了起来。|近义词：agony, torment, distress, pain, suffering|反义词：joy, happiness, comfort, relief|noun

anguish:/ˈæŋɡwɪʃ/| v. |使极度痛苦，感到悲痛|To cause or feel extreme mental or physical suffering.|He was anguished by the thought of losing her.  想到失去她，他感到非常痛苦。|近义词：grieve, suffer, agonize|反义词：rejoice, celebrate|verb
