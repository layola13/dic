gynandrous:/ɡaɪˈnændrəs/ | adj. |雌雄同体的，具有两性特征的 | Having both male and female sexual characteristics in the same individual; hermaphrodite. | Example 1: Some species of fish are gynandrous, possessing both ovaries and testes.  一些鱼类是雌雄同体的，同时拥有卵巢和睾丸。

Example 2:  The gynandrous flower exhibited both stamens and pistils. 这朵雌雄同体的花同时展现了雄蕊和雌蕊。| Synonym: hermaphroditic, bisexual, monoecious | Antonym: dioecious | Word type: Adjective

Etymology:  From Greek *gynē* (woman) + *anēr*, *andros* (man)
