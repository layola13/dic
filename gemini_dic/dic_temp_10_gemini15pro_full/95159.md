inarticulately:/ˌɪnɑːrˈtɪkjələtli/ | adv. |  表达不清地，不善言辞地 | Not able to express oneself clearly or fluently. |  He mumbled inarticulately, his words barely audible. 他含糊不清地咕哝着，几乎听不见他的话。| 近义词: unclearly, indistinctly, unintelligibly | 反义词: articulately, clearly, eloquently | 词性: 副词

inarticulate:/ˌɪnɑːrˈtɪkjələt/ | adj. |  表达不清的，不善言辞的 |  Not able to express oneself clearly or fluently. |  She was so shocked by the news that she became inarticulate. 她被这个消息震惊得说不出话来。| 近义词: speechless, tongue-tied, dumb | 反义词: articulate, eloquent, fluent | 词性: 形容词
