clearnesses:/ˈklɪərnɪsɪz/
noun
清晰度；清楚；明晰
The state or quality of being clear.
The clearness of the water allowed us to see the bottom of the lake.  湖水的清澈使我们能够看到湖底。
clarity, lucidity, transparency, limpidity
opacity, murkiness, cloudiness, vagueness
