It refers to a state of reckless disorder and chaotic behavior. |  His harum-scarumness in handling the project resulted in a complete mess. 他处理这个项目时的鲁莽导致一团糟。 |  recklessness, impulsiveness, irresponsibility, carelessness, folly | prudence, caution, carefulness, deliberation | noun
