overburden:/ˌoʊvərˈbɜːrdən/| v. n.|负担过重，过载；覆盖层|To load with too great a burden; overload.  The soil and rock overlying a mineral deposit.|The manager was overburdened with paperwork. 经理被文书工作压得喘不过气来。|Synonyms: overload, overtax, overwhelm|Antonyms: underload, lighten|verb

overburden:/ˌoʊvərˈbɜːrdən/| n.|负担，重负；覆盖层|Something that burdens excessively; an excessive load.  The soil and rock overlying a mineral deposit.|The overburden of paperwork prevented him from taking a vacation.  过多的文书工作使他无法休假。|Synonyms: load, burden, weight|Antonyms: relief, ease|noun
