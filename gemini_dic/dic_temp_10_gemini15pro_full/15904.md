词源:  The term "ballpark" originated in the late 19th century, referring literally to a field for playing ball, especially baseball.  “ballpark”一词起源于19世纪后期，字面意思是打球的场地，尤指棒球。 The figurative use, meaning "a general range or approximation," arose in the mid-20th century. 比喻用法，意思是“大致范围或近似值”，出现在20世纪中期。
