deniability:/dɪˌnaɪəˈbɪləti/
n.
可否认性，否认的可能性
The ability to deny involvement in or knowledge of something, especially something reprehensible.
The CIA director insisted on plausible deniability in any covert operations. 中央情报局局长坚持任何秘密行动都要有可信的推诿责任的余地。

例句：
The government sought to maintain plausible deniability in the affair. 政府试图在此事件中保持可信的推诿责任。
He insisted on deniability, so he left no written record of the plan. 他坚持要推脱责任，所以他没有留下任何书面的计划记录。

近义词：disavowal, repudiation, disclaimer
反义词：accountability, responsibility
词性: 名词
