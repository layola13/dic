lugubrious:/ləˈɡuːbriəs/ | adj. | 悲哀的，忧郁的 | Looking or sounding sad and dismal. |  He had a lugubrious expression on his face. 他脸上带着忧郁的表情。

The lugubrious weather matched his mood. 阴沉的天气与他的心情相符。| 近义词：mournful, gloomy, melancholy, somber | 反义词：cheerful, happy, joyful | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *lūgubris* (“mournful, doleful”), from *lūgēre* (“to mourn”).
