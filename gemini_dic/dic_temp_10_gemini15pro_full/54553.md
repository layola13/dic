dislocatedly:/dɪsˈlōˌkātidli/
adv.
脱臼地，错位地
in a dislocated manner; out of its proper or usual place or relationship.
His shoulder was dislocatedly hanging, a testament to the brutal attack. 他的肩膀脱臼地垂着，证明了那次残酷的袭击。
The narrative jumped dislocatedly between past and present, confusing the reader.  叙述在过去和现在之间错乱地跳跃，使读者感到困惑。
近义词: out of place, disorderedly, incoherently
反义词: orderly, coherently, connectedly
词性: 副词

词源:  From dislocate + -ed + -ly.  Dislocate comes from the Latin dis- (apart) + locare (to place).
