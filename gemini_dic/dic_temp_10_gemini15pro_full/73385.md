foreheadless:/fɔːrˈhedlɪs/| adj.|无头的，没有领导的|Lacking a head; lacking intelligent direction or leadership.  Origin: 'fore-' (front) + 'head' + '-less' (without).  The literal meaning refers to the physical absence of a head, while the figurative meaning implies a lack of leadership or direction.|The foreheadless horseman rode through the night. (literal) 无头的骑士在夜里骑行。 The company is currently foreheadless and adrift. (figurative)  这家公司目前群龙无首，处于漂泊状态。|近义词：headless, leaderless, rudderless|反义词:headed, led|词性:形容词
