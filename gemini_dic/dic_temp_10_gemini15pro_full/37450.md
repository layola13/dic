commiserators:/kəˈmɪzəˌreɪtərz/
|n.|慰问者，表示同情的人|People who express sympathy or sorrow to someone else, often sharing in their grief or disappointment.|The commiserators patted her on the back and offered words of comfort. 慰问者拍了拍她的背，并给予安慰。|
| |The losing team was surrounded by commiserators.  失败的队伍被慰问者包围着。|
