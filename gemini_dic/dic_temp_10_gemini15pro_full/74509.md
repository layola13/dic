frappeing:/fræˈpiːɪŋ/
adjective
令人震惊的; 令人不安的
striking; causing anxiety or distress
例句：The news of the accident was frappeing.  事故的消息令人震惊。

例句：Her sudden disappearance was a frappeing mystery. 她突然失踪是一个令人不安的谜团。

近义词: alarming, disturbing, shocking, startling, upsetting
反义词: calming, reassuring, comforting, soothing
词性: adjective


frappe:/fræp/
noun
冰咖啡
a drink made of iced coffee, often flavored with syrup
例句：I ordered a caramel frappe. 我点了一杯焦糖冰咖啡。

例句：The cafe is known for its delicious frappes. 这家咖啡馆以其美味的冰咖啡而闻名。

近义词: iced coffee, blended coffee drink
反义词: hot coffee
词性: noun


frappe:/fræp/
verb
用冰镇
to chill (a drink)
例句：Please frappe this bottle of champagne. 请把这瓶香槟冰镇一下。

例句：He frapped the wine before serving it. 他在上酒之前把酒冰镇了一下。

近义词: chill, ice
反义词: heat, warm
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *frapper*, "to strike, chill". Ultimately from Frankish *hrappan*. Related to English "rap".
