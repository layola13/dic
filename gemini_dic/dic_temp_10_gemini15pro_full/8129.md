anti-Birmingham:/ˌæntiˈbɜːrmɪŋəm/ | adj. | 反伯明翰的，反对伯明翰的 | Opposed to the policies or actions of the city of Birmingham, or its associated institutions or people. |  The anti-Birmingham sentiment was strong among some groups. 一些团体中反伯明翰的情绪很强烈。 | Example: The anti-Birmingham protesters gathered outside the council house.  反伯明翰的抗议者聚集在市政厅外。 | Near synonyms: opposed to Birmingham, against Birmingham | Antonyms: pro-Birmingham, supporting Birmingham | adjective

Etymology:
"anti-" (prefix): from Greek "anti" meaning "against, opposite of"
"Birmingham":  the name of the city in England
