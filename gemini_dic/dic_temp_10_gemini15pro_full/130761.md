nonamphibiousness:/ˌnɒnæmˈfɪbiəsnəs/
noun
非两栖性；非水陆两栖性
The state or quality of not being amphibious; inability to live both on land and in water.
The turtle's nonamphibiousness restricted it to the pond.  这只乌龟的非两栖性使它只能待在池塘里。
non-adaptability to land and water, terrestrial nature
