grade: /ɡreɪd/ | vt.| 评分；分级；评估；渐渐变化 | to arrange in or assign to grades; to evaluate | The teacher graded the essays. 老师给作文评分。 | synonyms: assess, evaluate, rank, categorize | antonyms: jumble, mix | verb
