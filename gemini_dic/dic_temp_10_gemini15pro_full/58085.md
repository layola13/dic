droopingly:/ˈdruːpɪŋli/ | adv. | 垂头丧气地，无精打采地 | in a drooping manner; with a drooping posture or appearance.  It describes something hanging down limply or sadly. | The flowers drooped droopingly in the intense heat.  花儿在酷热中无精打采地垂着。| The dog walked droopingly after being scolded.  小狗被骂后无精打采地走着。|Synonyms: dejectedly, listlessly, sadly, limply | Antonyms: perkily, energetically, uprightly | word property: adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From droop + -ing + -ly. Droop comes from Old Norse *drupa, meaning "to droop."
