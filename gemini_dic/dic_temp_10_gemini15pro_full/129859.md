non-indictment:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈdaɪtmənt/ | n. |不起诉；不予起诉|A formal decision by a grand jury not to bring charges against someone accused of a crime.| The grand jury returned a non-indictment, effectively ending the case. 大陪审团做出了不起诉的决定，案件就此了结。|  The prosecutor announced the non-indictment of the police officer. 检察官宣布不起诉该警官。 | synonyms: acquittal, discharge, exoneration | antonyms: indictment, charge, prosecution | noun
