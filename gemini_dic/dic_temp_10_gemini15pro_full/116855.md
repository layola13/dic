maximized:/ˈmæksɪmaɪzd/

maximized:/ˈmæksɪmaɪzd/
adjective|最大化的,最佳化的|having been increased to the greatest possible amount or degree. |例句：The software is designed for maximized efficiency.  该软件旨在实现效率最大化。|近义词：greatest, highest, utmost|反义词: minimized, least|adj.
