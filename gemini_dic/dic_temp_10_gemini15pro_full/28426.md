carbonizers:/ˈkɑːrbənaɪzərz/
n.
碳化器；炭化装置
A device or substance that carbonizes something.
The carbonizers used in the factory efficiently convert organic matter into charcoal.  工厂里使用的碳化器能有效地将有机物转化为木炭。

The wood is heated in carbonizers to produce charcoal. 木材在碳化器中加热以生产木炭。

Synonyms: charcoal burners, carbonizing units, charring ovens
Antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym for a device)
词性: noun
