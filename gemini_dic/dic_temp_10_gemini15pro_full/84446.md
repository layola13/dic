halock:/ˈheɪlɒk/ | n. | 干草堆，草垛 | A large pile of hay, straw, or similar material, especially one built outdoors for storing. |  The farmer stored the hay in a large halock for the winter. 农民把干草堆成一个大草垛过冬。| synonym: haystack, rick, stack | antonym: N/A | noun
