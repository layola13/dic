cancelli:/kænˈtʃɛli/ | n. | lattices, gratings, railings (plural of cancello) | A structure of crossed wooden or metal strips usually forming a barrier or guard.  It is the plural form of the Italian word "cancello." | The cancelli of the balcony were intricately designed.  阳台的栏杆设计复杂精巧。 | Synonyms: railings, bars, grilles, latticework | Antonyms: opening, gap | Noun
