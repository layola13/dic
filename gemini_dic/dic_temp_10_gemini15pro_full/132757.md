nondominants:/nɒnˈdɒmɪnənts/| adj. n.|非优势的，非支配的；非优势者，非支配者|Not dominant; lacking in dominance or control.  Can refer to either a characteristic (like a nondominant hand) or a group/individual in a subordinate position (though this usage is less common).|例句：He writes with his nondominant hand. 他用他的非惯用手写字。

|近义词：subordinate, secondary, recessive|反义词:dominant, superior, controlling|词性:adjective, noun


nondominant:/nɒnˈdɒmɪnənt/| adj. |非优势的，非支配的|Not dominant; lacking in dominance or control. Refers to a characteristic, like a nondominant hand or eye.|例句：Her nondominant eye is her left eye. 她的非优势眼是左眼。
|近义词：subordinate, secondary, recessive|反义词:dominant, superior, controlling|词性:adjective


nondominant:/nɒnˈdɒmɪnənt/| n. |非优势者，非支配者|A person or group that is not dominant or in control. This usage is less common than the adjectival form.|例句：The nondominants in the society often face discrimination. 社会中的非支配群体经常面临歧视。
|近义词：subordinate, underdog|反义词:leader, dominant, ruling class|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (meaning "not") + dominant (from Latin dominans, present participle of dominari "to rule, control").
