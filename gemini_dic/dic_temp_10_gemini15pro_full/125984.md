musketries: /ˈmʌskɪtriz/
n.
火枪射击，火枪齐射; musketry fire
The sound of musketries filled the air.  空中充满了火枪射击的声音。
The musketries of the enemy were relentless. 敌人的火枪齐射毫不留情。
近义词: gunfire, volley, fusillade
反义词: silence, peace


词源:
From French *mousqueterie*, from mousquet 'musket'.
