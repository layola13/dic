hedonic:/hɪˈdɑːnɪk/ /hiːˈdɒnɪk/| adj.| 享乐的，快乐的，愉悦的|Of, relating to, or characterized by pleasure.  Concerned with or seeking pleasure.|例句：Hedonic consumption is driven by the pursuit of pleasure. 享乐型消费是由追求快乐驱动的。|例句：The spa offers a range of hedonic treatments.  这家水疗中心提供一系列享乐疗法。|近义词：pleasurable, enjoyable, delightful|反义词：unpleasant, displeasing, ascetic|词性:形容词

hedonics:/hɪˈdɑːnɪks/ /hiːˈdɒnɪks/| n.| 享乐主义|The ethical theory that pleasure (in the sense of the satisfaction of desires) is the highest good and proper aim of human life.  The study of pleasure and happiness.|例句：Hedonics is a branch of psychology that studies pleasure and well-being. 享乐主义是心理学的一个分支，研究快乐和幸福。|例句：The company's marketing strategy focuses on the hedonics of their products. 该公司的营销策略侧重于其产品的享乐性。|近义词：pleasure-seeking, epicureanism|反义词：asceticism, self-denial|词性:名词
