arctic:/ˈɑːrktɪk/ (US) /ˈɑːktɪk/ (UK)| adj. n.|北极的，极寒的；北极地区|Relating to the North Pole or the region around it.  Characterized by very cold temperatures.  Also, can refer to the North Pole region itself. |The arctic fox has thick fur to survive the harsh climate. 北极狐有厚厚的皮毛以抵御严酷的气候。| The Arctic is a fragile environment. 北极是一个脆弱的环境。|近义词：polar, frigid, freezing|反义词：tropical, equatorial|词性:形容词,名词

Etymology: From Late Middle English *artik*, from Old French *artique*, and from Latin *arcticus*, from Greek *arktikos* "of the north," literally "of the Bear," from *arktos* "bear," referring to the constellation Ursa Major, the Great Bear, which is close to the north celestial pole.
