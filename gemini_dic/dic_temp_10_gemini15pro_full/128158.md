nettlelike:/ˈnɛt(ə)lˌlaɪk/
adjective
像荨麻的; 类似荨麻的
Resembling a nettle, especially in its stinging or irritating properties.  Covered with stinging hairs similar to those of a nettle.
The plant had nettlelike leaves that caused a rash when touched.  这种植物有类似荨麻的叶子，触摸时会引起皮疹。
The caterpillar's spines were nettlelike and caused a burning sensation. 毛毛虫的刺像荨麻一样，会引起灼烧感。

近义词:stinging, prickly, urticating
反义词:smooth, soft


词源:
From nettle +‎ -like.
