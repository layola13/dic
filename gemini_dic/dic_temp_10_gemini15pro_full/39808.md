consortium:/kənˈsɔːrtiəm/ /kənˈsɔːrʃəm/| n.|财团，联合企业，银团|A consortium is an association of two or more companies, organizations, or individuals formed to undertake a joint venture or project.|Several banks formed a consortium to finance the project. 几家银行组成了一个财团为该项目融资。|Example: A consortium of investors bought the failing company.  一群投资者组成的财团收购了这家濒临倒闭的公司。|Synonyms: syndicate, alliance, coalition, partnership, association|Antonyms: None (lack of a direct antonym, potentially competition or individual entity)|Noun
