inertness:/ɪˈnɜːrtnəs/
|n.|惰性；迟钝；不活动|Lack of activity or reactivity; the state of being inert.|The inertness of the gas makes it safe to use in certain applications. 这种气体的惰性使其可安全地用于某些应用。|Synonyms: inactivity, passivity, sluggishness, dormancy, quiescence|Antonyms: activity, reactivity, liveliness, dynamism|noun

inert:/ɪˈnɜːrt/
|adj.|惰性的；迟钝的；不活动的|Not readily reactive with other elements; inactive; sluggish.|Noble gases are inert. 惰性气体是惰性的。|Synonyms: inactive, unreactive, passive, sluggish, dormant|Antonyms: active, reactive, lively, dynamic|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *iners*, meaning "unskilled, inactive," from *in-* (not) + *ars* (skill, art).
