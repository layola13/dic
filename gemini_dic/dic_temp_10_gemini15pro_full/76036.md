furl:/fɜːrl/| v. n.|卷起，收拢； furlough 休假|To roll or fold up and secure neatly (something such as a flag, sail, umbrella, or banner).|The captain ordered the sailors to furl the sails. 船长命令水手们收起帆。|近义词：roll up, fold, stow, unfurl|反义词:unfurl, spread, extend|词性:动词, 名词

furl:/fɜːrl/| n.|卷，折叠|The act of furling something.|The furl of the flag signaled the end of the ceremony.  旗帜的卷起标志着仪式的结束。|近义词：roll, fold|反义词:unfurling, spreading|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French ferler, from Frankish *ferlingan, from Proto-Germanic *farlijaną (“to furl, roll up”).  Cognate with Old High German ferlingan (“to furl”), Dutch vellen (“to fell, cut down”), Old English fealgian (“to harrow, break up ground”).
