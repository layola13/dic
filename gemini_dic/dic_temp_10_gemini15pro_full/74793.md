frell:/frɛl/| v. int. n.| 发牢骚；抱怨；咒骂；诅咒；(表示恼怒、沮丧等的)感叹词 |A fictional Minbari word from the science fiction television series Babylon 5, used to express anger, frustration, annoyance, or surprise.  It is often described as untranslatable but similar to a curse word.| Example: "Frell! I left my communicator on the White Star."  该死！我把通讯器落在白星号上了。| Example: He frelled about the long lines at the customs office. 他抱怨海关的队伍太长了。| 近义词：curse, damn, swear, complain, grumble | 反义词: praise, bless | 词性: verb, interjection, noun
