hyperarid:/ˌhaɪpərˈærɪd/ | adj. | 极度干旱的 |  Excessively arid; having less than one-tenth the precipitation of a normal desert. Characterized by extreme dryness, receiving very little rainfall. | The Atacama Desert in Chile is a hyperarid environment. 智利的阿塔卡马沙漠是一个极度干旱的环境。|  The surface of Mars is a hyperarid landscape. 火星表面是一片极度干旱的景观。 | Near synonyms: extremely arid, ultra-arid, desiccated, parched | Near antonyms: humid, moist, wet, lush | adjective
