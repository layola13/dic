necessaries:/ˈnesəsəriz/| n.|必需品|Things that are essential or indispensable, especially for life or comfort.  These often include food, clothing, and shelter.|例句：She packed only the bare necessaries for her camping trip. 她只为露营打包了最基本的必需品。

例句：The government provided necessaries to the earthquake victims. 政府向地震灾民提供了必需品。|近义词：essentials, requisites, necessities|反义词:luxuries, nonessentials|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *necessarie*, from Old French *necessarie*, from Latin *necessarius* (“unavoidable, indispensable, necessary”), from *necesse* (“necessary”).
