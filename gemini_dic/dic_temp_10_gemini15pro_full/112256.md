lop-sided:/ˌlɒpˈsaɪdɪd/ (US) /ˌlɑːpˈsaɪdɪd/| adj.|不平衡的，一边倒的|Unevenly balanced; heavier, larger, or lower on one side than the other.  Having one side lower or smaller than the other.|The painting hung lop-sided on the wall.  这幅画歪歪斜斜地挂在墙上。|asymmetrical, uneven, unbalanced|symmetrical, even, balanced|adjective

lop-sided:/ˌlɒpˈsaɪdɪd/ (US) /ˌlɑːpˈsaɪdɪd/| adj.|一边倒的(比赛)|Heavily weighted in favor of one side; one-sided (especially of a contest).|It was a lop-sided victory for the home team. 对主队来说，这是一场一边倒的胜利。|one-sided, unequal|even, close, balanced|adjective
