nutritionist:/njuːˈtrɪʃənɪst/ | n. |营养学家；营养师|A person who studies or is an expert in nutrition.| I consulted a nutritionist about my diet. 我咨询了一位营养学家关于我的饮食。|  She's a registered nutritionist. 她是一位注册营养师。 | synonyms: dietitian, nutrition expert | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps someone promoting unhealthy eating habits) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From nutrition + -ist.  Nutrition comes from the Late Latin nutritiōn- (stem of nutritiō ),  "nourishment," from the past participle stem of nutrire "to nourish,"  ultimately from PIE root *nau- "to enjoy, have use of."
