nale: /neɪl/ | adj. | 无效的，失效的 | No longer legally valid; null and void. | The contract was deemed nale after the company went bankrupt.  合同在公司破产后被视为无效。| Invalid, null, void | Valid, effective | adjective

nale: /neɪl/ | v. | 使无效，废除 | To make something legally null and void; to invalidate. | The judge naled the contract. 法官宣布合同无效。| Invalidate, annul, nullify | Validate, ratify, confirm | verb


词源:

From Middle French *naler, from Latin *nallāre, from nullus (“none, not any”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ne (“not”) + *h₂óylos (“whole”). Related to English null.
