ethnographers:/ɛθˈnɒɡrəfərz/
n.
民族志学者；人种学者
A person who studies human cultures, typically by living among them and observing their behavior.
Ethnographers spent years living with the tribe to understand their customs.  民族志学者花了数年时间与该部落一起生活，以了解他们的习俗。

Ethnographers often conduct interviews and collect artifacts to supplement their observations. 民族志学者经常进行访谈和收集文物来补充他们的观察。

anthropologists, sociologists, researchers
