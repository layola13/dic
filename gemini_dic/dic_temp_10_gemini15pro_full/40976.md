coordain:/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪt/ /kəʊˈɔːdɪneɪt/
|v. |协调; 协同; 配合|To organize, harmonize, or synchronize activities or elements to work together efficiently. |The dancers coordinated their movements perfectly. 舞蹈演员们的动作配合得天衣无缝。|synonyms: harmonize, synchronize, integrate, orchestrate |antonyms:  conflict, clash, disorganize|verb
coordain:/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪt/ /kəʊˈɔːdɪneɪt/
|adj. |同等的; 并列的; 等位的|Equal in rank, status, or importance. |They held coordinate positions in the company. 他们在公司担任同等职位。|synonyms: equal, equivalent, parallel |antonyms: subordinate, unequal, inferior|adjective
coordain:/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪt/ /kəʊˈɔːdɪneɪt/
|n. |坐标; (颜色相配的) 衣服; 配套服装| A set of numbers or values that locate a point in space. Also, clothing items designed to be worn together.|The GPS gave me the coordinates of the location. GPS 给了我这个位置的坐标。|synonyms: position, location, (for clothing) outfit, ensemble|antonyms: (no direct antonym for coordinate as a noun)|noun
