dispossessors:/dɪspəˈzɛsərz/
n.
剥夺者，驱逐者
People who deprive others of their possessions, often land or property.
The dispossessors forced the families from their homes. 剥夺者强迫这些家庭离开家园。
The dispossessors claimed the land as their own. 这些强占者声称这片土地是他们自己的。
Synonyms: usurpers, expropriators, evictors
Antonyms:  benefactors, donors, grantors
