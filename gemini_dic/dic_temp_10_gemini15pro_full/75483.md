frustrations:/frʌˈstreɪʃənz/
n.|挫折，沮丧，失败|The feeling of being upset or annoyed, especially because of inability to change or achieve something.|例句：He vented his frustrations on his family. 他把他的挫败感发泄在了家人身上。
例句：Dealing with daily frustrations is part of the job.  处理日常挫折是工作的一部分。
近义词：disappointments, setbacks, obstacles, hindrances
反义词：successes, achievements, triumphs
词性: 名词(plural)

frustration:/frʌˈstreɪʃən/
n.|挫折，沮丧，失败|The feeling of being upset or annoyed, especially because of inability to change or achieve something.|例句：She couldn't hide the frustration in her voice. 她无法掩饰声音中的沮丧。
例句：The project was cancelled, much to everyone's frustration.  这个项目被取消了，让每个人都很沮丧。
近义词：disappointment, setback, obstacle, hindrance
反义词：success, achievement, triumph
词性: 名词(singular)


词源: From Late Latin frustrationem,  from Latin frustrare "to deceive, disappoint, make vain," from frustra "in vain."
