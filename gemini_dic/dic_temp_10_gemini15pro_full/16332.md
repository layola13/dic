ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *barbarismus, from Greek barbarismos "foreign speech, barbarism," from barbarizein "to speak like a foreigner," from barbaros (adj.) "foreign, non-Greek," probably imitative of unintelligible speech, like bar-bar-bar.
