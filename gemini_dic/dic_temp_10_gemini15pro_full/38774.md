conducible:/kənˈdjuːsɪbl/ /kənˈduːsəbl/
adjective
有益的；有助于…的
tending to promote or assist; contributing
例句：Regular exercise is conducible to good health.  规律的运动有助于健康。

例句：A quiet environment is conducible to study. 安静的环境有利于学习。

近义词：helpful, beneficial, advantageous, favorable
反义词：detrimental, harmful, unfavorable, hindering
词性: adjective
