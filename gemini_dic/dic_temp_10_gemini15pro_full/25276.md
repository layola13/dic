buffoon:/bəˈfuːn/| n.|小丑；滑稽可笑的人|A buffoon is a person who amuses others by clowning, by tricks, jokes, odd gestures and postures, etc.  |例句：He acted the buffoon to get a laugh. 他扮小丑逗人笑。

例句：The king enjoyed the antics of his buffoon. 国王很喜欢他的小丑的滑稽动作。|近义词：clown, jester, fool, comedian|反义词：sage, intellectual|词性:noun
