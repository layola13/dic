nondeliberate:/ˌnɒndɪˈlɪbərət/ | adj. | 非故意的，无意的 | Not done consciously or intentionally; unintentional. |  The mistake was nondeliberate; it was a simple oversight.  这个错误并非故意，只是一时疏忽。

The comment, though nondeliberate, still caused offense.  尽管并非故意，这条评论仍然造成了冒犯。 | 近义词: unintentional, accidental, inadvertent, unplanned, unconscious | 反义词: deliberate, intentional, purposeful, conscious, premeditated | 词性: adjective

词源:  The word "nondeliberate" is formed by the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") added to the adjective "deliberate."  "Deliberate" comes from the Latin word "deliberatus," the past participle of "deliberare," meaning "to weigh in mind, consider, deliberate."
