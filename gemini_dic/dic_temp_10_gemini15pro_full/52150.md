diaphanotype:/daɪˈæfənətaɪp/ | n. | 透印版；透印照片 | A photographic positive printed on transparent material, viewed by transmitted light.  A method of producing a positive transparency on glass or other transparent material. |  The museum displayed several diaphanotypes from the 19th century.  博物馆展出了几幅19世纪的透印照片。|  transparency, slide | negative, print | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *dia- (“through”) + *phan- (“to show, make visible”) + *-type (“type”).
