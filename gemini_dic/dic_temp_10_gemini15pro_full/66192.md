excipulum:/ɛkˈsɪpjʊləm/
|n.|外囊盘，囊托，托|The outer layer or rim of the apothecium of a lichen or other fungus, formed by the thallus.|The excipulum of this lichen species is distinctly warty.  这种地衣的外囊盘明显地呈疣状。|Synonyms: receptacle, support, base|Antonyms: N/A (lack of a specific anatomical opposite)|noun
