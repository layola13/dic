errs:/ɜːrz/ | v. | 犯错，出错 |  To make a mistake or mistakes. | He errs on the side of caution. 他宁愿过于谨慎也不愿犯错。 | synonyms: mistakes, blunders, slips, fails | antonyms: succeeds, perfects, corrects | verb

Etymology:
From Middle English *erren*, from Old French *errer*, from Latin *errare* (“to wander, stray, err”), from Proto-Italic *ersāre*, from Proto-Indo-European *h₁ers- (“to move, wander”).
