oscillators:/ˈɑːsəleɪtərz/ (US), /ˈɒsɪleɪtəz/ (UK)| n.|振荡器，振子|A device that produces regular electrical or mechanical oscillations.|The radio uses a crystal oscillator to control its frequency. 这台收音机使用晶体振荡器来控制频率。|Synonyms: vibrator, resonator|Antonyms: stabilizer, damper|Noun
