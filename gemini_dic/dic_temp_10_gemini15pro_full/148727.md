overtread:/ˌoʊvərˈtrɛd/| v.|践踏，蹂躏，侵犯|To tread over or across; to trample down; to infringe upon.|例句：The invading army overtread the conquered lands. 入侵的军队蹂躏了被征服的土地。

例句：They overtread the rights of the indigenous people. 他们侵犯了原住民的权利。|近义词：trample, infringe, violate|反义词：respect, uphold|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + tread.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "above," "across," "excessively," or "completely." "Tread" comes from Old English *tredan, meaning "to walk, step, tread."
