overapprehension:/ˌoʊvərˌæprɪˈhɛnʃən/| n.|过度忧虑，过分担心|Excessive worry or anxiety about something that might happen.|He suffered from overapprehension about the upcoming exam. 他对即将到来的考试过度忧虑。|Example:  The child's overapprehension about the dark made it difficult for him to sleep.  这个孩子对黑暗的过度恐惧让他难以入睡。

|Synonyms: anxiety, worry, solicitude, apprehension, fear, dread|Antonyms: calmness, composure, tranquility, serenity|noun
