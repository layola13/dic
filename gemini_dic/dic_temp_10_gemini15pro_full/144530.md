outvanishing:/ˌaʊtˈvænɪʃɪŋ/
adjective
超越消失的，比...消失得更彻底的
Surpassing or exceeding in vanishing; disappearing more completely or thoroughly than something else.  This word emphasizes the degree or extent of disappearance, suggesting a more complete or absolute vanishing than something else. It implies a comparison.
例句：The magician's outvanishing act left the audience speechless.  魔术师的超越消失的表演让观众哑口无言。

例句：Her beauty was outvanishing, eclipsing even the memory of her youth. 她的美貌正在消逝，甚至超越了她年轻时的记忆。
近义词：outlasting, surpassing, exceeding
反义词：enduring, persisting, remaining
词性: adjective
