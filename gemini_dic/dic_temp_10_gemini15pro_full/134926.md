nonmutinous:/ˌnɒnˈmjuːtɪnəs/
adjective
非叛变的；忠诚的；顺从的
Not mutinous; obedient, loyal.
The crew remained nonmutinous despite the harsh conditions. 尽管条件恶劣，船员们仍然保持忠诚。
Synonyms: loyal, obedient, compliant, submissive, docile
Antonyms: mutinous, rebellious, insurgent, seditious
词性: adjective
