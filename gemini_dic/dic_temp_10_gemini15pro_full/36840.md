colonizationist:/ˌkɒlənɪˈzeɪʃənɪst/ | /ˌkɑːlənɪˈzeɪʃənɪst/ | n. | 殖民主义者 | A person who supports or participates in colonization, the act of establishing political control over another territory and its people. |  The colonizationist exploited the resources of the conquered land. 殖民主义者剥削了被征服土地的资源。 |  The colonizationist's actions led to the displacement of the indigenous population. 殖民主义者的行为导致了土著居民的流离失所。 | 近义词: colonizer, imperialist | 反义词: anti-colonialist, native | 词性: 名词
