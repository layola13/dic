dishabilitate:/ˌdɪshəˈbɪlɪteɪt/
verb
使丧失能力，使残疾
to make someone unable to work or do things normally because of illness or injury
The accident dishabilitated him, leaving him unable to walk.  这起事故使他残疾，无法行走。
disable, incapacitate, cripple
enable, empower
