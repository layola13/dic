molokane:/ˌmoʊloʊˈkɑːneɪ/ /ˌmɒləˈkeɪn/
n.
精神牛奶主义者; Molokan
A member of a Russian Christian sect, similar to the Dukhobors, that rejects the Orthodox Church and its rituals, including the use of icons and sacraments.  Molokans emphasize spiritual milk, referring to the pure word of God found in the Bible, as the true sustenance for believers.
例句：The Molokane community maintains a strong tradition of pacifism.  精神牛奶主义者社区保持着强烈的和平主义传统。

例句：Many Molokane emigrated from Russia to North America in the late 19th and early 20th centuries. 许多精神牛奶主义者在19世纪末和20世纪初从俄罗斯移民到北美。
近义词：Spiritual Christian, sectarian
反义词：Orthodox Christian
词性：名词
