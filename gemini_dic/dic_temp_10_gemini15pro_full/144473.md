outthinks:/ˌaʊtˈθɪŋks/
verb
智胜；在思考上胜过
to think more cleverly and effectively than someone else
She always outthinks her opponents in negotiations.  她在谈判中总是能智胜对手。
outsmart, outwit, outmaneuver
