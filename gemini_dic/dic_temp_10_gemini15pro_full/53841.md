disconsolations:/dɪsˌkɒnsəˈleɪʃənz/
n.
悲痛，伤心事，慰藉不了的悲伤
a state of being deeply unhappy and unable to be comforted.

She suffered many disconsolations in her life, including the loss of her parents and her home.
她一生遭受了许多痛苦，包括失去父母和家园。

The disconsolations of exile were almost unbearable.
流放的痛苦几乎无法忍受。


Synonyms: grief, sorrow, sadness, distress, misery, heartache, anguish, despair
Antonyms: comfort, solace, consolation, cheer, joy, happiness

词性: noun
