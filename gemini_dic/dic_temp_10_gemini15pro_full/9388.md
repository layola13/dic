antiqued:/ænˈtiːkt/
adjective
古董的，做旧的
Made to look old or like an antique.
The shop sold antiqued furniture and other vintage items. 这家商店出售仿古家具和其他复古物品。
The mirror frame was antiqued to give it a more classic look.  镜框被做旧以使其看起来更具古典气息。
synonyms: aged, vintage, antique-looking, distressed
antonyms: modern, contemporary, new
adjective
