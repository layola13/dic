incumbition:/ɪnˌkʌmˈbɪʃən/
n.
责任; 职务; 在职期间
The act of holding an office or position of responsibility.  The time during which someone holds an office or position.
During his incumbition as mayor, he focused on improving public transportation.  在他担任市长期间，他专注于改善公共交通。
Synonym: tenure, term, office, position
Antonym: vacancy
noun

incumbency:/ɪnˈkʌmbənsi/
n.
责任; 职务; 在职期间
The holding of an office or the period during which one is held.
The president's incumbency was marked by economic growth. 总统的任期以经济增长为标志。
Synonym: tenure, term, office, position
Antonym: vacancy
noun
