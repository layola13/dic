increasingly:/ɪnˈkriːsɪŋli/
adverb|越来越多地，日益|becoming greater in amount, number, or degree|例句：The world is becoming increasingly interconnected. 世界正变得越来越相互关联。|近义词：progressively, more and more, increasingly|反义词:decreasingly, less and less|词性:副词
