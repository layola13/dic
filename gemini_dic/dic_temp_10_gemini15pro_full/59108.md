dyker:/ˈdaɪkər/| n.|潜水员，筑堤者|A person who builds or repairs dikes, or a scuba diver. |例句：The dyker checked his equipment before entering the water. 潜水员在入水前检查了他的设备。

例句：The dyker reinforced the dike to prevent flooding.  筑堤者加固了堤坝以防止洪水。|近义词：diver, underwater welder (for underwater dykers), dike builder|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
