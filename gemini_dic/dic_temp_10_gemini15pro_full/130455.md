nonabsorbent:/ˌnɒnəbˈzɔːbənt/ /ˌnɑːnəbˈsɔːrbənt/| adj.|不吸水的，不吸收的|Not able to absorb liquids or other substances.|This raincoat is made of a nonabsorbent material. 这件雨衣是用不吸水的材料做的。|The table has a nonabsorbent surface, so spills are easy to clean. 这张桌子表面不吸水，所以洒出的东西很容易清理。|近义词：impermeable, waterproof, water-resistant|反义词: absorbent, permeable, porous|词性:形容词

Etymology:
non- + absorbent

non-:  prefix meaning "not"
absorbent: from Latin absorbēre "to swallow down," from ab- "away" + sorbēre "to suck up."
