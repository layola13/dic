farcify: /ˈfɑːrsɪfaɪ/ | v. | 填充；塞满；填鸭式地灌输 | To stuff or fill tightly; to cram, especially with food or information.  |  The teacher farcified the students with facts before the exam.  老师在考试前给学生们填鸭式地灌输了许多知识。 | 近义词: cram, stuff, pack, fill | 反义词: empty, deplete | 词性: 动词

词源:  From Late Latin *farcire "to stuff," from Latin farcire "to stuff with sausages or seasoned meats."

例句：
He farcified the turkey with a bread and herb stuffing. 他把火鸡塞满了面包和香草馅。
The history professor farcified his lectures with dates and names. 历史教授在他的讲座中塞满了日期和名字。
