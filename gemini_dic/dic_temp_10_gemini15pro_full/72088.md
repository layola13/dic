floating:/ˈfloʊtɪŋ/ (US) /ˈfləʊtɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. |漂浮的，浮动的，流动的|being buoyed up by water or other liquid; not sinking, also can describe something that is not fixed or settled|The boat was floating gently on the lake. 那艘船在湖面上轻轻地漂浮着。|近义词：drifting, buoyant, hovering|反义词: sinking, submerged|adjective

floating:/ˈfloʊtɪŋ/ (US) /ˈfləʊtɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |漂浮，浮动|the act of floating|The floating of the new company on the stock market was successful. 新公司在股票市场的上市很成功。|近义词：drifting, buoyancy|反义词: sinking, submergence|noun
