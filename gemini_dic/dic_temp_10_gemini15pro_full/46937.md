darned: /ˈdɑːrnd/ (US) /ˈdɑːnd/ (UK)| adj. | 讨厌的，可恶的(damn的委婉说法) |  Used as a mild oath or expression of annoyance.  | This darned computer keeps crashing! 这该死的电脑老是崩溃！| Synonyms: cursed, blasted, confounded | Antonyms: blessed, fortunate | adjective
