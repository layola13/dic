hitchily:/ˈhɪtʃɪli/ | adv. |  断断续续地，不顺畅地 |  In a halting or jerky manner, characterized by interruptions or difficulties. | The old car moved hitchily down the road.  这辆旧车在路上行驶得很颠簸。| jerkily, unevenly, falteringly | smoothly, steadily | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From hitch + -ly.  Hitch (meaning a temporary obstacle or stoppage) dates back to Old English *hicc, of uncertain origin. The suffix -ly forms adverbs from adjectives.
