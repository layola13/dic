banger:/ˈbæŋər/ (US), /ˈbæŋə(r)/ (UK)| n. |香肠; 劲爆的歌曲; 老爷车|Something excellent, impressive, or exciting, especially a song.  Originally British slang for "sausage," its modern usage refers to a hit song. It can also refer to an old, dilapidated car.|例句：That new song is a total banger! 那首新歌真是太棒了！

例句：They played a string of bangers at the party. 他们在派对上播放了一系列劲爆的歌曲。

近义词：hit, smash, anthem, tune, great song, awesome song|反义词:dud, flop|词性:noun

banger:/ˈbæŋər/ (US), /ˈbæŋə(r)/ (UK)| n. |香肠|A sausage.|例句：We had bangers and mash for dinner. 我们晚饭吃了香肠和土豆泥。

例句：The smell of frying bangers filled the air. 煎香肠的味道弥漫在空气中。
