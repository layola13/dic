|近义词：parliament, legislature, house of representatives|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "executive branch")|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From German, composed of "Abgeordnete" (deputies, representatives) + "Haus" (house). "Abgeordnete" itself comes from "abgeordnet" (assigned, delegated), the past participle of "abordnen" (to delegate, to assign).  The word ultimately traces back to Proto-Germanic roots.

USAGE->

The word "Abgeordnetenhaus" primarily functions as a noun and doesn't have verb conjugations or adjectival forms like "hello" in the example.  It's used in sentences much like any other noun referring to a legislative body.
