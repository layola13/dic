gyrogonite:/ˈdʒaɪrəˌɡoʊnaɪt/
n. |轮藻化石，石果|A small, calcareous, fossilized fruit of certain freshwater algae (charophytes).|The gyrogonites found in the sediment helped to date the lake's formation.| 近义词: charophyte fossil|反义词: (No direct antonym)| 词性: Noun
