misbinding:/ˌmɪsˈbaɪndɪŋ/
noun
错误装订；装订错误
an act of binding something incorrectly, or the state of being bound incorrectly.
The misbinding of the book resulted in several pages being out of order. 这本书装订错误导致几页顺序颠倒了。
misprint, misalignment
correct binding
n.
