career women: /kəˈrɪər ˌwɪmɪn/
noun
职业女性
Women who are focused on and prioritize their professional work and advancement.
例句：Many career women find it difficult to balance work and family life. 许多职业女性发现很难平衡工作和家庭生活。

例句：The company is actively promoting career women to leadership positions.  这家公司正在积极提拔职业女性担任领导职位。

近义词：professional women, working women
反义词: homemaker, housewife
词性: noun
