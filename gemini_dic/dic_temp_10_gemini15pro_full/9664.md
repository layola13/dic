antitakeover:/ˌæntiˈteɪkoʊvər/
adjective
反收购的
Designed to prevent or discourage hostile takeovers.
The company adopted several antitakeover measures to protect itself from unwanted suitors.  公司采取了几项反收购措施，以保护自己免受不想要的收购者的青睐。
The board approved a poison pill as an antitakeover defense. 董事会批准了一项毒丸计划作为反收购防御措施。
Synonyms: takeover-proof, anti-acquisition
Antonyms: pro-takeover, acquisition-friendly
词性: 形容词
