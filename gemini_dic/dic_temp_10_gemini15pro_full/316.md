abettals: /əˈbɛtəlz/
n.
教唆；煽动；怂恿；同谋
Acts of encouraging or assisting someone to do something wrong or illegal.
The abettals of the coup were arrested and charged with treason. 政变的教唆者被逮捕并被指控犯有叛国罪。

His abettals in the crime were never caught.  他在犯罪中的同谋从未被抓获。

近义词:  assistance, aid, support, encouragement, complicity, collusion
反义词:  hindrance, obstruction, opposition, discouragement
词性: 名词(plural)

词源:
From Anglo-French *abeter, from abeter 'to bait,' from a- 'to' + beter 'to bait.'
