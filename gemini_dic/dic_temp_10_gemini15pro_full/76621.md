galloper:/ˈɡæləpər/| n.|奔驰者，飞驰的马，快马, 疾驰的人或物|A galloper is a horse that gallops, or a person or thing that moves very fast. |The young boy dreamed of owning a swift galloper and riding across the open plains.  这个小男孩梦想拥有一匹飞驰的骏马，驰骋在广阔的平原上。|近义词：racer, runner, steed|反义词:slowpoke, laggard|词性:noun
词源: From gallop + -er.  From Middle French *galoper, of uncertain origin.
