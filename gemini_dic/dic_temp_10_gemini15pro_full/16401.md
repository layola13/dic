barcarolle:/ˌbɑːrkəˈroʊl/ /ˈbɑːkərɒl/| n. |船歌；威尼斯船夫歌|A typically Venetian folk song with a rhythm suggestive of the movement of a gondola, or a piece of music imitating such a song.  A song sung by Venetian gondoliers, or a musical composition written in the style of these songs. It is characterized by a swaying, lilting rhythm in 6/8 or 12/8 time, often with a distinctive accompaniment pattern. |Offenbach's "Barcarolle" from The Tales of Hoffmann is a famous example.  奥芬巴赫的歌剧《霍夫曼的故事》中的《船歌》就是一个著名的例子。|近义词：gondola song, boat song|反义词: - |noun
