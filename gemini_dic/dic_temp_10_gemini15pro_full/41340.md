coriander: /ˌkɔːrɪˈændər/ (UK) /ˌkɔːriˈændər/ (US)| n.|芫荽，香菜|An aromatic plant whose leaves and seeds are used as a culinary herb, especially in Asian and Mexican cooking. The leaves are sometimes called cilantro in North America.|例句：I love the fresh taste of coriander in Vietnamese food. 我喜欢越南菜里芫荽的新鲜味道。

例句：She added a pinch of ground coriander to the curry.  她在咖喱里加了一撮芫荽粉。
