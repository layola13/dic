bounce lighting:/ˈbaʊns laɪtɪŋ/
n.|反光照明，跳闪|A lighting technique where the light from a flash or other light source is bounced off a surface such as a ceiling or wall to create a softer, more diffused light.|The photographer used bounce lighting to avoid harsh shadows on the model's face. 摄影师使用反光照明以避免模特脸上出现刺眼的阴影。|diffused lighting, indirect lighting|direct lighting|noun
bounce:/baʊns/
v. n.|弹跳，反弹；跳动；(使)上下晃动|To spring back after hitting a surface.  (n.) The act of springing back.|The ball bounced twice before he caught it.  球弹了两下他才接住。|rebound, spring, hop|fall, drop|verb, noun
lighting:/ˈlaɪtɪŋ/
n.|照明，灯光|The arrangement or type of lights in a place.|The lighting in the restaurant was very romantic.  餐厅的灯光很浪漫。|illumination|darkness|noun
