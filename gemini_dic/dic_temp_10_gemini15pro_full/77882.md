genevanism:/ʒɪˈniːvənɪzəm/
|n.|日内瓦主义；加尔文主义|The principles and doctrines characteristic of Geneva, especially in the 16th century, particularly the theological system of Calvin and his followers. 指的是16世纪日内瓦的原则和学说，尤指加尔文及其追随者的神学体系。|Historians often discuss the impact of Genevanism on the Reformation. 历史学家经常讨论日内瓦主义对宗教改革的影响。|Synonyms: Calvinism, Reformed theology|Antonyms: Arminianism, Catholicism|noun
