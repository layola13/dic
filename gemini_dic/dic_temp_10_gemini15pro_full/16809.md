baseline:/ˈbeɪslaɪn/| n. adj.|基线，基准；底线|A starting point or standard against which progress or comparisons can be made.  A line serving as a basis; especially : one of known measure or position used (as in surveying) to calculate or locate something.|The company’s profits have fallen below the baseline for the third quarter in a row. 该公司的利润连续三个季度低于基准线。|synonyms: standard, benchmark, starting point|antonyms: peak, apex|noun

baseline:/ˈbeɪslaɪn/| adj.|基线的，基础的|Relating to or constituting a baseline.|The baseline data will be used to assess the effectiveness of the new program.  这些基线数据将用于评估新计划的有效性。|synonyms: basic, fundamental, foundational|antonyms: advanced, complex|adjective
