mergence:/ˈmɜːrdʒəns/
n.
合并，融合
The mergence of the two companies created a powerful new entity.  两家公司的合并创造了一个强大的新实体。
The mergence of different cultures can lead to exciting new art forms. 不同文化的融合可以导致令人兴奋的新艺术形式的产生。
近义词：amalgamation, union, fusion, integration, coalescence
反义词：separation, division, split, divergence
