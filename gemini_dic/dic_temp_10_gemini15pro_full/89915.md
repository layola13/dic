horse:/hɔːrs/ (British), /hɔːrs/ (American)| n.|马|A large, solid-hoofed, herbivorous quadruped, Equus caballus, domesticated since prehistoric times, used as a beast of burden, a draft animal, and especially for riding.|The horse galloped across the field.  马儿飞奔过田野。|synonyms: steed, mount, pony, charger|antonyms: None (horse is a concrete noun, it doesn't typically have antonyms)|noun
