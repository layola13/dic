chucked:/tʃʌkt/ | v. |扔, 丢弃, 放弃, 停止, 解雇| to throw something carelessly or casually; to give something up or stop doing something; to dismiss someone from a job. |He chucked his old shoes in the bin. 他把旧鞋子扔进了垃圾桶。|Synonyms: tossed, threw, discarded, abandoned, quit, fired|Antonyms: kept, retained, continued, hired|verb

chucked:/tʃʌkt/ | adj. | (非正式) 生气的，恼火的 | (informal) annoyed or irritated. | She was chucked when he cancelled their date.  他取消了他们的约会，她很生气。 | Synonyms: annoyed, irritated, miffed | Antonyms: pleased, happy | adjective
