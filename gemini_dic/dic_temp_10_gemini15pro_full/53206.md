dipyridyl: /daɪˈpɪrɪdɪl/ | n. |联吡啶，二吡啶|An organic compound with two pyridine rings linked together, often used as a ligand in coordination chemistry.  It exists as several isomers, the most common being 2,2'-bipyridine (also known as bipy or bipyridine).|Example 1: The dipyridyl ligand formed a stable complex with the metal ion. 联吡啶配体与金属离子形成了稳定的络合物。

Example 2:  Researchers are exploring the use of dipyridyl derivatives in new catalytic systems. 研究人员正在探索在新的催化体系中使用联吡啶衍生物。|Synonym: bipyridine, bipy|Antonym: N/A|noun
