bestest:/ˈbɛstɪst/ | adj. | 最最好的 | A non-standard and informal superlative form of "best," used to emphasize the highest degree of something. It is considered grammatically incorrect in formal writing. |  She makes the bestest cookies in the world. 她做的饼干是世界上最最好吃的。| Near Synonyms: best, greatest, finest | Antonyms: worst, poorest | Word Class: Adjective
