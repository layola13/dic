agible:/ˈædʒɪbəl/| adj.|可读的，易读的|Readable; easy to read.  Referring to handwriting or print that is clear and easy to decipher.|The document was old and faded, but still agible. 这份文件很旧也很褪色，但仍然清晰可辨。|近义词：legible, readable, decipherable, clear|反义词：illegible, unreadable, indecipherable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *agibilis, from Latin agere "to do, drive, lead, act," + -ibilis "-able."  The sense evolution is from "that can be driven" to "that can be read."
