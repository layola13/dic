finalising:/ˈfaɪnəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
完成；最后确定；定案
The act of putting the finishing touches on something, making it complete and ready.
They are finalising the details of the contract. 他们正在最后确定合同的细节。
Synonyms: completing, concluding, finishing
Antonyms: beginning, starting, initiating
词性: 动词现在分词
