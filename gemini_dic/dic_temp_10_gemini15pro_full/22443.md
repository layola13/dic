bookies:/ˈbʊkiz/
n.
 букмекеры (Russian), 庄家 (Chinese)
A bookie, short for bookmaker, is someone who takes bets, especially on horse races or other sporting events.
例句：He lost a lot of money to the bookies. 他在庄家那里输了很多钱。

例句：The bookies have made him the favorite to win the race.  庄家认为他是赢得比赛的热门人选。

近义词：bookmaker, turf accountant
反义词: gambler, punter
词性: noun (plural)


bookie:/ˈbʊki/
n.
 букмекер (Russian), 庄家 (Chinese)
A bookie, short for bookmaker, is someone who takes bets, especially on horse races or other sporting events.
例句：She went to the bookie to place a bet on the Derby. 她去找庄家在德比赛马上赌了一注。

例句：The bookie refused to take his bet. 庄家拒绝接受他的赌注。

近义词：bookmaker, turf accountant
反义词: gambler, punter
词性: noun (singular)
