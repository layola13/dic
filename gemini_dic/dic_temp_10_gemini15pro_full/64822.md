espaliered: /ɪˈspælɪərd/
adjective
中文翻译： espalier 训练的；(指树木)用棚架支撑使其沿墙或架子平铺生长的
英文解析：Trained to grow flat against a wall or other support, typically a trellis, often in a decorative pattern.  Refers to both the action of training a plant in this way and the resulting form of the plant.
例句：The espaliered apple trees lined the garden wall, their branches heavy with fruit.   espalier 训练的苹果树沿着花园的墙壁排列，树枝上果实累累。

例句：She carefully pruned the espaliered pear tree, encouraging its growth along the wires. 她小心翼翼地修剪了用棚架支撑的梨树，引导它沿着铁丝生长。

近义词：trained, supported, lattice-trained
反义词：untrained, unsupported, free-standing
词性：adjective


Etymology:
From French *espaliers*, plural of *espalier* "trellis, shoulder-piece of armor," from Old Italian *spalliera* "something for the shoulders," from *spalla* "shoulder," from Latin *spatula* "shoulder blade, broad piece."
