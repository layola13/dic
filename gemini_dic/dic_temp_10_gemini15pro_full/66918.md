expenses:/ɪkˈspɛnsɪz/
n.
费用，支出
The cost required for something; the money spent on something.
例句：The company will reimburse you for your travel expenses. 公司会报销你的差旅费。

例句：The monthly expenses are quite high. 每月的开支相当高。

近义词：costs, charges, outgoings, expenditure
反义词：income, revenue, profit
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French expenses (plural), from Latin expensa, neuter plural of expensus (“paid out”), past participle of expendere (“to weigh out, pay out”), from ex- (“out”) + pendere (“to weigh, pay”).
