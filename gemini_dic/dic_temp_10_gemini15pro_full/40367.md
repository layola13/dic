contractually:/kənˈtræktʃuəli/
adverb
根据合同；契约上
In a way that is specified in a contract.
The company is contractually obligated to provide health insurance.  这家公司根据合同有义务提供健康保险。
The land is contractually protected from development. 这片土地受合同保护，免于开发。
Synonym: legally, formally, officially
Antonym: informally, unofficially
词性: 副词
