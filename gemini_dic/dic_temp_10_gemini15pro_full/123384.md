monomorphic:/ˌmɑːnəˈmɔːrfɪk/ | adj. |单型的，单态的，单形的| Having a single form or structure. Existing in only one form. |  The population of cheetahs is so small that they are nearly monomorphic, lacking genetic diversity.| Synonym: uniform, homogenous | Antonym: polymorphic, heteromorphic | adjective
