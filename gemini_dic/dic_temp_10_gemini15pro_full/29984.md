cavillers:/ˈkævələr z/
n.
批评家；吹毛求疵的人；(尤指)反对不重要细节的人
People who raise trivial objections or make frivolous criticisms, especially about unimportant details.
The cavillers complained about the font size on the poster, ignoring the overall message.
批评家们抱怨海报上的字体大小，却忽略了整体信息。

近义词:critics, nitpickers, objectors, faultfinders
反义词:supporters, admirers, approvers

词性: noun (plural)

词源: From Middle French *caveilleur*, from *caveler* "to cavil," from Latin *cavillari* "to jest, mock, banter."
