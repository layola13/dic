give-the-hook:/ɡɪv ðə hʊk/
|v.|解雇；开除；抛弃；停止；终止；淘汰|To dismiss or reject someone or something, often abruptly or unceremoniously.  It can also mean to end a relationship or discontinue something.|The director gave the actor the hook after he missed too many rehearsals. 导演因为那个演员错过太多排练而把他开除了。|Synonyms: dismiss, fire, sack, reject, ditch, dump, discontinue, terminate|Antonyms: hire, employ, accept, continue|verb
