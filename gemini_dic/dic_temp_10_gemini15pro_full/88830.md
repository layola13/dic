hoggish:/ˈhɒɡɪʃ/ (US) /ˈhɑːɡɪʃ/| adj.|贪婪的；猪一般的；自私的|Resembling a hog in habits or behavior; greedy, gluttonous, selfish.|He had a hoggish attitude towards food, always taking more than his share. 他对食物的态度很贪婪，总是拿超过他应得的份量。|Synonyms: greedy, gluttonous, selfish, piggish, ravenous, rapacious|Antonyms: generous, selfless, altruistic|Adjective
