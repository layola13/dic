melanotype:/ˈmɛlənəˌtaɪp/ | n. | 黑色相片，黑色素照片 | A photographic process producing a black and white positive image on a non-transparent support, especially one using collodion.  | Example 1: The melanotype was a popular photographic method in the mid-19th century. 黑色相片在19世纪中期是一种流行的摄影方法。

Example 2:  He collected antique melanotypes, fascinated by the stark beauty of the images. 他收集古董黑色相片，被图像的鲜明美感所吸引。 | 近义词: ferrotype, tintype | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'color photograph') | noun
