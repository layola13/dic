frijole:/friˈhoʊli/ | n. |菜豆，小豆，四季豆 |A small, dried kidney bean, especially the variety cultivated in Mexico and the southwestern United States, often cooked and eaten whole. |例句：Frijoles are a staple food in Mexican cuisine.  菜豆是墨西哥菜肴中的主食。

|例句：She added frijoles to the chili for extra protein.  她在辣椒里加了菜豆以增加蛋白质。|近义词：bean, kidney bean, pinto bean |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
