magellanic:/ˌmædʒəˈlænɪk/| adj.|麦哲伦的，与麦哲伦有关的|Relating to Ferdinand Magellan, the Portuguese explorer, or his voyages, especially his voyage around the world.|The Magellanic Clouds are two irregular dwarf galaxies visible in the Southern Hemisphere.  麦哲伦星云是在南半球可见的两个不规则矮星系。|The magellanic penguin breeds in South America. 麦哲伦企鹅在南美洲繁殖。|近义词: circumnavigational (referring to the voyage)|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ferdinand Magellan (Fernão de Magalhães in Portuguese), a Portuguese explorer who organized the Spanish expedition to the East Indies from 1519 to 1522, resulting in the first circumnavigation of the Earth.
