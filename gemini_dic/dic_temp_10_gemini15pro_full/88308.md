hippe:/ˈhɪpi/| adj. n.|嬉皮士的；嬉皮士|A person of unconventional appearance, typically having long hair and wearing beads, associated with a subculture involving a rejection of conventional values and the taking of hallucinogenic drugs.|He lived a hippe lifestyle in the 1960s. 他在20世纪60年代过着嬉皮士的生活方式。|近义词：bohemian, nonconformist|反义词：conformist, traditionalist|词性:形容词,名词
词源:Shortened from hipster, which originally referred to jazz aficionados in the 1940s.  The term "hip" meaning "aware" or "in the know" dates back further.
