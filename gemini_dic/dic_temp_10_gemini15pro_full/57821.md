drengage:/ˈdrɛnɪdʒ/| n.|排水; 排水系统; 排出物|The drengage system of the city is outdated. 这个城市的排水系统已经过时了。|The drengage from the factory polluted the river. 工厂的排出物污染了河流。|近义词: drainage, outflow, discharge|反义词: influx, intake|词性:名词

词源:  From Middle English *drengage, from Old Norse *drengja (“to drain”). Cognate with Danish drænge, Swedish dränga.  Essentially a doublet of *drain*, which took a different path of development in English.  While "drengage" is now obsolete or rare, "drain" has become the common term.
