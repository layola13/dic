bountifuls:/ˈbaʊntɪfʊlz/
noun
大量，丰富之物
A large quantity of something, especially of good things.
The harvest yielded bountifuls of fruits and vegetables. 丰收带来了大量的水果和蔬菜。
Synonyms: abundance, plenty, profusion
Antonyms: scarcity, dearth
