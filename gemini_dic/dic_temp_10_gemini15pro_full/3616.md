ageism:/ˈeɪdʒɪzəm/ | n. | 年龄歧视；年龄主义 | Prejudice or discrimination on the basis of a person's age. |  Example 1: Ageism in the workplace can prevent older people from getting hired.  职场上的年龄歧视会阻止老年人被录用。

Example 2:  She experienced ageism when she was passed over for promotion in favor of a younger colleague.  当她被晋升时，由于一位更年轻的同事而受到了年龄歧视。 | Synonyms: age discrimination, prejudice against the elderly,  | Antonyms:  respect for elders, intergenerational equity | noun
