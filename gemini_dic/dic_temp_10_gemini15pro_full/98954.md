inter-dominion:/ˌɪntər dəˈmɪnjən/ | adj. | 跨治权的；自治领之间的 |  Relating to or involving relations between self-governing territories within a larger political entity, especially within the British Empire/Commonwealth. | Example: Inter-dominion trade flourished in the early 20th century.  20世纪初，自治领之间的贸易蓬勃发展。 | Example:  Inter-dominion conferences were held regularly to discuss matters of common concern. 定期举行自治领间会议，讨论共同关心的问题。 |Synonym: intergovernmental, international (in a limited sense) | Antonym: intra-dominion | adjective
