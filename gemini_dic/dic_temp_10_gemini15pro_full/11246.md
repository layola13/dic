archosauromorph:/ˌɑːrkoʊˈsɔːrəmɔːrf/
n.
主龙形类动物
A reptile belonging to the clade Archosauromorpha, which includes archosaurs (crocodilians, birds, and extinct dinosaurs) and their closest relatives.
The protosaur *Tanystropheus* is considered an archosauromorph. 原龙 *长颈龙* 被认为是主龙形类动物。

archosauromorph:/ˌɑːrkoʊˈsɔːrəmɔːrf/
adj.
主龙形类的
Relating to or characteristic of archosauromorphs.
*Trilophosaurus* displays some archosauromorph characteristics. *三棱龙* 表现出一些主龙形类的特征。
