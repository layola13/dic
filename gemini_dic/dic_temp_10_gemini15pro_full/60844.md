electrotonic:/ɪˌlɛktrəˈtɒnɪk/ /ɪˌlɛktroʊˈtɑːnɪk/| adj.|电紧张的；电调的|Relating to electrotonus, the passive spread of current in neurons and other excitable cells.|Electrotonic potentials decay with distance from the site of stimulation. 电紧张电位随着与刺激部位距离的增加而衰减。|The electrotonic spread of current allows for communication between different parts of a neuron. 电流的电紧张传播允许神经元不同部分之间进行通讯。|近义词:passive, graded|反义词:active, propagating|词性:形容词

electrotonus:/ɪˌlɛktrəˈtəʊnəs/  /ɪˌlɛktroʊˈtoʊnəs/| n.|电紧张|The passive spread of current in neurons and other excitable cells, which decays with distance from the site of stimulation.|Electrotonus plays a role in synaptic integration. 电紧张在突触整合中发挥作用。|The electrotonus of the membrane changed with the application of the current.  膜的电紧张随着电流的施加而改变。|近义词:passive spread, graded potential|反义词:action potential|词性:名词


词源:  From electro- + -tonic, from Greek tonos "tension."
