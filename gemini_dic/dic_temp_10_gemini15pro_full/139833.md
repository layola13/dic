octonaphthene:/ˌɒktəʊˈnæfθiːn/
n.
八氢化萘
A saturated hydrocarbon with the chemical formula C10H18, consisting of two fused cyclohexane rings.  It is also known as decahydronaphthalene or decalin.
Octonaphthene is used as a solvent in some industrial applications. 八氢化萘在一些工业应用中用作溶剂。
Synonyms: decahydronaphthalene, decalin
Antonyms: None (lacks a direct opposite as a chemical compound)
