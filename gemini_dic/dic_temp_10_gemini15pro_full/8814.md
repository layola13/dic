antiepilepsy:/ˌæntiˈɛpɪˌlɛpsi/| adj.|抗癫痫的|Used to describe drugs or treatments that prevent or reduce epileptic seizures.  Relating to or used in the prevention or control of epilepsy.|例句：She takes antiepilepsy medication daily. 她每天服用抗癫痫药物。

例句：The doctor prescribed an antiepilepsy drug to control his seizures. 医生开了抗癫痫药来控制他的癫痫发作。|近义词：anticonvulsant, antiseizure|反义词:  (No direct antonym, but a contrasting concept would be "proconvulsant")|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + epilepsy. "Anti-" is a Greek prefix meaning "against" or "opposite." "Epilepsy" comes from the Greek word "epilēpsia," meaning "seizure."
