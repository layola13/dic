一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他靠什么谋生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you listening to? = 你在听什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? = 她正在做什么晚餐？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned recently? = 你最近学到了什么？
What has he done to his hair? = 他对他的头发做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has she been reading this week? = 她这周一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? = 我看到她的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? = 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had happened before the police arrived? = 警察到达之前发生了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 在她换专业之前她一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will happen if I press this button? = 如果我按下这个按钮会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting tomorrow? = 明天会议上他们会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by the time we get back? = 等我们回来的时候会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2030? = 到2030年你将已经做什么十年了？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业的时候她将已经学习什么多久了？
