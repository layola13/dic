forswonk:/fɔːrˈswɒŋk/
adjective
筋疲力尽的，劳累不堪的
exhausted by toil; worn out.
The forswonk travelers finally reached the oasis. 筋疲力尽的旅行者终于到达了绿洲。
近义词: exhausted, worn out, fatigued, weary
反义词: energetic, refreshed, invigorated
词性: adjective

词源:
From Middle English *forswonken, from Old English *forswuncen, past participle of forswincan (“to labor excessively, toil, exhaust by labor”), equivalent to for- + swink (“to toil”).
