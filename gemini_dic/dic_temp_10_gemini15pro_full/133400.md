nonfacts:/nɑːnˈfækts/ | n. | 不实信息；虚假陈述 |  Things that are presented as facts but are not actually true.  They can be unintentional mistakes, deliberate misinformation, or simply made-up stories. |  Many nonfacts are circulating on social media these days.  现在社交媒体上流传着许多不实信息。| synonyms: falsehoods, untruths, inaccuracies, fabrications, myths, fallacies | antonyms: facts, truths, realities, certainties | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "non-" (not) + "facts" (things that are known or proved to be true).
