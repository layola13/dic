cacuminal:/kəˈkʌmɪnəl/ | adj. | 舌尖音的；卷舌音的 |  Of or relating to the tip of the tongue; formed by curling the tip of the tongue upward and backward against the hard palate. |  The "t" in the word "retroflex" is a cacuminal sound.  这个单词"retroflex"中的"t"是一个卷舌音。 |  retroflex, apical |  non-cacuminal | adjective
