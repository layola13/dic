agrotourists:/ˈæɡroʊˌtʊərɪsts/ | n. |农业游客| Tourists who visit agricultural areas, often to participate in farm activities, learn about local produce, or enjoy the rural environment. |Example 1: The agrotourists enjoyed picking strawberries at the local farm. 农业游客们喜欢在当地农场采摘草莓。

Example 2: The region is attracting increasing numbers of agrotourists interested in sustainable agriculture. 该地区吸引了越来越多的对可持续农业感兴趣的农业游客。| 近义词: farm tourists, rural tourists | 反义词: urban tourists |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
agro- (from Greek agrós, meaning "field") + tourist
