chummed:/tʃʌmd/
verb
结伴，友好相处， chum的过去式和过去分词
spent time with someone in a friendly way.
He chummed around with the other boys at camp. 他在营地里和其他男孩友好相处。
近义词：socialized, fraternized, palled around
反义词：ignored, shunned, avoided
词性：Verb

chum:/tʃʌm/
noun
好友，伙伴
a close friend.
They've been chums since childhood. 他们从小就是好朋友。
近义词：friend, pal, buddy
反义词：enemy, foe, adversary
词性：Noun


chum:/tʃʌm/
verb
chum的现在式和过去分词形式
to become friends with someone.
They chummed up quickly during freshman orientation. 他们在新生入学指导期间很快就成了朋友。
近义词：befriend, pal up with
反义词：ignore, shun, avoid
词性：Verb


词源：
From the Romany word *chomba*, meaning "roommate" or "companion."
