histrionics:/ˌhɪstriˈɑːnɪks/ | n. |  装腔作势；做作的举动；夸张的言行 | Exaggerated emotional behavior calculated for effect; theatrics. |  Her histrionics were embarrassing to watch. 她的装腔作势令人尴尬。| synonyms: dramatics, theatrics, tantrums, melodrama, affectation | antonyms: understatement, reserve, restraint, composure, sincerity | noun

histrionic:/ˌhɪstriˈɑːnɪk/ | adj. |  做作的；装腔作势的；夸张的 |  Overly theatrical or melodramatic in character or style. | His histrionic outburst was clearly intended to draw attention. 他夸张的爆发显然是为了吸引注意力。| synonyms: theatrical, dramatic, melodramatic, affected, exaggerated | antonyms: understated, reserved, restrained, composed, sincere | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *histrionicus*, from Latin histrio (“actor”), from Etruscan *ister* (“actor”).
