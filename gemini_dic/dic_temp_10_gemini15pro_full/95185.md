inaugurated: /ɪˈnɔːɡjəreɪtɪd/ | v. | 就职；开创；举行开幕式 |  formally begin or introduce (a system, policy, or period). | The president was inaugurated in January. 总统在一月就职。| The new theater was inaugurated with a performance of Shakespeare's Hamlet. 新剧院以演出莎士比亚的《哈姆雷特》开幕。| 近义词：begin, commence, launch, initiate, introduce | 反义词：conclude, terminate, end | 词性: 动词

词源:  From Latin inaugurare "to take omens from birds before entering upon a course of action; to consecrate, install," from in- "in, on" + augurare "to predict, foretell," from augur "soothsayer, diviner,"
