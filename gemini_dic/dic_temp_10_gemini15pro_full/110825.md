linoleic:/ˌlɪnəˈleɪɪk/ | adj. | 亚油酸的；含亚油酸的 | An unsaturated fatty acid essential in the human diet, found in vegetable oils such as safflower, sunflower, corn, and soybean oils.  |  Linoleic acid is an essential fatty acid. 亚油酸是一种必需脂肪酸。 |  Linoleic acid is important for maintaining healthy skin and hair. 亚油酸对维持健康的皮肤和头发很重要。 | Synonyms: unsaturated, essential fatty acid | Antonyms: saturated | adjective
