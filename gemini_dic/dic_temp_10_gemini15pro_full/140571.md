oligist:/ˈɑːlɪdʒɪst/ /ˈɒlɪdʒɪst/
n.
寡头政治家；寡头政治拥护者
A person who advocates or supports oligarchy (rule by a small group of people).
The oligist argued that a small group of experts would govern more efficiently than the masses.  这位寡头政治拥护者认为一小群专家比大众治理更有效率。
The oligist believed that power should be concentrated in the hands of a select few.  这位寡头政治拥护者相信权力应该集中在少数人手中。
近义词: autocrat, plutocrat, elitist
反义词: democrat
词性: 名词
