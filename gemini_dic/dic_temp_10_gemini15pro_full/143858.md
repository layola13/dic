outpleasing: /ˌaʊtˈpliːzɪŋ/
verb
取悦过度；过分讨好
Trying too hard to please others, often to the detriment of oneself.
He exhausted himself outpleasing his demanding boss. 他为了取悦他那苛刻的老板而精疲力竭。
近义词: fawning, overeager to please, subservient
反义词: assertive, independent, self-assured
词性: verb

Etymology:  A combination of the prefix "out-" (meaning to surpass or exceed) and the verb "pleasing" (from "please").  The "out-" prefix indicates doing something to an excessive degree.
