imbibe:/ɪmˈbaɪb/| v. |吸收，吸取（液体、知识等）| To absorb or assimilate something, especially information or ideas, into one's mind. |例句：The students imbibed the wisdom of their teacher. 学生们汲取了老师的智慧。

例句：Plants imbibe water through their roots. 植物通过根部吸收水分。|近义词：absorb, assimilate, drink, soak up|反义词:excrete, expel|词性:动词
