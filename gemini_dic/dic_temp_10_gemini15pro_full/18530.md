benefic:/bɪˈnɛfɪk/| adj.|有益的，有利的|Producing good or helpful results or effects; beneficial.|例句：The benefic influence of good music.  好的音乐带来的有益影响。|例句：A benefic policy that helped the local economy.一项有利于当地经济的政策。|近义词：advantageous, beneficial, helpful, profitable, salutary|反义词：detrimental, harmful, injurious, prejudicial|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin beneficus, from bene (“well”) + -ficus (akin to facere (“to do, make”)).
