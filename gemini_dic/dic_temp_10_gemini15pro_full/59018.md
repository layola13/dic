一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? = 他晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned recently? = 你最近学了什么？
What has she bought at the store? = 她在商店买了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? = 你整个星期都在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last night? = 你昨晚做了什么？
What time did she arrive? = 她什么时候到的？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加聚会穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you eaten before you got sick? = 你生病前吃了什么？
What had she done before she left? = 她离开前做了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been studying before you changed your major? = 你换专业前一直在学什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们下次会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你会完成什么？
What will she have learned by the time she finishes the course? = 当她完成课程时，她将学到什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will they have been studying by the time they graduate? = 到他们毕业的时候，他们将已经学习什么多久了？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
What would she have done if she had known the truth? = 如果她知道真相，她会怎么做？

被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being built next to the library? = 图书馆旁边正在建什么？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:

She doesn't understand what you're saying. = 她不明白你在说什么。
