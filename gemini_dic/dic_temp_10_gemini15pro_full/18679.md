bequeathers:/bɪˈkwiːðərz/
n.
遗赠者，立遗嘱的人
A person who bequeaths something, especially in a will.
My grandfather was a generous bequeather, leaving his entire estate to charity. 我的祖父是一位慷慨的遗赠者，把他的全部财产都留给了慈善机构。
Synonyms: testator, donor, legator
Antonyms: heir, beneficiary, recipient
noun
