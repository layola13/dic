colemouse:/ˈkəʊlˌmaʊs/| n. |睡鼠|A small, arboreal, nocturnal rodent with a long, bushy tail, found in Europe and Asia. It hibernates for long periods.|The colemouse curled up in its nest for the winter. 睡鼠在窝里蜷缩着过冬。|近义词：dormouse|反义词:|名词
