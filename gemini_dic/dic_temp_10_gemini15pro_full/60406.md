eighteen-wheeler:/ˌeɪˈtiːn ˈwiːlər/| n. |十八轮卡车；牵引式挂车|A large truck with a trailer and a total of eighteen wheels (ten on the tractor and eight on the trailer).  It is commonly used to transport goods over long distances.|Example: The eighteen-wheeler jackknifed on the icy highway. 十八轮卡车在结冰的高速公路上折叠起来。

Example:  We saw several eighteen-wheelers parked at the truck stop. 我们看到几辆十八轮卡车停在卡车停靠站。|近义词：semi-truck, tractor-trailer, big rig|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"eighteen" + "wheeler" -  "eighteen" refers to the number of wheels, and "wheeler" refers to a vehicle with wheels.  The term became popular in the mid-20th century with the rise of interstate trucking.
