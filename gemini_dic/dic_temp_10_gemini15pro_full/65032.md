etagere:/ˌeɪtəˈʒɛər/ /ˌetəˈʒɛr/ | n. | étagère;  Whatnot |A piece of furniture with open shelves for displaying ornaments, books, etc., often of an ornamental character. | She displayed her collection of china on the etagere. 她把她的瓷器收藏品陈列在架子上。|The etagere in the corner held a variety of antique books and figurines. 角落里的架子上摆放着各种各样的古董书籍和雕像。|Synonyms: shelf, bookcase, stand, whatnot| Antonyms: cupboard, cabinet | Noun
