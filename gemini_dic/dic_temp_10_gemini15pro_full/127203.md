naturopaths:/ˈneɪtʃərəpæθs/
n.|自然疗法医生；自然疗法专家|A naturopath is a practitioner of naturopathy, a system of alternative medicine based on the theory that diseases can be successfully treated or prevented without the use of drugs, by techniques such as control of diet, exercise, and massage.|
例句：I consulted a naturopath about my chronic fatigue. 我咨询了一位自然疗法医生关于我的慢性疲劳。

例句：Many naturopaths recommend herbal remedies. 许多自然疗法医生推荐草药疗法。

近义词：herbalist, holistic practitioner
反义词：conventional doctor, allopathic physician
词性：名词（复数）


ETYMOLOGY->
From *nature* + *-path*.
*Nature* from Old French, from Latin *nātūra* "birth, origin, nature," from *nātus*, past participle of *nāscī* "to be born."
*-path* from Greek *-pathēs*, meaning "one suffering from a (specified) disease," or "one skilled in a (specified) art," used as a suffix forming nouns denoting a practitioner of a specified therapy or method of treatment.
