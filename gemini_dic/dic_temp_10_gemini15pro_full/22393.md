boogeyman: /ˈbʊɡimæn/ (US), /ˈbʊɡɪmæn/ (UK)| n. |夜魔，妖怪，用来吓唬小孩的假想怪物|An imaginary creature used to frighten children. |例句：The children were scared of the boogeyman hiding under their beds. 孩子们害怕躲在床底下的妖怪。

例句：Don't be silly, the boogeyman isn't real. 别傻了，妖怪不是真的。

近义词：monster, goblin, bogey, bugbear|反义词: (No direct antonym, could be considered "protector", "guardian")|词性: noun
