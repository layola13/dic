cofferwork: /ˈkɒfəwɜːk/ (UK) /ˈkɔːfərwɜːrk/ (US)
n.
格子天花板；藻井；方格装饰
Ornamental work in recessed panels, especially on ceilings.
The cofferwork in the cathedral ceiling was breathtakingly intricate. 大教堂天花板上的格子装饰精美绝伦，令人叹为观止。

The banquet hall boasted elaborate cofferwork. 宴会厅以其精致的方格装饰而自豪。

Synonym: paneling, caissons, lacunar
Antonym: plain surface


Etymology:
From "coffer" (a recessed panel) + "work" (the product of activity)

"Coffer" comes from Old French "cofre," ultimately from Latin "cophinus," meaning "basket."
