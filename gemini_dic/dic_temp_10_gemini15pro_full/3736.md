aghastness:/əˈɡæstnɪs/
n.
惊骇；惊恐
A feeling of great shock and horror.
She stared at the scene with utter aghastness. 她惊恐地盯着眼前的景象。
近义词: horror, shock, dismay, terror
反义词: calmness, composure, tranquility
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From aghast + -ness. Aghast is from a- (intensive prefix) + gast (Middle English, "frightened," of Scandinavian origin).
