becuna:/bɪˈkjuːnə/
noun
金枪鱼，鲔鱼
A large, fast-swimming tuna (Thunnus thynnus) found in warm seas, important as a food and game fish.  It is also known as the bluefin tuna.
The becuna is a prized game fish. 金枪鱼是一种珍贵的游钓鱼。
tuna, bluefin tuna
