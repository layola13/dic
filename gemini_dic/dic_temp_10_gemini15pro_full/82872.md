gullish:/ˈɡʌlɪʃ/
adjective
容易受骗的，轻信的
easily tricked or fooled because of being too trusting
He was gullish enough to believe the con artist. 他很容易受骗，竟然相信了那个骗子。
近义词:naive, credulous, gullible
反义词:shrewd, astute, discerning
词性:形容词

词源:
From gull (noun) + -ish (suffix).  来源于名词gull（鸥，容易受骗的人）+ 后缀-ish。 Gull（鸥）被认为很容易被诱捕，因此引申为“容易受骗的人”。
