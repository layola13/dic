helplessly:/ˈhɛlpləsli/ | adv. | 无助地，无力地 | In a way that shows one is unable to manage or improve a situation; in a way that cannot be helped. |  She watched helplessly as the car rolled down the hill. 她眼睁睁地看着汽车滚下山坡，却无能为力。| synonyms: powerlessly, weakly, feebly | antonyms: capably, effectively, powerfully | adverb

helpless:/ˈhɛlpləs/ | adj. | 无助的，无能的 | Unable to manage or improve a situation; unable to help oneself. | The baby birds were helpless without their mother.  没有妈妈的小鸟很无助。 | synonyms: powerless, dependent, vulnerable | antonyms: capable, independent, powerful | adjective
