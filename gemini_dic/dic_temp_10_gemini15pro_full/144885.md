over-ripenesses:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪpənəsɪz/
noun
过度成熟
The state of being excessively ripe, past the point of optimal ripeness.
The over-ripenesses of the bananas made them mushy. 香蕉过度成熟使它们变得糊状。
近义词: overmaturity, overripeness
反义词: unripeness, immaturity
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + ripeness.  "Over-" is an Old English prefix meaning "above," "beyond," "excessive,"  and "ripeness" comes from the Old English "ripnes," meaning the state of being ripe.
