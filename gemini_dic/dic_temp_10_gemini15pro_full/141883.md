ordain:/ɔːrˈdeɪn/
verb
规定；命令；任命；注定
To officially establish or decree something, often by law or religious authority; to confer holy orders upon someone.
The king ordained that everyone should pay taxes. 国王下令每个人都应该纳税。
He was ordained as a priest last year. 他去年被任命为牧师。
appoint, decree, order, command
destine, predestine
