flavine:/ˈfleɪviːn/ | n. | 核黄素; 黄素 | Any of a class of organic compounds that are based on the heterocyclic ring isoalloxazine, some of which function as coenzymes of flavoproteins and are essential for cellular respiration.  | Riboflavin, also known as vitamin B2, is a flavine. 核黄素，也称为维生素B2，是一种黄素。| Synonyms: riboflavin, vitamin B2 | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a chemical compound) | noun

flavin:/ˈfleɪvɪn/ | n. | 黄素 | A yellow crystalline compound, C17H20N4O6, that constitutes the prosthetic group of various flavoproteins, including some important respiratory enzymes.  | Flavins are involved in many important biological processes. 黄素参与许多重要的生物过程。 | Synonyms: riboflavin, vitamin B2 | Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a chemical compound) | noun
