dowdyism:/ˈdaʊdiɪzəm/| n.| 邋遢，不修边幅|The state or quality of being dowdy; a dowdy style or appearance.  A general unfashionable and unattractive appearance, especially in clothing.|Her dowdyism made her stand out in the fashionable crowd. 她在时髦的人群中因为不修边幅而显得格格不入。|近义词：shabbiness, frumpiness, unfashionableness|反义词：chic, elegance, stylishness|词性:noun

Etymology: From dowdy + -ism.  Dowdy itself is of uncertain origin, possibly related to "doughty" in the sense of "heavy, stout."
