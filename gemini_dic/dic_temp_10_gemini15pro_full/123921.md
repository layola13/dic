morendo:/məˈrɛndoʊ/ /mɔːˈrɛndoʊ/
noun
渐弱
A gradual decrease in tempo and volume of a piece of music.
The piece ends with a gentle morendo.  这首曲子以轻柔的渐弱结束。
Synonyms: diminuendo, decrescendo
Antonyms: crescendo
