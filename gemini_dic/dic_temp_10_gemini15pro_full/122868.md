mollifies:/ˈmɒlɪfaɪz/ /ˈmɑːlɪfaɪz/| v. | 平息；抚慰；缓和 | to make someone less angry or upset; to soothe or calm |  The manager tried to mollify the angry customer by offering a refund. 经理试图通过提供退款来安抚愤怒的顾客。

Her kind words mollified his hurt feelings. 她友善的话语抚慰了他受伤的感情。| synonyms: appease, placate, soothe, pacify, conciliate | antonyms: aggravate, provoke, irritate, exacerbate | verb
