/ˈkʌmli/| adj.|美丽的，清秀的，好看的|Pleasing in appearance; attractive.|She was a comely young woman with a pleasant smile. 她是一位美丽的年轻女子，面带愉悦的微笑。|近义词: attractive, beautiful, pretty, handsome, good-looking|反义词: ugly, unattractive, plain, homely|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *comli*, from Old English *cȳmlīc* (“comely, lovely, splendid, elegant, delicate, choice, noble, glorious”), from Proto-Germanic *kōmlīkaz* (“suitable, proper”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱem- (“to fit”).  Related to Old Frisian *kemlik* (“suitable”), Old Saxon *kōmlik* (“fitting”), Middle Dutch *coemeliic* (“comely”), Dutch *koemelijk* (“pleasant, comfortable”), Old High German *kuomalih* (“comely”), German *kömlich* (“comfortable, sufficient”), and Old Norse *hœmiligr* (“homely”).  The original sense of “fitting, suitable” is largely obsolete.
