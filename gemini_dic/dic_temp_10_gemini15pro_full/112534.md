love-ins:/ˈlʌvɪnz/
noun
集体示爱活动；爱之集会
A gathering or demonstration of love and peace, typically involving music, dancing, and other forms of artistic expression, popular especially in the 1960s.
There were several love-ins during the Summer of Love in 1967.  1967年的“爱之夏”期间举办了几场集体示爱活动。
The park hosted a love-in to promote peace and unity.  公园举办了一场集体示爱活动来促进和平与团结。

近义词:gathering, festival, celebration
反义词:protest, riot
