bread-and-butter:/ˈbred ən ˈbʌtər/| adj. n.|谋生的；基本的；主要的；面包和黄油|Relating to basic needs; providing a livelihood; simple or ordinary.  Also literally, bread and butter.|例句：Teaching is my bread-and-butter job. 教书是我的谋生之道。

bread-and-butter:/ˈbred ən ˈbʌtər/| n.|面包和黄油|Slices of bread spread with butter.|例句：We had bread and butter with our soup. 我们喝汤配面包和黄油。

例句：Bread and butter is a classic combination. 面包和黄油是经典搭配。|近义词：sandwich filling|反义词:None|词性:noun
