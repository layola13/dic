nonactive:/nɒnˈæktɪv/ | adj. | 不活跃的，不活动的，闲置的 | Not active; inactive.  Not engaging in or involving any or much physical activity. | The volcano has been nonactive for centuries. 这座火山几个世纪以来一直处于非活动状态。|  The nonactive members of the club haven't paid their dues.  俱乐部不活跃的成员还没有缴纳会费。 | 近义词: inactive, dormant, inert, passive | 反义词: active, energetic, lively | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- + active.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Active" comes from the Latin "activus," meaning "engaged in action."
