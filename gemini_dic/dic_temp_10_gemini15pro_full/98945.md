inter-choking:/ˌɪntərˈtʃoʊkɪŋ/
adjective
互相扼制; 互相阻塞
The act of two or more things obstructing or hindering each other, often in a restrictive or suffocating manner.  This word is a neologism, formed by combining "inter-" (meaning "between" or "among") and "choking."
The inter-choking vines made it impossible to navigate the jungle.  互相扼制的藤蔓使得在丛林中穿行变得不可能。
Near synonyms: inter-blocking, mutually obstructive, mutually inhibiting
Antonyms: facilitating, enabling
词性: adjective
