misoperation:/ˌmɪsˌɑːpəˈreɪʃn/ | n. |操作失误，误操作| An incorrect or faulty operation, especially of equipment or machinery. |  The plane crash was caused by a misoperation of the flight controls. 飞机失事是由飞行控制器的误操作造成的。

The misoperation of the machinery resulted in a serious accident.  机器的误操作导致了一起严重的事故。


|近义词：malfunction, error, mistake, blunder, slip-up |反义词: proper operation, correct handling |词性: noun
