boles:/boʊlz/| n. |树干，躯干|The boles of the ancient trees were massive. 古树的树干很粗壮。|Example: The logger measured the boles of the felled trees.  伐木工人测量了砍伐的树木的树干。

Example:  The smooth, grey boles of the beech trees stood out against the dark green foliage.  光滑灰色的山毛榉树干在深绿色的树叶衬托下格外显眼。|Synonyms: trunks, stems|Antonyms: branches, twigs|noun
