geek-out:/ɡik aʊt/| vi. n.|沉迷于，对…狂热；尽情畅谈|To become overly enthusiastic about a particular topic, typically one of specialist or niche interest.  It can also refer to the instance of such enthusiasm.|例句：He geeked out over the new Star Wars movie. 他对新的星球大战电影非常着迷。

例句：We had a total geek-out about vintage computers. 我们尽情畅谈了老式电脑。|近义词：obsess over, rave about, nerd out|反义词: be indifferent to, be apathetic to|词性:不及物动词, 名词

词源: "Geek" originally referred to a carnival performer, often involving bizarre acts. Over time, it evolved to mean someone with intense, often eccentric, interests.  "Out" in this context intensifies the verb, suggesting a full expression of this enthusiasm.
