chares:/ˈkɛəriːz/| n. |家务,杂务；差事,任务|Chores are routine tasks, especially household tasks. |例句：I hate doing chores around the house. 我讨厌做家务。|例句：He has several chores to complete before he can go out and play. 他有几项差事要做完才能出去玩。|近义词：tasks, duties, errands, jobs|反义词:leisure, fun|noun
