chloasma:/kləˈæzmə/ /kloʊˈæzmə/
noun
黄褐斑，妊娠斑
A tan or dark skin discoloration, particularly common during pregnancy.
Example 1:  The dermatologist diagnosed her skin condition as chloasma. 皮肤科医生诊断她的皮肤状况为黄褐斑。

Example 2:  Hormonal changes during pregnancy can cause chloasma. 怀孕期间的荷尔蒙变化会导致黄褐斑。
synonym:melasma, hyperpigmentation
antonym:hypopigmentation
n.
