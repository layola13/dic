dilative:/daɪˈleɪtɪv/ /ˈdaɪləˌteɪtɪv/
adjective
扩张的，膨胀的
Causing or tending to cause dilation.  Enlarging; expanding.
例句：The doctor prescribed dilative eye drops. 医生开了扩张瞳孔的眼药水。
例句：The yoga instructor guided us through a series of dilative breathing exercises.  瑜伽教练指导我们做了一系列扩张呼吸的练习。
近义词：expanding, widening, enlarging, stretching
反义词：constricting, contracting, shrinking, compressing
词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin *dīlātīvus, from dīlāt-, past participle stem of dīlātāre (“to spread out, extend, enlarge, amplify”), from dī- + lātus (“wide, broad”).
