extenuatory:/ɪkˈstɛnjuːətɔːri/ /ɪkˈstɛnjuːəˌtɔri/
adjective
减轻罪责的; 情有可原的
Lessening the seriousness of a wrong or an offense.
例句：The jury found extenuatory circumstances in the defendant's difficult upbringing.  陪审团认为被告的艰难成长环境是情有可原的。

例句：There were extenuating circumstances which caused her to arrive late. 有一些情有可原的情况导致她迟到。


近义词：mitigating,  palliating,  excusable
反义词：incriminating,  aggravating
词性: adjective

词源：From Late Latin *extenuatorius, from Latin extenuare (“to make thin, lessen, weaken”), from ex- (“out”) + tenuis (“thin, slight”).
