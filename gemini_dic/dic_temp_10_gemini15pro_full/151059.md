paraphrast: /ˈpærəˌfreɪst/ | n. |释义者，解释者；改述者|One who paraphrases, especially one who paraphrases texts or musical works.  A person who expresses the meaning of something written or spoken using different words, especially in order to achieve greater clarity. |The paraphrast skillfully rendered the complex poem into modern language. 这位释义者巧妙地将这首复杂的诗歌译成了现代语言。| synonyms: interpreter, expounder, translator, commentator | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "distorter" or "misinterpreter") | noun
