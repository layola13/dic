despotical: /dɪˈspɒtɪkəl/ /dɪˈspaːtɪkəl/ | adj. | 专制的，暴虐的 | Of or relating to a despot; tyrannical. Characterized by absolute power and arbitrary or oppressive control. |  The despotical ruler showed no mercy to his subjects.  这个专制的统治者对他的臣民毫不留情。

The despotical regime imprisoned anyone who dared to speak out. 这个暴虐的政权监禁了任何敢于直言的人。 | 近义词: tyrannical, autocratic, dictatorial, authoritarian | 反义词: democratic, liberal, egalitarian | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From French *despotique*, from Late Latin *despoticus*, from Greek *despotikos* (“of a master”), from *despótēs* (“master, lord”).
