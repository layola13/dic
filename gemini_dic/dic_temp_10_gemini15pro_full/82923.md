gumminesses: /ˈɡʌminɪsɪz/
n.
gummy candies (plural)
软糖（复数）
These gumminesses are shaped like bears. 这些软糖的形状像熊。
近义词：gummy bears, gummies, jelly candies
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a type of candy)


gumminess: /ˈɡʌminəs/
n.
the quality of being gummy; stickiness
粘性；胶粘性
The gumminess of the candy made it difficult to chew.  这糖果的粘性使它难以咀嚼。
近义词：stickiness, adhesiveness, tackiness
反义词: dryness, brittleness


gummy: /ˈɡʌmi/
adj.
sticky; like gum
粘的；像胶一样的
The child's hands were gummy from eating candy. 孩子的手因为吃了糖果而发粘。
近义词：sticky, adhesive, tacky
反义词: dry, brittle
