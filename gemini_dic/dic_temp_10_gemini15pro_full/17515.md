beasts:/biːsts/ | n. | 野兽；动物（尤指体型庞大或危险的）；凶残的人；令人厌恶的人 |  Wild animals considered collectively; mammals of a large or impressive size and appearance, especially one that is considered dangerous; a cruel and savage person; a person who behaves very badly | The hunter tracked the beasts through the forest. 猎人追踪野兽穿过森林。| 近义词: animals, creatures, monsters, brutes | 反义词: humans,  pets | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bestes*, from Old French *bestes* (plural of *beste*), from Latin *bestia* ("beast, animal").
