holonic:/hɒˈlɒnɪk/ /hoʊˈlɑːnɪk/ | adj. |整体的，具有整体性的|  Relating to or characteristic of a holon, a system that is simultaneously a whole and a part of a larger system. | A holonic manufacturing system is designed to be flexible and adaptable.  一个整体制造系统被设计成灵活和可适应的。 |近义词：modular, hierarchical, integrated |反义词: monolithic, centralized |词性:形容词

holon:/ˈhɒlɒn/ /ˈhoʊlɑːn/ | n. |整体，子整体| Something that is simultaneously a whole and a part of a larger system. |  A cell is a holon, being a whole in itself but also a part of a larger organism. 一个细胞是一个子整体，它本身是一个整体，但也是一个更大有机体的一部分。 |近义词：module, component, unit |反义词: whole, entirety |词性:名词
