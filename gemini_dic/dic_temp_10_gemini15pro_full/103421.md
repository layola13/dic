jitteriest:/ˈdʒɪtəriɪst/
adjective
最紧张的，最不安的
Superlative adjective of jittery: most jittery.  Characterized by extreme nervousness or anxiety.
Her voice was small and jittery as she addressed the crowd. 她面对人群时，声音细小而颤抖。
The jitteriest contestant forgot his lines.  最紧张的选手忘了他的台词。
近义词:nerviest, most anxious, edgiest
反义词:calmest, most relaxed, most composed
词性:adjective

jitter:/ˈdʒɪtər/
noun
紧张不安，抖动
A state of nervousness or anxiety. Also, a slight irregular movement, variation, or oscillation.
He had the jitters before his performance.  演出前他很紧张。
There was a jitter in the video signal. 视频信号有抖动。
近义词: anxiety, nervousness, tremor, flicker
反义词:calmness, stillness, stability
词性:noun

jitter:/ˈdʒɪtər/
verb
紧张不安，抖动
To act or move nervously or erratically; to tremble slightly.
He jittered as he waited for the results. 他等待结果时紧张不安。
The image on the screen jittered. 屏幕上的图像抖动。
近义词: fidget, tremble, quiver
反义词:relax, steady, stabilize
词性:verb


词源:
From the noun "jitter," which originated in the early 20th century, possibly as an alteration of "chitter," meaning to tremble or chatter with the teeth.
