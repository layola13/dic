neutering:/ˈnjuːtərɪŋ/ /ˈnuːtərɪŋ/| n.|绝育，阉割|The neutering of pets is important for population control. 宠物绝育对控制数量很重要。|Example:  Neutering your cat can prevent unwanted kittens. 给你的猫做绝育手术可以防止不必要的生育。

Example:  Low-cost neutering programs are available in many cities. 许多城市都有低成本的绝育项目。|Synonym: castration, spaying (specifically for females), desexing|Antonym: breeding|noun
