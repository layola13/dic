excursioned:/ɪkˈskɜːrʒənd/
verb
去旅行；远足
having gone on an excursion; traveled
They excursioned to the coast for the weekend.  他们周末去海边旅行了。
We excursioned through the mountains. 我们在山里远足。
近义词: toured, journeyed, traveled
反义词: stayed, remained
