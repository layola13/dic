bitcoin:/ˈbɪtkɔɪn/ | n. | 比特币 | A decentralized digital currency, without a central bank or single administrator, that can be sent from user to user on the peer-to-peer bitcoin network without the need for intermediaries. | Bitcoin is becoming increasingly popular as an investment. 比特币作为一种投资越来越受欢迎。| synonyms: cryptocurrency, virtual currency, digital currency | antonyms: fiat currency, physical currency | noun
