gonged:/ɡɒŋd/ (British English), /ɡɔːŋd/ (American English)| v. |被锣声赶下台；被淘汰；被解雇|To be dismissed or interrupted by the striking of a gong, often in a comedic or dramatic context; can also be used metaphorically to describe a sudden and decisive end to something.| The comedian was gonged off stage after his jokes fell flat.  那个喜剧演员因为笑话不好笑而被锣声赶下台。|  The project was gonged after the company ran out of funding.  公司资金耗尽后，该项目被叫停了。|synonyms: dismissed, rejected, ejected, terminated|antonyms: accepted, approved, continued|verb
