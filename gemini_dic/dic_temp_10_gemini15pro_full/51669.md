devil:/ˈdevəl/ (US), /ˈdɛvəl/ (UK)| n.|魔鬼，恶魔;家伙，淘气鬼;不幸，倒霉事|A malevolent supernatural being, often associated with evil and temptation.  Also used informally to refer to a mischievous or troublesome person, or a difficult situation.|例句：The devil made me do it!  是魔鬼让我做的！

|例句：He's a little devil, always getting into trouble. 他是个小淘气鬼，总是惹麻烦。

|近义词：demon, fiend, Satan, imp, rascal, scoundrel|反义词：angel, god|词性：名词
