graze:/ɡreɪz/| n. v.|擦伤，轻擦; 放牧，吃草|A slight injury where the skin is scraped; the act of grazing.|He suffered a graze on his knee. 他的膝盖擦伤了。 The sheep were put out to graze. 羊被放出去吃草。|近义词：(n.) scratch, abrasion (v.) browse, nibble, scrape, skim|反义词: (v.) starve|词性:名词, 动词

词源: From Old English *græsian, from græs "grass".
