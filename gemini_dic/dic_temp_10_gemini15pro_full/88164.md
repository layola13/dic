hillock:/ˈhɪlək/| n.|小山丘；小土堆|A small hill or mound.|例句：The children rolled down the grassy hillock. 孩子们从长满草的小山丘上滚下来。

例句：A hillock offered a vantage point to survey the surrounding landscape.  一个小山丘提供了一个有利位置来俯瞰周围的景观。|近义词：knoll, mound, hummock|反义词:valley, depression|词性:noun

词源:
From Middle English *hilloke, from Old English *hylloc, diminutive of hyll (“hill”).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *hulniz (“hill, knoll”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱel- (“to rise, be elevated”).
