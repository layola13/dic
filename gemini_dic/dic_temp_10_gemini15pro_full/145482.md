overclemency: /ˌoʊvərˈklɛmənsi/ | n. | 过度宽容，过度仁慈 | Excessive leniency or mercy; showing too much forgiveness or understanding, especially towards someone who has committed a wrong. | The judge was accused of overclemency in sentencing the repeat offender.  法官因对累犯的判决过于宽大而受到指责。|  Synonyms: leniency, indulgence, forbearance | Antonyms:  severity, harshness, strictness | noun
