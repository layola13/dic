lubricates:/ˈluːbrɪkeɪts/| v. |润滑，使润滑|applies a lubricant to (something) in order to reduce friction between its moving parts. |例句：The mechanic lubricates the engine regularly.  机械师定期润滑发动机。

例句：Saliva lubricates the food, making it easier to swallow. 唾液润滑食物，使其更容易吞咽。|近义词：oil, grease, anoint|反义词:dry, roughen|词性:动词

词源: From Latin lubricus "slippery, smooth," related to labi "to glide, slip."
