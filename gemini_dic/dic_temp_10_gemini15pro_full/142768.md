ottomite:/ˈɑtəmˌaɪt/ | n. | 奥托米人；奥托米语 | A member of an indigenous people of central and southern Mexico, or their language. |  The Ottomite people have a rich cultural heritage. 奥托米人拥有丰富的文化遗产。

| The museum showcased beautiful examples of Ottomite pottery.  博物馆展示了精美的奥托米陶器。 | synonyms: Otomi (more common and preferred) | antonyms: N/A | noun
