catbird:/ˈkætˌbɜːrd/ (US), /ˈkætˌbəːd/ (UK)| n.|猫鹊，灰猫鸟|A North American songbird ( *Dumetella carolinensis*) known for its catlike meows and ability to mimic other birds' songs.|例句：The catbird mimicked the robin's song perfectly. 猫鹊完美地模仿了知更鸟的歌声。

例句：We spotted a catbird hiding in the bushes. 我们发现一只猫鹊藏在灌木丛里。|近义词：mockingbird, mimic thrush|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *cat* + *bird*, referring to the catlike meowing call of the bird.
