amplitude:/ˈæmplɪtjuːd/| n.| 幅度，振幅；广阔，广大|The amplitude of the ocean waves was impressive. 海浪的振幅令人印象深刻。|magnitude, extent, range, scope|smallness, narrowness|noun
Etymology-> From Latin *amplitūdō*, from *amplus* (“large, spacious, of great compass") + *-itūdō* (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality).
