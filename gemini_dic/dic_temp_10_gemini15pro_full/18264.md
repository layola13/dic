belime:/bɪˈlaɪm/ | v. | 污蔑，诽谤，中伤 | To falsely and maliciously accuse someone of wrongdoing. |  He belimed his opponent's reputation with false accusations. 他用虚假的指控诽谤了他的对手的名声。

| Example:  The newspaper article belimed the mayor, accusing him of corruption without any evidence. 这篇报纸文章诽谤了市长，毫无根据地指责他腐败。| Synonym: slander, defame, libel, smear, calumniate | Antonym: praise, commend, extol, laud | word type: verb
