exacerbation:/ɪɡˌzæsərˈbeɪʃən/ | n. | 加剧，恶化 | an increase in the severity of a disease or its symptoms | Stress can lead to an exacerbation of his condition. 压力会导致他的病情恶化。| aggravation, intensification | improvement, alleviation | noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from late Latin exacerbatio(n-), from the verb exacerbare ‘irritate’.
