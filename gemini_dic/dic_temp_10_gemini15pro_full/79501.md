globbiest:/ˈɡlɒbiɪst/| adj.|最粘稠的，最粘糊糊的|Superlative form of "globby," meaning extremely sticky, viscous, or gelatinous.|The globbiest slime I've ever touched was at the science museum. 我在科学博物馆摸到的粘液是我摸过最粘的。|近义词：stickiest, gooiest, slimiest|反义词：runniest, thinnest|词性:形容词
词源: From "glob" + "-y" + "-est."  "Glob" likely imitative of the sound of a thick liquid dropping. "-y" forms adjectives indicating having the quality of. "-est" is the superlative suffix.
