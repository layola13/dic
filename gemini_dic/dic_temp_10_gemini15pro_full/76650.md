gals:/ɡælz/ | n. |女孩们;姑娘们（gal的复数）|Informal, often humorous plural of gal.  It refers to a group of females. |例句：The gals went out for a night on the town. 姑娘们晚上去城里玩了。

|例句：Those gals sure know how to have fun. 那些姑娘们确实知道怎么玩得开心。
