nominalised:/ˈnɒmɪnəlaɪzd/ | adj. |名词化的；名词化了的|Converted into a noun or noun phrase.  The process of forming a noun from a verb or adjective is called nominalisation.|The nominalised form of "run" is "running".  “Run”的名词化形式是“running”.| 近义词: nouned | 反义词: verbalised |词性:形容词
