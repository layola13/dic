oversmooth:/ˌoʊvərˈsmuːð/| adj.| 过度平滑的；过度平滑化|Made too smooth, often losing important details or variations in the process.  It implies an excessive application of smoothing techniques, resulting in a loss of desirable characteristics.|The oversmoothed image lacked detail and looked artificial.|近义词：overrefined, overprocessed|反义词: rough, jagged|词性:形容词

Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "smooth" (adjective meaning "having an even and regular surface; free from projections or indentations")
