decollectivized:/ˌdiːkəˈlɛktɪvaɪzd/
Verb
去集体化的，非集体化的
Removed from collective ownership or control; made individual or private.
The farms were decollectivized in the early 1990s.
20世纪90年代初，农场实行了去集体化。
deprivatized, individualized
