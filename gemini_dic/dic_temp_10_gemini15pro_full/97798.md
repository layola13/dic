injure:/ˈɪndʒər/| v.| 伤害，损害|To do physical harm or damage to someone or something.|The accident injured several people. 事故中有几人受伤。|近义词：harm, damage, wound, impair|反义词: heal, repair, benefit|词性:动词

Etymology: From Old French  *enjurer*, from Latin *iniuriari* "to do wrong to, inflict injury upon," from *in- "not" + *iuria* "right, law."
