epiotic:/ˌɛpaɪˈɒtɪk/ | adj. | 上鳃的，耳上的 | Situated above or on the ear.  Relating to the epiotic bone, a bone in the skull of many vertebrates that forms part of the otic capsule. |  The epiotic bone forms the upper posterior part of the otic capsule. 上鳃骨形成耳囊的后上部。|  The epiotic process is a prominent feature in some fish species. 上鳃突是某些鱼类的显著特征。|Synonyms: supraotic| Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps ventral to the ear)| Adjective
