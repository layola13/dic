debonairty:/ˌdɛbəˈnɛəti/
n.
温文尔雅；潇洒
confident, stylish, and charming manner
He exuded an air of debonairty. 他举手投足间流露出潇洒的气质。
Near Synonyms:  suavity, urbanity, polish, charm, grace, elegance
Antonyms: awkwardness, clumsiness, gaucheness
noun
