one-way:/ˌwʌnˈweɪ/ | adj. adv. |单程的，单向的|Moving or operating in only one direction.|例句：I booked a one-way ticket to Paris. 我订了一张去巴黎的单程票。|例句：The street is one-way. 这条街是单行道。|近义词：unidirectional, single, outbound (for tickets)|反义词：round-trip, return, two-way|词性:形容词/副词

one-way:/ˌwʌnˈweɪ/ | n. |单行道|A street or road on which traffic travels in only one direction.|例句：Be careful, this is a one-way.  小心，这是单行道。|例句：He turned the wrong way down a one-way. 他在单行道上拐错了方向。|近义词：one-way street|反义词：two-way street|词性:名词
