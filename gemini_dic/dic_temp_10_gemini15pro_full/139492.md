obstruct:/əbˈstrʌkt/
verb
阻碍，妨碍
to block or get in the way of something or someone
They were charged with obstructing justice. 他们被指控妨碍司法公正。
Synonyms: block, hinder, impede
Antonyms: aid, assist, facilitate
词性: 动词

词源:
From Latin obstructus, past participle of obstruere "to build in front of, block up, stop up," from ob "against" (see ob-) + struere "to pile, build" (see structure (n.)).
