half-proven:/ˌhæf ˈpruːvən/
adjective
半证实的，未完全证实的
Partially proven; not fully substantiated or confirmed.  The evidence is half-proven, so we need to investigate further.  证据只是半证实的，所以我们需要进一步调查。
partly proven, partially substantiated
fully proven, completely substantiated
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
"half-" (Old English healf) + "proven" (past participle of "prove," from Old French prover, from Latin probare "to test, approve")
