PHRASE->
(Since "darin" is archaic, it's unlikely to be used in modern phrases.  However, using its modern equivalent "therein" we can create examples.)

1. The devil is in the details, and the solution lies therein.  细节决定成败，答案就在其中。
2. The contract is complex, and therein lies the problem.  合同很复杂，问题就在于此。
3. He found the answer, and therein lay his salvation. 他找到了答案，他的救赎就在于此。
4. The document outlines the rules, and all members must abide therein. 文件概述了规则，所有成员都必须遵守。
5. She found comfort in the scriptures, and therein found peace. 她在经文中找到了安慰，并在其中找到了平静。


注意事项:

这个词语现在已经很少使用，主要用于一些方言或古英语中。在现代英语中，使用"therein," "within," 或 "inside" 更为常见。
