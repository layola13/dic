berming:/ˈbɜːmɪŋ/| n.|护堤，筑堤，堤岸，斜坡|The process of constructing a berm, a raised bank or path, especially at the edge of a road or canal.  It can also refer to the berm itself.|The berming along the canal helped prevent flooding. 运河沿线的筑堤有助于防止洪水泛滥。|近义词：embankment, levee,  bund|反义词:excavation, ditch|noun
