eloin:/ɪˈlɔɪn/ | vt. | 使远离；使疏远 | To remove or separate (someone or something) from something else. |  He was eloined from his family by the war.  他因战争与家人分离。 | 近义词: remove, separate, banish, exile | 反义词: unite, join, connect | 词性: 动词

词源: From Old French *esloinier, from esloin "far, distant," from Vulgar Latin *exlongāre, from Latin ex- "out" + longus "long."
