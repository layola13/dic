hygrophthalmic:/ˌhaɪɡrɒfˈθælmɪk/ | adj. |  吸湿的；湿润的 |  Affected by moisture; readily absorbing moisture from the surrounding atmosphere.  Relating to or characterized by hygrophthalmy (swelling of tissues due to moisture absorption). | The hygrophthalmic nature of the material made it unsuitable for outdoor use. 这种材料的吸湿性使其不适合户外使用。 |  hydroscopic, moisture-absorbing | water-repellent, waterproof | adjective
