ordeal:/ɔːrˈdiːl/ | n. | 磨难，严峻考验 | A difficult or painful experience, a severe test. |  He went through a terrible ordeal when he was captured by bandits. 他被土匪抓住后经历了一场可怕的磨难。

She faced the ordeal of giving evidence in court. 她面临着在法庭上作证的严峻考验。

| 近义词：trial, tribulation, hardship, test | 反义词：comfort, ease, pleasure | 词性：名词
