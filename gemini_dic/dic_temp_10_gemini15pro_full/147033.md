overlauding:/ˌoʊvərˈlɔːdɪŋ/ | v. | 过分称赞；过誉 | Praising excessively; giving too much praise. | He was overlauding her performance, perhaps to gain favor. 他过分称赞她的表演，可能是为了讨好她。| The critic was accused of overlauding the mediocre film.  评论家被指责过誉了这部平庸的电影。| 近义词：overpraising, exaggerating, gushing | 反义词：understating, criticizing, downplaying | 词性: 动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "laud" (verb meaning "to praise")
