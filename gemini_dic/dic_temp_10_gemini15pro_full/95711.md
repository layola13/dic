incongruously: /ˌɪnkɒŋˈɡruːəsli/ (UK) /ˌɪnkɑːŋˈɡruːəsli/ (US)
| adv. | 不协调地，不适宜地，不相称地 | in a way that seems strange because it is different from other things around it or from what is generally expected |  He was wearing a suit, incongruously paired with sneakers. 他穿着一套西装，不协调地搭配着运动鞋。| synonyms: inappropriately, unsuitably, oddly, bizarrely, unexpectedly | antonyms: appropriately, suitably, fittingly, harmoniously | 词性: 副词

incongruous: /ɪnˈkɒŋɡruəs/ (UK) /ɪnˈkɑːŋɡruːəs/ (US)
| adj. | 不协调的，不适宜的，不相称的 | not in harmony or keeping with the surroundings or other aspects of something | The modern sculpture looked incongruous in the ancient temple. 现代雕塑在古老的寺庙中显得格格不入。| synonyms: inappropriate, unsuited, unsuitable, out of place, out of keeping, inharmonious, discordant, clashing, jarring | antonyms: appropriate, suitable, fitting, harmonious, concordant | 词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From Late Latin *incongruus, from in- "not" + congruus "agreeing, suitable".
