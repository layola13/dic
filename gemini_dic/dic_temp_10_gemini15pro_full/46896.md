darkeners:/ˈdɑːrkənərz/
n.|变暗的东西；使变暗的物质|Substances or things that cause something to become darker.|The photographer used darkeners to reduce the glare in the photo. 摄影师使用遮光剂来减少照片中的眩光。|synonyms: dimmers, obscurers|antonyms: brighteners, lighteners|noun

darkener:/ˈdɑːrkənər/
n.|变暗的东西；使变暗的物质|A substance or thing that darkens something else.|She added a fabric darkener to the dye bath to achieve a deeper shade of black.  她向染缸中添加了一种织物加深剂，以获得更深的黑色。|synonyms: dimmer, shader|antonyms: brightener, lightener|noun
