expurgators:/ˈekspərˌɡeɪtərz/
|n.|净化者；删节者；修订者|People who remove objectionable material from books, films, etc.  Those who cleanse or purify something, often texts, by removing offensive or inappropriate content.|The expurgators worked tirelessly to remove all traces of blasphemy from the ancient manuscripts. 净化者们孜孜不倦地工作，从古代手稿中删除所有亵渎神明的痕迹。|Synonyms: censors, editors, bowdlerizers, purifiers|Antonyms: corrupters, contaminators|noun
