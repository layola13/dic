hittable:/ˈhɪtəbl/| adj.|可击中的，可命中的|Able to be hit.  Something that is susceptible to being struck or impacted.|The target was small and not very hittable.  目标很小，很难击中。|近义词：vulnerable, exposed, reachable|反义词:invulnerable, untouchable, protected|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "hit" + "-able". "Hit" comes from Old English *hittan.  "-able" is a suffix of Latin origin meaning "capable of being".
