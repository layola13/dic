earthbank:/ˈɜːrθˌbæŋk/ | n. | 土堤，土岸 | A bank made of earth.  A raised mass of earth, especially one made artificially to form a defense or to support a road or railway. |  The farmer built an earthbank to prevent flooding.  农民修建了土堤以防止洪水。 | synonym: embankment, levee, mound | antonym: ditch, trench | noun
