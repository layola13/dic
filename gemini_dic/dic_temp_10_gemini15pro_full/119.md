abalones:/ˈæbəˌloʊniz/ (US) /ˈæbəˌləʊniːz/ (UK)| n.|鲍鱼|Abalones are large, edible sea snails with a flat, oval shell lined with mother-of-pearl.  They are considered a delicacy in many cultures.|We dined on fresh abalones at the seaside restaurant. 我们在海边餐厅吃了新鲜的鲍鱼。

The abalones were farmed sustainably. 这些鲍鱼是可持续养殖的。|近义词：sea snails, ear shells|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a type of shellfish)|词性:Noun
