hardcover:/ˈhɑːrdˌkʌvər/ (US), /ˈhɑːdkʌvə(r)/ (UK)| n. adj.|精装本；精装的|A book binding with stiff, durable covers.  A book that has a hardcover.|例句：I prefer hardcovers because they last longer. 我更喜欢精装书，因为它们更耐用。|近义词：hardbound, casebound|反义词:paperback, softcover|词性:名词, 形容词
Etymology:  "hard" + "cover". "Hard" meaning firm and rigid, and "cover" referring to the outer protective layer of a book.
