overslowly:/ˈoʊvərˈsloʊli/
adverb
太慢地，过分缓慢地
Moving or operating at a speed that is excessively slow.
The car moved overslowly through the traffic jam. 汽车在拥堵的交通中行驶得非常缓慢。
He overslowly turned the pages of the ancient book. 他极其缓慢地翻阅着那本古书。
近义词: too slowly, excessively slowly, very slowly
反义词: quickly, rapidly, swiftly
词性: adverb

词源:
"over-" (prefix denoting excess) + "slowly" (adverb)
