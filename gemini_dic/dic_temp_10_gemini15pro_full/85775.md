hayfield:/ˈheɪfiːld/| n.|干草地，牧草地|A field where grass or other plants are grown to be made into hay.|The farmer cut the hay in his hayfield every summer. 农民每年夏天都在他的牧草地割干草。|近义词：meadow, pasture, grassland|反义词:|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hēgfeld (attested as oblique case hēgfelde), from hēg "hay" + feld "field".
