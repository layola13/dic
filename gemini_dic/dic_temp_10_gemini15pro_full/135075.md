nonnutrient:/ˌnɒnˈnjuːtriənt/ | /ˌnɑːnˈnuːtriənt/ | adj. n. | 非营养的；非营养物质 | Not providing nourishment.  A substance that does not provide nourishment. | Example (adj.):  Artificial sweeteners are nonnutrient additives.  人造甜味剂是非营养添加剂。

Example (n.):  Many processed foods contain nonnutrients. 许多加工食品含有非营养物质。 | Synonym (adj.): non-nutritive, innutritious | Synonym (n.): additive,  ingredient (sometimes) | Antonym (adj.): nutrient, nutritive, nourishing | Antonym (n.): nutrient | 词性: 形容词, 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + nutrient (from Latin nutriēns, present participle of nutrire "to nourish").
