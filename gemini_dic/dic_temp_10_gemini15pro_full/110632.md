limpingness:/ˈlɪmpɪŋnɪs/| n.|跛行，蹒跚|The state or quality of limping.  The noticeable and persistent limping gait affected his confidence. |例句：His limpingness was apparent after the injury. 他的跛行在受伤后很明显。

例句：The dog's limpingness was caused by a thorn in its paw. 狗的跛行是由于爪子里有刺造成的。


|近义词：lame, halting, hobble, claudication|反义词：agility, soundness, swiftness|词性: noun


Etymology:
From "limping" + "-ness"
"limping" comes from the Middle English "limpen," from Old English "limpan," meaning "to go lamely."
"-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, denoting a state, quality, or condition.
