epichirema:/ˌɛpɪˈkaɪriːmə/
|n.|复杂三段论；带有证明的推论；|A rhetorical syllogism in which the premises are supported by reasons. A complex argument in which the major and minor premises are themselves supported by other arguments.|The lawyer presented a compelling epichirema, meticulously laying out the evidence and legal precedents to support his client's claim. 律师提出了一个令人信服的复杂三段论，一丝不苟地列出了证据和法律先例来支持其当事人的主张。|Synonyms: chain argument, polysyllogism, sorites|Antonyms: N/A|noun
