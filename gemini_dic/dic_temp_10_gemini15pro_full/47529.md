deathblow:/ˈdɛθbloʊ/ (US) /ˈdɛθbləʊ/ (UK)
noun
致命一击，致命打击
A decisive act or event that causes something to fail or end completely.
The loss of their biggest client dealt a deathblow to the small company.  失去他们最大的客户对这家小公司来说是致命一击。
The scandal delivered a deathblow to his political career.  这桩丑闻给了他的政治生涯致命一击。
coup de grâce, finishing stroke, final blow
survival, continuation
