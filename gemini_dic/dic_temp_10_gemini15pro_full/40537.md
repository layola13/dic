controverser:/ˈkɒntrəvɜːsə(r)| n. | 争论者，辩论者 |  A person who engages in controversy or disputes; a disputant. | He was a known controverser, always ready for a heated debate. 他是一个有名的辩论者，随时准备进行激烈的辩论。| 近义词:disputant, debater, arguer, polemicist | 反义词:proponent, supporter, advocate | 词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *controversator, from Latin controversari "to dispute, debate," from controversus "turned against, disputed," from contra "against" + versus, past participle of vertere "to turn."
