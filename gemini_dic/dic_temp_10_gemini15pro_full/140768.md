omnivory:/ˈɒmnɪvɔːri/ | n. | 杂食性 | The practice of consuming both animal and plant matter as food. | Many primates exhibit omnivory, adapting their diets to available resources.  许多灵长类动物都表现出杂食性，使它们的饮食适应可用的资源。 | 近义词: polyphagy | 反义词: herbivory, carnivory | noun

omnivorous:/ɒmˈnɪvərəs/ | adj. | 杂食的 |  Feeding on both animal and plant substances. | Bears are omnivorous creatures, consuming berries, fish, and insects. 熊是杂食动物，吃浆果、鱼和昆虫。 | 近义词: polyphagous | 反义词: herbivorous, carnivorous | adjective
