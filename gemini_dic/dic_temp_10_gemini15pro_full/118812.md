metamorphize:/ˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfaɪz/ | v. | 变形，变质 | To change greatly and completely, usually into a more advanced or mature state. | The caterpillar metamorphized into a beautiful butterfly. 毛毛虫变成了美丽的蝴蝶。| synonyms: transform, convert, transmute | antonyms: stagnate, fix, remain | verb

metamorphize:/ˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfaɪz/ | n. | 变形，变质 | The process of changing greatly and completely, usually into a more advanced or mature state. | The rock underwent a slow metamorphize over millions of years.  岩石在数百万年的时间里经历了缓慢的变质。 | synonyms: transformation, conversion, transmutation | antonyms: stagnation, fixation | noun
