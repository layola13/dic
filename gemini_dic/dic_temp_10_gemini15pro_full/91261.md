hydrofoil:/ˈhaɪdrəfɔɪl/ (US), /ˈhaɪdrəfɔɪl/ (UK)| n.|水翼船；水翼|A hydrofoil is a wing-like structure mounted on struts below the hull of a boat or ship. As the craft gains speed, the hydrofoils lift the hull out of the water, reducing drag and allowing greater speeds.|The hydrofoil skimmed across the bay. 水翼船掠过海湾。|近义词：wingship|反义词:|noun

hydrofoil:/ˈhaɪdrəfɔɪl/ (US), /ˈhaɪdrəfɔɪl/ (UK)| v.|用水翼推进|To move through the water on hydrofoils.|The boat hydrofoiled across the lake. 这艘船用水翼划过湖面。|近义词:|反义词:|verb
