fluvial:/ˈfluːviəl/| adj.|河流的，由河流作用形成的|Of, relating to, or produced by rivers.|例句：Fluvial deposits are sediments laid down by a river. 河流沉积物是由河流沉积下来的。|例句：The fluvial erosion shaped the valley over millions of years. 数百万年来，河流侵蚀塑造了山谷。|近义词：riverine, alluvial|反义词：marine, oceanic|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *fluvialis*, from *fluvius* ("river")
