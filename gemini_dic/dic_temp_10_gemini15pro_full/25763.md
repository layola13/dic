burbs:/bɜːrbz/
noun
郊区
The suburbs are residential areas outside of a city center.
We live in the burbs about 30 minutes from the city. 我们住在离市中心大约30分钟车程的郊区。
suburbia, outskirts
city center, urban area
n.
