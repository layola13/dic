nancees:/nænˈsiːz/| n.| 娘娘腔的男人（复数）; 懦夫（复数）|A derogatory term for effeminate or unmasculine men, often implying weakness or cowardice.|  They called him a nancee because he didn't like sports. 他们因为他不喜欢运动而叫他娘娘腔。| 近义词：sissy, wimp, coward | 反义词: macho, brave, masculine | 词性: 名词

词源:  Likely a variant of "nancy," itself derived from the female given name "Nancy," used as a derogatory slang term for an effeminate man since the early 20th century.  Its plural form, "nancees," simply indicates more than one such individual.
