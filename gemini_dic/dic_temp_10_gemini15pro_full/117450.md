meditations:/ˌmedɪˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 冥想，沉思，思考 |  A series of thoughts, reflections, or contemplations on a particular subject, often of a religious or philosophical nature.  | She practices daily meditations to find inner peace. 她每天练习冥想以找到内心的平静。 | Example: His meditations on the meaning of life led him to a new career path. 他对人生意义的思考让他走上了一条新的职业道路。 | Synonyms: contemplations, reflections, musings, ponderings | Antonyms: distractions, frivolities | Noun
