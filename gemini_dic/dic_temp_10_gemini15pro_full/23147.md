bout:/baʊt/
n.
一回合，较量; 一阵; 发作;
a short period of intense activity of a specified kind.
例句：He had a bout of flu. 他得了流感。
例句：The two boxers were evenly matched during the first bout.  两位拳击手在第一回合势均力敌。
近义词：spell, period, fit, attack
反义词：lull, respite
词性: 名词

bout:/baʊt/
prep. (古用法，等于 about)
关于
例句：(Archaic) He knew naught bout it. (古)他对此一无所知。
例句：(Archaic) 'Twas bout time. (古)是时候了。
近义词：about, concerning, regarding
反义词： 无
词性: 介词 (主要做名词使用)
