hrushchevsky:/ˌkruːˈʃɔːfski/ /ˌkruːˈʃɛfski/ | n. (proper noun) |赫鲁晓夫|  Referring to Nikita Khrushchev (1894-1971), the former Premier of the Soviet Union, or anything related to his policies or era.  Derived from his surname, "Khrushchev".|  The Khrushchevsky Thaw refers to the period of de-Stalinization in the Soviet Union. 赫鲁晓夫解冻指的是苏联去斯大林化时期。| synonym:  Khrushchev,  (for the era) post-Stalinist | antonym: Stalinist | proper noun
