cable-car:/ˈkeɪ.bl̩ kɑːr/ (US) /ˈkeɪ.bl̩ kɑː(r)/ (UK)| n. |缆车；索道|A vehicle suspended from a continuously circulating cable and used for transporting passengers up and down steep inclines, especially mountains.|We took a cable-car up the mountain. 我们乘缆车上了山。|近义词：gondola, aerial tramway|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "bus" or "train" for alternative transportation) |noun
