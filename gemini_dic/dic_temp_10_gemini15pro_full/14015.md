aurora:/əˈrɔːrə/ /ɔːˈrɔːrə/| n.|极光；曙光；黎明|A natural electrical phenomenon characterized by the appearance of streamers of reddish or greenish light in the sky, especially near the northern or southern magnetic pole.|例句：The aurora borealis danced across the night sky.  北极光在夜空中舞动。

例句：We were lucky enough to see the aurora australis on our trip to Tasmania. 我们很幸运在塔斯马尼亚之旅中看到了南极光。|近义词：polar lights, northern lights (for aurora borealis), southern lights (for aurora australis)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin Aurora, the Roman goddess of the dawn.
