charnico:/ˈtʃɑːrnɪkoʊ/ | n. |烤肉; 炭烤(尤指拉丁美洲) | A method of grilling or barbecuing meat, especially in Latin America. |  Example: We had a delicious charnico of beef and chorizo. 我们吃了一顿美味的烤牛肉和西班牙香肠。

Example: The charnico was seasoned perfectly with chimichurri sauce.  这道烤肉用香辣酱调味，非常完美。

| 近义词：barbecue, grill, asado | 反义词: boil, stew | 词性: 名词
