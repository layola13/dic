edgingly:/ˈɛdʒɪŋli/
adverb|逐渐地，一点点地|Moving or advancing gradually, little by little, especially in a hesitant or tentative way.  It implies a slow, careful, and often uncertain progression.|He edged his way through the crowd. 他在人群中一点点地挤过去。|近义词：gradually, slowly, cautiously, tentatively, incrementally|反义词：quickly, rapidly, abruptly, suddenly|词性: adverb
