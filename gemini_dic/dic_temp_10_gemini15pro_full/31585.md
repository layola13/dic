chassidism:/ˈxɑːsɪdɪzəm/ /ˈhɑːsɪdɪzəm/
n.
哈西德教，哈西德主义
A Jewish religious movement characterized by its emphasis on mystical experience, joy, and the leadership of charismatic rabbis.
The rabbi's teachings were deeply rooted in Chassidism.  这位拉比的教义深植于哈西德教。
Hasidism, Hasidic Judaism
Misnagdim (historically, opponents of the early Hasidic movement)
noun
