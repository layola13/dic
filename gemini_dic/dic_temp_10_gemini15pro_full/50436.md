deprecated:/ˈdɛprɪkeɪtɪd/| adj.|已弃用的，不赞成的|No longer recommended or supported because a better alternative exists; discouraged from further use.|例句：The use of this function is deprecated.  这个函数的使用已被弃用。|近义词：obsolete, outdated, superseded|反义词:supported, current, recommended|词性:形容词

deprecated:/ˈdɛprɪkeɪtɪd/| v.|不赞成，反对，弃用|Express disapproval of.|例句：The committee deprecated the use of violence. 委员会反对使用暴力。|近义词：disapprove of, condemn, criticize|反义词:approve, support, endorse|词性:动词

词源: From Latin *deprecatus*, past participle of *deprecari* "to avert by prayer, pray against," from *de-* "down, away" + *precari* "to pray."  The sense shifted from "pray against" to "express disapproval of."
