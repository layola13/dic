histamines:/ˈhɪstəmiːnz/ | n. | 组胺 | A compound found in bodily tissues that causes dilation of capillaries, contraction of smooth muscle, and stimulation of gastric acid secretion, involved in allergic reactions.  It is released in response to injury or inflammation. |  High levels of histamines can cause itching. 高水平的组胺会导致瘙痒。

The release of histamines causes the symptoms of an allergic reaction. 组胺的释放导致过敏反应的症状。 | 近义词:  vasoactive amines | 反义词: antihistamines | 词性: noun
