cathedrallike:/kəˈθiːdrəlˌlaɪk/
adjective
像大教堂的，宏伟的
Resembling a cathedral, especially in size, grandeur, or atmosphere.
The music swelled to a cathedrallike volume.  音乐的音量增强到像大教堂里一样宏大。
The redwood forest had a cathedrallike quality.  红杉林有一种像大教堂般庄严的气氛。
synonyms: majestic, imposing, grand, awe-inspiring, monumental
antonyms: small, humble, insignificant, unimpressive
词性: adjective
