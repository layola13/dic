misunderstands:/ˌmɪsʌndərˈstændz/ | v. | 误解，误会 |Fails to understand correctly; interprets wrongly. | He often misunderstands my intentions. 他经常误解我的意图。 | She misunderstands the instructions and did it wrong. 她误解了指示，做错了。 | misinterprets, misconstrues | understands, comprehends | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (Old English, meaning "wrongly, badly") + understand (from Old English understandan, meaning "to comprehend").
