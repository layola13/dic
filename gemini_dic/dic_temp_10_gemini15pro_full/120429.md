miniwarehouses:/ˈmɪni ˌwɛərˌhaʊzɪz/| n. |小型仓库|Small warehouses, typically used for personal storage by individuals or small businesses.  They are often rented out in various sizes.|Example 1:  We rented a miniwarehouse to store our excess furniture during our move. 我们在搬家期间租了一个小型仓库来存放多余的家具。

Example 2: Miniwarehouses are popping up all over the city due to the increasing demand for storage space. 由于对存储空间的需求不断增加，小型仓库在城市中随处可见。|Synonym: self-storage, storage unit, storage locker|Antonym: warehouse (in a general, larger sense)|noun
