ideal:/aɪˈdiːəl/ | adj. n. | 理想的；完美的；理想；典范 | existing only in the imagination; desirable or perfect but not likely to become a reality.  |  She has the ideal home for a large family. 她拥有一个非常适合大家庭的理想家园。 | synonyms: perfect, optimal, model, exemplary | antonyms: real, flawed, imperfect, actual | adjective

ideal:/aɪˈdiːəl/ | n. | 理想；典范 | a principle or value that one actively pursues or upholds. |  Equality and liberty are Western ideals. 平等和自由是西方的理想。 | synonyms: principle, value, standard, model | antonyms: reality, flaw, imperfection | noun
