gloom:/ɡluːm/| n.  | 忧郁；昏暗；阴暗 |Partial or total darkness; a state of sadness or depression. |The room was filled with gloom after the bad news. 房间里充满了坏消息带来的忧郁。| synonyms: darkness, dimness, sadness, melancholy, despair | antonyms: brightness, light, happiness, cheer, joy | noun
gloom:/ɡluːm/| v. |  变得阴沉；使忧郁|To appear or become dark or somber; to make someone sad or depressed.|The sky gloomed over as the storm approached.  暴风雨来临，天空变得阴沉。|synonyms: darken, cloud, sadden, depress | antonyms: brighten, lighten, cheer, gladden | verb
