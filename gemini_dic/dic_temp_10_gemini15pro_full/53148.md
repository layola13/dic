diplomatists:/dɪˈpləʊmətɪsts/
n.
外交官（复数）
Officials who represent their country in dealings with other countries.
The diplomats worked tirelessly to negotiate a peace treaty.  外交官们不知疲倦地致力于谈判和平条约。
The two countries recalled their diplomats. 这两个国家召回了各自的外交官。

Synonyms: envoys, ambassadors, negotiators, representatives
Antonyms:  none (antonym would be a concept, not a person, e.g., non-diplomat or warmonger)
词性: noun
