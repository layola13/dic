ampulliform:/æmpəˈlɪfɔːrm/ | adj. | 瓶状的；烧瓶状的 | Shaped like a flask or bottle, often with a swollen base and a narrower neck. | The ampulliform vase was a beautiful addition to the collection.  那个瓶状的花瓶是收藏的一个漂亮补充。|  The fungus produced ampulliform spores. 这种真菌会产生瓶状孢子。 | 近义词: flask-shaped, bottle-shaped | 反义词: linear, tubular | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *ampulla* (bottle, flask) + *-formis* (having the form of).
