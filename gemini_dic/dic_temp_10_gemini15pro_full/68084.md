fade-out:/ˈfeɪd aʊt/| n.|淡出，渐隐|A gradual decrease in the visibility or audibility of something, such as a scene in a film or a sound in a piece of music.|The movie ended with a slow fade-out to black.|近义词：fade away, dissolve|反义词:fade-in|名词
fade-out:/ˈfeɪd aʊt/| v.|（使）淡出，（使）渐隐|To cause something to gradually decrease in visibility or audibility.|The director chose to fade out the music as the scene ended.|近义词：fade away, dim|反义词:fade-in|动词
