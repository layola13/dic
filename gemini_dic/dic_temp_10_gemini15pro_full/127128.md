nativistic:/ˌneɪtɪˈvɪstɪk/
adjective
本土主义的；排外的
Relating to or supporting nativism.  Characterized by the policy of protecting the interests of native inhabitants against those of immigrants.
Example: The nativistic movement gained traction in the early 20th century.  本土主义运动在20世纪初获得了关注。

Example:  His nativistic views led him to oppose any further immigration. 他的本土主义观点使他反对任何进一步的移民。
synonym:xenophobic, anti-immigrant, protectionist
antonym:cosmopolitan, internationalist, inclusive
