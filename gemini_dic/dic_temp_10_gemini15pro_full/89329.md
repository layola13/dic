homogenizes:/həˈmɒdʒənaɪzɪz/ /həˈmɑːdʒənaɪzɪz/
verb
使均匀；使均质化
to make something uniform or consistent throughout
The milk is homogenized to prevent the cream from separating. 牛奶经过均质化处理以防止奶油分离。
The globalization of culture homogenizes the world's artistic expressions. 文化全球化使世界各地的艺术表达趋于同质化。
synonyms: standardize, uniform, blend, mix, integrate
antonyms: diversify, vary, separate, segregate
词性: 动词

homogenization:/həˌmɒdʒənɪˈzeɪʃn/ /həˌmɑːdʒənɪˈzeɪʃn/
noun
均质化；同化
The process of making something uniform or consistent throughout
The homogenization of milk is a common practice in the dairy industry. 牛奶的均质化是乳制品行业中的常见做法。
Cultural homogenization can lead to the loss of unique traditions. 文化同质化会导致独特传统的丧失。
synonyms: standardization, uniformity, blending, mixing, integration
antonyms: diversification, variation, separation, segregation
词性: 名词

homogeneous:/ˌhɒməˈdʒiːniəs/ /ˌhɑːməˈdʒiːniəs/
adjective
同质的；均匀的
of the same kind; alike
The student body was fairly homogeneous, mostly from middle-class families. 学生群体相当同质，主要来自中产阶级家庭。
The mixture should be smooth and homogeneous. 混合物应该是光滑且均匀的。
synonyms: uniform, consistent, similar, alike
antonyms: heterogeneous, diverse, varied, different
词性: 形容词
