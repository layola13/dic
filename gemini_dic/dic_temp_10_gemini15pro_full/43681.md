credentialling: /krɪˈdɛnʃəlɪŋ/
n.|资格认证，资格审查|The process of verifying and assessing someone's qualifications, usually for a professional role.  This involves checking licenses, certifications, education, and experience to ensure they meet the required standards.|The hospital is currently credentialling several new doctors.  这家医院目前正在对几名新医生进行资格审查。|certification, accreditation, licensing|disqualification, rejection|noun

credentialled: /krɪˈdɛnʃəld/
adj.|持有资格证书的，被认可的|Having been granted credentials; certified or licensed.|Only credentialled therapists are allowed to practice in this state. 只有持有资格证书的治疗师才能在这个州执业。|certified, licensed, accredited|uncertified, unlicensed, unaccredited|adjective
