alluvial:/əˈluːviəl/ | adj. | 冲积的；淤积的 | Relating to or derived from alluvium; deposited by running water. | The alluvial soil in the delta is very fertile. 三角洲的冲积土壤非常肥沃。 | The river deposited a thick layer of alluvial mud. 这条河沉积了一层厚厚的冲积泥。 | 近义词: sedimentary, deposited, fluvial | 反义词: igneous, metamorphic | 词性: adjective

alluvium:/əˈluːviəm/ | n. | 冲积层；冲积土 | A deposit of clay, silt, sand, and gravel left by flowing streams in a river valley or delta, typically producing fertile soil. | The farmers benefited from the rich alluvium deposited by the river. 农民们受益于河流沉积的肥沃的冲积土。 | The ancient civilization thrived thanks to the fertile alluvium. 古老的文明因肥沃的冲积土而繁荣。 | 近义词: sediment, deposit, silt | 反义词: bedrock | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *alluvius*, from alluere "to wash against," from ad- "to, toward" + -luere, combining form of lavere "to wash."
