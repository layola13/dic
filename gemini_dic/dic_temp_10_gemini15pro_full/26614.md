caching:/ˈkæʃɪŋ/ | n. | 缓存，高速缓存 | The process of storing data in a temporary storage area (a cache) to speed up access to it later. |  The website uses caching to improve loading times.  该网站使用缓存来提高加载速度。 | synonyms: buffering, storing, saving | antonyms: retrieving, deleting | noun
