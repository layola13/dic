customizes:/ˈkʌstəˌmaɪzɪz/
verb
定制，个性化
modifies something to suit a particular individual or task.
The company customizes cars to meet each customer's specifications.  这家公司根据每个客户的具体要求定制汽车。
The software can be customized to fit your needs.  这个软件可以根据你的需要进行定制。
personalizes, adapts, modifies, tailors, adjusts
standardizes, generalizes
词性:动词
