blanches:/blɑːnʃɪz/| n. | 蔬菜焯水;  (脸色)变白;  (金属等)变白; |The process of briefly boiling vegetables, usually to partially cook them or to set their color. Also, to turn pale or whiten (as of metal). |She blanches the almonds before chopping them. 她先把杏仁焯一下水再切碎。|近义词：pale, whiten, parboil, scald|反义词:darken, redden, flush|verb

blanches:/blɑːnʃɪz/| v. | (脸色)变白;  使变白;  焯(蔬菜); |To become pale or whiten. To briefly boil (vegetables, etc.).|His face blanched when he saw the ghost. 他看到鬼魂时脸色变得苍白。|近义词：pale, whiten, parboil, scald|反义词:darken, redden, flush|verb
