fenceful:/ˈfɛnsfʊl/
adjective
一篱笆的；足够围篱笆的
Enclosed by or containing a fence or fences; enough to fill a fence.
The farmer had a fenceful of sheep.  那位农民有一篱笆的羊。
The truck delivered a fenceful of lumber. 卡车运送了一卡车能围成篱笆的木材。
Synonyms: fenced, enclosed
Antonyms: open, unfenced
词性: adjective
