miscellanea:/ˌmɪsəˈleɪniə/| n. |杂项，杂集|A collection of various items or information, especially of a literary or artistic nature.|The book was a miscellanea of poems, essays, and short stories. 这本书是诗歌、散文和短篇小说的杂集。|synonyms: collection, assortment, mixture, medley, anthology, compilation, potpourri, hodgepodge, gallimaufry |antonyms: uniformity, homogeneity|noun

miscellaneous:/ˌmɪsəˈleɪniəs/| adj. |各种各样的，混杂的|Consisting of diverse things or members.|The shop sells a miscellaneous assortment of goods. 这家商店出售各种各样的商品。|synonyms: diverse, varied, assorted, mixed, sundry, heterogeneous|antonyms: uniform, homogeneous, similar|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *miscellanea, neuter plural of miscellaneus (“mixed”), from Latin miscellus (“mixed”), past participle of miscere (“to mix”).
