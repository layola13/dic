crustalogical:/krʌstəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
甲壳纲的;与甲壳纲动物有关的
Relating to or characteristic of crustaceans.
Crustaceans, such as crabs and lobsters, are characterized by their hard, crust-like exoskeletons.
近义词:crustacean
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-crustacean)
词性: adjective
