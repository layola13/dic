alerter:/əˈlɜːrtər/ | n. | 警报器；报警装置；报警人 | A person or device that gives a warning or alert. |  The smoke alerter went off, alerting the family to the fire. 烟雾报警器响了，提醒家人注意火灾。 | synonyms: alarm, warning device, informant, whistleblower | antonyms: silencer, suppressor | noun

alerter:/əˈlɜːrtər/ | n. |  发出警报的人 | Someone who alerts or warns others. | The alerter called 911 after seeing the suspicious activity.  报警人在看到可疑活动后拨打了911。| synonyms: informant, warner, notifier  | antonyms: concealer, hider | noun
