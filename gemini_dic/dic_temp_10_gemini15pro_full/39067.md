conformingly:/kənˈfɔːrmɪŋli/ | adv. | 相应地，顺应地 | In a way that is in agreement with or corresponds to something else. |  She dressed conformingly to the company's dress code. 她按照公司的着装规定穿着。 |  Example: The students behaved conformingly to the school rules. 学生们按照学校的规定行事。 | 近义词: accordingly, correspondingly, similarly, likewise | 反义词: unconformably, differently, discordantly | 词性: 副词

词源: From the adjective "conforming" + "-ly." "Conforming" comes from the verb "conform," which ultimately derives from the Latin "conformare," meaning "to shape in accordance with."
