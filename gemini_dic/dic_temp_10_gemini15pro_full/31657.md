chatterer:/ˈtʃætərər/ | n. | 喋喋不休的人;饶舌的人;话匣子 |  A person who talks rapidly and continuously, especially about trivial matters. | The child was a chatterer, constantly talking about her toys.  这个孩子是个话匣子，不停地谈论她的玩具。| synonyms: talker, gabber, prattler | antonyms: quiet person, reticent person | noun

chatterbox: /ˈtʃætərbɒks/ | n. | 喋喋不休的人 | A person, especially a child, who talks a lot. | My niece is a real chatterbox! 我的侄女真是个话匣子！| synonyms: chatterer, talker | antonyms: quiet person | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *chaterere*, from *chateren* (“to chatter”), frequentative of *chaten* (“to chat”).  Ultimately of imitative origin.
