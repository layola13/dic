imperialistic:/ɪmˌpɪəriəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
帝国主义的；帝国主义者的
Of, relating to, or characterized by imperialism; advocating or practicing imperialism.
The country's foreign policy has been criticized as imperialistic.  这个国家的对外政策被批评为帝国主义。
Her imperialistic ambitions led her to conquer neighboring territories. 她的帝国主义野心导致她征服了邻近的领土。

近义词:expansionist, annexationist, colonialist, hegemonic
反义词:anti-imperialist, pacifist

词性:adjective
词源: From *imperialist* + *-ic*. Ultimately from Latin *imperium* (“command, power, empire”).
