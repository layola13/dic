nab:/næb/ | v. | 抓住，逮捕，抢夺 | To catch or arrest someone, especially a criminal; to snatch or grab something quickly. | The police nabbed the thief as he was trying to escape. 警察抓住小偷，因为他正试图逃跑。|  The eagle nabbed a fish from the river.  老鹰从河里抓了一条鱼。 | synonyms: arrest, capture, seize, grab, snatch | antonyms: release, free, give, miss | verb
