flash-in-the-pan:/ˌflæʃ ɪn ðə ˈpæn/| n.|昙花一现的人或事|something or someone that becomes successful for a short time but then fails|The band was a flash in the pan.  这个乐队只是昙花一现。|Synonym: one-hit wonder, briefly successful|Antonym: lasting success, enduring achievement|noun
flash-in-the-pan:/ˌflæʃ ɪn ðə ˈpæn/| adj.|昙花一现的|briefly successful but then failing|His political career was flash-in-the-pan. 他的政治生涯昙花一现。|Synonym: short-lived, ephemeral|Antonym: long-lasting, enduring|adjective
