outblown:/ˌaʊtˈbloʊn/
adjective
吹散的，凋谢的，过时的
blown out; extinguished.  Also, faded, withered.
The outblown rose petals fell to the ground. 吹落的玫瑰花瓣落到地上。
The outblown tulips were a sign that spring was ending.  凋谢的郁金香预示着春天即将结束。
近义词: faded, withered, expired, extinguished
反义词: blooming, flourishing, vibrant
词性: adjective
词源:  'out-' (meaning "out, forth, completely") + 'blown' (past participle of 'blow', meaning "moved by a current of air").  Essentially meaning "completely blown out or away".
