/ɪˈmædʒ/ | n. | 图像；影像；想象；想象力 | A visual representation of something, either real or imaginary. |  She saw an image of her childhood home in her dream.  她梦到了她童年之家的景象。 | synonym: picture, vision, likeness | antonym: reality, actuality | noun

/ɪˈmædʒ/ | v. | 想象；设想；反映 | To form a mental picture or concept of something. |  I can't image life without you.  我无法想象没有你的生活。 | synonym: visualize, envision, depict | antonym:  - | verb
