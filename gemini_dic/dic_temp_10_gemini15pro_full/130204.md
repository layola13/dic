non-segmental:/nɒnˈsɛɡmɛntl/ (UK) /nɑːnˈsɛɡmɛntl/ (US)| adj.|非音段的，超音段的|Relating to features of pronunciation (such as pitch, stress, and intonation) that are not confined to single sounds but extend over syllables, words, or phrases.|Intonation is a non-segmental feature of pronunciation. 语调是发音的非音段特征。|suprasegmental|segmental|adjective
