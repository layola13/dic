averters:/əˈvɜːrtərz/
| n. | 避免者；防止者；转移者 | People or things that avert something (usually something bad). | The averters of the crisis were praised for their quick thinking.  危机避免者因其快速思考而受到赞扬。| Synonyms: preventers, obstructors, deflectors | Antonyms: causers, instigators, promoters
