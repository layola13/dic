once-over:/ˈwʌnsˌoʊvər/ /ˈwʌnsˌəʊvə(r)/
n.|匆匆一看，粗略的检查|A quick, superficial inspection or examination.|She gave the report a quick once-over before submitting it. 她提交报告前匆匆地看了一遍。|近义词:glance, scan, quick look|反义词:thorough examination, detailed inspection|词性:名词
