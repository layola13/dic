indigestibly:/ˌɪndɪˈdʒɛstɪbli/ | adv. | 难以消化地，难理解地 | In a manner that is difficult or impossible to digest; in a manner that is difficult to understand or assimilate. | The author's prose was dense and indigestibly written.  作者的文章写得晦涩难懂。| Synonyms: unreadably, impenetrably, obscurely, densely | Antonyms: clearly, understandably, simply, lucidly | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + digestible + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Digestible" comes from the Latin "digerere" meaning "to separate, distribute, digest."
