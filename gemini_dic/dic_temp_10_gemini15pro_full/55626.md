disyoke:/dɪsˈjoʊk/
verb
解开；卸下；分离
to separate from a yoke; to unyoke; to detach
The farmer disyoked the oxen at the end of the day.  农民在一天结束时把牛从轭上解下来。
disjoin, uncouple, separate, detach
yoke, join, connect, couple
v.
