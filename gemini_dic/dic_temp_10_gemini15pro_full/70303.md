fidgety:/ˈfɪdʒɪti/| adj.|烦躁的，坐立不安的|Restless and uneasy; tending to fidget.|The children were fidgety and restless during the long car ride. 孩子们在漫长的车程中烦躁不安。|Synonyms: restless, antsy, jittery, twitchy|Antonyms: calm, composed, relaxed, still|词性:形容词

fidget:/ˈfɪdʒɪt/| n. vi.|烦躁不安，坐立不安|A state of restlessness or unease.|He has a fidget and can't sit still. 他很烦躁，坐不住。|Synonyms: restlessness, unease|Antonyms: calmness, composure|词性:名词/动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the late 16th century.  Origin uncertain, possibly related to  *fike*, meaning "to be restless or uneasy."  It may also be related to the now obsolete verb *fidget*, meaning "to move restlessly."
