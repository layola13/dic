Etymology:
From Old French *chimere (modern French chimère), from Latin chimaera (“fire-breathing she-monster”), from Ancient Greek Χίμαιρα (Khímaira, “she-goat”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰey- (“winter”).
