nonexultantly:/ˌnɒnɪɡˈzʌltəntli/
adverb
非兴高采烈地；不欢欣鼓舞地
in a way that does not express triumphant joy or jubilation.
She accepted the award nonexultantly, acknowledging her team's contribution. 她非兴高采烈地接受了奖金，感谢了团队的贡献。
The news was received nonexultantly, given the difficult circumstances. 考虑到艰难的处境，这个消息的接受并非欢欣鼓舞。
近义词:  soberly, calmly, quietly, reservedly
反义词: exultantly, triumphantly, joyfully
词性: adverb
词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "exultant" (adjective meaning "triumphantly joyful") + "-ly" (adverb suffix)
