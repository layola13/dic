faithlessness:/ˈfeɪθləsnəs/
n.
不忠;背信;无信仰
The state of being faithless; lack of faith or loyalty.
Her faithlessness to her husband ended their marriage. 她对丈夫的不忠结束了他们的婚姻。
近义词：disloyalty, infidelity, treachery, perfidy
反义词：faithfulness, loyalty, fidelity, constancy
词性：名词

faithlessness:/ˈfeɪθləsnəs/
n.
不信; 怀疑
Lack of religious belief.
His faithlessness made it difficult for him to find comfort in times of trouble. 他的不信让他在困境中难以找到慰藉。
近义词：disbelief, skepticism, doubt
反义词：faith, belief, trust
词性：名词
