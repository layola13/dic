brawned:/brɔːnd/
| adj. |强壮的，肌肉发达的|Muscular and strong; having well-developed muscles.|(of a person) physically strong; muscular.|例句：The brawned blacksmith easily lifted the heavy anvil. 强壮的铁匠轻松地举起了沉重的铁砧。

例句：She admired his brawned physique. 她欣赏他强壮的体格。|近义词：muscular, brawny, strong, powerful, strapping, burly, hefty, robust|反义词：weak, feeble, frail, delicate, puny|词性:形容词

词源: From *brawn* (muscular strength) + *-ed* (forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Old French *braon* (fleshy part of the body, muscle).
