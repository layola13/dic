awaited:/əˈweɪtɪd/| adj.|期待的，被等待的|Something that is awaited is expected or looked forward to.  It describes something that people are waiting for.|The long-awaited sequel finally hit theaters.  这部期待已久的续集终于上映了。|近义词：expected, anticipated, hoped-for|反义词:unexpected, unanticipated|词性:形容词

Etymology: From the verb "await," which comes from the Old English "awāītan," meaning "to watch over" or "to expect."  The prefix "a-" intensifies the meaning, while "-wait" comes from a Germanic root meaning "to watch."
