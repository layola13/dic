burros:/ˈbɜːroʊz/ (US), /ˈbʌrəʊz/ (UK)| n.|驴子（复数）|Small donkeys, especially ones used as pack animals.  Originally from the Spanish word "burro".|例句：The burros carried heavy loads up the mountain trail. 驴子驮着沉重的货物沿着山路向上走。

|例句：We saw a group of wild burros grazing in the desert. 我们看到一群野驴在沙漠里吃草。

|近义词：donkeys, asses|反义词:|noun
