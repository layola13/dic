muggletonian:/ˌmʌɡəlˈtoʊniən/ | n. | 马格尔顿教派信徒 | A follower of Lodowick Muggleton (1609–98), a journeyman tailor who founded a sect based on the rejection of the Trinity and belief that God had appeared on earth in human form. |  He was known as a staunch Muggletonian, adhering strictly to the sect's unusual beliefs. 他以坚定的马格尔顿教派信徒而闻名，严格遵守该教派不同寻常的信仰。| Synonym: sectarian | Antonym: orthodox | noun
