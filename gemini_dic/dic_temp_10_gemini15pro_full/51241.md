destruction:/dɪˈstrʌkʃən/| n.|毁灭，破坏，摧毁|The earthquake caused widespread destruction. 地震造成了大范围的破坏。|例句：The destruction of the rainforest is a serious environmental problem.  雨林的破坏是一个严重的环境问题。

例句：The hurricane left a trail of destruction in its wake.  飓风过后留下一片狼藉。

近义词：ruin, devastation, demolition|反义词:construction, creation, preservation|词性:名词

Etymology: From Late Middle English *destruccion, *distruccion, from Old French destruction, from Latin dēstructiō (“a pulling down, demolition”), from dēstruō (“I pull down, demolish”).
