cropping-up:/ˈkrɒpɪŋ ʌp (UK) / ˈkrɑːpɪŋ ʌp (US)| phrasal verb |突然出现，冒出来| Appearing unexpectedly or suddenly.  The verb "crop" originally referred to harvesting a crop, then extended to mean "appear unexpectedly." "Up" adds to the sense of sudden emergence.| Problems kept cropping up during the project. 项目期间问题不断涌现。|Synonyms: appearing, emerging, arising, surfacing| Antonyms: disappearing, vanishing, subsiding| verb phrase
