delactation:/ˌdiːlækˈteɪʃən/ | n. |断奶，停止哺乳|The process of weaning a baby or young animal from its mother's milk; cessation of lactation.|After six months, the mother began the delactation process. 六个月后，母亲开始断奶。|The delactation was gradual to minimize discomfort for both mother and baby.  断奶是逐渐进行的，以尽量减少母亲和婴儿的不适。|近义词：weaning|反义词：lactation|词性：名词

词源：de- (Latin prefix meaning "from," "down," "away," "off") + lactation (from Latin lac, lactis, meaning "milk")
