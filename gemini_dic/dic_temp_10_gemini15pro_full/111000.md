liquifying:/ˈlɪkwɪˌfaɪɪŋ/
verb
液化；使液化
turning or becoming liquid
The heat is liquifying the ice.  热量正在使冰融化。

liquefying:/ˈlɪkwəˌfaɪɪŋ/
verb
液化；使液化
turning or becoming liquid
The substance is liquefying at room temperature.  这种物质在室温下会液化。

Synonyms: melting, dissolving, thawing
Antonyms: solidifying, freezing, hardening
verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English: from late Latin liquefacere, from Latin lique- ‘liquid’ + facere ‘to make.’
