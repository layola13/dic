contrite:/kənˈtraɪt/ /ˈkɑːnˌtraɪt/| adj.|悔恨的，痛悔的|Feeling or expressing remorse or penitence; affected by guilt.|例句：He offered a contrite apology for his behavior. 他对自己的行为表示了悔恨的歉意。|例句：Her contrite tears moved everyone in the room. 她悔恨的泪水打动了房间里的每一个人。|近义词：remorseful, penitent, repentant, apologetic|反义词：unrepentant, impenitent, unapologetic|词性:形容词

词源:
From Late Latin contritus, past participle of conterere "to grind, bruise," figuratively "to crush, break down," from com- "together" (see com-) + terere "to rub, grind" (see throw (v.)). Figurative sense of "crushed in spirit" led to meaning "penitent."
