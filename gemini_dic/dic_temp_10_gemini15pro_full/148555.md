overswung:/ˌoʊvərˈswʌŋ/ | adj. | 过度摆动的，矫枉过正的 |  Having swung or been swung too far; carried to excess.  Specifically, in printing, having excessively long serifs that curve dramatically.  | The pendulum overswung, knocking over a vase. 钟摆过度摆动，撞翻了一个花瓶。 | Near synonyms: excessive, immoderate, exaggerated, extreme | Near antonyms: moderate, restrained, understated | adjective

overswung:/ˌoʊvərˈswʌŋ/ | past participle of overswing |过度摆动（overswing的过去分词）| Used as the past participle of the verb "overswing".  | The chandelier had overswung and hit the ceiling. 枝形吊灯摆动过度，撞到了天花板。| Near synonyms: swung too far | Near antonyms: swung moderately | verb (past participle)
