bequeathable:/bɪˈkwiːðəbl/
adjective
可遗赠的
Able to be bequeathed (left to someone in a will).
My grandmother's antique jewelry is bequeathable. 我祖母的古董珠宝是可以遗赠的。
He ensured that all his assets were bequeathable. 他确保他所有的资产都是可以遗赠的。
近义词: inheritable, devisable, testable
反义词: non-bequeathable
