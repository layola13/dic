bade:/beɪd/ /bæd/| v. |吩咐，命令，告别|Past tense of bid (meaning "command").  It can also be the past tense of bid (meaning "to offer a price"). However, this usage is less common. |She bade him leave the room. 她吩咐他离开房间。

The knight bade farewell to his lady love. 骑士向他的爱人告别。|近义词：commanded, ordered, instructed, farewell |反义词:allowed, permitted|verb
