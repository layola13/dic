half-mummified:/ˌhæf ˈmʌmɪfaɪd/| adj.|半木乃伊化的|Partially mummified; preserved in a state between fresh and fully mummified, often through natural processes like desiccation.|The explorers found a half-mummified body in the cave, its skin leathery and dried but some tissue still intact.|近义词：partially preserved, desiccated, semi-mummified|反义词:decomposed, putrefied|词性:形容词

half:/hæf/ | n. adj. adv.|一半，半个|One of two equal or approximately equal parts of a divisible whole.|She ate half the cake. 她吃掉了蛋糕的一半。|近义词：one-half, moiety|反义词:whole, double|词性:名词，形容词，副词

mummified:/ˈmʌmɪfaɪd/ | adj. v. (past participle of mummify)|木乃伊化的|Preserved by embalming or some similar method.|The mummified remains were discovered in a tomb.  木乃伊化的遗骸是在一座坟墓中发现的。|近义词：embalmed, preserved|反义词:decayed, decomposed|词性:形容词，动词(mummify的过去分词)
