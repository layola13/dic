ascetical: /əˈsɛtɪkəl/
adjective
禁欲的，苦行的
Characterized by or suggesting the practice of severe self-discipline and abstention from all forms of indulgence, typically for religious reasons.
He lived an ascetic life, devoid of luxury.  他过着苦行的生活，没有任何奢侈享受。
The monk's ascetic lifestyle involved fasting and prayer.  这位僧侣的苦行生活包括禁食和祈祷。
近义词: austere, spartan, abstinent, self-denying
反义词: hedonistic, indulgent, luxurious, self-indulgent
词性: adjective

ascetic: /əˈsɛtɪk/
noun
苦行者，禁欲者
A person who practices severe self-discipline and abstention.
The ascetic lived in a cave, meditating for hours each day.  这位苦行者住在山洞里，每天冥想数小时。
She became an ascetic after a profound spiritual experience. 在一次深刻的灵性体验之后，她成了一名苦行者。
近义词: hermit, monk, nun, recluse
反义词: hedonist, sybarite
词性: noun

词源: From Late Latin *asceticus, from Greek askētikos "laborious, athletic, ascetic," from askētēs "monk, hermit, athlete," from askein "to exercise, train, practice," especially athletics, later of spiritual exercises.
