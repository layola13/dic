cephalothorax:/ˌsefələˈθɔːræks/| n. |头胸|The fused head and thorax of certain arthropods, such as crustaceans and arachnids.  节肢动物（如甲壳类和蛛形纲动物）融合的头部和胸部|例句：The spider's cephalothorax is covered in fine hairs. 蜘蛛的头胸部覆盖着细毛。

例句：The crab’s cephalothorax is protected by a hard carapace. 蟹的头胸部受到坚硬外壳的保护。|近义词：prosoma|反义词:abdomen|词性:noun
