antinationalists:/ˌæntɪˈnæʃənəlɪsts/
|n.|反民族主义者|People who oppose nationalism or patriotism, often advocating for internationalism or globalism.  They believe that national interests should not be prioritized over global cooperation and understanding.|The conference attracted a diverse group of antinationalists from around the world.  这次会议吸引了来自世界各地的各种反民族主义者。

The antinationalists argued that prioritizing national interests would ultimately lead to conflict.  这些反民族主义者认为，优先考虑国家利益最终会导致冲突。
|近义词：cosmopolitan, internationalist, globalist|反义词：nationalist, patriot, chauvinist|词性:noun
