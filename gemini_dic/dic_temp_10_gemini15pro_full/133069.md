nonequalizing:/nɒnˈiːkwəlaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
非均衡的，不均等的
Not making equal; not equalizing.  This term describes something that doesn't create or promote equality or balance.
The nonequalizing distribution of resources led to social unrest. 资源分配不均导致了社会动荡。
The tax policy had a nonequalizing effect on the economy, benefitting the wealthy disproportionately. 这项税收政策对经济产生了不均衡的影响，使富人受益不成比例。
近义词:disproportionate, uneven, unbalanced, asymmetric
反义词:equalizing, balancing, leveling, equitable
词性:adjective
