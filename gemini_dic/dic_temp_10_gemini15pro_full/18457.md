bemoans:/bɪˈmoʊnz/
verb
哀叹，悲叹，惋惜
expresses sorrow or disapproval over something
The poet bemoans the loss of traditional values. 诗人哀叹传统价值观的丧失。
She bemoaned her fate. 她哀叹自己的命运。
synonyms: laments, bewails, deplores
antonyms: celebrates, rejoices
词性: 动词
