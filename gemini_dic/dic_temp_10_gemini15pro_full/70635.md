filterer's:/ˈfɪltərərz/ | n. | 过滤者的，过滤器的 |  Possession form of "filterer," indicating something belongs to or is associated with a person or thing that filters. | The filterer's job is to remove impurities. 过滤者的工作是去除杂质。| The filterer's mesh was clogged. 过滤器的网眼堵塞了。| Synonyms: purifier's, strainer's | Antonyms: contaminator's, polluter's | noun, possessive

ETYMOLOGY->
From "filter" + "-er" (agent suffix) + "-'s" (possessive suffix). "Filter" comes from Old French "filtre," from Medieval Latin "filtrum," of uncertain origin.
