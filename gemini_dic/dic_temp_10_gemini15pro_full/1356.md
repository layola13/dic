accumulated: /əˈkjuːmjəleɪtɪd/
adjective
积累的，积聚的
gathered or collected over a period of time.
The accumulated snow made it difficult to drive.  积雪使开车变得困难。

Over the years, she accumulated a vast fortune. 多年来，她积累了巨额财富。

synonym: amassed, gathered, collected, accrued, stockpiled
antonym: dispersed, scattered, dissipated, spent, depleted
词性: adjective


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Accumulated dust covers the furniture. = 积累的灰尘覆盖了家具。
The company has accumulated significant debt. = 这家公司积累了巨额债务。

将来时:
