dowl:/daʊl/ | n. | 绒毛，羽绒，软毛 |  A soft feather or fiber, like that from a bird or plant, often used as insulation or padding. | The jacket was filled with warm down and dowl.  这件夹克填充了温暖的羽绒和绒毛。| synonym: fluff, down, plume | antonym: bristle, spine | noun

dowl:/daʊl/ | n. | 木钉，暗榫 | A wooden peg or pin used to fasten timbers or other materials together. |  He used a dowl to secure the two pieces of wood. 他用木钉固定住两块木头。| synonym: peg, pin, dowel | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "adhesive" as a contrasting method of joining) | noun
