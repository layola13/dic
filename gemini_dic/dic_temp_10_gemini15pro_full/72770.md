fogy:/ˈfoʊɡi/| n.|老古董，守旧者|A fogy is someone with old-fashioned ideas.  老古董是指思想守旧的人。|例句：He's such a fogy! 他真是个老古董！|近义词：conservative, traditionalist, diehard|反义词:modernist, progressive|词性:名词

词源:  From the obsolete adjective *foggy* meaning "fuddled, dull-witted," probably related to *fog*.  源于已废弃的形容词 *foggy*，意为“糊涂的，迟钝的”，可能与 *fog*（雾）有关。
