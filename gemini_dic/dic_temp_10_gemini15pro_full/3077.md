aerometer:/ˌɛərˈɒmɪtər/ (US) /ˌɛərˈɑːmɪtər/| n. | 气体比重计，空气密度计 | An instrument for measuring the weight or density of air and gases. | The scientist used an aerometer to determine the density of the gas sample.  科学家使用气体比重计来测定气体样本的密度。

The aerometer indicated a change in atmospheric pressure. 气体比重计显示大气压力发生了变化。| 近义词：gasometer, densimeter | 反义词:  None | 词性: noun
