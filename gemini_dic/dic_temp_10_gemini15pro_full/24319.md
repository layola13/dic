brilliance:/ˈbrɪljəns/| n.|光辉；光彩；才华；杰出|The brilliance of the diamond dazzled her. 钻石的光辉使她眼花缭乱。|近义词：radiance, splendor, luminosity, genius, intelligence|反义词：dullness, mediocrity|词性:名词
Etymology: From Middle French *brillance*, from briller (“to shine”), from Italian brillare, ultimately from Vulgar Latin *berillare*, of uncertain origin, possibly from *beryllus* (“beryl”).
