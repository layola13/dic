begrudger:/bɪˈɡrʌdʒər/| n.|吝啬鬼，嫉妒者|A person who feels resentment or envy towards someone else's possessions, good fortune, or success. |He's such a begrudger; he can't stand to see anyone happy. 他真是个嫉妒鬼，看到别人开心就受不了。|近义词：envier, resister, malcontent|反义词: philanthropist, altruist|词性: noun
