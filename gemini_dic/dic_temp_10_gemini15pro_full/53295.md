dirtless:/ˈdɜːrtlɪs/
adj.
无尘的，干净的
Free from dirt; clean.
The operating room must be kept dirtless.  手术室必须保持无尘。
近义词：clean, spotless, immaculate, pristine
反义词：dirty, filthy, soiled, grimy
词性：形容词

词源:  "dirt" (污垢) + "-less" (无，没有)  The suffix "-less" is used to form adjectives meaning "without" or "free from."
