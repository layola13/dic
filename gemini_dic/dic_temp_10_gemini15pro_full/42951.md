coverages:/ˈkʌvərɪdʒɪz/
n.
保险范围，覆盖范围，新闻报道
The extent to which something is covered; the amount of something that covers something else, such as insurance protection or media attention.
We offer a wide range of insurance coverages to meet your needs.  我们提供范围广泛的保险，以满足您的需求。
The media coverage of the event was extensive.  该事件的媒体报道非常广泛。
synonyms: insurance, protection, reporting, reporting, media
antonyms: lack of insurance, vulnerability, neglect, censorship
noun
