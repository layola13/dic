circumlittoral:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈlɪtərəl/ | adj. | 近岸的，沿岸的 |  Relating to or inhabiting the shallow water zone near the shore, specifically the region extending from the low tide mark to a depth of about 200 meters. It encompasses the sublittoral zone. | The circumlittoral zone is rich in marine life. 近岸区域海洋生物丰富。| The study focused on the circumlittoral distribution of seagrass species. 这项研究的重点是海草物种的近岸分布。 | nearshore, coastal, sublittoral |  pelagic, oceanic | adjective
