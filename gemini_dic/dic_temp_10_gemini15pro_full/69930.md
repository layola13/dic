fervidnesses:/ˈfɜːrvɪdnəsɪz/
noun
热烈，热情，狂热 (plural)
The state of being fervid; intense zeal or passion.
Her fervidnesses for social justice inspired many. 她对社会正义的热情鼓舞了很多人。
His fervidnesses for the cause was evident in his speeches.  他对这项事业的热情在他的演讲中显而易见。
synonyms: ardors, passions, enthusiasms, zeals
antonyms: apathy, indifference, coldness, detachment
n.


Etymology:
From fervid + -ness.  Fervid comes from Latin *fervidus, from fervere "to boil, glow."
