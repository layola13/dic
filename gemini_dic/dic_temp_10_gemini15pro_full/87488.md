heteroatom:/ˈhɛtərəʊˌætəm/ (US) /ˈhetərəʊˌætəm/ (UK)| n.|杂原子|An atom other than carbon or hydrogen in an organic molecule.|例句：The presence of a heteroatom in the ring alters the chemical properties of the compound.  杂原子在环中的存在改变了化合物的化学性质。

例句：Nitrogen and oxygen are common heteroatoms in organic chemistry. 氮和氧是有机化学中常见的杂原子。|近义词：non-carbon atom, other atom|反义词: carbon atom, hydrogen atom|词性:noun
