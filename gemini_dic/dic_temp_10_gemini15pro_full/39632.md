consequential:/ˌkɒnsɪˈkwenʃəl/ /ˌkɑːnsɪˈkwenʃəl/| adj.|重要的，重大的；随之发生的，作为结果的|Following as a result or effect; important. |The decision had consequential impacts on the entire industry.  这个决定对整个行业产生了重大影响。|significant, important, momentous, far-reaching|trivial, insignificant, unimportant|adjective

consequent:/ˈkɒnsɪkwənt/ /ˈkɑːnsɪkwənt/| adj.|作为结果的，随之发生的|Happening as a result of something. |The rise in unemployment was consequent upon the economic downturn. 失业率上升是经济衰退的结果。|resulting, ensuing, following|preceding, prior, antecedent|adjective
