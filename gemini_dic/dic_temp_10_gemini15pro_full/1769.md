acquisitory: /əˈkwɪzɪtəri/
adjective
获取的，收购的
Relating to acquisition or acquiring something.
The company pursued an acquisitory strategy, buying up smaller competitors. = 这家公司奉行收购战略，收购规模较小的竞争对手。

Her acquisitory instincts led her to amass a vast collection of antique dolls. = 她的收藏本能使她积累了大量的古董娃娃。

近义词:  acquiring, possessive, greedy, avaricious
反义词:  giving, generous, altruistic
词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *acquisitorius*, from acquisitus, past participle of acquirere 'acquire'.
