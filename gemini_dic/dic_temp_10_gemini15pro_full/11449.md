arguers:/ˈɑːrɡjʊərz/
n.
争论者，辩论者
People who engage in arguments or debates.
The arguers couldn't agree on a solution. 争论者无法就解决方案达成一致。
The two arguers finally reached a compromise.  两位争论者最终达成妥协。
debaters, disputants
agreers, conciliators
