mcflurry:/məkˈflɜːri/| n. |麦旋风|A type of ice cream dessert sold by McDonald's, consisting of soft-serve ice cream mixed with various candies or cookies.|例句：I ordered a McFlurry for dessert. 我点了一个麦旋风作为甜点。

例句：The Oreo McFlurry is my favorite. 奥利奥麦旋风是我的最爱。

近义词：ice cream sundae, milkshake|反义词: None|词性:noun
