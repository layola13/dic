ammunition:/ˌæmjʊˈnɪʃən/ | n. | 弹药，军火 | Materials used in the discharge of firearms, such as bullets, shells, cartridges, or explosive missiles.  | The soldiers were running low on ammunition. 士兵们的弹药快用完了。 |  synonyms: weaponry, arms, ordnance, munitions | antonyms: peace, disarmament | noun

ammunition:/ˌæmjʊˈnɪʃən/ | n. | （应对困境的）资料，信息 | facts or information that can be used to support an argument or help to achieve something. |  She used the statistics as ammunition in her fight for equal pay. 她用这些统计数据作为争取同工同酬的武器。| synonyms: information, data, evidence, support, material | antonyms: misinformation, disinformation | noun
