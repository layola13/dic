disprivilege:/ˌdɪsˈprɪvəlɪdʒ/
noun
丧失权利，处于弱势，被剥夺权利
The state of being deprived of a right or advantage enjoyed by others.
例句：They studied the effects of disprivilege on educational attainment. 他们研究了丧失权利对教育程度的影响。

例句：The disprivilege faced by minority groups often leads to social unrest. 少数群体面临的权利被剥夺常常导致社会动荡。

近义词：disadvantage, deprivation, hardship, inequality
反义词：privilege, advantage, benefit

词性: n.
