gulled:/ɡʌld/
verb
受骗的，容易上当的
deceived or tricked easily
He was gulled into believing their lies. 他被骗相信了他们的谎言。
近义词:duped, tricked, deceived, cheated, swindled
反义词:astute, discerning, perceptive, shrewd, wise
词性:Verb (Past Participle)


gulled:/ɡʌld/
adjective
受骗的，容易上当的
easily deceived or tricked
He felt gulled after realizing the investment was a scam.  意识到投资是骗局后，他感到自己被骗了。
近义词:credulous, naive, gullible
反义词:skeptical, suspicious, discerning
词性:Adjective


词源:
From gull (noun), meaning a young bird, later extended to a person who is easily tricked.  Ultimately from probably imitative origin.  源自gull（名词），意思是雏鸟，后来引申为容易受骗的人。最终可能源于模仿词源。
