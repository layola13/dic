incredulousness:/ˌɪnkrɪˈdʒuːləsnəs/| n. |怀疑；不相信；难以置信|The state of being incredulous; disbelief.|He stared at her with a look of utter incredulousness. 他带着完全不相信的表情盯着她。|近义词：disbelief, skepticism, doubt|反义词：belief, credulity, faith|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *incredulōsus + -ness.  *incredulōsus from in- + credulōsus (credulous). Ultimately from Latin crēdō (believe).
