dop:/dɔp/ | v. | 给…用药；掺杂；在…中加兴奋剂 | To administer drugs to; to add a substance to. | The trainer was accused of doping the horses.  训练员被指控给马匹服用兴奋剂。 | 近义词：drug, spike, adulterate | 反义词：cleanse, purify | 词性: 动词
