feedback: /ˈfiːdbæk/ | n. | 反馈，意见 |  Information about reactions to a product, a person's performance of a task, etc. which is used as a basis for improvement. |  I received positive feedback on my presentation. 我收到了关于我演讲的积极反馈。| synonyms: response, reaction, comment, input, review | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider: silence, apathy, indifference) | noun
