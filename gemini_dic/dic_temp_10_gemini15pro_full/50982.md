desire:/dɪˈzaɪər/ (US) /dɪˈzaɪə(r)/ (UK)| n. vt.|渴望，欲望；要求|A strong feeling of wanting to have something or wishing for something to happen.|She has a strong desire to travel the world. 她强烈渴望环游世界。|He desired a quiet life in the countryside. 他渴望在乡下过平静的生活。|近义词：craving, yearning, longing, wish, want|反义词：aversion, dislike, indifference|noun, verb
