cobwebby:/ˈkɒbwɛbi/ (US) /ˈkɑːbwɛbi/ (UK)| adj.|蜘蛛网似的；布满蜘蛛网的；陈旧的|Covered with cobwebs; resembling cobwebs; antiquated or neglected.|The attic was dark and cobwebby. 阁楼又黑又满是蜘蛛网。|The old library had a cobwebby feel to it.  这间古老的图书馆给人一种陈旧的感觉。|近义词：dusty, musty, fusty, antiquated|反义词：clean, modern, new|词性:形容词
词源: From cobweb + -y.  "Cobweb" itself comes from "coppeweb" (Middle English), where "coppe" refers to a spider.
