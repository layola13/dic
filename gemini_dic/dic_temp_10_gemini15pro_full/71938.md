flickery:/ˈflɪkəri/| adj.|闪烁的，摇曳的，忽明忽暗的|Burning unsteadily; flickering. |The candle flame was flickery in the draft.  蜡烛的火焰在风中摇曳。|近义词：wavering, unsteady, flickering, intermittent|反义词：steady, constant, stable|adjective
