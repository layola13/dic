curvilineal:/ˌkɜːrvɪˈlɪniəl/| adj.|曲线形的，曲线的|Consisting of or bounded by curved lines.  Characterized by curved lines.|The architect favored curvilineal designs, incorporating sweeping arches and rounded walls.  这位建筑师喜欢曲线设计，融合了 sweeping arches 和圆墙。|Synonyms: curved, sinuous, winding, meandering|Antonyms: straight, linear, rectilinear|adjective
