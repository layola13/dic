curtailing:/kɜːrˈteɪlɪŋ/
verb
削减，缩短
reducing in extent or quantity; imposing a restriction on.
The government is curtailing public spending. 政府正在削减公共开支。
Synonyms: reducing, decreasing, lessening, shortening, truncating
Antonyms: increasing, extending, expanding, lengthening
verb

curtail:/kɜːrˈteɪl/
verb
削减，缩短
reduce in extent or quantity; impose a restriction on.

Antonyms: increase, extend, expand, lengthen
