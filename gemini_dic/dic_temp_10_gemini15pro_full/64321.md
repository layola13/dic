equivorous:/ɪˈkwɪvərəs/
adj.
食草的；以植物为食的
Feeding on plants; herbivorous.
Horses are equivorous animals. 马是食草动物。
Equivorous insects can cause significant damage to crops. 食草昆虫会对农作物造成重大损害。
近义词:herbivorous, phytophagous
反义词:carnivorous, omnivorous
词性:adjective

词源: From Latin *equus* (“horse”) + *-vorus* (“-eating”), ultimately from *vorare* (“to devour”).  Essentially meaning "horse-eating", but used in the broader sense of "plant-eating".
