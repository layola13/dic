misattributes:/ˌmɪsəˈtrɪbjuːts/
verb
错误地归因于；误认为…是…所为
incorrectly attributes something to someone or something else
The article misattributes the quote to the wrong author. 这篇文章错误地将这句话归因于另一位作者。
misascribes, misassigns
correctly attributes
