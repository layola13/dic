mother-of-all:/ˈmʌðər əv ɔːl/ | adj. | 最大的；最典型的；最厉害的 |  Used hyperbolically to describe something as the greatest or most extreme example of its kind.  It often implies irony or humor.  Originates from Saddam Hussein's description of the Gulf War as "the mother of all battles." | That was the mother-of-all traffic jams! 那是一场超级大堵车！| 近义词: biggest, greatest, ultimate, supreme | 反义词: smallest, least, insignificant | 词性: 形容词
