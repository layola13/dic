burnishers:/ˈbɜːrnɪʃərz/
noun
抛光工具；抛光剂；磨光机
tools or substances used to polish something, making it shiny
The craftsman used burnishers to give the silver a gleaming finish. 工匠用抛光工具使银器焕发光泽。

Synonyms: polishers, buffers, glossers
Antonyms: rougheners, abraders
词性: 名词(plural)
