pastured:/ˈpæstʃərd/| adj.|牧场的；吃草的；在牧场上饲养的|Raised or fed on pasture.  Relating to or characteristic of pastureland.|例句：The farm boasts pastured chickens and grass-fed beef.  这个农场以牧养的鸡和草饲牛肉为傲。

例句：We bought some pastured eggs from the local farmer's market. 我们从当地农贸市场买了一些牧场鸡蛋。|近义词：grazed, free-range|反义词：factory-farmed, caged|词性:形容词
