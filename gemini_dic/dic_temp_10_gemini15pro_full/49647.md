demilitarizing: /diːˈmɪlɪtəˌraɪzɪŋ/
verb
使非军事化；解除武装
The act of removing military forces, weapons, or fortifications from a place.
例句：The treaty called for demilitarizing the border region.  该条约要求边境地区非军事化。

例句：They are demilitarizing the area after the ceasefire.  停火后，他们正在使该地区非军事化。

近义词：disarming, neutralizing
反义词：militarizing, fortifying
词性：动词(present participle of demilitarize)

demilitarization: /diːˌmɪlɪtərɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
非军事化；解除武装
The action or process of demilitarizing a place or thing.
例句：The demilitarization of the zone was a key part of the peace agreement.  该地区的非军事化是和平协议的关键部分。

例句：The successful demilitarization led to a significant reduction in regional tensions.  成功的非军事化导致区域紧张局势显著缓和。

近义词：disarmament, neutralization
反义词：militarization, fortification
词性：名词


词源: de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + military + -ize (suffix forming verbs) + -ing (present participle suffix)
