cityful:/ˈsɪtiˌfʊl/| adj.|充满城市的，都市化的|A cityful experience. 一种充满都市感的体验。|例句：The novel captures the cityful energy of New York. 这部小说捕捉到了纽约充满活力的都市能量。

例句：The street was cityful, bustling with people and traffic. 街道充满了都市气息，熙熙攘攘的行人和车辆。|近义词：urban, metropolitan|反义词：rural, rustic|词性：形容词

词源：city (n.) + -ful (suffix).  "City" comes from Old French "cité"  and ultimately from Latin "civitas" meaning "citizenship, community." "-ful" is a suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by."
