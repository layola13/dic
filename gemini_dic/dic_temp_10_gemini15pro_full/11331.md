arecoline: /əˈrɛkəliːn/ | n. |槟榔碱| An alkaloid found in the areca nut (betel nut), with parasympathomimetic properties. It stimulates the central nervous system and is used in veterinary medicine. | Arecoline is used to treat intestinal worms in animals. 槟榔碱用于治疗动物的肠道蠕虫。|  Arecoline increases saliva production. 槟榔碱会增加唾液分泌。 | Synonyms:  choline ester | Antonyms: atropine, scopolamine | noun
