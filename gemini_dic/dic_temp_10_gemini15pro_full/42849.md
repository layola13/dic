court-martialling: /ˌkɔːrt ˈmɑːrʃəlɪŋ/ /ˌkɔːrt ˈmɑːrʃəliŋ/
noun
军事法庭审判，军事法庭的传讯
The act of bringing someone before a military court and formally accusing them of breaking military law.
The soldier faced court-martialling for desertion. 这名士兵因擅离职守面临军事法庭审判。
court-martial, trial by court-martial
acquittal
n.
