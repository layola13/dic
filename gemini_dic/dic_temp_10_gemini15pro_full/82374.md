growthful:/ˈɡrəʊθfʊl/| adj.|富有成长的，促进增长的|Characterized by or promoting growth; conducive to growth.  This word is less common than "growing" or "growth-oriented".|例句：The company fostered a growthful environment for its employees.  公司为员工营造了一个有利于成长的环境。

例句：The growthful economy provided many job opportunities. 蓬勃发展的经济提供了许多就业机会。|近义词：growing, developing, burgeoning, flourishing, thriving|反义词：stagnant, declining, shrinking|词性:形容词
