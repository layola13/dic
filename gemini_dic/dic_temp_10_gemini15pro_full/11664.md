armless:/ˈɑːrmləs/| adj.|无臂的，没有扶手的|Lacking arms; having no arms.  Also, lacking armrests (as in a chair).|The armless statue stood in the center of the courtyard.  这座无臂雕像矗立在庭院中央。

The armless chair was uncomfortable to sit in for long periods. 这张没有扶手的椅子坐久了很不舒服。|Near Synonyms: limbless, handless (for people),  without armrests (for furniture)|Opposite: armed, having arms, having armrests|adjective
