indiest:/ˈɪndiɪst/| adj.|最独立的, 最另类的 (superlative of indie)|This is the indiest band I've ever heard. 这是我听过的最另类的乐队。|近义词:most alternative, most unconventional, most non-mainstream|反义词:most mainstream, most commercial, most conventional|词性:形容词

词源: "indiest" is the superlative form of "indie." "Indie" is short for "independent," originally referring to music produced outside major record labels.  The term has broadened to encompass other forms of art, culture, and fashion that are perceived as unconventional, non-mainstream, or alternative.
