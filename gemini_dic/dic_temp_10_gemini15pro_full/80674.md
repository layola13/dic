gore:/ɡɔːr/| n.|血块，血污；三角形布; (衣服上的)楔形布|a piece of cloth shaped like a triangle or a thin piece of rubber, etc. that is sewn into something such as a piece of clothing or the side of a tire to make it wider or stronger; blood, especially blood from a wound that has become thick|The movie was full of gore.|近义词：bloodshed, carnage|反义词: peace, tranquility|词性:名词

gore:/ɡɔːr/| v.|用角顶伤；(尤指动物)用角抵伤|to wound with a horn or tusk|The bull gored the matador.|近义词：stab, pierce|反义词: heal, soothe|词性:动词
