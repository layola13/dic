linoleumed:/lɪˈnəʊliəmd/
|adj.|铺了油毡的；覆盖着油毡的|Covered or furnished with linoleum.|The linoleumed floor was easy to clean. 油毡地板很容易清洁。|近义词: linoleum-covered|反义词: (No direct opposite, consider "uncovered," "bare," "wooden," "carpeted," etc. depending on the specific alternative flooring.)|词性:形容词
Etymology->
From linoleum + -ed. Linoleum itself comes from Latin *linum* (flax) + *oleum* (oil).
