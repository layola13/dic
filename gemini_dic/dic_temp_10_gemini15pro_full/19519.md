bifacial:/baɪˈfeɪʃəl/
adjective
双面的；两面的
Having two faces or surfaces.
This plant has bifacial leaves, with different characteristics on the top and bottom. 这种植物有双面叶片，顶部和底部的特征不同。

Bifacial solar panels generate electricity from both sides, increasing their efficiency. 双面太阳能电池板两面都能发电，从而提高了效率。

synonym:double-sided, two-sided
antonym:unilateral, single-sided
词性:adjective
