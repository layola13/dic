hoppingly:/ˈhɒpɪŋli/ /ˈhɑːpɪŋli/| adv.|跳跃地，一蹦一跳地|in a hopping manner; by hopping.  It describes the way something is done, specifically by hopping.|The rabbit moved hoppingly across the field. 兔子一蹦一跳地穿过田野。|近义词：jumpily, skipingly|反义词:smoothly, steadily|词性:副词
词源:From hop (v.) + -ing + -ly.  "Hop" comes from Old English *hoppan, of Germanic origin.
