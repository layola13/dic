inter-campus:/ˌɪntərˈkæmpəs/| adj.|校际的，校园间的|Existing or occurring between two or more campuses of a university or college.|例句：The inter-campus bus service is very convenient. 校际巴士服务非常方便。|例句：There is a strong inter-campus rivalry in sports. 校园间的体育竞争非常激烈。|近义词：intercollegiate, interuniversity|反义词：intra-campus|词性：形容词

词源:
"inter-" (prefix): Latin, meaning "between," "among," "mutually," "reciprocally."
"campus": Latin, meaning "field," "plain."
