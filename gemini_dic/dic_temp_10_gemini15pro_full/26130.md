bustier:/ˈbʌsti.eɪ/ (US), /ˈbʌstɪeɪ/ (UK)| n. |紧身胸衣，塑身衣|A close-fitting strapless garment for women that supports or shapes the breasts and upper torso, often worn as lingerie or under other clothing.|She wore a black bustier under her sheer blouse. 她穿了一件黑色紧身胸衣在透明衬衫里面。|近义词：corset, basque|反义词:bra (a more general term)|noun
