nomocracy:/nɒˈmɒkrəsi/ | n. | 法治；依法治国 | A system of governance where all are subject to and accountable under the law. |  The ideal of nomocracy is that everyone, including those in power, are bound by the same laws. 法治的理想是每个人，包括当权者，都受同样的法律约束。

The protesters demanded nomocracy and an end to corruption. 抗议者要求法治和结束腐败。 | 近义词: rule of law, constitutionalism | 反义词: tyranny, despotism, autocracy | 词性: noun
