head-off:/hɛd ˈɒf/ (US) /hed ˈɒf/ (UK)| phrasal verb |阻止，防止，拦住|To prevent something from happening, especially something unpleasant.|The police managed to head off a riot. 警察设法阻止了一场骚乱。|
head-off:| phrasal verb | 拦截; 抢在...之前|To intercept someone or something.|They headed him off at the pass. 他们在山口拦截了他。|
head-off:| phrasal verb |  使改变方向; 使偏离|To cause someone or something to change direction.|The shepherd headed off the sheep before they reached the cliff. 牧羊人在羊群到达悬崖之前把它们赶走了。|近义词:prevent, avert, intercept, forestall, preempt|反义词:allow, permit, encourage|词性:phrasal verb
