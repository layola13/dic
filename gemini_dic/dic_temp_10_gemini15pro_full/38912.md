confession:/kənˈfeʃən/ | n. | 忏悔，供认，坦白 | an admission of wrongdoing or guilt; a statement declaring one's religious beliefs | He made a full confession to the police.  他向警方做了完整的供认。 | synonyms: admission, acknowledgment, avowal | antonyms: denial, concealment | noun

confess:/kənˈfes/ | v. | 承认，坦白，忏悔 | admit something, typically reluctantly; declare one's sins formally to a priest |  I must confess I didn't expect him to win. 我必须承认，我没有料到他会赢。 | synonyms: admit, own, concede | antonyms: deny, conceal | verb
