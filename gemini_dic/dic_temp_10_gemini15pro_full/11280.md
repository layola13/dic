arctogeal:/ɑːrkˈtoʊdʒiːəl/
adjective
北极地区的，与北极有关的
Relating to the Arctic region or its animals and plants.
The arctogeal flora is adapted to the harsh climate.  北极地区的植物群已经适应了恶劣的气候。
Polar bears are an example of arctogeal fauna. 北极熊是北极地区动物群的一个例子。
近义词:Arctic, polar, northern
反义词:Antarctic, southern
词性:adjective

词源:
From Greek *arktos* "bear" (also, by extension, "Great Bear" constellation and "north") + -geal, a suffix meaning "pertaining to".
