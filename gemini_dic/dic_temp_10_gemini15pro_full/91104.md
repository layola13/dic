hyaena:/haɪˈiːnə/ /haɪˈɛnə/| n. |鬣狗|A doglike carnivorous mammal of Africa and southern Asia, having coarse fur, a sloping back, and powerful jaws for crushing bones. |例句：The hyaena laughed eerily in the night. 夜晚鬣狗发出令人毛骨悚然的叫声。

例句：Hyaenas are often scavengers, but also hunt in packs. 鬣狗通常是食腐动物，但也成群结队地捕猎。|近义词：scavenger, predator|反义词: prey|词性:noun
