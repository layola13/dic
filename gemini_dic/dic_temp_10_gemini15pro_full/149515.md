西班牙海鲜饭(paella的复数)
A saffron-flavored dish of rice cooked with shellfish, chicken, ham, etc., in a large shallow pan.  A Spanish dish of rice, saffron, chicken, seafood, etc., cooked and served in a large shallow pan.
