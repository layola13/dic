leaches:/ˈliːtʃɪz/| n.|水蛭; 榨取他人钱财者|Small, bloodsucking worms that live in water. Also used figuratively to describe people who exploit others for personal gain. |The leeches attached themselves to the swimmer's legs. 水蛭附着在游泳者的腿上。|The landlord was a leech, constantly raising the rent.  房东像个吸血鬼，不停地提高租金。|Synonyms: parasites, bloodsuckers, sponges, freeloaders|Antonyms: benefactors, donors, contributors|noun

leaches:/ˈliːtʃɪz/| v.|吸取; 滤掉|To drain away; to remove by dissolving and washing away. Also used figuratively to describe draining resources or emotions.|The rain leached the nutrients from the soil. 雨水把土壤中的养分滤掉了。|His negativity leached the joy from the room. 他的消极情绪榨干了房间里的快乐。|Synonyms: drain, seep, filter, extract, deplete|Antonyms: replenish, nourish, augment|verb
