fly-off-the-handle:/ˌflaɪ ɔːf ðə ˈhændl/
idiom
突然发怒，勃然大怒
to suddenly lose your temper and become angry
例句：He's got a really bad temper and flies off the handle at the slightest thing. 他脾气很坏，一点点小事就勃然大怒。

例句：When I told her the news, she completely flew off the handle. 当我告诉她这个消息时，她完全失控了。
近义词：lose one's temper, get angry, blow up, hit the roof, go ballistic
反义词：keep one's cool, remain calm, stay composed
