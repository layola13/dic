floorwalker:/ˈflɔːrˌwɔːkər/ /ˈflɔːrˌwɑːkər/
noun
巡视员，楼层管理员
A person employed to supervise the sales staff and customers in a large shop or store.
The floorwalker directed the confused customer to the shoe department.  巡视员将这位困惑的顾客指引到鞋部。
