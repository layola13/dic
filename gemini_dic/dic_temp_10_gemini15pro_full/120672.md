misadd:/ˌmɪsˈæd/
verb
错误地加；加错
to add incorrectly
I misadded the figures and got the wrong total. 我把数字加错了，得到了错误的总数。
The cashier misadded the bill and overcharged me. 收银员把账单加错了，多收了我钱。
miscalculate, miscompute
calculate correctly, compute correctly
v.
