cocounselled:/ˌkoʊˈkaʊnsəld/
verb
共同辩护，共同担任法律顾问
To act as a joint legal counsel, working alongside another lawyer or team of lawyers on the same case.
The experienced attorney cocounselled the case with a junior partner.  经验丰富的律师与一位资浅合伙人共同处理此案。
The two lawyers cocounselled on the complex litigation. 两位律师共同处理了这起复杂的诉讼。
Synonyms: collaborated, partnered, teamed up
Antonyms: worked independently, solo representation
词性: verb
