meltingly:/ˈmɛltɪŋli/| adv.|融化地，感人地|In a way that suggests melting, either literally or figuratively.  It implies a smooth, gradual, and often deeply affecting transition.|She looked at him meltingly. 她深情款款地看着他。|Synonyms: tenderly, softly, sweetly, affectionately|Antonyms: coldly, harshly, indifferently|Word Type: Adverb
