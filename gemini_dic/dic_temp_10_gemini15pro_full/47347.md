de-stratify: /diːˈstrætɪfaɪ/
verb
消除分层，使均匀
To eliminate or reduce the stratification of something, typically a liquid or gas, so that it becomes more uniform throughout.
The engineers de-stratified the lake by introducing air bubbles. 工程师们通过引入气泡来消除湖泊的分层。
The process is used to de-stratify the temperature gradients in the storage tank.  这个过程用于消除储罐中的温度梯度。
