horsepower:/ˈhɔːrsˌpaʊər/ (US), /ˈhɔːsˌpaʊə(r)/ (UK)| n.|马力|A unit of power equal to 550 foot-pounds per second (745.7 watts).|The car has 200 horsepower. 这辆车有200马力。|Synonyms: power, capacity|Antonyms: weakness, impotence|noun

horsepowers:/ˈhɔːrsˌpaʊərz/ (US), /ˈhɔːsˌpaʊəz/ (UK)| n.|马力(复数)|Plural form of horsepower.|Both engines produce over 1,000 horsepowers. 两个引擎都能产生超过1000马力。|Synonyms: powers, capacities|Antonyms: weaknesses, impotences|noun
