branchial: /ˈbræŋkiəl/| adj.|鳃的，鳃状的|Relating to the gills of fish and other aquatic animals.  Pertaining to or resembling gills.|The branchial arches support the gills of a fish. 鱼的鳃弓支撑着它的鳃。|Synonyms: gill,  gill-like|Antonyms: pulmonary|adjective

branchial: /ˈbræŋkiəl/| n.|鳃|A branchial structure. |The tadpole's branchials gradually disappeared as it metamorphosed into a frog. 蝌蚪的鳃在它变形成青蛙的过程中逐渐消失。|Synonyms: gill|Antonyms: lung|noun
