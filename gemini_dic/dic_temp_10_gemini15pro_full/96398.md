indispensability:/ˌɪndɪˌspɛnsəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可或缺性；必要性 |The quality of being absolutely necessary or essential. |  The indispensability of water to life is undeniable.  水对生命的重要性是不可否认的。| synonyms:  essentiality, necessity, vitalness, cruciality | antonyms: dispensability, unimportance, inessentiality | noun

indispensable: /ˌɪndɪˈspɛnsəbəl/ | adj. | 不可缺少的；绝对必要的 | Absolutely necessary; essential. | A good dictionary is indispensable for learning a foreign language.  一本好词典对于学习外语是必不可少的。| synonyms: essential, crucial, vital, necessary, requisite | antonyms: dispensable, unnecessary, nonessential, unimportant | adjective
