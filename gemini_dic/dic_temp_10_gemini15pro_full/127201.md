naturopathic:/ˌneɪtʃəroʊˈpæθɪk/ |adj.|自然疗法的，顺势疗法的|Relating to or characteristic of naturopathic medicine, a system of alternative medicine based on the theory that diseases can be successfully treated or prevented without the use of drugs, by techniques such as control of diet, exercise, and massage.|She sought treatment from a naturopathic physician. 她向一位自然疗法医生寻求治疗。|Synonyms: holistic, alternative, complementary|Antonyms: conventional, allopathic|adjective

naturopathy:/ˌneɪtʃəˈrɒpəθi/ |n.|自然疗法|A system of alternative medicine based on the theory that diseases can be successfully treated or prevented without the use of drugs, by techniques such as control of diet, exercise, and massage.|She is a strong believer in naturopathy. 她非常相信自然疗法。|Synonyms: holistic medicine, alternative medicine|Antonyms: conventional medicine, allopathic medicine|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From nature + -pathy (from Greek -patheia "suffering, feeling").  The term "naturopathy" was coined in the late 19th century.
