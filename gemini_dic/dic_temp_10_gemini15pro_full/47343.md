de-sludges:/diːˈslʌdʒɪz/
verb
除淤；清除污泥
Removes sludge or sediment from something.
The workers de-sludged the water tank. 工人们清除了水箱中的污泥。
The machine de-sludges the sewage automatically.  这台机器自动清除污水中的污泥。
Synonyms: clean, clear, purge
Antonyms: contaminate, pollute
v.

de-sludging:/diːˈslʌdʒɪŋ/
noun
除淤；清除污泥
The act of removing sludge or sediment.
The de-sludging of the tank is necessary to maintain water quality. 清理水箱中的污泥对于维持水质是必要的。
Regular de-sludging prevents the buildup of harmful bacteria. 定期清除污泥可以防止有害细菌的滋生。
Synonyms: cleaning, clearing, purging
Antonyms: contamination, pollution
n.
