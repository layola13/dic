accomplishers: /əˈkʌmplɪʃərz/
n.
完成者；成就者；成功人士
People who have accomplished something significant; achievers.
The awards ceremony honored the accomplishers in various fields.  颁奖典礼表彰了各个领域的成就者。

The company recognized the accomplishers of the sales team. 公司表彰了销售团队的佼佼者。

近义词: achievers, doers, performers, winners
反义词: failures, underachievers
词性: 名词(plural)

词源:
From the verb "accomplish" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting a person who performs the action of the verb).  "Accomplish" comes from Old French "accomplir," from Latin "ad" (to) + "complere" (to fill up, complete).
