oxidize:/ˈɑːksɪdaɪz/ (US), /ˈɒksɪdaɪz/ (UK)| v. |氧化，生锈|To combine or become combined chemically with oxygen.|Iron oxidizes to form rust. 铁氧化形成锈蚀。|近义词: rust, corrode, tarnish|反义词: reduce|词性:动词

oxidize:/ˈɑːksɪdaɪz/ (US), /ˈɒksɪdaɪz/ (UK)| n. |氧化|The process or result of oxidizing or being oxidized.|The oxidize on the copper gave it a green patina. 铜上的氧化物使其呈现绿色铜锈。|近义词: oxidation, rusting, corrosion|反义词: reduction|词性:名词

词源: From French *oxyder*, from *oxygène* (oxygen) + *-iser* (suffix forming verbs). Ultimately from Greek *oxys* (sharp, acid) + *genes* (forming).
