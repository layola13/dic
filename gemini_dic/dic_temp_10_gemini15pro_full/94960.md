in-so-many-words:/ɪn ˌsoʊ ˈmeni ˈwɜːrdz/
adv.
直截了当地；明白地说
explicitly; plainly
He didn't say so in so many words, but he implied that I was lying. 他没有明说，但暗示我在撒谎。
近义词: explicitly, directly, plainly, bluntly, frankly
反义词: indirectly, implicitly, vaguely, subtly
