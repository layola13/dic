non-breeding:/ˌnɒnˈbriːdɪŋ/ | adj. | 非繁殖的，非繁殖期的 | Not involved in breeding; relating to a period when animals do not breed. | Many birds migrate to warmer climates during the non-breeding season. 许多鸟类在非繁殖季节迁徙到气候温暖的地区。|  Birds often have duller plumage during their non-breeding season. 鸟类在非繁殖季节的羽毛通常比较暗淡。 | synonyms: non-reproductive | antonyms: breeding, reproductive | adjective
