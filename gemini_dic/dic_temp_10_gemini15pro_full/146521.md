overgesticulated: /ˌoʊvərjɛˈstɪkjʊleɪtɪd/
verb (past tense)
做手势过度；夸张地做手势
used to describe someone who made excessive or exaggerated hand gestures, often to emphasize a point but sometimes to the point of distraction.
The nervous presenter overgesticulated throughout his speech, distracting the audience from his message.  这位紧张的演讲者在整个演讲过程中都做手势过度，分散了观众的注意力。

He overgesticulated wildly while telling the story, making it seem even more unbelievable. 他在讲故事时疯狂地做着手势，使故事显得更加难以置信。

Synonym: overacted, exaggerated
Antonym: understated, subdued
