cupulate:/ˈkʌpjʊleɪt/ | adj. | 杯状的；像杯子的 | Shaped like a cup; cup-shaped. |  The petals of the flower are cupulate, forming a small bowl that collects rainwater.  这朵花的花瓣是杯状的，形成了一个小碗，可以收集雨水。| Synonym: cup-shaped, concave, hollowed | Antonym: convex, bulging | Adjective

cupulate:/ˈkʌpjʊleɪt/ | v. | 形成杯状 | To form into the shape of a cup. | The craftsman carefully cupulated the silver sheet into a small bowl.  工匠小心地把银片做成一个小碗的形状。| Synonym: mold, shape, hollow | Antonym: flatten, straighten | Verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cupulatus, past participle of *cupulare "to shape like a cup," from cupula "small cask, little cup," diminutive of cupa "tub, cask, vat."
