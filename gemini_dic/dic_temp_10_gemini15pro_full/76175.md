fussation:/fʌˈseɪʃən/
n.
小题大做；激动；不安
A state of agitation, excitement, or excessive activity, often about something unimportant.
She was in a fussation about the upcoming party. 她对即将到来的派对感到非常不安。
anxiety, agitation, excitement, worry, flutter
calm, composure, tranquility
noun
