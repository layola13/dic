malarial:/məˈleriəl/ | adj. |疟疾的，患疟疾的| Relating to, caused by, or typical of malaria. |  She suffered from malarial fever. 她患了疟疾。 |Synonyms: malaric | Antonyms: healthy, well | Adjective


Etymology->
From French *malarien* (19th century), from Italian *mal'aria* ("bad air"), ultimately from Latin *mala* ("bad") + *aria* ("air").  The disease was formerly thought to be caused by bad air, especially from swamps.
