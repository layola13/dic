megaphyton:/ˈmeɡəˌfaɪtɒn/| n. |巨叶植物；大型叶|A plant, especially a fern, with large, frondlike leaves, typically more than 20 centimetres long.  A megaphyll. |Example 1: Ferns are a classic example of a megaphyton, with their large, complex fronds.  蕨类植物是巨叶植物的典型例子，它们有大型复杂的叶子。

Example 2: The evolution of the megaphyton was a key step in the development of terrestrial plants. 巨叶植物的进化是陆生植物发展中的关键一步。|近义词：macrophyll|反义词:microphyll|词性:noun
