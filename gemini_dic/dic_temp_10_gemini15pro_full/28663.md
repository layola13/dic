carina:/kəˈraɪnə/ /kəˈriːnə/| n. |龙骨，气管隆突|A keel-shaped structure or ridge.  Specifically, a. The keel-like cartilage at the bifurcation of the trachea (windpipe). b. A downward projection of the breastbone of birds.|

例句：The carina is a crucial landmark for bronchoscopy.  气管隆突是支气管镜检查的关键标志。

例句：The bird's powerful carina facilitates its flight.  这只鸟强壮的龙骨有助于它的飞行。

近义词：keel (for anatomical sense), ridge|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
