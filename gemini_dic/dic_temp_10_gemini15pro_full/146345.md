词源:

"Over-" (prefix) + "fierce" (adjective) + "-ness" (suffix)

* "Over-" indicates an excess or exaggeration.
* "Fierce" means exhibiting or given to extreme intensity of feeling or behavior.
* "-ness" transforms the adjective "fierce" into a noun, signifying the state or quality of being fierce.  The plural "-es" indicates multiple instances of this quality.
