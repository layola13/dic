embracingly:/ɪmˈbreɪsɪŋli/
adverb
拥抱地，亲切地
in a way that shows affection or acceptance, often with a hug.
She greeted him embracingly, her arms wrapped tightly around him. 她热情地拥抱他，双臂紧紧地搂着他。
近义词:affectionately, warmly, lovingly
反义词:coldly, distantly, indifferently
词性:adverb

embrace:/ɪmˈbreɪs/
verb/noun
拥抱/拥抱
(v.) hold (someone) closely in one's arms, especially as a sign of affection; accept readily or enthusiastically. (n.) an act of holding someone closely in one's arms.
(v.) He embraced her tenderly. 他温柔地拥抱她。
(n.)  She gave him a warm embrace. 她给了他一个温暖的拥抱。
词性:verb/noun
