nonemigrants:/ˌnɑːnˈemɪɡrənts/ | n. |非移民；非移居者|People who enter a country temporarily, for a specific purpose such as tourism, work, or study, without the intention of permanently settling there.  Those who hold a non-immigrant visa.|例句：The nonemigrants were required to leave the country after their visas expired.  非移民的签证到期后必须离开该国。

例句：The government has implemented new regulations for nonemigrants seeking employment. 政府对寻求就业的非移民实施了新的规定。|近义词：visitors, temporary residents, aliens|反义词：immigrants, permanent residents, citizens|词性:Noun
