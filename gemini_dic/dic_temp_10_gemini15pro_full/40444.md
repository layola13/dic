contrastingly:/kənˈtrɑːstɪŋli/
adv.
对比地，截然不同地
in a way that shows a striking difference; by contrast
Example: Contrastingly, his brother is very outgoing.  与之相反，他的兄弟非常外向。
Example: The two sisters, contrastingly, had very different personalities.  这对姐妹的性格截然不同。
Synonyms: differently, conversely, on the other hand, in contrast
Antonyms: similarly, likewise, comparably
词性: 副词
