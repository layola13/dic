canalising:/ˈkænəlaɪzɪŋ/ /ˈkænəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
引导的；限制的；管控的
Restricting or directing something into a particular course.
The government is trying to canalise the flow of refugees. 政府正试图控制难民潮。
近义词: channel, direct, guide, restrict
反义词: disperse, scatter, spread
词性: adjective

canalize:/ˈkænəlaɪz/ /ˈkænəˌlaɪz/
verb
引导；限制；管控
To restrict or direct something into a particular course.
His energies were canalized into writing.  他的精力都倾注于写作。
近义词: channel, direct, guide, restrict
反义词: disperse, scatter, spread
词性: verb


Etymology: From French canaliser, from canal (“canal”).
