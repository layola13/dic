halfcock:/ˈhæfˌkɑːk/ | adv. adj. n.| 半待发状态;  不完全的; |At halfcock, the gun is dangerous. 在半待发状态下，枪很危险。|近义词：half-cocked, unprepared |反义词: fully cocked, prepared |词性: 副词, 形容词, 名词

Etymology:
From "half" + "cock" (referring to the action of cocking a firearm).  The term originally referred to the position of a firearm's hammer halfway between fully cocked and uncocked.
