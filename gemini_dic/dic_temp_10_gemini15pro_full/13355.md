atm:/eɪ tiː ˈɛm/ (US), /eɪ tiː ˈɛm/ (UK)| n. |自动取款机|Automated Teller Machine, a machine that allows bank customers to withdraw cash, make deposits, and check their account balances.|I stopped at the atm to get some cash before going to the concert. 我在去音乐会前在自动取款机取了些现金。|Synonym: cash machine, cashpoint (UK)|Antonym: bank teller|noun

atm:/eɪ tiː ˈɛm/ (US), /eɪ tiː ˈɛm/ (UK)| abbreviation. | 在…的大气压下|at the pressure of the atmosphere|The experiment was conducted atm.  实验是在一个大气压下进行的。|Synonym: at atmospheric pressure|Antonym: in a vacuum|abbreviation


ETYMOLOGY->
