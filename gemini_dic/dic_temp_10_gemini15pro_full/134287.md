noninheritability:/ˌnɒnɪnˌhɛrɪtəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可遗传性，非遗传性 | The noninheritability of acquired characteristics is a cornerstone of modern genetics.  |  The study demonstrated the noninheritability of the learned behavior.  | 近义词: non-heredity | 反义词: heritability | 词性: 名词

词源:  non- (not) + inherit (继承) + -ability (名词后缀，表示性质或状态)  Ultimately from Latin *in- + *hereditare, from heres (heir).
