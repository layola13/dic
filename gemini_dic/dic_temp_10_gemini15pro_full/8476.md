antiatheists:/ˌæntiˈeɪθiɪsts/
n.|反无神论者|Someone who opposes antiatheism, which is opposition to atheism.  They essentially defend atheists and the right to be an atheist.  They may or may not be atheists themselves. | Antiatheists argue that discrimination against atheists is unjust. 反无神论者认为歧视无神论者是不公平的。|
Synonyms:  pro-atheism advocates, defenders of atheism,  secular humanists (sometimes)
Antonyms: anti-atheist, critic of atheism (These are closer to describing the opposite stance, though not perfect antonyms as the focus is different)
词性: noun
