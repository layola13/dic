nonpragmatics: /ˌnɒnpræɡˈmætɪks/ (British), /ˌnɑːnpræɡˈmætɪks/ (American)| n.| 非语用学|  The study of aspects of language that are not directly related to the context of use, such as grammar, phonology, and semantics.  It focuses on the literal meaning and formal structure of language rather than its practical application in communication. |  His research in nonpragmatics focuses on the evolution of grammatical structures. 他的非语用学研究集中在语法结构的演变上。|  Nonpragmatic analysis of the sentence revealed its underlying syntactic ambiguity. 对该句子的非语用分析揭示了其潜在的句法歧义。| 近义词: non-pragmatic (adj.), formal linguistics | 反义词: pragmatics | 词性: noun
