loopy-loo:/ˈluːpi luː/| adj.|傻乎乎的，疯疯癫癫的|Silly or crazy; often used playfully or affectionately.|例句：He's been acting a bit loopy-loo lately. 他最近行为有点疯疯癫癫的。

例句：She gave me a loopy-loo grin. 她冲我傻乎乎地咧嘴一笑。|近义词：crazy, silly, goofy, wacky, nutty|反义词：sane, sensible, rational|词性:形容词

词源:  Likely a playful reduplication of "loopy," which itself is derived from "loop," referring to something twisted or out of shape.  The reduplication adds emphasis and a childish or affectionate tone.
