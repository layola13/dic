annualised:/ˈænjuəlaɪzd/ | adj. |年度化的，按年计算的 |Expressed or calculated as an annual rate. | The annualised return on this investment is 8%. 这项投资的年化回报率为 8%。|  The company's annualised revenue was $1 billion. 这家公司的年收入为 10 亿美元。 | Synonyms: yearly, annual, per annum | Antonyms: monthly, quarterly, daily | adjective

annualize:/ˈænjuəlaɪz/ | v. |按年计算，年化 |To calculate or express (something, such as a rate of return) as an annual rate. | We annualize the figures to compare them year to year. 我们将这些数字按年计算以便进行年度比较。 | The bank annualizes interest rates on savings accounts. 银行按年计算储蓄账户的利率。 | Synonyms: calculate yearly, compute annually | Antonyms: N/A | verb
