commissural:/kəˈmɪʃərəl/| adj.|连合的；连合纤维的|Relating to a commissure, a band of nerve tissue connecting corresponding parts on opposite sides of the brain or spinal cord.|The commissural fibers connect the two hemispheres of the brain.|近义词：connecting, joining, uniting|反义词:separating, dividing|adjective

commissure:/ˈkɒmɪʃə(r)/| n.|连合；接缝|A point or line of union, especially between two anatomical parts, such as the point of junction between two bones or parts of an organ. Also: a band of nerve tissue connecting corresponding parts on opposite sides of the brain or spinal cord.|The corpus callosum is the largest commissure in the brain.|近义词：junction, joint, connection|反义词:separation, division|noun
