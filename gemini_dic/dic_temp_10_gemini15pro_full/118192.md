mentoring:/ˈmentərɪŋ/ /ˈmɛntɔːrɪŋ/
n.|指导, 辅导, 导师制|The act or process of helping and advising someone, especially a younger or less experienced person, over a period of time.|She benefited greatly from his mentoring. 她从他的指导中受益匪浅。|guidance, coaching, tutoring|neglect, abandonment|noun
