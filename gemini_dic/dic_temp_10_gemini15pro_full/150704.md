parabola:/pəˈræbələ/ (US), /pəˈræbəlʌ/ (UK)| n. |抛物线|A symmetrical open plane curve formed by the intersection of a cone with a plane parallel to its side.  The path of a projectile under the influence of gravity ideally follows a parabolic curve.|The ball followed a parabola through the air.  球在空中划出一道抛物线。|Synonyms:  quadratic curve|Antonyms:  N/A|Noun
