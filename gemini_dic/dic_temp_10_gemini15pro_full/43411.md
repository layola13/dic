crannog:/ˈkrænɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈkrænɔːɡ/ (US)| n. |人工岛，湖上居所|An ancient artificial island or dwelling built in a lake, river, or marsh, especially in Ireland and Scotland. |例句：Archaeologists discovered the remains of a crannog in the lake. 考古学家在湖中发现了人工岛的遗迹。

例句：The crannog provided a safe refuge from invaders.  这个人工岛提供了一个躲避入侵者的安全避难所。|近义词：lake dwelling, artificial island|反义词: mainland |词性:noun
