nonrustic:/nɒnˈrʌstɪk/ | adj. | 非乡村的，非质朴的 | Not rustic; characteristic of or suitable for a city or town; sophisticated, elegant. |  She prefers a nonrustic, modern style of decorating. 她更喜欢非乡村的，现代的装修风格。 | The restaurant had a decidedly nonrustic atmosphere. 这家餐馆的氛围绝对不是乡村风格的。 | 近义词: urban, sophisticated, polished, refined, elegant | 反义词: rustic, rural, country, simple, unsophisticated | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- + rustic.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not". "Rustic" comes from the Latin word "rusticus," meaning "rural" or "of the country."
