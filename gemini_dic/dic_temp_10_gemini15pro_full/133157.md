nonethnologic: /ˌnɒnˌɛθnəˈlɒdʒɪk/ /ˌnɑːnˌɛθnəˈlɑːdʒɪk/
adjective
非民族学的
Not pertaining to ethnology; not related to the study of human cultures and their comparative development.
This artifact's significance is nonethnologic, relating more to early metallurgy.  这个文物的意义不在于民族学，而更多地与早期冶金学有关。
His research interests are primarily nonethnologic, focusing on social psychology. 他的研究兴趣主要是非民族学的，集中在社会心理学上。
近义词: non-anthropological, non-cultural
反义词: ethnological, anthropological, cultural
词性: adjective
