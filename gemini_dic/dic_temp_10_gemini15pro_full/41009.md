copans:/ˈkoʊpənz/| n.|优惠券，代金券|A certificate entitling the holder to a discount or other benefit.|I have some copans for the grocery store. 我有一些杂货店的优惠券。|近义词：coupon, voucher, certificate|反义词: surcharge, extra charge|名词

词源:  "Copans" appears to be a misspelling of "coupons."  The word "coupon" comes from French "couper" meaning "to cut." Originally, coupons were physically cut from a larger document.
