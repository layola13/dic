nonorganization:/ˌnɒnɔːɡənɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. | 非组织，缺乏组织 | Absence of organization; lack of systematic arrangement or structure. |  The nonorganization of the event led to chaos. 活动缺乏组织导致一片混乱。| The nonorganization of his files made it difficult to find anything.  他的文件缺乏组织，很难找到任何东西。 | 近义词: disorder, disorganization, chaos, mess | 反义词: organization, order, structure, system | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "organization"
