non-spatially:/nɒnˈspeɪʃəli/ | adv. | 非空间地 | Not related to or involving space; not arranged or existing according to spatial distribution. |  The data was analyzed non-spatially, focusing on temporal trends.  数据以非空间方式分析，重点关注时间趋势。| Synonym: nonspatially | Antonym: spatially | Adverb

non-spatially:/nɒnˈspeɪʃəli/ | adj. | 非空间的 | Not related to or involving space; not arranged or existing according to spatial distribution. (Although less common as an adjective, it can be used attributively.) |  A non-spatial analysis of the data revealed interesting patterns. 对数据的非空间分析揭示了有趣的模式。| Synonym: nonspatial | Antonym: spatial | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "spatial" (related to space). "Spatial" ultimately derives from Latin "spatium" (space).
