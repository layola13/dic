ingestible:/ɪnˈdʒɛstəbl/| adj.|可食用的，可摄入的|Something that is ingestible can be safely swallowed and taken into the body.|例句：This new type of sensor is ingestible, allowing doctors to monitor patients' internal conditions. 这种新型传感器是可食用的，可以让医生监测病人的内部状况。

例句：The capsule contains an ingestible camera that takes pictures of the digestive tract.  这个胶囊含有一个可摄入的摄像头，可以拍摄消化道的照片。|近义词：edible, consumable, swallowable|反义词: inedible, non-consumable, poisonous|词性:adjective
