epitomize: /ɪˈpɪtəmaɪz/ | v. | 体现；是…的典型；代表 |  to be a perfect example of a quality or type of thing |  Her work epitomizes the best of Canadian literature. 她的作品是加拿大文学的精髓。 | synonyms: exemplify, embody, typify, represent | antonyms: misrepresent, distort | verb

epitomized: /ɪˈpɪtəmaɪzd/ | v. | 体现了；是…的典型；代表了（epitomize的过去式和过去分词） |  past tense and past participle of epitomize | The building epitomized the spirit of the age. 这座建筑体现了那个时代的精髓。 | synonyms: exemplified, embodied, typified, represented | antonyms: misrepresented, distorted | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French épithétiser, from Greek epithētos, meaning 'attributed, added'.  Ultimately from epi- ('upon, in addition') + tithenai ('to put, place').
