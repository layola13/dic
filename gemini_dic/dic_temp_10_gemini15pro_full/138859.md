nurser:/ˈnɜːrsər/ (US) /ˈnɜːsə(r)/ (UK)| n.|保育员；育婴员；托儿所；苗圃|A person who cares for young children or plants.  A place where young children are cared for during the day, or where young plants are grown for sale.|例句：The nurser cared for the babies with great tenderness. 保育员非常温柔地照顾婴儿们。

例句：They bought their rose bushes from a local nurser. 他们从当地的苗圃买了玫瑰丛。|近义词：caregiver, childminder, nanny (for people); nursery, garden center (for plants)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
