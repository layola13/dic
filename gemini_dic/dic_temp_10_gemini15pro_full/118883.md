metastatically:/ˌmɛtəˈstætɪkli/
adverb|转移地，扩散地|In a way that involves metastasis; by metastasis.  The process by which cancer spreads from one place in the body to another.|The cancer had spread metastatically to her lungs.|Synonym: diffusively, widely|Antonym: locally, contained
word root: meta- (change) + -stasis (standing) + -ic (adjective) + -ally (adverb)
