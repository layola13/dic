briberies: /ˈbraɪbəriz/ | n. | 贿赂（复数）| The act of giving or offering bribes; instances of being bribed.  It refers to multiple instances of the act of bribery. | The investigation uncovered a series of briberies involving government officials. 这项调查揭露了一系列涉及政府官员的贿赂案件。

The company was accused of briberies to win contracts.  这家公司被指控为了赢得合同而行贿。 | 近义词：graft, corruption, payoffs | 反义词：honesty, integrity | 词性: noun

词源: From the Old French word "briber" meaning "to beg, steal," later evolving to "to give a piece of bread to a beggar," and finally to "to give money to influence someone."  The suffix "-ery" denotes a place where an activity is carried out or a collection of things.
