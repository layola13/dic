half-shouted:/ˈhɑːf ˈʃaʊtɪd/ | adj. | 半喊半叫的，低声喊出的 |  Combining "half" and "shouted," it describes something said in a voice louder than normal speech but not a full shout. |  He half-shouted a warning to the children playing in the street. 他半喊半叫地向在街上玩耍的孩子们发出警告。 | Near synonyms: muttered loudly, called out softly | Near antonyms: whispered, murmured | adjective

half-shouted:/ˈhɑːf ˈʃaʊtɪd/ | v. | 半喊半叫地说 |  The past participle of "half-shout," used as an adjective to describe something uttered in a voice between a speak and a shout. | "Get back here!" he half-shouted.  “回来！”他半喊半叫道。 | Near synonyms: called out, exclaimed | Near antonyms: whispered, muttered | verb (past participle used as an adjective)
