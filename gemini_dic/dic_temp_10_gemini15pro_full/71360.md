flag:/flæɡ/ | n. v. |旗帜，标记；衰退，变弱|A piece of cloth, usually rectangular, of distinctive colour and design, used as a symbol, standard, signal, or emblem.|The American flag waved in the breeze. 美国国旗在微风中飘扬。|近义词：banner, standard, ensign|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用"symbol of surrender"表示投降的标志)|noun, verb

flag:/flæɡ/ | v. |标记，标示；衰退，变弱|To mark or identify with a flag; to become tired, weaker, or less enthusiastic.|I flagged the important points in the document. 我在文件中标记了重点。|近义词：mark, label, signal, decline, weaken|反义词: strengthen, revive|verb
