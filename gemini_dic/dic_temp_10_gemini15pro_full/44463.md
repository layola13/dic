cross-wire:/ˈkrɒswaɪə(r)/ /ˈkrɔːswaɪər/ | adj. |  交叉的，相反的，不一致的 |  Placed or lying across wires; also, characterized by conflict or inconsistency. | The two departments' plans were completely cross-wire, leading to confusion and delays.  两个部门的计划完全不一致，导致混乱和延误。 |Synonyms: conflicting, contradictory, opposed, at odds | Antonyms: consistent, harmonious, agreeing, in accord | adjective
