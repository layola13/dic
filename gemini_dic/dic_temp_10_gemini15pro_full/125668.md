munch:/mʌntʃ/| v. n.|大声咀嚼，嚼碎，咬碎|To chew steadily and often audibly.|例句：He was munching on an apple. 他正在津津有味地嚼着一个苹果。|近义词：chew, champ, gnaw, nibble|反义词: sip, swallow|词性:动词

munch:/mʌntʃ/| n.|咀嚼，咬碎|The act of munching.|例句：She took a big munch of her sandwich. 她狠狠地咬了一大口三明治。|近义词：bite, chew|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *munchen, of imitative origin.  Related to Dutch *monken*, *munken*, German *munschen*.
