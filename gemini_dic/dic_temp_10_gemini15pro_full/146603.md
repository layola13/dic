overgrievously: /ˌoʊvərˈɡriːvəsli/ | adv. | 过度悲伤地，极其沉痛地 | excessively grievously; to a degree that causes extreme grief or sorrow |  He mourned overgrievously for the loss of his pet. 他为失去宠物过度悲伤。| 近义词：excessively, extremely, immoderately, unduly | 反义词：mildly, moderately | 词性：副词

Etymology:
* over- (prefix): Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubari, meaning "above, beyond, across"
* grievously (adverb): from grievous + -ly
* grievous (adjective): from Old French *grevos, from Late Latin *gravis, meaning "heavy, burdensome, serious"
