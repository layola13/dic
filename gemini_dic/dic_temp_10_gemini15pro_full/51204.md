destining:/ˈdɛstɪnɪŋ/
verb
注定; 预定
The act of assigning a fate or purpose to someone or something.  It implies an inevitable outcome or predetermined course.
He believed he was destining for greatness. 他相信自己注定要成就一番伟业。
Synonyms: predetermining, foreordaining, predestining
Antonyms: freeing, liberating
