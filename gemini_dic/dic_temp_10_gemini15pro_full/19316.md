ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *biblis*, from Old French *bible*, from Medieval Latin *biblia*, from Latin *biblia*, plural of *biblion* ("book, paper, scroll"), from Ancient Greek *βιβλίον* (*biblion*, “book”), diminutive of *βίβλος* (*biblos*, “papyrus, book”), of pre-Greek origin.
