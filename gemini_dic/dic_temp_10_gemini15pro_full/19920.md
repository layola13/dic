biocellate: /ˌbaɪoʊˈsɛleɪt/
adjective
双细胞的; 有两个小室的
Having two cells or compartments.
This rare species of algae is biocellate, with each cell containing a unique set of organelles.  这种稀有的藻类是双细胞的，每个细胞都包含一组独特的细胞器。
The biocellate structure of this organism allows for specialized functions within each compartment.  这种生物的双细胞结构允许在每个隔室中进行专门的功能。

反义词: unicellular
词性: adjective
词源: bio- (life) + -cell (cell) + -ate (suffix forming adjectives)
