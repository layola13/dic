legationary:/ˌlɛɡəˈʃɛnəri/ | adj. n. | 使馆的；使馆人员 | Relating to a legation; a member of a legation staff. | The legationary staff was small, consisting of only a handful of diplomats. 使馆工作人员很少，只有几个外交官。 | The legationary delivered the official message from his government. 使节递交了来自他的政府的官方信息。 | 近义词: diplomatic, consular | 反义词: n/a | 词性: adjective, noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *legationarius, from Latin legatio (“legation, embassy”), from legare (“to depute, send as an ambassador”), from legatus (“envoy, ambassador”), from legare (“to send with a commission, depute, bequeat”), from lex (“law, condition”).
