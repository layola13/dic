cherrylike: /ˈtʃɛriˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 像樱桃的；樱桃似的 | Resembling a cherry in color, shape, or taste. |  Her lips were a cherrylike red. 她的嘴唇是樱桃般的红色。|  The candies were small and round, cherrylike in appearance.  这些糖果又小又圆，外观像樱桃。| 近义词: cherry-red, cerise | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  cherry + like.  "Cherry" comes from Old North French "cherise," ultimately from Latin "cerasum," meaning "cherry tree." "Like" comes from Old English "gelic," meaning "similar, resembling."
