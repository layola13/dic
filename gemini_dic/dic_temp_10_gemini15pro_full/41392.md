corn-syrups:/ˈkɔːrn ˌsɪrəps/ | n. |玉米糖浆|A thick, sweet syrup made from cornstarch, used as a sweetener in processed foods and beverages. It is a mixture of glucose, maltose, and other sugars. |Many processed foods contain corn syrups. 许多加工食品含有玉米糖浆。| The recipe calls for high-fructose corn syrup.  这个食谱需要高果糖玉米糖浆。|  synonyms: glucose syrup, fructose syrup | antonyms: sugar, honey | noun
