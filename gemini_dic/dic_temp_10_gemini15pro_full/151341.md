parkade:/pɑːrˈkeɪd/ | n. |停车场，停车库| A building or part of a building designed for parking motor vehicles.  A covered or enclosed area, often multi-storied, intended for the parking of vehicles. |  We parked the car in the parkade downtown. 我们把车停在市中心的停车库里。|  The hotel has a secure underground parkade.  这家酒店有一个安全的地下停车场。| synonyms: parking garage, parking structure, car park | antonyms:  N/A | noun
