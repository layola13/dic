expounder:/ɪkˈspaʊndər/| n.| 解释者，阐述者| someone who explains a theory, plan, etc. in detail | The professor is a renowned expounder of ancient philosophy.  这位教授是一位著名的古代哲学阐释者。|近义词：interpreter, commentator, analyst, elucidator |反义词: obfuscator, misinterpreter | noun

expound:/ɪkˈspaʊnd/| v.| 解释，阐述| to explain or talk about something in detail |  She expounded her views on the subject with great clarity. 她非常清晰地阐述了她对这个主题的看法。|近义词：explain, interpret, elucidate, clarify |反义词: confuse, obscure | verb
