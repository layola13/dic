atemporality:/ˌeɪtɛmpəˈrælɪti/
n.
永恒性；时间无关性；非时间性
The state of being unaffected by time; timelessness.
Einstein's theory of relativity suggests a certain atemporality of the universe.  爱因斯坦的相对论表明宇宙具有一定的永恒性。
The artwork explores themes of atemporality and the human condition.  这件艺术作品探讨了永恒性和人类生存状态的主题。
近义词: timelessness, eternity, perpetuity
反义词: temporality, transience, ephemerality
词性: noun

词源:  From a- (not) + temporal (relating to time) + -ity (forming abstract nouns).  Ultimately from Latin tempus (time).  源自 a-（不）+ temporal（与时间相关的）+ -ity（构成抽象名词）。最终来自拉丁语 tempus（时间）。
