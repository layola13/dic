nondivisional:/ˌnɒndɪˈvɪʒənəl/
adjective
非部门的；非事业部的
Relating to or denoting a company or other organization that is not divided into separate operating divisions.
The company adopted a nondivisional structure, allowing for greater flexibility and collaboration.  这家公司采用了非部门结构，以便实现更大的灵活性和协作。
The nondivisional nature of the organization facilitated communication across different teams.  该组织的非部门性质促进了不同团队之间的沟通。
non-departmental, unified, integrated
divisional, departmental
adjective
