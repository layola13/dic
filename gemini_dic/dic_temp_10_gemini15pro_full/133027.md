nonentrepreneurial:/ˌnɒnˌɒntrəprəˈnɜːriəl/ /ˌnɑːnˌɑːntrəprəˈnɜːriəl/
adjective
非创业的; 不具创业精神的
Not having the characteristics of an entrepreneur, such as the willingness to take risks and develop new businesses.
例句：He has a nonentrepreneurial mindset, preferring the stability of a regular job. 他有一种非创业的心态，更喜欢稳定常规的工作。

例句：The company culture became increasingly nonentrepreneurial, stifling innovation. 公司文化变得越来越不具创业精神，扼杀了创新。

近义词：unenterprising, conventional, risk-averse
反义词：entrepreneurial, enterprising, innovative
词性: adjective
