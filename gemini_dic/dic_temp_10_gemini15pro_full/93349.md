illegal:/ɪˈliːɡl/ (US), /ɪˈliːɡəl/ (UK)| adj.|非法的，违法的|Contrary to or forbidden by law, especially criminal law.|例句：It is illegal to drive without a license. 无证驾驶是违法的。

例句：Downloading copyrighted material without permission is illegal.  未经许可下载受版权保护的资料是非法的。

近义词：unlawful, illicit, prohibited, forbidden|反义词：legal, lawful, permissible|词性：形容词
