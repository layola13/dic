octodont:/ˈɒktəʊdɒnt/ (UK) /ˈɑːktəˌdɑːnt/ (US)| n. | 八齿鼠，南美啮齿动物 |A rodent of the family Octodontidae, native to South America, typically having long ears and a bushy tail.  They are characterized by having eight teeth that resemble the figure eight.|The zoo has several octodonts in its South American exhibit.|Synonym: degu (commonly used, but technically a specific type of octodont), viscacha (similar appearance) |Antonym: N/A|Noun
