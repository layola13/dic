enthuses:/ɪnˈθuːziz/ | v. | 使热情，使充满激情 | Expresses strong excitement and interest about something. | She enthuses about her new job. 她对她的新工作充满热情。|  He enthuses over the latest technological advancements. 他对最新的技术进步充满热情。 | 近义词: excites, inspires, motivates | 反义词: discourages, bores, dampens | 词性: 动词

词源: From the Greek "enthousiazein" meaning "to be inspired or possessed by a god," later evolving to "to be inspired" generally.
