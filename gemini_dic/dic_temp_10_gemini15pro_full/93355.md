illegals:/ɪˈliːɡlz/
noun
非法移民；非法滞留者
People who have entered or remained in a country without the required legal documentation.
The government is trying to reduce the number of illegals in the country. 政府正在努力减少该国非法移民的数量。
The news report focused on the plight of illegals seeking asylum.  新闻报道关注寻求政治庇护的非法移民的困境。
synonyms: undocumented immigrants,  unauthorized immigrants
antonyms: legal residents, citizens
词性: 名词
