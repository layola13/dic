consubstantiation:/ˌkɒnsəbˌstænʃiˈeɪʃən/ |/ˌkɑːnsəbˌstænʃiˈeɪʃən/ | n. |同质说，圣餐变体论 |The doctrine that the substance of the bread and wine in the Eucharist is changed into the body and blood of Christ, while the appearances of bread and wine remain.  圣餐中，饼和酒的实质变成了基督的身体和血，而饼和酒的外表仍然保留。| The theologian argued for consubstantiation, claiming both bread and wine and Christ's body and blood coexist in the Eucharist.  这位神学家主张同质说，声称饼和酒以及基督的身体和血在圣餐中共存。| 近义词: transubstantiation | 反义词: symbolic interpretation | 词性: noun
