bivalvular:/baɪˈvælvjʊlər/
adjective
双瓣的；有两瓣膜的
Having two valves.  Relating to, or affecting two valves (especially of the heart).
The doctor diagnosed her with bivalvular aortic stenosis. 医生诊断她患有双瓣主动脉瓣狭窄。
The bivalvular nature of the heart ensures unidirectional blood flow. 心脏的双瓣结构确保了血液的单向流动。
