heathenishly:/ˈhiːðənɪʃli/
adverb
异教徒式地；野蛮地
in a heathenish manner; like a heathen; barbarously.
He swore heathenishly at the broken machine. 他对着坏掉的机器破口大骂，像个野蛮人。
Synonyms: barbarously, savagely, crudely, uncivilizedly
Antonyms: piously, devoutly, reverently
词性: 副词
