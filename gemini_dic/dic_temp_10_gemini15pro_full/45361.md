cuneate:/ˈkjuːnieɪt/ /ˈkjuːniət/
| adj.|楔形的；楔状的|Shaped like a wedge; triangular with the narrow end at the base.|The cuneate leaves of the plant were a distinctive feature.  这种植物的楔形叶子是一个显著特征。|The artist used cuneate brushstrokes to create a sense of depth. 艺术家运用楔形的笔触来营造一种纵深感。|triangular, wedge-shaped|rounded, ovate|adjective
cuneate:/ˈkjuːnieɪt/ /ˈkjuːniət/
| n.|楔骨|A wedge-shaped bone, specifically the medial cuneiform bone of the foot.|The doctor examined the X-ray, pointing to a fracture in the cuneate. 医生检查了X光片，指着楔骨的骨折处。|The cuneate plays an important role in supporting the arch of the foot. 楔骨在支撑足弓方面起着重要作用。|wedge bone, medial cuneiform| |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cuneatus*, from cuneus (“wedge”).
