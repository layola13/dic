fixated:/ˈfɪkseɪtɪd/| adj.|固定的，专注的，着迷的|Excessively or obsessively attached to someone or something.|She's fixated on the idea of becoming a doctor. 她一心想成为一名医生。|近义词:obsessed, preoccupied, focused|反义词:indifferent, uninterested|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Latin *fixus*, past participle of *figere* "to fix, fasten."  The psychological sense emerged in the late 19th century.
