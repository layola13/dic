drawn:/drɔːn/
adjective
拉长的；紧张的；憔悴的
pulled out, as from a sheath; tense, strained; looking tired and unwell
Her face was drawn and pale after the ordeal.  她经历了这场磨难后，脸色憔悴苍白。
近义词: tense, strained, haggard
反义词: relaxed, refreshed

drawn:/drɔːn/
past participle of draw
绘制的；画的；吸引的
past participle of the verb "draw," meaning sketched, depicted, or attracted
The lottery numbers are drawn every Wednesday.  彩票号码每星期三开奖。
近义词: sketched, depicted, attracted, pulled
反义词: pushed, repelled


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *dragan (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *draganą (“to draw, drag, pull”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰregʰ- (“to draw, pull”). Cognate with Dutch dragen (“to carry, wear”), German tragen (“to carry, wear”), Old Norse draga (“to draw, pull”).
