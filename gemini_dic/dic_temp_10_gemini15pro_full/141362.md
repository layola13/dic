operationality:/ˌɒpəˌreɪʃəˈnælɪti/ /ˌɑːpəˌreɪʃəˈnælɪti/
n.
可操作性，实用性
The state or quality of being operational or capable of being put into operation; practicality.
The operationality of the new system is still being evaluated.  新系统的可操作性仍在评估中。

近义词: practicality, feasibility, workability, usability
反义词: impracticality, infeasibility
