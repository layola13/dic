correlational:/ˌkɔːrəˈleɪʃənl/ | adj. |相关的，关联的|Relating to, or based on correlation.  Showing a mutual relationship or connection between two or more things.|Studies have shown a correlational link between smoking and lung cancer. 研究表明吸烟和肺癌之间存在关联。|近义词:related, connected, associated|反义词:unrelated, independent, unconnected|词性:形容词
词源: From "correlation" + "-al". "Correlation" comes from the Latin "cor-" (together) and "relat-" (carried back, referred).
