destainers:/diːˈsteɪnərz/
n.|去污剂;去渍剂|Substances or agents used to remove stains. |The destainers worked effectively on the grass stains. 这些去污剂有效地去除了草渍。|近义词: stain removers, cleaning agents|反义词:staining agents|词性:名词

destain:/diːˈsteɪn/
v.|去除污渍，使褪色|To remove stains or discoloration from something. |I need to destain this tablecloth after the party.  聚会后我需要去除桌布上的污渍。|近义词: bleach, clean, whiten|反义词: stain, dye|词性:动词
