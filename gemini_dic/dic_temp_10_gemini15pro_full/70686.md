finalities:/faɪˈnælɪtiz/
n.|最终结果；定局；终结|The finalities of the legal process can be devastating.  法律程序的最终结果可能是毁灭性的。|
n.|确定性；定论；终结性|The finality of death is a difficult concept to grasp. 死亡的终结性是一个难以理解的概念。|
Synonyms: conclusions, outcomes, results, decisions, endings
Antonyms: beginnings, origins, uncertainties, indecisions
Etymology: From Late Latin *finalitas, from Latin finalis "final," from finis "end, limit, boundary."
Word type: Noun
