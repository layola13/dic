nonmoderate:/nɒnˈmɒdərət/
adjective
不温和的，非中庸的
Not moderate; immoderate.  Lacking moderation or restraint; excessive or extreme.
The group holds nonmoderate views on immigration.  该团体对移民持有不温和的观点。
The politician's nonmoderate stance alienated many voters.  这位政治家的非中庸立场疏远了许多选民。
近义词:extreme, radical, immoderate, extremist
反义词:moderate, temperate, mild, reasonable
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- + moderate.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "moderate" comes from Latin "moderatus," past participle of "moderare" meaning "to regulate, restrain."
