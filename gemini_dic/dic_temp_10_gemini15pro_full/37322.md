commemorations: /kəˌmɛməˈreɪʃənz/
n.
纪念活动；庆祝活动
acts of remembering and showing respect for someone or something important, especially people who have died or past events
The town held commemorations to mark the centenary of the end of the war.  该镇举行纪念活动，纪念战争结束一百周年。
synonyms: celebrations, observances, remembrances
antonyms: disregard, neglect
noun
