annuary: /ˈænjuəri/ | n. | 年鉴；年报 | A publication appearing yearly, often containing statistical information or a summary of the events of the year. | The school publishes an annuary every year with photos and details of all the students. 学校每年都会出版一本年鉴，里面有所有学生的照片和详细信息。| synonym: yearbook, annual, almanac | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps periodical which implies more frequent publication) | noun
