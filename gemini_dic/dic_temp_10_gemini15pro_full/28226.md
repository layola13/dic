capsize:/kæpˈsaɪz/ | v. |  倾覆，翻船 | to (cause a boat or ship to) turn upside down in the water | The boat capsized in the storm.  这艘船在暴风雨中翻了。| 近义词: overturn, upset, sink | 反义词: right, upright | 动词


capsize:/kæpˈsaɪz/ | n. | 倾覆，翻船 | the act of capsizing | The capsize of the ferry resulted in many casualties. 渡轮的倾覆导致许多人员伤亡。| 近义词: overturning, upsetting | 反义词: righting | 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *capuzar "to sink a ship by the head," from capa "cape, hood" (perhaps from Late Latin cappa).  The image is of a ship plunging headfirst like a hooded figure.
