chafeweed:/ˈtʃeɪfwiːd/
n.
糙叶紫茎泽兰
A tropical American shrub (Ageratina adenophora) that is poisonous to livestock and displaces desirable forage plants.
The chafeweed has become a noxious weed in many areas. 糙叶紫茎泽兰在许多地区已成为有害杂草。

近义词: eupatorium, snakeroot (some species are closely related)
反义词: N/A (No direct antonym for a specific plant)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "chafe" (to rub and irritate) + "weed" (undesirable plant).  The name likely refers to the plant's potential to irritate skin.
