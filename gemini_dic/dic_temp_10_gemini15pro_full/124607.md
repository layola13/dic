mousieur:/məˈsjɜː(r)/ /məˈsjɚ/| n.|先生；绅士|A French title of respect equivalent to Mr. or Sir.  A polite or respectful way to address a man.|例句：Bonjour, monsieur.  你好，先生。

例句：Excuse me, monsieur, do you have the time?  打扰一下，先生，您知道现在几点了吗？|近义词：sir, Mr., gentleman|反义词:madame, mademoiselle|词性:noun
