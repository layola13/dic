nonfastidious:/ˌnɑːnfæˈstɪdiəs/
adjective
不苛求的，不挑剔的
Not excessively particular, critical, or demanding; easy to please or satisfy.
This bacterium is nonfastidious and can grow on a variety of media.  这种细菌不苛求生长条件，可以在多种培养基上生长。
The plant is nonfastidious and thrives in various soil types. 这种植物不挑剔，在各种土壤类型中都能茁壮成长。

近义词:undemanding, adaptable, tolerant, easygoing
反义词:fastidious, picky, choosy, demanding
词性:adjective
