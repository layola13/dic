containerization:/kənˌteɪnərɪˈzeɪʃən/| n.|集装箱化，容器化|The process of packaging software code with its dependencies and libraries into a container image that can be run consistently across different computing environments.|Containerization has revolutionized software deployment. 集装箱化彻底改变了软件部署。| The company adopted containerization to simplify its application deployment process.  该公司采用容器化来简化其应用程序部署流程。 |近义词：virtualization, packaging|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly manual deployment)|词性: noun
