corralled:/kəˈrɔːld/
verb
被聚集; 被控制
gathered and confined (as in a corral)
例句：The police corralled the protesters in the park. 警察把抗议者聚集在公园里。

例句：The reporters were corralled in a small room for the press conference.  记者们被安排在一个小房间里参加新闻发布会。

近义词：gathered, rounded up, herded, assembled, confined
反义词：released, freed, dispersed, scattered
词性：v.


corral:/kəˈrɔːl/
noun
畜栏
a pen for livestock, especially cattle or horses
例句：The cowboys herded the cattle into the corral. 牛仔们把牛群赶进畜栏。

例句：The horses were kept in a corral overnight. 马匹被关在畜栏里过夜。

近义词：pen, enclosure, stockade
反义词：open field, pasture
词性：n.
