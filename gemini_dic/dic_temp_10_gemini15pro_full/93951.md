immunotherapy:/ˌɪmjuːnoʊˈθɛrəpi/| n.|免疫疗法|Treatment or prevention of disease by inducing, enhancing, or suppressing an immune response.|Immunotherapy has shown promising results in treating certain types of cancer. 免疫疗法在治疗某些类型的癌症方面已显示出良好的效果。|Immunotherapy is being used to treat his allergies. 免疫疗法正被用于治疗他的过敏症。|Synonyms: biological therapy, biotherapy|Antonyms: chemotherapy, radiotherapy|noun
