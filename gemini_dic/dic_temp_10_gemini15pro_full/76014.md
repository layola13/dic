furdle:/ˈfɜːrdl/| v.|卷起，收拢， furl的变体|To neatly fold and secure, typically sails or a flag.  将（通常指帆或旗帜）整齐地折叠并固定。|The sailor furdled the sails before the storm. 水手在暴风雨来临前收起了船帆。|近义词：furl, roll up, stow|反义词: unfurl, unroll|动词
