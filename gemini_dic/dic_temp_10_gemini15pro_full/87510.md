heterodactyl:/ˌhɛtərəˈdæktɪl/| adj.|异趾的，不等趾的|Having the toes arranged differently from the usual pattern, especially having the outer toe or toes turned backward.|The heterodactyl arrangement of the toes allows the bird to easily grip branches.|近义词：anisodactyl|反义词：zygodactyl|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From hetero- (different, other) + -dactyl (finger, toe).  Ultimately from Greek *héteros* (other) + *dáktulos* (finger).
