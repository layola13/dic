cocoas:/ˈkoʊkoʊz/| n.|可可粉；可可饮料（尤指一杯）|A powder made from roasted and ground cacao seeds, used to make chocolate and as a flavoring agent in drinks and other foods. Also refers to a drink made with this powder, especially a cup of hot cocoa.|例句：I enjoy a warm cup of cocoa on a cold day. 我喜欢在寒冷的日子里喝一杯热可可。

例句：She added two spoonfuls of cocoa to the cake batter.  她在蛋糕糊里加了两勺可可粉。|近义词：chocolate, hot chocolate|反义词:|noun
