malayans:/məˈleɪənz/
noun
马来人; 马来亚人
People of Malay descent or inhabitants of the Malay Peninsula or the Malay Archipelago.
The Malayans are known for their rich cultural heritage. 马来人以其丰富的文化遗产而闻名。
Old travel books often referred to the inhabitants of the region as Malayans.  老旧的旅行书籍经常将该地区的居民称为马来人。
synonyms: Malays
antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a nationality)
词性: 名词(plural noun)
