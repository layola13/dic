outmanoeuvred: /ˌaʊtməˈnuːvərd/ (British) /ˌaʊtməˈnuːvərd/ (American)
verb
以策略胜过，智胜
to use skill and intelligence to gain an advantage over someone, especially in a competition or battle
例句：The company was outmanoeuvred by its competitors in the battle for market share. 这家公司在争夺市场份额的战斗中被竞争对手以策略胜过。

例句：The chess player skillfully outmanoeuvred his opponent and won the match.  这位棋手巧妙地智胜了对手，赢得了比赛。

近义词：outwit, outsmart, surpass, defeat, overcome
反义词：be outwitted, be outsmarted, lose to

词性: verb
