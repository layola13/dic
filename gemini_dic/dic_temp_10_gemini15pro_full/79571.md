gloomfully:/ˈɡluːmfʊli/ | adv. | 忧郁地，闷闷不乐地 | In a gloomy manner; sadly and despondently. | He stared gloomfully out the window at the rain. 他忧郁地望着窗外的大雨。|  She walked gloomfully through the empty streets. 她闷闷不乐地走在空旷的街道上。 | 近义词: sadly, dejectedly, morosely, despondently | 反义词: cheerfully, happily, joyfully | 词性: 副词

gloom:/ɡluːm/ | n. | 忧郁，阴暗 | Partial or total darkness; sadness or depression. | A gloom settled over the room as the news spread. 随着消息的传播，房间里弥漫着忧郁的气氛。|  The forest was filled with an eerie gloom. 森林里弥漫着一种可怕的阴暗。 | 近义词: darkness, sadness, depression, melancholy | 反义词: brightness, happiness, cheerfulness | 词性: 名词
