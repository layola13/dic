abdicated: /ˈæbdɪkeɪtɪd/
verb
放弃；退位；逊位
(of a monarch) Renounce one's throne.  Give up (power, responsibility, or the like).
The king abdicated in favor of his son. 国王退位让位给他的儿子。

She abdicated all responsibility for the project. 她放弃了该项目的所有责任。

近义词: relinquish, renounce, resign, step down, quit, forgo, waive, surrender
反义词: assume, usurp, seize, retain, accept
词性: verb
