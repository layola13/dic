lovelike: /lʌvˈlaɪk/
adjective
像爱一样的；充满爱的
Resembling or characteristic of love; loving.
She gave him a lovelike look. 她给了他一个充满爱意的眼神。
Near Synonyms: loving, affectionate, adoring, devoted, fond, tender
Antonyms: hateful, unloving, cold, indifferent
词性: 形容词
