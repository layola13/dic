calcinable:/kælˈsaɪnəbl/
adjective
可煅烧的
Capable of being calcined; reducible to powder by heat.
Limestone is a calcinable material. 石灰石是一种可煅烧的材料。
The mineral is calcinable at high temperatures.  这种矿物在高温下可以煅烧。
synonyms:combustible, burnable
antonyms:non-combustible, fireproof
词性:adjective
