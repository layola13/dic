noncollaborations:/ˌnɒnkəˌlæbəˈreɪʃənz/
noun
非合作，不合作的行为
Instances or states of not working together; lack of collaboration.
The project's failure was largely due to noncollaborations between the departments.  这个项目的失败很大程度上是由于部门之间缺乏合作。
The artist was known for his noncollaborations, preferring to work solely on his projects.  这位艺术家以不合作而闻名，他更喜欢独自完成他的项目。
noncooperation, refusal to cooperate
collaboration, cooperation
