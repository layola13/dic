misstrike:/mɪsˈstraɪk/
n.
错误的击打；失误；误击
An unsuccessful or incorrect strike or blow.
The golfer’s misstrike sent the ball into the woods.  高尔夫球手的失误将球打进了树林。
近义词：miss, blunder, error, mistake
反义词：hit, success
词性：名词

misstrike:/mɪsˈstraɪk/
v.
打错；击错；误击
To strike or hit incorrectly or unsuccessfully.
He misstruck the ball and lost the point. 他击球失误，丢了分。
近义词：miss, mishit
反义词：hit, strike correctly
词性：动词
