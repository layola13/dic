copras:/ˈkoʊprəz/| n. |干椰肉，椰仁干|Dried coconut meat, the white flesh of the coconut after the water has been removed. It is used to extract coconut oil and is also used in cooking and animal feed.|The factory processes copras into coconut oil.  这家工厂将椰仁干加工成椰子油。|The shipment of copras arrived at the port yesterday.  这批椰仁干昨天抵达了港口。|近义词：desiccated coconut, coconut meat|反义词: None|词性: Noun
