endysis:/ˈɛndɪsɪs/
n.
蜕皮;换羽
The shedding of an outer layer or covering, such as the exoskeleton of an arthropod or the skin of a reptile.
The snake undergoes endysis several times a year. 这条蛇一年要蜕皮好几次。
近义词:molting, ecdysis
反义词: N/A
词性: 名词


Etymology: From Greek *éndysis*, from *endýein* "to put on, get into," from *en-* "in" + *dýein* "to enter, get into".
