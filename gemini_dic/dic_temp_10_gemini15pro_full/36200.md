cogged:/kɑːɡd/ | adj. | 齿状的；装有齿轮的；(比喻)相互联系的 | Having cogs or teeth, or resembling a cogwheel; figuratively, interconnected or interlocked. | The cogged wheels turned smoothly. 齿轮平稳地转动。| 近义词: toothed, geared, interlocking | 反义词: smooth, seamless | 词性: 形容词

cogged:/kɑːɡd/ | v. |  (cog的过去式和过去分词)给…装齿轮；(比喻)使…互相联系 | Past tense and past participle of "cog," meaning to furnish with cogs or teeth; figuratively, to connect or interlock. | The clockmaker cogged the wheels carefully. 钟表匠仔细地给齿轮装上齿。 | 近义词: geared, connected, linked | 反义词: disconnected, separated | 词性: 动词


词源: From Middle English *coggen, from Old Norse *kogga ("cog; projection"), related to kugge ("ball").
