administers:/ədˈmɪnɪstərz/
verb
管理，执行，给予
manages and directs; gives or applies something officially
The nurse administers the medication to the patient. 护士给病人服药。
The principal administers the school. 校长管理学校。

近义词: manages, directs, governs, executes, applies, dispenses, provides
反义词: neglects, mismanages, ignores

词性: verb

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The Red Cross administers aid to disaster victims. = 红十字会向灾民提供援助。
She administers the finances for her department. = 她管理她部门的财务。

一般过去时:
The doctor administered a flu shot. = 医生注射了流感疫苗。
The judge administered the oath of office. = 法官主持了就职宣誓。

现在完成时:
The government has administered a new policy. = 政府已经实施了一项新政策。
He has administered first aid to the injured man. = 他已经为受伤男子进行了急救。

过去完成时:
The nurse had administered the medicine before the doctor arrived. = 在医生到达之前，护士已经给病人服了药。
They had administered the test to all the students. = 他们已经对所有学生进行了测试。


将来时:
The school will administer the exam next week. = 学校下周将举行考试。
The organization will administer funds to support the project. = 该组织将管理资金以支持该项目。


虚拟语气:
If I were to administer the funds, I would prioritize education. = 如果由我来管理这些资金，我会优先考虑教育。
I wish I could administer a solution to this problem. = 我希望我能找到解决这个问题的办法。

被动语态:
The vaccine was administered to the children. = 疫苗被注射给了孩子们。
The test will be administered online. = 测试将在网上进行。

疑问句:
Who administers the program? = 谁管理这个项目？
How is the medication administered? = 药物是如何使用的？


否定句:
The company does not administer its own payroll. = 该公司不自行管理工资单。
She did not administer the test correctly. = 她没有正确地进行测试。


PHRASE->
administer justice = 执行正义
administer an oath = 主持宣誓
administer a test = 进行测试
administer a program = 管理一个项目
administer medication = 给药


词源:
From Latin *administrāre, present active infinitive of administrō (“I manage, direct, govern”), from ad (“to, towards”) + ministrō (“I serve, attend, furnish”).
