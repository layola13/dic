explore:/ɪkˈsplɔːr/ (UK), /ɪkˈsplɔːr/ (US)| v. |探索，探险，探究|To travel to or through (an unfamiliar area) in order to learn about it.  To inquire or discuss (a subject or issue) in detail.|例句：Scientists are exploring the possibility of life on other planets. 科学家们正在探索其他星球上存在生命的可能性。

例句：The children explored the woods behind their house. 孩子们探索了他们家后面的树林。|近义词：investigate, examine, discover, research, survey|反义词：ignore, neglect|词性:动词

词源: From Old French *explorer, from Latin explorare "search out, investigate," from ex- "out" + plorare "to cry out, wail, weep," related to pluvia "rain."  The original sense was "to scout, reconnoiter."
