loquacious:/loʊˈkweɪʃəs/| adj.|多话的，健谈的|Tending to talk a great deal; talkative.|She was very loquacious, and I could hardly get a word in edgewise. 她非常健谈，我几乎插不上话。|例句：The loquacious barber told stories while he cut hair.  健谈的理发师在理发的同时讲故事。

例句：He was naturally loquacious, which made him a great salesman. 他天生健谈，这使他成为了一名优秀的销售员。


|近义词：talkative, garrulous, voluble|反义词：taciturn, reticent, laconic|词性: adjective
