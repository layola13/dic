dimples:/ˈdɪmpəlz/ | n. | 酒窝; 浅凹; |A small hollow place, especially one that appears temporarily on a person's cheek when they smile. |She has dimples in both cheeks when she smiles. 她笑的时候两颊都有酒窝。| synonyms: indentations, hollows, depressions | antonyms: bumps, protrusions | noun
