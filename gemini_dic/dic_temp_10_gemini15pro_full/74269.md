foxy:/ˈfɒksi/ (UK) /ˈfɑːksi/ (US)| adj.|狡猾的，诡计多端的；性感的，有魅力的|Attractive in a sly, seductive way.  Can also mean cunning or clever, especially in a deceitful way.|She had a foxy look in her eyes. 她眼神里透着狡黠。|近义词：cunning, sly, seductive, attractive, charming|反义词：naive, ingenuous, artless|词性:形容词
词源: From "fox" + "-y".  The original meaning referred to the cunning attributed to foxes.
