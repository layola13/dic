intermarketing:/ˌɪntərˈmɑːrkɪtɪŋ/
n.
相互营销，交互营销，市场间的营销
The joint marketing efforts of companies operating in the same or related markets.
Intermarketing involves companies working together to promote each other's products or services. This can include joint advertising campaigns, cross-promotions, and shared customer loyalty programs.  交互营销是指在相同或相关市场中运营的公司共同开展的营销工作。这可以包括联合广告活动、交叉促销和共享客户忠诚度计划。

Example: The intermarketing campaign between the airline and the hotel chain was highly successful, boosting sales for both companies. 航空公司和连锁酒店之间的交互营销活动非常成功，促进了两家公司的销售。

The two software companies engaged in intermarketing to expand their reach to a wider customer base.  这两家软件公司开展交互营销，以将其业务范围扩展到更广泛的客户群。

Synonym:  cooperative marketing, joint marketing, synergistic marketing
Antonym: independent marketing, solo marketing
