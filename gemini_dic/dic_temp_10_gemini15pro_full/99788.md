intermentioning: /ˌɪntərˈmɛnʃənɪŋ/
| adj. |提及的，中间提到的|The act of mentioning something in passing, especially in the midst of other topics or discussions.  It implies a brief or incidental reference rather than a detailed explanation.|He kept intermentioning his new car during our conversation about the project.  他在我们讨论项目的过程中不断提到他的新车。|近义词: mentioning, alluding, referencing |反义词: omitting, ignoring |词性:形容词

词源:  "Inter-" (between) + "mentioning" (the act of referring to something).  The prefix "inter-" suggests that the mentioning happens between other things.
