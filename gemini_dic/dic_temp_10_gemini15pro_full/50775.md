descaler:/diːˈskeɪlər/| n.|除垢剂，除垢器|A substance or tool used to remove scale (usually limescale) from surfaces such as kettles, pipes, and showerheads.|例句：Use a descaler to remove the limescale from your kettle. 用除垢剂去除水壶里的水垢。

例句：This descaler is effective against hard water stains.  这种除垢剂对硬水渍有效。|近义词：cleaning agent, scale remover|反义词: scaler (in some contexts, as something that *adds* scale) |词性: noun
