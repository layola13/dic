glorify:/ˈɡlɔːrɪfaɪ/ /ˈɡlorəˌfaɪ/| vt.|赞美；美化；颂扬|To praise and honor something or someone, especially God, or to make something seem more important or beautiful than it really is.|The movie glorifies violence. 这部电影美化暴力。

The soldier's bravery was glorified in the newspapers.  这位士兵的英勇事迹在报纸上得到了颂扬。


近义词：praise, extol, exalt, celebrate|反义词：denounce, condemn, criticize|词性: verb
