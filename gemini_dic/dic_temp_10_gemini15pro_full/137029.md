nonstationarity: /ˌnɒnˌsteɪʃəˈnærəti/ /ˌnɑːnˌsteɪʃəˈnærəti/ | n. | 非平稳性 | The lack of statistical properties being constant over time.  A time series is nonstationary if its statistical properties, such as mean and variance, change over time. | The economist noted the nonstationarity of the data, indicating that traditional time series analysis methods couldn't be applied directly.  经济学家注意到数据的非平稳性，表明传统的时序分析方法不能直接应用。

The nonstationarity of the stock market makes it difficult to predict future prices. 股票市场的非平稳性使得预测未来价格变得困难。| 近义词: instability, volatility, fluctuation | 反义词: stationarity, stability | 词性: noun
