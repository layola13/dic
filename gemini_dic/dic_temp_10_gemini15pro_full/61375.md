embitterments:/ɪmˈbɪtərmənts/
n.
痛苦，怨恨，辛酸
The state of being made bitter or resentful.  The things that cause bitterness.
The constant criticism led to a growing sense of embitterment.  不断的批评导致越来越多的怨恨。
Her embitterments stemmed from a difficult childhood. 她的痛苦源于一个艰难的童年。
近义词: bitterness, resentment, acrimony, rancor
反义词: contentment, happiness, satisfaction, joy
词性: 名词
