paper-shuffler:/ˈpeɪpər ˌʃʌflər/
n.
纸面工作者，文牍主义者
Someone who does a lot of paperwork, often seen as unproductive or bureaucratic.
He's just a paper-shuffler, not a real decision-maker. 他只是一个处理文书的人，而不是真正的决策者。
bureaucrat, administrator
go-getter, dynamic leader
noun
