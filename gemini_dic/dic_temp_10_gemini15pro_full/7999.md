anthophagous:/ænˈθɒfəɡəs/
adjective
食人的；以人类为食的
Feeding on human flesh.
例句：
Ancient myths sometimes depict anthophagous monsters.
古代神话有时会描绘食人的怪物。

Some tribes were falsely accused of being anthophagous.
一些部落被诬告为食人族。

近义词：cannibalistic, man-eating
反义词：herbivorous, carnivorous (though not direct opposites, they represent contrasting dietary habits)
词性：adjective

词源：
From Greek *anthropophagos*, from *anthrōpos* "human being" + *phagein* "to eat."
