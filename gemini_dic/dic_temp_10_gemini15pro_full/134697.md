nonmasculinity:/ˌnɑːnˌmæskjʊˈlɪnɪti/
n.
非男性气质；非阳刚之气
The state or quality of not being masculine; lack of masculinity.  This can refer to traits, behaviors, or presentations that are typically not associated with traditional masculine norms.
He embraced his nonmasculinity, preferring gentler pursuits over competitive sports.  他欣然接受自己的非男性气质，喜欢温和的追求而不是竞技体育。
The advertisement challenged traditional notions of nonmasculinity. 这则广告挑战了传统的非男性气质观念。

近义词:femininity, effeminacy, unmanliness
反义词:masculinity, virility

词性:noun

词源:
Prefix "non-" (not) + "masculinity" (the quality of being masculine). The word "masculinity" comes from the Latin "masculinus" (male, manly), derived from "masculus" (male).
