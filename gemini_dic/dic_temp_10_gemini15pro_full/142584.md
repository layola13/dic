osteodystrophia: /ˌɒstiːəʊdɪˈstrɒfiə/ (British), /ˌɑːstioʊdɪˈstroʊfiə/ (American) | n. | 骨营养不良 | A general term for any disorder of bone development, especially one caused by faulty nutrition. | Renal osteodystrophia is a common complication of chronic kidney disease. 肾性骨营养不良是慢性肾病的常见并发症。|Synonym: osteopathy, bone disease | Antonym: bone health | noun
