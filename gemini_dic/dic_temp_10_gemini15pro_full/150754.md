paracyanogen:/ˌpærəsaɪˈænədʒən/
noun
仲氰，副氰
A brown, amorphous insoluble polymer of cyanogen, (CN)₂.  Formed by heating cyanogen gas or mercuric cyanide.
Paracyanogen is formed by the polymerization of cyanogen. 仲氰是由氰聚合形成的。
Paracyanogen can be converted back to cyanogen gas upon heating to 800°C.  仲氰加热到800°C可以转化回氰气。
synonym: (CN)x, polycyanogen
antonym:  N/A (Paracyanogen doesn't have a direct antonym as it's a specific chemical compound.)
