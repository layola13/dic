determent:/dɪˈtɜːrmənt/
n.|阻碍，损害，不利因素|something that deters; hindrance; disadvantage.  A factor that makes something worse or less likely to succeed.|The high cost of housing is a major determent to young people buying their first home. 高昂的房价是年轻人购买首套住房的主要障碍。|近义词:hindrance, obstacle, impediment, disadvantage, drawback|反义词:advantage, benefit, asset, aid, help|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *determent*, from Old French *déturbement*, from Latin *dētermentum*, from *dēterrere* "to frighten off, deter", from *de-* "away" + *terrere* "to frighten".
