overdries:/ˌoʊvərˈdraɪz/
verb
过度干燥; 使...干燥过度
to dry something too much
The hairdresser overdried my hair, and now it's brittle.  理发师把我的头发吹得太干了，现在很脆。
The sun overdried the ground, making it crack.  太阳把地面晒得太干了，使它裂开了。
overdry, desiccate, parch
moisten, dampen
v.
