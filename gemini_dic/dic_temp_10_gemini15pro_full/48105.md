decline:/dɪˈklaɪn/ | v. n. |下降; 衰退; 婉拒|To politely refuse; to gradually decrease in quantity or importance. |The company's profits declined by 10% last year. 该公司去年利润下降了10%。|近义词：decrease, refuse, reject, diminish, fall |反义词: increase, accept, rise, improve |词性:动词,名词
decline:/dɪˈklaɪn/ | n. |下降; 衰退; 斜坡|A gradual decrease in quantity or importance; a downward slope. |The decline in the housing market has affected the entire economy. 房地产市场的衰退影响了整个经济。|近义词：decrease, downturn, slump, descent |反义词: increase, rise, ascent, improvement |词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *decliner*, from Latin declinare "to bend down, turn aside," from de- "down" + clinare "to bend, lean."
