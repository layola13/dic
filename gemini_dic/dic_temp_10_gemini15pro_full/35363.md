co-inherent:/koʊɪnˈhɪərənt/| adj.|共同固有的，内在关联的|Existing together and inseparable; inherently related.|例句：The rights and responsibilities of citizenship are co-inherent. 公民的权利和责任是内在关联的。

例句：Freedom and responsibility are co-inherent aspects of democratic governance. 自由和责任是民主治理共同固有的方面。|近义词：interconnected, intertwined, inseparable, inherent, intrinsic|反义词：separate, independent, extrinsic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From co- + inherent.  "Co-" is a prefix meaning "together" or "jointly." "Inherent" comes from the Latin "inhaerēre," meaning "to stick in" or "to be fixed in."  Thus, "co-inherent" literally means "sticking together" or "fixed together."
