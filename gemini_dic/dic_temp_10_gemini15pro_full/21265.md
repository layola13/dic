bloodflower:/ˈblʌdˌflaʊər/| n. |血花，石蒜|A plant with bright red flowers, often associated with death or remembrance.  Specifically, it often refers to *Scadoxus multiflorus* (formerly *Haemanthus multiflorus*) or other related species.|The bloodflowers bloomed vividly against the dark soil. 血花在黑色的土壤映衬下开得鲜艳夺目。| The bloodflowers were planted in memory of the fallen soldiers. 这些血花是用来纪念阵亡将士的。 |Synonyms:  fireball lily, powderpuff lily, African blood lily|Antonyms: N/A|noun
