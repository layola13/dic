groundsel:/ˈɡraʊndsl/ | n. | 千里光；欧洲千里光 | A common Eurasian weed (Senecio vulgaris) of the daisy family, with yellow rayless flower heads. It is poisonous to livestock. | The field was overrun with groundsel.  这块田里长满了千里光。| synonyms: ragwort, senecio | antonyms: N/A | noun


groundsel:/ˈɡraʊndsl/ | n. |  (古语) 门槛；基石 | (archaic) A threshold; a groundsill. |  The groundsel of the old church was worn smooth by centuries of footsteps. 古老教堂的基石已被几个世纪的脚步磨平。| synonyms: threshold, sill, doorsill | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *grundeswelge, from grund "ground" + swelgan "to swallow."  The name refers to the plant's rapid growth, as if it were swallowing the ground.
