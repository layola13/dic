muscling:/ˈmʌsəlɪŋ/
verb
用力挤入;强挤;硬塞;（用强力）推进，移动
forcing one's way into a place or situation, often aggressively.
例句：He muscled his way to the front of the crowd. 他用力挤到了人群的最前面。

例句：The company muscled its way into the new market.  这家公司强行打入了新市场。

近义词：forcing, pushing, shoving, elbowing, imposing
反义词：yielding, retreating, withdrawing
词性 v.


muscling:/ˈmʌsəlɪŋ/
noun
肌肉发达;强健的体格
the development of muscles; muscular strength.
例句：He's been doing a lot of weightlifting and his muscling is impressive.  他一直在举重，肌肉发达，令人印象深刻。

例句：The muscling of the athletes was evident in their performance.  运动员的强健体魄在他们的表现中显而易见。


近义词：musculature, physique, build
反义词：weakness, frailty
词性 n.

词源：From the verb "muscle" (meaning "to exert strength"), which comes from the noun "muscle" (referring to body tissue). The noun "muscle" ultimately derives from the Latin word "musculus," meaning "little mouse" (because the shape of some muscles resembled mice).
