as long as:/æz lɔːŋ æz/
conjunction
只要; 如果; 在...的时候
As long as you promise to be careful, you can borrow my car. 只要你保证小心，你就可以借我的车。
So long as there is life, there is hope. 只要活着就有希望.
Synonyms: provided that, providing, on condition that, if, assuming that
Antonyms: unless, only if
词性: 连词
