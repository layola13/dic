USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she like to do on weekends? 她周末喜欢做什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching right now? 你现在在看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? 她用这笔钱做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been studying for the past few months? 他过去几个月一直在学习什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party last night? 昨晚的派对发生了什么事？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 开会的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? 她生病前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I arrived?  我来之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been discussing for hours? 他们已经讨论几个小时了什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will happen next? 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? 他们将在会议上讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by 2030? 到2030年会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will he have been studying by the time he graduates?  到他毕业时，他将已经学习什么多久了？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
I wish I knew what to do in this situation. 我希望我知道在这种情况下该怎么办。

被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to say. 我不知道该说什么。
There's nothing what I want here. 这里没有我想要的东西。


PHRASE->

What's the matter? 怎么了？
So what? 那又怎样？
