nonfluentnesses:/ˌnɒnˈfluːənsiːz/ | n. |不流畅，不流利|Lack of fluency, especially in speech; instances of halting or stumbling speech.  It refers to breaks, hesitations, or repetitions in someone's speech that disrupt the smooth flow of communication.|His nonfluentnesses during the presentation made it difficult to follow his argument.  他在演讲中的不流畅使得他的论点难以理解。|近义词：hesitations, stammering, stuttering|反义词: fluency, eloquence | noun
