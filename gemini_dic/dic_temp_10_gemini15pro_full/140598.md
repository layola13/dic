oliver: /ˈɑːlɪvər/ | n. | 奥利弗（男子名） | A male given name, from a Norman French surname, originally a byname from Old French *olivier "olive tree".  It may have been given to someone who lived near an olive tree, or it may have been a symbolic name. It gained popularity after the publication of Charles Dickens' novel *Oliver Twist*. | Oliver Twist is a classic novel.  奥利弗·特威斯特是一部经典小说。 | He named his son Oliver. 他给他的儿子取名为奥利弗。 | 近义词: (None - it's a proper noun) | 反义词: (None - it's a proper noun) | 词性: noun
