overpowerful:/ˌoʊvərˈpaʊərfl/
adj.
过分强大的，过于强势的
Having excessive power; too powerful.
The overpowerful emperor ruled with an iron fist.  过分强大的皇帝用铁腕统治。
The new software felt overpowerful for my simple needs.  这个新软件对于我的简单需求来说过于强大了。
近义词:dominant, overwhelming, overpowering
反义词:weak, powerless, feeble
词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + powerful.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," and "powerful" means "having great power or strength."
