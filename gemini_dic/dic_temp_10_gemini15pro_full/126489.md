mytilus:/ˈmɪtɪləs/| n. |贻贝；紫贻贝（指贻贝属的双壳类软体动物）|A marine bivalve mollusk of the genus Mytilus, typically having a dark elongated shell.  These mollusks are commonly known as mussels.|Mussels, such as Mytilus edulis, are often farmed for food.  紫贻贝，例如食用贻贝，通常被养殖作为食物。|近义词：mussel|反义词:|n. (noun)
