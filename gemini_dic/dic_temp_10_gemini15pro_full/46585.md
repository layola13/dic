damages:/ˈdæmɪdʒɪz/
n.
损害赔偿金，赔偿金
Money claimed or awarded in compensation for loss or injury.
The jury awarded her $5 million in damages. 陪审团判给她500万美元的赔偿金。

近义词: compensation, restitution, reparation
反义词: earnings, profit


词性: noun (plural)
词源:  From Anglo-French *damage, from damager "to injure," from Latin damnum "loss, hurt, damage."
