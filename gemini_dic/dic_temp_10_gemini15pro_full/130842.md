nonappreciatively:/ˌnɒnəˈpriːʃətɪvli/ /ˌnɑːnəˈpriːʃətɪvli/| adv.|不欣赏地，不领情地|in a way that shows a lack of appreciation or gratitude|He shrugged nonappreciatively at her efforts. 他对她的努力不以为然地耸了耸肩。|Synonym: ungratefully, disdainfully|Antonym: appreciatively, gratefully
Etymology: non- (not) + appreciative + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Appreciative" comes from the Late Latin "appretiatus," past participle of "appretiare" (to set a price to, value), from "ad-" (to) + "pretium" (price).
