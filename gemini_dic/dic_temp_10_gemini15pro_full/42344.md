counteractive:/ˌkaʊntərˈæktɪv/
adjective
抵消的；对抗性的
Acting against something in order to reduce its force or neutralize it.
The government has taken counteractive measures to combat inflation. 政府已采取抵消措施来对抗通货膨胀。
Near synonyms: neutralising, neutralizing, countervailing, counterbalancing
Near antonyms: exacerbating, aggravating
词性: 形容词
