filed:/faɪld/ | v. | 提交，归档，提出（申请等），锉 | Past tense and past participle of "file".  Meaning to arrange systematically, often in a folder or cabinet. Can also mean to submit (a legal document, application, or charge). Can also refer to the act of smoothing or shaping something with a file (a tool). | I filed my taxes last week. 我上周提交了我的税单。 | organize, submit, categorize | discard, neglect | verb

filed:/faɪld/ | adj. | 锉的，锉平的，有锉痕的 | Having been smoothed or shaped with a file. | The metal piece was filed smooth. 这块金属片被锉平了。 | smooth, polished, honed | rough, jagged | adjective
