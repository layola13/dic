effluviable:/ɪˈfluːviəbl/
adjective
可发散的；可流出的；可放散的
Capable of being given off as an effluvium.  Something that can emanate or be dispersed as a subtle or invisible emanation, like a scent or aura.
The effluviable scent of the roses filled the garden. 玫瑰可散发的气味弥漫了整个花园。
近义词:exhalable, diffusible, emanative
反义词:impermeable, non-volatile

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *effluviabilis, from Latin effluvium + -abilis.
