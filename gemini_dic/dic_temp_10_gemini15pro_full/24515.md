broadbill:/ˈbrɔːdbɪl/| n.|阔嘴鸟；阔嘴鸟型鱼|A bird or fish with a broad bill.|例句：The broadbill perched on the branch, its colorful plumage gleaming in the sunlight. 阔嘴鸟栖息在树枝上，其鲜艳的羽毛在阳光下闪闪发光。

例句：The fisherman caught a rare broadbill, its wide mouth gaping in surprise. 渔夫捕到一条稀有的阔嘴鸟型鱼，它的大嘴惊讶地张开着。|近义词：frogmouth (for certain types of broadbills)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun


broadbill:/ˈbrɔːdbɪl/| adj.|阔嘴的|Having a broad bill.|例句：The broadbill duck dabbled in the shallows. 阔嘴鸭在浅滩戏水。

例句：The broadbill swordfish sliced through the water. 阔嘴旗鱼划破水面。|近义词：wide-billed|反义词:narrow-billed|词性:adjective
