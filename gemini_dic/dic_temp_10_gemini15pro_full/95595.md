incommiscible:/ˌɪnkəˈmɪsəbl/ | adj. | 不能混合的，不互溶的 |  Not able to be mixed together. Two liquids are incommiscible if they do not form a homogeneous mixture when added together.  | Oil and water are incommiscible liquids. 油和水是不互溶的液体。| The paint was incommiscible with water, requiring a special solvent for cleaning.  这种油漆与水不能混合，需要特殊的溶剂清洗。 |  immiscible, non-miscible | miscible, soluble | adjective
