bleak:/blik/ | adj. | 荒凉的，凄凉的；阴冷的；暗淡的，无希望的 | Lacking vegetation and exposed to the elements; cold and miserable; depressing; offering little or no hope | The future looked bleak for the company. 这家公司的前景看起来很暗淡。| synonyms: desolate, barren, cold, dreary, dismal, depressing | antonyms: cheerful, bright, promising, hopeful, optimistic | adjective

bleaks:/bliːks/ | n. | 荒凉的地方；凄凉的景象 |  A bleak and desolate area or scene.  (This is a less common usage, typically pluralized to refer to multiple such areas or scenes) | The hiker trudged across the bleaks.  这名徒步旅行者艰难地走过荒凉之地。| synonyms: wastelands, barrens, wilderness | antonyms: paradises, oases, havens | noun
