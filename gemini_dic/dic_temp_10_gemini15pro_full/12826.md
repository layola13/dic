associability:/əˌsoʊʃiəˈbɪləti/ |n.|可交际性；合群性；亲和力|The quality of being associable; sociability.  The capability of being readily associated or learned.|Her natural associability made her popular in the office. 她天生的亲和力使她在办公室很受欢迎。|Synonyms: sociability, gregariousness, affability, friendliness|Antonyms: unsociability, aloofness, introversion|noun

associability:/əˌsoʊʃiəˈbɪləti/ |n.|结合性；连接性|The capacity of things to be connected or combined.|The associability of the two chemicals created a powerful reaction.  这两种化学物质的结合性产生了强烈的反应。|Synonyms: connectability, combinability, linkability|Antonyms:  incompatibility, disunity, separateness|noun
