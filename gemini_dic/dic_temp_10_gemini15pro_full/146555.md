overgoverned:/ˌoʊvərˈɡʌvərnd/
adjective
过度管理的；管制过度的
Ruled or managed with too much control or regulation.
The city is overgoverned, with too many bureaucratic regulations stifling innovation. 这个城市管理过度，太多的官僚规定扼杀了创新。
Overly regulated industries often suffer from reduced productivity.  过度监管的行业往往生产力下降。
Synonyms: overregulated, overcontrolled, excessively governed
Antonyms: undergoverned, unregulated, laissez-faire
词性: 形容词
