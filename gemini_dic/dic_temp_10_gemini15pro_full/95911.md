incrementing:/ˈɪŋkrəˌmɛntɪŋ/
adjective|递增的，增值的|increasing by successive increments; the act of increasing by increments.|The company is incrementing salaries by 5% each year.  这家公司每年将工资递增5%。|近义词: increasing, rising, escalating, advancing|反义词: decrementing, decreasing, reducing, diminishing|词性: adjective
Etymology: From Latin incrementum "growth, increase," from increscere "to grow in or upon," from in- "in, on" + crescere "to grow."
