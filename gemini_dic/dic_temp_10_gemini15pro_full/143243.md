outdebating:/ˌaʊtdɪˈbeɪtɪŋ/
verb
胜过辩论，在辩论中胜过
defeating someone in a debate
She outdebated her opponent with her quick wit and extensive knowledge. 她以敏捷的才思和广博的知识在辩论中击败了对手。
Synonyms: outarguing, besting, defeating in debate
Antonyms: losing to, being defeated by

Etymology:
"out-" (prefix meaning surpassing or exceeding) + "debate" (verb meaning to argue or discuss formally)
