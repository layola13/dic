enclitic:/ɪnˈklɪtɪk/| adj. n.|粘附词，附着词|A word or particle that is grammatically independent but phonologically bound to another word, typically following it and pronounced with it as a single unit.|The word "-n't" in "isn't" is an enclitic.|近义词: clitic, suffix|反义词: proclitic|词性: adjective, noun
