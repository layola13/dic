overprolixnesses:/ˌoʊvərprəˈlɪksnəsɪz/
noun
冗长；啰嗦
The state of being excessively wordy or long-winded.
His overprolixnesses made it difficult to follow his argument.  他过分的冗长使得他的论点难以理解。
Synonyms:verbosity, wordiness, prolixity, long-windedness
Antonyms:conciseness, brevity, succinctness, terseness
