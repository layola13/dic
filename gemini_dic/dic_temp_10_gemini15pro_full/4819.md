alhambra: /ælˈhæmbrə/ | n. | 阿尔罕布拉宫 | A palace and fortress complex located in Granada, Spain. It was originally constructed as a small fortress in AD 889 on the remains of Roman fortifications, and then largely ignored until its ruins were renovated and rebuilt in the mid-13th century by the Moorish emir Mohammed ben Al-Ahmar of the Emirate of Granada, who built its current palace and walls. |  Example 1: We visited the Alhambra during our trip to Spain. 我们在西班牙旅行期间参观了阿尔罕布拉宫。

Example 2: The Alhambra is a UNESCO World Heritage Site. 阿尔罕布拉宫是联合国教科文组织世界遗产。 |  Near Synonyms: palace, fortress, castle | Antonyms: hovel, shack | Noun
