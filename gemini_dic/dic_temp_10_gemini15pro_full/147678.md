overpowerfully:/ˌoʊvərˈpaʊərˌfʊli/| adv.|压倒性地，不可抗拒地，极其强大地|With great power; overwhelmingly; irresistibly.|The champion overpowerfully defeated his opponent. 冠军以压倒性的优势击败了他的对手。|The drug overpowerfully affected her senses.  这药物极大地影响了她的感官。|近义词: overwhelmingly, dominantly, irresistibly, compellingly|反义词: weakly, feebly, mildly|词性:副词

词源:  From "overpower" (verb) + "-ful" (suffix) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Overpower" comes from "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "power" (noun).
