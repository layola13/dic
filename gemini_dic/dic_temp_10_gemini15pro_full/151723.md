partyism: /ˈpɑːrtiɪzəm/ | n. |党派主义，党派偏见 | Excessive adherence to a political party, often leading to prejudice against opposing parties and a disregard for objective fairness.  | Example: Partyism can make it difficult to have productive political discussions. 党派主义使得进行富有成效的政治讨论变得困难。

Example:  The journalist was accused of partyism in his reporting.  这名记者被指责在他的报道中带有党派偏见。| 近义词: partisanship, factionalism | 反义词: nonpartisanship, impartiality | 词性: noun
