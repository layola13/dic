anticolonialisms:/ˌæntiˈkəˈlୋʊniəlɪzəmz/
|n.|反殖民主义（复数）|Theories of anticolonialisms offer different perspectives on resistance to colonial power.  反殖民主义理论为抵抗殖民势力提供了不同的视角。|
|The various anticolonialisms across the globe shared some common goals. 全球各地的各种反殖民主义运动有一些共同的目标。|
|近义词: decolonization, anti-imperialism|
|反义词: colonialism, imperialism|
|词性: noun|


Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek _anti_)
colonialism (noun): the policy or practice of acquiring full or partial political control over another country, occupying it with settlers, and exploiting it economically. (from _colony_ + _-ism_)
-s (suffix): plural marker
