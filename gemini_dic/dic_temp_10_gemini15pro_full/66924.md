expendably:/ɪkˈspɛndəbli/
adjective
可牺牲地，可消耗地
able to be expended or used up, and therefore not worth preserving.  Something that is expendable is considered not worth saving or protecting, and can be sacrificed if necessary.
The troops were regarded as expendable. 这些部队被认为是可以牺牲的。
Synonyms: dispensable, replaceable, nonessential, sacrificeable
Antonyms: indispensable, essential, crucial, vital
词性: adjective
