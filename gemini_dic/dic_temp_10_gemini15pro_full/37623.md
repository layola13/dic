communisms: /ˈkɒmjʊnɪzəmz/ | n. | 共产主义（复数） |  A theory or system of social organization in which all property is owned by the community and each person contributes and receives according to their ability and needs.  It often refers to the various forms of communism practiced in different countries, highlighting their differences and variations. |  Many different communisms emerged in the 20th century.  20世纪出现了许多不同的共产主义。| synonyms: Marxism-Leninism, Bolshevism, Maoism (depending on specific type) | antonyms: capitalism, free market economy | noun
