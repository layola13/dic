chorizos:/tʃəˈriːzoʊz/ /tʃəˈriːzɒz/
n.
西班牙辣香肠（复数）
A spicy Spanish pork sausage.
例句：We grilled the chorizos and served them with crusty bread. 我们烤了西班牙辣香肠，配着硬皮面包吃。

例句：The paella was full of flavorful chorizos and prawns.  这道西班牙海鲜饭里充满了美味的西班牙辣香肠和对虾。

近义词：sausages, salami
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "bland food" if contrasting flavor)
词性: noun


chorizo:/tʃəˈriːzoʊ/ /tʃəˈriːzɒ/
n.
西班牙辣香肠（单数）
A spicy Spanish pork sausage.
例句：I added some chorizo to the stew for extra flavor. 我在炖菜里加了一些西班牙辣香肠来提味。

例句：He sliced the chorizo thinly and added it to the pizza. 他把西班牙辣香肠切成薄片，加到披萨上。

近义词：sausage, salami
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "bland food" if contrasting flavor)
词性: noun


词源: From Spanish *chorizo*, ultimately of unknown origin. Possibly related to Latin *salsicium* "sausage".
