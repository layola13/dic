bluesiest:/ˈbluːziɪst/ | adj. | 最忧郁的，最悲伤的 |  Superlative form of "bluesy."  It means having the most pronounced characteristics of blues music, or evoking the strongest feelings of sadness and melancholy associated with the blues. | The bar had the bluesiest atmosphere I'd ever encountered, with dim lighting and mournful music.  酒吧的气氛是我从未遇到过的最忧郁的，灯光昏暗，音乐悲伤。| The singer's bluesiest song always makes me cry.  这位歌手最悲伤的歌总是让我哭。 |Synonyms: saddest, most melancholic, most mournful, most depressing | Antonyms: happiest, most cheerful, most joyful | adjective
