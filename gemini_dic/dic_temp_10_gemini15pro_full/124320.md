motherlessnesses:/ˈmʌðərləsnəsəz/
n.
没有母亲的状态；失去母亲的悲痛
The state of being without a mother; the sadness caused by the loss of one's mother.
The orphan's motherlessnesses haunted him throughout his life.  这个孤儿的丧母之痛终其一生都萦绕着他。
Synonyms: orphanhood, bereavement, loss
Antonyms: motherhood, maternal care
