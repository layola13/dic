nondieter:/ˌnɑːnˈdaɪətər/ | /ˌnɒnˈdaɪətər/ | n. | 非节食者，不控制饮食的人 | A person who does not restrict their food intake, especially for reasons of weight loss or health. |  She's a nondieter and enjoys all kinds of food. 她是一个不节食的人，喜欢各种各样的食物。

| My brother is a nondieter; he eats whatever he wants.  我的兄弟从不节食；他想吃什么就吃什么。 | 近义词: regular eater, unrestricted eater | 反义词: dieter | 词性: noun
