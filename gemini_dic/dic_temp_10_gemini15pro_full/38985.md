confirmability:/kənˌfɜːrməˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可证实性，可确认性 | The quality of being able to be confirmed or verified; the ability to be proven true or accurate. |  The study emphasized the importance of confirmability in scientific research. 这项研究强调了科学研究中可证实性的重要性。 | synonyms: verifiability, testability, demonstrability | antonyms: unverifiability,  unprovability | noun

confirmable:/kənˈfɜːrməbl/ | adj. | 可证实的，可确认的 | Capable of being confirmed or verified. | The results of the experiment were confirmable by other researchers.  实验结果可以被其他研究人员证实。| synonyms: verifiable, testable, demonstrable | antonyms: unverifiable, unprovable | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin confirmabilitas, from confirmabilis (confirmable) + -itas (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality). Confirmabilis is from Latin confirmare (to confirm), from con- (intensive prefix) + firmare (to strengthen), from firmus (strong, firm).
