airlift:/ˈeərlɪft/ | n. vt.|空运，空投|The act of transporting people or goods by aircraft, especially in an emergency. |  The government organized an airlift to supply the refugees with food and water. 政府组织了一次空运，为难民提供食物和水。 |  近义词：air transport | 反义词: land transport, sea transport | noun, verb


词源: air + lift,  "lift" meaning to raise or transport something.  The term gained prominence during the Berlin Airlift (1948-1949).
