antanagoge:/ænˌtænəˈɡoʊdʒi/ | n. | 安坦纳格, 反衬法 | A rhetorical device in which a fault is admitted to lessen the impact of criticism or to introduce a compensating merit.  It involves conceding a negative point and then immediately balancing it with a positive one. |  "Yes, the car is expensive, but it gets great gas mileage."  是的，这辆车很贵，但它很省油。 | Synonyms:  compensation, counterpoise, mitigation | Antonyms:  aggravation, exacerbation | noun
