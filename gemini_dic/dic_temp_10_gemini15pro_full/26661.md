cacostomia:/kækəˈstəʊmiə/ /kækəˈstoʊmiə/
noun
口臭
bad breath; halitosis.  A condition in which a person's breath has an unpleasant odor.
Example: The patient complained of cacostomia, which was affecting his social life. 病人抱怨口臭影响了他的社交生活。

Example:  The doctor recommended a mouthwash to treat the patient's cacostomia. 医生推荐了一种漱口水来治疗病人的口臭。

近义词: halitosis, bad breath, fetor oris
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider) fresh breath, pleasant breath
