exhumers:/ɪksˈhjuːmərz/
|n.|掘墓者；发掘者|People who exhume something, typically bodies.  Those who dig up something buried, especially corpses.|The exhumers carefully removed the coffin from the ground. 掘墓者小心地把棺材从地下移走。|The exhumers discovered ancient artifacts alongside the remains. 发掘者在遗骸旁边发现了古代文物。|近义词: excavators, disinterrers|反义词: buriers, interrers|Noun
