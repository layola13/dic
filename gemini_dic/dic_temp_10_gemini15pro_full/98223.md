insecurity:/ˌɪnsɪˈkjʊərɪti/ | n. | 不安全感，不牢靠；无把握 | Lack of confidence or assurance; the state of being open to danger or harm. |  She felt a deep sense of insecurity about her job. 她对自己的工作感到深深的不安。|  anxiety, doubt, self-doubt, vulnerability | confidence, security, certainty, stability | noun

insecure:/ˌɪnsɪˈkjʊr/ | adj. | 不安全的，不牢靠的；不自信的 | Not firm or firmly fixed; not stable or well-adjusted; not confident or assured. | He felt insecure about his ability to do the job. 他对自己的工作能力感到不自信。| unsafe, unstable, shaky, uncertain, timid | secure, safe, stable, confident, certain | adjective
