outslandering:/aʊtˈslændərɪŋ/
verb
诽谤，诋毁
The act of making false and damaging statements about someone.
He was outslandering his political opponents with malicious gossip. 他用恶意的流言蜚语诽谤他的政敌。
Synonyms: defaming, libeling, slandering, vilifying, maligning, calumniating
Antonyms: praising, commending, lauding, extolling
词性: 动词(gerund or present participle)
