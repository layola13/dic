consummate:/ˈkɒnsəmeɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsəmeɪt/ (US)| v. adj.|完成；使完美；圆房|To make (something) complete or perfect; to fulfill.|例句：The deal was consummated with a handshake.  这笔交易以握手成交。|近义词：complete, finalize, perfect, fulfill, achieve|反义词:begin, start, ruin, destroy|词性:动词

consummate:/ˈkɒnsəmɪt/ (UK)  /ˈkɑːnsəmɪt/ (US)| adj.|完美的；圆满的；技艺高超的|Extremely skilled and accomplished.|例句：He's a consummate professional. 他是一位技艺高超的专业人士。|近义词：perfect, supreme, ultimate, masterful, accomplished|反义词:imperfect, flawed, amateurish|词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from Latin consummat- ‘summed up, completed’, past participle of consummare, from con- ‘together’ + summa ‘sum, total’.
