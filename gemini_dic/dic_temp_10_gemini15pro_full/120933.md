miscellanarian:/ˌmɪsələˈnɛəriən/
n.|杂集作者；杂集收藏者；博学的人|A person who studies or collects miscellaneous items or information; a person with wide-ranging knowledge.|He was a true miscellanarian, with interests ranging from ancient history to modern art. 他是一位真正的杂家，兴趣广泛，从古代历史到现代艺术无所不包。|Synonyms: polymath, generalist, dilettante|Antonyms: specialist|noun

miscellany:/mɪˈsɛləni/
n.|杂集；混合物；各种各样|A collection of different items, especially literary works or musical pieces; a mixture of various things.|The bookstore had a miscellany of used books. 这家书店有各种各样的二手书。|Synonyms: assortment, collection, medley|Antonyms: uniformity, homogeneity|noun
