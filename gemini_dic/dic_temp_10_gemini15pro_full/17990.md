began:/bɪˈɡæn/ | v. |开始|Past tense of begin.  Indicates the commencement of an action or state. |The meeting began at 9 a.m. 会议在上午9点开始。

He began to feel unwell. 他开始觉得不舒服。|近义词：started, commenced, initiated|反义词：ended, finished, ceased|verb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I begin my day with a cup of coffee. 我每天以一杯咖啡开始。
He begins work at 8 a.m. 他早上8点开始工作。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am beginning to understand the concept. 我开始理解这个概念了。
She is beginning to feel better. 她开始感觉好些了。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have begun my homework. 我已经开始做作业了。
He has begun to learn Spanish. 他已经开始学习西班牙语了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been beginning to suspect something. 我开始怀疑一些事情了。
They have been beginning to plan their vacation. 他们已经开始计划他们的假期了。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
The rain began to fall. 雨开始下了。
She began to cry. 她开始哭了。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was beginning to feel tired when the phone rang. 电话响的时候我开始感到累了。
He was beginning to explain when he was interrupted. 他开始解释的时候被打断了。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
The movie had begun before we arrived. 我们到达之前电影已经开始了。
The party had begun by the time I got there. 我到那儿的时候派对已经开始了。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been beginning to think he was right. 我开始觉得他是对的了。
She had been beginning to doubt herself. 她开始怀疑自己了。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
The concert will begin at 8 p.m. 音乐会将于晚上8点开始。
School will begin next week. 学校下周开学。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be beginning my new job next month. 我下个月将开始我的新工作。
They will be beginning the project soon. 他们很快就会开始这个项目。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, I will have begun my master's degree. 到明年，我将开始攻读硕士学位。
By the end of the month, they will have begun construction. 到月底，他们将开始施工。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By the time you arrive, I will have been beginning to cook dinner. 当你到达时，我将已经开始做饭了。
By next week, they will have been beginning the process of moving. 到下周，他们将已经开始搬家的过程。


虚拟语气:
If I began earlier, I would finish sooner. 如果我早点开始，我会早点完成。
If she began studying harder, she would get better grades. 如果她开始更努力地学习，她会取得更好的成绩。


被动语态:
The project was begun last year. 该项目于去年启动。
The meeting was begun promptly at 9 a.m. 会议在上午9点准时开始。

疑问句:
When did the meeting begin? 会议什么时候开始的？
Did the movie begin yet? 电影开始了吗？

否定句:
The class hasn't begun yet. 课还没开始。
The game didn't begin on time. 比赛没有按时开始。
