bullfaced:/ˈbʊlˌfeɪst/| adj.|厚颜无耻的，蛮横的|Having a broad, coarse face like a bull; also, impudent, shamelessly bold.|He gave me a bullfaced stare when I challenged his decision. 当我质疑他的决定时，他厚颜无耻地瞪着我。|近义词：impudent, brazen, insolent, audacious|反义词：timid, shy, meek|词性:形容词

Etymology: bull + faced.  From the resemblance of a broad, coarse face to that of a bull. The figurative sense of "impudent" arose from the perceived association of such a face with aggressiveness and lack of sensitivity.
