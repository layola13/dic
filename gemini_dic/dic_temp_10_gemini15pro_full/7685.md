anointed:/əˈnɔɪntɪd/ | adj. |受膏礼的；被任命的；指定的|Consecrated or chosen by or as if by divine intervention;  smeared or rubbed with oil or ointment. |The king's anointed heir took the throne. 国王的指定继承人登基了。| Near Synonyms: chosen, designated, ordained, consecrated | Antonyms: unconsecrated, unchosen | adjective

anointed:/əˈnɔɪntɪd/ | n. |受膏者；被选中的人|A person who has been anointed. |He considered himself one of the anointed. 他认为自己是天选之人。| Near Synonyms: chosen one, elect | Antonyms: outcast, pariah | noun
