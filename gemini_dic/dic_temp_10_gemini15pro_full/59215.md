dysteleology:/dɪsˌtiːliˈɒlədʒi/
n.
缺乏目的性；无目的论
The absence of purpose or design in nature or human affairs; the doctrine that there is no design or purpose in nature or human affairs.
The philosopher argued for dysteleology, claiming the universe is governed by chance, not design. 这位哲学家主张无目的论，声称宇宙是由偶然性而非设计所支配的。
Near Synonyms: purposelessness, aimlessness, randomness
Antonyms: teleology, purposefulness, design
noun
