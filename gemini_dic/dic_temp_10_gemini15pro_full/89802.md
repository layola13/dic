horn:/hɔːrn/ (UK) /hɔːrn/ (US)| n. |角; 喇叭; 触角|A hard pointed part that grows on the head of some animals, or a similar part made of another material. A device that makes a loud sound as a warning or signal.|The bull lowered its horns and charged. 公牛 lowered its horns and charged。|近义词：antler, trumpet, siren|反义词: -- |noun

horn:/hɔːrn/ (UK) /hɔːrn/ (US)| v. |按喇叭; 用角顶;|To push or gore with a horn. To sound a horn.|He honked his horn at the jaywalker. 他对乱穿马路的人按喇叭。|近义词：toot, beep|反义词: -- |verb
