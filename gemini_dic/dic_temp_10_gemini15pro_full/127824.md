nematocyst:/ˈnɛmətəsɪst/| n.|刺丝囊|A specialized cell in the tentacles of a jellyfish or other coelenterate, containing a barbed or venomous coiled thread that can be projected in self-defense or to capture prey.|The jellyfish's nematocysts injected venom into the swimmer. 水母的刺丝囊将毒液注入游泳者体内。|Synonym: cnidocyst|Antonym: N/A|noun

nematocyst:/ˈnɛmətəsɪst/| n.|刺丝囊|A stinging organelle of certain cnidarians, such as jellyfish, that delivers a venomous sting.|The anemone paralyzed its prey with its nematocysts.  海葵用它的刺丝囊麻痹了猎物。|Synonym: stinging cell|Antonym: N/A|noun
