herring:/ˈherɪŋ/| n.|鲱鱼|A small, silver-coloured fish that swims in large groups in the sea, or its flesh eaten as food.|例句：We had herring for dinner. 我们晚饭吃了鲱鱼。

例句：Herring are an important food source for many marine animals. 鲱鱼是许多海洋动物的重要食物来源。


|近义词：sardine, pilchard, sprat|反义词:|词性:noun
