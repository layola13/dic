painsworthy:/ˈpeɪnzwɜːrði/
adjective
值得努力的；值得花费心思的
deserving of the effort or trouble involved.
例句：The restoration of the ancient temple was a painsworthy project.  修复古庙是一个值得努力的项目。

例句：Learning a new language can be painsworthy, but the rewards are great. 学习一门新的语言可能会很费力，但回报是巨大的。

近义词：worthwhile, rewarding, valuable
反义词：worthless, futile, pointless
词性: adjective

词源:  A compound of "pains" (meaning effort or trouble) and "worthy" (deserving of).  由“pains”（意为努力或麻烦）和“worthy”（值得的）组成。
