hostile:/ˈhɑːstaɪl/ (US), /ˈhɒstaɪl/ (UK)| adj. n.|敌对的，敌方的；敌意，敌对行动|Showing or feeling opposition or dislike; unfriendly.  A person or thing that is unfriendly.|The local people were hostile to the soldiers. 当地居民对士兵怀有敌意。|Synonyms: antagonistic, unfriendly, opposed, aggressive, belligerent|Antonyms: friendly, amicable, welcoming, hospitable|adjective

hostile:/ˈhɑːstaɪl/ (US), /ˈhɒstaɪl/ (UK)| n.|敌对者，敌军|A person or thing that is unfriendly or shows opposition.|The hostiles attacked the settlement at dawn.  敌军在黎明时分袭击了定居点。|Synonyms: enemy, adversary, opponent, foe|Antonyms: friend, ally, supporter|noun
