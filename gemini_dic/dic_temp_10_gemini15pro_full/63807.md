epidermal:/ˌɛpɪˈdɜːrməl/ | adj. |表皮的，外皮的 | Relating to the epidermis (the outer layer of skin). | The epidermal cells protect the underlying tissues. 表皮细胞保护下面的组织。| Example 2:  Sunburn is damage to the epidermal layer of the skin. 晒伤是对皮肤表皮层的损害。 |Synonyms: cutaneous, dermal | Antonyms: internal, subcutaneous | adjective
