garnishers:/ˈɡɑːrnɪʃərz/
n.
装饰物; 配菜
A person or thing that garnishes something, especially food.  Someone or something that adds decoration or embellishment.
The chef carefully arranged the garnishers on the plate.  厨师小心翼翼地在盘子上摆放装饰物。
The colorful garnishers made the dish more appealing.  色彩缤纷的配菜使这道菜更加诱人。
近义词:decorations, ornaments, trimmings
反义词:plainness
词性:名词
