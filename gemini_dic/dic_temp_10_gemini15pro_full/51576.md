deutoplasm:/ˈdjuːtəˌplæzəm/ | n. | 滋养层，卵黄 | Deutoplasm is the non-living part of the cytoplasm, usually containing yolk granules or fat droplets, that serves as food reserves for the developing embryo. 卵黄是细胞质的非生命部分，通常含有卵黄颗粒或脂肪滴，作为发育中胚胎的食物储备。| Example: The amount of deutoplasm present in an egg influences the type of cleavage that occurs during embryonic development.  卵中存在的卵黄量会影响胚胎发育过程中发生的卵裂类型。 | Synonym:  yolk, vitellus | Antonym:  (No direct antonym) | noun
