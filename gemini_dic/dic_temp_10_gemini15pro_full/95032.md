inaccurately: /ɪnˈækjərɪtli/ | adv. | 不准确地，错误地 | Not correctly; not exactly |  He inaccurately described the events of that night. 他对那天晚上发生的事情描述不准确。| The report inaccurately portrayed the company's financial situation. 这份报告不准确地描绘了公司的财务状况。 | 近义词: wrongly, erroneously, falsely, imprecisely | 反义词: accurately, correctly, precisely, exactly | 词性: 副词

词源:
From in- (not) + accurate + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Accurate" comes from Latin *accūrātus, past participle of accūrō (“I take care of, attend to diligently”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + cūra (“care, attention”).
