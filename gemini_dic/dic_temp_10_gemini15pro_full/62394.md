endocrinology:/ˌɛndəʊkrɪˈnɒlədʒi/ /ˌɛndoʊkrɪˈnɑːlədʒi/ | n. | 内分泌学 | The study of the endocrine system, its diseases, and its specific secretions known as hormones. | Endocrinology plays a crucial role in understanding and treating diabetes. 内分泌学在理解和治疗糖尿病方面起着至关重要的作用。 |  Endocrinologists specialize in endocrinology. 内分泌学家专门研究内分泌学。 |  近义词:hormonology | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

endocrine:/ˈɛndəʊkrɪn/ /ˈɛndoʊkrɪn/ | adj. | 内分泌的 | Relating to or denoting glands which secrete hormones or other products directly into the blood. | The endocrine system regulates various bodily functions. 内分泌系统调节各种身体机能。 |  The pancreas is an endocrine gland. 胰腺是一个内分泌腺。 | 近义词: hormonal | 反义词: exocrine | 词性: Adjective


Etymology:  From  endo- (within) + -crine (to separate) + -ology (study of).  Ultimately from Greek *endon (within) + krinein (to separate).
