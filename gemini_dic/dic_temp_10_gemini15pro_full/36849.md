colonnades: /ˌkɑːləˈneɪdz/ | n. | 柱廊，列柱 | A row of columns supporting a roof, often forming a covered walkway. |  The grand colonnades of the ancient temple were impressive. 古老寺庙的宏伟柱廊令人印象深刻。

The hotel's entrance was shaded by elegant colonnades.  酒店的入口处被优雅的柱廊遮蔽。 | 近义词: portico, arcade, peristyle | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "open area") | 词性: Noun

Etymology:
From French *colonnade*, from Italian *colonnata*, from Latin *columna* "column."
