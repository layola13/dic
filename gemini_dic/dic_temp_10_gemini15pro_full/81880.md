grievedly:/ˈɡriːvdli/| adv.|悲伤地，悲痛地|In a manner expressing grief or sorrow.  Showing or expressing grief.  |She looked at him grievedly. 她悲伤地看着他。|例句：He shook his head grievedly. 他悲伤地摇了摇头。|近义词：sorrowfully, sadly, mournfully|反义词：happily, joyfully|词性:副词
词源: From "grieved" + "-ly". "Grieved" comes from the verb "grieve," which originates from the Old French "grever," meaning "to burden, oppress, harm," ultimately from the Latin "gravare," meaning "to burden."
