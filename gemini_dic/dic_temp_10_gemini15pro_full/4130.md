air-droppable:/ˈɛərˌdrɒpəbəl/ (US), /ˈeəˌdrɒpəbl/ (UK)
adjective
可空投的
Suitable for being dropped from an aircraft, especially by parachute.
Supplies are being air-dropped to the refugees.  补给品正空投给难民。
The equipment was packaged in air-droppable containers.  设备被包装在可空投的集装箱里。
近义词:parachutable
反义词:non-air-droppable
词性:adjective
