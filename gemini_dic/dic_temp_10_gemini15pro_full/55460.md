distinguishment:/dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃmənt/
n.
区别，辨别，识别
The distinguishment between right and wrong is crucial. 明辨是非至关重要。
His distinguishment of the various types of birds was impressive. 他对各种鸟类的辨别能力令人印象深刻。
近义词: distinction, differentiation, discrimination
反义词: confusion, indistinguishability
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From late Middle English: distinguishing, from distinguish + -ment.
