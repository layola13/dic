canonry:/ˈkænənri/ | n. |教士职位；法政牧师职位；全体法政牧师|The position of a canon in a cathedral or collegiate church; also, the canons of a cathedral or collegiate church collectively.|He was appointed to a canonry at St. Paul's Cathedral. 他被任命为圣保罗大教堂的法政牧师。|Synonyms: prebend, canonship|Antonyms: laity (反义词的含义是“俗人”，与教士职位相对)|noun

canonry:/ˈkænənri/ | n. |教士的住宅|The residence of a canon.|The canonry was a beautiful old building next to the cathedral. 教士的住宅是座美丽的老建筑，就在大教堂旁边。|Synonyms: rectory, vicarage (these are more general terms for clergy residences)|Antonyms:  None (没有明确的反义词)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *canonirie*, from Medieval Latin *canonicaria*, from Latin *canonicus* "belonging to a rule, canonical," from Greek *kanonikos*, from *kanōn* "rule."
