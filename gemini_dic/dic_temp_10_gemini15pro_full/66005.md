examine:/ɪɡˈzæmɪn/ | v. |检查，审查，仔细观察|To inspect or scrutinize carefully; to investigate or test.|The doctor examined the patient carefully. 医生仔细检查了病人。|inspect, scrutinize, investigate|ignore, neglect|verb

examine:/ɪɡˈzæmɪn/ | v. |测试，考核|To test the knowledge or proficiency of.|The students will be examined on their history knowledge next week. 学生们下周将接受历史知识的测试。|test, assess, quiz|ignore, neglect|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin examinare "weigh accurately, test, examine,"  from Latin examen "tongue of a balance, weighing instrument," figuratively "examination, trial," from exigere "to drive out, weigh, consider, examine, demand," from ex- "out" + agere "to drive, lead, act."
