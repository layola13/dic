conferable:/kənˈfɜːrəbl/
adjective
可授予的；可赋予的
capable of being conferred or bestowed
例句：The title of professor is conferable by the university.  大学可以授予教授的头衔。

例句：Honorary degrees are conferable upon distinguished individuals.  荣誉学位可以授予杰出人士。
近义词：bestowable, grantable, awardable
反义词：non-transferable, inalienable
词性: adjective


confer:/kənˈfɜːr/
verb
授予，赋予；商谈，协商
1. grant or bestow (a title, degree, benefit, or right). 2. have discussions; exchange opinions.
例句：The university conferred an honorary degree on the renowned scientist. 大学授予这位著名科学家荣誉学位。

例句：The two sides conferred for hours, but they couldn't reach an agreement. 双方商谈了几个小时，但未能达成协议。
近义词：grant, bestow, award, discuss, consult
反义词：revoke, withdraw, withhold
词性: verb
