calendar:/ˈkælɪndər/| n.|日历；日程表|A system for dividing time into days, weeks, months, and years, often displayed in a chart or booklet.  It can also refer to a schedule of events.|I marked the date on my calendar. 我在日历上标记了日期。|synonyms: schedule, timetable, almanac|antonyms: N/A|noun
