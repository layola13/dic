half-quixotic:/ˌhæf kwɪkˈsɒtɪk/ | adj. | 半堂吉诃德式的；略带不切实际的 |  Partly or somewhat idealistic and impractical, like Don Quixote. He had a half-quixotic notion of riding across the country on horseback. 他有一个略带不切实际的想法，那就是骑马穿越全国。 | 近义词: idealistic, romantic, impractical, unrealistic, fanciful | 反义词: pragmatic, realistic, practical, down-to-earth | 词性: 形容词
