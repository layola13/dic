non-toxic:/ˌnɒnˈtɒksɪk/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈtɒksɪk/ (UK)| adj.|无毒的|Not poisonous or harmful.|This cleaning product is non-toxic and safe for use around children. 这种清洁产品无毒，可安全用于儿童周围。|The paint is non-toxic and environmentally friendly.  这种油漆无毒且环保。|近义词：harmless, safe, innocuous|反义词：toxic, poisonous|词性:形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
toxic (adjective): from French toxique, from Late Latin toxicus "poisoned," from Latin toxicum "poison," from Greek toxikon (pharmakon) "(poisonous) (drug)," from toxikon, neuter of toxikos "of or for a bow," from toxon "bow, arrow" (because arrows were often poisoned).
