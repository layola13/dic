inadequateness:/ɪnˈædɪkwətnəs/ | n. |不足;不适当;不充分|The feeling of inadequacy can be overwhelming. 不胜任的感觉可能会让人难以承受。|例句：He felt a deep sense of inadequacy when faced with the task. 面对这项任务，他感到深深的力不从心。

例句：The inadequacy of the current system is apparent.  当前系统的不足之处显而易见。

近义词：insufficiency, deficiency, incompetence, inability|反义词：sufficiency, adequacy, competence, capability|词性：名词

inadequate:/ɪnˈædɪkwət/ | adj. |不充分的;不足的;不胜任的|The funding is inadequate for the project.  这笔资金不足以完成这个项目。|例句：Her skills were inadequate for the job. 她的技能不足以胜任这项工作。

例句：The inadequate provisions led to widespread dissatisfaction.  准备不足导致了普遍的不满。

近义词：insufficient, deficient, lacking, incompetent|反义词：sufficient, adequate, competent, capable|词性：形容词
