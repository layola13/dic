nothingly:/ˈnʌθɪŋli/| adv. adj.|无价值地，微不足道地；无价值的，微不足道的|Of little or no value or importance; trifling.  Derived from "nothing" with the suffix "-ly."  Originally used as an adjective, now primarily as an adverb.|He spoke nothingly of his own achievements. 他对自己的成就轻描淡写。|近义词：insignificantly, trivially, slightly|反义词:significantly, importantly|词性:adverb, adjective

nothingly:/ˈnʌθɪŋli/| adj.|无价值的，微不足道的|Having little or no worth; insignificant.|He dismissed the idea as a nothingly concern. 他认为这个想法无关紧要，不予理会。|近义词：insignificant, trivial, paltry|反义词:significant, important, substantial|词性:adjective
