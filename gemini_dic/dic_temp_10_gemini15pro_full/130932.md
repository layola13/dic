nonaspirates:/ˌnɑːnˈæspərəts/ | n. | 非送气音 |  Sounds, typically consonants, produced without a noticeable puff of air. In English, /p/, /t/, and /k/ are aspirated at the beginning of a stressed syllable, but not after /s/.  | The /p/ in "spin" is a nonaspirate.  "spin"中的/p/是非送气音。 | 近义词: unaspirated consonants | 反义词: aspirates | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From "non-" (not) + "aspirate" (pronounced with a puff of air). "Aspirate" ultimately derives from Latin "aspirare" (to breathe on).
