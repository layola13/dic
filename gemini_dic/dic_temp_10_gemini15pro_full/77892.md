geniality's:/ˌdʒiːniˈælətiz/| n.|亲切，友善，和蔼|The possessive form of "geniality," referring to the quality of being friendly, cheerful, and warmly pleasant.|Her geniality's infectious nature made everyone around her feel comfortable. 她那富有感染力的亲切友善让周围的每个人都感到自在。|Synonyms: affability, amiability, cordiality|Antonyms: coldness, unfriendliness, hostility|Noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin genialitas, from Latin genialis ("pleasant")  + -ity (suffix forming abstract nouns).  The root *gen- means "birth, produce, race, kind," and is related to words like "generate" and "genus."
