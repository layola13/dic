homesourcing:/ˈhoʊmˌsɔːrsɪŋ/
n.
在家工作；居家外包
The practice of hiring people to work from their homes, often using the internet and other technology.
Companies are increasingly turning to homesourcing to reduce overhead costs.  公司越来越多地转向在家工作以降低管理费用。
Many companies find homesourcing a cost-effective solution. 许多公司发现居家外包是一个具有成本效益的解决方案。
telecommuting, working from home, remote work
outsourcing, offshoring
noun
