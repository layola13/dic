crampon:/ˈkræmpɒn/ (UK), /ˈkræmpɑːn/ (US)| n.|冰爪，攀登冰雪路面时固定在鞋上的金属爪|A metal frame with spikes, attached to boots for walking on ice or climbing.|例句：Mountaineers use crampons to climb icy slopes. 登山者使用冰爪攀登冰坡。

例句：He forgot his crampons and couldn't ascend the glacier. 他忘记带冰爪了，无法登上冰川。|近义词：spikes, cleats|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
