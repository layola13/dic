degeneratively:/dɪˈdʒɛnərətɪvli/
adv.
退化地，变性地
in a manner involving degeneration; progressively worse
His condition worsened degeneratively. 他的病情日益恶化。
近义词: progressively, increasingly, worsening
反义词: regeneratively, improving, healing
词性: adverb

词源: From the adjective "degenerative" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Degenerative" comes from "degenerate" (verb and adjective), which derives from the Latin "degenerare" (to decline from its kind, deteriorate), from "de-" (down from, away from) + "genus" (birth, race, kind).
