aways: /əˈweɪz/
| adv. | 总是；老是；不断地；永远；遥远地 | always means all the time or every time. It can also mean forever or at a great distance. | She always arrives early. 她总是早到。| synonyms: constantly, perpetually, invariably, forever | antonyms: never, rarely, seldom | adverb

aways: /əˈweɪz/
| n. | (非标准用法) 离开；离去 | a non-standard form of "away," meaning a departure or absence.  Rarely used and considered dialectal. | He's been on his aways for a week. 他已经离开一个星期了。(非标准用法) | synonyms: departure, absence | antonyms: arrival, presence | noun

被动语态:

疑问句:
Is she always this late? 她总是这么晚吗？

否定句:
He doesn't always tell the truth. 他不总是说实话。

PHRASE->
almost always 几乎总是

注意事项:  将来完成时和将来完成进行时与always的连用比较少见，因为always表示一直，而将来完成时强调在未来某个时间点之前完成，两者语义上不太契合。在例句中，虽然语法正确，但语义略显奇怪。  aways 作为名词的用法非常罕见且非正式，不建议在正式写作或场合中使用。
