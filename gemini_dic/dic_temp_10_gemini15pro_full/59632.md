ecclesiasticize:/ɪˌkliːziˈæstɪsaɪz/ | v. | 使教会化；使带有教会色彩；使宗教化 | To give a clerical or ecclesiastical character to; to imbue with the spirit, or subject to the usages, of the church. | The writer ecclesiasticized the language of his novels.  这位作家使他的小说语言带有教会色彩。 | The government tried to ecclesiasticize education. 政府试图使教育教会化。 | 近义词: clericalize, spiritualize | 反义词: secularize | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From Late Latin *ecclesiasticizare, from Late Latin ecclesiasticus (“ecclesiastical”) + -izare.  Surface analysis is ecclesiastic + -ize.
