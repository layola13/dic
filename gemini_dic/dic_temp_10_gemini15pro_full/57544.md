drains:/dreɪnz/| n. v.|排水沟，下水道；消耗，流干|A drain is a pipe or channel that carries away unwanted liquid, such as wastewater or rainwater. As a verb, it means to cause liquid to flow away from something, or to gradually use up a resource or supply.|The heavy rain overwhelmed the city's drains.  暴雨淹没了城市的排水系统。|近义词：sewers, channels, outlets, depletes, exhausts|反义词:fills, replenishes|词性:名词复数；动词第三人称单数
