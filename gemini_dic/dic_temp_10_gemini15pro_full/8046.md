anthropography:/ˌænθrəˈpɒɡrəfi/
n.
人类誌；人种誌
The study of human societies and cultures and their development.
例句：Anthropography provides valuable insights into the diversity of human experience. 人类誌为了解人类经验的多样性提供了宝贵的见解。

例句：Her research in anthropography focused on the rituals and traditions of a remote tribe. 她的民族誌研究集中在一个偏远部落的仪式和传统上。

近义词：ethnography, cultural anthropology
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a contrasting field like biogeography)
词性: noun
