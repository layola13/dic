carboxylic:/ˌkɑːrbɒkˈsɪlɪk/
adjective|羧基的；含羧基的|Relating to or containing the carboxyl group (-COOH).|Fatty acids are carboxylic acids.|近义词:acidic|反义词:basic|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From carbo- (carbon) + -oxyl + -ic.  Ultimately from Latin *carbo (coal, charcoal).
