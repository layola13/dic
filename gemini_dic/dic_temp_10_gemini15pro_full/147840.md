overpurchases:/ˌoʊvərˈpɜːrtʃəsɪz/
noun
过度购买；过量采购
The act of buying more than is necessary or desired.
They had to mark down prices to clear out overpurchases. 他们不得不降低价格来清理过多的采购。
overbuying, excessive purchasing
underpurchasing
n.
