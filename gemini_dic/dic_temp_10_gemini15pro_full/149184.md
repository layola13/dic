oxycymene:/ˈɒksɪsaɪmiːn/ (US) /ˈɒksɪsɪmiːn/ (UK)| n.|氧基伞花烃;异丙基甲苯酚|An organic compound with the formula C10H14O. It is a derivative of cymene with a hydroxyl group attached to the aromatic ring. It is found in some essential oils and can be used as a fragrance and flavoring agent.|Oxycymene is a component of some essential oils, such as ajowan oil.|近义词:isopropylcresol, carvacrol (depending on the specific isomer)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
