bragget:/ˈbræɡɪt/| n.|蜂蜜酒（一种用蜂蜜和香料酿造的酒精饮料）|An alcoholic beverage made from honey and spices, sometimes with herbs or malt added.|例句：The bragget was sweet and spicy, perfect for a cold winter evening. 蜂蜜酒香甜辛辣，非常适合寒冷的冬夜。

例句：He brewed a batch of bragget using a traditional medieval recipe. 他用中世纪的传统配方酿造了一批蜂蜜酒。|近义词：mead, honey wine|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bragot, from Welsh *bragawd (“malt liquor, bragget”), from Proto-Celtic *bracō- (“malt”). Cognate with Old Irish braich (“malt”).
