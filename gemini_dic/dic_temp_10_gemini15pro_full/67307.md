exsufflation:/ˌɛksʌˈfleɪʃən/
|n.|吹出，呼出; (尤指) 吹灭|The act of blowing out, especially the forceful expulsion of air from the lungs.|The exsufflation of the candles marked the end of the ceremony. 吹灭蜡烛标志着仪式的结束。|Synonyms: expiration, exhalation|Antonyms: inhalation, inspiration|noun
