doe:/doʊ/ /dəʊ/
|n.|母鹿，雌鹿，雌兔（或某些其他哺乳动物）|A female deer, hare, rabbit, or certain other mammals.|The doe and her fawn grazed peacefully in the meadow. 母鹿和小鹿在草地上安静地吃草。|Synonyms: hind (female deer), female|Antonyms: buck, stag (male deer)|Noun

doe:/doʊ/ /dəʊ/
|n.|无名氏女，珍·多，（用于法律文件中指不知姓名的女性）|Jane Doe, used in legal documents to refer to an unnamed female.|The police referred to the victim as Jane Doe until her identity could be confirmed.  警方将受害者称为无名氏女，直到她的身份得到确认。|Synonyms: N/A|Antonyms: John Doe (unnamed male)|Noun
