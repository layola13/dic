parchment:/ˈpɑːrtʃmənt/ | n. |羊皮纸， parchment paper 烤纸|A stiff, thin material made from the skin of a sheep or goat, used in the past for writing on.  A type of paper used in baking.|The lawyer unrolled the ancient parchment. 律师展开古老的羊皮纸。| The recipe called for lining the pan with parchment paper. 食谱要求在烤盘里铺上烤纸。|synonyms:vellum, manuscript, scroll|antonyms: paper (in a modern context) | noun

parchment:/ˈpɑːrtʃmənt/ | adj. |羊皮纸似的;  (of skin) dry and yellowish |Resembling parchment in texture or color, especially dry and yellowish. | Her skin was parchment-thin and fragile. 她的皮肤像羊皮纸一样薄而脆弱。| synonyms:dry, leathery, papery | antonyms:soft, supple, moist| adjective
