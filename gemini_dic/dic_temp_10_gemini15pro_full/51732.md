devoid:/dɪˈvɔɪd/
adj.
缺乏的，空的
entirely lacking or free from
例句：The landscape was bleak and devoid of life.  这片景色荒凉而毫无生气。

例句：He seemed devoid of any emotion. 他似乎没有任何情绪。

近义词：lacking, barren, empty, bereft, destitute
反义词：full, replete, abounding, teeming
词性: 形容词
