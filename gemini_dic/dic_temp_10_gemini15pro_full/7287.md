angst:/æŋkst/ | n. | 焦虑，担忧 | A feeling of deep anxiety or dread, typically an unfocused one about the human condition or the state of the world. |  She was filled with angst about her future. 她对自己的未来充满了焦虑。| 近义词: anxiety, dread, worry, apprehension | 反义词: calm, peace, tranquility | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From German *Angst* meaning "fear, anxiety."  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *angustaz* "narrow, constricted," from Proto-Indo-European *h₂énǵʰ- "narrow, tight."  The semantic shift likely occurred through the feeling of constriction or tightness associated with fear and anxiety.
