bankerly:/ˈbæŋkərli/
adjective
银行家的；像银行家的；谨慎的；精明的
characteristic of or suitable for a banker; cautious with money; prudent
He gave me some very bankerly advice about investments. 他给了我一些非常精明的投资建议。
近义词:prudent, cautious, circumspect, shrewd, astute
反义词:reckless, imprudent, extravagant
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From banker + -ly.  "Banker" itself comes from Old Italian "banchiere" (money-changer, banker), derived from "banco" (bench, counter). This refers to the benches or tables used by money changers in medieval marketplaces.
