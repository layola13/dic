Hanukkah: /ˈhɑːnəkə/ (US), /ˈhɑːnʊkə/ (UK)| n.|光明节|A Jewish festival lasting eight days and nights, celebrating the rededication of the Second Temple in Jerusalem in the second century BC.|We light the menorah each night of Hanukkah. 我们在光明节的每个晚上点燃烛台。| Festival of Lights, Feast of Dedication| Yom Kippur (although very different in tone and meaning, both are important Jewish holidays)|noun
