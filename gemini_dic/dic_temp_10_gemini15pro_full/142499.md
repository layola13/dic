osmosis:/ɒzˈməʊsɪs/ (US: /ɑːzˈmoʊsɪs/) | n. | 渗透；潜移默化 | The process by which a solvent passes through a semipermeable membrane from a less concentrated solution into a more concentrated one, thus equalizing the concentrations on each side of the membrane.  | Water enters the root hairs of plants by osmosis. 水通过渗透作用进入植物的根毛。 | synonyms: diffusion, permeation | antonyms: reverse osmosis | noun

osmotic:/ɒzˈmɒtɪk/ (US: /ɑːzˈmɑːtɪk/) | adj. | 渗透的 | Relating to or characterized by osmosis. | The osmotic pressure inside the cell is high. 细胞内的渗透压很高。 | synonyms: diffusive, permeable | antonyms: impermeable | adjective
