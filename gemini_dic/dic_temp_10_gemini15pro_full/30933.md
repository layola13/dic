chairman:/ˈtʃɛəmən/ (US), /ˈtʃɛəmən/ (UK)| n.|主席，董事长|A person who leads or heads a company, organization, or department.|She's the chairman of a large multinational corporation. 她是一家大型跨国公司的董事长。|president, CEO, head|employee, subordinate|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ceapmann "merchant, trader," from ceap "bargaining, business, purchase" + mann "man." The sense shifted to "president of a meeting" in the 18th century, perhaps influenced by the French word "chairman."
