cardio:/ˈkɑːrdioʊ/ (US), /ˈkɑːdiəʊ/ (UK)| n. adj.|有氧运动，心血管的|Relating to the heart and blood vessels.  Can be used as a noun, short for "cardiovascular exercise".|例句：I do cardio three times a week. 我每周做三次有氧运动。

|例句：Cardio is important for overall health. 有氧运动对整体健康很重要。|近义词：aerobic, cardiovascular|反义词:anaerobic|词性:noun, adjective
