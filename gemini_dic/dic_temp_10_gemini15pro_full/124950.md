muffle:/ˈmʌfəl/ (US) /ˈmʌfl/ (UK)| v. n.|使消声；裹住；抑制；消声器；围巾；头巾|To wrap or cover something in order to deaden its sound; to make a sound quieter or less distinct.|She muffled her cough with her hand. 她用手捂住嘴咳嗽。|近义词：stifle, suppress, mute, deaden, quieten|反义词：amplify, increase, intensify|verb

muffle:/ˈmʌfəl/ (US) /ˈmʌfl/ (UK)| n.|消声器；围巾；头巾|Something that muffles, especially a scarf or a part of a garment that is wrapped around the neck for warmth.|The thief wore a muffle to hide his face. 小偷用头巾蒙着脸。|近义词：scarf, shawl, wrap|反义词：-|noun
