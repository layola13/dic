no-hopers:/ˈnəʊˌhəʊpəz/| n.|没有希望的人；没有前途的人；注定要失败的人|People who are considered unlikely to succeed or improve their situation.  Those deemed to have no chance of success or positive change in their lives. |They were labelled as no-hopers by the school system.  他们被学校系统贴上了没有希望的人的标签。

The government has abandoned those no-hopers to their fate. 政府已经放弃了那些没有希望的人，任由他们自生自灭。|近义词：losers, failures, deadbeats, underachievers|反义词: high-fliers, achievers, winners|词性:noun
