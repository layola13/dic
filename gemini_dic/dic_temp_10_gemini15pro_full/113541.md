macabre:/məˈkɑːbrə/ /məˈkɑːb/| adj.|可怕的，恐怖的，令人毛骨悚然的|Suggesting or relating to death, especially in a gruesome or horrifying way.|The movie featured macabre scenes of torture and murder. 这部电影充斥着可怕的酷刑和谋杀场景。|近义词：gruesome, grisly, morbid, ghastly, lurid|反义词：pleasant, cheerful, delightful|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From French *macabre*, from Old French *danse macabre* "dance of death," of unknown origin. Possibly related to the Maccabees, Jewish martyrs whose story was the subject of medieval plays.
