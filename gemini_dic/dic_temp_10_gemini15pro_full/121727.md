misprograming: /ˌmɪsˈproʊɡræmɪŋ/ /ˌmɪsˈprəʊɡræmɪŋ/
noun
错误编程，编程错误
The act or process of writing or inputting a program incorrectly, resulting in errors or malfunctions.
The misprograming caused the robot to malfunction.  错误的编程导致机器人发生故障。
misprogramming, incorrect programming, faulty programming
correct programming, proper programming
n.
