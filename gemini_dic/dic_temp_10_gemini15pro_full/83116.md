gustiest:/ˈɡʌstiɪst/| adj.|阵风最大的；刮风最猛的|Characterized by or having the strongest gusts of wind. |例句：It was the gustiest day of the year.  这是今年风最大的日子。

例句：The gustiest winds are expected near the coast.  预计沿海地区风力最大。|近义词：windiest, breeziest, stormiest|反义词：calmest, stillest|词性:形容词
词源: From gust + -y + -est.  来自gust（阵风）+ -y（形容词后缀）+ -est（最高级后缀）。
