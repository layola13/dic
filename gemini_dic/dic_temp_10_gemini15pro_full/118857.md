metaphysis:/məˈtæfɪsɪs/
n.
干骺端; 骨骺线
The part of a long bone between the epiphysis and the diaphysis.  It is the region where the bone grows in length.
The fracture occurred in the metaphysis of the femur.  骨折发生在大腿骨的干骺端。
近义词:epiphyseal plate, growth plate
反义词:diaphysis (骨干)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *meta* (between, after, with, across) + *physis* (growth, nature).
