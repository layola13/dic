diphthongal:/dɪfˈθɒŋɡəl/ /daɪfˈθɑːŋɡəl/| adj.|双元音的；包含双元音的|Relating to or consisting of a diphthong (a sound formed by the combination of two vowels in a single syllable, in which the sound begins as one vowel and moves toward another).|The word "loud" has a diphthongal sound.  “loud”这个单词有一个双元音。|The pronunciation of "boil" is diphthongal. “boil”的发音是双元音的。|近义词:biphonemic|反义词:monophthongal|词性:形容词
