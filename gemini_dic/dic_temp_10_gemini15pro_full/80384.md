goliath:/ɡəˈlaɪəθ/| n.|巨人，庞然大物|A person or thing of enormous size or strength, often intimidating or overwhelming.  Derived from the biblical Philistine giant Goliath, defeated by David.|例句：The new skyscraper is a veritable goliath, dwarfing all other buildings in the city. 这座新的摩天大楼是一个名副其实的巨人，使城市里所有其他建筑物都相形见绌。

例句：The company has become a goliath in the tech industry, dominating the market. 这家公司已成为科技行业的巨头，主导着市场。|近义词：giant, behemoth, titan, colossus|反义词:dwarf, pygmy|词性:noun
