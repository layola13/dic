fuggy:/ˈfʌɡi/ | adj. | 闷热的，潮湿的，雾蒙蒙的 | Characterized by fog or mist; stuffy and humid. |  The room was fuggy with cigarette smoke. 房间里烟雾弥漫，闷热不堪。| The fuggy weather made it difficult to breathe. 闷热的天气让人呼吸困难。| 近义词: muggy, humid, stuffy, close | 反义词: airy, fresh, clear | 词性: 形容词

词源: From "fug," meaning stale air, from Middle English "fog," ultimately of uncertain origin.
