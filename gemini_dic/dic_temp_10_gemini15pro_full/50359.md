depopulacy:/diːˌpɒpjuˈleɪʃən/
n.
人口减少，人口稀少
The state of having been depopulated; a reduction in population.
The depopulacy of the rural areas is a serious concern for the government.  农村地区的人口减少让政府非常担忧。

The island suffered from depopulacy after the volcano erupted.  火山爆发后，岛上人口锐减。
近义词:depopulation, decline in population
反义词:population growth, overpopulation
词性:noun


Etymology:
From de- + population, from Late Latin populatio, from Latin populus "people".
