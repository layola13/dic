cheerful:/ˈtʃɪərfəl/| adj.|快乐的，高兴的，兴高采烈的|Noticeably happy and optimistic.|She had a cheerful disposition and always looked on the bright side. 她性格开朗，总是看到光明的一面。|近义词：happy, joyful, merry, bright, cheery|反义词：sad, gloomy, depressed, miserable|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cherful, from Old English *cearful (“sorrowful,” later “full of care, anxious”), equivalent to care +‎ -ful. More at care. Sense development from “anxious” to “happy” is unusual. Possibly influenced by cheer.
