hailing-from:/ˈheɪlɪŋ frəm/ |adj./adv. phrase|来自；出生于|Originating from; coming from a particular place.  This is generally considered an informal phrasing.|The chef, hailing from Italy, created an authentic pasta dish. 这位来自意大利的厨师做了一道正宗的意大利面。|Synonyms: from, originating from, native to|Antonyms: residing in, living in|phrase
