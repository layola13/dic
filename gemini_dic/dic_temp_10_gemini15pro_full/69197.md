fda:/ˌɛf diː ˈeɪ/ | n. | 美国食品药品监督管理局 (Food and Drug Administration) |  A federal agency of the United States Department of Health and Human Services responsible for protecting and promoting public health through the control and supervision of food safety, tobacco products, dietary supplements, prescription and over-the-counter pharmaceutical drugs (medications), vaccines, biopharmaceuticals, blood transfusions, medical devices, electromagnetic radiation emitting devices (ERED), cosmetics, animal foods & feed and veterinary products. | The FDA approved the new drug.  美国食品药品监督管理局批准了这种新药。 | 近义词: (None - as it's a specific agency) | 反义词: (None) | abbreviation
