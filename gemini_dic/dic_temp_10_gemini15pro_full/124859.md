mucous:/ˈmjuːkəs/| adj.| 粘液的；分泌粘液的| relating to mucus; secreting mucus|  The mucous membranes of the nose become inflamed during a cold. 感冒时鼻子的粘膜会发炎。|近义词: slimy, viscous|反义词: dry|词性:形容词
mucus:/ˈmjuːkəs/| n.| 粘液| a thick, slippery substance produced by the body, especially in the nose and throat, that protects and lubricates surfaces|  The cold caused a build-up of mucus in his chest.  感冒导致他胸部积聚粘液。|近义词: phlegm, slime|反义词: (没有明确的反义词)|词性:名词
