cooptate:/koʊˈɒpteɪt/| vt.|吸收；指派；任命|To appoint to membership of a committee or other body by invitation of the existing members.|The committee cooptated several new members to help with the increased workload. 委员会吸收了几名新成员以帮助处理增加的工作量。|Synonyms: elect, appoint, select, choose|Antonyms: dismiss, expel, reject|verb

cooptation:/koʊˌɑːpˈteɪʃn/| n.|吸收；指派；任命|The action or process of adding members to a committee or other body.|The cooptation of several new members brought fresh perspectives to the committee.  吸收几名新成员给委员会带来了新的视角。|Synonyms: appointment, election, selection|Antonyms: dismissal, expulsion, rejection|noun
