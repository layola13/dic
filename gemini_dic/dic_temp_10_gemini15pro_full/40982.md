coordinately:/koʊˈɔːrdɪnɪtli/| adv.|协调地，同步地|In a coordinated manner; together.  Acting in a way that is organized and harmonious.|The dancers moved coordinately, their steps perfectly synchronized. 舞蹈演员们协调地移动，他们的舞步完美同步。|近义词：harmoniously, synchronously, jointly, concurrently|反义词: independently, separately, discordantly|词性:副词
Etymology: From coordinate + -ly.  "Coordinate" comes from Late Latin "coordinatus," past participle of "coordinare,"  meaning "to arrange in order," from "co-" (together) + "ordinare" (to arrange).
