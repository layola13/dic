nonabstaining:/ˌnɒnəbˈsteɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 不戒酒的；不断饮酒的 | Not abstaining from alcohol; continuing to drink alcohol. |  He considered himself a nonabstaining Catholic. 他认为自己是一个不戒酒的天主教徒。|  He was a nonabstaining drinker, enjoying a glass of wine with dinner every night. 他是一个不断饮酒的人，每晚都喜欢在晚餐时喝一杯葡萄酒。 | 近义词: drinking, indulging | 反义词: abstaining, temperate, sober | 词性: adjective

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + abstaining (present participle of "abstain")  "abstain" comes from the Old French "astenir" and ultimately from the Latin "abstinere," meaning "to hold back from."
