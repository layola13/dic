enodal:/ˈiːnəʊdəl/ /ˈiːnoʊdəl/
adjective
节的；结的；位于结点的
Relating to or situated at a node.  A node can refer to a point in a stem where a leaf or branch arises, or more generally, a point of intersection or connection.
The enodal thickening on the bamboo stem provides structural support.  竹茎上的节状加厚提供了结构支撑。
The botanist studied the enodal patterns of the plant.  植物学家研究了植物的节状模式。
近义词:nodal
反义词:internodal
词性:adjective
