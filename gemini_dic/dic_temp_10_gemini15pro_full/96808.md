inertial:/ɪˈnɜːʃəl/ /ɪˈnɝːʃəl/
adjective
惯性的；惰性的
Relating to inertia; having inertia.  A property of matter by which it continues in its existing state of rest or uniform motion in a straight line, unless that state is changed by an external force.
例句：
An inertial navigation system uses gyroscopes to maintain its orientation.
惯性导航系统使用陀螺仪来保持其方向。

The inertial guidance system failed, causing the rocket to veer off course.
惯性制导系统失灵，导致火箭偏离航向。

近义词：momentum-based, massive, weighty
反义词：non-inertial

词性: adjective
