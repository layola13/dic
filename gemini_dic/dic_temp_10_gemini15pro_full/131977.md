nonconversationally:/ˌnɑːnkɑːnvərˈseɪʃənəli/ | adv. | 非对话式地，不以谈话方式地 |  In a manner that avoids or minimizes conversation; formally or stiffly. | She addressed the group nonconversationally, reading directly from her prepared notes. 她以非对话的方式向小组发表讲话，直接朗读她准备好的笔记。|  formally, stiffly, officially | conversationally, casually, informally | adverb

Etymology: non- (not) + conversational + -ly (adverbial suffix)
