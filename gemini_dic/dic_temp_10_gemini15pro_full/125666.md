mumsy:/ˈmʌmzi/| adj.|妈妈似的; 像妈妈的; 家庭妇女似的; (尤指上了年纪的妇女)衣着老式的，过分讲究的|A mumsy woman is slightly old-fashioned and not very stylish in her clothes and behaviour, especially a middle-aged or older woman. |例句：She looked rather mumsy in a floral dress. 她穿着一条碎花连衣裙，看起来很像家庭妇女。

例句：He found her mumsy appearance reassuring. 他觉得她妈妈般的装扮令人安心。|近义词：matronly, frumpy, dowdy|反义词：stylish, chic, fashionable|词性:形容词
