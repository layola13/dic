alcohometric:/ˌælkəˈhɒmɪtrɪk/ | adj. |酒精测量的；酒精计的 | Relating to the measurement of the amount of alcohol in a substance. | This alcohometric table shows the relationship between specific gravity and alcohol content. 这张酒精测量表显示了比重和酒精含量之间的关系。|  The brewery uses an alcohometric process to ensure consistent alcohol levels in their beer. 这家啤酒厂使用酒精测量工艺来确保其啤酒中的酒精含量一致。 | 近义词: alcohol-measuring | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From alcohol + -metric (from Greek metron "measure").
