niente:/ˈnjɛnteɪ/ (Italian pronunciation)| pron. n. adv.| nothing; not a thing; anything |  It is used as a pronoun, noun, and adverb meaning "nothing," "not a thing," or sometimes ironically "anything." It comes directly from Italian. |  Non ho niente da dire. (I have nothing to say.) |  nothing, zero, zilch | everything, something | Pronoun, Noun, Adverb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
C'è niente da fare? = Is there anything to do?
Non ho niente in tasca. = I have nothing in my pocket.


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Non sto facendo niente di speciale. = I'm not doing anything special.
Stai leggendo niente di interessante? = Are you reading anything interesting?


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Non ho visto niente di simile prima. = I haven't seen anything like this before.
Hai mangiato niente oggi? = Have you eaten anything today?


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Non ho fatto niente tutto il giorno. = I haven't been doing anything all day.
Cosa hai combinato niente ultimamente? = What have you been up to lately? (literally: What have you been combining nothing lately?) This example uses "niente" ironically.


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Non dissi niente. = I said nothing.
Non c'era niente da fare. = There was nothing to do.


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Non stavo facendo niente di male. = I wasn't doing anything wrong.
Non stava leggendo niente. = She wasn't reading anything.


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Non avevo visto niente di simile prima. = I hadn't seen anything like this before.
Non avevano detto niente. = They hadn't said anything.



过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
Non avevo fatto niente tutto il giorno. = I hadn't been doing anything all day.
Non aveva studiato niente per l'esame. = He hadn't been studying anything for the exam.


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Non farò niente di male. = I won't do anything wrong.
Non ci sarà niente da fare. = There will be nothing to do.



将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
Non starò facendo niente di speciale domani. = I won't be doing anything special tomorrow.
Non starà leggendo niente di interessante. = She won't be reading anything interesting.


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
Non avrò fatto niente entro domani. = I won't have done anything by tomorrow.
Non avranno detto niente entro la fine della settimana. = They won't have said anything by the end of the week.


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
Non avrò fatto niente per tutto il giorno. = I won't have been doing anything all day. (This tense is less common with "niente")
Non avrò studiato niente per tutto il semestre. = I won't have been studying anything all semester. (This tense is less common with "niente")



虚拟语气:
Se non avessi niente da fare, andrei al cinema. = If I had nothing to do, I would go to the cinema.
Vorrei non avere niente da fare. = I wish I had nothing to do.


被动语态 (Not applicable to "niente" as it cannot be acted upon)



疑问句:
Non hai niente da dire? = Don't you have anything to say?
C'è niente che posso fare per te? = Is there anything I can do for you?


否定句:
Non ho niente da dire. = I have nothing to say.
Non voglio niente. = I don't want anything.


PHRASE->
non...niente = not...anything

niente affatto = not at all
niente meno che = nothing less than
niente più che = nothing more than
