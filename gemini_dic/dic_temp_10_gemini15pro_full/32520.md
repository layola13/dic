adverb
廉价地，俗气地
in a cheap, gaudy, or showy manner, suggestive of chintz.

近义词:gaudy, cheaply, tawdrily, flashily
反义词:tastefully, elegantly, expensively, subtly
词性:adverb


chintzy:/ˈtʃɪntsi/
adjective
印花棉布的，廉价的，俗气的
of or resembling chintz; cheap and gaudy.
The curtains were made of a chintzy floral fabric. 窗帘是用俗气的花卉印花布做的。
He wore a chintzy suit to the wedding, which everyone noticed. 他穿了一套俗气的西装去参加婚礼，每个人都注意到了。
近义词:cheap, gaudy, tawdry, flashy
反义词:tasteful, elegant, expensive, subtle
词性:adjective


chintz:/tʃɪnts/
noun
印花棉布
a brightly printed and glazed cotton fabric.
She chose a chintz with a floral pattern for the curtains. 她选择了一种印有花卉图案的印花棉布做窗帘。
The sofa was covered in a cheerful chintz. 沙发上覆盖着鲜艳的印花棉布。
近义词:calico, printed cotton
反义词:(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑plain cotton 素色棉布)
词性:noun


词源:
From Hindi chī̃ṭ, from Sanskrit चित्रित citrita  "variegated, speckled".
