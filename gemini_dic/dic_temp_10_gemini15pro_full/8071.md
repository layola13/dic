anthropomorphite:/ˌænθrəpəˈmɔːrfaɪt/| n. |拟人论者，赋予人性论者|A person who attributes human characteristics to non-human entities, especially animals or inanimate objects.|例句：The anthropomorphite believed that his dog felt the same emotions as humans. 这位拟人论者相信他的狗和人类有着相同的情感。

例句：The author's anthropomorphic portrayal of the animals in the story made them relatable to young readers.  作者对故事中动物的拟人化描写使它们更容易被年轻读者所理解。|近义词：anthropomorphist|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
