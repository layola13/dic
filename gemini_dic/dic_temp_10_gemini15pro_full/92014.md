hyperoxygenate:/ˌhaɪpərˈɒksɪdʒəneɪt/ | v. |过度氧合，超氧合|To increase the oxygen concentration in a tissue or organ beyond normal levels. |The patient was hyperoxygenated before the procedure.  病人手术前进行了过度氧合。|The diver was hyperoxygenated before the deep dive. 潜水员在深潜前进行了过度氧合。| 近义词: oxygenate, superoxygenate | 反义词: deoxygenate |词性:动词
