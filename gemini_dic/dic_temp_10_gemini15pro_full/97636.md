inheritability:/ɪnˌhɛrɪtəˈbɪləti/| n. |遗传性，可遗传性|The quality of being heritable; the proportion of phenotypic variation in a population that is attributable to genetic variation among individuals.|The inheritability of eye color is high. 眼睛颜色的遗传性很高。|synonyms: heritability, transmissibility|antonyms: non-heritability|noun

inheritability:/ɪnˌhɛrɪtəˈbɪləti/| n. |遗传性，可遗传性|The degree to which a trait is genetically determined, and therefore likely to be passed from parents to offspring.|Researchers are studying the inheritability of intelligence. 研究人员正在研究智力的遗传性。|synonyms: heritability, genetic determination|antonyms: acquired trait|noun
