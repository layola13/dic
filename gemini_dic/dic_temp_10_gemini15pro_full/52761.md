dilated:/daɪˈleɪtɪd/ /ˈdaɪleɪtəd/| adj.|扩张的，扩大的|Made or become wider, larger, or more open.|Her pupils were dilated. 她的瞳孔放大了。|近义词：expanded, enlarged, widened, distended|反义词:constricted, contracted, narrowed|adjective

dilate:/daɪˈleɪt/ /ˈdaɪleɪt/| v.|（使）扩大，（使）膨胀|To make or become wider, larger, or more open.|The surgeon dilated the artery. 外科医生扩张了动脉。|近义词：expand, enlarge, widen, distend|反义词:contract, constrict, narrow|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin dilatatus, past participle of dilatare "to spread out," from Latin dis- "apart" + latus "wide."
