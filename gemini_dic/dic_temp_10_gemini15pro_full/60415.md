eightfold:/ˈeɪtfəʊld/ (US) /ˈeɪtfəʊld/ (UK)| adj. adv.|八倍的，八重的；八方面地|Multiplied by eight; having eight parts.  Relating to eight times as much or as many of something.|例句：The company's profits increased eightfold this year.  公司利润今年增加了八倍。|近义词：octuple, eight times|反义词：(no single antonym, consider "single," "double," etc.)|词性:形容词/副词
eightfold:/ˈeɪtfəʊld/ (US) /ˈeɪtfəʊld/ (UK)| n.|八倍|Something eight times as great as another.|例句：The investment yielded an eightfold return. 这项投资获得了八倍的回报。|近义词：octuple|反义词：(no single antonym)|词性:名词
