enhances:/ɪnˈhænsɪz/
verb
增强，提高
increases or improves the quality, value, or attractiveness of something
例句：Regular exercise enhances cardiovascular health.  规律的运动能增强心血管健康。

例句：The new software enhances the performance of the computer. 新软件提高了计算机的性能。
近义词：improves, boosts, strengthens, augments
反义词：diminishes, reduces, weakens, detracts
词性：v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *enhaucier (Modern French exhausser), from en- "in, on" + haut "high". Ultimately from Latin altus "high".
