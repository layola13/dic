overnarrow:/ˌoʊvərˈnæroʊ/ | adj. | 过于狭隘的，过于局限的 | Too limited or restricted in scope or outlook. |  The committee's focus was criticized for being overnarrow, ignoring crucial social factors. 委员会的重点被批评为过于狭隘，忽视了关键的社会因素。

The study's overnarrow methodology led to skewed results. 这项研究过于狭隘的方法导致了结果的偏差。 | 近义词: restricted, limited, narrow, parochial, insular | 反义词: broad, wide-ranging, comprehensive, inclusive, open-minded | 词性:形容词

詞源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "narrow" (adjective meaning "limited in extent, scope, or outlook").  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "narrow," indicating an excessive degree of limitation.  "Narrow" itself comes from Old English "nearu," meaning "confined, constricted."
