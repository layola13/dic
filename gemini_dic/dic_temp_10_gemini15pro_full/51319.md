detainingly:/dɪˈteɪnɪŋli/
adverb
拘留地，扣留地; 阻留地
in a way that detains someone or something.  Delaying someone's departure.
The customs officer detained him detainingly at the airport.  海关官员在机场拘留了他。
detainingly questioned the suspect.  拘留并审问了嫌疑人
近义词:restrictively, holdingly
反义词:releasingly, freely
词性:adverb

detain:/dɪˈteɪn/
verb
拘留；扣留；耽搁
keep (someone) from proceeding; hold back.
Police detained him for questioning. 警方拘留了他进行讯问。
近义词:hold, confine, delay
反义词:release, free
词性:verb
