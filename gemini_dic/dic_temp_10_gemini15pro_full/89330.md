homogenous:/ˌhoʊməˈdʒiːniəs/ |adj.|同质的，同类的，均匀的|Of the same kind or nature; consisting of similar parts or elements.|The population of the village is quite homogenous, mainly farmers and their families. 这个村庄的人口构成十分同质，主要是农民和他们的家人。|The chemist mixed the substances until they formed a homogenous solution.  化学家将这些物质混合，直到它们形成均匀的溶液。|近义词：uniform, similar, consistent, homogeneous|反义词：heterogeneous, diverse, varied|词性: 形容词
词源: From Ancient Greek ὁμογενής (homogenḗs, “of the same race, family, or kind”), from ὁμός (homós, “same”) + γένος (génos, “race, stock, kind”).
