octocerata: /ˌɒktəʊˈsɛrətə/ | n. | 八腕目 | An order of cephalopod molluscs that includes octopuses.  Characterized by eight arms with suckers, a beak-like mouth, and a soft body. |  Octocerata includes familiar animals like the common octopus. 八腕目包括我们熟悉的动物，例如普通章鱼。 | synonyms: Octopoda | antonyms: N/A | noun

Etymology:
From Greek *oktō* "eight" + *keras* "horn," referring to the eight arms. 源自希腊语 *oktō* “八” + *keras* “角”，指八只腕。
