monopolists:/məˈnɒpəlɪsts/ /məˈnɑːpəlɪsts/
n.
垄断者；独占者
A person or company that has a monopoly.  Someone who controls all or nearly all of the supply of a particular commodity or service.
The monopolists controlled the price of oil. 垄断者控制了石油价格。

The government is trying to prevent companies from becoming monopolists. 政府正试图阻止公司成为垄断者。

近义词:cartel, trust, syndicate
反义词:competitor, rival
词性:noun (plural)
