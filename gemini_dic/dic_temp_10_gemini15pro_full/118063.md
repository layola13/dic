mendelian:/mɛnˈdiːliən/
adjective|孟德尔的；符合孟德尔遗传定律的|Relating to Gregor Mendel, his theories of heredity, or the genetic principles he discovered. |Mendel performed his experiments on peas and discovered the basic laws of inheritance. 孟德尔在豌豆上进行了实验，发现了基本的遗传规律。| Mendelian genetics is the foundation of modern genetics. 孟德尔遗传学是现代遗传学的基础。|
近义词:genetic, hereditary|
反义词:non-Mendelian, acquired|
词性:adjective


mendelian:/mɛnˈdiːliən/
noun|孟德尔主义者|A supporter or follower of Mendel's theories of heredity.|Although initially ignored, Mendel's work was later rediscovered and championed by Mendelians. 虽然最初被忽视，但孟德尔的工作后来被孟德尔主义者重新发现并拥护。| Many early geneticists were Mendelians. 许多早期的遗传学家都是孟德尔主义者。|
近义词:geneticist|
反义词: (No direct antonym exists, but one could consider a proponent of Lamarckism or other opposing theories of inheritance)|
词性:noun
