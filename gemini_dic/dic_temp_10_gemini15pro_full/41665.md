correlativeness:/kəˌrɛlətɪvnəs/ | n. |相关性，相互关联性 |The correlativeness of the two events suggests a causal link.  这两个事件的关联性表明存在因果关系。|例句：The correlativeness between smoking and lung cancer is well-established. 吸烟和肺癌之间的相关性已得到证实。

例句：The study explored the correlativeness of social media usage and mental health.  这项研究探讨了社交媒体使用与心理健康之间的相关性。|近义词：interrelation, interdependence, connection, relationship|反义词：independence, disconnection|词性: noun

Etymology:  From correlate + -ive + -ness.  Correlate comes from the Latin "correlātus," past participle of "correlāre" (to bring into mutual relation), from "com-" (together) + "relātus" (related).
