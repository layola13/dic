blossom: /ˈblɑːsəm/ (US) /ˈblɒsəm/ (UK)| n. v.| 花，开花| A flower, especially of a fruit tree; the state of flowering.| The apple blossom is white and pink. 苹果花是白色和粉红色的。|近义词：flower, bloom, flourish, thrive|反义词: wither, fade, decay|词性:名词单数;动词

词源: From Middle English *blosme*, from Old English *blōstm* (“blossom, flower”), from Proto-Germanic *blōsmaz (“blossom, flower”).
