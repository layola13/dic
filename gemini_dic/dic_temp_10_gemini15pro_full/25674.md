bungest:/ˈbʌŋɪst/
adjective
最大的；最棒的
biggest and best.  Often used humorously or ironically.
例句：That's the bungest spider I've ever seen! 那是至今我见过的最大的蜘蛛！
例句：He told the bungest lie I've ever heard. 他说了我听过的最大的谎言。
近义词：biggest, largest, greatest, best, finest
反义词：smallest, worst
词性：adjective


词源:
Likely a humorous and informal blend of "biggest" and "best," originating in the late 19th or early 20th century. No definitive etymological source exists.
