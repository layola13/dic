captained:/ˈkæptɪnd/
verb
担任队长，指挥
acted as captain of a team or ship.
She captained the team to victory. 她带领团队取得了胜利。
He captained the ship through the storm. 他指挥着船只度过了风暴。
lead, command, head, skipper
follow
v.
