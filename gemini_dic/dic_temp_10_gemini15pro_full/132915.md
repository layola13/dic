nonelite:/ˌnɒnɪˈliːt/
adjective
非精英的；平民的
Not belonging to an elite; not of the highest quality or social status.
The nonelite members of the club felt excluded from the decision-making process.  俱乐部中的非精英成员感到被排除在决策过程之外。

近义词: ordinary, common, average, non-elite (this is often written without a hyphen)
反义词: elite, privileged, upper-class, exclusive

词性: adjective
