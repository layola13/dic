androgynism: /ænˈdrɒdʒɪnɪzəm/ | n. |雌雄同体，阴阳同体 |The state or quality of having both masculine and feminine characteristics, or of appearing neither male nor female. |  David Bowie's androgynous style became iconic in the 1970s.  大卫·鲍伊雌雄同体的风格在20世纪70年代成为了一种标志。

The fashion model cultivated an air of androgynism.  这位时装模特培养出一种雌雄同体的氛围。

| 近义词： androgyny, epicene, unisexuality | 反义词： masculinity, femininity | 词性: noun


androgynous: /ænˈdrɒdʒɪnəs/ | adj. |雌雄同体的，阴阳同体的 |Having both masculine and feminine characteristics, or appearing neither male nor female. | The androgynous character in the film was played by a woman.  电影中雌雄同体的角色由一位女性扮演。

Their clothing style is quite androgynous. 他们的服装风格很中性。

| 近义词： epicene, unisex | 反义词： masculine, feminine | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *androgynus, from Ancient Greek ἀνδρόγυνος (andrógunos, “hermaphrodite”), from ἀνήρ (anḗr, “man”) + γυνή (gunḗ, “woman”).
