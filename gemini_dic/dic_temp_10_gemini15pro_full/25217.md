budgerigar:/ˈbʌdʒərɪˌɡɑːr/ (US) /ˈbʌdʒərɪɡə/ (UK)| n.|虎皮鹦鹉|A small, long-tailed parrot, usually green and yellow, that is popular as a pet.|例句：My budgerigar loves to sing. 我的虎皮鹦鹉喜欢唱歌。|例句：The budgerigar flew around the room. 虎皮鹦鹉在房间里飞来飞去。|近义词：parakeet, shell parakeet|反义词:|词性:noun

budgies:/ˈbʌdʒiz/| n.|虎皮鹦鹉(复数)|Plural of budgerigar, a small, long-tailed parrot.|例句：I have two budgies, one green and one blue. 我有两只虎皮鹦鹉，一只绿色的，一只蓝色的。|例句：The budgies chirped happily in their cage.  虎皮鹦鹉在笼子里快乐地唧唧喳喳叫。|近义词：parakeets|反义词:|词性:noun
