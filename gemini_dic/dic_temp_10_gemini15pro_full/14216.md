autochthonous:/ɔːˈtɒkθənəs/ |adj.|土生土长的，原住民的|Indigenous; native to a particular place; originating where it is found.|例句：The autochthonous people of this island have a unique culture.  这个岛上的原住民有着独特的文化。|例句：The autochthonous flora and fauna of the region are being threatened by invasive species. 该地区的本土动植物正受到入侵物种的威胁。|近义词：indigenous, native, aboriginal|反义词：foreign, alien, introduced|词性: 形容词

autochthon:/ɔːˈtɒkθən/ |n.|原住民，土著|An indigenous inhabitant; a native.|例句：The autochthons of Australia have a deep connection to the land. 澳大利亚的土著居民与这片土地有着深厚的联系。|例句：Researchers are studying the languages of the autochthons of the Amazon rainforest. 研究人员正在研究亚马逊雨林土著居民的语言。|近义词：native, aboriginal|反义词：foreigner, alien|词性: 名词
