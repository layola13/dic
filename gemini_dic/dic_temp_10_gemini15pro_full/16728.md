barse:/bɑːrs/| n. | 河鲈 | A freshwater fish of the perch family, found in Europe and Asia. |  The barse in the lake were biting well that day. 那天湖里的河鲈咬钩很积极。 | 近义词：perch, bass | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific fish) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Middle English *bars, from Old French *bars, from Vulgar Latin *barbus, from Late Latin barbus “barbel”, ultimately from Latin barba “beard” (referring to the barbels or whisker-like sensory organs near the mouth).
