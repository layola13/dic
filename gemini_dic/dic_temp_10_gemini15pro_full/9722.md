antiuniversities:/ˈæntiˌjuːnɪˈvɜːrsətiz/
n.
反大学；非传统大学；自由大学
Institutions of higher learning that challenge traditional academic norms, often emphasizing practical skills, counter-cultural values, or experimental approaches to education.
The antiuniversity offered workshops on radical social theory and community organizing. 反大学开设了激进社会理论和社区组织的研讨会。
The rise of antiuniversities in the 1960s reflected a growing disillusionment with mainstream academia.  20 世纪 60 年代反大学的兴起反映了人们对主流学术界日益增长的幻灭感。
synonyms: free universities, alternative universities, experimental colleges
antonyms: traditional universities, conventional universities
noun
