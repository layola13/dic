non-homogeneity:/ˌnɒnˌhɒmədʒəˈniːɪti/ /ˌnɑːnˌhoʊmədʒəˈniːɪti/ | n. | 非均匀性，不均匀性，异质性 | The state or quality of not being homogeneous; consisting of dissimilar elements or parts. | The non-homogeneity of the soil caused problems for the construction. 土壤的非均匀性给施工带来了问题。| heterogeneity, dissimilarity, variance, unevenness | homogeneity, uniformity, similarity | noun
