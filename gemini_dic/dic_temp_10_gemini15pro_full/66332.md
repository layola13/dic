excruciatingly:/ɪkˈskruːʃieɪtɪŋli/
adverb
极其痛苦地，极度地
extremely painfully; intensely
例句：She described the pain as excruciatingly sharp. 她描述那种疼痛极其尖锐。

例句：The meeting was excruciatingly long and boring. 会议长得令人痛苦，而且很无聊。

近义词：agonizingly, intensely, terribly, extremely
反义词：mildly, slightly, gently
词性：副词


excruciate:/ɪkˈskruːʃieɪt/
verb
折磨，使极度痛苦
to inflict severe pain on; torture
例句：He was excruciated by the knowledge that he could have prevented the accident.  他知道自己本来可以阻止这场事故，这让他非常痛苦。

例句：The guilt excruciated her.  内疚折磨着她。

近义词：torment, torture
反义词：soothe, comfort
词性：动词


excruciating:/ɪkˈskruːʃieɪtɪŋ/
adjective
极痛苦的，折磨人的
extremely painful
例句：I have an excruciating headache. 我头痛欲裂。

例句：The waiting was excruciating. 等待的过程十分煎熬。

近义词：agonizing, torturous
反义词：pleasant, comfortable
词性：形容词
