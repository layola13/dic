effluvium:/ɪˈfluːviəm/| n. |恶臭，臭气；散发物，射出物|An unpleasant or harmful odor, gas, or vapor; something that flows out.|The effluvium from the factory was unbearable. 工厂散发出的臭气令人难以忍受。|近义词：odor, smell, stench, emanation, exhalation|反义词:fragrance, perfume|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Latin *effluvium*, from *effluere* "to flow out," from *ex-* "out" + *fluere* "to flow."
