matriculate: /məˈtrɪkjʊleɪt/ | v. | 入学，注册 | to enroll or be enrolled in a college or university | She matriculated at Harvard University last fall. 她去年秋天在哈佛大学注册入学。|  He matriculated as a student of law. 他注册成为法律系学生。| synonyms: enroll, register, enter | antonyms: withdraw, drop out | verb

matriculated: /məˈtrɪkjʊleɪted/ | adj. | 已注册的，已入学的 | enrolled or registered at a college or university |  a matriculated student 注册入学的学生|  The matriculated students attended the orientation.  已注册的学生参加了迎新会。| synonyms: enrolled, registered | antonyms: unenrolled, unregistered | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *matriculatus, past participle of matriculare "to enroll," from Latin matricula "public roll, register," diminutive of matrix "list, register."
