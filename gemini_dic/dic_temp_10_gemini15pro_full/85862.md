head-on:/ˌhedˈɒn/ (UK), /ˌhedˈɑːn/ (US)| adv. adj.|正面地，迎头地；正面的|Directly, facing forward; without trying to avoid something difficult.|The two cars collided head-on. 两辆汽车迎头相撞。|近义词：directly, frontally, straight on|反义词：indirectly, obliquely|词性:副词, 形容词

head-on:/ˌhedˈɒn/ (UK), /ˌhedˈɑːn/ (US)| adj.|正面的|Directly, facing forward; without trying to avoid something difficult.|It was a head-on collision. 这是一场正面碰撞。|近义词：direct, frontal, straight|反义词：indirect, oblique|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "head" + "on".  "Head" refers to the front, and "on" emphasizes the directness of the impact or confrontation.
