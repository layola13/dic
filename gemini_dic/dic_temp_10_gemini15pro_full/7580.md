annotatory:/ˈænətətɔːri/ | adj. | 注解的，注释的 | Serving to annotate; containing or characterized by annotations. |  The annotatory notes in the margins of the book were very helpful. 书页空白处的注释非常有帮助。 | 近义词: explanatory, expository, commentative, illustrative | 反义词: unexplained, uncommented | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *annotatorius, from annotatus (past participle of annotare "to mark, note"), from ad- "to" + notare "to mark".
