normann:/ˈnɔːrmən/| n.|诺曼人；诺曼底人|A member of a people of Viking origin who settled in Normandy (a region of northern France) in the 10th century and subsequently conquered England in 1066.  They also settled in other parts of Europe, notably Sicily and southern Italy.|The Normans introduced French language and culture to England.|近义词：Norman French, Viking (ancestor)|反义词:English (in the context of the Norman Conquest)|词性:noun
