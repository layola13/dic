godzilla: /ɡɒdˈzɪlə/ (British), /ɡɑːdˈzɪlə/ (American) | n. | 哥斯拉 | A giant, monstrous, dinosaur-like creature that destroys cities, originating from Japanese cinema.  It is often depicted as a metaphor for the destructive power of nuclear weapons. | Godzilla destroyed Tokyo in the movie. 哥斯拉在电影里摧毁了东京。 | synonyms: monster, kaiju, beast | antonyms: hero, savior | noun
