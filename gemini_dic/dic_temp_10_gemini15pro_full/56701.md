doomily:/ˈduːmɪli/| adv.|厄运地；不祥地；悲观地|In a manner suggesting an impending doom or misfortune.  Expressing or suggesting gloom or pessimism.|The bell tolled doomily. 钟声不祥地敲响。|Synonyms: gloomily, pessimistically, ominously, forebodingly|Antonyms: cheerfully, optimistically, hopefully|Word type: adverb
