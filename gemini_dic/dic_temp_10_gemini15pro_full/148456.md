overstraightened: /ˌoʊvərˈstreɪtnd/ | adj. | 过于拉直的，矫枉过正的 | Made excessively straight, often to the point of appearing unnatural or stiff.  This usually refers to hair but can also be used metaphorically. | Her overstraightened hair looked unnatural and severe. 她的头发拉得太直了，看起来不自然，很生硬。|  The government's overstraightened economic policies led to unforeseen consequences. 政府矫枉过正的经济政策导致了无法预料的后果。 | 近义词: overdone, excessive, rigid, stiff | 反义词: natural, relaxed, loose, curly | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "straighten" (verb meaning "to make or become straight") + "-ed" (past participle suffix forming an adjective)
