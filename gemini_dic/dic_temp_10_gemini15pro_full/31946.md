cheliform:/ˈkiːlɪfɔːrm/| adj.|螯形的，钳形的|Shaped like the claw of a crab or other crustacean.  Resembling a chela; pincer-shaped.|The beetle's cheliform mandibles allowed it to grasp and crush its prey.|近义词：chelate, forcipate|反义词:|adjective
