calendars: /ˈkælɪndərz/ (US), /ˈkælɪndəz/ (UK)
|n.|日历；日程表|A system for dividing time into days, weeks, months, and years, often displayed on a chart or in a book.  A schedule of events.|I marked the date on my calendar. 我在日历上标记了日期。|synonyms: schedule, timetable, agenda, almanac|antonyms: None|Noun
