oxen's:/ˈɑːksənz/
noun
公牛的；牛的
possessive form of oxen, meaning "belonging to oxen" or "of the oxen"
The oxen's yoke was heavy. 牛的轭很重。
The farmer repaired the oxen's cart. 农夫修理了牛车。
近义词:bulls', cattle's
反义词: (No direct antonym for a possessive.  Could consider something like "cows'" if focusing on gender, but not a true opposite.)
词性:possessive noun
