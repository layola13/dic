crazed:/kreɪzd/| adj.|疯狂的，狂热的，着迷的|Mentally deranged; insane. Driven to distraction; frantic.  Infatuated; obsessed.|The crazed fan chased after the singer's car.  那个疯狂的粉丝追着歌手的车跑。|近义词：insane, mad, demented, frantic, obsessed|反义词：sane, rational, calm, composed|词性:形容词
Etymology: From craze + -ed. Craze from Old Norse *krasa "to shatter."
