passant:/ˈpæsənt/, /pɑːˈsɑːnt/ | adj. | 过路兵，经过的 | (in chess) designating a pawn's capture of an opponent's pawn that has just moved two squares forward from its initial position.  | Example: He made a passant capture of the pawn. 他用过路兵吃掉了那个卒。 | Example: The passant move is a unique rule in chess. 过路兵走法是国际象棋中一个独特的规则。 | 近义词: passing, by-going | 反义词: stationary, fixed | 词性: adjective

词源: From French *passant*, present participle of *passer* "to pass," from Late Latin *passare*.
