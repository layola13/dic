backs:/bæks/ | n. | 背部；后部；背面；脊梁；靠背；支持者 | The part of the human body from the shoulders to the buttocks; the rear or reverse surface of something; a player in a team game who plays in a defensive position. | He injured his back lifting weights. 他举重时伤了背部。| synonyms: rear, reverse, posterior, spine, supporters | antonyms: front, forward | noun

backs:/bæks/ | v. | 支持；后退；下赌注于；背书；使后退；位于…的后面 | To give financial, material, or moral support to; to move backward; to bet on; to endorse; to cause to move backward; to be situated behind. |  She backed the winning horse. 她支持了获胜的马。 | synonyms: support, endorse, champion, retreat, reverse | antonyms: oppose, obstruct, advance | verb
