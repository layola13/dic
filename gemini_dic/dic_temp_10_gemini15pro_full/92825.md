iconological:/ˌaɪkənəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. |图像学的；图像象征的| Relating to the study of the meaning of visual images, especially in art, by considering their symbolism and cultural context. |  The professor gave an iconological interpretation of the painting, explaining the hidden meanings behind the symbols. 教授对这幅画进行了图像学解读，解释了符号背后的隐藏含义。 | Example: The art historian's iconological analysis revealed the painting's complex religious symbolism.  这位艺术史学家的图像学分析揭示了这幅画复杂的宗教象征意义。|  symbolic, emblematic, allegorical| literal, factual | adjective
