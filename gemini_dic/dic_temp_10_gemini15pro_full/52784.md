dilettante:/ˌdɪləˈtɑːnt/ | n. adj. | 业余爱好者；浅尝辄止的人 | A person who cultivates an area of interest, such as the arts, without real commitment or knowledge. | He's a dilettante artist; he paints for fun, not professionally. 他是一位业余艺术家；他画画是为了娱乐，而不是为了专业。| 近义词：amateur, dabbler, superficial | 反义词：professional, expert, connoisseur | 词性: 名词, 形容词

dilettante:/ˌdɪləˈtɑːnt/ | adj. | 业余的；浅尝辄止的 | Showing superficial interest, lacking in depth of knowledge. |  His dilettante approach to music irritated the serious musicians. 他对音乐浅尝辄止的态度激怒了那些认真的音乐家。| 近义词：amateurish, superficial, shallow | 反义词：professional, expert, profound | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From Italian dilettante, from the present participle of dilettare "to delight," from Latin delectare.
