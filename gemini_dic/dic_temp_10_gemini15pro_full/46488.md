daintiness:/ˈdeɪntinɪs/
n.
精致；优雅；美味
The quality of being dainty; delicate beauty or grace; deliciousness.
She admired the daintiness of the porcelain figurines. 她欣赏瓷制小雕像的精致。
The daintiness of the pastries made them almost too pretty to eat.  这些糕点的精致使它们几乎太漂亮而不忍心吃。
近义词:delicacy, elegance, grace, refinement, exquisiteness
反义词:coarseness, clumsiness, gaudiness, vulgarity
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *deinteth, *deynteth, from Old French daintie, deintié ("something pleasant, agreeable; pleasure, delight; kindness, courtesy; value, worth; esteem"), from Latin dignitatem (accusative singular of dignitas, "worthiness, dignity, rank, honor"). More at dignity.
