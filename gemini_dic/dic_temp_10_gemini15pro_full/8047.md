anthropoid:/ˈænθrəpɔɪd/| adj. n.|类人的，类人猿；类人猿的|Resembling a human, especially in shape or outward appearance.  Relating to or belonging to the group of primates comprising monkeys, apes, and humans.|例句：The museum displayed the skeleton of an anthropoid ape.  博物馆展出了一个类人猿的骨骼。|近义词：humanoid, simian|反义词:non-human|词性:adjective, noun


anthropoid:/ˈænθrəpɔɪd/| n.|类人猿|A primate, especially an ape or monkey, resembling a human.|例句：The gorilla is a large anthropoid. 大猩猩是一种大型类人猿。|近义词：ape, monkey, primate|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider lower primates like lemurs or non-primates)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *anthropoïde*, from Ancient Greek *ἀνθρωποειδής (anthropoeidēs)*, from *ἄνθρωπος (ánthrōpos)* "human being" + *-ειδής (-eidēs)* "having the form of".
