hirelings: /ˈhaɪərlɪŋz/ | n. | 雇佣兵；雇工；唯利是图的人 |  A person who is hired for a particular job, especially a mercenary soldier.  Often used with a negative connotation, suggesting that the person is motivated solely by money and lacks loyalty or principle. | The king surrounded himself with hirelings, trusting no one with true allegiance. 国王身边都是雇佣兵，不相信任何真正效忠的人。|  mercenaries, employees, workers | loyalists, volunteers | noun
