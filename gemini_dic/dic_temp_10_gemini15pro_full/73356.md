foregate:/ˈfɔːrɡeɪt/
n.
前门，外门
A gate in the outer wall of a fortified city.
The foregate of the castle was heavily guarded. 城堡的前门有重兵把守。
近义词: gateway, entrance, portal
反义词: back gate, rear entrance
词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *foregate, equivalent to fore- + gate.  Compare Dutch voorgat (“front hole, foregate”), German Vorgatter (“fore-court”).
