minutenesses:/ˌmaɪˈnjuːtnəsɪz/ | n. | 细微之处；琐碎细节 |  The state or quality of being minute; extreme smallness or insignificance of details. |  He was preoccupied with the minutenesses of the contract. 他专注于合同的细枝末节。 | synonyms: details, niceties, particulars, subtleties, trivialities | antonyms: generalities, wholes, essentials | noun

minuteness:/maɪˈnjuːtnəs/ | n. | 细微；微小；详细 | The quality of being extremely small; fineness of detail.  | The minuteness of the handwriting made it difficult to read. 字迹太小，难以辨认。| synonyms: smallness, tininess, diminutiveness, detail, precision | antonyms: largeness, hugeness, vastness | noun
