merger:/ˈmɜːrdʒər/ (US) /ˈmɜːdʒə(r)/ (UK)| n. |合并，并购|The act or process of combining two or more companies or organizations into one.|  The merger between the two companies created a giant in the industry. 这两家公司的合并造就了该行业的巨头。

The bank announced a merger with a rival. 该银行宣布与竞争对手合并。| 近义词：amalgamation, consolidation, union, integration | 反义词：demerger, separation, divestiture |词性: 名词
