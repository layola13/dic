collar:/ˈkɒlə(r) (UK), ˈkɑːlər (US)| n. v.| 衣领；颈圈；抓捕；控制 |A band of material around the neck of a shirt, dress, coat, etc., or a separate piece of material worn around the neck.  To seize someone by the collar; to apprehend. |例句：The dog's collar was too tight. 狗的项圈太紧了。

例句：The police collared the suspect as he tried to flee. 警察在嫌疑人试图逃跑时将其抓获。|近义词：neckband, lapel (for clothing); apprehend, arrest (for verb) |反义词: release, free (for verb)
词性: noun, verb
