deforesters:/ˌdiːfɔːrˈɛstərz/
noun
滥伐森林者；毁林者
People who clear forests, typically on a large scale, for other uses like agriculture or urban development.  Those who engage in deforestation.
The deforesters are destroying the Amazon rainforest at an alarming rate.  这些滥伐森林者正以惊人的速度破坏着亚马逊雨林。
synonyms: logger, clear-cutter, forest destroyer
antonyms: conservationist, environmentalist, forester
n.
