HIPAA:/ˈhɪpə/| n. |健康保险流通与责任法案 (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act)|A US law designed to provide privacy standards to protect patients' medical records and other health information provided to health plans, doctors, hospitals and other health care providers.|例句：The hospital is compliant with HIPAA regulations.  这家医院遵守HIPAA的规定。|例句：HIPAA requires the protection of patient health information. HIPAA要求保护患者的健康信息。|近义词：patient confidentiality, medical privacy|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑: public disclosure)|词性: noun
