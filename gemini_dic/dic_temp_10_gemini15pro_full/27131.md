calisthenics:/ˌkælɪsˈθɛnɪks/| n. |健美操，徒手体操|Exercises done using only the body weight as resistance.  These exercises are often performed rhythmically and with minimal equipment.|She practices calisthenics every morning to stay fit. 她每天早上练习健美操以保持健康。|Synonyms: bodyweight training, exercises, workout|Antonyms: weight training, inactivity|Noun

calisthenic:/ˌkælɪsˈθɛnɪk/| adj. |健美操的，徒手体操的|Relating to or involving calisthenics.|Calisthenic exercises are a great way to build strength and flexibility. 健美操是增强力量和柔韧性的好方法。|Synonyms: gymnastic, athletic|Antonyms: sedentary, inactive|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *kalos* (beautiful) + *sthenos* (strength).
