allowances:/əˈlaʊənsɪz/
| n. |津贴，补贴；限额；折扣；考虑|A sum of money paid regularly to a person, often to cover specific expenses.|
The company provides a generous housing allowance. 公司提供丰厚的住房补贴。
He receives a small allowance from his parents each week. 他每周收到父母给的一小笔零用钱。
|近义词：payment, stipend, grant, subsidy, ration, allocation|
|反义词：disallowance, prohibition|
|词性: 名词(plural)|


allowances:/əˈlaʊənsɪz/
| n. |允许；承认；体谅|The act of allowing or permitting something; consideration or acknowledgment of a mitigating factor.|
You must make allowances for his age. 你必须考虑到他的年龄。
The judge made allowances for the defendant's difficult childhood. 法官体谅了被告的艰难童年。
|近义词：consideration, concession, tolerance, acceptance|
|反义词：disregard, intolerance|
|词性: 名词(plural)|
