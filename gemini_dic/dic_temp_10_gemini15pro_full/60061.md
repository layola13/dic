edulious:/ɛˈd(j)uːliəs/
adjective
可食用的；无毒的
fit to be eaten; not poisonous.
Example: The berries of this plant are eduliedous. 这种植物的浆果是可以食用的。
Example:  While foraging, it's crucial to distinguish eduliedous mushrooms from poisonous ones. 觅食时，区分可食用蘑菇和有毒蘑菇至关重要。
Synonyms: edible, comestible, safe to eat
Antonyms: inedible, poisonous, toxic
词性: 形容词
