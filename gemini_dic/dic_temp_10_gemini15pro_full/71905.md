flexiblenesses:/ˌflɛksɪˈbɪlɪtɪz/| n. |柔韧性，灵活性|The quality of being flexible; pliancy.  The ability to be easily modified.| Her flexiblenesses in yoga are impressive. 她在瑜伽中展现的柔韧性令人印象深刻。|近义词：pliability, suppleness, adaptability|反义词：rigidity, stiffness|Noun

Etymology:
From flexible + -ness.  Flexible comes from Late Latin flexibilis, from Latin flectere "to bend."
