disinterments:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtɜːrmənts/
n.
掘尸；发掘（尤指从墓地中）；（从墓地中）挖出
The act of digging up something that was buried, especially a body from a grave.
The disinterments were carried out under the cover of darkness.  这些尸体是在夜幕的掩护下被挖出来的。
exhumations, unearthings
burials, interments
noun
