parkleaves:/ˈpɑːrklɪvz/ | n. | 落叶，枯叶 | Fallen leaves, especially in a park or wooded area.  These leaves have often changed color and fallen from the trees in autumn. | The parkleaves crunched underfoot as we walked. 我们走在落叶上发出嘎吱嘎吱的声音。| The wind swirled the parkleaves into colorful piles.  风把落叶卷成五颜六色的堆。 | Synonym: fallen leaves, autumn leaves | Antonym: new leaves, green leaves | noun
