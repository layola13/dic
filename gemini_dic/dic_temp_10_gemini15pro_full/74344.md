fragmenting:/ˈfræɡmɛntɪŋ/ /ˈfræɡməntɪŋ/
verb
分裂，破碎，使成碎片
breaking or causing something to break into small separate parts
The empire began fragmenting into independent states.  这个帝国开始分裂成独立的国家。
The report discusses the fragmenting of society. 这份报告讨论了社会的分裂。
synonyms:  splitting, breaking, dividing, shattering, disintegrating
antonyms: uniting, joining, merging, consolidating
verb
