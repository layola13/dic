PHRASE->
allow for: 考虑到, 顾及
The architect allowed for expansion when designing the building. 建筑师在设计建筑物时考虑到了扩建的可能性。
allow of: 容许
The rules allow of no exceptions. 规则不容许有任何例外。
as allowances go: 就津贴而言
As allowances go, it's not bad. 就津贴而言，这还不错。
allow oneself to be: 让自己被...
She allowed herself to be persuaded. 她让自己被说服了。
allow that: 承认
He allowed that he had made a mistake. 他承认他犯了一个错误。
