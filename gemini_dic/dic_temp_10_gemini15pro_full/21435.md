blowfly:/ˈbloʊflaɪ/ (US), /ˈbləʊflaɪ/ (UK)| n. |绿头苍蝇，丽蝇|A fly, typically metallic blue or green, that lays its eggs in decaying flesh.|例句：Blowflies were buzzing around the carcass.  绿头苍蝇在尸体周围嗡嗡作响。

例句：The smell attracted a swarm of blowflies.  这气味吸引了一大群绿头苍蝇。|近义词：carrion fly, bluebottle, greenbottle|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
