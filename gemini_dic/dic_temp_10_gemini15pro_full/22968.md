bottomry: /ˈbɒtəmri/ (US) /ˈbɒtəmri/ (UK)| n.| 海运抵押借款，船舶抵押|A loan secured by a ship or its cargo, repayable only if the voyage is completed successfully. The loan is forfeited if the ship is lost.| The shipowner took out a bottomry bond to finance the voyage.|近义词：maritime loan, respondentia (when the cargo is the security)|反义词: N/A| noun
