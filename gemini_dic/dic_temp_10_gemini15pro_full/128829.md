nightshirts:/ˈnaɪtʃɜːrts/
n.
睡衣；男式长睡衣
A loose, comfortable shirt-like garment worn in bed.
He wore an old, faded nightshirt. 他穿着一件褪色的旧睡衣。

近义词:nightgown, pajamas, sleepwear
反义词:(No direct opposite, possibly daywear)
词性:名词
