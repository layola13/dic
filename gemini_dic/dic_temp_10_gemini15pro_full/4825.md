alias:/ˈeɪliəs/ | n. |别名，化名|An assumed name. |例句：He used the alias "John Smith" to avoid detection. 他用化名“约翰·史密斯”来躲避侦查。|近义词：pseudonym, sobriquet, nickname|反义词：real name, true name|noun

alias:/ˈeɪliəs/ | vt. |给...起别名|To give an alias to. |例句：The program aliases the command "copy" to "cp". 该程序给命令 "copy"起了个别名 "cp"。|近义词：rename, nickname|反义词： (No direct antonym for the verb form) |verb
