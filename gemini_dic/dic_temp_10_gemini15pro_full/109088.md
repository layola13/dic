legato:/ləˈɡɑːtoʊ/ (US), /lɪˈɡɑːtəʊ/ (UK)
n.|连奏，连音|A smooth, flowing manner of playing or singing, without breaks between notes.|The pianist played the piece legato.|近义词:smooth, connected|反义词:staccato, detached|词性:名词, (音乐) 副词

legatos:/ləˈɡɑːtoʊz/ (US), /lɪˈɡɑːtəʊz/ (UK)
n.|连奏，连音 (复数)|Plural form of legato, referring to multiple instances of smooth, flowing playing or singing.|The composer indicated several legatos in the score.|近义词:smooth passages, connected notes|反义词:staccatos, detached notes|词性:名词

词源: Italian, from past participle of *legare* "to tie, bind," from Latin *ligare*.
