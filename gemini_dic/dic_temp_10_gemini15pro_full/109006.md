leftie: /ˈlɛfti/ (US), /ˈlɛfti/ (UK)| n. adj.|左撇子；左派；|A person who uses their left hand more skillfully than their right, especially for writing.  Also, someone with left-wing political views. |例句：He's a leftie and plays a great game of left-handed baseball.  他是个左撇子，左手棒球打得很好。

例句：She’s considered a leftie because of her socialist views. 她因为她的社会主义观点被认为是左派。|近义词：southpaw (n.), left-hander (n.), left-winger (n. political)|反义词：rightie (n. informal), right-hander (n.), right-winger (n. political)|词性: 名词, 形容词
