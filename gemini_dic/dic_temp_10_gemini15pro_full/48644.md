defense:/dɪˈfɛns/ /ˈdiːfɛns/ | n. | 防御，防护；辩护；答辩；防御工事 |  The act of protecting something from attack or harm. |  The castle's defense was strong. 城堡的防御很坚固。 | synonyms: protection, safeguard, resistance | antonyms: attack, offense, vulnerability | noun

defense:/dɪˈfɛns/ /ˈdiːfɛns/ | v. | 防守，保卫；为…辩护 | To protect from attack or harm; to act or speak in support of. |  He defended his friend against the bullies. 他保护他的朋友免受恶霸的欺负。 | synonyms: protect, guard, advocate | antonyms: attack, prosecute, abandon | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *defensse*, from Latin dēfēnsa (“protection, defense”), from dēfēnsus, past participle of dēfendere (“to ward off, defend”). Equivalent to de- + -fendere (from fendere, “to strike, thrust”).
