gonozooid:/ˌɡɒnəˈzoʊɔɪd/ | n. |生殖个员，生殖体|A member of a hydroid colony that produces reproductive cells, either male or female.  It is a specialized polyp that does not feed but instead produces medusae or gametes.| Example 1: The gonozooid releases medusae that swim freely and reproduce sexually. 生殖体释放出自由游动的水母，进行有性繁殖。

Example 2: The colony of hydroids consisted of feeding polyps and gonozooids.  水螅群体由摄食水螅和生殖体组成。| 近义词: reproductive polyp,  | 反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially feeding polyp depending on context) | noun
