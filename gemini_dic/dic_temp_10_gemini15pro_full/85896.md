headforemost:/ˌhedˈfɔːrmoʊst/| adv.|头朝前地，轻率地|head first; with the head in front.  Implies a headlong rush or plunge into something. |He jumped into the pool headforemost. 他头朝前跳进了游泳池。|近义词：headfirst, headlong, precipitately, rashly|反义词: cautiously, carefully|词性:副词

Etymology:
From "head" + "foremost" (from "fore" meaning "front" and "most" meaning "most").  Literally, "with the head at the frontmost position."
