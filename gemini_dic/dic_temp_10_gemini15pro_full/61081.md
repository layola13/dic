ellipticalnesses:/ɪˌlɪptɪkəlˈnɛsɪz/| n.|椭圆度，椭圆性|The degree to which something is elliptical.  It refers to the deviation of a shape from perfect circularity or sphericity.|The ellipticalnesses of the planets' orbits vary. 行星轨道的椭圆度各不相同。|Synonyms: ellipticity, eccentricity|Antonyms: circularity, sphericity|noun
