bibasic:/baɪˈbeɪsɪk/
adjective
二元的; 二碱的
Having two hydrogen atoms replaceable by basic atoms or radicals.  A bibasic acid is an acid that has two acidic hydrogen atoms in its molecule. These hydrogens can react with bases to form salts.
This bibasic acid can neutralize two molecules of a base.  这种二元酸可以中和两个分子的碱。
Synonyms: dibasic, diprotic
Antonyms: monobasic
词性: 形容词
