duller:/ˈdʌlər/ | adj. | 更迟钝的，更无趣的 | comparative form of dull: more dull; lacking interest or excitement. | The movie was duller than I expected. 这部电影比我想象的更无聊。|  The second half of the game was much duller than the first.  比赛的下半场比上半场更沉闷。| 近义词: more boring, more tedious, more monotonous | 反义词: more interesting, more exciting, more stimulating | 词性: 形容词
词源: From Middle English *duller, comparative of dul, from Old English dol.
