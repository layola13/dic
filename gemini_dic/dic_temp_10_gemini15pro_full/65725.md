eventually:/ɪˈventʃuəli/ /ɪˈvɛntʃʊəli/| adv.|最终，最后|After much deliberation, they eventually decided to go with the original plan. 经过深思熟虑后，他们最终决定采用最初的计划。|例句：Eventually, she achieved her goal of becoming a doctor. 最终，她实现了成为一名医生的目标。|近义词：finally, ultimately, in the end, at last|反义词：initially, originally, at first|词性:副词

词源: From Latin *eventualis, from eventus (“event, outcome”).
