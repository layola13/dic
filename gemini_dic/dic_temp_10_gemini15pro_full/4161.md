airbrush:/ˈɛərbrʌʃ/ (US), /ˈeəbrʌʃ/ (UK)| n. vt.|喷枪，气笔；用喷枪喷涂|A device that sprays paint by means of compressed air.  The artist airbrushed the model's face to perfection.|例句：He uses an airbrush to create realistic portraits. 他用喷枪创作逼真的肖像。

例句：She airbrushed the blemishes from the photograph. 她用喷枪修饰了照片上的瑕疵。|近义词：spray gun, atomizer|反义词:brush|词性:名词, 动词
