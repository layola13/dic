cataleptically: /ˌkætəˈlɛptɪkli/
| adv. |僵硬地，像患僵直症一样地 |In a manner resembling catalepsy; rigidly and motionlessly. |He stood cataleptically, staring straight ahead. 他僵硬地站着，直直地盯着前方。| The wax figure stood cataleptically in the museum. 蜡像僵硬地矗立在博物馆里。 | rigidly, stiffly, fixedly, motionlessly | flexibly, fluidly | adverb

catalepsy: /ˈkætəˌlɛpsi/
| n. |僵直症 |A medical condition characterized by a trancelike state with loss of voluntary motion and failure to react to stimuli. |Catalepsy is a rare neurological disorder. 僵直症是一种罕见的神经系统疾病。|  She suffered from catalepsy after the traumatic event.  她在创伤性事件后患上了僵直症。| rigidity, trance, immobility | flexibility, mobility | noun
