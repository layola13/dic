orthometric:/ˌɔːrθəˈmɛtrɪk/
adjective
正高的；正射投影的
Relating to or determined by orthometric height.  Pertaining to the height above the geoid.
The orthometric height of a point is the distance along a plumb line from that point to the geoid.
正射投影高度是指一个点沿铅垂线到大地水准面的距离。

The map uses an orthometric projection.
这张地图使用正射投影。

synonym:orthogonal, perpendicular
antonym: inclined, oblique
