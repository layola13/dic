abit:/əˈbɪt/ | adv. | 一点儿，稍微 | Abit is an informal way of saying "a bit". It means a small amount or degree of something. |  "I'm abit tired today." 我今天有点累。

"The music is abit loud."  音乐有点吵。 | 近义词: a bit, a little, slightly | 反义词: a lot, very, much | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "a bit," with the "a" and "bit" sometimes merged in pronunciation and spelling.  "Bit" ultimately comes from Old English "bita" meaning "piece of food, morsel."
