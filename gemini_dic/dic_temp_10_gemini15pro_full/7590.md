announcements:/əˈnaʊnsmənts/
n.
公告，通告；宣布，宣告
a formal public statement about a fact, plan, or intention
The president made an important announcement about the new policy. 总统就新政策发表了重要声明。

近义词：declaration, proclamation, notification, communiqué
反义词：concealment, suppression

词性: 名词(plural)

词源:  From Old French *anoncier (“to announce”), from Latin annuntiare (“to report, announce”), from ad- (“to”) + nuntiare (“to announce”)
