force-out:/ˈfɔːrs aʊt/| n.|强制出局，逼退|A play in baseball in which a runner is retired because he is forced to advance to a base already occupied by another runner.|The shortstop fielded the grounder and threw to second for the force-out.|近义词：putout, retirement|反义词: safe|词性:noun

force-out:/fɔːrs ˈaʊt/| v.|强制出局，逼退|To cause a runner to be retired in baseball by forcing him to advance to a base already occupied by another runner.|The pitcher forced out the runner at second.|近义词：retire, put out|反义词: allow to advance|词性:verb
