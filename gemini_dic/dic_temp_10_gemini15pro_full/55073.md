disrelate:/dɪsrɪˈleɪt/
verb
使…不相关；取消关联
to detach or separate (something) from something else : to deprive of connection or relation
Example: The study disrelates the two phenomena.  这项研究使这两种现象不相关。

Example:  The new evidence seems to disrelate the suspect from the crime. 新的证据似乎排除了嫌疑人与犯罪的关联。
近义词: disconnect, dissociate, separate, detach
反义词: relate, connect, associate
词性: verb
