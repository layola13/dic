nonviscidnesses:/ˌnɒnˈvɪskɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
无粘性，非粘稠性
The nonviscidness of the liquid allowed it to flow easily through the narrow pipe.  这种液体的无粘性使它很容易流过狭窄的管道。
The nonviscidness of the air is a key factor in aerodynamics. 空气的无粘性是空气动力学中的一个关键因素。
Near synonyms: fluidity, slipperiness, lubricity
Antonyms: viscosity, stickiness, adhesiveness


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + viscid + -ness.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not." Viscid comes from the Latin "viscidus," meaning "sticky" or "glutinous."  -ness is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality.
