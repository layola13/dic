logistically:/ləˈdʒɪstɪkli/
adverb|逻辑上地；后勤地|relating to logistics; in terms of logistics.  |The project is logistically challenging due to its remote location.  由于项目地处偏远，所以在后勤方面很有挑战性。|Synonyms: practically, operationally|Antonyms: impractically, unrealistically|词性: 副词

logistically:/ləˈdʒɪstɪkli/
adjective|后勤的|relating to logistics. |The army faces logistical problems in supplying its troops. 军队在向其部队提供补给方面面临后勤问题。|Synonyms: organizational, operational|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could use words like "impractical" in certain contexts)|词性: 形容词
