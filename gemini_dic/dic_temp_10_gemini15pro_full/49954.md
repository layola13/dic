deng:/dəŋ/
deng:| n. | 灯笼; 灯 | A traditional Chinese lamp, usually made of paper or silk and often hung during festivals. |  Example: The streets were decorated with colorful deng during the Lantern Festival.  街道上在元宵节期间装饰着五彩缤纷的灯笼。 | Synonym: lantern | Antonym: darkness
deng:| n. | (打更用的) 锣, 梆子 | A metal gong or wooden clapper used to announce the time in ancient China. | Example: The watchman struck the deng to signal the start of the night watch.  更夫敲响了梆子，标志着夜巡的开始。 | Synonym: gong, clapper | Antonym: silence
