areola:/əˈriːələ/ (US), /əˈriːələ/ (UK)| n. |乳晕；小孔；（某些植物的）气孔|A small circular area, especially the dark-pigmented area surrounding a nipple, or a small cavity or pit in a surface.|The doctor examined the areola for any unusual changes. 医生检查了乳晕是否有任何异常变化。|The cactus has areolas from which spines grow. 仙人掌有气孔，从中长出刺。|Synonyms: disc, ring, halo (for nipple areola); pore, pit (for other meanings)|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, depends on specific context)|Noun
