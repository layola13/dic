epanody:/ɪˈpænədi/ | n. | 回环反复，反复出现 | A rhetorical device in which a thought is repeated in a reversed or changed form. | The politician used an epanody, stating first that the people deserved better leadership and then that better leadership deserved the people.  这位政治家运用了一种回环反复的修辞手法，首先声明人民应该得到更好的领导，然后又说更好的领导应该得到人民的支持。|

epanody:/ɪˈpænədi/ | n. | 回环反复，反复出现 | Repetition of a word or phrase with a variation.  |  "Whether he is right or not, only time will tell; only time will tell if he is right or not." This sentence is an example of epanody. “无论他是对是错，只有时间能证明；只有时间能证明他是否正确。”这句话是回环反复的例子。 |  repetition, anadiplosis, epistrophe | antonym: N/A | noun
