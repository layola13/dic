misliver:/mɪsˈlɪvər/
verb
投递错误；误送
to deliver (something, such as a letter) to a wrong address or recipient
The postman mislivered our package to the neighbor's house. 邮递员把我们的包裹错投到邻居家了。
misdeliver, misdirect
deliver correctly
