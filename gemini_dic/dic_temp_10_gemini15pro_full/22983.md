bouch:/buːʃ/| n.|嘴，口；（尤指动物的）吻部；河口，瓶口|An opening; a mouth, especially of an animal or a river.  A river's bouch is its mouth or outlet into the sea or a lake.  In older usage, it can also refer to the mouth of a bottle or other container.|The river's bouch was wide and muddy.|近义词：mouth, opening, orifice, aperture|反义词:closure|词性:noun
