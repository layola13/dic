hoove:/huːv/| n. v.|蹄；（尤指马的）蹄铁；缓慢移动|A hoof is the hard part of the foot of a horse, cow, or similar animal.|The horse's hooves clattered on the cobblestones. 马的蹄子在鹅卵石路上发出咔哒咔哒的响声。|The farmer had to trim the cow's hooves. 农民不得不修剪牛的蹄子。|近义词：foot, shoe (for horses)|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)
词性:名词
hoove:/huːv/| v.|（使）缓慢移动|To move slowly or heavily.|The old man hooved down the street. 老人缓慢地走在大街上。|The cart hooved along the muddy road.  马车在泥泞的道路上缓慢移动。|近义词：lumber, plod|反义词:rush, sprint
词性:动词
