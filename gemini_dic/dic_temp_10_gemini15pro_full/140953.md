one-sidedness:/ˌwʌnˈsaɪdɪdnəs/| n. |片面性；单方面；不公正|The one-sidedness of the report led to much criticism.  这份报告的片面性招致了很多批评。|The agreement's one-sidedness benefited the company more than the workers.  这份协议的片面性使公司比工人受益更多。|近义词：bias, partiality, unfairness|反义词: impartiality, fairness, objectivity|词性:名词
Etymology:  From one-sided + -ness. One-sided is formed from one + sided. "Sided" comes from the Old English "sīde" meaning "side."  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives and indicates a state, quality, or condition.
