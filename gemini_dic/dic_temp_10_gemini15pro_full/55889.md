divisor:/dɪˈvaɪzər/| n. |除数，约数|A number that divides another number exactly.|例句：12 is a divisor of 36.  12是36的约数。|例句：The divisors of 10 are 1, 2, 5, and 10.  10的约数是1，2，5和10。|近义词：factor|反义词:multiple|词性:noun
