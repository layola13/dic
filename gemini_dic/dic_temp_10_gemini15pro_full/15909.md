ballroom: /ˈbɔːlruːm/ (US) /ˈbɔːlrʊm/ (UK)| n. |舞厅，宴会厅|A large room for formal dancing or other social events.|The hotel ballroom was decorated beautifully for the wedding. 酒店的宴会厅为婚礼装饰得非常漂亮。|例句：The couple met at a ballroom dance. 这对夫妇是在一次舞会上认识的。

例句：The ballroom was filled with elegantly dressed guests. 宴会厅里挤满了衣着优雅的客人。

|近义词：dance hall, function room|反义词: None|词性: noun

etymology:  From ball (n.) + room (n.).  Originally referred to a room specifically designed for playing ball games, but later evolved to mean a large room for dancing and social events.
