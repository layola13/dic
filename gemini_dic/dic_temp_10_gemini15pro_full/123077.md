moneylenders:/ˈmʌniˌlɛndərz/
n.|放债人；贷款人|A person or organization that lends money, typically at high rates of interest.|Many people struggling financially turn to moneylenders as a last resort. 许多经济困难的人会把求助于放债人作为最后一招。|
|synonyms: loan shark, creditor, financier|antonyms: borrower, debtor|noun|

ETYMOLOGY->
From "money" + "lender,"  "money" from Old French "moneie," ultimately from Latin "moneta" (mint, coinage); "lender" from "lend" + "-er," "lend" from Old English "lænan" (to lend).
