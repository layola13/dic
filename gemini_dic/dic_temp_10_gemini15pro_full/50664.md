derivative:/dɪˈrɪvətɪv/ | n. adj.| 衍生物，派生物；衍生的 |Something that is based on another source.  A derivative is something which has been developed or obtained from something else.| The TV series is a derivative of the original book. 这部电视剧是由原著改编的。 |synonyms: by-product, descendant, offspring | antonyms: original, source, primary | noun

derivative:/dɪˈrɪvətɪv/ | adj.| 衍生的，派生的 |Made up of elements that have been borrowed from or developed from other sources.|  His painting style is derivative of impressionism. 他的绘画风格源于印象派。| synonyms: derived, unoriginal, secondary | antonyms: original, innovative, primary | adjective
