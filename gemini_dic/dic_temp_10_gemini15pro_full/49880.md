demster:/ˈdɛmstər/ | n. |  裁判员；仲裁人 | A judge; an umpire; an arbiter.  One who held courts of judgment. |  The demster delivered his verdict.  裁判员作出了裁决。 | 近义词: judge, arbiter, umpire, referee | 反义词: defendant, accused | 词性: 名词

词源: From Scottish Gaelic *dempstare*, from Old Norse *dómsþórr*, meaning "judge-Thor,"  composed of *dómr* ("judgment") and *þórr* ("Thor"). Thor was associated with law and justice in Norse mythology.
