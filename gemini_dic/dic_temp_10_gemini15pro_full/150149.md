pan-fried:/ˈpænˌfraɪd/| adj.|煎的；平底锅煎的|Cooked in a frying pan with a small amount of fat.|例句：I had some delicious pan-fried dumplings for lunch. 我午餐吃了一些美味的煎饺。

例句：He prefers pan-fried fish to deep-fried. 他喜欢煎鱼胜过炸鱼。|近义词：sauteed, shallow-fried|反义词：deep-fried|词性:形容词
