lie-ins:/ˈlaɪ ɪnz/
n.
赖床，睡懒觉
A period of remaining in bed later than usual, especially in the morning.
例句：I love having lie-ins on Sundays.  我星期天喜欢睡懒觉。

例句：Lie-ins are a rare luxury when you have young children.  有小孩之后，睡懒觉是一种难得的奢侈。

近义词：sleeping in, staying in bed
反义词：getting up early, early rising
词性：名词
