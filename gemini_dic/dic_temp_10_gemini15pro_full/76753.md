gamekeeping:/ˈɡeɪmˌkiːpɪŋ/
n.
狩猎管理，猎场看守
the activity of managing land in order to breed and protect game animals for hunting
例句：Gamekeeping involves controlling predators and ensuring a healthy habitat for the game. 狩猎管理包括控制捕食者并确保猎物拥有健康的栖息地。

例句：He took a course in gamekeeping and now manages a large estate. 他参加了狩猎管理课程，现在管理着一个大型庄园。
近义词：wildlife management, hunting management
反义词：poaching
词性：名词
