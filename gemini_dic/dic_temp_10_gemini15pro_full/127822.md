nematocalyx:/ˌnɛmətəˈkeɪlɪks/| n.|线状花萼|A calyx (the outer part of a flower) composed of thread-like segments.|The nematocalyx of this rare orchid is its most distinctive feature.  这种稀有兰花的线状花萼是它最显著的特征。|近义词:calyx|反义词:无|词性:名词

词源:  From Greek *nēma*, *nēmat-*  "thread" + *kalyx* "cup of a flower, seed pod."
