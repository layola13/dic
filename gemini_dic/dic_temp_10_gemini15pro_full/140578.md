oligomerous: /ˌɒlɪˈɡɒmərəs/ | adj. | 少聚的，寡聚的 |  Having a small and definite number of parts, especially of floral parts (petals, sepals, stamens etc.).  Referring to a flower with fewer than the typical number of parts for its family or other grouping. | The oligomerous flower had only four petals, unlike its relatives with five. 这朵少聚的花只有四片花瓣，不像它的同类有五片。| The botanist identified the plant by its oligomerous inflorescence.  植物学家通过其少聚的花序识别了这种植物。 |  merous, few-parted | polymerous | adjective
