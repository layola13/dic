encoding:/ɪnˈkoʊdɪŋ/ (US), /ɪnˈkəʊdɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |编码，编码方式| The process of converting information from one form to another.  This can refer to character encodings (like UTF-8) or more generally to transforming data for storage or transmission.|  The website uses UTF-8 encoding.  这个网站使用UTF-8编码。| synonym: coding, cipher | antonym: decoding | noun

encodings:/ɪnˈkoʊdɪŋz/ (US), /ɪnˈkəʊdɪŋz/ (UK)| n. |编码方式（复数）| Plural form of "encoding," referring to multiple ways of converting information. | Different encodings are used for different languages. 不同的语言使用不同的编码方式。 | synonym: codes, ciphers | antonym: decodings | noun
