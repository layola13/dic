librettists: /lɪˈbrɛtɪsts/ | n. | 歌剧脚本作者，歌词作者 |  A librettist is a person who writes the text of an opera or other long vocal work, such as an oratorio or musical.  This text is often referred to as the libretto. | The librettists collaborated closely with the composer.  歌剧脚本作者与作曲家密切合作。

The librettists for this musical are incredibly talented. 这部音乐剧的歌词作者才华横溢。


| 近义词:  lyrics writer,  book writer (for musicals) | 反义词: composer (while not a direct opposite, it represents a contrasting role) | 词性: noun
