nontannin:/nɒnˈtænɪn/ | adj. | 非单宁的；非鞣质的 | Not containing tannin; not related to or derived from tannin. |  Leather made with nontannin agents is softer.  用非鞣质制剂制成的皮革更柔软。 | synonyms: tannin-free | antonyms: tannic | adjective


nontannins:/nɒnˈtænɪnz/ | n. | 非单宁 | Substances in plant tissues other than tannins, especially those extracted with tannins in making leather. | The nontannins in tea contribute to its flavor. 茶中的非单宁物质有助于其风味。 | synonyms: non-tannic substances | antonyms: tannins | noun
