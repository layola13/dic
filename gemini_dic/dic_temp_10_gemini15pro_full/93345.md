illatively:/ɪˈleɪtɪvli/
adv. |推论地，通过推理地|By way of inference or deduction; logically. |He argued illatively, drawing his conclusions step by step from the evidence. 他通过推理进行论证，从证据中逐步得出结论。|例句：The detective illatively connected the clues to identify the culprit. 侦探通过推理将线索联系起来，确定了罪犯。|近义词：logically, consequently, deductively, rationally|反义词：intuitively, empirically, randomly|词性:副词

词源: From Late Latin *illātīvus*, from Latin *illātus*, past participle of *inferre* ("to bring in, introduce, infer").
