duets:/duːˈets/
n.
二重唱，二重奏
a performance of a musical piece by two singers or instrumentalists.
The album features several duets with famous musicians. 这张专辑收录了几首与著名音乐家的二重唱。

n.
双人舞
a dance performed by two people.
The ballet included a beautiful duet performed by the principal dancers. 芭蕾舞剧中包含一段由主要舞蹈演员表演的优美的双人舞。

近义词:duettos, ensembles
反义词:solos
词性:名词
