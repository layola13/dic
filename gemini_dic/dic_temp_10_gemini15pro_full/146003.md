overdone:/ˌoʊvərˈdʌn/| adj.| 过度的，过分的；做得过火的；煮得太久的| Cooked or prepared for too long.  Excessively elaborate or dramatic. | The steak was overdone and dry. 牛排煮得太老了，很干。|近义词：overcooked, exaggerated, excessive, overblown |反义词: underdone, understated, subtle|词性:形容词
overdo:/ˌoʊvərˈduː/| v.|  做过头，过度；过分| To do something to excess; exaggerate. | Don't overdo the exercise, you might hurt yourself.  不要过度运动，你可能会伤到自己。|近义词：exaggerate, overact|反义词: underdo|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *overdon, from Old English *oferdon, past participle of oferdon (“to overdo”), equivalent to over +‎ do.
