misqualify: /ˌmɪsˈkwɒlɪfaɪ/ (US) /ˌmɪsˈkwɒlɪfaɪ/ (UK)| v. |使…丧失资格，取消…资格|To deprive someone of the qualifications or eligibility for something.|He was misqualified from the competition because he had accepted sponsorship from a banned company. 他因为接受了被禁公司的赞助而被取消了比赛资格。|The athlete was misqualified for using performance-enhancing drugs.  这名运动员因使用兴奋剂而被取消了参赛资格。|近义词：disqualify, bar, exclude|反义词：qualify, admit, include|词性:动词
