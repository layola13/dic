noneditorially:/ˌnɒnˌedɪˈtɔːriəli/ | adv. | 非编辑地；非评论地 | In a way that does not involve the opinions or judgments of an editor or commentator.  It refers to actions or content presented factually, without interpretation or bias. | The information was presented noneditorially, simply stating the facts of the case.  这些信息是非编辑性地呈现的，只是简单地陈述了案件的事实。| The report listed the candidates' names noneditorially, in alphabetical order. 报告中按字母顺序非编辑地列出了候选人的姓名。 | neutrally, objectively, impartially | editorially, subjectively | adverb
