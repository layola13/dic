in-good-part:/ɪn ɡʊd pɑːrt/ | adv. | 在很大程度上；部分地；相当程度上 | Largely; partly; to a considerable extent. in-good-part:/ɪn ɡʊd pɑːrt/ | adj. |  (This usage is less common and somewhat archaic) Kind; well-intentioned. | Having a kind or benevolent disposition. |  His in-good-part nature made him popular. (Archaic usage) 他善良的本性使他很受欢迎。 | Synonyms: kind, benevolent, well-meaning | Antonyms: unkind, malevolent, ill-intentioned | adjective
