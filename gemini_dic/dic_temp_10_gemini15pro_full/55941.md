dizzily:/ˈdɪzɪli/ /ˈdɪzəli/| adv.|头晕目眩地，茫然地|In a dizzying or confused manner.  Feeling lightheaded or unsteady.|She spun around dizzily until she collapsed on the floor. 她头晕目眩地旋转，直到倒在地板上。|Synonyms: giddily, lightheadedly, unsteadily|Antonyms: steadily, firmly, clearly|词性: 副词

dizzy:/ˈdɪzi/ /ˈdɪzi/| adj.|头晕目眩的，茫然的|Having or involving a sensation of spinning around and losing one's balance.|The heights made him feel dizzy. 高度让他感到头晕。|Synonyms: giddy, lightheaded, faint|Antonyms: steady, stable, clearheaded|词性: 形容词
