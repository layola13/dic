bucky:/ˈbʌki/| n.|巴基，雄鹿，美元|A nickname, often short for "Buckminster."  Can also refer to a dollar or a male deer (informal).|例句：Hey Bucky, wanna grab some lunch? 嘿，巴基，想去吃午饭吗？
