confederacies: /kənˈfɛdərəsiz/
|n.|
|联盟，邦联|
|A union of states or groups, especially of political organizations; a league.|

|The Southern Confederacy fought against the Union during the American Civil War.|
|南方邦联在美国内战期间与联邦作战。|

|The Iroquois Confederacy was a powerful alliance of Native American tribes.|
|易洛魁联盟是美洲原住民部落的强大联盟。|

|Synonym:|alliance, league, federation, coalition, union
|Antonym:|division, separation, disunity

词性: noun (plural)

词源: From Late Latin *confœderatio*, from Latin confoederare "to unite in a league", from com- "together" + foederare "to make a treaty", from foedus "treaty".
