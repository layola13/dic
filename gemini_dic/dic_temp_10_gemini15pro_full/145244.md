overblames:/ˌoʊvərˈbleɪmz/| v.|过分责备，过度指责|To blame someone excessively or unfairly for something.|She overblames herself for the accident, even though it wasn't entirely her fault. 她过分责备自己在事故中的责任，即使那并非完全是她的错。|He overblames his employees for minor mistakes. 他因为员工的小错误而过度指责他们。|近义词：overcriticize, overcondemn|反义词: excuse, forgive|词性:动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "blame" (verb meaning "to assign responsibility for a fault or wrong").  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "blame," indicating an excessive or disproportionate assignment of responsibility.
