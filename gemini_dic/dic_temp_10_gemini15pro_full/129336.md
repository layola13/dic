noisette:/nwɑːˈzɛt/ /nɔɪˈzɛt/| n. |榛子；榛子色；小块里脊肉|A small round piece of meat, especially lamb, or a hazelnut.  It comes from the French word for hazelnut, referring to its small, round shape.|例句：The recipe called for a noisette of lamb.  食谱上需要一小块羊里脊肉。

例句：She chose a dress in a beautiful noisette color. 她选了一件漂亮的榛子色连衣裙。|近义词：hazelnut, filbert (for the nut); medallion (for the meat)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

noisette:/nwɑːˈzɛt/ /nɔɪˈzɛt/| adj. |榛子色的|Having the color of a hazelnut; light brown.|例句：The walls were painted a warm noisette. 墙壁被漆成了温暖的榛子色。

例句：Her noisette hair shimmered in the sunlight. 她的榛子色头发在阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词：hazel, light brown, beige|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective
