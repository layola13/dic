bourgeoisified:/ˌbʊərʒwɑːzɪfaɪd/
|adj.|中产阶级化的，变得庸俗的|Having adopted the values and lifestyle of the middle class, often implying a loss of former radicalism or artistic integrity; rendered conventional or materialistic.|The artist's work became bourgeoisified after he achieved commercial success.  这位艺术家在获得商业成功后，作品变得庸俗了。|The once rebellious writer's style had become noticeably bourgeoisified over the years.  这位曾经叛逆的作家的风格多年来变得明显的中产阶级化了。|Synonyms: conventionalized, gentrified, materialized|Antonyms: radicalized, bohemian, avant-garde|adjective
