deject:/dɪˈdʒɛkt/
verb
使沮丧，使灰心
To make somebody sad and lose their enthusiasm.
The news of her friend's illness dejected her.  她朋友生病的消息让她很沮丧。
He was dejected by his failure to get the job. 他因为没有得到那份工作而感到沮丧。
synonyms:discourage, dispirit, demoralize, depress
antonyms:encourage, hearten, cheer
词性:v.


dejected:/dɪˈdʒɛktɪd/
adjective
沮丧的，灰心的
Sad and without enthusiasm.
She looked dejected when she heard the news. 听到这个消息后，她看起来很沮丧。
The dejected players trudged off the field. 沮丧的球员们沉重地走下赛场。
synonyms:sad, depressed, downcast, disheartened
antonyms:happy, cheerful, elated
词性:adj.
