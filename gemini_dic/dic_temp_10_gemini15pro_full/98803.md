integumentary:/ɪnˌtɛɡjʊˈmɛntəri/
adjective
外皮的；覆盖体组成的
Relating to or composed of an integument, which is a natural outer covering of an animal or plant body, such as skin, scales, or rind.
The integumentary system protects the body from external damage.  外皮系统保护身体免受外部伤害。
The course covered the various components of the integumentary system, including skin, hair, and nails.  这门课程涵盖了外皮系统的各个组成部分，包括皮肤、头发和指甲。
Synonyms: dermal, cutaneous
Antonyms: internal, visceral
