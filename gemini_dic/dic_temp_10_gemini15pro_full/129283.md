nodular:/ˈnɒdjʊlə(r)/ (US) /ˈnɑːdʒələr/| adj.|结节状的；有结节的|Having small lumps or knots.|The doctor discovered a nodular growth on the patient's thyroid. 医生在病人的甲状腺上发现了一个结节状的增生物。|Synonyms: lumpy, bumpy, knobby|Antonyms: smooth, even|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *nodularis, from Latin nodulus ("little knot"), diminutive of nodus ("knot").
