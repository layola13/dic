carved:/kɑːrvd/ | adj. | 雕刻的；雕花的 |  Shaped or decorated by cutting, especially wood or stone. | The table was carved from a single piece of oak. 这张桌子是用一块橡木雕刻而成的。| The museum displayed intricately carved ivory figurines. 博物馆展出了雕刻精美的象牙雕像。 |  sculpted, engraved, etched | smooth, plain | adjective
