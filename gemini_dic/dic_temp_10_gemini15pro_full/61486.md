embrocate:/ˈɛmbrəkeɪt/ /ɪmˈbroʊkeɪt/| v.|涂抹；揉擦(尤指用药)|To rub (a part of the body) with a medicinal liquid or lotion.|She embrocated her sore knee with liniment. 她用药膏涂抹她疼痛的膝盖。|近义词：rub, massage, anoint|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑：abstain from treatment)|词性: verb

embrocation:/ˌɛmbrəˈkeɪʃən/ /ɪmˌbroʊˈkeɪʃən/| n.|涂抹；揉擦；涂擦剂|The act of rubbing a medicinal liquid or lotion into a part of the body; a medicinal liquid or lotion for rubbing into the body.|The doctor prescribed an embrocation for her aching muscles. 医生给她开了涂抹酸痛肌肉的药。|近义词：liniment, ointment, lotion|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|词性: noun
