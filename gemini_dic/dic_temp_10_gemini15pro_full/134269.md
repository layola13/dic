noninfluential:/ˌnɒnɪnfluˈenʃl/ | adj. | 无影响的；不重要的 | Having no influence; not important. |  His role in the decision-making process was noninfluential. 他在决策过程中的作用无关紧要。| The noninfluential committee was eventually disbanded.  这个没有影响力的委员会最终被解散了。| 近义词：insignificant, unimportant, powerless | 反义词：influential, powerful, important | 词性: adjective

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + influential (adjective meaning "having influence")
