hypernaturalness:/ˌhaɪpərˈnætʃrəlnəs/| n. | 超自然性，超自然状态 |A state of being both natural and artificial, often exceeding naturalness in appearance or effect.  It refers to something that appears incredibly natural, yet is the product of artificial design or manipulation, often to an exaggerated degree. | The hypernaturalness of the computer-generated imagery in the movie was both impressive and slightly unsettling.  这部电影中电脑生成图像的超自然性既令人印象深刻，又略令人不安。| synonyms: artificial naturalness, exaggerated naturalism, simulated authenticity | antonyms:  authenticity, genuineness, naturalness | noun
