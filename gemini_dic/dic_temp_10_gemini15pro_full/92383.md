hypocrisy:/hɪˈpɑːkrəsi/ | n. | 伪善，虚伪 |  The practice of claiming to have moral standards or beliefs to which one's own behavior does not conform; pretense. | He condemned the hypocrisy of those who preached against greed but were themselves greedy. 他谴责了那些宣扬反对贪婪但却贪得无厌的人的虚伪。 | The politician's hypocrisy was evident in his public pronouncements versus his private actions. 这位政客的虚伪体现在他的公开声明与他的私人行为的对比上。 | synonyms: insincerity, duplicity, pretense, sanctimony | antonyms: sincerity, honesty, genuineness | noun
