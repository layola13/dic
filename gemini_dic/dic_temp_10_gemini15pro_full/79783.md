gluttingly:/ˈɡlʌtɪŋli/
adverb|过度地，贪婪地|in a way that suggests excessive indulgence or greed. |He ate gluttingly, barely pausing for breath. 他狼吞虎咽地吃着，几乎没有停下来喘口气。|Synonyms: excessively, greedily, voraciously|Antonyms: moderately, sparingly, temperately|词性: 副词

gluttingly:/ˈɡlʌtɪŋli/
Etymology:  From glut + -ing + -ly.  "Glut" comes from the Old French "glotir" meaning to swallow or devour.
词源: 来自glut + -ing + -ly。“Glut”来自古法语“glotir”，意思是吞咽或吞噬。
