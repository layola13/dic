inshaded:/ɪnˈʃeɪdɪd/
adjective
阴影的，阴暗的
covered or darkened by shade.
The inshaded area of the garden was cool and pleasant. 花园里阴凉的地方凉爽宜人。
近义词:shadowy, shaded, dim, dark
反义词:sunny, bright, light, illuminated
词性:adjective
