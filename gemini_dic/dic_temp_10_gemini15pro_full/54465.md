disintegration's:/dɪsˌɪntɪˈɡreɪʃənz/
noun
分解，瓦解，崩溃
The process of coming to pieces.  The disintegration's effects were felt throughout the organization.  这个瓦解的影响波及了整个组织。
例句：The disintegration's of the Roman Empire took centuries. 罗马帝国的瓦解历经数个世纪。

例句：The disintegration's of the rock was caused by weathering. 岩石的瓦解是由风化作用造成的。

近义词：decay, decomposition, breakdown, collapse, crumbling
反义词：integration, unification, consolidation, fusion

词性: 名词 (Noun), 所有格形式

词源:  From disintegrate + -ion (suffix forming nouns of action or condition) + -'s (possessive suffix).  Disintegrate comes from dis- (prefix meaning "apart, reversal") + integrate (from Latin integratus, past participle of integrare "to make whole").
