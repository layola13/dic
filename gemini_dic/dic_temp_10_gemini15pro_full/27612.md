cancellers:/ˈkænsələrz/
noun
取消者，抵制者
A person who withdraws support for (especially a public figure) after they have done or said something considered objectionable or offensive.
Many cancellers on social media were quick to criticize the celebrity's insensitive remarks. 社交媒体上的许多抵制者迅速批评了这位名人麻木不仁的言论。
critics, detractors, opponents
supporters, fans
n.
