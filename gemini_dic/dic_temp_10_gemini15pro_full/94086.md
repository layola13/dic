impatible: /ɪmˈpætɪbl/
adj.
不兼容的，不相容的，合不来的
unable to exist or work together without conflict
例句：The new software is impatible with older operating systems.  这个新软件与旧的操作系统不兼容。

例句：Their personalities are impatible, which leads to frequent arguments. 他们的性格不合，导致经常争吵。

近义词：incompatible, conflicting, discordant, antagonistic
反义词：compatible, harmonious, consonant
词性：形容词

词源：
"Impatible" is an older form of "incompatible," deriving from the Latin "in-" (not) and "compatibilis" (compatible). "Compatibilis" itself comes from "com-" (together) and "pati" (to suffer or endure).  The shift from "in-" to "im-" is due to assimilation with the following "p."
