loxodromy:/ˌlɒkˈsɒdrəmi/ |n.|斜航线，等角航线|A line on the surface of a sphere that crosses all meridians at the same angle; a rhumb line.  A course of constant bearing.|Sailors used loxodromy to navigate across the ocean.  水手们使用斜航线航行穿越海洋。|Synonyms: rhumb line, constant bearing course|Antonyms: orthodrome, great circle route|noun

loxodromic:/ˌlɒkˈsɒdrəmɪk/ |adj.|斜航的，等角航线的|Relating to or involving a loxodrome.|The loxodromic curve spirals towards the poles. 斜航曲线向两极盘旋。|Synonyms: rhumb, constant bearing|Antonyms: orthodromic, great circle|adjective
