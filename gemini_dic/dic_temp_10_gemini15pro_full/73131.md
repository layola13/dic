foraged:/ˈfɔːrɪdʒd/
verb, adjective|觅食的，搜寻的|searched for food or provisions, especially in a natural environment; having been obtained by foraging.|例句：They foraged for berries in the woods. 他们在树林里觅食浆果。|近义词：searched, hunted, gathered, rummaged|反义词：planted, cultivated
词性: 动词的过去分词形式，也可作形容词
