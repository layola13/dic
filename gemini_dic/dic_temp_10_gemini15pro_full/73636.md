forfeiter:/ˈfɔːrfɪtər/ | n. | 放弃者；没收者；丧失者 |  A person who forfeits something, such as a right, property, or privilege. | The forfeiter lost his deposit when he failed to complete the purchase.  放弃者因未能完成购买而损失了定金。| Synonyms: loser, defaulter | Antonyms: winner, gainer | noun

forfeit:/ˈfɔːrfɪt/ | n. vt. | 丧失；罚金；没收； | Something lost or given up as a penalty for a crime, fault, or mistake; to lose or give up something as a penalty. | He had to forfeit his driver's license after the DUI.  酒后驾车后，他不得不交出驾驶执照。 | Synonyms: penalty, fine, loss | Antonyms: reward, gain, prize | noun, verb

forfeiture:/ˈfɔːrfɪtʃər/ | n. | 丧失；没收；罚金 | The act of forfeiting something. | The forfeiture of his property was a consequence of his illegal activities.  他的财产被没收是他从事非法活动的后果。 | Synonyms: loss, surrender, confiscation | Antonyms:  acquisition, retention | noun
