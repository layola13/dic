glebe:/ɡliːb/
n.
耕地，教堂所属的田地
A plot of land belonging to a parish church or ecclesiastical benefice.
The vicarage was built on glebe land.  牧师住宅建在教堂所属的土地上。
Synonyms:  church land, parish land, benefice
Antonyms:  common land, public land
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *glēbe, from Late Latin glēba (“clod, lump of earth, soil”), from Latin glaeba (“lump of earth”). Cognate with French glèbe, Spanish gleba.
