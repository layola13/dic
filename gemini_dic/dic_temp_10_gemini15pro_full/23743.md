braunite:/ˈbrɔːnaɪt/
n.
褐锰矿
A brownish-black mineral consisting of manganese oxide, an important ore of manganese.
Braunite is often found in metamorphic rocks. 褐锰矿常发现于变质岩中。
Braunite is a significant source of manganese. 褐锰矿是锰的重要来源。
近义词:hausmannite, manganite (as manganese ores)
反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific mineral)
词性:noun

词源:Named after Wilhelm von Braun (1802-1874), a German mineralogist.
