faceted:/ˈfæsɪtɪd/ | adj. |多面的，多方面的|Having multiple sides or aspects. |例句：The diamond was multifaceted and sparkled brilliantly.  这颗钻石有多个切面，闪耀着光芒。

例句：The problem is multifaceted and requires a comprehensive solution. 这个问题是多方面的，需要一个全面的解决方案。|近义词：multidimensional, complex, varied |反义词：simple, unidimensional |词性: adjective

facet:/ˈfæsɪt/ | n. | (事物的) 面，方面|A particular aspect or feature of something.|例句：She studied every facet of the issue before making a decision. 她做决定前研究了这个问题的每一个方面。

例句：He has many facets to his personality. 他的性格有很多方面。|近义词：aspect, side, angle |反义词：whole, entirety |词性: noun
