effund:/ɪˈfʌnd/ | v. | 倾泻，流出；吐露（感情等） | To pour out or forth; to express (feelings, etc.) freely and openly. | She effunded her sorrows to her best friend. 她向她最好的朋友倾诉了她的悲伤。|  He effunded his anger on the unfortunate clerk. 他把怒气发泄在了那个不幸的职员身上。 | 近义词: pour out, vent, express, gush | 反义词: suppress, conceal, restrain | 词性: 动词

effusion:/ɪˈfjuːʒən/ | n. | 倾泻，流出；（感情的）迸发，流露 |  The act of pouring out or forth; an unrestrained expression of feelings. |  The poet's effusion of grief was heartbreaking. 这位诗人悲痛的流露令人心碎。 | There was an effusion of joy when the team won. 球队获胜时，人们欣喜若狂。 | 近义词: outpouring, outburst, expression, gush | 反义词: suppression, concealment, restraint | 词性: 名词
