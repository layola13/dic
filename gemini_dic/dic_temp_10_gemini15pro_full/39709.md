consigne:/kɒnˈsaɪn/ (UK) /kənˈsaɪn/ (US)| n.|寄存物，委托物；(尤指旅客的) 托运行李|Something that is entrusted to someone's care, especially luggage that is checked in by a traveler. |The hotel will hold your consigne until you check in.  酒店会保管您的寄存行李，直到您办理入住手续。|Synonyms: luggage, baggage, belongings, effects, baggage check, parcel|Antonyms: N/A|noun


consigne:/kɒnˈsaɪn/ (UK) /kənˈsaɪn/ (US)| n.|收货人|The person or company to whom something is shipped or delivered. |The consigne of the shipment was a company in London. 这批货物的收货人是伦敦的一家公司。|Synonyms: recipient, receiver, addressee|Antonyms: sender, shipper, consignor|noun
