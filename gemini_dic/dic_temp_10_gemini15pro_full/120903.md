miscaption:/ˌmɪsˈkæpʃən/
verb
错误地加标题；配错说明文字
to provide (an illustration, etc.) with an incorrect caption
The photograph was miscaptioned in the newspaper.  这张照片在报纸上配错了说明文字。
近义词: mislabel, misname
反义词: caption correctly
词性: verb

miscaption:/ˌmɪsˈkæpʃən/
noun
错误的标题；错误的说明文字
an incorrect caption
The miscaption under the photo made it seem like something else entirely.  照片下面的错误说明文字使它看起来完全像另一回事。
近义词: mislabeling, misnomer
反义词: correct caption
词性: noun
