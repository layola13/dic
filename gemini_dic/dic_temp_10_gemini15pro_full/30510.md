centrist:/ˈsentrɪst/| n. adj.|中间派，中间派的|A person who holds moderate political views.|例句：The centrist candidate appealed to voters from both parties.  中间派候选人吸引了两党的选民。

例句：He described himself as a centrist Democrat. 他把自己描述为一个中间派民主党人。|近义词：moderate, middle-of-the-roader|反义词：extremist, radical|词性: 名词, 形容词
Etymology: From center + -ist.  Center comes from the Latin "centrum" meaning "center point."  The suffix "-ist" denotes a person who adheres to a particular doctrine or system.
