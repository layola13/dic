no-doubt:/noʊ daʊt/| adv. |毫无疑问地，肯定地|Used to emphasize that something is almost certainly true.  Expressing certainty or strong probability.| No doubt she'll be late. 她肯定要迟到。 |Synonyms: certainly, definitely, surely, undoubtedly, without a doubt, unquestionably, indubitably|Antonyms: doubtfully, possibly, probably, questionably|adverb

否定句:
