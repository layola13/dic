coquette:/kɒˈkɛt/ /koʊˈkɛt/| n.| 卖弄风情的女子，轻佻女子 |A woman who flirts playfully.| She's such a coquette, batting her eyelashes at every man in the room. 她真是个轻佻女子，对房间里的每个男人都抛媚眼。| 近义词：flirt, tease, vamp | 反义词：prude | 词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *coquette*, from Old French *coquet*, masculine form of *coquette*, from *coquet*, diminutive of *coc* "rooster".  The original sense was "little hen," referring to a woman who flirts.
