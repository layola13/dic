nonslipperiest:/ˌnɑːnˈslɪpəriɪst/ | adj. | 最不滑的 |  Superlative form of "nonslippery," meaning least slippery.  Formed by adding "-iest" to "nonslippery." "Non-" is a prefix indicating negation. "Slippery" describes a surface that is difficult to hold onto or walk on because it is smooth, wet, or icy. |  The nonslipperiest shoes are essential for hiking in these conditions.  在这种情况下，最不滑的鞋子对于徒步旅行至关重要。 | The nonslipperiest tiles were chosen for the bathroom floor.  浴室地板选择了最不滑的瓷砖。 | Near synonyms:  most grippy, most stable,  least slippery | Antonyms: slickest, slipperiest, iciest | Word type: Adjective
