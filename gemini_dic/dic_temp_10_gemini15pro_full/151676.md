partitionable:/pɑːrˈtɪʃənəbl/
adjective
可分割的
Capable of being partitioned or divided into separate parts or sections.
The large dataset is partitionable into smaller, more manageable chunks for analysis.  这个大型数据集可以分割成更小、更容易管理的块进行分析。
The hard drive is partitionable, allowing you to create separate sections for different operating systems.  这个硬盘是可分割的，允许你为不同的操作系统创建单独的区域。
synonyms: divisible, separable, segmentable
antonyms: indivisible, inseparable
词性: adjective
