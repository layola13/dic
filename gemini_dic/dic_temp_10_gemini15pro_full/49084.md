deia:/ˈdeɪə/| n.|女神; (尤指) 女战神; 女英雄; 优秀的女性|A female deity, especially a war goddess; a female hero or a woman of excellence.  From Greek *deia* "goddess," feminine of *deios* "divine".|The ancient Greeks worshipped many deia. 古希腊人崇拜许多女神。|She was considered a deia for her bravery and skill in battle. 她因其勇敢和战斗技巧而被视为女战神。|近义词：goddess, heroine, amazon|反义词:mortal, coward|词性:noun
