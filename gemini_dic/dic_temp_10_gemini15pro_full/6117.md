ametabolous:/ˌeɪmɪˈtæbələs/
adj.
无变态的，不完全变态的
Undergoing little or no metamorphosis, as certain insects and other invertebrates.  Developing without metamorphosis.
Silverfish are ametabolous insects.  衣鱼是不完全变态昆虫。
The springtail is an ametabolous insect. 跳虫是一种无变态昆虫。
Synonyms: direct development
Antonyms: holometabolous, hemimetabolous
词性: 形容词
