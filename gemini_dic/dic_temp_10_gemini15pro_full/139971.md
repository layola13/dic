odor:/ˈoʊdər/ (US) /ˈəʊdə(r)/ (UK)| n.|气味，香味，臭味|A distinctive smell, especially an unpleasant one.|The room was filled with the odor of stale cigarette smoke. 房间里充满了难闻的陈旧香烟味。|Synonyms: smell, scent, aroma, fragrance (pleasant); stench, stink, reek (unpleasant)|Antonyms: fragrance (when odor is unpleasant)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *odor*, from Latin *odor*, meaning "smell," related to *olere*, "to smell."
