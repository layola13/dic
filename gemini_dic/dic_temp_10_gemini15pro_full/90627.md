humanist:/ˈhjuːmənɪst/ /ˈjuːmənɪst/| n. adj.|人文主义者，人道主义者；人文主义的，人道主义的|A humanist is someone who emphasizes human values and reason, believing that human potential and experience are important.  As an adjective, it describes things related to this philosophy.|例句：She is a well-known humanist and advocate for human rights. 她是一位著名的人文主义者和人权倡导者。|近义词：humanitarian, freethinker, rationalist|反义词：dogmatist, bigot|词性: noun, adjective
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin humanista ‘teacher of humanities,’ from humanus (see human).
