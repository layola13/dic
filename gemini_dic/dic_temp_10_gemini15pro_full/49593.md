demerse:/dɪˈmɜːrs/
verb
浸入；沉入；使沉浸
to plunge into a liquid; sink; immerse
例句：
The submarine demersed quickly. 潜水艇迅速下沉。
He demersed himself in his studies. 他沉浸在学习中。

近义词：immerse, submerge, plunge, sink
反义词：emerge, surface
