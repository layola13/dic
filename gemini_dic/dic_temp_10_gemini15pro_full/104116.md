jumpily:/ˈdʒʌmpɪli/| adv.|跳跃地，神经质地|in a jumpy or jerky manner.  Characterized by sudden movements or changes.|The hummingbird flitted jumpily from flower to flower.  蜂鸟跳跃式地从一朵花飞到另一朵花。|近义词：jerkily, nervously, fitfully|反义词:smoothly, steadily|词性:副词

Etymology:  From "jumpy" + "-ly."  "Jumpy" originates from "jump" (from Old French "jumper"), meaning "to leap," with the suffix "-y" added to form an adjective.
