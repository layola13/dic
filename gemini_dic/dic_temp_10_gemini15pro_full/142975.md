outbake:/ˌaʊtˈbeɪk/
Verb
比...烤得更好, 烤的时间比...长
to bake longer or better than
例句：She outbaked her competitor in the pie contest. 她在馅饼比赛中比她的对手烤得更好。
例句：He outbaked the bread, resulting in a hard crust. 他把面包烤过头了，导致面包皮很硬。
近义词：surpass, excel, outdo
反义词：underbake
词性：verb
