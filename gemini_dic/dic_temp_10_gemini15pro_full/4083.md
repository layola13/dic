ailed:/eɪld/ | v. |  troubled, afflicted, or unwell |  Suffering from illness or trouble.  | The company has been ailed by financial problems for years.  这家公司多年来一直受到财务问题的困扰。 | synonyms: afflicted, troubled, diseased, ill, sick, unwell | antonyms: healthy, well, thriving | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *ailen, from Old Norse *eila, from Proto-Germanic *ailjaną (“to trouble, pain, afflict”). Cognate with Old English eglian (“to trouble, pain”). More at ail.
