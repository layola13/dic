barers:/ˈbɛərərz/| n. |持票人，不记名票据持有人|People who hold or carry something, especially a bearer bond or other financial instrument that is payable to whoever possesses it.  The barers of the winning lottery ticket will receive a grand prize.|The barers of the company's bonds will receive interest payments. 公司债券的持有人将收到利息。|近义词：holders, possessors|反义词: issuers|词性:noun
