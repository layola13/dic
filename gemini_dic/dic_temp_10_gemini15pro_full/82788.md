guimpe:/ˈɡɪmp/| n.|胸衣，高领衬衣，一种轻薄的织物|A guimpe is a piece of clothing, usually a short blouse or dickey, worn underneath a low-necked dress or bodice to fill in the neckline.  It can also refer to a lightweight fabric.|She wore a lace guimpe under her velvet gown. 她穿了一件蕾丝高领衬衣在她的天鹅绒礼服里面。|Synonyms: chemisette, dickey, insert, modesty piece|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but consider "low-cut top")|Noun
