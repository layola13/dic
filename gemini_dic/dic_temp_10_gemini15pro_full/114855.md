mammilliform:/ˌmæməˈlɪfɔːrm/
adjective
乳头状的，乳头形的
Resembling a nipple in shape.
The cactus had mammilliform protuberances all over its surface.  仙人掌的表面布满了乳头状的突起。
Near synonyms: nipple-shaped, teat-shaped, papillary
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a shape.  One might consider "smooth" or "flat" if contrasting the texture.)
词性: adjective
