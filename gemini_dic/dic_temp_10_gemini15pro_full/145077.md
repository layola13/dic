overanimatedly: /ˌōvərˈænɪˌmeɪtɪdli/
adverb|过度活跃地，夸张地|in an excessively animated manner; with too much excitement or enthusiasm|She told the story overanimatedly, waving her hands and mimicking the characters. 她绘声绘色地讲着故事，挥舞着双手，模仿着人物。| excitedly, exuberantly, dramatically| calmly, quietly, subtly|词性: 副词


Etymology:
over- (prefix meaning "excessively") + animated (adjective meaning "full of life or excitement") + -ly (adverb suffix)
