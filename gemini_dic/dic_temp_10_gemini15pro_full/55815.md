divestment:/daɪˈvɛstmənt/ | n. |撤资，剥离|The act of selling off or disposing of assets, such as stocks, property, or a subsidiary company.| The company's divestment of its non-core assets allowed it to focus on its main business. 公司剥离非核心资产使其能够专注于主营业务。| synonyms: disposal, sale, liquidation | antonyms: investment, acquisition | noun

divest:/daɪˈvɛst/ | vt. |剥夺，使摆脱| To deprive someone of something, or to rid oneself of something.| The court divested him of his parental rights. 法院剥夺了他的父母权利。  He divested himself of his shares in the company. 他出售了他在公司的股份。 | synonyms: deprive, strip, rid | antonyms: invest, endow | verb
