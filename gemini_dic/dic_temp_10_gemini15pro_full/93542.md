imaginate:/ɪˈmædʒɪneɪt/| v. |想象，设想|To form a mental image or concept of something.  It's often used informally, especially in some dialects, as a stronger or more emphatic form of "imagine".|例句：I can't imaginate living without music. 我无法想象没有音乐的生活。

例句：Can you imaginate winning the lottery? 你能想象中彩票吗？


近义词：imagine, conceive, envision, visualize|反义词: - |词性:动词
