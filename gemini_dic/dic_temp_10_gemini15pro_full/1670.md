acne: /ˈækni/ | n. | 痤疮，粉刺 |  A skin condition characterized by red pimples on the face, back, and chest, typically affecting teenagers. |  She suffered from acne as a teenager. 她十几岁时脸上长了粉刺。

My son uses a special face wash for his acne. 我儿子用一种特殊的洗面奶来治疗他的痤疮。| 近义词：pimples, blemishes, zits | 反义词：clear skin | 词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek akmē "point, highest point," hence "rash on the face, acne," perhaps related to akḗ "point, edge."
