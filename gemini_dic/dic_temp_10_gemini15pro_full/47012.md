dashpot: /ˈdæʃpɒt/ (UK) /ˈdæʃpɑːt/ (US) | n. | 缓冲器；阻尼器；减震器 | A mechanical device that resists sudden motion or changes in velocity, typically by using the viscosity of a fluid. | The door closed slowly because of the dashpot. 门因为缓冲器而慢慢关闭。 | The dashpot prevented the needle from vibrating excessively. 阻尼器防止指针过度振动。 | 近义词: damper, shock absorber, snubber | 反义词: accelerator | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dash (to strike violently) + pot (a container).  Originally referred to a container of oil or other viscous fluid used to dampen the motion of a mechanical part.
