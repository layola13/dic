impersonal:/ɪmˈpɜːrsənl/ | adj. | 非个人的，客观的，冷淡的 | Not influenced by, showing, or involving personal feelings; lacking human warmth or sympathy. | The hospital has a very impersonal atmosphere. 这家医院的气氛很冷淡。 |  The rules are applied impersonally to everyone. 这些规则不带个人感情地应用于每个人。 | 近义词: objective, detached, indifferent, unemotional | 反义词: personal, subjective, warm, friendly | 词性: adjective

impersonal:/ɪmˈpɜːrsənl/| adj. | 非人称的（语法） | (of a verb) used only with a formal subject, typically ‘it’, and not referring to a particular person, e.g. ‘it’s snowing’. |  It was snowing heavily.  雪下得很大。| It is important to follow the rules. 遵守规则很重要。| 近义词: generic | 反义词: personal | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *impersonalis, from in- 'not' + personalis 'personal'.
