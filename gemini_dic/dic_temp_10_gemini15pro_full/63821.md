epididymis:/ˌɛpɪˈdɪdəmɪs/| n. | 附睾 | A highly convoluted duct behind the testis, along which sperm passes to the vas deferens.| The epididymis stores sperm until they mature. 附睾储存精子直到它们成熟。|  The doctor examined the epididymis for any abnormalities. 医生检查了附睾是否有异常。 |Synonyms: None (This is a specific anatomical term)| Antonyms: None | Noun
