mirrored:/ˈmɪrərd/ | adj. | 镜像的，反映的 | Reflecting something, like a mirror; having the characteristics of a mirror. | The mirrored sunglasses reflected the bright sunlight. 镜像的太阳镜反射着明亮的阳光。 | 近义词: reflected, glassy, polished | 反义词: dull, matte | 词性:形容词

mirrored:/ˈmɪrərd/ | v. | 反映，镜像 | To reflect something as a mirror does. |  The still water mirrored the surrounding trees. 静静的水面映照着周围的树木。 | 近义词: reflect, echo, replicate | 反义词: distort, misrepresent | 词性:动词

词源: From mirror + -ed.  "Mirror" comes from Old French "mireor," from Late Latin "miratorium," from Latin "mirari" meaning "to wonder at, admire."
