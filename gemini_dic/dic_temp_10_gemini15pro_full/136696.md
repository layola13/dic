nonsegmentary:/nɒnˈsɛɡməntəri/
adjective
非分段的，非节段的
Not divided into segments or sections; lacking clear divisions or boundaries.
The Nuer have a nonsegmentary lineage system.  努尔人有一个非分段的世系系统。
Anthropologists study nonsegmentary societies to understand social organization.  人类学家研究非分段社会以理解社会组织。
Synonyms: unified, whole, integrated, continuous, undifferentiated
Antonyms: segmented, divided, partitioned, fragmented, sectioned
Etymology:  From non- + segmentary, from Latin segmentum "a piece cut off, section."
