cuneiform:/ˈkjuːnɪfɔːrm/ | adj. n. |楔形的；楔形文字|A system of writing used in ancient Mesopotamia, characterized by wedge-shaped marks impressed on clay tablets. |The museum exhibited a collection of cuneiform tablets.  博物馆展出了一批楔形文字泥板。|近义词:wedge-shaped|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "alphabetic" or "modern") |词性: adjective, noun
Etymology: From Latin *cuneiformis*, from *cuneus* (wedge) + *forma* (form).
