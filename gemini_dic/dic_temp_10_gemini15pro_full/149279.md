ozoneless:/ˈoʊzoʊnləs/| adj.|无臭氧的，缺乏臭氧的|Lacking an ozone layer or having a depleted ozone layer.  This term describes an atmosphere or region that is deficient in ozone, a gas that absorbs harmful ultraviolet radiation from the sun.|The ozoneless atmosphere of early Earth allowed harmful UV radiation to reach the surface.  早期地球无臭氧的大气层允许有害的紫外线辐射到达地表。|近义词：ozone-depleted, ozone-deficient|反义词: ozone-rich|词性:形容词
