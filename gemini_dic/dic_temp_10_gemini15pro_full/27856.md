canonizers:/ˈkænənaɪzərz/
n.
册封圣徒的人；认可…为正典的人；使…成为经典的人
People who officially declare someone a saint; people who declare something to be canonical or authoritative.
The canonizers of literary history often overlook women writers.  文学史上的经典认可者常常忽视女性作家。
The church's canonizers declared her a saint. 教会的册封圣徒者宣布她为圣徒。
Synonyms: sanctifiers, enshriners, validators
Antonyms: detractors, critics, denouncers
noun
