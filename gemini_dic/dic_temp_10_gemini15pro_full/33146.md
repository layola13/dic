chromascope:/ˈkrəʊməˌskəʊp/ /ˈkroʊməˌskoʊp/| n. |色谱仪，色度计，万花筒|An instrument for showing the optical effects of color, sometimes by the use of revolving disks or prisms.  A kaleidoscope.|例句：The child was mesmerized by the vibrant patterns created by the chromascope. 孩子被色谱仪创造的鲜艳图案迷住了。

例句：The chromascope projected a dazzling array of colors onto the wall. 色谱仪在墙上投射出一系列令人眼花缭乱的颜色。|近义词：kaleidoscope, colorimeter, spectroscope|反义词: None|词性: noun
