call-out:/ˈkɔːlaʊt/ (US) /ˈkɔːlaʊt/ (UK)| n. |出动；点名；大声疾呼；(书中)插图说明；(软件)标注 |A request for someone, such as a plumber or a doctor, to come to a particular place to do a job; A public expression of criticism or disapproval; A short piece of text beside an illustration or in a technical drawing, giving further information. |例句：The fire department received a call-out to a house fire. 消防部门接到了一起房屋火灾的出动请求。

例句：There was a call-out for volunteers to help with the cleanup.  招募志愿者来帮忙清理。


|近义词：alert, summons, challenge, denunciation|反义词:concealment, suppression|词性:noun
