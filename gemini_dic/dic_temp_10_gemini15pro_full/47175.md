dax:/dæks/
n.
dachshund
腊肠犬
A dachshund is a short-legged, long-bodied breed of dog.
例句：My dax loves to burrow under blankets. 我的腊肠犬喜欢钻到毯子下面。
例句：The dax chased a squirrel up a tree.  这只腊肠犬追着一只松鼠上了树。

近义词：sausage dog, wiener dog
反义词: (No direct antonym for a breed of dog)
词性: noun
