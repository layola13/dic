claustrophobic:/ˌklɔːstrəˈfəʊbɪk/ /ˌklɑːstrəˈfoʊbɪk/| adj.|幽闭恐惧的；患幽闭恐怖症的|Experiencing or inducing a fear of enclosed spaces.|例句：She felt claustrophobic in the elevator. 她觉得电梯里很闷，有幽闭恐惧感。

例句：He gets claustrophobic in small spaces. 他在狭小的空间里会感到幽闭恐惧。|近义词：confined, restricted, stifled|反义词：agoraphobic, open, spacious|词性:形容词

Etymology:  From Latin *claustrophobia*, from *claustrum* ("bolt, bar, enclosed place") + *-phobia* ("fear").
