cryptovolcanic:/ˌkrɪptoʊvɒlˈkænɪk/
adjective
隐火山作用的；潜火山作用的
Relating to or denoting volcanic activity in which the lava fails to reach the surface, resulting in the formation of structures such as laccoliths and domes.
The cryptovolcanic structure was identified by its unique geological features.  这隐火山结构是通过其独特的地质特征来识别的。
The region is known for its cryptovolcanic domes.  该区域以其潜火山穹丘而闻名。
近义词:subvolcanic
反义词:extrusive (volcanic)
