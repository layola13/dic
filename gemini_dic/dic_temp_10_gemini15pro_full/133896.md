nonguarantees:/ˌnɒnˌɡærənˈtiːz/
noun
无担保，无保证
Absence of guarantees; things not guaranteed.
The contract explicitly states the nonguarantees associated with the investment.  合同明确规定了与投资相关的无担保条款。
This investment comes with certain nonguarantees. 这项投资附带某些无担保条款。
近义词:  uncertainties, risks
反义词: guarantees, assurances
词性: noun
