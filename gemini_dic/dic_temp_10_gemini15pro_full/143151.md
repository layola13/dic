outcharm:/ˌaʊtˈtʃɑːrm/
verb
胜过；以魅力取胜
to surpass in charm; to be more charming than
She easily outcharmed everyone else at the party. 她在聚会上轻易地胜过了其他所有人，更有魅力。
outclass, outshine, surpass
dull
v.

ETYMOLOGY->
