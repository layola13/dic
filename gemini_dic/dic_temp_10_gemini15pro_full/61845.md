employee: /ɪmˈplɔɪiː/ (UK) /ɪmˈplɔɪiː/ (US)| n.|雇员，员工|A person employed for wages or salary, especially at non-executive level. |例句：The company has 100 employees.  这家公司有一百名员工。

例句：She is a valued employee of the company. 她是一位公司重要的员工。

近义词：worker, staff member, personnel|反义词: employer, boss|词性:noun
