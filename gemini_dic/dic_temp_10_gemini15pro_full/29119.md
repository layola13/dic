词源: From Spanish *cáscara* (“husk, shell, bark”), from *cascar* (“to break, crack, burst”)

注意事项:  Cascara can interact with certain medications, so it is important to consult a doctor before using it, especially if you have any underlying health conditions.  While generally safe, overuse can lead to dependence and electrolyte imbalance.
