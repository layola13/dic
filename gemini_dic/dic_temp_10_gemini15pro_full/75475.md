frustratedly:/ˈfrʌstreɪtɪdli/ | adv. | 沮丧地，懊恼地 | In a frustrated manner; showing or expressing frustration.  | She sighed frustratedly and threw her pen down. 她沮丧地叹了口气，把笔扔了下去。 | He kicked the door frustratedly after realizing he'd forgotten his keys. 他意识到自己忘了钥匙后，沮丧地踢了门。 | 近义词: angrily, irritably, disappointedly | 反义词: patiently, calmly, contentedly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From the adjective "frustrated," which comes from the past participle of the verb "frustrate."  "Frustrate" comes from the Latin word "frustrare," meaning "to deceive" or "to disappoint."
