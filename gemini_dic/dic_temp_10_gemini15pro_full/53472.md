disarms:/dɪsˈɑːrmz/
verb
解除武装；使息怒；缓和；消除
takes away weapons; makes less angry or hostile; wins over; removes a fuse or other safety device from

例句：
The police disarmed the robber. 警察解除了抢匪的武装。

The negotiator's calm demeanor disarmed the angry crowd.  谈判代表冷静的态度平息了愤怒的群众。

反义词：arm, provoke, antagonize
词性：v.

ETYMOLOGY->
