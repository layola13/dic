impasti:/ɪmˈpɑːsti/ /ɪmˈpæsti/
|n.| (尤指绘画中) 厚涂颜料；厚涂法；（食物，尤指面包的）质感|A thick application of paint, as in impasto painting; the texture of a food, especially bread.|The artist used impasto to create a textured effect on the canvas. 艺术家用厚涂法在画布上创造了一种纹理效果。|The bread had a wonderful, chewy impasto.  这面包有着很棒的，耐嚼的质感。|texture, consistency, body|thinness, flatness|noun
impasto:/ɪmˈpɑːsti/ /ɪmˈpæsti/
|adj.| 厚涂的；用厚涂法画的|Applied thickly, as paint.|The impasto brushstrokes gave the painting a three-dimensional quality. 厚涂的笔触使这幅画具有立体感。|thick, textured, heavy|thin, flat, smooth|adjective
