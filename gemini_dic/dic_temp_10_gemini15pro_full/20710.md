PHRASE->
blag it =  to achieve something you want or need by persuading someone or by being clever:  Let’s blag it and see if they believe us. 让我们巧言哄骗，看看他们是否相信我们。

blag (something) from (someone) = to get something from someone by persuading them or by being clever:  I managed to blag a free ticket from the organizer. 我设法从组织者那里骗到了一张免费门票。

blag (one's) way into (somewhere) = to succeed in entering a place or gaining access to something by using clever talk and deception: He blagged his way into the exclusive party. 他骗过了保安混进了专属派对。

blag (something) out of (someone) =  to persuade someone to give you something, often by using clever talk or deception: She managed to blag a discount out of the salesperson. 她设法说服了销售员给她打折。

blag about (something) = to talk boastfully and often dishonestly about something: He was blagging about his connections to famous people. 他在吹嘘自己与名人的关系。
