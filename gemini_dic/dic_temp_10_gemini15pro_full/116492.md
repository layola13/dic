materialness:/məˌtɪəriˈælnəs/
n.
物质性；实质性；重要性
The state or quality of being material; relevance or importance.
The materialness of the evidence was questioned by the defense.  证据的实质性受到了辩方的质疑。
relevance, importance, significance, substantiality
immateriality, insignificance, unimportance
noun
