firewire:/ˈfaɪərwaɪər/| n.|火线，高速串行总线|A high-speed serial bus standard used for connecting peripherals to computers, especially camcorders and other multimedia devices.  It's also known as IEEE 1394.|Example: I connected my camcorder to my computer using a FireWire cable. 我用火线连接了我的摄像机和电脑。|Example:  FireWire offers faster data transfer rates than USB 1.1. 火线提供比USB 1.1更快的传输速率。|近义词：IEEE 1394, i.LINK|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps slower interfaces like USB 1.1 could be considered)|词性:noun
