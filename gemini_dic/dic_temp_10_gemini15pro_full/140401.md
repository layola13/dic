oinomania:/ˌɔɪnəˈmeɪniə/ | n. | 购物癖，购买狂 | An abnormal and persistent urge to buy things. |  She struggled with oinomania, often finding herself with closets full of unworn clothes. 她与购物癖作斗争，经常发现自己的衣橱里堆满了从未穿过的衣服。|  Example:  His oinomania led him into serious debt.  他的购物癖使他债台高筑。| 近义词: compulsive shopping, oniomania, shopping addiction | 反义词: frugality, thrift | noun
