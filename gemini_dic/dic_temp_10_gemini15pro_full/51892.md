注意事项:
"Dhamma" (singular) and "dhammas" (plural) are Pali words. They are often translated as "teachings," "doctrines," "principles," or "phenomena" depending on the context. There isn't a single perfect English equivalent.  It's important to understand the Buddhist context when encountering this word.
