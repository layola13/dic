filberts:/ˈfɪlbərts/ | n. |榛子；榛树|A filbert is the nut of the hazel tree.  |例句：I love to snack on roasted filberts. 我喜欢吃烤榛子。

例句：The squirrels are busy burying filberts for the winter. 松鼠们正忙着埋藏榛子过冬。|近义词：hazelnuts |反义词: N/A |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *philbert, from Saint Philibert, whose feast day (August 20th, near harvest time) coincided with the ripening of the nuts.
