outroot:/ˌaʊtˈruːt/ | v. | 根除，彻底消灭 | To remove or destroy something completely, especially something bad. |  The government is trying to outroot corruption. 政府正在努力根除腐败。

The gardener outrooted the weeds. 园丁把杂草连根拔起。 | 近义词: eradicate, exterminate, eliminate | 反义词: plant, cultivate, establish | 词性: 动词
