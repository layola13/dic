palais:/pæˈleɪ/ (US), /ˈpæl.eɪ/ (UK)| n.|宫殿，豪华住宅|A large, splendid house or building, especially a royal residence or the home of a nobleman. |例句：The president lives in the presidential palais. 总统住在总统府。

例句：They held a grand ball at the palais. 他们在宫殿里举行了一场盛大的舞会。|近义词：palace, mansion, chateau|反义词：hut, shack|noun
