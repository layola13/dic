overfreights:/ˌoʊvərˈfreɪts/
n. |超载货物，过多的货物|The ship was dangerously overfreights. 这艘船严重超载。|例句：The truck was fined for carrying overfreights. 卡车因运载货物超载而被罚款。

例句：The additional overfreights caused the ship to list.  额外的货物导致船倾斜。

近义词：excess cargo, overload, surplus freight
反义词：underload
词性：名词


overfreight:/ˌoʊvərˈfreɪt/
vt. |使…装载过多，超载|They overfreighted the ship, making it unstable. 他们使船超载，使其不稳定。|例句：Be careful not to overfreight the truck; it could be dangerous.  小心不要让卡车超载；这很危险。

例句：The airline overfreighted the plane, causing delays. 航空公司使飞机超载，导致延误。

近义词：overload, overburden
反义词：underload
词性：动词

词源： "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "freight" (n. goods transported by a carrier)
