crank's:/kræŋks/
noun
怪人的；古怪的；脾气坏的
belonging to a crank (an eccentric or odd person, especially one with an unusual obsession).
That's just one of the crank's ideas. 那只是那个怪人的想法之一。
synonyms: eccentric's, oddball's, peculiar person's
antonyms: normal person's, conventional person's
词性: noun (名词所有格)
