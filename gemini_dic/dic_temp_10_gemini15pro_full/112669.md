lowermost:/ˈloʊərˌmoʊst/| adj.|最低的；最下面的|situated at the lowest level.  The lowermost branches of the tree were bare.  这棵树最下面的枝条是光秃秃的。|近义词：lowest, bottom, bottommost|反义词：highest, topmost, uppermost|词性:形容词

Etymology: From 'lower' (comparative of 'low') + '-most' (superlative suffix).  'Low' comes from Old English 'hlāw',  meaning "not high, short." '-most' is a superlative suffix derived from Old English '-mest'.
