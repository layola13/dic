misbalance:/ˌmɪsˈbæləns/ | n. |不平衡，失衡 |lack of balance; disproportion |The misbalance of power led to conflict. 权力失衡导致了冲突。|The team suffered from a misbalance of experience. 这个团队缺乏经验上的平衡。|近义词: imbalance, disproportion, asymmetry, inequality |反义词: balance, equilibrium, symmetry, equality | noun

misbalance:/ˌmɪsˈbæləns/ | vt. |使失去平衡，使不均衡 |to cause to be unbalanced |The new trade agreement misbalanced the economy. 新的贸易协定使经济失去了平衡. | The sudden influx of refugees misbalanced the social structure of the small town.  难民的突然涌入打破了小镇的社会结构平衡。|近义词: unbalance, upset, destabilize |反义词: balance, stabilize, equilibrate | verb
