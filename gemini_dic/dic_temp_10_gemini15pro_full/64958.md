estheticians:/ɛsθəˈtɪʃənz/
noun
美容师；美容专家
A person who is trained to give beauty treatments.
例句：The estheticians at the spa provided excellent service.  水疗中心的美容师提供了优质的服务。

例句：Many estheticians recommend using sunscreen daily. 许多美容师建议每天使用防晒霜。

近义词：cosmetologists, beauty therapists
反义词: None

词性: noun (plural)
