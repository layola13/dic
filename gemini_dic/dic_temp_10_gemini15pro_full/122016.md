misstayed:/mɪsˈsteɪd/
Verb
逾期逗留，过期停留
To stay beyond the permitted or intended time.
He misstayed his visa and was deported. 他签证过期逗留，被驱逐出境。
Near synonyms: overstay, outstay
Antonyms: leave, depart
词性: 动词
