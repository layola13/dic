desecrates:/ˈdɛsɪkreɪts/
verb
亵渎，玷污
To treat (a sacred place or thing) with violent disrespect; violate.
The vandals desecrated the church by spray-painting graffiti on the altar.  那些破坏者在祭坛上喷涂涂鸦，亵渎了教堂。
violates, profanes, defiles
revere, venerate, hallow
