germination:/ˌdʒɜːrmɪˈneɪʃn/ | n. | 发芽，萌芽 |The process by which a plant grows from a seed.  The development of something, especially from a simple or basic form. | The warm spring weather encourages seed germination.  温暖的春天有利于种子发芽。| synonyms: sprouting, budding,  development, emergence | antonyms: dormancy, inactivity | noun

germinate:/ˈdʒɜːrmɪneɪt/ | v. | 发芽，萌芽 |(of a seed or spore) begin to grow and put out shoots after a period of dormancy. (of an idea, plan, etc.) come into existence and develop. | The seeds will germinate in about two weeks.  这些种子大约两周后就会发芽。 The idea germinated from a discussion with colleagues. 这个想法源于与同事的讨论。 | synonyms: sprout, bud, develop, grow, spring up | antonyms: wither, die, stagnate | verb
