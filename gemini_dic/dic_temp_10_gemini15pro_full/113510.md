ma'am:/mæm/ | n. | 女士，夫人 | A polite term of address for a woman.  Used especially in the US and by people in service occupations when addressing a customer. | Excuse me, ma'am, you dropped your wallet.  对不起，女士，你掉了钱包。 | Ma'am, may I help you with your luggage? 女士，我可以帮您拿行李吗？| 近义词: madam, lady, miss | 反义词: sir, mister | noun
