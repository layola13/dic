decortication:/diːˌkɔːrtɪˈkeɪʃn/
n.
去皮;剥皮;去壳; (尤指脑部的)皮质切除;皮层剥离
The surgical removal of the cortex or outer layer of an organ or structure, especially the brain.  This can refer to removing the bark from a tree, the husk from grain, or the outer layer of a kidney, for example. In neurosurgery, it refers to the removal of portions of the cerebral cortex.
The doctor performed a decortication of the lung to remove the thickened pleural membrane. 医生对肺部进行了去皮术，以去除增厚的胸膜。
decortication of the kidney  肾脏去皮术
近义词:excoriation, peeling, shelling, husking, ablation (especially in a medical context)
反义词:coating, covering
