fresnel:/freɪˈnɛl/ | adj. n.| 菲涅耳，菲涅耳透镜的 |Relating to or designating Augustin-Jean Fresnel or his work, especially in optics.  Also, a type of lens or mirror that uses concentric stepped rings to focus light, often used in lighthouses, spotlights, and projection systems. | The lighthouse used a Fresnel lens to project the beam further.  灯塔使用菲涅耳透镜来投射光束更远。 |  Synonyms: lenticular, dioptric (for the lens) | Antonyms:  N/A (No direct antonym for a scientist's name or a specific type of lens) | adjective, noun

fresnel:/frɛˈnɛl/ | n.| 菲涅耳 | A unit of frequency equal to one terahertz (10^12 hertz).  Less commonly used than terahertz. | The frequency of the radiation was measured in fresnels.  辐射的频率是用菲涅耳测量的。 | Synonyms: terahertz | Antonyms: N/A | noun
