dangerless:/ˈdeɪndʒərləs/
adjective
无危险的；安全的
Free from danger; safe.
The path seemed dangerless, so we continued our hike. 这条路看起来很安全，所以我们继续徒步旅行。
Near synonyms: safe, secure, harmless
Antonyms: dangerous, perilous, hazardous
词性: 形容词
