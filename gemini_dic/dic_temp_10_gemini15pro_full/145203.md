overbalancing:/ˌoʊvərˈbælənsɪŋ/| v. n. adj.|过度平衡，失去平衡，过重|The act of causing something to lose balance by making one side too heavy.  As an adjective, it describes something that causes this loss of balance.|
例句：His sudden movement caused the canoe to overbalance and tip over. 他的突然移动导致独木舟失去平衡并翻倒了。
例句：The overbalancing weight on one side of the scale made it impossible to get an accurate reading.  天平一侧过重的重量使得无法获得准确的读数。
近义词：tipping, upsetting, capsizing, outweighing|反义词: stabilizing, balancing|词性: verb, noun, adjective (less common)

overbalance:/ˌoʊvərˈbæləns/| v. n.|失去平衡，过重|To cause something to lose balance by making one side too heavy; the act of losing balance in this way.|
例句：He overbalanced and fell into the water. 他失去平衡，掉进了水里。
例句：The overbalance of power in the region led to conflict. 该地区力量的失衡导致了冲突。
近义词：tip, upset, capsize, outweigh|反义词: stabilize, balance|词性: verb, noun


词源: "over-" (excessive) + "balance" (equilibrium)
