fatigue:/fəˈtiːɡ/ /fəˈtiːɡ/ | n.  |疲劳，疲乏；劳累 |Extreme tiredness resulting from mental or physical exertion or illness. |  She was suffering from fatigue. 她感到疲惫不堪。| weariness, exhaustion, tiredness | energy, vigor | noun


fatigue:/fəˈtiːɡ/ /fəˈtiːɡ/ | vt. |使疲劳，使疲乏 |To cause someone to feel tired or exhausted.  | The long journey fatigued the children. 漫长的旅程使孩子们疲惫不堪。 | exhaust, tire, wear out | energize, refresh | verb


Etymology:  From French *fatigue*, from fatiguer "to tire," from Late Latin *fatigare* "to weary," from Latin *fatigatus*, past participle of *fatigare* "to tire out."
