disorganized:/dɪsˈɔːrɡənaɪzd/ (US), /dɪsˈɔːɡənaɪzd/ (UK)| adj.|混乱的，无序的|Lacking order or organization; messy or chaotic.|例句：Her desk is always disorganized. 她的桌子总是很乱。|近义词：messy, cluttered, chaotic, disordered|反义词：organized, tidy, orderly|词性:形容词

disorganized:/dɪsˈɔːrɡənaɪzd/ (US), /dɪsˈɔːɡənaɪzd/ (UK)| v.|使混乱，扰乱（较少用作动词）|To cause to be disorganized.|例句：The sudden change disorganized the entire team. 突然的变化扰乱了整个团队。|近义词：disrupt, disorder|反义词：organize, order|词性:动词
