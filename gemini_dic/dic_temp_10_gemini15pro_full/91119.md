hyan:/ˈhaɪən/| n. |鬣狗|A dog-like carnivorous mammal of Africa and southern Asia, having coarse fur, a sloping back, and powerful jaws.|The hyenas cackled over their kill. 鬣狗对着它们的猎物发出尖笑。|Synonyms:  carrion-eater, scavenger|Antonyms: herbivore|Noun
