blogosphere:/ˈblɒɡəsfɪə(r)/ /ˈblɑːɡəsfɪr/| n. |博客圈；博客世界|The online community of all blogs and blog writers.|The political blogosphere was abuzz with the news. 政治博客圈对这条新闻议论纷纷。|
近义词:blogging world, online community|反义词:None|词性:noun


Etymology:
blog + sphere.  "Blog," short for "weblog," combines "web" and "log."  "Sphere" refers to a particular environment or world.
