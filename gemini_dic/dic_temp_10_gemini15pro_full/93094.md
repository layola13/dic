idylls: /ˈɪdɪlz/ /ˈaɪdɪlz/ | n. |田园诗; 牧歌; 悠闲的时光 | A short poem or other piece of writing that describes a peaceful and happy scene, especially in the countryside, or such a scene itself.  |  例句：The poem is a nostalgic idyll of childhood summers. 这首诗是对童年夏天的怀旧田园诗。

例句：They spent a few idyllic days in the countryside. 他们在乡下度过了几天田园诗般的生活。

近义词：pastoral, bucolic, arcadian | 反义词：cacophony, pandemonium | 词性: 名词
