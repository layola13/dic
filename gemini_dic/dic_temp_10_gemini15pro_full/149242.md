oxytonical: /ˌɒksɪˈtɒnɪkəl/ | adj. | 氧顿的；重音在最后一个音节上的 | Having the acute accent on the ultimate or last syllable. |  The word "hotel" is not oxytonical in English, but it is in French.  酒店这个词在英语中不是氧顿词，但在法语中是。|  Classical Greek poetry makes frequent use of oxytonical words. 古典希腊诗歌中经常使用氧顿词。 |  accented, stressed | unaccented, unstressed | adjective
