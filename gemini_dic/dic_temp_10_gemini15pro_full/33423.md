churchwardenly:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃˌwɔːrdənli/ | adj. | 教区委员似的；像教堂管理员的；古板的，一本正经的 | Resembling or characteristic of a churchwarden, typically in being overly formal, pompous, or officious.  It suggests a fussy and overly concerned attitude, often about minor matters related to tradition or propriety. | He puffed out his chest and gave me a churchwardenly look of disapproval. 他挺起胸膛，带着教堂管理员般不满的神情看着我。 |  Synonyms: pompous, officious, self-important, priggish, sanctimonious, fussy, overly formal | Antonyms: informal, casual, relaxed, easygoing | adjective
