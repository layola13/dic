epileptogenous:/ɪˌpɪləpˈtɒdʒɪnəs/
adjective
致痫的，诱发癫痫的
Causing epilepsy or epileptic seizures.
Certain stimuli, such as flashing lights, can be epileptogenous in susceptible individuals.  某些刺激，例如闪光，在易感个体中可能是致痫的。
Exposure to certain chemicals can be epileptogenous. 接触某些化学物质可能导致癫痫。
synonym: epileptogenic, convulsant, ictogenic
antonym: antiepileptic, anticonvulsant
词性: adjective
