doodah:/ˈduːdɑː/ (US), /duːˈdɑː/ (UK)| n.| 小玩意儿；装饰品；不重要的东西|A thing, especially a small ornament or gadget, whose name is unknown or forgotten. Also used to refer to something unimportant or insignificant.|例句：He was fiddling with some doodah on his keychain. 他一直在摆弄他钥匙链上的一个小玩意儿。

|例句：  Don't worry about that doodah, it's not important.  别担心那个小玩意儿，它不重要。

|近义词：gadget, thingamajig, whatchamacallit, trinket|反义词:|词性:noun
