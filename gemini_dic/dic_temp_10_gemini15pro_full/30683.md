certainest:/ˈsɜːtənɪst/ | adj. | 最确定的，最可靠的 |  Superlative form of certain; most certain. Expressing the highest degree of certainty. |  例句：That's the certainest way to succeed. 那是最可靠的成功方法。| 近义词：surest, most definite, most reliable | 反义词：least certain, most doubtful | 词性：形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *certeinest, from certain + -est.  Ultimately from Latin *certānus.
