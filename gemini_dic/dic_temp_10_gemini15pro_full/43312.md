craie:/kʁɛ/ (French)| n.|粉笔; 白垩|A soft white or grey rock, mainly calcium carbonate, formed from the skeletal remains of sea creatures. Used for writing on blackboards and in the manufacture of cement, plaster, and quicklime. |She picked up a piece of craie and began to write on the blackboard. 她拿起一支粉笔开始在黑板上写字。|Synonyms: chalk|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for the material itself; perhaps "marker" or "pen" in the context of writing implements)|Noun
