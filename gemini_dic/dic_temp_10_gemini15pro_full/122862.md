mollifiable:/ˈmɒlɪfaɪəbl/ /ˈmɑːlɪfaɪəbl/| adj.|可软化的，可缓和的|Able to be mollified; appeasable.  Something that can be made softer or less rigid, or a feeling that can be made less intense or severe.|The king's anger seemed mollifiable, and the advisors hoped to persuade him to change his mind. 国王的怒气似乎可以缓和，顾问们希望说服他改变主意。

The usually strict teacher was mollifiable today, accepting late homework without penalty.  通常严格的老师今天变得温和了，接受迟交的作业而没有惩罚。|近义词：appeasable, placable, conciliable, mitigable|反义词：implacable, unyielding, inflexible, intransigent|词性:adjective

词源: From Late Latin mollificabilis, from mollificare "to soften," from Latin mollis "soft" + facere "to make."
