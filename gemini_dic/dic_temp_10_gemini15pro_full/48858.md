deformational:/ˌdiːfɔːrˈmeɪʃənəl/
adjective
变形的；形变的
Relating to or characterized by deformation.
The deformational processes involved in mountain building are complex.  山脉形成过程中涉及的变形过程非常复杂。

The study focuses on the deformational history of the region. 这项研究的重点是该地区的变形历史。

近义词: distorted, misshapen, warped
反义词:  undeformed, intact
