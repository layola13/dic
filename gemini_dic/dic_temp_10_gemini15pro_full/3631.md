agendum:/əˈdʒɛndəm/| n.|议程，待办事项|A matter to be discussed or voted on, usually at a meeting.|The first agendum for the meeting was the budget proposal. 会议的第一个议程是预算提案。|近义词：item, topic, issue|反义词:|noun

agendums /əˈdʒɛndəmz/ (Less Common) /əˈdʒɛndə/ (More Common)| n.|议程，待办事项 (复数)|Plural form of agendum, although "agenda" is more commonly used.|The chairman listed the agendums for the next meeting. 主席列出了下次会议的议程。（更常见的用法是：The chairman listed the agenda for the next meeting.）|近义词：items, topics, issues|反义词:|noun
