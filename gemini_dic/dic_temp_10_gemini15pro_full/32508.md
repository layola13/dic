chinoline: /ˈkɪnəliːn/ (US), /ˈkwɪnəliːn/ (UK) | n. |喹啉|A heterocyclic aromatic organic compound with the chemical formula C9H7N. It is a colorless hygroscopic liquid with a strong odor. It is a structural isomer of isoquinoline. |Quinoline is used in the manufacture of dyes, pharmaceuticals, and other chemicals. It is also used as a solvent and as a starting material for the synthesis of other compounds.| Quinoline is used as a solvent for resins and terpenes.  喹啉用作树脂和萜烯的溶剂。

Quinoline derivatives are used in the treatment of malaria. 喹啉衍生物用于治疗疟疾。| synonym: 1-azanaphthalene, benzo[b]pyridine | antonym:  None (It's a specific chemical compound, not a concept with an opposite) | noun
