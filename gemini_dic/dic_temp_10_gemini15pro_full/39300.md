conico:/ˈkɒnɪkəʊ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnɪkoʊ/ (US)| adj.|圆锥形的; 圆锥体的|Having the shape of a cone; conical.|The volcano has a conico shape. 这座火山呈圆锥形。|The pine tree has a conico form. 这棵松树呈圆锥形。|近义词：conical, cone-shaped|反义词: (No direct opposite, consider shapes like cubic, cylindrical)|词性: adjective
Etymology: From Latin *conicus*, from Greek κωνικός (kōnikós), from κῶνος (kônos, “cone”).
