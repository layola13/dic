hungriest:/ˈhʌŋɡriɪst/| adj.|最饥饿的|Superlative form of "hungry," meaning experiencing the greatest need or desire for food.|例句：She was the hungriest of all the children. 她所有孩子中最饿的。


例句：He’s always the hungriest after a workout. 他锻炼完后总是最饿。|近义词：starving, ravenous, famished|反义词：full, satiated|词性:形容词
