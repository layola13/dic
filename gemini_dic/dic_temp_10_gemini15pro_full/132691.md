nondissidents:/ˌnɒndɪˈsɪdənts/ | n. | 非异议人士；顺从者 |  People who do not express disagreement with a political regime or policy.  Those who conform to the established system or authority. |  The government's propaganda was primarily aimed at the nondissidents, hoping to solidify their support. 政府的宣传主要针对非异议人士，希望巩固他们的支持。

The nondissidents in the country rarely spoke out against the ruling party.  这个国家的非异议人士很少公开反对执政党。| 近义词: conformists, loyalists, adherents | 反义词: dissidents, rebels, objectors | 词性: 名词

词源：non- (not) + dissident (person who publicly disagrees with an official policy, especially that of an authoritarian state)  来自拉丁语 dissidere,  dis- (apart) + sedere (to sit).  因此，nondissident 字面意思是“不分开坐的人”，引申为“不反对的人”。
