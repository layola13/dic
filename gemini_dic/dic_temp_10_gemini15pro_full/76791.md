gaminesses:/ˈɡeɪminəsɪz/
n.
野味; 腥膻味; 粗野
The quality of being gamy, having a strong, pungent flavor or odor, often associated with wild game or aged meat.  It can also refer to a rawness or coarseness of manner.
The gaminesses of the venison were too strong for some of the guests.  鹿肉的腥膻味对一些客人来说太浓了。
近义词: pungency, rankness, wildness, coarseness
反义词: blandness, mildness, tameness
