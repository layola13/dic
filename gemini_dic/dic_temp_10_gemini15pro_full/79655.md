glossolalia:/ˌɡlɒsəˈleɪliə/ (US) /ˌɡlɒsəˈleɪliə/ (UK)| n. | 说方言； glossolalia|Speaking in an unknown language, especially in religious worship. It is also known as speaking in tongues.|She experienced glossolalia during the religious service.  她在宗教仪式上说起了方言。|The pastor interpreted the glossolalia as a message from God. 牧师将这种方言解释为来自上帝的信息。|Synonyms: speaking in tongues, xenoglossy|Antonyms: articulate speech|noun
