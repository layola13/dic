amylobacter:/əˈmaɪləʊˌbæktər/ | n. |淀粉杆菌|A genus of anaerobic bacteria that produce butyric acid from carbohydrates, including starch.  They are commonly found in soil and the digestive tracts of animals.|Amylobacter are important for the degradation of complex carbohydrates in anaerobic environments. 淀粉杆菌对于厌氧环境中复杂碳水化合物的降解很重要。| Amylobacter species are being studied for their potential in biofuel production.  淀粉杆菌物种正在被研究其在生物燃料生产中的潜力。|近义词: Clostridium (in some contexts) |反义词: None (It's a specific genus of bacteria, not a concept with an opposite.) |词性:noun
