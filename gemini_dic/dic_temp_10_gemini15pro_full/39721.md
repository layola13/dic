consignors:/kənˈsaɪnərz/
n.
发货人；委托人；寄售人
People or companies who deliver goods to another party for sale.  The consignee sells the goods on behalf of the consignor.
The consignors shipped the goods by sea.  发货人通过海运发货。
The consignors agreed on a commission rate with the consignee. 发货人与收货人就佣金比例达成了一致。
synonyms: shippers, vendors, sellers, exporters
antonyms: consignees, buyers, importers
noun
