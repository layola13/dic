amusedly:/əˈmjuːzɪdli/ | adv. | 愉悦地，饶有兴致地 | In a way that shows you are being entertained or think something is funny. | She watched him amusedly. 她饶有兴致地望着他。| 近义词: mirthfully, playfully, humorously | 反义词: seriously, solemnly | 词性: 副词

词源: From amused + -ly.  "Amused" comes from the Old French "amuser" meaning "to divert, entertain," ultimately from the Latin "ad-" (to) + "musus" (muse).
