overtips:/ˈoʊvərˌtɪps/| v. |给太多小费；过度倾斜|He overtipped the waiter out of guilt. 他出于内疚给了服务员太多小费。 The boat overtipped and capsized. 船过度倾斜并翻了。|近义词：overpay|反义词：undertip|词性：动词

词源：over- (prefix) + tip (verb).  "Over-" signifies "too much" or "excessive".  "Tip" in this context means to give gratuity or to cause something to tilt.
