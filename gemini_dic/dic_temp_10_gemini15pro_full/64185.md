equerryship:/ˈɛkwərɪʃɪp/
|n.|侍从身份；马夫职位|The equerryship was a position of considerable responsibility.  侍从身份是一个责任重大的职位。|The young nobleman aspired to the equerryship of the king.  这位年轻的贵族渴望成为国王的侍从。|近义词：attendantship, service|反义词：mastership, leadership|词性：名词

Etymology:
From *equerry* + *-ship*.  *Equerry* comes from French *écuyer* "squire, shield-bearer," ultimately from Late Latin *scūtārius* "shield-bearer."  *-ship* is a suffix forming abstract nouns of state, condition, or quality.
