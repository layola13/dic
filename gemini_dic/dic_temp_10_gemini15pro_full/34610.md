cliché: /kliːˈʃeɪ/ (US), /ˈkliːʃeɪ/ (UK) | n. | 陈词滥调；老生常谈 | a phrase or opinion that is overused and betrays a lack of original thought. |  例句：The plot of the movie was full of clichés.  这部电影的情节充满了陈词滥调。| 近义词：platitude, banality, stereotype, truism | 反义词：originality, freshness, novelty | 词性: noun

cliché: /kliːˈʃeɪ/ (US), /ˈkliːʃeɪ/ (UK) | adj. | 陈腐的；老套的 | (of a phrase or opinion) overused and betrays a lack of original thought. |  例句：The ending of the novel was disappointingly cliché. 这部小说的结局令人失望地落入俗套。| 近义词：banal, hackneyed, stale, trite | 反义词：original, fresh, novel | 词性: adjective
