breezier:/ˈbriːziər/ | adj. | 微风轻拂的；凉爽的；轻松愉快的 | Characterized by a gentle breeze; refreshingly cool; lighthearted and carefree. |  The breezier air of the coast was a welcome change.  沿海凉爽的空气令人感到欣喜的变化。| synonyms: airy, breezy, cooler, fresher, lighter | antonyms: stuffy, stifling, hot, sultry, serious | adjective
