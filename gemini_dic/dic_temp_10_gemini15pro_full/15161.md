back-projection:/ˌbæk prəˈdʒekʃən/ | n. | 反投影，逆投影 | The process of projecting an image onto a surface from behind, often used in cinematography and image processing.  It involves reconstructing an image from its projections. |  The film used back-projection to create the illusion of the actors driving a car. 这部电影使用反投影技术来制造演员开车的错觉。

Back-projection was a common technique in older films. 反投影是老电影中常用的技术。 | synonyms:  retroprojection | antonyms: projection | noun
