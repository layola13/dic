mediated:/ˈmiːdieɪtɪd/| adj.|调解的，介导的|influenced, controlled, or caused by something|例句：The conflict was mediated by a neutral third party. 冲突是由中立的第三方调解的。|近义词：arbitrated, negotiated, facilitated, reconciled|反义词：unmediated, direct|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *mediatus*, past participle of *mediare* "to be in the middle, to divide in half," from *medius* "middle."
