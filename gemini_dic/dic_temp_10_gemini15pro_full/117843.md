mellon:/ˈmɛlən/| n. |瓜，甜瓜; (古) 朋友，伙伴|A large, round fruit with sweet juicy flesh and many seeds, or the plant on which this fruit grows. In archaic usage, it also means a friend or companion. |例句：The farmer grew watermelons and cantaloupes in his field.  农民在他的田里种植西瓜和哈密瓜。

例句：In Tolkien's works, "mellon" is the Elvish word for "friend". 在托尔金的作品中，“mellon”是精灵语中“朋友”的意思。|近义词：fruit, cantaloupe, watermelon, friend (archaic) |反义词:enemy (archaic)|词性:noun
