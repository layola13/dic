incentively:/ɪnˈsɛntɪvli/| adv.|激励地，有动机地|In a way that motivates or encourages someone to do something.|The company incentivized its employees to work harder by offering bonuses. 公司通过提供奖金来激励员工更加努力地工作。|近义词：motivatingly, encouragingly|反义词: discouragingly, dissuasively|词性:副词

词源: From "incentive" + "-ly". "Incentive" comes from Late Latin "incentivus" (inciting, stimulating), from "incent-" (past participle stem of "incendere" - to kindle, inflame) + "-ivus" (forming adjectives).
