assignable:/əˈsaɪnəbl/
adjective
可分配的；可指定的；可让渡的
Capable of being assigned or transferred.
The copyright is assignable.  这项版权可转让。
synonyms:transferable, conveyable
antonyms:nontransferable, non-assignable
词性:adjective
