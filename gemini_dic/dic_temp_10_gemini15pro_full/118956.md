meteoromancy:/ˌmiːtiəˈrɒmənsi/ | n. | 占星术，陨星占卜 | Divination by observation of meteors.  The practice of attempting to foretell the future by observing meteors. |  Meteoromancy was practiced in ancient Greece. 古代希腊人会进行陨星占卜。 |  Meteoromancy was a less common form of divination than astrology.  陨星占卜比占星术更不常见。 |  astromancy, divination | N/A | noun
