half-praised:/ˌhæfˈpreɪzd/
| adj. | 半赞扬的；赞扬不足的 |  Partially or incompletely praised; praised with reservations or faint praise. | The book received a half-praised review, acknowledging its strengths but also pointing out its weaknesses. 这本书收到了半赞扬的评论，肯定了它的优点，但也指出了它的缺点。 | Near Synonyms:  under-praised, faintly praised, lukewarmly received, moderately praised | Antonyms: highly praised, lauded, acclaimed, celebrated | adjective
