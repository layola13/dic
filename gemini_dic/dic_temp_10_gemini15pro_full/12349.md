ashanti: /əˈʃænti/ /æˈʃænti/
n.
阿散蒂（人）；阿散蒂语；加纳的民族和语言
A member of a people living mainly in Ghana.  The language of the Ashanti people, a member of the Kwa branch of the Niger-Congo family.
The Ashanti are known for their rich culture and history.  阿散蒂人以其丰富的文化和历史而闻名。
The Ashanti speak the Twi language.  阿散蒂人讲特威语。
近义词: Akan (a broader ethnic group that includes the Ashanti)
词性: noun
