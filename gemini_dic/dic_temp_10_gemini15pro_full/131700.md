noncondiment:/nɑːnˈkɑːndɪmənt/
n.
非调味品
Something that is not a condiment; a food that is not used to enhance the flavor of another food.
She brought a variety of snacks to the party, both condiments and noncondiments. 她带了各种零食去派对，包括调味品和非调味品。
近义词: staple food, main dish
反义词: condiment, seasoning
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + condiment.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not". "Condiment" comes from the Old French "condement", meaning "spice, seasoning," ultimately from the Latin "condire," meaning "to season, preserve."
