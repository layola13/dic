furless:/ˈfɜːrlɪs/| adj.|无毛的，没有皮毛的|Without fur; lacking a coat of fur.|The furless cat shivered in the cold. 这只无毛的猫在寒冷中瑟瑟发抖。|近义词：hairless, bald|反义词:furry, hairy|词性:形容词
词源: fur (n.) + -less (suffix).  "Fur" comes from Old French "forre," ultimately from Germanic. "-less" is a native English suffix meaning "without."
