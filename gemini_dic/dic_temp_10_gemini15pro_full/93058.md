idolatry: /aɪˈdɑːlətri/ (US) /aɪˈdɒlətri/ (UK)| n. |偶像崇拜；盲目崇拜|The excessive admiration of or devotion to something.  It refers to the worship of an idol or a false god, but can also be used figuratively to describe excessive devotion to anything.|例句：Their idolatry of the rock star was excessive. 他们对摇滚明星的崇拜太过火了。

例句：He was accused of idolatry for his devotion to money.  他因对金钱的热爱而被指责为拜金主义。

近义词：worship, adoration, reverence, devotion|反义词: disdain, contempt, aversion|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *idolātria*, from Greek *eidōlatreia*, from *eidōlon* (“image, idol”) + *-latreia* (“worship”).
