diplex:/ˈdaɪplɛks/ /ˈdiːpleks/| adj. n.|双工的，双路的；双工制，双路传输|A diplexer is a device that combines or separates two signals that operate at different frequencies.|The antenna uses a diplexer to transmit and receive simultaneously.|近义词：duplex, two-way|反义词：simplex|词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:  From di- (two) + -plex (fold, intertwined).  Ultimately from Latin *duplex, from duo (two) + plicō (to fold).
