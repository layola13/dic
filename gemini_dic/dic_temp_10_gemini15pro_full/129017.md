nishi:/ˈniːʃiː/ | n. | 西 (方向), 西方 |  A term, often used in Japanese, referring to the west or western direction.  Can also refer to things associated with the west, like Western culture or Western countries. |  The sun sets in the nishi.  太阳落在西边。 | Synonyms: west, occident | Antonyms: east, orient | noun

nishi:/ˈniːʃiː/ | adj. | 西方的 | Located in or toward the west; also, relating to the culture of Western countries. | We took a nishi route to avoid traffic. 我们走了一条西边的路来避开交通。 | Synonyms: western, occidental | Antonyms: eastern, oriental | adjective
