agonothete: /ˈæɡənəˌθiːt/ | n. |  裁判，仲裁者；（尤指古希腊运动会的）裁判员 |  A judge of contests, especially in ancient Greek games.  |  The agonothete oversaw the chariot races at the Olympic Games. 奥运会上的裁判员负责监督战车比赛。|  judge, umpire, referee, arbiter |  competitor, contestant, player |  noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ἀγωνοθέτης (agōnothétēs), from ἀγών (agṓn, “contest”) +‎ -θέτης (-thétēs, “setter, putter”).
