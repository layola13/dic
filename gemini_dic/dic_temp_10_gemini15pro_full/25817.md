burgherly: /ˈbɜːɡəli/ | adj.| 市民的，资产阶级的；小城市的，市井的 | Characteristic of a burgher or burghers; pertaining to the middle class, especially the comfortable and sometimes complacent middle class of a town. |  The burgherly concerns of the town council were focused on road repairs and tax rates.  市议会的市民关注点集中在道路维修和税率上。|  The new bakery catered to the burgherly tastes of the neighborhood. 新面包店迎合了附近居民的资产阶级口味。 | 近义词: bourgeois, middle-class, provincial, townish | 反义词: aristocratic, rural, bohemian | 词性: adjective

词源: From burgher + -ly.  Burgher comes from Middle Dutch *burger, from burg 'borough, town'.
