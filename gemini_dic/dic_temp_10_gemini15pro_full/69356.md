federated:/ˈfedəreɪtɪd/
adjective
联合的，联邦的
Constituting or formed by a federation: a federated republic.
例句：The federated states agreed to a common defense policy. 这些联邦州同意共同的防御政策。

例句：The company has a federated structure, with regional offices managing their own affairs. 这家公司拥有联邦制结构，各区域办事处管理自己的事务。

synonym: united, allied, joined, combined, confederated, leagued
antonym: independent, separate, autonomous, disunited
词性: adjective
