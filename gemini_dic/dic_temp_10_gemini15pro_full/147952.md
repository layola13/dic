|n.|过度的责备，过分的申斥|excessive reprimands|The child suffered from the overreprimands of his strict parents.  这个孩子遭受了来自他严厉父母的过分责备。|Synonyms: excessive criticism, overscolding, harsh rebuke|Antonyms: praise, commendation, encouragement|noun
