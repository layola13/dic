fertilize: /ˈfɜːrtəlaɪz/ (US) /ˈfɜːtɪlaɪz/ (UK)| vt.|施肥，使肥沃，使受精|To supply with nourishment for plants by adding suitable substances to the soil; to make fertile or productive; to cause fertilization to occur.|例句：He fertilized the lawn with bone meal. 他用骨粉给草坪施肥。

例句：Bees fertilize the flowers. 蜜蜂给花授粉。

近义词：enrich, nourish, inseminate|反义词:sterilize|词性：动词

fertilizer: /ˈfɜːrtəlaɪzər/ (US) /ˈfɜːtɪlaɪzər/ (UK)| n.|肥料|A substance added to soil to make it more fertile.|例句：The farmer spread fertilizer on the fields. 农民在地里撒肥料。

例句：Organic fertilizer is better for the environment.  有机肥料对环境更好。

近义词：manure, compost|反义词:|词性：名词
