militarizes:/ˈmɪlɪtəˌraɪzɪz/
verb
军事化
To equip or train for war; to adapt for military purposes.  To give a military character to something; to increase the military presence or influence in something.
The government militarizes the police force by providing them with military-grade weapons. 政府通过向警察部队提供军用级武器来使其军事化。

The small island nation began to militarize its borders in response to the growing threat from its neighbor.  这个岛国开始军事化其边界，以应对来自邻国的日益增长的威胁。

近义词: arm, fortify, strengthen
反义词: demilitarize, disarm, pacify
词性: verb
