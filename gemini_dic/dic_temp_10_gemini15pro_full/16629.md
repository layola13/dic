baronry:/ˈbærənri/
n.
男爵领地；男爵爵位
The domain or jurisdiction of a baron.
The baron consolidated his power over the baronry. 男爵巩固了他对男爵领地的权力。

近义词:barony, domain, fief, lordship
反义词: (No direct antonym for a territorial unit.  Perhaps "common land" if contrasted with privately held land.)
