crystallizes:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪzɪz/
verb
结晶；使明确；使具体化
cause to form crystals or assume crystalline form; make free from confusion or ambiguity; give definite or concrete form to
The solution crystallizes as it cools.  溶液冷却时会结晶。
The events of that day crystallized his determination to leave. 那一天的事件使他下定决心离开。
近义词: solidify, harden, concretize, define
反义词: melt, dissolve, liquefy
词性: verb
