metropolis: /məˈtrɒpəlɪs/ (UK) /məˈtrɑːpəlɪs/ (US)| n. |大都市；首府|A very large city, often the most important city in a large area or country. |例句：New York is a major metropolis. 纽约是一座主要的大都市。

例句：Tokyo is a bustling metropolis with a population of over 35 million. 东京是一个拥有超过3500万人口的繁华大都市。


近义词：city, urban area, megalopolis|反义词:village, hamlet, countryside|词性:noun
