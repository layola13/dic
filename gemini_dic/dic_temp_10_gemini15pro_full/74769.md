freeze-frame:/ˈfriːz freɪm/| n. |定格画面，静止帧|A still image taken from a film or video recording, or the effect of stopping a film or video recording on a particular image.|The director used a freeze-frame to emphasize the character's expression.|近义词：still image, still frame, snapshot|反义词:motion picture, moving image|词性:noun


freeze-frame:/ˈfriːz freɪm/| adj. |定格的|Relating to or characterized by a freeze-frame.|The freeze-frame effect highlighted the actor's tears.|近义词：static, still, motionless|反义词:moving, dynamic|词性:adjective


词源:  Compound of "freeze" (from Old French "freisier," meaning "to freeze") and "frame" (from Old English "framian," meaning "to be helpful, benefit").  The term gained popularity with the advent of film and video technology.
