dyestuff:/ˈdaɪstʌf/| n.|染料|A substance used to color materials such as fabrics, yarn, or hair.|The company specializes in manufacturing natural dyestuffs.|近义词：dye, pigment, coloring, colorant|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology:
From 'dye' + 'stuff'. 'Dye' comes from Old English 'deag', which in turn comes from Proto-Germanic '*daujaną'. 'Stuff' comes from Old French 'estoffe', ultimately from Latin 'stuppa' (tow, oakum).
