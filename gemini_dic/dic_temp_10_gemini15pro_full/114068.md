magnetizes:/ˈmæɡnɪˌtaɪzɪz/
verb
使磁化；吸引
To make something magnetic; to attract strongly, as if with a magnet.
The iron filings were magnetized by the bar magnet. 铁屑被条形磁铁磁化了。

He was magnetized by her beauty. 他被她的美貌所吸引。

Synonyms: attract, mesmerize, captivate, enthrall
Antonyms: repel, deter, disenchant
