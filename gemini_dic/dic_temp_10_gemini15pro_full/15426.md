bacterion:/bækˈtɪəriən/| n. |细菌; 微生物|A single-celled microscopic organism, typically one that causes disease.  A member of a large group of unicellular microorganisms that have cell walls but lack organelles and an organized nucleus, including some that can cause disease.|例句：The bacterion was identified as the cause of the infection.  这种细菌被确定为感染的病原体。|例句：The scientist studied the bacterion under a microscope.  科学家在显微镜下研究了这种细菌。|近义词：microbe, germ, bacterium|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑“macroorganism” 大型生物体)|词性:noun
