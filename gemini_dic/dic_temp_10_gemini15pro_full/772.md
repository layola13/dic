absolves:/əbˈzɑːlvz/ /əbˈsɒlvz/| v.|赦免，免除|To absolve someone from blame or responsibility means to declare them free from guilt or obligation.  It often implies a formal or authoritative declaration of innocence or forgiveness.|The court absolved him of all responsibility for the accident. 法院免除了他对此次事故的所有责任。

The priest absolved the sinner of his sins.  神父赦免了罪人的罪孽。|近义词：exonerate, acquit, pardon, forgive|反义词:blame, condemn, incriminate|词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The court absolves him of all charges. = 法院赦免他所有罪名。
The priest absolves the penitent. = 神父赦免忏悔者。

一般过去时:
The judge absolved the defendant of the crime. = 法官赦免了被告的罪行。
The church absolved him from his vows. = 教会免除了他的誓言。

现在完成时:
The jury has absolved her of any wrongdoing. = 陪审团已经免除了她的一切不当行为。
The confession has absolved him of his guilt. = 忏悔已经洗清了他的罪孽。

过去完成时:
The king had absolved the traitor before he died. = 国王在他死前赦免了叛徒。
The pope had absolved the knight of his sins before he went to battle. = 教皇在骑士上战场前赦免了他的罪过。

将来时:

被动语态:
He was absolved of all blame. = 他被免除了一切责备。
She was absolved by the court. = 她被法院赦免了。

疑问句:
Does the court absolve him of responsibility? = 法院是否免除他的责任？

否定句:
The court did not absolve him of guilt. = 法院没有免除他的罪责。
The priest doesn't absolve everyone who confesses. = 不是每个忏悔的人都会得到神父的赦免。
