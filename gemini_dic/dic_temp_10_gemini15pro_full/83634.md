haemoglobin:/ˈhiːməˌɡloʊbɪn/ (US), /ˈhiːməˌɡləʊbɪn/ (UK)| n. |血红蛋白|A red protein responsible for transporting oxygen in the blood of vertebrates. |例句：Hemoglobin carries oxygen from the lungs to the rest of the body. 血红蛋白将氧气从肺部输送到身体其他部位。

例句：A low hemoglobin level can lead to anemia. 血红蛋白水平低会导致贫血。|近义词：- (No direct synonym for the protein itself, but related terms include:  erythrocyte, red blood cell)|反义词: -|词性:noun
