envious:/ˈɛnviəs/| adj.|羡慕的，嫉妒的|Feeling or showing envy; wanting something that someone else has.|She was envious of her sister's new car. 她很羡慕她姐姐的新车。|Example: He was envious of his friend's success. 他嫉妒朋友的成功。|Synonyms: jealous, covetous, resentful|Antonyms: generous, admiring, appreciative|adjective


envious:/ˈɛnviəs/| adj.|令人羡慕的|So desirable as to arouse envy.|She has an envious position in the company. 她在公司里有一个令人羡慕的职位。|Example:  She lives in an envious location overlooking the ocean. 她住在一个可以俯瞰大海的令人羡慕的地方。|Synonyms: desirable, enviable, coveted|Antonyms: undesirable, unenviable|adjective
