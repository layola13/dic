confestly:/kənˈfɛstlɪ/ | adv. |坦白地，公开地 | Openly and frankly; without concealment. | He confessedly had little knowledge of the subject. 他坦率承认自己对这个话题知之甚少。 | Example 2: She confessedly admired his work ethic. 她公开地表达了对他的职业道德的钦佩。 | 近义词: frankly, openly, candidly, avowedly | 反义词: secretly, covertly, privately | 词性:副词
词源: From confess + -ly.  "Confess" comes from the Old French *confesser, which comes from the Latin confessus, the past participle of confiteri "to acknowledge, admit, confess," from com- "together" + fateri "to acknowledge."
