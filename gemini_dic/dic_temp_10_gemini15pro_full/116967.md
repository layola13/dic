mbe:/ɛm biː iː/ | abbreviation | 少数族裔企业（Minority Business Enterprise）| A business that is at least 51% owned and operated by a member of a minority group. | Example: The government is encouraging procurement from MBEs. 政府正在鼓励从少数族裔企业采购。|Synonym: minority-owned business| Antonym: N/A | noun
