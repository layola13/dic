outwatches:/ˌaʊtˈwɒtʃɪz/
verb
胜过…的观察力；比…更警觉
outsmart someone in watching or observing; to surpass someone in vigilance or alertness.
He outwatched the security guard and slipped into the building unnoticed. 他比保安更警觉，偷偷溜进了大楼。
近义词: outmaneuver, outwit, outsmart
反义词: be outsmarted, be outwitted
