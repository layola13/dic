hillinesses:/ˈhɪlinəsɪz/
n.
丘陵起伏；多丘陵的地带
The state of being hilly; a series of hills.
The hillinesses of the region made hiking challenging.  该地区的丘陵地貌使徒步旅行颇具挑战性。

The constant hillinesses of the countryside were breathtaking. 乡村连绵起伏的丘陵令人叹为观止。

近义词：undulations, unevenness, bumps, ridges
反义词：flatness, levelness, plainness
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From hilly + -ness + -es.  Hilly itself comes from hill + -y.  Ultimately from Old English *hyll (hill).
