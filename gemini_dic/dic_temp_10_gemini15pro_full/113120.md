lungfishes:/ˈlʌŋfɪʃɪz/
n.
肺鱼
A type of fish that can breathe both in and out of water, having both gills and a lung-like organ.
Lungfishes are ancient fish that can breathe air. 肺鱼是一种古老的鱼类，可以呼吸空气。
Lungfishes estivate during dry periods. 在干旱时期，肺鱼会进行夏眠。
Synonyms: Dipnoi
Antonyms: None
