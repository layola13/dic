From specific facts to a general conclusion.|Scientists proved the theory inductively. 科学家们通过归纳法证明了这个理论。|The conclusion was reached inductively from the available data.  这个结论是从现有数据归纳得出的。|Synonym: inferentially, by induction|Antonym: deductively, by deduction|词性:副词
