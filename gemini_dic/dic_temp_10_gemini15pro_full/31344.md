characteristical:/ˌkærəktəˈrɪstɪkəl/
adjective
特有的，典型的
Relating to, or revealing of, the character of a person or thing; typical; distinctive.
Her characteristical laugh made her easily recognizable in a crowd. 她特有的笑声让她在人群中很容易被认出来。
Synonyms: typical, representative, distinctive, characteristic
Antonyms: atypical, unusual, uncommon
词性: 形容词
