liquidate:/ˈlɪkwɪdeɪt/| v.|清算，清偿， ликвидировать (Russian)|To settle a debt, claim, or other obligation by converting (assets) into cash.  To eliminate, especially by killing. |The company was forced to liquidate its assets to pay off its debts. 公司被迫清算资产以偿还债务。

The dictator ordered his troops to liquidate the rebels. 独裁者命令他的军队消灭叛军。|近义词：sell off, dissolve, eliminate, abolish, exterminate|反义词：establish, found, create, build|词性:动词

词源: From Late Latin liquidare "to make liquid, to melt," from Latin liquidus "liquid, flowing, clear." The financial sense "to convert into cash" is from the 16th century.
