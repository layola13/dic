overfastidious:/ˌoʊvərˌfæˈstɪdiəs/ | adj. | 过度讲究的，过分挑剔的 | Excessively attentive to details and prone to criticism; difficult to please.  Characterized by excessive attention to detail and a tendency to find fault. |  He was so overfastidious about his appearance that he changed his outfit three times before leaving the house. 他对自己的外表过于挑剔，出门前换了三次衣服。|  She is overfastidious about grammar and constantly corrects other people's mistakes. 她对语法过于挑剔，总是纠正别人的错误。 | 近义词: fussy, finicky, picky, meticulous, scrupulous, exacting | 反义词: easygoing, undemanding, tolerant, casual | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fastidiosus*, from *fastidium* "loathing, disgust," from *fastus* "pride, arrogance" + *-idium* (noun suffix). The prefix *over-* intensifies the meaning, indicating an excessive degree of fastidiousness.
