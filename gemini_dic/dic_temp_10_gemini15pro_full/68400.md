falsehood:/ˈfɔːlshʊd/ /ˈfɑːlshʊd/| n. |谎言；谬误；虚假|An untrue statement; a lie.  A false belief.|例句：He was accused of spreading falsehoods about the company. 他被指控散布关于公司的谎言。

例句：The report exposed the falsehoods behind the government's claims. 这份报告揭露了政府声明背后的谎言。|近义词：lie, untruth, fabrication, fiction, fallacy |反义词:truth, verity, fact|词性:noun
