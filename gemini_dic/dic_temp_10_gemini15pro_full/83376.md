gyroscopically: /ˌdʒaɪrəˈskɒpɪkli/ (US) /ˌdʒaɪərəˈskɒpɪkli/ (UK)| adj.| 陀螺的；陀螺仪的|Relating to or characteristic of a gyroscope; using a gyroscope.
