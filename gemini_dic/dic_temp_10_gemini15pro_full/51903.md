dhing:/dɪŋ/
n.
thing (often used humorously or dismissively)
A thing; a whatsit; a whatchamacallit.
Example:  Pass me that dhing, will you?  把那个玩意儿递给我好吗？
Example:  I've got this dhing I need to fix. 我有个这玩意儿需要修理。
Synonym: thingamajig, thingamabob, gadget, whatchamacallit
Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a generic term like "dhing")
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
"Dhing" is a humorous, informal variant of "thing." It likely originated through playful alteration of the word, perhaps influenced by similar slang terms like "thingamajig" or "thingamabob."  It doesn't have a formal etymology like words with established historical roots.
