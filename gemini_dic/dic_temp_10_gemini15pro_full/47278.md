de-democratization:/diːˌdɪmɒkrətaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 去民主化，民主倒退 | The weakening or elimination of democratic institutions and practices within a society or political system.  |  The rise of authoritarianism led to a period of de-democratization in the country.  该国的威权主义兴起导致了一段时间的民主倒退。| synonyms: backsliding, regression, authoritarianism | antonyms: democratization, liberalization | noun
