gestures:/ˈdʒɛstʃərz/ /ˈdʒɛstʃɚz/| n.|姿势，手势；姿态|A movement of part of the body, especially a hand or the head, to express an idea or meaning.|He made a gesture of apology. 他做了一个道歉的手势。|近义词：motions, signs, signals|反义词: stillness, inaction|noun

gestures:/ˈdʒɛstʃərz/ /ˈdʒɛstʃɚz/| v.|做手势；示意|To make a gesture or gestures.|She gestured towards the door. 她朝门口做了个手势。|近义词：signal, motion, indicate|反义词: be still|verb
