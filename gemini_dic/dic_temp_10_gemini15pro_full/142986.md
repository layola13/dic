outbargaining:/ˌaʊtˈbɑːrɡənɪŋ/
verb
在讨价还价中胜过，比…会砍价
The act of bargaining more effectively than someone else, resulting in a better deal.
He outbargained the car salesman and got a great price. 他在讨价还价中胜过了汽车销售员，得到了一个很棒的价格。
近义词: outsmart, outmaneuver, outtrade
反义词: be outbargained, lose a negotiation
词性: verb (动词)
