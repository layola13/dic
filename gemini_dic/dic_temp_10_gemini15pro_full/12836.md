association:/əˌsoʊsiˈeɪʃən/ |əˌsəʊʃiˈeɪʃn/| n.|协会，联盟；联系，关联；联想|A group of people organized for a joint purpose.  A connection or relationship between people or things. The act of connecting something with something else in the mind.|例句：He joined the trade association. 他加入了贸易协会。

例句：There is a strong association between smoking and lung cancer. 吸烟和肺癌之间有很强的联系。|近义词：organization, union, connection, link, relationship|反义词：separation, disconnection|Noun
