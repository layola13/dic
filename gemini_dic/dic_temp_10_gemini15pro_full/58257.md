drummed:/drʌmd/
v.
敲鼓，打鼓
to play a drum or drums.
He drummed a rhythm on the table with his fingers. 他用手指在桌子上敲打着节奏。
近义词:beat, tap, rap
反义词: (无明显的反义词，可以考虑silence)
词性:动词


drummed:/drʌmd/
v.
反复灌输
to instill something by persistent repetition.
The teacher drummed the importance of grammar into the students. 老师反复向学生强调语法的重要性。
近义词:instill, inculcate, implant
反义词: (无明显的反义词)
词性:动词
