catapulted:/ˈkætəpʌltɪd/ | v. |  投射，发射，突然提升 | To hurl or launch something with force; to be suddenly propelled into a particular state or situation. | The novel's success catapulted her to fame. 这部小说的成功使她一举成名。

The incident catapulted him into the national spotlight.  这起事件使他突然成为全国关注的焦点。


| 近义词: launched, propelled, thrust, projected | 反义词: dropped, lowered, demoted | 词性: 动词
