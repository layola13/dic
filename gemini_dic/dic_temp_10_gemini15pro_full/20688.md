bladderwort:/ˈblædərˌwɜːrt/ (US) /ˈblædərˌwɔːt/ (UK)| n. |狸藻|A free-floating or submerged aquatic plant with small, bladder-like traps for catching insects and other small aquatic animals.|
The bladderwort is a fascinating carnivorous plant. 狸藻是一种迷人的食肉植物。

The pond was covered in bladderwort. 池塘里长满了狸藻。

近义词: carnivorous plant 食肉植物

反义词:  (No direct antonym as it's a specific plant.  Could consider non-carnivorous aquatic plant)  非食肉水生植物


词性: noun
