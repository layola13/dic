dissentient:/dɪˈsɛntiənt/| adj. n.| 不同意的，持异议的；持异议者| expressing or holding an opinion different from the majority; a person who opposes a majority or official opinion|  Two judges recorded a dissenting opinion. 两位法官记录了不同意见。|synonyms: disagreeing, dissenting, nonconforming, opposing, objecting | antonyms: agreeing, concurring, assenting, consenting| adjective, noun

dissentient:/dɪˈsɛntiənt/| n.| 持异议者| a person who opposes a majority or official opinion|  The dissentients were overruled.  反对者被否决了。|synonyms: objector, dissenter, protester, opponent, critic | antonyms: supporter, advocate, proponent, backer| noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin dissentientem (nominative dissentiens), present participle of dissentire (“to disagree”), from dis- (“apart, away”) + sentire (“to feel, think”).
