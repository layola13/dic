fee:/fiː/
n.|费用，酬金|A payment made to a professional person or to a professional or public body in exchange for advice or services.|The lawyer's fee was very high. 律师的费用很高。|charge, cost, price|gratuity (小费, 赏钱, 贈品)|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fee*, from Old French *fie*, from Medieval Latin *feudum*, meaning "fief," "feudal estate."  Originally, a fee was the land granted to a vassal in return for service. The meaning later shifted to the payment itself.
