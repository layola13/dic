carte:/kɑːrt/| n.| 菜单; 地图; 纸牌; |A menu; a map; a playing card.|例句：The restaurant's carte offered a wide variety of dishes.  这家餐厅的菜单提供各种各样的菜肴。

例句：He spread the carte across the table and pointed to our destination. 他把地图铺在桌子上，指着我们的目的地。|近义词：menu, list (for menu); map, chart (for map); card (for playing card)|反义词: None|词性:noun


carte:/kɑːrt blɑ̃ʃ/| n.|全权委托; 空白支票|Unconditional authority; complete freedom to act as one wishes.|例句：He gave the designer carte blanche to remodel the entire house. 他给设计师全权委托改造整栋房子。

例句：She was given carte blanche to choose the actors for the play. 她被全权委托选择这出戏的演员。|近义词：free rein, blank check, full authority|反义词: restriction, limitation|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French "carte," from Latin "charta," meaning "paper, papyrus leaf."  Ultimately from Greek "khártēs," meaning "papyrus leaf."
