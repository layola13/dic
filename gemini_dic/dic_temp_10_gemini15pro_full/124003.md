mornward:/ˈmɔːrnwərd/| adv.| 向早晨，向着早晨的方向|Moving or facing towards the morning or east.  It suggests a direction or orientation related to the morning. While not commonly used, it conveys a poetic or literary sense of moving towards the dawn or the beginning of the day.|The traveler journeyed mornward, eager to greet the sunrise. 旅行者向早晨的方向走去，渴望迎接日出。|近义词：eastward, dawnward|反义词: westward, eveningward|词性:副词
