belligerent:/bəˈlɪdʒərənt/| adj. n.|好战的，交战的；交战国，交战者|inclined to or exhibiting assertiveness, hostility, or combativeness.|He gave me a belligerent stare. 他挑衅地瞪了我一眼。|近义词：aggressive, hostile, warlike, pugnacious|反义词：peaceful, conciliatory, amicable|词性:形容词, 名词
词源: Late Middle English: from Latin belligerent- ‘waging war’, from the verb belligerare, from bellum ‘war’ + gerere ‘wage’.
