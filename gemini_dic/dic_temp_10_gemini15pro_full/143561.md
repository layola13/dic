outhires:/ˈaʊtˌhaɪərz/
verb
外包；外聘
To hire external contractors or companies to perform tasks or services that were previously done by in-house employees.
The company outhires its customer service operations to a call center in India.  这家公司将其客户服务业务外包给印度的一家呼叫中心。
The firm outhires some of its IT work. 该公司将其部分IT工作外包。
近义词:outsources, subcontracts
反义词:insources
