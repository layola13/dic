hymenopterous:/ˌhaɪmɪˈnɒptərəs/| adj.|膜翅目的|Belonging or pertaining to the Hymenoptera, an order of insects including bees, wasps, ants, and sawflies, characterized by two pairs of membranous wings.|例句：The hymenopterous insect injected venom into its prey.  这只膜翅目昆虫将毒液注入猎物体内。

例句：Many hymenopterous species play crucial roles in pollination. 许多膜翅目物种在授粉中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：insect-like, apian (specifically for bees)|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective
