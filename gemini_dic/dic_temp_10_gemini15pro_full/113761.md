macrophysical:/ˌmækroʊˈfɪzɪkəl/
adjective
宏观的，宏观物理的
Relating to large-scale physical properties and processes, as opposed to microscopic ones.
The macrophysical properties of the atmosphere, such as temperature and pressure, influence weather patterns. 大气的宏观物理特性，例如温度和压力，会影响天气模式。
The study focused on the macrophysical characteristics of the material, rather than its atomic structure. 这项研究的重点是材料的宏观物理特性，而不是其原子结构。
近义词: macroscopic, large-scale
反义词: microscopic, small-scale
词性: adjective

词源:
"macro-" (Greek: μακρός, makros, meaning "long", "large") + "physical"
