hose's:/hoʊziz/
Noun
软管（hose）的所有格形式；软管的
The hose's nozzle was broken.
软管的喷嘴坏了。
The fireman attached the hose's coupling to the hydrant.
消防员把软管的接头连接到消防栓上。
Synonyms: hose pipe's,  garden hose's (depending on context)
Antonyms: N/A (Possessive forms don't typically have antonyms)
词性: 名词所有格

Etymology:
From Middle English *hose, from Old French hose, from Frankish *hosa, from Proto-Germanic *husǭ. Related to Dutch hoos, German Hose. The '-s' is the possessive marker in English.
