inconfutable:/ˌɪnkənˈfjuːtəbl/ | adj. | 不可辩驳的，无可争议的 | Not capable of being refuted or disproved; indisputable. | The evidence against him was inconfutable.  证据对他非常不利，无可辩驳。| Synonyms: indisputable, undeniable, irrefutable, unassailable | Antonyms: disputable, debatable, questionable, refutable | adjective
