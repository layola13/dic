camerawork: /ˈkæmərəwɜːrk/ (US) /ˈkæmrəwɜːk/ (UK)| n.|摄影技巧，运镜，镜头运用|The camerawork in that film was stunning, making even mundane scenes look beautiful. 那部电影的摄影技巧令人惊叹，即使是平凡的场景看起来也很美。|The documentary's camerawork was shaky and amateurish.  这部纪录片的运镜晃动而且不专业。|近义词：cinematography, photography, filming|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology:  "camera" + "work". "Camera" ultimately derives from Latin "camera" meaning "vaulted room" (later used for the camera obscura).  "Work" comes from Old English "weorc."
