descried:/dɪˈskraɪd/
verb
发现；看出；辨别出
To catch sight of something, especially something distant or obscure.
From a distance, I descried two figures walking towards the house. 我远远地看到两个人正朝着房子走来。

The sailor descried a ship on the horizon. 水手在地平线上发现了一艘船。

spot, perceive, discern, espy
overlook, miss, fail to see
词性:动词
