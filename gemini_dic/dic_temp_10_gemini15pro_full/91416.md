hyena:/haɪˈiːnə/ /haɪˈɛnə/| n.|鬣狗|A dog-like carnivorous mammal of Africa and southern Asia, having coarse fur, a sloping back, and large teeth that are adapted to crushing bones.|Hyenas are known for their scavenging habits. 鬣狗以其食腐的习性而闻名。|Synonyms: scavenger, predator|Antonyms: prey|Noun
