hyperbaric:/ˌhaɪpərˈbærɪk/| adj.|高压的，超过常压的|Relating to or involving a gas at a pressure greater than atmospheric pressure.|例句：Hyperbaric oxygen therapy can be used to treat carbon monoxide poisoning. 高压氧疗法可以用来治疗一氧化碳中毒。

例句：Divers often work in hyperbaric conditions. 潜水员经常在高压环境下工作。

近义词：pressurized, compressed|反义词：hypobaric|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From hyper- + baric, from Greek baros "weight, pressure."  The prefix "hyper-" means "over, above, beyond."
