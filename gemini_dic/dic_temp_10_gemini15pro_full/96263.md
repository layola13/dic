indictability:/ɪnˌdɪktəˈbɪləti/
n.
可起诉性；可被控告性
The quality or state of being indictable.  The possibility of being formally accused of a crime.
The indictability of the offense is dependent on the evidence.  该罪行的可起诉性取决于证据。
The prosecutor is assessing the indictability of the suspect. 检察官正在评估嫌疑人的可被控告性。
Near Synonyms: chargeability, accussability, impeachability
Antonyms:  immunity, impunity
