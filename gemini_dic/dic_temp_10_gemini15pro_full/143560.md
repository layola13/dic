outhired:/ˌaʊtˈhaɪərd/
verb
外包雇佣；以更低的价格雇佣外部人员
The company outhired its IT department to a firm in India. 这家公司将其IT部门外包给了一家印度公司。
The factory outhired some of its production to a contractor to save costs.  这家工厂将其部分生产外包给承包商以节省成本。
近义词: outsourced, subcontracted
反义词: insourced
词性: verb
