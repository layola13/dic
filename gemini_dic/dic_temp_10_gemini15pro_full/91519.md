hyoidean:/haɪˈɔɪdiən/
adjective
舌骨的；与舌骨相关的
Relating to the hyoid bone, a U-shaped bone in the neck that supports the tongue.
The hyoidean muscles are involved in swallowing.  舌骨肌参与吞咽。
The doctor examined the patient's hyoidean apparatus. 医生检查了病人的舌骨器官。
近义词:  lingual (related to the tongue)
反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "non-hyoidean" could be considered in specific contexts)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hyoeides*  meaning "U-shaped," from *hy* "upsilon" (the Greek letter 'Y') + *-oeides*  "shaped like."
