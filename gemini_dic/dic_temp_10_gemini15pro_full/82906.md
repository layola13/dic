gumbo:/ˈɡʌmboʊ/ (US), /ˈɡʌmbəʊ/ (UK)| n.|秋葵浓汤，浓稠的炖菜|A stew thickened with okra or filé powder, often containing meat or shellfish and vegetables, typically served with rice. |例句：My grandmother makes a delicious shrimp gumbo.  我祖母做了一份美味的虾秋葵浓汤。

例句：Gumbo is a popular dish in Louisiana. 秋葵浓汤是路易斯安那州的流行菜。|近义词：stew, soup|反义词:|名词
