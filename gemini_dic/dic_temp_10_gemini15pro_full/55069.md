disregardful:/ˌdɪsrɪˈɡɑːrdfəl/
adjective
漠视的，不顾的，不尊重的
showing a lack of consideration or respect; ignoring
例句：He was disregardful of his wife's feelings. 他漠视妻子的感受。

例句：She was utterly disregardful of the consequences. 她完全不顾后果。

近义词：inconsiderate, negligent, unmindful, heedless, indifferent
反义词：considerate, respectful, attentive, mindful

词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation) + regardful.  "Regard" comes from Old French "regarder" meaning "to look at attentively, watch, guard, keep, consider."
