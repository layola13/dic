ethnologist:/ɛθˈnɒlədʒɪst/ /ɛθˈnɑːlədʒɪst/| n. |民族学家|A scholar who studies the characteristics of various peoples and the differences and relationships between them.|例句：The ethnologist spent years living with the tribe to study their customs.  这位民族学家花了数年时间与该部落一起生活，以研究他们的习俗。

例句：The ethnologist compared the marriage rituals of different cultures. 这位民族学家比较了不同文化的婚姻仪式。

|近义词：anthropologist, cultural anthropologist|反义词: None|词性: Noun
