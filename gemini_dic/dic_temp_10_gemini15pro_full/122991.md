monandry:/məˈnændri/| n. |一雄一雌；单雄性；一夫一妻制|The condition or practice of having only one husband or male mate at a time.  It refers to a mating system where one male mates with only one female during a breeding season or even for life.|Monandry is rare in mammals but is seen in some bird species. 一雄一雌在哺乳动物中很少见，但在一些鸟类中可以看到。|Synonym: monogamy|Antonym: polyandry|Noun
