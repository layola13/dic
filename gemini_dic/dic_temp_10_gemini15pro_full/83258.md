gymnocytode:/ˌdʒɪmnəˈsaɪtoʊd/| n. |裸露细胞；裸原质团|A cell, such as a slime mold, that is not enclosed within a cell wall.  A mass of protoplasm not enclosed by a cell wall.|例句：The gymnocytode moved slowly across the agar. 裸露细胞缓慢地穿过琼脂。

例句：Gymnocytodes are characteristic of certain slime molds.  裸原质团是某些黏菌的特征。|近义词：protoplast, plasmodium|反义词: N/A |词性:noun

Etymology: From Ancient Greek γυμνός (gumnós, “naked, bare”) + κύτος (kútos, “hollow vessel, container; a cell”) + -ode.
