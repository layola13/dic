de-polishing:/diːˈpɒlɪʃɪŋ/
verb
去光，磨光，抛光（去除光泽）
The process of removing the polish or shine from a surface.
The jeweler was de-polishing the ring to prepare it for a new setting. 珠宝商正在将戒指去光，以便为其镶嵌新的底座。
dulling, matting, roughening
polishing, shining, burnishing
verb

de-polishing:/diːˈpɒlɪʃɪŋ/
noun
去光，磨光，抛光（去除光泽的动作或过程）
The action or process of removing the polish or shine from a surface.
The de-polishing of the car's paint made it look dull. 汽车油漆的去光处理使其看起来很暗淡。
dulling, matting, roughening
polishing, shining, burnishing
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "remove, reverse") + polishing (present participle of polish, from Old French polir, from Latin polire "to smooth, polish").
