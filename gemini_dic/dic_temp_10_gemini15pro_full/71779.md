fleck:/flek/
n.|斑点，雀斑，少量|A small mark or spot, especially one of a different color; a tiny particle or bit.|The horse's coat was a rich brown with flecks of gold.|speck, spot, dot|patch, expanse
v.|使…有斑点，点缀|To mark or dot with small spots or patches.|The baker flecked the pastry with chocolate.|spot, dot, speckle|clear, erase

词性: 名词, 动词

词源: From Middle English *flekke*, from Old Norse *flekkr* ("spot, stain"). Cognate with Swedish *fläck* ("spot, stain"), Danish *plet* ("spot").
