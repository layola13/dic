measuring-up:/ˈmɛʒərɪŋ ʌp/ |phrasal verb| 衡量，测量；符合，达到标准 |The act of assessing something or someone against a standard. It can refer to literal measurement or a more figurative assessment of abilities or qualities.|  The tailor was measuring him up for a new suit.  裁缝正在给他量尺寸做一套新西装。
