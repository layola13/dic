aerodynamics:/ˌɛroʊdaɪˈnæmɪks/ /ˌeərəʊdaɪˈnæmɪks/| n.|空气动力学|The study of the properties of moving air, and especially of the interaction between air and solid bodies moving through it.|The car's aerodynamics have been improved to reduce drag. 这辆车的空气动力学性能得到了改进，以减少阻力。

The aerodynamics of the plane are crucial for its performance.  这架飞机的空气动力学特性对其性能至关重要。|近义词：fluid dynamics, gas dynamics|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From aero- (air) + -dynamics (study of forces and motion).  Ultimately from Greek ἀήρ (aēr, “air”) and δύναμις (dýnamis, “power”).

USAGE->
The principles of aerodynamics are essential for aircraft design. = 空气动力学原理对于飞机设计至关重要。

Aerodynamics plays a key role in the performance of Formula 1 cars. = 空气动力学在F1赛车的性能中起着关键作用。
