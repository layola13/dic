paradigmatical:/ˌpærədɪɡˈmætɪkl/ | adj. | 范例的；典范的； paradigm的形容词形式|Serving as a typical example of something. |  This case is paradigmatical of the problems we face.  这个案例是我们面临的问题的典型例子。|  The company's success is paradigmatical of the benefits of innovation. 该公司的成功是创新带来好处的典范。|exemplary, typical, model, classic | atypical, unusual, irregular | adjective

paradigm:/ˈpærədaɪm/ | n. | 范例；典范；样式；词形变化表 | A typical example or pattern of something; a model. |  A new paradigm of leadership. 一种新的领导模式。 | The discovery of DNA was a paradigm shift in biology. DNA的发现是生物学中的一个范式转变。| model, example, pattern, archetype | anomaly, exception, irregularity | noun
