innit:/ˈɪnɪt/| contraction|是不是; 对不对; 是吧|It's a contraction of "isn't it," used as a tag question to seek confirmation or agreement.|例句：It's cold today, innit? 今天很冷，是不是？|近义词：right?, yeah?, isn't it?|反义词:|contraction

注意事项:

"innit" 主要用于非正式场合，尤其在英国英语口语中常见。在正式场合或书面语中应避免使用。  It's generally considered informal and even slang.  While geographically associated with Britain, it's used by some other English speakers too.  It's NOT generally used in American English.  Avoid using "innit" in formal situations.
