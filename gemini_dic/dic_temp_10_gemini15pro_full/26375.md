by-law:/ˈbaɪˌlɔː/ (US) /ˈbaɪˌlɔː/ (UK)| n. |规章制度；地方法规；内部章程|A rule made by a local authority or by a corporation or society to control its members or the people living in a particular area.|The company’s by-laws state that all members must pay their dues by January 1st. 公司的规章制度规定所有成员必须在1月1日前缴纳会费。|Synonym: regulation, ordinance, rule, statute|Antonym: deregulation|noun


by-law:/ˈbaɪˌlɔː/ (US) /ˈbaɪˌlɔː/ (UK)| n. |规章制度；地方法规；内部章程|A rule or law made by a local council or other organization for its own area or members.|The club’s by-laws prohibit smoking on the premises. 俱乐部的规章制度禁止在场所内吸烟。|Synonym: rule, regulation, ordinance|Antonym: lawlessness|noun

Etymology:  From Middle English *bilawe, equivalent to *bi (beside, by) + law.  Originally referred to supplementary laws or regulations, literally "by the side of" the main law.
