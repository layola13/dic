fahrenheit:/ˈfærənhaɪt/ /ˈfɑːrənhaɪt/| n. adj.|华氏度；华氏温度计|A temperature scale in which water freezes at 32 degrees and boils at 212 degrees.|The temperature outside is 80 degrees Fahrenheit. 外面的温度是80华氏度。|Synonym: Fahrenheit scale, Fahrenheit thermometer|Antonym: Celsius, Centigrade|noun, adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
Named after Daniel Gabriel Fahrenheit (1686–1736), a German physicist who invented the alcohol thermometer and mercury thermometer.
