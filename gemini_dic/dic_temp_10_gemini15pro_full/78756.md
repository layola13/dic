gild: /ɡɪld/ | v. | 镀金，虚饰，使光彩夺目 | to cover thinly with gold; to give a specious or deceptively attractive appearance to |  The frame was gilded with 24-carat gold.  画框镀了24克拉的黄金。

The setting sun gilded the clouds. 落日给云朵镀上了一层金边。 | 近义词： adorn, embellish, decorate, enhance | 反义词： tarnish, dull, spoil, mar | 词性: 动词

gild: /ɡɪld/ | n. | 行会，协会，同业公会 | an association of persons of the same trade or pursuits, formed to protect mutual interests and maintain standards | The goldsmiths' gild was powerful in the Middle Ages.  中世纪金匠行会很有势力。 | 近义词： association, union, society, league | 反义词：  (无明确的反义词) | 词性: 名词
