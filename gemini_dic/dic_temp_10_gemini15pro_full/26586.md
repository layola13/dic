cabriolet: /ˌkæbrioʊˈleɪ/ (US) /ˌkæbriəˈleɪ/ (UK)| n. |敞篷车|A car with a retractable roof, often folding or collapsing into the rear deck or boot.|例句：He drove his cabriolet along the coast. 他沿着海岸驾驶他的敞篷车。

例句：She bought a new red cabriolet. 她买了一辆新的红色敞篷车。|近义词：convertible, roadster, drop-top|反义词: sedan, coupe, hardtop|词性: noun
