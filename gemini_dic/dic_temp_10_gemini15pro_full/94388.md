implementable:/ɪmˌplɪˈmɛntəbl/
adjective
可实施的，可执行的
capable of being implemented; practicable
The plan is implementable with the resources we have.  这个计划用我们现有的资源是可以实施的。
The recommendations in the report are not immediately implementable. 报告中的建议无法立即执行。
synonym: achievable, feasible, practicable, viable, workable
antonym: impracticable, infeasible, unachievable, unworkable
词性: 形容词
