haplessness:/ˈhæpləsnəs/| n.|不幸；倒霉；厄运|The state of being unlucky or unfortunate.|His life was marked by a series of haplessness and misfortune. 他的一生充满了不幸和厄运。|近义词：misfortune, bad luck, adversity|反义词：fortune, good luck, prosperity|词性:名词
Etymology: From hapless + -ness.  Hapless from hap (n.) "chance, fortune" + -less.  Hap from Old Norse *happ.
