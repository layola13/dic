fordo:/ˈfɔːrdoʊ/| n.|毁灭，破坏|The act of ruining or destroying something.|The fordo of the city after the earthquake was complete. 地震后这座城市彻底毁灭了。|Synonyms: ruin, destruction, devastation|Antonyms: construction, creation, restoration|noun
