banquette:/bæŋˈkɛt/ | n. |长凳，宴会 |A long upholstered bench, often built into a wall, especially in a restaurant.  A feast; a banquet.| The restaurant had comfortable banquettes along one wall.  这家餐厅沿着一面墙摆放着舒适的长凳。| synonyms: bench, seat, sofa, couch | antonyms: chair, stool | noun

banquette:/bæŋˈkɛt/ | n. | 宴会 |A ceremonial dinner party for numerous people.|  The king held a lavish banquette in honor of the visiting dignitaries. 国王为来访的贵宾举办了盛大的宴会。 | synonyms: feast, dinner, celebration | antonyms: snack, fast | noun
