niagara:/naɪˈæɡərə/ | n. |尼亚加拉|Niagara refers to the Niagara River, Niagara Falls, or the region surrounding them. It's derived from the Iroquois word "Onguiaahra," meaning "the strait."|We visited Niagara Falls last summer. 我们去年夏天参观了尼亚加拉大瀑布。|Near Niagara Falls, there are many wineries. 尼亚加拉瀑布附近有很多酿酒厂。|Synonyms: Niagara River, Niagara Falls, the Falls |Antonyms: N/A |noun
