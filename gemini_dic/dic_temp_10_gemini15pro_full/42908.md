coussinet:/ku.si.nɛ/(French)| n.|轴瓦; 轴承衬套; 垫块; 垫圈|A bearing pad or bushing, typically made of a soft metal or other material, designed to reduce friction and wear between moving parts.  It also refers to a cushion or pad.|例句：The coussinet in the engine needs to be replaced. 发动机里的轴瓦需要更换。

例句：He used a coussinet to protect the furniture from scratches. 他用垫块来保护家具不被刮伤。|近义词：bearing, bushing, pad, washer|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Old French *coissinet, diminutive of coisse ("cuisse," thigh), from Latin coxa (hip).  The semantic shift likely occurred due to the resemblance of a bearing pad to the shape of a thigh.
