anteing:/ˈæntiɪŋ/
verb
下注；预付赌注金
The act of placing an initial bet or wager, especially in poker.
He was anteing up $100 a hand. 他每局下注 100 美元。
Synonyms: betting, wagering
Antonyms: folding, withdrawing
词性: 动词
