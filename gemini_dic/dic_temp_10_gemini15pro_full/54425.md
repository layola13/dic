disinfecting:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛktɪŋ/
verb
消毒; 给…杀菌
The process of cleaning something with a chemical to destroy bacteria.
例句：They are disinfecting the hospital to prevent the spread of infection. 他们正在给医院消毒以防止感染蔓延。

例句：Disinfecting surfaces regularly is crucial to maintaining a hygienic environment.  定期对表面进行消毒对于维护卫生环境至关重要。

近义词：sterilizing, sanitizing
反义词：contaminating, polluting
词性：动词 (present participle of disinfect)


disinfect:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛkt/
verb
消毒; 给…杀菌
To clean something with a chemical to destroy bacteria.
例句：We need to disinfect the wound to prevent infection. 我们需要对伤口进行消毒以防止感染。

例句：The staff disinfected the entire building after the outbreak.  疫情爆发后，工作人员对整栋楼进行了消毒。

近义词：sterilize, sanitize
反义词：contaminate, pollute
词性：动词

词源：
From dis- (expressing negation) + infect.
