merismatic:/ˌmɛrɪsˈmætɪk/| adj.|分生组织的，分裂的|Of, relating to, or designating a meristem; capable of dividing and producing new cells.|The merismatic tissues of plants are responsible for growth.|近义词：dividing, proliferating|反义词:non-dividing, differentiating|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *meristos* "divided," from *merizein* "to divide."
