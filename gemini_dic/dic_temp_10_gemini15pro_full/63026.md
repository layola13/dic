enragement:/ɪnˈreɪdʒmənt/| n. |愤怒，暴怒|The state of being enraged; fury.|His enragement at their betrayal was palpable. 他对他们背叛的愤怒显而易见。|The constant delays caused him considerable enragement.  不断的延误让他相当愤怒。|近义词：fury, rage, wrath, anger, indignation|反义词：calmness, composure, serenity|词性:名词
词源: From enrage + -ment,  enrage (v.) "to make furious," from en- (1) "make, put in" + rage (n.).
