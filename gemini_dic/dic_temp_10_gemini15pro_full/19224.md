bezoartic:/ˌbiːzəʊˈɑːtɪk/ | adj. | 结石的；由毛团形成的 |  Relating to or resembling a bezoar (a mass of undigested material found in the stomach or intestines). | The surgeon removed a large bezoartic mass from the patient's stomach. 外科医生从病人的胃里取出了一大块毛团结石。| Synonyms: calculous, concretionary | Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider "digestible" in a broader context) | Adjective


bezoar:/ˈbiːzɔːr/ | n. | 胃石，毛团 | A hard mass of undigested material, such as hair or fiber, that forms in the stomach or intestines. |  She had a bezoar in her stomach from swallowing her hair. 她的胃里因为吞食头发而形成了毛团。 | Synonyms: hairball (specifically for hair), concretion | Antonyms: (No direct antonyms) | Noun
