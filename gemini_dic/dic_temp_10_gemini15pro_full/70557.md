filipendulous:/fɪlɪˈpɛndʒʊləs/| adj.|悬垂的，下垂的|Hanging down, drooping; often used to describe flowers or fruits that hang by a slender stalk.|The willow tree's filipendulous branches swayed gently in the breeze. 柳树悬垂的枝条在微风中轻轻摇曳。|The ripe, filipendulous mangoes looked delicious. 成熟的、垂下来的芒果看起来很美味。|近义词：pendulous, drooping, hanging|反义词：erect, upright|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Latin *filipendulus*, from *filum* (thread) + *pendulus* (hanging).
