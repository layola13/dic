malleably:/ˈmæliəbli/ | adv. | 可锻地，延展地，易适应地 | In a malleable manner; capable of being shaped or formed, easily influenced or adaptable. | The metal was so malleable that it could be easily shaped into intricate designs.  这种金属延展性很好，可以很容易地塑造成复杂的图案。 | 近义词: flexibly, pliantly, adaptably | 反义词: rigidly, inflexibly, stubbornly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *malleabilis, from Latin malleus (“hammer”) + -abilis.
