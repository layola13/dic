pachometer: /pəˈkɪmɪtər/ | n. | 角膜厚度计 | An instrument used to measure the thickness of the cornea, the transparent front part of the eye. | The ophthalmologist used a pachometer to measure the patient's corneal thickness before the LASIK procedure. 眼科医生在LASIK手术前使用角膜厚度计测量患者的角膜厚度。|  The doctor used the pachometer to assess the patient's risk for glaucoma. 医生使用角膜厚度计评估患者患青光眼的风险。 | Synonyms: corneal thickness gauge | Antonyms: None | noun
