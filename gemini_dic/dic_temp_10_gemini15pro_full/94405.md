implicating:/ˈɪmplɪˌkeɪtɪŋ/
verb
暗示；牵连；表明（某事）与（不好的事）有关
suggesting that someone is involved in something bad or criminal
The evidence implicates him in the robbery.  证据表明他参与了抢劫。
synonyms:incriminate, involve, connect, link
antonyms:exonerate, absolve
词性:v.


implicating:/ˈɪmplɪˌkeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
牵连的；暗示的
Connecting or involving something bad or criminal
The implicating evidence led to his arrest. 牵连的证据导致了他的逮捕。
synonyms:incriminating, suggestive
antonyms:exonerating, absolving
词性:adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *implicatus, past participle of implicare (“to involve, entangle”), from in- (“in, on, upon”) + plicare (“to fold”).
