hydropathist:/ˌhaɪdrəˈpæθɪst/ | n. | 水疗法医师；水疗拥护者 | A person who practices or advocates hydropathy. |例句：The hydropathist recommended cold baths and compresses. 水疗法医师建议冷水浴和敷布。
