nonfacetiously:/ˌnɑːnfeɪˈsiːʃəsli/
adverb
并非开玩笑地；认真地
Seriously; not jokingly.
He nonfacetiously claimed to be the best chess player in the world. 他认真地声称自己是世界上最好的棋手。
Synonym: seriously, earnestly, genuinely
Antonym: facetiously, jokingly, playfully
词性: 副词
