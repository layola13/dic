myxomycetes:/ˌmɪksəˈmaɪsiːtiːz/| n. |黏菌|A class of primitive organisms containing the true slime molds. They are characterized by a vegetative phase consisting of a naked, multinucleate mass of protoplasm called a plasmodium.|Myxomycetes are fascinating organisms that can be found in damp environments. 黏菌是一种迷人的生物，可以在潮湿的环境中找到。|The forest floor was covered with various myxomycetes after the rain. 雨后，森林地面上覆盖着各种各样的黏菌。|Synonyms: slime molds, plasmodial slime molds|Antonyms: None|Noun
