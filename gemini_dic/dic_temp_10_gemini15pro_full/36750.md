colloquist:/ˈkɒləkwɪst/ (US) /ˈkɑːləkwɪst/ (UK)| n. |腹语表演者；口技演员|A person who performs ventriloquism, the art of speaking without moving their lips, making it seem like a puppet or other object is talking. |例句：The colloquist amazed the children with his dummy's witty remarks. 腹语表演者用他假人的诙谐言语使孩子们惊叹不已。

例句：The skilled colloquist made the puppet seem to have a life of its own.  这位技艺高超的腹语表演者使木偶看起来栩栩如生。|近义词：ventriloquist|反义词: listener, audience|词性: noun
