cluckhead:/ˈklʌkˌhed/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A foolish or stupid person.  This word is informal and often used as a mild insult.|例句：Don't be a cluckhead and forget your keys again.  别再犯傻把钥匙忘了。

例句：He drove like a cluckhead and nearly caused an accident. 他开车像个傻瓜，差点造成事故。|近义词：idiot, fool, moron, nitwit, dimwit, airhead, blockhead|反义词:genius, smart person|noun
