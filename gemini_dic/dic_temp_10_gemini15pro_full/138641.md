nudation:/njuːˈdeɪʃn/
n.
裸露，剥蚀
the act of making something bare or stripping something of its covering.
The process of nudation by weathering and erosion shaped the landscape.  风化和侵蚀的剥蚀作用塑造了这片景观。

The nudation of the hillside revealed the underlying rock strata. 山坡的剥蚀露出了下面的岩层。
近义词: exposure, denudation, stripping, uncovering
反义词: covering, concealment, accretion

Etymology:
From Latin *nudatio*, from nudare "to make naked, strip bare," from nudus "naked."
