cohesive:/koʊˈhiːsɪv/ /kəʊˈhiːsɪv/| adj.|粘着的；有结合力的；紧密的；团结的|Characterized by or causing cohesion; tending to stick together; unified.|The team demonstrated a cohesive spirit throughout the tournament.  这支队伍在整个锦标赛中展现了团结的精神。|synonyms: united, close-knit, bonded, coherent|antonyms:  disunited, fragmented, divided, incoherent|adjective

cohesion:/koʊˈhiːʒən/ /kəʊˈhiːʒən/| n.|凝聚；凝聚力；结合；内聚性|The action or fact of forming a united whole.|The cohesion of the group was remarkable considering their diverse backgrounds.  考虑到他们不同的背景，这个群体的凝聚力非同寻常。|synonyms: unity, solidarity, togetherness, coherence|antonyms:  division, disunity, fragmentation, incoherence|noun
