drooled:/druːld/
verb
流口水；垂涎
let saliva flow from the mouth
The baby drooled on its bib.  婴儿的口水流到围嘴上。

近义词: dribbled, salivated
反义词: (No direct antonym, but one could consider "swallowed" in certain contexts)
词性: verb
