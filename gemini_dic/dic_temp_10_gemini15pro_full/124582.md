mous:/maʊs/ | n. |老鼠，鼠标|A small rodent with a pointed snout, small rounded ears, and a long, almost hairless tail.  Also a device that controls the movement of the cursor on a computer screen. |The cat chased the mous across the floor. 猫追着老鼠跑过地板。| He clicked the mous to open the file. 他点击鼠标打开了文件。|近义词：rodent (啮齿动物), cursor controller (光标控制器) |反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 predator 捕食者)| noun
