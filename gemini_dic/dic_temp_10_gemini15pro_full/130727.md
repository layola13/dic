nonalluvial:/ˌnɒnəˈluːviəl/
adjective
非冲积的
Not consisting of or deposited by alluvium; not alluvial.
The vineyard was planted on nonalluvial soil derived from volcanic rock.  这片葡萄园种植在源自火山岩的非冲积土壤上。
The nonalluvial slopes were less fertile than the valley floor.  非冲积的斜坡不如谷底肥沃。
synonym: non-sedimentary, upland
antonym: alluvial, sedimentary
词性: adjective
