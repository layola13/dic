avulsion:/əˈvʌlʃən/
n.|撕脱, 扯落; 剥离|The sudden tearing away of a body part, such as a finger or toe.  A forceful separation or detachment.|The storm caused the avulsion of the cliff face.  暴风雨导致了悬崖面的撕裂。|近义词：rupture, tear, separation, detachment|反义词:attachment, connection|词性:noun
Etymology:  From Latin avulsio (“a tearing away”), from avellere (“to tear away”), from a- (“away from”) + vellere (“to pluck, tear”).
