hospitalize: /ˈhɒspɪtəlaɪz/ (British) /ˈhɑːspɪtəlaɪz/ (American)
|v.|住院治疗|To admit someone to a hospital for treatment.|He was hospitalized for a week after the accident. 他在事故后住院一周。|She was hospitalized with pneumonia. 她因肺炎住院。|admit, intern|discharge, release|verb
hospitalization: /ˌhɒspɪtəlaɪzeɪʃən/ (British) /ˌhɑːspɪtəlaɪzeɪʃən/ (American)
|n.|住院治疗|The act of admitting someone to a hospital for treatment.|His hospitalization was unexpected. 他的住院出乎意料。|The lengthy hospitalization weakened him considerably. 长期住院使他身体非常虚弱。|admission, confinement|discharge, release|noun

Etymology:
From hospital + -ize.  Ultimately from Latin *hospitale "guest house," from hospes "host."
