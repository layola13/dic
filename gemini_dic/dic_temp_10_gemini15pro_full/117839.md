melliphagous:/məˈlɪfəɡəs/| adj.|食蜜的；以蜜为食的|Feeding on honey; honey-eating.  Derived from Greek *méli* (honey) + *-phagos* (eater).|The melliphagous bat visited the flower for its nectar.|Near synonyms: nectarivorous, honey-eating|Antonyms: carnivorous, herbivorous|adjective
