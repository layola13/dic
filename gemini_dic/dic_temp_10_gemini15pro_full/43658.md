creatureliness:/ˈkriːtʃərlinəs/
n.|受造物性；生物性；尘世性|The state or quality of being a creature; the inherent limitations and vulnerabilities of being a created being, often associated with mortality, physical needs, and dependence.|Her poetry explores the themes of creatureliness and the fragility of human existence. 她的诗歌探讨了生物性和人类生存的脆弱性。|synonyms: mortality, finitude, physicality, vulnerability|antonyms: divinity, immortality, transcendence|noun
