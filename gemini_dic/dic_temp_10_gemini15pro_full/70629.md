filoselle:/ˌfɪləˈsɛl/ | n. | 绒线；毛线；丝线 | A soft, loosely twisted silk or floss silk yarn. |  She embroidered the delicate design using fine filoselle. 她用精细的丝线绣制了精致的图案。 | The filoselle shimmered in the candlelight.  烛光下，丝线闪闪发光。 | 近义词: floss, thread, yarn | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *filosella*, diminutive of *filosa*, feminine of *filoso* "threadlike", from *filo* "thread". Ultimately from Latin *filum* "thread".
