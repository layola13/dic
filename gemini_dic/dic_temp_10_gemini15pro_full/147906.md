overreflections:/ˌoʊvərɪˈflɛkʃənz/
noun
过度反思
Excessive reflection or contemplation, often leading to anxiety or indecision.
She suffered from overreflections about her past mistakes. 她过度反思过去的错误。
Overreflections can be paralyzing. 过度反思可能会让人陷入瘫痪。
rumination, brooding, introspection
impulsiveness, thoughtlessness
