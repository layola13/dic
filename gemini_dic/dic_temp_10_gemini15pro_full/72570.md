fluviograph:/ˈfluːvioʊɡræf/ | n. | 河流流量记录仪；水位记录仪 | An instrument that automatically records the stage or discharge of a stream or river. | The fluviograph provided valuable data on the river's flow during the flood.  河流流量记录仪在洪水期间提供了有关河流流量的宝贵数据。 | synonym:  stream gauge, river gauge, water level recorder | antonym: N/A | noun

fluviographic:/ˌfluːvioʊˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. | 河流流量记录的 | Relating to or produced by a fluviograph. | The fluviographic data showed a significant increase in river flow. 河流流量记录数据显示河流流量显着增加。| synonym:  hydrographic | antonym: N/A | adjective
