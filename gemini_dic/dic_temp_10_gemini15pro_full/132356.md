nondeleteriousness:/ˌnɒndɛlɪˈtɪəriəsnəs/
noun
无害性；无毒性
The state or quality of being not deleterious; harmlessness.
The researcher emphasized the nondeleteriousness of the new drug. 研究人员强调了这种新药的无害性。

近义词:harmlessness, innocuousness, safety
反义词:harmfulness, deleteriousness, toxicity
词性:noun

词源:
From non- + deleterious + -ness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Deleterious" comes from the Latin "dēlēteriuss," meaning "hurtful, noxious."  "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
