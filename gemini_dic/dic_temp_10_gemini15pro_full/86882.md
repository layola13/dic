hemiptera:/hɛˈmɪptərə/
|n.|半翅目昆虫|An order of insects characterized by piercing-sucking mouthparts, forewings modified into hemelytra, and incomplete metamorphosis.  This order includes true bugs, aphids, cicadas, leafhoppers, etc.|Aphids are members of the order Hemiptera. 蚜虫属于半翅目。|近义词:true bugs|反义词:None|词性:noun

hemipteran:/hɛˈmɪptərən/
|adj.|半翅目的|Relating to or belonging to the order Hemiptera.|The hemipteran insects have specialized mouthparts for piercing and sucking. 半翅目昆虫有专门的口器用于刺穿和吸吮。|近义词:None|反义词:None|词性:adjective

hemipteron:/hɛˈmɪptərɒn/
|n.|半鞘翅|The modified forewing of a hemipteran insect, typically thickened at the base and membranous at the tip.|The hemipteron protects the delicate hindwings. 半鞘翅保护着脆弱的后翅。|近义词:hemelytron|反义词:None|词性:noun
