minifarm: /ˈmɪniˌfɑːrm/ | n. | 小型农场，微型农场 | A small-scale farm, often located in an urban or suburban area, focusing on intensive production in a limited space. |  My uncle started a minifarm in his backyard, growing vegetables and raising chickens. 我叔叔在他家后院开办了一个小型农场，种植蔬菜和饲养鸡。

| Example 2: Minifarms are becoming increasingly popular as a way to provide fresh, local produce. 小型农场作为一种提供新鲜本地农产品的方式正变得越来越流行。 | Synonyms: smallholding, market garden, urban farm | Antonyms: large-scale farm, plantation | noun
