doublecrosser:/ˌdʌbəlˈkrɒsər/ /ˌdʌbəlˈkrɑːsər/| n.|背叛者，出卖者，欺骗者|A person who betrays or deceives someone who trusts them, especially by switching loyalties or breaking a promise.|He turned out to be a doublecrosser, selling our secrets to the competition.|近义词：traitor, betrayer, deceiver, cheat|反义词: loyalist, ally|词性:noun
