asserters:/əˈsɜːrtərz/
n.
断言者，主张者
People who state something firmly as true.
The asserters of this theory have yet to provide conclusive evidence.  这个理论的拥护者还没有提供确凿的证据。

近义词:advocates, proponents, claimants
反义词:deniers, opponents
