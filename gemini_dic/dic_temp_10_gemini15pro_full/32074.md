chervil: /ˈtʃɜːr.vɪl/ (US) /ˈtʃɜː.vəl/ (UK)| n. |细叶芹；香芹|From Old French *cherfoil, from Late Latin *caerefolium, from Greek khairaiphullon, from khairo "I rejoice" + phullon "leaf".
