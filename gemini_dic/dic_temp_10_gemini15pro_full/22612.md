booze-up:/ˈbuːz ʌp/ | n. | 畅饮，狂欢 | A social gathering where a lot of alcohol is consumed. |  We had a big booze-up after the exam.  我们考试后好好喝了一顿。| synonyms:  drinking bout, bender, binge, session | antonyms:  teetotalism, abstinence | noun


Etymology:  'Booze' (alcohol) + 'up' (intensifying particle indicating completion or thoroughness). 'Booze' comes from Middle Dutch *būsen, meaning "to drink heavily."
