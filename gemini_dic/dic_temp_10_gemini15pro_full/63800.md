epidemiologically:/ˌɛpɪˌdiːmiəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 流行病学地 | In a way that relates to the study of the spread and control of diseases. | The findings suggest, epidemiologically, that the virus is spreading rapidly. 研究结果从流行病学的角度表明，病毒正在迅速传播。|  Synonym: statistically, demographically | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps anecdotally) | 词性: 副词
