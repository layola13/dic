cryptal: /ˈkrɪptəl/ | adj. | 秘密的，隐秘的 | Relating to or involving a crypt or crypts; hidden, secret. | The cryptal passage led to a hidden chamber.  这条秘密通道通向一个隐藏的房间。|  近义词: secret, hidden, concealed, clandestine | 反义词: open, public, overt | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "crypt" (地下室，墓穴) + "-al" (形容词后缀).  "Crypt" ultimately comes from the Greek word "kryptē" meaning "hidden place" or "vault."
