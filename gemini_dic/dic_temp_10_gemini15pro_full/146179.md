overexcite:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsaɪt/| v.|过度兴奋，过分激动|To cause (someone) to become excessively excited or agitated.|The loud music and flashing lights overexcited the children. 喧闹的音乐和闪烁的灯光让孩子们过度兴奋。|近义词：overstimulate, agitate, inflame|反义词：calm, soothe, pacify|词性:动词
overexcited:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈsaɪtɪd/| adj.|过度兴奋的，过分激动的|Being in a state of excessive excitement or agitation.|The overexcited fans cheered wildly. 过度兴奋的粉丝们疯狂地欢呼。|近义词：overstimulated, agitated, worked up|反义词：calm, composed, relaxed|词性:形容词

词源: over- (prefix) + excite (verb)
"over-" 表示 "过度"， "excite" 来自拉丁语 "excitare," 意思是 "激起，唤醒."
