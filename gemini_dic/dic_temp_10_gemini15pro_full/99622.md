interjurisdictional:/ˌɪntərˌdʒʊərɪsˈdɪkʃənəl/
adjective
跨司法管辖区的
Relating to or involving different legal or governmental jurisdictions.
The creation of the new airport required complex interjurisdictional agreements between the city and the surrounding counties.  新机场的建设需要城市和周边县之间达成复杂的跨司法管辖区协议。
The interjurisdictional task force worked together to combat drug trafficking. 跨司法管辖区的专案组共同打击贩毒。
近义词: interstate, multi-jurisdictional
反义词: intrajurisdictional (within a single jurisdiction)
词性: adjective
词源: inter- (between) + jurisdiction (legal authority) + -al (adjective suffix)
