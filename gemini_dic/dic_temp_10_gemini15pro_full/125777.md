mure:/mjʊər/ | n. |围墙，城墙|A high wall, especially one surrounding a town or castle. |The ancient city was protected by a sturdy mure. 这座古城受到坚固城墙的保护。| Synonyms: wall, rampart, fortification | Antonyms: opening, gap | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *mure*, from Latin *murus* ("wall").
