descender:/dɪˈsendər/| n.|下伸部（字母）|The part of a letter that extends below the baseline, as in the letters g, j, p, q, and y.  字母中延伸到基线以下的部分，例如字母 g、j、p、q 和 y。|The descender on the 'y' was barely visible. 这个'y'的下伸部几乎看不见。|近义词:tail (informal)|反义词:ascender|词性:名词

descender:/dɪˈsendər/| n.|下降者；下行者|One who descends, as from a mountain or in a lineage.  从山上下来的人或世系中的后代。|The descenders from the mountain looked weary. 从山上下来的人看起来很疲惫。|近义词:offspring, descendant|反义词:ancestor, ascender|词性:名词
