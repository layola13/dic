mixableness:/ˈmɪksəbəlnəs/
noun
可混合性，可搅拌性
The quality of being mixable; the ease with which something can be mixed.
The mixableness of the paint was poor, resulting in a lumpy finish.  油漆的可混合性很差，导致表面凹凸不平。
mixability, blendability
immiscibility, non-mixability
n.
