belier: /ˈbɛljeɪ/ (UK) /ˈbeljeɪ/ (US)| n. |公羊;撞锤 |A male sheep, especially one kept for breeding; a battering ram. |例句：The belier butted the gate open. 公羊用头撞开了大门。|例句：The ancient army used a belier to breach the city walls. 古代军队用撞锤攻破城墙。|近义词：ram, tup |反义词:ewe (母羊)|词性:noun

词源: From Old French *belier, from beler "to bellow," from Late Latin belare, of imitative origin.
