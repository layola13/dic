churchwardens:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃˌwɔːrdnz/| n.|教区委员，教堂管理员|An officer in the Church of England, elected by the parishioners, who is responsible for the upkeep of the church building and its contents, and assists the rector or vicar in the administration of the parish.|The churchwardens arranged for the roof to be repaired.| 近义词: vestrymen, church officers| 反义词: N/A| Noun
