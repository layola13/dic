carbonated:/ˈkɑːrbəneɪtɪd/
adjective
碳酸化的；充二氧化碳的
Containing carbon dioxide gas, which makes it bubbly.
例句：I prefer carbonated water to still water. 我更喜欢碳酸水而不是静水。

例句：The carbonated drink fizzed when I opened it.  当我打开碳酸饮料时，它嘶嘶作响。

近义词：fizzy, sparkling, bubbly
反义词：flat, still
词性: adjective
