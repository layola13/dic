idiotical:/ˌɪdiˈɒtɪkəl/
adjective
愚蠢的，白痴般的
extremely stupid or foolish
His suggestion was absolutely idiotical. 他的建议绝对是愚蠢的。
That was an idiotical thing to do. 那样做真是愚蠢。
synonyms: stupid, foolish, absurd, silly, inane, asinine, moronic
antonyms: intelligent, smart, wise, sensible, rational
