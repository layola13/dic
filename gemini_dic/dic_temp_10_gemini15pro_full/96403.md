indispensably:/ˌɪndɪˈspɛnsəbli/| adv.|必不可少地，不可或缺地|absolutely necessary; essential|例句：Water is indispensably important for life.  水对于生命至关重要。|近义词：essentially, necessarily, vitally|反义词:unnecessarily, needlessly|词性:副词
Etymology: From Late Latin indispensabilis, from in- "not" + dispensabilis "that can be dispensed with," from dispensare "to dispense."
