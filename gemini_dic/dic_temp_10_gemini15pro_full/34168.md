clammed:/klæmd/
verb
闭嘴，沉默，(尤指因紧张或恐惧而) 蚌壳式地紧闭，合拢
refuse to speak, especially when you are nervous or frightened.  Become silent suddenly.

例句：
He clammed up when the police started asking questions.
他当警察开始问问题时就闭口不言了。

例句：
She clammed up as soon as her father entered the room.
她父亲一进房间，她就一声不吭了。

近义词：
shut up,  fall silent,  be quiet

反义词：
open up, speak out,  be talkative

词性
v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Old English *clammi, related to clam (noun).  The verb sense likely arose from the image of a clam tightly closing its shell.
