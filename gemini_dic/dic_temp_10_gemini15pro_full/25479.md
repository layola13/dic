bulletproofing:/ˈbʊlɪtˌpruːfɪŋ/| n.|防弹; 装甲防护|The process of making something resistant to bullets.  This involves adding protective layers of materials like steel, Kevlar, or ceramics.|The president's car underwent extensive bulletproofing.|Near synonyms: armoring, fortifying|Antonym:  weakening|noun

bulletproof:/ˈbʊlɪtˌpruːf/| adj.|防弹的;刀枪不入的;不受批评(或攻击)的|Impervious to bullets; not susceptible to criticism or attack.|The police officer wore a bulletproof vest.|Near synonyms: impenetrable, impervious, invulnerable|Antonym: vulnerable|adjective
