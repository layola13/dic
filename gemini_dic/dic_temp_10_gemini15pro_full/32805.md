choirlike:/ˈkwaɪərlaɪk/| adj.|像唱诗班的，合唱队般的|Resembling or characteristic of a choir, especially in harmonious sound. |The choirlike singing of the birds filled the forest. 鸟儿像唱诗班一样的歌声充满了森林。|近义词：harmonious, melodic, choral|反义词：discordant, cacophonous|词性:形容词

词源:
From "choir" + "-like".  "Choir" comes from Old French "cuer" meaning "chorus," ultimately from Latin "chorus," from Greek "khoros" (a band of dancers and singers).  "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of."
