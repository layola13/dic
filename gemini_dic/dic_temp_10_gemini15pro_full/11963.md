artemia:/ɑːrˈtiːmiə/ | n. |豐年蝦，鹵蟲|A small crustacean that lives in saltwater lakes and is used as food for fish.  It's also known as brine shrimp. | 例句：Artemia are often used to feed aquarium fish. 豐年蝦常被用來餵養水族箱裡的魚。

| 例句：The artemia cysts hatched quickly in the warm water. 豐年蝦卵在溫水中迅速孵化。| 近義词：brine shrimp| 反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific organism) | 词性: noun
