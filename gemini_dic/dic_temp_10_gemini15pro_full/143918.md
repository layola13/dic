outpricing:/ˌaʊtˈpraɪsɪŋ/
verb
定价低于…;以低于…的价格出售
The act of selling a product or service at a lower price than a competitor.
Example: They are outpricing their competitors by 10%. 他们以低于竞争对手10%的价格出售产品。
Example: The new company is aggressively outpricing established businesses. 这家新公司正在以极低的价格与老牌企业竞争。
Synonyms: underselling, undercutting
Antonyms: overpricing, overcharging
