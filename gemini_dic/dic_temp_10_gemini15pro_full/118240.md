mercenariness:/ˈmɜːrsənɛrɪnɪs/ | n. | 唯利是图；贪财 | The state of being motivated solely by a desire for money or material gain. |  His mercenariness was evident in his willingness to switch sides for a higher salary.  他为了更高的薪水而愿意改变立场，这表明了他唯利是图的本性。 | synonyms: avarice, greed, cupidity, venality | antonyms: altruism, generosity, selflessness | noun

mercenariness:/ˈmɜːrsənɛrɪnɪs/ | n. | 雇佣性; 市侩 |  The quality of being primarily concerned with making money at the expense of ethics. | The mercenariness of professional sports is often criticized.  职业体育的市侩作风经常受到批评。 | synonyms: materialism, commercialism | antonyms: idealism, principle | noun
