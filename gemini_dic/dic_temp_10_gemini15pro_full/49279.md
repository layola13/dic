deliberatively:/dɪˈlɪbərətɪvli/
adv.
故意地，慎重地
in a way that is slow and careful because you are thinking a lot about what you are doing
She spoke slowly and deliberatively. 她说话缓慢而慎重。
近义词:carefully, thoughtfully, slowly, cautiously, intentionally
反义词:hastily, rashly, impulsively, carelessly
词性:副词

deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbərət/
adj.
故意的，深思熟虑的
done slowly and carefully
a deliberate attempt to provoke conflict  蓄意挑起冲突的企图
近义词:intentional, conscious, planned, purposeful
反义词:unintentional, accidental, spontaneous
词性:形容词

deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪt/
v.
仔细考虑，商议
to think about or discuss something very carefully, especially before making a decision
The jury deliberated for five days before reaching a verdict. 陪审团商议了五天，才作出裁决。
近义词:consider, contemplate, ponder, discuss, debate
反义词:rush, hasten
词性:动词

词源: From Latin *dēlīberātus, past participle of dēlīberāre (“to weigh in the mind, consider, reflect upon, deliberate”), from de- (“from”) + lībra (“scales, balance”).
