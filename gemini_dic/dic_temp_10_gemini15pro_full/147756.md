overprogramming:/ˌoʊvərˈproʊɡræmɪŋ/
|n.|过度编程|The act of creating excessively complex or unnecessarily detailed code, often leading to inefficiency and difficulty in maintenance.  It can also refer to scheduling too many activities for a child, leading to stress and burnout.|The project suffered from overprogramming, making it difficult to debug.  这个项目过度编程，导致难以调试。|近义词：overengineering, overcomplication|反义词: underprogramming, simplification|词性:名词

overprogram:/ˌoʊvərˈproʊɡræm/
|v.|过度编程|To create excessively complex or unnecessarily detailed code, or to schedule too many activities, especially for a child.|They overprogrammed the robot, causing it to malfunction. 他们对机器人进行了过度编程，导致它发生故障。|近义词：overengineer, overcomplicate|反义词: underprogram, simplify|词性:动词
