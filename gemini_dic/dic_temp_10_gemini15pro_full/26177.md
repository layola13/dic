butchers:/ˈbʊtʃərz/| n. |屠夫，肉店|A person who slaughters animals for food or sells meat. |The butchers are preparing meat for the market. 屠夫们正在为市场准备肉。|近义词：meat cutter, slaughterer|反义词:vegetarian, vegan|noun

butchers:/ˈbʊtʃərz/| v. |屠宰，糟蹋，搞砸|To slaughter an animal for food; to ruin or spoil something. |He butchers the pig for the feast. 他为宴会屠宰了这头猪。

The director completely butchered the play. 导演完全搞砸了这出戏。|近义词：slaughter, ruin, spoil|反义词:repair, fix, improve|verb


词源: From Old North French *bochier, from boc "male goat," from Vulgar Latin *buccus, from Gaulish *bucco- "male goat."  Originally referring to a person who slaughtered goats.
