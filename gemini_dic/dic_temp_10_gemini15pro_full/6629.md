anaerobic:/ˌænəˈroʊbɪk/ | adj. | 厌氧的；无氧的 | Relating to, involving, or requiring an absence of free oxygen.| Anaerobic exercise involves short bursts of intense activity. 无氧运动包括短时间的高强度活动。| anoxic, airless | aerobic | adjective
