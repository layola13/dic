mislearn:/ˌmɪsˈlɜːrn/ | v. | 学错；误学 | To learn something incorrectly or acquire a false understanding of something. | She mislearned the formula and got the wrong answer. 她把公式学错了，所以答案错了。

He mislearned the capital of Australia as Sydney. 他把澳大利亚的首府误认为是悉尼。| 近义词: learn wrongly, misunderstand, misapprehend | 反义词: learn correctly, understand | 词性: 动词
