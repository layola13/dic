indulgers:/ɪnˈdʌldʒərz/| n.|沉溺者；放纵者|People who indulge in something, especially something considered pleasurable or enjoyable.  Those who yield to their desires or whims.|The indulgers at the all-you-can-eat buffet barely left any food.  那些自助餐的沉溺者几乎没留下什么食物。|synonyms: hedonists, sybarites, epicureans, bon vivants, voluptuaries|antonyms: ascetics, abstainers, puritans|noun
