overobjectification: /ˌoʊvərəbˌdʒɛktɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度物化 |  The excessive treatment of a person as an object, often focusing solely on their physical attributes and ignoring their personality, dignity, or feelings.  | The overobjectification of women in the media is a serious issue. 媒体对女性的过度物化是一个严重的问题。 | 近义词: objectification, sexualization, dehumanization | 反义词: humanization, personification | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
"over-" (prefix): from Old English "ofer" meaning "above, beyond, across"
"objectification": from "object" (n.) + "-ification" (suffix)
"object" (n.): from Latin "objectum" meaning "something presented to the mind, something aimed at"
