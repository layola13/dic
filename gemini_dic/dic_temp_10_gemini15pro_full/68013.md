factor-in:/ˈfæktər ɪn/| phrasal verb | 考虑到，把…计算在内 | To include or consider something when making a decision or calculation. |  Example 1: We need to factor in the cost of travel when budgeting for the trip. 我们在规划旅行预算时需要把旅行费用考虑进去。

Example 2:  Don't forget to factor in the time difference when scheduling the meeting. 安排会议时间时别忘了把时差考虑进去。 | synonyms: consider, account for, include, take into account | antonyms: disregard, ignore, exclude | verb phrase
