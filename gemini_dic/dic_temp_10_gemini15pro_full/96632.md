induna:/ɪnˈduːnə/| n.|酋长，首领，队长|A headman or official in a Southern African tribe, especially among the Zulu and Nguni peoples.|The induna called a meeting of the tribe. 酋长召集部落开会。|近义词：chief, headman, leader|反义词:follower, subordinate|词性:noun
Etymology: From Zulu *induna*, from *induna* (“male; man; fellow; husband; chief”)
