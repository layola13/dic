charterable:/ˈtʃɑːrtərəbl/| adj.|可包租的，可特许的|Able to be hired or rented, especially of a vehicle or aircraft; able to be granted a charter.|例句：The yacht is charterable for private events. 这艘游艇可以包租用于私人活动。|例句：Is this plane charterable for flights to the Caribbean? 这架飞机可以包租飞往加勒比海吗？|近义词：rentable, hireable, leasable|反义词：unavailable, non-rentable|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From "charter" (n. & v.) + "-able" (suffix). "Charter" comes from Old French "chartre" and ultimately from Latin "charta," meaning "paper, writing." The suffix "-able" forms adjectives meaning "capable of being," "fit to be," or "worthy of being."
