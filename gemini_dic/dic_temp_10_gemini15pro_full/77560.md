gear:/ɡɪər/ (US) /ɡɪə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|齿轮，传动装置；（从事某活动的）设备，用具；档位|A toothed wheel that works with others to alter the relation between the speed of a driving mechanism (such as the engine of a vehicle) and the speed of the driven parts (such as the wheels). Equipment or apparatus that is used for a particular purpose, often a sport or other activity. A particular setting or adjustment of a vehicle's transmission that determines the ratio of engine speed to wheel speed. |The car's gears were grinding. 这辆车的齿轮在嘎嘎作响。|synonyms: equipment, apparatus, tackle, kit, outfit|antonyms: - |noun, verb
