jowler:/ˈdʒaʊlər/| n. |咆哮者；猎犬|A creature, typically a dog, that jowls or howls. A hunting dog, especially a foxhound.|The jowler bayed at the moon. 那只猎犬对着月亮吠叫。|近义词：hound, dog, barker|反义词:whimperer, silent creature|noun

jowler:/ˈdʒaʊlər/| n. |下垂的脸颊；双下巴|Loose flesh hanging from the lower jaw.|(Often used humorously or disapprovingly.) Look at the jowls on that bulldog! 看看那只斗牛犬的垂肉！|近义词：dewlap, wattle|反义词: N/A|noun
