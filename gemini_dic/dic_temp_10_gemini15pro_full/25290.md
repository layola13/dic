bugaboo:/ˈbʌɡəˌbuː/| n.|妖怪; 难题; 令人生畏的事物|Something that causes worry or dismay; a source of concern.  It originates from the late 18th century and may be related to "bugbear".|例句：
The tax increase is the latest bugaboo for small businesses.
对小企业来说，增税是最新的难题。

例句：
Public speaking is a real bugaboo for her.
公开演讲对她来说真是一个可怕的事情。|近义词：problem, difficulty, worry, concern, fear, nightmare, headache, hassle|反义词:solution, ease, comfort, blessing|词性:noun
