gaselier:/ˌɡæsəˈlɪr/ | n. |枝形煤气灯；煤气吊灯|A branched lighting fixture suspended from the ceiling, designed to burn gas. | The antique gaselier cast a warm glow over the dining room.  古董煤气吊灯在餐厅里投下温暖的光芒。| Example 2: The gaselier in the opera house was a magnificent piece of craftsmanship. 歌剧院里的煤气吊灯是一件宏伟的工艺品。| synonym: chandelier, gaslight | antonym: lamp, light bulb | noun
