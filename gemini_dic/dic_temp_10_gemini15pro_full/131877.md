ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + contagious + -ness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Contagious" comes from the Latin "contagiosus," meaning "touching," from "contingere," meaning "to touch." "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
