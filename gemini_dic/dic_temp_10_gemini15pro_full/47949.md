decidedly:/dɪˈsaɪdɪdli/| adv.|明确地；坚决地；无疑地|Clearly; definitely; without question.  It shows that something is very clear or obvious, or that someone is very sure about something.|Her performance was decidedly better than last week's. 她这次的演出明显比上周好。|近义词：definitely, certainly, absolutely, unequivocally, clearly, obviously, plainly, patently, unmistakably|反义词：indecisively, doubtfully, questionably|词性:副词
