antagonises:/ænˈtæɡənaɪzɪz/
verb
使对抗；引起…的敌意；激怒
to cause someone to become hostile
Her remarks antagonised many people at the meeting. 她的言论激怒了会上很多人。
Synonyms: alienate, anger, annoy, irritate
Antonyms: appease, conciliate, placate
词性: 动词
