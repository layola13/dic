drawdowns:/ˈdrɔːdaʊnz/| n.|资金回撤；下降；减少|A drawdown is a peak-to-trough decline during a specific period for an investment, trading account, or fund. It is usually quoted as the percentage between the peak and the subsequent trough.|The fund experienced a significant drawdown during the financial crisis.|近义词：decline, decrease, reduction, slump|反义词:increase, gain, rise|noun
