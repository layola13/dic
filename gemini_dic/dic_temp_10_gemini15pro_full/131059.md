nonbankable:/ˌnɒnˈbæŋkəbl/ | adj. | 不可银行化的，无银行担保的 | Not acceptable to a bank as security for a loan; too risky for a bank to finance.  | Example: The project was deemed nonbankable due to its uncertain profitability.  这个项目由于盈利不确定而被认为是不可银行化的。

| Example:  His business proposal was nonbankable because of his poor credit history. 他的商业计划由于信用记录不良而无法获得银行贷款。 | Synonym: unfinanceable, high-risk, speculative | Antonym: bankable, secure, low-risk | word property: adjective
