breadboard:/ˈbredˌbɔːrd/| n.| 面包板；电路试验板 |A board for making temporary electrical connections, typically used for prototyping electronic circuits.| I used a breadboard to test my circuit before soldering it. 我在焊接电路之前用面包板测试了我的电路。|synonyms:  prototyping board, circuit board |antonyms:  printed circuit board (PCB)|noun
