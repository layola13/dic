outvoyage:/ˈaʊtvɔɪɪdʒ/
noun
海上航行; 出航
A journey by sea, especially one to a distant place.
例句：The outvoyage to the new colony was long and arduous.  前往新殖民地的航行漫长而艰辛。

例句：The sailors prepared meticulously for their outvoyage across the Atlantic.  水手们为横渡大西洋的航行做了精心的准备。

近义词：voyage, journey, expedition
反义词：homeward voyage, return journey
