outgoing:/ˌaʊtˈɡoʊɪŋ/ | adj. | 外向的；友好的；即将离职的；流出的 |  Friendly and socially confident. Also used to describe something that is leaving or departing. | She's a very outgoing person, always making new friends. 她是一个非常外向的人，总是结交新朋友。| The outgoing president gave a farewell speech. 即将离任的总统发表了告别演说。 | 近义词: sociable, extroverted, gregarious, departing | 反义词: shy, introverted, reserved, incoming | 词性: 形容词

out-goinger (非标准英语): /ˌaʊtˈɡoʊɪŋər/ | n. |  一个外向的人 |  A more outgoing person; someone who is more sociable and extroverted.  This is not standard English and is considered informal. |  He's a real out-goinger, always the life of the party. 他真是个外向的人，总是聚会的焦点。| 近义词: extrovert, social butterfly | 反义词: introvert, wallflower | 词性: 名词 (非正式)
