omnivagant:/ɒmˈnɪvəɡənt/
adjective
漫游的，四处漂泊的
Wandering everywhere; roving.
The omnivagant storyteller captivated audiences with tales from his travels across the globe.  这位漫游的说书人用他周游世界的故事吸引了观众。
The omnivagant spirit of the young artist led her to explore various art forms and cultures.  这位年轻艺术家四处漂泊的精神引领她探索各种艺术形式和文化。
近义词:nomadic, wandering, itinerant, roving, peripatetic
反义词:settled, stationary, static, resident

Etymology:
From Latin *omnivagus*, from *omni-* (all) + *vagus* (wandering).
