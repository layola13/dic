nephelodometer:/ˌnɛfɪloʊˈdɒmɪtər/ | n. |浊度计；混浊度计 | An instrument for measuring the cloudiness of a liquid or transparent solid, such as the atmosphere or water, by measuring the amount of light scattered by suspended particles. | The nephelodometer measured the turbidity of the river water after the storm.  暴风雨过后，浊度计测量了河水的浊度。|  turbidimeter, opacity meter | clarity meter | noun
