dmt:/ˌdiː ɛmˈtiː/ /ˈdiː ɛm tiː/ | n. | 二甲基色胺 (Dimethyltryptamine) |  A powerful psychedelic compound found in some plants and animals. It is known for producing intense hallucinations and altered states of consciousness. | DMT is a Schedule I controlled substance in the United States. 二甲基色胺在美国是一级管制药物。|  DMT is considered one of the most potent hallucinogens known to man. 二甲基色胺被认为是人类已知的最强致幻剂之一。 | synonyms:  psychedelic, hallucinogen, entheogen | antonyms:  depressant, sedative | noun
