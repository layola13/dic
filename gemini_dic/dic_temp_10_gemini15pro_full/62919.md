enlivenment:/ɪnˈlaɪvənmənt/| n. |活跃，生动，激励|The addition of bright colors brought an enlivenment to the room. 添加明亮的色彩为房间增添了活力。|近义词：animation, invigoration,  stimulation|反义词：dulling, deadening|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From en- (meaning "to put into" or "to cause to be") + liven (meaning "to make lively") + -ment (a suffix forming nouns denoting the result or product of an action).  Ultimately from the Latin word "vivus" meaning "alive."
