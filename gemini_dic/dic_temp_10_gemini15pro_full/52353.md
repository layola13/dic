dictatorial:/ˌdɪktəˈtɔːriəl/ | adj. |独裁的，专制的| Of or characteristic of a dictator; tyrannical. | The dictator ruled with an iron fist, his dictatorial regime crushing any opposition.  这位独裁者用铁腕统治，他的独裁政权粉碎了任何反对派。|Synonyms: tyrannical, autocratic, despotic, authoritarian, totalitarian | Antonyms: democratic, liberal, egalitarian |adjective

dictatorial:/ˌdɪktəˈtɔːriəl/ | adj. |独裁的，专制的| Exercising absolute power; imperious. |  His dictatorial manner made it difficult for his employees to offer suggestions. 他专横的态度使他的员工难以提出建议。|Synonyms: overbearing, domineering, imperious, high-handed | Antonyms: humble, modest, unassuming |adjective
