citator:/ˈsaɪteɪtər/| n.|引用者，引证者|A citator is a legal research tool that lists the sources that have cited a particular case, statute, or other legal authority.  It helps determine whether a legal precedent is still valid and how it has been interpreted over time.|She used a citator to check the subsequent history of the case. 她使用引证器来检查该案例的后续历史。|近义词: legal research tool, case finder|反义词: N/A|noun
