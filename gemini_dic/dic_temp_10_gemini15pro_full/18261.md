believingly:/bɪˈliːvɪŋli/
adverb
相信地，深信不疑地
in a way that shows you believe something is true
She nodded believingly as he told his story. 她一边听着他的故事，一边深信不疑地点头。
近义词: trustingly, credulously
反义词: skeptically, doubtfully
词性: adverb

词源: From "believe" + "-ing" + "-ly". "Believe" comes from Old English "belīefan" (to believe, trust), from "be-" (intensifying prefix) + "lēof" (dear, beloved).  The suffix "-ing" forms a present participle or gerund, and "-ly" forms an adverb.
