bad:/bæd/
adjective
坏的；不好的；差的；严重的；有害的；令人不快的；腐败的；低劣的；不舒服的；不健康的
of poor quality or a low standard; not satisfactory.
例句：The food was bad and made us sick.  食物不好，让我们生病了。

例句：He has a bad back. 他背不好。

近义词：awful, terrible, horrible, poor, unpleasant
反义词：good, excellent, wonderful
