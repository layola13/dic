{given}  what /wɒt; hwɒt (US) wʌt/ |pron. det. adv. int.| 什么，多么 |  Used to ask for information about something. | What is your name? 你叫什么名字？ | which, who | (No single antonym for "what") | pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection

/wɑːt; hwɑːt (US) wʌt/

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃什么？
What does she like to do in her free time? = 她空闲时间喜欢做什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在看什么电视节目？
What is he doing in the garden? = 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she cooked for dinner? = 她晚饭做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么书？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party last night? = 昨晚的派对发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 开会的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had they discussed before the decision was made? = 做决定之前他们讨论了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I arrived? = 我来之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们将在会议上讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by 2050? = 到2050年会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been studying for ten years by then? = 到那时你将已经学习什么十年了？
What will she have been working on by the time she retires? = 到她退休时她将一直从事什么工作？

虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
What would have happened if I hadn't been there? = 如果我不在那里会发生什么？

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what you mean. = 她不明白你的意思。
