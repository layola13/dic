crouchingly:/ˈkraʊtʃɪŋli/| adv.|蹲着地，蜷伏地|In a crouching manner; in a low, bent posture.|He moved crouchingly through the bushes. 他蹲着穿过灌木丛。|近义词：squattingly, hunchedly|反义词:standing, uprightly|词性:副词
Etymology: From "crouch" (verb) + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverb suffix).  "Crouch" comes from Old French "crochir" (to bend, hook), ultimately from a Germanic source.
