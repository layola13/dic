alluvium:/əˈluːviəm/ | n. | 冲积层；冲积土 |  A deposit of clay, silt, sand, and gravel left by flowing streams in a river valley or delta, typically producing fertile soil. | The fertile alluvium of the Nile delta has supported agriculture for millennia. 尼罗河三角洲肥沃的冲积层已经支撑了数千年的农业。 | synonyms: sediment, deposit, silt, alluvion | antonyms: bedrock | noun

alluviums:/əˈluːviəmz/ | n. | 冲积层；冲积土（复数） | Deposits of clay, silt, sand, and gravel left by flowing streams.  | The farmer enriched his fields with alluviums from the nearby river.  这位农民用附近河流的冲积土来肥沃他的田地。 | synonyms: sediments, deposits, silts, alluvions | antonyms: bedrocks | noun
