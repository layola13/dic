humblebee:/ˈhʌmbəlˌbiː/| n.|大黄蜂|A large hairy bee. It makes a loud humming sound when it flies. |例句：The bumblebee buzzed lazily around the flowers.  大黄蜂懒洋洋地在花丛中嗡嗡叫。

例句：The garden was alive with the sound of bumblebees. 花园里充满了大黄蜂的嗡嗡声。|近义词：bee|反义词:N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *hummelen-bee* "humming bee," from *hummelen* (verb) + *bee* (noun).  The verb *hummelen* is imitative of the bee's humming sound.
