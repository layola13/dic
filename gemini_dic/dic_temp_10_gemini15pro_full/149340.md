pachinkos:/pəˈtʃɪŋkoʊz/ |n.| 柏青哥游戏机（复数） | A Japanese gambling game played on a vertical pinball machine.  It involves shooting small metal balls into a playing field, hoping they land in certain prize-winning pockets. |  Many tourists enjoy playing pachinkos in Japan. 许多游客喜欢在日本玩柏青哥。| Synonyms: pinball (loosely), slot machines (in terms of gambling function) | Antonyms: N/A | noun
