face-saving:/ˈfeɪsˌseɪvɪŋ/
adjective
保全面子的，顾全脸面的
Intended to protect someone's reputation or dignity.
She made a face-saving excuse for her husband's rudeness. 她为丈夫的粗鲁找了个保全面子的借口。
近义词:dignified, respectable, honorable
反义词:humiliating, embarrassing
词性:adjective

face-saving:/ˈfeɪsˌseɪvɪŋ/
noun
保全面子，顾全脸面
The act of protecting someone's reputation or dignity.
The meeting was a face-saving exercise for both parties. 这次会议对双方来说都是一次保全面子的举动。
近义词:tact, diplomacy
反义词:humiliation, embarrassment
词性:noun
