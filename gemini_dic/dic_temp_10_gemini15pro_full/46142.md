cymoid:/ˈsaɪmɔɪd/ | adj. |  sigmoid的，S形的 | Relating to or resembling a sigmoid; sigmoid curve. | The bacteria exhibited a cymoid growth pattern. 这些细菌呈现出S形生长模式。 | Example: The graph displayed a cymoid shape, indicating a gradual increase followed by a plateau.  图表显示了一个S形，表明逐渐增加，然后趋于平稳。 | Synonym: sigmoid, S-shaped | Antonym: linear, straight | Adjective
