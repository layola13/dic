moover:/ˈmuːvər/| n.|推动者，搬运工，搬家公司|Someone or something that moves something else, or facilitates a move.  A person or company that helps people move their possessions from one place to another.| The moovers arrived early in the morning to pack up the furniture.  搬运工一大早就来打包家具了。| The family hired moovers to help them relocate to their new home.  这家人雇了搬家公司帮他们搬到新家。| synonyms: mover, transporter, relocator | antonyms: stayer, settler | noun
