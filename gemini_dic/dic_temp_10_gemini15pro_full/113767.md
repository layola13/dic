macropodous:/ˌmækrəˈpoʊdəs/| adj.| 大脚的，长脚的|Having large or long feet.  Relating to or belonging to the Macropodidae family (kangaroos, wallabies, etc.).|The macropodous kangaroo hopped across the plains.  这只大脚的袋鼠跳过平原。|近义词：long-legged, megapod|反义词:brachypterous, short-legged|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Greek *makros* "large" + *pous* "foot".
