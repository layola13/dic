moresque:/ˌmɔːˈrɛsk/ /ˌmɔːrˈɛsk/ | adj. n. | 摩尔式的，摩尔式装饰; 摩尔式装饰品 | Characteristic of or resembling the Moors or their artistic or architectural style, especially the style prevalent in Spain after the expulsion of the Moors.  A type of ornamentation in this style. |  例句：The intricate moresque patterns on the walls were breathtaking. 墙上复杂的摩尔式图案令人叹为观止。

例句：The Alhambra palace is a stunning example of moresque architecture. 阿尔罕布拉宫是摩尔式建筑的绝佳典范。 | 近义词：Moorish, arabesque, Islamic | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "European" in a specific architectural context) | 词性: adjective, noun
