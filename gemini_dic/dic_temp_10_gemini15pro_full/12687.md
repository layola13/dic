assentingly:/əˈsɛntɪŋli/| adv.|赞同地，同意地|In a manner that expresses agreement or consent.  Showing approval or acceptance.|She nodded assentingly as he explained his plan. 她在他解释他的计划时赞同地点了点头。|Synonyms: approvingly, concurringly, agreeably|Antonyms: disapprovingly, dissenting, objecting|adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From assent + -ing + -ly.  "Assent" comes from the Old French "assentir," from the Latin "assentire," meaning "to think the same," from "ad-" (to) + "sentire" (to feel).
