circumcised:/ˈsɜːrkəmˌsaɪzd/| adj.|已受割礼的，割过包皮的|Having had the foreskin surgically removed from the penis.|He was circumcised as a baby. 他小时候就受了割礼。|Synonyms: excised, cut|Antonyms: uncircumcised|adjective

circumcise:/ˈsɜːrkəmˌsaɪz/| vt.| 割包皮，行割礼|To surgically remove the foreskin of the penis.|Jewish males are traditionally circumcised. 犹太男性传统上要行割礼。|Synonyms: excide, cut|Antonyms:  (no direct antonym for the verb, but "restore foreskin" describes the reverse procedure)|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *circumcisus, past participle of circumcidere, from Latin circum (around) + caedere (to cut).
