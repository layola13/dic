habitude:/ˈhæbɪtjuːd/ /həˈbɪtʃuːd/ | n. |习惯，习性| A settled tendency or usual manner of behavior.  A behavior pattern acquired by frequent repetition or physiologic exposure that shows itself in regularity or increased facility of performance.|She has a habitude of biting her nails when she's nervous.| 近义词: habit, custom, practice, routine | 反义词: irregularity | 词性: 名词
