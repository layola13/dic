cephalostyle: /ˈsefələˌstaɪl/| n. |头柱；头茎|A stalk-like structure supporting the head or capitulum, especially in certain invertebrates.|The cephalostyle of the bryozoan is crucial for feeding.|近义词: stalk, peduncle|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology:  From cephalo- (head) + -style (stalk, pillar).  "Cephalo-" comes from Greek "kephalē" (head). "-style" comes from Greek "stylos" (pillar, column).
