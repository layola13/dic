cyberspatial:/ˈsaɪbərˌspeɪʃəl/| adj.|网络空间的|Relating to or existing within cyberspace.  Existing, occurring, or carried on in cyberspace; of or relating to cyberspace.|例句：Cyberspatial interaction is becoming increasingly common. 网络空间互动变得越来越普遍。|例句：The cyberspatial environment presents unique challenges for law enforcement. 网络空间环境给执法部门带来了独特的挑战。|近义词：virtual, online, digital, internet-based|反义词：physical, real-world|词性:形容词


Etymology:
cyber- + spatial.  "Cyber-"  short for "cybernetics," ultimately from Greek *kubernetes* "pilot, steersman." "Spatial" from Latin *spatium* "space, room, distance."
