bedside:/ˈbɛdsaɪd/| n. adj.|床边，床头；临终|The space beside a bed.  Can also refer to the time just before someone dies.|例句：She kept a glass of water on her bedside table. 她床头柜上放了一杯水。

例句：The priest gave him the last rites at his bedside.  神父在他临终前为他举行了临终圣事。|近义词：bedhead, nightstand|反义词: N/A|词性:名词/形容词

bedside:/ˈbɛdsaɪd/|adj.|床边的|Used to describe something located near a bed, or related to the time just before someone dies.|例句：He kept a bedside lamp for reading. 他放了一盏床头灯用来阅读。

例句：His bedside manner was comforting. 他临终前的态度令人欣慰。|近义词：bedhead, nightstand|反义词: N/A|词性:形容词
