inequivalve:/ɪnɪˈkwɪvælv/
adjective
不等瓣的；两瓣壳不相等的
Having two valves or halves that are unequal in size or shape.
The oyster is an inequivalve mollusk, with one shell much larger than the other. 牡蛎是一种不等瓣的软体动物，其中一个壳比另一个大得多。

The inequivalve shells of the clam provided a secure home.  蛤蜊的不等瓣壳提供了一个安全的家。

近义词:asymmetrical, unequal
反义词:equivalve, equal
词性:adjective

词源:
From Latin *inequivalvis, from in- 'not' + equi- 'equal' + valva 'leaf of a folding door, valve'.
