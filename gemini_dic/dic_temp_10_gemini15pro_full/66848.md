expectably:/ɪkˈspektəbli/| adv.|可预期地，不出所料地|As expectably, the traffic was heavy during rush hour. 正如预期的那样，高峰时段交通拥堵。|例句：Expectably, she won the competition.不出所料，她赢得了比赛。|近义词：predictably,foreseeably, unsurprisingly|反义词:unexpectedly, surprisingly|词性:副词
Etymology: From "expect" (from Latin exspectare "to look out for, await") + "-able" (suffix forming adjectives) + "-ly" (suffix forming adverbs).
