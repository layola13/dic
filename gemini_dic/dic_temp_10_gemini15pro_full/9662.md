antisynod:/ˌæntiˈsɪnəd/
| n. |反对宗教会议者；反对教会会议(或宗教会议)的人；反对宗教会议(或教会会议)的团体|A person or group opposed to a synod or church council.|The antisynod published a pamphlet denouncing the council's decisions. 反对宗教会议者发表了一份小册子，谴责宗教会议的决定。

|The antisynod believed the council's decisions were heretical. 反对宗教会议者认为该委员会的决定是异端的。|
|Synonyms: dissenter, opponent, protestor|
|Antonyms: supporter, advocate|
|noun|

Etymology:
From anti- + synod.  "Anti-" is a Greek prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to." "Synod" comes from the Late Latin synodus and ultimately from the Greek synodos, meaning "meeting" or "assembly."
