deaness:/ˈdiːnɪs/
n.
女院长；女系主任
a woman who is the dean of a college, faculty, or cathedral
例句：The deaness addressed the graduating class. 女院长向毕业班致辞。

例句：The deaness of students is responsible for student welfare.  学生事务主任负责学生福利。

近义词：dean, principal, headmistress
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps consider "subordinate")


词性: noun
