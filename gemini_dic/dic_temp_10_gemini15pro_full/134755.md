nonmelodramatically: /ˌnɒnˌmɛlədrəˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 非戏剧性地，不夸张地 |  In a way that avoids being overly dramatic or emotional.  It describes an action or expression done without exaggerated displays of emotion or distress, typically associated with melodrama. |  She accepted the bad news nonmelodramatically, simply nodding and saying, "I understand." 她平静地接受了这个坏消息，只是点点头说：“我明白了。” | She reacted nonmelodramatically to the news, surprising everyone with her composure.  她对这个消息的反应很平静，她镇定的态度让每个人都感到惊讶。 | calmly, quietly, soberly, composedly, restrainedly | dramatically, theatrically, hysterically, emotionally | adverb
