bacterio:/bækˈtɪəri.oʊ/
combining form|细菌-
|A combining form meaning “bacteria,” used in the formation of compound words: bacteriophage|Bacteriologists study bacteria and their effects. 细菌学家研究细菌及其影响。
|近义词:None (as a combining form)|反义词:None (as a combining form)
词性:combining form

Etymology:
From Greek *baktērion* "small staff, stick," diminutive of *baktron* "staff."
