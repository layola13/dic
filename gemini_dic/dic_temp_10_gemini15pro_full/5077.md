all-wool-and-a-yard-wide:/ˌɔːl ˈwʊl ənd ə ˈjɑːrd waɪd/ | adj. | 真材实料的；十足的；彻底的；不折不扣的 | Genuine, of the highest quality, thorough, authentic.  Implies something or someone is genuine and of excellent quality. |  She's all wool and a yard wide, always willing to help. 她是个十足的好人，总是乐于助人。 | Example: He's all wool and a yard wide, a true gentleman. 他是个十足的绅士，一个真正高尚的人。 | 近义词: genuine, authentic, real, true | 反义词: fake, counterfeit, sham, phony | 词性: 形容词

词源:
This idiom originated in the 19th century, referring to high-quality, durable woolen fabric. A "yard wide" was a standard and generous width for fabric, adding to the sense of quality and value.  Over time, the phrase evolved to describe people and things of genuine and excellent quality.
