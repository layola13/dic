chaffwax:/ˈtʃæfwæks/
n.
蜡渣，蜂窝渣
Residue from melted honeycomb, containing pollen, bee bread, and other impurities.
The beekeeper removed the chaffwax from the rendered beeswax. 养蜂人将蜂窝渣从提炼的蜂蜡中去除。
近义词:beeswax residue, slumgum
反义词:pure beeswax
词性:名词
