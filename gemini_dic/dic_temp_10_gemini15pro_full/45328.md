cummerbunds:/ˈkʌmərˌbʌndz/| n.|腰带，腰围|A broad sash worn around the waist, typically with a tuxedo.  A cummerbund is a wide band of fabric worn around the waist, often as part of formal attire, particularly with a tuxedo.|He wore a crisp white shirt, a black tuxedo, and a silk cummerbund.|近义词：sash, waistband, girdle|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps belt as it is a different type of waist adornment)|noun
