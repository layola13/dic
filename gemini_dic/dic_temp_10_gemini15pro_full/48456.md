deejays:/ˈdiːdʒeɪz/| n.|唱片骑师; 电台音乐节目主持人|A person who plays recorded music for an audience, typically at a nightclub or on radio or television.|The deejays played a mix of classic and modern tracks. 唱片骑师播放了经典和现代曲目的混合.|Synonyms: DJ, disc jockey|Antonyms: listener, audience|Noun
