cradle:/ˈkreɪdl/| v. |轻轻地抱|To hold gently as if in a cradle.|例句：She cradled the newborn baby in her arms. 她轻轻地抱着新生儿。|近义词：hold, cuddle, rock|反义词:drop|v.
