hardily:/ˈhɑːrdɪli/ | adv. | 坚强地，大胆地，强壮地，耐寒地 | In a hardy manner; with boldness and resolution; strongly; robustly. |  The plants grew hardily despite the harsh winter. 这些植物尽管冬天严酷，但仍然生长得很好。| 近义词: boldly, courageously, stoutly, robustly, strongly | 反义词: timidly, fearfully, weakly, delicately | 词性: 副词

hardily:/ˈhɑːrdɪli/ | adv. | 几乎不，不大可能 |  Scarcely; barely; with difficulty. |  He could hardily walk after the marathon. 马拉松赛跑后，他几乎走不动路了。| 近义词: scarcely, barely, hardly | 反义词: easily, readily, definitely | 词性: 副词
