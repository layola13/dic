orthoceratite:/ˌɔːrθəˈsɛrəˌtaɪt/| n. |直角石|An extinct marine animal of the cephalopod class, having a long, straight, conical shell.|Example:  The museum displayed a well-preserved orthoceratite fossil.  博物馆展出了一块保存完好的直角石化石。

Example: Orthoceratites thrived in the Paleozoic era. 直角石在古生代繁盛。|近义词:cephalopod, nautiloid |反义词: None (since it's an extinct species, there's no direct opposite)|词性:noun
