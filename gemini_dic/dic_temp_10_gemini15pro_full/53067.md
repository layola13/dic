diorite:/ˈdaɪəˌraɪt/| n. |闪长岩|A grey, coarse-grained igneous rock, typically composed of plagioclase feldspar and hornblende or other mafic minerals.|The countertop was made of polished diorite.  这个台面是用抛光的闪长岩制成的。|Synonyms:  gabbro, andesite|Antonyms:  None (It's a specific rock type, no direct opposite)|noun
