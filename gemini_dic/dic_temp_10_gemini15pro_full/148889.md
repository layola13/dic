overwhelmed: /ˌoʊvərˈwelmd/ /ˌəʊvəˈwelmd/ | adj. |  不知所措的，压倒的，淹没的 | Feeling overpowered and defeated by something intense or difficult to manage. |

例句：
I was overwhelmed by the amount of work I had to do.
我被我需要做的工作量压得喘不过气。

She was overwhelmed with grief after her father's death.
父亲去世后，她悲痛欲绝。


近义词：overpowered, inundated, swamped, overcome
反义词：calm, composed, in control
词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *oferwhelman, from ofer- (over) + *hwelman (to turn over, overwhelm).
