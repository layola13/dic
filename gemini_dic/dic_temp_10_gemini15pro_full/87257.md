heritable:/ˈhɛrɪtəbl/| adj.|可遗传的，可继承的|Able to be passed from parent to offspring genetically.|Eye color is a heritable trait. 眼睛的颜色是一种可遗传的特征。|Synonyms: genetic, inheritable, transmissible|Antonyms: acquired, non-heritable|adjective

heritable:/hɛˈrɪtəbl/| adj.|可继承的（指财产、头衔等）|Able to be passed down by inheritance (as of property or title).|The estate is heritable by the eldest son.  这片地产可由长子继承。|Synonyms: inheritable|Antonyms: non-inheritable|adjective
