antiscriptural:/ˌæntiˈskrɪptʃərəl/
adjective
违反圣经的；与圣经教义相悖的
Contrary to or inconsistent with the teachings of the Bible.
His antiscriptural views caused him to be excommunicated from the church. 他的反圣经观点导致他被逐出教会。
Synonyms: irreligious, profane, ungodly, impious
Antonyms: scriptural, biblical, pious, devout
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek anti-)
scriptural (adjective): relating to or based on the Bible (from Late Middle English scriptural, from Old French scriptural, from Late Latin scripturalis, from scriptura "writing")
