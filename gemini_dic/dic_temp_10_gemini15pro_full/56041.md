dobbin:/ˈdɒbɪn/ (UK) /ˈdɑːbɪn/ (US)| n.|老马；温顺的马；农耕马|A horse, especially a workhorse; a gentle, plodding horse.|The old farmer relied on his trusty dobbin for all the heavy work on the farm. 老农依靠他那匹可靠的老马完成农场里所有的重活。|近义词：nag, steed, horse|反义词: (no direct antonym, perhaps a spirited, racehorse)|词性: noun
