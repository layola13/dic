implausible: /ɪmˈplɔːzəbl/ | adj. |难以置信的，不可信的 | Not seeming reasonable or probable; failing to convince. |  His explanation for the missing money was completely implausible. 他对丢失的钱的解释完全不可信。

Her story about being abducted by aliens was implausible. 她关于被外星人绑架的故事令人难以置信。

| 近义词: unbelievable, improbable, doubtful, questionable | 反义词: plausible, believable, credible, likely | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + plausible (believable), from Latin plausibilis (worthy of applause), from plaudere (to applaud).
