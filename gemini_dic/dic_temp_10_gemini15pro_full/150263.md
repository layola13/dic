pandoras-boxes:/pænˈdɔːrəz ˈbɒksɪz/ | n. | 潘多拉的盒子；灾祸之源 | A source of numerous unforeseen troubles or problems.  It alludes to the Greek myth of Pandora's box, which, when opened, released all the evils of the world. | Opening that old file cabinet was a real Pandora's box; it was full of forgotten and complicated issues. 打开那个旧文件柜就像打开了潘多拉的盒子；里面充满了被遗忘的复杂问题。 | Pandora's box has unleashed a torrent of problems for the company. 潘多拉的盒子给公司带来了大量的麻烦。 | synonyms: can of worms, hornet's nest, minefield, trouble spot | antonyms: blessing, godsend | noun
