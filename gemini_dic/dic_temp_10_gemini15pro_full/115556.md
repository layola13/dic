maquette:/mæˈkɛt/ | n. | 模型，草图，原型 | A preliminary model, often smaller than the final product, used to visualize or plan a design. | The architect presented a maquette of the proposed skyscraper. 建筑师展示了拟建摩天大楼的模型。|  The artist created a maquette for his sculpture. 艺术家为他的雕塑创作了一个模型。| synonym: model, prototype, mock-up | antonym: finished product | noun
