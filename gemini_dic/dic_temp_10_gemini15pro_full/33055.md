chow-down:/ˈtʃaʊˌdaʊn/
noun
大吃一顿;狼吞虎咽
a hearty meal; a feast.  Informal.
例句：We had a real chow-down after the game.  比赛结束后，我们大吃了一顿。
例句：Let's have a chow-down this weekend. 这周末我们好好吃一顿吧。
近义词：feast, banquet, pig out, gorge
反义词：snack, nibble
词性：noun

chow-down:/ˈtʃaʊˌdaʊn/
verb
狼吞虎咽地吃
to eat heartily and enthusiastically. Informal.
例句：They chowed down on pizza and wings. 他们狼吞虎咽地吃着披萨和鸡翅。
例句：We chowed down on barbecue at the picnic. 我们在野餐时尽情享用了烧烤。
近义词：gobble, devour, wolf down
反义词：nibble, pick at
词性：verb


词源: "Chow" originally comes from the Pidgin English word "chow-chow" meaning "food," borrowed from the Chinese word  炒 (chǎo) meaning "to stir-fry" or "to cook."  "Down" in this context likely refers to the act of consuming the food.
