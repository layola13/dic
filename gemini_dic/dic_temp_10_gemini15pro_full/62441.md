endor:/ɛnˈdɔːr/ |v. | 背书，认可，赞同 | To give support or approval to somebody/something, especially publicly. | The newspaper has endorsed the conservative candidate for mayor.  这家报纸已经公开支持保守党候选人竞选市长。 | 近义词: support, approve, back, champion | 反义词: oppose, reject, condemn | 词性: 动词

词源:
From Old French *endorser "write on the back of," from en- "on" + dors "back," from Latin dorsum.
