feminist:/ˈfɛmɪnɪst/| n. adj.|女权主义者；女权主义的|A person who supports feminism, the advocacy of women's rights on the grounds of the equality of the sexes.|She is a passionate feminist and fights for equal pay. 她是一位充满热情的女权主义者，为同工同酬而奋斗。|近义词：women's rights advocate, suffragette|反义词：anti-feminist, sexist|词性: 名词, 形容词

feminist:/ˈfɛmɪnɪst/| adj.|女权主义的|Relating to or supporting feminism.|She has a feminist perspective on the issue. 她对这个问题持有女权主义的观点。|近义词：pro-feminist|反义词：anti-feminist, sexist|词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *féministe*, from *féminin* (“feminine”), from Latin *fēmina* (“woman”).
