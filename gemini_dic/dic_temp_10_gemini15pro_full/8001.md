anthophore:/ˈænθəfɔːr/ | n. | 花托；花柄 | The part of a flower stalk bearing the flower or flowers, situated above or within the calyx. | The anthophore elevates the flower above the sepals.  花托将花朵抬高到萼片之上。| The bee landed on the anthophore to collect nectar. 蜜蜂落在花托上采集花蜜。| 近义词: receptacle, torus | 反义词:  (无明确反义词) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From New Latin anthophorum, from Ancient Greek ἀνθοφόρος (anthophóros, “flower-bearing”), from ἄνθος (ánthos, “flower”) + φέρω (phérō, “I carry”).
