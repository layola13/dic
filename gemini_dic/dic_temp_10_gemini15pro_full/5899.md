ambidextrousness:/ˌæmbɪˈdɛkstrəsnəs/
|n.| 双手灵巧；左右开弓；|The ambidextrousness of the surgeon allowed him to operate with either hand.  这位外科医生的双手灵巧使他能够用任何一只手进行手术。|English Explanation: The state of being able to use both hands equally well.|例句：Her ambidextrousness is a great asset in her chosen profession of surgery. 她的双手灵巧在她选择的外科医生职业中是一项巨大的财富。|近义词：versatility, adaptability, two-handedness|反义词：one-handedness, clumsiness|词性: noun

ambidextrous:/ˌæmbɪˈdɛkstrəs/
|adj.| 双手灵巧的；左右开弓的；|He's an ambidextrous tennis player, equally skilled with both his forehand and backhand. 他是一位双手灵巧的网球运动员，正反手都一样熟练。|English Explanation: Able to use both hands equally well.|例句：Being ambidextrous gave him an advantage in baseball.  双手灵巧让他在棒球比赛中占了优势。|近义词：versatile, adaptable, two-handed|反义词：one-handed, clumsy|词性: adjective
