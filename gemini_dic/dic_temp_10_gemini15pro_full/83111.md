gustavo:/ɡʊˈstɑːvoʊ/| n. |古斯塔沃|A male given name, of Spanish and Portuguese origin, meaning "staff of the Goths".  It is derived from the Germanic elements *gautaz "Goth" and *stabaz "staff".|My friend Gustavo is a talented musician. 我的朋友古斯塔沃是一位才华横溢的音乐家。|Synonyms: Gus (nickname) |Antonyms: N/A|noun
