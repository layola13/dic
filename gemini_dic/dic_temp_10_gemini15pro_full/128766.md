nigh-on:/ˌnaɪˈɒn/| adv.|几乎，差不多|It took nigh-on two hours to get there. 到那里差不多花了两个小时。|例句：The project cost nigh-on a million dollars.  这个项目差不多花费了一百万美元。|近义词：almost, nearly, practically, virtually|反义词:not at all, far from|副词

词源：From "nigh" (near) + "on" (towards).  "Nigh" is an Old English word meaning "near," related to the German "nah." "On" adds a sense of approaching or reaching the specified point.  Therefore, "nigh-on" means very close to a particular number or time.
