concurred: /kənˈkɜːrd/ /kənˈkɜːrd/
verb
同意，一致
agreed; happened at the same time
例句：The judges concurred with the jury's verdict.  法官同意陪审团的裁决。

例句：The two events concurred to produce a disastrous outcome.  两件事同时发生，导致了灾难性的后果。

近义词：agreed, coincided, corresponded
反义词：disagreed, differed, clashed
词性：verb

concur: /kənˈkɜːr/ /kənˈkɜːr/
verb
同意，一致; 同时发生
to agree; to happen at the same time
例句：I concur with your assessment of the situation. 我同意你对形势的评估。

例句：The accident concurred with the start of the holiday rush. 事故恰好发生在假日高峰期开始的时候。

近义词：agree, coincide, correspond
反义词：disagree, differ, clash
词性：verb
