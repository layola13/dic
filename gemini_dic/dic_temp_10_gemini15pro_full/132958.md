nonemphatic:/ˌnɒnɪmˈfætɪk/ (US) /ˌnɒnɛmˈfætɪk/ (UK)| adj.|非强调的，不加重语气|Not emphatic; without emphasis.  Expressing something in a way that does not draw special attention to it.|She delivered the news in a nonemphatic tone. 她以不带强调的语气宣布了这个消息。|Synonyms: unemotional, neutral, matter-of-fact, low-key, subdued|Antonyms: emphatic, stressed, highlighted, forceful, pronounced|adjective
