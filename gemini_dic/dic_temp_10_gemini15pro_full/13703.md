attrition:/əˈtrɪʃən/| n.|损耗，磨损，消耗|a gradual reduction in the number of people who work for an organization, that is achieved by not replacing those who leave|The company is trying to reduce staff through natural attrition.  公司正试图通过自然减员来减少员工数量。|例句：The school suffered from attrition when several teachers retired.  几名教师退休后，学校出现了人员流失。|近义词：erosion, depletion, weakening|反义词:growth, increase|noun
