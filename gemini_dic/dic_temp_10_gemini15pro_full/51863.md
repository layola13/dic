dextrorotary: /ˌdɛkstroʊˈroʊtəri/ /ˌdɛkstrəˈrəʊtəri/ | adj. | 右旋的 | Rotating to the right, especially of polarized light.  A dextrorotary substance rotates the plane of polarized light to the right (clockwise). | This sugar is dextrorotary.  这种糖是右旋的。

| The solution was found to be dextrorotary.  经检测发现该溶液是右旋的。 | 近义词: clockwise | 反义词: levorotary | 词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *dexter* (right) + *rotatorius* (rotating).
