disrobes:/dɪsˈroʊbz/
verb
脱衣服，脱去礼服
removes clothes, especially ceremonial robes
The king disrobed in his private chamber. 国王在他的私人房间里脱去了衣服。

近义词:undresses, strips, disinvests
反义词:dresses, robes, attires
词性:v.
