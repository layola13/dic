chimerically:/kaɪˈmɛrɪkli/ | adv. | 异想天开地，妄想地 | In a wildly fanciful or unrealistic manner; impractically. |  He chimerically believed he could fly to the moon using a giant kite. 他异想天开地认为可以用一个巨大的风筝飞到月球上去。 | 近义词：fancifully, unrealistically, delusionally, quixotically | 反义词：realistically, practically, pragmatically | 词性: 副词

chimerical:/kaɪˈmɛrɪkəl/ | adj. | 异想天开的，空想的 | Wildly fanciful or imaginative; unrealistic. | The idea of building a city on Mars seemed chimerical just a few decades ago.  几十年前，在火星上建造城市的想法似乎是异想天开的。 | 近义词：fanciful, unrealistic, imaginary, visionary | 反义词：realistic, practical, down-to-earth | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *chimérique*, from Late Latin *chimaera*, from Latin *Chimaera* (a mythological fire-breathing monster), from Greek *Khimaira* (“she-goat”).
