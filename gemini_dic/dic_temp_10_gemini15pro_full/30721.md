ceruminous:/səˈruːmɪnəs/ /sɪˈr(j)uːmɪnəs/
adjective
耳垢的；产生耳垢的
relating to or consisting of earwax.
Earwax, also known by the medical term cerumen, is a hydrophobic protective substance secreted in the human ear canal.
耳垢，也称为医学术语耳垢，是在人耳道中分泌的疏水性保护物质。
Ceruminous glands secrete earwax.
耳垢腺分泌耳垢。
Synonyms: waxy, wax-like
Antonyms: N/A
词性: adjective
