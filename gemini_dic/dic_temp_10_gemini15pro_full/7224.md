anglicised:/ˈæŋɡlɪsaɪzd/ |adj.|英语化的，英国化的|Made or adapted to conform to English modes of spelling, pronunciation, grammar, or usage.| Many foreign words have been anglicised over time. 许多外来词随着时间的推移已经被英语化了。|例句：The restaurant serves an anglicised version of Thai cuisine. 这家餐厅供应经过英国化改良的泰国菜。

例句：His name was anglicised from Schmidt to Smith. 他的名字从Schmidt英语化成了Smith。|近义词：anglified, adapted, modified|反义词：foreignized|词性：形容词


anglicise:/ˈæŋɡlɪsaɪz/ |v.|使英语化，使英国化|To make or adapt something to conform to English modes of spelling, pronunciation, grammar, or usage.|He anglicised his name when he moved to England.  他移居英国后把自己的名字英语化了。|例句：Many Irish surnames were anglicised during British rule. 在英国统治时期，许多爱尔兰姓氏被英语化了。

例句：They anglicised the spelling to make it easier for English speakers. 他们将拼写英语化，以便英语使用者更容易理解。|近义词：anglify, adapt, modify|反义词：foreignize|词性：动词
