imitatress:/ˈɪmɪteɪtrɪs/
noun
女模仿者；女演员
A woman who imitates someone or something.
She was a talented imitatress, able to mimic the voices of several celebrities. 她是一位才华横溢的女模仿者，能够模仿几位名人的声音。
synonyms: mimic, impersonator, copycat
antonyms: originator, innovator
