cloth-eared:/ˈklɔːθˌɪərd/
adjective
充耳不闻的；对音乐无欣赏力的
insensitive to musical sounds or lacking musical appreciation.
He's so cloth-eared, he can't tell the difference between a violin and a cello. 他对音乐太不敏感了，分不清小提琴和大提琴。
Synonyms: tone-deaf, unmusical
Antonyms: musical, discerning
