checkrooms:/ˈtʃɛkˌruːmz/
n.
衣帽间，行李寄存处
A room, as in a hotel, restaurant, or theater, where guests may leave coats, hats, or baggage in safekeeping.
The attendant took our coats to the checkrooms. 服务员把我们的大衣拿到衣帽间去了。
