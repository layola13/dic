nonwage:/ˌnɑːnˈweɪdʒ/
adjective
非工资的
Not consisting of or relating to wages.  Relating to benefits other than wages, such as health insurance or retirement plans.
Many employees value nonwage benefits such as flexible work hours. 许多员工重视弹性工作时间等非工资福利。
Companies are increasingly offering nonwage compensation to attract and retain employees.  公司越来越多地提供非工资报酬来吸引和留住员工。
Synonyms: fringe, employee, ancillary
Antonyms: wage, salary, monetary
词性: adjective
