limited-access highways:/ˈlɪmɪtɪd ˈækses ˈhaɪweɪz/| n.|有限制出入的高速公路|Highways designed for high-speed traffic, with on and off ramps controlling access, and generally without intersections or at-grade crossings.|Limited-access highways are much safer than regular roads.|Synonyms: freeways, expressways, motorways, turnpikes, thruways|Antonyms: surface streets, local roads|noun
