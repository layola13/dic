newsworthy:/ˈnjuːzwɜːrði/ /ˈnuːzwɜːrði/| adj.|有新闻价值的；值得报道的|Sufficiently interesting to be reported in a news broadcast or publication.|The royal wedding was a newsworthy event.  皇室婚礼是一个有新闻价值的事件。|Synonyms: notable, significant, important, remarkable|Antonyms: insignificant, trivial, unimportant|adjective
