indo-europeans:/ˌɪndoʊˌjʊərəˈpiːənz/| n.|印欧语系民族|People who speak languages descended from Proto-Indo-European (PIE), a prehistoric language.  These languages include English, Spanish, French, German, Russian, Hindi, Persian, and many others.|The Indo-Europeans migrated across vast territories thousands of years ago.|Synonyms:  Indo-European people, speakers of Indo-European languages|Antonyms: N/A|noun
