ablutions: /əˈbluːʃənz/
n.
洗礼仪式, 沐浴，净体
the act of washing oneself, especially as a religious ritual.
She performed her ablutions in the river before prayer.  她在祈祷前在河里进行了沐浴。
The pilgrims made their ablutions at the sacred spring. 朝圣者在圣泉进行了洗礼。
近义词: washing, cleansing, purification
反义词:  defilement, contamination
词性: 名词(plural noun)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French ablution, or from Latin ablutio(n-), from abluere ‘wash away’, from ab- ‘away’ + luere ‘wash’.
