believeth:/bɪˈliːvɪθ/
verb
相信
Archaic third-person singular present indicative form of believe.  It means "believes."
He believeth that the earth is flat. 他相信地球是平的。
近义词:trusts, has faith in
反义词:doubts, disbelieves
词性:verb

Etymology:
From Middle English *bileven, from Old English belīefan (“to believe, trust, hold dear; have confidence in”), from Proto-Germanic *galauƀijaną (“to believe, hold dear”), equivalent to be- +‎ lief. Cognate with Old Frisian biliva (“to believe”), Dutch geloven (“to believe”), German glauben (“to believe, think”). More at be-, lief.  The -eth ending is a archaic third person singular present tense ending.
