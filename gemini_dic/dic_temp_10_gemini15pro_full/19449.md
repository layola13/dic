bicrural: /baɪˈkruːrəl/
adjective
两足动物的；两腿的
Having two legs; relating to two legs.
A bicrural primate.  一个两足灵长类动物。
近义词: bipedal, two-legged
反义词: quadrupedal


词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *bicrūrālis, from bi- (“two”) + crūs (“leg”) + -ālis.
