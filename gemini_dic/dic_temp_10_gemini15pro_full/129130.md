nmr:/ˈenɛmˈɑːr/ (US) /ˈenɛmˈɑː(r)/ (UK)| n. |核磁共振 (Nuclear Magnetic Resonance)|A spectroscopic technique used to observe local magnetic fields around atomic nuclei. It's used in medical imaging (MRI) and various other scientific fields. |The researchers used nmr to analyze the structure of the protein.|近义词: magnetic resonance spectroscopy (MRS)|反义词: None|noun
