nonstrident:/nɒnˈstraɪdənt/ /nɑːnˈstraɪdənt/| adj. |非高音的，非尖锐的|A nonstrident sound is not loud or harsh.  It's smooth and gentle. |The flute produced a nonstrident, mellow tone.|近义词:soft, gentle, mellow, quiet|反义词:strident, harsh, loud, shrill|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From non- + strident.  "Strident" comes from the Latin "stridēre" meaning "to make a harsh noise."  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not."
