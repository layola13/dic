neohexane:/ˌniːoʊˈhɛkseɪn/| n. |新己烷|A branched-chain alkane with the molecular formula C6H14.  It is one of the five structural isomers of hexane.|Neohexane is used as a solvent in organic chemistry. 新己烷在有机化学中用作溶剂。|Synonyms: 2,2-dimethylbutane|Antonyms: N/A|noun
