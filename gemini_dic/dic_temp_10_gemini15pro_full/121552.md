mismeasuring:/ˌmɪsˈmɛʒərɪŋ/
verb
错误测量，测量不准
measuring incorrectly; making an inaccurate assessment of size, quantity, or degree.
The tailor was accused of mismeasuring the customer for his suit.  裁缝被指控错误地测量了顾客的西装尺寸。
The economist was criticized for mismeasuring the impact of the new policy.  这位经济学家因错误评估新政策的影响而受到批评。
近义词: miscalculating, misjudging, underestimating, overestimating
反义词: measuring accurately, assessing correctly
