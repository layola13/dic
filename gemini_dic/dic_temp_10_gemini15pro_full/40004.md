consultatory:/kənˈsʌltətɔːri/ | adj. | 咨询的，顾问的 |  Relating to or characterized by consultation.  | The group holds a consultatory role in the decision-making process. 该小组在决策过程中扮演咨询角色。

The government set up a consultatory committee to advise on environmental issues. 政府设立了一个咨询委员会，就环境问题提供建议。 | 近义词: advisory, consultative | 反义词: executive, decisive | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *consultatorius, from Latin consultatus, past participle of consultare “to consult,” from consulere “to consult, deliberate, consider,” from com- “together” + -sulere “to sit” (akin to sedere “to sit”)
