coranach:/ˈkɒrənɑːx/ /ˈkɔːrənɑːx/| n.|挽歌；悲歌；哀乐|A traditional Gaelic funeral lament or dirge.|The haunting coranach echoed through the glen.  凄凉的挽歌回荡在峡谷中。|近义词：lament, dirge, elegy, requiem|反义词:celebration, rejoicing|词性:名词

词源: From Scottish Gaelic *coranach*, from *comh-* (“with, together”) + *rann-* (“cry, shout”).
