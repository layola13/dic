ghastful:/ˈɡæstfəl/ (US), /ˈɡɑːstfəl/ (UK)| adj.|可怕的，恐怖的；令人毛骨悚然的|Inspiring shock or horror; frightful.  Evoking a sense of dread or terror.|例句：The ghastful apparition sent shivers down my spine.  恐怖的幽灵让我脊背发凉。|例句：She recounted the ghastful details of the accident. 她讲述了事故的可怕细节。|近义词：horrible, dreadful, terrifying, frightful, appalling|反义词：pleasant, delightful, agreeable|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *gastful, from Old English gāstful (“spiritual, inspiring awe”), equivalent to ghost +‎ -ful.  The meaning shifted from "inspiring awe" to "horrifying" over time.
