caudad:/ˈkɔːdæd/| adj. adv.|尾侧的，向尾侧|Situated toward the tail or posterior end of the body, especially in quadrupeds.  (In bipeds, the equivalent term is caudal.)|The surgeon made a caudad incision.  外科医生做了一个尾侧切口。|近义词：posterior, caudal, distal (in certain contexts)|反义词：cephalad, cranial, rostral, proximal (in certain contexts)|词性:形容词, 副词
Etymology: From Latin *caudātus* (“having a tail”), from *cauda* (“tail”).
