non-usurious:/ˌnɒn juːˈʒʊərɪəs/ |adj.|非高利贷的，合理的|Not charging excessive or exorbitant interest; at a fair or legal rate of interest.|The loan agreement was structured with non-usurious interest rates to protect the borrower. 这份贷款协议规定了非高利贷利率以保护借款人。|The bank offers non-usurious loans to small businesses. 这家银行向小型企业提供非高利贷的贷款。|Synonyms: fair, reasonable, moderate, just, equitable|Antonyms: usurious, exorbitant, excessive|adjective
