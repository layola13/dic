memoir:/ˈmemwɑːr/ (US), /ˈmɛmwɑː/ (UK)| n. |回忆录；自传|A historical account or biography written from personal knowledge or special sources.|例句：He is writing his memoirs. 他正在写他的回忆录。

例句：Her memoir of her time in Africa was a bestseller. 她关于在非洲那段时光的回忆录成了畅销书。

近义词：autobiography, reminiscence, life story, account, chronicle

反义词: N/A

词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *mémoire* "memory, reminiscence, memorandum," from Latin *memoria* "memory."
