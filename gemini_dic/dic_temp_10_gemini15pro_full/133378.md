nonextrinsic:/ˌnɑːnɪkˈstrɪnzɪk/
adjective
非外在的；内在的；固有的
Not extrinsic; inherent; intrinsic.
Her motivation for helping others is nonextrinsic; she simply enjoys making a difference. 她帮助别人的动机不是外在的；她只是喜欢做出改变。
Near Synonyms: intrinsic, inherent, innate, inborn, ingrained
Antonyms: extrinsic, external, outward, outer, superficial
词性: 形容词
