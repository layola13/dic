cathedralic:/kəˈθiːdrəlɪk/
adjective
大教堂的；主教座堂的；像大教堂的
Resembling or characteristic of a cathedral; stately and grand.
The music was cathedralic in its grandeur.  这音乐庄严宏伟，如同在大教堂里演奏一般。
The organ's cathedralic tones filled the hall.  管风琴雄浑的音调充满了大厅。
近义词:grand, majestic, imposing, stately
反义词:humble, modest, simple
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *cathedralicus, from Latin cathedra (“seat, chair”), from Ancient Greek καθέδρα (kathédra, “seat, chair”).
