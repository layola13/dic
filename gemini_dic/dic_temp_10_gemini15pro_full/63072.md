enroot:/ɪnˈruːt/ | v. | 使生根，使扎根；使固定；使深植 | To establish deeply and firmly, as if by roots. | The new CEO aimed to enroot the company's values in every employee. 新任首席执行官的目标是让公司的价值观植根于每位员工心中。

The refugees struggled to enroot themselves in their new homeland. 难民们努力在新家园扎根。| 近义词: establish, implant, embed, fix, settle | 反义词: uproot, dislodge, displace | 词性: 动词
