hyponasty:/ˈhaɪpənæsti/ | n. | 向地性，下偏生长 | Upward bending of a plant part resulting from more rapid growth of the lower side. 植物一部分由于下侧生长更快而向上弯曲。| The hyponasty of the leaves was evident after the heavy rain.  大雨过后，叶子的向地性很明显。| The hyponasty of the dandelion flower stalk allows the seed head to press against the ground. 蒲公英花茎的向地性使种子头能够贴着地面。| 近义词: epinasty (though technically the opposite process) | 反义词: epinasty | 词性: noun
