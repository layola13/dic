miscomfort:/dɪsˈkʌmfərt/ | n. | 不适，不舒服 | A feeling of slight pain or physical uneasiness. |  I felt some discomfort in my chest. 我感到胸部有些不适。

The tight shoes caused him considerable discomfort.  这双紧的鞋子让他感到很不舒服。| 近义词: unease, malaise, distress | 反义词: comfort, ease, well-being | 词性: noun

Etymology: From dis- (expressing negation) + comfort.  The word "comfort" comes from Old French "confort", ultimately from Latin "confortare" meaning to strengthen much.
