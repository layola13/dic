characterlessness: /ˈkærəktərləsnəs/
n.
缺乏个性；平庸；缺乏特征
lack of distinctive qualities or features; blandness; lack of character.
His characterlessness made him easily manipulated. 他的缺乏个性使他很容易被操纵。
近义词: blandness, insipidity, mediocrity, anonymity
反义词: individuality, distinctiveness, personality, charisma
词性: 名词

characterless: /ˈkærəktərləs/
adj.
缺乏个性的；平庸的；缺乏特征的
lacking distinctive qualities or features; bland; lacking character.
The building was a characterless block of concrete. 这栋建筑是一块毫无特色的混凝土块。
近义词: bland, insipid, mediocre, nondescript
反义词: distinctive, individual, characteristic, memorable
词性: 形容词
