noneclectics:/ˌnɒnɪˈklɛktɪks/ | n. | 非折衷主义者；不兼容并蓄者 |  People who do not adhere to an eclectic approach, preferring a singular or pure style or philosophy.  They tend to focus on one specific area or system of belief, rejecting the combination of different sources or styles. |  The noneclectics in the architectural community criticized the building's blend of modern and classical elements.  这些建筑界的非折衷主义者批评了这座建筑融合现代和古典元素的做法。| 近义词: purist, traditionalist | 反义词: eclectic, syncretist | 词性: 名词

noneclectic:/ˌnɒnɪˈklɛktɪk/ | adj. | 非折衷的；不兼容并蓄的 | Not characterized by eclecticism; adhering to a single style, doctrine, or approach. | The architect's noneclectic design focused solely on minimalist principles.  这位建筑师的非折衷设计完全专注于极简主义原则。| 近义词: purist, orthodox, unmixed | 反义词: eclectic, diverse, varied | 词性: 形容词


词源:  "Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "eclectic" (from Greek "eklektikos" meaning "selective").
