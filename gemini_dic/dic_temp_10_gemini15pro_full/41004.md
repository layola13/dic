copaiba:/koʊˈpaɪbə/ /kəˈpeɪbə/
noun
古巴香脂，科佩巴油
A resin obtained from South American trees of the genus Copaifera, used in varnishes and lacquers and formerly in medicine.
Copaiba oil is known for its anti-inflammatory properties.  古巴香脂油以其抗炎特性而闻名。
Copaiba balsam is used in some traditional medicines.  古巴香脂香脂用于一些传统药物中。
近义词:copaiba balsam, copaiba oil
反义词:None
词性:n.

copaiba:/koʊˈpaɪbə/ /kəˈpeɪbə/
adjective
古巴香脂的，科佩巴油的
Made from or containing copaiba.
Copaiba trees are native to South America.  古巴香脂树原产于南美洲。
This is a copaiba-based remedy.  这是一种基于古巴香脂的药物。
近义词:None
反义词:None
词性:adj.
