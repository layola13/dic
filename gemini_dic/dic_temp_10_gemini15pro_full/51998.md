diagnostically:/ˌdaɪəɡˈnɒstɪkli/ /ˌdaɪəɡˈnɑːstɪkli/| adv.|诊断地；用于诊断地|Used to describe something related to or used for diagnosis.|The doctor examined the patient diagnostically.|近义词:analytically, critically|反义词:therapeutically|词性:副词

Etymology:
From the adjective "diagnostic" + "-ally" (adverbial suffix). "Diagnostic" comes from the Greek "diagnōstikos," from "diagignōskein" (to distinguish, discern), from "dia-" (through, across) + "gignōskein" (to know).
