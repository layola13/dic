dogmatist:/ˈdɒɡmətɪst/ (UK) /ˈdɑːɡmətɪst/ (US)| n.|教条主义者；独断家|A person who asserts opinions as if they were facts. |例句：He is a dogmatist, unwilling to consider any views other than his own. 他是一个教条主义者，不愿意考虑除了自己以外的任何观点。

例句：The dogmatist refused to listen to any arguments against his beliefs.  这个教条主义者拒绝听取任何反对其信仰的论点。

近义词：doctrinaire, authoritarian, rigid, inflexible|反义词：freethinker, skeptic, open-minded, pragmatist|词性：noun
