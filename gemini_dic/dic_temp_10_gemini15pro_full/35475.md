coachman: /ˈkoʊtʃmən/ | n. | 马车夫；赶马车的人 | A man who drives a coach, especially a horse-drawn carriage. | The coachman expertly guided the horses through the crowded streets. 马车夫熟练地驾驶马车穿过拥挤的街道。| The coachman flicked the reins, and the horses trotted off. 马车夫轻弹缰绳，马儿小跑起来。 | synonyms: driver, charioteer | antonyms: passenger | noun
