dachshund: /ˈdɑːksˌhʊnd/ (US), /ˈdæksˌhʊnd/ (UK)| n. |腊肠犬|A breed of dog with short legs and a long body, originally bred in Germany to hunt badgers. |例句：My dachshund loves to burrow under blankets. 我的腊肠犬喜欢钻到毯子下面。

例句：The dachshund's short legs make it difficult for it to climb stairs. 腊肠犬的短腿使它爬楼梯很困难。

近义词：sausage dog, wiener dog|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
