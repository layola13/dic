balakliya:/bɑːlɐˈklɪjə/
n.
巴拉克利亚
Balakliya is a city in Kharkiv Oblast, Ukraine. 巴拉克利亚是乌克兰哈尔科夫州的一个城市。
Balakliya was occupied by Russian forces in 2022. 巴拉克利亚在2022年被俄罗斯军队占领。
近义词: city, town
反义词: (无)
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Ukrainian Балаклія (Balaklija).  The origin of the name is uncertain.  Some theories link it to Turkic origins, potentially relating to a type of fish or a geographical feature.
