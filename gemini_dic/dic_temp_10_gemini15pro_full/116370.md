masticin:/ˈmæstɪsɪn/| n. |乳香脂，乳香树脂|A resin obtained from the mastic tree (Pistacia lentiscus), used in varnishes, adhesives, and chewing gum.  It also has some medicinal properties.|The artist used masticin in his varnish to give it a unique sheen.|近义词:resin, gum, dammar|反义词:|名词


Etymology:
From Late Latin *masticinum, from Latin mastiche, from Ancient Greek μαστίχη (mastíkhē).
