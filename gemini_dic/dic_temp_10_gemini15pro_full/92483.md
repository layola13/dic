hypothalamus: /ˌhaɪpoʊˈθæləmʌs/ | n. | 下丘脑 | A region of the forebrain below the thalamus which coordinates both the autonomic nervous system and the activity of the pituitary, controlling body temperature, thirst, hunger, and other homeostatic systems, and involved in sleep and emotional activity. | The hypothalamus plays a crucial role in regulating body temperature. 下丘脑在调节体温方面起着至关重要的作用。 | The hypothalamus releases hormones that control the pituitary gland. 下丘脑释放控制垂体的激素。 |  synonyms: none (It's a specific anatomical term) | antonyms: none (It's a specific anatomical term) | noun
