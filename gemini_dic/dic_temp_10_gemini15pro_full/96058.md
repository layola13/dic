indecisions:/ˌɪndɪˈsɪʒənz/| n. |犹豫不决，优柔寡断|The state of being unable to make a decision; a lack of resolve.|Her indecisions about her career path caused her much stress. 她对职业道路的犹豫不决让她倍感压力。|Synonyms: hesitation, vacillation, wavering, doubt, uncertainty|Antonyms: decisiveness, resolution, determination|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + decision (from Latin decidere, to cut off, decide).  The suffix -ion forms nouns of action or condition.  The suffix -s marks the plural form.
