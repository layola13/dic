handiness:/ˈhændinəs/| n.|方便，便利；灵巧，熟练|The handiness of having a supermarket nearby is undeniable. 附近有超市真是太方便了。|近义词：convenience, dexterity, skill|反义词:inconvenience, clumsiness, awkwardness|词性:名词
Etymology: From handy + -ness.  "Handy" comes from Old English *hendi (attested only in compounds), probably from hand + -y.
