nonsurrealistic:/ˌnɒnsərɪəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非超现实主义的 | Not characteristic of or related to surrealism, a 20th-century avant-garde movement in art and literature that sought to release the creative potential of the unconscious mind, for example by the irrational juxtaposition of images. |  Her paintings, while abstract, were nonsurrealistic, rooted more in geometric forms than in dreamlike imagery. 她的画作虽然抽象，但并非超现实主义，更多地植根于几何形式而非梦境般的图像。

| The architect's designs were praised for their clean, nonsurrealistic lines and functional elegance.  这位建筑师的设计因其简洁、非超现实主义的线条和功能性优雅而受到赞誉。 | 近义词: realistic, representational, conventional | 反义词: surreal, surrealist, dreamlike | 词性: adjective

Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + surrealist + -ic (suffix forming adjectives)  词源：non-（前缀，表示“不”）+ surrealist + -ic（后缀，构成形容词）
