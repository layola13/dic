panderism:/ˈpændərɪzəm/| n.|拉皮条行为，迎合(低级趣味)的行为|The film was criticized for its blatant panderism to the lowest common denominator. 这部电影因其公然迎合最低级趣味而受到批评。|例句：The politician's panderism to special interest groups cost him the election.  这位政客迎合特殊利益集团的行为让他输掉了选举。

例句：The company's marketing campaign was accused of panderism, exploiting people's insecurities to sell products. 该公司的营销活动被指责为迎合大众，利用人们的不安全感来销售产品。

近义词：exploitation, appeasement, accommodation, sycophancy|反义词:resistance, defiance|词性:noun

pander:/ˈpændər/| v. |迎合，拉皮条|The media often panders to the public's desire for sensationalism. 媒体经常迎合公众对耸人听闻的消息的渴望。|例句：He refused to pander to the whims of the wealthy donors. 他拒绝迎合富裕捐赠者的奇思妙想。

例句：Some newspapers pander to their readers' prejudices. 一些报纸迎合读者的偏见。|近义词：indulge, cater to, gratify|反义词:resist, defy|词性:verb
