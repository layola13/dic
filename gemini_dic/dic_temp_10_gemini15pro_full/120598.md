The antique mirrorscope revealed intricate details of the insect's wings.  这台古董镜像显微镜展现了昆虫翅膀上复杂的细节。 | 近义词: reflecting microscope | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "conventional microscope" or "lens-based microscope") | 词性: noun
