enumerative:/ɪˈnjuːmərətɪv/ /ɪˈnuːmərətɪv/| adj.|枚举的，列举的|Serving to enumerate; counting off or listing details, one by one.|The speaker gave an enumerative list of all the benefits of the new policy.|近义词：listing, numbering, counting, itemizing, detailing|反义词:general, summary|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin enumerativus, from Latin enumerare "to count out, enumerate," from ex- "out" + numerare "to number, count," from numerus "number."
