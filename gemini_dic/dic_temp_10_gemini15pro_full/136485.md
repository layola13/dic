nonrhythm:/ˈnɒnˌrɪðəm/ | n. |无节奏；无韵律；不规则| Absence of rhythm or regularity. |The doctor detected a nonrhythm in her heartbeat. 医生发现她的心跳没有规律。|Near synonyms: arrhythmia, irregularity, disorder|Antonyms: rhythm, regularity|noun

nonrhythmic:/nɒnˈrɪðmɪk/ | adj. |无节奏的；无韵律的；不规则的|Lacking rhythm or regularity. |Her breathing was nonrhythmic and shallow. 她的呼吸没有规律而且很浅。|Near synonyms: arrhythmic, irregular, erratic|Antonyms: rhythmic, regular|adjective
