manibus:/ˈmɑːnɪbʊs/| n. (plural)|手; (比喻)力量,控制; (碑文缩写)D.M. (Dis Manibus) 献给亡灵|By the hands; with the hands; in the hands. Often used in the phrase *Dis Manibus* (D.M.), "to the Manes," an inscription on Roman tombstones.|D.M. Aurelio Valeriano.  To the Manes of Aurelius Valerianus.|Synonyms:  (in the sense of "power") control, grasp, possession|Antonyms: (in the sense of "power")  weakness, impotence|Noun (plural)
