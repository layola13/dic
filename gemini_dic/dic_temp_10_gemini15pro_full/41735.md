corrosibleness:/kəˈroʊsɪvlnəs/ |n.|腐蚀性，侵蚀性|The corrosibleness of the acid damaged the metal.  这种酸的腐蚀性损坏了金属。|Synonyms: corrosivity, causticity|Antonyms: inertness, stability|noun

corrosive:/kəˈroʊsɪv/ |adj.|腐蚀性的，侵蚀性的|The corrosive liquid ate through the pipe.  这种腐蚀性液体腐蚀了管道。|Synonyms: caustic, erosive|Antonyms: inert, protective|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *corrosivus, from Latin corrodere "to gnaw to pieces," from com- "together" + rodere "to gnaw."  -ness is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, meaning "the state or quality of being."
