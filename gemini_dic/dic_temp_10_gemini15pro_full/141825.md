n.
失落感; 孤寂感；丧失感

The orbitude of the refugees was palpable.  难民们的丧失感显而易见。
Synonyms: bereavement, desolation, loneliness, grief, sorrow
Antonyms: joy, happiness, contentment, fulfillment
