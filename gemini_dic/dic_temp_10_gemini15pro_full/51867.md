dextrose:/ˈdɛkstroʊs/| n. |葡萄糖|A simple sugar (a monosaccharide) also known as glucose or blood sugar. It is an important carbohydrate in biology and is used to treat low blood sugar.|Patients with hypoglycemia are often given dextrose. 低血糖患者通常会给予葡萄糖。|Synonyms: glucose, grape sugar|Antonyms: None|Noun

dextrose:/ˈdɛkstroʊs/| adj. |右旋的|Rotating the plane of polarized light to the right, specifically of sugars.|Dextrose is a dextro-rotatory sugar. 葡萄糖是一种右旋糖。|Synonyms: dextrorotatory|Antonyms: levorotatory|Adjective
