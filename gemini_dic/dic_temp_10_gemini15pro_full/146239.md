overexpressing:/ˌōvərɪkˈsprɛsɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度表达的，过量表达的 | Expressing a gene or protein at a higher level than normal. |  The researchers found that overexpressing this gene led to increased tumor growth. 研究人员发现，过度表达该基因会导致肿瘤生长加速。 | synonyms: overproducing, hyper-expressing | antonyms: underexpressing, downregulating | Verb/adjective


overexpress:/ˌōvərɪkˈsprɛs/ | vt. | 过度表达，过量表达 | To express a gene or protein at a higher level than normal. | The cells were engineered to overexpress the protein. 这些细胞被设计成过度表达这种蛋白质。 | synonyms: overproduce | antonyms: underexpress, downregulate | Verb
