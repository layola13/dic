imbed:/ɪmˈbed/ /ɪmˈbɛd/| vt.|嵌入，埋置|To fix something firmly within a surrounding mass.|He imbedded the jewel in the crown. 他把宝石镶嵌在王冠上。|近义词：embed, insert, implant|反义词:extract, remove|verb
