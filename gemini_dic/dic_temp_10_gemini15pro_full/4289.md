airworthiness:/ˈɛərwɜːrðɪnɪs/ | n. |适航性；耐飞性|The state of an aircraft being fit and safe to fly.  This involves meeting certain standards of design, construction, maintenance, and operation. |The airworthiness of the aircraft was questioned after the incident.  这架飞机的事故发生后，其适航性受到了质疑。|Synonyms:  fitness to fly, safety in flight |Antonyms: unserviceability, unairworthiness| noun
