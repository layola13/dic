moon: /muːn/ | n. | 月亮；卫星；月球 | The natural satellite of the Earth, visible (chiefly at night) by reflected light from the sun. | I saw the full moon last night. 我昨晚看到了满月。

The moon orbits the Earth. 月球绕地球运行。

| synonym: satellite | antonym: sun | noun
