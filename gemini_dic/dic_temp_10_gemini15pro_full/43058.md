cowlike:/ˈkaʊlaɪk/
adjective
像牛的;迟钝的
Resembling a cow in appearance or behavior; placid and slow-witted.
He stared at her with a cowlike expression. 他带着迟钝的表情盯着她。
近义词: bovine, stolid, placid, slow
反义词: alert, quick-witted, intelligent, lively
词性: adjective
