earthmad:/ˈɜːrθmæd/| adj.|疯狂的，狂热的（尤指对环境保护）| Excessively enthusiastic or passionate, especially about environmental protection.  Origin: A blend of "earth" and "mad." |例句：She's earthmad and spends all her time campaigning for green initiatives. 她对环境保护非常狂热，把所有时间都花在宣传绿色倡议上。

例句：He's become utterly earthmad, even refusing to use plastic bags. 他变得完全痴迷于环保，甚至拒绝使用塑料袋。|近义词：eco-conscious, environmentally aware, green, eco-friendly|反义词:apathetic, indifferent, unconcerned|词性:adjective
