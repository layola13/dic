malacopterygii:/ˌmæləˈkɒptəˈrɪdʒiaɪ/ /ˌmæləˈkɑːptəˈrɪdʒiːaɪ/
noun
软骨鱼纲
A large and diverse group of ray-finned fishes characterized by having fins supported by flexible, segmented rays.  They are the largest class of vertebrates, comprising over 30,000 species.
The majority of familiar fish, such as trout, salmon, and tuna, belong to the Malacopterygii.  大多数我们熟悉的鱼类，如鳟鱼、鲑鱼和金枪鱼，都属于软骨鱼纲。
The study focused on the evolutionary history of the Malacopterygii.  这项研究的重点是软骨鱼纲的进化史。
近义词:  soft-rayed fishes
反义词: Acanthopterygii (spiny-rayed fishes)
词性: noun
