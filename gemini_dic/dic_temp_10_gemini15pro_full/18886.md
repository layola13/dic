besiegers:/bɪˈsiːdʒərz/
n.
包围者，攻城者
Those who surround a place, typically a fortified location, with armed forces in order to capture it.
The besiegers bombarded the city walls day and night.  攻城者日夜轰炸城墙。
The besiegers finally breached the defenses.  攻城者最终突破了防御工事。
synonyms: attackers, assailants, invaders, beleaguerers
antonyms: defenders, protectors, besieged
noun
