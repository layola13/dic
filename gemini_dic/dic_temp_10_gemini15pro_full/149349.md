pachyderm: /ˈpækɪdɜːrm/ (US), /ˈpækɪdəːm/ (UK)| n. | 厚皮动物，尤指大象 | A large mammal with thick skin, especially an elephant, rhinoceros, or hippopotamus. |  Elephants are pachyderms known for their intelligence. 大象是厚皮动物，以它们的智力而闻名。

The zoo's pachyderm house featured elephants, rhinos, and hippos.  这家动物园的厚皮动物馆里有象、犀牛和河马。 | 近义词: elephant, rhinoceros, hippopotamus | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From French *pachyderme*, from Greek *pakhús* "thick" + *dérma* "skin".
