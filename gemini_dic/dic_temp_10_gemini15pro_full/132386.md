nondemocracies:/ˌnɒn dɪˈmɒkrəsiːz/ (US) /ˌnɑːn dɪˈmɑːkrəsiːz/
n.|非民主国家|Countries that are not governed democratically, lacking free and fair elections and often restricting citizens' rights.|Many nondemocracies have limited freedom of speech.|Synonyms: authoritarian states, dictatorships, totalitarian regimes|Antonyms: democracies, republics|Noun
