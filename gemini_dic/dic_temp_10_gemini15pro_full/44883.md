crying:/ˈkraɪɪŋ/| adj. n.|哭泣的，令人想哭的；哭泣，哭叫|The act of shedding tears, usually accompanied by sobs or other sounds of distress.  A crying shame. |例句：The baby was crying because it was hungry.  婴儿因为饿了而哭泣。

例句：It was a crying shame that the team lost the championship. 球队输掉冠军赛真是太可惜了。|近义词：weeping, sobbing, wailing, lamenting|反义词：laughing, rejoicing|词性: adjective, noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cryinge*, present participle of *cryen*, from Old French crier, from Latin quiritare (“to shriek, scream”), frequentative of quirito, of uncertain origin.
