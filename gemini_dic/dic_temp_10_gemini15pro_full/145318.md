overbroadened:/ˌoʊvərˈbrɔːdnd/| adj.| 过度扩展的，过度概括的|Extended or generalized to an excessive degree, encompassing too much or lacking necessary specificity.| The study's conclusions were overbroadened, failing to account for key demographic differences.  这项研究的结论过于宽泛，未能考虑到关键的人口差异。|例句：The lawyer argued that the law was overbroadened and infringed on free speech. 律师辩称，该法律过于宽泛，侵犯了言论自由。|近义词：overgeneralized, sweeping, expansive|反义词:narrow, specific, limited|词性:形容词

Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "broadened" (past participle of "broaden," meaning "to make or become broad or broader") The term "broad" ultimately derives from the Old English word "brād."
