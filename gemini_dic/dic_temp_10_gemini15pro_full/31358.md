characterless:/ˈkærɪktərlɪs/| adj.|缺乏个性的；平淡无奇的；缺乏特色的|Lacking in distinct or individual characteristics; bland and uninteresting.|The new building was a characterless block of concrete.  这栋新楼只是一块毫无特色的混凝土块。|The soup was rather characterless. 这汤没什么特色。|近义词：bland, insipid, nondescript, dull, uninspiring|反义词：characteristic, distinctive, individual, unique, interesting|词性: 形容词
