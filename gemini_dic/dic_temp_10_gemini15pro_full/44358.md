cross-channel:/ˌkrɒsˈtʃæn(ə)l/ /ˌkrɔːsˈtʃæn(ə)l/| adj.|跨渠道的，跨海峡的|Relating to or involving two or more different methods of communication or distribution, especially online and offline channels, or between two sides of a channel of water.|The company launched a cross-channel marketing campaign to reach a wider audience.  该公司发起了一项跨渠道营销活动，以接触更广泛的受众。|multi-channel, omnichannel|single-channel|adjective

cross-channel:/ˌkrɒsˈtʃæn(ə)l/ /ˌkrɔːsˈtʃæn(ə)l/| n.|跨海峡旅程，跨渠道策略|A journey across a channel of water, or a strategy involving multiple channels.|The cross-channel ferry was delayed due to bad weather.  跨海峡渡轮因恶劣天气延误了。|channel crossing, multi-channel strategy|-|noun
