diagrams:/ˈdaɪəɡræmz/
noun
图表，示意图
A simplified drawing showing the components of something and how they connect, interact, or are arranged.
The teacher drew a diagram of the human heart. 老师画了一张人体心脏的示意图。

diagrams:/ˈdaɪəɡræmz/
noun
图解，图示
A graphic representation of an abstract concept.
The diagrams helped to explain the complex theory. 图表帮助解释了这个复杂的理论。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *diagramma, from Greek diágramma (“geometric figure, outline, plan”), from diagráphein (“to mark out by lines, delineate”), from diá (“through, across”) + gráphō (“to write, draw”).
