例句：She gave a mirific performance, earning a standing ovation. 她做了精彩的表演，赢得了全场起立鼓掌。|近义词：wonderful, marvelous, extraordinary, amazing, splendid, superb|反义词：ordinary, mundane, commonplace, terrible, awful|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *mirificus, from Latin mirus (“wonderful, amazing, strange”) + -ficus (suffix forming adjectives meaning “making, doing, causing”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *smey- (“to smile, be amazed”).
