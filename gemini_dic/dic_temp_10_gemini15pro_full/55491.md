distractingly:/dɪˈstræktɪŋli/ | adv. | 分散注意地，令人分心的 |  in a way that prevents someone from concentrating on something. | The music was distractingly loud. 音乐吵得让人分心。| synonyms: disturbingly, distractingly, annoyingly | antonyms:  soothingly, calmingly, focusing | 词性: 副词

distract:/dɪˈstrækt/ | v. | 使分心，分散注意力 | to prevent (someone) from giving full attention to something. | The noise from the street distracted me from my work. 街上的噪音使我无法专心工作。| synonyms: divert, disturb | antonyms: focus, concentrate | 词性: 动词

distracted:/dɪˈstræktɪd/ | adj. | 分心的，心不在焉的 | unable to concentrate because one's mind is preoccupied. | He seemed distracted and didn't hear what I said. 他似乎心不在焉，没听到我说的话。| synonyms: preoccupied, inattentive | antonyms: attentive, focused | 词性: 形容词

distraction:/dɪˈstrækʃən/ | n. | 分心，分心的事物 | a thing that prevents someone from giving full attention to something else. | The TV was a constant distraction. 电视一直让人分心。| synonyms: diversion, disturbance | antonyms: focus, concentration | 词性: 名词
