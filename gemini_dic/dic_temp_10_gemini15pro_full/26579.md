cabooses:/kəˈbuːsiz/
|n.|守车（铁路货运列车尾部的车厢）|A caboose is a railroad car coupled at the end of a freight train, typically used by the train crew.|The caboose rattled at the end of the long train.|synonyms: guard's van, brake van|antonyms: engine, locomotive
|noun|
