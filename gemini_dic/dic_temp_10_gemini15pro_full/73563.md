forestay:/ˈfɔːr.steɪ/ (US) /ˈfɔː.steɪ/ (UK)| n.|前支索|A strong rope or cable leading from the top of the mast to the bow of a sailboat, used to support the mast.|The forestay snapped during the storm, causing the mast to collapse.  暴风雨中前支索断了，导致桅杆倒塌。|Synonyms: headstay, jibstay|Antonyms: backstay|noun
