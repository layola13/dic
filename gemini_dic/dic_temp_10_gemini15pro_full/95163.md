inarticulation:/ɪnɑːrˌtɪkjʊˈleɪʃən/
n.|表达不清，不善言辞|Inability to express oneself clearly or effectively, especially in speech; lack of clear or distinct pronunciation.|His inarticulation made it difficult to understand his intentions. 他的表达不清使得很难理解他的意图。|近义词：inarticulacy, incoherence, mumbling, stammering|反义词：articulacy, eloquence, fluency|词性:名词

inarticulate:/ˌɪnɑːrˈtɪkjʊlət/
adj.|说不清楚的，表达不清的|Unable to express oneself clearly or effectively in speech; not clearly expressed or pronounced.|He was inarticulate with rage. 他气得说不出话来。|近义词：inarticulable, dumb, speechless, tongue-tied|反义词：articulate, eloquent, fluent|词性:形容词

词源:
From Latin *inarticulatus*, from in- "not" + articulatus "jointed, distinct," past participle of articulare "to utter distinctly," from articulus "joint," diminutive of artus "joint."
