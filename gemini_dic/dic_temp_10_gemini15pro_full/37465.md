commissionate: /kəˈmɪʃənət/ | v. | 委任，委托 | To give someone the official authority to do something, especially in the armed forces. | The governor commissionated him as a captain in the state militia. 州长委任他为州民兵队的上尉。 |  He was commissionated to paint a portrait of the queen. 他受委托为女王画肖像。 |  appoint, authorize, delegate | dismiss, discharge | verb

commissionate: /kəˈmɪʃənət/ | adj. (obsolete, rare) |  Holding a commission; commissioned. | The commissionate officer led the troops into battle.  这位受委任的军官率领部队投入战斗。 (This usage is archaic and rarely encountered.)| appointed, authorized |  uncommissioned | adjective
