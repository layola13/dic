nonsolvent:/ˈnɒnˈsɒlvənt/ (US) /ˈnɑːnˈsɑːlvənt/| n. |非溶剂|A liquid that does not dissolve a particular substance, but instead precipitates it out of solution.|The polymer was precipitated from solution by adding a nonsolvent.|Synonyms: precipitant, antisolvent|Antonyms: solvent|noun

nonsolvent:/ˈnɒnˈsɒlvənt/ (US) /ˈnɑːnˈsɑːlvənt/| adj. |非溶解的|Not able to dissolve a particular substance.|The nonsolvent liquid caused the polymer to solidify.|Synonyms: non-dissolving|Antonyms: solvent, dissolving|adjective
