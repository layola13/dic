brotherly:/ˈbrʌðərli/
adj.

Characteristic of or befitting a brother; affectionate and protective.

近义词：fraternal, amicable, friendly
反义词：unfriendly, hostile

brother:/ˈbrʌðər/
n.
兄弟；同胞；教友
A male sibling. A person who is one's peer or who shares one's interests, experiences, or background.
He is my younger brother. 他是我的弟弟。
近义词：sibling, kin, relative
反义词：sister
词性: 名词



词源：
From Old English brōðor, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch broeder and German Bruder, from an Indo-European root shared by Latin frater.  The suffix "-liness" is used to form nouns from adjectives and denotes a state or quality.  The plural "brotherlinesses" is less common and refers to multiple instances or types of brotherly feeling.
