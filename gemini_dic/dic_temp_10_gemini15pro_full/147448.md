overobsequiousness:/ˌoʊvərˌɒbsɪˈkwiːəsnəs/
|n.| 过分殷勤，奴颜婢膝 |Excessively eager to please or obey; fawning.  Characterized by excessive deference or servility.|His overobsequiousness made his colleagues uncomfortable. 他的过分殷勤让他的同事感到不舒服。|Synonyms: servility, subservience, fawning, sycophancy|Antonyms: independence, assertiveness, boldness, defiance|noun
