bonnier:/ˈbɒniə(r)/ /ˈbɑːniər/ | adj. | 更好的；更漂亮的；更愉快的 | More bonny; more pleasant or agreeable.  | The weather is bonnier today than yesterday. 今天的天气比昨天更好。| Synonyms: prettier, more attractive, more pleasant, lovelier | Antonyms: uglier, less attractive, less pleasant, nastier | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *boner, comparative of *bon, from Old French bon “good,” from Latin bonus.  The -er ending is a comparative suffix.
