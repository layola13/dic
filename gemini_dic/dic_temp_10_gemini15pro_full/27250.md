calorific: /ˌkæləˈrɪfɪk/ | adj. | 产热的；高热量的 | Relating to the amount of heat or energy produced by something, especially food. |  This cake is very calorific.  这个蛋糕热量很高。 |Synonyms: high-calorie, energy-rich, fattening | Antonyms: low-calorie, diet, slimming | adjective

calorific: /ˌkæləˈrɪfɪk/ | adj. |  产热的；高热量的 |  Producing heat; having a high calorific value. | Burning coal is a calorific process. 燃烧煤是一个产热过程。| Synonyms:  heat-producing, exothermic | Antonyms: endothermic | adjective
