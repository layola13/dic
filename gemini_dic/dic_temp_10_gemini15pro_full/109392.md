lentiginous:/lɛnˈtɪdʒɪnəs/
adjective
多雀斑的；有黑斑的
Covered with lentigines; freckled.
Example:  The patient presented with lentiginous skin on her face and arms.  这位病人的脸部和手臂上出现了黑斑。

Example:  Lentiginous changes are common with sun exposure.  日晒会导致出现黑斑的变化。

synonym: freckled, spotted, mottled
antonym: clear, unblemished
词性: adjective
