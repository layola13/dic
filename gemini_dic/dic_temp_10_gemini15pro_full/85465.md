haslet: /ˈhæslɪt/ (US) /ˈheɪzlɪt/ (UK)| n.|肉杂碎；猪内脏香肠|A dish made from the chopped innards of a pig, often mixed with herbs and spices, and then baked or boiled.|例句：My grandmother used to make delicious haslet for Sunday lunch. 我祖母过去常常在星期天午餐做美味的肉杂碎。

例句：Haslet is a traditional dish in many parts of England. 肉杂碎是英格兰许多地区的传统菜肴。|近义词：offal, entrails, variety meat|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "prime cuts" as an opposite concept)|词性:noun
