corroding:/kəˈrəʊdɪŋ/
verb
腐蚀，侵蚀
gradually destroying or damaging something by chemical action or disease
The acid is corroding the metal. 酸正在腐蚀金属。
eroding, rusting, deteriorating
restoring, preserving, protecting
v.


corrosive:/kəˈrəʊsɪv/
adjective
腐蚀性的，侵蚀性的
having the power to cause corrosion
The effect of the acid was highly corrosive.  这种酸的腐蚀性很强。
caustic, erosive, abrasive
protective, preventative, inert
adj.


corrosion:/kəˈrəʊʒən/
noun
腐蚀，侵蚀
the process of corroding or being corroded
Rust is a common form of corrosion.  锈蚀是腐蚀的一种常见形式。
erosion, decay, deterioration
preservation, protection, restoration
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *corrodere, from com- "together" + rodere "to gnaw".
