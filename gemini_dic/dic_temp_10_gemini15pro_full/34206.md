clandestine:/klænˈdɛstɪn/ | adj. | 秘密的，暗中的，私下的 | Kept secret or done secretively, especially because illicit. |  They held a series of clandestine meetings in the back room of the cafe. 他们在咖啡馆的后屋举行了一系列秘密会议。

The clandestine operation was designed to overthrow the government.  这个秘密行动旨在推翻政府。

| 近义词：secret, covert, furtive, surreptitious, stealthy | 反义词：open, public, overt | 词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *clandestin*, from Latin *clandestinus* "secret," from *clam* "secretly," related to *celare* "to hide."
