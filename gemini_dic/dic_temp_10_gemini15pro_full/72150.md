flook:/fluːk/
noun
侥幸；偶然事件；意外的成功
a stroke of luck; an accidental happening that has a positive outcome
He won by a sheer flook. 他纯粹是侥幸获胜。
近义词:fluke, stroke of luck, godsend, windfall
反义词:misfortune, bad luck
