counterdeployments:/ˌkaʊntər dɪˈplɔɪmənts/ | n. | 反部署，对抗部署 | The act of deploying forces or resources to counter or offset an opponent's deployment.  It often involves positioning forces to defend against a perceived threat or to create a strategic advantage. |  Example 1: The counterdeployments of troops to the border helped deter further aggression.  边境部队的反部署有助于阻止进一步的侵略。

Example 2: The navy's counterdeployments were a direct response to the enemy's naval exercises. 海军反部署是对敌人海军演习的直接回应。 |  Synonyms: countermeasures, countermoves | Antonyms: deployments | noun
