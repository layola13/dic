nonincumbents:/ˌnɒnɪnˈkʌmbənts/ | n. | 非现任者；非在职者 |  A person who does not currently hold a particular office or position.  It typically refers to those running for office against an incumbent. | The nonincumbents are hoping to capitalize on public dissatisfaction with the current administration. 非现任者希望能利用公众对现任政府的不满。

The nonincumbents face an uphill battle in the upcoming election. 非现任者在即将到来的选举中面临着一场艰苦的战斗。| 近义词: challenger, contender, aspirant | 反义词: incumbent | 词性: 名词
