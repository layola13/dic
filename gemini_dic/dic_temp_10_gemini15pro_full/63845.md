epiglottises:/ˌɛpɪˈɡlɒtɪsiːz/ (US) /ˌɛpɪˈɡlɒtɪsiːz/ (UK)
n.
会厌（复数）
A flap of cartilage at the root of the tongue, which is depressed during swallowing to cover the opening of the windpipe.
During swallowing, the epiglottises prevent food from entering the trachea.  吞咽时，会厌防止食物进入气管。
The doctor examined the patient's epiglottises for inflammation. 医生检查了病人的会厌是否有炎症。
近义词: (singular) epiglottis
反义词:  无
词性: noun
