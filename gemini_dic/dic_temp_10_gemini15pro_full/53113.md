diphyozooid: /ˌdɪfaɪˈzoʊɔɪd/ | n. |  二体型个员，二体型游动体 | A free-swimming, sexually reproducing zooid of a colonial tunicate that produces gonads and buds off new blastozooids. It is the disseminating phase in the complex tunicate life cycle. |  The diphyozooid is crucial for the dispersal and propagation of the tunicate colony. 二体型个员对于海鞘群落的传播和繁殖至关重要。|  blastozooid |  oozooid | noun
Etymology:  From Greek *di- "two" + *phyo- "producing" + *zoo- "animal" + *-oid "resembling".  源自希腊语 *di- “二” + *phyo- “产生” + *zoo- “动物” + *-oid “类似”。
