over-trust:/ˌoʊvərˈtrʌst/| v. |过度信任；过分信赖|To trust someone or something more than is wise or safe. |He over-trusted his business partner and lost a lot of money. 他过度信任他的生意伙伴，损失了很多钱。|近义词：over-rely, over-depend, overestimate|反义词:distrust, doubt, suspect|词性:动词

over-trust:/ˌoʊvərˈtrʌst/| n. |过度信任；过分信赖|The act of trusting someone or something more than is wise or safe. |His over-trust in his advisor proved to be his downfall.  他对顾问的过度信任证明是他的败笔。|近义词：over-reliance, over-dependence|反义词:distrust, doubt, suspicion|词性:名词
