nuttily:/ˈnʌtɪli/
adverb
疯狂地；古怪地；傻傻地
In a nutty, crazy, or eccentric manner.
He grinned nuttily and started juggling oranges.  他傻笑着开始玩杂耍橘子。

She nuttily decided to dye her hair bright pink. 她疯狂地决定把头发染成亮粉色。
近义词: crazily, eccentrically, foolishly, insanely, oddly, peculiarly, strangely, wildly
反义词: sanely, rationally, sensibly
词性: adverb

nutty:/ˈnʌti/
adjective
坚果味的；多坚果的；古怪的；疯狂的
Containing or resembling nuts; crazy or eccentric.
This cake has a rich, nutty flavor. 这款蛋糕有着浓郁的坚果味。

He has some pretty nutty ideas. 他有一些非常古怪的想法。
近义词: crazy, eccentric, insane, mad, daft (British), bonkers (British)
反义词: sane, rational, sensible
词性: adjective

词源: From nut (n.) + -y (suffix forming adjectives).  The adverbial form, nuttily, is formed by adding -ly to the adjective nutty.
