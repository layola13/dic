词源：
From French *cadet*, from Gascon *capdet* “captain, chief, cadet”, from *cap* "head" (from Latin *caput*) + *-det* (diminutive suffix). The sense evolution in Gascon is from "head of a junior branch of a family" to "younger son serving as a soldier", later generalized to "military trainee".
