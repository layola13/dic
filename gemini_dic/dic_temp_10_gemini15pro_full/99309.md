intercreate:/ˌɪntərkriˈeɪt/
verb
互相创造；共同创造；相互作用而产生
To create through mutual interaction or reciprocal influence.
The artists intercreated a stunning mural, each building upon the others' contributions.  艺术家们共同创作了一幅令人惊叹的壁画，每个人都在其他人的贡献基础上进行创作。
The software allows users to intercreate and share their designs.  该软件允许用户共同创作和分享他们的设计。
Synonyms: collaborate, co-create, synergize
Antonyms:  isolate, separate, individualize
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- (between, among) + create (to bring into existence).
