creamish:/ˈkriːmɪʃ/ | adj. | 奶油色的，略带奶油色的 |  Of a pale cream color; resembling cream. |  The walls were painted a creamish white. 墙壁被漆成了奶油白色。|  The dress was a soft, creamish yellow. 这件连衣裙是柔和的奶油黄色。| 近义词: cream-colored, off-white, ivory | 反义词: dark, black | 词性:形容词


Etymology: cream + -ish.  "-ish" is a suffix used to form adjectives meaning "somewhat like," "rather," "somewhat," or "approximately."  Therefore, "creamish" means "somewhat like cream in color."
