flirtiest:/ˈflɜːrtiɪst/ | adj. | 最爱调情的；最轻浮的 | Superlative form of flirty: most flirty.  It describes someone who behaves in a way that suggests a playful romantic or sexual interest in others, to a greater degree than anyone else being considered. | She was the flirtiest girl at the party, batting her eyelashes at everyone. 她是在派对上最爱调情的人，对每个人都抛媚眼。

She gave him her flirtiest smile. 她给了他一个最轻浮的微笑。| 近义词: coquettish, playful, teasing | 反义词: reserved, shy, demure | 词性: 形容词
