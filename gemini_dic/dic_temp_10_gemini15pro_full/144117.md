outscored:/ˌaʊtˈskɔːrd/
verb
以较高的得分击败
To defeat by obtaining a higher score.
例句：The Lakers outscored the Celtics in the final quarter. 湖人队在最后一节得分超过了凯尔特人队。

例句：They outscored their opponents by 30 points. 他们比对手多得了30分。
近义词：beat, defeat, triumph over, surpass
反义词：lose to, be defeated by
词性：v.
