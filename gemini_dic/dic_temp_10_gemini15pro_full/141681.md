optometry: /ɒpˈtɒmətri/ (UK), /ɑːpˈtɑːmətri/ (US) | n. | 验光; 验光学; |The science and practice of examining the eyes for visual defects and prescribing corrective lenses or other visual aids. | She studied optometry at university. 她在大学学习验光。|  Synonyms: eye examination, vision care, ophthalmology (partially) | Antonyms: None | noun

Etymology: From French *optométrie*, from Ancient Greek *ὀπτός* (optos, “visible”) + *μέτρον* (metron, “measure”).
