gottfried: /ˈɡɒtfriːd/ (German pronunciation varies)| n. |戈特弗里德|A German given name, meaning "God's peace" or "protected by God". It is derived from the Germanic elements *god* "god" and *fridu* "peace".|My grandfather's name was Gottfried. 我的祖父叫戈特弗里德。|Gottfried Leibniz was a prominent German polymath. 戈特弗里德·莱布尼茨是一位杰出的德国博学家。|近义词: (As a name, no direct synonyms, but other German male names)|反义词: N/A|词性: Noun (Proper Noun)
