dynamo:/ˈdaɪnəmoʊ/ (US), /ˈdaɪnəməʊ/ (UK)| n.| 发电机；精力充沛的人；原动力|A machine that generates electricity; an energetic person; a driving force.|The old car needed a new dynamo. 这辆旧车需要一个新的发电机。|synonyms: generator, alternator, powerhouse, livewire|antonyms: deadbeat, damp squib|noun

dynamo:/ˈdaɪnəmoʊ/ (US), /ˈdaɪnəməʊ/ (UK)| adj.(informal)|  充满活力的|Energetic and forceful.|He's a real dynamo in the office, always getting things done. 他在办公室里真是个精力充沛的人，总是把事情做好。|synonyms: energetic, dynamic, vigorous, powerful|antonyms: lethargic, sluggish, inactive|adjective
