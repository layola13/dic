extracorporeal:/ˌekstrəkɔːrˈpɔːriəl/
adjective
体外的；体外进行的
Existing or occurring outside the body.
例句：Extracorporeal membrane oxygenation (ECMO) is a life-support technique used when a patient's lungs or heart are unable to function adequately.  体外膜肺氧合 (ECMO) 是一种在患者的肺或心脏无法充分发挥功能时使用的生命支持技术。

例句：Extracorporeal lithotripsy uses shock waves to break up kidney stones without surgery. 体外碎石术使用冲击波在无需手术的情况下击碎肾结石。

近义词：external, outside, outer
反义词：internal, inside, inner
词性：adjective
