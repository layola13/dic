depress:/dɪˈprɛs/ /dɪˈprɛs/| v. |使沮丧，使消沉；降低，减少；按下|To make someone feel sad and without hope; to reduce the level or strength of something; to press something down.|The news depressed him. (这个消息使他沮丧。) / The recession has depressed house prices. (经济衰退降低了房价。) /  Depress the button to start the machine. (按下按钮启动机器。) | synonyms: sadden, discourage, lower, reduce | antonyms: elate, encourage, raise, increase | verb

depress:/dɪˈprɛs/ /dɪˈprɛs/| adj. | 沮丧的，消沉的；萧条的 | Feeling sad and without hope; experiencing a period of reduced economic activity. |  He felt depressed after losing his job. (他失业后感到沮丧。) / The economy is in a depressed state. (经济处于萧条状态。) | synonyms: downcast, dejected, gloomy | antonyms: cheerful, happy, buoyant | adjective
