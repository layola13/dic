overwhip: /ˌōvərˈhwɪp/ | vt. | 过度搅拌，过度打发 | To beat (cream, eggs, etc.) too much, resulting in a stiff or dry texture. |  The chef overwhipped the cream, and it became grainy.  厨师过度搅拌了奶油，导致它变得颗粒状。| overbeat, overmix | underwhip, underbeat | verb

overwhip: /ˌōvərˈhwɪp/ | n. | 过度搅拌的状态 | The state of being overwhipped. | The overwhip of the egg whites made the meringue brittle. 蛋白过度搅拌使得蛋白酥脆易碎。| - | - | noun
