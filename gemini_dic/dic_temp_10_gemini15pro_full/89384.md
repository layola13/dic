homophylic:/ˌhɒməˈfɪlɪk/ |adj.|同嗜性的，同性的，|Tending to associate with others of like type, especially with reference to sexual orientation.  Relating to or exhibiting homophily.|Homophylic relationships are common in social networks. 同嗜性关系在社交网络中很常见。|Synonyms: like-minded, similar, kindred, like-attracts-like|Antonyms: heterophilic, dissimilar, unlike|adjective
