ideologically:/ˌaɪdiəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 思想上地，意识形态上地 | In a way that relates to or is influenced by a particular set of ideas or beliefs, especially concerning political or economic theory and policy. |  The two parties are ideologically opposed. 这两个党派在意识形态上是对立的。|  The book was ideologically motivated. 这本书是出于意识形态的动机而写的。|Synonyms: doctrinally, theoretically, politically | Antonyms: pragmatically, practically | 词性: 副词
