floes:/floʊz/
noun
浮冰群；大块浮冰
Large flat masses of ice floating on the sea.
例句：The ship navigated carefully through the treacherous floes.  这艘船小心翼翼地在危险的浮冰群中航行。

例句：Polar bears often hunt seals on the edges of the floes. 北极熊经常在浮冰边缘捕猎海豹。

近义词：ice floes, ice sheets, pack ice
反义词：(No direct antonym, possibly open water)
词性:n.
