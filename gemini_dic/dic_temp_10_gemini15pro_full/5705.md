alumni: /əˈlʌmnaɪ/ (plural) /əˈlʌmnəs/ (singular masculine) /əˈlʌmnə/ (singular feminine)
noun
毕业生；校友
A former student or pupil of a particular school, college, or university.
例句：The alumni of Harvard University have made significant contributions to society. 哈佛大学的校友们为社会做出了重大贡献。

例句：The university invited all its alumni to the anniversary celebration.  大学邀请了所有的校友参加周年庆典。


近义词：graduate, former student
反义词: current student,  undergraduate
词性: noun
