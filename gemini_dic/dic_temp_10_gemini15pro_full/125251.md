multidisciplinary:/ˌmʌltiˈdɪsəplɪnəri/ | adj. | 多学科的，跨学科的 | Combining or involving several academic disciplines or professional specializations. |  Example: The university offers a multidisciplinary program in environmental studies. 这所大学提供环境研究的多学科课程。 | Example: A multidisciplinary team of doctors and therapists worked with the patient. 一个由医生和治疗师组成的多学科团队与病人一起工作。 | Synonyms: interdisciplinary, cross-disciplinary | Antonyms: specialized, unintegrated | word type: adjective
