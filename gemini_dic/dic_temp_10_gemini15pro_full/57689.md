drawlingnesses:/ˈdrɔːlɪŋnəsɪz/
n.
慢吞吞的说话方式; 拖长腔调的说话习惯
The quality or state of being drawling; a slow, lengthened manner of speaking.
His drawlingnesses made it difficult to understand what he was saying. 他的拖腔拉调的说话方式让人很难听懂他在说什么。
近义词: drawls, slow speech, prolonged speech
反义词: rapid speech, quick speech
词性: noun
