aetiological:/ˌiːtiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ /ˌiːtioʊˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
adjective
病因学的；病原学的
Relating to the causes or origins of diseases.
The aetiological factors of lung cancer are complex and multifactorial. 肺癌的病因复杂且多因素。
The study focuses on the aetiological role of viruses in various cancers. 这项研究集中于病毒在各种癌症中的病因作用。
Synonyms: causal, causative
Antonyms: resultant, consequential
词性: 形容词
