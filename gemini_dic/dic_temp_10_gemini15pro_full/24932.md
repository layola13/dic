brunhild: /ˈbruːnhɪld/ | n. | 布伦希尔德 | A female given name, taken from the heroine of the Germanic and Norse legend, a Valkyrie and shield maiden.  | Brunhild was a powerful warrior queen in Norse mythology. 布伦希尔德是北欧神话中一位强大的战士女王。|  Brunhild defied Odin and was punished by being put into a magical sleep. 布伦希尔德违抗了奥丁，被惩罚陷入魔法沉睡。 | 近义词: Valkyrie, shieldmaiden | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Proto-Germanic *Brunjōhildō, composed of *brunją (“breastplate, coat of mail”) + *hildjō (“battle”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰerǵʰ- (“high, elevated”) and *kel- (“to strike, cut”).
