linkwork: /ˈlɪŋkwɜːk/ (US), /ˈlɪŋkwɜːk/ (UK)
noun
连杆机构，联杆系统
A system of interconnected rods or links, used to transmit motion and force in a machine.
The clock's intricate linkwork keeps precise time.  时钟复杂的连杆机构保持着精准的时间。
The robotic arm's linkwork allows for a wide range of movement.  机械手臂的连杆机构使其能够进行大范围的运动。
mechanism, linkage, gearing
