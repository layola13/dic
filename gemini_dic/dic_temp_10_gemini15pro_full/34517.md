cleanest:/ˈkliːnɪst/ /ˈkliːnəst/ | adj. |最干净的|Superlative form of clean; free from dirt, marks, or impurities to the greatest extent.|例句：This is the cleanest room in the house. 这是房子里最干净的房间。|例句：She has the cleanest car I've ever seen. 她有我见过的最干净的车。|近义词：tidiness, purest, immaculate, spotless |反义词：dirtiest, filthiest|词性:形容词

词源：From Middle English *clenest, *clennest, from Old English clǣnost (superlative of clǣne (“clean”)). Cognate with West Frisian kleanst (“cleanest”), Dutch schoonst (“cleanest”), German cleanest (“cleanest”), Swedish renaste (“cleanest”).
