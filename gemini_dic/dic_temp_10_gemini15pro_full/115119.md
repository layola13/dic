mangily:/ˈmæŋɡɪli/| adv.| 残缺地，不完整地，拙劣地| in a mangled or damaged condition; clumsily; badly; imperfectly | The report was written mangily, with numerous errors and inconsistencies. 这份报告写得很糟糕，错误百出，前后矛盾。|近义词：badly, clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly, poorly|反义词: skillfully, expertly, adeptly, proficiently|词性:副词

词源: From "mangle" + "-ly."  "Mangle" comes from the Old French "mangonel," meaning a catapult or siege engine, later referring to a machine for smoothing linen, which could also tear or crush fabric if misused.  This led to the meaning of "to mutilate or damage."
