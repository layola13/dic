bouillabaisse:/ˌbuːjəˈbeɪs/ | n. | 法式海鲜汤 | A rich, spicy stew or soup made with various kinds of fish, shellfish, and vegetables, typically flavored with saffron, fennel, and garlic. |  We had a delicious bouillabaisse at the restaurant by the sea. 我们在海边的餐厅吃了一顿美味的法式海鲜汤。 |  seafood stew, fish stew | soup, chowder | noun

Etymology:
From French *bouillabaisse*, from Occitan *bolhabaissa*, from *bolhir* (to boil) + *abaissar* (to lower the heat). The cooking process involves boiling the ingredients and then reducing the heat to simmer.
