leveraged:/ˈliːvərɪdʒd/ /ˈlɛvərɪdʒd/| adj.|杠杆化的，举债经营的|Using borrowed capital for (an investment), expecting the profits made to be greater than the interest payable.|The company is highly leveraged and vulnerable to interest rate hikes. 这家公司杠杆率很高，很容易受到利率上调的影响。|Synonyms: geared, borrowed, financed|Antonyms: unleveraged, debt-free|adjective


leveraged:/ˈliːvərɪdʒd/ /ˈlɛvərɪdʒd/| v. |利用；对…施加影响（leverage的过去式和过去分词）|To use (something valuable) to maximum advantage.|She leveraged her connections to get the job. 她利用她的人脉关系得到了这份工作。|Synonyms: utilize, exploit, capitalize on|Antonyms: waste, neglect|verb
