deaf's:/dɛfs/ | n. | 聋人的（财产、权利等） |  Possessive form of "deaf," referring to something belonging to or associated with deaf people.  Rarely used, often considered grammatically awkward.  It's generally better to rephrase. |  The deaf's rights should be protected. (Better: The rights of deaf people should be protected.) | 近义词:  (No direct synonym - rephrase using "of the deaf") | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性:  Noun (possessive)
