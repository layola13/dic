chromolithographic:/ˌkroʊməlɪθəˈɡræfɪk/| adj.|彩色石印的|Relating to or produced by chromolithography, a method of printing in color from a series of stone or zinc plates.|The antique book contained beautiful chromolithographic illustrations. 这本古董书包含精美的彩色石印插图。|Near Synonyms: color-printed, lithographic, multicolored|Antonyms: monochrome, black and white|Etymology: chromo- (color) + litho- (stone) + -graphic (writing)|
