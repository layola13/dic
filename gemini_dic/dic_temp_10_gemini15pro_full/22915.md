bothrenchyma: /ˌboʊθrɛŋˈkaɪmə/ | n. | 坑状薄壁组织 | A type of parenchyma tissue with large intercellular spaces, characteristic of aquatic plants. |  The aerenchyma, a type of bothrenchyma, helps the aquatic plant float.  坑状薄壁组织，一种具有大型细胞间隙的薄壁组织，是水生植物的特征。| Synonyms: aerenchyma, spongy parenchyma | Antonyms: sclerenchyma | Noun
