maturely:/məˈtʃʊrli/ /məˈtʃʊərli/
adverb|成熟地，稳重地|In a manner characteristic of maturity; in a way that shows wisdom and good judgment.|She handled the difficult situation maturely.|近义词:responsibly, sensibly, prudently, wisely|反义词:immaturely, childishly, foolishly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From "mature" + "-ly".  "Mature" comes from the Old French "maturer" and ultimately from the Latin "maturus" meaning "ripe" or "early." The suffix "-ly" is a common adverbial ending.
