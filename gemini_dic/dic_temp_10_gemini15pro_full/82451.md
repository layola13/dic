grumbly: /ˈɡrʌmbli/ (US), /ˈɡrʌmbli/ (UK)| adj.|脾气坏的，爱抱怨的|Inclined to grumble; bad-tempered.  Characterized by or expressing grumbling.|He was grumbly all morning because he hadn't had enough sleep. 他整个早上都脾气暴躁，因为他没睡够。|近义词：grouchy, grumpy, irritable, cantankerous|反义词：cheerful, good-natured, pleasant|词性:形容词
词源: From grumble + -y.  Grumble itself comes from Middle English *grumbeln, of imitative origin.
