lovey:/ˈlʌvi/| n.|爱人，亲爱的，宝贝|A term of endearment, similar to "sweetheart" or "darling".|例句：He called his wife "lovey". 他叫他的妻子“宝贝”。

例句：She gave her lovey a kiss. 她吻了她的爱人。


近义词：sweetheart, darling, dear, honey, babe|反义词:enemy, foe|词性:noun
