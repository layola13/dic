imperceived:/ˌɪmpərˈsiːvd/
adjective
未被察觉的，未被注意到的
Not noticed or realized.
The subtle shift in power went imperceived by most people. 权力微妙的转移大多数人都没有察觉到。
The bird flew away imperceived. 这只鸟飞走了，没有人注意到。

近义词:unnoticed, unobserved, unseen, undetected
反义词:perceived, noticed, observed, seen, detected
词性:adjective

词源:
From in- (not) + perceived (past participle of perceive, from Latin percipere, "to seize thoroughly, grasp," from per- "thoroughly" + capere "to take").
