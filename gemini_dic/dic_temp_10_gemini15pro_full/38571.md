concolorous: /kənˈkʌlərəs/
adjective
同色的，单色的
Having the same color throughout; uniformly colored.
The concolorous plumage of the bird made it difficult to spot against the sky.  这只鸟的单色羽毛使它很难在天空中被发现。
The artist used a concolorous palette of blues and greens. 艺术家使用了蓝色和绿色的单色调色板。
Synonyms: monochromatic, uniform, self-colored, one-colored, solid-colored
Antonyms: multicolored, variegated, polychromatic, motley
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *concolor*, from *con-* (together, with) + *color* (color).
