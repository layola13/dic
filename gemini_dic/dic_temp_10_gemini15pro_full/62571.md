energizing:/ˈɛnərdʒaɪzɪŋ/| adj.|充满活力的，使人精神焕发的|Giving energy; invigorating.  Making someone feel more energetic.|例句：A brisk walk in the morning can be very energizing. 早上轻快的散步可以让人精神焕发。|近义词：invigorating, stimulating, refreshing, revitalizing|反义词：enervating, tiring, exhausting|词性:形容词
Etymology: From energize + -ing.  Energize comes from energy + -ize. Energy ultimately derives from the Greek "energeia" meaning "activity, operation."
