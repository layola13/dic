dispossesses:/dɪspəˈzɛsɪz/
verb
剥夺；使失去
to deprive someone of land, property, or other possessions.
The landlord dispossessed the tenant for not paying the rent. 房东因房客不付房租而将其驱逐。
Synonyms: evict, oust, expel, deprive
Antonyms: provide, give, endow
词性: 动词
