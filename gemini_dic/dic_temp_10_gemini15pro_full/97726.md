inirritative: /ɪnˈɪrɪtətɪv/
adjective
无刺激性的；非刺激性的
Not irritative; not causing irritation.
This cream is inirritative and suitable for sensitive skin.  这款面霜无刺激性，适合敏感肌肤。
The bandage is inirritative and won't cause further discomfort.  这种绷带没有刺激性，不会引起进一步的不适。
近义词: nonirritating, soothing, bland
反义词: irritating, stimulant, irritant
词性: adjective
