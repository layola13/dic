diversly:/daɪˈvɜːrsli/
adverb
不同地，多样地
in different ways; variously.
The funds are diversly invested in stocks and bonds. 这些资金以不同的方式投资于股票和债券。
Near synonyms: variously, differently, diversely
Antonyms: similarly, uniformly
词性: 副词
