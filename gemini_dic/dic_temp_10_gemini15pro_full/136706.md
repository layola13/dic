nonseizure:/ˌnɒnˈsiːʒə(r)/
adjective
非癫痫性的
Not relating to or characterized by a seizure (especially an epileptic seizure).
The doctor determined the episode was a nonseizure event. 医生确定这次发作不是癫痫发作。
The patient experienced a nonseizure syncopal episode.  病人经历了一次非癫痫性晕厥。
近义词: nonepileptic
反义词: seizure, epileptic

词性: adjective
