martyrdom:/ˈmɑːrtərdəm/ | n. |殉道；殉教；牺牲；折磨；痛苦|The state of being a martyr, especially of being killed for one's religious beliefs or suffering greatly for a cause. |She endured years of martyrdom for her political beliefs. 她因为自己的政治信仰遭受了多年的折磨。|Synonyms: sacrifice, immolation, suffering, agony, torment|Antonyms: comfort, ease, pleasure|Noun

martyrdom:/ˈmɑːrtərdəm/ | n. |殉难；殉教；（为事业而受的）苦难|The condition of being a martyr. |He suffered martyrdom for his faith. 他因信仰而殉难。|Synonyms: sacrifice, immolation, suffering|Antonyms: comfort, bliss|Noun
