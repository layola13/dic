heralded: /ˈherəldɪd/ | v. | 预示，预告；宣布（…的到来）；赞扬，称颂 |  publicly announced or praised something or someone's arrival or appearance. | The invention of the internet was heralded as a new era of communication. 互联网的发明被誉为通讯新纪元。|  The warm sunshine heralded the arrival of spring. 温暖的阳光预示着春天的到来。| synonyms: announced, proclaimed, signaled, foreshadowed, acclaimed, praised | antonyms: concealed, hidden, suppressed, ignored | verb

heralding: /ˈherəldɪŋ/ | n. | 预示，预告；宣布 | the act of publicly announcing or praising something or someone's arrival or appearance. | The heralding of a new product often involves a large marketing campaign. 新产品的发布通常伴随着大规模的营销活动。 | The robin's song is a heralding of spring. 知更鸟的歌声预示着春天的到来。 | synonyms: announcement, proclamation, signaling, foreshadowing, acclaim, praise | antonyms: concealment, suppression, ignorance | noun
