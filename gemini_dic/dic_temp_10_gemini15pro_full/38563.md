concocting:/kənˈkɒktɪŋ/ /kənˈkɑːktɪŋ/
verb
调制，配制；编造，捏造
Creating or devising something, typically a dish, drink, or story, by combining various ingredients or elements.
She is concocting a new recipe for her bakery. 她正在为她的面包店调制一个新配方。
The children were concocting a story about a magical land. 孩子们正在编造一个关于魔法王国的故事。
Synonyms: devising, formulating, preparing, inventing, fabricating, brewing
Antonyms: analyzing, deconstructing, dismantling
