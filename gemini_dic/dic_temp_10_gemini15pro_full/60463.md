She either walks or takes the bus to work. 她要么步行要么乘公共汽车去上班。
He doesn't like either of the options. 他两个选项都不喜欢。

Present Continuous:
I'm not sure which book I want, either one is fine. 我不确定我想要哪本书，哪本都行。
They are either working or studying right now. 他们现在不是在工作就是在学习。


Present Perfect:
I haven't been to either of those restaurants. 我没去过那两家餐馆。
She has either finished the project or is very close to finishing. 她要么已经完成了项目，要么就快完成了。


Present Perfect Continuous:
They have either been working on this problem all day or just haven't started.  他们不是一整天都在处理这个问题，就是还没开始。
He hasn't been feeling well, either physically or mentally.  他的身体或精神状况一直不太好。

Simple Past:
We either ate at home or ordered takeout last night. 我们昨晚要么在家吃饭，要么叫了外卖。
He didn't like either movie. 他两部电影都不喜欢。


Past Continuous:

She wasn't either sleeping or studying, she was just daydreaming. 她既没在睡觉也没在学习，只是在做白日梦。

Past Perfect:
I had either forgotten or lost my keys. 我不是忘了就是丢了钥匙。
They had either finished the project or decided to abandon it. 他们要么完成了项目，要么决定放弃。


Past Perfect Continuous:

Simple Future:
I will either go to the party or stay home. 我要么去参加派对，要么待在家里。
She will not go either way.  她哪边都不会去。


Future Continuous:
This time next week, I will either be traveling or relaxing at home. 下周的这个时候，我要么在旅行，要么在家放松。
They will either be working or on vacation. 他们要么在工作，要么在度假。


Future Perfect:
By next year, I will have either finished my degree or decided to change majors. 到明年，我要么完成我的学位，要么决定换专业。
She will have either moved out or found a roommate. 她要么会搬出去，要么会找到一个室友。


Future Perfect Continuous:
By next month, I will have either been working here for a year or have quit. 到下个月，我要么在这里工作满一年，要么已经辞职了。

Subjunctive Mood:

Passive Voice:

Interrogative sentences:
