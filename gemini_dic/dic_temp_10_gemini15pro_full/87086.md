heptoic:/hɛpˈtoʊɪk/
adjective
庚酸的；含有七个碳原子的
relating to or containing heptane, especially in having a chain of seven carbon atoms in a molecule.
Heptoic acid is a carboxylic acid. 庚酸是一种羧酸。
Heptoic acid has an unpleasant rancid odor. 庚酸有一种令人不愉快的腐臭味。
近义词:heptanoic
反义词:(No common antonym)
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From heptane + -oic acid.  Heptane ultimately derives from Greek *heptá "seven".
