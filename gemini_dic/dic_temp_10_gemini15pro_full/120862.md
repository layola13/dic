misbelief:/ˌmɪsbɪˈliːf/
n.
错误的信念，误信
An incorrect belief.
例句：His misbelief about the effectiveness of the treatment led him to refuse conventional medicine. 他对这种疗法有效性的误信导致他拒绝接受传统药物治疗。

例句：Common misbeliefs about nutrition can be harmful to one's health. 对营养的常见误解可能对健康有害。

近义词：fallacy, delusion, misconception
反义词：belief, truth, fact
词性：名词

词源：mis- (prefix meaning "wrong" or "bad") + belief (noun meaning "acceptance that something exists or is true")
