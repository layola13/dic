deflated:/dɪˈfleɪtɪd/
adjective
泄气的；灰心的；瘪的
Reduced in size or importance; emptied of air or gas.

The deflated balloon lay on the floor.  瘪了的气球躺在地板上。

His ego was deflated after failing the exam.  考试不及格后，他的自尊心受到了打击。

近义词: discouraged, disheartened, depressed, flattened, emptied
反义词: inflated, encouraged, elated, pumped up
词性: adjective

deflate:/dɪˈfleɪt/
verb
放气；使泄气；使灰心
To release air or gas from; to reduce in size or importance.

He deflated the tires before storing the car for the winter. 他在冬天存放汽车之前把轮胎放了气。

The harsh criticism deflated her confidence.  严厉的批评打击了她的自信心。

近义词: collapse, shrink, flatten, empty, discourage
反义词: inflate, expand, encourage, boost
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *deflātus*, past participle of *deflāre* (“to blow down, deflate”), from *de-* (“down, away from”) + *flāre* (“to blow”).
