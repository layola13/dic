metamerism:/ˈmɛtəmərɪzəm/| n. |异构，同分异构现象，分节同构|The phenomenon of having the same empirical formula but different structural formulas, or of different compounds exhibiting similar or identical spectral properties.|The researchers studied metamerism in butterflies to understand their camouflage. 研究人员研究了蝴蝶的异构现象，以了解它们的伪装。|Synonyms: isomerism, segmentation|Antonyms: homogeneity, uniformity|noun

metamerism:/ˈmɛtəmərɪzəm/| n. |分节同构，体节重复|The repetition of body parts in a series of segments, as in earthworms.|Earthworms display metamerism, with repeating segments along their bodies. 蚯蚓表现出分节同构，身体上有重复的节段。|Synonyms: segmentation, metameric segmentation|Antonyms:  non-segmentation|noun
