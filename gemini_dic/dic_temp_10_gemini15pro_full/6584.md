anacanthini:/ænəˈkænθɪnaɪ/
noun
棘鳍鱼类
A suborder of fishes, characterized by the absence of spiny fin rays.  They are mostly deep-sea fish.
The cod and haddock are members of the Anacanthini. 鳕鱼和黑线鳕是棘鳍鱼类。
Synonyms: Anacanthiformes
No antonyms found.
