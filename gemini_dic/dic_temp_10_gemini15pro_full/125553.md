multistriate:/ˌmʌltiˈstraɪeɪt/
adjective
多纹的，多条纹的
Having multiple striae or stripes.  Relating to or involving multiple striate areas of the brain, particularly those beyond the primary visual cortex (V1).
The multistriate areas of the brain play a crucial role in processing complex visual information. 大脑的多纹状区在处理复杂的视觉信息中起着至关重要的作用。
Synonyms: multistriped, polystriate
Antonyms: unstriped, smooth
词性: adjective
