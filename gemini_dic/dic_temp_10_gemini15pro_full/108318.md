laurinol:/ˈlɔːrɪnɒl/ | n. |月桂醇，十二烷醇| A fatty alcohol found in bayberries and used in perfumes and cosmetics.  It is a white crystalline solid at room temperature. |  Laurinol is a common ingredient in many shampoos. 月桂醇是许多洗发水中常见的成分。| Laurinol gives some soaps their characteristic lather. 月桂醇赋予一些肥皂特有的泡沫。 | synonyms: dodecanol, dodecyl alcohol | antonyms: N/A | noun
