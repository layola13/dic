limed:/laɪmd/
v.|涂上石灰,用石灰处理,变得像石灰一样(颜色或质地)|The walls were limed white. 墙壁被涂成了白色。|whitewashed, plastered|unlimed|verb
limed:/laɪmd/
adj.|涂过石灰的,像石灰一样的|The limed walls gave the room a clean, bright feel.  涂了石灰的墙壁使房间感觉干净明亮。|whitewashed, chalky|dark, unlimed|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From lime (n.) + -ed.  The verb sense comes from the practice of using lime (calcium oxide) for various purposes, including whitewashing and treating soil.
