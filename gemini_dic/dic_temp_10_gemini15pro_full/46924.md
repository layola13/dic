darlingest:/ˈdɑːrlɪŋɪst/ /ˈdɑːrlɪŋɡɪst/| adj.|最亲爱的，最心爱的|A term of endearment, a superlative form of "darling," expressing great affection.|例句：My darlingest friend, I miss you so much. 我最亲爱的朋友，我非常想念你。

例句：Happy birthday, my darlingest daughter. 生日快乐，我最亲爱的女儿。

近义词：dearest, beloved, precious, sweetheart|反义词:detested, hated|词性:形容词
