haying:/ˈheɪɪŋ/ | n. | 割草；干草；制作干草 | the process of cutting grass and drying it for use as animal fodder |  They were haying in the field all day. 他们在田里割了一整天草。 | 近义词：grass cutting, haymaking | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun
词源：From Old English *hēgung (unattested), from hēg "hay".
