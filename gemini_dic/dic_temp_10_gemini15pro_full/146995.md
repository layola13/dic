overkill:/ˌoʊvərˈkɪl/ | n. | 过度杀伤；矫枉过正；画蛇添足 |  The use of excessive force or action that is much greater than is necessary. | Using a nuclear bomb to kill a mosquito would be overkill. 用核弹杀死一只蚊子就是杀鸡用牛刀。 | overkill: excess, superfluity, overkill. | understatement, insufficiency | noun


overkill:/ˌoʊvərˈkɪl/ | v. |  过度杀伤；做得过分 | To use excessive force or action; to do something to an excessive degree. |  They completely overkilled the advertising campaign; it was on every channel and in every magazine. 他们的广告宣传做得太过火了，每个频道和每本杂志上都有。| overdo, exaggerate, overplay | underdo, understate | verb
