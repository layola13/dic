dabbling:/ˈdæblɪŋ/
dabbling| n. | 浅尝辄止，涉猎 | The act of engaging in something superficially or intermittently. |  He's been dabbling in photography for a few years. 他玩摄影好几年了。 |  experimentation, tinkering |  immersion, mastery | noun

dabbling:/ˈdæblɪŋ/
dabbling| adj. | 浅尝辄止的，涉猎的 |  Engaging in something superficially or intermittently. |  He's a dabbling artist, but not a professional. 他是个业余艺术家，但不是专业的。 |  amateur, superficial |  professional, dedicated | adjective

dabble:/ˈdæbl/
dabble| v. | 浅尝，涉猎，戏水 | To engage in something superficially or intermittently; to splash playfully. | She dabbles in painting but doesn't have much time for it. 她偶尔画画，但没有太多时间。 |  experiment, tinker |  master, specialize | verb
