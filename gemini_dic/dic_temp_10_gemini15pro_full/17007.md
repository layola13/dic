bat-the-breeze:/bæt ðə briːz/
verb phrase
闲聊，吹牛
to chat idly or casually, especially about unimportant matters.
例句：They spent the afternoon batting the breeze on the porch. 他们在门廊上闲聊了一下午。

例句：We were just batting the breeze about the old days. 我们只是在闲聊过去的日子。

近义词：chat, gab, shoot the breeze, chew the fat
反义词：discuss seriously, debate, negotiate
词性：动词短语
