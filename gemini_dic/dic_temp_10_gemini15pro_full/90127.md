hot-wire:/ˈhɒtˌwaɪə(r)/ (US) /ˈhɑːtˌwaɪr/| v. |启动(汽车等); 绕过(安全系统); |To start (a car) by connecting the ignition wires directly, often bypassing the ignition switch.  Also, to bypass any security system. |He hot-wired the car and sped off. 他启动了汽车的电路，然后飞驰而去。|Synonyms: bypass, jump, circumvent|Antonyms: secure, protect|verb

hot-wired:/ˈhɒtˌwaɪəd/ (US) /ˈhɑːtˌwaɪrd/ | adj. | (非正式)兴奋的，紧张的 | Excited, nervous, or edgy. |The kids were hot-wired after all the sugar they ate. 孩子们吃了那么多糖后都兴奋不已。 |Synonyms: excited, wired, edgy, jittery|Antonyms: calm, relaxed, tranquil|adjective
