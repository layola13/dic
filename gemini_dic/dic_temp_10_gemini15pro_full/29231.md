cassandra:/kəˈsændrə/ | n. | 卡桑德拉 |  In Greek mythology, Cassandra was a Trojan princess cursed to utter true prophecies but never to be believed.  A "Cassandra" refers to someone who predicts misfortune or disaster, especially one who is disregarded. |  He was a Cassandra, warning of the impending financial crisis, but no one listened. 他就像卡桑德拉一样，警告即将到来的金融危机，但没有人听。| synonym: prophetess, seer, doomsayer | antonym: optimist | noun

cassandra:/kəˈsændrə/ | n. |卡桑德拉（分布式NoSQL数据库） | A free and open-source distributed wide-column store NoSQL database management system designed to handle large amounts of data across many commodity servers, providing high availability with no single point of failure. |  We chose Cassandra for our database because of its scalability and fault tolerance. 我们选择Cassandra作为我们的数据库是因为它的可扩展性和容错性。| synonym: database, data store | antonym: N/A | noun
