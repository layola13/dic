linesmen:/ˈlaɪnzmən/| n.|边线裁判；线路工人|An official who assists the referee in sports such as soccer or American football, primarily by judging whether the ball has gone out of play at the sidelines. Also, a person who installs or maintains power lines.|The linesman raised his flag to signal the ball was out of bounds. 边线裁判举旗示意球出界了。|The linesmen worked tirelessly to restore power after the storm. 线路工人们在暴风雨后不知疲倦地工作以恢复电力。|Synonyms: assistant referee (sports), lineman (singular), lineworker|Antonyms: N/A|noun
