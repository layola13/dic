alluringness:/əˈlʊrɪŋnɪs/ | n. | 魅力，诱惑力 | The power to entice or attract through personal charm. | The alluringness of the tropical island made it a popular tourist destination.  热带岛屿的魅力使其成为热门旅游胜地。|Synonyms: temptress, siren, enchantress (for a person) , appeal, draw | Antonyms: repellent, deterrent | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From alluring + -ness.  Alluring comes from the Middle English *aluren*, from Anglo-French *alurer* "to lure, entice," ultimately from Germanic origin. The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
