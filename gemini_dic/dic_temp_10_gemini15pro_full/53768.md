discomfortingly:/dɪsˈkʌmfərtɪŋli/
adverb|令人不安地；不舒服地|In a way that makes someone feel slightly uncomfortable or worried. |The silence in the room was discomfortingly long. 房间里的沉默长得令人不安。|近义词:uneasily, disturbingly, unsettlingly|反义词:comfortably, reassuringly|词性:副词
词源:From discomforting + -ly. Discomforting from discomfort + -ing. Discomfort from dis- + comfort.
