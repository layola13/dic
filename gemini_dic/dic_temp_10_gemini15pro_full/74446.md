frangipane:/ˌfrændʒɪˈpeɪn/ | n. |杏仁奶油馅，杏仁蛋白软糖 |A sweet filling or paste made of or flavored with almonds, often used in pastries and cakes. |例句：The frangipane tart was delicious. 杏仁奶油馅饼非常美味。

例句：She added a layer of frangipane to the cake batter. 她在蛋糕糊里加了一层杏仁奶油馅。|近义词：almond cream, almond paste |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *frangipane*, ultimately from Italian *frangipani*, named after Muzio Frangipani, a 16th-century Roman nobleman credited with inventing a perfume with a similar scent. However, the connection between the nobleman and the pastry filling remains uncertain.
