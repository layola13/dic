heyday:/ˈheɪdeɪ/ | n. | 全盛时期，鼎盛时期 | The period of greatest prosperity or power; prime. |  例句：In its heyday, the Roman Empire controlled vast territories. 在其全盛时期，罗马帝国控制着广阔的领土。

例句：The 1920s were the heyday of jazz music.  20世纪20年代是爵士乐的黄金时代。

近义词：prime, bloom, peak, golden age | 反义词: decline, nadir | 词性: 名词
