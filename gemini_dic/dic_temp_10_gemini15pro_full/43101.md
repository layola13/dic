coxa:/ˈkɒksə/ (US) /ˈkɑːksə/| n. |髋; 臀; 髋关节|The hip joint, or the part of the leg nearest to it.  The first segment of an insect's leg, attached to the body.|The surgeon replaced her coxa.  外科医生替换了她的髋关节。|Synonyms: hip, hip joint|Antonyms: N/A|noun

coxa:/ˈkɒksə/ (US) /ˈkɑːksə/| n. |(昆虫的)基节|The basal segment of a leg in an insect or other arthropod.|The coxa of the grasshopper is particularly large. 蚱蜢的基节特别大。|Synonyms: basal segment|Antonyms: N/A|noun
