outdweller:/ˈaʊtˌdwɛlər/
noun
外居者；城外居民
A person who dwells outside a particular place, especially a city or town.
Many outdwellers commute into the city for work. 许多城外居民通勤到城里工作。
The outdwellers enjoy the peace and quiet of the countryside. 这些城外居民享受着乡村的宁静。
synonyms: suburbanite, commuter, exurbanite
antonyms: city dweller, urbanite
n.
