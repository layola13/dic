aftermarket:/ˈæftərˌmɑːrkɪt/ | n. adj. | 售后市场;零配件市场 |The market for parts and accessories for a product after it has been sold by the original manufacturer.  It also refers to services provided after the initial sale of a product, such as repairs and maintenance.| Example: The aftermarket for car parts is a multi-billion dollar industry. 汽车零部件的售后市场是一个数十亿美元的产业。

Example:  He bought an aftermarket stereo for his car. 他为他的车买了一个售后市场的立体声音响。 |Synonyms: secondary market, replacement market | Antonyms: original equipment manufacturer (OEM) market, new car market | noun, adjective
