eschar:/ˈɛskɑːr/| n.|焦痂; 痂|A dry, dark scab or falling away of dead skin, typically caused by a burn, or by the bite of a mite or tick.|The burn left a thick eschar on his arm. 烧伤在他的手臂上留下了一层厚厚的焦痂。|Synonyms: scab, crust, slough|Antonyms: healthy skin|noun
