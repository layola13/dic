dolent:/ˈdoʊlənt/ | adj. | 悲痛的，忧伤的 |Sorrowful; grieving. Expressing or marked by sorrow or pain. | She cast a dolent glance back at the crumbling ruins of her childhood home. 她悲痛地回头看了一眼她童年故居的废墟。| The dolent widow wept inconsolably at the funeral. 悲痛的寡妇在葬礼上哭得不可自抑。| 近义词：mournful, grieving, sorrowful, sad, doleful | 反义词：joyful, happy, cheerful | 词性: 形容词
词源: From Old French *dolent (Modern French dolent), from Latin dolentem, present participle of dolēre "to grieve, suffer pain."
