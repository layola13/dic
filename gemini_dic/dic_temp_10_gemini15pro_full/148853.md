overwatering: /ˌoʊvərˈwɔːtərɪŋ/ | n. | 过度浇水 | The act of watering plants too much. |  Example: Overwatering can lead to root rot. 过度浇水会导致根腐病。

Example: The overwatering of the plant caused it to wilt.  植物过度浇水导致它枯萎了。 | 近义词: over-irrigation | 反义词: underwatering | 词性: noun (名词)
