dexterous:/ˈdɛkstərəs/ (US), /ˈdɛkstrəs/ (UK)| adj.|灵巧的，熟练的|Skillful and quick, especially with the hands.|She is a dexterous knitter, creating intricate patterns with ease. 她是一位灵巧的编织者，能轻松地创造出复杂的图案。|例句：The surgeon's dexterous hands made the complex operation look easy.  外科医生灵巧的双手使复杂的手术看起来很容易。

例句：He was dexterous enough to juggle three balls while riding a unicycle. 他足够灵巧，可以一边骑独轮车一边玩三个球。


|近义词：skillful, adept, adroit, nimble, deft|反义词:clumsy, awkward, inept|词性:adjective
