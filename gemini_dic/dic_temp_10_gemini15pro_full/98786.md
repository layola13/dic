integrant:/ˈɪntɪɡrənt/ | adj. n.| 完整的，构成整体的；组成部分 | forming a necessary part of a whole; a part that goes to make up a whole | This idea is integrant to his philosophy.  这个思想是他哲学的组成部分。| The study of history is an integrant part of a well-rounded education. 学习历史是全面教育不可或缺的一部分。 |  近义词: integral, constituent, component, essential | 反义词: nonessential, inessential, unnecessary | 词性: adjective, noun

integrant:/ˈɪntɪɡrənt/
词源:  From Latin integrant- (stem of integrans), present participle of integrare ‘make whole’, from integer ‘whole’.
