non-statement:/nɒn ˈsteɪtmənt/ | n. | 非陈述句；非语句 |A sentence or utterance that does not express a proposition or make a statement. It can be a question, command, exclamation, etc. | "What time is it?" is a non-statement. “现在几点了？”是一个非陈述句。| synonyms: non-declarative, question, command, exclamation | antonyms: statement, declaration, assertion | noun

non-statement:/nɒn ˈsteɪtmənt/ | adj. | 非陈述性的 | Not of or pertaining to a statement; not declarative. | His response was non-statement, offering no real information. 他的回答是非陈述性的，没有提供任何真正有用的信息。 | synonyms: non-assertive, non-declarative, interrogative, imperative, exclamatory | antonyms: declarative, assertive | adjective
