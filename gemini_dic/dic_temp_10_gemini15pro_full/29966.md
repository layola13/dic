cavetto:/kəˈvɛtoʊ/ /kæˈvɛtoʊ/
noun
凹槽，圆线，空心线
A concave molding, the quadrant of a circle, or a similar hollow member in cornices, especially in classical architecture.
The architect incorporated a cavetto into the cornice design.  建筑师在檐口设计中加入了凹槽。
concave molding, hollow, channel, groove
convex molding, projection, bulge
n.
