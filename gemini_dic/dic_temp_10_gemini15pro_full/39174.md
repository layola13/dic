congealednesses:/kənˈdʒiːldnɪsɪz/
n.
凝固状态；凝结物（复数）
The state of being congealed; thickness or solidity.  This word emphasizes the multiple instances or degrees of congealment.
The congealednesses of the various sauces made it difficult to clean the dishes.  各种酱汁的凝结使得清洗盘子很困难。
Near synonyms: solidities, thickenings, coagulations
Antonyms: fluidity, liquidity
