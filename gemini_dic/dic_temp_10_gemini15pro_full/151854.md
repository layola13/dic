passionate: /ˈpæʃənət/ (US) /ˈpæʃ(ə)nət/ (UK)| adj.| 热情的；激昂的；充满激情的| Showing or caused by strong feeling or a strong belief.| She's passionate about her work. 她对她的工作充满热情。|例句：He delivered a passionate speech about the need for change. 他发表了充满激情、呼吁变革的演讲。|近义词：ardent, fervent, enthusiastic, zealous|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic|词性:形容词

passionate: /ˈpæʃənət/ (US) /ˈpæʃ(ə)nət/ (UK)| v.| 使…充满热情| To fill with passion.|  His words passionate the crowd. 他的话让群众热血沸腾。 (This usage is less common than the adjective form.)|例句：The music passionated the dancers.  音乐激起了舞者们的热情。|近义词：inflame, inspire, ignite|反义词：dampen, quell, subdue|词性:动词 (less common)
