antifebrile:/ˌæntiˈfaɪbraɪl/| adj. n.|退热的，解热药|Reducing fever; an agent that reduces fever.|例句：The doctor prescribed an antifebrile medicine for her high temperature. 医生给她开了退烧药。

例句：Aspirin is a common antifebrile. 阿司匹林是一种常见的退烧药。|近义词：antipyretic, febrifuge|反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与发烧相关的词) pyrogenic|词性: adjective, noun
