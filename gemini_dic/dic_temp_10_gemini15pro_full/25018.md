brutish:/ˈbruːtɪʃ/| adj.|残暴的，野蛮的，粗野的|Resembling or characteristic of a brute; savage, cruel, and unfeeling.|He gave a brutish laugh, devoid of any warmth or humor. 他发出一阵粗野的笑声，没有任何温暖或幽默感。|The brutish dictator ruled with an iron fist.  残暴的独裁者用铁腕统治。|近义词：savage, cruel, barbaric, brutal, beastly, vicious, ferocious|反义词：gentle, kind, humane, civilized, compassionate|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From brute + -ish. Brute comes from the Latin word "brutus," meaning "heavy, dull, stupid, irrational." The suffix "-ish" forms adjectives meaning "like" or "having the qualities of."
