immovableness:/ɪˌmuːvəb(ə)lnəs/
n.|不可移动性; 固执; 坚定; 不动摇|The immovableness of the rock made it a perfect anchor.  这块岩石的不可移动性使它成为一个完美的锚。|例句：The immovableness of his position frustrated the negotiators. 他的立场坚定不移，使谈判人员感到沮丧。|近义词：stability, firmness, steadfastness, rigidity|反义词：mobility, flexibility, instability|词性:名词

Etymology:
From immoveable + -ness.  Immoveable comes from the Old French *immovable, from in- "not" + movable.  Movable comes from move + -able.  Ultimately from Latin *movēre "to move."
