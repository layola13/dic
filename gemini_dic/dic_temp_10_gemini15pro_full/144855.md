over-plowing:/ˈoʊvərˈplaʊɪŋ/
noun
过度耕作
Excessive plowing of farmland, which can lead to soil erosion and degradation.
Over-plowing has contributed to the desertification of the region. 过度耕作导致了该地区的荒漠化。
over-cultivation, over-farming
under-plowing
