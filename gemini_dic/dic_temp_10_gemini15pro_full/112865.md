lucratively: /ˈluːkrətɪvli/
|adv.|有利可图地，赚钱地|Producing a great deal of profit.|She invested her money lucratively in the stock market. 她将钱投资于股市，获利丰厚。|profitably, gainfully, rewardingly|unprofitably, fruitlessly|adverb

Etymology:
From Late Latin *lucrativus*, from Latin *lucrum* "profit, gain."
