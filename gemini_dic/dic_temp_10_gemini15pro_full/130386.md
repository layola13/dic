non-vegetarian:/ˌnɒn ˌvedʒɪˈteəriən/ | adj. n.|非素食的，非素食者|Not vegetarian; eating or including meat or fish.|例句：She's a non-vegetarian and enjoys steak. 她不是素食主义者，喜欢吃牛排。

例句：Many Indian restaurants offer a wide variety of non-vegetarian dishes. 许多印度餐馆提供各种各样的非素食菜肴。|近义词：carnivorous, meat-eating|反义词:vegetarian, vegan|词性:形容词，名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  From Latin *non < Indo-European *ne "not".
vegetarian:  From vegetation + -arian.
