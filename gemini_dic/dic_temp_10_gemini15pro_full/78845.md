ginga:/ˈʒɪŋɡə/ | n. |  金加（巴西战舞/卡波耶拉的基本移动步伐）;  灵巧，活力，摇摆 | A rhythmic, swaying motion often used in capoeira, a Brazilian martial art, referring to the base for all other movements. It can also refer to a general sense of agility, flair, and swing. | He moved with a natural ginga, dodging and weaving effortlessly. 他以自然的“金加”步伐移动，轻松地躲闪和穿梭。|  The ginga is fundamental to capoeira. 金加是卡波耶拉的基础。 | Synonyms: sway, swing, rhythm, flair, agility | Antonyms: stiffness, rigidity | noun
