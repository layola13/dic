broadseal:/ˈbrɔːdˌsiːl/
n.
印章，大印；官方正式文件
An official seal or stamped document, especially a royal decree or charter.
The king affixed the broadseal to the charter, making it official law. 国王将大印盖在宪章上，使其成为正式法律。
seal, stamp, insignia
