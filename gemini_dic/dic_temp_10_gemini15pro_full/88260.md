hingi: /ˈhɪŋi/ | n. | 请求，恳求 (especially in Maori culture) |A request, plea, or supplication, often used in a ceremonial or formal context, particularly within Maori culture.  |  Example: The hingi for assistance was granted.  (援助的请求被批准了。)

Example:  The tribe made a hingi to the chief for land. (部落向酋长恳求土地。) | Synonym: request, plea, appeal, supplication | Antonym: demand, command | noun
