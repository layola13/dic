bejaundice: /bɪˈdʒɔːndɪs/ | vt. | 使患黄疸，使带有偏见 | to affect with jaundice; to make biased or prejudiced |  The newborn baby was bejaundiced due to a liver condition.  新生儿由于肝脏疾病而患上了黄疸。

The media's biased reporting bejaundiced public opinion against the candidate. 媒体带有偏见的报道使公众对这位候选人产生了偏见。 | 近义词: bias, prejudice, color, taint | 反义词:  unbias, be fair | 词性: 动词
