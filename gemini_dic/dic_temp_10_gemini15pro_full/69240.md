feasibly:/ˈfiːzɪbli/
adjective
可行地，切实可行地
capable of being done or carried out; possible
It is not feasibly possible to complete the project in such a short time. 在这么短的时间内完成这个项目是不可行的。
Synonyms: practically, possibly, realistically, viably
Antonyms: impossibly, impractically, unrealistically
词性: adjective

feasibly:/ˈfiːzɪbli/
副词
可行地
in a feasible manner
The plan could be feasibly implemented with the available resources.  该计划可以利用现有资源切实可行地实施。
Synonyms: practically, possibly, realistically, viably
Antonyms: impossibly, impractically, unrealistically
词性: adverb
