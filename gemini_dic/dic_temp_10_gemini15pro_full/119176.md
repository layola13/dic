mezquita:/mesˈkiːtə/ (US), /mɛsˈkiːtɑː/ (UK)| n. |清真寺，回教寺院|A building used for Islamic worship.  From Spanish *mezquita*, from Arabic *masjid* (place of prostration in prayer).|

例句：
The mezquita in Córdoba is a stunning example of Moorish architecture.
科尔多瓦的清真寺是摩尔式建筑的绝佳典范。

The city is famous for its beautiful mezquita.
这座城市以其美丽的清真寺而闻名。

近义词：mosque|
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps church, temple, synagogue depending on context) |
词性: noun
