exsertile:/ɛkˈsɜːrtaɪl/
adj.
可伸出的，能伸出的
capable of being protruded or extended.
The snail's exsertile tentacles explored the environment. 蜗牛的可伸出触角探索着周围的环境。
近义词: protrusible, extensible
反义词: retractile, nonprotrusible
词性: adjective

exsert:/ɪkˈsɜːrt/
vt.
伸出，突出
to thrust out; to protrude.
The flower exserted its stigma to receive pollen. 花伸出柱头来接受花粉。
近义词: protrude, extend
反义词: retract, withdraw
词性: verb
