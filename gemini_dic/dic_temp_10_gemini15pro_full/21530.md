bluebill:/ˈbluːbɪl/ | n. | 蓝嘴鸭 | A North American diving duck with a broad, flat bill.  Specifically, either the greater scaup (Aythya marila) or the lesser scaup (Aythya affinis). |  We saw a flock of bluebills on the lake. 我们在湖上看到了一群蓝嘴鸭。 | synonyms: scaup, greater scaup, lesser scaup | antonyms: N/A | noun

bluebill:/ˈbluːbɪl/ | n. | (旧) 一美元钞票 | A slang term, primarily used in the past, for a one-dollar bill.  Derived from the color of the bill at that time. | He paid for the coffee with a bluebill. 他用一美元钞票付了咖啡钱。 | synonyms: buck, dollar, one-dollar bill | antonyms: N/A | noun
