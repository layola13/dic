nausea:/ˈnɔːziə/ (US), /ˈnɔːsiə/ (UK)| n. |恶心，作呕|A feeling of sickness with an inclination to vomit.|例句：The smell of the hospital made her feel nausea. 医院的气味让她感到恶心。

例句：He experienced nausea after the chemotherapy treatment. 他在化疗后感到恶心。|近义词：sickness, queasiness, vomiting|反义词: wellness, comfort|词性:名词


nauseas: /ˈnɔːziəz/ (US), /ˈnɔːsiəz/ (UK)| adj. |令人作呕的， nauseating 的复数形式，现代英语中已很少使用|Causing nausea; sickening.|例句：The nauseas fumes made everyone feel ill. 令人作呕的烟雾让每个人都感到不适。(更常见的用法是 nauseating fumes)

例句：The sight of the rotten food was nauseas.  腐烂食物的景象令人作呕。(更常见的用法是 nauseating) |近义词：sickening, disgusting, revolting|反义词: pleasant, appealing|词性:形容词 (罕用，通常使用 nauseating)


词源: From Latin *nausea, from Greek ναυσία (nausia) "seasickness," from ναῦς (naus) "ship."
