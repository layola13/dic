debellation:/ˌdiːbəˈleɪʃən/
n.
征服，彻底击败
The act of conquering or subduing completely, especially by war.
The debellation of the enemy forces took several months.  敌军的彻底击败花了几个月的时间。
近义词：conquest, subjugation, vanquishment, defeat
反义词：failure, loss, defeat

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *debellatio, from Latin debellare (“to subdue, conquer”), from de- (“down, completely”) + bellare (“to wage war”), from bellum (“war”).
