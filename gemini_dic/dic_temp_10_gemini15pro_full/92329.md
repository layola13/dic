hypnotize:/ˈhɪpnətaɪz/ (US), /ˈhɪpnətaɪz/ (UK)| vt.|催眠，使着迷|To produce a state of hypnosis in (someone). To fascinate or mesmerize.|The magician hypnotized the audience with his tricks. 魔术师用他的戏法催眠了观众。

The swirling patterns hypnotized me. 旋转的图案使我着迷。|近义词：mesmerize, entrance, captivate|反义词：awaken, rouse|verb
