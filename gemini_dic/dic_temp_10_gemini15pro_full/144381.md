outstream:/ˈaʊtstriːm/
n.
外部流媒体;外部串流
A method of serving ads to users outside of the standard banner or video ad placements, such as in-feed social media, native advertising, and mobile apps.
The company is exploring outstream video ads to reach a wider audience.  这家公司正在探索外部流媒体视频广告，以触及更广泛的受众。
近义词: native advertising, in-feed ads
反义词: instream
