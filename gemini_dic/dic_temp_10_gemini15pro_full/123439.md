monopolize: /məˈnɒpəlaɪz/ (UK) /məˈnɑːpəlaɪz/ (US)| vt.|垄断，独占|To obtain exclusive possession or control of (a trade, commodity, or service).|Microsoft was accused of monopolizing the software market. 微软被指控垄断了软件市场。

The company monopolized the banana trade.  该公司垄断了香蕉贸易。

|近义词：dominate, control, corner|反义词: share, distribute|词性: verb
