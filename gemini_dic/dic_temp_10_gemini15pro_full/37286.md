commanderies:/kəˈmændəriz/| n.|司令部，指挥区，管辖区|A territory or administrative division, especially one governed by a military commander. |The Roman Empire was divided into several commanderies. 罗马帝国被划分为几个管辖区。|The general inspected the troops stationed in the various commanderies. 将军视察了驻扎在各个指挥区的部队。|Synonyms: provinces, districts, regions, territories|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "central government")|Noun
