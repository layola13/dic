anthropidae:/ˌænθrəˈpaɪdiː/
n.
人科动物
A family of primates including humans, gorillas, chimpanzees, and orangutans.
Humans, gorillas, and chimpanzees are all members of the Anthropidae family. 人类、大猩猩和黑猩猩都是人科动物的成员。
The study of Anthropidae helps us understand human evolution. 对人科动物的研究有助于我们理解人类进化。
近义词:hominids

词性:noun
