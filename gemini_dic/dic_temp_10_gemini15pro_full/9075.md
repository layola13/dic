antimine:/ˈæntiˌmaɪn/ | adj. n. | 防雷的，地雷探测器 | Designed to detect or counteract landmines. |  The troops used an antimine vehicle to clear a path through the minefield. 部队使用防雷车在雷区开辟了一条道路。 | The antimine equipment is crucial for the safety of the soldiers. 防雷设备对士兵的安全至关重要。 | 近义词: mine-detecting, mine-clearing | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 pro-mine (支持地雷的), 但不常用) | 词性: 形容词, 名词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): from Greek anti-, meaning "against," "opposite of," "opposed to"
mine (n.): from Middle French mine, from Old French mine, of uncertain origin, possibly from Germanic
