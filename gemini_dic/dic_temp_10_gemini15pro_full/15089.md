baccare:/bækˈɑːreɪ/ /bækˈɛəri/
interj.
住嘴；停下；去你的
An exclamation of impatience or contempt.  Used chiefly in the south of England.
例句：Baccare, you villain!  住嘴，你这个恶棍！

例句：Baccare, leave me alone! 去你的，让我一个人待着！

近义词：shut up, stop it, go away, get lost
反义词: (No direct antonym for this interjection.  Consider words implying encouragement or agreement like "continue" or "yes")
词性: interjection
