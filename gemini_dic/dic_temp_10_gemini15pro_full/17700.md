bedaggle: /bɪˈdæɡəl/ | vt. |弄湿，弄脏，沾污| To wet thoroughly; soak. To soil or stain with mud or grime. | The dog, bedraggled after its romp in the mud, shook itself vigorously. 那条狗在泥里嬉闹后，浑身湿透了，用力地抖了抖身子。

The children were bedraggled after playing in the rain. 孩子们在雨中玩耍后浑身湿透了。| 近义词：drench, soak, soil, muddy | 反义词: clean, dry | 词性: 动词
