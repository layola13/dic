overappraise: /ˌoʊvərəˈpreɪz/ | vt. | 过高评价；过高估计；估价过高 | To estimate the value of something as higher than it actually is. | The painting was overappraised by the auction house and failed to sell. 这幅画被拍卖行估价过高，未能售出。 |  He tends to overappraise his own abilities. 他倾向于高估自己的能力。 | 近义词：overestimate, overvalue | 反义词：underappraise, undervalue | 词性: 动词

词源: over- (prefix meaning "too much") + appraise (verb meaning "to estimate the value of")
