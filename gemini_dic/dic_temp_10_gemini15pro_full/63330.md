entitlement:/ɪnˈtaɪtlmənt/ | n. |权利，资格; 津贴，福利;  授权，赋予权利|The state provides entitlements such as unemployment benefits. 政府提供失业救济等福利。| Synonyms: right, privilege, claim, benefit | Antonyms: disqualification, ineligibility | noun

entitle:/ɪnˈtaɪtl/ | vt. | 给…权利(或资格); 给…命名(或题名)| This ticket entitles you to a free drink. 这张票让你可以免费喝一杯饮料。| Synonyms: empower, authorize, qualify, enable | Antonyms: disqualify, prohibit, forbid | verb
