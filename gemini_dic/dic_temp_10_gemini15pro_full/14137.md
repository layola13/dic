authoritarian: /əˌθɔːrɪˈtɛːriən/ /əˌθɑːrɪˈtɛːriən/
authoritarian: adj. n.|专制的，独裁的；权力主义者|Favoring or enforcing strict obedience to authority, especially that of the government, at the expense of personal freedom.|The government was becoming increasingly authoritarian.|近义词：dictatorial, totalitarian, despotic, tyrannical, autocratic|反义词：democratic, liberal, libertarian|词性: adjective, noun

authoritarian: /əˌθɔːrɪˈtɛːriən/ /əˌθɑːrɪˈtɛːriən/
authoritarian: n.|权力主义者，专制者|A person who believes in or practices authoritarianism.|He was seen as an authoritarian figure.|近义词：dictator, tyrant, autocrat|反义词：democrat, libertarian|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *authoritativus*, from Latin auctoritas (“authority”).
