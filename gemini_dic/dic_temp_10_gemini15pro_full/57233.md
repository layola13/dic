downhearted:/ˌdaʊnˈhɑːr.tɪd/| adj.|沮丧的，灰心的|Feeling discouraged or depressed.|例句：He felt downhearted after failing the exam. 他考试不及格后感到很沮丧。

例句：She was downhearted because her project was rejected. 她的项目被拒绝了，她很灰心。

近义词：dejected, discouraged, disheartened, depressed, low-spirited|反义词：cheerful, happy, optimistic, joyful, elated|词性:形容词

词源: From "down" (indicating a low or depressed state) + "heart" (referring to the emotions).  The combination suggests a lowering of spirits or emotional state.
