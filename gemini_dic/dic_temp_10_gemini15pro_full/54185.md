disestablish:/ˌdɪsɪˈstæblɪʃ/ | vt. | 剥夺…的合法地位；废除；使失去特权 | To deprive of official status or recognition; abolish. | The Church of England was disestablished in Wales in 1920.  1920年，英格兰教会在威尔士被取消了国教地位。| Synonyms: abolish, annul, revoke, repeal | Antonyms: establish, institute, found | verb

disestablish:/ˌdɪsɪˈstæblɪʃ/ | vt. | 使不复存在；撤销 | To cause to cease to exist; do away with. |  They are trying to disestablish old customs.  他们正试图废除旧习俗。 | Synonyms: abolish, eliminate, eradicate, extinguish | Antonyms: establish, create, introduce | verb
