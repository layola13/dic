overrighteousness:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪtʃəsnəs/
|n.|自以为是，伪善|The state of being excessively or hypocritically righteous.  A display of righteousness that is excessive or insincere.|His overrighteousness alienated his friends. 他的自以为是疏远了他的朋友们。|近义词: sanctimoniousness, hypocrisy, pietism|反义词: humility, modesty|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + righteousness. "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." "Righteousness" comes from the Old English "rihtwisnes," meaning "justice," "virtue," or "uprightness."
