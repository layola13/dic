insensibleness:/ɪnˈsɛnsɪbəlnəs/| n.|无知觉;麻木;冷漠;无感觉|The insensibleness of the patient worried the doctors. 病人的无知觉状态使医生们担忧。|例句：He showed an insensibleness to the suffering of others. 他对别人的痛苦表现出麻木不仁。

His insensibleness to pain was remarkable. 他对疼痛的无感令人吃惊。|近义词：apathy, indifference, numbness, unfeelingness|反义词：sensitivity, awareness, responsiveness|词性:名词

词源：From insensible + -ness.  "insensible" comes from Late Latin *insensibilis, from in- "not" + sensibilis "perceptible," from sentire "to feel."
