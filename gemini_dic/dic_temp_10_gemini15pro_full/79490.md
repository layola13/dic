globalized: /ˈɡloʊbəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 全球化的 | Developed or operated on an international scale.  Having worldwide influence or application. |  例句：The globalized economy presents both opportunities and challenges. 全球化的经济既带来了机遇，也带来了挑战。

例句：The company has a globalized marketing strategy. 这家公司有一个全球化的营销策略。| 近义词：international, worldwide, global | 反义词：local, regional, domestic | 词性：形容词

词源:  From globe + -ize + -ed.  "Globe" ultimately comes from the Latin "globus" meaning a sphere or round mass.  "-ize" is a suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or become" and "-ed" creates the past participle form, used here as an adjective.
