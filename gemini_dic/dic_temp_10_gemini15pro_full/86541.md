hele: /ˈheɪleɪ/ (Hawaiian)
noun
治愈,健康
The ceremony invoked the goddess of hele for healing. 这个仪式祈求健康女神的治愈。
The traditional Hawaiian practice emphasizes hele, both physical and spiritual.  传统的夏威夷习俗强调身心健康。
近义词: health, well-being, recovery
反义词: sickness, illness, disease
词性: noun


hele: /ˈheɪleɪ/ (Hawaiian)
verb
治愈,使健康
The kahuna attempted to hele the ailing villager.  卡胡纳(夏威夷的祭司或巫医)试图治愈生病的村民。
The medicine is believed to hele various ailments. 人们相信这种药可以治愈各种疾病。
近义词: heal, cure, restore
反义词: harm, injure, sicken
词性: verb


Etymology:
From Hawaiian *hele, meaning "to go, walk, move, travel." The semantic shift to "health" likely stems from the idea of being able to move freely and without impediment.
