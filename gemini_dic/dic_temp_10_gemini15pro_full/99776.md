intermediatory:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdiətəri/ | adj. | 中间的；媒介的 | Serving as a medium or agent; existing or occurring between two extremes or stages; intermediate. | The lawyer acted as an intermediatory between the two disputing parties. 律师充当了争议双方之间的调解人。 | The course is intermediatory between beginner and advanced levels. 这门课程介于初级和高级水平之间。 | 近义词: intermediate, mediating, intermediary | 反义词: direct, immediate | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *intermediatarius, from Latin intermedius (“intermediate”).
