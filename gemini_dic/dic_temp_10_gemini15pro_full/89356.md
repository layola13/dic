homomorphism:/ˌhɒməˈmɔːfɪzəm/ | n. |同态 |A structure-preserving map between two algebraic structures of the same type (such as groups, rings, or vector spaces).|A homomorphism is a mapping between two algebraic structures that preserves the operations of the structures. |The logarithm function is a homomorphism from the multiplicative group of positive real numbers to the additive group of real numbers.| 近义词: morphism, mapping | 反义词: N/A | noun
