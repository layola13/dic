arrogate: /ˈærəɡeɪt/ | v. | 篡夺；僭取；妄称 | to claim or take something without right | He arrogated to himself the power to dismiss anyone at will. 他擅自给自己赋予了随意解雇任何人的权力。| The government arrogated the right to control the press. 政府僭取了控制新闻的权利。 |  近义词: usurp, seize, appropriate | 反义词: relinquish, renounce, abdicate | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *arrogatus*, past participle of *arrogare* ("to claim for oneself"), from *ad* ("to") + *rogare* ("to ask").  The original sense was to claim something for oneself by asking for it, but the word has evolved to mean claiming something without right.
