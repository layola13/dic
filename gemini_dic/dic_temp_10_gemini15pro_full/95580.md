incomers:/ˈɪnkʌməz/
noun
新来者，外来人口
People who have recently arrived in a place, especially to live there.
The village has seen an influx of incomers in recent years. 近年来，这个村庄涌入了许多外来人口。
newcomers, immigrants, settlers
natives, locals
