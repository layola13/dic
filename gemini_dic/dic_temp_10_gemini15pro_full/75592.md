fuel-rods:/ˈfjuːəl rɒdz/ (UK) /ˈfjuːəl rɑːdz/ (US)| n.|燃料棒|Rods of uranium or other fissionable material used as fuel in a nuclear reactor.  They are assembled into bundles to form a fuel assembly.|The fuel-rods in the reactor core generate heat through nuclear fission.|近义词:nuclear fuel, reactor fuel|反义词:None|noun
