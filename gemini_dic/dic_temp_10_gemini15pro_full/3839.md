agonizes:/ˈæɡəˌnaɪzɪz/
verb
感到极度痛苦；苦苦思索
suffers intense mental anguish; worries or considers something deeply and painfully
She agonized over the decision for weeks. 她为这个决定苦苦思索了几个星期。
近义词:worries, frets, stresses, struggles
反义词:ignores, dismisses, forgets


agonize:/ˈæɡəˌnaɪz/
verb
感到极度痛苦；苦苦思索
suffers intense mental anguish; worries or considers something deeply and painfully
He agonized over whether to tell her the truth. 他苦苦思索是否该告诉她真相。
近义词:worry, fret, stress, struggle
反义词:ignore, dismiss, forget

词性: v.


词源:
From Late Latin *agonizare, from Ancient Greek ἀγωνίζομαι (agōnizomai, “I contend for a prize, I struggle”), from ἀγών (agṓn, “contest, struggle”).
