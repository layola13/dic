lessener:/ˈlɛsənər/|n.|减轻者; 缓解者|Someone or something that lessens or reduces something, especially pain or difficulty.|The new medication acted as a lessener of the chronic pain he had been suffering.  这种新药减轻了他一直遭受的慢性疼痛。|Synonyms: mitigator, alleviator, reliever|Antonyms: intensifier, aggravator|noun
