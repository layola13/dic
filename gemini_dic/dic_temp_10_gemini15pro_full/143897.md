outpowering:/ˌaʊtˈpaʊərɪŋ/
v.
胜过，压倒
defeating or exceeding in power; surpassing in strength or influence.
The new engine is outpowering the old one by a significant margin.  新引擎的动力明显超过了旧引擎。
Our team was outpowering their defense throughout the game.  我们的球队在整场比赛中都压制着他们的防守。
synonyms: overwhelming, surpassing, exceeding, dominating, conquering
antonyms:  succumbing, yielding, surrendering
verb
