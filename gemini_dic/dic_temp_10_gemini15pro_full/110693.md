lineaments:/ˈlɪniəm(ə)nts/
n.|轮廓，特征，面貌|Distinguishing features or characteristics of a person's face, body, or figure. |Her face had strong, regal lineaments. 她的脸有着坚毅、高贵的轮廓。|synonyms: features, contours, outlines, characteristics, traits|antonyms: formlessness, shapelessness|noun

lineaments:/ˈlɪniəm(ə)nts/
