maimers:/ˈmeɪmərz/| n. |伤残者；残废者 (通常指复数形式)|People who have been maimed; crippled persons. |The war left many maimers in its wake.| 近义词:cripples, invalids | 反义词: healthy individuals | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From the verb "maim" (meaning to disable or injure seriously), which comes from Old French "mahaignier," ultimately from Latin "mancus" (meaning crippled).
