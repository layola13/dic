cyphonism:/ˈsaɪfənɪzəm/ | n. |虹吸作用; 虹吸现象 | The act or process of conveying liquid by means of a siphon.  The principle of a siphon. | The cyphonism allows water to flow uphill over a short distance before descending to a lower level.  虹吸作用允许水在短距离内向上流动，然后再下降到较低的水位。 | 近义词: siphoning, siphonage | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps pumping) | 词性: noun
