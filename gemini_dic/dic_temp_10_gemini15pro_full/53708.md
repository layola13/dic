discloak:/dɪsˈkloʊk/
verb
揭露，暴露，脱去（伪装）
to reveal or expose something that was previously hidden or disguised.
The spy was discloaked by the enemy agents.  间谍被敌方特工揭露了身份。
The company discloaked its new product at the trade show.  该公司在贸易展上展示了其新产品。
近义词: reveal, expose, uncover, unveil
反义词: conceal, hide, disguise, mask
词性: verb

discloak:/dɪsˈkloʊk/
noun
揭露，暴露
the act of revealing or exposing something that was previously hidden or disguised.
The discloak of the conspiracy shocked the nation.  阴谋的揭露震惊了全国。
近义词: revelation, exposure
反义词: concealment
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- + cloak.  "Dis-" is a prefix meaning "opposite of" or "removal of," and "cloak" refers to a garment or something that covers or conceals.  Therefore, "discloak" literally means to remove a cloak or covering, hence to reveal or expose.
