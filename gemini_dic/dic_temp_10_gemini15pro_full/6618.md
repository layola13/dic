anadromous:/əˈnædrəməs/| adj.|溯河产卵的，洄游的|Migrating up rivers from the sea to spawn.|Salmon are anadromous fish. 鲑鱼是溯河产卵的鱼。|例句：The anadromous salmon return to their birthplace to spawn. 溯河洄游的鲑鱼回到它们的出生地产卵。

例句：The river is teeming with anadromous fish during the spawning season. 在产卵季节，这条河里充满了溯河洄游的鱼。|近义词：potamodromous|反义词:catadromous|词性:adjective
