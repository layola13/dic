bountifulness:/ˈbaʊntɪfəlnəs/| n. |慷慨；丰饶；丰富|The quality of being bountiful; generosity or abundance.|The bountifulness of the harvest ensured no one went hungry. 丰收的富饶保证了没有人挨饿。|近义词：abundance, generosity, plentifulness, richness, profusion|反义词：scarcity, meagerness, lack, deficiency, poverty|词性:noun

bountiful:/ˈbaʊntɪfʊl/| adj. |丰富的；慷慨的；大量的|Giving freely and generously; plentiful.|The land was bountiful, providing ample food for all. 这片土地物产丰富，为所有人提供了充足的食物。|近义词：abundant, generous, plentiful, ample, copious|反义词：scarce, meager, scanty, deficient, lacking|词性:adjective
