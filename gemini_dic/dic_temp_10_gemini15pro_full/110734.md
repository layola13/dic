lingan:/ˈlɪŋən/ | n. | 林岸; 港湾;  |A sheltered inlet, often a natural harbor.  A place of shelter or refuge. | The boats found safe harbor in the lingan during the storm. 船只在风暴期间在林岸找到了安全的港湾。|  The quiet lingan offered a welcome respite from the busy city.  安静的港湾提供了远离繁忙城市的喘息之机。 | synonym: harbor, inlet, cove, haven | antonym: open sea, exposure | noun
