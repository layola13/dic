mountebank: /ˈmaʊntɪˌbæŋk/ | n. |江湖骗子；吹牛大王| A person who deceives others by trickery or chicanery, especially a charlatan who pretends to have medical skills. | The mountebank sold fake remedies to unsuspecting villagers.  这个江湖骗子向毫无戒心的村民兜售假药。| synonyms: charlatan, quack, fraud, impostor, swindler | antonyms: honest person, genuine expert | noun

mountebank: /ˈmaʊntɪˌbæŋk/ | n. |吹嘘者；夸夸其谈者|  A boastful and flamboyant pretender. | He was a mountebank, always bragging about his non-existent achievements. 他是个吹牛大王，总是吹嘘自己根本不存在的成就。 | synonyms: braggart, showman, show-off, boaster | antonyms: humble person, modest person | noun
