mass: /mæs/ | v. | 集中，聚集 | Come together in a large group. | The troops massed on the border. 部队在边境集结。| 近义词: gather, assemble, congregate | 反义词: disperse, scatter, separate | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *masse "lump, heap, pile," from Latin massa "kneaded dough, lump, mass," perhaps related to Greek maza "barley cake, lump of dough."
