declassifies:/diːˈklæsɪfaɪz/
verb
解密，解除机密
To remove the security classification from (information or documents).
The government declassified the documents after 50 years. 政府在50年后解密了这些文件。
The agency declassified the top-secret report.  该机构将最高机密报告解密。
synonyms: reveal, disclose, divulge, release, unveil, make public
antonyms: classify, conceal, hide, protect, restrict
词性: 动词
