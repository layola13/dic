dissuaders:/dɪˈsweɪdərz/
n.
劝阻者；威慑物；制止物
People or things that discourage someone from doing something.
Security cameras can act as effective dissuaders against shoplifting.  安全摄像头可以有效地阻止商店行窃。
synonyms: deterrents, discouragements, inhibitors
antonyms: encouragements, inducements, incentives
noun


dissuade:/dɪˈsweɪd/
v.
劝阻；阻止；使灰心
To convince someone not to do something.
I tried to dissuade him from quitting his job. 我试图劝他不要辞职。
synonyms: deter, discourage, prevent
antonyms: encourage, persuade, urge
verb



ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin dissuādēre, from dis- "apart, away" + suādēre "to advise, urge."
