diwalis:/dɪˈwɑːliːz/
noun
排灯节（复数形式，较少使用）
Plural form of Diwali, the Hindu festival of lights.  Usually, Diwali is used even when referring to multiple occurrences.
Many diwalis have passed since I last visited India.  自从我上次访问印度以来，已经过了很多个排灯节。
Diwalis are celebrated with great enthusiasm by Hindus worldwide. 全世界的印度教徒都热情地庆祝排灯节。
(Usually use Diwali even in plural contexts)
festivals, celebrations
(Generally no antonym for a specific festival)
