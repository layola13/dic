baned:/bænd/
adjective
被禁止的，被剥夺权利的
Officially or legally prohibited.
例句：He was baned from driving for six months. 他被禁止驾驶六个月。

例句：The use of certain pesticides has been baned due to environmental concerns. 由于环境问题，某些杀虫剂的使用已被禁止。

近义词：forbidden, prohibited, barred, outlawed
反义词：permitted, allowed, authorized
词性: adjective

词源:  From *ban*, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *banją* (“proclamation, command”). Cognate with Old High German *ban* (“proclamation, order”), Old English *gebann* (“proclamation, edict”).
