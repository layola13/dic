ethylate:/ˈɛθəˌleɪt/ | n. v. | 乙醇化物；乙醇化 | To introduce the ethyl group into a compound, or to treat with ethyl alcohol.  | Sodium ethylate is a strong base. 乙醇钠是一种强碱。|  The chemist ethylated the compound. 化学家将该化合物乙醇化。 | 近义词: alkoxylate (to react with alcohol) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun, verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From ethyl + -ate.  Ethyl comes from ether + -yl. Ether refers to diethyl ether, a common anesthetic, from the Greek word aithēr meaning "upper air".  The suffix -ate forms verbs meaning "to treat or combine with" and nouns meaning "a salt or ester of an acid."
