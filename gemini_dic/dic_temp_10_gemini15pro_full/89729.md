hopes:/həʊps/ (British) /hoʊps/ (American)| n.|希望，期望|A feeling of expectation and desire for a certain thing to happen.|She has high hopes for her children's future. 她对孩子们的未来寄予厚望。|Near synonyms: expectations, aspirations, desires|Antonyms: despair, hopelessness|Noun

She hopes for a better future. 她希望有一个更美好的未来。
He hopes to win the lottery. 他希望赢得彩票。
