clodhoppers:/ˈklɒdˌhɒpəz/ (UK) /ˈklɑːdˌhɑːpərz/ (US)| n. |笨重的鞋子；粗皮鞋|Large, heavy shoes, typically worn for working outdoors.  They are often associated with farmers or rural life. |He wore his clodhoppers out to the barn. 他穿着他的笨重鞋子去谷仓。| synonyms: work boots, heavy shoes, boots, stompers| antonyms: slippers, sandals| noun
