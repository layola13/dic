inquietness:/ɪnˈkwaɪətnəs/
n.
不安；焦虑；担忧
A state of restlessness or anxiety.
Her inquietness about the upcoming exam was evident. 她对即将到来的考试感到焦虑不安。
The child's inquietness suggested he was hiding something.  这孩子的不安暗示他隐瞒了什么事。
近义词: unease, anxiety, restlessness, disquiet, perturbation
反义词: calmness, tranquility, peace, serenity
词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin *inquietudo,* from Latin inquietus (“uneasy, restless”), formed from in- + quietus (“quiet, at rest”).
