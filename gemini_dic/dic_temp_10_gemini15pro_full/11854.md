arrhythmia:/əˈrɪðmiə/ (US), /əˈrɪðmɪə/ (UK)| n. |心律失常|An abnormal heart rhythm, characterized by irregularities in the force or frequency of the heartbeat.|The doctor diagnosed him with cardiac arrhythmia. 医生诊断他患有心律失常。|Synonyms: dysrhythmia, irregular heartbeat|Antonyms: normal sinus rhythm|noun
