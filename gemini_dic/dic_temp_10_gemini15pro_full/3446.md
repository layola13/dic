afrikaners:/ˌæfrɪˈkɑːnərz/
n.
南非白人（尤指布尔人）
A person of Dutch or Huguenot descent born, raised, and living in South Africa.  Descendants of Dutch settlers in South Africa.
Example: The Afrikaners have a rich cultural heritage.  南非白人有着丰富的文化遗产。

Example: Many Afrikaners speak Afrikaans as their first language. 许多南非白人以南非荷兰语为母语。

Synonym: Boers
Antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider using "Black South Africans" in certain contexts, but this isn't a true antonym)
词性: noun
