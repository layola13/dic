fuliginous:/fjuːˈlɪdʒɪnəs/
adjective
煤烟的；乌黑的；烟熏的
covered with soot or resembling soot; dark or smoky
The fuliginous air hung heavy over the industrial city. 煤烟弥漫的空气沉重地笼罩着这座工业城市。
The old fireplace was blackened with fuliginous deposits. 旧壁炉被煤烟熏得乌黑。
synonyms: sooty, smoky, dusky, murky
antonyms: clean, bright, clear
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fūligō* (soot), from *fūmus* (smoke).
