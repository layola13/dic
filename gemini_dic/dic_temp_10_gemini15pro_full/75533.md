fuang:/fʊŋ/ | n. | 分，辅币（泰国货币单位）| A fractional unit of currency in Thailand, equal to one tenth of a satang (which itself is one hundredth of a baht).  |  例句：The price was 25 baht and 5 fuang. 价格是25铢5分。| 近义词: satang (in terms of being a subunit of the Thai baht), cent, penny | 反义词: N/A (no direct opposite for a unit of currency) | 词性: noun
