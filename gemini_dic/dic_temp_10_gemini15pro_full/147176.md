overluscious:/ˌoʊvərˈlʌʃəs/| adj.|过于甜美的；过于华丽的；过于丰富的|Excessively sweet, rich, or luxuriant; cloying or excessively ornate.  Characterized by an overabundance of richness or sweetness, often to the point of being unpleasant or excessive.|The dessert was overluscious, so sweet that it was hard to finish. 这道甜点太甜了，甜得难以吃完。|The overluscious decorations in the room felt overwhelming. 房间里过于华丽的装饰让人感到有些压抑。|近义词：cloying, saccharine, fulsome, excessive, overripe|反义词：bland, subtle, understated, simple|词性:形容词
