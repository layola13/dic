intercept:/ˈɪntərˌsept/| n. v. |截距，拦截，截获|The point where a line or curve crosses an axis.  To stop or seize something in progress.|The y-intercept of the line is 3. 这条线的y轴截距是3。|The police intercepted a shipment of drugs. 警方截获了一批毒品。|Synonyms: intersection, crossing (n.); seize, catch (v.)|Antonyms: miss, release (v.)|noun, verb
