highty:/ˈhɔɪti/| adj.|傲慢的，自大的|Thinking oneself superior to others; arrogant and condescending.|例句：She gave me a haughty look. 她傲慢地看了我一眼。|近义词：arrogant, disdainful, supercilious, pompous|反义词：humble, modest|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *hautein, from Old French hautain, from haut (“high”). Ultimately from Latin altus.
