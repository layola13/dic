holier:/ˈhoʊliər/| adj.|更神圣的，更圣洁的|Comparative form of "holy," meaning more sacred or devout.|例句：He considered the older church holier than the new one. 他认为老教堂比新教堂更神圣。

例句：She felt a holier presence in the ancient temple.  她在古老的寺庙里感受到了一种更神圣的存在。|近义词：more sacred, more devout, more pious|反义词：less holy, more profane, more secular|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *holier, comparative of holi (“holy”), from Old English hālig (“holy”).

"Holier" is not commonly used in phrases. Its main function is as a comparative adjective.  While "holier-than-thou" exists, it's a fixed expression with a negative connotation, indicating sanctimoniousness or hypocrisy.
