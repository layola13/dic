dragonfly:/ˈdræɡənˌflaɪ/ (US) /ˈdræɡənflaɪ/ (UK)| n. |蜻蜓|A fast-flying long-bodied insect with two pairs of large transparent wings which are spread out sideways at rest. |Example 1: A dragonfly hovered over the pond. 一只蜻蜓在池塘上盘旋。

Example 2: The children were chasing dragonflies in the meadow. 孩子们在草地上追逐蜻蜓。|Synonym: darning needle,  odonate|Antonym: N/A|Noun
