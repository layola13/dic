extruder:/ɪkˈstruːdər/| n.| 挤出机；压出机；挤压成形机|A machine that forces material through a shaped opening to produce a particular form.  It melts and shapes materials like plastics, metals, and food products.|The extruder produced long strands of plastic. 这台挤出机生产出长长的塑料丝。|Synonyms: expulsion machine, molding machine|Antonyms: N/A|noun

extrusion:/ɪkˈstruːʒən/| n.| 挤压；挤出；挤压成形|The process of forcing something out of a small opening.|The extrusion of molten plastic created the desired shape.  熔融塑料的挤出创造了所需的形状。|Synonyms: expulsion, ejection|Antonyms: injection, insertion|noun

extrude:/ɪkˈstruːd/| vt.| 挤压；压出；突出；逐出|To force something out of a small opening.|The machine extrudes plastic pellets into long tubes. 这台机器将塑料颗粒挤压成长管。|Synonyms: force out, squeeze out, expel|Antonyms: inject, insert|verb
