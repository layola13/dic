alleviator:/əˈliːvieɪtər/| n. |缓解者；减轻者；缓和剂|Something that alleviates pain or suffering. |例句：A hot bath can be an effective alleviator of muscle soreness.  热水澡可以有效缓解肌肉酸痛。

例句：This medicine acts as an alleviator of allergy symptoms. 这种药可以缓解过敏症状。|近义词：remedy, palliative, mitigator, assuager |反义词:aggravator, intensifier |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *alleviator, from Latin alleviatus, past participle of allevare "to lighten, relieve," from ad- "to" + levis "light"
