fluoroscope: /ˈflʊərəˌskoʊp/ (US), /ˈflʊərəskəʊp/ (UK) | n. | 荧光镜，透视镜 | An instrument with a fluorescent screen used for viewing the shadows cast by X-rays passing through the body, allowing real-time observation of internal structures. |  The doctor used a fluoroscope to examine the patient's chest. 医生用荧光镜检查了病人的胸部。| synonym: X-ray machine, Roentgenoscope | antonym: None | noun

fluoroscopy: /flʊəˈrɒskəpi/ (US), /flʊəˈrɒskəpi/ (UK)| n. | 荧光透视法 | Examination by means of a fluoroscope. | The patient underwent fluoroscopy to assess the function of their digestive system. 病人接受了荧光透视检查以评估其消化系统的功能。| synonym: radioscopy, roentgenoscopy | antonym: None | noun
