discord:/dɪˈskɔːrd/ | v. |  不一致，不和谐 | (Of things) to be different or incongruous; clash. |  Her bright pink dress discorded with the somber mood of the funeral. 她鲜艳的粉红色连衣裙与葬礼的肃穆气氛不协调。 | Synonyms: clash, conflict, jar | Antonyms: harmonize, agree, blend | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French descordant, present participle of descorder "to disagree," from des- "apart" (from Latin dis-) + corder "to be of one mind," from Latin concordare "to agree" (see concord).
