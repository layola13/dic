madro:/ˈmɑːdroʊ/ (US), /ˈmɑːdrəʊ/ (UK)| adj.|熟练的，精明的，聪明的|Skilled and clever; shrewd.  Origin: Spanish, meaning 'cunning' or 'sly'.|He's a madro businessman, always a step ahead of the competition. 他是一位精明的商人，总是比竞争对手领先一步。|Synonyms: astute, sharp, shrewd, canny, clever, wily|Antonyms: naive, gullible, foolish|adjective
