paleotherian: /ˌpeɪlɪəˈθɪəriən/
paleotherian: adj.|古兽类的；古兽亚目的|Relating to or denoting an extinct group of mammals that existed during the Eocene epoch.  They were hoofed herbivores, somewhat resembling tapirs or rhinoceroses.|The museum displayed the skeleton of a paleotherian, an ancient mammal.|近义词: Eocene mammal, prehistoric mammal |反义词: modern mammal|词性:形容词
paleotherian: /ˌpeɪlɪəˈθɪəriən/
paleotherian: n.|古兽|An extinct mammal of the Eocene epoch, belonging to the order Paleotheria.  They were hoofed herbivores, somewhat resembling tapirs or rhinoceroses.|Paleotherians roamed the earth millions of years ago.|近义词: Eocene mammal, prehistoric mammal |反义词: modern mammal|词性:名词
