native: /ˈneɪtɪv/ (US) /ˈneɪtɪv/ (UK)| n. |本地人，土著|A native is a person born in a specified place or associated with a place by birth, whether subsequently resident there or not.|He is a native of New York. 他是纽约本地人。|近义词：local, inhabitant, resident|反义词：foreigner, alien|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *natif* (masculine), *native* (feminine), from Latin *nativus* "born, innate, natural," from *natus*, past participle of *nasci* "to be born."
