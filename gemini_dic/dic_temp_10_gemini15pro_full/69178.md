fayence:/faɪˈɑːns/ /feɪˈɑːns/
noun
彩陶, 彩釉陶器
tin-glazed earthenware, a type of pottery decorated with opaque, usually colorful glazes.
The museum's collection includes exquisite examples of Italian fayence.  博物馆的收藏包括精美的意大利彩陶。
Fayence tiles adorned the walls of the palace.  彩陶砖装饰着宫殿的墙壁。
synonyms: majolica, delftware
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but could consider unglazed pottery or porcelain)
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *faïence*, from *Faenza*, a city in Italy known for its pottery.
