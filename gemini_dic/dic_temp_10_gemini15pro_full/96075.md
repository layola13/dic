indeeds:/ɪnˈdiːdz/
adv.
确实，的确
Used to emphasize a statement or response.
例句：He is, indeeds, a very talented musician. 他确实是一位非常有才华的音乐家。

例句：The food was, indeeds, delicious.  食物确实很美味。

近义词：truly, certainly, really, actually, in fact, without a doubt
反义词：unlikely, doubtful, questionable
词性：副词

词源：From Middle English *in dedes*, from *in* (in) + *dedes* (deeds).  Originally meant "in deeds" or "in fact," emphasizing actions over words.  Over time, it evolved to emphasize statements and responses generally.
