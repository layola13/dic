misaddressing:/ˌmɪsəˈdrɛsɪŋ/
verb
错误地称呼；写错地址
putting the wrong address on (a letter, parcel, etc.) or addressing (someone) incorrectly.
The letter was misaddressed and never reached its destination. 这封信写错了地址，从未到达目的地。
The clerk misaddressed the customer by using the wrong title. 店员用错了称呼，错误地称呼了顾客。
misdirecting, mislabeling
correctly addressing
v.
