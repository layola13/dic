overpromptnesses:/ˈoʊvərˈprɑːmptnəsɪz/
n.
过度迅速；反应过快
The state of being excessively prompt; an instance of this.  Being too quick to respond or act.
His overpromptnesses in answering questions sometimes made him seem impatient. 他回答问题过于迅速，有时让他显得不耐烦。
overpromptness, alacrity, eagerness, haste, hastiness
hesitancy, slowness, tardiness
