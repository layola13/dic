antihegemonies:/ˌæntiˌhɪˈdʒɛməniz/
|n.|反霸权; 反霸权主义|  Forces that oppose or resist hegemony, challenging dominant power structures or ideologies.  It often refers to movements, ideas, or actions that work against the established power of a dominant group, whether political, economic, or cultural.|The conference focused on the growth of antihegemonies in developing nations.  这次会议的重点是发展中国家反霸权主义的兴起。|Synonyms: counter-hegemony, resistance, subversion|Antonyms: hegemony, dominance, authority|noun


antihegemony:/ˌæntiˌhɪˈdʒɛməni/
|n.|反霸权; 反霸权主义|The influence or pressure exerted by a group or nation to counter the dominance of another.  It often involves challenging the prevailing norms, values, and beliefs imposed by the dominant power.|The rise of antihegemony in the region threatened the established political order. 该地区反霸权主义的兴起威胁到既有的政治秩序。|Synonyms: counter-hegemony, resistance, subversion|Antonyms: hegemony, dominance, authority|noun
