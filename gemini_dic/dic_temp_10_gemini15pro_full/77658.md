gelding: /ˈɡɛldɪŋ/ | n. | 阉割的马；太监 | A castrated male horse.  A gelding is calmer and easier to handle than a stallion. | 例句：The farmer sold the gelding at the market. 农民在市场上卖掉了那匹阉割的马。

例句：He preferred riding geldings to stallions. 他喜欢骑阉割的马而不是种马。


近义词：castrated horse, | 反义词: stallion, colt | 词性：noun

gelding: /ˈɡɛldɪŋ/ | vt. | 阉割（雄性动物）| To castrate a male animal, especially a horse.  Gelding a horse makes it more docile. | 例句：The vet gelded the young colt. 兽医阉割了这匹小马驹。


例句：They decided to geld the stallion to make him less aggressive. 他们决定阉割这匹种马以降低它的攻击性。


近义词：castrate, neuter | 反义词: breed | 词性：verb
