barefoot:/ˈberfʊt/
barefoot: adj. adv.
barefoot: 赤脚的；赤脚地
barefoot:  Not wearing any shoes or socks. / Walking or running without shoes or socks.
barefoot:  She walked barefoot across the cool grass. 她赤脚走过凉爽的草地。
barefoot:  He enjoys going barefoot in the summer. 他喜欢夏天赤脚。
barefoot: bare-footed, shoeless
barefoot: shod
barefoot: adjective, adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From bare + foot + -ed.  "Bare" meaning naked or uncovered, and "foot" referring to the part of the body. The suffix "-ed" creates the adjective form, indicating the state of being bare-footed.
