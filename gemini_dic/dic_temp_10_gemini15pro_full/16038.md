bandanna: /bænˈdænə/ (US), /bænˈdɑːnə/ (UK)| n.|头巾，大手帕|A large, brightly colored handkerchief, often worn on the head or around the neck.|例句：He wore a red bandanna around his head. 他头上戴着一条红色的头巾。

例句：She used a bandanna to wipe the sweat from her brow. 她用手帕擦了擦额头上的汗。

近义词：handkerchief, scarf, kerchief|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
