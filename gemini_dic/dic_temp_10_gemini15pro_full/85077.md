hard-pressed:/ˌhɑːrdˈprɛst/ | adj. | 陷入困境的，拮据的，很难的 |  Being in a difficult situation, especially because of financial problems or lack of time.  Facing difficulty in doing something. |  The company is hard-pressed to meet the deadline.  这家公司很难按期完成任务。 |  Many families are hard-pressed financially. 许多家庭经济拮据。 | Synonyms: strained, struggling, under pressure, in difficulty | Antonyms: affluent, prosperous, well-off | adjective
