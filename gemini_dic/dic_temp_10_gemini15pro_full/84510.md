hamadryad:/ˌhæməˈdraɪæd/ /ˌhæməˈdriːæd/| n. |森林女神，树神;眼镜王蛇|A wood nymph or tree spirit. A venomous elapid snake (Ophiophagus hannah) of southern Asia, the longest venomous snake in the world. |The hamadryad whispered secrets to the wind. 森林女神向风低语秘密。| The hamadryad reared its hood, hissing menacingly. 眼镜王蛇竖起兜帽，发出威胁的嘶嘶声。| 近义词：nymph, dryad, tree spirit (for mythical meaning); king cobra (for snake meaning)| 反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:名词
