accordable:/əˈkɔːrdəbl/
adjective
可负担得起的，可以给予的
Able to be accorded; grantable.  Something that can be agreed upon, granted, or afforded.
The price of the car was accordable, fitting within their budget. 这辆车的价格是可以负担得起的，符合他们的预算。
The terms of the treaty were accordable to both nations.  该条约的条款对两国都是可以接受的。
近义词:affordable, reasonable,  grant-able, agreeable
反义词:expensive, unaffordable, exorbitant, unacceptable
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From accord (v.) + -able.  "Accord" ultimately comes from the Latin "accordare," meaning "to agree" or "to grant."
