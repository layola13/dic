coaxingly:/koʊˈæksɪŋli/| adv.|哄骗地，劝诱地|In a manner intended to persuade by gentle urging or flattery.|She coaxingly whispered promises in his ear. 她在他耳边哄骗地低语着承诺。|例句：He coaxingly urged her to try just one bite of the cake. 他哄着她让她尝一口蛋糕。|近义词：persuasively, winningly, charmingly, flatteringly, wheedlingly|反义词：forcefully, aggressively, demandingly|词性:副词

coax:/koʊˈæks/| vt.|哄骗，劝诱|To persuade someone gently and patiently to do something.|She coaxed the cat down from the tree. 她哄着猫从树上下来。|例句：He coaxed his son into taking his medicine. 他哄着儿子吃药。|近义词：persuade, wheedle, cajole|反义词：force, compel|词性:动词
