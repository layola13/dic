burglarized:/ˈbɜːrɡləraɪzd/| v. |被盗窃，被非法闯入|To enter a building illegally with intent to commit a crime, especially theft. |例句：My house was burglarized last night. 我的房子昨晚被盗了。

例句：The store was burglarized and all the cash was stolen.  这家商店被盗了，所有的现金都被偷走了。

近义词：robbed, broken into, raided|反义词:protected, secured|词性:动词
