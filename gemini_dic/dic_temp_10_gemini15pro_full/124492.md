moulding:/ˈməʊldɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmɔːldɪŋ/ (US)| n.| 模塑;造型;装饰线条 |A decorative strip of shaped wood or other material used for ornamentation, especially around the edge of a wall, ceiling, door, or window. | The ornate moulding around the fireplace added a touch of elegance to the room.  房间壁炉周围华丽的装饰线条增添了一丝优雅。 |synonyms: trim, molding (US), cornice, border | antonyms: plainness, simplicity | noun


moulding:/ˈməʊldɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈmɔːldɪŋ/ (US)| n.| 模制品;铸件 |Something that has been moulded. |The plastic mouldings were then assembled to form the toy. 塑料模制品随后被组装成玩具。|synonyms: casting, forming, shaping | antonyms:  N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *moldyng, *mouldyng, from Anglo-Norman *molder, *mouldre (“to mould”), from Latin modulus (“a small measure, standard”), diminutive of modus (“measure, manner, mode”).
