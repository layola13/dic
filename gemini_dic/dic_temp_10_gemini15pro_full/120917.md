miscatalog:/ˌmɪsˈkætəlɒɡ/ (US) /ˌmɪsˈkætəlɔːɡ/ (UK)
verb
错误分类
To incorrectly categorize or classify something, usually in a library or other system of organization.
The librarian miscataloged the new biography, placing it in the fiction section. 图书管理员把这本新传记错误地归类到了小说区。
misclassify, misfile, mislabel
correctly catalog, categorize properly
v.
