musicologist:/ˌmjuːzɪˈkɒlədʒɪst/ | n. |音乐学家；音乐学者|A person who studies the history, theory, and forms of music. |例句：The musicologist specialized in the study of medieval chant.  这位音乐学家专门研究中世纪圣歌。

例句：The musicologist delivered a fascinating lecture on the evolution of the symphony. 这位音乐学家就交响乐的演变发表了一场精彩的演讲。|近义词：music scholar, music historian|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From music + -o- + -logist.  "Music" comes from the Greek "mousikē" (art of the Muses). "-logist" denotes one who studies or is an expert in a particular subject.
