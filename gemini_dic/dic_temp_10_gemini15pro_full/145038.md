overages:/ˈoʊvərɪdʒɪz/
n.
超额，超支，额外费用
amounts exceeding a specified limit or quota; surpluses
The company charged us for overages on our data plan. 公司对我们数据套餐的超额部分收费了。
synonyms: surplus, excess, overruns
antonyms: shortages, deficits, underages
noun


overage:/ˈoʊvərɪdʒ/
adj.
超龄的，过期的
older than the usual or required age
He was overage for the youth soccer league. 他超龄了，不能参加青年足球联赛。
synonyms: older, expired
antonyms: underage, current
adjective
