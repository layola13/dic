harped:/hɑːrpt/ | v. | 喋喋不休；反复讲；弹奏竖琴 | to talk or write persistently and tediously on (a particular topic). To play on a harp. | He harped on the same subject all night. 他整晚都在喋喋不休地谈论同一话题。 She harped on her harp, creating a beautiful melody. 她弹奏着竖琴，创造出优美的旋律。 | 近义词：dwelt, persisted, reiterated | 反义词：ignored, neglected | 词性: 动词

词源: From Middle English *harpen, from Old English *hearpian, from hearpe (“harp”).
