ebon:/ˈɛbən/| adj. n.|乌木制的，乌木；漆黑的，黑暗|A hard, heavy, durable wood, most varieties of which are black or dark brown.  Deeply black.|例句：Her ebon hair cascaded down her back. 她的乌黑秀发倾泻而下。

例句：He had a walking stick carved from ebon. 他有一根用乌木雕刻的手杖。|近义词：black, dark, jet, ebony|反义词：white, light, pale|词性：形容词, 名词
