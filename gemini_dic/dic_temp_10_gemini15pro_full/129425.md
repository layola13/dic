nominators:/ˈnɒmɪneɪtəz/ (UK) /ˈnɑːmɪneɪtərz/ (US)
n.|提选者，提名者|A person who nominates someone or something.|The nominators for the award will announce their choices next week.  颁奖礼的提名者将于下周公布他们的选择。|Synonyms: proposer, nominator, picker, selector|Antonyms: nominee, candidate|Noun

nominate:/ˈnɒmɪneɪt/ (UK) /ˈnɑːmɪneɪt/ (US)
v.|提名，推荐|To propose or formally enter as a candidate for election or for an honor or award.|I nominate John for president. 我提名约翰为主席。|Synonyms: propose, suggest, recommend, put forward|Antonyms: reject, oppose|Verb
