pannikel:/ˈpænɪkəl/| n.| 圆锥花序；散穗花序 |A branched, indeterminate inflorescence with flowers on short flower stalks along the axis.  A panicle is a type of flower cluster.  It's like a pyramid of flowers, with the oldest flowers at the bottom and the youngest at the top.|The plant's panicle was covered in tiny white flowers.  这种植物的圆锥花序上覆盖着小白花。|Synonyms: inflorescence, flower cluster, raceme|Antonyms: N/A|noun
Etymology: From New Latin *panicula, diminutive of Latin panus (“ear of millet”), from Proto-Indo-European *peh₂- (“to swell, ripen”).
