bosset:/ˈbɒsɪt/| n.|小老板；小主管；包工头|A bosset is a person in charge of a small group of workers, often in a construction or similar industry.  They typically act as a foreman or supervisor.  |The bosset told the crew to start digging the foundation.|近义词：foreman, supervisor, overseer, gaffer|反义词: worker, subordinate|noun

Etymology:
From French "bossette," diminutive of "bosse" meaning "hump" or "lump," likely referring to a slightly elevated position or status.
