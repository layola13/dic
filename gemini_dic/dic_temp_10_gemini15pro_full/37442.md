commiserates: /kəˈmɪzəreɪts/ | v. | 表示同情，怜悯；慰问 | expresses or feels sympathy or pity for someone | She commiserates with her friend over the loss of her job. 她对朋友失去工作表示同情。| synonyms: sympathizes, condoles, empathizes | antonyms: ignores, disregards | verb

commiserate: /kəˈmɪzəreɪt/ | v. | 表示同情，怜悯；慰问 | expresses or feels sympathy or pity for someone | He commiserated with the flood victims. 他对洪水灾民表示慰问。| synonyms: sympathize, condole, empathize | antonyms: ignore, disregard | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *commiseratus, past participle of commiserārī (“to pity, feel pity for”), from Latin com- (“together”) + miserārī (“to pity”), from miser (“wretched, pitiable”).
