endorsive:/ɪnˈdɔːrsɪv/ | adj. | 支持的，赞同的；表示认可的 | Showing support or approval.  Conveying endorsement. | The report was highly endorsive of the new policy. 这份报告高度赞扬了新政策。 |  Her smile was endorsive of my idea. 她的微笑表明她赞同我的想法。 | 近义词: approving, supportive, commendatory | 反义词: critical, disapproving, opposing | 词性: adjective

词源: From endorse + -ive.  "Endorse" comes from Old French endosser "to write on the back of," from en- "on" + dos "back."
