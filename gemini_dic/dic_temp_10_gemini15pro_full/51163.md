despotism:/ˈdɛspətɪzəm/| n. |专制统治；暴政|The exercise of absolute power, especially in a cruel and oppressive way. |The country suffered under the king's despotism. 这个国家遭受了国王的暴政。|Synonyms: tyranny, dictatorship, autocracy, absolutism|Antonyms: democracy, freedom, liberty|noun

despotisms:/ˈdɛspətɪzəmz/| n. |专制统治（despotism的复数形式）|Plural form of despotism, referring to multiple instances of absolute and oppressive rule. |History is filled with examples of despotisms rising and falling. 历史上充满了专制统治兴衰的例子。|Synonyms: tyrannies, dictatorships, autocracies|Antonyms: democracies, republics|noun
