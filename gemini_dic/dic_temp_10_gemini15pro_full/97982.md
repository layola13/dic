inoculate:/ɪˈnɒkjʊleɪt/ (US) /ɪˈnɑːkjəleɪt/ (UK)| vt.|接种，灌输|To introduce something, such as a serum, vaccine, or an idea, into the mind or body.|The doctor inoculated the children against measles. 医生给孩子们接种了麻疹疫苗。|近义词：vaccinate, immunize, inject, introduce, instill|反义词: infect, contaminate|verb

inoculate:/ɪˈnɒkjʊleɪt/ (US) /ɪˈnɑːkjəleɪt/ (UK)| n.| 接种，接种物|The act of inoculating or the substance used for inoculation.|The inoculate was administered to the patient. 接种物被给予了病人。|近义词：vaccine, serum, injection|反义词: - |noun
