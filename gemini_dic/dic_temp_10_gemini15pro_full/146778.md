overidealizing: /ˌoʊvərˌaɪdiəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
过分理想化
The act of portraying or viewing something as better than it actually is, often ignoring flaws or negative aspects.
He was overidealizing the relationship, ignoring all the red flags. 他过分理想化这段关系，忽视了所有的危险信号。
She tends to overidealize her favorite authors, seeing them as flawless geniuses. 她倾向于过分理想化她最喜欢的作家，把他们视为完美的天才。
近义词: romanticizing, glorifying, extolling
反义词: denigrating, disparaging, belittling
词性: verb (present participle)

overidealized: /ˌoʊvərˌaɪdiəˌlaɪzd/
adjective
过分理想化的
Having been portrayed or viewed as better than it actually is.
He had an overidealized view of marriage. 他对婚姻抱有过分理想化的看法。
The movie presented an overidealized version of the war. 这部电影呈现了对战争过分理想化的版本。
近义词: romanticized, glorified, extolled
反义词: denigrated, disparaged, belittled
词性: adjective

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "idealize" (from "ideal" + "-ize" (suffix meaning "to make or cause to be"))
