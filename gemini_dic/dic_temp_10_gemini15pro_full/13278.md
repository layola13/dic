atheists:/ˈeɪθiɪsts/
n.
无神论者
People who do not believe in the existence of God or gods.
例句：The group of atheists held a meeting to discuss philosophical issues. 这群无神论者举行了一次会议来讨论哲学问题。

例句：Some atheists argue that morality doesn't require a belief in God. 一些无神论者认为道德不需要信仰上帝。

近义词：nonbelievers, unbelievers, skeptics
反义词：theists, believers
词性：noun (plural)
