diatomicities:/ˌdaɪətəˈmɪsətiz/
|n.|双原子性；双原子分子数|The diatomicity of oxygen is 2. 氧气的双原子性是2。|Diatomic molecules are molecules composed of only two atoms, of the same or different chemical elements.  The diatomicity of a sample refers to the number or proportion of diatomic molecules present.|例句：The diatomicity of the gas mixture affects its reactivity. 这种气体混合物的双原子性会影响其反应性。

例句：Understanding the diatomicity of elements is crucial in chemistry. 理解元素的双原子性在化学中至关重要。|近义词：diatomic character|反义词：monatomicity|词性：noun
