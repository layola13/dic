homerooms: /ˈhəʊmrʊmz/ (UK) /ˈhoʊmrʊmz/ (US) | n. | 班级教室；导师时间；(指在导师时间进行的) 班级会议 | A classroom in which a group of students assemble daily with the same teacher before going to other classes. Also, a period of time set aside for such an assembly.  |  例句：My homeroom is on the second floor. 我的班级教室在二楼。

例句：We have homeroom every morning at 8:00 am. 我们每天早上8点有导师时间。 | 近义词：classroom, advisory period | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词
