outrageousness:/ˌaʊtˈreɪdʒəsnəs/
n.
暴行，骇人听闻，令人愤慨
The outrageousness of the crime shocked the nation.  这起罪行的残暴震惊了全国。
The outrageousness of his behavior was appalling. 他行为的恶劣令人震惊。
atrocity, enormity, flagrancy, scandalousness
decency, propriety, reasonableness

ETYMOLOGY->
From outrageous + -ness.  Outrageous comes from Old French outrageus, from outrage (noun), ultimately from ultra "beyond" + Lat. *rabium "rage".
