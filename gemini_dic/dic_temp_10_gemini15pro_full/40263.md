contingentness:/kənˈtɪndʒəntnəs/| n.|偶然性，可能性，依情况而定|The contingentness of the project's success relies heavily on funding.  这个项目的成功与否很大程度上取决于资金。| contingency, uncertainty, dependence|certainty, inevitability|noun
contingent:/kənˈtɪndʒənt/| adj. n.|偶然的，依情况而定的；小分队|The trip is contingent on the weather.  这次旅行取决于天气。|conditional, dependent, accidental|independent, certain, planned|adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *contingentia, from Latin contingens (present participle of contingere “to touch, befall”), from com- “together” + tangere “to touch”.
