headstrong:/ˈhedstrɔːŋ/| adj.|固执的，任性的|Determined to have one's own way, not easily controlled or persuaded.|She's a headstrong child and rarely listens to advice. 她是一个固执的孩子，很少听取建议。|He was too headstrong to admit he was wrong. 他太固执了，不肯承认自己错了。|近义词：willful, stubborn, obstinate, unruly|反义词：compliant, docile, obedient|词性: adjective
