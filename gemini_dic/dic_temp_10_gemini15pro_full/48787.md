deflecting:/dɪˈflɛktɪŋ/
adjective|偏转的，转向的|Causing something to change direction; turning aside from a straight course.|The goalie made a deflecting save with his glove. 守门员用手套挡出了偏转的球。|diverting, redirecting|attracting, confronting
verb|（使）偏斜；（使）转向|To cause something to change direction by hitting or touching it; to turn or be turned aside from a straight course.|The bullet was deflected by the metal plate. 子弹被金属板挡开了。|divert, redirect|attract, confront
