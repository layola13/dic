misconsecrated:/ˌmɪskɒnsɪˈkreɪtɪd/
adjective
亵渎的，未正确神圣化的
wrongly consecrated; made sacred in an invalid or improper way.
The building was deemed misconsecrated due to a flaw in the ceremony.  这栋建筑由于仪式上的缺陷被认为是未被正确神圣化的。
The unconsecrated ground was deemed unsuitable for burial. 这片未经祝圣的土地被认为不适合埋葬。
近义词:profane, desecrated, unholy
反义词:consecrated, hallowed, sacred
词性:adjective

词源:
"mis-" (prefix of Latin origin meaning "wrong," "bad," or "ill") + "consecrate" (from Latin "consecratus," past participle of "consecrare" -  "to make sacred, dedicate, devote," from "com-" (intensive prefix) + "sacer" - "sacred") + "-ed" (past participle suffix)
