heckler:/ˈhɛklər/ (US), /ˈhɛklər/ (UK)| n. |诘问者；起哄者；扰乱分子|a person who heckles|The hecklers were escorted out of the auditorium.  扰乱分子被护送出礼堂。| synonyms: interrupter, disruptor, jeerer | antonyms: supporter, admirer| noun
