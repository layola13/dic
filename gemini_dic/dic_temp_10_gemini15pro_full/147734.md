overprocess:/ˌoʊvərˈprɑːses/| v. |过度加工，过度处理|To process excessively, often to the point of diminishing returns or negative consequences.  To subject something to too many steps in a process, often making it worse.|The vegetables were overprocessed and had lost all their flavor. 这些蔬菜被过度加工，失去了所有的味道。|近义词：overdo, overwork, overrefine|反义词: underprocess|词性:动词

overprocess:/ˌoʊvərˈprɑːses/| n. |过度加工，过度处理|The act of processing excessively.  The state of being overprocessed.|The overprocess made the final product less desirable. 过度加工使得最终产品不那么令人满意。|近义词：overdoing, overworking, overrefinement|反义词: underprocessing|词性:名词
