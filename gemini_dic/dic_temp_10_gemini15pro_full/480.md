abnodate: /æbˈnoʊdeɪt/
verb
放弃；正式放弃（权利、要求等）
to formally renounce or relinquish a right, claim, or privilege
The king abnodated his throne. 国王放弃了王位。
He abnodated all claims to the property. 他放弃了所有财产所有权。
近义词: renounce, relinquish, abdicate, forgo
反义词: claim, assert, maintain
词性: verb
