noneternal:/ˌnɒnɪˈtɜːnəl/ | /ˌnɑːnɪˈtɜːrnəl/ | adj. | 非永恒的，暂时的 | Not eternal; having a limited duration. |  The beauty of flowers is noneternal, they eventually wither.  花儿的美丽是非永恒的，它们最终会枯萎。|  Human life is noneternal. 人生苦短。 | 近义词: temporary, transient, finite, ephemeral | 反义词: eternal, everlasting, permanent, infinite | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- + eternal.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "eternal" means lasting forever.
