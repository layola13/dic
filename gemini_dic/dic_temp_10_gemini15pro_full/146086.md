overeducating:/ˌoʊvərˈɛdʒʊkeɪtɪŋ/
|v.|过度教育|The act of providing someone with more education than is necessary or beneficial for their chosen career or life path.  It implies a potential mismatch between educational attainment and practical application or employment opportunities.|Some argue that we are overeducating our young people for the jobs that are available.  有些人认为我们对年轻人的教育程度超过了现有工作的需要。|over-educating, overschooling|under-educating|verb

overeducated:/ˌoʊvərˈɛdʒʊkeɪtɪd/
|adj.|受教育过度的|Having more education than is necessary or beneficial for one's job or life situation.|He felt overeducated and underemployed in his current role.  他觉得自己在目前的职位上学历过高，大材小用。|overschooled|undereducated, uneducated|adjective
