development:/dɪˈvɛləpmənt/ /dəˈvɛləpmənt/| n.|发展，开发，进展|a new stage in the development of technology 技术发展的新阶段|The latest developments in the story are shocking. 故事的最新进展令人震惊。|近义词：growth, progress, evolution, advancement|反义词：decline, stagnation, regression|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *développement*, from *développer* "to unwrap, unfold, develop," from *des-* (from Latin *dis-*) "apart, away" + *voloper* "to wrap" (from Frankish or some other Germanic source).
