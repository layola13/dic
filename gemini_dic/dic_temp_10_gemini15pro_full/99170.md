interchromosomal:/ˌɪntərˈkroʊməˌsoʊməl/
adjective
染色体间的，异染色体的
Occurring between different chromosomes.  Relating to or involving two or more chromosomes.
例句：Interchromosomal recombination occurs during meiosis. 染色体间重组发生在减数分裂期间。

例句：Interchromosomal translocations can lead to genetic disorders. 染色体间易位会导致遗传疾病。

反义词：intrachromosomal
词性：adjective
