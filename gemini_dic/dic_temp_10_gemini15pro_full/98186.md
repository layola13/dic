insculpture:/ɪnˈskʌlptʃər/
|v.|雕刻；铭刻|To carve into a surface, especially so as to create a three-dimensional image or inscription.|He insculptured his initials on the tree. 他把他的姓名首字母刻在树上。|近义词: carve, engrave, etch|反义词: erase, efface|动词

insculpture:/ɪnˈskʌlptʃər/
|n.|雕刻品；铭刻|A carved figure or inscription.|The insculpture on the tombstone was weathered and hard to read. 墓碑上的铭刻被风化了，很难辨认。|近义词: carving, engraving, etching|反义词: erasure|名词
