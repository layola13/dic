hagiographer:/ˌhæɡiˈɒɡrəfər/
noun
圣徒传记作者；吹捧者
A writer of hagiographies, especially one who idealizes the subject.  A person who praises someone excessively.
The hagiographer portrayed the politician as a saintly figure, ignoring his many flaws.  这位圣徒传记作者将这位政治家描绘成一个圣人般的人物，而忽略了他的许多缺点。
He's not a journalist, he's a hagiographer, always writing glowing profiles of his subjects. 他不是记者，他是个吹捧者，总是写他主题的光辉形象。
synonyms: biographer, eulogist, panegyrist, idolater
antonyms: critic, detractor, debunker
