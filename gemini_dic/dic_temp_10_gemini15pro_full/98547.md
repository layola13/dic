instiller:/ɪnˈstɪlər/| n.|灌输者；谆谆教诲者|A person who instills something, such as an idea or feeling, into another person.|例句：He was the instiller of moral values in his students. 他是他学生们的道德价值观的灌输者。

例句：She is a great instiller of confidence in young people.  她非常善于培养年轻人的自信心。

近义词：teacher, instructor, mentor, guide|反义词：corrupter, demoralizer|词性：noun
