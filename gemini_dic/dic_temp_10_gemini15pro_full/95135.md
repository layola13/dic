inapplication:/ˌɪnˌæplɪˈkeɪʃn/
n.
不适用,不应用,未应用
lack of application or not putting something into action.
The policy's inapplication to part-time workers caused resentment. 这项政策不适用于兼职工人，引起了他们的不满。
inapplicability, non-application, disuse
application, implementation, use
noun
