lucullite:/ˈluːkjʊˌlaɪt/ /ˈlʌkjʊˌlaɪt/ | n. | 透辉石，辉石的一种 | A variety of pyroxene, a mineral. |  Lucullite is a beautiful dark green mineral often used in jewelry. 透辉石是一种美丽的深绿色矿物，常用于珠宝。

Lucullite can be found in metamorphic rocks. 透辉石可以在变质岩中找到。| 近义词: diopside, hedenbergite | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
