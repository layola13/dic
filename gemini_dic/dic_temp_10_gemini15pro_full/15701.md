balaclava:/ˈbæləˌklɑːvə/ (US), /ˌbæləˈklɑːvə/ (UK)| n. |巴拉克拉法帽，面罩|A close-fitting garment covering the whole head and neck except for parts of the face, typically worn for protection from cold weather.|The skiers wore balaclavas to protect their faces from the wind and snow. 滑雪者戴着巴拉克拉法帽，以保护他们的脸免受风雪侵袭。|Synonyms: ski mask, face mask|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
