entwist:/ɪnˈtwɪst/ | v. |缠绕，交织，使纠缠|To twist or twine together; interweave.|The vines entwisted around the old oak tree. 藤蔓缠绕着古老的橡树。|近义词：intertwine, interweave, tangle|反义词：untwist, disentangle|词性:动词

词源: From en- + twist.  "En-" acts as a prefix meaning "to cause to be," or "to put into."  "Twist" comes from the Old English *twist, meaning "rope, cord."
