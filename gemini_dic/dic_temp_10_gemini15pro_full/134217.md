noninducible:/ˌnɒnɪnˈdjuːsɪbl/ |adj.|不可诱导的|Not able to be induced or activated; unresponsive to a stimulus.  Referring to a gene, enzyme, or other biological system that cannot be activated or expressed in response to a specific stimulus. |The noninducible enzyme remained inactive despite the presence of the substrate.| 近义词:constitutive, unreactive, unresponsive |反义词:inducible, responsive, reactive|adjective
