paltock:/ˈpɔːltɒk/ (UK), /ˈpɑːlˌtɑːk/ (US)| n. |大衣；粗呢外套；（尤指）骑兵大衣|A long, loose overcoat, especially one worn by soldiers.|He wore a heavy paltock against the biting wind. 他穿着一件厚重的骑兵大衣抵御刺骨的寒风。|近义词：overcoat, greatcoat, cloak|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly light jacket)|词性:noun

词源:
From obsolete Dutch *paltsrok, from palt "short coat" + rok "coat, skirt".  Ultimately from a Germanic root related to "fold".
来自已废弃的荷兰语 *paltsrok，由 palt “短外套” + rok “外套，裙子” 组成。最终来自与“折叠”相关的日耳曼语词根。
