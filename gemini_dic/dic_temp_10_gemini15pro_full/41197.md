coquelicot:/kɔkliˈkoʊ/ (US), /kɒkliˈkəʊ/ (UK)| n. |虞美人；罂粟花；猩红色|A European wild poppy with bright red flowers.  It also refers to the vibrant red-orange color of these flowers.|例句：The field was ablaze with coquelicots. 田野里盛开着鲜红色的虞美人。

例句：She wore a dress the color of coquelicots. 她穿着一件虞美人红色的连衣裙。


近义词：poppy, corn poppy|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
