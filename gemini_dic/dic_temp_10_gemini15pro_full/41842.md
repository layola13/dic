cory:/ˈkɔːri/| n. |科里,一种淡水鱼|A small fish of the carp family, native to East Asia and popular in aquariums. Corydoras is often shortened to cory.|I have five corys in my fish tank. 我的鱼缸里有五条科里鱼。|近义词：catfish, Corydoras|反义词: None|词性:noun
