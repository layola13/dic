beeps:/biːps/| v. (third-person singular simple present beeps, present participle beeping, simple past beeped, past participle beeped)|发出哔哔声|To emit a series of short, high-pitched sounds. |The car beeped as I reversed. 当我倒车时，汽车发出哔哔声。|近义词：chirps, blips|反义词:mute|verb
