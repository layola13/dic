giddy:/ˈɡɪdi/| adj.|头晕的，眼花的；轻率的，轻浮的|Feeling dizzy or lightheaded; frivolous and flighty. |She felt giddy with excitement. 她兴奋得头晕目眩。|近义词：dizzy, lightheaded, frivolous, flighty|反义词:serious, sober, level-headed|词性:adjective
giddy:/ˈɡɪdi/| v.|使头晕|To make someone feel dizzy or lightheaded.|The heights made him giddy. 高度使他头晕目眩。|近义词：dizzy, unsettle|反义词:steady, stabilize|词性:verb
