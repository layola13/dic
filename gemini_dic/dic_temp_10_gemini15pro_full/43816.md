creole:/ˈkriːoʊl/ (US), /ˈkriːɒl/ (UK)| n. adj.|克里奥尔人，克里奥尔语；克里奥尔式的|A person of European descent born in the West Indies or Spanish America, especially during colonial times, or a descendant of such a person. Also, a language that has developed from a mixture of different languages.|例句：The creole cuisine of Louisiana is world-renowned. 路易斯安那的克里奥尔菜肴世界闻名。

例句：She spoke fluent Creole. 她讲一口流利的克里奥尔语。|近义词：pidgin, dialect|反义词:standard language|词性:名词, 形容词
