bpc:/biː piː siː/ |abbr.| 每字节比特数 (bits per character)| bits per character, a measure of data compression in text files.  It calculates the average number of bits used to represent each character. |The bpc of ASCII text is typically 8. | 近义词: bpc (no direct synonyms as it's a technical term), character encoding efficiency | 反义词: N/A | abbreviation
