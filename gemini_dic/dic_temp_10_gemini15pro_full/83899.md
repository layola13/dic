half-Asiatic:/ˌhæf eɪʒiˈætɪk/ | adj. | 半亚裔的 |  Partially of Asian descent. Usually refers to someone of mixed Asian and non-Asian heritage. |  She identified as half-Asiatic, with a Japanese mother and a British father. 她认为自己是半亚裔，母亲是日本人，父亲是英国人。 | Mixed-race, Eurasian (if one parent is European) |  Pure-blooded | adjective
