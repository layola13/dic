noncovetousness:/ˌnɒnkʌvɪtəsnəs/
n.
不贪婪，无贪欲
The state of not coveting or desiring something that belongs to another.
He stressed the importance of noncovetousness in a truly spiritual life.  他强调了在真正的精神生活中不贪婪的重要性。

近义词: contentment, satisfaction, detachment, generosity
反义词: covetousness, greed, avarice, envy, jealousy
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + covetousness (noun form of covet, from Old French cuveitier, ultimately from Latin cupidus "eager, desirous").
