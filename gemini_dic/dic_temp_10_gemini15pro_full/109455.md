lepidodendroid: /ˌlɛpɪdoʊˈdɛndrɔɪd/
adjective
鳞木状的；像鳞木的
Resembling or characteristic of the Lepidodendron, an extinct genus of tree-like plants related to club mosses.  Relating to or resembling the Lepidodendrales, an extinct order of tree-like lycopods.
The fossilized bark displays a distinctive lepidodendroid pattern.  这块石化树皮展现出独特的鳞木状纹理。
Near synonyms: scale-like, lepidodendralean
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, as it describes a specific extinct plant group.  One could use antonyms related to appearance, like "smooth" if discussing bark texture, but these are highly contextual)
词性: adjective
