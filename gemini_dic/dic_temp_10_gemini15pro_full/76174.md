fuss:/fʌs/| n. vi. vt.|忙乱，大惊小怪，小题大做；(为小事)烦恼，过于讲究；抱怨，抗议；无谓的活动；|To make a fuss is to show excessive concern about something unimportant.|Don't fuss over such small details. 不要为这些小细节大惊小怪。|近义词：fret, worry, niggle|反义词：ignore, disregard|词性:名词,动词


ETYMOLOGY->
Possibly from Low German fussen (“to pant, to blow”), of imitative origin.
