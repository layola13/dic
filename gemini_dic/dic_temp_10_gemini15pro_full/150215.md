pandanus:/pænˈdeɪnəs/| n.|露兜树；班丹树；|A tropical tree or shrub with long, spiny leaves and woody prop roots, bearing large, fragrant fruits.|例句：The pandanus tree is common in Southeast Asia. 露兜树在东南亚很常见。
