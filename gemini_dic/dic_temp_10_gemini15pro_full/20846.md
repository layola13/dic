blastosphere:/ˈblæstəˌsfɪr/ | n. |囊胚|A hollow sphere of cells, or blastula, formed in the early development of an embryo, especially of a mammal.| The blastosphere is a crucial stage in embryonic development. 囊胚是胚胎发育中的一个关键阶段。| The mammalian blastosphere consists of an outer layer called the trophoblast and an inner cell mass.  哺乳动物的囊胚由称为滋养层的外层和内细胞团组成。 |  blastula, embryoblast |  | noun
