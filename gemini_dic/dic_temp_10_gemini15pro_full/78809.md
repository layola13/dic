gimleting:/ˈɡɪmlɪtɪŋ/| adj. |钻孔的；尖锐的；敏锐的|piercing; sharp; penetrating.  Used to describe something that is sharp and penetrating, often like a gimlet (a small hand tool used for boring holes).|The gimleting wind cut through my thin coat. 刺骨的寒风穿透了我单薄的外套。|近义词：piercing, penetrating, sharp, keen, acute|反义词:dull, blunt|词性:形容词
gimlet:/ˈɡɪmlɪt/| n. |手钻；鸡尾酒|A small hand tool used for boring holes; A cocktail traditionally made with gin and lime juice.|He used a gimlet to make a hole for the screw. 他用手钻钻了一个螺丝孔。|近义词：drill, auger, borer|反义词:N/A|词性:名词
