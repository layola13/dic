The word is often used euphemistically or jokingly, but its core meaning is always negative and often carries a strong connotation of sexual abuse, particularly of children. |He was known as a diddler who took advantage of vulnerable people. 他是一个臭名昭著的骗子，专门欺骗弱势群体。|近义词：swindler, cheat, con artist, fraudster, pedophile (specifically for child sexual abuse) |反义词: philanthropist, humanitarian|词性: noun
