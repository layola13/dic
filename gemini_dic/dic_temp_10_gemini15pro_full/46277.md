czarist:/ˈzɑːrɪst/| adj. n.|沙皇的；沙皇支持者|Relating to or characteristic of a tsar or the tsarist system of government.  A supporter of the tsarist system.|例句：The czarist regime was overthrown in 1917. 沙皇政权于1917年被推翻。|近义词：imperial, monarchical, autocratic|反义词：democratic, republican|词性:adjective, noun

czarist:/ˈzɑːrɪst/| n.|沙皇支持者|A supporter of the tsarist system.|例句：Many czarists fled Russia after the revolution. 许多沙皇支持者在革命后逃离了俄罗斯。|近义词：monarchist, royalist|反义词：revolutionary, republican|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Tsar + -ist.  Tsar comes from the Latin Caesar, referring to the Roman emperors.
