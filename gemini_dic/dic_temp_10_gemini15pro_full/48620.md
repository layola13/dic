defence:/dɪˈfɛns/ (UK) /dɪˈfɛns/ (US)| n.|防御；保卫；辩护；答辩|The act of defending against attack or criticism; something that defends or protects.|The castle's defences were strong. 城堡的防御工事很坚固。|近义词：protection, safeguard, resistance|反义词:attack, offense|noun

defence:/dɪˈfɛns/ (UK) /dɪˈfɛns/ (US)| v.|保卫；防守|To protect someone or something from attack or criticism.|He defended his title successfully. 他成功地卫冕了他的冠军头衔。|近义词：protect, guard, shield|反义词:attack, prosecute|verb
