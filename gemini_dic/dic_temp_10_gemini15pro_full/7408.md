animation's:/ˌænɪˈmeɪʃənz/
noun
动画的; 生气的
belonging to or associated with animation.  The possessive form of "animation."
例句：The animation's vibrant colors captivated the audience. 动画鲜艳的色彩吸引了观众。

例句：The animation's smooth transitions were impressive. 动画流畅的过渡令人印象深刻。

近义词：cartoon's, animated film's
反义词:inanimate's, still's
词性:noun, possessive


Etymology:
From Late Latin animationem, accusative of animatio (“a giving of life”), from anima (“breath, soul”) + -tion.
