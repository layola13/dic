brownism:/ˈbraʊnɪzəm/| n.|布朗主义，罗伯特·布朗的宗教观点|Brownism refers to the religious views of Robert Browne, a 16th-century English Protestant reformer who advocated for the separation of church and state and the autonomy of individual congregations.|Robert Browne's Brownism was a significant influence on the development of Congregationalism. 罗伯特·布朗的布朗主义对公理会的发展有着重要的影响。|近义词：Separatism, Independency|反义词：Conformism|词性: noun
