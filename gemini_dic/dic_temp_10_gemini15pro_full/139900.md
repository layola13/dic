oddness:/ˈɒdnəs/ (US) /ˈɑːdnəs/ (UK)| n. |古怪；奇特；奇异|The state or quality of being odd; strangeness or peculiarity.|There's a certain oddness about him that I can't quite put my finger on. 他身上有一种我说不清的古怪。|Synonyms: peculiarity, strangeness, eccentricity, quirkiness, bizarreness|Antonyms: normality, usualness, commonness|noun

oddness:/ˈɒdnəs/ (US) /ˈɑːdnəs/ (UK)| n. |奇数性|The quality of being an odd number.|The oddness of the number 7 is a fundamental concept in mathematics. 数字 7 的奇数性是数学中的一个基本概念。|Synonyms: imparity|Antonyms: parity, evenness|noun
