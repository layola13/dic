enlumine:/ɪˈluːmɪn/ /ɛnˈluːmɪn/ | v. |照亮，阐明；装饰，彩饰 (尤指用金或鲜艳的色彩)| To decorate (a book or manuscript) by adding elaborate designs and illustrations, often using gold or bright colors; to make something clearer or easier to understand. | The medieval monks enlumined the pages of the holy books with intricate designs. 中世纪的僧侣们用复杂的图案装饰圣书的页面。|  The setting sun enlumined the clouds with a fiery glow. 落日用火红的辉光照亮了云层。 | synonyms: illuminate, brighten, adorn, decorate, illustrate | antonyms: darken, obscure, dull | verb
