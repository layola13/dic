hard-charging:/ˌhɑːrdˈtʃɑːrdʒɪŋ/
adjective
积极进取的；充满活力的；精力充沛的
Energetic and ambitious; aggressively pursuing success.
Example:  She's a hard-charging executive who works 12 hours a day. 她是一位精力充沛的执行官，每天工作 12 小时。

Example:  The hard-charging team was determined to win the championship.  这支充满活力的团队决心赢得冠军。

近义词: ambitious, driven, assertive, aggressive, dynamic, go-getting
反义词: lazy, passive, unmotivated, lethargic


ETYMOLOGY->
"Hard" meaning with great force or energy.  "Charging" from the verb "to charge," meaning to rush forward, attack, or move with great energy. The combined term emphasizes a forceful and driven approach to achieving goals.
