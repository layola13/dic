luting:/ˈluːtɪŋ/| n.|粘固，粘结|The luting of the crown was done with a special dental cement.  牙冠的粘固是用一种特殊的牙科水泥完成的。|The luting agent provided a strong bond between the two materials.  粘结剂在两种材料之间提供了牢固的结合。|近义词：cementing, bonding, fixing|反义词：removal, detachment|词性：noun
词源：From the Latin word "lutum" meaning mud or clay, referring to substances used for sealing or joining. 源于拉丁语单词 "lutum"，意思是泥或粘土，指的是用于密封或连接的物质。
