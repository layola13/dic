outcried:/ˈaʊtkraɪd/| v. |大声疾呼，强烈抗议|To have protested loudly and vehemently against something. |The public outcried against the new tax law. 公众强烈抗议新的税法。|Synonyms: protested, objected, denounced, condemned|Antonyms: supported, approved, endorsed|verb

outcry:/ˈaʊtkraɪ/| n. |强烈抗议，大声疾呼|A strong protest or loud complaint. |There was a public outcry over the proposed cuts to education.  公众对提议的教育削减强烈抗议。|Synonyms: protest, clamor, uproar, commotion|Antonyms: support, approval, endorsement|noun
