词源: From Late Middle English ensigncie, from Anglo-Norman enseignacie, enseignesie, from enseigner (“to teach, instruct; to show, indicate; to assign, appoint”) + -ie (suffix forming nouns).  The word originally referred to the office or position of an ensign, later also to the flag itself.
