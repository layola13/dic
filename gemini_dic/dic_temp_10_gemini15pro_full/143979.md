outraging:/ˈaʊtreɪdʒɪŋ/| adj.|令人愤慨的；骇人的|Causing shock and anger; extremely offensive.|例句：The outraging behavior of the rioters shocked the nation.  暴徒令人愤慨的行为震惊了全国。|例句：His outraging comments about the tragedy were met with widespread condemnation. 他关于这场悲剧的骇人言论遭到了广泛谴责。|近义词：appalling, shocking, scandalous, offensive, egregious|反义词： commendable, laudable, praiseworthy|词性:形容词

词源: From outrage (v.) + -ing. Outrage comes from Old French "outrage," ultimately from Latin "ultra" (beyond) + "-agium" (related to "agere," to do).
