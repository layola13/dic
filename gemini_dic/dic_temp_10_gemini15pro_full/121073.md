词源:
From mis- + construe.  "Mis-" is a prefix indicating wrong or bad. "Construe" comes from the Latin word "construere" meaning "to build, pile up, arrange, construct." Therefore, "misconstrue" literally means to construct wrongly or interpret incorrectly.
