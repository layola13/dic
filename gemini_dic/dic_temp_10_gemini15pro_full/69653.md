fence-in:/fɛns ˈɪn/ | phrasal verb | 围起来，限制，禁锢 | To enclose or confine with or as if with a fence; restrict. |  The farmer fenced in his cattle. 农民用栅栏围住了他的牛。

The new regulations fence in our freedom. 新规定限制了我们的自由。

| 近义词：enclose, confine, restrict, hem in | 反义词: free, liberate, release | 词性: 动词短语
