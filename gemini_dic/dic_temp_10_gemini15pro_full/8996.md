antiliteracy:/ˌæntɪˈlɪtərəsi/
n.|反 literacy, 文盲主义；对识字的反对|Opposition to literacy or the spread of literacy.  It often involves actively discouraging people from learning to read or write.|The regime promoted antiliteracy among its citizens, fearing an educated populace.|近义词: illiteracy, ignorance|反义词: literacy, education|词性:名词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek anti-)
literacy (noun): the ability to read and write
