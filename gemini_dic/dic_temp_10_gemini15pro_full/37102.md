combless:/ˈkoʊmlɪs/| adj.|无梳的，没有冠的|Lacking a comb, especially referring to certain breeds of poultry.|The combless hen blended in with the others.|Near synonyms: crestless, smooth-headed|Opposite: combed, crested
Etymology: comb + -less.  "Comb" comes from Old English *camb, from Proto-Germanic *kambaz, meaning "comb, crest". The suffix "-less" is from Old English -lēas, meaning "without".
词性: 形容词
