begorra:/bɪˈɡɒrə/ | int. | 我的天哪；哎呀 | An exclamation of surprise or emphasis, chiefly Irish.  A mild oath, used as a euphemism for "by God". | Begorra, it's cold today!  我的天哪，今天真冷！| 近义词: by golly, gosh, heavens | 反义词: None (as it's an interjection) | 词性: Interjection
