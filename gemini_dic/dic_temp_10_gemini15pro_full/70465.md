figurines:/ˈfɪɡjəriːnz/
noun
小雕像，小塑像
Small statues or sculpted figures, often collectible.
She collects porcelain figurines of cats. 她收集瓷猫小雕像。
synonyms: statuettes, miniatures, models, ornaments
antonyms: giants, colossi
n.
