misreform:/ˌmɪsrɪˈfɔːrm/
verb
错误改革；改革不当
To reform in a way that is misguided, ineffective, or harmful.
The government's attempt to misreform the healthcare system resulted in higher costs and reduced access. 政府试图错误改革医疗保健系统，导致成本上升，获得医疗服务的机会减少。
mismanage, misdirect
reform, improve
v.
