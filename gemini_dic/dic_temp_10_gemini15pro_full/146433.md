overforwardly:/ˌoʊvərˈfɔːrwərdli/
|adv.|过分向前地，过于热情地|Excessively forward; too eager or bold in manner or action, often to the point of being presumptuous or intrusive.  The word combines "over" (meaning excessively) and "forwardly" (meaning in a forward manner).|He acted overforwardly with his boss, offering unsolicited advice and suggestions.|近义词：presumptuously, intrusively, impudently, boldly, brashly|反义词：reservedly, timidly, shyly, cautiously|词性:副词
