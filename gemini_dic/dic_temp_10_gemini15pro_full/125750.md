mural:/ˈmjʊərəl/ (US), /ˈmjɔːrəl/ (UK)| n. |壁画|A painting applied directly to a wall or ceiling surface.|The artist was commissioned to paint a mural on the side of the building. 艺术家受委托在建筑的侧面绘制壁画。|
The museum boasts a stunning mural depicting scenes from local history.  这家博物馆拥有一幅描绘当地历史场景的精美壁画。|synonyms: fresco, wall painting|antonyms: miniature, easel painting|noun
