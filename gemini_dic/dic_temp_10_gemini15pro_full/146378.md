overfloridly:/ˌoʊvərˈflɔːrɪdli/
| adv. | 过分华丽地，过于修饰地 | Excessively ornate or flowery in style; too elaborate or embellished. |  His prose is often criticized for being overfloridly descriptive. 他的散文经常因描写过于华丽而受到批评。 |
|例句：The author's overfloridly written novel lost the reader's interest.  这位作者写得过于华丽的小说失去了读者的兴趣。|
|近义词：ornately, elaborately, excessively, flamboyantly |
|反义词：simply, plainly, subtly |
|词性: 副词(adverb) |
