humane:/hjuːˈmeɪn/ | adj. | 人道的；仁慈的；亲切的 | Having or showing compassion or benevolence. |  例句：It's important to treat animals in a humane way.  对待动物要人道。

例句：The prison offers humane conditions for inmates.  这所监狱为囚犯提供人道的条件。

近义词：compassionate, kind, merciful, benevolent, empathetic | 反义词：cruel, inhumane, brutal, callous, uncaring | 词性：形容词

词源：Late Middle English (in the sense ‘pertaining to or characteristic of human beings’): from late Latin humanus, from homo ‘man’. The current sense dates from the late 18th century.
