nonresuscitable: /ˌnɑːnˌrɪˈsʌsɪtəbəl/ /ˌnɒnˌrɪˈsʌsɪtəbəl/ | adj. | 不可复苏的，不予心肺复苏的 | Not suitable for or not responding to resuscitation.  A patient is deemed nonresuscitable when it is determined that efforts to restart the heart and breathing would be futile or go against the patient's wishes. | The patient wore a "Do Not Resuscitate" bracelet, indicating they were nonresuscitable.  这位病人戴着“不予心肺复苏”的手镯，表明他们不可复苏。

The medical team deemed the patient nonresuscitable due to the extent of their injuries.  医疗团队认为病人由于伤势严重，无法复苏。 | 近义词: incurable, terminal, hopeless | 反义词: resuscitable, treatable | 词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix): not, the opposite of
resuscitable (adjective): able to be resuscitated
resuscitate (verb): from Latin resuscitatus, past participle of resuscitare "revive," from re- "again" + suscitare "to rouse, lift up," from sub- "up from below" + citare "to summon, rouse."
