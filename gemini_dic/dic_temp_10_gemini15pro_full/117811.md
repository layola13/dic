melisma:/məˈlɪzmə/ | n. |滑音，装饰音|A group of notes sung to one syllable of text. | The soprano's melisma at the end of the aria was breathtaking. 女高音在咏叹调结尾处的滑音令人叹为观止。| 近义词:cadenza, flourish | 反义词:monotone | 词性:noun
Etymology: From Late Latin melisma, from Greek μέλισμα (mélisma, “song, air, melody”) (from μέλος (mélos, “limb, member; song, tune, strain”)), from Proto-Indo-European *mel- (“to strike softly, grind”).
