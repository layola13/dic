counterpicketed:/ˌkaʊntərˈpɪkɪtɪd/ | v. | 反抗性纠察；对…进行反纠察 |  To picket in opposition to another picket line. | The union members counterpicketed the company's attempt to hire non-union workers. 工会成员对公司雇用非工会工人的企图进行了反纠察。| Synonyms: counter-protest, counter-demonstrate | Antonyms: support, endorse | verb

counterpicketed:/ˌkaʊntərˈpɪkɪtɪd/ | adj. | 被反纠察的 | Having been the target of a counterpicket. | The counterpicketed factory remained closed. 被反纠察的工厂仍然关闭。| Synonyms: opposed, resisted | Antonyms: supported, endorsed | adjective
