antihydrophobic:/ˌæntiˌhaɪdrəˈfəʊbɪk/
adjective
抗疏水的
Resisting or counteracting the tendency to repel water.  A material that attracts or absorbs water rather than repelling it.
This new coating is antihydrophobic, allowing the paint to adhere to damp surfaces.  这种新型涂层具有抗疏水性，可以让油漆附着在潮湿的表面上。
Near Antonyms: hydrophobic, water-repellent
Antonyms: hydrophilic
