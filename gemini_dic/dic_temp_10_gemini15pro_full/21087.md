blingest:/ˈblɪŋɪst/| adj.|最闪亮的，最奢华的|Superlative form of "blingy," meaning extremely flashy or ostentatious, typically due to being covered in jewelry or other expensive accessories.|She wore the blingest dress at the party, covered in sequins and diamonds. 她穿着派对上最闪亮的裙子，上面缀满了亮片和钻石。|近义词：flashiest, gaudiest, most ostentatious|反义词：plainest, simplest, most understated|词性:形容词

blingest:/ˈblɪŋɪst/| n.|最闪亮的东西|The item with the most bling; the most ostentatious or flashy thing.|Of all the jewelry, the diamond necklace was the blingest. 在所有的珠宝中，钻石项链是最闪亮的。|近义词：showstopper, centerpiece|反义词：plain item, simple accessory|词性:名词

词源：blingest 源自 bling，bling 最初是拟声词，模仿珠宝闪光的声音。后来发展成名词，指闪亮的珠宝或配饰，之后又演变成形容词 blingy，表示“闪亮的，奢华的”。 blingest 是 blingy 的最高级形式。
