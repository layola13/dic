cemented:/sɪˈmɛntɪd/
adjective
巩固的，加固的
Made firm or secure.
例句：Their friendship was cemented by shared experiences.  他们的友谊因共同的经历而更加巩固。

例句：The bricks were cemented together.  砖块被水泥粘合在一起。

近义词：strengthened, solidified, fixed, secured, established
反义词：weakened, loosened, unstable
词性: adjective


cemented:/sɪˈmɛntɪd/
verb
粘合，巩固
To join or make firm by cement.  Also used figuratively to mean to strengthen or solidify.
例句：He cemented the bricks with mortar. 他用水泥砂浆粘合砖块。

例句：The agreement cemented the relationship between the two countries.  这项协议巩固了两国之间的关系。

近义词：bonded, united, fused, adhered, consolidated
反义词：separated, divided, fractured
词性: verb (past participle)
