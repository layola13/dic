mince: /mɪns/ | v. n. | 切碎；剁碎；碎肉；矫揉造作 | to cut food, especially meat, into very small pieces, typically by using a machine |  She minced the garlic finely. 她把大蒜切得很碎。| synonyms: chop, dice, grind | antonyms: combine, coalesce | verb

mince: /mɪns/ | n. | 碎肉；矫揉造作 | meat, usually beef, that has been minced |  He made a shepherd's pie with minced lamb. 他用羊肉碎做了牧羊人馅饼。| synonyms: ground meat | antonyms: whole meat, slab of meat | noun
