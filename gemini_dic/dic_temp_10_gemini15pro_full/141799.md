orbicula:/ɔrˈbɪkjʊlə/| n. |小盘，小圆板，(解剖) 小圆肌|A small disc, a small circular area, (anatomy) a small round muscle.|The orbicularis oculi is the orbicula around the eye.| 近义词: disc, disk, circle, ring |反义词: N/A | noun

orbicular:/ɔrˈbɪkjʊlər/ | adj. |圆形的，环形的，球形的|Having the shape of a circle or orb; circular; spherical.|The orbicular muscles around the mouth help us to form different facial expressions. |近义词: circular, round, spherical, globe-shaped |反义词: linear, straight | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *orbicula*, diminutive of *orbiculus*, diminutive of *orbis* ("circle, orb").
