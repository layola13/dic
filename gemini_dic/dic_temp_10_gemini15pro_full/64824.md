espaliers:/ɪˈspæljərz/ | n. |  果树墙；棚架；墙树 | A lattice or framework on which fruit trees or ornamental shrubs are trained to grow flat against a wall or other support. | The gardener carefully pruned the branches of the apple espaliers. 园丁仔细地修剪了苹果树墙的枝条。| Synonym: trellis, lattice | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "free-standing tree") | Noun

espalier:/ɪˈspæljər/ | v. |  将…缚于棚架；使…在墙上成片生长 | To train (a tree or shrub) to grow flat against a wall or other support. | He espaliered the pear trees against the south-facing wall. 他把梨树缚在朝南的墙上。| Synonym: train, trellis | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "untrain," "let grow freely") | Verb
