domiciles:/ˈdɒmɪsaɪlz/ (UK) /ˈdɑːməsaɪlz/ (US)| n. |住所，住处|A person's residence or dwelling place; a place where a person lives. |例句：The company's registered domicile is in Delaware. 该公司的注册地址在特拉华州。

domiciles:/ˈdɒmɪsaɪlz/ (UK) /ˈdɑːməsaɪlz/ (US)| v. |使定居，安置|To establish a domicile or residence. |例句：The refugees were domiciled in temporary housing. 难民被安置在临时住所。

domicile:/ˈdɒmɪsaɪl/ (UK) /ˈdɑːməsaɪl/ (US)| n. |住所，住处|A person's residence or dwelling place; a place where a person lives. |例句：He established his domicile in Paris. 他在巴黎定居。


近义词: residence, abode, dwelling, habitation, home
反义词:  None (Domicile refers to a legal residence, not its opposite.)
词性: noun (primarily), verb (less common)

词源：From Old French *domicilium*, from Latin domicilium "dwelling, abode," from domus "house."
