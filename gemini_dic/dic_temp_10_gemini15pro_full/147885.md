overreadiness:/ˌōvərˈredinəs/
|n.| 过度准备；过早准备；准备过度|The overreadiness of the team led to burnout before the competition even began. 团队的过度准备导致比赛还没开始就精疲力尽了。|Overreadiness can sometimes be detrimental to performance. 过度准备有时会对表现有害。|近义词: preparedness, overpreparation|反义词: unpreparedness, lack of preparation|词性:名词

词源: over- + readiness
over-:  prefix meaning "too much," "excessively,"  from Old English *ofer- (cognate with Old High German ubar- "over, above, beyond").
readiness: from ready + -ness. Ready from Old English rǣde.
