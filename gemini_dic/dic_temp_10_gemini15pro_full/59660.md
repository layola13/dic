echinidan:/ɪˈkɪnɪdən/
n.
针鼹；针鼹鼠
A spiny anteater; a small, burrowing, nocturnal mammal native to Australia and New Guinea, with a long snout, a sticky tongue, and sharp spines covering its back.
Echidnas are fascinating creatures that lay eggs. 针鼹是产卵的迷人动物。
Echidnas use their long snouts to probe for ants and termites. 针鼹用它们的长鼻子探测蚂蚁和白蚁。
近义词:spiny anteater
反义词:(无明确的反义词)
词性:noun


echinidan:/ɪˈkɪnɪdən/
n.
(动物学)针鼹科动物
(zoology) any of several egg-laying, toothless mammals of the family Tachyglossidae, native to Australia, Tasmania, and New Guinea, having a long snout and a body covered with spines.
The short-beaked echidna is found throughout Australia. 短吻针鼹遍布澳大利亚。
Long-beaked echidnas are found in New Guinea. 长吻针鼹则在新几内亚被发现。
近义词:tachyglossid
反义词:(无明确的反义词)
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *Echinidna, from Ancient Greek ἐχῖνος (ekhînos, “hedgehog, sea urchin”) + εἶδος (eîdos, “form, appearance”).
