analogies:/əˈnælədʒiz/ | n. | 类比；比拟；相似 | A comparison between two things, typically for the purpose of explanation or clarification. |  He drew an analogy between the human brain and a computer. 他将人脑和电脑作了类比。 | synonyms: comparisons, parallels, similarities, likenesses | antonyms: differences, dissimilarities | noun
