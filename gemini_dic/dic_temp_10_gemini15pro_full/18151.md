词源：
"Beijing" comes from the Mandarin pronunciation of the two Chinese characters 北京.  "Bei" (北) means "north," and "jing" (京) means "capital."  Therefore, Beijing literally translates to "Northern Capital."  The older name, "Peking," is a romanization derived from an earlier pronunciation.
