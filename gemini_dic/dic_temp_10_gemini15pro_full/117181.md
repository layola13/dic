measly:/ˈmiːzli/| adj.|少得可怜的，微不足道的|Contemptibly small or few.|He offered me a measly five dollars for my antique watch. 他居然只出五美元买我的古董表。|近义词：paltry, meager, pitiful, insignificant|反义词: generous, substantial, ample|adjective

measly:/ˈmiːzli/| adj.|患麻疹的 (旧用法)|infected with measles (archaic)|The child had a measly rash.  这个孩子出了麻疹的疹子。(旧用法，现已少用)|近义词：none (for this meaning)|反义词: healthy|adjective
