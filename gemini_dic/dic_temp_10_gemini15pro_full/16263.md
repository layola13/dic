banyans:/ˈbænjənz/| n.|榕树|A fig tree of tropical and subtropical regions that sends down aerial roots from its branches to form additional trunks. |例句：The banyans provided welcome shade from the scorching sun. 榕树提供了受欢迎的遮荫，以避开炎热的太阳。

例句：We sat beneath the ancient banyans, marveling at their size. 我们坐在古老的榕树下，惊叹于它们的大小。|近义词：fig tree|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
