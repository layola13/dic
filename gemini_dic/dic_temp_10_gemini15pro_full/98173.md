inscription:/ɪnˈskrɪpʃən/ | n. |题词，铭文，刻印|A piece of writing inscribed on a surface, such as stone or metal. |例句：The inscription on the tombstone was barely legible. 墓碑上的铭文几乎难以辨认。

例句：The inscription on the ring commemorated their anniversary.  戒指上的铭文是为了纪念他们的周年纪念日。


近义词：carving, lettering, engraving, epitaph |反义词: erasure, deletion |词性: noun
