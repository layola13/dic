assailment:/əˈseɪlmənt/| n. |攻击，袭击，抨击|A violent or aggressive attack, criticism, or barrage. |The castle walls crumbled under the relentless assailment of the enemy's cannons. 城堡的城墙在敌人大炮无情的攻击下崩塌了。

The newspaper launched a scathing assailment on the politician's character.  这家报纸对这位政治家的人品进行了严厉的抨击。|近义词：attack, assault, onslaught, barrage, bombardment, criticism, denunciation|反义词：defense, protection, support, praise|词性:名词
