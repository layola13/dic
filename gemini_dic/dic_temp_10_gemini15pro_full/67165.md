expostulate:/ɪkˈspɒstʃʊleɪt/ /ɛkˈspɑːstʃəleɪt/
verb
抗议；劝诫；告诫；责难
express strong disapproval or disagreement of something, usually to try to persuade someone not to do something.
例句：I expostulated with her about the dangers of driving too fast. 我劝诫她开车太快很危险。

例句：He expostulated with the referee about the unfair decision. 他向裁判抗议不公平的判决。

近义词：remonstrate, protest, object, argue, reason with
反义词：agree, approve, condone, support
词性：verb
