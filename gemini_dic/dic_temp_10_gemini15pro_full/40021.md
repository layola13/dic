consumer-friendliness:/kənˈsuːmər ˈfrɛndlinəs/| n.|用户友好性；消费者友好度|The degree to which a product, service, or system is easy to use and understand by consumers.|This new software boasts improved consumer-friendliness, making it accessible to a wider audience. 这个新软件以改进的用户友好性为荣，使其能够被更广泛的受众使用。|Synonyms: user-friendliness, accessibility, usability, ease of use|Antonyms: user-unfriendliness, inaccessibility, complexity, difficulty|noun
