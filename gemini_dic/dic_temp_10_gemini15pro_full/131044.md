nonavoidablenesses:/ˌnɒnəˈvɔɪdəblnəsɪz/
noun
不可避免性
The state or quality of being unavoidable; inevitability.
The nonavoidablenesses of aging are something we all must face.  我们都必须面对衰老的不可避免性。
War brought with it the nonavoidablenesses of suffering and loss. 战争带来了不可避免的苦难和损失。
近义词:inevitability, inescapability, unavoidability
反义词:avoidability, preventability
词性:noun

Etymology:
From non- (not) + avoidable + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality).  Avoidable is from avoid + -able. Avoid ultimately comes from Old French esvuidier.
