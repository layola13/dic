embeddedness:/ɪmˈbɛdɪdnəs/| n.|嵌入性，根植性|The state of being embedded or deeply involved in something, especially a social or cultural context.  It refers to the degree to which economic activity is constrained by non-economic institutions.|例句：The embeddedness of the company in the local community is a key factor in its success.  该公司在当地社区的根植性是其成功的关键因素。

例句：The study explored the embeddedness of social capital in rural communities.  这项研究探讨了农村社区中社会资本的嵌入性。|近义词：integration, rootedness, involvement|反义词: detachment, isolation|词性:noun
