lurry:/ˈlɜːri/ (UK), /ˈlʌri/ (US)| n. |卡车，货车|A large, heavy motor vehicle for transporting goods; a truck.|The lorry driver was delivering goods to the supermarket. 卡车司机正在向超市运送货物。|The lorry was involved in a serious accident on the motorway. 这辆卡车在高速公路上卷入了一起严重事故。|近义词：truck, articulated lorry, semi-trailer|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑car, van等小型车辆) |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb 'lurry' (meaning to haul or carry), which is of uncertain origin, possibly related to dialectal 'lur' (to drag or pull).  First recorded in the early 20th century.
