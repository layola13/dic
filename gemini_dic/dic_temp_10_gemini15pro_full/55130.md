dissatisfactions: /dɪsˌsætɪsˈfækʃənz/
n.
不满，不满意；令人不满的事情
lack of satisfaction; things that cause one to be dissatisfied
The survey revealed widespread dissatisfactions among customers.  这项调查揭示了顾客普遍存在的不满情绪。
The employees voiced their dissatisfactions with the new management policies. 员工表达了他们对新管理政策的不满。
近义词: grievances, complaints, discontents
反义词: satisfactions, contentment, pleasure
词性: 名词 (plural noun)
