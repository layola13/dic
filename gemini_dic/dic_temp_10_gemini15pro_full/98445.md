inspeximus:/ɪnspɛkˈsiːməs/
noun
审查副本；经核实的副本
a certified copy of a document, especially a charter, after official examination.
The king issued an inspeximus of the town's charter. 国王发布了该镇宪章的经核实的副本。
synonyms: certified copy, authenticated copy, verified copy
antonyms:  forged copy, fake copy
n.
