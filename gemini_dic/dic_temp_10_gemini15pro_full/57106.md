doupe:/duːp/| n.|替身；复制品；翻版；（尤指电影中的）特技替身演员|A doupe is a stand-in, a copy, or a duplicate, especially a stunt double in a film.|The director used a doupe for the dangerous scenes.|近义词：duplicate, copy, stand-in, replica, substitute|反义词:original|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *doupe*, from Old French *double*, meaning "double". Ultimately from Latin *duplus*.
