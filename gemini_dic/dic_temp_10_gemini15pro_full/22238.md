bonder:/ˈbɒndər/ (UK) /ˈbɑːndər/ (US)| n.| 粘合剂; 键合剂;  保税仓库经营者;  建立关系的人|A person or thing that bonds or causes bonding.  A substance used to create a bond between materials.|The bonder used in the construction ensured the stability of the structure.  建筑中使用的粘合剂确保了结构的稳定性。|Synonyms: adhesive, binder, cement, glue, joiner, linker, uniter|Antonyms:  separator, divider|noun
