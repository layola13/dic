hastelessness:/ˈheɪstləsnəs/
n.
从容；不慌不忙；沉着
The state of being not hasty; lack of haste.
He approached the task with hastelessness and precision.  他从容不迫且精确地处理这项任务。
近义词: calmness, composure, deliberateness, patience, serenity
反义词: haste, hurry, rush, impatience
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From haste + -less + -ness.  Haste comes from Middle English *hast, from Old Norse *hastr, from Proto-Germanic *haifstiz.  The suffix -less indicates lack of something, and -ness forms a noun from an adjective.
