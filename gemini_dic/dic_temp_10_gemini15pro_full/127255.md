Navajos:/ˈnævəˌhoʊz/
n.
纳瓦霍人
A member of a Native American people of the southwestern United States, especially Arizona, New Mexico, and Utah.
The Navajos have a rich cultural heritage. 纳瓦霍人拥有丰富的文化遗产。
The Navajo Nation is the largest Native American reservation in the United States. 纳瓦霍族是美国最大的印第安人保留地。
Synonyms: Navajo people, Diné (Diné is the Navajo autonym)
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a people group)
