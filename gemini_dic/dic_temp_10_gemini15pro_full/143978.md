outrages:/ˈaʊtreɪdʒɪz/
n.|暴行，恶行，愤慨|Acts of extreme violence or viciousness; a feeling of righteous anger.|The outrages committed during the war will never be forgotten.  战争期间犯下的暴行将永远不会被忘记。| atrocities, abuses, offenses | kindness, compassion |noun
outrage:/ˈaʊtreɪdʒ/
n.|愤怒，义愤，暴行|An act that violates accepted standards of behavior; extreme annoyance.|The public outrage over the scandal led to his resignation.  公众对丑闻的愤怒导致了他的辞职。|fury, indignation, wrath|calmness, peace|noun
outrage:/aʊtˈreɪdʒ/
