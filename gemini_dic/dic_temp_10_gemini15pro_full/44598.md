crowbar:/ˈkroʊbɑːr/ (US), /ˈkroʊbɑː/ (UK)| n.|撬棍; 铁棍|A heavy iron bar with a flattened, slightly curved end, used as a lever.|He used a crowbar to force the door open. 他用撬棍把门撬开了。|
Synonyms: lever, pry bar, wrecking bar|
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical tool. Could consider "key" if the crowbar is used to force a lock, but context-dependent.)|
