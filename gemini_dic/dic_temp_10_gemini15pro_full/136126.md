nonrefinements:/nɒn.rɪˈfaɪn.mənts/ | n. | 未精炼物，粗糙之处，未改进之处 |  Lack of refinements; things that are crude, unpolished, or not improved.  It refers to aspects that are basic, simple, or lacking sophistication. | The nonrefinements of the early prototype were obvious, but we were more focused on functionality. 早期原型的粗糙之处很明显，但我们更关注功能性。 |  The apartment, while functional, was full of nonrefinements. 这套公寓虽然实用，但到处都是未改进的地方。 | synonyms:  crudities, imperfections, flaws, shortcomings, deficiencies | antonyms: refinements, improvements, enhancements, perfections, polish | noun
