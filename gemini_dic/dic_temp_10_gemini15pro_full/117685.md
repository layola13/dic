meitnerium:/maɪtˈnɪəriəm/
noun
钅麦特纳
a synthetic radioactive element, atomic number 109.
Meitnerium is a highly radioactive, synthetic element. 钅麦特纳是一种高放射性合成元素。
Meitnerium has no known stable isotopes. 钅麦特纳没有已知的稳定同位素。
synonym: element 109 (Mt)
antonym:  (No direct antonym for a chemical element)
