outlabor:/ˌaʊtˈleɪbər/
verb
胜过工作；通过更努力工作胜过
To surpass or defeat someone by working harder or longer.
The team outlabored their opponents and secured a victory.  这个团队比他们的对手工作更努力，取得了胜利。
The factory workers outlabored the machines, producing more goods than predicted. 工厂工人比机器工作更努力，生产的货物比预期多。
近义词: outwork, outperform
反义词: underperform
词性: v.
