overclaims:/ˈoʊvərˌkleɪmz/ | v. | 过度声称；夸大其词 | To state or assert something excessively or beyond what is justified or true. | The company overclaimed the effectiveness of their new product.  这家公司夸大了他们新产品的功效。|  The politician overclaimed his accomplishments during the debate.  这位政治家在辩论中夸大了自己的成就。 | exaggerate, overstate | understate | verb

overclaims:/ˈoʊvərˌkleɪmz/ | n. | 过度声称；夸大之词  |  An exaggerated or excessive claim. | The advertisement was full of overclaims about the product's benefits.  这则广告充满了关于产品功效的夸大之词。|  The investigation revealed several overclaims made by the company. 调查揭示了该公司做出的几项过度声称。 | exaggeration, overstatement | understatement | noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much") + "claim" (verb meaning "to state or assert that something is the case, typically without providing evidence or proof").
