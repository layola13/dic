clothier:/ˈkloʊðiər/
n.
服装商，成衣商
A person who makes, sells, or deals in clothes.
My grandfather was a clothier, and he made the most beautiful coats. 我祖父是一位服装商，他做的外套非常漂亮。
近义词：garment maker, outfitter, tailor, dressmaker
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


词源:
From Middle English *clothyer, from Anglo-Norman clothier, from Old Northern French *clotier, from cloth + -ier.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *klaþiz.
