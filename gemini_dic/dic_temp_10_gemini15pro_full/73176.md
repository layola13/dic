forbidding:/fərˈbɪdɪŋ/ | adj. | 禁止的，令人生畏的，险恶的 | Inspiring fear or awe; daunting.  Unfriendly or threatening in appearance. |  The old house had a forbidding appearance.  那栋老房子外观令人生畏。| synonyms: daunting, ominous, threatening, hostile, austere, uninviting | antonyms: welcoming, inviting, friendly, approachable, encouraging | adjective

forbidding:/fərˈbɪdɪŋ/ | adj. |  禁止的 | Not allowed; prohibited.  |  Smoking is forbidding in this area.  这个区域禁止吸烟。| synonyms: prohibited, forbidden, banned, outlawed, proscribed | antonyms: permitted, allowed, authorized, sanctioned | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *forbeden*, from Old English *forbēodan* ("to forbid, prohibit, command against, renounce"), from *for-* (prefix indicating prohibition or destruction) + *bēodan* ("to command, offer"). Cognate with German *verbieten* ("to forbid"), Dutch *verbieden* ("to forbid").
