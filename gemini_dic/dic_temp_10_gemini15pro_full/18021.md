beginning:/bɪˈɡɪnɪŋ/ /bɪˈɡɪnɪŋ/
noun|开始，开端，起源|The beginning of the story is rather slow. 故事的开头相当慢。
verb (begin的现在分词)|开始，着手|He is beginning to learn Chinese. 他开始学习中文。
adjective|初始的，开始的|The beginning stage of a project is crucial. 项目的初始阶段至关重要。


Etymology->
From Middle English beginninge, from Old English beginnung, equivalent to begin +‎ -ing.

Synonyms->
start, commencement, outset, inception, origin, source

Antonyms->
end, conclusion, finish, termination
