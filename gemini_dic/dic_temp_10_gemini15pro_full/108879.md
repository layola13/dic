lectors:/ˈlektərz/| n.|讲师，朗读者，大学讲师|A person who gives lectures, especially in a university setting; someone who reads aloud, especially for a recording or broadcast.|The lectors at the university are highly qualified. 大学的讲师素质很高。|The lectors recorded the audiobook in a professional studio. 朗读者们在一个专业的录音棚里录制了有声读物。|synonyms: readers, speakers, instructors, teachers|antonyms: students, listeners, audience|noun
