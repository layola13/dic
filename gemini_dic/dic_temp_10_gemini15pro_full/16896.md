basipodite:/ˈbeɪsɪpəˌdaɪt/| n. |基节；底肢节|The basipodite is the segment of an arthropod limb that attaches to the body.|The crab’s basipodite was injured.  螃蟹的基节受伤了。|近义词:coxa|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, potentially the distal segments like dactyl or propodus could be considered functionally opposite) |词性:noun

Etymology:
From  *basi- (“base”) +‎ *pod- (“foot”) +‎ *-ite (“part of”). Ultimately from Ancient Greek βάσις (básis, “base”) +‎ πούς (poús, “foot”).
