adjective
瑞士的；瑞士人的；赫尔维氐人的
Relating to Switzerland, its people, or the Helvetii (an ancient Celtic tribe that inhabited the region).
Switzerland is known for its Helvetian scenery. 瑞士以其瑞士风光而闻名。
