amphictyonic:/æmˈfɪktɪˌɒnɪk/ | adj. | 两栖的，同盟的 |  Relating to an amphictyony, an ancient Greek association of neighboring states for the purpose of protecting a religious shrine or for other common purposes.  Derived from Greek "amphiktyones," meaning "dwellers around."  Refers to both the physical proximity and the shared religious or political obligations. |  The amphictyonic council met annually to discuss matters of common interest.  两栖同盟委员会每年开会讨论共同关心的问题。 |  The Delphic Amphictyony was one of the most powerful in ancient Greece.  德尔斐同盟是古希腊最强大的同盟之一。 |  allied, confederate, federal |  independent, separate | adjective
