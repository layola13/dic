cajeput: /ˈkædʒəpʊt/ | n. |白千层；白千层油|A tall East Indian tree (Melaleuca leucadendra) of the myrtle family, yielding a pungent, medicinal oil.  The oil from this tree, used medicinally and as an insecticide. |The cajeput tree is native to Southeast Asia. 白千层树原产于东南亚。

The cajeput oil has a strong camphoraceous odor. 白千层油有一种强烈的樟脑气味。
