fakirism:/fəˈkɪərɪzəm/ | n. | 苦行僧主义; 禁欲主义 | The practice of a fakir; extreme asceticism.  A lifestyle characterized by self-denial and poverty, often for religious or spiritual purposes. |  He practiced fakirism, sleeping on a bed of nails. 他奉行苦行僧主义，睡在钉床上。|  Gandhi's simple life was seen by some as a form of fakirism. 有些人认为甘地的简朴生活是一种苦行僧主义。| synonyms: asceticism, austerity, self-denial | antonyms: hedonism, luxury, indulgence | noun
