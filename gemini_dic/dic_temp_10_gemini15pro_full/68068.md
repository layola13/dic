faddies: /ˈfædiz/
Noun
时尚；一时流行的狂热；爱好
Fads, short-lived enthusiasms or trends, often capture the public's attention but quickly fade away.  时尚，短暂的热情或潮流，通常会吸引公众的注意，但很快就会消失。
The latest faddies are all about sustainable living. 最新的时尚都是关于可持续生活的。
Synonyms: trends, crazes, fashions
Antonyms: traditions, classics
n.
