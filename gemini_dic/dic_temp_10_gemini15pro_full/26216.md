butterfats:/ˈbʌtərˌfæts/ | n. | 乳脂，奶油脂肪 | The natural fatty constituents of milk, present as an emulsion of globules. Butter is made by churning cream to separate the butterfats from the buttermilk. | Butterfats give butter its rich flavor and texture.  乳脂赋予黄油浓郁的风味和质地。| Butter is almost entirely made of butterfats. 黄油几乎完全由乳脂制成。 | synonyms: milk fat, butter oil, dairy fat | antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps low-fat milk) | noun
