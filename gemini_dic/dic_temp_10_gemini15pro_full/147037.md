overlavishly: /ˌoʊvərˈlævɪʃli/
adverb
过度奢侈地；过分浪费地
in an overlavishly generous or extravagant manner.
He entertained his guests overlavishly. 他过度奢侈地款待客人。
近义词: extravagantly, profusely, excessively
反义词: sparingly, frugally, moderately
词性: 副词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "lavish" (adjective meaning "very generous or extravagant") + "-ly" (adverb suffix).  "Lavish" comes from Old French "lavasse" meaning "deluge, downpour," suggesting an outpouring of generosity or extravagance.
