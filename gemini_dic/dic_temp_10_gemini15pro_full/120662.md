misact: /mɪsˈækt/
verb
行为不当；做错事
To behave badly or illegally; to commit a wrongdoing.
He misacted by revealing confidential information. 他因泄露机密信息而行为不当。
The company's CEO misacted and was forced to resign. 该公司首席执行官行为不端，被迫辞职。
Synonyms: misbehave, transgress, err, offend
Antonyms: behave, comply, obey
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- + act.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "wrong" or "badly". "Act" comes from the Latin "actus", meaning "a doing, a driving, an impulse."
