hydrous:/ˈhaɪdrəs/| adj.|含水的，水合的|Containing water, especially water of crystallization.|例句：Hydrous minerals are those that contain water in their structure. 含水矿物是指在其结构中含有水的矿物。

例句：Hydrous copper sulfate is blue. 水合硫酸铜是蓝色的。|近义词：hydrated, aqueous|反义词：anhydrous|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *hydōr* (water) + *-ous* (adjective suffix).
