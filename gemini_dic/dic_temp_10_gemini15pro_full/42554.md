countermyths:/ˈkaʊntərˌmɪθs/| n.|反驳迷思；揭穿谬误；|Debunking common countermyths about nutrition is crucial for public health.  揭穿关于营养的常见谬误对公众健康至关重要。|Synonyms: debunking, refutation, exposing, challenging|Antonyms: perpetuating, promoting, supporting|noun
Etymology:  "counter-" (against) + "myths" (traditional stories, especially ones explaining some natural or social phenomenon, and typically involving supernatural beings or events).  "Counter-" comes from Old French "contre" (against), ultimately from Latin "contra". "Myth" comes from Greek "mythos" (story, tale).|
