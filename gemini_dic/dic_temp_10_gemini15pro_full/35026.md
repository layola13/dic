clootie:/ˈkluːti/| n.|破布；魔鬼；左脚；（苏格兰）布丁|A clootie is a piece of cloth, especially a rag or an old piece of clothing.  In Scottish folklore, it can also refer to the Devil.  It can also be a term for a left foot, or a type of steamed pudding (Clootie Dumpling) made in a cloth.|例句：The child clung to his clootie. 孩子紧紧抓住他的破布。

例句：The clootie is said to roam the moors at night.  据说魔鬼在夜间漫游荒野。|近义词：rag, devil, Satan, (for pudding) dumpling|反义词: angel (for devil)|词性:noun
