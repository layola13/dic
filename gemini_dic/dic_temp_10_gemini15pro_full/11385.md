what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US)|pron. det. adv.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information specifying something.|例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

例句：What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！|近义词：which|反义词: N/A|pronoun

what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US)|det.|什么，哪一个，哪一些|Used with nouns to ask for information specifying something or someone.|例句：What book are you reading? 你在读什么书？

例句：What colors do you like? 你喜欢什么颜色？|近义词：which|反义词:N/A|determiner

what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US)|adv.|多么，何等，在…方面|Used to emphasize the degree or extent of something.|例句：What a mess! 多么乱啊！
