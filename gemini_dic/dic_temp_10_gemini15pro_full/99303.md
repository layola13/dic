intercounty:/ˌɪntərˈkaʊnti/| adj.|郡际的，跨郡的|Existing or taking place between counties.|The intercounty hurling championship final drew a huge crowd. 郡际投球锦标赛决赛吸引了大批观众。|近义词：cross-county|反义词：intracounty|词性:形容词

Etymology:
"inter-" + "county".  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among", derived from Latin. "County" comes from Old French "conté," meaning "jurisdiction of a count," ultimately from Latin "comitatus".
