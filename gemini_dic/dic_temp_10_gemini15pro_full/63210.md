enteradenography: /ˌɛntərˌædɪˈnɒɡrəfi/ | n. |肠腺造影术| The radiographic visualization of the glands of the intestines. | Enteradenography was performed to evaluate the small bowel. 肠腺造影术被用来评估小肠。| 近义词:  small bowel series | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

词源:  "enter-" (from Greek "enteron," intestine) + "adeno-" (from Greek "aden," gland) + "-graphy" (from Greek "graphein," to write).
