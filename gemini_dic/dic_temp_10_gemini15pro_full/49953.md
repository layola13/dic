denegation:/ˌdɛnɪˈɡeɪʃən/
|n.|否认，否定，拒绝|The defendant's denegation of the charges was unconvincing.  被告对指控的否认 unconvincing。|
|例句：His denegation of responsibility angered the victims. 他推卸责任激怒了受害者。|
|例句：The government issued a denegation of the rumors of a coup. 政府发表声明否认了有关政变的谣言。|
|近义词：denial, repudiation, refutation, contradiction|
|反义词：admission, acceptance, affirmation, confirmation|
|词性：名词|

词源：
From Late Latin *denegationem, accusative of denegatio (“denial”), from the past participle stem of denegare (“to deny”), from de- (“not”) + negare (“to deny”).
