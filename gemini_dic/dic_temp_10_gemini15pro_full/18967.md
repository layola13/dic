bestialize:/ˈbɛstiəlaɪz/| vt.|使兽性化；使堕落；使野蛮化|To make bestial; to deprive of human qualities and reduce to the level of a beast. |The dictator's regime bestialized its citizens, stripping them of their rights and dignity. 独裁者的政权使公民兽性化，剥夺了他们的权利和尊严。

The prolonged isolation had bestialized him, making him act more on instinct than reason. 长时间的隔离使他兽性化，使他更多地凭本能而不是理性行事。|近义词：debase, degrade, brutalize, corrupt, demoralize|反义词：humanize, civilize, refine, ennoble, uplift|词性:动词
