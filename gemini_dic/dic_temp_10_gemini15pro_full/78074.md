geodesic:/ˌdʒiːəˈdɛsɪk/ /ˌdʒiːoʊˈdɛsɪk/| n. adj.|测地线，测地线的|A line representing the shortest path between two points on a curved surface, such as the Earth.|例句：The airplane followed a geodesic route to minimize travel time. 飞机沿着测地线飞行以减少旅行时间。

geodesic:/ˌdʒiːəˈdɛsɪk/ /ˌdʒiːoʊˈdɛsɪk/| n. adj.|测地线，测地线的|The geodesic dome is a remarkably strong structure. 测地线穹顶是一种非常坚固的结构。|近义词：shortest path, shortest route|反义词:|noun, adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *geodaisia* "division of the Earth,"  from *ge* "earth" + *daiein* "to divide."
