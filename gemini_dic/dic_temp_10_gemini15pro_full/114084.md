magnificate: /ˈmæɡnɪfɪkeɪt/ | v. | 放大，夸大，赞美 | To make something appear larger or more important than it really is; to praise highly or extol. | He magnified his accomplishments in the interview. 他在面试中夸大了自己的成就。 |  Synonyms: exaggerate, amplify, extol, laud, glorify | Antonyms: minimize, diminish, downplay | verb

magnificate: /ˈmæɡnɪfɪkeɪt/ | adj. (obsolete) | 宏伟的，壮丽的 (古语) | Lofty or impressive in appearance, quality, or execution.  |  The magnificate castle stood atop the hill. 宏伟的城堡矗立在山顶上。| Synonyms: grand, majestic, splendid | Antonyms: humble, modest, unimpressive | adjective
