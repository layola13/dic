dovetail:/ˈdʌvˌteɪl (US), ˈdʌvteɪl (UK)| n. v.|鸠尾榫;吻合;密切配合|A type of joint used in woodworking where two pieces of wood are interlocked by a series of projections shaped like a dove's tail.|The carpenter skillfully dovetailed the two pieces of wood together. 木匠巧妙地将两块木头用鸠尾榫连接在一起。|近义词：interlock, mesh, coincide, harmonize|反义词：clash, conflict|词性: 名词, 动词

dovetail:/ˈdʌvˌteɪl (US), ˈdʌvteɪl (UK)| v.|吻合;密切配合|To fit or cause to fit together closely or harmoniously.|Our plans dovetailed perfectly with theirs. 我们的计划与他们的计划完美吻合。|近义词：interlock, mesh, coincide, harmonize|反义词：clash, conflict|词性: 动词
