foundationless:/faʊnˈdeɪʃənləs/
adj.
无基础的，没有根据的
Lacking a foundation; groundless; unsubstantiated.
His argument was completely foundationless and based on mere speculation. 他的论点完全没有根据，仅仅基于推测。
近义词: groundless, baseless, unsubstantiated, unfounded
反义词: founded, grounded, substantiated, well-founded
词性: 形容词
