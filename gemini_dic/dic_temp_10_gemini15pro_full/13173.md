at-best:/æt bɛst/| adverbial phrase | 至多；充其量 |  Used to indicate that something is only just good enough, or only true or possible in the best circumstances. | The food was, at best, mediocre. 这食物最多只能说是平庸。 | Synonyms: at most, not more than, in the best-case scenario | Antonyms: at worst, at least | phrase
