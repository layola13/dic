fumarolic:/ˌfjuːməˈrɒlɪk/ /ˌfjuːməˈrɑːlɪk/
adjective
喷气的；火山喷气孔的
Relating to or resembling a fumarole; produced by a fumarole.  A fumarole is an opening in or near a volcano, through which hot sulfurous gases emerge.
例句：The fumarolic activity increased dramatically before the eruption. 火山爆发前，喷气活动显著增加。

例句：Scientists studied the fumarolic gases to predict volcanic activity. 科学家们研究喷气孔的气体来预测火山活动。

近义词：volcanic, geothermal
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective
词源: From Italian *fumarola*, from fumare "to smoke," from Latin fumus "smoke."
