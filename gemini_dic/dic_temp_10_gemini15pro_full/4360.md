n.|雪花石膏; 雪花石膏制品|A finely granular variety of gypsum, often white and translucent, used for carving ornaments.|The artist carved a delicate flower from alabaster.|近义词:gypsum, marble, onyx|反义词:|名词

adj.|雪花石膏制的; 雪白的|Made of alabaster; smooth and white like alabaster.|Her skin was alabaster and her hair like spun gold.|近义词:pale, white, smooth|反义词:dark, rough|形容词
