evaporate:/ɪˈvæpəreɪt/ | v. |蒸发，挥发；消失，消散|To change from a liquid or solid state into vapor; to disappear; to vanish.|The water in the puddle evaporated in the sun. 水坑里的水在阳光下蒸发了。|Synonyms: vaporize, disappear, vanish, dissipate |Antonyms: condense, materialize, appear|verb

evaporation:/ɪˌvæpəˈreɪʃn/ | n. |蒸发，挥发；消失，消散|The process of changing from a liquid or solid state into vapor.|The evaporation of water from the lake helps to cool the surrounding area. 湖水的蒸发有助于冷却周围地区。|Synonyms: vaporization, disappearance |Antonyms: condensation, materialization|noun
