例句：The witch cast a nefarious spell on the princess.  女巫对公主施放了邪恶的咒语。


近义词：baleful, sinister, evil, harmful, pernicious, inauspicious|反义词:beneficial, auspicious, favorable|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *nefastus (“inauspicious, unlucky”), from ne- (“not”) + fāstus (“lawful, permitted by divine law; of good omen”).
