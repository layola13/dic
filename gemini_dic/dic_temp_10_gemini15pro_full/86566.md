helicoid:/ˈhɛlɪkɔɪd/| n. adj.|螺旋面；螺旋形的|A helicoid is a three-dimensional surface shaped like a spiral or a twisted ramp. As an adjective, it describes something having this shape.|
The museum's staircase was a graceful helicoid.  博物馆的楼梯是一个优美的螺旋面。
The DNA molecule has a helicoid structure. DNA分子具有螺旋结构。|
近义词：spiral, helical|反义词: - |n. adj.
