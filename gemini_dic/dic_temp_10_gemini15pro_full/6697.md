analgesia:/ˌænəlˈdʒiːziə/ /ˌænəlˈdʒiːʒə/
noun
无痛法；镇痛；痛觉缺失
Absence of pain sensation.
The doctor administered analgesia before the surgery. 医生在手术前进行了镇痛。
analgesic, pain relief
pain, hyperalgesia
n.

analgesias:/ˌænəlˈdʒiːziəz/ /ˌænəlˈdʒiːʒəz/
noun
无痛法；镇痛；痛觉缺失（analgesia的复数形式）
The plural form of analgesia, referring to multiple instances or types of pain relief.
The patient was given two different analgesias to manage post-operative pain.  病人被给予两种不同的镇痛药来控制术后疼痛。
analgesics, pain relievers
pains, hyperalgesias
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀν- (an-, “without”) + ἄλγος (álgos, “pain”) + -ia.
