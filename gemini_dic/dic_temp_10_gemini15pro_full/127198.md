naturists:/ˈneɪtʃərɪsts/
n.
自然主义者；裸体主义者
People who believe in naturism and often practice nudism.  Those who advocate a return to nature and natural ways of living.
例句：The naturists enjoyed sunbathing on the secluded beach.  自然主义者们在僻静的海滩上享受日光浴。

例句：Some naturists believe in living life without the constraints of modern society. 一些自然主义者相信过一种没有现代社会约束的生活。

近义词：nudists, naturalists
反义词：
词性：noun
