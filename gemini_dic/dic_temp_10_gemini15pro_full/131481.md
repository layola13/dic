noncoincidentally:/ˌnɒnkəʊˌɪnsɪˈdɛntəli/ /ˌnɑːnkəʊˌɪnsɪˈdɛntəli/
adverb
并非巧合地；不出所料地
not by coincidence; unsurprisingly.  It suggests a strong connection between two events or facts.
Noncoincidentally, the rise in crime coincided with a decrease in police funding.  并非巧合的是，犯罪率的上升与警察经费的减少同时发生。
The best restaurants are, noncoincidentally, often the most expensive. 最好的餐厅通常也是最贵的，这并非巧合。
近义词:unsurprisingly, not accidentally, significantly, meaningfully
反义词:coincidentally, accidentally, by chance
词性:adverb
