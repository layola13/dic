例句：He was involved in a depraved and scandalous affair. 他卷入了一桩堕落且不光彩的韵事。


近义词：corrupt, wicked, evil, degenerate, immoral, perverse|反义词：virtuous, moral, righteous, upright, good|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

From Latin *depravatus*, past participle of *depravare* "to pervert, corrupt," from *de-* "from, down" + *pravus* "crooked, perverse."
