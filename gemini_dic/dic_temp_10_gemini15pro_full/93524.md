imagery:/ˈɪmɪdʒri/| n.|意象，图像，形象|Visually descriptive or figurative language, especially in a literary work. The formation of mental images, figures, or likenesses of things, or of such images collectively.|The author's use of imagery effectively conveyed the desolate atmosphere of the abandoned town. 作者运用形象的语言有效地传达了废弃城镇的荒凉气氛。|synonyms: symbolism, metaphor, figures of speech, mental pictures, visualization|antonyms: literalness, plainness|noun
