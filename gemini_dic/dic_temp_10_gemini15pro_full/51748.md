devolver:/ˌdeɪˈvɒlvər/ (BrE), /dɪˈvɑːlvər/ (AmE)| v. |归还，交还（尤指权力或责任）；移交；退回（商品）|To give back (something, especially power or responsibility) to a lower level or to its original owner; to transfer; to return (merchandise).|例句：The central government devolved power to the regions. 中央政府将权力下放给地方。|例句：I had to devolver the faulty goods to the store for a refund. 我不得不把有缺陷的商品退回商店退款。|近义词：return, restore, transfer, delegate|反义词：retain, centralize, keep|词性:动词
