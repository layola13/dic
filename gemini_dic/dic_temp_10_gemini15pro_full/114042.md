magnetic-dip:/mæɡˈnɛtɪk dɪp/ | n. | 磁倾角 | The angle that a magnetic needle makes with the horizontal plane.  It varies from place to place on the Earth's surface. | The magnetic-dip at the magnetic poles is 90 degrees.  在地磁两极，磁倾角为90度。

The surveyor measured the magnetic-dip to help determine their location. 测量员测量了磁倾角以帮助确定他们的位置。 | 近义词: inclination, dip angle | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but a related concept is magnetic declination) | 词性: noun
