chiastic:/kaɪˈæstɪk/
adjective
交错的，交错配列法的
Relating to or characterized by chiasmus, a rhetorical or literary figure in which words, grammatical constructions, or concepts are repeated in reverse order, in the same or a modified form.
Her speech used a chiastic structure, mirroring the first half in the second. 她的演讲使用了交错配列法，将前半部分反映在后半部分。
The poem’s chiastic arrangement of themes emphasized the cyclical nature of life.  这首诗对主题的交错排列强调了生命的循环性。
synonyms: crisscross, inverted, mirrored, symmetrical
antonyms: linear, sequential
