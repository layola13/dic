morpheme:/ˈmɔːrfiːm/ | n. |词素|A morpheme is the smallest meaningful unit in a language.  It cannot be further divided into smaller meaningful parts. | Example: The word "unbreakable" has three morphemes: "un-", "break", and "-able".  | 同义词: unit, element | 反义词: (No direct antonym exists for morpheme) | 词性: Noun
