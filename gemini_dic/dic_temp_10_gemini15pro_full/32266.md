chid:/tʃaɪd/ | v. | 责骂，斥责 | To scold or rebuke mildly. |  She chided him for his carelessness. 她责备他的粗心大意。

| 近义词: scold, rebuke, reprimand, reproach | 反义词: praise, commend, laud | 词性: 动词

chid:/tʃaɪd/ | n. | 责骂，斥责 | A rebuke or scolding. |  He ignored her chids and continued playing. 他不理会她的责备，继续玩耍。
