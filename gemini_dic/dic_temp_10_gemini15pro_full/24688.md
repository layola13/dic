bronchitis: /brɒŋˈkaɪtɪs/ (British) /brɑːŋˈkaɪtɪs/ (American)
n.
支气管炎
Inflammation of the mucous membrane in the bronchial tubes. It typically causes bronchospasm and coughing.
例句：My grandfather suffers from chronic bronchitis. 我的祖父患有慢性支气管炎。

例句：Smoking is a major cause of bronchitis. 吸烟是支气管炎的主要原因。

近义词：chest infection, respiratory infection
反义词:  (Bronchitis doesn't have a direct antonym.  The opposite of being ill would be healthy, but that doesn't directly relate to the specific condition.)
词性: noun
