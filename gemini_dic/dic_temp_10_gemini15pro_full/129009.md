nirvanist:/nɪrˈvɑːnɪst/ | n. |涅槃主义者，追求涅槃的人 | A person who believes in or seeks nirvana, a state of perfect peace and enlightenment, especially in Buddhism. | He considered himself a nirvanist, dedicating his life to meditation and spiritual practices. 他认为自己是一个涅槃主义者，将毕生精力奉献于冥想和修行。 | 近义词: seeker, ascetic | 反义词: materialist, hedonist | 词性: noun

nirvanistic:/ˌnɪrvəˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. |涅槃的，与涅槃有关的 | Relating to or characteristic of nirvana. |  His nirvanistic pursuits led him to a remote monastery in the Himalayas.  他对涅槃的追求使他来到喜马拉雅山脉的一个偏远寺院。 | 近义词: transcendental, spiritual | 反义词: worldly, materialistic | 词性: adjective
