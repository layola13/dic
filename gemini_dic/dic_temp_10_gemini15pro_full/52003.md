diagonal:/daɪˈæɡənəl/ |adj. n.|对角线的，斜线的；对角线，斜线|A diagonal line or movement goes from one corner to another, not along one of the sides. |例句：Draw a diagonal line across the square. 在正方形上画一条对角线。|近义词：oblique, slanting|反义词：horizontal, vertical|词性:形容词,名词

diagonal:/daɪˈæɡənəl/ |n.|对角线，斜线|A straight line joining two opposite corners of a square, rectangle, or other four-sided figure.|例句：The ball rolled across the diagonal of the field. 球滚过球场的对角线。|近义词：oblique line, slant|反义词：side, edge|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *diagonalis, from Greek diagonios "from angle to angle," from dia- "through, across" + gonia "angle, corner."
