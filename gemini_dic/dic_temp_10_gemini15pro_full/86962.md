hencoop:/ˈhenˌkup/| n. |鸡舍，鸡笼|A small building or enclosure for keeping chickens.|The farmer built a new hencoop for his growing flock of chickens. 农民为日益壮大的鸡群建造了一个新的鸡舍。|近义词：chicken coop, hen house|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑 open field, free range)|词性: noun

词源:
From "hen" + "coop," a cage or enclosed area.  "Hen" comes from Old English "henn," and "coop" comes from Middle Dutch "kupe," meaning a basket or cage.
