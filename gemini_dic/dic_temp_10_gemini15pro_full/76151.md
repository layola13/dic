fuse:/fjuːz/ (US) /fjuːz/ (UK)| n. v.|保险丝; 熔断器; 导火索; 导火线; 融合; 合并|A safety device that protects an electric circuit from becoming overloaded. As a verb, it means to melt or join together.|例句：The fuse blew and the lights went out. 保险丝烧断了，灯灭了。|近义词：melt, combine, blend, merge|反义词:separate, divide|noun, verb

fuse:/fjuːz/ (US) /fjuːz/ (UK)| v.|融合; 合并|To melt or join together.|例句：The two companies fused to form a larger corporation.  两家公司合并成了一家更大的公司。|近义词：melt, combine, blend, merge|反义词:separate, divide|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fūsus, past participle of fundere “to pour, melt”.
