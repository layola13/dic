intensive:/ɪnˈtɛnsɪv/ | adj. | 集中的，密集的，加强的 | Concentrated on a single area or subject or a single activity or skill.  Characterized by intensity; forceful or energetic. |  The doctor recommended an intensive course of physiotherapy. 医生建议进行一个密集的物理治疗疗程。 | synonyms: concentrated, focused, thorough, rigorous, demanding, exhaustive | antonyms: superficial, relaxed, easy, gentle | adjective

intensive:/ɪnˈtɛnsɪv/ | n. | 加强器 | Something that intensifies. | The final week of training was an intensive.  训练的最后一周是强化训练。 | synonyms:  concentrate, booster | antonyms:  dilution, reduction | noun
