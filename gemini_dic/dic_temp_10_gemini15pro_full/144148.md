outshaming:/aʊtˈʃeɪmɪŋ/
|n.|公开羞辱，网络羞辱|The act of publicly shaming someone, especially online, for behavior considered unacceptable or offensive.|Example: Outshaming has become a common tactic used by online communities to police behavior. 网络羞辱已经成为网络社区用来监管行为的常见策略。
|Example:  He faced widespread outshaming after his controversial comments were leaked.  他的争议性言论泄露后，他面临着广泛的网络羞辱。
|近义词: public shaming, online shaming, shaming, humiliation|反义词: praising, commending, celebrating|词性:名词
