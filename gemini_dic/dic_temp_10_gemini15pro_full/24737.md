brood-over:/bruːd ˈoʊvər/| phrasal verb | 沉思；念念不忘；耿耿于怀 | To think and worry about something a lot. | She brooded over the argument all day. 她整天都在为那场争论而耿耿于怀。

|She couldn't stop brooding over her failure. 她无法停止对自己的失败念念不忘。 | 近义词：dwell on, ponder, mull over, obsess over | 反义词：forget, ignore, dismiss | verb phrase
