dissimilarities:/ˌdɪsɪməˈlærɪtiz/
|n.|不同点，差异|The report highlighted the dissimilarities between the two markets. 报告强调了两个市场之间的差异。|The dissimilarities between the twins are striking. 这对双胞胎之间的差异很显著。|近义词：differences, discrepancies, variations|反义词：similarities, resemblances|词性:名词
Etymology: From dis- (not) + similarity (likeness), ultimately from Latin similis (like).
