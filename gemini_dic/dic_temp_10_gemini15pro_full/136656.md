nonsculpturally:/nɑːnˈskʌlptʃərəli/
adjective
非雕塑地，非雕刻地
Not in a sculptural manner; not characterized by sculpture.
The artist chose to express herself nonsculpturally through performance art.  这位艺术家选择通过行为艺术来非雕塑地表达自己。
The design was nonsculpturally flat.  这个设计非雕塑地平坦。
近义词: plainly, simply
反义词: sculpturally

词性: adjective
