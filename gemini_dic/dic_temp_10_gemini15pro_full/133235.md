nonexculpable:/ˌnɑːnɛksˈkʌlpəbl/
adjective
不可原谅的，不能免罪的
Not able to be exculpated; inexcusable.  Freeing someone from blame or guilt.
例句：His actions were deemed nonexculpable due to the severity of the consequences. 他的行为由于后果的严重性被认为是不可原谅的。

例句：The judge found the defendant's excuse nonexculpable and sentenced him to prison. 法官认为被告的辩解不能免除罪责，并判处他监禁。

近义词：inexcusable, unjustifiable, unforgivable
反义词：excusable, justifiable, forgivable
词性：adjective
