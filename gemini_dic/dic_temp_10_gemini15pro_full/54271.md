disgruntled:/dɪsˈɡrʌnt(ə)ld/
adjective
不满的，不高兴的
Feeling or expressing dissatisfaction or resentment.
The disgruntled employees complained about the new company policy.  不满的员工抱怨公司的新政策。
The team was disgruntled after their loss.  球队输球后很不高兴。
近义词:dissatisfied, displeased, discontented, resentful, grumpy
反义词:happy, content, satisfied, pleased
词性:adjective

disgruntled:/dɪsˈɡrʌnt(ə)ld/
词源:From dis- (expressing negation) + gruntle (to grumble, complain), from the frequentative of grunt.  由dis-（表示否定）+ gruntle（抱怨，发牢骚）组成，gruntle 源于 grunt 的重复形式。
