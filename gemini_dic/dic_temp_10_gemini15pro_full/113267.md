lustwort:/ˈlʌstˌwɜːrt/ | n. | 松萝，石蕊 | A type of lichen, especially those formerly used as a dye. It typically grows on rocks and trees and comes in various colors. |  The ancient Egyptians used lustwort to make purple dye for their clothing. 古埃及人用松萝来制作衣服的紫色染料。 |  The hills were covered with gray-green lustwort.  这些山上覆盖着灰绿色的松萝。 | synonyms: orchil, lichen, moss | antonyms: N/A | noun

lustwort:/ˈlʌstˌwɜːrt/ | n. | 顺从草 | A mythical herb believed to incite lust. |  In medieval folklore, lustwort was said to make anyone who consumed it fall madly in love. 在中世纪的民间传说中，据说吃了顺从草的人会疯狂地坠入爱河。 |  He searched the forest for the legendary lustwort, hoping to win the heart of his beloved. 他在森林里寻找传说中的顺从草，希望能赢得他爱人的芳心。 | synonyms: love-in-idleness, love potion | antonyms: N/A | noun
