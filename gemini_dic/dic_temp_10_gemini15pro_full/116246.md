mass-observer:/ˈmæs əbˈzɜːrvər/ | n. | 群众观察者，大众观察员 |  A person who observes the behavior and opinions of the general public, often to understand trends or predict outcomes. | The mass-observer noted a shift in public opinion regarding the upcoming election. 大众观察员注意到公众对即将举行的选举的看法发生了转变。| The mass-observer predicted the product would be a success based on social media trends. 大众观察员根据社交媒体趋势预测该产品将会成功。 | synonyms: public opinion analyst, social researcher, trend spotter | antonyms:  N/A (lack of a direct antonym; perhaps "insider" or "elite" could be considered conceptually opposite) | noun
