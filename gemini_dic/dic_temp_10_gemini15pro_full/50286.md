depictors:/dɪˈpɪktərz/| n.| 描绘者，描述者 |People or things that depict something; those who represent or portray something. |The cave paintings are silent yet powerful depictors of prehistoric life. 洞穴壁画是史前生活的无声而有力的描绘者。|  The artists were skilled depictors of the natural world. 这些艺术家是自然世界的熟练描绘者。| synonyms:  portrayers, illustrators, representers, artists, painters, describers | antonyms:  obscurers, distorters, misrepresenters | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "depict" + "-or" (suffix forming nouns denoting an agent or person performing the action).  "Depict" comes from the Latin "depingere," from "de-" (down, completely) + "pingere" (to paint).
