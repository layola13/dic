homotaxic:/ˌhɒməˈtæksɪk/| adj.|同层的，水平相等的，同轴的|Having the same taxonomic arrangement or occurring on the same taxonomic level.  Referring to geological strata or layers that are of the same age and are correlated across different geographic locations.|The homotaxic layers of rock provided evidence of a simultaneous volcanic eruption across the region.|近义词：isochronous, contemporaneous|反义词：heterochronous|词性: adjective

Etymology:  From Greek *homos* (same) + *taxis* (arrangement, order).
