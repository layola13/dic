adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From aconitine + -ine.  Aconitine itself derives from Aconitum, the genus name of the plant monkshood, from which the alkaloid is derived.  The ultimate origin is likely Greek *akóniton, of uncertain etymology, possibly related to akónē (“whetstone”).
