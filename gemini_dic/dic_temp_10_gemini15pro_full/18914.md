besot:/bɪˈsɒt/ (UK) /bɪˈsɑːt/ (US)| vt. |使沉醉；使迷恋；使糊涂|To intoxicate or infatuate; to obsess or make foolish, typically with alcohol or love.|He was completely besotted with her. 他完全被她迷住了。|Synonyms: infatuate, intoxicate, captivate, enamor|Antonyms: disenchant, sober|Verb

besot:/bɪˈsɒt/ (UK) /bɪˈsɑːt/ (US)| adj. |(古)沉醉的，迷恋的|(Archaic) Intoxicated; infatuated.|He gave her a besotted look. 他痴迷地看着她。|Synonyms: infatuated, intoxicated, smitten|Antonyms: sober, clear-headed|Adjective
