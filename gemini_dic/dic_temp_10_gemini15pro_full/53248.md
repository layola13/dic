directress:/daɪˈrɛktrɪs/ /dəˈrɛktrɪs/
n.
女主管，女校长，女导演
A woman who directs or manages something, especially a school or other institution.
The directress of the orphanage was a kind and compassionate woman. 孤儿院的女院长是一位善良而富有同情心的女人。
The directress guided the actors through the rehearsal. 女导演指导演员们进行排练。

synonyms: director, manager, administrator, superintendent, principal, headmistress
antonyms: subordinate, employee, follower
