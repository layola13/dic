hanger-ons:/ˈhæŋər ɒnz/ | n. | 奉承者，追随者，食客 | People who associate with someone else, especially someone rich or famous, in the hope of gaining some advantage. |  He has a lot of hanger-ons who are hoping to benefit from his success. 他有很多追随者，希望从他的成功中获益。

| Example:  She attracted a lot of hanger-ons when she became a famous actress. 她成为著名演员后吸引了许多追随者。

| Synonyms: followers, sycophants, parasites, leechers, groupies | Antonyms: leaders, independents | Noun
