darnel:/ˈdɑːrnəl/ | n. |毒麦，稗子|A common weedy annual grass (Lolium temulentum) that grows as a weed among cereal crops. It bears poisonous seeds that can contaminate flour and cause illness if consumed.| The farmer was concerned about the darnel growing in his wheat field.  农民担心他的麦田里长着毒麦。| Synonyms: tares, ryegrass, weed | Antonyms: crop, grain | noun
