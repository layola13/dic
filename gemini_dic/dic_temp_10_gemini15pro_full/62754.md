engorge:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒ/ /ɛnˈɡɔːrdʒ/
|v.|使充盈；使充血；狼吞虎咽|to cause to swell with blood, water, or another fluid; to eat greedily.|The mosquito engorged itself with blood.  蚊子吸饱了血。|近义词：gorge, stuff, cram, fill, bloat|反义词：drain, empty, deplete|verb

engorger:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒər/ /ɛnˈɡɔːrdʒər/
|n.|暴食者；充盈物|One who, or that which, engorges.|The python, an engorger of large prey, swallowed the rabbit whole. 巨蟒，一个吞食大型猎物的动物，把兔子整个吞了下去。|近义词：glutton, gourmand|反义词：dieter|noun
