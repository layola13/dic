mini-industry:/ˈmɪni ˈɪndəstri/| n. |小型产业，微型产业|A small-scale industry, often involving specialized products or services.|The town's mini-industry of handcrafted jewelry attracts many tourists. 这个镇的手工珠宝小型产业吸引了许多游客。|Example: The rise of online platforms has facilitated the growth of mini-industries focused on niche products.  在线平台的兴起促进了专注于利基产品的小型产业的增长。|Synonyms: small business, cottage industry, micro-enterprise|Antonyms: large corporation, conglomerate, multinational|noun
