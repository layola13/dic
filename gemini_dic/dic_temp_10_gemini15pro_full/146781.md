overidentified:/ˌoʊvər aɪˈdɛntɪfaɪd/
adjective
过度认同的
Excessively identifying with someone or something, often to an unhealthy degree.  This term is frequently used in psychology.
例句：The young boy was overidentified with his father, mimicking his every mannerism.  这个小男孩过度认同他的父亲，模仿他的一举一动。

例句：She was so overidentified with her company that she felt lost when she was laid off. 她对公司过度认同，以至于被解雇后感到迷失。

近义词：infatuated, obsessed, fixated
反义词：detached, indifferent, apathetic
词性：adjective
