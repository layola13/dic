paludism: /pəˈluːdɪzəm/ /pəˈljuːdɪzəm/
n.
疟疾
A disease caused by a parasite that is transmitted by the bite of infected mosquitoes.  It typically causes recurring bouts of fever and shivering.
例句：Paludism is a serious health problem in many tropical countries. 疟疾是许多热带国家的严重健康问题。

例句：The doctor diagnosed her with paludism after she returned from Africa.  她从非洲回来后，医生诊断她患有疟疾。

近义词：malaria
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a disease.  One could say "health" as the opposite state, but this is not a true antonym.)
词性: noun
