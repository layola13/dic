excerptor:/ɪkˈsɜːptər/ /ekˈsɜːrptər/
n.
摘录者，选段者
A person who excerpts something, especially a literary work.
The excerptor chose the most poignant passages from the novel for the anthology.  摘录者从小说中挑选了最感人的段落，用于选集。
extractor, compiler, selector
