contextualized:/kənˈtekstʃuəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 语境化的，置于语境中的 | Placed or studied in context.  Relating to, or determined by, context.  | The meaning of the word is contextualized by the surrounding sentences. 这个词的意思是由周围的句子语境化的。 | contextual, situated, embedded | decontextualized | adjective

contextualized:/kənˈtekstʃuəlaɪzd/ | v. | 语境化，置于语境中 | To place (something, such as a word or activity) in a context. | The teacher contextualized the historical event by discussing its social and political background. 老师通过讨论其社会和政治背景，将这一历史事件置于语境之中。| contextualize, place in context, situate | decontextualize | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From context + -ual + -ize. Context comes from Latin contextus, meaning "a joining together."
