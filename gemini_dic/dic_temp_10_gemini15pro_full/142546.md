ostensible:/ɒˈstensɪbl/ /ɑːˈstensəbl/ | adj. | 表面的，貌似真实的 | Appearing or claiming to be one thing when it is really something else. |  例句：The ostensible reason for his visit was to see his family, but he was really there on business. 他来访的表面原因是看望家人，但实际上他是来出差的。

例句：Her ostensible cheerfulness concealed a deep sadness. 她表面上的快乐掩饰着内心的深深悲伤。

近义词：apparent, seeming, alleged, professed | 反义词：actual, real, genuine, true | 词性：adjective
