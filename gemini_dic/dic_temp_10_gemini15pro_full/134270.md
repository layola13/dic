noninfluentially:/ˌnɑːnɪnfluˈɛnʃəli/
| adv. | 无影响地；没有影响地 | Without influence; in a way that does not exert influence. |  The committee's decision was reached noninfluentially, based solely on the facts presented. 委员会的决定是无偏见地做出的，完全基于所提出的事实。|
| The external pressure acted noninfluentially on the judge’s verdict. 外界压力并没有影响法官的判决。 |
| neutrally, impartially, objectively |
