lychnoscope:/ˈlɪknəˌskoʊp/| n. |窥视孔，小观察窗|A small window in a church wall, often splayed inwards, enabling a person outside to see the altar or the elevation of the Host.|例句：The leper could view the mass through the lychnoscope. 麻风病人可以通过窥视孔观看弥撒。

例句：The lychnoscope allowed those who were unable to enter the church to still participate in the service. 窥视孔允许那些无法进入教堂的人仍然可以参加礼拜。|近义词：squint, hagioscope|反义词:|名词
