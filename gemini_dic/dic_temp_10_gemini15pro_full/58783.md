dunnock:/ˈdʌnək/ | n. | 雀科鸟类，篱雀 | A small, brown European bird of the accentor family, Prunella modularis, found in gardens and hedges. |  I saw a dunnock foraging for insects in the garden. 我看到一只篱雀在花园里觅食昆虫。| synonym: hedge sparrow, hedge accentor | antonym: N/A | noun

dunnock:/ˈdʌnək/ | n. | 笨蛋，傻瓜 (informal, less common) | A foolish or stupid person. | He's a bit of a dunnock, isn't he?  他有点傻，不是吗？ | synonym: fool, idiot, simpleton | antonym: genius, intellectual | noun
