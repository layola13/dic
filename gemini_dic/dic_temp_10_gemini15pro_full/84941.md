hanukkahs:/ˈhɑːnəkəz/ |n.|光明节（复数）|The plural form of Hanukkah, referring to multiple occurrences or celebrations of the Jewish festival of lights.  It is less common than simply using "Hanukkahs" to refer to multiple years in which Hanukkah was celebrated.| We celebrated three Hanukkahs together as a family. 我们一起庆祝了三个光明节。| The family exchanged gifts during all eight nights of both Hanukkahs. 这家人在两个光明节的八个晚上都交换了礼物。| synonym:Festival of Lights (singular),  Chanukah, Feast of Dedication| antonym: N/A | noun
