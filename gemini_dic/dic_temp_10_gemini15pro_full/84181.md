half-questioningly:/ˌhɑːfˈkwɛstʃənɪŋli/
adverb|半疑问地，略带疑问地|In a manner that suggests a question, but not directly or explicitly.  It implies a slight hesitation or uncertainty.|He smiled half-questioningly at her. 他半疑问地对她笑了笑。|Example:  She tilted her head half-questioningly. 她略带疑问地歪着头。|Near Synonyms: hesitantly, tentatively, uncertainly, doubtfully|Antonyms: confidently, assertively, decidedly|词性: 副词

half-questioningly:/ˌhɑːfˈkwɛstʃənɪŋli/
词源|From "half" + "questioning" + "-ly". "Half" indicates a partial or incomplete state. "Questioning" refers to the act of asking a question. "-ly" is a suffix that forms adverbs.| | | | | |词性: 副词
