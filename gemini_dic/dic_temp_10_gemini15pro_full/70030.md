The company's logo has become fetishized by its fans.  这家公司的标志已经被其粉丝所迷恋。| 近义词: idolized, romanticized, obsessed over | 反义词: disregarded, neglected, ignored | 词性: adjective

词源: From the French word *fétiche*, meaning "object believed to have magical powers," from Portuguese *feitiço*, meaning "artificial," ultimately from Latin *facticius*, "made by art."
