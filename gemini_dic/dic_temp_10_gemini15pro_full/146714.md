overhomeliness:/ˌoʊvərˈhoʊmlɪnɪs/ | n. | 过于家常，过于朴素 | The overhomeliness of the decor made the restaurant feel a bit dated.  这过于家常的装饰让这家餐厅感觉有点过时了。 |  例句：The overhomeliness of her dress made her look out of place at the gala. 她的穿着过于朴素，让她在晚会上显得格格不入。

例句：He appreciated the overhomeliness of the bed and breakfast, finding it cozy and comforting. 他很欣赏这家住宿加早餐旅店的朴素，觉得它温馨舒适。 | 近义词：simplicity, plainness, homeliness | 反义词：sophistication, elegance, elaborateness | 词性：名词
