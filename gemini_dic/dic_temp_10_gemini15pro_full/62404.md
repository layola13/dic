endogen:/ˈɛndəʊdʒən/ (US) /ˈɛndoʊdʒən/| n. |内源物质，内生变量|A substance, factor, or process originating internally.|Endogens like hormones can influence our mood.  像激素这样的内源性物质会影响我们的情绪。|Synonyms: internal factor, intrinsic factor|Antonyms: exogenous factor, extrinsic factor|noun
