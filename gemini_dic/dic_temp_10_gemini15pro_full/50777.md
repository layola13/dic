descaling:/diːˈskeɪlɪŋ/
noun
除垢，清除水垢
The process of removing scale (a hard deposit of minerals) from surfaces, typically in kettles, pipes, or appliances.
例句：Regular descaling of your kettle will prolong its life. 定期为你的水壶除垢会延长它的寿命。

例句：The descaling process involves using a special solution to dissolve the mineral deposits. 除垢过程包括使用一种特殊的溶液来溶解矿物质沉积物。
近义词：cleaning, removal of scale
反义词: scaling, buildup
