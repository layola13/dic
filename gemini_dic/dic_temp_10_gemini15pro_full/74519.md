fraternalism:/frəˈtɜːrnəlɪzəm/
n.
兄弟情谊，博爱精神
The spirit or feeling of brotherhood; brotherly love.
Example:  The fraternalism of the firefighters was evident as they supported each other through the difficult rescue. 消防员们在艰难的救援过程中互相支持，展现了他们的兄弟情谊。

Example:  The fraternity promotes fraternalism and encourages members to support one another.  兄弟会提倡兄弟情谊，鼓励成员互相支持。

近义词：brotherhood, camaraderie, fellowship
反义词：enmity, hostility, antagonism
词性: noun

fraternal:/frəˈtɜːrnəl/
adj.
兄弟般的，友爱的
Of or relating to brothers; brotherly.
Example:  He felt a fraternal bond with his fellow soldiers. 他对他的战友们有一种兄弟般的情谊。

Example: They share a fraternal affection. 他们之间有着兄弟般的感情。
近义词：brotherly, amicable
反义词：unfriendly, hostile
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fraternalis, from Latin fraternus (“brotherly”), from frater (“brother”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰréh₂tēr (“brother”).
