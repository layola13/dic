habit:/ˈhæbɪt/| n.|习惯，习性|A behavior pattern acquired by frequent repetition or physiologic exposure or a tendency to act in a certain way.|She has a habit of biting her nails when she's nervous. 她紧张的时候有咬指甲的习惯。|近义词：custom, practice, routine|反义词:infrequency, irregularity|noun
