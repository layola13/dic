auditees:/ˌɔːdɪˈtiːz/
n.
被审计者，被审计单位
people or organizations that are being audited.
The auditees were nervous during the external audit.  被审计单位在外部审计期间很紧张。
The auditees prepared all the necessary documents for the audit. 被审计者准备了所有必要的审计文件。
synonyms: subjects of audit, inspected parties
antonyms: auditors, examiners
noun
