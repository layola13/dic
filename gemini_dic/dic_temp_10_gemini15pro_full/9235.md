antiparticle:/ˈæntiˌpɑːrtɪkəl/ /ˈæntiˌpɑːrdɪkəl/| n. |反粒子|A subatomic particle that has the same mass as another particle, but opposite values for some other property or properties, such as electric charge.  For example, the antiparticle of the electron is the positron.|Every particle of matter has a corresponding antiparticle. 每个物质粒子都有一个对应的反粒子。

The collision of a particle and its antiparticle results in the annihilation of both and the release of energy. 粒子与其反粒子的碰撞导致两者湮灭并释放能量。|近义词: counterpart (in some contexts) |反义词: particle |词性: noun
