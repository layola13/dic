The droopingness of the branches after the ice storm was significant.  冰暴过后，树枝明显下垂。|近义词：droop, sag, wilt, flaccidity|反义词: firmness, erectness|词性:名词

Etymology:  From "drooping" + "-ness". "Drooping" comes from the verb "droop," which originates from Old Norse *drupa "to droop, sink," related to Old English *dreopan "to drop, drip."  The suffix "-ness" is a common Old English suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
