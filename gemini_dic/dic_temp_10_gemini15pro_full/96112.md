indeliberated: /ˌɪndɪˈlɪbəreɪtɪd/
adjective
未经深思熟虑的，仓促的
not carefully thought out or planned; done without deliberation
He made an indeliberated remark that he later regretted. 他说了一句未经深思熟虑的话，后来后悔了。
The decision was indeliberated and rash. 这个决定是仓促而不经考虑的。
近义词: hasty, rash, impulsive, unpremeditated, unplanned, unconsidered
反义词: deliberate, planned, considered, premeditated, thoughtful
