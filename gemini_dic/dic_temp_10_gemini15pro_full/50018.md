denominating:/dɪˈnɒmɪneɪtɪŋ/
| adj. |命名为…的；以…计值的| expressing a value in a specified unit |The bond is denominating in US dollars.  该债券以美元计价。|
|v. |命名；称呼|to give a name or epithet to |They denominate the child Elizabeth. 他们给孩子取名为伊丽莎白。|
|近义词:|naming, calling, designating, labeling|
|反义词:| (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 contexts like "misnaming", "mislabeling" if applicable to the specific situation)|
|词性:|adjective, present participle of denominate (verb)|
|词源:|From Latin *dēnōminātus*, past participle of *dēnōmināre*  'to name, call', from *dē-* 'completely' + *nōmināre* 'to name'.|
