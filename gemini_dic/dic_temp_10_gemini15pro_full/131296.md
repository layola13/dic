nonceremonial:/ˌnɒnsɛrɪˈməʊniəl/ /ˌnɑːnsɛrɪˈmoʊniəl/
adjective
非正式的；非礼仪性的
Not involving or characterized by ceremony; informal.
The president made a nonceremonial visit to the troops. 总统对部队进行了非正式访问。

The two leaders held a nonceremonial meeting to discuss the crisis.  两位领导人举行了一次非正式会议，讨论危机。

近义词: informal, casual, unofficial, relaxed
反义词: formal, ceremonial, official, ritualistic
词性: adjective

词源:
From non- + ceremonial.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "ceremonial" refers to a formal act or series of acts performed on a special occasion.
