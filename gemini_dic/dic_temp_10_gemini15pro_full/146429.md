overformalizes:/ˌoʊvərˈfɔːrməlaɪzɪz/
verb
过分拘泥形式，过分正式化
Makes something excessively formal, adhering rigidly to rules and conventions, often to the detriment of naturalness or effectiveness.
The company's communication style overformalizes even simple requests, creating unnecessary bureaucracy.  公司在沟通方面即使是简单的请求也过于正式，造成了不必要的官僚作风。
The new regulations overformalize the process, making it cumbersome and inefficient. 新的规章制度使流程过于正式，使其变得繁琐低效。
Synonyms: ritualize, rigidify, bureaucratize
Antonyms: simplify, streamline, informalize
词性: 动词
