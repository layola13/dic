bindingness:/ˈbaɪndɪŋnɪs/
n.
约束力；拘束力
The quality or state of being binding; the state of being obligatory.
The bindingness of the contract is questionable.  这份合同的约束力值得怀疑。
近义词：obligation, validity, force
反义词：invalidity, nullity
词性：名词

Etymology:
From bind + -ing + -ness.  "Bind" comes from Old English "bindan," meaning "to tie, fasten."
