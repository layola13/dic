downhaul: /ˈdaʊnˌhɔːl/
noun
下拉索，下降索
A rope used to pull down or tighten something, especially a sail on a boat.
The sailor adjusted the downhaul to flatten the sail.  水手调整了下拉索以使帆变平。
The downhaul helps control the shape of the sail. 下拉索有助于控制帆的形状。
synonyms: outhaul, vang
antonyms: halyard, uphaul
n.
