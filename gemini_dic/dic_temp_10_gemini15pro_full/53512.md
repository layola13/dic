disassociates:/dɪsəˈsoʊʃieɪts/ /dɪsəˈsəʊʃieɪts/
|v.|分离，脱离，撇清关系|To separate from association or connection; to detach oneself.|He disassociated himself from the extremist group. 他脱离了那个极端组织。|Synonyms: detach, separate, disconnect, disengage|Antonyms: associate, connect, join, unite|Verb

disassociate:/dɪsəˈsoʊʃieɪt/ /dɪsəˈsəʊʃieɪt/
|v.|分离，脱离，撇清关系|To separate from association or connection; to detach oneself.|She disassociated the unpleasant memory from her childhood. 她把童年不愉快的记忆从脑海中抹去。|Synonyms: detach, separate, disconnect, disengage|Antonyms: associate, connect, join, unite|Verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- + associate.  "Dis-" is a prefix meaning "apart," "asunder," "away," expressing separation, reversal, or negation. "Associate" comes from the Latin "associare," meaning "to join to, unite with."
