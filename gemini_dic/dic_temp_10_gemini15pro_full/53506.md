disassiduity:/ˌdɪsæsɪˈdjuːɪti/
|n.|怠惰，懒散|Lack of diligence or application; neglect of duty.|His disassiduity in his studies led to poor grades.  他学习上的懒散导致成绩很差。|Example: The student's disassiduity resulted in his failing the class.  这个学生的懒惰导致他考试不及格。|Synonyms: indolence, laziness, slothfulness, neglect, remissness|Antonyms: diligence, assiduity, industry, application|noun
