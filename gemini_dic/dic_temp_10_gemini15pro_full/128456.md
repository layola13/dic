newsfeed:/ˈnjuːzfiːd/| n. |新闻推送，动态消息|A stream of online content presented in reverse chronological order, typically from sources a user has subscribed to or follows.|I check my newsfeed every morning to see what's happening in the world. 我每天早上都会查看我的新闻推送，看看世界上发生了什么。|Synonyms: feed, stream, timeline|Antonyms: archive (could be considered an antonym in a sense)|noun
