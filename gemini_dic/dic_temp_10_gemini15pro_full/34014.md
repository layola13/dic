citizen's arrest: /ˈsɪtɪzənz əˈrɛst/ | n. | 民间逮捕，公民逮捕 |  An arrest made by a private citizen, not a police officer, of a person believed to have committed a crime. | Example: The shopkeeper made a citizen's arrest after witnessing the theft. 店主在目睹盗窃后进行了公民逮捕。| Example:  He performed a citizen's arrest on the suspected vandal. 他对这名疑似破坏公物者执行了公民逮捕。 | Synonyms:  private arrest, civilian arrest | Antonyms:  police arrest, official arrest | noun
