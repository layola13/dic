endpaper:/ˈɛndˌpeɪpər/
n.|衬页，扉页|A sheet of paper attached to the inner surface of a book cover, often decorated or used for maps or illustrations.|The antique book had beautiful marbled endpapers.|Synonyms: flyleaf, pastedown|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym exists, perhaps "book cover" in a loose sense as the external counterpart.)
n.|衬页，扉页|
The endpaper was torn, revealing the previous owner's inscription.|这本衬页被撕破了，露出了前一位主人的题字。
The map on the endpaper showed the fictional world of the novel.|衬页上的地图展示了小说中的虚构世界。
Synonyms: flyleaf, pastedown
Antonyms: (No direct antonym)
