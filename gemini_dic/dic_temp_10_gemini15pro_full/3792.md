agminate:/ˈæɡmɪneɪt/| adj. v. |聚集的，簇生的；聚集，成群|clustered together; to cluster or form into a group|The butterflies agminated around the flowering bush. 蝴蝶聚集在开花的灌木丛周围。|The bacteria agminated into visible colonies. 细菌聚集成了可见的菌落。|近义词：cluster, aggregate, congregate|反义词：disperse, scatter|词性:形容词，动词
Etymology: From Latin *agminatus*, past participle of *agminare* "to gather into a flock, herd, or troop," from *agmen* "a train, a troop on the march, a flock, a swarm."
