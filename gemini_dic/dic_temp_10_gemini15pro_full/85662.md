hautein: /oʊˈtæ̃/ | adj. | 傲慢的，高傲的 | Haughty; disdainfully proud.  Showing an attitude of superiority; condescending. |  She gave him a hautein look, clearly unimpressed. 她傲慢地看了他一眼，显然没有被打动。|  He dismissed the question with a hautein wave of his hand. 他傲慢地挥了挥手，对这个问题不屑一顾。| 近义词: arrogant, supercilious, disdainful, haughty | 反义词: humble, modest, unpretentious | 词性: 形容词

词源: From French *hautain*, from Old French *halt* (high) + *-ain* (suffix indicating a characteristic). Ultimately from Frankish *halt*.
