descends:/dɪˈsendz/| v.|下降，下来，遗传| to move from a higher place to a lower one; to be inherited from an ancestor |The plane descends slowly. 飞机缓缓下降。 The property descends to the eldest son.  财产传给长子。|近义词：go down, drop, sink, fall, derive, inherit|反义词:ascend, climb, rise|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French descendre  "to come down, descend, go down; fall; originate," from Latin descendere "climb down, come down, dismount," from de- "down" + scandere "to climb."
