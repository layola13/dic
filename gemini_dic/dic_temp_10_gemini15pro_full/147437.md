overobjected:/ˌoʊvərəbˈdʒɛktɪd/
adjective
过度反对的
Having raised too many objections; excessively opposed.
The proposal was overobjected to by the committee, leading to its rejection.  该提案遭到了委员会的过度反对，最终被否决。
The defense attorney overobjected during the trial, annoying the judge.  辩护律师在审判期间过度反对，惹恼了法官。
Synonym: excessively opposed, overly challenged
Antonym: readily accepted, unopposed
词性: adjective
