outfeeding: /ˈaʊtˌfiːdɪŋ/
noun
外饲;外部供给;外输
The process of transferring materials or products from one process or location to another, often for further processing or distribution.
The factory's outfeeding system was efficient, ensuring a steady supply of components to the assembly line.  工厂的外输系统效率很高，确保了向装配线稳定供应零部件。
The outfeeding of data from the sensor to the computer is crucial for real-time monitoring.  从传感器到计算机的数据外输对于实时监控至关重要。
exporting, supplying, delivering
importing, receiving
