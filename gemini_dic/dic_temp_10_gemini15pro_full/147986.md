overrichness:/ˌoʊvərˈrɪtʃnəs/ | n. | 过于丰富，过剩 | An overrichness of nutrients in the soil can be detrimental to some plants. 土壤中养分过于丰富会对某些植物有害。|  The overrichness of the dessert made it too sweet for my taste. 这道甜点过于丰富，对我来说太甜了。 | 近义词：abundance, excess, superfluity, plethora | 反义词：scarcity, lack, deficiency, dearth | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above, beyond") + "richness" (from "rich" (Old English rīce "powerful, mighty, wealthy, splendid") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns of quality or state)).  Essentially, it signifies a state of being "overly rich" or having an excessive amount of richness.
