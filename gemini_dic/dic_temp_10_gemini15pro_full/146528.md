overgild:/ˌoʊvərˈɡɪld/| v. |过度镀金；过分装饰；修饰过分|To gild excessively; to cover with too much gold or ornamentation; to embellish excessively.  This often implies making something appear better than it actually is.|He overgilded the lily by adding so many unnecessary details to the story. 他给故事添加了太多不必要的细节，画蛇添足。|Synonyms: embellish, exaggerate, overdo, overdecorate|Antonyms: understate, downplay|verb
