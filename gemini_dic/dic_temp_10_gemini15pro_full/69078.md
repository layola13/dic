fauna's:/ˈfɔːnəz/
noun, possessive form
动物群的
The possessive form of "fauna," referring to something belonging to or associated with the animal life of a particular region or period.
The Amazon rainforest's fauna is incredibly diverse. 亚马逊雨林的动物群极其多样化。
The impact of climate change on the region's fauna is devastating. 气候变化对该地区动物群的影响是毁灭性的。
Synonym: animal life, wildlife
Antonym: flora (植物群)
