disengaging:/dɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
脱离，解脱，退出
releasing or detaching from something; ceasing to be involved or connected
The soldier was slowly disengaging the bayonet from his rifle. 士兵正慢慢地从步枪上卸下刺刀。
disconnecting, withdrawing, detaching
engaging, attaching, connecting
