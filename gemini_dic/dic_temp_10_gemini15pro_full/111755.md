lodes:/loʊdz/| n. |矿脉；矿藏|A rich source of something; a vein of metal ore.|The California Gold Rush began after prospectors discovered lodes of gold in 1848. 1848年，探矿者发现了金矿后，加州淘金热开始了。|Synonyms: veins, deposits, seams|Antonyms: barrenness, scarcity|noun
