orgiasticism:/ˌɔːrdʒiˈæstɪsɪzəm/ |n.| 狂欢; 放荡; 纵欲 |The excessive indulgence in pleasure or excitement, often characterized by unrestrained revelry and abandon.  A state of intense excitement and emotional frenzy, often associated with wild, unrestrained behavior. |The ancient Roman Bacchanalia were known for their orgiasticism. 古罗马的酒神节以其狂欢作乐而闻名。| The rock concert descended into orgiasticism as the crowd surged forward.  摇滚音乐会随着人群向前涌动而变成了狂欢。| 近义词: revelry, debauchery, bacchanalia, frenzy |反义词: restraint, moderation, asceticism |词性: noun
