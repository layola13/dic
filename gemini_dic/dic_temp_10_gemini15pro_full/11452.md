arguing:/ˈɑːrɡjuɪŋ/ | v. | 争论，辩论 |  The act of presenting reasons for or against something; debating. | They were arguing about which movie to watch. 他们正在争论看哪部电影。| synonyms: debating, disputing, quarreling, wrangling | antonyms: agreeing, concurring, harmonizing | verb

arguing:/ˈɑːrɡjuɪŋ/ | adj. | 好辩的，爱争论的 | Given to expressing divergent or opposite views. (Usually used as a participle.) | He has an arguing nature. 他好辩的性子。| synonyms: contentious, quarrelsome | antonyms: agreeable, amicable | adjective
