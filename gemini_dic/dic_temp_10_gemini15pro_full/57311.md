downswings:/ˈdaʊnswɪŋz/
noun
衰退；下降；低迷时期
A downswing is a period of reduced activity or a decline in economic conditions.
The company's profits suffered during the recent economic downswing.  这家公司的利润在最近的经济衰退中受到了影响。
The stock market experienced a sharp downswing after the news broke.  消息传出后，股市经历了急剧下跌。
synonyms: decline, downturn, slump, recession, decrease
antonyms: upswing, upturn, boom, increase, rise
n.
