architector:/ˈɑːrkɪtektər/| n.|建筑师，设计师|A person who designs buildings and other structures.|例句：The architector designed a beautiful and functional building. 建筑师设计了一座美观又实用的建筑。|例句：She hired a famous architector to design her new house. 她聘请了一位著名的建筑师来设计她的新房子。|近义词：designer, planner, builder|反义词:demolisher|词性:noun
