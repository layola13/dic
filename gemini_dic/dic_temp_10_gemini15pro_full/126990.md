nasalised:/ˈneɪzəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 鼻音化的；带鼻音的 |  Pronounced with the voice resonating in the nasal cavity.  A sound is nasalised when some of the air escapes through the nose during its production. | The singer's voice was heavily nasalised, giving it a unique quality. 歌手的声音鼻音很重，赋予了它独特的音质。

The French language uses many nasalised vowels. 法语使用很多鼻音元音。 | 近义词: nasal | 反义词: oral, non-nasal | 词性: adjective


nasalize:/ˈneɪzəlaɪz/ | vt. | 使带鼻音；用鼻音说 | To pronounce (a sound) with the voice resonating in the nasal cavity. |  Try to nasalize the vowel sound in "bon." 试着把"bon"中的元音发成鼻音。

He tends to nasalize his vowels when he's tired. 他累的时候倾向于用鼻音发元音。| 近义词: - | 反义词: - | 词性: verb
