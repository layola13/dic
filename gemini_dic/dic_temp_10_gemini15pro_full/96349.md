indiligent: /ˈɪndɪlədʒənt/ | adj. | 勤奋的，勤勉的 | Showing care and effort in your work or duties.  Characterized by steady, earnest, and energetic effort in a pursuit or study. |  She is an diligent student, always studying late into the night. 她是一个勤奋的学生，总是学习到深夜。

He is an diligent worker, always the first to arrive and the last to leave. 他是一位勤勉的员工，总是第一个到，最后一个走。 | 近义词：hardworking, assiduous, studious, industrious | 反义词：lazy, indolent, slothful | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *diligent- (stem of diligens), present participle of diligere "to value highly, love," from dis- "apart" + legere "to choose, gather, read."  The notion is of "loving application" to something.
