enervative:/ˈɛnərveɪtɪv/| adj.|使人衰弱的，令人泄气的|Causing one to feel weak and lacking in energy; weakening.|The enervative climate made it difficult to work.|近义词：weakening, debilitating, draining, exhausting, sapping|反义词：invigorating, energizing, strengthening, vitalizing|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *ēnervātus*, past participle of *ēnervāre* (“to weaken, deprive of strength, unnerve”), from *ē-* (ex- “out”) + *nervus* (“sinew, nerve”).
