nonlyricalness:/ˌnɒnˈlɪrɪkəlnəs/ /ˌnɑːnˈlɪrɪkəlnəs/| n.|非抒情性；缺乏抒情性|The nonlyricalness of the poem surprised many readers. 这首诗缺乏抒情性让很多读者感到惊讶。|The report's nonlyricalness made it difficult to connect with emotionally.  这份报告的非抒情性使得人们难以与之产生情感共鸣。|近义词:prosaicness, matter-of-factness, unpoeticalness|反义词:lyricism, poeticalness|词性:名词

词源: non- + lyrical + -ness
non-:  prefix meaning "not"
lyrical:  from Greek *lurikos "suitable for lyre accompaniment," from lura "lyre"
-ness: suffix forming nouns from adjectives
