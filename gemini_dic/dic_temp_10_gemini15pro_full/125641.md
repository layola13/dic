mummed:/ˈmʌmd/| adj. | 伪装的；乔装的；沉默的；(尤指) 扮成木乃伊的 | disguised or masked, especially as a mummy; silent | The children went mummed for Halloween. 孩子们在万圣节扮成木乃伊。 | 近义词：masked, disguised, silent, speechless | 反义词：unmasked, undisguised, talkative | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From the noun "mummy," influenced by the verb "mum" (to be silent).  最终源自模仿闭嘴的声音。
