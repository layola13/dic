compensational:/ˌkɒmpənˈseɪʃənl/ /ˌkɑːmpənˈseɪʃənl/| adj.|补偿性的，赔偿的|Relating to or providing compensation; making up for something lacking or inadequate.|例句：He received compensatory damages for his injuries. 他因受伤获得了赔偿金。|近义词：remedial, corrective, restorative|反义词：punitive|词性:形容词

compensational:/ˌkɒmpənˈseɪʃənl/ /ˌkɑːmpənˈseɪʃənl/| n.|补偿，赔偿（罕用作名词）|Something given to compensate for something else. (Rarely used as a noun)|例句：The government offered a compensatory payment to the victims. 政府向受害者提供了赔偿金。|近义词：compensation, redress, reparation|反义词：penalty|词性:名词
