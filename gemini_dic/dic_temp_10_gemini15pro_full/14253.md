autodigestion: /ˌɔːtəʊdaɪˈdʒɛstʃən/ | n. | 自体消化；自溶 | The process by which tissues or cells are broken down by their own enzymes. |  The pancreas produces enzymes that can cause autodigestion of the gland if they are activated prematurely. 胰腺会产生酶，如果这些酶过早被激活，就会导致腺体自体消化。

Autodigestion of the stomach lining can occur in some diseases. 在某些疾病中，可能会发生胃壁的自体消化。 |  Synonyms: self-digestion, autolysis | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider processes that build up tissues, like anabolism) | Noun
