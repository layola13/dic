extraparochial:/ˌekstrəpəˈrəʊkiəl/
adjective
堂区外的；不受教会管辖的；超教区的
Not subject to the authority of a parish, especially in ecclesiastical matters.  Outside the usual bounds of parish organization. Figuratively, beyond the scope of ordinary or routine matters.
The extraparochial area was not subject to the same laws as the rest of the town.  这个堂区外区域不受该镇其他地区相同法律的约束。

The hermit lived an extraparochial existence, detached from the concerns of the village.  这位隐士过着超脱尘世的生活，远离村庄的烦扰。

synonym
nonparochial, outlying, independent, detached
antonym
parochial, local, within, inside
词性
adjective
