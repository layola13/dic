cachet:/kæˈʃeɪ/ /kɑːˈʃeɪ/
noun
声望，威望，光环; 特征，标记
A mark of distinction, prestige, or authenticity.  A distinguishing feature or quality.

The restaurant’s Michelin stars give it a certain cachet. 这家餐厅的米其林星级赋予它一定的声望。

This wine has the cachet of coming from a small, family-owned vineyard.  这款葡萄酒拥有来自小型家族葡萄园的独特标记。


近义词: prestige, distinction, status, aura, hallmark
反义词: stigma, disrepute, obscurity
词性: n.
