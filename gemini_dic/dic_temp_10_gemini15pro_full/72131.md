flooders:/ˈflʌdərz/| n.| 泛滥者，涌入者; (尤指网络)灌水者 |People who flood a system or area, especially online with information or messages.  They can overwhelm servers or forums with excessive posts or traffic.|The forum was plagued by flooders posting irrelevant content.  这个论坛被发布无关内容的灌水者所困扰。|Near synonyms: spammers, abusers, trolls |Antonyms: moderators, administrators|Noun
