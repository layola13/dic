crownwork: /ˈkraʊnˌwɜːrk/ (US) /ˈkraʊnˌwɜːk/ (UK)
n.|冠状修复体；冠部工事|A dental restoration that completely encases the clinical crown of a natural tooth or dental implant.  In fortification, a crownwork is a fortified structure at the highest part of a castle or fortress.|The dentist recommended crownwork to repair the damaged tooth. 牙医建议用冠状修复体来修复受损的牙齿。 The crownwork provided a commanding view of the surrounding countryside.  冠部工事提供了周围乡村的全景。|近义词: dental crown, cap (dental), rampart, bastion|反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性:名词
