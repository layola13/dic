boarding:/ˈbɔːrdɪŋ/ | n. | 登机，上船，寄宿 | The process of getting onto a plane, ship, or other form of transportation, or the provision of accommodation and meals. | Example: Boarding will begin in 30 minutes.  登机将在 30 分钟后开始。

Example:  The school offers boarding facilities for international students. 这所学校为国际学生提供寄宿设施。| 近义词: embarkation, entering, lodging | 反义词: disembarkation, leaving | 词性: noun


boarding:/ˈbɔːrdɪŋ/ | adj. | 寄宿的 | Relating to or providing accommodation and meals. | Example:  She attends a boarding school in Switzerland.  她在瑞士的一所寄宿学校上学。

Example: He paid for boarding and lodging. 他支付了食宿费用。 | 近义词: residential | 反义词: day (as in day school/student) | 词性: adjective
