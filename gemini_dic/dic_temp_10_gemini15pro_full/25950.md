burqa:/ˈbɜːrkə/ /ˈbɜːrkɑː/ | n. | 面纱，罩袍 | A long, loose garment covering the whole body from head to toe, worn in public by some Muslim women. |  She wore a burqa that concealed her entire face. 她穿着一件遮住整个脸的面纱。|  The burqa offered protection from the sun and sand. 面纱提供了防晒和防沙的保护。|近义词：niqab, veil, chador | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps Western clothing) | 词性: noun

burqas:/ˈbɜːrkəz/ /ˈbɜːrkɑːz/ | n. | 面纱，罩袍（复数） | Plural form of burqa.  |  Many burqas were seen in the marketplace. 在市场上看到了许多罩袍。 | The shop sold burqas in a variety of colors. 这家商店出售各种颜色的罩袍。 | 近义词：niqabs, veils, chadors | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
