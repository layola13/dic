mail-bombing:/ˈmeɪlˌbɒmɪŋ/
n.
邮件炸弹
The act of sending a massive amount of email to a specific person or system in an attempt to overwhelm the recipient's inbox or mail server.
The activist group used mail-bombing as a form of protest against the corporation.  这个激进组织使用邮件炸弹作为抗议公司的一种形式。
