flats:/flæts/ | n. |公寓；单元房；平底鞋；低洼地|Apartments or units in a building; shoes without heels; areas of low-lying land.|She lives in one of the new flats downtown. 她住在市中心的一套新公寓里。|The salt flats stretched for miles. 盐碱地绵延数英里。|近义词：apartments, units, condominiums (for living spaces); loafers, ballet flats (for shoes); lowlands, marshes (for land)|反义词：houses (for living spaces); heels (for shoes); highlands (for land)|词性:名词

flats:/flæts/ | adj. |平的；平坦的；扁平的|Level and even; without heels (shoes); deflated (tire)|The road was long and flat. 这条路又长又平。|My bike tire went flat. 我的自行车轮胎没气了。|近义词：level, even, smooth (for surfaces); deflated (for tires)|反义词：hilly, bumpy, uneven (for surfaces); inflated (for tires)|词性:形容词


词源: From Old English *flet "floor, dwelling, hall," from Proto-Germanic *flataz "flat," from PIE root *plat- "to spread, flat."
