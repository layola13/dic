bigotries: /ˈbɪɡətriz/
n.
偏见，偏执，顽固
Strong, unreasonable prejudices against a person, group, or belief.
The candidate's speech was filled with bigotries against immigrants.  这位候选人的演讲充满了对移民的偏见。
His bigotries made it difficult for him to work with people from different backgrounds. 他的偏执让他很难与来自不同背景的人一起工作。
近义词: prejudices, biases, intolerance, discrimination
反义词: tolerance, acceptance, open-mindedness
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

词源:
From French bigoterie "sanctimoniousness, hypocrisy," from bigot "sanctimonious person, religious hypocrite."
