buzzardlike:/ˈbʌzərdˌlaɪk/| adj.|像秃鹰的，贪婪的，目光锐利的|Resembling a buzzard, especially in having keen eyesight or predatory habits.  Like a buzzard, often suggesting a scavenging or menacing quality.|He gave me a buzzardlike stare, as if he suspected I was hiding something. 他用鹰隼般锐利的目光盯着我，好像怀疑我藏着什么。|近义词：hawklike, rapacious, predatory, avaricious|反义词：generous, benevolent|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From buzzard +‎ -like. Buzzard from Middle English *busard, from Old French busart, of uncertain origin. -like from Old English -līc.
