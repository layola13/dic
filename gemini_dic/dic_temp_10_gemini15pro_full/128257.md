neuromere:/ˈnjʊərəˌmɪr/ /ˈnʊərəˌmɪr/| n. |神经节|A segmental subdivision of the embryonic neural tube, which gives rise to the segmental arrangement of spinal nerves and some cranial nerves. |The development of neuromeres plays a crucial role in the organization of the vertebrate nervous system. 神经节的发育在脊椎动物神经系统的组织中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词:  rhombomere (specifically for hindbrain neuromeres)|反义词: None|词性: noun

neuromere:/ˈnjʊərəˌmɪr/ /ˈnʊərəˌmɪr/| n. |神经体节|One of a series of repeating units in the developing nervous system, particularly evident in the hindbrain.|Each neuromere contributes to a specific part of the adult brain. 每个神经体节都构成成年大脑的特定部分。|近义词:  rhombomere (specifically for hindbrain neuromeres)|反义词: None|词性: noun
