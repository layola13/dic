inflamer:/ɪnˈfleɪmər/| n.|煽动者，激怒者|Someone or something that inflames or excites strong feelings, often of anger or hostility.|The politician's rhetoric was seen as an inflamer of tensions between the two countries. 这位政治家的言辞被视为加剧了两国之间紧张关系的煽动者。|His inflammatory speech acted as an inflamer of the crowd's emotions. 他煽动性的演讲激起了人群的情绪。|agitator, instigator, provocateur|peacemaker, mediator|noun
