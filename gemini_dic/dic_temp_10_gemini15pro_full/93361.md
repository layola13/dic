USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你认为如何？
What does she want for her birthday? = 她生日想要什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing right now? = 他现在在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she eaten for lunch? = 她午饭吃了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? = 你这周一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? = 你说了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about yesterday? = 她昨天在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting started? = 会议开始之前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I arrived? = 我来之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她转专业之前一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will happen tomorrow? = 明天会发生什么？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们在下次会议上会讨论什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by next month? = 到下个月你将完成什么？
What will they have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底他们将取得什么成就？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业的时候，她将已经学习了什么多久？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
What would she say if she knew the truth? = 如果她知道真相，她会说什么？


被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在对这个问题采取什么措施？


疑问句:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What are your plans for the summer? = 你暑假的计划是什么？


否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该做什么。
She doesn't understand what you mean. = 她不明白你的意思。
