deriding:/dɪˈraɪdɪŋ/
verb
嘲笑，奚落
Expressing contempt or ridicule.
He was constantly deriding his colleagues' efforts. 他不断嘲笑同事们的努力。
The critics derided her performance as amateurish.  评论家嘲笑她的表演很业余。
synonyms: mocking, ridiculing, taunting, jeering, scoffing, sneering, lampooning
antonyms: praising, commending, lauding, applauding
v.
