authoritativeness:/əˌθɔːrɪˈteɪtɪvnəs/| n. |权威性；威信；可信度|The authoritativeness of the report was unquestionable due to the extensive research and credible sources. 该报告由于广泛的研究和可靠的来源，其权威性毋庸置疑。|例句：The authoritativeness of his voice commanded respect. 他说话的权威性令人肃然起敬。|近义词：authority, credibility, reliability, trustworthiness|反义词：incertitude, doubt, unreliability|词性:名词

authoritative:/əˈθɔːrɪtətɪv/| adj. |权威的；有权威的；专断的|His authoritative manner left no room for argument. 他专断的态度不容置疑。|例句：She spoke in an authoritative tone. 她以权威的语气说话。|近义词：official, reliable, definitive, commanding|反义词：unofficial, unreliable, uncertain, hesitant|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *autoritatif, from Old French autoritatif, from Medieval Latin *auctoritativus, from Latin auctoritas (“authority”) + -ivus (suffix forming adjectives).
