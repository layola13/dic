limniad:/ˈlɪmniæd/| n. |湖泊女神，湖仙|A nymph inhabiting a lake or spring.  A freshwater nymph.|例句：The limniad danced gracefully in the moonlight on the lake's surface. 湖泊女神在月光下于湖面上翩翩起舞。

例句：Legends speak of limniads luring travelers to watery graves. 传说讲述湖仙引诱旅人至水中坟墓。|近义词：naiad, water nymph|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "land spirit")|词性:noun
