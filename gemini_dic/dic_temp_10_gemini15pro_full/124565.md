mourid:/ˈmʊərid/ | n. |穆里德，穆里德兄弟会成员 | A member of a Sufi Muslim brotherhood in North Africa, particularly Senegal, founded by Amadou Bamba in the late 19th century, characterized by its emphasis on peaceful work, religious devotion, and obedience to their spiritual leader (marabout). | Example: The mourid community is known for its entrepreneurial spirit.  穆里德社群以其创业精神而闻名。

Example:  Many mourids make a pilgrimage to Touba, Senegal, each year. 许多穆里德每年都会前往塞内加尔的图巴朝圣。 | 近义词: follower, disciple | 反义词: opponent, adversary | 词性: noun
