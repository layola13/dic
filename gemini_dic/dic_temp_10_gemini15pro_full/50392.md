depose:/dɪˈpoʊz/| v. | 免职；罢免；废黜；作证 | to remove someone important from a powerful position; to state something, especially in a law court, as a witness | The president was deposed in a coup d'état. 总统在政变中被罢免了。| synonyms: oust, remove, unseat, dethrone, testify | antonyms: install, reinstate, enthrone | verb

deposed:/dɪˈpoʊzd/| adj. | 被罢免的；被废黜的 | removed from a position of power | The deposed king lived in exile. 被废黜的国王流亡国外。| synonyms: ousted, removed, unseat, dethroned | antonyms: installed, reinstated, enthroned | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *deposer* (to lay down, place, deposit, depose), from Latin *dēpōnere* (to lay down, put aside), from *dē* (down, away from) + *pōnere* (to put, place).
