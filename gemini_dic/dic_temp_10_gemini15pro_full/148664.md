overtightness:/ˈoʊvərˈtaɪtnəs/| n.|过度紧绷，过紧|The overtightness of the strings made it difficult to tune the guitar.  琴弦绷得太紧，很难调音。|近义词：tension, tightness|反义词: looseness, slackness|词性:名词

Etymology: "over" (Old English "ofer," meaning "above, beyond") + "tight" (Old Norse "þettr," meaning "watertight, dense") + "-ness" (Old English "-nes," forming nouns of state or quality)
