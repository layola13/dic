epitomic:/ˌɛpɪˈtɒmɪk/ | adj. | 摘要的；概要的；典型的 | Representing the essential features of something larger: a condensed account; typical. |  This passage is epitomic of the entire book. 这段文字是整本书的缩写。|  The single mother is epitomic of those struggling in today's economy. 这位单身母亲是那些在当今经济中挣扎的人们的典型代表。| synonymous: representative, typical, exemplary, epitomical | antonymous: atypical, unusual, aberrant, anomalous | adjective


epitome:/ɪˈpɪtəmi/ | n. | 缩影；摘要；典范 |  A person or thing that is a perfect example of a particular quality or type. A summary of a written work; an abstract. | She is the epitome of elegance and grace. 她是优雅和高贵的缩影。 |  The book provides a clear epitome of the author's main arguments. 这本书对作者的主要论点作了清晰的概括。| synonymous: embodiment, personification, quintessence, abstract, summary | antonymous: expansion, enlargement,  elaboration | noun
