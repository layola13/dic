legerity:/ləˈdʒɛrɪti/
noun
轻盈；敏捷；轻快
nimbleness or agility of body or mind.
The gymnast performed with remarkable legerity. 体操运动员的动作轻盈优美。
Synonyms: agility, nimbleness, swiftness, fleetness, alacrity
Antonyms: clumsiness, awkwardness, sluggishness
