detrainments:/diːˈtreɪnmənts/
|n.| 下车; (尤指)中途下火车; (气象学)夹卷;  (云的)补偿下沉气流 |The detrainments of passengers occurred smoothly.  旅客们顺利下车了。|
|英文解析|  Plural form of detrainment.  The process of passengers leaving a train, especially at an intermediate stop. Also used in meteorology to describe downdrafts within clouds that compensate for updrafts, or the mixing of air from a cloud with the surrounding environment.|
|例句|The scheduled detrainments allowed passengers to stretch their legs.  预定的下车时间让乘客们可以伸展一下腿。|
|例句|The detrainments of cloud air played a crucial role in the storm's development. 云中空气的下沉气流对风暴的发展起了至关重要的作用。|
