interfretted:/ˌɪntərˈfrɛtɪd/
adjective
相互缠绕的，交织的
interwoven, intertwined, interlaced, interconnected.  Something that is interfretted is intricately interwoven or interlaced.
例句：The branches of the trees were interfretted, creating a dense canopy. 树枝相互交织，形成了茂密的树冠。

例句：The design featured interfretted Celtic knots.  该设计以交织的凯尔特结为特色。
近义词：interwoven, intertwined, interlaced, interconnected
反义词：separate, detached, disentangled
词性: adjective

词源:
From "inter-" (between) + "fret" (a network of lines, especially in decorative art).  "Fret" itself comes from Old French "freter" (to interlace).
