hylaeosaurus:/ˌhaɪliːəˈsɔːrəs/
n.
林龙（一种已灭绝的恐龙）
A genus of ankylosaurian dinosaur from the Early Cretaceous period of England.  It is one of the first dinosaurs to have been discovered and described.
The hylaeosaurus was a herbivore that lived during the Early Cretaceous period.  林龙是生活在早白垩纪时期的一种食草动物。
Hylaeosaurus is known from relatively incomplete fossil remains.  人们对林龙的认识来源于相对不完整的化石遗骸。
Synonym: Polacanthus, Ankylosaur
