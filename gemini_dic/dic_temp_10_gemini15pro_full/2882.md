advertent:/ədˈvɜːrtənt/
adjective
注意的，留意的
Paying attention; heedful.
She made an advertent choice to pursue a career in medicine. 她刻意选择从事医学事业。
The error was advertent, not accidental. 这个错误是故意的，不是意外。
synonyms: attentive, mindful, heedful, conscious, deliberate, intentional
antonyms: inattentive, unmindful, heedless, unconscious, accidental, unintentional
词性: adjective


USAGE->
She was advertent to the needs of her students. = 她很留心学生的需要。
He made an advertent effort to avoid any misunderstandings. = 他特意努力避免任何误解。
