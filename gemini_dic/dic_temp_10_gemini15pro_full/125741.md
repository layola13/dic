muntjac:/ˈmʌntdʒæk/ | n. | 麂，赤麂 |A small deer of Southeast Asia, with small antlers and tusks. |例句：The muntjac barked as it sensed danger.  麂感觉到危险后叫了起来。

例句：Muntjacs are known for their distinctive barking call. 赤麂以其独特的吠叫而闻名。


|近义词：barking deer, rib-faced deer |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


muntjaks:/ˈmʌntdʒæks/ | n. | 麂，赤麂（复数） |Plural form of muntjac.  | 例句： Several muntjaks grazed peacefully in the meadow. 几只麂在草地上平静地吃草。
