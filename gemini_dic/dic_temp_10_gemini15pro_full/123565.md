monsterlike:/ˈmɒnstəlaɪk/ (US) /ˈmɒnstərlaɪk/ (UK)| adj.|像怪物的；怪兽般的|Resembling a monster in appearance, behavior, or size.  Having qualities characteristic of a monster, such as being frightening, enormous, or cruel.|The shadow cast a monsterlike figure on the wall.  影子在墙上投射出一个像怪物一样的身影。|Synonyms: monstrous, beastly, fiendish, ogreish, gigantic, colossal|Antonyms: tiny, miniature, angelic, beautiful, pleasant|adjective
