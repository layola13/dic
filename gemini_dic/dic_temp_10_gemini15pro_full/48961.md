degender:/diːˈdʒɛndər/
verb
去除性别, 消除性别色彩
To remove gendered aspects from something, typically language or roles.
The company decided to degender job titles to promote inclusivity.  公司决定去除职位名称中的性别色彩以促进包容性。
They are working to degender children's toys. 他们正在努力消除儿童玩具的性别色彩。
