divisionary:/dɪˈvɪʒənəri/ | adj. | 分裂的，制造分裂的 | Intended to cause disagreement or disunity. | The politician used divisionary tactics to distract from the real issues.  这位政治家使用分裂策略来分散人们对真正问题的注意力。 | Example: Spreading rumors is a divisionary tactic. 散布谣言是一种制造分裂的策略。| Synonyms: divisive, disruptive, factional, schismatic | Antonyms: unifying, conciliatory, harmonizing | Word type: Adjective
