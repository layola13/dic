anti-cynical:/ˌæntiˈsɪnɪkl/
adjective
反愤世嫉俗的; 非玩世不恭的
opposed to or rejecting cynicism; believing that people are generally good and that things will work out well.
She has an anti-cynical view of the world, always looking for the best in people. 她对世界持有反愤世嫉俗的观点，总是寻找人们最好的一面。
The movie's anti-cynical message was refreshing in today's world. 这部电影反愤世嫉俗的信息在当今世界令人耳目一新。

近义词:optimistic, hopeful, positive, idealistic
反义词:cynical, pessimistic, negative, jaded
词性:adjective
