overprosperous: /ˌōvərˈprɑspərəs/ | adj. | 过于繁荣的，过度兴旺的 | Excessively prosperous; flourishing to an excessive degree.  Characterized by an abundance of wealth and material success, often to a point of excess or ostentation. | The overprosperous economy led to inflation. 过于繁荣的经济导致了通货膨胀。 | The overprosperous family bought a third vacation home.  这个过度富裕的家庭买了第三套度假别墅。 | 近义词: affluent, wealthy, opulent | 反义词: impoverished, poor, destitute | 词性: adjective

词源:
From over- + prosperous.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," "too much." "Prosperous" comes from the Latin word "prosperus," meaning "successful," "favorable," or "fortunate."
