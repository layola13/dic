highmost:/ˈhaɪmoʊst/| adj.|最高的，至高的|A literary or archaic term meaning "highest."  It emphasizes the extreme height or elevation, often with a poetic or elevated tone. |The highmost boughs of the ancient redwood reached towards the sky.  古老红杉最高的枝桠伸向天空。|The knight ascended to the highmost tower of the castle. 骑士登上了城堡最高的塔楼。|近义词：highest, uppermost, topmost|反义词：lowest, bottommost|词性:adjective
词源: Old English *hēahmōst (unattested), from hēah "high" + -mōst, superlative suffix.
