coadjutor:/koʊˈædʒətər/ | n. |助手，副手，助理主教|A coadjutor is an assistant, especially one who shares responsibilities with someone of higher rank.|The archbishop appointed a coadjutor to help him manage the large diocese. 大主教任命了一位助理主教来帮助他管理这个大的教区。|Synonyms: assistant, helper, aide, deputy, auxiliary |Antonyms: superior, boss, leader, chief | noun

coadjutor:/koʊˈædʒətər/ | adj. |辅助的，协助的 | Assisting; helping.  This usage is less common than the noun form. |He served in a coadjutor role to the principal. 他担任校长助理的角色。|Synonyms: assisting, auxiliary, supporting|Antonyms: leading, primary, main | adjective
