explicators:/ˈɛksplɪkeɪtərz/
n.
说明者；解释者
people who explain something; those who make something clear
The explicators provided a detailed commentary on the complex text.  说明者们对这复杂的文本提供了详细的注释。
The museum hired explicators to guide visitors through the exhibits.  博物馆聘请了解说员引导游客参观展品。
synonyms: interpreters, explainers, commentators, elucidators
antonyms: obfuscators, confounders
noun
