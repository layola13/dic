gumboil:/ˈɡʌmˌbɔɪl/ | n. |牙龈脓肿；牙床肿块|A small, painful swelling on the gums, usually caused by an infection.| I had a painful gumboil that made it difficult to eat. 我牙龈上长了一个疼的脓肿，吃饭都很困难。|Synonyms: abscess, boil, parulis|Antonyms: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From gum + boil.  "Gum" refers to the fleshy tissue surrounding the teeth, and "boil" refers to a localized swelling filled with pus.
