damozels:/ˈdæməzɛlz/| n.|少女，闺女|A young unmarried woman, especially one of noble birth, typically unmarried and idealized for her beauty and innocence.  This word is archaic and primarily used in literary contexts, particularly poetry.|例句：The knight rescued the fair damozel from the dragon. 骑士从龙那里救出了美丽的少女。

例句：The damozels danced in the meadow. 少女们在草地上跳舞。|近义词：maiden, damsel, lass, girl|反义词: matron, crone |词性:noun
