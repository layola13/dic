dotted:/ˈdɒtɪd/ (British) /ˈdɑːtɪd/ (American)| adj.|点缀的，有斑点的|Marked with dots or spots.|例句：She wore a dotted dress. 她穿着一件点缀着圆点的连衣裙。|例句：The landscape was dotted with small farms.  风景中点缀着小农场。|近义词：spotted, speckled, freckled|反义词：plain, solid|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From the verb "dot" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Dot" comes from Middle English "dotte," probably of Low German origin.
