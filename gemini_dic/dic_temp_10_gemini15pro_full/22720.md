boringly:/ˈbɔːrɪŋli/ | adv. | 乏味地，无聊地 | In a boring manner; tediously. | The lecture was delivered boringly, and many students fell asleep.  讲座进行得枯燥乏味，许多学生都睡着了。 | The meeting dragged on boringly for hours. 会议乏味地拖了好几个小时。| 近义词：tediously, monotonously, dully | 反义词：interestingly, excitingly, engagingly | 词性: 副词

词源: From boring + -ly.  "Boring" comes from the verb "bore," which has roots in Old English and ultimately relates to the idea of piercing or drilling.
