integrality:/ɪnˈtɛɡrəlɪti/| n. |完整性，完全性|The state of being whole and undivided; the quality of being honest and having strong moral principles.|例句：The integrality of the data is crucial for the experiment's validity.  数据的完整性对于实验的有效性至关重要。

例句：He stressed the integrality of his commitment to the project. 他强调了他对这个项目承诺的不可分割性。|近义词：completeness, wholeness, unity, entirety, soundness, honesty, integrity|反义词：incompleteness, fragmentation, dishonesty, corruption|词性:noun
