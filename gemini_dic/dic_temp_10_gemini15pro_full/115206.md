manifestationist:/ˌmænɪfɛˈsteɪʃənɪst/
n.
显化论者，主张思想可以转化为现实的人
A person who believes in manifestation, the idea that thoughts can be transformed into reality.
The manifestationist claimed she could attract wealth simply by visualizing it.  这位显化论者声称她可以仅仅通过想象就能吸引财富。
synonyms: believer in the law of attraction, proponent of manifestation
antonyms: skeptic, doubter
noun
