The dovishness's of the new administration was welcomed by many.|The president's dovishness's on the issue surprised many. 总统在这个问题上的鸽派立场让许多人感到惊讶。|Synonyms: pacifism, moderation, leniency|Antonyms: hawkishness, belligerence, aggressiveness|noun
