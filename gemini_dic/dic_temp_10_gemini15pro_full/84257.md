half-squadrons:/ˌhɑːf ˈskwɒdrənz/ /ˌhæf ˈskwɑːdrənz/
noun
半中队
A group of soldiers, aircraft, or ships half the size of a standard squadron.
The air force deployed two half-squadrons to the region. 空军向该区域部署了两个半中队。
detachments, units, groups
full squadrons, whole squadrons
