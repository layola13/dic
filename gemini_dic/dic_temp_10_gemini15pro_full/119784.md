migraine: /ˈmaɪɡreɪn/ (US) /ˈmiːɡreɪn/ (UK)| n. |偏头痛|A severe recurring headache, typically affecting one side of the head, often accompanied by nausea and disturbed vision.|例句：She suffers from migraines. 她患有偏头痛。|例句：The bright lights triggered a migraine.  明亮的灯光引发了偏头痛。|近义词：headache |反义词: (No direct antonym for a medical condition) |词性:noun

migraine: /ˈmaɪɡreɪn/ (US) /ˈmiːɡreɪn/ (UK)| adj. |偏头痛的|Relating to or affected by migraine.|例句：Migraine pain can be debilitating. 偏头痛的疼痛会使人衰弱。|例句：She experienced migraine symptoms. 她经历了偏头痛症状。|近义词：headache-inducing |反义词: (No direct antonym for a medical condition) |词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *migraine (modern French migraine), from Medieval Latin hemicrania, from Late Latin hemīcrania (“pain in one side of the head”), from Ancient Greek ἡμικρανία (hēmikranía), from ἡμι- (hēmi-, “half”) + κρανίον (kraníon, “skull”).
