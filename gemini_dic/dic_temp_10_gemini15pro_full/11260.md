archways: /ˈɑːrtʃweɪz/ | n. | 拱门，拱道 | A curved structure supporting a wall or other weight above an opening.  A passage or way under a series of arches. |  We walked through the cool, dark archways of the old monastery. 我们穿过古老修道院凉爽阴暗的拱门。 | The cathedral's archways were beautifully carved.  大教堂的拱门雕刻精美。 | synonyms: arches, vaults, passages | antonyms:  lintels, beams | noun
