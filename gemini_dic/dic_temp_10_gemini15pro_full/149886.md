palingenesy:/ˌpælɪnˈdʒɛnɪsi/ | n. |重生，再生，复兴|The spiritual rebirth or regeneration of a person; a transformation or renewal; the reconstitution of a thing after its destruction.|The myth of the phoenix rising from the ashes is a classic example of palingenesy.|Synonyms: regeneration, rebirth, renewal, resurrection, renaissance, revival|Antonyms: destruction, decay, decline, degeneration, extinction|noun
