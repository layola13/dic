generant:/ˈdʒɛnərənt/
n.
生成元; 生成物;
That which is generated. An element of a set, considered as generating the entire set through some operation.
The generant of this group is 3.  这个群的生成元是3。

近义词:generator, producer, origin
反义词:product, result, outcome
词性:名词
