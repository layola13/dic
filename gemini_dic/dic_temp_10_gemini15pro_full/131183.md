nonbrowsings:/ˌnɒnˈbraʊzɪŋz/| n.| 非浏览内容；非浏览行为|  Referring to content or actions that are not related to casual browsing or exploration. This often relates to focused searching or specific tasks online as opposed to leisurely exploration. |  The search engine algorithm filtered out nonbrowsings like specific product searches from general website traffic data.  搜索引擎算法从一般的网站流量数据中过滤掉了像特定产品搜索这样的非浏览行为。| synonyms: non-exploration, targeted searching, focused activities | antonyms: browsing, exploring, surfing, perusing | noun
