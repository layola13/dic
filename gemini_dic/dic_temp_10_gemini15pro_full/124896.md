muddlehead:/ˈmʌdəlˌhɛd/| n.|糊涂虫，笨蛋|A muddlehead is a person who is confused and disorganized, often making mistakes because they cannot think clearly. |He's such a muddlehead; he always forgets where he put his keys. 他真是个糊涂虫，总是忘记把钥匙放在哪里了。|Synonyms: scatterbrain, airhead, ditz|Antonyms: genius, mastermind|Noun
