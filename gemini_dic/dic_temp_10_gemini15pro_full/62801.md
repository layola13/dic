engulfments:/ɪnˈɡʌlfmənts/| n. |吞没，吞噬，淹没|The act of engulfing or the state of being engulfed; swallowing up or overwhelming.  It refers to the process of something being completely surrounded and covered by something else, often a liquid or a large mass.|The city was threatened by engulfments of lava from the erupting volcano. 这座城市受到火山爆发熔岩吞没的威胁。|Synonyms: inundations, submersions, overwhelming, consuming|Antonyms: releases, exposures, uncovering|noun
