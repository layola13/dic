besmirch:/bɪˈsmɜːrtʃ/
verb
玷污，损害（名誉）
To damage the reputation of (someone or something).
The scandal besmirched the reputation of the entire family.  这桩丑闻玷污了整个家族的名誉。
His good name was besmirched by the false accusations.  他的好名声被这些不实的指控玷污了。
Synonym: tarnish, sully, smear, soil, stain, defile
Antonym: honor, praise, extol, laud, acclaim
词性:动词
