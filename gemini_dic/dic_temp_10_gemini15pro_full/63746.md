epicedian:/ˌɛpɪˈsiːdiən/ | adj. n. | 挽歌的，哀悼的；挽歌，哀歌 | Of or relating to an epicedium; a funeral song or poem. |  The epicedian poem moved the mourners to tears. 这首挽歌使送葬者感动得流下了眼泪。 |  The bard recited an epicedian for the fallen hero.  吟游诗人为倒下的英雄吟诵了一首挽歌。 | 近义词：elegiac, mournful, lamenting | 反义词：joyful, celebratory | 词性: adjective, noun

词源: From Late Latin *epicedium, from Greek epikēdeion (melos) “(song) sung at a burial,” from epi “upon, at” + kēdos “sorrow, grief, lamentation.”
