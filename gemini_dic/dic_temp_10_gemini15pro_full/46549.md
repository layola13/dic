dallasites:/ˈdæləsaɪts/| n. |达拉斯人，达拉斯居民|A person who lives in Dallas, Texas.|Dallasites are known for their friendly nature. 达拉斯人以友好著称。|Residents, citizens, locals|Outsiders, foreigners, non-residents|noun
Etymology->
From Dallas + -ite (a suffix used to form nouns denoting inhabitants of a place).  Dallas itself is named after George Mifflin Dallas, the eleventh Vice President of the United States. The origin of his surname is unclear, with possible connections to places in Scotland or England.
