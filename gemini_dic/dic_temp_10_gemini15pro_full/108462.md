lay-byes:/ˈleɪˌbaɪz/ (UK), /ˈleɪˌbaɪs/ (US)
n.
分期付款购买;预留商品
A system of paying for goods in installments, where the goods are kept by the retailer until the final payment is made.
I put the TV on lay-by and paid it off over six months. 我把电视机分期付款购买，并在六个月内付清了款项。
lay-by is a useful way to purchase expensive items if you can't afford to pay for them outright. 如果你无力一次性付清昂贵商品的费用，分期付款是一个有用的购买方式。
近义词: installment plan, layaway (US)
反义词: outright purchase
词性:名词
