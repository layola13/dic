listening: /ˈlɪsənɪŋ/ (US) /ˈlɪsnɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. | 注意倾听的|Describes someone who pays attention to what others say. |She is a good listening partner. 她是一个很好的倾听伙伴。| 近义词: attentive, receptive | 反义词: inattentive, uninterested | adjective
