hospitable:/ˈhɑːspɪtəbl/ |adj.|好客的，殷勤的；适宜的|friendly and welcoming to guests and visitors; (of an environment) pleasant and favorable for living in|例句：The local people were very hospitable to us. 当地人对我们非常热情好客。|例句：The climate is hospitable to a wide variety of plants. 这种气候适宜各种各样的植物生长。|近义词：welcoming, friendly, genial, convivial, agreeable|反义词：inhospitable, unfriendly, unwelcoming|词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *hospitālis*, from hospes (“host, guest”).
