infused:/ɪnˈfjuːzd/ | adj. | 注入的，灌输的；浸泡的 | Filled with a particular feeling or quality. | The room was infused with the scent of lavender. 房间里充满了薰衣草的香味。 | steeped, imbued, saturated |  drained, lacking | adjective
