considerable:/kənˈsɪdərəbl/| adj.|相当大的，重要的，值得考虑的|Large in size, amount, or extent; significant; worthy of consideration.|例句：She inherited a considerable fortune. 她继承了一笔巨额财富。|例句：The project requires a considerable amount of time and effort.  这个项目需要相当多的时间和精力。|近义词：significant, substantial, large, ample|反义词：insignificant, small, negligible|词性:形容词
considerables:/kənˈsɪdərəblz/ | n. | (plural)  重要的事情；大量的东西| Important matters or large quantities of things.  This word is less common than "considerable" as an adjective. |例句：After selling the business, they had considerable funds at their disposal. 卖掉生意后，他们手头有大量资金。|例句：The negotiators discussed several considerables before reaching an agreement. 谈判人员在达成协议之前讨论了几个重要事项。|近义词：assets, resources, valuables|反义词：trifles, insignificances|词性:名词 (复数)
