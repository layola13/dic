autocentric:/ˌɔːtəʊˈsɛntrɪk/| adj.|自我中心的；以自我为中心的|Focusing on oneself and one's own needs and perspectives, often at the expense of others.  Considering oneself and one's own culture as the center of the world.|例句：His autocentric worldview prevents him from understanding other cultures.  他以自我为中心的世界观使他无法理解其他文化。

例句：She has a very autocentric personality and rarely considers the needs of others. 她性格非常以自我为中心，很少考虑别人的需要。|近义词：egocentric, self-centered, narcissistic|反义词：altruistic, other-centered|词性:形容词
