epidemic:/ˌɛpɪˈdɛmɪk/ | n. adj. | 流行病，传染病；流行性的 |A widespread occurrence of an infectious disease in a community at a particular time.|  The flu epidemic of 1918 was devastating. 1918年的流感疫情是毁灭性的。| 近义词：outbreak, plague, pandemic | 反义词：sporadic, isolated | 词性: 名词, 形容词

epidemic:/ˌɛpɪˈdɛmɪk/ | adj. | 流行的，盛行的；传染性的 | Affecting or tending to affect a disproportionately large number of individuals within a population, community, or region at the same time. |  Crime and violence are epidemic in this city. 犯罪和暴力在这座城市很猖獗。| 近义词：widespread, rampant, pervasive | 反义词：rare, uncommon, isolated | 词性: 形容词
