hexagonal:/hɛkˈsæɡənəl/| adj.|六角形的，六边的|Having six sides and six angles.  Relating to or shaped like a hexagon.|例句：The honeycomb has a hexagonal structure. 蜂窝具有六边形结构。|近义词：sexangular, six-sided|反义词:round, circular|词性:adjective
hexagonal:/hɛkˈsæɡənəl/| n.|六边形|A polygon with six sides and six angles.|例句：A regular hexagonal has equal sides and angles. 正六边形有相等的边和角。|近义词：sexagon|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
