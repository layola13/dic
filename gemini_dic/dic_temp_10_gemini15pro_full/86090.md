hearsay:/ˈhɪrseɪ/ | n. |传闻，道听途说 |Information received from other people that one cannot adequately substantiate; rumor. | The court case was dismissed because the evidence was based on hearsay. 法庭案件被驳回了，因为证据是基于传闻。| Example:  The rumor about the company merger is just hearsay at this point. 关于公司合并的传言目前只是传闻。| Synonyms: rumor, gossip, report, tale | Antonyms: evidence, proof, fact | noun
