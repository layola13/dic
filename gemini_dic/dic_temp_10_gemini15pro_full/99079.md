interaxis:/ˌɪntərˈæksɪs/
noun
轴间距；中心距
The distance between the center lines of two shafts or axles.
The interaxis of the two wheels is crucial for proper alignment.  两个轮子的中心距对于正确的对齐至关重要。
The engineer carefully measured the interaxis of the gears.  工程师仔细测量了齿轮的中心距。
synonyms: center distance, shaft spacing, axle spacing
antonyms: N/A
