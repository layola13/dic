delicatessens:/ˌdelɪkəˈtesənz/
n.
熟食店，熟食
A store selling prepared foods, such as cheeses, salads, and cooked meats.
例句：I love going to the delicatessens for a quick lunch. 我喜欢去熟食店吃顿快餐。

例句：The delicatessens down the street has the best pastrami on rye. 街角那家熟食店的熏牛肉黑麦面包最好吃。

近义词：deli, grocery, market
反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑 restaurant if 指的是提供现做食物的地方)
词性: 名词
