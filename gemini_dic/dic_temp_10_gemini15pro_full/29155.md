caseum:/ˈkeɪsiəm/| n.|干酪样坏死物，干酪|A cheese-like, friable material formed in certain types of necrosis.  It is most commonly encountered in tuberculosis. |The biopsy revealed caseum consistent with tuberculosis.|近义词:necrotic tissue, cheesy material|反义词:healthy tissue|词性:noun
Etymology: From Latin *caseus* meaning "cheese".
