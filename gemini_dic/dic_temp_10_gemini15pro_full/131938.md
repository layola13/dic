noncontracts:/nɒnˈkɒntrækts/ | n. |非合同，非契约|Agreements or arrangements that do not meet the legal requirements to be considered formal contracts.|  Many verbal agreements are considered noncontracts due to lack of specific terms. 许多口头协议由于缺乏具体条款而被视为非合同。| Synonyms: informal agreements, understandings | Antonyms: contracts, agreements | noun

noncontract:/nɒnˈkɒntrækt/ | adj. | 非合同的 | Not pertaining to or involving a formal contract. |  The noncontract work was paid on an hourly basis. 非合同工作按小时支付。| Synonyms: non-binding, informal | Antonyms: contractual, binding | adjective
