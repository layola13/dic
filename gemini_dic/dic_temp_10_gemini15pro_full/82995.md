gunning:/ˈɡʌnɪŋ/| v.| 猛烈前进；加速；(非正式) 努力争取；力求；(非正式) 用枪射击；|The driver was gunning the engine. 司机猛踩油门。|近义词：accelerating, striving, shooting|反义词：braking, decelerating|verb

gunning:/ˈɡʌnɪŋ/| adj.|  (非正式) 渴望的；一心想得到的；|He's gunning for a promotion. 他一心想得到晋升。|近义词：eager, keen, desirous|反义词：apathetic, indifferent|adjective
