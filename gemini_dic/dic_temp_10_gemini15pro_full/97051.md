infer:/ɪnˈfɜːr/ (US) /ɪnˈfɜː(r)/ (UK)| v. |推断，推论|To form an opinion or reach a conclusion based on evidence or reasoning; to deduce or conclude (something) from evidence and reasoning rather than from explicit statements.|From the evidence presented, we can infer that the suspect was at the scene of the crime. 从提供的证据来看，我们可以推断嫌疑犯当时在犯罪现场。|deduce, conclude, gather, surmise|state, declare|verb
