GCSE:/ˌdʒiː siː ɛs iː/ | n. | 中等教育普通证书 (General Certificate of Secondary Education) |  A qualification in a range of subjects typically taken by school students aged 14-16 in England, Wales, and Northern Ireland. | She passed her GCSEs with flying colours. 她以优异的成绩通过了中等教育普通证书考试。| N/A | N/A | noun

Etymology:
Acronym from General Certificate of Secondary Education.  Introduced in 1988 in England, Wales and Northern Ireland to replace the former O Levels and CSEs.
