outdodged:/ˌaʊtˈdɒdʒd/
verb
以更灵活的方式躲避; 巧妙地躲避
To evade or avoid something by being more clever or agile.
The fox outdodged the hounds.  狐狸比猎犬更机敏地躲开了。
outmaneuver, outwit, circumvent
