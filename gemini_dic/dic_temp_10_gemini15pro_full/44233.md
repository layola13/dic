crocodilia:/ˌkrɒkəˈdɪliə/ | n. | 鳄目动物 | An order of large reptiles, including crocodiles, alligators, caimans, and gharials, characterized by long snouts, powerful jaws, and a body covered in scales. | Crocodilia are found in tropical and subtropical regions. 鳄目动物分布在热带和亚热带地区。 |  Crocodiles are members of the order Crocodilia. 鳄鱼是鳄目动物的成员。 | synonyms: crocodylians | antonyms: N/A | noun
