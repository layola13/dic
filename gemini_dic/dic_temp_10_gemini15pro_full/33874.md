circumspection:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈspɛkʃən/| n.|谨慎，小心，慎重|The lawyer advised his client to exercise circumspection in his business dealings. 律师建议他的客户在商业往来中要谨慎行事。|例句：He showed great circumspection in his choice of words. 他在措辞上表现得非常谨慎。

例句：She proceeded with circumspection, carefully considering each step. 她谨慎地进行，仔细考虑每一步。

近义词：caution, prudence, care, vigilance|反义词：recklessness, carelessness, imprudence|词性:名词

circumspections:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈspɛkʃənz/| n.|谨慎，小心，慎重（复数）|These circumspections are necessary to avoid any legal issues. 这些谨慎措施对于避免任何法律问题是必要的。|例句：His circumspections were understandable given the sensitive nature of the situation. 考虑到情况的敏感性，他的谨慎是可以理解的。

例句：Her numerous circumspections eventually led to paralysis by analysis. 她过多的谨慎最终导致了分析瘫痪。


近义词：cautions, prudences, cares, vigils|反义词：recklessnesses, carelessnesses, imprudences|词性:名词


词源: Late Middle English: from Old French circomspection or Latin circumspectio(n-), from circumspect- ‘looked around’, from the verb circumspicere .
