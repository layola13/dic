grouseless:/ˈɡraʊslɪs/| adj.|无松鸡的；没有松鸡的；缺乏松鸡的|Lacking grouse; without grouse.  Refers to a place where there are no grouse, or a hunting trip that yielded no grouse.|The hunter returned grouseless from his trip to the moors. 猎人从沼泽地旅行回来，两手空空，没抓到松鸡。|Synonyms: barren, empty, unproductive|Antonyms: teeming (with grouse), abundant (in grouse)|adjective
