intelligentias: /ɪnˌtɛlɪˈdʒɛntsiəz/
n.
知识分子，知识阶层
a group of people who are highly educated and involved in intellectual or artistic activities
例句：The intelligentsias of the country were critical of the government's policies.  这个国家的知识分子批评了政府的政策。

例句：The intelligentsias often gather in cafes to discuss politics and art. 知识分子经常聚集在咖啡馆讨论政治和艺术。

近义词：intellectuals, educated class, literati
反义词：masses, common people, uneducated
词性：noun
