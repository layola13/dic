overprovisions:/ˌōvərprəˈvɪʒənz/ | n. | 过度供应，超额配置 | The act of supplying or providing more of something than is necessary or likely to be used. |  The cloud provider overprovisions resources to ensure high availability. 云提供商过度配置资源以确保高可用性。 |  Overprovisioning disk space can be costly. 过度配置磁盘空间可能很昂贵。 | synonyms: oversupply, excess, surplus | antonyms: undersupply, shortage, deficit | noun
