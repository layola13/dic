outdoing:/ˌaʊtˈduːɪŋ/
verb
胜过，超过
surpassing or exceeding someone in achievement or performance.
He was always trying to outdo his older brother in sports. 他总是试图在运动方面超过他哥哥。

outdoing:/ˌaʊtˈduːɪŋ/
近义词: excelling, surpassing, beating, besting, topping
反义词: failing, losing, underperforming


ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + do.  The prefix "out-" implies exceeding or surpassing.
