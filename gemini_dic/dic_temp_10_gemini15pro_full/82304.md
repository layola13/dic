groupware:/ˈɡruːpwɛər/
n.|群件，协同软件|Software designed to facilitate collective working by a group of people.|Our company uses groupware to manage projects and share documents. 我们公司使用群件来管理项目和共享文档。|Synonyms: collaborative software, groupwork software|Antonyms: standalone software|noun
