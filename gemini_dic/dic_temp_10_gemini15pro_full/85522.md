hatable:/ˈheɪtəbl/| adj.|可恨的，令人讨厌的|Deserving of or causing hatred; detestable.|例句：His behavior was so hateful that everyone avoided him. 他的行为如此可恨，以至于每个人都躲着他。|近义词：detestable, abhorrent, loathsome, odious, despicable|反义词：lovable, admirable, likeable|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *hatable, from Old French haitable (modern French haïssable), from hair (“to hate”) + -able.
