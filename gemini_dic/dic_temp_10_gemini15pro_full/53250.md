directs:/dəˈrɛkts/ /daɪˈrɛkts/| v.| 指导，指挥，管理，导演，指示，命令 | The manager directs the team's activities. 经理指导团队的活动。|例句：The conductor directs the orchestra. 指挥家指挥管弦乐队。|近义词：manages, guides, leads, instructs, orders, commands|反义词：follows, obeys|词性:动词

directs:/dəˈrɛkts/ /daɪˈrɛkts/| n.| 指示，命令 | He followed the director's directs. 他遵照导演的指示。|例句：The directs were clear and concise. 这些指示清晰而简明。|近义词：instructions, orders, commands|反义词：suggestions, requests|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *directif, from Latin directus, past participle of dirigere "to guide, direct," from dis- "apart" + regere "to guide, rule."
