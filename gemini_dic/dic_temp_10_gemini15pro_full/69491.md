felicitation:/fəˌlɪsɪˈteɪʃən/| n.|祝贺，恭贺|an expression of congratulations|例句：Please accept my felicitations on your promotion. 请接受我对您升职的祝贺。|例句：They offered their felicitations on his marriage. 他们对他结婚表示祝贺。|近义词：congratulation, commendation|反义词：condolence|词性:名词

felicitate:/fəˈlɪsɪteɪt/| vt.|祝贺，恭贺|to congratulate someone|例句：I felicitated him on his success. 我祝贺他取得了成功。|例句：We felicitate you on your recent achievement. 我们祝贺你最近取得的成就。|近义词：congratulate, commend|反义词：condole|词性:动词
