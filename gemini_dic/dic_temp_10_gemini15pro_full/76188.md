fusspot:/ˈfʌsˌpɒt/ (UK) /ˈfʌsˌpɑːt/ (US)| n. |大惊小怪的人；小题大做的人；吹毛求疵的人|A person who is excessively anxious or particular about small details; someone who complains or worries too much about unimportant things.|例句：He's such a fusspot about his food – everything has to be perfect. 他对食物太挑剔了——每样东西都必须完美无缺。

例句：My grandmother is a terrible fusspot, always worrying about the house being tidy. 我祖母是个非常爱大惊小怪的人，总是担心房子是否整洁。|近义词：worrier, nitpicker, perfectionist, complainer|反义词：easygoing person, relaxed person|词性:noun
