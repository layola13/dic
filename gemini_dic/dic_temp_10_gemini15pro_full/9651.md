antisuicide:/ˌæntiˈsuːɪsaɪd/
adjective
反自杀的，防止自杀的
Relating to or intended to prevent suicide.
The hospital has an antisuicide policy.  这家医院有反自杀政策。
The government launched an antisuicide campaign. 政府发起了一项反自杀运动。
近义词：suicide prevention
反义词: pro-suicide (This term is highly sensitive and should be used with extreme caution.)
词性: adjective
