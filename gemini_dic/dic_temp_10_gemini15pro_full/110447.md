likert: /ˈlɪkərt/ | adj. n.| 李克特式的，李克特量表 |A Likert scale is a psychometric scale commonly involved in research that employs questionnaires. It is the most widely used approach to scaling responses in survey research, such as that the term is often used interchangeably with rating scale, although there are other types of rating scales.| Participants were asked to rate their agreement with the statements on a five-point Likert scale. 参与者被要求用五点李克特量表对陈述的同意程度进行评分。|近义词: rating scale|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun
