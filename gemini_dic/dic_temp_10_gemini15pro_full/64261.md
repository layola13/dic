equiponderate:/ˌiːkwɪˈpɒndəreɪt/ /ˌiːkwɪˈpɑːndəreɪt/
verb
等重; 均衡
to be equal in weight, force, or importance; to balance
例句：The two sides in the conflict equiponderate each other. 冲突双方势均力敌。

例句：The arguments for and against the proposal seem to equiponderate.  支持和反对这项提议的论点似乎势均力敌。
近义词：balance, counterbalance, offset, equalize
反义词：outweigh, overbalance, preponderate
词性:v.
