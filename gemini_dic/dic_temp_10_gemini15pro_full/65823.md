evilly: /ˈiːvɪlli/ | adv. | 邪恶地，恶毒地 | In a wicked or morally wrong manner. | He laughed evilly as he revealed his wicked plan. 他邪恶地笑着，揭示了他的邪恶计划。| She glared evilly at her rival. 她恶狠狠地瞪着她的对手。 |  near synonyms: wickedly, malevolently, malignantly, diabolically | near antonyms: kindly, benevolently, virtuously | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From evil + -ly.  "Evil" traces back to Old English "yfel," ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*ubilaz," meaning "bad, evil." The suffix "-ly" transforms adjectives into adverbs.
