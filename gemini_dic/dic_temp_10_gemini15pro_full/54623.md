dismember:/dɪsˈmɛmbər/
verb
肢解，分割
to cut or tear (a body) into pieces; to divide into parts
The serial killer dismembered his victims.  连环杀手肢解了他的受害者。
synonyms: mutilate, dissect, tear apart
antonyms: assemble, join, unite
词性: 动词
