addict:/ˈædɪkt/| n. |瘾君子；沉迷者|A person who is addicted to a particular substance, typically an illegal drug. |He became a drug addict at the age of 15. 他15岁就成了瘾君子。|近义词：dependent, junky, fiend |反义词:moderate user|noun
addict:/əˈdɪkt/| vt. |使沉迷；使上瘾|To cause someone to become addicted to a substance or activity.|He was addicted to gambling. 他沉迷于赌博。|近义词：hook, enthrall |反义词:wean|verb


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is addicted to video games. = 他沉迷于电子游戏。
She is a coffee addict. = 她是个咖啡迷。

一般过去时:
He was addicted to heroin for many years. = 他吸食海洛因很多年了。
She became addicted to shopping after losing her job. = 她失业后沉迷于购物。

现在完成时:
He has been addicted to nicotine for over a decade. = 他吸尼古丁已经超过十年了。
She has become addicted to social media. = 她沉迷于社交媒体。

过去完成时:
He had been addicted to alcohol before he sought help. = 他在寻求帮助之前就已经酗酒成瘾了。
She had become addicted to painkillers after her surgery. = 她在手术后对止痛药上瘾了。

将来时:
If he continues like this, he will become addicted to gambling. = 如果他继续这样下去，他会赌博成瘾。
She will be addicted to this new mobile game soon. = 她很快就会沉迷于这款新的手机游戏。


虚拟语气:
If I were addicted to drugs, I would seek professional help. = 如果我吸毒成瘾，我会寻求专业帮助。
I wish I weren't addicted to sugar. = 我希望我不沉迷于糖。

被动语态:
He was addicted to drugs. = 他吸毒成瘾。(主动语态，被动语态一样)


疑问句:
Is he addicted to gambling? = 他赌博成瘾吗？
What is she addicted to? = 她对什么上瘾？

否定句:
He is not addicted to alcohol. = 他不酗酒。
She is not a drug addict. = 她不是瘾君子。

become addicted to = 对...上瘾
