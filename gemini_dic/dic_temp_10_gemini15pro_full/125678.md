mund:/mʊnd/ | n. | hand, protection |A hand; also, more generally, protection or guardianship.  From Old English and Old Norse, related to "hand."|He offered his mund to the frightened child. 他向受惊的孩子伸出了手（给予保护）。|Near synonyms: protection, guardianship, safekeeping|Near antonyms:  danger, peril|noun
