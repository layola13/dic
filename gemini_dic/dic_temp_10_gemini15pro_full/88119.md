hijack:/ˈhaɪdʒæk/| v. n.|劫持，挟持，篡改|To seize or commandeer (a vehicle, aircraft, or ship) in transit by force.  To take control of something and use it for your own purposes, often in a dishonest or illegal way.|The plane was hijacked by terrorists. 这架飞机被恐怖分子劫持了。|Synonyms: seize, commandeer, appropriate, usurp, pirate|Antonyms: release, surrender, return|verb

hijack:/ˈhaɪdʒæk/| n.|劫持，挟持|An act of hijacking.|The hijack ended peacefully. 劫持事件和平结束。|Synonyms: seizure, commandeering|Antonyms: release, surrender|noun
