paraded:/pəˈreɪdɪd/ | v. | 游行，炫耀 |  past tense and past participle of "parade," meaning to walk or march in a procession, especially for display or celebration; to show off something with pride. | The soldiers paraded through the streets after their victory. 士兵们在胜利后在街上游行。| The peacock paraded its colorful feathers.  孔雀炫耀着它五彩缤纷的羽毛。 | synonyms: marched, processed, flaunted, displayed, showed off | antonyms: hid, concealed, downplayed | verb

parade:/pəˈreɪd/ | n. | 游行，炫耀 | a public procession, especially one celebrating a special day or event; a showy display or exhibition. |  A colorful parade celebrated the town's anniversary.  一个色彩缤纷的游行庆祝了小镇的周年纪念日。|  His constant bragging is just a parade of his ego. 他不断的吹嘘只是在炫耀他的自我。 | synonyms: procession, march, demonstration, display, exhibition | antonyms: concealment, hiding | noun
