enthralment:/ɪnˈθrɔːlmənt/| n.|着迷，迷恋，吸引|The enthralment of the audience was palpable.  观众的着迷显而易见。|例句：The magician's tricks held the children in enthralment. 魔术师的戏法让孩子们着迷。|近义词：fascination, captivation, enchantment|反义词：boredom, disinterest|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French  *enthraler*, from en- (“in, into”) + thral (“slave, servant”). Ultimately from Old Norse *þræll* (“slave”).
