non-redeemable:/ˌnɒn rɪˈdiːməbl/ | /ˌnɑːn rɪˈdiːməbl/ | adj. | 不可赎回的，不可兑现的 | Not capable of being redeemed, exchanged, or recovered. |  The bonds are non-redeemable until maturity.  这些债券到期前不可赎回。|  Example:  These shares are non-redeemable, meaning you cannot exchange them for cash. 这些股票是不可赎回的，意味着你不能把它们兑换成现金。 | Synonyms:  irredeemable, nonrefundable, nonexchangeable | Antonyms: redeemable, refundable, exchangeable | adjective
non-redeemable:/ˌnɒn rɪˈdiːməbl/ | /ˌnɑːn rɪˈdiːməbl/ | n. | 不可赎回之物 | Something that cannot be redeemed, exchanged, or recovered. | The company issued non-redeemables as part of its fundraising efforts. 该公司发行不可赎回债券作为其融资工作的一部分。| Example: The non-redeemables were a risky investment. 这些不可赎回的投资品是一项风险投资。 | Synonyms: irredeemable security | Antonyms: redeemable security | noun
