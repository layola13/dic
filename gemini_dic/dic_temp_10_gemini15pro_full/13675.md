attractivenesses:/əˈtræktɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
魅力，吸引力（复数）
The various attractivenesses of the city draw tourists from all over the world.  这座城市的各种魅力吸引了来自世界各地的游客。
例句：The actress's attractivenesses include her talent and her beauty.  这位女演员的魅力包括她的才华和美貌。

例句：He considered the attractivenesses of the different job offers. 他考虑了不同工作机会的吸引力。

近义词：allure, appeal, charm, magnetism
反义词：unattractiveness, repulsion
词性：名词

Etymology: From attractive + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *tractivus, from trahere "to draw".
