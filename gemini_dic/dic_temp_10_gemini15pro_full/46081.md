cyclopedist:/ˌsaɪkləˈpiːdɪst/
n.
百科全书编纂者; 博学者
A person who compiles or edits an encyclopedia, or one who has a wide range of knowledge.
Johnson considered himself a cyclopedist, having expertise in various fields.  约翰逊认为自己是一位百科全书编纂者，在各个领域都有专长。
He was a true cyclopedist, conversant in everything from art to zoology. 他是一位真正的博学者，从艺术到动物学无所不知。
