loopy:/ˈluːpi/| adj.|愚蠢的，傻的；疯狂的，古怪的；环状的|Characterized by silliness, craziness, or eccentricity; having a loop or loops.|例句：After staying up all night, I felt a bit loopy.  熬夜之后，我感觉有点傻。|例句：She has a loopy idea about starting a business selling pet rocks. 她有一个疯狂的想法，要开一家卖宠物石头的公司。|近义词：crazy, silly, eccentric, foolish, insane, daft|反义词：sensible, sane, rational|词性:形容词
词源:From loop + -y.  来自 loop（环）+ -y（形容词后缀）。
