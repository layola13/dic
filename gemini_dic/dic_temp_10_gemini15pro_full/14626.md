avoidable:/əˈvɔɪdəbl/
adjective
可避免的
Capable of being avoided or prevented.
Many traffic accidents are avoidable. 许多交通事故是可以避免的。
The mistake was entirely avoidable.  这个错误完全是可以避免的。
近义词:preventable, avertable
反义词:inevitable, unavoidable
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From avoid + -able.  Avoid comes from Old French esvuidier "to empty out, get rid of," from ex- "out" + vuider "to empty."
