grantedly:/ˈɡrɑːntɪdli/ | adv. | 的确，诚然 |  It is used to introduce a statement that concedes a point, often followed by a contrasting statement. | Grantedly, the weather was bad, but we still had a good time. 的确，天气不好，但我们仍然玩得很开心。 | admittedly, certainly, indeed | arguably, possibly, doubtfully | adverb
