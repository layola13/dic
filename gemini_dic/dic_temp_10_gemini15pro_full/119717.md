midsized:/ˈmɪdˌsaɪzd/| adj.|中型的，中等大小的|Relatively small; not large.  Something that is between small and large in size.  Often used in business contexts to describe companies.|例句：They are a midsized company specializing in software development.  他们是一家中型软件开发公司。|例句：We're looking for a midsized SUV that's good on gas. 我们正在寻找一辆省油的中型SUV。|近义词：medium-sized, average-sized|反义词:small, large, oversized|词性:adjective
