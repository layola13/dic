decadal:/dɪˈkeɪdəl/| adj.| 十年的，十年间的|Occurring or being done every ten years; lasting for ten years. |The decadal census provides valuable demographic data. 十年一度的人口普查提供了宝贵的人口统计数据。|The government is implementing a decadal plan to improve infrastructure. 政府正在实施一项十年计划来改善基础设施。|近义词：ten-year, decennial|反义词: annual, yearly|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *decadālis, from decas (“a group of ten”) + -ālis.
