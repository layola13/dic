megaliths: /ˈmɛɡəlɪθs/ | n. | 巨石阵, 巨石建筑 | A large prehistoric monument made of one or more large stones, often arranged in a circle or other geometric pattern. |  Stonehenge is perhaps the most famous example of megaliths. 巨石阵也许是巨石建筑最著名的例子。

The purpose of these megaliths remains a mystery. 这些巨石建筑的用途仍然是个谜。 | 近义词: monuments, henges | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "small stones") | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek μέγας (mégas, “great”) + λίθος (líthos, “stone”).
