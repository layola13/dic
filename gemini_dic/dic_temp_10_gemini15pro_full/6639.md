anaesthetised:/əˈniːsθətaɪzd/| adj.|麻醉的，失去知觉的|Deprived of feeling or sensation, especially by a drug.|The patient was anaesthetised before the surgery. 病人在手术前被麻醉了。|The dog was anaesthetised before the vet performed the operation.  兽医在给狗做手术之前先麻醉了它。|Synonyms: numb, insensible, unconscious|Antonyms: conscious, aware, sensitive|adjective

anaesthetize:/əˈniːsθətaɪz/| vt.|麻醉|To deprive of feeling or sensation, especially by a drug.|The doctor anaesthetized the patient before the surgery. 医生在手术前给病人麻醉。|The vet anaesthetized the cat before stitching the wound. 兽医在缝合伤口之前先麻醉了猫。|Synonyms: numb, drug, sedate|Antonyms: stimulate, awaken, arouse|verb
