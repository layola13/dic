disembarrassment:/dɪsɪmˈbærəsmənt/| n. |摆脱尴尬; 解脱; 消除窘境|The state of being freed from embarrassment or awkwardness.  The act of freeing someone from embarrassment or difficulty.|  After the awkward silence, his joke brought a welcome disembarrassment.  尴尬的沉默之后，他的笑话带来了令人欣慰的解脱。| synonyms: relief, liberation, extrication | antonyms: embarrassment, awkwardness, discomfort| noun

disembarrass:/ˌdɪsɪmˈbærəs/| vt. |使摆脱尴尬; 使摆脱困境; 使解脱|To free from embarrassment, difficulty, or awkwardness.| He disembarrassed her by changing the subject. 他通过改变话题来帮她摆脱尴尬。 | synonyms: relieve, liberate, extricate | antonyms: embarrass, hinder, encumber| verb
