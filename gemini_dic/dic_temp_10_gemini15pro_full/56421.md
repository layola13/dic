dollarise:/ˈdɒləraɪz/ (US) /ˈdɑːləraɪz/ (UK)| v. |美元化|To convert assets or transactions into US dollars; to cause a country's economy to become dependent on the US dollar.|例句：Many countries are trying to dollarise their economies. 许多国家正试图使它们的经济美元化。

例句：The company's debts were dollarised, increasing their burden with the fluctuating exchange rate.  公司的债务被美元化了，随着汇率的波动，他们的负担加重了。|近义词：monetize, denominate (in dollars)|反义词: N/A |词性: verb
