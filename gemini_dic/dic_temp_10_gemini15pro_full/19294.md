biauriculate:/ˌbaɪɔːˈrɪkjʊlət/
adjective
双耳的，有两耳垂的
Having two auricles or ear-shaped appendages.
The biauriculate pitcher plant uses its two ear-like structures to attract insects.  这种双耳状的猪笼草利用其两个耳状结构来吸引昆虫。
The ancient Roman amphora was biauriculate, allowing for easy pouring.  这个古罗马的双耳陶罐有两个耳状把手，便于倾倒。
近义词:two-eared
反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially earless)
词性: adjective
