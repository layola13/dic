misa:/ˈmiːsə/ | n. |弥撒; 圣餐|The Catholic Mass is a ritual reenactment of the Last Supper. |天主教弥撒是对最后晚餐的仪式性重现。| Eucharist, Holy Communion |  | noun |

Etymology: From Late Latin *missa, from Latin missa (“dismissal”), feminine past participle of mittere (“to send, let go”). Originally used in the concluding formula of the service, Ite, missa est ("Go, the congregation is dismissed").
