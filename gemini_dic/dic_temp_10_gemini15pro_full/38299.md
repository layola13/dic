concavity:/kɒnˈkævɪti/ /kɑːnˈkævəti/| n. |凹度，凹面|a curved inward shape or surface; a hollow area|The mirror had a slight concavity, distorting the reflection.  镜子略微凹陷，使反射的影像变形。|Synonyms: hollowness, depression, indentation|Antonyms: convexity, bulge, protuberance|noun

concavity:/kɒnˈkævɪti/ /kɑːnˈkævəti/| n. |凹度，凹陷|A hollowed-out area or surface; a depression.|The concavity of the spoon made it perfect for scooping ice cream.  勺子的凹陷使其非常适合舀冰淇淋。|Synonyms:  indentation, hollow, dip|Antonyms: convexity, prominence, bulge|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *concavitatem, from Latin concavus (“hollow”), from com- (“together”) + cavus (“hollow”).
