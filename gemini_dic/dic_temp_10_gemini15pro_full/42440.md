counterdemonstrates:/ˌkaʊntərˈdɛmənˌstreɪts/
verb
举行对抗性示威；反示威
To demonstrate or protest against something that others are demonstrating or protesting in favor of.
Thousands of people counterdemonstrated against the proposed cuts to education. 数千人举行反示威，反对拟议中的教育削减。
The group counterdemonstrated against the far-right rally. 该团体举行了反示威，反对极右集会。
Synonyms: protest against, oppose, resist, object to
Antonyms: support, endorse, advocate for
词性: 动词
