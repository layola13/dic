fleeting:/ˈfliːtɪŋ/| adj.|短暂的，转瞬即逝的|passing swiftly; vanishing quickly|The moment of happiness was fleeting, but the memory remained. 幸福的时刻转瞬即逝，但记忆犹存。|The bird made a fleeting appearance in the garden. 那只鸟在花园里一闪而过。|近义词：brief, ephemeral, transient, momentary|反义词：lasting, enduring, permanent|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *fletyng, *fletand, present participle of fleten (“to float, flow, fly, flee”), from Old English flēotan (“to float, swim, sail”), from Proto-Germanic *fleutaną (“to float”), from Proto-Indo-European *plewd- (“to flow, float”). Cognate with Old High German flioȥan (“to flow”), Old Norse fljóta (“to float”). More at float, fleet.
