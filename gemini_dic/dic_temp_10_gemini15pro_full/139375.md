obsequies: /ˈɒbsɪkwɪz/ (UK) /ˈɑːbsəkwiz/ (US)| n. |葬礼，丧葬仪式|Funeral rites or ceremonies.  A formal expression for funeral services or ceremonies.|The king’s obsequies were elaborate and lasted for several days. 国王的葬礼非常隆重，持续了数天。|The family held private obsequies for their beloved grandmother. 家人为他们敬爱的祖母举行了私人葬礼。|Synonyms: funeral, burial, interment, last rites|Antonyms: birth, nativity|Noun
