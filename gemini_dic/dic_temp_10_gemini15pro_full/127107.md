nationalize: /ˈnæʃənəlaɪz/ | vt. | 国有化 | To transfer (a major branch of industry or commerce) from private to state ownership or control. | The government decided to nationalize the railways. 政府决定将铁路国有化。|  The government nationalized the mining industry. 政府将采矿业国有化了。 | 近义词: socialize, expropriate | 反义词: privatize | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From nation + -ize.  "Nation" comes from Old French "nacion" meaning "birth, rank, family, species, people, country", ultimately from Latin "natio" (birth). "-ize" is a suffix forming verbs meaning "to make into, convert into, subject to".
