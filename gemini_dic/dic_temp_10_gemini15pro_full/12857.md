assonant:/ˈæsənənt/ | adj. |  谐音的，音近的 | Containing or characterized by assonance; having vowels that sound alike. |  The poet used assonant words like "moon" and "June" to create a sense of calm. 诗人用“moon”和“June”等谐音词来营造一种平静的感觉。 | synonyms:  euphonious, harmonious, melodic, rhyming | antonyms: dissonant,  cacophonous | Adjective

assonance: /ˈæsənəns/ | n. |  谐音，母音韵 |  In poetry, the repetition of the sound of a vowel or diphthong in nonrhyming stressed syllables near enough to each other for the echo to be discernible (e.g., penitence, reticence ). | The use of assonance in the poem added to its musicality.  诗中谐音的运用增加了它的音乐性。 | synonyms: vowel rhyme,  near rhyme | antonyms: consonance | Noun
