natterjack:/ˈnætərˌdʒæk/| n.| 蟾蜍，滑舌蟾蜍|A small, warty toad (Epidalea calamita) found in sandy coastal areas of Europe. It is known for its loud, rasping call.| The natterjack toad is a protected species in the UK. 滑舌蟾蜍在英国是受保护的物种。|Synonyms: running toad, |Antonyms: N/A|noun
Etymology: From "natter" (meaning to chatter or jabber) + "jack" (a familiar term for a man or animal).  The name refers to the toad's distinctive, loud call.
