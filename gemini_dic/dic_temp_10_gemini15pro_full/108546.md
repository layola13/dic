lazily:/ˈleɪzɪli/| adv.|懒惰地，缓慢地|in a lazy manner; without much activity or effort|He stretched lazily and yawned. 他懒洋洋地伸了个懒腰，打了个哈欠。|She lazily flipped through the pages of the magazine. 她懒散地翻阅着杂志。|近义词：idly, sluggishly, indolently|反义词：energetically, diligently, actively|词性:副词

词源: From Middle English *laseli, *lasli (“lazily”), equivalent to lazy +‎ -ly.  Ultimately from Anglo-Norman *lase (“lazy”), from Old French lasche (“loose, slack, cowardly”), from Frankish *laisk (“lazy, weak”).
