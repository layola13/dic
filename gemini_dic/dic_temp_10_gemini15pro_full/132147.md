noncultures:/ˈnɒnˌkʌltʃəz/ /ˈnɑːnˌkʌltʃərz/
noun
非文化; 非培养物
Groups or collections that are specifically defined by their *lack* of shared cultural practices, beliefs, or values, often in contrast to dominant or recognized cultures.  This term is less common than "subcultures" or "countercultures" and often implies a more fragmented or less cohesive group.
Researchers studied the noncultures that emerged in online communities dedicated to specific, niche interests.  这些研究人员研究了在致力于特定、小众兴趣的在线社区中出现的非文化。
Nonconformists often form noncultures as a way to resist mainstream societal norms.  不遵守传统的人经常形成非文化，以此来抵制主流社会规范。
