meteorically:/ˌmiːtiˈɒrɪkli/ /ˌmiːtiˈɔːrɪkli/
adverb|迅速地，像流星一样地|Very rapidly, like a meteor.|Her career rose meteorically.|近义词：rapidly, swiftly, quickly, dramatically|反义词：slowly, gradually, steadily|词性: 副词

Etymology: From meteor + -ical + -ly.  "Meteor" comes from the Greek "meteōros," meaning "high in the air."
