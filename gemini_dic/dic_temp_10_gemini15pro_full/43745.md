creeper:/ˈkriːpər/ (UK) /ˈkriːpər/ (US)| n.| 爬行者；攀缘植物；匍匐植物；鬼鬼祟祟的人|A plant that grows along the ground, a wall, or other support.  A person who gives an impression of being slightly strange or unpleasant, especially one who tries to ingratiate themselves with someone in a way that is not sincere or open.|The ivy is a fast-growing creeper. 常春藤是一种生长迅速的攀缘植物。

He's a bit of a creeper, always hanging around the girls' dormitory. 他有点鬼鬼祟祟，总是徘徊在女生宿舍附近。|近义词：climber, crawler, sneaker, weirdo|反义词: (For plant) non-climbing plant, (For person)  extrovert|词性:noun
