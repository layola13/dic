gnomon:/ˈnoʊmɑːn/ (US) /ˈnəʊmɒn/ (UK)| n. |(几何学)剩下的图形|In geometry, a gnomon is the piece that remains when a similar shape is removed from a corner of a larger shape. 在几何学中，gnomon是指从较大形状的一个角移除一个相似形状后剩下的部分。|例句：The gnomon of a square is an L-shape. 正方形的gnomon是L形。|近义词: complement (in geometry)|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
