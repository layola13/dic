attendment:/əˈtendmənt/
n.
服务; 照顾; 伴随; 随从
The act of attending or accompanying someone or something.

例句：
The king's attendment included several loyal knights. 国王的随从包括几位忠诚的骑士。

She required constant medical attendment. 她需要持续的医疗照顾。


近义词：
service, care, attendance, accompaniment, retinue

反义词：
neglect, abandonment


词性: 名词


词源:
From the verb "attend" + suffix "-ment".  "Attend" comes from Old French "atendre"  (to wait for, expect, pay attention to), from Latin "attendere" (to stretch toward, give heed to).  The suffix "-ment" forms nouns from verbs and indicates the result or product of an action.
