counteracter:/ˌkaʊntərˈæktər/| n.|抵消剂，对抗者|Something that counteracts something else neutralizes or prevents its effect.|The medicine acted as a counteracter to the poison.|近义词：antidote, remedy, neutralizer, opponent, adversary|反义词：agonist, catalyst, enhancer|noun

counteract:/ˌkaʊntərˈækt/| v.|抵消，对抗，中和|To act against something in order to reduce its force or neutralize it.|The government took measures to counteract inflation.|近义词：neutralize, offset, negate, thwart, oppose|反义词：enhance, amplify, augment, promote, aid|verb
