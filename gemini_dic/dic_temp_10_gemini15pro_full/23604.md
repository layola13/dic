brame:/breɪm/| n. | 渴望, 渴望的事物, 嘶鸣声 |A loud, harsh cry, especially of a deer during the mating season; also, a yearning or longing. |The stag let out a loud brame across the glen. 雄鹿在峡谷中发出一声巨响。| 近义词: bellow, roar, yearn, longing | 反义词: silence, apathy | 词性: noun

brame:/breɪm/| v. | 渴望, 嘶鸣 |To utter a loud, harsh cry, especially of a deer during the mating season; also, to yearn or long for. |The deer bramed for its mate. 鹿为它的伴侣嘶鸣。| 近义词: bellow, roar, crave, desire | 反义词: whisper, ignore | 词性: verb
