ottos:/ˈɑːtəʊz/ (UK) /ˈɑːtoʊz/ (US)| n. |奥托(人名复数形式), 奥托发动机(复数)|A plural form of Otto, a male given name of German origin, or a shortened form referring to Nikolaus Otto, the German engineer who developed the four-stroke internal combustion engine.  It can also refer to multiple Otto engines.|My neighbors, the Ottos, are very friendly. 我的邻居奥托一家非常友好。

The museum displays several early Ottos.  博物馆展出几台早期的奥托发动机。|近义词：(For the name) No direct synonyms, as it's a proper noun. (For the engine) four-stroke engine|反义词: None|词性:Noun
