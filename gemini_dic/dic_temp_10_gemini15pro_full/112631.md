low-paid:/ˌloʊ ˈpeɪd/| adj.|低工资的，低收入的|Low-paid often describes jobs or workers who receive a small amount of money for their work.  It implies that the salary is not enough to live comfortably.|Many low-paid workers struggle to make ends meet. 许多低收入的工人难以维持生计。|Low-paid jobs are often physically demanding. 低收入的工作通常对体力要求很高。|Synonyms: poorly paid, underpaid, low-income, low-wage|Antonyms: high-paying, well-paid, high-income|adjective
