gabbiest:/ˈɡæbiɪst/
| adj.|最健谈的，最饶舌的|Superlative adjective of gabby: excessively talkative, especially on trivial matters.|She was the gabbiest person in the room, chattering nonstop about her new shoes.|近义词：loquacious, talkative, voluble, garrulous|反义词：reticent, taciturn, quiet, reserved|词性:形容词
词源: From gab + -iest (superlative suffix). Gab, meaning idle talk, is of uncertain origin, possibly imitative.
