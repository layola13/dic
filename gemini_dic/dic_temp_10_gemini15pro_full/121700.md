mispraise:/mɪsˈpreɪz/| n. vt.|诽谤，诋毁；错误地赞扬|An act of criticizing someone or something falsely or unjustly; undeserved blame or censure.  The act of praising someone or something incorrectly or inappropriately.|He heaped mispraise upon the innocent man. 他对这个无辜的人大加诽谤。|近义词：slander, defamation, calumny, denigration, disparagement, backbiting, vilification;  misjudge|反义词：praise, commend, laud, extol, acclaim|词性:名词, 动词
