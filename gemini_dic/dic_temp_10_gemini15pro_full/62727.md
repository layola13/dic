What does she want? = 她想要什么？

What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing here? = 他在这里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned? = 你学到了什么？
What has she eaten? = 她吃了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been reading lately? = 你最近一直在读什么？
What has he been working on all day? = 他一整天都在做什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加聚会穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had she said about the project? = 关于这个项目她说了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the interruption? =  被打断之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将会取得什么成就？
What will have changed by then? = 到那时会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been studying by the time you graduate? = 到你毕业的时候你将一直在学习什么？

虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
What if it rains tomorrow? = 如果明天下雨怎么办？

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是哪里？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
