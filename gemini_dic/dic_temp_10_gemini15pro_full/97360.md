infractible:/ɪnˈfræktɪbl/
adj.
不可违反的；不屈不挠的；倔强的
Not capable of being violated or infringed; unyielding; stubborn.
His infractible spirit helped him overcome many challenges. 他不屈不挠的精神帮助他克服了许多挑战。
近义词:inviolable, unbreakable, adamant, resolute, obstinate
反义词:flexible, yielding, compliant
词性:形容词

infractible:/ɪnˈfræktɪbl/
adj.
不碎的
Unbreakable
The infractible material was used to build the protective casing.  这种不易碎的材料被用来建造保护壳。
近义词:shatterproof, indestructible
反义词:fragile, breakable
词性:形容词


词源:
From Late Latin *infractibilis, from in- (“not”) + Latin fractus, past participle of frangere (“to break”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰreg- (“to break”).
