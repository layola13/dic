biradiate:/ˌbaɪˈreɪdieɪt/
adjective
双射的；有两条射线的
Having two rays or radiating parts.
The biradiate spicules of certain sponges provide structural support.  某些海绵的双射骨针提供结构支撑。
近义词:two-rayed, diactinal
反义词:multiradiate
词性:adjective

biradiate:/ˌbaɪˈreɪdieɪt/
noun
双射动物
An organism with two rays or radiating parts.
Certain types of protozoa are classified as biradiates. 某些类型的原生动物被归类为双射动物。
近义词:diactinal organism
反义词:multiradiate organism
词性:noun
