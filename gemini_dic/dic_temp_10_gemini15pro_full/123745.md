moonwalker:/ˈmuːnwɔːkər/| n.|月球漫步者；模仿迈克尔·杰克逊太空步的人|A person who performs the moonwalk, a dance move popularized by Michael Jackson.  It can also refer to someone who has walked on the moon.|He's a fantastic moonwalker; he really looks like Michael Jackson! 他是一位出色的月球漫步者，看起来真像迈克尔·杰克逊！|The Apollo astronauts are the most famous moonwalkers.  阿波罗号宇航员是最著名的月球漫步者。| astronaut, dancer| - |noun
