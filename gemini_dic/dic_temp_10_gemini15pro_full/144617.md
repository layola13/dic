outwiggle:/ˌaʊtˈwɪɡəl/
verb
胜过扭动；比…扭动得更厉害
To wiggle or wriggle more or better than someone or something else.
The toddler outwiggled his older sister in the dance contest, charming the judges with his energetic moves. 这个蹒跚学步的孩子在舞蹈比赛中比他姐姐扭动得更厉害，他充满活力的动作迷住了评委们。
He tried to hold the eel, but it outwiggled him and slipped away. 他试图抓住那条鳗鱼，但它扭动得比他厉害，溜走了。
Synonyms: outmaneuver, surpass, outperform
Antonyms: be outdone, lose to
词性:动词
