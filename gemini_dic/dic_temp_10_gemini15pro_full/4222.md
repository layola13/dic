airlift:/ˈɛərˌlɪft/| n. vt.|空运，空投；空运的物资|An airlift is the organized transport of supplies or people by aircraft, especially in an emergency.|The Berlin Airlift was a major operation to supply West Berlin during the Soviet blockade. 柏林空运是苏联封锁期间向西柏林提供物资的主要行动。|The government organized an airlift to evacuate refugees from the war zone. 政府组织空运撤离战区的难民。|近义词：air transport, air delivery|反义词: ground transport|名词|
