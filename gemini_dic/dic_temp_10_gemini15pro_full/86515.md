heisler:/ˈhaɪzlər/| n.|海斯勒图；海斯勒图表|A chart used in heat transfer calculations, particularly for transient conduction in simple shapes (like infinite slabs, infinite cylinders, and spheres). It provides a graphical solution for determining temperature distributions and heat transfer rates over time.|The engineer used a Heisler chart to determine the temperature at the center of the steel rod after one hour of heating. 工程师使用海斯勒图来确定钢棒加热一小时后中心处的温度。|近义词:temperature chart, heat transfer chart|反义词: N/A|noun
