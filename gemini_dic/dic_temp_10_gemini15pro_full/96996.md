infantlike:/ˈɪnfəntlaɪk/| adj.|婴儿般的，幼稚的|Resembling or characteristic of an infant, especially in behavior or appearance.| Her infantlike innocence charmed everyone. 她婴儿般的纯真迷住了所有人。|近义词：childlike, babyish, infantile|反义词：mature, adult, grown-up|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From infant + -like.  "Infant" comes from the Latin "infans," meaning "unable to speak," from "in-" (not) + "fari" (to speak). "-like" is a suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
