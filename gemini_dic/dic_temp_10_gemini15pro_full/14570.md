aviary:/ˈeɪviəri/ (US), /ˈeɪvɪəri/ (UK)| n. |鸟舍，鸟类饲养场|A place, such as a large cage or enclosure, for keeping birds.|We visited the aviary at the zoo and saw many colorful parrots. 我们参观了动物园的鸟舍，看到了许多色彩艳丽的鹦鹉。|近义词：birdhouse, cage, enclosure|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "open sky" figuratively)|Noun
