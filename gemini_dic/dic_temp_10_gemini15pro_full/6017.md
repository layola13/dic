ameliorable:/əˈmiːliərəbl/
adjective
可改善的，可改进的
capable of being made better or improved.
The patient's condition is ameliorable with proper treatment.  病人的病情通过适当的治疗可以得到改善。
The economic situation in the country is ameliorable.  该国的经济状况是可以改善的。

近义词:improvable, amendable, reformable, betterable
反义词:incurable, irremediable, unimprovable
词性:adjective
