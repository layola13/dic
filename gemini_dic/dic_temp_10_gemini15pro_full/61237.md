emailing:/ˈiːmeɪlɪŋ/
verb
发电子邮件，用电子邮件发送
the act of sending messages by email
例句：I was emailing my friend when the power went out. 我正在给我的朋友发邮件时停电了。

例句：She spends hours emailing clients every day. 她每天花几个小时给客户发电子邮件。

近义词: messaging, corresponding, communicating
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "calling" or "meeting in person")
词性: verb (present participle of "email")
