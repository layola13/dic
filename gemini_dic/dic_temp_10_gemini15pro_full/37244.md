comfortment:/ˈkʌmfətmənt/ | n. |安慰；舒适；慰藉|The act of comforting or the state of being comforted.  A feeling of relief or encouragement. |She found comfortment in the support of her friends. 她从朋友的支持中找到了慰藉。| 近义词: comfort, solace, consolation | 反义词: distress, discomfort, affliction | 词性: 名词

词源:  From the verb "comfort" + suffix "-ment" which forms nouns of action or result.  Ultimately from Old French "confort".
