limbos:/ˈlɪmboʊz/
n.
边缘状态；不确定状态；被遗忘的状态
a situation in which you are not certain what to do next, or in which you are not able to do anything
The project is in limbo until we get more funding.  该项目在获得更多资金之前处于搁置状态。

近义词:uncertainty, oblivion, abeyance
反义词:certainty, clarity, resolution
词性:名词

limbos:/ˈlɪmboʊz/
n.
（加勒比宗教）灵魂滞留地；地狱边境
(in some Caribbean religions) a place where souls go after death if they are not good enough to go to heaven, or if they have not been baptized
Many believed their souls would reside in limbo. 许多人相信他们的灵魂将滞留在阴间。

近义词:purgatory
反义词:heaven, paradise
词性:名词
