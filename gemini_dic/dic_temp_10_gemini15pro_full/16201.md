banner-ads:/ˈbænər ædz/ | n. | 横幅广告 |  A graphical web advertising unit, typically rectangular in shape, displayed across the top or bottom of a website or within the content of a webpage. |  Example 1: The website generates revenue through banner-ads placed strategically throughout its pages.  网站通过在其页面中策略性地放置横幅广告来产生收益。

Example 2:  Clicking on the banner-ads can take you to the advertiser's website. 点击横幅广告可以带您进入广告商的网站。 | 近义词: display ads, web banners, online ads | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially "print ads" as a contrasting medium) | 词性: noun
