deselectable:/ˌdiːsəˈlɛktəbəl/
adjective
可取消选择的，可反选的
able to be deselected.  Something that can be reversed or unchosen after having been selected.
The items in the list are deselectable.  列表中的项目可以取消选择。
The checkbox allows you to make the option deselectable.  该复选框允许您取消选择该选项。
近义词: reversible, unselectable, cancellable
反义词: selectable, non-reversible
词性: adjective
