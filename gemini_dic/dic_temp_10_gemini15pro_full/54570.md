disloyal:/dɪsˈlɔɪəl/
adjective
不忠的，不忠诚的
Not loyal or faithful; betraying trust.
He was accused of being disloyal to his country. 他被指控不忠于自己的国家。

She felt disloyal talking about her friend behind her back.  她在背后谈论朋友，觉得对朋友不忠。


近义词: faithless, treacherous, unfaithful, untrue
反义词: loyal, faithful, true, devoted
词性: adjective
