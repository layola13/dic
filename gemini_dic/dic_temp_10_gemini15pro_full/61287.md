embalmment:/ɪmˈbɑːmənt/| n.|防腐处理，尸体保存|The embalmment of the pharaoh was a complex ritual. 法老的防腐处理是一个复杂的仪式。|The process of preserving a body from decay, especially after death, by treating it with preservatives.|例句：The embalmment prevented the body from decaying. 防腐处理防止了尸体腐烂。

例句：His family chose embalmment so that they could have an open-casket funeral.  他的家人选择了防腐处理以便他们可以举行一个开放式棺材的葬礼。

近义词：preservation, mummification|反义词:decay, decomposition|词性:名词
