desmidian:/dɛzˈmɪdiən/
adjective|鼓藻类的，鼓藻目植物的|Relating to or belonging to the Desmidiaceae, a family of single-celled freshwater green algae characterized by their symmetrical, often elaborately sculpted cell walls.|Desmidian algae are commonly found in clean, acidic waters. 鼓藻类通常生长在干净的酸性水中。|
noun|鼓藻|A single-celled freshwater green alga belonging to the family Desmidiaceae.|The desmidian exhibited a beautiful star-shaped structure.  这个鼓藻呈现出美丽的星形结构。|
synonym|none (highly specific)|
antonym|none (highly specific)|
etymology|From Desmidiaceae + -ian. Desmidiaceae is derived from Desmidium, the type genus of the family,  from Greek *desmos* "bond, chain" + Latin *-ium* (suffix forming nouns).|
