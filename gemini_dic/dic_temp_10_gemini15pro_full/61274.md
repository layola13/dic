emasculative:/ɪˈmæskjʊˌleɪtɪv/
adjective
使丧失男子气概的；使柔弱的
Depriving a man of his male role or identity; making someone weaker or less effective
例句：
He felt emasculated by being laid off from his job.
他因为被解雇而感到失去了男子气概。

例句：
The constant criticism from his boss was emasculating.
老板不断的批评使他感到很无力。

近义词：weakening, debilitating, unmanning
反义词：empowering, strengthening
词性：adjective
