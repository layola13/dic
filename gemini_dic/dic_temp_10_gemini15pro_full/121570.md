misnavigating: /ˌmɪsnævɪɡeɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 错误导航，导航错误 | Navigating incorrectly, failing to reach a destination due to errors in navigation.  | Example: Misnavigating through the dense forest, they ended up lost and disoriented.  他们在茂密的森林里导航错误，最终迷失了方向。

Example:  The ship was misnavigating in the storm and ran aground.  这艘船在暴风雨中航行错误，搁浅了. | 近义词: getting lost, going astray, taking a wrong turn | 反义词: navigating correctly, finding one's way | 词性: 动词 (present participle)
