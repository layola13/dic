moratorium:/ˌmɔːrəˈtɔːriəm/ /ˌmɔːrəˈtɔːriəm/ | n. | 暂停，中止，延期 | A temporary prohibition of an activity. | The government imposed a moratorium on commercial whaling. 政府暂停了商业捕鲸。|
近义词：suspension, postponement, delay, halt, freeze | 反义词：resumption, continuation, commencement | 词性：名词

moratorium:/ˌmɔːrəˈtɔːriəm/ /ˌmɔːrəˈtɔːriəm/ | n. | 暂停，中止，延期 | A legally authorized period of delay in the performance of a legal obligation or the payment of a debt. | The bank declared a moratorium on loan repayments for businesses affected by the flood. 银行宣布对受洪水影响的企业暂停偿还贷款。|
近义词：reprieve, grace period | 反义词：enforcement, demand | 词性：名词
