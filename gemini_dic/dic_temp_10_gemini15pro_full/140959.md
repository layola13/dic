one-to-one:/ˌwʌn tə ˈwʌn/ | adj.|一对一的，一一对应的|A one-to-one relationship or correspondence involves two people or things that relate to each other individually, exclusively, or specifically.|Each child has one-to-one tuition with a specialist teacher. 每个孩子都由一位专业教师进行一对一教学。|Synonyms: individual, private, single|Antonyms: group, collective|adjective

one-to-one:/ˌwʌn tə ˈwʌn/ | n.|一对一的关系；一对一的会议|A meeting or relationship between just two people.|I had a one-to-one with my boss. 我和我的老板进行了一对一的会谈。|Synonyms: private meeting, individual session|Antonyms: group meeting, conference|noun
