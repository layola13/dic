alterer:/ˈɔːltərər/| n.|修改者，变更者|A person who alters something; someone who makes changes or modifications.|The alterer of the document was never identified. 修改文件的人始终未被确认。|近义词：modifier, changer, reviser|反义词：preserver, maintainer|词性: noun

Etymology:
From the verb "alter" + "-er" (suffix forming agent nouns).  "Alter" comes from Old French "alterer"  and ultimately from Latin "alterare" (to change), from "alter" (other).
