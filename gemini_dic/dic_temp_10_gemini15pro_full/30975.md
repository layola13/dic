chalcocite:/ˈkælkəˌsaɪt/| n. |辉铜矿|A dark, metallic sulfide mineral (Cu2S) that is an important ore of copper.|Chalcocite is a valuable ore due to its high copper content. 辉铜矿因其高铜含量而是一种有价值的矿石。|
Chalcocite is often found in secondary enrichment zones. 辉铜矿常在次生富集带中发现。|Synonyms: copper glance, redruthite|Antonyms: N/A|noun
