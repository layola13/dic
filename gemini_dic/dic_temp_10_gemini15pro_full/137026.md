nonstatements:/nɒnˈsteɪtmənts/
noun
非陈述, 不表态
Expressions that do not assert or deny anything.  They can be questions, exclamations, commands, or expressions of emotion.
Her response was a series of nonstatements, leaving me more confused than before. 她的回答是一系列不表态，让我比以前更困惑了。
Synonyms: equivocations, ambiguities
Antonyms: statements, assertions
词性: 名词
