briolette:/briːəˈlɛt/
noun
长方钻; 梨形宝石
a pear-shaped gemstone or bead, especially a diamond, having triangular or rectangular facets
She wore a stunning briolette diamond pendant. 她戴着一个令人惊艳的梨形钻石吊坠。
synonyms: drop, pendant, teardrop
antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a shape.  Could consider opposites in terms of cut style, like "cabochon" which is a smooth, unfaceted cut.)
