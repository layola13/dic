over-imports:/ˈoʊvərˌɪmpɔːrts/ | n. | 过度进口；进口过剩 | The country's over-imports have led to a trade deficit.  这个国家的过度进口导致了贸易逆差。 |  Excessive imports, importing more than a country needs or can sustain.  进口过多，超过一个国家的需求或承受能力。|  The over-imports of cheap goods hurt domestic producers. 廉价商品的过度进口损害了国内生产商的利益。| oversupply, surplus |  under-imports, shortage | noun
