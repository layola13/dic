broadsiding:/ˈbrɔːdsaɪdɪŋ/| n. v.| 猛烈抨击，侧击，用舷侧炮轰击 |The candidate delivered a broadsiding against his opponent.  候选人对他的对手进行了猛烈抨击。|  The ship was broadsiding the enemy vessel. 这艘船正用舷侧炮轰击敌舰。|近义词：attacking, criticizing, lambasting,  assaulting |反义词: praising, supporting, defending|词性:名词，动词

Etymology: From "broadside," referring to the simultaneous firing of all guns on one side of a warship.  源自“broadside”，指的是战舰一侧所有火炮的同时开火。
