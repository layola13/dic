bargained:/ˈbɑːrɡɪnd/| v.|讨价还价，商定，预料，指望|The union bargained for better wages. 工会为争取更好的工资而讨价还价。|近义词：negotiated, haggled, dealt|反义词：accepted, conceded|词性:动词

Etymology: From Middle English *bargaynen, from Old French bargaigner (“to haggle, bargain”), of uncertain origin, probably from Vulgar Latin *barcaniare.
