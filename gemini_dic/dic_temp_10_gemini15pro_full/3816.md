agnostic:/æɡˈnɑːstɪk/ | n.  adj. | 不可知论者；不可知论的 | A person who believes that nothing is known or can be known of the existence or nature of God or of anything beyond material phenomena; relating to agnostics or agnosticism. |  Many scientists are agnostic. 许多科学家是不可知论者。|  Synonym: doubter, skeptic, unbeliever | Antonym: believer, devotee | Noun/Adjective
