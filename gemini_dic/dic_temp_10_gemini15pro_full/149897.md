palisading:/ˌpælɪˈseɪdɪŋ/ | n. | 栅栏；栅栏状排列；栅状排列 | A fence made of pointed wooden stakes or iron railings fixed in the ground, forming a defense against attack. Also, an arrangement resembling a palisade, such as columnar basalt formations. | The Roman fort was surrounded by a sturdy palisading. 罗马堡垒周围环绕着坚固的栅栏。|近义词：fence, barrier, barricade, stockade |反义词: opening, gap
