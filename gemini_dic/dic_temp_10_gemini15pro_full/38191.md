compulsory:/kəmˈpʌlsəri/ | adj. | 必须的，强制的，义务的 | Required by law or a rule; obligatory. |  Attendance at school is compulsory for children of certain ages.  某些年龄段的儿童必须上学。| synonyms: mandatory, obligatory, required, necessary | antonyms: optional, voluntary, elective | adjective

compulsory:/kəmˈpʌlsəri/ | adj. | 必修的 | Forming a required part of a course of study. | Several compulsory courses must be taken to obtain this degree.  要获得这个学位，必须修几门必修课。| synonyms: required, core, mandatory | antonyms: elective, optional | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *compulsorius, from Latin compulsus, past participle of compellere "to compel."
