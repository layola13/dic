paleocrystic:/ˌpeɪliːoʊˈkrɪstɪk/ | adj. | 古冰的，多年冰的 |  Relating to or denoting very old and thick sea ice, persisting for several years or more. | The paleocrystic sea ice is a harsh environment for marine life.  古冰海冰是海洋生物的恶劣环境。| Example 2: Scientists study paleocrystic ice to understand past climates. 科学家研究古冰以了解过去的气候。 | Synonym: multiyear ice, polar ice | Antonym: young ice, first-year ice | adjective
