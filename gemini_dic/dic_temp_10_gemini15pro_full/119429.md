micrographic: /ˌmaɪkrəˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
微图形的;显微照相的
Relating to or involving micrographs; made by means of a micrograph.
The researchers used micrographic techniques to study the structure of the metal. 研究人员使用显微照相技术来研究金属的结构。
Micrographic images revealed the intricate details of the insect's wings. 显微图像揭示了昆虫翅膀的复杂细节。
近义词: microscopic, minute
反义词: macroscopic, large
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From micro- (small) + -graphic (relating to writing or drawing).  Ultimately from Greek *mikros* (small) and *graphein* (to write).
