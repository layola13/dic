ecotopia:/iːˈkoʊtəʊpiə/ | n. | 生态乌托邦；理想的生态环境 | An imagined place or state of things in which the environment is ideal or perfect. |  例句：The author describes an ecotopia where humans live in harmony with nature.  作者描述了一个人类与自然和谐相处的生态乌托邦。

例句：She dreams of creating an ecotopia powered by renewable energy. 她梦想创造一个由可再生能源驱动的生态乌托邦。 | 近义词：utopia, paradise, ideal world | 反义词：dystopia | 词性: noun
