demisemiquaver:/ˌdɛmiˈsɛmiˌkweɪvər/ /ˌdɛmiˈsɛmiˌkweɪvər/ | n. | 三十二分音符 |A musical note with a time value equal to one thirty-second of a whole note.  It is represented by a filled-in notehead with three flags or beams on the stem.| The composer used a flurry of demisemiquavers in the cadenza. 作曲家在华彩乐段中使用了一连串的三十二分音符。| synonym: thirty-second note | antonym:  breve (relatively) | noun
