favorless:/ˈfeɪvərləs/
adjective
无味的；平淡的；不受欢迎的
Lacking flavor; bland; not popular or well-liked.
The soup was disappointingly favorless. 这汤淡而无味，令人失望。
近义词:bland, insipid, tasteless, unappetizing, unpopular
反义词:flavorful, tasty, delicious, savory, popular
词性:adjective

词源:
From favor + -less.  "Favor" in this context originally meant "taste" or "savor," deriving from Old French "faveur." The suffix "-less" indicates the absence of something.
