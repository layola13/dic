cyclonic:/saɪˈklɒnɪk/ (US) /saɪˈklɑːnɪk/ (UK)| adj.|旋风的；气旋的；暴风雨般的|Of or relating to a cyclone; rotating with a low-pressure center.|例句：The cyclonic winds caused widespread damage. 气旋风造成了大范围的破坏。

例句：The weather forecast predicted cyclonic conditions in the region. 天气预报预测该地区将出现气旋状况。

近义词：stormy, tempestuous, turbulent|反义词：calm, peaceful|词性:形容词
