deregulator:/diːˈreɡjʊleɪtər/ | n. |  撤销管制者，解除管制者 |  A person or entity that removes regulations or controls, typically in a particular industry or sector. | The government appointed a deregulator to oversee the liberalization of the telecommunications market. 政府任命了一名撤销管制者来监督电信市场的自由化。 | synonym: liberalizer, reformer | antonym: regulator, controller | noun
