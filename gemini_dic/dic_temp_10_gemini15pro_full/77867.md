genethliatic:/ˌdʒɛnəˈθlɪætɪk/
adjective
基因星盘的；算命的；占星术的
Relating to genethliacal astrology; astrological.  Specifically, pertaining to the casting of horoscopes based on the time of birth.
Genethliatic astrology was popular in ancient Greece.  基因星盘占星术在古希腊很流行。
Genethliatic charts can offer insights into personality. 基因星盘图可以提供对个性的洞察。
Near Synonyms: astrological, natal, horoscopic
Antonyms: non-astrological, scientific
