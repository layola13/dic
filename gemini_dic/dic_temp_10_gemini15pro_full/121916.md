misses:/ˈmɪsɪz/ | n. v. (third-person singular simple present)| 小姐们; 未击中; 未赶上; 想念 |  As a noun, it's the plural of "miss," a title for an unmarried woman. As a verb, it's the third-person singular present tense of "miss," meaning to fail to hit, reach, catch, or experience something. | Example (noun): The misses enjoyed the party. 小姐们在派对上玩得很开心。

Example (verb): He misses the train every morning. 他每天早上都赶不上火车。 | Near synonyms (verb): overlooks, lacks, yearns for | Near antonyms (verb): hits, catches, finds | Noun, Verb
