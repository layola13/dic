nuphar:/ˈnjuːfɑːr/ (US), /ˈnjuːfɑː(r)/ (UK)| n. |睡莲；萍蓬草属植物|A genus of aquatic plants with floating leaves and yellow flowers, commonly known as water lilies or pond lilies.  This genus belongs to the Nymphaeaceae family.| The yellow nuphar bloomed brightly in the pond.  池塘里黄色的萍蓬草开得很鲜艳。|近义词：water lily, pond lily, spatterdock, cow lily|反义词: N/A|名词
