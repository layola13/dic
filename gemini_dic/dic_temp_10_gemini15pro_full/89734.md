hopis:/ˈhoʊpiːz/| n.|霍皮族人|A Native American tribe primarily inhabiting northeastern Arizona.|The Hopis are known for their distinctive pottery and kachina dolls. 霍皮族人以其独特的陶器和卡奇纳玩偶而闻名。|近义词:Pueblo people|反义词:None|词性:noun

词源:From the Hopi word Hopisinom, meaning "peaceful people" or "the peaceful ones".
