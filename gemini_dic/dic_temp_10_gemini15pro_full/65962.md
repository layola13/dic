exaggerate: /ɪɡˈzædʒəreɪt/ | v. | 夸大，夸张 | To represent something as larger, better, or worse than it really is. | Don't exaggerate – it wasn't that bad.  不要夸大其词——其实没那么糟。 | overstate, amplify, embellish, inflate | understate, downplay, minimize | verb
