dramatized:/ˈdræməˌtaɪzd/| v. |戏剧化；改编成戏剧|to present a story or event in a dramatic way, often by making it seem more exciting or important than it really is|The author dramatized his experiences in the war in his latest novel.  作者在他最新的小说中戏剧化了他战争中的经历。

The life of Nelson Mandela has been dramatized in several films.  纳尔逊·曼德拉的一生已被改编成几部电影。|近义词：adapted, staged, theatricalized, exaggerated|反义词：understated, minimized, downplayed|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From drama (n.) + -tize + -ed.  Drama comes from the Greek "δρᾶμα" (drama), meaning "action, deed, play."
