coed:/ˌkoʊˈɛd/| n. |女大学生；男女同校的学生 (尤指女生)|A female student at a coeducational institution.  It can also refer to students of both sexes at a coeducational institution.|例句：The coeds were excited about the upcoming dance. 女大学生们对即将到来的舞会感到兴奋。

coed:/ˌkoʊˈɛd/| adj. |男女同校的|Relating to or involving both male and female students.|例句：The university became coed in the 1970s. 这所大学在20世纪70年代开始实行男女同校。
