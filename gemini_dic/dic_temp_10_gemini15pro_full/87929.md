hieronymite:/ˈhaɪərənɪˌmaɪt/ | n. |修道士；隐士 (尤指圣哲罗姆派的) | A member of a religious order founded by St. Jerome in the late 4th century, known for their scholarship and asceticism. |  The hieronymite spent his days in prayer and study.  这位修道士终日祈祷和学习。 | synonyms: monk, ascetic, hermit | antonyms: worldling, hedonist | noun

hieronymite:/ˈhaɪərənɪˌmaɪt/ | adj. |圣哲罗姆派的；隐士般的 | Relating to or characteristic of St. Jerome or the religious order he founded. | The hieronymite monastery was renowned for its library. 这座圣哲罗姆派的修道院以其图书馆而闻名。| synonyms: monastic, ascetic, hermitical | antonyms: secular, worldly | adjective
