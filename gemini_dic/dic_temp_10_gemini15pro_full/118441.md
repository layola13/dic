mesamoeboid:/ˌmɛsəˈmiːbɔɪd/
adjective
中变形虫样的；间质变形虫样的
Resembling or characteristic of a mesamoeba, a type of amoeba that inhabits the mesohyl (the gelatinous matrix) of sponges.  Specifically, refers to amoeboid cells found within the mesohyl of sponges.
Mesamoeboid cells are found in the mesohyl of some sponges.  中变形虫样细胞存在于一些海绵的间质中。
The researcher identified a mesamoeboid cell migrating through the sponge tissue. 研究人员发现了一个中变形虫样细胞在海绵组织中迁移。
近义词: amoeboid, archaeocyte (though not strictly synonymous, they relate to similar cell types in sponges)
反义词: N/A (Difficult to provide a true antonym for a descriptive biological term.)
词性: adjective

词源:
"meso-" (Greek: μέσος, mésos) meaning "middle" + "-amoeba" (Greek: ἀμοιβή, amoibe, meaning "change") + "-oid" (Greek: -ειδής, -eidēs, meaning "resembling," "having the form of").  Therefore, "mesamoeboid" refers to something resembling an amoeba found in a middle layer or matrix.
