herpetotomy:/ˌhɜːrpɪˈtɒtəmi/ | n. |爬行动物解剖学；爬行动物外科手术| The study of the dissection of reptiles and amphibians, or surgical procedures performed on them.  It involves the examination of their internal organs and systems.|  Herpetotomy is a specialized field requiring detailed knowledge of reptilian and amphibian anatomy. 爬行动物解剖学是一门需要爬行动物和两栖动物解剖学详细知识的专业领域。|  The veterinarian performed herpetotomy on the injured snake. 兽医对受伤的蛇进行了外科手术。 |Synonyms:  reptile surgery, amphibian surgery|Antonyms: None|noun
