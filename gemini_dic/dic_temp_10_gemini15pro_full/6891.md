anchoret:/ˈæŋkərɪt/| n.|隐士，遁世者|A person who lives in seclusion, typically for religious reasons; a hermit.|The old anchoret lived in a cave in the mountains.  这位老隐士住在山里的一个洞穴里。|近义词：hermit, recluse, ascetic|反义词：socialite, extrovert|词性: 名词

词源: From Old French *ancrete, feminine of ancret, from Late Latin *ancreta, from Greek anachōrētēs "one who has retired, recluse," from ana- "back, up" + chōrein "to withdraw, give way."
