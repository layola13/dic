deliquescent:/ˌdɛlɪˈkwɛsənt/| adj.|潮解的，易潮解的|Becoming liquid by absorbing moisture from the air.|The deliquescent nature of calcium chloride makes it useful as a desiccant. 氯化钙的潮解性使其可用作干燥剂。|Synonyms: hygroscopic, liquefiable|Antonyms: non-hygroscopic, anhydrous|adjective
