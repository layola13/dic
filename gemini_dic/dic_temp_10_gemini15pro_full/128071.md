nervier:/ˈnɜːrviər/ /ˈnɝːviər/| adj.|更紧张的，更神经质的|More nervous or anxious than before or than someone else. |例句：He seemed nervier than usual before the big game.  他在大赛前似乎比平时更紧张。

例句：The audience grew nervier as the magician's trick became more dangerous. 随着魔术师的戏法变得越来越危险，观众越来越紧张。


近义词：more anxious, more tense, edgier, jumpier|反义词:calmer, more relaxed, less anxious|词性: 形容词 (Comparative adjective)
