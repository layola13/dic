hyperintelligence:/ˌhaɪpərɪnˈtelɪdʒəns/ | n. | 超级智能，超智力 |  A level of intelligence significantly surpassing that of the brightest and most gifted human minds. This can involve advanced cognitive abilities, rapid learning, exceptional problem-solving skills, and potentially even self-awareness and consciousness in artificial systems. |  Scientists are debating the potential risks and benefits of hyperintelligence. 科学家们正在讨论超级智能的潜在风险和益处。|  With the rapid advancement of AI, hyperintelligence might not be a distant future. 随着人工智能的快速发展，超级智能或许不再是遥远的未来。| 近义词：superintelligence, ultra-intelligence | 反义词: unintelligence, stupidity | 词性: noun
