oppositionless:/ˌɒpəˈzɪʃənləs/
adj.
无反对的，没有反对的
Lacking any opposition; unopposed.
The bill passed oppositionless through the parliament.  该法案在议会毫无异议地通过。
近义词: unopposed, undisputed, unchallenged
反义词: opposed, contested, challenged
词性: adjective

词源:
From "opposition" + "-less".  "Opposition" comes from the Old French "oposicion" and ultimately from the Latin "oppositio", meaning "a setting against".  The suffix "-less" is of Old English origin and indicates a lack or absence of something.
