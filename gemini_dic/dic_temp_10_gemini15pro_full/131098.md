| Example: He led a nonbibulous life, preferring tea to stronger beverages.  他过着不饮酒的生活，喜欢喝茶而不是烈性饮料。

| Synonyms:  abstinent, teetotal, sober, temperate | Antonyms: bibulous, alcoholic, inebriated, drunken | word property: adjective
