pantisocratist:/ˌpæntiˈsɒkrətɪst/ | n. | 平权主义者；主张两性平等者 | A believer in or advocate of pantisocracy (a utopian system of government in which all are equal and share equally in the administration of the state). | Example: The pantisocratists dreamt of a society free from the constraints of traditional hierarchy.  平权主义者梦想着一个摆脱传统等级制度束缚的社会。

Example:  She was a fervent pantisocratist, dedicated to achieving true equality for all. 她是一位热情的平权主义者，致力于实现所有人的真正平等。 | Synonym: egalitarian, feminist (in some contexts) | Antonym: hierarch, elitist | noun


Etymology:  From Greek *pan* (all) + *isos* (equal) + *-kratia* (rule, power).  源自希腊语 *pan* (所有) + *isos* (平等) + *-kratia* (统治，权力)。
