obstructionary:/əbˈstrʌkʃənəri/
|adj.|
|阻碍的，妨碍的|
|Relating to or causing obstruction; hindering.  Characterized by or prone to obstruction.|
|The obstructionary tactics of the opposition party delayed the passage of the bill. 反对党的阻碍策略拖延了该法案的通过。|

|The patient's obstructionary sleep apnea was causing him significant health problems.  病人的阻塞性睡眠呼吸暂停给他带来了严重的健康问题。|

|近义词：obstructive, hindering, impeding|
|反义词：helpful, facilitating, aiding|
|词性: 形容词|
