bibliotheca:/ˌbɪbliəˈθiːkə/
n.
library, collection of books
A building or room containing collections of books, periodicals, and sometimes films and recorded music for people to read, borrow, or refer to.
The bibliotheca at Alexandria was one of the greatest of the ancient world.  亚历山大图书馆是古代世界最伟大的图书馆之一。
The bibliotheca holds a vast collection of medieval manuscripts.  这个图书馆收藏了大量的中世纪手稿。
近义词:library, archive
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑表示"知识匮乏"的词，例如: ignorance)
词性: noun
