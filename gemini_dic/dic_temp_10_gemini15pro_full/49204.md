delay:/dɪˈleɪ/| n. v.|延误，延迟；耽搁|a period of time by which something is late or postponed|The flight was delayed due to bad weather. 由于天气恶劣，航班延误了。|近义词：postponement, deferral, hold-up|反义词:advancement, expedition|noun
delays:/dɪˈleɪz/| n.|延误，延迟；耽搁（delay的复数形式）|periods of time by which something is late or postponed|The project suffered numerous delays. 该项目遭受了多次延误。|近义词：postponements, deferrals, hold-ups|反义词:advancements, expeditions|noun
delay:/dɪˈleɪ/| v.|延误，延迟；耽搁|to make something happen at a later time than planned|Heavy traffic delayed my arrival. 交通拥堵延误了我的到达。|近义词：postpone, defer, hold up|反义词:advance, expedite|verb
