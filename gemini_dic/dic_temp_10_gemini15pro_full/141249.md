opaque:/oʊˈpeɪk/| adj.|不透明的，浑浊的，难懂的|Not able to be seen through; not transparent.|例句：The windows were opaque with steam. 窗户上满是蒸汽，变得不透明了。|例句：His explanation was opaque to me. 他的解释让我一头雾水。|近义词：impenetrable, nontransparent, obscure|反义词:transparent, clear, translucent|词性:形容词

opacous:/oʊˈpeɪkəs/| adj.|不透明的，浑浊的，难懂的|(Rarely used.  Opaque is generally preferred.) Not able to be seen through; not transparent.|例句：The liquid was thick and opacous.  这种液体又浓又浑浊。|例句：The artist used opacous paints to create a textured effect. 艺术家使用不透明的颜料来创造纹理效果。|近义词：impenetrable, nontransparent, obscure|反义词:transparent, clear, translucent|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *opacus* "shaded, dark, obscure," from *ob* "in front of, against" + root related to Greek *skepein* "to cover, to look at."
