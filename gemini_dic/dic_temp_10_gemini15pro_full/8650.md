anticonservation:/ˌæntiˌkɑːnsərˈveɪʃən/ |n.| 反保育，反保护 |Opposition to environmental conservation or preservation.  It often involves advocating for the exploitation of natural resources for economic gain, regardless of the ecological impact.|  The mining company's anticonservation stance led to protests from environmental groups. 矿业公司的反保育立场引发了环保组织的抗议。|  The politician's anticonservation policies were criticized by scientists.  这位政治家的反保育政策受到了科学家的批评。| 近义词: exploitation, unsustainable development | 反义词: conservation, preservation | 词性: 名词
