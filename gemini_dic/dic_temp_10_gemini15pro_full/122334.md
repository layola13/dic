词源:  From mixable + -ness.  "Mixable" comes from "mix" (from Old French "mischier," from Frankish "miskjan," meaning "to mix") + "-able" (suffix meaning "capable of being"). "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality.
