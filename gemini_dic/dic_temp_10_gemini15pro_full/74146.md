founderous: /ˈfaʊndərəs/
adjective
易崩溃的，不牢固的；不稳定的
liable to founder; unstable.
The old bridge is founderous and needs to be replaced.  这座老桥摇摇欲坠，需要更换。
The company's finances are in a founderous state.  这家公司的财务状况岌岌可危。
近义词: shaky, unstable, precarious, rickety, tottering, ramshackle
反义词: stable, secure, solid, firm
词性: adjective
词源: From the verb "founder," meaning "to sink or collapse," ultimately from Old French "fondrer" (to melt, dissolve).
