dictatory:/ˈdɪktəˌtɔːri/ /ˌdɪktəˈtɔri/ | adj.|独裁的，专横的|Characterized by or resembling a dictator or dictatorship; autocratic; imperious.|The king's dictatory rule led to widespread unrest. 国王的独裁统治导致了广泛的动荡。|The principal's dictatory approach to discipline alienated the students. 校长专横的纪律管理方式使学生们疏远了他。|近义词：autocratic, tyrannical, despotic, authoritarian, imperious|反义词：democratic, liberal, permissive|词性:形容词


Etymology:
From Late Latin *dictatorius, from Latin dictator (“dictator”).
