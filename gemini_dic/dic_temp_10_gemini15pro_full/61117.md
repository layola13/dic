elohistic: /ˌɛloʊˈhɪstɪk/
adjective
エロヒスト的；属于エロヒム派的
Relating to the Elohist, one of the hypothesized sources of the Pentateuch (the first five books of the Bible), characterized by the use of Elohim as the name of God.
The elohistic source is believed to have been written in the northern kingdom of Israel.  エロヒストの文献はイスラエルの北王国で書かれたと考えられています。
Scholars have identified several elohistic passages in Genesis. 学者たちは創世記の中にエロヒストの箇所をいくつか特定している。
近义词: Elohim-using, priestly (in some contexts)
反义词: Yahwistic
词性: adjective
