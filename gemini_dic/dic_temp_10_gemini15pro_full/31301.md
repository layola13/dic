chaplainship: /ˈtʃæplɪnʃɪp/
n.
牧师的职位；牧师的任期
The position or term of office of a chaplain.
He accepted the chaplainship of the college. 他接受了学院牧师的职位。
近义词: chaplaincy
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: noun


Etymology:
From chaplain + -ship.  "Chaplain" comes from Late Latin *capellanus, from capella "chapel," ultimately from Late Latin cappa "cloak, cope."  "-ship" is an Old English suffix denoting "state, condition, office, dignity, skill."
