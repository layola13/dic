palinodial:/ˌpælɪˈnəʊdiəl/| adj. n.| 撤回的，翻供的；撤回声明，翻供|A palinodial is a statement that retracts or reverses a previous statement; a recantation.  It can be an adjective describing such a statement, or a noun referring to the statement itself.|The politician issued a palinodial, retracting his earlier inflammatory remarks.  这位政治家发表了翻供声明，撤回了此前煽动性的言论。

The governor's palinodial speech surprised everyone. 州长的翻供演讲让所有人感到惊讶。|Synonyms: retraction, recantation, abjuration, disavowal|Antonyms: affirmation, confirmation, assertion|Adjective, Noun
