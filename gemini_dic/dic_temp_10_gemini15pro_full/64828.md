espauliere:/ɛˈspɔːljɛər/ | n. | 护肩，肩甲 | A piece of armor protecting the shoulder. |  The knight polished his espauliere until it gleamed. 骑士把他的护肩擦得闪闪发亮。| Synonym: shoulder piece, shoulder guard, pauldron | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps vulnerability) | Noun

Etymology:
From Old French *espauliere*, from *espaule* (shoulder), from Latin *spatula* (shoulder blade).
