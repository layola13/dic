disputatious:/ˌdɪspjuˈteɪʃəs/
adjective
爱争论的，好辩的
Inclined to dispute; argumentative.  Fond of or given to disputation.
He was known for his disputatious nature, always ready to argue about anything. 他以好辩的性格而闻名，随时准备就任何事情进行争论。

The disputatious customer argued with the cashier for ten minutes about the price of a candy bar.  这位爱争论的顾客和收银员就一块糖果的价格争论了十分钟。

synonym: argumentative, contentious, quarrelsome
antonym: agreeable, amicable, conciliatory
词性: adjective
