imperil:/ɪmˈpɛrəl/
verb
危及，使陷于危险
to put something or someone in danger
The toxic fumes imperiled the lives of the trapped miners.  这些有毒气体危及了被困矿工的生命。

Synonyms: endanger, jeopardize, threaten, risk
Antonyms: protect, safeguard, secure
词性: 动词
