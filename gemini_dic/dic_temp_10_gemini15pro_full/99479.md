interferingly:/ˌɪntərˈfɪərɪŋli/
| adv. |干扰地，阻碍地|In a way that causes interference or obstruction.  Acting to hinder or prevent something.|The loud music was interferingly playing while I was trying to study.  吵闹的音乐在我试图学习时干扰着我。|Synonyms: obstructively, hinderingly, disruptively|Antonyms: helpfully, supportively|词性: 副词

interfere:/ˌɪntərˈfɪr/
| v. |干涉，干扰|To intervene in a situation without invitation or necessity.  To prevent something from working effectively or from happening as planned.|Please don't interfere in my personal life. 请不要干涉我的私生活. |Synonyms: meddle, intervene, intrude|Antonyms: assist, aid, support|词性: 动词

interference:/ˌɪntərˈfɪərəns/
| n. |干涉，干扰|The act of interfering; an instance of intervening in something.|There was too much interference from the government. 政府干预太多了。|Synonyms: intervention, meddling, intrusion|Antonyms: assistance, aid, support|词性: 名词
