brachyural: /ˌbrækiˈjʊərəl/ | adj. | 短尾的 | Having a short tail, specifically referring to the shortened abdomen of crabs, folded under the cephalothorax. | Example: Brachyural crabs are well-adapted to life on land. 短尾蟹非常适应陆地生活。

Example: The brachyural decapods are a diverse group. 短尾十足目动物是一个多样化的群体。 | 近义词: short-tailed | 反义词: long-tailed | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Greek *brakhus* "short" + *oura* "tail"
