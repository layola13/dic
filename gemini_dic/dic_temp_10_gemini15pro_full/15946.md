balneography:/ˌbælniˈɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 温泉疗法；温泉学；沐浴疗法 | The study of the therapeutic use of baths, especially mineral baths. |  Balneography is the study of the therapeutic use of baths, especially mineral baths, including their chemical composition and physiological effects. 温泉疗法是对沐浴疗法，特别是矿物浴的治疗用途的研究，包括其化学成分和生理效应。|  The doctor recommended balneography for her arthritis. 医生建议她用温泉疗法治疗关节炎。 | synonyms: balneology, balneotherapy | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps aversion to bathing/water therapy) | noun
