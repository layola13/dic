HIMARS:/ˈhaɪmɑːrz/ | n. | 高机动性火箭炮系统 (High Mobility Artillery Rocket System) | A lightweight mobile rocket launcher developed by Lockheed Martin. It can carry six rockets or one ATACMS missile and is highly mobile, allowing it to quickly move and fire from different locations. |  The HIMARS system has been crucial in providing Ukrainian forces with long-range fire capabilities. 高机动性火箭炮系统对于向乌克兰军队提供远程火力至关重要。

The U.S. military has deployed HIMARS to various locations around the world. 美国军方已将高机动性火箭炮系统部署到世界各地。 | 近义词: MLRS (Multiple Launch Rocket System) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
