ecological:/ˌiːkəˈlɒdʒɪkl/ (US) /ˌɛkəˈlɑːdʒɪkl/
adjective
生态的；生态学的
relating to ecology or the environment
例句：The ecological balance of the rainforest is fragile.  雨林的生态平衡很脆弱。

例句：The construction of the dam had a disastrous ecological impact.  大坝的建设对生态造成了灾难性的影响。
近义词：environmental, natural, green
反义词：artificial, unnatural
