flow-on:/ˈfloʊ ɒn/ (US) /ˈfləʊ ɒn/ (UK)| adj. n.|后续的，连锁反应的；后续影响|A flow-on effect is a consequence that follows directly from another event or action.|The increased cost of fuel has had a flow-on effect on the price of food.  燃料成本的增加对食品价格产生了连锁反应。|
The recent economic downturn has had a flow-on effect on businesses. 最近的经济衰退对企业产生了后续影响。|consequential, knock-on, ripple |primary, initial|adjective, noun
