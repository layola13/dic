agitatedly:/ˈædʒɪteɪtɪdli/
adverb
激动地，不安地
in an agitated manner; nervously
She paced the room agitatedly, waiting for the news. 她焦急地在房间里踱来踱去，等待着消息。
He spoke agitatedly, his voice trembling slightly. 他激动地说着，声音微微颤抖。
近义词: nervously, anxiously, frantically, restlessly
反义词: calmly, peacefully, serenely
词性: adverb

词源: From the adjective "agitated" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Agitated" comes from the Latin word "agitatus," the past participle of "agitare," meaning "to move, stir, shake, or disturb."
