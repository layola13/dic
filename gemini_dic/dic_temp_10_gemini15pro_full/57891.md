driftbolt:/ˈdrɪftboʊlt/| n.|漂移螺栓|A long, heavy bolt used to secure timbers or heavy framing members, often driven into a predrilled hole and clinched on the opposite side.  It's designed to resist shear forces.|The carpenter used several driftbolts to connect the large beams. 木匠用几个漂移螺栓连接大梁。|近义词：drift pin, drive bolt|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps screw, which relies on threads rather than friction)|noun
