fooling:/ˈfuːlɪŋ/| adj.  v.| 愚弄的，开玩笑的； 欺骗，愚弄|Fooling someone means tricking them or making them believe something that isn't true.  It can also refer to the act of joking around or acting playfully silly.| He was only fooling when he said he was quitting his job. 他说他要辞职只是在开玩笑。|synonyms: tricking, deceiving, joking, kidding, playing around |antonyms:  being serious, being honest, telling the truth| Verb, Adjective
