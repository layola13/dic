nonsymbolical:/ˌnɒnsɪmˈbɒlɪkəl/ | /ˌnɑːnsɪmˈbɑːlɪkəl/ | adj. | 非象征性的；不象征的 | Not symbolic; literal; lacking symbols. | The artist's later work was nonsymbolical, focusing on realistic portrayals of everyday life.  这位艺术家后期的作品是非象征性的，专注于对日常生活的现实描绘。

The instructions were clear and nonsymbolical, leaving no room for misinterpretation.  这些指示清晰明了，没有象征意义，没有留下任何误解的余地。 | 近义词：literal, concrete, representational | 反义词：symbolic, allegorical, figurative | 词性: adjective
