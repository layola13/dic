impetuous:/ɪmˈpetʃuəs/| adj.|冲动的，鲁莽的，轻率的|Acting or done quickly and without thought or care. |He made an impetuous decision to quit his job. 他一时冲动决定辞掉工作。|
impetuous:/ɪmˈpetʃuəs/| adj.|猛烈的，激烈的|(of natural forces) Moving with great force. |The impetuous river flooded the town.  汹涌的河水淹没了小镇。|
impetuous:/ɪmˈpetʃuəs/| adj.|奔放的，热情的|Characterized by sudden or rash action or emotion. |Her impetuous nature often led her into trouble. 她冲动的性格经常使她陷入困境。|
近义词:rash, impulsive, reckless, hasty|反义词:cautious, prudent, deliberate, considered|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *impetuosus, from Latin impetus (“attack, assault, onset, impulse, force, violence”).
