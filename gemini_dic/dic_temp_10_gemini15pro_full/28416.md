carbonisable:/ˈkɑːrbənaɪzəbl/ | adj. | 可碳化的，可炭化的 | Capable of being carbonized (converted into carbon or a carbon-containing residue by heating). |  Coal is a carbonisable material. 煤是一种可碳化的物质。

The wood became carbonisable after being subjected to intense heat.  这块木头在经受高温后变得可以碳化。| 近义词: charrable | 反义词: non-carbonisable | 词性: adjective
