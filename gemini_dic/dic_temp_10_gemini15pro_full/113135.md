lunisolar:/ˈluːnɪˌsoʊlər/ (US), /ˈljuːnɪˌsəʊlə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|阴阳的，阴阳历的|Relating to or involving both the moon and the sun, especially in relation to a calendar based on the cycles of both.|The Chinese calendar is lunisolar, incorporating both lunar cycles and the solar year.|Synonym: lunar-solar|Antonym: solar (purely solar)|Adjective
