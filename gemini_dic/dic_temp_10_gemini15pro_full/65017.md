estuaries:/ˈestʃʊeriz/ | n. | 河口；港湾 |  A partially enclosed coastal body of brackish water with one or more rivers or streams flowing into it, and with a free connection to the open sea. |  The Chesapeake Bay is one of the largest estuaries in the United States. 切萨皮克湾是美国最大的河口之一。| synonyms: inlets, firths, sounds | antonyms: uplands, mountains | noun

estuaries:/ˈestʃʊəriz/ | n. | 河口；港湾 |  The tidal mouth of a large river, where the tide meets the stream. |  Many species of fish breed in estuaries. 许多种类的鱼在河口繁殖。| synonyms: river mouths, deltas | antonyms: headwaters, sources | noun
