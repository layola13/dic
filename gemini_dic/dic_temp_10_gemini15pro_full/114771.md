malthusian: /mælˈθuːziən/ /mælˈθuːʒən/
adjective| 马尔萨斯的; 马尔萨斯学说的; 人口过剩论的|Of or relating to Malthus or his theory that population tends to increase at a faster rate than its means of subsistence and that unless it is checked by moral restraint or disaster (as disease, famine, or war) widespread poverty and degradation inevitably result.|The current food crisis has revived Malthusian fears about the planet's capacity to feed a burgeoning population.|近义词: neo-Malthusian|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用表示人口控制反对观点的词语，例如 "pro-natalist")|词性: adjective

malthusian: /mælˈθuːziən/ /mælˈθuːʒən/
noun| 马尔萨斯主义者; 人口过剩论者|A person who believes in Malthusian theories.|Some environmentalists are seen as modern-day Malthusians.|近义词: neo-Malthusian|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Derived from Thomas Robert Malthus (1766–1834), English economist and demographer + -ian.
