demirelief:/ˌdɛmi rɪˈliːf/
noun
半浮雕
A sculptural relief in which the figures project slightly from the background.
The artist specialized in demirelief carvings of mythological scenes.  艺术家专门雕刻神话场景的半浮雕。

Near synonyms:  bas-relief, low relief
Antonyms: high relief, alto-relievo
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French demi-relief, from demi- "half" + relief "relief." Ultimately from Latin dimidius "half" and relevare "to raise."
