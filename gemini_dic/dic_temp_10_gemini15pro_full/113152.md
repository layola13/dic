ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *lupas*, accusative singular of *lupus* ("wolf"). The connection to "wolf" in the disease lupus is thought to be due to the characteristic facial rash, which was believed to resemble a wolf's bite.  The magnifying glass sense comes from its resemblance to a wolf's eye.
