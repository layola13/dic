nonpastoral:/nɒnˈpɑːstərəl/ | adj. | 非田园的，非牧歌的 | Not related to or characteristic of a peaceful rural life, especially one idealized as simple and idyllic. | The novel presented a starkly nonpastoral view of country life, focusing on the poverty and hardship of the farmers. 这部小说呈现了乡村生活完全非田园的一面，着重描写了农民的贫困和艰辛。 |  The film's nonpastoral depiction of farm life was a refreshing change from the usual romanticized portrayal. 这部电影对农场生活的非田园式描写，与通常浪漫化的描写相比，令人耳目一新。| 近义词：urban, city-like, mundane, prosaic | 反义词：pastoral, idyllic, bucolic, rustic | 词性：形容词

词源：
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
pastoral (adjective): from Late Latin pastoralis "of a shepherd," from Latin pastor "shepherd"
