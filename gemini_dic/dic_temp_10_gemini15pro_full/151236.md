parenchymous:/pəˈrɛŋkɪməs/| adj.|薄壁组织的，柔软的|Relating to or consisting of parenchyma, the fundamental tissue of plants and certain animal organs.|The stem of the plant is primarily parenchymous.  植物的茎主要是薄壁组织的。| The soft, fleshy part of the fruit is parenchymous. 水果柔软的肉质部分是薄壁组织。 |Synonyms: soft, spongy, fleshy|Antonyms: hard, woody, fibrous|adjective

parenchyma:/pəˈrɛŋkɪmə/| n.|薄壁组织|The fundamental tissue of plants and certain animal organs, typically composed of thin-walled cells.|The parenchyma of the lung is responsible for gas exchange. 肺的薄壁组织负责气体交换。| The plant's parenchyma stores water and nutrients.  植物的薄壁组织储存水分和养分。|Synonyms: tissue, ground tissue|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could contrast with specific tissue types like sclerenchyma or xylem)|noun
