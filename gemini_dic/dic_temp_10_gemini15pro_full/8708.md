anticriticalnesses:/ˌæntiˈkrɪtɪkəlnəsɪz/
n.
反对批评的态度；不愿接受批评的状态
The state of being opposed to or resistant to criticism.
His anticriticalnesses prevented him from accepting valuable feedback. 他不愿接受批评的态度让他无法接受宝贵的反馈意见。
近义词: defensiveness, resistance to criticism, intolerance of criticism
反义词: openness to criticism, receptiveness to feedback
