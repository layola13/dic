outvie: /ˌaʊtˈvaɪ/
verb
超越，胜过
to surpass or defeat someone in competition or rivalry
He outvied his rivals in the race. 他在比赛中胜过对手。
Synonyms: surpass, exceed, outdo, outstrip, eclipse
Antonyms: lose to, be defeated by, fall behind
词性: 动词
