outbranching:/ˌaʊtˈbræntʃɪŋ/
n.
分支；分叉；衍生
The process of producing branches or offshoots.  This can refer to literal branches of a tree, or metaphorical branches of a family, a company, or an idea.
The outbranching of the company into new markets was successful. 公司向新市场的扩张是成功的。
ramification, bifurcation, forking
convergence, consolidation
