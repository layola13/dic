carpeted:/ˈkɑːrpɪtɪd/
adjective
铺有地毯的
Covered with carpet.
The room was carpeted in a luxurious deep red. 这个房间铺着豪华的深红色地毯。
The stairs were carpeted for warmth and comfort. 楼梯铺着地毯，既保暖又舒适。

近义词:carpet-covered, floored
反义词:bare, uncarpeted
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From carpet + -ed.  "Carpet" originates from Old North French "carpite" (thick cloth, rug), from Medieval Latin "carpeta" (coarse cloth).  The "-ed" suffix transforms the noun "carpet" into an adjective, indicating a state of being covered with carpet.
