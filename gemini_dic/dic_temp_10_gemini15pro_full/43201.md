crabby:/ˈkræbi/| adj.|易怒的，暴躁的|Bad-tempered and irritable.|例句：He's been very crabby all day. 他一整天都很暴躁。|近义词：grumpy, irritable, bad-tempered, cross, cantankerous|反义词：good-natured, cheerful, pleasant|词性:形容词
词源:crab + -y.  The adjective likely originated from the perceived behavior of crabs, which are often seen as quick to pinch and therefore, irritable.
