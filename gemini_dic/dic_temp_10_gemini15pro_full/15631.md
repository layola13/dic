bairnish:/ˈbɛːrnɪʃ/| adj.|孩子气的，幼稚的|Resembling or characteristic of a child, especially in being playful, naive, or immature.  Having qualities that are associated with children, like innocence, simplicity, or a lack of worldly experience.|例句：His bairnish delight at the new toy was infectious. 他对新玩具孩子气的喜悦很有感染力。

例句：She retained a certain bairnish charm even in adulthood. 即使在成年后，她仍然保留着某种孩子气的魅力。|近义词：childish, puerile, infantile, juvenile, naive|反义词：mature, sophisticated, grown-up, adult|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Scots *bairnish*, from bairn (“child”) + -ish (adjectival suffix).  Ultimately from Old English *bearn* (“child”).
