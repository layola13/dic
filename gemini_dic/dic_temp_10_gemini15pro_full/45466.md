curator: /kjʊˈreɪtər/ | n. | 馆长；监护人；管理者 | A person who is in charge of the objects or works of art in a museum, art gallery, etc., or a person who organizes an exhibition. | The museum curator gave a tour of the new exhibit.  博物馆馆长带领参观了新的展览。| synonyms: manager, director, administrator, superintendent, guardian, custodian | antonyms: None | noun

curator: /kjʊˈreɪtər/ | n. | (尤指展览的) 策展人 |  Someone who selects and presents artistic or cultural material for public display, often with an accompanying explanation or critical interpretation. | The curator of the photography exhibition chose images that explored themes of identity and belonging. 摄影展的策展人选择了探索身份和归属感主题的图像。 | synonyms: organizer, planner, compiler, selector | antonyms: None | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cūrātor*, from *cūrāre* "to take care of," from *cūra* "care."
