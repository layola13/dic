anisostemonous:/ˌænˌaɪsoʊˈstɛmənəs/
adj.
不等雄蕊的;  雄蕊数目或长度不相等的
Having stamens of unequal length or number.
Example: The anisostemonous flower had some long and some short stamens.  这朵不等雄蕊的花有一些长雄蕊和一些短雄蕊。
Example:  Anisostemonous flowers are a common characteristic of certain plant families. 不等雄蕊的花是某些植物科的共同特征。
Synonym: heterostemonous
Antonym: isostemonous
词性: 形容词
