nightingales:/ˈnaɪtɪŋɡeɪlz/ | n. |夜莺（复数）|A small songbird known for its beautiful, especially at night.  Old English *nihtegale, from niht "night" + galan "to sing." |The nightingales sang sweetly in the moonlight. 夜莺在月光下甜美地歌唱。|Near synonyms: songbirds, warblers |Antonyms: crows, ravens | Noun

nightingale:/ˈnaɪtɪŋɡeɪl/ | n. |夜莺（单数）|A small songbird known for its beautiful, especially at night.  Old English *nihtegale, from niht "night" + galan "to sing." |I heard a nightingale singing in the woods. 我听到树林里有一只夜莺在歌唱。|Near synonyms: songbird, warbler |Antonyms: crow, raven | Noun
