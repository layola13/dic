cavalcades:/ˌkævəlˈkeɪdz/| n. | 骑兵队伍，行列；(人或车的)队伍，行列；一连串，一系列 | A procession of people on horseback, or a series of people or things. |  The president's inauguration featured a cavalcade of floats and marching bands. 总统的就职典礼以花车和游行乐队的队列为特色。

The king's cavalcade made its way through the city streets. 国王的骑兵队伍穿过城市街道。 | 近义词：procession, parade, array, series, succession | 反义词：disorder, chaos | 词性：名词
