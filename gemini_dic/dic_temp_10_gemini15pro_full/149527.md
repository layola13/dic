paganist:/ˈpeɪɡənɪst/| n.|异教徒；非基督教徒|A person who is not a Christian, Jew, or Muslim, or who believes in more than one god. |例句：The Romans persecuted early Christians, seeing them as a threat to traditional paganist beliefs.  罗马人迫害早期的基督徒，视他们为对传统异教信仰的威胁。

例句：He was raised a Christian, but later in life became a paganist. 他从小被抚养为基督徒，但后来成了异教徒。|近义词：heathen, infidel, idolater|反义词：Christian, Muslim, Jew|词性:noun
