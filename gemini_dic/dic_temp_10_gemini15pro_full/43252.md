cracovienne:/krækəˈvjɛn/ |krækəˈviːən/
n.|克拉科夫舞曲；克拉科维亚克舞曲|A lively Polish dance in 2/4 time, originating in Kraków.  It is characterized by syncopated rhythms and frequent changes of tempo.|The orchestra played a spirited cracovienne.|Synonyms: Krakowiak, Polish dance|Antonyms: N/A|noun
