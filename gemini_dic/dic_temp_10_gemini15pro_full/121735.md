mispronounce:/ˌmɪsprəˈnaʊns/ | v. | 发音错误，读错 | to pronounce a word or sound incorrectly. |  She mispronounced my name. 她把我的名字念错了。| misreads, misarticulates | pronounces correctly, articulates correctly | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- + pronounce.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Old English and Old French origin meaning "wrong," "badly," "wrongly," or "unsuitably."  "Pronounce" comes from Old French "prononcer"  meaning "to proclaim, pronounce," from Latin "pronuntiare" meaning "to announce publicly."
