arillated:/ˈærɪˌleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 假种皮的；有假种皮的 | Having an aril. An aril is an extra seed covering, typically fleshy and often brightly colored, that develops from the stalk of the ovule. |  The yew tree has bright red arillated seeds. 紫杉有鲜红色的假种皮种子。|  arillode |  | adjective
Etymology: From Latin *arillus + -ate.  The Latin word arilla means "a dried grape."
