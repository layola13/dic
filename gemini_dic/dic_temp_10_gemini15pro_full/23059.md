bounciness:/ˈbaʊnsɪnəs/
noun
彈性；彈力；活潑
The quality of being bouncy; the ability to bounce.
The bounciness of the basketball makes it easy to dribble.  篮球的弹性使它很容易运球。
elasticity, resilience, springiness
stiffness, rigidity
