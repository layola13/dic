degust:/dɪˈɡʌst/ /dɪˈɡʊst/ | v. | 品尝，品味 | To taste something carefully, so as to appreciate its full flavor. |  
例句：The sommelier degusted the wine before presenting it to the diners.侍酒师在将葡萄酒送给用餐者之前先品尝了一下。

例句：We spent the afternoon degusting different varieties of cheese. 我们用了一下午的时间品尝不同种类的奶酪。

近义词：savor, relish, sample, taste | 反义词：gobble, gulp | 词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *déguster*, from Latin *dēgustāre* ("to taste"), from *dē* ("down, completely") + *gustāre* ("to taste").
