mustn't: /ˈmʌsnt/ (UK) /ˈmʌsnt/ (US) | modal verb | 禁止；不准；不可以 | Contraction of "must not," expressing prohibition or strong advice against doing something. | You mustn't smoke in here. 你不准在这里吸烟。

You mustn't tell anyone our secret. 你一定不能把我们的秘密告诉任何人。


近义词: cannot, may not, should not | 反义词: may, can, should | 词性: modal verb
