coworker:/ˈkoʊˌwɜːrkər/ (US) /ˈkəʊˌwɜːkə(r)/ (UK)| n.|同事，共同工作者|A person who works with another person, especially in the same department or office.|例句：My coworker helped me finish the project. 我的同事帮我完成了这个项目。|例句：I get along well with all my coworkers. 我和所有的同事都相处得很好。|近义词：colleague, associate, peer|反义词:|n.
