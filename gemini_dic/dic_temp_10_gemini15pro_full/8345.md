anti-platonist:/ˌæntiˈpleɪtənɪst/ | n. adj. | 反柏拉图主义者；反柏拉图主义的 |  Someone who opposes or rejects the philosophical views of Plato, especially his theory of Forms.  A philosophy that rejects Platonism. | Example: The anti-platonist argued that abstract concepts like "justice" and "beauty" have no independent existence.  反柏拉图主义者认为，像“正义”和“美丽”这样的抽象概念没有独立的存在。| Example:  His anti-platonist stance led him to focus on the empirical world. 他的反柏拉图主义立场使他专注于经验世界。 | 近义词: nominalist, empiricist | 反义词: Platonist | 词性: noun, adjective
