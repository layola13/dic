berryless:/ˈberɪləs/
adjective
无果实的; 不结果实的
lacking berries; without berries.
The berryless bushes looked barren and desolate in the late autumn.  秋天晚些时候，这些不结果实的灌木看起来荒芜而凄凉。
The unusually cold spring resulted in berryless brambles this year. 今年异常寒冷的春天导致荆棘没有结果。
近义词:fruitless, barren, sterile
反义词:fruitful, productive, berry-laden
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From berry + -less.
