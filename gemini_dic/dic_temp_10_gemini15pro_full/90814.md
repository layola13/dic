humulin:/ˈhjuːmjʊlɪn/| n.|优泌林，人胰岛素|A brand name for human insulin, a medication used to treat diabetes.|例句：My doctor prescribed Humulin to manage my blood sugar levels. 我的医生开了优泌林来控制我的血糖水平。

例句：Humulin is crucial for many people with diabetes to live a healthy life. 优泌林对许多糖尿病患者过上健康生活至关重要。|近义词：insulin, Novolin, Lantus|反义词: None (Humulin is a medicine; it doesn't have a direct opposite.)|词性: noun
