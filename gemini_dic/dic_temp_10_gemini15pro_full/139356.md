obscured:/əbˈskjʊrd/ /ɒbˈskjʊəd/ | adj. | 模糊的，昏暗的，晦涩的 | Hidden, unclear, difficult to understand |
The moon was obscured by clouds. | 月亮被云遮住了。|
His meaning was obscured by his complicated language. | 他的意思被他复杂的语言弄得晦涩难懂。|
近义词: hidden, unclear, vague, ambiguous, indistinct, veiled |
反义词: clear, obvious, apparent, distinct, transparent |
词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin obscuratus, past participle of obscurare (“to darken, cover over”), from obscurus (“dark, dim, indistinct”).
