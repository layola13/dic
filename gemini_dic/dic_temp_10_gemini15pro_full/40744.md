conveyers:/kənˈveɪərz/| n.|传送带，输送机|A machine with a moving belt or chain that carries things from one place to another.|The factory uses conveyers to move products along the assembly line. 工厂使用传送带沿着装配线移动产品。|synonyms:belts, transports, carriers|antonyms:receivers, holders|noun


conveyor:/kənˈveɪər/| n.|传送带，输送机|A machine with a moving belt or chain that carries things from one place to another.|The conveyor belt transported the luggage to the baggage claim area.  传送带将行李运送到行李提取处。|synonyms:belt, transport, carrier|antonyms:receiver, holder|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Anglo-French conveier, from Old French convoier, from Late Latin *conviare, from Latin via “way, road.”
