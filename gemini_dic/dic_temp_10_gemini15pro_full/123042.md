monera:/məˈnɪərə/ | n. | 原核生物界；原核生物 | A kingdom that contains unicellular organisms with a prokaryotic cell organization (having no nuclear membrane), such as bacteria. |  Monera are single-celled organisms without a nucleus. 原核生物是单细胞生物，没有细胞核。| Monera, such as bacteria, play a crucial role in ecosystems.  像细菌这样的原核生物在生态系统中起着至关重要的作用。 | synonyms: prokaryotes, bacteria (in older classifications) | antonyms: eukaryotes | noun
