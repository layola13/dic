metagnathous:/ˌmɛtəˈɡneɪθəs/
| adj. |后颚的，下颚突出的|Having the lower jaw projecting beyond the upper jaw, as in humans and some other mammals.|例句：The metagnathous skull structure is a defining characteristic of Homo sapiens.  后颚的颅骨结构是智人的一个 определяющая характеристика.|近义词：prognathous|反义词：orthognathous|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From meta- (Greek, "after, behind, changed") + gnathos (Greek, "jaw").
