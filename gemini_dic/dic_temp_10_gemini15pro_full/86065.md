hearable:/ˈhɪərəbl/| adj.|可听见的，可听清的|Something that is hearable is loud enough to be heard.  It refers to sound that is within the range of human hearing and of sufficient volume to be perceived.|例句：The music was barely hearable over the noise of the traffic.  音乐几乎被交通噪音盖住了，难以听见。|近义词：audible, perceptible|反义词：inaudible, imperceptible|词性:形容词
