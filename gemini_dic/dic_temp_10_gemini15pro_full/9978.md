aphorism: /ˈæfərɪzəm/ | n. | 格言，警句，箴言 | A concise statement of a general truth or principle, often expressed in a memorable way. | Example 1:  "Actions speak louder than words" is a well-known aphorism.  行动胜于雄辩是一句众所周知的格言。

Example 2:  He peppered his speech with aphorisms about the importance of hard work. 他在演讲中穿插了许多关于努力工作重要性的格言。 | 近义词：maxim, adage, proverb, saying | 反义词：nonsense, fallacy | 词性: noun
