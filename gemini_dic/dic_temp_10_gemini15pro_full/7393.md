animated:/ˈænɪmeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 活泼的，生动的，动画的 | full of life and energy; made to look like it is moving |  The animated discussion continued late into the night.  热烈的讨论一直持续到深夜。 | lively, energetic, spirited, vibrant | dull, lifeless, listless, inanimate | adjective

animated:/ˈænɪmeɪtɪd/ | v. | 使有生气，使活泼，制作动画 | to give life or movement to something; to make a film or cartoon using animation | Her presence animated the entire room. 她的出现让整个房间充满了活力。 | enliven, invigorate, stimulate, inspire | deaden, suppress, stifle | verb
