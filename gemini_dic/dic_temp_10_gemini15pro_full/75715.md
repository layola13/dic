full-grown:/ˌfʊlˈɡroʊn/ (US) /ˌfʊlˈɡrəʊn/ (UK)| adj.| 成熟的；成年的；完全长成的|Having reached full size or maturity.|The garden was shaded by a full-grown oak tree. 花园里有一棵成年橡树遮荫。|The horse was now a full-grown stallion. 这匹马现在是一匹成年种马了。|近义词: mature, adult, developed|反义词: immature, young, undeveloped|词性:形容词

词源:
From full + grown, the past participle of grow.  "Full" emphasizes the completeness of the growth process.
