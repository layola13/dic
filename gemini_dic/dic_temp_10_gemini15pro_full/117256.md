mechanically:/məˈkænɪkli/| adv.|机械地，自动地，无意识地|In a mechanical manner; by a machine or mechanism; without thought or spontaneity. |The robot arm moved mechanically, repeating the same task over and over.  机器人手臂机械地移动，一遍又一遍地重复着同样的任务。|Synonyms: automatically, routinely, mindlessly|Antonyms: manually, thoughtfully, creatively|adverb
mechanically:/məˈkænɪkli/| adj.(rare)|机械的|Relating to machines or mechanics.  |While not common, "mechanically" can sometimes be used as an adjective. |The mechanically complex device required specialized training to operate.  这种机械复杂的装置需要专门的培训才能操作。|Synonyms: machine-like, automated|Antonyms: manual, handmade|adjective
