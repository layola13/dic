hunkerism:/ˈhʌŋkərɪzəm/ | n. | 蹲守主义；闭关自守；保守主义；固守陈规 | A conservative or reactionary attitude characterized by a preference for traditional values and practices.  |  The politician's hunkerism prevented any meaningful progress on social issues.  这位政治家的保守主义阻碍了社会问题的任何有意义的进展。 | synonyms: conservatism, traditionalism, reactionism | antonyms: progressivism, liberalism, radicalism | noun
