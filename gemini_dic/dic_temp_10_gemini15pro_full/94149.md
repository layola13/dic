impeller:/ɪmˈpɛlər/| n. |叶轮；推进器|A rotating device with blades for moving a fluid, as in a pump or compressor.  It converts rotational energy to kinetic energy in the fluid.|The impeller of the pump was damaged, causing a leak.|近义词：rotor, fan, propeller|反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps stator as it is a stationary component)|noun
