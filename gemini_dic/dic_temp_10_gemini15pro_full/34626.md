cleg:/klɛɡ/
n.
虻，马蝇
A bloodsucking fly, especially of the genus *Haematopota*, that is stout-bodied and has patterned wings.
The cleg bit the horse's leg, making it flinch.  虻叮了马的腿，使它畏缩。

近义词:horsefly, gadfly
反义词:None (Clegs don't have a direct opposite.)
词性:Noun
