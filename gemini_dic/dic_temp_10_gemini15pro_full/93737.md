immaterialist:/ɪməˈtɪəriəlɪst/
n.|唯心主义者；精神主义者|A person who believes that material things have no independent existence and that the only reality is spiritual or mental.|He was an immaterialist, believing that consciousness was the primary reality.|近义词：idealist, spiritualist|反义词：materialist|词性:noun
immaterial:/ˌɪməˈtɪəriəl/
adj.|无关紧要的；非物质的；精神上的|Not important or relevant; not consisting of matter; spiritual.|The exact date is immaterial; just get it done soon. 具体日期无关紧要，只要尽快完成就好。|近义词：insignificant, irrelevant, unimportant; spiritual, incorporeal|反义词：material, relevant, important|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *immaterialis*, from in- "not" + materialis "material."
