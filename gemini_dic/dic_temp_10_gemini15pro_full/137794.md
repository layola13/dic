nonvenous:/nɒnˈviːnəs/
adjective
非静脉的
Not of, pertaining to, or originating in the veins.  Relating to or involving routes other than the veins.
The doctor administered the medication through a nonvenous route. 医生通过非静脉途径给药。
Nonvenous delivery methods are being explored for this drug.  这种药物的非静脉给药方法正在探索中。
近义词: extravascular
反义词: venous
词性: adjective
