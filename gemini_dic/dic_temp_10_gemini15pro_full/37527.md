commonality:/ˌkɒməˈnælɪti/ /ˌkɑːməˈnælɪti/| n.|共性，共同点，共通性|A feature or trait shared by two or more people or things.|The study revealed surprising commonalities between the two cultures. 这项研究揭示了两种文化之间惊人的相似之处。|Synonyms: similarity, resemblance, likeness, affinity, shared characteristic|Antonyms: difference, dissimilarity, divergence, distinction|noun

commonality:/ˌkɒməˈnælɪti/ /ˌkɑːməˈnælɪti/| n.|普遍性，普通性，平凡|The state of being common or ordinary.|The commonality of the experience made it less special. 这种经历的普遍性使它不那么特别了。|Synonyms: prevalence, ordinariness, usualness, normalcy|Antonyms: rarity, unusualness, uniqueness, singularity|noun
