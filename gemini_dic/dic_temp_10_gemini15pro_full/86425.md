heedlessness:/ˈhiːdlɪsnəs/| n.|粗心大意；疏忽；轻率|Lack of attention or care; thoughtlessness.|His heedlessness led to a serious accident. 他的粗心大意导致了一场严重的事故。|Synonyms: carelessness, negligence, inattention, recklessness|Antonyms: carefulness, caution, prudence, attention|noun

heedlessness:/ˈhiːdlɪsnəs/| n.|不注意；不理会|The state of not paying attention to something.|Her heedlessness of the warnings resulted in disaster. 她对警告的漠视导致了灾难。|Synonyms: disregard, indifference, neglect|Antonyms: regard, attention, concern|noun
