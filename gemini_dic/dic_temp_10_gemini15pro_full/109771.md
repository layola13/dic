levitant: /ˈlɛvɪtənt/ | n. adj. | 漂浮者；悬浮的 | Someone or something that levitates; exhibiting or capable of levitation. |  A skilled magician can appear to make a person levitant.  一个熟练的魔术师可以让一个人看起来漂浮起来。 |The levitant ball hovered mysteriously in the air.  漂浮的球神秘地悬在空中。 | 近义词: floating, hovering, weightless | 反义词: grounded, weighted | 词性: 名词, 形容词

词源: From Latin *levitans*, present participle of levitare (“to lift up, lighten”), from levis (“light”).
