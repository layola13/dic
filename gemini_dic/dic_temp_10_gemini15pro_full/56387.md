doldrums: /ˈdoʊldrəmz/ /ˈdɒldrəmz/ | n. | 萧条，低迷，郁闷；赤道无风带 | A state of inactivity or stagnation, as in business or art; a period of listlessness and depression.  Also, a part of the ocean near the equator abounding in calms, squalls, and light baffling winds. | The economy has been in the doldrums for months. 经济已经持续数月低迷。 | synonyms: slump, depression, stagnation, apathy, lethargy | antonyms: boom, prosperity, activity, energy, enthusiasm | noun
