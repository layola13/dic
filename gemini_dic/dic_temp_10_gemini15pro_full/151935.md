pastil:/ˈpæstɪl/ (US), /ˈpæstil/ (UK)| n. |药片，锭剂；香丸，香锭；彩色蜡笔|A small medicated or flavored lozenge.  A small, sweet-smelling tablet that is burned to make a room smell pleasant. A stick of colored wax, charcoal, or chalk used for drawing.|例句：She sucked on a cough pastil. 她含着一颗止咳药片。

例句：The room was filled with the scent of a lavender pastil. 房间里充满了薰衣草香锭的香味。

近义词：lozenge, tablet, troche (for medication); incense, fragrance (for scent); crayon, pastel (for art)|反义词: N/A |词性:noun

pastil:/ˈpæstɪl/ (US), /ˈpæstil/ (UK)| adj. |柔和的，淡彩色的 (主要指艺术作品)|Pale and light in color.|例句：The artist used pastil colors to create a dreamy landscape.  这位艺术家用柔和的色彩创造了一个梦幻般的风景。

例句：She wore a dress in pastil shades of pink and blue. 她穿着一件淡粉色和淡蓝色的连衣裙。

近义词：pale, light, soft, muted|反义词:bright, vivid, intense|词性:adjective


词源: From Old French *pastille, diminutive of paste "paste," from Latin pasta "dough, pastry."  The sense of "medicated lozenge" arose from the original form of these being small pastes.
