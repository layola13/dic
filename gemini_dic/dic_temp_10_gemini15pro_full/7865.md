antecedence:/ˌæntɪˈsiːdəns/| n.|优先,居先,领先|The concept of antecedence is crucial in logic, establishing the temporal order of events.|在逻辑学中，优先的概念至关重要，它确定了事件的时间顺序。|例句：His antecedence in the field gave him a significant advantage. 他在该领域的领先地位给了他显著的优势。

例句：The antecedence of the cause is essential for understanding the effect.  原因的居先性对于理解结果至关重要。

|近义词：priority, precedence, precession|反义词: subsequence, posterity|词性:名词
