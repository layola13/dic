borderers:/ˈbɔːrdərərz/ | n. | 边民，边境居民 | People who live on or near a border between countries or regions. | The borderers often traded goods across the border. 边民经常跨境交易货物。

The borderers maintained a distinct culture, influenced by both sides of the border. 边境居民保持着独特的文化，受到边界两边的影响。 | synonyms: frontiersmen, border people, inhabitants of the border | antonyms: city dwellers, inland residents | noun
