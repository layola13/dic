evincement:/ɪˈvɪnsmənt/
noun
显示；表明；证明
The act of evincing or making evident; the state of being evinced.
His evincement of sadness was obvious through his tears. 他悲伤的表现通过他的眼泪显而易见。
近义词: demonstration, manifestation, display, exhibition
反义词: concealment, suppression, hiding
