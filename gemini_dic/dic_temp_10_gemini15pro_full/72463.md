flung:/flʌŋ/
verb
投掷，抛，猛冲
past tense and past participle of fling
He flung the door open and stormed out. 他猛地打开门，冲了出去。
近义词：threw, cast, hurled, tossed
反义词：caught, held


flung:/flʌŋ/
adjective
投掷的，抛出的
(used informally) thrown or moved carelessly or suddenly
Her hair was flung back over her shoulders. 她的头发随意地披在肩后。
近义词：thrown, cast, hurled, tossed
反义词：caught, held
