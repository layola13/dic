ambassadorial:/ˌæmbəˈsɔːriəl/
adjective
大使的；大使职务的；大使派头的
of, relating to, or characteristic of an ambassador; suitable for an ambassador
例句：He maintained an ambassadorial manner throughout the negotiation. 他在整个谈判过程中保持着大使的风度。

例句：She has ambassadorial qualities, such as diplomacy and tact. 她具有大使般的品质，例如外交手腕和机智。

近义词：diplomatic, consular, representative
反义词：unofficial, non-diplomatic
词性：adjective
