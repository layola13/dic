depositing:/dɪˈpɒzɪtɪŋ/
verb
存放，存入
the act of putting something, especially money, in a bank or safe place
She is depositing a check in her account. 她正在往她的账户里存入一张支票。
synonyms: placing, putting, lodging, saving
antonyms: withdrawing, removing, taking out
