neostriatum:/ˌniːoʊstraɪˈeɪtəm/ | n. | 新纹状体 |  A subcortical part of the forebrain, part of the basal ganglia system, involved in motor control, planning, and reward processing. It's composed of the caudate nucleus and the putamen. | The neostriatum plays a crucial role in motor learning. 新纹状体在运动学习中起着至关重要的作用。 |  striatum, dorsal striatum |  | noun
