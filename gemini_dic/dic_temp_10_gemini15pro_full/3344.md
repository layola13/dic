affrayed:/əˈfreɪd/| adj.|害怕的，恐惧的|Frightened or scared.|例句：She was affrayed of the dark. 她害怕黑暗。|近义词：frightened, scared, terrified, afraid|反义词：brave, courageous, bold|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old French *affraié, past participle of affraier (“to frighten”), from Vulgar Latin *exfridāre, from Latin frigidus (“cold”).  The sense development is from "chilled with fear" to simply "fearful."
