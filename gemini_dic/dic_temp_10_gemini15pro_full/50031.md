denormalizations:/diːˌnɔːrmələˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 反规范化，非规范化 | The process of reversing normalization, typically in a database, by adding redundant data to improve read performance. This is often done at the expense of write performance and data integrity. |  Example 1: The denormalizations in the database improved query speed but made updates more complex. 数据库中的反规范化提高了查询速度，但使更新更加复杂。

Example 2:  Excessive denormalizations can lead to data inconsistencies. 过度的反规范化会导致数据不一致。 | synonyms: redundancy, data duplication | antonyms: normalization, optimization | noun
