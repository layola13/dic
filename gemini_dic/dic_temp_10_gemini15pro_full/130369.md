non-united:/ˌnɒn juˈnaɪtɪd/ | adj. | 不团结的，不统一的 | Not formed into a unit; not joined together; lacking unity or agreement. | The non-united tribes were easily conquered.  不团结的部落很容易被征服。| The political parties were non-united on the issue of healthcare.  各政党在医疗保健问题上意见不统一。| 近义词: divided, disunited, separate, fragmented, discordant | 反义词: united, unified, integrated, cohesive, harmonious | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "united" (past participle of "unite," from Old French "unir," ultimately from Latin "unus" meaning "one").
