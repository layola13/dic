denize:/dɪˈnaɪz/| vt.|使入海；使成为海；淹没|To make something into a sea or lake, or to submerge something in the sea or a large body of water.|They denized the valley by building a dam. 他们通过修建大坝将山谷变成了湖泊。|近义词：flood, inundate, submerge|反义词:drain, dry|verb
