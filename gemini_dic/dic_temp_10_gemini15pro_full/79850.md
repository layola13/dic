Etymology: From Middle English *glister*, from Old French *clystère*, from Late Latin *clyster*, from Ancient Greek *κλυστήρ* (klystḗr, “instrument for injecting fluids”), from *κλύζω* (klýzō, “I wash out, rinse”).
