compulsive:/kəmˈpʌlsɪv/| adj. n.|强迫性的，强制的；强迫症患者|Having a very strong desire to do something, especially something that is considered to be bad or harmful.  Driven by an irresistible urge.|例句：He's a compulsive gambler. 他是个嗜赌成瘾的人。

例句：She has a compulsive need to be in control. 她有一种控制一切的强迫性需求。|近义词：obsessive, uncontrollable, involuntary|反义词：controlled, voluntary, optional|词性:形容词

compulsives:/kəmˈpʌlsɪvz/| n.|强迫症患者|People with compulsive behaviors or disorders.|例句：Support groups can be helpful for compulsives. 互助小组对强迫症患者很有帮助。

例句：The clinic specializes in treating compulsives. 这家诊所专门治疗强迫症患者。|近义词：obsessives, addicts|反义词： (No direct antonym for people)|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin compulsivus, from compuls- ‘driven, compelled’ (see compel).
