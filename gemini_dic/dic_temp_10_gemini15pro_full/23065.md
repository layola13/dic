bound-in:/ˌbaʊnd ˈɪn/
adjective
装订好的，内置的
Bound-in pages are sewn into the cover of a book.  装订好的页面被缝进书的封皮里。
The book has several bound-in maps. 这本书有几张装订好的地图。
Synonyms:  integrated, built-in, attached
Antonyms: loose-leaf, detachable, removable
词性: adjective
