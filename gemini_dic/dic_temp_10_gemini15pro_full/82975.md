gunmanship:/ˈɡʌnmənʃɪp/
n.
枪法; 枪技
skill in shooting, especially with a handgun.
He demonstrated impressive gunmanship at the competition. 他在比赛中展现了令人印象深刻的枪法。

近义词: marksmanship, shooting skill
反义词: ineptitude with firearms

词性: 名词 (noun)

词源：gunman (枪手) + -ship (表示技巧或技艺的后缀)
