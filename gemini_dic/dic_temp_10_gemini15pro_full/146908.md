overinstructively: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈstrʌktɪvli/
| adv. | 过于指导性地，过于教导地 |  in a way that gives too much instruction or guidance, often to the point of being tedious or patronizing. | The museum tour was overinstructively presented, leaving little room for personal discovery.  博物馆的导览讲解过于教导，几乎没有留下个人探索的空间。 | Example: He overinstructively explained the simple task, making me feel incompetent. 他过于详细地解释了这项简单的任务，让我觉得自己很无能。|Synonyms: pedantically, didactically, patronizingly | Antonyms: succinctly, briefly, concisely | word type: adverb
