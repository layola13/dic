leach:/liːtʃ/| v. n.|过滤，沥滤；(使)渗出，滤去|To remove (nutrients or salts) from soil or rock by the action of percolating water.|Rain leached the nutrients out of the soil. 雨水把土壤中的养分都冲走了。|近义词：filter, percolate, drain|反义词:absorb, retain|verb

leach:/liːtʃ/| n.|沥滤；沥滤液|The process of removing nutrients or salts from soil or rock by the action of percolating water, or the solution obtained by this process.|The leach from the landfill contaminated the groundwater. 垃圾填埋场的渗滤液污染了地下水。|近义词：filtrate, seepage|反义词: N/A|noun
