overgreasiness:/ˌoʊvərˈɡriːsɪnəs/| n. |过度油腻|The state of being excessively greasy.|The overgreasiness of the pizza made it difficult to eat. 比萨饼太油腻了，很难吃。|The overgreasiness of his hair made him look unkempt. 他的头发太油了，让他看起来很邋遢。|近义词：oiliness, greasiness|反义词: dryness, aridity|词性:名词

词源: "over" (prefix denoting excess) + "greasiness" (from "greasy" + "-ness" suffix forming nouns of quality or state)
