diastatic:/ˌdaɪəˈstætɪk/ | adj. |  淀粉分解的；促淀粉酶的 | Relating to or causing diastasis, especially the conversion of starch into sugar. | Diastatic malt powder is used in baking to help break down starches. 淀粉分解麦芽粉用于烘焙，以帮助分解淀粉。|  近义词: amylolytic | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially inhibitory) | 词性: adjective


diastase: /ˈdaɪəsteɪz/ | n. | 淀粉酶 | Any of a group of enzymes that catalyze the hydrolysis of starch to sugar. | Diastase is present in saliva and pancreatic juice. 淀粉酶存在于唾液和胰液中。 | 近义词: amylase | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
