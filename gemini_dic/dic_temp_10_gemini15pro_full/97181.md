一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she like to do in her free time?  她空闲时间喜欢做什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? 他晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she accomplished this year?  她今年完成了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading this week? 他这周一直在读什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? 她对这部电影说了些什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was he thinking about when you saw him? 你看到他时他在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? 她生病前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting? 他们会在会议上讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到今年年底你将取得什么成就？
What will they have finished by tomorrow afternoon? 明天下午之前他们将完成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past ten years by 2030? 到2030年，你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业的时候，她将已经学习什么多久了？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
What would she say if she were here? 如果她在这里，她会说什么？

被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what you mean. 她不明白你的意思。
