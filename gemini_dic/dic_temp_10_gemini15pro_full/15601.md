bailed:/beɪld/| v.|保释，帮助…摆脱困境，舀出，跳伞|To release (someone) from custody on payment of bail. To help someone out of a difficult situation. To remove water from something. To abandon a situation or activity. |例句：He bailed his friend out of jail. 他保释了他的朋友出狱。

例句：She bailed me out when I forgot my wallet. 当我忘记带钱包时，她帮了我一把。

例句：They bailed water out of the sinking boat. 他们把下沉的船里的水舀了出来。

例句：The pilot bailed out of the plane. 飞行员跳伞逃生了。|近义词：rescued, helped, scooped, parachuted, abandoned, left|反义词:jailed, imprisoned, sank|词性:动词

bailed:/beɪld/| v.|（用干草）打捆|To gather and bind (hay) into bales. |例句：The farmer bailed the hay. 农民把干草打成捆。

例句：They bailed the hay and stored it in the barn. 他们把干草打成捆并储存在谷仓里。|近义词：bundled, packed|反义词:scattered|词性:动词
