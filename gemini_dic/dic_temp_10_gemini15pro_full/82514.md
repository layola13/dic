gsm:/dʒiːɛsˈɛm/ | n. | 全球移动通信系统（Global System for Mobile communications） | A digital mobile telephony system that operates at 900 MHz or 1.8 GHz and is the standard in Europe and many other parts of the world. |  My phone uses GSM technology. 我的手机使用GSM技术。| synonyms: cellular, mobile | antonyms: N/A | noun
