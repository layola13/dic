humanoid:/ˈhjuːmənɔɪd/| adj. n.|类人的；人形机器人|Resembling a human in appearance or form; a being that resembles a human.|例句：The robot was surprisingly humanoid in its movements.  这个机器人的动作惊人地像人类。|近义词：anthropomorphic, humanlike|反义词：non-humanoid, inhuman|词性:形容词,名词
humanoid:/ˈhjuːmənɔɪd/| n.|人形机器人|A being that resembles a human.|例句：The movie featured a friendly humanoid alien.  这部电影以一个友善的人形外星人为主角。|近义词：android, cyborg|反义词：non-humanoid, inhuman|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From human + -oid (suffix meaning "resembling," "like").  The suffix -oid comes from the Greek -oeidēs, from -o- + -eidēs "having the form of," related to eidos "form," "shape."
