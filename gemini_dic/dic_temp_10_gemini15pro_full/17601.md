|n.(non-standard)|美人；美好的事物| "Beautifuls" is not standard English.  The word "beautiful" is an adjective and does not have a plural form when used in the typical sense.  However, it can sometimes be used informally as a plural noun to refer to beautiful people or things, though this is considered non-standard.|Example (non-standard):  "Look at all the beautifuls in that magazine!" 看看那本杂志上的所有美人！|近义词：(as an adjective) pretty, attractive, gorgeous, stunning |反义词：(as an adjective) ugly, unattractive, hideous|词性: (non-standard usage as) noun


beautiful: /ˈbjuːtɪfʊl/
|adj.|美丽的；美好的|Pleasing the senses or mind aesthetically.|Example: She has a beautiful voice. 她有一副好嗓子。

Example:  We had a beautiful day at the beach. 我们在海滩度过了美好的一天。|近义词：pretty, attractive, gorgeous, stunning|反义词：ugly, unattractive, hideous|词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *beautifull, from Anglo-Norman *beauteful, from Old French biau "beautiful" + -ful.
