antiresonance:/ˌæntiˈrɛzənəns/ | n. | 反共振，反谐振 |The condition of a system driven by an oscillating force in which the system vibrates with minimal amplitude at a particular frequency.  This frequency is the antiresonant frequency. | The bridge experienced dangerous vibrations at its antiresonant frequency.  桥梁在其反共振频率下经历了危险的振动。 | Synonyms: anti-resonance | Antonyms: resonance | noun
