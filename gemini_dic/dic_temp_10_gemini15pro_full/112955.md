lugworms:/ˈlʌɡwɜːrmz/| n. |沙蚕，钓饵|A large marine worm (Arenicola marina) that burrows in sand and is used as bait by anglers.|例句：Lugworms are a popular bait for sea fishing. 沙蚕是海钓常用的诱饵。

例句：The fisherman dug up some lugworms for bait. 渔夫挖了一些沙蚕作诱饵。


|近义词：sandworm, baitworm|反义词: None|词性: Noun
