morselization:/ˌmɔːrsəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|摩尔斯化，分割，碎片化
|The act or process of converting something into Morse code, or of breaking something down into smaller, disconnected parts.|
|The morselization of the message made it difficult to decipher. 这条信息的摩尔斯化使其难以破译。|
|fragmentation, division, decomposition, atomization|
|unification, integration, consolidation|
|noun|


morselize:/ˈmɔːrsəlaɪz/
|vt.|将…转换成摩尔斯电码；将…分割成小块
|To convert (a message, etc.) into Morse code, or to break something down into smaller, disconnected parts.|
|The spy morselized the secret message before transmitting it. 间谍在发送之前将秘密信息转换成摩尔斯电码。
The artist morselized the image, creating a fragmented and abstract piece.  这位艺术家将图像碎片化，创作出一幅支离破碎的抽象作品。|
|fragment, divide, decompose, atomize|
|unite, integrate, consolidate|
|verb|
