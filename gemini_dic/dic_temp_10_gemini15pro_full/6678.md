anakim:/ˈænəkɪm/| n. |亚衲族人，巨人|A race of giants mentioned in the Bible, inhabiting the land of Canaan before the Israelites conquered it. They were reputed to be of great stature and strength.|例句：The Anakim were feared by the Israelites. 以色列人惧怕亚衲族人。

例句：The spies sent by Moses reported that the Anakim were giants. 摩西派出的探子报告说亚衲族人是巨人。|近义词：giants, titans|反义词:dwarfs|Noun
