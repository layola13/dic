enforceable:/ɪnˈfɔːrsəbl/ | adj. |可执行的；可强制执行的|Able to be enforced, such as by law or contract. | The contract is legally enforceable.  该合同具有法律效力。|Synonyms: valid, binding, effective, actionable|Antonyms: unenforceable, invalid, null, void|adjective

enforceable:/ɪnˈfɔːrsəbl/ | adj. |可实施的|Capable of being put into effect or made to happen. |The new regulations are enforceable immediately. 新规章立即生效。|Synonyms: applicable, practicable, feasible|Antonyms: impracticable, unworkable|adjective
