baffle:/ˈbæfl/ (US), /ˈbæfəl/ (UK)| vt. n.|使困惑，阻碍；挡板|To confuse or frustrate someone; a device used to deflect or regulate the flow of air, light, or liquid.|The complex instructions completely baffled me. 复杂的说明完全把我搞糊涂了。|Synonyms: bewilder, perplex, confuse, confound, frustrate, foil, thwart|Antonyms: clarify, enlighten, explain, aid, assist|verb

baffle:/ˈbæfl/ (US), /ˈbæfəl/ (UK)| n.|挡板|A device used to deflect or regulate the flow of air, light, or liquid.|The car has a baffle to reduce engine noise. 这辆车有一个挡板用来降低引擎噪音。|Synonyms: deflector, screen, shield, barrier|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical object)|noun
