ecphonema:/ɛkˈfoʊniːmə/ | n. | 突发感叹; 呼喊; 感叹语 | An outburst of emotion, especially in speech; an exclamation. | Example 1: "O tempora! O mores!" is a famous ecphonema from Cicero.  西塞罗的名言“哦，时代！哦，道德！”就是一个著名的突发感叹。

Example 2: Her sudden ecphonema of grief startled everyone in the room. 她突然的悲痛呼喊吓到了房间里的所有人。 | Near Synonyms: exclamation, outcry, ejaculation, interjection | Antonyms: whisper, murmur, understatement | noun
