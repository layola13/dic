hooked:/hʊkt/
adjective
着迷的；上瘾的
enjoying something so much that you are unable to stop having it, watching it, doing it, etc.
例句：I'm hooked on this new TV show. 我迷上了这部新的电视剧。

例句：He's completely hooked on video games. 他完全沉迷于电子游戏。

近义词：addicted, captivated, obsessed
反义词：uninterested, bored, indifferent
词性：adjective


hooked:/hʊkt/
verb
(hook的过去式和过去分词)钩住；迷住
past tense and past participle of hook
例句：The fish was hooked and struggled to get free. 鱼被钩住了，拼命挣扎想要挣脱。

例句：The movie's opening scene really hooked me. 这部电影的开场戏真的把我迷住了。

近义词：caught, snagged, fascinated
反义词：released, freed, uninterested
词性：verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "hook," meaning "to catch with a hook." The metaphorical sense of being "hooked" on something developed later, referring to being caught or captivated by something.
