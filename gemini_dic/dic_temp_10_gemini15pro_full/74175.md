four-dimensional:/ˌfɔːrdaɪˈmɛnʃənl/ | adj. | 四维的 | Existing in four dimensions (typically three spatial dimensions and one time dimension). |  例句：Einstein's theory of relativity deals with a four-dimensional space-time continuum. 爱因斯坦的相对论涉及四维时空连续体。

例句：The artist attempted to represent the four-dimensional object on a two-dimensional canvas.  这位艺术家试图在二维画布上表现四维物体。

| 近义词：hyperdimensional | 反义词: two-dimensional, three-dimensional | 词性: adjective
