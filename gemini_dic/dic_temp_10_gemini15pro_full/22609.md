boow:/buː/ | int. | 嘘；呸； | An exclamation of disapproval or disgust. | Example: "Boow! That's a terrible idea!" 呸！那是个糟糕的主意！ | Example:  "Boow!  He cheated on the test." 嘘！他考试作弊了。| 近义词: boo, pshaw, fie | 反义词: bravo, hurrah | interjection
