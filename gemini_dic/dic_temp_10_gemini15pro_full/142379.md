orthorrhaphous:/ˌɔːrθəˈræfəs/
adjective
直翅目的
(of an insect pupa) having the wings and appendages free and visible, rather than glued to the body.
例句：The grasshopper is an orthorrhaphous insect. 蚱蜢是直翅目昆虫。

例句：Orthorrhaphous insects undergo incomplete metamorphosis. 直翅目昆虫经历不完全变态。
近义词：exopterygote
反义词：obtect
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ὀρθός (orthós, “straight”) + ῥαφή (rhaphḗ, “seam”).
