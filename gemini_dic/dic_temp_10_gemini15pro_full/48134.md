decollate:/dɪˈkɒleɪt/| adj.|被斩首的，被砍头的|Having had the head severed from the body.|The decollate body was found in the woods. 被斩首的尸体在树林里被发现。|Synonyms: headless, decapitated|Antonyms: whole, complete|adjective
