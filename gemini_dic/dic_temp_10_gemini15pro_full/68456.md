familiarises:/fəˈmɪliəraɪzɪz/
verb
使熟悉；使了解
to make someone familiar with something; to get to know something
The training course familiarises new employees with the company's procedures.  培训课程使新员工熟悉公司的程序。
synonyms: acquaint, accustom, introduce
antonyms: estrange, alienate
词性:动词


familiarize:/fəˈmɪliəraɪz/
verb
使熟悉；使了解
to make someone familiar with something; to get to know something
She took a few days to familiarize herself with the new software. 她花几天时间熟悉了新软件。
synonyms: acquaint, accustom, introduce
antonyms: estrange, alienate
词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English familiarisen, from Old French familiariser, from Late Latin familiarizare, from Latin familiaris (“of the family, intimate, friendly”).
