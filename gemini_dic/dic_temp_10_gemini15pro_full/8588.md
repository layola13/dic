anticlastic:/ˌæn.tɪˈklæs.tɪk/| adj.|反向曲线的，鞍形的|Curved in opposite directions, like a saddle.  A surface that curves upwards in one direction and downwards in the perpendicular direction.|The Pringles potato chip is a familiar example of an anticlastic surface.|近义词:hyperbolic, saddle-shaped|反义词:synclastic|词性:形容词
