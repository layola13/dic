chorepiscopal:/ˌkɔːriəˈpɪskəpəl/
| adj. |助理主教的；乡村主教的| Relating to a chorepiscopus (a rural bishop, historically a bishop's assistant with limited powers). | The chorepiscopal functions were gradually absorbed by archdeacons. 助理主教的职能逐渐被总执事所吸收。| 近义词：suffragan | 反义词: None | 词性: Adjective


chorepiscopus:/ˌkɔːriəˈpɪskəpəs/
| n. |助理主教；乡村主教| A rural bishop, historically a bishop's assistant with limited powers. | The chorepiscopus oversaw a number of rural parishes. 乡村主教负责管理一些乡村教区。 | 近义词：suffragan bishop | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun
