beguilingly:/bɪˈɡaɪlɪŋli/
adverb
迷人地；诱人地
in a way that is charming and attractive, but often in a deceptive way
She smiled beguilingly at him. 她迷人地对他笑了笑。
Synonyms:charmingly, attractively, enticingly, seductively
Antonyms:repulsively, unattractively, off-puttingly
词性: 副词
