megagrants:/ˈmɛɡəɡrænts/
noun
巨额赠款
Large grants, typically given by governments or foundations to fund major research projects or initiatives.
The university received a megagrant to establish a new center for artificial intelligence research. 这所大学收到了巨额拨款，用于建立一个新的人工智能研究中心。
synonyms:  substantial grants, major grants
antonyms:  small grants, minor grants
词性: 名词
