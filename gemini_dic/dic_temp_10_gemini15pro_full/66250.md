excluder:/ɪkˈskluːdər/| n.|排除器; 隔离物; 排除装置|A device or structure that prevents something from entering or passing through a particular space.|Beekeepers use an excluder to keep the queen bee out of the honey supers. 养蜂人使用隔王板以防止蜂王进入蜂蜜浅箱。

The cat flap has a built-in excluder to prevent other animals from entering the house.  猫洞有一个内置的隔离装置，以防止其他动物进入房子。|近义词:barrier, screen, separator|反义词:includer, admitter|noun
