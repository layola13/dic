dingey:/ˈdɪŋi/ (US), /ˈdɪŋɡi/ (UK)| n. |小艇，小舢板|A small boat, often carried or towed by a larger vessel.|We took the dingey ashore to explore the island. 我们乘小艇上岸去探索这个岛屿。|
The family went fishing in their dingey. 这家人乘他们的小艇去钓鱼了。|
synonyms: rowboat, skiff, tender|antonyms: ship, yacht|noun
