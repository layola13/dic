granite:/ˈɡrænɪt/ | adj. |花岗岩的；坚硬的；坚定的|Resembling granite in hardness, firmness, or texture.  |He had a granite-like will. 他有着花岗岩般坚定的意志。 | Her expression was granite hard. 她的表情坚硬如花岗岩。 |近义词:firm, hard, resolute, unyielding | 反义词:soft, yielding, irresolute | 词性: adjective
