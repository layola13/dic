oversubtlety:/ˌoʊvərˈsʌtəlti/| n.| 过于精细; 过于微妙; 矫揉造作 | An excessive refinement of thought or expression, often to the point of obscurity or artificiality. oversubtle:/ˌoʊvərˈsʌtəl/| adj.| 过于精细的; 过于微妙的; 矫揉造作的 | Excessively subtle; too refined or elaborate.
