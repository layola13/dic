aridities:/əˈrɪdətiz/| n. |干旱，干燥|The aridities of the desert make it difficult for most plants to survive. 沙漠的干旱使得大多数植物难以生存。|例句：The prolonged aridities caused widespread crop failures. 长期干旱导致大面积农作物歉收。

例句：The region is known for its extreme aridities and lack of rainfall. 该地区以极端干旱和缺乏降雨而闻名。

近义词：dryness, drought, barrenness|反义词:humidity, moisture, wetness|词性:名词
