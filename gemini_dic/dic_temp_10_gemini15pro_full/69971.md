festlich:/ˈfɛstlɪç/| adj.|节日的，喜庆的，欢乐的|Relating to or suitable for a festival or celebration; festive.  Characterized by festivity and celebration. |The festlich atmosphere filled the town square. 节日的气氛弥漫着整个城镇广场。|Synonyms: festive, celebratory, merry, joyous, jolly|Antonyms: somber, gloomy, mournful, depressing|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From German *festlich*, derived from *Fest* (festival) + *-lich* (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *fastuz* (firm, fixed, solemn), related to Old English *fæst* (firm, fast).
