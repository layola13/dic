fugleman:/ˈfjuːɡəlmən/| n.|领队，先导，带头人|A soldier formerly posted in front of a body of troops to act as a guide in the movements of drill; hence, one who takes the lead or sets an example in any movement or concerted action.|The CEO acted as the fugleman for the company's new initiative.|近义词：leader, pioneer, vanguard, ringleader|反义词:follower|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Flügelmann*, from *Flügel* (wing) + *Mann* (man). Originally referred to a soldier who stood on the flank (wing) of a formation and guided the movements of others.
