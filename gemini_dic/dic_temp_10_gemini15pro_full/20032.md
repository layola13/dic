biomes:/ˈbaɪoʊmz/ | n. | 生物群落 | A large naturally occurring community of flora and fauna occupying a major habitat, e.g., forest or tundra. |  The Earth has many different biomes, each with its own unique climate and species. 地球上有许多不同的生物群落，每个生物群落都有其独特的气候和物种。 | synonyms: ecosystems, habitats, ecoregions | antonyms: N/A | noun
