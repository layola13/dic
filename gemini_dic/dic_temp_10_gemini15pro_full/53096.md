diphthongic:/dɪfˈθɒŋɪk/ /daɪfˈθɑːŋɪk/| adj.|双元音的|Relating to or consisting of a diphthong (a sound formed by the combination of two vowels in a single syllable, in which the sound begins as one vowel and moves towards another).|The word "boy" has a diphthongic sound.  单词"boy"包含一个双元音。|diphthongal|monophthongic|adjective
diphthong:/ˈdɪfθɒŋ/ /ˈdaɪfθɔːŋ/| n.|双元音|A sound formed by the combination of two vowels in a single syllable, in which the sound begins as one vowel and moves towards another.|The "oi" in "boil" is a diphthong. "boil"中的"oi"是一个双元音。|vowel glide, vowel combination|monophthong|noun
