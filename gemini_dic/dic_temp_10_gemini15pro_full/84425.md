hallucinate:/həˈluːsɪneɪt/ /həˈlusɪˌneɪt/| v. | 幻觉，产生幻觉 |To experience hallucinations; to see, hear, or sense things that are not really there. |  He began to hallucinate due to lack of sleep. 他由于缺乏睡眠开始出现幻觉。

She was hallucinating that spiders were crawling all over her. 她产生幻觉，觉得蜘蛛在她身上爬来爬去。


|近义词：fantasize, imagine, envision, delude|反义词: perceive, comprehend |词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin hallucinatus, past participle of hallucinari "to wander in mind, talk idly, dream," from Latin alucinari "to wander in mind," probably from Greek alyein "to be distraught, wander in mind."
