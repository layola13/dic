longiroster:/ˌlɒŋɪˈrɒstər/ | adj. | 长名册的；长名单的 | Having a long roster or list, typically of names. |  The coach had a longiroster of potential players to choose from. 教练有一份很长的潜在球员名单可供选择。

The longiroster team faced challenges in coordinating practice schedules.  长名单的队伍在协调练习时间表方面面临挑战。| 近义词: extensive, lengthy | 反义词: short, concise | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  "longiroster" is a combination of "long" and "roster."  "Long" comes from Old English "lang," meaning "having considerable linear extent." "Roster" comes from Dutch "rooster," meaning "grid" or "list," ultimately from Latin "ruber," meaning "red," referring to the practice of marking names on a list with red chalk.
