overriding:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪdɪŋ/| adj.|首要的，压倒一切的，最重要的|Having superior power and influence; prevailing over all other considerations.  It describes something that is more important than anything else.|例句：The overriding concern was the safety of the passengers.  最重要的考虑是乘客的安全。

例句：His overriding ambition is to become a world-renowned surgeon. 他最大的抱负是成为世界闻名的外科医生。|近义词：dominant, paramount, principal, chief, main, primary|反义词：secondary, subordinate, minor, insignificant|词性:形容词
词源:  From the verb "override," which combines "over" (indicating superiority or excess) and "ride" (originally meaning to travel on horseback, later extended to mean to dominate or control).
