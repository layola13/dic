inframedian:/ˌɪnfrəˈmiːdiən/
adjective
中间以下的；低于中值的
situated below the median
The inframedian nerve runs along the underside of the forearm.  尺侧中神经沿着前臂下侧延伸。
近义词:submedian, below average
反义词:supramedian, above average
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *infra* (below) + *medianus* (middle).
