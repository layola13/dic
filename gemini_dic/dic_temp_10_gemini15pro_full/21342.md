bloomingly:/ˈbluːmɪŋli/| adv.|盛开地，繁盛地|In a blooming manner; flourishingly; thrivingly.  Describing something that is growing or developing successfully and beautifully, often used figuratively.|The business is bloomingly successful.  生意兴隆。|近义词：flourishingly, thrivingly, prosperously|反义词：decliningly, witheringly|词性:副词

词源: From "blooming" + "-ly." "Blooming" comes from the verb "bloom," which has its roots in the Old Norse "blóma" meaning "to blossom" or "to flower."
