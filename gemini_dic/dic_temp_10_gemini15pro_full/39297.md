conicalnesses:/kɒnɪkəlnəsɪz/
noun
锥度，锥形度
the state or quality of being conical; the degree to which something is conical
The conicalnesses of the various volcano peaks differed significantly. 各个火山峰的锥度差异很大。
近义词: conicality, taper
反义词: cylindricalness, flatness
词性: noun

conicalness:/kɒnɪkəlnəs/
noun
锥度，锥形度
the state or quality of being conical; the degree to which something is conical
The conicalness of the volcano's peak was striking. 火山峰的锥度引人注目。
近义词: conicality, taper
反义词: cylindricalness, flatness
词性: noun

Etymology:
From conical + -ness.  Conical comes from cone + -ical. Cone ultimately derives from the Greek word *konos*.
