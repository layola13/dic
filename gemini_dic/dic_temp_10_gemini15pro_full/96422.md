indissoluble:/ˌɪndɪˈsɒljʊbl/ /ˌɪndɪˈsɑːljəbl/| adj.|不能溶解的；牢不可破的|Not able to be dissolved; (of a relationship or feeling) permanent. |例句：Their friendship was an indissoluble bond. 他们的友谊牢不可破。|近义词：permanent, lasting, unbreakable|反义词：dissoluble, breakable, separable|词性:形容词

indissoluble:/ˌɪndɪˈsɒljʊbl/ /ˌɪndɪˈsɑːljəbl/| adj.|不能分解的；永久的|Not able to be destroyed; lasting. |例句：The partners signed an indissoluble agreement. 合伙人签署了一份永久协议。|近义词：permanent, lasting, enduring|反义词：temporary, transient, fleeting|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *indissolubilis, from in- ‘not’ + dissolubilis ‘dissoluble’ (from dis- ‘apart’ + solvere ‘to loosen’).
