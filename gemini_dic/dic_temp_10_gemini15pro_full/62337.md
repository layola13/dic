endemically:/enˈdemɪkli/
adjective
地方性地；特有地
regularly found among particular people or in a certain area
Malaria is endemic in many hot countries. 疟疾在很多炎热的国家流行。
近义词: native, indigenous, aboriginal
反义词: non-native, introduced, exotic
词性: adjective
词源: From Greek *éndēmos* "native," from *en* "in" + *dēmos* "people."
