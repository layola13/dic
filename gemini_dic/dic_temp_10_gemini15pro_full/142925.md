out-tell:/ˌaʊtˈtɛl/
|v.|胜过；讲述胜过；比……讲得好|To surpass or excel in telling; to tell a story better than another.|He out-told all the other storytellers with his vivid descriptions and engaging characters. 他以生动的描写和引人入胜的人物胜过了所有其他讲故事的人。|Synonyms: outdo, surpass, excel|Antonyms:  be inferior to, fall short of|verb
out-tell:/ˌaʊtˈtɛl/
|v.|泄露（秘密）；揭发|To reveal or disclose (a secret).|She out-told his secret to everyone in the office. 她把他的秘密泄露给了办公室里的每个人。|Synonyms: reveal, disclose, divulge|Antonyms: conceal, hide|verb
