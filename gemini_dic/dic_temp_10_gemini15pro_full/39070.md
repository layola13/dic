conformists:/kənˈfɔːrmɪsts/| n.|墨守成规的人；遵奉者；顺从者|People who conform to accepted behavior or established practices.  Those who accept and follow the prevailing norms and rules of a society or group.|Many teenagers rebel against being conformists and strive to express their individuality. 许多青少年反抗墨守成规，努力表达自己的个性。|synonyms: followers, traditionalists, adherents|antonyms: rebels, nonconformists, dissenters|noun

conformist:/kənˈfɔːrmɪst/| adj.|墨守成规的；顺从的|Adhering to established customs or doctrines.  Behaving in a way that is considered socially acceptable.|His conformist attitude prevented him from challenging the status quo. 他墨守成规的态度使他无法挑战现状。|synonyms: conventional, orthodox, compliant|antonyms: rebellious, unconventional, nonconforming|adjective
