goad:/ɡoʊd/ (US), /ɡəʊd/ (UK)| n. vt.|刺激；激励；尖棒；驱使|Something that encourages, urges, or drives someone to do something.|The constant criticism acted as a goad, pushing her to improve her skills. 不断的批评就像一种刺激，促使她提高自己的技能。|He goaded the bull into charging. 他用尖棒刺激公牛冲锋。|近义词：spur, prod, urge, incite, provoke|反义词：deterrent, discouragement|noun, verb
