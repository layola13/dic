global:/ˈɡloʊbl/ (US) /ˈɡləʊbl/ (UK)| adj.|全球的，全世界的；总体的，全局的|Relating to the whole world; overall, taking everything into account.|例句：Global warming is a serious threat to our planet. 全球变暖是对我们星球的严重威胁。|例句：The company has a global network of offices. 这家公司拥有遍布全球的办事处网络。|近义词：worldwide, international, universal|反义词：local, regional, national|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *globalis, from Latin globus (“sphere, globe”).
