moraine:/məˈreɪn/| n. |冰碛|A mass of rocks and sediment carried down and deposited by a glacier.|The hikers scrambled over the steep moraine.  徒步旅行者爬过陡峭的冰碛。|Synonyms: glacial drift, till, debris|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "bedrock")|Noun


moraine:/məˈreɪn/| n. |冰碛物|An accumulation of earth and stones deposited by a glacier.|The valley was filled with moraines left by the retreating glacier.  山谷里充满了冰川消退后留下的冰碛物。|Synonyms:  glacial deposit, glacial till|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|Noun
