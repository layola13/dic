It describes a process where one element or component is added or incorporated without replacing or displacing another existing element.  This term is often used in scientific contexts, particularly in chemistry and materials science. |  The atoms were incorporated nonsubstitutionally into the crystal lattice. 这些原子非取代地结合到晶格中。 |  directly, additionally, integratively |  substitutionally | adverb
