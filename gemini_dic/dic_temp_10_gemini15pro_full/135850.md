nonprosecution:/ˌnɑːnprɑːsɪˈkjuːʃən/ | n. |不起诉，不予起诉|The act of not prosecuting someone for a crime.  The decision not to pursue legal action against someone. | The district attorney agreed to nonprosecution in exchange for his cooperation. 地区检察官同意不起诉他，以换取他的合作。

The nonprosecution of these cases sends the wrong message. 这些案件不予起诉传递了错误的信息。 | synonyms: immunity, pardon, amnesty | antonyms: prosecution, indictment | noun
