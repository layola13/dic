adonize:/ˈæd.ə.naɪz/ | v. |崇拜；极度爱慕；把…奉若神明|To love or admire someone excessively, almost to the point of worship.|She absolutely adonizes her older brother. 她绝对崇拜她的哥哥。

He adonized his wife, treating her like a goddess. 他把他的妻子奉若神明，把她当作女神一样对待。|近义词：worship, idolize, adore, venerate|反义词：despise, loathe, scorn|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Adonis, the beautiful youth loved by Aphrodite in Greek mythology.  Adonis became a symbol of male beauty and the object of intense love and admiration.  The verb "adonize" thus developed to mean treating someone with such intense admiration.  From Greek Adōnis.
