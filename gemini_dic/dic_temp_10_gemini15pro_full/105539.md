kiltlike:/ˈkɪltˌlaɪk/
adjective
像苏格兰短裙的
Resembling a kilt, especially in being pleated or hanging like a kilt.
The dancer wore a kiltlike garment.  舞者穿着一件像苏格兰短裙的服装。
kilt-like
similar to a kilt, pleated
skirt-like, kilted
formal, unlike a kilt
