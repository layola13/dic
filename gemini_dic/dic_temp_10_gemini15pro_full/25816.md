burgher:/ˈbɜːrɡər/ (US), /ˈbɜːɡə/ (UK)| n.|市民，公民；中产阶级|A citizen of a town or city, typically a member of the middle class.|例句：The burghers of the town gathered to discuss the new tax. 城镇的市民聚集在一起讨论新的税收。

例句：Many burghers were merchants and tradesmen. 许多市民是商人和工匠。|近义词：citizen, townsman, bourgeois|反义词:peasant, aristocrat|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *burger, from burg (“town, fortified place”) + -er (agent suffix). Cognate with German Bürger. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *burgs (“fortress, castle”).
