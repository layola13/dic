antiskeptical:/ˌæntiˈskɛptɪkəl/
adjective
反怀疑论的; 反对怀疑主义的
Opposed to or resisting skepticism; characterized by a lack of skepticism.
He presented an antiskeptical argument for the existence of God. 他提出了一个反对怀疑论的上帝存在的论点。
近义词:credulous, trusting, believing
反义词:skeptical, doubting, questioning
词性:adj.
