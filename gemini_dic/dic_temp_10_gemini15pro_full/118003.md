memorisable:/ˈmɛmərəbl̩/ (UK), /ˈmɛmərəbəl/ (US)| adj.|值得纪念的；容易记住的|Something that is memorable is easy to remember and worth remembering. It's likely to be remembered for a long time because it's special, significant, or enjoyable.|The trip to the Grand Canyon was a truly memorable experience.  去大峡谷的旅行是一次真正令人难忘的经历。|Synonyms: unforgettable, noteworthy, remarkable|Antonyms: forgettable, insignificant, unremarkable|adjective
