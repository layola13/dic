anthropometry:/ˌænθrəˈpɒmɪtri/ | n. |人体测量学|The study of human body measurements, especially on a comparative basis. |例句：Anthropometry plays a crucial role in ergonomics. 人体测量学在人体工程学中起着至关重要的作用。

例句：The researchers used anthropometry to design chairs that were comfortable for a wide range of people. 研究人员利用人体测量学设计适合各种人群的舒适座椅。|近义词：biometrics,  morphometry |反义词: None |词性: noun


Etymology: From French *anthropométrie*, from Ancient Greek ἄνθρωπος (ánthrōpos, “human being”) + μέτρον (métron, “measure”).
