ichthyotomist: /ˌɪkθiˈɒtəmɪst/
n.
鱼类解剖学家
A person who dissects or studies the anatomy of fishes.
The ichthyotomist carefully examined the internal organs of the shark. 鱼类解剖学家仔细检查了鲨鱼的内部器官。
The museum hired a skilled ichthyotomist to prepare the new specimens.  博物馆聘请了一位熟练的鱼类解剖学家来准备新的标本。
近义词: fish anatomist, fish dissector
反义词:  None


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ikhthus* (fish) + *-tomos* (cutter, dissector)
