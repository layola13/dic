compactors:/kəmˈpæktərz/
noun
压缩机; 压实机
Machines that compress material into a smaller volume.
The city uses compactors to reduce the volume of landfill waste. 城市使用压缩机来减少垃圾填埋场的垃圾量。
The company invested in new compactors to improve efficiency. 公司投资了新的压缩机以提高效率。
synonyms:crushers, balers, compacting machines
antonyms:expanders
n.
