chilognath:/ˈkaɪləɡnæθ/| n. |千足虫，倍足纲动物|A myriapod of the class Chilopoda, having a flattened body composed of numerous segments, each bearing one pair of legs. chilognath:/ˈkaɪləɡnæθ/| n. |千足虫，倍足纲动物|A myriapod of the class Diplopoda, having a cylindrical body composed of numerous double segments, most of which bear two pairs of legs.
