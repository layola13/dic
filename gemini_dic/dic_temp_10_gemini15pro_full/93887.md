immortal:/ɪˈmɔːrtl/ (US) /ɪˈmɔːtl/ (UK)| adj. n.|不朽的，永生的；不朽的人物|Not subject to death; living forever; enduring or remembered forever.|例句：The immortal gods of Olympus. 奥林匹斯山上的不朽之神。|近义词：eternal, everlasting, undying|反义词：mortal, perishable, finite|词性:形容词

immortal:/ɪˈmɔːrtl/ (US) /ɪˈmɔːtl/ (UK)| n.|不朽的人物，神仙|A being that lives forever.|例句：He was considered an immortal in the world of literature. 他被认为是文学界的不朽人物。|近义词：god, deity, divinity|反义词：mortal|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *immortel*, from Latin *immortalis* (“undying, immortal”), from *in-* (“not”) + *mortalis* (“mortal, subject to death”), from *mors* (“death”).
