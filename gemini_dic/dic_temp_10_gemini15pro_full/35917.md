cocreators:/ˌkoʊkriˈeɪtərz/
n.
共同创造者，共同创作者
People who create something together.
The cocreators of the project were recognized for their innovative work.  这个项目的共同创造者因其创新工作而受到表彰。
collaborators, partners, joint authors
opponents, adversaries
noun
