cindered:/ˈsɪndərd/
adjective
被煤渣覆盖的；烧成灰烬的
Covered with cinders; reduced to ashes.
The cindered remains of the house lay smoldering.  这栋房子烧成灰烬的残骸还在冒烟。
The landscape was cindered after the volcanic eruption. 火山爆发后，大地一片焦土。
Near Synonyms: ashen, charred, burnt
Near Antonyms: pristine, untouched, unburnt
词性: 形容词
