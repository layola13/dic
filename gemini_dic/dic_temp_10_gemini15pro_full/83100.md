gussy /ˈɡʌsi/| v. |打扮；装饰；修饰|To make something more attractive, especially in a showy or frivolous way.|She gussied herself up for the party. 她为派对精心打扮了一番。|近义词：decorate, adorn, embellish, primp|反义词：simplify, strip down|词性:动词
词源:  From the earlier term "gussie up," likely influenced by the proper name "Gussie," a diminutive of "Augustus" or "Augusta," sometimes used as a stereotypical name for a fashionable or flamboyant person.
