non-usable:/ˌnɒnˈjuːzəbl/ /ˌnɑːnˈjuːzəbl/| adj.|不可用的，无法使用的|Not able to be used.  Something that is broken, ineffective, or unsuitable for a particular purpose.|This pen is non-usable because it's out of ink. 这支笔不能用了，因为没墨了。|The software is non-usable on this operating system.  这个软件在这个操作系统上无法使用。|近义词：unusable, unusable, inoperable, dysfunctional|反义词：usable, functional, operational|词性:形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  From Latin *non, meaning "not".  This prefix negates the meaning of the word it is attached to.
usable (adjective): From the verb "use" + the suffix "-able", meaning "capable of being".
