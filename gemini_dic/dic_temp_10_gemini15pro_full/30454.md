centrepiece:/ˈsɛntərpiːs/| n.|核心，中心装饰品，|A decorative object placed in the centre of a table, such as a vase of flowers or a bowl of fruit.  指放置在桌子中央的装饰物，例如一瓶花或一碗水果。|The elaborate ice sculpture was the centrepiece of the buffet table. 精致的冰雕是自助餐桌的中心装饰。|synonyms: centerpiece, focal point, decoration, ornament|antonyms: periphery, edge, margin|noun

centrepiece:/ˈsɛntərpiːs/| n.|最重要的部分，|The most important or prominent part of something. 指某事物最重要或最突出的部分。|The reduction of the deficit was the centrepiece of the government's economic policy. 减少赤字是政府经济政策的核心。|synonyms: cornerstone, core, heart, crux|antonyms: minor detail, triviality, insignificance|noun
