carrells:/kəˈrɛlz/ | n. | 小单间（尤指图书馆中的） | Small individual study rooms or cubicles, typically found in libraries.  |  The library has carrells for students who need a quiet place to study. 图书馆为需要安静学习的学生提供了小单间。 |  例句：She spent hours in one of the library's carrells working on her research paper. 她花了好几个小时在图书馆的一个小单间里写研究论文。

例句：The carrells in the library were all occupied during finals week. 期末考试周图书馆的小单间都被占用了。 | 近义词：cubicles, study rooms, booths | 反义词: open space | 词性: noun
