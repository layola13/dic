covert:/ˈkʌvərt/ | adj. | 隐蔽的；秘密的 | Not openly acknowledged or displayed; secret. |  He glanced covert looks at the other diners. 他偷偷地瞥了其他用餐者一眼。 | synonyms: secret, clandestine, stealthy, furtive, surreptitious | antonyms: overt, open, public, unconcealed, manifest | adjective
