例句：She gave him a real jobation about leaving his dirty dishes in the sink. 她因为他把脏盘子留在水槽里狠狠地训斥了他一顿。

近义词：scolding, reprimand, lecture, rebuke, dressing-down|反义词:praise, commendation|词性:名词
