affiliated:/əˈfɪlieɪtɪd/ | adj. |附属的，隶属的|Closely associated with or connected to another organization or group; attached as a member or branch.|例句：The hospital is affiliated with the university. 这家医院附属于这所大学。|近义词：associated, connected, related, linked|反义词：independent, separate|词性:形容词

affiliate:/əˈfɪlieɪt/ | v. |使隶属，使附属|Officially attach or connect (a subsidiary group or person) to a larger organization.|例句：The local TV station is affiliated with a national network.  这家地方电视台隶属于一个全国性的电视网络。|近义词：join, attach, connect, link|反义词：separate, disconnect|词性:动词

affiliate:/əˈfɪlieɪt/ | n. |分支机构，附属机构|A person or organization officially attached to a larger body.|例句：Our New York affiliate is handling the account.  我们在纽约的分支机构正在处理这个账户。|近义词：branch, subsidiary, associate|反义词：parent company, headquarters|词性:名词
