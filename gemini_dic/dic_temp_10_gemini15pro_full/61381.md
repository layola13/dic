emblazoner:/ɪmˈbleɪzənər/
n.
纹章绘制员；装饰者
A person who emblazons coats of arms or other heraldic devices.  Someone who decorates something richly or elaborately.
The skilled emblazoner carefully painted the family crest onto the shield.  这位熟练的纹章绘制员小心翼翼地在盾牌上绘制了家族徽章。
The interior designer emblazoned the walls with vibrant murals.  室内设计师用鲜艳的壁画装饰墙壁。
近义词:decorator, ornamenter, herald
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider words like "deface" or "strip" in certain contexts)



ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *emblazonen*, from Old French *emblazoner* "to describe heraldically," from *blason* "coat of arms." Ultimately from Frankish *blaso* "horn," referring to the horn blown at tournaments to announce the arrival of a knight.
