conscience-stricken:| /ˈkɒnʃənsˌstrɪkən/ (US) /ˈkɒnʃənsˌstrɪkən/ (UK)| adj.|良心不安的，受良心谴责的| Experiencing a deep sense of guilt due to having acted against one's moral principles.|She was conscience-stricken after she lied to her parents.  她对父母撒谎后，感到良心不安。|近义词：guilty, remorseful, ashamed|反义词：unashamed, remorseless|词性:形容词
