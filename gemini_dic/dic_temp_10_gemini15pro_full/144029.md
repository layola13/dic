outreproducing:/ˌoʊtrɪprəˈdusɪŋ/ | v. |过度繁殖，生育过多|Reproducing at a rate that exceeds the carrying capacity of the environment. |例句：The rabbits are outreproducing their predators and are becoming a pest. 兔子繁殖速度超过了捕食者的承受能力，正在成为一种害虫。

例句：The bacteria are outreproducing and overwhelming the host's immune system. 细菌繁殖速度过快，压垮了宿主的免疫系统。|近义词：overproducing, overpopulating |反义词: underproducing, dwindling |词性:动词
