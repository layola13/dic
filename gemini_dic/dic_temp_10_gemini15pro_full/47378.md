dead:/ded/ | adj. adv. n.| 死的，无生命的；完全的，彻底的；麻木的；沉寂的 |  No longer alive; inanimate; complete; utter; numb; inactive. | The flowers are dead. 这些花死了。|Synonyms: deceased, lifeless, inanimate, extinct, departed | Antonyms: alive, living, animate, extant | adjective

dead:/ded/ | adv.| 完全地，绝对地；直接地，正对着地 | Completely, absolutely; directly, straight. | I was dead tired. 我累死了。|Synonyms: completely, absolutely, totally, utterly | Antonyms: partially, slightly, somewhat | adverb

dead:/ded/ | n.| 死者，亡灵 | The dead, deceased persons. | The dead were buried. 死者被埋葬了。|Synonyms: deceased, departed | Antonyms: living | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *dēad, from Proto-Germanic *daudaz, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰewh₂- (“to die”). Cognates include Dutch dood, German tot, and Latin fūnus (“funeral”).
