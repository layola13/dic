chordates:/ˈkɔːrdeɪts/ | n. | 脊索动物门 |Animals belonging to the phylum Chordata, possessing a notochord, a dorsal hollow nerve cord, pharyngeal slits, an endostyle, and a post-anal tail at some point during their development. |例句：Humans are chordates. 人类是脊索动物。


例句：Chordates include fish, amphibians, reptiles, birds, and mammals. 脊索动物包括鱼类、两栖类、爬行类、鸟类和哺乳类。|近义词：vertebrates (脊椎动物 -  most chordates are vertebrates) |反义词: invertebrates (无脊椎动物)|词性:noun
