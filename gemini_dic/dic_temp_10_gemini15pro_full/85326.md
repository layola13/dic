harplike:/ˈhɑːrpˌlaɪk/| adj.|像竖琴的，竖琴似的|Resembling a harp in sound or shape.|The wind chimes produced a delicate, harplike sound. 风铃发出了一种精致的、像竖琴一样的声音。|The willow branches, hanging down like a curtain, swayed in the breeze, creating a harplike effect. 柳树枝像窗帘一样垂下来，在微风中摇曳，营造出一种竖琴般的感觉。|近义词：lyrical, melodious, musical|反义词:discordant, harsh|词性:adjective

词源:
harp (n.) + -like (suffix)

"harp" comes from Old English "hearpe," from Proto-Germanic "*harpo," ultimately from Proto-Indo-European "*kerp-" (to pluck, curve).

"-like" is a suffix forming adjectives and meaning "resembling, characteristic of."
