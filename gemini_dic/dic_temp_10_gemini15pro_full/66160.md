excerpting:/ɪkˈsɜːptɪŋ/ | v. | 摘录，节选 | Taking a short extract from a film, broadcast, or piece of music or writing. |  She's excerpting from her new novel for the magazine. 她正在为杂志摘录她的新小说。|  He excerpted a few lines from the poem. 他从诗中摘录了几行。| synonyms: extracting, quoting, citing, abstracting | antonyms:  including, inserting, expanding | verb

excerpt:/ˈek.sɝːpt/ | n. | 摘录，节选 | A short extract from a film, broadcast, or piece of music or writing. | This excerpt is taken from her latest novel. 这段摘录取自她最新的小说。|  The article included excerpts from presidential speeches. 这篇文章收录了总统演讲的摘录。| synonyms: extract, passage, quotation, citation, abstract | antonyms: whole, entirety | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin excerptus, past participle of excerpere "to pick out," from ex- "out" + carpere "to pluck, harvest."
