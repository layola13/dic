osiris:/oʊˈsaɪrɪs/| n.|奥西里斯|Osiris is the Egyptian god of the afterlife, the underworld, and the dead. He is also associated with resurrection and fertility.  He is classically depicted as a green-skinned man with a pharaoh's beard, partially mummy-wrapped at the legs, wearing a distinctive atef crown, and holding a symbolic crook and flail.|Osiris is a prominent figure in Egyptian mythology. 奥西里斯是埃及神话中的一个重要人物。| The myth of Osiris and Isis is a central story in ancient Egyptian religion. 奥西里斯和伊西斯的神话是古埃及宗教的核心故事。|近义词:god, deity|反义词:mortal|词性:noun
