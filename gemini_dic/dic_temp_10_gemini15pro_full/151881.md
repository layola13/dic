passivizable:/ˈpæsɪvˌaɪzəbl/
| adj. |可被动化的|Able to be made passive; able to have a passive form.|The verb "eat" is passivizable – "The apple was eaten by John."|近义词: convertible (to passive),  |反义词: non-passivizable, active only|adjective
