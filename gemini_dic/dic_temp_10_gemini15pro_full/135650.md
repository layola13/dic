nonpredicative:/ˌnɒnprɪˈdɪkətɪv/ | adj. |非谓语的，非表语的 | Referring to a word or phrase, especially an adjective, that does not function as a predicate but modifies a noun directly.  A nonpredicative adjective directly modifies a noun, it's part of the noun phrase, and it doesn't describe the subject of the sentence.  | Example: The *running* man tripped. (running modifies man directly) | Synonym: attributive, | Antonym: predicative | Adjective
