alfalfa:/ælˈfælfə/| n.|紫花苜蓿|A leguminous plant with clover-like leaves and bluish-violet flowers, widely cultivated as fodder and as a soil-improving crop.  It is also known as lucerne.|例句：The farmer planted alfalfa in the field. 农民在地里种了紫花苜蓿。

例句：Alfalfa is a nutritious feed for livestock. 紫花苜蓿是牲畜的营养饲料。|近义词：lucerne|反义词:|名词
