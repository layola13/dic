milkiness:/ˈmɪlkɪnɪs/ | n. | 乳白色，乳状，牛奶般 |The milkiness of the liquid suggested it was contaminated.  | The opal had a beautiful milkiness. 欧泊有一种美丽的乳白色。 | 近义词: opacity, cloudiness | 反义词: clarity, transparency | 词性: 名词

词源:  From milky + -ness.  Ultimately from Old English *meoluc, from Proto-Germanic *meluks, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂mélǵ-. Cognate with Dutch melk, German Milch, Latin mulgeo (“to milk”).
