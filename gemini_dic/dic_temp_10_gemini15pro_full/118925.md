metempirics:/ˌmɛtɛmˈpɪrɪks/ | n. |形而上学经验论，超验经验论|The study of knowledge or evidence claimed to be beyond or outside of ordinary experience. It often involves exploring the nature of reality, consciousness, and spirituality, using methods that go beyond empirical observation and scientific experimentation.|  He delved into metempirics, seeking answers beyond the physical world. 他钻研形而上学经验论，在物质世界之外寻求答案。| Synonym: metaphysics, transcendentalism | Antonym: empiricism, materialism | Noun
