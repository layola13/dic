mooruk:/ˈmʊərʊk/| n. |摩洛加鸡，摩洛加巨鸡|A large flightless bird (Megapodius wallacei) native to the Moluccas and other islands of eastern Indonesia and Australasia. It builds large mounds of earth and decaying vegetation in which its eggs are incubated by the heat generated by decomposition. |例句：The mooruk lays its eggs in a mound of earth. 摩洛加鸡把蛋产在一个土堆里。

例句：The mooruk is a protected species in some areas. 在一些地区，摩洛加鸡是受保护的物种。|近义词：Megapode, Scrubfowl|反义词: (None - it's a specific species) |词性:noun
