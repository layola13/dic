adeptly:/ˈædəptli/ | adv. | 熟练地，巧妙地 | In a skillful and proficient manner. | She adeptly handled the difficult situation. 她巧妙地处理了这个棘手的局面。

He adeptly navigated the narrow streets. 他熟练地穿梭于狭窄的街道。

近义词: skillfully, expertly, proficiently, deftly, adroitly | 反义词: clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "adept" + "-ly."  "Adept" comes from Latin "adeptus" (past participle of "adipisci" - to attain, obtain), from "ad-" (to) + "apisci" (to get).  The suffix "-ly" is a common adverbial ending.

USAGE->
修饰动词:
She adeptly managed the project. = 她熟练地管理了这个项目。
He adeptly repaired the broken vase. = 他巧妙地修补了破碎的花瓶。

修饰形容词(较少见):
He was adeptly skillful in his craft. = 他在他的工艺方面非常熟练。 (虽然语法上可以接受，但不如直接说"He was very skillful in his craft.")


PHRASE->
(副词通常不构成短语，以下列出包含"adept"的短语)
adept at =  擅长于...
adept in = 精通于...
become adept at =  变得擅长...
highly adept at =  非常擅长...
less adept at =  不太擅长...
