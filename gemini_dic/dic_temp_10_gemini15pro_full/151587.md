parthenogenesis:/ˌpɑːrθənəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/| n.|单性生殖，孤雌生殖|Reproduction from an ovum without fertilization, especially as a normal process in certain invertebrates and lower plants.|例句：Parthenogenesis is a common form of reproduction in aphids. 蚜虫的常见繁殖方式是单性生殖。

例句：Some lizards reproduce by parthenogenesis. 一些蜥蜴通过单性生殖繁殖。|近义词：apomixis, asexual reproduction|反义词：sexual reproduction|词性:noun

Etymology: From Greek *parthenos* (virgin) + *genesis* (origin, creation).
