nonlyricisms:/nɒnˈlɪrɪsɪzəmz/
n.
缺乏抒情性；非抒情性
Absence of lyricism or lyrical qualities; the state of being non-lyrical.
His recent poems are marked by a certain nonlyricism, focusing more on narrative than emotional expression. 他最近的诗歌以某种非抒情性为特征，更多地关注叙事而不是情感表达。
近义词：prosaicness, matter-of-factness, unpoeticalness
反义词：lyricism, poeticness, melodiousness
词性：名词
