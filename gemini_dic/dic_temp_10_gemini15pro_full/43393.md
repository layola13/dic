crankier: /ˈkræŋ.ki.ər/ (US) /ˈkræŋ.kɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更暴躁的，更易怒的|Comparative form of cranky: more cranky.  Meaning more irritable or bad-tempered.|例句：He's been much crankier since he got sick. 他自从生病以来就变得更加暴躁了。|近义词：grumpier, more irritable, more bad-tempered|反义词：cheerier, happier, more good-natured|词性:形容词
crankier:|Etymology: From crank + -y + -er.  Crank (in the sense of irritable) is of uncertain origin, possibly related to an earlier sense of "weak" or "ill."|词源：来自 crank + -y + -er。Crank（易怒的意思）来源不确定，可能与早期“虚弱”或“生病”的意思有关。
