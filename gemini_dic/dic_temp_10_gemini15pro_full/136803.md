nonsharing:/ˌnɒnˈʃɛərɪŋ/ | adj. | 不共享的，非共享的 | Not sharing; used to describe something that is not shared or intended to be shared between multiple users or systems. |  This printer is nonsharing and designated for the design team only.  这台打印机是非共享的，仅供设计团队使用。

| Example: The nonsharing nature of the data made collaboration difficult.  数据的非共享性使得协作变得困难。 | Synonyms: exclusive, private, dedicated | Antonyms: shared, common, joint | adjective
