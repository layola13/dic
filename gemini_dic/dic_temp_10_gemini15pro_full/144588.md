outwaved:/ˌaʊtˈweɪvd/
verb
超过，胜过（在挥手方面）
surpass or defeat someone by waving more vigorously or enthusiastically.
The enthusiastic fans outwaved the visiting team's supporters.  热情的球迷在挥手中胜过了客队的支持者。
The children outwaved their parents as the ship pulled away from the dock. 当船驶离码头时，孩子们向父母挥手告别，挥手的力度超过了父母。
Synonym: outdo, surpass, excel
Antonym:  be outdone by, be surpassed by
词性: 动词
