contemplators:/ˈkɒntəmpleɪtəz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntəmpleɪtərz/ (US)| n. |沉思者；冥想者；考虑者|People who contemplate; those who engage in deep thought or reflection.|The contemplators sat in silence, pondering the meaning of life. 沉思者们静静地坐着，思考着生命的意义。|Example: The artist portrayed the contemplators as figures lost in deep thought.  这位艺术家将沉思者描绘成陷入沉思的人物。|Synonyms: thinkers, meditators, ponderers, reflectors, ruminators|Antonyms: doers, actors, activists|Noun

contemplate:/ˈkɒntəmpleɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntəmpleɪt/ (US)| v. |沉思；冥想；考虑|To think profoundly and at length; to consider thoroughly.|She contemplated the implications of her decision. 她仔细考虑了她决定的含义。|Example:  He contemplated the vastness of the universe. 他思考着宇宙的浩瀚。|Synonyms: consider, ponder, meditate, reflect, mull over|Antonyms: disregard, ignore, neglect|Verb
