nonprescribed:/ˌnɑːnprɪˈskraɪbd/ /ˌnɒnprɪˈskraɪbd/
adjective
未开处方的，非处方药的
not prescribed by a doctor; over-the-counter.
She bought some nonprescribed pain relievers at the pharmacy. 她从药店买了一些非处方止痛药。
synonyms: over-the-counter, nonprescription, off-the-shelf
antonyms: prescribed, prescription
词性: adjective
