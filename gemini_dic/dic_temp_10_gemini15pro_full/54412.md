disinclination's:/ˌdɪsɪnklɪˈneɪʃənz/
n.
不情愿，不愿意
lack of willingness or desire; reluctance
Her disinclination's to participate were obvious. 她明显不愿意参加。
近义词: reluctance, unwillingness, aversion
反义词: inclination, willingness, eagerness
词性: 名词 (Noun, specifically possessive form)

词源:
From dis- (not) + inclination (a tendency to do something)
