antiministerial:/ˌæntiˌmɪnɪˈstɪəriəl/
adjective
反对政府的；反内阁的
Opposed to the government or the ministry.
The newspaper published several antiministerial articles. 这家报纸发表了几篇反对政府的文章。
Synonyms:  antigovernment, oppositional
Antonyms: pro-government, supportive
词性: 形容词
