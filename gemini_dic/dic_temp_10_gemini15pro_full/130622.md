nonadjudicatively:/ˌnɒnəˈdʒuːdɪkətɪvli/
adv.
非裁决地，非判决地
Without making a formal judgment or ruling; informally.  The matter was resolved nonadjudicatively through mediation. 这件事是通过调解非裁决地解决的。
例句：
The issue was discussed nonadjudicatively in a private meeting.
这个问题在一个非正式会议上非裁决性地进行了讨论。

The dispute was settled nonadjudicatively, saving both parties time and money.
这场纠纷以非裁决的方式解决，为双方节省了时间和金钱。

近义词:informally, extrajudicially
反义词:judicially, formally
词性:副词
