incensive:/ɪnˈsɛntɪv/| adj. n.|激励的，刺激的；刺激，鼓励|Something that incites or tends to incite to action or greater effort, as a reward offered for increased productivity.|A bonus can be a good incentive for better work. 奖金可以成为鼓励更好工作的良好激励。|近义词：motivation, stimulus, inducement, encouragement|反义词：deterrent, discouragement|词性:形容词,名词
Etymology: From Late Latin *incentivus, from Latin incendere “to kindle, set on fire,” from in- “in” + candēre “to glow, shine.”
