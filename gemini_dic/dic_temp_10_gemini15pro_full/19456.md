bicycled: /ˈbaɪsɪkəld/
verb
骑自行车，骑自行车旅行
past tense and past participle of bicycle  (是bicycle的过去式和过去分词)
She bicycled to work every day. 她每天骑自行车上班。
近义词: cycled, rode (a bike)
反义词: walked, drove, flew
词性: verb (动词)


bicycle: /ˈbaɪsɪkəl/
noun
自行车
a vehicle with two wheels, a seat, and pedals
He got a new bicycle for his birthday. 他生日得到了一辆新自行车。
近义词: bike, cycle
反义词: car, bus, train
词性: noun (名词)


bicycle: /ˈbaɪsɪkəl/
verb
骑自行车
to ride a bicycle
I bicycled to the park yesterday. 我昨天骑自行车去了公园。
近义词: cycle, ride (a bike)
反义词: walk, drive, fly
词性: verb (动词)


词源: bi- (two) + cycle (wheel, circle)  来自于拉丁语 bi- (两个) +  希腊语 kyklos (圆圈, 轮子)
