local-area-networks: /ˈləʊkəl ˈɛəriə ˈnɛtwɜːks/ | n. | 局域网 | A computer network that interconnects computers within a limited area such as a residence, school, laboratory, university campus or office building. | Our office uses local-area-networks to share files and printers. 我们的办公室使用局域网共享文件和打印机。| Synonym: LANs, Ethernet | Antonym: Wide-area-networks (WANs) | Noun
