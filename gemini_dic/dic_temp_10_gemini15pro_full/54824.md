dispersibility:/dɪˌspɜːrsəˈbɪləti/ | n. |分散性，分散能力|The dispersibility of the pigment in the paint is crucial for a smooth finish. 颜料在油漆中的分散性对于光滑的表面至关重要。|例句：The high dispersibility of the powder makes it easy to mix with water.  这种粉末的高分散性使其易于与水混合。

例句：The dispersibility of the nanoparticles was enhanced by the addition of a surfactant.  通过添加表面活性剂增强了纳米颗粒的分散性。|近义词：dispersity, spreadability |反义词：agglomeration, clumping |词性:noun

例句：Police used tear gas to disperse the protesters. 警察用催泪瓦斯驱散抗议者。|近义词：scatter, distribute, disseminate |反义词：gather, collect, congregate |词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dispersabilis, from Latin dispers- (stem of dispersus), past participle of dispergere "to scatter," from dis- "apart" + spargere "to strew, scatter".
