accustoming: /əˈkʌstəmɪŋ/
verb
使习惯于
The process of making someone or something familiar with or used to something.
She is accustoming herself to the new work schedule. 她正在让自己适应新的工作时间表。

He is accustoming the horse to loud noises. 他正在让马习惯于巨大的噪音。

近义词: adapting, adjusting, habituating, inuring
反义词: unaccustoming, disaccustoming
词性: 动名词 (present participle)

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "accustom," which comes from Old French "acoustumer," meaning "to make accustomed to." This is further derived from the Latin "ad" (to) + "consuetudo" (custom, habit).
