bustlingly:/ˈbʌslɪŋli/
adverb
喧闹地；熙熙攘攘地
In a busy and energetic way.
The market was bustlingly full of people. 市场里熙熙攘攘，挤满了人。
The bees flew bustlingly around the hive. 蜜蜂在蜂巢周围嗡嗡地飞来飞去。
近义词: busily, actively, energetically
反义词: quietly, calmly, peacefully
词性: adverb

bustle:/ˈbʌsl/
verb/noun
喧闹；熙熙攘攘；忙碌
(v.) To move about in a hurried and energetic way.
(n.) Energetic and noisy activity.
(v.)  She bustled around the kitchen, preparing dinner.  她在厨房里忙着准备晚餐。
(n.) The bustle of the city streets excited her. 城市街道的喧嚣让她兴奋。
近义词: (v.) rush, hurry, dash; (n.) activity, commotion, hustle
反义词: (v.) dawdle, linger; (n.) quiet, stillness, calm
词性: verb/noun

-ly:/-li/
suffix
构成副词的后缀
Used to form adverbs from adjectives.
quickly, slowly, happily
快速地，缓慢地，快乐地
近义词: N/A (suffixes don't have synonyms)
反义词: N/A (suffixes don't have antonyms)
词性: suffix

词源:
From bustle + -ly. Bustle itself comes from the Middle English *bustelen*, frequentative of *busen*, "to prepare, make ready," probably of Scandinavian origin.
