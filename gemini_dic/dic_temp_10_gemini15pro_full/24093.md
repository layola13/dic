breviary:/ˈbriːviəri/ /ˈbreɪviˌɛri/| n. |祈祷书；摘要；概要|A book containing the daily prayers, hymns, and readings prescribed for use by priests and other clergy, especially in the Roman Catholic Church.  A shortened or concise version of a longer work.|The priest read from his breviary. 神父读着他的祈祷书。|synonym: abstract, compendium, epitome, summary, synopsis|antonym: expansion, elaboration, detail|noun
