cirrate:/ˈsɪreɪt/ | adj. | 卷须状的，有卷须的 | Having cirri (thin, flexible, hairlike appendages) | Barnacles are cirrate animals, using their feathery cirri to filter food from the water. 藤壶是卷须动物，用它们羽毛状的卷须从水中过滤食物。|  近义词: ciliated, fringed | 反义词: smooth, bare | 词性: 形容词

cirri:/ˈsɪraɪ/ | n. | 卷须 | (plural of cirrus) A slender, flexible, hairlike or threadlike appendage of an animal or plant. | The cirri of the sea anemone waved gently in the current. 海葵的卷须在水流中轻轻摇摆。| 近义词: tentacle, filament | 反义词:  (No direct opposite) | 词性: 名词

词源: From Latin *cirrus* "curl, tuft of hair," from Proto-Indo-European *\*kers-* "to project, run."
