dullest:/ˈdʌlɪst/| adj.|最迟钝的，最无聊的，最阴暗的|Superlative form of dull: most dull.  It means lacking interest or excitement; monotonous. It can also refer to lacking brightness, shine, or vibrancy. Finally, it can describe someone slow to understand or perceive.|The dullest lecture I've ever attended.  我参加过的最无聊的讲座。|Synonyms:  tedious, boring, monotonous, drab, gloomy|Antonyms:  interesting, exciting, vibrant, bright, sharp|adjective
