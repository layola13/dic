evangelizer: /ɪˈvændʒəlaɪzər/ | n. | 福音传道者，传播者 | A person who seeks to convert others to their religion or belief system, especially Christianity.  One who actively promotes or spreads a doctrine or cause. |  Example: The evangelizer traveled the country, sharing his beliefs.  这位福音传道者走遍全国，分享他的信仰。

Example:  She's a passionate evangelizer for organic food. 她是一位热情的有机食品推广者。 | 近义词: missionary, apostle, preacher, advocate, proponent | 反义词: opponent, antagonist, critic | 词性: noun
