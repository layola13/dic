diplomatic:/ˌdɪpləˈmætɪk/| adj.|外交的；圆滑的；老练的|Skilled in dealing with sensitive matters or people; tactful.  Relating to diplomacy and international relations.|She gave a diplomatic answer, avoiding any mention of the controversial issue. 她给出了一个外交辞令式的回答，避免提及任何有争议的问题。|Synonyms: tactful, discreet, polite, sensitive, judicious|Antonyms: undiplomatic, tactless, blunt, clumsy, insensitive|adjective

diplomatically:/ˌdɪpləˈmætɪkli/| adv.|外交地；圆滑地；老练地|In a tactful and sensitive way.|He diplomatically handled the delicate situation. 他老练地处理了这个微妙的局面。|Synonyms: tactfully, discreetly, politely, sensitively, judiciously|Antonyms: undiplomatically, tactlessly, bluntly, clumsily, insensitively|adverb
