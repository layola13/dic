fleshliest:/ˈflɛʃliɪst/
adj.|最肉体的，最肉欲的，最世俗的|Most carnal, sensual, or worldly.|例句：His fleshliest desires consumed him. 他最肉欲的欲望吞噬了他。|例句：She described the scene with fleshliest details. 她用最肉体的细节描述了这个场景。|近义词：carnal, sensual, earthly, worldly|反义词：spiritual, ascetic, ethereal|词性:形容词

词源:
From flesh + -ly + -est.  "Flesh" comes from Old English *flæsc, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *flaiska-. "-ly" is a suffix forming adjectives from nouns, from Old English -līc. "-est" is a superlative suffix, from Old English -est.
