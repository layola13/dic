noncorrelation:/ˌnɒnkɒrəˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 无相关性，不相关 | Absence of a relationship between two or more variables; the variables do not influence each other. | There is a noncorrelation between hair color and intelligence.  头发颜色和智力之间没有关联性。|  The study showed a noncorrelation between income level and happiness. 研究表明，收入水平和幸福感之间没有相关性。| independence, unrelatedness | correlation, relationship | noun
