misprescribes:/ˌmɪsprɪˈskraɪbz/
verb
错误地开处方；开错药
to prescribe (medication) wrongly or incorrectly.
The doctor misprescribed the medication, leading to adverse side effects.  医生开错了药，导致了不良副作用。
The antibiotic was misprescribed, and the patient's condition worsened.  抗生素开错了，病人的病情恶化了。
misprescribe, overprescribe, underprescribe
prescribe correctly
