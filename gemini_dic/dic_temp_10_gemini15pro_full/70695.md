finalizing:/ˈfaɪnəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
完成；敲定；定稿
The process of putting the final touches on something, making it complete and ready.
They are finalizing the details of the contract. 他们正在敲定合同的细节。
Synonyms: concluding, completing, finishing
Antonyms: beginning, commencing, initiating
词性: 动词

finalized:/ˈfaɪnəlaɪzd/
verb
完成；敲定；定稿（finalize的过去式和过去分词形式）
Completed, finalized, put into final form.
The agreement was finalized last week. 协议上周已敲定。
Synonyms: concluded, completed, finished
Antonyms:  unresolved, incomplete, pending
词性: 动词


词源: From the verb "finalize," which is formed from "final" + "-ize." "Final" comes from the Latin "finalis," meaning "of or pertaining to the end."
