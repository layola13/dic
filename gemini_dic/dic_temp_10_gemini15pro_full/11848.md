arrha:/ˈɑːrə/ | n. |定金; 订金; 保证金 (尤指结婚时的)| An earnest payment; a pledge, especially in the context of marriage.  It originates from the ancient Roman custom of giving a token or down payment to secure a contract, particularly a marriage agreement.|  The groom gave an arrha to the bride's father to seal the betrothal.  新郎给新娘的父亲一笔定金以确定婚约。 | synonyms: deposit, earnest money, pledge, down payment | antonyms: refund, reimbursement | noun
