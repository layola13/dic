outbuys:/ˌaʊtˈbaɪz/
|v.| 超过…的购买力；买得比…多|
|verb (used with object), out·bought, out·buy·ing.|
|To buy more or at a greater cost than another; to surpass in buying.|
|例句：Company A outbuys Company B in raw materials. A公司在原材料采购上超过了B公司。|
|例句：The wealthy collector outbought everyone at the auction.  这位富有的收藏家在拍卖会上出价超过了所有人。|
|近义词：outspend, surpass, exceed|
|反义词：underspend|
|词性：动词|
