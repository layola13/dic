cover-up:/ˈkʌvərˌʌp/ | n. | 掩盖；隐瞒 | An attempt to prevent something from becoming known. | The government tried to cover up the scandal. 政府试图掩盖这起丑闻。| Example: The mayor's sudden resignation was a cover-up for his illegal activities. 市长突然辞职是为了掩盖他的非法活动。 | Synonyms: concealment, suppression, whitewash | Antonyms: revelation, disclosure | noun

cover-up:/ˈkʌvərˌʌp/ | v. | 掩盖；隐瞒 | To attempt to prevent something from becoming known. | They tried to cover up their mistakes. 他们试图掩盖他们的错误。 | Example: The company tried to cover up the data breach. 公司试图掩盖数据泄露事件。 | Synonyms: conceal, suppress, whitewash | Antonyms: reveal, disclose | verb
