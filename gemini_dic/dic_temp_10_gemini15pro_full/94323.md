impetuously: /ɪmˈpɛtʃuəsli/ | adv. | 冲动地，鲁莽地 | Done suddenly and without thinking carefully about the consequences. | She impetuously quit her job without another one lined up. 她一时冲动辞掉了工作，却没有找到另一份工作。| He impetuously kissed her, surprising both of them. 他冲动地吻了她，让他们俩都感到惊讶。 | 近义词: rashly, impulsively, hastily | 反义词: cautiously, deliberately, thoughtfully | 词性: 副词 | 词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin impetuosus, from impetus ‘attack, impulse’.
