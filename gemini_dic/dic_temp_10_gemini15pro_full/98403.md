insolvent: /ɪnˈsɒlvənt/ (UK) /ɪnˈsɑːlvənt/ (US)| adj. |无力偿还的，破产的|Unable to pay debts owed.  |  The company was declared insolvent after accumulating massive losses.  这家公司因累计巨额亏损而被宣布破产。 | bankrupt, broke | solvent, affluent | adjective

insolvent: /ɪnˈsɒlvənt/ (UK) /ɪnˈsɑːlvənt/ (US)| n. |破产者，无力偿还者|A person or company that is insolvent. | The insolvent was forced to sell his assets to pay off his creditors.  这个破产者被迫出售资产以偿还债权人。 | bankrupt, debtor | creditor, millionaire | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *insolvens, present participle of insolvere “not to loosen, not to pay,” from in- “not” + solvere “to loosen, pay.”
