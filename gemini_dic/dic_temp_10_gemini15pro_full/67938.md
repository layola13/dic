facet:/ˈfæsɪt/| n. |方面，面，刻面|One of the flat polished surfaces cut on a gemstone or other precious stone. A particular aspect or feature of something.|The diamond had 12 facets.|Synonyms: aspect, feature, side, angle, characteristic, element, factor, component|Antonyms: whole, entirety|noun

facet:/ˈfæsɪt/| v. |刻面|Cut facets on (a gemstone).|The stones are faceted to enhance their brilliance.|Synonyms: cut, shape, polish|Antonyms: roughen, dull|verb
