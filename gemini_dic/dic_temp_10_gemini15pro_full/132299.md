nondefiant:/ˌnɒndɪˈfaɪənt/
adjective
不服从的，不反抗的
Not exhibiting defiance; submissive or cooperative.
The nondefiant prisoner cooperated with the guards.  不反抗的囚犯与警卫合作。
He raised his hands in a nondefiant gesture. 他举起双手，做出一个不反抗的姿势。
近义词:submissive, compliant, yielding, obedient
反义词:defiant, rebellious, resistant, opposing
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- + defiant.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not," and defiant means "showing resistance or opposition."
