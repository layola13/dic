blether:/ˈblɛðər/ (UK), /ˈblɛðər/ (US)| v. n.|闲聊，胡扯；废话；喋喋不休|To talk at length, especially about unimportant things.|She blethered on for ages about her new shoes. 她絮絮叨叨地讲了很久她的新鞋子。|近义词：chatter, prattle, ramble, babble|反义词：be quiet, be silent|词性:动词,名词
blether:/ˈblɛðər/ (UK), /ˈblɛðər/ (US)| n.|闲聊，胡扯|Foolish or pointless talk.|I've had enough of your blether! 我受够了你的废话！|近义词：nonsense, gibberish, drivel|反义词：sense, wisdom|词性:名词
