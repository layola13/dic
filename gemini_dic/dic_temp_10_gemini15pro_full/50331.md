deplore:/dɪˈplɔːr/
verb
公开谴责；强烈反对；对…深感遗憾
express strong disapproval of; regret deeply; lament
The UN deplored the invasion as a violation of international law. 联合国谴责这次入侵，认为它违反了国际法。
synonyms: lament, bewail, condemn, criticize, denounce
antonyms: approve, commend, praise, admire
词性: 动词

deplore:/dɪˈplɔːr/
verb
对…深感遗憾；痛惜
feel or express strong disapproval of (something)
She deplored the lack of funding for the arts. 她对艺术资金的匮乏深感遗憾。
synonyms: regret, lament, mourn, grieve
antonyms: celebrate, rejoice, welcome
词性: 动词
