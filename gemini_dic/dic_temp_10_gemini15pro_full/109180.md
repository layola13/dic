legitimize: /lɪˈdʒɪtɪmaɪz/ | vt. | 使合法化，使正当化 | To make something that was previously considered illegal or unacceptable, legal or acceptable. | The government passed a law to legitimize gambling. 政府通过了一项法律使赌博合法化。

The company tried to legitimize its questionable practices by claiming they were industry standard.  这家公司试图通过声称其有问题的做法是行业标准来使其合法化。


近义词: validate, justify, authorize, legalize | 反义词: outlaw, prohibit, ban, criminalize | 词性: verb (动词)


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English: from Old French legitimer, from medieval Latin legitimare, from Latin legitimus ‘lawful’.
