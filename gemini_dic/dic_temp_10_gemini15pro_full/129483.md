non-amortizable:/ˌnɒn əˈmɔːrtaɪzəbl/ (US) /ˌnɒn əˈmɔːtɪzəbl/ (UK)| adj.|不可摊销的|Not subject to amortization; not able to be gradually written off over a period of time.|The purchase of land is considered a non-amortizable asset. 购买土地被认为是不可摊销的资产。|Non-depreciable, non-amortisable|Amortizable, depreciable|adjective
词源->From non- + amortizable, from amortize + -able. Ultimately from Latin *admortire, from ad- (“to, towards”) + mors (“death”).
