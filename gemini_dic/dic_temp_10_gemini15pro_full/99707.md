interloper: /ˈɪntərˌloʊpər/ (US), /ˈɪntəˌləʊpə(r)/ (UK)| n. |侵入者，干涉者，无理插手他人事务者 |Someone who becomes involved in a place or situation where they are not wanted or are considered not to belong. | The manager viewed the consultant as an interloper in his department. 经理认为这位顾问是他部门里的不速之客。

He resented the interloper who tried to interfere in his family's affairs. 他憎恨那个试图干涉他家事的入侵者。


近义词: intruder, trespasser, meddler, busybody
反义词: guest, invitee
词性: noun
