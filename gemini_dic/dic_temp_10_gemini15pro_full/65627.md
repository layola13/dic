evangelism:/ɪˈvændʒəlɪzəm/
|n.|福音主义，传福音|The act of preaching the gospel or spreading religious beliefs.|例句：Her evangelism led her to missionary work in Africa. 她的福音主义使她前往非洲从事传教工作。
|例句：Christian evangelism is central to the faith. 基督教的传福音是信仰的核心。
|近义词：proselytism, missionary work, preaching|反义词：secularism, atheism|词性：名词

evangelize:/ɪˈvændʒəlaɪz/
|v.|传福音，宣传|To preach the gospel; to convert or seek to convert someone to Christianity.|例句：They evangelized in remote villages. 他们在偏远的村庄传福音。
|例句：He felt a calling to evangelize to the lost. 他感到了一种向迷途之人传福音的使命。
|近义词：proselytize, preach, convert|反义词：discourage, dissuade|词性：动词

evangelistic:/ˌiːvænˈdʒelɪstɪk/
|adj.|福音的，传福音的|Of or relating to evangelism.|例句：He has a strong evangelistic zeal. 他有很强的传福音的热情。
|例句：The evangelistic campaign drew large crowds. 传福音运动吸引了大批群众。
|近义词：missionary, evangelical, proselytizing|反义词：secular, nonreligious|词性：形容词
