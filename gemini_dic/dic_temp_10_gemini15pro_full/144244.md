outsmelling:/ˌaʊtˈsmɛlɪŋ/
adjective
胜过…的气味; 比…难闻
surpassing in smell; emitting a stronger or more unpleasant odor than something else.
例句：The durian was outsmelling the garbage.  榴莲的气味比垃圾还难闻。

例句：His cologne was outsmelling everyone else's perfume. 他的古龙水比其他人的香水味道都浓烈。

近义词：outstinking
反义词：fragrant, sweet-smelling
词性：adjective
