countertransport:/ˈkaʊntərˌtrænspɔːrt/| n. |反向转运，对向转运|The simultaneous transport of two different molecules across a cell membrane in opposite directions.|Example 1: Glucose transport into some cells occurs by countertransport with sodium ions. 葡萄糖进入某些细胞是通过与钠离子的反向转运发生的。

Example 2:  The countertransport mechanism helps maintain cellular homeostasis. 反向转运机制有助于维持细胞稳态。|近义词：antiport, exchange diffusion|反义词: cotransport, symport|词性: noun
