nazgûl:/ˈnɑːzgʊl/ | n. |戒灵，纳兹古尔|A Nazgûl, also called a Ringwraith, is a fictional creature in J.R.R. Tolkien's Middle-earth legendarium, particularly his most famous work, The Lord of the Rings. They were once Men, great kings of Men, who were corrupted by Sauron and enslaved by nine Rings of Power. |The Nazgûl pursued Frodo relentlessly. 戒灵无情地追捕着弗罗多。
