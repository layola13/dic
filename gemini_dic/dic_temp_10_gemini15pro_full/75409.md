fructuary:/ˈfruːk.tʃu.er.i/| adj. n.|收益的，享有收益的；收益人|Relating to the right to use and enjoy the products or profits of something, but not to own it; a person who has this right.|She holds a fructuary interest in the estate. 她持有该地产的收益权。|近义词：beneficiary, usufructuary|反义词:owner, proprietor|词性:adjective, noun
Etymology: From Latin *fructuarius* "fruitful," from *fructus* "fruit, enjoyment, profit."
