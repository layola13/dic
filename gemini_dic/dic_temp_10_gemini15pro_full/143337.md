outequivocates:/ˌaʊtɪˈkwɪvəkeɪts/
verb
用模棱两可的话胜过；以含糊其辞的方式击败
outdoes someone in equivocating; surpasses in the use of ambiguous language.
The politician outequivocated his opponent, leaving the audience confused but impressed.  这位政客用模棱两可的话胜过了他的对手，让观众感到困惑但印象深刻。
近义词:outmaneuvers, outsmarts
反义词:clarifies, specifies
词性:v.
