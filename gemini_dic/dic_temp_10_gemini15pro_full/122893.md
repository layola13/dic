mollycoddles:/ˈmɒlɪˌkɒd(ə)lz/| v.|娇惯，溺爱|To treat someone, especially a man, in an indulgent or overprotective way. |He was mollycoddled as a child and never learned to be independent. 他从小被娇生惯养，从未学会独立。|近义词：pamper, spoil, cosset, indulge|反义词:neglect, discipline|词性:动词
