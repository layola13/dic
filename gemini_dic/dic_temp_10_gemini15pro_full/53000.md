dinners:/ˈdɪnərz/
|n.|晚餐，正餐|The main meal of the day, typically eaten in the evening.|We had a delicious dinner at a new restaurant last night. 我们昨晚在一家新餐馆吃了一顿美味的晚餐。|
|近义词：meals, suppers|反义词：breakfasts, lunches|词性：名词|
dinners:/ˈdɪnərz/
|n.|宴会，晚宴|A formal evening meal, often for a special occasion.|The company held a celebratory dinner for its employees. 公司为员工举办了一场庆祝晚宴。|
|近义词：banquets, feasts|反义词：snacks, light refreshments|词性：名词|
