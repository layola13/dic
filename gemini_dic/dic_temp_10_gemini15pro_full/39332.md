conjectured:/kənˈdʒɛktʃərd/
conjectured|adj.|推测的，猜想的|Past participle of "conjecture," meaning something inferred or guessed, but not necessarily proven.|It was conjectured that the painting was a forgery. 人们推测这幅画是赝品。|
conjectured|v.|推测，猜想|Past tense of "conjecture," meaning to form an opinion or supposition about something on the basis of incomplete information.|He conjectured that the stock market would crash. 他推测股市将会崩盘。|
Synonyms: supposed, assumed, presumed, hypothesized, theorized|
Antonyms: proven, demonstrated, confirmed, established|
Word type: Verb (past tense/past participle), Adjective


Etymology:
From Late Latin conjecturatus, past participle of conicere "to throw together," from com- "together" + jacere "to throw."
