paperless:/ˈpeɪpərlɪs/| adj.|无纸的|Characterized by the absence of paper; conducted or recorded electronically.|例句：The company is striving to become a paperless office.  这家公司正努力成为无纸化办公。|例句：Paperless billing is becoming increasingly popular. 无纸化账单越来越受欢迎。|近义词：digital, electronic|反义词：paper-based|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From paper + -less.  "Paper" originates from the Latin "papyrus," referring to the ancient Egyptian writing material.  "-less" is a suffix indicating the absence of something.
