nuclear-energies:/ˈnjuːkliər ˈɛnərdʒiz/ | n. | 核能（复数形式，不常用） | Nuclear energy is the energy released during nuclear fission or fusion. |  Nuclear-energies (less common than the singular form) are being explored as alternatives to fossil fuels. 核能（复数形式）正在被探索作为化石燃料的替代品。| 近义词: nuclear power, atomic energy | 反义词: fossil fuels | 词性: 名词

词源:
nuclear: From Latin *nuclearis, from nucleus (“kernel, nut, stone of a fruit”).
energy: From Late Latin energia, from Ancient Greek ἐνέργεια (enérgeia, “activity, operation”), from ἐν (en, “in, at”) + ἔργον (érgon, “work”).
