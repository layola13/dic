deformation:/ˌdiːfɔːrˈmeɪʃn/ /ˌdiːfɔːrˈmeɪʃən/| n. |变形，形变|alteration in the shape or dimensions of an object as a result of the application of stress.  |The deformation of the metal was caused by excessive heat.  金属的变形是由过热引起的。|distortion, contortion, warping| reformation, restoration|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin dēformātiōn- (stem of dēformātiō ), from dēformāre (see deform).
