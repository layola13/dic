albumose:/ˈælbjʊmoʊs/
noun
蛋白胨
A water-soluble product of protein hydrolysis that is not coagulable by heat but is precipitated by saturation with ammonium sulfate.  It represents an early stage in the breakdown of proteins.
Albumose is a product of protein digestion. 蛋白胨是蛋白质消化后的产物。
Albumose is used in some culture media. 蛋白胨用于一些培养基中。
synonym:proteose, peptone
antonym:protein
