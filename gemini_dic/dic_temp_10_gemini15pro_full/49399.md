deliverability:/dɪˌlɪvərəˈbɪləti/| n. |可送达性，投递率|The ability of an email message to reach the recipient's inbox.  指电子邮件到达收件人收件箱的能力。|Example 1:  The email marketing campaign suffered from poor deliverability due to a high number of bounces.  由于大量退信，电子邮件营销活动的可送达性很差。

Example 2:  Improving email deliverability is crucial for maximizing the reach of your message.  提高电子邮件的可送达性对于最大限度地扩大信息覆盖范围至关重要。|Synonyms: reachability, deliverableness|Antonyms: undeliverability|noun
