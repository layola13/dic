commercialized: /kəˈmɜːʃəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 商业化的 |  Made commercial; exploited for profit.  Having embraced or been altered by principles of commerce. | The once pristine beach has become completely commercialized, filled with souvenir shops and fast-food restaurants.  曾经原始的海滩已经完全商业化，到处都是纪念品商店和快餐店。|Synonyms: marketed, merchandized, monetized | Antonyms: non-commercial, unmarketable | adjective

commercialize: /kəˈmɜːʃəlaɪz/ | v. | 商业化 | To make commercial; to exploit for profit. To develop something so that it can be sold or used to make a profit. | They commercialized the new technology as quickly as possible. 他们尽快将新技术商业化。 |Synonyms: market, merchandise, monetize | Antonyms: decommercialize | verb
