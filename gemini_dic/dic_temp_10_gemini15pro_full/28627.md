caretuned:/ˈkɛərˌtuːnd/ | adj. | 微调的，精心调整的 |  Refers to a large language model (LLM) that has been further trained on a specific dataset to improve its performance on a particular task or domain.  It involves taking a pre-trained model and fine-tuning its parameters on a smaller, more focused dataset.  |  The team used a caretuned version of BERT for sentiment analysis.  团队使用了一个微调版的BERT模型进行情感分析。  |  fine-tuned, adjusted, optimized | untrained, general | adjective
