overdebating: /ˌoʊvərdiˈbeɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度辩论；过度争论
Engaging in excessive or prolonged debate, often to the point of being unproductive or counterproductive.
They were overdebating the issue and not reaching a decision. 他们在这个问题上过度辩论，没有达成任何决定。

The committee spent hours overdebating minor details. 委员会花了几个小时过度辩论一些细枝末节。

Synonym: overdiscussing, overanalyzing
Antonym: underdiscussing, skimming over
