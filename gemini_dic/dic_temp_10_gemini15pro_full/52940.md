dined:/daɪnd/
verb
用过正餐；吃饭；宴请
past tense of "dine" - to eat dinner.
We dined at a fancy restaurant last night. 我们昨晚在一家豪华餐厅用餐。
They dined on lobster and champagne. 他们享用了龙虾和香槟。
近义词: ate, supped, feasted
反义词: fasted, starved
词性: verb
