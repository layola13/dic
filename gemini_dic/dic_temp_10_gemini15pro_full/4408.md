allantoin:/əˈlæntɔɪn/ | n. |尿囊素;乙内酰脲|A crystalline oxidation product of uric acid, C₄H₆N₄O₃, present in many mammals (except higher primates) and used topically to promote healing and as a cosmetic.|Allantoin is often used in skincare products to soothe irritated skin.  尿囊素常用于护肤品中，以舒缓受刺激的皮肤。|Synonyms:  glyoxyldiureide |Antonyms: N/A|noun
