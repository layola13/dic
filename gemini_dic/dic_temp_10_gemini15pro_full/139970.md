odontotormae:/ˌoʊdɒntoʊˈtɔːrmiː/ | n. | 牙胚 |  Tooth germs; the earliest recognizable stage in the development of teeth.  They develop from the dental lamina, a band of epithelial tissue in the developing jaw. | The odontotormae of the permanent teeth develop later than those of the deciduous teeth.  恒牙的牙胚比乳牙的牙胚发育得晚。| The researchers studied the development of odontotormae in mice. 研究人员研究了小鼠牙胚的发育。 | synonyms: tooth buds, tooth primordia | antonyms: N/A | noun
