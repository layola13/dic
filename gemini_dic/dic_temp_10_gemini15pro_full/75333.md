frostfish:/ˈfrɒstfɪʃ/ (UK) /ˈfrɔːstfɪʃ/ (US)| n. |冰鱼，霜鱼|A long, slender, scaleless fish found in shallow waters, particularly in colder regions. Often found washed ashore after storms, especially during winter months.|The frostfish is a delicacy in some cultures.  冰鱼在某些文化中是一道美味佳肴。|  Local fishermen often catch frostfish during the winter. 当地的渔民经常在冬天捕捞冰鱼。|Synonyms:  icefish, frost cod, whitebait (sometimes)|Antonyms:  None specific|Noun
