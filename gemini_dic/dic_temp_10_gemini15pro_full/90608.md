human resources:/ˈhjuːmən ˈriːsɔːrsɪz/ |n.|人力资源|The department responsible for managing employees, including hiring, training, and compensation.  This encompasses the individuals within an organization and refers to the combined knowledge, skills, experience, and abilities of the workforce.|  The human resources department is hiring new employees. 人力资源部门正在招聘新员工。|Synonyms: personnel, workforce, staff| Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "automation" in some contexts) | Noun
