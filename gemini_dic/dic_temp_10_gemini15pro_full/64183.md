equerry:/ˈɛkwəri/ | n. |侍从武官；马夫| An officer of a royal or noble household, charged with the care of horses. |例句：The Queen's equerry rode beside her carriage. 女王的侍从武官骑马伴随在她的马车旁。

例句：He served as equerry to the Prince of Wales. 他担任威尔士亲王的侍从武官。|近义词：groom, attendant, squire |反义词: master |词性: noun
