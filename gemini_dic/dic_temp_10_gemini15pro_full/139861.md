octylic: /ɒkˈtɪlɪk/ /ɑːkˈtɪlɪk/
adjective
辛基的; 辛酸的;  
relating to or derived from octane. Containing eight carbon atoms.
Octylic acid is a fatty acid found in some plants. 辛酸是一种存在于某些植物中的脂肪酸。
Octylic alcohol is used in the production of perfumes. 辛醇用于香水的生产。


近义词: caprylic (especially for the acid)
反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it refers to a specific chemical structure)
词性: adjective
