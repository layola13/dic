outdancing: /ˌaʊtˈdɑːnsɪŋ/ /ˌaʊtˈdænsɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的舞蹈；跳得比…好
Surpassing someone in dancing; dancing better than someone.
He outdanced everyone at the party.  他在派对上跳舞跳得比所有人都好。
Near Synonyms: outperforming, surpassing, excelling
Antonyms: being outdanced, losing to
词性: 动词
