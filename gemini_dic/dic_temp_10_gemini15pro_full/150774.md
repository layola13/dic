paradisaic:/ˌpærəˈdaɪsɪk/ /ˌpærəˈdɪzɪk/| adj.|天堂般的，极乐的|Resembling or suggesting paradise; heavenly.|例句：The island boasts paradisaic beaches.  这个岛屿拥有天堂般的海滩。|例句：The hotel had a paradisaic garden.  这家酒店有一个天堂般的花园。|近义词：heavenly, blissful, idyllic, utopian|反义词：hellish, infernal, dystopian|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *paradisīacus, from Latin paradīsus (“paradise”) + -iacus.  From Ancient Greek παράδεισος (parádeisos, “park, paradise”). Ultimately from Old Iranian *parādaiĵah (“walled enclosure”).
