indulge:/ɪnˈdʌldʒ/| v. |放纵；沉溺；满足；纵容|To allow oneself or another person to have or do something enjoyable, especially something that is considered bad for you.|She indulged in a long hot bath. 她泡了个长时间的热水澡。|近义词：pamper, spoil, gratify, cater to|反义词：deny, deprive, abstain|词性:动词
indulged:/ɪnˈdʌldʒd/ |v. |放纵的；沉溺的 (过去式和过去分词) |Having or showing a tendency to allow oneself to enjoy pleasures, especially those considered bad for you.|He had an indulged childhood. 他度过了一个放纵的童年。|近义词：pampered, spoiled|反义词：deprived|词性:动词(过去式和过去分词)
