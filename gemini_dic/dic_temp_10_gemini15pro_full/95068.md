inadvertence:/ˌɪnədˈvɜːrtəns/ | n. | 疏忽，不注意 | Lack of attention; an unintentional oversight. |  I deleted the file by inadvertence. 我不小心删除了文件。| synonyms: oversight, carelessness, negligence, inattention | antonyms: care, attention, diligence, heedfulness | noun

inadvertent:/ˌɪnədˈvɜːrtənt/ | adj. | 非故意的，无意的 | Not resulting from or achieved through deliberate planning. |  An inadvertent error led to the system crash. 一个无意的错误导致了系统崩溃。| synonyms: unintentional, accidental, unintended, unplanned | antonyms: deliberate, intentional, planned, purposeful | adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *inadvertentia*, from in- "not" + advertere "to turn to," from ad- "to" + vertere "to turn."
