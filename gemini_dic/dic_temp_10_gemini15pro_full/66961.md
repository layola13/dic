experimentalist:/ɪkˌspɛrɪˈmɛntəlɪst/
n.
实验者，实验家
A person who conducts experiments, especially in science.
例句：The experimentalist carefully recorded the results of each trial.  实验者仔细记录了每次试验的结果。

例句：As an experimentalist, she was always looking for new ways to test her theories.  作为一名实验家，她总是在寻找新的方法来检验她的理论。

近义词：researcher, scientist, investigator
反义词：theorist
词性：noun
