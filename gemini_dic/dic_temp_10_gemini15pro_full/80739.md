goshan:/ˈɡoʊʃən/| n.| 歌珊;庇护所;丰饶之地|A place of plenty or safety; a fertile region.|The Israelites found Goshen a fertile land during the famine in Egypt. 以色列人在埃及饥荒期间发现歌珊是一片肥沃的土地。|近义词：haven, sanctuary, refuge, oasis|反义词：danger zone, wasteland|词性:名词

词源: From Hebrew גֹּשֶׁן (gōšen), of uncertain etymology. Possibly related to Egyptian *qeSen (cultivated land) or *geS (border, frontier).
