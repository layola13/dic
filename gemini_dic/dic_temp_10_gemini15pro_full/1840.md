acrolectal: /ˌækrəˈlɛktəl/
adjective
高层的，上层的（指语言变体）
Relating to or denoting the most prestigious variety of a language, especially in a multilingual community where it is used as a standard.
The acrolectal form of the language is used in formal settings.  这种语言的高层变体用于正式场合。

The government promotes the acrolectal variety in education and media. 政府在教育和媒体中推广高层语言变体。

近义词: standard, prestigious, formal
反义词: basilectal, vernacular, informal
词性: adjective

词源:  From acrolect + -al.  Acrolect is from Greek ákros "highest, topmost" + léktos "spoken."
