hypophrygian:/ˌhaɪpəˈfrɪdʒiən/
adjective
下弗里吉亚调式的; 阴郁的; 忧郁的
Relating to or characteristic of a musical mode or scale having the same intervals as the Phrygian mode but with a lower leading tone, typically conveying a dark or melancholic mood.
The composer used a hypophrygian melody to create a sense of foreboding. 作曲家使用下弗里吉亚旋律来营造一种不祥的预感。
The hypophrygian chant echoed through the ancient temple. 下弗里吉亚圣歌在古老的寺庙中回荡。
Near Synonyms: Phrygian, doleful, melancholic, mournful
Antonyms: cheerful, bright, major
