backlogs:/ˈbæklɒɡz/ (US) /ˈbæklɒɡz/ (UK)| n.|积压的工作，待办事项|An accumulation of unfinished work or unprocessed items.|例句：The company is struggling to clear the backlog of orders.  这家公司正努力清理积压的订单。

例句：There's a huge backlog of cases waiting to be heard in court.  大量案件积压等待法庭审理。

近义词：arrears, accumulation, buildup|反义词:advance, surplus|词性:名词
