outsaint:/ˌaʊtˈseɪnt/
verb
胜过…的圣洁;比…更像圣徒
to surpass in holiness or saintly qualities.
She outsaintes all the other nuns in the convent with her constant acts of charity. 她凭借不断的慈善行为，比修道院里的所有其他修女都更圣洁。
Synonyms: outdo, surpass, excel, exceed
Antonyms:  fall short of, be inferior to
词性: verb
