lorentia:/lɔːˈrɛnʃɪə/ /lɔːrˈɛnʃə/
n.
Laurentia (大陆)；劳伦大陆
A large continental craton that forms the ancient geological core of North America.  It is sometimes used synonymously with the North American Craton.
Laurentia was a major continent in the Proterozoic.  劳伦大陆是元古代时期的一个主要大陆。
Laurentia collided with other continents to form larger landmasses. 劳伦大陆与其他大陆碰撞形成了更大的陆块。
近义词:North American Craton
反义词:None
词性:noun
