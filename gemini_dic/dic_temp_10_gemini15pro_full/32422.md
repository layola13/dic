chilostomatous:/ˌkaɪləˈstɒmətəs/| adj.|苔藓虫类的；有唇口的|Having a hinged operculum that closes the orifice of the zooid (individual animal) in certain bryozoans (moss animals).|The fossil record shows a rich diversity of chilostomatous bryozoans.|近义词：cheilostomatous|反义词:|词性: adjective
