battleground:/ˈbætlɡraʊnd/| n. |战场，争论场所|A place where a battle is fought or a place where a struggle or debate takes place.|例句：The city became a battleground for rival gangs. 这座城市成了敌对帮派的战场。

例句：Social media has become a battleground for political debate. 社交媒体已成为政治辩论的战场。|近义词：arena, field, front, theater|反义词:peace zone, sanctuary|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bataylground*, from Old French *bataille* (battle) + *ground* (ground).
