inelasticity:/ˌɪnɪlæˈstɪsɪti/ | n. |无弹性，非弹性|The property of a substance or material that causes it to resist deformation or change in shape. |The inelasticity of demand for gasoline means that even a large price increase leads to only a small decrease in consumption.| near synonyms: rigidity, stiffness, inflexibility | antonyms: elasticity, flexibility, plasticity | noun
