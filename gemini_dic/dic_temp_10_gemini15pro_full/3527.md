词源:
From Middle English *ayein, *agein, from Old English ongegn (“against, toward, opposite, in return, back”), from Proto-Germanic *anʒeʒaniz (“against”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂enḱ- (“to reach, attain, obtain”). Cognate with Scots again (“again”), West Frisian oan (“on”), Dutch aan (“on, to”), German an (“on, at”), Old Norse í gegn (“through”).
