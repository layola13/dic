ligniform:/ˈlɪɡnɪfɔːrm/
adjective
木质的，像木头的
Resembling wood in appearance or texture.
The fungus had a ligniform texture. 这种真菌具有木质纹理。
Near Synonyms: woody, wood-like, ligneous
Antonyms: soft, fleshy
adjective
