cubicity:/kjuːˈbɪsɪti/
n.
立方性，三次方
The cubicity of a cube is determined by the length of its sides.  一个立方体的立方性由其边的长度决定。
The calculation involves determining the cubicity of the object. 计算涉及确定物体的立方性。
近义词:volume, cubic capacity
