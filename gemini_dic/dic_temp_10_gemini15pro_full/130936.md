nonaspirins:/ˌnɒnˈæspərɪnz/
noun
非阿司匹林类药物
Drugs that are not aspirin, typically referring to pain relievers or anti-inflammatory medications.
Patients with sensitivities to aspirin may be prescribed nonaspirins.  对阿司匹林敏感的患者可能会被开非阿司匹林类药物。
Nonaspirins like ibuprofen can also help with pain relief.  像布洛芬这样的非阿司匹林类药物也能帮助缓解疼痛。
synonyms: non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (NSAIDs), analgesics (excluding aspirin)
antonyms: aspirins
