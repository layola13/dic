iridescent:/ˌɪrɪˈdɛsənt/ | adj. | 彩虹色的，闪光的 | Showing luminous colors that seem to change when seen from different angles. | The hummingbird's feathers were iridescent, shimmering in the sunlight.  蜂鸟的羽毛五彩斑斓，在阳光下闪闪发光。 |  Example 2: The oil slick on the water created an iridescent sheen. 水面上的油污形成了一层彩虹色的光泽。| synonyms: shimmering, opalescent, pearly, lustrous | antonyms: dull, matte, lackluster | adjective


iridescence:/ˌɪrɪˈdɛsəns/ | n. | 彩虹色，闪光 | The quality or state of being iridescent. | The iridescence of the butterfly's wings was captivating. 蝴蝶翅膀的虹彩令人着迷。| Example 2: The soap bubble displayed a beautiful iridescence. 肥皂泡展现出美丽的彩虹色。 | synonyms: shimmer, opalescence, pearl, luster | antonyms: dullness, flatness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *iridiscus, from iris (genitive iridis) "rainbow" + -escent "beginning to be."  Ultimately from Greek ἶρις (îris) "rainbow."
