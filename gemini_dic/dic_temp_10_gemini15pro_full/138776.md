numerous:/ˈnjuːmərəs/ (US), /ˈnjuːmərəs/ (UK)| adj.|许多的，众多的|A large number; many.|例句：There are numerous books on the subject.  这个主题有很多书籍。|近义词：many, plentiful, abundant, copious|反义词:few, scarce, limited|词性:形容词
词源:Late Middle English: from Old French nombreux, from Latin numerosus, from numerus ‘number’.
