outsweetened:/ˌaʊtˈswiːtənd/
Verb
使...甜度过高；比...更甜
To make something excessively sweet, or sweeter than something else.
The dessert was completely outsweetened, making it inedible. 这道甜点甜得过分，让人无法下咽。
The artificial sweetener outsweetened the natural sugar in the tea.  人工甜味剂使茶比天然糖更甜。
Synonyms: oversweeten, cloy
Antonyms: unsweetened, tart, sour
