indigent:/ˈɪndɪdʒənt/| adj.|贫穷的，贫困的|Poor; needy; lacking the necessities of life.|例句：The clinic provides free care for indigent patients.  这家诊所为贫困病人提供免费护理。

例句：Many indigent families rely on government assistance. 许多贫困家庭依靠政府援助。|近义词：poor, impoverished, destitute, needy, penniless|反义词：wealthy, affluent, rich, prosperous|词性:形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from Latin *indigens, indigent- ‘lacking’, present participle of indigere ‘need, lack’, from in- ‘not’ + egere ‘need’.
