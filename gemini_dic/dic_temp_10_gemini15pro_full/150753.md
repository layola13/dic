paracrystallinity:/ˌpærəˌkrɪstəˈlɪnɪti/ | n. |  近晶性，次晶性 | The state or quality of being paracrystalline, exhibiting a degree of order intermediate between crystalline and amorphous.  A material exhibiting paracrystallinity has short-range order but lacks long-range order. | The polymer showed a high degree of paracrystallinity.  这种聚合物表现出高度的近晶性。 |  mesomorphism, partial crystallinity | amorphousness, disorder | noun
