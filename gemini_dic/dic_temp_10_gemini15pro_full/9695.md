antithetically:/ˌæntɪˈθɛtɪkli/
adverb
对立地，相反地
in a manner characterized by antithesis; in opposite ways.
He presented his argument antithetically, highlighting the stark contrast between the two opposing viewpoints. 他以对立的方式提出了他的论点，突出了两种对立观点之间的鲜明对比。
近义词:oppositely, contrarily, conversely
反义词:similarly, likewise
词性:副词
