ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *historique*, from Latin historicus (“historical”), from historia (“history”), from Ancient Greek ἱστορία (historía, “inquiry; knowledge acquired by investigation; account, story, history”), from ἵστωρ (hístōr, “knower, witness, judge”), from Proto-Indo-European *wid-tor- (“one who sees”), from *weid- (“to see; to know”).  Cognate with Sanskrit वेत्तृ (véttṛ, “knower”), Latin vidēre (“to see”).
