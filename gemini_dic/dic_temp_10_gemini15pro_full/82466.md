grumpy:/ˈɡrʌmpi/| adj.|脾气坏的；爱抱怨的|Bad-tempered and irritable.|The old man was grumpy and complained about everything.  这位老人脾气暴躁，对一切都抱怨。|近义词：irritable, bad-tempered, crabby|反义词：cheerful, good-natured|adjective
