cygnets:/ˈsɪɡnɪts/| n. |小天鹅（复数）|Young swans, typically in their first year of life.|The cygnets were swimming gracefully behind their mother. 小天鹅们优雅地跟在妈妈身后游泳。|近义词：young swans, baby swans|反义词:adult swans|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *signet, from Old French cignet, diminutive of cigne (“swan”), from Latin cygnus, from Ancient Greek κύκνος (kýknos).
