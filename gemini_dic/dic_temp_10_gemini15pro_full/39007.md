confiscable:/kənˈfɪskəbl/| adj.|可没收的，可充公的|Something that is confiscable can be taken away from you officially, usually by the government or other authority.|The government declared that all illegal drugs were confiscable. 政府宣布所有非法药物均可没收。|The smuggled goods were confiscable by customs. 走私货物可被海关没收。|Synonym: seizable, forfeitable|Antonym: retainable, returnable|词性:形容词
