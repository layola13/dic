outsatisfy: /ˌaʊtsætɪsˈfaɪ/
verb
超越满意；使更满意
to satisfy more thoroughly or completely than another
The new restaurant outsatisfied its competitors with its exceptional service and innovative menu. 这家新餐厅以其卓越的服务和创新的菜单超越了竞争对手，让顾客更加满意。
近义词: outdo, surpass, excel
反义词: disappoint, dissatisfy
词性: verb

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "surpassing," "exceeding") + "satisfy" (verb meaning "to fulfill the desires, needs, or expectations of")
