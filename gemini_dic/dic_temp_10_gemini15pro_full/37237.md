comforter:/ˈkʌmfərtər/ (US), /ˈkʌmfətə(r)/ (UK)| n.|安慰者；棉被；慰藉物|A person or thing that comforts someone. Also, a thick, padded quilt or bedspread.|例句：The child clutched his comforter tightly.  孩子紧紧地抓着他的慰藉物。

例句：She snuggled under the warm comforter. 她依偎在温暖的棉被下。

近义词：consoler, quilt, duvet, blanket|反义词: distresser, tormentor|词性: noun
