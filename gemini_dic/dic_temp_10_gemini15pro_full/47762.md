decametres:/ˈdekəmiːtəz/ /ˈdɛkəˌmitɚz/
noun
十米；公丈
A decametre is a unit of length in the metric system equal to ten metres.
The race was ten decametres long. 这场比赛长达十公丈。
Synonyms: dekameters, dam
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement.  Consider smaller units like centimetres or millimetres)
n.
