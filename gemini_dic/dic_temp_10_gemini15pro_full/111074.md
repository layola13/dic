listeric:/lɪˈstɛrɪk/ | adj. | 李斯特菌的；由李斯特菌引起的 | Relating to or caused by Listeria, a type of bacteria that can cause food poisoning. | Example: Listeric infections can be serious, especially for pregnant women. 李斯特菌感染可能很严重，特别是对孕妇而言。

Example: The outbreak was traced to listeric contamination in the factory.  疫情被追溯到工厂的李斯特菌污染。 | 近义词: bacterial（细菌的） | 反义词:  None (没有直接的反义词，可以用non-listeric表示“非李斯特菌的”) | 词性: adjective

词源:  Derived from the genus name *Listeria*, named after Joseph Lister, a British surgeon known for his work in antiseptic surgery.  源于属名*Listeria*，以英国外科医生约瑟夫·李斯特的名字命名，他以在防腐手术方面的工作而闻名。
