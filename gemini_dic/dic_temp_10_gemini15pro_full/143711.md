outlinear:/ˌaʊtˈlɪniər/ | adj. | 大纲的；概要的；略图的 |  Relating to or presented in the form of an outline; giving only the main points. | The outlinear structure of the presentation made it easy to follow.  演示文稿的提纲结构使其易于理解。 | 近义词： summarized, skeletal, cursory | 反义词： detailed, comprehensive | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "outline" + "-ar" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Outline" itself comes from the Dutch "uitlijnen," meaning "to align."
