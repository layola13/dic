inseparability:/ˌɪnˌsɛpərəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可分割性 | The quality or condition of being inseparable; not able to be separated. | The inseparability of mind and body is a central tenet of some philosophies.  身心不可分是某些哲学的核心信条。| synonyms: indivisibility, unity, coherence, cohesion | antonyms: separability, divisibility, disunity | noun

inseparable:/ˌɪnˈsɛpərəbl/ | adj. | 不可分割的 | Not able to be separated. |  The two friends were inseparable throughout their childhood. 这两个朋友童年时期形影不离。| synonyms: indivisible, united, intertwined, close-knit | antonyms: separable, divisible, detached | adjective
