nieces:/ˈniːsiz/| n. |侄女；外甥女|A daughter of one's brother or sister, or of one's brother-in-law or sister-in-law.|My nieces are coming to visit next week. 我的侄女们下周要来看我。|Synonyms:  (no single perfect synonym, context-dependent - could be female relatives, girls, young women)|Antonyms: nephews (male counterparts)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *niece, niece, from Latin neptis (“granddaughter, niece”). Doublet of neptis and granddaughter.
