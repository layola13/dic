democra:/ˈdɛməkrə/| n. |民主; 民主政体; 民主国家|A system of government by the whole population or all the eligible members of a state, typically through elected representatives. |例句：The United States is a democra. 美国是一个民主国家。

例句：He believes in democra and freedom. 他信仰民主和自由。|近义词：republic, self-government, autonomy|反义词：dictatorship, tyranny, autocracy|词性:noun
Etymology: From French *démocratie*, from Ancient Greek δημοκρατία (dēmokratía, “democracy”), from δῆμος (dêmos, “people”) + -κρατία (-kratía, “power, rule”).
