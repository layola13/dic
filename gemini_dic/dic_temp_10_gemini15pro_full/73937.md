forth:/fɔːrθ/ /fɔːrθ/| adv.|向前，向外，继续，|Moving forward from a starting point.  |例句：Go forth and conquer.  勇往直前，征服一切。|例句：From that day forth, he never smoked again. 从那天起，他再也不吸烟了。|近义词：forward, onward, ahead, out|反义词：back, backward|副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *foreþ, from *forþ, from Proto-Germanic *furþiz (compare West Frisian foarút “forward,” Dutch voort “forth, on,” German fort “away, gone, forth”), from Proto-Indo-European *pr̥- “forward, in front” + *-th₂-  adverbial suffix.
