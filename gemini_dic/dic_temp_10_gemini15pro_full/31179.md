changedness:/ˈtʃeɪndʒdnəs/
n.
已改变的状态，变化性
The state of having been changed; altered condition.
The changedness of the landscape after the fire was shocking. 火灾过后，地貌的变化令人震惊。
近义词: alteration, modification, transformation
反义词: sameness, constancy, stability
词性: 名词

Etymology:  From "changed" (past participle of "change") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition).  "Change" ultimately derives from the Old French "changier,"  from Late Latin "cambiare," meaning "to barter, exchange."
