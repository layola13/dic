pamphlet: /ˈpæmflɪt/ (US) /ˈpæmflɪt/ (UK)| v. |散发小册子|To distribute or disseminate pamphlets.|The group pamphleted the neighborhood to raise awareness about the upcoming election.  这个团体在社区散发传单以提高人们对即将到来的选举的认识。|近义词：distribute, circulate, disseminate|反义词: collect, gather|verb
