effluvia:/ɪˈfluːviə/ | n. | 难闻的气味，臭气; (usu. pl.)  | An unpleasant or harmful odor, smell, or emanation. | The effluvia from the factory was unbearable. 工厂排出的臭气令人难以忍受。 |  The room was filled with the effluvia of stale cigarette smoke. 房间里充满了陈腐的香烟味。 | synonyms: emanations, exhalations, fumes, miasma, odors, smells | antonyms: fragrance, perfume, scent | noun
