afloat:/əˈfloʊt/| adj.|漂浮的；经济上周转的|Being buoyant or able to stay above the surface of a liquid; having enough money to pay debts.|The boat stayed afloat despite the storm.  这艘船尽管遇到风暴，仍然漂浮着。|The company managed to stay afloat during the recession.  这家公司在经济衰退期间设法维持下去。|近义词：floating, buoyant, solvent|反义词: submerged, sunk, bankrupt|词性:形容词
Etymology: From a- (on) + float.  源自 a- (在…上) + float (漂浮)。
