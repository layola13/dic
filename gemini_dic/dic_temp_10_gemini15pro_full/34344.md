class:/klæs/ | n. | 班级；课；种类；等级 | A group of students who are taught together.  A period of teaching in a school. A set of things that are similar. A rank or level in a society. |  The class is studying Shakespeare. 这个班正在学习莎士比亚。| synonym: category, group, grade, rank | antonym: individual, unique | noun

class:/klæs/ | v. | 分类；归类 | To put things into groups according to their type. | The librarian classed the books by subject. 图书管理员按主题对书籍进行了分类。| synonym: categorize, group, classify | antonym: separate, divide, distinguish | verb
