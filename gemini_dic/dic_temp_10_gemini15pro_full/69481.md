feldspars:/ˈfɛldˌspɑːrz/ /ˈfɛldˌspɑːrs/| n.|长石|Any of a group of crystalline minerals containing aluminum silicates of potassium, sodium, calcium, or barium, and constituting about 60% of the earth's crust.|Feldspars are the most abundant minerals in the Earth's crust. 长石是地壳中最丰富的矿物。|
|Synonyms: aluminosilicate, mineral|Antonyms: N/A|Noun|
