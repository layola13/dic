oughtn't: /ˈɔːtnt/ | modal verb | 不应该，不应当 |  Expresses obligation or duty, but in the negative. It suggests something is not the right or advisable thing to do. | You oughtn't to smoke so much. 你不应该抽那么多烟。| You oughtn't to speak to your mother like that. 你不应该那样和你母亲说话。 | synonyms: should not, must not | antonyms: should, must | verb (auxiliary/modal)

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He oughtn't to eat so much junk food. 他不应该吃那么多垃圾食品。
They oughtn't to leave the door unlocked. 他们不应该不锁门。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):  Oughtn't is rarely used in continuous tenses.  Its meaning is more about general obligation rather than ongoing actions.  However, it can be used with a continuous infinitive to express disapproval of an ongoing action.

You oughtn't to be watching TV now, you have an exam tomorrow. 你现在不应该看电视，你明天有考试。
He oughtn't to be spending all his money on video games. 他不应该把所有的钱都花在电子游戏上。


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
She oughtn't to have said that. 她不应该说那样的话。
They oughtn't to have left so early. 他们不应该那么早离开。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous): Oughtn't is rarely used in perfect continuous tenses.

He oughtn't to have been working so hard. 他不应该一直这么辛苦地工作。
They oughtn't to have been wasting their time. 他们不应该一直在浪费时间。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous): Oughtn't is rarely used in past continuous tenses.

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):  Oughtn't is rarely used in past perfect continuous tenses.

They oughtn't to have been neglecting their studies. 他们不应该一直忽略他们的学业。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
You oughtn't to go there alone. 你不应该独自去那里。
He oughtn't to spend all his money. 他不应该把所有的钱都花光。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):  Oughtn't is not typically used with future continuous.

By then, they oughtn't to have spent all their savings. 到那时，他们不应该花光所有的积蓄。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous): Oughtn't is rarely used with future perfect continuous.

By then, she oughtn't to have been working on that project for so long. 到那时，她不应该一直在做那个项目这么久。



PHRASE->

You oughtn't to... 你不应该...
He/She/It oughtn't to... 他/她/它不应该...
We oughtn't to... 我们不应该...
They oughtn't to... 他们不应该...


注意事项:
Oughtn't is more formal and less common than shouldn't.  It's more prevalent in British English than American English.  In spoken English, "shouldn't" is almost always preferred.
