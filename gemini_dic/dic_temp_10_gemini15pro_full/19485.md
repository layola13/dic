bidding's:/ˈbɪdɪŋz/
noun
命令，吩咐；出价；邀请
The process of making offers or bids, especially at an auction.  The bidding's for the antique vase started at $500.  那个古董花瓶的竞价从500美元开始。
auction, offer, tender
command, prohibition
n.
