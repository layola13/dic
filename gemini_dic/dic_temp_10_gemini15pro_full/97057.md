inferences:/ˈɪnfərənsɪz/
n.|推论，推断，结论|The detective made several inferences based on the evidence.  侦探根据证据做出了几个推论。|
例句：His inferences were drawn from a careful analysis of the data.  他的推论是从对数据的仔细分析中得出的。
例句：Based on the available data, we can draw several inferences about consumer behavior. 基于现有数据，我们可以对消费者行为得出一些推论。
近义词：deductions, conclusions, assumptions|反义词：facts, evidence, premises|词性:noun
