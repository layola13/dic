clarity:/ˈklærəti/| n.|清晰，明晰|Clarity of thought is essential for effective communication.  清晰的思路对于有效沟通至关重要。|The clarity of the image was impressive.  图像的清晰度令人印象深刻。|近义词：clearness, lucidity, precision, distinctness|反义词：vagueness, obscurity, ambiguity, confusion|词性：名词

词源: From Middle French *clarité, from Latin clāritās (“clearness, brightness”), from clārus (“clear, bright”).
