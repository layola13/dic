cancrinite:/ˈkæŋkrɪˌnaɪt/ | n. | 钙霞石 | A complex silicate mineral, typically white, yellow, orange, or pink, containing sodium, calcium, aluminum, silicon, oxygen, and carbonate ions. It is found in alkaline igneous rocks. | Cancrinite is a relatively rare mineral found in nepheline syenites. 钙霞石是一种相对稀有的矿物，发现于霞石正长岩中。

Cancrinite is sometimes used as a gemstone. 钙霞石有时被用作宝石。| 近义词: mineral, silicate | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific mineral) | 词性: noun
