distractive:/dɪˈstræktɪv/
adjective
分散注意力的；使人分心的
Tending to distract; distracting the attention.
The TV is very distractive when I'm trying to study.  当我试图学习时，电视很让人分心。
Social media can be very distractive. 社交媒体很容易让人分心。

近义词: distracting, diverting, disturbing, intrusive
反义词: focusing, concentrating, helpful, absorbing
词性: adjective
