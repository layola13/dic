nondecisions:/ˌnɑːndɪˈsɪʒənz/
|n.|未作决定；未决事项|The report criticized the government's nondecisions on key environmental issues.  该报告批评了政府在关键环境问题上的不作为。|A series of nondecisions led to the project's ultimate failure. 一系列的未作决定导致了该项目的最终失败。|近义词：inaction, indecision, procrastination|反义词：decisions, resolutions, actions|词性:名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
decision (noun): from Old French decision, from Latin decisionem (nominative decisio) "a decision, settlement," from decis- past participle stem of decidere "to decide, determine," from de- "off" + caedere "to cut"
