contradictive:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪktɪv/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪktɪv/| adj.| 矛盾的，反驳的，对立的|Inclined to contradict; given to contradicting.  Characterized by or involving contradiction. |Her account of the accident was contradictive to the police report. 她对事故的描述与警方报告相矛盾。|Synonyms: inconsistent, conflicting, opposing, contrary, antithetical|Antonyms: consistent, harmonious, agreeing, supporting|adjective

contradictory:/ˌkɒntrəˈdɪktəri/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈdɪktəri/| adj.| 矛盾的，反驳的，对立的|Mutually opposed or inconsistent.  Involving, causing, or constituting a contradiction.  Logic (of two propositions) so related that one and only one must be true. |The witnesses gave contradictory accounts of the incident. 目击者对事件的描述相互矛盾。|Synonyms: inconsistent, conflicting, opposing, contrary, antithetical|Antonyms: consistent, harmonious, agreeing, supporting|adjective
