implode:/ɪmˈploʊd/ (US), /ɪmˈpləʊd/ (UK)| v. |内爆；崩溃；瓦解|To collapse or cause to collapse violently inward.|The submarine imploded under the immense pressure of the deep ocean. 潜水艇在深海巨大的压力下内爆了。

The old building was imploded to make way for a new skyscraper.  为了给新的摩天大楼腾出地方，这栋老建筑被爆破拆除了。|近义词：collapse, burst inward|反义词:explode, burst outward|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin implodere, from in- "in" + plaudere "to clap, applaud."  The original sense was "to clap or strike upon," later evolving to "drive in" and then to its current meaning.
