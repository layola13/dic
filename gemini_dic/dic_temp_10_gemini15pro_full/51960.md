diachylum:/daɪˈækɪləm/ | n. |  橡皮膏；橡皮硬膏 | A type of adhesive plaster, often medicated.  A plaster originally made of diachylon, a lead-based emollient ointment. Now, usually an adhesive bandage. | The doctor applied a diachylum to the wound.  医生在伤口上贴了一块橡皮膏。 | Synonyms: plaster, bandage, adhesive tape | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "suture" if considering closing a wound) | noun
