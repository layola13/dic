counter-step:/ˈkaʊntərˌstɛp/ | n. | 反击；应对措施；对策 | An action taken to oppose or counteract another action. |  The government's counter-step to the protests was to impose a curfew. 政府应对抗议的对策是实行宵禁。

The company developed a counter-step to their competitor's new product launch.  这家公司针对竞争对手的新产品发布制定了应对策略。 | 近义词: countermeasure, response, reaction | 反义词:  inaction, passivity | 词性: 名词

counter-step:/ˈkaʊntərˌstɛp/ | v. | 采取反击；采取对策 | To take action to oppose or counteract something. | The army counter-stepped the enemy's advance.  军队反击了敌人的进攻。

The company counter-stepped by lowering their prices. 公司采取了降低价格的对策。 | 近义词: counteract, oppose, retaliate | 反义词:  submit, yield | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "counter-" (against, opposite) + "step" (a movement made by lifting the foot and setting it down again in a new position).  "Counter-" comes from the Old French "contre-" and ultimately from the Latin "contra" (against). "Step" comes from Old English "stæpe."
