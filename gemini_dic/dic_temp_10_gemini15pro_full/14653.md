avowal:/əˈvaʊəl/
n.
公开声明；承认；坦白
an open statement of something; an affirmation; a frank acknowledgment or admission
He made an avowal of his love for her. 他公开声明对她的爱。
近义词：declaration, affirmation, confession, admission, acknowledgment
反义词：denial, repudiation, disavowal
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *avoual, from avouer (“to avow”), from Latin advocare (“to summon, call to one's aid, claim as one's own”), from ad- (“to, toward”) + vocare (“to call”).
