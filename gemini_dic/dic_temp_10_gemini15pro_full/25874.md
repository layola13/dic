burlapped:/bərˈlæpt/
adjective
包着粗麻布的，用粗麻布覆盖的
covered or wrapped with burlap.
The roots of the young tree were burlapped for transplanting. 这棵小树的根部用粗麻布包裹着以便移植。
The furniture was burlapped and stored in the barn. 家具用粗麻布包起来，存放在谷仓里。
近义词:covered, wrapped, protected
反义词:uncovered, exposed


词性:adjective
