fussing:/ˈfʌsɪŋ/ | n. | 忙乱，大惊小怪 | The act of worrying or complaining about something, especially something unimportant. |  There was a lot of fussing and flapping, but eventually everyone got on the plane.  很多人忙乱和慌张，但最终大家都登上了飞机。 | synonyms: worrying, fretting, bustling, commotion | antonyms: calmness, tranquility | noun

fussing:/ˈfʌsɪŋ/ | adj. | 瞎操心的，过于讲究的  | Excessively concerned with minor details or matters; fastidious.  | She's a fussing mother who worries constantly about her children. 她是一位瞎操心的母亲，总是担心她的孩子们。 | synonyms: fastidious, fussy, particular, meticulous | antonyms: easygoing, relaxed, unconcerned | adjective
