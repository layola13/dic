microalgae:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈældʒiː/| n. |微藻| Microscopic algae, typically found in freshwater and marine systems, encompassing a diverse group of photosynthetic organisms. | Microalgae are a promising source of biofuel. 微藻是很有前景的生物燃料来源。

Microalgae blooms can sometimes be harmful to marine life. 微藻爆发有时会对海洋生物有害。|近义词：phytoplankton|反义词: N/A| Noun
