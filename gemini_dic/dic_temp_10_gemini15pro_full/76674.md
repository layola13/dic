galvanize:/ˈɡælvənaɪz/ /ˈɡælvəˌnaɪz/| v. |激励；刺激；镀锌|To stimulate or excite as if by electric shock; to coat (iron or steel) with zinc.|The coach's speech galvanized the team into action. 教练的讲话激励了队员们行动起来。 The steel is galvanized to prevent rust. 钢铁镀锌以防生锈。|近义词：stimulate, excite, rouse, inspire, energize, electrify, coat, plate|反义词：depress, discourage, weaken, soothe, dull|词性：动词

galvanizing:/ˈɡælvənaɪzɪŋ/ /ˈɡælvəˌnaɪzɪŋ/| adj. |刺激的；激励的；振奋人心的| Thrilling, exciting, stimulating.|The galvanizing performance left the audience speechless.  这场激动人心的表演让观众哑口无言。 His words had a galvanizing effect on the crowd. 他的话对人群起到了激励的作用。|近义词：stimulating, exciting, inspiring, electrifying|反义词：depressing, discouraging, boring|词性：形容词
