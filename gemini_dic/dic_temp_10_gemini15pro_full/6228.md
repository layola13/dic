amnesties: /ˈæmnəstiz/ | n. | 大赦，特赦 | An official pardon for people who have been convicted of political offenses. |  The government granted amnesty to all political prisoners. 政府对所有政治犯给予了特赦。

The amnesty covered a wide range of offenses.  这次特赦涵盖了范围广泛的罪行。

| 近义词: pardon, reprieve, forgiveness | 反义词: prosecution, punishment, conviction | 词性: noun
