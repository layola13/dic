askew:/əˈskjuː/
adjective
歪斜的，斜的
Not straight or level; tilted or crooked.
Her hat was askew on her head. 她的帽子歪戴在头上。

The picture hung askew on the wall.  这幅画歪挂在墙上。

awry, crooked, tilted, lopsided
straight, level, even
词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From a- + skew (adjective and verb), from Old Norse *skeifr "crooked," related to Old English sceag "crooked."
