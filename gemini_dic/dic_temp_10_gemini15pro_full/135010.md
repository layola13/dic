nonnegligently:/ˌnɑːnˈnɛɡlɪdʒəntli/
adverb
非疏忽地；无过失地
Without negligence; in a nonnegligent manner.  This means acting with due care and attention, not carelessly or recklessly.
The doctor acted nonnegligently in treating the patient.  医生在治疗病人时没有疏忽。
The company nonnegligently maintained its equipment. 公司认真维护其设备。
近义词:carefully, prudently, diligently
反义词:negligently, carelessly, recklessly
词性:adverb
