infusorial:/ˌɪnfjʊˈsɔːriəl/
adjective
注入的；浸渍的; 草履虫的
Relating to or resembling an infusoria (a minute aquatic creature).  Typically used in older scientific texts to describe microscopic organisms found in infusions of decaying organic matter.
The scientist examined the infusorial population of the pond water.  科学家检查了池塘水中草履虫的数量。
The 19th-century biologist made detailed drawings of infusorial animals.  这位19世纪的生物学家绘制了草履虫动物的详细图画。
Near synonyms:  microscopic, protozoan
Antonyms:  macroscopic
