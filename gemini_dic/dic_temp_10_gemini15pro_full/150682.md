par for the course:/pɑːr fɔːr ðə kɔːrs/ | adj. | 常有的事；意料之中的事；不出所料 | typical, usual, or expected for a particular situation | The traffic was terrible, but that's par for the course on holiday weekends. 交通拥堵很严重，但在假日周末，这是意料之中的事。 |  The long hours are par for the course in this industry.  在这个行业，长时间工作是常有的事。 | synonyms: normal, typical, usual, expected, standard, routine | antonyms: unusual, atypical, unexpected, surprising, extraordinary | adjective

Etymology:
From the golfing term "par for the course," meaning the expected number of strokes to complete a course.  This golfing term originated in the late 19th century.
