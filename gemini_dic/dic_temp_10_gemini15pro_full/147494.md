overornamenting: /ˌoʊvərˈɔːrnəˌmɛntɪŋ/
verb
过度装饰
Adding excessive decoration to something, making it overly elaborate or fussy.
The architect was criticized for overornamenting the building's facade.  建筑师因过度装饰建筑物的外立面而受到批评。
Her prose is sometimes overornamenting, obscuring the meaning with too many flourishes. 她的散文有时过于华丽，用太多的辞藻掩盖了意义。
近义词: overdecorating, embellishing excessively, overembellishing
反义词: simplifying, understating, minimizing


词性: v. (verb, 动词)

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "ornament" (verb meaning "to decorate") + "-ing" (present participle suffix)  由表示“过度”的前缀"over-"，动词"ornament"（意为“装饰”）和现在分词后缀"-ing"构成。
