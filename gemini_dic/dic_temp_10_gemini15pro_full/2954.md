adware:/ˈædweər/| n.|广告软件|Software that automatically displays or downloads advertising material (often unwanted) when a user is online.|My computer is running slow because it's infected with adware. 我的电脑运行缓慢，因为它感染了广告软件。

Adware often comes bundled with free software. 广告软件通常与免费软件捆绑在一起。|近义词：malware, spyware|反义词:antivirus software|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From advertisement + software.  "Ad" is a shortened form of "advertisement".  This blending of words creates a concise term for software that presents advertisements.
