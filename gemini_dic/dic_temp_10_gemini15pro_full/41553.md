corpora:/ˈkɔːrpərə/ /kɔrˈpɔːrə/ | n. | 语料库；文集 | A collection of written or spoken material stored electronically and used for linguistic research. |  Large corpora are crucial for training machine learning models in natural language processing.  大型语料库对于训练自然语言处理中的机器学习模型至关重要。 | synonyms: collection, compilation, dataset, archive | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "fragment" or "sample" in certain contexts) | noun
