glittering:/ˈɡlɪtərɪŋ/ /ˈɡlɪt̬ɚɪŋ/| adj.|闪闪发光的，灿烂的|Shining brightly with many small flashes of light.|The city lights were glittering in the distance. 城市的灯光在远处闪闪发光。|Synonyms: sparkling, shimmering, gleaming, twinkling, flashing|Antonyms: dull, dim, matte|adjective


glittering:/ˈɡlɪtərɪŋ/ /ˈɡlɪt̬ɚɪŋ/| n.|闪光，闪烁|The act or state of shining brightly with many small flashes of light.  (This noun usage is less common.)|The glittering of the diamonds caught her eye. 钻石的闪耀吸引了她的目光。|Synonyms: sparkle, shimmer, gleam, twinkle, flash|Antonyms: dullness, dimness|noun
