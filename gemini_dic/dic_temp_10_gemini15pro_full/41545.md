coronis:/kəˈrəʊnɪs/| n.|弯曲符号，省略符号，古希腊诗歌的一种格律|A mark used in ancient Greek poetry to indicate a break or pause, or a metrical unit of two short syllables followed by a long one; also, a mark (´) used to indicate the omission of letters in a word, similar to an apostrophe.|The manuscript contained several coronides, indicating omissions by the scribe.|近义词：apostrophe, omission mark|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

coronis:/kəˈrəʊnɪs/| n.|钩状突起|A hook-shaped projection, especially in anatomy.|The coracoid process is a coronis of the scapula.|近义词：process, projection, hook|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
