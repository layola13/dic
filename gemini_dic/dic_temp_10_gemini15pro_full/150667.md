papule:/ˈpæpjuːl/ | n. |丘疹；小疙瘩|A small, raised, solid pimple or swelling, often inflamed but not producing pus.|  The rash presented as numerous small, red papules on the patient's arms. 病人的手臂上出现了许多红色的小丘疹。| Synonyms: pimple, blemish, spot, nodule | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider describing the opposite condition, e.g., smooth skin) | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *papula* ("pimple, pustule"), diminutive of *papa* ("nipple, teat").
