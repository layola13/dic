cembalo:/ˈtʃɛmbəloʊ/ (US), /ˈtʃɛmbɑːloʊ/ (UK)| n. |大键琴|A keyboard instrument that produces sound by plucking strings with quills or plectrums.  It was widely used in Baroque music. |例句：The cembalo has a delicate and distinctive sound.  大键琴的声音精致而独特。

例句：Bach wrote many beautiful pieces for the cembalo. 巴赫为大键琴创作了许多优美的作品。|近义词：harpsichord|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps modern piano)|词性:noun
