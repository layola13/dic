antisoporific:/ˌæntiˌsɒpəˈrɪfɪk/ /ˌæntiˌsɑːpəˈrɪfɪk/
adjective
防止睡眠的; 抗催眠的
Preventing or counteracting sleepiness; tending to keep one awake.
The comedian's jokes were surprisingly antisoporific; I was wide awake even after a long day.  这位喜剧演员的笑话出奇地提神；即使在漫长的一天后，我也很清醒。
The strong coffee had an antisoporific effect, allowing me to study late into the night.  浓咖啡有提神的作用，让我可以学习到深夜。
synonyms: stimulating, invigorating, awakening, arousing
antonyms: soporific, sleep-inducing, hypnotic, sedative
词性: 形容词
