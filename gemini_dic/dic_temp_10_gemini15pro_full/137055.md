nonstocks:/ˈnɑːnˌstɑːks/ | n. | 非股票，非库存商品 | Goods or assets that are not stocks, typically referring to items a company holds that are not intended for sale. |  The company's nonstocks include office furniture and equipment.  这家公司的非股票资产包括办公家具和设备。 | synonyms: non-inventory items, fixed assets | antonyms: stocks, inventory | noun

nonstocks:/ˈnɑːnˌstɑːks/ | n. | 非股票 | Investments or financial instruments that are not stocks, such as bonds or real estate. | He diversified his portfolio by investing in nonstocks like bonds and real estate. 他通过投资债券和房地产等非股票来分散投资组合。 | synonyms: non-equity investments, alternative investments | antonyms: stocks, equities | noun
