impedimental:/ɪmˌpedɪˈmɛntəl/
adjective
妨碍的，阻碍的
hindering or obstructing progress.
The cumbersome bureaucratic process proved impedimental to the project's timely completion.  繁琐的官僚程序证明对项目的及时完成造成了阻碍。
The high cost of living in the city was impedimental to her dream of opening a bakery.  城市的高生活成本阻碍了她开面包店的梦想。
hindering, obstructing, hampering
facilitative, helpful, aiding
adjective
