insatiety:/ˌɪnseɪˈʃaɪəti/ |n.|不知足，贪得无厌|The state of being unable to be satisfied.  A persistent feeling of not having had enough.|His insatiety for power led to his downfall. 他对权力的贪得无厌导致了他的垮台。|Synonyms: greed, avarice, gluttony, voracity |Antonyms: contentment, satisfaction, satiety|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *insatietas*, from insatiatus (“unsatisfied”) + -tas (“-ty, -ness”).  Insatiatus is from in- (“not”) + satiatus (“satisfied”).
