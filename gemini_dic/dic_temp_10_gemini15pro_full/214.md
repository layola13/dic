abbreviation: /əˌbriːviˈeɪʃn/
n.
缩写;缩写词
A shortened form of a word or phrase.
The abbreviation for "Mister" is "Mr."  “先生”的缩写是“Mr.”

The word "laser" is an abbreviation. “laser”这个词是一个缩写词。

近义词:shortening, contraction, acronym
反义词:expansion, elaboration
词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin abbreviationem (nominative abbreviatio) "a shortening," noun of action from past-participle stem of abbreviare "to shorten," from Latin ad- "to" + brevis "short."
