opy:/ˈɒpi/| n.| 拷贝；复制品 |A copy is a thing made to be the same as another thing. |例句：He made a copy of the document. 他复制了这份文件。

例句：This painting is a copy of the original. 这幅画是原作的复制品。|近义词：duplicate, replica, reproduction|反义词:original|词性:名词
