dispensing:/dɪˈspɛnsɪŋ/
n.|分发; 配药; 免除;|The act of distributing or providing something, especially medication. |The pharmacist was careful in his dispensing of the medication.|distribution, allocation, providing| withholding, retaining
v.|分发; 配药; 免除;| To distribute or provide something, especially medication.  |The machine dispenses snacks and drinks.|distribute, allocate, provide| withhold, retain
adj.|分配的;配药的;|Relating to the act of dispensing. |The dispensing chemist prepared the prescription.|distributive| --
词性: 可以是名词, 动词, 或形容词.

词源: From Old French "dispense", from Latin "dispensare" - to distribute, manage.
