bobo:/ˈboʊboʊ/| n. |富裕的自由主义者；中产阶级知识分子；波波头 (旧)|A person, typically with liberal views, who is educated and affluent.  Sometimes used disparagingly to suggest pretension or lack of practicality.  Can also refer to a short haircut.|例句：Bobos tend to shop at organic markets and drive hybrid cars.  波波族倾向于在有机市场购物并驾驶混合动力汽车。
