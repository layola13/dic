giving: /ˈɡɪvɪŋ/ | n. adj.|给予，赠送；捐赠|The act of bestowing something. |例句：Giving is better than receiving.  赠予比接受更好。

例句：The giving of gifts is a common tradition during holidays.  赠送礼物是节日期间的一个常见传统。|近义词：donating, bestowing, contributing, offering |反义词: receiving, taking, accepting |词性: noun

giving: /ˈɡɪvɪŋ/ | adj.|慷慨的；有奉献精神的|Generous and willing to share. |例句：She has a giving nature. 她有着慷慨的天性。

例句：He was a giving person, always ready to help those in need. 他是一个乐于奉献的人，总是准备好帮助那些需要帮助的人。|近义词：generous, charitable, benevolent, altruistic |反义词: selfish, stingy, mean |词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *giefan (West Saxon giefan, Anglian gefan), from Proto-Germanic *gebaną (“to give”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰébʰ- (“to give, take”). Cognate with Old Frisian jeva, Old Saxon geban, Old High German geban, Old Norse gefa, Gothic 𐌲𐌹𐌱𐌰𐌽 (giban).
