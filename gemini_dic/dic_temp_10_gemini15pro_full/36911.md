colorizer:/ˈkʌləˌraɪzər/| n.|着色器，彩色化工具|A person or thing that adds color, especially to a black and white film or photograph.|The colorizer brought the old black and white movie back to life. 着色器使这部黑白老电影焕发了生机。|近义词: colorist, tinter|反义词: bleacher, fader|词性: noun

colorize:/ˈkʌləˌraɪz/| v.|着色，彩色化|To add color to something, especially a black and white film or photograph.|They colorized the old family photos. 他们把旧的家庭照片着色了。|近义词: tint, color, paint|反义词: bleach, fade|词性: verb
