disobeyers:/ˌdɪsəˈbeɪərz/
n.
不服从者，违抗者
People who refuse to obey rules or commands are called disobeyers.
The disobeyers were punished for their actions.  不服从者因其行为受到了惩罚。
近义词: rebels, nonconformists, insubordinates
反义词: conformists, followers, adherents
词性: Noun (Plural)

disobey:/ˌdɪsəˈbeɪ/
v.
不服从，违抗
To disobey is to refuse or fail to comply with rules, commands, or instructions.
Children should not disobey their parents. 孩子们不应该不服从他们的父母。
近义词: defy, contravene, infringe
反义词: obey, comply, conform
词性: Verb

disobedient:/ˌdɪsəˈbiːdiənt/
adj.
不服从的，违抗的
Disobedient describes someone who refuses to obey.
The disobedient child was sent to his room.  不服从的孩子被送回了他的房间。
近义词: rebellious, unruly, defiant
反义词: obedient, compliant, submissive
词性: Adjective
