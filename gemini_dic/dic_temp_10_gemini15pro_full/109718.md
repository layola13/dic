leveler:/ˈlɛvələr (US), ˈlɛvlə (UK)| n.|水平仪, 平整器; 使平等的人(或物);|A tool or machine used to make a surface level. Also, a person or thing that promotes equality.|The contractor used a laser leveler to ensure the foundation was perfectly flat. 承包商使用激光水平仪确保地基完全平坦。|近义词：equalizer, grader|反义词:discriminator|Noun

leveler:/ˈlɛvələr (US), ˈlɛvlə (UK)| n.|使平等的人(或物);|Something that removes or reduces differences or inequalities.|Education is often seen as a social leveler. 教育通常被视为一种社会平衡器。|近义词： equalizer|反义词: segregator|Noun
