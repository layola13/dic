outprices:/ˌaʊtˈpraɪsɪz/
verb
定价过高，价格超过
charges more than someone else for something, so that they are unable to compete
The smaller shops were being outpriced by the supermarkets.  小型商店的价格比不过超市。
近义词:price out, undersell
反义词:underprice
词性:v.
