cormophyte:/ˈkɔːrməˌfaɪt/ | n. |  cormophytes (plural) | 维管植物；茎叶植物 |A vascular plant of the subkingdom Cormophyta (now usually treated as the division Tracheophyta); a tracheophyte.  |  Example: Ferns, conifers, and flowering plants are all cormophytes.  蕨类植物、针叶植物和开花植物都是维管植物。 | Example:  Cormophytes have specialized tissues for transporting water and nutrients. 维管植物具有专门的组织来运输水分和养分。 | Synonym: tracheophyte, vascular plant | Antonym:  thallophyte (non-vascular plant) | Noun
