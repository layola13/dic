engulfment:/ɪnˈɡʌlfmənt/ | n. |吞没，包围，吞噬|The engulfment of the town by the flood waters was a tragedy.  小镇被洪水吞没是一场悲剧。| The flames quickly spread, leading to the engulfment of the entire building. 火势迅速蔓延，导致整栋楼被大火吞噬。| 近义词: submersion, immersion, overwhelming, enveloping | 反义词: release, exposure | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From en- (expressing inclusion or surrounding) + gulf (a deep inlet of the sea) + -ment (forming nouns of action or process).  Ultimately from the Old French engolfer (to swallow up).
