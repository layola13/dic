classic: /ˈklæsɪk/ (US) /ˈklæsɪk/ (UK)| adj. n.| 经典的；一流的；典型的；古典的；名著|A work of art of recognized and established value; of the first or highest quality, class, or rank|  This car is a classic.  这辆车是辆经典款。|近义词: typical, standard, exemplary, vintage|反义词: modern, contemporary, inferior|词性:形容词

classic: /ˈklæsɪk/ (US) /ˈklæsɪk/ (UK)| n.| 经典作品；名著|A work of enduring excellence|  "Pride and Prejudice" is a classic. 《傲慢与偏见》是一部经典之作。|近义词: masterpiece, standard, archetype|反义词: dud, flop|词性:名词


词源:  From Late Latin classicus, from Latin classis "class, division (of citizens)," thus "of the first class."
