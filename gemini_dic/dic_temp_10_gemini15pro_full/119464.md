micrometeorite: /ˈmaɪkroʊˌmiːtiəraɪt/ | n. | 微陨石 | A very small meteorite or particle of space dust.  A micrometeorite is usually less than a millimeter in size. |  Micrometeorites constantly bombard the Earth's atmosphere. 微陨石不断轰击地球大气层。

Micrometeorites can be collected from rooftops and other exposed surfaces.  微陨石可以从屋顶和其他暴露的表面收集。 | 近义词: space dust, cosmic dust | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "macrometeorite" - though not a standard term) | 词性: noun
