catamount:/ˈkætəˌmaʊnt/ | n. |美洲狮，山狮；狂暴的人|A large wild cat, native to North and South America, also known as a cougar, puma, or mountain lion.  Figuratively, it can also refer to a fierce or wild person. |例句：The catamount stalked its prey through the forest. 美洲狮在森林里跟踪它的猎物。

例句：He fought like a catamount, defending his family. 他像美洲狮一样战斗，保卫着他的家人。|近义词：cougar, puma, mountain lion, panther |反义词: (no direct antonym, could consider "prey", "domestic cat")|词性:noun
