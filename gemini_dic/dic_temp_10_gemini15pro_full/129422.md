nominatively:/ˈnɒmɪnətɪvli/ | adv. | 主格地，主格形式地 | In Latin, nouns are declined nominatively, genitively, datively, accusatively, ablatively, and vocatively. 在拉丁语中，名词有主格、属格、与格、宾格、夺格和呼格的变化。 |  He used the word "I" nominatively in the sentence. 他在这个句子中以主格形式使用了“我”这个词。 | 近义词:subjectively | 反义词:objectively | 词性:副词


Etymology:
From Late Latin *nominativus, from Latin nominativus (“of or belonging to naming”), from nominare (“to name”), from nomen (“name”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₁nómn̥ (“name”).
