It encompasses a wide range of skills, techniques, and ingredients used to transform raw ingredients into palatable and enjoyable meals.|She took a course in French cookery. 她参加了一个法式烹饪课程。|Synonyms: cooking, cuisine, gastronomy|Antonyms: N/A (abstract noun)|noun
