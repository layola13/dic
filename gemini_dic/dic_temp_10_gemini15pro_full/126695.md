name-dropping:/ˈneɪmˌdrɒpɪŋ/ | n. | 炫耀交际; 故意提名人名以提高身价; |The conversation was full of name-dropping.  谈话中充斥着炫耀交际。|  Name-dropping is considered rude in many cultures. 在许多文化中，炫耀交际被认为是粗鲁的。 | 近义词：boasting, bragging | 反义词：humility, modesty | 词性: noun

Etymology:  "name" + "dropping," from the verb phrase "to drop names" which emerged in the early 20th century.  The image is of casually mentioning famous names as if they were insignificant, like letting something drop.
