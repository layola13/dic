antacid:/ænˈtæsɪd/
noun|抗酸剂；抗酸药|A substance that neutralizes acidity, especially in the stomach.|She took an antacid to relieve her heartburn. 她服用了抗酸药来缓解胃灼热。|synonyms: antacid agent, alkali, base|antonyms: acid|n.

antacid:/ænˈtæsɪd/
adjective|抗酸的|Neutralizing acidity, especially of the stomach.|Antacid tablets can provide quick relief. 抗酸片可以快速缓解症状。|synonyms: alkaline, neutralizing|antonyms: acidic|adj.
