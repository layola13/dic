amassing:/əˈmæsɪŋ/
verb
积累，积聚
gathering or accumulating something, typically in large quantities.
He is amassing a fortune in the stock market. 他在股市积累了一笔财富。
Synonyms: accumulating, gathering, collecting, hoarding, stockpiling
Antonyms: distributing, dispersing, scattering, spending, squandering
词性:动词
