antimethodicalness:/ˌæntiˌmɛθˈɒdɪkəlnəs/ | n. | 无条理性；杂乱无章 | Lack of method or order; disorderliness.  The state of being unsystematic or lacking in method. |  Her antimethodicalness made it difficult to find anything in her office. 她的杂乱无章使得在她的办公室里很难找到任何东西。| synonyms: disorder, chaos, disorganization, messiness | antonyms: order, organization, system, methodicalness | noun

antimethodical:/ˌæntiˌmɛθˈɒdɪkəl/ | adj. |  无条理的；杂乱无章的 | Not methodical; lacking method or order; unsystematic. | His antimethodical approach to research frustrated his colleagues. 他无条理的研究方法让他的同事们感到沮丧。| synonyms: disordered, chaotic, disorganized, messy | antonyms: methodical, organized, systematic, orderly | adjective
