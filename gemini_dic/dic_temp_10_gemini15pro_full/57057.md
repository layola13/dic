doubtingly:/ˈdaʊtɪŋli/| adv.|怀疑地，疑惑地|In a manner expressing doubt or uncertainty.  She looked at him doubtingly, unsure if he was telling the truth.|例句：He nodded doubtingly, not quite convinced. 他怀疑地点点头，不太相信。

例句：She smiled doubtingly, as if she didn't believe him. 她怀疑地笑了笑，好像不相信他。|近义词：hesitantly, uncertainly, skeptically|反义词：confidently, certainly, assuredly|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From doubt + -ing + -ly.  "Doubt" comes from Old French "douter"  (to doubt, be uncertain), ultimately from Latin "dubitare" (to hesitate, waver in opinion).
