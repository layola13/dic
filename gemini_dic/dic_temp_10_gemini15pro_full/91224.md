hydrocarbons:/ˌhaɪdrəˈkɑːrbənz/ | n. | 碳氢化合物 | An organic compound consisting entirely of hydrogen and carbon. | Crude oil is a complex mixture of hydrocarbons. 原油是碳氢化合物的复杂混合物。 |  Methane is the simplest hydrocarbon. 甲烷是最简单的碳氢化合物。 | synonyms:  alkanes, alkenes, alkynes | antonyms: N/A | noun
