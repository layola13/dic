fake:/feɪk/| adj. n. v. |假的，伪造的；赝品；伪造，假装|A fake is something that is not genuine.  To fake something is to pretend or to make something appear to be genuine when it is not.|例句：The painting was a fake. 这幅画是赝品。|近义词：false, counterfeit, forged, phony, sham|反义词：genuine, real, authentic|词性:形容词

fake:/feɪk/| n. |赝品，骗子|A fake is a person who pretends to be something they are not, or a thing that is made to look like something else, especially something valuable.|例句：He was exposed as a fake. 他被揭露是个骗子。|近义词：fraud, imposter, sham|反义词：genuine article|词性:名词

fake:/feɪk/| v. |伪造，假装|To fake something is to make something appear to be genuine when it is not, or to pretend to have a feeling or quality.|例句：She faked her own death. 她伪造了自己的死亡。|近义词：feign, simulate, pretend, counterfeit|反义词：be genuine|词性:动词
