conglomerative:/kənˈɡlɒmərətɪv/ (US) /kənˈɡlɑːmərətɪv/ (UK)| adj.|聚集的；成团的；砾岩的|Formed of parts from various sources or of various kinds.|The company is a massive conglomerate, owning businesses in everything from newspapers to theme parks.|近义词：aggregate, combined, composite|反义词: discrete, separate|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin conglomeratus, past participle of conglomerare (“to roll together, heap up”), from com- (“together”) + glomerare (“to wind into a ball”), from glomus (“ball”).
