batistas:/bæpˈtiːstəs/
noun
浸信会教友（复数）
Baptists are Christians distinguished by Baptist churches and their adherence to a doctrine that baptism should be performed only for professing believers (believer's baptism, as opposed to infant baptism), and that it must be done by complete immersion.
例句：The Baptists in our town have a large and active congregation. 我们镇上的浸信会教友有一个庞大而活跃的群体。

例句：Many Baptists hold strong beliefs about the importance of adult baptism. 许多浸信会教友对成人洗礼的重要性持有坚定的信念。
近义词：Christians, Protestants
反义词：(No direct antonym, potentially members of other denominations like Catholics or Methodists)
词性:noun
