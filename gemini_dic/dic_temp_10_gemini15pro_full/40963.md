cooperativization:/koʊˌɑːpərətɪvɪˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|合作化，集体化|The process of forming a cooperative or transforming something into a cooperative.  The act of organizing something on a cooperative basis.|The cooperativization of agriculture in the 1950s had a profound impact on rural communities. 20世纪50年代的农业合作化对农村社区产生了深远的影响。|Synonym: collectivization|Antonym: privatization|noun

Etymology->
From cooperate + -ization.  "Cooperate" comes from the Latin "co-" (together) and "operari" (to work). "-ization" is a suffix denoting the process of making or becoming.
