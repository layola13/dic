oversquare:/ˌoʊvərˈskwɛər/| adj.|过正方形的；（发动机）缸径大于冲程的|An oversquare engine has a cylinder bore that is larger than its piston stroke.|The car has an oversquare engine, which provides more horsepower at higher RPMs. 这辆车的发动机缸径大于冲程，在更高的转速下提供更大的马力。|近义词：short-stroke (engine)|反义词：undersquare|词性:形容词

oversquare:/ˌoʊvərˈskwɛər/| n.|过正方形；缸径大于冲程的发动机|An oversquare refers to an engine where the cylinder bore is larger than the piston stroke.|The oversquare allows for larger valves and better breathing, leading to increased power.  这种过正方形设计允许更大的阀门和更好的呼吸，从而提高功率。|近义词：short-stroke (engine)|反义词：undersquare|词性:名词
