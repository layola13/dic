overpartialnesses: /ˌoʊvərˌpɑːrʃəlˈnɛsɪz/ | n. | 过度偏袒，过分偏爱 |  An excessive tendency to be partial or biased, showing too much favoritism. This word is not commonly used. |  His overpartialnesses towards his youngest child caused resentment among his other children. 他对最小的孩子的过度偏袒引起了其他孩子的怨恨。 | 近义词: bias, favoritism, partiality | 反义词: impartiality, fairness, neutrality | 词性: 名词

词源:  Formed from "over-" (meaning excessive) + "partial" (inclined to favor one side unfairly) + "-ness" (forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  Ultimately from Latin "pars" (part).
