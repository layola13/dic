aphides: /ˈeɪfɪdz/ (US), /ˈeɪfɪdiːz/ (UK)| n. |蚜虫|Small insects that feed by sucking sap from plants. They reproduce rapidly and can cause significant damage to crops.| The roses were covered in aphides. 玫瑰上爬满了蚜虫。| The aphides are destroying my garden! 这些蚜虫正在摧毁我的花园！| synonyms: greenfly, blackfly, plant louse | antonyms: N/A | noun
