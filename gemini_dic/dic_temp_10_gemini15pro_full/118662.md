meta:/ˈmɛtə/| adj. adv. n.| 元的，超越的，后面的 | referring to itself or to the conventions of its genre; self-referential | The movie is very meta, constantly reminding the audience that they are watching a movie.  这部电影非常“元”，不断提醒观众他们正在看电影。 | 近义词：self-referential, reflexive | 反义词: literal, direct | 词性: adjective, adverb, noun

meta:/ˈmɛtə/| adj. adv. n.| 后面的，在…以后的 | situated behind or beyond | The metacarpal bones are in the hand, meta-tarsal bones are in the feet. 掌骨在手上，跖骨在脚上。| 近义词：posterior, after | 反义词: anterior, before | 词性: adjective, adverb, noun
