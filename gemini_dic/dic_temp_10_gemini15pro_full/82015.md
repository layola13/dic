注意事项:
“Grippa” is primarily used in informal British English. It's less common in American English, where "flu" or "influenza" are preferred.  There isn't a direct antonym for a specific illness like "grippa."  "Health" or "wellness" could be considered opposite concepts, but not direct antonyms.
