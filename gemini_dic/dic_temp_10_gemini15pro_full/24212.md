bridge-builder:/ˈbrɪdʒ ˌbɪldər/| n.|桥梁建造者；搭建桥梁的人；消除隔阂的人|A person who constructs bridges, either literally or metaphorically (by fostering connections or reconciliation).|例句：He was a renowned bridge-builder in the community, bringing together people from different backgrounds. 他是社区里著名的桥梁建造者，把不同背景的人聚集在一起。

例句：The diplomat acted as a bridge-builder between the two warring nations.  这位外交官充当了两个交战国之间的桥梁建造者。|近义词：mediator, conciliator, unifier|反义词：divider, separatist|词性:noun


bridge-builder:/ˈbrɪdʒ ˌbɪldər/| adj.|桥梁建设的；搭建桥梁的|Relating to the construction of bridges or the act of building bridges, either literally or metaphorically.|例句：The bridge-building project is expected to be completed next year.  这座桥梁建设项目预计明年完工。

例句：Her bridge-building efforts helped to heal the rift between the two families. 她搭建桥梁的努力帮助弥合了两个家庭之间的裂痕。|近义词：connective, unifying|反义词：divisive, separating|词性:adjective
