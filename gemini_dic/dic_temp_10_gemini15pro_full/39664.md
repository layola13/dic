conservatorial:/kənˌsɜːrvəˈtɔːriəl/| adj.|保守的，保护的，温室的|Relating to or characteristic of a conservatory, especially a school of music or dramatic art.  Also, relating to the careful preservation or restoration of something, or disposed to preserve existing conditions, institutions, etc., or to restore traditional ones, and to limit change.|The Royal Academy of Music is a well-known conservatorial institution. 皇家音乐学院是一所著名的音乐学院。  He has a very conservatorial approach to education. 他对教育持非常保守的态度。|Synonyms: traditional, conventional, orthodox, cautious, protective|Antonyms: radical, progressive, innovative, revolutionary|adjective
