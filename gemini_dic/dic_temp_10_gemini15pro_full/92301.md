hypnic:/ˈhɪpnɪk/| adj.|催眠的，与睡眠有关的|Relating to sleep or the state of being hypnotized.  It often describes the transitional state between wakefulness and sleep.|例句：Hypnic jerks are sudden involuntary muscle spasms that occur as a person is falling asleep.  入睡抽搐是指人们在入睡时发生的突然的不自主的肌肉痉挛。

例句：She experienced vivid hypnic hallucinations just before drifting off. 她入睡前经历了生动的催眠性幻觉。|近义词：somnolent, soporific|反义词：wakeful, alert|词性:adjective
