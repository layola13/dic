go-after:/ɡoʊ ˈæftər/ /ɡəʊ ˈɑːftə(r)/| phrasal verb |追求，追逐，追捕；争取，谋求|To pursue or try to obtain something.|例句：He's going after the top job. 他正在争取最高职位。|例句：The police went after the robbers. 警察追捕抢劫犯。|近义词：pursue, chase, seek, strive for|反义词：give up, abandon, surrender|词性：动词短语

词源: "Go" comes from Old English "gān," meaning "to walk, advance, depart." "After" comes from Old English "æfter," meaning "following in place or time."  The combination creates the sense of moving toward something that is ahead or further along.
