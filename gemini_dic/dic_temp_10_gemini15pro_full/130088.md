non-procreative:/ˌnɒn prəʊˈkriːətɪv/ /ˌnɑːn proʊˈkriːeɪtɪv/ | adj. |非生育的，不生育的|Not involved in or intended for reproduction or the creation of offspring.| Some couples choose a non-procreative lifestyle.  一些夫妇选择非生育的生活方式。|  Non-procreative relationships can be just as fulfilling as those that involve children. 非生育关系和有孩子的关系一样可以令人满足。|近义词：childless, sterile, infertile |反义词: procreative, fertile |词性:形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
procreative (adjective):  from Late Latin procreativus, from procreare "to beget," from pro- "forth" + creare "to create."
