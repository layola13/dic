ghastliest:/ˈɡæstliɪst/ | adj. | 最可怕的，最恐怖的 |  Superlative form of ghastly: most ghastly.  It describes something extremely shocking, horrifying, or unpleasant. | The ghastliest thing I've ever seen was the aftermath of the car crash. 我见过的最可怕的事情是车祸的后果。| The abandoned house had a ghastliest atmosphere. 这栋废弃的房子有一种最恐怖的气氛。 |  horrifying, terrifying, frightful, dreadful, appalling | pleasant, delightful, agreeable | adjective
