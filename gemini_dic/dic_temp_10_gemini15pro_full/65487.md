europeanization:/jʊərəpiːənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 欧洲化 | The process by which a country or region adopts the political, social, and economic norms and practices of the European Union. |  The candidate countries underwent a period of rapid europeanization before joining the EU. 候选国在加入欧盟之前经历了一段快速欧洲化的时期。

The europeanization of the Balkans has been a long and complex process. 巴尔干半岛的欧洲化是一个漫长而复杂的过程。 | 近义词: westernization, integration | 反义词: isolation, fragmentation | 词性: noun


Etymology:  From Europe + -an + -ize + -ation.  Ultimately from Greek Eurṓpē (Europe) + -an (forming adjectives) + -ize (forming verbs meaning "to make or become like") + -ation (forming nouns of action or process).
