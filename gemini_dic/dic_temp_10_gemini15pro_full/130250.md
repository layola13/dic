non-specific:/ˌnɒn spəˈsɪfɪk/
adjective
非特异性的；非专一的；不明确的
Not relating to any specific thing or category; general.
The patient's symptoms were non-specific, making diagnosis difficult. 病人的症状非特异性，使诊断变得困难。
The doctor prescribed a non-specific antibiotic. 医生开了一种非特异性抗生素。
近义词:general, generic, unspecific, vague, broad, imprecise
反义词:specific, precise, definite, particular, targeted
