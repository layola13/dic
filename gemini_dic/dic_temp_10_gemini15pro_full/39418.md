connatural:/kəˈnætʃərəl/ |adj.|同自然的，天然的，固有的|Existing with or inherent in something else; innate.  Sharing the same nature.|例句：A connatural impulse to help others.  帮助他人的内在冲动。|例句：Connaturally, he also has a knack for making a profit. 他天生也有赚钱的本领。|近义词：innate, inherent, intrinsic, natural, ingrained|反义词：acquired, learned, extrinsic, unnatural|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *connaturalis*, from Latin *con-* (with, together) + *naturalis* (natural).
