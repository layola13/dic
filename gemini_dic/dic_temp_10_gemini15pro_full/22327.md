bonnetless:/ˈbɒnɪtləs/
adjective
无帽的；没戴帽子的
Without a bonnet.  A bonnet is a kind of hat, typically tied under the chin and worn by women or girls.
The bonnetless girl braved the cold wind.  没戴帽子的女孩顶着寒风。
Near synonyms:  bareheaded, hatless, uncovered
Antonyms:  bonneted, hatted, covered
词性: adjective
