hypersensitively:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɛnsɪtɪvli/
adverb|过度敏感地|in a way that is too easily upset, offended, or affected by something|She reacted hypersensitively to the criticism, bursting into tears.|近义词:overly sensitively, excessively sensitively|反义词:insensitively, indifferently|词性:副词
Etymology: From hyper- (meaning "over, excessive") + sensitive + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Sensitive" comes from Late Latin *sensitivus*, from Latin sentire "to feel."
