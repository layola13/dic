conspurcate:/kənˈspɜːrkeɪt/
verb
玷污，败坏，污染
to make impure or unclean; to defile, pollute, or corrupt morally
The scandal conspurcated the politician's reputation. 这桩丑闻玷污了这位政治家的名誉。
Synonyms: defile, pollute, taint, corrupt, sully, besmirch
Antonyms: purify, cleanse, sanctify
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *conspurcāre*  from *con-* (together, with) + *spurcāre* (to defile), from *spurcus* (dirty, filthy).
