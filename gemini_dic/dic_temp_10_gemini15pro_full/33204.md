chromule: /ˈkrəʊmjʊl/ | n. | 色素体，色素粒 | A plastid containing pigment.  Specifically, a chromoplast other than a chloroplast. | Example: The bright red color of tomatoes is due to chromules within the fruit cells. 番茄鲜红的颜色是由于果细胞内的色素体造成的。

Example: Chromules are responsible for the vibrant colors found in many flowers and fruits. 色素体是许多花卉和水果鲜艳颜色的原因。 | 近义词: plastid, chromatophore | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源:  From Greek *χρῶμα (khrôma, “color”) +‎ *-ουλον (-oulon)
