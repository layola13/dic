coniferous:/kəˈnɪfərəs/
adjective
松柏科的；针叶树的
Relating to or belonging to the conifers; bearing cones.
The mountains are covered in dense coniferous forest.  这些山脉覆盖着茂密的针叶林。
The coniferous trees provide year-round greenery. 这些针叶树常年保持绿色。
近义词: evergreen, pine-bearing
反义词: deciduous
词性: adjective
