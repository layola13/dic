misaccompt: /ˌmɪsəˈkɑːmpt/
noun
误算，失算；错误的估计
An error in calculation or accounting; a miscalculation or mismanagement.
He blamed the company's failure on a series of financial misaccompts. 他将公司的失败归咎于一系列的财务失算。
miscalculation, mismanagement, blunder, error, mistake
accurate calculation, correct estimation, proper management
