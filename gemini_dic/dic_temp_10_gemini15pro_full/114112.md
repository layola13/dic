magnum:/ˈmæɡnəm/| n.|大酒瓶；大容量瓶；（尤指）1.5升装葡萄酒瓶；大尺寸的东西|A large bottle, especially of wine, holding 1.5 liters (1.76 imperial pints). A thing of large size.|He ordered a magnum of champagne. 他点了一大瓶香槟。|Near synonyms: large bottle, double bottle|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "small bottle")|noun
magnums:/ˈmæɡnəmz/| n.|大酒瓶（复数）；大容量瓶（复数）|Plural of magnum.  Large bottles, especially of wine.|They brought out several magnums of red wine for the celebration. 他们拿出几大瓶红酒来庆祝。|Near synonyms: large bottles, double bottles (plural)|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could consider "small bottles")|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
