drafters:/ˈdræftərz/
n.
起草人，制图员
People who create preliminary versions of documents or drawings.
The drafters worked late into the night to finish the blueprints.  制图员们工作到深夜才完成蓝图。
drafters spent hours perfecting the legal document. 起草人花了数小时完善法律文件。

近义词:writers, authors, designers, architects, composers, planners
反义词:editors, reviewers, critics

词性:noun (plural)

词源:From "draft" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Draft" comes from the Old Norse "draught" meaning "to draw, pull, drag."  来源于“draft” + “-er”（代理后缀）。“Draft”来自古挪威语“draught”，意思是“拉，拖”。
