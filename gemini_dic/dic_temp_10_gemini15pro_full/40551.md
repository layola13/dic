controverter:/ˈkɒntrəvɜːtə(r)/ | n. | 争论者，辩论者 | Someone who engages in controversies or disputes; a disputant. | He was a known controverter of established scientific theories. 他是一位知名的对既定科学理论的争论者。 |  Example: The controverter refused to back down from his position.  这位争论者拒绝放弃他的立场。 | Synonyms: disputant, arguer, debater, polemicist | Antonyms:  proponent, supporter, advocate | Noun
