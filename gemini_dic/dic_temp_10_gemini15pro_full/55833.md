dividend:/ˈdɪvɪdend/| n.|股息；红利；回报；被除数|A sum of money paid regularly (typically annually) by a company to its shareholders out of its profits (or reserves).  A benefit from an action or policy.|The company announced a dividend of 50 cents per share. 公司宣布每股派息50美分。|synonyms: payout, distribution, return, yield|antonyms: debt, loss|noun
