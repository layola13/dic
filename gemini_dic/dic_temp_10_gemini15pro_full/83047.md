gurgeon:/ˈɡɜːrdʒən/| n. |鲦鱼，欧洲鰷鱼|A small freshwater fish of the cyprinid family, found in European rivers. It is similar to the roach but has a longer, more pointed snout.|The river teemed with gurgeons. 这条河里有很多鲦鱼。|近义词：roach, dace|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
