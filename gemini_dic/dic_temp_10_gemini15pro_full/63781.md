epicurean:/ˌɛpɪˈkjʊəriən/ | adj. n. | 好美食的；享乐主义的；伊壁鸠鲁学派的；享乐主义者 | Fond of or adapted to luxury or indulgence in sensual pleasures; devoted to pleasure; of or relating to Epicurus or his philosophy. | He is an epicurean, always seeking the finest wines and cheeses. 他是个美食家，总是寻找最好的葡萄酒和奶酪。 |  He lived an epicurean lifestyle, indulging in every pleasure imaginable. 他过着享乐主义的生活，沉溺于一切可以想象的乐趣。 | 近义词: hedonistic, luxurious, gourmet, sybaritic | 反义词: ascetic, austere, spartan | 词性: adjective, noun

epicureous: /ˌɛpɪˈkjʊəriəs/ | adj. | 好美食的；享乐的 |  Fond of or adapted to luxury or indulgence in sensual pleasures. |  She prepared an epicureous feast for her guests. 她为客人准备了一场盛宴。 |  They enjoyed an epicureous lifestyle, filled with fine dining and travel.  他们享受着充满美食和旅行的奢华生活方式。 | 近义词: luxurious, gourmet, hedonistic, sybaritic | 反义词: ascetic, austere, spartan | 词性: adjective


词源:  From Late Latin *Epicurēus, from Latin Epicūrus + -eus. Ultimately from Ancient Greek Ἐπίκουρος (Epíkouros).
