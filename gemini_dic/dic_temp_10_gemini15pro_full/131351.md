nonchemists:/ˌnɒnˈkɛmɪsts/ /ˌnɑːnˈkɛmɪsts/| n. |非化学家；非化学专业人士|People who are not chemists or not specialized in chemistry. |Many nonchemists find the subject of organic chemistry daunting. 许多非化学专业人士觉得有机化学这门学科令人生畏。| Nonchemists often misunderstand the role of chemicals in everyday life. 非化学专业人士经常误解化学物质在日常生活中的作用。|synonyms:laypeople, nonspecialists|antonyms:chemists, chemical specialists|noun
