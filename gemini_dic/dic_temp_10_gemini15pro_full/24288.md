brigandry:/ˈbrɪɡəndri/| n.|土匪行为；抢劫；盗匪行径|The act of a brigand; robbery and plundering.|The travelers feared brigandry on the lonely road. 旅行者们害怕在荒凉的路上遇到抢劫。|近义词：banditry, robbery, pillage, plunder, highway robbery|反义词:lawfulness, order, security|名词

Etymology:
From French "brigandage," from Italian "brigandaggio," from "brigando" (brigand), of uncertain origin, possibly from a Vulgar Latin word "*brigant-" related to Old High German "brechan" (to break) and Old English "brecan" (to break).
