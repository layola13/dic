detesting:/dɪˈtɛstɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
厌恶；憎恶
feeling intense dislike or hatred for.
She was detesting the way he spoke to her. 她很讨厌他跟她说话的方式。
近义词: loathing, abhorring, hating, despising
反义词: adoring, loving, liking
词性: verb (present participle)
