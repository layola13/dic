calamity:/kəˈlæməti/
n.
灾难，灾祸
A great misfortune or disaster.
The earthquake was a calamity for the entire region.  这场地震对整个地区来说都是一场灾难。
The flood was a major calamity. 洪水是一场重大灾难。
disaster, catastrophe, cataclysm, tragedy
fortune, blessing
noun


Etymology->
From Old French *calamité, from Latin calamitas (“misfortune, damage, injury, loss, defeat”), from calamus (“reed, stalk of grain”). Possibly related to Greek κάλαμος (kálamos, “reed”).  The sense is probably of a storm laying low standing grain.
