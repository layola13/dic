interagreement:/ˌɪntərəˈɡriːmənt/ | n. | 内部协议，相互协议 | An agreement between different parts of the same organization or between different members of the same group. | Example 1:  The interagreement between the marketing and sales departments ensured a smooth product launch.  市场部和销售部之间的内部协议确保了产品的顺利发布。

Example 2: The family reached an interagreement about how to divide the inheritance.  这家人就如何分配遗产达成了一项内部协议。 | synonym: accord, pact, contract, understanding | antonym: disagreement, dispute, conflict | noun
