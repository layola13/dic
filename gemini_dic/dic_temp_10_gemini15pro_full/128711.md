niello:/niˈɛloʊ/| n.|金银嵌黑细工；嵌黑装饰品|A black metallic alloy, traditionally of silver, copper, lead, and sulfur, used as an inlay for decorating engraved or etched metal, especially silver or gold.  A technique of decorating metal by engraving or etching a design and then filling the incised lines with niello.|The antique silver box was decorated with intricate niello.|近义词：inlay, enamel|反义词:None|词性:noun

niello:/niˈɛloʊ/| v.|用金银嵌黑细工装饰|To decorate (metal) with niello.|The craftsman nielloed the silver locket with a delicate floral pattern.|近义词：inlay, engrave|反义词:None|词性:verb
