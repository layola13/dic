ambidextral: /ˌæmbɪˈdɛkstrəl/
adjective
双撇子；左右手都灵巧的
Able to use both hands equally well.
She's an ambidextrous artist, equally skilled with both hands. 她是一位左右手都非常熟练的艺术家。
Ambidextrous tennis players have an advantage. 双手皆可灵活运用网球的球员更有优势。

近义词: versatile, adaptable
反义词: left-handed, right-handed
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ambidexter, from ambi- (“both”) + dexter (“right, skillful”).  The original Latin word had negative connotations, implying being "double-dealing" or "two-faced."
