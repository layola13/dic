bulldoggednesses:/ˈbʊldɔːɡɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
顽强, 坚韧, 固执
The quality of being bulldogged; tenacious persistence.
He showed remarkable bulldoggedness in pursuing his goals. 他在追求目标的过程中表现出非凡的顽强精神。
近义词: tenacity, persistence, perseverance, determination
反义词: weakness, irresolution, vacillation
词性: 名词

词源:  From bulldogged + -ness.  Ultimately from bulldog (a breed of dog known for its tenacity) + -ed (forming the past participle and sometimes adjectives) + -ness (forming nouns expressing a state or quality).
