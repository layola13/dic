gamosepalous:/ˌɡæmoʊˈsɛpələs/| adj.|合萼的|Having the sepals united or joined, at least at the base, forming a tube or cup.|The Hibiscus flower is gamosepalous, its sepals fused into a single structure.|近义词：synsepalous|反义词：polysepalous|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Greek *gamos* (marriage, union) + sepalous (relating to sepals)
