noteful:/ˈnoʊtfʊl/| adj.|值得注意的；重要的；显著的|Taking note of something; noteworthy.  Worthy of attention.  Significant. |例句：That was a noteful event in the history of the company.  那是公司历史上一个值得注意的事件。|近义词：significant, important, noteworthy, remarkable, memorable|反义词：insignificant, unimportant, trivial|词性:形容词
词源: note (n.) + -ful (suffix).  "Note" comes from Old French "note" meaning "mark, sign, melody." "-ful" is a suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by."
