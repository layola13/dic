haggard:/ˈhæɡərd/| adj.|憔悴的，形容枯槁的|Having a gaunt, worn, or exhausted appearance, especially from prolonged suffering, exertion, or anxiety.|例句：He looked haggard after his long journey.  他长途旅行后看起来很憔悴。|例句：The refugees arrived looking tired and haggard.  难民们到达时看起来疲惫不堪，形容枯槁。|近义词：gaunt, drawn, exhausted, worn, weary|反义词：healthy, robust, vibrant|词性:形容词
