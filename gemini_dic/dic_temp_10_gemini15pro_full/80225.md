godsons:/ˈɡɒdsʌnz/ (British) /ˈɡɑːdsʌnz/ (American)| n. |教子（复数）|A male child who is sponsored by an adult at baptism.  The adult, the godfather, takes responsibility for the child's religious education.|He was godfather to several of his friends' children, and he took his role as a godson seriously. 他是他几个朋友孩子的教父，而且他认真对待自己作为教父的角色。|Synonyms: godchild (if gender-neutral is needed)|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
