favoring:/ˈfeɪvərɪŋ/ | adj. | 有利的；偏袒的；赞同的 | Showing or indicating preference or partiality; conducive to.  | The current economic climate is favoring investors. 当前的经济环境对投资者有利。 |  The judge's decision was favoring the defendant. 法官的判决偏袒被告。 | 近义词：advantageous, beneficial, preferential, biased | 反义词：unfavorable, detrimental, impartial | 词性: adjective

favoring:/ˈfeɪvərɪŋ/ | v. | 偏爱；偏袒；有利于 (present participle of favor) | To show preference or partiality to; to be conducive to. |  Warm weather is favoring the growth of the plants.  温暖的天气有利于植物生长。 | He's favoring his left leg because of the injury. 因为他腿受伤了，所以他偏向用左腿。 | 近义词：supporting, promoting, aiding | 反义词：opposing, hindering, impeding | 词性: verb
