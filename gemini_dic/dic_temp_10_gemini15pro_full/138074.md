normalization:/ˌnɔːrmələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 正常化，标准化 |The process of bringing or returning something to a normal condition or state.  It often involves establishing a standard or norm. |Data normalization is crucial for accurate analysis. 数据规范化对于精确分析至关重要。| The normalization of relations between the two countries is a positive development. 两国关系正常化是一个积极的进展。| standardization, regularization | deviation, irregularity | noun
