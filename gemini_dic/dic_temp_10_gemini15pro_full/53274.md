dirigible:/ˈdɪrɪdʒəbəl/ /ˈdɪrɪdʒɪbəl/| n. |飞艇|A lighter-than-air aircraft that can be steered, typically a large cigar-shaped balloon with propellers.|The dirigible floated gracefully across the sky. 飞艇优雅地飘过天空。|Synonym: airship, blimp|Antonym: airplane, helicopter|noun
dirigible:/ˈdɪrɪdʒəbəl/ /ˈdɪrɪdʒɪbəl/| adj. |可操纵的|Capable of being steered or directed.|The dirigible balloon was a marvel of engineering.  可操纵的气球是工程学的奇迹。|Synonym: steerable, controllable|Antonym: unsteerable, uncontrollable|adjective
