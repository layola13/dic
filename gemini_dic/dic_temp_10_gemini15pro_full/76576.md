gallically:/ɡəˈlɪkəli/
|adv.|
|像高卢人一样；傲慢地；尖刻地|
|In a manner characteristic of the ancient Gauls, or more generally, in a bold, defiant, or spirited manner; sometimes implying a sharp or caustic wit.|
|He responded gallically, with a shrug and a witty retort.|他以高卢人的方式回应，耸耸肩并机智地反驳。

|She gallically dismissed his criticisms with a wave of her hand.|她傲慢地挥了挥手，驳回了他的批评。

|近义词：boldly, defiantly, spiritedly, sharply, caustically|
|反义词：submissively, meekly, timidly, politely|
|词性: 副词|
