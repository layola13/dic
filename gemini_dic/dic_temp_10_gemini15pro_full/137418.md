nontesting:/ˌnɒnˈtɛstɪŋ/ | adj. | 非测试的，不进行测试的 | Not involving or relating to testing. |  The company adopted a nontesting approach to software development, relying on rigorous code reviews instead.  公司采用了一种非测试的软件开发方法，依靠严格的代码审查来代替测试。 | Example: They have a nontesting policy for pregnant women. 他们对孕妇有不进行测试的政策。 | Synonyms: test-free, examination-free | Antonyms: testing, examinational | adjective
