blueprinter:/ˈbluːˌprɪntər/| n.|蓝图绘制者；设计者；规划者|A person who makes blueprints or plans, especially of buildings or machines.  Someone who creates detailed plans or designs for something.|The architect hired a skilled blueprinter to prepare the final drawings for the new skyscraper.  这位建筑师聘请了一位熟练的蓝图绘制员来绘制这座新摩天大楼的最终图纸。|近义词：designer, planner, architect, draftsman|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
