all-rage:/ɔːl-reɪdʒ/
adjective
风靡一时；盛怒的；非常流行的
extremely popular; very angry
The all-rage fashion trend is neon colors.  荧光色是风靡一时的时尚潮流。
He was all-rage when he found his car had been vandalized. 当他发现他的车被破坏了，他怒不可遏。
近义词:popular, trendy, fashionable, furious, enraged
反义词:unpopular, outdated, calm, peaceful
词性:adjective

词源: "all" + "rage".  "All" intensifies the meaning of "rage", either in the sense of extreme popularity (like a craze) or extreme anger. “all”增强了“rage”的含义，既可以指极度流行（像一股狂潮），也可以指极度愤怒。
