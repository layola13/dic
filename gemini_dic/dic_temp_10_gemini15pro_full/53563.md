disbelief's:/ˌdɪsbɪˈliːfs/
noun
不信；怀疑
The state of not believing something; lack of belief.
She stared at him in disbelief. 她难以置信地盯着他。
近义词: incredulity, skepticism, doubt
反义词: belief, faith, trust
词性: 名词

词源:  From dis- (expressing negation) + belief.
