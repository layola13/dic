do justice:/duː ˈdʒʌstɪs/ |v. | 公平对待；妥善处理；充分展现…的优点；使…获得应有的评价 | to treat someone or something in a way that is fair and shows their or its true qualities | The photos don't do justice to the magnificent scenery.  这些照片未能充分展现壮丽的景色。 | synonyms: represent fairly, portray accurately, give a good impression of | antonyms: misrepresent, distort, undersell | verb phrase

do justice:/duː ˈdʒʌstɪs/ |v. | 充分发挥；尽力做好；不辜负 | to perform or deal with something as well as possible |  She didn't do herself justice in the exam. 她考试没有发挥出自己的水平。 | synonyms: perform well, excel, live up to expectations | antonyms: underperform, fail, disappoint | verb phrase
