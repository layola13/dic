lieutenant:/luːˈtɛnənt/ /ljuːˈtɛnənt/| n.|中尉；副官|A lieutenant is a commissioned officer in the armed forces, ranking below a captain.  A lieutenant is also an officer who acts as a deputy or assistant to a more senior officer.| The lieutenant gave the order to advance. 中尉下令前进。|Synonyms: deputy, assistant, second-in-command|Antonyms: commander, chief, leader|noun

lieutenant:/luːˈtɛnənt/ /ljuːˈtɛnənt/| n.|代理人；副手|A lieutenant can also be someone who acts as a deputy or assistant in a civilian organization. |She served as the president's lieutenant during the campaign. 在竞选期间，她担任总统的副手。|Synonyms: representative, agent, aide|Antonyms: principal, head, superior|noun
