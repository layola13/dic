inoculator:/ɪˈnɑːkjəleɪtər/ | n. | 接种器；接种者 | A device or person used to introduce a substance, such as a vaccine or a culture of microorganisms, into something else. | The scientist used an inoculator to introduce the bacteria into the petri dish. 科学家使用接种器将细菌引入培养皿。 |  The nurse acted as the inoculator for the flu vaccine.  护士担任了流感疫苗的接种员。| Synonyms: vaccinator, inoculant, introducer | Antonyms: None | Noun

inoculate:/ɪˈnɑːkjəleɪt/ | v. | 接种；灌输 | To introduce a substance, such as a vaccine or a culture of microorganisms, into something else. Also, to introduce an idea or attitude into someone's mind. | The doctor inoculated the children against measles. 医生给孩子们接种了麻疹疫苗。 |  He tried to inoculate his students with a love of learning. 他试图向他的学生灌输对学习的热爱。 | Synonyms: vaccinate, immunize, inject, implant, infuse | Antonyms: infect, contaminate | Verb
