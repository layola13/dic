detonate:/ˈdɛtəneɪt/ (US), /ˈdɛtəneɪt/ (UK)| v. |引爆，爆炸|To cause (something, such as a bomb) to explode.|The terrorists detonated the bomb in a crowded marketplace. 恐怖分子在拥挤的市场引爆了炸弹。|explode, blow up|defuse, disarm|verb
detonate:/ˈdɛtəneɪt/ (US), /ˈdɛtəneɪt/ (UK)| n. |爆炸; 引爆|The act of exploding or causing something to explode.|The detonation of the bomb destroyed the building. 炸弹的爆炸摧毁了建筑物。|explosion, blast|suppression, prevention|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dētonātus*, past participle of *dētonāre*  "to thunder down," from *dē-* "down, completely" + *tonāre* "to thunder."
