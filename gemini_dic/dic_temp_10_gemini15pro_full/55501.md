distrainable:/dɪˈstreɪnəbl/| adj.|可扣押的，可 distraint 的|Subject to distraint, especially for the payment of rent or taxes.  Able to be legally seized to satisfy a debt.|例句：The landlord confirmed that the tenant's goods were distrainable for unpaid rent. 房东确认租户的物品可以因未付租金而被扣押。

例句：His property was distrainable due to outstanding tax debts. 他的财产由于未缴税款而可被扣押。|近义词：seizable, attachable, forfeitable|反义词:exempt|词性:adjective
