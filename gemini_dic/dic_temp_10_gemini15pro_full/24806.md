broths:/brɒθs/, /brɑːθs/
|n.| 肉汤，鱼汤 |Thin, watery soups, typically made by simmering meat or vegetables.|  She made a nourishing chicken broth for the invalid. 她为病人做了一份营养丰富的鸡汤。|
|近义词: stocks, bouillon, consommé |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "thick soup") | 词性: 名词
