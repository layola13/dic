denuders:/dɪˈnjuːdərz/| n. |脱除器，剥离器|A device or substance that removes a coating or layer from something, especially gaseous pollutants from air.  It often refers to a device used in atmospheric chemistry research to remove specific gases from an air stream before analysis.|The scientists used denuders to measure the concentration of ammonia in the air. 科学家们使用脱除器来测量空气中氨的浓度。|Synonyms: stripper, scrubber, cleaner |Antonyms: coater, applicator|noun
