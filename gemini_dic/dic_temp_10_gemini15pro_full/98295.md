insider:/ˈɪnˌsaɪdər/| n.|内部人士；知情人；圈内人|A person who has special knowledge or access to confidential information within a group or organization. |例句：The insider leaked crucial information to the press.  这位内部人士向媒体泄露了关键信息。

例句：Company insiders are buying up stock. 公司内部人士正在大量购入股票。

近义词：expert, authority, confidant|反义词:outsider, stranger|词性:noun
