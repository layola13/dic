militations:/ˌmɪlɪˈteɪʃənz/
| n. | 影响；不利因素；反对 | factors or influences that work against or hinder something; objections or arguments against something |  His poor health militated against his chances of success.  他糟糕的健康状况影响了他成功的机会。|近义词：hindrances, obstacles, objections, drawbacks, disadvantages |反义词:advantages, benefits, assets | noun

militates:/ˈmɪlɪteɪts/
| v. | 产生不利影响；阻碍；反对；抵触 | (of a fact or circumstance) have a forceful effect; weigh heavily (often followed by *against*); to operate against; work against | His poor health militates against his career advancement. 他糟糕的健康状况阻碍了他的职业发展。 | 近义词：works against, counts against, hinders, obstructs, opposes | 反义词:supports, promotes, aids, assists | verb
