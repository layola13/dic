nonignominiousness:/ˌnɒnɪɡnəˈmɪniəsnəs/
noun
非耻辱，不丢脸，体面
The state or quality of not being ignominious; respectability.
His nonignominiousness was evident in his upright posture and confident demeanor. 他的正直姿态和自信的风度表明了他并非不光彩。
Synonyms: respectability, honorableness, creditableness
Antonyms: ignominy, shame, disgrace
词性: 名词
