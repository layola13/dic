nonspherical:/ˌnɒnˈsfɛrɪkəl/ /ˌnɑːnˈsfɪrɪkəl/
adjective
非球形的
Not having the shape of a sphere; not spherical.
The Earth is nonspherical, bulging slightly at the equator.  地球不是球形的，在赤道略微凸起。
Red blood cells have a nonspherical, biconcave disc shape. 红细胞具有非球形的双凹圆盘形状。
近义词:aspherical
反义词:spherical
词性:adjective
