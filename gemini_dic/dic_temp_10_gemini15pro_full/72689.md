focal:/ˈfoʊkl/| adj.| 焦点的，重要的，中心的|Relating to the focus, or point at which rays of light or energy meet, often used figuratively to describe something central or crucial. |The focal point of the discussion was the budget. 讨论的焦点是预算。|synonyms: central, crucial, key, main, primary|antonyms: peripheral, unimportant, minor, secondary|adjective

focal:/ˈfoʊkl/| n.| 焦点，中心点|(Less common as a noun) The focus or center of activity or interest.|The town square is the focal of community life. 城镇广场是社区生活的中心。|synonyms: center, core, heart, hub|antonyms: periphery, edge, margin|noun
