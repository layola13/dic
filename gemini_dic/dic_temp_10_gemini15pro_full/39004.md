confirmor:/kənˈfɜːrmər/ | n. | 确认者；证实者 | Someone who confirms something; a person who attests to the truth or validity of something. | The lawyer acted as a confirmor for the witness's testimony. 律师充当了证人证词的确认者。 |  The bank acted as confirmor for the letter of credit. 银行充当了信用证的确认者。|  Synonym:  verifier, attester, corroborator | Antonym: denier, refuter | noun
