interlocation:/ˌɪntərloʊˈkeɪʃən/| n.|相互作用，相互联系，相互位置|The interlocation of the various parts of the machine is crucial for its proper functioning.  这台机器各个部件的相互位置对其正常运转至关重要。|例句：

The interlocation of economic and social factors is complex. 经济和社会因素的相互作用是复杂的。

The architect considered the interlocation of the buildings on the site. 建筑师考虑了场地内建筑物之间的相互位置。

|近义词：interaction, interconnection, interplay|反义词:isolation, separation|词性:名词
