agglomerate: /əˈɡlɒməreɪt/ (US) /əˈɡlɒməreɪt/ (UK)| n. v. adj.| 聚集体；凝聚；聚集；成团的| (n.) A mass or collection of things gathered together. (v.) To gather into a mass or ball; to collect. (adj.) Collected into a mass. |The rock was an agglomerate of various minerals. 这块岩石是由各种矿物聚集而成的。  The company agglomerated several smaller businesses into a single entity.  该公司将几家小企业合并成一个实体。 The agglomerate mass of snow eventually formed an avalanche. 堆积的雪最终形成了雪崩。|近义词：aggregate, amass, conglomerate, accumulate|反义词：separate, disperse, scatter|词性: noun, verb, adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *agglomeratus*, past participle of *agglomerare* "to wind into a ball," from *ad* "to" + *glomerare* "to wind into a ball," from *glomus* "ball."
