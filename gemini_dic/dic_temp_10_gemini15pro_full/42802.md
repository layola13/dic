coupon:/ˈkuːpɒn/ (US) /ˈkuːpɒn/ (UK)| n.|优惠券，礼券，息票|A voucher entitling the holder to a discount for a particular product.|I have a coupon for 10% off my next purchase. 我有一张下次购买可享九折的优惠券。|Synonym: voucher, ticket, discount|Antonym: surcharge, extra charge|noun

coupon:/ˈkuːpɒn/ (US) /ˈkuːpɒn/ (UK)| n.|配给券，定量供应券|A ticket or document that can be exchanged for goods, especially during rationing.|During the war, families were given coupons for essential food items. 战争期间，每个家庭都配给基本食物的配给券。|Synonym: ration ticket, token|Antonym: unlimited supply|noun
