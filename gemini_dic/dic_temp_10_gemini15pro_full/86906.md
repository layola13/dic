hemline:/ˈhemlaɪn/| n.|裙摆；底边|The hemline of her dress was uneven. 她的裙摆 uneven. |例句：The hemline of her skirt rose a few inches above her knees. 她的裙子下摆高出膝盖几英寸。

Her hemline was just below the knee. 她的裙摆刚好在膝盖以下。|近义词：bottom edge, lower edge|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps neckline if contrasting parts of a garment)|词性:noun
