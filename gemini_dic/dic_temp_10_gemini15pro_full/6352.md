amphibian:/æmˈfɪbiən/ | n. adj. |两栖动物，两栖的|An animal, such as a frog, that lives both on land and in water.  Relating to or characteristic of amphibians. |例句：Frogs are amphibians. 青蛙是两栖动物。|例句：Amphibian vehicles can travel on both land and water. 水陆两用车可以在陆地和水上行驶。|近义词：frog, toad, newt, salamander (n.); amphibious (adj.) |反义词: terrestrial, aquatic |词性: noun, adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek amphibios, from amphi- "both" + bios "life"
