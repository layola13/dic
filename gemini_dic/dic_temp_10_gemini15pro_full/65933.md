exacerbation:/ɪɡˌzæsərˈbeɪʃən/ /ɪkˈsæsərˌbeɪʃən/ | n. | 加剧，恶化 | An increase in the severity of a disease or its signs and symptoms. |  The humid weather exacerbated her asthma. 潮湿的天气加剧了她的哮喘。

The stress of the situation only exacerbated his anger.  这种紧张的局势只会加剧他的愤怒。


近义词: aggravation, worsening, intensification | 反义词: improvement, amelioration, alleviation | 词性: noun

exacerbate: /ɪɡˈzæsərˌbeɪt/ /ɪkˈsæsərˌbeɪt/ | vt. | 使恶化，加剧 | To make (a problem, bad situation, or negative feeling) worse. | The lack of rain exacerbated the already serious drought conditions.  缺少降雨加剧了本已严重的干旱状况。

His insensitive comments only exacerbated the tension in the room. 他不敏感的评论只会加剧房间里的紧张气氛。


近义词: worsen, aggravate, intensify | 反义词: improve, alleviate, ameliorate | 词性: verb
