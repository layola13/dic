parabling:/ˈpærəblɪŋ/
n.
比喻; 寓言化;  用寓言表达
The act of expressing something using parables.  The process of forming or constructing a parable.
He used parabling to explain the complex concept to the children. 他用比喻的方式向孩子们解释这个复杂的概念。
The preacher's sermon was full of parabling. 牧师的布道充满了寓言。
Near Synonyms: allegorizing, metaphorizing, symbolizing
Antonyms: literalizing, explaining directly
Word type: Noun
