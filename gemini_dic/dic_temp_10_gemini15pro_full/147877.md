overreacting:/ˌoʊvərriˈæktɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
反应过度
reacting too strongly or emotionally to something
例句：
He was overreacting to the news. 他对这个消息反应过度了。

She tends to overreact in stressful situations.  她在压力大的情况下容易反应过度。

近义词：overdramatizing, exaggerating, making a fuss, making a mountain out of a molehill
反义词：underreacting, downplaying
词性：动词的现在分词
