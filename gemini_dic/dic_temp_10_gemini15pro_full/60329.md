egoist: /ˈiːɡəʊɪst/ (UK) /ˈiːɡoʊɪst/ (US) | n. | 利己主义者；自私自利的人 | A person who is excessively self-centered and concerned primarily with their own interests, pleasures, or welfare, regardless of others. |  He was an egoist, caring little for the feelings of others. 他是一个利己主义者，很少关心别人的感受。| synonyms: narcissist, self-seeker, egomaniac | antonyms: altruist, philanthropist | noun

egoistic: /ˌiːɡəʊˈɪstɪk/ (UK) /ˌiːɡoʊˈɪstɪk/ (US)| adj. | 利己的；自私自利的 |  Characterized by egoism; self-centered. | His egoistic behavior alienated his friends. 他自私的行为疏远了他的朋友。| synonyms: selfish, self-centered, narcissistic | antonyms: altruistic, selfless | adjective

egoistically:  /ˌiːɡəʊˈɪstɪkəli/ (UK) /ˌiːɡoʊˈɪstɪkəli/ (US) | adv. | 自私地；利己地 | In a self-centered or egoistic manner. | He acted egoistically, putting his own needs above everyone else's. 他自私地行事，把自己的需求置于他人之上。| synonyms: selfishly, self-centeredly | antonyms: altruistically, selflessly | adverb
