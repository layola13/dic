historys:/ˈhɪstəriz/ | n. | 历史（通常用history，historys是非标准用法）|  A non-standard plural form of "history," referring to multiple historical accounts or narratives.  History itself encompasses past events, especially those relating to human affairs, and the study of those events. While "histories" might be used informally to suggest different versions or interpretations of the past, "history" is the generally accepted singular and plural form. |  "He's been reading up on the histories of different cultures."
