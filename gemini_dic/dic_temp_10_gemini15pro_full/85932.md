headlining:/ˈhedˌlaɪnɪŋ/
adjective
作为头条新闻的；担任主角的
acting as or having the most important or prominent part in a performance, event, etc.
The band is headlining at the festival this weekend.  这个乐队本周末将在音乐节上担任主角。
近义词: starring, featuring, top-billed
反义词: supporting, minor
词性: adjective

headlining:/ˈhedˌlaɪnɪŋ/
noun
头条新闻；主角地位
the act of being the main performer or attraction
Her headlining of the concert was a major coup for the organizers. 她担任音乐会的主角对组织者来说是一大成功。
近义词: star billing, top spot
反义词: supporting role
词性: noun
