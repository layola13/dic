draughtboards:/ˈdrɑːftbɔːrdz/ /ˈdræftbɔːrdz/
noun
西洋跳棋盘; 国际跳棋盘
A draughtboard is a checkered gameboard used for playing draughts (checkers).
The draughtboard was set up, ready for a game. 西洋跳棋盘已经摆好，准备开始游戏。
They spent hours hunched over the draughtboard, lost in their game. 他们几个小时都弓着身子趴在棋盘上，沉浸在游戏中。
synonyms: checkerboard
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "blank board" or "empty table")
词性: noun
