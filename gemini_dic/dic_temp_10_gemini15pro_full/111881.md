logographic:/ˌlɒɡəˈɡræfɪk/ / ˌlɑːɡəˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. |  表意的；使用语标文字的 |  Relating to a writing system in which each symbol represents a word or morpheme (meaningful unit of language).  A logographic system uses characters that represent words or concepts directly, rather than representing sounds like an alphabet. |  Chinese is a logographic language, with thousands of characters representing different words and concepts.  汉语是一种语标文字，有数千个汉字代表不同的词语和概念。

| 近义词:  ideographic | 反义词: alphabetic, phonetic | 词性: adjective
