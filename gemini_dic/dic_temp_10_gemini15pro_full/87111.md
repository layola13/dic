heraud:/ˈhɛrɔːd/ | n. | 传令官，通报者 | A herald is an official messenger, especially one representing a monarch or a state.  A herald can also be something that foreshadows or announces something else. | The king's herald announced the royal decree. 国王的传令官宣布了皇家法令。

The robin is a herald of spring. 知更鸟是春天的使者。 |  messenger, announcer, precursor, harbinger |  None | noun
