dimerous:/ˈdaɪmərəs/
adjective
二聚的，二数的
Having two parts; specifically (Botany) having two petals or two parts in each whorl of the perianth.  A dimerous flower has only two petals, two sepals, two stamens, etc.
The small, dimerous flowers of this plant are often overlooked. 这种植物的二数小花常被忽略。
近义词: two-parted, diplostemonous (specifically referring to stamens)
反义词: polymerous, trimerous, tetramerous, etc.
词性: adjective
