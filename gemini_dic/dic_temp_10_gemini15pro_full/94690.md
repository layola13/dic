improbable:/ɪmˈprɒbəbl/ (US) /ɪmˈprɑːbəbl/| adj.|不可能的，未必发生的|Not likely to be true or to happen.|It's improbable that she'll arrive on time given the traffic.  鉴于交通状况，她不太可能准时到达。

It is improbable that he will win the lottery. 他不太可能中彩票。|近义词：unlikely, doubtful, dubious|反义词：probable, likely, possible|词性:形容词
