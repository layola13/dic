amniotes:/ˈæmniəʊts/| n.|羊膜动物|Animals that develop an amnion during embryonic development, including reptiles, birds, and mammals.  These animals are characterized by having eggs that are equipped with an amnion, a membrane that forms a fluid-filled sac around the embryo, providing protection and allowing development outside of water.|Birds are amniotes that evolved from dinosaurs. 鸟类是由恐龙进化而来的羊膜动物。|Reptiles, birds, and mammals are all amniotes. 爬行动物、鸟类和哺乳动物都是羊膜动物。|synonyms: amniota|antonyms: anamniotes|noun
