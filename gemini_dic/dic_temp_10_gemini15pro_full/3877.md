agrarianize:/əˈɡrɛəriəˌnaɪz/
verb
使农业化；耕作；把…分成耕地
To divide into small farms or holdings; to give (land) over to agricultural uses; to convert (a population) to agricultural pursuits.
The government planned to agrarianize the vast estates and distribute the land among the peasants. 政府计划将广阔的庄园农业化，并将土地分配给农民。
The formerly nomadic tribe gradually agrarianized, settling down to farm the fertile valley.  以前游牧的部落逐渐农业化，定居下来在肥沃的山谷耕作。
synonyms: cultivate, farm, till, husband, ruralize
antonyms: industrialize, urbanize
词性: 动词
