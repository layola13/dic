overdepresses:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈprɛsɪz/
verb
过度压抑；过分抑制
To depress excessively or too much; to cause someone to feel excessively sad or hopeless.
The constant negativity overdepresses her and makes it hard for her to function.  不断的负面情绪让她过度压抑，难以正常生活。
His constant criticism overdepresses me. 他不断的批评让我感到非常沮丧。
Synonyms: deject, demoralize, dispirit
Antonyms: encourage, uplift, inspire
词性: 动词
