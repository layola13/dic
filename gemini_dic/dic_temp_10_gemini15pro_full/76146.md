fusel:/ˈfjuːzl/ | n. | 杂醇油 | A colorless oily liquid with a disagreeable odor and taste, formed as a byproduct in the fermentation of alcoholic beverages.  It consists mainly of amyl alcohols. | The cheap whiskey had a strong fusel taste.  这廉价威士忌有很重的杂醇味。 | synonyms: bad liquor, rotgut | antonyms: fine liquor, pure alcohol | noun
