adorative:/əˈdɔːrətɪv/
adjective
崇拜的；敬慕的
Characterized by or expressing adoration; worshipful.
She gazed at him with adorative eyes. 她用崇拜的眼神注视着他。
The puppy gave its owner an adorative look. 小狗用崇拜的眼神看着它的主人。

synonyms: worshipping, adoring, admiring, reverent, devoted, affectionate, loving
antonyms: critical, disapproving, scornful, contemptuous
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adorativus, from Latin adorare (“to adore”) + -ivus. Equivalent to adore + -ative.
