maintained:/meɪnˈteɪnd/ | v. | 保持，维持，维修，坚持，断言 | kept in good condition; continued in or at a specified state, level, or rate; caused or enabled (something) to continue; provided with necessities for life or existence; stated as a fact; asserted. | The company has maintained a steady growth rate.  这家公司保持了稳定的增长率。| 近义词：preserve, sustain, uphold, assert, contend | 反义词：neglect, abandon, discontinue, deny | 词性: 动词

词源: From Old French maintenir, from Latin manu tenere "to hold in the hand."
