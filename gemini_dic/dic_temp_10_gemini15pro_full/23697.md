brasilein: /brəˈzɪliɪn/ | n. | 巴西木素 | A crystalline red dye obtained from brazilwood. |  Brazilin is oxidized to brasilein, the actual dyeing compound. 巴西木素被氧化成巴西红素，即实际的染色化合物。

The deep red color of the fabric comes from brasilein. 这种织物的深红色来自巴西红素。| 近义词: brazilein (Note: This is effectively the same word, differing only in minor spelling variations.), brazilin (its reduced form) | 反义词: None (It's a specific chemical compound, not a concept with an opposite.) | 词性: noun
