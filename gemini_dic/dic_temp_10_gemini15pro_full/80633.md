goosewing:/ˈɡuːswɪŋ/| n.|鹅翼，鹅翼帆，(指帆)顺风航行|A goosewing is a fore-and-aft sail set on a boom extending forward of the mast. It was often used on small boats. |The small boat set its goosewing and sailed downwind. 小船张开鹅翼帆顺风航行。|近义词：sail, spinnaker|反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑与逆风航行的帆相对，例如"close-hauled sail")|noun
