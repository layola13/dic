earth-shatteringly:/ˈɜːrθˌʃætərɪŋli/ /ˈɝːθˌʃætərɪŋli/
adverb
惊天动地地；极其重要地
in a way that is earth-shattering; extremely
The news was earth-shatteringly important.  这个消息惊天动地般重要。
The performance was earth-shatteringly bad. 表演糟糕透顶。
Synonyms:  momentously, profoundly, significantly, dramatically, remarkably
Antonyms: insignificantly, trivially, mildly, slightly
词性: 副词
