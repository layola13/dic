enrollment:/ɪnˈrəʊlmənt/ (British) /ɪnˈroʊlmənt/ (American)| n. |登记；注册；入学；入会|The act of enrolling or being enrolled.|Example: Enrollment at the university has increased this year. 今年大学的入学人数增加了。|Example:  The school saw a significant drop in enrollment after the tuition hike. 学费上涨后，学校的入学人数显著下降。|Synonyms: registration, admission, enlistment|Antonyms: withdrawal, disenrollment|noun
