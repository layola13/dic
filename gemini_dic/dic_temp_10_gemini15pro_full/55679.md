ditto:/ˈdɪtoʊ/ (US) /ˈdɪtəʊ/ (UK)| adv. n. adj.|同上，同前，复制|Used to avoid repeating something already written or said. |The figures for 1993 are given below; ditto those for 1994. 1993年的数字如下所示；1994年的数字同上。|Synonyms: same, likewise, similarly, identically|Antonyms: different, opposite, unlike|adverb

ditto:/ˈdɪtoʊ/ (US) /ˈdɪtəʊ/ (UK)|n.|同上，复制品|A thing or remark that is the same as one already mentioned.|If you order the same dish as your companion, you can say "ditto" to the waiter. 如果你点的菜和你同伴一样，你可以对服务员说“同上”。|Synonyms: copy, replica, duplicate|Antonyms: original, unique|noun

ditto:/ˈdɪtoʊ/ (US) /ˈdɪtəʊ/ (UK)|adj.|相同的，同样的|Being the same as what has just been said.|"Ditto" is less commonly used as an adjective but can still be found in informal contexts. "Ditto"作为形容词不太常用，但在非正式语境中仍然可以找到。|Synonyms: identical, alike, similar|Antonyms: different, dissimilar, unlike|adjective
