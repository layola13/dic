counter-spell:/ˈkaʊntərˌspɛl/ (US), /ˈkaʊntəˌspɛl/ (UK)| v. |反咒语；对抗魔法；消除魔法|To cast a spell that negates or opposes another spell.|She counter-spelled the curse before it could take hold.  在她被诅咒控制之前，她对诅咒施了反咒语。|Synonyms: counteract, neutralize, nullify|Antonyms: spell, charm, enchant|Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- + spell.  Counter- is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposite." Spell refers to a magical incantation or formula.
