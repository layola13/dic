miscellanist:/ˌmɪsəˈleɪnɪst/
n.
杂集作者；杂文家；博学家
a writer of miscellanies; a person with wide-ranging knowledge or interests
Johnson considered himself a miscellanist, contributing to various periodicals on a wide range of topics. 约翰逊自认为是一位杂文家，为各种期刊撰写了广泛主题的文章。
polymath, scholar, writer, essayist
specialist
noun
