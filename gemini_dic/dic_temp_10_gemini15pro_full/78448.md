geten: /ˈɡɛtən/ (Archaic) | v. | 得到，获得 | An archaic past participle of "get". | He hath geten himself a new coat. (He has gotten himself a new coat.) | 近义词: gotten, obtained, acquired | 反义词: lost, forfeited | 词性: verb (past participle)


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I get up early every day. = 我每天早起。
She gets good grades in school. = 她在学校成绩很好。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I am getting ready for work. = 我正在准备上班。
He is getting tired of waiting. = 他等累了。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have gotten a new car. = 我买了一辆新车。
She has gotten used to the weather. = 她已经习惯了这里的天气。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been getting better at playing the piano. = 我一直在练习弹钢琴，进步了。
They have been getting complaints about the noise. = 他们一直收到关于噪音的投诉。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I got a promotion last year. = 我去年升职了。
She got home late last night. = 她昨晚很晚才回家。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was getting ready to leave when the phone rang. = 我正准备离开时电话响了。
He was getting angry with me. = 他正在生我的气。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had gotten a headache before the movie started. = 电影开始前我就头疼了。
She had gotten lost on her way to the party. = 她在去派对的路上迷路了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

虚拟语气:
If I got a raise, I would buy a new car. = 如果我加薪了，我会买一辆新车。
I wish I got more sleep. = 我希望我能多睡点觉。

被动语态 (Note: "Get" is not commonly used in the passive voice in this sense of "obtain".  More natural would be "The book was obtained...")
The book was gotten from the library. = 这本书是从图书馆借来的。（不自然）
The information was gotten from a reliable source. = 信息是从可靠来源获得的。（不自然）


疑问句:

否定句:
