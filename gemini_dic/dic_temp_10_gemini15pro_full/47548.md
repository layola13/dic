deathward:/ˈdɛθwərd/| adv., adj.|向着死亡，死亡方向的|Moving or facing in the direction of death; relating to death or dying.|He felt himself drifting deathward. 他感到自己正走向死亡。|近义词：dying, moribund, expiring|反义词: living, alive|词性:副词，形容词
Etymology: From "death" + "-ward" (suffix indicating direction).  "Death" comes from Old English "dēaþ," and "-ward" comes from Old English "-weard."
