haberdashery:/ˈhæbərdæʃəri/
n.
男子服饰用品；缝纫用品；
Men's clothing and accessories; sewing notions.
He bought a new tie at the haberdashery. 他在男子服饰用品店买了一条新领带。
Synonyms: men's wear, notions, accessories
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, consider "women's wear" for a contrasting term)
noun

haberdasher:/ˈhæbərdæʃər/
n.
男子服饰用品商；
A dealer in men's clothing and accessories.
The haberdasher helped him choose a suitable hat.  这位男子服饰用品商帮他选了一顶合适的帽子。
Synonyms: outfitter, clothier, mercer
Antonyms: (No direct antonym)
noun
