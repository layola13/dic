lupuline:/ˈluːpjʊlɪn/ /ˈluːpjʊlaɪn/ | n. | 蛇麻素；啤酒花苦味酸 |A yellowish powder found in the female flowers of the hop plant (Humulus lupulus), containing the bitter principles and essential oils that give beer its characteristic flavor and aroma.|  The lupuline in this hop variety is particularly potent.  这种啤酒花品种的蛇麻素特别强劲。|  The brewer carefully measured the lupuline to achieve the desired bitterness.  酿酒师仔细测量蛇麻素以达到所需的苦味。| synonyms: hop resin, hop powder | antonyms: None | noun

lupulin:/ˈluːpjʊlɪn/ | adj. | 蛇麻素的；含蛇麻素的 | Relating to, containing, or resembling lupulin. |  The lupulin glands of the hop flower are responsible for its aroma. 啤酒花的蛇麻腺体决定了它的香味。 |  The beer had a distinctly lupulin aroma.  这种啤酒有一种明显的蛇麻香味。 | synonyms: hoppy | antonyms: None | adjective
