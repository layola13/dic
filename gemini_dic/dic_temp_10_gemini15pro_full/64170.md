equatable:/ˈiːkwətəbəl/ | adj. | 可相等的，可比较的 | Able to be equated or compared; capable of being treated as equivalent. |

例句：
The two values are equatable in this context.
在这语境下，这两个值是相等的。

The two issues are not equatable, as they have different underlying causes.
这两个问题不可相提并论，因为它们有不同的根本原因。


近义词：comparable, similar, equivalent |
反义词：incomparable, dissimilar, different |
词性：adjective |

equate:/ɪˈkweɪt/ | v. | 使相等，等同 | Consider one thing to be the same as or equivalent to another. |

例句：
You can't equate success with happiness.
你不能把成功等同于幸福。


He equated wealth with happiness.
他把财富等同于幸福。

近义词：compare, liken, associate |
反义词：differentiate, distinguish |
词性：verb |
