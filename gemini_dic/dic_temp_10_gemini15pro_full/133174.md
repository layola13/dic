nonevanescent:/ˌnɒnˌɛvəˈnɛsənt/
adjective
不消失的，持久的
not disappearing, lasting
The nonevanescent glow of the embers illuminated the campsite.  余烬不消失的光芒照亮了营地。
Her nonevanescent beauty captivated everyone in the room. 她那不消失的美丽迷住了房间里的每一个人。
近义词:permanent, enduring, persistent, abiding
反义词:evanescent, fleeting, transient, temporary
词性:adjective

词源:
"non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "evanescent" (from Latin "evanescere" - to vanish)
