annihilatory:/əˈnaɪəˌleɪtəri/
adjective
毁灭性的，歼灭性的
Causing destruction or complete obliteration.
The annihilatory power of the bomb was evident in the devastation it caused.  这颗炸弹的毁灭性力量在其造成的破坏中显而易见。
The dictator’s annihilatory policies led to the deaths of millions.  独裁者的毁灭性政策导致了数百万人的死亡。
近义词:destructive, devastating, ruinous
反义词:creative, constructive, beneficial
词性:adjective
词源:From Late Latin annihilatorius, from Latin annihilatus, past participle of annihilare (“to reduce to nothing”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + nihil (“nothing”).
