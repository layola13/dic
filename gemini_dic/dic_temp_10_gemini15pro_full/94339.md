impinge-on:/ɪmˈpɪndʒ ɑːn/
verb
侵犯，影响
to have a negative effect on something, especially by limiting it; to encroach on
The government's spending cuts will impinge on the education budget. 政府的削减开支将影响教育预算。
Their rights impinge on the rights of other citizens. 他们的权利侵犯了其他公民的权利.
Synonyms: encroach, infringe, intrude, impact
Antonyms: avoid, assist, help, support
