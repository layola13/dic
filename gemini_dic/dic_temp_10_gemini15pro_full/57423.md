drabble:/ˈdræbəl/| n. v.| 短篇小说（尤指100字左右的），涂鸦，草草写成|A short work of fiction, typically of exactly 100 words.  It can also refer to writing something quickly and carelessly.|He wrote a drabble for the online competition. 他为在线比赛写了一篇100字的小小说。|She just drabbled a few notes in her diary. 她只是在日记里草草地写了几句话。|近义词：short story, sketch, vignette, doodle, scribble|反义词：novel, epic|词性:名词, 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the UK game show "A Way with Words" where contestants had to write a story in exactly 100 words.  The word itself is likely a humorous formation, suggesting something small and insignificant, perhaps related to "dribble" or "drab."
