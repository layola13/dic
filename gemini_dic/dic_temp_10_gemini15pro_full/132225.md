nondecaying:/ˌnɒndɪˈkeɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 不衰减的，不朽的 | Not decaying; not diminishing or weakening over time.  | The nondecaying orbit of the planet ensures its stable trajectory.  这颗行星不衰减的轨道确保了它稳定的轨迹。| The monument stands as a nondecaying symbol of their love. 这座纪念碑象征着他们不朽的爱情。 | 近义词: enduring, persistent, everlasting, imperishable | 反义词: decaying, diminishing, fading, transient | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "decaying" (present participle of "decay").  "Decay" comes from the Old French "decair" meaning "to fall, decline, fail."
