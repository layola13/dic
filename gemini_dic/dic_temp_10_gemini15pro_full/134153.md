nonimpressionistic:/ˌnɒnɪmˈprɛʃənɪstɪk/
adjective
非印象派的
Not relating to or characteristic of impressionism, especially in art or literature.
Her style is stark and nonimpressionistic, focusing on clear lines and realistic detail. 她的风格是鲜明和非印象派的，专注于清晰的线条和现实的细节。
Near synonyms: realistic, naturalistic, objective, representational
Antonyms: impressionistic, subjective, abstract
词性: 形容词
