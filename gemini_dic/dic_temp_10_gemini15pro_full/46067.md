cyclometry:/saɪˈklɒmətri/ | n. | 圆周测量法，三角函数表|The branch of mathematics concerned with the trigonometric functions and their inverse functions, and their applications to geometry. | Cyclometry plays a crucial role in calculating angles and distances in surveying. 圆周测量法在测量中的角度和距离计算中起着至关重要的作用。| 近义词: trigonometry | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Modern Latin *cyclometria*, from Ancient Greek κύκλος (kúklos, “circle”) + μέτρον (métron, “measure”).
