greased:/ɡriːst/ /ɡriːzd/| adj.|涂了油的；油腻的|Covered with grease; oily or greasy.|The mechanic's hands were greased after working on the car. 技工修完车后，手上沾满了油污。|Synonyms: oiled, lubricated, greasy|Antonyms: clean, dry|Adjective

grease:/ɡriːs/ /ɡriːs/| n. |油脂；润滑油|A thick oily substance, especially one used to lubricate machinery.|He applied grease to the bearings. 他给轴承上了润滑油。|Synonyms: oil, lubricant, fat|Antonyms:  detergent, cleaner|Noun

grease:/ɡriːs/ /ɡriːz/| vt.|涂油；贿赂|To apply grease to; to bribe.|He greased the pan before pouring in the batter. 他在倒入面糊之前给平底锅涂了油。  He tried to grease the palm of the official to get the permit. 他试图贿赂官员以获得许可证。|Synonyms: lubricate, oil, bribe|Antonyms: clean|Verb
