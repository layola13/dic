dear:/dɪr/| adj. n.|亲爱的, 昂贵的|Beloved or valued; costly; expensive. |例句：My dear friend, how are you? 我亲爱的朋友，你好吗？

例句：The dress was dear, but I bought it anyway. 这条裙子很贵，但我还是买了。|近义词：beloved, precious, expensive, costly|反义词：cheap, inexpensive, unloved|词性:形容词, 名词

dear:/dɪr/| n.|亲爱的(人)|A beloved person; used as a term of endearment.|例句：He wrote a letter to his dear. 他给他的爱人写了一封信。

例句：She called her child "dear". 她叫她的孩子“亲爱的”。|近义词：beloved, darling, sweetheart|反义词：enemy, foe|词性:名词
