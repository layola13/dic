mumblers:/ˈmʌmblərz/ | n. |含糊不清说话的人；咕哝者|People who speak indistinctly, often quietly and without proper articulation.|The mumblers in the back of the class were hard to understand. 班级后面那些咕哝的人很难听懂。|synonyms: mutterers, murmurers, whisperers|antonyms: orators, speakers, articulators|noun
