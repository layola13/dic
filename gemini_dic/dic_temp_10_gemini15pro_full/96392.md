indiscriminating:/ˌɪndɪˈskrɪmɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
不加区分的；不加选择的；不加鉴别的
Showing or characterized by a lack of discrimination.
例句：She has indiscriminate taste in music. 她对音乐的品味不加选择。

例句：The indiscriminate bombing of the city caused many civilian casualties. 对该城市不加区分的轰炸造成许多平民伤亡。

近义词：unselective, undiscerning, uncritical, random
反义词：selective, discerning, critical, discriminating
词性: adjective
