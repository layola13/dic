blockheaded:/ˌblɒkˈhedɪd/ (US) /ˌblɑːkˈhedɪd/ (UK)| adj.|愚笨的，迟钝的，顽固的|Stupid, dull-witted, obstinate.  Characterized by a lack of intelligence or good judgment.|He made a blockheaded decision to invest all his money in a single stock. 他愚蠢地决定把所有的钱都投资在一支股票上。|Synonyms: dense, doltish, obtuse, thick-skulled, stupid|Antonyms: intelligent, bright, clever, sharp, astute|adjective

blockhead:/ˈblɒkhed/ (US) /ˈblɑːkhed/ (UK)| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A stupid person; a blockhead.|He's such a blockhead, he can't even tie his shoes. 他真是个笨蛋，连鞋带都系不好。|Synonyms: idiot, fool, dunce, nitwit|Antonyms: genius, intellectual, savant|noun
