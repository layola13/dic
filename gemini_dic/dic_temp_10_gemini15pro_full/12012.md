arthropoda:/ɑːrˈθrɒpədə/ | n. | 节肢动物门 | A phylum of invertebrate animals that includes insects, arachnids, crustaceans, and myriapods, characterized by jointed appendages and a segmented exoskeleton. | Insects, spiders, and crabs belong to the phylum Arthropoda. 昆虫、蜘蛛和螃蟹都属于节肢动物门。| synonyms:  invertebrate, arthropod | antonyms: vertebrate | noun
