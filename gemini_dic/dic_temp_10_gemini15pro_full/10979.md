arbitrations:/ˌɑːrbɪˈtreɪʃənz/
n.
仲裁；调停
The process by which a dispute is settled by a neutral third party.
The two companies agreed to settle their dispute through binding arbitration.  两家公司同意通过具有约束力的仲裁来解决他们的争端。
The arbitration hearing lasted for three days. 仲裁听证会持续了三天。
Synonym:mediation, adjudication, reconciliation
Antonym:litigation, lawsuit
noun
