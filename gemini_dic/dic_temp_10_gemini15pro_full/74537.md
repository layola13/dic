fraternizes:/ˈfrætərnaɪzɪz/
verb
交往; fraternize的第三人称单数
To associate or mingle with others in a friendly way.
The soldiers were warned not to fraternize with the enemy. 士兵们被警告不要与敌人 fraternize.
近义词: associates, mingles, socializes
反义词: shuns, avoids, isolates
词性: verb


fraternize:/ˈfrætərnaɪz/
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From French fraterniser, from fraternité ‘brotherhood’, from Latin fraternitas, from frater ‘brother’.
