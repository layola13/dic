amnestied:/æmˈnɛstiːd/
|v. |赦免的|Granted an official pardon for a past offense.|The rebels were amnestied after the war. 战后叛乱分子被赦免了。|Synonyms: pardoned, forgiven, excused, absolved|Antonyms: convicted, sentenced, punished|Verb

amnestied:/æmˈnɛstiːd/
|adj. |被赦免的|Having received an amnesty.|The amnestied prisoners were released. 被赦免的囚犯被释放了。|Synonyms: pardoned, forgiven, excused, absolved|Antonyms: convicted, sentenced, punished|Adjective
