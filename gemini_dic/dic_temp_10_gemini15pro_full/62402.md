endogamous:/ˌɛn.doʊˈɡæ.məs/| adj.|近亲结婚的，同族通婚的|Marrying only within the limits of a local community, clan, or tribe.|The community is traditionally endogamous, with marriages arranged within the group. 这个社群传统上是近亲结婚的，婚姻在群体内部安排。|例句：The isolated island community remained strictly endogamous for centuries.  这个与世隔绝的岛屿社区几个世纪以来一直严格实行族内通婚。

例句：Endogamous marriages are common in some cultures.  在某些文化中，族内通婚很常见。|近义词：inbred, intra-group|反义词：exogamous|词性：adjective
