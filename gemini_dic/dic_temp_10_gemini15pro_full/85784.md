hayrake:/ˈheɪreɪk/| n.|干草耙，搂草机|A farm implement with a long handle and a row of teeth or prongs, used for gathering hay or straw into rows.|The farmer used a hayrake to gather the hay into windrows.  农民用干草耙把干草搂成草垄。|近义词：tedder, windrower, rake|反义词:|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *hay* + *rake*.
