agapeically:/ˌæɡəˈpeɪɪkli/
adv.
博爱地，无私地
In a manner characterized by agape, selfless and unconditional love.
He served the community agapeically, never asking for anything in return. 他无私地服务社区，从不求回报。
近义词: selflessly, altruistically, charitably
反义词: selfishly, egoistically
