cocksurenesses:/ˌkɒkˈʃʊənɪsɪz/ /ˌkɑːkˈʃʊrnɪsɪz/
n.
自负，过于自信
The state of being excessively confident, often to the point of arrogance.
His cocksureness irritated many of his colleagues. 他的自负激怒了他的许多同事。
近义词：arrogance, overconfidence, presumptuousness, conceitedness
反义词：humility, modesty, diffidence
词性：名词
