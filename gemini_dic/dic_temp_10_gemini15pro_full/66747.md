exorcizing:/ˈɛksɔːrsaɪzɪŋ/
v.
驱魔；驱除（坏事）
the act of driving out an evil spirit from a person, place, or thing; getting rid of something bad.
The priest was exorcizing the demons from the possessed man.  神父正在为被附身的人驱魔。
They tried to exorcise the bad memories from their minds. 他们试图驱除脑海中的不愉快记忆。

近义词:purifying, cleansing, purging
反义词:conjuring, invoking
