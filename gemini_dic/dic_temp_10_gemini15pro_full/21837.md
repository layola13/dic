bodes:/boʊdz/| v.| 预示，预兆| This dark cloud bodes ill for our picnic plans. 这片乌云预示着我们的野餐计划不妙。| The sunny weather bodes well for the harvest. 晴朗的天气预示着丰收。|foretells, presages| portends, augurs| verb
Etymology->
From Middle English *boden*, from Old English *bodian* (“to announce, foretell, proclaim, predict”), from Proto-Germanic *baudijaną* (“to command, order, announce”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰewdʰ- (“to be aware, make aware”). Cognate with Old Saxon *bodian* (“to announce, preach”), Old High German *boten*, *boeten* (“to announce, preach”), Old Norse *boða* (“to announce, foretell, proclaim”), Gothic *𐌱𐍉𐌳𐌴𐌹𐌽* (bōdein, “to command”). Related to bid.
