internationalized:/ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəlaɪzd/
| adj. |国际化的|Made suitable for use in different countries and cultures; adapted or designed to be used worldwide.|The company has internationalized its website, offering versions in multiple languages.  该公司已将其网站国际化，提供多种语言版本。|近义词：globalized, worldwide, cosmopolitan|反义词:localized, regional|adjective

internationalize:/ˌɪntərˈnæʃənəlaɪz/
| vt. |使国际化|To make something suitable for use in different countries and cultures; to adapt or design something to be used worldwide.|The company plans to internationalize its operations by opening offices in several new countries.  该公司计划通过在几个新国家开设办事处来使其业务国际化。|近义词：globalize|反义词:localize|verb
