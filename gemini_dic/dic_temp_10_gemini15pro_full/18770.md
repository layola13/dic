berm:/bɜːrm/| n.|护堤，路肩，(尤指战壕边的)土堤|A narrow shelf, path, or ledge typically at the top or bottom of a slope; especially : a mound or wall of earth or other material.  A raised bank or path, especially at the side of a road or canal.|The cyclists swerved to avoid the berm.|近义词:bank, embankment, shoulder|反义词:ditch, trench|noun
