criticizingly:/ˈkrɪtɪsaɪzɪŋli/
adv.
批判地，批评地
in a criticizing manner; with criticism.
He looked at her criticizingly, noting every flaw in her outfit. 他批判性地打量着她，注意到她着装上的每一个瑕疵。
近义词: disapprovingly, censoriously, judgmentally
反义词: approvingly, supportively, commendably
词性: adverb

criticize:/ˈkrɪtɪsaɪz/
v.
批评，批判
indicate the faults of (someone or something) in a disapproving way.
She criticized his decision to leave the company. 她批评了他离开公司的决定。
近义词: censure, condemn, denounce
反义词: praise, commend, laud
词性: verb

critic:/ˈkrɪtɪk/
n.
批评家，评论家
a person who expresses an unfavorable opinion of something.
He is a well-known film critic. 他是一位著名的电影评论家。
近义词: reviewer, analyst, judge
反义词: supporter, admirer, fan
词性: noun

词源：From criticizing + -ly.  Ultimately from the Greek *kritikós* meaning "able to discern," from *krínein* "to separate, decide."
