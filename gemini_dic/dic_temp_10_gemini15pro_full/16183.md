bankrolls:/ˈbæŋkroʊlz/| n. |资金，赌本|A sum of money available for gambling or financing a project.  This refers to the funds set aside specifically for a particular venture, often implying a significant amount.  It suggests a dedicated pool of money, rather than just general funds.|He lost his entire bankroll at the casino. 他在赌场输光了所有的赌本。|Synonyms: stake, funds, capital, resources|Antonyms: debt, liabilities|noun


bankroll:/ˈbæŋkroʊl/| vt. |为…提供资金，资助|To finance or fund something, typically a project or venture.|A wealthy investor bankrolled the new startup. 一位富有的投资者资助了这家新创公司。|Synonyms: finance, fund, back, support, subsidize|Antonyms: defund, divest|verb
