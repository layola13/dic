glitzier:/ˈɡlɪtsiər/ | adj. | 更闪耀的，更华丽的 | More glitzy; more ostentatiously showy or elaborate. | The new hotel was glitzier than the old one. 新酒店比旧酒店更闪耀。 |  The awards ceremony was a glitzier affair this year. 今年的颁奖典礼更加华丽。 | 近义词：sparklier, flashier, showier, gaudier | 反义词：drabber, duller, simpler, plainer | 词性: 形容词

glitzy:/ˈɡlɪtsi/ | adj. | 闪耀的，华丽的 |  Ostentatiously showy or elaborate.  |  She wore a glitzy dress to the party. 她穿着一件闪耀的连衣裙去参加聚会。 | The casino had a glitzy atmosphere. 赌场的气氛很华丽。 | 近义词：sparkly, flashy, showy, gaudy | 反义词：drab, dull, simple, plain | 词性: 形容词
