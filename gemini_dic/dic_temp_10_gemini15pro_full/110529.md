limburger:/ˈlɪmbɜːrɡər/| n.|林堡干酪|A semisoft surface-ripened cheese with a strong, pungent odor, originally made in the historical Duchy of Limburg, now divided among Belgium, Germany, and the Netherlands.|The limburger cheese was so strong, it could be smelled from across the room. 林堡奶酪的味道太浓了，整个房间都能闻到。|Synonyms: cheese, strong cheese|Antonyms: mild cheese|noun

limburger:/ˈlɪmbɜːrɡər/| adj.|林堡干酪的|Relating to or characteristic of Limburger cheese.|He ordered a limburger sandwich. 他点了一份林堡奶酪三明治。|Synonyms: pungent, strong-smelling|Antonyms: mild, bland|adjective
