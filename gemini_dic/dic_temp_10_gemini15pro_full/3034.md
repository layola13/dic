aerobicized:/ɛˈroʊbɪsaɪzd/
verb
有氧化的，需氧的
Made or become aerobic; adapted to thrive in the presence of free oxygen.
The compost was thoroughly aerobicized to promote decomposition.  堆肥经过彻底的氧化处理以促进分解。
The scientist aerobicized the bacteria culture. 科学家使细菌培养物需氧。
近义词:oxygenated, aerated
反义词:anaerobic
词性:v.
