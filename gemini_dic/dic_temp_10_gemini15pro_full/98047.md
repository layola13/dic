一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you listening to? = 你在听什么？
What is he working on right now? = 他现在正在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she been up to lately? = 她最近在忙些什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been reading recently? = 你最近一直在读什么？
What has he been complaining about? = 他一直在抱怨什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last night? = 你昨晚做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 聚会上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加聚会时穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

What had she eaten before she got sick? = 她生病之前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
