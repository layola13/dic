overdiversified: /ˌoʊvərdaɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 过度多元化的 |  Having too much diversity, especially in investments, to the point where it becomes detrimental rather than beneficial.  It implies spreading resources too thinly across a wide range of assets, increasing complexity and potentially reducing returns. |  His portfolio was overdiversified, diluting the impact of his best investments. 他的投资组合过于分散，削弱了他最佳投资的影响。| synonyms: excessively diversified, overspread | antonyms: concentrated, focused, specialized | adjective
