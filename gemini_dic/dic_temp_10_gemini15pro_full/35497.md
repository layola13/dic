coadjust:/ˌkoʊəˈdʒʌst/
verb
共同调整，协调
to adjust or regulate (something) in relation to something else.
例句：The gears need to be coadjusted for the machine to work properly.  机器正常工作需要齿轮相互协调。

例句：The two departments coadjusted their plans to avoid overlapping responsibilities.  两个部门协调了各自的计划，以避免职责重叠。

近义词：coordinate, harmonize, reconcile, integrate
反义词：misalign, disorganize
词性：verb

coadjustment:/ˌkoʊəˈdʒʌstmənt/
noun
共同调整，协调
The act or process of coadjusting.
例句：The coadjustment of the different parts of the engine is crucial.  发动机不同部件的协调至关重要。

例句：Successful project management requires the coadjustment of resources and timelines.  成功的项目管理需要协调资源和时间表。
近义词：coordination, harmonization, reconciliation, integration
反义词：misalignment, disorganization
词性：noun
