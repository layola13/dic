expiry:/ɛkˈspaɪri/ | n. |到期，失效，满期|The expiry of the contract caused a lot of problems. 合同的到期造成了很多问题。|例句：Check the expiry on that yogurt before you eat it. 吃酸奶之前检查一下有效期。|近义词：expiration, termination, end|反义词:renewal, commencement, beginning|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *expirye, from Old French expirer, from Latin exspīrāre (“to breathe out, exhale, die”), from ex (“out”) + spīrāre (“to breathe”).

USAGE->

The contract expires next month. 合同下个月到期。
My membership expires annually. 我的会员资格每年到期。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
My visa is expiring soon. 我的签证很快就要到期了。
The offer is expiring at midnight.  优惠在午夜到期。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
The milk has expired. 牛奶过期了。
His driver's license has expired. 他的驾照过期了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
The medicine has been expiring gradually.  这药的药效一直在逐渐失效。
This offer has been expiring for a while now. 这个提议已经失效一段时间了。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
The coupon expired last week.  优惠券上周到期了。
The warranty expired yesterday. 保修昨天到期了。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
The deal was expiring when we finally made a decision.  我们最终做出决定时，交易快要到期了。
The discount was expiring while I was checking out. 我结账的时候折扣快要到期了。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
The offer had expired before I could use it. 在我使用之前，优惠已经过期了。
The lease had expired before they found a new apartment. 在他们找到新公寓之前，租约已经到期了。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
The food had been expiring slowly in the heat.  食物在高温下慢慢变质。
The promotion had been expiring for several days before they noticed.  促销活动在他们注意到之前已经过期好几天了。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
The contract will expire next year. 合同明年到期。
Your license will expire soon. 你的执照很快就要到期了。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
The agreement will be expiring soon. 协议很快就要到期了。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next week, the discount will have expired. 到下周，折扣将已经过期。
By the time we get there, the tickets will have expired. 等我们到那里的时候，票就已经过期了。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next month, the contract will have been expiring for a year. 到下个月，合同将已经到期一年了。  (This example is a bit awkward, as it implies the contract continues to be in a state of expiry. A better example might focus on the duration leading up to the expiry: By next month, we will have been working with this expiring contract for a year.)
By the time he graduates, his student visa will have been expiring. 到他毕业的时候，他的学生签证将已经过期。（This example has the same awkwardness as the previous one. A better option might be: By the time he graduates, he will have been living on a soon-to-expire student visa for four years.)



虚拟语气:

I wish the deadline hadn't expired yet. 我希望截止日期还没有到期。


被动语态:
It was noted that the contract had been expired. 有人指出合同已经过期。
The expiration date is clearly displayed on the product. 产品上清楚地标明了有效期。



疑问句:
When does your passport expire? 你的护照什么时候到期？

否定句:
