erythroid:/ɪˈrɪθrɔɪd/
adjective
红细胞的；类红细胞的
Relating to red blood cells or their development.
Erythroid precursors are developing red blood cells. 红细胞前体细胞是正在发育的红细胞。

The erythroid lineage gives rise to red blood cells.  红系细胞谱系产生红细胞。

近义词:  erythrocytic, hematinic
反义词:  None (No direct antonym for a cell lineage)
词性: adjective
