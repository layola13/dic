giambologna:/ˌdʒæmboʊˈloʊnjə/ | n. |詹博洛尼亚| Giambologna, born Jean de Boulogne, was a Flemish sculptor active in Italy during the Mannerist period. | Giambologna's most famous work is the Rape of the Sabine Women in Florence. 詹博洛尼亚最著名的作品是佛罗伦萨的《萨宾妇女的掠夺》。 | synonyms: Jean Boulogne (birth name), Giovanni da Bologna (Italianized name)| antonyms: N/A | noun
