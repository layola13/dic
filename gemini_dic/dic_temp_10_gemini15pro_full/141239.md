一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think about this book?  你觉得这本书怎么样？
What does she usually eat for breakfast? 她早餐通常吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? 你在看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? 她用这笔钱做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading all afternoon? 他整个下午都在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? 你说了什么？
What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么事？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you came here? 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had they decided before the announcement? 宣布之前他们决定了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you?  在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow?  明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):

What will they have finished by tomorrow afternoon?  到明天下午他们将完成什么？
