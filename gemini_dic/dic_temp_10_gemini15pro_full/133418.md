nonfamily:/ˌnɑːnˈfæmɪli/ | adj. | 非家庭的；非亲属的；无家属的 | Not related to family; not part of a family unit; without family. |  Many nonfamily households consist of single people or unrelated roommates. 许多非家庭住户由单身人士或不相关的室友组成。| Example: The nonfamily section of the restaurant was less crowded.  餐厅的非家庭区人比较少。| Near Synonyms: unrelated, non-kin, extrafamilial | Antonyms: family, kin, related | adjective
