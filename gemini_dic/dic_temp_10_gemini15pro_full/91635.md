hyperbole:/haɪˈpɜːrbəli/ (US) /haɪˈpɜːbəli/ (UK)| n. |夸张；夸张法|An extravagant statement or figure of speech not intended to be taken literally, used to create a strong impression.|例句：He used hyperbole to describe his fishing trip, claiming he caught a fish as big as a car. 他用夸张的描述他的钓鱼之旅，声称他钓到了一条像汽车一样大的鱼。

例句：Her statement about dying of embarrassment was pure hyperbole. 她关于羞愧而死的说法纯粹是夸张。|近义词：exaggeration, overstatement, amplification|反义词: understatement, litotes|词性:noun
