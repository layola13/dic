bruschetta:/bruːˈskɛtə/ /bruːˈʃɛtə/
n.
意式烤面包
An appetizer consisting of toasted bread topped with various ingredients.
The restaurant is known for its delicious bruschetta. 这家餐厅以其美味的意式烤面包而闻名。
近义词: appetizer, antipasto
反义词: None (It's a specific food item, not a concept with an opposite.)
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Italian *bruschetta*, from *bruscare* "to roast over coals," from Roman dialect *brusca* "brush."  The word refers to the process of toasting the bread.
