narceine:/ˈnɑːrsiːn/ /nɑːrˈsiːɪn/| n. |那可汀|A crystalline alkaloid, C23H27NO8, found in opium, used as a mild analgesic and sedative.|Narceine is less potent than morphine. 那可汀的药效不如吗啡。|Synonyms:  analgesic, anodyne, opiate, narcotic|Antonyms: stimulant|noun

narceine:/ˈnɑːrsiːn/ /nɑːrˈsiːɪn/| n. |麻醉剂|A drug that causes temporary loss of bodily sensation.|Narceine can be used as a mild anesthetic. 那可汀可以作为一种温和的麻醉剂使用。|Synonyms: anesthetic, soporific, hypnotic|Antonyms: stimulant|noun
