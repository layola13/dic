corbelled:/kɔːrˈbɛld/ | adj. | 叠涩的，用突出石头砌成的 | built or projecting in such a way that each course of masonry extends farther outward than the one below. | The castle walls were corbelled, creating a strong defensive structure. 城墙是叠涩建造的，形成了坚固的防御结构。 |Synonyms: cantilevered, projecting, overhanging | Antonyms: recessed, indented | adjective

corbel:/ˈkɔːrbəl/ | n. | 叠涩；托座；（古建筑突出的）梁托 | a projection jutting out from a wall to support a structure above it. | The corbels supporting the balcony were intricately carved. 支撑阳台的托座雕刻精美。 | Synonyms: bracket, support, console | Antonyms: N/A | noun

corbel:/ˈkɔːrbəl/ | v. | 用叠涩法建造；突出 | to build or decorate with corbels. | They corbelled the archway to give it a more impressive appearance. 他们用叠涩法建造了拱门，使其看起来更令人印象深刻。 | Synonyms: cantilever, project, overhang | Antonyms: recess, indent | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *corbel, diminutive of corb "raven," from Latin corvus, because of the resemblance of a corbel to a raven's beak.
