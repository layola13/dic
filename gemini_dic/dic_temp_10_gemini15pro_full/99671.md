interlinear:/ˌɪntərˈlɪniər/ | adj. n. |行间的，插在行间的；逐行注释 | Written or printed between the lines of a text, typically with a translation or commentary. Also, a book or edition containing such annotations. | This edition of the Bible has interlinear glosses to help with understanding the original Hebrew. 这本圣经版本有逐行注释，有助于理解原始希伯来语。

The interlinear translation made the difficult poem accessible to students. 逐行翻译使学生能够理解这首难懂的诗。 | 近义词: annotated, glossed | 反义词: unannotated, unglossed | 词性: adjective, noun

词源: From Latin *interlinearis*, from *inter* "between" + *linea* "line."
