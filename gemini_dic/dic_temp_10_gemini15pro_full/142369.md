orthopinacoid:/ˌɔːrθoʊˈpɪnəkɔɪd/
n.|正交轴面；{晶}斜方柱|A crystal face parallel to the vertical and macro axes.|The orthopinacoid is a prominent feature of this mineral.|近义词:pinacoid|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: ortho- (Greek, "straight," "right," "true") + pinakoid (German, from Greek pinax, "board," "tablet," referring to the flat, tabular appearance of these crystal faces).
