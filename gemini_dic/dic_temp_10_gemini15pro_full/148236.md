oversimplifies:/ˌoʊvərˈsɪmplɪfaɪz/| v.| 过度简化 | To describe or explain something in a way that makes it seem less complex than it really is, often leaving out important details. | The report oversimplifies the complex economic situation.  这份报告过度简化了复杂的经济形势。| synonyms: reduces, trivializes, dumbs down | antonyms: complicates, elaborates, nuances | verb

oversimplified:/ˌoʊvərˈsɪmplɪfaɪd/| adj.| 被过度简化的 | Having been described or explained in a way that makes it seem less complex than it really is. |  The teacher gave an oversimplified explanation of the theory. 老师对这个理论作了过于简化的解释。 | synonyms: reductive, simplistic | antonyms: complex, nuanced | adjective

oversimplification:/ˌoʊvərˌsɪmplɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/| n.| 过度简化 | The act of describing or explaining something in a way that makes it seem less complex than it really is. |  The oversimplification of the issue led to misunderstandings. 对这个问题的过度简化导致了误解。 | synonyms: reduction, trivialization | antonyms: complication, elaboration | noun
