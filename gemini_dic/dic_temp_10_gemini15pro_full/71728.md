flawing:/ˈflɔːɪŋ/| adj.|有缺陷的，有瑕疵的|Having or containing a flaw or flaws; imperfect.|The report was flawed by a lack of evidence.  这份报告因缺乏证据而有缺陷。|Synonyms: imperfect, defective, faulty, deficient|Antonyms: perfect, flawless, impeccable|adjective

flaw:/flɔː/| n. vt.|瑕疵，缺点；使有瑕疵|A mark, fault, or other imperfection that mars a substance or object; to cause a flaw in.|The diamond had a tiny flaw that was only visible under magnification. 这颗钻石有一个微小的瑕疵，只有在放大镜下才能看到。  The accident flawed his previously perfect driving record.  这起事故在他的先前完美的驾驶记录上留下了污点。|Synonyms: defect, blemish, fault, imperfection|Antonyms: perfection, strength, asset|noun, verb
