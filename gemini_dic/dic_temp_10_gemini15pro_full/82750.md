guile:/ɡaɪl/| n.|狡猾，诡计，欺诈|sly or cunning intelligence or skill in attaining a goal; crafty or artful deception; duplicity.|例句：He used guile and flattery to get what he wanted. 他用诡计和奉承来得到他想要的东西。|例句：She achieved her aims through a combination of guile and hard work. 她通过诡计和努力工作实现了她的目标。|近义词：cunning, deceit, trickery, craftiness, slyness, duplicity|反义词：honesty, sincerity, candor, frankness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *guile, gille* "trick, deceit," from Norman *wile,* from Proto-Germanic *wigila,* related to Old English *wigle* "trick, magic," and *wiglian* "to divine, practice sorcery." Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *wegʰ-*, meaning "to move, shake, weave."
