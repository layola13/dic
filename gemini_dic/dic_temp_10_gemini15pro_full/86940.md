hemself: /hemˈself/ | pronoun | 他/她/它自己 (性别中性或不明确时) |  A reflexive pronoun used when the gender of the subject is unknown, unspecified, or non-binary. It avoids the gendered pronouns "himself" or "herself". |  Example:  Each student should evaluate hemself on their progress.  每个学生都应该评估自己的学习进度。  | Synonym:  themself (more common), theirself | Antonym: N/A | Pronoun
