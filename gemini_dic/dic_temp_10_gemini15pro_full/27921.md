cantharidin:/kænˈθærɪdɪn/ | n. |斑蝥素;芫菁素|A poisonous crystalline substance obtained from dried beetles (especially Spanish fly) and used as a vesicant and aphrodisiac.|Cantharidin is a potent vesicant and should be handled with extreme care. 斑蝥素是一种强效起疱剂，应格外小心处理。|Cantharidin is sometimes used in topical medications for warts. 斑蝥素有时用于治疗疣的局部药物中。|Synonyms: Spanish fly, blistering agent|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider demulcent - a soothing substance)|noun
