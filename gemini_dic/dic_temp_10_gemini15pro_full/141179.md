oohed:/uːd/ | int. | 哦，啊（表示惊讶或赞叹） | An exclamation expressing surprise or admiration. |  The crowd oohed and aahed at the fireworks display.  人群对烟火表演发出“哦”和“啊”的赞叹声。 | Example:  She oohed over the beautiful dress. 她对那条漂亮的裙子赞叹不已。 | 近义词: aahed, exclaimed, gasped | 反义词: (No direct antonym for an exclamation) | 词性: interjection

PHRASE->
