mechanization:/ˌmɛkənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |机械化|The process of using machines to do work that was previously done by people or animals. |The mechanization of agriculture has greatly increased crop yields. 农业机械化极大地提高了农作物产量。|Synonyms: automation, industrialization |Antonyms: manual labor, hand labor | noun
