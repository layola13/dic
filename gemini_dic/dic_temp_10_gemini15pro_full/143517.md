outgoings:/ˈaʊtɡəʊɪŋz/
noun
支出，开销
Amounts of money spent regularly, especially on ordinary running costs.
Our monthly outgoings are too high.  我们的每月开销太高了。
expenses, expenditure, spending
income, earnings
