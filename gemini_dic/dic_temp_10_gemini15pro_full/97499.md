ingestion:/ɪnˈdʒɛstʃən/| n.|摄取，吸收，消化|the process of taking food, drink, or another substance into the body by swallowing or absorbing it.|例句：The ingestion of large amounts of caffeine can be harmful.  摄入大量的咖啡因可能是有害的。

例句：Proper food ingestion is crucial for good health.  适当的食物摄取对健康至关重要。|近义词：consumption, intake, absorption|反义词:excretion, elimination|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin ingestionem,  from Latin ingestus, past participle of ingerere "to carry in, introduce," from in- "in" + gerere "to carry, bear."
