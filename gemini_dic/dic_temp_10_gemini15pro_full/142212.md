ornithologically:/ˌɔːrnɪθəˈlɒdʒɪkli/
adverb
鸟类学地
From the point of view of ornithology; in a way relating to the study of birds.
The area is ornithologically rich, with over 200 species recorded.  这个地区鸟类资源丰富，记录在案的鸟类超过200种。
Synonym:  avianly
Antonym: N/A (No direct antonym)
词性: adverb
