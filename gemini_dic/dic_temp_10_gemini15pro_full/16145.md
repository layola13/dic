banishers:/ˈbænɪʃərz/
noun
驱逐者；放逐者
People who banish others; those who exile or expel.
The banishers drove the criminals out of the kingdom. 驱逐者将罪犯赶出了王国。
The king sent his banishers to deal with the rebels. 国王派他的驱逐者去处理叛乱分子。
exiles, expellers, deporters
welcomers, admitters
n.
