ghawazi:/ˈɣɑːwɑːzi/ | n. | Ghawazee (复数),  Ghaziya (单数)|  A group of traditionally itinerant female entertainers in Egypt, known for their dancing and music.  Often associated, sometimes inaccurately, with Romani communities.|  The ghawazi dancers were renowned for their vibrant costumes and expressive movements.  加瓦齐舞者以其鲜艳的服装和富有表现力的动作而闻名。|Synonyms:  dancing girls,  entertainers |Antonyms:  audience, spectators | Noun

ghaziya:/ɣɑːˈziːjə/ | n. | Ghaziya (单数), Ghawazee (复数)| A female member of the ghawazi, a group of traditionally itinerant entertainers in Egypt. | The ghaziya's performance captivated the audience. 这名加齐亚的表演吸引了观众。|Synonyms: dancer, entertainer, performer| Antonyms: audience, spectator | Noun
