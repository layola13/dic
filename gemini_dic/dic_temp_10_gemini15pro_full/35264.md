cluster:/ˈklʌstər/ (US) /ˈklʌstə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|簇，群; 聚集，丛生|A cluster is a group of similar things that are close together.|例句：A cluster of grapes. 一串葡萄。|近义词：group, bunch, collection|反义词:scattering, dispersal|noun
cluster:/ˈklʌstər/ (US) /ˈklʌstə(r)/ (UK)| v.|聚集，丛生|To cluster is to gather or grow in a close group.|例句：The children clustered around the teacher. 孩子们聚集在老师周围。|近义词：gather, assemble, congregate|反义词:disperse, scatter|verb
