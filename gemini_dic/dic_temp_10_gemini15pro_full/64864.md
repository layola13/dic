espousing:/ɪˈspaʊzɪŋ/
Verb
支持；拥护；信奉
Adopting or supporting a cause, belief, or way of life.
He was known for espousing unpopular political views. 他以支持不受欢迎的政治观点而闻名。
The organization espouses the principles of non-violent protest.  该组织信奉非暴力抗议的原则。

近义词: advocating, supporting, championing, upholding, endorsing
反义词: opposing, rejecting, denouncing, condemning
词性: Verb (动词 -ing 形式，现在分词)

词源:
From Old French *espouser "to marry," from Latin sponsare "to promise, betroth," from sponsus "bridegroom, betrothed," past participle of spondere "to promise solemnly."  The meaning shifted from "to marry" to "to take up, adopt" in the 16th century.
