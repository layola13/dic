lixiviation:/ˌlɪksɪˈveɪʃən/ | n. | 沥滤，浸析 |The process of separating soluble components from a solid material by dissolving them in a liquid solvent.  This process is often used in mining and metallurgy to extract valuable metals from ore. |The lixiviation of gold from the ore was done using cyanide. 从矿石中浸析金是用氰化物完成的。| leaching, percolation, extraction |  precipitation, deposition | noun
