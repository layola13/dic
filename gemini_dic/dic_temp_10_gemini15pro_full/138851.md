nursed:/nɜːrst/ | v. |照顾，护理；怀抱；喂奶；培养；(少量地)喝；精心守护|She nursed her sick mother for years. 她多年来一直在照顾她生病的母亲。|English Explanation:  Past tense and past participle of "nurse," meaning to care for someone, especially someone who is sick or injured.  It can also refer to breastfeeding a baby or harboring a feeling or thought. |例句：The nurse nursed the patient back to health. 护士护理病人恢复了健康。

例句：She nursed a secret ambition to become a writer. 她怀抱着成为作家的秘密抱负。|近义词：tended, cared for, fostered, cherished|反义词：neglect, abandon|词性:动词
