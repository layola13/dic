concertgoer: /ˈkɑːnsərtˌɡoʊər/ | n. | 音乐会观众；常去听音乐会的人 | A person who regularly attends concerts. |  Many concertgoers brought flowers for the lead singer. 许多音乐会观众给主唱带了花。|  The concertgoer cheered enthusiastically after each song.  这位音乐会观众在每首歌结束后都热情地欢呼。 |  music lover, audience member, fan, attendee |  None | noun

Etymology:
concert + -goer.  "Concert" comes from the Italian "concerto," ultimately from the Latin "concertare" meaning "to contend, strive together." "-goer" is a suffix denoting a person who frequently attends or participates in a specified activity.
