hopperings:/ˈhɒpərɪŋz/ | n. | 残渣，渣滓，酒糟 | Leftover grains or other material after processing, often used as animal feed. | The farmer fed the hopperings to his pigs. 农民把酒糟喂猪。 | 近义词: dregs, lees, remains, residue | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 "whole", "unprocessed" 表示未经处理的完整状态) | 词性: 名词


词源:  From "hopper," a container for grain or other material, + "-ings," a suffix indicating a product or result of an action.  最终来源于古英语 *hoppan, 意思是 "to hop, jump, dance," 指的是用于盛放谷物或其他材料的容器，其形状可能使内容物看起来像在跳跃或移动。
