crackles:/ˈkrækəlz/ (US), /ˈkrækəlz/ (UK)| n.|噼啪声，爆裂声；（医学）啰音|Short, sharp, popping sounds.|The fire crackled merrily in the fireplace. 壁炉里的火愉快地噼啪作响。|Synonyms: pops, snaps, clicks|Antonyms: silence, quiet|noun
