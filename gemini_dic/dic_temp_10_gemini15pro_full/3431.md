Example 2: The call-and-response structure of the song is a common africanism in many musical genres. 这首歌的呼应结构是非洲音乐中常见的元素。 | 近义词: African characteristic, African trait | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "Westernism" or "Eurocentrism" as contrasting concepts) | 词性: noun
