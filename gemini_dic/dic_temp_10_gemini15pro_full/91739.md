hyperdevelopment:/ˌhaɪpər dəˈvɛləpmənt/ | n. |过度发展，超速发展|Excessive or accelerated development, often beyond a healthy or sustainable level.  It suggests a rapid and often uncontrolled growth or expansion, potentially leading to negative consequences.| The hyperdevelopment of the coastal region has led to environmental damage.  沿海地区的过度开发导致了环境破坏。|The city's hyperdevelopment has resulted in traffic congestion and a shortage of affordable housing.  这个城市的过度发展导致了交通拥堵和经济适用房短缺。|近义词：overdevelopment, rapid growth, accelerated development|反义词: underdevelopment, stagnation|词性:noun


Etymology:  From hyper- (Greek, "over, excessive") + development.
