asiatic:/eɪʒiˈætɪk/ /eɪʃiˈætɪk/
adjective
亚洲的; (尤指)亚洲东部的;亚洲人
Belonging to or characteristic of Asia or its people, especially East Asia.
例句：Asiatic cholera originated in India.  亚洲霍乱起源于印度。

例句：She has delicate Asiatic features. 她有精致的亚洲人容貌。

近义词：Asian, Oriental (dated and potentially offensive)
反义词：European, African, American, etc. (depending on context)
词性: adjective

词源: From French asiatique, from Latin Asiaticus, from Asia. Ultimately from Ancient Greek  Ἀσία (Asía).
