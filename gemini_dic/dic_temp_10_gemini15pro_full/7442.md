aniseeds:/ˈænɪsiːdz/| n.|茴香籽|The small, dry fruits of the anise plant (Pimpinella anisum), used as a spice and flavoring.|I added aniseeds to the cake batter for a licorice-like flavor. 我在蛋糕糊里加了茴香籽，以获得类似甘草的味道。|Synonym: anise, anise seed|Antonym: N/A|noun

aniseed:/ˈænɪsiːd/| n. adj.|茴香;茴香籽;茴香味的|Referring to anise or its flavor.|The aniseed cookies were a delicious treat. 茴香饼干是一种美味的享受。|Synonym: anise, anise-flavored|Antonym: N/A|noun, adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *anyssed*, from Old French *anis*, from Latin *anisum*, from Ancient Greek *ἄνισον (ánison)*.
