colourways:/ˈkʌləweɪz/
n.
配色方案；颜色组合
The variations of colours available for a particular product.
The new car is available in a range of exciting colourways. 这款新车有多种令人兴奋的配色方案可供选择。
synonyms: color schemes, color palettes, color combinations
antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "monochromatic")
noun


colourways:/ˈkʌləweɪz/
n.
颜色方式；颜色变化
The different ways in which a product is colored.
The artist explored various colourways in his latest series of paintings.  这位艺术家在他最新的系列绘画中探索了各种颜色变化。
synonyms: color variations, color options
antonyms: (No direct antonym)
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"colour" (from Old French "colour," ultimately from Latin "color") + "way" (from Old English "weg")
