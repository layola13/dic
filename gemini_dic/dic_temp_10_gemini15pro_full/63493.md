entwined:/ɪnˈtwaɪnd/ | adj. | 缠绕的，交织的 | Closely twisted or interwoven together. |  The lovers walked hand in hand, their fingers entwined. 恋人们手牵着手走着，手指交缠在一起。

The vines were entwined around the tree trunk. 藤蔓缠绕在树干上。

| 近义词: intertwined, interwoven, tangled, twisted | 反义词: separated, untangled, disentangled | 词性: adjective
