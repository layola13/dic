luciferian: /ˌluːsɪˈfɪəriən/ | n. |信奉路西法的人；路西法信徒 | A person who adheres to Luciferianism, a belief system that often venerates Lucifer as a symbol of enlightenment, rebellion, or individual will.  |  Some luciferians reject the notion of Lucifer as an evil entity. 一些路西法信徒反对将路西法视为邪恶实体的观念。 | The group of luciferians met in secret. 这群路西法信徒秘密集会。 | 近义词: Satanist | 反义词: Christian, Muslim (and other adherents to traditional religions)| 词性: 名词

词源: From Latin "Lucifer" (light-bearer), the name given to the planet Venus when seen in the morning. Later associated with the fallen angel in Christian tradition.
