disowner:/dɪsˈoʊnər/| n. |断绝关系者；否认者|A person who disowns someone or something.|He became a disowner of his family after the argument.  他与家人争吵后断绝了关系。|近义词：repudiator, denier|反义词: acceptor, acknowledger|noun


disowner:/dɪsˈoʊnər/| vt. |否认；脱离关系；拒绝承认|To refuse to acknowledge or accept any responsibility for; renounce.|She disowned her son after he committed the crime.  在他犯罪之后，她与儿子断绝了关系。|近义词：renounce, repudiate, reject|反义词: acknowledge, accept, claim|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
