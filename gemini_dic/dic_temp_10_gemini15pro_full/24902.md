bruit:/bruːt/| n. vt.|谣言，传闻；散布（谣言）|A piece of news or a rumour, often of an alarming or sensational nature.  To spread a rumour widely.|例句：The bruit of his resignation spread quickly through the office. 他辞职的传闻迅速在办公室传开。|近义词：rumor, report, gossip, hearsay|反义词:silence, truth|noun
bruit:/bruːt/| vt.|散布（谣言）|To make (something) widely known.|例句：The newspapers bruited the scandal abroad. 报纸把这件丑闻传遍了各地。|近义词：spread, circulate, disseminate, promulgate|反义词:suppress, conceal|verb
