palsying:/ˈpɔːlziɪŋ/| v. |使瘫痪，使麻痹；使丧失力量|Causing paralysis or loss of control; severely impairing the effectiveness of something.|The disease is palsying his legs.  这种疾病使他双腿瘫痪。|Synonyms: paralyzing, crippling, debilitating, incapacitating|Antonyms: strengthening, invigorating, empowering|verb

palsy:/ˈpɔːlzi/| n. |瘫痪，麻痹|Paralysis, especially that which is accompanied by involuntary tremors.|Cerebral palsy affects muscle control. 脑瘫会影响肌肉控制。|Synonyms: paralysis, paresis|Antonyms: mobility, strength|noun
