词源：From Old English *brōðel, from brōð "broth," used figuratively to mean "vices," + -el, diminutive suffix.  The original meaning likely referred to a low-class eating house frequented by prostitutes.  Later, the term evolved to refer to the place itself where prostitution occurred.
