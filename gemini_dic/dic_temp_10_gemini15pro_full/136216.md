nonremediably:/ˌnɒnrɪˈmiːdiəbli/
adverb
不可挽回地，无法补救地
In a way that cannot be remedied or corrected.
The ecosystem was nonremediably damaged by the oil spill. 生态系统被石油泄漏不可挽回地破坏了。
The patient was nonremediably ill. 病人病情已无法治愈。
近义词: irreparably, incurably, permanently
反义词: remediably, curably, fixably
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From non- + remediable + -ly.  "Remediable" comes from Late Latin "remediabilis," from "remediare" (to heal, cure), from "re-" (again) + "mederi" (to heal).
