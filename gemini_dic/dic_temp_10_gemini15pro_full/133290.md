nonexpeditiously:/ˌnɒnˌɛkspɪˈdɪʃəsli/
adverb
不迅速地，拖延地
in a way that is not quick or efficient; slowly.
The matter was handled nonexpeditiously, resulting in further delays. 这件事处理得并不迅速，导致了进一步的延误。
The paperwork was processed nonexpeditiously.  这些文件被处理得很慢。
近义词: slowly, inefficiently, sluggishly, tardily
反义词: quickly, efficiently, promptly, expeditiously
词性: adverb

词源:
From non- + expeditious + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "expeditious" means "done with speed and efficiency."  The suffix "-ly" turns the adjective "expeditious" into an adverb.
