levelism:/ˈlɛvəlɪzəm/
n.|平均主义;平等主义|A belief in or the practice of treating everyone equally, regardless of their social standing, wealth, etc.|The company's policy of levelism ensures equal opportunities for all employees. 公司的平等主义政策确保所有员工享有平等的机会。|Example: The candidate's levelism resonated with voters who felt left behind by the current system.  这位候选人的平等主义思想引起了那些感觉被现行体制抛弃的选民的共鸣。|Synonyms: egalitarianism, equality, fairness|Antonyms: elitism, hierarchy, discrimination|noun
