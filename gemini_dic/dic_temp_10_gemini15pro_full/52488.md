dietitian:/ˌdaɪəˈtɪʃən/ | n. |营养师，营养学家|A health professional who gives advice about food and eating habits, especially to treat a medical condition.|例句：The dietitian helped me create a meal plan to manage my diabetes. 营养师帮我制定了控制糖尿病的饮食计划。

例句：I consulted a dietitian for advice on healthy eating. 我咨询了一位营养师关于健康饮食的建议。|近义词：nutritionist|反义词:|名词
