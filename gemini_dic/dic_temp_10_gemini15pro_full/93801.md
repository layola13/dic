immersionist:/ɪˈmɜːrʒənɪst/| n. |沉浸主义者；全身心投入者|A person who advocates or practices immersion, especially in language learning, virtual reality, or a particular activity or interest.|例句：The language immersionist encouraged students to only speak the target language, even outside of class.  这位语言沉浸主义者鼓励学生即使在课外也只说目标语言。

例句：As an immersionist gamer, he invested in a high-quality VR headset for a truly immersive experience. 作为一名沉浸式游戏玩家，他投资了一款高质量的VR头显，以获得真正的沉浸式体验。|近义词：enthusiast, devotee, aficionado|反义词：dilettante, dabbler|词性: noun
