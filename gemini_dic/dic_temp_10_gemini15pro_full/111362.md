liveried:/ˈlɪvərid/ | adj. | 穿制服的，着 livery 的 | Wearing a livery; uniformed. |  The liveried chauffeur opened the door of the limousine. 穿制服的司机打开了豪华轿车的门。| The hotel employed liveried doormen. 这家酒店雇佣了穿制服的门卫。 | 近义词：uniformed, clad | 反义词: plainclothes, civilian | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From livery + -ed.  "Livery" itself comes from Old French *livree* meaning "delivery," particularly of provisions or clothing given to retainers or servants.
