concocters:/kənˈkɒktəz/
noun
调制者；捏造者；策划者
Those who concoct something, especially a dish, drink, story, or plan.
The concocters of this new energy drink claim it improves athletic performance.  这种新型能量饮料的调制者声称它可以提高运动成绩。
inventors, creators, devisers, formulators
destroyers
