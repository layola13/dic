indemonstrable:/ˌɪndɪˈmɒnstrəbl/ /ˌɪndɪˈmɑːnstrəbl/
adjective
不可证明的；无法论证的
not capable of being demonstrated or proved

The existence of God is often considered indemonstrable.
上帝的存在常被认为是不可证明的。

His theory is indemonstrable with current technology.
以目前的科技，他的理论无法得到证明。

近义词:unprovable, unprovable, unarguable
反义词:provable, demonstrable, arguable
词性:adjective

词源:
Late Middle English: from medieval Latin indemonstrabilis, from in- ‘not’ + demonstrabilis (see demonstrable).
