abjure:/əbˈdʒʊr/ /əbˈdʒɝː/| vt.|正式放弃，发誓放弃；弃绝|He abjured his religion/his former beliefs. 他放弃了他的宗教信仰/他以前的信仰。|近义词：renounce, relinquish, forswear, repudiate|反义词：affirm, embrace, uphold|动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *abjuren*, from Old French *abjurer*, from Latin *abiūrāre* (“to deny on oath, forswear, renounce”), from *ab* (“away from”) + *iūrāre* (“to swear”).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He abjures all forms of violence. = 他放弃一切形式的暴力。
She abjures her former lifestyle. = 她放弃了她以前的生活方式。

一般过去时:
He abjured his allegiance to the king. = 他放弃了对国王的效忠。
She abjured her claim to the throne. = 她放弃了对王位的要求。

现在完成时:
He has abjured his bad habits. = 他已经戒掉了他的坏习惯。
She has abjured her membership in the organization. = 她已经放弃了在该组织的成员资格。

过去完成时:
He had abjured his faith before he died. = 他在去世前放弃了他的信仰。
She had abjured her love for him before she left. = 她离开之前就放弃了对他的爱。

将来时:
He will abjure his citizenship if he moves to another country. = 如果他移居到另一个国家，他将放弃他的公民身份。
She will abjure her right to an inheritance. = 她将放弃她的继承权。

虚拟语气:
If he were to abjure his past, he might find peace. = 如果他放弃过去，他可能会找到平静。
I wish she would abjure her harmful beliefs. = 我希望她能放弃她有害的信仰。

被动语态:
His former beliefs were abjured by him. = 他以前的信仰被他放弃了。
The treaty was abjured by the government. = 该条约被政府放弃了。


PHRASE->
abjure a claim = 放弃索赔
abjure a belief = 放弃信仰
abjure a right = 放弃权利
abjure a lifestyle = 放弃一种生活方式
abjure violence = 放弃暴力
