analogous: /əˈnæləɡəs/ | adj. | 类似的，相似的 | similar or comparable in some respect; equivalent |  The relationship between a teacher and student is analogous to that of a parent and child.  老师和学生的关系类似于父母和孩子的关系。| similar, comparable, akin, parallel, like | dissimilar, different, unlike | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *analogus, from Ancient Greek ἀνάλογος (análogos, “proportionate”), from ἀνά (aná, “according to”) + λόγος (lógos, “ratio, proportion, reckoning, word”).
