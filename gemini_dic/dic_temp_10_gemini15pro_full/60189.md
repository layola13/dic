The correct spelling is "efficiency".  There is no English word "efficience".

efficiency: /ɪˈfɪʃənsi/ | n. | 效率，效能 | The ability to avoid wasting materials, energy, efforts, money, and time in doing something or in producing a desired result. | The company is striving to improve efficiency.  这家公司正在努力提高效率。| The new machine greatly increased the efficiency of the production process. 新机器大大提高了生产过程的效率。 | synonyms: effectiveness, productivity, performance, competence | antonyms: inefficiency, incompetence, wastefulness | noun
