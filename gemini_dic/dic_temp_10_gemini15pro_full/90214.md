houndstooth:/ˈhaʊndzˌtuːθ/ /ˈhaʊndzˌtuːθ/| n. adj.|千鸟格，犬牙纹|A woven textile pattern characterized by broken checks or abstract four-pointed shapes, often in black and white, resembling a canine tooth.|She wore a houndstooth jacket. 她穿了一件千鸟格外套。|近义词：check, plaid, glen plaid, dogtooth|反义词: (no direct antonym, perhaps "solid color")|noun
houndstooth:/ˈhaʊndzˌtuːθ/ /ˈhaʊndzˌtuːθ/| adj.|千鸟格的，犬牙纹的|Made of or patterned with houndstooth.|The houndstooth pattern is classic and timeless. 千鸟格图案是经典而永恒的。|近义词：checked, plaid|反义词: (no direct antonym, perhaps "plain")|adjective
