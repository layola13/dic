matric:/ˈmeɪtrɪk/| n. |入学考试; 大学入学考试; 录取资格;|Matric is short for matriculation, which refers to the final exams taken at the end of high school, especially in some countries like South Africa. These exams determine university entrance eligibility.|He passed his matric with flying colours. 他以优异的成绩通过了入学考试。|近义词：matriculation, entrance exam, school leaving examination|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
