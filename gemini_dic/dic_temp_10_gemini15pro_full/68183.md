faintishnesses:/ˈfeɪntɪʃnəsɪz/
noun
昏厥，晕厥（复数）
A state of near fainting; slight faintness.  The plural form of "faintishness."
She experienced several faintishnesses throughout the day due to the heat.  由于天气炎热，她一整天都感到阵阵晕眩。
Near synonyms: dizziness, lightheadedness, faintness, giddiness
Antonyms: alertness, consciousness, wakefulness

faintishness:/ˈfeɪntɪʃnəs/
noun
昏厥，晕厥
A state of near fainting; slight faintness.
The faintishness passed after she sat down and drank some water. 她坐下喝了些水后，晕眩感就消失了。
Near synonyms: dizziness, lightheadedness, faint, giddiness
Antonyms: alertness, consciousness, wakefulness

faintish:/ˈfeɪntɪʃ/
adjective
有点晕的，微弱的
Slightly faint; feeling weak or dizzy.
She felt faintish after standing for too long.  站得太久，她感到有点晕。
Near synonyms: dizzy, lightheaded, weak, giddy
Antonyms: strong, alert, vigorous

faint:/feɪnt/
adjective
微弱的，模糊的，虚弱的; vi. 昏厥，晕倒
Lacking brightness, clarity, or strength; feeling weak and dizzy and about to lose consciousness. / Lose consciousness for a short time because of a temporarily insufficient supply of oxygen to the brain.
The music was so faint she could barely hear it.  音乐声太微弱了，她几乎听不见。 / He fainted from the heat. 他因高温而晕倒了。
Near synonyms: weak, dim, pale, feeble / swoon, collapse, black out
Antonyms: strong, bright, clear, vivid / revive, recover
