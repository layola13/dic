lineman:/ˈlaɪnmən/| n.|线路工人；（橄榄球）锋线队员|A lineman is a person who installs or repairs electrical or telephone lines.  A lineman is also a player on the line of scrimmage in football.|The lineman worked through the night to restore power after the storm.  暴风雨过后，线路工人通宵工作以恢复电力。

The lineman blocked the defensive end, allowing the quarterback to complete the pass.  锋线队员阻挡了防守端锋，让四分卫完成了传球。|Synonyms: linesman, cableman, wireman (for electrical/telephone lines); offensive lineman, defensive lineman (for football) |Antonyms: N/A|noun


lineman:/ˈlaɪnmən/| n.|（铁路）扳道工|A lineman is a person who operates the switches and signals on a railroad. |The lineman switched the tracks so the train could enter the siding. 扳道工切换了轨道，以便火车可以进入侧线。|Synonyms: switchman, pointsman|Antonyms: N/A|noun
