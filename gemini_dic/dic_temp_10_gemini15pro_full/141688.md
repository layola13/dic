opulent:/ˈɒpjʊlənt/ (British), /ˈɑːpjələnt/ (American)| adj.|豪华的，富丽堂皇的，丰富的|Characterized by lavishness, abundance, and richness; ostentatiously magnificent and luxurious. |例句：The opulent hotel lobby was filled with marble statues and crystal chandeliers.  富丽堂皇的酒店大堂里摆满了大理石雕像和水晶吊灯。

例句：She threw an opulent party, sparing no expense on the food, decorations, or entertainment. 她举办了一场奢华的派对，在食物、装饰和娱乐上毫不吝啬。

近义词：luxurious, lavish, sumptuous, grand, rich, affluent|反义词：austere, simple, modest, plain, poor, impoverished|词性: adjective
