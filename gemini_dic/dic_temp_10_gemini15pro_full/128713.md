nielsen:/ˈniːlsən/| n. |尼尔森|A surname, originally a patronymic meaning "son of Niels".  It's commonly associated with the Nielsen Company (now NielsenIQ and Nielsen Media Research), a global measurement and data analytics company known for its market research, particularly in television ratings. |The Nielsen ratings showed a significant drop in viewership. 尼尔森收视率显示收视率大幅下降。|近义词: (For the company)  market research firm, data analytics company |反义词: N/A|noun
