flounce:/flaʊns/| n. vi.| 突然离去，甩动，荷叶边，|A sudden, ostentatious movement, typically of leaving a place or tossing the head or body, often as an expression of annoyance or impatience. A wide ornamental strip of gathered or pleated material attached to a garment, typically around the hemline.|She flounced out of the room in a huff. 她怒气冲冲地离开了房间。|近义词：toss, swagger, sashay, ruffle, frill|反义词：glide, slink|词性：名词，动词
