blandiloquence:/blænˈdɪləkwəns/
noun
奉承；甜言蜜语
The art of flattery; flattering speech.
He used blandiloquence to win her favor. 他用甜言蜜语来赢得她的欢心。
Synonyms: flattery, adulation, sweet talk, honeyed words
Antonyms: criticism, reproach, condemnation
