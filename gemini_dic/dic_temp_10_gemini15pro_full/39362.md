conjugates:/ˈkɑːndʒəɡɪts/ | n. | 共轭物，结合物 |Two numbers are conjugates if they differ only in the sign of their imaginary parts. |  The conjugate of 3 + 4i is 3 - 4i.  3 + 4i 的共轭复数是 3 - 4i。 | synonyms: counterparts, complements | antonyms: N/A | noun

conjugates:/ˈkɑːndʒəɡeɪts/ | v. |  (使)结合，(使)成对 | In chemistry, two molecules conjugate when they join together. | The enzyme conjugates the two molecules. 酶将这两个分子结合在一起。 | synonyms: combine, unite, link | antonyms: separate, divide, split | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin conjugatus, past participle of conjugare ("to yoke together"), from com- ("together") + jugum ("yoke").
