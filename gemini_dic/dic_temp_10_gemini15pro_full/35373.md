co-occur:/ˌkoʊ əˈkɜːr/ (US), /ˌkəʊ əˈkɜː(r)/ (UK)| v.|共同出现，同时发生|To happen or exist at the same time or in the same place.|The two species co-occur in the same habitat. 这两个物种在同一栖息地共同出现。|Synonyms: coexist, coincide, concur|Antonyms: diverge, separate|Verb

co-occurrence:/ˌkoʊ əˈkɜːrəns/ (US), /ˌkəʊ əˈkʌrəns/ (UK)| n.|共同出现，同时发生|The fact of happening or existing at the same time or in the same place.|The co-occurrence of these events is not a coincidence. 这些事件的同时发生并非巧合。|Synonyms: coexistence, coincidence, concurrency|Antonyms: divergence, separation|Noun
