fashioners:/ˈfæʃənərz/
n.
时装设计师；塑造者
People who create or design fashion, or those who shape or influence something.
The fashioners of this new trend are largely unknown.  这个新趋势的缔造者们大多不为人知。
The fashioners of public opinion have a great responsibility.  舆论的塑造者肩负着巨大的责任。
近义词: designers, creators, makers, stylists
反义词: destroyers, imitators
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "fashion" (meaning to make or shape), which comes from Old French "façon" (manner, shape), ultimately from Latin "factio" (a making, doing), from "facere" (to make, do).  The suffix "-er" denotes a person who performs the action of the verb.
