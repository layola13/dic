blomary: /ˈblʊməri/ | n. | 炼铁炉; 锻铁场 | A bloomary is a type of furnace used for smelting iron from its oxides. |  The village blacksmith operated a small bloomary. 村里的铁匠经营着一个小小的炼铁炉。|  The bloomary was the center of the iron industry in medieval times. 在中世纪时期，炼铁炉是钢铁工业的中心。 | synonyms: forge, furnace, ironworks | antonyms: N/A | noun
