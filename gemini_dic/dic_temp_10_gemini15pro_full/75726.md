full-time:/ˌfʊlˈtaɪm/ (US), /ˌfʊlˈtaɪm/ (UK)| adj. adv.|全职的；全日制的|Working or operating the customary number of hours in a week considered a full working schedule.|She works full-time as a teacher.|近义词：full, complete|反义词: part-time|词性:adjective, adverb
