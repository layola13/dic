nonleaking:/ˌnɒnˈliːkɪŋ/
adjective
无泄漏的，不漏的
Preventing leaks; not allowing liquid or gas to escape.
This thermos is nonleaking, so you can put it in your bag without worry.  这个保温瓶是不漏水的，所以你可以放心地把它放在包里。
The new pipe system is guaranteed to be nonleaking. 新的管道系统保证不会泄漏。
leakproof, airtight, sealed, impermeable
leaky, permeable, porous
