miscarry:/ˌmɪsˈkæri/| v. |流产；失败；被误送|To fail to achieve the intended purpose; to end prematurely and unsuccessfully, especially of a pregnancy; to be sent to the wrong address. |例句：The project miscarried due to lack of funding.  该项目因缺乏资金而失败。

例句：Sadly, she miscarried in the first trimester.  不幸的是，她在妊娠早期流产了。|近义词：fail, abort, go wrong, backfire|反义词:succeed, thrive, flourish|词性:动词
