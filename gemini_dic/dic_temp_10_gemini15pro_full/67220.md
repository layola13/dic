expressway:/ɪkˈsprɛsweɪ/ | n. | 高速公路，快速路 | A major highway designed for high-speed traffic, typically with controlled access and multiple lanes in each direction. |  We took the expressway to get to the city quickly. 我们走高速公路很快就到了市区。

| Example: The expressway was congested during rush hour.  高速公路在高峰时段拥堵。| Synonym: freeway, highway, motorway, turnpike, thruway | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider) side road, back road, local road | Noun
