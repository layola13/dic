field:/fiːld/| n. |田地，场地，领域，字段|A piece of land, an area of activity, or a section of a data record.|例句：The farmer plowed the field. 农民耕田。|近义词：area, ground, domain, territory|反义词:none|词性:noun
field:/fiːld/| v. |处理，接住，派…上场|To deal with (a question or request), to catch a ball, to put a team or player into a game.|例句：She fielded questions from the press. 她回答了记者的提问。|近义词：handle, manage, answer, catch|反义词:avoid|词性:verb
