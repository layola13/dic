filipinos:/ˌfɪlɪˈpiːnoʊz/ | n. |菲律宾人|People from the Philippines, both male and female.  While "Filipino" is generally accepted for both genders, "Filipina" is sometimes used specifically for women. |Many Filipinos work overseas. 许多菲律宾人在海外工作。| Filipinos celebrate their Independence Day on June 12th. 菲律宾人在6月12日庆祝他们的独立日。 |Synonyms: Filipinos (plural), Filipinas (female plural, sometimes used), Philippine nationals |Antonyms:  None (nationality has no direct opposite) |Noun
