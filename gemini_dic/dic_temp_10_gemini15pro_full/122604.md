modernize:/ˈmɒdənaɪz/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdərnaɪz/ (US)| v. | 使现代化；更新；现代化|To make something more modern; to update something to contemporary standards. |例句：They are modernizing the factory with new equipment. 他们在用新设备使工厂现代化。

例句：The government is trying to modernize the education system. 政府正在努力使教育系统现代化。|近义词：update, innovate, revamp, upgrade, improve|反义词：preserve, maintain, keep, retain|词性: 动词
