half-congealed:/ˌhæf kənˈdʒiːld/
adjective
半凝固的
Partly solidified; thickened but not completely set.
The half-congealed gravy was unappetizing.  半凝固的肉汁看起来很不 appetizing。
The pudding was still half-congealed when I took it out of the fridge. 我把布丁从冰箱里拿出来时，它还是半凝固的。
近义词:semi-solid, partially solidified, thickening
反义词:liquid, runny, fluid
词性:adjective

词源:
"half-" (Old English healf) + "congeal" (from Old French congeler, from Latin congelare "to freeze," from com- "together" + gelare "to freeze").
