bakistre: /bɑːˈkɪstrə/ | n. | 后台，幕后 |  A backstage area, especially in a theater.  Refers to the areas of a theater or other performance venue that are not visible to the audience, including dressing rooms, wings, and storage spaces.  Likely derived from French "bas" (low) and a now-obsolete word related to "coulisse" (sliding panel or curtain), suggesting a low or hidden place behind the scenes. | The actors gathered in the bakistre before the performance.  演员们在演出前聚集在后台。 |  Backstage, behind the scenes, wings | Forestage, onstage | Noun
