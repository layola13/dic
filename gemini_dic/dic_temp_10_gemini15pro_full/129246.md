nocturnally:/nɒkˈtɜːnəli/ | adv. |夜间地；在夜里 | Happening, done, or active at night. | Bats fly nocturnally.  蝙蝠在夜间飞行。| synonym:nightly, by night | antonym:diurnally | adverb

nocturnal:/nɒkˈtɜːnl/ | adj. |夜间的；夜间活动的 |  Relating to or occurring at night. | Owls are nocturnal birds. 猫头鹰是夜行鸟类。| synonym:nightly, night-time | antonym:diurnal | adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin * nocturnalis *, from Latin nocturnus (“of the night”), from nox (“night”).
