contraction:/kənˈtrækʃən/| n.|收缩，缩写，宫缩|The contraction of the heart muscle pumps blood through the body. 心肌的收缩将血液泵送到全身。|例句：The contraction of the metal in cold weather caused the bridge to crack.  金属在寒冷天气下的收缩导致桥梁开裂。

例句：He uses contractions like "can't" and "won't" in informal writing. 他在非正式写作中使用像 "can't" 和 "won't" 这样的缩写。|近义词：shortening, abbreviation, reduction|反义词:expansion, dilation|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Latin *contractio(n-), from contrahere ‘draw together’.
