cantatory:/ˈkæntətɔːri/ | adj. |歌唱的，适合吟唱的 |Of, relating to, or characterized by singing or chanting; suitable for singing. |The cantatory style of the poem made it perfect for a musical setting. 这首诗的吟唱风格使它非常适合配乐。| The monk's cantatory voice resonated through the chapel.  僧侣吟唱的声音在教堂里回荡。 |近义词：melodic, musical, lyrical, chanting |反义词：spoken, instrumental, silent |词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *cantatorius*, from Latin *cantator* (“singer”), from *cantare* (“to sing”), frequentative of *canere* (“to sing”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱen₂- (“to sing”).
