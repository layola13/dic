blurrier:/ˈblɜːriər/ (US), /ˈblɜːrɪər/ (UK)| adj.|更模糊的，更不清楚的|Comparative form of blurry: more blurry.  Something that is blurrier is less distinct or clear than something else that is already blurry.|The photo became even blurrier when I zoomed in.  照片放大后变得更加模糊了。|The further away the car got, the blurrier its taillights became. 汽车越远，尾灯就越模糊。|Near synonyms:  hazier, fuzzier, less distinct, less clear, more indistinct, more unclear|Near antonyms: clearer, sharper, more distinct, more focused|adjective
