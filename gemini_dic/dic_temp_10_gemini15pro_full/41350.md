corinthianism:/kəˈrɪnθiəˌnɪzəm/| n.|科林斯主义；奢侈糜烂的生活方式；追求物质享受的风气|A lifestyle characterized by luxury, extravagance, and the pursuit of material pleasures, often associated with moral corruption.  It derives from the ancient Greek city of Corinth, which was known for its wealth and perceived decadence.|The rampant corinthianism of the Roman Empire ultimately contributed to its decline.  罗马帝国猖獗的享乐主义最终导致了它的衰落。|近义词：hedonism, debauchery, extravagance, luxury|反义词：asceticism, austerity, frugality|词性:noun
