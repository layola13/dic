non-irritant:/ˌnɒnˈɪrɪtənt/ /ˌnɑːnˈɪrɪtənt/| adj.|无刺激性的；不刺激的|Not causing irritation to the skin or other tissues.|This cream is non-irritant and suitable for sensitive skin.  这款面霜无刺激性，适合敏感肌肤。|Synonyms:  soothing, gentle, mild, bland|Antonyms: irritant, irritating|adjective

non-irritant:/ˌnɒnˈɪrɪtənt/ /ˌnɑːnˈɪrɪtənt/| n.|无刺激物|A substance that does not cause irritation.|The company developed a new non-irritant cleaning solution. 该公司开发了一种新型无刺激性清洁溶液。|Synonyms:  soothing agent, demulcent|Antonyms: irritant|noun
