lutidine:/ˈluːtɪdiːn/ | n. |  卢剔啶 | Any of several isomeric methylpyridines, especially 2,4-dimethylpyridine,  used as solvents and chemical intermediates. | Lutidine is used as a catalyst in organic synthesis.  卢剔啶在有机合成中用作催化剂。| Lutidine is often used in the production of pharmaceuticals. 卢剔啶常用于药物生产。 | synonyms: dimethylpyridine, methylpyridine | antonyms: N/A | noun
