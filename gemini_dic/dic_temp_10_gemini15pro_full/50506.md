deprivation:/ˌdɛprɪˈveɪʃən/ | n. | 剥夺，丧失，匮乏 | the damaging lack of material benefits considered to be basic necessities in a society |  Many children suffer from sleep deprivation. 许多孩子遭受睡眠不足。|  loss, dispossession, poverty | privilege, abundance | noun

deprive:/dɪˈpraɪv/ | vt. | 剥夺，使丧失 | prevent (a person or place) from having or using something | The city was deprived of electricity during the storm.  暴风雨期间，这座城市断电了。| dispossess, strip, divest | provide, give, endow | verb
