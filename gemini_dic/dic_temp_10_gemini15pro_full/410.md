abjugate:/ˈæbdʒʊɡeɪt/
verb
放弃；正式放弃；公开否认
to renounce or reject something formally and publicly
He abjured his religion. 他放弃了他的宗教信仰。
She abjured her former lifestyle. 她放弃了她以前的生活方式。
近义词: renounce, repudiate, forswear, recant, relinquish
反义词: affirm, uphold, embrace, maintain
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *abjugare*, from *ab-* (away from) + *jugare* (to yoke).  The sense of "removing from a yoke" led to the metaphorical meaning of "renouncing" or "casting off."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He abjugates his claim to the throne. = 他放弃了他对王位的要求。
She abjugates the use of violence. = 她公开宣布放弃使用暴力。

一般过去时:

They abjured their allegiance to the old regime. = 他们宣布放弃对旧政权的效忠。

现在完成时:
He has abjured his former beliefs. = 他已经放弃了他以前的信仰。
She has abjured all her worldly possessions. = 她已经放弃了她所有的世俗财产。

过去完成时:
By the time he reached the monastery, he had abjured all earthly pleasures. = 到他到达修道院的时候，他已经放弃了所有的尘世享乐。
She had abjured her past life before entering the convent. = 在进入修道院之前，她已经放弃了过去的生活。

将来时:

虚拟语气:
