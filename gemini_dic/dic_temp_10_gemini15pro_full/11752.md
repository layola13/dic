arose:/əˈroʊz/
verb
出现，发生，产生
came into being; emerged
A problem arose during the project. 项目进行中出现了一个问题。
The opportunity arose for her to travel abroad. 她得到了一个出国旅行的机会。
synonyms: emerged, appeared, occurred, developed
antonyms: subsided, disappeared, vanished
verb
