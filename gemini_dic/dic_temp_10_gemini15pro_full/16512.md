barite:/ˈbɛərˌaɪt/ /ˈberˌaɪt/| n. |重晶石|A dense, white, or colorless mineral, barium sulfate (BaSO4), used as a source of barium and in paints, drilling muds, and medical applications.|Barite is used as a weighting agent in drilling fluids. 重晶石在钻井液中用作加重剂。|synonyms: barytes, heavy spar|antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a mineral. Could consider minerals with opposite properties like lightness - e.g., pumice)|noun
