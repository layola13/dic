cadenza:/kəˈdɛntsə/ /kəˈdɛnzə/
noun
华彩乐段; 华彩段落
A virtuosic passage, usually improvised or written in an improvisatory style, performed near the end of a piece of music, often just before the final cadence.
The pianist dazzled the audience with a brilliant cadenza. 钢琴家以一段精彩的华彩乐段使观众眼花缭乱。
solo, flourish, embellishment
