ecologically:/ˌiːkəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ /ˌiːkəˈlɑːdʒɪkli/
adverb|生态地，从生态学的角度|In a way that relates to the relationships between living organisms and their environment.|例句：It's important to think ecologically and consider the impact our actions have on the environment.  从生态角度思考并考虑我们的行为对环境的影响很重要。

例句：The project was designed ecologically, minimizing its impact on local wildlife. 这个项目在设计上注重生态，最大限度地减少了对当地野生动物的影响。
|近义词：environmentally, sustainably|反义词: unsustainably
词性: 副词
