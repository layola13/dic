lipothymic:/ˌlɪpəˈθɪmɪk/| adj.| 昏厥的，快要昏倒的|Relating to or characterized by lipothymia, a feeling of faintness or light-headedness.| Example:  She felt lipothymic after standing in the sun for too long.  她在太阳底下站得太久，感到快要昏倒了。| Example: The patient reported experiencing lipothymic episodes several times a week.  病人报告说每周都会有几次快要昏倒的经历。|Near Synonyms: faint, dizzy, lightheaded, weak, swooning |Antonyms: alert, energetic, strong, vigorous| Adjective

Etymology: From French *lipothymie*  and directly from  Greek *leipein* "to leave, quit" + *thymos* "spirit, soul, mind, courage".
