amber:/ˈæmbər/ (US), /ˈæmbə(r)/ (UK)| n. adj.|琥珀；琥珀色|A hard translucent fossilized resin produced by extinct coniferous trees of the Tertiary period, typically yellowish in color. As an adjective, it describes something having the color of amber.|
例句：The necklace was made of Baltic amber. 这条项链是用波罗的海琥珀制成的。

例句：Her amber eyes shone in the firelight.  她琥珀色的眼睛在火光中闪耀。

近义词：gold, yellow, orange (for color)|
反义词: (no direct antonym for the substance)  blue, green, violet (for color)|
词性: noun, adjective
