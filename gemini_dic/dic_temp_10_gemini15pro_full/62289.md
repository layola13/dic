endall:/ˈɛndɔːl/
noun
终结；结束；万灵药
Something that is supposed to put an end to all problems or difficulties.
The new policy is supposed to be an endall to the traffic congestion.  这项新政策被认为是解决交通拥堵的万灵药。
synonyms: panacea, cure-all, solution, remedy
antonyms:  problem, difficulty, issue, complication
n.


endall:/ˈɛndɔːl/
adjective
最终的；决定性的
Serving as a final or decisive conclusion.  (This usage is less common.)
His final argument was endall. 他最后的论证是决定性的。
synonyms: final, conclusive, ultimate, decisive
antonyms:  temporary, provisional, interim, inconclusive
adj.
