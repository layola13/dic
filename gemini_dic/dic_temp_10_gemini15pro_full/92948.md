ideograph:/ˈaɪdiəɡræf/ | n. |表意文字，意符|A written symbol that represents an idea or object directly rather than a particular word or speech sound, as in Chinese characters. | Chinese characters are ideographs, representing whole words or concepts. 汉字是表意文字，代表整个单词或概念。| The ancient Egyptians used ideographs in their hieroglyphic writing system. 古埃及人在他们的象形文字书写系统中使用表意文字。 | synonym: logogram, symbol | antonym: phonogram | noun
