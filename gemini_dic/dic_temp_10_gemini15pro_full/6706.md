analog /ˈænəlɒɡ/ (US) /ˈænəlɔːɡ/ (UK)| adj. n.| 类似物；模拟的 |A device or system that represents changing values as continuously variable physical quantities.|The clock on the wall is analog, not digital.  墙上的钟是模拟的，不是数字的。|Near synonyms: comparable, similar, parallel |Antonyms: digital, discrete|adjective, noun

analog /ˈænəlɒɡ/ (US) /ˈænəlɔːɡ/ (UK)| n.| 类似物；模拟 |Something that is similar or comparable to something else.|His analog in the other company holds a similar position.  他在另一家公司的类似职位的人也担任着类似的职位。|Near synonyms: counterpart, equivalent, parallel |Antonyms: digital, discrete|noun
