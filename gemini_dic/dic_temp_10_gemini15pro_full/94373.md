implanting:/ɪmˈplɑːntɪŋ/
verb
植入，嵌入
The surgeon is implanting a new heart valve.  外科医生正在植入一个新的心脏瓣膜。
The idea was firmly implanted in his mind. 这个想法牢牢地印在他的脑海里。
近义词:inserting, embedding, grafting
反义词:removing, extracting, explanting
词性:v.


implant:/ɪmˈplɑːnt/
noun
植入物
The patient received a cochlear implant. 病人接受了人工耳蜗植入。
verb
植入，嵌入
The doctor implanted a pacemaker in the patient's chest. 医生在病人的胸腔植入了一台起搏器。
近义词:(n.) graft, prosthesis; (v.) insert, embed
反义词:(v.) remove, extract
词性:n. v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *implantare, from in- + plantare “to plant”
