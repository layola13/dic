ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + dispersal.  Dispersal comes from the Late Middle English *dispersen*, from the Old French *disperser*, from the Latin *dispersus*, past participle of *dispergere* "to scatter," from *dis-* "apart, asunder" + *spargere* "to strew, scatter."
