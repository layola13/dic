detractor:/dɪˈtræktər/| n.|诽谤者，诋毁者|Someone who disparages or belittles something or someone.|The project's detractors claimed it was a waste of public money.  这个项目的诋毁者声称这是浪费公共资金。|Synonyms: critic, disparager, slanderer, backbiter|Antonyms: supporter, advocate, proponent|noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *detractor, from Latin detractus (“drawn away”), past participle of detrahere (“to draw away, detract”), from de- (“away from”) + trahere (“to draw, pull”).
