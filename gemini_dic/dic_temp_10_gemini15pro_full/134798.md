nonmicroscopic:/ˌnɒnˌmaɪkrəˈskɒpɪk/ | adj. | 非微观的，肉眼可见的 | Not microscopic; visible to the naked eye. |  The damage to the car was nonmicroscopic; a huge dent was clearly visible. 汽车的损坏是非微观的；一个巨大的凹痕清晰可见。| The tumor was nonmicroscopic and easily detected during the physical exam.  肿瘤是非微观的，在体检中很容易被发现。| 近义词: macroscopic, visible, discernible | 反义词: microscopic, invisible | 词性: 形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non < PIE *ne "not"
micro- (prefix): from Greek mikrós "small"
scopic (suffix): from Greek -skopikos, from skopeîn "to look at"
