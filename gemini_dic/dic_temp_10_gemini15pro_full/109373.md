lensless:/ˈlɛnzləs/| adj.|无透镜的|Without a lens; operating without the use of a lens. |Scientists have developed a lensless camera that can capture images using computational imaging. 科学家们开发了一种无透镜相机，可以使用计算成像技术捕捉图像。|A lensless telescope uses a coded aperture instead of a lens to gather light. 无透镜望远镜使用编码孔径而不是透镜来收集光线。|Near synonyms:  aperture-based, computational|Antonyms: lensed, refractive|adjective
