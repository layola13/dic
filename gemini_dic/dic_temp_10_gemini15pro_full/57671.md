drawgloves:/ˈdrɔːɡlʌvz/| n. |  绘图手套 | Gloves designed to prevent smudging or friction when drawing or using a graphics tablet.  | Example:  He uses drawgloves to avoid smudging his charcoal drawings. 他使用绘图手套以避免弄脏他的炭笔画。
