passed-over:/ˌpɑːst ˈoʊvər/ | adj. | 被忽略的，被跳过的 | Ignored, overlooked, especially for promotion or other benefits.  Not chosen. | Example 1:  She felt passed over when her colleagues were promoted, and she wasn't. 她觉得自己被忽略了，因为她的同事都升职了，而她没有。

Example 2:  He was passed over for the lead role in the play, which was a huge disappointment. 他没有被选中出演戏剧的主角，这让他非常失望。 | 近义词: overlooked, neglected, bypassed, rejected | 反义词: chosen, selected, promoted | 词性: 形容词
