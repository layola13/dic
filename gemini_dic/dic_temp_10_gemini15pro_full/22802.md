bosman:/ˈbɒzmən/| n.|博斯曼规则；博斯曼法案；博斯曼判例|The Bosman ruling allows football players in the European Union to move to another club at the end of their contract without a transfer fee being paid.|博斯曼规则允许欧盟的足球运动员在合同结束后无需支付转会费即可转投其他俱乐部。|Synonyms: ruling, law, precedent|Antonyms: restriction, prohibition|noun
