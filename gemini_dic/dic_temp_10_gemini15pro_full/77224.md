gastritis:/ɡæˈstraɪtɪs/
n.
胃炎
Inflammation of the lining of the stomach.
He was diagnosed with gastritis after experiencing persistent stomach pain. 他持续的胃痛之后被诊断为胃炎。
The doctor prescribed medication to soothe his gastritis. 医生开了药来缓解他的胃炎。
近义词:inflammation of the stomach
反义词:(none -  gastritis is a condition, not a descriptive quality with an opposite)
词性:noun
