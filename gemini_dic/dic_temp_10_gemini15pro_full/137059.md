nonstorages:/ˌnɒnˈstɔːrɪdʒɪz/ | n. | 非存储器，非存储设备 | Devices or systems that do not store data persistently. This could refer to components like RAM or temporary buffers that lose data when power is off, or to systems designed to process information without retaining it long-term. |  The system uses nonstorages for real-time processing, discarding data after analysis.  该系统使用非存储设备进行实时处理，分析后丢弃数据。

| Near synonyms: volatile memory, temporary storage, transient storage | Antonyms: storage, memory, persistent storage | noun |
