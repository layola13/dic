divot:/ˈdɪvət/| n.|草皮，草皮块，(高尔夫球)被击起的草皮|A piece of turf dug out of the ground, especially by a golf club or by horses' hooves.|He took a mighty swing and sent a divot flying. 他用力一挥，打飞了一块草皮。|synonyms: turf, sod, clod|antonyms: (none specific - perhaps 'smooth surface')|noun

divot:/ˈdɪvət/| n.|凹痕，坑|A small hollow or indentation in a surface.|The impact of the golf ball left a divot in the green. 高尔夫球的撞击在果岭上留下了一个小坑。|synonyms: dent, hollow, indentation|antonyms: bump, bulge, protrusion|noun
