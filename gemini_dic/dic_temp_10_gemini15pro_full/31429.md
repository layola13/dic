chariot:/ˈtʃæriət/| n.|战车；（尤指古代的）双轮马车|A two-wheeled horse-drawn vehicle used in ancient times for war or racing.  |The charioteer skillfully maneuvered his chariot around the track.  驭手熟练地驾驶着他的战车绕着赛道行驶。|Synonyms: carriage, wagon, coach|Antonyms: car, truck|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *charriot*, diminutive of char* (modern French *char*), from Late Latin *carrus* ("two-wheeled cart"), from Gaulish *karros*.
