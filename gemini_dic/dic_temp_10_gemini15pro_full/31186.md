changeling:/ˈtʃeɪndʒlɪŋ/| n. | 替身；被替换的孩子；反复无常的人|A child believed to have been secretly substituted by fairies for the parents' real child; a person or thing that is mysteriously different from what it seems to be.|例句：In folklore, a changeling is a fairy child left in place of a human child. 在民间传说中，妖精会用自己的孩子替换走人类的孩子。

例句：Her moods were so unpredictable, she seemed like a changeling. 她的情绪如此难以捉摸，就像一个被替换了的孩子。|近义词：substitute, imposter, double|反义词：original, genuine|词性:名词
