nonforfeitures:/ˌnɑːnfɔːrˈfɪtʃərz/
|n.| 不可丧失权益；（保险）不失效条款|A clause in an insurance policy that protects the policyholder's investment in the policy from being lost even if premium payments are missed or discontinued. It guarantees a reduced paid-up policy or a cash value surrender option.|Example: The nonforfeiture options on my life insurance policy include cash surrender, reduced paid-up insurance, and extended term insurance. 我的寿险保单的不可丧失权益选项包括现金退保、减少的缴清保险和延长期限保险。
|Example: Understanding the nonforfeiture provisions is crucial when choosing a life insurance policy. 在选择人寿保险单时，了解不丧失权益条款至关重要。
|Synonyms: nonforfeitable values, nonforfeitable options, guaranteed values|Antonyms: forfeiture, lapse|noun
