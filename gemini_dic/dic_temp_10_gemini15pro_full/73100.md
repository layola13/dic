foppery:/ˈfɒpəri/ /ˈfɑːpəri/| n. |愚蠢的行为；虚荣；矫揉造作；纨绔子弟|Foppery is the behavior or appearance of a fop, someone who is excessively vain and concerned with their appearance and manners.  It refers to silly or frivolous behavior, often associated with excessive attention to fashion and trivial matters.| He was known for his foppery and extravagant spending habits. 他以其纨绔作风和奢侈的生活习惯而闻名。| The king was surrounded by courtiers more interested in foppery than in affairs of state. 国王身边都是些更关心穿着打扮而不是国家大事的朝臣。|近义词：vanity, affectation, pretentiousness, dandyism|反义词：modesty, humility, simplicity|词性:noun

Etymology:
From French *fop*, a fool.
