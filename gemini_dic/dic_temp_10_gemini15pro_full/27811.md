cannons:/ˈkænənz/| n.|加农炮，大炮|Large-caliber artillery pieces, typically mounted on a carriage.|The cannons roared to life, shaking the ground. 加农炮轰鸣着，震动着大地。|The fort was defended by powerful cannons. 这座堡垒由强大的加农炮保卫。|近义词：artillery, ordnance|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可考虑peace, diplomacy)|词性:名词

Etymology: From Old French *canon* (modern French *canon*), from Italian *cannone*, augmentative of *canna* "reed, tube", from Latin *canna* "reed, cane".
