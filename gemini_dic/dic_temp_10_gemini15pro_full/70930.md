firebrick:/ˈfaɪərbrɪk/| n.|耐火砖|A brick capable of withstanding high temperatures, typically used in lining furnaces, fireplaces, and other high-heat applications.|The pizza oven was built with firebricks to retain heat.  披萨烤炉是用耐火砖建造的，以保持热量。|synonyms: refractory brick, fireclay brick|antonyms: common brick|noun

firebrick:/ˈfaɪərbrɪk/| adj.|耐火的|Made of firebrick; able to withstand high temperatures.|The firebrick lining of the furnace prevented heat loss.  熔炉的耐火砖衬里防止了热量损失。|synonyms: refractory, heat-resistant|antonyms: non-refractory|adjective
