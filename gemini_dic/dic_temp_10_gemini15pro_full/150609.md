paperweights:/ˈpeɪpərˌweɪts/| n. |镇纸；压纸器|A small, heavy object placed on papers to prevent them from being blown away or disordered.|She used a glass paperweight shaped like a cat to keep her notes in place. 她用一个猫形的玻璃镇纸压住她的笔记。|Synonyms: paperweight, desk weight |Antonyms: None|Noun
