chavish:/ˈtʃɑːvɪʃ/| adj.|俗气的；粗俗的；暴发户似的；缺乏教养的|Displaying or characteristic of a chav, especially in terms of appearance, behavior, or taste.  Associated with ostentatious displays of wealth, designer clothing (often counterfeit), and a perceived lack of sophistication or refinement.|He wore a chavish gold chain. 他戴着一条俗气的金链子。|Near synonyms: tacky, gaudy, flashy, vulgar, ostentatious, tasteless, unrefined, uncouth, common, lowbrow, naff (British), yobbish (British)|Near antonyms: sophisticated, refined, elegant, tasteful, classy, understated|Adjective
