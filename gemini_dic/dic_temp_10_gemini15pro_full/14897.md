azaleas: /əˈzeɪliəz/
n.
杜鹃花 (复数)
A group of flowering shrubs in the genus Rhododendron, known for their vibrant and colorful blossoms, often blooming in spring.
The azaleas in my garden are a riot of color.  我花园里的杜鹃花五彩缤纷。
近义词：rhododendrons, shrubs
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑非开花植物) non-flowering plants
词性: noun


azalea: /əˈzeɪliə/
n.
杜鹃花 (单数)
A flowering shrub in the genus Rhododendron, known for its vibrant and colorful blossoms, often blooming in spring.
That azalea bush is particularly beautiful this year. 那棵杜鹃花灌木今年格外漂亮。
近义词：rhododendron, shrub
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑非开花植物) non-flowering plant
词性: noun

词源: From the Greek word *azaleos*, meaning "dry," likely referring to the plant's preference for well-drained soil.
