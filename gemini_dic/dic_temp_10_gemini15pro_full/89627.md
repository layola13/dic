hook:/hʊk/| n. vt. vi.|钩，钩状物；陷阱，圈套；（拳击中的）勾拳；吸引人的东西|A curved piece of metal or other hard material used for catching, holding, or pulling something. Also, a method or trick used to attract attention or interest.|The fish swallowed the hook. 鱼吞下了鱼钩。|近义词：snag, catch, clasp, grapple, lure, attract|反义词：release, repel|词性:noun, verb

hook:/hʊk/| vt. vi.|钩住，挂住；吸引，引诱；弯成钩状|To catch, fasten, or pull something with a hook. Also, to attract or entice someone.|The coat was hooked on a nail. 外套挂在钉子上了。|近义词：snag, catch, fasten, secure, entice, lure|反义词：unhook, detach, repel|词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hōc, from Proto-Germanic *hakaz, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)keh₂ḱ- (“to bend, crook”). Cognate with Old Norse haki (“hook”), Dutch haak (“hook”), German Haken (“hook”).
