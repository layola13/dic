antiparallel:/ˌæntiˈpærəlel/ | adj. | 反向平行的，反平行的 | Running in opposite directions, but parallel.  | Example 1: The two strands of DNA are antiparallel.  两条 DNA 链是反向平行的。

Example 2: The cars were parked antiparallel to each other.  这些车彼此反向平行停放。 | Near Synonyms: oppositely directed, inversely parallel | Antonyms: parallel, aligned | Word type: adjective

antiparallel:/ˌæntiˈpærəlel/ | n. | 反向平行 | The state of being antiparallel. | Example 1: The antiparallel arrangement of the strands is crucial for DNA replication. 链的反向平行排列对于 DNA 复制至关重要。

Example 2: The antiparallel nature of the magnetic fields caused them to cancel each other out. 磁场的反平行特性导致它们相互抵消。 | Near Synonyms: opposite orientation | Antonyms: parallelism, alignment | Word type: noun
