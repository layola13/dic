nonaccumulating:/ˌnɒnəˈkjuːmjəleɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非累积的，不累加的
Not accumulating or increasing in quantity or intensity over time; not cumulative.
This is a nonaccumulating type of pension plan. 这是一种非累积型养老金计划。
The discount is nonaccumulating, meaning you can't combine it with other offers.  这个折扣是非累积的，意味着你不能把它和其他优惠一起使用。
近义词:noncumulative
反义词:accumulating, cumulative
词性:adjective

词源:
non- (prefix) + accumulating (present participle of accumulate)

"non-"  a prefix meaning "not," freely used as an English formative, usually with native or Latin elements.

"accumulate" From Latin accumulare "to heap up, amass,"  from ad- "to" (see ad-) + cumulare "to heap," from cumulus "heap."
