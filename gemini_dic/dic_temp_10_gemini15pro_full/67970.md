facing:/ˈfeɪsɪŋ/ (US), /ˈfeɪsɪŋ/ (UK)| prep. adj. n.|面向，面对；覆盖物|The house is facing the sea. 这房子面朝大海。|Synonyms: confronting, opposite, toward, fronting|Antonyms: backing, behind|Preposition

facing:/ˈfeɪsɪŋ/ (US), /ˈfeɪsɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|面向的；即将发生的|He is facing a difficult challenge. 他面临着一个艰巨的挑战。|Synonyms: impending, upcoming, forthcoming|Antonyms: past, bygone|Adjective

facing:/ˈfeɪsɪŋ/ (US), /ˈfeɪsɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|饰面；覆盖物|The building has a stone facing. 这栋建筑有石材饰面。|Synonyms: veneer, cladding, covering|Antonyms: interior, inside|Noun

Etymology:  From the Old French *facier* "to face, confront," from the Latin *facies* "face, appearance, surface."
