electrocoat: /ɪˈlɛktrəˌkoʊt/ | n. v. | 电泳涂层，电泳涂装 |  A process of coating metal by electrodeposition, where the metal to be coated acts as an electrode in a bath containing paint particles. The paint particles are attracted to the metal and form a uniform coating. | The car body was electrocoated to prevent rust.  车身经过电泳涂装以防止生锈。|  The company electrocoats all its metal parts. 公司对其所有金属零件进行电泳涂装。 | 近义词:  electrodeposition, electrophoretic deposition | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for the process itself) | 词性: noun, verb

electrocoat: /ɪˈlɛktrəˌkoʊt/ | n. | 电泳漆 |  The paint or coating material used in the electrocoating process. |  The electrocoat provided a durable and even finish.  这种电泳漆提供了耐用且均匀的表面。 | The new electrocoat is more environmentally friendly. 新的电泳漆更加环保。 | 近义词:  electrophoretic paint, e-coat | 反义词: (No direct antonym for the material itself) | 词性: noun
