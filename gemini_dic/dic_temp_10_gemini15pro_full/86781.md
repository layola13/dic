helvite:/ˈhɛlvaɪt/| n.|铍锰矿，赫尔文石|A relatively rare mineral, a beryllium manganese silicate, typically found in granite pegmatites. It is often yellowish or greenish-brown in color.|Helvite was found in the granite pegmatite. 在花岗伟晶岩中发现了铍锰矿。|Synonyms: danalite, genthelvite|Antonyms: N/A|noun
