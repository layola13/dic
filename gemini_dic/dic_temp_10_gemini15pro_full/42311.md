counter-plea: /ˈkaʊntərˌpliː/ /ˈkaʊntərˌpliː/ | n. | 反驳，抗辩 | A plea made in reply to another plea, especially one made by a defendant in answer to the plaintiff's replication. |  The defendant offered a counter-plea to the charges, claiming self-defense.  被告对指控提出了反驳，声称是自卫。

The prosecution anticipated the counter-plea and had prepared evidence to refute it.  控方预料到了反驳，并准备了证据来反驳它。 | synonym: defense, rejoinder, rebuttal, reply | antonym:  acceptance, concession, admission | noun
