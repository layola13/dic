heterodoxness:/ˌhɛtərəˈdɑːksnəs/| n.|异端性，非正统性|The state or quality of being heterodox; unorthodoxy.|His heterodoxness in religious matters led to his excommunication from the church. 他的宗教异端导致他被逐出教会。|Synonyms: unorthodoxy, dissent, heresy, nonconformity|Antonyms: orthodoxy, conformity, conventionality|noun


heterodox:/ˈhɛtərəˌdɑːks/| adj.|异端的，非正统的|Not conforming with accepted beliefs or doctrines.|Her heterodox views on education surprised the conservative school board. 她对教育的非正统观点让保守的学校董事会感到惊讶。|Synonyms: unconventional, unorthodox, dissenting, heretical|Antonyms: orthodox, conventional, conforming|adjective
