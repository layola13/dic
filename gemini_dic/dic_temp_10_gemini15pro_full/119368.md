microcook:/ˈmaɪkroʊkʊk/| v. n.|微波炉烹调，微波炉食物|To cook or heat food in a microwave oven.  It can also refer to food cooked in a microwave oven.|例句：I'll microcook the leftovers from dinner. 我会用微波炉加热晚餐的剩菜。

例句：I prefer to microcook vegetables to preserve their nutrients. 我更喜欢用微波炉烹饪蔬菜以保留其营养。|近义词：microwave, nuke|反义词: bake, roast|词性:动词, 名词
