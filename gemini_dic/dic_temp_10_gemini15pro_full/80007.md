go-out:/ɡəʊ aʊt/ (UK) /ɡoʊ aʊt/ (US)| phrasal verb |外出；熄灭；过时；约会|To leave one's home for a social activity, to become extinguished, to become unfashionable, to have a romantic relationship with someone.|例句：They're going out for dinner tonight. 他们今晚要出去吃晚饭。|近义词：date, socialize, exit, leave|反义词:stay in, come in, ignite|词性:动词短语

go-out:/ɡəʊ aʊt/ (UK) /ɡoʊ aʊt/ (US)| n. |外出；郊游|An instance of leaving one's home for a social activity.|例句：We had a fun go-out to the beach last weekend. 上周末我们去海滩玩得很开心。|近义词：outing, excursion, trip|反义词:staying in|词性:名词
