evictors:/ɪˈvɪktərz/
|n.|驱逐者，赶出者|People who evict someone from a property.  Those who force others to leave a place, often due to non-payment of rent or violation of a lease agreement.|The evictors arrived early in the morning to remove the tenants.  驱逐人员一大早就来把房客赶走了。|Synonyms: expellers, ejectors, ousts|Antonyms: protectors, defenders|Noun
