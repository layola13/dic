eulachon:/ˈjuːləkɒn/ (US) /ˈjuːləkən/ (UK)| n. |油胡瓜鱼，烛鱼|A small anadromous fish (Thaleichthys pacificus) of the smelt family, found in the North Pacific. It is rich in oil and was historically used by Native Americans as a source of food and oil for lamps.|例句：Eulachon are an important food source for many First Nations communities. 油胡瓜鱼是许多原住民社区的重要食物来源。

例句：The eulachon run is a spectacular natural event. 油胡瓜鱼洄游是一个壮观的自然事件。|近义词：candlefish, oolichan, smelt|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
