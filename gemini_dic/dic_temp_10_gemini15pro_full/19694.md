bilharzia:/ˌbɪlˈhɑːrzɪə/ (US) /ˌbɪlˈhɑːziə/ (UK)| n. |血吸虫病|A parasitic disease caused by blood flukes (schistosomes) of the genus *Schistosoma*.  It is spread by contact with contaminated fresh water.| He contracted bilharzia while swimming in the lake. 他在湖里游泳时感染了血吸虫病。|Synonyms: schistosomiasis|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

bilharziasis:/ˌbɪlhɑːrˈzaɪəsɪs/|n.|血吸虫病| Another term for schistosomiasis, a parasitic disease caused by blood flukes. | Bilharziasis is a serious health concern in many tropical countries. 血吸虫病是许多热带国家的一个严重的健康问题。|Synonyms: schistosomiasis|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
