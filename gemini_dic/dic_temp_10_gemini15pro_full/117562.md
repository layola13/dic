megaderm:/ˈmɛɡədɜːrm/| n. |巨耳蝠|A bat of the family Megadermatidae, characterized by large ears and a nose-leaf.|例句：The megaderm is a nocturnal hunter. 巨耳蝠是一种夜间活动的猎食者。

例句：Megaderms are found in tropical regions of Africa, Asia, and Australia. 巨耳蝠分布在非洲、亚洲和澳大利亚的热带地区。|近义词：false vampire bat, leaf-nosed bat|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a specific bat genus)|词性: Noun
