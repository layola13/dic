overtimidness:/ˌoʊvərˈtɪmɪdnəs/| n.| 过分胆怯，过度羞怯|Excessive shyness or timidity.| Her overtimidness prevented her from speaking up in class. 她过度胆怯，以至于不敢在课堂上发言。|近义词：shyness, timidity, diffidence|反义词：boldness, confidence, assertiveness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From over- + timidness.  "Over-" acts as a prefix indicating "excessive" or "too much". "Timidness" derives from the adjective "timid," which ultimately comes from the Latin "timidus" meaning "fearful."
