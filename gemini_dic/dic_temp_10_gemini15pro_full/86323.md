hebrician:/hɪˈbriːʃən/
noun
希伯来人学者，精通希伯来语和希伯来文化的人
A scholar of Hebrew and Hebrew culture.
He is a renowned hebrician, known for his expertise in ancient Hebrew texts. 他是一位著名的希伯来学者，以其在古代希伯来文本方面的专业知识而闻名。
Hebraisist, Hebrew scholar
(No clear antonym)
