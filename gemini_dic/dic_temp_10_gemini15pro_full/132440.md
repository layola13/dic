nondepletory:/ˌnɒndɪˈpliːtəri/
adjective
非消耗性的，非递减的
Not depleting; not reducing the amount of something available.
Using nondepletory energy sources like solar and wind power is crucial for a sustainable future. 使用像太阳能和风能这样的非消耗性能源对可持续的未来至关重要。
Farmers are exploring nondepletory farming methods to maintain soil health. 农民正在探索非消耗性耕作方法来保持土壤健康。
近义词: renewable, sustainable, inexhaustible
反义词: depleting, exhaustible, nonrenewable
词性: adjective

词源:
From non- (not) + deplete (to reduce the amount of something) + -ory (forming adjectives).  "Deplete" comes from the Latin word "deplēre," meaning "to empty out."
