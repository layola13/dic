grandee:/ɡrænˈdiː/
noun
大人物，权贵，贵族
a person of high rank, especially in Spain or Portugal.
The Spanish grandee looked down upon the commoners.  这位西班牙贵族看不起平民。
The aging grandee still held considerable influence in the court. 年迈的权贵在宫廷中仍然拥有相当大的影响力。
synonyms: nobleman, aristocrat, lord, magnate
antonyms: commoner, peasant, plebeian
