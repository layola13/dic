mismatch:/ˌmɪsˈmætʃ/ | n. v. | 不匹配，错配；使不匹配 |A mismatch occurs when two things that should go together don't fit or work well together. | The curtains were a complete mismatch for the carpet. 窗帘和地毯完全不搭。| synonyms: disparity, incompatibility, incongruity | antonyms: match, compatibility, harmony | noun

mismatch:/ˌmɪsˈmætʃ/ | v. | 使不匹配，错配 |To mismatch is to pair two things that are not suitable or compatible with each other. |  The coach mismatched the players, resulting in a crushing defeat. 教练错误地安排了球员，导致惨败。 | synonyms: mispair, misjoin | antonyms: match, pair, coordinate | verb
