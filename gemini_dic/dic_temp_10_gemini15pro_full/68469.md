familiarizing:/fəˈmɪliəˌraɪzɪŋ/
verb
使熟悉，使了解
The training program is designed to familiarize new employees with the company's procedures.  这个培训项目旨在使新员工熟悉公司的程序。
present participle of familiarize
familiarizing oneself with something
使自己熟悉某事
Before traveling to a new country, it's important to familiarize yourself with the local customs.  在去一个新的国家旅行之前，让自己熟悉当地的风俗习惯是很重要的。
Synonyms: acquainting, orienting, briefing
Antonyms:  disorienting, confusing
verb (present participle)
