ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lustihede*, from *lusti* (“pleasant, joyful, beautiful, delicious”) + *-hede* (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality). *Lusti* comes from Old English *lust* (“pleasure, delight; desire”) (whence also *list* (“desire”)), from Proto-Germanic *lustiz* (“joyful”), from Proto-Indo-European *lewdʰ- (“to grow”).
