avalonia:/ˌævəˈloʊniə/ | n. | 阿瓦隆尼亚 |A mythical island paradise in Arthurian legend, often associated with healing and rebirth. It is sometimes said to be the place where King Arthur went after his death or injury at the Battle of Camlann. |  Legend says King Arthur went to Avalon to heal after the Battle of Camlann. 传说亚瑟王在卡姆兰战役后去了阿瓦隆疗伤。| synonyms: paradise, utopia, Elysium | antonyms: hell, inferno | noun
