neurine:/ˈnjʊəriːn/ /ˈnʊəˌriːn/
n.
神经碱；胆碱
A poisonous, syrupy ptomaine, C5H13NO, formed by the decomposition of lecithin and other phosphatides, and also obtainable synthetically; trimethylvinylammonium hydroxide.
Neurine is a toxic chemical formed during the putrefaction of biological tissues. 神经碱是一种在生物组织腐败过程中形成的有毒化学物质。
Neurine poisoning can cause muscle weakness and respiratory distress. 神经碱中毒会导致肌肉无力和呼吸窘迫。
近义词: choline, amanitine, muscarine

词性: noun
