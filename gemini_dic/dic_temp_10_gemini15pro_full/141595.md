oppugn: /əˈpjuːn/ | vt. | 质疑，挑战，反对 | To call into question; to challenge as false or wrong. |  The lawyer oppugned the witness's testimony. 律师质疑证人的证词。

The senator oppugned the president's decision to veto the bill. 参议员反对总统否决该法案的决定。

| 近义词：challenge, question, dispute, contest, gainsay | 反义词：support, uphold, defend, endorse, champion | 词性：动词
