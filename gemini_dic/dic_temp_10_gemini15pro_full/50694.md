dermatophyte:/ˌdɜːrmætəˈfaɪt/ | n. | 皮肤癣菌 | A fungus that causes skin diseases, especially ringworm, athlete's foot, and jock itch. |  Ringworm is caused by a dermatophyte.  癣是由皮肤癣菌引起的。 | synonyms: tinea, ringworm fungus | antonyms: N/A | noun
