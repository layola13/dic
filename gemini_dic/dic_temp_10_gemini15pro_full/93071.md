词源：idolizer comes from the verb "idolize," which in turn comes from "idol" + "-ize."  "Idol" comes from the Old French "idole," ultimately from the Greek "eidolon" (image, phantom).  The suffix "-ize" forms verbs meaning "to make or cause to be" or "to treat or regard as."
