monogenetic:/ˌmɑːnədʒəˈnɛtɪk/ /ˌmɒnədʒəˈnɛtɪk/| adj.|单基因的；单成因的|Having a single origin; developed from a single source.  Relating to or arising from a single gene.|例句：The disease is thought to be monogenetic.  这种疾病被认为是单基因引起的。|例句：The volcano is monogenetic, formed by a single eruption. 这座火山是单成因的，由一次喷发形成。|近义词：monogenic, unigenic|反义词: polygenetic, multigenic|词性:形容词
monogenetic:/ˌmɑːnədʒəˈnɛtɪk/ /ˌmɒnədʒəˈnɛtɪk/| n.|单基因性|The state or quality of being monogenetic.|例句：Monogenetic inheritance is simpler to study than polygenetic inheritance. 单基因遗传比多基因遗传更容易研究。|例句：The monogenetic nature of the volcano explains its symmetrical cone shape. 这座火山的单成因特性解释了它对称的锥形形状。|近义词：monogeny|反义词: polygeny, multigeny|词性:名词
