familiarising:/fəˈmɪliəraɪzɪŋ/
verb
使熟悉；使了解
The purpose of the training is to familiarize new employees with the company's procedures.  培训的目的是让新员工熟悉公司的流程。
Present participle of familiarize.  familiarize的现在分词形式。
Synonyms: acquainting, introducing, orienting
Antonyms:  unfamiliarizing,  estranging
verb
