cul-de-sac: /ˈkʌl də sæk/ /ˈkʊl də sæk/
cul-de-sac: n.
cul-de-sac: 死胡同，死路，困境
cul-de-sac: A street or passage closed at one end.  A situation in which further progress is impossible.
cul-de-sac:  The car chase ended in a cul-de-sac. 汽车追逐在一条死胡同里结束了。
cul-de-sac:  The negotiations reached a cul-de-sac. 谈判陷入了僵局。
cul-de-sac: 近义词: dead end, blind alley, impasse
cul-de-sac: 反义词: through road, thoroughfare
