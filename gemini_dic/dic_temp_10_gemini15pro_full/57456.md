draffy:/ˈdræfi/| adj.|通风的，有穿堂风的|A draffy room or building has currents of cold air blowing through it.|The old house was draffy and cold in the winter. 冬天这栋老房子通风，很冷。|例句：The hallway was draffy and chilly. 走廊里通风很冷。

例句：Close the window, it's draffy in here. 关上窗户，这里有穿堂风。|近义词：airy, breezy, draughty|反义词：airtight, stuffy|词性:形容词

词源: From draft + -y.  "draft" in this sense refers to a current of air.
