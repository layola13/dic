moringa:/məˈrɪŋɡə/| n. |辣木|A fast-growing, drought-resistant tree of the family Moringaceae, native to the Indian subcontinent, cultivated in the tropics, and having edible leaves, pods, and seeds rich in protein, vitamins, and minerals.|Moringa leaves are a good source of protein. 辣木叶是蛋白质的良好来源。|Moringa is often used in traditional medicine. 辣木常用于传统医学。|Synonyms: drumstick tree, horseradish tree|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
