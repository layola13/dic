nondomesticating:/ˌnɒn.dəˈmes.tɪ.keɪ.tɪŋ/ | adj. | 非驯化的，未驯化的 | Not adapted to living with humans; not tamed or domesticated. |  Wild animals like wolves are nondomesticating. 野生动物像狼一样是非驯化的。 | The nondomesticating nature of the fox makes it unsuitable as a pet. 狐狸未驯化的本性使其不适合作为宠物。 |  wild, feral, untamed | domestic, tame | adjective
