minimalistic:/ˌmɪnɪməˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 极简主义的；简约的 | Characterized by extreme spareness and simplicity. |  She decorated her apartment in a minimalistic style, with only a few pieces of furniture and a neutral color palette. 她用极简主义风格装饰了她的公寓，只有一些家具和中性色调。

|  The website has a minimalistic design, making it easy to navigate.  这个网站采用了极简主义的设计，使其易于浏览。 | 近义词: simple, spare, austere, uncluttered | 反义词: ornate, elaborate, cluttered, maximalist | 词性: adjective

minimalistic:/ˌmɪnɪməˈlɪstɪk/ | n. | 极简主义者 | A person who advocates or practices minimalism. |  He's a minimalistic who believes in owning only what he needs. 他是一个极简主义者，只相信拥有他需要的东西。| 近义词: minimalist | 反义词: maximalist | 词性: noun (rarely used)
