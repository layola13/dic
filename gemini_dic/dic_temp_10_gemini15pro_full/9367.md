antipyretic:/ˌæntipaɪˈrɛtɪk/| adj. n.|退热的，退热药|Reduces fever; an agent that reduces fever.|例句：The doctor prescribed an antipyretic for her high fever. 医生给她开了退烧药。

例句：Aspirin is a common antipyretic. 阿司匹林是一种常见的退烧药。|近义词：antifebrile, febrifuge|反义词:  N/A (No direct antonym, but one might consider words related to causing fever, like pyrogenic)|词性: adjective, noun
