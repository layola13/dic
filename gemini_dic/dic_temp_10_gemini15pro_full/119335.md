microbrewery:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌbruːəri/ | n. |小型酿酒厂；精酿啤酒厂 | A brewery that produces a small amount of beer, typically less than 15,000 barrels annually.  |  Example 1:  The town boasts a popular microbrewery that attracts tourists from all over.  这个小镇拥有一家很受欢迎的小型酿酒厂，吸引了来自各地的游客。

Example 2:  He started his own microbrewery after years of home brewing. 他在多年家庭酿造后开办了自己的小型酿酒厂。 |  Synonyms: craft brewery, brewpub | Antonyms: macrobrewery, large brewery | noun
