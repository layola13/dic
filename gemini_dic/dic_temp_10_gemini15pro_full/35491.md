coactor:/koʊˈæktər/| n. |合作者，共同行动者|A person who acts jointly with another or others; a fellow actor.|例句：She and her coactor worked tirelessly on the project. 她和她的合作者不知疲倦地致力于这个项目。

例句：The coactors in the play developed a strong bond.  这出戏里的合作演员建立了牢固的联系。|近义词：collaborator, partner, associate|反义词: opponent, adversary|词性:noun

Etymology: From Latin co- (together) + actor (actor, doer).
