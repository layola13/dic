opisthotic:/ˌɒpɪsˈθɒtɪk/ | adj. n.| 后耳骨的，后耳骨 |Relating to the opisthotic bone, a bone in the skull of some vertebrates, forming part of the otic capsule and located posterior to the ear.  The opisthotic bone itself. |The opisthotic bone is a significant element in the fish skull. 后耳骨是鱼类颅骨中的重要组成部分。|  The researcher identified the fossil by its distinctive opisthotic features. 研究人员通过其独特的耳后特征识别出这块化石。|Synonyms:  tympanic, periotic (when referring to the region), auditory| Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, as it's an anatomical term.  Could potentially use "anterior otic" for an opposite location.) | noun, adjective
