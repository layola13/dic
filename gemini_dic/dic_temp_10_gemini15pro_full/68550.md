fanfaron:/ˌfænˈfærən/ | n. | 自夸者，吹牛大王 | A braggart; a swaggering, boastful person. | He was a fanfaron, always boasting about his achievements. 他是个吹牛大王，总是吹嘘自己的成就。|  He was such a fanfaron, bragging about his non-existent yacht. 他真是个爱吹牛的人，吹嘘着他根本不存在的游艇。 | 近义词: braggart, boaster, swaggerer | 反义词: modest person, humble person | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *fanfaron*, from Italian *fanfarone*, ultimately of uncertain origin. Possibly related to *fanfare*.
