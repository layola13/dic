hexagon:/ˈhɛksəɡɒn/ (UK) /ˈhɛksəɡɑːn/ (US)| n.|六边形，六角形|A plane figure with six straight sides and angles.|A bee's honeycomb is made up of hexagons. 蜜蜂的蜂巢是由六边形组成的。|The board game is played on a hexagon-shaped grid.  这个棋盘游戏是在一个六边形的网格上玩的。|synonyms: sexangle, polygon|antonyms: N/A|noun
