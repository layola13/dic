cakiest:/ˈkeɪkiɪst/| adj.|最做作的，最矫情的|Resembling or characteristic of a person who is excessively sentimental or insincere.| Her cakiest smile made me suspicious. 她那矫揉造作的笑容让我起了疑心。|近义词：affected, artificial, insincere, pretentious|反义词：genuine, sincere, natural|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From "cake" (possibly referring to a heavily frosted, overly sweet cake as a symbol of artificiality) + "-y" (adjective suffix) + "-est" (superlative suffix).  The word "caky" emerged first, meaning overly sweet and rich, then extended figuratively to describe behavior.
