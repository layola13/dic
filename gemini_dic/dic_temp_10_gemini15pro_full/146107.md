overembellish: /ˌoʊvərɪmˈbɛlɪʃ/ | v. | 过分装饰；过度修饰 | To embellish something excessively, adding unnecessary or exaggerated details. |  She overembellished her story about the trip, making it sound much more exciting than it actually was. 她过度修饰了她关于旅行的故事，使它听起来比实际情况更令人兴奋。

The wedding cake was overembellished with so many flowers that it looked gaudy. 婚礼蛋糕上装饰了太多的花，看起来很俗气。


| 近义词: exaggerate, overstate, adorn excessively | 反义词: understate, downplay, simplify | 词性: verb
