oestrogenization:/iːstrədʒənaɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
雌激素化
The process of becoming oestrogenized, or the state of being oestrogenized; the administration of oestrogen to produce female characteristics.
Oestrogenization of the male brain during development can have profound effects on behavior.  雄性大脑在发育过程中雌激素化会对行为产生深远的影响。

Oestrogenization can lead to the development of secondary sexual characteristics. 雌激素化会导致第二性征的发育。
Synonym: feminization
Antonym: masculinization
