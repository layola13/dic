artotyrite:/ɑːrˈtɒtɪˌraɪt/
n.
artotyrite (面包崇拜者)
A member of a sect in the early Church who celebrated the Eucharist with bread and cheese.
The artotyrites were considered heretical by the mainstream Church.  阿尔托泰利特派被主流教会视为异端。
The artotyrites used cheese in their Eucharist. 阿尔托泰利特派在圣餐礼中使用奶酪。
Near synonyms: heretic, sectarian
Antonyms: orthodox, conformist
Noun
