acclimatization, acclimatisation: /əˌklaɪmətaɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. | 适应，驯化 | The process of adjusting to a new climate, altitude, or environment. |  It took him a few weeks for acclimatization to the high altitude. 他花了几个星期才适应高海拔。

The acclimatization period for the new employees is one month. 新员工的适应期是一个月。 | 近义词: adaptation, adjustment, habituation | 反义词: maladaptation | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *acclimatation*, from *acclimater* "to accustom to a climate," from *à* "to" + *climat* "climate," ultimately from Greek *klima* "region, slope, zone."
