concoctive:/kənˈkɒktɪv/ | adj. | 调制的；捏造的；调配的 | Of or relating to concocting or concoction; tending to concoct.  |  She had a concoctive mind, always dreaming up new stories. 她有着丰富的想象力，总是编造新的故事。 |  inventive, creative, imaginative | factual, real, truthful | adjective

concoctive:/kənˈkɒktɪv/ | n. | 捏造物；调制品 | Something concocted, a concoction.  | The drink was a strange concoctive of various juices and spices. 这种饮料是由各种果汁和香料调制而成的奇怪混合物。 | concoction, mixture, blend |  N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *concoctivus, from Latin concoctus (past participle of concoquere “to cook together, digest, ripen, mature”), from com- (together) + coquere (to cook).
