overcloseness: /ˌoʊvərˈkloʊsnəs/
noun
过度亲密
A state of excessive emotional or physical intimacy, often leading to discomfort or a feeling of being smothered.
The overcloseness of the family made it difficult for the children to develop independence.  这个家庭过度亲密，使孩子们难以培养独立性。
近义词: intimacy, closeness, suffocation
反义词: distance, detachment
词性: n.
