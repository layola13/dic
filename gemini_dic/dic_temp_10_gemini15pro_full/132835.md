noneditorial:/ˌnɒnˌɛdɪˈtɔːriəl/ | adj. | 非编辑的；非社论的 | Not related to the editing or editorial content of a publication.  Relating to the business or production aspects of publishing, rather than the creative content. | The noneditorial staff handles advertising and subscriptions. 非编辑人员负责广告和订阅。|  The noneditorial sections of the newspaper, like the classifieds, brought in significant revenue.  报纸的非编辑部分，例如分类广告，带来了可观的收入。 | synonyms:  non-editorial, administrative, business, production, managerial | antonyms: editorial, journalistic | adjective
