fishs: /fɪʃɪz/ (This form is generally considered incorrect) | n. (incorrect plural of fish)| 鱼（错误的复数形式）| "Fishs" is not the standard plural form of "fish." The correct plural is usually "fish."  In some specific contexts, "fishes" is acceptable when referring to different species of fish. | *Incorrect:* I caught three fishs today.  *Correct:* I caught three fish today.  *Acceptable (referring to species):* The fishes of the Amazon are diverse and colorful. | 近义词: (for the singular) fish, aquatic animal | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun (incorrect plural)

fish: /fɪʃ/ | n. v. | 鱼；钓鱼 | A cold-blooded vertebrate animal that lives in water, typically breathes using gills, and usually has fins and scales.  As a verb, it means to try to catch fish. | I like to eat fish. 我喜欢吃鱼。 He went fishing yesterday. 他昨天去钓鱼了。 | 近义词: (noun) seafood, (verb) angle | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun, Verb

fishes: /ˈfɪʃɪz/ | n. | 鱼（不同种类）|  Plural of "fish," primarily used when referring to different species of fish. | The coral reef is home to many colorful fishes. 珊瑚礁是许多色彩斑斓的鱼类的家园。 We studied the fishes of the Pacific Ocean. 我们研究了太平洋的鱼类。| 近义词: fish (general plural) | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun
