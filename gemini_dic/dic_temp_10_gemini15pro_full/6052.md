amends:/əˈmendz/
noun
赔偿；补偿
compensation or reparation for a wrongdoing or injury.
She made amends for her mistake by apologizing. 她为自己的错误道歉以示弥补。
synonyms: reparation, restitution, compensation, recompense
antonyms: damage, injury, offense
