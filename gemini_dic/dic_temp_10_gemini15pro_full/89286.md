homodynamic:/ˌhəʊməʊdaɪˈnæmɪk/
adjective
血流动力学相同的
Having the same hemodynamics or circulatory dynamics.  Relating to the forces involved in the circulation of blood.
The patient exhibited homodynamic responses to the medication.  该患者对药物表现出血流动力学相同的反应。
Near synonyms:  isodynamic, isohaemic
Antonyms:  heterodynamic
词性: adjective
