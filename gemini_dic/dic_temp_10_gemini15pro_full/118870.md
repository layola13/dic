metasilicate:/ˌmɛtəˈsɪlɪkeɪt/ | n. |偏硅酸盐 |A salt or ester of metasilicic acid.  It contains the SiO₃²⁻ anion.| Sodium metasilicate is a common ingredient in detergents. 硅酸钠是洗涤剂中的常见成分。| synonym: silicate | antonym: N/A | noun

metasilicic:/ˌmɛtəsɪˈlɪsɪk/ | adj. |偏硅酸的| Relating to or derived from metasilicic acid (H₂SiO₃). |Metasilicic acid is unstable and readily polymerizes to form silica gel. 偏硅酸不稳定，容易聚合成硅胶。 | synonym: silicic | antonym: N/A | adjective
