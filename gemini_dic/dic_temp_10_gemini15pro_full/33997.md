citer:/ˈsaɪtər/ (US), /ˈsʌɪtər/ (UK)| n.|引用者，引证者; 召唤者|Someone who quotes or cites a passage, book, author, etc. |The citer of the poem was unknown.  这首诗的引用者不详。|近义词：quoter, referencer|反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "fabricator" or "plagiarist" for someone misrepresenting sources)|noun
