outwrenching:/ˌaʊtˈrɛntʃɪŋ/
| adj. | 痛苦的，极其悲伤的 | Causing extreme emotional pain or distress, as if by wrenching or tearing away. |  Her outwrenching grief was palpable after the loss of her child. 她失去孩子后，极度悲伤，令人感同身受。 |近义词：heartbreaking, agonizing, harrowing | 反义词：joyful, comforting | 词性: 形容词

outwrenching:/ˌaʊtˈrɛntʃɪŋ/
| n. | 痛苦，极度悲伤 | The act of causing extreme emotional pain or distress. | The outwrenching of losing a loved one is difficult to describe. 失去挚爱的痛苦难以言喻。| 近义词： heartbreak, agony, torment | 反义词：joy, comfort | 词性: 名词


词源:  "out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "wrenching" (from "wrench," meaning to twist or pull violently and causing pain or injury).  组合了前缀"out-"表示超过或胜过，以及"wrenching"，源自"wrench"，意为猛烈扭曲或拉扯并造成疼痛或伤害。
