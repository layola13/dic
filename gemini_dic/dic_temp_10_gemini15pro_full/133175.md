nonevanescently:/ˌnɒnˌɛvəˈnɛsəntli/
adverb
不消失地，持续地
Not evanescently; in a way that does not fade away or disappear; persistently.
The memory of that day remains nonevanescently etched in my mind. 那一天的记忆深深地铭刻在我的脑海中，永不消失。
The stars shone nonevanescently in the clear night sky. 星星在晴朗的夜空中持续闪耀。
近义词: permanently, enduringly, perpetually, lastingly
反义词: evanescently, fleetingly, temporarily, briefly
词性: adverb

nonevanescent:/ˌnɒnˌɛvəˈnɛsənt/
adjective
不消失的，持续的
Not evanescent; not fading away or disappearing; persistent.
The nonevanescent beauty of the mountains captivated her.  群山永恒的美丽深深地吸引了她。
He left a nonevanescent mark on the world of art. 他在艺术界留下了不朽的印记。
近义词: permanent, enduring, perpetual, lasting
反义词: evanescent, fleeting, temporary, brief
词性: adjective

evanesce:/ˌɛvəˈnɛs/
verb
逐渐消失，消散
To fade away; to disappear gradually.
The mist evanesced as the sun rose. 随着太阳升起，薄雾逐渐消散。
His youthful dreams seemed to evanesce with the passing of time.  他年轻时的梦想似乎随着时间的流逝而消失了。
近义词: disappear, fade, vanish, dissolve
反义词: appear, materialize, emerge, persist
词性: verb

词源:
From Latin *nonevanescens, from non- 'not' + evanescens, present participle of evanescere 'to vanish'.  由拉丁语 *nonevanescens 构成，来自 non- '不' + evanescens，evanescere '消失' 的现在分词。
