anteportico:/ˌæntiˈpɔːrtɪkoʊ/
n.
前廊，门廊
A porch or portico in front of a building, especially a temple or church.
The anteportico of the cathedral provided a sheltered space for visitors to gather.  大教堂的前廊为游客提供了一个遮蔽的聚集空间。
The temple's grand anteportico was supported by towering columns.  寺庙宏伟的前廊由高耸的柱子支撑。
synonyms: porch, portico, vestibule, narthex
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps the back or rear of a building)
noun
