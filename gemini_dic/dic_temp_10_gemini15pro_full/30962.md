chalaza:/kəˈleɪzə/| n. |系带，卵带|A twisted cordlike structure attaching the yolk to the enclosing membrane at either end of the shell of a bird's or reptile's egg.|The chalaza keeps the yolk centered in the egg.  系带使蛋黄保持在鸡蛋的中心。|Synonyms:  none (highly specific term)|Antonyms: none|noun

chalaza:/kəˈleɪzə/| n. |植物胚珠基部|The part of an ovule where the integuments and nucellus are joined.|The chalaza is important for nutrient transport to the developing embryo. 胚珠基部对于营养物质向发育中的胚胎的运输非常重要。|Synonyms: none (highly specific term)|Antonyms: none|noun
