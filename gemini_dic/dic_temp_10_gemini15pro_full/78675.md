giftedly:/ˈɡɪftɪdli/| adv.|有天赋地；有才华地|Having exceptional talent or natural ability.|She is giftedly talented in music. 她极具音乐天赋。|近义词：talentedly, brilliantly, exceptionally, remarkably|反义词：poorly, inadequately, ineptly|词性: 副词

词源:
From "gifted" + "-ly". "Gifted" comes from the verb "gift," meaning "to endow with a gift," from the noun "gift."  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *giftiz.
