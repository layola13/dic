metagnathism:/mɛˈtæɡnəˌθɪzəm/| n. |后颚 |A condition in which the gnathal (jaw) points are located posterior to the cranium.  It is the typical jaw configuration in most vertebrates, including mammals, birds, and reptiles. |The crocodile exhibits prominent metagnathism. 鳄鱼展现出显著的后颚特征。|The specimen displayed clear metagnathism, indicating it was likely a reptile.  该标本展现出清晰的后颚特征，表明它很可能是一种爬行动物。| 近义词:opisthognathism | 反义词:prognathism | noun
