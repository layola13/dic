noncorroding:/ˌnɒnkəˈrəʊdɪŋ/
adjective
不腐蚀的，耐腐蚀的
Not subject to corrosion; resistant to corrosion.
Stainless steel is a noncorroding material. 不锈钢是一种不腐蚀的材料。
This paint provides a noncorroding protective layer.  这种油漆提供了一层不腐蚀的保护层。
Synonyms: rustproof, corrosion-resistant, stainless, non-corrosive
Antonyms: corroding, corrosive, rusting
词性: adjective
