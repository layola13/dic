misprints:/ˈmɪsprɪnts/| n.|印刷错误；误印|An error in printing; a typographical mistake.|The book was full of misprints and had to be recalled. 这本书充满了印刷错误，不得不被召回。| synonyms: typos, errata, errors, mistakes | antonyms: corrections, perfections | noun

misprint:/ˈmɪsprɪnt/| vt.|印错；错误地印刷|To print incorrectly.|He misprinted the date on the invitations. 他把请柬上的日期印错了。| synonyms: misspell, typo, err | antonyms: print correctly | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (wrongly) + print (to produce text or images).  The prefix "mis-" is of Old English origin, meaning "wrongly" or "badly". "Print" comes from the Old French "printer," ultimately from the Latin "premere," meaning "to press."
