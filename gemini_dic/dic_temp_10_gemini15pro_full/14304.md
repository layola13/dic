automat:/ˈɔːtəmæt/ (US), /ˈɔːtəmɑːt/ (UK)| n. | 自动售货机；小餐馆 | A self-service restaurant or cafe providing food and drink from vending machines. |  Example:  He grabbed a sandwich from the automat. 他从自动售货机里拿了个三明治。

Example: The automat was a popular dining option in the early 20th century.  自动售货机餐厅在20世纪初是一种很受欢迎的餐饮选择。 | 近义词: vending machine, cafeteria | 反义词: restaurant (with table service) | 词性: noun
