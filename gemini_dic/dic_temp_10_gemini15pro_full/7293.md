angstrom:/ˈæŋstrəm/ | n. | 埃，埃格斯特朗 |A unit of length equal to one ten-billionth of a meter (10⁻¹⁰ meter), used chiefly in expressing wavelengths of electromagnetic radiation and interatomic distances.| The wavelength of visible light is measured in angstroms. 可见光的波长用埃来测量。 | synonym:  Å, one ten-billionth of a meter | antonym: N/A | noun
