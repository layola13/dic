mesitylene:/ˈmɛsɪtiˌliːn/ | n. |均三甲苯；1,3,5-三甲苯|A colorless liquid hydrocarbon, C9H12, derived from coal tar and petroleum and used as a solvent and thinner and in organic synthesis.  It is a symmetrical trimethylbenzene.| Mesitylene is used as a solvent in the laboratory. 均三甲苯在实验室中用作溶剂。|Mesitylene is an important starting material for the synthesis of various dyes. 均三甲苯是合成各种染料的重要起始原料。|Synonyms: 1,3,5-trimethylbenzene, sym-trimethylbenzene|Antonyms: N/A|noun
