listeria: /lɪˈstɪəriə/
| n. | 李斯特菌 | A genus of bacteria that can cause food poisoning, especially in pregnant women, newborns, and people with weakened immune systems. |  Listeria can be found in unpasteurized milk and soft cheeses. 李斯特菌可以在未经巴氏消毒的牛奶和软奶酪中找到。 | 近义词:  N/A (Specific genus of bacteria) | 反义词: N/A (Bacteria don't have direct opposites.) | Noun
