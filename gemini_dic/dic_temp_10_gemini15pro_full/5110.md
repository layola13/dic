allegedly: /əˈledʒɪdli/ | adv. | 据称；据说 | Used to convey that something is claimed to be the case or to have taken place, although there is no proof. | He was allegedly involved in the robbery. 他被指控参与了抢劫。|  synonyms: reportedly, supposedly, ostensibly | antonyms:  provenly, demonstrably, undeniably | adverb
