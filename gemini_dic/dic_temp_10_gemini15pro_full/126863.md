nappas:/ˈnæpəz/| n. |餐巾, (尤指)餐巾纸|A small piece of cloth or paper used to protect clothing or wipe the mouth and hands while eating. |She carefully unfolded the linen nappas and placed one on each guest's lap. 她小心翼翼地展开亚麻餐巾，放在每位客人的腿上。|Example:  He used a nappa to wipe the sauce from his chin. 他用餐巾擦了擦下巴上的酱汁。|Synonyms: napkin, serviette|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|Noun
