morogo:/məˈrɔːɡoʊ/| n. |非洲绿叶蔬菜，野菜|A general term for any of a variety of edible wild or cultivated African leafy green vegetables.|Example 1:  We had morogo and sadza for dinner. 我们晚饭吃了野菜和玉米糊。

Example 2:  She went to the market to buy some fresh morogo. 她去市场买了一些新鲜的野菜。|Synonym:  greens, spinach (loosely), African spinach, edible leaves|Antonym:  (No direct antonym,  perhaps processed food)|noun
