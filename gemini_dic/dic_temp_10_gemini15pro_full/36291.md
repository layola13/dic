cohere:/koʊˈhɪr/ (US), /kəʊˈhɪə(r)/ (UK)| v.| 粘合，凝聚，连贯| to stick together; to be united; to be logical and consistent| The particles cohere to form a solid mass. 颗粒凝聚形成固体。| synonyms: adhere, cling, unite, bind, integrate | antonyms: separate, disintegrate, fall apart | verb

cohesion:/koʊˈhiːʒn/ (US), /kəʊˈhiːʒn/ (UK)| n.| 粘合，凝聚，凝聚力，连贯性| the act or state of cohering; the tendency of particles to stick together; logical connection | The cohesion of the group was remarkable. 这个群体的凝聚力非常强。| synonyms: unity, coherence, adhesion, bond | antonyms: separation, disunity, division | noun

coherent:/koʊˈhɪrənt/ (US), /kəʊˈhɪərənt/ (UK)| adj.| 连贯的，一致的，条理清楚的| logically connected; consistent; understandable |  She gave a coherent account of the accident. 她对事故作了连贯的叙述。| synonyms: logical, consistent, rational, lucid, intelligible | antonyms: incoherent, illogical, inconsistent, rambling, confused | adjective
