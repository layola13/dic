nondisclosure: /ˌnɒn.dɪˈskləʊ.ʒər/ (US) /ˌnɒn.dɪˈskləʊ.ʒə(r)/ (UK)| n. |保密，不公开，保密协议|The act of not disclosing something, typically confidential information. |例句：The company required employees to sign a nondisclosure agreement.  公司要求员工签署保密协议。

例句：The nondisclosure of the test results caused a public outcry.  测试结果的不公开引起了公众的强烈抗议。

近义词：confidentiality, secrecy, silence|反义词: disclosure, revelation |词性: 名词
