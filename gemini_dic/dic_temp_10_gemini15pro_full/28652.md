caricature: /ˈkærɪkətʃər/ (US), /ˈkærɪkətʃʊə(r)/ (UK)| n. vt.|漫画；讽刺画；滑稽模仿|A picture, description, or imitation of a person in which certain striking characteristics are exaggerated in order to create a comic or grotesque effect. |The politician was easily identified by his caricature in the newspaper.  这位政治家很容易从报纸上的漫画中被认出来。|Synonyms: parody, cartoon, satire, burlesque, lampoon|Antonyms: portrait, likeness, representation|noun

caricature: /ˈkærɪkətʃər/ (US), /ˈkærɪkətʃʊə(r)/ (UK)| vt.|把…画成漫画；滑稽地模仿|To represent or imitate someone in a caricature. |The comedian caricatured the president's mannerisms perfectly.  这位喜剧演员完美地模仿了总统的举止。|Synonyms: parody, mimic, satirize, mock|Antonyms: portray, depict, represent|verb
