homogentisic: /ˌhoʊmoʊdʒɛnˈtɪsɪk/ /ˌhɒmədʒɛnˈtɪsɪk/ | adj. |  尿黑酸的；由尿黑酸组成的 | Relating to or derived from homogentisic acid, a product of tyrosine metabolism. |  Homogentisic acid is an intermediate in the metabolism of tyrosine and phenylalanine. 尿黑酸是酪氨酸和苯丙氨酸代谢的中间体。|  Homogentisic aciduria is a rare genetic disorder. 尿黑酸尿症是一种罕见的遗传疾病。| 近义词: (No direct synonyms, but related terms include: alkaptonuric, related to alkaptonuria, the condition caused by excess homogentisic acid) | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective
