distractedness:/dɪˈstræktɪdnəs/
n.
心烦意乱；注意力分散
The state of being distracted; inability to concentrate.
Her distractedness during the meeting was noticeable.  她在会议期间心不在焉，很明显。
inattention, absent-mindedness, preoccupation
attention, focus, concentration
noun
