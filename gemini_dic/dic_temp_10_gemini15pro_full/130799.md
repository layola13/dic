nonantagonistic:/nɒnˌæntæɡəˈnɪstɪk/
adjective
非对抗性的
Not characterized by antagonism; not hostile or unfriendly.
The two countries maintained a nonantagonistic relationship despite their political differences.  这两个国家尽管政治上存在分歧，但仍保持着非对抗性关系。

The scientist advocated for a nonantagonistic approach to wildlife management.  这位科学家提倡用非对抗性的方法来管理野生动物。

synonym:  cooperative, collaborative, amicable, friendly, peaceful, harmonious
antonym:  antagonistic, hostile, adversarial, unfriendly, aggressive, combative
词性: adjective
