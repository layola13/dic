edificant:/ˈɛdɪfʌɪɪŋ/| adj.|有教益的，启发的|Providing moral or intellectual instruction.|例句：Reading history is an edificant experience. 阅读历史是一种有益的经历。|例句：The museum offers edificant exhibits for all ages.  这家博物馆为所有年龄段的人提供有教育意义的展品。|近义词：instructive, enlightening, informative, enriching|反义词：uninformative, worthless, frivolous|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French edifier, from Latin aedificare ‘build’, from aedes ‘building’ + facere ‘make’. The original sense was ‘to build up morally or spiritually’, later ‘to improve intellectually’.
