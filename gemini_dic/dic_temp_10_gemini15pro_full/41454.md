cornicle:/ˈkɔːrnɪkl/| n. |角状管；蜜管|A small, tube-like projection on the abdomen of some aphids and other insects, through which they secrete a waxy substance.|Aphids use their cornicles to excrete honeydew. 蚜虫用它们的角状管分泌蜜露。|Near synonyms: siphunculi, wax tubes|Antonyms: N/A (lack of a cornicle is not a specific anatomical feature)|noun
