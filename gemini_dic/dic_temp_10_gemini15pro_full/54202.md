disfavor:/dɪsˈfeɪvər/
n.|不喜爱，厌恶，失宠|lack of favor or approval; disapproval|His constant complaining earned him the disfavor of his colleagues. 他不断的抱怨让他失去了同事们的好感。|Synonyms: disapproval, dislike, disinclination, antipathy|Antonyms: favor, approval, liking, fondness|noun

disfavor:/dɪsˈfeɪvər/
vt.|不喜欢，厌恶|to regard or treat with disfavor; dislike|The coach disfavored the new training method. 教练不喜欢新的训练方法。|Synonyms: dislike, disapprove of, oppose|Antonyms: favor, approve of, support|verb
