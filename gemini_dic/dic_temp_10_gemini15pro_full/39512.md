conoidic:/kəˈnɔɪdɪk/
adjective
圆锥形的；近似圆锥形的
Resembling a cone; somewhat conical.
The shell has a conoidic shape.  这个贝壳呈圆锥形。
近义词: conical, cone-shaped
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider shapes like "cubic", "spherical" depending on context)
词性: adjective

conoid:/ˈkoʊnɔɪd/
noun
圆锥体；类似圆锥体的物体
A surface or solid generated by a straight line moving so that it always intersects a fixed circle and passes through a fixed point not in the plane of the circle.  Also, anything resembling this shape.
The architect incorporated a conoid into the building's design. 建筑师在建筑设计中加入了一个圆锥体。
近义词: cone
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider shapes like "cube", "sphere" depending on context)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *kōnoeides*  "cone-shaped," from *kōnos* "cone" + *-eides*  "having the form of."  The suffix *-eides* is related to *eidos* "form, shape, appearance."
