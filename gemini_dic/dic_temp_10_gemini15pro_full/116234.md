masqueraders:/ˌmæskəˈreɪdərz/
noun
假面舞会参加者；伪装者
People wearing masks or disguises, often at a masquerade ball.
The masqueraders danced the night away, their identities hidden behind elaborate masks.  假面舞会参加者整夜跳舞，他们的身份隐藏在精致的面具后面。
The spies were masqueraders, pretending to be tourists. 间谍是伪装者，假扮成游客。
Synonyms:  dissemblers, pretenders, impersonators, posers, deceivers
Antonyms:  honest people, truthful individuals, genuine people
