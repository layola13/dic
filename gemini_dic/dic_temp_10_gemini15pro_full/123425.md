monopoler:/məˈnɒpələr/ | n. |  垄断者；独占者 | A person or company that has a monopoly. | The government is trying to prevent the company from becoming a monopoler in the market. 政府正在努力阻止该公司在市场上成为垄断者。 | Synonyms: monopolist, trust, cartel | Antonyms: competitor, rival | noun

monopoler:/məˈnɒpələr/ | n. |  单极子；单极 |  A hypothetical elementary particle in particle physics that is an isolated magnet with only one magnetic pole. |  Scientists are still searching for evidence of the existence of magnetic monopoles. 科学家们仍在寻找磁单极子存在的证据。 | Synonyms:  magnetic monopole | Antonyms: dipole | noun
