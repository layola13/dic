bilateralnesses:/ˌbaɪˌlætərəlnəsɪz/
| n. |双边性；双侧性|The state or quality of being bilateral; involving two sides, parties, or countries.|The treaty emphasized the bilateralnesses of the agreement, ensuring both nations benefited equally.|近义词：bilaterality, two-sidedness|反义词：unilateralism, one-sidedness|词性:名词

词源:
From bilateral + -ness.  "Bilateral" comes from the Latin "bi-" (two) and "lateralis" (of the side). "-ness" is an Old English suffix forming nouns denoting state or quality.
