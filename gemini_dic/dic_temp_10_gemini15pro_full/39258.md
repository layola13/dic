congregations: /ˌkɒŋɡrɪˈɡeɪʃənz/ (UK) /ˌkɑːŋɡrɪˈɡeɪʃənz/ (US)
| n. | 集会；会众；（尤指教堂的）教友；信众 | A group of people assembled for religious worship; a gathering. | The church congregations sang hymns together. 教堂的会众一起唱赞美诗。 | synonyms: assemblies, gatherings, audiences, crowds | antonyms: dispersions, scatterings
