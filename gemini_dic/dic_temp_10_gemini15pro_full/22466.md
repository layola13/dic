bookmaking:/ˈbʊkˌmeɪkɪŋ/
n.
博彩业，赌博生意;  制书籍
The activity of accepting and paying out bets on horse races, sporting events, etc., or the business of a bookmaker. / The production of books.
The government is considering new regulations on bookmaking. 政府正在考虑对博彩业实施新的监管。
Bookmaking has been a lucrative business for centuries.  几个世纪以来，博彩业一直是一项利润丰厚的生意。
He learned the craft of bookmaking from his father. 他从他父亲那里学到了制书工艺。
The museum has an exhibit on the history of bookmaking.  博物馆有一个关于制书历史的展览。
近义词: gambling, betting, publishing
反义词: None (for the betting sense);  None (for the publishing sense)
词性: noun
