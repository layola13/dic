affably:/ˈæfəbli/| adv.|和蔼地；亲切地|In a friendly and easy manner; genially.|He greeted them affably and invited them in. 他和蔼地跟他们打招呼，并邀请他们进去。|近义词：amiably, cordially, genially, pleasantly|反义词：unamiably, curtly, coldly, rudely|词性:副词
Etymology: From Late Latin affabilis (“easy to speak to”), from Latin ad- + fari (“to speak”).
