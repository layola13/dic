amentaceous:/ˌæmənˈteɪʃəs/
adjective
柳絮状的，有柔荑花序的
bearing catkins; resembling catkins.
The amentaceous flowers of the willow tree are a sign of spring. 柳树的柔荑花序是春天的标志。
The hazelnut tree has long, drooping, amentaceous flowers. 榛子树有长长的、下垂的柔荑花序。
近义词: catkin-bearing, catkinate
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider terms like "flowerless" for plants without this specific inflorescence type)
词性: adjective
