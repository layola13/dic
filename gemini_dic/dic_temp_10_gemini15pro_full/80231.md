Goebbels: /ˈɡœbəlz/ | n. |戈培尔|Joseph Goebbels (1897-1945),  Reich Minister of Propaganda in Nazi Germany, known for his skills in public speaking and propaganda, which were instrumental in manipulating public opinion in support of the Nazi regime. | Hitler relied heavily on Goebbels' propaganda to maintain power. 希特勒非常依赖戈培尔的宣传来维持权力。|synonyms: propagandist, demagogue | antonyms: truth-teller,  dissident | noun
