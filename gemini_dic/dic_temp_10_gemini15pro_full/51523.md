detritus:/dɪˈtraɪtəs/
n.
碎屑；残渣；废物
Waste or debris of any kind.
例句：The detritus of a party littered the floor.  派对后的残渣散落在地板上。

例句：The ocean floor is covered with detritus from dead marine life.  海底覆盖着海洋生物死亡后的碎屑。

近义词：debris, remains, waste, rubble
反义词：growth,  creation


detrituses:/dɪˈtraɪtəsiːz/
n.
碎屑；残渣；废物（detritus 的复数形式，较少使用）
Waste or debris of any kind.
例句：The detrituses of several meals were piled in the sink.  好几顿饭的残渣堆在水槽里。（此例句略有不自然，通常用detritus）

例句：The archaeologist sifted through the detrituses of the ancient city. 考古学家仔细检查了古城的废墟。（此例句略有不自然，通常用detritus）

近义词：debris, remains, waste, rubble
反义词：growth, creation


词性：名词


词源：From Latin dētrītus, past participle of dēterere "to rub or wear away," from dē- "down, away" + terere "to rub, grind."
