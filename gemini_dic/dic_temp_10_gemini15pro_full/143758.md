outmantle:/ˌaʊtˈmæntl/
verb
覆盖，超越，胜过
to cover or envelop something completely; to surpass or excel
The climber outmantled the perilous cliff face. 攀登者征服了危险的悬崖峭壁。
近义词:surpass, excel, exceed, outdo, outstrip, eclipse
反义词:fail, fall short of
词性:v.


outmantle:/ˌaʊtˈmæntl/
noun
外衣，覆盖物
an outer covering or cloak
The fog served as an outmantle, obscuring the city. 雾就像一件外衣，遮蔽了整个城市。
近义词:cloak, mantle, covering, shroud
反义词:interior, inside
词性:n.
