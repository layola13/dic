furtherer:/ˈfɜːrðərər/
noun
推进者，促进者
Someone or something that furthers something, especially a cause, belief, or plan.
He was a furtherer of radical political ideas. 他是一个激进政治思想的推动者。
Synonyms: promoter, advancer, developer, supporter, backer
Antonyms: hinderer, obstructer, opponent
词性: 名词
