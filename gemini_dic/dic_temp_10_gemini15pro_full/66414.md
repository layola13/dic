executives:/ɪɡˈzɛkjətɪvz/ | n. | 高管，主管，行政人员 |  A person with senior managerial responsibility in a business organization. | The executives of the company decided to expand into new markets. 公司高管决定拓展新市场。| synonyms: managers, administrators, directors, officers, officials, supervisors, bosses | antonyms: subordinates, employees, workers | noun


executives:/ɪɡˈzɛkjətɪvz/ | adj. | 行政的，管理的 |  Having the power to put plans, actions, or laws into effect. |  The executive branch of government is responsible for implementing laws. 政府的行政部门负责执行法律。| synonyms: administrative, managerial, governing, directing | antonyms: non-executive, subordinate,  clerical | adjective
