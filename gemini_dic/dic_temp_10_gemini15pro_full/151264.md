parentlike: /ˈpɛərəntˌlaɪk/
adjective
像父母的；父母般的
Resembling or characteristic of a parent; acting as a parent would.
He took on a parentlike role with his younger siblings. 他对弟弟妹妹们扮演了类似父母的角色。
synonyms: parental, motherly, fatherly, nurturing, protective
antonyms: uncaring, neglectful, indifferent
词性: 形容词
