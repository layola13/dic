dehusk:/dɪˈhʌsk/| v. |脱壳，去壳|To remove the husk from something, such as an ear of corn or a coconut.|例句：We need to dehusk the corn before we can cook it. 我们需要先把玉米剥皮才能煮。

例句：The machine can dehusk coconuts efficiently. 这台机器可以高效地剥椰子壳。

近义词：shell, peel, husk|反义词:cover, encase|词性:动词
