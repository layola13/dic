loftiness:/ˈlɔːftɪnɪs/
n.
崇高，高尚；高傲；高耸
The quality of being lofty; elevation, especially in character or style.
The loftiness of his ideals inspired many.  他的崇高理想激励了很多人。
近义词：nobleness, grandeur, sublimity, majesty, elevation, haughtiness, arrogance
反义词：humility, modesty, lowness, baseness


ETYMOLOGY->
From lofty + -ness.  Lofty from Middle English *lofti, from Old Norse lofti (“air, sky”), from Proto-Germanic *luftuz (“air, sky”), from Proto-Indo-European *lewp- (“to peel, break off, flay”).
