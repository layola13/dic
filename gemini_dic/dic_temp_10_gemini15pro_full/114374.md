majun: /ˈmɑːdʒuːn/ | n. | 麻将; 麻酱 | A tile-based game played by four players, popular in East Asia, or a sesame paste used in various cuisines. |  例句：We played mahjong all night. 我们打了一整夜麻将。

例句：I like to add mahjong to my noodles. 我喜欢在面条里加麻酱。 | 近义词：(for the game) mah-jongg, mahjongg | 反义词:  (no direct antonym for either meaning) | 词性: noun
