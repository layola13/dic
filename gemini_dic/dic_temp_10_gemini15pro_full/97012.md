infaust:/ɪnˈfɔːst/ | adj. | 不吉利的，不幸的 | Unlucky; ill-omened; unfavorable.  |  The infaust beginning to the journey foreshadowed the difficulties to come.  旅程不吉利的开始预示着未来的困难。 | synonyms: ominous, unlucky, unfortunate, unfavorable, ill-fated | antonyms: auspicious, fortunate, lucky, favorable, propitious | adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *infaustus*, from in- "not" + *faustus* "favorable, auspicious, lucky".
