defaulter:/dɪˈfɔːltər/
n.|违约者，拖欠者|A person or entity that fails to fulfill a financial obligation or meet a commitment.|The company was labeled a defaulter after failing to make its loan payments.  这家公司因未能偿还贷款而被贴上违约者的标签。|近义词：non-payer, delinquent, debtor|反义词：payer, creditor|词性: 名词
defaulter:/dɪˈfɔːltər/|n.|不履行义务者，失职者|A person who fails to perform a duty or obligation.|He was considered a defaulter after missing several mandatory meetings. 他因缺席几次强制性会议而被认为是失职者。|近义词：neglecter, shirker|反义词：dutiful person, responsible person|词性: 名词
