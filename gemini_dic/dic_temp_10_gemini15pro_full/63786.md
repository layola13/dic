epicurize:/ˈɛpɪkjʊˌraɪz/ | v. | 追求享乐，沉溺于美食 | To indulge in luxurious pleasures, especially those of eating and drinking. | He spent his retirement epicurizing in the south of France. 他在法国南部享受着退休后的奢华生活，尽情享受美食。| She epicurized on fine wines and gourmet cheeses. 她沉迷于美酒和美味的奶酪。 | 近义词: indulge, luxuriate, revel, feast | 反义词: abstain, forbear, deny oneself | 词性: 动词

词源:  From Epicurus (341-270 BC), a Greek philosopher who advocated pleasure as the highest good, but in a moderate and refined way.  His name became associated with a refined pursuit of pleasure, especially in food and drink.
