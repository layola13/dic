honky-tonker:/ˈhɑːŋkiˌtɑːŋkər/
n.
喧闹的酒吧常客；乡村音乐爱好者；在喧闹酒吧演奏音乐的人
A person who frequents honky-tonks, especially a musician who plays country music in such establishments.
He's a real honky-tonker, always at the bar listening to country music. 他是一个真正的乡村音乐爱好者，总是在酒吧里听乡村音乐。
Synonym: barfly, regular, habitué
Antonym: teetotaler, abstainer
Noun

honky-tonker:/ˈhɑːŋkiˌtɑːŋkər/
n.
喧闹的乡村音乐酒吧
A cheap or disreputable bar or nightclub, typically one featuring country music. (Chiefly US)
That honky-tonker down the street always has live music. 街那头的喧闹酒吧总是有现场音乐表演。
Synonym: dive bar, roadhouse
Antonym: upscale bar, lounge
