noncomparable:/ˌnɒnkəmˈpærəbl/ | /ˌnɑːnkəmˈpærəbl/ | adj. | 不可比较的，无与伦比的 | Not able to be compared; so good or great that nothing else is similar |  The experience was noncomparable to anything else I've ever done. 这次经历与我做过的任何其他事情都无法相比。|  This wine is noncomparable; it's in a class of its own.  这种酒无与伦比；它独树一帜。 | 近义词：incomparable, unique, matchless | 反义词：comparable, similar | 词性：形容词

词源:
non- (prefix) + comparable (adjective)
"non-" comes from Latin and means "not".
"comparable" comes from Late Latin "comparabilis", from "comparare"  "to compare", from "com-" (together) + "parare" (to make ready, prepare).
