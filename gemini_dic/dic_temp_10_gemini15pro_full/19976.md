biofuels:/ˈbaɪoʊˌfjuːəlz/ | n. | 生物燃料 | Fuels produced from living organisms or their byproducts. | Brazil is a leading producer of biofuels, particularly ethanol from sugarcane. 巴西是生物燃料的主要生产国，尤其是来自甘蔗的乙醇。|  Biofuels offer a renewable alternative to fossil fuels. 生物燃料为化石燃料提供了一种可再生替代品。 | synonyms: biofuel, renewable fuel, sustainable fuel | antonyms: fossil fuels, non-renewable fuels | noun
