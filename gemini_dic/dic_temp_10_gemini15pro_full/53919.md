discouragingly:/dɪˈskʌrɪdʒɪŋli/
adverb
令人沮丧地，气馁地
in a way that makes someone feel less confident, enthusiastic, and positive about something, or less willing to do something
The team played discouragingly in the first half.  该队上半场打得令人沮丧。
Synonym: dishearteningly, depressingly, disappointingly
Antonym: encouragingly, inspiringly, hopefully
词性: 副词
