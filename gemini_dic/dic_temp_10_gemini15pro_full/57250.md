downlink:/ˈdaʊnlɪŋk/| n. |下行链路|A communications link that transmits signals from a satellite or other space vehicle to a receiving station on Earth.  The process of transmitting such signals.|The satellite's downlink provided real-time data to the research team.|Synonyms: down channel, downleg|Antonyms: uplink|noun

downlink:/ˈdaʊnlɪŋk/| v. |下行传输|To transmit (data or signals) from a satellite or other space vehicle to a receiving station on Earth.|The team downlinked the images from the space station.|Synonyms: transmit, send, beam down|Antonyms: uplink|verb
