fetishistic:/fəˈtɪʃɪstɪk/ /feˈtɪʃɪstɪk/
adjective
恋物的；迷信的；盲目崇拜的
Characterized by or relating to fetishism, the excessive or irrational devotion to an object or body part.  A fetishistic person finds sexual arousal or gratification through unusual objects or practices.
His fetishistic obsession with her shoes was unsettling. 他对她鞋子的恋物癖令人不安。
Synonyms:  obsessive, fixated, compulsive, idolatrous
Antonyms:  uninterested, indifferent, apathetic
词性: adjective
