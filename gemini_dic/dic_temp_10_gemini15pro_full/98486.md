installment-plans:/ɪnˈstɔːlmənt plænz/ | n. | 分期付款计划 | A system for paying for something in regular amounts over a period of time, rather than all at once. | Example: They offer interest-free installment plans for furniture purchases. 他们提供家具购买的免息分期付款计划。 | Example: She's paying for her new car in installment plans. 她正在分期付款购买她的新车。 | Synonym: payment plan, hire purchase, deferred payment | Antonym: lump sum, upfront payment | noun
