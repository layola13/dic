banteng:/ˈbæntɛŋ/ | n. | 爪哇野牛，班腾 | A species of wild cattle found in Southeast Asia. |  The banteng is a close relative of domestic cattle. 爪哇野牛与家牛是近亲。

The banteng is an endangered species due to habitat loss and hunting. 由于栖息地丧失和狩猎，爪哇野牛是一个濒危物种。 | 近义词: wild ox, wild cattle | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "domestic cattle" as a contrasting term) | 词性: noun
