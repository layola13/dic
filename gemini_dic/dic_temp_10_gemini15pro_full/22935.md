bottle-feeds:/ˈbɒtl fiːdz/ (UK) /ˈbɑːtl fiːdz/ (US)| v. |用奶瓶喂养|To feed a baby or young animal with milk or formula from a bottle, rather than from the breast or udder.|The mother bottle-feeds her baby every three hours. 母亲每三个小时用奶瓶喂一次孩子。|Synonym: formula-feed|Antonym: breastfeed|verb

bottle-feeds:/ˈbɒtl fiːdz/ (UK) /ˈbɑːtl fiːdz/ (US)| n. |奶瓶喂养|The act of feeding with a bottle.|Bottle-feeds are sometimes necessary when breastfeeding is not possible. 当母乳喂养不可行时，有时需要奶瓶喂养。|Synonym: formula-feeding|Antonym: breastfeeding|noun
