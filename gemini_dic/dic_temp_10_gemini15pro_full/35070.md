closets: /ˈklɒzɪts/ (UK) /ˈklɑːzɪts/ (US)| n.|衣橱；壁橱；密室；私下，秘密地(引申义)|A closet is a small room or a space in a wall with a door, used for storing things, especially clothes.  Figuratively, it can refer to something kept secret, particularly about one's identity or lifestyle. |例句：She hung her clothes in the closet. 她把衣服挂在衣橱里。

例句：He kept his sexuality a secret; he was in the closet. 他对自己的性取向保密；他还没有出柜。|近义词：wardrobe, cupboard, cabinet (for furniture); secret, hidden (for figurative meaning)|反义词：open, public (for figurative meaning)|词性:noun
