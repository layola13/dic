enneagonal:/ɪˈniːəɡənəl/ /ɛˈniːəɡənəl/
adjective
九邊形的；九角形的
Having nine sides and nine angles.
A regular enneagon has nine equal sides and nine equal angles.  一个正九边形有九条相等的边和九个相等的角。
The architect incorporated an enneagonal design into the building's facade.  建筑师将九边形设计融入建筑的立面。
近义词:nonagonal
反义词: (No direct opposite, but consider shapes with different numbers of sides, like "triangular," "square," "pentagonal," etc.)
词性: adjective
