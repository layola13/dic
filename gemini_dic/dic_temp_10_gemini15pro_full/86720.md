helminthic:/hɛlˈmɪnθɪk/| adj.|蠕虫的，驱虫的|Relating to or caused by parasitic worms (helminths).|例句：The doctor prescribed helminthic medication for the patient's intestinal worms. 医生给病人开了驱虫药来治疗肠道寄生虫。

例句：Helminthic infections are common in tropical regions. 蠕虫感染在热带地区很常见。|近义词：vermicular, vermifuge|反义词:|词性:adjective

helminth:/ˈhɛlmɪnθ/| n.|蠕虫，寄生虫|A parasitic worm; a helminth. |例句：The veterinarian identified the helminth in the dog's stool sample. 兽医在狗的粪便样本中发现了寄生虫。

例句：Several species of helminths can infect humans.  几种寄生虫会感染人类。|近义词：worm, parasite|反义词:|词性:noun
