cherishable:/ˈtʃɛrɪʃəbl/
adjective
值得珍惜的, 宝贵的
Worth keeping and treasuring.
例句：She gave him a cherishable gift on his birthday. 她在他生日那天送给他一件值得珍惜的礼物。

例句：The memories we made together are cherishable. 我们共同创造的回忆弥足珍贵。

近义词：precious, treasured, valuable, dear
反义词：worthless, valueless, insignificant
词性: adjective
