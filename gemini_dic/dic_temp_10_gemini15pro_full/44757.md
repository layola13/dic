crumbly:/ˈkrʌmbli/| adj.|易碎的，脆的|Easily broken into small fragments or pieces; friable.|The cake was delicious but a little crumbly. 这蛋糕很好吃，就是有点碎。|近义词：brittle, friable, flaky|反义词:tough, firm|词性:形容词
Etymology: From crumb + -ly.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *krum-, meaning "crooked" or "bent."
