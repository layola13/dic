mediocritization:/ˌmiːdiːɒkrɪtaɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.| 平庸化；庸俗化 |The mediocritization of education is a serious concern. 教育的平庸化是一个严重的问题. |The increasing reliance on standardized tests has contributed to the mediocritization of teaching. 越来越依赖标准化考试导致了教学的平庸化。| 近义词:deterioration, decline, degradation| 反义词:improvement, enhancement, elevation| 词性:名词

Etymology:
From mediocre (meaning "of only moderate quality; not very good") + -ization (a suffix forming nouns of action or process).  Ultimately from Latin *mediŏcris*  "middling," literally "halfway up a mountain."
