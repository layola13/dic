bowerlike: /ˈbaʊərˌlaɪk/
adjective
像凉亭的; 像树荫的
Resembling a bower; shady and secluded.
The garden had a bowerlike quality, cool and inviting.  这个花园有一种凉亭般的感觉，凉爽宜人。
The willow tree created a bowerlike space perfect for reading. 柳树创造了一个像凉亭一样的空间，非常适合阅读。
近义词: shady, sheltered, secluded, leafy
反义词: exposed, open, sunny
