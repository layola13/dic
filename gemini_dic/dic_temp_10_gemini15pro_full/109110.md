leggiero:/ledˈdʒɛːroʊ/ | adj. | 轻巧的，轻快的 | Light and graceful; delicate. | The pianist played a leggiero passage with effortless ease. 钢琴家轻松地演奏了一段轻快的乐段。 |  Synonyms: light, delicate, airy, graceful | Antonyms: heavy, ponderous, labored | adjective

Etymology:
From Italian *leggiero*, from *leggero* "light," from Latin *levis*.
