groove:/ɡruːv/
|n.| 凹槽，沟；最佳状态；常规，习惯 |A long narrow cut in a surface. A settled routine or habit. A pronounced enjoyable rhythm in music.|例句：The wheels of the car got stuck in a groove in the mud. 车轮陷在泥里的沟里了。 He's in the groove tonight. 他今晚状态极佳。|近义词：rut, furrow, channel, routine, habit|反义词：ridge, peak|名词

groove:/ɡruːv/
|v.| 开槽于；形成沟 |Make a groove in (something). Enjoy oneself and especially dance to popular music.|例句：Rain grooved channels in the mud. 雨水在泥里冲出了沟渠。  We were grooving to the music all night. 我们整晚都随着音乐摇摆。|近义词：gouge, furrow, channel|反义词：smooth|动词
