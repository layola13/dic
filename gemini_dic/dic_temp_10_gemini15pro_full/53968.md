discreetly:/dɪˈskriːtli/| adv.|谨慎地，小心地|In a careful and prudent manner, so as not to attract attention or give offense.|She discreetly slipped him a note. 她谨慎地递给他一张纸条。|近义词：carefully, cautiously, prudently, subtly, tactfully|反义词：openly, publicly, indiscreetly|词性:副词
Etymology: From Late Latin *discrētus*, past participle of *discernere* "to separate, distinguish," from *dis-* "apart" + *cernere* "to sift, distinguish."  The sense evolution went from "separate, distinct" to "prudent, circumspect" in Late Latin.
