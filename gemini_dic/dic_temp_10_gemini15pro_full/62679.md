engelbart:/ˈɛŋɡəlˌbɑːrt/| n.|恩格尔巴特 (人名);|Douglas Engelbart, an American engineer and inventor, is best known for his work on interactive computing. 美国工程师兼发明家道格拉斯·恩格尔巴特以其在交互式计算方面的工作而闻名。|例句：Engelbart's invention of the mouse revolutionized computer interaction. 恩格尔巴特发明鼠标彻底改变了人机交互方式。

例句：The Engelbart Award recognizes visionaries in the field of interactive computing. 恩格尔巴特奖旨在表彰交互计算领域的远见卓识者。|近义词: inventor, pioneer, innovator|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
