nonreinforcements:/ˌnɒnriːɪnˈfɔːsmənts/ | n. | 不强化，未强化 | Absence of reinforcement, especially in behavioral psychology; the act of not providing reinforcement.  This can lead to the extinction of a learned behavior. | The nonreinforcements led to a decrease in the frequency of the lever presses. 未强化导致按压杠杆的频率降低。

The therapist suggested nonreinforcements of the negative behavior. 治疗师建议不要强化负面行为。 | 近义词: non-reward, absence of reinforcement | 反义词: reinforcements, rewards | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "reinforcement" (from reinforce, ultimately from French "renforcer," meaning "to strengthen").
