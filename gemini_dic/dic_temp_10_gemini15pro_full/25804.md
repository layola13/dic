burgeon:/ˈbɜːrdʒən/
verb
迅速发展，蓬勃发展；萌芽
begin to grow or increase rapidly; flourish
例句：The city's population has burgeoned in recent years.  近年来，该市的人口迅速增长。

例句：New industries burgeoned in the area after the discovery of oil.  石油的发现促使该地区新兴产业蓬勃发展。

近义词：flourish, thrive, prosper, boom, expand, mushroom
反义词：decline, dwindle, shrink, contract, wither
词性：v.
