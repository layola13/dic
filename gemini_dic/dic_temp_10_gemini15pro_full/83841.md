halachic:/hɑːˈlɑːxɪk/ |adj.| 依据犹太律法的；与犹太律法相关的 | Relating to or prescribed by Jewish law; conforming to Jewish law. |  This is a halachic question that requires a rabbi's expertise. 这是一个需要拉比专业知识的犹太律法问题。

The family observes halachic dietary laws. 这个家庭遵守犹太律法的饮食规定。 | 近义词:  Jewish legal, Talmudic | 反义词: secular, non-halachic | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Hebrew *halakha* (הלכה), meaning "the way to go," referring to Jewish religious law derived from the Torah and Talmud.
