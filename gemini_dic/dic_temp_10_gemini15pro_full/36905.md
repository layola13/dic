coloristically: /ˌkʌlərˈɪstɪkli/
adverb
在色彩方面地，就色彩而言地
in terms of color; with regard to the use of color
例句：The film was coloristically stunning. 这部电影在色彩方面令人惊艳。

例句：The painting is coloristically rich and vibrant. 这幅画色彩丰富，充满活力。

Synonym: chromatically
Antonym: (No direct antonym, consider using phrases like "drably" or "monochromatically" depending on context)
词性: adverb
