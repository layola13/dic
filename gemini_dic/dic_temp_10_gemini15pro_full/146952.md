overinvestment:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈvɛstmənt/ | n. | 过度投资 | Excessive investment, often beyond the point of profitability or sustainability.  The act of investing too much capital in something. | The dot-com bubble was a period of significant overinvestment in internet-based companies.  互联网泡沫时期是对互联网公司过度投资的时期。

Overinvestment in the housing market led to a crash in prices. 对房地产市场的过度投资导致了价格暴跌。 | near synonyms: excess investment, overcapitalization, speculative bubble | antonyms: underinvestment, divestment | noun
