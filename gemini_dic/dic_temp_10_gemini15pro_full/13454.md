verb
tread upon; trample
He atrede the flowers in the garden. 他踩踏了花园里的花。
Synonyms: trample, tread on
Antonyms: avoid, protect
词性: 动词

Etymology:
From Middle English *atreden, from Old English *ætredan, from Proto-Germanic *at-tredaną (“to tread at, tread on”), equivalent to at + tread. Cognate with Old Frisian atreda (“to tread upon”), Middle Dutch attreden (“to tread upon”), Old High German attretan (“to tread upon”), German attreten.
