crushable: /ˈkrʌʃəbl/
| adj. | 易碎的；可压碎的；(非正式)令人心动的，迷恋的 |  Able to be crushed; (informal) inspiring a feeling of infatuation or love. | This type of plastic is crushable and recyclable. 这种塑料是可压碎和可回收的。  | 近义词: fragile, breakable, squeezable; adorable, cute, lovable | 反义词: unbreakable, sturdy, robust; unattractive, unappealing | 词性: adjective

**词源:**

From "crush" + "-able" + "-ities". "Crush" originates from Old French "croissir" meaning "to break, crack." The suffix "-able" forms adjectives meaning "capable of being," and "-ities" forms the plural of abstract nouns.
