apes:/eɪps/ | n. | 猿; 类人猿 | Large primates, closely related to monkeys but lacking a tail. |  The apes swung effortlessly through the trees. 猿猴轻松地在树间荡来荡去。 | synonyms: primates, simians | antonyms: humans | noun

apes:/eɪps/ | v. (third-person singular simple present apes, present participle aping, simple past and past participle aped) | 模仿; 效颦 | To imitate someone, often in a mocking or exaggerated way.  | He aped the teacher's mannerisms to make his friends laugh. 他模仿老师的举止来逗他的朋友们笑。 | synonyms: imitate, mimic, mock | antonyms: originate, innovate | verb
