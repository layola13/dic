choo-chooed:/ˌtʃuːˈtʃuːd/
verb
发出火车行驶的声音；模仿火车的声音
to make the sound of a train
The little boy choo-chooed as he pushed his toy train along the floor. 小男孩一边推着他的玩具火车在地板上跑，一边发出“呜——呜——”的火车声。
近义词: chugged, puffed
反义词: (No clear antonym)
词性: verb


choo-choo:/ˈtʃuːˈtʃuː/
noun
(儿童用语)火车
a child's word for a train
The toddler pointed at the train and shouted, "Choo-choo!"  幼儿指着火车大喊：“呜——呜——！”
近义词: train, locomotive (more formal)
反义词: (No clear antonym)
词性: noun


词源:  Onomatopoeia (拟声词), imitating the sound of a steam train.
