hysteranthous:/ˌhɪstərˈænθəs/
adjective
后出叶的，花先于叶开放的
Appearing or developing after the leaves have expanded; (of flowers) developing before the leaves.
The magnolia tree is hysteranthous, with its beautiful blossoms appearing before its foliage. 玉兰树是后出叶的，它美丽的花朵先于叶子出现。
Colchicums are hysteranthous, pushing up their flowers in autumn while the leaves don't appear until spring. 秋水仙是后出叶的，在秋天开花，而叶子直到春天出现。
近义词:proteranthous
反义词:synanthous
词性:adjective
