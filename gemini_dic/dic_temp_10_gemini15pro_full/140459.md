old-fashioned:/ˌoʊldˈfæʃənd/| adj.|老式的，过时的，守旧的|Characteristic of a past era or representing a style or custom that is no longer current.  It suggests something that is not modern or contemporary, and can sometimes have a slightly negative connotation, implying that something is outdated or no longer relevant.  However, it can also be used positively, suggesting something classic or traditional.|She was wearing an old-fashioned dress. 她穿着一件老式的连衣裙。

His views on marriage are rather old-fashioned. 他对婚姻的看法相当守旧。|近义词：outdated, antiquated, archaic, vintage, retro, traditional, classic|反义词：modern, contemporary, current, fashionable, trendy|词性:adjective
