monoousian:/ˌmɒnəʊˈuːsiən/ | adj. | 同本质的，单一本体的 | Of or relating to the doctrine of Monoousianism, which asserts that Jesus Christ is of the same substance as God the Father.  |  The Council of Nicaea affirmed the monoousian doctrine.  尼西亚会议肯定了同本质的教义。 |  homousian |  heterousian | adjective
近义词:homousian
反义词:heterousian
词性:adjective
词源:From Greek *monoousios* (μόνοούσιος), from *monos* (μόνος, “only, single, alone”) + *ousia* (οὐσία, “being, essence, substance”).
