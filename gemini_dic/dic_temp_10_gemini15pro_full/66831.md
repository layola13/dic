expatiate:/ɪkˈspeɪʃieɪt/ /ɛkˈspeɪʃɪˌeɪt/ | v. | 详述，细说 | to speak or write at length |  He expatiated on the benefits of vegetarianism. 他详细阐述了素食主义的好处。

The professor expatiated upon the history of the Roman Empire. 教授详细讲述了罗马帝国的历史。

| 近义词：elaborate, expand, enlarge, discuss at length | 反义词: summarize, condense, abbreviate | 词性: 动词
