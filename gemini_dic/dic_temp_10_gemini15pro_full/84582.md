hammy:/ˈhæmi/| adj.|做作的，夸张的，过火的|Exaggeratedly theatrical; overacted.  Often used to describe acting that is too dramatic or artificial.|His performance was so hammy it was almost comical. 他的表演太做作了，简直滑稽。|近义词：overdramatic, theatrical, melodramatic, over-the-top|反义词：understated, subtle, natural, realistic|词性:形容词

词源: 1920s, from ham actor + -y.  "Ham actor" refers to a performer who overacts.  This term likely originated from minstrel shows, where burnt cork, used for blackface makeup, was sometimes kept in a ham fat container, suggesting amateurishness or low quality.
