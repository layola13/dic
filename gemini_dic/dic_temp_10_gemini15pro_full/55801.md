divertingness:/daɪˈvɜːrtɪŋnɪs/| n. |娱乐性，趣味性|The quality of being diverting; entertaining or amusing.|The divertingness of the movie made the time fly by. 这部电影的趣味性让时间过得飞快。|近义词：amusement, entertainment, fun, interest, pleasure|反义词：boredom, dullness, tediousness|词性:名词
Etymology: From diverting + -ness.  Diverting comes from the Old French divertir, from Latin divertere "to turn aside, distract," from dis- "aside" + vertere "to turn."
