identifiable:/aɪˌdɛntɪˈfaɪəbl/ | adj. |可识别的，可辨认的 | able to be recognized or identified. | The suspect was easily identifiable by his distinctive tattoo.  嫌疑犯很容易通过他独特的纹身被认出来。| recognizable, distinguishable, distinct | unidentifiable, indistinguishable, anonymous | adjective

identifiable:/aɪˌdɛntɪˈfaɪəbl/ | adj. |可识别的，可辨认的 | capable of being identified. | The house is easily identifiable by its bright red door. 这房子很容易通过它鲜红色的门被辨认出来。| discernible, noticeable | unrecognizable, obscure | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From identify + -able.  Identify comes from Late Latin identificare "to make identical," from Latin idem "same" + facere "to make."
