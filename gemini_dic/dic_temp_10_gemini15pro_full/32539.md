chipmunk:/ˈtʃɪpˌmʌŋk/| n. |花栗鼠|A small, striped rodent of the squirrel family, typically with large cheek pouches.|I saw a chipmunk scampering up a tree. 我看到一只花栗鼠跑上树。|The chipmunk buried its nuts for the winter.  这只花栗鼠把坚果埋起来过冬。|近义词：ground squirrel, squirrel|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Ojibwe word *ačitamo·nk*, meaning "striped squirrel".  Borrowed into English via Odawa.
