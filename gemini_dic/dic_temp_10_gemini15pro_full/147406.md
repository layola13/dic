overnormalities:/ˌoʊvərˌnɔːrˈmælɪtiz/| n.| 过度正常化；过度常态化|The state of being excessively or abnormally normal, often in a way that seems artificial or unsettling.  A situation where things appear normal on the surface, but underlying issues or tensions are being suppressed or ignored. | The overnormalities of suburban life can be stifling to some. 郊区生活过于常态化，对某些人来说可能会感到压抑。| synonyms: hypernormality, excessive normalcy, artificial normalcy | antonyms: abnormality, deviance, eccentricity | noun
