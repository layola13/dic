monomictic:/ˌmɒnəˈmɪktɪk/ | adj. | 單循環的 |  Relating to a lake or other body of water that mixes from top to bottom once a year. | Lake Superior is a monomictic lake, mixing only once a year in the fall. 苏必利尔湖是一个单循环湖，每年秋天只混合一次。|  holomictic | meromictic, dimictic, polymictic | adjective
