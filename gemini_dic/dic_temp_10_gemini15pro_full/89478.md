honeyedness:/ˈhʌnidnɪs/ | n. | 甜蜜，甜言蜜语，奉承 | The quality of being honeyed; sweetness; excessive flattery. | Her voice dripped with honeyedness, but I knew she wasn't being sincere. 她的声音充满了甜蜜，但我知道她并不真诚。|  The honeyedness of his words couldn't mask the underlying threat. 他甜言蜜语掩盖不了潜在的威胁。 | 近义词：sweetness, flattery, blandishment | 反义词：bitterness, acrimony, harshness | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From honeyed + -ness.  Honeyed itself comes from honey + -ed.  Ultimately from Old English hunig.
