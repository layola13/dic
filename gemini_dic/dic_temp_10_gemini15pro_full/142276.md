orach:/ˈɒrətʃ/ /ˈɔːrətʃ/ | n. |海滨藜，海滩植物| A kind of plant that grows on seashores, also known as saltbush or sea purslane. It is often used in salads or as a cooked vegetable. | Example: We picked some fresh orach from the beach and added it to our salad. 我们从海滩采摘了一些新鲜的海滨藜，并把它添加到沙拉中。

Example: Orach is a hardy plant that can tolerate salty soil. 海滨藜是一种耐寒的植物，可以耐受含盐的土壤。| 近义词:saltbush, sea purslane | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词
