effacement:/ɪˈfeɪsmənt/
n.
抹去，消除; 擦掉; 湮没
the act of erasing or removing something
The effacement of the inscription made it impossible to date the tombstone.  碑文的抹去使得墓碑的年代无法确定。

近义词:obliteration, erasure, deletion, removal
反义词:preservation, restoration, enhancement
