crower:/ˈkrəʊər/| n. |啼叫者，鸣叫者|A crower is something or someone that crows, typically referring to a rooster. |The crower awoke everyone at dawn.  公鸡在黎明时分叫醒了所有人。|近义词：rooster, cock |反义词: None |词性: noun
Etymology: From the verb "to crow" + "-er" (agent suffix).  来源于动词 "crow" (啼叫) + "-er" (表示行为者的后缀).
