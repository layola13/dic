churners:/ˈtʃɜːrnərz/
noun
客户流失者，频繁更换服务提供商的人
People who frequently change service providers, especially those who leave a company for a competitor.
The company is trying to reduce the number of churners by offering better customer service.  这家公司正试图通过提供更好的客户服务来减少客户流失。
High churn rates can be a problem for businesses. 高流失率对企业来说可能是个问题。
synonyms: defectors, switchers
antonyms: loyal customers
n.
