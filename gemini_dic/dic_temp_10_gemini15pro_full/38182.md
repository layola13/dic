comptometer:/kəmˈpɑːmɪtər/ |n.|计算器|A key-driven calculating machine that adds, subtracts, multiplies, and divides.  It was widely used before electronic calculators became common.|Example 1: The accountant used a comptometer to quickly add up the figures. 会计师用计算器快速地将数字加起来。

Example 2:  Comptometers were essential tools in offices before the digital age. 在数字时代之前，计算器是办公室必不可少的工具。|Synonym: calculator, adding machine|Antonym: abacus (arguably, as it's a manual calculation device)|noun
