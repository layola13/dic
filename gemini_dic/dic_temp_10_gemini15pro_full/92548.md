hyrse:/ˈhɪərsə/ | n. | millet | A cereal grass cultivated for its grain and used as food for humans and livestock. | The farmer planted hyrse in the field. 农民在地里种了小米。| 近义词: millet | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a type of grain) | 词性: noun


Etymology:
From Old English *hyrse, from Proto-Germanic *hirsi, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱers-, meaning "dry, rough".  Cognate with Dutch gierst, German Hirse.
