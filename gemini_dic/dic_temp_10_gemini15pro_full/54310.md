dishabited:/dɪsˈhæbɪtɪd/
adjective
无人居住的；荒无人烟的
No longer inhabited; deserted.
The dishabited village stood silent and empty.  这个无人居住的村庄寂静而空旷。
The explorers discovered a dishabited island. 探险家们发现了一个荒无人烟的岛屿。
Synonyms: deserted, abandoned, uninhabited, desolate
Antonyms: inhabited, populated, occupied
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation, reversal) + habit (to dwell) + -ed (forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin habitare (to dwell).
