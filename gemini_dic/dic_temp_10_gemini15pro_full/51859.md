dextroglucose:/ˌdɛkstroʊˈɡluːkoʊs/ | n. | 右旋葡萄糖 | A form of glucose that rotates plane-polarized light to the right.  It is the most common naturally occurring form of glucose. | Dextroglucose is the primary source of energy for the human body. 右旋葡萄糖是人体主要的能量来源。 |  Dextroglucose is often used in intravenous solutions. 右旋葡萄糖常用于静脉注射液。 | synonyms: D-glucose, glucose | antonyms: L-glucose | noun
