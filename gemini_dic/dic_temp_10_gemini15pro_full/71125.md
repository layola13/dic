fishily:/ˈfɪʃɪli/| adv.|鱼腥地；可疑地；冷淡地|In a manner suggestive of a fish; suspiciously; coldly.|He looked at her fishily. 他用怀疑的眼光看着她。|近义词：suspiciously, distrustfully, dubiously|反义词：trustingly, openly, frankly|词性:副词

词源: From fish + -ily.  The original sense was literally "like a fish," referring to the smell. The figurative sense of "suspiciously" developed later, perhaps based on the idea of a fish's cold, unblinking stare.
