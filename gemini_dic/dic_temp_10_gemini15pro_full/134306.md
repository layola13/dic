noninsects:/nɒnˈɪnsɛkts/ | n. | 非昆虫 | Animals that are not insects; any member of the animal kingdom that is not an insect.  |  Example: Spiders, mites, and centipedes are noninsects. 蜘蛛、螨虫和蜈蚣都是非昆虫。 | Example: Many people mistakenly think spiders are insects, but they are actually noninsects. 很多人误以为蜘蛛是昆虫，但它们实际上是非昆虫。 | Synonyms: invertebrates (some), non-insect animals | Antonyms: insects | Noun
