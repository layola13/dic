nonoiliest: /nɒnˈɔɪliɪst/ | adj. | 最不油腻的 |  Formed with the double negative prefix "non-" and the suffix "-iest" added to the adjective "oily."  It means least oily or having the least amount of oil. | This is the nonoiliest sunscreen I've ever used. 这是我用过最不油腻的防晒霜。 | The chicken breast was surprisingly nonoiliest. 这块鸡胸肉出奇地不油腻。 | 近义词: least oily, dry, non-greasy | 反义词: oiliest, greasy, oily | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From "non-" (not) + "oily" + "-est" (superlative suffix). "Oily" ultimately comes from the Old English word "ælig."
