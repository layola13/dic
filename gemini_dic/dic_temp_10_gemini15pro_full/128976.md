nip-and-tuck:/ˌnɪp ən ˈtʌk/| adj. n.|势均力敌的，不相上下的; 拉皮手术，整容手术|A nip-and-tuck race or contest is very close and exciting because the competitors are so equal.  A nip and tuck is also a cosmetic surgery operation to remove wrinkles and other defects of the skin.|例句：The election was nip and tuck until the very end. 选举直到最后都势均力敌。

例句：She had a nip and tuck to make herself look younger. 她做了个拉皮手术让自己看起来更年轻。|近义词：close, tight (for race/contest); facelift, cosmetic surgery (for operation)|反义词：one-sided, decisive (for race/contest)|词性：adjective, noun
