aggregable:/ˈæɡrɪɡəbl/
adjective
可聚合的
Able to be aggregated or collected into a mass or sum.
The data is aggregable by region and time period.  这些数据可以按地区和时间段进行汇总。
The individual components are aggregable into a larger system.  单个组件可以聚合到一个更大的系统中。
近义词: combinable, collectable, summable
反义词: indivisible, inseparable
词性: adjective

词源: From aggregate + -able.  来自 aggregate + -able。
