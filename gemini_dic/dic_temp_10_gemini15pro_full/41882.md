cosherer:/ˈkoʊʃərər/| n.|犹太教律法认可的屠宰员；犹太教饮食监督员|A person who slaughters animals for food according to Jewish dietary laws or supervises the preparation of food to ensure it conforms to kashrut.|例句：The kosherer expertly prepared the meat for the Sabbath. 屠宰员熟练地为安息日准备了肉。

例句：The restaurant employed a kosherer to oversee the kitchen. 这家餐馆雇佣了一位犹太教饮食监督员来监督厨房。|近义词：shochet (specifically for slaughtering)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Yiddish כּשר (kosher, "ritually fit"), from Hebrew כָּשֵׁר (kasher).
