nonchronical: /nɑːnˈkrɑːnɪkəl/
adjective
非慢性的；非长期的
Not chronic; not of long duration or frequent recurrence.
His cough was nonchronical and cleared up within a week. 他的咳嗽并非慢性病，一周内就好了。
近义词: acute, temporary, transient
反义词: chronic, persistent, long-lasting
词性: adjective
词源:  "non-" (not) + "chronical" (chronic). "Chronic" comes from the Greek "khronikos" (relating to time), from "khronos" (time).
