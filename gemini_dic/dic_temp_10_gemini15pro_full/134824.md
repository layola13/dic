nonminorities:/ˌnɒnmaɪˈnɒrɪtiz/ |n.| 非少数族裔；多数族裔 |  People who do not belong to a minority group.  This term often refers to the dominant group in a society. |  The survey focused on the opinions of nonminorities.  这项调查重点关注非少数族裔的意见。 | The government implemented policies to benefit nonminorities. 政府实施了有利于非少数族裔的政策。 | synonyms: majority, dominant group | antonyms: minorities, minority groups | noun
