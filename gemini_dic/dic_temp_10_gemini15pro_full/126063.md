PHRASE->
pass muster: 符合要求，合格 | His work didn't quite pass muster. 他的工作不太合格。
muster up: 鼓起，振作 | I couldn't muster up the enthusiasm to go to the party. 我提不起劲去参加聚会。
muster (something) up: 聚集，召集 | We managed to muster up a team for the competition. 我们设法组建了一支队伍参加比赛。
muster (someone/something) out: 解散，遣散 | The soldiers were mustered out after the war. 战后士兵们被遣散了。
muster strength/courage: 鼓起力量/勇气 | He mustered the strength to carry on. 他鼓起力量继续前进。


注意事项: 注意muster既可以作名词也可以作动词，意义有所区别。
