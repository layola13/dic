exciteful: /ɪkˈsaɪtfəl/ | adj. | 令人兴奋的;  激动人心的 |  This word is not standard English.  While it seems like a logical formation (excite + -ful), the standard adjective is "exciting." It means causing feelings of excitement and enthusiasm. |  While "exciteful" might be used informally, it is generally considered incorrect. Try using "exciting" instead.  |  近义词: exciting, thrilling, stimulating | 反义词: boring, dull, tedious | 词性: 形容词 (incorrect usage, use "exciting" instead)
