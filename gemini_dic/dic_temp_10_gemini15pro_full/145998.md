overdomesticating:/ˌoʊvərdoʊˈmɛstɪkeɪtɪŋ/ |v. |过度驯化|The act of domesticating something to an excessive degree, often resulting in negative consequences.| We've overdomesticated our pets, to the point where many breeds suffer from health problems. 我们过度驯养了我们的宠物，以至于许多品种都患有健康问题。|Over-domestication led to a decline in the wild instincts of the animals. 过度驯化导致动物的野生本能下降。| 近义词: over-taming, over-cultivating | 反义词: rewilding |词性: 动词
