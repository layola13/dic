obsequy: /ˈɒbsɪkwɪ/ (UK) /ˈɑːbsəkwɪ/ (US)| n. |葬礼，丧葬仪式；哀悼| a funeral rite or ceremony |例句：The obsequies were held in the local church. 葬礼在当地教堂举行。

例句：She performed the last obsequies for her deceased husband. 她为她已故的丈夫举行了最后的葬礼。

obsequy: /ˈɒbsɪkwɪ/ (UK) /ˈɑːbsəkwɪ/ (US)| n. (plural obsequies) | 哀悼; 悼念(的言行)  |An expression of mourning; something done to show respect for the dead|例句：The flowers were a touching obsequy. 这些花是对死者的感人悼念。
