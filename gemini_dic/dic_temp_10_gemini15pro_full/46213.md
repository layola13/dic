cyst's:/sɪsts/
noun
囊肿的；包囊的
Possessive form of cyst, meaning belonging to or associated with a cyst.
The cyst's membrane was thick.  囊肿的膜很厚。
The doctor examined the cyst's contents. 医生检查了囊肿的内容物。
Synonyms:  (For cyst itself) growth, lump, tumor
Antonyms: (For cyst itself)  health, normalcy
词性: noun (possessive)
