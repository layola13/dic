foxfish:/ˈfɑːksˌfɪʃ/| n. |狐鱼，海鲂|A type of marine fish, also known as gurnard, characterized by large pectoral fins and a bony head.  These fish are bottom-dwellers and are known for their ability to "grunt" or make noises.|We caught a foxfish while fishing off the coast. 我们在海岸边钓鱼时捕到了一条狐鱼。|近义词：gurnard, sea robin|反义词:|n.
