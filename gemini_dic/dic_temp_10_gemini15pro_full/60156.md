effeminate:/ɪˈfɛmɪnət/ | adj. | 柔弱的，女性化的 | Having or showing characteristics regarded as typical of a woman; unmanly. |  He had an effeminate manner that some people found off-putting. 他有一种女人气的举止，有些人觉得令人反感。|  Synonyms: womanish, unmanly, sissy, girly | Antonyms: manly, masculine, virile | adjective


effeminate: /ɪˈfɛmɪnət/ | v. | 使柔弱，使女性化 (较少用作动词) | To make or become effeminate. | Constant pampering had effeminated him.  不断的娇惯使他变得柔弱。 | Synonyms: emasculate, weaken | Antonyms: strengthen, masculinize | verb


Etymology: From Latin *effeminatus*, past participle of *effeminare* "to make womanish," from *ex-* "out" (see ex-) + *femina* "woman" (see feminine).
