freudianisms:/frɔɪdiənɪzəmz/
|n.|弗洛伊德主义，弗洛伊德学说；精神分析论；|Theories or interpretations based on the work of Sigmund Freud, especially relating to the unconscious mind, sexual drives, and dream analysis.|His constant talk about mothers and childhood traumas was full of freudianisms. 他不断谈论母亲和童年创伤，充满了弗洛伊德主义。|近义词:psychoanalysis, Freudian theories|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider opposing schools of psychological thought)|词性:名词
