orphrey:/ˈɔːrfri/| n.|金线绣花边，法衣上的装饰带|A richly embroidered band or border, especially one on an ecclesiastical vestment.|The priest's orphrey depicted scenes from the life of Christ.  神父的法衣上的绣花边描绘了基督生平的场景。|The orphrey was adorned with gold thread and precious stones. 这条金线绣花边饰有金线和宝石。|Synonyms: embroidery, trimming, decoration|Antonyms: plain, undecorated|noun
