cools:/kuːlz/| n. (plural simple present)|凉爽，凉快|A state of being cool or cooler.|The evening cools are welcome after a hot day. 炎热的一天过后，傍晚的凉爽令人惬意。|近义词：chill, coolness|反义词:heat, warmth|noun
