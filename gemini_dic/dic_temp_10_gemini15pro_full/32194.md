chibuya:/tʃiˈbuːjə/
n.
涩谷（日本东京的一个区）
Shibuya is a special ward in Tokyo, Japan, known for its scramble crossing and trendy fashion scene.
Shibuya is one of the most vibrant and fashionable areas in Tokyo.  涩谷是东京最具活力和时尚的地区之一。
The iconic Shibuya scramble crossing is featured in many movies and TV shows.  标志性的涩谷十字路口出现在许多电影和电视节目中。
近义词: district, area, ward
反义词: (无明确的反义词，可以考虑用rural area如果要表达与都市的对比)
