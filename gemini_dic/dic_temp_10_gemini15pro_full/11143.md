archeological:/ˌɑːrkiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ (US) /ˌɑːrkɪəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/| adj.|考古学的；考古学上的|Relating to the study of human history and prehistory through the excavation of sites and the analysis of artifacts and other physical remains.|例句：The archeological dig unearthed a Roman mosaic. 考古挖掘出土了一幅罗马马赛克画。|例句：The team made an archeological discovery of great significance. 该团队取得了一项具有重大意义的考古发现。|近义词：archaeologic|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective
