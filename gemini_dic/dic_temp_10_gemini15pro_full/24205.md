bridesmaids:/ˈbraɪdzmeɪdz/| n. |伴娘|A woman who attends a wedding as an assistant and companion to the bride.|例句：The bridesmaids wore matching pink dresses. 伴娘们穿着配套的粉色连衣裙。

例句：She asked her best friend to be her bridesmaid. 她邀请她最好的朋友做她的伴娘。|近义词：maid of honor (首席伴娘), attendant (服务员)|反义词: groomsmen (伴郎), best man (男傧相) | noun
