lts:/ɛl tiː ɛs/ |abbr.| 长期支持 |Long Term Support.  A product lifecycle management policy in which a stable release of a software program is maintained for a longer period of time than the standard edition. This allows users to rely on a specific version for an extended duration without the need for frequent upgrades.|The LTS version of Ubuntu is a good choice for servers. |Ubuntu LTS版本是服务器的理想选择。|近义词:stable release|反义词:short-term support|n.
