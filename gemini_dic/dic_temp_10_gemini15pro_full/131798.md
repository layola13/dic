nonconscriptable:/ˌnɒnkənˈskrɪptəbl/ | adj. | 免役的，不能被征召的 | Not liable or eligible to be conscripted (enrolled into compulsory military service). |  Example:  He was declared nonconscriptable due to a medical condition. 他由于身体状况被宣布免役。

Example:  Many religious groups advocate for the rights of nonconscriptable citizens. 许多宗教团体倡导免役公民的权利。 | Synonym: exempt, ineligible | Antonym: conscriptable, eligible | Word type: Adjective
