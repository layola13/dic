curveyer:/ˈkɜːrvɪər/| n.|曲线仪，弯道测量器|A device used to measure curves, especially in roads or railways.|The surveyor used a curveyer to accurately map the winding mountain road. 测量员使用曲线仪精确地绘制了蜿蜒的山路。|近义词：curvimeter, odometer|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "straightedge" in a metaphorical sense)|noun

Etymology:  "curve" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting instruments or agents). "Curve" ultimately comes from Latin "curvus" (bent, curved). "-er" is a common Germanic suffix.
