citrange:/ˈsɪtrɪndʒ/ | n. |香吉士，柑橘橙|A hybrid fruit produced by crossing a sweet orange (Citrus sinensis) with a trifoliate orange (Poncirus trifoliata). It is typically smaller than an orange and has a tart flavor.|The citrange tree is hardy and can withstand colder temperatures than most orange trees.  香吉士树很耐寒，比大多数橙子树更能承受低温。| The citrange is a popular rootstock for other citrus varieties. 香吉士是其他柑橘品种的常用砧木。|Synonyms:  citrus hybrid|Antonyms: N/A|noun
