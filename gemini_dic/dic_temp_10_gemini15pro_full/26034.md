bushelful:/ˈbʊʃəlˌfʊl/ (US) /ˈbʊʃl̩fʊl/ (UK)| n. | 一蒲式耳；大量的 | A bushel is a unit of dry volume equal to 8 gallons (approximately 36 liters), and a bushelful is the amount that fills a bushel. It can also be used figuratively to mean a large quantity. |  The farmer harvested a bushelful of apples. 农民收获了一蒲式耳的苹果。| synonym: a lot, abundance, plenty | antonym: little, scarcity, lack | noun
