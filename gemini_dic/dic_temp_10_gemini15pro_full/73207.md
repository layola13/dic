forcednesses:/ˈfɔːrsɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
强制性，被迫性
The state of being forced or compelled.
The forcednesses of the situation led to several regrettable decisions.  形势的逼迫导致了几个令人遗憾的决定。
Synonyms: compulsions, constraints, obligations
Antonyms: voluntariness, willingness, freedom
