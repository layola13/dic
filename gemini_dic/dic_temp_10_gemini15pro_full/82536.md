guadalupe:/ˌɡwɑːdəˈluːpeɪ/ /ˌɡwɑːdəˈluːpi/
proper noun|瓜达卢佩|A Spanish given name, derived from Arabic, meaning "river of the wolf". It also refers to various places named after Our Lady of Guadalupe, a Catholic title of the Virgin Mary.|Our Lady of Guadalupe is highly venerated in Mexico.|近义词: (For the name) Maria, (For the places) none|反义词: none|词性:proper noun

guadalupe:/ˌɡwɑːdəˈluːpeɪ/ /ˌɡwɑːdəˈluːpi/
place name|瓜达卢佩|Various places named after Our Lady of Guadalupe, including a river, towns, and islands.|Guadalupe Island is a popular destination for great white shark cage diving.|近义词: none|反义词: none|词性:place name
