ovicapsule:/ˈoʊvɪˌkæpsjuːl/
noun
卵囊，卵壳
A membrane enclosing an ovum.  A capsule containing an egg.
The ovicapsule protects the developing embryo. 卵囊保护发育中的胚胎。
Fish lay their eggs in ovicapsules. 鱼在卵囊中产卵。
Synonyms: egg case, egg capsule, egg membrane
Antonyms: N/A (lack of a direct antonym for a biological structure)
词性: n.
