busked:/bʌskt/
|v.|街头卖艺；以卖艺为生|Performed music or other entertainment in a public place for money. |He busked on the streets of Paris to earn a living. 他在巴黎街头卖艺谋生。|Synonyms: perform, play, entertain|Antonyms: retire, quit|verb

busking:/ˈbʌskɪŋ/
|n.|街头卖艺|The activity of performing music or other entertainment in a public place for money.|Busking is a common way for young musicians to gain experience. 街头卖艺是年轻音乐家积累经验的常见方式。|Synonyms: street performing, performing arts|Antonyms:  formal employment, professional performance|noun
