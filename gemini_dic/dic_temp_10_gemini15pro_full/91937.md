hypermetaphoric:/ˌhaɪpərˌmɛtəˈfɔːrɪk/ | adj. | 超级隐喻的，极度隐喻的 |  Excessively or elaborately metaphoric; using metaphors layered upon metaphors.  It goes beyond a simple comparison and creates a complex, multi-layered image through extended or interwoven metaphors. | The poet's hypermetaphoric language made his work dense and difficult to interpret.  这位诗人极度隐喻的语言使他的作品晦涩难懂。

Her hypermetaphoric description of the city as a "concrete jungle teeming with steel predators" was striking. 她将这座城市超级隐喻地描述为“钢筋水泥丛林，到处都是钢铁捕食者”，这令人印象深刻。 | 近义词：excessively metaphoric, highly figurative, elaborately metaphorical | 反义词：literal, plain, unfigurative | 词性: adjective
