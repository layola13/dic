mozarab: /ˈmoʊzəræb/| n. adj.|摩尔人统治下的基督教徒; 受摩尔文化影响的基督教徒|A Mozarab was a Christian living under Muslim rule in medieval Spain.  The term can also refer to their culture, especially their Romance language.| The Mozarabic Rite is a liturgy once used by Mozarabs. 摩尔基督教仪式是摩尔人统治下的基督教徒曾经使用的一种礼拜仪式。 | The Mozarabic language was influenced by Arabic. 摩尔基督教语言受到阿拉伯语的影响。|Synonyms:  Musta'rib (Arabic term),  Dhimmi (general term for non-Muslims under Muslim rule) | Antonyms: Muslim, Moor| Noun, Adjective
