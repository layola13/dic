armrack: /ˈɑːmˌræk/ | n. | 武器架；枪架 | A framework, typically made of wood or metal, designed to hold firearms securely. |  Example: The soldier carefully placed his rifle in the armrack. 士兵小心翼翼地把步枪放在枪架上。
