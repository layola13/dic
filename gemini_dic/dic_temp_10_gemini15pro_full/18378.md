bellycheat:/ˈbɛliˌtʃit/| n.|欺骗，作弊（尤指在比赛中通过不正当手段获胜）|An act of cheating or deception, especially in a competition, often involving hidden or unfair methods.|He won the game by a bellycheat, hiding a card up his sleeve. 他通过藏牌在袖子里作弊赢得了比赛。|近义词：cheating, deception, fraud, trickery|反义词：fairness, honesty|词性: 名词

词源：belly (腹部) + cheat (欺骗).  This is a relatively informal and less common word. The "belly" part likely refers to the idea of concealing something close to one's body, or perhaps to the gut feeling of dishonesty associated with the act.  It doesn't have a well-documented etymology.
