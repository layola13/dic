hydroxyl:/haɪˈdrɒksɪl/ /haɪˈdrɑːksɪl/| n. |羟基; 氢氧基|A hydroxyl is a functional group consisting of an oxygen atom covalently bonded to a hydrogen atom.  It is represented by the formula -OH and is characteristic of alcohols, phenols, and carboxylic acids.|Ethanol contains a hydroxyl group. 乙醇含有一个羟基。|synonyms: hydroxide, alcohol group|antonyms: N/A|noun
