overintellectual:/ˌoʊvərˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəl/
adjective
过度理智的；过cerebral的
Thinking too much about something; excessively intellectual.
He was criticized for being overintellectual and out of touch with ordinary people. 他被批评为过度理智，脱离群众。
近义词: cerebral, highbrow, intellectualistic
反义词: simple, unintellectual, pragmatic
词性: adjective

overintellectual:/ˌoʊvərˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəl/
noun
过度理智的人
A person who thinks too much about something; an excessively intellectual person.
The group was composed mainly of overintellectuals who spent more time theorizing than acting. 这个团体主要由一些过度理智的人组成，他们把更多的时间花在理论化而不是行动上。
近义词: intellectual, egghead
反义词: simpleton
词性: noun
