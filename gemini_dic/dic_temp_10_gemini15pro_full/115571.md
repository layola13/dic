maramani: /mɑːrɑːˈmɑːniː/
n.
maramani (plural maramanis)
马拉马尼（一种东非的树脂，用作熏香和药用）
Maramani is a resin used in traditional East African medicine and as incense.
马拉马尼是一种用于东非传统医学和熏香的树脂。
The scent of burning maramani filled the air.  燃烧的马拉马尼的香味弥漫在空气中。
近义词: frankincense, myrrh
反义词:  None (lacks a direct opposite)
词性: noun
