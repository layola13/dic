nonstudiousness:/ˌnɒnˌstjuːdiəsnəs/ | n. | 不爱学习；不用功 | Lack of studiousness; the characteristic of not being studious or diligent in study. | His nonstudiousness resulted in poor grades. 他的不爱学习导致成绩很差。|  He showed a marked nonstudiousness, preferring to play video games than read.  他表现出明显的不爱学习，宁愿玩电子游戏也不愿读书。 | synonyms: indolence, laziness, idleness, inactivity, apathy, indifference | antonyms: studiousness, diligence, assiduity, application, industry | noun
