infinitesimalness:/ˌɪnfɪnɪˈtɛsɪməlnəs/| n. |无限小；极微小；微不足道|The infinitesimalness of the dust particles made them invisible to the naked eye.|例句：The infinitesimalness of the change made it practically imperceptible.  这种变化极其微小，几乎难以察觉。

例句：He stressed the infinitesimalness of the risk involved in the procedure. 他强调了该程序风险的微不足道。|近义词：minuteness, tininess, microscopicity|反义词：immensity, vastness, enormity|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From infinitesimal + -ness.  Infinitesimal comes from Late Latin infinitesimus, meaning "infinite-th,"  based on Latin infinitus ("infinite")  + -esimus (ordinal number suffix).
