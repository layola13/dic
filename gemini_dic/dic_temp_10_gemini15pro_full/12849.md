associator:/əˈsoʊʃiˌeɪtər/ |n.| 关联者，结合者，联想者 |Someone or something that associates or connects things.  A person who joins with others for a common purpose, especially in business. In mathematics, an element in a non-associative algebra that measures the failure of associativity.|例句：He is an associator of the local historical society. 他是当地历史学会的会员。

例句：The associator of the two companies benefited both.  两家公司的合作使双方都受益。| 近义词：partner, colleague, affiliate, member |反义词：opponent, rival| noun
