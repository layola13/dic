miniaturists:/ˈmɪnɪətʃərɪsts/| n. |微型画家；微型艺术家；微型复制品制作者|A person who paints or creates miniature works of art, especially portraits.|The museum held an exhibition showcasing the intricate work of 18th-century miniaturists.|Synonyms: miniature painter, limner|Antonyms: muralist, large-scale artist|noun


miniature:/ˈmɪnɪtʃər/ | adj. n. |微型的；缩影的；微型复制品；缩影|A small version of something; a very small painting, especially a portrait.|She collects miniature porcelain dolls.|Synonyms: tiny, small, diminutive|Antonyms: giant, huge, enormous|adjective, noun
