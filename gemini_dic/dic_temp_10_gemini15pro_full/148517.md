oversum:/ˈoʊvərˌsʌm/| n.| 总数，总和|A value obtained by adding together all the elements in a set or list.  The oversum of the numbers 1, 2, and 3 is 6. |The oversum of everyone's donations was surprisingly high.|近义词：sum, total, aggregate|反义词: deficit, shortfall|词性:名词

词源:  A combination of "over" (indicating totality or completeness) and "sum" (meaning the result of addition).  While not a standard dictionary entry, it is used in specialized fields like mathematics and computer science.
