double-parks:/ˈdʌbəl pɑːrks/ (US) /ˈdʌbl pɑːks/ (UK)| v.| 并排停车; 双排停车|The act of parking a vehicle parallel to another vehicle that is already parked at the curb, thus blocking the other vehicle and obstructing traffic.| He double-parked his car outside the store. 他把车双排停在商店外面.|Synonyms: parallel park illegally, block traffic|Antonyms: park legally, park in a designated spot|verb

double-parks:/ˈdʌbəl pɑːrks/ (US) /ˈdʌbl pɑːks/ (UK)| n.| 并排停车; 双排停车 (指这种行为)|The act of parking a vehicle parallel to another vehicle that is already parked at the curb, thus blocking the other vehicle and obstructing traffic.| Double-parking is illegal in most cities. 在大多数城市，双排停车是违法的.|Synonyms: illegal parking, traffic obstruction|Antonyms: legal parking|noun
