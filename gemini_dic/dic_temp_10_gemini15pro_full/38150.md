compression:/kəmˈprɛʃən/ | n. |压缩，浓缩，压迫|The act of pressing or squeezing something together to reduce its size or volume. Also, the reduction in size or volume resulting from this act. |Data compression helps save storage space. 数据压缩有助于节省存储空间。 | The compression bandage helped reduce the swelling.  压缩绷带帮助消肿了。 | synonyms: compaction, condensation, constriction | antonyms: expansion, decompression, inflation | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *compressionem, from Latin compress-, past participle stem of comprimere "to press together," from com- "together" + premere "to press."
