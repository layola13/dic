cragginess:/ˈkræɡɪnɪs/
n.
崎岖，陡峭
The quality of being craggy; ruggedness.
The cragginess of the cliffs made climbing difficult. 悬崖的陡峭使得攀登变得困难。
ruggedness, steepness, unevenness
smoothness, flatness
