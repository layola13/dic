fatefully:/ˈfeɪtfʊli/ | adv. | 宿命地；命运般地；不幸地 | in a way that is controlled by fate; in a way that has an unpleasant and often disastrous effect |  He looked at her fatefully. 他带着宿命感看着她。|  The expedition ended fatefully. 这次探险不幸结束了。 |近义词：ominously, disastrously, tragically | 反义词：fortunately, luckily | 词性:副词

Etymology->
From *fate* (from Latin *fatum* "that which has been spoken; destiny, fate") + *-ful* (meaning "full of") + *-ly* (adverbial suffix).
