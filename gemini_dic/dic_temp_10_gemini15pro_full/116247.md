mass-produce:/ˌmæs prəˈdjuːs/ /ˌmæs prəˈduːs/ | v. | 大量生产；批量生产 | To manufacture (something) in very large quantities, especially by using standardized mechanical processes. |  Many companies now mass-produce inexpensive furniture. 许多公司现在都在大量生产廉价家具。

The factory mass-produces cars for export. 这家工厂批量生产汽车供出口。

近义词：manufacture, produce in bulk, churn out

反义词：handcraft, custom-make

词性：动词
