mandolin:/ˈmændəlɪn/ | n. | 曼陀林 | A small, stringed musical instrument, similar to a lute, with a pear-shaped body and a fretted neck. Played with a plectrum. |  He played a lively tune on his mandolin. 他用他的曼陀林演奏了一首轻快的曲子。 | synonyms: lute, bandurria | antonyms: None | noun

mandolin:/ˈmændəlɪn/ | n. | 曼陀铃 | A kitchen utensil with sharp blades for slicing vegetables, etc., into thin strips. |  She used a mandolin to slice the potatoes thinly for chips. 她用曼陀铃把土豆切成薄片做薯条。 | synonyms: slicer, vegetable slicer | antonyms: None | noun
