millings:/ˈmɪlɪŋz/
n.
金属屑，铣屑
small pieces of metal removed by a milling machine
The millings from the lathe were collected for recycling.  车床上的金属屑被收集起来回收利用。
chips, shavings, filings
scraps, waste
