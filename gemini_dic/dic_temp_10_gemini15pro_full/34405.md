classy:/ˈklæsi/ (US), /ˈklɑːsi/ (UK)| adj.|优等的；高级的；优雅的|Stylish and sophisticated.|She wore a classy black dress to the gala. 她穿着一件优雅的黑色礼服参加了晚会。|近义词：elegant, stylish, sophisticated, chic, refined|反义词：tacky, vulgar, tasteless, cheap, gaudy|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From class + -y.  Originally meaning "pertaining to a social class," the word evolved to imply the upper classes and their associated elegance.
