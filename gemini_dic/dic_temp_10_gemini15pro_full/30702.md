certifier:/ˈsɜːrtɪfaɪər/| n.|认证员，鉴定员|A person or organization that officially attests or confirms something, especially the quality or standard of something.|The software company is a certified certifier of online security systems. 这家软件公司是经过认证的网络安全系统认证机构。|近义词：validator, authenticator, accreditor|反义词: N/A|名词

certify:/ˈsɜːrtɪfaɪ/| vt.|证明，保证；发证书|To formally attest or confirm something, especially the quality or standard of something.|The doctor certified the patient's death. 医生证明了病人的死亡。|近义词：confirm, attest, validate, authenticate|反义词:deny, reject|动词
