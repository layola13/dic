bushfighting: /ˈbʊʃˌfaɪtɪŋ/
noun
丛林战，灌木丛战斗
Fighting that takes place in dense vegetation, such as jungles or forests.
The soldiers were trained in bushfighting techniques before being deployed to the jungle. 这些士兵在被派往丛林之前接受了丛林战技术的训练。
Guerrilla warfare, jungle warfare
Conventional warfare, open warfare
n.
