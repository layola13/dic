overman:/ˈoʊvərˌmæn/| n. |超人，上人，卓越的人|A superior human being, especially one who transcends conventional morality or limitations; a person of extraordinary power or ability. |Nietzsche envisioned the overman as someone who creates their own values. 尼采将超人设想为创造自己价值观的人。| He strove to be an overman, pushing his physical and mental limits. 他努力成为一个超人，不断挑战自己的身心极限。|近义词：superman, superior being, transcendent individual|反义词:underman, common man|Noun
