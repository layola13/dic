distad:/ˈdɪstæd/| adj. adv.| 向远侧的，向外侧的；向远侧地，向外侧地 | Situated away from the center of the body or from the point of attachment; outwards. | The fins extend distad from the body of the fish. 鱼鳍从鱼的身体向外延伸。|近义词：peripheral, outward, distal |反义词: proximal, proximad, central |词性: adjective, adverb
Etymology: From Latin *distātus (assumed past participle of *distāre, "to stand apart"), from dis- + stāre (“to stand”).  Compare French distad.  First attested in the mid 19th century.
