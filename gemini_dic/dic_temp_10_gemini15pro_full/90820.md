hun:/hʌn/ |n.| 亲爱的，宝贝 |A term of endearment, often used between spouses or lovers. | Example:  Hun, can you pass the salt?  亲爱的，你能把盐递给我吗？

Example:  See you later, hun.  待会儿见，宝贝。|Synonym: darling, sweetheart, dear, babe |Antonym: (No direct antonym for a term of endearment) |noun



ETYMOLOGY->
Shortened form of "honey," which itself originated as a term of endearment likely influenced by the sweetness of honey.
