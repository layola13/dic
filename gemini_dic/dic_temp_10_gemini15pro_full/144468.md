outteasing: /ˌaʊtˈtiːzɪŋ/
|v.|戏弄，逗弄，超过|Outteasing someone involves teasing or provoking them in a playful or competitive way, often surpassing them in skill or wit.|He was outteasing his brother with his clever riddles. 他用巧妙的谜语逗弄他的弟弟。|近义词: outsmarting, outwitting, besting, surpassing|反义词:  being outdone, being outsmarted|词性: 动词

outteasing: /ˌaʊtˈtiːzɪŋ/
|n.|戏弄，逗弄|The act of teasing or provoking someone in a playful or competitive way, often surpassing them in skill or wit.|His outteasing of his brother was all in good fun. 他对弟弟的戏弄只是好玩而已。|近义词: banter, teasing, raillery|反义词: seriousness, solemnity|词性: 名词
