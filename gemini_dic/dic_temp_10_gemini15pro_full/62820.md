enhedge:/ɪnˈhɛdʒ/| vt.|限制，约束，包围|To enclose or confine within a hedge; to surround or fortify with or as if with a hedge; to protect.|例句：The king enhedged himself with loyal advisors. 国王用忠诚的顾问包围自己。

例句：Regulations enhedge the freedom of businesses. 法规限制了企业的自由。|近义词：restrict, limit, confine, enclose, surround, protect|反义词：liberate, free, release|词性：动词

词源: From en- + hedge (n.).  "En-" is a prefix meaning "in, into, on, upon."  "Hedge" originally referred to a fence or boundary formed by bushes or shrubs.  Therefore, "enhedge" literally means to enclose within a hedge.
