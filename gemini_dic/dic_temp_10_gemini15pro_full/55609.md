disvalue:/dɪsˈvæljuː/
verb|贬低，轻视|To regard or represent as being of little worth.|He disvalued the work of his colleagues.|近义词：devalue, depreciate, belittle, underrate|反义词：value, appreciate, esteem, overrate
noun|低估，贬值|The state of being undervalued; depreciation.|The disvalue placed on women's work in society.|近义词：devaluation, depreciation, undervaluation|反义词：valuation, appreciation


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation, reversal) + value.
