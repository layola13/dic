oriel: /ˈɔːriəl/ (UK), /ˈɔːriəl/ (US) | n. | 飘窗，凸窗 | A large bay window, usually on an upper floor, projecting from the wall and supported by brackets or corbels. | The oriel window offered a stunning view of the garden. 飘窗提供了花园的壮丽景色。| The oriel provided a cozy nook for reading. 凸窗提供了一个舒适的阅读角落。| synonym: bay window, bow window | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps recessed window) | noun
