breadthwise:/ˈbrɛdθwaɪz/| adv.| 横向地，宽度方向地|In a direction parallel to the breadth of something; across.  Across something in the direction of its breadth.|The shelf is 60cm breadthwise. 这个架子宽60厘米。|近义词：across, crosswise, sideways|反义词:lengthwise|词性:副词

breadthwise:/ˈbrɛdθwaɪz/| adj.| 横向的|Extending or measured along the breadth.|The breadthwise dimension of the room is 10 feet. 房间的宽度是10英尺。|近义词：broad, wide|反义词:narrow, lengthways|词性:形容词
