nonpolitical:/ˌnɒnpəˈlɪtɪkl/ /ˌnɑːnpəˈlɪtɪkl/
adjective
非政治的
Not connected with or influenced by politics; not political.
例句：
The organization insists that its work is strictly nonpolitical.  这个组织坚称其工作完全是非政治性的。

The discussion focused on nonpolitical issues like the environment and education. 讨论集中在环境和教育等非政治性问题上。

近义词：apolitical, non-partisan
反义词：political, partisan
词性：adjective
