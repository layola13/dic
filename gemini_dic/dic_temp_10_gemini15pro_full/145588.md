overconcern:/ˌoʊvər kənˈsɜːrn/| v.|过分担心，过度关心|To worry excessively about something or someone.|He overconcerned himself with his children's safety. 他过分担心孩子们的安全。|近义词：fret, fuss|反义词：neglect, ignore|verb
