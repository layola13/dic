ornithosauria:/ˌɔːrnɪθəˈsɔːriə/| n. |鸟掌翼龙类|An extinct order of flying reptiles, also known as pterosaurs. They lived during the Mesozoic Era and were characterized by their membranous wings supported by an elongated fourth finger.|The ornithosauria, like Pterodactylus, were masters of the prehistoric skies.  鸟掌翼龙类，例如翼手龙，是史前天空的主宰。

Ornithosauria fossils have been found on every continent. 鸟掌翼龙类的化石在各大洲都有发现。|近义词：pterosaur|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
