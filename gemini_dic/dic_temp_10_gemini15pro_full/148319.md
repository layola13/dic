oversorrow:/ˌoʊvərˈsɑːroʊ/
verb
过度悲伤，极度悲伤
to feel excessive sorrow; to grieve to an excessive degree
She was oversorrowed by the loss of her child. 她因失去孩子而过度悲伤。
近义词: grieve excessively, mourn deeply, lament, bewail
反义词: rejoice, celebrate
词性: verb
