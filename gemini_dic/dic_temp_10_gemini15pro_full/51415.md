deterrent:/dɪˈtɜːrənt/| n. adj.|威慑物，制止物；威慑的，制止的|Something that discourages or prevents a certain action, typically because of fear of the consequences.|The high security fence acts as a deterrent to thieves.  高高的安全栅栏对小偷起到了威慑作用。|Synonyms: discouragement, obstacle, impediment, curb, restraint|Antonyms: encouragement, incentive, inducement|noun

deterrent:/dɪˈtɜːrənt/| adj.|威慑的，制止的|Tending to deter.|The deterrent effect of the new law is still unknown.  新法律的威慑作用尚不清楚。|Synonyms: discouraging, preventative|Antonyms: encouraging, inciting|adjective
