lifestring:/ˈlaɪfˌstrɪŋ/| n.|命脉，生命线，最重要的东西|Something that is vital or essential to someone's survival or well-being.|His business was his lifestrong. 他的事业是他的命脉。|例句：Her faith was the lifestrong that helped her through difficult times. 她的信念是她度过难关的生命线。

例句：Education is the lifestrong of a nation's progress. 教育是一个国家进步的命脉。|近义词：linchpin, cornerstone, essential, vital|反义词:unimportant, insignificant|词性:noun
