conformational:/ˌkɒnfɔːˈmeɪʃənl/ /ˌkɑːnfɔːrˈmeɪʃənl/
adjective|构象的，构型的|Relating to the spatial arrangement of atoms in a molecule that can be interconverted by rotations about single bonds, without breaking any bonds.|The conformational change of the protein allows it to bind to the receptor. 蛋白质的构象变化使其能够与受体结合。|近义词:structural, steric|反义词:None|词性:adjective

conformational:/ˌkɒnfɔːˈmeɪʃənl/ /ˌkɑːnfɔːrˈmeɪʃənl/
noun|构象体|A particular shape or arrangement of atoms in a molecule resulting from rotation around single bonds.|The molecule can exist in several different conformations. 该分子可以以几种不同的构象存在。|近义词:conformer, rotamer|反义词:None|词性:noun
