grainier:/ˈɡreɪniər/ | adj. |颗粒更粗的；纹理更粗糙的；（照片）颗粒感更强的|Having a coarser or more granular texture; more grainy. | The old photo was grainier than the new one. 这张老照片比新照片颗粒感更强。 | 近义词: coarser, rougher, granular | 反义词: smoother, finer | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From grain + -y + -er (comparative suffix).  "Grain" comes from Old French "grain," from Latin "granum" meaning "seed, grain."
