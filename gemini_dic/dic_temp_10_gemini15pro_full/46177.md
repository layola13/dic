cypher:/ˈsaɪfər/ (US) /ˈsaɪfə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|密码，暗号；零；加密，解码|A secret or disguised way of writing; a zero; to encode or decode a message.|例句：He used a complex cypher to protect his messages. 他使用复杂的密码来保护他的信息。|近义词：code, password, enigma, zero, nil, naught|反义词:plain text, solution, key|noun, verb

cypher:/ˈsaɪfər/ (US) /ˈsaɪfə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|零，无价值之物|Something of no value; a nonentity.|例句：His opinion on the matter is a cypher. 他对此事的意见毫无价值。|近义词：zero, nil, naught, nonentity, nobody|反义词:something, somebody, important|noun, verb

cypher:/ˈsaɪfər/ (US) /ˈsaɪfə(r)/ (UK)| v.|加密，解码|To encode or decode a message using a cypher.|例句：She cyphered the message before sending it.  她在发送之前对消息进行了加密。|近义词：encode, decode, encrypt, decrypt|反义词:decipher, decrypt|verb
