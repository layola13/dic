compactification:/kəmˌpæktɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 致密化，紧致化 | The process of making something more compact or dense.  This often refers to mathematical concepts in topology and string theory, but can also be used in more general contexts. | The compactification of the city center led to higher population density.  城市中心的致密化导致了更高的人口密度。| synonyms: compression, condensation, consolidation | antonyms: expansion, dilation, dispersion | noun


compactify:/kəmˈpæktɪfaɪ/ | v. | 使紧密，使致密 | To make something more compact or dense. |  They compactified the soil before building the foundation. 他们夯实了土壤才建造地基。| synonyms: condense, compress, consolidate | antonyms: expand, dilate, disperse | verb
