ovoviviparous:/ˌoʊvəvɪˈvɪpərəs/
adjective
卵胎生的
Producing eggs that hatch within the female's body, so that the young are born alive but without placental attachment, unlike viviparous animals.  The eggs are retained within the mother's body until they hatch.  The developing embryo derives nourishment from the yolk within the egg, rather than directly from the mother.
例句：
The guppy is an ovoviviparous fish.
孔雀鱼是一种卵胎生鱼类。

Some snakes are ovoviviparous.
有些蛇是卵胎生的。

近义词：
viviparous (in a looser sense)

反义词：
oviparous
