moodiness:/ˈmuːdinəs/| n.|喜怒无常，情绪多变|The state of being moody; given to frequent changes of mood; temperamental.|Her moodiness made it difficult to work with her. 她的喜怒无常让人很难和她一起工作。|近义词：temperamentality, capriciousness, volatility, fickleness|反义词：stability, even-temperedness, cheerfulness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From moody + -ness.  Moody traces back to Old English *mōdig "proud, arrogant," from mōd "mind, mood, courage." The meaning shifted over time to encompass changing moods.
