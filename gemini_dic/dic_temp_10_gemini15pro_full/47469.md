dealing:/ˈdiːlɪŋ/| n.|交易，买卖，交往; 待遇，对待|Dealing refers to the act of buying or selling something, or the way someone treats other people.|Example: He has a lot of dealings with foreign companies. 他与外国公司有很多业务往来。
