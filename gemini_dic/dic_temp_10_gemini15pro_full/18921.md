bespangle: /bɪˈspæŋɡl/ /biˈspæŋɡəl/
verb
点缀；使闪耀
To cover with or as if with spangles; adorn brilliantly.
The night sky was bespangled with stars. 夜空中点缀着星星。
The dancer's costume was bespangled with sequins. 舞者的服装上缀满了亮片。
近义词: adorn, decorate, embellish, ornament, glitter
反义词: dull, tarnish
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + spangle.  "Be-" is a prefix meaning "all over, completely," while "spangle" refers to a small, thin piece of glittering metal or plastic used for ornamentation.
