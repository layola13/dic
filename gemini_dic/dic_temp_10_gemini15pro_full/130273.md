non-subordinate:/ˌnɒn səˈbɔːdɪnət/ | adj. | 非下级的，独立的，并列的 | Not subordinate; independent; of equal or parallel importance. |  The two clauses are non-subordinate, meaning they have equal grammatical weight. 这两个从句是非下级的，意味着它们具有相等的语法权重。| Example:  He holds a non-subordinate position in the company. 他在公司担任一个非下级职位。 | Synonyms: independent, coordinate, equal, equivalent | Antonyms: subordinate, dependent, ancillary | adjective
