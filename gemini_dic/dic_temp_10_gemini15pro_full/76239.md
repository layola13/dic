futuristic:/ˌfjuːtʃəˈrɪstɪk/| adj.|未来的；未来派的；超前新潮的|Having or involving very modern ideas or designs, especially concerning the future.|例句：The futuristic city was full of flying cars and towering skyscrapers.  未来主义的城市里到处都是飞车和高耸入云的摩天大楼。

例句：Her futuristic dress looked like something out of a science fiction movie. 她的未来主义连衣裙看起来像是科幻电影里的东西。|近义词：modern, advanced, innovative, visionary, cutting-edge|反义词：old-fashioned, antiquated, outdated, traditional, archaic|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From future + -istic.  Ultimately from Latin *futūrus*, future participle of *esse*, to be.
