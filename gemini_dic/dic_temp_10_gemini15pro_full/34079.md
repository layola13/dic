civilist:/sɪˈvɪliən/| n. |平民; 平民百姓|A person not in the armed services or police force. |例句：Many civilians were killed in the bombing. 许多平民在轰炸中丧生。

例句：The soldiers were ordered not to harm any civilians. 士兵们被命令不要伤害任何平民。|近义词：noncombatant, citizen|反义词：soldier, military personnel|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *civilein*, from Old French *civelein*, *civilein*, from Medieval Latin *civilis*, from Latin *civilis* ("relating to citizens, civil, civic").
