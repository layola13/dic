fictional: /ˈfɪkʃənəl/ | adj. | 虚构的；小说的 |  Formed from "fiction" with the suffix "-al," meaning "of or relating to."  Describes something that is invented or imagined, not based on fact. |  The characters in the book are entirely fictional.  这本书中的人物完全是虚构的。 |  She enjoys reading fictional stories. 她喜欢读虚构的故事。|  imaginary, invented, fabricated, make-believe | real, factual, true, actual | adjective
