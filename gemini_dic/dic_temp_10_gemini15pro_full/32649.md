chist:/tʃɪst/ | n. | 宝箱，钱箱 | A small box or chest, especially one for valuables. |  He kept his gold coins in a chist. 他把他的金币放在一个钱箱里。| Synonym: coffer, chest, strongbox | Antonym: N/A | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *chiste*, from Old French *chiste*, from Latin *cista*. Ultimately from Greek *kistē* (κίστη), meaning "box" or "chest."
