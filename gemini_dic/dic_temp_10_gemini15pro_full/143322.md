outearns:/ˌaʊtˈɜːrnz/
verb
挣得比…多；收入超过
to earn more money than someone else
例句：She outearns her husband by a considerable margin. 她比她丈夫挣得多得多。

例句：He outearns all his siblings. 他的收入超过了他所有的兄弟姐妹。

近义词：outstrip, surpass, exceed
反义词：be outearned by

词性: v.
