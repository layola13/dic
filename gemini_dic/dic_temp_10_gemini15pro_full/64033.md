epithelioma: /ˌɛpɪθiːliˈoʊmə/ /ˌɛpɪθiːlɪˈəʊmə/
noun
上皮瘤
A malignant tumor derived from epithelial tissue.  A carcinoma.
The doctor diagnosed the lesion as an epithelioma. 医生诊断该病灶为上皮瘤。
Synonyms: carcinoma, cancer, tumor, neoplasm
Antonyms: healthy tissue
