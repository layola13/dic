erosive:/ɪˈroʊsɪv/ (US), /ɪˈrəʊsɪv/ (UK)| adj.|腐蚀的，侵蚀的|Tending to erode or to cause erosion; wearing away.|The erosive power of the river gradually carved out the canyon. 河流的侵蚀力逐渐雕刻出了峡谷。|Synonyms: corrosive, abrasive, destructive, wearing|Antonyms: constructive, restorative, protective|adjective
