affinity-cards:/əˈfɪnɪti kɑːrdz/ | n. | 亲和卡；认同卡；关联卡 | A type of credit card issued in partnership between a financial institution and a specific organization or group, often offering special benefits or perks to cardholders and contributing a portion of the card's fees or purchases to the affiliated organization. |  Example 1: He applied for an affinity-card offered by his university alumni association, which gave him discounts on university merchandise. 他申请了由他大学的校友会提供的亲和卡，这张卡可以让他在购买大学商品时获得折扣。

Example 2:  The airline's affinity-card program allows cardholders to earn frequent flyer miles with every purchase. 该航空公司的亲和卡计划允许持卡人每次购物都能获得飞行里程。 |  Synonyms: co-branded card, partnership card | Antonyms:  N/A | noun
