manuring:/məˈnjʊərɪŋ/
n.|施肥，施肥的过程|The act of applying manure to soil to enrich it. |Regular manuring is essential for healthy plant growth. 定期施肥对植物健康生长至关重要。|fertilizing, enriching|depleting
词性:名词

manure:/məˈnjʊər/
n.|肥料，粪肥|Animal dung used for fertilizing land. |The farmer spread manure on the fields. 农民在地里施肥。|fertilizer, compost|--
词性:名词
manure:/məˈnjʊər/
vt.|施肥，给…施肥|To apply manure to (land).|He manured the garden with cow dung. 他用牛粪给花园施肥。|fertilize, enrich|deplete
词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *manouvrer, from Latin manu operari "to work by hand," from manus "hand" + operari "to work."  The sense shifted from "cultivating land by hand" to "applying manure" as manure became a key element of land cultivation.
