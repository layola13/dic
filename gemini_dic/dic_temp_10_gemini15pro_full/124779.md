msps:/ˈɛm ɛs pʰiː ˈɛs/ |abbr.| 每秒百万样本 | Million samples per second.  A unit of data transfer rate, commonly used in digital signal processing and telecommunications. |The analog-to-digital converter operates at 100 msps.  该模数转换器以每秒100百万个样本的速率运行。|近义词: samples per second, sampling rate|反义词:  (No direct antonym, but a lower sampling rate would represent the opposite concept)|abbr.
