breathableness:/ˈbriːðəbəlnəs/
n.
透气性，呼吸能力
The breathability of a fabric is important for comfort.  一种织物的透气性对于舒适性很重要。
This new sportswear boasts excellent breathability. 这款新的运动服拥有极佳的透气性。
porosity, permeability, ventilation
impermeability, airtightness
noun
