meagerly:/ˈmiːɡərli/| adv.|缺乏地，不足地，贫乏地|In a way that is small in quantity and poor in quality; sparsely.|He ate meagerly, saving every scrap of food. 他吃得很少，每一点食物都舍不得浪费。|近义词: sparsely, sparingly, skimpily, poorly, inadequately|反义词: abundantly, lavishly, generously, plentifully|词性:副词
词源: From Middle English *megreli, from Old French maigrelement, from maigre (“lean, thin”) + -ment (suffix forming adverbs). Doublet of macerly.
