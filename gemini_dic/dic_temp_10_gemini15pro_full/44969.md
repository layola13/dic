crystallic:/krɪˈstælɪk/
adjective
结晶的; 晶体的
Relating to, or having the characteristics of crystals; crystalline.
The cave walls were covered in crystallic formations. 洞穴的墙壁上覆盖着晶体结构。
crystalline, glassy
amorphous
