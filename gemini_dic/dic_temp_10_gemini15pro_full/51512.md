detrimentality:/ˌdɛtrɪmɛnˈtælɪti/
n.
损害性，有害性
The state or quality of being detrimental; harmfulness.
Excessive sun exposure can lead to detrimentality to the skin. 过度日晒会对皮肤造成损害。
Near synonyms: harmfulness, noxiousness, perniciousness
Antonyms:  benefit, helpfulness, advantageousness
词性: 名词
