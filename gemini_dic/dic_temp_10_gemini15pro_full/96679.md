indwelling:/ɪnˈdwɛlɪŋ/
adjective
内在的，固有的；(医疗器械)留置的
Existing permanently within something or someone.  A medical device that remains inside the body for a prolonged period of time.
例句：An indwelling catheter was inserted to drain the bladder.  插入留置导尿管以排空膀胱。

例句：She had an indwelling sense of peace. 她内心有一种固有的平静感。
近义词：inherent, innate, implanted, resident, permanent
反义词：external, removable, temporary
词性: adjective
