contraries:/ˈkɒntrəriz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntreriz/ (US)| n.|相反的事物；对立面；矛盾|Things that are contraries are so opposed in nature that they cannot both be true but both may be false.  |例句：Hot and cold are contraries.  冷和热是对立面。

|例句：Love and hate are often seen as contraries. 爱和恨通常被视为对立面。|近义词：opposites, antitheses|反义词：similarities, parallels|Noun
