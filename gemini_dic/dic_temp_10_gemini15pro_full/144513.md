outtravelling:/ˌaʊtˈtrævəlɪŋ/
verb
超过…的旅行(距离、频率等); 旅行胜过
surpassing someone in the extent or frequency of travel; travelling further or more extensively than someone else
例句：He outtravelled all his colleagues, visiting over thirty countries in a single year.  他比所有同事都走得更远，一年之内访问了三十多个国家。

例句：She outtravelled her rivals, securing new business deals in emerging markets. 她比竞争对手跑得更多，在新兴市场达成了新的商业协议。

近义词：globetrotting, journeying extensively
反义词：staying put, remaining home
词性：v.
