minim:/ˈmɪnɪm/ | n. | 最小量，微量；（音乐）二分音符；（旧时）液量单位 | A minim is a very small amount of something, especially a liquid. In music, a minim is a note having the time value of two crotchets. Historically, it was a unit of liquid volume. |  The doctor prescribed the medicine in minims. 医生开了几滴药。| Synonyms: drop, bit, iota, speck, trace | Antonyms: maximum, abundance | noun

minim:/ˈmɪnɪm/ | n. | (印刷)小写字母m或n；小写字母 | In printing, a minim refers to the lowercase letters 'm' or 'n', or a lowercase letter in general. | The font size was so small, the minims were barely legible. 字体太小了，小写字母几乎看不清。 | Synonyms: lowercase (letter), minuscule | Antonyms: majuscule, capital (letter) | noun
