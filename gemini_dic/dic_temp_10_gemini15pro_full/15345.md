backstay: /ˈbæk.steɪ/
n.
后支索，后拉索
A strong rope or wire running from the top of a mast to the stern of a ship, used to support the mast.
The backstay snapped in the storm, causing the mast to collapse.  暴风雨中后支索断了，导致桅杆倒塌。
近义词: stay, support
反义词: None
词性: 名词

backstay: /ˈbæk.steɪ/
v.
支撑，支持 (比喻义)
To support or strengthen something.
The government's policies are backstayed by strong public opinion. 政府的政策得到了强烈的公众舆论的支持。
近义词: support, reinforce, bolster
反义词: undermine, weaken
词性: 动词
