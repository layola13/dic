neptunist:/ˈnɛptʃʊnɪst/ | n. |海王星论者；Neptunist|A proponent of the now obsolete theory of Neptunism, which attributed the formation of all rocks to aqueous processes. |The neptunist argued that even granite was formed through precipitation from water. 海王星论者认为即使是花岗岩也是由水中沉淀形成的。

The museum displayed writings of prominent neptunists of the 18th century.  博物馆展出了18世纪著名海王星论者的著作。|近义词:Wernerian (沃纳主义者，因为Neptunism主要由Abraham Gottlob Werner提出)|反义词:plutonist (火成论者)|词性:noun
