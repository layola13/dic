chain-reacting:/ˌtʃeɪn riˈæktɪŋ/| adj.|连锁反应的，链式反应的|A process in which one event sets off a series of similar events, each caused by the previous one.|The chain-reacting explosions caused widespread damage. 连锁爆炸造成了广泛的破坏.|Near synonyms: cascading, domino effect, snowballing|Antonyms: isolated, independent|adjective
