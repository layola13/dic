metasome: /ˈmɛtəˌsoʊm/ | n. | 后体 | The posterior part of an insect's body, comprising the abdomen (excluding the first segment).  It is formed by the fusion of embryonic segments. | The metasome of the ant contains its vital organs. 蚂蚁的后体包含其重要器官。 | synonym: abdomen (in a broader sense) | antonym:  prosoma (or cephalothorax in some groups) | noun
