ETYMOLOGY->
From "addressable" + "-ities".  "Addressable" comes from "address" (meaning "to direct something towards a specific location or person") + "-able" (a suffix indicating capability or possibility). The root of "address" traces back to Old French "adresser," meaning "to direct or prepare." "-ities" is a suffix used to form plural nouns denoting qualities or states.
