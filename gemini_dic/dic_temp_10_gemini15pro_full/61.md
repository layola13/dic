aair:/ˈeɪər/ | n. | 空气，大气 | The mixture of gases that surrounds the earth and that we breathe. |  We need clean air to breathe. 我们需要呼吸干净的空气。

The air is thin at high altitudes.  在高海拔地区，空气稀薄。| 近义词: atmosphere, oxygen | 反义词: vacuum | 词性: 名词

The air smells fresh after the rain. = 雨后空气清新。
The air is polluted in the city center. = 市中心的空气被污染了。
