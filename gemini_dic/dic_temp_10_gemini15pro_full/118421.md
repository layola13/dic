merrymaking:/ˈmɛriˌmeɪkɪŋ/
n.
欢宴；庆祝活动；狂欢作乐
noisy festivities or revelry.
The wedding was a scene of much merrymaking. 婚礼现场充满了欢声笑语。
celebration, festivity, revelry, jollification, carousal
gloom, sadness, mourning
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English meri making, from meri (“merry”) + making (“making”).
