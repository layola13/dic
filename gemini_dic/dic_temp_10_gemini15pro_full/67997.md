factionist:/ˈfækʃənɪst/| n. |宗派主义者；派系主义者|A person who promotes internal conflict or division within a group or organization; someone excessively devoted to a faction.|The factionist's disruptive behavior threatened the unity of the party.  这个宗派主义者的破坏性行为威胁到了党的团结。|Synonyms: partisan, sectarian, splitter|Antonyms: conciliator, unifier, mediator|noun

factionist:/ˈfækʃənɪst/| adj. |宗派的；派系的|Relating to or characteristic of a factionist; divisive.|His factionist tendencies made him unpopular with his colleagues.  他的派系倾向使他不受同事欢迎。|Synonyms: partisan, sectarian, divisive|Antonyms: unifying, conciliatory, mediating|adjective
