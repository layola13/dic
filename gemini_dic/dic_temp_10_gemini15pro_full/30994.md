chalkboard:/ˈtʃɔːkbɔːrd/ (US) /ˈtʃɔːkbɔːd/ (UK)| n. |黑板|A dark, smooth surface, usually green or black, used in schools and offices for writing or drawing on with chalk.|The teacher wrote the equation on the chalkboard. 老师把方程式写在黑板上。|近义词：blackboard, whiteboard|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps digital screen or projector)|noun
