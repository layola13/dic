lazarus:/ˈlæzərəs/| n.|拉撒路；死而复生的人；|A person who has recovered from a serious illness or near-death experience.  Refers to the biblical story of Lazarus, whom Jesus raised from the dead.|He was like Lazarus, rising from his sickbed after everyone thought he was done for. 他就像拉撒路一样，在大家都认为他不行了之后从病床上站了起来。|近义词:survivor, returnee|反义词:deceased, dead|noun
