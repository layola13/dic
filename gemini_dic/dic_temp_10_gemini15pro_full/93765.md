immediate:/ɪˈmiːdiət/| adj.|立即的，直接的，最接近的|happening or done without delay; existing now and needing attention; nearest in time, relationship, or rank|例句：I need an immediate response. 我需要立即回复。|近义词：instant, prompt, direct, next|反义词：delayed, eventual, distant, later|词性:形容词

immediate:/ɪˈmiːdiət/| n.|直接相关的事物|something that is direct or closest|例句：The immediate is more important than the future. 眼下比未来更重要。|近义词：present, current|反义词：future, distant|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *immediatus*, from Latin in- (“not”) + mediatus (“mediated”).
