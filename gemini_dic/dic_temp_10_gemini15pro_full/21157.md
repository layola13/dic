blizzard: /ˈblɪzərd/ | n. |暴风雪；大量；铺天盖地而来的一大批 |A severe snowstorm with high winds and low visibility. |  The blizzard made travel impossible. 暴风雪使旅行无法进行。


|例句：The blizzard raged for three days, burying the town in snow. 暴风雪肆虐了三天，把整个城镇都埋在了雪里。


|例句：We were snowed in by a fierce blizzard. 我们被一场猛烈的暴风雪困住了。


|近义词：snowstorm, whiteout |反义词:sunshine, calm


|词性:noun
