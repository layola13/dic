allocates: /ˈæləkeɪts/ | v. | 分配，分派 | To distribute (resources or duties) for a particular purpose. | The government allocates funds for education. 政府分配资金用于教育。|  The manager allocates tasks to team members. 经理给团队成员分配任务。 | assigns, distributes, apportions | withholds, retains | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *allocatus, past participle of allocare "to place, allot," from ad- "to" + locare "to place," from locus "a place."
