craglike:/ˈkræɡlaɪk/
adjective
像峭壁的，崎岖的
Resembling a crag; rugged and rocky.
The craglike cliffs offered a challenge to the climbers.  像峭壁一样的悬崖对攀登者提出了挑战。
The old man's face was craglike, weathered by years of sun and wind. 老人的脸像峭壁一样，饱经风霜。
近义词:rocky, rugged, clifflike, precipitous
反义词:smooth, flat, even
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From crag + -like.  Crag (from Celtic origin) refers to a steep, rugged rock. -like is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling," "characteristic of."
