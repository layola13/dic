brisked:/brɪskt/| v.|活跃起来，变得轻快，使...活泼|Past tense and past participle of brisk.  It is the past tense and past participle form of the verb "brisk," meaning to make or become more lively, energetic, or active. |The wind brisked and the clouds scudded across the sky. 风变得强劲，云朵在天空中飞掠。|The cold air brisked him up after his long nap.  午睡后，冷空气让他精神焕发。|近义词：enlivened, invigorated, quickened|反义词：sluggish, slowed, calmed|词性:verb
