carnivoracity:/ˌkɑːrnɪˈvɔːrəsi/ /ˌkɑːrnɪˈvɔːrəsɪti/| n. | 肉食性；贪婪；暴食 |The state of being carnivorous; voracity; rapacity.  The tendency to eat flesh. An extremely strong desire to devour. | The lion's carnivoracity was evident in its ferocious attack on the zebra.  狮子的肉食性在其对斑马的猛烈攻击中显而易见。|  近义词：voracity, rapacity, gluttony | 反义词：herbivorousness, vegetarianism | 词性: noun

carnivorous:/kɑːrˈnɪvərəs/ /kɑːrˈnɪvərəs/| adj. | 肉食性的；食肉的 |Feeding on flesh; predatory.  Relating to or characteristic of a carnivore. | Lions are carnivorous animals. 狮子是肉食性动物。 | 近义词：predatory, flesh-eating | 反义词：herbivorous, vegetarian | 词性: adjective
