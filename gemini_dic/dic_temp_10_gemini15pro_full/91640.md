hyperbolicity:/ˌhaɪpərboʊˈlɪsɪti/
n.
夸张；双曲性；夸张法
The quality or state of being hyperbolic; exaggerated representation; hyperbole.  This mathematical concept describes the negative curvature of a space.
The architect's description of the building's innovative design bordered on hyperbolicity.  这位建筑师对建筑物创新设计的描述近乎夸张。
Near synonyms: exaggeration, overstatement, extravagance, embellishment
Opposite: understatement,  minimization
noun

hyperbolic:/ˌhaɪpərˈbɒlɪk/
adj.
夸张的；双曲线的
Of, relating to, or employing hyperbole; exaggerated.  Also, relating to a hyperbola.
He made hyperbolic claims about the effectiveness of the new drug. 他对这种新药的疗效夸大其词。
Near synonyms: exaggerated, inflated, overblown, extravagant
Opposite: understated, moderate
adjective
