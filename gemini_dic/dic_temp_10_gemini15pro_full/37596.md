communicant:/kəˈmjuːnɪkənt/ | n. | 领圣餐者；信徒；通讯者 | A person who receives Holy Communion.  A person who communicates; one who makes known or imparts information. |  例句：The communicants knelt to receive the bread and wine. 领圣餐者跪下接受面包和葡萄酒。

例句：He was a regular communicant at St. Mary's Church. 他是圣玛丽教堂的常客。 | 近义词：worshipper, believer, parishioner, correspondent | 反义词：non-communicant, unbeliever | 词性：noun

communicant:/kəˈmjuːnɪkənt/ | adj. | 交流的；传达的；(尤指)领圣餐的 | Communicating; imparting; especially, receiving Holy Communion. | 例句：The communicant members of the church gathered for the service. 教会的交流成员聚集在一起参加礼拜。

例句：The communicant power of art can transcend cultural barriers. 艺术的交流力量可以超越文化障碍。| 近义词：communicative, sharing, imparting | 反义词：non-communicative, unsharing | 词性：adjective
