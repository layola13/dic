bomb:/bɒm/ (UK) /bɑːm/ (US)| n. vi. vt.|炸弹，轰炸，失败|An explosive device fused to explode under specific conditions.|The terrorists planted a bomb in the city center. 恐怖分子在市中心安放了一枚炸弹. |The play bombed on Broadway. 这部戏在百老汇演砸了. |synonyms: explosive, device, shell, grenade, flop, failure |antonyms: success, triumph|noun, verb


bomb:/bɒm/ (UK) /bɑːm/ (US)| vi. vt.|快速移动，轰炸|To move very quickly.|He bombed down the hill on his skateboard. 他踩着滑板飞快地冲下山坡. |The planes bombed the enemy positions. 飞机轰炸了敌人的阵地.|synonyms: speed, rush, zoom, blitz, attack|antonyms: crawl, creep|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *bombe*, from Italian *bomba*, from Latin *bombus* "a deep, hollow sound," probably of imitative origin.
