euryalida:/jʊˌraɪəˈlaɪdə/ | n. | 蛇尾纲真蛇尾目 | An order of brittle stars (class Ophiuroidea) characterized by a large disk and relatively short arms, often with spines or hooks.  They are found in deep-sea environments. | 例句：Euryalida are fascinating deep-sea creatures.  蛇尾纲真蛇尾目是迷人的深海生物。

例句：Researchers discovered a new species of Euryalida in the Pacific Ocean. 研究人员在太平洋发现了一种新的蛇尾纲真蛇尾目物种。 | 近义词: brittle star (广义上的近义词，因为Euryalida是蛇尾纲的一个目) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
