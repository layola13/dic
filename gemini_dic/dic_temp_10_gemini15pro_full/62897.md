enlightenment:/ɪnˈlaɪtnmənt/ |n.|启蒙；启迪；教化；开明|the state of having knowledge or understanding; the act of giving someone knowledge or understanding| The Enlightenment was a time of great intellectual ferment. 启蒙运动是一个思想大动荡的时期。|synonyms: awareness, insight, awakening, education|antonyms: ignorance, delusion, confusion|noun

enlighten:/ɪnˈlaɪtn/ |v.|启发；启迪；教导|to give someone information so that they understand something better| Can you enlighten me on this subject? 你能给我解释一下这个主题吗？|synonyms: educate, inform, teach, illuminate|antonyms: confuse, mislead, obscure|verb
