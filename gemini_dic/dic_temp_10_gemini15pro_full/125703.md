munging:/ˈmʌndʒɪŋ/
n.
数据清洗，数据处理
The process of transforming or manipulating data in a haphazard or messy way, often to clean or format it.
Data munging is essential for preparing data for analysis. 数据清洗对于准备分析数据至关重要。
He spent hours munging the data before he could analyze it. 他花了几个小时清洗数据才能够进行分析。
data wrangling, data cleaning, data transformation
data fabrication, data falsification
noun
