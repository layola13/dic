gorging:/ˈɡɔːrdʒɪŋ/| n. |狼吞虎咽；暴食；塞饱|The act of eating a large amount of food greedily.|He felt sick after a weekend of gorging on junk food.  他周末狂吃垃圾食品后感到恶心。|近义词：stuffing, cramming, overeating, bingeing|反义词:dieting, fasting|noun

gorge:/ɡɔːrdʒ/| v. |狼吞虎咽；塞饱；充满|To eat a large amount of food greedily; to fill something completely.|He gorged himself on pizza. 他狼吞虎咽地吃了披萨。 The river gorged itself with melted snow. 河水充满了融化的雪。|近义词：stuff, cram, overeat, binge|反义词:starve, diet|verb
