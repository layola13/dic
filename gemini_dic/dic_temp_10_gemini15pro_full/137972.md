nonyl:/ˈnɒnɪl/ (UK) /ˈnɑːnɪl/ (US)| adj. n.|壬基, 九碳烷基|Relating to or containing nine carbon atoms.  Specifically, referring to a univalent radical, C9H19, derived from nonane.|Nonylphenol is a common surfactant. 壬基酚是一种常见的表面活性剂。|近义词:  C9 alkyl |反义词: None (It's a specific chemical group, not a concept with an opposite.)|词性: Adjective, Noun

Etymology: From *nonane* (nine carbons) + *-yl* (indicating a radical or substituent group).  Ultimately from Latin *nonus* (ninth).
