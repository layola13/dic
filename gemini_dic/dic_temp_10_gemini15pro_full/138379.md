notochordal:/ˌnəʊtəˈkɔːrdl/
adjective
脊索的
Relating to or having a notochord, a flexible rod-like structure that forms the main support of the body in the lowest chordates and in the embryos of the higher vertebrates.
The notochordal process is crucial for the development of the nervous system. 脊索突对于神经系统的发育至关重要。
The researchers studied the notochordal cells in zebrafish embryos. 研究人员研究了斑马鱼胚胎中的脊索细胞。
Synonyms: chordal
Antonyms: None
词性: adjective
