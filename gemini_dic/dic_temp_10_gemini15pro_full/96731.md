inelaborate:/ɪnɪˈlæbərət/ | adj. | 未经详细说明的，不详尽的 | Not elaborated; not worked out in detail. | The plan was inelaborate and lacked specific details. 这个计划不够详尽，缺乏具体的细节。| The instructions were inelaborate, leaving me confused.  这些说明不够详细，让我很困惑。 | 近义词: sketchy, vague, rudimentary, undeveloped, simplistic | 反义词: elaborate, detailed, comprehensive, thorough, specific | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From in- (not) + elaborate (developed in detail).  Essentially meaning "not elaborate."
