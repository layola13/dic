casteless:/ˈkɑːstləs/ /ˈkæstləs/
adjective
无种姓的；不分种姓的；没有社会等级的
Without a caste; not belonging to a caste; not involving or based on a caste system.
The casteless people faced significant discrimination.  无种姓的人面临严重的歧视。
The organization promotes a casteless society. 该组织提倡一个没有种姓制度的社会。
近义词:classless, egalitarian
反义词:caste-based, stratified
词性:adjective
