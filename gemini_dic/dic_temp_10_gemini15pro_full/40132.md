contemplativeness:/kənˌtɛmplətɪvnəs/
| n. |沉思，冥想；深思熟虑|The state of being contemplative; a tendency towards prolonged thought and reflection.|His contemplativeness allowed him to make wise decisions. 他的深思熟虑使他能够做出明智的决定。|近义词：thoughtfulness, pensiveness, reflection, meditation|反义词：rashness, impulsiveness|词性:noun


词源: From Late Middle English contemplatifnes, from Old French contemplatif + -nes. Equivalent to contemplative + -ness.
