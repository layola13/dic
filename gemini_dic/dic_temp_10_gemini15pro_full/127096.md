nationalising:/ˈnæʃnəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
国有化
The act of bringing a privately owned industry or asset under the control of the government.
The government is nationalising the railways. 政府正在将铁路国有化。
The nationalising of key industries was a central part of their political platform. 将关键产业国有化是他们政治纲领的核心内容。
Synonyms:  socializing, collectivizing, expropriating
Antonyms: privatizing, denationalizing
词性: 动词 (verb)


nationalise:/ˈnæʃnəlaɪz/
verb

Many countries nationalised their coal industries after the war. 战后许多国家将煤炭产业国有化。

Antonyms: privatize, denationalize

nationalisation:/ˌnæʃnəlaɪˈzeɪʃn/
noun
国有化
The process of bringing a privately owned industry or asset under the control of the government.
The nationalisation of the steel industry led to widespread protests. 钢铁行业的国有化引发了广泛的抗议。
The government announced the nationalisation of all major airlines. 政府宣布将所有主要航空公司国有化。
Synonyms: socialization, collectivization, expropriation
Antonyms: privatization, denationalization
词性: 名词 (noun)
