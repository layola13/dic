paradisian:/ˌpærəˈdɪziən/ /ˌpærəˈdɪʒən/| adj. n.|天堂般的；天国的；极乐的；天堂的居民|Of, relating to, or resembling paradise.  A person who lives in paradise.|例句：The garden was a paradisian oasis of calm.  这个花园是一片宁静的天堂般的绿洲。|近义词：heavenly, blissful, celestial, idyllic|反义词：hellish, infernal, diabolical|词性:形容词

paradisian:/ˌpærəˈdɪziən/ /ˌpærəˈdɪʒən/| n.|天堂的居民|An inhabitant of paradise.|例句：The paradisian enjoyed eternal bliss.  天堂的居民享受着永恒的幸福。|近义词：celestial being, angel|反义词：devil, demon|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *paradisen*, from Old French *paradisien*, from Late Latin *paradisianus*, from Latin *paradisus* (“paradise”), from Ancient Greek *παράδεισος* (*parádeisos*).
