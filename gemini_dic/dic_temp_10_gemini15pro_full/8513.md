anticapitalism:/ˌæntiˈkæpɪtəlɪzəm/
n.|反资本主义|Opposition to capitalism; the belief that capitalism is harmful and should be replaced by a different economic system.|Many young people are drawn to anticapitalism as a solution to economic inequality. 许多年轻人被反资本主义吸引，将其视为解决经济不平等的方案。|Synonyms: anti-capitalism |Antonyms: capitalism, pro-capitalism|noun
