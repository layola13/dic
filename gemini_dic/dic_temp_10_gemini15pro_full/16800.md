baseboards:/ˈbeɪsbɔːrdz/| n. |踢脚板，护壁板|A narrow board running along the bottom of an interior wall, covering the joint where the wall meets the floor. It protects the wall from scuffs and damage and can be decorative.|The baseboards in the old house were intricately carved. 老房子的踢脚板雕刻精美。|Synonyms: skirting boards, skirting, mopboards|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|Noun
