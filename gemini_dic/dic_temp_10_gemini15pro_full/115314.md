mannerliness:/ˈmænərlɪnɪs/| n. |礼貌；彬彬有礼|The state or quality of being polite, well-mannered, and observing social etiquette.  It encompasses courteous behavior, respectful interactions, and adherence to social norms.|Her mannerliness charmed everyone at the dinner party. 她的礼貌在晚宴上迷住了每个人。|His mannerliness made a good impression on her parents. 他的彬彬有礼给她的父母留下了好印象。|近义词：politeness, courtesy, civility, decorum|反义词：rudeness, impoliteness, incivility, disrespect|词性:noun
