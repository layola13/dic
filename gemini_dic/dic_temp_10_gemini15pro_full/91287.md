hydrological:/ˌhaɪdrəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ (US) /ˌhaɪdrəˈlɑːdʒɪkl/| adj.|水文的；水文学的|Relating to the study of water, especially its properties, distribution, and effects on the earth's surface.|The hydrological cycle is the continuous movement of water on, above and below the surface of the Earth. 水文循环是地球表面、上方和下方水的连续运动。|The area has complex hydrological systems. 该地区拥有复杂的水文系统。|近义词:water-related, hydrographic|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From hydro- + -logical.  "Hydro-" comes from the Greek "hydōr" (ὕδωρ), meaning "water". "-logical" comes from the Greek "-logikos" (-λογικός), meaning "pertaining to the study of".
