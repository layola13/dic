inenarrable:/ɪnˈɛnərəbl/
adj.
无法形容的，难以言表的
too extraordinary or unusual to be described
The sunset was an inenarrable spectacle of color and light.  日落是一场难以言表的色彩与光的光景。
The pain of losing a child is inenarrable. 失去孩子的痛苦是难以言表的。
近义词: indescribable, inexpressible, ineffable
反义词: describable, expressible
词性: 形容词
