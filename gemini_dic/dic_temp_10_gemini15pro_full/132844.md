noneffervescent:/ˌnɒnˌɛfərˈvɛsənt/
adjective
无泡的；沉闷的；不兴奋的
Not effervescent; without bubbles.  Figuratively, lacking spirit or liveliness.
His noneffervescent personality made him a poor choice for a party host. 他沉闷的性格使他成为一个糟糕的派对主办人。
近义词:flat, dull, lifeless, insipid
反义词:effervescent, bubbly, lively, sparkling
词性:adjective
