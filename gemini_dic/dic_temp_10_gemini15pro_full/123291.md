monogastric:/ˌmɒnəʊˈɡæstrɪk/ /ˌmɑːnəˈɡæstrɪk/| adj. n.|单胃的，单胃动物|Having a single-chambered stomach; an animal with a single-chambered stomach.|Pigs are monogastric animals. 猪是单胃动物。|The digestive system of a monogastric organism is simpler than that of a ruminant. 单胃动物的消化系统比反刍动物的简单。|近义词:non-ruminant|反义词:ruminant|词性:adjective, noun
Etymology:  From mono- (one) + gastric (relating to the stomach). Ultimately from Greek monos (single, alone) and gaster (stomach).
