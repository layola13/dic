fireproofing:/ˈfaɪərˌpruːfɪŋ/
n.|防火；耐火处理；防火措施|The process of making something fire-resistant or the materials used in this process.  |Example: The building's fireproofing prevented the spread of the flames. 这栋建筑的防火措施阻止了火焰的蔓延。|Synonyms: fire resistance, fire protection|Antonyms: flammability, combustibility|noun


fireproof:/ˈfaɪərˌpruːf/
adj.|防火的；耐火的|Not easily set on fire; able to withstand fire or great heat.|Example:  Fireproof materials are essential in building construction.  防火材料在建筑施工中至关重要。|Synonyms: non-flammable, fire-resistant, incombustible|Antonyms: flammable, combustible|adjective
v.|使…防火；使…耐火|To make something fire-resistant.|Example: They fireproofed the curtains with a special chemical spray. 他们用一种特殊的化学喷雾剂对窗帘进行了防火处理。|Synonyms: fire-resist, protect from fire|Antonyms: ignite, kindle|verb
