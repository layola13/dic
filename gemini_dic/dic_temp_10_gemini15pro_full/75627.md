fugitive:/ˈfjuːdʒɪtɪv/ (US) /ˈfjuːdʒətɪv/ (UK)| n. adj.|逃亡者，逃犯；短暂的，易逝的|Someone who is running away or hiding, usually from the law.  Also, describes something fleeting or temporary.|The fugitive was last seen near the border.  那名逃犯最后一次出现是在边境附近。|Synonyms: escapee, runaway, deserter|Antonyms:  captive, prisoner|noun

fugitive:/ˈfjuːdʒɪtɪv/ (US) /ˈfjuːdʒətɪv/ (UK)|adj.|短暂的，易逝的|Lasting for a very short time; fleeting.|A fugitive thought crossed his mind.  一个短暂的想法掠过他的脑海。|Synonyms: fleeting, ephemeral, transient|Antonyms: lasting, permanent, enduring|adjective
