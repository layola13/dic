ghostess: /ˈɡoʊstɪs/ | n. | 女鬼 | A female ghost. |  She told a chilling tale of a ghostess haunting the old manor. 她讲述了一个令人毛骨悚然的故事，关于一个女鬼在古老的庄园里游荡。| synonyms: specter, phantom, apparition, wraith | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a supernatural being) | noun

Etymology:  A combination of "ghost" and the feminine suffix "-ess."  "Ghost" comes from Old English "gast" meaning "spirit, soul."
