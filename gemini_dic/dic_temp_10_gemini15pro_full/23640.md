brand-name:/ˈbrænd neɪm/ |n.|品牌名称；商标名称|A brand name is a name, term, design, symbol, or other feature that distinguishes one seller's product from those of others.  A brand name is part of a brand identity.|Example 1:  The brand name "Coca-Cola" is recognized worldwide.  可口可乐的品牌名称在世界范围内得到认可。

Example 2:  Choosing the right brand name is crucial for a new product. 为新产品选择合适的品牌名称至关重要。|Synonyms: trademark, brand, label|Antonyms: generic name, non-proprietary name|noun
