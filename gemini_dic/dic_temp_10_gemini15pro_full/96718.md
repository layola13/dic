ineffervescibility:/ˌɪnɪfərvɛˈsɛnsɪbiːlɪti/
noun
无生气; 平淡; 不兴奋
lack of effervescence; the inability to effervesce; the quality of being flat
His speeches are marked by a singular ineffervescibility. 他的演讲的特点是异常平淡。
Synonyms: flatness, dullness, lifelessness
Antonyms: effervescence, liveliness, vibrancy
词性: 名词

ineffervescible:/ˌɪnɪfərvɛˈsɛnsɪbəl/
adjective
无生气的; 平淡的; 不兴奋的
not effervescent; lacking liveliness or sparkle
He gave an ineffervescible performance. 他的表演毫无生气。
Synonyms: flat, dull, lifeless
Antonyms: effervescent, lively, vibrant
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + effervescible (capable of bubbling or fizzing) + -ity (noun suffix forming abstract nouns).  Effervescible comes from Latin effervescere (to boil over, ferment), from ex- (out) + fervere (to boil).
