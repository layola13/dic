architecturalization:/ˌɑːrkɪˈtɛktʃərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n. |架构化，体系化|The process of giving something an architectural structure or design; the process of becoming architectural.  The act of organizing something into a coherent system or structure, resembling the design and planning of a building.|The architecturalization of software development has led to more modular and reusable code. 软件开发的架构化带来了更模块化和可复用的代码。|structuring, systematization, organization|fragmentation, disorganization|noun
