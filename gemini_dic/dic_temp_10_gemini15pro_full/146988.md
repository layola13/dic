overkick: /ˌoʊvərˈkɪk/ | v. | 过度踢球；过度抬腿（尤指芭蕾舞）| To kick (a ball) higher than intended, especially in ballet, to raise the leg too high. | The ballerina overkicked and lost her balance.  芭蕾舞女演员过度抬腿，失去了平衡。| The football player overkicked the ball, sending it sailing over the goalposts. 足球运动员把球踢得过高，球飞过了球门柱。| 近义词: miskick | 反义词: underkick | 词性: 动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "kick" (verb meaning "to strike with the foot")
