clampdown:/ˈklæmpdaʊn/| n.|压制，取缔，打击|a sudden action taken by a government or other authority to stop or control something|The government imposed a clampdown on illegal immigration. 政府对非法移民进行了打击。|近义词：crackdown, suppression, curb|反义词:allowance, permission|noun


clampdown:/klæmpˈdaʊn/| v.|压制，取缔，打击|to take firm action to stop or control something|The police are clamping down on drug trafficking. 警方正在打击贩毒活动。|近义词：crack down on, suppress, curb|反义词:allow, permit|verb
