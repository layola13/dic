differences:/ˈdɪfrənsɪz/| n.|差异，不同|The differences between the two reports are significant. 这两份报告之间的差异很显著。|Example: There are many differences between British and American English.  英式英语和美式英语之间有很多不同之处。|Synonyms: discrepancies, variations, distinctions, dissimilarities|Antonyms: similarities, likenesses, resemblances|noun

Etymology: From Old French *difference*, from Latin *differentia* ("a difference"), from *differens*, present participle of *differre* ("to differ, to be different"), from *dis-* ("apart, away") + *ferre* ("to carry, to bear").
