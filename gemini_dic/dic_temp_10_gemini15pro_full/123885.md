morbid:/ˈmɔːrbɪd/| adj. |病态的，不健康的，（对死亡等）着迷的|He had a morbid fascination with death. 他对死亡有种病态的迷恋。|近义词：unhealthy, gruesome, macabre|反义词: healthy, wholesome|adjective

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin *morbidus, from Latin morbidus ‘diseased’, from morbus ‘disease’.
