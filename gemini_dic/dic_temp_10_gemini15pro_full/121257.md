misestimation:/ˌmɪsɛstɪˈmeɪʃən/
n.
误判，错误估计
An incorrect estimation of something's value, size, or cost.
The architect's misestimation of the project's cost led to significant delays.  建筑师对项目成本的错误估计导致了严重的延误。
misjudgment, underestimation, overestimation
accurate estimation, correct assessment
noun
