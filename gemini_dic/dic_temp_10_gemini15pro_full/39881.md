constituency:/kənˈstɪtʃuənsi/| n.|选区，选民，支持者|A body of voters who elect a representative to a legislative body.|The MP worked hard to address the concerns of his constituency.  这位议员努力解决他选区选民的关切。|synonyms: electorate, voters, supporters|antonyms: non-voters, opposition|noun


constituency:/kənˈstɪtʃuənsi/| n.|支持者，顾客群|A body of customers or supporters of a particular business, organization, or cause.|The magazine hopes to expand its constituency by offering online content. 这家杂志希望通过提供在线内容来扩大其读者群。|synonyms: clientele, following, patrons, audience|antonyms: competitors, rivals|noun
