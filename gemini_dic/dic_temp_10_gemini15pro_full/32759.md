choanoid:/ˈkoʊ.ənɔɪd/ | adj. n. |  领鞭毛虫样的，领鞭毛细胞 |  Relating to or resembling a choanoflagellate, a single-celled organism considered to be the closest living relatives of animals. Also, a cell resembling a choanoflagellate, especially a cell with a collar of microvilli surrounding a flagellum, found in sponges and other animals.  | Choanoid cells play a crucial role in filter feeding. 领鞭毛细胞在滤食中起着至关重要的作用。 | Choanoflagellate cells are the hallmark of the choanoid group. 领鞭毛虫细胞是领鞭毛虫类的标志。 |  choanoflagellate, collar cell | - | adjective, noun
