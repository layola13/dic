ordainers:/ɔːrˈdeɪnərz/
n.
授予圣职者；任命者；制定者
People who ordain someone, especially as a priest or minister; those who establish or decree something.
The ordainers laid their hands on the new priests. 那些授予圣职者按手在新任神父的头上。
The city council are the ordainers of the new traffic laws. 市议会是新交通法规的制定者。
Synonyms: consecrators, commissioners, appointers, legislators
Antonyms: recipients, followers, subjects
noun
