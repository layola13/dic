composite's:/ˈkɒmpəzɪts/| n.|复合材料的，合成物的|Belonging to or associated with a composite material or structure.  This is the possessive form of "composite."|The composite's strength was tested rigorously.  复合材料的强度经过了严格的测试。|synonyms: compound's, mixture's|antonyms: element's, component's|possessive noun

composite:/ˈkɒmpəzɪt/| adj.|复合的，合成的|Made up of various parts or elements.|This is a composite image made from several photographs.  这是一张由多张照片合成的图片。|synonyms: compound, combined, mixed|antonyms: simple, basic, elemental|adjective

composite:/ˈkɒmpəzɪt/| n.|复合材料，合成物|A material made from two or more constituent materials with significantly different physical or chemical properties that, when combined, produce a material with characteristics different from the individual components.|Carbon fiber is a popular composite material used in aircraft construction. 碳纤维是一种用于飞机制造的流行复合材料。|synonyms: compound, mixture, blend|antonyms: element, component|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English: from late Latin compositus ‘put together’, past participle of componere (see compose).
