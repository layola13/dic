bookend:/ˈbʊkˌɛnd/| n. v.|书挡；支撑物；用作...的开头和结尾|A support placed at the end of a row of books to keep them upright. To enclose or finish something with similar elements or events.|例句：The heavy bookends kept the large volumes from tipping over. 厚重的书挡防止大部头书倾倒。|近义词：support, brace, prop, bracket|反义词:|n.
bookend:/ˈbʊkˌɛnd/| v.|用作...的开头和结尾；构成...的开头和结尾|To enclose or finish something with similar elements or events.|例句：The concert was bookended by two of the band's most popular songs. 这场音乐会的开头和结尾都是乐队最受欢迎的两首歌。|近义词：begin and end, frame, enclose|反义词:|v.
