obviously:/ˈɒbviəsli/ (UK) /ˈɑːbviəsli/ (US)| adv.|显然地，明显地|In a way that is easy to see or understand.|He was obviously very happy. 他显然非常高兴。|clearly, evidently, plainly, patently|obscurely, unclearly|adverb
Etymology: From Late Latin *obvius (“meeting the sight, obvious”), from Latin obviam (“in the way, meeting,” accusative of obvius), from ob (“towards”) + via (“way, road”).
