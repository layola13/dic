indicators:/ˈɪndɪkeɪtərz/ | n. | 指示器，指标，标志 |Something that shows or suggests the state or level of something. |The economic indicators suggest a recession is coming. 经济指标表明经济衰退即将到来。| synonyms: signs, signals, markers, measures | antonyms:  concealments,  obscurations | noun


/ˈɪndɪkeɪtərz/ | n. |指示剂| A substance that changes color to show that a particular chemical reaction has happened or that a particular substance is present. | Litmus paper is an indicator of acidity. 石蕊试纸是酸碱指示剂。| synonyms: reagent, marker | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from late Latin *indicator, from Latin indicat- ‘pointed out’, from the verb indicare, from in- ‘towards’ + dicare ‘proclaim’.
