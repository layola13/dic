capillose:/ˈkæpɪloʊs/| adj.|毛状的，毛细的，有细毛的|Covered with fine hairs or filaments.|例句：The capillose surface of the leaf traps moisture. 叶子的毛状表面可以锁住水分。

例句：The fungus had a capillose texture. 这种真菌的质地是毛状的。|近义词：hairy, pilose, filamentous, pubescent|反义词：glabrous, smooth|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *capillōsus*, from capillus (“hair”) + -ōsus (suffix forming adjectives indicating fullness or abundance).
