chilliness:/ˈtʃɪlinəs/| n.|寒冷，冷淡，寒意|A state of being chilly; coldness.|There was a distinct chilliness in the air this morning. 今早空气中明显有一股寒意。|近义词：coldness, coolness, frigidity|反义词:warmth, heat|词性:名词
Etymology: From chilly + -ness, from chill + -y.  "Chill" comes from Old English *ciele, from Proto-Germanic *kaliz, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *gel- (cold).
