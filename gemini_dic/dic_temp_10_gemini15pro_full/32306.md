chiffony:/ʃɪˈfɒni/
adjective
薄如蝉翼的；轻飘飘的
light and thin like chiffon; delicate and translucent
Her chiffony dress flowed in the breeze. 她的薄纱连衣裙在微风中飘动。
The curtains were made of a chiffony material. 窗帘是用薄纱材料做的。
近义词:sheer, diaphanous, gauzy, filmy
反义词:thick, heavy, opaque
词性:adjective

词源:
From chiffon + -y.  Ultimately from French *chiffe* “rag”.
