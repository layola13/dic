fatalness:/ˈfeɪtlnəs/
noun
致命性; 宿命性
The quality or state of being fatal; deadliness.
The fatalness of the disease shocked everyone.  这种疾病的致命性震惊了所有人。
Synonyms: lethality, deadliness, mortality
Antonyms: harmlessness, innocuousness, safety
词性: noun

Etymology: From Middle English *fatalnesse, derived from fatal + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin fātālis (“ordained by fate”) + -ness.
