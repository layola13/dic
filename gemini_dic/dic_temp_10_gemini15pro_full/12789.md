assimilator:/əˈsɪməleɪtər/ | n. | 同化者;吸收者;同化器 |  A person or thing that assimilates, especially information or experience.  Someone or something that absorbs and integrates new knowledge, ideas, or cultures. | The company's new CEO proved to be a quick assimilator of the complex business model.  这家公司的新任首席执行官被证明能够快速吸收复杂的商业模式。|  The human digestive system is a complex assimilator of nutrients. 人类的消化系统是一个复杂的营养吸收器。 | synonyms: absorber, integrator, learner, student | antonyms: excluder, rejector | noun
