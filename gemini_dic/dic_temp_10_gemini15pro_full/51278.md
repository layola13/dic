desynchronizing:/diːˈsɪŋkrənaɪzɪŋ/
| v. |去同步化，失步，不同步|The process of causing something to no longer be synchronized or operate at the same time or rate. |The server is desynchronizing from the network. 服务器正在与网络去同步化。|近义词: disconnecting, mistiming, misaligning |反义词: synchronizing, coordinating, integrating|词性:动词的现在分词形式


desynchronize:/diːˈsɪŋkrənaɪz/
| v. |去同步化，失步，不同步|To cause something to no longer be synchronized or operate at the same time or rate. |The storm desynchronized the clocks in the building.  暴风雨导致大楼里的时钟不同步了。|近义词: disconnect, mistime, misalign |反义词: synchronize, coordinate, integrate|词性:动词

词源:  "de-" (prefix meaning removal or reversal) + "synchronize" (from Greek "syn-" meaning together and "chronos" meaning time).
