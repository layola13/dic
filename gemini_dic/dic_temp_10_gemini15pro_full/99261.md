intercomprehensibility:/ˌɪntərˌkɑːmprɪˌhensɪˈbɪləti/ | n. | 相互理解性，互通度 |The ability of speakers of different but related languages to understand each other. |  Spanish and Portuguese exhibit a high degree of intercomprehensibility. 西班牙语和葡萄牙语表现出高度的互通度。| synonym: mutual intelligibility | antonym: incomprehensibility | noun

intercomprehensible:/ˌɪntərˌkɑːmprɪˈhensəbl/ | adj. | 可互相理解的，互通的 |  Mutually intelligible; capable of being understood by speakers of different but related languages. | Norwegian and Danish are largely intercomprehensible. 挪威语和丹麦语在很大程度上是互通的。| synonym: mutually intelligible | antonym: unintelligible | adjective
