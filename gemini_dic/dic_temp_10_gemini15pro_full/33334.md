chuggers:/ˈtʃʌɡəz/
n.
慈善募捐者（尤指街头募捐者）
People who approach passers-by in the street asking for donations to charity, often employed by professional fundraising organizations.
Chuggers are often seen in busy shopping areas.  慈善募捐者经常出现在繁忙的购物区。
The chuggers were very persistent and wouldn't leave me alone. 那些慈善募捐者非常执着，不肯放过我。
近义词：fundraisers, charity collectors, street fundraisers
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用donor - 捐赠者)
词性: 名词

词源：
"Chuggers" is a portmanteau word combining "charity" and "muggers." It originated in the UK in the late 1990s and reflects a sometimes negative perception of street fundraisers as being overly persistent or aggressive.
