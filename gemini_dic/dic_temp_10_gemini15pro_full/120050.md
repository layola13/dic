millpond: /ˈmɪlˌpɑːnd/ (US) /ˈmɪlˌpɒnd/ (UK)| n. |磨坊池塘；平静水面|A body of water behind a mill dam, often characterized by its calmness and stillness.  It can also be used figuratively to describe a very calm and peaceful situation.|The lake was as smooth as a millpond.  湖面像磨坊池塘一样平静。|近义词: lake, pond, pool, still water|反义词: rough water, choppy water|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *myllepond*, from Old English *mylenpond*, a compound of *mylen* ("mill") and *pond*.
