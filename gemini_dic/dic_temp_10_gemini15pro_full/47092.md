daubiest:/ˈdɔːbiɪst/
adjective
涂抹得最多的；污迹斑斑的
most smeared or stained with a sticky substance.
The child's face was the daubiest of the group, covered in chocolate.  这孩子的脸是这群孩子里涂抹得最厉害的，沾满了巧克力。
The walls of the abandoned building were daubiest with graffiti.  废弃建筑的墙壁上涂满了涂鸦，污迹斑斑。
近义词:messiest, dirtiest, smudgiest
反义词:cleanest, tidiest
词性:adjective

词源:
From daub + -y + -est.  "Daub" comes from Old French "dauber" meaning "to plaster, coat."
