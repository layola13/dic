nonemploying:/ˌnɑːnɪmˈplɔɪɪŋ/
adjective
非雇用的；不雇佣的
Not employing workers; not providing jobs.  This term describes entities that do not hire or employ individuals.
The nonemploying sector of the economy includes private households and nonprofit organizations.  非雇佣经济部门包括私人家庭和非营利组织。
These grants are aimed at stimulating the nonemploying sectors. 这些拨款旨在刺激非雇佣部门。
近义词: jobless, unemployed (referring to people), non-hiring
反义词: employing, hiring
词性: adjective
