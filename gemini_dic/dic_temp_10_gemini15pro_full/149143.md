oxidizes:/ˈɒksɪdaɪzɪz/ (UK) /ˈɑːksɪdaɪzɪz/ (US)
verb
氧化
To combine or become combined chemically with oxygen.  The metal oxidizes in air.  金属在空气中氧化。
Iron oxidizes, forming rust. 铁氧化形成锈。
Synonym: rust, corrode, tarnish
Antonym: reduce, deoxidize
词性: 动词
