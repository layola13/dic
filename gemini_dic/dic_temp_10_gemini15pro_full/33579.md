cilicious: /sɪˈlɪʃəs/
adjective
美味的，鲜美的
Covered with cilia; resembling cilia.  Also, in general use, delicious, delightful. (Primarily a humorous or archaic usage.)
例句：The cilicious aroma of the freshly baked bread wafted through the kitchen.  新鲜出炉的面包的香味飘满了整个厨房。

例句：He described the meal as absolutely cilicious. 他形容这顿饭绝对美味。
近义词：delicious, tasty, savory, delectable, luscious
反义词：unappetizing, disgusting, unpalatable, tasteless
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *cilicius, from cilium (“eyelash, eyelid”).
