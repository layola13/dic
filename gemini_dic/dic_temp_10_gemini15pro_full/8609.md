anticoagulants:/ˌæntikoʊˈæɡjələnts/ | n. | 抗凝剂，抗凝血剂 |  Substances that prevent blood from clotting. |  The doctor prescribed anticoagulants to prevent blood clots after the surgery. 医生开了抗凝剂以防止术后出现血栓。 | Synonyms: blood thinners | Antonyms: coagulants, procoagulants | noun

anticoagulant:/ˌæntikoʊˈæɡjələnt/ | adj. | 抗凝的，抗凝血的 | Preventing blood from clotting.  | He's on anticoagulant medication. 他正在服用抗凝药物。| Synonyms:  antithrombotic | Antonyms: coagulant, procoagulant | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- (against) + coagulant (causing coagulation).  Coagulant comes from Latin coagulāre (to curdle), from coagulum (curd).
