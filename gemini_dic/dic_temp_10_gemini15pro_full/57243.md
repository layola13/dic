downinesses: /ˈdaʊninɪsɪz/
noun
柔软；绒毛；轻柔
The soft, fluffy quality of something, like the down of a bird or the fuzz on a peach.  This word refers to multiple instances of this quality.
The downinesses of the chicks made them look incredibly cuddly.  小鸡的绒毛使它们看起来非常可爱。
The various downinesses of the different types of moss provided a textured landscape.  不同种类苔藓的各种绒毛构成了一个富有纹理的景观。
Synonyms: fluffiness, softness, fuzziness
Antonyms: harshness, roughness, coarseness
