marron:/məˈrɒn/ (UK), /məˈrɑːn/ (US)| n. adj.|栗色；栗子；逃亡黑奴|A reddish-brown colour, like that of a chestnut. Also, a large, edible chestnut, especially a European variety with a single large kernel. Historically, also used to refer to a runaway slave who lived in the wilderness.|
例句：
She wore a marron dress. 她穿着一件栗色连衣裙。

The chef roasted the marrons for a delicious side dish.  厨师烤了栗子作为美味的配菜。


近义词：chestnut, auburn,  brown|

词性: noun, adjective
