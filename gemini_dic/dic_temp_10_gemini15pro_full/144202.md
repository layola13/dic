outsighs: /ˈaʊtsaɪz/
n.
叹息，叹气; 泄露的秘密;
A deep sigh expressing sadness, relief, or exhaustion.  A secret or private matter that is inadvertently revealed.
例句：Her outsighs betrayed her inner turmoil. 她的叹息暴露了她内心的混乱。

例句：The diary contained many outsighs about her unhappy marriage. 这本日记包含了许多关于她不幸婚姻的秘密。

近义词：sighs, whispers, revelations, disclosures
反义词：concealments, secrets (as verbs)
词性：名词

词源： "Outsigh" is a relatively recent word, likely formed by analogy with "outcry," combining "out" (meaning outward expression) and "sigh." Its usage remains relatively infrequent.
