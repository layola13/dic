grippingly:/ˈɡrɪpɪŋli/ | adv. | 扣人心弦地，引人入胜地 | In a way that holds the attention intensely. | The story was grippingly told, keeping the audience on the edge of their seats.  这个故事讲得扣人心弦，让观众听得津津有味。 |  rivetingly, captivatingly, enthrallingly | dully, tediously | adverb

词源: From "gripping" (adjective) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Gripping" comes from the verb "grip," meaning to hold firmly.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *gripaną.
