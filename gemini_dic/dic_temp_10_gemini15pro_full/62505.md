endowed:/ɪnˈdaʊd/ | adj. | 赋予的，被赋予的；天生具有…的 | provided or supplied or equipped with (especially as by inheritance or nature) |  She was endowed with great musical talent. 她天生具有非凡的音乐天赋。 | gifted, talented, blessed | deprived, lacking | adjective

endow:/ɪnˈdaʊ/ | vt. | 赋予，捐赠 | to provide with something freely or naturally |  The wealthy businessman endowed the university with a large sum of money.  这位富商向这所大学捐赠了一大笔钱。| bestow, donate, grant | deprive, divest | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *endouer, *endower (“to provide with a dowry”), from en- (“in, into”) + douer (“to give, endow”), from Latin dotare (“to endow”), from dos (“dowry”).
