colley:/ˈkɒli/ (UK) /ˈkɑːli/ (US)| n.|柯利牧羊犬; 苏格兰牧羊犬|A breed of herding dog, including the Rough Collie and the Smooth Collie, originating in Scotland.|例句：The colley herded the sheep with skill. 这只柯利牧羊犬熟练地放牧羊群。|例句：Lassie, a famous fictional colley, was known for her intelligence and loyalty.  著名的虚构柯利犬莱西以其聪明和忠诚而闻名。|近义词：sheepdog, herding dog|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

词源: From Scottish dialect *collie, *coley, probably from the black-faced sheep of Scotland, also called *colley.
