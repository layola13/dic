basipterygoid:/ˌbeɪsɪptɛrɪˈɡɔɪd/ | adj. n. | 基蝶骨的，基蝶骨 | Relating to the base of the pterygoid process of the sphenoid bone.  It can also refer to the basipterygoid processes themselves (a pair of bony projections). |  The basipterygoid processes articulate with the pterygoid bones. 基蝶骨突与翼骨相连。|  The snake's skull shows a distinct basipterygoid articulation.  这条蛇的头骨显示出一个明显的基蝶骨关节。|  Synonym: (as an adjective) sphenopterygoid | Antonym: (no direct antonym, as it describes a specific anatomical location) | adjective, noun

Etymology:
basi- (from Greek basis, meaning "base") + pterygoid (from Greek pteron, meaning "wing," and -eidos, meaning "form" or "shape")
