intensely:/ɪnˈtɛnsli/| adv.|强烈地，剧烈地，紧张地|In a way that shows great concentration, power, or strength.|She stared intensely at the computer screen. 她目不转睛地盯着电脑屏幕。|近义词：strongly, powerfully, fiercely, extremely|反义词：mildly, gently, weakly|词性:副词
词源: From intense + -ly.  "Intense" comes from the Late Latin "intensus," past participle of "intendere," meaning "to stretch out, strain."
