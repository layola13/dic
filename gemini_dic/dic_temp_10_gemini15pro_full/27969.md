cantrip:/ˈkæntrɪp/| n.|戏法，小魔术|A magic trick; a spell.  A small act of magic, often mischievous or playful, and typically one that is relatively easy to perform.  Originally, a charm or spell.|The wizard cast a simple cantrip to light his pipe.|近义词：trick, spell, charm, hex|反义词: -(not applicable)|noun
