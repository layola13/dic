nonpolemic:/ˌnɑːnpəˈlɛmɪk/ | adj. n.| 非争论性的，不引起争论的；非争论性的著作 | Avoiding controversial subjects, not engaging in or marked by controversy. | The meeting was remarkably nonpolemic, focusing on solutions rather than assigning blame.  这次会议非常和谐，重点在于寻找解决方案而不是互相指责。|  The author is known for his nonpolemic approach to complex issues. 这位作者以其对复杂问题不带争议性的处理方法而闻名。| 近义词：conciliatory, pacific, irenic | 反义词：polemical, controversial, contentious | 词性: 形容词, 名词

nonpolemic:/ˌnɑːnpəˈlɛmɪk/ | n. | 非争论性著作 | A book or other work that avoids controversy. | His latest publication is a nonpolemic on the benefits of meditation. 他最新出版的书籍是一本关于冥想益处的非争议性著作。|  She prefers to read nonpolemics, seeking relaxation rather than intellectual sparring. 她更喜欢读一些非争议性的作品，寻求放松而不是智力上的争论。| 近义词：treatises | 反义词：polemics, diatribes | 词性: 名词

词源:  From non- + polemic, ultimately from Greek polemikos "warlike," from polemos "war."
