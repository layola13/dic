erythrocytes: /ɪˈrɪθrəˌsaɪts/ /ɪˈreθrəˌsaɪts/ | n. |红细胞，红血球 | Red blood cells, the cells that carry oxygen throughout the body. |  A complete blood count (CBC) measures the number of erythrocytes in your blood. 全血细胞计数 (CBC) 测量您血液中的红细胞数量。

The doctor examined the patient's erythrocytes under a microscope. 医生在显微镜下检查了病人的红细胞。

| 近义词: red blood cells | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps consider leukocytes (white blood cells)) | 词性: noun
