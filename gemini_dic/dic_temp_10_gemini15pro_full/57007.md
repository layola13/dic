double-parker:/ˈdʌbəl ˈpɑːrkər/ | n. | 并排停车者；双重停车者 |  Someone who parks their car alongside another car that is already parked at the curb, thus blocking it in. | Example 1: The double-parker blocked me in for an hour.  那个并排停车的人把我堵了一个小时。

Example 2:  I hate double-parkers; they're so inconsiderate. 我讨厌并排停车的人；他们太不体贴了。| 近义词: inconsiderate parker, selfish parker | 反义词: considerate parker | 词性: 名词


double-park: /ˈdʌbəl pɑːrk/ | v. | 并排停车；双重停车 | To park one's car beside another that is already parked, usually illegally. | Example 1: He double-parked outside the store while he ran in to get milk. 他在商店外面并排停车，跑进去买牛奶。

Example 2: Don't double-park; it's illegal and very inconsiderate. 不要并排停车；这是违法的，而且很不体贴。 | 近义词: park illegally | 反义词: park legally | 词性: 动词
