hard-headed:/ˌhɑːrdˈhɛdɪd/ | adj. |顽固的；冷静实际的；精明的；清醒的|  Stubbornly unyielding;  practical and realistic, especially in business matters; shrewd and unsentimental. | He's a hard-headed businessman who always puts profits first. 他是一个精明的商人，总是把利润放在第一位。|  She took a hard-headed approach to the problem. 她用冷静实际的方式处理这个问题。 | Synonyms: stubborn, obstinate, pragmatic, realistic, shrewd, unsentimental, level-headed | Antonyms: soft-hearted, sentimental, impractical, unrealistic, naive | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From hard (adj.) + headed (adj.).  "Hard" in this sense means "unyielding" or "firm," while "headed" refers to the mind or intellect.  The combination implies a mind that is "firm" in its opinions or approach.
