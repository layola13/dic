missia:/ˈmɪsiə/| n.|弥撒，圣餐|The celebration of the Eucharist in the Catholic Church.  It is a ritual reenactment of the Last Supper.|She attends missia every Sunday. 她每个星期天都参加弥撒。|近义词：Mass, Eucharist, Holy Communion|反义词: None|词性:noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *missa, from the concluding words of the service *Ite, missa est* (“Go, the dismissal is made”).
