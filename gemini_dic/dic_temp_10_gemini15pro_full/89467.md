honeyberry:/ˈhʌniˌbɛri/ | n. | 蓝靛果忍冬的果实；蜂蜜浆果 | A type of edible berry with a sweet and slightly tart flavor, produced by the honeyberry plant (Lonicera caerulea).  A shrub related to honeysuckle that produces edible blue berries. |  I made a honeyberry pie for the first time, and it was delicious. 我第一次做了蓝靛果馅饼，味道真不错。
