mallenders:/ˈmæləndərz/
noun
马腿腕内侧湿疹
A cracked or eczematous condition affecting the back of the knee of a horse's foreleg.
The horse was suffering from mallenders and couldn't be ridden. 这匹马患有马腿腕内侧湿疹，不能骑。
近义词:grease heel, cracked heels
