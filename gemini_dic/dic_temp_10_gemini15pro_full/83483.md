hackathons:/ˈhækəθɑːnz/
n.
黑客马拉松，编程马拉松
An event, usually lasting several days, in which a large number of people meet to engage in collaborative computer programming.
例句：Many students participated in the hackathon to develop innovative software solutions. 很多学生参加了黑客马拉松来开发创新的软件解决方案。

例句：The company hosted a hackathon to encourage creativity and collaboration among its employees.  这家公司举办了一场黑客马拉松，以鼓励员工之间的创造力和协作。

近义词：coding jams, programming competitions, hackfests
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "leisure time" or "vacation" in a contrasting context)
词性: noun


词源:  "hackathon" is a portmanteau of "hack" and "marathon". "Hack" in this context refers to exploratory programming, not its negative connotation of computer cracking.
