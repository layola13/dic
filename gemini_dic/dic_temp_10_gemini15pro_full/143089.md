outbreaking:/ˈaʊtbreɪkɪŋ/
adjective
爆发的；突发的
suddenly beginning and spreading quickly
例句：The outbreaking war caused widespread panic. 突发的战争引起了广泛的恐慌。

例句：The outbreaking epidemic forced the city into lockdown.  爆发的疫情迫使城市封锁。

近义词：erupting, burgeoning, developing, spreading
反义词：subsiding, waning, declining
词性：adjective

outbreak:/ˈaʊtbreɪk/
noun
爆发；突发
a sudden occurrence of something unwelcome, such as war or disease
例句：There was an outbreak of measles in the school. 学校里爆发了麻疹。

例句：The outbreak of violence shocked the community. 暴力事件的爆发震惊了社区。

近义词：eruption, upsurge, burst, flare-up
反义词：decline, decrease, reduction
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From out + break.  "Break" in this context refers to a sudden bursting forth or eruption. "Out" intensifies the sense of suddenness and widespread impact.
