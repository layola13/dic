fickler:/ˈfɪk(ə)lər/| adj.|善变的，反复无常的|Likely to change, especially due to caprice and whim; not constant or loyal in affections.|The fickler nature of fashion makes it difficult to predict what will be popular next season. 时尚变化无常的特性使得很难预测下一季的流行趋势。|Example: He's such a fickler friend; he's always changing his mind about who he likes. 他真是个善变的朋友，他总是改变他对谁的喜好。
The weather here is notoriously fickle. 这里的天气是出了名的反复无常。|Synonyms: capricious, changeable, erratic, fickle, flighty, inconsistent, mercurial, temperamental, variable, volatile|Antonyms: constant, dependable, faithful, loyal, reliable, resolute, stable, steadfast, steady, unwavering|adjective
