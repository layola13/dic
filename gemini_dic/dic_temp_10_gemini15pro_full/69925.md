fervid:/ˈfɜːrvɪd/ /ˈfɜːrvəd/| adj.|热烈的，狂热的|Characterized by intense emotion; passionate.|She delivered a fervid speech about the importance of education. 她就教育的重要性发表了热情洋溢的演讲。|近义词：passionate, ardent, zealous, fiery|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, calm|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *fervidus*, from fervere (“to boil, glow”).
