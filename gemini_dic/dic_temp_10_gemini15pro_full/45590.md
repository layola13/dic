currant:/ˈkʌrənt/ (UK), /ˈkɜːrənt/ (US)| n. |醋栗；无核小葡萄干|A small dried seedless grape, often used in baking. Also, a small berry, especially one of several kinds of black, red, or white currant.|例句：I added currants and raisins to the cake batter. 我在蛋糕糊里加了醋栗和葡萄干。

currant:/ˈkʌrənt/ (UK), /ˈkɜːrənt/ (US)| n. |(尤指红醋栗或黑醋栗的)小果实|A small berry, especially a red, black or white currant.|例句：The bush was laden with ripe red currants. 灌木丛上挂满了成熟的红醋栗。
