parenthesize:/pəˈrɛnθəˌsaɪz/ /pəˈrɛnθɪˌsaɪz/| v. |加括号；用括号括起来|To enclose (something) in parentheses.|Example: He parenthesized the extra information. 他将补充信息放在括号内.|Example:  The author parenthesized a brief explanation of the term. 作者在括号中对该术语进行了简要说明.| 近义词: bracket, enclose, insert |反义词: remove, extract|词性:动词

parenthesized:/pəˈrɛnθəˌsaɪzd/ /pəˈrɛnθɪˌsaɪzd/| adj. |括在括号中的|Enclosed in parentheses.|Example: The parenthesized clause clarified the meaning of the sentence. 括号中的从句阐明了句子的含义。|Example:  The dates were parenthesized in the document.  日期在文件中用括号括起来了。| 近义词: bracketed, enclosed |反义词:  unbracketed, unenclosed|词性:形容词

parenthesization:/pəˌrɛnθəsəˈzeɪʃən/ /pəˌrɛnθɪsaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |加括号|The act of enclosing something in parentheses.|Example: The parenthesization of the dates made them stand out. 将日期放在括号内使它们更加突出。|Example: Consistent parenthesization is important for clarity. 一致的括号使用对于清晰度很重要。| 近义词: bracketing, enclosure |反义词: removal, extraction |词性:名词

There are not many common set phrases using "parenthesize."  Its usage is generally straightforward. However, here are some examples of how it can be used in context:

He added (parenthetically) that he disagreed with the decision. 他（顺便）补充说他不同意这个决定。
It's important to parenthesize (clarifying information) for your readers.  为读者用括号括起（澄清信息）很重要。
Remember to parenthesize (any additional notes) in your research paper.  记住在你的研究论文中用括号括起（任何额外的注释）。
She decided to parenthesize (the less relevant details) to avoid confusion.  她决定用括号括起（不太相关的细节）以避免混淆。
He carefully parenthesized (the technical terms) for clarity.  他仔细地用括号括起了（技术术语）以使其清晰。
