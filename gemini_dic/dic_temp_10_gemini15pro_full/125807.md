murksome: /ˈmɜːrksəm/ (UK) /ˈmɜːrksəm/ (US)| adj. |黑暗的，阴沉的；朦胧的；隐秘的|Gloomy, dark, and obscure; suggestive of something sinister or unpleasant. |The murksome alley was the perfect setting for a thriller.  黑暗的小巷是惊悚片的完美场景。|
|The murksome depths of the ocean hold countless mysteries.  幽暗的海洋深处蕴藏着无数的奥秘。|近义词: gloomy, dark, shadowy, obscure, dismal|反义词: bright, sunny, cheerful, clear|词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From murk + -some.  Murk meaning darkness or gloom, ultimately from Old Norse myrkr. -some is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "tending to."
