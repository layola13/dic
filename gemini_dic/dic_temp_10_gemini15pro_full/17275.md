baysnet:/ˈbeɪznɛt/| n.|刺刀|A short knife or other pointed weapon designed to be fitted to the muzzle of a rifle and used in hand-to-hand combat.|The soldier fixed his bayonet to his rifle before the charge. 士兵在冲锋前把刺刀装到步枪上。|近义词：dagger, knife, blade|反义词:scabbard|词性:名词

Etymology: From French *baïonnette*, from *Bayonne*, the name of a city in southwestern France where bayonets are believed to have been first manufactured.
