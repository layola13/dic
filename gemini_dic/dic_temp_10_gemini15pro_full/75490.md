frutescent:/fruːˈtɛsənt/
adjective
灌木状的；像灌木的
Resembling a shrub; shrubby.  Relating to or having the characteristics of a shrub, especially in having woody stems.
The frutescent plant grew taller than expected, reaching almost four feet in height.  这种灌木状的植物长得比预期的要高，几乎达到了四英尺高。
The botanist studied the frutescent flora of the region.  植物学家研究了该地区的灌木状植物群。
近义词: shrubby, bushy, suffrutescent
反义词: herbaceous, arboreal
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *frutescentem, present participle of frutescere (“to become shrubby”), from frutex (“shrub”).
