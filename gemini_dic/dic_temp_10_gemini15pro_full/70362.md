fiercest:/ˈfɪəsɪst/| adj.|最凶猛的，最激烈的|superlative form of fierce;  meaning most aggressive, violent, or intense.|The fiercest battles were fought in the north. 最激烈的战斗发生在北方。|近义词：most aggressive, most violent, most intense, harshest, severest|反义词：gentlest, mildest, calmest|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *fers*, from Anglo-Norman *fers*, *fiers* (“wild, cruel, proud”), from Latin *ferus* (“wild, untamed”).  Cognate with French *fier*.
