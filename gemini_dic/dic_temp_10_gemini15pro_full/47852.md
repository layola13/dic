decedent:/dɪˈsiːd(ə)nt/
noun
死者，被继承人
a person who has died
The decedent's will stipulated that his estate be divided equally among his children.  死者的遗嘱规定他的遗产由他的孩子们平分。
The police are trying to identify the decedent. 警方正在努力确认死者的身份。
synonyms: deceased, the departed, the late
antonyms:  living, survivor
n.
