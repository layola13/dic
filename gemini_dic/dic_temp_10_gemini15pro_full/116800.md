ETYMOLOGY->
From German *maukisch "sickly, squeamish," from Mauke "mallow" (related to Middle Dutch maluwe), because mallows were used medicinally, but thought to be insipid.  The sense evolution was from "sickly sweet" (of taste) to "sickly sentimental."
