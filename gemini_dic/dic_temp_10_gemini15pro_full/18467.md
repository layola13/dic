bemusedly:/bɪˈmjuːzɪdli/ | adv. | 困惑地；茫然地 | In a manner showing that you are confused or bewildered. | He looked at her bemusedly, trying to understand what she meant. 他困惑地看着她，试图理解她的意思。| She smiled bemusedly at his sudden outburst. 她对他突然的爆发感到困惑地笑了笑。 | 近义词: confusedly, bewilderedly, perplexedly | 反义词: clearly, knowingly, certainly | 词性: 副词
