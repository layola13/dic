mis-evaluating:/ˌmɪsɪˈvæljueɪtɪŋ/

They mis-evaluated the risks involved in the investment. 他们错误地评估了投资中的风险。|近义词:misjudging, underestimating, overestimating|反义词:correctly evaluating, accurately assessing|词性:动词
