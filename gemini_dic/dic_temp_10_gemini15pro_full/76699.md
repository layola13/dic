gamay:/ˈɡɑːmaɪ/| n.| 婚姻; 婚礼 |A marriage ceremony or wedding.  It derives from the Tagalog word "gamay," which means "wedding" or "marriage."|The whole village celebrated the gamay of the young couple. 全村都庆祝这对年轻夫妇的婚礼。|近义词：wedding, marriage, nuptials|反义词: divorce, separation|词性: 名词
