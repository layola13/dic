lithely:/ˈlaɪðli/| adv.|轻盈地，灵活地|Moving and bending with ease and grace; nimbly.|The dancer moved lithely across the stage. 舞蹈演员轻盈地穿过舞台。|近义词：nimbly, gracefully, agilely, deftly|反义词：clumsily, awkwardly, stiffly|词性:副词

词源: From Middle English *lithli, from Old English *liðlice, from liðe (“gentle, mild, soft, meek; pleasant, agreeable; supple, pliant”) +‎ -lice (adverbial suffix). Cognate with Old Saxon liðliko (“mildly”), Old Frisian liðlik (“mildly”), Dutch lijdelijk (“passive, submissive”), German leidlich (“tolerable”). More at lithe.
