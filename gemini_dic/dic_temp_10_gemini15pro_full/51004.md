desistence:/dɪˈzɪstəns/
n.
停止，放弃
the act of desisting; cessation
After repeated warnings, the company finally showed desistence from its illegal practices.  在多次警告之后，该公司最终停止了其非法行为。
cessation, discontinuation, abandonment
persistence, continuation, perseverance
noun


desistences:/dɪˈzɪstənsɪz/
n. (plural)
停止，放弃（复数）
plural form of desistence
