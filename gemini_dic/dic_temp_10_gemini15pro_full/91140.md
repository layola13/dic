hybridized:/ˈhaɪbrɪdaɪzd/ | adj. | 杂交的，混合的 |  Made by combining different elements; crossbred.  |  The hybridized rose had a unique color.  杂交玫瑰有着独特的颜色。 | synonyms: crossbred, mixed, combined, blended | antonyms: pure, unmixed, homogeneous | adjective

hybridize:/ˈhaɪbrɪdaɪz/ | v. | 杂交，混合 | To crossbreed or combine different elements. | Scientists hybridized the two plant species to create a new variety. 科学家将这两种植物杂交以创造一个新品种。 | synonyms: crossbreed, interbreed, mix, blend, merge, combine | antonyms: separate, segregate, purify | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From hybrid + -ize.  "Hybrid" comes from the Latin "hybrida," meaning "offspring of a tame sow and a wild boar."  The suffix "-ize" forms verbs from nouns and adjectives, meaning "to make or become like."
