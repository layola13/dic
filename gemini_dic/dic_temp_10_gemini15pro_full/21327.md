bloody-mindedness:/ˌblʌdiˈmaɪndɪdnəs/
|n.|顽固; 倔强; 不服输; 故意刁难|A deliberate and stubborn contrariness.  It implies a perverse determination to do the opposite of what is expected or desired, often out of sheer obstinacy.|He showed his bloody-mindedness by refusing to cooperate. 他以拒绝合作来表示他的顽固。|stubbornness, obstinacy, perversity, contrariness|compliance, cooperation|noun
