aqueous:/ˈeɪkwiəs/| adj.|水的，水溶性的|of or containing water, typically as a solvent or medium.|例句：An aqueous solution of sodium chloride. 氯化钠水溶液。|近义词：watery, hydrous|反义词：anhydrous|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *aquosus*, from aqua (“water”).
