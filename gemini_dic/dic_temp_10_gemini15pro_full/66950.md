experiencer:/ɪkˈspɪəriənsər/| n.| 经验者；体验者；感受者|Someone who has a particular experience or feeling. |例句：The experiencer of the dream felt a sense of unease. 梦的体验者感到一种不安。

例句：The researchers interviewed the experiencers of the phenomenon. 研究人员采访了这种现象的经历者。


近义词：perceiver, undergoer, subject|反义词: observer (sometimes)|词性:noun
