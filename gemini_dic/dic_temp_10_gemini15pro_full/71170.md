fissiped:/ˈfɪsɪped/ | adj. | 裂足的，趾行的 | Having separate toes, not webbed.  Relating to animals, especially mammals, whose toes are not webbed or joined together. |  Dogs and cats are fissiped animals.  狗和猫是裂足动物。 | The raccoon is a nocturnal, fissiped mammal. 浣熊是一种夜行性裂足哺乳动物。 | 近义词: digitigrade | 反义词: pinniped, web-footed | 词性: 形容词
