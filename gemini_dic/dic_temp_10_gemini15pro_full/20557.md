blackball:/ˈblækˌbɔːl/ (US), /ˈblækˌbɔːl/ (UK)| n. v.| 黑球；反对票；排斥 |A small black ball used in voting, especially to indicate rejection of a candidate. The act of rejecting someone or something in this way; ostracism.  To vote against (someone); reject (an applicant).|The committee blackballed his application. 委员会否决了他的申请。|近义词：veto, reject, bar, exclude|反义词：approve, accept, elect|词性: 名词, 动词
