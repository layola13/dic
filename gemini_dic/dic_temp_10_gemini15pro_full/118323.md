meretricious: /ˌmɛrɪˈtrɪʃəs/ | adj. | 华而不实的；俗艳的；徒有其表的 | Apparently attractive but having in reality no value or integrity. |  Her prose style is meretricious, all glitter and no substance. 她的散文风格华而不实，只有浮华，没有实质内容。

Her meretricious beauty may attract admirers, but it won't win her true friends. 她那俗艳的美貌可能会吸引一些爱慕者，但不会赢得真正的朋友。


| 近义词:  gaudy, tawdry, flashy, showy, ostentatious | 反义词:  genuine, authentic, real, understated, simple | 词性: adjective
