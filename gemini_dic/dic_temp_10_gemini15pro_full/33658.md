cinephiles:/ˈsɪnəˌfaɪlz/
n.
电影爱好者，影迷
A person who is fond of or enthusiastic about cinema; a devotee of motion pictures.
Martin Scorsese's films are often targeted towards cinephiles. 马丁·斯科塞斯的电影通常面向电影爱好者。
The festival is a must-see for cinephiles.  这个电影节是影迷的必看之选。
近义词:movie buffs, film enthusiasts, movie lovers
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps someone who dislikes movies)


词性: noun (名词)

词源: From French *cinéphile*, from *ciné-* (short for *cinématographe*, "cinema") + *-phile* (lover of), from Greek *philos* (loving).
