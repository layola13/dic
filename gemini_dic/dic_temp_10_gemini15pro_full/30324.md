censorships:/ˈsensərʃɪps/
n.
审查制度; 审查机构
The practice of officially examining books, movies, etc., and suppressing unacceptable parts.
The government imposed strict censorships on the media. 政府对媒体实施了严格的审查制度。
censorship, suppression, restriction
freedom, openness
noun
