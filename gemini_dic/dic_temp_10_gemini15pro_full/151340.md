parka:/ˈpɑːrkə/ (US), /ˈpɑːkə/ (UK)| n. |派克大衣，风雪大衣|A hip-length or longer jacket with a hood, often lined with fur or other warm material, designed to protect against cold weather.|He wore a thick parka to protect himself from the biting wind. 他穿了一件厚厚的派克大衣来抵御刺骨的寒风。|Example: The explorer needed a warm parka for the Arctic expedition.  这位探险家需要一件保暖的派克大衣进行北极探险。|Synonyms: anorak, windbreaker, winter coat|Antonyms: shorts, t-shirt|Noun
