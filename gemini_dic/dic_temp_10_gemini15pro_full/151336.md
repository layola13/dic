parity:/ˈpærəti/ | n. |平等，同等；奇偶性；平价 |the state or condition of being equal, especially regarding status or pay; the fact of a number being even or odd; the official value of one currency in terms of another|The company is committed to achieving gender parity in leadership roles. 该公司致力于在领导角色中实现性别平等。|Synonyms: equality, equivalence, uniformity, levelness |Antonyms: disparity, inequality, difference, imbalance |noun

parity:/ˈpærəti/ | n. |奇偶校验 | a system of checking for errors in computer data by counting whether the number of 1s (or 0s) in a sequence of bits is odd or even|Parity checking is a simple way to detect single-bit errors in data transmission. 奇偶校验是检测数据传输中单比特错误的简单方法。|Synonyms: error detection, check, verification |Antonyms: N/A |noun
