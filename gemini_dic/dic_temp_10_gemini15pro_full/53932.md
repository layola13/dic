discourteousness:/dɪsˈkɜːrtiəsnəs/
n.
失礼；无礼
lack of courtesy; rudeness
Her discourteousness to the staff was shocking. 她对员工的无礼令人震惊。
Synonyms: rudeness, impoliteness, incivility, disrespect, bad manners
Antonyms: courtesy, politeness, civility, respect, good manners
