deviation:/ˌdiːviˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 偏差，背离，偏离 | a variation from the common standard, type, etc. |  The deviation from the plan caused some delays.  偏离计划造成了一些延误。 | synonyms: divergence, departure, variance, aberration | antonyms: conformity, adherence, compliance | noun

deviation:/ˌdiːviˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 标准差，离差 |  (statistics) the difference between a particular number and the average or mean of a set of numbers | The standard deviation is a measure of the spread of a dataset. 标准差是衡量数据集分散程度的指标。 | synonyms: variance, dispersion, spread | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "central tendency" as a contrasting concept) | noun
