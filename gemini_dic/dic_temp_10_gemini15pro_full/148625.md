overthickly: /ˌoʊvərˈθɪkli/
adverb
过厚地，过于浓厚地
in an excessively thick manner
The paint was applied overthickly, resulting in a rough, uneven finish. 油漆涂得太厚了，导致表面粗糙不平。
The sauce was overthickly ladled onto the pasta. 酱汁浇在意大利面上太厚了。
近义词: too thickly, excessively thickly
反义词: thinly, sparsely
词性: adverb

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "thick" (adjective) + "-ly" (adverb suffix)
