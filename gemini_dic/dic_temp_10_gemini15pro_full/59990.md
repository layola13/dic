editioner:/ɛˈdɪʃənər/ | n. |版本编辑；版本制作人|A person who prepares or is responsible for an edition of a book, newspaper, or other publication.|The editioner carefully reviewed the manuscript before sending it to the printer. 版本编辑在将稿件送交印刷厂之前仔细地审查了它。|近义词：editor, compiler |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


Etymology: From "edition" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Edition" comes from the French "édition," ultimately from the Latin "editus," past participle of "edere" (to publish).
