loathest:/ˈloʊðɪst/| adj.|最讨厌的，最厌恶的|Superlative form of loath: most loath.  Feeling intense dislike or disgust for something.|例句：He was the man she loathed most in the world. 他是她在这个世界上最讨厌的人。

例句：Of all the chores, she loathed ironing the most. 在所有的家务中，她最讨厌熨衣服。|近义词：detest, abhor, despise, hate|反义词：love, adore, cherish|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *lothest, from Old English lāðost (“most hateful, most hostile”), superlative degree of lāð (“hostile, hateful, loathsome, evil”). Cognate with Scots laith (“loath, unwilling”), West Frisian leed (“ugly, nasty, loathsome”), Dutch leed (“sad, sorrowful, loathsome”), German leid (“sorrowful, painful”), Old Norse leiðr (“loathsome, weary”). Related to loathe.
