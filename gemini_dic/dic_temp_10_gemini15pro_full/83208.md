guzzler:/ˈɡʌzlər/| n.|耗油量大的汽车；狂饮者|A vehicle that consumes a large amount of fuel. A person who drinks large quantities of liquid.|That old car is a real gas guzzler. 那辆旧车真是个油老虎。 He's a notorious beer guzzler. 他是个臭名昭著的啤酒狂饮者。|近义词：gas hog, heavy drinker, imbiber|反义词: fuel-efficient car, teetotaler|词性:名词

Etymology: From the verb "guzzle" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Guzzle" comes from the Middle French "gosillier" (to guzzle, devour), from "gosier" (throat).
