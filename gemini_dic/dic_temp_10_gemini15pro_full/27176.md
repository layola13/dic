calligrapher: /kəˈlɪɡrəfər/ | n. | 书法家 | A person who practices or specializes in calligraphy (the art of beautiful handwriting). | The calligrapher carefully drew each stroke of the Chinese character.  这位书法家仔细地描绘了每一个汉字的笔画。|  The calligrapher's work was displayed in the museum.  这位书法家的作品在博物馆展出。 | synonym: scribe, penman | antonym:  (No direct antonym, possibly "typis") | noun
