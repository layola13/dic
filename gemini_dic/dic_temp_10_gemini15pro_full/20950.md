bleeds:/bliːdz/
verb
流血
loses blood; sheds blood
The cut on my finger bleeds. 我的手指上的伤口在流血。

bleeds:/bliːdz/
noun
流血（bleed的名词复数）
instances of losing blood
The nurse cleaned up the bleeds. 护士清理了血迹。


Etymology: Old English *blēdan (attested only in past participle blēden), from Proto-Germanic *blōdjaną "to bleed," from *blōdam "blood."

Synonyms: hemorrhages, leaks, oozes, flows, gushes, weeps (for slight bleeding)
Antonyms: clots, congeals, heals, scabs
