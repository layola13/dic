what:/wɒt/(UK) /wɑːt/(US) |pron. det. adv.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information about something. |What is your name? 你叫什么名字？
What color is her hair?  她的头发是什么颜色？|近义词: which, whatever |反义词: N/A |pron., det., adv.
how:/haʊ/ |adv. |如何，怎样| In what way or manner; by what means. |How are you? 你好吗？
How do you get to school? 你怎么去上学？|近义词: however |反义词: N/A |adv.


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you usually eat for breakfast? 你早餐通常吃什么？
What does she think about the new policy? 她对新政策有什么看法？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done to improve her skills? 她做了什么来提高她的技能？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last weekend? 你上周末做了什么？
What happened at the party? 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had they discussed before the decision was made? 在做出决定之前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I arrived? 在我到达之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen if we don't take action? 如果我们不采取行动会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? 他们将在下次会议上讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by the time we get back? 等我们回来的时候会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2030? 到2030年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
If I were you, what would I do in this situation? 如果我是你，在这种情况下我会做什么？

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? 会议上说了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the summer? 你暑假的计划是什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what he's saying. 她不明白他在说什么。
