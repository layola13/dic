einsteins:/ˈaɪnstaɪnz/| n.|爱因斯坦们（复数）; (非正式)天才;|A group of highly intelligent people, often used humorously or ironically to refer to someone very smart.  指的是一群非常聪明的人，通常幽默或讽刺地用来指代某个非常聪明的人。|例句：We've got a team of einsteins working on this problem. 我们有一群天才在研究这个问题。|例句：He thinks he's an einstein, but he can't even figure out how to use the coffee machine. 他自以为是天才，但他连怎么用咖啡机都搞不懂。|近义词：geniuses, intellectuals, brainiacs|反义词：idiots, fools, dummies|词性:名词
