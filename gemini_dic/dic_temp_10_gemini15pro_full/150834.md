ETYMOLOGY->
From "paragliding" + -'s (possessive suffix).  "Paragliding" is a blend of "para-" (meaning alongside or beyond) + "gliding." "Para-" comes from Greek "para" (beside, alongside). "Gliding" comes from "glide," from Old English "glidan."
