despairing:/dɪˈspeərɪŋ/
adjective
绝望的，感到无望的
Giving up all hope; filled with despair.
She gave him a despairing look. 她绝望地看了他一眼。
近义词:hopeless, despondent, desperate
反义词:hopeful, optimistic
词性:adjective

despairing:/dɪˈspeərɪŋ/
verb
绝望
Losing all hope. (Present participle of "despair")
He was despairing of ever finding a job. 他对找到工作感到绝望。
近义词:losing hope
反义词:being hopeful
词性:verb
