featurely:/ˈfiːtʃərli/ | adv. | 特别地，显著地 |  In a way that features something prominently; conspicuously.  | The magazine featurely displayed the new fashion trends. 这本杂志特别展示了新的时尚潮流。| 近义词: prominently, conspicuously, notably | 反义词: insignificantly, subtly, unobtrusively | 词性: 副词

词源:  Derived from the noun "feature" + the adverbial suffix "-ly".  From the Old French "faiture" (making, fashioning), and ultimately from the Latin "facere" (to do, to make).
