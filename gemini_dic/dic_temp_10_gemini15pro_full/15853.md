baller:/ˈbɔːlər/ (US), /ˈbɔːlə(r)/ (UK)| n. |高手；能人；富人；篮球运动员|A person who is exceptionally good at something, especially in sports, or someone who is rich and successful, often flaunting their wealth.  It can also refer specifically to a basketball player.|例句：He's a real baller on the court. 他在球场上是个高手。

例句：She's living the baller lifestyle with her new yacht. 她开着新游艇过着富裕的生活。|近义词：player, hustler, high-roller, big shot, athlete (especially in basketball)|反义词:amateur, loser, nobody|词性:noun
