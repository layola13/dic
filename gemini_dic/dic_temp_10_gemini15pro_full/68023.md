factorial:/fækˈtɔːriəl/ | adj. n. |阶乘的；阶乘 | of or relating to factorials; the product of all the integers from one up to a given integer.  |The factorial of 6 is 720 (1*2*3*4*5*6=720). | 6的阶乘是720（1*2*3*4*5*6=720）。| 近义词: multiplicative | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective, noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *factorialis "of a maker or doer," from Latin factor "maker, doer" (see factor (n.)).  Mathematical sense is from 1808, translating French factorielle.

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The factorial of a number represents the product of all positive integers less than or equal to that number.
一个数的阶乘表示小于或等于该数的所有正整数的乘积。

Five factorial is written as 5! and equals 120.
五的阶乘写成5！，等于120。
