homiletical:/ˌhɒmɪˈlɛtɪkəl/ /ˌhoʊmɪˈlɛtɪkəl/
adjective
说教的；讲道的
Of or relating to homilies or preaching; marked by a moralizing or sermonizing tone.
The preacher's homiletical skills were renowned throughout the region.  这位传教士的讲道技巧闻名整个地区。
The professor's lectures were often quite homiletical, focusing on moral lessons. 教授的讲座常常带有说教意味，侧重于道德教训。
近义词:preachy, sermonizing, moralistic, didactic
反义词:secular, non-moralistic
词性:adjective
