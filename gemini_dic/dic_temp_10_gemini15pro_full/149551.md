pagehood:/ˈpeɪdʒˌhʊd/| n.|少年时代，童稚时代|The joys and anxieties of pagehood. 少年时代的欢乐和焦虑。|近义词:childhood, boyhood, girlhood, adolescence, youth|反义词:adulthood, maturity|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From page (boy servant) + -hood (state, condition).  Originally referring to the period of being a page, it has broadened to encompass a more general sense of youth.
