epistemically:/ˌɛpɪˈstiːmɪkli/ | adv. | 认识论地 |  Relating to knowledge or the conditions for acquiring it; from the standpoint of knowledge. |  Epistemically, his argument is weak because it relies on anecdotal evidence. 认识论上，他的论点很薄弱，因为它依赖轶事证据。|  cognitively, intellectually | practically, empirically | adverb
