cabalas:/kəˈbɑːləz/ | n. | 卡巴拉，犹太神秘哲学；阴谋；秘密结社 | A system of Jewish mystical interpretation of the Bible. Also refers to secret plots or intrigues. |  The student delved into the complexities of the cabalas.  这个学生深入研究了卡巴拉的复杂性。

The politicians were accused of forming a cabala to manipulate the election. 这些政客被指控结成秘密团体操纵选举。 | 近义词：plots, intrigues, conspiracies, schemes | 反义词：transparency, openness | 词性: noun

词源: From Late Latin *cabala, from Hebrew qabbālāh "received (lore)," from qibbel "to receive."
