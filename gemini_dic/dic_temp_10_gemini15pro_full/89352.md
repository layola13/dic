homologue:/ˈhɒməlɒɡ (UK), ˈhɑːməlɔːɡ (US)/| n.|同系物，同源物|Something that corresponds to something else in a different context, especially a similar organ, part, or chemical compound.  A chromosome that is similar in physical attributes and genetic information to another chromosome with which it pairs during meiosis.|The two genes are homologues.|近义词：counterpart, equivalent, analog, parallel|反义词: N/A|noun


homologous:/həˈmɒləɡəs (UK), həˈmɑːləɡəs (US)/| adj.|同源的|Having the same relation, relative position, or structure.|The wings of a bat and the arms of a human are homologous structures.|近义词：corresponding, similar, analogous|反义词: analogous (sometimes)|adjective
