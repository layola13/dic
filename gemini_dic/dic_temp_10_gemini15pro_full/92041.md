hyperphalangia:/ˌhaɪpərˌfælænˈdʒiːə/
|n.|多指（趾）畸形|A congenital condition characterized by the presence of extra phalanges (bones) in the fingers or toes.|The child was born with hyperphalangia in both hands.|近义词:polydactyly, polyphalangism|反义词:oligodactyly, brachydactyly|noun
