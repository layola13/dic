nonostentation:/ˌnɒnˌɒstɛnˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 不炫耀，不夸示 |  The state or quality of being modest and unpretentious; avoidance of ostentation. |  Her nonostentation about her wealth was admirable. 她对自己的财富不炫耀，令人钦佩。|  modesty, humility, unpretentiousness, simplicity | ostentation, showiness, flamboyance | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + ostentation (display intended to impress others).  Ostentation ultimately derives from Latin *ostentāre, frequentative of ostendere (“to show”).
