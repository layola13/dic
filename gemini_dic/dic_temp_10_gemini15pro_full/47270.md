de-anglicizes:/diːˈæŋɡləˌsaɪzɪz/
verb
使非英国化；去除英语化
To remove English influence or characteristics from something, particularly language or culture.
The government de-anglicized street names after gaining independence. 政府在独立后将街道名称去英语化。
The author consciously de-anglicizes his prose to give it a more local flavor.  作者有意识地去除其散文的英语化色彩，赋予其更浓郁的当地风味。
Synonyms: localize, vernacularize, indigenize
Antonyms: anglicize, westernize
词性:v.
