carpetbagger: /ˈkɑːrpɪtˌbæɡər/ (US), /ˈkɑːpɪtˌbæɡə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 投机政客；外来剥削者 | A person from the northern United States who went to the South after the Civil War to profit from the Reconstruction. | Example 1: Many southerners resented the carpetbaggers who came to their states seeking political power. 许多南方人憎恨那些来到他们州寻求政治权力的投机政客。

Example 2: The carpetbaggers were accused of exploiting the vulnerable South for their own gain.  这些外来剥削者被指控为了自己的利益而剥削脆弱的南方。| 近义词: opportunist, profiteer, exploiter | 反义词: native, local, resident | 词性: noun
