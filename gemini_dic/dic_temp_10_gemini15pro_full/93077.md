idolous: /ˈaɪdələs/ | adj. | 崇拜偶像的；盲目崇拜的 | Given to worshipping idols; excessively or blindly devoted. |  She was idolous of her favorite singer, attending every concert and buying every piece of merchandise. 她非常崇拜她最喜欢的歌手，参加每一场演唱会，购买每一件周边商品。

He had an idolous devotion to his football team, bordering on obsession. 他对他的足球队有着近乎痴迷的盲目崇拜。| 近义词: idolatrous, worshipping, adoring | 反义词: critical, skeptical, irreverent | 词性: 形容词
