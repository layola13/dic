conservational:/ˌkɒnsəˈveɪʃənəl/ (UK), /ˌkɑːnsərˈveɪʃənəl/ (US)| adj.| 保守的，保护的，会话的|Relating to or concerned with the preservation or restoration of the natural environment, or inclined to avoid depletion of natural resources;  pertaining to or suggesting conversation; restrained, moderate.|例句：She has a conservational approach to using water. 她对用水采取保守的态度。|近义词：protective, preservationist, restrained, moderate|反义词：wasteful, extravagant, radical|词性:形容词
