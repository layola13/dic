amphibrach:/ˈæmfɪbræk/ | n. | 短长格,抑扬扬格 | A metrical foot consisting of a short syllable between two long syllables or (in accentual verse) an unstressed syllable between two stressed syllables. |  The word "amphibrach" itself is an amphibrach. “amphibrach”这个词本身就是一个短长格。|  The poet used amphibrachs throughout the poem to create a lilting rhythm. 诗人整首诗都用了短长格，以营造轻快的节奏。| 近义词: trisyllable, ternary foot | 反义词: iamb, trochee | 词性: noun
