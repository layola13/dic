brittle:/ˈbrɪtl/| adj.|易碎的，脆弱的|Easily broken or cracked; fragile.  Not flexible; easily damaged or destroyed. |The old vase was so brittle that it crumbled when I touched it.  这个旧花瓶太脆了，我一碰就碎了。

The dry leaves are brittle and break easily.  干枯的叶子很脆，很容易碎。|Near synonyms: fragile, frail, delicate, crisp, friable|Near antonyms: tough, resilient, flexible, pliable, durable|adjective

brittles:/ˈbrɪtlz/| n.|脆糖，硬糖|A type of hard, brittle candy, typically made with nuts and caramelized sugar. |My grandmother always makes peanut brittles for Christmas. 我祖母总是在圣诞节做花生脆糖。

The brittles were so delicious that I couldn't stop eating them.  这些脆糖太好吃了，我停不下来。|Near synonyms: toffee, hard candy, nut brittle|Near antonyms: fudge, caramel (softer candies)|noun
