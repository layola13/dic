electrifier:/ɪˈlɛktrɪfaɪər/| n.|电气化者，充电器|A person or thing that electrifies something.  Someone or something that excites or thrills people.|Tesla was a pioneer and an electrifier of the modern world. 特斯拉是现代世界的先驱和电气化者。|Synonyms: energizer, stimulator, galvanizer|Antonyms: damper, depressant|noun

electrify:/ɪˈlɛktrɪfaɪ/| vt.|使电气化，使兴奋，使激动|1. To charge with electricity. 2. To thrill or excite.|The news electrified the nation.  这条消息使全国振奋。|Synonyms: excite, thrill, stimulate, energize|Antonyms: bore, calm, soothe|verb
