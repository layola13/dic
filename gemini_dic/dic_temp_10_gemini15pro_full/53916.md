discouragers:/dɪˈskɜːrɪdʒərz/
n.
令人沮丧的人或事物
People or things that discourage.
The constant rain and cold weather were discouragers for the team.  持续的阴雨和寒冷的天气让团队士气低落。
His critical comments were real discouragers. 他那些批评的言论真的很让人沮丧。
Synonyms: deterrents, obstacles, impediments
Antonyms: motivators, encouragers, inspirations
noun
