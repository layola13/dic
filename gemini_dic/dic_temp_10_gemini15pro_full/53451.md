disapprovable:/ˌdɪsəˈpruːvəbl/| adj.|不值得赞同的，可非难的|Something that is disapprovable is considered to be wrong or bad and deserving of disapproval.|Her behavior was highly disapprovable. 她的行为非常不值得赞同。|Synonyms: objectionable, reprehensible, censurable, blameworthy|Antonyms: commendable, laudable, praiseworthy|Word Origin: Late 19th century: from dis- + approvable|

disapprove:/ˌdɪsəˈpruːv/| v.|不赞成，反对|To have or express an unfavorable opinion of.|He strongly disapproved of her methods. 他强烈反对她的方法。|Synonyms: object to, deprecate, condemn|Antonyms: approve, endorse, commend|Word Origin: Middle English: from dis- (expressing reversal) + approve|
