capsulate:/ˈkæpsjʊleɪt/ /kæpˈsʊleɪt/| v. |封装，概括|To enclose something in a capsule or as if in a capsule.  To summarize or condense.| The company encapsulates the vitamins in a soft gel.  这家公司将维生素封装在软胶囊中。|近义词：enclose, summarize, condense, epitomize|反义词:expand, elaborate|词性:动词

capsulate:/ˈkæpsjʊleɪt/ /kæpˈsʊleɪt/| adj. |胶囊状的|Shaped like a capsule.| The bacteria have a capsulate form. 这些细菌呈胶囊状。|近义词：capsular|反义词:|词性:形容词


capsulate:/ˈkæpsjʊleɪt/ /kæpˈsʊleɪt/| n. |胶囊|A small case or container, especially one containing medicine in powder or liquid form.| The doctor prescribed capsules for the infection. 医生开了胶囊治疗感染。|近义词：capsule|反义词:|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *capsulatus, past participle of *capsulare "to put in a box," from Latin capsula "small box," diminutive of capsa "box, case."
