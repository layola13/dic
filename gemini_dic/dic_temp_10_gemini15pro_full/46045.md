cyclic:/ˈsaɪklɪk/ /ˈsɪklɪk/| adj.|循环的，周期的|Occurring in cycles; regularly repeated.|例句：The cyclic nature of the seasons.  季节的周期性。|近义词：recurring, periodic, circular|反义词:linear, acyclic|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin *cyclicus, from Ancient Greek κυκλικός (kuklikós, “circular, cyclic”), from κύκλος (kúklos, “circle, cycle”).
