oblatum:/əˈbleɪtəm/ /ɒˈblɑːtəm/ | n. | 供奉物，祭品；奉献物 | Something offered, especially a sacrifice or religious offering.  Derived from the Latin word "offerre," meaning "to offer." It is the past participle of "offero".  It often appears in religious or historical contexts referring to something offered to a deity or a superior power. | The oblatum was placed on the altar.  供奉物被放置在祭坛上。

The monks presented oblatums of bread and wine.  僧侣们献上了面包和葡萄酒作为供品。 | 近义词: offering, sacrifice, donation, tribute | 反义词: receipt, acceptance (in the sense of receiving rather than giving) | 词性: noun
