dramaturges:/ˈdræmətɜːrdʒz/ /ˈdrɑːmətɜːrdʒz/
noun
剧作家；戏剧顾问
A dramaturge is a literary editor on the staff of a theater who consults with authors and edits texts.
The dramaturge helped the playwright develop the characters in the play.  这位剧作家帮助剧作家发展了戏剧中的人物。
synonyms: playwright, dramatist, scriptwriter, screenwriter
antonyms:  N/A (Dramaturge doesn't have a direct opposite)
词性: noun
