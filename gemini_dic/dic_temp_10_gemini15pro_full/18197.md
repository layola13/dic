belay:/bɪˈleɪ/| v. n.|停止，固定，保护（登山），证实，确认|To secure a climber by attaching the climbing rope to an anchor point. To stop or cease.  To confirm or make certain.|例句：The climber shouted "Belay on!" to signal his partner to secure the rope. 登山者喊“保护！”，示意他的同伴固定绳索。

例句：Belay that order!  停止执行那道命令！|近义词：secure, fasten, stop, cease, confirm, verify|反义词：release, loosen, start, continue, deny, refute|词性:动词, 名词

belay:/bɪˈleɪ/| n.|绳索的固定点，保护点|The act of belaying or the place where a rope is secured. |例句：The belay held firm, preventing the climber from falling.  保护点很牢固，防止了登山者坠落。

例句：Make sure you have a solid belay before you start climbing. 确保你在开始攀登之前有一个牢固的保护点。|近义词：anchor, securing point|反义词：N/A|词性:名词
