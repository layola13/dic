nonbiodegradable:/ˌnɒnˌbaɪəʊdɪˈɡreɪdəbl/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˌbaɪoʊdɪˈɡreɪdəbl/
adjective
不可生物降解的
Not able to be broken down by natural processes, especially by the action of bacteria.
Plastic bags are nonbiodegradable and contribute to environmental pollution.  塑料袋是不可生物降解的，会导致环境污染。
Many plastics are non-biodegradable, posing a significant threat to the environment. 许多塑料是不可生物降解的，对环境构成了重大威胁。
Synonyms: non-decomposable, persistent
Antonyms: biodegradable, decomposable
词性: adjective
