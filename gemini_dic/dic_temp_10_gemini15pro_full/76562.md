gallerylike:/ˈɡæləriˌlaɪk/| adj.|类似画廊的，像画廊一样的|Resembling or characteristic of an art gallery, often in terms of spaciousness, lighting, or display.  It suggests a space is arranged for the display of items, often artistic or visually appealing, in a manner similar to how artwork is presented in a gallery.|The room, with its high ceilings and strategically placed lighting, had a very gallerylike feel.  这个房间，有着高高的天花板和精心布置的灯光，有一种非常像画廊的感觉。| The minimalist apartment had a gallerylike atmosphere, with white walls and carefully curated furniture.  这间极简主义风格的公寓有一种画廊般的氛围，白色的墙壁和精心挑选的家具。|近义词：gallery-esque, museumlike|反义词: cluttered, cramped|词性:形容词
