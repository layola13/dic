adjective

Suitable to be sold at an auction.
The antique vase was deemed auctionable due to its rarity and value.  这个古董花瓶因其稀有性和价值被认为可以拍卖。
His collection of vintage cars is auctionable. 他的老爷车收藏可以拍卖。
Synonyms: salable, marketable, vendable
Antonyms: unsalable, unmarketable
