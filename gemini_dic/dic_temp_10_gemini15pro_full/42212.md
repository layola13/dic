councilwomen: /ˈkaʊnsəlˌwɪmɪn/
n.
女议员（复数）
A female member of a council, a group of people elected or appointed to make decisions or give advice.
The councilwomen voted unanimously to approve the new budget. 女议员们一致投票通过了新的预算。
The councilwomen debated the issue for hours. 女议员们就这个问题辩论了几个小时。
近义词：councilors, council members
反义词：(无直接反义词，可考虑非议员或反对者等)
词性：名词
