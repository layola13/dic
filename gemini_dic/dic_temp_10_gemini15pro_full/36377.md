coinheritance:/koʊɪnˈhɛrɪtəns/| n.|共同继承，共遗传|The act of inheriting something jointly with another or others.  The simultaneous inheritance of two or more characteristics.|例句：Coinheritance of these two genes is rare. 这两个基因的共遗传很少见。

例句：The study investigated the coinheritance of eye color and hair color. 这项研究调查了眼睛颜色和头发颜色的共同遗传。|近义词：joint inheritance, simultaneous inheritance|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
