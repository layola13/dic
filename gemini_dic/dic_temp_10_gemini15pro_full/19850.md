bindheimite:/ˈbɪndhaɪmaɪt/ | n. | 菱锌矿 | A hydrous antimonate of lead, Pb2Sb2O6(O,OH), occurring as a secondary mineral in lead deposits. It is an oxidation product of other antimony-bearing lead minerals. | Bindheimite is a common secondary mineral found in oxidized lead deposits. 菱锌矿是一种常见的次生矿物，发现于氧化的铅矿床中。 | Bindheimite is often associated with cerussite and galena. 菱锌矿通常与白铅矿和方铅矿伴生。| Synonyms: None specifically for this mineral, but similar minerals include stibiconite and valentinite. | Antonyms: N/A | noun
