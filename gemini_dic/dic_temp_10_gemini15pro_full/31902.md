cheesesteaks:/ˈtʃiːzsteɪks/| n.|芝士牛排;|A sandwich consisting of thinly sliced steak and melted cheese in a long roll.|例句：I had a delicious cheesesteak for lunch. 我午餐吃了一个美味的芝士牛排。

例句：Philadelphia is famous for its cheesesteaks. 费城以其芝士牛排而闻名。|近义词：steak sandwich, Philly cheesesteak|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "salad" as a contrasting food)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "cheese" + "steak".  Originated in Philadelphia, USA in the early 20th century.
