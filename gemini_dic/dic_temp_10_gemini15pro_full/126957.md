narrow-gaged:/ˈnæroʊ ɡeɪdʒd/
adj.
窄轨距的
Having a narrower gauge than standard (usually of a railroad).  A narrow gauge railroad has rails spaced closer together than the standard gauge of 4 feet 8 1/2 inches.
The narrow-gaged railway wound its way through the mountains. 这条窄轨铁路蜿蜒穿过群山。
近义词:narrow-gauge, small-gauge
反义词:broad-gauge, wide-gauge
词性:adjective
