darner:/ˈdɑːrnər/ | n. |修补者；缝补工具|A person or tool used for darning, especially socks or other knitted items.| My grandmother was a skilled darner, always mending our socks. 我的祖母是一位熟练的缝补者，总是修补我们的袜子。|  She used a darner to repair the hole in her sweater. 她用缝补工具修补了毛衣上的洞。| 近义词: mender, repairer | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑tearer撕裂者，但语境不同) | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Middle English *darner, derived from the verb "darn" (to mend by interweaving yarn or thread).  "Darn" itself comes from Old English *dernan, meaning to conceal or hide, possibly referring to the way the mend covers the hole.
