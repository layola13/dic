misaligns:/ˌmɪsəˈlaɪnz/
verb
使错位；排列不齐
to align incorrectly or improperly.
The technician misaligned the laser, causing the experiment to fail.  技术人员使激光器错位，导致实验失败。
The text was misaligned on the page, making it difficult to read.  页面上的文本排列不齐，难以阅读。
misplace, misregister
align, adjust
v.
