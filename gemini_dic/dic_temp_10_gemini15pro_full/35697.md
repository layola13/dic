cobweb:/ˈkɒbwɛb/ (UK) /ˈkɑːbwɛb/ (US)| n.|蜘蛛网，蛛丝；陈腐的东西，过时的事物|A cobweb is a net of sticky threads made by a spider to catch insects. It can also refer to something old, unused, and covered in dust, figuratively representing something outdated or neglected. |例句：The old house was filled with cobwebs.  这栋老房子里满是蜘蛛网。

例句：His ideas about economics are covered in cobwebs. 他关于经济学的思想已经过时了。|近义词：spiderweb, gossamer|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
