discountenance:/dɪsˈkaʊntənəns/
verb
使气馁；使沮丧；反对；不赞成
To discourage by cold treatment or show of disapproval.  To look upon with disfavor.
例句：Her father's stern look discountenanced her from telling him the truth. 她父亲严厉的目光使她不敢告诉他真相。

例句：The school discountenances smoking on school grounds. 学校不赞成在校园内吸烟。

近义词：deter, discourage, disapprove, oppose
反义词：encourage, support, approve
词性:v.
