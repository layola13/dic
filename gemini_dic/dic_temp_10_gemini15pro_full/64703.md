escargatoire:/eskɑːrɡɑːˈtwɑːr/
n.
snail farm; snailery; place where snails are raised for food
A place where edible snails are bred and fattened.
Snails are raised in an escargatoire for consumption.  蜗牛在蜗牛养殖场养殖以供食用。
The chef visited the escargatoire to select the finest snails for his dish.  厨师参观了蜗牛养殖场，为他的菜肴挑选最好的蜗牛。
Synonyms: snailery, snaileries
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps "farm" in a broader context)
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *escargotière*, from *escargot* (snail) + *-ière* (suffix forming nouns denoting places).  Ultimately from Old French *escargol*.
