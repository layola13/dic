countergovernment:/ˌkaʊntərˈɡʌvərmənt/| n. |反政府；影子内阁；对抗性政府|A group or organization that opposes the existing government and seeks to replace it, often through unconstitutional means. |The exiled group formed a countergovernment, hoping to regain power.  流亡团体组建了一个反政府组织，希望重新掌权。| The countergovernment broadcast propaganda from a clandestine radio station.  反政府组织从一个秘密电台播送宣传。|Synonyms: shadow government, opposition, rebel government|Antonyms: incumbent government, ruling party|noun
