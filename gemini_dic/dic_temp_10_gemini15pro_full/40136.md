contemporaneity:/kənˌtɛmpəˈreɪnɪti/ | n. |同时代性；同时期；当代性|The contemporaneity of the two artists' work is striking. 这两位艺术家的作品的时代性令人震惊。|例句：The exhibition explores the contemporaneity of these two seemingly disparate artistic movements.  这个展览探索了这两个看似迥异的艺术运动的时代性。

例句：The contemporaneity of their lives allowed them to share experiences unique to their generation.  他们生活在同一时代，这使得他们能够分享他们这一代人特有的经历。|近义词：simultaneity, synchronicity, coincidence |反义词： anachronism, non-contemporaneity |词性:名词

contemporary:/kənˈtɛmpərəri/ | adj. |当代的；同时代的 | Contemporary art is often challenging. 当代艺术通常具有挑战性。|例句：He is a contemporary artist, known for his innovative use of mixed media. 他是一位当代艺术家，以其对混合媒介的创新运用而闻名。

例句：She prefers contemporary furniture to antique pieces. 她喜欢当代家具，而不是古董。|近义词：modern, current, present-day |反义词：ancient, old, antique |词性:形容词
