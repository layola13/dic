metagenome:/ˈmɛtəˌdʒiːnoʊm/| n. |宏基因组; 元基因组|The collective genomes of the microorganisms that reside in an environmental niche.  The metagenome encompasses the entire genetic material recovered from a sample, including DNA from bacteria, archaea, viruses, and eukaryotic organisms. |Researchers are studying the gut metagenome to understand its role in human health. 研究人员正在研究肠道宏基因组以了解其在人类健康中的作用。|近义词：environmental DNA, microbial community genome|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, potentially "individual genome")|词性:noun
