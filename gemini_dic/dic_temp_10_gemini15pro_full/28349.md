carapax:/ˈkærəpæks/| n. |壳，背甲|The turtle retreated into its carapace.  乌龟缩回了它的壳里。|A crab has a hard carapace.  螃蟹有一个坚硬的背甲。|近义词：shell, covering|反义词:plastron (特指腹甲)|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *carapax*, perhaps from *carere* "to lack" + *pax* "peace," thus meaning "something lacking peace" (because easily attacked?).  More likely related to *corium* “leather, hide.”
