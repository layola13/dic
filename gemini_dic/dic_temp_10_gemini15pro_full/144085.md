胜过航行，在航行中超越

例句：Despite their best efforts, they were outsailled by the more experienced crew. 尽管他们尽了最大努力，他们还是被更有经验的船员超越了。
近义词：outmaneuver, surpass, beat
反义词：lag behind, fall behind
