beaconage:/ˈbiːkənɪdʒ/
n.
灯塔维护费，灯塔使用费
A toll or tax levied for the maintenance or use of a beacon.
The ships paid beaconage for the safety provided by the lighthouse.  这些船只支付了灯塔提供的安全保障的灯塔使用费。
近义词：toll, fee, charge
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "gratuity" in some contexts)
