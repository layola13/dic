drawings:/ˈdrɔːɪŋz/
n.
图画，素描，绘图
A picture or diagram made with a pencil, pen, or crayon rather than paint.
The architect showed us the drawings for the new building.  建筑师给我们看了新大楼的设计图。
sketches, illustrations, diagrams
paintings, photographs
