gymnogen:/ˈdʒɪmnəˌdʒɛn/| n. |裸子植物|A plant whose seeds are not enclosed within an ovary.  Gymnosperms include conifers, cycads, and ginkgoes.|The pine tree is a common gymnosperm found in many forests.|近义词:nonflowering plant, seed plant|反义词:angiosperm|词性:noun
