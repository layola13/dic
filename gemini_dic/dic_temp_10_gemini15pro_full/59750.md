evolutionary: /ˌiːvəˈluːʃəneri/ | adj. | 进化论的，进化的；演化的，发展的 | relating to or denoting the process by which different kinds of living organism are believed to have developed from earlier forms during the history of the earth | The museum has an impressive display of evolutionary biology.  这家博物馆有一个令人印象深刻的进化生物学展览。|  The evolutionary process can take millions of years. 进化过程可能需要数百万年。| synonyms: developmental, transformative, phylogenetic | antonyms: static, unchanging | adjective
