moonshiner:/ˈmuːnˌʃaɪnər/ | n. | 私酒贩子，非法酿酒者 | A person who makes and sells illegal whiskey, especially in the countryside. |  例句：The moonshiner hid his still deep in the woods.  这个私酒贩子把他的酿酒器藏在森林深处。

例句：During Prohibition, moonshiners were common in the Appalachian Mountains.  在禁酒令期间，阿巴拉契亚山脉有很多私酒贩子。| 近义词：bootlegger | 反义词: law enforcement officer | 词性: noun
