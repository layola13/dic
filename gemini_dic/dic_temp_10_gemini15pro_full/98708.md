insuppressible:/ˌɪnsəˈpresəbl/| adj.|抑制不住的，无法克制的|Not capable of being suppressed; irrepressible.|Her enthusiasm was insuppressible. 她的热情抑制不住。|近义词: irrepressible, uncontrollable, unquenchable|反义词: suppressible, controllable, restrainable|词性:形容词
词源:  From in- (not) + suppressible (capable of being suppressed), from suppress + -ible. Ultimately from Latin supprimere (to press down).
