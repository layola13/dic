affiliating:/əˈfɪlieɪtɪŋ/
verb|附属，加入，隶属|The process of officially joining or connecting with a larger organization or group.|The local club is affiliating with a national organization.  这个地方俱乐部正在加入一个全国性组织。|joining, associating, connecting|disassociating, separating|v.

affiliate:/əˈfɪlieɪt/
noun|分支机构，附属机构|An organization that is officially connected with or controlled by another, larger organization.|The company has affiliates in several countries.  这家公司在几个国家有分支机构。|branch, subsidiary, associate|independent, separate|n.

affiliate:/əˈfɪlieɪt/
verb|使隶属，使加入|To officially attach or connect (a subsidiary group or a person) to an organization.|The school is affiliated with the university.  这所学校隶属于这所大学。|join, associate, connect|disassociate, separate|v.
