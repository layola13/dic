doucet:/ˈduːset/ (US), /ˈduːsɪt/ (UK)| adj.| 甜的；芬芳的；悦耳的| Of a sweet and pleasant nature, often used to describe aromas, sounds, or personalities.  Derived from Old French "douz" meaning sweet.| The doucet aroma of honeysuckle filled the air. 空气中弥漫着金银花的芬芳。|Synonyms: sweet, fragrant, melodious, pleasant, agreeable|Antonyms: sour, acrid, unpleasant, disagreeable|adjective
