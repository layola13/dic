mather:/ˈmæðər/| n.|脓, 腐肉|A purulent or other morbid discharge from a wound or sore.|The wound oozed a foul-smelling mather.|近义词:pus, discharge, suppuration|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑“healthy tissue” 健康组织)|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *materen, *materiȝen, from Old French materier (“to fester, suppurate”), from Vulgar Latin *materiāre, from Latin materia (“matter, material”).
