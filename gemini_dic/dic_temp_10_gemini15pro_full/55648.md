ditherers:/ˈdɪðərərz/
n.
犹豫不决的人；优柔寡断的人
People who are unable to make decisions quickly; indecisive people.
The ditherers couldn't decide which movie to watch. 犹豫不决的人无法决定要看哪部电影。
He was known as a ditherer, always changing his mind. 他优柔寡断，总是改变主意。
近义词: waverers, hesitators, procrastinators
反义词: decisive people, determined people
词性: 名词
