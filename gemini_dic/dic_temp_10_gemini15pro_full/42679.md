countersues:/ˌkaʊntərˈsuːz/
verb
反诉，提起反诉
to sue someone who has already sued you
例句：The company countersued, claiming breach of contract.  该公司提起反诉，声称对方违反合同。

例句：After being sued for defamation, the celebrity countersued for malicious prosecution. 在被控诽谤后，这位名人反诉恶意起诉。

近义词：files a countersuit
反义词：drops a suit, settles out of court
词性 v.
