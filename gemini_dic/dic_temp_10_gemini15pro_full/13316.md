The word "athlin" isn't a standard English word.  It's likely a misspelling of "athlete."  Therefore, the following analysis is for "athlete":

athlete:/ˈæθliːt/| n.|运动员|A person who is proficient in sports and other forms of physical exercise.|Example: The athlete trained hard for the Olympics.  这位运动员为奥运会刻苦训练。|Example:  She is a natural athlete and excels at all sports. 她是一位天生的运动员，擅长所有运动。|近义词：sportsman, sportswoman|反义词:couch potato, non-athlete|词性:noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *athlētēs, from Greek athlētēs “contestant in games,” from athlein “to contend for a prize,” from athlon “prize,” from athlos “contest,” of uncertain origin.
