awnless:/ˈɔːnlɪs/| adj.|无芒的；无颖的|Lacking awns, which are bristle-like appendages extending from the glumes or lemmas of grasses or similar plants.|The farmer chose an awnless variety of wheat because it was easier to harvest.|Near synonyms: smooth, beardless|Near antonyms: awned, bearded|adjective
