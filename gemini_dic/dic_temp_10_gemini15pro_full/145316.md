overbroad:/ˈəʊvəbrɔːd/ (UK) /ˌoʊvərˈbrɔːd/ (US)| adj.| 过于宽泛的，过于广泛的|Too broad or inclusive; not sufficiently specific or limited.|The law was criticized for being overbroad and infringing on free speech. 这项法律因过于宽泛而受到批评，因为它侵犯了言论自由。|近义词：too wide, excessively broad, sweeping, all-encompassing|反义词:narrow, specific, limited|词性:形容词

overbroad:|词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much") + "broad" (adjective meaning "wide in extent")|来源于古英语"brād", 意思是"宽的"| 构成方式简单直接，通过前缀"over-" 增强了"broad"的含义，表示"过于宽泛"。
