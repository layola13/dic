biomechanist: /ˌbaɪəʊˈmɛkənɪst/ | n. | 生物力学家 | A scientist who studies the mechanics of living organisms, especially the forces exerted by muscles and gravity on the skeletal structure. |  Biomechanists often work with athletes to improve their performance. 生物力学家经常与运动员合作以提高他们的成绩。| synonyms: biokinesiologist, kinesiologist | antonyms: N/A | noun
