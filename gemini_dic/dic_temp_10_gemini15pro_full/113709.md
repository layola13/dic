macro-evolution:/ˌmækroʊˌɛvəˈluːʃən/ | n. |宏观进化| Major evolutionary change at or above the level of species over geological time, resulting in the formation of new taxonomic groups. | The fossil record provides evidence for macro-evolution, showing the development of new lineages and the extinction of others.  化石记录为宏观进化提供了证据，显示了新谱系的出现和其他谱系的灭绝。| Example: The macro-evolution of whales from land mammals is a well-documented example of evolutionary change. 鲸鱼从陆地哺乳动物的宏观进化是一个有充分记录的进化变化的例子。|Synonym:  large-scale evolution, transpecific evolution | Antonym: micro-evolution | Noun
