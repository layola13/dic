filchers:/ˈfɪltʃərz/
noun
小偷，顺手牵羊的人
People who steal things, especially small, insignificant items.
The filchers were caught stealing candy from the store. 那些小偷在商店里偷糖果时被抓住了。
近义词: thieves, pilferers, shoplifters
反义词: donors, philanthropists
词性: noun (plural)

filcher:/ˈfɪltʃər/
noun
小偷，顺手牵羊的人
A person who steals things, especially small, insignificant items.
The filcher was caught red-handed stealing a pen.  那个小偷在偷笔时被当场抓获。
近义词: thief, pilferer, shoplifter
反义词: donor, philanthropist
词性: noun (singular)


词源:
From the verb "filch," which comes from Middle English "filchen," of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old English "fylcian" (to roll up).
