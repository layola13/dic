nubble:/ˈnʌbəl/| n. |小块；小瘤；小突起；小丘|A small lump, knob, or protuberance, especially on a rock or hill.|The coastline is dotted with nubbles of rock. 海岸线上点缀着岩石小块。|Synonyms: knob, lump, bump, protuberance, hillock|Antonyms: hollow, depression|noun

nubble:/ˈnʌbəl/| n. |小块；小瘤；小突起；小丘|A small piece of something.|He broke off a nubble of cheese. 他掰下一小块奶酪。|Synonyms: bit, piece, morsel, fragment|Antonyms: whole|noun
