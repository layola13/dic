deeprootedness:/ˌdiːpˈruːtɪdnəs/ | n. | 根深蒂固，牢固 |The deeprootedness of the tradition makes it difficult to change.  这个传统的根深蒂固使其难以改变。|  deep-rootedness is the quality of being firmly established. Deeprootedness implies that something is deeply ingrained and difficult to remove or alter. |  His deeprootedness in the community made him a respected figure. 他在社区的根深蒂固使他成为受人尊敬的人物。 |  entrenchment, ingrainedness, fixture | superficiality, transience | noun
