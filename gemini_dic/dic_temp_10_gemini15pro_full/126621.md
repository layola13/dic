nailbiting's:/ˈneɪlˌbaɪtɪŋz/
noun
紧张的时刻；令人焦急的时刻
The moments of suspense or anxiety.
The final minutes of the game were nailbiting's.  比赛的最后几分钟令人十分紧张。
近义词: tension, suspense, anxiety, apprehension
反义词: calm, relaxing, peaceful
词性: 名词 (n.)

nailbiting:/ˈneɪlˌbaɪtɪŋ/
adjective
令人焦急的；紧张的
Causing suspense or anxiety.
It was a nailbiting finish to the race. 这是一场扣人心弦的比赛。
近义词: tense, suspenseful, gripping, thrilling
反义词: calming, relaxing, boring
词性: 形容词 (adj.)

词源:  nail (指甲) + biting (咬)  -> nailbiting (咬指甲的) -> nailbiting (令人紧张的)  最初指人们在紧张时会咬指甲的行为，后来引申为形容紧张的情形。
