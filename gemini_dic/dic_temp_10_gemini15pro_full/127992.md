nephalist: /ˈnɛfəlɪst/ | n. | 酗酒者；纵饮者 | A person who habitually drinks intoxicating beverages to excess; a drunkard. | He was a notorious nephalist, often seen stumbling out of the local tavern. 他是一个臭名昭著的酒鬼，经常被人看到从当地的小酒馆里踉跄而出。 | 近义词: drunkard, alcoholic, inebriate, sot, dipsomaniac | 反义词: teetotaler, abstainer | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From Greek *νηφάλιος (nēphalios, “sober, temperate, abstinent”) + -ist.
