gigantical: /dʒaɪˈɡæntɪkəl/
adjective
巨大的，庞大的
extremely large; gigantic
例句：They unveiled a gigantical statue in the town square. 他们在市镇广场上揭幕了一座巨大的雕像。
例句：The company suffered a gigantical loss in the last quarter.  该公司在上个季度遭受了巨大的损失。
近义词：enormous, huge, immense, colossal, mammoth, gigantic
反义词：tiny, small, minuscule, diminutive
词性：adjective
词源：From gigantic + -al.  Essentially a redundant form of gigantic.
