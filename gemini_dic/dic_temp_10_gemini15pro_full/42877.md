courtliest:/ˈkɔːrtliɪst/ | adj. | 最有礼貌的，最殷勤的 |  Superlative form of 'courtly':  most polite and refined in manners, especially in a way that is characteristic of a royal court. | He gave her the courtliest bow he could manage. 他向她行了所能做到的最优雅的鞠躬。| 近义词：most polite, most gracious, most courteous, most refined | 反义词：rudest, most impolite, most discourteous, most vulgar | 词性: 形容词
