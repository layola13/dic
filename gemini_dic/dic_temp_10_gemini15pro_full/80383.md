goliardery:/ˈɡoʊliɑːrdəri/
n.
放荡不羁的行为；流浪学生的狂欢作乐；滑稽诗文
boisterous revelry; the carefree, often ribald, lifestyle of a wandering student or scholar, especially in the Middle Ages; poetry or songs typical of goliards.
The students engaged in a night of goliardery, singing bawdy songs and drinking copious amounts of ale. 学生们纵情狂欢了一夜，唱着下流的歌曲，喝着大量的啤酒。
Synonyms: revelry, carousing, debauchery, merrymaking, roistering
Antonyms: sobriety, restraint, decency, decorum
noun
