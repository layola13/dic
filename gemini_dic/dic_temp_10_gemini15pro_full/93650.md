imbrication:/ˌɪmbrɪˈkeɪʃən/| n.|叠瓦状，覆瓦状，交叠|The imbrication of the roof tiles protects the house from rain. 屋顶瓦片的叠瓦状排列保护房屋免受雨淋。|例句：The geologist studied the imbrication of the rock layers. 地质学家研究了岩层的叠瓦状结构。

例句：The feathers on the bird's wings showed a complex imbrication. 鸟翅膀上的羽毛呈现出复杂的叠瓦状排列。|近义词：overlapping, layering, shingle, tiling|反义词:separation, detachment|词性:noun

词源: From Late Latin *imbricātiō, from imbricātus, past participle of imbricāre (“to cover with tiles”), from imbrex (“curved tile”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂embh- (“to curve, bend”).
