gambol:/ˈɡæmbəl/ (US), /ˈɡæmbɒl/ (UK)| vi. n.|雀跃；嬉戏；跳跃|To run or jump about playfully.|The lambs gamboled in the spring meadow. 羊羔在春天的草地上嬉戏。|The children gamboled on the beach. 孩子们在沙滩上玩耍嬉戏。|近义词：frolic, frisk, caper, skip, romp, play|反义词：trudge, plod|verb/noun

Etymology:
From Old French *gamboler "to leap, dance," perhaps of Germanic origin, related to Old Norse gambra "to quiver."
