what /wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US) |pron. det. adv. int.|什么，多么| Used to ask for information about something.| What is your name? 你叫什么名字？| which, how, who |(No single antonym)| pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think about this book? 你觉得这本书怎么样？
What does she usually do on weekends? 她周末通常做什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? 你在看什么？
What is he doing right now? 他现在在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned so far? 你到目前为止学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? 她用这笔钱做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? 你说了什么？
What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么事？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? 我看到她时她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I came? 我来之前你做了什么？
What had they decided before the meeting? 会议之前他们决定了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the phone rang? 电话响之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? 她换专业之前一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? 你下周末要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? 他们在下次会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by next month? 到下个月你将完成什么？
What will they have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底他们将取得什么成就？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学习了什么专业多久了？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
If I were you, what would I do? 如果我是你，我会做什么？


被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？


疑问句:
What is the capital of France? 法国的首都是哪里？
What are your plans for the summer? 你暑假的计划是什么？


否定句:
I don't know what to do. 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what I'm saying. 她不明白我在说什么。



PHRASE->
What's up?  怎么了？/你好吗？
What about going to the movies? 去看电影怎么样？
What if it rains? 如果下雨怎么办？
What for? 为了什么？
So what? 那又怎样？
