dactylology:/ˌdæktɪˈlɒlədʒi/ | n. | 手语；指语 | The communication of words by finger spelling, used by and with people who are deaf or hard of hearing. |  She learned dactylology to communicate with her deaf friend. 她学习了手语以便与她聋哑的朋友交流。 | Synonym: finger spelling, sign language | Antonym: speech, verbal communication | noun

dactylology:/ˌdæktɪˈlɒlədʒi/ | n. | 手语；指语 |  A system of representing letters and numbers by finger positions, used by and with people who cannot hear or speak. | He used dactylology to spell out his name. 他用手语拼出了自己的名字。 | Synonym: finger alphabet | Antonym: spoken language | noun
