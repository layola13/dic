collaborators:/kəˈlæbəreɪtərz/| n. |合作者， collaborators|A person who works jointly on an activity or project with others; an associate.|例句：The scientists were collaborators on a groundbreaking research project.  这些科学家在一个开创性的研究项目上合作。

例句：She thanked all her collaborators for their hard work on the book. 她感谢所有合作者在这本书上的辛勤工作。|近义词：partners, associates, colleagues, allies, confederates|反义词：opponents, adversaries, rivals|词性:名词

collaborator:/kəˈlæbəreɪtər/| n. |合作者，通敌者|One who works with another; especially : one who assists an enemy force occupying one's country.|例句：He was accused of being a collaborator during the war.  他被指控在战争期间通敌。

例句：The government imprisoned several collaborators after the liberation. 解放后，政府囚禁了几名通敌者。|近义词：partners, associates, colleagues, allies, confederates|反义词：opponents, adversaries, rivals, patriots|词性:名词
