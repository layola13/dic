hammerers:/ˈhæmərərz/| n.|锤击者，锻工|People who use hammers, especially blacksmiths or metalworkers.  Those who hammer something repeatedly. |例句：The hammerers shaped the glowing metal into intricate designs.  锻工们将炽热的金属塑造成复杂的图案。

例句：The relentless hammerers finally broke through the stone wall.  不懈的锤击者终于凿穿了石墙。|近义词：blacksmiths, metalworkers, pounders|反义词:|noun
