doormen: /ˈdɔːrmən/ (US), /ˈdɔːmən/ (UK)| n. |门卫，门房|A person employed to open doors, assist visitors, and provide security at the entrance of a building, especially a hotel or apartment building.| The doorman hailed a cab for us. 门卫为我们叫了一辆出租车。| The doormen helped carry our luggage. 门卫帮我们搬运行李。| synonyms: porter, attendant, concierge | antonyms: resident, tenant | noun
