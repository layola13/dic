non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
suppress (verb): from Late Latin suppress-, past participle stem of supprimere "press down, repress," from Latin sub "down, under" + premere "to press"
-ive (suffix): forming adjectives meaning "tending to; having the nature or quality of"
-ness (suffix): forming nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality
