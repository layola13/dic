hotkeys:/ˈhɑːtkiːz/ (US) /ˈhɒtkiːz/ (UK)| n. |快捷键|A combination of keys that, when pressed, performs a specific command or action within a computer program or operating system.|Example:  I use hotkeys to copy and paste text quickly. 我使用快捷键来快速复制和粘贴文本。
Example:  Setting up custom hotkeys can improve your workflow. 设置自定义快捷键可以改进你的工作流程。|近义词：shortcut keys, keyboard shortcuts|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
