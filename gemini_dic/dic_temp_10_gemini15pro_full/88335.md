hippocratism:/hɪpəˈkrætɪzəm/ | n. | 俱状指（趾）;杵状指;希波克拉底面容 | Clubbing of the fingers and toes, often a sign of certain diseases, especially of the heart and lungs; also, the sunken, pinched facial appearance associated with impending death. | The doctor noted the patient's hippocratism as a potential indicator of lung disease. 医生注意到病人的杵状指可能是肺部疾病的征兆。 | Example: The patient exhibited hippocratism, suggesting a chronic respiratory condition. 病人表现出杵状指，提示患有慢性呼吸系统疾病。| Synonym: clubbing | Antonym: (No direct antonym, but absence of clubbing indicates healthy condition) | Noun
