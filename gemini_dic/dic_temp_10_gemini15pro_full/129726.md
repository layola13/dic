non-endemic:/ˌnɒn-enˈdemɪk/ (US) /ˌnɑːn-enˈdemɪk/ (UK)| adj.|非本地性的，非地方性的|Not native or restricted to a certain region or population; occurring in a wider geographic area than usual.|The virus, once non-endemic, has now become a regular threat in the region.  这种病毒曾经不是地方性的，现在已经成为该地区的常规威胁。|The non-endemic species was introduced to the island accidentally.  这个非本地物种是偶然被引入岛上的。|Synonyms: foreign, introduced, exotic, non-native, alien|Antonyms: endemic, native, indigenous|Adjective
