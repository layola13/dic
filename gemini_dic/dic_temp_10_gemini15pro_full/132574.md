nondieters:/ˌnɒnˈdaɪətəz/ |n.| 非节食者；不控制饮食的人 | People who do not restrict their food intake for the purpose of weight loss or other health reasons. |  Many nondieters maintain a healthy weight through intuitive eating. 许多非节食者通过直觉饮食保持健康的体重。| Nondieters often have a more relaxed relationship with food. 非节食者通常与食物的关系更轻松。 | synonyms: normal eaters, unrestricted eaters | antonyms: dieters, weight watchers | noun
