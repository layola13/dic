misbelieve: /ˌmɪsbɪˈliːv/
Verb
中文翻译：不信；怀疑；误信
英文解析：To not believe; to have no faith in; to doubt the truth or accuracy of something.  Can also mean to believe wrongly or mistakenly.
例句：He misbelieved the rumors and spread them further. 他误信了谣言，并且还进一步散播。

例句：She misbelieved his promises and was ultimately disappointed. 她不相信他的承诺，最终失望了。

近义词：disbelieve, doubt, distrust
反义词：believe, trust, have faith in
词性：v.
