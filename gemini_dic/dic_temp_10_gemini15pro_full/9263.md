antipatriotisms: /ˌæntiˌpeɪtriətɪzəmz/
|n.|反爱国主义（复数）|The acts or sentiments of being opposed to one's country; opposition to patriotism.  The plural form highlights multiple instances or expressions of this sentiment.|
例句：The antipatriotisms expressed by some citizens during wartime shocked the nation.  一些公民在战时表达的反爱国主义情绪震惊了全国。

例句：Historians have documented various antipatriotisms throughout history, often linked to specific political contexts. 历史学家记录了历史上各种各样的反爱国主义，通常与特定的政治背景有关。
|近义词：treachery, sedition, disloyalty|
|反义词：patriotism, loyalty, nationalism|
|词性：名词|


Etymology:
anti- (Greek) "against, opposite" + patriotism (from patriot + -ism)
