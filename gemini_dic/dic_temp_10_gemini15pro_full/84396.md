hallmarked:/ˈhɔːlmɑːkt/| adj.|带有标记的；品质保证的；典型的；象征性的|Officially marked as meeting a certain standard of quality, or showing a typical feature or characteristic.|例句：The painting is hallmarked by the artist's distinctive brushstrokes. 这幅画带有艺术家独特的笔触特征。

例句：This antique silver is hallmarked, guaranteeing its authenticity.  这件古董银器带有标记，保证其真品。|近义词：certified, guaranteed, typical, characteristic, symbolic|反义词：uncertified, unguaranteed, atypical|词性:形容词

Etymology: From "hallmark," a mark stamped on articles of precious metal as a guarantee of quality, originally made at Goldsmiths' Hall in London.  From "hall" + "mark."
