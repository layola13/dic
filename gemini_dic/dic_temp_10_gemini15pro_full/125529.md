multisonic:/ˌmʌltiˈsɒnɪk/ | /ˌmʌltiˈsɑːnɪk/ | adj. | 多音速的，超音速的 | Relating to or involving speeds several times the speed of sound. |  A multisonic aircraft could travel from New York to London in under an hour.  多音速飞机可以在一小时内从纽约飞到伦敦。| supersonic, hypersonic | subsonic | adjective

Etymology:
"multi-" (Latin, "many") + "sonic" (from Latin "sonus," meaning "sound")
