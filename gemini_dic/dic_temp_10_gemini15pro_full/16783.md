basalt:/ˈbæsɔːlt/ |n.|玄武岩|A dark, fine-grained volcanic rock that sometimes displays a columnar structure.|The Hawaiian Islands are largely composed of basalt. 夏威夷群岛主要由玄武岩构成。| synonym: igneous rock, trap rock | antonym: limestone, sandstone | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *basalte*, from Late Latin *basaltes*, perhaps a misreading of Latin *basanites* "touchstone," from Greek *basanitēs (lithos)* "(stone) used for testing," from *basanos* "touchstone."  Ultimately from Egyptian *bʒn* "black stone."
