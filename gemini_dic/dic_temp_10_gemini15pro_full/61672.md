emmarble:/ɪmˈmɑːrbl/ /ɛmˈmɑːrbl/ | vt. | 使大理石化；使坚硬如大理石；使冷酷无情 | To make something resemble marble in appearance or hardness, or to make someone become cold and unfeeling. |  The sculptor emmarbled the clay, giving it a smooth, hard finish. 雕塑家使粘土大理石化，赋予它光滑、坚硬的表面。

| The tragic events of his childhood seemed to have emmarbled his heart. 他童年悲惨的经历似乎使他的心变得冷酷无情。 | 近义词: petrify, harden | 反义词: soften, melt | Verb
