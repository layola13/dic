all-roundness:/ˌɔːlˈraʊndnəs/ | n. | 全面性；多才多艺；全能 |The state or quality of being all-round; versatility; competence in many areas. |  He was admired for his all-roundness in sports. 他因在体育方面的全面性而受到钦佩。

all-roundness:/ˌɔːlˈraʊndnəs/ | n. | 全能;多面性 | The quality of having many different skills and abilities. | She demonstrated her all-roundness by excelling in academics, sports, and music. 她通过在学术、体育和音乐方面的出色表现展现了她的多面性。

all-roundness:/ˌɔːlˈraʊndnəs/ | n. |  全面发展 | The state of being well-developed in all aspects. |  The school aims to promote the all-roundness of its students. 学校旨在促进学生的全面发展。


近义词: versatility, adaptability, flexibility, competence, proficiency
反义词:  incompetence, inadequacy, deficiency, limitation


词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From all-round + -ness.  "All-round" meaning having abilities in many different areas, dates back to the mid-19th century.  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state, quality, or condition.
