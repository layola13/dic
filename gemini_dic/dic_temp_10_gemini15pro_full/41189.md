copyrighters:/ˈkɑːpiˌraɪtərz/
n.
版权所有人；版权撰稿人
A person who writes copy, especially for advertisements or publicity material.
The company employs several copyrighters to create compelling ad campaigns.  这家公司雇佣了几位版权撰稿人来创作引人注目的广告活动。
synonyms: copywriter, writer, author, journalist, wordsmith
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider plagiarist as a contrasting term)
词性: 名词
