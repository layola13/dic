ardently:/ˈɑːrdəntli/
adverb
热烈地；热情地
With great passion and enthusiasm.
She ardently defended her beliefs. 她热烈地捍卫自己的信仰。

He ardently pursued his dream of becoming a musician. 他热烈地追求成为音乐家的梦想。

近义词:passionately, fervently, enthusiastically, eagerly
反义词:apathetically, indifferently, coldly
词性:adverb

词源:
From Late Latin *ardent- (stem of ardens), present participle of ardēre "to burn," from PIE root *ās- "to burn, glow."
