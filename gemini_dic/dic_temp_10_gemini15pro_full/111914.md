loiterers:/ˈlɔɪtərərz/| n. |闲逛者，游荡者|People who linger idly or aimlessly in a public place. |The police dispersed the loiterers who were blocking the entrance to the store. 警察驱散了堵在商店入口的闲逛者。|例句：Groups of loiterers were hanging around the street corner.  一群群闲人在街角闲逛。|近义词：idlers, loafers, dawdlers|反义词:hustlers, workers|词性:noun

词源: From the verb "loiter" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Loiter" comes from Middle Dutch *loteren "to shake, dangle," of uncertain origin.
