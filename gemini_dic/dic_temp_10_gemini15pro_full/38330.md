conceitednesses:/kənˈsiːtɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
自负，骄傲自大
The state of being excessively proud and self-centered.
His conceitednesses made him unpopular. 他的自负让他不受欢迎。
conceit, arrogance, vanity, egotism
humility, modesty
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From conceitedness + -es (plural suffix). Conceitedness from conceited + -ness. Conceited from conceit (n.) + -ed. Conceit from Old French conceite "concept, idea, thought; opinion," from Latin conceptus "a conceiving, thing conceived," past participle of concipere "to take in, conceive," from com- "together" + capere "to take."
