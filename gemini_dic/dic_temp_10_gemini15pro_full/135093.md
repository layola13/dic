nonobservable:/ˌnɒnəbˈzɜːvəbl/ (US: /ˌnɑːnəbˈzɜːrvəbl/)| adj.|不可观察的，无法观测的|Not able to be observed or noticed.|The effects of the new policy were nonobservable in the short term.  新政策的影响在短期内是无法观测到的.|Synonyms: imperceptible, undetectable, invisible, unnoticeable|Antonyms: observable, perceptible, detectable, visible, noticeable|adjective
nonobservable:/ˌnɒnəbˈzɜːvəbl/ (US: /ˌnɑːnəbˈzɜːrvəbl/)| n.|不可观察的事物|Something that cannot be observed or noticed.|Dark matter is considered a nonobservable in astrophysics. 暗物质在天体物理学中被认为是不可观察的事物.|Synonyms: imponderable, unknown|Antonyms: observable, phenomenon|noun
