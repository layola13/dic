out-ask:/ˌaʊtˈæsk/ | v. | 比…要求更多；在出价上超过 | To ask for a higher price or make a higher bid than someone else. |  He out-asked all the other bidders at the auction. 他在拍卖会上出价超过了所有其他竞标者。 |  The seller out-asked the buyer's initial offer. 卖家对买家的初始报价提出了更高的要求。 | outbid, surpass | underbid | verb

词源: out- (prefix meaning "exceeding; surpassing") + ask (verb)
