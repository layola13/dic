dict:/dɪkt/ | n. | 字典；词典 | A book or electronic resource that lists the words of a language (typically alphabetically) and gives their meanings, or gives the equivalent words in a different language, often also providing information about pronunciation, origin, and usage. | I looked up the word in the dictionary. 我在字典里查了这个词。 | synonyms: lexicon, glossary, vocabulary | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical object like a dictionary) | noun

dict:/dɪkt/ | v. | 口述；命令 | To say or read aloud (something) so that it can be written down.  (Often used with "to") | He dictated a letter to his secretary. 他向秘书口述了一封信。 | synonyms: pronounce, recite, deliver, command | antonyms: transcribe, listen, obey | verb
