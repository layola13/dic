nondebilitative:/ˌnɒndɪˈbɪlɪteɪtɪv/
adjective
非衰弱性的；不使人虚弱的
Not causing debility or weakness; not debilitating.
The treatment was nondebilitative, allowing him to continue working throughout. 这种治疗方法不会使人虚弱，让他能够在整个过程中继续工作。
Near Synonyms:  non-weakening, invigorating, strengthening, energizing
Antonyms: debilitating, weakening, enervating
词性: adjective
