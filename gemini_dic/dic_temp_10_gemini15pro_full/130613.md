nonadhesively:/ˌnɒnədˈhiːsɪvli/ /ˌnɑːnədˈhiːsɪvli/
adverb
非粘合地；不粘地
In a manner that does not adhere or stick.
The bandage was applied nonadhesively to the wound.  绷带非粘合地敷在伤口上。
The protective film peels off nonadhesively. 保护膜可以非粘合地剥离。
近义词:loosely, detachably
反义词:adhesively, stickily
词性:adverb

Etymology:
non- (prefix): not
ad- (prefix): to, toward
haes- (root): to stick
-ive (suffix): tending to; of the nature of
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs
