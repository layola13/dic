calyptra:/kəˈlɪptrə/
noun
帽状体；根冠；盖膜
A covering, hood, or lid-like structure, especially in plants or fungi, such as the root cap or the covering of a moss capsule.
The calyptra protects the developing sporophyte. 根冠保护正在发育的孢子体。
The botanist carefully removed the calyptra to examine the spores.  植物学家小心地取下盖膜来检查孢子。
Synonyms: cap, hood, covering, lid
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, perhaps uncovered, exposed)
