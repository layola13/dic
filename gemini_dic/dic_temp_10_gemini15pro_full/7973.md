anthocarpous:/ænθəˈkɑːrpəs/
adjective
具花果的，假果的
Having a fruit-like body which includes the persistent perianth and receptacle together with the true fruit.
The fig is an example of an anthocarpous fruit, where the fleshy part we eat is derived from the receptacle, not the ovary.
近义词:  accessory, pseudocarpous
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider 'true fruit' or 'eucarpous' as conceptually opposite)
词性: adjective
