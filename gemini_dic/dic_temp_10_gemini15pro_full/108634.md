leads:/liːdz/ | n. |线索，领先，导线|  A piece of information that helps you find something or someone you are looking for; the position of being in front of others in a competition or race; electrical wires. | The detective followed several leads in the case. 侦探追查了此案的几条线索。| synonyms: clues, tips, hints, advantages, wires, cables | antonyms: disadvantages, hindrances, dead ends | noun

leads:/liːdz/ | v. | 领导，引导，导致| To show someone the way, often by going in front of them; to be in charge of a group of people, a country, or a situation; to cause something to happen or exist. |  The guide leads the tourists through the museum. 导游带领游客穿过博物馆。| synonyms: guide, direct, escort, command, control, cause, result in | antonyms: follow, mislead, obstruct, prevent | verb
