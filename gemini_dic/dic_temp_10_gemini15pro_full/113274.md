lutation: /luːˈteɪʃən/
noun
洗涤，清洗 (尤指身体部位)
The act of washing or cleansing, especially of a part of the body.
Ritual lutation before prayer is customary in some religions. 在一些宗教中，祈祷前的仪式性洗涤是一种习惯。
The nurse performed a careful lutation of the wound. 护士仔细清洗了伤口。
近义词: washing, cleansing, ablution
反义词: soiling
