maskable:/ˈmɑːskəbl/ | adj. | 可屏蔽的，可掩蔽的 | Able to be masked or hidden; capable of being concealed or covered. | Example 1: The interrupt is maskable, meaning it can be disabled.  这个中断是可屏蔽的，意味着它可以被禁用。

Example 2:  Certain data fields are maskable for security reasons.  由于安全原因，某些数据字段是可屏蔽的。| Synonyms: concealable, hidable, coverable | Antonyms: unmaskable, unconcealable, visible | Word Type: Adjective
