leanness:/ˈliːnnəs/| n. | 瘦；贫乏；精干 |The state of being lean; thinness, lack of fat. Also, a lack of richness or abundance.|The leanness of the meat made it a healthy choice.  这块肉很瘦，因此是健康的选择。|Synonyms: thinness, slenderness, meagreness, spareness |Antonyms: fatness, obesity, richness, abundance|noun
