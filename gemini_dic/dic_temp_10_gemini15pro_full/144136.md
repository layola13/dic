outseeking:/ˌaʊtˈsiːkɪŋ/
adjective
外求的；向外寻求的
Actively searching for something outside of oneself or one's usual environment.
He has an outseeking personality, always looking for new experiences and adventures. 他性格外向，总是寻求新的体验和冒险。
近义词：adventurous, exploring, searching, inquisitive
反义词：introverted, withdrawn, complacent
词性: adjective

outseeking:/ˌaʊtˈsiːkɪŋ/
noun
外求；向外寻求
The act of actively searching for something outside of oneself or one's usual environment.
Her outseeking led her to travel the world and experience different cultures. 她的外求精神让她周游世界，体验不同的文化。
近义词：exploration, quest, search
反义词：introspection, withdrawal
词性: noun
