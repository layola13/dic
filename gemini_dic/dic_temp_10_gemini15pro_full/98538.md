instigator:/ˈɪnstɪɡeɪtər/| n. |煽动者，教唆者|A person who incites or urges on to some action or course, especially one of a violent or unlawful character.|例句：He was identified as the main instigator of the riot. 他被认定为这场骚乱的主要煽动者。

例句：The instigator of the fight remains unknown.  这场打架的煽动者仍然不明。

近义词：agitator, inciter, provoker|反义词: peacemaker, mediator|词性:noun
