navigo:/ˈnævɪɡoʊ/ (US), /ˈnævɪɡəʊ/ (UK)| v. |航行，导航，指引|To travel by water or air, especially over a long distance or by a planned route; to plan and direct the course of a ship, aircraft, or other form of transportation, especially by using instruments or maps.|We navigated the river by canoe. 我们乘独木舟沿河航行。|近义词：sail, steer, pilot, guide, direct|反义词：drift, wander|verb
