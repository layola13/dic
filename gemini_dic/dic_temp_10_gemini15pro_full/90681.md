humbucker:/ˈhʌmbʌkər/
noun|抗干扰拾音器，消除哼声的装置|A type of electric guitar pickup that reduces hum. |My Les Paul has two humbuckers. 我的Les Paul吉他有两个抗干扰拾音器。|noise-canceling pickup, hum-reducing pickup|single-coil pickup
word type|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *hum* (a low, continuous droning sound) + *buck* (to resist, oppose).  The term was coined because this type of pickup "bucks" or opposes the hum.
