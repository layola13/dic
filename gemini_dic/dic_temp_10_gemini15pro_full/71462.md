flameproofed:/ˈfleɪmpruːft/
adjective
防火的，防火焰的
Treated with a chemical substance to make it resistant to catching fire.
The curtains in the theatre were flameproofed for safety. 剧院里的窗帘经过防火处理以确保安全。
The children's pajamas were flameproofed. 孩子们的睡衣经过了防火处理。

近义词:fire-resistant, fireproof, non-flammable
反义词:flammable, inflammable
词性:adjective
