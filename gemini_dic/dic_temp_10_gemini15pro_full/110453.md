liking:/ˈlaɪkɪŋ/| n.|喜爱，喜好，嗜好|A feeling of pleasure and enjoyment that you have when you do or experience something that you like. |例句：She had a liking for strong coffee. 她喜欢喝浓咖啡。

例句：He developed a liking for classical music.  他逐渐喜欢上了古典音乐。

|近义词：fondness, preference, taste, inclination|反义词：dislike, aversion, hatred|词性:noun
