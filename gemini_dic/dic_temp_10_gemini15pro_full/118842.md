metaphorist:/ˈmetəfɔːrɪst/ | n. | 比喻家；善用比喻的人 | A person skilled in using metaphors; a creator or user of original or striking metaphors. |  Shakespeare was a master metaphorist.  莎士比亚是一位比喻大师。 | synonyms:  figure of speech user,  imagery user,  poet,  writer | antonyms: literalist | noun
