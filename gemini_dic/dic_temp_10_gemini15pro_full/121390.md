mishears:/ˌmɪsˈhɪrz/ | v. | 误听，听错 | To hear something incorrectly, often misunderstanding what was said. |  She misheard his name and thought he introduced himself as "John" instead of "Ron." 她把他的名字听错了，以为他自称是“约翰”而不是“罗恩”。

| 近义词：misinterprets, misunderstands | 反义词：hears correctly, understands | 词性：动词
