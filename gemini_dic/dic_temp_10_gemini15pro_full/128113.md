nestling's:/ˈnestlɪŋz/
noun
雏鸟的； 
belonging to a nestling (a young bird that has not yet left the nest).
The nestling's chirps were barely audible. 雏鸟的叫声几乎听不见。
近义词: fledgling's, chick's
反义词: adult bird's, mature bird's
词性: possessive noun (所有格名词)
