denunciatory:/dɪˈnʌnsiəˌtɔːri/ /dɪˈnaʊnsiəˌtɔːri/
adjective
谴责的；告发的
Expressing strong disapproval; condemning.
The senator delivered a denunciatory speech against the proposed legislation.  参议员发表了谴责拟议立法的演讲。

The article was denunciatory of the government's policies. 这篇文章谴责了政府的政策。

近义词:condemnatory, critical, accusatory, incriminatory
反义词:laudatory, complimentary, approving
词性:adjective
