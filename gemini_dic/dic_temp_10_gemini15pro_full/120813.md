misassignment:/ˌmɪs əˈsaɪnmənt/
n.
错误分配，分配错误
an act of assigning incorrectly.
The misassignment of the task led to project delays.  任务分配错误导致项目延误。
The paper retracts its claims due to a misassignment of cell lines.  由于细胞系的分配错误，该论文撤回了其声明。
近义词: misallocation, error, mistake
反义词: correct assignment, proper allocation
词性: noun
