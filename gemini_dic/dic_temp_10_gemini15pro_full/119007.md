methodically:/məˈθɑːdɪkli/ /məˈθɒdɪkli/
adverb|有条理地，有条不紊地|In a systematic or organized way, following a defined method or plan.|She methodically sorted the documents into different folders. 她有条不紊地将文件整理到不同的文件夹中。|例句：He methodically checked all the equipment before the experiment. 他在实验前有条不紊地检查了所有设备。|近义词：systematically, carefully, diligently, precisely|反义词：haphazardly, randomly, carelessly, erratically|词性: 副词
