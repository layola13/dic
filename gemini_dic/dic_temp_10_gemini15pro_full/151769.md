paso:/ˈpæsoʊ/ (US), /ˈpɑːsoʊ/ (UK)| n. |步伐；舞步；关口；要隘|A step in walking, dancing, or running; a narrow passage or mountain pass.|She took a slow paso towards the door. 她慢慢地走向门口。| 近义词：step, stride, gait, pass, passage| 反义词: standstill, halt|noun

paso:/ˈpæsoʊ/ (US), /ˈpɑːsoʊ/ (UK)| n. |（斗牛中的）躲闪步伐|A movement in bullfighting in which the matador passes the bull's horns close to his body.|The matador executed a daring paso. 斗牛士做了一个大胆的躲闪步伐。| 近义词：sidestep, dodge| 反义词: confront|noun
