pard:/pɑːrd/| n.|伙伴，同伴；(尤指)豹；(古)豹皮|A close friend or companion, especially a leopard.  Also, archaically, leopard skin.|He and his pard went hunting together. 他和他的伙伴一起去打猎了。|近义词：friend, companion, pal, chum, mate, buddy, associate, partner, comrade, colleague|反义词:enemy, foe|名词
