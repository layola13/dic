bigot:/ˈbɪɡət/| n. |偏执者，顽固者|A person who is intolerant towards those holding different opinions.|The bigot refused to listen to anyone who disagreed with him.  这个顽固分子拒绝听任何与他意见相左的人说话。|近义词：racist, prejudiced person, fanatic, extremist|反义词:tolerant person, open-minded person|noun
Etymology: From Old French *bigot, of uncertain origin. Possibly from a Germanic source.
