overcrowdedly:/ˌoʊvərˈkraʊdɪdli/
| adv. |过度拥挤地|In a way that is too full of people or things.  The refugees were overcrowdedly packed into the small boat.|例句：The train was overcrowdedly packed with commuters during rush hour.  高峰时段，通勤者把火车挤得水泄不通。

例句：The small room was overcrowdedly filled with furniture, making it difficult to move around.  小房间里塞满了家具，很难走动。|近义词：densely, excessively, |反义词: sparsely, thinly|词性:副词
