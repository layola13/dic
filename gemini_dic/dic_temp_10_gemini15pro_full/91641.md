hyperboliform:/ˌhaɪpərˈbɒlɪfɔːrm/ | adj. | 双曲形的，近似双曲线的 | Resembling a hyperbola in form.  Having the nature of a hyperbola. | The architect designed a hyperboliform roof for the new stadium. 建筑师为新体育场设计了一个双曲形的屋顶。 | The shell of the nautilus is hyperboliform. 鹦鹉螺的壳是双曲形的。 | 近义词: hyperbolic, hyperboloid | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  From hyperbola + -iform.  "Hyperbola" comes from the Greek ὑπερβολή (hyperbolē), meaning "excess,"  from ὑπερβάλλειν (hyperballein), "to exceed," from ὑπέρ (hyper), "over" + βάλλειν (ballein), "to throw."  "-iform" is a suffix meaning "having the form of," from Latin -formis.
