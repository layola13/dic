dermobranchiate:/ˌdɜːrməˈbræŋkiət/
adjective|皮肤鳃的，用皮肤呼吸的|Relating to or characteristic of Dermobranchia, a subclass of sea slugs that respire through projections of the body wall.|The dermobranchiate sea slug is known for its vibrant colors.|近义词:nudibranch (although not all nudibranchs are dermobranchiate)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "lung-breathing" in a broader biological context)|词性:adjective

dermobranchiate:/ˌdɜːrməˈbræŋkiət/
noun|皮肤鳃动物|A sea slug of the subclass Dermobranchia, which respires through projections of the body wall.|The researchers observed several different species of dermobranchiate during their dive.|近义词:nudibranch (in a general sense)|反义词:(No direct antonym)|词性:noun
