indomitable:/ɪnˈdɒmɪtəbl/ (US) /ɪnˈdɑːmɪtəbl/| adj. |不屈不挠的，不屈服的|Impossible to subdue or defeat; unconquerable.|例句：She showed indomitable spirit in the face of adversity.  她在逆境中表现出不屈不挠的精神。

例句：His indomitable will helped him overcome numerous obstacles. 他不屈不挠的意志帮助他克服了重重障碍。

|近义词：unyielding, resolute, steadfast, tenacious|反义词：submissive, yielding, weak, compliant|词性: 形容词
