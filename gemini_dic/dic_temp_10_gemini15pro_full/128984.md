nippers:/ˈnɪpərz/| n.|钳子，镊子，指甲刀；(尤指小孩的)牙齿|A small pincer-like implement used for gripping or cutting something, especially fingernails or toenails.  Sometimes refers to a young child's teeth.|The doctor used nippers to remove the splinter. 医生用镊子取出了碎片。|Synonyms: pliers, tongs, tweezers, clippers|Antonyms: (For the tool meaning, there isn't a direct antonym.  Perhaps something that joins or expands rather than grips.)|noun

nippers:/ˈnɪpərz/| n.|小男孩，小家伙|Informal term for young boys.|A group of nippers were playing in the park. 一群小男孩在公园里玩耍。|Synonyms: lads, boys, children, youngsters|Antonyms: adults, grown-ups|noun
