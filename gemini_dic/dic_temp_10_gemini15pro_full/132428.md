nondependency:/ˌnɑːndɪˈpɛndənsi/| n.|非依赖性，独立性|The state or quality of not being dependent on something or someone else.  The achievement of nondependency is a key goal for many developing nations.|  The country celebrated its nondependency from foreign aid. 这个国家庆祝其摆脱了对外援的依赖。|  She valued her financial nondependency. 她很珍惜自己的经济独立。|independence, self-reliance, autonomy|dependence, reliance|noun
