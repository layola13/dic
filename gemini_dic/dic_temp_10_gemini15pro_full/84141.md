half-muslim:/ˌhæf ˈmʊzlɪm/ | adj. | 半穆斯林的；部分信仰伊斯兰教的 | Someone who is half-Muslim has one parent who is Muslim and one who is not, or who identifies partially with the Muslim faith but not fully.  It can be considered a sensitive term and should be used with caution, as self-identification is paramount. |  "He described himself as half-Muslim, having been raised by a Muslim father and a Christian mother." 他形容自己是半个穆斯林，由一位穆斯林父亲和一位基督教母亲抚养长大。| Near Synonyms: part-Muslim, partially Muslim (use with caution) | Antonyms: fully Muslim, non-Muslim | Adjective
