decliner:/dɪˈklaɪnər/| n.|拒绝者；衰退者；下降的东西|A person who refuses something; something that decreases or diminishes.|The company president is a decliner of interviews. 公司总裁拒绝接受采访。|The stock market has been a steady decliner this week. 本周股市持续下跌。|Synonyms: refuser, rejector, objector, dissenter, non-accepter, denier; falling, decreasing, diminishing, dwindling, shrinking, contracting, waning|Antonyms: accepter, receiver, taker; increasing, rising, growing, expanding|noun


decline:/dɪˈklaɪn/| v.|下降；衰退；婉拒|To decrease or diminish; to politely refuse.|The company's profits declined by 10% last year.  公司利润去年下降了10%。|He declined the invitation to the party. 他婉拒了参加聚会的邀请。|Synonyms: decrease, diminish, dwindle, shrink, contract, wane; refuse, reject, turn down|Antonyms: increase, rise, grow, expand; accept, receive, take|verb

decline:/dɪˈklaɪn/| n.|下降；衰退；斜坡|A decrease or diminution; a downward slope.|There has been a steady decline in the quality of education. 教育质量一直在稳步下降。|The house is situated on a gentle decline. 这栋房子坐落在一个缓坡上。|Synonyms: decrease, diminution, dwindling, shrinking, contraction, waning; downslope, descent|Antonyms: increase, rise, growth, expansion; upslope, ascent|noun
