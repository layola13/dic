amped:/æmpt/
adjective
兴奋的，激动的
Feeling energized and enthusiastic, often due to excitement or anticipation.
例句：I'm amped for the concert tonight! 我今晚去看演唱会，太兴奋了！

例句：The team was amped up for the championship game.  球队为冠军赛兴奋不已。

近义词：excited, enthusiastic, thrilled, pumped, psyched
反义词：lethargic, apathetic, unenthusiastic
词性: adjective

词源:  Shortened form of "amplified," from the idea of increasing energy or excitement.
