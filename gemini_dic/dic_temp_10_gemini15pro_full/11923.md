arsenals:/ˈɑːrsənəlz/ /ˈɑːrsnəlz/
n.
军火库，武器库；（比喻）大量储备
A place where weapons and military equipment are stored or manufactured; a collection or supply of anything.
The country's nuclear arsenals are a cause for international concern.  这个国家的核武库引起了国际社会的关注。

The chef has an arsenal of recipes at his disposal.  这位厨师掌握了大量的菜谱。
Synonyms: stockpile, store, hoard, cache, reserve, depository, repository
Antonyms: shortage, lack, dearth
noun
