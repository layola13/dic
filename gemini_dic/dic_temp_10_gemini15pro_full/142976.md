outbakes:/ˌaʊtˈbeɪks/
verb
烘烤得比…更好;在烘烤方面胜过
Surpasses someone in baking.

例句：
She outbakes everyone in the neighborhood.
她烘烤的技术比街坊邻居都好。

He consistently outbakes his competitors at the county fair.
他在县集市上总是烘烤得比他的竞争对手好。

近义词：outcooks, surpasses (in baking)
反义词：is outbaked by

词性:v.
