fracas:/ˈfreɪkəs/ (US) /ˈfrækɑː/ (UK)| n.|争吵，吵闹，喧嚷|A noisy disturbance or quarrel.|例句：A fracas broke out in the bar after the football game. 足球比赛结束后酒吧里爆发了一场争吵。

例句：The political fracas dominated the news headlines.  这场政治争吵占据了新闻头条。

近义词：brawl, melee, uproar, commotion, disturbance, quarrel, fight, clash|反义词：peace, calm, tranquility, harmony|词性：名词

词源：From French *fracas* "crash, din, uproar," from Italian *fracasso* "crashing, clatter," of imitative origin.
