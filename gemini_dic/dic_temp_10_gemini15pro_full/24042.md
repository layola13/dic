breezeway:/ˈbriːzweɪ/| n.|有屋顶的通路，凉亭|A covered passage, open on the sides, connecting two buildings or parts of a building, often a house and garage.|We stored the bikes in the breezeway between the house and the garage. 我们把自行车存放在房子和车库之间的有顶过道里。|The breezeway provided a welcome respite from the hot sun. 凉亭提供了躲避炎炎烈日的舒适场所。|近义词：passageway, walkway, corridor, portico|反义词:|名词
