belooche:/bəˈluːtʃ/ | adj. n. |俾路支人的，俾路支人|Relating to or characteristic of Balochistan or its people.  A member of a Muslim people inhabiting chiefly Balochistan.|例句：He speaks Belooche fluently. 他能说一口流利的俾路支语。

例句：The Belooche are known for their hospitality. 俾路支人以热情好客而闻名。|近义词：Baluchi, Baluch|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Baluchi, the name of the language spoken by the Baloch people.  The ultimate origin is uncertain, but possibly related to ancient Median.
