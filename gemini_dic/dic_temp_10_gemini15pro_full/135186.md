nonordered:/ˌnɒnˈɔːdəd/ | adj. | 非排序的，无序的 | Not arranged in any particular order; random. | The data was presented in a nonordered list. 数据以非排序列表的形式呈现。| The cards were shuffled into a nonordered pile.  这些卡片被洗成了无序的一堆。| 近义词: unordered, random, unsorted | 反义词: ordered, sorted, sequenced | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "ordered" (past participle of "order," from Latin "ordinare," meaning "to arrange").
