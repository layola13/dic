monopode:/ˈmɒnəpɒd/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnəpɑːd/ (US)| n. |单脚架，独脚架|A single staff or pole used as a support, especially for a camera or other equipment. |例句：The photographer used a monopode to steady his camera for the long exposure. 摄影师用单脚架稳定相机进行长时间曝光。

例句：A monopode is useful for taking selfies or videos when you don't have anyone to hold the camera. 当你没有人帮你拿相机时，单脚架对于自拍或录像很有用。|近义词：unipod, single-leg tripod|反义词: tripod (三脚架), bipod (双脚架)|词性:noun
