bowery:/ˈbaʊəri/| n.|贫民窟；下等剧院区；低级娱乐场所|A run-down and often disreputable part of a town or city, typically inhabited by the poor or homeless and frequented by vagrants and drunks.  It originally referred to a district in New York City.|He grew up in the bowery, surrounded by poverty and crime. 他在贫民窟长大，周围都是贫穷和犯罪。|slum, ghetto, skid row|uptown, affluent district|noun
