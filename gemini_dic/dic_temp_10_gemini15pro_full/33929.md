cirrhotic:/sɪˈrɒtɪk/ (US) /sɪˈrɑːtɪk/ (UK)| adj.|肝硬化的；肝硬变的|Affected with or relating to cirrhosis of the liver.|例句：The patient's cirrhotic liver was failing.  病人的肝硬化正在恶化。

例句：Cirrhotic changes in the liver can lead to serious complications.  肝脏的肝硬化变化会导致严重的并发症。|近义词：sclerotic|反义词:healthy|词性:形容词
