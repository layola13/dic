dissociating:/dɪˈsəʊʃɪeɪtɪŋ/ /dɪˈsoʊsieɪtɪŋ/
verb, adjective
分离，游离，使脱离
breaking the association between something; relating to or involving dissociation.
The compound is dissociating into its constituent elements. 该化合物正在分解成其组成元素。
He has been dissociating himself from the group. 他一直在试图脱离这个团体。
Synonyms: separating, disconnecting, detaching, isolating
Antonyms: associating, connecting, uniting, integrating
verb

dissociate:/dɪˈsəʊʃɪeɪt/ /dɪˈsoʊsieɪt/
verb, noun
分离，游离，使脱离；游离分子，游离基
to separate from something; a molecule that has split into simpler groups of atoms.
He tried to dissociate himself from the scandal. 他试图把自己与丑闻撇清关系。
Free radicals are highly reactive dissociates. 自由基是高活性的游离基。
Synonyms: separate, disconnect, detach, isolate
Antonyms: associate, connect, unite, integrate
verb, noun
