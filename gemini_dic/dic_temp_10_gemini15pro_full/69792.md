fern:/fɜːrn/ (US) /fɜːn/ (UK)| n. |蕨类植物|A vascular plant that reproduces via spores and has neither seeds nor flowers.|The forest floor was covered with ferns.|Synonyms: bracken, pteridophyte|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a plant type.  Could consider "flowering plant" as a contrasting category)|noun
