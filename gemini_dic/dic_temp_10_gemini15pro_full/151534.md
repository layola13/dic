parsley: /ˈpɑːrsli/ (US) /ˈpɑːsli/ (UK)| n. | 欧芹；香芹 |A biennial herb (Petroselinum crispum) of the parsley family, native to the Mediterranean region, having either curled leaf clusters (French parsley) or flat compound leaves (Italian parsley) cultivated for use as a garnish or as a flavoring ingredient. |  I added some fresh parsley to the pasta dish. 我在意大利面里加了些新鲜的欧芹。| synonym: chervil, coriander | antonym: None (Parsley doesn't have a direct opposite in terms of culinary herbs.) | noun
