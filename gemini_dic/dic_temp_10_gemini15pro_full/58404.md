dubitable:/ˈdjuːbɪtəbl/ /ˈduːbɪtəbl/| adj.|可疑的，不确定的|Open to doubt or question; uncertain.|Her claim to be a princess was highly dubitable.|近义词：doubtful, questionable, uncertain, suspect|反义词：indubitable, unquestionable, certain, sure|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *dubitabilis, from Latin dubitare "to doubt," from dubius "doubtful."
