acquiring:/əˈkwaɪərɪŋ/
v.|获得; 取得; 学到; 养成|The company is acquiring new customers at a rapid rate.  该公司正快速获得新客户。|
acquiring:/əˈkwaɪərɪŋ/
adj.|收购的; 购买的|The acquiring company will merge with the smaller company. 收购公司将与较小的公司合并。|
acquiring:/əˈkwaɪərɪŋ/
n.|获得; 取得; 学到; 养成|The acquiring of knowledge is a lifelong process. 获取知识是一个终生的过程。|The process of acquiring language skills is complex. 语言技能的习得过程很复杂。|近义词: obtaining, gaining, learning, developing|反义词: losing, forfeiting, abandoning|词性:动名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *aquyren*, from Anglo-Norman *aquere*, from Old French *aquérir*, from Latin *acquirere* (“to acquire, get, obtain, procure”), from *ad* (“to”) + *quaerere* (“to seek, ask”).
