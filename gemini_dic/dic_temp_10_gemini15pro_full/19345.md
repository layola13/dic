bibliomaniacal: /ˌbɪbliəˈmeɪniækəl/
| adj. | 藏书癖的；书籍狂的 | Excessively fond of collecting and possessing books, often regardless of their content or readability. | He was a bibliomaniacal hoarder, his house overflowing with dusty tomes. 他是一个有藏书癖的囤积者，他的房子里堆满了满是灰尘的大部头书籍。|  Example:  Her bibliomaniacal tendencies led her to purchase every edition of her favorite novel. 她的藏书癖使她购买了她最喜欢的小说的每一个版本。 | Synonym:  bookish, bibliophilic | Antonym:  apathetic (towards books), indifferent (to reading) |词性: 形容词

bibliomania: /ˌbɪbliəˈmeɪniə/
| n. | 藏书癖；书籍狂 | An excessive fondness for collecting and possessing books. | His bibliomania led him to spend his life savings on rare first editions. 他的藏书癖导致他把毕生的积蓄都花在了珍稀的初版书籍上。 | Example:  His bibliomania resulted in a library that rivaled any public collection. 他的藏书癖造就了一个可以与任何公共图书馆相媲美的私人图书馆。 | Synonym:  book madness, bibliophilism | Antonym:  lack of interest in books | 词性: 名词
