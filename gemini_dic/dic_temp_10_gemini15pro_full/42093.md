cotransferred:/ˌkoʊtrænsˈfɜːrd/
verb
共同转移；共同转让
The plasmid and the gene were cotransferred into the recipient cell.  质粒和基因共同转移到受体细胞中。
The property was cotransferred to the siblings. 这处房产共同转让给了兄弟姐妹。
co-transferred (hyphenated form, less common) /ˌkoʊˈtrænsfɜːrd/
verb
共同转移；共同转让
The plasmid and the gene were co-transferred into the recipient cell.  质粒和基因共同转移到受体细胞中。
The rights were co-transferred to both parties.  权利共同转让给了双方。
近义词: transferred together, jointly transferred
反义词: separately transferred, individually transferred
词性: verb (past participle)
词源: co- (prefix meaning "together") + transferred (past participle of "transfer")
