greyhounds:/ˈɡreɪhaʊndz/
n.
灰狗（复数）
A breed of tall, slender dog, known for its speed and often used for racing.
Greyhounds are the fastest dog breed. 灰狗是最快的犬种。
Greyhounds were used to hunt rabbits. 灰狗曾被用来猎兔。
synonyms: whippets, lurchers, salukis
antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a breed of dog, perhaps "small dog breeds" or "slow dog breeds" in context)
