disunionist:/ˌdɪsˈjuːnjənɪst/
n.
分裂主义者，主张分裂的人
A person who advocates or supports disunion, especially secession from a political union.
The disunionist movement threatened to tear the country apart. 分裂主义运动有可能撕裂这个国家。
Synonyms: secessionist, separatist, autonomist
Antonyms: unionist, federalist
noun

disunion:/ˌdɪsˈjuːnjən/
n.
分裂，不团结
The state of being disunited; lack of unity or harmony.
The disunion within the party led to its defeat in the election. 党内分裂导致了选举失败。
Synonyms: division, discord, disunity, separation
Antonyms: unity, harmony, concord
noun
