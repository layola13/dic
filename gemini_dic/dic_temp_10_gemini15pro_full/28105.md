capitalistic:/ˌkæpɪtəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
资本主义的
Relating to or based on the principles of capitalism.
Example:  The capitalistic system emphasizes private ownership. 资本主义制度强调私有制。

Example:  They criticized the capitalistic nature of the healthcare industry. 他们批评了医疗行业的资本主义性质。


近义词:  capitalist, free-market, private-enterprise
反义词: socialist, communist, collectivist
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "capitalist" + "-ic".  "Capitalist" comes from "capital" (from Old French "capital," ultimately from Latin "caput" meaning "head"), referring to accumulated wealth or assets used in production.  The suffix "-ic" forms adjectives meaning "of or relating to."
