nondemonstrable:/ˌnɒndɪˈmɒnstrəbl/ | adj. | 不可论证的，无法证明的 | Not capable of being demonstrated or proved. |  The existence of ghosts is nondemonstrable. 鬼魂的存在是无法证明的。|  The benefits of this new policy are as yet nondemonstrable. 这项新政策的好处还有待证明。 | synonyms: unprovable, indemonstrable, unproven | antonyms: demonstrable, provable, evident | adjective
