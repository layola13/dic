miseducates:/ˌmɪsˈɛdʒʊkeɪts/
verb
错误地教育，误导
To educate improperly or incorrectly, often by providing inaccurate or incomplete information or by promoting harmful stereotypes.
The textbook miseducates students about the true history of the conflict. 这本教科书对冲突的真实历史进行了错误的教育。
misinforms, misleads
educates, informs
v.
