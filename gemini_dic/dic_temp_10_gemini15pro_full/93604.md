imbecilely:/ˌɪmbəˈsɪlli/ | adv. | 愚蠢地，痴呆地 | In a foolish or stupid manner; like an imbecile. | He grinned imbecilely at the camera.  他对着镜头傻傻地咧嘴笑。| Synonyms: stupidly, foolishly, idiotically, moronically | Antonyms: intelligently, wisely, cleverly, smartly | Part of Speech: Adverb

Etymology:
From imbecile + -ly. Imbecile comes from French imbécile, from Latin imbecillus "weak," from in- "not" + baculum "staff."
