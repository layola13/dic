by-all-odds:/baɪ ɔːl ɑːdz/
| adv. |毫无疑问地，肯定地|Without any doubt; certainly.|例句：By all odds, she is the best candidate for the job.  毫无疑问，她是这份工作的最佳人选。|近义词：certainly, undoubtedly, without a doubt, definitely, absolutely|反义词：doubtfully, possibly, perhaps|词性：副词

by-all-odds:/baɪ ɔːl ɑːdz/
| adj. |最可能的；最有可能的|Most likely; most probable. |例句：He is, by all odds, the most likely to succeed. 他是最有可能成功的人。|近义词：most likely, most probable |反义词：least likely, least probable|词性：形容词
