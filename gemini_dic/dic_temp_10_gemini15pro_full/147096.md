overliking:/ˌōvərˈlaɪkɪŋ/ |n.| 过度喜爱，过分喜欢 | An excessive fondness or preference for something or someone, often beyond what is considered reasonable or healthy.  It implies an emotional attachment that goes beyond simple enjoyment. | She has an overliking for sweets, which is affecting her health. 她过分喜欢吃甜食，这正在影响她的健康。| 近义词: infatuation, obsession, excessive fondness | 反义词: dislike, aversion, indifference | 词性: 名词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "liking" (noun form of "like")
