non-performing:/ˌnɒn pəˈfɔːmɪŋ/ (US) /ˌnɒn pəˈfɔːmɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|不履约的；不执行的；不工作的；无效的|Not performing or functioning as expected or required; failing to meet obligations or produce results.|The bank is burdened with a high number of non-performing loans.  这家银行背负着大量的不良贷款。|Synonyms: delinquent, defaulting, unproductive, ineffective, insolvent|Antonyms: performing, productive, effective, solvent|adjective

non-performing:/ˌnɒn pəˈfɔːmɪŋ/ (US) /ˌnɒn pəˈfɔːmɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|不良资产|An asset, typically a loan, that is not producing income or that has stopped performing.|Non-performings are a major concern for financial institutions. 不良资产是金融机构的主要担忧。|Synonyms: bad debt, bad loan |Antonyms: performing asset|noun
