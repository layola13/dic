epitaphing: /ˈɛpɪˌtæfɪŋ/
v.
铭刻墓志铭; 为...撰写墓志铭
Inscribing or composing an epitaph.
He was epitaphing his father's tombstone.  他正在为他父亲的墓碑刻墓志铭。
The poet was epitaphing a fallen comrade. 这位诗人正在为一位牺牲的战友撰写墓志铭。
近义词:  inscribing, commemorating, memorializing
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑：obliterating, forgetting)
词性: 动词 (verb)

词源:  From "epitaph" + "-ing".  "Epitaph" comes from the Greek "epitaphion," meaning "funeral oration," from "epi-" (upon) + "taphos" (tomb).
