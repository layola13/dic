bromoiodized:/ˌbroʊmoʊˈaɪəˌdaɪzd/
adjective
溴碘化的
Containing or treated with both bromine and iodine.
Bromoiodized table salt is often used in areas with iodine deficiency.  溴碘化食盐常用于缺碘地区。
Bromoiodized vegetable oil was used in the study. 这项研究使用了溴碘化植物油。
近义词: iodinated, halogenated
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "untreated")
词性: adjective
