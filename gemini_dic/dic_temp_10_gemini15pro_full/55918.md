divulging:/daɪˈvʌldʒɪŋ/
verb
泄露，透露
the act of making something known that was previously secret or private.
He was punished for divulging company secrets. 他因泄露公司机密而受到惩罚。
synonyms: revealing, disclosing, unveiling, exposing
antonyms: concealing, hiding, suppressing
v.
