minuteman:/ˈmɪnɪtmæn/ | n. |民兵, 一分钟人|A member of a militia during and before the American Revolution who could be ready to fight in a minute's notice. | The minutemen played a crucial role in the battles of Lexington and Concord.  民兵在列克星敦和康科德战役中发挥了至关重要的作用。 | synonyms: militiaman, patriot | antonyms: loyalist, redcoat | noun
