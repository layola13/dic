innuendo:/ˌɪnjuˈɛndoʊ/ | n. |影射，暗讽|An indirect or subtle, usually derogatory, implication in expression; an insinuation.|Her reputation was damaged by innuendo. 她的名誉因影射而受损。|Synonyms: insinuation, implication, suggestion, hint, allusion|Antonyms: direct statement, explicit remark|noun
