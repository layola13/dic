asepticism:/əˈsɛptɪˌsɪzəm/| n.|无菌状态，无菌操作|The state of being free from pathogenic microorganisms; the process of achieving this state by excluding all microorganisms.  The practice of maintaining sterility in medical and surgical procedures.|The surgeon ensured asepticism throughout the operation.  外科医生确保整个手术过程无菌。|Synonyms: sterility, asepsis|Antonyms: sepsis, contamination|noun
