gutturine:/ˈɡʌtərɪn/| adj.|喉音的；似喉音的；粗哑的|Relating to or resembling the guttural sounds produced in the throat.  Characterized by a harsh, rasping sound, produced in the throat.|例句：He had a deep, guttural voice that commanded attention. 他嗓音低沉沙哑，很有气势。

例句：The guttural sounds of the language were difficult for foreigners to master. 这门语言的喉音对外国人来说很难掌握。|近义词：hoarse, rasping, gravelly, throaty|反义词:smooth, clear, melodious|词性:adjective
