calcification: /ˌkæl.sə.fɪˈkeɪ.ʃən/ | n. |钙化| The process of hardening by deposit of calcium salts. |例句：

The calcification of the artery walls can lead to heart disease.
动脉壁的钙化会导致心脏病。

Calcification of the cartilage can restrict joint movement.
软骨钙化会限制关节活动。

|近义词：hardening, ossification|反义词: decalcification, softening|词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from late Latin calcificatio(n-), from calcificare ‘turn into lime’, from calx, calc- ‘lime’.
