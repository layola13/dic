non-verbally: /nɒnˈvɜːrbəli/ | adv. | 非语言地 | Without using spoken language; through gestures, facial expressions, body language, etc. | She communicated her discomfort non-verbally through a series of frowns and fidgeting. 她通过一系列皱眉和坐立不安的动作非语言地表达了她的不适。|  He nodded non-verbally in agreement. 他默默地点头表示同意。| 近义词: silently, wordlessly | 反义词: verbally, orally | 词性: 副词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "verbally" (derived from "verb," ultimately from Latin "verbum" meaning "word").
