amphibolic:/ˌæmfɪˈbɒlɪk/
adjective
两性的；兼有分解和合成的
(of a metabolic pathway) functioning in both anabolism and catabolism, relating to or exhibiting both synthesis and breakdown.
Amino acids from protein breakdown can be used in amphibolic pathways. 蛋白质分解产生的氨基酸可以在两性代谢途径中使用。
近义词: dual-purpose, versatile
反义词: specialized, unidirectional
词性: adjective

词源:
From Greek amphi- "both" + ballein "to throw" + -ic.
