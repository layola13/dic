chenu: /ʃəˈnuː/ | n. | 陈奴，旧时中国南方对仆人的称呼 | A derogatory term used in old southern China to refer to a servant. |  He called his servant "chenu". 他叫他的仆人“陈奴”。 | 近义词：servant, slave | 反义词: master | 词性: 名词

词源: From Cantonese 茶奴 (caa4 nou4), literally "tea slave," referring to servants who served tea.  Originally, it might have been a more neutral term, but over time, it acquired a derogatory connotation.
