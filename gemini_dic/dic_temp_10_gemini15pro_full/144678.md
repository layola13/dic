outyelp: /ˌaʊtˈjɛlp/ | v. |  大声喊叫，盖过…的喊叫声 | to cry out louder than (someone or something else) | The fans outyelped the referee.  球迷的喊叫声盖过了裁判的声音。| 近义词: outshout, drown out | 反义词: be drowned out, be outshouted | 词性: 动词

词源: out- + yelp.  "out-"  作为前缀表示"超过，胜过"， "yelp" 意为“尖声叫喊”。
