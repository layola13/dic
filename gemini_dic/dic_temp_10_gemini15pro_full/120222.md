minestrone:/ˌmɪnɪˈstrəʊni/ (UK), /ˌmɪnɪˈstroʊni/ (US)| n. |蔬菜通心粉汤；意大利蔬菜浓汤|A thick Italian soup containing vegetables, pasta, and often beans.|例句：We had a hearty minestrone soup for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了丰盛的蔬菜通心粉汤。

例句：The minestrone was full of fresh seasonal vegetables. 这份蔬菜通心粉汤里充满了新鲜的时令蔬菜。|近义词：vegetable soup, Italian soup|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a specific clear broth soup could be considered)|词性:noun
