noninclinational:/ˌnɑːnɪnˌklɪˈneɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 无倾向性的；不偏不倚的；客观的 | Not having or showing any inclination or preference; unbiased; objective. |  The judge delivered a noninclinational verdict, weighing the evidence carefully.  法官做出了不偏不倚的判决，仔细权衡了证据。

The scientist maintained a noninclinational stance throughout the experiment.  科学家在整个实验过程中保持了客观的立场。 | 近义词：impartial, unbiased, objective, neutral | 反义词：biased, prejudiced, partial, subjective | 词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix): not
inclinational (adjective): relating to inclination
inclination (noun) from Latin inclinatio, from inclinare "to lean, bend," from in- "toward" + clinare "to bend"
