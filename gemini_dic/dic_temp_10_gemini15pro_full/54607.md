dismayedness:/dɪsˈmeɪdɪdnəs/
n.
沮丧，惊慌；灰心丧气
The state of being dismayed; consternation.
She couldn't hide her dismayedness at the news. 她无法掩饰听到这个消息时的沮丧。
Near Synonyms: consternation, alarm, discouragement, despondency, dismay
Antonyms: calmness, composure, encouragement, hopefulness
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From dismayed + -ness.  Dismay comes from the Old French *desmayer, meaning "to lose courage."
