pant:/pænt/ | n. vi. |喘气；气喘吁吁；渴望 |The act or sound of breathing quickly and heavily. Also, a strong desire for something.|
The dog was panting after its run.  狗跑完后气喘吁吁。
He panted for a glass of cold water. 他渴望一杯冷水。|
近义词：gasp, puff, yearn, crave |反义词: exhale |词性: 名词, 动词

pant:/pænt/ | v. |喘气；气喘吁吁；渴望|To breathe quickly and heavily. Also, to long for something.|
She panted up the steep hill. 她气喘吁吁地爬上了陡峭的山坡。
He panted for recognition of his work. 他渴望他的工作得到认可。|
