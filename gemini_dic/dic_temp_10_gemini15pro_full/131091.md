nonbeneficialnesses:/ˌnɒnbɛnɪˈfɪʃəlnəsɪz/
noun
无益；无益处
The state or quality of not being beneficial.
The nonbeneficialnesses of the policy quickly became apparent. 这项政策的无益性很快就显现出来了。
Near Synonyms:  uselessness, futility, disadvantage, detriment
Antonyms:  benefit, advantage, usefulness, helpfulness
词性: 名词
