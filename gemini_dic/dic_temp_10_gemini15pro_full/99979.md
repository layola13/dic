interownership:/ˌɪntərˈoʊnərʃɪp/
n.
相互拥有，互相参股，交叉持股
The situation where two or more companies or entities own stakes in each other.
Example: Interownership between media companies can limit competition and diversity of viewpoints. 媒体公司之间的交叉持股会限制竞争和观点的多样性。
Example: The complex interownership structure made it difficult to determine who ultimately controlled the company. 复杂的交叉持股结构使得难以确定谁最终控制了该公司。
Synonym: cross-ownership, mutual ownership
Antonym: sole ownership, independent ownership
noun
