blather:/ˈblæðər/ (US), /ˈblæðə(r)/ (UK)| n.|喋喋不休；胡说八道；废话|Foolish or nonsensical talk.|His speech was nothing but blather. 他的演讲只不过是一派胡言。|近义词：babble, chatter, prattle, nonsense|反义词: sense, wisdom|词性:名词

词源:  From Old Norse *blaðra "to talk nonsense," related to Old English blæðre "bladder," suggesting the image of something inflated and empty.
