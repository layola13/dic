forearming:/fɔːrˈɑːrmɪŋ/
verb
预先武装；预先准备；预先警告
preparing or equipping (someone or oneself) in advance for a particular situation or task.
例句：
Forearming myself with knowledge of the local customs, I felt more confident about my trip.
预先了解了当地习俗，我对这次旅行更有信心了。

例句：
They were forearmed against the attack by intelligence reports.
他们通过情报报告预先防备了这次袭击。

近义词：preparing, equipping, alerting, warning
反义词：unprepared, unsuspecting

词性：v.
