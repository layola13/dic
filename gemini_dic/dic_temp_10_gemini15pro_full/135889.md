nonprovocative: /ˌnɒnprəˈvɒkətɪv/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnprəˈvɑːkətɪv/ (US) | adj. | 非挑衅性的，不刺激的 | Not tending or intended to provoke, irritate, or cause resentment. |  The government took a nonprovocative stance in the negotiations. 政府在谈判中采取了非挑衅性的立场。 | The diplomat used nonprovocative language to avoid escalating the conflict.  外交官使用非挑衅性的语言以避免冲突升级。 | 近义词: conciliatory, diplomatic, inoffensive, peaceable, unprovocative | 反义词: provocative, inflammatory, agitating, antagonistic, belligerent | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + provocative (from Latin provocare "to call forth, challenge")
