consignificative:/kənˌsɪɡˈnɪfɪkətɪv/
adjective
共同表示的；共同含义的；有共同意义的
Having the same meaning or signification; signifying the same thing.  Expressing the same idea or concept.
The words "large" and "big" are consignificative.  “large”和“big”这两个词意思相同。
The terms "liberty" and "freedom" are often used consignificatively. “自由”和“解放”这两个词经常表达相同的意思。
近义词: synonymous, equivalent, similar
反义词: antonymous, opposite, different
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *consignificativus, from consignificare "to signify together," from com- "together" + significare "to signify."
