decamps:/dɪˈkæmps/
verb
撤营；突然离开；逃走
To break up camp and depart; to leave suddenly or secretly.
The circus decamped in the middle of the night. 马戏团半夜撤走了。
近义词:depart, leave, flee, abscond
反义词:arrive, stay, remain
词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From French "décamper," from "dé-" (prefix meaning "away, from") + "camper" (to encamp).  Ultimately from Latin "campus" (field).
