overwildness:/ˌoʊvərˈwaɪldnɪs/
|n.|过度野蛮; 极度荒凉; 过度狂野|The overwildness of the landscape made it both beautiful and dangerous. 这片土地极度的荒凉使其既美丽又危险。|例句：The overwildness of the region attracted adventurers but repelled settlers.  这片区域的极度荒凉吸引了冒险家，却吓退了定居者。

例句：The film portrayed the overwildness of human nature in extreme circumstances. 这部电影描绘了在极端环境下人性的过度野蛮。|近义词：savagery, wildness, ferocity, untamedness|反义词：tameness, civility, domesticity|词性: 名词
