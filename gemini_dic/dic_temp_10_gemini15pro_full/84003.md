half-destroyed:/ˌhæf dɪˈstrɔɪd/
adjective
半毁的，部分摧毁的
partially ruined or demolished; damaged but not completely destroyed
The half-destroyed building stood as a stark reminder of the war. 这座半毁的建筑是战争的残酷提醒。
The hurricane left many homes half-destroyed. 飓风过后，许多房屋半毁。
Near synonyms: partially destroyed, partly ruined, damaged, wrecked
Antonyms: intact, undamaged, pristine, whole
词性: 形容词
