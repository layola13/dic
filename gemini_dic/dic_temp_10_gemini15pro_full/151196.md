parchmentlike:/ˈpɑːrtʃməntˌlaɪk/
adjective
像羊皮纸的
Resembling parchment in texture, appearance, or color; thin, dry, and yellowish.
The old woman's skin was parchmentlike and wrinkled. 老妇人的皮肤像羊皮纸一样又薄又干，布满了皱纹。
The desert wind had left the leaves parchmentlike and brittle. 沙漠的风使得树叶像羊皮纸一样又薄又脆。
近义词:papery, dry, thin, brittle
反义词:smooth, soft, supple, moist
词性:adjective

词源:
parchment (n.) + -like (suffix)
parchment: from Old French parchemin, from Late Latin pergamina "parchment," from feminine of pergamenus "of Pergamum," from Greek Pergamos, name of a city in Mysia, Asia Minor, where parchment is said to have been invented.
-like: Old English -lic, from Proto-Germanic *-lika- (source also of Old Saxon -lik, Old Frisian -lik, Old Norse -ligr, Dutch -lijk, Old High German -lih, German -lich), from PIE *-likos, adjectival suffix of likeness.
