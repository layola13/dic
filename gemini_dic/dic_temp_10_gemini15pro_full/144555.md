outwaits:/ˌaʊtˈweɪts/
verb
比...等得更久; 比...更有耐心
waits out someone or something; waits longer than someone or something else.
He outwaited his rivals and eventually won the contract. 他比他的竞争对手更有耐心，最终赢得了合同。
The protestors outwaited the police, who eventually left. 抗议者比警察等得更久，警察最终离开了。
outlast, outstay
