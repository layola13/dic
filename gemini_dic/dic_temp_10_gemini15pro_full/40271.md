continuance:/kənˈtɪnjuəns/
n.
延续；继续；持续；延期
the state of continuing or being continued.

The continuance of the strike caused hardship for many families.
罢工的持续给许多家庭带来了困难。

The project's continuance depends on funding.
该项目的继续取决于资金。

The court granted a continuance in the trial.
法庭批准了审判的延期。


近义词: continuation, duration, persistence, prolongation
反义词: cessation, discontinuation, interruption, termination
词性: 名词
