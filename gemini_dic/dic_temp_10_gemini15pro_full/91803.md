hyperfemininity:/ˌhaɪpərˌfɛmɪˈnɪnɪti/ | n. | 超级女性化；极端女性化 |  An exaggerated adherence to stereotypically feminine gender roles, often involving an emphasis on physical appearance, submissiveness, and emotional expressiveness.  |  Her hyperfemininity was evident in her elaborate hairstyle and delicate mannerisms. 她的极端女性化在她精心制作的发型和优雅的举止中显而易见。 | synonyms: ultra-femininity, exaggerated femininity | antonyms: masculinity, androgyny | noun
