fibril:/ˈfaɪbrəl/ /ˈfɪbrɪl/| n. |细丝，纤丝，原纤维|A small or slender fiber or filament, especially one that is a component of a larger fiber or structure. A fibril is a fine thread-like structure that is often part of a larger network or bundle.|Collagen fibrils provide structural support to connective tissues.|近义词：fiber, filament, thread|反义词:|noun
