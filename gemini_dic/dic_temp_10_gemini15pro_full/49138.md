deity:/ˈdiːɪti/ (US), /ˈdeɪɪti/ (UK)| n.|神，女神；神性|A deity is a god or goddess, or the state or quality of being divine. |The ancient Greeks worshipped a pantheon of deities. 古希腊人崇拜众神。|The deity of the sun was revered by many cultures.  许多文化都崇敬太阳神。|synonyms: god, goddess, divine being, immortal, celestial being|antonyms: mortal, human|noun
