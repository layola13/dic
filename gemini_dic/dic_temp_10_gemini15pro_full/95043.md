inactivation:/ɪnˌæktɪˈveɪʃən/| n.|失活，灭活|The inactivation of the virus is crucial for developing a vaccine. 病毒的灭活对于开发疫苗至关重要。|例句：The inactivation of the enzyme by heat is irreversible.  热对酶的灭活是不可逆的。

The drug works by inactivation of a specific protein. 该药物通过灭活特定蛋白质起作用。

|近义词：deactivation, neutralization|反义词: activation|词性:名词
