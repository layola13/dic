hydrometallurgy:/ˌhaɪdrəʊˈmɛtəlɜːrdʒi/ /ˌhaɪdroʊˈmɛtəlɜːrdʒi/
n.
湿法冶金
The extraction of metals from their ores by dissolving them in aqueous solutions.
Hydrometallurgy is often used to extract copper from low-grade ores. 湿法冶金常用于从低品位矿石中提取铜。
The process of hydrometallurgy involves leaching, purification, and precipitation. 湿法冶金过程包括浸出、净化和沉淀。
leaching, extraction, chemical processing
pyrometallurgy (火法冶金)
noun
