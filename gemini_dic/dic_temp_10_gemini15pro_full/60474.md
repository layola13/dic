eject:/ɪˈdʒekt/| v.|弹出；驱逐；喷射|To force or throw something out, especially from inside something else.  The pilot ejected from the failing aircraft. 飞行员从失灵的飞机中弹射出来。|近义词：expel, oust, remove, emit|反义词:insert, inject, admit|verb

eject:/ɪˈdʒekt/| n.|弹射；排出物|The act of ejecting something or something that has been ejected. The eject mechanism failed. 弹射装置失灵了。|近义词：emission, expulsion|反义词:insertion, injection|noun
