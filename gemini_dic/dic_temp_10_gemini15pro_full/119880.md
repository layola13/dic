militantnesses:/ˈmɪlɪtəntnɪsɪz/
n.
好战性，战斗性
The state or quality of being militant.
The militantnesses of the two opposing factions made negotiation nearly impossible.  这两个对立派系的好战性使得谈判几乎不可能。
近义词: aggressiveness, belligerence, combativeness
反义词: pacifism, peacefulness, gentleness
词性: Noun


Etymology:
From militant + -ness.  Militant comes from Latin *militantem (nominative militans), present participle of militare "to serve as a soldier," from miles (genitive militis) "soldier."
