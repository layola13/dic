drugget:/ˈdrʌɡɪt/| n.|粗毛地毯；粗麻布|A coarse woolen fabric, used for floor coverings, curtains, etc. |例句：She bought a cheap drugget to cover the worn floorboards. 她买了一块廉价的粗麻布来盖住磨损的地板。

例句：The drugget was stained and faded. 这块粗毛地毯已经褪色并且染上了污渍。|近义词：carpet, rug, matting|反义词:(none, as it refers to a specific material not a concept like expensive/cheap)|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French "droguet," a coarse woolen fabric, from "drogue," meaning "trash," "inferior merchandise."  Ultimately of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old Norse "dragg," meaning "something dragged along the ground."
