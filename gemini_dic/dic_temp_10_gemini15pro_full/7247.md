anglo-norman:/ˈæŋɡloʊ ˈnɔːrmən/
adjective|盎格鲁-诺曼的|Relating to the variety of French used by the Normans in England, especially from the 12th to the 14th centuries.  It influenced the development of Middle English.|The anglo-norman language had a significant impact on English vocabulary.|近义词:Norman French|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps Old English or Anglo-Saxon)|词性:adjective
