paraplegia:/ˌpærəˈpliːdʒə/ | n. | 截瘫，下身麻痹 | Paralysis of the lower half of the body, involving both legs, usually caused by damage to the spinal cord. |  He suffered paraplegia after a motorcycle accident. 他在一场摩托车事故后下肢瘫痪。 |  The doctor explained the challenges of living with paraplegia. 医生解释了截瘫生活的挑战。 | 近义词: paralysis, palsy | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider: mobility, able-bodiedness) | 词性: noun
