meanie: /ˈmiːni/ | n. | 坏蛋，卑鄙小人 | A meanie is a person who is unkind, cruel, or nasty. |  He's such a meanie, always teasing his little sister. 他真是个坏蛋，总是捉弄他妹妹。

| Example: Don't be such a meanie! 别这么刻薄！| Synonyms: bully, jerk, cruel person | Antonyms: kind person, nice person | noun
