dont:/doʊnt/ (US), /dəʊnt/ (UK)| contraction |不要，别| Contraction of "do not". Used to form the negative of the present tense of the verb "do". | Don't touch that!  别碰那个！|  Don't worry, be happy.  别担心，要开心。 | synonyms: avoid, refrain from | antonyms: do, allow | verb (auxiliary)

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I don't like spinach. 我不喜欢菠菜。
They don't go to school on Sundays. 他们星期天不上学。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I'm not working on that project anymore. 我不再做那个项目了。（用"not"而不是"don't"）
She isn't playing the piano right now. 她现在没在弹钢琴。（用"isn't"而不是"don't"）

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I haven't finished my homework yet. 我还没完成我的作业。
They haven't seen that movie. 他们还没看过那部电影。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I haven't been feeling well lately. 我最近感觉不太好。（用"haven't been"而不是"don't"）
She hasn't been sleeping well. 她最近一直睡不好。（用"hasn't been"而不是"don't"）

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I didn't go to the party last night. 我昨晚没去参加派对。
They didn't know the answer. 他们不知道答案。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I wasn't working when you called. 你打电话时我没在工作。（用"wasn't"而不是"don't"）
She wasn't playing the piano at that time. 她那时没在弹钢琴。（用"wasn't"而不是"don't"）

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I hadn't finished my homework before dinner. 晚饭前我还没完成作业。
They hadn't seen that movie before. 他们之前没看过那部电影。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I hadn't been feeling well before the trip. 旅行前我感觉不太好。（用"hadn't been"而不是"don't"）
She hadn't been sleeping well for weeks. 她好几周都没睡好了。（用"hadn't been"而不是"don't"）

PHRASE->
Don't worry.  别担心。

注意事项:  "Don't" specifically contracts "do not."  Other negative contractions like "isn't," "aren't," "wasn't," "weren't," "hasn't," "haven't," "hadn't," "won't," etc., are used for other tenses and forms of the verb "to be" and other auxiliary verbs.  The examples provided illustrate this distinction.
