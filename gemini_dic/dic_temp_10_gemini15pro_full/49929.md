denazification:/diːˌnɑːtsɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
去纳粹化
The systematic removal of Nazi ideology and influence from German society and institutions after World War II.
After the war, Germany underwent a period of denazification. 战后，德国经历了一段去纳粹化的时期。

Synonyms: purging, cleansing, purification
Antonyms: Nazification
