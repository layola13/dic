cooer:/ˈkuːər/| n.|咕咕叫的人/动物; 温柔低语的人|A person or animal that coos, especially a dove or pigeon; someone who speaks softly and lovingly.|The cooer in the dovecote announced the dawn.  鸽舍里的咕咕叫的鸽子宣告了黎明的到来。|近义词：dove, pigeon, murmurer, whisperer|反义词: shouter, screamer|词性:noun
Etymology: From the verb "coo" + "-er" (agent suffix), ultimately from imitative origin.  来自动词"coo" + "-er" (施动者后缀)，最终源于拟声词。
