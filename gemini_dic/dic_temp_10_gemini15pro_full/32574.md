chirognomy: /ˌkaɪˈrɒɡnəmi/ /ˌkaɪˈrɑːɡnəmi/
noun
手相术；手相学
The study of the hand as a means of indicating character and foretelling the future.
例句：She consulted a chirognomist to have her fortune told through chirognomy. 她咨询了一位手相学家，想通过手相术来预测自己的命运。

例句：Chirognomy is considered a pseudoscience by many. 手相术被许多人认为是伪科学。

近义词：palmistry, hand-reading
反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially scientific analysis of personality)
词性: noun
