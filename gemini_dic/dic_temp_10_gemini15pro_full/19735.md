billage:/ˈbɪlɪdʒ/ | n. | 村庄; 村庄的整体; 村庄的范围 | The billage encompasses the entire community within its boundaries. 这个村庄包含了它边界内的整个社区。|  The billage held a festival to celebrate the harvest.  这个村庄举办了一个节日来庆祝丰收。 | synonyms: village, hamlet, settlement | antonyms: city, metropolis | noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *billage, from Old French *billage, from bille (“tree trunk, log”), ultimately from Gaulish *bilios (“tree”).  Related to French billot (“log, block of wood”). The sense shifted from "a collection of logs" to "a collection of houses."
