formularistic:/ˌfɔːrmjʊləˈrɪstɪk/
adjective
公式化的；刻板的
Characterized by or given to the use of set forms or formulas, especially in an unimaginative or uninspired way.
His writing style is dry and formularistic. 他的写作风格枯燥且公式化。
近义词: formulaic, stereotyped, clichéd, conventional, unoriginal
反义词: original, creative, imaginative, innovative
