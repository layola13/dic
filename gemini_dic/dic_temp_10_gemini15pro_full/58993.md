duuk:/duːk/ | n. |  duke | A nobleman of the highest hereditary rank, especially in continental Europe. | The Duke of Wellington was a famous British general.  威灵顿公爵是一位著名的英国将军。 | 近义词:  noble, lord, peer | 反义词: commoner | 词性: 名词

词源: From Old French duc, from Latin dux (“leader, general”), from ducere (“to lead”).
