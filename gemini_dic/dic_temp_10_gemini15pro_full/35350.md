co-favourite:/kəʊˈfeɪvərɪt/
n.
共同的最受欢迎的人/物
One of two or more participants considered equally likely to win.
例句：The two horses are co-favourites for the race.  这两匹马是这场比赛的共同热门。

例句：She and her teammate are co-favourites to win the gold medal. 她和她的队友是夺得金牌的共同热门。
近义词：joint favourite, equal favourite
反义词：underdog, outsider
词性：名词
