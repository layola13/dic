gauche:/ɡoʊʃ/ (US), /ɡəʊʃ/ (UK)| adj.|笨拙的，不善社交的，粗鲁的|Lacking social grace; awkward and clumsy in social situations; tactless.  Origin: French, literally 'left' (considered the clumsy or sinister side).|She made a gauche remark about his weight. 她对他的体重说了一句不得体的话。|synonyms: awkward, clumsy, inept, tactless, uncouth|antonyms: graceful, elegant, sophisticated, tactful, polished|adjective

gauche:/ɡoʊʃ/ (US), /ɡəʊʃ/ (UK)| n.|笨拙，不善交际|Awkwardness or clumsiness in social behavior.|Her gauche was evident when she spilled wine on the host.  当她把酒洒在主人身上时，她的笨拙显而易见。|synonyms: awkwardness, clumsiness, ineptitude|antonyms: grace, elegance, sophistication|noun
