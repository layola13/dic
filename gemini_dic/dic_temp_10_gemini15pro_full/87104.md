heraldists:/ˈhɛrəldɪsts/| n.|纹章学家；纹章官；|Experts in heraldry, they are skilled in the design, identification, and interpretation of coats of arms and other heraldic symbols.|The heraldists authenticated the family's claim to the ancient coat of arms. 纹章学家证实了该家族对古代盾形纹章的所有权。

The heraldists debated the significance of the griffin in the crest. 纹章学家们争论着狮鹫在徽章中的意义。|Synonyms:  heralds, armorists|Antonyms: None|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *heraldiste (modern French héraldiste), from heralt "herald" + -iste.  Ultimately from Frankish *harja-waldaz "army commander."
