haematoblast:/ˌhiːmætəʊˈblɑːst/ /ˌhemætəˈblæst/| n. |血母细胞|A primitive cell, especially in the bone marrow, from which all blood cells arise.|The haematoblast differentiates into various types of blood cells. 血母细胞分化成各种类型的血细胞。|Synonyms:  hemocytoblast, blood cell precursor|Antonyms: N/A|noun
