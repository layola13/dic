cultivators:/ˈkʌltɪveɪtərz/
n.
耕耘者，栽培者，培养者
A person or machine that cultivates land.  A person who promotes or improves the growth of something.
The cultivators prepared the fields for planting. 耕作者们整理田地准备播种。
Modern cultivators can cover vast areas of land quickly. 现代耕耘机可以快速地耕作大片土地。
farmers, growers, gardeners
destroyers
