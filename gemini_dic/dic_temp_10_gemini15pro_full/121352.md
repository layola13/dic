misgoverns:/ˌmɪsˈɡʌvərnz/
verb
统治不善，管理不当
To govern badly or wrongly.
The king misgoverns his people and taxes them unfairly. 国王对人民施政不善，课以不公平的税收。
The company was misgoverned and eventually went bankrupt.  这家公司管理不善，最终破产了。
mismanage, misrule
govern well, administer effectively
词性:动词
