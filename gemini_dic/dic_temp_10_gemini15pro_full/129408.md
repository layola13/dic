nominalizes:/ˈnɑːmɪnəˌlaɪzɪz/ | v. | 名词化，将…变成名词 |  The process of turning a verb or adjective into a noun.  For example, "failure" is a nominalization of the verb "to fail." | The report nominalizes the act of failing, referring to it as "failure" rather than saying "the student failed." | 报告将失败的行为名词化，称之为“失败”而不是说“学生失败了”。 |  nouns, verbal nouns |  - | verb
nominalization:/ˌnɑːmɪnələˈzeɪʃn/ | n. | 名词化 | The act or process of forming a noun from a verb or adjective. | The frequent use of nominalization can make writing dense and difficult to understand. | 频繁使用名词化会使写作显得冗长且难以理解。 |  nouning |  - | noun
