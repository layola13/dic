norm:/nɔːrm/ | n. | 规范，标准，准则 | A standard or pattern, especially of social behavior, that is typical or expected. | The social norms of the community dictate appropriate behavior. 这个社区的社会规范规定了适当的行为。 | synonyms: standard, rule, criterion, convention | antonyms: anomaly, aberration, exception | noun

norm:/nɔːrm/ | n. | 平均数，平均水平 | An average level or standard. |  Rainfall this year has been above the norm. 今年的降雨量高于平均水平。 | synonyms: average, mean, median | antonyms: extreme, outlier | noun
