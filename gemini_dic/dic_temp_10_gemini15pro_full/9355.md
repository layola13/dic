antiprostitution:/ˌæntiˌprɒstɪˈtjuːʃən/ | adj. | 反卖淫的，反对卖淫的 | Opposed to prostitution; advocating the abolition of prostitution. | The organization launched an antiprostitution campaign.  该组织发起了一场反卖淫运动。| Synonyms: abolitionist (in relation to prostitution) | Antonyms: pro-prostitution | Adjective

Etymology->

From anti- + prostitution.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to."  "Prostitution" comes from the Latin word "prostituere" meaning "to expose publicly, offer for sale."
