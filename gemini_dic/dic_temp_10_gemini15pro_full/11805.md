arranging: /əˈreɪndʒɪŋ/
verb (present participle) | 安排，整理 | The act of putting things in order or planning something. | I am arranging the flowers in a vase. 我正在把花插在花瓶里. | organizing, planning, coordinating | disorganizing, messing up
verb | 动词

arranging: /əˈreɪndʒɪŋ/
adjective | 安排的，整理的 |  Describes something that involves putting things in order.  |  He has an arranging mind. 他有条理的思维。  | ordering, organizing, systematizing |  disordered, chaotic
adjective | 形容词


Etymology: From Old French "arengier" (to put in a rank or row), from "reng" (rank, row), ultimately from Frankish "*hring" (ring, circle).
