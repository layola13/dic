longitudinal:/ˌlɒndʒɪˈtjuːdɪnl/ /ˌlɑːndʒɪˈtuːdɪnl/| adj.|纵向的，经度的|Extending lengthwise; in the direction of the length.  Relating to longitude.|例句：A longitudinal study followed the group of participants for five years.  一项纵向研究对这组参与者进行了五年的跟踪。|近义词：lengthwise, axial|反义词：transverse, latitudinal|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *longitudinalis, from Latin longitūdō (“length”).
