eurhythmics:/jʊˈrɪðmɪks/
n.
韵律体操；韵律教学法
A system of musical instruction and physical movement, often used in early childhood education, that aims to develop a child's sense of rhythm, coordination, and musical expression.
Children benefit greatly from eurhythmics classes, developing their musicality and coordination.  孩子们从韵律体操课程中受益匪浅，培养了他们的音乐性和协调性。
Eurhythmics helps children express themselves through movement.  韵律体操帮助孩子们通过运动表达自己。

近义词:rhythmics, music education, movement education
反义词:(No direct antonym)
词性:noun
