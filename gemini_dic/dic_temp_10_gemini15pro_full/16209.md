bannerlike:/ˈbænərlaɪk/
adjective
像旗帜的；横幅状的
Resembling a banner; having the shape or appearance of a banner.
The clouds stretched across the sky in bannerlike formations. 云彩像旗帜一样在空中延伸。
The protest signs were bannerlike, long and rectangular. 抗议标语像横幅一样，又长又方。
近义词:flaglike, streamerlike
反义词:small, compact
词性:adjective
