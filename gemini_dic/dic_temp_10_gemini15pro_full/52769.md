dilator:/ˈdaɪˌleɪtər/ /ˈdɪleɪtər/| n.|扩张器,扩张肌|A tool or device used to stretch or enlarge an opening or passage in the body.|The surgeon used a dilator to widen the patient's artery.  外科医生使用扩张器来扩大病人的动脉。|近义词：expander, stretcher|反义词:constrictor|noun

dilate:/daɪˈleɪt/ /dɪˈleɪt/| v.|扩张,扩大|To make or become wider, larger, or more open.|The pupils of her eyes dilated in the dark.  她的瞳孔在黑暗中扩大了。|近义词：expand, widen, enlarge|反义词:contract, constrict|verb
