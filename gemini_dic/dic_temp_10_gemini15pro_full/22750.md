boroughmongery: /ˈbʌrəˌmʌŋɡəri/
n.
操纵选区，买卖议席
The manipulation of borough elections for political advantage, especially by bribery or other corrupt practices.
In the 19th century, boroughmongery was a widespread problem in British politics.  19世纪，操纵选区是英国政治中普遍存在的问题。
Rotten boroughs were a key element of boroughmongery, allowing wealthy individuals to control parliamentary seats.  腐败选区是操纵选区的一个关键因素，它允许富人控制议会席位。
Synonyms: corruption, bribery, graft, political manipulation
Antonyms: fair elections, democracy, political integrity
词性: noun
