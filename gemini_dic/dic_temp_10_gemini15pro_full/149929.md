palliate: /ˈpæliˌeɪt/ | v. | 减轻，缓和（痛苦或疾病）; 掩饰，辩解（罪行等） | to make a serious illness or problem seem less serious, without completely removing it; to try to excuse or soften the effect of (something bad) |  The doctor tried to palliate the patient's pain with morphine. 医生试图用吗啡来减轻病人的疼痛。

The government tried to palliate the public's anger over the scandal. 政府试图平息公众对丑闻的愤怒。 | 近义词: alleviate, ease, mitigate, soothe, relieve, extenuate, excuse | 反义词: aggravate, exacerbate, worsen, intensify | 词性: 动词
