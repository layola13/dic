galleylike:/ˈɡæliˌlaɪk/
adjective
像船上厨房的；狭长拥挤的
Resembling a ship's galley, especially in being long and narrow.
The apartment was galleylike, with a tiny kitchen at one end. 这间公寓像船上厨房一样，一头有个小厨房。
The corridor was long and galleylike. 走廊又长又像船舱的厨房。
similar: cramped, narrow, confined
opposite: spacious, roomy
词性: 形容词
