broaching:/ˈbroʊtʃɪŋ/| n. v.|钻孔；开始讨论；开瓶|The act of piercing, especially with a broach; The act of beginning to talk about a difficult subject.|例句：The broaching of the subject made everyone uncomfortable. 开始讨论这个话题让每个人都不舒服。|近义词：introducing, raising, beginning, piercing, tapping|反义词:concealing, avoiding, closing|词性:noun, verb

broaching:/ˈbroʊtʃɪŋ/| n. v.|开始讨论；提出|The act of beginning to talk about a difficult subject.|例句：He considered broaching the subject of a pay raise with his boss. 他考虑跟老板提出加薪的话题。|近义词：raising, addressing, mentioning, proposing|反义词:avoiding, ignoring, suppressing|词性:noun, verb
