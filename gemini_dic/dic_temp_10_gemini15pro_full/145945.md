overdiscreetness:/ˌoʊvərˌdɪˈskriːtnəs/
noun
过度谨慎；过分小心
Excessive discretion or caution; being too reserved or restrained in one's actions or speech.
She showed overdiscreetness in not mentioning the sensitive topic. 她对这个敏感话题闭口不谈，表现得过于谨慎。
Her overdiscreetness prevented her from expressing her true feelings. 她过分的谨慎让她无法表达自己的真实感受。
Synonym: reserve, reticence, cautiousness, prudence
Antonym: indiscretion, imprudence, recklessness, boldness
词性: 名词
