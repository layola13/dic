heterophagy:/ˌhɛtərˈɒfədʒi/
n.
异体吞噬，异体消化
The process by which a cell degrades material taken up from outside the cell.
Lysosomes are involved in heterophagy, digesting external substances brought into the cell. 溶酶体参与异体吞噬，消化带入细胞的外来物质。
The immune system uses heterophagy to eliminate pathogens. 免疫系统利用异体吞噬来清除病原体。
近义词: autophagy (somewhat), endocytosis
反义词: None (it's a cellular process, not a concept with a direct opposite)
词性: noun

heterophagic:/ˌhɛtərəˈfædʒɪk/
adj.
异体吞噬的
Relating to or characterized by heterophagy.
Heterophagic processes are essential for cellular health. 异体吞噬过程对细胞健康至关重要。
The amoeba engulfed the food particle through a heterophagic process. 变形虫通过异体吞噬过程吞噬食物颗粒。
近义词: None (highly specific scientific term)
反义词: None
词性: adjective


词源:  From hetero- (Greek, "other," "different") + -phagy (Greek, "eating," "devouring").
