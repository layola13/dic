chromite: /ˈkroʊmaɪt/ (US) /ˈkrəʊmaɪt/ (UK)| n. |铬铁矿|A brownish-black mineral consisting of iron and chromium oxides. It is the chief ore of chromium.|Chromite is mined in South Africa and Kazakhstan.|Synonyms: chrome iron ore,  chromic iron|Antonyms: N/A|noun
