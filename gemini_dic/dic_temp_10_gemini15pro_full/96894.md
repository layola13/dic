inexpensively:/ˌɪnɪkˈspɛnsɪvli/
adverb
便宜地，经济地
In a way that does not cost a lot of money.
We ate inexpensively at a local cafe. 我们在一家当地咖啡馆吃了一顿便宜的饭。
She furnished her apartment inexpensively with secondhand furniture. 她用二手家具便宜地布置了她的公寓。
Synonyms: cheaply, affordably, economically
Antonyms: expensively, lavishly
词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + expensive + -ly (adverbial suffix).  The word "expensive" comes from Old French "espens" meaning "cost, expense," ultimately from Latin "expendere" meaning "to weigh out, pay out."
