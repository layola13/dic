dogsbody:/ˈdɒɡzˌbɒdi/ (UK) /ˈdɔɡzˌbɑːdi/ (US)| n. |杂役，苦工，打杂的人|A person who is given all the boring or unpleasant jobs to do.|例句：He was treated like the office dogsbody, fetching coffee and making copies for everyone. 他被当作办公室的杂役，为大家取咖啡和复印文件。

例句：She resented being the family dogsbody, always having to do the chores while everyone else relaxed. 她讨厌成为家里的苦力，总是要做家务，而其他人却在休息。|近义词：gofer, drudge, factotum, minion|反义词:boss, manager, supervisor|词性:名词
