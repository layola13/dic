infrastructurability:/ɪnfrəˌstrʌktʃərəˈbɪləti/
n.
可基础设施化；基础设施建设能力
The capability of something to be developed with infrastructure.  The ease with which infrastructure can be built or established in a particular area or for a particular purpose.
The infrastructurability of the remote region posed a significant challenge to the development project.  这个偏远地区的可基础设施化程度对发展项目构成了重大挑战。

The government is exploring the infrastructurability of high-speed rail in the country. 政府正在探索该国高铁的可基础设施化程度。
近义词:  developability, feasibility
反义词:  undevelopability
