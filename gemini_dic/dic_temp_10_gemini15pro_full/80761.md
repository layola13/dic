gossat:/ˈɡɒsɪp/ | n. | 流言蜚语，闲话；爱说长道短的人 | idle talk or rumor, especially about the personal or private affairs of others; a person who habitually reveals personal or sensational facts about others | She's such a gossat, always spreading rumors. 她真是个爱说闲话的人，总是散布谣言。 | synonyms: gossip, gossiper, rumormonger | antonyms: reticent person, quiet person | noun
