bantam:/ˈbæntəm/| n. adj.|矮脚鸡，小型，好斗的|A small, aggressive breed of chicken. Also used figuratively to describe a small but spirited person or thing.|例句：He struts around like a bantam rooster. 他像一只矮脚鸡一样昂首阔步。

例句：She's a real bantam, always ready for a fight. 她真是个小辣椒，随时准备开战。|近义词：miniature, diminutive, small, tiny, pugnacious, feisty|反义词：large, giant, huge, timid, cowardly|词性:名词，形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Bantam, formerly Banten, a port in Java, Indonesia, from where these small chickens were originally exported.
