outwhirled: /ˌaʊtˈhwɜːrld/
verb
旋转超越，胜过旋转
to whirl faster or more powerfully than
例句：The dervish outwhirled his companions in the ecstatic dance.  托钵僧在狂喜的舞蹈中旋转胜过了他的同伴们。

例句：The new turbine outwhirled the older models, generating significantly more power.  新涡轮机的旋转速度超过了旧型号，产生了更多的能量。
近义词：outspin, surpass, exceed
反义词：lag, fall behind
词性：v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + whirl.  由out- + whirl组成。
