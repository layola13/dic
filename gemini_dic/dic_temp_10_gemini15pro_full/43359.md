cranes:/kreɪnz/| n.|起重机；鹤|A tall metal structure with a long arm, used for lifting and moving heavy objects. Also, large, long-legged, long-necked birds.|The construction site used several cranes to lift steel beams.|近义词：hoists, derricks (for machines); herons, storks (for birds)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


cranes:/kreɪnz/| v.|伸长脖子看|To stretch one's neck in order to see something.|She craned her neck to see over the fence.|近义词：stretch, peer, strain|反义词: N/A|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *cran, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch kraan and German Kranich (the bird), also to Old Norse kran 'crane (bird),' probably of imitative origin.
