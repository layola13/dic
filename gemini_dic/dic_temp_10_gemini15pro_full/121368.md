misguided:/ˌmɪsˈɡaɪdɪd/
adjective
误导的；被误导的
wrong because based on bad judgment or on wrong information or beliefs.
例句：She had been given some misguided advice. 她得到了一些误导性的建议。

例句：The company's strategy was completely misguided. 该公司的战略完全是错误的。

近义词：mistaken, erroneous, wrong, incorrect, inaccurate, fallacious, unsound, unwise, ill-advised, ill-judged, imprudent, foolish
反义词：wise, sound, sensible, prudent, judicious, well-advised, well-judged

词性: adjective
