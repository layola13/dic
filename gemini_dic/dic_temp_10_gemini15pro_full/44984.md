crystallizable:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪzəbl/| adj.|可结晶的，可具体化的|Able to be crystallized or to form crystals; able to be given a definite form.|The solution was crystallizable, forming beautiful geometric shapes as it cooled.  这种溶液是可结晶的，冷却时会形成美丽的几何形状。|The plan was initially vague, but we managed to make it crystallizable.  这个计划最初很模糊，但我们设法使其具体化。|近义词：solidifiable, concretizable|反义词: amorphous, unsolidifiable|词性: adjective

crystallize: /ˈkrɪstəlaɪz/ | v. |结晶，具体化|1. To form or cause to form crystals. 2. To make something definite or clear.|The salt crystallized as the water evaporated. 随着水分蒸发，盐结晶了。|His ideas crystallized into a concrete plan. 他的想法具体化成了一个具体的计划。|近义词：solidify, concretize, materialize|反义词: dissolve, melt|词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *cristallizable*, from Old French *cristaliser* (modern French *cristalliser*), from Medieval Latin *crystallizāre*, from Latin *crystallus*, from Ancient Greek *κρύσταλλος* (krústallos, “ice, crystal”).
