icy:/ˈaɪsi/ (US), /ˈaɪsi/ (UK)| adj.|冰冷的；结冰的；冷淡的|Covered with ice; very cold; unfriendly and unemotional.|The road was icy and dangerous. 道路结冰了，很危险。|Synonyms: frosty, freezing, glacial, frigid, chilly, cold, aloof, distant, unfeeling|Antonyms: warm, hot, friendly, cordial|adjective
