animalculist:/ˌænɪˈmælkjəlɪst/
n.|微生物学家；精原论者|A person who believes in or studies animalcules, especially one who believed that a miniature preformed individual was contained within the sperm or ovum.|Leeuwenhoek, a pioneering animalculist, was among the first to observe microorganisms through a microscope. 列文虎克是一位微生物学的先驱，也是最早通过显微镜观察微生物的人之一。|近义词:microscopist|反义词: (无明确反义词)|词性: noun

词源: From Late Latin *animalculum* (diminutive of *animal*, "animal") + *-ist*.  The word reflects the early belief that sperm or ova contained tiny, fully formed organisms.
