overobjecting:/ˌoʊvərəbˈdʒektɪŋ/
| adj. | 过度反对的，过分反对的 |  Excessively raising objections; objecting too much or too often.  Characterized by excessive or unreasonable objection. |  He was constantly overobjecting to every proposal, making it difficult to reach a consensus. 他不断地过度反对每一个提议，使得很难达成共识。 | Synonyms:  hypercritical, captious, faultfinding, nitpicking, carping | Antonyms: agreeable, amenable, compliant, acquiescent | adjective

overobjecting:/ˌoʊvərəbˈdʒektɪŋ/
| n. | 过度反对，过分反对 | The act of objecting excessively or too frequently. | Her constant overobjecting became tiresome for everyone involved.  她不断的过度反对让所有参与的人都感到厌烦。 | Synonyms:  hypercriticism, captiousness, faultfinding, nitpicking, carping | Antonyms: agreement, compliance, acquiescence | noun
