necessity:/nəˈsesəti/ | n. |必需品；必要性；必然性|Something that is needed or required; the condition of being needed; inevitability.|例句：Food is a necessity of life. 食物是生活的必需品。|近义词：need, requirement, essential, requisite|反义词:luxury, option, nonessential|词性:noun

necessity:/nəˈsesəti/ | n. |必需品；必要性；必然性|Something that must be done or had; an unavoidable circumstance or condition; something that is necessary or indispensable.|例句：He felt the necessity of leaving at once. 他感到必须马上离开。|近义词:obligation, must, essential|反义词:choice, option, luxury|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *necessite*, from Latin necessitas ("necessity"), from necesse ("necessary").
