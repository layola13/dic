argu:/ˈɑːrɡjuː/
词性:  This is not a valid English word.  It seems to be a truncated form, possibly of "argue."

If you meant "argue":

/ˈɑːrɡjuː/ (British) /ˈɑːrɡjuː/ (American)
词性: verb
中文翻译: 争论，辩论，主张，说服
英文解析: To give reasons or cite evidence in support of an idea, action, or theory, typically with the aim of persuading others to share one's view.  Can also mean to quarrel angrily or engage in a heated dispute.
例句：
They argued about the best course of action.  他们争论最佳行动方案。

He argued that the evidence was insufficient. 他辩称证据不足。

近义词: debate, dispute, contend, reason, plead
反义词: agree, concede, concur
