downwind:/ˌdaʊnˈwɪnd/| adj. adv.|顺风的，在下风处的；顺风地，在下风处|In a direction downwind; with the wind. |例句：The birds were flying downwind. 鸟儿们顺风飞翔。|近义词：leeward|反义词: upwind, windward|词性:形容词, 副词

downwind:/ˌdaʊnˈwɪnd/| n.|顺风处，下风处|The direction downwind. |例句：They live downwind of the factory. 他们住在工厂的下风处。|近义词：leeward|反义词: upwind, windward|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "down" + "wind".  "Down" indicating direction and "wind" referring to the movement of air.  The combination signifies a direction or position in line with the wind's flow.
