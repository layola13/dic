ingratiate:/ɪnˈɡreɪʃieɪt/ |v. | 讨好；巴结；迎合 |To bring oneself into favor with someone by flattering or trying to please them. |He tried to ingratiate himself with the boss by offering to work overtime. 他主动加班，试图讨好老板。

|Synonyms: flatter, charm, win over, curry favor with|Antonyms: alienate, offend, repel|verb
