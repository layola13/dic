misintention:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɛnʃən/
n.
恶意；不良企图
The act of having a wrong or improper intention; ill will.
He acted with misintention, hoping to sabotage the project. 他心怀恶意，希望破坏这个项目。

The lawyer argued that the defendant's actions were a result of misintention, not negligence. 律师辩称，被告的行为是恶意而非疏忽造成的。
近义词:malice, malevolence, ill will, bad faith
反义词:benevolence, goodwill, good faith
