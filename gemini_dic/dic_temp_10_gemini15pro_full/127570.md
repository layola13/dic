nectareous:/nɛkˈtɛəriəs/
adjective
甘甜的；花蜜的
Resembling nectar; sweet and delicious.
The nectareous juice of the ripe mango was a delight.  成熟芒果的甘甜汁液令人愉悦。
近义词:sweet, delicious, honeyed, sugary
反义词:bitter, sour, acrid
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *nectareus, from nectar ("nectar") + -eus (adjective suffix). Ultimately from Greek νέκταρ (néktar, "drink of the gods").
