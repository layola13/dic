aeriality:/ˌɛəriˈælɪti/
noun
空中性；轻盈；飘逸
The quality of being aerial, especially in performance; lightness or grace of movement.
The gymnast's aeriality was breathtaking.  这体操运动员的空中技巧令人叹为观止。
Her performance was full of aeriality and grace. 她的表演充满了轻盈和优雅。
synonyms: lightness, grace, airiness, buoyancy, nimbleness
antonyms: heaviness, clumsiness, awkwardness
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *aerialitas, from Latin aerius ("of the air") + -itas (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality).

USAGE->
The dancer's aeriality made her seem to float across the stage. = 舞者的轻盈使她看起来像漂浮在舞台上。

The aeriality of the acrobats' performance was astonishing. = 杂技演员表演的空中技巧令人惊叹。


PHRASE->
