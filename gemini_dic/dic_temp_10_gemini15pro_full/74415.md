franchised:/ˈfræntʃaɪzd/
adj.
特许的，获得特许经营权的
Granted a franchise; authorized to sell or distribute a company's goods or services in a particular area.
McDonald's is a franchised restaurant chain.  麦当劳是一个特许经营的连锁餐厅。
近义词：licensed, authorized
反义词：independent, unaffiliated
词性: adjective

词源:  From the Old French word "franchir," meaning "to free," referring to the granting of freedom or exemption from certain obligations.  源于古法语单词 "franchir"，意为“解放”，指的是授予免除某些义务的自由或豁免。
