ghazal:/ˈɡʌzəl/ | n. | غزل (ghazal) | A lyric poem with a fixed number of verses and a repeated rhyme and refrain, common in Arabic, Persian, Urdu, and other Near Eastern literatures. Each line of the ghazal must have the same meter, and the first, second, and every other line must end in the same rhyme. The last verse includes the poet's signature (takhallus).|  She recited a beautiful ghazal that captivated the audience. 她朗诵了一首优美的 غزل，深深吸引了观众。

He was inspired to write a ghazal after reading the works of Rumi. 读了鲁米的诗歌后，他受到启发写了一首 غزل。| synonym: lyric, ode, poem | antonym: prose, story, novel | noun
