euphorbin:/juːˈfɔːrbɪn/
n.
大戟毒素
A toxic protein found in certain spurges (Euphorbia species).
Euphorbin is a potent toxin that can cause severe skin irritation. 大戟毒素是一种强效毒素，会导致严重的皮肤刺激。
Certain species of Euphorbia contain euphorbin, which can be harmful if ingested.  某些种类的大戟属植物含有大戟毒素，如果摄入会对人体有害。
近义词:toxin, poison
反义词:antidote, remedy
词性:noun
