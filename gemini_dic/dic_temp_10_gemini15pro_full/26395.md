by-the-same-token:/baɪ ðə seɪm ˈtoʊkən/| adverbial phrase| 同样地；出于同样的原因|Indicates that something else is also true, especially as a result of the reason just given.  It's used to introduce a similar or related point that supports the preceding statement. |She's extremely intelligent, and, by the same token, very ambitious. 她非常聪明，同样地，也很有野心。

He's a diligent worker and, by the same token, a loyal friend. 他是一个勤奋的工人，同样也是一个忠诚的朋友。|近义词：similarly, likewise, in the same way, equally, for the same reason|反义词：conversely, on the other hand, in contrast|词性: adverbial phrase
