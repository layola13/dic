a-man-color: /ˈɑːmən ˈkʌlər/ | adj. | 杏仁色 | A light yellowish-brown color, like that of an almond. | The walls were painted a warm a-man-color. 墙壁被漆成了温暖的杏仁色。 | Her dress was an elegant a-man-color. 她的裙子是优雅的杏仁色。 | 近义词: almond, beige, cream, off-white | 反义词: black, dark brown | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From almond + color.  "Almond" comes from Old French *almande, ultimately from Arabic *al-luwz. "Color" comes from Old French colour, from Latin color.
