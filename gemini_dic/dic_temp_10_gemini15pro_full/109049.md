legalised: /ˈliːɡəlaɪzd/ | v. |使合法化；合法化|To make something legal; to give legal status to something that was previously illegal. | Marijuana has been legalised in some states.  一些州已经将大麻合法化了。| The government legalised gambling. 政府使赌博合法化了。 | legalize, legitimize | illegalize, criminalize, prohibit, ban | Verb
legalized: /ˈliːɡəlaɪzd/ | adj. |合法化的|Made legal; having been given legal status. | The sale of legalised marijuana is strictly regulated. 合法大麻的销售受到严格监管。 |  The newly legalized industry is booming. 新近合法化的产业正在蓬勃发展。 | legal, legitimate | illegal, illicit, prohibited | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From legal + -ize + -ed.  "Legal" comes from Old French "legal," ultimately from Latin "lex" (law). "-ize" is a suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or become." "-ed" is the past participle suffix.
