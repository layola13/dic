inciting:/ɪnˈsaɪtɪŋ/
adjective
煽动的；刺激的；激起……的
encouraging or urging (someone) to do something violent, illegal, or wrong
例句：His inciting words led to a riot. 他的煽动性言论引发了一场暴乱。

例句：The inciting speech fueled the protesters' anger.  这煽动性的演讲激起了抗议者的愤怒。

近义词：inflammatory, provocative, agitating, instigating
反义词：calming, soothing, pacifying, appeasing
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English: from Old French enciter, from en- ‘in’ + citer ‘summon, urge,’ from Latin citare ‘rouse, summon.’
