alohas:/əˈloʊhəz/| n.|再见，祝福|A Hawaiian word used to express affection, hello, or goodbye.  It embodies the spirit of love, peace, and compassion.|Example: We exchanged alohas as we parted ways. 我们分别时互相道别(祝福)。|近义词：goodbyes, farewells|反义词: greetings, hellos|noun
