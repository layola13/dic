muckier:/ˈmʌkiər/| adj.|更脏的，更污秽的|Comparative adjective of 'mucky'.  Means more dirty, messy, or muddy.|The path was much muckier after the rain. 雨后，小路变得更加泥泞了。|近义词：dirtier, messier, muddier|反义词:cleaner, tidier|adjective

詞源: From 'muck' + '-ier'. 'Muck' comes from Middle English 'muk', of Scandinavian origin.
