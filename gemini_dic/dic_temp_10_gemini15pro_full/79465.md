gloaming:/ˈɡloʊmɪŋ/| n.|暮色，黄昏|The gloaming is the time of day between daylight and darkness.  黄昏是白天和黑夜之间的时段。|例句：The birds were singing their last songs of the day in the gloaming.  鸟儿们在暮色中唱着一天的最后一首歌。

例句：As the sun set, the forest was enveloped in the soft gloaming.  随着太阳落山，森林笼罩在柔和的暮色中。


近义词：twilight, dusk, eventide|反义词:dawn, daybreak|词性:名词
