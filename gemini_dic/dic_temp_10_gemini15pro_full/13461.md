atrium:/ˈeɪtriəm/ (US), /ˈeɪtriəm/ (UK)| n. |中庭，心房|A large open space inside a building, often with a glass roof, or the main upper chamber of the heart.|例句：The hotel has a beautiful atrium filled with plants.  这家酒店有一个美丽的充满植物的中庭。

例句：Blood flows from the veins into the right atrium of the heart. 血液从静脉流入心脏的右心房。|近义词：courtyard, lobby, hall (for building); chamber (for heart)|反义词:ventricle (for heart)|词性:noun
