manufacturable:/ˌmænjuˈfæktʃərəbl/
adjective
可制造的；可生产的
Capable of being manufactured.
This new material is lightweight and easily manufacturable.  这种新材料重量轻且易于制造。
The design needs to be manufacturable at a reasonable cost.  该设计需要以合理的成本制造。
synonyms: producible, makeable, fabricable
antonyms: unproducible, non-manufacturable
