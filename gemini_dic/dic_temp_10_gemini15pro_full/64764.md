esculin:/ˈɛskjʊlɪn/ | n. | 七叶苷;七叶灵 | A coumarin glycoside found in the bark of the horse chestnut tree, used as an anti-inflammatory and to reduce capillary fragility.  |  Esculin is used in some culture media to aid in the identification of certain bacteria.  七叶苷在某些培养基中用于辅助鉴定某些细菌。 |  Horse chestnut extract, containing esculin, is sometimes used in supplements for vein health.  含有七叶苷的七叶树提取物有时用于静脉健康的补充剂中。 |  aesculin, esculoside |  None | noun
