ichorous:/ˈaɪkərəs/| adj.|恶臭的；污秽的|Having an offensive smell; foul.|The ichorous smell from the basement was overwhelming. 地下室里散发出的恶臭令人难以忍受。|Synonyms: fetid, putrid, noisome|Antonyms: fragrant, aromatic|adjective
