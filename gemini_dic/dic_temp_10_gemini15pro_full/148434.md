overstimulated: /ˌoʊvərˈstɪmjuleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过度刺激的；过度兴奋的 | Having received too much stimulation, resulting in a feeling of being overwhelmed or agitated. |  The child was overstimulated after spending the day at the amusement park.  这个孩子在游乐园玩了一天后过度兴奋了。|  overstimulated, overwhelmed, overexcited | understimulated, bored, calm | adjective

overstimulate: /ˌoʊvərˈstɪmjuleɪt/ | vt. | 过度刺激；使过度兴奋 | To stimulate excessively; to bombard with too much sensory input. | The flashing lights and loud music overstimulated the baby. 闪爍的灯光和嘈杂的音乐过度刺激了婴儿。 | overexcite, overwhelm | understimulate, soothe | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + stimulate.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much." "Stimulate" comes from the Latin word "stimulare," meaning "to goad, prick, or urge."
