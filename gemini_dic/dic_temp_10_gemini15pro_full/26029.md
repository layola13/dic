bushel:/ˈbʊʃəl/| n.|蒲式耳（容量单位）|A unit of dry capacity equal to 4 pecks or 32 quarts or approximately 35.2 liters.  A large quantity of something.|The farmer harvested 100 bushels of wheat. 农民收获了100蒲式耳小麦。|Synonyms: peck, dry quart|Antonyms: N/A|noun
