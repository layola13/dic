esoteric:/ˌesəˈtɛrɪk/ | adj. | 秘传的；深奥的；难理解的 | Intended for or likely to be understood by only a small number of people with a specialized knowledge or interest. |  例句：He was drawn into the esoteric world of Japanese tea ceremony. 他被吸引进了深奥的日本茶道世界。

例句：The book is full of esoteric philosophical discussions. 这本书充满了深奥的哲学讨论。 | 近义词：abstruse, arcane, obscure, recondite | 反义词：exoteric, common, popular, simple | 词性: adjective
