outter:/ˈaʊtər/ | adj. | 外面的，外部的 | Situated on or toward the outside; external.  This word is often misspelled as "outter," the correct spelling is "outer." |  The outer layer of the onion is papery. 洋葱的外层像纸一样薄。

The outer suburbs are less densely populated.  郊区外围人口密度较小。 | 近义词: external, outside, exterior | 反义词: inner, inside, interior | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Old English *ūtera, comparative of *ūt (out).
