cross-promote:/ˌkrɔːs prəˈmoʊt/ | v. |交叉推广；互相宣传 | To market (two or more products) together, especially those of different companies.  | The two companies agreed to cross-promote each other's products. 这两家公司同意互相推广对方的产品。 |  We cross-promoted our new book with a popular website. 我们与一个热门网站交叉推广了我们的新书。 | 近义词: co-market, jointly promote | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "isolate marketing" or "separate promotion") | 词性: 动词

cross-promotion:/ˌkrɔːs prəˈmoʊʃən/ | n. | 交叉推广；互相宣传 | The act of cross-promoting. |  The cross-promotion was very successful, boosting sales for both companies. 交叉推广非常成功，促进了两家公司的销售。| Their cross-promotion efforts led to a significant increase in brand awareness. 他们的交叉推广工作显著提高了品牌知名度。| 近义词: joint promotion, co-marketing | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "independent marketing") | 词性: 名词

词源:  "cross-" (meaning across, joint, or shared) + "promote" (from Latin *promotus*, past participle of *promovere* meaning "to move forward").
