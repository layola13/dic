hyperbarism: /ˈhaɪpərˌbærɪzəm/ | n. | 高压症，高压病 | A medical condition resulting from exposure to increased environmental pressure, typically greater than atmospheric pressure.  It is characterized by a variety of symptoms depending on the affected tissues and organs. | Divers can suffer from hyperbarism if they ascend too quickly. 潜水员如果上升太快，可能会患上高压症。|  Hyperbaric oxygenation therapy involves the use of hyperbaric chambers to treat certain medical conditions. 高压氧疗法使用高压氧舱来治疗某些疾病。 | 近义词: barotrauma, decompression sickness | 反义词: hypobarism | 词性: noun
