confiscated:/ˈkɒnfɪskeɪtɪd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnfɪskeɪtɪd/ (US)| adj.|被没收的，被充公的|Something that has been confiscated has been taken away from someone as a punishment or because it is illegal. |The government confiscated the criminal's assets. 政府没收了罪犯的资产。|Synonyms: seized, impounded, appropriated|Antonyms: returned, restored|Adjective


confiscate:/ˈkɒnfɪskeɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnfɪskeɪt/ (US)| vt.|没收，充公|To take something away from someone as a punishment or because it is illegal. |The police confiscated the drugs. 警察没收了毒品。|Synonyms: seize, impound, appropriate|Antonyms: return, restore|Verb
