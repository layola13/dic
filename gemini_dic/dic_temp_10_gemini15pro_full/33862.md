circumscriber:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈskraɪbər/ | n. |限定者; 限制者 |  Someone or something that circumscribes something else; that draws a line around it, limiting its power, size, or area of influence.  | The government acts as a circumscriber of individual liberties in certain cases. 政府在某些情况下充当个人自由的限制者。

The new regulations circumscribe the company's ability to expand. 新规定限制了公司扩张的能力。 | 近义词: limiter, restrictor, confiner | 反义词: expander, extender, enlarger | 词性: 名词
