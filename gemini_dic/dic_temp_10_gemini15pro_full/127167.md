naturalism:/ˈnætʃrəlɪzəm/| n.|自然主义；自然主义；博物学|A philosophical viewpoint according to which everything arises from natural properties and causes, and supernatural or spiritual explanations are excluded or discounted.  A style and theory in art and literature based on the accurate depiction of detail.|例句：Naturalism in art is the attempt to portray nature as it really is. 艺术中的自然主义是试图描绘自然的本来面目。

例句：He was a proponent of naturalism in literature. 他是文学自然主义的倡导者。|近义词：realism, materialism|反义词：idealism, spiritualism|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *naturalisme*, from *naturel* (“natural”) + *-isme* (“-ism”).  Ultimately from Latin *nātūrālis* (“natural”).
