interdicting:/ˌɪntərˈdɪktɪŋ/| v.|禁止，阻止，阻断|The government is interdicting the sale of certain drugs. 政府正在禁止某些药物的销售。|例句：The court interdicted him from seeing his children. 法院禁止他探望孩子。

例句：They were interdicting the flow of supplies to the enemy. 他们正在阻断敌方的补给线。|近义词：prohibiting, forbidding, banning, barring|反义词:allowing, permitting, authorizing|词性:动词(present participle)

词源: From Latin *interdictus*, past participle of interdicere (to forbid, prohibit), from inter- (between) + dicere (to say, speak).
