africanize:/ˈæfrɪkənaɪz/ |v. |使非洲化；赋予非洲特色|To cause to adopt or be influenced by African customs, culture, or languages.  To place under the control of Africans.|例句：The government's policy is to africanize the civil service. 政府的政策是使公务员队伍非洲化。

例句：Many formerly European-owned businesses have been africanized. 许多以前由欧洲人拥有的企业已经非洲化了。|近义词：localize, indigenize|反义词：westernize, europeanize|词性：动词
