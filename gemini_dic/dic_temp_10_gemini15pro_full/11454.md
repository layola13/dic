argument:/ˈɑːrɡjumənt/ | n. | 争论，论点，论据 | A reason or set of reasons given with the aim of persuading others that an action or idea is right or wrong. |  Example 1: They had a heated argument about politics. 他们就政治问题进行了激烈的争论。

Example 2:  His main argument was that the policy would cost too much. 他的主要论点是这项政策成本太高。| synonyms: dispute, debate, quarrel, contention | antonyms: agreement, harmony, accord | noun
