hybridists:/ˈhaɪbrɪdɪsts/
n.
杂交者，混合者
A person who produces hybrids, especially of plants or animals.
例句：The hybridists were able to create a new strain of corn. 杂交者培育出了玉米新品种。

例句：Hybridists play a crucial role in developing new varieties of flowers. 杂交者在培育花卉新品种方面发挥着至关重要的作用。

近义词：crossbreeder, hybridizer
反义词：(No direct antonym, potentially "purist" in certain contexts)
词性：noun
