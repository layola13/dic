ethernet:/ˈiːθərnɛt/ | n. | 以太网 | A system for connecting a number of computer systems to form a local area network, with protocols to control the passing of information and to avoid simultaneous transmission by two or more systems. |  例句：Our office uses ethernet for its network connection. 我们的办公室使用以太网进行网络连接。

例句：The ethernet cable connects the computer to the router.  以太网电缆将计算机连接到路由器。 | 近义词：LAN (Local Area Network) | 反义词:  None (No direct antonym for a technology) | 词性: noun


词源:  From ether (referring to the then-theoretical luminiferous aether, believed at one time to be the medium through which electromagnetic waves traveled) + net (short for network).
