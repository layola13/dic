cudden:/ˈkʌdən/| n.| 嚼过的反刍食物; 反刍物|A cudden is the portion of food that a ruminant animal regurgitates from its rumen to chew a second time.|The cow chewed its cudden contentedly. 牛心满意足地嚼着反刍的食物。|近义词:cud|反义词:None|Noun
