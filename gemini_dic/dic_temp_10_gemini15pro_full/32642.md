chisellike:/ˈtʃɪzəlˌlaɪk/| adj.|凿子般的，像凿子一样的|Resembling a chisel, especially in being sharp and beveled.  Having a sharp, beveled edge like that of a chisel.|The sculptor's hands moved with precision, using a chisellike tool to shape the stone.  雕刻家精准地移动着双手，用凿子般的工具塑造石头。|
The bird's beak was chisellike, perfect for cracking seeds.  这只鸟的喙像凿子一样，非常适合嗑种子。|近义词：sharp, pointed, beveled, wedge-shaped|反义词:blunt, rounded|词性:adjective


Etymology:
chisel (n.) + -like (suffix)

chisel (n.): from Old French cisel "chisel," from Vulgar Latin *cisellum, from Latin caesellum, diminutive of caedere "to cut"

-like (suffix): Old English -lic, from Proto-Germanic *-likan, meaning "having the form or qualities of."
