medevacking:/ˈmɛdɪvækɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
医疗后送，医疗撤离
The act or process of evacuating someone for medical reasons, typically by aircraft.
They were medevacking the wounded soldier to the nearest hospital.  他们正用飞机将受伤的士兵后送到最近的医院。
The pilot skillfully medevacked the injured hikers from the remote mountain area. 飞行员熟练地将受伤的徒步旅行者从偏远的 ​​山区用飞机送走。
Synonyms:  medical evacuation, airlifting, evacuating
Antonyms: N/A (The opposite would be something like "keeping in place" or "refusing transport," which are not direct antonyms.)
verb

medevac:/ˈmɛdɪvæk/
noun
医疗后送，医疗撤离（飞机或直升机）
An aircraft or helicopter used for medical evacuation.
The medevac landed on the hospital roof. 医疗后送直升机降落在医院屋顶上。
A medevac was dispatched to the scene of the accident. 一架医疗后送飞机被派往事故现场。
Synonyms: air ambulance, medical transport
Antonyms: N/A
noun
verb

Etymology:  A clipped form of "medical evacuation," first used in the Vietnam War.  源自“medical evacuation”（医疗后送）的缩写形式，最早用于越南战争。
