*hello*/həˈloʊ/ (US) /hɛˈləʊ/ (UK)| interjection, noun, verb| 你好；喂；表示问候|Used as a greeting or to begin a telephone conversation.  A greeting.|
例句：Hello, how are you? 你好，你好吗？

例句：Hello, is this John? 喂，是约翰吗？

近义词：hi, hey, greetings|反义词: goodbye, farewell| 词性: interjection, noun, verb

*what*/wɒt/ (UK) /wʌt/ (US)| pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection| 什么；多么|Used to ask for information specifying something.|
音标:/wɒt/ (UK) /wʌt/ (US)|
词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection|
中文翻译: 什么；多么|
英文解析: Used to ask for information specifying something.|
例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

例句：What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！

近义词：which, who|反义词: (No single antonym, context-dependent)|



USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you do for a living?  你靠什么谋生？
What does she like to eat? 她喜欢吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you doing now? 你现在在做什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? 她正在做什么晚餐？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you done today? 你今天做了什么？
What has she learned in this class? 她在这堂课学到了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has she been reading this week? 她这周一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say to him? 她对他说什么了？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? 她生病前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I arrived? 我来之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major?  她转专业前一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do tomorrow? 你明天要做什么？
What will she bring to the party? 她会带什么来参加派对？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be studying next semester? 她下学期会学什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by next month? 到下个月你将完成什么？
What will she have learned by the end of the course? 到课程结束时她将学到什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past ten years by 2030? 到2030年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
