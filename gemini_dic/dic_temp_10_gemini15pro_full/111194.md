lithologic:/ˌlɪθəˈlɒdʒɪk/
adjective
岩性的，岩石学的
Relating to the study of rocks, specifically their physical characteristics and classification.
The geologist studied the lithologic properties of the samples.  地质学家研究了样本的岩性。
The lithologic units were mapped in detail. 岩性单元被详细绘制在地图上。
synonyms: petrographic, petrological
antonyms: None. (It's a descriptive term, not a polar opposite.)
