deviationists:/ˌdiːviˈeɪʃənɪsts/
noun
离经叛道者，背离者
People who depart from an established norm or standard, especially in social or political beliefs.  Those who dissent from an accepted doctrine or course of action.
The party leader expelled the deviationists who challenged his authority.  该党领导人驱逐了挑战他权威的离经叛道者。
The deviationists were ostracized by the community for their unorthodox views.  这些离经叛道者因其非正统的观点而被社会排斥。
synonyms: dissenters, heretics, nonconformists, rebels, mavericks
antonyms: conformists, traditionalists
