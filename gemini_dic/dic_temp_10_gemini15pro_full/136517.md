nonrudimental:/ˌnɒnˌruːdɪˈmɛntəl/
adjective
非基本的，非初步的，发达的
Not rudimentary; developed beyond the basic or elementary stage.
Her knowledge of astrophysics is nonrudimental, extending far beyond the basics. 她对天体物理学的知识是非基本的，远远超出了基础知识。
The artist's skills are nonrudimental, showcasing a mastery of technique.  这位艺术家的技艺是非基本的，展现了对技巧的精通。
近义词: advanced, developed, sophisticated, complex
反义词: rudimentary, basic, elementary, simple
词性: adjective
词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "rudimental" (from Latin "rudimentum" meaning "first beginning, first attempt, ABC").
