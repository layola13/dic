half-annoyingly:/ˌhæf əˈnɔɪɪŋli/
| adv. | 半恼人地；略微令人讨厌地 |  Modifying an action in a way that is slightly irritating but not entirely unpleasant. |  He half-annoyingly pointed out my mistake. 他略带恼人地指出了我的错误。 |  mildly irritatingly, somewhat irritatingly |  pleasingly, delightfully | adverb

half-annoyingly:/ˌhæf əˈnɔɪɪŋli/
|  |  |  | She half-annoyingly hummed a tune while I was trying to concentrate. 她在我努力集中注意力的时候，略带恼人地哼着小曲。 |  slightly irritatingly | soothingly | adverb
