desynchronizes:/diːˈsɪŋkrənaɪzɪz/
verb
使不同步，失去同步
to cause (something) to no longer be synchronized; to disrupt the synchronization of (something)
例句：The intense electromagnetic pulse desynchronized the satellite's communication systems.  强烈的电磁脉冲使卫星的通信系统不同步了。

例句：Jet lag desynchronizes your internal clock. 时差会使你的生物钟紊乱。
近义词：disrupts, uncouples, misaligns
反义词：synchronizes, coordinates
词性：v.
