coinventing:/ˌkoʊɪnˈvɛntɪŋ/
verb
共同发明；联合发明
the act of inventing something together with someone else.
Marie Curie and her husband are coinventing the theory. 玛丽·居里和她丈夫共同发明了这个理论。
coinvent, create jointly, develop together, devise jointly, originate together
invent separately, develop independently
v.
