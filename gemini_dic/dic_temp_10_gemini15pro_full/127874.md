neocon:/ˈniːoʊkɒn/ | n. | 新保守主义者 | A person who advocates a return to traditional conservative values, often with an emphasis on free-market capitalism, a strong national defense, and an assertive foreign policy. |  Example:  The neocons advocated for the invasion of Iraq. 新保守主义者主张入侵伊拉克。

Example: She was labeled a neocon for her hawkish views on foreign policy. 由于她对外交政策的强硬观点，她被贴上了新保守主义者的标签。 | 近义词: conservative, right-winger | 反义词: liberal, left-winger | 词性: noun
