overcatch:/ˈoʊvərˌkætʃ/| n.|过度捕捞|The act of catching more fish than a population can replace through natural reproduction, leading to a decline in the fish population.|Overfishing, including bycatch and overcatch, is decimating fish populations worldwide. 过度捕捞，包括误捕和过捕，正在摧毁世界各地的鱼类种群。|近义词：overfishing, overexploit|反义词:underfishing|noun
