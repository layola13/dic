nonbuoyant:/ˌnɒnˈbɔɪənt/ | adj. | 非浮力的，不能浮起的 | Not able to float; not buoyant.  | The nonbuoyant material sank quickly to the bottom of the lake. 非浮力材料迅速沉到了湖底。| The anchor, being nonbuoyant, kept the boat from drifting. 锚因为不会浮起，防止了船漂流。 | 近义词: heavy, dense, sinking | 反义词: buoyant, floaty, light | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
buoyant (adjective): from Middle French boyant "floating," present participle of boier "to float," from Old French buier, from Vulgar Latin *boiare, of uncertain origin.
