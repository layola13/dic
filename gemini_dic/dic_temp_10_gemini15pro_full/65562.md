eutychian: /juːˈtɪkiən/ | adj. n. |  优提克斯派的，优提克斯派信徒 | Relating to Eutyches, a 5th-century monk who held a Christological doctrine considered heretical by the Council of Chalcedon.  A follower of Eutyches. |  He was accused of holding Eutychian views. 他被指控持有优提克斯派的观点。 | The Eutychian heresy was condemned by the church. 优提克斯的异端邪说被教会谴责。 | 近义词: monophysite | 反义词: dyophysite | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *Eutychianus, from Greek Eutychēs, the name of the founder of Eutychianism.
