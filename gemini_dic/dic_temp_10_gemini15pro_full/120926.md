miscategorizes:/ˌmɪskætəɡəˈraɪzɪz/| v. |错误分类，归错类|To categorize incorrectly; to place in a wrong category.|例句：The librarian miscategorized the new biography as fiction.  图书管理员把这本新的传记错分到小说类去了。

例句：The software miscategorizes some emails as spam.  该软件将一些电子邮件错误地归类为垃圾邮件。|近义词：misclassify, mislabel, misfile|反义词: classify correctly, categorize correctly|词性:动词
