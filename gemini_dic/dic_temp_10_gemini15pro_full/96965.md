infancy:/ˈɪnfənsi/| n.|婴儿期，幼年期|The period of a child's life from birth to about two years of age; early childhood.  A beginning or early stage of something.|The company is still in its infancy. 这家公司仍处于创业初期。|近义词：babyhood, childhood, beginnings|反义词:adulthood, maturity|词性:名词
Etymology: From Old French *enfance*, from Latin *infantia* "inability to speak, speechlessness, infancy,"  from *infans* "unable to speak, young," literally "not speaking," from *in-* "not, opposite of" (see in- (1)) + *fans* (present participle of *fari* "to speak")
