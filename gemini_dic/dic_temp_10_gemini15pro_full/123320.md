monographers:/məˈnɒɡrəfəz/
n.
专著作家；专题论文作者
Writers of monographs; writers of scholarly papers on single specialized subjects.
Several distinguished monographers have contributed to this volume.  几位著名的专著作家为这卷书撰稿。
n.
专著作者
monograph writers
There are few monographers specializing in this narrow field.  在这个狭窄的领域里，很少有专门的专著作者。

近义词:
specialists, experts, scholars
反义词:
amateurs, generalists
