explainer:/ɪkˈspleɪnər/| n. | 解释者，说明者，讲解员，解释性报道| someone or something that explains something | The museum hired an explainer to guide visitors through the exhibit.  博物馆聘请了一名讲解员引导游客参观展览。| synonyms: commentator, analyst, interpreter | antonyms: obfuscator, confuser | noun

explainer:/ɪkˈspleɪnər/| n. | 解释性文章，解释性视频| a piece of content (article, video, etc.) that explains a complex issue in a simple way |  I watched an explainer video on quantum physics. 我看了一段关于量子物理学的解释性视频。| synonyms:  exposition, elucidation, commentary | antonyms: mystification, complication | noun
