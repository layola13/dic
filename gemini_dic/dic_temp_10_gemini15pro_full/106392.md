kook:/kʊk/
noun
怪人，疯子
a strange or crazy person
He's a bit of a kook, but he's harmless. 他有点怪，但无害。

近义词:crank, eccentric, weirdo, nut, nutcase, oddball
反义词:normal person, conformist

词性:n.
