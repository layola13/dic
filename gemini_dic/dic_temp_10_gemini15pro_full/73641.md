forfeiture:/ˈfɔːrfɪtʃər/| n. |没收，丧失，罚金|The forfeiture of property is a possible penalty for certain crimes.  某些罪行的可能惩罚是财产的没收。|例句：He suffered the forfeiture of his driver's license. 他被吊销了驾驶执照。

例句：The company faces forfeiture of its operating license. 这家公司面临着营业执照被吊销的风险。

近义词：penalty, loss, confiscation|反义词: gain, reward|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *forfeture, from forfetre “to forfeit,” from for- “outside, away” + faire “to do, make” (from Latin facere “to do, make”).
