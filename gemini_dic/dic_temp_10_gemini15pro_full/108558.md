lazzaroni:/ˌlɑːtsəˈroʊni/ | n. |  流浪汉；乞丐；游手好闲的人 |  (from Italian) Originally referring to Neapolitan beggars, now used more generally to describe idle or lazy people. |  The lazzaroni of Naples were known for their colorful clothes and carefree attitude. 那不勒斯的流浪汉以其色彩鲜艳的衣服和无忧无虑的态度而闻名。|  synonyms: beggars, vagrants, idlers, loafers | antonyms: workers, diligent people | noun
