baptizers:/ˈbæptaɪzərz/
noun
施洗者
People who administer the Christian rite of baptism.
John the Baptist was one of the most famous baptizers in history. 施洗约翰是历史上最著名的施洗者之一。
The baptizers prepared the candidates for the ceremony. 施洗者让候选人准备好参加仪式。
近义词: baptist, christener
反义词: None
词性: n.

baptizer:/ˈbæptaɪzər/
noun
施洗者
A person who baptizes.
He acted as baptizer for his newborn nephew. 他为他的新生侄子施洗。
The baptizer poured water over the convert's head. 施洗者将水倒在皈依者的头上。
近义词: baptist, christener
反义词: None
词性: n.
