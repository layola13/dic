incessant:/ɪnˈsɛsənt/ | adj. | 不停的，持续不断的 | Continuing without interruption; ceaseless. | The incessant rain caused flooding.  不停的雨造成了洪水。| continuous, constant, perpetual, unremitting | intermittent, occasional, sporadic | adjective

incessably:/ɪnˈsɛsəbli/ | adv. | 不停地，持续不断地 |  In a manner that continues without interruption. | The dog barked incessantly all night. 狗整夜不停地吠叫。| continuously, constantly, perpetually | intermittently, occasionally, sporadically | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *incessans, present participle of incessare "to press on, not cease," from in- "not" (see in- (1)) + cessare "to cease, stop" (from PIE root *ked- "to fall, yield, withdraw").
