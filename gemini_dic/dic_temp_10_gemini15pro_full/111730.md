loculate:/ˈlɒkjʊleɪt/ /ˈlɑːkjʊleɪt/| adj.|分室的，多室的|Divided into loculi (small cavities or compartments).|The loculate fruit of the plant contained numerous seeds.  这种植物的多室果实包含许多种子。|The poppy's loculate capsule holds numerous tiny seeds. 罂粟的多室蒴果里有许多微小的种子。|近义词：compartmentalized, multilocular|反义词: unilocular|词性:adjective
