ferrous:/ˈfɛrəs/| adj.|亚铁的，含铁的|Containing or consisting of iron, especially in the divalent state.|例句：Ferrous sulfate is commonly used as an iron supplement. 硫酸亚铁通常用作铁补充剂。

例句：The soil is rich in ferrous compounds. 土壤中富含亚铁化合物。|近义词：iron-containing|反义词：ferric|词性：形容词

词源:
From Latin *ferrōsus*, from ferrum (“iron”).
