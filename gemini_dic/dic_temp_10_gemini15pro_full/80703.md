gorhen:/ˈɡɔːrhɛn/| n.|母松鸡；雌赤松鸡|A female grouse, especially a red grouse.|例句：The gorhen led her chicks through the heather. 母松鸡带着小鸡穿过石南丛。

例句：The hunter spotted a gorhen hiding in the undergrowth. 猎人发现一只母松鸡藏在灌木丛中。|近义词：hen grouse, female grouse|反义词:cock grouse (雄松鸡)|词性:名词
