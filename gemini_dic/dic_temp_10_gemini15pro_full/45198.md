culicid:/kjuˈlisɪd/ | n. |蚊；蚊科昆虫|A mosquito or other insect of the family Culicidae.|例句：Malaria is transmitted by culicids. 疟疾是由蚊子传播的。

例句：The study focused on the breeding habits of culicids in the region. 这项研究的重点是该地区蚊子的繁殖习性。|近义词：mosquito |反义词: N/A |词性: 名词

词源: From Latin *culex*, *culic-* (stem of *culex* "gnat, mosquito") + *-idae* (suffix forming zoological family names).
