consonantal:/ˌkɑːnsəˈnæntl/
adjective
辅音的；与辅音相关的
Relating to or characterized by consonants; consisting of consonants.
The word "strength" has a high consonantal density.  这个单词“strength”辅音密度很高。
The consonantal sounds in the word create a harsh effect.  这个词中的辅音创造了一种刺耳的效果。
