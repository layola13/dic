brutalized:/ˈbruːtəlaɪzd/| v. | 残暴对待，野蛮对待，摧残|To treat someone or something with extreme cruelty and violence, often to the point of physical damage or emotional trauma.  To make something coarse or unfeeling.|The soldiers brutalized the captured prisoners. 士兵们残暴地对待被俘的囚犯。

The experience of war brutalized him. 战争的经历使他变得冷酷无情。|近义词：battered, abused, mistreated, savaged|反义词：cherished, protected, nurtured|词性:动词

brutalizing:/ˈbruːtəlaɪzɪŋ/| adj. | 残暴的，野蛮的| Characterized by extreme cruelty and violence.|The brutalizing effects of war. 战争的残酷影响。|近义词：savage, barbaric, cruel, inhuman|反义词：gentle, kind, humane, compassionate|词性:形容词
