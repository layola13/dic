confusional:/kənˈfjuːʒənəl/
adjective
混乱的，困惑的
Relating to or characterized by confusion; bewildered.
The patient was in a confusional state after the surgery.  病人术后处于神志不清的状态。
The medication can cause confusional episodes. 这种药物可能会引起意识混乱的症状发作。

近义词: bewildered, disoriented, perplexed, addled
反义词: lucid, clearheaded, coherent, oriented

词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin *confusionalis, from Latin confusio (“a mingling together, disorder”), from confundere (“to pour together, mix up, confuse”), from con- (“together”) + fundere (“to pour”).
