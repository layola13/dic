nonutopian:/ˌnɒnjuːˈtəʊpiən/ | adj. | 非乌托邦的，非理想化的 | Not characteristic of or pertaining to a utopia; realistic, practical, acknowledging imperfections or negative aspects. |  A nonutopian view of society acknowledges the existence of poverty and crime.  非乌托邦的社会观承认贫困和犯罪的存在。

|  The novel presents a nonutopian future where environmental disasters are commonplace. 这部小说呈现了一个非乌托邦的未来，环境灾难司空见惯。 | Near synonyms: realistic, pragmatic, practical, down-to-earth | Antonyms: utopian, idealistic, visionary | adjective
