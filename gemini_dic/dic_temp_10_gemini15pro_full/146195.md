overexpanded:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspændɪd/
adjective
过度扩张的
expanded excessively; having expanded beyond safe or reasonable limits
The company overexpanded during the boom years and is now struggling to stay afloat.  这家公司在繁荣时期过度扩张，现在正艰难地维持下去。
overextended, inflated
contracted, reduced
