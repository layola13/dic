cockamaroo:/ˌkɒkəmərˈuː/ | n. |凤头鹦鹉(尤指澳洲产的大型鹦鹉), 经常用于昵称或爱称|A large crested parrot, especially of an Australian kind. Often used as a nickname or term of endearment. |例句：He called his friend "cockamaroo" affectionately. 他亲昵地称他的朋友为“凤头鹦鹉”。

例句：The cockamaroo squawked loudly from its perch. 凤头鹦鹉在栖木上大声鸣叫。

近义词：parrot, cockatoo |反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific type of bird) |词性：名词


词源:  Likely an alteration of "cockatoo," influenced by "cocky," a common nickname in Australia.  The "-roo" suffix is a common diminutive in Australian English.
