moonshee:/ˈmuːnʃiː/
noun
算命先生，女巫，通灵者
A person who claims to have magic powers or be able to tell the future.
She visited a moonshee to ask about her future husband. 她拜访了一位算命先生，询问她未来的丈夫。
fortune teller, psychic, soothsayer
