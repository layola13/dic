counter-myth:/ˈkaʊntər mɪθ/ | adj. | 反神话的，驳斥神话的 |  Formed by combining "counter-" (meaning "against" or "opposite") with "myth." It describes something that opposes or debunks a myth. | The book presents a counter-myth narrative about the founding of the city. 这本书提出了关于城市建立的反神话叙事。 |  myth-busting, debunking | myth-making, perpetuating | adjective

counter-myth:/ˈkaʊntər mɪθ/ | n. | 反神话的说法/叙事 |  A narrative or statement that directly challenges a prevailing myth. |  Her research provides a compelling counter-myth to the traditional narrative. 她的研究为传统叙事提供了一个令人信服的反神话说法。 | myth-buster, debunking argument | myth, traditional narrative | noun
