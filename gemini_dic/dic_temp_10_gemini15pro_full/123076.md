moneylender:/ˈmʌniˌlɛndər/| n. |放债人；贷款人|A person or organization whose business is lending money to others who pay interest.|The moneylender charged exorbitant interest rates.  放债人收取过高的利率。|Synonyms: loan shark, usurer, creditor|Antonyms: borrower, debtor|noun

moneylender:/ˈmʌniˌlɛndər/| n. |放债者；借款人|Someone who lends money, often at high interest rates.|He borrowed money from a moneylender to start his business. 他从一个放债者那里借钱来创业。|Synonyms: financier, lender, creditor|Antonyms: borrower, debtor|noun
