overcorrupting: /ˌoʊvərˈkərʌptɪŋ/
|v.|过度腐败; 极度败坏|Making excessively corrupt; corrupting to an extreme degree.|The overcorrupting influence of unchecked power led to the downfall of the empire.|近义词: debasing, perverting, demoralizing|反义词: purifying, ennobling|词性: Verb (present participle)
Etymology: From "over-" (excessively) + "corrupting" (present participle of "corrupt"), from Latin "corruptus" (past participle of "corrumpere" - to break up, destroy, bribe).
