indefeasible:/ˌɪndɪˈfiːzəbl/| adj.|不可废止的；不能取消的；不可剥夺的|Not capable of being annulled, voided, or forfeited.|例句：He has an indefeasible right to the property. 他对这处房产拥有不可剥夺的权利。|例句：The judge declared the contract indefeasible. 法官宣布该合同不可废止。|近义词：incontestable, absolute, unassailable|反义词：defeasible, revocable, contestable|词性:形容词|词源:Late Middle English: from Old French *indefaisable, from in- ‘not’ + defaisable (from defaisir ‘undo, annul’, based on Latin dis- ‘un-’ + facere ‘do’).
