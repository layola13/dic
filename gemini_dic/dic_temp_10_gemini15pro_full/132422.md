nondependableness:/ˌnɑndɪˈpɛndəbəlnəs/ | n. | 不可依赖性，不可靠性 |  The lack of reliability or trustworthiness; the quality of being unable to be depended on.  | The nondependableness of the old car made it a risky choice for a long road trip.  这辆旧车的不可靠性使它成为长途公路旅行的冒险选择。 | Synonyms: unreliability, undependability, instability, precariousness | Antonyms: reliability, dependability, trustworthiness, stability | noun
