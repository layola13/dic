amanuensis:/əˌmænjuˈɛnsɪs/
n.
抄写员；书记；笔录员
A person employed to write what another dictates or to copy what has been written.
例句：The author dictated his novel to an amanuensis.  这位作家向一位抄写员口述他的小说。

例句：The lawyer's amanuensis transcribed the complex legal document. 律师的书记抄录了复杂的法律文件。

近义词：scribe, secretary, clerk, copyist
反义词：author, dictator (in the sense of one who originates text)
词性：noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *amanuensis*, "taking dictation, writing by hand," from *a manu*, "by hand" + *-ensis*, a suffix forming adjectives.
