chestnuts:/ˈtʃɛsnʌts/ (US), /ˈtʃɛsnʌts/ (UK)| n.|栗子; 栗树; 栗色; (非正式)老掉牙的笑话或故事|A smooth-shelled, edible nut, contained in a prickly bur.  Figuratively, an old, stale joke or story.|We roasted chestnuts over the open fire. 我们在篝火上烤栗子。|Synonyms: nuts, (for the tree) chestnut tree|Antonyms: N/A|Noun


chestnut:/ˈtʃɛsnʌt/ (US), /ˈtʃɛsnʌt/ (UK)| adj.|栗色的|Of a reddish-brown color.|The horse had a beautiful chestnut coat. 这匹马有着美丽的栗色皮毛。|Synonyms: auburn, reddish-brown|Antonyms: N/A|Adjective
