genus:/ˈdʒiːnəs/
n.
属
A principal taxonomic category that ranks above species and below family, and is denoted by a capitalized Latin name
The genus Rosa includes many different species of roses.  蔷薇属包含许多不同种类的玫瑰。
synonyms: category, classification, taxon
antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a taxonomic rank)
