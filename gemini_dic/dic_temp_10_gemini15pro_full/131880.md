noncontaminative:/ˌnɒnkənˈtæmɪnətɪv/
| adj. |无污染的，不污染的|Not contaminating; free from contamination.|The process is noncontaminative and environmentally friendly.  这个过程无污染，对环境友好。|近义词：nonpolluting, clean, uncontaminated|反义词：contaminating, polluting|词性:形容词
词源: non- (not) + contaminative (from contaminate, from Latin contaminatus, past participle of contaminare "to pollute")
