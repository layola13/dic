hellos:/həˈləʊz/ (British), /hɛˈloʊz/ (American)| n.| 你好们 (Informal, often humorous or childish)|A pluralized, non-standard form of "hello."  Generally used informally or playfully.  It's not grammatically correct in formal English. |Example: Hellos everyone!  大家好！|Example: Hellos from the kids!  孩子们向你们问好！|Synonyms: hi, greetings (in a more formal context)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a greeting) | Noun
