disturb:/dɪˈstɜːrb/ (US), /dɪˈstəːb/ (UK)| vt.|打扰，扰乱，使不安|To interrupt the quiet, peace, or order of something or someone.|例句：The loud noise disturbed my sleep. 吵闹的声音打扰了我的睡眠。|近义词：interrupt, bother, upset, agitate|反义词：soothe, calm, pacify|verb

disturb:/dɪˈstɜːrb/ (US), /dɪˈstəːb/ (UK)| adj.(rare, archaic) | 心神不安的，精神错乱的 |  Mentally or emotionally upset or agitated. | 例句: He seemed disturbed by the news.  他似乎被这个消息弄得心神不宁。| 近义词：unsettled, troubled, agitated | 反义词：calm, composed, serene | adjective
