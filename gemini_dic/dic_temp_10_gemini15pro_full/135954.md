nonpursuantly:/ˌnɒnpərˈsuːəntli/
adverb|非追踪地；不追赶地|In a manner that does not involve pursuit or chase.  Not in accordance with a pursuit or chase.|The suspect was released nonpursuantly due to lack of evidence. 由于缺乏证据，嫌疑人未被追捕而被释放。|Synonym:  without pursuit, passively|Antonym: pursuantly, actively
词性: 副词
