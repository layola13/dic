paean:/ˈpiːən/| n.|赞歌，颂歌|A song of praise or triumph.|The crowd broke into a spontaneous paean of praise.|近义词：hymn, ode, anthem, tribute|反义词：dirge, lament|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek παιάν (paián, “healing song, song of thanksgiving”), from Παιών (Paiṓn, “Paean, physician of the gods”). Originally a hymn of invocation or thanksgiving to Apollo, later extended to other gods and then to mortals.
