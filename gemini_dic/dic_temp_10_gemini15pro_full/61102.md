elocutional: /ˌɛləˈkjuːʃənəl/ | adj. | 演说的，雄辩的 | Relating to elocution, or the art of public speaking, especially with attention to delivery, pronunciation, and gesture. |  She took elocutional lessons to improve her public speaking skills. 她上了演讲课来提高她的公开演讲技巧。|  The elocutional style of the speaker captivated the audience. 演讲者雄辩的风格吸引了观众。| 近义词: rhetorical, oratorical, expressive | 反义词: inarticulate, mumbled, unclear | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *ēlocūtiōn- (stem of ēlocūtiō ) "a speaking out, discourse," from ēlocūtus, past participle of ēloquī "speak out, declaim," from ex- "out" (see ex- ) + loquī "to speak" (see locution ).
