multilaminate:/ˌmʌltiˈlæmɪneɪt/ | adj. n.|多层的，多层压制的；多层结构|Consisting of many layers or laminae; laminated many times.|The multilaminate structure of the material makes it very strong. 这种材料的多层结构使其非常坚固。|multilayered, laminated|single-layer|adjective, noun
multilaminate:/ˌmʌltiˈlæmɪneɪt/ | v.|多层压制，多层粘合|To construct by uniting several layers or laminae.|They multilaminated the wood to create a durable countertop.  他们将木头多层压制以制造耐用的台面。|layer, laminate|separate|verb
