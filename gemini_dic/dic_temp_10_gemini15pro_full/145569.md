overcomplexities:/ˌoʊvərˌkəmˈpleksɪtiz/ | n. | 过度复杂性；过于复杂的情况 | The overcomplexities of the legal system make it difficult for ordinary people to understand.  法律体系的过度复杂性使普通人难以理解。|  The report highlighted the overcomplexities of the current tax code. 报告强调了现行税法的过度复杂性。|近义词: complications, intricacies | 反义词: simplicities, straightforwardness | 词性: 名词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "complexity" (from complex + -ity)
