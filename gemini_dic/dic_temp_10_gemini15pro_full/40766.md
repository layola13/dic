convincedly:/kənˈvɪnsɪŋli/| adv.|令人信服地，坚定地|In a manner that expresses firm belief and leaves no doubt.|She spoke convincedly about the need for change. 她令人信服地谈到了变革的必要性。|近义词：persuasively, confidently, assuredly|反义词：hesitantly, uncertainly, doubtfully|词性:副词
词源: From convince + -ed + -ly.  Convince comes from Latin convincere "to overcome, conquer," from com- "together" + vincere "to conquer."
