doxing:/ˈdɒksɪŋ/ (US) /ˈdɒksɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|人肉搜索，曝光个人信息|The act of publicly revealing private information about an individual or organization, typically with malicious intent.|Example: The activist was targeted for doxing after speaking out against the government.  这名活动家因公开反对政府而成为人肉搜索的目标。

Example: Doxing can lead to harassment and even violence. 人肉搜索可能导致骚扰甚至暴力。|近义词：exposure, revealing, unmasking|反义词: protecting privacy, concealing|词性:noun
