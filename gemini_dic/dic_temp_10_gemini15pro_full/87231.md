heresies:/ˈherəsiːz/
n.
异端邪说，谬论
Opinions or doctrines at variance with established religious beliefs, especially the accepted beliefs of the Christian church.
Many people were burned at the stake for their heresies. 很多人因为他们的异端邪说而被火刑处死。
近义词：fallacies, unorthodoxies, dissents, apostasies
反义词：orthodoxies, dogmas
词性：名词(plural of heresy)

heresy:/ˈherəsi/
n.
异端邪说，谬论
An opinion or doctrine at variance with established religious beliefs, especially the accepted beliefs of the Christian church.
The church regards the denial of the Holy Trinity as heresy. 教会将否认圣三位一体视为异端邪说。
近义词：fallacy, unorthodoxy, dissent, apostasy
反义词：orthodoxy, dogma
词性：名词
