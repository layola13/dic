diplomas:/dɪˈploʊməz/ (US) /dɪˈpləʊməz/ (UK)| n. |文凭，毕业证书；资格证书|A document conferring some honor, privilege, or power; especially a document certifying the successful completion of a course of study.|She received her diploma at graduation. 她毕业时获得了文凭。|synonyms: certificate, degree, qualification|antonyms: disqualification|noun

diplomas:/dɪˈploʊməz/ (US) /dɪˈpləʊməz/ (UK)| n. |执照；许可证|An official document or license conferring a privilege.|He showed his diploma to practice medicine. 他出示了他的行医执照。|synonyms: license, permit, authorization|antonyms: prohibition, ban|noun
