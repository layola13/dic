bushily:/ˈbʊʃɪli/| adv.|茂密地，浓密地|In a bushy manner; thickly and spread out like a bush.  The plant grew bushily, covering the entire trellis.|例句：The squirrel's tail was bushily furred. 松鼠的尾巴毛茸茸的。

例句：His eyebrows grew bushily over his eyes. 他的眉毛浓密地盖住了眼睛。|近义词：thickly, densely, profusely|反义词: sparsely, thinly|词性:副词

词源: From *bushy* + *-ly*. *Bushy* comes from Old English *byscig*.
