normally:/ˈnɔːrməli/ | adv. | 通常地，正常地 |  In the usual or ordinary way; regularly. | I normally go to bed around 10 pm. 我通常晚上10点左右睡觉. |  She normally eats a light lunch. 她通常午饭吃得很少. | 近义词: usually, typically, generally, ordinarily | 反义词: unusually, abnormally, exceptionally, rarely | 词性: 副词
