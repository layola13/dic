colonised:/ˈkɒlənaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːlənaɪzd/ (US)| adj.|殖民化的；被殖民的|Having been made into a colony; settled or exploited by colonists.|The British colonised many countries around the world. 英国在世界各地殖民了许多国家。|Near synonyms: settled, occupied, subjugated, controlled, dominated|Antonyms: independent, liberated, free, autonomous|adjective

colonise:/ˈkɒlənaɪz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːlənaɪz/ (US)| v.|殖民；拓殖|To establish a colony in; to settle among and establish political control over (an indigenous people of a place).|European powers colonised much of Africa in the 19th century. 欧洲列强在19世纪殖民了非洲的大部分地区。|Near synonyms: settle, populate, occupy, conquer, subjugate|Antonyms: liberate, free, decolonize|verb
