derusts:/diːˈrʌsts/
verb
除锈，除去铁锈
removes rust from something.
The mechanic derusts the car's undercarriage.  技工除去了汽车底盘的锈。
近义词: clean, polish, scour, remove rust
反义词: rust, corrode
词性: verb


Etymology:
de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + rust (noun)
