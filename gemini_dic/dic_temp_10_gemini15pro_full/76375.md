gadolinite:/ˈɡæd(ə)lɪˌnaɪt/ | n. | 硅铍钇矿 |A brownish-black or greenish-black mineral consisting of a silicate of yttrium, cerium, iron, and beryllium. | Gadolinite is a rare earth mineral. 硅铍钇矿是一种稀土矿物。|  Gadolinite was discovered in Ytterby, Sweden. 硅铍钇矿是在瑞典的伊特比发现的。 | synonyms: ytterbite | antonyms: N/A | noun
