fraternate:/frəˈtɜːrnət/
adjective
兄弟般的；兄弟似的
Of or befitting brothers; brotherly.
例句：He felt a fraternate bond with his teammates.  他和他的队友们感觉像兄弟一样。

例句：The fraternate atmosphere of the club made him feel welcome. 俱乐部兄弟般的氛围让他感到宾至如归。

近义词：brotherly, fraternal
反义词：unbrotherly, hostile
词性: adjective

fraternate:/ˈfrætərˌneɪt/
verb
结为兄弟；亲如兄弟
To associate or unite as brothers.
例句：The two groups fraternated after the competition. 比赛结束后，两组人亲如兄弟。

例句：They fraternated with the locals during their vacation.  他们在假期期间与当地人打成一片。

近义词：associate, socialize, mingle
反义词：isolate, segregate
词性: verb


词源:  From Late Latin *fraternatus, from Latin frater ("brother").
