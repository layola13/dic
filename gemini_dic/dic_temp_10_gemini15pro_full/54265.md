degradation:/ˌdeɡrəˈdeɪʃn/
n.|降级，退化，堕落|The environmental degradation caused by pollution is a serious concern. 污染造成的环境退化是一个严重的问题。|例句：The degradation of the soil made it impossible to grow crops. 土壤退化使得种植作物成为不可能。|近义词：deterioration, decline, decay|反义词：improvement, enhancement|词性:名词

degradation:/ˌdeɡrəˈdeɪʃn/
n.|羞辱，丢脸|He suffered the degradation of being publicly criticized. 他遭受了被公开批评的羞辱。|例句：She felt a sense of degradation after losing her job. 失去工作后，她感到一种羞辱感。|近义词：humiliation, disgrace, shame|反义词：honor, respect|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dēgradātiōnem,  from Latin dēgradātus, past participle of dēgradārī (“to reduce in rank, degrade”), from dē- (“down from”) + gradus (“step, rank”).
