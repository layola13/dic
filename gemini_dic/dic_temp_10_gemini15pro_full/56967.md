double-blind:/ˌdʌbəl ˈblaɪnd/ | adj. | 双盲的 |  An experiment or clinical trial in which neither the subjects nor the researchers know which subjects are receiving the active medication or treatment and which are receiving a placebo.  This is done to prevent bias and ensure the objectivity of the results. |  The study was conducted double-blind to eliminate the placebo effect.  这项研究采用双盲法进行，以消除安慰剂效应。

The researchers designed a double-blind experiment to test the new drug's efficacy. 研究人员设计了一个双盲实验来测试新药的疗效。 | 近义词: unbiased, objective | 反义词: open-label, single-blind | 词性: 形容词
