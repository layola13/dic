adverb

in an extremely tragic manner
例句：The young couple died hypertragically in a car accident. 这对年轻夫妇在一场车祸中极其悲惨地丧生。

例句：The story ended hypertragically, leaving the audience in tears.  这个故事的结局极其悲惨，让观众泪流满面。

近义词：extremely tragically, devastatingly, heartbreakingly
反义词：joyfully, happily
词性：副词
