envie:/ˈɑ̃ːviː/ (French), /ˈenvi/ (anglicized)| n.|羡慕，渴望|A feeling of longing for something that someone else has.|She felt a pang of envie when she saw her friend's new car.|Synonyms: jealousy, covetousness, desire, yearning|Antonyms: contentment, satisfaction|noun

注意事项:

"Envie" is more common in French than in English.  In English, "envy" is more frequently used.  While "envie" can be used as a noun, "envy" can be used as both a noun and a verb.
