nefariousness:/nəˈfɛəriəsnəs/
n.
邪恶; 凶恶; 恶毒
The quality or state of being nefarious; wickedness.
His nefariousness was evident in his cruel treatment of animals.  他的邪恶体现在他对动物的残忍虐待上。
Near Synonyms: wickedness, evil, villainy, iniquity, depravity
Antonyms: goodness, righteousness, virtue
