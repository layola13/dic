deliberating:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
仔细考虑；商议
carefully considering or discussing something
The jury is deliberating the verdict. 陪审团正在商议裁决。
The committee is deliberating on the proposal. 委员会正在仔细考虑这项提议。
近义词: considering, pondering, discussing, contemplating
反义词: impulsive, rash, hasty
词性: verb (present participle)
词源: From Latin *dēliberātus*, past participle of *dēliberāre*  "to weigh well, ponder," from *dē* "from" + *librāre* "to weigh," from *libra* "balance, scales."
