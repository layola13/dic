novation:/noʊˈveɪʃən/ |n.| 革新；更新；取代；债务更新 |The act of replacing an existing contract, obligation, or party with a new one.| We signed a novation agreement with the bank, transferring the loan to the new company. 我们与银行签署了债务更新协议，将贷款转移给了新公司。| synonyms: replacement, substitution, renewal, innovation | antonyms: continuation, stagnation| noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *novationem*, from Latin *novatus*, past participle of *novare* "to make new, renew, alter," from *novus* "new."
