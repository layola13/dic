citrination:/ˌsɪtrɪˈneɪʃən/| n.|柠檬酸化；柑橘化；黄变|The process of making something yellow, or lemon-colored, often through chemical treatment. Also refers to the yellowing of biological tissues.  |The citrination of the old photograph gave it a vintage look. 这张老照片的柠檬酸化使其具有复古外观。|The citrination of the ivory made it appear older than it was. 象牙的黄变使其看起来比实际年龄更老。|yellowing, discoloration|bleaching, whitening|noun
