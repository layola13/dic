outsmiles:/ˌaʊtˈsmaɪlz/
verb
胜过…的笑容;以微笑胜过
He outsmiled her, his expression a mixture of amusement and affection. 他对她报以微笑，表情中既有开心，又有爱意。
He outsmiled everyone in the room. 他的笑容胜过房间里的所有人。
近义词: outcharm, outshine
反义词: frown at, glare at
词性:v.
