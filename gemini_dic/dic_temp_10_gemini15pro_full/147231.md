overmedicate: /ˌoʊvərˈmɛdɪkeɪt/
verb
过度用药
To prescribe or administer too much medication or too many medications.
The doctor was accused of overmedicating his patients.  医生被指控过度用药。
Some elderly patients are overmedicated, which can lead to serious side effects.  一些老年病人被过度用药，这会导致严重的副作用。
近义词: overprescribe, overdose
反义词: undermedicate
词性: verb
