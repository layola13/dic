abiotic:/ˌeɪbaɪˈɒtɪk/
adjective
非生物的; 无生命的
Characterized by the absence of life or living organisms. Relating to the non-living parts of an environment.
Abiotic factors, such as temperature and rainfall, can influence the distribution of plant species.  非生物因素，如温度和降雨量，会影响植物物种的分布。

The abiotic components of a pond include water, rocks, and sunlight.  池塘的非生物成分包括水、岩石和阳光。

synonyms: non-living, inanimate, inorganic
antonyms: biotic, living, organic
词性: adjective
