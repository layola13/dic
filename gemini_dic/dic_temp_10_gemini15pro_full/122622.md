modi:/ˈmoʊdiː/ | adj. | 喜怒无常的，情绪化的 | Subject to rapid and unpredictable changes of mood; temperamental. | She's been very moody lately, snapping at everyone. 她最近情绪很不好，动不动就对人大吼大叫。| He was a moody teenager, prone to fits of anger. 他是一个喜怒无常的青少年，容易发脾气。 |近义词：temperamental, volatile, capricious, mercurial |反义词：stable, even-tempered, cheerful | 词性: 形容词
