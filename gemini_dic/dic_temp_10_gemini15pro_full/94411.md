implicatively:/ɪmˈplɪkətɪvli/
adverb
暗示地；含蓄地
in a way that implies something; by implication.
His silence implicatively agreed to the terms. 他的沉默暗示着同意了这些条款。
implicatively:/ɪmˈplɪkətɪvli/
adverb
推论地；必然地
in a way that follows logically; necessarily.
If A implies B, and B implies C, then A implicatively implies C. 如果A蕴含B，B蕴含C，那么A必然蕴含C。


近义词:implicitly, tacitly, by implication
反义词:explicitly, directly, overtly
词性:adverb
