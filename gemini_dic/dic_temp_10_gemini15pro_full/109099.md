legends:/ˈlɛdʒəndz/ | n. | 传说；传奇人物；图例 | A traditional story sometimes popularly regarded as historical but unauthenticated.  A person whose fame or notoriety makes him or her a source of exaggerated or romanticized tales. An explanatory list of the symbols on a map or chart. | The legends of King Arthur are well known.
