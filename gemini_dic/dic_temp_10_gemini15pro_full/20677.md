blackwater:/ˈblækˌwɔːtər/ (US), /ˈblækˌwɔːtə/ (UK)| n.|黑水|Dark, often polluted water, typically associated with swamps, sewage, or industrial waste. Also, euphemistically, sewage or toilet waste.|The hurricane caused the blackwater to rise and flood the streets. 飓风导致黑水上涨并淹没了街道。|Synonyms: sewage, wastewater, effluent|Antonyms: freshwater, clean water|Noun

blackwater:/ˈblækˌwɔːtər/ (US), /ˈblækˌwɔːtə/ (UK)| adj.|黑水的|Relating to or characteristic of blackwater.|The blackwater swamps of the Everglades are home to a variety of wildlife.  大沼泽地的黑水沼泽是各种野生动物的家园。|Synonyms: swampy, murky|Antonyms: clear, pristine|Adjective
