gramineous:/ɡrəˈmɪniəs/| adj.|禾本科的；似草的；草绿色的|Relating to or resembling grass; characteristic of the family Gramineae (Poaceae).|The vast, gramineous plains provided ample grazing for the bison.  广阔的草原为野牛提供了充足的牧草。|The farmer planted a gramineous cover crop to prevent soil erosion.  农民种植了禾本科覆盖作物以防止土壤侵蚀。|近义词：grassy, herbaceous|反义词:woody, ligneous|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *graminius*, from gramen (“grass”).
