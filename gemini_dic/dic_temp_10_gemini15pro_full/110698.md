linearity:/ˌlɪniˈærəti/ | n. |线性，线性度| The property of being linear.  A linear relationship exists between two variables if one can be expressed as a constant multiple of the other. |The linearity of the graph indicates a direct proportionality between the two variables. 这张图的线性表明两个变量之间成正比。| linearity: direct proportionality, straightness | nonlinearity, curvature | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *linearitas, from Latin linearis (“of or pertaining to a line”), from linea (“line”).
