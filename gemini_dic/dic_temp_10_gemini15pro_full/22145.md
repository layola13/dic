boma:/ˈboʊmə/| n.|畜栏，围栏，临时营地|An enclosure, usually made of thorn bushes, for protecting livestock at night, especially in Africa.  A temporary fortified encampment or settlement.|例句：The cattle were safe inside the boma for the night. 牛在夜间被安全地关在畜栏里。

例句：The explorers set up a boma in the wilderness. 探险家们在荒野中建立了一个临时营地。|近义词：enclosure, corral, kraal, compound, camp|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "open field")|词性:noun
