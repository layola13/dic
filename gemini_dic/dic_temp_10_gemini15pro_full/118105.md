menispermaceous:/ˌmɛnɪspərˈmeɪʃəs/
adj.
防己科的;  属于防己科的
Belonging to or resembling the Menispermaceae family of plants, which are characterized by their woody vines and often contain alkaloids.
Menispermaceous plants are often found in tropical and subtropical regions. 防己科植物常见于热带和亚热带地区。
Several menispermaceous species are used in traditional medicine.  一些防己科植物用于传统医学。

ETYMOLOGY->
From Menispermum (the type genus of the family) + -aceous (a suffix meaning "belonging to" or "resembling"). Menispermum comes from Greek mēniskos "crescent" (referring to the shape of the seed) + sperma "seed."
