namer's:/ˈneɪmərz/| adj.|命名者的；命名的|Belonging to or associated with a namer; used in naming.  Relating to the act or process of giving a name.  |This is the namer's responsibility. 这是命名者的责任。|近义词:nominator's, baptizer's, christener's|反义词:anonymous, nameless|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From "namer" + "'s" (possessive suffix).  "Namer" comes from the verb "name" (from Old English *namian, from Proto-Germanic *namōjaną, related to Old Norse nafn "name"),  plus the agentive suffix "-er".
