nonrevenues:/ˌnɒnˈrevənjuːz/
noun
非收入；非收益
income that is not generated from the core business operations of a company, typically referring to things like donations, grants, or investment income.
The hospital's nonrevenues helped offset the cost of providing free care to low-income patients.  医院的非收益收入帮助抵消了向低收入患者提供免费护理的成本。
The museum relies on nonrevenues, such as donations and grants, to fund its operations.  博物馆依靠捐款和拨款等非收益收入来维持运营。
Synonyms: non-operating income, other income
Antonyms: revenues, operating income
