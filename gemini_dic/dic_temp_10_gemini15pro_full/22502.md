boomburb:/ˈbuːmbɜːrb/| n.|快速发展的郊区|A rapidly growing suburb, typically located some distance from a major city, characterized by a large, affluent population and extensive commercial and residential development.|The families migrated to the boomburbs seeking larger homes and better schools.|近义词:exurb, suburb|反义词:inner city|词性:名词

Etymology:  A blend of "boom" (referring to rapid growth) and "suburb".
