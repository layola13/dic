micropublish:/ˈmaɪkroʊˈpʌblɪʃ/| v. n.|微出版，微发布|The act of self-publishing content online, often in small chunks or updates, directly to an audience, bypassing traditional publishing platforms.|She decided to micropublish her poetry on her own website. 她决定在自己的网站上微出版她的诗歌。|近义词：self-publish, blog, post|反义词: traditionally publish|词性:动词/名词


micropublishing:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈpʌblɪʃɪŋ/| n.|微出版，微发布|The practice of micropublishing.|Micropublishing allows authors to connect directly with their readers. 微出版允许作者直接与读者联系。|近义词：self-publishing, blogging|反义词: traditional publishing|词性:名词
