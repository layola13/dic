collineation:/kɒlɪnɪˈeɪʃn/ | n. | 共线化，直射变换 | A transformation that maps lines to lines, though not necessarily preserving parallelism or distance.  It's a concept in projective geometry. | The collineation preserved the intersection points of the lines.  共线化保留了线的交点。|  The collineation transformed the triangle into another triangle.  共线化将三角形转换为另一个三角形。 | synonyms: projective transformation, homography | antonyms: N/A | noun
