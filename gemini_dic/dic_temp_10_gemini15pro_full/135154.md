nononerousness:/ˌnɒnˈəʊnərəsnəs/
noun
轻松；容易；无负担
The state or quality of not being onerous; lack of burdensomeness.
The nononerousness of the task surprised him.  这项任务的轻松让他感到惊讶。
Near synonyms: easiness, lightness, facility, simplicity
Antonyms: onerousness, burdensomeness, difficulty, hardship
