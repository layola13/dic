cogitatingly:/ˈkɒdʒɪteɪtɪŋli/ /ˈkɑːdʒɪteɪtɪŋli/| adv.| 沉思地，思考地 | in a manner characterized by deep thought or reflection. | He paced the room cogitatingly, trying to find a solution. 他在房间里踱来踱去，沉思着，试图找到一个解决方案。|例句：She stared cogitatingly out the window, lost in her thoughts. 她若有所思地凝视着窗外，沉浸在自己的思绪中。| 近义词：thoughtfully, pensively, reflectively | 反义词：rashly, impulsively | 词性：副词

Etymology:
From cogitate + -ing + -ly.  Cogitate comes from the Latin word "cogitare," meaning "to think, consider, ponder."  "-ing" forms the present participle, and "-ly" creates the adverb form.
