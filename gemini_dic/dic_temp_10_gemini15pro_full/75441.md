The state of being fruitful; producing good results abundantly.
The fruitfulnesses of the land were evident in the bountiful harvest.  这片土地的肥沃在丰收中显而易见。

Etymology:
From Middle English *fruitfulnes, from Old English *frēotfulnes (“fruitfulness”), equivalent to fruitful +‎ -ness.  Cognate with Dutch vruchtbaarheid (“fruitfulness”), German Fruchtbarkeit (“fertility”).
