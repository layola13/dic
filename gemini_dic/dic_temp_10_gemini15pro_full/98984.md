inter-talked:/ˌɪntərˈtɔːkt/ | v. |互相交谈，相互谈话 |To have a conversation or discussion between two or more parties.  It implies a reciprocal exchange of words or ideas.| The two groups inter-talked for hours, trying to find common ground. 两个小组互相交谈了几个小时，试图找到共同点。| synonyms: conversed, discussed, dialogued, conferred | antonyms: ignored, shunned, avoided |verb

Etymology:
"inter-" (Latin, "between, among") + "talked" (past tense of "talk," from Middle English *talken, of uncertain origin).
