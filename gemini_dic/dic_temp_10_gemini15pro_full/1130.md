accessorizing: /əkˈsesəˌraɪzɪŋ/
noun|配饰，装饰，搭配配件|The act of adding accessories to something, typically clothing, to enhance its appearance.|She spent hours accessorizing her outfit with jewelry and scarves. 她花了几个小时用珠宝和围巾来搭配她的服装。|Synonyms: adorning, embellishing, ornamenting|Antonyms: stripping, simplifying|n.

accessorize: /əkˈsesəˌraɪz/
verb|搭配配件，装饰|To add accessories to something, typically clothing, to enhance its appearance.|She accessorized her little black dress with a pearl necklace. 她用一条珍珠项链来搭配她的小黑裙。|Synonyms: adorn, embellish, ornament|Antonyms: strip, simplify|v.
