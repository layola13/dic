cadger's:/ˈkædʒəz/| n.|乞丐的；骗子的|Something that belongs to a cadger, a person who begs or obtains things by asking for them persistently and shamelessly.  It implies that the possession is likely obtained through such means.|例句：The cadger's tattered coat offered little protection from the cold.  乞丐破烂的外套几乎不能抵御寒冷。

例句：He wouldn't even give a penny to charity, claiming it was all part of a cadger's trick. 他甚至不愿意捐一分钱给慈善机构，声称这完全是骗子的伎俩。|近义词：beggar's, sponger's, freeloader's|反义词:donor's, philanthropist's|词性:possessive noun (名词所有格)
