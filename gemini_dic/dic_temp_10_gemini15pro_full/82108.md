groggery: /ˈɡrɑːɡəri/ (US), /ˈɡrɒɡəri/ (UK) | n. | 低级酒馆；廉价酒店；烈酒 | A place that sells alcoholic beverages, especially a low-class bar or tavern. Often implies a place that is rundown or disreputable. |  例句：The old sailor stumbled out of the groggery. 老水手跌跌撞撞地从低级酒馆里走了出来。

例句：He spent most of his wages at the local groggery. 他把大部分工资都花在了当地的廉价酒馆里。

近义词：bar, pub, tavern, saloon | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps) upscale bar, restaurant | 词性: noun
