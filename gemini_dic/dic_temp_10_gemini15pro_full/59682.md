echo:/ˈekəʊ/ (UK), /ˈekoʊ/ (US)| n. v.|回声，回音；模仿，重复|n. a sound that is caused by the reflection of sound waves from a surface back to the listener. v. to repeat a sound or statement; to be similar to something and therefore remind you of it. |n. The echo of her scream was chilling. 她的尖叫声的回音令人毛骨悚然。 v. The empty room echoed his footsteps. 空荡荡的房间回荡着他的脚步声。 |近义词：n. reverberation, resonance, reflection; v. repeat, mimic, imitate|反义词:n. silence; v. originate|noun, verb
