incitement:/ɪnˈsaɪtmənt/
n.
煽动；激励；刺激
The act of encouraging or urging someone to do something, especially something violent or unlawful.
His speeches were seen as an incitement to violence. 他的演讲被视为煽动暴力。

The rock band's energetic performance was pure incitement to jump and dance.  这支摇滚乐队的活力四射的表演纯粹是让人跳起来跳舞的激励。

encouragement, motivation, stimulation, provocation
deterrent, discouragement, suppression

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French encitement, from Late Latin incitamentum, from incitare ‘urge on’, from in- ‘towards’ + citare ‘rouse’.
