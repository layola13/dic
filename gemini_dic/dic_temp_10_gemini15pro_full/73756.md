forlornnesses:/fərˈlɔːrnɪsɪz/
n.
凄凉，孤寂，被遗弃的状态
The state of being forlorn; desolation; loneliness.
The forlornnesses of the abandoned houses were palpable.  被遗弃的房屋弥漫着一种凄凉的氛围。
The sheer forlornnesses of the landscape overwhelmed her. 荒凉的景色让她感到十分压抑。
Synonyms: desolation, loneliness, abandonment, bleakness, dreariness
Antonyms: comfort, cheerfulness, happiness, hopefulness
