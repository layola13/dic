paraphrasable:/ˌpærəˈfreɪzəbl/| adj.|可释义的，可意译的|Able to be paraphrased; capable of being expressed in different words while retaining the same meaning.|The lecturer's complex ideas were surprisingly paraphrasable, making them easier for students to understand. 讲师复杂的思想竟然可以被释义，让学生更容易理解。|synonyms: rewordable, restatable, expressible in other words|antonyms: unparaphrasable, inexplicable, ineffable|adjective
