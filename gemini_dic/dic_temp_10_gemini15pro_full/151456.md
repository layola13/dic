paror:/ˈpærɔːr/ | n. | blunder, mistake, error | A mistake or error in speech or action. | He made a paror when he called her by the wrong name. 他叫错了她的名字，犯了个错误。| synonyms: mistake, error, blunder, slip, gaffe, faux pas | antonyms: accuracy, correctness | noun


Etymology:
From Latin *paror*, from parāre "to prepare, provide," related to par "equal, pair." The original sense was "too little, insufficient," which evolved into "a defect, fault."
