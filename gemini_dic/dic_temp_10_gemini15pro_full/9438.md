antireality:/ˌænti riˈælɪti/
noun
反现实；非现实
A state of mind or a depiction of a situation that is detached from or opposed to the observable facts or the generally accepted standards of reality.
His writing explores themes of antireality and the subjective nature of experience. 他的作品探索了反现实的主题和经验的主观性。
Synonyms: unreality, fantasy, illusion, dream, surrealism
Antonyms: reality, actuality, truth, fact
