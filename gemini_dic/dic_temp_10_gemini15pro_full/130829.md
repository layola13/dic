nonappeasing:/ˌnɒnəˈpiːzɪŋ/
adj.
不绥靖的，不姑息的
Not giving in to demands; uncompromising.
Example: The government took a nonappeasing stance against the terrorists. 政府对恐怖分子采取了不绥靖的立场。

Example:  His nonappeasing attitude towards bullies earned him respect. 他对恶霸不姑息的态度为他赢得了尊重。

近义词:uncompromising, resolute, firm, steadfast, unyielding
反义词:appeasing, conciliatory, accommodating, submissive, yielding
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + appeasing (present participle of appease, from Old French apaisier "to pacify," from a- "to" + pais "peace").
