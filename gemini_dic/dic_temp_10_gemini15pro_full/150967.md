paramento:/ˌpærəˈmɛntoʊ/| n. |装饰物，帷幔，祭坛布|An ornamental covering, especially for a wall or an altar. |The paramento of the altar was richly embroidered. 祭坛的帷幔绣得很精美。|近义词：decoration, adornment, drapery, vestment|反义词:bareness, plainness|noun


paramento:/ˌpærəˈmɛntoʊ/| n. |（尤指石头的）外层，饰面|The outer layer or facing, especially of stone. |The paramento of the building was made of granite.  这栋建筑的外层是用花岗岩做的。|近义词：facing, veneer, cladding|反义词:interior, inside|noun

词源: From Latin *paramentum, from parare "to prepare."
