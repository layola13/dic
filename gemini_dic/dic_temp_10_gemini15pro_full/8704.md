anticrime:/ˌæntiˈkraɪm/
adjective
反犯罪的，打击犯罪的
Relating to or directed against crime.
The mayor announced new anticrime initiatives. 市长宣布了新的打击犯罪举措。
Synonyms: law-enforcement, crime-fighting, crime-prevention
Antonyms: pro-crime, criminal
