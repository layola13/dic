equilibria:/ˌiːkwɪˈlɪbriə/ /ˌɛkwɪˈlɪbriə/
n.
平衡；均衡
a state in which opposing forces or influences are balanced.
The government has to strike a balance between economic growth and environmental protection. 政府必须在经济增长和环境保护之间取得平衡。
balance, stability, symmetry
imbalance, instability, asymmetry
noun
