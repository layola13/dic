cracked:/krækt/| adj.|破裂的，裂开的；声音嘶哑的；精神失常的；技艺高超的|broken, split; having a rough or harsh voice; mentally unstable; very skilled or proficient|例句：The vase is cracked. 花瓶破裂了。|近义词：broken, fractured, split, chipped, damaged, flawed, insane, mad, demented, skilled, expert, proficient, adept|反义词：intact, whole, sound, sane, unskilled|词性:形容词

cracked:/krækt/| v.|（使）破裂；（使）发出爆裂声；解开（难题）；崩溃；开始（做某事）|break, split; make a sharp explosive sound; solve a problem; lose control of one's emotions; begin to do something|例句：The ice cracked under the weight. 冰在重压下裂开了。|近义词：break, fracture, split, snap, decipher, solve, collapse, crumble, commence, begin, start|反义词：mend, repair, heal|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *craked, *crakede, past participle of craken (“to crack”), from Old English cracian (“to crack, crash, resound”), of imitative origin. Related to Old Norse kraka (“to crow, crack”), Middle High German krachen (“to crack, crash”).
