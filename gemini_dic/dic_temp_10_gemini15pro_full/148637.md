overthoughtfully: /ˌoʊvərˈθɔːtfəli/ | adv. | 过度思虑地，想得太多地 | In a way that involves thinking too much about something, often to the point of causing anxiety or stress. |  She stared overthoughtfully out the window, worrying about the upcoming exam. 她过度思虑地盯着窗外，担心即将到来的考试。|  He overthoughtfully analyzed every possible outcome, paralyzing himself with indecision. 他过度思虑地分析了每一个可能的结果，让自己陷入了犹豫不决的瘫痪。 |近义词：excessively, too much, analytically | 反义词：intuitively, instinctively, impulsively | 词性: 副词

词源: "overthoughtfully" is derived from "overthink" + "fully". "Overthink" is a combination of "over-" (meaning "excessive") and "think."  "-fully" is a suffix that transforms an adjective into an adverb.
