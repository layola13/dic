nonfacetious:/ˌnɒnfəˈsiːʃəs/ | adj. | 严肃的，认真的 | Not joking or meant to be humorous; serious. |  "His nonfacetious tone indicated the seriousness of the situation." 他的严肃语气表明了情况的严重性。 |  earnest, serious, sincere, solemn, sober | facetious, frivolous, flippant, joking, playful | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + facetious (joking).  Facetious ultimately comes from Latin *facetia "jest, wit," related to facetus "witty, elegant."
