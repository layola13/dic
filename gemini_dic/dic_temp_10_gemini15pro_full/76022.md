furfurol:/ˈfɜːrfjʊˌrɒl/ (US) /ˈfɜːrfjʊˌrɔːl/ (UK)| n.|糠醛，麸醛|An aromatic aldehyde, C5H4O2, derived from bran, corn cobs, and other plant materials, used in the manufacture of nylon, resins, and other products.|Furfurol is used in the production of various resins. 糠醛用于生产各种树脂。|
Furfurol is a valuable chemical in the biorefinery industry. 糠醛是生物炼制行业中一种有价值的化学品。

|Synonyms: furaldehyde, 2-furaldehyde|Antonyms: N/A|noun|

Etymology: From German *Furfurol*, from *Furfural* + *-ol*.  *Furfural* is from Latin *furfur* ("bran") + English *-al* (aldehyde suffix).
