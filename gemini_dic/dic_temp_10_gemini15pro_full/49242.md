deleter:/dɪˈliːtər/| n.|删除者，删除程序|A person or thing that deletes something.  Someone or something that removes or eliminates something, often information or data.|The deleter removed all traces of the file from the system. 删除者从系统中删除了该文件的所有痕迹。|近义词：remover, eraser, expunger|反义词：creator, adder, restorer|词性:noun
Etymology: From the verb "delete," which comes from the Latin word "dēlēre," meaning "to destroy, wipe out, blot out."  The "-er" suffix denotes an agent or instrument that performs the action of the verb.
