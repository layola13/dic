induviae:/ɪnˈduːviiː/ | n. |蜕皮; (昆虫或其他节肢动物)蜕下的外壳| The shed exoskeleton of an insect or other arthropod. |  The researcher carefully collected the induviae of the cicadas. 研究人员仔细收集了蝉蜕。| synonyms: exuviae, cast skin, shed skin | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms exist, as induviae refers to a discarded covering.  Potentially, "new skin" or "integument" could be considered loosely antonymous in implying the opposite state.) | noun
