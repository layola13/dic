hassled:/ˈhæsld/ (US), /ˈhæsəld/ (UK)| v. adj.|烦扰，困扰（动词），忙碌的，被烦扰的（形容词）|To annoy or bother someone, often with repeated requests or questions.  As an adjective, it describes the state of being annoyed or bothered in this way.  Derived from "hasten" and later influenced by "hustle."|

例句：
I'm feeling hassled by all these phone calls.
我被这些电话烦扰着。

They hassled me for money all day.
他们整天缠着我要钱。

The shop owner was hassled by a group of teenagers.
店主被一群青少年骚扰。

She had a hassled look on her face.
她脸上带着烦恼的表情。

近义词：bothered, annoyed, pestered, harassed, stressed, troubled|
反义词：relaxed, calm, peaceful|
词性：verb, adjective
