mindboggling:/ˈmaɪndˌbɑːɡlɪŋ/| adj.|令人难以置信的；令人震惊的|Overwhelmingly impressive, astonishing, or difficult to comprehend.|The complexity of the universe is truly mindboggling. 宇宙的复杂性真是令人难以置信。|The special effects in the movie were mindboggling.  这部电影的特效令人叹为观止。|Synonyms: astonishing, amazing, incredible, unbelievable, staggering, astounding|Antonyms: unimpressive, ordinary, mundane|adjective
