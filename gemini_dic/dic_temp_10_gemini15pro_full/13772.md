audience:/ˈɔːdiəns/ (US) /ˈɔːdiəns/ (UK)| n.|观众；听众；读者；接见；正式会见|A group of people who watch and listen to a performance, speech, etc., or an intended readership for a piece of writing.|The audience applauded wildly at the end of the concert. 音乐会结束时，观众们热烈鼓掌。|Synonyms: spectators, listeners, viewers, readership|Antonyms: performer, speaker, writer|Noun
