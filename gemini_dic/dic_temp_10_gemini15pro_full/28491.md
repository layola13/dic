carcinomatous:/ˌkɑːrsɪˈnɒmətəs/| adj.|癌的；癌性的|Relating to or affected with carcinoma (a type of cancer that starts in the epithelial tissue).|The biopsy revealed carcinomatous cells.  活检显示癌细胞。

The patient had a carcinomatous tumor. 病人患有癌性肿瘤。|Synonym: cancerous, malignant, neoplastic|Antonym: benign, healthy|adjective
