effectualnesses:/ɪˈfɛktʃuəlˌnɛsɪz/
noun
有效性，效力
The quality of being effectual; effectiveness.
The effectualnesses of the new policy are yet to be seen. 新政策的有效性还有待观察。
Synonyms: effectiveness, efficacy, potency, efficiency
Antonyms: ineffectiveness, inefficiency, impotence
