overlenient:/ˌoʊvərˈliːniənt/
adjective
过分宽容的；过于仁慈的
Too lenient; excessively tolerant or merciful.
The overlenient judge let the repeat offender off with a warning.  这个过于宽容的法官只是警告了惯犯。
The teacher's overlenient grading policy resulted in inflated grades. 老师过于宽容的评分政策导致分数虚高。
近义词: too lenient, excessively tolerant, indulgent, permissive
反义词: strict, severe, harsh, firm
词性: adjective
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "lenient" (from Latin "lenis" meaning "gentle, mild")
