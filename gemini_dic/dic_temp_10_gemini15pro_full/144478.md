outthreaten: /ˌaʊtˈθrɛtən/
verb
以威胁超越；威胁更大
to threaten to a greater degree than someone or something else.
The new virus outthreatens the previous one due to its higher infection rate.  这种新病毒由于其更高的感染率，比以前的病毒威胁更大。
North Korea outthreatens its neighbors with nuclear weapons. 朝鲜用核武器威胁其邻国。
近义词: surpass in threat, outmatch in menace
反义词: be less threatening, pose a smaller threat
词性: verb
