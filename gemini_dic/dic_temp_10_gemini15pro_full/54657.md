disobey:/ˌdɪsəˈbeɪ/ /ˌdɪsəˈbeɪ/| v. |不服从，违抗|To refuse or fail to comply with rules, requests, or commands. |The soldier disobeyed a direct order.  这个士兵违抗了直接命令。

The child disobeyed his parents and stayed out late.  这个孩子不违抗父母，在外面待到很晚。|近义词：defy, ignore, disregard, violate|反义词：obey, comply, follow, heed|词性:动词

词源:  Late Middle English: from dis- (expressing reversal) + obey.
