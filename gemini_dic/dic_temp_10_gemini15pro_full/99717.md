interlucent:/ˌɪntərˈluːsənt/ | adj. | 半透明的，透光的 | Allowing light to pass through diffusely; translucent. | The lampshade was made of interlucent glass, creating a soft glow in the room.  灯罩是用半透明的玻璃做的，在房间里营造出柔和的光芒。 | Example 2: The interlucent wings of the butterfly shimmered in the sunlight. 蝴蝶半透明的翅膀在阳光下闪闪发光。| 近义词: translucent, semi-transparent | 反义词: opaque, impermeable | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *interlucens*, present participle of *interlucēre* "to shine between," from *inter-* "between" + *lucēre* "to shine."
