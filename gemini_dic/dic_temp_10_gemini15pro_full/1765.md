acquisitiveness:/əˌkwɪzɪtɪvnəs/| n.|贪婪，占有欲|The state of being acquisitive; excessive desire to acquire and possess things.|His acquisitiveness led him to amass a huge collection of rare books. 他的占有欲使他积累了大量的珍本书。

Her acquisitiveness knows no bounds. 她的贪得无厌是无止境的。

|近义词：greed, avarice, covetousness, materialism|反义词：generosity, altruism|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *acquisitīvus, from Latin acquīsītus (“acquired”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *kʷey- (“to seek, get”).

USAGE->
Her acquisitiveness has led her to a vast fortune. = 她的贪婪使她拥有了巨大的财富。

His acquisitiveness made him unpopular. = 他的贪婪让他不受欢迎。


PHRASE->
unchecked acquisitiveness =  不受控制的贪婪
insatiable acquisitiveness =  永不知足的贪婪
growing acquisitiveness =  日益增长的贪婪
acquisitiveness for power =  对权力的贪婪
acquisitiveness of knowledge = 对知识的渴望 (注意：这个词组的含义通常是积极的)
