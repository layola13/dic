allemannic:/ˌæləˈmænɪk/ | adj. | 阿勒曼尼的，阿勒曼尼人的 | Relating to the Alemanni, a Germanic people who inhabited the upper Rhine region in ancient times, or their language or culture. |  The museum has a fascinating exhibit on Allemannic art from the early Middle Ages.  博物馆里有一个关于中世纪早期阿勒曼尼艺术的精彩展览。| Synonyms: Swabian, Alsatian (sometimes) | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could use non-Germanic or a specific other Germanic group, e.g., Frankish) | adjective

allemannic:/ˌæləˈmænɪk/ | n. | 阿勒曼尼语 | The dialects of German spoken in southwestern Germany, Switzerland, Alsace, and Austria, descended from the language of the Alemanni. |  She speaks a distinct form of Allemannic.  她说一种独特的阿勒曼尼语。| Synonyms: Alemannic German | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, could use Standard German or another specific Germanic language) | noun
