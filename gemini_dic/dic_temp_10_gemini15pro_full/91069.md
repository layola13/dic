hutches:/ˈhʌtʃɪz/| n.|笼子（尤指养兔子等小动物的）;碗橱;小棚屋|A hutch is a cage, usually made of wood and wire mesh, for keeping small animals such as rabbits. It can also refer to a cupboard or a small shed.|The rabbit lives in a hutch in the garden. 兔子住在花园里的笼子里。

The antique hutch in the kitchen held all of her grandmother's china. 厨房里的古董碗橱里放着她祖母所有的瓷器。|近义词：cage, pen, coop, cupboard, cabinet|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以根据具体含义选择，例如如果指笼子，反义词可以是"open space" 开阔空间)|词性:noun (plural)
