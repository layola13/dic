engages:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒɪz/| v.|参与，从事，吸引|To occupy or attract someone's interest or attention; to participate or become involved in something.|The speaker engaged the audience with his humorous anecdotes. 演讲者用幽默的轶事吸引了观众。|近义词：involve, occupy, captivate|反义词:disengage, ignore, detach|verb

engaged:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒd/| adj.|已订婚的，忙碌的，参与的|Having formally agreed to marry; busy or occupied; involved in something.|The engaged couple planned a destination wedding.  已订婚的夫妇计划举行一个目的地婚礼。
The CEO was engaged in an important meeting. 首席执行官正在参加一个重要的会议。|近义词：betrothed, busy, involved|反义词:unengaged, free, uninvolved|adjective
