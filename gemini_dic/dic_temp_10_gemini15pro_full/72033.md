flippantnesses:/ˈflɪpəntnəsəz/
n.
轻率；轻浮；油嘴滑舌(复数)
The state or quality of being flippant; remarks or actions that are disrespectful and not serious.
His flippantnesses during the serious meeting were inappropriate. 他在严肃的会议上的轻浮举止是不恰当的。
frivolity, levity, impertinence
seriousness, gravity, respectfulness
noun

flippantness:/ˈflɪpəntnəs/
n.
轻率；轻浮；油嘴滑舌
The state or quality of being flippant; remarks or actions that are disrespectful and not serious.
Her flippantness about the situation angered everyone. 她对这种情况的轻浮态度激怒了所有人。
frivolity, levity, impertinence

noun

flippant:/ˈflɪpənt/
adj.
轻率的；轻浮的；油嘴滑舌的
Not showing a serious or respectful attitude.
He gave a flippant response to a serious question. 他对一个严肃的问题作出了轻率的回答。
frivolous, cheeky, impertinent
serious, respectful, solemn
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
