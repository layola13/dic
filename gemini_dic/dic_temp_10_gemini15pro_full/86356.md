hectograph: /ˈhɛktəˌɡræf/ | n. |  油印机；复写版；用油印机印出的复制品 | A duplicating machine that uses a gelatin or other surface from which a number of copies can be made.  A copy made on such a machine. |  The teacher used a hectograph to make copies of the test. 老师用油印机印了试卷。 | synonyms: duplicator, copier, mimeograph | antonyms: original | noun

hectograph: /ˈhɛktəˌɡræf/ | vt. | 用油印机复制 | To copy using a hectograph. | He hectographed the newsletter and distributed it to the class. 他用油印机复制了通讯稿，并分发给全班。 | synonyms: duplicate, copy, reproduce | antonyms: originate | verb
