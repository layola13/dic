expressness:/ɪkˈsprɛsnəs/
n.
表达性，明确性
The quality of being expressive or explicit.
Her expressness left no room for doubt about her feelings. 她的明确表达使她的感受不容置疑。
explicitness, directness, articulateness, clarity
vagueness, ambiguity, obscurity
