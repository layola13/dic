despondingly:/dɪˈspɒndɪŋli/ /dɪˈspɑːndɪŋli/
adverb
沮丧地，失望地
in a despondent manner; showing or feeling profound hopelessness.
She sighed despondingly and turned away. 她沮丧地叹了口气，转过身去。
He shook his head despondingly. 他沮丧地摇了摇头。

近义词: dejectedly, despairingly, glumly, hopelessly, miserably, sadly
反义词: cheerfully, hopefully, joyfully, optimistically

词性: adverb

词源: From despondent + -ly. Despondent comes from the Latin despondēre  "to lose heart, give up," from de- "down" + spondere "to promise, engage oneself."  The original sense was of "renouncing hope."
