ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + accuracy.  Accuracy comes from Latin *accūrātia, from accūrātus (“prepared with care, exact, accurate”), past participle of accūrō (“I take care of”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + cūra (“care”).
