overgesticulative: /ˌōvərjəˈstɪkjʊlətɪv/
adjective
过于手势化的；过分做手势的
Characterized by excessive or exaggerated use of gestures.
The overgesticulative speaker made it difficult to focus on the content of his presentation.  过于手势化的演讲者让人很难集中注意力在他的演讲内容上。
His overgesticulative mannerisms distracted the audience.  他过分做手势的习惯分散了观众的注意力。
Synonyms: demonstrative, animated, exuberant, theatrical
Antonyms: restrained, subdued, understated
词性: adjective
