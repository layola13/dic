flowerier:/ˈflaʊəriər/ (US), /ˈflaʊəriə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更加华丽的，更加辞藻华丽的|More flowery; having more flowers or flowery decorations.  Characterized by elaborate or ornate language.|Her writing style is much flowerier now than it used to be. 她的写作风格现在比以前华丽多了。|近义词：ornate, elaborate, flamboyant, embellished|反义词：plain, simple, unadorned|词性:adjective

Etymology: From flower + -y + -er (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from Old French *florir (to flower).
