bulldozes:/ˈbʊldəʊzɪz/
verb
推平；摧毁；强行通过
Third person singular present tense form of bulldoze.  It refers to the action of clearing land or demolishing something using a bulldozer, or metaphorically, forcing something through.
The construction company bulldozes the old buildings to make way for the new shopping mall.  建筑公司推倒了旧建筑，为新的购物中心腾出空间。
The government bulldozes through unpopular legislation. 政府强行通过不受欢迎的立法。
synonyms:demolishes, levels, flattens, clears, forces
antonyms:constructs, builds, preserves, protects
v.
