ceratosaurus:/ˌsɛrətoʊˈsɔːrəs/| n. | 角鼻龙 | A large, carnivorous dinosaur of the late Jurassic period, characterized by a horn on its nose and two smaller horns above its eyes. |  The ceratosaurus was a fearsome predator. 角鼻龙是一种可怕的掠食者。| The museum displayed a complete ceratosaurus skeleton.  博物馆展出了一具完整的角鼻龙骨骼。 | synonym: theropod, carnivore | antonym: herbivore | noun
