corollaceous: /ˌkɒrəˈleɪʃəs/ | adj. | 花冠状的；合瓣花的 | Having petals united to form a corolla, characteristic of plants in the family Corollaceae. | Example: The corollaceous flowers of the morning glory vine climbed the trellis. 牵牛花的合瓣花爬上了棚架。| Example:  Many members of the nightshade family have corollaceous flowers. 许多茄科植物都有合瓣花。| Synonym: gamopetalous, sympetalous | Antonym: polypetalous | Word Type: Adjective
