bulbil:/ˈbʌlbɪl/ | n. | 珠芽；小鳞茎；气生鳞茎 |A small bulblike organ produced in the leaf axils or on flower stalks of certain plants, such as the tiger lily or some onions, and capable of developing into a new plant.|  The tiger lily propagates through bulbils that form at the base of its leaves.  虎皮百合通过叶基部形成的珠芽繁殖。

The bulbils on the onion plant can be planted to grow new onions.  洋葱植株上的珠芽可以种植以培育新的洋葱。 | synonyms: bulblet, offset, propagule | antonyms: N/A | noun
