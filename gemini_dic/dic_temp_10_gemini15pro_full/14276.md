autographed: /ˈɔːtəɡræft/ | adj. | 亲笔签名的 | Signed by the person mentioned; bearing an autograph. |  例句：I have an autographed copy of her latest novel. 我有一本她最新小说的亲笔签名版。

例句：The autographed baseball was his most prized possession.  这个签名的棒球是他最珍贵的财产。


近义词：signed | 反义词：unsigned | 词性：adjective
