adapts:/əˈdæpts/| v.|适应，改编|To change something to suit different conditions or uses.|例句：The chameleon adapts its skin color to its surroundings. 变色龙会根据周围环境改变肤色。

The play was adapted from a novel.  这出戏是由小说改编的。|近义词：adjusts, modifies, conforms, accommodates|反义词:resists, remains|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adaptare, from Latin ad- "to" + aptare "to fit"

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He adapts quickly to new situations. = 他能很快适应新环境。
She adapts her teaching methods to suit different students. = 她调整教学方法以适应不同的学生。

一般过去时:
The company adapted its marketing strategy to the changing market. = 公司根据不断变化的市场调整了营销策略。
They adapted the old house into a modern office. = 他们把老房子改建成了现代化的办公室。

现在完成时:
The animals have adapted to the harsh climate. = 动物们已经适应了恶劣的气候。
The director has adapted the novel for the screen. = 导演已经将这部小说改编成了电影。

过去完成时:
By the time we arrived, they had adapted to the local customs. = 等我们到达时，他们已经适应了当地的风俗习惯。
She had adapted the recipe before she tried it. = 在尝试之前，她已经修改了食谱。


将来时:
We will adapt to the new rules soon. = 我们很快就会适应新规则。
They will adapt the building for disabled access. = 他们将改造这栋建筑以方便残疾人出入。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would adapt to the situation. = 如果我是你，我会适应这种情况。
I wish he would adapt his behavior. = 我希望他能改变他的行为。

被动语态:
The story was adapted for children. = 这个故事被改编成了儿童版。
The car has been adapted to run on electricity. = 这辆车已经被改装成电动车。


疑问句:
How do animals adapt to their environment? = 动物如何适应它们的环境？
Has the script been adapted for television? = 剧本是否已改编成电视剧？

否定句:
He doesn't adapt well to change. = 他不适应改变。
The play wasn't adapted from a real story. = 这部戏不是根据真实故事改编的。

PHRASE->
adapt to = 适应
adapt from = 改编自
well adapted = 非常适应
poorly adapted = 不适应
adapt oneself to = 使自己适应
