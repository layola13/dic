engel:/ˈɛŋɡəl/ | n. | 天使; 安琪儿 | A spiritual being believed to act as an attendant, agent, or messenger of God, conventionally represented in human form with wings. |  She believes her deceased grandmother is her guardian angel. 她相信她已故的祖母是她的守护天使。


| | | | The child painted a picture of an angel with large white wings.  孩子画了一幅长着大白翅膀的天使图画。


| 近义词: seraph, cherub | 反义词: devil, demon | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *engel, from Old English engel (akin to Old Saxon engil, Old Frisian engel, Old High German engil, Old Norse engill, Gothic aggilus), all from Late Latin angelus, from Greek ἄγγελος (ángelos, “messenger”), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂énǵelos (“messenger”).
