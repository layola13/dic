colluding:/kəˈluːdɪŋ/
verb
串通，勾结
acting together secretly or illegally
The companies were accused of colluding to fix prices. 这些公司被指控串通操纵价格。
近义词:conspiring, plotting, scheming
反义词:cooperating, collaborating
词性:Verb (present participle of collude)

collude:/kəˈluːd/
verb
串通，勾结
to act together secretly or illegally
Several officials colluded in the cover-up. 几名官员串通一气掩盖真相。
近义词:conspire, plot, scheme
反义词:cooperate, collaborate
词性:Verb

词源:
From Latin colludere "to play together," from com- "together" (see com-) + ludere "to play" (see ludicrous). The notion is of "playing into each other's hands."  Meaning "conspire, intrigue" is first recorded 1610s.
