forewend:/fɔːrˈwɛnd/
verb
预示，预兆
to have a presentiment of; forebode.  It also can mean to avert, prevent, or ward off.
The dark clouds forewended a storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨。
He tried to forewend the disaster by warning everyone. 他试图通过警告所有人来避免灾难。
近义词:forebode, portend, presage, augur, foreshadow
反义词:None readily available, possibly invite, encourage (depending on context)
词性:Verb
