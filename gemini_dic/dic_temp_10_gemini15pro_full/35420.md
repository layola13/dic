co-ordinating:/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
协调的，同等的
bringing different elements into a relationship that ensures efficiency or harmony.
The co-ordinating committee meets monthly to discuss progress on the project.  协调委员会每月开会讨论项目进展。
She has excellent co-ordinating skills and can manage multiple tasks efficiently. 她具有优秀的协调能力，可以高效地管理多项任务。
近义词:harmonizing, synchronizing, integrating
反义词: conflicting, clashing
词性:adjective

co-ordinate:/koʊˈɔːrdɪneɪt/
verb
协调，配合
to bring into a common action, movement, or condition : regulate, adjust.
We need to co-ordinate our efforts to achieve the best results. 我们需要协调我们的努力以达到最佳效果。
The dancers co-ordinated their movements perfectly. 舞蹈演员们的动作配合得天衣无缝。
近义词:organize, harmonize, synchronize
反义词:disorganize, separate
词性:verb

co-ordinate:/koʊˈɔːrdɪnət/
noun
坐标
any of a set of numbers used to specify the location of a point.
The GPS co-ordinates will lead you to the treasure. GPS坐标将引导你找到宝藏。
Please provide the precise co-ordinates of the accident site. 请提供事故现场的精确坐标。
近义词:position, location
反义词: N/A
词性:noun
