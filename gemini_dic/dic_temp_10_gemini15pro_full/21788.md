bobbish:/ˈbɒbɪʃ/ | adj. | 略微醉酒的，微醺的 | Slightly drunk; tipsy. | He was feeling a bit bobbish after a couple of beers. 他喝了几杯啤酒后感觉有点微醺。 |  Example: After a couple of glasses of wine, she felt quite bobbish.  几杯酒下肚后，她感觉有点微醺。|  Synonyms: tipsy, merry, slightly drunk | Antonyms: sober, clear-headed | Adjective
