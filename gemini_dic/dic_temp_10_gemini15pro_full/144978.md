overaccumulates:/ˌoʊvərəˈkjuːmjəleɪts/
|v.|过度积累|accumulates excessively or to an excessive degree.  The company overaccumulates inventory, leading to storage problems.|近义词:overstock, hoard|反义词:deplete, reduce|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + accumulate.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much". "Accumulate" comes from the Latin "accumulare," meaning "to heap up."
