overinformed:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈfɔːrmd/
adjective
过度知情的；被过多信息包围的
Having or receiving too much information, often to the point of confusion or inaction.
The overinformed consumer may actually be less likely to make a purchase.  过度知情的消费者实际上可能不太可能购买。
He felt overinformed and overwhelmed by the sheer volume of data. 他感觉信息过载，被海量的数据压得喘不过气。
近义词: oversaturated, bombarded (with information)
反义词: uninformed, underinformed
词性: adjective
