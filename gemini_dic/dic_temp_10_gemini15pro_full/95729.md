inconsiderableness:/ɪnkənˌsɪdərəb(ə)lnəs/| n.|微不足道，不重要|The inconsiderableness of the amount made it hardly worth mentioning.  数额微不足道，几乎不值一提。|The inconsiderableness of the issue led to its being overlooked. 这个问题无关紧要，导致它被忽视了。|近义词：insignificance, triviality, unimportance|反义词：importance, significance, consequence|词性:noun

Etymology:
From in- (not) + considerable (worthy of consideration) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  Ultimately from Latin *considerabilis* (worthy of consideration), from *considerare* (to consider).
