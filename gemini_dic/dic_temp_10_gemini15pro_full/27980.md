canvas:/ˈkænvəs/ (US) /ˈkænvəs/ (UK)| n. |帆布；画布；油画|A strong, closely woven cloth used for making tents, sails, and artists' canvases. Also refers to the painting itself.|The artist stretched the canvas before beginning to paint. 艺术家在开始绘画前先把画布绷紧。|近义词：fabric, cloth, material|反义词: None|词性:noun

canvas:/ˈkænvəs/ (US) /ˈkænvəs/ (UK)| v. |游说；拉选票|To solicit votes or support, especially in a political context.|The candidates canvassed the neighborhood looking for votes. 候选人在附近游说以争取选票。|近义词：solicit, campaign|反义词: None|词性:verb
