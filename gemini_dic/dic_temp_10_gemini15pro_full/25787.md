bureaucratically: /ˌbjʊərəˈkrætɪkli/ | adv. | 官僚主义地；官样文章地 | In a way that relates to or involves excessive and complicated administrative procedures. | The request was bureaucratically processed, taking several weeks to complete.  这个请求被官僚地处理，花了几个星期才完成。 | Example: The issue was dealt with bureaucratically.  这个问题被官僚地处理了。| Synonyms: officiously, formally, rigidly, by the book | Antonyms: informally, flexibly, efficiently |  word type: adverb

Etymology: From bureaucrat + -ically.  Bureaucrat comes from French bureaucrate, from bureau (desk, office) + -crate (from Greek -kratia, meaning "power, rule").
