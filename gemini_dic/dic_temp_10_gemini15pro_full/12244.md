ascendancy:/əˈsɛndənsi/ | n. |优势，支配地位，权势 |A position of dominant power or influence. |例句：The ascendancy of the new government brought about significant changes. 新政府的掌权带来了巨大的变化。

例句：Their party's ascendancy in the polls was short-lived.  他们在民意调查中的领先地位是短暂的。

近义词：dominance, supremacy, control, power, authority |反义词: subordination, weakness, impotence |词性: 名词

ascendant:/əˈsɛndənt/ | adj. |上升的，有影响力的 |Rising in power or influence. |例句：The ascendant star of the tech industry is rapidly gaining market share.  这家科技行业的后起之秀正在迅速抢占市场份额。

例句：He is an ascendant political figure in the region. 他是该地区一个正在崛起的政治人物。

近义词：rising, influential, powerful |反义词: declining, waning, falling |词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin *ascendentia, from Latin ascendens, present participle of ascendere "to climb up."
