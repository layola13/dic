impressionably:/ɪmˈprɛʃənəbli/
adjective
易受影响地
easily influenced by feelings and emotions; susceptible to impressions
例句：Children at that age are very impressionable.  那个年龄的孩子很容易受影响。

例句：He's at an impressionable age. 他正处在一个易受影响的年龄。

近义词：susceptible, sensitive, receptive, vulnerable, persuadable
反义词：impervious, resistant, unimpressionable
词性：adjective
