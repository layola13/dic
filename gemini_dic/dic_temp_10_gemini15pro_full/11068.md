archaeologies:/ˌɑːrkiˈɒlədʒiz/
n.
考古学（archaeology的复数形式）
the study of human history and prehistory through the excavation of sites and the analysis of artifacts and other physical remains.
例句：New archaeologies are constantly challenging established theories.  新的考古学研究不断挑战既有的理论。

例句：The archaeologies of these ancient civilizations reveal much about their cultures.  这些古代文明的考古学揭示了关于他们文化的许多信息。

近义词：excavations, studies
反义词：(无直接反义词)
词性：名词
