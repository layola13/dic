arundinaceous:/əˌrʌndɪˈneɪʃəs/
adjective
芦苇的；像芦苇的
Resembling or consisting of reeds or canes.  Made of or like reeds.
The arundinaceous stems swayed gently in the breeze.  芦苇般的茎秆在微风中轻轻摇曳。
The bird built its nest among the arundinaceous plants. 这只鸟在芦苇丛中筑巢。
近义词:reedy, cany
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps woody or solid)
词性: adjective
