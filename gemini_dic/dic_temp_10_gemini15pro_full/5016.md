all-consuming:/ɔːl-kənˈsuːmɪŋ/
adjective
耗尽一切的；全身心的；支配一切的
Occupying all of one's time and attention.
His all-consuming passion for art left him little time for anything else. 他对艺术的全身心投入让他几乎没有时间做其他事情。

The all-consuming nature of her work meant she often neglected her personal life. 她工作耗尽了她所有的时间和精力，这意味着她经常忽略自己的个人生活。

Synonyms: obsessive, engrossing, captivating, immersive
Antonyms:  moderate, casual, superficial, half-hearted
词性: 形容词
