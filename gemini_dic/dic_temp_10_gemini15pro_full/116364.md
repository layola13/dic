mastication:/ˌmæstɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 咀嚼; 细嚼; 磨碎 | The process of chewing food.  The thorough chewing of food is important for good digestion. | Example: Proper mastication is crucial for digestion.  良好的咀嚼对消化至关重要。

Example:  The cow's slow mastication of the grass was hypnotic. 母牛缓慢地咀嚼着草，看得人昏昏欲睡。| Synonym: chewing, grinding | Antonym: swallowing, gulping | noun
