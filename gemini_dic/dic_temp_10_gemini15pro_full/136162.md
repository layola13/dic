nonregressively:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈɡrɛs.ɪv.li/ | adv. | 非退化地，不衰退地 |  In a manner that does not regress or decline; progressively.  It implies maintaining or improving a current state or condition, not falling back to a previous, less desirable one. | The company's profits have increased nonregressively for the past five years. 该公司的利润在过去五年里持续增长，没有出现衰退。| The patient's condition improved nonregressively after the surgery. 病人在手术后病情持续好转，没有出现反复。 | 近义词: progressively, steadily, continuously | 反义词: regressively, decliningly, deterioratingly | 词性: adverb
