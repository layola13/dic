afterthoughts:/ˈæftərθɔːts/ | n. | 事后的想法；后思；补充；附言 | An afterthought is something that you think of or add later, after the main thing has already happened or been decided. | I added a postscript to the letter as an afterthought. 我在信后又加了附言作为补充。 | synonyms: reconsideration, second thought, hindsight, addendum | antonyms: forethought, premeditation | noun

afterthought:/ˈæftərθɔːts/ | n. | 事后的想法；后思；补充；附言 |  Something you think of or mention later, that you did not include originally. |  She only invited him as an afterthought. 她只是事后想起才邀请了他。 | synonyms:  addendum, supplement, appendix, postscript | antonyms:  forethought, plan, premeditation | noun
