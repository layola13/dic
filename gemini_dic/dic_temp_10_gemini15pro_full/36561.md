collapsed:/kəˈlæpst/ /kəˈlæpst/| adj.|倒塌的，崩溃的，折叠的|Having fallen down or in; broken down or weakened suddenly:|The roof collapsed under the weight of the snow. 屋顶在雪的重压下坍塌了。|Synonyms: fallen, crumbled, broken down|Antonyms: upright, standing, intact|Adjective


collapsed:/kəˈlæpst/ /kəˈlæpst/| v.|倒塌，崩溃，折叠|To fall down or in; to break down or weaken suddenly:|The old building finally collapsed.  这栋老建筑最终倒塌了。|Synonyms: fall, crumble, give way|Antonyms: build, erect, strengthen|Verb


Etymology: From Latin *collāpsus*, past participle of *collābi* ("to fall together, collapse"), from *com-* ("together") + *lābi* ("to slip, slide, fall").
