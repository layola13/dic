outshoulder:/ˌaʊtˈʃoʊldər/ | v. | 超车（尤指在弯道外侧）; 比…肩膀宽 | To overtake (another vehicle) by passing on the outside of a curve or bend. To have broader shoulders than. | The racing driver outshouldered his opponent on the final bend.  赛车手在最后一个弯道外侧超过了他的对手。 |  Her coat outshouldered her by several inches. 她的外套比她的肩膀宽了好几英寸。 | overtake, pass |  - | verb

outshoulder:/ˌaʊtˈʃoʊldərɪŋ/ | n. |  外侧超车; 比…肩膀宽 | The act of overtaking a vehicle on the outside of a curve. The state of having broader shoulders than something. |  The outshouldering maneuver was risky but successful. 外侧超车很冒险，但成功了。 | overtaking, passing | - | noun
