duumvir: /duːˈʌmvər/ | n. | 双执政官；二人执政员 |  A Roman magistrate, one of two officials jointly holding the same public office, especially in municipalities or colonies. |  The duumvirs were responsible for maintaining order in the town.  双执政官负责维持城镇的秩序。|  Synonyms: co-ruler, joint magistrate | Antonyms: monarch, sole ruler | noun

duumviri: /duːˈʌmvəriː/ | n. | 双执政官（复数） | The plural form of duumvir. | The duumviri oversaw public works projects. 这两位双执政官监督公共工程项目。| Synonyms: co-rulers, joint magistrates | Antonyms: monarchs, sole rulers | noun
