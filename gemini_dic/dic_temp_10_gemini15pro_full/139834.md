octonary: /ˈɒktənəri/ (US) /ˈɒktɪnəri/ (UK)| adj. n.|八进制的，八进制数|Relating to or based on the number eight; a system of numerical notation that has 8 rather than 10 as a base.|The octonary system is less common than the binary or decimal systems.|近义词: base-eight, octal|反义词: decimal, binary|词性: adjective, noun
