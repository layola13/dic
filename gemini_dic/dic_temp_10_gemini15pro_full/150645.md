papist:/ˈpeɪpɪst/| n. |天主教徒（贬义）|A derogatory term for a Roman Catholic, implying excessive devotion to the Pope and the doctrines of the Roman Catholic Church.  Historically used by Protestants, especially during times of religious conflict. |例句：He denounced his opponents as papists and traitors.  他谴责他的对手是天主教徒和叛徒。

例句：The pamphlet was filled with anti-papist rhetoric. 这本小册子充满了反天主教的言辞。|近义词：Catholic (neutral), Roman Catholic (neutral)|反义词：Protestant|词性：名词

Historically, phrases like “damned papist” or “popish plot” were common.  Such phrases are now considered offensive and are rarely used.
