gallinaceous:/ˌɡælɪˈneɪʃəs/| adj.|鸡形的，类似鸡的|Relating to or resembling fowl of the order Galliformes, such as chickens, turkeys, and pheasants.|例句：The guinea fowl, though not closely related, has a gallinaceous appearance.  珍珠鸡虽然关系不密切，但外观像鸡形目动物。

例句：Gallinaceous birds are known for their ground-foraging habits. 鸡形目鸟类以在地面觅食的习性而闻名。|近义词：chicken-like, fowl-like, pheasant-like|反义词: (No clear antonym exists, perhaps non-gallinaceous)|词性: adjective
