overdiscouragement:/ˌoʊvər dɪsˈkɜːrɪdʒmənt/ | n. |过度灰心，极度沮丧|  The state of being excessively discouraged; extreme discouragement. |  His repeated failures led to a state of overdiscouragement. 他屡次失败导致他极度沮丧。| 近义词: despondency, despair, hopelessness | 反义词: encouragement, hope, optimism | 词性: 名词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "discouragement" (from "discourage," from Old French "descoragier")
