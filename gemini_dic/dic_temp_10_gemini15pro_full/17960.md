befogs:/bɪˈfɒɡz/
verb
使模糊，使困惑
Obscures or confuses, as with fog.
The dense fog befogs the road ahead, making it difficult to see. 浓雾使前方的道路模糊不清，难以看清。
The complicated instructions completely befogged me. 复杂的说明让我完全糊涂了。
synonyms: obscures, confuses, muddles, clouds
antonyms: clarifies, elucidates, illuminates
v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + fog.  "Fog" comes from Old Norse "fok" (spray, drift).  The prefix "be-" adds the sense of "covering with" or "affecting with".
