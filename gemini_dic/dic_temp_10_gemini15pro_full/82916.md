gummers:/ˈɡʌmərz/
noun
牙医用器械;磨牙器;磨床工人
Tools used by dentists; people who sharpen tools; grinding machine operators
The dentist used a set of gummers to smooth the patient's teeth. 牙医用一套磨牙器来磨平病人的牙齿。
Synonyms: grinders, sharpeners, honing tools
Antonyms: N/A
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "gum," meaning "to chew" (Old English "gōma," jaw) + "-er," denoting an agent or tool.  The sense of "grinding machine operator" likely developed from the action of the machine resembling chewing or grinding.
