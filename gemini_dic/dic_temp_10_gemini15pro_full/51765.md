devotements:/dɪˈvəʊtmənts/| n. |奉献，献身；致力；热爱|Acts or instances of devoting oneself to a cause, activity, or person; the state of being devoted. |She made many devotements to her art. 她为她的艺术做出了许多奉献。|近义词：dedications, commitments, devotions|反义词：abandonments, neglects|词性: 名词

词源: From the Old French *devoement*, derived from the verb *devoer* "to vow, dedicate," ultimately from Latin *vovere* "to vow."
