endpoint:/ˈɛndˌpɔɪnt/ | n. | 终点，端点 |A point marking the end of a line segment or the end of a process or activity. | The endpoint of the race was marked by a checkered flag. 赛跑的终点用方格旗标出。| synonym: termination, destination, terminus | antonym: beginning, origin, starting point | noun

endpoint:/ˈɛndˌpɔɪnt/ | n. |（计算机网络）端点 | A device or node that communicates with a network. |  The server acts as an endpoint for client requests. 服务器充当客户端请求的端点。 | synonym: node, terminal | antonym: hub, central point | noun
