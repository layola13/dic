levy:/ˈlevi/ (US), /ˈlɛvi/ (UK)| n. vt.|征收，征税；税款|An act of collecting something, such as taxes or troops, by authority or force.  The money or goods collected.|
The government imposed a new levy on imported goods. 政府对进口商品征收了新的税款。
The king levied a tax on salt. 国王征收盐税。|
Synonyms: tax, duty, impost, assessment, tariff|
Antonyms: refund, rebate, remission|
