nonperseverant:/ˌnɒnpɜːsɪˈvɪərənt/ (US) /ˌnɒnpɜːsəˈvɪərənt/ (UK)| adj.|不持之以恒的，缺乏毅力的|Lacking perseverance; not persistent in pursuing something.|He was a brilliant but nonperseverant student, often starting projects but rarely finishing them. 他是一个聪明的但缺乏毅力的学生，经常开始项目但很少完成。|近义词：inconsistent, fickle, erratic, unsteady|反义词：perseverant, persistent, tenacious, determined|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From non- + perseverant.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "perseverant" comes from the Latin "perseverare," meaning "to persist," from "per-" (thoroughly) + "severus" (strict, earnest).
