all-out:/ˌɔːl ˈaʊt/| adj.|全力以赴的，竭尽全力的|Characterized by maximum effort; using all available resources.|The team made an all-out effort to win the championship.  团队为了赢得冠军赛竭尽全力。|近义词：wholehearted, full-blown, maximum|反义词：half-hearted, minimal, restrained|词性:形容词
词源:  From "all out," originally a sporting term meaning "with all players attacking" (especially in cricket), attested from 1920s. Figurative sense of "using all one's resources" from 1939.
