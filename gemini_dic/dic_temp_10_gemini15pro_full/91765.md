hyperemotivity:/ˌhaɪpərɪˌmoʊˈtɪvɪti/ | n. |过度情绪化，情感过度敏感 | Excessive emotional reactivity; a tendency to experience and express emotions with greater intensity than is considered typical. | She exhibited hyperemotivity after the accident, crying and laughing uncontrollably. 事故发生后，她表现出过度情绪化，无法控制地哭笑。 | He struggled with hyperemotivity, often feeling overwhelmed by his feelings. 他与过度情绪化作斗争，经常感到被自己的情绪所淹没。 | 近义词: hypersensitivity, emotional lability | 反义词: emotional stability, apathy | 词性: noun

词源:  From hyper- + emotivity. "Hyper-" is a prefix meaning "over, excessive," from Greek *hupér (over, above, beyond). "Emotivity" refers to the quality of being emotional or easily moved, derived from "emotion."
