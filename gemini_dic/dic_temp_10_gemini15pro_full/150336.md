panical:/ˈpænɪk(ə)l/| n.| 恐慌，惊慌，慌乱 | A sudden overwhelming fear, with or without cause, that produces hysterical or irrational behavior. |  The fire caused a panical in the theatre. 剧院里发生了火灾，引起了一阵恐慌。| synonyms: fear, terror, fright, alarm, dread | antonyms: calmness, composure, tranquility | noun

panicked:/ˈpænɪkt/ | adj. | 惊慌的，恐慌的 | Affected by or exhibiting panic. |  The panicked shoppers rushed for the exits. 惊慌失措的购物者冲向出口。| synonyms: frightened, terrified, alarmed, scared | antonyms: calm, composed, tranquil | adjective
