fury:/ˈfjʊri/ (US) /ˈfjʊəri/ (UK)| n.|愤怒，狂暴，激烈|wild or violent anger|例句：His face was contorted with fury. 他的脸因愤怒而扭曲。

例句：The storm unleashed its fury on the coastal towns.  暴风雨将它的狂怒倾泻在沿海城镇。

近义词：rage, anger, wrath, indignation, violence|反义词：calm, peace, tranquility|词性: noun


fury:/ˈfjʊri/ (US) /ˈfjʊəri/ (UK)| n.|激烈,狂暴|intense, violent, or uncontrolled activity|例句：The battle was fought with unrelenting fury. 这场战斗以持续不断的猛烈进行。

例句：The fury of the rapids made it impossible to navigate the river. 急流的湍急使得在河上航行成为不可能。


近义词：ferocity, vehemence, intensity|反义词：gentleness, mildness, calmness|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *furie, from Old French furie, from Latin furia (“rage, madness, frenzy; a Fury”), from furere (“to rage, be mad”).
