crevasse: /krɪˈvæs/ | n. | 裂缝，裂隙 | A deep open crack, especially in a glacier or other ice mass. |  The climbers had to be careful to avoid the crevasses in the glacier. 登山者必须小心避开冰川上的裂缝。

The crevasse opened up suddenly, swallowing the unsuspecting skier. 裂缝突然裂开，吞没了毫无戒备的滑雪者。


近义词: crack, fissure, chasm, rift | 反义词: closure, seal | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *crevasse*, from Old French *crevace*, ultimately from Latin *crepāre* "to crack, break, burst".
