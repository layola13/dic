marzipanning:/ˈmɑːrzɪˌpænɪŋ/ | n. | 杏仁糖覆盖; 用杏仁糖装饰 | The act of covering or decorating something with marzipan. | The cake was finished with elaborate marzipanning.  这个蛋糕最后用精心制作的杏仁糖装饰。 | 近义词: icing, frosting | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词 (Gerund derived from verb)

Etymology: From marzipan + -ing.  Marzipan itself comes from Italian marzapane, probably ultimately from Arabic.
