combin:/ˈkɒmbɪn/ (UK) /kəmˈbaɪn/ (US)| n. |联合收割机|A combine harvester, or simply combine, is a machine that harvests grain crops. |The farmer used a combine to harvest the wheat. 农民用联合收割机收割小麦。|Synonym: combine harvester, harvester|Antonym: N/A|noun

combin:/ˈkɒmbɪn/ (UK) /kəmˈbaɪn/ (US)| v. |结合，联合|To join or merge to form a single unit or substance.|Hydrogen and oxygen combine to form water. 氢和氧结合形成水。|Synonym: merge, unite, blend, mix|Antonym: separate, divide, split|verb
