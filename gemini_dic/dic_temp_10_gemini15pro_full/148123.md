oversee:/ˌəʊvəˈsiː/ (British) /ˌōvərˈsē/ (American)| v. n.|监督，监管；监工|To watch over and direct the work of others; to supervise.|The manager oversees the daily operations of the store.  经理监督商店的日常运营。|Synonyms: supervise, superintend, manage, direct|Antonyms: neglect, ignore|verb

oversee:/ˈəʊvəsiː/ (British) /ˈoʊvərˌsiːr/ (American)| n.|监工，监督者|A person who watches over and directs the work of others; a supervisor.|The construction overseer ensured the project was completed on time. 建筑监工确保项目按时完成。|Synonyms: supervisor, superintendent, manager, director|Antonyms: subordinate, employee|noun
