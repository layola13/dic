blether:/ˈblɛðər/ (UK), /ˈblɛðɚ/ (US)| vi. n.|闲聊，胡扯；废话|To talk foolishly or at length, especially about unimportant things.  It can also refer to the talk itself.|例句：
They blethered on for hours about nothing.
他们闲聊了几个小时，聊的都是些无关紧要的事情。

例句：
He was full of blether about his latest business venture.
他滔滔不绝地说着他最新的商业冒险，净是些废话。|近义词：chatter, prattle, babble, ramble|反义词：be quiet, be silent|词性:verb, noun

blether:/ˈblɛðər/ (UK), /ˈblɛðɚ/ (US)|n.|闲聊，胡扯；废话|Foolish or idle talk.|例句：
Don't listen to his blether; he doesn't know what he's talking about.
别听他胡扯，他根本不知道自己在说什么。

例句：
It was all just meaningless blether.
那全是毫无意义的废话。|近义词：chatter, prattle, babble, ramble|反义词：sense, truth|词性:noun
