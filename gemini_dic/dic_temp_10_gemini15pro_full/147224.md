overmaturities:/ˌoʊvərˌmətʃʊrətiːz/
| n. | 过期债务；逾期未偿债务 | Debts or loans that have extended past their due date. |  The company's financial report showed a significant increase in overmaturities. 该公司的财务报告显示逾期债务大幅增加。 | Synonyms: overdue debts, past-due loans, expired debts | Antonyms: current debts, paid debts | noun

overmaturity:/ˌoʊvərˌmətʃʊrəti/
| n. | 过熟；过度成熟 | The state of being past the prime or ideal stage of maturity. | The overmaturity of the fruit made it taste unpleasant. 水果过熟导致味道不佳。| Synonyms: overripeness, senescence | Antonyms: immaturity, unripeness | noun
