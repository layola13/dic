lordlinesses:/ˈlɔːrdlɪnɪsɪz/
n.
傲慢；尊贵；威严
The state or quality of being lordly; haughty dignity; imperiousness.
He affected an air of lordliness that alienated his colleagues. 他装出一副高傲的样子，使他的同事们疏远了他。
Synonyms:haughtiness, arrogance, superciliousness, imperiousness, pomposity, aloofness
Antonyms:humility, modesty, meekness, humbleness
词性: noun (plural)
