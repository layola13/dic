anticlassical:/ˌæntiˈklæsɪk(ə)l/
adjective
反古典的，非古典的
Opposed to or rejecting the principles of classicism.
Anticlassical art often rejects traditional forms and embraces experimentation. 反古典艺术通常拒绝传统形式，并拥抱实验。
His anticlassical approach to composition challenged the established norms. 他反古典的创作方法挑战了既定的规范。
近义词:modernist, avant-garde, unconventional, nonclassical
反义词:classical, traditional, conventional
词性:adjective
