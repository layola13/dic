overstrow: /ˌoʊvərˈstroʊ/
verb
覆盖；散布；战胜；击败
to cover completely; to scatter or spread something over a surface; to defeat or conquer
例句：
The field was overstrown with wildflowers. 田野里开满了野花。

The defeated army was overstrown across the battlefield.  败军遍布战场。


近义词：cover, strew, scatter, defeat, overcome, vanquish
反义词：uncover, gather, collect, lose, surrender
词性：v.


overstrow: /ˌoʊvərˈstroʊ/
noun (archaic)
覆盖物；散布物
something that covers or is scattered over a surface
例句：
An overstrow of leaves covered the forest floor. 一层落叶覆盖着森林地面。

The overstrow of confetti made the streets festive.  五彩纸屑的散布使街道充满了节日气氛。


近义词：covering, layer, scattering
反义词：(None -  The noun form is archaic and doesn't have a direct antonym)
词性：n. (archaic)


词源: From Middle English *overstrewen, from Old English *oferstrēowian, from ofer- (over) + strēowian (to strew).
