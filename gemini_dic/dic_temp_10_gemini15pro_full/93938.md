immunodeficient:/ˌɪmjuːnoʊdɪˈfɪʃənt/ | adj. | 免疫缺陷的 | Having an impaired immune system; lacking the normal ability to resist infection or disease. |  People with HIV are immunodeficient and more susceptible to infections.  HIV感染者免疫缺陷，更容易受到感染。| synonyms: immunocompromised, immunosuppressed | antonyms: immunocompetent | adjective

immunodeficient:/ˌɪmjuːnoʊdɪˈfɪʃənt/ | n. | 免疫缺陷者 | A person who has an impaired immune system. |  Immunodeficients require special care to avoid infections. 免疫缺陷者需要特别护理以避免感染。| synonyms: immunocompromised individual | antonyms: healthy individual | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "immuno-" (relating to the immune system) + "deficient" (lacking).  "Immuno-" ultimately derives from Latin "immunis" (exempt, free), while "deficient" comes from Latin "deficiēns" (failing, wanting).
