gollum:/ˈɡɒləm/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːləm/ (US)| n.|咕噜，咕鲁|A slimy, wretched creature from J.R.R. Tolkien's fantasy novel *The Hobbit* and *The Lord of the Rings*. Gollum was once a hobbit-like creature named Sméagol, but the One Ring corrupted him, twisting his body and mind. He is obsessed with the Ring, referring to it as "my precious."|例句：Gollum crept through the tunnels, whispering to himself about his precious. 咕噜在隧道中爬行，自言自语地说着他的宝贝。

例句：The movie's portrayal of Gollum was groundbreaking, using motion capture technology to bring the character to life.  这部电影对咕噜的刻画是开创性的，使用了动作捕捉技术将这个角色赋予了生命。
