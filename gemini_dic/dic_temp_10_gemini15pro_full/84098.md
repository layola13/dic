half-intellectual:/ˌhæf ˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəl/
|adj.| 半吊子知识分子；略有学识的；附庸风雅的|Someone who is considered to have some intellectual pretensions but not genuine intellectual depth or rigor.|He dismissed his critics as half-intellectual snobs. 他把批评他的人斥为附庸风雅的半吊子知识分子。|Near synonyms: pseudo-intellectual, dilettante, partly educated|Antonyms: intellectual, scholarly, erudite
|词性: 形容词
