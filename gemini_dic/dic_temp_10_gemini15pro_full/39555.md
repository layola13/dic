conscient:/ˈkɒnʃənt/ | adj. | 有意识的，知道的，觉察到的 | Aware; conscious.  This word is somewhat archaic and less common than "conscious." | He was conscient of his guilt. 他意识到自己的罪行。| 近义词: conscious, aware, mindful | 反义词: unconscious, unaware, oblivious | 词性: adjective

词源: From Old French conscient, from Latin conscientem, present participle of conscire (“to be conscious”), from con- (“with”) + scire (“to know”).
