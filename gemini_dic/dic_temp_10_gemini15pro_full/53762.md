discomfort:/dɪsˈkʌmfərt/| n. |不适，不舒服；不安|A feeling of slight pain or physical uneasiness.|例句：The tight shoes caused him considerable discomfort.  这双紧的鞋子让他感到很不舒服。|近义词：unease, distress, pain, soreness|反义词:comfort, ease, relief|noun
discomfort:/dɪsˈkʌmfərt/| vt. |使不适，使不安|To cause someone to feel slight pain or physical uneasiness; to make someone feel anxious or embarrassed.|例句：The bright lights discomfited her. 刺眼的灯光让她感到不舒服。|近义词：disturb, trouble, upset, bother|反义词:comfort, soothe, reassure|verb
