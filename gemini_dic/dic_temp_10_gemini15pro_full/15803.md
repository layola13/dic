balkanize:/ˈbɔːlkənaɪz/ /ˈbælkənaɪz/| vt.|巴尔干化|To divide (a region or body) into smaller mutually hostile states or groups.|例句：The country was balkanized after the war.  战后，这个国家分裂成几个相互敌对的小国。

例句：Politicians fear that the new law will balkanize the education system. 政治家们担心新法律会使教育系统分裂。|近义词：fragment, divide, split, partition|反义词：unite, integrate, consolidate|词性：动词

balkanizing:/ˈbɔːlkənaɪzɪŋ/ /ˈbælkənaɪzɪŋ/| n. |巴尔干化|The act of balkanizing; division into smaller, often hostile units.|例句：The balkanizing of the region has led to increased tensions. 该地区的巴尔干化导致紧张局势加剧。

例句：The report warns of the dangers of balkanizing the internet. 该报告警告了互联网分裂的危险。|近义词：fragmentation, division, splitting, partitioning|反义词：unification, integration, consolidation|词性：名词
