conviviality:/kənˌvɪviˈælɪti/| n.|欢乐；宴饮； convivial: /kənˈvɪviəl/ adj. |The atmosphere of conviviality at the party was infectious.  派对上欢乐的气氛很有感染力。|  Friendliness and good cheer, especially when associated with eating and drinking together.  特别是与一起吃喝相关的友好和愉快的气氛。|例句：The conviviality of the gathering made everyone feel welcome. 聚会的欢乐气氛让每个人都感到宾至如归。

例句：The restaurant had a reputation for its conviviality and lively atmosphere. 这家餐厅以其欢乐和热闹的气氛而闻名。

近义词：joviality, sociability, cheerfulness, festivity, merriment|反义词:dourness, gloom, sullenness|词性:noun

convivial:/kənˈvɪviəl/| adj.|欢乐的；好交际的；|Friendly, lively, and enjoyable, especially when associated with eating and drinking together. 特别是与一起吃喝相关的友好、活泼和愉快的。|例句：We had a convivial evening with friends, enjoying good food and wine. 我们和朋友们度过了一个愉快的夜晚，享受着美味佳肴和葡萄酒。

例句：He was a convivial host, always making sure his guests were comfortable and entertained. 他是一位热情好客的主人，总是确保他的客人们感到舒适和受到款待。

近义词：jovial, sociable, cheerful, festive, merry|反义词:unsociable, unfriendly, dour, gloomy, sullen|词性:adjective
