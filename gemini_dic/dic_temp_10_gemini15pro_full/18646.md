benzoin:/ˈbɛnzoʊɪn/ | n. |安息香，苯偶姻|A fragrant resin obtained from the bark of certain trees in Southeast Asia, used in perfumes, incense, and medicine. Also, an aromatic ketone used in organic synthesis.|The perfume had a distinct scent of benzoin. 这款香水有明显的安息香气味。|近义词:gum benjamin, styrax,  |反义词: None | noun

benzoin:/ˈbɛnzoʊɪn/ | n. |苯偶姻|An aromatic ketone used in organic synthesis.|Benzoin condensation is a classic reaction in organic chemistry. 苯偶姻缩合是有机化学中的一个经典反应。|近义词:2-hydroxy-1,2-diphenylethanone|反义词: None | noun
