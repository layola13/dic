nonperiodically:/ˌnɒnˌpɪəriˈɒdɪkli/ /ˌnɑːnˌpɪriˈɑːdɪkli/| adv.|非周期性地，不定期地|Not occurring at regular intervals; not periodic.|The comet appears nonperiodically, making its orbit unpredictable. 这颗彗星不定期出现，使得它的轨道无法预测。| The machine malfunctions nonperiodically, causing frustration for the operators.  这台机器不定期地发生故障，这使用户感到很沮丧。 |近义词：aperiodically, irregularly, sporadically|反义词: periodically, regularly|词性:副词

Etymology:
non- + periodically.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "periodically" means "at regular intervals."  The root of "periodically" is "period," ultimately from the Greek "periodos" (circuit, cycle).
