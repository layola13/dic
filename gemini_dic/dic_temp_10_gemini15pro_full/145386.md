overcast:/ˌoʊvərˈkæst/ /ˌəʊvəˈkɑːst/| adj.|阴天的，多云的|Covered with clouds; cloudy.|例句：The sky was overcast and threatening rain. 天空阴沉，预示着要下雨。|例句：It was a cold, overcast day. 这是一个寒冷阴天。|近义词：cloudy, dull, gray|反义词：sunny, clear, bright|词性:形容词

overcast:/ˌoʊvərˈkæst/ /ˌəʊvəˈkɑːst/| v.|(使)阴暗，遮蔽|To cover with clouds or darkness.|例句：Clouds overcast the sky. 云层遮蔽了天空。|例句：A shadow overcast his face.  一个阴影遮蔽了他的脸。|近义词：darken, obscure, shadow|反义词：brighten, illuminate|词性:动词

词源: From Old English *oferceast, past participle of *ofercastan "to overthrow, overcome," from ofer- "over" + -ceast, related to German werfen "to throw."  The meaning shifted from "overthrown" to "covered over" (as if by clouds).
