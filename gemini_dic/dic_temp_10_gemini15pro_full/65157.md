ethnicism:/ˈeθnɪˌsɪzəm/
n.|种族主义，种族歧视|The belief in the inherent superiority of one's own ethnic group or culture.  A tendency to view alien groups or cultures from the perspective of one's own.|His speech was filled with ethnicism, alienating many in the diverse audience. 他的演讲充满了种族主义，疏远了众多不同族裔的听众。|近义词：racism, bigotry, prejudice, discrimination|反义词:tolerance, acceptance, multiculturalism|词性:noun
