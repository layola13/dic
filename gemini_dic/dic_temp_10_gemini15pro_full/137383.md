nontemporizingly:/nɒnˈtɛmpəraɪzɪŋli/ /nɑːnˈtɛmpəraɪzɪŋli/
adverb
果断地，毫不拖延地
Without delay; resolutely and decisively.
The general nontemporizingly ordered the attack. 将军果断地下令进攻。
The company nontemporizingly addressed the customer complaints. 公司毫不拖延地处理了客户的投诉。
近义词: decisively, resolutely, promptly, immediately, directly
反义词: hesitantly, indecisively, slowly, procrastinatingly
词性: adverb

词源:
From "nontemporizing" + "-ly".  "Nontemporizing" is formed from "non-" (not) + "temporizing" (delaying). "Temporizing" comes from the Late Latin "temporizare" (to comply with the times), from "tempus" (time).
