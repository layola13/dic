abstemiousness:/ˌæbˈstiːmiəsnəs/| n.|节制，禁欲|The state of being abstemious, marked by moderation or abstinence, especially in eating and drinking.|He was known for his abstemiousness, rarely indulging in rich foods or alcohol. 他以节制闻名，很少吃油腻的食物或喝酒。

She practiced abstemiousness in her spending habits. 她消费习惯节俭。|近义词：moderation, temperance, sobriety, restraint|反义词：gluttony, indulgence, excess|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *abstemiōsus* ("abstaining from wine"), from Latin *ab* ("from") + *temum* ("strong drink, wine").
