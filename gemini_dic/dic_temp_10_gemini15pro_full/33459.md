chutes:/ʃuːts/ | n. | 斜槽，滑道，瀑布 | A sloping channel or slide for conveying things to a lower level.  Also, a waterfall or rapids. | The log flume uses chutes to carry the logs down the mountain. 原木水道使用滑槽将原木运下山。| synonyms: slides, ramps, channels, waterfalls, rapids | antonyms: ascents, climbs | noun

chutes:/ʃuːts/ | n. | 降落伞（尤指引导飞机紧急着陆的）| A parachute, especially one used to make an emergency landing of an aircraft. | The pilot deployed the chutes after the engine failed.  发动机故障后，飞行员展开了降落伞。| synonyms: parachutes | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French "chute" (fall, drop), from the verb "choir" (to fall), from Latin "cadere" (to fall).
