alluvia: /əˈluːviə/ | n. | 冲积层，冲积土 | deposits of earth, sand, and other matter left by rivers, floods, etc. | The fertile alluvia of the Nile Valley have supported agriculture for millennia. 尼罗河谷肥沃的冲积土养育了数千年的农业。 |  The river deposited rich alluvia on the floodplain. 这条河在漫滩上沉积了丰富的冲积土。 | synonyms: sediment, deposit, silt, alluvion | antonyms: bedrock | noun
