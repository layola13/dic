houseman:/ˈhaʊsmən/| n.|男住客；房客|A male resident of a house, especially a lodging house.|例句：The houseman complained about the noise from the street.  男房客抱怨街上的噪音。

例句：The landlady asked the houseman to pay his rent. 女房东要求男房客付房租。|近义词：resident, tenant, lodger|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun


词源: From "house" + "man".  The meaning related to a hospital resident developed in the US in the 20th century.
