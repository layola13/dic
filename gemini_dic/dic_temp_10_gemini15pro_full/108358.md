lavishers:/ˈlævɪʃərz/
noun
挥霍者；慷慨给予者
People who spend or give something freely or generously.
The lavishers of praise were few and far between.  赞美之词少之又少。
Synonyms: spendthrifts, prodigals, benefactors
Antonyms: misers, hoarders
词性: 名词(plural)

lavish:/ˈlævɪʃ/
adjective
奢华的；丰富的；慷慨的
Characterized by extravagance and abundance; bestowed liberally or generously.
They lived a lavish lifestyle. 他们过着奢华的生活。
Synonyms: luxurious, opulent, profuse, generous
Antonyms: meager, sparse, stingy
词性: 形容词

lavish:/ˈlævɪʃ/
verb
 lavish something on somebody/something  
慷慨地给予；挥霍
To expend or bestow something liberally or generously.
He lavished attention on his guests. 他对客人关怀备至。
Synonyms: shower, bestow, heap
Antonyms: withhold, stint, begrudge
词性: 动词
