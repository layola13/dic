czars:/ˈzɑːrz/ /ˈsɑːrz/| n.|沙皇；独裁者|A person with great power and authority, especially one who controls an area of activity.  Originally, the title of the emperors of Russia.|例句：He was known as the energy czar. 他被称为能源沙皇。

例句：The drug czar announced a new initiative. 禁毒署署长宣布了一项新举措。|近义词：autocrat, dictator, ruler, monarch, emperor, sovereign, potentate, tycoon, magnate, mogul, chief, boss, leader, head, overlord|反义词:subordinate, follower|词性:名词
