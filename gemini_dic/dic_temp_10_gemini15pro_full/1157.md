accite: /əˈsaɪt/
verb
召唤，传唤
to summon or call forth
The king accited his advisors to the throne room. 国王把他的顾问们召集到王座室。

The court accited the witness to testify. 法院传唤证人作证。

近义词: summon, call, invite, bid
反义词: dismiss, send away
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin accitus, past participle of accio ("I summon").
