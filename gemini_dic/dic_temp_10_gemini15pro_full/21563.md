blues:/bluːz/
n.|布鲁斯；忧郁；沮丧|A type of slow, sad music that developed among African Americans in the southern US.|She listened to the blues to soothe her broken heart. 她听着布鲁斯音乐来抚慰她破碎的心。|synonyms: melancholy, depression, sadness|antonyms: happiness, joy, elation|noun
blues:/bluːz/
n.|蓝色制服；蓝颜色|Blue clothing, often a uniform.|The police officer was wearing his blues. 那位警官穿着他的蓝色制服。|synonyms: blue, uniform|antonyms: - |noun
