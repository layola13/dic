mitigatory:/ˈmɪtɪɡətɔːri/
mitigatory| adj.| 减轻的，缓和的|Having the effect of making something less harmful, serious, or painful.|The government announced mitigatory measures to address the rising cost of living. 政府宣布了缓解措施以应对不断上涨的生活成本。|近义词:  alleviating, palliative, extenuating,  remedial|反义词: aggravating, exacerbating|词性:形容词

mitigate:/ˈmɪtɪɡeɪt/
mitigate| vt.| 减轻，缓和|Make (something bad) less severe, serious, or painful.|The judge took his previous good behaviour into account as a mitigating factor. 法官将他的良好行为记录作为减轻处罚的因素考虑在内。|近义词: alleviate, lessen, reduce, diminish|反义词: aggravate, worsen, exacerbate|词性:动词

mitigation:/ˌmɪtɪˈɡeɪʃn/
mitigation| n.| 减轻，缓和|The action of reducing the severity, seriousness, or painfulness of something.|The company is investing in flood mitigation measures. 该公司正在投资防洪措施。|近义词: alleviation, reduction, diminution|反义词: aggravation, exacerbation|词性:名词
