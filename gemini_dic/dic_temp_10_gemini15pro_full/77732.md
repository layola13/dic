gemsbok:/ˈɡɛmzˌbɒk/ |ˈdʒɛmzˌbɑːk/| n. |大羚羊，直角羚|A large, strikingly marked antelope (Oryx gazella) of southern Africa, having long, straight horns.|例句：The gemsbok is known for its ability to tolerate high temperatures. 大羚羊以其耐高温的能力而闻名。

例句：We saw a herd of gemsbok grazing in the grasslands. 我们看到一群大羚羊在草原上吃草。|近义词：oryx, antelope |反义词: N/A |词性:noun

Etymology->
From Afrikaans gemsbok, from Dutch gemsbok, from gemze (chamois) + bok (buck, male goat). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *gaimizon and *bukkon.
