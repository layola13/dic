hendecagon:/hɛnˈdɛkəɡɒn/ /hɛnˈdɛkəɡɑːn/
noun
十一边形；十一边形
A polygon with eleven sides and eleven angles.
A hendecagon has eleven sides.  一个十一边形有十一条边。
The interior angles of a regular hendecagon add up to 1620 degrees.  一个正十一边形的内角和为1620度。
synonym: undecagon
antonym: (No direct antonym for a geometric shape)
