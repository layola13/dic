actuate: /ˈæktʃueɪt/
verb
启动，驱动，激励
to cause (a machine or device) to operate
The valve is actuated by a lever. = 该阀门由杠杆驱动。

He was actuated by malice. = 他受恶意驱使。

近义词: motivate, drive, trigger, prompt, stimulate, activate
反义词: halt, stop, deactivate
词性: verb

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The thermostat actuates the heating system. = 恒温器启动了加热系统。
Fear often actuates people to do strange things. = 恐惧常常促使人们做出奇怪的事情。

一般过去时:
A small motor actuated the door. = 一个小马达启动了门。
His desire for revenge actuated him to commit the crime. = 他对复仇的渴望促使他犯下了罪行。

现在完成时:
The sensor has actuated the alarm. = 传感器已经启动了警报。
His words have actuated a strong response from the public. = 他的话引起了公众的强烈反响。

过去完成时:
The timer had actuated the sprinkler system before the fire spread. = 在火势蔓延之前，计时器已经启动了洒水系统。
Her kindness had actuated him to help her in return. = 她的善良促使他回报她。

将来时:
The button will actuate the mechanism. =  这个按钮将启动该机制。
This new policy will actuate economic growth. = 这项新政策将推动经济增长。

虚拟语气:
If the lever were to actuate the machine, it would start immediately. = 如果杠杆启动机器，它会立即启动。
I wish something would actuate him to change his ways. = 我希望有什么东西能促使他改变他的方式。

被动语态:
The machine is actuated by a small motor. = 这台机器由一个小马达驱动。
The alarm was actuated by the sensor. = 警报是由传感器触发的。

疑问句:
What actuates him to behave this way? = 是什么促使他这样做？
Does the button actuate the light? = 这个按钮能启动灯吗？

否定句:
The switch doesn't actuate the fan. = 开关不能启动风扇。
His words didn't actuate any change in their plans. = 他的话并没有改变他们的计划。



PHRASE->
actuate by = 由...驱动/激励
actuate a machine = 启动机器
actuate a response = 引起反应
actuate a change = 促成改变
actuate a system = 启动系统
