intercrusts:/ˈɪntərkrʌsts/
n.
intercrusts (usually plural)
地壳间的岩层;  地壳之间的夹层
layers of rock between crusts, especially of the Earth.  Also used metaphorically.
The intercrusts of the Earth are still largely unexplored. 地球的地壳间岩层很大程度上仍然未被探索。
The novel explored the intercrusts of society, revealing hidden tensions. 这部小说探索了社会的夹层，揭示了隐藏的紧张关系。
近义词: layers, strata,  intercalations
反义词: surface
