ETYMOLOGY->
From the Middle English *favored/favoured*, derived from the verb *favor/favour*, which comes from the Old French *favor*, ultimately from the Latin *favor* "goodwill, kindness, grace, favor". The "-ed" suffix indicates a past participle, transforming the verb into an adjective.
