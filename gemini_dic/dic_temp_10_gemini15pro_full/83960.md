half-contemptuous:/ˌhɑːf kənˈtɛmptʃuəs/
half-contemptuous: |adj.|略带轻蔑的；半轻蔑的| Showing a mild or moderate degree of contempt; expressing a slight disdain or scorn. |He gave a half-contemptuous smile. 他轻蔑地笑了笑。|Synonyms: disdainful, scornful, sneering, derisive|Antonyms: respectful, admiring, reverent|Adjective
