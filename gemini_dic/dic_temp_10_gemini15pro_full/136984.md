nonspinning:/ˌnɒnˈspɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 非旋转的，无自旋的 | Not spinning; having no spin.  This term is often used in physics and engineering to describe particles or objects that lack angular momentum. |  Electrons can be either spinning or nonspinning. 电子可以是自旋的，也可以是非自旋的。| The nonspinning top simply fell over.  这个不旋转的陀螺直接倒了。|  nonrotating, still, stationary | rotating, spinning | adjective
