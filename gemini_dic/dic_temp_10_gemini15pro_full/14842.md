axite:/ˈæksaɪt/| n. |斧石|A mineral, a variety of jadeite.|The museum displayed a rare axite artifact. 博物馆展出了一件稀有的斧石文物。|近义词：jadeite, jade|反义词: N/A |词性: noun

词源:  From Latin *axia, from Greek ἀξίνη (axínē, “axe”), because of its resemblance to the shape of an axe head.
