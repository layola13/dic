nonspeech:/ˈnɑːnˌspiːtʃ/ /ˈnɒnˌspiːtʃ/ | n. | 非言语; 非言语交际; 非言语行为 |Communication that does not involve speech, such as body language, facial expressions, and gestures. |  Her nonspeech communication, like a slight nod, conveyed agreement.  她非语言的交流，比如轻微的点头，表达了同意。| synonyms: nonverbal communication, body language | antonyms: speech, verbal communication | noun

nonspeech:/ˈnɑːnˌspiːtʃ/ /ˈnɒnˌspiːtʃ/ | adj. | 非言语的 | Relating to communication that does not involve speech. |  Nonspeech cues, like eye contact, are crucial in social interaction.  非言语的线索，比如眼神交流，在社会交往中至关重要。| synonyms: nonverbal | antonyms: verbal, spoken | adjective
