diligence:/ˈdɪlɪdʒəns/| n.|勤奋，勤勉，用功|Careful and persistent work or effort.|She achieved success through hard work and diligence. 她通过努力工作和勤奋取得了成功。|近义词: industry, assiduity, perseverance|反义词: laziness, negligence, idleness|词性:名词

词源: From Old French *diligence "attention, care," from Latin diligentia "attentiveness, carefulness," from diligens "attentive, careful," present participle of diligere "to value or esteem highly, love; single out, select; attend to carefully," from dis- "apart" (see dis-) + legere "to choose, gather, read" (see lecture (n.)).
