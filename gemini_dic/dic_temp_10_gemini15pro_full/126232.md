mycetoid:/ˈmaɪsɪˌtɔɪd/| adj.|类真菌的，似真菌的|Resembling a fungus; fungus-like.  Having the appearance or characteristics of a fungus, but not necessarily being a true fungus.|The organism exhibited mycetoid growth, spreading in a network of filaments.|近义词：fungoid, fungal|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-fungal")|词性: adjective

Etymology:  Derived from the Greek "mykēs" (μύκης) meaning "fungus" + "-oid" meaning "resembling" or "having the form of".
