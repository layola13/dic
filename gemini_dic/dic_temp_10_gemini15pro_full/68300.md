falconine:/ˈfɔːlkənɪn, -ˌaɪn/
adjective
像猎鹰的；猎鹰似的
Resembling or characteristic of a falcon; of or relating to falcons.
The falconine beak is sharp and hooked. 猎鹰的喙又尖又弯。
近义词:hawklike, raptorial
反义词: (No clear antonym exists, potentially dove-like for symbolic contrast)
词性:adjective


falconine:/ˈfɔːlkənɪn, -ˌaɪn/
noun
猎鹰
A falcon. (Less common usage)
The falconine soared through the sky.  猎鹰翱翔在天空中。
