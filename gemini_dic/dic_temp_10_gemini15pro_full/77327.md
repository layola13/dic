Gatorade: /ˈɡeɪtəˌreɪd/ | n. | 佳得乐（一种运动饮料） | A sweetened and flavored electrolyte beverage marketed by PepsiCo and designed to help athletes rehydrate. It was originally developed for the University of Florida Gators football team, hence the name. | I drank some Gatorade after my workout to replenish electrolytes. 我在锻炼后喝了一些佳得乐来补充电解质。 |  He grabbed a bottle of Gatorade from the cooler. 他从冷藏箱里拿了一瓶佳得乐。| 近义词: sports drink, electrolyte drink | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps water or juice could be considered contrasting beverages) | 词性: noun
