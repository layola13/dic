djereed: /dʒəˈriːd/
noun
单簧管，一种双簧管乐器
A double reed wind instrument, similar to an oboe, used in Middle Eastern music.
The mournful sound of the djereed filled the air.  哀伤的单簧管声弥漫在空气中。
Synonyms: mijwiz, arghul
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific instrument.  Could use "silence" as a contrasting concept.)
词性: 名词
