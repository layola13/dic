hangers-on:/ˈhæŋərz ɒn/ (US) /ˈhæŋəz ɒn/ (UK)| n.| 奉承者，追随者，食客 |People who associate with someone more important or successful than themselves, usually in hope of gaining some advantage.| The prince had a large retinue of hangers-on. 王子身边有一大群追随者。| synonyms: followers, sycophants, parasites, leechers | antonyms: leaders, pioneers, independents | noun

hangers-on:/ˈhæŋərz ɒn/ (US) /ˈhæŋəz ɒn/ (UK)| n.| 剩余物，残留物 |Something that remains after the main part is gone or used.| The hangers-on of the feast were quickly cleared away. 宴席的残羹剩饭很快就被清理干净了。| synonyms: remnants, leftovers, residue | antonyms:  main course, essentials | noun
