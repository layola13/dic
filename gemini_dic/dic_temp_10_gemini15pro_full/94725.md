impropriety:/ˌɪmprəˈpraɪəti/| n. |不适当；不得体；不恰当|The impropriety of his behavior shocked everyone. 他的行为不得体，震惊了所有人。|例句：His impropriety cost him his job. 他的不当行为让他丢了工作。|例句：She was accused of financial impropriety. 她被指控财务不当。|近义词：indecency, indecorum, unseemliness|反义词：propriety, decency, decorum|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French impropriete, from late Latin improprietas, from impropritas ‘unsuitableness’, from in- ‘not’ + proprius ‘one's own’.
