compilation:/ˌkɒmpɪˈleɪʃn/ (British) /ˌkɑːmpɪˈleɪʃn/ (American)| n.|汇编；编纂；编辑；汇编物|A compilation is a collection of things, especially songs or pieces of writing, that have been put together and published in a book, CD, etc. | This CD is a compilation of their greatest hits. 这张CD是他们最热门歌曲的合集。| synonyms: collection, anthology, album, selection | antonyms: separation, division | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *compilatio, from Latin compilare "to plunder, pillage, rob," later "to collect, gather, assemble," from com- "together" + pilare "to compress into a ball." The negative sense of "plagiarism" is largely obsolete.
