baroque:/bəˈrɒk/ /bəˈroʊk/| adj. n.|巴洛克式的，巴洛克风格；巴洛克式建筑，巴洛克艺术|Characterized by extravagance, complexity, and elaborate ornamentation, especially in art, architecture, and music of the 17th and 18th centuries.  Originating from the Portuguese word "barroco," meaning "irregularly shaped pearl."|The palace is a magnificent example of baroque architecture. 这座宫殿是巴洛克式建筑的宏伟典范。|The composer's music is known for its baroque flourishes. 这位作曲家的音乐以其巴洛克式的华丽装饰而闻名。|近义词:ornate, elaborate, flamboyant|反义词:simple, plain, austere|词性:adjective, noun
