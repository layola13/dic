nonconfidences:/ˌnɒnkɒnfɪdənsiz/ | n. | 不受信任的事情；秘密泄露；泄密事件 | Matters that are not confidential; instances of revealing secrets. |  The recent string of nonconfidences has damaged the company's reputation. 最近一连串的泄密事件损害了公司的声誉。 | 近义词: disclosures, leaks, betrayals | 反义词: secrets, confidences | 词性: 名词

词源:  Formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") and the noun "confidences" (plural of "confidence," meaning "a secret or private matter told to someone"). 源自前缀“non-”（意为“不”）和名词“confidences”（“confidence”的复数形式，意为“告诉某人的秘密或私事”）。
