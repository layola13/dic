galloped:/ˈɡæləpt/ | v. | 疾驰，飞奔 |  (of a horse or other quadruped) run at a fast gait, with each stride including a phase in which all four feet are off the ground together. | The horse galloped across the field.  马飞奔穿过田野。 | 近义词: raced, dashed, sped, bolted | 反义词: ambled, strolled, crawled | 词性: 动词

词源: From Old French *galoper (unattested), probably of Germanic origin.
