illicit:/ɪˈlɪsɪt/
adjective
非法的，违禁的
Forbidden by law, rules, or custom.
The police discovered an illicit drug operation. 警方发现了一起非法毒品交易。
近义词：illegal, unlawful, illegitimate, prohibited
反义词：legal, lawful, legitimate, permitted
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *illicitus, from in- (“not”) + licitus (“lawful”).
