bathtub:/ˈbæθtʌb/| n. |浴缸（单数）|A large container for holding water in which a person may bathe.|例句：I filled the bathtub with hot water. 我把浴缸里放满了热水。|近义词：tub, bath|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑shower淋浴)|名词


词源:  From *bath + tub*.  "Tub" originally referred to any open-topped, relatively large container.  The combination "bathtub" came into common use in the 19th century with the increased availability of indoor plumbing.
