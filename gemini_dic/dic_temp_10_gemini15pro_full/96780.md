inequity:/ɪnˈɛkwɪti/ | n. | 不公平，不公正 | Lack of fairness or justice. | The inequity of the healthcare system is a major concern. 医疗体系的不公平是一个主要问题。|  The growing inequity between the rich and the poor is alarming.  贫富差距日益扩大令人担忧。 | 近义词: injustice, unfairness, disparity, inequality | 反义词: equity, fairness, justice, equality | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *inequité*, from Latin iniquitas, from iniquus (“unequal, unjust”), from in- (“not”) + aequus (“equal, level, just”).
