nonrecyclable:/ˌnɒnriːˈsaɪkləbl/ /ˌnɑːnriːˈsaɪkləbl/
|adj.|不可回收的|Not able to be recycled or processed for reuse.|Many plastic bags are nonrecyclable and end up in landfills. 许多塑料袋是不可回收的，最终被填埋。|The company is trying to reduce its use of nonrecyclable packaging. 该公司正在努力减少使用不可回收的包装。|近义词：non-reusable, disposable|反义词: recyclable, reusable|词性: 形容词

nonrecyclable:/ˌnɒnriːˈsaɪkləbl/ /ˌnɑːnriːˈsaɪkləbl/
|n.|不可回收物|Something that cannot be recycled.|Nonrecyclables should be disposed of in the general waste bin. 不可回收物应丢弃在普通垃圾桶内。|The city has separate bins for recyclables and nonrecyclables. 该市为可回收物和不可回收物设置了不同的垃圾桶。|近义词：non-reusable item, disposable item|反义词: recyclable, reusable item|词性: 名词
