myelitis:/ˌmaɪəˈlaɪtɪs/| n.|脊髓炎|Inflammation of the spinal cord.  This inflammation can disrupt the normal responses from the brain to the rest of the body and from the body to the brain.|He was diagnosed with transverse myelitis, a rare neurological disorder.|Near synonyms: spinal cord inflammation,  myelopathy|Antonym: spinal cord health|Noun
