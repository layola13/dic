graciosities: /ɡreɪʃiˈɒsɪtiz/ /ɡreɪʃiˈɑːsətiz/
noun
优雅的行为；殷勤的举动；礼貌；客气话
acts of kindness or courtesy; polite and refined acts or expressions.

The queen acknowledged the crowd with a series of graciosities. 女王以一系列优雅的举动向人群致意。

His graciosities charmed everyone at the party.  他的殷勤举止迷住了派对上的每个人。

Synonyms: courtesies, civilities, kindnesses, pleasantries, refinements
Antonyms: rudeness, impoliteness, incivility, discourtesy, crudeness
词性: 名词 (plural noun)
