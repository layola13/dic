desilt:/diːˈsɪlt/ | vt. | 除淤，清除淤泥 | To remove silt or mud from (a river, harbor, etc.). | The engineers desilted the harbor to allow larger ships to enter.  工程师们疏浚了港口，以便让更大的船只进入。

The canal was desilted regularly to maintain its depth. 运河定期清淤以保持其深度。| 近义词：dredge, clear, clean | 反义词：silt up | 词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "remove") + silt (noun).
