archontate: /ˈɑːrkɒnteɪt/ (US) /ˈɑːkɒnteɪt/ (UK)| n. |执政官的职位或统治时期; 执政官的管辖区域|The period of rule by an archon, especially in ancient Athens; the territory ruled by an archon.|The Athenian archontate lasted for a year. 雅典执政官的任期为一年。|The archontate controlled a vast area.  执政官管辖着广阔的区域。|Synonyms: reign, rule, dominion, jurisdiction|Antonyms: subordination, subjection|noun
