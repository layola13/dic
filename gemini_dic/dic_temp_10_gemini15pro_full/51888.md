dhaba:/ˈdɑːbɑː/ (UK) /ˈdʌbə/ (US)| n.|小餐馆，路边小吃摊|A roadside restaurant or eatery, typically one that serves Punjabi cuisine.  It is often a basic establishment, but can also be more elaborate.|We stopped at a dhaba on the highway for a delicious and inexpensive meal. 我们在高速公路旁的一家dhaba停下来吃了一顿美味又便宜的饭。|Synonyms: eatery, cafe, restaurant, snack bar|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, possibly) fine dining restaurant|noun
