dracunculus:/drəˈkʌŋkjʊləs/ | n. |麦地那龙线虫; 龙线虫病; 天南星科植物| A parasitic nematode worm (Dracunculus medinensis) that infects humans and some other mammals, causing dracunculiasis (Guinea worm disease). Also refers to a genus of plants in the Araceae family. | Example 1: Dracunculus medinensis is a parasitic worm that causes painful blisters. 麦地那龙线虫是一种寄生虫，会导致疼痛的水疱。
