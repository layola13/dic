endoscope:/ˈɛndəskoʊp/ (US), /ˈɛndəskəʊp/ (UK)| n.|内窥镜|An instrument for visually examining the interior of a body cavity or hollow organ such as the colon, bladder, or stomach.|The doctor used an endoscope to examine the patient's stomach.|近义词:boroscope, fiberscope|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
