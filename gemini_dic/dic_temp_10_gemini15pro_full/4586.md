alcoholometer:/ˌælkəˈhɒləmɪtər/ | n. | 酒精比重计；酒精浓度计 | An instrument used to measure the alcoholic strength of a liquid. |  The brewer used an alcoholometer to determine the alcohol content of the beer.  酿酒师用酒精比重计来测定啤酒的酒精含量。 | hydrometer, alcoholmeter | None | noun

Etymology->
From alcohol + -meter.  Alcohol from Medieval Latin alcohol "powdered antimony," from Arabic al-kuḥl. -meter from Greek metron "measure."
