eschewer:/ɪˈʃuːər/
| n.|回避者；躲避者|Someone who deliberately avoids something.|She is an eschewer of all things mainstream. 她是一个回避一切主流事物的人。|近义词：avoider, shunner, abstainer|反义词：embracer, seeker, pursuer|词性: noun
eschew:/ɪsˈtʃuː/
| vt.|避免；回避；避开|To deliberately avoid using or doing something.|He eschewed publicity and lived a quiet life. 他避开公众注意，过着平静的生活。|近义词：avoid, shun, abstain from, refrain from|反义词：embrace, seek, pursue|词性: verb
