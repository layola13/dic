paramilitary:/ˌpærəˈmɪlətəri/ | adj. n. |准军事的；准军事部队|A paramilitary group is organized like an army but is not part of a country's official armed forces. |
The government used paramilitary forces to suppress the uprising. 政府动用了准军事部队来镇压起义。|
The paramilitary group was accused of human rights abuses.  这个准军事组织被指控侵犯人权。|
Near synonyms:  militia, irregular, vigilante |
Antonyms:  military, regular army, police |
adjective, noun
