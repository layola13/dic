outsucks:/ˌaʊtˈsʌks/ | verb | 比...更糟糕，胜过...的糟糕 | to be worse than something else that is already bad |  My day outsucked yours - I got food poisoning. 我的郊游比你的更糟糕——我食物中毒了。| Example 2: This new phone outsucks the last one; the battery barely lasts an hour. 这款新手机比上一款更糟糕；电池几乎撑不到一个小时。 | synonyms: surpasses in awfulness, is even worse than | antonyms: is better than, is less bad than | verb
