overvaluableness:/ˌōvərˌvæljuəbəlnəs/ | n. | 过高评价; 过于重视 | The state or quality of being overvalued.  The overvaluableness of the company's stock led to a significant market correction. 公司股票的过高估值导致了重大的市场调整。| 近义词：excessiveness, extravagance | 反义词: undervaluation, cheapness | 词性: noun
Etymology:  From "overvalue" + "-able" + "-ness". "Overvalue" comes from "over-" (excessively) + "value" (from Old French "valuer," from Latin "valere" - to be strong, be worth).
