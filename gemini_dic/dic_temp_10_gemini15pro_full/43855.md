crescendo:/krəˈʃendoʊz/ /krəˈʃenˌdoʊz/
| n. |渐强，(声音)逐渐增强；高潮|a gradual increase in loudness, or a passage played with a gradual increase in loudness; the loudest point reached in a gradually increasing sound; a progressive increase in force or intensity|The music rose to a crescendo.  音乐声逐渐增强到最高点。|近义词：climax, peak, culmination|反义词: diminuendo, decrescendo|noun
