arthropods:/ˈɑːrθrəpɒdz/ (US) /ˈɑːθrəpɒdz/ (UK)| n. |节肢动物|Invertebrate animals having an exoskeleton (external skeleton), a segmented body, and jointed appendages (paired appendages).  Includes insects, arachnids, myriapods, and crustaceans.|例句：Arthropods are the largest phylum in the animal kingdom. 节肢动物是动物界中最大的门。|例句：Insects, spiders, and crabs are all arthropods. 昆虫、蜘蛛和螃蟹都是节肢动物。|近义词：invertebrate |反义词: vertebrate |词性:noun
