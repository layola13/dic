back-hander:/ˈbækˌhændər/ (UK), /ˈbækˌhændər/ (US)| n.|暗中贿赂；不正当的交易；反手击球|A bribe or illicit payment, often given discreetly. Also, a hit made with the back of the hand, as in tennis.|He offered the official a back-hander to ensure the contract went his way. 他向官员行贿，以确保合同顺利进行。|Synonyms: bribe, kickback, payoff, under-the-table payment (for illicit payment); backhand (for tennis)|Antonyms: legitimate payment, fair play|noun

back-hander:/ˈbækˌhændər/ (UK), /ˈbækˌhændər/ (US)| n.|反手击球|A stroke played with the back of the hand facing the direction of the stroke, as in tennis.|He won the point with a powerful back-hander. 他以一记强有力的反手击球赢得了这一分。|Synonyms: backhand|Antonyms: forehand|noun
