groveller:/ˈɡrɒvələ(r)/| n.| 卑躬屈膝的人；奉承者| A person who grovels; a sycophant. | He was a groveller, always trying to please the boss. 他是一个卑躬屈膝的人，总是试图取悦老板。| 近义词: sycophant, flatterer, toady | 反义词: rebel, nonconformist | 词性: noun

grovel:/ˈɡrɒvəl/| vi.| 卑躬屈膝；匍匐前进；奴颜婢膝| To behave in a subservient or demeaning manner; to crawl on the ground. | He grovelled to the king, begging for mercy. 他向国王卑躬屈膝，乞求怜悯。| 近义词: crawl, cringe, fawn | 反义词: defy, resist | 词性: verb

词源:  From Middle English *grovelen, *grufelen, frequentative of *gruven, *groven  "to dig, burrow," probably of Scandinavian origin.
