The correct spelling is "crowd".

crowd:/kraʊd/| n. v.|人群，群众；拥挤，挤满|A large number of people gathered together in a relatively small space.  A group of people or things.|例句：A large crowd gathered to watch the parade.  一大群人聚集在一起观看游行。

The singer played to a capacity crowd. 歌手对着爆满的观众演唱。|近义词：throng, multitude, swarm, mob, horde|反义词:few, scattering|词性:名词,动词


crowd:/kraʊd/| v.|拥挤，挤满；挤入，涌入|To gather in large numbers; to fill a space by pressing together.|例句：People crowded around the celebrity, hoping for an autograph. 人们簇拥着这位名人，希望能得到签名。

Tourists crowded the streets of the old city. 游客挤满了老城的街道。|近义词：pack, cram, throng, swarm|反义词:disperse, scatter|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English "crūdan" (to press, urge, hasten), related to Old Frisian "krūda" (to press, oppress). The sense of "to gather in numbers" developed later.
