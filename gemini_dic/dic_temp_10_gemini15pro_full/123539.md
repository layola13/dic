monotypic:/ˌmɑːnəˈtɪpɪk/ | adj. |单型的，单一类型的 | Having only one type or representative, especially having only one species in a genus or one genus in a family. | The genus *Ginkgo* is monotypic, containing only one extant species, *Ginkgo biloba*.  银杏属是单型的，仅包含一个现存物种，银杏。 |  The family contains a single monotypic genus.  该科包含一个单型属。 | monophyletic, unispecific | polytypic | adjective
