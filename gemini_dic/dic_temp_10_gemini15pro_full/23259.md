bowssen: /ˈbaʊsən/ | n. | 船首斜帆 | The bowssen is a small triangular sail set at the bow of a ship. | The ship set its bowssen to catch the light wind. 这艘船升起了船首斜帆以捕捉微风。 | 近义词: foresail, jib | 反义词: mainsail | 词性: noun

bowssen: /ˈbaʊsən/ | v. | 装备船首斜帆 | To equip a ship with a bowssen. |  They bowssened the ship before setting sail. 他们在起航前给船装上了船首斜帆。| 近义词: rig | 反义词: unrig | 词性: verb


词源：From Middle Dutch *būchseil, from būch (“bow”) + seil (“sail”).
