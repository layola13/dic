nimbiferous:/nɪmˈbɪfərəs/| adj.|降雨的，多雨的|Bringing or indicating rain; of or relating to clouds, especially rain clouds.  Carrying nimbi (rain clouds).|例句：The nimbiferous clouds promised a downpour.  乌云密布预示着将有一场倾盆大雨。

例句：The air was heavy and nimbiferous, hinting at an impending storm. 空气沉闷潮湿，预示着即将来临的暴风雨。|近义词：rainy, precipitous, showery|反义词:arid, dry|词性:adjective
词源:From Latin *nimbifer, from nimbus (“rain cloud”) + -fer (“bearing”).
