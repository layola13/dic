overbearing:/ˌoʊvərˈberɪŋ/| adj.|专横的，傲慢的，盛气凌人的|Having or showing a tendency to force one's will on others; domineering.|例句：He had an overbearing manner that made it difficult to work with him.  他专横跋扈的态度让人很难和他共事。

例句：The overbearing heat made it impossible to work outdoors.  酷热难耐，无法在户外工作。|近义词：domineering, arrogant, haughty, imperious, tyrannical|反义词：humble, modest, meek, submissive, unassuming|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over + bear (v.).  Originally literal, meaning "overspreading, prevalent." The figurative sense of "domineering" developed in the late 16th century.  来自 over + bear (v.)。最初的字面意思是“遍布的，盛行的”。“专横的”的比喻意义出现在16世纪后期。
