dipteral:/ˈdɪptərəl/
adjective
双列柱式的；有两排柱子的
Having two rows of columns.
The temple was a magnificent dipteral structure, with two rows of columns surrounding the central cella. 这座寺庙是一座宏伟的双列柱式建筑，中央的圣殿周围环绕着两排柱子。
The architect designed a dipteral plan for the new museum.  这位建筑师为新博物馆设计了一个双列柱式的平面图。

近义词: colonnaded, peristyle
反义词: (No direct antonym, could use terms like "single-colonnaded" or "uncolonnaded")
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *dípteros* (having two wings, two-winged), from *di-* (two) + *pterón* (wing, feather).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\*pet-/* (to fly, to fall).
