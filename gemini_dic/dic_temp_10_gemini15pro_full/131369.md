noncircuitous:/ˌnɒnsərˈkjuːɪtəs/
adjective
直接的，不绕圈子的
Going straight to the point; not roundabout.
The professor gave a noncircuitous explanation of the complex theory, making it easy for everyone to understand. 教授对这个复杂的理论进行了直接的解释，使每个人都能理解。
She took a noncircuitous route to work, arriving much earlier than usual. 她走了一条直达上班的路线，比平常早到很多。
近义词:direct, straightforward, linear
反义词:circuitous, indirect, roundabout
词性:adjective
