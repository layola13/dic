distinguish-between:/dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ bɪˈtwiːn/| v.|区分，辨别|To recognize or understand the difference between two or more things or people.|He could distinguish between the different types of birds by their songs. 他能根据鸟的歌声区分不同种类的鸟。|近义词：differentiate, discriminate|反义词：confuse, mistake|verb

distinguish-between:/dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ bɪˈtwiːn/| v.|区分，辨别|To treat someone or something differently. |The law does not distinguish between accidental and intentional killing. 该法律对意外杀人与故意杀人没有区别对待。|近义词：separate, tell apart|反义词：equate, conflate|verb
