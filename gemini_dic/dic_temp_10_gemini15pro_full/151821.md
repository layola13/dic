passengered:/ˈpæsɪndʒərd/
adjective|载有乘客的；客运的|Carrying or conveying passengers; used for carrying passengers.|The passengered train arrived at the station. 载有乘客的火车抵达了车站。|近义词:occupied, loaded|反义词:empty, unladen|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "passenger" + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives). "Passenger" itself comes from the Old French "passager," ultimately from the Late Latin "passagium," meaning "passage" or "passing through."
