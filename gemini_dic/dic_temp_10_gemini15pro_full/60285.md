eggery:/ˈeɡəri/ | n. | 捣蛋，胡闹;  过分热情(尤指令人讨厌的); |A place or situation characterized by excessive and often intrusive enthusiasm or activity, especially of a trivial or frivolous nature.  Often implies a sense of bustling activity, but with a negative connotation of fussiness or silliness. |例句：The office was a scene of eggery with everyone rushing around preparing for the holiday party. 办公室里一片忙乱，每个人都在为节日派对做准备。

例句：I can't stand all the eggery surrounding the awards ceremony. 我受不了颁奖典礼上所有这些过分的热情。|近义词：fuss, bustle, commotion, flurry, ado, to-do, palaver|反义词：calm, quiet, tranquility, peace, stillness|词性:noun
