housewares:/ˈhaʊswɛrz/
noun
家庭用品，家居用品
Articles of household use, especially utensils, appliances, and furnishings.
The store specializes in housewares and kitchen appliances. 这家商店专营家居用品和厨房电器。
synonyms: household goods, domestic goods, home furnishings
