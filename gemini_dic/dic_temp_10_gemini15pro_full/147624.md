overplot:/ˌoʊvərˈplɑːt/ /ˌəʊvəˈplɒt/| v. n.|过度绘制，覆盖绘制；过分复杂的剧情|To add a secondary plot to a main plot, often resulting in a confusing or overly complicated narrative. As a noun, it refers to such a secondary plot. |The movie was overplotted, making it difficult to follow. 这部电影情节过于复杂，让人难以理解。|Synonym: overcrowd, complicate|Antonym: simplify, streamline|Verb, Noun

overplot:/ˌoʊvərˈplɑːt/ /ˌəʊvəˈplɒt/| n.|过分复杂的剧情|A secondary plot added to a main plot, often resulting in a confusing or overly complicated narrative. |The overplot involving the secret society detracted from the main story.  关于秘密社团的额外情节分散了主要故事的注意力。|Synonym: subplot, side story|Antonym: main plot, primary narrative|Noun
