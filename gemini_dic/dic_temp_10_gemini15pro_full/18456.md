bemoaningly:/bɪˈmoʊnɪŋli/
adv.
悲叹地，哀叹地
in a way that expresses sadness or disappointment
She bemoaningly recounted the loss of her beloved pet. 她悲叹地讲述了她心爱宠物的离去。
He shook his head bemoaningly at the news. 他听到这个消息，悲叹地摇了摇头。
Synonyms: plaintively, mournfully, dolefully, sadly
Antonyms: cheerfully, joyfully, happily
词性: 副词
