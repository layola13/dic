greeters:/ˈɡriːtərz/| n.|迎宾员，接待员|People who greet others, often at the entrance of a place, to welcome them.|The greeters at the restaurant were very friendly and welcoming. 餐厅的迎宾员非常友好热情。|近义词：receptionists, welcomers|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps bouncers as they sometimes turn people away)|Noun
