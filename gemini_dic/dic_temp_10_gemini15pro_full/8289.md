anti-malthusianism: /ˌæntiˈmɔːlθuːziənɪzəm/ | n. | 反马尔萨斯主义 | The belief that Malthus's predictions about population growth outstripping resources were wrong, or that they won't happen because of technological advances or other factors. |  Anti-Malthusianism gained traction as technological advancements increased food production. 反马尔萨斯主义随着技术进步提高粮食产量而受到关注。

Anti-Malthusianism suggests that human ingenuity can overcome resource limitations. 反马尔萨斯主义认为，人类的聪明才智可以克服资源限制。 | 近义词: Cornucopianism | 反义词: Malthusianism | 词性: 名词
