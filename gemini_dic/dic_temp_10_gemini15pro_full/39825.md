词源:
From Late Latin *conspirantem, present participle of conspirare (“to agree, plot”), from Latin con- (“together”) + spirare (“to breathe”).  源自晚期拉丁语 *conspirantem，conspirare（“同意，密谋”）的现在分词，来自拉丁语 con-（“一起”）+ spirare（“呼吸”）。
