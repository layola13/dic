liturgiologist:/ˌlɪtərˈdʒiɒlədʒɪst/
n.
礼拜学学者，礼仪学家
A scholar of liturgy, the study of forms of public worship.
The liturgiologist explained the historical significance of the different vestments worn by the priest.  礼拜学学者解释了神父所穿不同法衣的历史意义。
The liturgiologist is giving a lecture on the evolution of the Eucharist. 礼拜学学者正在做一个关于圣餐礼演变的讲座。

近义词:  liturgical scholar
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider someone opposed to organized religion)
词性: noun
