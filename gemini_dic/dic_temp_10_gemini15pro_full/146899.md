overinsists:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈsɪsts/| v. |过度坚持，过分强调|To insist too much or repeatedly on something.|He overinsists on the importance of following the rules. 他过分强调遵守规则的重要性.|近义词：overemphasize, belabor, harp on|反义词: downplay, understate|词性:动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "insist" (from Latin "insistere," meaning "to stand upon, persist")
