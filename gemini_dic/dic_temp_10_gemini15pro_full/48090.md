declassifying: /diːˈklæsɪfaɪɪŋ/
verb
解密;取消…的机密等级
removing the security classification from (information or documents)
The government is declassifying some wartime documents. 政府正在解密一些战时文件。
synonyms: releasing, disclosing, unveiling, revealing
antonyms: classifying, concealing, protecting, restricting
verb (present participle)
