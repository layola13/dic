hindbrains:/ˈhaɪndˌbreɪnz/ /ˈhaɪndˌbreɪn/| n. |后脑|The part of the brain comprising the cerebellum, pons, and medulla oblongata, responsible for vital functions like breathing and heart rate.  It is the most primitive part of the vertebrate brain.|The hindbrain controls essential bodily functions. 后脑控制基本的生理功能。

The doctor explained that damage to the hindbrain can be life-threatening. 医生解释说，后脑的损伤可能危及生命。|近义词：rhombencephalon|反义词: (No direct antonym exists for a specific brain region.  One could possibly consider the forebrain as a functionally contrasting area.)|词性:noun
