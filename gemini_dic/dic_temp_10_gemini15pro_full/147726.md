overprinted:/ˌoʊvərˈprɪntɪd/| adj.|套印的，重印的|Printed over something else, especially with a different design or color.  An overprinted image or text appears on top of an existing image or text.|The stamps were overprinted with the new country's name. 这些邮票套印了新国家的名称。|The banknotes were overprinted with a higher denomination.  这些钞票套印了更高的面值。|Synonyms: superimposed, surprinted|Antonyms: underprinted (less common), original (in the sense of not altered)|adjective
