felicitously:/fəˈlɪsɪtəsli/ | adv. | 恰当地；得体地；巧妙地；幸福地 |  In a way that is well-chosen or suited to the circumstances; aptly.  Also, in a happy or delightful manner. |  He felicitously phrased his acceptance speech.  他措辞得体地发表了获奖感言。 | appropriately, suitably, aptly, luckily, fortunately, happily | inappropriately, unsuitably, inaptly, unfortunately, unhappily | adverb

Etymology: From Latin *felicitōsus*, from *fēlīcitās* ('happiness, fertility') + *-ōsus* (adjective suffix).
