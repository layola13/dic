lored:/lɔːrd/ | adj. | 诱惑的；引诱的 | Allured, tempted, enticed. Often used in a poetic or archaic context to describe something desirable or seductive. | The lored traveler followed the siren's song.  受诱惑的旅行者循着塞壬的歌声而去。 |  Near synonyms:  alluring, enticing, tempting, seductive | Antonyms:  repelling, unappealing, unattractive | adjective
