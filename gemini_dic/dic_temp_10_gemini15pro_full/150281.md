panegyrize: /ˈpænɪdʒəˌraɪz/ | v. | 颂扬，赞美，称颂 | to praise someone or something very highly and publicly | The poet panegyrized the king's bravery in battle. 这位诗人歌颂了国王在战斗中的英勇。

The critics panegyrized her performance as groundbreaking.  评论家们称赞她的表演是开创性的。

| 近义词: eulogize, extol, laud, acclaim | 反义词: criticize, condemn, denounce | 词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *panegyricus, from Ancient Greek πανηγυρικός (panēgyrikós, “fit for a public assembly”), from πανήγυρις (panḗgyris, “a public festival, a general assembly”), from πᾶν (pân, “all”) + ἀγορά (agorá, “assembly, gathering”).
