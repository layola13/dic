auger:/ˈɔːɡər/| n. |螺旋钻, 钻孔机, 预兆, 占卜师|A tool with a helical bit for boring holes, especially in wood or ice.  Also, something that foreshadows a future event.|例句：The carpenter used an auger to drill holes for the dowels. 木匠用螺旋钻钻孔装销钉。

例句：These dark clouds auger a storm. 这些乌云预示着暴风雨的来临。|近义词：drill, borer, gimlet, portend, presage|反义词: none |词性: noun

auger:/ˈɔːɡər/| v. |用螺旋钻钻孔, 预示|To bore or drill with an auger; to presage or foreshadow.|例句：He augered holes in the ice for fishing. 他在冰上钻孔钓鱼。

例句：The declining stock market augurs a recession. 股票市场的衰退预示着经济衰退。|近义词：drill, bore, foretell, predict|反义词: none |词性: verb

augers:/ˈɔːɡərz/ | n. | 螺旋钻(复数), 占卜师(复数) | Plural form of "auger," referring to multiple tools or individuals.|例句：The workers used several augers to complete the project quickly. 工人们使用多个螺旋钻快速完成了项目。

例句：The augers predicted a prosperous future for the kingdom. 占卜师们预言王国将会有一个繁荣的未来。|近义词：drills, borers, soothsayers, prophets|反义词: none |词性: noun (plural)
