fuzzier:/ˈfʌziər/ | adj. | 更模糊的，更绒毛状的 | More fuzzy; covered with more fuzz.  | The picture on the old TV is fuzzier than the one on the new one.  旧电视上的图像比新电视上的图像更模糊。|  synonyms: blurrier, less distinct, hazier | antonyms: clearer, sharper, more distinct | adjective

fuzzier:/ˈfʌziər/ | adj. | 更难理解的，更不明确的 | More difficult to understand; less clear or definite.  | The legal definition of "self-defense" is fuzzier than most people realize.  “自卫”的法律定义比大多数人意识到的更模糊。 | synonyms: vaguer, less precise, ambiguous | antonyms: clearer, more precise, definite | adjective
