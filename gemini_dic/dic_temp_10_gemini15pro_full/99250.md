intercomparable:/ˌɪntərˈkɒmpərəbl/ | adj. | 可互相比较的 | Able to be compared with each other. | Example: The two datasets are intercomparable, allowing for a meaningful analysis. 这两个数据集可以相互比较，从而进行有意义的分析。 | Example:  The results of the two studies are intercomparable because they used similar methodologies.  这两项研究的结果是可以相互比较的，因为它们使用了相似的方法。 | Synonym: comparable, commensurable | Antonym: incomparable, incommensurable | word property: adjective
