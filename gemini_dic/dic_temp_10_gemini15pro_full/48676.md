deferment:/dɪˈfɜːrmənt/ /dɪˈfər mənt/
n.
延期，推迟，延缓
the act of delaying something that is supposed to happen, or an occasion when something is delayed

例句：
He requested a deferment of his student loan payments.  他要求延期偿还学生贷款。

He got a deferment from military service. 他获得了兵役延期。

近义词：postponement, delay, adjournment
反义词：advancement, expedition

词性：名词
