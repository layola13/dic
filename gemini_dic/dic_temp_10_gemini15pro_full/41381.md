植物体系统发育学
The study of the evolutionary relationships among groups of land plants based on their body plans and other characteristics.
Cormophylogeny helps us understand the diversification of land plants over millions of years. 植物体系统发育学帮助我们了解数百万年来陆地植物的多样化。
The recent advances in molecular biology have revolutionized cormophylogeny. 最近分子生物学的进步彻底改变了植物体系统发育学。
