misshapes:/ˌmɪsˈʃeɪps/ | n. | 畸形，变形 | things that are badly shaped or formed |  The artist intentionally created misshapes in the sculpture. 艺术家故意在雕塑中制造了一些畸形。| synonyms: deformities, malformations, distortions | antonyms: perfections, regularities | noun

misshapes:/ˌmɪsˈʃeɪps/ | v. | 使…变形，弄歪 | to give something a bad or incorrect shape | The intense heat misshaped the plastic.  高温使塑料变形了。| synonyms: deform, distort, contort | antonyms: shape, form, mold | verb
