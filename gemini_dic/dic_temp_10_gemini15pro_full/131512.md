noncolors:/ˈnɑːnˌkʌlərz/ |n.| 非彩色;素色;无彩色|Colors other than those of the visible spectrum, such as black, white, and gray.  These are not technically colors because they do not have a specific wavelength of light.|She prefers noncolors like black and white for her wardrobe.|Synonyms: achromatic colors, neutrals|Antonyms: colors, chromatic colors|noun
