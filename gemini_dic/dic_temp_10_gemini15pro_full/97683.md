inhumane:/ˌɪn.hjuːˈmeɪn/ | adj. | 不人道的，残忍的 | lacking compassion, sympathy, or consideration for humans or animals; cruel and barbaric. |  The inhumane treatment of prisoners of war is a war crime.  战俘的不人道待遇是战争罪。

The conditions in the factory farm were inhumane.  工厂化农场的条件是不人道的。| 近义词：cruel, brutal, barbaric, merciless, callous, heartless | 反义词：humane, compassionate, kind, merciful | 词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + humane (having or showing compassion or benevolence). Ultimately from Latin humanus (human).
