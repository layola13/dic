disabling:/dɪˈseɪblɪŋ/
adjective
使丧失能力的，致残的
causing someone to lose their physical or mental abilities; making something difficult or impossible to use
The accident left him with a disabling injury.  这场事故给他留下了致残的伤。
His disabling shyness prevented him from making friends. 他极度的害羞使他难以交友。
近义词: crippling, incapacitating, debilitating
反义词: enabling, empowering, strengthening
词性: adjective


disabling:/dɪˈseɪblɪŋ/
adjective
令人沮丧的，令人灰心的
making you feel very unhappy and without hope
It was a disabling thought that she might never walk again.  想到她可能再也走不了路了，这让她感到非常沮丧。
The news was disabling. 这消息令人沮丧。
近义词: disheartening, discouraging, demoralizing
反义词: encouraging, uplifting, heartening
词性: adjective
