lygodium:/laɪˈɡoʊdiəm/| n. | 海金沙属植物; climbing fern |A genus of ferns, typically characterized by twining fronds.  | Example: The lygodium clambered over the trellis, creating a delicate green screen. 海金沙爬满了棚架，形成了一道精致的绿色屏风。

| Example:  Lygodium palmatum, also known as the American climbing fern, is a rare and delicate species. 海金沙掌叶，也被称为美洲攀缘蕨，是一种稀有而精致的物种。| 近义词: climbing fern, Japanese climbing fern | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
