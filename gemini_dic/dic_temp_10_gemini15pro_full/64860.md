espousement:/ɪˈspaʊzmənt/
n.
拥护，支持；信奉；赞成；结婚
the act of supporting or adopting a cause, belief, or way of life; marriage.
He announced his espousement of the Republican cause. 他宣布拥护共和党的事业。
The espousement of this new technology has revolutionized the industry.  这项新技术的采用彻底改变了这个行业。

近义词:adoption, advocacy, support, championship, embrace, acceptance, marriage, wedding
反义词:rejection, renunciation, opposition, abandonment, divorce

词性:noun

词源: From Middle English *espousen, from Old French espouser, from Late Latin spōnsāre (“to betroth, promise in marriage”), from Latin spōnsa (“bride, betrothed woman”), feminine of spōnsus (“bridegroom, betrothed man”), ultimately related to spondere (“to promise solemnly”).
