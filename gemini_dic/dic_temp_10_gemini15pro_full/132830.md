nonedible:/ˌnɒnˈedɪbl/| adj.|不可食用的，不能吃的|Not fit to be eaten.|例句：These berries are nonedible and may cause stomach upset. 这些浆果不可食用，可能会引起胃部不适。|例句：The plant's leaves are nonedible, but its roots are a valuable food source.  这种植物的叶子不能吃，但它的根是宝贵的食物来源。|近义词：inedible, poisonous, toxic|反义词:edible, safe to eat|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + edible (suitable for eating).  "Edible" comes from the Latin "edibilis," meaning "eatable," from "edere" (to eat).
