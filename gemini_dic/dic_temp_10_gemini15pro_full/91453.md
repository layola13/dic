hygrometer:/haɪˈɡrɒmɪtər/ | n. | 湿度计 | An instrument used to measure the humidity of the air. |  The hygrometer indicated a high level of humidity. 湿度计显示湿度很高。

The botanist used a hygrometer to monitor the moisture levels in the greenhouse.  植物学家使用湿度计监测温室中的湿度水平。 | synonyms: moisture meter, humidistat | antonyms: N/A | noun


hygroscope:/ˈhaɪɡrəskoʊp/ | n. | 吸湿器，湿度计 | A device used to measure the humidity of the air, or a substance that readily absorbs moisture from the air. | The chemist used a hygroscope to determine the moisture content of the sample. 化学家用吸湿器来测定样品的含水量。

The antique hygroscope used human hair to measure the changes in humidity.  这个古董湿度计用人的头发来测量湿度的变化。 | synonyms: hygrometer, humidistat | antonyms: N/A | noun
