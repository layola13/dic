distriction:/dɪˈstrɪkʃən/ |n.| 区；行政区；地区 |A distriction is an area of a country or city, especially one characterized by a particular feature or activity. |The fashion distriction of New York is full of trendy boutiques. 纽约的时尚区满是时尚精品店。|近义词:region, area, zone, quarter, locality, province, territory|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用"whole"或" entirety"表示整体)|词性: 名词

词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French destriction, from Latin districtio ‘drawing asunder’, from distringere (see distrain).  The original sense was ‘seizure of goods’, later ‘affliction, distress’; the current sense dates from the late 16th century.
