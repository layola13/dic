metonic:/mɪˈtɒnɪk/ |adj.|默冬的，默冬周期的|Relating to or involving the Metonic cycle, a period of 19 years in which the phases of the moon recur on the same dates of the solar year.|The Metonic cycle is used to calculate the date of Easter. 默冬周期被用来计算复活节的日期。|近义词:lunar, cyclical|反义词:non-cyclical|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *Metōnikos (kyklos)* "Metonic (cycle)",  named after Meton, a Greek astronomer of the 5th century BC who discovered the cycle.
