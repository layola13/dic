macrobiotic:/ˌmækroʊbaɪˈɑːtɪk/
adjective
长寿的;养生的
Relating to or involving a diet thought to promote health and longevity, especially one based on whole grains and vegetables.
例句：She adopted a macrobiotic diet in hopes of improving her health. 她采取了长寿饮食，希望能改善健康。

例句：The macrobiotic restaurant offered a variety of brown rice dishes. 这家养生餐厅提供各种糙米菜肴。

synonym:healthful, wholesome, nutritious
antonym:unhealthy, unwholesome

macrobiotic:/ˌmækroʊbaɪˈɑːtɪk/
noun
长寿饮食;长寿学
A way of life and especially a diet thought to promote health and longevity, especially one based on whole grains and vegetables.
例句：Macrobiotics is more than just a diet; it's a philosophy of life.  长寿饮食不仅仅是一种饮食方式，更是一种生活哲学。

例句：He's studying macrobiotics to learn more about healthy eating. 他正在学习长寿学，以了解更多关于健康饮食的知识。


synonym:health food, health regime
antonym:junk food, unhealthy lifestyle

Etymology: From Greek *makros* (long, large) + *bios* (life).
