areole: /ˈæriˌoʊl/ (US), /ˈæriˌəʊl/ (UK) | n. | 乳晕；（仙人掌的）刺座 | A small circular area on a cactus from which spines, hairs, and flowers grow; the pigmented area around the nipple of the breast. | The cactus's areoles were clearly visible, each bearing a cluster of spines.  仙人掌的刺座清晰可见，每个刺座都有一簇刺。

The areola around her nipple darkened during pregnancy.  她乳晕周围的区域在怀孕期间变黑了。 | 近义词: (for cactus) spine cushion, (for breast) nipple area | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun
