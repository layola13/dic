candidate:/ˈkændɪdət/ (US) /ˈkændɪdeɪt/ (UK)| n.|候选人，应试者|A person who applies for a job or is nominated for election.|例句：She is a strong candidate for the position. 她这个职位很有竞争力。|近义词：applicant, nominee, contender, aspirant|反义词：incumbent|词性：名词

candidate:/ˈkændɪdət/ (US) /ˈkændɪdeɪt/ (UK)| adj.|合适的，适宜的 (obsolete, rare)|Suitable, fitting. (This usage is now rare and mostly obsolete.)|例句：He was considered a candidate location for the new factory.  他被认为是新工厂的合适选址。(古旧用法)|近义词：suitable, appropriate, fitting|反义词：unsuitable, inappropriate|词性：形容词
