debase:/dɪˈbeɪs/| vt.|贬低，降低，贬损|To reduce the quality or value of something; to lower the status or character of someone.|The politician debased himself by accepting bribes.  这位政客因接受贿赂而降低了自己的身份。|Synonyms: degrade, demean, cheapen, corrupt,  |Antonyms: elevate, enhance, ennoble, uplift|verb
debase:/dɪˈbeɪs/| vt.|降低（货币）的价值|To reduce the value of a currency by reducing the amount of precious metal in it.|The king debased the coinage by adding base metals to the gold. 国王通过在黄金中添加贱金属来降低货币的价值。|Synonyms: devalue|Antonyms: revalue|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *debaser*, from de- "down" + bas "low". Ultimately from Late Latin *bassus* "thick, low".
