freakish:/ˈfriːkɪʃ/| adj.|古怪的，反常的，怪诞的|Unusual and unexpected, strange or bizarre.  Relating to or characteristic of a freak of nature.|The freakish weather has caused a lot of damage.  这种反常的天气造成了很大的破坏。|Synonyms: bizarre, odd, strange, unusual, weird, eccentric, aberrant, anomalous, atypical, deviant, irregular, outlandish, peculiar, preternatural, uncanny, unnatural|Antonyms: normal, ordinary, usual, typical, common, conventional, regular, standard|adjective
