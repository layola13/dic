hand:/hænd/ | n. | 手 | The part of the body at the end of the arm, including the fingers and thumb. | She held the baby's hand. 她握着婴儿的手。|  Synonym: palm, fist | Antonym: foot | Noun

hand:/hænd/ | vt. |递; 交付; 帮助…上下(交通工具) | To give or pass something to someone. | Please hand me the salt. 请把盐递给我。| Synonym: give, pass | Antonym: take, receive | Verb
