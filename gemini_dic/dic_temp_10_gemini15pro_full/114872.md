mammonistic:/ˌmæməˈnɪstɪk/
adjective
拜金主义的;贪婪的
characterized by or motivated by a devotion to wealth and material possessions.
He lived a mammonistic lifestyle, always chasing the next big deal. 他过着拜金主义的生活方式，总是追逐下一笔大生意。
The mammonistic culture of the 1980s was marked by conspicuous consumption. 20世纪80年代的拜金主义文化以炫耀性消费为特征。
近义词: avaricious, greedy, materialistic, mercenary
反义词: altruistic, charitable, generous, philanthropic
词性: adjective
词源: From Mammon, the Aramaic word for wealth, regarded in the New Testament as a personification of riches or greed.
