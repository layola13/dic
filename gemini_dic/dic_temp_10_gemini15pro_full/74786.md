freighter:/ˈfreɪtər/| n. |货船；货轮|A large ship for carrying freight.  A vessel, especially a large ship or aircraft, designed to carry cargo.|The freighter was loaded with containers of manufactured goods. 这艘货船装满了制成品集装箱。|Synonyms: cargo ship, merchant ship, vessel, transporter|Antonyms: passenger ship|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From freight + -er.  Freight itself comes from Middle Dutch vrecht meaning "freight, fare, cargo".
