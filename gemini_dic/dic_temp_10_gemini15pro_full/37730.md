compartition:/ˌkɒmpɑːˈtɪʃn/ /ˌkɑːmpɑːrˈtɪʃn/| n. |分隔，隔间；隔墙；隔板|One of the parts into which an enclosed space is divided.|The office was divided into small compartments.|近义词：partition, section, chamber, compartment|反义词: whole, entirety|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French compartition, from compartir "to divide," from Latin compartiri "to divide with," from com- "together" + partire "to divide."
