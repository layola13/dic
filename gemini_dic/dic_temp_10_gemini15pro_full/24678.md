bromide:/ˈbroʊmaɪd/| n.|溴化物；镇静剂；陈词滥调|A bromide is a binary compound of bromine with another element or radical.  A bromide can also refer to a calming or sedative drug, or a trite and commonplace remark.|Potassium bromide is used as a sedative. 溴化钾用作镇静剂。|The politician's speech was full of bromides. 这位政治家的演讲充满了陈词滥调。|Synonyms: platitude, cliché, commonplace|Antonyms: original, fresh, innovative|noun
