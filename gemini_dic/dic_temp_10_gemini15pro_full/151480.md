parakeet:/ˈpærəkiːt/| n. |长尾鹦鹉，小鹦鹉|A small, usually brightly colored parrot with a long tail.|例句：The parakeet perched on my finger. 长尾鹦鹉栖息在我的手指上。

例句：We bought a parakeet for my son's birthday.  我们为我儿子的生日买了一只长尾鹦鹉。


|近义词：parrot, budgerigar (budgie)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
