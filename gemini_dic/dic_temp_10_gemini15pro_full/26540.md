cabined:/ˈkæbɪnd/| adj.|被限制的，狭小的，幽禁的|Confined or restricted within a small space; cramped.  Origin: From the noun "cabin," ultimately from Late Latin *capanna, meaning "hut."|The writer felt cabined by the strict rules of the haiku form. 这位作家感到被俳句的严格规则所限制。|近义词：confined, restricted, cramped, limited|反义词：spacious, unconfined, free, liberated|词性:形容词
