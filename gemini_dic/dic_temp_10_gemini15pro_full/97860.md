enlighten:/ɪnˈlaɪtn/ | vt. | 启发，启蒙，开导 | To give someone greater knowledge and understanding about a subject or situation. | Example: The teacher enlightened the students about the history of the Roman Empire. 老师给学生们讲解了罗马帝国的历史。

Example:  Reading philosophy can enlighten your mind. 阅读哲学可以启迪你的思想。

近义词: educate, inform, illuminate, edify | 反义词: confuse, mislead, obscure | 词性: 动词
