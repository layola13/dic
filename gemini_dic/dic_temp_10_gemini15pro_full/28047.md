caped:/keɪpt/ | adj. |披斗篷的；戴披肩的 |Wearing a cape; cloaked. |例句：The caped crusader leaped from the building. 披着斗篷的勇士从大楼跳下。|例句：She wore a long, caped gown to the ball. 她穿着一件长的，带披肩的礼服去参加舞会。|近义词：cloaked, hooded |反义词：uncaped, bare |词性:形容词

词源: From cape + -ed.  "Cape" comes from the Old French "cape" meaning a headland, which later came to refer to a sleeveless outer garment.
