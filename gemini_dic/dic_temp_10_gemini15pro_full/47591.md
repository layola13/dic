debated:/dɪˈbeɪtɪd/
verb
已辩论的，讨论过的
past participle of "debate" -  having been discussed or argued about.
The topic was hotly debated at the conference.  这个话题在会议上被热烈讨论。
The debated proposal was eventually rejected.  经过辩论的提案最终被否决了。
Synonym: discussed, argued, disputed
Antonym: agreed upon, undisputed
verb


debate:/dɪˈbeɪt/
verb, noun
辩论，讨论；考虑；争论
verb:  To engage in a formal discussion on a particular topic in a public meeting or legislative assembly, in which opposing arguments are put forward.
noun: A formal discussion on a particular matter in a public meeting or legislative assembly, in which opposing arguments are put forward.
We debated the pros and cons of the new policy. 我们讨论了新政策的利弊。
There was a lively debate about the election results. 关于选举结果有一场激烈的辩论。
Synonym: discuss, argue, dispute (v); discussion, argument, dispute (n)
Antonym: agree, concur (v); agreement, concord (n)
verb, noun
