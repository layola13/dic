ineptical:/ɪˈnɛptɪk(ə)l/
adjective
笨拙的，无能的，不称职的
Having or showing no skill; clumsy.
His ineptical handling of the situation only made matters worse. 他对局势的笨拙处理只会使事情变得更糟。
inept, awkward, clumsy, incompetent
skillful, adept, competent, proficient
adjective
