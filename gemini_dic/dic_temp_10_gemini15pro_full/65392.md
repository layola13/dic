euphemism: /ˈjuːfəmɪzəm/ | n. |委婉语，委婉说法 | A mild or indirect word or expression substituted for one considered to be too harsh or blunt when referring to something unpleasant or embarrassing. |  "Passed away" is a euphemism for "died".  “去世”是“死亡”的委婉说法。 | synonyms: understatement, circumlocution, polite term | antonyms: dysphemism, bluntness, directness | noun
