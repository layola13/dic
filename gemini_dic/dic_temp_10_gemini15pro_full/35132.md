cloudlet:/ˈklaʊdlət/| n.|小型云，云端小站|A small-scale cloud computing facility located at the edge of the network, closer to users and devices.  It provides low-latency computing resources for applications that require real-time processing.|Example 1: Cloudlets can improve the performance of mobile applications by reducing latency. 小型云可以通过减少延迟来提高移动应用程序的性能。

Example 2:  The company deployed cloudlets in several cities to support its IoT network.  该公司在几个城市部署了小型云以支持其物联网网络。|近义词：edge server, micro data center|反义词: cloud|词性:名词
