factor:/ˈfæktər/ | vt. | 把…分解成因子；代理经营 | To resolve into factors; to sell or buy through a factor. | The company factors its receivables to improve cash flow.  这家公司通过代理经营其应收账款来改善现金流。| synonym: broker, agent | antonym: none | verb
