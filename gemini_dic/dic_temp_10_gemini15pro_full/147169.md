overloyalty:/ˌoʊvərˈlɔɪəlti/ | n. | 过度忠诚，过分忠诚 | Excessive loyalty, often to the point of being detrimental to oneself or others.  It implies a blind allegiance that overlooks flaws or wrongdoing. | His overloyalty to the company blinded him to its unethical practices.  他对公司的过度忠诚使他对公司的不道德行为视而不见。 | loyalty, devotion, allegiance | disloyalty, treachery, betrayal | noun
