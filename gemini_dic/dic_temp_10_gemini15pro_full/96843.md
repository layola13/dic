inevitably:/ɪnˈevɪtəbli/| adv.|不可避免地，必然地|In a way that cannot be avoided. |例句：Inevitably, the conversation turned to politics.  对话不可避免地转向了政治。|近义词：necessarily, certainly, unavoidably|反义词:avoidably, possibly, preventably|词性:副词
Etymology: From Late Latin *inevitabilis, from in- ‘not’ + evitabilis ‘avoidable’.
