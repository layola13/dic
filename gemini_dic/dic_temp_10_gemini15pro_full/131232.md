noncapitalists:/nɒnˈkæpɪtəlɪsts/
n.|非资本家|People who are not capitalists; those who do not believe in or support capitalism as an economic system.|The noncapitalists advocated for worker cooperatives and communal ownership.|近义词:socialists, communists, anti-capitalists|反义词:capitalists|词性:名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non*, meaning "not"
capitalist: from capital + -ist, ultimately from Latin *caput* meaning "head," referring to livestock as a form of capital.  The suffix "-ist" denotes a person who adheres to a particular doctrine or system.
