baccy:/ˈbæki/
noun
烟草，烟丝
Informal British slang for tobacco.
例句：He offered me some of his baccy. 他给了我一些他的烟草。
例句：Fancy a bit of baccy? 想来点烟草吗？
近义词：tobacco, shag, weed
反义词:None (No direct opposite for a consumable item.)
词性:noun

Etymology:
Shortening of "tobacco."  Likely influenced by similar slang formations like "chocky" for chocolate.
