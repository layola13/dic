anthropophuism:/ˌænθrəpəˈfjuːɪzəm/| n.|拟人化，拟人观|The attribution of human characteristics or behavior to a god, animal, or object.|例句：The children's book was full of anthropomorphism, with the animals talking and wearing clothes.  这本童书充满了拟人化，动物们会说话，穿着衣服。

例句：Anthropomorphism is a common literary device used to make animals or inanimate objects more relatable. 拟人化是一种常见的文学手法，用于使动物或无生命物体更具相关性。|近义词：personification|反义词:objectification|词性:noun
