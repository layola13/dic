convoyed:/kənˈvɔɪd/ | v. |护航，护送|To accompany and protect (ships, vehicles, etc.) while traveling, especially through dangerous areas.|The warships convoyed the merchant ships through the pirate-infested waters. 军舰护送商船穿过海盗猖獗的水域。|近义词：escorted, accompanied, guarded, protected|反义词:abandoned|词性:动词
