overkeep: /ˌoʊvərˈkiːp/
verb
过度保留；过久地保留
to keep something for too long, especially food or supplies, so that it becomes spoiled or useless
The grocer overkept the fruit and it rotted.  杂货商把水果放得太久了，结果水果都烂了。
We overkept the potatoes and they started to sprout. 我们把土豆放得太久了，它们开始发芽了。
近义词: hoard, stockpile
反义词: discard, throw away
词性: verb


Etymology:
From "over-" (meaning "excessively") + "keep" (meaning "to retain possession of"). The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "keep," indicating an excessive or prolonged duration of keeping.
