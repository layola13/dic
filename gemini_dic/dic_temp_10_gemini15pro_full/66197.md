excise:/ˈɛksaɪz/ | n. | 消费税;货物税;国产税 | A tax levied on certain goods and commodities produced or sold within a country. | The government increased the excise on tobacco. 政府提高了烟草的消费税。|近义词：duty, tax, levy |反义词: subsidy |词性: 名词

excise:/ɪkˈsaɪz/ | vt. | 切除;删去 | To remove or cut out completely. | The surgeon excised the tumor. 外科医生切除了肿瘤。|近义词：remove, delete, expunge |反义词: insert, add |词性: 动词

词源: From Old French "excise" and directly from Latin "excisus," past participle of "excidere"  meaning "to cut out," from "ex-" (out) + "caedere" (to cut).
