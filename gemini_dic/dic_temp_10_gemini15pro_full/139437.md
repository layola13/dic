obsigillation:/ˌɒbsɪɡɪˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 封印，盖章; 阻挠，压制 | The act of sealing or fastening; the state of being sealed or fastened; also, the act of suppressing or obstructing. | The king's obsigillation of the decree prevented any further debate. 国王对法令的封印阻止了进一步的辩论。| 近义词：sealing, closure, suppression, obstruction | 反义词：opening, release | 词性: 名词

词源: From Latin obsigillationem, accusative of obsigillatio, from obsigillare "to seal up," from ob- "against, toward" + sigillare "to seal," from sigillum "seal."
