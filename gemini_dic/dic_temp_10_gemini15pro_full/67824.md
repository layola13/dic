eyewitnesses:/ˈaɪˈwɪtnəsɪz/
n.
目击者；见证人
A person who has personally seen something happen and so can give a first-hand description of it.
The eyewitnesses to the robbery gave conflicting accounts of what happened.  抢劫案的目击者对发生的事情说法不一。

近义词: observer, witness, onlooker
反义词:  N/A (No direct antonym for a person who sees something)
词性: noun (plural)
