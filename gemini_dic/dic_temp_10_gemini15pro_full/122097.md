mistimed:/ˌmɪsˈtaɪmd/
adjective
不合时宜的；不适时的
Done or occurring at the wrong time.
The joke was mistimed and fell flat.  这个笑话不合时宜，没有引起任何反应。
miscalculated, inappropriate, ill-timed, inopportune
well-timed, opportune, appropriate
词性: 形容词
