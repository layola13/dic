nonstratified:/nɒnˈstrætɪfaɪd/ | adj. | 非分层的，未分层的 | Not divided or arranged into strata or layers. | The soil sample was nonstratified, making it difficult to determine its age. 土壤样本未分层，因此难以确定其年代。| The archaeologist found a nonstratified deposit of artifacts. 考古学家发现了一处未分层的文物堆积。 | 近义词: unstratified, homogeneous, uniform | 反义词: stratified, layered, heterogeneous | 词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix) : not, opposite of  (来自拉丁语 non)
stratified (verb/adjective) : formed or arranged into strata or layers (来自拉丁语 stratum，意为“覆盖物，铺开的床单”，后引申为“地层”)
