overclocked:/ˌoʊvərˈklɑːkt/ /ˌəʊvəˈklɒkt/| adj.|超频的|Increased in operating speed beyond the manufacturer's specifications, typically of a computer component like a CPU or GPU.  This is done to achieve higher performance, but can lead to instability or damage if not done carefully.|My overclocked CPU runs significantly faster than the stock speed. 我的超频CPU比标准速度运行得快得多。|The overclocked graphics card allowed for smoother gameplay at higher resolutions. 超频的显卡可以在更高的分辨率下实现更流畅的游戏体验。|近义词：boosted, enhanced, supercharged|反义词：underclocked, standard, stock|词性:形容词
overclock:/ˌoʊvərˈklɑːk/ /ˌəʊvəˈklɒkt/| vt.|超频|To increase the operating speed of (a computer component) beyond the manufacturer's specifications.|He overclocked his CPU to improve gaming performance. 他对CPU进行超频以提高游戏性能。|She managed to overclock her graphics card without any issues. 她成功地对显卡进行了超频，没有任何问题。|近义词：boost, enhance|反义词：underclock|词性:动词
