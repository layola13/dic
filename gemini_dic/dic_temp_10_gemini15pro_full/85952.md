headmold:/ˈhɛdməʊld/| n.|头模;头部模型|A headmold is a mold of a person's head, typically used in the creation of masks, wigs, or other headwear.  It captures the unique shape and contours of an individual's head, allowing for a precise and customized fit.|The wigmaker used a headmold to create a perfectly fitting wig.  假发制作师用头模制作了一个完全合适的假发。|synonyms: head cast, head form|antonyms: N/A|noun
