animation:/ˌænɪˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 动画；动画片；生气；活力 | The process of making animated cartoons.  The state of being full of life or vigor.|
例句：The animation in the movie was breathtaking.  这部电影的动画效果令人叹为观止。

例句：Her face was full of animation as she described her trip. 她描述旅行的时候，脸上充满了活力。

近义词：liveliness, vibrancy, cartoon, animated film | 反义词：inactivity, dullness | 词性：名词
