nonintrospectivenesses:/ˌnɒnɪntrəʊˈspɛktɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
缺乏自省，缺乏内省
The quality or state of not being introspective; an absence of introspection.
His nonintrospectivenesses led him to make the same mistakes repeatedly. 他缺乏自省导致他反复犯同样的错误。
Her nonintrospectivenesses made it difficult for her to understand her own motivations. 她缺乏自省使她难以理解自己的动机。
Near Synonyms: unreflectiveness, unexamined life
Antonyms: introspectiveness, reflectiveness, self-examination
