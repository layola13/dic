crewnecks:/ˈkruːneks/ | n. | 圆领衫，套头衫 | A crew neck is a type of neckline, most commonly associated with T-shirts and sweaters, that is round and close-fitting to the base of the neck. | He prefers to wear crewnecks in the winter. 他冬天更喜欢穿套头衫。 |  Example: She bought a pack of three crewnecks. 她买了一包三件套头衫。|  Synonyms: sweatshirt, pullover, jumper | Antonyms: V-neck, button-down | noun
