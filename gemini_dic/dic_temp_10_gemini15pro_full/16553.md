barmier:/ˈbɑːrmiər/| adj.|更古怪的，更疯狂的|More eccentric or strange; more foolish.|例句：He's gotten barmier with age.  他随着年龄的增长变得更加古怪了。

例句：Her ideas are getting barmier by the day. 她的想法一天比一天更疯狂了。|近义词：crazier, weirder, more eccentric, more peculiar|反义词：saner, more sensible, more rational|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From barmy + -er (comparative suffix).  Barmy itself comes from "balmy," meaning "foolish" or "slightly mad," likely influenced by the idea of someone having a "soft" or "balmy" head. Ultimately from balsam, a fragrant resin.
