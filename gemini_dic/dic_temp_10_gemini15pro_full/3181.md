affable:/ˈæfəbl/ | adj. | 和蔼可亲的；友善的；容易交谈的 | Friendly and easy to talk to. |  She has an affable manner that makes everyone feel comfortable. 她有一种和蔼可亲的态度，让每个人都感到舒适。|  He was an affable and approachable man. 他是一个和蔼可亲、平易近人的男人。 | 近义词: amiable, approachable, cordial, genial, friendly | 反义词: unapproachable, unfriendly, aloof, hostile, surly | 词性: 形容词

affablest:/ˈæfəblɪst/ | adj. | 最和蔼可亲的；最友善的；最容易交谈的 |  (Superlative of affable) Most friendly and easy to talk to. | He was voted the affablest teacher in the school. 他被选为学校里最和蔼可亲的老师。 | She was the affablest hostess I had ever met. 她是我遇到的最友善的女主人。 | 近义词: most amiable, most approachable, most cordial, most genial, most friendly | 反义词: least approachable, least friendly, most aloof, most hostile, most surly | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin affabilis, from affari (to speak to) + -bilis (able).  最终来源于拉丁语 fari (to speak).
