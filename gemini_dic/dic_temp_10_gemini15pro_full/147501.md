overpackaged:/ˌoʊvərˈpækɪdʒd/
adjective
过度包装的
Wrapped in more layers of packaging than necessary.
The doll was overpackaged in a huge box filled with plastic and Styrofoam.  这个娃娃过度包装，装在一个装满塑料和泡沫聚苯乙烯的巨大盒子里。
The product was so overpackaged, it felt wasteful.  这个产品包装过度，感觉很浪费。
Near synonyms: excessively packaged, unnecessarily packaged
Antonyms: minimally packaged, simply packaged
词性: adjective
