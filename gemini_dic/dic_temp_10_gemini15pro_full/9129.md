antimystical:/ˌæntiˈmɪstɪkəl/
adjective
反神秘主义的
opposed to or dispelling mysticism
例句：His antimystical stance led him to reject any explanation that couldn't be scientifically proven. 他反神秘主义的立场使他拒绝任何无法用科学证明的解释。

例句：The author's antimystical approach to religion focused on the practical aspects of faith. 作者对宗教的反神秘主义方法侧重于信仰的实践方面。

近义词：rational, empirical, pragmatic
反义词：mystical, occult, esoteric
词性: adjective
