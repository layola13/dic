opt-out:/ˌɑːpt ˈaʊt/ | n.  |选择退出；拒绝参加| The form allows parents to opt out of vaccinations for their children.  该表格允许父母选择不让他们的孩子接种疫苗。|近义词：decline, refuse, withdraw |反义词: opt-in, join, participate |词性:名词/动词短语

opt-out:/ˌɑːpt ˈaʊt/ | v. |选择退出；拒绝参加|  Employees can opt out of the company's pension scheme. 员工可以选择不参加公司的养老金计划。 |近义词：decline, refuse, withdraw |反义词: opt-in, join, participate |词性:名词/动词短语

词源: "Opt" comes from the Latin "optare," meaning "to choose" or "to wish." "Out" indicates a withdrawal or removal from something.
