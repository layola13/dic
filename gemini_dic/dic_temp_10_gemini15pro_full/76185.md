fussiness:/ˈfʌsinɪs/| n.| 过分讲究; 爱挑剔; 小题大做; 难伺候|Excessive concern about minor details; a tendency to complain or be easily upset.|The child's fussiness about food worried his mother. 孩子对食物的挑剔让他的母亲很担心。|She showed a surprising fussiness about her clothes. 她对衣服表现出惊人的挑剔。|近义词: pickiness, fastidiousness, choosiness, finickiness|反义词: easygoingness, adaptability, flexibility|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From fussy + -ness.  "Fussy" originates from the now obsolete word "fust" meaning "stale or musty smelling," later evolving to describe a person easily annoyed or irritated, likely due to the unpleasant sensation of a fusty smell.
