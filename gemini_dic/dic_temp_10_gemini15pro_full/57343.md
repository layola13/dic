downy:/ˈdaʊni/ (US), /ˈdaʊni/ (UK)| adj.|绒毛的，柔软的|Covered with soft, fine hair or feathers; like down.  Soft and fluffy.|例句：The baby bird had downy feathers.  这只雏鸟有绒毛般的羽毛。|例句：The blanket was soft and downy.  这条毯子柔软蓬松。|近义词：fluffy, soft, velvety|反义词：coarse, rough, bristly|词性:形容词

downy:/ˈdaʊni/ (US), /ˈdaʊni/ (UK)| n.|绒毛|Soft, fine feathers or hair.|例句：The downy of a young goose is highly prized. 幼鹅的绒毛非常珍贵。|例句：She collected the downy to fill a pillow. 她收集绒毛来填充枕头。|近义词：fluff, fuzz|反义词: - |词性:名词
