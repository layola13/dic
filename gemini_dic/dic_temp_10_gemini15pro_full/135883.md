例句：Her nonprovincially manner and conversation made her stand out in the small town. 她不落俗套的举止和谈吐使她在小镇上脱颖而出。|近义词：cosmopolitanly, broadly, open-mindedly|反义词：provincially, narrowly, locally|词性:副词
