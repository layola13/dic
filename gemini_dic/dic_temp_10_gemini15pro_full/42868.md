courtier:/ˈkɔːrtiər/ (US) /ˈkɔːtɪə(r)/ (UK)| n. |廷臣，朝臣；奉承者|A person who attends a royal court as a companion or advisor to the king or queen.  A person who tries to please someone in order to gain an advantage.|例句：The courtier bowed low before the king. 朝臣在国王面前深深鞠躬。

例句：He acted like a courtier, flattering the boss constantly. 他表现得像个朝臣，不断地奉承老板。|近义词：attendant, sycophant, flatterer|反义词：rebel, dissident|词性: noun
