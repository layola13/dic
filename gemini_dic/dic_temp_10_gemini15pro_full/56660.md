donut's:/ˈdoʊnʌts/ | n. |甜甜圈（複数、所有格）| Donut's is the possessive form of donut, referring to something belonging to or associated with a donut.  It is grammatically incorrect to use "donut's" as a plural. The plural of donut is "donuts." While "donut's" might occasionally appear in informal contexts, it's generally best to avoid it. | Example (showing correct possessive usage with a different noun): The bakery's donuts are delicious.  这家面包店的甜甜圈很好吃。|近义词:doughnuts (plural)|反义词: (No direct antonym for a food item) | 词性:Noun (possessive, though grammatically incorrect for plural)
