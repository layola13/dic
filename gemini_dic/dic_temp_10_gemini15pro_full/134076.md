nonillusively:/ˌnɒnɪˈluːsɪvli/
adv.
非虚幻地；真实地
In a way that is not illusory or deceptive; realistically.
He described the situation nonillusively, acknowledging both the challenges and opportunities. 他如实地描述了情况，承认了挑战和机遇。
近义词: realistically, truly, factually, genuinely, authentically
反义词: illusively, deceptively, falsely, unrealistically
词性: 副词

词源:
non- (prefix):  not, absence of  (非，没有)
illusory (adjective): based on illusion; not real (虚幻的；错觉的)
-ly (suffix): adverbial suffix (副词后缀)
