heavenlinesses:/ˈhɛvənlɪnɪsɪz/
n.
神圣;天堂般的美好;极乐

近义词:divinity, blessedness, sublimity, sanctity
反义词:profaneness, wickedness, worldliness
词性:名词(复数)
