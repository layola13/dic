paste:/peɪst/| n. vt.| 浆糊；糊状物；粘贴|A thick, soft, moist substance, typically made of flour and water and used for sticking things together.  |She mixed flour and water to make a paste. 她把面粉和水混合做成浆糊。|He pasted the picture into his scrapbook. 他把照片粘贴到剪贴簿里。|近义词：glue, adhesive, stick|反义词:separate, detach|词性:名词, 动词

paste:/peɪst/| v.| 粘贴；复制|To stick something to something else using paste or glue; To insert copied text or other data into a document or file.|She pasted the picture onto the card. 她把照片粘贴到卡片上。|I pasted the text from the website into my document. 我把网站上的文字复制粘贴到我的文档里。|近义词：glue, stick, affix, insert, copy|反义词:cut, remove, delete|词性:动词
