overargues:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːrɡjuːz/
verb
过度争论；过分辩论
To argue or debate a point excessively, past the point of being productive or persuasive.
He overargued his case and ended up alienating the jury. 他过度争论他的案件，最终疏远了陪审团。
The lawyer overargued the point and lost the judge's sympathy.  律师过度争论这一点，失去了法官的同情。
synonyms: belabor, overstate, overemphasize
antonyms: concede, acquiesce, yield
