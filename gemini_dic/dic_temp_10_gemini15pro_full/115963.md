marrow:/ˈmærəʊ/ (UK), /ˈmeroʊ/ (US)| n.| 骨髓；精华；活力；西葫芦|The soft fatty substance in the cavities of bones. Also, the essential part of something; the gist.  A type of vegetable marrow, also known as zucchini.|例句：He was chilled to the marrow. 他冷得彻骨。

例句：The marrow of his argument was that education should be free. 他论证的核心是教育应该免费。|近义词：pith, essence, core, gist, substance, zucchini, squash|反义词：superficiality, exterior|词性:noun
