cabanas:/kəˈbɑːnəz/
n.
棚屋，小屋；更衣室；海滩棚
A small, simple building, often open-sided, used for shelter or changing clothes, especially on a beach or by a swimming pool.
We rented a cabana on the beach for the day. 我们在海滩上租了一间小屋，待了一整天。
cabins, huts, sheds
houses, mansions
noun
