consecutive: /kənˈsɛkjətɪv/
adjective
连续的，连贯的
following one after another in a continuous series or order.
She won the championship for three consecutive years. 她连续三年赢得了冠军。
The team has lost five consecutive games.  该队已经连续输了五场比赛。

近义词: successive, sequential, uninterrupted, continuous
反义词: nonconsecutive, intermittent, sporadic, broken
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *consecutivus, from Latin consecutus, past participle of consequi (“to follow closely”), from com- (“together”) + sequi (“to follow”).
