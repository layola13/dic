anointment:/əˈnɔɪntmənt/ | n. | 涂油，膏油，涂油礼 |The act of anointing someone or something with oil, typically as part of a religious ceremony or as a symbol of consecration.  Also refers to the oil itself. | The king's anointment took place in the cathedral. 国王的涂油礼在教堂举行。|  consecration, inauguration, ordination | desecration | noun

anointment:/əˈnɔɪntmənt/ | n. |任命；指定 |The act of appointing someone to a position, especially one of authority or importance. (Figurative Usage)|  His anointment as CEO came as a surprise. 他被任命为首席执行官真是出乎意料。 | appointment, designation, nomination | dismissal, removal | noun
