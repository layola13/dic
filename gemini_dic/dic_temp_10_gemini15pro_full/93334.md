ill-will:/ˌɪlˈwɪl/| n. |敌意；恶意；憎恨|A feeling of dislike or hostility.|Example 1: There's no ill-will between us despite our disagreements. 尽管我们意见不合，但我们之间并没有恶意。

Example 2:  She bore him no ill-will for leaving. 她对他离开并没有恶意。|近义词：animosity, hostility, malice, resentment, rancor|反义词:goodwill, benevolence, kindness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *ilwill*, from Old English *illwilla* (“ill will, malevolence”), from *ill* (“bad, evil”) + *willa* (“will”).  Cognate with Dutch *ille wil* (“ill will”).  Surface analysis is *ill* + *will*.
