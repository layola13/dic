deriving:/dɪˈraɪvɪŋ/ | v. | 获得，取得；源自，衍生 | Obtaining something from a source or origin.  |例句：He is deriving great pleasure from his new hobby. 他从他的新爱好中获得了极大的乐趣。

例句：Many words in English are deriving from Latin. 英语中的许多单词源自拉丁语。 | 近义词：obtaining, gaining, acquiring, stemming, originating | 反义词：losing, forfeiting, relinquishing | 词性：动词

deriving:/dɪˈraɪvɪŋ/ | adj. | 衍生的，派生的 | Formed or developed from something else. | 例句：The company's success is deriving from its innovative products.  这家公司的成功源于其创新产品。

例句：This new theory is deriving from recent research.  这个新理论源于最近的研究。| 近义词：derived, originating, stemming | 反义词：original, primary | 词性：形容词 (现在分词形式，用作形容词)
