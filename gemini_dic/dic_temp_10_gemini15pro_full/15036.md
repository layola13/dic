babul:/ˈbɑːbuːl/| n.|巴布尔树，金合欢树|A thorny tree (Acacia nilotica) of the mimosa family, native to Africa and India.  Its bark is used in tanning and its gum in medicine. It's also known as the Egyptian acacia or gum arabic tree.|The babul tree provided much-needed shade in the scorching heat. 巴布尔树在酷热中提供了急需的树荫。

The babul's gum is used in traditional medicine. 巴布尔树的树胶用于传统医学。|近义词：acacia, mimosa|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific tree)|词性:noun
