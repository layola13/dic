nonpopulousnesses:/ˌnɒnˈpɒpjʊləsnɪsɪz/
noun
人口稀少
The state of having a small population.
Many remote islands are known for their nonpopulousnesses. 许多偏远岛屿以人口稀少而闻名。
The vast desert regions are characterized by their extreme nonpopulousnesses.  广袤的沙漠地区以其极端的人口稀少为特征。

synonym
sparsely populated, underpopulated, thinly populated
antonym
