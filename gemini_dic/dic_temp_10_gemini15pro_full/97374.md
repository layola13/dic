inframaxillary:/ˌɪnfrəmæˈksɪləri/
adjective
下颌下的；位于下颌骨之下的
Situated below the lower jaw.
The inframaxillary gland is located beneath the jawbone. 下颌下腺位于下颌骨下方。
Synonyms: submaxillary, submandibular
Antonyms: supramaxillary


Etymology:
infra- (Latin, prefix meaning "below, beneath") + maxillary (from Latin maxilla, meaning "jawbone")
