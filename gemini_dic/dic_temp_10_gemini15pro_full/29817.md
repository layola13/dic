cauldron:/ˈkɔːldrən/ (US) /ˈkɔːldrən/ (UK)| n. |大锅; 大汽锅; (常指女巫用的)熬炼魔药的大锅|A large metal pot with a lid and handle, used for cooking over an open fire, especially one used for boiling liquids or cooking stews.|The witch stirred a bubbling potion in her cauldron. 女巫在她的大锅里搅拌着冒泡的药水。|synonyms: pot, kettle, vat|antonyms: None|noun

cauldron:/ˈkɔːldrən/ (US) /ˈkɔːldrən/ (UK)| n. |(比喻) 熔炉; 险恶的环境|A situation of great unrest or danger.|The city became a cauldron of racial tension. 这座城市变成了种族紧张的熔炉。|synonyms: melting pot, hotbed|antonyms: None|noun
