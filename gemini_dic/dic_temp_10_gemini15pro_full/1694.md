aconitine:/əˈkɒnɪtiːn/ /əˈkɑːnɪtiːn/| n.|乌头碱|A highly poisonous alkaloid derived from aconite plants.|Aconitine is a potent neurotoxin and cardiotoxin. 乌头碱是一种强效的神经毒素和心脏毒素。

Aconitine poisoning can cause nausea, vomiting, and paralysis. 乌头碱中毒会导致恶心、呕吐和瘫痪。|近义词:neurotoxin, cardiotoxin|反义词:antidote|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *aconitine*, from *aconit* (“aconite”) + *-ine* (alkaloid suffix).  Ultimately from Ancient Greek *ἀκόνιτον* (akóniton, “aconite”).
