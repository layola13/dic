detachednesses:/dɪˈtætʃtɪdnəsɪz/
n.
客观公正;超然;冷漠;分离
The state of being detached or uninvolved; aloofness.
He observed the situation with a certain detachedness. 他以一种超然的态度观察着局势。
Synonyms: aloofness, indifference, objectivity, neutrality, impartiality
Antonyms: involvement, engagement, attachment, bias, subjectivity
词性: 名词
