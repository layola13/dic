obituarian: /əˈbɪtʃʊˌɛriən/ | n. | 讣告撰写人；（尤指）报刊的讣闻栏编辑 | a person who writes obituaries, especially for a newspaper | The obituarian had a knack for capturing the essence of a life in just a few paragraphs.  这位讣告撰写人善于用几段文字概括人生的精髓。 | synonyms: obituary writer, death notice writer | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps biographer for someone who writes about the lives of the living) | noun


obituary: /əˈbɪtʃʊˌɛri/ | n. | 讣告，讣闻 | a notice of a death, especially in a newspaper, typically including a brief biography of the deceased person | I read her obituary in the newspaper this morning. 我今天早上在报纸上读到了她的讣告。 | synonyms: death notice, necrology | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps birth announcement) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *obituarius* (relating to death), from *obitus* (death, decease).
