overrepresentatively:/ˌoʊvərˌrɛprɪˈzɛntətɪvli/
| adv. | 过度代表地；代表性过高地 |  In a way that overrepresents something or someone; to a degree that is disproportionately large. |  Certain demographics are overrepresentatively affected by the new policy.  某些人群受新政策的影响过大。 |近义词: disproportionately, excessively |反义词: underrepresentatively, insufficiently | 词性: 副词
