andesite:/ˈændɪsaɪt/| n.|安山岩|A dark, fine-grained, brown or grayish volcanic rock.  It is an extrusive equivalent of diorite.  |The Andes Mountains contain large quantities of andesite. 安第斯山脉含有大量的安山岩。|Near synonyms:  volcanic rock, igneous rock, extrusive rock|Antonyms:  N/A (Rock types don't typically have direct opposites)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Andes + -ite,  named after the Andes mountains where it is common.
