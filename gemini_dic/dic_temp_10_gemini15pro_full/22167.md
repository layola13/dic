bombast:/ˈbɑːmbæst/ /ˈbɒmbæst/| n.|夸大的言辞；浮夸的言论|High-sounding language with little meaning, used to impress people.|His speech was full of bombast, but lacked substance. 他的演讲充满了夸夸其谈，但缺乏实质内容。|近义词：bluster, rhetoric, grandiloquence|反义词：understatement, simplicity, conciseness|词性:noun

bombastic:/bɒmˈbæstɪk/ /bɑːmˈbæstɪk/| adj.|夸大的；言过其实的|Using high-sounding but empty language.|The politician's bombastic pronouncements failed to impress the voters.  这位政客夸夸其谈的声明未能打动选民。|近义词：inflated, pompous, overblown|反义词：understated, simple, modest|词性:adjective
