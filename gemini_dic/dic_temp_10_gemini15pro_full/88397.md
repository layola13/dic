hirohito:/ˌhiːroʊˈhiːtoʊ/ /ˌhɪrəˈhiːtəʊ/ | n. | 裕仁天皇 |  The 124th Emperor of Japan, reigning from 1926 until his death in 1989. His reign encompassed Japan's militarization, aggression in Asia, defeat in World War II, and subsequent recovery and modernization. | Emperor Hirohito was a controversial figure due to his role during the war. 裕仁天皇由于其在战争期间的角色而备受争议。 | Hirohito was known for his interest in marine biology.  裕仁天皇以其对海洋生物学的兴趣而闻名。 | synonym: Emperor Shōwa, 昭和天皇 | antonym: N/A | noun
