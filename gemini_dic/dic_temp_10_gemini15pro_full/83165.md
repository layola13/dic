guttiferous:/ˌɡʌtˈɪfərəs/
adjective
产树脂的；树脂状的
Producing or exuding gum or resin.
The guttiferous tree oozed a sticky sap. 这棵产树脂的树渗出粘稠的树液。
近义词:resinous, gummy
反义词:non-resinous


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *guttifer, from gutta (drop) + -fer (bearing).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *gʰewd- (to pour).
