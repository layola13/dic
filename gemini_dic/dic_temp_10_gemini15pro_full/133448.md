nonfatally: /ˌnɒnˈfeɪtəli/ | adv. | 非致命地 | In a way that does not cause death. | The driver was nonfatally injured in the crash.  司机在车祸中受了非致命伤。|  The cyclist was thankfully nonfatally injured.  谢天谢地，骑自行车的人没有受致命伤。 |  Synonym: harmlessly, innocuously | Antonym: fatally, lethally | adverb

Etymology:
non- (Latin prefix meaning "not") + fatally (from "fatal," ultimately from Latin "fatum" meaning "fate, destiny")
