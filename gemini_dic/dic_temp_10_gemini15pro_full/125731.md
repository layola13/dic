-munity:/ˈmjuːnɪti/
词性: n.
中文翻译: 社区；共同体；群体
英文解析: A group of people living in the same place or having a particular characteristic in common.  A feeling of fellowship with others, as a result of sharing common attitudes, interests, and goals.
例句：The local community came together to support the family. 当地社区团结起来支持这个家庭。

例句：There is a strong sense of community in this town. 在这个小镇里，有一种强烈的社区意识。

近义词: society, group, neighborhood, public, collective
反义词: individual, isolation
