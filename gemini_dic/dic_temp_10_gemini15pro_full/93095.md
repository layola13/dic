idyls: /ˈɪdɪlz/ /ˈaɪdɪlz/ | n. | 田园诗; 牧歌; 浪漫的插曲 | A short poem or prose piece describing a simple, pleasant scene of rural or pastoral life; a carefree episode or experience, often romanticized. |  The poet's idyls celebrated the beauty of the natural world. 这位诗人的田园诗歌颂了自然世界的美丽。

The couple spent an idyllic weekend in the countryside. 这对夫妇在乡下度过了一个浪漫的周末。 | 近义词: pastorals, eclogues, romances | 反义词: realities, tragedies | 词性: 名词 (Noun)
