nonsupplications:/ˌnɑːnsʌplɪˈkeɪʃənz/
noun
非恳求；不祈求
lack of supplications; absence of pleading or begging.
The king was angered by the nonsupplications of his subjects, interpreting their silence as defiance. 国王对臣民不祈求的行为感到愤怒，将他们的沉默理解为蔑视。
nonsupplication (singular)
lack of entreaty, absence of petition
The nonsupplication of the defendant suggested a lack of remorse.  被告没有恳求表明他缺乏悔意。

近义词:  non-entreaty, indifference, apathy, silence
反义词:  supplications, pleas, entreaties, petitions
词性: noun (plural)
