groaningly:/ˈɡrəʊnɪŋli/ (US) /ˈɡrəʊnɪŋli/ (UK)
adverb
呻吟地；抱怨地
in a way that involves groaning, typically to express pain, annoyance, or disapproval.
He groaningly lifted the heavy box. 他呻吟着抬起了沉重的箱子。
The old door groaningly swung open. 老门吱吱呀呀地打开了。
近义词: complainingly, grumblingly, whiningly
反义词: cheerfully, willingly, enthusiastically
词性: adverb

词源: From groan + -ing + -ly.  "Groan" comes from Old English *grānian, of Germanic origin.
