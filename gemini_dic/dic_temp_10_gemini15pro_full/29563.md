catchline:/ˈkætʃlaɪn/| n.|广告标语；妙句；吸引人的标题|A short, memorable phrase used in advertising or promotion.|The catchline for the new movie is "Prepare to be amazed." 这部新电影的广告标语是“准备好大吃一惊吧”。|近义词：slogan, tagline, headline|反义词:|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From 'catch' (meaning to attract attention) + 'line' (meaning a phrase or sentence).  The word emerged in the late 19th/early 20th centuries with the rise of modern advertising. 由“catch”（意为吸引注意力）和“line”（意为短语或句子）组成。这个词出现在19世纪末/20世纪初，随着现代广告的兴起。
