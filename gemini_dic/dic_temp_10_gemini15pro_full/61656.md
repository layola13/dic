emissives:/ɪˈmɪsɪvz/| adj.|发射的，散发性的| Relating to or having the power to emit something, such as light, heat, or radiation.| The emissive properties of the material make it ideal for use in solar panels. 这种材料的发射特性使其非常适合用于太阳能电池板。|近义词：radiant, emitting|反义词:absorbent, non-emitting|词性:adjective
emissive:/ɪˈmɪsɪv/| n.|发射体，散发物|Something that emits something, such as light, heat, or radiation.|The sun is a powerful emissive of light and heat. 太阳是光和热的强大发射体。|近义词：emitter, radiator|反义词:absorber|词性:noun
