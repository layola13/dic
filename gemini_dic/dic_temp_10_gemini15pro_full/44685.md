crucifying:/ˈkruːsɪfaɪɪŋ/| adj.| 极其痛苦的，折磨人的；使人极度尴尬的|  Causing intense mental or physical suffering; extremely embarrassing or difficult to endure.|  It was a crucifying experience to watch my team lose in the final seconds.  看着我的球队在最后几秒钟输掉比赛，真是一次痛苦的经历。| synonyms: agonizing, torturous, excruciating, harrowing, mortifying | antonyms: pleasant, enjoyable, comforting, soothing | Adjective
