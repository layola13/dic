orthodoxian:/ˌɔːrθəˈdɒksiən/
| adj. n.|正统的，正统的人；正统派信徒|Relating to or conforming to the approved or generally accepted form of any doctrine, philosophy, ideology, etc.; conventional.|例句：He held orthodoxian views on education. 他持有正统的教育观点。|例句：She is considered an orthodoxian in her political party.  她在她的政党中被认为是正统派。|近义词：orthodox, conventional, traditional|反义词：heterodox, unconventional, radical|词性：形容词, 名词

Etymology:  From orthodoxy + -ian.  "Orthodoxy" comes from Late Latin *orthodoxia, from Greek orthodoxia "right opinion," from orthos "right, true, straight" + doxa "opinion, glory, praise."
