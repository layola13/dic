interjectionally:/ˌɪntərˈdʒɛkʃənəli/
adverb
感叹地，插入语式地
Used to describe words or phrases spoken as interjections, expressing sudden emotion.
"Wow," she said interjectionally, "that's amazing!"  “哇，”她感叹道，“太棒了！”
Near Synonyms:  exclamatorily, parenthetically
Antonyms:  calmly, unexcitedly
词性: adverb (副词)
