interglacial:/ˌɪntərˈɡleɪʃəl/ | adj. n. |间冰期的，间冰期 |Relating to or denoting a period of milder climate between two glacial periods.  A period of milder climate between two glacial periods. |The fossils were found in interglacial deposits. 这些化石是在间冰期沉积物中发现的。| The last interglacial period ended about 115,000 years ago.  上一次间冰期大约在115,000年前结束。 | Synonyms: interstadial | Antonyms: glacial | adjective, noun
