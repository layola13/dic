nontransparence:/ˌnɒntrænsˈpærəns/ |n.|不透明性，不透明度|Lack of transparency; the quality of being nontransparent.  The inability to see through something, either literally or figuratively. |The nontransparence of the frosted glass provided privacy.  毛玻璃的不透明性提供了隐私。|The nontransparence of the government's decision-making process led to public distrust. 政府决策过程的不透明导致了公众的不信任。|近义词: opacity, opaqueness |反义词: transparency, clarity |词性: 名词
