eurasiatio:/jʊˌreɪʒiˈeɪʃioʊ/ /jʊˌreɪʒiˈeɪʃən/
adjective
欧亚的
Relating to or characteristic of both Europe and Asia.  Formed by combining "Eurasia" with the suffix "-atio" or "-ation" which implies a connection or characteristic.
The eurasiatic steppe stretches across a vast expanse. 欧亚草原绵延广阔。
The eurasiatic landmass is home to diverse cultures. 欧亚大陆拥有多元文化。
近义词: Eurasian
反义词: (None - as it relates to a geographical area)
词性: adjective
