bishoply:/ˈbɪʃəpli/| adj.|主教的；像主教的；庄严的|Resembling or characteristic of a bishop, especially in manner or appearance; dignified and stately.|例句：He had a bishoply air about him. 他有一种主教般的气质。|近义词：dignified, stately, solemn, pontifical, episcopal|反义词：undignified, frivolous, irreverent|词性:形容词

bishoply:/ˈbɪʃəpli/| adv.|以主教的方式|In a manner resembling or characteristic of a bishop.|例句：He spoke bishoply, offering blessings and pronouncements. 他像主教一样讲话，给予祝福和声明。|近义词：solemnly, pontifically|反义词：irreverently, frivolously|词性:副词
