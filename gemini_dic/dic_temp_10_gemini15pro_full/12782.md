assimilate:/əˈsɪməleɪt/ /əˈsɪmɪleɪt/
verb
吸收; 同化
to absorb and integrate (people, ideas, or culture) into a wider society or culture.
Many immigrants have successfully assimilated into American society. 许多移民已经成功地融入了美国社会。

assimilate:/əˈsɪməleɪt/ /əˈsɪmɪleɪt/
verb
(被)吸收; (被)同化
to be absorbed and integrated into a wider society or culture.
The new information was quickly assimilated by the students. 新信息很快就被学生们吸收了。

assimilate:/əˈsɪməleɪt/ /əˈsɪmɪleɪt/
verb
消化吸收
to absorb and digest (food or nutrients).
The body assimilates nutrients from the food we eat. 身体从我们吃的食物中吸收营养。


近义词: absorb, integrate, incorporate, digest
反义词: reject, segregate, exclude
词性: verb
