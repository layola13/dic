misreprinting:/ˌmɪsˌriːˈprɪntɪŋ/
n.|误印，错印|The act of reprinting something incorrectly, with errors or omissions.|The misreprinting of the article led to a correction being published the following day. 这篇文章的误印导致第二天发表了更正。|misprint, incorrect printing|correct printing
