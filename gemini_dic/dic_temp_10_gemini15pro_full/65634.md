evangelize: /ɪˈvændʒəlaɪz/ (US), /ɪˈvændʒɪlaɪz/ (UK)
| v. | 传福音；使皈依；宣传；推广 | To preach the gospel to; to convert to Christianity; to promote or advocate something with enthusiasm. |  Missionaries were sent to evangelize the local population.  传教士被派去向当地居民传福音。

| | | | The company is trying to evangelize the benefits of its new software. 这家公司正努力宣传其新软件的优势。 | preach, proselytize, promote, advocate | discourage, oppose, suppress | verb
