decant:/dɪˈkænt/| v.|倾倒，滗出|Carefully decant the wine into a carafe. 小心地将葡萄酒倒入醒酒器。|例句：He decanted the wine into a crystal decanter. 他把酒倒进了一个水晶醒酒器。|例句：She decanted the soup into bowls for her guests. 她把汤倒进碗里给客人。|近义词：pour, transfer, siphon|反义词:retain, keep|verb

decant:/dɪˈkænt/| n.|倾析，滗|The decant of the wine improved its flavor. 醒酒改善了葡萄酒的风味。(此处decant作为名词，较少见)|例句：The decant was clear and bright. 倾析出的液体清澈明亮。|例句：The decant was then aged for another six months. 倾析后的液体又陈酿了六个月。|近义词：pouring, transfer, siphoning (as nouns - usage is less common)|反义词:retention, keeping (as nouns)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *decanten*, from Old French *decante*, from Latin *decantō* (“to sing loudly, to chant, to charm away, to pour off”), from *de-* (“down, away”) + *cantō* (“to sing, to chant”).
