chiaroscuro:/ˌkjɑːrəˈskʊəroʊ/ (US), /ˌkjɑːrəˈskjʊərəʊ/ (UK)| n.|明暗对照法；强烈对比|The painting is a masterful use of chiaroscuro, with dramatic contrasts between light and shadow. 这幅画巧妙地运用了明暗对比法，光影对比强烈。|例句：The artist used chiaroscuro to create a sense of depth and drama. 艺术家运用明暗对比法创造了深度和戏剧性的感觉。

例句：The film noir style is characterized by its use of chiaroscuro lighting. 黑色电影风格的特点是使用明暗对比的灯光。|近义词：contrast, light and shade|反义词: monotone, flatness|词性:noun
