implying:/ɪmˈplaɪɪŋ/
verb
暗示，意味着
suggesting something indirectly rather than stating it directly
"Are you implying that I'm fat?" 她是在暗示我很胖吗？
synonyms: suggesting, hinting, intimating
antonyms: stating, declaring, expressing
verb
