clearinghouse:/ˈklɪərɪŋˌhaʊs/| n.|清算所，票据交换所，信息交换中心|An institution that settles mutual claims and accounts between parties, especially in financial or commercial transactions.|The clearinghouse processed millions of transactions daily. 清算所每天处理数百万笔交易。|Synonyms: clearing house, exchange, center|Antonyms: N/A|noun

clearinghouse:/ˈklɪərɪŋˌhaʊs/| n.|信息交换中心|A central agency for the collection, classification, and distribution of information.|The organization acts as a clearinghouse for information on endangered species. 该组织充当濒危物种信息的交换中心。|Synonyms: information center, hub, repository|Antonyms: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "clearing" (meaning settling accounts) + "house" (meaning a place of business).  The term originally referred to a place where banks exchanged checks and settled balances.
