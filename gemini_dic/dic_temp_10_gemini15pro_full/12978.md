astigmatism: /əˈstɪɡmətɪzəm/
n.
散光
A defect in the eye or in a lens caused by a deviation from spherical curvature, which results in distorted images, as light rays are prevented from meeting at a common focus.
I have astigmatism and need to wear glasses. 我有散光，需要戴眼镜。
My eye doctor told me my astigmatism has worsened. 我的眼科医生告诉我我的散光加重了。
近义词: eye defect, refractive error
反义词: (No direct antonym for a medical condition)
