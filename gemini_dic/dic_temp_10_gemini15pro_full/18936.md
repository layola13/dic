bespit:/bɪˈspɪt/
verb
吐唾沫于；以唾沫弄脏
to spit on; to cover with spit
He bespat the ground in disgust. 他厌恶地朝地上吐了口唾沫。
近义词: spit on, expectorate on
反义词: None
词性：v.

Etymology:
From be- + spit.  "Be-" is a prefix meaning "thoroughly" or "all over."  "Spit" comes from Old English "spittan."
