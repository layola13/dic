blazoners:/ˈbleɪzənərz/
n.
纹章官；纹章描述者；宣扬者
One who blazons, especially one who designs or describes coats of arms.
The blazoners meticulously recorded the details of each knight's shield.  纹章官一丝不苟地记录了每个骑士盾牌的细节。
The king's blazoners created a magnificent coat of arms for the new prince. 国王的纹章官为新王子设计了一件华丽的盾形纹章。
Synonyms: herald, armorist
Antonyms: N/A
