disturbedly:/dɪˈstɜːbdli/
| adv. |不安地，心神不定地|In a manner showing anxiety, worry, or mental distress.|She paced the room disturbedly, wringing her hands. 她不安地在房间里踱来踱去，绞着双手。|近义词：anxiously, nervously, worriedly, agitatedly|反义词：calmly, peacefully, serenely|词性:副词
Etymology: From "disturbed" + "-ly". "Disturbed" comes from the Old French "destourber" (to disturb, trouble), which in turn derives from the Latin "turbare" (to disturb, confuse).
