dinnerless:/ˈdɪnərlɪs/
adjective
无晚餐的，未进晚餐的
Without dinner; having no dinner.
The travelers were dinnerless due to the delayed flight.  旅客们因为航班延误而没有吃晚餐。
The poor family went dinnerless many nights.  这个贫困的家庭很多晚上都没有吃晚餐。
近义词:hungry, unfed
反义词:fed, well-fed
词性:adjective

词源:
From "dinner" + "-less" (suffix meaning "without").  "Dinner" comes from Old French "disner" (meaning "to break one's fast"), ultimately from Vulgar Latin "disjunare" (meaning "to break one's fast").
