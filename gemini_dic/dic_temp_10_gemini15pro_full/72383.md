flucan:/ˈfluːkæn/| n. |氟康唑|Antifungal medication used to treat a variety of fungal infections.|She was prescribed fluconazole for a yeast infection. 她被开了氟康唑来治疗酵母菌感染。|Synonyms: Diflucan (brand name),  fluconazole |Antonyms:  N/A|noun


Etymology:  "fluc-" relates to the chemical structure (a triazole antifungal), "-conazole" is a common suffix for azole antifungals.
