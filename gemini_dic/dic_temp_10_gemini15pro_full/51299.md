/ˈdiːteɪl/ /ˈdiːtəl/ | vt.| 详述，详细说明 |  to tell or describe something completely. | He detailed his plans for the new company. 他详细说明了他对新公司的计划. |elaborate, expound, specify| summarize, abridge| verb|

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *detail*, from *detailler* "to cut into pieces," from *de-* "apart" + *tailler* "to cut."
