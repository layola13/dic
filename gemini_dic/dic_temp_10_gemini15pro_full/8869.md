antifilibuster:/ˌæntiˈfɪlɪbʌstər/
adjective|反冗长辩论的，阻止阻挠议事的|Opposed to the use of the filibuster, a tactic used in legislative bodies to delay or block a vote on a bill or other measure.|The senator was a staunch antifilibuster advocate, believing that the tactic stifled productive debate.|近义词: anti-obstructionist|反义词: pro-filibuster
noun|反冗长辩论者，阻止阻挠议事者|A person who opposes the use of the filibuster.|The group of antifilibusters worked tirelessly to reform Senate rules.|近义词: anti-obstructionist|反义词: filibusterer
词性: adjective, noun
