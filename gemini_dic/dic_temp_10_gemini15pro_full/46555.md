dalliers:/ˈdæliərz/ | n. |  闲荡者；游手好闲的人；拖延者 | People who dally or delay; lingerers.  Those who spend their time idly or wastefully. |  The dalliers were reprimanded for not finishing their work on time.  那些闲荡者因为没有按时完成工作而受到斥责。| 近义词：lingerers, loiterers, idlers, procrastinators | 反义词：hustlers, go-getters, workers | 词性: noun (plural)

Etymology:
From the verb "dally," from Middle English *dalien*, probably from Old Norse *dvelja* "to delay, tarry."
