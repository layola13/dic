exudation:/ˌɛksjʊˈdeɪʃən/ | n. | 渗出, 渗出物 | The process of exuding; something exuded, like sweat, sap, or pus. |  The exudation of sap from the tree was a sign of disease. 树液的渗出是疾病的迹象。 | 近义词: secretion, discharge, oozing | 反义词: absorption, intake | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin *exsūdātiō*, from Latin exsūdāre (“to sweat out”), from ex (“out”) + sūdāre (“to sweat”).
