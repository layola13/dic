outgate:/ˈaʊtɡeɪt/
n.
出口，外出通道
An exit; a gate for going out.
The outgate of the airport was crowded with travelers. 机场出口挤满了旅客。

Passengers exited the stadium through the designated outgate.  乘客从指定的出口离开了体育场。

近义词: exit, egress, way out
反义词: ingate, entrance

词性: noun (名词)

词源:  "out" (meaning outside) + "gate" (meaning a barrier or passage).  A compound word formed from straightforward elements.
