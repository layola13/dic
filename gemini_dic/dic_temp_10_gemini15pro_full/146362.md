overflattened:/ˌoʊvərˈflætnd/
adjective
过度扁平的，过度平坦的
Made excessively flat.  Flattened beyond what is normal or desirable.
The dough was overflattened, resulting in a cracker-like crust. 面团被压得太扁了，导致饼皮像饼干一样。
The overflattened cushions provided little comfort.  这些过于扁平的垫子几乎不能提供舒适感。
近义词: excessively flattened, too flat
反义词: puffed up, inflated
词性: adjective
