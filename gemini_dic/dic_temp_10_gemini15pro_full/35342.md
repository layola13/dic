co-determine:/ˌkoʊdɪˈtɜːrmɪn/
verb
共同决定；共同参与决定；共同确定
To decide or determine something together with another person or group.
Employees in Germany have the right to co-determine major company decisions.  德国员工有权共同决定公司的重大决策。
The board and the employees co-determine the future direction of the company. 董事会和员工共同决定公司的未来方向。

近义词: share decision-making, participate in decision-making, jointly decide
反义词: dictate, impose, unilaterally decide
词性: verb

词源: From the German word "Mitbestimmung," meaning "co-determination."  The prefix "co-" means "together" or "jointly," and "determine" means "to decide or establish definitely."
