buoy:/bɔɪ/ /buːi/| n. vt.|浮标，救生圈；使浮起，使振作|A buoy is a floating object anchored in water to mark a location or a hazard.  As a verb, buoy means to keep afloat or to uplift someone's spirits.|The bright red buoy marked the entrance to the harbor.  明亮的红色浮标标志着港口的入口。|近义词：float, life preserver, encourage, uplift|反义词:sink, depress|noun, verb

buoy:/bɔɪ/ /buːi/| v.|使浮起，使振作|To buoy someone's spirits means to make them feel more cheerful or hopeful.|The good news buoyed their spirits. 好消息使他们振作起来。|近义词：encourage, uplift|反义词:depress, discourage|verb
