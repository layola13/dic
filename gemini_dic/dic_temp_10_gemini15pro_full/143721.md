outlipped: /ˌaʊtˈlɪpt/
verb
超过，胜过（在言辞或争论中）
To surpass or defeat someone in speaking or argument, especially by being more persuasive or witty.
He outlipped his opponent in the debate with his quick wit and sharp arguments. 他在辩论中以其敏捷的才思和犀利的论据胜过了对手。
She easily outlipped the other salespeople and closed the deal. 她轻松地胜过其他销售人员，完成了交易。
synonym: outtalk, outargue, surpass
antonym: lose to, be defeated by
词性: 动词
