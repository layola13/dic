cod's:/kɒdz/| n.|鳕鱼的；属于鳕鱼的|Belonging to or associated with a cod (fish).|例句：Cod's liver oil is a good source of vitamin D.  鳕鱼肝油是维生素D的良好来源。

例句：He caught a large cod's head in his net.  他的网里捕到一个很大的鳕鱼头。|近义词:fish's|反义词:N/A|词性:所有格

词源: From Middle English *coddes, genitive of codde (“cod”), from Old Norse koddi (“cushion, pillow; testicle; cod”), from Proto-Germanic *kudō (“bag, scrotum”), ultimately of uncertain origin.  More at cod.
