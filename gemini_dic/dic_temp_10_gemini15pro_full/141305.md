open-mouthednesses:/ˌoʊpənˈmaʊðɪdnəsɪz/
| n. |张口结舌；目瞪口呆|The state of being open-mouthed, especially as a result of astonishment or wonder.|Her open-mouthedness at the news was evident. 她听到这个消息后目瞪口呆的样子很明显。|Synonyms: amazement, astonishment, awe, wonder|Antonyms: indifference, apathy|noun

open-mouthed:/ˌoʊpənˈmaʊðd/
| adj. |张着嘴的；目瞪口呆的|With one's mouth open, especially as a result of astonishment or wonder.|He stared open-mouthed at the spectacle. 他目瞪口呆地盯着那景象。|Synonyms: agape, gaping, stunned, amazed|Antonyms: closed-mouthed, impassive|adjective

mouth:/maʊθ/
| n. |嘴；口|The opening in the face used for eating, speaking, etc.|She opened her mouth to speak. 她张开嘴说话。|Synonyms: lips, orifice, opening|Antonyms: none|noun
