centenarianism: /ˌsɛntɪˈnɛəriənɪzəm/
n.
百岁老人现象;  百岁人瑞之盛行; 长寿
The state or condition of being a centenarian; the increasing prevalence of centenarians.
The study of centenarianism is gaining traction as more people live to be 100 and beyond.  对百岁老人现象的研究越来越受到关注，因为越来越多的人活到100岁以上。
Advances in medicine have contributed to the rise of centenarianism. 医学的进步促成了百岁老人现象的兴起。
Synonyms: longevity, long life
Antonyms:  short life span, premature death
