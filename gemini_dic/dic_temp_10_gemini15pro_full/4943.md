alisphenoid:/ælɪˈsfɪnɔɪd/| n. |蝶翼骨|A compound bone forming part of the lateral wall of the cranium in vertebrates. It is situated in front of the parietal and squamosal and behind the orbital.  In mammals, it forms part of the sphenoid bone.|The alisphenoid forms a significant portion of the dog's skull. 蝶翼骨构成了狗头骨的重要部分。|The alisphenoid protects important nerves and blood vessels in the reptile brain. 蝶翼骨保护爬行动物大脑中的重要神经和血管。|Synonyms: greater wing of sphenoid|Antonyms: None|Noun
