herniotomy:/ˌhɜːrniˈɒtəmi/ | n. |疝修补术|Surgical repair of a hernia.  The operation to correct a hernia. It involves pushing the herniated tissue back into place and repairing the weakened muscle wall. |例句：The patient underwent a herniotomy to repair an inguinal hernia. 患者接受了疝修补术以修复腹股沟疝。

例句：The surgeon performed a laparoscopic herniotomy.  外科医生进行了腹腔镜疝修补术。|近义词：herniorrhaphy|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
