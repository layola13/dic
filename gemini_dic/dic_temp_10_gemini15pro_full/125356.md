multilobes:/ˈmʌltiˌloubz/ | n. | 多叶体；多裂片体 |  A structure or organ having multiple lobes.  Having many lobes, as a leaf or a body part. | The liver is a multilobed organ. 肝脏是一个多叶器官。 | The multilobes of the lung allow for efficient gas exchange.  肺的多叶结构允许有效的气体交换。| synonyms: multipartite, multifid | antonyms: entire, simple | noun
