nondieting:/ˌnɒnˈdaɪətɪŋ/ | adj. | 非节食的，不限制饮食的 |  Relating to or characteristic of an approach to health and well-being that does not involve restrictive dieting.  It emphasizes intuitive eating, body acceptance, and overall health rather than weight loss. |  She's adopted a nondieting approach to food and feels much happier. 她采取了非节食的饮食方法，感觉快乐多了。|  The nondieting movement promotes self-care and body positivity. 非节食运动提倡自我保健和身体积极性。 | Synonyms:  intuitive eating, mindful eating, anti-diet  | Antonyms: dieting, restrictive eating | adjective
