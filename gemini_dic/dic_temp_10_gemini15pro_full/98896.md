intensest:/ɪnˈtɛnsɪst/| adj.|最激烈的，最强烈的|superlative adjective of intense; meaning of the greatest intensity|The intensest heat of the summer made everyone sluggish. 夏天最酷热的时候让每个人都昏昏欲睡。|近义词：strongest, greatest, most extreme, most intense|反义词：mildest, weakest, slightest|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *intens*, from Old French *intens*, from Latin *intensus* ("stretched out, strained, violent, vehement"), past participle of *intendere* ("to stretch out, strain").
