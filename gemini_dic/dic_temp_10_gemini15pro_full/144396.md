outstriking: /ˌaʊtˈstraɪkɪŋ/
verb
超过打击; 打败; 胜过
Striking out further or more effectively than something else.
The boxer's outstriking ability gave him a significant advantage.  这位拳击手的超强打击能力给了他明显的优势。
The new marketing campaign is outstriking the old one in terms of reach and engagement. 新的营销活动在覆盖面和参与度方面都超过了旧的。
近义词: surpassing, exceeding, outperforming, outdoing
反义词: underperforming, lagging behind
词性: verb
