emeu:/ˈiːmjuː/ (US), /ˈiːmjuː/ (UK)| n.|鸸鹋|A large, flightless bird native to Australia, similar to an ostrich but smaller.|The emu is the second-largest living bird by height, after its cousin, the ostrich. 鸸鹋是世界上第二高的现存鸟类，仅次于它的近亲鸵鸟。

The zoo has a breeding pair of emus.  动物园有一对用于繁殖的鸸鹋。|近义词：ostrich, rhea|反义词: N/A (no direct opposite for a specific animal)|词性:noun
