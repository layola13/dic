filigraned:/ˈfɪlɪɡriːnd/
adjective
饰以金银丝细工的; (比喻)精细复杂的
decorated with filigree; (figuratively) delicately intricate and complex
The antique box was filigraned with gold.  古董盒子上饰有金丝。
Her story was filigraned with subtle details and hidden meanings. 她的故事充满了微妙的细节和隐藏的含义。
近义词: ornate, elaborate, intricate, delicate
反义词: plain, simple, unadorned
词性: adjective

词源:
From French *filigrané*, past participle of *filigraner* "to filigree," from Italian *filigrana* "filigree," from *filo* "thread" (from Latin *filum*) + *grana* "grain" (from Latin *granum*).
