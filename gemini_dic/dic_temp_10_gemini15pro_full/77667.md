gelidnesses:/dʒɛlɪdnɪsɪz/
noun
极寒；冰点
Extreme coldness; the state of being gelid.
The explorer faced the gelidnesses of the Arctic winter. 探险家面临着北极冬天的严寒。
近义词: frigidities, icinesses
反义词: warmths, heats
词性: noun (plural)

gelid:/ˈdʒɛlɪd/
adjective
极寒的；冰冷的
Extremely cold; icy.
The gelid wind whipped across the frozen landscape. 刺骨的寒风吹过冰冻的原野。
近义词: frigid, icy, freezing
反义词: warm, hot
词性: adjective
