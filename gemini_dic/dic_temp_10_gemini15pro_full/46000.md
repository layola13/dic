cyberbully:/ˈsaɪbərˌbʊli/| v. |网络欺凌|To use electronic communication to bully a person, typically by sending messages of an intimidating or threatening nature.|She was cyberbullied relentlessly for months. 她连续几个月遭受网络欺凌。|近义词：harass online, intimidate online|反义词:support, encourage|动词
