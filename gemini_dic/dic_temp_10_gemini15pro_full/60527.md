elaeoptene:/ɪˈliːɒptiːn/ | n. |香精油,液态烃|The liquid portion of an essential oil, as distinguished from the stearoptene. |The elaeoptene of rose oil gives it its characteristic fragrance. 玫瑰油中的香精油赋予了它独特的香味。|Synonyms: essential oil fraction, liquid component, terpene|Antonyms: stearoptene|Noun
