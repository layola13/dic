atomising:/ˈætəmaɪzɪŋ/ (UK), /ˈætəˌmaɪzɪŋ/ (US)| v. |雾化；喷雾|Converting a liquid into a fine spray or mist.|The paint was atomised for an even coat. 油漆被雾化以便均匀涂抹。|The perfume atomised into the air. 香水雾化到空气中。|synonyms: spraying, vaporizing, nebulizing|antonyms: condensing, solidifying|verb

atomised:/ˈætəmaɪzd/ (UK), /ˈætəˌmaɪzd/ (US)| adj. |雾化的|Having been converted into a fine spray or mist.|The atomised fuel ignited quickly. 雾化的燃料迅速点燃。|The atomised water cooled the engine. 雾化的水冷却了发动机。|synonyms: sprayed, vaporized, nebulized|antonyms: condensed, solidified|adjective
