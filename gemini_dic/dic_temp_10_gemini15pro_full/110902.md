lipases:/ˈlaɪpeɪsiz/ /ˈlɪpeɪsiz/| n. |脂肪酶|Enzymes that catalyze the hydrolysis of fats (lipids) into glycerol and fatty acids.  They play a crucial role in digestion, fat metabolism, and other biological processes.| Pancreatic lipases are essential for digesting fats in the small intestine. 胰脂肪酶对于在小肠中消化脂肪至关重要。|  Lipases are used in various industrial applications, such as the production of biodiesel. 脂肪酶用于各种工业应用，例如生物柴油的生产。| synonyms: esterases (a broader category) | antonyms:  (no direct antonym, but inhibitors block lipase activity) | noun
