interchain:/ˈɪntərˌtʃeɪn/| adj.|链间的，跨链的|Occurring between or involving two or more chains, especially in a polymer or blockchain context.  Relating to interaction or communication between different blockchains.|Example 1: Interchain communication protocols enable different blockchains to share data and value. 不同区块链之间的跨链通信协议使它们能够共享数据和价值。

Example 2:  The interchain operability is crucial for the future of decentralized finance. 跨链互操作性对于去中心化金融的未来至关重要。|近义词：cross-chain, interoperable|反义词:intrachain|词性:形容词
