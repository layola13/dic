dictatorships:/dɪkˈteɪtərʃɪps/ | n. | 独裁统治，专政 | A form of government in which absolute power is concentrated in the hands of a dictator or a small group, and individual rights are suppressed. |  The country suffered for decades under a brutal dictatorship.  这个国家在残酷的独裁统治下遭受了数十年之苦。

| Synonyms: tyranny, autocracy, despotism, absolutism | Antonyms: democracy, republic, self-government | Noun
