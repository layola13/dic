buggiest:/ˈbʌɡiɪst/| adj.|最多缺陷的；故障最多的|Having the most bugs or glitches (especially in software or hardware).|This is the buggiest software I've ever used.  这是我用过的故障最多的软件。

The new phone is reportedly the buggiest model they've released. 据报道，这款新手机是他们发布的缺陷最多的型号。|近义词：glitchiest, flakiest|反义词：most reliable, most stable|词性:形容词

词源:
From buggy + -est.  Buggy itself originates from "bug" meaning an error or defect, especially in computer code. This usage likely arose from the idea of insects causing malfunctions in early electromechanical devices.
