nonassonant:/nɒnˈæsənənt/
adjective
不谐音的，非谐音的
lacking assonance; not containing assonance.  Assonance is the repetition of vowel sounds in nearby words.  Nonassonant words, therefore, avoid this repetition.
The poet deliberately used nonassonant words to create a jarring effect. 诗人特意使用不谐音的词语来制造一种不和谐的效果。
The speaker's nonassonant delivery made the poem sound choppy. 演讲者不谐音的表达使得这首诗听起来支离破碎。

近义词:dissonant, inharmonious
反义词:assonant, harmonious
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- (not) + assonant (containing assonance).  "Assonant" ultimately derives from Latin "assonare" (to respond to).
