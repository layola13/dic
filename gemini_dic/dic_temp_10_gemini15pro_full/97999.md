inofficiously:/ˌɪnəˈfɪʃəsli/ | adv. | 非正式地，不礼貌地，违反习俗地 |  In a manner contrary to natural duty or affection, especially towards relatives; unkindly, unobligingly.  Not officially; informally. | He acted inofficiously by excluding his sister from the will. 他把他的妹妹排除在遗嘱之外，这种做法违反了亲情。|  Synonyms: unkindly, uncivilly, disrespectfully, improperly, wrongly | Antonyms: kindly, respectfully, dutifully, properly | word class: adverb
