hemitropy:/hɪˈmɪtrəpi/ |n.|半晶；双晶；偏形；孪晶|The phenomenon of hemitropy is commonly observed in certain crystalline structures.|半晶现象在某些晶体结构中很常见。|The hemitropy of the mineral sample made it difficult to analyze.|矿物样本的孪晶使其难以分析。|Synonym: twinning|Antonym: single crystal|noun


hemihedry:/ˌhɛmiˈhiːdri/ |n.|半面象；半面晶族|The crystal exhibited hemihedry, with only half the usual number of faces.|该晶体呈现半面象，只有通常面数的一半。|The hemihedry of the crystal affected its optical properties.|晶体的半面晶族影响了它的光学特性。|Synonym: merohedrism|Antonym: holohedrism|noun
