nontragedies:/ˌnɒnˈtrædʒɪdiz/
noun
非悲剧；非惨剧
Stories that are not tragedies; events or situations that do not result in great suffering or disaster.
Many of the stories he tells are nontragedies, focusing on everyday life. 他讲述的很多故事都不是悲剧，而是关注日常生活。
Synonyms: comedies, light stories, happy endings
Antonyms: tragedies, disasters, catastrophes
词性: 名词
