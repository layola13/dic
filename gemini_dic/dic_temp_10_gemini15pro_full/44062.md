crinite: /ˈkraɪnaɪt/ | adj. |  毛发的，长毛的; 多毛的，（尤指植物）被毛的 | (especially of a plant) covered with long hairs or bristles; hairy. | The crinite leaves of the fern were a vibrant green.  蕨类植物的多毛叶子是鲜艳的绿色。 | hairy, pilose, hirsute | glabrous, smooth | adjective
