overbend:/ˌoʊvərˈbɛnd/| v. n.|过度弯曲，过度弯曲（的状态）|To bend or flex something beyond its normal or intended limit.  It can also refer to the state of being bent excessively.|

例句：
The guitarist overbent the string, causing it to snap.
吉他手过度弯曲琴弦，导致琴弦断裂。

The overbend of the tree branch made it susceptible to breaking.
树枝的过度弯曲使其容易折断。


近义词：overflex, strain, overstrain|
反义词:underbend, straighten|
词性:verb, noun|


overbend:/ˌoʊvərˈbɛnd/| n.|过度弯曲（的状态）|The state of being bent excessively.|

例句：
The overbend in the pipe caused a leak.
管道过度弯曲导致泄漏。

The gymnast's overbend was impressive but risky.
体操运动员的过度弯曲令人印象深刻，但也很冒险。


近义词：overflexion, strain|
反义词:straightness|
词性:noun|
