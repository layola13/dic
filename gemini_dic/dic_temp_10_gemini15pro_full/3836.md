agonizedly:/ˈæɡənaɪzdli/| adv.|痛苦地，极度痛苦地|In a way that shows or involves great mental or physical suffering.|She looked at him agonizedly, tears streaming down her face. 她痛苦地看着他，泪水顺着她的脸流了下来。|He waited agonizedly for news of his son. 他焦急地等待着儿子的消息。|近义词：painfully, excruciatingly, tormentedly|反义词: comfortably, easily|词性:副词
词源: From agonized + -ly. Agonized comes from the Late Latin word "agonizare," meaning "to struggle in contest," from the Greek "agonizesthai," meaning "to contend for a prize."
