bachelorhood:/ˈbætʃələrhʊd/| n.|单身汉的身份，单身生活|The state of being a bachelor; the time during which a man is a bachelor.|He enjoyed his bachelorhood, but eventually decided to settle down. 他享受单身生活，但最终决定安定下来。|近义词：single life, celibacy|反义词：marriage, matrimony|词性:名词
Etymology: From Middle English *bachelerhed*, from Old French *bacelerie*, from *baceler* (“bachelor”). Ultimately from Late Latin *baccalarius* (“vassal farmer”).
