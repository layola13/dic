bannition:/bæˈnɪʃən/
n.
放逐，驱逐
the act of officially forcing someone to leave a country or area permanently
例句：His bannition from his homeland caused him great sorrow.  他被驱逐出祖国，这使他非常悲伤。

例句：The bannition of the troublesome prince brought peace to the kingdom.  驱逐麻烦的王子给王国带来了和平。

近义词：exile, expulsion, deportation, ostracism
反义词：repatriation, return, welcome
词性: 名词
