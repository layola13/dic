cowriting:/ˈkoʊˌraɪtɪŋ/
n.
共同写作，合著
The act of writing something together with one or more other people.
We did some cowriting on the project. 我们在这个项目上进行了一些共同写作。
collaboration, joint authorship
sole authorship
noun
