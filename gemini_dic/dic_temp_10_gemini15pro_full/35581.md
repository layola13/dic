coanchor:/ˈkoʊˌæŋkər/| n. v.|共同主持人，共同主播；共同主持，共同主播|A person who shares anchoring duties with another person, typically on a news program. |例句：She coanchored the evening news with a veteran journalist. 她和一位资深记者共同主持了晚间新闻。

例句：They decided to coanchor the show to appeal to a wider audience. 他们决定共同主持这个节目以吸引更广泛的观众。|近义词：co-presenter, co-host|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 solo anchor)|词性:名词, 动词
