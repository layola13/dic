flanneled: /ˈflænəld/ | adj. | 法兰绒的；穿着法兰绒衣服的 | Made of flannel; dressed in flannel. |  The old man was flanneled from head to toe against the cold. 这位老人从头到脚都穿着法兰绒衣服御寒。|  The flanneled lumberjack rested against a tree.  穿着法兰绒的伐木工人靠在一棵树上休息。 | Near synonyms:  woolen, fleecy | Antonyms: cotton, silk | adjective

词源: From flannel + -ed.  "Flannel" itself comes from Welsh "gwlanen," meaning "woolen article."
