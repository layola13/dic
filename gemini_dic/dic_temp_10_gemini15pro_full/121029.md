miscomputes:/ˌmɪskəmˈpjuːts/
verb
错误地计算；算错
Calculates incorrectly.
The computer miscomputed the trajectory and the rocket crashed. 计算机错误地计算了轨道，火箭坠毁了。
The cashier miscomputed the total and overcharged me. 收银员算错了总价，多收了我钱。
Synonym: miscalculate, err
Antonym: calculate correctly
词性: 动词
