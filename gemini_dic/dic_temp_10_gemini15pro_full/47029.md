dasyure:/ˈdæsiˌjʊər/ (US), /ˈdæsiˌjʊə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 袋鼬 | A small, carnivorous marsupial native to Australia and New Guinea, characterized by its pointed snout and sharp teeth. | The dasyure is a nocturnal hunter. 袋鼬是一种夜间活动的猎手。| 近义词：quoll, marsupial, carnivore | 反义词: herbivore | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Modern Latin *Dasyurus*, from Ancient Greek δασύς (dasús, “hairy, shaggy”) + οὐρά (ourá, “tail”).
