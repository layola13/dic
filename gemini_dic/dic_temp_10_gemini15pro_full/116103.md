martyrology:/ˌmɑːrtəˈrɒlədʒi/ /ˌmɑːrtəˈrɑːlədʒi/
n.
殉道者名录；殉道史；殉道录
A list or history of martyrs, especially of those of a particular church or period.
The martyrology of the early church is full of inspiring stories. 早期教会的殉道录充满了鼓舞人心的故事。

The professor is an expert in the martyrology of the Reformation.  这位教授是宗教改革时期殉道史的专家。

近义词:  history, chronicle, record, account
反义词:  None (No direct antonym exists for a specialized term like martyrology)
词性: noun
