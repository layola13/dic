lullingly:/ˈlʌlɪŋli/| adv.|催眠地，使人安静地|in a way that makes someone feel calm and sleepy|The gentle waves lapped lullingly against the shore.  温柔的海浪轻拍着海岸，令人昏昏欲睡。|The soft music played lullingly in the background.  轻柔的音乐在背景中播放，令人心旷神怡。|近义词：soothingly, calmingly, tranquilly|反义词:agitatingly, disturbingly|词性:副词
Etymology: From lull (verb) + -ing (present participle suffix) + -ly (adverb suffix).  Lull comes from Middle Low German and Dutch *lullen "to hum, lull."
