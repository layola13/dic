abiogenesis:/ˌeɪˌbaɪəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
noun
自然发生; 无生源说
The study of abiogenesis attempts to explain how life arose from non-living matter.
对自然发生的研究试图解释生命是如何从无生命物质中产生的。

Abiogenesis is the natural process by which life arises from non-living organic compounds.  
自然发生是由无生命的化合物产生生命的自然过程。

synonym
spontaneous generation, autogenesis
antonym
biogenesis
词性
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From a- (not) + bio- (life) + genesis (origin).  "A-" comes from Greek, "bio-" comes from Greek "bios" (life), and "genesis" comes from Greek "genesis" (origin, creation).

Scientists are exploring different hypotheses about abiogenesis. = 科学家们正在探索关于自然发生的各种不同假设。
