fly-pasts:/ˈflaɪˌpɑːsts/ /ˈflaɪˌpæsts/| n.|低空飞越，掠过飞行|A ceremonial flight by one or more aircraft, typically low over a specific location.|The air show featured impressive fly-pasts by fighter jets.  航展上有战斗机令人印象深刻的低空飞越表演。|例句：The Queen's birthday celebrations included a fly-past by the Red Arrows. 女王的生日庆典包括红箭飞行队的低空飞行表演。

例句：Several fly-pasts were scheduled for the anniversary of the battle.  为纪念这场战役，安排了几次低空飞行。|近义词：flyover, flyby|反义词:|noun
