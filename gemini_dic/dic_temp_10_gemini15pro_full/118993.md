methenyl:/ˈmɛθənɪl/ | n. | 甲叉，次甲基 | A trivalent radical, CH, derived from methane by removal of three hydrogen atoms.  It is also called methylidyne. |  Methenyl group is a reactive intermediate in organic chemistry. 甲叉基是有机化学中一种活泼的中间体。|  Methenyl tetrahydrofolate is an important cofactor in various metabolic pathways.  甲叉四氢叶酸是多种代谢途径中的重要辅因子。 | Synonyms: methylidyne | Antonyms: N/A | noun
