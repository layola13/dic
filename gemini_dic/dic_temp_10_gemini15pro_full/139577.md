obvert:/ɒbˈvɜːt/ /ɑːbˈvɝːt/
verb
反转；变换；(逻辑学)变形
to turn or change; in logic, to transform (a proposition) by negation of both the subject and predicate, the resulting proposition being equivalent in truth value to the original.
The philosopher obverted the proposition to clarify its meaning. 哲学家将命题反转以阐明其含义。
synonyms: transform, convert, change, modify
antonyms: maintain, preserve, retain
词性: 动词(verb)
