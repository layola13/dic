eupeptic:/juːˈpɛptɪk/
adjective
心情愉快的；消化良好的
Having good digestion; cheerful.
She was feeling eupeptic after a good meal and a walk in the sunshine. 她吃了一顿美餐，又在阳光下散步后心情愉快。

Etymology: From Greek *eupeptos* "easily digested," from *eu-* "well" + *peptos* "digested," from *peptō* "I digest."

Synonyms: cheerful, lighthearted, happy, jolly, merry, contented, satisfied, well, healthy
Antonyms: dyspeptic, gloomy, melancholic, depressed, sad, dejected, unwell
