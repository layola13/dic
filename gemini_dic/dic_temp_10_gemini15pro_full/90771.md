humorize:/ˈhjuːməraɪz/ | v. | 迎合；迁就；顺应 | to comply with the whims of (someone) especially in order to keep the person happy |  He humored his mother by agreeing to wear the ridiculous hat. 他为了让母亲高兴，同意戴上那顶滑稽的帽子。

She humored her child by pretending to believe his fantastical stories. 她假装相信孩子那些天马行空的故事来迁就他。

| 近义词：indulge, pander to, cater to | 反义词：disagree with, oppose, contradict | 词性: 动词
