officiousness:/əˈfɪʃəsnəs/
|n.|多管闲事；爱指手画脚|Excessive eagerness in offering unwanted services or advice; meddlesome behavior.|Her officiousness annoyed everyone, as she constantly offered unsolicited advice.|近义词:intrusiveness, meddlesomeness, interference|反义词:reserve, diffidence, aloofness|词性:名词
Etymology: From Late Latin *officiosus* "obliging, dutiful," from Latin *officium* "duty, service." The negative connotation developed later in English.
