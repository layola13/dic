lowbrowism:/ˈloʊbraʊɪzəm/
n.
低俗，庸俗；媚俗

That movie is pure lowbrowism, full of slapstick comedy and silly jokes.  那部电影纯粹是低俗之作，充满了闹剧喜剧和愚蠢的笑话。
lowbrow, vulgarity, kitsch
highbrowism, sophistication, refinement
