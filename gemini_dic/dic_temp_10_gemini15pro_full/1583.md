achromatize: /eɪˈkroʊməˌtaɪz/
verb
使脱色；使消色；使变成无色的
To remove color from; to make achromatic.
The photographer achromatized the image to create a vintage feel. 摄影师将图像脱色以营造复古感。
The chemical process achromatized the dye in the fabric.  化学过程使织物中的染料脱色。
近义词: bleach, decolorize, neutralize
反义词: colorize, dye, pigment
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From a- (not) + chroma (color) + -ize (to make).  Ultimately from Greek roots. 源自 a-（不）+ chroma（颜色）+ -ize（使...）。最终来自希腊词根。
