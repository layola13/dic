micrometre:/ˈmaɪkrəʊˌmiːtər/ (UK) /ˈmaɪkroʊˌmiːtər/ (US)| n. |微米；测微计|A unit of length equal to one millionth of a metre.|The diameter of the red blood cell is about 7 micrometres.|Synonyms: micron, micrometer|Antonyms: kilometer,  metre|noun
micrometres:/ˈmaɪkrəʊˌmiːtəz/ (UK) /ˈmaɪkroʊˌmiːtərz/ (US)| n. |微米们(复数形式)；测微计们(复数形式)|Plural form of micrometre, a unit of length equal to one millionth of a metre.|The tolerances for these components are measured in micrometres.|Synonyms: microns, micrometers|Antonyms: kilometers, metres|noun
