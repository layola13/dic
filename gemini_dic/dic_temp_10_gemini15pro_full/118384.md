merlon:/ˈmɜːrlən/| n. | 城垛；雉堞 | A solid upright section of a battlement, alternating with an embrasure (open space). | The castle walls were topped with merlons, providing cover for archers. 城墙顶部建有城垛，为弓箭手提供掩护。| Synonym: crenel, battlement | Antonym: embrasure | noun
