admirably:/ˈædmərəbli/| adv.|令人钦佩地，极好地，值得赞赏地|In a way that deserves admiration; excellently.|She handled the difficult situation admirably. 她 admirable地处理了这个棘手的局面。|近义词：excellently, superbly, wonderfully, remarkably|反义词：poorly, badly, terribly|词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *admirablement*, from Late Latin *admirabiliter*, from Latin *admirabilis* ("worthy of admiration") + *-iter* (adverbial suffix).

USAGE->
修饰动词:
He played the piano admirably. = 他钢琴弹得令人钦佩。
She spoke admirably at the conference. = 她在会议上发言出色。

修饰形容词:
The house is admirably suited to their needs. = 这房子非常适合他们的需要。
The food was admirably prepared. = 食物做得非常好。

修饰整个句子:
Admirably, she kept her composure throughout the ordeal. = 令人钦佩的是，她在整个考验中都保持了镇定。
Admirably, he refused to give up. = 值得赞赏的是，他拒绝放弃。


PHRASE->
