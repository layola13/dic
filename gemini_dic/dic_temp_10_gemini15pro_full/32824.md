choke:/tʃoʊk/ (US) /tʃəʊk/ (UK)| v. n. |窒息，哽咽；阻塞；（因紧张等）失常，失败；抑制；节流；阻风门 |

*Choke* can refer to the act of obstructing the airway, causing difficulty in breathing, or to a sudden inability to perform due to pressure or nervousness.  It can also be used as a noun to refer to a valve or device that restricts airflow.

例句：
He choked on a piece of bread.  他被一块面包噎住了。

The smoke choked me. 烟把我呛住了。

He choked in the final round of the tournament. 他在锦标赛的最后一轮失常了。

The weeds are choking the flowers in the garden.  杂草阻碍了花园里花朵的生长。

近义词：suffocate, gag, stifle, strangle, constrict, inhibit
反义词:breathe, release, encourage
词性: verb, noun
