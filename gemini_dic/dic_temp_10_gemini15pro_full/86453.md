heftiness:/ˈheftɪnɪs/| n. |沉重；重要性；份量|The heftiness of the book made it difficult to carry. 这本书的沉重使它难以携带。|The heftiness of the evidence against him was overwhelming. 对他不利的证据的重要性是压倒性的。|近义词：weight, bulk, massiveness, substantiality|反义词：lightness, slightness|词性:名词

Etymology: From hefty + -ness.  "Hefty" originates from the  late 16th century, perhaps from "heft" (meaning weight, from Middle English "heve," to lift)  + "-y."  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
