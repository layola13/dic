outfable:/ˌaʊtˈfeɪbəl/
verb
胜过讲述；编造比…更精彩的故事
to tell a story that is more interesting or fantastic than (another story)
The bard tried to outfable his rival, spinning a tale of dragons and lost princesses.  吟游诗人试图胜过他的对手，讲述了一个关于龙和失踪公主的故事。
Synonyms: surpass, outdo, excel, top
Antonyms: be inferior to, fall short of, be outdone by
词性: 动词


outfable:/ˌaʊtˈfeɪbəl/
noun
胜过讲述；编造比…更精彩的故事
an act of outfabling
His outfable of the original myth captivated the audience. 他对原始神话的改编迷住了观众。
Synonyms: surpassing, excelling
Antonyms: failing, being outdone
词性: 名词
