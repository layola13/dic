neurocele:/ˈnʊərəˌsiːl/| n. |神经膨出|A protrusion of nervous tissue through a defect in the skull or vertebral column, forming a cyst-like swelling.  It's a type of birth defect.|Example 1: The infant was diagnosed with a neurocele shortly after birth.|例句 1：婴儿出生后不久就被诊断出患有神经膨出。

Example 2:  Surgery was required to repair the neurocele and prevent further complications.|例句 2：需要手术来修复神经膨出并防止进一步的并发症。|近义词：myelocele, encephalocele|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From neuro- + -cele, from Greek *neuron "nerve, tendon, sinew" + kēlē "tumor, hernia, swelling."
