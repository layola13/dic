noncausal:/nɒnˈkɔːzəl/ | adj. | 非因果的 | Not related by cause and effect; not proceeding from a cause. | The relationship between the two variables is noncausal, meaning one doesn't directly influence the other. 这两个变量之间的关系是非因果的，意味着一个不直接影响另一个。|  The study showed a noncausal correlation between ice cream sales and crime rates. 研究显示冰淇淋销售和犯罪率之间存在非因果关系。 |Synonyms:  non-causal, acausal, unrelated | Antonyms: causal, causative | adjective
