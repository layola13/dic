clone:/kloʊn/ (US) /kləʊn/ (UK)| n. v.|克隆，复制；无性繁殖|A cell, group of cells, or organism that is produced asexually from and is genetically identical to a single ancestor.|Scientists have successfully cloned a sheep. 科学家们成功地克隆了一只羊。|近义词：copy, duplicate, replica|反义词:original|noun
clone:/kloʊn/ (US) /kləʊn/ (UK)| v.|克隆，复制；无性繁殖|To make an exact copy of an organism or cell.|Researchers are working to clone human organs for transplant. 研究人员正在努力克隆用于移植的人体器官。|近义词：copy, duplicate, replicate|反义词:originate|verb
