euthanizing:/ˌjuːθəˌnaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 安乐死，对…实施安乐死 | The process of ending the life of an animal or person suffering from an incurable and painful disease in a relatively painless way. |  The vet euthanized the dog because it was suffering from a terminal illness.  兽医对这只狗实施了安乐死，因为它患有不治之症。 | kill, put down | resuscitate, revive | verb

euthanize:/ˈjuːθəˌnaɪz/ | v. | 安乐死，对…实施安乐死 | To kill (a person or animal) painlessly, especially to relieve suffering from an incurable illness. |  The vet had to euthanize the injured horse.  兽医不得不将受伤的马安乐死。 | kill, put down | resuscitate, revive | verb
