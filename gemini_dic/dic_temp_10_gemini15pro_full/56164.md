dodecagons:/doʊˈdekəɡɑːnz/
noun
十二边形
A dodecagon is a polygon with twelve sides and twelve angles.
A regular dodecagon has all sides and angles equal.
例句：The architect incorporated dodecagons into the building's design.  建筑师将十二边形融入建筑设计中。

例句：A dodecagon has twelve vertices. 十二边形有十二个顶点。
synonym:polygon
antonym: (No direct antonym for a specific shape)
