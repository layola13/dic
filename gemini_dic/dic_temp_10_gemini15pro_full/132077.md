noncreativity:/ˌnɒnkriːeɪˈtɪvɪti/
n.|缺乏创造力；无创造性|The state or quality of lacking creativity or originality.|His noncreativity was a major obstacle to his success in the advertising industry. 他缺乏创造力是他广告业成功的绊脚石。|Synonyms: uncreativeness, unimaginativeness, barrenness, sterility|Antonyms: creativity, inventiveness, originality, imagination|noun
