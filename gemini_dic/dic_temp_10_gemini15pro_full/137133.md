nonsubsistences:/ˌnɒnsəbˈsɪstənsɪz/
n.
缺乏，不存在，无法生存的状态
The state of not existing or being able to survive.
Due to the prolonged drought, the region faced nonsubsistences, with crops failing and people struggling to find food. 由于长期干旱，该地区面临生存危机，农作物歉收，人们难以找到食物。
Near synonyms: nonexistence, absence, lack, privation, destitution
Antonyms: existence, presence, abundance, prosperity
