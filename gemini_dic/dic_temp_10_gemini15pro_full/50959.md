designers:/dɪˈzaɪnərz/
noun
设计师们
People who create and develop plans or drawings for how something is to be made, especially clothes, buildings, products, etc.
The designers worked late into the night to finish the project.  设计师们工作到深夜才完成这个项目。
近义词: creators, architects, stylists, planners, developers
反义词: users, consumers
