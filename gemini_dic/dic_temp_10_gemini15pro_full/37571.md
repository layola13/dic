communal:/kəˈmjuːnəl/
adjective
公共的，共有的，社区的
Shared by all members of a community; for or by a group rather than individuals.
Example:  Communal gardens are becoming increasingly popular in urban areas. 城市地区的公共花园越来越受欢迎。

Example:  The students share a communal kitchen. 学生们共用一个公共厨房。

近义词:shared, collective, joint, public
反义词:private, individual, personal
词性:adjective
