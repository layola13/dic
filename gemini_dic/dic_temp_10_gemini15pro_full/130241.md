non-soluble:/ˌnɒnˈsɒljʊbl/ /ˌnɑːnˈsɑːljəbl/| adj.|不溶的，不能溶解的|Not able to be dissolved in a liquid.|Oil is non-soluble in water. 油不溶于水.|insoluble, indissoluble|soluble|adjective
Etymology->
From non- + soluble, from Latin solubilis, from solvere "to loosen, dissolve."
