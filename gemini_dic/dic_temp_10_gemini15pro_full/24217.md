bridgehead:/ˈbrɪdʒˌhed/| n.|桥头堡，据点|A position secured in enemy territory, from which further advances can be made.  A strong position or foothold from which to advance or develop something. |The troops established a bridgehead across the river.  部队在河对岸建立了一个桥头堡。

The company sees its new software as a bridgehead into the lucrative mobile market.  该公司将其新软件视为进军利润丰厚的移动市场的桥头堡。|foothold, stronghold, base|weakness, vulnerability|noun
