parentis:/pəˈrɛntɪs/ | n. | 父母 |  A parent (also called a caregiver) is a biological or social relative who tends to the needs of a child.  |  例句：The school sent a letter to all the parents. 学校给所有家长寄了一封信。 | 近义词：guardian, protector | 反义词: child, offspring | 词性: 名词

parentis (plural parentes):/pəˈrɛntiːz/ | n. | (plural) 祖先; | An ancestor or forefather. | 例句：He could trace his parentes back to the 16th century. 他可以追溯他的祖先到16世纪。 | 近义词：ancestors, forefathers | 反义词: descendants | 词性: 名词


词源: From Latin *parentēs, plural of *parēns (“parent”), present participle of pariō (“I give birth to, beget, produce”).
