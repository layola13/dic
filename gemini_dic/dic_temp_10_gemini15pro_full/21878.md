bodyboard:/ˈbɒdiˌbɔːrd/ (UK), /ˈbɑːdiˌbɔːrd/ (US)| n.|趴板，俯式冲浪板|A rectangular piece of buoyant material, typically of polystyrene or polyurethane, used for riding waves in a prone position.|He caught a great wave on his bodyboard. 他用趴板冲了个很棒的浪。|近义词：boogie board, surf mat|反义词: (None - no direct opposite)|名词
