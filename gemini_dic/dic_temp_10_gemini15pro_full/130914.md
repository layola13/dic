nonascendencies:/ˌnɒnəˈsɛndənsiz/| n.| 非优势；非支配地位；没有优势| Absence of ascendency; lack of dominance or controlling influence.  It refers to a state where there is no clear dominant power or influence. | The nonascendencies of any particular group allowed for a more balanced power structure within the organization.  各个群体都没有优势，使得组织内部的权力结构更加平衡。|The treaty aimed to ensure the nonascendencies of any single nation. 该条约旨在确保任何一个国家都不会占据支配地位。| near synonyms: subordination, subjection, inferiority, disadvantage| antonyms: dominance, supremacy, ascendancy, superiority| noun
