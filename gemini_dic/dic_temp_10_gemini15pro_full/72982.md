foolhardy:/ˈfuːlˌhɑːrdi/ /ˈfulˌhɑrdi/| adj.|鲁莽的，蛮干的|Showing reckless daring; rash.|例句：It was foolhardy to try to climb the mountain in such bad weather.  在如此恶劣的天气里尝试爬山是鲁莽的。|例句：His foolhardy actions nearly cost him his life. 他鲁莽的行为几乎让他付出了生命的代价。|近义词：reckless, rash, impetuous, imprudent|反义词：cautious, prudent, sensible|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From fool + hardy.  "Fool" meaning lacking good sense or judgment, and "hardy" originally meaning bold or daring. The combination creates a sense of boldness without wisdom.
