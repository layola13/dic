drysuits: /ˈdraɪsuːts/ | n. | 防水服，干式潜水服 | A garment made of waterproof material that seals tightly around the wrists and neck, allowing the wearer to stay dry in wet conditions, especially when immersed in water.  It is commonly used in activities like diving, kayaking, and sailing. | Example 1: Divers often wear drysuits to stay warm in cold water.  潜水员经常穿干式潜水服以在冷水中保暖。

Example 2:  Kayakers use drysuits to stay dry and comfortable in rough waters. 皮划艇运动员穿干式潜水服是为了在波涛汹涌的水域中保持干燥和舒适。 | 近义词: diving suit, immersion suit, waterproof suit | 反义词: wetsuit | 词性: 名词
