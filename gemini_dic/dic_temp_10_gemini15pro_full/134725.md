nonmeasurabilities:/ˌnɒnˌmɛʒərəˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |不可测性；不可测量性|The quality or state of being impossible to measure.  It often refers to abstract concepts or qualities that defy quantification.|The nonmeasurabilities of human emotions make them difficult to study scientifically. 人类情感的不可测性使其难以进行科学研究。|近义词：immeasurabilities, intangibles|反义词: measurabilities, quantifiables|词性:Noun (plural)
