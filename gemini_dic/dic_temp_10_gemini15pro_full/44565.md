crotonine:/ˈkrəʊtənɪn/ | n. |巴豆碱;巴豆毒蛋白|A highly poisonous alkaloid found in the seeds of Croton tiglium (purging croton).|Crotonine is a potent purgative and can be fatal if ingested in large doses. 巴豆碱是一种强泻剂，如果大量摄入可能是致命的。|Crotonine is extracted from the seeds of the Croton tiglium plant and is used in traditional medicine as a purgative. 巴豆碱是从巴豆植物的种子中提取的，在传统医学中用作泻药。|Synonym: crotin|Antonym: None|Noun
