mistutors:/ˌmɪsˈtjuːtərz/ | n. |  家庭教师（复数）|  Plural form of "mistutor," meaning private teachers who are not suitable or effective for their role.  It implies a negative connotation, suggesting incompetence or a mismatch between the tutor and the student's needs. | The mistutors assigned to my brother did more harm than good.  我的弟弟被分配到的那些不称职的家庭教师弊大于利。

The mistutors charged exorbitant fees but delivered subpar instruction. 这些不称职的家庭教师收费高昂，但教学质量却很差。 | Near synonyms:  bad tutors, incompetent tutors, unqualified tutors, ineffective tutors | Antonyms: good tutors, competent tutors, qualified tutors, effective tutors | noun
