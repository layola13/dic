extirpation: /ˌɛkstərˈpeɪʃən/ | n. | 根除，灭绝，彻底消除 | The extirpation of a species can have devastating effects on the ecosystem.  一个物种的灭绝会对生态系统造成毁灭性的影响。| The surgeon performed an extirpation of the tumor. 外科医生进行了肿瘤切除手术。 | 近义词：eradication, elimination, removal, destruction | 反义词：introduction, establishment, preservation | 词性: noun

词源: From Late Latin extirpationem,  from Latin exstirpare "to root out, pluck up by the roots," from ex- "out" + stirpare "to root up."
