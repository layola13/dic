cittern: /ˈsɪtərn/ (US), /ˈsɪtən/ (UK)| n. |西特琴；一种类似吉他的古乐器|A stringed instrument similar to a guitar, popular in Europe from the Renaissance to the Baroque period. It typically has a pear-shaped body and a flat back.|例句：The musician played a beautiful melody on the cittern.  这位音乐家在西特琴上演奏了一曲优美的旋律。

例句：Citterns were often used in folk music during the Renaissance. 西特琴在文艺复兴时期经常用于民间音乐。|近义词：guitar, lute, mandolin |反义词:  (No direct antonym for a musical instrument)|词性:noun
