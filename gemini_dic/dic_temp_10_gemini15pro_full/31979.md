chemolysis:/kɪˈmɒlɪsɪs/
noun
化学分解，化学溶解
Decomposition or dissolution of organic matter, especially by bacterial or fungal action.
Chemolysis of organic pollutants can be achieved through advanced oxidation processes.  高级氧化工艺可以实现有机污染物的化学分解。
The chemolysis of the rock formation led to the creation of the cave. 岩石的化学溶解导致了洞穴的形成。
decomposition, dissolution, breakdown, disintegration
synthesis, formation
