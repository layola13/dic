hornbook:/ˈhɔːrnˌbʊk/ | n. |识字课本，入门书|A thin board with a handle, covered with a sheet of transparent horn and containing the alphabet, religious texts, etc., used in teaching children to read.|Children in the 17th century learned to read from a hornbook.|近义词：primer, abecedary|反义词: None|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hornbok*, from *horn* (horn) + *bok* (book).  The "horn" refers to the thin, transparent sheet of animal horn that covered and protected the printed page.
