palate:/ˈpælɪt/ | n. | 味觉；腭；口味；爱好 | The roof of the mouth, separating the cavities of the mouth and nose in vertebrates. Also, a person's appreciation of taste and flavor, especially when sophisticated and discriminating. |  The chef created a dish to tantalize the palate.  厨师创作了一道菜来挑逗味蕾。| synonyms: taste, gusto, relish, appetite, inclination | antonyms: distaste, aversion | noun
