hoaxes:/ˈhoʊksiːz/
n.
骗局，恶作剧
a trick or deception
The Piltdown Man was one of the most famous hoaxes in history. 皮尔当人是历史上最著名的骗局之一。

近义词:tricks, scams, frauds, deceptions
反义词:truths, realities, facts
词性:名词(plural)


hoax:/ˈhoʊks/
n.
骗局，恶作剧
something intended to deceive or mislead
The story about the alien invasion was a hoax.  关于外星人入侵的故事是一个骗局。
vt.
欺骗，恶作剧
to deceive or mislead by a hoax
He hoaxed the public with a fake lottery win. 他用假的彩票中奖骗了公众。

近义词:trick, deceive, fool, dupe
反义词:be honest, tell the truth
词性:名词，动词


词源:
From the verb *hocus*, a shortening of *hocus pocus*, a pseudo-Latin term used in magic. 最初来源于动词 *hocus*，是 *hocus pocus* 的缩写，*hocus pocus* 是一个用于魔术的伪拉丁语词汇。
