disoxidate:/diːˈsɒksɪdeɪt/| v. |脱氧，还原|To remove oxygen from a substance; to reduce from an oxide. |例句：The process disoxidates the metal ore.  这个过程使金属矿石脱氧。

例句：The chemist disoxidated the compound in the experiment.  化学家在实验中还原了化合物。|近义词：reduce, deoxidize|反义词: oxidize, oxygenate|词性:动词
