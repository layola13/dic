fightbacks:/ˈfaɪtbæks/| n. |反击，抵抗，反攻|An attempt to regain something lost, such as power, control, or a lead, especially after a setback or defeat.|The team staged a dramatic fightback in the second half, scoring three goals to win the game.  球队在下半场进行了戏剧性的反击，进了三个球赢得了比赛。|近义词：counterattack, resistance, comeback, rally|反义词: surrender, submission|noun
