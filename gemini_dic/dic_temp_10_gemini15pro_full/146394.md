overfluencies: /ˌoʊvərˈfluːənsiz/ | n. |  冗余表达；不流畅 |  Extra sounds, words, or phrases that interrupt the flow of speech, such as "um," "uh," "like," "you know," repetitions, and false starts. |  Example: The speaker's overfluencies, such as frequent "ums" and "ahs," distracted from the message. 演讲者频繁的“嗯”和“啊”等冗余表达分散了信息的注意力。

Example:  Her overfluencies made it difficult to follow her train of thought. 她的不流畅表达使人难以跟上她的思路。 | 近义词:  hesitations, fillers, disfluencies | 反义词: fluency, eloquence | 词性: 名词
