discomposes:/ˌdɪskəmˈpoʊzɪz/ /ˌdɪskəmˈpəʊzɪz/
verb
分解，腐烂
To separate into constituent parts or elements; to rot or decay.
The body decomposes rapidly in hot weather.  尸体在炎热的天气下迅速腐烂。
The fungus decomposes organic matter.  真菌分解有机物。
synonyms: decay, rot, putrefy, break down
antonyms: compose, synthesize, build up
v.
