overlove:/ˌoʊvərˈlʌv/| v.|过分地爱，溺爱|To love excessively or to an unhealthy degree.  It implies a love that is smothering, possessive, or potentially harmful. It can also describe an excessive fondness for something non-human, like a hobby or object.|She overloved her son, never letting him make his own decisions. 她过分地爱她的儿子，从不让他自己做决定。

He overloved his vintage car, spending every weekend polishing it. 他过分喜爱他的老爷车，每个周末都花时间擦拭它。|近义词：dote on, smother, coddle|反义词：neglect, ignore|词性:动词
