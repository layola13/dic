instructed:/ɪnˈstrʌktɪd/
verb
指示，命令，吩咐
past tense and past participle of instruct
The teacher instructed the students to complete the exercise. 老师指示学生完成练习。
He was instructed to report to headquarters immediately. 他接到指示立即向总部报告。
Synonyms: directed, commanded, ordered, told
Antonyms:  misguided, misled
词性: 动词
