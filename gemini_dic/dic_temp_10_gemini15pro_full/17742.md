bedevil:/bɪˈdɛvəl/ | v. |  折磨；使痛苦；困扰 | To trouble or torment persistently; plague. |  Technical problems bedevilled the launch of the new spacecraft.  技术问题困扰着新航天器的发射。| synonyms: torment, plague, harass, beset, afflict | antonyms: aid, assist, help, soothe, comfort | verb

bedevil:/bɪˈdɛvəl/ | v. | 使迷惑；使困惑 | To confuse or bewilder.  | The mystery surrounding his disappearance has bedevilled investigators for years.  围绕着他失踪的谜团多年来一直困扰着调查人员。 | synonyms: confuse, bewilder, perplex, puzzle, confound | antonyms: clarify, enlighten, explain, illuminate | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + devil, meaning "to treat as possessed by a devil."  First recorded in the 16th century.
