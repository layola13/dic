minimally:/ˈmɪnɪməli/| adv.|最小限度地，最低程度地|In a minimal way; to a minimal extent; at a minimum.  Characterized by the smallest possible quantity or degree.|The surgery was minimally invasive.  手术的创伤很小。|近义词：slightly, marginally, barely, hardly|反义词：maximally, greatly, substantially|词性:副词
Etymology: From "minimal" + "-ly". "Minimal" comes from Late Latin "minimalis," from Latin "minimus" (smallest).
