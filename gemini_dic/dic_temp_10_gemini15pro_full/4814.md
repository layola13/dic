algum:/aɫˈɡũ/ (Brazilian Portuguese), /aɫˈɣũ/ (European Portuguese)| determiner, pronoun |一些，某个，任何一个（肯定句），一些，某个，任何一个（否定句、疑问句）| Used in Portuguese to refer to an unspecified or non-specific person or thing.  It can be used in both affirmative, negative, and interrogative sentences, but its meaning changes depending on the context.  In affirmative sentences, it means "some" or "a certain." In negative and interrogative sentences, it means "any." |

例句：
Tenho algum dinheiro. = I have some money.
Não tenho nenhum dinheiro. (Note: 'nenhum' is used in negative sentences) = I don't have any money.
Você tem algum dinheiro? = Do you have any money?

近义词：qualquer (any), certo (a certain), um (one)|
反义词:nenhum (none, no)|
词性:determiner, pronoun|
