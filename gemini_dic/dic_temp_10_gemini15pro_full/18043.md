begot:/bɪˈɡɒt/ (UK) /bɪˈɡɑːt/ (US)| v.| 生育，产生|A past tense of *beget*.  It means to father or produce (offspring).  Primarily used in literary or formal contexts. |例句：Abraham begat Isaac. 亚伯拉罕生了以撒。

例句：He begat many children. 他有很多孩子。

近义词：fathered, sired, engendered, generated, produced|反义词: N/A |词性: 动词
