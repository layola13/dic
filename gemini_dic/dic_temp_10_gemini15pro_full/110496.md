liman:/ˈliːmən/ | n. | 潟湖；河口湾 | A shallow coastal lagoon or embayment formed at the mouth of a river where current flow is weak and sandbars or spits are deposited. | The Dniester Liman is an important wetland area.  德涅斯特河口湾是一个重要的湿地地区。| synonym: lagoon, estuary, bay | antonym: ocean, sea | noun

liman:/ˈliːmən/ | n. | 港湾; 静水区 | A place of shelter; a haven. (Figurative) |  The small town provided a liman from the stresses of city life.  这个小镇提供了一个远离城市生活压力的港湾。 | synonym: haven, refuge, sanctuary | antonym:  exposure, danger | noun
