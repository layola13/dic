cockchafer:/ˈkɒktʃeɪfər/ (UK) /ˈkɑːktʃeɪfər/ (US)| n. |金龟子；五月虫|A large brown European beetle that flies at dusk in late spring. The larvae live in the soil and are destructive to crops.|例句：The cockchafers were buzzing around the trees at dusk. 黄昏时分，金龟子在树周围嗡嗡作响。

例句：The farmer was worried about the cockchafers damaging his crops. 农民担心金龟子会损害他的庄稼。|近义词：beetle, May bug, June bug|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
