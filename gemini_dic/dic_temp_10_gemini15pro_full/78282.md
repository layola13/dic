germen:/ˈdʒɜːrmən/| n.|胚芽，萌芽，起源，根源|A germen is the initial stage of development of something, like a seed or an idea. It refers to the earliest form from which something grows or develops.|The germen of the plant pushed through the soil. 植物的胚芽破土而出。|Synonyms: seed, origin, root, beginning, source, embryo|Antonyms: end, conclusion, death, result, outcome|noun


germen:/ˈdʒɜːrmən/| n.|细菌，病菌 (古语，现已少用)|In older usage, germen could also refer to a germ or bacteria.  This meaning is now less common.|The scientist studied the germen under a microscope. 科学家在显微镜下研究细菌。(古语用法)|Synonyms: germ, bacteria, microbe, pathogen|Antonyms:  sterility, cleanliness|noun
