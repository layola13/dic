emasculate:/ɪˈmæskjʊˌleɪt/ /ɪˈmæskjəˌleɪt/
verb
阉割；使柔弱；删节；削弱
deprive (a man) of his male role or identity.
例句：The surgery emasculated him.  手术使他丧失了男性特征。

例句：Critics claim the new law emasculates freedom of speech. 批评人士声称，这项新法律削弱了言论自由。
近义词：weaken, enfeeble, castrate, geld, neuter
反义词：strengthen, empower
词性 v.


emasculated:/ɪˈmæskjʊˌleɪtɪd/ /ɪˈmæskjəˌleɪtɪd/
adjective
被阉割的；柔弱的；被删节的；被削弱的
deprived of strength or vigor; weakened.
例句：He felt emasculated after losing his job. 失业后，他感到很无力。

例句：The edited version of the film felt emasculated and lacked impact.  这部电影的删减版感觉被削弱了，缺乏冲击力。
近义词：weakened, powerless, impotent
反义词：strong, powerful, potent
词性 adj.
