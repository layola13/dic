donate:/dəʊˈneɪt/ (UK), /ˈdoʊneɪt/ (US)| v. |捐赠，捐献|To give something, especially money, to a person or an organization in order to help them.|He donated $100 to the charity. 他向慈善机构捐赠了100美元。|Synonyms: contribute, give, bestow|Antonyms: receive, take, accept|verb

donate:/dəʊˈneɪt/ (UK), /ˈdoʊneɪt/ (US)| n. |捐赠物|Something that is donated, especially money.|The donations will be used to build a new hospital. 捐款将用于建造一家新医院。|Synonyms: contribution, gift, offering|Antonyms: receipt, taking|noun
