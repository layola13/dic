fulmination:/ˌfʊlmɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 猛烈抨击；严厉谴责；怒吼 |  An expression of vehement protest or denunciation. | The senator's fulmination against the proposed law was heard throughout the chamber.  参议员对拟议法律的猛烈抨击响彻整个会议厅。| synonyms: denunciation, tirade, diatribe, invective, criticism, condemnation | antonyms: praise, commendation, approval, endorsement | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin fulminatio, from Latin fulminare (“to lighten, to strike with lightning”), from fulmen (“lightning”).
