onest:/ˈɒnɪst/ | adj. | 诚实的，正直的 |  Free from deceit and untruthfulness; sincere. |  She is an honest person, always telling the truth. 她是一个诚实的人，总是说实话。 |  truthful, sincere, upright, honorable | dishonest, deceitful, untruthful | adjective


Etymology:
From Middle English *honest*, from Old French *honeste*, from Latin *honestus* ("honorable, respected, respectable"), from *honos* ("honor, reputation, dignity").
