nonintelligently:/ˌnɒnɪnˈtelɪdʒəntli/ | adv. | 不明智地，愚蠢地 |  In a way that is not intelligent; stupidly. | He acted nonintelligently by investing all his money in one stock.  他把所有的钱都投资在一只股票上，这是不明智的行为。| 近义词：stupidly, foolishly, unwisely, thoughtlessly | 反义词：intelligently, wisely, smartly, prudently | 词性：副词

词源：
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," "opposite of," from Latin non "not."
intelligent (adjective): From Latin intelligentem (nominative intelligens), present participle of intelligere "to understand," from inter "between" + legere "choose, gather, read."
-ly (suffix):  Forms adverbs from adjectives, meaning "in a ... manner."
