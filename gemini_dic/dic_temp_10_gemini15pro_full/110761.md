linguacious:/lɪŋˈɡweɪʃəs/
adjective
健谈的; 流利的; 善于表达的
Fluent in or having a good command of language, especially several languages.
She is a linguacious individual, always ready with a witty remark or a clever turn of phrase. 她是一个健谈的人，总是能说出机智的话语或巧妙的措辞。
Synonyms: articulate, eloquent, fluent, voluble, talkative
Antonyms: inarticulate, taciturn, reticent
词性: 形容词
