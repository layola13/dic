doum:/duːm/ | n. | 杜姆棕榈 | An African fan palm (Hyphaene thebaica) with a branching trunk, edible fruit, and leaves used for various purposes. |  The doum palm's fruit is a staple food in some regions of Africa.  杜姆棕榈的果实在非洲一些地区是主食。| synonyms: gingerbread tree, doom palm | antonyms: N/A | noun

doum:/duːm/ | n. | (杜姆棕榈的)果实 | The edible fruit of the doum palm. |  Doum is eaten fresh or dried and can also be made into a sweet drink. 杜姆可以新鲜食用或晒干食用，也可以制成甜饮料。| synonyms:  doum nut, doom nut | antonyms: N/A | noun
