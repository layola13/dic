exaggerations:/ɪɡˌzædʒəˈreɪʃənz/
n.
夸大；夸张；言过其实
statements that represent something as better or worse than it really is.
He told us some amazing stories, full of exaggerations. 他给我们讲了一些神奇的故事，充满了夸张。
overstatements, hyperboles, embellishments
understatements, minimizations
noun
