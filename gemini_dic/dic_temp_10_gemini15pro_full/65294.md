eudaemonia:/ˌjuːdɪˈmoʊniə/ | n. | 幸福；福祉；繁荣 | A state of happiness and flourishing, often associated with living a virtuous life. | 例句：Aristotle believed that eudaemonia was the ultimate goal of human life. 亚里士多德认为幸福是人生的终极目标。
