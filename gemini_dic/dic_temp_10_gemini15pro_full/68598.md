fantasises:/ˈfæntəsaɪzɪz/ (UK) /ˈfæntəˌsaɪzɪz/ (US)
verb
幻想，做白日梦
indulge in fantasies about something, especially something that is unlikely to happen.
He fantasises about winning the lottery. 他幻想中彩票。
fantasize (US), daydream, imagine, dream, muse
- (No direct opposite, but consider "face reality")
词性: 动词
