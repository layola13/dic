believers:/bɪˈliːvərz/| n. |信徒，相信者|People who accept something as true, especially a religious doctrine.|例句：The believers gathered for prayer. 信徒们聚集在一起祈祷。

例句：The believers in the prophecy were disappointed when it didn't come true.  相信预言的信徒们在预言没有成真时感到失望。


|近义词：adherents, followers, disciples|反义词:skeptics, doubters, disbelievers|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bilever*, from Old English *belēafa* (belief) + *-er* (agent suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bilaubijan* (to believe).
