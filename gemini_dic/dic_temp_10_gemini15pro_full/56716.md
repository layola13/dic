doonan:/ˈduːnən/| n.|杜南（姓氏）|A surname of Irish origin, an Anglicized form of the Irish Gaelic Ó Dúnáin meaning "descendant of Dúnán."  Dúnán itself is a diminutive of "dún" meaning fort.|例句：I met a Mr. Doonan at the conference. 我在会议上遇到了一位杜南先生。

例句：Doonan is a fairly common surname in Ireland.  杜南在爱尔兰是一个相当常见的姓氏。|近义词：None (as it's a proper noun)|反义词:None (as it's a proper noun)|词性:Noun
