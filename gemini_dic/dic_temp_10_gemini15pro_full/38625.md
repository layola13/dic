concrimination:/kənˌkrɪmɪˈneɪʃn/
n.
相互指责，互相crimination
the act of accusing or blaming in return; mutual accusation.
The courtroom echoed with concrimination as each defendant blamed the other. 法庭上回荡着相互指责的声音，每个被告都指责对方。
近义词：accusation, recrimination, countercharge, blame
反义词：exoneration, acquittal, vindication
