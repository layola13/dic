disobligatory:/ˌdɪsəˈblɪɡətɔːri/
adjective
不强制的；非义务的；无义务的
Not obligatory; not compulsory.  Relieving from obligation.
It is disobligatory to attend the meeting.  参加会议不是强制性的。
He felt it was disobligatory to respond to the rude email. 他觉得没有义务回复那封粗鲁的电子邮件。
近义词: optional, non-compulsory, non-mandatory, voluntary
反义词: obligatory, compulsory, mandatory, required
词性: adjective

词源:  From dis- (not) + obligatory (obligatory).  The root of "obligatory" is Latin "obligare" (to bind).
