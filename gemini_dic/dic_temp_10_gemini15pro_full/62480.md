endosperm:/ˈɛndəʊspɜːm/ (UK) /ˈɛndoʊspɜːrm/ (US)| n.|胚乳|The nutritive tissue surrounding the embryo within seeds of flowering plants.  It provides nourishment for the developing embryo.|The endosperm of the coconut is the edible white flesh.|Synonym: albumen|Antonym: N/A|noun
