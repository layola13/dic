anaesthetizing:/əˈniːsθəˌtaɪzɪŋ/ /æˈniːsθəˌtaɪzɪŋ/
verb
麻醉；使麻木
Administering an anesthetic to cause loss of sensation or consciousness.
The doctor is anaesthetizing the patient before the surgery. 医生正在手术前麻醉病人。
synonyms:  numbing, desensitizing, benumbing
antonyms:  stimulating, sensitizing, awakening
词性: verb (动词)

anaesthesia:/ˌænəsˈθiːʒə/ /ˌænəsˈθiːziə/
noun
麻醉；麻木
Loss of sensation or consciousness induced by an anesthetic.
The patient was under general anaesthesia during the operation. 病人在手术期间处于全身麻醉状态。
synonyms:  numbness, insensibility, unconsciousness
antonyms:  sensation, consciousness, awareness
词性: noun (名词)

anaesthetic:/ˌænəsˈθetɪk/ /ˌænəsˈθɛtɪk/
adjective
麻醉的；麻木的
Relating to or producing anesthesia.
The anaesthetic drug took effect quickly. 麻醉药很快生效了。
synonyms:  numbing, soporific, narcotic
antonyms:  stimulating, invigorating, awakening
词性: adjective (形容词)


词源:  From Greek "anaisthesia,"  meaning "lack of sensation," from "an-" (without) + "aisthesis" (sensation).
