aus:/aʊs/
n.|out, from, of (German preposition)|外面的，从...出来的，...的（德语介词）|This word is German and means "out of" or "from".  It is often used in compound words.  For example, "Ausgang" means "exit".|这个词是德语，意思是“外面的”或“从...出来的”。它经常用在复合词中。例如，“Ausgang”的意思是“出口”。|近义词:out of, from|反义词:in, into|词性:preposition (in German)
