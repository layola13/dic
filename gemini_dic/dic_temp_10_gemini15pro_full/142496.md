osmograph: /ˈɒzməɡrɑːf/ (UK) /ˈɑːzmoʊɡræf/ (US)
noun
渗透压记录器；渗透压描记器
An instrument for measuring variations in osmotic pressure.
The osmograph recorded a significant change in osmotic pressure when the solute was added.  渗透压记录器记录到添加溶质后渗透压的显著变化。
The researchers used an osmograph to monitor the cells' response to the changing environment. 研究人员使用渗透压记录器来监测细胞对环境变化的反应。
Synonyms: osmometer
Antonyms: None
词性: n.
