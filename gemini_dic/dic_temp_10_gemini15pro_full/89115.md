hombres:/ˈɔmbreɪs/ (Spanish)| n.|男人，人们（阳性复数）|The Spanish word "hombres" is the plural masculine form of "hombre," meaning "man." It refers to a group of men or can be used in a more general sense to mean "people" when the gender is known to be masculine or when a mixed group is addressed in a masculine form (common in some Spanish dialects). It comes from the Latin word "homo," meaning "man" or "human being."|例句：Los hombres están trabajando en el campo.  The men are working in the field.

例句：Muchos hombres y mujeres asistieron a la fiesta. Many men and women attended the party.|近义词：individuos (individuals), personas (people), varones (males) (more formal)|反义词:mujeres (women)|词性:Noun
