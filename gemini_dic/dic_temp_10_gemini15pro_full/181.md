abaxial:/ˌæbˈæksiəl/
adjective
远轴的;背轴的
Situated on the side away from the axis.
The abaxial surface of a leaf is the underside.  叶子的远轴面是它的下面。

The markings are more prominent on the abaxial surface of the petals. 花瓣远轴表面上的斑纹更加明显。

近义词: dorsal
反义词: adaxial
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *abaxialis, from ab- + axis + -alis.

USAGE->
The abaxial side of the leaf is lighter green. = 叶子的背面颜色较浅。

The stomata are mainly located on the abaxial surface. = 气孔主要位于远轴表面。

PHRASE->
abaxial surface = 远轴表面
abaxial side = 远轴侧
abaxial leaf surface = 叶片远轴面
abaxial epidermis = 远轴表皮
abaxial trichomes = 远轴毛状体
