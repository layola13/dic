epidotic:/ˌɛpɪˈdɑːtɪk/ | adj. | 附生的，后生的；次生的 |  Relating to or occurring after the main formation of a rock or mineral; secondary.  | The epidote crystals were epidocic, forming after the granite had solidified. 附生的绿帘石晶体是在花岗岩凝固后形成的。 | Example 2: The geologist identified the epidote as an epidocic mineral. 地质学家将绿帘石鉴定为一种后生矿物。 | synonyms: secondary, accessory, subsequent | antonyms: primary, original, initial | adjective
