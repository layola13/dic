detente:/deɪˈtɑːnt/ /deɪˈtɒnt/
noun
缓和，缓和关系
A relaxing of tension, especially between nations, as by negotiations or agreements.
Example: The period of détente between the United States and the Soviet Union during the Cold War. 冷战时期美苏之间的缓和时期。

Example:  The two countries entered a period of détente after years of hostility.  两国在多年敌对之后进入了一个缓和时期。

Synonym:  rapprochement, easing, relaxation, thaw
Antonym:  tension, hostility, conflict, escalation
词性: 名词
