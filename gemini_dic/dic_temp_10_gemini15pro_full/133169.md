nonevadable:/ˌnɒnɪˈveɪdəbl/
adjective
不可避免的
Unable to be evaded or avoided.
The consequences of her actions were nonevadable. 她行为的后果是不可避免的。
The meeting was nonevadable, despite his busy schedule. 尽管他日程繁忙，会议还是不可避免的。
近义词:inevitable, unavoidable, inescapable
反义词:avoidable, preventable, escapable
词性:adjective
词源:  "non-" (not) + "evadable" (able to be evaded). "Evadable" comes from "evade" (escape or avoid), which ultimately derives from the Latin "evadere" (to go out, escape).
