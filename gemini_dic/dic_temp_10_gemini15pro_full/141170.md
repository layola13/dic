ooetocoid:/oʊ.iːˈtɒkɔɪd/| adj.|卵胎生的|Relating to or denoting animals that are ovoviviparous, i.e., produce eggs that hatch inside the mother's body.|The ooetocoid lizard gives birth to live young, even though the young initially develop inside eggs within the mother. 卵胎生的蜥蜴产下幼崽，即使幼崽最初是在母体内卵内发育的。|Synonyms: ovoviviparous|Antonyms: oviparous, viviparous|Adjective
