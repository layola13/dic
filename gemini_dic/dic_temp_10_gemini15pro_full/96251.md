indicator:/ˈɪndɪkeɪtər/ (US), /ˈɪndɪkeɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|指示器，指标，标志|Something that shows or suggests the state or level of something.|The economic indicators suggest a recession is coming. 经济指标表明经济衰退即将到来.|synonyms: sign, signal, measure, gauge, barometer|antonyms:  concealer, hider|noun


indicator:/ˈɪndɪkeɪtər/ (US), /ˈɪndɪkeɪtə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|指示的，标示的|Serving as a sign or indication of something.|The flashing light is an indicator that the battery is low. 闪烁的灯指示电池电量低。|synonyms: indicative, suggestive, representative|antonyms: concealing, hiding|adjective
