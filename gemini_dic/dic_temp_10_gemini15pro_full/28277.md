captivators:/ˈkæptɪveɪtərz/
n.
令人着迷的人或物
people or things that captivate
The captivating performance of the dancers held the audience spellbound.  舞者迷人的表演让观众如痴如醉。
Synonyms: charmers, enchanters, fascinators
Antonyms: bores, repellents
noun
