back-bone's:/ˈbækˌboʊnz/
noun
支柱，骨干，脊梁的
This refers to something that is the main support or foundation of something else, often used metaphorically.  It's the possessive form of "backbone," indicating ownership or belonging.
The dedicated teachers are the backbone's strength of this school.  这些敬业的老师是这所学校的中坚力量。
The backbone's principles of democracy are freedom and equality. 民主制度的支柱是自由和平等。
Synonyms: core, foundation, support, spine, pillar
Antonyms: weakness, periphery, auxiliary, accessory
