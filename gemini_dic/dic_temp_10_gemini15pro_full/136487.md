nonrhythms:/ˈnɒnˌrɪðəmz/ (UK), /ˈnɑːnˌrɪðəmz/ (US)| n. |非节奏，无节奏|Absence or lack of rhythm; irregular beats or patterns.|The nonrhythms of his heartbeat concerned the doctor. 他的心跳无规律让医生很担心。|The dancer's nonrhythms made the performance jarring. 舞者的无节奏动作让表演很不协调。|近义词: arrhythmia, irregularity, disorder|反义词: rhythm, regularity, order|词性:名词

Etymology:
"non-" + "rhythm"
"non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "without."
"rhythm" comes from the Greek "rhythmos" meaning "measured motion, time, proportion."
