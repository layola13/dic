lunatic: /ˈluːnətɪk/ (US) /ˈluːnətɪk/ (UK)| n. adj.|疯子，精神错乱的；疯狂的|A person who is mentally ill (not in technical use); wildly foolish or irresponsible.|例句：He drives like a lunatic. 他开车像疯子一样。|近义词：madman, insane person, maniac|反义词：sane person|词性：名词，形容词

lunatics: /ˈluːnətɪks/ (US) /ˈluːnətɪks/ (UK)| n.|疯子们，精神错乱的人们|Plural form of lunatic.|例句：They acted like a bunch of lunatics. 他们表现得像一群疯子。|近义词：madmen, insane people, maniacs|反义词：sane people|词性：名词

词源: From Latin *lunaticus*, from luna (“moon”).  The belief was that changes in the moon caused intermittent insanity.
