myristone:/ˈmɪrɪstoʊn/ | n. | 肉豆蔻酮; 十四烷酮 |A white crystalline ketone, C₁₄H₂₈O, found in nutmeg and used in perfumery and organic synthesis.| Myristone is a component of nutmeg oil. 肉豆蔻酮是肉豆蔻油的一种成分。|  Myristone is used in the manufacture of certain perfumes. 肉豆蔻酮用于制造某些香水。 | synonym: tetradecanone | antonym: N/A | noun
