outschemes:/ˌaʊtˈskiːmz/
verb
用计胜过，智取
To surpass or defeat someone in scheming or plotting; to outmaneuver someone through cleverness or cunning.
He outschemed his rivals and won the contract. 他智胜了对手，赢得了合同。
outsmart, outwit, outmaneuver
be outschemed, be outwitted, be outsmarted
v.
