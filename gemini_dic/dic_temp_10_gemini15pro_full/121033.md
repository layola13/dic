misconceived:/ˌmɪskənˈsiːvd/
adjective
误解的；构想错误的
incorrectly understood or interpreted; based on a wrong idea or understanding
The project was misconceived from the start.  这个项目从一开始就构想错误。
His intentions were completely misconceived. 他的意图被完全误解了。
misunderstood, misinterpreted, erroneous, fallacious
correct, accurate, valid, sound
词性:形容词
