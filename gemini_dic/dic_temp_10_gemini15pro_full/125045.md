n.|覆盖物，护根物|A protective covering of organic or inorganic material spread on the ground around plants.|I need to buy some mulch for the garden. 我需要买些覆盖物用于花园。|近义词：covering, bedding, compost, peat|反义词:|名词
mulch:/ˈmʌltʃ/
vt.|用覆盖物覆盖|To cover (a plant or area of ground) with mulch.|He mulched the flowerbeds with straw. 他用稻草覆盖花坛。|近义词：cover, protect|反义词: uncover, expose|动词
