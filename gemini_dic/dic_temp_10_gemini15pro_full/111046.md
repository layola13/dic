lispy:/ˈlɪspi/| adj.|言语不清的，咬舌的|Characterized by a lisp; speaking with a lisp.|例句：He had a lispy way of speaking. 他说话有点咬舌。|近义词：slurred, inarticulate|反义词: articulate, clear|词性:形容词
Etymology: From lisp + -y.  "Lisp" itself likely comes from Old English *wlispian, of imitative origin.
