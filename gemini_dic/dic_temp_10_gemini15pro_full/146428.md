overformalized:/ˌoʊvərˈfɔːrməlaɪzd/
adjective
过度形式化的
Characterized by excessive adherence to formal rules or procedures, often resulting in a rigid and inflexible system.
The company's overformalized procedures hindered innovation and efficiency.  这家公司过度形式化的程序阻碍了创新和效率。
The application process was overformalized and unnecessarily complex. 申请流程过于形式化，不必要地复杂。
Synonyms: rigid, inflexible, bureaucratic, stuffy
Antonyms: informal, casual, relaxed, flexible
词性: 形容词
