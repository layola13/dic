avenging:/əˈvendʒɪŋ/
adjective
复仇的；报复的
inflicting or seeking to inflict punishment in return for an injury or wrong.
例句：The avenging angel wielded a fiery sword. 复仇天使挥舞着一把燃烧的剑。

例句：He set out on an avenging mission. 他开始了一项复仇任务。

近义词：retaliatory, vindictive, vengeful
反义词：forgiving, merciful
词性：adjective
