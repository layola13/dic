lichenin:/ˈlaɪkənɪn/ | n. | 地衣淀粉 | A complex carbohydrate found in certain lichens, especially *Cetraria islandica* (Iceland moss). It is a form of starch. |  Lichenin is extracted from Iceland moss and used as a food thickener. 地衣淀粉从冰岛苔藓中提取出来，用作食品增稠剂。 |  Reindeer rely on lichens, which contain lichenin, as a food source during winter.  驯鹿在冬季依靠含有地衣淀粉的地衣作为食物来源。 |Synonyms: Iceland moss starch | Antonyms: None | Noun
