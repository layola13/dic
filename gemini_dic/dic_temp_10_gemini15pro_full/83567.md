haemapod:/ˌhiːməˈpɒd/ | n. | 血足动物; (尤指) 蚊子，吸血昆虫 | An animal, especially an insect like a mosquito, that feeds on blood. |  Mosquitoes are haemapods that can transmit diseases. 蚊子是传播疾病的吸血昆虫。 | The tick, engorged with blood, was a clear example of a haemapod.  这只吸饱了血的蜱虫，显然是一个吸血动物。 | synonyms: bloodsucker, sanguivore | antonyms: herbivore | noun
