commensality:/ˌkɒmɛnˈsælɪti/ /ˌkɑːmɛnˈsælɪti/| n. |共餐；共同进餐；共食性|The act or practice of eating together, sharing a meal.|例句：Commensality fosters a sense of community. 共餐能促进社群意识。

例句：The commensality of the Thanksgiving dinner is a cherished tradition. 感恩节晚餐的共餐是一个珍贵的传统。|近义词：companionship, fellowship, conviviality|反义词:isolation, solitude|词性:noun

commensality:| n. |(生物)共生；共栖|A symbiotic relationship between two organisms in which one organism benefits and the other is neither harmed nor benefited.|例句：The bird exhibits commensality with the rhino, eating insects stirred up by the rhino's movement.  这种鸟与犀牛表现出共生关系，吃着犀牛走动时搅起的昆虫。

例句：The bacteria's commensality with the human gut is complex. 细菌与人类肠道的共生关系很复杂。|近义词：symbiosis, mutualism (sometimes)|反义词:parasitism, predation|词性:noun
