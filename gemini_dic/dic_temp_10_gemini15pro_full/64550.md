erraticness:/ɪˈrætɪknəs/| n.|古怪；反复无常；不稳定性|The erraticness of the stock market makes it a risky investment. 股票市场的反复无常使其成为一项高风险投资。|例句：The erraticness of her behavior concerned her friends. 她的行为反复无常，让她的朋友们很担心。

例句：Due to the erraticness of the weather, we had to cancel the picnic. 由于天气变化无常，我们不得不取消野餐。|近义词：unpredictability, instability, inconsistency, capriciousness, volatility|反义词：stability, consistency, predictability, regularity|词性:名词

词源:  From erratic + -ness.  Erratic itself comes from Late Latin erraticus "wandering, irregular," from errare "to wander, stray, err," from Proto-Italic *ersare, from Proto-Indo-European *ers- "to move, wander."
