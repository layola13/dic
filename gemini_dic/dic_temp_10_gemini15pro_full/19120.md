betweenity:/bɪˈtwiːnɪti/
n.
中间性；中间状态；过渡性

He experienced a sense of betweenity, no longer a student but not yet a professional. 他感受到一种中间状态，不再是学生，但还不是专业人士。
intermediacy, transition, limbo
extremity, definiteness
