cappella:/ɑː kəˈpɛlə/ | adv. adj.| 无伴奏合唱的 |  In a style without instrumental accompaniment.  From Italian, literally "in the chapel style".  Originally referring to church music sung without instruments. | The choir sang a beautiful piece a cappella. 合唱团无伴奏地演唱了一首优美的曲子。| The group performed an a cappella version of the popular song.  这个团体表演了这首流行歌曲的无伴奏版本。| 近义词: unaccompanied | 反义词: accompanied | 词性: adverb, adjective
