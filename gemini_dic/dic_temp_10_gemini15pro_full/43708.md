creditrix:/ˈkrɛdɪtrɪks/
noun
女债权人；女性债主
A female creditor.  A woman to whom money is owed.
例句：The creditrix demanded full payment of the debt. 女债权人要求全额偿还债务。

例句：The business owed a significant sum to the creditrix. 这家公司欠了女债权人一大笔钱。

近义词：creditor, lender
反义词：debtor, borrower
词性：n.
