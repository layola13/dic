derailleur:/dɪˈreɪlər/ /dəˈreɪlər/| n.|变速器，拨链器|A derailleur is a mechanism for shifting a bicycle chain between sprockets of different sizes so as to vary the gear ratio.|I need to adjust the derailleur on my bike. 我的自行车上的变速器需要调整。|The derailleur slipped and caused the chain to come off. 变速器打滑导致链条脱落。|近义词：gear mechanism, gear shifter|反义词: None|词性:noun
