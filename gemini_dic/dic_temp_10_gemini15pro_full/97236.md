inflectional:/ɪnˈflɛkʃənəl/ | adj. | 屈折的，词形变化的 | Relating to inflection; undergoing inflection; marked by inflection. |  The inflectional ending "-ed" indicates past tense.  词尾“-ed”表示过去时。 |  derivational, grammatical | invariant, unchanging | adjective

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The inflectional ending "-s" marks the third-person singular present tense.  词尾“-s”标志着第三人称单数现在时。
She walks to school every day. 她每天步行上学。


He sings beautifully. 他唱歌很好听。
他唱歌很好听。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
The inflectional ending "-ing" is used to form the present participle. 词尾“-ing”用来构成现在分词。
They are playing football. 他们在踢足球。


She is studying for her exams. 她在为考试学习。
她在为考试学习。


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
The inflectional ending "-ed" (or irregular past participle) is used with "have/has" to form the present perfect. 词尾“-ed”（或不规则过去分词）与“have/has”连用构成现在完成时。
I have finished my homework. 我已经完成了我的家庭作业。


He has gone to the store. 他去商店了。
他去商店了。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
The inflectional ending "-ing" is used with "have/has been" to form the present perfect continuous.  词尾“-ing”与“have/has been”连用构成现在完成进行时。
We have been waiting for an hour. 我们已经等了一个小时了。


She has been working on this project for months.  她已经在这个项目上工作好几个月了。
她已经在这个项目上工作好几个月了。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
The inflectional ending "-ed" (or irregular past tense form) indicates past tense. 词尾“-ed”（或不规则过去式）表示过去时。
I walked to school yesterday. 我昨天步行去上学。


They played basketball last weekend.  他们上周末打篮球了。
他们上周末打篮球了。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
The inflectional ending "-ing" is used with "was/were" to form the past continuous.  词尾“-ing”与“was/were”连用构成过去进行时。
He was reading a book when I called.  我打电话时他在看书。


She was cooking dinner. 她正在做晚饭。
她正在做晚饭。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
The inflectional ending "-ed" (or irregular past participle) is used with "had" to form the past perfect.  词尾“-ed”（或不规则过去分词）与“had”连用构成过去完成时。
I had finished my work before he arrived. 在他到达之前我已经完成了我的工作。


They had already eaten. 他们已经吃过了。
他们已经吃过了。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
The inflectional ending "-ing" is used with "had been" to form the past perfect continuous.  词尾“-ing”与“had been”连用构成过去完成进行时。
She had been studying for hours before the exam. 考试前她已经学习了好几个小时。


We had been waiting for the bus for a long time. 我们等公交车等了很久。
我们等公交车等了很久。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Will + base form of the verb. "Will" is not an inflectional ending. “will”+动词原形。“will”不是屈折词尾。
I will go to the party tomorrow. 我明天会去参加聚会。


She will finish the report next week. 她下周会完成报告。
她下周会完成报告。



将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
Will be + present participle (-ing). "Will" and "be" are not inflectional endings. “will be”+现在分词（-ing）。“will”和“be”不是屈折词尾。
I will be traveling next month. 我下个月将会去旅行。


He will be working on the project. 他将会进行这个项目。
他将会进行这个项目。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
Will have + past participle (-ed or irregular). "Will" and "have" are not inflectional endings.  “will have”+过去分词（-ed或不规则）。“will”和“have”不是屈折词尾。
By next year, I will have finished my degree. 到明年，我将完成我的学位。


She will have written her book by then. 到那时，她将已经写完了她的书。
到那时，她将已经写完了她的书。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
Will have been + present participle (-ing). "Will," "have," and "been" are not inflectional endings. “will have been”+现在分词（-ing）。“will”、“have”和“been”不是屈折词尾。
By next year, I will have been living here for five years. 到明年，我将已经在这里住了五年了。


She will have been working on this project for two years. 到那时，她将已经在这个项目上工作了两年。
到那时，她将已经在这个项目上工作了两年。
