contemporariness:/kənˌtɛmpəˈrɛrɪnɪs/
n.
当代性；同时代性
The state or quality of being contemporary; existence at the same time as something else.
The exhibition explored the contemporariness of different artistic movements.  这个展览探讨了不同艺术运动的当代性。
近义词:modernity, currency, presentness
反义词:antiquity, archaism, pastness
词性:noun


contemporary:/kənˈtɛmpərəri/
adj.
当代的；同时代的
Living or occurring at the same time.
He was a contemporary of Shakespeare. 他是莎士比亚的同代人。
近义词:modern, current, present-day
反义词:ancient, archaic, old
词性:adjective


contemporary:/kənˈtɛmpərəri/
n.
同代人；当代人
A person or thing living or existing at the same time as another.
She is a contemporary of mine. 她是我的同龄人。
近义词:peer, compeer
反义词:predecessor, ancestor
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English contemporarie, from Latin contemporarius (“of the same time”), from con- (“together with”) + temporarius (“of time, temporary”), from tempus (“time”).
