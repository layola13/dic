manograph: /ˈmænəɡræf/ | n. |压力计，压力图|An instrument for recording variations of pressure, especially blood pressure.  It also refers to a record produced by such an instrument.|The doctor used a manograph to monitor the patient's blood pressure. 医生用压力计监测病人的血压。|Synonyms: sphygmomanometer, pressure gauge|Antonyms: None|Noun
