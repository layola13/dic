noninterstate:/ˌnɑːnˈɪntərˌsteɪt/ | adj. | 非州际的 | Not relating to or involving a system of highways connecting major cities across a state or country. |  The noninterstate roads were poorly maintained.  非州际公路维护得很差。 | Synonyms: intrastate, local | Antonyms: interstate, national | adjective

noninterstate:/ˌnɑːnˈɪntərˌsteɪt/ | n. | 非州际公路 | A road or highway that is not part of the interstate system. |  We took the noninterstate to avoid the heavy traffic. 我们走了非州际公路以避开拥堵的交通。 | Synonyms: local road, county road | Antonyms: interstate highway, freeway | noun
