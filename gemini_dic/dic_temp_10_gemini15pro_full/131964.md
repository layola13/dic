noncontumaciously: /ˌnɒnkɒntjuːˈmeɪʃəsli/ /ˌnɑːnkɑːntuːˈmeɪʃəsli/
adverb
不轻蔑地，不反抗地，顺从地
In a manner that is not stubbornly disobedient or rebellious; respectfully and obediently.
He noncontumaciously accepted the court's decision. 他顺从地接受了法庭的判决。
The defendant noncontumaciously answered the judge's questions. 被告恭敬地回答了法官的问题。

近义词: obediently, respectfully, submissively, compliantly
反义词: contumaciously, defiantly, rebelliously, disobediently
词性: adverb

词源:
From non- (not) + contumacious (stubbornly disobedient) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Contumacious comes from Latin *contumax, from con- (with) + tumēre (to swell).  The original sense was "swelling with pride or arrogance".
