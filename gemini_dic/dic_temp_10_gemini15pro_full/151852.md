passional:/ˈpæʃənəl/| adj.|充满激情的，热烈的|Characterized by or expressive of strong emotion, especially romantic love or sexual desire.|She wrote him passional letters filled with yearning. 她给他写了充满渴望的热情洋溢的信。|近义词：ardent, fervent, impassioned, passionate|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, cold|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French passional, from late Latin passionalis, from passio(n-) ‘suffering, passion’ (see passion).
