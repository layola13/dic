monothecal:/ˌmɒnəˈθiːkəl/ | adj. | 单鞘的；单一鞘膜的 | Enclosed within a single sheath, especially referring to a nerve or tendon.  | Example: The monothecal nerve was easily identifiable during the surgery. 手术过程中单鞘神经很容易识别。

Example:  The injury involved a monothecal tendon in the wrist.  手腕受伤涉及一根单鞘肌腱。 | Near Synonyms: unisheathing, single-sheathed (less common) | Antonyms:  polysheathing, multi-sheathed (describing structures with multiple sheaths - less common, often context-specific) | 词性: 形容词
