interdependently:/ˌɪntər.dɪˈpen.dənt.li/| adv.|相互依赖地，互相依存地|in a way that shows a mutual dependence between things|例句：The different parts of the ecosystem function interdependently. 生态系统的不同部分相互依赖地发挥作用。|例句：Countries in the global economy are increasingly interdependently linked. 全球经济中的各国联系日益相互依存。|近义词：mutually, reciprocally, symbiotically|反义词：independently, autonomously|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- (prefix meaning "between, among, mutually, reciprocally") + dependent + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Dependent" comes from the Latin word "dependēns," present participle of "dependēre" meaning "to hang down, hang from, depend on."
