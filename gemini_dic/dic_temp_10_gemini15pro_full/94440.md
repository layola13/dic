imploring:/ɪmˈplɔːrɪŋ/| adj.|恳求的，哀求的|Making an earnest or desperate plea; begging.|She gave him an imploring look, hoping he would change his mind. 她恳求地看着他，希望他能改变主意。|近义词：beseeching, pleading, supplicating|反义词：demanding, commanding|adjective

imploring:/ɪmˈplɔːrɪŋ/| v.|恳求，哀求|(present participle of implore) To beg someone earnestly or desperately to do something.|He was imploring her to stay. 他恳求她留下。|近义词：beseeching, pleading, supplicating|反义词：demanding, commanding|verb
