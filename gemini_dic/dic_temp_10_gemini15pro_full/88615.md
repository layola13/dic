hiya:/haɪˈjɑː/ | int. | 你好，嗨 | An informal greeting, similar to "hi" or "hey." | Hiya, what's up? 你好，怎么了？| 近义词: hi, hey, hello | 反义词: goodbye, farewell | 词性:感叹词

词源:  A contraction of "hi" and "you," originating in the mid-20th century.

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Hiya, how are you doing today? 你好，你今天过得怎么样？
Hiya, what's new? 你好，有什么新鲜事吗？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Hiya, what are you working on? 你好，你在忙什么？
Hiya, are you still watching that movie? 你好，你还在看那部电影吗？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Hiya, have you finished your homework yet? 你好，你完成作业了吗？
Hiya, have you seen the new exhibition at the museum? 你好，你看过博物馆的新展览了吗？
