hydrotrope:/ˈhaɪdrəˌtroʊp/| n. |增溶剂，水溶助长剂|A hydrotrope is a compound that increases the solubility in water of another substance, which is normally insoluble or only slightly soluble.| Hydrotropes are used in detergent formulations to increase the solubility of surfactants. 增溶剂用于洗涤剂配方中以增加表面活性剂的溶解度。|Synonyms: solubilizer, cosolvent|Antonyms: precipitant|Noun
