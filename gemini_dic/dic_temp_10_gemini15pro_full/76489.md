galah:/ˈɡælə/ | n. | 凤头鹦鹉；傻瓜，笨蛋 | A noisy and conspicuous Australian cockatoo, often considered foolish or silly. Also used informally to refer to a foolish or eccentric person. |例句：The galahs were screeching in the trees. 凤头鹦鹉在树上尖叫。

例句：He's a bit of a galah sometimes. 他有时有点傻。|近义词：fool, idiot, clown, buffoon |反义词：genius, sage| 词性: 名词

galahs:/ˈɡæləz/ | n. | 凤头鹦鹉（复数）；傻瓜，笨蛋（复数）| Plural form of "galah". |例句：A flock of galahs landed on the field. 一群凤头鹦鹉降落在田野里。
