arbitratrix:/ˌɑːrbɪˈtreɪtrɪks/
noun
女仲裁员，女裁决人
A female arbitrator; a woman who has the power to settle a dispute.
She was appointed arbitratrix in the contract dispute between the two companies. 她被任命为两家公司合同纠纷的仲裁员。
Synonyms: arbitrator, judge, mediator, adjudicator
Antonyms: disputant, litigant
n.
