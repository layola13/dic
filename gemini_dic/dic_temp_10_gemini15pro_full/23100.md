bountihead:/ˈbaʊntihed/| n.|悬赏金，赏格|A sum of money offered as a reward for the capture of a criminal or for the return of a lost or stolen item. |The government placed a bountihead on the notorious bandit's head. 政府对这个臭名昭著的强盗进行了悬赏。|Synonyms: reward, bounty, premium|Antonyms: penalty, fine|noun
