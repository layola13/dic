overlorded:/ˌoʊvərˈlɔːrdɪd/ | v. | 支配；控制；盛气凌人 | To dominate, control, or rule over someone or something in an oppressive or arrogant manner.  It can also mean overburdened or overwhelmed. |  The corporation overlorded the small town, dictating its economic policies.  这家公司控制着小镇，支配着它的经济政策。

The manager overlorded his staff, micromanaging every detail. 经理对他的员工颐指气使，对每一个细节都进行微观管理。 | 近义词: dominated, controlled, tyrannized, oppressed, overburdened | 反义词: served, obeyed, liberated | 词性: 动词

overlord:/ˈoʊvərˌlɔːrd/ | n. | 君主；霸主；最高统治者 | A ruler, especially one who has general authority over others. | The overlord demanded absolute loyalty from his subjects.  这位霸主要求他的臣民绝对忠诚。

The feudal overlord controlled vast tracts of land. 封建领主控制着大片土地。| 近义词: ruler, master, sovereign, lord | 反义词: subject, servant, subordinate | 词性: 名词
