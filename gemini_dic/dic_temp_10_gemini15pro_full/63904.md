epiphanies: /ɪˈpɪfəniz/ | n. |顿悟，突然的理解；灵感闪现 | A sudden, intuitive perception of or insight into the reality or essential meaning of something, often initiated by some simple, homely, or commonplace occurrence or experience. |  She had an epiphany and realized that she was in love with him. 她顿悟了，意识到自己爱上了他。| synonyms:  revelation, realization, insight, inspiration, flash, illumination | antonyms: confusion, misunderstanding, ignorance | noun

epiphanies: /ɪˈpɪfəniz/ | n. | (Epiphany的复数) 主显节（纪念耶稣显现的节日）| Christian feast celebrating the manifestation of the divine nature of Jesus to the Gentiles as represented by the Magi. | Many cultures celebrate epiphanies with special foods and traditions. 许多文化会用特殊的食品和传统来庆祝主显节。 | synonyms:  Theophany, Twelfth Day, Three Kings' Day | antonyms: N/A | noun
