conferee:/ˌkɒnfəˈriː/ (US) /ˌkɑːnfəˈriː/ (UK)
n.
与会者，参加会议的人
a person who takes part in a conference or meeting
The conferees reached an agreement after two days of intense negotiations.  与会者经过两天的紧张谈判达成了协议。
The conferees were given a tour of the facility after the meeting. 会议结束后，与会者参观了该设施。
participant, attendee, delegate
absentee
noun
