pannary:/ˈpænəri/
noun
面包店，食品储藏室
A place where bread and other baked goods are made or sold; a larder.
The village baker's pannary was always filled with the aroma of fresh bread. 村里的面包师的烘焙坊总是弥漫着新鲜面包的香味。
Synonyms: bakery, bakehouse, larder, pantry
Antonyms: None (lack of a direct antonym)
n.


Etymology:
From Late Middle English *panary, from Old French *panarie, from Latin *panarium "bread basket," from panis "bread."
