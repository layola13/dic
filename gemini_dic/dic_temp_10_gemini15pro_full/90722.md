humidnesses:/hjuːˈmɪdnɪsɪz/| n. |湿度；潮湿程度|The plural form of "humidness," referring to multiple instances or levels of humidity.  It is rarely used.  Most speakers would use "humidity levels" or similar phrasing.|The humidnesses of the different rainforests varied significantly. 不同雨林的湿度差异很大。|近义词：humidity levels, moisture levels|反义词：dryness, aridity|词性:Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From humid + -ness.  "Humid" comes from the Latin "humidus" meaning "moist, wet."
