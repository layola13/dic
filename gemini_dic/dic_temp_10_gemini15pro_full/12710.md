assertory:/əˈsɜːrtəri/
adjective
断言的，肯定的
Affirming or declaring positively; asserting.
The lawyer presented assertory evidence to support his client's claim. 律师出示了肯定的证据来支持他客户的主张。
synonyms: affirmative, positive, declarative, assertive
antonyms: questioning, doubtful, hesitant
adjective


assertory:/əˈsɜːrtəri/
noun
断言，主张
A positive statement; an assertion.
The defendant's assertory was that he was nowhere near the scene of the crime.  被告的断言是他根本不在犯罪现场附近。
synonyms: assertion, declaration, affirmation, statement
antonyms: denial, refutation, contradiction
noun
