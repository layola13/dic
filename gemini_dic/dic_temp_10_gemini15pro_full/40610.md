convene:/kənˈviːn/| v. |召集，集合|To come together formally for an official or public purpose; to cause a group of people to come together in this way.|The committee convened at 10 a.m. 委员会上午10点召开会议。|The president convened a meeting of his advisors. 总统召集了他的顾问们开会。|近义词：assemble, gather, summon|反义词: adjourn, disperse, dismiss|词性:动词
