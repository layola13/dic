inpainting:/ɪnˈpeɪntɪŋ/
n.
图像修复，图像补全
The process of reconstructing lost or damaged parts of an image.
The museum used inpainting to restore the damaged fresco. 博物馆用图像修复技术修复了受损的壁画。
digital inpainting, image restoration, image completion
damage, destruction
noun
