palatableness:/ˈpælətəbəlnəs/| n. |可口性，美味，合意|The palatableness of the dish was enhanced by the chef's special sauce. 这道菜的美味被厨师的特殊酱汁提升了。|例句：The palatableness of the new policy to the public remains to be seen. 新政策对公众是否合意还有待观察。|近义词：acceptability, agreeableness, deliciousness, savoriness|反义词:unpalatableness, unpleasantness, distastefulness|词性:名词

palatable:/ˈpælətəbl/| adj. |可口的，美味的，合意的|The food was simple but palatable. 食物很简单但很可口。|例句：He made the bad news more palatable by presenting it with a touch of humor. 他用一点幽默的方式来呈现这个坏消息，使其更容易接受。|近义词：tasty, appetizing, agreeable, acceptable|反义词:unpalatable, distasteful, disagreeable, unacceptable|词性:形容词
