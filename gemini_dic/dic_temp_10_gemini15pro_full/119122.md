metropolitanization:/məˌtrɒpəlɪtənaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /məˌtrɑːpəlɪtənaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
都市化；大都市化
The process by which a region or area becomes increasingly urbanized and takes on the characteristics of a metropolis, such as a high population density, diverse economic activities, and a complex infrastructure.
The rapid metropolitanization of the area has led to a housing shortage.  该地区的快速都市化导致了住房短缺。
