besiege:/bɪˈsiːdʒ/| vt.|围攻，包围；困扰，使应接不暇|The army besieged the city for months.  军队围攻了这座城市数月。|近义词：surround, encircle, beleaguer, harass, overwhelm|反义词:liberate, free|verb
besiege:/bɪˈsiːdʒ/| vt.|包围；困扰|The reporters besieged the actress with questions.  记者们的问题让这位女演员应接不暇。|近义词：bombard, pester|反义词:ignore, neglect|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *asegier (modern French assiéger), from Vulgar Latin *assediare, from Latin assedēre (“to sit down to, besiege”), from ad- (“to, at”) + sedēre (“to sit”).
