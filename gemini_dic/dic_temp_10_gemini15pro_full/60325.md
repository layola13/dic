egohood:/ˈiːɡoʊhʊd/
noun
自我中心区域；以自我为中心的社区
A neighborhood defined by the residents' shared sense of identity and belonging, often associated with a particular lifestyle or social group. It emphasizes the individual's perception and experience of their neighborhood as a reflection of their own identity and values.
The residents of this trendy egohood are fiercely protective of their neighborhood's image. 这个时尚的自我中心区域的居民极力维护着他们社区的形象。

The coffee shop is a central hub in this artist's egohood. 这家咖啡店是这个艺术家自我中心区域的核心枢纽。

近义词:community, neighborhood
反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps) anonymity, isolation
词性:n.
