frustrated:/ˈfrʌstreɪtɪd/| adj.| 沮丧的，挫败的，失意的| Feeling or expressing distress and annoyance, especially because of inability to change or achieve something.| I felt frustrated when I couldn't solve the puzzle. 我无法解开谜题时感到很沮丧。|  I was frustrated by the lack of progress.  缺乏进展让我感到沮丧。| synonyms:  disappointed, discouraged, thwarted, defeated, annoyed, vexed, exasperated | antonyms: satisfied, content, pleased, gratified | adjective
