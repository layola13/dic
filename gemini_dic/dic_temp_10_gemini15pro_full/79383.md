gliddery:/ˈɡlɪdəri/ | adj. | 滑溜的，光滑的 | Smooth and slippery, often causing difficulty in walking or standing. |  The path was gliddery after the rain. 雨后的路很滑。| 近义词：slippery, slick, greasy | 反义词:rough, coarse | 词性:形容词
词源: From glide + -ery.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *glīdaną (to glide).
