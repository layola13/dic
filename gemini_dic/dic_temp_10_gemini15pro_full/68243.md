fairylands:/ˈfeəriˌlændz/
noun
仙境；奇境；梦幻世界
A magical, enchanting place, often imaginary.
She dreamed of living in fairylands. 她梦想着住在仙境里。
The gardens were like something out of a fairyland. 这些花园就像仙境一般。
synonyms:wonderland, dreamland, fantasyland, enchanted land, magic kingdom
antonyms:reality, real world
