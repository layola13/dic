ashed:/æʃt/ | adj. | 灰色的；化为灰烬的；覆盖着灰的 | Reduced to ashes; covered in ashes. | The ashed remains of the building stood as a stark reminder of the fire.  这栋建筑化为灰烬的残骸，成为火灾的鲜明提醒。| 近义词: incinerated, burnt, cremated | 反义词: unburnt, intact | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Old English *æscian, from Proto-Germanic *askōną, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂es- (“to burn”). Cognate with Old Norse askja (“box, ashes”), Old High German asca (“ashes”). The ending "-ed" is a common adjectival suffix meaning "having the quality of" or "characterized by".
