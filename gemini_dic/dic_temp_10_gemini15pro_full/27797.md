cannonaded:/ˌkænəˈneɪdɪd/
verb
炮轰，轰击
subjected to heavy fire from artillery.
The fort was cannonaded for days before it surrendered.  堡垒被炮轰了数日才投降。
The troops cannonaded the enemy positions.  部队炮轰了敌人的阵地。
近义词:bombarded, shelled, attacked
反义词:defended, protected
词性:v.

Etymology: From French *cannonade*, from *canon* "cannon."
