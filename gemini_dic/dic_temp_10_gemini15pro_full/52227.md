diatonic:/ˌdaɪəˈtɒnɪk/ /ˌdaɪəˈtɑːnɪk/ | adj.| 全音阶的，自然音阶的|Relating to or consisting of the notes, intervals, or keys natural to a major or minor scale; not chromatic.|The diatonic scale of C major contains no sharps or flats. C大调的全音阶不包含升号或降号。|近义词：heptatonic|反义词：chromatic|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek διατονικός (diatonikós, “pertaining to tones separated by an interval”), from διά (diá, “through”) + τόνος (tónos, “tone”).
