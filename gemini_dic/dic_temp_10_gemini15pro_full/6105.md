americium:/ˌæməˈrɪsiəm/| n.|镅|A synthetic radioactive metallic element, atomic number 95, belonging to the actinoid series.  It is fissile and used in smoke detectors and neutron sources.|Americium-241 is used in smoke detectors.  镅-241用于烟雾探测器。|

Americium is named after America, analogous to europium. 镅以美洲命名，类似于铕。|近义词: (As a radioactive element, it doesn't have direct synonyms.  Consider "actinide" or "transuranic element" for broader categories.)|反义词: (No antonyms for chemical elements.)|词性:noun
