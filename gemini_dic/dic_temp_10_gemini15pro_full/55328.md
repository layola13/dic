dissuade:/dɪˈsweɪd/ | v. | 劝阻，阻止 | To advise against something; to turn someone away from a course of action by persuasion. |  He tried to dissuade her from leaving. 他试图劝阻她不要离开。 |  The doctor dissuaded him from smoking. 医生劝他戒烟。 |  deter, discourage, advise against | persuade, encourage, urge | verb
