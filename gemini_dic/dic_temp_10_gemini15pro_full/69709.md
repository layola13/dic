fenny:/ˈfɛni/| adj.|沼泽的，有沼泽气味的|Characterized by fens; marshy; boggy; swampy.|The air was heavy and fenny, thick with the smell of decaying vegetation. 空气沉闷，带着沼泽的气味，充满了腐烂植被的味道。|近义词：marshy, boggy, swampy, miry|反义词：dry, arid|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *fenni, equivalent to fen + -y.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *fanją (“mud, mire”).
