cooky:/ˈkʊki/ (US), /ˈkʊki/ (UK)| n. |小甜饼，曲奇饼|A small, flat, usually sweet cake made from flour, sugar, butter, and eggs, and often containing chocolate chips, nuts, or other ingredients.|例句：My grandma bakes delicious chocolate chip cookies. 我的奶奶烤的巧克力曲奇饼很好吃。

例句：I like to dunk my cookies in milk. 我喜欢把我的曲奇饼泡在牛奶里吃。|近义词：biscuit, cookie (UK)|反义词: None|词性:noun
