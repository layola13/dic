intercolonization:/ɪntərˌkɒlənɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |相互殖民，殖民地之间的相互影响|The intercolonization between the British and French colonies in North America led to cultural exchange and conflict. 北美英法殖民地之间的相互殖民导致了文化交流和冲突。|Example: The intercolonization of languages has resulted in a rich tapestry of dialects. 语言的相互殖民导致了丰富多彩的方言。|Synonym: interaction, cross-colonization, interchange |Antonym: isolation, segregation |noun
