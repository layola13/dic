archiver:/ˈɑːrkaɪvər/| n.|存档器；归档程序；档案管理员|A person or thing that archives something.  A computer program that combines multiple files into a single archive file.|The archiver automatically backs up my files every night.|近义词: archivist, custodian, recorder|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


archiver:/ˈɑːrkaɪvər/| n.|存档器；归档程序；档案管理员|A computer program used for creating and managing archive files, often used for compression and backup.|I used an archiver to compress all my photos into a single zip file.|近义词: compressor, backup software|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From archive + -er.  "Archive" ultimately derives from the Greek "arkheion" (ἀρχεῖον), meaning "public building," later "public records."
