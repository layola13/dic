grainy:/ˈɡreɪni/| adj.|颗粒状的，粗糙的|Having a coarse or granular texture, as from composed of small particles or crystals; not smooth or fine.  Resembling grains or consisting of grains.  (Of a photograph or image) appearing to be made up of numerous small dots or specks, as a result of enlargement or poor resolution.|The old photo was grainy and faded. 这张老照片颗粒感很强，而且褪色了。|近义词：coarse, granular, gritty, rough, speckled|反义词：smooth, fine, clear|词性:形容词
词源: From grain + -y.
