bloodwit:/ˈblʌdˌwɪt/| n.|血金，赎罪金|A payment made to the family of a slain person to avoid a blood feud.|The custom of bloodwit was common in many ancient Germanic societies.  古代日耳曼社会普遍存在血金的习俗。|synonyms: wergild, compensation, restitution|antonyms: revenge, retribution|noun
