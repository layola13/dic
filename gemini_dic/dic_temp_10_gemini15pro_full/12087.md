artiodactyl:/ˌɑːrtiəˈdæktɪl/
n.
偶蹄动物
An even-toed ungulate mammal.  Artiodactyls are characterized by having an even number of toes (two or four) on each foot, with the weight borne equally by the third and fourth toes.
Cows, sheep, and deer are all artiodactyls.  牛、羊和鹿都是偶蹄动物。

近义词:even-toed ungulate
反义词:perissodactyl (奇蹄动物)
词性:noun
