overcounts:/ˌoʊvərˈkaʊnts/ | n. | 多计，重复计算 | An act of counting something more than once, resulting in a higher than accurate total. | The cashier overcounted the items and I ended up paying too much. 收银员多算了商品，我最后多付了钱。| overestimates, exaggerates | undercounts, underestimates | noun

overcount:/ˌoʊvərˈkaʊnt/ | v. | 多计，重复计算 | To count something more times than necessary, leading to an inflated total. | The pollsters overcounted the number of supporters, leading to an inaccurate prediction. 民意调查员多算了支持者的数量，导致了不准确的预测。| overestimate, exaggerate | undercount, underestimate | verb
