nondisingenuousness:/ˌnɒndɪsɪnˈdʒɛnjuəsnəs/
|n.|
|真诚，坦率|
|The state or quality of being nondisingenuous; lack of disingenuousness; sincerity; frankness.|
|Her nondisingenuousness was refreshing in a world of political spin.|她在一堆政治辞令中的真诚让人耳目一新。
|sincerity, honesty, candor, forthrightness, openness|
|disingenuousness, insincerity, dishonesty, deceptiveness, guile|
