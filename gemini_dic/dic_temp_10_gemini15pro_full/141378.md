opercle: /ˈɒpɜːk(ə)l/ (UK), /ˈɑːpərkl/ (US) | n. | 鳃盖 | A bony flap that covers and protects the gills of fishes and some amphibians. | The opercle of the fish opened and closed rhythmically. 鱼的鳃盖有节奏地开合。| synonyms: gill cover, branchiostegal membrane | antonyms: None | noun

operculum: /əˈpɜːkjʊləm/ (UK), /oʊˈpɜːrkjʊləm/ (US) | n. | 盖, 덮개; 鳃盖 | A lid or covering, especially a structure like a lid or flap in plants or animals, such as the gill cover of a fish.  This word is more scientific and broader than "opercle," encompassing various lid-like structures.  | The operculum of the snail sealed the entrance to its shell. 蜗牛的盖子封住了壳的入口。| synonyms: lid, cover, opercle | antonyms: None | noun
