imaginers:/ɪˈmædʒɪnərz/
n.
想象者；构想者；幻想家
A person who imagines or conceives of new ideas, especially in a creative or artistic context.
Walt Disney Imagineers are the creative minds behind the Disney theme parks.  华特迪士尼幻想工程师是迪士尼主题公园背后的创意人才。
The imaginers behind the film created a breathtakingly realistic alien world.  这部电影背后的构想者创造了一个惊人的逼真外星世界。

近义词: creators, visionaries, innovators, dreamers
反义词: realists, pragmatists
词性: 名词 (plural)
