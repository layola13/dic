biennial:/baɪˈɛniəl/| adj. n.|两年一次的，两年生植物|Occurring every two years; lasting for two years.|例句：The biennial flower show is a major event in the city.  这座城市的两年一度的花展是一项盛事。

例句：This biennial plant will flower in its second year. 这种两年生植物会在第二年开花。|近义词：two-yearly|反义词：annual, perennial|词性:形容词, 名词

biennial:/baɪˈɛniəl/| n.|两年生植物，两年一次发生的事情|A plant that completes its life cycle in two years; an event occurring every two years.|例句：The Chelsea Flower Show is a popular biennial. 切尔西花展是一个很受欢迎的两年一度的活动。

例句：Many gardeners enjoy growing biennials. 许多园丁喜欢种植两年生植物。|近义词：two-yearly event|反义词：annual, perennial|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *biennalis, from Latin bi- "two" + annus "year".
