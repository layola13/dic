fifteener:/ˌfɪfˈtiːnər/ | n. | 十五行诗; 十五人组;  | A poem of fifteen lines, usually iambic pentameter, with a rhyme scheme. Also, a team or group of fifteen people. |  She wrote a beautiful fifteener for the poetry competition. 她为诗歌比赛写了一首美丽的十五行诗。 |  He was selected for the fifteener rugby team. 他被选入了十五人橄榄球隊。| synonym: sonnet (for the poem), team, group | antonym:  (No direct antonym, depends on the context. For a team, maybe "individual") | noun
