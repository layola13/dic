gingka:/ˈɡɪŋkə/| n.|银河 (特指日本动漫《金属对决 战斗陀螺》中的主角)|A protagonist in the Japanese anime series *Metal Fight Beyblade*.|Gingka is a determined Blader. 银河是一位意志坚定的陀螺战士。|He battles with his bey, Pegasus. 他用他的陀螺“天马”进行战斗。|近义词:Blader (陀螺战士, 在这个语境下)|反义词:None (无明确的反义词)|词性:noun

词源: "Gingka" is a Japanese name with no direct English translation.  The kanji used for his name (銀河) literally translates to "Galaxy."  This is reflected in the name of his Beyblade, "Pegasus," which is often depicted with cosmic and galaxy-themed designs.
