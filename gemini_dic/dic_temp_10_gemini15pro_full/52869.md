diminisher:/dɪˈmɪnɪʃər/
|n.|减少者；减损者；贬低者|A person or thing that diminishes something.|The constant criticism was a diminisher of her self-esteem.  不断的批评降低了她的自尊心。|近义词:reducer,  lessener,  detractor|反义词:enhancer, amplifier, increaser|词性:名词

diminish:/dɪˈmɪnɪʃ/
|v.|减少；减小；降低；贬低|Make or become less.|The pain will gradually diminish. 疼痛会逐渐减轻。|近义词:reduce, decrease, lessen|反义词:increase, enhance, enlarge|词性:动词
