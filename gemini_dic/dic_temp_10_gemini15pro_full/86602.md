heliometer:/ˌhiːliˈɒmɪtər/ | n. |日冕仪，太阳测量仪 | An instrument for measuring the diameter of the sun, or, more commonly, the angular distance between any two celestial bodies. |例句：The astronomer used a heliometer to measure the sun's diameter. 天文学家用日冕仪测量太阳的直径。

例句：A heliometer was crucial for determining the slight variations in the moon's diameter.  日冕仪对于确定月球直径的微小变化至关重要。|近义词：astronomical instrument, astrolabe |反义词: None |词性: noun
