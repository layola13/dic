emerald:/ˈem(ə)rəld/| n. adj.|祖母绿；翠绿色|A precious stone consisting of a deep green variety of beryl.|She wore an emerald necklace. 她戴着一条祖母绿项链。|近义词：green, jade|反义词: ruby, diamond|词性:名词, 形容词
Etymology: From Old French *esmeraude, from Latin smaragdus, from Greek σμάραγδος (smáragdos). Ultimately from Semitic, related to Akkadian barraqtu (“emerald”).
