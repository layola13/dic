nonett:/nəʊˈnɛt/| n.|九重奏，九重唱|A piece of music written for nine instruments or voices.|The nonett was beautifully performed by the talented musicians.|近义词:ensemble|反义词:(No direct antonym, possibly solo, duet, trio etc. depending on context)|词性:noun

词源: From Italian *nonetto*, diminutive of *nono* "ninth," from Latin *nonus*.
