nonproteins:/ˌnɒnˈproʊtiːnz/ |n.|非蛋白质|Substances that are not proteins, typically referring to organic molecules other than proteins found in living organisms or food. |Dietary fiber and carbohydrates are examples of nonproteins. 膳食纤维和碳水化合物是非蛋白质的例子。|synonyms: non-protein compounds, nonproteinaceous material| antonyms: proteins|noun
