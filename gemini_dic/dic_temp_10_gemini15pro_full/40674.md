conversantly:/kənˈvɜːrsəntli/ | adv. | 流利地，健谈地 | In a manner that shows familiarity with a topic and ease of speaking about it. | She spoke conversantly about the new technology. 她流利地谈论着这项新技术。 | Example: He discussed the topic conversantly, demonstrating his deep understanding. 他流利地讨论了这个话题，展现了他深刻的理解。 | Synonyms: fluently, eloquently, smoothly, easily, readily, freely | Antonyms: haltingly, awkwardly, hesitantly | word type: adverb

Etymology:
From Late Latin conversant-, conversans, present participle of conversari "to associate with," frequentative of convertere "to turn about, be engaged in," from com- "together" + vertere "to turn."
