haggises:/ˈhæɡɪsɪz/| n. |羊杂碎；羊肚包饭（haggis的复数）|A Scottish dish consisting of a sheep's or calf's offal mixed with suet, oatmeal, and seasoning and boiled in a bag, traditionally one made from the animal's stomach.|例句：Haggis is a traditional Scottish dish. 羊杂碎是传统的苏格兰菜肴。

Haggis is often served with neeps and tatties. 羊杂碎通常与芜菁和土豆一起食用。|近义词：offal, entrails|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a specific dish)|Noun
