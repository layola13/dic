bikh:/biːk/ | n. | 印度乌头（毒性植物） | A highly poisonous plant native to the Himalayas, used in traditional medicine and as a poison.  | The bikh plant is known for its toxicity. 印度乌头以其毒性而闻名。 |  Aconitum napellus, wolfsbane, monkshood | N/A | noun
Etymology: From Hindi विष (viṣ) meaning "poison".
