artfulness:/ˈɑːrtfʊlnəs/| n.|狡猾，诡诈，巧妙|The artfulness of the scheme impressed even his enemies. 这个计划的巧妙之处甚至让他的敌人都印象深刻。|例句：The artfulness of her disguise allowed her to slip past security unnoticed. 她巧妙的伪装让她在不被注意的情况下通过了安检。 她伪装的巧妙之处让她得以不被察觉地溜过安检。|近义词：cunning, craftiness, guile, slyness, ingenuity|反义词：honesty, sincerity, artlessness, naiveté|词性:名词

Etymology->
From artful + -ness.  Artful itself comes from art (in the sense of skill) + -ful. Ultimately from Latin *artem (skill).
