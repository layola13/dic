nonimmune:/ˌnɒnɪˈmjuːn/ | /ˌnɑːnɪˈmjuːn/ | adj. | 非免疫的，无免疫力的 | Not immune; lacking immunity. |  Example:  The nonimmune population is particularly vulnerable to the disease.  非免疫人群特别容易感染这种疾病。

Example:  Nonimmune individuals should take precautions during flu season.  没有免疫力的人应该在流感季节采取预防措施。 | Synonyms: susceptible, vulnerable, unprotected | Antonyms: immune, resistant, protected | adjective
