compart:/kəmˈpɑːrt/ |n.|隔间，车厢|A separate section or compartment of something.|The train was divided into several compartments.|近义词: compartment, section, chamber|反义词: whole, entirety|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From French compartiment "compartment," from compartir "to divide, share," from com- "together" + partir "to divide."
