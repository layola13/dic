boxiness:/ˈbɑːksinəs/
n.
方正；笨重；箱状
The boxiness of the design makes it look dated.  这个设计的方正感让它看起来过时了。
The boxiness of the car made it difficult to park. 这辆车的笨重使得难以停车。
The room had a certain boxiness to it, with its high ceilings and square shape.  这个房间有一种方形的感觉，有着高高的天花板和方形的形状。
Near Synonyms:  bulkiness, chunkiness, squareness, angularity
Antonyms: sleekness, stream-lined, curvaceousness
Word origin: From box + -iness.
