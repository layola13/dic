ETYMOLOGY->

From Middle English *escarbuncle, from Old French escarbuncle, from Late Latin carbunculus, diminutive of Latin carbo (“coal, ember”).  The gemstone sense arose from the perceived resemblance of the glowing red color of the stone to the redness of the skin infection.
