panther:/ˈpænθər/ (US), /ˈpænθə(r)/ (UK)| n.|豹，黑豹|A large, wild cat, usually black, that lives in hot countries.  Specifically, a leopard (especially a black one) of the species Panthera pardus, or any of various similar large cats such as the cougar or jaguar.|例句：The panther stalked its prey through the jungle.  黑豹在丛林中跟踪猎物。

例句：We saw a black panther at the zoo. 我们在动物园看到了一只黑豹。|近义词：leopard, cougar, jaguar, puma|反义词: (No direct antonym for an animal)|词性:noun
