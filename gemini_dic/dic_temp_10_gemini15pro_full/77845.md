generosity:/ˌdʒenəˈrɒsəti/ | n. |慷慨，大方|The quality of being kind and generous.|He was known for his generosity to the poor. 他因对穷人的慷慨而闻名。|Synonyms: liberality, munificence, bounty|Antonyms: stinginess, meanness, miserliness|noun

generosity:/ˌdʒenəˈrɒsəti/ | n. |宽宏大量|The willingness to forgive someone for something.|She showed great generosity in forgiving him.  她在原谅他时表现出了极大的宽宏大量。|Synonyms: magnanimity, forbearance, tolerance|Antonyms: resentment, vindictiveness, rancor|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *generosité*, from Latin *generōsitās*, from *generōsus* (“noble, generous”), from *genus* (“birth, race, stock, kind”).
