freighters:/ˈfreɪtərz/
n.
货船；货轮
Large ships used for carrying goods, especially across the sea.
The freighters were loaded with containers bound for Asia. 这些货船装满了运往亚洲的集装箱。
近义词:cargo ships, merchant ships
反义词:passenger ships
词性:名词

Etymology:
From freight + -er.  Freight itself comes from Middle Dutch *vrecht, meaning "freight, cargo, profit."
