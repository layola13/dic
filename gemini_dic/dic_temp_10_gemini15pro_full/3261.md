affirmable:/əˈfɜːrməbl/
adjective
可断言的; 可证实的
That the statement is true is affirmable.  这个陈述是真实的，这是可以断言的。
The suspect's alibi was not affirmable. 嫌疑人的不在场证明无法证实。
synonym: verifiable, confirmable, attestable, assertable
antonym:  unverifiable, unconfirmable, deniable, refutable
Etymology: From Late Latin *affirmabilis, from Latin affirmāre (to affirm) + -bilis (able to be).  Ultimately from ad- (to) + firmāre (to strengthen).
