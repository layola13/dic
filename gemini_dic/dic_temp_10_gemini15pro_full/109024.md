lefty: /ˈlɛfti/ | n. adj. |左撇子；左派；左翼分子 | A person who uses their left hand more skillfully than their right, especially for writing; also can refer to someone with left-wing political views.  | Example: He's a lefty, so he sits on the left side of the table when he writes. 他是个左撇子，所以写字时坐在桌子的左边。

Example:  The party's lefty faction pushed for more social programs. 该党的左翼派别推动了更多的社会项目。 | 近义词: southpaw, left-hander | 反义词: righty, right-hander | 词性: 名词，形容词
