bowdlerizations:/ˌboʊdləraɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n.|删节；净化；(对书籍、剧本等的) 删改；(尤指) 删除其中被认为不道德的章节或词语|The bowdlerizations of the play removed all of the suggestive dialogue.  这出戏的删改删除了所有暗示性的对话。|The editor made several bowdlerizations to the text before publication. 编辑在出版前对文本进行了多次删改。|近义词：censorship, expurgation, redaction|反义词:inclusion,  restoration|noun
