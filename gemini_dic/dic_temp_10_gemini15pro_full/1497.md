acetate: /ˈæsɪteɪt/ (US), /ˈæsɪtɪt/ (UK)| n. | 醋酸盐; 醋酸酯; 醋酸纤维素 |A salt or ester of acetic acid.  A fabric or plastic made from cellulose acetate.|

例句：
Ethyl acetate is commonly used as a solvent. = 乙酸乙酯通常用作溶剂。

Cellulose acetate is used in photographic film. = 醋酸纤维素用于照相胶片。


近义词：ethanoate (especially for the ion), salt, ester |
反义词: N/A |
词性: noun |

ETYMOLOGY->
From acetic + -ate (suffix forming nouns denoting salts or esters of acids). Acetic comes from Latin *acēticus, from acētum (“vinegar”).
