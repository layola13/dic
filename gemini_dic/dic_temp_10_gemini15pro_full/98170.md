inscribes:/ɪnˈskraɪbz/ | vt. |铭刻；题写；内切；登记|To write, carve, or engrave (words or symbols) on something, especially as a formal or permanent record. Also, to draw (a figure) within another so that their boundaries touch but do not intersect.|The artist inscribed his name on the bottom of the sculpture. 艺术家把他的名字刻在了雕塑的底部。|inscribe, engrave, etch|erase, obliterate|verb
