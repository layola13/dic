guarantees:/ˌɡærənˈtiːz/
n.|保证，担保，保修单|A formal promise or assurance (typically in writing) that certain conditions will be fulfilled, especially that a product will be repaired or replaced if not of a specified quality. |The store guarantees satisfaction with their products. 这家商店保证顾客对他们的产品满意。|
近义词:assurances, pledges, warranties
反义词:denials, disclaimers
词性:名词(plural of guarantee)

guarantee:/ˌɡærənˈtiː/
n.|保证，担保，保修单|A formal promise or assurance (typically in writing) that certain conditions will be fulfilled, especially that a product will be repaired or replaced if not of a specified quality. |The product comes with a two-year guarantee. 该产品提供两年保修。|
近义词:assurance, pledge, warranty
反义词:denial, disclaimer
词性:名词

guarantee:/ˌɡærənˈtiː/
v.|保证，担保|To undertake to do something or ensure that something will happen. |I guarantee you'll love this restaurant. 我保证你会喜欢这家餐厅。|
近义词:assure, promise, warrant
反义词:deny, disclaim
词性:动词

词源: From Old French "garantir"  (to warrant), from Frankish "*warjan," (to defend, protect), from Proto-Germanic "*warōną" (to heed, beware).
