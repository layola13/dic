lumbago:/lʌmˈbeɪɡoʊ/| n. |腰痛，腰背痛|Pain in the muscles and joints of the lower back.|例句：He suffered from lumbago after lifting heavy boxes. 他抬了重箱子后腰痛。

例句：Gardening often gives me lumbago. 园艺活经常让我腰痛。

近义词：backache, lower back pain|反义词:|词性:noun
