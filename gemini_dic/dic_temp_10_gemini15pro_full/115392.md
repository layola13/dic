manslaughtering:/ˈmænˌslɔːtərɪŋ/
|v.| 屠杀; 过失杀人 |The act of killing a person unlawfully but without malice aforethought. | He was charged with manslaughtering his neighbor in a drunken rage. 他被指控在醉酒狂怒中过失杀害了他的邻居。|
|近义词：killing, slaying|
|反义词：saving, rescuing|
|词性: 动名词 (gerund)|


manslaughter:/ˈmænˌslɔːtər/
|n.| 过失杀人; 屠杀|The crime of killing a human being without malice aforethought, or otherwise in circumstances not amounting to murder.|He was convicted of manslaughter. 他被判过失杀人罪。|
|近义词：homicide, killing|
|反义词：saving, rescuing|
|词性: 名词 (noun)|
