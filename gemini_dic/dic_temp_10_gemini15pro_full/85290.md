harmonizer:/ˈhɑːrmənaɪzər/ /ˈhɑːrməˌnaɪzər/ | n. | 和声器；协调者；调和者 |A device or person that produces harmony, either in music or in other contexts.  A harmonizer in music blends different sounds or pitches to create a pleasing effect. In a broader sense, it can refer to someone who brings different elements together to create a unified whole. | The guitarist used a harmonizer pedal to create a rich, layered sound. 吉他手使用和声器踏板来创造丰富的层次感声音。

The UN acts as a harmonizer in international disputes. 联合国在国际争端中充当协调者的角色。 | 近义词：coordinator, conciliator, mediator, blender, unifier | 反义词：discordant, antagonist | 词性: noun

Etymology: From the verb "harmonize," which comes from the Late Latin "harmonizare,"  derived from the Greek "harmonia" meaning "joint, agreement, concord of sounds."
