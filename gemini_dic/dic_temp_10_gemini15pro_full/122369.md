mizzle: /ˈmɪzəl/ (US), /ˈmɪzl/ (UK) | v. n. | 毛毛雨，下毛毛雨 | To rain in very fine drops; a fine rain; drizzle.  | It began to mizzle as we left the house. 我们离开房子时开始下毛毛雨了。|  The mizzle made the road slippery. 毛毛雨使路面很滑。| 近义词: drizzle, sprinkle, mist | 反义词: downpour, torrent | 词性: 动词, 名词 | 词源: From Middle Low German *müselen, frequentative of Old Saxon misiljan (“to urinate”), from Proto-Germanic *mihsilaz (“urine”).
