overplans:/ˌoʊvərˈplænz/ | v. | 过度计划，过分计划 | to plan excessively or in too much detail |  Example:  He overplans every vacation, leaving no room for spontaneity.  他每次度假都计划过度，没有留下任何自发的空间。

Example:  By overplanning, she ironically created more stress for herself.  具有讽刺意味的是，由于过度计划，她反而给自己制造了更多压力。 | 近义词: overorganize, overschedule | 反义词: underplan, improvise | 词性: 动词
