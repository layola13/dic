fortuitously:/fɔːrˈtuːɪtəsli/
adverb
偶然地，意外地
happening by chance rather than intention
The meeting happened fortuitously. 这次会议是偶然发生的。
Fortuitously, I ran into an old friend at the airport. 我很幸运地在机场碰到了一个老朋友。
近义词:accidentally, luckily, incidentally
反义词:deliberately, intentionally
词性:adverb
