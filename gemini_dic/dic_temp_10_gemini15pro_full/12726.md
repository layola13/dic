assessorships:/əˈsesərʃɪps/
n.
估价员的职位；估价员的职务；估税员的职位
The position or office of an assessor.  The collective term for multiple such positions.
He held several assessorships concurrently in different counties.  他在不同的县同时担任几个估税员的职位。
The conference brought together assessorships from across the state.  这次会议汇集了来自全州的估税员。
Synonyms:  assessors' positions, appraisal posts, valuation offices
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider) unemployment, vacancy
词性: noun
