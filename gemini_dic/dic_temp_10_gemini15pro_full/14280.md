autographing:/ˈɔːtəˌɡræfɪŋ/
verb
签名
the action of writing one's name on something for a fan or admirer
例句：He spent hours autographing copies of his new book. 他花了几个小时为他的新书签名。

例句：Autographing memorabilia is a common practice at fan conventions. 在粉丝见面会上，为纪念品签名是一种常见的做法。

近义词：signing, endorsing
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider: refusing to sign, declining to autograph)
词性: verb (present participle)
