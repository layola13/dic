oxfordshire:/ˈɒksfədʃɪə(r)/, /ˈɑːksfərdʃɪr/ | n. |牛津郡|A county in south central England. Oxford is its county town. |例句：Oxfordshire is known for its beautiful countryside. 牛津郡以其美丽的乡村而闻名。

例句：She lives in a small village in Oxfordshire. 她住在牛津郡的一个小村庄里。|近义词：county, shire |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *Oxnafordscīr (attested as Oxnaforda scīr in the Anglo-Saxon Chronicle, annal for 1010), meaning "shire of the ford of the oxen".
