inexpectable: /ˌɪnɪkˈspɛktəbl/
adjective
无法预料的，意料之外的
not expected; unforeseen.  This word is not commonly used; unexpected is the preferred and more common term.
The inestimable damage caused by the earthquake was inestimable.  这场地震造成的无法估量的损失是无法估量的。（此处例句使用了inestimable，请改为inexpectable的例句）The inexpected storm caused chaos in the city. 这场意料之外的风暴给城市带来了混乱。
近义词: unexpected, unforeseen, unanticipated
反义词: expected, foreseen, anticipated
词性: adjective
