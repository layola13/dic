ever:/ˈevər/| adv.|曾经，永远，究竟|Used in questions and negative sentences to mean "at any time". Also used for emphasis.|Have you ever been to Paris? 你曾经去过巴黎吗？|Synonyms: always, at all times|Antonyms: never|Adverb

Do you ever go to the cinema? = 你曾经去过电影院吗？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
