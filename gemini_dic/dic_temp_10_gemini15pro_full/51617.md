developed: /dɪˈveləpt/ (UK) /dɪˈveləpt/ (US)| adj.|发达的，先进的；成熟的；发育完全的|Having grown or progressed to a specified stage, often one of maturity or sophistication; elaborated or brought to a desired state.|例句：The United States is a developed country. 美国是一个发达国家。|近义词：advanced, sophisticated, mature, grown, progressed|反义词：underdeveloped, primitive, immature|词性:形容词

developed: /dɪˈveləpt/ (UK) /dɪˈveləpt/ (US)| v.|发展，开发；冲洗（照片）；患（病）；逐渐产生；详细阐述|Grow or cause to grow and become more mature, advanced, or elaborate.|例句：She developed her skills through practice. 她通过练习提高了她的技能。|近义词：evolve, progress, advance, grow, expand, elaborate|反义词：stagnate, decline, regress, contract, diminish|词性:动词

词源: From the Old French *desveloper*, from des- + voloper "to wrap".  The original meaning was "to unwrap," hence "to unfold, reveal."
