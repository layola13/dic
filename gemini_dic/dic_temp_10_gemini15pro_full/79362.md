glenny:/ˈɡlɛni/ | n. |峡谷；狭窄的山谷 |A deep, narrow valley, typically one with a river flowing through it. | We hiked through the glenny, admiring the steep cliffs and rushing water. 我们徒步穿越峡谷，欣赏着陡峭的悬崖和奔腾的河水。|  Example: The glenny was filled with wildflowers in the spring. 春天，峡谷里开满了野花。 | synonym: valley, ravine, gorge, canyon | antonym: mountain, peak, hill | noun
