accomplishes: /əˈkʌmplɪʃɪz/
verb
完成，实现
succeeds in doing or finishing something, especially something difficult or demanding.
She accomplished her goal of running a marathon. 她完成了跑马拉松的目标。

The team accomplished the project ahead of schedule.  团队提前完成了项目。

近义词: achieves, attains, fulfills, executes, completes
反义词: fails, neglects


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *acomplesen*, from Old French *accomplir*, from Late Latin *accomplēre* (“to fulfill”), from Latin *ad* (“to”) + *complēre* (“to complete, fill up”).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He accomplishes his tasks efficiently. = 他有效地完成他的任务。
She accomplishes more in a day than most people do in a week. = 她一天完成的工作比大多数人一周完成的还要多。


一般过去时:
They accomplished their mission despite the difficulties. = 尽管困难重重，他们还是完成了任务。
He accomplished everything he set out to do. = 他完成了所有他计划要做的事情。


现在完成时:
She has accomplished a great deal in her career. = 她在事业上取得了很大的成就。
We have accomplished all of our objectives for this quarter. = 我们已经完成了本季度的所有目标。


过去完成时:

She had accomplished her dream of traveling the world. = 她实现了环游世界的梦想。

We will accomplish this project on time and within budget. = 我们将按时并在预算内完成这个项目。


虚拟语气:
If I were to accomplish one thing this year, it would be to learn a new language. = 如果我今年要完成一件事，那就是学习一门新的语言。
I wish I could accomplish everything I set my mind to. = 我希望我能完成我决心要做的一切。


被动语态:
