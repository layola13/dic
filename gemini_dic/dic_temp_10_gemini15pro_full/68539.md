The party was a real fandango, with music and dancing until dawn.  派对真是热闹非凡，音乐和舞蹈一直持续到天亮。| 近义词：flamenco, revelry, celebration, festivity, merrymaking | 反义词：boredom, stillness, quiet | 词性: noun
