exchanging:/ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒɪŋ/
verb
交换，交流
the act of giving something to someone and receiving something else from them
They were exchanging Christmas gifts. 他们在交换圣诞礼物。
synonyms: trading, swapping, bartering
antonyms: keeping, retaining
v.
