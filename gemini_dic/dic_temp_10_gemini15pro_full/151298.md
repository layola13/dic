pariahisms:/pəˈraɪəˌɪzəmz/ | n. |贱民化；被排斥的状态；被排斥的行为|The act of treating someone as a pariah, or the state of being treated as such.  It refers to the practices, behaviors, or attitudes that contribute to the social exclusion and ostracization of individuals or groups.|The politician's inflammatory rhetoric led to the pariahisms of immigrant communities. 这位政客煽动性的言论导致了移民群体被排斥。|Synonyms: ostracism, exclusion, marginalization, alienation, social rejection|Antonyms: inclusion, acceptance, integration, belonging|noun
