bodaciously:/boʊˈdeɪʃəsli/
adv. |大胆地，放肆地，惊人地|Remarkably; extraordinarily; notably.  Showing a disregard for convention or propriety; boldly or brazenly.| She bodaciously wore a bright pink dress to the funeral. 她胆大妄为地穿着亮粉色连衣裙参加葬礼。|Synonyms: audaciously, brazenly, daringly, outrageously | Antonyms: timidly, cautiously, conventionally, discreetly|词性: 副词

bodacious:/boʊˈdeɪʃəs/
adj. |大胆的，放肆的，惊人的|Remarkable; extraordinary; notable. Excellent, admirable, or attractive.|  He made a bodacious claim about his abilities. 他对自己的能力夸下海口。 | Synonyms: excellent, admirable, remarkable, outstanding, audacious, brazen | Antonyms: unremarkable, ordinary, timid, cautious |词性: 形容词
