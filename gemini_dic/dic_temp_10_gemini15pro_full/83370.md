gyronny:/ˈdʒaɪrəni/
adjective
旋转的，环状的
Divided into six or more triangular sections radiating from the center of a shield.  A heraldic term describing a field or charge divided into six or more triangular sections, all meeting at a central point.
The shield was gyronny of eight, or and azure.  盾牌被分成八个旋转的部分，金色和蓝色相间。
compony, checky
n/a
词性: adjective
