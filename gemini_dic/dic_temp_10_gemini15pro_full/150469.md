pantheonic:/ˌpænθiˈɒnɪk/ | adj. | 殿堂级的；众神殿的；伟人祠的 | Relating to or resembling a pantheon, especially in terms of grandeur, influence, or inclusiveness.  |  The museum boasts a pantheonic collection of Renaissance art.  这个博物馆拥有殿堂级的文艺复兴艺术收藏。 |  august, majestic, imposing, monumental |  insignificant, minor, trivial | adjective

pantheon:/ˈpænθiən/ | n. | 万神殿；伟人祠；（某一领域的）名流 |  1. A temple dedicated to all the gods. 2. All the gods of a people or religion collectively. 3. A building in which the illustrious dead of a nation are buried or commemorated. |  1. The Pantheon in Rome is a remarkable feat of architecture. 罗马的万神殿是一项非凡的建筑壮举。 2.  He is considered part of the pantheon of great American writers. 他被认为是美国伟大作家殿堂中的一员。 | temple, shrine, sanctuary, mausoleum | - | noun
