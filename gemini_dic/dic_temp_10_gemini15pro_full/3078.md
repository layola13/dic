aerometric: /ˌɛərəˈmɛtrɪk/
adjective
空气测量的
relating to the measurement of the weight or density of air or other gases.
The aerometric measurements showed a significant increase in pollution levels.  空气测量结果显示污染水平显著升高。
The scientist used an aerometric instrument to study the atmosphere. 科学家使用空气测量仪器来研究大气。
近义词: pneumatic, barometric
反义词: None
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From aero- (combining form meaning "air") + -metric (suffix meaning "pertaining to measurement").  "Aero-" comes from the Greek "aer" (ἀήρ) meaning "air," and "-metric" comes from the Greek "metron" (μέτρον) meaning "measure."
