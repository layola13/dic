gelatination:/dʒɪˌlætɪˈneɪʃən/
n.
胶凝，胶凝化
The process of forming a gel, a semi-solid, jelly-like substance.
The gelatination of the agar solution occurred upon cooling.  琼脂溶液冷却后发生胶凝。
gel formation, solidification, coagulation
melting, liquefaction
noun
