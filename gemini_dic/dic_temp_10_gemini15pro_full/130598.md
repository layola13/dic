nonadaptive:/ˌnɒnəˈdæptɪv/ | adj. | 非适应性的 | Not able to adapt or change to suit different conditions. |  The company's nonadaptive strategy ultimately led to its downfall.  这家公司不适应的策略最终导致了它的垮台。 | Examples: The nonadaptive nature of the software made it difficult to use on different operating systems.  该软件的非适应性使其难以在不同的操作系统上使用。 The dinosaur's nonadaptive traits led to its extinction. 恐龙不适应环境的特性导致了它的灭绝。| Synonyms: inflexible, rigid, unyielding | Antonyms: adaptive, flexible, adjustable | adjective
