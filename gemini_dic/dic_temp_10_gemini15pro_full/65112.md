etheriform:/ˈiːθərɪfɔːrm/
adjective
醚状的，像乙醚的
Resembling ether; having the nature of ether.
The substance had an etheriform quality, light and airy.  这种物质有一种醚状的性质，轻盈而通透。
The clear, etheriform liquid evaporated quickly.  这种透明的、醚状的液体蒸发得很快。
近义词: ethereal, airy
反义词: dense, heavy
词性: adjective

词源:
From ether + -iform.  Ether comes from Late Latin aether, from Ancient Greek αἰθήρ (aithḗr, “upper air”).  The suffix -iform means "having the form of".
