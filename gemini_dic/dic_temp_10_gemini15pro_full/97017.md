infeasibleness:/ˌɪnˌfiːzəˈbɪlnəs/
|n.|不可行性，不可实现性|The infeasibleness of the project became apparent after a thorough analysis.  这个项目在彻底分析后显现出不可行性。|The infeasibleness of his proposal was immediately obvious. 他的提议的不可行性显而易见。|近义词：impracticability, impossibility, unworkability|反义词：feasibility, possibility, practicability|词性: 名词

infeasible:/ɪnˈfiːzəbl/
|adj.|不可行的，办不到的|It's infeasible to complete the project within the given timeframe. 在规定的时间内完成这个项目是不可行的。|Building a bridge across the Atlantic is infeasible with current technology. 用现在的技术建造一座跨越大西洋的桥梁是不可行的。|近义词：impractical, impossible, unworkable|反义词：feasible, possible, practical|词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + feasible (doable, achievable) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality).  Feasible comes from Old French faisable "that can be done," from faire "to do," ultimately from Latin facere "to make, do."
