eddies:/ˈɛdiz/| n. | 漩涡；涡流 | A circular movement of water, counter to a main current, causing a small whirlpool. | The kayaker skillfully navigated the treacherous eddies. 皮划艇运动员巧妙地绕过了危险的漩涡。|
eddies:/ˈɛdiz/| v. |  (水) 旋转，起漩涡；(烟、雾等) 缭绕 | To move in a circular way, like a whirlpool. | Smoke eddied around the campfire. 烟雾在篝火周围缭绕。|


近义词: swirls, whirlpools, vortexes
反义词: currents, streams
词性: noun, verb


词源: From Old Norse *iða, related to Old English ýð "wave."
