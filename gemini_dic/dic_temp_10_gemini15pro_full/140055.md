oesophagi: /ɪˈsɒfədʒaɪ/ /ɪˈsɑːfəɡaɪ/
n.
食管（复数）
The plural form of "oesophagus," referring to the muscular tubes connecting the throat to the stomach.
Endoscopies are often used to examine the oesophagi of patients with swallowing difficulties. 内窥镜经常被用来检查吞咽困难病人的食道。
Barrett's oesophagus is a condition affecting the lining of the oesophagi. 巴雷特食管是一种影响食管内壁的疾病。
esophaguses, gullets
(No commonly used antonym for a body part)
