machinator:/ˈmækɪneɪtər/| n.|策划者，阴谋家， maquinador|A person who schemes or plots, especially to do harm.|The machinators of the coup were arrested. 政变的策划者被逮捕了。|Synonyms: plotter, schemer, conspirator, intriguer|Antonyms:  peacemaker, conciliator|noun

machinator:/ˈmækɪneɪtər/| n.|机械师， maquinista|A person who works with machines, especially one who designs, builds, or repairs them.|He was a skilled machinator, capable of fixing any engine. 他是一位技艺娴熟的机械师，能够修理任何发动机。|Synonyms: mechanic, engineer, technician|Antonyms:  saboteur, wrecker|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *machinator, from Latin machinatus, past participle of machinārī (“to devise, contrive”), from machina (“machine, device, engine, contrivance”).
