aerate:/ˈeəreɪt/ /ˈɛəˌreɪt/| vt.|充气，透气，使…暴露于空气中|To introduce air into a substance, such as soil or a liquid. To expose to the circulation of air.|例句：The gardener aerated the soil before planting the seeds. 园丁在播种前给土壤充气。

The wine was aerated to improve its flavor.  这酒经过了醒酒以提升风味。|近义词：oxygenate, ventilate, air, carbonate|反义词: suffocate, smother|verb

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He aerates the lawn every spring. = 他每年春天都给草坪透气。
She aerates the compost pile regularly. = 她定期给堆肥充气。

They aerated the fish tank yesterday. = 他们昨天给鱼缸充了气。
We aerated the soil before planting the new flowers. = 我们在新花种植前给土壤透气。


现在完成时:
The wine has been aerated for an hour. = 这酒已经醒了一个小时了。
