carburize:/ˈkɑːrbjʊˌraɪz/ /ˈkɑːrbəˌraɪz/| v. |渗碳，使增碳|To introduce carbon into (a metal, especially steel) in order to harden its surface.|The gears were carburized to increase their durability. 齿轮经过渗碳处理以提高其耐用性。|Example: The process is used to carburize low carbon steel.  这个工艺用于给低碳钢渗碳。|Synonyms: carbonize, harden|Antonyms: decarburize, soften|verb

carburizing:/ˈkɑːrbjʊˌraɪzɪŋ/ /ˈkɑːrbəˌraɪzɪŋ/| n. |渗碳，增碳|The process of introducing carbon into a metal to harden its surface.|Carburizing is a common heat treatment for steel. 渗碳是钢的常见热处理方法。|Example: The carburizing process took several hours. 渗碳过程花了几个小时。|Synonyms: carbonization, hardening|Antonyms: decarburization, softening|noun
