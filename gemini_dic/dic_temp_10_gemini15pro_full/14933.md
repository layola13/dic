azotous:/əˈzoʊtəs/ |adj.|亚硝酸的；含氮的|Containing or derived from nitrogen, especially in a lower valence state than nitric acid.  Relating to or denoting nitrous acid or its salts (nitrites). |Sodium nitrite is an azotous compound. 亚硝酸钠是一种亚硝酸盐化合物。| Nitrous, nitrogenous|Nitric|Adjective
