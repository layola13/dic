ashlering: /ˈæʃlərɪŋ/ | n. |琢石；琢石饰面；用琢石砌成的墙 |  The process of building with ashlar, or the ashlar facing itself.  It refers to carefully worked (dressed) masonry stones, typically rectangular cuboids. | The ashlering on the old building was impressive, showcasing the skill of the stonemasons.  这座古老建筑上的琢石令人印象深刻，展现了石匠的技艺。 | Synonyms: stonework, masonry | Antonyms: rubble | Noun
