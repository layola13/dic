bolsheviki:/ˌbɒlʃɪˈviːkiː/ /ˌboʊlʃəˈviːkiː/
noun
布尔什维克（派）
Members of the majority faction of the Russian Social Democratic Workers' Party, which seized power in the October Revolution of 1917.  This term is often used in the plural form, Bolsheviks.
The Bolsheviki, led by Lenin, overthrew the provisional government. 布尔什维克在列宁的领导下推翻了临时政府。
synonyms: communists, revolutionaries, Marxists
antonyms: Mensheviks, Tsarists, capitalists
n.
