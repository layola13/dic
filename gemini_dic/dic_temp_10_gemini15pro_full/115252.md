manipulates:/məˈnɪpjuleɪts/
verb
操纵，控制
controls or influences (something or someone) cleverly, unfairly, or unscrupulously.
He manipulates people for his own advantage. 他为了自己的利益操纵别人。
manipulate, control, influence, exploit, maneuver
leave alone, neglect, assist, support
