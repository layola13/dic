digitated:/ˈdɪdʒɪteɪtɪd/
adjective
指状的，有指的
Resembling a finger or fingers; having finger-like projections.
The leaves of the horse chestnut tree are digitated. 七叶树的叶子是指状的。

digitated:/ˈdɪdʒɪteɪtɪd/
adjective
（动物）有趾的，有指的
(of animals) having digits (fingers, toes, or similar appendages).
The digitated foot of the raccoon allows it to manipulate objects. 浣熊的有趾足使它能够操纵物体。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *digitatus, from digitus (“finger, toe”).

SYNONYMS->
fingered, digitate, branched

ANTONYMS->
webbed, undivided, unbranched
