intelligibilities: /ɪnˌtɛlɪdʒəˈbɪlətiz/
noun
可理解性；清晰度 (plural)
the quality of being intelligible or understandable.
The intelligibilities of the various speakers varied greatly.  各个演讲者的清晰度差异很大。
intelligibility
unintelligibilities
n.

intelligible: /ɪnˈtɛlɪdʒəbl/
adjective
可理解的；清晰的
able to be understood.
His explanation was clear and intelligible. 他的解释清晰易懂。
understandable, clear, comprehensible
unintelligible, obscure, confusing
adj.
