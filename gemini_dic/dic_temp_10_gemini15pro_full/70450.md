figurativenesses:/ˌfɪɡjərətɪvnɪsɪz/| n.| 比喻性，象征性|The quality of using or containing figures of speech, especially metaphors.|His poetry is marked by its figurativenesses, relying heavily on symbolic language. 他的诗歌以其比喻性为特征，大量依赖象征性语言。|synonyms: symbolism, metaphor, imagery, allegory|antonyms: literalness, directness|noun
