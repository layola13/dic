fillets:/ˈfɪlɪts/ /fɪˈleɪz/| n.|鱼片，肉片，（去骨的）净肉|A boneless piece of meat or fish. |例句：We had salmon fillets for dinner. 我们晚餐吃了鲑鱼片。
