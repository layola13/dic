nondestruction:/ˌnɒndɪˈstrʌkʃən/ |n.| 非破坏性，无损检测 |The bridge underwent nondestruction testing to assess its structural integrity.  这座桥进行了无损检测以评估其结构完整性。| 近义词: non-destructive testing, preservation | 反义词: destruction, demolition | 词性: 名词

nondestructive:/ˌnɒndɪˈstrʌktɪv/ |adj.| 非破坏性的，无损的 | Nondestructive testing methods are used to inspect welds.  非破坏性测试方法用于检查焊缝。| 近义词: non-damaging, harmless | 反义词: destructive, damaging | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + destruction.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "destruction" comes from the Latin word "destructio," meaning "a tearing down."
