beo:/beɪoʊ/ | n. | 披肩，披风; 一种粗糙的羊毛布料| A cloak or mantle; a kind of coarse woollen cloth. | He wore a beo made of rough wool to protect himself from the cold. 他穿着一件粗羊毛制成的披肩来御寒。| 近义词: cloak, mantle, shawl | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词) | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Old English *beo, from Proto-Germanic *bewaz, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bʰew- (“to beat, strike”).  The original meaning likely referred to a cloth made by beating or felting wool.
