interdepending:/ˌɪntər.dɪˈpɛndɪŋ/
adjective
相互依存的
(of two or more people or things) dependent on each other.
例句：The two companies have an interdepending relationship.  这两家公司有着相互依存的关系。

例句：The success of the project is interdepending on the efforts of all team members.  项目的成功取决于所有团队成员的努力。

近义词：interrelated, interconnected, interdependent
反义词：independent, autonomous
词性：adjective
