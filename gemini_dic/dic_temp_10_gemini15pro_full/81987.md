gringo: /ˈɡrɪŋɡoʊ/ (US), /ˈɡrɪŋɡəʊ/ (UK) | n. | 外国人（尤指说英语的外国人，有时含贬义）, 老外 | A foreigner, especially an English-speaker, often used disparagingly. | Example:  Some locals called him a gringo, but others were very welcoming. 一些当地人称他为老外，但其他人非常热情。

Example:  She didn't like being called a gringo. 她不喜欢被称为老外。


| 近义词: foreigner, alien, outsider | 反义词: native, local | 词性: noun
