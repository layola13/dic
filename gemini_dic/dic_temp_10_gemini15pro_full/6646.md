anaesthetizations:/ˌænɪsθətɪˈzeɪʃənz/ /əˌnɛsθətɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |麻醉；麻醉状态|The process of inducing a loss of sensation, especially of pain, by administering an anesthetic agent.  Multiple instances of this process. |The patient underwent several anaesthetizations during the course of treatment. 病人在治疗过程中经历了几次麻醉。|Synonyms: numbings, narcoses|Antonyms: awakenings, revivals|noun
