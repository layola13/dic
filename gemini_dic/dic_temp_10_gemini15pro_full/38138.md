comprehensivization:/ˌkɒmprɪˌhensɪvaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 综合化，全面化 | The act or process of making something comprehensive; the state of being comprehensive.  This refers to the process of broadening or deepening one's understanding or knowledge of a subject to encompass all or most of its relevant aspects. | The comprehensivization of the healthcare system has led to improved patient outcomes. 医疗保健系统的综合化带来了更好的患者疗效。| synonyms:  integration, consolidation, unification, systematization | antonyms: fragmentation, division, specialization, reduction | noun
