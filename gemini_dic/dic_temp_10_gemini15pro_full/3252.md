affine:/əˈfaɪn/ | adj. | 仿射的；密切相关的 | Relating to or involving geometric transformations that preserve straight lines and ratios of distances along them.  | Example: Affine geometry deals with properties that are invariant under affine transformations. 仿射几何处理在仿射变换下不变的性质。

Example: The two companies have an affine relationship. 这两家公司关系密切。 | 近义词: related, connected, kindred | 反义词: unrelated, disconnected, disparate | 词性: adjective

affine:/ˈæfaɪn/ | n. | 仿射变换；亲戚；姻亲 | A transformation that preserves collinearity and ratios of distances. Also, a relative, especially by marriage. | Example: An affine transformation maps lines to lines. 仿射变换将线映射到线。

Example: He is an affine of mine through marriage. 他是我的姻亲。 | 近义词: relative, kin, relation | 反义词: stranger, nonrelative | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *affine*, from Latin *affinis* ("bordering on, adjacent to, neighboring; related to, connected with, kin to"), from *ad* ("to, towards") + *finis* ("end, boundary, limit").
