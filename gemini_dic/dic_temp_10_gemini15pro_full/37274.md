commandable:/kəˈmændəbl/ (US), /kəˈmɑːndəbl/ (UK)| adj.|可指挥的，可控制的|Able to be commanded or controlled; capable of inspiring respect or obedience.|The troops were highly trained and commandable.  这些军队训练有素，指挥得当。|例句：The general had a commandable presence. 将军具有令人敬畏的风采。|近义词：controllable, manageable, governable|反义词：uncontrollable, unruly, disobedient|词性:形容词

词源:
From late Middle English *commaundable, from Anglo-Norman comandable, from Old French comander + -able. Equivalent to command + -able.
