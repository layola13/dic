pass-on:/ˈpɑːs ɒn/ /ˈpæs ɑːn/ | phrasal verb |传递，去世，拒绝，略过| To give something to someone else, especially after receiving it yourself; to die; to decline or reject something; to choose not to do something. | My grandmother passed on her love of baking to me. 我的祖母把她对烘焙的热爱传给了我。

The sad news is that Mr. Smith passed on last night.  令人悲伤的消息是史密斯先生昨晚去世了。

I'll pass on the dessert, I'm full. 我不吃甜点了，我饱了。

I think I’ll pass on going to the party tonight. 我想我今晚不去参加派对了。


|近义词：transfer, transmit, convey, bequeath, die, decline, reject, skip|反义词:keep, accept, receive, live|v.
