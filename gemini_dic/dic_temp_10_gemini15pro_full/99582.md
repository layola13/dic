interinhibitive:/ˌɪntərɪnˈhɪbɪtɪv/
|adj.|相互抑制的，互相抑制的|Characterized by or relating to mutual inhibition; inhibiting one another.|The interinhibitive effect of the two drugs meant that neither worked effectively. 这两种药物的相互抑制作用意味着它们都不能有效发挥作用。| The interinhibitive nature of the neurons prevents overstimulation. 这些神经元的相互抑制作用防止了过度刺激。|近义词：mutually inhibitory, reciprocally inhibitory|反义词：synergistic, mutually stimulating|词性: adjective
词源: inter- (prefix meaning "between, among, mutually") + inhibit (verb meaning "to restrain, hinder") + -ive (suffix forming adjectives)
