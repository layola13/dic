hazardously:/ˈhæzərdəsli/ | adv. | 危险地 | In a way that is dangerous or likely to cause harm. | The chemicals were stored hazardously. 这些化学品被危险地储存着。|  The road was hazardously icy. 这条路危险地结冰了。| 近义词：dangerously, precariously, riskily | 反义词：safely, securely | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From hazard + -ous + -ly.  "Hazard" comes from Old French "hasard" (chance, risk), ultimately from Arabic "az-zahr" (the die).
