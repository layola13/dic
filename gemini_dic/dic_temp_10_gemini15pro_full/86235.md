heatings:/ˈhiːtɪŋz/
noun
供暖；加热；暖气
The act of making something warm or hot.  The provision of warmth to a building.
例句：The heatings in this building are very efficient.  这栋楼的供暖系统非常高效。

例句：The cost of heatings has risen sharply this year. 今年供暖费用急剧上升。

近义词：warming, heating systems
反义词：cooling, air conditioning
词性：n. (noun)
