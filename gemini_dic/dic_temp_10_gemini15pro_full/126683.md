namby-pamby:/ˈnæmbi ˈpæmbi/| adj. n.|优柔寡断的，婆婆妈妈的；没有骨气的人|Lacking strength or firmness of character; weak; indecisive. Also, someone who is weak and indecisive.|He's such a namby-pamby, he can never make up his mind.|近义词：wishy-washy, spineless, feeble, effeminate|反义词:strong, decisive, resolute|adjective/noun
