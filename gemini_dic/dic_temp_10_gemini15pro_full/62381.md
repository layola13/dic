endocardiac:/ˌɛndəʊˈkɑːdiæk/ | adj. | 心内膜的 | Relating to the endocardium (the thin, smooth membrane which lines the inside of the chambers of the heart and forms the surface of the valves). |  Endocarditis is an inflammation of the endocardium. 心内膜炎是心内膜的炎症。 |  The endocardiac surface is smooth. 心内膜表面是光滑的。 | 近义词: intracardiac | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps epicardiac - relating to the outer layer of the heart) | 词性: adjective
