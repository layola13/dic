obeisant:/oʊˈbeɪsənt/ /əˈbeɪsənt/ | adj. | 顺从的，恭顺的，敬重的 | Showing respect and obedience; dutiful.  |  The obeisant servant bowed low before the king. 恭顺的仆人在国王面前深深鞠躬。 |  respectful, deferential, submissive, compliant |  disrespectful, defiant, rebellious | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *obeissant*, present participle of obeir "to obey," from Latin oboedire "to obey, be obedient to," from ob- "toward" + audire "to hear."
