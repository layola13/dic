over-arousal:/ˈoʊvər əˈraʊzəl/| n.|过度兴奋，过度唤醒|A state of heightened physiological and emotional activity, often experienced as anxiety, stress, or hyperactivity.  It's a common symptom in conditions like PTSD and ADHD.|The child's over-arousal made it difficult for him to focus in class.  这个孩子过度兴奋，导致他难以集中注意力上课。|Synonyms: hyperstimulation, overexcitation|Antonyms: calmness, tranquility|noun
