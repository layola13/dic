entozoa:/ˌɛntəˈzoʊə/
noun
内寄生虫；体内寄生虫
Animals that live as parasites inside the bodies of other animals.
例句：Entozoa can cause a variety of health problems in their hosts.  内寄生虫会导致宿主出现各种健康问题。

例句：The study of entozoa is important for understanding disease transmission. 研究内寄生虫对于了解疾病传播非常重要。

近义词：endoparasite, internal parasite
反义词：ectoparasite
词性：n.
