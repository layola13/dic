caron:/ˈkærən/| n.|尖音符，抑扬符|A diacritical mark (ˇ) placed over certain letters (especially vowels) to indicate a particular sound quality, such as the pronunciation of ⟨č⟩, ⟨š⟩, and ⟨ž⟩ in some Slavic languages.|The Czech word "čaj" (tea) uses a caron over the "c".  捷克语单词"čaj"（茶）在字母"c"上使用了一个尖音符。|近义词：haček, wedge|反义词:None|noun
