noncorrupters:/ˌnɒn kəˈrʌptəz/
n.
不腐败的人; 不受腐蚀的人
People who do not engage in corrupt practices; those who resist corruption.
The noncorrupters in the government worked tirelessly to expose the scandal. 政府中不腐败的人员不知疲倦地工作，以揭露这起丑闻。
Synonyms: incorruptibles, purists, moralists
Antonyms: corrupters, grafters, criminals
