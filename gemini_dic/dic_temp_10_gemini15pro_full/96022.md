indaba:/ɪnˈdɑːbɑː/
n.
会议，讨论，商议
A conference or discussion, especially on important matters, typically involving community leaders in South Africa.
The village elders held an indaba to discuss the drought. 村里的长老们举行了一次会议来讨论干旱问题。
The indaba on land reform lasted for several days. 土地改革会议持续了数天。
synonym: conference, meeting, discussion, council, assembly
antonym: disagreement, argument, dispute
Part of Speech: Noun
