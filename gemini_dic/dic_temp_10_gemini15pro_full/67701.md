exultant:/ɪɡˈzʌltənt/ /ɪɡˈzʌltənt/| adj.|欣喜若狂的；欢欣鼓舞的|Feeling or expressing great joy or triumph.|例句：The team was exultant after their victory. 球队在胜利后欣喜若狂。|近义词：jubilant, triumphant, elated, joyous|反义词：dejected, despondent, miserable|词性:形容词
exult:/ɪɡˈzʌlt/ /ɪɡˈzʌlt/| vi.|感到欢欣鼓舞；兴高采烈|To show or feel a lively or triumphant joy; rejoice exceedingly; be highly elated or jubilant.|例句：She exulted at her success. 她为自己的成功而欢欣鼓舞。|近义词：rejoice, triumph, revel|反义词：mourn, grieve|词性:动词
