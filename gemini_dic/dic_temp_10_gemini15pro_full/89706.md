hop-it:/ˈhɒpɪt/ (UK), /ˈhɑːpɪt/ (US)| n.|跳房子游戏；跳房子格|A children's game played by hopping through a pattern of squares drawn on the ground.|例句：The children were playing hop-it in the playground. 孩子们在操场上玩跳房子。|例句：She drew a hop-it grid with chalk on the sidewalk. 她在人行道上用粉笔画了一个跳房子格子。|近义词：hopscotch|反义词:|名词
