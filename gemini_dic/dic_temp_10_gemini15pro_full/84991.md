haptically:/ˈhæptɪkli/| adv.|触觉地；通过触觉|Relating to the sense of touch; by means of touch.|She explored the sculpture haptically, running her fingers over its smooth surface. 她通过触觉探索雕塑，用手指抚摸其光滑的表面。|例句：The device provides haptically rich feedback, allowing users to feel virtual textures.  该设备提供丰富的触觉反馈，允许用户感受虚拟纹理。|近义词：tangibly, tactilely|反义词:visually, auditorily|词性:副词
