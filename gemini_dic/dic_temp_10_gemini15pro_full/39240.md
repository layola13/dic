congratulational:/kənˌɡrætʃəˈleɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 祝贺的，庆贺的 | Expressing or conveying congratulations. | Example:  A congratulational message was sent to the winning team.  祝贺消息发给了获胜的队伍。 | Example: He gave a congratulatory speech. 他发表了祝贺演讲。|  Near Synonyms: congratulatory, celebratory, complimentary | Antonyms:  critical, disapproving | word type: adjective

Etymology:
From congratulate + -ional.  Ultimately from Latin gratulari "to wish joy," from gratus "pleasing, thankful."
