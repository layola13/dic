connascent:/kəˈnæsənt/
adjective
同生的，天生的，与生俱来的
Growing together from or by a common base; congenitally united.
Example: The connascent twins were successfully separated by the surgical team. 这对连体双胞胎被外科团队成功分离。
Example:  The artist's creativity and passion seemed connascent, inseparable parts of his being.  这位艺术家的创造力和热情似乎是与生俱来的，是他存在中不可分割的部分。
Synonym:  connate, congenital, inherent, innate
Antonym: acquired, learned, developed
词性: adjective
