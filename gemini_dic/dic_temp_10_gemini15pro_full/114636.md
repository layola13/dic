maltreat:/ˌmælˈtriːt/ | v. | 虐待，苛待 | To treat someone cruelly or badly. |  The animals were severely maltreated by their owner.  这些动物受到了主人的严重虐待。

The child was maltreated and neglected. 这个孩子被虐待和忽视。


近义词: abuse, mistreat, ill-treat, misuse | 反义词: cherish, care for, nurture, protect | 词性: verb
