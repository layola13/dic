endodermis:/ˈɛndəʊdɜːrmɪs/| n. |内皮层|The innermost layer of the cortex in land plants, surrounding the vascular tissue.|The endodermis plays a crucial role in regulating water and nutrient uptake by roots. 内皮层在调节根部水分和养分吸收方面起着至关重要的作用。|近义词:inner layer|反义词:|n.
