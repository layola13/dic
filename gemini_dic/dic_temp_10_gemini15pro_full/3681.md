aggravate:/ˈæɡrəveɪt/ | vt. | 加重，恶化；激怒，使恼火 | To make a bad situation, an illness, or an injury worse; to annoy someone, especially deliberately |  The humid weather aggravated my eczema. 潮湿的天气加重了我的湿疹。

The constant noise aggravated the headache.  持续不断的噪音加剧了头痛。 | 近义词: worsen, exacerbate, irritate, annoy, provoke | 反义词: alleviate, improve, soothe, calm | 词性: 动词

aggravate:/ˈæɡrəveɪt/ |  vt. | 使恼火，激怒 |  To annoy someone, especially deliberately | He was aggravated by their constant questions.  他们不断地提问让他很恼火。

She was aggravated by his condescending tone. 他那居高临下的语气让她很生气。| 近义词: annoy, irritate, provoke, exasperate | 反义词: placate, appease, calm | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin aggravatus, past participle of aggravare ("to make heavier, to burden"), from Latin ad- ("to") + gravis ("heavy").
