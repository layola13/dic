cinchonaceous:/ˌsɪŋkəˈneɪʃəs/| adj.|金鸡纳科的；金鸡纳树的|Relating to or belonging to the Cinchona family, a family of flowering plants that includes the source of quinine.|This cinchonaceous plant is known for its medicinal properties. 这种金鸡纳科植物以其药用特性而闻名。|Synonyms: quinine-yielding, rubiaceous|Antonyms: N/A|adjective

cinchonaceous:/ˌsɪŋkəˈneɪʃəs/| n.|金鸡纳科植物|A plant belonging to the Cinchona family.|Cinchonaceous species are often found in tropical regions. 金鸡纳科植物常发现于热带地区。|Synonyms: quinine tree,  rubiaceous plant|Antonyms: N/A|noun
