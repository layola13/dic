ceases:/ˈsiːsɪz/
verb
停止，终止
to stop happening or existing; to stop something from happening or existing
The rain ceased and the sun came out. 雨停了，太阳出来了。
Synonyms: stop, halt, end, terminate, discontinue
Antonyms: begin, start, commence, continue, resume
词性: 动词
