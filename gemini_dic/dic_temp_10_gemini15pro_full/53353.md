disadventure:/ˌdɪsədˈvɛntʃər/ | n. |不幸的遭遇，灾难|An unfortunate or unpleasant mishap or event; a misadventure.| Example: Their camping trip turned into a disadventure when their tent collapsed in a storm. 他们的露营之旅变成了一场灾难，因为他们的帐篷在暴风雨中倒塌了。

Example:  He recounted the disadventure of losing his passport on the first day of his vacation. 他讲述了在假期第一天丢失护照的不幸遭遇。 | 近义词:mishap, misfortune, misadventure, setback, adversity | 反义词:fortune, good luck, success, blessing | 词性:noun

Etymology: From Middle French *desaventure* (unattested), from *desaventurer* "to have an unfortunate adventure," from *des-* "un-, dis-" + *aventure* "adventure."
