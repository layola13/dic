gunboats:/ˈɡʌnboʊts/ (US) /ˈɡʌnbəʊts/ (UK)| n.|炮舰|A small warship equipped with guns, typically used in shallow waters or rivers for bombardment and patrol.|The gunboats patrolled the river delta. 炮舰在河流三角洲巡逻。|The gunboats shelled the enemy positions. 炮舰炮击了敌人的阵地。|Synonyms: warship, corvette, frigate|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "merchant ship" in a loose sense)|Noun

gunboat:/ˈɡʌnboʊts/ (US) /ˈɡʌnbəʊts/ (UK)| n.|炮艇外交|Diplomacy backed by the threat of military force, particularly naval force.|The country employed gunboat diplomacy to secure its trade interests. 这个国家采用炮舰外交来确保其贸易利益。|The threat of deploying gunboats was enough to persuade the smaller nation. 部署炮舰的威胁足以说服这个小国。|Synonyms:  coercive diplomacy, big stick diplomacy|Antonyms:  peaceful negotiation, soft power|Noun
