embark: /ɪmˈbɑːrk/ (UK), /ɛmˈbɑːrk/ (US)
verb
开始，着手；上船，上飞机
To begin (a course of action, especially one that is important or demanding); to go on board a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle.
例句：
They embarked on a new business venture.
他们开始了一项新的商业冒险。

The passengers embarked the plane.
乘客们登上了飞机。

近义词：begin, commence, start, board
反义词：disembark, debark
词性: verb


embarkation: /ɪmˌbɑːrˈkeɪʃən/ (UK), /ɛmˌbɑːrˈkeɪʃən/ (US)
noun
上船，登机，开始，着手
The action of embarking.
例句：
The embarkation process was smooth and efficient.
登船过程顺利而高效。

The embarkation point is clearly marked.
登船地点有明确标记。

近义词：boarding, beginning, commencement
反义词：disembarkation
词性: noun
