ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English matrimonie, from Old French matrimoine, from Latin mātrimōnium (“marriage, wedlock”), from māter (“mother”) + suffix -monium. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *méh₂tēr (“mother”).
