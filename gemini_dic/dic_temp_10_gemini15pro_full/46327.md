dabs:/dæbz/ | n. | 轻拍；少量；大麻浓缩物 |  A small amount of something, often a semi-liquid substance, applied lightly. Also refers to a type of cannabis concentrate. |  She applied a few dabs of paint to the canvas.  她在画布上涂了几点颜料。 He took a dab of the ointment and rubbed it on his wound. 他取了一点药膏擦在伤口上。  He heats the dabs with a blowtorch. 他用喷灯加热大麻浓缩物。 | 近义词：touch, bit, drop, spot, dollop (for semi-liquids), dab (for cannabis concentrate) | 反义词: lot, heap | 词性: 名词

dabs:/dæbz/ | v. | 轻拍；轻触 | To touch something lightly and briefly, often with a soft or absorbent material. | She dabbed her eyes with a tissue. 她用纸巾轻擦了擦眼睛。 He dabbed at the stain with a damp cloth. 他用湿布轻擦污渍。 | 近义词：pat, touch, blot | 反义词: rub, smear | 词性: 动词
