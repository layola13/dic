interinfluences:/ˌɪntərˈɪnfluənsɪz/
n.
相互影响
the ways in which two or more things affect each other.
The interinfluences between climate and vegetation are complex. 气候和植被之间的相互影响很复杂。
interaction, interplay, reciprocity
independence, isolation
noun
