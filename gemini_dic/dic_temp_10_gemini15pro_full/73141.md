foraminiferous:/ˌfɔːrəˈmɪnɪfərəs/
|adj.|
有孔虫的；多孔的|
Bearing or containing foraminifera.  Containing numerous small holes or foramina.|
例句：Foraminiferous limestone is a common type of sedimentary rock. 有孔虫石灰岩是一种常见的沉积岩。

例句：The ocean floor is covered in foraminiferous ooze. 海底覆盖着有孔虫软泥。
|近义词：porous, perforated|
反义词：impermeable, solid|
词性：adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *foraminifer*, from *foramen* ("opening, hole") + *-fer* ("bearing").
