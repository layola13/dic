carbon-dated:/ˈkɑːrbən deɪtɪd/ | adj. | 碳测年的，经碳测定的 | Determined the age of something using the carbon-14 dating method. | The artifact was carbon-dated and found to be over 2,000 years old.  这件文物经过碳测定，发现它有2000多年的历史。 | 近义词: radiocarbon-dated | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but possibly "undated," "age-unknown") | 词性: 形容词

carbon-date:/ˈkɑːrbən deɪt/ | vt. |  用碳测定年代 | To determine the age of something using the carbon-14 dating method. | Scientists carbon-dated the ancient scrolls to verify their authenticity. 科学家用碳测定古代卷轴的年代来验证它们的真伪。 | 近义词: radiocarbon-date | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: 动词
