headliner:/ˈhedˌlaɪnər/| n.|领衔主演者；头条新闻；主要事件|A person or act that is the star attraction of a show, especially a theatrical or musical performance.  A leading story in a newspaper or broadcast.|例句：The headliner for tonight's concert is a Grammy Award winner. 今晚音乐会的领衔主演是格莱美奖得主。

例句：The scandal became a headliner in the national news. 这桩丑闻成了全国新闻的头条。|近义词：star, lead, main act, top story|反义词:supporting act, minor news|词性:noun

headlining:/ˈhedˌlaɪnɪŋ/| adj.|领衔主演的；作为头条的|Serving as the featured performer or act.|例句：The headlining band drew a huge crowd.  领衔乐队吸引了大批观众。

例句：The headlining news story was about the election. 头条新闻是关于选举的。|近义词：starring, leading, featured|反义词:supporting, minor|词性:adjective
