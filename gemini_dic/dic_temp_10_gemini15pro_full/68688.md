farcin:/ˈfɑːrsɪn/| n.| 鼻疽；马鼻疽|A contagious and fatal disease of horses, characterized by ulcerous nodules in the skin, mucous membranes, and internal organs, caused by the bacterium Burkholderia mallei. |The horse was diagnosed with farcin and had to be euthanized.|近义词:glanders|反义词:health|词性:noun
farcy:/ˈfɑːrsi/| n.| 鼻疽| Another term for farcin, a contagious and often fatal bacterial disease affecting horses, mules, and donkeys, characterized by nodules and ulcerations in the skin, lungs, and other organs.  | The vet suspected farcy due to the horse's persistent cough and skin lesions. |近义词:glanders, farcin|反义词:health|词性:noun
