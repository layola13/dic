chloritic:/klɔːˈrɪtɪk/
adjective
绿泥石的；含绿泥石的
containing or characteristic of chlorite (a green mineral)
The soil sample was chloritic, indicating the presence of weathered volcanic rock. 土壤样本中含有绿泥石，表明存在风化的火山岩。
The rock is chloritic schist. 这块岩石是绿泥石片岩。
synonyms:chlorite-rich, chlorite-bearing
antonyms: (No direct antonym, but could use terms like "chlorite-free" or describe a rock composed of different minerals)
词性: adjective
