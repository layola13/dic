half-profiles:/ˌhɑːf ˈproʊfaɪlz/ | n. | 半侧面像；侧面轮廓 | A side view of a face or head, showing only one side of the features. | The artist sketched a series of half-profiles, capturing the subjects' contemplative expressions.  艺术家画了一系列的半侧面像，捕捉了对象沉思的表情。| synonyms: side view, profile | antonyms: full-face portrait | noun

half-profiles:/ˌhɑːf ˈproʊfaɪlz/ | n. | 半身侧面照；半截侧面图 | A representation of something (like a building or vehicle) viewed from the side, showing only half of its form. | The architect presented half-profiles of the proposed skyscraper, highlighting its unique facade design. 建筑师展示了拟建摩天大楼的半截侧面图，突出了其独特的外观设计。| synonyms: side elevation, partial view | antonyms: full view, complete representation | noun
