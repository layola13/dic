hearty-hale:/ˈhɑːrti heɪl/
adj.
健壮的，矍铄的
Healthy and vigorous.
He was a hearty-hale old man, still working on his farm at ninety. 他是一位健壮矍铄的老人，九十岁了还在农场工作。
近义词：robust, hale, healthy, vigorous
反义词：frail, weak, sickly, feeble
词性: 形容词

词源:
hearty (from Old English *heortīg "heartfelt, brave, spirited") + hale (from Old Norse heill "whole, healthy")
