metr:/ˈmɛtər/ (US), /ˈmiːtər/ (UK)| n.|米，公尺|A standard unit of length in the metric system, equal to 100 centimeters.|例句：The table is two meters long.  桌子有两米长。

例句：The race is 100 meters. 这场比赛是100米。|近义词：m (abbreviation) |反义词: N/A |词性:noun


metr:/ˈmɛtrɪk/ (US), /ˈmɛtrɪk/ (UK)| adj.|公制的，米制的|Relating to the metric system of measurement.|例句：Most countries use the metric system. 大多数国家使用公制。

例句：The recipe uses metric units.  这个食谱使用公制单位。|近义词：decimal |反义词:imperial, US customary |词性:adjective
