mob:/mɒb/ (British) /mɑːb/ (American)| n.  vt.|暴民，乌合之众；围攻，聚众闹事|A large crowd of people, especially one that is disorderly and intent on causing trouble or violence.  To crowd around someone, especially in order to attack or admire them.|The angry mob surrounded the police car.  愤怒的暴民包围了警车。|The fans mobbed the singer as he left the concert hall. 歌迷们把这位歌手团团围住，因为他离开了音乐厅。|近义词：crowd, horde, throng, swarm|反义词:individual, few|名词, 动词
