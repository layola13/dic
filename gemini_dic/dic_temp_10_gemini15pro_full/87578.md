heteropodous:/ˌhɛtərˈɒpədəs/ | adj. |异足的；不同足的 | Having feet of different kinds or adapted for different purposes. | Example 1:  Heteropodous mollusks have modified feet for swimming. 异足类软体动物的足经过改良，适合游泳。

Example 2: The heteropodous nature of the creature allowed it to both climb and swim efficiently.  这种生物异足的特性使它能够高效地攀爬和游泳。 | Near synonyms: anisoped, unequal | Antonym: homoped | Word type: adjective
