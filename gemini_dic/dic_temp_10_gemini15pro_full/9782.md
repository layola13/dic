antrovert:/ˈæntrəvɜːrt/ (US), /ˈɪntrəvɜːt/ (UK)| n. |性格内向的人|A shy, reticent person who finds it difficult to talk to people.|She's a bit of an antrovert and prefers to stay home and read rather than go to parties.|近义词: introvert, shy person, recluse|反义词: extrovert, outgoing person|词性:名词

Etymology:
From German *antrovert, from Latin *introvertere, from intro- "inward" + vertere "to turn".  The word likely arose through confusion or blending with *introvert, and is generally considered a nonstandard or less common variant.  It's recommended to use "introvert."
