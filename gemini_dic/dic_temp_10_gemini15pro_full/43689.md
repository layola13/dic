credit-ratings:/ˈkrɛdɪt ˈreɪtɪŋz/
n.
信用评级
An assessment of the creditworthiness of a borrower, especially a business or government.  It represents the likelihood that the borrower will default on its debt obligations.
The company's credit-ratings were downgraded after the scandal. 这家公司的信用评级在丑闻后被下调。
Synonyms: credit scores, credit assessments, bond ratings
Antonyms: N/A (Credit ratings are evaluations, not inherently positive or negative.  A low rating is bad, but not the opposite of the rating itself.)
