mugient:/ˈmjuːdʒiənt/
adjective
低沉的，像牛叫的
A mournful, low, bellowing sound, like that of a cow.
The mugient sounds emanating from the barn indicated the cows were restless.  从谷仓里传来的低沉的牛叫声表明奶牛们焦躁不安。
The fog horn let out a long, mugient moan.  雾号发出了一声长长的、低沉的呻吟。
Synonyms: lowing, bellowing, moaning
Antonyms: high-pitched, shrill, silent
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From Latin *mugiens, present participle of mugire (“to bellow, low”),  from Proto-Italic *mugī-, perhaps ultimately of imitative origin.
