nonadjudicative:/ˌnɒnəˈdʒuːdɪkeɪtɪv/
adjective
非裁决性的，非判决性的
Not involving the judgment of a legal case; not pertaining to the function of judging.
The mediator's role in the nonadjudicative process was to facilitate communication between the parties. 调解员在非裁决性过程中的作用是促进双方之间的沟通。

The committee conducted a nonadjudicative inquiry into the allegations. 委员会对这些指控进行了非裁决性调查。

Synonym: non-judicial, extrajudicial, informal
Antonym: adjudicative, judicial, legal
词性: adjective
