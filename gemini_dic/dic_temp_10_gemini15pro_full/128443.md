news:/njuːz/ /nuːz/| n. |新闻，消息|reports of recent events, especially those broadcast on radio or television or published in newspapers|
例句：I heard some interesting news on the radio this morning. 我今天早上在收音机里听到了一些有趣的新闻。

例句：The news of his death came as a great shock. 他去世的消息令人震惊。


近义词：information, reports, tidings, updates|
反义词: silence, secrecy|
词性: noun
