brittlely:/ˈbrɪt(ə)lli/| adv.|脆弱地，易碎地|In a brittle manner; easily broken or cracked.  Characterized by stiffness and a lack of warmth or sensitivity.|The dry leaves crumbled brittlely underfoot. 干枯的叶子在脚下脆脆地碎裂。|Synonyms:  fragilely, precariously, stiffly, coldly|Antonyms:  flexibly, resiliently, warmly, sympathetically|词性: 副词

brittle:/ˈbrɪt(ə)l/| adj.|脆弱的，易碎的|Easily broken or cracked; lacking warmth or sensitivity.|The old bone was brittle and snapped easily.  这块老骨头很脆，很容易折断。|Synonyms:  fragile, frail, crisp, curt|Antonyms:  flexible, resilient, pliable, warm|词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *brittol (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *brutilaz, from Proto-Indo-European *bhreu- (“to break, cut”). Cognate with Dutch brokkel (“crumb”), German bröckeln (“to crumble”).
