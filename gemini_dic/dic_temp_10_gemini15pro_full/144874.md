over-reduction:/ˌoʊvər-rɪˈdʌkʃən/ |n.| 过度还原，过度简化 |The over-reduction of complex issues to simple soundbites can mislead the public. 把复杂的问题过度简化为简单的口号会误导公众。| simplification, oversimplification |  complication, elaboration | noun
over-reduction:/ˌoʊvər-rɪˈdʌkʃən/ |adj.| 过度还原的，过度简化的|  His over-reductionist approach to history ignores important nuances. 他对历史的过度简化方法忽略了重要的细微差别。| simplified, oversimplified | complex, nuanced | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
over- (prefix): from Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubar, meaning "above, over, beyond"
reduction (noun): from Late Middle English reduccion, from Late Latin reductionem, from Latin reducere "to lead back," from re- "back" + ducere "to lead"
