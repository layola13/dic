ampliate:/ˈæmplieɪt/| v.|扩大，扩展，放大|To enlarge the scope of; broaden or extend.|He ampliated his remarks with a detailed explanation. 他用详细的解释扩展了他的言论。|近义词：expand, enlarge, extend, amplify, augment|反义词：reduce, condense, diminish, curtail|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *ampliatus*, past participle of *ampliare* "to enlarge, widen," from Latin *amplus* "large, spacious, great."
