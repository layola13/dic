antifashion:/ˌæntiˈfæʃən/ | n. | 反时尚；非主流时尚 |  A style of dressing that rejects or opposes the current fashion trends, often deliberately unconventional or provocative.  It can also refer to a general opposition to the fashion industry and its practices. |  Her ripped jeans and oversized t-shirt were a statement of antifashion. 她破洞的牛仔裤和超大的T恤衫是一种反时尚的宣言。 |  She embraced antifashion, preferring vintage clothing and handmade accessories. 她崇尚反时尚，喜欢复古服装和手工配饰。 | Synonyms: nonconformity, unconventionality, counterculture | Antonyms: fashion, trend, vogue | Noun
