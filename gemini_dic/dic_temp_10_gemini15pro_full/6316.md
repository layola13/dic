amos:/ˈeɪməs/| n.|阿摩司|A Hebrew prophet of the 8th century BC, whose book is the third of the Twelve Minor Prophets in the Old Testament. He preached against social injustice and idolatry in the northern kingdom of Israel.|Amos prophesied the destruction of Israel. 阿摩司预言了以色列的毁灭。|近义词:prophet, seer|反义词:N/A|noun
