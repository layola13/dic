|n.|少将职位；少将军衔（复数）
|The position or rank of major general, especially in a historical context.  It often refers to multiple such positions.|
|During the English Civil War, Cromwell appointed several major-generalships to govern the country.|在英国内战期间，克伦威尔任命了几位少将管理国家。
|The book detailed the various major-generalships held during the Protectorate.|这本书详细介绍了护国公时期担任的各个少将职位。
|近义词: major general commands, major general ranks|
|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider lower ranks like brigadierships, colonelships)|
