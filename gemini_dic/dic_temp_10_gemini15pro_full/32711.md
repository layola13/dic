chlorinates:/ˈklɔːrɪneɪts/
verb
氯化；给…加氯
treats or combines with chlorine.
The city chlorinates its water supply to kill bacteria.  这个城市对其供水进行氯化以杀死细菌。
The pool is chlorinated regularly. 游泳池定期加氯消毒。
近义词:disinfect, sterilize, sanitize
反义词:decontaminate (in a different sense - removing contaminants, not specifically chlorine)
词性:v.

chlorinating:/ˈklɔːrɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
氯化的;加氯的
describing the act of adding chlorine.
The chlorinating agent is added to the water.  氯化剂被添加到水中。
There was a strong chlorinating smell coming from the pool.  游泳池里传来一股强烈的氯化剂气味。
近义词:disinfecting, sterilizing
反义词:N/A
词性:adj.

chlorination:/ˌklɔːrɪˈneɪʃən/
noun
氯化（作用）
the process of treating or combining with chlorine.
Chlorination is a common method of water purification. 氯化是常见的水净化方法。
The chlorination of the pool takes place every week.  游泳池每周进行一次氯化。
近义词:disinfection, sterilization
反义词:N/A
词性:n.
