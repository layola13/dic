overprolifically:/ˌoʊvərproʊˈlɪfɪkli/ | adv. | 过度多产地，过度生育地 |  In a manner characterized by excessive production or reproduction, especially of offspring.  Producing or reproducing too much or too frequently. | The rabbits reproduced overprolifically, quickly overpopulating the small island. 兔子过度繁殖，迅速使小岛人口过剩。|  The author published overprolifically in his later years, sacrificing quality for quantity.  这位作家晚年作品过多，牺牲了质量而追求数量。 | synonym: excessively, abundantly, copiously | antonym: sparsely, sparingly, moderately |词性: 副词
