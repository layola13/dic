occluding:/əˈkluːdɪŋ/
adjective
闭塞的，遮挡的
blocking or obstructing something
例句：The large building is occluding my view of the mountains.  这栋大楼挡住了我看山的视线。

例句：A blood clot occluding a major artery can cause a heart attack. 血块阻塞主要动脉会导致心脏病发作。
synonym: blocking, obstructing, covering, obscuring
antonym: revealing, exposing, uncovering, opening
词性: adjective


occlude:/əˈkluːd/
verb
使闭塞，遮挡
to stop, close, or obstruct
例句：The dentist placed a filling to occlude the cavity. 牙医用填充物堵住了蛀牙。

例句：Heavy clouds occluded the sun. 厚厚的云层遮住了太阳。
synonym: block, obstruct, cover, obscure
antonym: reveal, expose, uncover, open
词性: verb
