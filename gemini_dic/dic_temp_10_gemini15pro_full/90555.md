hugger:/ˈhʌɡər/| n.|拥抱者, 喜欢拥抱的人|A person who hugs other people frequently and readily.|She's a real hugger, always greeting people with a warm embrace. 她真是个喜欢拥抱的人，总是热情地拥抱别人。|近义词：embracer|反义词： (No direct opposite, perhaps someone who avoids physical contact)|noun
