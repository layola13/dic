bouldery: /ˈboʊldəri/ (US), /ˈbəʊldəri/ (UK)| adj.|多岩石的，布满巨砾的|Covered with large rocks; rocky.|

例句：
The terrain was bouldery and difficult to traverse.
地形崎岖不平，难以通行。

The bouldery landscape provided a challenging hike.
遍布巨砾的地貌使得徒步旅行充满挑战。


近义词：rocky, stony, craggy|
反义词: smooth, flat|
词性: adjective|

词源: From boulder + -y.  "Boulder" comes from the Middle Swedish "bulder."
