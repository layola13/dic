noncumulative:/ˌnɑːnˈkjuːmjəleɪtɪv/
adjective
非累积的，非渐增的
Not cumulative; not increasing in quantity or intensity with repeated doses or applications.
This medication has a noncumulative effect, so you don't have to worry about it building up in your system. 这种药物没有累积效应，所以你不必担心它会在你的体内积聚。
The judge imposed noncumulative sentences for each of the crimes. 法官对每项罪行都判处了非累积的刑罚。
non-additive, discrete
cumulative, additive
