impudently:/ˈɪmpjədəntli/| adv.|放肆地，无礼地，厚颜无耻地|In an impudent manner; boldly and disrespectfully.|He impudently contradicted his teacher. 他厚颜无耻地反驳了他的老师。|She impudently demanded more money. 她无礼地要求更多钱。|近义词: insolently, brazenly, audaciously|反义词: respectfully, politely, humbly|词性: 副词

impudent:/ˈɪmpjədənt/| adj.|放肆的，无礼的，厚颜无耻的|Not showing due respect for another person; impertinent.|He gave me an impudent stare. 他厚颜无耻地盯着我。|It was impudent of him to suggest that. 他竟然那样建议，真是太无礼了。|近义词: insolent, brazen, audacious|反义词: respectful, polite, humble|词性: 形容词


词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French impudent, from Latin impudentem,  from in- ‘not’ + pudens ‘ashamed’ (related to pudere ‘feel shame’).
