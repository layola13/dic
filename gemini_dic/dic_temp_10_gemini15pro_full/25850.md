burial: /ˈberiəl/ (US), /ˈbɛriəl/ (UK) | n. | 埋葬; 葬礼; 弃置; 废弃 | The act or ceremony of burying a dead body. The disposal of something by burying it or leaving it underground. |  The burial took place at the local cemetery.  葬礼在当地墓地举行。

The burial of hazardous waste is strictly regulated. 危险废物的掩埋受到严格管制。 | interment, funeral, sepulture | exhumation, resurrection | noun
