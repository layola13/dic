metafile:/ˈmɛtəˌfaɪl/| n. |元文件|A file containing information about other files, often used for graphics or other multimedia content. It typically stores data in a format that is independent of specific applications or devices, allowing it to be used across different platforms.|The metafile contains instructions for recreating the image, rather than the image data itself.|近义词:raster file, vector file (depending on the type of metafile)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "raw data file")|词性:noun

Etymology: meta- (Greek, meaning "beyond," "after," or "higher") + file
