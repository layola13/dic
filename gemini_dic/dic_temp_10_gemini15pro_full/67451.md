extirpators:/ɪkˈstɜːrpətərz/| n. |根除者，消灭者|People dedicated to completely destroying or removing something, often something harmful.  Those who root out or eradicate something completely.|例句：The extirpators of the invasive species worked tirelessly to remove every last plant.  消灭入侵物种的人孜孜不倦地清除每一株植物。

例句：The government acted as extirpators of corruption, seeking to eliminate it from all levels of bureaucracy. 政府充当腐败的根除者，力求将其从各级官僚机构中清除。|近义词：eradicator, exterminator, destroyer, eliminator|反义词：preserver, protector, conserver|词性:noun
