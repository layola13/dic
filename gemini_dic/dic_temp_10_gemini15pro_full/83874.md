half-addressed:/ˌhæf əˈdrɛst/ | adj. | 半处理的，未完全处理的 | Partially addressed, not fully dealt with or completed.  Often refers to a task, issue, or problem that has received some attention but not enough to be considered finished. | The half-addressed issue of employee morale led to a decline in productivity.  员工士气这个未完全处理的问题导致生产力下降。

The report was half-addressed and lacked crucial data. 这份报告只处理了一半，缺少关键数据。 | synonyms: partially addressed, incomplete, unfinished, unresolved | antonyms: completed, finished, resolved, addressed | adjective
