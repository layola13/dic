distemperance:/dɪsˈtɛmpərəns/
n.|放纵， intemperance;  失调，紊乱|Lack of moderation or restraint, especially in relation to alcoholic beverages; intemperance.  A disordered or abnormal condition.|He showed signs of distemperance in his youth, frequently indulging in excessive drinking and gambling. 他年轻时表现出放纵的迹象，经常过度饮酒和赌博。|近义词：intemperance, excess, immoderation, dissipation|反义词：temperance, moderation, abstinence, sobriety|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *destemprer, from des- (expressing negation, reversal) + temprer "to temper, moderate", from Latin temperare "to mix, blend, combine properly, qualify, regulate."  The sense shifted from a general lack of moderation to a specific focus on excessive drinking.
