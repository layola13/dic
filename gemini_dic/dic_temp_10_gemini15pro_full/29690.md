cathetus: /kəˈθiːtəs/ | n. | 垂线; 直角三角形的直角边 |A cathetus (plural: catheti) is a side of a right triangle that is adjacent to the right angle.  It is also known as a leg. | The two catheti of this right triangle measure 3cm and 4cm.  这个直角三角形的两条直角边分别为3厘米和4厘米。| synonym: leg | antonym: hypotenuse | noun
