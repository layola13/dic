mesosperm:/ˈmɛzəˌspɜːrm/| n. | 中层；中果皮 |The middle layer of the pericarp of a fruit, between the endocarp and exocarp.  It is often the fleshy part of the fruit. |The mesosperm of a peach is the sweet, juicy part that we eat.|Synonyms: middle layer, sarcocarp|Antonyms: endocarp, exocarp|noun
