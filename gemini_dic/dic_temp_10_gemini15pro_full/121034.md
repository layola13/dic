misconceiver:/ˌmɪskənˈsiːvər/
|n.|误解者，错误理解者|Someone who misunderstands or misinterprets something.|He was a misconceiver of the instructions and performed the task incorrectly. 他误解了指示，错误地执行了任务。|近义词：misinterpreter, malinterpreter|反义词：interpreter, understander|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From mis- (meaning "wrongly," "badly," or "incorrectly") + conceive (meaning "to form an idea or understanding of something") + -er (suffix forming agent nouns, denoting someone who performs an action).
