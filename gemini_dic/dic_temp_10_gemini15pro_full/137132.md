nonsubsistence:/ˌnɒnsəbˈsɪstəns/ |adj.|非维持生存的；非生计的|Relating to activities or goods that are not essential for survival, such as leisure pursuits or luxury items.  It describes something that is not related to providing the basic necessities of life.|Many people find themselves spending more money on nonsubsistence goods like entertainment and travel.|近义词:nonessential, luxury, discretionary|反义词:essential, subsistence, necessary|词性:adjective

词源: "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "subsistence" (from Latin "subsistere" - "to stand firm, remain, exist")
