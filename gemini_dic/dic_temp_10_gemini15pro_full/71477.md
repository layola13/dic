ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *flamengo*, from Provençal *flamenc* “flamingo, Fleming”, related to *flamant* "bright red". Ultimately from Late Latin *flammicus* "flaming", from Latin *flamma* "flame."  The connection is likely due to the bird's bright pink or reddish plumage.
