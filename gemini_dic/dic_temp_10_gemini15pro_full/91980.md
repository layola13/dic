hypernatraemic:/ˌhaɪpərnəˈtriːmɪk/ |adj.|高钠血症的|Characterized by an abnormally high concentration of sodium in the blood.|The patient presented with hypernatraemic dehydration. 该病人表现为高钠血症脱水。|Synonyms: hypernatremic|Antonyms: hyponatremic|adjective


Etymology->
hyper- (Greek): prefix meaning "over," "above," or "excessive"
natr- (Latin, natrium): referring to sodium
-aemic (Greek, haima): relating to blood
-ic: adjective suffix
