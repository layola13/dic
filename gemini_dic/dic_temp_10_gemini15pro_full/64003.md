epistolizer: /ɪˈpɪstəlaɪzər/
n.
写信人，致信者
A person who writes letters, especially long or formal ones.
She was a prolific epistolizer, sending letters to friends and family all over the world. 她是一位多产的写信人，信件寄往世界各地的朋友和家人。
The epistolizer expressed his concerns in a strongly worded letter to the editor.  这位写信人在给编辑的措辞强烈的信中表达了他的担忧。
Synonyms: letter-writer, correspondent
Antonyms: recipient
