overinstructive:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈstrʌktɪv/
adjective
过度指导的；过于详细指导的
Giving too much instruction; excessively didactic.
The overinstructive manual made the simple task seem complicated.  这本过于详细指导的手册让简单的任务显得复杂。
The training program was so overinstructive that it bored the participants.  这个培训项目指导过度，让参与者感到厌烦。
近义词:didactic, pedantic, patronizing
反义词:unhelpful, vague, concise
词性:adjective

词源:
over- (prefix) + instructive
"over-" 表示 "过度", "过于"
"instructive" 来自 instruct (指导), 源于拉丁语 instruere (建造, 排列, 教导)
