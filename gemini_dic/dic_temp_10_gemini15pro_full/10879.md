arachnitis:/ərækˈnaɪtɪs/| n. |蛛网膜炎|Inflammation of the arachnoid membrane, one of the three membranes (meninges) that surround and protect the brain and spinal cord.|She was diagnosed with arachnitis after experiencing severe headaches and neck stiffness.|近义词:meningitis|反义词:None|词性:noun

词源: From Greek *aráchnē* (spider) + *-itis* (inflammation).
