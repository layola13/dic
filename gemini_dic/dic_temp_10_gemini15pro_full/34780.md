climatic:/klaɪˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 气候的；与气候有关的 | Relating to climate; characteristic of a particular climate. | The climatic conditions in the region are harsh. 该地区的气候条件恶劣。| The climatic changes are affecting agriculture. 气候变化正在影响农业。 | 近义词:meteorological, atmospheric | 反义词: None | 词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *climatique*, from Latin *climaticus*, from Greek *klima*, meaning "region, zone, slope, inclination" - referring to the inclination of the earth's axis.  The Greek term was later adopted to refer to a particular region's weather conditions, leading to the modern meaning related to climate.
