nonfatalities:/nɒn.fəˈtæl.ɪ.tiz/ | n. | 非致命事件；非死亡人数 |  People injured in nonfatalities accidents.  在非致命事故中受伤的人。 |  Example: The report focused on nonfatalities, highlighting the number of injuries sustained.  报告重点关注非致命事件，突出了受伤人数。 |  Synonym: nonfatal injuries, nonfatal casualties | Antonym: fatalities, deaths | Noun

Etymology:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "fatality" (from Late Latin "fatalitas," meaning "fate, destiny," ultimately from Latin "fatalis," meaning "ordained by fate").
