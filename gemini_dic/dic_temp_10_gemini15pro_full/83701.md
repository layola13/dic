hagiocracy:/ˌheɪɡiˈɒkrəsi/
n.
神权政治；圣人统治
A government by those considered holy or by a priesthood.  A state or society governed by a priesthood.
The island nation had become a hagiocracy, ruled by religious leaders who imposed strict moral codes.  这个岛国变成了一个神权政治国家，由实行严格道德准则的宗教领袖统治。

The strict religious leaders established a hagiocracy, where every aspect of life was dictated by their interpretation of divine law. 这些严格的宗教领袖建立了一个神权政治，生活的方方面面都由他们对神圣律法的解释来决定。

Synonym:
theocracy, hierocracy
Antonym:
secularism, democracy
Noun
