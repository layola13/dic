numbskulls:/ˈnʌmskʌlz/ | n. |笨蛋，傻瓜 |A stupid or foolish person. |例句：Those numbskulls locked their keys in the car. 那些笨蛋把钥匙锁在车里了。

例句：Don't be such a numbskull! 别这么傻！|近义词：idiot, fool, moron, dunce |反义词：genius, intellectual|词性: 名词(plural)

词源:  "Numbskull" combines "numb," meaning lacking sensation, and "skull," referring to the head.  It suggests someone with a head that feels numb, hence unintelligent.
