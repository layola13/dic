magnetize:/ˈmæɡnɪtaɪz/| v. |磁化；吸引|To make something magnetic; to strongly attract someone. |The iron filings were magnetized by the bar magnet. 铁屑被条形磁铁磁化了。

He was magnetized by her beauty. 他被她的美貌所吸引。|近义词：attract, mesmerize, captivate, charm|反义词：demagnetize, repel|词性:动词
