antiromantics:/ˌæntiroʊˈmæntɪks/
noun
反浪漫主义者；不相信浪漫的人
People who reject or are opposed to romantic ideals, often seen as cynical or pragmatic.
The antiromantics in the group scoffed at the idea of a candlelit dinner.  小组里的反浪漫主义者嘲笑烛光晚餐的想法。
Antiromantics believe that love is simply a chemical reaction. 反浪漫主义者认为爱情只是一种化学反应。
synonyms: cynics, pragmatists, realists
antonyms: romantics, idealists, sentimentalists
n.


antiromantic:/ˌæntiroʊˈmæntɪk/
adjective
反浪漫的；不相信浪漫的
Characterized by a rejection of romantic ideals or sentiments.
He has a very antiromantic view of marriage, seeing it as a purely economic arrangement. 他对婚姻持有一种非常反浪漫的观点，认为这纯粹是一种经济安排。
Her antiromantic attitude made it difficult for her to connect with people on an emotional level. 她反浪漫的态度使她很难在情感层面上与人联系。
synonyms: cynical, pragmatic, realistic, unsentimental
antonyms: romantic, idealistic, sentimental
adj.
