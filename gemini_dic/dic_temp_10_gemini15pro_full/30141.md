ceilings: /ˈsiːlɪŋz/ | n. |天花板；上限；最高限度 |The ceilings in this old house are very high. 这座老房子的天花板很高。 | ceilings are the overhead surfaces of rooms.| 例句：The ceilings were adorned with intricate plasterwork. 天花板上装饰着复杂的灰泥图案。| 近义词：roof, top | 反义词: floor, base | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From Middle English *seling, from Old French ciel "sky, heaven," from Latin caelum "sky, heaven." The sense shift from "sky" to "ceiling" likely occurred through the notion of the ceiling as the "inner sky" of a room.
