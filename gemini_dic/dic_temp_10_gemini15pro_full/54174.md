disepalous:/daɪˈsɛpələs/
adj.
两萼片的
Having two sepals.
The disepalous flower attracted the bee. 这朵两萼片的花吸引了蜜蜂。
近义词: two-sepalled
反义词: polysepalous (多萼片的)
词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From di- (two) + sepal + -ous (adjectival suffix).  The prefix "di-" comes from Greek, meaning "two,"  "sepal" comes from New Latin *sēpalum, altered from Greek skepē 'covering', and "-ous" is a suffix forming adjectives.
