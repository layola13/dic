logogram:/ˈlɒɡəɡræm/ (UK), /ˈlɑːɡəɡræm/ (US)| n.|语标，表意文字|A written character that represents a word or morpheme (meaningful unit of language), as opposed to representing a sound or sounds, as in an alphabet.|Chinese characters are logograms. 汉字是语标。|synonym: ideogram, ideograph|antonym: phonogram|noun
