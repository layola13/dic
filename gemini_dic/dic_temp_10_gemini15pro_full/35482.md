coachy:/ˈkoʊtʃi/ | adj. |指导性的，像教练的|Having the characteristics of a coach; excessively offering advice and instruction, often in a way that is perceived as intrusive or presumptuous.| He gave me some coachy advice about my career. 他给了我一些关于我职业生涯的指导性建议。|Synonyms: instructive, mentoring, didactic, advisory|Antonyms: unhelpful, unsupportive, discouraging|adjective
