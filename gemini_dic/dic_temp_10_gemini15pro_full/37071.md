combats:/ˈkɒmbæts/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmbæts/ (US)| n. v.|战斗，搏斗；对抗，抗争|A fight or contest between individuals or groups, especially one involving the use of weapons; the act of fighting or contending against something.|The two knights engaged in fierce combats. 两位骑士进行了激烈的战斗。|近义词：battles, fights, struggles, conflicts|反义词：peace, truces, harmony|词性:名词复数

combats:/kəmˈbæts/ (UK) /kəmˈbæts/ (US)| v.|战斗，搏斗；对抗，抗争|To fight or contend against something; to oppose or resist something.|The government is combating inflation. 政府正在对抗通货膨胀。|近义词：battles, fights, struggles, opposes, resists|反义词：surrenders, yields, accepts|词性:动词第三人称单数

词源: From Old French *combattre, from com- "together" + battre "to beat".
