heartlings:/ˈhɑːrtlɪŋz/
n.
心肝宝贝；亲爱的人们
People dear to one's heart; loved ones.
The queen addressed her heartlings with a warm smile. 女王带着温暖的笑容向她的子民们讲话。
近义词：loved ones, dears, darlings
反义词：enemies, foes
词性：名词

词源：
From "heart" + "-ling" (a diminutive suffix).  The suffix "-ling"  is used to form nouns meaning "a young, small, or inferior specimen of something."  Therefore, "heartling" implies someone small and dear to one's heart.
