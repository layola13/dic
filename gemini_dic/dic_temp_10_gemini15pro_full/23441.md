bradded:/ˈbrædɪd/| adj.| 编织的，编成辫子的|Decorated or fastened with braid.  Made by braiding.| The horse had a braded mane and tail.  这匹马的鬃毛和尾巴都编成了辫子。|近义词：plaited, braided, woven|反义词:unbraided, loose|词性:形容词
Etymology: From braid + -ed.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bregdaną "to weave, braid."
