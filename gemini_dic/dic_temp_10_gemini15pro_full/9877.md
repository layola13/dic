apart:/əˈpɑːrt/ | adj. adv.| 分离的，隔开的；除外 | separated by a distance or interval; separately, aside from | The two houses stood 50 meters apart. 这两栋房子相距50米。| synonyms: separated, divided, asunder | antonyms: together, joined, united | word type: adjective/adverb

apart:/əˈpɑːrt/ | adv.| 除……之外；此外 | besides, except for | Apart from a few scratches, the car was undamaged. 除了几处划痕外，汽车没有损坏。| synonyms: besides, aside from, except for | antonyms: including, within | word type: adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *apart (Modern French à part), from Latin ad "to, toward" + pars "part, side, portion".
