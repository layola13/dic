encirclements:/ɪnˈsɜːrkəlmənts/
|n.|包围，环绕，围困|The act of encircling or the state of being encircled.  The enemy completed the encirclement of the city. 敌人完成了对城市的包围。|例句：The encirclements of the enemy troops were broken. 敌军的包围圈被突破了。

例句：Several encirclements of the city were attempted. 对这座城市进行了几次包围。

|近义词：surroundings, enclosures, besiegements, blockades|
|反义词：liberation, release, freedom|
|词性：名词|
