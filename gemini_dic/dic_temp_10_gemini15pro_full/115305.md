mannequin:/ˈmænɪkɪn/| n. |人体模型；时装模特儿|A life-sized dummy used to display clothes.|The mannequins in the shop window were dressed in the latest fashions. 商店橱窗里的人体模型穿着最新款式的服装。|Synonyms: dummy, model, figure|Antonyms: person, human|noun

mannequin:/ˈmænəkɪn/| n. |(尤指女)时装模特儿|A person employed to model clothes.|She worked as a mannequin in Paris. 她在巴黎当过时装模特。|Synonyms: model|Antonyms: customer, client|noun
