arabesquing:/ˌærəˈbɛskɪŋ/
v.
用蔓草花纹装饰; 跳阿拉贝斯克舞姿
performing arabesques (ballet move); decorating with arabesque patterns
The ballerina was arabesquing gracefully across the stage.  芭蕾舞女演员在舞台上优雅地跳着阿拉贝斯克舞姿。
The artist spent hours arabesquing the border of the manuscript. 艺术家花了几个小时用蔓草花纹装饰手稿的边缘。
近义词: embellishing, ornamenting, decorating, adorning;  posing
反义词: simplifying, stripping
词性: verb (present participle)


arabesque:/ˈærəˌbɛsk/
n.
蔓草花纹; 阿拉贝斯克舞姿
a complex, ornate design of intertwined lines; a ballet position in which one leg is extended backward
The wallpaper featured a delicate arabesque pattern.  壁纸上有一个精致的蔓草花纹图案。
She held the arabesque pose for an impressive amount of time. 她保持阿拉贝斯克舞姿保持了很长时间，令人印象深刻。
近义词: ornamentation, decoration, flourish; pose
反义词: plainness, simplicity
词性: noun
