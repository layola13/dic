antidissidents:/ˌæntiˈdɪsɪdənts/
n.|反异议人士，持不同政见者|People who actively oppose dissidents or dissent.  Those who persecute, suppress, or silence those who express views that differ from the established authority. |The government employed antidissidents to infiltrate protest groups. 政府雇佣反异议人士渗透抗议团体。| synonyms:counterrevolutionaries, reactionaries |antonyms:dissidents, rebels, activists| noun

antidissident:/ˌæntiˈdɪsɪdənt/
adj.|反异议的|Opposed to or against dissidents and their activities. Characterized by opposition to dissent. |The antidissident policies of the regime led to widespread unrest. 该政权的反异议政策导致了广泛的动乱。| synonyms:repressive, authoritarian |antonyms:liberal, tolerant| adjective
