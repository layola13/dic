fibre:/ˈfaɪbər/ (US) /ˈfaɪbə(r)/ (UK)| n.|纤维；光纤；质地；性格；素养|A thread or filament from which a vegetable tissue, mineral substance, or textile is formed. |例句：The cloth is made of natural fibre.  这块布料是由天然纤维制成的。

例句：Dietary fibre is important for digestive health.  膳食纤维对消化系统健康很重要。|近义词：strand, filament, thread, fiber (US spelling)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fibre, from Old French fibre, from Latin fibra (“fiber, filament”), related to Latin filum (“thread”).
