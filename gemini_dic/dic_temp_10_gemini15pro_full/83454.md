habitually:/həˈbɪtʃuəli/| adv.|习惯地；通常地|In a habitual manner; customarily; usually.  It describes actions done regularly or consistently.|He habitually arrives late for meetings. 他习惯性地开会迟到。|Synonyms: routinely, regularly, customarily, usually, normally, ordinarily, typically|Antonyms: rarely, infrequently, occasionally, exceptionally|adverb

Etymology:
From Late Latin *habitualis*, from Latin habitus (“condition, appearance, dress, attire”), past participle of habeō (“to have, hold, possess, wear”).  The English word was formed in the mid-16th century.
