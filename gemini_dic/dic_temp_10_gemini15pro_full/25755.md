burbler:/ˈbɜːrblər/ (US), /ˈbɜːblə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 喋喋不休的人，健谈的人；（尤指）鸟类鸣禽|A person who talks continuously and rapidly, especially about unimportant things; a bird, especially a songbird, that makes a bubbling or warbling sound.|The little girl was a real burbler, chatting non-stop about her toys.|近义词：chatterbox, talker, gabber|反义词：quiet person, introvert|词性: noun

burblers:/ˈbɜːrblərz/ (US), /ˈbɜːbləz/ (UK)| n. | 喋喋不休的人们，健谈的人们；（尤指）鸟类鸣禽（复数）|Plural form of "burbler". Refers to multiple individuals who talk continuously and rapidly, or multiple birds that make a bubbling or warbling sound.|The birdwatchers were delighted to spot a flock of burblers in the trees.|近义词：chatterboxes, talkers, gabbers|反义词：quiet people, introverts|词性: noun
