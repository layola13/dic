coterie:/ˈkoʊtəri/| n.|小圈子，集团，同行|A close circle of friends or associates, often exclusive.|He was accepted into the coterie of artists. 他被接纳进了艺术家的小圈子。|近义词：clique, circle, group, set|反义词:outsiders, general public|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *coterie* "group of tenants," from *cote* "hut, cottage" (from Frankish *kot* "hut"), ultimately from Proto-Germanic *kut* "hut." The sense shifted from a group sharing a dwelling to a close-knit social group.
