mayes:/meɪz/ | n. | 迷宫，曲径，错综复杂的事物 | A network of paths and hedges designed as a puzzle through which one has to find a way.  A complex and confusing arrangement or state of affairs. |  例句：The children got lost in the corn maze. 孩子们在玉米迷宫里迷路了。

例句：The legal system is a maze of rules and regulations. 法律体系是一个由规则和条例组成的迷宫。 | 近义词：labyrinth, network, tangle, web | 反义词:simplicity, clarity | 词性:名词
