numerologies:/ˌnʊmərˈɑːlədʒiz/ | n. | 命理学；数字命理学 | The study of the occult significance of numbers.  It assigns symbolic meanings to numbers and uses them to interpret character or predict the future. |  She's really into numerologies and believes her lucky number is 7. 她对命理学很着迷，相信她的幸运数字是7。 | Numerology is often used in conjunction with astrology. 命理学常与占星术结合使用。 |  Near synonyms: divination, fortune-telling | Antonyms:  science, logic | noun
