globalistic:/ˌɡloʊbəˈlɪstɪk/
adj.
全球主义的；全球化的
Relating to or characteristic of globalism.  Favoring or promoting globalization.
The candidate's globalistic policies focused on international cooperation and free trade.  这位候选人的全球主义政策侧重于国际合作和自由贸易。
The CEO outlined the company's globalistic vision, emphasizing expansion into new markets. 首席执行官概述了公司的全球主义愿景，强调向新市场扩张。
近义词:global, international, worldwide, cosmopolitan
反义词:nationalistic, isolationist, protectionist
词性:形容词
