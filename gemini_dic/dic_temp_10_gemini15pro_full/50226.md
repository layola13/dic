depend:/dɪˈpɛnd/| v. |依靠，依赖，取决于|To rely on someone or something for support, help, or supply; to be contingent on.|例句：The success of the project depends on everyone's cooperation. 这个项目的成功取决于每个人的合作。|近义词：rely, count on, hinge on|反义词：support, sustain|词性:动词

depend:/dɪˈpɛnd/| v. |信赖，信任|To have trust or confidence in someone or something.|例句：You can depend on me to keep your secret. 你可以相信我会保守你的秘密。|近义词：trust, believe in|反义词：distrust, doubt|词性:动词
