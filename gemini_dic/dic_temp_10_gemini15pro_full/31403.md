chargeless:/ˈtʃɑːrdʒləs/| adj.|无电荷的，中性的|Having no electric charge; neutral.|The neutron is a chargeless particle. 中子是不带电的粒子。|The experiment required a chargeless environment. 实验需要一个无电荷的环境。|近义词：neutral, uncharged|反义词: charged, electrified|词性:形容词
词源: charge + -less.  "charge" comes from Old French "charger" meaning "to load."  "-less" is an Old English suffix meaning "without."
