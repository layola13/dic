chirological:/ˌkaɪrəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
手相学的
Relating to or based on chirology; of or relating to the study of the hands.
Chirological analysis suggests a strong personality.  手相分析表明他性格很强。
Chirological readings were once a popular form of fortune-telling. 手相解读曾经是一种流行的算命方式。
Synonyms:  palmistry,  palm reading
Antonyms:  None

词性: adjective
