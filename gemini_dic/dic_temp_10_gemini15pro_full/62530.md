endurers:/ɪnˈdjʊərərz/
noun
忍受者，坚忍不拔的人
People who endure hardship or pain; those who can tolerate difficult situations.
The endurers of the harsh winter emerged stronger in the spring. 经历了严冬的磨难，坚忍不拔的人们在春天变得更加强大。
synonyms: sufferers, tolerators, survivors, persisters
antonyms: quitters, weaklings
