mismeasured:/ˌmɪsˈmeʒərd/
| adj. | 测量错误的，尺寸不准的 | Incorrectly measured; having the wrong measurements. | The tailor mismeasured the fabric and the suit was too small.  裁缝量错了布料，所以西装太小了。| 近义词: wrongly measured, inaccurately measured | 反义词: accurately measured, correctly measured |词性:形容词


mismeasured:/ˌmɪsˈmeʒərd/
| v. |  错误地测量，量错 | To measure incorrectly. |  He mismeasured the room and bought the wrong size carpet. 他量错了房间的尺寸，所以买了尺寸错误的地毯。 | 近义词: measured wrongly, measured inaccurately | 反义词: measured accurately, measured correctly | 词性:动词


词源:

"mis-" (prefix):  Old English "mis-" meaning "wrongly," "badly," "unsuitably."
"measured" (past participle of "measure"):  From Old French "mesurer," ultimately from Latin "mēnsūra" meaning "measure."
