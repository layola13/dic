mule:/mjʊl/ | n. | 骡子；杂交动物；固执的人；拖鞋 | A hybrid offspring of a male donkey and a female horse, known for its strength and surefootedness. Also, a stubborn person or a type of backless slipper. | The farmer used a mule to carry heavy loads up the mountain. 农民用骡子把重物驮到山上。| synonyms: hybrid, crossbreed, donkey, obstinate person, slipper | antonyms: horse, donkey, compliant person | noun

mule:/mjʊl/ | v. |  惩罚；拖；拉；苦干 | To punish, tow, pull, or work hard. (This verb form is less common.) |  He muled the heavy equipment across the field. 他在田野里拖着沉重的设备。 | synonyms: tow, haul, drag,  punish,  labor  | antonyms: assist, push,  reward, rest | verb
