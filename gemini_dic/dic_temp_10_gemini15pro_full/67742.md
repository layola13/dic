eyeballed:/aɪˈbɔːld/| v. |粗略估计，目测|To look at something or someone, often in a quick or casual way, especially to make a judgment or assessment.|I eyeballed the distance across the river. 我目测了一下河的宽度。|Synonyms: estimate, gauge, assess, appraise|Antonyms: measure, calculate|verb

eyeballed:/aɪˈbɔːld/| adj. |目测的|Estimated or assessed visually.|The eyeballed measurements were surprisingly accurate. 目测的尺寸竟然惊人的准确。|Synonyms: estimated, approximate|Antonyms: measured, precise|adjective
