memorable:/ˈmɛmərəbl/ (US), /ˈmɛmrəbl/ (UK)| adj.|值得纪念的；难忘的|Worth remembering; not easily forgotten. It describes something so special or significant that it stays in your memory.|例句：It was a memorable trip to Europe. 这是一次令人难忘的欧洲之旅。

例句：Her wedding day was a memorable occasion. 她的婚礼是一个值得纪念的日子。


近义词：unforgettable, noteworthy, significant, remarkable|反义词:forgettable, unremarkable, insignificant|词性:adjective
