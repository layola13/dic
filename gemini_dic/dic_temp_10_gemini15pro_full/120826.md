misauthorize:/ˌmɪsˈɔːθəraɪz/| vt.|未授权，错误授权|To grant authorization incorrectly or without proper authority. To authorize something that should not have been authorized. |The bank teller misauthorized a large withdrawal from the customer's account. 银行出纳员错误地授权从客户账户中提取了大量资金。

The software update misauthorized access to sensitive data. 软件更新错误地授权访问敏感数据。|Near Synonyms:  misapprove, wrongly authorize,  unlawfully authorize|Antonyms: authorize, approve, sanction|Verb
