nondiscretionary:/ˌnɑːndɪˈskreʃəneri/ | adj. | 非酌情性的，强制性的 | Not subject to individual judgment or choice; mandatory. |例句：Nondiscretionary spending includes entitlement programs like Social Security. 非酌情性支出包括社会保障之类的福利项目。

例句：The company has a nondiscretionary policy on employee safety. 公司对员工安全有一项强制性政策。


近义词：mandatory, obligatory, compulsory, required | 反义词：discretionary, optional, elective | 词性: adjective
