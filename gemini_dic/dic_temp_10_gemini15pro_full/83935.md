half-clear:/ˌhɑːf ˈklɪər/| adj.|半晴朗的，半透明的|Partially clear; not completely opaque.  Refers to something that is translucent or to weather conditions where the sky is partly clear and partly cloudy.|The sky was half-clear, with patches of blue showing through the clouds. 天空半晴朗，朵朵白云间露出片片蓝色。|The liquid was half-clear, allowing some light to pass through.  这种液体是半透明的，可以让一些光线透过。|Synonyms: partly clear, semi-transparent, hazy|Antonyms: overcast, opaque, murky|adjective
