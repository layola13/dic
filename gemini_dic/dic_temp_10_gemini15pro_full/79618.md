gloser:/ˈɡloʊsər/ | n. | 注釋者，注释；词汇表 | A person who writes glosses or glossaries; a collection of glosses or explanations of words or passages of a text. |  Example 1: The gloser meticulously explained the archaic terms in the manuscript.  这位注释者一丝不苟地解释了手稿中的古语。

Example 2: The gloser provided helpful annotations for the difficult poem.  注释者为这首难懂的诗提供了有益的注释。 | Synonyms: annotator, commentator, expounder, lexicographer | Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, could consider obfuscator, someone who makes things unclear) | noun
