fossilising:/ˈfɒsəlaɪzɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈfɑːsəlaɪzɪŋ/ (US)| v. | 使…成化石；僵化；使…过时 |  The process of becoming a fossil; becoming rigid and unchanging in attitudes or behaviour. | The dinosaur bones were fossilised over millions of years. | 近义词: petrifying, hardening, ossifying | 反义词: modernizing, evolving, developing | 词性: 动词

fossilising:/ˈfɒsəlaɪzɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈfɑːsəlaɪzɪŋ/ (US)| adj. |  使…成化石的；僵化的 |  Relating to or causing fossilization; making something rigid and unchanging. | The fossilised remains provided valuable insights into prehistoric life. | 近义词: petrifying, hardening, ossifying | 反义词: modernizing, evolving, developing | 词性: 形容词 (rarely used)
