hyperduly:/ˌhaɪpərˈduːli/ | adv. | 过分地，过度地 | Excessively; more than is necessary or proper.  From "hyper-" (over, excessive) + "duly" (properly, appropriately).  The word suggests an excessive adherence to formality or propriety, often to a ridiculous degree. | He thanked her hyperduly for her small kindness. 他对她的小小善意表示了过分的感谢。|  He was hyperduly polite, bowing deeply and using overly formal language. 他过分客气，深深鞠躬，使用过于正式的语言。 | 近义词: excessively, overly, unduly, too much | 反义词: insufficiently, inadequately, appropriately | 词性: 副词
