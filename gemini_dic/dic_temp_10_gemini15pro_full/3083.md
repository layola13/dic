aeronautical:/ˌɛrəˈnɔːtɪkəl/
adj.
航空的
Relating to the design, building, and flying of aircraft.
He's an aeronautical engineer. 他是一位航空工程师。

Aeronautical research has led to significant advancements in flight technology. 航空研究带来了飞行技术的重大进步。

近义词:aviation, aerial, flight
反义词:N/A
词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French aéronautique, from aéro- + -nautique (from Greek nautes ‘sailor’).
