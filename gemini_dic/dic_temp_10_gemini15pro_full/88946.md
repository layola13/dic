hole's:/hoʊlz/| n.|洞的；孔的|The possessive form of "hole," meaning belonging to or associated with a hole.|例句：The fox disappeared into the hole's darkness. 狐狸消失在洞穴的黑暗中。

例句：The hole's depth was surprising. 这个洞的深度令人惊讶。|近义词：cavity's, pit's, opening's|反义词: mound's, hill's|词性:名词所有格
