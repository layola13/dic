cheti: /ˈtʃeti/ | n. | 证书; 执照; 凭证 | A document certifying something.  A formal document testifying to facts or events, especially of a person's qualifications or achievements. | Example:  She received her teaching cheti after completing the course. 她完成课程后获得了教师证书。

Example: He needed a cheti to prove his qualifications. 他需要一张证书来证明他的资格。| Synonym: certificate, license, diploma, credential | Antonym: disqualification | Noun
