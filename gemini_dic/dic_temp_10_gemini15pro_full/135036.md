nonnihilism:/ˌnɒnˈnaɪɪlɪzəm/ | n. |非虚无主义 |The philosophy of non-nihilism asserts that there is meaning and value in life. |  Non-nihilism offers a counterpoint to the bleakness of nihilism. 非虚无主义为虚无主义的凄凉提供了一个对照点。| synonyms:  anti-nihilism,  existentialism (in some forms), humanism (in some forms) | antonyms: nihilism | noun
