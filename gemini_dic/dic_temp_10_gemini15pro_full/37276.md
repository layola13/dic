commandant:/ˌkɒmənˈdɑːnt/ /ˌkɑːmənˈdɑːnt/| n.|主管，负责人|The commandant of the prison was responsible for the safety of the inmates.|监狱主管负责囚犯的安全。|synonyms: director, superintendent, manager, administrator|antonyms: subordinate, follower|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French commandant, present participle of commander "to command," from Latin commendare "to entrust, recommend, praise," from com- "together" + mandare "to entrust, order."
