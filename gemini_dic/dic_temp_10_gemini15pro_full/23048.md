bounceable:/ˈbaʊnsəbl/| adj.|有弹性的，可弹跳的|Something that is bounceable is able to bounce or be bounced.  It springs back when dropped or hit against something.|The children loved the bounceable castle at the party. 孩子们喜欢派对上的充气城堡。|近义词：resilient, springy, elastic|反义词:rigid, stiff|词性:形容词
Etymology: From bounce + -able. "Bounce" comes from Middle Dutch *būnsen "to beat, strike."
