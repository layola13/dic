inconnexedly:/ˌɪnkəˈnɛktɪdli/
adverb
不连贯地，无联系地
in a disconnected or incoherent manner; without logical connection.
He spoke inconnexedly, jumping from one topic to another. 他说话不连贯，从一个话题跳到另一个话题。
The events of that day seemed to occur inconnexedly. 那天发生的事情似乎毫无关联。
Synonyms: disjointedly, ramblingly, incoherently, disconnectedly
Antonyms: coherently, connectedly, logically, systematically
词性: 副词
