aniseed:/ˈænɪsiːd/ | n. | 茴香籽；茴香酒 | The seed of the anise plant, used as a flavouring. An alcoholic drink flavoured with aniseed. | I added a pinch of aniseed to the cookies. 我在饼干里加了一撮茴香籽。 | synonyms: anise, fennel seed | antonyms: None | noun
