foredoom:/fɔːrˈduːm/
verb
注定失败，预先注定
to destine beforehand, especially to an unhappy fate; predestine.
His reckless driving foredoomed him to an early death. 他鲁莽的驾驶预示着他会早逝。
近义词: predestine, condemn, doom
反义词: bless, save
词性: verb
