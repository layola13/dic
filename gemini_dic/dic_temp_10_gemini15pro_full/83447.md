habitats:/ˈhæbɪtæts/
n.
栖息地，生境
The natural home or environment of an animal, plant, or other organism.
Polar bears are losing their habitats due to climate change.  北极熊由于气候变化正在失去它们的栖息地。

The rainforest is a habitat for a wide variety of species.  雨林是多种物种的栖息地。

近义词:
environments, homes, territories, domains
反义词:
none

词性:
noun (plural)
