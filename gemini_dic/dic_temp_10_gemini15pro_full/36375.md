coinhabitant:/ˌkoʊɪnˈhæbɪtənt/ |n.|同居者；共同居住者|A person or animal that lives in the same dwelling or habitat as another.|例句：The two coinhabitants shared a small apartment in the city. 这两个同居者在城市里合租了一间小公寓。

例句：The monkeys and birds are coinhabitants of the rainforest. 猴子和鸟类是雨林的共同居住者。|近义词：roommate, housemate, cohabitater, dweller, resident|反义词：stranger, foreigner|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From co- (together) + inhabitant (one who dwells in a place).  The word "inhabitant" comes from the Latin "inhabitare," meaning "to dwell in."
