heteronymic:/ˌhɛtərəˈnɪmɪk/| adj.|异名的，同形异音异义的|Having different sounds and meanings but the same spelling as another word.|The words "lead" (the metal) and "lead" (to guide) are heteronymic.|近义词：heteronymous|反义词：homonymic, homonymous|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ἕτερος (héteros, “other”) + ὄνομα (ónoma, “name”).
