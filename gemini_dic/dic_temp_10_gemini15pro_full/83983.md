half-crumbled|adj.|半碎的，部分破碎的|Partially broken into small fragments or pieces; not completely crumbled.|The half-crumbled wall stood as a testament to the earthquake. 半 crumbling 的墙壁是地震的证明。|The half-crumbled cookie was still delicious. 半碎的饼干仍然很美味。|Synonyms: partially broken, partly disintegrated, fragmenting|Antonyms: whole, intact, solid
