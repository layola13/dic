bumf:/bʌmf/
noun
廢紙；無聊的文件
useless or tedious printed documents
The desk was covered in bumf.  桌子被废纸覆盖了。
近义词:paperwork, documents, red tape
反义词:essential documents, important papers
词性:n.

Etymology:
Origin uncertain; perhaps related to "bum-fodder" (toilet paper), implying worthlessness.
