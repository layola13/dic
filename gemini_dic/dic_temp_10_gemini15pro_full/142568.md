ETYMOLOGY->
From osteo- (bone) + arthr- (joint) + -itis (inflammation).  The plural form "osteoarthritises" is less common than the singular "osteoarthritis" when referring to the general condition. The plural is more often used when discussing distinct instances of the disease in multiple joints or multiple individuals.
