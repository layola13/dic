intelligibility:/ɪnˌtɛlɪdʒəˈbɪləti/| n. |可理解性，清晰度|The quality of being easy to understand.  This can refer to speech, writing, or any other form of communication.|The intelligibility of her speech was hampered by a heavy accent. 她的讲话由于浓重的口音而难以理解。| clarity, comprehensibility, understandability | unintelligibility, incomprehensibility | noun
