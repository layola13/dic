biflagellate:/baɪˈflædʒəˌleɪt/
adjective
双鞭毛的
Having two flagella.
Example:  The biflagellate algae moved quickly through the water. 双鞭毛藻在水中快速移动。

Example:  Scientists studied the movement of the biflagellate organism under a microscope. 科学家在显微镜下研究了双鞭毛藻生物的运动。

近义词: two-flagellated
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider: multiflagellate, aflagellate)
词性: adjective


biflagellate:/baɪˈflædʒəˌleɪt/
noun
双鞭毛生物
An organism that has two flagella.
Example:  The biflagellate was identified as a new species.  这种双鞭毛生物被鉴定为一个新物种。

Example:  Researchers observed several biflagellates in the sample. 研究人员在样本中观察到 several 双鞭毛生物。

近义词: (No direct synonym, perhaps describe the specific type of organism, e.g., biflagellate algae)
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider: multiflagellate organism, aflagellate organism)
词性: noun


词源：bi- (two) + flagellate (having flagella).  From Latin "bi-" meaning "two" and "flagellum" meaning "whip".
