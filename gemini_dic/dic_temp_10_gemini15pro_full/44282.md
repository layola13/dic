cronstedtite:/ˈkrɒnstɛdˌtaɪt/ | n. | 克朗斯泰矿 | A dark-greenish black hydrated iron silicate mineral. It is a member of the serpentine group. | Cronstedtite is a relatively rare mineral found in metamorphic rocks. 克朗斯泰矿是一种相对稀有的矿物，发现于变质岩中。| Cronstedtite can be identified by its distinct hexagonal crystal structure. 克朗斯泰矿可以通过其独特的六方晶体结构来识别。 | synonyms: serpentine (general group), chamosite (similar mineral) | antonyms: None (mineral names generally don't have antonyms) | noun
