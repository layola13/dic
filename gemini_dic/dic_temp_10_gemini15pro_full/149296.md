ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English _hwæt_, neuter of _hwā_ ("who").  Cognate with Old Frisian _hwat_, Old Saxon _hwat_, Old High German _waz_, Old Norse _hvat_, Gothic _ƕa_.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *_hwat_, from Proto-Indo-European *_kʷod_.
