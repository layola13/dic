escorting: /ɪˈskɔːrtɪŋ/ /ɛˈskɔrtɪŋ/
verb
护送；陪同；伴随
The act of accompanying someone or something, especially for protection or guidance.
The president's car was seen escorting by several police motorcycles. 总统的汽车由几辆警用摩托车护送。
She escorted him to the door. 她把他送到门口。

近义词: accompanying, attending, guiding, shepherding, convoying
反义词: abandoning, deserting, leaving

词性: verb (present participle of escort)
词源: From Old French escorte "escort, convoy," from Italian scorta, from scorgere "to guide, lead," literally "to perceive, discern," from Latin ex "out" + corrigere "to correct, set right"
