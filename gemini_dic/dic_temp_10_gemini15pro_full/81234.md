grandsons: /ˈɡrændsʌnz/
n.
孙子们；外孙们
A son of one's son or daughter.
My two grandsons are coming to visit me next week. 我的两个孙子下周要来看我。

近义词: descendants, offspring
反义词: ancestors, forefathers
词性: 名词(复数)

词源:
From Middle English *grandsones*, from Old French *grandson*, from *grand* (“big, great”) + *son* (“son”). Equivalent to *grand* +‎ *son*.
