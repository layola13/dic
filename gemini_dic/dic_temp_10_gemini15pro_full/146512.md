overgenerosities: /ˌoʊvərˌdʒɛnəˈrɑːsɪtiz/
noun
过度慷慨的行为；过分的慷慨
Excessive acts of generosity; extreme generosity.
His overgenerosities eventually led him to financial ruin. 他的过度慷慨最终导致他经济破产。
Synonyms: extravagances, lavishness, prodigality
Antonyms: stinginess, miserliness, parsimony
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "generosity" (from Latin "generositas," meaning "nobility of birth, kindness, generosity").
