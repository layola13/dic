incircle:/ɪnˈsɜːkəl/ (US), /ɪnˈsɜːkl/ (UK)| n. |内切圆, 内接圆|A circle that touches all sides of a polygon internally.  It is the largest possible circle that can be contained within the polygon.|The incircle of the triangle was small.  这个三角形的内切圆很小。|近义词: inscribed circle|反义词: circumcircle|词性: noun
