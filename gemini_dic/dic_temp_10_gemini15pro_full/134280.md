noninhabitabilities:/ˌnɒnɪnhæbɪtəˈbɪlɪtiz/
|n.| 非宜居性；无法居住的状态|The noninhabitabilities of the planet made colonization impossible. 这个星球的非宜居性使得殖民不可能。|
| 英文解析: The state or quality of being uninhabitable; the multiple factors that make a place unsuitable for habitation.  It often refers to a combination of environmental factors, such as extreme temperatures, lack of resources, or toxic conditions.|
| 例句：The report detailed the noninhabitabilities of the newly discovered planet, citing its toxic atmosphere and extreme surface temperatures.  报告详细描述了新发现的行星的非宜居性，列举了它有毒的大气和极端的表面温度。|

| 例句：The noninhabitabilities of the desert region are due to the scarcity of water and extreme heat.  沙漠地区的非宜居性是由于缺水和酷热造成的。|

| 近义词：uninhabitableness, unlivability|
| 反义词：habitability, livability|
| 词性: Noun|
