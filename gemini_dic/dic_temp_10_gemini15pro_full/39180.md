congee:/ˈkɒndʒiː/ (UK) /ˈkɑːndʒiː/ (US)| n.|粥，稀饭|A type of rice porridge or gruel boiled and served hot, often with other ingredients such as meat, fish, or vegetables.|例句：I love having congee for breakfast. 我喜欢早餐吃粥。

例句：She made a delicious chicken congee for dinner. 她晚饭做了一锅美味的鸡肉粥。


|近义词：porridge, gruel|反义词:|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Tamil கஞ்சி (kañci, “boiled rice, gruel, water in which rice has been boiled”).
