discouragement:/dɪsˈkʌrɪdʒmənt/ | n. | 沮丧，气馁；令人沮丧的事物 |  The lack of funding was a major discouragement to the researchers.  缺乏资金对研究人员来说是一个主要的打击。 | Example:  His constant criticism led to a feeling of discouragement in the team. 他不断的批评导致团队成员感到沮丧。  | Synonyms:  dejection, despondency, disappointment, dispiritedness | Antonyms: encouragement, inspiration, motivation | noun


discourage:/dɪsˈkʌrɪdʒ/ | vt. | 使气馁，使沮丧；劝阻 |  The rain discouraged us from going on a picnic. 雨使我们打消了去野餐的念头。 | Example:  The coach discouraged the players from arguing with the referee. 教练劝球员不要与裁判争吵。  | Synonyms: deter, dissuade, hinder | Antonyms: encourage, inspire, motivate | verb
