conniption:/kəˈnɪpʃən/
n.
一阵愤怒或歇斯底里; 发脾气
a sudden fit of anger or hysterics
She threw a conniption when she found out her car had been towed.  她发现她的车被拖走了，大发雷霆。
fit, tantrum, outburst
calm, composure
noun

n.
(conniption的所有格形式)
possessive form of conniption
