esg:/iː ɛs ˈdʒiː/ | n. | 环境、社会和治理 (缩写) |  Environmental, Social, and Governance (ESG) criteria are a set of standards for a company’s operations that socially conscious investors use to screen potential investments. | Many investors are now considering ESG factors when making investment decisions. 许多投资者现在在做出投资决策时都会考虑ESG因素。

The company improved its ESG rating by reducing its carbon footprint. 该公司通过减少碳足迹提高了其ESG评级。 | 近义词: sustainability, corporate social responsibility (CSR) | 反义词: unethical business practices | abbreviation
