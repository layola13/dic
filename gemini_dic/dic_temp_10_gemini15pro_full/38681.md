condensability:/kənˌdensəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可凝结性；凝结能力 | The ability of a substance to be condensed, usually from a gas or vapor to a liquid or solid state. | The condensability of water vapor is crucial for the formation of clouds. 水蒸气的可凝结性对于云的形成至关重要。 |  The condensability of the gas allowed it to be easily liquefied for storage. 这种气体的可凝结性使其易于液化储存。 |  condensation, liquefaction | volatility | noun
