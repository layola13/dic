nonbeneficialness:/ˌnɒnbɛnɪˈfɪʃəlnəs/
noun
无益；无用
lack of benefit or usefulness.
The nonbeneficialness of the new policy was apparent after only a few months.  这项新政策的无益性在仅仅几个月后就显现出来了。
The study demonstrated the nonbeneficialness of the treatment. 这项研究证明了这种疗法的无效性。

近义词:uselessness, futility, ineffectiveness
反义词:usefulness, benefit, effectiveness
词性:noun

Etymology:
non- (prefix): not
beneficial (adjective): advantageous or helpful
-ness (suffix): forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality
