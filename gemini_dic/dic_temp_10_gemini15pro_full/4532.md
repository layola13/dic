alburnous:/ælˈbɜːrnəs/
adjective
白木质的，边材的
Relating to, or resembling, alburnum; consisting of sapwood.
The alburnous layer of the tree is softer and lighter in color.  这棵树的白木质层更柔软，颜色更浅。
The carpenter explained that the alburnous wood wasn't as strong as the heartwood. 木匠解释说，边材不如心材结实。
近义词: sapwood
反义词: heartwood
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
