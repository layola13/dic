grego:/ˈɡreɪɡoʊ/ (US), /ˈɡriːɡoʊ/ (UK)| n.| 羊群; 牧群 (尤指绵羊或山羊); 普通民众; 非犹太人|A flock or herd, especially of sheep or goats; the common people; a non-Jew. |The shepherd led his grego up the mountain. 牧羊人把他的羊群赶上山。|近义词:flock, herd, multitude, masses|反义词:elite, aristocracy|词性:名词

词源: From Spanish *grego*, from Late Latin *grex, gregis* "flock, herd," from Proto-Indo-European *ger- "to gather."  Related to English "gregarious."
