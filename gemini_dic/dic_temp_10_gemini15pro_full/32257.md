chicly:/ˈʃiːkli/| adv.| 优雅地，时髦地 |In a chic and stylish manner.  |She was chicly dressed in a black dress and pearls. 她穿着黑色连衣裙，戴着珍珠项链，显得非常优雅时髦。|Example: He chicly decorated his apartment with modern art. 他用现代艺术作品把他的公寓装饰得很时尚。|Synonyms: stylishly, fashionably, elegantly, smartly |Antonyms: dowdily, frumpily, shabbily, unfashionably |Word Class: Adverb
