furnishes:/ˈfɜːrnɪʃɪz/
verb
提供家具；装备；供应
supplies (someone) with (furniture or equipment); provides; equips
The company furnishes all its employees with laptops.  这家公司为所有员工配备了笔记本电脑。
The apartment is furnished with modern appliances.  这套公寓配备了现代化的家电。

近义词: equip, supply, provide, outfit
反义词: strip, deprive, divest
词性: verb
