family-physician:/ˈfæmɪli fɪˈzɪʃən/| n. |家庭医生|A doctor who provides comprehensive medical care to patients of all ages, focusing on preventative care and the overall health of individuals and families. |例句：My family-physician has been taking care of me since I was a child. 我的家庭医生从我还是个孩子起就一直在照顾我。

例句：I need to schedule an appointment with my family-physician for a check-up. 我需要预约我的家庭医生做一次检查。|近义词：general practitioner, primary care physician, family doctor |反义词:specialist|词性:noun

family:/ˈfæmɪli/| n. |家庭|A group of people related by blood or marriage, typically living together.  |例句：My family is very important to me. 我的家庭对我来说很重要。

例句：We had a big family gathering for Christmas.  圣诞节我们举行了一个大型家庭聚会。|近义词：relatives, kin, household |反义词:enemy|词性:noun

physician:/fɪˈzɪʃən/| n. |医生|A person qualified to practice medicine. |例句：The physician prescribed me some medication for my cough. 医生给我开了一些治咳嗽的药。

例句：I consulted a physician about my persistent headaches. 我咨询了一位医生关于我持续头痛的问题。|近义词：doctor, medical practitioner, medic |反义词:patient|词性:noun
