discanonize:/dɪsˈkænənaɪz/
v.
取消圣籍；宣布…不再正统；宣布…不再有效
To officially remove someone or something from a position of authority or legitimacy, especially in a religious or political context; to declare something no longer valid or accepted.
The council voted to discanonize the former saint due to newly discovered evidence of his misdeeds.  该委员会投票决定取消这位前圣徒的圣籍，原因是新发现了他的不法行为的证据。
The scientific community discanonized the theory after numerous experiments failed to replicate the original results. 科学界在无数次实验未能复制最初结果后，宣布该理论不再有效。
近义词:invalidate, reject, repudiate, denounce, condemn, abolish
反义词:canonize, sanctify, validate, authorize, approve
词性:verb
