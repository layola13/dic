freaks:/friːks/| n.|怪人，怪胎；狂热爱好者；畸形物|A person or thing that is markedly unusual or abnormal, especially in a disturbing way.  Can also refer to someone with an obsessive interest in something.|例句：The circus sideshow featured freaks of nature. 马戏团的杂耍表演以自然界的畸形生物为特色。

例句：He's a real fitness freak. 他是个真正的健身狂。|近义词：oddities, eccentrics, weirdos, fanatics, enthusiasts|反义词：normals, regulars|词性：名词

freak:/friːk/| v.| (使)变得反常；(使)强烈反应；吓坏了|To become or cause to become extremely emotional or irrational, often from fear, shock, or excitement.|例句：The loud noise freaked me out. 巨大的噪音把我吓坏了。

例句：He freaked when he saw the spider. 他看到蜘蛛时吓坏了。|近义词：panic, flip out, lose it|反义词：calm down, relax|词性：动词
