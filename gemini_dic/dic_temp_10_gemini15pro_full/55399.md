distensible:/dɪˈstɛnsəbəl/
adjective
可膨胀的，可扩张的
Capable of being stretched or expanded; elastic.
The lungs are distensible organs that fill with air. 肺是充满空气的可扩张器官。
A balloon is made of a distensible material. 气球是由可膨胀的材料制成的。

近义词:expandable, stretchable, elastic, flexible
反义词:inflexible, rigid, inelastic, nonelastic
词性:adjective


词源:
From Late Latin distensibilis, from Latin distendere "to stretch out," from dis- "apart" + tendere "to stretch."
