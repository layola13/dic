frolicky: /ˈfrɒlɪki/ (UK) /ˈfrɑːlɪki/ (US) | adj. |  嬉戏的，欢乐的，爱闹着玩的 | Playful and full of energy; merry. |  The lambs were frolicking in the field. 羊羔在田野里嬉戏。|  The children spent a frolicky afternoon at the beach. 孩子们在海滩上度过了一个欢乐的下午。| 近义词: playful, frisky, merry, sportive, lively | 反义词: serious, somber, subdued, quiet | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From the Middle English *frolik*, meaning "joyful, merry,"  likely related to the Dutch *vrolijk* and German *fröhlich*.
