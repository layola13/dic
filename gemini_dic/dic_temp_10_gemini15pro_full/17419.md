beamlike:/ˈbiːmlaɪk/
adjective
像光束的；光束状的
Resembling a beam of light; radiating, straight, and narrow.
A beamlike ray of light shot across the sky. 一道光束状的光线划过天空。

The lighthouse emitted a beamlike light. 灯塔发出光束状的光。
近义词：raylike, shaftlike, linear
反义词：diffuse, scattered, spread
词性：adjective
