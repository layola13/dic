fatiscence:/fætɪˈsɛns/ | n. | 开裂；裂开；成熟开裂 | The act or process of dehiscence, as the spontaneous opening at maturity of a fruit, anther, or sporangium.  The splitting open of a mature plant structure, such as a fruit, to release its seeds. | The fatiscence of the seed pod allowed the seeds to disperse. 种荚的开裂使种子得以散播。| synonym: dehiscence, splitting, bursting | antonym: closure, sealing | noun
