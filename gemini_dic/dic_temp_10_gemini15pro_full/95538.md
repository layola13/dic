incoact:/ɪnˈkoʊækt/ | adj. | 未生效的，未开始的，未启动的 | Not yet in action; not yet operative or brought into effect.  | The new law is incoact until January 1st.  新法律到1月1日才生效。 | 近义词: inactive, dormant, latent | 反义词: active, operational, effective | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  From Latin *in-*, "not" + *coactus*, past participle of *cogere*, "to compel, force, collect, gather".  Essentially meaning "not yet forced or driven into action."
