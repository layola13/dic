looser:/ˈluːsər/| adj. n.|更宽松的，更松散的；失败者|Someone or something that loses, especially repeatedly.  Less tight or firm. |The screws were looser than I expected.  这些螺丝比我预想的要松。 He felt like a loser after failing the exam. 他考试不及格后觉得自己像个失败者。|近义词：slack, baggy (adj.); failure, also-ran (n.)|反义词: tighter, winner|词性:形容词, 名词

looser:/ˈluːzər/| n.|失败者|A person or thing that loses or has lost something, especially a competition or game.  |He felt like a loser after his team lost the championship.  他所在的队输掉了冠军赛后，他觉得自己像个失败者。  She was labeled a loser by her classmates after failing the test. 她在考试不及格后被同学们称为失败者。|近义词：failure, non-winner, also-ran|反义词: winner, victor, champion|词性:名词
