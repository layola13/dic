nonsensory:/nɑːnˈsɛnsəri/
adjective
非感官的；非感觉的
Not involving or relating to the senses.
例句：The nonsensory aspects of the experience were just as important.  这次经历的非感官方面也同样重要。

例句：Nonsensory stimuli, such as thoughts and emotions, can also trigger physical responses.  非感官刺激，例如思想和情绪，也能引发身体反应。

近义词：non-sensory, extrasensory (in some contexts, referring to perception outside the known senses)
反义词：sensory, perceptual
词性：adjective
