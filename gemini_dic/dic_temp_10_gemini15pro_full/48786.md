deflected:/dɪˈflɛktɪd/ | adj. | 偏斜的，偏转的 |  turned aside from a straight course; caused to change direction | The bullet was deflected by the metal plate. 子弹被金属板偏转了。| synonyms: diverted, deviated, veered | antonyms:  straight, direct | word origin:  From Latin deflectere, from de- "from" + flectere "to bend"


deflect:/dɪˈflɛkt/ | v. |  (使)偏斜，(使)转向 | to cause something to change direction |  The goalkeeper deflected the ball over the crossbar. 守门员把球挡出了横梁。| synonyms: divert, deviate, veer | antonyms:  maintain, continue | word origin:  From Latin deflectere, from de- "from" + flectere "to bend"
