negligible:/ˈnɛɡlɪdʒəbl/| adj.|可忽略的，微不足道的|The cost was negligible compared to the potential profits. 与潜在利润相比，成本微不足道。|例句：The damage to the car was negligible. 车辆的损坏微不足道。

The difference in price was negligible. 价格差异微不足道。|近义词：insignificant, trifling, trivial|反义词：significant, substantial, considerable|词性:adjective
