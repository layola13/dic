fumarole:/ˈfjuːməˌroʊl/ (US), /ˈfjuːməˌrɒl/ (UK)| n.|喷气孔;火山喷气孔|An opening in or near a volcano, from which hot sulfurous gases emerge.|例句：The fumaroles emitted a sulphurous smell. 喷气孔散发出一股硫磺味。

例句：Scientists studied the gases emanating from the fumaroles. 科学家们研究了从喷气孔散发出的气体。

|近义词：vent, opening, geyser|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
