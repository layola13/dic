apertured:/ˈæpərtʃərd/
adjective
有孔的，开孔的
Having an aperture or apertures; perforated.
The apertured metal sheet allowed light to filter through.  有孔的金属板允许光线透过。
The camera lens uses an apertured diaphragm to control light. 相机镜头使用带孔的隔膜来控制光线。
近义词: perforated, pierced, holed
反义词: solid, unperforated, intact
词性: adjective

Etymology: From aperture + -ed.  Aperture comes from Latin *apertūra, from aperīre "to open."
