crunchingly:/ˈkrʌntʃɪŋli/| adv.|嘎吱作响地，发出嘎吱声地|Describing an action or sound that involves a loud, crisp crushing or grinding noise.|The snow crunched crunchingly under her boots. 雪在她靴子下嘎吱作响。|近义词：crisply, cracklingly|反义词:softly, quietly|词性:副词
Etymology: From "crunch" + "-ing" + "-ly".  "Crunch" likely imitates the sound it describes.
