coldest:/ˈkoʊldɪst/| adj.|最冷的|Superlative form of cold; having the lowest temperature.|This is the coldest winter in decades. 这是数十年来最冷的冬天。|近义词：frostiest, iciest, chilliest|反义词：warmest, hottest|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old English "ceald" (cold), ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*kaldaz" (cold). The "-est" suffix is a superlative marker.
