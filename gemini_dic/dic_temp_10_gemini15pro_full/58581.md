dulcimer: /ˈdʌlsɪmər/ (US), /ˈdʌlsɪmə(r)/ (UK)| n.|扬琴，一种弦乐器|A musical instrument, typically trapezoidal in shape, with strings stretched over a soundboard.  It is played by striking the strings with small hammers or by plucking them.|She played a haunting melody on her dulcimer. 她用扬琴演奏了一曲萦绕心头的旋律。|Near Synonyms: hammered dulcimer, Appalachian dulcimer, zither, psaltery|Antonyms: None (It's a specific instrument, not a concept with an opposite.)|Noun
