Ahmad: /ˈɑːmɑːd/ | n. | 阿马德 | A male given name, common in Arabic-speaking countries, meaning "most praiseworthy" or "highly praised".  It is derived from the Arabic root حَمِدَ (ḥ-m-d), meaning "to praise" or "to thank". | Ahmad is a popular name in many Muslim communities. 阿马德在许多穆斯林社区是一个流行的名字。| Synonyms: Ahmed, Muhammed, Mahmoud | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
