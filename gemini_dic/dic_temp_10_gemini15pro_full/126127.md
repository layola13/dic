mutic: /ˈmjuːtɪk/ | adj. | 多刺的，尖刺多的 | Covered with sharp points; prickly. |  The mutic rose bush was difficult to prune because of its numerous thorns. 多刺的玫瑰丛因为尖刺太多而难以修剪。| The cactus was covered in mutic spines. 仙人掌上覆盖着许多尖刺。| 近义词：spiny, thorny, prickly, barbed | 反义词：smooth, soft | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *mūticus, from mūtilus "maimed, mutilated," from the idea of pointed, as if cut short.
