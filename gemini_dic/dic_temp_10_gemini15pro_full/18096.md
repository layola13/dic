behaviouristic:/bɪˈheɪvjərɪstɪk/ | adj. | 行为主义的；行为主义派的 | Relating to behaviourism, a school of psychology that emphasizes the study of observable behaviour rather than internal mental processes. |  His approach to teaching was very behaviouristic, focusing on rewards and punishments. 他的教学方法非常行为主义，注重奖励和惩罚。| The study employed a behaviouristic methodology, observing the rats' responses to stimuli. 这项研究采用了行为主义的方法，观察老鼠对刺激的反应。| 近义词: behavioral | 反义词: cognitive, mentalistic | 词性: 形容词
