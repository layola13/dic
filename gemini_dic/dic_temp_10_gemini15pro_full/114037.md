magnesite:/ˈmæɡnəsaɪt/| n. |菱镁矿|A white, yellowish, or grayish-white mineral consisting of magnesium carbonate (MgCO3), used as a source of magnesium and in refractories.|Magnesite is used in the production of magnesium oxide. 菱镁矿用于生产氧化镁。| Magnesite bricks are used in high-temperature furnaces. 菱镁砖用于高温炉。 |Synonyms: magnesium carbonate|Antonyms: None|Noun
