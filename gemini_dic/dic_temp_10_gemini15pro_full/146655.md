overhasten:/ˌoʊvərˈheɪsən/| v. |加速；过分催促；操之过急|To hasten excessively; precipitate.  To cause something to happen too soon or too quickly.|They overhastened the project and ended up making costly mistakes. 他们过分催促这个项目，最终犯了代价高昂的错误。|The general overhastened the attack and suffered heavy losses. 将军操之过急地发动了进攻，遭受了严重的损失。|近义词：rush, precipitate, accelerate, expedite|反义词：delay, postpone, hinder, retard|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + hasten.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much." "Hasten" comes from the Middle English "hasten," from Old Norse "hasta," meaning "to hurry."
