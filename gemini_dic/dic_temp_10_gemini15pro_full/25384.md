bulbose:/ˈbʌlbəs/| adj.|球茎状的，球根状的；膨胀的，肿大的|Resembling a bulb, especially in roundness and swelling.|The plant has a bulbose root.  这种植物有球茎状的根。|The onion's bulbose shape makes it easy to grip. 洋葱的球茎状使其易于抓握。|近义词：bulbous, swollen, rounded|反义词：slender, thin, narrow|词性:形容词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin bulbosus, from Latin bulbus ‘bulb’.
