macho:/ˈmɑːtʃoʊ/ (US) /ˈmætʃəʊ/ (UK)| adj. n.|男子气概的，大男子主义的；大男子主义者|Showing exaggerated masculinity; aggressively assertive.|He has a very macho attitude. 他有一种非常大男子主义的态度。|He's too macho to admit he was hurt. 他太 macho 了，不肯承认自己受伤了。|近义词：masculine, manly, virile|反义词：feminine, effeminate|词性:形容词, 名词

Etymology: From Spanish *macho* "male animal," from Late Latin *masculus*, from Latin *mas* "male."
