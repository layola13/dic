一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she like to do in her free time? 她空闲时间喜欢做什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are they doing right now? 他们现在在做什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? 他晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned so far? 你到目前为止学到了什么？
What has she accomplished this year? 她今年完成了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading this week? 他这周一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? 她对这部电影说了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was he thinking about during the meeting? 开会时他在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? 她生病前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I arrived? 我来之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been discussing for hours? 他们已经讨论几个小时了，讨论的是什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? 你下周末要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? 他们下次会议将讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have finished by the end of the day? 你今天结束前会完成什么？
What will she have achieved by next year? 到明年她会取得什么成就？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by then? 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
What will he have been studying by the time he graduates? 到他毕业时，他将已经学习什么多久了？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
If I were you, what would I do in this situation? 如果我是你，在这种情况下我会做什么？

被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? 法国的首都是哪里？
What are your plans for the summer? 你暑假的计划是什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. 我不知道该做什么。
She didn't tell me what she wanted. 她没告诉我她想要什么。
