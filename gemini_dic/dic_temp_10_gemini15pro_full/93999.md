impale:/ɪmˈpeɪl/| vt.|刺穿，钉住|To pierce with a sharp stake or point.|The knight impaled the dragon with his lance. 骑士用他的长矛刺穿了龙。|近义词：pierce, transfix, spear|反义词:extricate, remove|verb

impale:/ɪmˈpeɪl/| vt.|使陷入困境，使处于尴尬境地|To put in a difficult or embarrassing situation.|His inappropriate comment impaled him in an awkward silence.  他不得体的评论让他陷入了尴尬的沉默。|近义词：trap, corner|反义词:free, liberate|verb
