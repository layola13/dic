overpreciseness:/ˌoʊvərprɪˈsaɪsnəs/
n.|过度精确; 过于严谨|The state of being excessively precise or exact.  Characterized by an undue emphasis on minor details and a rigid adherence to rules.|His overpreciseness made him difficult to work with, as he would get bogged down in insignificant details.|meticulousness, fastidiousness, punctiliousness|laxity, carelessness|noun
