coaxes:/ˈkoʊksɪz/ (US), /ˈkəʊksɪz/ (UK)| v.|哄劝；劝诱|To persuade someone gently and patiently to do something.|例句：She coaxed the child to take the medicine. 她哄着孩子吃药。

例句：He coaxed a smile from her. 他哄得她笑了。

近义词：persuade, cajole, wheedle, entice|反义词：dissuade, discourage|词性：动词
