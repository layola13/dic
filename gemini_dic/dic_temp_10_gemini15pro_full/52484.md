dietician:/ˌdaɪəˈtɪʃən/ /ˌdaɪəˈtɪʃn/| n. |营养师，营养学家|A health professional who is trained to give advice about food and eating. |例句：The dietician recommended a low-sodium diet. 营养师建议低钠饮食。

例句：I consulted a dietician about improving my eating habits. 我咨询了一位营养师关于改善我的饮食习惯。|近义词：nutritionist, dietitian |反义词: (无直接反义词) |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek diaita "mode of life, diet" + -ician.
