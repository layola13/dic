partiers:/ˈpɑːrtiərz/
n.
参加聚会的人，寻欢作乐的人
People who attend and enjoy parties, often characterized by a focus on socializing, drinking, and having fun.
The partiers danced until dawn.  参加聚会的人跳舞跳到天亮。
The hotel was full of noisy partiers.  酒店里满是吵闹的寻欢作乐的人。
近义词:revelers, celebrants, carousers
反义词:introverts, homebodies
词性:名词
