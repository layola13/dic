nonoxygenous:/ˌnɑːnˌɑːksɪˈdʒɛnəs/
adjective
非产氧的
Not producing oxygen; anaerobic.  Referring to photosynthetic bacteria that do not produce oxygen as a byproduct of photosynthesis.
Nonoxygenous photosynthetic bacteria use other molecules as electron donors, such as hydrogen sulfide. 非产氧光合细菌使用其他分子作为电子供体，例如硫化氢。
Purple sulfur bacteria are nonoxygenous phototrophs. 紫色硫细菌是非产氧光养生物。
近义词:anaerobic
反义词:oxygenic
