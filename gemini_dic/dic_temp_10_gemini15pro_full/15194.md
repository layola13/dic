backchannel:/ˈbækˌtʃænəl/ | n. | 幕后沟通；私下交流；(尤指会议中)私下交谈；(谈话中的)附和，回应 |  A secondary or covert route of communication.  A form of feedback that listeners give to speakers during a conversation. This feedback can be verbal (e.g., "uh-huh," "right") or nonverbal (e.g., nodding, smiling). | During the meeting, there was a lot of backchannel chatter among the attendees. 会议期间，与会者之间有很多私下交谈。 | synonyms: side conversation, murmur, whisper, feedback, acknowledgment | antonyms: silence, open communication | noun


backchannel:/ˈbækˌtʃænəl/ | adj. | 幕后的；私下的；秘密的 | Conducted privately or through an indirect route. |  The negotiators used a backchannel to communicate with each other. 谈判代表使用幕后渠道相互沟通。 | synonyms: private, covert, secret, confidential | antonyms: public, open, transparent | adjective
