disputant:/dɪˈspjuːtənt/ /dɪˈspjutənt/
n.| 争论者，辩论者 |A person who takes part in a dispute; someone who argues.|The disputants could not agree on a solution.  争论者无法就解决方案达成一致。|The two disputants finally agreed to binding arbitration. 这两位争论者最终同意进行具有约束力的仲裁。| synonyms:  arguer, debater, contestant, litigant, opponent | antonyms:  supporter, ally, proponent | noun
