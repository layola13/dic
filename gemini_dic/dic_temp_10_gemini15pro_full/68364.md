fallfish:/ˈfɔːlfɪʃ/| n. |秋鱼，落鱼|A North American freshwater fish (Semotilus corporalis) of the carp family, abundant in streams and rivers.  It is silvery with a dark stripe along its side, becoming bronze in the breeding season. |The fallfish is a common game fish in the eastern United States. 秋鱼是美国东部常见的游钓鱼。|近义词：chub, dace|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific fish species) |noun
