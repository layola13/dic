passeres:/ˈpæsərɪz/ /ˈpæsəˌriz/ | n. |雀形目鸟类|A large order of birds comprising over half of all known species, characterized by perching feet with three toes pointing forward and one backward, and including songbirds, sparrows, finches, crows, etc. |例句：The passeres are the most diverse order of birds. 雀形目是最多样化的鸟类目。

例句：Many passeres are known for their beautiful songs. 许多雀形目鸟类以其优美的歌声而闻名。|近义词：songbirds, perching birds|反义词: (No specific antonym, perhaps non-passerine birds) |词性: noun (plural)
