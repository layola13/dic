overawed:/ˌəʊvərˈɔːd/| adj.|敬畏的，慑服的| Filled with respect and wonder, often mixed with a slight fear; intimidated.|例句：The young musician was overawed by the famous conductor.  年轻的音乐家对这位著名的指挥家感到敬畏。

例句：She was overawed by the grandeur of the cathedral. 她被大教堂的宏伟所震撼。|近义词：awestruck, intimidated, daunted|反义词：unimpressed, bold, audacious|词性:形容词

词源: From "overawe," a verb formed from "over-" (excessive) and "awe" (respectful fear).
