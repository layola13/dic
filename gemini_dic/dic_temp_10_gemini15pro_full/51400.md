determiner:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnər/ | determiner | 限定词 | A word (such as "a," "the," "some," "any," "my") that comes before a noun to show how the noun is being used. |  Example: "The cat sat on the mat." (Here, "the" is the determiner.) | Synonyms:  article, quantifier | Antonyms: N/A | word class: determiner

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What book are you reading? = 你正在读什么书？
The dog always chases the ball. = 这只狗总是追着球跑。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
My sister is reading a fascinating book right now. = 我妹妹现在正在读一本引人入胜的书。
They are playing their favorite game. = 他们正在玩他们最喜欢的游戏。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have finished reading that book. = 我已经读完了那本书。
She has visited several countries this year. = 她今年已经访问了几个国家。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been reading this book all day. = 我一整天都在读这本书。
He has been working on this project for months. = 他几个月来一直在做这个项目。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
