hippodrome:/ˈhɪpəˌdroʊm/| n. |跑马场，竞技场|An oval stadium for chariot and horse races, or other similar spectacles.|The hippodrome was packed with cheering spectators.|synonyms: racetrack, stadium, arena|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "theater" for a contrasting venue)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *hippodrome*, from Latin *hippodromus*, from Ancient Greek *ἱππόδρομος* (*hippódromos*), from *ἵππος* (*híppos*, “horse”) + *δρόμος* (*drómos*, “course, race”).
