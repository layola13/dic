depoliticization:/diːpəˌlɪtɪsaɪˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|去政治化，非政治化
|The process of removing political influences or considerations from something.|
|The depoliticization of education is a controversial topic.  教育的去政治化是一个有争议的话题。|
|The commission aimed for the depoliticization of the judiciary. 该委员会旨在使司法机构非政治化。|
