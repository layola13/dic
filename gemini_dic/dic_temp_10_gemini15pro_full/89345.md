homological:/ˌhɒməˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ /ˌhoʊməˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/| adj.|同源的，同调的|Relating to homology, a concept in mathematics and biology that describes structures or sequences derived from a common ancestor or having a similar relationship or structure.|Homologous chromosomes have the same genes but may have different alleles. 同源染色体具有相同的基因，但可能有不同的等位基因。|The homological structure of the bat wing and the human hand indicates a shared ancestry. 蝙蝠翅膀和人手的同源结构表明它们有共同的祖先。|analogous, similar, corresponding|dissimilar, different|adjective
