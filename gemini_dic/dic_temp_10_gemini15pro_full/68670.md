faraway:/ˌfɑːr əˈweɪ/ (US) /ˌfɑːr əˈweɪ/ (UK)| adj.|遥远的；久远的|situated at a great distance; remote in space or time|例句：She dreamt of faraway lands. 她梦想着遥远的国度。|例句：He had a faraway look in his eyes. 他的眼神看起来很茫然。|近义词：distant, remote, far-off|反义词：near, nearby, close|词性:形容词

faraway:/ˈfɑːrəˌweɪ/ (US) /ˈfɑːrəˌweɪ/ (UK)| n.|远方|a distant place or time|例句：The faraway seemed to call to her. 远方似乎在召唤她。|例句：His thoughts drifted to the faraway. 他的思绪飘向了远方。|近义词：distance|反义词：nearness, proximity|词性:名词


词源:  From *far* + *away*.  "Far" comes from Old English *feor*, from Proto-Germanic *ferra-*, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *per-*. "Away" comes from Old English *onweg*, from *on* ("on") + *weg* ("way").
