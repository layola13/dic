overballasting:/ˌoʊvərˈbæləstɪŋ/
n.
过度压载
The act of placing excessive ballast in a ship or other vessel, making it too low in the water.
The ship was dangerously overballasted and struggled in the heavy seas. 这艘船过度压载，在汹涌的大海中艰难航行。
overloading, overweighting
