demur:/dɪˈmɜːr/ (US), /dɪˈmɜː(r)/ (UK)| v. n.|反对；异议；犹豫|To express doubt or objection; to hesitate|例句：She demurred at the idea of working late. 她对加班的想法表示反对。

例句：He demurred at the high price. 他对高昂的价格表示异议。

近义词：object, hesitate, protest, resist|反义词：accept, agree, consent, acquiesce|词性：动词

demur:/dɪˈmɜːr/ (US), /dɪˈmɜː(r)/ (UK)| n.|反对；异议；犹豫|An objection or hesitation|例句：Her only demur was the cost. 她唯一的反对意见是费用。

例句：Without demur, he agreed to her request. 他毫不犹豫地同意了她的请求。

近义词：objection, hesitation, protest, resistance|反义词：acceptance, agreement, consent, acquiescence|词性：名词

词源: From Old French *demorer "delay, linger," from Latin demorari "to linger, loiter, tarry," from de- "down, completely" + morari "to delay."
