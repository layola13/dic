attesting:/əˈtɛstɪŋ/
v. |证明；证实；表明|Providing or serving as clear evidence of something.
|The lawyer presented documents attesting to his client's innocence. 律师出示了证明其委托人无罪的文件。|
|Her fluency in French attests to her years spent living in Paris. 她流利的法语证明了她在巴黎生活多年。|
|certifying, verifying, confirming, corroborating|
|denying, refuting, contradicting|
verb

attest:/əˈtɛst/
v. |证明；证实；表明|To declare or affirm solemnly as true.
|I can attest to the quality of their work. 我可以证明他们工作的质量。|
|The witness attested that she had seen the defendant at the scene of the crime.  证人证明她曾在犯罪现场看到被告。|
|certify, verify, confirm, corroborate|
