absurdness:/əbˈsɜːrdnəs/ /əbˈzɜːrdnəs/| n. |荒谬，荒诞，愚蠢|The absurdity of the situation was apparent to everyone.  这种情况的荒谬性显而易见。|例句：The sheer absurdity of his proposal made everyone laugh. 他提议的纯粹荒谬让每个人都笑了。

The absurdity of her excuse was obvious. 她借口的荒谬显而易见。|近义词：foolishness, silliness, ridiculousness, preposterousness|反义词：sensibility, reasonableness, logic|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *absurdite, from Middle French absurdité, from Latin absurditās (“absurdity, incongruity”), from absurdus (“absurd”).
