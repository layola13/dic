contain:/kənˈteɪn/| v.|包含，容纳，控制|to hold or have within; to keep within bounds or under control|This box contains old photographs. 这个盒子里面装着旧照片。|Synonyms: hold, encompass, include, comprise|Antonyms: exclude, omit, release|verb

contain:/kənˈteɪn/| v.|抑制，克制|to restrain or control (e.g., oneself, one's feelings)|She struggled to contain her laughter. 她强忍着不笑出来。|Synonyms: restrain, suppress, control, curb|Antonyms: unleash, release, express|verb
