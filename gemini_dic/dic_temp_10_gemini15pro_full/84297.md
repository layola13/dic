half-vellums:/ˌhɑːfˈvɛləmz/
n.
半羊皮纸书籍（通常指装订精美，用上等纸张印刷的书籍）
Books bound in half-vellum, often indicating a high-quality binding and paper.
The library's rare book collection includes several beautiful half-vellums.  图书馆的珍本书收藏包括几本精美的半羊皮纸书籍。
The antiquarian bookseller displayed a first edition bound in half-vellum. 古籍书商展示了一本装订成半羊皮纸的第一版书籍。
近义词:fine bindings, deluxe editions
反义词:paperbacks, cheap editions
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"half" + "vellum"
"half" (Old English healf) meaning one of two equal parts.
"vellum" (Old French velin, from Latin vitulinus "made of calf," from vitulus "calf")  referring to a fine parchment made from calfskin.
