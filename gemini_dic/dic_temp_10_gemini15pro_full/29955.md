caver:/ˈkeɪvər/| n.|洞穴探险者；挖洞者|A person who explores caves, especially as a sport.  A person who digs or makes caves.|例句：The caver carefully descended into the dark abyss.  洞穴探险者小心翼翼地进入黑暗的深渊。
