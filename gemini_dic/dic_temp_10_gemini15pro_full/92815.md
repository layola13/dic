iconodulist: /aɪˈkɒnəˌdʌlɪst/ /aɪˈkɑːnəˌdʌlɪst/
noun
破坏圣像者；反对崇拜圣像者
A person who opposes the veneration of religious images.
Martin Luther was initially an iconodulist, believing that the veneration of images bordered on idolatry. 马丁·路德最初是一个反对崇拜圣像者，认为崇敬圣像近乎偶像崇拜。
synonyms: image-breaker, iconoclast
antonyms: iconophile, iconolater
n.
