naggingnesses:/ˈnæɡɪŋnɪsɪz/
|n.|唠叨，不断抱怨|The constant naggingnesses about my weight are starting to get to me.  不断地唠叨我的体重让我开始感到厌烦。|
|英文解析: The state or quality of being nagging; persistent complaints or criticisms.|
|例句：His naggingnesses about the mess were relentless. 他对脏乱的唠叨没完没了。|
|例句：Her naggingnesses finally drove him to clean his room.  她的唠叨最终迫使他打扫房间。|
|近义词：carping, complaining, faultfinding, griping|
|反义词：praise, compliment|
|词性：名词|

词源:
From nag + -ing + -ness + -es.  "Nag" comes from Old Norse *nagga "to gnaw, bite, grumble".
