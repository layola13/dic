dings:/dɪŋz/
n.
叮当声，碰撞声
a ringing, clanging, or jingling sound.
The cash register made a series of dings as the cashier rang up the items.  收银员结账时，收银机发出了一连串叮当声。

近义词:clangs, jingles, rings
反义词:silence, quiet
