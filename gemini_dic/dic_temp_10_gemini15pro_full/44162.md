criticism:/ˈkrɪtɪsɪzəm/| n.|批评，批判；评论|The play received harsh criticism.  这出戏受到了严厉的批评。|Synonyms: censure, disapproval, condemnation|Antonyms: praise, approval, commendation|noun
criticize:/ˈkrɪtɪsaɪz/| v.|批评，批判；评论|He criticized her handling of the situation. 他批评了她对局势的处理。|Synonyms: censure, condemn, denounce|Antonyms: praise, commend, applaud|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin criticismus, from Ancient Greek κριτικισμός (kritikismós, “art of judging”), from κριτικός (kritikós, “able to discern, critical”), from κρίνω (krínō, “I separate, judge”).
