aggregator:/ˈæɡrɪɡeɪtər/| n. |聚合器，集成商，汇总者|A website or program that collects related items of content and displays them or links to them.|Price comparison websites are aggregators that gather information from many different retailers.  价格比较网站是收集来自许多不同零售商信息的聚合器。|synonyms: collector, compiler, assembler|antonyms: distributor, disperser|noun
aggregators:/ˈæɡrɪɡeɪtərz/| n. |聚合器，集成商，汇总者（复数）|Multiple websites or programs that collect related items of content and display them or links to them.|News aggregators help users stay up-to-date on current events.  新闻聚合器帮助用户及时了解时事。|synonyms: collectors, compilers, assemblers|antonyms: distributors, dispersers|noun
