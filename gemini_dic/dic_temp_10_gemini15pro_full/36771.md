collusory:/kəˈluːsəri/
| adj. |
|共谋的，串通的，假装的|
|Characterized by collusion; fraudulent; deceitful.|

|例句：The collusory bidding at the auction drove up the price artificially. 拍卖会上串通的竞价人为地抬高了价格。|

|例句：The investigators uncovered a collusory scheme between the company and its suppliers. 调查人员揭露了该公司与其供应商之间的共谋计划。|

|近义词：fraudulent, deceitful, conspiratorial, clandestine|
|反义词：genuine, honest, aboveboard|
|词性: 形容词|
