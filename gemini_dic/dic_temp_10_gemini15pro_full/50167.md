deodar:/ˈdiːoʊdɑːr/ (US), /ˌdeɪəˈdɑːr/ (UK)| n.|雪松，香柏|A large evergreen coniferous tree native to the Himalayas, having drooping branches and fragrant wood.  It is often planted as an ornamental tree. |例句：The deodar is a majestic tree that can grow to over 200 feet tall.  雪松是一种雄伟的树，可以长到200多英尺高。

例句：The fragrant wood of the deodar is highly valued for building and furniture making. 雪松的芳香木材因其建筑和家具制造而备受重视。|近义词：cedar, Himalayan cedar|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
