optimised:/ˈɒptɪmaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈɑːptɪmaɪzd/ (US)| adj.|优化的，最佳化的|Made as effective, perfect, or useful as possible.|例句：The website has been optimised for mobile viewing.  该网站已针对移动设备进行了优化。|近义词：improved, enhanced, perfected, refined|反义词: unoptimized, inefficient, ineffective|词性:形容词
optimise:/ˈɒptɪmaɪz/ (UK) /ˈɑːptɪmaɪz/ (US)| v.|优化，最佳化|Make the best or most effective use of (a situation, opportunity, or resource).|例句：We need to optimise our use of resources. 我们需要优化资源的使用。|近义词：improve, enhance, maximize, refine|反义词: worsen, impair, neglect|词性:动词
