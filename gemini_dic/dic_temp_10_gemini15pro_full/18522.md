benefaction:/ˌbenɪˈfækʃən/
n.
善行，捐赠
an act of kindness or generosity, especially a gift of money or property to a charity or other good cause
The museum was built thanks to a generous benefaction from a local businessman.  这家博物馆的建成得益于当地一位商人的慷慨捐赠。
donation, gift, contribution, endowment
malefaction, detriment, injury
noun
