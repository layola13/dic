inadvisable:/ˌɪnədˈvaɪzəbl/ | adj. |不明智的，不妥当的 | Not recommended; unwise. |  It is inadvisable to swim in the sea during a storm. 在暴风雨中游泳是不明智的。|  Driving after drinking alcohol is inadvisable.  酒后驾车是不明智的。| 近义词: unwise, imprudent, impolitic, ill-advised | 反义词: advisable, wise, prudent, sensible |词性:形容词
