motif:/moʊˈtiːf/ (US), /məʊˈtiːf/ (UK)| n. |主题; 基调; 母题;  (装饰图案中的)基本图案|A recurring subject, theme, idea, etc., especially in a literary, artistic, or musical work.  A distinctive and recurring form, shape, figure, etc., in a design, as in a painting or on wallpaper.|The wallpaper has a floral motif.  壁纸上有花卉图案。|synonyms: theme, subject, topic, idea, concept, element, leitmotif, trope, figure, pattern, design, device, image, symbol |antonyms: N/A |noun
