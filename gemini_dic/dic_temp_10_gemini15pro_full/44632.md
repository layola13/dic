crowned:/kraʊnd/| adj.|戴着王冠的；加冕的；圆满完成的；顶端的|topped with a crown; having been made a king or queen in a special ceremony; brought to a successful conclusion; at the highest part of something.|例句：The newly crowned king addressed his subjects. 新加冕的国王向他的臣民讲话。|例句：Their efforts were crowned with success. 他们的努力获得了圆满成功。|近义词：coroneted, inaugurated, capped, topped, culminating|反义词：uncrowned, dethroned|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *crouned*, past participle of *crownen*, from Old French *coroné*, past participle of *coroner*, from Late Latin *coronāre*, from Latin *corōna* ("crown, garland").
