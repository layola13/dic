pace:/peɪs/| n.  vi. vt.|步伐，速度，节奏；踱步，调整节奏|A single step taken when walking or running. The rate of movement or progress. To walk back and forth across a small area, especially because you are worried or nervous. To set the speed of something.|He paced back and forth, waiting for the news. 他来回踱步，等待着消息。|synonyms: speed, rate, tempo, stride|antonyms: slowness, inactivity|noun, verb


Etymology: From Old French *pas*, from Latin *passus* "a step, pace."
