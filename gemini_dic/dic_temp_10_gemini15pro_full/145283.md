overbounteousness:/ˌoʊvərˈbaʊntiəsnəs/ | n. | 过分慷慨，过度丰盛 | The overbounteousness of the harvest led to a surplus of food. | 例句：The overbounteousness of the harvest led to a surplus of food.  丰收的过度丰盛导致了食物过剩。

例句：Her overbounteousness in giving gifts sometimes made people uncomfortable. 她送礼物的过度慷慨有时会让人感到不舒服。 | 近义词：generosity, lavishness, profusion, exuberance | 反义词：stinginess, meanness, scarcity, meagerness | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + bounteous + -ness.  "Over-" signifies "excessive" or "too much." "Bounteous" means "generous and plentiful."  "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.  Therefore, "overbounteousness" literally means the state of being excessively generous or plentiful.
