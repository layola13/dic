glumella:/ɡluːˈmelə/| n.|颖片，小花的内稃|The glumella is the inner bract of a floret in grasses.|The glumella of this species is particularly small.  这种植物的内稃特别小。|Synonyms: palea, inner bract|Antonyms: lemma (sometimes considered the opposite as the outer bract), |noun
Etymology: From Latin *gluma* (“husk”) + *-ella* (diminutive suffix).  Related to "glume".
