excruciate:/ɪkˈskruːʃieɪt/ |v. |折磨，使极度痛苦|To inflict severe pain or agony upon; to torture. |The interrogators excruciated the prisoner for days, trying to extract a confession. 审讯者连续几天折磨囚犯，试图逼供。

excruciating:/ɪkˈskruːʃieɪtɪŋ/ |adj. |极度痛苦的，折磨人的|Extremely painful; agonizing. |She suffered excruciating pain after the surgery. 手术后她遭受了极大的痛苦。

The movie was so bad it was an excruciating experience. 这部电影太糟糕了，简直是一场折磨。|近义词: agonizing, torturous, unbearable|反义词: pleasant, comfortable, soothing|词性:形容词
