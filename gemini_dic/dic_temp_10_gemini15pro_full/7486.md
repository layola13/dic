ankylosaurs:/ˈæŋkɪləˌsɔːrz/ | n. | 甲龙类 | Ankylosaurs were a group of herbivorous dinosaurs characterized by heavily armored bodies and, often, a club-like tail.  They lived during the Cretaceous period. |  Ankylosaurs were well-protected from predators. 甲龙类对捕食者有很好的防御能力。|  Synonym: armored dinosaurs | Antonym: (No direct antonym, but perhaps lightly-armored dinosaurs could be considered a contrasting group) | noun
