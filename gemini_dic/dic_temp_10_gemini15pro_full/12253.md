ascendible:/əˈsɛndəbl/
adj.
可攀登的，可上升的
capable of being ascended; climbable.
例句：The mountain is ascendible, but it's a challenging climb. 这座山可以攀登，但这是一次具有挑战性的攀登。

例句：The stairs were steep but ascendible.  楼梯很陡，但可以爬上去。

近义词：climbable, scalable, mountable
反义词：inascendible, unclimbable, impassable
词性：形容词


词源:
From ascend + -ible.  Ascend comes from the Latin "ascendere,"  meaning "to climb up," from "ad-" (to) + "scandere" (to climb).
