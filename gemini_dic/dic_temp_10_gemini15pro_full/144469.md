outtell:/ˌaʊtˈtel/
verb
胜过；讲述；比……讲得更详细
to surpass in telling; to relate in greater detail
He outtold all the other storytellers with his captivating tales. 他以引人入胜的故事胜过所有其他讲故事的人。
The old woman outtold her grandson's version of the events, adding vivid details he had omitted. 老妇人讲述了她孙子版本的事件，补充了他省略的生动细节。
surpass, exceed, outdo
