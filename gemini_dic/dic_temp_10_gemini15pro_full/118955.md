meteorology:/ˌmiːtiəˈrɒlədʒi/ (US) /ˌmiːtiəˈrɑːlədʒi/ (UK)| n.|气象学|The science dealing with the atmosphere and its phenomena, including weather and climate.|Meteorology plays a crucial role in predicting extreme weather events.|近义词：atmospheric science, climatology|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *meteorologia, from Ancient Greek μετεωρολογία (meteōrología, “study of things high up; astronomy”), from μετέωρος (metéōros, “high in air; lofty; raised off the ground”) + -λογία (-logía, “-logy”).
