lumberer:/ˈlʌmbərər/| n. |伐木工人；笨拙行走的人|A person who cuts down trees for timber; someone who moves clumsily or heavily.|The lumberer swung his axe with practiced ease. 伐木工人熟练地挥舞着斧头。|近义词：logger, woodsman|反义词:None|noun

lumberer:/ˈlʌmbərər/| n. |笨拙行走的人|A person who moves in a clumsy or heavy way.|He lumbered across the room like a bear. 他像熊一样笨拙地走过房间。|近义词：plodder|反义词:None|noun
