coruscate:/ˈkɔːrəskeɪt/ /ˈkɔːrəˌskeɪt/| v. | 闪耀，闪烁，焕发 | To emit flashes of light; sparkle; gleam or glitter. | The fireworks coruscated in the night sky. 烟花在夜空中闪耀。

The diamonds coruscated under the spotlight. 钻石在聚光灯下闪耀。

近义词：sparkle, glitter, gleam, shimmer, flash, glint
反义词：dim, dull, fade
词性: verb

Etymology:
From Latin *coruscāre*, present active infinitive of *coruscō* (“I flash, glitter”), from *coruscus* (“shaking, quivering, flashing”).
