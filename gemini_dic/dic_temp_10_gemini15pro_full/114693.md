malingering:/məˈlɪŋɡərɪŋ/| n. |装病，诈病|The act of pretending to be ill in order to avoid work, duty, or responsibility.|He was accused of malingering to get out of military service. 他被指控装病以逃避兵役。|近义词：feigning, faking, simulating, shamming|反义词:genuinely ill, truly sick|noun

malinger:/məˈlɪŋɡər/| v. |装病，诈病|To pretend to be ill in order to avoid work, duty, or responsibility.|He malingered for weeks to avoid taking the exam.  他装病数周以逃避考试。|近义词：feign, fake, simulate, sham|反义词: be genuinely ill, be truly sick|verb
