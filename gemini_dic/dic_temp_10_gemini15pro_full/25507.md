bullying:/ˈbʊliɪŋ/ | n. |欺凌，霸凌| The act of intimidating a weaker person to do something, especially such repeated coercion. |  He was subjected to relentless bullying at school. 他在学校遭受了无情的欺凌。| synonyms: intimidation, harassment, persecution, victimization | antonyms: supporting, protecting, befriending | noun

Etymology: From bully + -ing.
