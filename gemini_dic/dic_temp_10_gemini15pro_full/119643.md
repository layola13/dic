middle-ranking:/ˈmɪdl ˈræŋkɪŋ/
adjective
中等职位的；中级的
Occupying a position about halfway between the top and the bottom of a scale or hierarchy.
He is a middle-ranking civil servant. 他是一位中层公务员。
Synonyms:mid-level, intermediate
Antonyms:top-ranking, low-ranking, senior, junior
