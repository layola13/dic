|n.|不可治愈性；无法医治|The incurablenesses of some diseases are a tragic reality.  一些疾病的不可治愈性是一个悲惨的现实。|The doctor stressed the incurablenesses of the patient's condition. 医生强调了病人病情的不可治愈性。|近义词:incurability, irremediableness|反义词:curability, remediableness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From incurable + -ness + -es.
"incurable" comes from Late Middle English: from Old French incurable, from in- ‘not’ + curable (see curable).
"-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, meaning ‘state or quality of being…’, e.g. goodness, kindness, hardness.
"-es" is a plural suffix.
