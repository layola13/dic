hobber:/ˈhɒbə(r) (UK), ˈhɑːbər (US)/
n.
滚齿机；滚刀；滚削机；使用滚齿机的人
A machine for cutting gear teeth, or a person who operates such a machine.
The hobber is essential for creating precise gears.  滚齿机对于制造精密齿轮至关重要。
The skilled hobber ensured the gears were perfectly cut.  熟练的滚刀操作员确保齿轮被完美切割。
近义词:gear cutter, gear cutting machine, gear shaper
反义词: None
