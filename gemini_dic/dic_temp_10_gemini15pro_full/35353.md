co-foundress:/ˌkoʊˈfaʊndrəs/
n.
女性共同创始人
A woman who establishes something (such as a business or organization) jointly with another or others.
例句：The co-foundress of the company gave an inspiring speech. 公司的女共同创始人发表了鼓舞人心的演讲。

例句：She and her co-foundress built the business from the ground up. 她和她的女共同创始人白手起家建立了这家公司。

近义词：co-founder, joint founder
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps consider "employee," "subordinate")
词性:noun
