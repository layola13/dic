castest:/ˈkɑːstɪst/| v.|你投擲 (古英语第二人称单数)|This archaic form of the verb "to cast" is the second-person singular present indicative form, used in Early Modern English and before.  It means "you throw" or "you cast."|例句：Thou castest the die. 你掷骰子。|近义词：throw, hurl, fling, pitch, toss|反义词:catch, receive|verb
