azured:/ˈæʒərd/| adj.|蔚蓝的，天蓝色的|Having a light, purplish blue color, like that of a clear sky.|The azured sky was dotted with fluffy white clouds.|近义词：sky-blue, cerulean, azure|反义词：gray, dark|词性:形容词
词源: From Old French *azuré, based on azure (noun), from Arabic لَازَوَرْد (lāzaward, “lapis lazuli”), from Persian لاژورد (lâžvard, “lapis lazuli”). Ultimately from Sanskrit राजवर्त (rājavarta, “king of the precious stones”).
