incharitable:/ɪnˈtʃærɪtəbl/
adj.
无情的，刻薄的，不仁慈的
Lacking in charity; unkind, unsympathetic, or harsh in judging others.
例句：It was an incharitable remark about her appearance.  关于她外貌的评论很不友善。

例句：It's incharitable to assume the worst of people.  假设别人最坏的一面是不仁慈的。

近义词：unkind, unsympathetic, harsh, cruel, mean, ungenerous, merciless, ruthless
反义词：charitable, kind, compassionate, sympathetic, generous, benevolent
词性: 形容词
