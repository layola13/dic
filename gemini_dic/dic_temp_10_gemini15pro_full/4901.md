alimentariness:/ˌælɪmɛnˈtɛrɪnɪs/
n.
营养性；食物性
The state or quality of being alimentary or nutritious.
The alimentariness of the new diet was questioned by many experts.  许多专家对新饮食的营养性提出了质疑。
近义词:nutritiousness, nourishment
反义词:indigestibility, unhealthiness
词性:noun


词源:
From Latin alimentarius (“nourishing”), from alimentum (“nourishment”).
