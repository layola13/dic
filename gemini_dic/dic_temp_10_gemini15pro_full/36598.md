colleague:/ˈkɒliːɡ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːliːɡ/ (US)| n.|同事|A person with whom one works, especially in a profession or business.|例句：I get along well with my colleagues. 我和我的同事相处得很好。

例句：She discussed the issue with her colleagues. 她和她的同事讨论了这个问题。

近义词：co-worker, associate, peer|反义词:rival, competitor|词性:noun
