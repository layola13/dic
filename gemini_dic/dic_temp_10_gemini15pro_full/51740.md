devolutionaries:/ˌdiːvəˈluːʃənɛriz/
noun
权力下放主义者；地方分权主义者
Those who advocate devolution, the transfer of power from a central government to regional or local governments.
The devolutionaries argued for greater regional autonomy. 权力下放主义者主张更大的区域自治。
Devolutionaries in Scotland have long campaigned for independence. 苏格兰的权力下放主义者长期以来一直为独立而奔走。
Synonyms: decentralists, regionalists, autonomists
Antonyms: centralists, unionists
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From devolution + -ary.  Devolution comes from Late Latin dēvolūtiōn- (stem of dēvolūtiō ), from dēvolvere "to roll down," from dē- "down" + volvere "to roll."
