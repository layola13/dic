ekebergite:/ˈɛkəbɜːrɡaɪt/
n.
埃克伯格石
A rare scapolite-group mineral, a sodium calcium chloro-aluminosilicate, occurring as colorless, white, gray, or pink tetragonal crystals.  It is named after the locality where it was discovered, Ekeberg, Norway.
Ekebergite crystals were found in the quarry. 在采石场发现了埃克伯格石晶体。

近义词:scapolite (方柱石 -  a broader mineral group to which ekebergite belongs)
反义词:None (Minerals don't typically have antonyms)
词性:noun
