colder:/ˈkoʊldər (US), ˈkəʊldər (UK)| adj.|更冷的，更寒冷的|The comparative form of "cold," meaning having a lower temperature or feeling less warm. It describes something that is less warm than something else being compared. |It's colder today than it was yesterday. 今天比昨天冷。|The weather is getting colder. 天气越来越冷了。|近义词：chillier, icier, frostier|反义词：warmer, hotter|词性：形容词
Etymology: From Old English "caldra," comparative of "ceald" (cold), from Proto-Germanic "*kaldaz," from Proto-Indo-European "*gel-," meaning "cold."
