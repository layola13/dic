misdivide:/ˌmɪsdɪˈvaɪd/
verb
错误地划分；划分错误
To divide incorrectly.
The surveyor misdivided the land, leading to a boundary dispute.  勘测员错误地划分了土地，导致了边界纠纷。
The teacher misdivided the class for the group project. 老师错误地划分了小组项目的班级。
Near Synonyms: miscalculate, misallocate, apportion incorrectly
Antonyms: divide correctly, apportion correctly
词性: 动词
