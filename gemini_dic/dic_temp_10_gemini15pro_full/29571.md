catchwater:/ˈkætʃˌwɔːtər/ | n. |集水沟，截水沟，集水渠|A ditch or channel for catching surface water.  A structure, such as a ditch or embankment, designed to intercept and collect rainwater. |例句：The farmers built a catchwater to irrigate their crops. 农民们修建了一条集水沟来灌溉庄稼。

例句：The catchwater prevented the hillside from eroding. 集水沟防止了山坡的侵蚀。|近义词：drain, ditch, culvert, channel |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "watershed" in the sense of an area *drained* by a river) |词性:noun
