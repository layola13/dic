ether:/ˈiːθər/ /ˈiːθɚ/ | n. | 乙醚; 以太 |A pleasant-smelling, colourless, volatile liquid used as an anaesthetic.  A hypothetical medium supposed to fill all space and transmit electromagnetic waves.| The doctor administered ether to the patient before the surgery. 医生在手术前给病人施用了乙醚。| synonyms: anaesthetic,  medium | antonyms: stimulant | noun


ethers:/ˈiːθərz/ /ˈiːθɚz/ | n. | 乙醚（复数）; 以太（复数）| The plural form of "ether," referring to more than one type of ether or a larger quantity of ether.  Also, can refer to the vast expanse of space.|  The chemist was working with various ethers in the lab.  化学家在实验室里用各种乙醚工作。| synonyms: anaesthetics, mediums | antonyms: stimulants | noun
