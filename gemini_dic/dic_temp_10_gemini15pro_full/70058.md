fetus:/ˈfiːtəs/ (US) /ˈfiːtəs/ (UK)| n.|胎儿; 胚胎|An unborn offspring of a mammal, in particular an unborn human baby more than eight weeks after conception.|例句：The doctor monitored the development of the fetus throughout the pregnancy. 医生在整个怀孕期间监测胎儿的发育。

例句：Exposure to certain chemicals can harm the fetus. 接触某些化学物质会损害胎儿。|近义词：embryo, unborn child|反义词:newborn, infant|词性:noun
