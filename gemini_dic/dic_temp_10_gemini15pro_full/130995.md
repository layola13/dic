nonattainabilities: /ˌnɒnətənˌeɪnəˈbɪlətiz/ | n. | 不可实现性；无法达到 | The nonattainabilities of his goals led to frustration. |  His dreams were plagued by inherent nonattainabilities. | 近义词:impossibilities, unattainableness | 反义词: possibilities, attainabilities | 词性: noun

词源:  From "non-" (not) + "attainable" (achievable) + "-ity" (suffix forming abstract nouns) + "-es" (plural suffix). Ultimately from Latin "attingere" (to touch, attain).
