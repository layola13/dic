brawniest:/ˈbrɔːnɪɪst/| adj.|最强壮的；最结实的|Most physically strong or muscular.|例句：He was the brawniest lad in the village. 他是村里最强壮的小伙子。

例句：She was the brawniest of the three sisters. 她是三姐妹中最强壮的。|近义词：strongest, sturdiest, most muscular|反义词：weakest, frailest|词性:形容词
词源:From brawn + -iest.  Brawn comes from Old French braon, meaning "fleshy part of the body," ultimately from a Germanic source.
