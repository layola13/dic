antifundamentalist:/ˌæntiˌfʌndəˈmɛntəlɪst/
n., adj.|反原教旨主义者(的)|Someone who opposes religious fundamentalism.  A person who believes that religious texts should not be interpreted literally and that religion should adapt to modern society.|The journalist wrote an article interviewing several antifundamentalists. 这位记者写了一篇文章，采访了几位反原教旨主义者。|Near Antonyms: fundamentalist, traditionalist|Antonyms: fundamentalist|noun, adjective

antifundamentalism:/ˌæntiˌfʌndəˈmɛntəlɪzəm/
