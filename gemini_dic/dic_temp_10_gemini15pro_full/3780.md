aglarus: /ˈæɡlərəs/ | n. | 黑头鸥 (a genus of gulls) | A genus of gulls characterized by a black head during the breeding season.  This genus includes species like the Black-headed Gull (Chroicocephalus ridibundus), which used to be classified as Larus ridibundus. | Example: The aglarus, specifically the Black-headed Gull, is a common sight in coastal areas of Europe.  黑头鸥，特指红嘴鸥，在欧洲沿海地区很常见。

Example: Ornithologists reclassified the Black-headed Gull from Larus ridibundus to Chroicocephalus ridibundus, removing it from the aglarus genus. 鸟类学家将红嘴鸥从鸥属红嘴鸥重新分类为笑鸥属红嘴鸥，将其从黑头鸥属中移除。 | 近义词: gull, Larus (formerly) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
