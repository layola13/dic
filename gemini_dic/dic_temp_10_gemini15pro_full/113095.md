luncheon:/ˈlʌntʃən/ (US), /ˈlʌntʃən/ (UK)| n. |午餐;午宴;正式的午餐会|A midday meal.  A formal lunch, often for a special occasion. | We were invited to a luncheon in honor of the visiting professor. 我们被邀请参加为来访教授举办的午宴。|  We had a pleasant luncheon at a charming bistro. 我们在一家迷人的小酒馆享用了愉快的午餐。 |synonyms: lunch, midday meal, repast, banquet (when formal) | antonyms: breakfast, dinner, supper | noun
