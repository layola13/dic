multiplying:/ˈmʌltɪplaɪɪŋ/ | v. | 乘；增加；繁殖 |  present participle of *multiply* meaning to increase in number or quantity. | The bacteria are multiplying rapidly. 细菌正在迅速繁殖。| synonyms: increasing, amplifying, augmenting | antonyms: dividing, decreasing, diminishing | verb

multiplying:/ˈmʌltɪplaɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 倍增的；乘法的 | Describes something that multiplies or increases. |  The multiplying effect of the drought was devastating. 干旱的倍增效应是毁灭性的。 | synonyms: proliferating, expanding, escalating | antonyms: reducing, shrinking, contracting | adjective
