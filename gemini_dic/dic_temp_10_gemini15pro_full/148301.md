oversolemn: /ˌoʊvərˈsɑːləm/ | adj. | 过于严肃的，过分庄重的 | Excessively solemn or serious; characterized by excessive gravity or formality. | He delivered the speech in an oversolemn tone, making the audience feel uncomfortable. 他以一种过于严肃的语气发表演讲，让观众感到不舒服。| The funeral service was oversolemn, lacking any sense of joy or celebration of life.  葬礼过于庄重，缺乏任何喜悦或对生命的庆祝。| 近义词: grave, serious, somber, pompous | 反义词: lighthearted, cheerful, frivolous, casual | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "over-" (excessively) + "solemn" (serious, grave).  "Solemn" ultimately derives from Latin "sollemnis" (annual, religious).
