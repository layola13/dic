octahedrite:/ˈɑːktəˌhedraɪt/ | n. | 八面体陨铁 | A common type of iron meteorite that exhibits a characteristic octahedral crystalline structure when etched. |  Example 1: The museum's collection includes a large octahedrite meteorite found in Arizona.  博物馆的收藏包括一块在亚利桑那州发现的大型八面体陨铁。

Example 2:  Octahedrites are primarily composed of iron and nickel.  八面体陨铁主要由铁和镍组成。 | 近义词: iron meteorite | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider stony meteorite or stony-iron meteorite as contrasting types) | noun
