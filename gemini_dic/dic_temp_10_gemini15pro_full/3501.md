afterpiece:/ˈæftərpiːs/
n.
余兴节目；餐后甜点
a short play performed after the main play in a theatre
例句：The afterpiece was a hilarious farce. 余兴节目是一出滑稽的闹剧。

例句：The audience enjoyed the afterpiece more than the main play.  观众比正剧更喜欢余兴节目。

近义词：coda, finale, epilogue
反义词:prelude, prologue
词性:noun
