cephalology:/ˌsefəˈlɑːlədʒi/
n.
头部学
The study of the head, especially its shape and size.
Cephalology is a branch of anthropology that studies the measurements of the human head. 头部学是人类学的一个分支，研究人类头部的测量。
The cephalology of Neanderthals is distinct from that of modern humans. 尼安德特人的头部特征与现代人截然不同。
