parallelism:/ˈpærəˌlɛlɪzəm/| n.|平行性，并行性，对仗|The state of being parallel or of corresponding in some way.  The use of successive verbal constructions in poetry or prose that correspond in grammatical structure, sound, meter, meaning, etc.|The parallelism between the two stories is striking. 这两个故事的相似性令人吃惊。|synonyms: correspondence, similarity, analogy, resemblance, likeness|antonyms: divergence, difference, dissimilarity|noun
