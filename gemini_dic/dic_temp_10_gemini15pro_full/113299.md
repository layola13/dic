lutheranize:/ˈluːθərənaɪz/ | v. | 使路德化，使接受路德教义 | To cause to become Lutheran; to make conform to the doctrines or practices of Martin Luther or the Lutheran Church. |  The prince attempted to lutheranize the entire region. 王子试图让整个地区路德化。 |  The king's marriage to a Protestant princess helped to lutheranize the court. 国王与一位新教公主的婚姻有助于使宫廷路德化。 | 近义词: protestantize, reform | 反义词: catholicize | 词性: 动词
