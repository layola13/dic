oilseed:/ˈɔɪlˌsiːd/ | n. |油料种子；油籽|A seed from which oil is extracted, such as flaxseed, rapeseed, or sunflower seed.|例句：Farmers in this region primarily cultivate oilseeds like soybeans and sunflowers. 这个地区的农民主要种植大豆和向日葵等油料作物。

例句：The global demand for oilseeds has been steadily increasing. 全球对油料种子的需求一直在稳步增长。|近义词：oil-bearing seed, oleaginous seed |反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "non-oilseed crops") |词性: noun
