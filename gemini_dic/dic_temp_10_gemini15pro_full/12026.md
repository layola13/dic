arthurians:/ɑːrˈθʊəriənz/
|n.|亚瑟王时代的臣民；亚瑟王传说爱好者|People, especially the knights, of the court of King Arthur; enthusiasts of Arthurian legends.|The Arthurians gathered at the Round Table. 亚瑟王时代的臣民聚集在圆桌旁。|The conference attracted Arthurians from all over the world. 会议吸引了来自世界各地的亚瑟王传说爱好者。|synonyms: knights of the round table, followers of Arthur|antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps enemies of Arthur)|noun
