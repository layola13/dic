belligerence:/bəˈlɪdʒərəns/| n.|好战性，交战状态|A warlike or aggressively hostile attitude, atmosphere, or disposition.|His belligerence surprised everyone. 他的好战性让每个人都感到惊讶。|Example: The country's belligerence led to war.  这个国家的好战性导致了战争。

Example:  Her belligerence made her difficult to work with. 她的好战性使她难以与人共事。

|Synonyms: aggressiveness, hostility, pugnacity, combativeness|Antonyms: peacefulness, pacifism, friendliness, amity|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin bellum ("war") + gerere ("to wage").
