astun:/əˈstʌn/
verb
使震惊；使惊骇
to surprise or shock someone very much
例句：
The news astounded everyone.  这消息震惊了所有人。

Her courage astounded us. 她的勇气使我们惊叹。

近义词：amaze, astonish, shock, surprise, stun
反义词：bore, tire
词性：动词
