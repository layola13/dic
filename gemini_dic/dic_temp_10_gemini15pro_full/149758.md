palates:/ˈpælɪts/ /ˈpæləts/| n.|味觉；口味；爱好；（尤指）鉴赏力|A person's ability to taste and judge good food and drink, or their enjoyment of good food and drink.|例句：She has a discerning palate for fine wines. 她对葡萄酒有很高的鉴赏力。

例句：The restaurant caters to discerning palates. 这家餐厅迎合挑剔的食客。

近义词：taste, gusto, appreciation|反义词:|词性:noun


palate:/ˈpælɪt/ /ˈpælət/| n.|上颚；腭|The roof of the mouth.|例句：The hard palate is the bony front part of the palate. 硬腭是腭的骨质前部。

例句：The soft palate is the fleshy back part of the palate. 软腭是腭的肉质后部。
