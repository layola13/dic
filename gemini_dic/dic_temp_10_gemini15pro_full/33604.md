cinch:/sɪntʃ/| n. vt.|容易的事，必然发生的事；束带；用带子束紧|Something that is easy to do or certain to happen; a strong strap used for fastening something or holding it tightly; to fasten something with a cinch.|例句：The exam was a cinch. 考试很容易。|近义词：breeze, snap, piece of cake, walkover, pushover|反义词：challenge, difficulty|词性:名词,动词

cinch:/sɪntʃ/| vt.|用带子束紧|To fasten something with a cinch.|例句：He cinched the saddle tight. 他把马鞍勒紧。|近义词：fasten, secure, tighten|反义词：loosen, unfasten|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *cincha* "girth," from Latin *cingula* "girdle, belt."
