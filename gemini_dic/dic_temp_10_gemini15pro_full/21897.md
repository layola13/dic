bodying:/ˈbɑːdiɪŋ/
verb
体现；完美展现；（在说唱比赛中）击败
present participle of body
例句：She's bodying that dance routine. 她完美地演绎了那段舞蹈。

bodying:/ˈbɑːdiɪŋ/
adjective
（非正式）极好的；令人印象深刻的
Derived from the verb "body"
例句：That new song is bodying!  那首新歌太棒了！

近义词:conquering, dominating, excelling, slaying, crushing, killing it (slang), nailing it (slang)
反义词:failing, losing, underperforming
词性:verb, adjective (informal)
