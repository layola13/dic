atlases:/ˈætləsiz/
n.
地图册；地图集
Collections of maps, typically bound into a book.
He consulted several atlases to plan his trip across Europe. 他查阅了几本地图册，计划他的欧洲之旅。
synonyms: map collections, cartographic compilations
antonyms: globes (arguably, as they present information differently)
noun

atlases:/ˈætləsiz/
n.
(解剖学)寰椎
The first cervical vertebra of the spine, which supports the head.
Damage to the atlases can be very serious. 寰椎受损可能非常严重。
synonyms:  C1 vertebra, first cervical vertebra
antonyms: None (specific anatomical term)
noun
