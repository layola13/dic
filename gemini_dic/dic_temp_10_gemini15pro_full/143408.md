outflanker:/ˌaʊtˈflæŋkər/
|n.|包抄者，侧翼攻击者|Someone who outflanks, especially in a military maneuver.|The general sent outflankers to surround the enemy. 将军派出了侧翼攻击部队包围敌人。|近义词:attacker, assailant|反义词:defender, protector|词性:名词

outflanker:/ˌaʊtˈflæŋkər/
|n.|以智取胜者，策略胜过者|Someone who gains an advantage over another, especially by superior strategy or tactics, not necessarily in a military context.|In the business negotiation, she proved to be a skilled outflanker, securing a better deal than anyone expected. 在商业谈判中，她证明了自己是一个熟练的策略家，获得了一份比任何人预期的都要好的协议。|近义词:strategist, tactician|反义词:loser, defeated|词性:名词

词源:
From the verb "outflank" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Outflank" comes from "out-" (meaning beyond or outside) + "flank" (the side of an army or formation).
