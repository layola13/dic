emulator:/ˈemjuleɪtər/ (US) /ˈemjʊleɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |模拟器; 仿真器|A device or program that imitates the behavior of another computer system or device, allowing software or other input designed for the original system to be used on the emulator.  |I use an emulator to play old Nintendo games on my PC. 我用模拟器在我的电脑上玩老任天堂游戏。|Synonyms: simulator, imitator|Antonyms: original, authentic|Noun
