gemmation:/dʒɛˈmeɪʃən/
n.
出芽, 芽殖, 发芽
The process of reproduction by budding, in which a new organism develops from an outgrowth or bud due to cell division at one particular site.
Hydra reproduce by gemmation. 水螅通过出芽生殖繁殖。
budding, sprouting, burgeoning
fission
