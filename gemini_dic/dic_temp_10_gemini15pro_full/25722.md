bunting:/ˈbʌntɪŋ/ | n. | 彩旗；燕雀 | Flags or other decorations made of thin cloth, often used for festive occasions; a group of finches. |  The streets were decorated with colorful bunting for the festival.  街道上装饰着五彩缤纷的彩旗，庆祝节日。| synonym: decorations, flags, pennants; finch, sparrow | antonym: plainness, bareness | noun

bunting:/ˈbʌntɪŋ/ | n. | 触击 | A short, light hit in baseball, intended to advance a base runner. | He laid down a perfect bunt to advance the runner to third. 他做了一个完美的触击，使跑垒员推进到三垒。| synonym: sacrifice, tap | antonym: home run, grand slam | noun
