autobahn:/ˈaʊtəˌbɑːn/ (US), /ˈɔːtəʊˌbɑːn/ (UK)| n. |高速公路|A German expressway, typically one with multiple lanes and no speed limit (in some sections).|We drove on the autobahn at high speed. 我们在高速公路上高速行驶。|近义词：expressway, freeway, motorway|反义词: (No direct opposite, perhaps "local road" or "side street")|Noun
