noncomputation:/ˌnɒnkɒmpjuˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 非计算，非计算性 | The process or state of not being computed or calculable; something that cannot be computed. |  The artist argued that true creativity resided in the realm of noncomputation, beyond the reach of algorithms. 艺术家认为真正的创造力存在于非计算领域，超越了算法的范围。

The philosopher explored the limits of artificial intelligence, focusing on aspects of human experience like consciousness and emotion that fall under the umbrella of noncomputation. 哲学家探讨了人工智能的局限性，重点关注了人类经验中诸如意识和情感等属于非计算范畴的方面。 | 近义词: incalculability, non-computability | 反义词: computation, calculability | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "computation" (from compute, from Latin "computare" - to count, sum up, reckon).
