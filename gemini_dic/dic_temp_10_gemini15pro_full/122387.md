mlo:/ˈɛmɛloʊ/ |n.| 机器学习操作 (Machine Learning Operations) |A set of practices that aims to deploy and maintain machine learning models in production reliably and efficiently. | Example:  MLOps is becoming increasingly important for businesses that want to leverage the power of AI.  机器学习操作对于想要利用人工智能力量的企业来说正变得越来越重要。

Example: The company invested heavily in MLOps to streamline their AI development process.  该公司在机器学习操作上投入巨资以简化其人工智能开发流程。

| 近义词: AIOps (Artificial Intelligence for IT Operations), DataOps | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
