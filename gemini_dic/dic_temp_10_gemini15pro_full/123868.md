morassy:/məˈræsi/| adj.|沼泽的；泥泞的；困境的|Resembling a marsh; boggy. Also, resembling a morass in being tangled, confusing, or troublesome.  |The expedition became lost in the morassy terrain. 探险队在沼泽地带迷路了。|近义词：marshy, swampy, boggy, miry, quaggy; perplexing, confusing, bewildering|反义词：dry, arid, clear, simple|词性:形容词

morassy:/məˈræsi/| n.|沼泽；困境|A marsh or bog; something that entangles, confuses, or impedes. |He found himself in a morassy of legal problems. 他发现自己陷入了法律问题的泥潭。|近义词：marsh, swamp, bog, quagmire; predicament, dilemma|反义词：desert, clarity, solution|词性:名词


词源: From Late Latin *moracium, from Latin morosus (“peevish, fretful; fastidious, difficult to please”) + -y. Ultimately from mos (“custom, habit”) (compare moral).
