geogetto:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈɡɛtoʊ/ | n. |贫民区，少数民族聚居区| A ghetto is a part of a city, especially a slum area, occupied by a minority group or groups. |  The Jewish ghetto in Warsaw during World War II was a place of immense suffering.  华沙犹太人区在二战期间是一个充满苦难的地方。

The band emerged from the ghettos of Detroit. 这支乐队来自底特律的贫民区。| synonyms: slum, barrio, shantytown | antonyms: suburb, affluent neighborhood | noun
