inspirationist: /ˌɪnspəˈreɪʃənɪst/
n.| 灵感主义者；默示论者 |A believer in inspiration, especially divine inspiration.| The preacher described himself as an inspirationist, claiming his sermons came directly from God.|近义词: prophet, seer, visionary|反义词: skeptic, doubter|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From inspiration + -ist.  Inspiration from Latin inspiratio, from inspirare "to breathe into," from in- "in" + spirare "to breathe."
