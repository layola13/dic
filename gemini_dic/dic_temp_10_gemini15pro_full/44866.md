cruxes: /ˈkrʌksiːz/
n.
关键；症结；核心问题
The crux of the matter is that we don't have enough money. 这件事的关键是我们没有足够的钱。
The crux of the argument is whether the government should increase taxes.  这场争论的症结在于政府是否应该增税。
近义词: core, heart, gist, nub, kernel, essence
反义词: periphery, exterior, surface
