outchattered:/ˌaʊtˈtʃætərd/
verb
胜过…的讲话；比…说得更多
talked more or longer than someone else
She outchattered him at the party.  她在聚会上比他说得更多。
He outchattered everyone in the room. 他在房间里比任何人都说得更多。
