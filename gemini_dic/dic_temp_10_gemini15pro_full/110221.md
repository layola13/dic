life-changing:/ˈlaɪfˌtʃeɪndʒɪŋ/
adjective
改变人生的；有 transformative 效果的
Having an important and lasting effect on one's life, often in a positive way.
Meeting her was a life-changing experience.  遇到她是一次改变人生的经历。
The invention of the internet was a life-changing event. 互联网的发明是一个改变人生的事件。
synonyms: transformative, pivotal, momentous, impactful
antonyms: insignificant, trivial, uneventful
word type: adjective
