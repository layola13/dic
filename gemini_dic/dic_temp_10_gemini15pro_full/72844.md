folivore:/ˈfəʊlɪvɔːr/ (US) /ˈfoʊləˌvɔr/| n.|食叶动物|An animal that feeds chiefly on leaves.  A herbivore that specializes in eating leaves.|Koalas are folivores, their diet consisting almost entirely of eucalyptus leaves.  考拉是食叶动物，它们的食物几乎完全由桉树叶组成。

The sloth is a folivore that spends most of its life in the trees.  树懒是一种食叶动物，一生中的大部分时间都生活在树上。|近义词:herbivore, browser|反义词:carnivore, predator|词性:名词

Etymology: From Latin *folium* "leaf" + *-vorus* "-eating," from *vorare* "to devour."
