词源:  From "heavy hand" + "-ed" + "-ly".  "Heavy hand" refers to a literal heavy hand, or figuratively, oppressive or clumsy action.  The suffix "-ed" creates the adjective "heavy-handed", and "-ly" transforms it into the adverb "heavy-handedly".
