gaddish:/ˈɡædɪʃ/| adj.|轻浮的；爱闲逛的；追求享乐的|Frivolous and flighty; enjoying gadding about; given to pleasure-seeking.|She led a rather gaddish life, flitting from party to party.|近义词：frivolous, flighty, giddy, irresponsible|反义词：serious, responsible, steady|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "gad" (to roam about in search of pleasure) + "-ish" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Gad" likely comes from Old Norse "gaddr" (spike, goad).
