intern:/ˈɪntɜːrn/ (US) /ˈɪntɜːn/ (UK)| n.  vi. vt.|实习生，实习，拘留|A student or recent graduate who works for a period of time at a job in order to get experience.  To confine (someone, such as a prisoner of war) within prescribed limits.|
例句：She worked as an intern at a law firm last summer. 她去年夏天在一家律师事务所实习。

例句：He was interned by the enemy forces during the war.  他在战争期间被敌军拘留。

近义词：trainee, apprentice,  practitioner (for n.); confine, detain (for vt.)|
反义词：professional, expert (for n.); release, liberate (for vt.)|
词性：noun, intransitive verb, transitive verb|
