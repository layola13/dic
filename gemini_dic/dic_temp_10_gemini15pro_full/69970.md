festivous:/ˈfɛstɪvəs/| adj.|欢乐的，节日的|Relating to or characteristic of a festival, especially Christmas; merry and celebratory.  Marked by or suggestive of festivity and rejoicing.|例句：The festive atmosphere filled the room with warmth and cheer. 节日的气氛让房间充满了温暖和欢乐。

例句：Everyone was in a festive mood, singing carols and exchanging gifts. 每个人都沉浸在节日的氛围中，唱着颂歌，交换着礼物。|近义词：jolly, merry, joyful, celebratory, convivial|反义词：somber, gloomy, mournful, serious|词性:adjective
词源:Late Middle English: from Old French festiff, from feste ‘feast’.
