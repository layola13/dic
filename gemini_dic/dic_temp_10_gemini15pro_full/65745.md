everlasting:/ˌevərˈlæstɪŋ/ | adj. | 永恒的，永久的 | Lasting forever; eternal. |  The everlasting love between a mother and her child. 母亲和孩子之间永恒的爱。| synonyms: eternal, perpetual, permanent, enduring, infinite | antonyms: temporary, transient, fleeting, ephemeral | adjective

everlasting:/ˌevərˈlæstɪŋ/ | n. | 永恒的事物 | Something that lasts forever. | The search for an everlasting truth. 对永恒真理的追求。| synonyms: eternity, perpetuity | antonyms: ephemerality, transience | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *ēfre + *læstende (lasting), from Proto-Germanic *aiwīz + *lastandiz.  The modern spelling emerged in the Middle English period.
