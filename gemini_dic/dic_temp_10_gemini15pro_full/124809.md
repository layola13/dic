mucic:/ˈmjuːsɪk/| adj.|粘液酸的；粘液的|Relating to or derived from mucic acid, a crystalline acid formed by the oxidation of milk sugar, dulcite, and other substances.|Mucic acid is formed by the nitric acid oxidation of galactose or galactose-containing compounds like lactose, dulcite, galactans, and gums.|近义词:saccharic|反义词:|adjective
