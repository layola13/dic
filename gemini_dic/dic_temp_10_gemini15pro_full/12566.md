aspirers:/əˈspaɪrərz/
n.
有抱负的人；渴望成功的人；追求者
People who have ambition and strong desire to achieve something; those who seek to attain a particular goal.
Many young aspirers move to big cities hoping to find fame and fortune. 许多胸怀大志的年轻人都涌向大城市，希望能功成名就。
Synonyms: hopefuls, dreamers, seekers, strivers
Antonyms: pessimists, underachievers
词性: 名词
