canyons:/ˈkænjənz/| n.|峡谷|A deep, narrow valley with steep sides, often carved by a river.|We hiked through the stunning canyons of the national park. 我们徒步穿越了国家公园里令人叹为观止的峡谷。|近义词：gorges, ravines, chasms|反义词:plains, plateaus|Noun

词源: From Spanish *cañón* "tube, cannon," from Old French *canon* (same meaning), ultimately from Latin *canna* "reed, cane."  The geological sense developed from the resemblance of a canyon to a tube or channel.
