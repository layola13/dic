damnability:/ˌdæmnəˈbɪləti/
n.
可谴责性，该罚性
The state or quality of being damnable; deserving of condemnation.
His damnability was clear after he confessed to the crime. 他承认罪行后，他的罪恶昭彰。
Near Synonyms: wickedness, depravity, iniquity, culpability
Antonyms: virtue, righteousness, innocence
词性: 名词
