decrement:/ˈdekrəmənt/ (US), /ˈdɛkrɪmənt/ (UK)| n. v.|减少量，减量；递减|A decrement is a reduction or decrease in a quantity or value.  It can also refer to the act of decreasing something.|The decrement in sales this quarter is concerning.  这个季度销售额的下降令人担忧。|decrement (v.) the program decrements i by one each time through the loop. 该程序每次循环都会将 i 减 1。|synonyms: decrease, reduction, decline|antonyms: increment, increase, rise|noun, verb

decrement:/ˈdekrəmənt/ (US), /ˈdɛkrɪmənt/ (UK)| v.|减少，递减|To decrement something means to reduce it by a fixed amount.|The software automatically decrements the inventory count after each sale.  每次销售后，软件会自动减少库存数量。|synonyms: reduce, lessen, diminish|antonyms: increment, increase, augment|verb
