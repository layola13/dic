coalesce: /ˌkəʊəˈlɛs/ (UK) /ˌkoʊəˈlɛs/ (US)| v. |合并，联合，结合|To grow together or into one body; to unite into a whole.|The puddles on the sidewalk eventually coalesced into a small stream. 人行道上的水坑最终汇聚成一条小溪。|The various groups coalesced into a powerful political force.  不同的团体联合起来形成一股强大的政治力量。|近义词：merge, combine, unite, fuse, blend|反义词：separate, divide, split, disperse|词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin coalescere "to grow together," from com- "together" + alescere "to grow up," inchoative of alere "to nourish."
