aswooned:/əˈswuːnd/
adjective
昏厥的，神魂颠倒的
in a state of swooning or ecstasy.
She was aswooned with happiness when he proposed. 她被他求婚时欣喜若狂，几乎昏厥。
Near Antonyms: conscious, alert, aware
Antonyms:清醒的，警觉的，有意识的
Synonyms: ecstatic, rapturous, entranced
近义词：欣喜若狂的，狂喜的，入迷的


ETYMOLOGY->
From a- + swooned, past participle of swoon.  "Swoon" comes from Old English *geswōgen, past participle of *geswōgan "to faint," from Proto-Germanic *swōgjaną "to suffocate, be silent," from PIE root *sweHg- "to be silent, still."
