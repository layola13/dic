oaring:/ˈɔːrɪŋ/ /ˈɔːrɪŋ/ | adj.| 喧闹的，轰鸣的；(生意)兴隆的 | characterized by a loud, deep sound; thriving, prosperous | The roaring twenties were a time of great economic prosperity.  喧嚣的二十年代是经济繁荣的时期。|  The engine was roaring as the plane took off.  飞机起飞时，引擎轰鸣作响。 | 近义词: booming, thunderous, resounding, flourishing, thriving | 反义词: quiet, silent, still, failing, declining | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *roren, from Old English *rārian, from Proto-Germanic *rōrjaną (“to roar”). Cognate with West Frisian roarje (“to roar”), Dutch roeren (“to stir, to touch”), German röhren (“to roar”).
