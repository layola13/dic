baboonery:/ˌbæˈbuːnəri/
n.
愚蠢的行为；粗鲁的行为；胡闹
Extreme silliness or foolishness; boisterous or unruly behavior.
His baboonery at the party embarrassed his wife. 他在派对上的胡闹让他的妻子感到尴尬。
