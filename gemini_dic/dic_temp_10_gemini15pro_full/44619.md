crowdsourcing:/ˈkraʊdsɔːrsɪŋ/
n.
众包；群体外包
The process of obtaining needed services, ideas, or content by soliciting contributions from a large group of people, especially an online community, rather than from traditional employees or suppliers.
Many companies are now using crowdsourcing to gather ideas for new products. 许多公司现在使用众包来收集新产品的想法。
crowdfunding, open innovation, collaborative innovation
outsourcing, traditional methods
noun
