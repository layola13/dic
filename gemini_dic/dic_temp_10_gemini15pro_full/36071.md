coefficient:/ˌkoʊɪˈfɪʃənt/| n. |系数，率|A numerical or constant quantity placed before and multiplying the variable in an algebraic expression (e.g., 4 in 4x y).|The coefficient of friction is a measure of how easily one object slides against another.|近义词:factor, multiplier, constant|反义词:N/A|noun
