felinities:/fɪˈlɪnɪtiz/
n.
猫的特性；猫科动物的特征
The characteristics of cats; the qualities of felines.
Her graceful movements and aloof demeanor were the epitome of felinities. 她的优雅的动作和冷漠的态度是猫科动物特性的缩影。
The documentary explored the various felinities of the big cats of Africa.  这部纪录片探索了非洲大型猫科动物的各种特性。
近义词:feline qualities, catlike characteristics, cattishness
反义词:canine qualities, dog-like traits
