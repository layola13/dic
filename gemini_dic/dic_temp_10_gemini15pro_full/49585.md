demerging:/diːˈmɜːrdʒɪŋ/
n.|反合并，分拆|The act of separating a company into two or more independent companies.|The demerging of the company resulted in two separate entities. 公司的反合并导致了两个独立实体的产生。|demerger, separation, divestiture|merger, consolidation|noun

demerge:/diːˈmɜːrdʒ/
v.|反合并，分拆|To separate a company into two or more independent companies.|The board decided to demerge the telecommunications business from the main company. 董事会决定将电信业务从总公司中分拆出来。|separate, divest, split off|merge, consolidate|verb
