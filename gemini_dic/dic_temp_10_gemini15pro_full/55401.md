distensive:/dɪˈstɛnsɪv/
adjective
膨胀的，扩张的
Causing or tending to cause distension; expanding.
Gas in the stomach can be distensive and uncomfortable. 胃胀气会引起腹胀和不适。
近义词:expansive, swelling, dilating
反义词:contractive, constricting
词性:adjective
词源:From Latin distendere "to stretch out," from dis- "apart" + tendere "to stretch."
