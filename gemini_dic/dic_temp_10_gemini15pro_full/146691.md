overheartiness:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrtinəs/
noun
过分热情，过分殷勤
excessive heartiness; being too enthusiastic or friendly, often in a way that seems insincere.
He greeted me with such overheartiness that I felt suspicious. 他如此热情地跟我打招呼，让我觉得很可疑。
Near Synonyms: effusiveness, exuberance, boisterousness
Near Antonyms: coldness, aloofness, indifference
