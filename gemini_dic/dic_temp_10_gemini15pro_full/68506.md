fanatic:/fəˈnætɪk/| n. adj.| 狂热者，狂热的|A person filled with excessive and single-minded zeal, especially for a cause or an idea; excessively enthusiastic.|He's a football fanatic and watches every game. 他是个足球狂热分子，每场比赛都看。|  She's fanatic about keeping her house clean. 她对保持房子清洁非常狂热。|近义词：zealot, enthusiast, devotee|反义词:moderate, apathetic|词性:名词，形容词
fanatical:/fəˈnætɪkl/| adj.| 狂热的|Marked by excessive enthusiasm and often intense uncritical devotion.|He has a fanatical devotion to his work. 他对工作有着狂热的奉献精神。 |She's fanatical about healthy eating. 她对健康饮食非常狂热。|近义词：zealous, fervent, obsessive|反义词:moderate, indifferent|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fanaticus*, "inspired by a deity, frenzied," from *fanum*, "temple."  The word originally referred to someone inspired by a god, but later acquired the negative connotation of excessive and unreasonable zeal.
