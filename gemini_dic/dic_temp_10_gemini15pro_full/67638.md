extricate:/ˈɛkstrɪkeɪt/ |v. | 解救，使摆脱，使脱离 | To free or remove (someone or something) from a difficult or unpleasant situation. | The firefighters extricated the survivors from the wreckage. 消防队员将幸存者从残骸中解救出来。|  He extricated himself from the awkward situation with a joke. 他用一个笑话摆脱了尴尬的处境。 | 近义词: free, release, disentangle, rescue | 反义词: entangle, involve, trap | 词性: 动词
