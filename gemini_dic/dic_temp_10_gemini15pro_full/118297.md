mercurialize:/mɜːrˈkjʊəriəlaɪz/ | v. | 使 mercurialization mercurialize 汞化；使变化无常；使活泼 | To make or become mercurial; to treat or combine with mercury.  To make unpredictable or erratic. |  His moods mercurialized quickly, shifting from joy to anger in an instant. 他的情绪变化无常，瞬间从喜悦转为愤怒。 | The process mercurialized the gold, making it easier to extract. 这个过程使黄金汞化，使其更容易提取。 | 近义词：fluctuate, vacillate, vary | 反义词：stabilize, remain constant | 词性: 动词

词源: From mercury + -ize.  Ultimately from Latin Mercurius (the Roman god Mercury, associated with eloquence and swiftness).
