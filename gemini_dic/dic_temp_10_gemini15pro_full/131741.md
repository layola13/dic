nonconflicting:/ˌnɒnkənˈflɪktɪŋ/ | adj. |无冲突的，不冲突的| Not in conflict; compatible; harmonious. |  Example:  They have nonconflicting schedules, so they can work together. 他们时间安排没有冲突，所以可以一起工作。| Example: The two proposals are nonconflicting and can be implemented simultaneously.  这两个提案并不冲突，可以同时实施。 |Synonyms: compatible, harmonious, consistent, agreeable, concordant | Antonyms: conflicting, incompatible, discordant, clashing | Adjective
