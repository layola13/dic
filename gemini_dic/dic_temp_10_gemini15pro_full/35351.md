co-feoffee:/koʊ-fiːˈfiː/
|n.|共同受封者，共同封地人|A person who holds land jointly with another under the same feudal title.|John and Mary were co-feoffees of the estate.  约翰和玛丽是这片地产的共同受封者。|Synonym: joint tenant|Antonym: sole owner|Noun
