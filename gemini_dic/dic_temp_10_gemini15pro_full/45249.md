cultirostral:/kʌltɪˈrɒstrəl/
adjective
耕作的喙；用于耕作的喙状物
Relating to or characterized by a beak adapted for digging or cultivating the soil.
The ornithologist described the bird's cultirostral beak as perfectly suited for foraging in the earth. 鸟类学家将这种鸟的耕作喙描述为非常适合在地上觅食。
Near Synonyms:  fossorial,  foraging
Antonyms:  non-fossorial
