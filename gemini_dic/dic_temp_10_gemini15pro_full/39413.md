connascence:/kəˈnæsəns/ |n.| 共生性，内聚性 | Connascence is a measure of the interdependencies between software modules. It describes the degree to which a change in one module necessitates a change in another.  |  High connascence can make software difficult to maintain and evolve, as changes in one area can have ripple effects throughout the system. |  cohesion, coupling |  decoupling, modularity | noun
