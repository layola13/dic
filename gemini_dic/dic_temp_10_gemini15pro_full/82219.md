grottiness:/ˈɡrɒtɪnəs/ /ˈɡrɑːtɪnəs/ | n. | 肮脏；污秽；粗俗 | The state of being grotty; unpleasantness, dirtiness, or squalor. | The grottiness of the student apartments was appalling. 学生公寓的脏乱程度令人震惊。| The sheer grottiness of the place made me want to leave immediately. 这个地方的肮脏让我立刻就想离开。 | 近义词: dirtiness, filthiness, squalor, uncleanliness, untidiness | 反义词: cleanliness, neatness, tidiness | 词性: noun

詞源: From grotty + -ness.  "Grotty" originated in British slang, possibly influenced by "grotesque" and words like "grit."
