nonwinged:/ˈnɒnˌwɪŋd/ (British) /ˈnɑːnˌwɪŋd/ (American)| adj.|无翅的，没有翅膀的|Not having wings; wingless.|The nonwinged insects crawled along the ground. 无翅的昆虫沿着地面爬行。|The researcher studied the behavior of nonwinged ants.  研究人员研究了无翅蚂蚁的行为。|近义词：wingless, apterous|反义词:winged, alate|词性:形容词

Etymology:
non- + winged

non-:  prefix meaning "not," "without," from Latin non "not"

winged: from Old English *winged, past participle of wingian "to provide with wings," from wing (n.).
