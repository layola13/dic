millwright:/ˈmɪlˌraɪt/| n.| 磨坊工；机械安装修理工；装配工|A person who designs, builds, or maintains mills.  More broadly, a millwright installs, maintains, and repairs heavy machinery in a variety of settings, including factories, power plants, and construction sites.|The millwright was called in to fix the broken turbine at the power plant.  磨坊工被叫去修理发电厂坏了的涡轮机。|Synonyms: mechanic, machinist, fitter, repairman|Antonyms: N/A|noun
