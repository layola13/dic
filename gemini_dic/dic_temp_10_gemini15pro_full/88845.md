hogshead:/ˈhɒɡzhɛd (UK), ˈhɑːɡzˌhɛd (US)/| n. |大桶，大酒桶（尤指容量为63加仑的）|A large cask, especially one of a capacity of 63 gallons. |例句：The hogshead of wine was rolled into the cellar.  大桶葡萄酒被滚进了地窖。

例句：They imported several hogsheads of rum from Jamaica. 他们从牙买加进口了几桶朗姆酒。|近义词：cask, barrel, butt|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'small barrel' or 'flask' for contrasting size)|词性:noun
