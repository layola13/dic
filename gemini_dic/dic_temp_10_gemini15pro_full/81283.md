grantee:/ɡrænˈtiː/ /ɡrænˈtiː/
n.
受让人，被授予者
a person to whom something is granted (such as land or property)
例句：The grantee will receive the deed to the property. 受让人将收到该房产的契据。

例句：The grantee is responsible for paying the property taxes. 受让人有责任支付房产税。

近义词：recipient, beneficiary
反义词：grantor
词性：noun
