calving:/ˈkɑːvɪŋ/ /ˈkælviŋ/
noun
产犊; 冰川崩解; 分裂
The process of a cow giving birth to a calf.  The breaking away of a mass of ice from a glacier.  A splitting or separating.
例句：The farmer assisted with the difficult calving.  农民协助了难产的母牛。

例句：Calving season is a busy time on the ranch.  牧场上的产犊季节是一个繁忙的时期。


近义词：parturition (for animals), birthing, breaking off, splitting
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

calving:/ˈkɑːvɪŋ/ /ˈkælviŋ/
verb (intransitive)
(指母牛)产犊; (冰川)崩解; 分裂
(of a cow) To give birth to a calf. (of a glacier) To break off a mass of ice. To split or separate.
例句：The glacier was calving into the sea.  冰川崩解入海。

例句：The cows are expected to start calving next month.  预计奶牛们下个月开始产犊。


近义词：parturient (for animals), birthing, breaking off, splitting
反义词: N/A
词性: verb
