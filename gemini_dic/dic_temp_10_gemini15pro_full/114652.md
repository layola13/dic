malgracious:/mælˈɡreɪʃəs/
adjective
不礼貌的，粗鲁的
Unkind or discourteous in a way that suggests resentment or ill will.
例句：He gave a malgracious reply to her polite question. 他对她的礼貌提问给出了一个粗鲁的回答。

例句：She offered a malgracious apology, muttering under her breath. 她不情愿地道歉，小声咕哝着。

近义词：rude, impolite, ungracious, churlish, surly, uncivil
反义词：gracious, polite, courteous, civil

词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *malgracieux*, from mal- "bad, ill" + gracieux "gracious".
