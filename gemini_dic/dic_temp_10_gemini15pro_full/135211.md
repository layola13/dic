unostentatious:/ˌʌnɒstɛnˈteɪʃəs/ | adj. | 不炫耀的，不招摇的 | Not displaying wealth, knowledge, or achievement in a way that attracts attention; modest. |  She lived an unostentatious life, despite her considerable wealth. 尽管她非常富有，但她过着不张扬的生活。|  He gave an unostentatious but generous donation to the charity. 他向慈善机构捐了一笔不张扬但很慷慨的捐款。 | Synonyms: modest, unassuming, humble, low-key, understated | Antonyms: ostentatious, showy, flamboyant, pretentious | adjective
