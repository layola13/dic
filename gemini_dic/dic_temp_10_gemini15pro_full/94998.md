in-tow:/ɪn ˈtoʊ/
| adverbial phrase |被拖着，跟随；紧随其后|following closely behind someone or something, often literally being towed or pulled along.  Figuratively, it can mean under someone's control or influence.|The little girl followed her mother in-tow through the grocery store. 小女孩紧跟着妈妈穿过杂货店。|近义词：following, trailing, accompanying, tagging along|反义词：leading, preceding|phrase
