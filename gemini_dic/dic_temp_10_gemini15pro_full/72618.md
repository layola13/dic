flyby:/ˈflaɪbaɪ/| n.|飞越，掠过，近距离探测|A flight past a point, especially a close approach to a planet by a spacecraft in order to obtain scientific information.|The spacecraft made a flyby of Jupiter.  这艘宇宙飞船飞越了木星。|close approach, pass|landing|noun

flyby:/ˈflaɪbaɪ/| adj.|飞越的，掠过的|Relating to or involving a flyby.|The flyby mission gathered valuable data.  这次飞越任务收集了宝贵的数据。|passing, close-approach|stationary|adjective
