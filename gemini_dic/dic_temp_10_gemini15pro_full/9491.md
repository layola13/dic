antiroyal:/ˌæntiˈrɔɪəl/
adjective
反皇室的，反对君主制的
opposed to monarchy or royalism.
The antiroyal sentiment was strong in the country after the king's actions.  这个国家在国王采取行动后，反皇室情绪高涨。
The newspaper published several antiroyal articles. 这家报纸发表了几篇反君主制的文章。

synonyms:republican, anti-monarchist
antonyms:royalist, monarchist
word type:adjective
