charitably:/ˈtʃærɪtəbli/
adverb
慈善地；宽厚地；仁慈地
in a kind way, judging people’s mistakes and faults in a kind way
She spoke charitably of her ex-husband, despite their bitter divorce. 她宽厚地谈论她的前夫，尽管他们离婚时闹得很僵。
charitably:/ˈtʃærɪtəbli/
adverb
从慈善角度；为了慈善事业
for charitable purposes
The proceeds from the auction will be charitably donated to the local hospital. 拍卖所得将捐赠给当地医院。

近义词:kindly, generously, benevolently
反义词:uncharitably, cruelly, harshly
词性:adverb
