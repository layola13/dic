nonlosers:/ˌnɒnˈluːzərz/ | n. | 非失败者；成功者；赢家 |  Refers to individuals or teams who have not lost, often implying they have won or are successful. Formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to "losers." | Example: The nonlosers of the competition celebrated their victory. 比赛的获胜者们庆祝他们的胜利。 | Example:  The nonlosers will advance to the next round.  非失败者将晋级下一轮。 | Synonyms: winners, victors, champions | Antonyms: losers, defeated | Noun
