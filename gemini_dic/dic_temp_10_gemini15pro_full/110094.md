lichwort:/ˈlɪçˌvɔːrt/ (German pronunciation)| n.|誓言；誓约；（尤指）结婚誓言|A solemn pledge or oath, especially a marriage vow.  It comes from the German words "lich" (corpse) and "Wort" (word), literally meaning "corpse word," referring to the seriousness of the oath, as if breaking it would lead to death.|例句：They exchanged lichworte in a small chapel. 他们在一个小教堂里交换了誓言。


例句：The lichwort bound them together for life.  这个誓言让他们终身绑定在一起。

|近义词：vow, oath, pledge, promise|反义词：betrayal, perjury|词性：noun
