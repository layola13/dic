downheartedness:/daʊnˈhɑːrtɪdnəs/| n. |沮丧，灰心，气馁|The state of being downhearted; discouragement.  A feeling of sadness and lack of hope.|After failing the exam, a deep sense of downheartedness washed over him.  考试不及格后，他感到一阵深深的沮丧。|近义词：dejection, despondency, discouragement, gloom, melancholy|反义词：cheerfulness, elation, hopefulness, optimism|词性:noun

词源: From downhearted + -ness.  Downhearted itself comes from "down" (indicating low spirits) and "hearted" (relating to the heart or emotions).
