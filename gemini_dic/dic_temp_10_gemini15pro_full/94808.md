impulsively:/ɪmˈpʌlsɪvli/| adv.|冲动地，一时兴起地|In an impulsive manner; without forethought or consideration.|She impulsively bought a bright pink hat. 她一时兴起买了一顶亮粉色的帽子。|He impulsively quit his job without another lined up. 他一时冲动辞掉了工作，没有找好下家。|近义词：rashly, spontaneously, capriciously|反义词：deliberately, cautiously, thoughtfully|词性:副词
Etymology: From Late Latin *impulsivus, from Latin impulsus (“impulse”), past participle of impellō (“I push, drive, set in motion”).
