overimpressed: /ˌōvərɪmˈprɛst/ | adj. | 过于印象深刻的；过分感动的 | Excessively impressed; affected too strongly or favorably. | He seemed overimpressed by my modest achievements. 他似乎对我的小小成就印象过于深刻了。| The tourists were overimpressed by the ancient city. 游客们对这座古城印象非常深刻。| 近义词: awed, overwhelmed, dazzled | 反义词: unimpressed, indifferent | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "impressed" (past participle of "impress," from Latin "impressus," past participle of "imprimere" - to press into, imprint)
