guttate:/ˈɡʌteɪt/ | adj. |点状的，滴状的|Marked with spots or droplike markings.  Characterized by small, distinct spots resembling drops. |The patient presented with a guttate psoriasis rash. 病人出现点滴状牛皮癣皮疹。|spotted, speckled, dotted|diffuse, widespread|adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "guttatus" (dropped, spotted), past participle of "guttāre" (to drop), from "gutta" (a drop).
