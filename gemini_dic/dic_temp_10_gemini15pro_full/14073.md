australias:/əˈstreɪliəz/
noun
澳大利亚（复数，非标准用法）
Plural form of "Australia," typically considered incorrect.  While grammatically possible, it's almost never used as Australia is a singular entity.  One might encounter it humorously or in highly specific, unusual contexts.
Australias might be used in a science fiction context where there are parallel universes.
"In all the Australias I visited, this one had the best beaches."
Near Synonyms: (None, as it's a proper noun)
Antonyms: (None, as it's a proper noun)
