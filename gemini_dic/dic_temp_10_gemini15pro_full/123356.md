monologue: /ˈmɒnəlɒɡ/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnəlɔːɡ/ (US)
n. | 独白;  (戏剧、电影等中的)独角戏 | A long speech by one actor in a play or film, or as part of a theatrical or broadcast programme. |  Hamlet's famous "To be or not to be" monologue. 哈姆雷特的著名独白“生存还是毁灭”。| soliloquy, speech, address | dialogue, conversation | noun
