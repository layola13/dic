ichthyosis: /ˌɪkθiˈoʊsɪs/| n. | 鱼鳞癣；鱼鳞病 |A group of skin disorders characterized by dry, thickened, and scaly skin resembling fish scales.  It is caused by genetic mutations that affect the way skin cells are produced and shed. |  Example 1: The doctor diagnosed the baby with ichthyosis vulgaris, a common form of the condition.  医生诊断这个婴儿患有寻常型鱼鳞病，这是一种常见的鱼鳞病。

Example 2:  Living with ichthyosis can be challenging, requiring daily moisturizing and specialized skincare.  患有鱼鳞病的生活可能充满挑战，需要每天保湿和专门的皮肤护理。 | 近义词:  xeroderma, fish-scale disease | 反义词:  None (There is no direct opposite for a medical condition.) | 词性: noun
