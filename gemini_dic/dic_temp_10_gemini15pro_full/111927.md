loletas:/lɒˈliːtəz/ /loʊˈliːtəz/ | n. |洛丽塔（复数）|  Plural form of "lolita," a term derived from Vladimir Nabokov's novel "Lolita."  It refers to a sexually precocious young girl, or a style reminiscent of such a girl.  Its usage is often considered controversial and potentially offensive due to its association with the sexualization of minors. | The photographer specialized in portraits of loletas, a style he defended as artistic but others found disturbing.  这些摄影师专门拍摄洛丽塔风格的肖像，他辩称这是一种艺术，但其他人觉得很不安。 |Lolitas often wear clothing like short skirts and Peter Pan collars.  洛丽塔们经常穿短裙和彼得潘领之类的衣服。 | synonyms:  nymphets (highly controversial and offensive),  young girls (general and less specific) | antonyms:  matrons, adults (general antonyms conveying age difference) | noun
