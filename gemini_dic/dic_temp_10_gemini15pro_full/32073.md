cherup:/ˈtʃɛrəp/ | vi. | 啾啾叫，唧唧喳喳地叫 | To make a cheerful, chirping sound, like a bird. | The birds cheruped merrily in the trees. 鸟儿在树上欢快地啁啾。| 近义词: chirp, twitter, peep | 反义词: squawk, croak | verb

Etymology:  Imitative origin, echoing the sound.  Related to "chirp."
