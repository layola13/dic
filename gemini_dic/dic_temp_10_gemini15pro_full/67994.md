factionary:/ˈfækʃənəri/ | adj. | 派系的，宗派的；党派的 | Of or relating to a faction; partisan. |  The factionary disputes within the party threatened to tear it apart.  党内的派系纠纷有可能导致党的分裂。 | synonyms: partisan, sectarian, cliquish | antonyms: united, harmonious | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *factionarius, from Latin factio (faction).
