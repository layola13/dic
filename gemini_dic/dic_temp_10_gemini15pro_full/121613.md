misoriented:/ˌmɪsɔːriˈɛntɪd/ | adj. | 迷失方向的，不辨方向的 | Incorrectly oriented; having lost one's bearings or sense of direction. | The jet lag left her feeling completely misoriented. 时差让她完全迷失了方向。 | synonyms: lost, bewildered, confused, disoriented | antonyms: oriented, directed, guided | adjective

misoriented:/ˌmɪsɔːriˈɛntɪd/ | adj. | 弄错方向的，方向错误的 | Positioned or aligned incorrectly. | The crystals in the metal were misoriented, causing it to fracture. 金属中的晶体方向错误，导致其断裂。 | synonyms: misaligned, misplaced, awry | antonyms: aligned, positioned, correctly oriented | adjective
