blacking:/ˈblækɪŋ/| n.|涂黑；黑鞋油；擦鞋|A substance used to make something black, especially shoes.|He gave his shoes a good blacking. 他把他的鞋好好地擦黑了。|近义词：polish, dye|反义词:whitening|noun

blacking:/ˈblækɪŋ/| n.|涂黑；遮蔽|The act of making something black or dark.|The blacking of the windows made the room dark.  把窗户涂黑让房间变暗了。|近义词：darkening, obscuring|反义词:illumination|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *blaking, from Old English *blæcing, verbal noun from blæcian (“to blacken, make black”), from blæc (“black”).  Cognate with Dutch blacking, German Blackung.
