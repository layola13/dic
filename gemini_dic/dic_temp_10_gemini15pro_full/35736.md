cochairing:/ˌkoʊˈtʃɛrɪŋ/ | v. n. | 共同主持，联合主持 | The act of presiding over a meeting or other event jointly with another person.  |  Example: They are cochairing the conference this year. 他们今年共同主持这次会议。 | Example:  The cochairing of the event ensured a smooth transition between speakers.  共同主持这项活动确保了演讲者之间的顺利过渡。 | Synonyms: co-presiding, joint chairmanship | Antonyms: sole chairmanship | verb/noun

cochair:/ˈkoʊˌtʃɛr/ | n. v. | 共同主席，联合主席；共同主持，联合主持 |  A person who presides over a meeting or other event jointly with another person. | Example:  She was the cochair of the fundraising committee. 她是筹款委员会的联合主席。 | Example: He will cochair the meeting with his colleague. 他将和他的同事共同主持会议。 | Synonyms: co-president, joint chairman | Antonyms: sole chairman | noun/verb
