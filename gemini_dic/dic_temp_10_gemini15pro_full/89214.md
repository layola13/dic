hometown:/ˈhoʊmtaʊn/ (US) /ˈhəʊmtaʊn/ (UK)| n. |家乡；故乡|The place where one was born or grew up; the place of one's principal residence during one's childhood. |I always enjoy going back to my hometown. 我总是喜欢回到我的家乡。|Synonyms: birthplace, native place, home city, home village |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "foreign land," "abroad")|noun
