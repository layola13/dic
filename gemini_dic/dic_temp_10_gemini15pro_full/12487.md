asperate:/ˈæspərət/| adj. |送气的；粗糙的；发怒的|pronounced with an exhalation of breath; rough; irritated|The aspirated consonants are harder to pronounce. 送气辅音更难发音。|近义词：rough, harsh, irritated|反义词：smooth, soft, calm|词性：形容词
