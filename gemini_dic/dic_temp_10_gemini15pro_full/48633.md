defended:/dɪˈfɛndɪd/
verb
已辩护，已防护
past tense and past participle of defend
He defended his actions. 他为自己的行为辩护。
近义词：protected, guarded, shielded, advocated for
反义词：attacked, assaulted, prosecuted, surrendered
词性: verb

defend:/dɪˈfɛnd/
verb
保卫，辩护，防护
to protect someone or something from attack
She defended her child from the dog. 她保护她的孩子免受狗的攻击。
近义词：protect, guard, shield, advocate for
反义词：attack, assault, prosecute, surrender
词性: verb
