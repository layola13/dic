brucelloses:/bruːsəˈlʊsiːz/| n. |布鲁氏菌病|An infectious bacterial disease that mainly affects cattle, sheep, goats, and pigs, causing undulant fever. It can be transmitted to humans through contact with infected animals or contaminated animal products.|

例句：Brucellosis is a zoonotic disease that can cause serious health problems in humans. 布鲁氏菌病是一种人畜共患病，会对人类健康造成严重危害。

例句：The farmer vaccinated his cattle against brucellosis. 农民给他的牛接种了布鲁氏菌病疫苗。

近义词：Undulant fever, Malta fever, Mediterranean fever|反义词: (No direct antonym for a disease) |词性:noun


词源：From Brucella + -osis. Named after Sir David Bruce, who identified the causative bacterium.
