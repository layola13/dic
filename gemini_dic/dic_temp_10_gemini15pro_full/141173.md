ooezoa: /ˌoʊiˈzoʊə/ | n. |  卵生动物 | Animals that reproduce by laying eggs.  |  Ooezoa include birds, reptiles, and most fish. 卵生动物包括鸟类、爬行动物和大多数鱼类。| 近义词: oviparous animals | 反义词: viviparous animals | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek  *ōo-* (egg) + *zōion* (animal).
