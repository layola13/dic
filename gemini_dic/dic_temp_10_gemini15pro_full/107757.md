lapfuls:/ˈlæpfʊlz/| n. |满怀，一抱之量|The child gathered lapfuls of fallen leaves. 孩子收集了满满一怀的落叶。|例句：She carried lapfuls of kittens into the house. 她抱着一堆小猫进了屋子。

例句：He brought in lapfuls of firewood. 他抱进来满满一怀的柴火。|近义词：armfuls, handfuls|反义词:|词性:名词

词源:
From "lap" (one's lap) + "-ful" (suffix indicating fullness). "Lap" comes from Old English "læppa" and Proto-Germanic "*lappō". "-ful" comes from Old English "-full".
