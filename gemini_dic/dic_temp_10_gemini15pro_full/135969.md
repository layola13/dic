nonrabbinical:/ˌnɑːnˌræˈbɪnɪkəl/
adjective
非拉比的；非犹太教律法学者的
Not pertaining to, characteristic of, or adhering to the teachings or interpretations of the rabbis.
This commentary offers a nonrabbinical perspective on the Old Testament.  这本评论集提供了对旧约的非拉比视角。
The book explored nonrabbinical Jewish traditions. 这本书探讨了非拉比的犹太传统。
近义词: non-rabbinic, secular (in a Jewish context)
反义词: rabbinical, rabbinic
