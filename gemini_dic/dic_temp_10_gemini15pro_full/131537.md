noncommendable:/ˌnɒnkəˈmɛndəbl/ | adj. | 不值得称赞的，不值得推荐的 | Not deserving praise or recommendation; not commendable. |  His behavior was noncommendable and resulted in his dismissal. 他的行为不值得称赞，导致他被解雇。

His noncommendable actions led to his suspension from the team. 他不值得称赞的行为导致他被停赛。 | 近义词: undesirable, unacceptable, reprehensible, blameworthy | 反义词: commendable, laudable, praiseworthy | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Latin origin, meaning "not"
commendable:  From Late Middle English *commendable, from Anglo-Norman and Old French commendable, from commendar (“to commend, praise, entrust”) + -able (“capable of, worthy of”). Ultimately from Latin commendō (“I entrust, recommend”).
