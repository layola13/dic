metacausation:/ˌmɛtəkɔːˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |元因果; 超因果 |The philosophical concept of causation at a higher level, often involving the causes of causes or the influence of higher-order factors on lower-level causal processes. It explores the relationship between different levels of causation. |  Metacausation is a complex concept that explores how higher-level systems influence lower-level causal processes. 元因果是一个复杂的概念，探讨了更高级别的系统如何影响低级别的因果过程。

Metacausation might explain how social structures influence individual behavior. 元因果可以解释社会结构如何影响个人行为。 | 近义词: meta-causality, higher-order causation | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: noun
