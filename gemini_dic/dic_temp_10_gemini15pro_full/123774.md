moos:/muːz/| v. n.|哞哞叫；低沉的叫声|The cow moos loudly.  母牛大声地哞哞叫。|例句：The cows were mooing in the field. 母牛在地里哞哞叫。

The lonely foghorn moos across the bay. 孤独的雾号在海湾上发出低沉的鸣响。|近义词：low, bellow|反义词:whisper, murmur|词性:verb, noun

Etymology:  From Middle English *mowen, from Old English *mūgan (“to low”), of imitative origin. Cognate with Dutch moeën (“to low”), German muhen (“to moo”).
