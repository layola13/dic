concessively:/kənˈsesɪvli/
adverb
让步地；承认地
in a way that acknowledges that something is true or valid, often reluctantly or unwillingly.
"All right," he said concessively, "I'll wait till tomorrow." “好吧，”他勉强同意地说，“我等到明天。”
concedingly, reluctantly, grudgingly
assertively, definitively, stubbornly
词性: 副词

concessively:/kənˈsesɪvli/
adverb
退一步说；迁就地
Making a concession; yielding.
"Perhaps you are right," she said concessively. “也许你是对的，”她让步地说。
yielding, accommodating, compromising
intransigent, unyielding, inflexible
词性: 副词
