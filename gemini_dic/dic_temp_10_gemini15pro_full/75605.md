fufus:/ˈfuːfuːz/| n. |非洲淀粉类食物，通常由煮熟后捣碎的块茎类蔬菜制成|A thick paste made from starchy vegetables like yam, cassava, or plantains, boiled and pounded. It is a staple food in West Africa. |She served the stew with a generous portion of fufus. 她用大量的非洲淀粉食物配炖菜。|近义词：fufu, pounded yam, akpu (depending on the base ingredient)|反义词: (No direct antonym for a food item)|词性:名词
