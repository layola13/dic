extravagantly: /ɪkˈstrævəɡəntli/ | adv. | 挥霍地，奢侈地 | In a way that spends more money than is reasonable or necessary. | She lived extravagantly, spending thousands of dollars on clothes each month. 她生活奢侈，每个月在衣服上花费数千美元。 | He furnished his apartment extravagantly with designer furniture. 他用名牌家具奢侈地装饰了他的公寓。| 近义词： lavishly, profusely, immoderately | 反义词： frugally, economically, thriftily | 词性: 副词

extravagant: /ɪkˈstrævəɡənt/ | adj. | 挥霍的，奢侈的 | Spending more money than is reasonable or necessary. |  Her extravagant lifestyle led her into debt. 她奢侈的生活方式让她负债累累。 | He made extravagant promises that he couldn't keep. 他做出了无法兑现的奢侈承诺。 | 近义词： lavish, profuse, immoderate | 反义词： frugal, economical, thrifty | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Late Latin *extravagans*, present participle of extravagari "wander outside or beyond," from Latin extra "outside" + vagari "to wander."  The original sense was "deviating from a defined limit," later "excessive, unrestrained."
