orientable:/ˈɔːriənˌtəbl/ |adj.|可定向的|Able to be assigned a consistent orientation, especially having two sides (such as an inside and an outside).|A Möbius strip is not orientable. 莫比乌斯环是不可定向的。|The sphere is an orientable surface. 球面是一个可定向曲面。|近义词:two-sided|反义词:non-orientable|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From orient + -able.  Orient ultimately derives from Latin *oriens*, present participle of *orīrī* "to rise".
