obituarily:/oʊˈbɪtʃuˌɛrɪli/ | adv. | 讣告地；在讣告中；有关讣告地 | In a manner relating to or characteristic of an obituary; in a way that commemorates the deceased. | He spoke obituarily of his lost friend. 他以讣告的方式谈到了他失去的朋友。 |  Synonym: commemoratively, elegiacally | Antonym:  joyfully,  celebratorily | Part of Speech: adverb

obituary:/oʊˈbɪtʃuˌɛri/ | n. | 讣告，讣闻 | A notice of a death, especially in a newspaper, typically including a brief biography of the deceased person. | I read her obituary in the local paper. 我在当地报纸上读到了她的讣告。 | Synonym: death notice, necrology | Antonym: birth announcement | Part of Speech: noun
