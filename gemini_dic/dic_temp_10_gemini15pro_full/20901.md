bleached:/blitʃt/| adj.|漂白的；褪色的；（脸色）苍白的|Whitened by exposure to sunlight or chemicals; having lost its original color.|例句：The bleached bones of a long-dead animal lay in the sun.  早已死去的动物的漂白骨头躺在阳光下。

例句：Her hair was bleached blonde. 她的头发漂成了金色。|近义词：whitened, faded, pale, blanched|反义词：colored, dyed, tanned, dark|词性:形容词

词源: From bleach + -ed,  bleach comes from Old English *blǣcan "to make white, pale," from Proto-Germanic *blaikijanan "to bleach" (source also of Old Norse bleikja "to bleach," Middle Dutch bleken, Dutch bleken, Old High German bleichen, German bleichen "to bleach").
