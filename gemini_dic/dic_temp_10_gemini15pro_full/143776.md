outmodedness:/ˌaʊtˈmoʊdɪdnəs/ | n. | 过时，陈旧 | The outmodedness of their equipment put them at a disadvantage. | 例句：The outmodedness of their technology hindered their progress.  他们的技术过时阻碍了他们的进步。

例句：The outmodedness of his views made him seem out of touch. 他的观点过时让他看起来与时代脱节。 | 近义词：obsolescence, antiquatedness, oldness, archaism | 反义词：modernity, currency, newness | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From outmoded + -ness.  Outmoded itself comes from out- + mode + -ed.  "Mode" comes from French, ultimately from Latin "modus," meaning "manner, measure, method."  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
