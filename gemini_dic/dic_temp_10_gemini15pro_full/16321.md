barb:/bɑːrb/| n.|倒钩，倒刺；尖刻的话|A sharp projection near the end of an arrow, fishhook, or similar object, angled away from the main point so as to make it difficult to remove.|The fishhook has a barb to prevent the fish from escaping. 鱼钩上有倒钩，防止鱼逃脱。|近义词：point, spike, prickle|反义词:bluntness|noun

barb:/bɑːrb/| v.|装倒钩于|To furnish with a barb or barbs.|He barbed the wire to make a more secure fence. 他在铁丝上装了倒钩，使围栏更安全。|近义词：sharpen, point|反义词:blunt|verb
