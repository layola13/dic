cusd:/kʌsd/ | n. | 加密美元 |  An abbreviation for "Crypto USD," a stablecoin pegged to the US dollar. |  Example: I bought some CUSD to avoid the volatility of other cryptocurrencies. 我买了一些加密美元以避免其他加密货币的波动性。 | Example: The price of CUSD remained stable despite the market fluctuations. 尽管市场波动，加密美元的价格保持稳定。 | Synonym: stablecoin, digital dollar | Antonym: Bitcoin, Ethereum | noun
