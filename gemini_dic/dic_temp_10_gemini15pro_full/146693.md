overhearty:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrti/| adj.|过分热情的；过分殷勤的；过于丰盛的|Excessively hearty; too enthusiastic or effusive; too rich or abundant (especially of food).|He gave us an overhearty welcome, which made us feel a little uncomfortable. 他对我们过于热情的欢迎让我们感到有些不自在。|The overhearty meal left me feeling sluggish. 过于丰盛的饭菜让我感到很迟钝。|近义词：excessive, effusive, lavish, exuberant|反义词：reserved, restrained, moderate, understated|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "hearty" (adjective meaning "warmly friendly and cheerful; (of food) wholesome and substantial").
