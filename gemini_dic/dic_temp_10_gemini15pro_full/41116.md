coproducing:/koʊprəˈdusɪŋ/
verb
共同制作；联合制作
The film was coproduced by companies in the US and Japan. 这部电影是由美国和日本的公司联合制作的。
coproduce (present participle)
例句：
They are coproducing a new Broadway musical. 他们正在联合制作一部新的百老汇音乐剧。
The two countries are coproducing a documentary on climate change. 这两个国家正在联合制作一部关于气候变化的纪录片。

近义词：collaborating, joint producing
反义词：solely producing

词性：v.

词源：co- (together) + producing (present participle of produce)
