belatedness:/bɪˈleɪtɪdnəs/
n.
迟到；延误；晚发生
the fact of being late or delayed
例句：Please accept our apologies for the belatedness of this reply.  对于此回复的延迟，请接受我们的歉意。

例句：The belatedness of the announcement caused confusion.  公告的延迟引起了混乱。

近义词：lateness, tardiness, delay
反义词：promptness, timeliness
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From belated + -ness. Belated comes from the Old French belater "to leave till late," from be- "thoroughly" + later "late."
