danceress:/ˈdænsərɪs/ | n. | 女舞蹈家，女舞者 | A female dancer.  |  例句：The danceress captivated the audience with her graceful movements.  这位女舞蹈家以其优雅的动作吸引了观众。 | 近义词：dancer, ballerina (for ballet dancers) | 反义词：None (lack of a direct antonym, could consider audience, spectator as opposites in terms of role) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Middle English *daunseresse, from Old French danceresse, from Late Latin dantia and the suffix -issa. Ultimately from Latin *dantiare, frequentative of dantare, to dance.
