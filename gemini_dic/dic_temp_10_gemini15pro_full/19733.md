billabong:/ˈbɪləbɒŋ/ (UK), /ˈbɪləbɔːŋ/ (US)| n. | 死水潭，洼地，干河床 |A billabong is a stagnant pool of water left behind after a river changes course, typically found in Australia.  It's an isolated stretch of water formed when a river changes course and leaves a section behind. |  Example:  The kangaroos gathered at the billabong to drink. 袋鼠聚集在死水潭边喝水。

Example:  The billabong was a haven for wildlife during the drought. 在干旱期间，死水潭是野生动物的避难所。| 近义词:oxbow lake, lagoon, backwater | 反义词: river, stream | 词性:noun
