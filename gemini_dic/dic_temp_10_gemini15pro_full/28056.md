capellmeister: /kəˈpɛlˌmaɪstər/ | n. | 乐队指挥，管弦乐队指挥 | A person who directs an orchestra or choir, especially in Germany or Austria.  A conductor, especially of an opera or church choir. |  Example: The renowned capellmeister led the orchestra in a breathtaking performance of Beethoven's Fifth Symphony.  著名的乐队指挥带领乐团进行了一场激动人心的贝多芬第五交响曲的演出。

Example:  The capellmeister meticulously rehearsed each section of the opera. 乐队指挥一丝不苟地排练了歌剧的每个部分。 | Synonym: conductor, director, maestro | Antonym: N/A | Noun
