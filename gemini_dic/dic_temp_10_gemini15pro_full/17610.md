beaver:/ˈbiːvər/ (US), /ˈbiːvə/ (UK)| n.|海狸，河狸|A large semi-aquatic rodent with strong teeth and a flat tail, known for building dams and lodges.|例句：The beaver built a dam across the stream. 海狸在溪流上筑起了一座水坝。

例句：Beavers are known for their industriousness. 海狸以勤劳著称。

近义词：rodent, |反义词:None|noun
