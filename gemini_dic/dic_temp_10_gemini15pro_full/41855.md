coryphodon:/kɔːˈrɪfədɒn/ | n. | 冠齿兽 | An extinct genus of large, browsing mammals, characteristic of the early Eocene epoch. |  Coryphodon fossils have been found in North America and Europe.  冠齿兽化石在北美和欧洲都有发现。|  The coryphodon was a herbivore that lived in swampy areas.  冠齿兽是一种生活在沼泽地区的食草动物。 | synonyms: pantodont, uintathere | antonyms: N/A | noun
