elucidatory:/ɪˈlusɪdeɪtəri/ /ɪˈlusɪˌtɔːri/
adjective
阐明的，解释性的
Making something clear or easy to understand.
Her elucidatory comments helped everyone grasp the complex theory. 她的解释性评论帮助每个人理解了这个复杂的理论。

The professor's elucidatory lecture clarified the confusing topic. 教授的阐释性讲座阐明了这个令人困惑的话题。

explanatory, clarifying, illuminating, interpretive
confusing, obscure, ambiguous
