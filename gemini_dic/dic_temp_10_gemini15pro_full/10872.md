arachnid:/əˈræk.nɪd/ | n. |节肢动物门蛛形纲动物|An arthropod of the class Arachnida, such as a spider, scorpion, mite, or tick.|Spiders are arachnids, not insects. 蜘蛛是蛛形纲动物，而不是昆虫。|The scorpion is a venomous arachnid. 蝎子是一种有毒的蛛形纲动物。|近义词:spider, scorpion, mite, tick|反义词:insect (in a broader sense, as arachnids are not insects) |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀράχνη (arákhnē, “spider”) + -id.
