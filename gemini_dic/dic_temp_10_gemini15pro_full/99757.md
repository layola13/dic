intermede:/ˈɪntərmiːd/| n.|幕间节目，插曲，间奏曲|A short entertainment performed between the acts of a play or other dramatic performance, or between the sections of a concert.|The orchestra played a lively intermede between the first and second acts.|近义词：interlude, interval, intermission|反义词:main act, principal performance|词性:noun
Etymology: From French *intermède*, from Latin *intermedius* ("intermediate").
