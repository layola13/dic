consultant:/kənˈsʌltənt/ | n. | 顾问，咨询师 | A person who provides expert advice professionally. |  We hired a consultant to help us improve our marketing strategy. 我们聘请了一位顾问来帮助我们改进营销策略。

The consultant's advice proved invaluable to the project.  这位顾问的建议对该项目 proved invaluable. | 近义词: advisor, expert, specialist | 反义词: amateur, novice | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From the Late Latin *consultant-, present participle of consultare "to consult," from Latin consulere "to take counsel, deliberate, consult."
