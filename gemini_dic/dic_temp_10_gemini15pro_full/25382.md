bulblike:/ˈbʌlbˌlaɪk/| adj.|球茎状的，像球茎的|Resembling a bulb in shape; rounded and swollen.|The plant had bulblike roots. 这种植物有球茎状的根。|近义词：bulbous, rounded, swollen|反义词：slender, narrow, elongated|词性:形容词
Etymology: bulb + like.  "Bulb" comes from Late Latin bulbus, from Greek bolbos, meaning "onion" or any bulbous plant.  "Like" comes from Old English gelic, meaning "similar, resembling."
