addictive:/əˈdɪktɪv/| adj.|使人上瘾的；令人沉迷的|Something that is addictive is so enjoyable that it makes you want to do it or have it repeatedly.|Video games can be addictive. 电子游戏很容易让人上瘾。|近义词：habit-forming, compelling, captivating|反义词：repulsive, off-putting|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin addictus (assigned, devoted), past participle of addicere (to assign, devote).  The modern sense of "causing addiction" developed in the late 19th century.

USAGE->
Attributive Usage (修饰名词):

These addictive painkillers can lead to serious health problems. = 这些容易上瘾的止痛药会导致严重的健康问题。
The addictive nature of social media is a growing concern. = 社交媒体的成瘾性是一个日益令人担忧的问题。


Predicative Usage (作表语):

Gambling can be highly addictive. = 赌博很容易上瘾。
This new game is so addictive! = 这个新游戏太让人上瘾了！


With "find" or "consider" something addictive:

Many people find coffee addictive. = 很多人觉得咖啡容易上瘾。
I consider shopping to be addictive. = 我认为购物会上瘾。


Used with adverbs like "highly," "very," "extremely":

Nicotine is a highly addictive substance. = 尼古丁是一种高度上瘾的物质。
This show is extremely addictive. = 这个节目非常令人上瘾。



PHRASE->
highly addictive = 非常容易上瘾的
addictive personality = 容易上瘾的性格
addictive behavior = 成瘾行为
addictive substance = 成瘾物质
addictive drug = 成瘾性药物
