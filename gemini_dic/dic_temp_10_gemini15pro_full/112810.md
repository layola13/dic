lucernaria:/ˌluːsərˈnɛəriə/
n.
灯笼水母;冠水母
A stalked jellyfish of the class Stauromedusae.
Lucernaria quadricornis is a species of stalked jellyfish.
灯笼水母四角水母是一种带柄的水母。
近义词: stalked jellyfish, stauromedusa
反义词: None (Specific antithetical term doesn't exist for a biological organism)
词性: Noun


Etymology:  From Latin *lucerna* ("lamp, lantern") + *-aria* (suffix forming nouns relating to place or things).  So named because of its resemblance to an oil lamp.
