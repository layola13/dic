hedgingly:/ˈhedʒɪŋli/
| adv. |小心翼翼地，谨慎地，不置可否地|In a manner that avoids commitment or taking a definite position.  Expressing oneself cautiously or evasively.|He answered hedgingly, unwilling to commit to a specific course of action. 他回答得含糊其辞，不愿承诺采取具体的行动方案。|Synonyms: cautiously, guardedly, noncommittally, evasively, ambiguously|Antonyms: directly, frankly, openly, forthrightly|词性:副词

hedging:/ˈhedʒɪŋ/
| n. |对冲，规避|The act of protecting oneself against loss on a bet or investment by undertaking a countervailing transaction.|The company uses currency hedging to protect itself from exchange rate fluctuations.  这家公司使用货币对冲来保护自己免受汇率波动的影响。|Synonyms: safeguard, protection, insurance|Antonyms: speculation, gamble|词性:名词
