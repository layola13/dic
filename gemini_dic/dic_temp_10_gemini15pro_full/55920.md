divvied:/dɪˈvaɪd/ | v. | 分配，分摊 | To share or distribute something among a group of people. | They divvied up the profits from the sale. 他们分配了销售的利润. | We divvied the cake between us. 我们把蛋糕分了. | 近义词：distribute, share, split, allocate | 反义词： hoard, keep, combine | 词性: 动词

词源:  "divvy" is a back-formation from "dividend," which comes from the Latin "dividendum," meaning "something to be divided."
