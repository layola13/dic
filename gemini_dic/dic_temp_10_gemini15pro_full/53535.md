disavowable:/ˌdɪsəˈvaʊəbl/
adjective
可否认的，可拒绝承认的
Capable of being disavowed; that may be denied.
The politician's past statements were disavowable, allowing him to shift his stance.  这位政治家过去的声明是可以否认的，这让他能够改变立场。
The contract contained a disavowable clause, protecting the company from liability.  合同中包含一个可否认的条款，保护公司免于承担责任。
近义词:repudiable, deniable, recantable
反义词:acknowledgeable, admissible, undeniable
词性:adjective

词源:
From disavow + -able.  Disavow comes from the Old French desavouer, from des- (expressing reversal) + avouer "to avow," from Latin advocare "to summon."
