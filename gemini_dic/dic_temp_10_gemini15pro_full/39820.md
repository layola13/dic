conspicuous consumption:/kənˈspɪkjuəs kənˈsʌmpʃən/ | n. | 炫耀性消费 |  The spending of money on and the acquiring of luxury goods and services to publicly display economic power—either the buyer's income or the buyer's accumulated wealth. |  He engaged in conspicuous consumption, buying expensive cars and clothes to impress others. 他进行炫耀性消费，购买昂贵的汽车和衣服以给他人留下深刻印象。 | synonyms: ostentatious consumption, extravagant spending, lavish display,炫耀, 挥霍 | antonyms: frugality, thrift, modesty,  节俭, 朴素 | noun
