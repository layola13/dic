disopinion:/ˌdɪsəˈpɪnjən/
n.
difference of opinion; disagreement
A difference of opinion; disagreement.  The two parties held a strong disopinion on the matter.  双方在这个问题上存在严重分歧。
He voiced his disopinion about the new policy. 他表达了对新政策的不同意见。
近义词: disagreement, dissent, discord, variance
反义词: agreement, concord, harmony, accord
词性: 名词
