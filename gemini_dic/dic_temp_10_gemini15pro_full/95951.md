inculcate:/ɪnˈkʌlkeɪt/ /ɪnˈkʌlˌkeɪt/ | vt. |灌输；谆谆教诲 |To instill (an attitude, idea, or habit) by persistent instruction.|例句：The teacher inculcated in her students the importance of hard work. 老师向学生灌输努力学习的重要性。

例句：Parents try to inculcate a sense of responsibility in their children. 父母试图培养孩子的责任感。

近义词：instill, implant, ingrain, impress, imbue |反义词: eradicate, efface, extirpate |词性: verb (动词)
