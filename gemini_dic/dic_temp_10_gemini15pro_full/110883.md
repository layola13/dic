loneliest:/ˈloʊnliɪst/ | adj. |最孤独的，最寂寞的| Superlative form of lonely: most lonely.  Experiencing, causing, or marked by the state of being without companions; solitary.|  例句：She felt loneliest at night. 她晚上感觉最孤独。| 近义词：most solitary, most isolated, most desolate | 反义词：most sociable, most gregarious | 词性：形容词
