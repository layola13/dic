outwhirl:/ˌaʊtˈhwɜːrl/
verb
超过旋转，胜过旋转
To whirl faster or more powerfully than.
The new turbine design is expected to outwhirl all previous models. 新的涡轮机设计预计将超过所有以前的型号。
The smaller, more agile fighter plane was able to outwhirl its larger opponent.  更小、更灵活的战斗机能够胜过它更大的对手。
近义词: outspin, surpass, exceed
反义词: lag, trail
