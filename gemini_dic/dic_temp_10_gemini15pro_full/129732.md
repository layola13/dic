non-enteric:/nɒn-enˈtɛrɪk/
adjective
非肠道的，不经肠道的
Not pertaining to, affecting, or involving the intestines.  Relating to a route of administration other than through the digestive tract (e.g., intravenous, intramuscular).
The doctor prescribed non-enteric coated aspirin to avoid stomach upset. 医生开了非肠溶阿司匹林以避免胃部不适。
Non-enteric medications are absorbed directly into the bloodstream. 非肠道药物直接吸收到血液中。
Synonyms: parenteral, extra-intestinal
Antonyms: enteric, intestinal
