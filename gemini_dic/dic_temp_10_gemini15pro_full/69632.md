feminization:/ˌfɛmɪnɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌfɛmənɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 女性化 | The process of becoming more feminine, or making something more feminine. | The feminization of education has led to more female teachers and administrators. 教育的女性化导致了更多的女性教师和管理人员。|  The feminization of poverty is a growing concern. 贫困女性化是一个日益严重的问题。 | synonyms: effeminacy, softening | antonyms: masculinization | noun
