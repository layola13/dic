dissociate:/dɪˈsoʊsieɪt/ /dɪˈsəʊʃɪeɪt/ |adj. |分离的，分裂的|Not associated or connected.|dissociative disorders 分离性障碍|synonyms: separate, detached, disconnected|antonyms: associated, connected, united|adjective
