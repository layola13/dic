assessed:/əˈsɛst/| v. |评估，评定|To determine or fix the value or amount of something.  To judge the quality or importance of something. |The damage to the car was assessed at $2,000.  这辆车的损失估计为2000美元。|Synonyms: evaluated, appraised, judged, estimated|Antonyms: ignored, disregarded|verb
