nonaffecting:/ˌnɑːnəˈfɛktɪŋ/
adj.
不影响的，无关的
Not producing any significant or noticeable effect; not influencing or altering something.
The nonaffecting nature of the new law means things will continue as before. 这项新法律的不影响性质意味着事情将照旧进行。
The change in leadership was nonaffecting to the company's daily operations. 领导层的变动对公司的日常运营没有影响。
近义词:  insignificant, inconsequential, neutral, harmless
反义词:  affecting, influencing, impactful
词性: 形容词
