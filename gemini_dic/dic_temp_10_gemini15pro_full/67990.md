factionalism:/ˈfækʃənəlɪzəm/
n.
派系主义；党派之争
The tendency to form or promote factions; dissent and conflict within a group or organization.
The factionalism within the political party threatened to tear it apart.  该政党的派系斗争可能会使其分裂。
Internal factionalism has weakened the union. 内部派系斗争削弱了工会的力量。
近义词: infighting, dissension, discord, strife, division
反义词: unity, harmony, solidarity, cooperation
词性: noun

Etymology:  Late 18th century: from faction + -alism.
