cirrhotics:/sɪˈrɒtɪks/ | n. | 肝硬化患者 | People with cirrhosis, a late stage of scarring (fibrosis) of the liver caused by many forms of liver diseases and conditions, such as hepatitis and chronic alcoholism. | The cirrhotics in the study showed improved liver function after the treatment.  这项研究中的肝硬化患者在治疗后肝功能有所改善。 |  近义词: patients with cirrhosis, individuals with cirrhosis | 反义词: healthy individuals, people without liver disease | 词性: 名词

cirrhotic:/sɪˈrɒtɪk/ | adj. | 肝硬化的 | Relating to or affected with cirrhosis of the liver. | The patient's cirrhotic liver was severely damaged.  病人的肝硬化肝脏严重受损。| 近义词: relating to cirrhosis, affected by cirrhosis | 反义词: healthy, non-cirrhotic | 词性: 形容词
