masticate:/ˈmæstɪkeɪt/ (US), /ˈmæstɪkeɪt/ (UK)| v. | 咀嚼，细嚼；深思熟虑 | To chew (food) slowly and thoroughly.  To think about (something) carefully and thoroughly. |  The cow was slowly masticating its cud.  牛正在慢慢地咀嚼反刍的食物。

The committee masticated the proposal for weeks before making a decision. 委员会在做出决定之前，对这项提议仔细斟酌了数周。 |  chew, munch, ruminate, ponder, deliberate | swallow, gulp, skim, ignore | verb

masticating:/ˈmæstɪkeɪtɪŋ/ (US), /ˈmæstɪkeɪtɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. | 咀嚼的; 磨碎的 |  Involved in or relating to the act of chewing. | The masticating muscles are powerful. 咀嚼肌非常强壮。

The cow has strong masticating teeth. 牛有强壮的咀嚼齿。 | chewing, grinding, munching | swallowing, gulping | adjective
