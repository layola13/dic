enframe:/ɪnˈfreɪm/ | vt. | 装框, 构成框架, 加框 | To enclose in or as if in a frame; to give structure or form to something. | The photograph was enframed in a simple silver frame. 这张照片装在一个简单的银框里。

The constitution enframes the fundamental rights of the citizens. 宪法构成了公民基本权利的框架。 | 近义词: frame, enclose, encompass, surround | 反义词: release, free | verb
