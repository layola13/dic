overattentions:/ˌoʊvərəˈtɛnʃənz/ | n. | 过度关注，过分关心 | Excessive attention, often to the point of being intrusive or overwhelming. |  He showered her with overattentions, which made her uncomfortable. 他对她过分关心，让她感到不舒服。|  The child's overattentions to details can be a sign of autism. 孩子对细节的过度关注可能是自闭症的迹象。| 近义词: fussiness, solicitude, overcarefulness | 反义词: neglect, inattention | 词性: 名词
