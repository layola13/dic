ETYMOLOGY->
de- (prefix): from Latin, meaning "down, away, off, thoroughly"
criminal (root): from Latin "criminalis" (relating to crime), from "crimen" (crime, accusation)
-ise/-ize (suffix):  forms verbs meaning "to make or become"
