creolizes:/ˈkriːəlaɪzɪz/
verb
克里奥尔化；融合；混合
to cause to become creolized; to blend or mix (different cultures, languages, etc.)
The language creolized over time, incorporating elements of French and African languages.  这种语言随着时间的推移而克里奥尔化，融合了法语和非洲语言的元素。
The culture of the island has creolized, blending indigenous traditions with those of European settlers.  岛上的文化已经克里奥尔化，融合了本土传统和欧洲殖民者的传统。
近义词: blend, mix, merge, fuse, combine, amalgamate
反义词: separate, divide, segregate
词性: v.
