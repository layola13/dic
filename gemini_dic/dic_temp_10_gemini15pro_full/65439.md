euplexoptera:/juːplɛkˈsɒptərə/
n.
革翅目；蠼螋
earwigs; an order of insects, characterized by cerci modified into forceps-like pincers.
Earwigs belong to the order Euplexoptera. 蠼螋属于革翅目。
Euplexoptera are small to medium-sized insects. 革翅目昆虫体型从中小型。
近义词:Dermaptera
反义词:None
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *eu- (good, true) + *plex- (folded) + *ptera (wings), referring to the way the hindwings fold under the short, leathery forewings.
