nonprogressiveness:/ˌnɒnprəˈɡrɛsɪvnəs/
n.
非进步性，不进步，无进展
lack of progress or development; the state of being nonprogressive.
The nonprogressiveness of the company led to its eventual downfall.  这家公司的停滞不前最终导致了它的垮台。
His nonprogressiveness frustrated his ambitious colleagues. 他的不思进取让雄心勃勃的同事们感到沮丧。
近义词: stagnation, inertia, standstill
反义词: progress, development, advancement
词性: 名词

nonprogressive:/ˌnɒnprəˈɡrɛsɪv/
adj.
非进步的，不进步的，无进展的
not progressive; opposed to progress or reform.
They criticized the government's nonprogressive policies. 他们批评政府不思进取的政策。
The nonprogressive attitude of the community hindered development.  这个社群故步自封的态度阻碍了发展。
近义词: conservative, reactionary, traditional
反义词: progressive, forward-thinking, innovative
词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + progressive + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)
