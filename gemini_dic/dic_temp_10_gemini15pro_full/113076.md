lunacies:/ˈluːnəsiːz/
noun
疯狂行为；愚蠢举动；精神错乱(lunacy的复数)
Acts of utter lunacy.
完全疯狂的行为。

The lunacies of the fashion industry.
时装界的疯狂行为。

Their wartime lunacies.
他们战时的疯狂行为。

近义词: insanities, follies, madness, craziness
反义词: sanity, rationality, wisdom
词性: noun (plural)

Etymology:
From Middle English *lunacie, from Old French lunacie, from lunatique (“lunatic”), from Late Latin lunaticus (“moonstruck”), from Latin lūna (“moon”).  The belief was that changes in the moon caused intermittent insanity.
