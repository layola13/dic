inspectable:/ɪnˈspɛktəbl/| adj.|可检查的，可视察的|able to be inspected or examined.  Something that is inspectable can be officially visited and examined to check that rules are being obeyed or that everything is satisfactory.|The building is inspectable by fire officials annually. 该建筑物每年都由消防官员检查。|The code is clean and inspectable, making it easy to maintain. 代码简洁易检查，方便维护。|Synonym: examinable, reviewable, observable, verifiable|Antonym: hidden, concealed, unobservable|adjective
