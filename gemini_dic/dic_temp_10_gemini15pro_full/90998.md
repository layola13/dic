hurus:/ˈhʊrəs/| n.|仙女，美丽的少女 (通常指神话或传说中的)|A hurus is a beautiful maiden, often depicted in mythology or legends.|The hurus of paradise danced gracefully. 天堂里的仙女翩翩起舞。|近义词：nymph, sprite, fairy|反义词:hag, witch|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Persian حوری (ḥūrī), from Arabic حُورِيّ (ḥūriyy), feminine of حُور (ḥūr), plural of أَحْوَر (ʾaḥwar, “having intensely black eyes with intensely white whites”). Ultimately from Akkadian 𒄷𒌑 (ḫūru), meaning "young woman; bride; female prostitute".
