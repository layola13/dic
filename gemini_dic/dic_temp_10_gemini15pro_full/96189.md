indeterminate:/ˌɪndɪˈtɜːrmɪnət/ | adj. | 不确定的，不明确的；无限期的；模糊的 | Not exactly known, established, or defined; having no exact limits; vague. | The future remains indeterminate. 未来仍然是不确定的。| The length of the project is indeterminate at this time.  这个项目的时长目前还不确定。| 近义词: uncertain, indefinite, vague, ambiguous | 反义词: definite, precise, specific, determinate | 词性: 形容词

indeterminate:/ˌɪndɪˈtɜːrmɪnət/ | n. | 不定式；未定式 | An indeterminate form in mathematics. | The equation resulted in an indeterminate. 方程式得出一个不定式。 |  He encountered an indeterminate while solving the calculus problem. 他在解决微积分问题时遇到了一个不定式。 | 近义词: unknown, variable | 反义词: constant, known | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *indeterminatus*, from in- (“not”) + determinatus (“limited, bounded”), past participle of determinare (“to limit, bound”), from de- (“completely”) + terminare (“to set bounds to”), from terminus (“boundary, limit”).
