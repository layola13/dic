bobbies:/ˈbɒbiz/| n.|警察（尤指英国的男警察）|A British slang term, primarily used in London, for a police officer.  It originates from the name of Sir Robert Peel, who established the Metropolitan Police Force.|例句：The bobbies were patrolling the streets. 警察在街上巡逻。

例句：Two bobbies on the beat stopped to help the lost child. 两个巡逻的警察停下来帮助迷路的孩子。


近义词：policemen, officers, cops|反义词:criminals, thieves|词性:noun
