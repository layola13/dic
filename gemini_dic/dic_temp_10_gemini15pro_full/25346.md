builders:/ˈbɪldərz/| n.| 建设者，建造者| People or companies that construct buildings or other structures.  They are responsible for the physical creation of structures.| The builders are working hard to finish the project on time.  建设者们正在努力工作以按时完成项目。| synonyms: constructors, developers, erectors| antonyms: demolishers, destroyers| noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bilder*, from Old English *byldere*, equivalent to *build* + *-er*. Cognate with Dutch *bouwer*, German *Bauer* (“farmer, peasant”).  The original meaning referred to anyone who built, including farmers building houses and barns.  Over time, the meaning specialized to refer primarily to those who construct buildings.
