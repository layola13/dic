nanofiber:/ˈnænoʊˌfaɪbər/| n. |纳米纤维|A fiber with a diameter of less than 100 nanometers.|Researchers are exploring the use of nanofibers in creating new materials.|Synonyms: nanowire (when very long), filament|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider "macrofiber" or "bulk material")|noun
