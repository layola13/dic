disprize:/dɪˈspraɪz/
verb
轻视，蔑视
to regard or treat as of little worth.
He disprized the opinions of those he considered beneath him. 他轻视那些他认为不如他的人的意见。

verb
贬低
to lower in value or esteem.
The scandal disprized his reputation. 这桩丑闻败坏了他的名声。

近义词: scorn, disdain, despise, contemn, belittle
反义词: admire, respect, esteem, value, appreciate
词性: verb
