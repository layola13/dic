easterlies: /ˈiːstərliz/ | n. | 东风 | Winds blowing from the east. | The easterlies brought warm, humid air to the islands. 东风给岛屿带来了温暖潮湿的空气。| 近义词: east winds | 反义词: westerlies | 词性: 名词

词源:  From "easterly" + "-s" (plural). "Easterly" comes from "east" + "-erly" (suffix indicating direction).  "East" ultimately derives from Proto-Germanic *aust-, meaning "east," related to the rising sun.
