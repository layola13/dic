copyist: /ˈkɒpiɪst/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpiɪst/ (US) | n. | 抄写员；誊写员；复制者 | A person who copies documents or other material, especially by hand. |  The monk worked as a copyist, meticulously transcribing ancient texts.  这名僧侣担任抄写员，一丝不苟地抄写古代文献。

The museum hired a copyist to reproduce the damaged painting.  博物馆聘请了一位复制者来复制受损的画作。 | 近义词: scribe, transcriber, duplicator | 反义词: author, originator | 词性: 名词
