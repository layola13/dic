nepotism: /ˈnɛpətɪzəm/ | n. | 裙带关系，任人唯亲 | the practice among those with power or influence of favoring relatives or friends, especially by giving them jobs. |  He was accused of nepotism when he made his nephew vice-president of the company. 他任命侄子为公司副总裁，因而被指责为任人唯亲。 |  favoritism, cronyism |  impartiality, meritocracy | noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *nepotismus*, from Latin nepōs (“nephew, grandson”).
