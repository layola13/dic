muggers:/ˈmʌɡərz/
n.
抢劫犯，行凶强盗
People who attack and rob others, often in public places.
The muggers targeted lone pedestrians late at night.  夜里抢劫犯盯上了独自步行的路人。
synonyms: robbers, assailants, attackers, bandits
antonyms: victims, protectors, guardians
noun

mugger:/ˈmʌɡər/
n.
抢劫犯，行凶强盗
A person who attacks and robs another, often in a public place.
The mugger grabbed her purse and ran. 抢劫犯抢了她的钱包就跑了。
synonyms: robber, assailant, attacker, bandit
antonyms: victim, protector, guardian
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "mug," meaning to attack and rob someone.  The verb "mug" likely originated from the Scandinavian word "mugga" meaning "to strike, to hit."
