laurasia:/lɔːˈreɪʒə/ | n. | 劳亚古陆 | A large landmass believed to have existed in the northern hemisphere in the Mesozoic Era, comprising what are now North America, Europe, and Asia north of the Himalayas. |  The breakup of Laurasia led to the formation of the North Atlantic Ocean. 劳亚古陆的分裂导致了北大西洋的形成。

Laurasia and Gondwana were once joined together. 劳亚古陆和冈瓦纳大陆曾经连接在一起。 | 近义词: continent, landmass | 反义词: Gondwana | 词性: noun
