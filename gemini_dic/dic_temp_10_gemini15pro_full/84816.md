handyman:/ˈhændimæn/| n. |杂工，修理工|A person skilled at a variety of small repairs or maintenance tasks, typically around the home.|例句：We hired a handyman to fix the leaky faucet. 我们雇了一个杂工来修理漏水的水龙头。|例句：My grandfather was a handyman and could fix anything. 我的祖父是一个杂工，什么都能修。|近义词：fixer, repairman, handyperson|反义词: specialist (in a particular field)|词性:noun
