nondivisive:/ˌnɒndɪˈvaɪsɪv/
adjective
非分裂性的；没有分歧的
Not causing disagreement or division within a group.
The candidate's speech was intentionally nondivisive, focusing on shared values rather than controversial issues.  候选人的演讲有意地避免分裂，集中于共同的价值观，而不是有争议的问题。
The new policy is expected to be nondivisive and widely accepted by all parties. 这项新政策预计不会引起分歧，并将被各方广泛接受。

近义词: unifying, conciliatory, harmonious, agreeable
反义词: divisive, controversial, polarizing, discordant
词性: adjective

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + divisive (adjective meaning "tending to cause disagreement or hostility between people")
