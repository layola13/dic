encamps:/ɪnˈkæmps/ | v. |  扎营，宿营 | to set up a camp; to pitch tents for a temporary stay | The army encamped near the river. 军队在河边扎营。| synonyms: camp, bivouac, pitch tents | antonyms: decamp, break camp | verb

encampment:/ɪnˈkæmpmənt/ | n. | 营地，宿营地 | the act of encamping or the place where a camp is set up | The refugees set up an encampment outside the city. 难民在城外建立了一个营地。| synonyms: camp, campsite, bivouac | antonyms: departure | noun
