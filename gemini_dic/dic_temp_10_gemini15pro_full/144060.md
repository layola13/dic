outroared:/ˌaʊtˈrɔːrd/
verb
大声抗议；盖过…的声音
to shout louder than someone or something else
The protesters outroared the police trying to quiet them. 抗议者的声音盖过了试图让他们安静下来的警察。
近义词: outshout, drown out
反义词: be drowned out, be silenced
词性: verb

词源:  "out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "roar" (a loud, deep cry)
