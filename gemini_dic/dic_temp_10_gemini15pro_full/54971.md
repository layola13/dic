disproportionately:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənətli/
| adv. |不成比例地，不相称地| In a way that is too large or too small in relation to something else. |  Minority groups are disproportionately affected by unemployment. 少数族裔受失业的影响尤为严重。| disproportionally large/small, excessively, unduly | proportionately, equally | adverb
