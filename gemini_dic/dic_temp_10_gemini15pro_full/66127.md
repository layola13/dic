except-that:/ɪkˈsɛpt ðæt/| conj.|除了...之外；只是...|Used to introduce a qualification or an exception to a statement.  It functions similarly to "but" or "however".|She would have gone to the party, except that she had a prior engagement. 她本来会去参加聚会的，只是她有之前的约会。|Synonyms: but, however, save that, notwithstanding, only|Antonyms: including, additionally|Conjunction
