defrost:/diːˈfrɒst/ (UK), /diːˈfrɔːst/ (US)| v. n.|除霜，解冻|To remove frost or ice from something, or to become free of frost or ice.|I need to defrost the freezer. 我需要除霜冰箱。|近义词：thaw, melt|反义词:freeze|词性:动词, 名词

defrost:/diːˈfrɒst/ (UK), /diːˈfrɔːst/ (US)| n.|除霜，解冻|The act of removing frost or ice from something.|The defrost of the windshield took a few minutes.  挡风玻璃除霜花了幾分鐘。|近义词：thawing, melting|反义词:freezing|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (indicating removal) + frost.  "De-" 源自拉丁文，表示“去除”；"frost" 指的是“霜”。
