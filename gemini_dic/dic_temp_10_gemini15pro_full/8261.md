anti-institutionally:/ˌæntiˌɪnstɪˈtuːʃənəli/
adjective
反体制地，反机构地
opposed to or distrustful of established institutions or the way they operate
His art was seen as a form of anti-institutional protest. 他的艺术被视为一种反体制的抗议。
The group's anti-institutional stance resonated with many voters.  该组织的反体制立场引起了许多选民的共鸣。

近义词:nonconformist, rebellious, dissident, iconoclastic
反义词:conformist, conventional, orthodox, establishmentarian
词性:adjective
