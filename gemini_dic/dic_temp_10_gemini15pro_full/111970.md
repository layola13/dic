lomentaceous:/ˌlɒmənˈteɪʃəs/| adj.|豆荚状的；节荚的|Having fruits that are lomentaceous, that is, resembling a loment; constricted between the seeds and breaking there into one-seeded segments.|The plant's lomentaceous pods contain small, edible seeds.|近义词：segmented, jointed, articulated|反义词: continuous, unsegmented|词性: adjective
