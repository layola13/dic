domesticism: /dəˈmɛstɪˌsɪzəm/
n.|家庭生活；家务；家居|Devotion to home and family life, often seen as a traditional female role.  A focus on household affairs and activities.|She embraced domesticism after having children, finding fulfillment in creating a comfortable home. 她有了孩子后欣然接受了家庭生活，在营造舒适的家中找到了满足感。|近义词：homemaking, housewifery|反义词：careerism, worldliness|词性:名词

domesticism: /dəˈmɛstɪˌsɪzəm/
n.|（政治）内政主义|A focus on domestic affairs over international relations.|The new government's policy of domesticism led to reduced involvement in foreign conflicts. 新政府的内政主义导致其减少了对外国冲突的介入。|近义词：isolationism, non-interventionism|反义词：internationalism, globalism|词性:名词
