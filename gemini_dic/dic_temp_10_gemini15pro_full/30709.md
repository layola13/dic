certitudes:/ˈsɜːrtɪtjuːdz/
n.
确定性；确信的事情
things that someone firmly believes are true
The lawyer questioned the witness about the certitudes of his testimony. 律师询问证人关于他证词的确定性。

近义词:certainties, convictions, assurances
反义词:doubts, uncertainties, ambiguities
词性:名词(plural noun)
