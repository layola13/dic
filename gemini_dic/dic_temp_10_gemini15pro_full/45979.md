cyano:/ˈsaɪænoʊ/ |adj.|氰基的，含氰基的|A combining form meaning “containing the cyano group —CN,” used in the formation of compound words: cyanoacrylate.  Relating to, or containing, cyanogen. |例句：Cyanoacrylate adhesives are commonly known as "super glues" or "crazy glues". 氰基丙烯酸酯粘合剂通常被称为“超级胶水”或“疯狂胶水”。

例句：Sodium cyanide is a highly toxic cyano compound. 氰化钠是一种剧毒的氰化物。|近义词：cyanide (noun form, often for the ionic form)|反义词：N/A|词性：Combining form, Adjective
