on-even-keel:/ɒn-ˈiːvən-kiːl/
adjective
平稳的，稳定的，镇定的
calm, steady, and well-balanced; emotionally stable.
例句：Despite the market fluctuations, the company managed to stay on an even keel. 尽管市场波动，公司设法保持稳定。
例句：She always seems to be on an even keel, even in stressful situations. 即使在压力很大的情况下，她也总是显得镇定自若。
近义词：stable, steady, balanced, calm, composed
反义词：unstable, unsteady, agitated, emotional, volatile
词性: adjective


词源: This idiom originates from sailing terminology. A ship that is "on an even keel" is balanced and stable, not tilting to one side or the other. This nautical meaning was then extended metaphorically to describe emotional and mental states, as well as situations in general.
