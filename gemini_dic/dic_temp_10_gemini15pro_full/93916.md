immoveables:/ɪˈmuːvəblz/
|n.|不动产，无法移动的东西|Things that cannot be moved, typically real estate or property fixed to the land.|例句：The immoveables included the land, the house, and the barn. 不动产包括土地、房屋和谷仓。

例句：He invested heavily in immoveables. 他在不动产方面投入了大量资金。|近义词：real estate, property, land, fixtures|反义词：moveables, chattels|词性:名词

immoveable:/ɪˈmuːvəbl/
|adj.|不能移动的，固定的|Not able to be moved; fixed.|例句：The large rock was immoveable. 这块大石头一动不动。

例句：His face remained immoveable despite the bad news. 尽管听到了坏消息，他的表情依然毫无变化。|近义词：fixed, immobile, stationary, permanent|反义词：movable, mobile, portable|词性:形容词
