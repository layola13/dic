pancreatization: /ˌpæŋkrɪətɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌpænkrɪətɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
胰腺化，胰腺组织形成
The process of becoming like pancreatic tissue or of forming pancreatic tissue.
Pancreatization of tissues surrounding the pancreas can occur in severe cases of pancreatitis.  胰腺周围组织的胰腺化会在严重的胰腺炎病例中发生。
The biopsy showed pancreatization of the lymph nodes. 活检显示淋巴结发生了胰腺化。
