highfield:/ˈhaɪfiːld/| n.|高地; 高田; 高原; (尤指)郊区住宅区|A highfield is an area of high ground, especially one used for building houses in a suburb.|We bought a new house in the highfield overlooking the city. 我们在俯瞰城市的高地买了一套新房子。|近义词：upland, plateau, hill|反义词:lowland, valley|noun
