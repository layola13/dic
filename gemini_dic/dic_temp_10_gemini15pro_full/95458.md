incisors: /ɪnˈsaɪzərz/ (US) /ɪnˈsaɪzəz/ (UK)
n.
门牙，切牙
The sharp, front teeth used for cutting food.
Rabbits use their incisors to gnaw on carrots. 兔子用门牙啃胡萝卜。
Synonyms: front teeth, cutting teeth
Antonyms: molars, premolars
noun
