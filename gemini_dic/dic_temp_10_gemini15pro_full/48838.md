defoliator:/diːˈfoʊliˌeɪtər/| n. |落叶剂；食叶昆虫|Something, such as a chemical or an insect, that causes the leaves of plants to fall off.|The defoliator stripped the trees bare in a matter of days.  这种落叶剂在几天内就把树上的叶子剥光了。|Synonyms: defoliant, leaf-eater, leaf stripper|Antonyms: fertilizer, growth stimulant|noun

defoliator:/diːˈfoʊliˌeɪtər/| n. |食叶动物|An animal that eats leaves.|Caterpillars are common defoliators in many gardens. 毛毛虫是许多花园里常见的食叶动物。|Synonyms: herbivore, folivore|Antonyms: carnivore|noun
