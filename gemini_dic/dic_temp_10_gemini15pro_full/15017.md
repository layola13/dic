babingtonite:/bæˈbɪŋtəˌnaɪt/ | n. | 巴巴丁顿石 | A triclinic mineral, Ca₂Fe²⁺Fe³⁺Si₅O₁₄(OH), occurring in dark, greenish-black prismatic crystals. It is a sorosilicate of calcium, iron, and silicon. | Babingtonite is a relatively rare mineral found in cavities in igneous rocks. 巴巴丁顿石是一种相对稀有的矿物，发现于火成岩的空腔中。 |  Babingtonite crystals can be quite beautiful, exhibiting a deep greenish-black luster. 巴巴丁顿石晶体非常漂亮，呈现出深绿黑色的光泽。 | 近义词: None (as it's a specific mineral) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
