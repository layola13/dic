consummation's:/ˌkɒnsəˈmeɪʃənz/ | n. | 完成，成就；圆满；终结 |The consummation of the deal took months of negotiation. 这笔交易的完成耗费了数月的谈判。|  Example: The consummation of their marriage was a grand celebration.  他们的婚礼是一场盛大的庆典。| Synonyms: completion, fulfillment, achievement, realization | Antonyms: beginning, commencement, inception, start | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French consummation, or from late Latin consummatio(n-), from the verb consummare ‘to sum up, complete’.
