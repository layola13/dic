eulogised:/ˈjuːlədʒaɪzd/
verb
赞扬；颂扬；称赞
praised highly in speech or writing, especially in a formal eulogy.
The deceased was eulogised for his contributions to science.  这位已故的科学家因其对科学的贡献而受到赞扬。
Nelson Mandela was eulogised by world leaders after his death.  纳尔逊·曼德拉去世后，世界各国领导人对他表示了赞扬。
synonyms: praised, extolled, lauded, acclaimed, celebrated, commended
antonyms: criticized, condemned, denounced
