whirlwind:/ˈwɜːrlˌwɪnd/ (US), /ˈwɜːlˌwɪnd/ (UK)| n. | 旋风，一阵风； whirlwind of sth. 一连串的活动;  whirlwind romance/tour/visit 闪电式的恋爱/旅行/访问 |A column of air that spins very fast and pulls things up into it. Also used to describe something that happens very quickly and energetically.| The whirlwind ripped through the town, destroying everything in its path.  旋风席卷了小镇，摧毁了一切。| synonyms: vortex, cyclone, tornado, gale | antonyms: calm, stillness | noun

whirlwind:/ˈwɜːrlˌwɪnd/ (US), /ˈwɜːlˌwɪnd/ (UK)| adj. | 旋风般的，飞快的 |Happening very quickly and energetically.|  It was a whirlwind romance that ended in marriage after only two months.  这是一场旋风般的恋情，仅仅两个月后就以结婚告终。| synonyms: rapid, swift, fast, hasty | antonyms: slow, leisurely | adjective
