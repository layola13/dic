forerunner:/ˈfɔːrˌrʌnər/ (US) /ˈfɔːˌrʌnə(r)/ (UK)| n.|先驱，先行者，预兆|A person or thing that precedes the coming or development of someone or something else.|The Model T Ford was a forerunner of today's mass-produced cars.  福特T型车是当今批量生产汽车的先驱。

The sudden drop in temperature was a forerunner of the coming snowstorm. 气温骤降是暴风雪即将来临的预兆。|近义词：precursor, predecessor, herald, harbinger|反义词: follower, successor|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *forrenner*, equivalent to *for(e)-* + *renner* (“runner”).  From Old English *for(e)* (“before”) + *rinnan* (“to run”).
