nonreclusive:/ˌnɑːn.rɪˈkluː.sɪv/
adjective
非隐居的；不闭塞的
Not reclusive; enjoying or seeking the company of others; sociable.
例句：She leads a nonreclusive life, filled with social engagements and travel. 她过着非隐居的生活，充满了社交活动和旅行。

例句：He's a nonreclusive artist who enjoys collaborating with others. 他是一位不隐居的艺术家，喜欢与他人合作。

近义词：sociable, gregarious, outgoing, extroverted
反义词：reclusive, withdrawn, solitary, introverted
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not" or "opposite of"
reclusive (adjective): from Latin "recludere" (to shut up, confine)
