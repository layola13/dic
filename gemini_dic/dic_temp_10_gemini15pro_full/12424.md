askance:/əˈskæns/ | adv. |斜视地，怀疑地，不赞成地 | with an attitude or look of suspicion or disapproval |  He looked askance at her suggestion. 他对她的建议表示怀疑。 | synonyms: suspiciously, distrustfully, skeptically | antonyms: trustfully, approvingly | adverb

askance:/əˈskæns/ | adj. |(古旧用法) 歪斜的 | (archaic) turned to one side | The picture hung askance. 这幅画挂歪了。| synonyms: awry, askew | antonyms: straight | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

From Middle French *à escons*, from Old French *escons*, *esconses* "obliquely," perhaps from Frankish *skantja* "slanting," from Proto-Germanic *skænþaną* "to jump, leap."  The original sense was "sideways, obliquely," later developing the figurative meaning of "with disapproval."
