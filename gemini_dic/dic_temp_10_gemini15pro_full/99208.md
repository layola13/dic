interclouds:/ˈɪntərklaʊdz/| n.|云间隙，云间|The airplane ascended above the interclouds, revealing a breathtaking view of the Earth below. 飞机上升到云间隙上方，展现出下方地球的壮丽景色。|The pilot skillfully navigated the aircraft through the interclouds. 飞行员熟练地驾驶飞机穿过云间隙。|近义词：gaps between clouds, breaks in the clouds|反义词：cloud cover, overcast skies|词性：名词

词源:  "inter-" (Latin) meaning "between" + "clouds" (Old English) meaning "masses of condensed water vapor"
