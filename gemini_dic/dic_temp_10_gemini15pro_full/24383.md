brining:/ˈbraɪnɪŋ/| n.| 盐水浸泡，腌制|The process of soaking food in a salt solution (brine) to preserve or season it.  |  We're brining the turkey for Thanksgiving. 我们正在用盐水浸泡感恩节火鸡。|  synonyms:  salting, pickling, marinating | antonyms: drying, dehydrating | noun


brine:/braɪn/| n. vt.| 盐水；用盐水浸泡|A solution of salt in water, used for pickling or preserving food; to soak in brine. |  The chef brined the chicken overnight. 厨师把鸡在盐水里泡了一夜。 |  synonyms:  saltwater, pickle, marinade (as a verb) | antonyms: freshwater | noun, verb
