oversteadfastly:/ˌoʊvərˈstɛdfæstli/
| adv. | 过于坚定地，过于执着地 | excessively steadfast; too firm or resolute | He clung oversteadfastly to his beliefs, refusing to consider other perspectives. 他过于执着于自己的信仰，拒绝考虑其他观点。 |  She oversteadfastly pursued her goals, neglecting her health and relationships. 她过于执着地追求自己的目标，忽略了健康和人际关系。 | 近义词: obstinately, pertinaciously, doggedly | 反义词: flexibly, waveringly, irresolutely | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "steadfast" (adjective meaning "resolutely or dutifully firm and unwavering") + "-ly" (adverb suffix)
