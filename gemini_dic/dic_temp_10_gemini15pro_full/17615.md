beavers:/ˈbiːvərz/| n.|海狸；海狸皮毛|Large semiaquatic rodents known for building dams and lodges.|Beavers build dams to create ponds. 海狸筑坝形成池塘。
The beavers felled several trees to build their lodge. 海狸砍伐了几棵树来建造它们的巢穴。|近义词:rodents|反义词:|noun
