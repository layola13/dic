electro-slag: /ɪˈlɛktroʊ slæɡ/ | adj. n.| 电渣的，电渣重熔；电渣熔焊|A type of welding that uses the heat generated by an electric current passing through a molten slag bath to melt and fuse metal.|The bridge was constructed using electro-slag welding. 这座桥是用电渣焊建造的. |The electro-slag process refines the ingot. 电渣工艺提炼钢锭。|Synonyms: electroslag, ESR (Electro-Slag Remelting)|Antonyms: N/A|adjective, noun
