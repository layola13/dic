defections:/dɪˈfɛkʃənz/| n. | 背叛，变节，叛逃 | The act of abandoning a person, cause, or allegiance, often to join an opposing one.  |  Many defections from the ruling party weakened its position. 许多人从执政党叛逃，削弱了它的地位。 |  The team suffered a series of defections to rival clubs.  该队有多名队员叛逃到竞争对手俱乐部。 | synonyms: desertions, abandonments, apostasies | antonyms: loyalties, fidelities | noun
