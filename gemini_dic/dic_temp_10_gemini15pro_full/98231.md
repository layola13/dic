inseminations:/ɪnˌsɛmɪˈneɪʃənz/
|n.|授精；人工授精|The act of introducing semen into the vagina or uterus otherwise than by sexual intercourse, especially for the purpose of achieving pregnancy.  The process of insemination can be done naturally or artificially.|The farmer used artificial insemination to breed his cows.  这位农民用人工授精的方法繁殖他的奶牛。|Synonyms: impregnation, fertilization, conception |Antonyms: sterilization, contraception|Noun
