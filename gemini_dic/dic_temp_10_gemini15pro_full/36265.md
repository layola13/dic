cogwheels:/ˈkɒɡwiːlz/ (UK), /ˈkɑːɡwiːlz/ (US)| n. |齿轮|A set of toothed wheels that work together to transmit motion in a machine.|The cogwheels of the clock were intricate and fascinating.  时钟的齿轮复杂而迷人。|The complex machinery was driven by a series of interconnected cogwheels. 这台复杂的机器由一系列相互连接的齿轮驱动。|synonyms: gears, cogs|antonyms: N/A|noun
