expedite:/ˈekspədaɪt/| v. |加快；加速；促进|To make a process happen more quickly.|The company expedited the shipping of the order. 公司加快了订单的运送。|近义词：accelerate, hasten, quicken|反义词:delay, hinder, slow|verb

expedited:/ˈekspədaɪtɪd/| adj. |加速的；快速的|Done more quickly than usual.|The expedited delivery arrived within 24 hours. 加急递送在 24 小时内到达。|近义词：accelerated, hastened, quickened|反义词:delayed, hindered, slowed|adjective
