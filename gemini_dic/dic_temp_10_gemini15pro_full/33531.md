cicutoxin:/saɪˈkjuːtɒksɪn/ | n. |毒芹毒素| A highly poisonous neurotoxin found in water hemlock (Cicuta maculata). It is one of the most lethal toxins found in North America. |  Cicutoxin poisoning can occur from ingesting any part of the water hemlock plant.  毒芹毒素中毒可能因摄入水芹植物的任何部分而发生。

Cicutoxin can cause seizures and death. 毒芹毒素会导致癫痫发作和死亡。 | 近义词:neurotoxin, poison | 反义词:antidote, remedy |词性:noun
