collectivist:/kəˈlɛktɪvɪst/
adjective
集体主义的
Relating to or characteristic of collectivism; prioritizing the needs of the group over the individual.
Collectivist societies emphasize the importance of community. 集体主义社会强调社区的重要性。
The kibbutz is a classic example of a collectivist community. 基布兹是集体主义社区的典型例子。
近义词:communal, socialist, cooperative
反义词:individualistic, capitalist, libertarian
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *collectiviste*, from *collectif* (collective) + *-iste* (-ist).  Ultimately from Latin *colligere* (to collect).
