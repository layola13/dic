counterview:/ˈkaʊntərˌvjuː/
n.|反驳；相反的观点|An opposing view or perspective.|例句：Her counterview to the proposal was well-researched and persuasive. 她对这项提议的反驳经过充分研究，很有说服力。

例句：The documentary presented a counterview to the official government narrative.  这部纪录片提出了与官方政府说法相反的观点。
|近义词：rebuttal, counterargument, rejoinder|反义词：agreement, concurrence|词性：名词
