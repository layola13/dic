pandiculation:/pænˌdɪkjʊˈleɪʃən/| n.| 舒展，伸懒腰|The act of stretching and yawning, typically upon waking or after a period of inactivity.  This involves stretching the limbs, spreading the fingers, arching the back, and often yawning.|Example 1:  The cat awoke from its nap with a long, luxurious pandiculation.  这只猫从小睡中醒来，舒舒服服地伸了个懒腰。

Example 2:  After sitting at his desk for hours, he stood up and indulged in a full-body pandiculation. 在他的办公桌前坐了几个小时后，他站起来尽情地伸展了一下全身。|Synonym: stretching, yawning,  |Antonym: contracting, tensing|noun
