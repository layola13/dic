braziletto:/ˌbræzɪˈlɛtoʊ/| n.|巴西红木，苏木|A reddish wood from several tropical trees, used in dyeing and cabinetmaking.  A tree yielding such wood, especially Caesalpinia echinata.|例句：The antique table was made of beautiful braziletto. 这张古董桌子是用美丽的巴西红木制成的。

例句：Braziletto was a valuable commodity in the 18th century. 巴西红木在18世纪是一种珍贵的商品。|近义词：brazilwood, Pernambuco wood|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Portuguese *brasileto*, diminutive of *brasil* "Brazilwood," from *brasa* "ember, live coal" (referring to the wood's red color). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *brasa*.
