做作，矫揉造作
The quality of being affected; artificiality or pretension in behavior or speech.

近义词：artificiality, pretension, affectation

词性：名词

词源：From affectedness + -'s (possessive suffix).  Affectedness comes from affected + -ness. Affected comes from the past participle of the verb affect, from the Latin *affectāre*, frequentative of *afficere* "to do something to, act on, influence," from *ad-* "to" + *facere* "to do, make."
