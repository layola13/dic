baccara:/ˈbækərə/ (US), /bɑːkəˈrɑː/ (UK)| n.|百家乐|A card game played in casinos, in which two hands are dealt and bets are placed on which will have a point total closest to nine, or whether the two hands will tie. |Baccarat is a popular game in high-roller rooms. 百家乐是豪客厅的热门游戏。|The casino offers several versions of baccara. 这家赌场提供几种版本的百家乐。| 近义词:chemin de fer, punto banco| 反义词:None|noun
