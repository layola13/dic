housing's:/ˈhaʊzɪŋz/
noun

Relating to houses or the provision of houses.

synonyms: accommodation, lodging, shelter,  residences
antonyms: homelessness, displacement


ETYMOLOGY->
From "housing," which is derived from the verb "house" (meaning "to provide shelter or accommodation"), ultimately from Proto-Germanic *husan. The added -'s indicates possession or a relationship.
