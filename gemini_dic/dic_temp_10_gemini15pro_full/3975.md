agrypnotic: /ˌæɡrɪpˈnɒtɪk/ /ˌæɡrɪpˈnɑːtɪk/
adjective
失眠的；防睡眠的
Preventing or hindering sleep; inducing wakefulness.
She suffered from agrypnotic symptoms after drinking too much coffee. 她喝了太多咖啡后出现了失眠症状。
The loud noise had an agrypnotic effect on the baby.  巨大的噪音让婴儿无法入睡。
近义词: insomniac, sleepless, wakeful
反义词: hypnotic, soporific, sleep-inducing
词性: adjective
