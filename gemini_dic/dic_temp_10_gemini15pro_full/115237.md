manila:/məˈnɪlə/ | n. adj.| 马尼拉；马尼拉麻；马尼拉纸 |A city, the capital of the Philippines.  A type of fiber made from the abacá plant, used for rope, paper, etc.  A type of paper originally made from Manila hemp, typically strong and light brown.| Manila is a bustling metropolis. 马尼拉是一个繁华的大都市。| The rope was made of sturdy manila. 这根绳子是用结实的马尼拉麻制成的。| synonyms:  (for city) capital, metropolis; (for fiber) abacá, hemp; (for paper) kraft paper | antonyms: (for city) province, countryside | noun, adjective
