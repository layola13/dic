contemplate:/ˈkɒntɛmpleɪt/ (UK), /ˈkɑːntəmˌpleɪt/ (US)| v.| 沉思；仔细考虑；思忖；考虑；注视；凝视| to spend time considering something carefully or to look thoughtfully at something |例句：She sat and contemplated the meaning of life. 她坐着沉思人生的意义。|近义词：consider, ponder, muse, meditate, reflect, ruminate, deliberate|反义词：ignore, disregard, neglect|verb

contemplate:/ˈkɒntɛmpleɪt/ (UK), /ˈkɑːntəmˌpleɪt/ (US)| v.| 预期；想到( unpleasant things)|to expect something to happen, especially something unpleasant|例句：The committee has contemplated the possibility of failure. 委员会已经考虑到了失败的可能性。|近义词：anticipate, foresee, envision|反义词：disregard|verb
