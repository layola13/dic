mulch:/mʌltʃ/| n. v.|覆盖物，护根物；用覆盖物覆盖|A protective covering spread on the ground to reduce evaporation, suppress weeds, or enrich the soil.  To apply mulch.|The garden needs a fresh layer of mulch.  花园需要一层新的覆盖物。 He mulched the flowerbeds with straw. 他用稻草覆盖花坛。|近义词: compost, straw, bark|反义词: (v.) expose|noun, verb

词源: From Middle English *molsh*, from Old English *molde* (“earth, soil, dust”). Related to mould.
