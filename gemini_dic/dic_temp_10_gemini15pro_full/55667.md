ditrochee:/ˈdaɪtrəʊkiː/
noun
双扬抑格
A metrical foot consisting of two trochees.
The poem is written in ditrochees. 这首诗是用双扬抑格写的。
近义词: trochaic dimeter
反义词: iamb
词性: n.
