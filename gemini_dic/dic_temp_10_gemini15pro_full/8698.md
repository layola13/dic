anticreationist:/ˌæntiˌkriːˈeɪʃənɪst/
n.
反创造论者
Someone who opposes creationism (the belief that the universe and living organisms originate from specific acts of divine creation, as in the biblical account, rather than by natural processes like evolution).
Richard Dawkins is a well-known anticreationist.  理查德·道金斯是一位著名的反创造论者。
opponent of creationism, evolutionist
