asymmetry:/ˈeɪsɪmetri/ /eɪˈsɪmətri/| n.|不对称，不对称性|Lack of equality or equivalence between parts or aspects of something; lack of symmetry.|The painting was notable for its asymmetry. 这幅画的特点是不对称。|近义词：imbalance, disproportion, unevenness|反义词: symmetry, balance, equality|词性:noun

Etymology:
From French asymétrie, from Ancient Greek ἀ- (a-, 'not') + συμμετρία (summetría, 'symmetry').
