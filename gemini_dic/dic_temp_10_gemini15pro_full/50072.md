dentalities:/dɛnˈtælɪtiz/
n.
牙列；齿系；牙列特征
The arrangement, type, and number of teeth in a given species, considered as a characteristic of that species.
Paleontologists use dentalities to classify extinct mammals.  古生物学家利用牙列特征对已灭绝的哺乳动物进行分类。
The dentalities of the saber-toothed tiger are quite distinctive. 剑齿虎的牙列特征非常独特。
Synonyms: dentition, teeth
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps lack of teeth/edentulousness)
