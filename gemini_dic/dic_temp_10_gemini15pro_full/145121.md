overarguing:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːrɡjuɪŋ/
verb
过度争论，争论过久
The act of arguing for too long or too intensely.
They were overarguing a minor point and wasting valuable time. 他们在一个小问题上争论过久，浪费了宝贵的时间。
overarguing the point| verb phrase |过度争论观点|He was overarguing the point and wouldn't let it go. 他在过度争论这个观点，不愿放手。
近义词: overdisputing, quarreling, bickering
反义词: agreeing, conceding, compromising
词性: verb (动名词)
