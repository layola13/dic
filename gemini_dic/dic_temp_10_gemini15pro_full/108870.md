lecithins:/ˈlɛsɪθɪnz/ /ˈliːsɪθɪnz/
n.
卵磷脂
A group of fatty substances containing phosphorus and choline, found in cell membranes and egg yolks.  They are used in food processing as emulsifiers.
Lecithins are vital for cell function. 卵磷脂对细胞功能至关重要。
Lecithin is added to chocolate to improve its texture. 卵磷脂被添加到巧克力中以改善其质地。
synonyms: phospholipids
antonyms: (No direct antonym, but substances that inhibit emulsification could be considered)
noun
