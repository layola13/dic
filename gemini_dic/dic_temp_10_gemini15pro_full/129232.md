noctiluca:/ˌnɒktɪˈluːkə/ | n. |夜光虫 |A free-swimming bioluminescent marine dinoflagellate, *Noctiluca scintillans*, that can cause the surface of the sea to glow at night. |例句：The shimmering bioluminescence in the bay was caused by a bloom of noctiluca. 海湾里闪烁的生物荧光是由夜光虫大量繁殖引起的。

例句：Noctiluca are fascinating organisms that create beautiful light displays in the ocean. 夜光虫是一种迷人的生物，它们在海洋中创造出美丽的灯光秀。 |近义词：sea sparkle, dinoflagellate |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
