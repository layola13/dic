afflictingly:/əˈflɪktɪŋli/
adverb
痛苦地，折磨人地
in a way that causes pain or suffering
The disease afflictingly impacted her quality of life.  这种疾病痛苦地影响了她的生活质量。
He coughed afflictingly, clutching his chest. 他痛苦地咳嗽着，捂着胸口。
近义词: painfully, distressingly, agonizingly
反义词: soothingly, comfortably, pleasantly
词性: adverb

词源:
From the verb "afflict" + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverb suffix).  "Afflict" comes from the Latin "affligere," meaning "to strike down, weaken, damage."
