offendress: /əˈfɛndərɪs/
noun
女冒犯者; 女性罪犯
A female offender.  A woman who commits an offense.
She was charged as an offendress in the case.  她在案件中被指控为罪犯。
The offendress was sentenced to five years in prison.  这名女罪犯被判处五年监禁。

Synonym: female offender, female culprit, woman criminal
Antonym: victim
