biffs:/bɪfs/| n. pl. | 打架，争吵；(尤指拳击比赛中的)击打，猛击 | A series of blows, especially in a boxing match, or a fight or quarrel.|  He got into a biffs with another guy at the bar. 他在酒吧里和另一个人打了一架。 | 近义词：fights, punches, blows, scraps | 反义词: agreements, truces | 词性: 名词复数


词源:  Likely a shortening of "biff," itself likely imitative of the sound of a blow.
