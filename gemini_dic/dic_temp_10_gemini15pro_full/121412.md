misinference:/ˌmɪsˈɪnfərəns/
n.
错误推论；曲解
A misinference is a conclusion that is not logically justified by the evidence.
He made a misinference about her intentions based on a single comment. 他根据一句评论就对她意图做出了错误的推断。
misinterpretation, misconstruction, misjudgment
correct inference, accurate deduction
noun
