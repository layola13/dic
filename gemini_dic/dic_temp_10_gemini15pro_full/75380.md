frowns:/fraʊnz/
| n. | 皱眉；不悦之色 | An expression of displeasure or concentration characterized by a furrowing of the brow. | She greeted the news with a frown. 她皱着眉头回应了这个消息。| synonyms: scowl, grimace | antonyms: smile, grin | noun
frowns:/fraʊnz/
| v. | 皱眉；表示不悦 | To wrinkle one's brow, typically as an expression of displeasure, disapproval, or concentration. | He frowned at the complicated instructions. 他对着复杂的说明书皱起了眉头。| synonyms: scowl, glare | antonyms: smile, beam | verb
