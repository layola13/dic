metronymic:/ˌmɛtrəˈnɪmɪk/
n.|从母姓的；母姓的|A name derived from the name of one's mother or a female ancestor.|Her surname is a metronymic, passed down through generations of women.|近义词：matrilineal, matronymic|反义词：patronymic|词性: 名词
