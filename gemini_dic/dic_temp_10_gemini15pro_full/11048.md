arch-abbey:/ˌɑːrtʃˈæbi/ | n. | 大修道院；总修道院；大教堂 | A monastery or abbey of the highest rank, presided over by an abbot or abbess who has jurisdiction over other monasteries or abbeys.  |  The arch-abbey of St. Martin's was a center of learning. 圣马丁大修道院是学习的中心。 | synonyms: cathedral, monastery, abbey | antonyms: None | noun
