de-Americanizing: /diː.əˈmɛrɪkəˌnaɪzɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
去美国化;  消除美国化影响
The process of removing American influence or characteristics from something.
The politician campaigned on a platform of de-Americanizing the country's education system.  这位政治家以将国家教育体系去美国化的政纲进行竞选。
The local community is trying to de-Americanize its cultural festivals.  当地社区正试图消除其文化节日的美国化影响。
近义词:  de-Westernizing,  localization
反义词: Americanizing
词性: 动词 (gerund or present participle)


de-Americanize: /diː.əˈmɛrɪkəˌnaɪz/

de-Americanization: /diː.əˌmɛrɪkə.nɪˈzeɪ.ʃən/
