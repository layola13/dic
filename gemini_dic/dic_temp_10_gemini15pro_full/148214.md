overshrink:/ˌoʊvərˈʃrɪŋk/| v.|过度缩水，缩水过 much|To shrink excessively, usually referring to fabric shrinking more than expected when washed or dried.|The wool sweater overshrunk in the hot wash and now it fits my dog.  这件羊毛衫在热水里洗缩水过度了，现在只能给我的狗穿了。|My new jeans overshrunk after the first wash. 我的新牛仔裤第一次洗后就缩水过度了。|近义词: shrink excessively, contract too much|反义词: stretch, expand|词性:动词

词源: over- + shrink.  "Over-" acts as a prefix indicating an excess or going beyond a normal limit.  "Shrink" comes from Old Norse *skrink- "to shrink, wither."
