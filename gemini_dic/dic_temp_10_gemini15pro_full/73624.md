foreword:/ˈfɔːrwɜːrd/| n.|前言，序言|An introductory note or preface, especially to a book, typically written by someone other than the author.|This book has a foreword written by a famous scientist.|Synonyms: preface, introduction, prologue|Antonyms: afterword, epilogue|noun
