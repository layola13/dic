homiletics:/ˌhɒmɪˈlɛtɪks/ /ˌhoʊmɪˈlɛtɪks/| n.|讲道术，讲道法|The art of preaching or writing sermons; a branch of rhetoric that treats the composition and delivery of sermons or other religious discourses.|He studied homiletics in seminary to improve his preaching skills. 他在神学院学习讲道术以提高他的讲道技巧。|近义词：preaching, sermonizing|反义词: None|词性: Noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *homiletica*, from Greek *homiletikos* "conversational," from *homilein* "to converse, associate with," from *homilos* "assembly, crowd."
