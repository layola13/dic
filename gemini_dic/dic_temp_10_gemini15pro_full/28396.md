carbon-dating:/ˈkɑːrbən deɪtɪŋ/ |n.|放射性碳定年法;碳14年代测定法|The process of determining the age of organic material by measuring the amount of carbon-14 remaining in it. |The archaeologists used carbon-dating to establish the age of the ancient settlement. 考古学家使用放射性碳定年法确定了古代定居点的年代。| 近义词: radiocarbon dating | 反义词: None | Noun
