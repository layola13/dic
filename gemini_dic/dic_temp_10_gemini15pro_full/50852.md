desegregated:/diːˈsɛɡrɪɡeɪtɪd/
adjective
取消种族隔离的
Ended the separation of people based on race, especially in schools, public facilities, and housing.
The desegregated school welcomed students of all races. 这所取消种族隔离的学校欢迎所有种族的学生。
The Civil Rights Act of 1964 desegregated public accommodations. 1964年的民权法案取消了公共场所的种族隔离。
近义词: integrated, mixed
反义词: segregated, separated
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "remove, reverse") + segregate (from Latin segregare, "to set apart") + -ed (past participle suffix).
