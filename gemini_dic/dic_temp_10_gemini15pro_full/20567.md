blackberrylike:/ˈblækˌbɛriˌlaɪk/
adjective
像黑莓的；形似黑莓的
Resembling a blackberry in appearance, taste, or texture.
The berries of this plant are small and blackberrylike. 这种植物的浆果小而像黑莓。
Near synonyms:  bramble-like, mulberry-like
antonym: Unlike a blackberry
