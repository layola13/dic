aerosiderite:/ˈeɪəroʊˌsaɪdəraɪt/
n.
陨石，铁陨石
A stony or metallic mass that has fallen to the earth's surface from outer space.
例句：The museum displayed a large aerosiderite that had been found in the desert. 博物馆展出了一块在沙漠中发现的大陨石。

例句：Scientists studied the aerosiderite to learn more about the composition of other planets. 科学家们研究了这块陨石，以了解更多关于其他行星的组成。

近义词：meteorite, siderite
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From aero- (Greek ἀήρ, aēr, "air") + siderite (Greek σίδηρος, sideros, "iron"). Literally "air-iron".
