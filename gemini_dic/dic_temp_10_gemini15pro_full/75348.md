frostwort:/ˈfrɔːstwɜːrt/| n. |霜苔，冰霜草|A small, moss-like plant that grows in cold, damp places, often used in alchemy and traditional medicine. It is known for its frost resistance.|例句：The alchemist gathered frostwort from the snowy mountainside.  炼金术士从雪山上采集霜苔。

例句：Frostwort is said to have healing properties. 据说霜苔具有治疗功效。|近义词：None (specific plant)|反义词:None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "frost" + "wort".  "Frost" refers to the plant's ability to withstand frost. "Wort" is an Old English word meaning "plant" or "herb," often used in the names of medicinal plants.
