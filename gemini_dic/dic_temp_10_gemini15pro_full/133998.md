nonhomogeneity:/ˌnɒnhɒmədʒɪˈniːɪti/ /ˌnɑːnhɑːmədʒəˈniːɪti/ | n. | 非均匀性，不均匀性，异质性 |  The lack of uniformity or the state of being composed of different elements or parts; heterogeneity.  | The nonhomogeneity of the soil caused problems for the construction project. 土壤的非均匀性给建筑工程带来了问题。| synonyms: heterogeneity,  disuniformity,  variability,  diversity,  inconsistency | antonyms: homogeneity, uniformity, consistency, similarity | noun

nonhomogeneous:/ˌnɒnhɒməˈdʒiːniəs/ /ˌnɑːnhɑːməˈdʒiːniəs/ | adj. | 非均匀的，不均匀的，异质的 | Not uniform in structure or composition throughout. | The cake was nonhomogeneous, with some parts denser than others. 蛋糕的质地不均匀，有些部分比其他部分更密。| synonyms: heterogeneous,  disuniform,  variable,  diverse,  inconsistent | antonyms: homogeneous, uniform, consistent, similar | adjective
