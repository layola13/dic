carbineer:/ˌkɑːr.bɪˈnɪər/| n.|卡宾枪骑兵；轻骑兵|A soldier armed with a carbine (a short-barreled rifle).|例句：The carbineers charged the enemy line. 轻骑兵向敌人的防线冲锋。
