malabar:/ˈmæləˌbɑr/| adj. n.| 马拉巴尔（印度西南部海岸地区）; 马拉巴尔海岸的；来自马拉巴尔海岸的 |Relating to or from the Malabar Coast of southwest India.|The Malabar Coast is known for its beautiful beaches. 马拉巴尔海岸以其美丽的海滩而闻名。|The Malabar pepper is highly valued. 马拉巴尔胡椒价值很高。|近义词：Keralan, South Indian|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun
Etymology: From Malayalam *Malaparam*, meaning "mountain slope".
