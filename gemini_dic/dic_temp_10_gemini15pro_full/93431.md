illuminate: /ɪˈluːmɪneɪt/ (US) /ɪˈljuːmɪneɪt/ (UK)
verb
照亮，阐明
to light something up; to make something clearer or easier to understand
The streets were illuminated with festive lights.  街道上闪烁着节日的灯光。
The report illuminated the problems faced by the company.  这份报告阐明了公司面临的问题。
近义词: light, brighten, clarify, explain, elucidate
反义词: darken, obscure, confuse
词性: verb

illuminate: /ɪˈluːmɪneɪt/ (US) /ɪˈljuːmɪneɪt/ (UK)
adjective
被照亮的
lit up
The illuminated manuscript was a beautiful work of art.  这本被照亮的稿件是一件美丽的艺术品。
Her face was illuminated by the candlelight. 她的脸被烛光照亮了。
近义词: lit, bright
反义词: dark, dim
词性: adjective
