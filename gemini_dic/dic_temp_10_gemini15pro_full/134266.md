noninflectional:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈflɛkʃənəl/ | adj. |非屈折的，非活用 |Not undergoing inflection; unchanging in form.  A noninflectional language does not use grammatical inflections to mark distinctions in tense, person, number, or gender. | English prepositions are noninflectional. | 近义词: invariable, uninflected | 反义词: inflectional | 词性: adjective
