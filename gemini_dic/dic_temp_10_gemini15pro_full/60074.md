eed:/iːd/| n.| 需要; 渴望; 缺乏; 必需品|A desperate need for food and water. 对食物和水的迫切需要。|近义词:necessity, requirement, want, lack|反义词:abundance, surplus|词性:名词

词源: From Old English *ēad, from Proto-Germanic *auðiō (“wealth, riches, abundance, easy circumstances, happiness, good fortune”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ew- (“to help, to promote”).
