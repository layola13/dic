barbed:/bɑːrbd/| adj.|带刺的；尖刻的；讽刺的|Covered with barbs or points, or (of a remark) sharply critical or unpleasant.|例句：The barbed wire fence kept the cattle in. 带刺的铁丝网把牛围了起来。|例句：She made a barbed comment about his lack of experience. 她对他的缺乏经验发表了尖刻的评论。|近义词：pointed, sharp, thorny, prickly, cutting, caustic, sarcastic|反义词:kind, gentle, complimentary|词性:形容词

barbed:/bɑːrbd/| v.|装倒钩|To furnish with a barb or barbs. (This usage is less common than the adjective form)|例句：He barbed the hook with a small piece of metal. 他用一小块金属给钩子装上了倒钩。|例句：The fishhook was carefully barbed to ensure a good catch.  鱼钩被小心地装上了倒钩，以确保能钓到鱼。|近义词：hook, spear|反义词:blunt|词性:动词
