glut:/ɡlʌt/| n. v.|供过于求，过剩；充斥，充满|An oversupply of something.|The market was glutted with cheap imports. 市场充斥着廉价的进口商品。|近义词：surplus, excess, oversupply|反义词：shortage, scarcity, dearth|词性:名词,动词

glut:/ɡlʌt/| v. |使…充满；使…过饱|Fill to excess.|He glutted himself with rich food. 他吃油腻的食物吃撑了。|近义词：satiate, gorge, stuff|反义词：starve, deprive|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *glouter (unattested), from Latin gluttīre (“to swallow, gulp down”), from gula (“throat, gullet”).
