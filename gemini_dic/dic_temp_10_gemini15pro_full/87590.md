heterospecific:/ˌhɛtərəʊspɪˈsɪfɪk/ | adj. | 异种的，不同物种的 | Belonging to a different species.  Relating to or involving individuals of different species. |  Heterospecific competition can influence the distribution of species. 异种竞争会影响物种的分布。|  Heterospecific pollen transfer can sometimes lead to hybridization.  异种花粉传播有时会导致杂交。|  conspecific | allospecific, interspecific | adjective
