amplify-on:/ˈæmplɪfaɪ ɒn/ | phrasal verb | 详述，进一步阐述 | To expand or elaborate on a statement or idea, providing more details and information. |  例句：Could you amplify on your earlier remarks about the project's budget? 你能详细说明一下你之前关于项目预算的评论吗？

例句：The CEO amplified on the company's new strategy during the press conference. 首席执行官在新闻发布会上详细阐述了公司的新战略。 | 近义词：elaborate on, expand on, expound on | 反义词：summarize, condense, abbreviate |词性：动词短语
