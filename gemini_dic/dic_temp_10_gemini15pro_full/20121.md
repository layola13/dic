bipartible:/baɪˈpɑːrtɪbl/
adjective
可二分的, 可分成两部分的
Capable of being divided into two parts.
The bipartible graph can be colored with two colors.  这个二分图可以用两种颜色着色。
The land was bipartible between the two brothers. 这块土地可以在两兄弟之间分割。
近义词: divisible, separable
反义词: indivisible, inseparable
词性: adjective

bipartite:/baɪˈpɑːrtɪt/
adjective
由两部分组成的，两方的
Having two parts; involving two parties.
They signed a bipartite agreement. 他们签署了一份双边协议。
The bipartite system of government ensures checks and balances. 两党制的政府系统确保了制衡。
近义词: dual, two-sided
反义词: unified, single
词性: adjective
