agritourism:/ˈæɡrɪtʊərɪzəm/| n. |农业旅游|A form of tourism in which tourists stay on farms or in agricultural areas, participating in farm activities or experiencing rural life.|Example 1: Agritourism is becoming increasingly popular as people seek authentic travel experiences. 农业旅游正变得越来越受欢迎，因为人们正在寻找真实的旅行体验。

Example 2: The family enjoyed their agritourism vacation, picking apples and learning about farm animals. 这家人很享受他们的农业旅游假期，摘苹果并学习农场动物的知识。|近义词：farm tourism, rural tourism|反义词: urban tourism|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *agri-* (combining form of agriculture) + *tourism*.  The term gained popularity in the late 20th century.
