embroilment:/ɪmˈbrɔɪlmənt/
n.
卷入，纠缠
the state of being involved in an argument or a difficult situation.
He was drawn into the bitter embroilment. 他被卷入了这场激烈的争论。
involvement, entanglement, imbroglio
disentanglement, detachment
