motorboaters:/ˈməʊtəbəʊtəz/ (UK) /ˈmoʊtərboʊtərz/ (US)| n.|摩托艇驾驶者；玩摩托艇的人|People who operate or ride in motorboats. |例句：The motorboaters sped across the lake. 摩托艇驾驶者飞快地驶过湖面。

例句：Several motorboaters were enjoying the sunny afternoon.  几个摩托艇驾驶者正在享受阳光明媚的下午。|近义词：boaters, yachtsmen (if the motorboat is a yacht)|反义词:pedestrians, cyclists|词性:noun


motorboater:/ˈməʊtəbəʊtə(r)/ (UK) /ˈmoʊtərboʊtər/ (US)| n.|摩托艇驾驶者；玩摩托艇的人|A person who operates or rides in a motorboat. |例句：The motorboater waved as he passed by.  那个摩托艇驾驶者经过时挥了挥手。

例句：A motorboater caused a large wake, upsetting the canoes. 一个摩托艇驾驶者激起了很大的尾流，弄翻了独木舟。|近义词：boater, yachtsman (if the motorboat is a yacht)|反义词:pedestrian, cyclist|词性:noun


词源: motor + boat + -er

motor (n.) from Old French "moteur" meaning "mover," from Latin "motor"  meaning "one who moves."
boat (n.) from Old English "bāt"
-er (suffix) forms agent nouns, indicating someone who performs an action or is associated with something.
