brutishly:/ˈbruːtɪʃli/| adv.|野蛮地，粗暴地|In a cruel and violent way, like a brute (a savage animal or person).|He brutishly attacked the old man. 他野蛮地袭击了那个老人。|The guard brutishly shoved the prisoner into the cell. 警卫粗暴地把囚犯推进了牢房。|近义词：savagely, cruelly, brutally, barbarously|反义词：gently, kindly, tenderly|词性:副词
Etymology: From brute + -ish + -ly.  "Brute" comes from the Latin word "brutus," meaning "dull, senseless, irrational."
