fertigation:/ˌfɜːrtɪˈɡeɪʃən/
n.
灌溉施肥
The application of fertilizers or other water-soluble products through an irrigation system.
例句：Fertigation is a common practice in modern agriculture.  灌溉施肥是现代农业中常见的做法。

例句：The farmer uses fertigation to deliver nutrients directly to the plant roots.  农民使用灌溉施肥将养分直接输送到植物根部。

近义词：chemigation (化学灌溉), nutrient application (营养施用)
反义词: N/A (没有明确的反义词)
词性: noun


Etymology:
A portmanteau of "fertilize" and "irrigation".  由“fertilize”（施肥）和“irrigation”（灌溉）两个词组合而成。
