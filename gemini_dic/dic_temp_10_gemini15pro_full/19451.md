bicuspid:/baɪˈkʌspɪd/| adj. n.|二尖瓣的；二尖牙；|Having two cusps or points, typically referring to a tooth or heart valve.|The bicuspid valve controls blood flow between the left atrium and left ventricle of the heart.|近义词：mitral (for the heart valve), premolar (for the tooth)|反义词:tricuspid|词性:adjective, noun

bicuspid:/baɪˈkʌspɪd/| n.|二尖瓣；二尖牙|A tooth with two cusps or points, specifically a premolar tooth, or the mitral valve of the heart.|She had a cavity in her bicuspid.|近义词：premolar, mitral valve|反义词:molar, tricuspid valve|词性:noun

词源: From Latin *bi-*, meaning "two," and *cuspis*, meaning "point."
