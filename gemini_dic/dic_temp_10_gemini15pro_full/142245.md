oromo: /ɔˈroʊmoʊ/ | n. adj.|奥罗莫人；奥罗莫语|Oromo refers to an ethnic group and their language primarily inhabiting Ethiopia, parts of Kenya, and Somalia.  It's the most widely spoken Cushitic language. |The Oromo people have a rich cultural heritage. 奥罗莫人拥有丰富的文化遗产。|He speaks Oromo fluently. 他说奥罗莫语很流利。|近义词: (for the ethnic group) Cushitic (more general) |反义词: None|词性: Noun, Adjective

Etymology:  The origin of the name "Oromo" is debated, with various theories proposed. Some suggest it's derived from the word "ilma orma" meaning "sons of men" or "children of Orma." Others link it to "ora" meaning "people."  More research is needed for a definitive etymology.
