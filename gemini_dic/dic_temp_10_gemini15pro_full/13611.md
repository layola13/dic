attested:/əˈtɛstɪd/ | adj. | 已证实的，已证明的 | Proven or verified through evidence or documentation. |  The theory is attested by numerous experiments. 这个理论已被大量实验所证实。 | Synonyms: verified, confirmed, documented, substantiated, proven | Antonyms: unverified, unconfirmed, undocumented, unsubstantiated, unproven | adjective

attested:/əˈtestɪd/ | v. (past participle of attest) | 证实，证明 | To affirm to be true or genuine; to bear witness to. |  The document attests to his innocence. 这份文件证明了他的清白。| Synonyms: certify, corroborate, validate, verify, confirm | Antonyms: deny, contradict, refute, disprove | verb (past participle)
