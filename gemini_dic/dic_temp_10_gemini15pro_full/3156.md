aetiologically:/ˌiːtiəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 病因学地 |  In a way that relates to the causes or origins of a disease or condition.  Relating to or concerned with etiology. | The disease is aetiologically linked to a genetic defect.  这种疾病在病因学上与基因缺陷有关。| Synonym: causally | Antonym: N/A | Part of Speech: adverb

aetiology:/ˌiːtiˈɒlədʒi/ | n. | 病因学 | The study of the causes or origins of diseases or abnormal conditions. |  The aetiology of the disease is still unknown. 这种疾病的病因仍然未知。 | Synonym: causation, pathogenesis | Antonym: N/A | Part of Speech: noun
