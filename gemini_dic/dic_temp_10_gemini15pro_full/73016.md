footbrakes: /ˈfʊtbreɪks/ | n. | 脚刹，脚制动器 | A brake operated by a pedal, usually in a car or other vehicle. | The driver slammed on the footbrakes to avoid hitting the pedestrian. 司机猛踩脚刹以避免撞到行人。|  The car's footbrakes failed, causing a serious accident. 汽车的脚刹失灵，导致了一起严重事故。 | 近义词: brakes,  pedal brake | 反义词: accelerator | 词性: noun
