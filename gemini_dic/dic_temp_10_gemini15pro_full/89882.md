horrendously:/hɔːˈrɛndəsli/ /hɑːˈrɛndəsli/
adjective
可怕地；骇人听闻地
in a horrendously bad or unpleasant way.
The play was horrendously bad.  这出戏糟透了。
The traffic was horrendously congested. 交通拥堵得可怕。
synonyms: terribly, awfully, dreadfully, frightfully, appallingly, shockingly
antonyms: wonderfully, beautifully, delightfully, pleasantly
词性: 副词 (adverb)
