outquaffed: /ˌaʊtˈkwæft/
verb
胜过(某人)的酒量; 比…喝得更多
To drink more than someone else.
He outquaffed everyone at the party and ended up singing karaoke. 他在聚会上比任何人都喝得多，最后唱起了卡拉OK。
I tried to outquaff him, but he's a seasoned drinker. 我试图比他喝得更多，但他是个经验丰富的酒鬼。
Synonyms: outdrink, out-drink
Antonyms: be outdrunk
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->

From out- + quaff.  "Out-" is a prefix meaning "to surpass or exceed." "Quaff" means to drink heartily.
