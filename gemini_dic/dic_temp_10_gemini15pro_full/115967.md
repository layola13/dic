marrowfat:/ˈmærəʊfæt/ (UK) /ˈmærˌoʊfæt/ (US)| n. adj.|大粒豌豆；由大粒豌豆制成的|A large, smooth type of pea, typically dried and used for making soup. Also used as an adjective to describe something made from these peas.|例句：She made a hearty marrowfat pea soup. 她做了一道丰盛的大粒豌豆汤。

例句：Marrowfat peas are a traditional ingredient in British cuisine. 大粒豌豆是英国菜肴的传统食材。|近义词：pea, field pea|反义词: N/A|词性:名词, 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From marrow (meaning soft, inner substance) + fat (meaning well-nourished or plump). The name refers to the large, plump size of these peas.
