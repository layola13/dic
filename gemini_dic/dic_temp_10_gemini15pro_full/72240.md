floridities:/flɔːˈɪdɪtiz/| n.| 华丽的辞藻；过分修饰的语言；|  Excessively ornate or flowery language or writing.  |  His prose is marked by floridities and a lack of substance. 他的散文以华丽的辞藻和缺乏实质内容为特点。| synonyms: embellishments, flourishes, ornamentation | antonyms: plainness, simplicity | noun

floridity:/flɔːˈɪdɪti/| n.| 华丽；绚丽；|The quality of being florid. | The floridity of his language made the speech sound insincere. 他华丽的语言使得演讲听起来不真诚。| synonyms: flamboyance, ornateness | antonyms: simplicity, plainness | noun
