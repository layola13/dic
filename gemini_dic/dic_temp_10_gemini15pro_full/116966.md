Munhwa Broadcasting Corporation (韩国文化广播公司)
South Korean broadcasting company.
MBC is one of the leading broadcasting companies in South Korea. 韩国文化广播公司是韩国主要的广播公司之一。

MBC produces a variety of programs, including news, dramas, and entertainment shows. 韩国文化广播公司制作各种节目，包括新闻、戏剧和娱乐节目。
synonym: broadcasting company, media outlet
antonym:  (No direct antonym for a company, could be considered as "independent production" or "silence")
