cafila:/kəˈfiːlə/ /kæˈfiːlə/ | n. | 行队，商队 | A group of people or animals traveling together, especially in a long line.  A company of travelers; a caravan. |  The cafila wound its way slowly across the desert. 商队蜿蜒缓慢地穿过沙漠。|  The cafila of camels carried goods across the Silk Road. 骆驼商队沿着丝绸之路运送货物。|Synonyms: caravan, procession, train, convoy | Antonyms: individual, lone traveler | noun
