intermixable:/ˌɪntərˈmɪksəbl/ | adj. |可混合的，可混淆的|Capable of being mixed together without separating or reacting negatively.| The two liquids are intermixable and can be combined in any proportion. 这两种液体可以混合，可以按任何比例混合。| Synonyms: miscible, blendable, combinable | Antonyms: immiscible, unblendable, incompatible | adjective

intermix:/ˌɪntərˈmɪks/ | v. | 混合，混淆 | To mix together; blend. | He intermixed the different varieties of seeds. 他把不同品种的种子混合在一起。| Synonyms: mix, blend, mingle, combine | Antonyms: separate, divide, sort | verb
