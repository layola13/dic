lysimeter:/laɪˈsɪmɪtər/
n.|渗漏测定计，蒸散计|An instrument for measuring percolation and leaching, especially for determining the amount of water that percolates through soil and the quantity and quality of dissolved materials carried by the water. It typically consists of a container of soil from which drainage water can be collected and analyzed.|A lysimeter was installed to measure the amount of water and nutrients leaching from the fertilized field.  一个渗漏测定计被安装用于测量从施肥田地中渗出的水和养分的数量。|Synonyms: percolation gauge, drainage gauge|Antonyms: N/A|noun

lysimeter:/laɪˈsɪmɪtər/
n.|蒸散计|An instrument, often consisting of a tank filled with soil and vegetation, used to measure evapotranspiration by monitoring changes in weight or water content.|The lysimeter data helped researchers understand the water balance of the ecosystem. 蒸散计数据帮助研究人员了解生态系统的水平衡。|Synonyms: evapotranspirometer|Antonyms: N/A|noun
