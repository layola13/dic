macroeconomic: /ˌmækroʊˌiːkəˈnɑːmɪk/ /ˌmækrəʊˌiːkəˈnɒmɪk/ | adj. |宏观经济的| Relating to the branch of economics concerned with large-scale or general economic factors, such as interest rates and national productivity.|例句：Macroeconomic policy affects the entire economy. 宏观经济政策影响着整个经济。|近义词：economy-wide, aggregate |反义词: microeconomic |词性:形容词

macroeconomics: /ˌmækroʊˌiːkəˈnɑːmɪks/ /ˌmækrəʊˌiːkəˈnɒmɪks/ | n. |宏观经济学| The part of economics concerned with large-scale or general economic factors, such as interest rates and national productivity.|例句：He studied macroeconomics in college. 他在大学学习宏观经济学。|近义词：|反义词: microeconomics |词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From macro- (large) + economic.  "Macro-" comes from the Greek "makros" meaning "long" or "large."
