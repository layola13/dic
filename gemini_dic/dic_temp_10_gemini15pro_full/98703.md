insupportable:/ˌɪnsəˈpɔːrtəbl/ | adj. |无法忍受的，不能支持的|Unable to be endured or tolerated; not justifiable.|The pain in his leg was insupportable. 他的腿疼得难以忍受。|Synonyms: unbearable, intolerable, unendurable, unsustainable|Antonyms: bearable, tolerable, supportable, sustainable|adjective

insupportable:/ˌɪnsəˈpɔːrtəbl/ | adj. |站不住脚的，不能成立的|Not justifiable; lacking sound basis.|Her argument was logically insupportable. 她的论点在逻辑上站不住脚。|Synonyms: untenable, indefensible, flimsy, weak|Antonyms: sound, valid, justifiable, defensible|adjective
