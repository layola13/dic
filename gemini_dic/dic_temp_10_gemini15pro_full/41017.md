copartnership:/koʊˈpɑːrtnərʃɪp/
n.|合伙；合伙企业；合伙关系|A business organization in which two or more individuals agree to share in the profits or losses of a business venture.|Example: They formed a copartnership to develop the new software. 他们建立了合伙企业来开发新的软件。|Example: The copartnership dissolved after a year due to disagreements.  由于分歧，该合伙企业一年后解散了。|Synonym: partnership, alliance, collaboration|Antonym: sole proprietorship, corporation|noun
