foreignly:/ˈfɔːrɪnli/
adv.
像外国人一样地；在外国地；外来地；不熟悉地
In a foreign manner; as a foreigner would; abroad; unnaturally.
She behaved foreignly in her native land after living abroad for so long.  她在国外生活了这么久，回到家乡后举止像个外国人。
近义词:strangely, unnaturally, exotically
反义词:naturally, domestically, familiarly
词性:副词

词源:
From foreign + -ly.  由foreign（外国的）加上副词后缀-ly构成。
