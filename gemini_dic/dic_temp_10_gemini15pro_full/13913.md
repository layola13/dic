augustulus:/ɔːˈɡʌstjʊləs/
|n.|小奥古斯都，末代皇帝|A diminutive of Augustus, used specifically to refer to Romulus Augustulus, the last Western Roman Emperor.  He was given this somewhat derisive nickname, meaning "Little Augustus."|Romulus Augustulus, often referred to as Augustulus, was deposed in 476 AD.|近义词:last emperor, final ruler|反义词:first emperor, founding ruler|词性:noun
