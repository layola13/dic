hushest:/ˈhʌʃɪst/| adj.|最安静的，最寂静的|Superlative form of hush: most hush.  It describes the state of being extremely quiet or silent.|The hushest whisper could be heard in the library. 在图书馆里，即使是最轻的耳语也能听到。|近义词：quietest, stillest, silentest|反义词：loudest, noisiest|词性:形容词
词源：From Middle English *hushtest, superlative of *husht (“silent, hushed”), from hush +‎ -est.
