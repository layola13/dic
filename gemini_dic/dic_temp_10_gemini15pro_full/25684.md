bungo:/ˈbʌŋɡoʊ/
n.
文学作品；大作；（贬义）矫揉造作的文学作品
A literary work, especially a pretentious or overwritten one.
He considers his latest novel a bungo, but the critics disagree. 他认为他的最新小说是一部大作，但评论家并不同意。
synonym:opus, work, composition, tome
antonym:trash, drivel
n.

bungo:/ˈbʌŋɡoʊ/
n.
(俚语)作家；文人；（贬义）卖弄文墨的人
(Slang) A writer, especially one who is pretentious or affected.
That bungo is always showing off his vocabulary. 那个文人总是在炫耀他的词汇量。
synonym:author, writer, novelist, litterateur
antonym:amateur, layman
