nonappeasable:/ˌnɒnəˈpiːzəbl/
adjective
无法安抚的，不可平息的
not capable of being appeased, soothed, or pacified
His nonappeasable hunger for power led to his downfall. 他对权力永不满足的渴望导致了他的垮台。
The bereaved family felt nonappeasable grief.  这个丧失亲人的家庭感到无法平息的悲痛。
近义词:implacable, unyielding, relentless
反义词:appeasable, placable, conciliatory
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- + appeasable, from appease + -able.  "Non-" prefix meaning "not" combined with "appeasable," which comes from the verb "appease" (to pacify or soothe) and the suffix "-able" (capable of being).
