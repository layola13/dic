metamorphoses:/ˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfəsiːz/ | n. | 变形，变质，蜕变 | A process of transformation, a marked change in appearance, character, condition, or function. |  The butterfly undergoes a series of metamorphoses. 蝴蝶经历了一系列的蜕变。

The artist's style underwent a series of metamorphoses throughout his career.  这位艺术家的风格在他整个职业生涯中经历了一系列的变迁。 | synonyms: transformations, changes, mutations, alterations | antonyms: stagnation, fixity, invariance | noun
