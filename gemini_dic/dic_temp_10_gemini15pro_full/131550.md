noncommitments:/ˌnɒnkəˈmɪtmənts/ | n. | 不承诺，不表态，未作承诺的事情 |  The state of being noncommittal; lack of commitment or a definite position.  A refusal or failure to commit oneself.  An instance of this.  |  His speech was full of noncommitments, leaving the audience unsure of his true intentions. 他的演讲充满了不确定性，让听众不清楚他的真实意图。|  The government's noncommitments on the issue have angered many voters. 政府在这个问题上的不表态激怒了许多选民。 | synonyms: evasiveness, vagueness, ambiguity, indecision | antonyms: commitment, promise, pledge, assurance | noun
