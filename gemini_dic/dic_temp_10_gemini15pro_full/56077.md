docketing:/ˈdɑːkɪtɪŋ/
n.
归档；登记；记录
The act of placing a document on a docket or list, especially a legal document for court proceedings.
The docketing of the case took longer than expected.  案件的归档比预期的要长。
record-keeping, filing, registering, listing
