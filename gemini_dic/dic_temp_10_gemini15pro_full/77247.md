gastromyces:/ˌɡæstrəˈmaɪsiːz/ | n. | 腹菌 | A gasteromycete, or gasteroid fungus, is any fungus belonging to the Basidiomycota which bear their spores internally, in a closed fruiting body. |  Gastromyces are a fascinating group of fungi. 腹菌是一组迷人的真菌。 | Many puffballs are gastromyces. 许多马勃菌都是腹菌。 | synonyms: gasteroid fungus, puffball (in some cases) | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps agaric, which bears spores externally) | noun
