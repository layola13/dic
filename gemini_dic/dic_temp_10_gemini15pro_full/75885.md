fundamentalism:/ˌfʌndəˈmɛntəlɪzəm/ | n. | 基要主义，原教旨主义 | A movement or attitude stressing strict and literal adherence to a set of basic principles (usually religious, but sometimes political).  It often involves a rejection of modern interpretations and a belief in the absolute authority of the original text or doctrine. | The rise of fundamentalism in the region has led to increased social tensions. 该地区原教旨主义的兴起导致社会紧张局势加剧。|  The politician's rhetoric appealed to the fundamentalism of a segment of the population. 这位政客的言辞迎合了部分人口的基要主义。 |近义词：conservatism, orthodoxy, traditionalism |反义词：modernism, liberalism, progressivism |词性: noun
