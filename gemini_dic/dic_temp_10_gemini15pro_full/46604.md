damassin:/ˈdæməsɪn, ˌdæməˈsiːn/| n. adj.|大马士革锦缎；大马士革钢；大马士革刀；大马士革的|A type of woven fabric or inlaid metalwork originating from Damascus, Syria. Also used to describe things from or relating to Damascus. | The museum displayed a beautiful damassin sword.  博物馆展出了一把精美的大马士革刀。|近义词：brocade, inlaying, damascened|反义词: (no direct antonym)|名词, 形容词
