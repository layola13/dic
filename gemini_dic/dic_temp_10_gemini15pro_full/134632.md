nonmajorities:/ˌnɒn məˈdʒɒrɪtiz/ | n. | 非多数群体；少数群体；少数派 |  A group or groups that do not constitute a majority.  Those not part of the largest group within a larger population. |  Nonmajorities often face discrimination in political systems designed for majority rule.  非多数群体在为多数统治设计的政治体系中经常面临歧视。

The museum showcased art created by various nonmajorities.  博物馆展示了由各种少数群体创作的艺术。 | 近义词: minorities,  underrepresented groups | 反义词: majorities, dominant groups | 词性: 名词
