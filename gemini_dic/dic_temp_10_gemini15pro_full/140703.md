omnibus:/ˈɒmnɪbʌs/ (UK) /ˈɑːmnɪbʌs/ (US)| adj. n.|公共汽车；总括的；综合性的|A bus or a vehicle designed to carry many passengers; something that includes many items or provisions within one collection.|例句：The omnibus edition of the fantasy series included all seven books. 这套奇幻系列的合订本包含了全部七本书。|近义词：comprehensive, collective, inclusive|反义词：limited, specific|词性: adjective, noun
