antitheologians:/ˌæntiθiːəˈloʊdʒənz/
|n.|反神學者；反神學論者|People who oppose or criticize theology, often questioning or rejecting its core tenets.|The antitheologians argued that traditional doctrines were outdated and irrelevant. 反神學者認為傳統教義已經過時且無關緊要。|Critics of theology, dissenters|Theologians, believers|noun
