enterprising: /ˈentərpraɪzɪŋ/
adjective
有进取心的，有事业心的，有创业精神的
Characterized by great initiative and a readiness to undertake new projects.
She was an enterprising young woman who started her own business at 22. 她是一位有进取心的年轻女性，22岁就创办了自己的企业。
He had an enterprising spirit, always looking for new opportunities. 他有创业精神，总是寻找新的机会。
近义词: ambitious, resourceful, energetic, driven, motivated, proactive
反义词: lazy, indolent, unambitious, passive
词性: adjective

词源: From enterprise + -ing.  "Enterprise" itself comes from Old French "entreprise," meaning "an undertaking."
