interambulacrum:/ˌɪntəræmˈbjʊləkrəm/ | n. |間步帶|An area on the surface of a sea urchin or other echinoderm between the ambulacra (the areas with tube feet). It is typically covered with spines. | The spines on the interambulacrum of this sea urchin are particularly long.  这只海胆間步帶上的棘特别长。|Near synonyms: interambulacral zone, interambulacral area| Antonyms: ambulacrum, ambulacral area| noun
