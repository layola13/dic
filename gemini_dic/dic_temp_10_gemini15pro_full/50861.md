deselecting:/ˌdiːsəˈlɛktɪŋ/
verb
取消选择，撤销选择
The act of reversing a selection, typically in a computer interface.
I am deselecting all the files except this one. 我正在取消选择除这一个以外的所有文件。
He deselected the "bold" option in the text editor. 他在文本编辑器中取消选择了“粗体”选项。
synonyms: unchecking, deactivating, clearing
antonyms: selecting, choosing, activating
