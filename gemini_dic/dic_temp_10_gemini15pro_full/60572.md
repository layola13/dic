elastin:/ɪˈlæstɪn/| n.|弹性蛋白|A protein forming the main constituent of elastic connective tissue, found especially in the dermis of the skin, the lungs, tendons, and blood vessel walls.| Elastin allows skin to return to its original shape after being stretched or pinched.  弹性蛋白使皮肤在被拉伸或挤压后恢复到原来的形状。|Synonyms:  protein, scleroprotein|Antonyms: collagen (in terms of function, not structure) |noun
