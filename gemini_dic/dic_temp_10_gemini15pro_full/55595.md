disuniters:/dɪsjuːˈnaɪtərz/
|n.|分裂者，不团结的人|People who cause disunity or discord; those who break apart a group or organization. |The disuniters in the group were eventually asked to leave.  该团体中的分裂分子最终被要求离开。|Synonyms: dividers, separatists, schismatics|Antonyms: uniters, joiners, harmonizers|noun
disunite:/ˌdɪsjuːˈnaɪt/
|v.|使分裂，使不和|To cause disunity or discord; to separate or break apart a group or organization. |Their conflicting opinions disunited the team.  他们相互矛盾的意见使团队分裂。|Synonyms: divide, separate, sever|Antonyms: unite, join, connect|verb
