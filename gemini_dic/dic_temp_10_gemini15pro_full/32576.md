chirographer:/kaɪˈrɑːɡrəfər/
n.
书法家；代笔人；文牍员
A person skilled in handwriting, especially one who writes formal documents or legal papers.
The chirographer meticulously penned the elaborate contract.  书法家一丝不苟地写下了这份复杂的合同。
The king employed a chirographer to draft his royal decrees. 国王雇佣了一名文牍员来起草他的皇家法令。
synonym: calligrapher, scribe, penman
antonym: typist (arguably)
词性: noun

chirographic:/ˌkaɪrəˈɡræfɪk/
adj.
亲笔的；书法体的
Of or relating to handwriting, especially elegant or formal handwriting.
The chirographic evidence suggested the letter was authentic. 书法证据表明这封信是真实的。
His chirographic style was distinctive and easily recognizable. 他的书法风格独特，易于辨认。
synonym: handwritten, calligraphic
antonym: typed, printed
词性: adjective

chirography:/kaɪˈrɑːɡrəfi/
n.
书法；笔迹
Handwriting, especially elegant or formal handwriting.
The expert analyzed the chirography to determine the author of the note. 专家分析了笔迹以确定留言的作者。
He studied chirography as a form of art. 他把书法作为一种艺术形式来学习。
synonym: handwriting, penmanship, calligraphy
antonym: typing, printing
词性: noun
