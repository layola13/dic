babied:/ˈbeɪbid/| v. adj.|溺爱，娇惯; 被溺爱的，娇惯的|To treat (someone) as a baby; pamper.|She babied her youngest son. 她溺爱她的小儿子。|近义词：pampered, coddled, spoiled|反义词：neglected, ignored|词性:动词(过去式和过去分词), 形容词

词源: From baby + -ed,  meaning "treated like a baby."
