cleanly:/ˈkliːnli/| adv.|干净地，整洁地；利落地，彻底地|in a clean way; completely and effectively; honestly and fairly|She sliced the bread cleanly. 她干净利落地切开了面包。|近义词：neatly, tidily, efficiently, effectively, completely, thoroughly|反义词：messily, sloppily, incompletely, dishonestly|词性:副词

词源: From Middle English *clenly, *clenliche, from Old English clǣnlīċe (“purely, cleanly, splendidly, brightly, elegantly, clearly”), equivalent to clean +‎ -ly. Cognate with Scots clenlie (“cleanly, neatly”), West Frisian kleanlik (“cleanly”), Dutch keurig (“nice, neat, tidy, exquisite”).
