advocacy: /ˈædvəkəsi/
n.|拥护，倡导，辩护|The organization does advocacy work for children's rights.  这个组织致力于倡导儿童权利。|Public advocacy for the new law is growing. 新法律的公众支持率正在增长。|近义词：support, promotion, defense|反义词：opposition, resistance|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *advocatia*, from Latin advocatus (“called to one's aid”), past participle of advocare (“to summon, to call to one's aid”).  来自晚期拉丁语 *advocatia*，源自拉丁语 advocatus（“被召唤来帮助某人”），advocare（“召唤，请求帮助”）的过去分词。

USAGE->
一般现在时:
His advocacy for the homeless is commendable. = 他对无家可归者的倡导值得称赞。
Her advocacy focuses on environmental protection. = 她的倡导重点是环境保护。

一般过去时:
Their advocacy played a key role in changing the law. = 他们的倡导在改变法律方面发挥了关键作用。
The group's advocacy helped to raise awareness of the issue. =  该组织的倡导有助于提高人们对这个问题的认识。

现在完成时:
Her advocacy has made a real difference in the community. = 她的倡导在社区中产生了真正的影响。
The organization's advocacy has brought about significant changes. = 该组织的倡导带来了重大变化。

过去完成时:
His advocacy had already gained considerable support before the vote. = 在投票之前，他的倡导已经获得了相当多的支持。
They had been engaged in advocacy for years before they saw any real progress. = 在看到任何真正的进展之前，他们已经从事了多年的倡导工作。

将来时:
Their advocacy will continue until the law is changed. = 他们的倡导将持续到法律修改为止。
His advocacy will be crucial to the success of the campaign. = 他的倡导对于竞选的成功至关重要。

被动语态:
Strong advocacy was demonstrated for the proposed changes. = 对提议的变更表达了强烈的支持。
Effective advocacy is being carried out by several organizations. =  几个组织正在进行有效的倡导。

PHRASE->
environmental advocacy = 环境倡导
patient advocacy = 病人权益倡导
legal advocacy = 法律援助
public advocacy = 公众倡导
community advocacy = 社区倡导
