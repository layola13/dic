folklore:/ˈfoʊklɔːr/ (US) /ˈfəʊklɔː(r)/ (UK)| n. |民俗，民间传说|The traditional beliefs, customs, and stories of a community, passed through the generations by word of mouth.|Irish folklore is filled with tales of fairies and leprechauns. 爱尔兰民俗充满了仙女和小妖精的故事。|近义词：tradition, legend, myth, lore|反义词:fact, history|名词

folklore:/ˈfoʊklɔːr/ (US) /ˈfəʊklɔː(r)/ (UK)| adj. |民俗的|Relating to traditional beliefs, customs, and stories.|This is a folklore festival celebrating local traditions. 这是一个庆祝当地传统的民俗节日。|近义词：traditional, customary|反义词:modern, contemporary|形容词
