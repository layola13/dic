baklava:/bɑːkˈlɑːvə/ /bɑːkləˈvɑː/
n.
果仁千层酥，巴卡拉瓦
A rich, sweet pastry made of layers of filo pastry filled with chopped nuts and sweetened with syrup or honey.
My favorite dessert is baklava. 我最喜欢的甜点是果仁千层酥。
The bakery is famous for its delicious baklava. 这家面包店以其美味的果仁千层酥而闻名。

近义词:pastry, sweet
