gladsome:/ˈɡlædsəm/| adj.|高兴的；令人高兴的|Pleased and happy; causing joy.|例句：The gladsome tidings of the birth of their first child filled them with joy.  他们第一个孩子出生的喜讯让他们欣喜万分。

例句：The children were gladsome at the prospect of a trip to the zoo. 孩子们对去动物园的前景感到高兴。|近义词：joyful, cheerful, merry, joyous|反义词：sad, sorrowful, unhappy, gloomy|词性:形容词
词源:From Old English *glædsum, from glæd "glad" + -sum "-some."  来自古英语 *glædsum，由 glæd “高兴的” + -sum “-some” 组成。
