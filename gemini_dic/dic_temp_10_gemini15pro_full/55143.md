dissecting:/dɪˈsektɪŋ/ /daɪˈsektɪŋ/
verb
解剖；仔细分析
Methodically cutting up (a plant or animal) in order to study its internal parts.  Carefully analyzing something in detail.
The students were dissecting a frog in biology class. 学生们在生物课上解剖青蛙。

The lawyer dissecting the witness's testimony. 律师仔细分析证人证词。
近义词: analyzing, examining, studying, scrutinizing
反义词: synthesizing, constructing, combining
词性: verb (present participle of dissect)

dissect:/dɪˈsekt/ /daɪˈsekt/
verb
解剖；仔细分析
Methodically cut up (a plant or animal) in order to study its internal parts. Carefully analyze something in detail.
We dissected a frog in biology class. 我们在生物课上解剖了一只青蛙。
The critic dissected the novel, exploring its themes and symbolism.  评论家仔细分析了这部小说，探讨了它的主题和象征意义。
近义词: analyze, examine, study, scrutinize
反义词: synthesize, construct, combine
词性: verb

Etymology: From Latin dissecare "to cut apart," from dis- "apart" + secare "to cut."
