lums:/lʌmz/ | n. | 校友，毕业生（尤指苏格兰圣安德鲁斯大学的）|  Specifically refers to alumni, particularly those of the University of St Andrews in Scotland.  Derived from "alumnus," shortened and pluralized in Scots. |  The lums gathered for the annual reunion. 校友们聚集在一起参加年度聚会。| synonym: alumni, graduates | antonym:  (no direct antonym, could consider current students) | noun
