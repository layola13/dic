Iberville: /ˈaɪbərˌvɪl/ | n. | 伊贝维尔 | A surname and place name, most notably referring to Pierre Le Moyne d'Iberville, a French Canadian soldier, explorer, and founder of the French colony of Louisiana, and the Parish of Iberville in Louisiana, named after him.  |  Iberville Parish is located in Louisiana. 伊贝维尔堂区位于路易斯安那州。 | The explorer Iberville claimed Louisiana for France. 探险家伊贝维尔宣布路易斯安那为法国所有。 | 近义词: (For the person) explorer, founder; (For the place) parish, county, district | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun
