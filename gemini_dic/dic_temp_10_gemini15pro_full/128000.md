nephilim: /ˈnɛfɪlɪm/ | n. |巨人;堕落天使之子|Offspring of angels and humans, often described as giants.  They are mentioned in the Book of Genesis and other biblical texts. |The nephilim were said to roam the earth before the Great Flood.  据说在大洪水之前，巨人漫游在地球上。| The Nephilim were feared for their great size and strength. 巨人因其巨大的体型和力量而令人恐惧。 | synonyms: giants, titans | antonyms: dwarfs, midgets | noun
