auxin:/ˈɔːksɪn/| n.|生长素；植物激素|A plant hormone which promotes root formation and bud growth.|例句：Auxin plays a crucial role in plant development. 生长素在植物发育中起着至关重要的作用。

例句：The scientists conducted experiments to study the effects of auxin on plant growth.  科学家们进行了实验来研究生长素对植物生长的影响。|近义词：plant hormone, growth regulator|反义词: (No direct antonym, but some plant hormones have opposing effects)|词性:noun
