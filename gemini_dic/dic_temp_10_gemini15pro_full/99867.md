intermural:/ˌɪntərˈmjʊrəl/ | adj. | 校内的，学院内的 | Existing or carried on within the bounds of an institution, especially a school or college.  Relating to activities involving teams from the same school or college. | Example: The university has a vibrant intermural sports program. 这所大学有一个充满活力的校内体育项目。

Example:  Intermural debates are a popular activity at our school.  校内辩论是我们学校很受欢迎的活动。 | 近义词:  intramural, internal, domestic | 反义词: extramural, intercollegiate, external | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *inter* (between, among) + *murus* (wall).  Originally referred to activities within the walls of an institution.
