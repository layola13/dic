celluloid:/ˈsɛljʊlɔɪd/| n. adj.|赛璐珞；电影胶片|A flammable plastic material made from nitrocellulose and camphor, formerly used for photographic film.|Old movies are sometimes referred to as "celluloid dreams."  老电影有时被称为“赛璐珞之梦”。|The film was shot on celluloid. 这部电影是用赛璐珞胶片拍摄的。|近义词：film, movie, plastic|反义词: None|词性: noun, adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *celluloïde*, from cellulose + -oid.  Coined in 1870.
