circulatory:/ˈsɜːrkjələtɔːri/ (US) /ˈsɜːkjʊlətəri/ (UK)| adj.|循环的，循环系统的|Relating to the circulation of blood or other fluids in the body.|The circulatory system is responsible for transporting oxygen and nutrients throughout the body. 循环系统负责将氧气和营养物质输送到全身。|
circulatory:/ˈsɜːrkjələtɔːri/ (US) /ˈsɜːkjʊlətəri/ (UK)| adj.|循环的，循环系统的|The heart plays a vital role in the circulatory process. 心脏在循环过程中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词: cardiovascular, vascular|反义词: None|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *circulatorius, from Latin circulator "messenger, peddler," from circulatus, past participle of circulare "to make round, go around," from circulus "circle," diminutive of circus "circle, ring."
