igniter:/ɪɡˈnaɪtər/| n. |点火器，点火装置|A device used to start combustion, such as in a gas appliance or an internal combustion engine.|例句：The igniter on the stove stopped working, so we couldn't light the burner. 炉子上的点火器坏了，所以我们无法点燃炉头。

例句：He used an electric igniter to start the campfire. 他用电子点火器点燃了篝火。|近义词：lighter, starter, detonator|反义词:extinguisher|词性:名词
