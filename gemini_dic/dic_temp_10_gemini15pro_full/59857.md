ectropion: /ɛkˈtroʊpiɒn/ | n. | 外翻；外翻症 |  The eversion of the edge of an eyelid (usually the lower eyelid), exposing the inner surface.  This can lead to dryness, irritation, and excessive tearing. | My grandmother suffers from ectropion, and needs to use eye drops regularly. 我的祖母患有眼睑外翻，需要定期使用眼药水。 | The ectropion caused his eye to become red and irritated. 眼睑外翻导致他的眼睛发红和不适。 |  eversion, turning outward | entropion, inversion | noun
