nonflux:/ˈnɑːnflʌks/ | n. |无流, 不变, 稳定|Absence of flow or movement; stability. |The nonflux of the market surprised analysts. 市场的不变让分析师们感到惊讶。|近义词：stability, stagnation, standstill|反义词：flux, change, flow|词性:名词

Etymology:  From non- "not" + flux "flow".  "Flux" comes from Latin "fluxus," past participle of "fluere" "to flow."
