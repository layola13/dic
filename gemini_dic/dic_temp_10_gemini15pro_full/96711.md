ineffectualities:/ˌɪnɪˌfɛkˈtʃuːælɪtiz/
|n.|无效；无能；低效|The report highlighted the various ineffectualities of the current system.  报告强调了当前系统的各种低效之处。|
|English Explanation: The state of being ineffectual; lack of effectiveness; the quality or fact of not producing any significant or desired effect.|
|例句：The ineffectualities of the committee led to its disbandment. 委员会的低效导致其解散。|
|例句：The government acknowledged the ineffectualities of its policies in addressing poverty. 政府承认其政策在解决贫困方面的无效性。|
|近义词：inefficiencies, inadequacies, shortcomings, failures|
|反义词：effectiveness, efficiency, efficacy, potency|
|词性: Noun (plural)|
