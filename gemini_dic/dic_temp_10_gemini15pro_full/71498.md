flanged:/flændʒd/| adj.|带凸缘的，有法兰的|Having a flange or flanges.  A flange is a projecting rim, edge, or rib on an object, often used for strengthening or attaching. |The pipe was flanged for easy connection.  这个管道带有法兰以便于连接。|The wheels of the train were flanged. 火车的轮子是有凸缘的。|近义词：rimmed, edged, lipped|反义词: flangeless|adjective
