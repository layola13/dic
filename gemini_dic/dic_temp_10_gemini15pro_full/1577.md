achromatic:/ˌeɪkrəˈmætɪk/| adj.|无色的，消色的|Transmitting light without separating it into constituent colors.|The telescope uses achromatic lenses to minimize chromatic aberration. 这台望远镜使用消色差透镜以最大限度地减少色差。|Synonyms: colorless, uncolored, monochrome, neutral|Antonyms: chromatic, colorful, colored|adjective

achromatic:/ˌeɪkrəˈmætɪk/| n.|消色差透镜|A lens or system of lenses that is achromatic.|He bought a new achromatic for his telescope. 他为他的望远镜买了一个新的消色差透镜。|Synonyms: achromat|Antonyms: N/A|noun


USAGE->
Attributive Usage (as an adjective):

Achromatic lenses are essential for high-quality telescopes. = 消色差透镜对于高质量望远镜至关重要。

This achromatic is designed to reduce color fringing. =  这个消色差透镜旨在减少色边。
