overliberalize: /ˌoʊvərˈlɪbərəlaɪz/ | v. | 过度自由化，过度放鬆管制 | To liberalize excessively; to remove too many restrictions or controls, often with negative consequences. | The government was criticized for overliberalizing the financial sector, which contributed to the economic crisis. 政府因过度放鬆金融部门管制而受到批评，这导致了经济危机。|  The country's overliberalized banking system led to a wave of reckless lending.  这个国家过度自由化的银行体系导致了一波鲁莽的贷款。 | 近义词: deregulate, loosen,  | 反义词: restrict, regulate, control | 词性: 动词
