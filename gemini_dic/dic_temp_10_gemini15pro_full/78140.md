geometrize:/ˌdʒiːˈɒmɪtraɪz/ | v. | 几何化 | To represent or interpret in geometric terms; to give a geometric form or arrangement to. |  Mathematicians attempted to geometrize algebra in the 19th century.  数学家们在 19 世纪试图将代数几何化。 |  formalize, systematize, structure | amorphize, disorganize | verb

geometrization:/ˌdʒiːəˌmɛtrɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 几何化 | The act or process of geometrizing. | The geometrization of physics has been a major focus of research. 物理学的几何化一直是研究的重点。 | formalization, systematization |  disorganization | noun

Etymology: From French *géométriser*, from géométrie ("geometry") + -iser (suffix forming verbs from nouns or adjectives). Ultimately from Ancient Greek γεωμετρία (geōmetría, “geometry”).
