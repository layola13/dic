hexatomic:/ˌhɛksəˈtɒmɪk/ /ˌhɛksəˈtɑːmɪk/| adj.|六原子的|Containing six atoms in the molecule.|例句：Ozone (O3) is a hexatomic molecule. 臭氧是一个六原子分子。

例句：Water (H2O) is not hexatomic; it is triatomic. 水不是六原子的；它是三原子的。|近义词：six-atom |反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider terms like "diatomic," "triatomic," etc. for molecules with different numbers of atoms)|词性:形容词
