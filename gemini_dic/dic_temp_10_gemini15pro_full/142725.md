otocrane:/ˈoʊtəkreɪn/| n. |汽车起重机|A crane mounted on a truck or other automotive vehicle, used for lifting and moving heavy objects.|The otocrane lifted the steel beams into place. 汽车起重机将钢梁吊到位。|近义词：mobile crane, truck crane|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:  "oto-" (from Greek "autos" meaning "self") + "crane" (from Old English "cran" ultimately from Proto-Germanic *kranuz).  The prefix implies self-propelled.
