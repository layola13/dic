nah:/nɑː/| interj.| 不，不是，没有 |An informal way of saying no.  It expresses a negative response, often casually or dismissively.| Nah, I don't feel like it.  不，我不想去。|近义词：no, nope |反义词:yeah, yep|感叹词


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
I nah go there often. = 我不经常去那里。
He nah like spicy food. = 他不喜欢辛辣的食物。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
I'm nah going there today. = 我今天不去那里。
She's nah working on that project anymore. = 她不再做那个项目了。


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I've nah seen him lately. = 我最近没见过他。
They've nah finished the report yet. = 他们还没有完成报告。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I've nah been feeling well recently. = 我最近感觉不太好。
He's nah been studying much for the exam. = 他最近没有怎么学习考试。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I nah go to the party last night. = 我昨晚没去参加派对。
She nah tell me what happened. = 她没有告诉我发生了什么。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was nah sleeping when you called. = 你打电话的时候我没在睡觉。
They were nah working on the project at that time. = 他们当时没有在做那个项目。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had nah finished my work before you arrived. = 在你到达之前我没有完成我的工作。
She had nah eaten anything all day. = 她一整天都没吃东西。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had nah been feeling well for a few days. = 我已经感觉不舒服好几天了。
They had nah been working on the project for very long. = 他们做这个项目的时间并不长。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I nah go to the meeting tomorrow. = 我明天不去开会。
She nah be able to come to the party. = 她不能来参加派对。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I nah be working at this time tomorrow. = 明天这个时候我不会在工作。
They nah be attending the conference. = 他们不会参加会议。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I nah have finished the project by next week. = 我下周之前不会完成这个项目。
She nah have graduated by then. = 到那时她不会毕业。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
I nah have been working on this project for a year by next month. = 到下个月，我将不会已经做这个项目一年了。
He nah have been studying French for very long by the time he goes to France. = 到他去法国的时候，他学习法语的时间不会很长。



虚拟语气:
If I wanted to go, I nah say no. = 如果我想去，我不会说不。
I wish I nah have to work today. = 我希望我今天不用工作。

被动语态:
The decision nah be made until next week. = 直到下周才会做出决定。
The project nah be completed on time. = 这个项目不会按时完成。

疑问句:
Nah you going to the party? = 你不去参加派对吗？
Nah she finish her homework yet? = 她还没完成作业吗？

否定句:
I nah going to tell him. = 我不会告诉他。
She nah agree with you. = 她不会同意你的观点。



PHRASE->
Nah, I'm good. = 不了，我没事。
Nah, that's alright. = 不了，没关系。
Nah, I don't think so. = 不，我不这么认为。
Oh nah, he didn't! = 哦，不，他没这么做！
Nah way! = 没门！/不可能！


注意事项:
"Nah" is very informal and should be used with caution in formal settings.  It's primarily used in spoken English.
