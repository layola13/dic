illumine:/ɪˈluːmɪn/
verb
照亮，启发
to light up; to make clear or understandable
The full moon illumined the path ahead. 满月照亮了前方的路。
Her explanation illumined the complex topic. 她的解释阐明了这个复杂的话题。
synonyms: enlighten, illuminate, brighten
antonyms: darken, obscure
词性:动词
