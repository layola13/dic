nontautologically:/ˌnɒntɔːtəˈlɒdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 非同义反复地 |  In a way that is not tautological; without needless repetition of an idea.  Derived from "non-" (not) + "tautological" (characterized by tautology, needless repetition). The word "tautology" comes from the Greek "tautologos," meaning "repeating what has been said," from "tauto" (the same) + "logos" (word). | He explained the concept nontautologically, using clear and concise language. 他用清晰简洁的语言，非同义反复地解释了这个概念。

He presented the argument nontautologically, ensuring each point added new information. 他非同义反复地提出了论点，确保每个点都添加了新的信息。| 近义词: distinctly, uniquely, originally | 反义词: tautologically, redundantly | 词性: adverb
