frequenting:/ˈfriːkwentɪŋ/
verb
经常光顾，频繁出入
visiting a place or person often.
He was known for frequenting the local bars. 他因经常光顾当地酒吧而出名。
She frequents the library, always looking for new books. 她经常去图书馆，总是寻找新书。
近义词:visiting, attending, patronizing
反义词:avoiding, shunning

词性:v.
词源:From Middle French *frequenter, from Latin frequentāre (“to visit often, resort to, haunt, crowd, throng”), present active infinitive of frequentō (“I visit often”), from frequēns (“crowded, frequent”).
