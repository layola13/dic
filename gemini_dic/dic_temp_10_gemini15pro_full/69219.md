fearingly:/ˈfɪərɪŋli/| adv.|害怕地，恐惧地|In a manner showing or expressing fear.  Expressing or suggesting fear.|She looked fearingly at the dark shadows. 她害怕地望着黑暗的阴影。|The child fearingly clung to his mother's hand. 孩子恐惧地抓着妈妈的手。|近义词：apprehensively, anxiously, timidly|反义词：boldly, bravely, confidently|词性: 副词

词源:
From the adjective "fearing," which is the present participle of the verb "fear."  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *feraz, meaning "danger."
