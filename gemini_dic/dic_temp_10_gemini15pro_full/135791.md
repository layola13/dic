nonprojecting:/ˌnɒn.prəˈdʒektɪŋ/
adjective
非突出的，不伸出的
Not projecting or protruding outwards.
The building has a nonprojecting facade.  这栋楼有一个不突出的正面。
The shelves are nonprojecting, maximizing floor space.  这些架子不突出，最大限度地利用了地面空间。
近义词:recessed, flush, flat
反义词:projecting, protruding, prominent
词性:adjective
