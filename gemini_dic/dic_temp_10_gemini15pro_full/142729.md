otolaryngologies:/ˌoʊtəˌlærɪŋˈɡɑːlədʒiz/
n.
耳鼻喉科学; 耳鼻咽喉科
The study and treatment of diseases of the ear, nose, and throat.
Otolaryngologies encompasses a wide range of medical conditions. 耳鼻喉科学包含了广泛的医疗状况。
Advances in otolaryngologies have improved the lives of countless patients. 耳鼻喉科学的进步改善了无数患者的生活。
近义词:ENT (Ear, Nose, and Throat), otorhinolaryngology
反义词:None


词性:Noun (Plural)
