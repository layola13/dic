canest:/ˈkeɪnɪst/ | n. | 篮子；藤条编织物 |A container made of interwoven strips of cane or other flexible material. | She carried a canest filled with fresh flowers. 她提着一个装满鲜花的篮子。|  basket, hamper | container, box | noun

Etymology: From Middle English *canest, from Old French canastre (“wicker basket”), from Italian canestro, from Latin canistrum (“wicker basket, bread-basket”), from Ancient Greek κάνιστρον (kánistron, “wicker basket”).
