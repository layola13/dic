lousily: /ˈlaʊzɪli/ | adv. | 糟糕地，差劲地 | In a lousy manner; poorly; badly.  |  He played the piano lousily. 他弹钢琴弹得很糟糕。 | The food was lousily prepared. 食物做得非常难吃。 | 近义词: badly, poorly, terribly, awfully | 反义词: well, excellently, superbly | 词性: 副词

词源: From the adjective "lousy," which originally meant infested with lice, and later came to mean anything unpleasant or of poor quality.  Ultimately from the Proto-Germanic *lusuz ("louse").
