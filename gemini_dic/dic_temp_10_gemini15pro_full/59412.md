earthenwares:/ˈɜːrθənˌwɛərz/
n.
陶器，土器
Dishes and other articles made of baked clay.
The museum has a fine collection of early Italian earthenwares.  这家博物馆收藏了精美的早期意大利陶器。
近义词：pottery, ceramics, crockery
反义词：(无直接的反义词，可以考虑用 materials like metal, plastic, glass 等表达非陶器的材质)
词性: 名词
