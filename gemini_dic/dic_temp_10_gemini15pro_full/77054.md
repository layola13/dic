garnering:/ˈɡɑːrnərɪŋ/
verb
收集，积累，获得
The film garnered several awards. 这部电影获得了几个奖项。
The proposal is garnering a lot of support. 这项提议获得了许多支持.
gather, collect, accumulate, amass
scatter, dissipate, lose
v.
