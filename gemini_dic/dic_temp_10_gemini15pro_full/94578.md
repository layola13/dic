impoverishment:/ɪmˈpɒvərɪʃmənt/ | n. | 贫困，贫穷化 |The state of being made poor or the process of becoming poor.  A reduction in the quality of something. | The region's impoverishment was a result of the prolonged drought. 这个地区的贫困是长期干旱的结果。|  The impoverishment of the soil due to overuse of pesticides is a serious concern. 由于过度使用杀虫剂，土壤贫瘠化是一个严重的问题。 | poverty, destitution, deprivation |  wealth, affluence, enrichment | noun
