awarded:/əˈwɔːrdɪd/
Verb
已授予的，已奖励的
past participle of "award" -  given as a prize or reward.
She was awarded a medal for her bravery. 她因勇敢而被授予一枚奖章。
awarded, granted, presented, bestowed
refused, denied, withdrawn
verb
