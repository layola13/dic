imprejudicate:/ɪmˈprɛdʒʊdɪkɪt/ | adj. | 无偏见的；公平的；不带成见的 | Not having or showing prejudice; impartial. | The judge remained imprejudicate throughout the trial. 法官在整个审判过程中保持公正。| The members of the jury must be imprejudicate. 陪审团成员必须保持公正。 | 近义词: unbiased, impartial, neutral, objective, fair, unprejudiced | 反义词: prejudiced, biased, partial, unfair | 词性: 形容词
Etymology:  From Late Latin *impreiudicatus, from in- (“not”) + praejudicatus (“prejudged”).  The Latin word praejudicare means "to judge beforehand".
