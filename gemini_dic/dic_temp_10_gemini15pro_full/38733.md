conditioning:/kənˈdɪʃənɪŋ/ | n. |  调节;  训练;  条件作用;  空调 | the process of training or accustoming a person or animal to behave in a certain way or to accept certain circumstances. | Example 1:  Classical conditioning involves associating a stimulus with a response.  经典条件反射包括将刺激与反应联系起来。

Example 2: The athlete underwent rigorous physical conditioning before the competition.  这位运动员在比赛前进行了严格的身体训练。 | Synonyms: training, preparation, adaptation, acclimatization | Antonyms: neglect, disregard | noun


Etymology: From the verb "condition," which comes from Old French "condicion" and ultimately from Latin "condicio" (agreement, condition).
