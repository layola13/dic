crestless:/ˈkrɛstlɪs/| adj.|无冠的，无顶的|Lacking a crest, referring to the absence of a tuft, plume, or similar adornment on the head of a bird or other animal.  It can also be used metaphorically to describe something lacking a distinguishing feature or culminating point.|The crestless cormorant is a common sight along the California coast. 加州海岸经常能看到无冠鸬鹚。| The crestless wave lapped gently at the shore.  无峰的波浪轻柔地拍打着海岸。|Synonyms: unadorned, plain, simple|Antonyms: crested, adorned, ornamented|adjective
