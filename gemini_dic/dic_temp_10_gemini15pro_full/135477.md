nonphenolic:/ˌnɒnˈfiːnɒlɪk/ | adj. |非酚的|Not relating to, derived from, or having the characteristics of phenol or phenols.  A substance that does not contain a phenolic group (a hydroxyl group bonded directly to an aromatic hydrocarbon group).|Nonphenolic antioxidants are less prone to discoloration. 非酚类抗氧化剂不易变色。

The wood contains nonphenolic extractives.  这种木材含有非酚类提取物。|近义词: non-phenolic (variant spelling) |反义词: phenolic|形容词
