half-clothed:/ˌhæf ˈkloʊðd/
adj.
半裸的，穿着不完整的
partially clothed; wearing only some clothes, not fully dressed
The man was found half-clothed and disoriented in the street.  这个男人被发现衣衫不整，在街上迷失方向。
The half-clothed children played in the sprinkler.  半裸的孩子们在洒水器里玩耍。
near synonyms:  partially dressed, scantily clad, underdressed
antonyms: fully clothed, dressed, overdressed
adjective
