inkinesses:/ˈɪŋkɪnɪsɪz/
n.
墨黑; 漆黑
the quality or state of being inky.
The inkinesses of the night sky were breathtaking. 夜空的漆黑令人叹为观止。
synonyms: blackness, darkness, obscurity
antonyms: brightness, lightness, whiteness
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From inky + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  Inky itself comes from ink + -y (adjective suffix). Ink ultimately derives from Old French "enque," from Late Latin "encaustum," meaning "burnt in," referring to a durable ink used in ancient times.
