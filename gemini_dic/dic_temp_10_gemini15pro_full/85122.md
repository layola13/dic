hardhead:/ˈhɑːrdhɛd/ (US) /ˈhɑːdhed/ (UK)| n. adj.|倔强的人，硬汉；执拗的，顽固的|A stubborn or obstinate person; unwilling to change their mind.  Also used as an adjective to describe this quality.|He's a real hardhead and won't listen to anyone's advice. 他真是个顽固的人，谁的劝告都不听。|近义词：stubborn, obstinate, pigheaded, inflexible, unyielding|反义词:flexible, open-minded, amenable, compliant|词性:noun, adjective
