aspire-after:/əˈspaɪər ˈɑːftər/
verb phrase
渴望; 追求; 向往
to have a strong desire to achieve or obtain something
He aspired after a career in music. 他渴望从事音乐事业。
近义词: desire, crave, yearn for, long for, seek, pursue
反义词: disdain, reject, shun
词性: 动词短语

词源:
"aspire" comes from the Latin "aspirare," meaning "to breathe upon, to long for."  "After" indicates the object of the aspiration.
