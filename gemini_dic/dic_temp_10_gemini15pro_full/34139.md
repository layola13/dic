claimed: /kleɪmd/ | v. | 声称；要求；索取；认领 | to say that something is true or is a fact, although you cannot prove it and other people might not believe it | He claimed that he had been cheated. 他声称自己被骗了。 | asserted, professed, declared | denied, refuted | verb

claimed: /kleɪmd/ | adj. | 被声称的；被要求的；被认领的 | said to be true or real, although this may not be the case | The claimed benefits of the new drug have not been proven. 这种新药所声称的益处尚未得到证实。 | alleged, purported, supposed | unclaimed, undisputed | adjective
