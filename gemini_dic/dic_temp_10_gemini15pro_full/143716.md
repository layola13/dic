outlingered:/ˌaʊtˈlɪŋɡərd/
verb
逗留得比…久; 比...停留更久
stayed longer than someone or something else
The guests outlingered the host. 客人们比主人待得更久。
近义词: outstay, overstay
反义词: leave early, depart quickly
词性: verb

词源:  "out-" (超过) + "linger" (逗留)
