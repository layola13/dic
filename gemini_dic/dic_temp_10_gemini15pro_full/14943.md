azurine:/ˈæʒʊrɪn, əˈzʊərɪn/| n. |天蓝色, 天青石|A bright greenish-blue colour similar to that of a summer sky. Also, a pigment or dye of this colour, or a substance dyed with it. |The azurine of the tropical sea was breathtaking. 热带海洋的天蓝色令人叹为观止。|近义词：azure, cerulean, sky-blue|反义词:None specified for colours, but could be dark, dull|词性:Noun
