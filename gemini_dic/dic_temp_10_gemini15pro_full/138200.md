nozzle: /ˈnɒzl/ (British) /ˈnɑːzl/ (American) | n. | 喷嘴; 管嘴; 嘴; 鼻孔| a projecting spout, tip, or orifice by which a fluid is discharged. |  The garden hose has an adjustable nozzle.  花园水管有一个可调节的喷嘴。 | Example: The fireman attached the nozzle to the hose and aimed it at the fire. 消防员把喷嘴接到水管上，对准了火。| Synonyms: spout, jet, tip, aperture, orifice | Antonyms: inlet, intake | noun
