detraining:/diːˈtreɪnɪŋ/
n.
停止训练; 训练的停止; 脱训练
The loss of physiological adaptations resulting from a cessation or substantial reduction in training.
After just two weeks of detraining, he noticed a decrease in his muscle mass.  仅仅两周不训练，他就注意到肌肉量下降了。
近义词: inactivity, cessation of training
反义词: training, exercising
