overprominence:/ˌoʊvərˈprɑːmɪnəns/| n.|过分突出；过分强调|The overprominence of the singer's voice made the other instruments hard to hear. 歌手的声音过分突出，使得其他乐器难以听到。|例句：The overprominence of the bass in the mix made the song sound muddy.  低音在混音中过分突出，使歌曲听起来浑浊。

The overprominence of his ego alienated many of his colleagues. 他过分突出的自我意识疏远了许多同事。|近义词：emphasis, prominence, salience|反义词:inconspicuousness, obscurity|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + prominence.  "Over-" signifies "excessive" or "too much." "Prominence" comes from the Latin "prominere," meaning "to project" or "to stand out."
