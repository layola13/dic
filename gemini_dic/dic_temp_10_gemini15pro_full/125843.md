murza:/ˈmʊərzə/| n.|穆尔扎，俄罗斯贵族头衔，鞑靼王子|A murza was a member of the Tatar nobility in Russia. 穆尔扎是俄罗斯鞑靼贵族的一员。|例句：The murza held considerable power in his region.  这位穆尔扎在他的地区拥有相当大的权力。

例句：Ivan the Terrible executed many murzas to consolidate his power. 伊凡雷帝处决了许多穆尔扎以巩固他的权力。|近义词：noble, prince|反义词:commoner, peasant|词性:noun

Etymology: From Russian мурза (murza), from Tatar morza, ultimately from Persian  میرزا (mirzā, “prince, son of an emir”).
