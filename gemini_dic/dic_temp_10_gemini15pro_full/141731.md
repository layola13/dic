orality:/ɔːˈrælɪti/ | n. | 口语性，口头性 | The quality of being spoken or verbally communicated, rather than written.  It refers to the traditions, practices, and expressions of cultures that rely primarily on spoken language for communication, knowledge transmission, and cultural preservation. |  Many indigenous cultures maintain a rich orality, passing down stories and traditions through spoken word. 许多原住民文化保持着丰富的口语性，通过口语传承故事和传统。|  verbalism, spokenness | literacy | noun
