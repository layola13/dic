metaphysics:/ˌmɛtəˈfɪzɪks/| n.|形而上学|The branch of philosophy that deals with the first principles of things, including abstract concepts such as being, knowing, substance, cause, identity, time, and space.|例句：He studied metaphysics in college. 他在大学学习形而上学。|例句：Her interests lie in the realm of metaphysics. 她的兴趣在于形而上学领域。|近义词：philosophy, ontology, cosmology|反义词:physics, empiricism|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *metaphysica*, neuter plural of *metaphysicus* "metaphysical," from Greek *ta meta ta physika* "the (works) after the Physics," title of Aristotle's treatise on the subject, placed after his works on physics.  *Meta* means "after" or "beyond."
