oracling:/ˈɔːrəklɪŋ/| n. |神谕; (从区块链外部获取数据)|The process of a blockchain obtaining information from the outside world.  This is often necessary for smart contracts to execute based on real-world events.|Example 1: The smart contract relies on oracling to get the latest price of Ethereum.  这个智能合约依赖于预言机获取以太坊的最新价格。

Example 2:  Secure oracling is crucial for the reliability of decentralized applications.  安全的预言机对于去中心化应用程序的可靠性至关重要。|Synonym: data fetching, external data retrieval|Antonym: N/A|noun
