overfranchised: /ˌoʊvərˈfrænˌtʃaɪzd/
adjective
过度特许经营的
Having too many franchises, especially in a particular area, leading to increased competition and potentially reduced profitability for each franchisee.
The market is overfranchised, with too many similar businesses competing for the same customers.  市场过度特许经营，太多类似的企业争夺相同的客户。
The overfranchised pizza market is struggling due to increased competition.  由于竞争加剧，过度特许经营的披萨市场举步维艰。
近义词: saturated, oversaturated
反义词: underfranchised
