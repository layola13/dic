curvaceous:/kɜːrˈveɪʃəs/
adj.
曲线美的，玲珑剔透的
(of a woman or her figure) having an attractively curved shape.
She was a tall, curvaceous blonde. 她是一位身材高挑、曲线优美的金发女郎。
近义词:shapely, voluptuous, full-figured
反义词:thin, slim, straight
词性:adjective
