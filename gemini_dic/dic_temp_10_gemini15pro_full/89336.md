homographic:/ˌhɒməˈɡræfɪk/ /ˌhoʊməˈɡræfɪk/| adj.|同形异义的|Having the same spelling as another word but different meaning and origin.|The words "lead" (the metal) and "lead" (to guide) are homographic.|近义词：homonymous, homomorphic|反义词：heterographic|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ὁμόγραφος (homógraphos, “written alike”), from ὁμός (homós, “same”) + γράφω (gráphō, “I write”).
