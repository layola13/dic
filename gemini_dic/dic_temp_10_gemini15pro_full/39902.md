constitutionist:/ˌkɒnstɪˈtjuːʃənɪst/ | n. | 宪政主义者；立宪主义者 | A person who strongly supports or advocates for a constitution or constitutional government. |  He was a staunch constitutionist, believing in the rule of law above all else. 他是一位坚定的宪政主义者，相信法律至上。

He argued, like a good constitutionist, that the president's actions were unconstitutional.  他像一个优秀的宪政主义者一样，认为总统的行为违宪。 | 近义词: constitutionalist, advocate (for constitutional government) | 反义词: dictator, authoritarian, tyrant | 词性: noun
