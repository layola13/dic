intermodillion:/ˌɪntərˈməʊdɪljən/ | n. | 小小間空飾; 小間空飾部分 | A small space between modillions (small brackets supporting a cornice).  A small, ornamental bracket used in series under a cornice. | The intricate carvings on the cornice included delicate intermodillions.  檐口上精细的雕刻包括精致的小小間空飾。 | The architect specified the precise dimensions of the intermodillions. 建築師指定了小小間空飾的精確尺寸。 | 近义词: space, interval, gap | 反义词: connection, junction | 词性: noun
