common-name:/ˈkɒmən neɪm/ | adj. | 常见的，普通的 |Common name refers to the everyday name of a living organism, as opposed to its scientific (Latin) name. | The common name for *Canis lupus familiaris* is dog.  *犬科动物*的俗称是狗。 | Synonym: vernacular name, trivial name | Antonym: scientific name, binomial name | adjective

common-name:/ˈkɒmən neɪm/ | n. |  俗名，通称 |Common name can also refer to a name that is shared by many people. |  Smith is a very common name in England. 史密斯在英国是一个很常见的姓氏。 | Synonym: usual name, frequent name | Antonym: rare name, unusual name | noun
