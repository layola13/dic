oaths:/oʊθs/
n.
誓言，誓约
A solemn promise, often invoking a divine witness, regarding one's future action or behavior.
They took oaths of allegiance to the king. 他们向国王宣誓效忠。

近义词: vows, pledges, affirmations
反义词: lies, falsehoods, perjuries
词性: 名词(plural)

词源:
From Old English "āð," from Proto-Germanic "*aiþaz" (source also of Old Norse "eiðr," Old Frisian "ēth," Old Saxon "ēth," Old High German "eid," Dutch "eed," German "Eid"), from PIE "*oi-to-" (source also of Latin "ait," he says).
