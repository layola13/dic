huskily:/ˈhʌskɪli/ | adv. | 嘶哑地；粗声地；沙哑地 | In a husky voice; hoarsely. |  She spoke huskily, her voice thick with emotion. 她沙哑地说着，声音里充满了感情。

He coughed huskily and cleared his throat. 他沙哑地咳嗽了一声，清了清嗓子。 | 近义词: hoarsely, roughly, gruffly | 反义词: clearly, smoothly, melodiously | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From husk + -ly.  Relating to the dry outer covering of some fruits or seeds.  A husky voice is rough and dry, like a husk.
