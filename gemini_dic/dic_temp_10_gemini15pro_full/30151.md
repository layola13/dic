celsius: /ˈselsiəs/ | adj. n. |摄氏度的，摄氏度|A scale of temperature in which water freezes at 0° and boils at 100° under standard conditions. |例句：The temperature today is 25 degrees Celsius. 今天气温是25摄氏度。|近义词：centigrade |反义词: Fahrenheit |词性: adjective, noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Anders Celsius (1701-1744), a Swedish astronomer.
