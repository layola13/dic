northernism:/ˈnɔːrðərnɪzəm/ | n. |北方主义，北方方言，北方人的特征|A custom, belief, or habit peculiar to the north, especially the northern part of a country.  A word or phrase characteristic of a northern dialect. |  Example 1: His northernism was apparent in his pronunciation of certain vowels. 他的北方口音在他某些元音的发音中很明显。

Example 2: The use of "aye" instead of "yes" is a common northernism.  用“aye”代替“yes”是北方常见的方言。 | 近义词: regionalism, dialect, localism | 反义词: southernism | 词性: noun
