didjeridoo:/ˌdɪdʒəriˈduː/ /ˌdɪdʒəˈriːduː/
noun
迪吉里杜管
A wind instrument developed by Indigenous Australians of northern Australia, made from a hollowed tree branch and played with vibrating lips.
He played a haunting melody on the didjeridoo. 他用迪吉里杜管吹奏了一曲萦绕心头的旋律。
Synonyms: didgeridoo (alternative spelling)
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific instrument)
