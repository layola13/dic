broadsheet: /ˈbrɔːdʃiːt/
n.
大报，宽幅印刷品
A newspaper printed on large sheets of paper, typically regarded as more serious and intellectual in content than tabloids.
The prime minister's speech was reported in all the broadsheets. 首相的讲话被所有的大报报道了。
近义词: newspaper, journal, quality paper
反义词: tabloid
词性: 名词

broadsheet: /ˈbrɔːdʃiːt/
n.
单张印刷品，传单
A large sheet of paper printed on one side only, used for posters, advertisements, etc.
Broadsheets were distributed advertising the upcoming concert. 传单分发出去宣传即将举行的音乐会。
近义词: flyer, handbill, poster
反义词: 无
词性: 名词
