non-consent:/ˌnɒn.kənˈsent/ | n. | 未同意，不同意 |  Lack of permission or agreement.  Absence of consent, especially in relation to sexual activity. | She reported the incident as non-consensual sex. 她举报了这起事件是非自愿性行为。 |  He took the car without her non-consent. 他未经她同意就拿走了车。 | 近义词：disagreement, refusal, objection | 反义词：consent, agreement, permission | 词性: 名词

non-consentual:/ˌnɒn.kənˈsenʃuəl/ | adj. | 未经同意的，非自愿的 |  Describing something done without permission or agreement, typically referring to sexual activity. |  The act was deemed non-consensual.  该行为被认为是未经同意的。| The police are investigating a non-consensual sexual encounter. 警方正在调查一起非自愿的性接触事件。 | 近义词：forced, involuntary, against one's will | 反义词：consensual, voluntary, agreed upon | 词性: 形容词


词源:

"non-"  a prefix meaning "not"

"consent" from Old French consentir, from Latin consentire "to feel together," from com- "together" + sentire "to feel."
