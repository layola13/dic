monocotyl:/ˈmɒnəkɒtɪl/ (US) /ˈmɒnəʊkɒtəl/ (UK)| n. |单子叶植物|A flowering plant with an embryo that bears a single cotyledon (seed leaf). Monocots also typically have parallel-veined leaves, fibrous root systems, and floral parts in multiples of three.|例句：Corn is a monocot. 玉米是单子叶植物。

|例句：Most grasses are monocots. 大多数草都是单子叶植物。|近义词：monocot |反义词:dicot (dicotyledon)|词性:noun
