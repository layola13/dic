lubriciously:/luːˈbrɪʃəsli/
adverb
淫荡地；油滑地；流畅地
in a way that is smooth and slippery, or suggesting sexual enjoyment.  Characterized by lewdness; sexually suggestive.
He lubriciously described the woman's curves. 他淫荡地描述了那个女人的曲线。
The salesman lubriciously talked his way into a deal.  这个销售员油嘴滑舌地达成了交易。
近义词: lewdly, lasciviously, salaciously, smoothly, slickly
反义词: chastely, modestly, roughly, coarsely
词性: adverb

词源: From Latin *lubricus* "slippery," from *lubricari* "to make slippery," related to *labor* "to slip, glide."
