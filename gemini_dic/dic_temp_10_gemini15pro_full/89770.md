horded:/ˈhɔːrdɪd/ | v. | 囤积，贮藏 | accumulated and stored away, often excessively |  He horded old newspapers in his garage. 他在他的车库里囤积旧报纸。 |  stockpiled, amassed, stashed | squandered, dispersed | verb
hoard:/hɔːrd/ | n. | 贮藏，囤积物 | a supply or accumulation that is hidden or carefully guarded |  She had a hoard of antique jewelry. 她有一堆古董珠宝。| stockpile, cache, reserve | lack, dearth | noun
hoarder:/ˈhɔːrdər/ | n. | 囤积者 | a person who hoards things | The hoarder's house was filled to the brim with junk.  囤积者的房子里堆满了垃圾。| collector, accumulator | spender, philanthropist | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English "hord" (treasure, hoard), from Proto-Germanic "*huzdą" (treasure), from Proto-Indo-European "*keudh-" (to hide, conceal).
