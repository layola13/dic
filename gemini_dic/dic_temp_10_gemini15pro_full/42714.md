counteruniversity:/ˌkaʊntərˌjuːnɪˈvɜːrsɪti/ | n. | 反大学，另类大学，与主流大学体系相反的教育机构 | An institution offering alternative, often radical, education outside the mainstream university system.  It emphasizes self-directed learning, community involvement, and social change. |  Example: The counteruniversity offered workshops on community organizing and sustainable living. 反大学提供了关于社区组织和可持续生活的研讨会。

Example:  He chose the counteruniversity because he felt traditional universities were too focused on career advancement and not enough on critical thinking. 他选择了反大学，因为他觉得传统大学过于注重职业发展，而对批判性思维的关注不足。 | 近义词: free university, alternative university, people's university | 反义词: traditional university, mainstream university | 词性: 名词
