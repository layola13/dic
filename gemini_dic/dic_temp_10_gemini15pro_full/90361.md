hoverers:/ˈhʌvərərz/
noun
盘旋者，徘徊者
People or things that hover.  A device designed to hover.
The hoverers observed the scene from above. 盘旋者从空中观察着现场。
drones, helicopters, aircraft
settled individuals, grounded objects
