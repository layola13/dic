outpoisoning:/ˌaʊtˈpɔɪzənɪŋ/| n. |超越毒害，以更强的毒药毒害|The act of poisoning someone with a more potent poison than they are currently suffering from or have been poisoned with. It's a relatively uncommon word, often used in a figurative sense.|The dictator tried to outpoison his rivals, both literally and figuratively, by spreading more vicious rumors than they could. 这个独裁者试图通过散播比对手更恶毒的谣言来“毒害”他们，无论是在字面上还是比喻意义上。|Near synonyms: outmaneuvering, surpassing, defeating, outwitting|Antonyms:  succumbing, yielding, being defeated|noun
