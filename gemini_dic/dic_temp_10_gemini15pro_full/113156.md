lupine:/ˈluːpaɪn/ (US), /ˈljuːpaɪn/ (UK)| adj. n.|狼的，狼似的；羽扇豆（属植物）|Relating to or resembling a wolf; a plant of the genus Lupinus.|The lupine predator stalked its prey.  狼一般的捕食者跟踪着它的猎物。|She planted lupines in her garden. 她在花园里种了羽扇豆。|近义词：(adj.) wolfish, feral; (n.) lupin|反义词: (adj.) tame, domesticated|词性: adjective, noun

lupine:/ˈluːpaɪn/ (US), /ˈljuːpaɪn/ (UK)| n.|羽扇豆|A plant of the genus Lupinus, typically having brightly coloured flowers.|The lupines bloomed in vibrant hues of blue and purple. 羽扇豆开出了鲜艳的蓝色和紫色花朵。|近义词: lupin|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *lupin, from Latin lupinus (“of the wolf”), from lupus (“wolf”). The plant was thought to deplete the soil, like a wolf devours its prey.
