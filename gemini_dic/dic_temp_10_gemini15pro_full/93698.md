imitative:/ˈɪmɪteɪtɪv/ | adj. | 模仿的，仿效的 | Characterized by or given to imitation; copying or following a model or example. | Her imitative style of painting lacked originality. 她模仿式的绘画风格缺乏 originality. |  His playful imitative cough made us laugh. 他开玩笑地模仿咳嗽声，逗得我们哈哈大笑。 | 近义词:  emulative, mimicking, copying, aping | 反义词: original, innovative, creative | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *imitativus, from Latin imitatus (past participle of imitari "to imitate"), from imago "image, likeness."
