ndugu: /nˈduːɡuː/ | n. | 兄弟；同胞；同志；朋友 |  A Swahili word meaning "sibling," "relative," or "friend," often used as a term of endearment or solidarity, especially among people of African descent. |  Example: Harambee, ndugu! Let's pull together, brothers and sisters!  团结一致，兄弟姐妹们！| Example: He greeted his ndugu with a warm embrace. 他热情地拥抱了他的朋友。| Synonym: brother, sister, friend, comrade, compatriot | Antonym: enemy, foe | Noun
