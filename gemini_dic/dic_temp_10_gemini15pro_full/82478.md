grungemann:/ˈɡrʌndʒmæn/ | n. | 垃圾摇滚乐迷；邋遢的人 | A fan of grunge music, often characterized by a messy or unkempt appearance.  | He looked like a stereotypical grungemann, with ripped jeans and unwashed hair. 他看起来就像一个典型的垃圾摇滚乐迷，穿着破洞牛仔裤，头发也没洗。 | 近义词:slacker, grunge rocker | 反义词: neatnik, preppy | 词性: noun

Etymology:  "Grunge" (referring to the music genre) + "man" (suffix indicating a person associated with something).  "Grunge" itself originated as slang for "dirt" or "grime."
