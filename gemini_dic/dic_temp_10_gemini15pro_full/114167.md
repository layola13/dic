ETYMOLOGY->
From *Mahommed* (an older, now considered inaccurate, spelling of *Muhammad*) + *-an* (forming adjectives and nouns referring to persons connected with the thing specified) + *-ism* (forming nouns denoting action, state, or doctrine).  The term reflects a misunderstanding of Islam, which emphasizes the worship of God (Allah), not Muhammad.
