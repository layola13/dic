nudibranch:/ˈnjuːdɪbræŋk/ | n. | 裸鳃亚目动物，裸鳃类动物 | A shell-less marine gastropod mollusk, noted for its brilliant colors and striking forms.  |  例句：The nudibranch crawled slowly across the coral.  裸鳃类动物缓慢地爬过珊瑚。

例句：Divers often spot colorful nudibranchs on reefs. 潜水员经常在珊瑚礁上发现色彩鲜艳的裸鳃类动物。 | 近义词：sea slug | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps shelled gastropod) | 词性: noun
