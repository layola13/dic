angels:/ˈeɪndʒəlz/| n.|天使; 守护神; 安琪儿 (人名)|A spiritual being believed to act as an attendant, agent, or messenger of God, conventionally represented in human form with wings and a long robe.|She believes her child is an angel. 她相信她的孩子是个天使。|The nurses in the hospital were like angels to the patients. 医院里的护士对病人来说就像天使一样。|Near synonyms: seraph, cherub, guardian angel|Antonym: demon|Noun
