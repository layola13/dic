nonmechanistic:/ˌnɒnˌmɛkəˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非机械论的 | Not pertaining to or explained by the principles of mechanics; not involving a view that regards natural processes as like those of a machine. |  The study provided nonmechanistic evidence for the efficacy of the treatment. 这项研究为治疗的疗效提供了非机械论的证据。 |  Non-mechanistic explanations often invoke concepts like vitalism or emergent properties. 非机械论的解释通常会援引活力论或涌现性等概念。 | 近义词: non-physical, vitalistic, holistic | 反义词: mechanistic, physical, reductionist | 词性: adjective
