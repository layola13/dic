greedless: /ˈɡriːdlɪs/
adjective
无贪婪的；不贪心的
Lacking greed; unselfish.
He lived a simple, greedless life, content with what he had.  他过着简朴、无贪婪的生活，满足于他所拥有的一切。
She greedlessly donated her winnings to charity. 她毫不贪婪地把奖金捐给了慈善机构。

近义词: generous, unselfish, altruistic, magnanimous
反义词: greedy, avaricious, covetous, rapacious
词性: adjective

词源: greed + -less.  From greed (n.) + -less (suffix forming adjectives meaning 'without').
