advertizings: /ˈædvərˌtaɪzɪŋz/
noun
广告活动（非标准用法，通常用advertising）
This word is not standard English. The plural form of "advertising" is generally not used.  "Advertising" itself usually refers to the activity or profession of producing and displaying advertisements. It's rarely used as a countable noun.
We invested heavily in advertizings. (Non-standard usage.  Should be: We invested heavily in advertising.)
He works in advertizings. (Non-standard usage. Should be: He works in advertising.)
synonyms:  promotion, marketing, publicity (for the general concept) campaigns (if referring to multiple specific advertising efforts)
antonyms:   concealment, secrecy, suppression
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From advertise + -ing.  "Advertise" comes from Middle French "advertiser," ultimately from Latin "advertere"  (to turn towards, direct attention to).


USAGE->
The word "advertizings" is generally considered incorrect. Here are examples using the correct term, "advertising":

一般现在时:
Advertising is a powerful tool for businesses. = 广告是企业的有力工具。
Advertising plays a crucial role in modern society. = 广告在现代社会中扮演着至关重要的角色。

一般过去时:
The company advertised its new product on television. = 这家公司在电视上宣传它的新产品。
They advertised their services in the local newspaper. = 他们在当地报纸上宣传他们的服务。


现在完成时:
We have been advertising our products online for years. = 我们多年来一直在网上宣传我们的产品。
She has advertised her business through social media. = 她通过社交媒体宣传她的业务。

过去完成时:
They had advertised the event before it was cancelled. = 他们在活动取消之前就宣传了该活动。
He had advertised his car for sale before he found a buyer. = 他在找到买家之前就宣传了他的汽车待售。

将来时:
We will be advertising our new service next month. = 我们下个月将宣传我们的新服务。
They will advertise their products on the radio. = 他们将在广播中宣传他们的产品。


被动语态:
The product was advertised on TV. = 该产品在电视上做广告。
The job is being advertised online. =  这个职位正在网上招聘。

疑问句:
Is advertising effective? = 广告有效吗？
How much did they spend on advertising? = 他们在广告上花了多少钱？

否定句:
They are not advertising their products during the holiday season. = 他们在节日期间不做产品广告。
We didn't advertise the event because of budget constraints. = 由于预算限制，我们没有宣传这次活动。



PHRASE->
(Using "advertising")
advertising campaign = 广告活动
advertising agency = 广告代理商
advertising budget = 广告预算
advertising revenue = 广告收入
false advertising = 虚假广告



注意事项:
Avoid using "advertizings."  Use "advertising" instead.  If referring to multiple distinct campaigns, you can use "advertising campaigns."
