bombardo:/bɒmˈbɑːrdoʊ/ (US), /bɒmˈbɑːdəʊ/ (UK)| v. |轰炸，炮击；大量发送（信息等）|To attack a place with bombs or shells repeatedly; to inundate someone with something, such as questions, letters, or information. |The enemy bombarded the city for days. 敌人连续几天轰炸了这座城市。| synonyms: shell, bomb, blitz, barrage, pepper, inundate, overwhelm | antonyms: defend, protect | verb

bombardment:/bɒmˈbɑːdmənt/ (US), /bɒmˈbɑːdmənt/ (UK)| n. |轰炸，炮击；大量发送（信息等）|The act of bombarding or being bombarded. |The city was under constant bombardment. 这座城市一直处于不断的轰炸之下。| synonyms: shelling, bombing, attack, barrage, onslaught | antonyms: defense, protection | noun
