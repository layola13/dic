dewrot:/ˈdjuːrɒt/ | n. |露腐病; 露腐|A rot, especially of plants, caused by prolonged exposure to dew.  It is a fungal disease that affects plants, particularly fruits and vegetables, when they are left in damp conditions for an extended period, leading to their decay. | The strawberries developed dewrot after being left out in the wet grass overnight. 草莓在潮湿的草地上过夜后得了露腐病。| The lettuce was ruined by dewrot. 生菜被露腐病毁了。| 近义词: blight, rot, decay | 反义词: growth, health | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From 'dew' + 'rot'. 'Dew' refers to the condensation of atmospheric water vapor that forms on cool surfaces at night, while 'rot' signifies decay or decomposition.
