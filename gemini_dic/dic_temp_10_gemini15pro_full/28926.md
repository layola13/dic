carries: /ˈkæriz/ (US), /ˈkæriz/ (UK)| v.| 携带，运送；承担；支撑；传送；持有；怀有；赢得；通过；报道； |This truck carries heavy loads. 这辆卡车运载重物。|近义词：bears, conveys, transports, supports|反义词: drops, abandons|verb (third-person singular simple present carries, present participle carrying, simple past carried, past participle carried)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English carien, from Old North French carier, from Late Latin carricāre (“to load”), from Latin carrus (“wagon”).
