contango:/kɒnˈtæŋɡəʊ/ (UK) /kənˈtæŋɡoʊ/ (US)| n. |期货溢价；现货贴水|A situation in the futures market where the price of a commodity for future delivery is higher than the spot price.|Example 1: The oil market is currently in contango, with futures prices exceeding spot prices. 石油市场目前处于期货溢价状态，期货价格超过现货价格。

Example 2:  The contango in the gold market encouraged investors to store the metal and sell futures contracts. 黄金市场的期货溢价鼓励投资者储存黄金并出售期货合约。|近义词: premium|反义词: backwardation, normal backwardation|词性:名词
