carve-ups:/ˈkɑːrv ʌps/ | n. |瓜分，分割，划分(尤指不公平的) | An act of dividing something into parts, often unfairly or with self-interest in mind. Typically used to describe the division of profits, territory, or power.  |  The carve-up of the company's assets left the smaller investors with almost nothing. 公司资产的瓜分使得小投资者几乎一无所有。 | The carve-up of the Balkans after the war created many new nations. 战后巴尔干半岛的划分创建了许多新的国家。 | division, split, partition |  union, merger | noun
