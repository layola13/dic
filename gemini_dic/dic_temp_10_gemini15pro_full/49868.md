demotivated:/diːˈmoʊtɪveɪtɪd/
adjective
失去动力的；泄气的
Having lost motivation; discouraged.
例句：The team felt demotivated after losing the final game. 球队在输掉决赛后感到很泄气。

例句：Constant criticism can be very demotivated for employees.  持续的批评会让员工非常泄气。


近义词：discouraged, disheartened, unmotivated, demoralized
反义词：motivated, inspired, enthusiastic
词性：adjective
