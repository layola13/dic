negotiates:/nɪˈɡoʊʃieɪts/ (US) /nɪˈɡəʊʃieɪts/ (UK)
verb
协商，谈判
To discuss something formally in order to make an agreement.
The government is negotiating with the unions to avoid a strike. 政府正在与工会谈判以避免罢工。
synonyms:  bargain, confer, discuss, mediate, parley
antonyms:  dictate, impose
词性: 动词
