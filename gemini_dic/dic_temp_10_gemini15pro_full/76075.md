furoin:/ˈfjʊərɔɪn/ |n.|糠偶姻；呋喃甲醇|A yellowish crystalline compound C10H8O4 formed by the action of alcoholic potassium cyanide on furfural.|Furoin is an intermediate in the synthesis of certain pharmaceuticals.|近义词: (No direct synonyms, possibly related compounds like furfural)|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology: From fur(fural) + -oin (suffix denoting a benzoin analog).  Furfural itself comes from Latin *furfur* (bran) + -al (aldehyde suffix).
