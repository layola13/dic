forehammer:/ˈfɔːrhæmər/| n.|前锤，初步破碎机|A heavy hammer used to crush ore before further processing.  A large hammer, often powered by water or steam, used in mining and metalworking to break large pieces of ore or metal into smaller pieces for further processing. |The forehammer pounded the rock into smaller pieces. 前锤把岩石捣碎成小块。|The old mining operation used a water-powered forehammer to crush the ore.  这个古老的采矿作业使用水力前锤来粉碎矿石。|Synonyms: hammer, crusher, pulverizer|Antonyms: N/A|noun
