on-the-shelf:/ˌɒn ðə ʃɛlf/ (UK), /ˌɑːn ðə ʃɛlf/ (US)| n.|现成的商品|Products that are readily available for purchase.|The bookstore had a limited selection of books on the shelf. 这家书店里现有的书籍种类有限。|They decided to buy an on-the-shelf component instead of building their own. 他们决定购买一个现成的组件，而不是自己制造。|近义词：stock, inventory|反义词:custom-made product|词性:名词
