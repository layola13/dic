eremitic:/ˌɛrəˈmɪtɪk/ | adj. |隐居的，遁世的|Characterized by solitude and seclusion, like a hermit. |He lived an eremitic life in the mountains, far from civilization. 他在远离尘嚣的山里过着隐居的生活。| 近义词: reclusive, solitary, secluded, ascetic | 反义词: gregarious, sociable | 词性: adjective

eremite:/ˈɛrəmaɪt/ | n. |隐士，遁世者|A person who lives in solitude, often for religious reasons. |The old hermit lived in a cave, surviving on berries and roots.  这位老隐士住在山洞里，靠浆果和树根生存。| 近义词: hermit, recluse, anchorite | 反义词: socialite | 词性: noun

词源: From Late Latin *eremiticus, from Greek erēmitēs "desert dweller," from erēmia "desert," from eremos "lonely, desolate."
