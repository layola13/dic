de-Americanizes: /diːəˈmɛrɪkənaɪzɪz/
verb
去美国化; 消除美国化影响
To cause to lose or shed American characteristics or influences.
The new government is trying to de-Americanize the country's culture. 新政府正试图去除该国文化的美国化影响。
The company is de-Americanizing its products to appeal to a global market.  该公司正在去除其产品的美国化特征，以吸引全球市场。
