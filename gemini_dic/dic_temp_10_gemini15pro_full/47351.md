de-stresses:/ˌdiːˈstrɛsɪz/
verb
减压，缓解压力
reduces stress or tension; helps someone to relax
Listening to music de-stresses me after a long day.  听音乐能让我在漫长的一天后放松下来。
She de-stresses by practicing yoga. 她通过练习瑜伽来减压。
synonyms: relaxes, calms, unwinds, soothes
antonyms: stresses, tenses, agitates, worries
