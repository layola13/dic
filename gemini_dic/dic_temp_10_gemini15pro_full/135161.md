nonoperative:/ˌnɒnˈɒpərətɪv/ /ˌnɑːnˈɑːpərətɪv/
adjective
非手术的；非操作的；无效的
Not involving or requiring surgery; not related to operation or functioning; not in effect.
The doctor recommended nonoperative treatment for his sprained ankle. 医生建议对他扭伤的脚踝进行非手术治疗。
His old passport is now nonoperative. 他的旧护照现在失效了。
近义词：nonsurgical, conservative, inactive, invalid
反义词：operative, surgical, active, valid
词性：adjective

Etymology:
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + operative (adjective meaning "functioning" or "involving surgery").  The word "operative" ultimately derives from the Latin "operari" meaning "to work."
