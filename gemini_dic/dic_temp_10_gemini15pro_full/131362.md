nonchronological:/ˌnɒnˌkrɒnəˈlɒdʒɪkl/ /ˌnɑːnˌkrɑːnəˈlɑːdʒɪkl/
adjective
非按时间顺序的
Not arranged in the order in which events happened.
The film uses a nonchronological narrative structure, jumping between different periods of the protagonist's life.  这部电影采用了非时间顺序的叙事结构，在主人公人生的不同时期之间跳跃。

The biography is presented in a nonchronological fashion, exploring different themes in his life.  这部传记以非时间顺序的方式呈现，探讨了他人生中不同的主题。


synonym: atemporal, asynchronous
antonym: chronological, sequential
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + chronological.  "Chronological" comes from the Greek "chronos" (time) and "logos" (word, study).
