directest:/dɪˈrɛktɪst/ /daɪˈrɛktɪst/
adj.
最直接的
superlative adjective of direct; most direct.
The directest route is often the fastest. 最直接的路线通常是最快的。
The directest way to get to the city center is by subway. 到市中心最直接的方式是乘地铁。

近义词:straightest, most direct, shortest
反义词:indirectest, longest, most roundabout
词性:形容词
