hyperflexions:/ˌhaɪpərˈflekʃənz/ | n. |过度屈曲，过度屈折| Excessive flexion of a limb or joint, beyond the normal range of motion.  It can also refer to excessive inflection in grammar, adding more grammatical markers than necessary.|

例句：

孩子的过度屈曲表明可能存在关节问题。


近义词：overflexion,  overbending|
反义词: hyperextension|
词性: noun


词源:  From "hyper-" (Greek: over, beyond) + "flexion" (Latin: flexio,  a bending).
