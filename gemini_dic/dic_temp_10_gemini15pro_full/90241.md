houseboat:/ˈhaʊsboʊt/ (US), /ˈhaʊsbəʊt/ (UK)| n.|船屋，水上住宅|A boat that is designed and equipped for use as a dwelling.|We spent a week on a houseboat, cruising down the river. 我们在船屋上待了一周，沿河而下。|近义词：barge, floating home|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑land house 陆上房屋)|noun
