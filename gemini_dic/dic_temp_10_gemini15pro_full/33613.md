cinchonism: /ˈsɪŋkənɪzəm/ | n. | 金鸡纳中毒；奎宁中毒 | A condition caused by excessive doses of cinchona bark or its alkaloids, especially quinine, characterized by symptoms such as tinnitus, headache, nausea, and disturbed vision. |  He experienced cinchonism after taking too much quinine for his malaria. 他在服用过量奎宁治疗疟疾后出现了金鸡纳中毒症状。 |  Quinine toxicity, quinism |  None | noun
