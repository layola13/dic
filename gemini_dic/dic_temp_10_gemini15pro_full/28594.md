caregivers:/ˈkerɡɪvərz/| n. |照顾者，护理员|A person who provides care for a child, an elderly person, or someone who is sick or disabled.|例句：The caregivers at the nursing home are very attentive to the residents' needs. 养老院的护理员非常关注居民的需求。

例句：Family caregivers often face emotional and physical challenges. 家庭护理员经常面临情感和身体上的挑战。|近义词：attendants, nurses, guardians, minders|反义词: ward, charge|Noun
