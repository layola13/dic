flutterless:/ˈflʌtərləs/
adjective
无振动的，稳定的
lacking flutter; steady.
The flutterless flight of the owl was impressive. 猫头鹰稳定无振动的飞行令人印象深刻。
Synonyms: steady, stable, smooth, unwavering
Antonyms: fluttering, shaky, unsteady, vibrating
词性: 形容词
