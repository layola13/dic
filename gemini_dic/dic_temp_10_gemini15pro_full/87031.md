hepatitises:/ˌhɛpətˈaɪtiːz/
|n.|肝炎（复数）|Inflammation of the liver, usually caused by a viral infection, but sometimes by exposure to toxic substances or autoimmune diseases.| Several different hepatitises are known, including hepatitis A, B, and C.  几种不同的肝炎是已知的，包括甲型肝炎、乙型肝炎和丙型肝炎。|Synonym: liver inflammation|Antonym: liver health
|noun|
