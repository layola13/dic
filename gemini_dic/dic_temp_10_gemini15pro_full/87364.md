heroism:/ˈhɛrəʊɪzəm/ (US) /ˈhɪərəʊɪzəm/ (UK)| n. |英雄主义；英勇行为|Great courage or strength shown in the face of adversity or danger. |例句：The firefighters showed incredible heroism in rescuing people from the burning building. 消防员在从燃烧的建筑物中救人时表现出了令人难以置信的英雄主义。

例句：Her heroism during the war earned her several medals.  她在战争中的英勇行为为她赢得了数枚奖章。|近义词：bravery, courage, valor, gallantry|反义词：cowardice, timidity|词性: noun
