globalizes:/ˈɡloʊbəˌlaɪzɪz/ /ˈɡləʊbəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
使全球化
To make something worldwide in scope or application.
The internet has globalized communication. 互联网使通讯全球化。
The company globalized its operations in the 1990s.  这家公司在20世纪90年代将其业务全球化。
Synonyms: internationalizes, universalizes, expands
Antonyms: localizes, restricts, confines
词性: 动词
