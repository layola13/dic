numberplate: /ˈnʌmbəpleɪt/ (British English) /ˈnʌmbərˌpleɪt/ (American English)| n. |车牌，牌照，汽车牌照 |A metal or plastic plate fixed to the front and rear of a motor vehicle, displaying its registration number. |例句：The police traced the car through its numberplate. 警方通过车牌追踪到了这辆车。

例句：He memorized the numberplate of the getaway car. 他记住了逃逸车辆的车牌号。

近义词：license plate (American English), registration plate |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
