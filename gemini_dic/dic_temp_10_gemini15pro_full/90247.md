housebote:/ˈhaʊsbɒt/| n. |木料采集权；房屋维修木材|The right of a tenant to take wood from the landlord's estate for the repair of their house. |The tenant exercised his right of housebote to repair the roof.  房客行使了采集木料的权利来修理屋顶。|Synonyms: estovers (especially for repairs), right of common|Antonyms: N/A|noun
