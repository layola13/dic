gourmet:/ˈɡʊərmeɪ/ (US), /ˈɡɔːmeɪ/ (UK)| n. adj.|美食家; 美味的|A connoisseur of good food; of high quality and often expensive food.|例句：He is a true gourmet. 他是一位真正的美食家。|近义词：epicure, connoisseur, foodie|反义词:glutton|n.

gourmet:/ˈɡʊərmeɪ/ (US), /ˈɡɔːmeɪ/ (UK)| adj.|美味的|Of high quality and often expensive food.|例句：We had a gourmet meal at the new restaurant. 我们在这家新餐厅享用了一顿美味佳肴。|近义词：delicious, delectable, exquisite|反义词:bland, tasteless|adj.
