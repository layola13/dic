gonidial:/ɡɒˈnɪdiəl/ /ɡəˈnaɪdiəl/| adj.|生殖的，芽殖的|Relating to or producing gonidia; reproductive.|Gonidial layer of lichen is crucial for its propagation. 地衣的生殖层对其繁殖至关重要.|近义词：reproductive, generative|反义词：vegetative, non-reproductive|词性：形容词

gonidium:/ɡəˈnɪdiəm/ /ɡɒˈnɪdiəm/| n.|生殖细胞，芽殖体|A specialized cell or group of cells that become detached to form a new individual, especially in algae, lichens, and some fungi.|The gonidia of the lichen were dispersed by the wind. 地衣的生殖细胞被风吹散了。|近义词：spore, propagule|反义词：-|词性：名词
