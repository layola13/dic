palmists:/ˈpɑːmɪsts/| n.| 手相家，掌纹家|A person who practices palmistry; someone who claims to be able to tell a person's future or character by examining the lines and other features of the palm of their hand.|Many palmists claim to be able to predict the future.|近义词：fortune teller, soothsayer, clairvoyant|反义词:skeptic|词性:noun
