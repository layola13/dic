hospitium:/hɒˈspɪtɪəm/ /hɑːˈspɪtɪəm/
n.
lodging, hospitality, entertainment
A place of lodging or shelter; hospitality; entertainment.
The monks offered hospitium to weary travelers. 修士们为疲惫的旅行者提供住宿。
The hospitium provided by the villagers was warm and welcoming. 村民提供的款待热情而友好。

近义词: shelter, accommodation, refuge, hospitality
反义词: inhospitality

词性: noun
