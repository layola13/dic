economizer: /iˈkɒnəmaɪzər/ (UK) /iˈkɑːnəmaɪzər/ (US) | n. | 节省器，节约器 | A device or system designed to reduce waste or consumption, especially of fuel or energy. |  The factory installed an economizer to reduce energy costs.  工厂安装了一个节能器以降低能源成本。| synonyms: saver, conserver, preserver | antonyms: waster, squanderer | noun
