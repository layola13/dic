Excessively elaborate or embellished language. | His writing suffers from overfloridnesses, making it difficult to understand the main points. 他的写作过于华丽，难以理解要点。|  The architect criticized the overfloridnesses of the building's design, finding it gaudy and tasteless.  建筑师批评了建筑设计过于华丽，认为它俗气且毫无品味。 | ornateness, flamboyance, elaborateness |simplicity, plainness | noun
