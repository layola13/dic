captivater:/ˈkæptɪveɪtər/| n.| 迷住人的人或事物 |Someone or something that captivates.|Her beauty was a captivater, drawing everyone's attention. 她的美令人着迷，吸引了所有人的目光。|近义词：charmer, enchanter, fascinator|反义词：bore, repellent| noun

captivate:/ˈkæptɪveɪt/| vt.| 迷住，使着迷 |To attract and hold the attention of someone by being interesting, pretty, etc.|The children were captivated by the magician's tricks.  孩子们被魔术师的戏法迷住了。|近义词：enchant, fascinate, charm, mesmerize|反义词：bore, repel, disgust| verb

词源: From Late Latin captivatus, past participle of captivare "to take captive," from Latin captivus "captive."
