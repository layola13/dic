fey:/feɪ/
adjective
给予的；超自然的；命中注定的；古怪的；心不在焉的；即将死亡的
giving an impression of vague unworldliness and slight unearthliness, as if belonging to another world; enchanted; magical; otherworldly; whimsical; absent-minded; doomed to die soon.

例句：
She had a fey, ethereal beauty.
她有一种奇异的、空灵的美。

He gave a fey smile.
他露出了一个古怪的笑容。


近义词：whimsical, ethereal, otherworldly, elfin, magical, enchanted, strange, eccentric
反义词：mundane, ordinary, practical, realistic

词性: adjective
