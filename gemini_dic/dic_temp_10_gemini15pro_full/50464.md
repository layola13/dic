depredatory:/ˈdɛprɪdəˌtɔːri/ (US), /ˈdɛprɪdətri/ (UK)| adj.|掠夺性的，破坏性的|Characterized by plundering, pillaging, or destructive raiding.|例句：The depredatory actions of the invading army left the city in ruins. 入侵军队的掠夺行为使这座城市成为废墟。

例句：The wolves' depredatory instincts led them to attack the farmer's sheep. 狼的掠夺性本能驱使它们攻击农民的羊。

近义词：predatory, plundering, pillaging, ravaging, destructive|反义词：protective, constructive, beneficial|词性：adjective

词源：From Late Latin *dēpraedātōrius*, from Latin dēpraedārī (“to plunder”), from de- (“from, down”) + praeda (“booty, prey”).
