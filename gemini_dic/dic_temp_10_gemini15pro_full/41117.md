coproduction:/ˌkəʊprəˈdʌkʃən/| n.| 联合制作；合作生产 |A joint production of a film, play, or other work by two or more production companies or countries. |例句：The film was a coproduction between France and Italy. 这部电影是法国和意大利的合拍片。

例句：This play is a coproduction between two renowned theatre companies.  这出戏是两个著名剧团的联合制作。|近义词：collaboration, joint production, joint venture|反义词: solo production, independent production|词性:noun
