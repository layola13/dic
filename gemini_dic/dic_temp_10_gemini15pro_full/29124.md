case:/keɪs/ /kæs/| n. |情况，案例，盒子，箱子|a particular situation or example of something; a container for storing or protecting something|例句：The lawyer presented a strong case. 律师提出了一个强有力的案例。|近义词：situation, circumstance, instance, box, container|反义词: |n.

case:/keɪs/ /kæs/| v. |仔细检查，掩盖|examine something carefully, typically in order to steal from it; cover|例句：The burglars cased the house before breaking in.  窃贼在破门而入之前仔细检查了房子。|近义词：examine, inspect, scrutinize, cover, enclose|反义词: |v.
