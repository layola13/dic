baud:/bɔːd/| n. | 波特 |A unit of signaling speed equal to the number of discrete signal events per second. One baud therefore corresponds to one bit per second in a train of alternating '1's and '0's, and to two bits per second in a train of four distinct states (e.g., 00, 01, 10, 11).|The modem operated at 14,400 bauds.  这个调制解调器以14,400波特的速度运行。|Synonym: signal rate|Antonym: N/A|noun
