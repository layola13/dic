misnarrating: /ˌmɪsnəˈreɪtɪŋ/
|v.|错误地叙述，歪曲叙述|Presenting a story or account inaccurately or misleadingly.|He was misnarrating the events of that night, leaving out crucial details. 他错误地叙述了那天晚上的事件，遗漏了关键的细节。|Synonyms: misrepresenting, distorting, falsifying, misreporting|Antonyms: accurately portraying, truthfully recounting|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (wrongly) + narrating (present participle of narrate, from Latin narrare "to tell," from gnarus "knowing").
