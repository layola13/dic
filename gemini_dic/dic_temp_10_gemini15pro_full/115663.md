mares:/mɛərz/| n. |母马（复数）|Plural form of mare, referring to adult female horses.|The mares grazed peacefully in the field. 母马们在田野里平静地吃草。|The farmer had several mares on his property.  这位农民在他的地产上养了几匹母马。|近义词：female horses|反义词:stallions, geldings|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Old English *mēre, from Proto-Germanic *marō, from Proto-Indo-European *márkos (“horse”). Cognate with Old Norse merr, Old Frisian mere, Old Saxon meri, Middle Dutch mere, Dutch merrie, Old High German meri, German Mähre (“mare”).
