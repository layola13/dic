affected:/əˈfɛktɪd/ | adj. | 做作的，不自然的；受影响的；假装的 |  influenced or touched by an external factor; artificial, pretentious, or unnatural in behavior | She had an affected way of speaking, always using overly formal language. 她说话的方式很做作，总是使用过于正式的语言。| synonyms: artificial, pretentious, contrived, unnatural, studied, assumed, feigned, phony, pseudo, bogus, insincere, theatrical, histrionic, melodramatic | antonyms: natural, genuine, sincere, unaffected, artless, unpretentious | adjective

affected:/əˈfɛktɪd/ | adj. | 受（疾病）侵袭的；患病的 | acted upon; diseased or infected |  The affected area of his skin was red and itchy. 他受感染的皮肤区域又红又痒。 | synonyms: afflicted, diseased, infected, unhealthy, ill | antonyms: healthy, well, unaffected | adjective
