orthomorphic:/ˌɔːrθəˈmɔːrfɪk/| adj.|正形的，保形的|Preserving shape; specifically in mathematics, relating to a mapping that preserves angles locally.|Conformal maps are orthomorphic, preserving the shape of infinitesimally small figures. 保形映射是正形的，保留了无穷小图形的形状。|The crystal structure exhibited orthomorphic properties. 晶体结构表现出正形特性。|synonyms: conformal, isogonal, shape-preserving|antonyms: non-conformal, distorted|adjective
