economising:/ɪˈkɒnəmaɪzɪŋ/ |v.|节省，节约|The act of saving money or using resources carefully.  |Example 1:  They are economising on fuel by driving less. 他们通过减少开车来节省燃料。

Example 2:  The company is economising by reducing staff. 公司正在通过裁员来节省开支。|近义词：saving, budgeting, conserving|反义词：spending, wasting|词性: 动名词 (gerund)


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "economise," which comes from the French "économiser" and ultimately from the Greek "oikonomia" meaning "household management."
