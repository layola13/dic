attestator:/əˈtestətər/
|n.|证明人，证人|A person who attests something; a witness.|The attestator confirmed the signature was genuine.  证明人确认签名是真实的。|synonyms: witness, verifier, corroborator|antonyms: denier, challenger|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English attestatour, from Old French attestateur, from Medieval Latin attestator, from Latin attestari "to bear witness," from ad- "to" + testari "to bear witness," from testis "witness."
