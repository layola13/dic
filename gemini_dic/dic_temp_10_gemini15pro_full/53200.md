diptych:/ˈdɪptɪk/| n. |双联画；双面板；双折书|A diptych is a work of art, especially a painting or carving, with two hinged panels which can be folded together. It can also refer to an ancient writing tablet consisting of two hinged leaves. |The museum displayed a beautiful diptych from the 15th century. 博物馆展出了一幅15世纪的精美双联画。|synonyms: twofold, double-panel, two-part|antonyms: single, monoptych|noun

diptych:/ˈdɪptɪk/| adj. |双联的|Having two hinged panels.|The diptych altarpiece was a masterpiece of the early Renaissance. 这幅双联祭坛画是早期文艺复兴时期的杰作。|synonyms: double-paneled, two-leaved|antonyms: single, monoptych|adjective
