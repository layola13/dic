nonsupplementary:/ˌnɒnˌsʌplɪˈmɛntəri/
|adj.|非补充的，不补充的|Not supplementary; not serving to supplement something else.|The nonsupplementary angles added up to 120 degrees.|近义词:nonadditional, extra, independent|反义词:supplementary, complementary|词性:形容词

词源:
non- (prefix): meaning "not"
supplementary (adjective): from supplement (verb), from Latin supplementum "a supply, filling up, reinforcement," from sub "up to" + plere "to fill."
