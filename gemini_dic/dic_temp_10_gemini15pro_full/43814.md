creole:/ˈkriːoʊl/ (US), /ˈkriːɒl/ (UK)| n. adj.|克里奥尔语；克里奥尔人；克里奥尔文化|A language that has developed from a mixture of different languages, especially a European language and the language of West Africa, and is used as a first language by a community. Also refers to a person of mixed European and black descent, especially in the Caribbean.|例句：The creole language spoken in Haiti is a blend of French and African languages. 海地人说的克里奥尔语是法语和非洲语言的混合体。

例句：She is proud of her Creole heritage. 她为自己的克里奥尔血统感到自豪。|近义词：pidgin (as a language), hybrid|反义词: (No direct antonym for the broad meaning of creole)|词性: noun, adjective
