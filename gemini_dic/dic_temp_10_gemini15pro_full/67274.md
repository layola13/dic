exquisitely:/ɪkˈskwɪzɪtli/ | adv. | 精致地；精巧地；优美地；剧烈地 | In a very beautiful and delicate way; to a high degree of excellence; intensely. | The cake was exquisitely decorated with delicate icing flowers. 蛋糕用精致的糖霜花装饰得非常漂亮。| The pain was exquisitely sharp. 疼痛剧烈而尖锐。 | 近义词: beautifully, delicately, finely, perfectly, superbly | 反义词: crudely, roughly, coarsely | 词性: 副词

exquisite:/ɪkˈskwɪzɪt/ | adj. | 精致的；精美的；剧烈的；敏感的 | Extremely beautiful and delicate; intensely felt. | She has exquisite taste in clothes. 她对服装有极高的品味。| He suffered exquisite pain. 他遭受了剧烈的疼痛。 | 近义词: beautiful, delicate, fine, perfect, superb | 反义词: crude, rough, coarse | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin exquisitus ‘carefully sought out’, past participle of exquirere, from ex- ‘out’ + quaerere ‘seek’.
