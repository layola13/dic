magisterial:/ˌmædʒɪˈstɪəriəl/ | adj. |权威的；威严的；专横的 | Having or showing great authority. | He delivered a magisterial performance. 他奉献了一场精彩绝伦的演出。|  Her magisterial presence commanded respect. 她威严的仪态令人肃然起敬。| 近义词: authoritative, commanding, imperious, dictatorial | 反义词: humble, submissive, meekly | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Late Latin *magisterialis*, from Latin magister (“master, teacher”) +‎ -alis.  最终源自拉丁语 magister（意为“大师，老师”）。
