frailness:/ˈfreɪlnəs/| n.|脆弱，虚弱|The frailness of the elderly woman was apparent in her slow, shuffling gait.  这位老妇人的虚弱在她缓慢的、拖着脚的步态中显而易见。|例句：The frailness of the old bridge made it unsafe. 这座老桥的脆弱使它变得不安全。|近义词：fragility, weakness, debility|反义词:strength, robustness|词性:noun
词源:From Middle English *frele + -ness, from Old French fraileté, from fraile (“frail”) + -té (“-ty”), from Latin fragilitās (“fragility”), from fragilis (“fragile”).
