neon:/ˈniːɒn/ (UK) /ˈniːɑːn/ (US)| n. |氖|A chemical element that is a gas at room temperature. It is colorless and odorless and is used in electric signs, fluorescent lamps, and some kinds of lasers.|The neon sign glowed brightly in the night. 霓虹灯在夜里发出明亮的光芒。|近义词: (As a light source) argon, krypton, xenon|反义词: None (It's a unique element)|词性:noun
