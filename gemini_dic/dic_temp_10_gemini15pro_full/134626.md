nonmagnetically:/ˌnɒn.mæɡˈnet.ɪ.kəl.i/
adv.
非磁性地
in a way that is not magnetic; without magnetic properties
The watch was designed nonmagnetically to prevent interference with sensitive equipment. 这款手表采用非磁性设计，以防止干扰敏感设备。
Near synonyms: non-magnetically (variant spelling)
Antonyms: magnetically
词性: 副词
