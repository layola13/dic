dermatopathic:/ˌdɜːrmətəˈpæθɪk/ | adj. | 皮肤病的；由皮肤病引起的 | Relating to or caused by skin disease.  | Example 1: Dermatopathic lymphadenopathy is a condition where lymph nodes become enlarged due to skin disease. 皮肤病性淋巴结病是一种由于皮肤病引起的淋巴结肿大的疾病。

Example 2:  He experienced dermatopathic changes after a severe allergic reaction.  他在严重的过敏反应后经历了皮肤病性变化。 | Synonyms: cutaneous, dermal | Antonyms: healthy (referring to skin) | Adjective
