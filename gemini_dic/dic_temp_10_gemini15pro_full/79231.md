glaringly:/ˈɡlɛərɪŋli/| adv.|耀眼地；明显地；显而易见地|In a way that is very obvious and easily noticed, often in a negative or critical way.  It suggests something is so obvious that it cannot be ignored, often implying a fault or problem.|The report glaringly omitted any mention of the financial implications.  这份报告明显地忽略了任何财务方面的影响。|The sun was glaringly bright. 太阳耀眼地明亮。| synonym: obviously, conspicuously, strikingly, blatantly, patently| antonym: subtly, inconspicuously, discreetly|词性:副词
