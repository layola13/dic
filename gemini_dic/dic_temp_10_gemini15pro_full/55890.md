divorce: /dɪˈvɔːrs/ (US), /dɪˈvɔːs/ (UK)| n. v. |离婚，分离|The legal dissolution of a marriage.  As a verb, it means to legally dissolve a marriage.|例句：They filed for divorce last year. 他们去年申请了离婚。|近义词：separation, annulment|反义词：marriage, union|词性: 名词, 动词
divorce: /dɪˈvɔːrs/ (US), /dɪˈvɔːs/ (UK)| v. |与...离婚，脱离|To legally separate from one's spouse.  Can also mean to separate or disunite.|例句：She divorced him after 20 years of marriage. 她和他结婚20年后离婚了。|近义词：separate, split|反义词：marry, unite|词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *divorcer*, from Latin *divortium* "separation, divorce," from *divertere* "to turn aside, go different ways," from *dis-* "apart" + *vertere* "to turn."
