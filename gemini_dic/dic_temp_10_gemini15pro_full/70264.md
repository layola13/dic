fictor:/ˈfɪktər/| n.|虚构者；创作人；小说家|A person who invents or creates something, especially stories or fictional characters.  A writer of fiction.|例句：The fictor skillfully crafted a world of magic and wonder.  这位小说家巧妙地创造了一个充满魔法和奇迹的世界。

例句：As a fictor, she has a vivid imagination and a talent for storytelling. 作为一名小说家，她拥有丰富的想象力和讲故事的天赋。|近义词：author, writer, novelist, storyteller, inventor, creator|反义词：N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fictor*, from *fingere* "to shape, fashion, feign," from Proto-Italic *feik-, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰeyǵʰ- "to form, build."
