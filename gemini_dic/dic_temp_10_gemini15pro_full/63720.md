ephippial:/ɛˈfɪpiəl/| adj.| 卵鞍形的，（水蚤）具耐久卵的|Relating to or resembling an ephippium; carrying or possessing an ephippium.  An ephippium is a thickened, chitinous saddle-like structure on the dorsal side of a water flea (Cladocera) that protects the winter eggs.|The ephippial eggs are resistant to harsh conditions.|近义词:resting, dormant|反义词:active, developing|词性:形容词

词源: From Greek *ephippion* "saddle-cloth," diminutive of *ephippos* "on a horse," from *epi* "on" + *hippos* "horse."
