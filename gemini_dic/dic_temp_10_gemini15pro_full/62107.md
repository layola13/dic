enchantress:/ɪnˈtʃæntrɪs/ | n. | 女巫；迷人的女人 | A woman who uses magic or sorcery; a fascinating or alluring woman. |例句：The enchantress cast a spell on the prince.  女巫对王子施了咒语。

例句：She was a true enchantress, captivating everyone in the room. 她真是个迷人的女人，吸引了房间里所有人的目光。| 近义词：sorceress, witch, charmer, siren | 反义词: bore | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Old French enchanteresse, feminine of enchanteor (“enchanter”), from enchanter (“to enchant”), from in- (“in, into”) + cantare (“to sing”).  Ultimately from Latin cantare (“to sing”).
