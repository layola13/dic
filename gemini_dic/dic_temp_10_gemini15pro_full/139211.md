objectify: /əbˈdʒɛktɪfaɪ/ /ɑːbˈdʒɛktɪfaɪ/ | vt. | 使…客观化；使…物化；将…视为物体 | To treat a person as an object, especially sexually, without regard to their personality or dignity. |  She felt objectified by the way men stared at her on the street. 她觉得男人在街上盯着她的方式让她感到被物化了。
