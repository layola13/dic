colugo:/kəˈluːɡoʊ/ (US), /kəˈluːɡəʊ/ (UK)| n.|猫猴，鼯猴|A mammal that lives in trees and can glide through the air using a membrane of skin.|Researchers studied the colugo's gliding technique. 研究人员研究了猫猴的滑翔技术。|近义词：flying lemur, dermoptera|反义词: N/A|noun
