over-egg:/ˌoʊvərˈeɡ/
v. |煮过头,做得过火|To cook an egg for too long, resulting in a hard yolk and sometimes a rubbery white.  To overdo or exaggerate something. | I over-egged the pudding and it came out too sweet. 我把布丁做得太甜了。 He tends to over-egg his stories to make them sound more interesting. 他倾向于夸大他的故事，让它们听起来更有趣。|overcook, overdo|undercook, understate|动词

over-egg:/ˌoʊvərˈeɡ/
n. |煮过头的蛋,过分的事|An egg that has been cooked for too long. Something that has been overdone or exaggerated.|The over-egg was rubbery and unpleasant to eat. 这个煮过头的鸡蛋很橡胶状，很难吃。 His constant boasting about his achievements was a bit of an over-egg. 他不断吹嘘自己的成就有点过分了。|overcooked egg, exaggeration|understatement|名词
