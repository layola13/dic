interoperably:/ˌɪntərˈɒpərəbli/ | adv. | 可互操作地 | In a way that allows different systems or components to work together seamlessly. |  Different software programs can communicate interoperably, exchanging data without difficulty. 不同的软件程序可以互操作地通信，轻松地交换数据。

The new system is designed to function interoperably with existing infrastructure. 新系统的设计使其能够与现有基础设施互操作。| 近义词: compatibly, portably | 反义词: incompatibly | 词性: 副词
