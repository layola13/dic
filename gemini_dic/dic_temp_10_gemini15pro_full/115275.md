mankier:/ˈmæŋkiər/| adj.|更破旧的，更糟糕的|More run-down, shabbier, or in worse condition than something already considered manky.|The old sofa looked even mankier after the cat shredded it.|近义词：shabbier, tattier, more dilapidated|反义词：pristine, immaculate, better|词性:形容词
词源: manky (adj.) + -er (comparative suffix).  "Manky" is of uncertain origin, possibly related to Romani "mangy" (shabby, mean) or influenced by "mucky" (dirty).
