druggy's:/ˈdrʌɡiz/
noun
吸毒者的（东西）
belonging to or associated with a drug addict.
例句：The police found drug paraphernalia in the druggy's apartment. 警察在吸毒者的公寓里发现了吸毒用具。

例句：The druggy's behavior was erratic and unpredictable. 吸毒者的行为古怪且难以预测。

近义词：addict's, user's
反义词:n/a
词性:noun (possessive form)


ETYMOLOGY->
From druggy + 's (possessive suffix).  "Druggy" itself comes from "drug" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives). "Drug" ultimately traces back to Old French "drogue" of uncertain origin.
