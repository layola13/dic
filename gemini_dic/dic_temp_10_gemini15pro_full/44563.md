croton: /ˈkroʊtn/ (US), /ˈkrəʊtn/ (UK)| n. |巴豆属植物；巴豆油|A tropical shrub or tree of the spurge family, many kinds of which are cultivated as ornamentals for their brightly colored foliage.  The seeds of some species yield croton oil, a purgative.|The croton in her garden had vibrant red and yellow leaves. 她花园里的巴豆叶子红黄相间，充满活力。|Synonyms: Codiaeum variegatum, Joseph's coat|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

croton oil: /ˈkroʊtn ɔɪl/ (US), /ˈkrəʊtn ɔɪl/ (UK)| n. |巴豆油|A viscous, brownish-yellow oil obtained from the seeds of the purging croton, used as a powerful purgative.|Croton oil was historically used as a laxative but is now rarely used due to its toxicity. 巴豆油过去曾用作泻药，但现在由于其毒性而很少使用。|Synonyms:  N/A|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
