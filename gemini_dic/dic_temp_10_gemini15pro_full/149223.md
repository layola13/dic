oxymuriatic:/ˌɒksɪmjʊəriˈætɪk/| adj.|氧化氯的；含氧化氯的|An obsolete term for chlorine, based on the then-incorrect belief that it was a compound of oxygen and hydrochloric acid (muriatic acid).|例句：Chemists used to refer to chlorine as oxymuriatic acid. 化学家过去把氯叫做氧化 muriatic 酸。

例句：The oxymuriatic process was once used for bleaching textiles. 氧化氯工艺曾用于漂白纺织品。|近义词：chlorine|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective
