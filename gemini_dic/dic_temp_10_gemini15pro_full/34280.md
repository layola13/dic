clarencieux:/ˈklærənʃuː/
n.
首席纹章官；克拉伦舍纹章官
The chief herald of arms on the south side of the River Trent in England.
The Clarencieux King of Arms granted the college its coat of arms in 1576. 克拉伦舍纹章官于1576年授予学院盾形纹章。
近义词:herald
反义词:N/A
词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *Clarenceau, ultimately derived from the honor of Clare, Suffolk, England.
