beerinesses:/ˈbɪərinəsɪz/
noun
啤酒味; 似啤酒的味道; 啤酒般的性质
The quality or condition of being beery.  The plural of beeriness.
The beerinesses of the various craft brews were distinct.  各种精酿啤酒的啤酒味都很独特。
Near Synonyms: beery flavors, beery qualities, beer-like tastes
Antonyms: blandness, flavorlessness
词性: 名词
