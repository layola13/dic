escharotic:/ˌɛskəˈrɒtɪk/
adjective
腐蚀性的；烧灼性的
Causing the death of tissue by burning or corrosion.
The doctor applied an escharotic substance to remove the wart. 医生使用了一种腐蚀性物质来去除疣。
Synonyms: caustic, corrosive, mordant
Antonyms: healing, soothing
词性: 形容词


escharotic:/ˌɛskəˈrɒtɪk/
noun
腐蚀剂；烧灼剂
A substance that causes the death of tissue by burning or corrosion.
Silver nitrate is a commonly used escharotic. 硝酸银是一种常用的腐蚀剂。
Synonyms: caustic, corrosive
Antonyms: balm, emollient
词性: 名词
