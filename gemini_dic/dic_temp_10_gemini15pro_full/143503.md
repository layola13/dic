outglowing: /ˌaʊtˈɡloʊɪŋ/
adjective
发出更强光芒的；胜过…的光辉的
Surpassing in brightness or radiance.
The outglowing light of the rising sun painted the sky with vibrant hues.  冉冉升起的太阳的光芒胜过一切，将天空染上了鲜艳的色彩。
Her beauty was outglowing, radiating warmth and kindness. 她的美胜过一切，散发着温暖和善良。
近义词: radiant, shining, brilliant, dazzling
反义词: dim, dull, faint
词性: adjective

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning surpassing or exceeding) + "glow" (verb meaning to emit a steady light)  由表示“超过”或“胜过”的前缀"out-"和动词"glow"（发出稳定的光）组合而成。
