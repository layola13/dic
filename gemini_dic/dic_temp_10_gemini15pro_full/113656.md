The organization or structure of something. |The factory invested in new machinery to increase production.  这家工厂投资了新机器以提高产量。|synonyms: apparatus, equipment, mechanism, tackle, gear|antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a concrete noun like machinery. One might consider "manual labor" or "simplicity" as contrasting concepts.)|noun
