paranotal:/ˌpærəˈnəʊtl/| adj.|侧背甲的，位于侧背甲附近的|Relating to or situated near the paranotum, a lateral expansion of the notum in some insects.|The paranotal lobes in some extinct insect groups may have been precursors to wings.|近义词:alary, pteral|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly 'median' or 'central' depending on context)|词性:adjective
