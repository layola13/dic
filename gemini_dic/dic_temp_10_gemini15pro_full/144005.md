outreasoned:/ˌaʊtˈriːzənd/
verb
以理服人，驳倒
persuade someone with reason or logic
He outreasoned his opponents in the debate. 他在辩论中以理服人了他的对手。
近义词: outargue, defeat, convince
反义词: lose to, be persuaded by
词性: verb

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "surpassing, exceeding") + "reason" (verb meaning "to think or argue logically")
