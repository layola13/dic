multibranched:/ˌmʌltiˈbrɑːntʃt/ | adj. | 多分支的，多枝的 | Having many branches; intricately branched. |  The multibranched tree provided ample shade.  这棵多枝的树提供了充足的阴凉。 | The multibranched coral reef was teeming with life.  这个多枝的珊瑚礁充满了生机。 | 近义词: ramified, branched, multi-branched | 反义词: unbranched, single-stemmed | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
multi- (prefix): from Latin multus "much, many"
branched (adjective): from branch (noun), ultimately from Vulgar Latin *branca, of uncertain origin.
