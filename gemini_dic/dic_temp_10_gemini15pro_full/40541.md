controversy:/ˈkɒntrəvɜːsi/ /ˈkɑːntrəvɜːrsi/| n.|争议，争论|Disagreement, typically when prolonged, public, and heated.|The new policy sparked a lot of controversy.|Synonym:dispute, debate, argument|Antonym: agreement, harmony, accord|noun
