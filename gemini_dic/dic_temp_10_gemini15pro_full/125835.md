murrelet: /ˈmɜːrlɪt/ (US), /ˈmʌrələt/ (UK) | n. | 海雀 | A small diving seabird of the auk family, found in the northern Pacific Ocean. |  Example: The marbled murrelet nests in old-growth forests.  大理石纹海雀在古老的森林里筑巢。

Example: Murrelets are excellent swimmers and divers. 海雀是优秀的游泳健将和潜水员。 | Synonym: auklet, guillemot | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a specific bird species) | noun
