fileting:/fɪˈleɪtɪŋ/
|v.| 剔除(鱼)骨，做成鱼片|He is fileting the fish for dinner. 他正在为晚餐剔鱼骨。|She fileted the cod and then pan-fried it. 她将鳕鱼剔骨后用平底锅煎。|近义词: filleting, boning|反义词: N/A|动词


词源:  From the French word "fileter," meaning "to fillet."  Ultimately from the Latin "filum," meaning "thread."  The idea is that the bones are removed like threads from the fish.
