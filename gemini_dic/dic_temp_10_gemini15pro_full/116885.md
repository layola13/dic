maybird:/ˈmeɪbɜːrd/ | n. | 五月鸟 (指欧洲的一种鸣禽，学名whimbrel，以其在五月鸣叫而得名) | A wading bird (Numenius phaeopus) related to the curlew, known for its distinctive call, often heard in May. | Example: The maybird's call echoed across the moors. 五月鸟的叫声回荡在荒野上。| Example:  We heard the distinctive cry of a maybird as we walked along the coast. 我们沿着海岸走的时候，听到了五月鸟独特的叫声。| 近义词: whimbrel, curlew | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑其他鸟类) | 词性: 名词

词源:
From "May" + "bird," referring to the time of year when its call is most often heard.
