industrialises:/ɪnˈdʌstriəlaɪzɪz/
verb
工业化
To convert (a country or region) to an industrial economy.  To develop industries in (a country or region) on a wide scale.
Many developing countries are rapidly industrialising. 许多发展中国家正在迅速工业化。
China industrialised rapidly in the late 20th century.  中国在20世纪后期迅速工业化。
synonyms: develop, modernize, mechanize
antonyms: deindustrialize, ruralize
词性:动词
