nonpreparation:/ˌnɒnprɛpəˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 无准备，缺乏准备 | Lack of preparation; the state of being unprepared. |  His nonpreparation for the exam resulted in a failing grade.  他没有为考试做好准备，结果不及格。

| Example: The team's nonpreparation was evident in their poor performance.  该队的缺乏准备在他们糟糕的表现中显而易见。 | Synonyms: unpreparedness, lack of preparation | Antonyms: preparedness, readiness | noun
