mass-producing:/ˌmæs prəˈdjuːsɪŋ/ | adj. | 大量生产的 | Producing something in large quantities, typically by using machinery. |  The factory is mass-producing cars. 工厂正在大量生产汽车。|  The company is mass-producing low-cost clothing. 这家公司正在大量生产低成本的服装。 | 近义词: mass manufacturing, churning out | 反义词: handcrafting, custom-making | 词性:  adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From "mass" (meaning large quantity) + "producing" (present participle of "produce," from Latin producere "to lead forth, bring out," from pro- "forth" + ducere "to lead").
