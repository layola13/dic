biomimicry:/ˌbaɪoʊˈmɪmɪkri/| n. |仿生学|The design and production of materials, structures, and systems that are modeled on biological entities and processes.|例句：Biomimicry has led to the development of new adhesives inspired by gecko feet.  仿生学促成了受壁虎脚启发的新型粘合剂的开发。

例句：Researchers are exploring biomimicry to create more sustainable building materials. 研究人员正在探索仿生学以创造更可持续的建筑材料。|近义词：biomimetics, bionics|反义词:|名词
