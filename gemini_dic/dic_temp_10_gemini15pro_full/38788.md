conducting:/kənˈdʌktɪŋ/ | adj. | 传导的；指挥的；引导的 |  having the quality of transmitting something (such as electricity or heat); directing the performance of a musical group | The conducting wire carried the electrical current.  导线传输电流。| synonyms: transmitting, conveying, leading, directing | antonyms: insulating, blocking, hindering | adjective

conducting:/kənˈdʌktɪŋ/ | n. |  传导；指挥；引导 | the act or process of transmitting something (such as electricity or heat); the act of directing the performance of a musical group |  The conducting of the orchestra was masterful.  乐队的指挥非常出色。| synonyms: transmission, conveyance, leadership, direction | antonyms: insulation, blockage, hindrance | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "conductus" (past participle of "conducere" - to lead together), from "com-" (together) + "ducere" (to lead).
