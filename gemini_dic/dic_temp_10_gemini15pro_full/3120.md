aerugo:/ɪˈruːɡoʊ/
n.
绿锈，铜绿
a green film formed on copper or bronze by oxidation.
The ancient bronze statue was covered in a layer of aerugo. 这座古老的铜像覆盖着一层铜绿。
The aerugo on the old coins made them difficult to identify. 旧硬币上的铜绿使它们难以识别。
patina, verdigris
rust
noun
