inconcealable:/ˌɪnkənˈsiːləbl/
adjective
无法隐藏的；显而易见的
Not able to be hidden or disguised.
His guilt was inconcealable. 他的罪行无法掩盖。
近义词: obvious, evident, manifest, patent, unmistakable, apparent
反义词: concealable, hidden, disguised, secret
