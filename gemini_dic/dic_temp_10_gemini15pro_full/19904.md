bins:/bɪnz/| n.|箱子，容器，储藏室|Large plastic bins are useful for storage.  大的塑料箱对储存很有用。|The supermarket has bins for recycling glass, plastic, and paper. 超市有回收玻璃、塑料和纸张的箱子。|containers, boxes, receptacles| |noun|

Etymology->
From Middle English *binnes*, plural of *binne*, from Old English *binn*, *binne* (“crib, manger, stall”), from Proto-Germanic *binnō* (“basket, crib”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰendʰ- (“to bind”). Cognate with Dutch *ben*, *bin* (“basket, hamper, bin”), German *Benne* (“skip, dumpster, tub”).
