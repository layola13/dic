ombre:/ˈɒmbreɪ/ (UK) /ˈɑːmbreɪ/ (US)| adj. n.|渐变的，晕染的；渐变色|A color that shades gradually from light to dark.|Her hair was styled in a fashionable ombre, going from dark brown at the roots to a light blonde at the tips. 她的头发被染成了时髦的渐变色，从发根的深棕色到发梢的浅金色。|近义词：gradient, shaded, dip-dyed|反义词: solid, uniform|词性:形容词, 名词

Etymology: French, from Old French *umbre, from Latin umbra "shade, shadow."
