filical:/ˈfɪlɪkl/| adj.| 孝顺的；子女的；蕨类的|Of or relating to a son or daughter; befitting a son or daughter. Also, of or relating to ferns.|例句：He showed filical obedience to his parents. 他对父母表现出孝顺的服从。

例句：Filical ferns covered the forest floor.  蕨类植物覆盖了森林地面。|近义词：dutiful, obedient, respectful, fernlike|反义词：disobedient, disrespectful|词性：形容词

词源: From Late Latin *filicalis, from Latin filius (“son”) + -alis.  The sense relating to ferns arose from the resemblance of fern fronds to a stylized bird's foot, which was in turn associated with the claws of an eagle, a symbol of royal power and hence, by extension, of paternal authority.
