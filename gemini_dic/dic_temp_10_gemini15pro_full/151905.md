pasteboardy:/ˈpeɪstbɔːrdi/
adjective
似纸板的；脆弱的
Resembling pasteboard; flimsy or unsubstantial.
The old book felt pasteboardy and brittle. 这本旧书摸起来像纸板一样，很脆。
近义词:flimsy, brittle, cardboardy, insubstantial
反义词: sturdy, robust, strong, durable
词性:adjective

pasteboardy:/ˈpeɪstbɔːrdi/
adjective
假的；不真诚的
Artificial; insincere or superficial.  (Less common usage)
His pasteboardy smile didn't fool anyone.  他那虚假的笑容骗不了任何人。
近义词: artificial, fake, insincere, superficial
反义词: genuine, sincere, authentic, real
词性:adjective
