counterstyles:/ˈkaʊntərstʌɪlz/
noun
对立风格；相反的风格
Styles that are markedly different from or opposed to another, prevailing style.
The architect incorporated counterstyles from different eras in his design.  这位建筑师在他的设计中融合了不同时代的对立风格。
synonyms: contrasting styles, opposing styles, alternative styles, divergent styles
antonyms: similar styles, uniform styles, consistent styles, harmonious styles
