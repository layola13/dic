incense:/ˈɪnsɛns/ (US), /ˈɪnsɛns/ (UK)| n. vt.|香，香气；激怒|A substance that produces a fragrant odor when burned.  To make someone very angry.|例句：The incense filled the temple with a sweet aroma.  香的气味弥漫了整个寺庙。


例句：The constant interruptions incensed her.  不断的打扰激怒了她。

近义词：(n.) aroma, fragrance, perfume; (v.) enrage, infuriate, anger|反义词:(v.) placate, soothe, calm|词性:名词,动词
