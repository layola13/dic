acetone:/ˈæsɪtoʊn/ (US) /ˈæsɪtəʊn/ (UK)| n.|丙酮|A colorless, volatile, flammable liquid ketone, (CH₃)₂CO, used as a solvent.|Nail polish remover often contains acetone. 洗甲水通常含有丙酮。

Acetone is used in the manufacture of many chemicals. 丙酮用于制造许多化学品。

|近义词:dimethyl ketone (二甲基酮，化学名称), solvent（溶剂，功能上的近义词）|反义词: None (没有直接的反义词)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Aceton*, from *Aketyl* (acetyl) + *-on* (ketone suffix). Ultimately from Latin *acetum* ("vinegar").  源自德语 *Aceton*，由 *Aketyl*（乙酰基）+ *-on*（酮后缀）组成。最终源自拉丁语 *acetum*（“醋”）。
