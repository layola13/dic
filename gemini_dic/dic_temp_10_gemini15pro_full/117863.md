melodica:/məˈlɒdɪkə/ (UK) /məˈlɑːdɪkə/ (US)| n.|簧管，吹奏键盘乐器|A wind instrument, similar to a harmonica but with a keyboard. Air is blown into a mouthpiece, and notes are produced by pressing keys on the keyboard.|He played a lively tune on his melodica. 他用他的簧管演奏了一首活泼的曲子。|近义词：keyboard harmonica, blow-organ|反义词: N/A|noun
