despoiling:/dɪˈspɔɪlɪŋ/
verb
掠夺，破坏，蹂躏
The act of damaging or ruining something, especially by stealing something valuable from it.
The invaders despoiled the countryside, burning farms and stealing livestock. 侵略者蹂躏了乡村，烧毁了农场，偷走了牲畜。
synonyms: plundering, pillaging, looting, ravaging
antonyms: preserving, protecting, restoring
