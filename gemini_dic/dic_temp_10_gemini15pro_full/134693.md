nonmasculinely:/ˌnɒnˈmæskjʊlɪnli/
adverb
非男性化地，不像男人的样子
in a way that is not considered typical of men.
He moved nonmasculinely, with a gentle sway. 他动作举止不阳刚，带有一种轻柔的摇摆。
Synonym: effeminately, unmanly
Antonym:masculinely, mannishly
词性: 副词
