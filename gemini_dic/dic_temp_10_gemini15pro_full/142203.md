ornithichnite:/ˌɔːrnɪˈθɪknaɪt/| n. |鸟迹，鸟足印|A fossilized footprint made by a bird.  It refers specifically to the impression left by a bird's foot, not other traces like feathers or nests.|例句：Researchers discovered a rare ornithichnite in the Jurassic rock formation. 研究人员在侏罗纪岩层中发现了一个罕见的鸟迹。

例句：The ornithichnite provided valuable evidence about the early evolution of birds.  这个鸟迹为鸟类的早期进化提供了宝贵的证据。|近义词：fossil footprint, track, imprint|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "modern bird footprint" for contrast)|词性:noun
