hastener:/ˈheɪsənər/| n.|促进者，催促者|Something that hastens a process or event.  A person who urges or encourages quick action.|The catalyst was a hastener in the chemical reaction. 这种催化剂加速了化学反应。|Synonyms: accelerator, expediter, promoter|Antonyms: delayer, hinderer, retarder|noun
