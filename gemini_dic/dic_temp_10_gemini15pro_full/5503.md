alpaca:/ælˈpækə/ | n. | 羊驼 |A domesticated South American mammal with long, soft fleece, closely related to the llama.  It is smaller than the llama and has a finer, denser fleece. |  Example 1: The alpaca's soft fleece is highly prized. 羊驼的柔软毛被非常珍贵。

Example 2:  We saw a herd of alpacas grazing in the Andes. 我们看到一群羊驼在安第斯山脉吃草。 | Synonym: llama, vicuña | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for an animal) | noun
