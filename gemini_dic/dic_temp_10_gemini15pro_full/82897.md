gum:/ɡʌm/ | n.  | 口香糖；树胶；牙龈；黏性物质 | A sticky substance, often chewed.  Derived from  Late Old English *gōma, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch gom and German Gummi. | I like chewing gum. 我喜欢嚼口香糖。| synonyms: chewing gum, resin, adhesive | antonyms: none | noun

gum:/ɡʌm/ | v. | 用胶粘；使…胶合；涂以树胶；分泌树胶 | To apply gum to something; to become sticky. |  He gummed the label onto the package. 他把标签粘到包裹上。| synonyms: stick, glue, paste | antonyms: unstick, unglue | verb
