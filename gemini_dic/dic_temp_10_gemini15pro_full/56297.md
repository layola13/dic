doggone:/dɒˈɡɒn/ (UK) /dɑːˈɡɔːn/ (US)| adj. int.|该死的，讨厌的；非常，极其|A mild oath or exclamation expressing surprise, annoyance, or emphasis.  It's a euphemism for "goddamn".|Doggone it! I forgot my keys!  该死的！我忘了带钥匙了！|Doggone, that pie is good!  天哪，那个派真好吃！|Synonyms: darn, blasted, confounded|Antonyms: blessed, delightful|adjective, interjection

doggone:/dɒˈɡɒn/ (UK) /dɑːˈɡɔːn/ (US)| adv.|非常，极其|Used as an intensifier.|That's a doggone good idea! 那真是个好主意！|It's doggone hot today. 今天真热。|Synonyms: extremely, very, awfully|Antonyms: slightly, barely|adverb
