dialectally:/ˌdaɪəˈlɛktəli/
adv.
方言地，用方言
Relating to dialects; in dialect form.
She spoke dialectally, using phrases common only in her region. 她用方言说话，使用一些只在她那个地区常见的短语。
Near Synonyms: regionally, provincially, locally
Antonyms: formally, standardly, nationally
PARTS OF SPEECH-> adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From dialect + -ally.  Dialect from Late Latin dialectus "local speech," from Greek dialektos "conversation, talk, language of a district," from dialegesthai "converse with each other," from dia- "across, between" + legein "to speak."
