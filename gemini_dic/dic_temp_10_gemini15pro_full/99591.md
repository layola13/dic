interinvolved:/ˌɪntərɪnˈvɒlvd/ | adj. |相互关联的，互相牵涉的 | Mutually or reciprocally involved; entangled or implicated with one another. | The two companies are interinvolved in a complex legal battle. 这两家公司相互卷入了一场复杂的法律纠纷。| The countries are interinvolved in a trade agreement. 这些国家互相参与了一项贸易协定。 |  近义词: intertwined, interconnected, interrelated | 反义词: separate, independent | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "inter-" (between, among) + "involved" (participating in or affected by something)
