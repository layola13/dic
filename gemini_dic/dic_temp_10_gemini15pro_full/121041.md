misconduct:/ˌmɪsˈkɒndʌkt/ (US) /ˌmɪsˈkɑːndʌkt/ (UK)| n.  | 不端行为，失职 | Improper or unprofessional behavior, especially by a person in a position of authority or responsibility. | The lawyer was accused of misconduct for accepting a bribe.  这名律师因收受贿赂而被指控行为不端。| synonyms: wrongdoing, impropriety, misbehavior, delinquency, malpractice | antonyms:  good conduct, propriety, rectitude | noun

misconduct:/ˌmɪsˈkɒndʌkt/ (US) /ˌmɪsˈkɑːndʌkt/ (UK)| vt. |  处理不当，行为不检 | To manage or behave oneself badly or improperly.  |  He misconducted himself during the trial and was reprimanded by the judge.  他在审判期间行为不检，受到了法官的斥责。 | synonyms: mismanage, mishandle | antonyms: manage well, conduct properly | verb
