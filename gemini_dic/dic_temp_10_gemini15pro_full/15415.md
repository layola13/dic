bactericidal:/ˌbækˈtɪrɪsaɪdəl/
adjective
杀菌的
Capable of destroying bacteria.
This new disinfectant is highly bactericidal.  这种新的消毒剂杀菌力很强。
The bactericidal properties of the antibiotic were tested.  测试了这种抗生素的杀菌性能。
synonyms: germicidal, disinfectant, antiseptic
antonyms: bacteriostatic
