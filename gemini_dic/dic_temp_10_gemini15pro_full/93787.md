immensurable:/ɪˈmenʃərəbl/
adjective
不可估量的；无限的；无边无际的
Too great to be measured; limitless.
例句：The immensity of the universe is truly immeasurable. 宇宙的浩瀚确实是不可估量的。

例句：The immeasurable vastness of space filled him with awe.  无边无际的太空让他充满了敬畏。

近义词：incalculable, infinite, boundless, limitless, vast
反义词：measurable, finite, limited
词性: adjective
