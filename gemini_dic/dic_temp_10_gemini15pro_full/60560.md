elasmobranch:/ɪˌlæzməˈbræŋk/ | n. adj. |  软骨鱼纲动物；软骨鱼纲的 | An elasmobranch is a fish of the class Chondrichthyes, having a cartilaginous skeleton, e.g., sharks, rays, and skates. | 例句：Sharks and rays are elasmobranchs.  鲨鱼和鳐鱼是软骨鱼纲动物。 | 近义词：chondrichthyan, cartilaginous fish | 反义词: teleost (硬骨鱼) | 词性：名词，形容词

elasmobranch:/ɪˌlæzməˈbræŋkiət/ | adj. | 软骨鱼纲的 | Belonging or pertaining to the Elasmobranchii, a subclass of fishes including sharks, rays, etc., having cartilaginous skeletons. | 例句：The elasmobranch fishes have skeletons made of cartilage. 软骨鱼类的骨骼是由软骨构成的。 | 近义词：chondrichthyan, cartilaginous fish | 反义词: teleost (硬骨鱼) | 词性: 形容词
