fades:/feɪdz/| v. (third person singular simple present fades, present participle fading, simple past and past participle faded)| 褪色，消失，衰退|To lose freshness, brightness, or strength gradually; to disappear slowly.|The photograph faded over time. 这张照片随着时间的推移褪色了。|近义词：diminishes, weakens, disappears, vanishes|反义词：brightens, strengthens, intensifies|词性:动词

fade:/feɪd/| n. | 褪色，逐渐消失|The act of fading; a gradual decrease in intensity or strength.|The fade of the music signaled the end of the show.  音乐的逐渐减弱标志着演出的结束。|近义词：decline, decrease, weakening|反义词：increase, intensification|词性:名词
