obstetrics:/əbˈstɛtrɪks/ /ɒbˈstɛtrɪks/
n.
产科学，助产术
The branch of medicine and surgery concerned with childbirth and the care of women giving birth.
She specialized in obstetrics and gynecology. 她专攻妇产科。

近义词：midwifery
反义词：（无直接反义词）
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *obstare* "to stand before, stand opposite to, attend upon," from *ob-* "before, toward" + *stare* "to stand."  Originally referred to the midwife standing before the woman giving birth.
