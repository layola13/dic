melee: /ˈmeɪleɪ/ (US), /ˈmɛleɪ/ (UK)| n.|混战，格斗，肉搏战|A confused hand-to-hand fight among several people.|The knights engaged in a fierce melee.|近义词：brawl, fray, scrimmage, tussle|反义词：truce, peace|词性: noun

词源: From Old French *meslee "mixture, confused fight," from mesler "to mix," from Latin miscellare "to mix," from miscere "to mix."
