nonreasonablenesses:/nɒnˈriːzənəbəlnəsɪz/
n.
不合理性；无理取闹
the state or quality of being unreasonable
The nonreasonablenesses of her demands led to the breakdown of negotiations.  她无理的要求导致谈判破裂。
近义词: absurdity, irrationality, unreasonableness
反义词: reasonableness, rationality, logic
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + reasonableness.  Reasonableness comes from reasonable + -ness. Reasonable is from reason + -able. Reason ultimately derives from Latin "ratio". Non- is a prefix meaning "not".
