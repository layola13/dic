embraces:/ɪmˈbreɪsɪz/| v.|拥抱，欣然接受，包含|To hold closely in one's arms, especially as a sign of affection; to accept or support (a belief, theory, or change) willingly and enthusiastically; to include or contain (something) as a constituent part.|She embraced her son warmly. 她热情地拥抱了她的儿子。|近义词：hug, accept, adopt, include|反义词：reject, exclude, repel|词性:verb

embraces:/ɪmˈbreɪsɪz/| n.|拥抱|An act of holding someone closely in one's arms, typically as a sign of affection; an act of accepting or supporting something willingly or enthusiastically.|Their embrace was long and heartfelt. 他们的拥抱漫长而 heartfelt.|近义词：hug|反义词：rejection|词性:noun
