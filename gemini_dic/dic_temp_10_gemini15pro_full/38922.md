confessionless:/kənˈfeʃənləs/
|adj.|无忏悔的，未坦白的|Lacking confession; without having admitted guilt or wrongdoing.|The police were baffled by the confessionless criminal. 警方对这个拒不认罪的罪犯感到困惑。|Near synonyms: unrepentant, unapologetic, remorseless|Antonyms: repentant, contrite, remorseful|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From confession + -less.  Confession comes from the Old French "confession" and ultimately from the Latin "confessio," meaning "acknowledgment."  The suffix "-less" is an Old English suffix meaning "without."
