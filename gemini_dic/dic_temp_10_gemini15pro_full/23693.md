brashness:/ˈbræʃnəs/| n.|鲁莽，冒失，轻率|The quality of being brash; self-assertiveness in a rude or noisy way.|His youthful brashness quickly subsided as he gained experience.  他年轻时的鲁莽随着经验的积累而迅速消退。|Synonyms: impudence, audacity, boldness, temerity|Antonyms: timidity, shyness, modesty, humility|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From brash + -ness.  Brash itself is of uncertain origin, possibly from a dialectal variant of crash.
