aperitif:/əˌpɛrɪˈtiːf/
noun
餐前开胃酒
An alcoholic drink taken before a meal to stimulate the appetite.
We enjoyed a refreshing aperitif before dinner.  我们晚餐前享用了一杯清爽的开胃酒。
synonyms: appetizer, pre-dinner drink
antonyms: digestif, after-dinner drink
n.
