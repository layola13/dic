efflorescence:/ˌɛfləˈrɛsəns/
n.
開花,風化,粉化;繁荣,兴盛
the state or a period of flowering
例句：The efflorescence of youth is beautiful. 青春的绽放是美丽的。

The garden is in full efflorescence. 花园里百花盛开。

近义词：blooming, blossoming, flowering, flourishing, thriving
反义词：decline, decay, deterioration

efflorescences:/ˌɛfləˈrɛsənsiz/
n.
開花,風化,粉化(复数); 繁荣,兴盛(复数)
the state or a period of flowering (plural)
例句：The spring efflorescences filled the air with fragrance. 春天的花朵使空气中充满了芬芳。

We are witnessing an efflorescences of new ideas in the field of technology. 我们正在见证科技领域新思想的蓬勃发展。

近义词：bloomings, blossomings, flowerings, flourishings, thrivings
反义词：declines, decays, deteriorations
