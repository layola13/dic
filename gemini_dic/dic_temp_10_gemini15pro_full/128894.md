nimble:/ˈnɪmbəl/| adj.|敏捷的，灵活的|Quick and light in movement or action; agile.|The nimble fingers of the pianist flew across the keys.  钢琴家灵活的手指在琴键上飞舞。|近义词：agile, quick, deft, swift, lithe|反义词：clumsy, awkward, slow|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *nimel, from Old English *nǣmel, from Proto-Germanic *namiz (“seize, catch”), from Proto-Indo-European *nem- (“to take, distribute, allot”).  Cognate with German nehmen (“to take”), Dutch nemen (“to take”), Swedish nymma (“to pinch”).  The original sense was probably "quick to grasp or seize."
