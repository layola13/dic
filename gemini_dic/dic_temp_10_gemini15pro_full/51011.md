desktop:/ˈdɛskˌtɒp/ (UK) /ˈdɛskˌtɑːp/ (US)| n. adj.|桌面；台式电脑；桌面型的|A desktop is the top surface of a desk. In computing, a desktop is the main screen area that you see after you turn on your computer and log in.  It can also refer to a desktop computer.| I keep my laptop on my desktop. 我把笔记本电脑放在桌面上。|Synonyms:  work surface, tabletop (for the physical desk); computer, PC (for desktop computer)|Antonyms:  underside (for physical desk); laptop, mobile device (for desktop computer)|noun, adjective

desktop:/ˈdɛskˌtɒp/ (UK) /ˈdɛskˌtɑːp/ (US)| adj.|桌面型的；台式的|Designed to be used on a desk; not portable.| He prefers a desktop computer to a laptop. 他更喜欢台式电脑而不是笔记本电脑。|Synonyms:  tabletop, non-portable|Antonyms:  portable, laptop, mobile|adjective
