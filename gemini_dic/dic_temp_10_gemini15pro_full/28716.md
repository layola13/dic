carminated: /ˈkɑːrmɪneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 洋红色的，胭脂红的 | Deep red or crimson colored; dyed with carmine. | The fabric was carminated a rich, vibrant hue. 这块布料被染成了鲜艳的洋红色。 | The carminated dress shimmered under the stage lights.  洋红色的裙子在舞台灯光下闪闪发光。| 近义词: crimson, scarlet, ruby, vermilion | 反义词: pale, light | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *carminatus*, past participle of *carminare* "to card wool," from *carmen* "card for wool," of uncertain origin. Later associated with *carminium* "carmine," a red pigment, perhaps due to the color of the wool being carded.
