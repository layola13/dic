momentously:/məˈmɛntəsli/
adverb
重大地；重要地
in a momentous way; significantly.
The judge momentously declared the defendant guilty. 法官庄严地宣布被告有罪。

近义词:significantly, importantly, consequentially, decisively
反义词:insignificantly, trivially, unimportantly
词性:adverb

momentous:/məˈmɛntəs/
adjective
重大的；重要的
of great importance or significance, especially in its bearing on the future.
The signing of the peace treaty was a momentous occasion. 签署和平条约是一个重大的时刻。

近义词:important, significant, historic, crucial, pivotal
反义词:insignificant, trivial, unimportant
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *momentōsus (“important, weighty”), from Latin momentum (“movement, motion, moving power, change, alteration, weight, influence, importance”), from movēre (“to move”).
