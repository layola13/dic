dispatched:/dɪˈspætʃt/ | v. |派遣，发送，迅速处理|to send someone or something somewhere for a particular purpose|The troops were dispatched to the border. 部队被派往边境。|近义词：send, deliver, forward|反义词: detain, hold, keep|verb

dispatched:/dɪˈspætʃt/ | adj. |已派遣的，已发送的|sent off or dealt with quickly and efficiently|The dispatched message was received within minutes. 已发送的消息几分钟内就收到了。|近义词：sent, delivered, completed|反义词: pending, unfinished, incomplete|adjective
