impugnability:/ɪmˌpʌɡnəˈbɪləti/| n.|可 impugn 的特性；可质疑；可责难|The impugnability of the witness's testimony led to the case being dismissed.  证人证词的可质疑性导致案件被驳回。|例句：The lawyer questioned the impugnability of the evidence presented by the prosecution. 律师质疑控方提供的证据的可质疑性。

The report's conclusions, due to their weak methodology, had a high degree of impugnability.  由于方法薄弱，该报告的结论具有很高的可质疑性。|近义词：challengeability, disputability, contestability|反义词：incontestability, irrefutability, unquestionability|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *impugnābilis, from Latin impugnāre (“to attack, assail”) (from in- (“in, on, against”) + pugnāre (“to fight”)) + -bilis (“-able”).
