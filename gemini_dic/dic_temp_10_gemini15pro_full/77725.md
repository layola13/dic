gemmosity:/dʒɛˈmɒsɪti/ |n.|宝石般的光辉，光彩夺目|The gemmosity of the diamond was breathtaking. 这颗钻石的光彩夺目，令人叹为观止。|English Explanation: The quality of resembling or consisting of gems; brilliance or splendor like that of gems.|例句：The gemmosity of the stained glass window illuminated the entire church. 彩色玻璃窗的宝石般的光辉照亮了整个教堂。

例句：The artist captured the gemmosity of the sunset in his painting. 艺术家在他的画作中捕捉到了日落的光彩。|近义词：brilliance, radiance, splendor, luminosity|反义词：dullness, opacity|词性：名词

词源: From Latin *gemmōsitās, from gemma (“bud, gem”) + -ōsitās, equivalent to English gem +‎ -osity.
