nonmutationally:/ˌnɒn.mjuːˈteɪ.ʃən.əl.i/ | adv. | 非突变地 | In a way that does not involve mutation. |  The gene was passed on nonmutationally to the next generation.  该基因以非突变的方式传递给了下一代。 |  Example: The trait was inherited nonmutationally.  这个特征是非突变遗传的。| Synonyms: hereditarily, congenitally | Antonyms: mutationally | Part of Speech: Adverb
