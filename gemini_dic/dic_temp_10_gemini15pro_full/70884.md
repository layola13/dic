fint:/fɪnt/
n.
鱼鳍
A fin, especially of a fish.
The fish used its fint to propel itself through the water. 鱼用鳍在水中推进。

近义词:fin, flipper
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "limb" in a broader context)
词性:noun


fint:/fɪnt/
v.
（鱼）用鳍游动
(of a fish) move its fins.
The trout finted in the shallows. 鳟鱼在浅滩中游动。

近义词:swim, dart
反义词:(No direct antonym)
词性:verb
