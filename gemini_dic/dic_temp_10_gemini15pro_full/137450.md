nonthinker:/ˈnɒnθɪŋkə(r)/ (US) /ˈnɑːnθɪŋkər/ (UK)| n. |不思考者，不动脑筋的人|A person who does not engage in intellectual activity; someone who fails to think critically or creatively. |He dismissed his critics as nonthinkers, incapable of grasping the brilliance of his plan. 他把批评他的人都斥为不动脑筋的人，认为他们无法理解他计划的精妙之处。|近义词：ignoramus, simpleton, dullard|反义词：intellectual, thinker, scholar|词性:名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + thinker (from the verb "think")
