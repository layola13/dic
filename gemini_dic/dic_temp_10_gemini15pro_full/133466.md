nonfecund:/nɒnˈfiːkənd/
adjective
不育的，不结果实的
Not productive; not fruitful; barren.
The nonfecund tree yielded no fruit this season.  这棵不育的树今年没有结出任何果实。
The couple struggled with nonfecundity for years.  这对夫妇多年来一直为不孕不育而苦恼。
近义词:infertile, sterile, barren
反义词:fertile, fecund, productive
词性:adjective

nonfecundity:/ˌnɒnfiˈkʌndɪti/
noun
不育，不结果实
The state or quality of being nonfecund; infertility; barrenness.
The nonfecundity of the soil made farming difficult. 土壤的贫瘠使耕作变得困难。
Nonfecundity can be a heartbreaking challenge for couples hoping to start a family. 不孕不育对于希望组建家庭的夫妇来说可能是一个令人心碎的挑战。
近义词:infertility, sterility, barrenness
反义词:fertility, fecundity, productivity
词性:noun

词源:
From non- + fecund.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "fecund" comes from the Latin "fecundus," meaning "fruitful" or "fertile."
