incorporally:/ɪnˈkɔːrpərəli/
adjective
无实体地；非物质地；精神上地
not in bodily form; not materially; spiritually.
The spirit existed incorporally, unseen by human eyes.  这灵魂以无形的方式存在，肉眼看不见。

近义词:immaterially, spiritually, intangibly
反义词:physically, bodily, materially
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *incorporalis, from in- "not" + corporalis "bodily," from corpus "body."
