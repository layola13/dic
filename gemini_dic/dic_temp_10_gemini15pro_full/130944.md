nonassertiveness:/ˌnɒnəˈsɜːtɪvnəs/ | n. |不自信; 不果断; 顺从| Lack of assertiveness; inability or unwillingness to express one's thoughts and feelings directly and firmly. | Example: Her nonassertiveness made her a target for bullies. 她的不自信使她成为欺凌的目标。| Example:  His nonassertiveness in meetings meant his ideas were often overlooked.  他在会议上的不果断意味着他的想法经常被忽视。 | Synonym: passiveness, submissiveness, timidity, meekness | Antonym: assertiveness, aggressiveness, confidence, boldness | Noun
