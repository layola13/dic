fond-of:/ˈfɑːnd əv/ | adj. | 喜爱的，爱好的 | Having a liking for something or someone. | She's fond of chocolate. 她喜欢巧克力。 | I'm very fond of my little sister. 我非常疼爱我的小妹妹。 | 近义词: attached to, keen on, partial to | 反义词: averse to, dislike | 词性: adjective | 词源: From Old French *fondre* "to melt, dissolve," figuratively "to become tender, loving."
