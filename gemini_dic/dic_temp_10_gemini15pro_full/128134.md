nethermost:/ˈnɛðəməʊst/ | adj. | 最低的；最底层的 | Situated at the lowest possible point; farthest down. | The nethermost circle of hell is described as a frozen wasteland. 最底层的地狱被描述为一片冰冻的荒地。 | The artifact was discovered in the nethermost chamber of the tomb.  这个文物是在墓穴最底层的房间里发现的。 | 近义词: lowest, bottommost, deepest | 反义词: uppermost, highest, topmost | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Old English *niðemest, superlative of neoðera (“lower”), from Proto-Germanic *niþaraz (“down, below”).  Related to nether.
