encumber:/ɪnˈkʌmbər/ /ɛnˈkʌmbɚ/| vt.|阻碍，妨碍，拖累|to burden or weigh down; to hamper or hinder the function or activity of|The hiker was encumbered by a heavy backpack.  这个徒步旅行者被一个沉重的背包拖累。|Synonyms: burden, hinder, obstruct, impede, hamper|Antonyms: assist, aid, help, facilitate|verb

encumber:/ɪnˈkʌmbər/ /ɛnˈkʌmbɚ/| n.|累赘，妨碍物|something that encumbers; a burden or impediment|Debt is a terrible encumber. 债务是一个可怕的累赘。|Synonyms: burden, impediment, obstacle, hindrance|Antonyms: aid, assistance, help|noun
