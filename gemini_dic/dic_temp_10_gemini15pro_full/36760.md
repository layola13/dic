collude:/kəˈluːd/| v.|串通，勾结，共谋|To conspire; to act together secretly or illegally for a deceitful or fraudulent purpose.|The companies colluded to fix prices.  这些公司串通起来操纵价格。|The rival companies colluded to raise prices.  竞争对手公司串通起来抬高价格。|Synonym: conspire, plot, scheme, intrigue|Antonym: cooperate (in a legitimate way), compete|verb
