nivose:/niːˈvəʊz/| n.|雪月 (法国共和历第四个月)|The fourth month of the French Republican Calendar (from late December to late January), associated with snow.  It corresponds roughly to the period between December 21st and January 19th.|The harvest festival was held during Nivose.  丰收节是在雪月举行的。|近义词:none (as it's a specific term)|反义词:none (as it's a specific term)|noun
