cluster's:/ˈklʌstərz/| n.|群的，簇的，串的|The possessive form of "cluster," indicating ownership or association.  It refers to something belonging to or associated with a cluster.|例句：The cluster's shape was irregular.  这串的形状是不规则的。|例句：The cluster's grapes were ripe. 这串葡萄熟了。|近义词：group's, bunch's, collection's|反义词: individual's, single's|词性:名词所有格

词源：From Old English *clyster "clod, lump" (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *klustrą "lump, clod, crowd" (compare Dutch klont "lump, clod," German Kloss "dumpling, lump"). The -er suffix is common in forming agent nouns. The apostrophe + s ('s) is the possessive marker.
