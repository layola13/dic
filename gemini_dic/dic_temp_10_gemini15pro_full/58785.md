dunny:/ˈdʌni/
noun
厕所，茅厕
(Australian and New Zealand slang) an outdoor toilet, typically a small wooden shed.
例句：The dunny was a bit smelly.  茅厕有点臭。

例句：He went out back to the dunny. 他去后面的茅厕了。

近义词:toilet, lavatory, outhouse, restroom, bathroom
反义词:(None -  lacks a direct opposite)
词性:noun
