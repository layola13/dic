avisement:/əˈvaɪzmənt/
noun
考虑；斟酌；建议
Careful consideration or deliberation before making a decision.
The matter was taken under advisement. 这件事已提交审议。
近义词: consideration, deliberation, reflection, contemplation
反义词: impulsiveness, recklessness
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Old French avisement, from aviser "to consider," from a- "to" + viser "to look at."
