drupaceous:/druːˈpeɪʃəs/
adjective
核果的；似核果的
Relating to, or resembling, a drupe.  A drupe is a fleshy fruit with thin skin and a central stone containing the seed, like a peach, plum, or cherry.
Peaches, plums, and cherries are drupaceous fruits.  桃子、李子和樱桃都是核果。
The tree bears small, drupaceous fruit. 这棵树结小核果。
synonyms: stone-fruit, drupe-like
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but could use terms like "non-drupaceous" or describe fruits of a different type, like "berry-like" or "pomaceous")
词性: adjective
