nonadorners:/nɒnəˈdɔːnəz/ | n. |  不加装饰者；朴素的人 |  People who do not adorn themselves or their surroundings with decorations.  They prefer simplicity and avoid embellishments. |  The nonadorners preferred the stark beauty of the desert landscape. 不加装饰者更喜欢沙漠景观的荒凉之美。

The nonadorners at the gala stood out amidst the elaborately dressed guests.  在晚会上，不加装饰的人在盛装打扮的宾客中显得格外突出。 | 近义词: minimalists, purists, ascetics | 反义词: decorators, embellishers, maximalists | 词性: 名词
