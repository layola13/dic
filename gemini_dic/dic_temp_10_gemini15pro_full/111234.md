词源：
From litho- + -tritor, from Latin *tritor "rubber, grinder," agent noun from terere "to rub, grind."

注意事项:  Lithotriptor and lithotripsy are often used interchangeably in informal contexts, but technically, lithotriptor refers to the instrument and lithotripsy refers to the procedure.
