amphipod:/ˈæmfɪpɒd/ (UK) /ˈæmfɪpɑːd/ (US)| n. |片脚类动物|A small crustacean with a laterally compressed body, typically found in aquatic environments.|例句：The amphipod is a common inhabitant of tide pools.  片脚类动物是潮汐池的常见居民。

例句：Scientists studied the effects of pollution on the amphipod population.  科学家研究了污染对片脚类动物种群的影响。|近义词：crustacean,  scud|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From the Greek "amphi" (both) + "pous" (foot), referring to the different types of legs these creatures possess.
