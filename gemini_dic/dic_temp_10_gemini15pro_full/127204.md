naturopathy:/ˌneɪtʃəˈrɒpəθi/ | n. |自然疗法|A system of alternative medicine based on the theory that diseases can be successfully treated or prevented without the use of drugs, by techniques such as control of diet, exercise, and massage.|She turned to naturopathy after conventional medicine failed to alleviate her symptoms. 她求助于自然疗法，因为传统医学未能缓解她的症状。|Synonyms: holistic medicine, alternative medicine, natural healing|Antonyms: conventional medicine, allopathy|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From nature + -pathy (from Greek pathos "suffering, feeling").  Coined in the late 19th century.
