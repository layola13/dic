anorthoscope:/ˈænɔːrθəˌskoʊp/
n.
歪像镜，变形镜
An optical instrument that transforms a distorted image into a regular one when viewed through a slotted disk.
The anorthoscope creates a recognizable image from a deliberately distorted picture when spun. 歪像镜旋转时，可以从一张故意扭曲的图片中创建一个可识别的图像。
The children were fascinated by the anorthoscope and its ability to create a clear picture from a jumbled mess. 孩子们被歪像镜及其将混乱的图像变成清晰图像的能力所吸引。
近义词:distorting mirror, anamorphic lens
反义词:N/A
词性:noun
