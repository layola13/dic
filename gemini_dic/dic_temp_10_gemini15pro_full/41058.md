copist:/ˈkɑːpɪɪst/ /ˈkɒpɪɪst/| n.|抄写员；誊写员|A person who copies written material, especially before printing was invented.|The copist worked diligently, transcribing the ancient manuscript. 抄写员勤奋地工作，抄写着古代的手稿。|近义词：scribe, transcriber, clerk|反义词:author, originator|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *copista, from Latin copia ("abundance, plenty; opportunity, power; transcript, copy") + -ista (agent suffix).  The Latin word copia is related to co- + ops ("wealth, resources, power").
