homoeostatic:/ˌhoʊmiːəˈstætɪk/ | adj. |体内平衡的；恒定的| Relating to homeostasis; tending to maintain internal stability.|  The body's homoeostatic mechanisms regulate temperature and blood sugar levels. 人体的体内平衡机制调节体温、血糖水平。| synonyms: regulatory, stabilizing, self-regulating | antonyms: unstable, fluctuating | adjective


homoestatic:/ˌhoʊmiːəˈstætɪk/ | adj. |体内平衡的；恒定的| Relating to homeostasis; tending to maintain internal stability.| The homoeostatic mechanisms of the body work constantly to maintain a stable internal environment.  身体的体内平衡机制不断地工作以维持稳定的内部环境。| synonyms:  regulatory, stabilizing, self-regulating | antonyms: unstable, fluctuating | adjective

词源:  From homeo- + -static.  Homeo- from Greek homoios ‘similar’. -Static from Greek statikos ‘causing to stand’, from histanai ‘to cause to stand’.
