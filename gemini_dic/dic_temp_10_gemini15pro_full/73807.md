formatted:/ˈfɔːrˌmætɪd/ | adj. | 格式化的，已格式化的 |  Arranged or set out according to a format; having a particular format. | The document was neatly formatted and easy to read.  该文件格式整齐，易于阅读。| formatted text has been arranged according to a specific style or layout. 文本根据特定样式或布局进行了排列。| synonymous: arranged, organized, structured, styled, laid out | antonymous: unformatted, disorganized, unstructured | adjective
