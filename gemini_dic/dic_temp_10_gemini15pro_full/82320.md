grouting: /ˈɡraʊtɪŋ/ | n. |灌浆；勾缝；水泥浆|The process of filling gaps or spaces in construction with a fluid material (grout) that hardens over time. This is used to strengthen, seal, or stabilize structures. |The grouting of the tiles prevented water damage.  瓷砖的灌浆防止了水损坏。|Synonyms: cementing, sealing, filling|Antonyms: excavating, removing|noun

grout: /ɡraʊt/ | n. vt.|水泥浆；灌浆；薄砂浆|A thin mortar used to fill gaps or spaces, especially between tiles or bricks. As a verb, it means to apply grout.|We need to grout the bathroom tiles. 我们需要给浴室瓷砖灌浆。|Synonyms: mortar, filler, sealant|Antonyms: solvent, thinner|noun, verb
