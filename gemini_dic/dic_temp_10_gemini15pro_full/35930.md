coculturing:/koʊˈkʌltʃərɪŋ/ | n. | 共培养，共同培养 | The process of growing two or more different types of cells or organisms together in the same environment. |  Example 1: Coculturing of different bacterial species can lead to the discovery of new antibiotics. 不同细菌种类的共培养可以帮助发现新的抗生素。

Example 2:  Researchers are coculturing cancer cells with immune cells to study their interactions. 研究人员正在将癌细胞与免疫细胞共培养以研究它们的相互作用。 | Synonyms: co-cultivation | Antonyms: monoculture | noun
