meditweet:/ˈmediˌtwit/| n.|医疗推文|A tweet (a short message posted on Twitter) related to medical topics, often used to share health information, personal experiences with illness, or opinions on healthcare.|Example:  I saw a fascinating meditweet about the latest advancements in cancer research. 我看到了一条关于癌症研究最新进展的精彩医疗推文。

Example:  Many doctors are now using meditweets to educate the public about important health issues. 现在许多医生都在使用医疗推文来教育公众重要的健康问题。|近义词:health tweet, medical post|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially irrelevant tweet)|词性:noun
