hick:/hɪk/ | adj. |乡下的，土气的|Characteristic of a hick; unsophisticated or unintelligent. |  Their style of dress was rather hick. 他们的穿着风格相当土气。|Synonyms: rustic, provincial, countrified | Antonyms: sophisticated, urbane | Adjective
