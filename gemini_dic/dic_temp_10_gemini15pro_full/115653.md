mardi gras: /ˈmɑːrdi ɡrɑː/ (US), /ˈmɑːdi ɡrɑː/ (UK)| n. |狂欢节，谢肉节|A festive day celebrated in some countries on Shrove Tuesday, the day before Ash Wednesday, often with parades and feasting, marking the beginning of Lent.|例句：The Mardi Gras parade is a spectacular event. 狂欢节游行是一场盛大的活动。

例句：We went to New Orleans for Mardi Gras. 我们去新奥尔良过狂欢节了。

近义词：Carnival, festival|反义词: Lent|词性: noun
