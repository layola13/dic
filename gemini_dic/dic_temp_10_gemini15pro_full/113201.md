lurker:/ˈlɜːrkər/ (US), /ˈlɜːkə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 潜伏者；窥探者；|Someone who reads discussions in an online forum or chat room but rarely or never participates, typically with the intention of learning or observing.|例句：He's a lurker on several online forums, preferring to read others' posts rather than contribute himself.  他经常潜伏在几个在线论坛里，宁愿看别人的帖子也不自己发帖。

例句：There are many lurkers in the chat room who never say anything.  聊天室里有很多潜水者，他们从来不说话。

近义词：observer, onlooker, reader, follower|反义词:participant, contributor|词性:noun


lurks:/lɜːrks/ (US), /lɜːks/ (UK)| v. | 潜伏；埋伏；躲藏|To be or remain hidden so as to wait in ambush for someone or something.|例句：Danger lurks around every corner in this dark alley.  在这条黑暗的小巷里，每个角落都潜伏着危险。

例句：A crocodile lurks beneath the surface of the water.  一条鳄鱼潜伏在水面之下。

近义词：hide, conceal, skulk, prowl|反义词:emerge, appear|词性:verb
