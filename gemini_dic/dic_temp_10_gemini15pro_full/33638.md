cineaste:/ˈsɪniˌæst/ /ˈsɪnɪæst/ | n. |电影迷；电影爱好者；电影制作人(尤指有艺术追求的)|A cineaste is a devoted movie enthusiast, often with a deep appreciation for film as an art form, sometimes also referring to a filmmaker with artistic aspirations. |例句：The cineaste eagerly awaited the release of the director's latest film.  这位电影迷热切地期待着导演最新电影的上映。

例句：He's a true cineaste, with a vast knowledge of film history. 他是一位真正的电影迷，对电影史有着渊博的知识。 |近义词：film buff, movie lover, film enthusiast, filmmaker |反义词: None (No direct antonym exists) |词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cinéaste*, from *ciné-* (combining form of *cinéma* "cinema") + *-aste* (suffix forming nouns denoting persons addicted to or professionally occupied with the thing denoted by the first element).  *Cinéma* ultimately comes from Greek *kínēma*  "movement."
