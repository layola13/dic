meliorater:/ˈmiːliəreɪtər/| n.|改良者，改进者|A person or thing that meliorates or improves something, especially land.|The farmer invested in new irrigation systems, becoming a true meliorater of the arid land.  这位农民投资了新的灌溉系统，成为了这片干旱土地的真正改良者。|近义词:improver, reformer, enhancer|反义词:worsener, degrader|词性:noun

meliorate:/ˈmiːliəreɪt/| v.|改善，改良|To make or become better; improve.|The new laws meliorated the working conditions in the factories. 新法律改善了工厂的工作条件。|近义词:improve, amend, better|反义词:worsen, deteriorate|词性:verb

melioration:/ˌmiːliəˈreɪʃn/| n.|改善，改良|The act or process of making something better; improvement.|The melioration of the soil increased crop yields. 土壤的改良增加了作物产量。|近义词:improvement, betterment, amelioration|反义词:worsening, deterioration|词性:noun
