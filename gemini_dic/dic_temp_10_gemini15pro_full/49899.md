demur:/dɪˈmɜːr/ (US), /dɪˈmɜː(r)/ (UK)| v. n.|反对，异议；犹豫|To express doubt or objection, often politely; to hesitate|例句：She demurred at the idea of working on the weekend. 她对周末加班的主意表示反对。|近义词：object, protest, hesitate, balk|反义词:acquiesce, consent, agree|词性:动词

demur:/dɪˈmɜːr/ (US), /dɪˈmɜː(r)/ (UK)| n.|反对，异议；犹豫|An objection or hesitation|例句：Her demurs about the project were overruled. 她对该项目的反对意见被否决了。|近义词：objection, protest, hesitation|反义词:agreement, consent|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *demourer*, from Latin *demorari* "to linger, delay," from *de-* "down, completely" + *morari* "to delay."
