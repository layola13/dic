democratizes: /dɪˈmɒkrətaɪzɪz/ | v. | 使民主化；使大众化 | To make something accessible to everyone, removing restrictions or privileges limited to a select group. | The internet has democratized information, making it available to anyone with a connection. 互联网使信息民主化，任何有连接的人都可以访问信息。|  The invention of the printing press democratized knowledge. 印刷机的发明使知识大众化。 |popularizes, spreads, disseminates | restricts, limits, monopolizes | verb
