balbucinate:/bælˈbjuːsɪneɪt/ /bɑːlˈbjuːsɪneɪt/ | v. | 含糊不清地说，结结巴巴地说 | To speak in a halting or stammering way; to mutter indistinctly. |  He balbucinated a few words, but they were too unclear to understand. 他含糊不清地说了几句话，但太不清楚了，听不懂。

He was so nervous that he balbucinated throughout the presentation. 他太紧张了，整个演讲都结结巴巴的。| 近义词：stammer, stutter, mumble, slur | 反义词：articulate, enunciate | 词性: 动词
