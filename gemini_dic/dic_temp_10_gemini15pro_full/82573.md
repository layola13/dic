guardable:/ˈɡɑːrdəbl/ | adj. | 可守卫的，可保护的 | Capable of being guarded or protected. | The castle, situated on a high cliff, was easily guardable.  这座城堡位于高高的悬崖上，很容易防守。| The jewels were kept in a guardable safe.  珠宝被存放在一个可以防护的保险箱里。 |  近义词：defensible, protectable, securable | 反义词：vulnerable, indefensible, exposed | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From "guard" (from Old French "garder," meaning "to watch, protect") + "-able" (suffix meaning "capable of being").
