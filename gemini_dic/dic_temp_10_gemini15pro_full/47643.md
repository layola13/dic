debits:/ˈdebɪts/| n.|借方; 借记; 借项|An amount recorded as a debt, especially one in a company's accounts.|例句：The debits and credits must balance. 借方和贷方必须平衡。|近义词：charges, expenses|反义词：credits|词性:名词
debited:/ˈdebɪtɪd/| v.|借记; 记入借方|To record a debit against (an account).|例句：My account was debited $50. 我的账户被扣除了50美元。|近义词：charged|反义词：credited|词性:动词
