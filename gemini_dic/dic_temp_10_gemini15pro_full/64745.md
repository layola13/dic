escorts:/ˈeskɔːrts/ /ɪˈskɔːrts/| n. v. |护送者，陪同人员；护航舰；护卫队；伴游|A person or group of people, vehicles, ships, or aircraft that accompany someone or something for protection or as a mark of honor.  Also, can refer to people hired to accompany others socially, often with romantic implications.|The president's car was flanked by motorcycle escorts. 总统的座驾两侧有摩托车护卫。|近义词：guards, companions, attendants, guides|反义词: abandons, deserts|词性:名词, 动词
