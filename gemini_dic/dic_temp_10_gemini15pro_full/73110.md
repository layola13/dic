for-dear-life:/fɔːr dɪər laɪf/
|adv.| 拼命地，紧紧地；为了活命|Holding on for dear life means holding on very tightly, as if your life depended on it.  It implies a desperate grip due to fear of falling or losing something.|The monkey was clinging to the branch for dear life. 猴子紧紧地抓住树枝。|Synonym: desperately, tightly, with all one's might|Antonym: loosely, gently|adverb
