ostracization:/ˌɒstrəsaɪˈzeɪʃn/ /ˌɑːstrəsaɪˈzeɪʃn/
n.
排斥，放逐，孤立
exclusion from a society or group
例句：He suffered years of ostracization after the scandal.  丑闻过后，他遭受了多年的排斥。

例句：The ostracization of the student made her feel lonely and isolated.  对这个学生的排斥让她感到孤独和孤立。

近义词：exclusion, banishment, isolation, rejection, blackballing
反义词：inclusion, acceptance, welcome, embrace
词性: 名词
