nappiest:/ˈnæpiɪst/| adj.|最困倦的，最想睡觉的|Most sleepy; most inclined to sleep.|例句：The baby was nappiest after his feeding. 婴儿喂完奶后最困了。|例句：I felt nappiest after a long day of work.  经过一天漫长的工作后，我感到最困了。|近义词：sleepiest, drowsiest|反义词：most awake, most alert|词性:形容词

词源:  From nap (meaning a short sleep) + -y (adjective suffix) + -est (superlative suffix).  最终来源于古英语 *hnappian，意思是打盹。
