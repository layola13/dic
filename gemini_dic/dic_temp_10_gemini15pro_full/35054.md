closely:/ˈkloʊsli/ (US) /ˈkləʊsli/ (UK)| adv.|仔细地，密切地，严密地|In a close manner; with attention to detail; carefully.  It also means in close proximity or nearness.|He listened closely to the instructions. 他仔细地听着指示。|Near, rigorously, carefully, attentively|Loosely, distantly, carelessly|Adverb
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *closeliche*, equivalent to close +‎ -ly.
