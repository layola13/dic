assign:/əˈsaɪn/| vt.|分配；指派；指定；委派；布置（作业）；转让；把…归因于|The teacher assigned us a lot of homework. 老师给我们布置了很多作业。|近义词:allocate, allot, apportion, distribute|反义词: withhold, keep, retain|verb

assign:/əˈsaɪn/| n.|受让人；受托人|The assign of the property is responsible for its upkeep.  房产的受让人负责其维护。|近义词: assignee, transferee, grantee|反义词: assignor, grantor, transferor|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French assigner, from Latin assignare (“to mark out, allot, appoint, ascribe”), from ad- (“to”) + signare (“to mark, designate”), from signum (“a mark, sign, token”).
