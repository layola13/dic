dispense:/dɪˈspɛns/ | v. | 分配；分发；提供；免除；执行(法律)；配(药) | to distribute or provide something | The government dispensed emergency food supplies to the flood victims. 政府向洪水灾民分发了紧急食品供应。| synonyms: distribute, allocate, provide, administer, execute | antonyms: withhold, retain, collect | verb

dispense with:/dɪˈspɛns wɪð/ | v. | 免除；摒弃 | to do without something; to no longer use or need something | They dispensed with the formalities and got right down to business. 他们免去了客套，直接谈生意。| synonyms: forgo, omit, eliminate, discard | antonyms: retain, keep, maintain | phrasal verb
