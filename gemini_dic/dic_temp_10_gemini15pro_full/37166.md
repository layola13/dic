come-upon:/kʌm əˈpɑːn/ | phrasal verb | 偶然发现，撞见；突然袭击 | To encounter or discover something or someone unexpectedly; to attack suddenly. | I came upon a rare antique while browsing at the flea market. 我在跳蚤市场闲逛时偶然发现了一件稀有的古董。| synonyms: encounter, stumble upon, discover, chance upon, find, attack | antonyms: miss, avoid, overlook | verb

come-upon:/kʌm əˈpɑːn/ | phrasal verb |  突然想起 | To suddenly remember something. | The answer came upon me in the middle of the night. 答案在半夜突然出现在我的脑海里。| synonyms: remember, recall, recollect | antonyms: forget | verb
