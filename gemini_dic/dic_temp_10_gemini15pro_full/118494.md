mesmerization:/ˌmezməraɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. | 迷惑，催眠，着迷 |The process of mesmerizing someone; the state of being mesmerized.  The act of holding someone's attention completely, as if by magic. | Her mesmerizing performance captivated the entire audience. 她令人着迷的表演吸引了所有观众。| synonyms: fascination, captivation, enchantment, hypnosis | antonyms: boredom, disinterest, apathy | noun

mesmerize:/ˈmezməraɪz/ | vt. | 使着迷，催眠 | To hold someone's attention completely, as if by magic; to hypnotize. | The magician mesmerized the audience with his tricks. 魔术师用他的戏法迷住了观众。| synonyms: fascinate, captivate, enchant, hypnotize | antonyms: bore, repel, disgust | verb
