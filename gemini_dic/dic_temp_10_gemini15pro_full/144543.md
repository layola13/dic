outvote:/ˌaʊtˈvoʊt/
verb
以票数胜过，投票击败
defeat in voting; to defeat by a higher number of votes
The proposal to build a new stadium was outvoted by the residents.  居民投票否决了建造新体育场的提案。
The chairman was outvoted by the board members. 董事长被董事会成员投票否决。
近义词: defeat, overrule
反义词: lose, be defeated
词性: verb
