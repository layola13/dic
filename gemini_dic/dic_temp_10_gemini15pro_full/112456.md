loudmouth:/ˈlaʊdmaʊθ/ (US) /ˈlaʊdmaʊθ/ (UK)| n.|大声喧哗的人，多嘴多舌的人|A person who talks too much, especially in a loud and boastful manner.|He's such a loudmouth; he can't keep anything to himself. 他真是个大嘴巴，什么秘密都守不住。|synonyms: braggart, blowhard, windbag|antonyms: reticent person, introvert|noun

loudmouth:/ˈlaʊdmaʊθ/ (US) /ˈlaʊdmaʊθ/ (UK)| adj.|大声喧哗的，多嘴多舌的|Given to loud and excessive talking.|Her loudmouth brother embarrassed her at the party. 她那大嘴巴的哥哥在派对上让她很尴尬。|synonyms: boisterous, vociferous|antonyms: quiet, reserved|adjective
