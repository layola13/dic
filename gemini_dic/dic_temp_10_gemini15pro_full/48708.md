defibrinize:/diːˈfaɪbrɪnaɪz/
verb
去除纤维蛋白
To remove fibrin from.
The blood was defibrinized to prevent clotting.  为了防止血液凝固，血液被去除了纤维蛋白。
The scientist defibrinized the blood sample for the experiment. 科学家为实验去除了血液样本中的纤维蛋白。
declotting,  anticoagulate
 clot

词性:v.
