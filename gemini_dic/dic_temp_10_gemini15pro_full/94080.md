impaste:/ɪmˈpeɪst/ | v. | 涂厚颜料;  使糊状 | To cover thickly with paint or pigment, often so that the surface has a raised, textured appearance; to make into a paste. | The artist impastoed the canvas with thick oil paint. 艺术家在画布上厚厚地涂上了油画颜料。 | The chef impastoed the ingredients into a smooth paste.  厨师把原料做成了光滑的糊状物。 |近义词: daub, plaster, coat | 反义词: thin, dilute | 词性: 动词

impaste:/ɪmˈpeɪst/ | n. | 厚涂颜料; 糊状物 | The technique of applying paint thickly so that it stands out from the surface; a thick application of paint or other material. | The impaste on the painting created a dramatic three-dimensional effect.  画作上的厚涂颜料营造出引人注目的三维效果。| The chef prepared an impaste of garlic and herbs. 厨师准备了大蒜和香草的糊状物。| 近义词: application, coating, layer | 反义词: wash, glaze | 词性: 名词
