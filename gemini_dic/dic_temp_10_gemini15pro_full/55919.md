divulsive:/daɪˈvʌlsɪv/| adj.|分裂的，导致分裂的，使人抽搐的|Tending to pull apart; causing disruption or convulsion.|The political rhetoric had a divulsive effect on the nation, driving people further apart. 这种政治言论对国家产生了分裂作用，使人们更加疏远。|近义词： disruptive, divisive, tearing, rending|反义词： unifying, uniting, cohesive|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *divulsus*, past participle of *divellere* "to tear apart," from *di-* "apart" + *vellere* "to pluck, tear."
