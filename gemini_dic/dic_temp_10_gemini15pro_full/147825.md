overpublicizes: /ˌoʊvərˈpʌblɪsaɪzɪz/
verb
过度宣传；过分宣扬
To publicize excessively; to give too much publicity to something.
The media overpublicized the celebrity's minor scandal. 媒体过度宣传了这位名人的小丑闻。
Synonyms: overhype, overpromote, oversaturate
Antonyms: underpublicize, downplay
词性: 动词
