unexpectedness:/ˌʌnɪkˈspektɪdnəs/
n.
意外；出乎意料
The unexpectedness of the event shocked everyone.  这个事件的意外性震惊了所有人。
The unexpectedness of his arrival surprised us. 他的突然到来让我们感到惊讶。
Etymology: From unexpected + -ness.  Unexpected comes from un- (not) + expected (anticipated). Expected ultimately derives from the Latin word "exspectare" (to look out for).
Synonyms: surprise, suddenness, abruptness, unpredictability
Antonyms: predictability, expectedness, anticipation, foreseeability
Noun
