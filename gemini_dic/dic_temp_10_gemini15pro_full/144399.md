outstripping:/ˌaʊtˈstrɪpɪŋ/
verb
超过，胜过
growing or increasing faster than something or someone else.
China's economic growth is outstripping that of many developed nations. 中国的经济增长超过了许多发达国家。
The demand for skilled workers is outstripping supply. 对熟练工人的需求超过了供给。
Synonyms: surpassing, exceeding, outpacing, overtaking
Antonyms: lagging behind, falling behind
