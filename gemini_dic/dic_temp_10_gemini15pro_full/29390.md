cat:/kæt/| n.|猫|A small domesticated carnivorous mammal with soft fur, short snout, and retractile claws. It is widely kept as a pet or for catching mice.|The cat sat on the mat. 猫坐在垫子上。|近义词：feline, kitty, pussycat|反义词:dog (a common contrasting pet)|noun
