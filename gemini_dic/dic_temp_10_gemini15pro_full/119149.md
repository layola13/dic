mewed:/mjuːd/ | v. | (猫)喵喵叫；发出呜咽声 | The kitten mewed softly. 小猫轻轻地喵喵叫。| The trapped cat mewed pitifully. 被困住的猫可怜地喵喵叫。 |近义词：miaowed, purred (for contented sounds) | 反义词: roared, hissed | verb

mewed:/mjuːd/ | n. | 喵喵叫；呜咽声 | The constant mewing of the cat kept me awake. 猫不停的喵喵叫让我睡不着。 |  We heard the plaintive mew of a lost kitten. 我们听到了丢失小猫的哀怨喵喵声。 |近义词：miaow, purr (for contented sounds) | 反义词: roar, hiss | noun

Etymology:  From Middle English *meuen*, *miauen*, from Old English *miāwan*, from Proto-Germanic *miwwaną*, from Proto-Indo-European *mey- (“to meow, to low”).  Cognate with Dutch *miauwen*, German *miauen*, Danish *miave*, Swedish *mjau*.
