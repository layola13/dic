buffel:/ˈbʌfəl/| n.|非洲水牛；南非一种牧草|A large African bovine mammal with curved horns and a thick hide, found both wild and domesticated.  A type of grass native to Africa, *Cenchrus ciliaris*, cultivated as fodder. |The buffel grazed peacefully on the savanna. 非洲水牛在热带草原上平静地吃草。| The farmer planted buffel to feed his livestock. 农民种植非洲牧草来喂养他的牲畜。|Synonyms: (animal)buffalo, ox; (grass)African foxtail grass, blue buffalo grass|Antonyms: (None for either meaning)|Noun
