opolchenie: /əˌpɒlˈtʃɛniɪ/ /əˌpɑːlˈtʃɛniɪ/
noun
民兵，国民自卫队
A body of volunteer or militia troops, especially in Russia and other Slavic countries.
The opolchenie played a crucial role in defending Moscow during Napoleon's invasion.  莫斯科民兵在拿破仑入侵期间对保卫莫斯科起了至关重要的作用。

The opolchenie was called up to supplement the regular army.  民兵被征召来补充正规军。

Synonym: militia, volunteer corps, irregular troops
Antonym: regular army, standing army
词性: 名词
