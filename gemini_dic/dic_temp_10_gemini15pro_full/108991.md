left-out:/ˌleft ˈaʊt/| adj.|被冷落的，被遗漏的|Feeling excluded or neglected.  Feeling as if one has not been included in something.|例句：The child felt left-out when his friends went to the movies without him.  孩子感到被冷落，因为他的朋友们没带他去看电影。|近义词：excluded, neglected, ostracized, overlooked|反义词:included, involved|词性:形容词
