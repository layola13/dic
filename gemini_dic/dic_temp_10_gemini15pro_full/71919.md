flexuous:/ˈflekʃuəs/| adj.|弯曲的，蜿蜒的，柔韧的|Characterized by regular curves, bends, or turns; winding; sinuous.  Also, lithe; supple.|The river followed a flexuous course through the valley. 这条河蜿蜒流过山谷。|近义词：winding, sinuous, serpentine, tortuous, meandering|反义词：straight, rigid, inflexible|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *flexuosus*, from flexus (“bent”), past participle of flectere (“to bend”).
