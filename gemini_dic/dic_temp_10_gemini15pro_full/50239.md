dependantly:/dɪˈpɛndəntlɪ/
adv.
依赖地，依靠地
in a way that shows you depend on someone or something.
He sat dependently, waiting for instructions. 他依赖地坐着，等待指示。
近义词: reliantly, subordinately
反义词: independently, autonomously
词性: 副词
