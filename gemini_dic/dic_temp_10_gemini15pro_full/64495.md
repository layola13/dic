eroded:/ɪˈroʊdɪd/
adjective
被侵蚀的，腐蚀的
worn away or destroyed by the action of water, wind, or acid
例句：The eroded coastline is a testament to the power of the ocean. 被侵蚀的海岸线证明了海洋的力量。

例句：The statue's features were eroded by wind and rain.  雕像的特征被风雨侵蚀了。

近义词：worn, weathered, corroded, abraded
反义词：preserved, intact, protected
词性：adjective


erode:/ɪˈroʊd/
verb
侵蚀，腐蚀
(of wind, water, or other natural agents) gradually wear away (soil, rock, or land). Gradually destroy or be gradually destroyed.
例句：The cliffs are eroding several feet a year. 悬崖每年都在被侵蚀几英尺。

例句：His confidence was slowly eroded by repeated failures. 他反复的失败逐渐侵蚀了他的自信心。
近义词：wear away, corrode, abrade, deteriorate
反义词：build up, strengthen, preserve
词性：verb
