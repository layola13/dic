astigmatic:/ˌæstɪɡˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 散光的；乱视的；【比喻】目光短浅的 |  Suffering from or affected by astigmatism; unable to focus properly because of uneven curvature of the cornea. Also used figuratively to describe a lack of clarity or focus in thinking or vision. |  My vision is astigmatic, so I need to wear glasses. 我的视力散光，所以我需要戴眼镜。 |  nearsighted, myopic, blurry | farsighted, clear-sighted | adjective

astigmatic:/ˌæstɪɡˈmætɪk/ | n. | 散光患者 | A person with astigmatism.  |  He's an astigmatic and has worn glasses since he was a child. 他是个散光患者，从小就戴眼镜。 |  nearsighted person, myope | farsighted person | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From a- (not) + stigmatic (point-like),  from Greek stigma (point, mark).  The term refers to the inability of the eye to focus light onto a single point on the retina.
