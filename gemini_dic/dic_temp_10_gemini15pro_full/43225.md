cracker-bonbon: /ˈkrækərˌbɒnbɒn/ (UK) /ˈkrækərˌbɑːnbɑːn/ (US)| n. |薄脆夹心巧克力; 薄脆夹心糖果|A type of confectionery consisting of a chocolate or other sweet filling sandwiched between two thin crackers or biscuits.|She offered me a cracker-bonbon with my coffee. 她给了我一块薄脆夹心巧克力配咖啡。| The cracker-bonbons were a delightful treat. 这些薄脆夹心糖果真是美味的享受。|近义词：wafer cookie, sandwich biscuit |反义词: None|词性: 名词

Etymology:
cracker: From Middle English *crakere, from craken (“to crack, make a sharp noise”), probably of imitative origin.
bonbon: From French bonbon ("good good," reduplication of bon "good").
