overinvest:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈvɛst/| v. |过度投资|To invest excessively, often to the point of diminishing returns or financial risk.|The company overinvested in new technology and neglected its core business.  这家公司在新技术上过度投资，而忽略了其核心业务。|例句：They overinvested in real estate and lost a lot of money. 他们在房地产上过度投资，损失了很多钱。|近义词：overspend, squander|反义词:underinvest|词性:动词
