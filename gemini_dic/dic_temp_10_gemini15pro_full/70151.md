fiasco:/fiˈæskoʊ/ (US), /fiˈæskəʊ/ (UK)| n. |惨败，彻底失败|A complete failure, especially in a ludicrous or humiliating way.|The opening night of the play was a total fiasco – the lead actor forgot his lines and the scenery collapsed.  这出戏的首演彻底失败了——男主角忘了台词，布景也倒塌了。|例句：The product launch was a fiasco due to poor planning. 产品发布会由于计划不周而以惨败告终。

例句：Her attempt to bake a cake for his birthday turned into a fiasco. 她试图为他生日烤蛋糕的尝试变成了一场灾难。|近义词：debacle, disaster, failure, flop, mess|反义词：success, triumph|词性: 名词
