naval:/ˈneɪvəl/| adj.|海军的，军舰的|Relating to a navy or warships.  Of or relating to ships; nautical.|例句：The country has a strong naval presence in the region. 这个国家在该地区拥有强大的海军力量。|例句：Naval warfare has changed dramatically over the centuries.  几个世纪以来，海战发生了巨大的变化。|近义词：maritime, nautical|反义词：land, air|词性：形容词
词源：From Old French *naval, from Latin nāvālis (“of or belonging to ships”), from nāvis (“ship”).
