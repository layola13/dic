materialize:/məˈtɪəriəlaɪz/ /məˈtɪriəlaɪz/| v. |实现，发生；突然出现|To become real or actual; to appear suddenly|例句：The promised funding failed to materialize.  承诺的资金未能兑现。

例句：Suddenly, a figure materialized out of the fog.  突然，一个身影从雾中出现。

近义词：appear, emerge, manifest, take shape|反义词:disappear, vanish, dematerialize|词性:动词
