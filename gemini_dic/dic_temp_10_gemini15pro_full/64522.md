eroticism:/ɪˈrɒtɪsɪzəm/ /ɪˈrɑːtɪsɪzəm/| n. |情色，性欲|The depiction of eroticism in art has a long and complex history.  艺术中对情色的描绘有着悠久而复杂的历史。|例句：The film is full of eroticism. 这部电影充满了情色意味。

例句：The novel explores themes of love and eroticism. 这部小说探讨了爱情和情色的主题。|近义词：sensuality, sexuality, lust|反义词：asceticism, prudery|词性: noun

词源: From French *érotisme*, from Ancient Greek *ἐρωτικός (erōtikós, “of love, amatory”)*, from *ἔρως (érōs, “love, sexual love”)*. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₁er- (“to excite; to set in motion”)*.
