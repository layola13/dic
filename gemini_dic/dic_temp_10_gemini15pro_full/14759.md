awing:/ˈɔːɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人敬畏的，使人惊叹的 | Inspiring awe; impressive. | The awing sight of the Grand Canyon left me speechless. 大峡谷令人敬畏的景象让我 speechless。|  The performance was absolutely awing.  这场表演绝对令人叹为观止。| synonyms: impressive, magnificent, breathtaking, stunning, awe-inspiring, wondrous | antonyms: unimpressive, ordinary, mundane, boring, tedious | adjective

Etymology:  From awe + -ing.  "Awe" comes from Old Norse *agi, meaning "terror, dread." Over time, the meaning shifted from fear to a sense of wonder and reverence.
