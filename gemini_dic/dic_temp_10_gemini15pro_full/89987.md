horsiest:/ˈhɔːrsiɪst/
adjective
最像马的；最爱好骑马的；最粗俗的
Most like a horse; most interested in horses; most vulgar or coarse.

例句：
He had the horsiest laugh I'd ever heard.
他笑起来的声音是我听过最像马嘶的。

Of all her friends, she was the horsiest, always talking about riding and stables.
在她所有的朋友中，她最喜欢骑马，总是谈论骑马和马厩。


近义词：equestrian, equine, horse-loving, coarse, vulgar
反义词：refined, elegant, sophisticated

词性
adjective
