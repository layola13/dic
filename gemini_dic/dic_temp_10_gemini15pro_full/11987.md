artfulnesses:/ˈɑːrtfʊlnəsɪz/
n.
狡猾；诡诈；巧妙
The quality of being artful; skillful deception or craftiness.
Her artfulnesses were legendary, she could talk her way out of any situation. 她的狡猾是出了名的，她能用花言巧语摆脱任何困境。

He employed several artfulnesses to win the competition, some of which were borderline unethical. 他为了赢得比赛使出浑身解数，有些手段近乎不道德。

synonym: cunning, guile, craftiness, slyness, trickery
antonym: honesty, sincerity, forthrightness, ingenuousness, artlessness
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From artful + -ness.  Artful itself comes from art (meaning skill) + -ful. Ultimately from Latin *artem (skill).
