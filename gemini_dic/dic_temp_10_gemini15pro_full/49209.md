delectabilities:/dɪˌlɛktəˈbɪlɪtiz/| n.|美味佳肴；令人愉快的事物|The restaurant is known for its delectabilities. 这家餐厅以其美味佳肴而闻名。|English Explanation:  Plural form of "delectability," referring to things that are extremely pleasing, especially to the taste.  Can also refer to things that are delightful or highly enjoyable in a more general sense.|例句：The chef prepared a feast of delectabilities.  厨师准备了一桌美味佳肴。

例句：The book was filled with delectabilities of language and imagery. 这本书充满了语言和意象的魅力。|近义词：delicacies, pleasures, delights, treats|反义词：unpleasantries, displeasures|词性:noun
