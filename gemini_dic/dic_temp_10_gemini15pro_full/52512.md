differentiated:/ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃɪeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 区分的，分化的，有差别的 | made or become different in the process of growth or development |  The cells have differentiated into various types. 细胞已经分化成各种类型。|  The company offers a range of differentiated products. 这家公司提供一系列差异化的产品。| 近义词: distinct, separate, specialized | 反义词: undifferentiated, homogeneous | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin differentiatus, past participle of differentiare "to make different," from different-, differens "different" (see different).
