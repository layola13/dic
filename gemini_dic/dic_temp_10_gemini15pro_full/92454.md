hyporadius:/ˌhaɪpəˈreɪdiəs/ | n. |下半径|The hyporadius is the radius of the inscribed circle of a triangle.|The hyporadius of this triangle is 2 cm.  这个三角形的内切圆半径是2厘米。|近义词:inradius|反义词:circumradius|词性:noun

Etymology:  From Greek *hypo* ("under") + Latin *radius* ("ray, spoke of a wheel").
