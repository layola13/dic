doggerel:/ˈdɒɡərəl/ (US) /ˈdɑːɡərəl/| n. |打油诗，歪诗，蹩脚的诗|Crude, poorly written verse, often humorous or trivial.|Example: His attempts at poetry were mere doggerel. 他尝试写的诗只不过是打油诗。|Example: The greeting card was filled with sentimental doggerel. 贺卡上充满了感伤的歪诗。|Synonym: verse, rhyme, jingle, limerick|Antonym: poetry, sonnet, epic|noun
