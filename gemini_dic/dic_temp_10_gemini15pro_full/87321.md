herniation:/ˌhɜːrniˈeɪʃən/ | n. |疝；突出|The abnormal protrusion of an organ or other body structure through a defect or natural opening in a covering membrane, muscle, or bone.| The doctor diagnosed a herniation of the spinal disc. 医生诊断出椎间盘突出。| Near synonyms: protrusion, prolapse, extrusion | Antonym: reduction | noun

herniate:/ˈhɜːrnieɪt/ | v. |形成疝；突出|To protrude through an abnormal opening.| The intervertebral disc herniated, causing severe back pain.  椎间盘突出，导致严重的背痛。| Near synonyms: protrude, bulge, rupture | Antonym: reduce | verb
