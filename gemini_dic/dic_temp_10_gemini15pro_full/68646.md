far-reaching:/ˌfɑːrˈriːtʃɪŋ/
adjective
影响深远的；广泛的
extending or having influence far into the future; having wide-ranging consequences
例句：The discovery of penicillin had far-reaching consequences for medicine. 青霉素的发现对医学产生了深远的影响。

例句：The new policy will have far-reaching effects on the economy. 新政策将对经济产生广泛的影响。

近义词：widespread, extensive, broad, significant, impactful
反义词：limited, narrow, insignificant, trivial
词性: adjective

词源:  From far + reaching, literally 'reaching far'.
