hostess-ships:/ˈhoʊstɪs ʃɪps/
n.
女主人身份；女主人职责
The role or duties of a hostess, often in a social or official capacity.
She gracefully performed her hostess-ships at the embassy gala. 她优雅地在使馆晚会上履行女主人的职责。
近义词：hostess duties, hostessing
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps guest status)
词性: 名词

hostess:/ˈhoʊstɪs/
n.
女主人
A woman who receives or entertains guests.
The hostess greeted us warmly at the door. 女主人在门口热情地迎接我们。
近义词：host, server
反义词: guest
词性: 名词

ship:/ʃɪp/
n.
船；舰
A large boat for transporting people or goods by sea.
The ship sailed across the ocean.  这艘船航行穿过海洋。
近义词：vessel, boat
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: 名词
ship:/ʃɪp/
vt.
运送
To transport by ship or other means.
They shipped the goods overseas. 他们把货物运到海外。
近义词：transport, deliver
反义词: receive
词性: 动词
