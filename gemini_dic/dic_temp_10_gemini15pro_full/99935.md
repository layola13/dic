internity:/ɪnˈtɜːrnɪti/ | n. | 实习期，实习职位 |A period of time during which someone works as an intern, typically to gain work experience in a particular field.  | Example: She secured a summer internity at a prestigious law firm. 她 secured a summer internity at a prestigious law firm. | Synonyms: internship, placement, traineeship | Antonyms:  permanent position, full-time employment | Noun
