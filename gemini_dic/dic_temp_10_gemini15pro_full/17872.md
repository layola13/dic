beehive:/ˈbiːhaɪv/| n.|蜂箱；蜂窝；熙熙攘攘的地方|A beehive is a structure where bees live and make honey.  蜂箱是蜜蜂居住和制造蜂蜜的构造。|The marketplace was a beehive of activity. 市场像蜂窝一样熙熙攘攘。|近义词：apiary, colony|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑空巢 empty nest)|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *bēo-hīfe (“beehive”), equivalent to bee + hive.  来自古英语 *bēo-hīfe（“蜂箱”），相当于 bee + hive。
