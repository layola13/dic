greyscale:/ˈɡreɪskeɪl/| adj. n.|灰阶，灰度|A range of gray shades from white to black, used in printing and photography.  It refers to an image where each pixel is represented by a single sample, representing only an amount of light, that is, carrying only intensity information.|The photo was converted to greyscale.|近义词：grayscale, monochrome, black and white|反义词:color, polychrome|词性:adjective, noun
greyscale:/ˈɡreɪskeɪl/| n.|灰度|The quality of being greyscale.|The greyscale of the image was adjusted.|近义词：grayscale, monochrome|反义词:color, polychrome|词性:noun
