metagenetic:/ˌmɛtədʒəˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
世代交替的
Relating to or characterized by metagenesis, a type of life cycle in which a sexual reproduction alternates with an asexual reproduction.
Certain cnidarians exhibit a metagenetic life cycle, alternating between polyp and medusa forms.  某些刺胞动物表现出世代交替的生命周期，在水螅型和水母型之间交替。
The study of metagenetic jellyfish provides insights into the evolution of complex life cycles. 对世代交替的水母的研究为了解复杂生命周期的进化提供了见解。
近义词:alternating generations
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) direct development
词性:adjective
