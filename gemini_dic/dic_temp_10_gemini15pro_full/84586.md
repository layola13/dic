hamper:/ˈhæmpər/ (US), /ˈhæmpə(r)/ (UK)| v. |阻碍，妨碍|To hinder or impede the movement or progress of something or someone.|The heavy rain hampered our picnic plans.  大雨阻碍了我们的野餐计划。|Synonyms: hinder, obstruct, impede, restrict, inhibit|Antonyms: facilitate, aid, assist, help|verb

hamper:/ˈhæmpər/ (US), /ˈhæmpə(r)/ (UK)| n. |(有盖的)大篮子，食盒|A large basket with a lid, often used for carrying food or laundry.|We packed a hamper full of goodies for the picnic. 我们装满了好东西的篮子去野餐。|Synonyms: basket, container|Antonyms: N/A|noun
