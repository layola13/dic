freepost:/friːˈpəʊst/| adj. n.|免费邮寄的，免费邮寄；预付邮资|A system where the recipient of mail, rather than the sender, pays the postage.|Send your application form back in the freepost envelope provided. 把你的申请表放在提供的免费邮资信封里寄回。|近义词：prepaid, postage paid|反义词: collect, postage due|词性:形容词, 名词
freepost:/friːˈpəʊst/|词源: free + post| "free" meaning without cost and "post" referring to mail. It originated in the UK.|用法: Used to describe mail that the recipient pays for, not the sender. Often seen on business reply envelopes.|例句：I sent my competition entry via freepost. 我通过免费邮寄的方式寄送了我的参赛作品。|例句：The company provided a freepost address for customer feedback. 公司提供了一个免费邮寄地址用于客户反馈。
