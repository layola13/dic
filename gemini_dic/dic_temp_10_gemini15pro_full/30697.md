certification:/ˌsɜːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. | 认证，证明 | The act of certifying or the state of being certified.  A document attesting to the truth of something. |  She received her teaching certification last year. 她去年获得了教师资格证书。| accreditation, license, diploma | denial, revocation | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *certificationem, from certificare "to certify," from Latin certus "certain" + facere "to make."
