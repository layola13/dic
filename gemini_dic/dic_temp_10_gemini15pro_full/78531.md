ghostest:/ˈɡoʊstɪɪst/ | adj. | 最像鬼的；最幽灵般的 |  This is the ghostest place I've ever been. 这是我去过的最像鬼的地方。| 例句：The old house was the ghostest on the street. 这栋老房子是街上最闹鬼的。| 近义词：spookiest, eeriest | 反义词：brightest, cheeriest | 词性：形容词

ghostest is the superlative form of "ghostly."  "Ghostly" is derived from "ghost" + "-ly."  "Ghost" comes from Old English "gast" meaning "spirit, soul."  The "-ly" suffix transforms a noun into an adjective, indicating resemblance or characteristic.
