nontopographical: /ˌnɒntɒpəˈɡræfɪkəl/ /ˌnɑːntɑːpəˈɡræfɪkəl/ | adj. | 非地形学的，非地形图的 | Not related to or based on the physical features of a place. |  Example:  The artist's work is nontopographical, focusing on abstract shapes and colors. 艺术家的作品是非地形学的，专注于抽象的形状和颜色。 | Example:  The novel's setting is nontopographical, existing only in the author's imagination. 小说的背景是非地形学的，只存在于作者的想象中。 | Synonyms: abstract, conceptual, nonrepresentational | Antonyms: topographical, representational, geographic | word type: adjective
