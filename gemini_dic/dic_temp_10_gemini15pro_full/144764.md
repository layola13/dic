over-designs:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈzaɪnz/ | v. | 过度设计 | To design something in an excessively complex or elaborate way, often to the detriment of its functionality or usability. | The engineer over-designed the bridge, making it unnecessarily expensive and difficult to maintain. 工程师过度设计了这座桥，使其造价过高，维护困难。| The software was so over-designed that it was difficult for users to navigate.  这个软件设计过度，用户很难操作。 | over-engineer, over-complicate | simplify, streamline | verb
