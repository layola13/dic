nonschematically:/ˌnɒnˌskiːˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 非图式地，非概要地 | Not in a schematic or simplified way; giving all the details or specifics.  |  The witness described the events nonschematically, providing a complete and detailed account.  目击者非概要地描述了事件，提供了一个完整而详细的说明。 |  completely, thoroughly, comprehensively | schematically, briefly, summarily | adverb

Etymology:  Derived from "schematic" + "non-" (not) + "-ally" (adverbial suffix). "Schematic" comes from Greek "schēma" (form, figure).
