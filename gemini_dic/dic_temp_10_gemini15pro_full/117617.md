megapolis:/ˌmɛɡˈæpəlɪs/ | n. | 大都市；特大城市 | A very large city, typically one with a population of over ten million people. |  Tokyo is a sprawling megapolis. 东京是一个庞大的特大城市。

The Pearl River Delta megapolis is a major economic hub. 珠江三角洲大都市圈是一个重要的经济中心。

| 近义词：megalopolis, metropolis, cosmopolis | 反义词：village, hamlet, small town | 词性：noun


Etymology:  From Greek *megalo- "great" + polis "city."
