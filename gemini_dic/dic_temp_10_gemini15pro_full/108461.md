lay-by:/ˈleɪbaɪ/ (UK) /ˈleɪˌbaɪ/ (US)| n.|路边停车处；分期付款|A designated parking area by the side of a road, often for a short stop or rest; A system of paying for goods in installments, where the goods are kept by the seller until the final payment is made.|例句：We pulled into a lay-by to admire the view. 我们把车停在路边停车处欣赏风景。

例句：She bought the furniture on lay-by. 她用分期付款的方式买了家具。|近义词：layaway (US), parking bay, rest area|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "through road" for the parking aspect)|词性:noun


lay-by:/ˈleɪbaɪ/ (UK) /ˈleɪˌbaɪ/ (US)| vt.|将…放在一边；分期付款购买|To put something aside; To purchase something by paying in installments.|例句：He laid-by some money each week for his holiday. 他每周都存一些钱准备度假。

例句：They are laying-by a new car. 他们正在分期付款购买一辆新车。|近义词：put aside, save, purchase on layaway|反义词: spend, squander (for saving aspect)|词性:verb
