erastianism:/ɪˌræstiənɪzəm/
n.
伊拉斯提主义（主张国家控制教会）
The doctrine that the state should have supremacy over the Church in ecclesiastical matters.
The king's actions were seen as a clear example of Erastianism. 国王的举动被视为伊拉斯提主义的明显例子。
Erastian principles guided the government's policy towards the church. 伊拉斯提主义原则指导着政府的教会政策。
近义词: secularism, state control of religion
反义词: theocracy, clericalism
词性:noun

Etymology: From the name of Thomas Erastus (1524-1583), a Swiss theologian.
