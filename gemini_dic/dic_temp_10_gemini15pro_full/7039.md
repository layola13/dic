anecdotal: /ˌænɪkˈdəʊtl/ (UK) /ˌænɪkˈdoʊtl/ (US) | adj. |轶事的;趣闻的;传闻的|Based on personal accounts rather than facts or research; informal.|例句：Anecdotal evidence suggests that the medicine is effective.  传闻证据表明这种药有效。|例句：The book is full of amusing anecdotes about his childhood. 这本书里满是关于他童年趣事的轶事。|近义词：informal, casual, unscientific, hearsay|反义词：scientific, empirical, factual|词性: adjective

Etymology: From the Greek "anekdota," meaning "things unpublished," from "an-" (not) + "ekdotos" (published).
