heterogeneity:/ˌhɛtərədʒəˈniːɪti/
n.|异质性，多样性|The heterogeneity of the population presents challenges for healthcare providers.  人口的异质性给医疗保健提供者带来了挑战。|
heterogeneous:/ˌhɛtərəˈdʒiːniəs/
adj.|异质的，多种多样的|The city is a heterogeneous mix of cultures and ethnicities. 这座城市是文化和种族的异质混合体。|例句：The study sample was heterogeneous, including people of different ages, backgrounds, and health conditions.  研究样本是异质的，包括不同年龄、背景和健康状况的人。|近义词：diversity, variety, dissimilarity, difference|反义词：homogeneity, uniformity, similarity|词性：形容词

heterogeneousness:/ˌhɛtərəˈdʒiːniəsnəs/
n.|异质性，多样性|The heterogeneousness of the data made it difficult to analyze. 数据的异质性使其难以分析。|例句：The heterogeneousness of the student body enriches the learning environment. 学生群体的异质性丰富了学习环境。|近义词：heterogeneity, diversity, variety|反义词：homogeneity, uniformity|词性：名词


词源: From Ancient Greek  ἕτερος (héteros, “other, different”) + γένος (génos, “kind, race, stock”)  + -ity.
