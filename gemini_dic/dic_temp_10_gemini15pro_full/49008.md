degrade:/dɪˈɡreɪd/| v. |降低；贬低；使降解|To lower in rank, status, or character; to reduce the quality of something.|例句：The scandal degraded the politician's reputation.  这个丑闻损害了政治家的名誉。|近义词：demote, diminish, cheapen, deteriorate, corrupt|反义词: elevate, enhance, improve, upgrade|词性:动词
degrade:/dɪˈɡreɪd/| v. |（使）降解；（使）分解|To break down or decompose (a substance) chemically.|例句：Plastic degrades very slowly in the environment. 塑料在环境中降解非常缓慢。|近义词：decompose, break down, rot|反义词: synthesize, build up|词性:动词
