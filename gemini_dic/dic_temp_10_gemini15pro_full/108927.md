leeches:/ˈliːtʃɪz/
leech:/liːtʃ/ | n. |水蛭；吸血鬼；榨取他人钱财的人|A bloodsucking worm that lives in water.  Figuratively, someone who takes advantage of others, especially financially. |Example 1: Leeches were once commonly used in medicine for bloodletting. 水蛭曾经在医学上普遍用于放血。

Example 2: He's a real leech, always borrowing money and never paying it back. 他真是个吸血鬼，总是借钱不还。|近义词：parasite, bloodsucker, freeloader |反义词: benefactor, donor |词性: noun (名词)
