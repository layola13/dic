mamba:/ˈmɑːmbə/ /ˈmæmbə/| n.|非洲树眼镜蛇；曼巴蛇|A venomous snake of the genus *Dendroaspis*, native to Africa and among the most venomous in the world. These snakes are known for their agility and aggressiveness.|例句：The black mamba is one of the deadliest snakes in Africa. 黑曼巴蛇是非洲最致命的蛇类之一。

例句：The mamba struck with lightning speed. 曼巴蛇以闪电般的速度发起攻击。|近义词：snake, serpent|反义词:|noun
