hundredths:/ˈhʌndrədθs/| n.| 百分之几 | Representing one part out of one hundred equal parts.  It is the plural form of "hundredth". |  0.03 can be read as three hundredths. 0.03可以读作百分之三。 | 近义词: percent, per cent | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


Etymology:
From Middle English *hundredethe, from Old English *hundrēoða, from hund (hundred) + -eða (ordinal suffix).  The -s ending marks the plural form.
