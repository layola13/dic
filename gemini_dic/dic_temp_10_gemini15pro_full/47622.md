debian:/ˈdeːbiən/| n.|Debian（操作系统）|A popular distribution of the Linux operating system, known for its emphasis on free software and its large software repository.|I installed Debian on my old laptop. 我在我的旧笔记本电脑上安装了 Debian。|近义词：Linux, Ubuntu, Fedora|反义词:Windows, macOS|词性:名词

词源:
The name "Debian" is a portmanteau of the names of its founder, Ian Bracey, and his then-girlfriend, Debra Lynn.  It combines "Deb" from Debra and "Ian" from Ian.
