creamy:/ˈkriːmi/| adj.|奶油的，乳脂的；柔滑的，细腻的|Smooth and rich like cream; containing a lot of cream.|The cake had a creamy frosting.  蛋糕上有一层奶油糖霜。|Synonyms: smooth, rich, velvety, buttery|Antonyms: thin, watery, grainy|adjective

Etymology->
From cream + -y.  Ultimately from the Latin *chrēma* (thick ointment).
