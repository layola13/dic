interhemal:/ˌɪntərˈhiːməl/
adjective
血管间的，体腔间的
Situated between blood vessels or coelomic cavities.
The interhemal spaces contain a loose connective tissue. 血管间的空间包含疏松的结缔组织。
The interhemal membrane separates the coelomic cavities. 体腔间膜分隔体腔。
近义词: intervascular, intercoelomic
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider terms like "intravascular" or "intracoelomic" for opposing locations)
词性: adjective
