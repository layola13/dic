hormogonium:/ˌhɔːrmoʊˈɡoʊniəm/| n. |藻殖段；连锁体|A motile filament of cells formed by some cyanobacteria, which detaches from the parent filament and glides away.  It is a means of asexual reproduction.|The hormogonia dispersed throughout the water column, colonizing new areas.|Synonyms: filament, trichome (in some contexts)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
