microscopists:/maɪˈkrɒskəpɪsts/
n.
显微镜学家；显微镜工作者
People who use microscopes to study small objects or structures.
Microscopists made significant contributions to our understanding of cells. 显微镜学家对我们理解细胞做出了重大贡献。
Microscopists are examining the tissue samples. 显微镜学家正在检查组织样本。
