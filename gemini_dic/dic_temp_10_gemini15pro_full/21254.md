blood-brother:/ˈblʌdˌbrʌðər/| n.|歃血为盟的兄弟，结拜兄弟|A male related to another by a ritual involving the mingling of blood, symbolic of brotherhood.|例句：They became blood-brothers as teenagers, pledging eternal loyalty. 他们十几岁时结为歃血为盟的兄弟，发誓永远忠诚。

例句：The two boys made a pact and became blood-brothers.  这两个男孩立下誓约，结为异姓兄弟。

|近义词：sworn brother, close friend|反义词:enemy, foe|词性:noun
