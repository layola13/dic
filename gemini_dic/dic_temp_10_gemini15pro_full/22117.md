bolster:/ˈboʊlstər/ (US) /ˈbəʊlstə(r)/ (UK)| n. v. |靠垫，枕垫；支持，支撑|A long, thick pillow that is placed under other pillows for support.  To support or strengthen.|
The soft bolster cushioned her head.  柔软的枕垫支撑着她的头部。
New economic data bolstered the president's claims. 新的经济数据支持了总统的说法。
|近义词:cushion, support, reinforce, strengthen|反义词:undermine, weaken|词性:名词, 动词
