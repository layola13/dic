monologists: /məˈnɒlədʒɪsts/ /məˈnɑːlədʒɪsts/
n.
独白者；擅长独白的人；进行独白表演的演员
A person who delivers a monologue, especially a long dramatic speech; a person who talks excessively about one subject.
The comedian was a skilled monologist, captivating the audience with his witty observations.  这位喜剧演员是一位技艺精湛的独白者，用他机智的观察力吸引了观众。
She’s such a monologist, she never lets anyone else get a word in. 她真是个独白者，从来不让别人插嘴。
近义词:solo performer, speaker, raconteur
反义词:listener, conversationalist
词性:名词
