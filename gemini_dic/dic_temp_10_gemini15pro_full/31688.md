chaunterie:/ˈtʃɔːntəri/ /ˈtʃæntəri/| n.|捐献的土地；唱诗班；唱诗班的经费|An endowment or grant of land or other revenues for the maintenance of a priest or priests to sing masses for the souls of the donor or others designated by the donor.  A building, often a chapel, endowed for such services.|The king established a chaunterie for the souls of his parents.|近义词：chapel, chantry|反义词:|名词
