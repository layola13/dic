leel: /liːl/
n.
诡计，骗局
A cunning or deceitful action or plan.
He used a leel to get her to sign the contract. 他用诡计让她签了合同。

近义词: trick, ruse, ploy, scheme, stratagem
反义词: honesty, forthrightness
词性: 名词

词源:  Likely a variant of "wile," from Old English "wigle," meaning "trick" or "stratagem." Related to Old High German "wihhila" and Old Norse "vél."
