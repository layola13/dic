guenon:/ɡəˈnɒn/ | n. |长尾猴; 弥猴|A small, long-tailed monkey of West Africa, typically brightly coloured. | 例句：The guenon swung effortlessly through the trees. 长尾猴轻松地在树间荡来荡去。

例句：The zoo had a variety of guenons, each with unique markings.  动物园里有各种各样的长尾猴，每只都有独特的斑纹。

近义词：monkey, primate |反义词:|词性：noun
