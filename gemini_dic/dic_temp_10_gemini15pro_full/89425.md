homunculus:/hɒˈmʌŋkjʊləs/ /hɑːˈmʌŋkjʊləs/| n. |侏儒；小人；矮人；（炼金术）人造人|A very small human or humanoid creature; a miniature representation of a human being.  It is often used in literature and philosophy to explore themes of creation, control, and the nature of consciousness.|She imagined a tiny homunculus living inside her head, controlling her thoughts and actions. 她想象一个微小的侏儒住在她的脑子里，控制着她的思想和行为。|Synonyms: dwarf, midget, pygmy, manikin|Antonyms: giant|noun

homunculus:/hɒˈmʌŋkjʊləs/ /hɑːˈmʌŋkjʊləs/| n. |（尤指神经学中）矮人模型|In neurology, a distorted representation of the human body, based on the proportion of the brain devoted to processing motor or sensory functions for different parts of the body.|The sensory homunculus shows how much brain area is dedicated to each part of the body.  感觉侏儒模型显示了大脑有多少区域专门用于身体的每个部位。|Synonyms:  manikin|Antonyms: N/A|noun
