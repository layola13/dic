hostel: /ˈhɒst(ə)l/ (British) /ˈhɑːstl/ (American) | n. | 旅舍，招待所 | A supervised lodging place for young travelers or other groups on bicycle tours, walking tours, etc. |  Example: We stayed in a hostel during our backpacking trip through Europe.  我们在欧洲背包旅行时住在一家旅舍。

Example: The hostel provides basic accommodation at a low price. 这家旅舍提供价格低廉的基本住宿。

近义词: inn, lodge, guesthouse, backpackers' | 反义词: hotel, resort | 词性: noun
