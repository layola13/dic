demand:/dɪˈmænd/ /dɪˈmænd/| n. vt.|需求，要求|A strong request or an insistent and peremptory claim for something. |The company couldn't meet the heavy demand for the new product. 这家公司无法满足对新产品的巨大需求。|近义词：request, require, claim, need|反义词:supply, offer|noun
demand:/dɪˈmænd/ /dɪˈmænd/| vt.|要求，需要|To ask for something firmly and forcefully. |The teacher demanded that the students be quiet. 老师要求学生们安静。|近义词：request, require, insist|反义词:offer, give|verb
