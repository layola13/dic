imboil:/ɪmˈbɔɪl/ | v. | 浸泡，煮沸 | To cause to boil; to heat to a boiling state. Especially to make syrupy by boiling. | The chef imboiled the fruit in sugar syrup.  厨师将水果浸在糖浆中煮沸。| 近义词: boil, simmer, stew | 反义词: cool, freeze | verb
