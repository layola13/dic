cuckolding: /ˈkʌkˌoʊldɪŋ/ | n. | 戴绿帽子; 妻子与他人通奸（指丈夫）| The act of a man's wife having sex with another man, especially when the husband is aware of it or is humiliated by it.  It derives from the cuckoo bird, which lays its eggs in other birds' nests. | He suspected his wife of cuckolding him. 他怀疑妻子给他戴绿帽子。|  Synonyms: infidelity, adultery, cheating | Antonyms: fidelity, faithfulness | noun

cuckold: /ˈkʌkˌoʊld/ | n. vt.| 被戴绿帽子的男人; 使戴绿帽子 | A man whose wife has sex with another man, especially without his knowledge or consent; To make a cuckold of (a man). |  He was a cuckold and everyone knew it except him.  他是个戴绿帽子的男人，除了他自己大家都知道。 / She cuckolded her husband with his best friend. 她和丈夫最好的朋友通奸，使他戴绿帽子。 | Synonyms: cheated husband, deceived husband (n.);  dishonor, shame (vt.) | Antonyms: faithful husband (n.); honor (vt.) | noun, verb
