cumin:/ˈkjuːmɪn/ (US), /ˈkʌmɪn/ (UK)| n. |孜然，枯茗|A small, crescent-shaped seed of an annual plant (Cuminum cyminum) in the parsley family, used as a spice, especially ground or roasted.|I added cumin and coriander to the stew. 我在炖菜里加了孜然和芫荽。|Synonyms: comino, jeera|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
