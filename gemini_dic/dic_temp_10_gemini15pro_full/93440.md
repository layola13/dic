illuminations:/ɪˌluːmɪˈneɪʃənz/
n.
照明；阐明；装饰彩灯
the lighting of something
例句：The illuminations of the festival created a magical atmosphere. 节日的彩灯营造了一种神奇的氛围。

The book provides illuminations on the history of the region. 这本书阐明了该地区的历史。
近义词：lights, decorations, clarification, elucidation
反义词：darkness, obscurity
词性：名词

illumination:/ɪˌluːmɪˈneɪʃən/
n.
照明；阐明；装饰彩灯
An act of illuminating or state of being illuminated.
例句：The street illuminations were beautiful during the holidays. 假日期间的街道照明很漂亮。
The teacher's explanation provided illumination on the difficult subject. 老师的解释阐明了这个难题。

近义词：lighting, brightness, enlightenment, insight
反义词：darkness, ignorance
词性：名词
