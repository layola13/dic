cafeneh: /ˌkæfɪˈneɪ/ | n. | 咖啡馆；咖啡店 | A small, informal restaurant serving coffee, tea, and light refreshments.  It often has a relaxed and social atmosphere, sometimes with outdoor seating. It originates from the Arabic word "qahveh khaneh," meaning "coffee house." |  I met my friend at the cafeneh for a chat. 我和朋友在咖啡馆见面聊天。|  The cafeneh offers a variety of pastries and sandwiches. 这家咖啡馆提供各种各样的糕点和三明治。 | synonyms: coffee shop, cafe, bistro | antonyms: restaurant, bar | noun
