decivilizing:/diːˈsɪvɪˌlaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
使不文明的；使野蛮化的；使腐化的
Making someone or something less civilized; causing a decline in moral or social standards.
The decivilizing effects of war are devastating. 战争的野蛮化效应是毁灭性的。
The constant exposure to violence has a decivilizing effect on children.  持续接触暴力对儿童有腐蚀作用。
近义词:barbarizing, brutalizing, corrupting, degrading
反义词:civilizing, refining, enlightening, cultivating
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From de- (prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove") + civilizing (present participle of civilize, from civil + -ize).  Ultimately from Latin *civis* "citizen".
