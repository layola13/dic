betrust:/bɪˈtrʌst/| vt.|委托；交付；托付|To give something to someone to look after or deal with; to place trust or confidence in someone.|例句：He betrusted his savings to a friend. 他把积蓄托付给一位朋友。|例句：She betrusted him with the secret. 她把秘密托付给他。|近义词：entrust, confide, commit|反义词:distrust, suspect|verb
