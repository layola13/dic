agadmator:/ˌæɡədˈmɑːtɔːr/ | n. | 国际象棋评论员，尤指在YouTube上进行评论的人 | A chess commentator, especially one who provides commentary on YouTube. |  Agadmator is a popular chess YouTuber.  阿加德马托是一位受欢迎的国际象棋YouTube主播。 |  synonyms: chess commentator, analyst, streamer | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "chess player" as a contrast) | noun

Etymology:  The name "agadmator" is a combination of "agad" (Serbo-Croatian for "right away") and "animator."  The creator chose this name because he aimed to provide immediate analysis of chess games.
