fortuitous:/fɔːrˈtjuːɪtəs/| adj.|偶然的，意外的；幸运的|Happening by accident or chance rather than design; lucky.|例句：It was fortuitous that we met the CEO at the conference.  我们在会议上遇到CEO真是太巧了。

例句：The timing of her arrival was fortuitous, as it allowed her to witness the historic event. 她的到达时间很巧，让她得以见证这一历史性事件。|近义词：lucky, fortunate, accidental, serendipitous, incidental|反义词:intentional, deliberate, planned, unlucky, unfortunate|词性:adjective
