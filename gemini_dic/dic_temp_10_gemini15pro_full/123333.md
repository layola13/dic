monogynian: /ˌmɒnəˈdʒɪniən/ /ˌmɑːnəˈdʒɪniən/
| adj. | 一雌的；单雌蕊的 | Having only one carpel or pistil (in a flower) or one queen (in a social insect colony). |  Some species of ants are monogynian, meaning their colonies have only one queen.  一些种类的蚂蚁是一雌的，这意味着它们的群体只有一只蚁后。|  The flower is monogynian, with a single pistil in the center.  这朵花是单雌蕊的，中心只有一个雌蕊。 |  monocarpous (referring to a single fruit from one flower)  | polygynian (having multiple carpels/pistils or queens) | adjective
