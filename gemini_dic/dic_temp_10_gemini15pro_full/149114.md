oxgang:/ˈɒksɡæŋ/ | n. | 牛耕地;  (旧时)  约等于15英亩的耕地面积 | A measure of land, usually about 15 acres, that could be plowed by a team of oxen in a year.  |  The farmer owned two oxgangs of land.  这位农民拥有两牛耕地。| synonyms: yoke, ploughland | antonyms: N/A | noun
