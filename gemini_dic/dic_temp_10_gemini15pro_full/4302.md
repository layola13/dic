aisling:/ˈæʃlɪŋ/ /ˈeɪʃlɪŋ/ | n. | 梦境；幻想，尤其是指美好的、充满希望的梦或幻想 | A vision, dream, or trance, especially a prophetic one or one of a blissful otherworld. | She had an aisling of a peaceful world. 她梦到一个和平的世界。|  Synonyms: vision, dream, reverie, fantasy | Antonyms: reality, nightmare | noun

aisling:/ˈæʃlɪŋ/ /ˈeɪʃlɪŋ/ | n. | (爱尔兰文学)一种诗歌类型，通常描述诗人在梦中或幻境中与一位美丽的女子相遇，这位女子象征着爱尔兰 | A genre of Irish poetry, usually describing a poet's encounter in a dream or vision with a beautiful woman who symbolizes Ireland. | He wrote an aisling lamenting the state of the nation. 他写了一首哀悼国家现状的爱尔兰式诗歌。| Synonyms: poem, verse | Antonyms: prose | noun
