drenchingly:/ˈdrɛntʃɪŋli/
adverb
湿透地；淋漓尽致地
so thoroughly wet as to soak or saturate
The rain fell drenchingly. 雨下得很大，把一切都淋透了。
Synonyms: soakingly, soppingly, thoroughly, extremely
Antonyms: dryly, slightly, aridly
词性: 副词
