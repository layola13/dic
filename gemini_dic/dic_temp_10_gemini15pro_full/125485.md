multiprogram:/ˈmʌltiˌproʊɡræm/ /ˈmʌltiˌprəʊɡræm/| n. |多道程序设计|The ability of a computer to execute more than one program concurrently by rapidly switching between them.|Modern operating systems use multiprogramming to increase CPU utilization. 现代操作系统使用多道程序设计来提高 CPU 利用率。|Synonym: multitasking, concurrent programming|Antonym: single-programming|noun

multiprogramming:/ˌmʌltiˈproʊɡræmɪŋ/ /ˌmʌltiˈprəʊɡræmɪŋ/| n. |多道程序设计|The technique of keeping several programs in main memory at the same time, competing for the CPU.|Multiprogramming allows for efficient use of system resources. 多道程序设计允许有效地利用系统资源。|Synonym: multitasking, concurrent programming|Antonym: uniprogramming|noun
