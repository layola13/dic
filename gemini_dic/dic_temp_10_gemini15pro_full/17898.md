beeper's:/ˈbiːpərz/
n.
寻呼机的，传呼机的
belonging to a beeper; of or relating to a beeper.
The beeper's incessant chirping annoyed everyone in the meeting.  这台寻呼机不停的响声惹恼了会议室里的每个人。
近义词:pager's
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "cell phone's" in a modern context)
词性:noun, possessive form
