committeeism:/kəˈmɪtiˌɪzəm/| n. |委员会主义；委员会统治；（尤指）效率低下的委员会管理|A system of administration or decision-making characterized by the excessive use of committees, often resulting in inefficiency, delays, and compromise solutions.|The project was bogged down by committeeism; decisions took forever. 这个项目陷入了委员会主义的泥潭，决策旷日持久。|近义词：bureaucracy, red tape|反义词：individual initiative, decisive leadership|词性:名词

Etymology: committee + -ism.  "committee" (from 1610s) + "-ism" (a suffix forming nouns of action, practice, state, condition, doctrine). The term "committeeism" itself seems to date from the mid-19th century.
