doublethreaded:/ˈdʌbəlˈθrɛdɪd/
adjective
双线的；双股的
Having two threads or strands twisted together.
This rope is doublethreaded for extra strength. 这根绳子是双股的，强度更高。
The fabric is doublethreaded for durability.  这种织物是双股线织成的，非常耐用。
近义词:double-stranded, two-ply
反义词:single-threaded, single-strand
词性:adjective
