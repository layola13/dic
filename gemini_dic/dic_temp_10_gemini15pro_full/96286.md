indifferent: /ɪnˈdɪfrənt/ | adj. |漠不关心的，冷淡的；无关紧要的| Having no particular interest or sympathy; unconcerned.|  He seemed indifferent to the news. 他似乎对这个消息漠不关心。|  The outcome of the game was indifferent to me.  比赛结果对我来说无关紧要。| synonyms: apathetic, uninterested, unconcerned, aloof | antonyms: interested, concerned, passionate, enthusiastic | adjective

indifferent: /ɪnˈdɪfrənt/ | adj. |中等的，一般的|  Neither good nor bad; mediocre. | The food was indifferent. 食物很一般。 | The movie received indifferent reviews. 这部电影得到的评价褒贬不一。| synonyms: mediocre, average, ordinary | antonyms: excellent, exceptional, outstanding | adjective
