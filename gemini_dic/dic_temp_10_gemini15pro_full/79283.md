glaucometer:/ɡlɔːˈkɒmɪtər/ | n. |青光眼计，眼压计 | An instrument used to measure intraocular pressure (IOP), the fluid pressure inside the eye, which is important in the diagnosis and management of glaucoma. | Example 1: The ophthalmologist used a glaucometer to check the patient's IOP. 眼科医生使用青光眼计检查病人的眼内压。

Example 2: Regular glaucometer readings are essential for monitoring glaucoma progression. 定期使用青光眼计测量对于监测青光眼进展至关重要。 | Synonym: tonometer | Antonym: N/A | Noun
