mis-fielding: /ˌmɪsˈfiːldɪŋ/
noun
失误；接球失误
An act of fielding a ball badly in a sport such as cricket or baseball.
The outfielder's mis-fielding cost them the game. 外野手的接球失误让他们输掉了比赛。
misplay, error, fumble
good fielding, clean fielding
n.
