non-disclosure:/ˌnɒn dɪˈskləʊʒər/ /ˌnɑːn dɪˈskloʊʒər/ | n. |保密；不泄露；保密协议|A legal agreement in which a person or organization promises to keep certain information secret. |例句：The company required all employees to sign a non-disclosure agreement.  公司要求所有员工签署保密协议。

例句：The non-disclosure of this information is critical to the success of the project. 对此信息的保密对项目的成功至关重要。

|近义词：confidentiality, secrecy, silence|反义词：disclosure, revelation|词性:名词
