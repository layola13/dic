canoodle:/kəˈnuːdl/ /kæˈnuːdl/
verb
搂抱爱抚；亲热
To engage in affectionate cuddling, kissing, and other forms of gentle intimacy.
The young couple canoodled on the park bench. 这对年轻情侣在公园的长椅上搂抱亲热。
They were spotted canoodling in a darkened movie theater. 有人看到他们在昏暗的电影院里亲热。
Synonyms: cuddle, snuggle, caress, fondle, neck, pet
Antonyms:  fight, argue, separate, ignore
词性: 动词
