conveyable:/kənˈveɪəbl/
adjective
可传达的，可表达的
able to be conveyed or communicated
Her feelings were not easily conveyable in words. 她的感受很难用言语表达。
近义词: communicable, expressible, transferable
反义词: incommunicable, inexpressible
