cabbalahs:/ˌkæbəˈlɑːz/
n.
卡巴拉，犹太神秘主义哲学; 卡巴拉教义
esoteric Jewish mystical tradition; the body of doctrines of Jewish mysticism
He studied the ancient cabbalahs. 他研究古代卡巴拉。
近义词: mysticism, esotericism
反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑理性主义 rationalism)
词性: noun


cabbalah:/ˌkæbəˈlɑː/
n.
卡巴拉，犹太神秘主义哲学; 卡巴拉教义
esoteric Jewish mystical tradition; the body of doctrines of Jewish mysticism
She was fascinated by the cabbalah. 她被卡巴拉深深吸引。
近义词: mysticism, esotericism
反义词: (无直接反义词，可以考虑理性主义 rationalism)
词性: noun


词源:  From Late Hebrew קַבָּלָה (qabālāh, “received lore, tradition”), from קִבֵּל (qibbēl, “to receive, accept”). Ultimately from Akkadian *kabālu.
