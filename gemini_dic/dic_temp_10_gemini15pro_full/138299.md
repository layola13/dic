notchier: /ˈnɑːtʃɪər/ (US) /ˈnɒtʃɪə(r)/ (UK) | adj. | 有更多缺口的；更显著的；更有特色的 | Having more notches or indentations; more pronounced or distinctive. | The new design is notchier and more modern. 新的设计更有特色，更现代。|  The knife had a notchier blade than the other one. 这把刀的刀刃比另一把有更多缺口。 | 近义词: more jagged, more serrated, more distinct, more pronounced | 反义词: smoother, less distinct, less pronounced | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From notch + -ier (comparative adjective suffix).  "Notch" comes from Old English *nocc, meaning "groove, indentation."
