nonanswers:/ˌnɑːnˈænsərz/ |n.| 无效回答；不作答；回避|Responses that do not directly address the question asked, or are evasive.|The politician gave a series of nonanswers when asked about the scandal.  这位政客在被问及丑闻时，给出了一系列无效回答。|Synonyms: evasions, hedging, equivocations|Antonyms: answers, responses, replies|Noun

nonanswer:/ˌnɑːnˈænsər/ |n.| 无效回答；不作答；回避|A single response that does not directly address the question asked, or is evasive.|His nonanswer made me suspect he was hiding something. 他的不作答让我怀疑他在隐瞒什么。|Synonyms: evasion, hedging, equivocation|Antonyms: answer, response, reply|Noun
