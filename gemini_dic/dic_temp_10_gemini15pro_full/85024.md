harassing:/ˈhærəsɪŋ/ /həˈræsɪŋ/ | v. |骚扰；不断烦扰| subjecting to aggressive pressure or intimidation | The paparazzi were harassing the celebrity outside her home. 狗仔队在她家门外骚扰这位名人。|近义词：tormenting, bothering, pestering, badgering|反义词：comforting, soothing|词性:动词(present participle of harass)

harassment:/ˈhærəsmənt/ /həˈræsmənt/ | n. |骚扰；不断烦扰| aggressive pressure or intimidation | She filed a complaint against her boss for sexual harassment. 她对老板提出了性骚扰的投诉。|近义词：persecution, intimidation, abuse|反义词：support, encouragement|词性:名词

词源: From Old French *harasser "to set a dog on," from *harer "to incite," perhaps of Germanic origin.
