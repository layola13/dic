libertarian:/ˌlɪbərˈtɛəriən/ | adj. n. |自由意志主义的；自由意志主义者 |  Advocating or based on the belief that individual freedom and autonomy are paramount.  |  He's a staunch libertarian who believes in minimal government intervention. 他是一位坚定的自由意志主义者，信奉最小限度的政府干预。| synonyms: individualist, autonomist, self-governing | antonyms: authoritarian, statist, totalitarian | adjective

libertarian:/ˌlɪbərˈtɛəriən/ | n. |自由意志主义者 | A person who believes in the doctrine of free will. |  Many libertarians advocate for lower taxes and less regulation. 许多自由意志主义者主张降低税收和减少监管。 | synonyms: individualist, autonomist | antonyms: authoritarian, statist | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *libertaire*, from Latin *lībertās* ("liberty") + *-ārius*.  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\leydʰ-* (to grow, appear).
