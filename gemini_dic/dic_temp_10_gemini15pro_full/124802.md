muchachos:/muˈtʃatʃos/| n.|guys, boys, lads, fellows (informal, often used with affection)|A Spanish word meaning "boys," "guys," or "lads." Often used informally and can convey a sense of camaraderie or affection. |例句：¡Hola, muchachos! How are you doing?|你好，伙计们！你们好吗？

例句：The muchachos were playing football in the park.|那些小伙子们正在公园里踢足球。|近义词：guys, boys, lads, fellows, dudes|反义词:girls, women|词性:Noun

注意事项:
词源: From Spanish, ultimately from Vulgar Latin *multos catsos "many lads," from Latin multus "many" + cattos "lads" (related to catulus "puppy").  This evolved over time to the modern Spanish "muchachos."
