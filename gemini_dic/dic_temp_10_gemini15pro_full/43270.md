craftily:/ˈkræftɪli/ | adv. | 狡猾地，巧妙地 | In a clever or skillful way, especially in order to deceive someone. | He craftily avoided answering the question. 他巧妙地避开了这个问题。 | The thief craftily picked the lock. 小偷巧妙地撬开了锁。 | 近义词: cunningly, slyly, artfully, skillfully | 反义词: honestly, openly, clumsily, artlessly | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From craft + -ly.  "Craft" comes from Old English *cræft*, meaning "strength, skill, power." The suffix "-ly" transforms the noun "craft" into an adverb.
