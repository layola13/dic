cemeteries:/ˈsɛməˌtɛriz/ /ˈsɛməˌtɛris/
n.
墓地，坟场
A place where dead people are buried.
Many old cemeteries are full of history. 许多古老的墓地充满了历史。
The family visited their ancestors' graves in the cemetery. 这家人去墓地拜祭了祖先的坟墓。
graveyards, burial grounds
tomb
noun
