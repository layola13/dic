contagiously:/kənˈteɪdʒəsli/| adv.|传染性地；有感染力地|In a way that spreads quickly from one person or place to another.|Her enthusiasm was contagious, and soon everyone was laughing. 她的热情很有感染力，很快大家都笑了。|Example: Laughter is contagious. 笑声是会传染的。
Example: The virus spreads contagiously through the air. 这种病毒通过空气迅速传播。|Synonyms: infectiously, catching, spreading|Antonyms: non-contagious, uninfectious|Word type: adverb

contagious:/kənˈteɪdʒəs/| adj.|传染性的；有感染力的|Able to be passed from one person or animal to another by close contact.|The flu is a contagious disease. 流感是一种传染病。|Example:  Measles is a highly contagious illness. 麻疹是一种高度传染性的疾病。
Example:  His good mood was contagious. 他的好心情很有感染力。|Synonyms: infectious, communicable, transmissible|Antonyms: non-contagious, noninfectious|Word type: adjective
