overingenuity:/ˌōvərˌɪndʒəˈnjuːɪti/| n. |过度巧妙; 过于精巧; 过分精巧|Excessive or misplaced ingenuity; cleverness that is more complicated than necessary.| The overingenuity of the plot made the story unbelievable.  情节的过度巧妙使得故事难以置信。|近义词：overelaboration, oversubtlety, sophistication|反义词:simplicity, artlessness|noun
