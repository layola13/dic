outequivocate:/aʊtɪˈkwɪvəkeɪt/
verb
用模棱两可的话胜过；用双关语胜过
to surpass in equivocation; to outdo in the use of ambiguous language
He outequivocated the politician with his masterful use of double meanings. 他以巧妙地运用双关语，在含糊其辞方面胜过了这位政客。
近义词:outwit, outsmart, outmaneuver
反义词:be straightforward, be direct
