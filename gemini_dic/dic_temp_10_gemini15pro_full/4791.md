algidness:/ˈældʒɪdnəs/
noun
寒冷，冰冷
A state of coldness, especially of the skin; chilliness.
His algidness was a symptom of the advancing illness. 他的冰冷是疾病加重的症状。
近义词:chilliness, coldness, frigidity
反义词:warmth, heat
词性:noun

Etymology:
From Latin algidus ("cold, chilly"), from algēre ("to be cold").
