imposable:/ɪmˈpoʊzəbl/ | adj. | 可施加的，可强加的 | Capable of being imposed; that may be laid on as a charge, tax, duty, or penalty. | The government imposed a new tax, demonstrating that the tax was imposable. 政府征收了一项新税，表明该税是可征收的。|  The new rule is imposable on all employees. 这项新规定适用于所有员工。 | synonyms: chargeable, levyable, taxable | antonyms: unchargeable, non-taxable | adjective
