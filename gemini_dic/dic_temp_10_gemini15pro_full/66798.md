expanded:/ɪkˈspændɪd/ | /ɪkˈspændəd/ | v. | 扩展，扩大，展开 (expand的过去式和过去分词) |  Made larger or more extensive. | They expanded the house by adding a new wing. 他们通过增加新的侧翼扩建了房子。 | synonyms: enlarged, widened, broadened, stretched, extended | antonyms: contracted, shrunk, reduced, diminished | verb
