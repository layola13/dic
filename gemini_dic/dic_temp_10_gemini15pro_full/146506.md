overgeneralization:/ˌoʊvərˌdʒɛnərələˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 过度概括，过分概括 | The process of extending the application of a rule or observation to cases where it does not apply. |  Example:  He made an overgeneralization about women based on his limited experience. 他根据有限的经验对女性做出了过度概括。| Synonym: overextension, sweeping generalization | Antonym:  specific instance, nuanced understanding | noun
