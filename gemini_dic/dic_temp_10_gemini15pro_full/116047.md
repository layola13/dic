martialism:/ˈmɑːrʃəlɪzəm/ | n. | 军国主义，尚武精神 | A belief or policy that emphasizes military preparedness and strength, often prioritizing military values and goals over other societal concerns.  | Example: The country's growing martialism led to increased military spending.  这个国家的军国主义日益增长，导致军事开支增加。| Example:  Many historians attribute the outbreak of the war to rampant martialism. 许多历史学家将战争的爆发归咎于猖獗的军国主义。 |  Synonyms: militarism, bellicosity, warmongering | Antonyms: pacifism, peacefulness | noun
