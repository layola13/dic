overtroubled:/ˌoʊvərˈtrʌbəld/
adjective
过度烦恼的；过于忧虑的
Excessively troubled; burdened with worry or anxiety.
Her overtroubled mind couldn't focus on the task at hand. 她过度烦恼的头脑无法集中精力于手头的任务。
The overtroubled nation longed for peace.  这个饱受困扰的国家渴望和平。

近义词: distressed, anxious, worried, distraught
反义词: calm, peaceful, serene
词性: adjective
