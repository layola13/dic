ditzy:/ˈdɪtsi/| adj.|轻浮的；傻气的；糊涂的|Silly, scatterbrained, and forgetful.|She's a bit ditzy sometimes and forgets things easily. 她有时有点傻，很容易忘事。|近义词：flighty, silly, airheaded, scatterbrained|反义词：serious, sensible, practical|词性:adjective
