antidemocracy:/ˌænti-dɪˈmɒkrəsi/ /ˌænti-dɪˈmɑːkrəsi/
n.|反民主；反民主主义|Opposition to democracy; principles or practices opposed to democracy.|The rise of antidemocracy is a worrying trend. 反民主主义的抬头是一个令人担忧的趋势.|Near synonyms: authoritarianism, totalitarianism, despotism, tyranny|Near antonyms: democracy,  republicanism, self-government|noun

antidemocracy:/ˌænti-dɪˈmɒkrəsi/ /ˌænti-dɪˈmɑːkrəsi/
adj.|反民主的|Opposed to or hostile towards democracy.|His antidemocracy views were not well received. 他的反民主观点不受欢迎。|Near synonyms: undemocratic, authoritarian, totalitarian|Near antonyms: democratic, pro-democratic, republican|adjective
