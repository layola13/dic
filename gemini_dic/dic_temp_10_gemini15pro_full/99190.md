intercivilization:/ˌɪntərˌsɪvɪlɪˈzeɪʃən/
adjective
文明间的；不同文明之间的
Relating to or involving interaction between different civilizations.
Example: Intercivilization dialogue is crucial for global peace.  文明间的对话对世界和平至关重要。

Example: The study of intercivilization relations can help us understand historical conflicts. 对不同文明之间关系的研究可以帮助我们理解历史冲突。

近义词: cross-cultural, intercultural
反义词: intracivilization
词性: adjective
