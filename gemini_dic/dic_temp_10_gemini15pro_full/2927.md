advisors:/ədˈvaɪzərz/| n. |顾问，指导者|A person who gives advice, especially in a particular field.|The company hired financial advisors to help with the merger. 公司聘请了财务顾问来协助合并。

The student sought advice from her academic advisors.  这个学生向她的学业顾问寻求建议。|近义词：consultant, counselor, mentor, guide|反义词: advisee|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "aviseur," from "aviser" (to advise), from Latin "ad" (to) + "visere" (to see, visit).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European "*weid-" (to see).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
Our financial advisors recommend diversifying your investments. = 我们的财务顾问建议你分散投资。
My advisors always give me good advice. = 我的顾问总是给我很好的建议。

一般过去时:
The advisors warned us about the potential risks. = 顾问们警告我们潜在的风险。
His advisors told him to withdraw from the deal. = 他的顾问告诉他退出这笔交易。


现在完成时:
The advisors have been working on this project for months. = 顾问们已经在这个项目上工作了好几个月了。
I have consulted several advisors before making this decision. = 在做出这个决定之前，我咨询了几位顾问。


过去完成时:
The advisors had already left before the meeting started. = 会议开始前，顾问们已经离开了。
She had talked to her advisors before accepting the offer. = 在接受这份工作之前，她已经和她的顾问谈过了。


将来时:
The advisors will present their findings next week. = 顾问们将在下周展示他们的发现。
We will contact our advisors for further guidance. = 我们将联系我们的顾问以获得进一步的指导。

虚拟语气:
