ignorantist:/ˈɪɡnərəntɪst/
noun
自以为是的人；无知者
A person who is wilfully ignorant or who pretends to be ignorant.
He's such an ignorantist, dismissing any scientific evidence that contradicts his beliefs.
他真是个自以为是的人，对任何与他信仰相悖的科学证据都置之不理。
近义词:know-nothing, philistine, ignoramus
反义词:intellectual, scholar, expert
词性:n.

ignorantist:/ˈɪɡnərəntɪst/
adjective
自以为是的；无知的
Characterized by willful ignorance or a pretense of ignorance.
His ignorantist attitude towards climate change is alarming.
他对气候变化的无知态度令人担忧。
近义词:ignorant, uninformed, uneducated
反义词:knowledgeable, informed, educated
词性:adj. (rarely used)
