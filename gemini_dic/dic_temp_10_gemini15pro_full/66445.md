exemplifiable:/ɪɡˈzɛmplɪˌfaɪəbl/
adjective
可举例的，可示范的
Capable of being exemplified; illustrable.
This principle is exemplifiable by several case studies.  这个原则可以通过几个案例研究来举例说明。
The concept of courage is exemplifiable through many historical figures.  勇气这个概念可以通过很多历史人物来举例说明。
synonyms: illustrable, demonstrable, typifiable
antonyms: unexemplifiable, abstract, unillustrable
词性: adjective
