dogwood:/ˈdɒɡwʊd/ (UK), /ˈdɑːɡwʊd/ (US)| n. |山茱萸，山茱萸木|A flowering tree or shrub of the genus Cornus, typically having small flowers surrounded by four large, showy bracts that are often mistaken for petals. The wood is hard and close-grained.|The dogwood trees were in full bloom, their white bracts creating a stunning display. 山茱萸树盛开着，白色的苞片构成了一幅令人惊叹的景象。|Synonyms: cornel, cornelian cherry|Antonyms: N/A|noun
