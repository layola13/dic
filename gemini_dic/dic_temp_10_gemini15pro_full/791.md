absorber:/əbˈzɔːrbər (UK), əbˈsɔːrbər (US)| n.|吸收器，吸收剂|A substance or device that absorbs energy, liquids, gases, or other materials.|The shock absorbers on my car need replacing. 我的汽车上的减震器需要更换。|近义词：sponge, buffer, damper, cushion|反义词:emitter, radiator|noun

absorber:/əbˈzɔːrbər (UK), əbˈsɔːrbər (US)| n.|吸收者|A person or thing that absorbs something.|Plants are absorbers of carbon dioxide. 植物是二氧化碳的吸收者。|近义词：receptor, consumer,  uptake|反义词:emitter, discharger|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "absorb" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting an agent or instrument). "Absorb" comes from Latin "absorbēre," from "ab-" (from, away) + "sorbēre" (to suck up).
