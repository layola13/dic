enforcive: /ɪnˈfɔːrsɪv/
adjective
强制的；强行的
Having the power to compel obedience.  Relating to enforcement.
例句：Enforcive measures were necessary to quell the riot.  强制措施对于平息骚乱是必要的。

例句：The government implemented enforcive policies to protect endangered species. 政府实施强制性政策来保护濒危物种。

近义词：compulsory, mandatory, obligatory, binding
反义词：optional, voluntary, permissive

词性: adjective
