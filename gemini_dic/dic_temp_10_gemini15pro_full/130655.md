nonadvantageously:/ˌnɒnˌædvænˈteɪdʒəsli/
adverb
不利地；无益地
In a way that does not provide any advantage.
The contract was nonadvantageously worded for the company.  这份合同的措辞对公司不利。
The new policy was implemented nonadvantageously, resulting in lower profits. 新政策的实施并不利，导致利润下降。
Synonyms: disadvantageously, unfavorably, detrimentally, poorly, badly
Antonyms: advantageously, favorably, beneficially, well
词性: adverb
