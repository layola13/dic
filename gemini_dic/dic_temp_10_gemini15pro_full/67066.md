explicitnesses:/ɪkˈsplɪsɪtnəsɪz/| n.|明确性，清晰度|The explicitnesses of the instructions left no room for misinterpretation. 说明书的清晰性不容误解。|近义词：clarity, precision, definiteness, unambiguousness|反义词：vagueness, ambiguity, obscurity, implicitness|词性:名词

词源: From explicit + -ness.  "explicit" comes from the Latin "explicitus," past participle of "explicare" (to unfold, explain), from "ex-" (out) + "plicare" (to fold).
