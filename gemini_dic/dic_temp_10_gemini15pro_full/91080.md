hutting:/ˈhʌtɪŋ/
n.|小屋生活；临时居住；搭建小屋|The act of living in huts, especially temporarily.
|They went hutting in the mountains for a week. 他们在山里住了一周小屋。|
近义词:camping, sheltering
反义词: residing, dwelling
词性:名词


hutting:/ˈhʌtɪŋ/
v.|住在小屋里；搭建小屋|(present participle of hut) Living in a hut; building a hut.
|They were hutting near the lake. 他们住在湖边的小屋里。|
近义词:camping, lodging
反义词: residing, settling
词性:动词
