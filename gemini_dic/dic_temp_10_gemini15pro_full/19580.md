bigfoot:/ˈbɪɡˌfʊt/| n.|大脚怪，野人|A large, hairy humanoid creature believed to inhabit forested regions, especially in the Pacific Northwest of North America.  It is a cryptid, meaning its existence is unsubstantiated. |例句：Many people believe in the legend of Bigfoot. 很多人相信大脚怪的传说。

例句：There have been numerous reported sightings of Bigfoot, but none have been definitively proven. 有许多关于大脚怪的目击报告，但没有一个得到最终证实。|近义词：Sasquatch, Yeti, Abominable Snowman|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
