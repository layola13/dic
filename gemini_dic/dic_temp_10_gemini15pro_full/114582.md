malbrouck:/ˈmælbrʊk/ | n. | 马尔伯勒公爵 | Duke of Marlborough, a title in the British peerage, famously held by John Churchill, 1st Duke of Marlborough. It is also the name of a popular French children's song about the duke's supposed death. |  The children sang "Malbrouck s'en va-t-en guerre." (The children sang "Marlborough is going to war.") | 近义词: Duke, nobleman | 反义词: commoner | 词性: noun
