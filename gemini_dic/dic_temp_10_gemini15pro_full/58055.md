drome:/droʊm/| n. |跑道；竞技场；赛马场|A course or track for racing, especially one for wheeled vehicles.  Also, a large stadium or building for sports and other public events.|例句：The new velodrome will host the cycling championships. 新的自行车赛车场将举办自行车锦标赛。

例句：The hippodrome was packed with spectators eager to see the chariot races.  竞技场挤满了渴望观看战车比赛的观众。|近义词：track, course, arena, stadium|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
