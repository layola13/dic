curbable:/ˈkɜːrbəbl/| adj.|可抑制的，可控制的|Able to be curbed or restrained.|Her enthusiasm was not easily curbable. 她的热情不容易抑制。|例句：His temper is not curbable. 他的脾气难以控制。|例句：The spread of the virus is curbable with proper measures.  通过适当的措施，病毒的传播是可以控制的。|近义词：controllable, restrainable, manageable|反义词：uncontrollable, irrepressible, rampant|词性:形容词
词源: From curb (v.) + -able.  "Curb" comes from Old French "curber" meaning "to bend, curve," ultimately from Latin "curvare" meaning "to bend."
