ETYMOLOGY->
From the character Pandarus in Chaucer's *Troilus and Criseyde* and Shakespeare's *Troilus and Cressida*, who acts as a go-between for the lovers.  Ultimately from the Greek *Pandaros*, a character in Homer's *Iliad*.
