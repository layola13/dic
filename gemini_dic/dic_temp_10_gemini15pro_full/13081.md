astrology: /əˈstrɒlədʒi/ (UK) /əˈstrɑːlədʒi/ (US)| n. |占星术；占星学|The study of the movements and relative positions of celestial bodies interpreted as having an influence on human affairs and the natural world. |例句：She’s really into astrology and reads her horoscope every day. 她非常喜欢占星术，每天都看她的星座运势。

例句：Many people don't believe in the claims of astrology. 很多人不相信占星术的说法。

近义词：horoscopy, stargazing|反义词: astronomy|词性:noun
