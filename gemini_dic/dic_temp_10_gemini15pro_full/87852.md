hidden:/ˈhɪdn/ | adj. | 隐藏的，秘密的 | concealed, not visible or apparent; kept secret | 近义词：concealed, secret, covert, disguised | 反义词：visible, obvious, apparent, overt | 词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hydde, past participle of hydan "to hide," from Proto-Germanic *hudjanan, from Proto-Indo-European *keudh- "to hide, conceal."
