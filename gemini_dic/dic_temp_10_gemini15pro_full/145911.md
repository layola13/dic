overdignify: /ˌoʊvərˈdɪɡnɪfaɪ/
verb
过分抬举；过分尊崇
To treat (someone or something) with more dignity or respect than is appropriate or deserved.
He tends to overdignify his minor accomplishments. 他倾向于过分夸大他微不足道的成就。
Overdignifying the mundane can make it seem ridiculous. 过分抬举平凡的事情会让它显得荒谬。
近义词: exalt, glorify, elevate
反义词: belittle, demean, disparage
词性: verb
