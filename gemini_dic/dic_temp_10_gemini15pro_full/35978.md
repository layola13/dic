codependency:/ˌkoʊdɪˈpɛndənsi/ | n. | 共同依赖；相互依赖 | A relationship in which a person is controlled or manipulated by someone who is affected by an addiction or mental disorder.  It also describes a relationship where one person supports or enables another person's addiction, poor mental health, immaturity, irresponsibility, or under-achievement. |  Her codependency on her alcoholic husband was obvious. 她对她酗酒丈夫的共同依赖显而易见。| synonyms: interdependence, interrelationship, interconnectedness | antonyms: independence, autonomy, self-reliance | noun


codependencies: /ˌkoʊdɪˈpɛndənsiz/ | n. | 共同依赖；相互依赖（复数） | Plural form of codependency.  Refers to multiple instances or types of codependent relationships. | The therapist specialized in treating families with complex codependencies.  这位治疗师专门治疗有复杂相互依赖关系的家庭。| synonyms: interdependencies, interrelationships | antonyms: independencies, autonomies | noun
