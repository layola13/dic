contenting:/kənˈtɛntɪŋ/
verb
使满足；使满意
satisfying or making someone satisfied
She contented herself with a simple lunch. 她吃了一顿简单的午餐就心满意足了。
近义词: satisfying, gratifying, pleasing
反义词: dissatisfying, displeasing
词性: verb (present participle of content)

content:/kənˈtɛnt/
adjective
满足的；满意的
in a state of peaceful happiness
He seemed fairly content with his life. 他似乎对自己的生活相当满意。
近义词: satisfied, happy, pleased
反义词: dissatisfied, unhappy, displeased
词性: adjective

content:/kənˈtɛnt/
noun
内容；目录；容量
the things that are held or included in something
The content of the course is very interesting. 这门课程的内容很有趣。
近义词: subject matter, substance, text
反义词: N/A
词性: noun

content:/kənˈtɛnt/
verb
使满足；使满意
to satisfy or make someone satisfied
My explanation seemed to content him. 我的解释似乎让他满意了。
近义词: satisfy, please, gratify
反义词: dissatisfy, displease
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *contenter, from Latin contentus, past participle of continēre (“to hold together, contain, restrain”), from con- (“together”) + tenēre (“to hold”).  Doublet of contain.
