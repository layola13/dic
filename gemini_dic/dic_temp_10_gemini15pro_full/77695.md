geminating:/ˈdʒɛmɪneɪtɪŋ/
| adj. |成双的，孪生的；芽接的|The process of doubling a consonant sound.  This often happens when a word ends in a consonant and the next word begins with the same consonant. |Example: The "t" sound is geminated in the phrase "hot tea." “热茶”这个短语中的“t”音是双写的。|Synonym: doubling, twinning, pairing|Antonym: separating, dividing|adjective

gemination:/ˌdʒɛmɪˈneɪʃən/
| n. |成双，成对；双生；芽接|The act or process of geminating.  The doubling of a consonant. |Example: Gemination of consonants is a common feature of some languages. 辅音的双写是一些语言的共同特征。|Synonym: doubling, reduplication|Antonym: simplification, reduction|noun
