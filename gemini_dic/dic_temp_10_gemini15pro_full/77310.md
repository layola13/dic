gateways:/ˈɡeɪtweɪz/
n.
通道，途径；网关
A place, structure, or means of access or entry.  A point of entry to a network or system.
The Golden Gate Bridge is a gateway to San Francisco. 金门大桥是通往旧金山的大门。
Network gateways connect different types of networks. 网络网关连接不同类型的网络。

近义词: entrances, portals, access points, doors
反义词: exits, barriers, obstacles
词性: 名词 (plural)

词源: From Middle English "gatewey," from "gate" + "wey" (way).
