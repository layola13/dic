lobsterlike:/ˈlɒbstəlaɪk/ (US) /ˈlɒbstəlaɪk/ (UK)| adj.|像龙虾的，龙虾似的|Resembling a lobster in form, appearance, or movement. |例句：The creature had lobsterlike claws.  这种生物有像龙虾一样的爪子。

例句：Her hands, red and swollen, looked lobsterlike. 她手又红又肿，看起来像龙虾。


近义词：crustaceanlike, lobsterish|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider using "unlike a lobster")|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From lobster + -like.  "Lobster" comes from the Old English "loppestre," possibly influenced by the Old Norse "humarr." "-like" is a common suffix denoting resemblance.
