cobblestone:/ˈkɑːbəlˌstoʊn/ (US), /ˈkɒblˌstəʊn/ (UK)| n.|鹅卵石，圆石|A small, round stone used for paving streets.|The streets of the old town are paved with cobblestones.  老城区街道是用鹅卵石铺成的。|Synonyms: paving stone, sett, cobble|Antonyms: asphalt, concrete|noun

cobblestone:/ˈkɑːbəlˌstoʊn/ (US), /ˈkɒblˌstəʊn/ (UK)| adj.|鹅卵石的，用鹅卵石铺成的|Made of cobblestones.|We walked along a cobblestone path. 我们沿着一条鹅卵石小路走。|Synonyms: cobbled, stoned|Antonyms: paved (with asphalt/concrete)|adjective
