hole-up:/ˈhoʊl ʌp/ (US) /ˈhəʊl ʌp/ (UK)| v. n.|躲藏；藏匿处|To stay in a safe place, often hiding from someone or something.  A place of hiding or refuge.|例句：The robbers holed up in an abandoned warehouse.  强盗们躲藏在一个废弃的仓库里。|例句：Their hole-up was a small cabin in the woods. 他们藏身的地方是树林里的小木屋。|近义词：hide, conceal, shelter, refuge|反义词:emerge, appear|词性:动词, 名词
