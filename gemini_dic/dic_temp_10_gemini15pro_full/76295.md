gabbiness:/ˈɡæbinəs/| n.|饶舌，多话，喋喋不休|The excessive gabbiness of the speaker made it hard to focus on the main points. 演讲者过度的饶舌让人很难集中注意力在要点上。|例句：Her gabbiness can be tiresome at times. 她有时很饶舌，让人厌烦。

|例句：The meeting was unproductive due to the gabbiness of some participants.  由于一些参与者的喋喋不休，会议没有成果。|近义词：loquacity, talkativeness, volubility|反义词: taciturnity, reticence|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From gabby + -ness.  Gabby itself likely originates from gab, an imitative word for idle talk.
