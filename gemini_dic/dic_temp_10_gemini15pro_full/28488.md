carcinoma: /kɑːrsɪˈnoʊmə/ /kɑːsɪˈnəʊmə/ | n. |癌；恶性肿瘤|A malignant tumor formed from epithelial cells.  It is a type of cancer that starts in the cells that line the skin, organs, or glands. | Doctors diagnosed a carcinoma in her lung. 医生诊断出她肺部患有癌。|  The patient's carcinoma was successfully removed. 病人的癌肿被成功切除。 |synonym: cancer, tumor, malignancy | antonym: benign tumor | noun
