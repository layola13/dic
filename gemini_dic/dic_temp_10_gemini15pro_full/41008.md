copan:/ˈkoʊpæn/ | n. |  棉签;拭子 | A swab, typically used for collecting samples for medical testing. | The nurse used a copan to collect a sample for the COVID-19 test.  护士用棉签采集了COVID-19测试的样本。 | 近义词: swab, applicator | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun

词源: Copan Diagnostics, Inc. is a company that manufactures collection and transport systems for infectious disease specimens. The term "copan" has become generalized to refer to the swabs they produce, particularly in medical contexts.  源自Copan诊断公司，该公司生产用于传染病标本的采集和运输系统。“copan”一词已被普遍用来指代他们生产的拭子，尤其是在医学环境中。
