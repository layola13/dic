myrings: /ˈmaɪˌrɪŋz/
noun
鼓膜; 耳膜
The thin membranes that separate the outer ear from the middle ear and vibrate in response to sound waves.
My doctor checked my myrings because I had an ear infection.  我的医生检查了我的耳膜，因为我得了耳部感染。
The loud noise ruptured his myrings. 巨大的噪音震破了他的耳膜。
Synonyms: eardrums, tympanic membranes
Antonyms: N/A
