beyond-question:/biˈjɑːnd ˈkwɛstʃən/ | adj. | 无可争议的，毋庸置疑的 |  Without any doubt; indisputable.  It means something is so clear and obvious that it cannot be reasonably doubted or disputed. |  His honesty is beyond question. 他的诚实是无可争议的。 | Synonyms: doubtless, indisputable, undeniable, unquestionable | Antonyms: doubtful, questionable, disputable, uncertain | adjective

beyond-question:/biˈjɑːnd ˈkwɛstʃən/ | adv. | 无可争议地，毋庸置疑地 | Without any doubt; indisputably. It describes the manner in which something is true or valid – with absolute certainty.  | He is beyond question the best candidate for the job. 他无疑是这项工作的最佳人选。 | Synonyms: undoubtedly, indisputably, undeniably, unquestionably | Antonyms: doubtfully, questionably, disputably, uncertainly | adverb
