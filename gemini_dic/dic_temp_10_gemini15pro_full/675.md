absans: /ˈæbsənz/
adjective
缺席的，不在场的
Not present at a place or event.
He was absans from school due to illness.  他因病缺课。

The chairman was notably absans from the meeting. 主席明显缺席了会议。

近义词: absent, missing, away, nonattendant
反义词: present, attending, here
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin absens, present participle of abesse "to be away, be absent," from ab "away, from" + esse "to be."

USAGE->
This word is considered archaic or obsolete and rarely used in modern English.  "Absent" is the preferred and more common term.  Therefore providing usage examples for various tenses is not applicable.  It is best to avoid using "absans" in modern writing or conversation.
