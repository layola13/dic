disguise:/dɪsˈɡaɪz/ /dɪsˈɡaɪs/| n. vt.|伪装，掩饰|A means of altering one's appearance or concealing one's identity.  The act of concealing or misrepresenting something.|She wore a disguise to avoid being recognized. 她乔装打扮以避免被认出来。|He disguised his voice on the phone. 他在电话里伪装了自己的声音。|近义词：camouflage, mask, cloak, cover, pretense|反义词：reveal, expose|词性:noun, verb

Etymology:
From Old French *desguisier "to alter the guise or appearance of," from des- "opposite of, away" + guise "manner, fashion," from Frankish *wisa "wise, manner," from Proto-Germanic *wiso- "condition, appearance, manner."
