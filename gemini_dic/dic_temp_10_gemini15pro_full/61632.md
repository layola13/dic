emigre:/ˈemɪɡreɪ, ˈemɪɡri/ /ˈɛmɪɡreɪ/ | n. |  移民；流亡者 | A person who has left their own country in order to settle in another, usually for political reasons. | Many emigres fled the country during the war. 许多移民在战争期间逃离了该国。 |  refugee, exile, expatriate | immigrant, native | noun

emigre:/ˈemɪɡreɪ, ˈemɪɡri/ /ˈɛmɪɡreɪ/ | adj. |  移民的；流亡的 | Relating to or characteristic of an emigre. |  The emigre community maintained its cultural traditions.  移民社区保持了其文化传统。 | expatriate, exiled | native, indigenous | adjective
