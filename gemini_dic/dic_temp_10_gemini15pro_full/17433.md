beanfeast:/ˈbiːnˌfiːst/ | n. | 盛宴；庆祝宴会；欢乐的聚会 | A celebratory feast or party, often provided by an employer for their employees.  Origin: Late 18th century, originally referring to a festive meal provided for employees or members of a club or society, often featuring baked beans.  | The company's annual beanfeast was a highlight of the year for the employees. 公司的年度宴会是员工们一年中的亮点。 | The club held a beanfeast to celebrate its centenary. 俱乐部举行了一场盛宴来庆祝其百年纪念。 | Synonyms: feast, banquet, celebration, party | Antonyms: fast, famine | noun
