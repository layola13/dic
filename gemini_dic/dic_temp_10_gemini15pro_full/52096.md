diameters:/daɪˈæmɪtərz/
n.
直径(复数)
A straight line passing from side to side through the center of a body or figure, especially a circle or sphere.
The diameter of the circle is 10 cm.  这个圆的直径是10厘米。
近义词:width, breadth
反义词:radius
词性:名词
