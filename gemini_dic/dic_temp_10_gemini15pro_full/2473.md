adjusts:/əˈdʒʌsts/| v.| 调整，调节，适应|  To change something slightly to make it more suitable for a particular purpose or situation. | She adjusted her glasses before reading. 她阅读前调整了眼镜。

He adjusted the volume on the radio. 他调整了收音机的音量。|近义词：modify, alter, adapt, regulate, tune, correct, amend |反义词：worsen, disarrange, upset, maintain |词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ajuster (Modern French ajuster), from Vulgar Latin *ad-iuxtare “to place near, join,” from Latin iuxta “near, next to.”

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He adjusts the thermostat every morning. = 他每天早上都会调整恒温器。
She adjusts her schedule to fit her client's needs. = 她调整自己的日程安排以适应客户的需求。

一般过去时:
The mechanic adjusted the brakes on my car. = 机械师调整了我汽车的刹车。
She adjusted her hair in the mirror before the interview. = 面试前，她在镜子里整理了头发。


现在完成时:
The company has adjusted its prices to reflect the current market. = 公司已调整价格以反映当前市场情况。
He has adjusted to his new job quickly. = 他很快就适应了新工作。


过去完成时:
After she had adjusted the settings, the picture became clearer. = 在她调整设置后，图像变得更清晰了。
They had adjusted their plans due to the unexpected rain. = 由于突如其来的雨，他们调整了计划。


将来时:
I will adjust the lighting when I get home. = 我回家后会调整灯光。
We will adjust our strategy based on the feedback we receive. = 我们将根据收到的反馈调整我们的策略。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would adjust my attitude. = 如果我是你，我会调整我的态度。
I wish I could adjust the temperature in this room. = 我希望我能调节这个房间的温度。


被动语态:
The settings were adjusted by the technician. = 设置由技术人员调整。
The recipe can be adjusted to your liking. = 食谱可以根据您的喜好进行调整。


疑问句:
How do you adjust the brightness on this screen? = 你如何调整这个屏幕的亮度？
Did you adjust the volume? = 你调整音量了吗？


否定句:
I didn't adjust the thermostat. = 我没有调整恒温器。
He doesn't adjust well to change. = 他不太适应变化。

PHRASE->
adjust to (something) = 适应（某事）
adjust for (something) = 调整以适应（某事）
make adjustments = 做出调整
adjust the settings = 调整设置
adjust the focus = 调整焦点
