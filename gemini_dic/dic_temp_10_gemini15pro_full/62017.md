enantiopathic:/ɪˌnæntiəˈpæθɪk/
adjective
对抗疗法的；对抗病的
Treating disease with remedies that produce effects opposite to those of the disease.
Enantiopathic medicine focuses on treating symptoms directly. 对抗疗法注重直接治疗症状。
Homeopathy is considered an enantiopathic approach to medicine.  顺势疗法被认为是一种对抗疗法的医学方法。
近义词: allopathic, conventional
反义词: homeopathic, alternative
词性: adjective
