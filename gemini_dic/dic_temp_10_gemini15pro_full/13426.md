atonally:/eɪˈtɒnəli/ /əˈtoʊnəli/
adverb|无调性地|Without tonality; in a way that avoids traditional harmonies and key signatures.|The piece was composed atonally, creating a dissonant and unsettling effect. 这部作品是无调性创作的，营造了一种不和谐和令人不安的效果。|The composer experimented with atonal music, abandoning traditional harmonic structures. 这位作曲家尝试了无调性音乐，放弃了传统的和声结构。|Synonym: nontonally|Antonym: tonally
词性: 副词
