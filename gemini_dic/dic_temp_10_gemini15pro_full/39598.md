consectary: /ˈkɒnsɪkˌteri/ (US) /ˈkɒnsɪktəri/ (UK)| n.|推论，必然结果|A proposition that follows from one already proved; a deduction, corollary.|The high cost of housing is a consectary of limited land supply and increasing demand. 高昂的房价是土地供应有限和需求不断增长必然的结果。|近义词：corollary, consequence, result, outcome|反义词：cause, origin, premise|词性: 名词

词源:
From Late Latin *consectarium, from Latin consecutus, past participle of consequi "to follow," from com- "together" + sequi "to follow."
