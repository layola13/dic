homopter:/ˈhɒmɒptər (UK), ˈhɑːmɑːptər (US)| n. |同翅目昆虫|An insect of the order Homoptera, which includes cicadas, aphids, and scale insects, characterized by piercing and sucking mouthparts.  They typically feed on plant sap.|例句：The homopteran sucked the sap from the rose bush. 这只同翅目昆虫吸食了玫瑰丛的汁液。

例句：Aphids, a type of homopteran, can be a serious pest for farmers.蚜虫是一种同翅目昆虫，对农民来说可能是一种严重的害虫。|近义词：Hemipteran (sometimes used interchangeably, but Hemiptera is a broader order)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ὁμόπτερος (homópteros, “having similar wings”), from ὁμός (homós, “same”) + πτερόν (pterón, “wing”).
