n.
哈西德派犹太教的教义、习俗和传统
The beliefs, customs, and traditions of Hasidic Judaism.
He was fascinated by the hassidisms he observed in the community.  他对他在这个社区观察到的哈西德派犹太教的习俗很着迷。
