dabbing:/ˈdæbɪŋ/ (US), /ˈdæbɪŋ/ (UK)| n. v.|轻拍，轻涂; (俚语)吸食大麻油; 一种舞蹈动作|The act of applying something lightly, typically a liquid or powder, with a dabbing motion. Also a slang term for inhaling cannabis oil, and a type of dance move.|She dabbed at her eyes with a tissue. 她用纸巾轻拭眼睛。

He was dabbing cannabis oil. 他在吸食大麻油。
