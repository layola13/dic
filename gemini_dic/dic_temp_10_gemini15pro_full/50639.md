deril:/ˈdɛrəl/| n.| 恶魔;恶棍 |A wicked or mischievous person; a rogue or rascal.  A devil or demon in older usage.|He was a deril on the playground, always causing trouble. 他在操场上是个小恶魔，总是惹麻烦。|近义词：scoundrel, rogue, rascal, devil, imp|反义词: angel, saint|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *derel, from Old French *derel, possibly a variant of Old French derei "disorder, misrule," from desreiier "to disorder,"  from des- "dis-" + reiier "to arrange."  Influenced by, and ultimately replaced by, "devil."
