carbuncle: /ˈkɑːrbʌŋkəl/ (US) /ˈkɑːbʌŋkl/ (UK)| n. |痈; 红宝石; 炭疽|A painful, localized bacterial infection of the skin and tissues beneath the skin, larger than a boil; a bright red gem, especially a garnet; (archaic) a burning coal or ember.|

例句：
The doctor diagnosed the painful swelling on his neck as a carbuncle.
医生诊断他脖子上的疼痛肿胀是痈。

He gave her a beautiful ring with a large carbuncle.
他送给她一枚镶嵌着一颗大红宝石的漂亮戒指。


近义词：boil, abscess, ruby, garnet|
反义词: (For the medical sense) health, wellness |
词性: noun
