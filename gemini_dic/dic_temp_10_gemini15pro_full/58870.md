duramen:/djʊˈreɪmən/, /dʒəˈreɪmən/
n.
心材，树心
The hard, central wood of a tree; heartwood.
The furniture is made of durable duramen. 这件家具是用耐用的心材制成的。

The carpenter prefers using duramen for his projects because of its strength.  这位木匠更喜欢用心材来做他的项目，因为它很结实。

近义词:heartwood
反义词:sapwood
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dūrāmen*, from *dūrāre* "to harden," from *dūrus* "hard."
