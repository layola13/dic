nonignitability:/nɒnɪɡˈnɪtəbɪləti/
noun
不可燃性; 不易燃性
The quality of being nonignitable; resistance to ignition.
The nonignitability of the material makes it safe for use in high-temperature environments.  这种材料的不可燃性使其可以安全地用于高温环境。
Near synonyms: incombustibility, fire resistance, flame resistance
Near antonyms: flammability, combustibility, ignitability
词性: noun
