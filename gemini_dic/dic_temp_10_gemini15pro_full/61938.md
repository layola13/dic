emulously:/ˈɛmjʊləsli/ | adv. | 竞争地，好胜地，仿效地 | In a way that shows you are trying to be like someone or something else, because you admire them. | He emulously tried to match his brother's athletic achievements. 他好胜地试图与他兄弟的运动成就相匹敌。| 近义词: competitively, vyingly, imitatively | 反义词: indifferently, apathetically | 词性: 副词

emulate:/ˈɛmjʊleɪt/ | vt. | 效仿，模仿，竞争 | To try to be like someone or something else, because you admire them. | Young writers often emulate the styles of their favorite authors. 年轻作家经常模仿他们最喜欢的作家的风格。| 近义词: imitate, copy, mimic, rival | 反义词: neglect, ignore | 词性: 动词

词源: Late Middle English: from Latin aemulatus, past participle of aemulari ‘rival’, from aemulus ‘rival’.
