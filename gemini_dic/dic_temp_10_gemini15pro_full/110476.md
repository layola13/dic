lilt:/lɪlt/| n. vi.|轻快的节奏，轻快的调子；轻快地唱或说|A lilt is a pleasant, gentle rising and falling of the voice when speaking or singing.|Her voice had a lovely Irish lilt. 她的声音带着可爱的 ایرلندی 轻快的调子。|She spoke with a lilt in her voice. 她说话的语调轻快。|近义词：cadence, rhythm, swing|反义词:monotone|词性:名词，动词
