conchitic:/kɒnˈkɪtɪk/
adjective
贝壳状的；由贝壳组成的
Composed of shells; resembling shells.
The geologist discovered a conchitic limestone formation. 地质学家发现了一个贝壳状的石灰岩地层。
The ancient artifact had a conchitic surface, indicating it was made from crushed shells. 这件古代人工制品有一个贝壳状的表面，表明它是用碎贝壳制成的。
synonyms: shelly, testaceous
antonyms: non-conchitic (This is a rarely used antonym, created for contrast)
word class: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *concha* (shell) + *-itic* (suffix forming adjectives).  The Latin *concha* is ultimately from Greek *κόγχη* (konkhē), meaning "mussel, shell".
