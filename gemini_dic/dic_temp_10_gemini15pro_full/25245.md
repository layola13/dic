buffalo:/ˈbʌfəloʊ/ (US), /ˈbʌfələʊ/ (UK)| n. |水牛；野牛|A large bovine mammal of the ox family. Several species exist, notably the African buffalo and the American bison.|例句：The buffalo roamed the plains. 野牛在平原上漫游。

例句：A herd of buffalo crossed the river. 一群水牛渡过了河。|近义词：bison, ox|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


buffalo:/ˈbʌfəloʊ/ (US), /ˈbʌfələʊ/ (UK)| vt. |恐吓；迷惑|To intimidate or confuse someone.|例句：He was buffaloed by the complex legal jargon. 他被复杂的法律术语搞糊涂了。

例句：Don't let them buffalo you into signing anything. 不要让他们吓唬你签任何东西。|近义词：intimidate, bewilder, confuse|反义词:encourage, clarify|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Portuguese *búfalo*, from Latin *būbalus*, from Ancient Greek *βούβαλος* (*boúbalos*). Ultimately probably of unknown African origin.
