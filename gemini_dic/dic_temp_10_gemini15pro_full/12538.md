asphyxiators: /əsˈfɪksiˌeɪtərz/ | n. |窒息物；导致窒息的东西| Something that causes asphyxiation, depriving a person or animal of oxygen and causing unconsciousness or death. | The fire produced toxic fumes and acted as asphyxiators. 大火产生了有毒烟雾，并导致了窒息。|  The faulty heater emitted carbon monoxide, one of the most common household asphyxiators. 故障的加热器排放出一氧化碳，这是一氧化碳是最常见的家庭窒息物之一。 | synonyms: suffocants, choking agents | antonyms: ventilators, resuscitators | noun
