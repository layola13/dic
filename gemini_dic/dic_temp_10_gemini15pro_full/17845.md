beechier:/ˈbiːtʃiər/
adjective
更多山毛榉树的；似山毛榉的
More resembling or characteristic of beech trees; having more beech trees.
The woods became beechier as we climbed higher.  我们爬得越高，树林里的山毛榉树就越多。
The furniture has a beechier finish than the last one. 这件家具比上一件更像山毛榉木的 finishes。
近义词:  beech-like
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could use descriptions like "less beech-like," "more oak-like," etc. depending on the context)
词性: adjective
