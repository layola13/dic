词源:  From Old French *cheater (modern French chasser "to chase, hunt"), from Late Latin *captiare "to try to seize," from Latin captare "to strive after, try to seize," frequentative of capere "to take."  The original sense was "to confiscate, to seize by escheat," then "to defraud, swindle."
