obstructs:/əbˈstrʌkts/
verb
阻碍，阻塞，妨碍
blocks or closes up with an obstacle; hinders
The fallen tree obstructs the road.  倒下的树挡住了道路。
近义词: blocks, hinders, impedes, bars
反义词: aids, assists, helps, facilitates
词性: verb
