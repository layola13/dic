governmental:/ˌɡʌvərˈmentl/
adjective
政府的；与政府有关的
relating to government or the process of governing
The governmental agency is responsible for overseeing environmental regulations.  这个政府机构负责监督环境法规。
近义词:official, public, state, administrative
反义词:private, nongovernmental
词性:adjective

词源:
From government + -al.  来自government + -al.
