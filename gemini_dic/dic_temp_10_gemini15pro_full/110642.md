limu:/ˈliːmuː/| n.|海藻，一种可食用的海藻|A type of edible seaweed, especially prevalent in Hawaiian cuisine.|例句：Limu is a staple ingredient in Hawaiian poke.  海藻是夏威夷poke的主要成分。

例句：She gathered limu from the tide pools. 她从潮池里采集海藻。|近义词：seaweed, algae|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "land plant")|词性:noun
