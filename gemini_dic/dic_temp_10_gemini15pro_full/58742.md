dungaree:/ˌdʌŋ.ɡəˈriː/
n.
工作服，粗棉布裤，粗棉布
A type of strong, heavy cotton cloth, often blue, used especially for making jeans. Also, (usually plural) trousers or overalls made of this cloth.
The workers were all wearing dungarees. 工人们都穿着工作服。
近义词:overalls, jeans
反义词:formal wear
词性:noun

dungaree:/ˌdʌŋ.ɡəˈriː/
n.
(印度)粗棉布
A coarse, heavy cotton cloth; also, clothes made from this.
The sail was made of heavy dungaree. 帆是用厚粗棉布做的。
近义词:calico, canvas
反义词:silk, satin
词性:noun
