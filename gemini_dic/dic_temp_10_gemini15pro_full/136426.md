nonretroactive:/ˌnɒnˌrɛtrəʊˈæktɪv/ /ˌnɑːnˌrɛtroʊˈæktɪv/| adj.|不溯及既往的|Not applying to a period before a particular date or event; not having retroactive effect.|The new law is nonretroactive and only applies to offenses committed after January 1st.  这项新法律不溯及既往，仅适用于1月1日之后发生的违法行为。|The tax increase is nonretroactive, so you won't have to pay more for previous purchases.  增税不溯及既往，因此您无需为之前的购买支付更多费用。|Synonyms: prospective, irretrospective|Antonyms: retroactive, retrospective|adjective
