digitalizations:/ˌdɪdʒɪtəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/
n.
数字化，数位化
the process of converting information into a digital format.
例句：The digitalizations of archives has made them more accessible to researchers.  档案的数字化使研究人员更容易查阅它们。

例句：The company is undergoing a period of rapid digitalizations. 该公司正处于快速数字化时期。
近义词：digitization, computerization, automation
反义词：analogization
词性：名词
