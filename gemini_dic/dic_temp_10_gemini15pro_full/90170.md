hotlist:/ˈhɒtlɪst/ (US) /ˈhɒtlɪst/ (UK)| n.| 热门名单，重点关注名单| A list of people or things that are considered to be important or worthy of special attention. |The police put him on the hotlist of suspects. 警方把他列入了嫌疑人重点关注名单。|近义词：watchlist, shortlist|反义词: N/A|名词

hotlist:/ˈhɒtlɪst/ (US) /ˈhɒtlɪst/ (UK)| vt.| 将…列入热门名单，将…列入重点关注名单| To add someone or something to a hotlist. |The company hotlisted several potential candidates for the job. 这家公司将几名潜在候选人列入了该职位的热门名单。|近义词：prioritize, flag|反义词: ignore, disregard|动词
