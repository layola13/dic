The data is transferred nonvisibly. 数据无形地传输。| 近义词: invisibly, imperceptibly | 反义词: visibly, noticeably | 词性: 副词


词源:

non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non.
visibly (adverb): From visible + -ly. Visible comes from Latin visibilis, meaning "that can be seen," from visus, the past participle of videre, "to see."
