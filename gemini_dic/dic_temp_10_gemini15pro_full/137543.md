nontransferabilities:/ˌnɑːntrænsˌfɜːrəˈbɪlətiz/
n.
不可转让性; 不可转移性
The quality or state of being nontransferable; things that cannot be transferred.
Airline tickets often have various nontransferabilities, meaning you can't give your ticket to someone else. 机票通常具有多种不可转让性，这意味着您不能将机票转让给他人。
近义词: non-assignability, inalienability
反义词: transferability, assignability
词性: 名词(plural)
