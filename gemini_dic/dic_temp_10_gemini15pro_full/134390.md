nonintoxicating:/ˌnɒnɪnˈtɒksɪkeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
无酒精的；不使人陶醉的
Not causing intoxication; not containing alcohol or other intoxicating substances.
Non-alcoholic beer is a popular nonintoxicating beverage.  无酒精啤酒是一种受欢迎的无酒精饮料。
This herbal tea is completely nonintoxicating.  这种凉茶完全不含酒精。
synonyms: non-alcoholic, sober, soft
antonyms: intoxicating, alcoholic, inebriating
adjective
