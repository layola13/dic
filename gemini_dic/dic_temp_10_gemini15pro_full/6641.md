anaesthetising:/əˈniːsθətaɪzɪŋ/
verb
麻醉；使麻木
Administering an anesthetic to cause a loss of sensation or consciousness.
The vet is anaesthetising the cat before the operation. 兽医正在给猫做手术前麻醉。
近义词:numbing, desensitizing
反义词:stimulating, sensitizing
词性:v.

anaesthetise:/əˈniːsθətaɪz/
verb
麻醉；使麻木
Administer an anesthetic to.
They anaesthetised the patient before the surgery. 他们在手术前给病人麻醉。
近义词:numb, desensitize
反义词:stimulate, sensitize
词性:v.


anaesthetic:/ænɪsˈθɛtɪk/
noun, adjective
麻醉剂；麻醉的
A substance that produces anesthesia.  Relating to or producing anesthesia.
The dentist used a local anaesthetic. 牙医使用了局部麻醉剂。
The anaesthetic wore off after a few hours. 麻醉几小时后就消失了。
近义词:(n.) narcotic, opiate (adj.) numbing
反义词:(adj.) stimulating
词性:n., adj.

词源: From Greek "anaisthesia," meaning "loss of sensation," from "an-" ("not") + "aisthesis" ("sensation").
