homochromous:/ˌhɒməˈkrəʊməs/ | adj. | 同色的，颜色相同的 | Having the same color throughout; uniformly colored. | The homochromous lizard blended perfectly with the sand.  这只同色蜥蜴与沙子完美地融合在一起。|  The homochromous walls gave the room a sense of uniformity.  同色的墙壁赋予了房间一种统一感。 | synonyms: monochromatic, unicolor, concolorous | antonyms: multicolored, varicolored | adjective
