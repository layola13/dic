devilled:/ˈdɛvəld/ (US), /ˈdɛvɪld/ (UK)| adj.|加辣的；加香料的；(尤指)加芥末的；捣蛋的，顽皮的|Highly seasoned, especially with hot spices or mustard.  Also, mischievous or playful.|例句：We had devilled eggs for a starter.  我们前菜吃了加辣的鸡蛋。

例句：The devilled child hid his mother's keys.  那个顽皮的孩子藏起了妈妈的钥匙。|近义词：spiced, seasoned, peppery, mischievous, naughty|反义词：bland, mild, well-behaved|词性:形容词

词源: From "devil" + "-ed."  The culinary sense likely refers to the fiery sensation of hot spices, while the mischievous sense derives from the association of the devil with troublemaking.
