barring:/ˈbɑːrɪŋ/ | prep. conj.|除…之外；除非|Except for; unless.|例句：Barring any unforeseen circumstances, we should arrive on time. 除非发生不可预见的意外情况，我们应该会准时到达。|例句：Barring accidents, they'll be here by nightfall. 除非发生意外，他们将在傍晚到达。|近义词：except for, excluding, save for, apart from, aside from|反义词：including|词性：介词，连词

barring:/ˈbɑːrɪŋ/ | n.|阻挡，障碍物|Something that obstructs or prevents progress or access.|例句：The heavy snow was a barring to travel. 大雪阻碍了出行。|例句：The barring on the door prevented entry. 门上的闩锁阻止了进入。|近义词：obstacle, barrier, impediment, obstruction|反义词：assistance, aid|词性：名词
