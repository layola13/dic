celection: /sɪˈlɛkʃən/ | n. | 选择，挑选；选集；精选品 | The process of carefully choosing someone or something as being the best or most suitable.  A collection of things that have been chosen because they are the best or most interesting of their kind. |  She has a wide selection of clothes in her wardrobe. 她衣柜里的衣服种类繁多。 | synonyms: choice, option, assortment, variety | antonyms: rejection, exclusion | noun
