nonpliably:/nɒnˈplaɪəbli/ | adv. | 不顺从地，顽固地 | In a manner that is not flexible or yielding; stubbornly; rigidly. | The old man clung nonpliably to his outdated beliefs.  老人顽固地坚持着他过时的信念。| The negotiator nonpliably refused to compromise.  谈判代表顽固地拒绝妥协。 | 近义词: inflexibly, rigidly, stubbornly, unyieldingly | 反义词: pliably, flexibly, yieldingly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From non- + pliable + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "pliable" means flexible or easily bent, and "-ly" is an adverbial suffix.
