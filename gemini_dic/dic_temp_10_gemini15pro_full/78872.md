ginglyform:/ˈdʒɪŋɡlɪfɔːrm/| adj.| 铰链形的，指关节状的|Resembling a hinge or having hinge-like joints.  Characterized by a series of interlocking parts that allow for flexibility and movement.|The ginglyform joints in the fingers allow for a wide range of motion.|近义词：hinge-like, articulated|反义词: rigid, fixed|词性: adjective
Etymology: From Latin *ginglymus* "hinge," from Greek *ginglymos* "hinge," of imitative origin. + -form "having the form of," from French *-forme* and directly from Latin *-formis*.
