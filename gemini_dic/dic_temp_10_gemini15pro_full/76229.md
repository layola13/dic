future-proof:/ˈfjuːtʃə pruːf/| adj.| 未来的,面向未来的,不会过时的|Designed or built so that it will not become obsolete in the near future; able to adapt to future changes.|例句：They're trying to build a future-proof stadium. 他们正在努力建造一个面向未来的体育场。|例句：Investing in renewable energy is a future-proof strategy. 投资可再生能源是一种面向未来的策略。|近义词：durable, long-lasting, sustainable, adaptable|反义词：obsolete, outdated, short-lived|词性:形容词
