macrura:/məˈkrʊərə/ | n. |长尾类 |Long-tailed decapod crustaceans; a suborder of Decapoda.  This suborder includes shrimps, prawns, lobsters, and crayfish. |Macrura are a diverse group, found in both freshwater and marine environments. 长尾类是一个多样化的群体，在淡水和海洋环境中都有发现。|Macrura are an important food source for many animals, including humans. 长尾类是许多动物的重要食物来源，包括人类。|近义词:decapod crustaceans 十足甲壳类动物|反义词:brachyura 短尾类|词性: noun
