back-gammoning:/ˌbækˈɡæmənɪŋ/
noun
西洋双陆棋戏；(西洋双陆棋中)把对方所有棋子都打回原位
The act of winning a game of backgammon by removing all of one's own checkers from the board before the opponent.
He was back-gammoning his opponent mercilessly. 他毫不留情地把对手的棋子都打回原位，赢得了比赛。
近义词:defeating (in backgammon)
反义词: being back-gammoned


back-gammoning:/ˌbækˈɡæmənɪŋ/
verb
(在西洋双陆棋中)把对方所有棋子都打回原位
To win a game of backgammon by removing all of one's own checkers from the board before the opponent can do so.
She back-gammoned him in the final game of the tournament.  她在锦标赛的最后一场比赛中把他所有的棋子都打回原位，赢得了比赛。
近义词:defeating (in backgammon)
反义词: being back-gammoned
