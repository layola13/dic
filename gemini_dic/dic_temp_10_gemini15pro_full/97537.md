ETYMOLOGY->
From ingrained + -ness.  Ingrained itself comes from in- + grained. "Grain" in this context refers to the texture of wood, highlighting the deep penetration and permanence.  The suffix "-ness" transforms the adjective "ingrained" into a noun signifying the state or quality of being ingrained.
