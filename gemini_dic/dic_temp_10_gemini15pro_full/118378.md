merl:/mɜːrl/ | n. |  黑鸟 (尤指雄性欧椋鸟) | A blackbird, especially a male European starling. |例句：The merl sang a cheerful tune from the treetop.  黑鸟在树梢唱着欢快的曲调。| 例句：The farmer disliked merls because they ate his crops. 农民不喜欢黑鸟，因为它们吃他的庄稼。 | 近义词：blackbird, starling | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

词源：From Middle English *merle, from Old French merle, from Vulgar Latin *merula, from Latin merula (“blackbird”).
