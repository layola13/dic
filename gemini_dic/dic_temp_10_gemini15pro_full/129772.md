non-fallacious:/ˌnɒn fəˈleɪʃəs/
adjective
非谬误的，合乎逻辑的
Not containing a fallacy; logically sound.
His argument was non-fallacious and persuasive. 他的论证无谬且有说服力。
non-fallacious:/ˌnɑːn fəˈleɪʃəs/
adjective
非谬误的，合乎逻辑的
Not containing a fallacy; logically sound.
Her reasoning was completely non-fallacious, leading to a clear conclusion. 她的推理完全没有谬误，得出了一个清晰的结论。
synonyms: valid, sound, logical, cogent, reasonable
antonyms: fallacious, illogical, invalid, unsound, unreasonable
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + fallacious.  "Fallacious" comes from Late Latin *fallaciosus, from Latin fallacia "deceit, trick, artifice," from fallax "deceitful, fallacious," related to fallere "to deceive, trip, cause to fall."
