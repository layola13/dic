lifelikenesses:/ˈlaɪflaɪknəsɪz/
n.
逼真；栩栩如生；酷似
The quality of appearing very similar to a real person or thing.
The wax museum was full of incredibly lifelike likenesses of famous figures.  这个蜡像馆里满是名人的栩栩如生的蜡像。
The artist captured her lifelikeness perfectly in the portrait.  这位艺术家在肖像画中完美地捕捉到了她的神韵。
synonyms: resemblance, similarity, verisimilitude, realism
antonyms: difference, dissimilarity, artificiality
noun
