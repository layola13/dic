bononia:/boʊˈnoʊniə/
n.
博洛尼亚 (意大利城市)
Bologna, a city in northern Italy.
Bologna is the capital of the Emilia-Romagna region in Northern Italy. 博洛尼亚是意大利北部艾米利亚-罗马涅大区的首府。
The University of Bologna is one of the oldest universities in the world. 博洛尼亚大学是世界上最古老的大学之一。
synonym: Felsina (ancient name)
antonym: N/A
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin Bonōnia, likely from Etruscan Velzna.
