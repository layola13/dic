basketlike:/ˈbɑːskɪtlaɪk/
adjective
像篮子的；篮状的
Resembling a basket in shape or form.
The hot air balloon was basketlike in appearance.  热气球看起来像个篮子。
The bird built a basketlike nest.  这只鸟筑了一个篮状的鸟巢。
Near Synonyms: basket-shaped, wickerlike
Antonyms: shapeless, formless
词性: 形容词
