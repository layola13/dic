lazier: /ˈleɪ.zi.ər/ (US) /ˈleɪ.zi.ə(r)/ (UK)| adj. | 更懒的 |Comparative form of "lazy," meaning less active or less willing to work. | He's even lazier than his brother. 他比他哥哥更懒。|  My cat is getting lazier and lazier. 我的猫越来越懒了。 | 近义词: idler, more indolent | 反义词: more industrious, more diligent | 词性: 形容词

lazy: /ˈleɪ.zi/ | adj. | 懒惰的 |Unwilling to work or use energy. | He's too lazy to walk to the store. 他懒得走去商店。|  She's a lazy student who never does her homework. 她是一个懒惰的学生，从不做作业。| 近义词: indolent, slothful | 反义词: industrious, diligent | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Old English *læsig, from læs "less" + *-ig (suffix forming adjectives).
