caterwaul: /ˈkætərˌwɔːl/ (US), /ˈkætəwɔːl/ (UK)
| n. vi.|猫叫春声，刺耳的尖叫声；喧闹的抗议声|A shrill howling or wailing noise, like that of a cat in heat; a loud, harsh cry or complaint. |The alley cats caterwauled all night.  巷子里的猫整夜都在叫春。|近义词：shriek, howl, wail, scream, screech|反义词：whisper, murmur, purr|词性:名词, 动词
