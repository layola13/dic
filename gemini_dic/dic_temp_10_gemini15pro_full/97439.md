infusable:/ɪnˈfjuːzəbl/| adj.|可灌输的，可注入的|Capable of being infused; able to be instilled or introduced. |The tea is infusable with various herbs and spices. 这种茶可以加入各种香草和香料。|The museum is trying to create an infusable atmosphere of creativity and innovation.  博物馆正试图营造一种可以激发创造力和创新的氛围。|近义词：imbuable, instill, introduce|反义词:resistant, impenetrable|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin *infusabilis, from Latin infusus (past participle of infundere "to pour in") + -abilis "-able."
