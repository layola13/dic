hypoaesthesia:/ˌhaɪpoʊˌɛsˈθiːʒə/ /ˌhaɪpoʊˌɛsˈθiːziə/
n.|感觉减退，知觉迟钝|A diminished capacity for physical sensation, especially of the skin.|The patient exhibited hypoaesthesia in his left arm after the surgery. 手术后，病人左臂出现感觉减退。|近义词：hypesthesia, numbness, reduced sensation|反义词：hyperesthesia, increased sensation
词性: 名词
