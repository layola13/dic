eulogizes: /ˈjuːləˌdʒaɪzɪz/
verb
赞扬，颂扬
praises; extols; speaks or writes in high praise of someone, typically someone who has recently died.
The speaker eulogized the artist's contributions to the community. 演讲者赞扬了这位艺术家对社区的贡献。
The article eulogized the fallen soldier for his bravery. 这篇文章赞扬了这位阵亡士兵的英勇。
Synonyms: praises, extols, lauds, commends, celebrates
Antonyms: criticizes, condemns, denounces, disparages
词性: 动词
