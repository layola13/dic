clotbur:/ˈklɒtbɜːr/ (US) /ˈklɑːtbɜːr/| n. |苍耳, 刺果, 蒺藜|A plant (Xanthium strumarium) having prickly, bur-like fruit that clings to clothing or fur.  It's also used metaphorically to describe something that sticks or clings persistently.|The dog came home covered in clotburs.|Synonyms: burdock, cocklebur, burr|Antonyms: N/A|noun
