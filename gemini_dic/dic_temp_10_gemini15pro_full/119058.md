metol:/ˈmɛtɒl/ (US) /ˈmiːtɒl/ (UK)| n.|米吐尔，对甲氨基苯酚硫酸盐|A developing agent used in photography, chemically known as methyl-p-aminophenol sulfate or Elon. It produces fine-grained images with good shadow detail.|Example: The photographer used metol in his developer solution for a softer tonal range. 摄影师在他的显影液中使用了米吐尔以获得更柔和的色调范围。|Example: Metol is often combined with hydroquinone in photographic developers. 米吐尔通常与对苯二酚一起用于摄影显影剂中。|Synonym: Elon, Rhodol, Pictol|Antonym: N/A|noun
