metasomatism:/ˌmɛtəˈsoʊmətɪzəm/ /ˌmetəˈsɒmətɪzəm/
|n.|交代作用，变质交代作用|The process of alteration of a rock by hydrothermal or other fluids that remove or introduce chemical constituents.|The metasomatism of the limestone produced a skarn deposit. 石灰岩的交代作用产生了一个矽卡岩矿床。|Synonyms: alteration, replacement, mineralization|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps petrification - which involves filling pores rather than replacing minerals)|Noun
