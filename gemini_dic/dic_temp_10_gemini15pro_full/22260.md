boneblack: /ˈboʊnˌblæk/ | n. | 骨黑，动物炭 | A black pigment made by charring bones. It is used as a pigment, in sugar refining, and as a deodorizer. | The artist used boneblack to create a deep, rich shadow in the painting. 艺术家用骨黑在画中创造了深邃而丰富的阴影。| Boneblack is a key ingredient in many black paints. 骨黑是许多黑色涂料的关键成分。| synonyms:  ivory black, animal charcoal | antonyms:  None | noun
