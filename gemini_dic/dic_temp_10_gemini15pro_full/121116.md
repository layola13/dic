misdating:/ˌmɪsˈdeɪtɪŋ/
n.

The misdating of the artifact led to confusion about its historical significance.  文物的错误日期标注导致对其历史意义的混淆。
He realized his misdating with her when she mentioned her actual birthday. 他意识到自己弄错了她的生日，当她提到她真正的生日时。

反义词:accurate dating
