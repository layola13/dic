internship:/ˈɪntɜːrnʃɪp/ (US) /ˈɪntɜːnʃɪp/ (UK)| n. |实习; 实习期|A period of work experience offered by an organization for a limited period of time, often to students or recent graduates.|She had a summer internship at a law firm. 她暑期在一家律师事务所实习。|Synonyms: traineeship, apprenticeship, placement|Antonyms: employment, job, career|noun
