gallicanism:/ˈɡælɪkənɪzəm/
n.
高卢主义，法国天主教自主运动
The doctrine that the French Roman Catholic Church should be largely independent of papal control.
Gallicanism was a powerful force in France for centuries. 高卢主义在法国几个世纪以来都是一股强大的力量。
The king's support of Gallicanism led to conflict with the Pope. 国王对高卢主义的支持导致了与教皇的冲突。
Synonyms: autonomy, independence, self-government
Antonyms: subservience, dependence, subjection
