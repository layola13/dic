nonchangeable:/ˌnɒnˈtʃeɪndʒəbl/ | adj. | 不可改变的，不变的 | Not able to be changed. | The laws of physics are nonchangeable. 物理定律是不可改变的。|  The company's policy on this matter is nonchangeable. 公司在这个问题上的政策是不可改变的。 | 近义词：immutable, unalterable, fixed, permanent, constant | 反义词：changeable, variable, mutable, alterable, inconstant | 词性: 形容词
Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + changeable (from change + -able, suffix meaning "capable of being")
