outweighed:/ˌaʊtˈweɪd/
verb
超过，胜过
to be greater or more important than something else
例句：The benefits of the new drug outweigh the risks. 这种新药的好处大于风险。

例句：Her enthusiasm outweighs her lack of experience. 她的热情弥补了经验的不足。

近义词：exceed, surpass, overbalance, override
反义词：be outweighed by, lose to

词性:v.
