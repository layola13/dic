frontward:/ˈfrʌntwərd/ | adj. adv.| 向前的，朝前的 | Situated at or directed toward the front; moving ahead. | The soldiers made a frontward advance. 士兵们向前推进。| 近义词：forward, forwards, ahead | 反义词: backward, rearward | 词性:形容词/副词
frontwards:/ˈfrʌntwərdz/ | adv.| 向前地 | Moving toward the front. | She stumbled frontwards and fell. 她向前踉跄了一下，摔倒了。| 近义词：forward, forwards, ahead | 反义词: backward, rearward | 词性:副词
