carvacrol:/ˈkɑːrvəkrɒl/ (US) /ˈkɑːvəkrɒl/ (UK)| n. |香芹酚；5-异丙基-2-甲基苯酚|A monoterpenoid phenol present in the essential oil of oregano and other plants.|Carvacrol is the main active component of oregano oil and is known for its antimicrobial properties. 香芹酚是牛至油的主要活性成分，以其抗菌特性而闻名。|Synonyms: 2-methyl-5-(1-methylethyl)phenol,  cymophenol|Antonyms: None|noun
