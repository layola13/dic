diuresis:/ˌdaɪjʊˈriːsɪs/
noun
利尿；排尿增多
Increased excretion of urine.  The process of excreting urine.
例句：The doctor prescribed a diuretic to promote diuresis. 医生开了利尿剂以促进排尿。

例句：The patient experienced diuresis after receiving intravenous fluids. 病人在接受静脉输液后出现了排尿增多的现象。

近义词：polyuria
反义词：oliguria, anuria
词性：noun
