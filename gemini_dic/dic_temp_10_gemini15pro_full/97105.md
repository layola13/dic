infibulation: /ɪnfɪbjʊˈleɪʃən/
noun
女性割礼，锁阴
the ritual removal of part or all of the external female genitalia and the stitching together of the vaginal opening, except for a small passage for urine and menstrual blood.  It is a form of female genital mutilation (FGM).
Infibulation is a harmful traditional practice. 女性割礼是一种有害的传统习俗。
The long-term health consequences of infibulation can be severe. 女性割礼的长期健康后果可能很严重。
Synonym:  female genital mutilation (FGM), circumcision (although this term is more accurately used for male circumcision)
Antonym: (No direct antonym exists, but one could consider terms like "genital integrity" or "bodily autonomy" as conceptually opposite.)
