buxom:/ˈbʌksəm/| adj.|丰满的，健美的（尤指女性）|Full-figured and healthy-looking, especially of a woman; having a large bosom and pleasing curves.|例句：The buxom waitress served us our drinks. 丰满的女服务员给我们上了饮料。

例句：Renaissance paintings often depict buxom women as symbols of fertility. 文艺复兴时期的绘画经常描绘丰满的女性作为生育的象征。|近义词：curvaceous, plump, ample, full-figured, voluptuous|反义词：slim, thin, slender|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *buxom, from Old English *būhsum (“pliant, obedient, submissive”), from *būgan (“to bend, bow, stoop, yield, submit”).  The meaning shifted over time from "obedient, obliging" to "lively, jolly" and then to "healthy-looking, plump."
