n.|除淤;清除淤泥|The de-sludging of the sewage treatment plant is scheduled for next month. 污水处理厂的除淤工作计划在下个月进行。|
v.|除淤;清除淤泥|We need to de-sludge the pond to improve water quality. 我们需要清理池塘的淤泥以提高水质。|
Synonym: cleaning, dredging, purging
Antonym: silting, accumulating
Etymology:  'de-' (prefix meaning removal) + 'sludge' (noun meaning thick, soft mud)
