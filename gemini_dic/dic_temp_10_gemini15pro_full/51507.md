detrains:/diːˈtreɪnz/
verb
下火车
alights from a train.
The passengers detrained at the next station.  旅客们在下一站下车。
近义词: disembarks, gets off
反义词: boards, embarks
词性:v.


detrain:/diːˈtreɪn/
verb
使…下火车
cause (someone) to alight from a train.
The soldiers were detrained near the front lines. 士兵们在前线附近下火车。
近义词: unload, discharge
反义词: load, embark
词性:v.
