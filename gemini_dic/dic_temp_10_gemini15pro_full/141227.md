oostegite:/ˈoʊ.ə.staɪt/| n. |卵壳板；育卵囊；抱卵肢|A plate-like expansion of the thoracic limb in some crustaceans, forming a brood pouch.  A structure in certain crustaceans that forms part of a brood chamber or pouch for carrying eggs.|The female uses her oostegites to protect her developing eggs.  雌性用她的育卵囊来保护她正在发育的卵。|Synonym: brood pouch|Antonym: N/A|noun
