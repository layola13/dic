balk: /bɔːk/ (US) /bɔːlk/ (UK)| v. n.| 阻碍，不愿接受，突然停止| To refuse to proceed or to do something.  To stop short and refuse to go on.| The horse balked at the jump. 这匹马在跳跃前突然止步。| synonyms: hesitate, resist, recoil, shy, flinch | antonyms: accept, embrace, proceed, continue | verb

balk: /bɔːk/ (US) /bɔːlk/ (UK)| n.| 障碍，梁木| A hindrance or obstacle. A roughly squared timber beam.| His indecision proved to be a major balk to the project. 他的犹豫不决成了项目的主要障碍。| synonyms: obstacle, hindrance, impediment, obstruction | antonyms: aid, assistance, help | noun
