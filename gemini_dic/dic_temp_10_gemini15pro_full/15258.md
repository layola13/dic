backhander: /ˈbækˌhændər/ (UK), /ˈbækˌhændər/ (US) | n. | 贿赂; 回扣; (网球等)反手击球 | A sum of money or other material reward given illicitly to someone as an inducement or reward. In sports, a shot played with the back of the hand facing the direction of the stroke. | He was accused of taking backhanders from building contractors.  他被指控收受建筑承包商的贿赂。 |  He won the point with a brilliant backhander. 他以一记精彩的反手击球赢得了这一分。 | synonyms: bribe, kickback, payoff, under-the-table payment (for illicit payment); backhand stroke, backhand volley (for sports) | antonyms: legitimate payment, donation (for illicit payment); forehand (for sports) | noun
