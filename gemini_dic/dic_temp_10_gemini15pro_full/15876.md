ballistics:/bəˈlɪstɪks/
n.
弹道学
The science that deals with the motion, behavior, and effects of projectiles, especially bullets, gravity bombs, rockets, or the like.
The police used ballistics to match the bullet to the gun. 警方使用弹道学来匹配子弹和枪支。
近义词:projectiles, trajectory
词性:名词
