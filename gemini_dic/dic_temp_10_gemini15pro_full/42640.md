countershots:/ˈkaʊntərʃɑːts/
noun
反击镜头; 反驳; 回击
shots that counter an opponent's shots, arguments that counter an opponent's arguments
The politician skillfully used countershots to deflect the journalist's pointed questions.  这位政治家巧妙地利用反驳化解了记者尖锐的提问。
The documentary offered countershots to the official government narrative. 这部纪录片对官方的政府说法提出了反驳。
Synonyms: rebuttals, counterarguments, counterattacks, ripostes
Antonyms: arguments, claims, assertions
n.
