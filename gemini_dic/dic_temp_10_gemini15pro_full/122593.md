modernism:/ˈmɒdərnɪzəm/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdərnɪzəm/ (US)| n. |现代主义|A movement in the arts and other fields that rejected traditional styles and embraced new forms of expression.|Modernism in literature was characterized by experimentation with stream of consciousness and fragmented narratives.  文学中的现代主义以对意识流和碎片化叙事的实验为特征。|Synonyms: avant-garde, modernity|Antonyms: traditionalism, classicism|Noun
