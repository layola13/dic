noncausation:/ˌnɒnkɔːˈzeɪʃən/
n.
非因果关系; 无因果关系
The principle of noncausation asserts that two events may be correlated without one causing the other.  非因果关系原则断言，两个事件可能相互关联，而一个事件并不导致另一个事件。
Noncausation between two variables doesn't necessarily mean they are unrelated. 两个变量之间不存在因果关系并不一定意味着它们不相关。
Synonyms: non-causality, absence of causation
Antonyms: causation, causality
