barneys:/ˈbɑːrniz/| n. |巴尼斯纽约精品店; 巴尼斯; |Barney's New York, often referred to simply as Barneys, was an American luxury department store chain founded in 1923.  It was known for its high-end designer collections, trendy merchandise, and upscale clientele.| Barney's was a popular destination for fashion-conscious shoppers. 巴尼斯是时尚达人们的热门去处。|Synonyms: department store, retailer, boutique |Antonyms: discount store, thrift store |noun

barney:/ˈbɑːrni/| n. |争吵; 纠纷; |A loud, angry argument or disagreement.|They got into a terrible barney about the car keys. 他们因为车钥匙大吵了一架。|Synonyms: argument, quarrel, dispute, row, squabble|Antonyms: agreement, harmony, accord|noun
