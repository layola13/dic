overbait:/ˌoʊvərˈbeɪt/
verb
过度诱饵；下饵过多
To use too much bait, especially when fishing.
The fish were wary because the angler had overbaited the hook. 鱼很警惕，因为钓鱼者在鱼钩上放了太多的诱饵。
He overbaited his hook and scared the fish away. 他在鱼钩上放了太多的诱饵，把鱼吓跑了。
synonyms: overfeed (in the context of fishing)
antonyms: underbait
