overstrident: /ˌoʊvərˈstraɪd(ə)nt/ | adj. | 过于刺耳的，声音过尖的 | Having or making a harsh, grating, or excessively shrill sound.  | Her overstrident voice made it difficult to listen to her for long periods. 她过于刺耳的声音让人很难长时间听她说话。 | The teacher's overstrident tone silenced the unruly class. 老师刺耳的声音让不守规矩的班级安静了下来。 | 近义词: shrill, harsh, strident, piercing | 反义词: soft, gentle, mellow, soothing | 词性: adjective

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "strident" (from Latin "stridens," present participle of "stridere" meaning "to creak, grate, or make a harsh sound").
