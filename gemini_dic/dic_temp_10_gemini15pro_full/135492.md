nonphonetical:/ˌnɒn.fəˈnet.ɪ.kəl/ | adj. | 非语音的，不表音的 | Not relating to or representing the sounds of speech.  Relating to a system of writing that does not represent pronunciation directly. |  The Chinese writing system is largely nonphonetical, making it difficult for learners to pronounce unfamiliar characters.  汉字系统很大程度上是非语音的，这使得学习者很难读出不熟悉的汉字。 | synonyms: non-phonetic, unphonetic | antonyms: phonetic, alphabetic | word type: adjective
