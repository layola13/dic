defeudalize:/diːˈfjuːdəlaɪz/
verb
废除封建制度；消除封建残余
To abolish feudalism; to free from feudal characteristics or influences.
The French Revolution sought to defeudalize French society. 法国大革命试图废除法国社会的封建制度。
Land reforms were implemented to defeudalize the agricultural sector. 土地改革是为了消除农业部门的封建残余而实施的。
近义词: modernize, liberalize, reform
反义词: feudalize
词性: verb
