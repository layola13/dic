oversimplification:/ˌoʊvərˌsɪmplɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/| n.|过度简化|The explanation of the complex issue was an oversimplification, leaving out many crucial details.|这个对复杂问题的解释过于简化，忽略了许多关键细节。|近义词：reductionism, distortion, misrepresentation|反义词:nuance, elaboration, complexity|词性:名词
oversimplify:/ˌoʊvərˈsɪmplɪfaɪ/| v.|过度简化|Politicians often oversimplify complex issues to appeal to a broader audience.|政治家经常过度简化复杂问题以吸引更广泛的受众。|近义词：reduce, distort, misrepresent|反义词:elaborate, nuance, complicate|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + simplification, simplification from simplify + -fication.  Simplify from simple + -ify. Ultimately from Latin *simplus "single, simple"
