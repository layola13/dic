cuspidated:/ˈkʌspɪdeɪtɪd/
|adj.|尖的，有尖头的|Having cusps or points; ending in a sharp point.|The cuspidated leaves of the holly tree are a distinctive feature. 冬青树尖尖的叶子是其显著特征。|近义词：pointed, sharp, acuminate, acute|反义词:blunt, rounded|词性:形容词
词源:From Latin *cuspidatus*, past participle of *cuspidare* "to make pointed," from *cuspis* "point."
