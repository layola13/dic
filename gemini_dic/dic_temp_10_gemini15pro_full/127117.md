nationless:/ˈneɪʃənləs/
adjective
无国籍的; 没有国籍的
Having no nationality; stateless.
Many refugees find themselves effectively nationless, without the protection of any government. 许多难民发现自己实际上是无国籍的，没有任何政府的保护。
Synonyms: stateless, denationalized, expatriated, displaced
Antonyms: national, citizen
词性: 形容词

nationless:/ˈneɪʃənləs/
adjective
无国家归属感的
Lacking national identity or patriotism.
He felt nationless, adrift in a world that no longer seemed familiar.  他感到没有国家归属感，漂泊在一个不再熟悉的世界里。
Synonyms: rootless, unpatriotic, alienated
Antonyms: patriotic, nationalistic
词性: 形容词
