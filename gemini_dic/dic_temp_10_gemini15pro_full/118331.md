mergences:/ˈmɜːrdʒənz/
|n.|合并，融合|The merger of the two companies created a giant in the industry. 两家公司的合并造就了该行业的巨头。| Mergers and acquisitions are common in the business world. 并购在商业世界中很常见。|近义词：amalgamations, consolidations, unions|反义词：separations, divisions, splits|词性:名词

Etymology:
From the verb "merge," which comes from Old French "mergier" and ultimately from Latin "mergere" meaning "to plunge, immerse, sink." The "-es" ending indicates the plural form.
