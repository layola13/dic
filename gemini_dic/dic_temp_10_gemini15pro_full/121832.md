misreligion:/ˌmɪsrɪˈlɪdʒən/
n.
错误的宗教信仰；异端邪说
A wrong or false religion; heresy.
例句：
He viewed their beliefs as a form of misreligion, a perversion of true faith.
他把他们的信仰看作是一种错误的宗教信仰，是对真正信仰的歪曲。

例句：
The pamphlet denounced the practices of other sects as misreligion.
这本小册子谴责其他教派的习俗为异端邪说。

近义词：heresy, heterodoxy, false doctrine, fallacy
反义词：orthodoxy, true faith, true religion
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (wrong, bad) + religion.  The prefix "mis-" comes from Old English "mis-" meaning "wrongly, badly, astray." "Religion" comes from Old French "religion" and ultimately from Latin "religio" meaning "respect for what is sacred, reverence for the gods, sense of right, moral obligation; conscientiousness, piety."
