anaesthetized:/əˈniːsθətaɪzd/ | adj. | 麻醉的，失去知觉的 |  Deprived of feeling or sensation, especially by a drug.  Rendered insensible to pain or other sensation. | The patient was anaesthetized before the surgery. 病人在手术前被麻醉了。| The anaesthetized lion was easily transported. 被麻醉的狮子很容易就被运走了。 |  numb, insensible, unconscious | conscious, aware, sensitive | adjective

anaesthesia:/ˌænɪsˈθiːʒə/ | n. | 麻醉，麻木 | Loss of sensation, especially pain, induced by drugs; the branch of medicine concerned with the administration of anaesthetics. | The patient was given general anaesthesia for the operation. 病人手术时进行了全身麻醉。| Local anaesthesia was used to numb the area. 局部麻醉被用来麻痹该区域。| narcosis, numbness | consciousness, sensation | noun
