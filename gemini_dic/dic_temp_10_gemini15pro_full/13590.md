attenuate:/əˈtenjʊeɪt/ /əˈtɛnjuˌeɪt/| v. adj.|减少，减弱，使稀薄|to reduce the force, effect, or value of something|The drug attenuates the effects of the virus.  这种药物减弱了病毒的作用。|近义词：weaken, diminish, lessen, reduce, decrease|反义词: strengthen, amplify, increase, enhance|词性:动词, 形容词

attenuate:/əˈtenjʊeɪt/ /əˈtɛnjuˌeɪt/| adj.|减弱的，稀薄的|reduced in force, effect, or value|The signal was attenuated by the thick walls. 信号被厚厚的墙壁减弱了。|近义词：weakened, diminished, lessened, reduced, decreased|反义词: strengthened, amplified, increased, enhanced|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin attenuatus, past participle of attenuare "to make thin, weaken," from ad- "to" + tenuis "thin."
