nonmaturest:/nɒn.məˈtjʊər.ɪst/ | adj. | 最不成熟的 |  Superlative form of nonmature (meaning not mature). It describes something as being the least developed or least ripe among a group of things or people. |  Example: Of all the apples in the basket, this one is the nonmaturest.  这篮子苹果里，这个最不成熟。| Example:  He exhibited the nonmaturest behavior at the conference, interrupting speakers and making inappropriate jokes. 他在会议上表现得最不成熟，打断发言者并开不恰当的玩笑。 | Synonyms: least mature, most immature, least developed, greenest | Antonyms: ripest, most mature, most developed | Word Type: Adjective
