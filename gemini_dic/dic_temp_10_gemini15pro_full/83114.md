gustful:/ˈɡʌstfəl/| adj.|充满阵风的，刮大风的|Characterized by strong gusts of wind. Subject to sudden outbursts, as of passion or emotion. |例句：The gustful winds howled through the trees. 阵阵狂风呼啸着穿过树林。

例句：His gustful temper often frightened his employees. 他暴躁的脾气经常吓坏他的员工。|近义词：windy, blustery, tempestuous, passionate, impetuous|反义词：calm, peaceful, tranquil|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From gust + -ful.  "Gust" comes from Old Norse *gustr "a sudden blast of wind". "-ful" is a suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by".
