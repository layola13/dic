mavourneen:/məˈvɔːrnɪn/ | n. |我的爱人，我的宝贝（爱尔兰英语）|A term of endearment, from Irish Gaelic *mo mhuirnín*, meaning "my darling".  It is often used in a romantic context, but can also be used affectionately towards family members. |例句：
Oh, mavourneen, my heart aches for you. 哦，我的爱人，我的心为你痛。

Come here, my mavourneen, and let me hold you. 过来，我的宝贝，让我抱抱你。

|近义词：darling, sweetheart, beloved, my love|反义词:enemy, foe|词性:名词
