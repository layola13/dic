idlenesses:/ˈaɪdl̩nəsɪz/
n.
懒惰，闲散，无所事事
The state of being idle; inactivity; laziness.
His idlenesses led to his failure in the exam. 他的懒惰导致他考试不及格。
synonyms: laziness, inactivity, sloth, indolence, shiftlessness
antonyms: industry, diligence, activity, busyness
noun
