defrauders:/dɪˈfrɔːdərz/
n.
诈骗犯，骗子
People who cheat others out of money or goods.
The defrauders were eventually caught and sentenced to prison. 那些诈骗犯最终被抓获并被判处监禁。
Synonyms: scammers, swindlers, cheats, con artists
Antonyms: philanthropists, donors
