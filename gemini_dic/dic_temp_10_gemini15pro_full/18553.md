benefiting:/ˈbɛnɪfɪtɪŋ/
Verb, present participle
受益；得益于
Receiving an advantage; experiencing something positive.
The charity is benefiting from the increased donations.  这个慈善机构受益于捐款的增加。
The new policy is benefiting the entire community. 新政策使整个社区受益。
Synonyms: profiting, gaining, improving
Antonyms: harming, hurting, damaging
Verb

Etymology: From the Old French bienfet, from bien (well) + fet (deed).
