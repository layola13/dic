outgoingness:/ˌaʊtˈɡoʊɪŋnəs/
n.
外向；开朗；友善；乐于交际
The quality of being friendly and enjoying meeting and talking to people.
Her outgoingness and enthusiasm made her a popular member of the team. 她的外向和热情使她成为团队中受欢迎的成员。
She has a natural outgoingness that draws people to her. 她有一种天生的外向性格，吸引着人们靠近她。
extroversion, sociability, gregariousness
introversion, shyness, reserve
noun
