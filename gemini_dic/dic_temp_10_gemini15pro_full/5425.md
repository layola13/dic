almightys:/ɔːlˈmaɪtiz/ | adj. |万能的，全能的|  An archaic or humorous form of almighty, used especially in exclamations. It's a plural form used for emphasis or in addressing a group of powerful entities (though grammatically unusual). |  "Almightys above! What have you done?"  万能的主啊！你做了什么？| Near Synonyms: omnipotent, all-powerful | Antonyms: powerless, weak | Word type: Adjective
