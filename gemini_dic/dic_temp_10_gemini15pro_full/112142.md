loofah:/ˈluːfə/ (US), /ˈluːfɑː/ (UK)| n. | 丝瓜；丝瓜络；丝瓜瓤 | A tropical climbing plant bearing a large, green, ridged fruit with a fibrous interior. Also refers to the dried fibrous interior of the fruit, used as a sponge. | I use a loofah in the shower. 我在淋浴时用丝瓜络。| synonym: sponge, scrubber | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "cloth") | noun

loofahs:/ˈluːfəz/ (US), /ˈluːfɑːz/ (UK)| n. | 丝瓜；丝瓜络；丝瓜瓤（复数） | Plural form of loofah. |  I bought some new loofahs at the market. 我在市场上买了一些新的丝瓜络。 | synonym: sponges, scrubbers | antonym: (No direct antonym) | noun
