antitheistic:/ˌæntiθiˈɪstɪk/
adjective
反神论的；反对神论的
Opposed to the belief in the existence of a god or gods.
His antitheistic views were not popular in the small, religious town. 他在那个宗教氛围浓厚的小镇里，他的反神论观点并不受欢迎。
The author's antitheistic stance is clear throughout the novel.  作者的反神论立场在整部小说中都非常明确。

synonyms:atheistic, godless, ungodly, irreligious
antonyms:theistic, religious, devout, pious
