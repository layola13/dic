hairdos:/ˈhɛrdoʊz/ (American) /ˈhɛːdəʊz/ (British)| n. |发型，发式|A style in which hair is cut and arranged.  This word is a plural form of "hairdo". |She tried out several different hairdos before the prom. 她尝试了几个不同的发型，为舞会做准备。|Synonyms: hairstyles, coiffures|Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a physical style.  One could consider "messy hair" or "unstyled hair")|Noun
