floodlights:/ˈflʌdˌlaɪts/ /ˈflʌd.laɪts/| n.|泛光灯，泛光照明设备|A large, powerful lamp used to illuminate an outdoor area at night, especially a sports field or other large space. |The floodlights illuminated the stadium for the night game.  泛光灯照亮了体育场，为夜间比赛做准备。

The floodlights were so bright they almost blinded me. 泛光灯太亮了，几乎让我眼花。|近义词： spotlights, searchlights |反义词: N/A|名词


floodlight:/ˈflʌdˌlaɪt/ /ˈflʌd.laɪt/| vt.|用泛光灯照亮|To illuminate with a floodlight. |They floodlit the building for the grand opening.  他们用泛光灯照亮了大楼，以庆祝盛大开幕。

The stage was floodlit, making the performers clearly visible. 舞台被泛光灯照亮，使表演者清晰可见。|近义词： illuminate, light up |反义词: darken |动词

Etymology:
From *flood* (n.) + *light* (n.).  Meaning "light that floods an area" is from 1905.
