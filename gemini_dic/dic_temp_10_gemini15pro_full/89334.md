homogony:/həˈmɒɡəni/ /həˈmɑːɡəni/| n. |同配生殖，同型生殖|Reproduction involving the union of gametes that are alike in size and structure.  The production of offspring whose parents are similar in form and structure.|The biologist studied the species' homogony as part of his research on reproductive strategies.  这位生物学家研究了该物种的同配生殖，作为其生殖策略研究的一部分。|近义词：isogamy|反义词：heterogamy|词性:noun

词源:
From Ancient Greek ὁμός (homós, “same”) + γόνος (gónos, “offspring”).
