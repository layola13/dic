haziest:/ˈheɪziɪst/| adj.|最朦胧的，最模糊的|Superlative form of hazy: most hazy.  Covered by or filled with haze; misty.  Vague or ill-defined.|The haziest days of summer are often the hottest. 夏天最朦胧的日子通常也是最热的日子。|Synonyms: mistiest, foggiest, murkiest, vaguest, unclear, obscure|Antonyms: clearest, brightest, sharpest, distinct|adjective
