nonplayer:/ˌnɒnˈpleɪə(r)/ /ˌnɑːnˈpleɪər/ | n. | 非玩家角色；非参赛选手 |  A character in a video game or role-playing game that is controlled by the game's artificial intelligence, rather than by a human player. Also refers to someone who doesn't participate in a game or sport. |  The nonplayer characters in this game are surprisingly intelligent.  这个游戏里的非玩家角色智能程度惊人。| Synonym: NPC, bystander, observer | Antonym: player, participant | noun

non-playing:/ˌnɒnˈpleɪɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnˈpleɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 非比赛的；非演奏的 | Not involved in playing a game or an instrument. | The non-playing members of the team provide support.  球队的非参赛队员提供支持。| Synonym: inactive, nonparticipating | Antonym: playing, participating, active | adjective
