dynastic:/dɪˈnæstɪk/ | adj. |王朝的，世袭的| Relating to a dynasty or line of hereditary rulers. | The dynastic struggles of the medieval period shaped the political landscape of Europe. 中世纪时期的王朝斗争塑造了欧洲的政治格局。

The museum houses a remarkable collection of dynastic artifacts from the Ming Dynasty.  这家博物馆收藏了大量明朝的王朝文物。| 近义词：hereditary, ancestral, familial | 反义词：non-hereditary, elective | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *dynastique*, from Late Latin *dynasticus*, from Greek *dunasteia* "lordship, power, rule," from *dunastes* "lord, master," from *dunamai* "to be able, to have power."
