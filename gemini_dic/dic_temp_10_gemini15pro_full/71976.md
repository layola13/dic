flimsiest:/ˈflɪmziɪst/| adj.|最薄的，最脆弱的，最不经用的|Superlative adjective of flimsy: most flimsy.  Meaning very thin, light, and easily damaged; not strong or solid; and (of an excuse, argument, etc.) weak and unconvincing.|The evidence against him was flimsiest. 对他的证据最薄弱。|近义词：weakest, thinnest, slightest, frailest, most fragile|反义词：strongest, sturdiest, toughest, most robust|词性:形容词
词源: From flimsy + -est.  Flimsy itself is of uncertain origin, possibly from a dialectal word meaning "light, shallow."
