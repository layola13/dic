closetful: /ˈklɒzɪtfʊl/ (UK) /ˈklɑːzɪtfʊl/ (US)
|n.|满满一柜子，满满一衣橱|A closetful is the amount that fills a closet.  It refers to a large quantity of something, usually clothing or other items typically stored in a closet.|She has a closetful of designer shoes. 她有一柜子的名牌鞋。|Synonyms: abundance, plethora, profusion|Antonyms: lack, scarcity|noun
