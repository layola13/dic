non-Darwinians:/ˌnɒn dɑːˈwɪniənz/ | n. | 非达尔文主义者 |  People who do not subscribe to the theory of evolution by natural selection as proposed by Charles Darwin. |  The non-Darwinians proposed alternative explanations for the diversity of life. 非达尔文主义者对生命的多样性提出了其他解释。| synonyms: anti-Darwinists, creationists (sometimes) | antonyms: Darwinists, evolutionists | noun

non-Darwinians:/ˌnɑːn dɑːrˈwɪniənz/ | adj. | 非达尔文主义的 | Relating to theories of evolution other than Darwinism. | Their non-Darwinian approach focused on sudden leaps in evolution rather than gradual change.  他们的非达尔文主义方法侧重于进化的突然飞跃，而不是渐进的变化。| synonyms: anti-Darwinian | antonyms: Darwinian | adjective
