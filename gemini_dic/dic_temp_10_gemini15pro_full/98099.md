inquorate:/ɪnˈkwɔːrət/
adjective
法定人数不足的，无效的
Not having enough members present to be able to make official decisions.
The meeting was inquorate, so no decisions could be made. 会议因人数不足而无法作出任何决定。
近义词：invalid, insufficient, deficient
反义词：quorate
词性：adjective
