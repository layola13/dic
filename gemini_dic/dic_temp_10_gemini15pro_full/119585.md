microzyme:/ˈmaɪkroʊzaɪm/| n. |微生物，酵素|A microorganism, especially one used in the production of fermented products.  It can also refer to an enzyme.|"Microzymes are added to the compost to accelerate the decomposition process."  微生物被添加到堆肥中以加速分解过程。|Synonyms: microorganism, enzyme, ferment|Antonyms: None|noun
