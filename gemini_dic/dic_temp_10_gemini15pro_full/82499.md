gruyere:/ɡruːˈjɛər/ /ɡriˈjɛər/
n.
格鲁耶尔干酪, 格吕耶尔奶酪
A hard yellow cheese, named after the town of Gruyères in Switzerland, having a nutty, slightly sweet flavor and characteristic holes.
Example: I love a good sandwich with gruyere and ham. 我喜欢夹着格鲁耶尔干酪和火腿的三明治。

Example: The chef grated some gruyere over the French onion soup.  厨师在法式洋葱汤上磨了一些格鲁耶尔干酪。


近义词:Swiss cheese, Emmental (similar cheeses with holes)
反义词:(No direct antonym for a specific type of cheese)
词性:noun
