ideographics:/ˌaɪdɪəˈɡræfɪks/| n.|表意文字|A system of writing in which each character represents an idea or concept, rather than a specific sound, as in alphabetic writing systems.|Chinese characters are ideographics, representing whole words or concepts. 汉字是表意文字，代表整个单词或概念。|近义词：ideograms, logograms|反义词：phonograms|词性:名词
