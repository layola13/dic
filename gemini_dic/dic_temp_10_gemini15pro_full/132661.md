nondisintegrating: /ˌnɒndɪsˈɪntɪɡreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不崩解的，不分解的 | Not breaking apart or decomposing; remaining whole or intact. | The nondisintegrating tablets are designed for sustained release. 这种不崩解的药片是为持续释放而设计的。| The archaeologist found a nondisintegrating piece of pottery. 考古学家发现了一块没有碎裂的陶器。|  近义词：stable, durable, lasting, enduring | 反义词：disintegrating, crumbling, decomposing | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  From Latin *non "not".
disintegrate (verb): From dis- "apart, asunder" + integrate (from Latin integratus, past participle of integrare "make whole").
