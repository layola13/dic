merostomata:/mɛrəˈstɒmətə/| n. |肢口纲|A class of chelicerate arthropods that includes horseshoe crabs.  They are characterized by having book gills, a hard carapace, and a long, spine-like telson.|Horseshoe crabs belong to the Merostomata.  鲎属于肢口纲。|Synonym: Xiphosura (Note: Some sources consider Xiphosura a subclass within Merostomata)|Antonym: N/A|Noun
