coniferous:/kəˈnɪfərəs/
adjective
针叶的；结球果的
Relating to or belonging to the conifers; bearing cones.
The forest is predominantly coniferous, with pine and fir trees dominating the landscape. 这片森林以针叶树为主，松树和冷杉树占据了主要景观。
Coniferous trees thrive in cold climates. 针叶树在寒冷的气候中茁壮成长。

近义词:cone-bearing
反义词:deciduous

词性:adjective
