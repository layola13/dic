caboose:/kəˈbuːs/| n.|守车，(尤指铁路货运列车末尾的)乘务员专用车厢|A caboose is a small car at the rear of a freight train, typically used by the train crew.|The caboose was brightly painted and stood out against the long line of freight cars. 守车被漆成鲜艳的颜色，在长长的货运列车中格外显眼。|The conductor made his way to the caboose to check the manifests. 列车长前往守车检查货物清单。|Synonyms: brake van, guard's van|Antonyms: engine, locomotive|noun
