formulates:/ˈfɔːrmjəleɪts/
verb
制定；规划；构想；确切地表达
creates or devises methodically (a strategy or a proposal).
The committee formulated a new plan. 委员会制定了一个新计划。
expresses (an idea or plan) in a precise or systematic way.
She carefully formulated her response. 她仔细地表达了自己的回应。
synonyms: devise, conceive, frame, develop, articulate, express
antonyms: dismantle, abandon, neglect
v.
