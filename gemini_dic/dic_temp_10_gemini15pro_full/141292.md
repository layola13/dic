open-ended:/ˌoʊpənˈɛndɪd/| adj.|开放式的，无限制的|Allowing or requiring a free response rather than a limited choice from a set of options.|The survey included several open-ended questions about their experiences.|近义词：unrestricted, unlimited, flexible|反义词: restricted, limited, closed, fixed|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "open" + "end" + "-ed".  "Open" traces back to Old English "open"; "end" from Old English "ende". The "-ed" suffix transforms it into an adjective.
