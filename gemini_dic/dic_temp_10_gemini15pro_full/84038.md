half-fascinated:/ˌhæfˈfæsɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
半着迷的，又爱又恨的
partially fascinated; intrigued but also slightly repelled or uneasy.
She watched the snake charmer with a half-fascinated, half-repelled expression. 她带着一种半是着迷半是厌恶的表情看着耍蛇人。
近义词:ambivalent, intrigued, captivated, curious
反义词:uninterested, bored, indifferent, repulsed
词性:adjective
