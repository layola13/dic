pastinesses:/ˈpeɪstinɪsɪz/| n. |苍白；黏稠；面色病态|The plural form of pastiness, referring to instances or areas of paleness or stickiness.  It can describe the unhealthy appearance of skin, or the thick, gluey texture of a substance.|Her pastinesses were exacerbated by the dim lighting, making her look unwell.  昏暗的灯光加剧了她病态的苍白，使她看起来很不舒服。

The pastinesses of the wallpaper paste made it difficult to apply smoothly.  壁纸糊的黏稠使其难以平滑地涂抹。|近义词：pallors, whiteness, stickiness, clamminess|反义词：ruddiness, healthiness, dryness|词性:noun
