overmasterfulness:/ˌoʊvərˈmæstərfʊlnəs/
noun
专横跋扈；盛气凌人
The state or quality of being overmastering; excessive dominance or imperiousness.
His overmasterfulness alienated his colleagues. 他的专横跋扈使他的同事们疏远了他。
Synonyms: imperiousness, dominance, arrogance, high-handedness, authoritarianism
Antonyms: humility, modesty, submissiveness, meekness


ETYMOLOGY->
From overmastering + -fulness.  Overmastering comes from the verb "overmaster," from "over-" (excessive) + "master" (to control).  "-fulness" is a suffix indicating the state or quality of being full of something.
