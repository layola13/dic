abrahamic:/ˌeɪbrəˈhæmɪk/
adjective
亚伯拉罕的；信奉亚伯拉罕诸教的
Relating to Abraham, the biblical patriarch, or the religions (Judaism, Christianity, and Islam) that revere him as their founder.
Judaism, Christianity, and Islam are Abrahamic religions. 犹太教、基督教和伊斯兰教都是亚伯拉罕宗教。

The Abrahamic covenant is central to these faiths. 亚伯拉罕之约是这些信仰的核心。
