onlooker: /ˈɒnˌlʊkər/ (British) /ˈɑːnˌlʊkər/ (American)| n.|旁观者，观看者|A person who watches something that is happening but is not involved in it.|Many onlookers watched the parade. 许多旁观者观看了游行。|Synonyms: spectator, observer, witness, bystander|Antonyms: participant, player|Noun
