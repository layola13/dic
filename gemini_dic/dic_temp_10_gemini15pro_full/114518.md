malagash:/ˌmæləˈɡæʃ/| n.|马拉加什(加拿大新斯科舍省的一个地方)|A small fishing village located in Nova Scotia, Canada, known for its beaches and warm waters.|We spent our summer vacation in Malagash, enjoying the beautiful beaches. 我们在马拉加什度过了暑假，享受美丽的海滩。|Synonyms: seaside town, coastal village|Antonyms: inland city, metropolis|Noun
Etymology:  Likely derived from the Mi'kmaq language, although the exact meaning is uncertain.  Some sources suggest a connection to the word "malikewechk," meaning "it breaks up into branches," possibly referring to the local river system.
