deplorably:/dɪˈplɔːrəbli/
adverb
悲惨地，凄惨地
in a way that is very bad and unacceptable
The situation was handled deplorably.  这个情况处理得非常糟糕。
The building was deplorably maintained.  这栋楼的维护状况非常糟糕。
近义词: lamentably, regrettably, shamefully, disastrously, terribly
反义词: admirably, commendably, excellently
