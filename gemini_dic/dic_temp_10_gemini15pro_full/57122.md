douter:/ˈdaʊtər/ (US), /ˈdaʊtə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 怀疑者；持怀疑态度的人 |A doubter is someone who questions or holds reservations about something. |  Many doubters questioned the feasibility of the project. 许多怀疑者质疑该项目的可行性。

He remained a doubter despite the evidence. 尽管有证据，他仍然持怀疑态度。|近义词：skeptic, cynic, questioner|反义词：believer, devotee, adherent|词性: noun
