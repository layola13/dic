Etymology:
From Late Middle English *mirre, from Old French mirre, from Latin myrrha, from Ancient Greek μύρρα (mýrra), from Semitic, perhaps ultimately from Aramaic ܡܪܝܪܐ (mərīrā, “bitter”).
