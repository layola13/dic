ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *folgian, from Proto-Germanic *fulgijaną, from Proto-Indo-European *pl̥ḱ-éyeti (“to follow”). Cognate with Dutch volgen (“to follow”), German folgen (“to follow”), Swedish följa (“to follow”).
