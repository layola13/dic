overcoat: /ˈəʊvəkəʊt/ (UK) /ˈoʊvərkoʊt/ (US)
n.
大衣，外套
A heavy coat worn over other clothes for warmth in cold weather.
例句：He wore a thick overcoat to protect himself from the biting wind.  他穿了一件厚大衣来抵御刺骨的寒风。

例句：My overcoat kept me warm throughout the winter. 我的大衣整个冬天都让我很暖和。

近义词：topcoat, greatcoat, winter coat
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) light jacket, summer clothes
词性: Noun
