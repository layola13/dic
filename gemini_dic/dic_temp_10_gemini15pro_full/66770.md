exoterical:/ˌeksəˈterɪkəl/| adj.|外界的，公开的，通俗的|Intended for or likely to be understood by the general public.|His exoterical teachings were easy to understand, while his esoteric doctrines were reserved for his inner circle. 他的公开教义很容易理解，而他的深奥教义只留给他的核心圈子。|Synonyms: public, popular, accessible, understandable|Antonyms: esoteric, private, secret, obscure|adjective
