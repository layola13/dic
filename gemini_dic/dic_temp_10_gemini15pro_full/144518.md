outtwine:/ˌaʊtˈtwaɪn/ | v. |缠绕，交织|To twist or twine around something, especially so as to enclose or entangle it.|The ivy outtwined the oak tree. 常春藤缠绕着橡树。|近义词:intertwine, entwine, interweave|反义词:untwine, disentangle|词性:动词

Etymology:
From out- + twine.  "Out-" acts as an intensifier, suggesting a thorough or complete entwining.  "Twine" comes from Old English *twinian, of Germanic origin.
