drunkest:/ˈdrʌŋkɪst/ | adj. | 最醉的 |  Superlative form of the adjective "drunk," meaning intoxicated by alcohol to the highest degree. | He was the drunkest person at the party. 他是聚会上最醉的人。|  The drunkest I've ever been was at my 21st birthday. 我最醉的一次是在我21岁生日那天。 | 近义词: most intoxicated, most inebriated | 反义词: soberest, most sober | 词性:形容词
