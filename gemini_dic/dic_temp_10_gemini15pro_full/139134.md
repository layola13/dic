obequitate: /ˌoʊbˈɛkwɪteɪt/
verb
骑马；使骑马
to ride on horseback; to cause to ride on horseback
He obequitated his new stallion across the fields. 他骑着他的新种马穿过田野。
The king obequitated the prince upon a magnificent white charger. 国王让王子骑上一匹壮丽的白马。
近义词: ride, mount
反义词: dismount, alight
词性: verb

PHRASE->
This word is rarely used and therefore doesn't have common phrases associated with it.  Since it means "to ride on horseback," phrases involving horseback riding could be used instead.  For example:

1. go horseback riding 去骑马
2. take a horseback ride  去骑马
3. horseback riding lessons 骑马课程
4. horseback riding trails  骑马路线
5. horseback riding adventures 骑马探险
