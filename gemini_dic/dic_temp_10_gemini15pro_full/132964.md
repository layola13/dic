nonemployee:/ˌnɒnɪmˈplɔɪiː/ /ˌnɑːnɪmˈplɔɪiː/
n.
非雇员；非员工
A person who is not employed by a particular company or organization.
The nonemployees were not allowed access to the company's confidential information.  非雇员不被允许访问公司的机密信息。
The company hired a consultant, a nonemployee, to advise on the project. 公司聘请了一位非员工顾问就该项目提供建议。
non-worker, freelancer, contractor, consultant, outside worker
employee, staff member, worker
noun
