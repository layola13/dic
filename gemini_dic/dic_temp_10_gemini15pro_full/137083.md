nonstudents:/ˌnɑːnˈstuːdənts/
noun
非学生
People who are not enrolled in any educational institution.
The library restricts access to nonstudents during exam periods.  图书馆在考试期间限制非学生进入。
Nonstudents are not allowed to use the university gym. 非学生不得使用大学体育馆。

synonym: non-academics, outsiders
antonym: students, pupils
n.
