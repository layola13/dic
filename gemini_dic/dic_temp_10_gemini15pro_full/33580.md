ciliform:/ˈsɪliəfɔːrm/| adj.|纤毛状的，毛状的|Having the form or shape of cilia; resembling cilia.|例句：The paramecium has ciliform appendages that help it move. 草履虫有纤毛状的附属物帮助它移动。

例句：The bacteria possess ciliform structures on their surface.  这种细菌表面具有纤毛状结构。|近义词：ciliated, hairy, pilose|反义词:smooth, glabrous|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ciliformis*, from cilium (“eyelash, eyelid”) + -formis (“-shaped”).
