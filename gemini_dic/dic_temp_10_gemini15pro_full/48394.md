dedesignates:/ˌdiːˈdɛzɪɡneɪts/
verb
取消指定；撤销任命
To remove the designation or appointment from someone or something; to officially state that something is no longer to be used for a particular purpose.
The area was dedesignated as a national park.  该地区被取消了国家公园的称号。
The president dedesignated him as special envoy. 总统解除了他的特使职务。

近义词: remove, revoke, cancel, withdraw, rescind
反义词: designate, appoint, assign

词性: verb (动词)
