deine:/ˈdaɪnə/|pron.|你的（德语）|Your (German possessive pronoun, informal singular, used for people you are close to).  It corresponds to "your" in English when addressing one person informally.|例句：Ist das deine Tasche? = Is that your bag? 这是你的包吗？

例句：Deine Augen sind wunderschön. = Your eyes are beautiful. 你的眼睛很漂亮。|近义词：(depending on context and formality) Ihr (formal singular), euer (plural informal), Ihr (formal plural)|反义词:mein (my), sein (his), ihr (her), unser (our), euer/Ihr (your plural) |词性:Possessive Pronoun
