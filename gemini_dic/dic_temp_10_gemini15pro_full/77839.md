genericize:/dʒəˈnɛrɪsaɪz/ | v. | 使通用化；使普及化；成为通用名称 | To make something generic or applicable to a general category; to become a generic term. | Kleenex has become genericized to refer to any facial tissue.  舒洁已经成为任何面巾纸的通用名称。

The company tried to prevent their brand name from being genericized.  该公司试图阻止其品牌名称被通用化。 | 近义词: generalize, popularize, universalize | 反义词: specialize, individualize, specify | 词性: 动词
