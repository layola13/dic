newsinesses:/ˈnjuːzɪnəsɪz/| n. |新闻价值；新闻性|The quality of being newsworthy; the extent to which a story is considered likely to interest the public. |The editor questioned the newsinesses of the story about the local dog show. 编辑质疑了关于当地狗展的报道的新闻价值。|The newsinesses of the event waned after the first few days.  几天后，该事件的新闻价值就减弱了。|近义词：newsworthiness, topicality, relevance|反义词：insignificance, triviality|词性:noun
