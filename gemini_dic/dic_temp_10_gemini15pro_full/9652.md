antisupernatural:/ˌæntiˌsuːpərˈnætʃrəl/
adjective
反超自然的
opposed to or denying the existence of anything supernatural; relating to such opposition or denial.
The professor presented an antisupernatural explanation for the strange occurrences. 教授对这些奇怪的事件提出了一个反超自然的解释。
Near Synonyms: nonspiritual, naturalist, secular, worldly, earthly
Antonyms: supernatural, paranormal, occult, mystic
词性: adjective
