nonaccommodating:/ˌnɒnəkɒməˈdeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
不迁就的，不妥协的，不乐于助人的
Unwilling to make adjustments or compromises to suit others' needs; unhelpful.
例句：The nonaccommodating hotel staff refused to change our room even though it was next to a noisy construction site.  不迁就的酒店员工拒绝给我们换房间，即使我们的房间紧挨着一个嘈杂的建筑工地。

例句：His nonaccommodating attitude made it difficult to work with him on the project. 他不妥协的态度使得在这个项目上很难与他合作。
synonym:inflexible, uncooperative, unhelpful, rigid, intractable
antonym:accommodating, helpful, cooperative, flexible, obliging
词性:adjective
