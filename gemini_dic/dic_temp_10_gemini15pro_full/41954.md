cosmotheism:/ˈkɒzməθiːɪzəm/ | n. |宇宙神论，泛神论的一种 |The belief that identifies God with the cosmos; a form of pantheism.  It considers the universe as a manifestation of God. | Cosmotheism blurs the lines between creator and creation. 宇宙神论模糊了造物主和造物之间的界限。

Cosmotheism offers a spiritual connection to the natural world. 宇宙神论提供了一种与自然世界的精神联系。| 近义词:pantheism, panentheism | 反义词:atheism, deism | 词性:noun
