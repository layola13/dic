non-ego:/nɒnˈiːɡəʊ/ (UK) /nɑːnˈiːɡoʊ/ (US)| n.| 非我; 无我 | Absence of the ego; the state of not being self-centered or preoccupied with one's own individual identity. It often refers to a state of transcendence or enlightenment in spiritual traditions. | In meditation, she sought to dissolve her non-ego and become one with the universe. 在冥想中，她试图消融她的自我，与宇宙融为一体。| Synonyms: selflessness, altruism, impersonality | Antonyms: ego, self, selfishness | noun
