electronic: /ɪˌlɛkˈtrɒnɪk/ (British) /ɪˌlɛkˈtrɑːnɪk/ (American) | adj. | 电子的 | Relating to electrons or to devices, systems, or processes that involve the control of electrons. | Electronic devices have revolutionized communication. 电子设备彻底改变了通信。 | 近义词: digital, computerized, automated | 反义词: analog, manual, mechanical | 词性: 形容词

词源: From modern Latin *electronicus, from Greek ēlektron "amber" (because static electricity was first observed by rubbing amber) + -ic.
