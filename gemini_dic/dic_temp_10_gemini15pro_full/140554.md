olibene: /ˈɒlɪˌbiːn/ | n. |橄榄石|A magnesium iron silicate mineral, (Mg,Fe)2SiO4, occurring in igneous and metamorphic rocks and meteorites. It is usually olive-green in colour.|Olivine is a common mineral in Earth's mantle.|近义词:peridot (when gem quality)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


词源: From French *olivine*, from its olive-green colour, from olive +‎ -ine.
