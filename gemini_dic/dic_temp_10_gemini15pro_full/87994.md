high-priestly:/ˌhaɪˈpriːstli/
adjective
大祭司的；祭司长的；像大祭司的
Relating to or characteristic of a high priest.
The high-priestly robes were ornate and symbolic. 大祭司的长袍华丽而具有象征意义。
He spoke with a high-priestly authority. 他以大祭司的权威说话。
Synonyms:  sacerdotal, priestly, ministerial, clerical
Antonyms: secular, lay
词性: 形容词
