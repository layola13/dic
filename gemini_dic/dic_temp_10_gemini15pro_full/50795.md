descendible:/dɪˈsendəbl/
adjective
可继承的; 可传递的
Capable of being inherited or passed down from one generation to the next.
The house and its contents are descendible to his heirs. 这座房子及其内部物品可由他的继承人继承。
Synonyms: heritable, inheritable, transmissible
Antonyms: non-inheritable, uninheritable
