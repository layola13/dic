contentiously:/kənˈtɛnʃəsli/ | adv. | 争论地，有争议地 | In a manner characterized by controversy or dispute; argumentatively. | The issue was debated contentiously.  这个问题引起了激烈的争论。| synonym: controversially, disputatiously, polemically | antonym: amicably, harmoniously, peacefully | adverb

Etymology:
From Late Latin *contentĭōsus*, from Latin contendere "to strive, struggle, contend," from com- "together" + tendere "to stretch, extend."
