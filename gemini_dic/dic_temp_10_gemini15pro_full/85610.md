haughtiest:/ˈhɔːtiɪst/ | adj. | 最傲慢的，最自大的 |  Superlative form of haughty: having or showing an attitude of superiority and contempt for people or things perceived to be inferior. |  She gave him her haughtiest stare. 她给了他一个最傲慢的眼神。|  The haughtiest of the nobles refused to speak to the commoners. 最傲慢的贵族拒绝与平民说话。 | 近义词: arrogant, supercilious, disdainful, condescending | 反义词: humble, modest, unassuming | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Middle English *hautiff, from Old French hautif, from haut (“high”), from Latin altus.
