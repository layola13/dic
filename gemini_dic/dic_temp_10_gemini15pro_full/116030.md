marsupial: /mɑːˈsuːpiəl/ | n. adj. | 有袋动物；有袋的 | A mammal, such as a koala, kangaroo, or opossum, whose young are born incompletely developed and are typically carried and suckled in a pouch on the mother's belly. |  例句：Kangaroos are marsupials native to Australia.  袋鼠是澳大利亚本土的有袋动物。

例句：The Tasmanian devil is a carnivorous marsupial. 袋獾是一种食肉的有袋动物。 | 近义词：pouched mammal | 反义词：placental mammal | 词性: 名词, 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *marsupium* "pouch," from Greek *marsypion* "little bag, purse."
