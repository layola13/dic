crucian:/ˈkruːʃən/ | n. |鲫鱼|A freshwater fish of the carp family, typically having a deep body and a single barbel on each side of the mouth. |例句：The crucian carp is a popular fish for anglers. 鲫鱼是垂钓者喜爱的鱼。

例句：Crucian carp are known for their hardiness and ability to survive in poor water conditions. 鲫鱼以其耐寒性和在恶劣水质条件下的生存能力而闻名。|近义词：carp, goldfish|反义词: None|词性: noun
