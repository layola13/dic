absinthism: /ˈæbsɪnθɪzəm/
n.
苦艾酒中毒; 酗饮苦艾酒的恶习
A chronic addiction to absinthe, often resulting in neurological damage.
He suffered from absinthism due to his excessive consumption of the spirit.  他因过度饮用苦艾酒而患上苦艾酒中毒。

Absinthe, once popular among artists and writers, was eventually banned in many countries due to concerns about absinthism.  苦艾酒曾经在艺术家和作家中很受欢迎，但由于人们担心苦艾酒中毒，最终在许多国家被禁。

近义词: alcohol addiction, substance abuse
反义词: sobriety, abstinence
词性: noun

词源: From French *absinthisme*, from *absinthe* (absinthe) + *-isme* (-ism). Ultimately from Latin *absinthium*, from Ancient Greek *ἀψίνθιον* (apsínthion).
