oviposit:/ˈəʊvɪpɒzɪt/ /ˈoʊvɪˌpɑːzɪt/ | v. | 产卵，放卵 | To deposit or lay eggs, especially by means of an ovipositor. | The female mosquito oviposits her eggs on the surface of the water.  雌蚊子把卵产在水面上。| 近义词：lay eggs, spawn | 反义词: hatch |词性: 动词

oviposit:/ˈəʊvɪpɒzɪt/ /ˈoʊvɪˌpɑːzɪt/ | n. | 产卵，放卵（名词化）| The act of depositing or laying eggs. |  The oviposit of the butterfly occurred on the underside of a leaf. 蝴蝶在叶子的背面产卵。| 近义词：egg-laying, spawning | 反义词: hatching |词性: 名词
