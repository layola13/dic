overpowering:/ˌoʊvərˈpaʊərɪŋ/| adj.|势不可挡的，压倒性的，强烈的|Having such great power or force as to be difficult to resist or overcome.|The heat was overpowering, forcing us to seek shade.  酷热难耐，我们不得不寻找阴凉处。|The smell of the flowers was overpowering. 花的香味太浓了。|synonyms: overwhelming, intense, potent, compelling, irresistible|antonyms: weak, mild, subtle, faint|adjective

overpower:/ˌoʊvərˈpaʊər/| v.|制服，压倒，征服|To defeat or overcome by greater strength or force.|The police overpowered the suspect and arrested him. 警察制服了嫌疑犯并逮捕了他。|Our team overpowered their opponents in the final match.  我们的团队在决赛中击败了对手。|synonyms: conquer, subdue, defeat, vanquish, overwhelm|antonyms: surrender, yield, submit|verb
