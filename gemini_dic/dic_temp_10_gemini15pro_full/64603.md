erupt:/ɪˈrʌpt/ | v. | 爆发，喷发 | to become active and eject lava, ash, and gases, as a volcano; to break out suddenly and violently | The volcano erupted with tremendous force. 火山猛烈地爆发了。| Synonyms: explode, burst, blow up | Antonyms: subside, calm, die down | verb

erupted:/ɪˈrʌptɪd/ | v. | 爆发了，喷发了 | past tense of "erupt," meaning to become active and eject lava, ash, and gases, as a volcano; to break out suddenly and violently |  The volcano erupted last year. 这座火山去年爆发了。 | Synonyms: exploded, burst, blew up | Antonyms: subsided, calmed, died down | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ērumpere*, from *ex- "out" + rumpere "to break".
