areopagite:/ˌæriˈɒpəˌɡaɪt/| n.|雅典最高法院法官；亚略巴古成员；精明的人|A member of the Areopagus, the supreme council of ancient Athens.  Also, a discerning or intelligent person.|例句：The areopagite listened carefully to the arguments presented before the council. 雅典最高法院法官仔细聆听了在议会前提出的论点。

例句：He was known as a shrewd areopagite in business circles. 他在商界被认为是一位精明的专家。|近义词：judge, councilor, expert, intellectual|反义词:fool, simpleton|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *Areopagita, from Greek Areopagítēs, from Áreios Págos ("Ares' Rock," the hill where the council met).
