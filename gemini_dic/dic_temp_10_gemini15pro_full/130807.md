nonantigenic:/ˌnɒnˌæn.tɪˈdʒɛn.ɪk/ | adj. | 非抗原性的 | Not antigenic; not causing the production of antibodies. |  The modified virus was nonantigenic and therefore did not trigger an immune response.  改良后的病毒是非抗原性的，因此不会引发免疫反应。| synonyms: non-immunogenic | antonyms: antigenic | adjective
