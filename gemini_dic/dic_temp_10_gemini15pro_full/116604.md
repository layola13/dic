matrilinearly:/ˌmæt.rɪˈlɪn.i.ər.li/
adverb
母系地
In a matrilinearly organized society, descent and inheritance are traced through the female line.
The family property was passed down matrilinearly.
近义词: through the female line
反义词: patrilinearly
词性: adverb

matrilineal:/ˌmæt.rɪˈlɪn.i.əl/
adjective
母系的
The tribe had a matrilineal system of inheritance.
In a matrilineal society, children belong to their mother's clan.
近义词: uterine
反义词: patrilineal
词性: adjective

matrilineage:/ˈmæt.rɪˌlɪn.i.ɪdʒ/
noun
母系
She could trace her matrilineage back several generations.
The matrilineage was responsible for passing down traditions and knowledge.
近义词: female line, maternal line
反义词: patrilineage
词性: noun

词源:  "matri-" (from Latin "mater," meaning "mother") + "linear" (from Latin "linea," meaning "line") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)
