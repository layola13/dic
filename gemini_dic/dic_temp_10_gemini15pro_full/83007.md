gunpowdery:/ˈɡʌnˌpaʊdəri/| adj.|火药味的；像火药的；易怒的|Resembling or suggestive of gunpowder; easily angered or provoked.|The air was thick and gunpowdery after the fireworks display. 烟花表演结束后，空气中弥漫着浓烈的火药味。|Synonyms: explosive, fiery, volatile, irascible|Antonyms: calm, peaceful, tranquil|Adjective

Etymology->
From gun + powder + -y.  "powder" traces back to Old French "poudre," ultimately from Latin "pulvis," meaning "dust."
