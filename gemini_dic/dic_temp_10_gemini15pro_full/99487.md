interfibrous:/ˌɪntərˈfaɪbrəs/| adj.|纤维间的，纤维状组织间的|Situated or occurring between fibers or fibrous tissues.  It refers to the space or location that exists between the fibers of a material. |The interfibrous matrix plays a critical role in the strength of the composite material. 纤维间的基质对复合材料的强度起着至关重要的作用。

The interfibrous adhesion is weak in this type of wood.  这种木材的纤维间粘合力很弱。|近义词：interfilamentous|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin *inter* (between) + *fibra* (fiber) + -ous (forming adjectives).
