nitrocellulose:/ˌnaɪtroʊˈsɛljʊloʊs/| n. |硝化纤维素；硝酸纤维素；火棉|A highly flammable substance made by treating cellulose with nitric acid, used in making explosives and lacquers.|Nitrocellulose is used in the production of some types of paint. 硝化纤维素用于生产某些类型的油漆。|Synonyms: guncotton, cellulose nitrate|Antonyms: None|noun
