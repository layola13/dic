incarcerate: /ɪnˈkɑːrsəreɪt/ /ɪnˈkɑːrsəˌreɪt/ | vt. |监禁，关押 | To imprison, confine, or put someone in jail. |  The court incarcerated the criminal for life. 法庭判处罪犯终身监禁。

The government incarcerated the political dissidents without trial. 政府未经审判就监禁了政治异议人士。


近义词: imprison, jail, confine, detain, lock up
反义词: release, liberate, free
词性: verb (动词)

Etymology:
From Late Latin incarceratus, past participle of incarcerare "to imprison," from Latin in- "in" + carcer "prison."
