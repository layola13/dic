organist:/ˈɔːɡənɪst/ | n. |风琴手；管风琴演奏家|A person who plays the organ, especially in a church or concert hall. |例句：The organist played a beautiful piece by Bach.  风琴手演奏了一首巴赫的优美作品。

例句：The church is looking for a new organist.  教堂正在寻找一位新的风琴手。


近义词：musician, keyboard player |反义词:  (No direct antonym exists) |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *organistre*, from Old French organiste, from Medieval Latin organista, from Latin organum (“organ”) + -ista (agent suffix).
