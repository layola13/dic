game-planning:/ˈɡeɪm ˌplænɪŋ/
noun
游戏策划；比赛计划；战略规划
The process of developing a strategy or plan for a game or competition.
The coach spent hours on game-planning for the upcoming match. 教练为即将到来的比赛花费了数小时进行比赛计划。
Their meticulous game-planning was the key to their victory. 他们细致的比赛计划是他们获胜的关键。
strategy, planning, tactics, preparation
lack of preparation, improvisation
