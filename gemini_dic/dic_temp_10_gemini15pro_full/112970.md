lukewarmnesses:/ˌluːkˈwɔːrmnɪsɪz/
n.
微温；不冷不热；不热情
lack of enthusiasm or conviction.
His lukewarmness about the project worried the team.  他对这个项目不冷不热的，这让团队很担心。
近义词:apathy, indifference, coolness
反义词:enthusiasm, fervor, passion
词性:名词
