mini-van:/ˈmɪniˌvæn/| n.|小型货车，面包车|A small van that is designed to carry passengers.|We took the mini-van on our family vacation. 我们全家度假时开了小型货车。|近义词：van, minivan, MPV (multi-purpose vehicle)|反义词:truck, lorry|noun

词源:
"mini-" + "van".  "mini-" is a prefix meaning small, and "van" is short for caravan, ultimately derived from the Persian word *kārvān*.
