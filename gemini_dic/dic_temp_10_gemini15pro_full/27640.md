candela:/kænˈdɛlə/ |n.| 坎德拉; 烛光 |A candela is a unit of luminous intensity in the International System of Units (SI). It measures how much light is emitted in a particular direction.  |The brightness of a typical candle is roughly equal to one candela.| 近义词:candlepower| 反义词: (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement) |词性: noun
