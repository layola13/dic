overcontrolling:/ˌoʊvərˌkənˈtroʊlɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度控制的，控制欲过强的 | Excessively controlling; dominating someone's life and restricting their freedom.  Characterized by attempting to regulate or manage every aspect of someone's life. | My overcontrolling parents wouldn't let me go out with my friends. 我的控制欲过强的父母不让我和朋友出去。 | He's so overcontrolling, he even dictates what his wife wears. 他控制欲太强了，甚至连他妻子穿什么都要管。 | 近义词: domineering, authoritarian, possessive, restrictive | 反义词: permissive, lenient, easygoing | 词性: adjective

overcontrol:/ˌoʊvərˌkənˈtroʊl/ | v. | 过度控制 | To control excessively; to dominate someone's life and restrict their freedom. | She tries to overcontrol every aspect of her children's lives. 她试图过度控制孩子们生活的方方面面. | He overcontrols his employees, micromanaging every task. 他过度控制他的员工，对每一项任务都进行微观管理. | 近义词: dominate, micromanage, restrict | 反义词: empower, liberate, free | 词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (meaning "excessive") + "controlling" (present participle of the verb "control," from Old French "contreroller" - to check or verify accounts - derived from "contre-" (against) and "rolle" (roll, list).
