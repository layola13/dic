inexistence:/ˌɪnɪɡˈzɪstəns/
n.
不存在；虚无
The state of not existing.
His belief in the inexistence of fairies was firm. 他坚信小精灵并不存在。

The theory postulates the inexistence of absolute truth.  该理论假定绝对真理不存在。

Near synonyms: nonexistence, absence, nothingness
Antonyms: existence, presence, reality
