choreic:/kɔːˈriːɪk/ /kɔːˈreɪɪk/ | adj. | 舞蹈症的；不规则的，痉挛性的 | Relating to or resembling chorea; characterized by involuntary, jerky movements. |  Her choreic movements made it difficult for her to hold a pen.  她的舞蹈症动作使她难以握笔。 | Synonyms: jerky, spasmodic, twitchy, involuntary | Antonyms: controlled, smooth, steady, voluntary | adjective
