nerding:/ˈnɜːrdɪŋ/ | n. | 书呆子行为；沉迷于某事 | The act of engaging in activities typically associated with nerds, such as studying, playing video games, or collecting comic books.  | He spent the weekend nerding out on his new computer. 他周末都沉迷于他的新电脑。 | Synonyms: geeking out, obsessing, indulging | Antonyms: socializing, partying | noun


nerding:/ˈnɜːrdɪŋ/ | v. |  表现得像个书呆子; 沉迷于...  |  Acting like a nerd; engaging intensely in a typically nerdy activity. | He was nerding out over the new Star Wars movie.  他痴迷于新的星球大战电影。 | Synonyms: geeking, obsessing, indulging | Antonyms: relaxing, chilling out | verb
