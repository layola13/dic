hectoliter: /ˈhɛktəˌliːtər/ (US), /ˈhɛktəʊˌliːtər/ (UK)| n.|百升; 公石|A hectoliter is a unit of volume in the metric system equal to 100 liters.|The farmer's silo can hold hundreds of hectoliters of grain. 这位农民的筒仓可以容纳数百百升的谷物。|Synonyms:  100 liters, 0.1 cubic meter|Antonyms:  microliter, milliliter|noun
