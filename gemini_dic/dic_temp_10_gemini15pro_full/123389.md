monomyarian:/ˌmɒnəʊˈmaɪəriən/ | adj. | 单肌的；只有一个闭壳肌的 | Having only one adductor muscle (specifically referring to bivalve molluscs). |  Example: The oyster is a monomyarian bivalve. 牡蛎是一种单肌双壳类动物。

Example: Monomyarian bivalves are believed to have evolved from dimyarian ancestors.  单肌双壳类动物被认为是从双肌祖先进化而来的。 | 近义词: uniadductor (less common) | 反义词: dimyarian | 词性: adjective
