harmine:/ˈhɑːrmiːn/ | n. |骆驼蓬碱；哈尔明|A fluorescent harmala alkaloid found in the seeds of Peganum harmala (Syrian rue) and Banisteriopsis caapi. It is a central nervous system stimulant, and a reversible inhibitor of monoamine oxidase A (MAO-A).|Harmaline and harmine are both present in the ayahuasca brew.  骆驼蓬碱和哈尔明都存在于死藤水药剂中。| Harmine exhibits antidepressant effects in animal models.  哈尔明在动物模型中表现出抗抑郁作用。|Synonyms: telepathine, banisterine|Antonyms: None|Noun
