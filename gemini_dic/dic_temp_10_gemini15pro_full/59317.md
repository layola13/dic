eared:/ɪərd/ | adj. | 有耳的；有...只耳朵的；（叶、果实等）有耳状突起的 | Having ears, or ear-like projections. | Rabbits are eared mammals. 兔子是有耳的哺乳动物。| The eared grebe has distinctive ear tufts. 角鸊鷉有独特的耳羽。| 近义词:auricular | 反义词:earless | 词性:形容词

词源: From Old English *ēarod, from ēare (ear) + -ed (suffix forming adjectives).
