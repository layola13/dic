leucoplast:/ˈluːkəˌplæst/ | n. | 白色体 |A colorless organelle found in plant cells, used for storing starch, oil, or protein.  |  Leucoplasts are essential for storing nutrients in plant cells. 白色体对于在植物细胞中储存营养物质至关重要。|  Amyloplasts are a type of leucoplast that stores starch.  淀粉体是一种储存淀粉的白色体。 |  chloroplast, chromoplast |  None | noun
