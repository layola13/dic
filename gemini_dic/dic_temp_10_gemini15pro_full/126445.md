mystifiedly:/mɪˈstɪfaɪdli/ | adv. |迷惑不解地，茫然地 | In a manner showing or expressing bewilderment or confusion. | He looked at her mystifiedly, unsure of what she meant. 他迷惑不解地看着她，不明白她的意思。|  She stared mystifiedly at the complex instructions. 她茫然地盯着复杂的说明书。 | 近义词: perplexedly, bewilderedly, confusedly | 反义词: knowingly, understandingly | 词性: 副词

词源:
From the verb "mystify" + "-ed" (past participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Mystify" comes from the French "mystifier," from "mystique" (mystic) + "-fier" (to make). Ultimately from the Greek "mystes" (one initiated into mysteries).
