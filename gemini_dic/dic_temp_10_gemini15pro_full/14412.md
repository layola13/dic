autotransformer:/ˌɔːtəʊtrænsˈfɔːmə(r)/ | n. | 自耦变压器 | An electrical transformer with only one winding. Part of the winding is common to both the primary and secondary circuits. |  The autotransformer provides a variable output voltage. 自耦变压器提供可变的输出电压。 | synonym: variable transformer, Variac (trademark) | antonym: isolation transformer | noun
