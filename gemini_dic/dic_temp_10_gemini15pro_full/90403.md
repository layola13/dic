howls:/haʊlz/ | n. v. | 嚎叫，怒吼；呼啸 |  A long, loud, mournful cry, often of a dog or wolf, but also used metaphorically for wind or other sounds.  The plural form of "howl." | The wolves howls echoed through the forest.  狼的嚎叫声回荡在森林里。 | synonyms: wails, cries, roars, shrieks, screams | antonyms: whispers, murmurs, whimpers | noun
