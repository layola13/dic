lumen:/ˈluːmən/| n. |流明|A unit of luminous flux in the International System of Units, equal to the amount of light emitted per second in a unit solid angle of one steradian from a uniform source of one candela.|The new projector has 4,000 lumens. 这台新的投影机有4000流明。|近义词:lux, candlepower|反义词:darkness|词性:noun

lumens:/ˈluːmənz/| n. |流明（复数）|Plural form of lumen, a unit of luminous flux.|The manufacturer claims the bulb produces 800 lumens. 制造商声称该灯泡产生800流明。|近义词:luxes, candlepowers|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
