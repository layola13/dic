dissertational:/ˌdɪsərˈteɪʃənl/
adj.
论文的，学位论文的
Of, relating to, or characteristic of a dissertation.
Her dissertational research focused on 18th-century literature. 她的学位论文研究集中在18世纪文学。
The dissertational phase of her doctoral program was the most challenging. 她博士课程的论文阶段是最具挑战性的。
近义词: thesis-related, academic, scholarly
反义词: non-academic, informal
词性: adjective
