embarrassing: /ɪmˈbærəsɪŋ/ /ɛmˈbærəsiŋ/ | adj. | 令人尴尬的，使人难堪的 |  Causing a feeling of self-consciousness or awkwardness. |  It was embarrassing when I forgot her name.  我忘记了她的名字，真尴尬。| synonyms: awkward, uncomfortable, humiliating, mortifying | antonyms: comfortable,  proud,  confident | adjective

embarrass: /ɪmˈbærəs/ /ɛmˈbærəs/ | vt. | 使尴尬，使难堪 | To cause someone to feel awkward, self-conscious, or ashamed. | He embarrassed her by asking personal questions. 他问私人问题让她很尴尬。 | synonyms: shame, humiliate, mortify | antonyms: comfort, reassure | verb
