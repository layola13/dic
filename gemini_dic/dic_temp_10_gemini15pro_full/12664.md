assemblers:/əˈsɛmblərz/| n.|汇编程序；汇编器；装配工|A program that converts assembly language into machine code.  A person who assembles something, especially a machine.|The assemblers worked quickly to build the new computers. 汇编程序员们快速地组装新的电脑。|Synonyms: compilers, builders, constructors|Antonyms: disassemblers, deconstructors|Noun
