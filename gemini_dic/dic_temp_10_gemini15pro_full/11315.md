arduousness:/ˈɑːrdʒuəsnəs/
n.
艰难; 艰巨; 费力
The quality of being arduous; difficulty.

Climbing Mount Everest is an undertaking of great arduousness.
攀登珠穆朗玛峰是一项非常艰巨的任务。

The arduousness of the journey tested the limits of their endurance.
旅程的艰辛考验了他们耐力的极限。

Synonyms: difficulty, hardship, laboriousness, strenuousness, toil
Antonyms: easiness, facility, simplicity

Etymology:  From Late Latin *arduōsus, from Latin arduus (“steep, difficult”).  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality.
