aleurone:/ˈæləˌroʊn/| n.|糊粉层，糊粉粒|The aleurone layer of a cereal grain is rich in protein and enzymes.  谷物籽粒的糊粉层富含蛋白质和酶。|The aleurone layer surrounds the endosperm in a seed. 糊粉层包围着种子中的胚乳。|近义词:endosperm (in some contexts), bran (in some contexts)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
