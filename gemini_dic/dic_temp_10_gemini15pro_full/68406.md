falser:/ˈfɔːlsər/| adj.| 更假的，更不真实的 |Comparative form of "false": more untrue; not genuine or correct.| This statement is even falser than the previous one. 这个说法比前一个更不真实。 | 近义词: more untrue, more deceptive, more fabricated | 反义词: truer, more accurate, more genuine | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *falsere*, comparative of *fals*, from Old French *fals*, *faus*, from Latin *falsus*.
