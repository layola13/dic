consecutively:/kənˈsekjətɪvli/
adverb|连续地，接连地|in a sequence; one after another without interruption.|The team has won three games consecutively.  这个队连续赢了三场比赛。|例句：He was absent from school for five consecutive days. 他连续五天没上学。|近义词：successively, sequentially, continuously|反义词: intermittently, sporadically|词性:副词
Etymology: From Late Latin *consecutivus, from Latin consequi ("to follow"), from con- ("together") + sequi ("to follow").
