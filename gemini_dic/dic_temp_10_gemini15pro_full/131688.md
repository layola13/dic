noncondemnation:/ˌnɑːnkɑːndɛmˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 不谴责，不责备 |  The state of not being condemned or judged guilty.  Absence of condemnation; refraining from blaming or criticizing. |  He felt a sense of noncondemnation from his therapist, which allowed him to open up. 他感到他的治疗师没有谴责他，这让他能够敞开心扉。|  forgiveness, acceptance, absolution, exoneration | condemnation, judgment, criticism, censure | noun
