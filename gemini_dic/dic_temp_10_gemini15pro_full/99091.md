interbreeds:/ˌɪntərˈbriːdz/
| v. |杂交繁殖；异种交配；（不同种族的人）通婚|to breed or cause to breed with members of another breed or group| Scientists are interbreeding different varieties of corn to develop a more drought-resistant strain. 科学家们正在杂交不同品种的玉米，以培育出更耐旱的品种。|
|
|Scientists interbred disease-resistant plants with high-yield varieties. 科学家们将抗病植物与高产品种杂交。|
|
|Synonyms: crossbreed, hybridize|
|
|Antonyms: inbreed, purebreed|
|
|verb|
