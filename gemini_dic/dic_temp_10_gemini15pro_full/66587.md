exhorting:/ɪɡˈzɔːrtɪŋ/
verb
劝告；勉励；敦促
strongly encouraging or urging someone to do something
The coach was exhorting his players to give their all in the final minutes of the game. 教练在比赛的最后几分钟敦促他的队员们全力以赴。
encouraging, urging, advising, persuading
dissuading, discouraging, forbidding, prohibiting
v.
