decoyers:/dɪˈkɔɪərz/
noun
诱饵；诱骗者
A person or thing used to lure or entice someone into a trap or dangerous situation.  Something used to distract attention from something else.
The hunters used decoys to attract the ducks. 猎人用诱饵来吸引鸭子。
The company used a decoy website to collect user data.  公司用一个假网站来收集用户数据。
synonyms: lure, bait, enticement, trap
antonyms: warning, deterrent
