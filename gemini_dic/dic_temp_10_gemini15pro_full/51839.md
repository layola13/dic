dewy:/ˈdjuːi/ /ˈduːi/| adj.|带露水的；湿润的；清新的；水灵灵的|Covered with or looking as if covered with dew; fresh and youthful.|The dewy grass sparkled in the morning sun.  清晨的阳光下，带露水的草地闪闪发光。|近义词：moist, damp, fresh, youthful|反义词：dry, arid|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *dewy*, from Old English *dēawig* (“dewy”), equivalent to *dēaw* (“dew”) + *-ig*. Cognate with West Frisian *dauwich* (“dewy”), Dutch *dauwig* (“dewy”), German *taugig* (“dewy”).
