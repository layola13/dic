detain:/dɪˈteɪn/| vt.|拘留，扣留，耽搁|to hold or keep someone in official custody; to delay or hinder someone or something|The police detained the suspect for questioning. 警方拘留了嫌疑人进行审讯。

The heavy traffic detained us for over an hour.  交通拥堵耽搁了我们一个多小时。|近义词：hold, keep, delay, restrain|反义词:release, liberate|verb
