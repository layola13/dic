icteroid:/ˈɪktərɔɪd/| adj.|黄疸的，类似黄疸的|Resembling jaundice; yellowish.  Having a yellowish discoloration of the skin, whites of the eyes, and mucous membranes, resembling that of jaundice.|The patient's skin had an icteroid tinge, suggesting liver problems.|近义词：jaundiced, yellowed|反义词: healthy, rosy|词性: adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From icterus (jaundice) + -oid (resembling).  Icterus comes from the Greek word ikteros, meaning "jaundice," which itself may be related to the word ikter, a yellow bird whose sight was believed to cure jaundice. The suffix -oid comes from the Greek -oeidēs, meaning "having the form of."
