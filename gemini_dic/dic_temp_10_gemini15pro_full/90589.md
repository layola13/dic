hullah:/ˈhʌlə/ | v. | 欢呼，呐喊 | To shout or cheer loudly. | The crowd hullah'd as the winning team took the field.  当获胜的队伍进场时，人群欢呼雀跃。 | 近义词: cheer, shout, acclaim | 反义词: be silent, hush | verb
