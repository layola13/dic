outgabbling:/ˌaʊtˈɡæblɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的饶舌；比…更健谈；说得比…多
talking more or better than someone else
The auctioneer was outgabbling all the bidders. 拍卖师说得比所有竞标者都快。
outtalking, outspeaking
- (This word doesn't have a clear antonym, as it describes a specific action of speaking more.  One could consider "being outgabbled" as a sort of antonym in a specific context.)
v.
