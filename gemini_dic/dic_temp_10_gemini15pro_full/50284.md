depiction:/dɪˈpɪkʃən/| n.|描绘，描述，刻画|The novel offers a vivid depiction of life in Victorian England. 这部小说生动地描绘了维多利亚时代英格兰的生活。|近义词：portrayal, representation, description|反义词：misrepresentation, distortion|词性：名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin depictionem, accusative of depictio (“a portraying”),  from Latin depictus, past participle of depingere (“to portray, paint, depict”), from de (“down, fully”) + pingere (“to paint”).
