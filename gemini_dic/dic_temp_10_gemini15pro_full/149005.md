ovulatory:/ˈɒvjuːlət(ə)ri/ (US) /ˈɒvjʊlətəri/ (UK)
adjective
排卵的
Relating to or occurring during ovulation.  The process of releasing a mature egg from the ovary.
The ovulatory phase of the menstrual cycle is when the egg is released. 月经周期的排卵期是卵子被释放的时候。
Ovulatory dysfunction can lead to infertility. 排卵功能障碍会导致不孕。
近义词:  ovulating
反义词: anovulatory
词性: adjective
