overthriftily:/ˌoʊvərˈθrɪftɪli/
adverb
过度节俭地
Excessively thriftily; with extreme economy or frugality.
She managed her household overthriftily, sometimes to the point of depriving herself of necessary comforts. 她把家管理得过于节俭，有时甚至到了剥夺自己必需的舒适的地步。
He dressed overthriftily, wearing patched clothes long after they should have been replaced.  他的穿着过于节俭，衣服补了又补，早就该换新的了。
近义词: excessively frugally, parsimoniously, stingily
反义词: extravagantly, lavishly, prodigally
词性: adverb

词源:
over- (prefix) + thriftily (adverb)
"over-" 表示"过度"，"thriftily" 源于 "thrift" (节俭) + "-ily" (副词后缀). "thrift" 最终源于古诺尔斯语 "þrif" (繁荣，兴旺).  最初，"thrift" 意味着繁荣和成功，后来演变成节俭和谨慎的管理资源的含义.
