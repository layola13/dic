ampersand:/ˈæmpərˌsænd/| n.|和号，& |A symbol (&) representing the word *and*.|The company name is written as "Johnson & Johnson." 公司名称写为“Johnson & Johnson”。|Example: Use an ampersand instead of writing out the word "and."  用“&”号代替“and”这个词。|Synonym: and|Antonym: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the phrase "and per se and," meaning "and by itself and," which was often recited in the alphabet. Over time, the phrase was slurred together and eventually written as a single symbol.
