deinotherium:/ˌdaɪnəˈθɪəriəm/| n. |恐兽|An extinct elephant-like mammal of the Miocene and Pliocene epochs, having tusks curving downwards from the lower jaw.|The deinotherium, with its downward-pointing tusks, must have presented a formidable sight. 恐兽向下弯曲的长牙一定呈现出一种可怕的景象。|近义词:proboscidean (长鼻目动物)|反义词:None (已灭绝动物，无明确反义词)|词性:noun

Etymology: From Greek *deinós* (terrible) + *thēríon* (beast).
