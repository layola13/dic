paned:/peɪnd/| adj.|镶嵌玻璃的；多格的|Having panes of glass; divided into panes.|The old house had beautiful paned windows. 这座老房子有着漂亮的镶嵌玻璃窗。|近义词：glazed, windowed|反义词：unpaned|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From pane + -ed.  Pane ultimately comes from the Latin *pannus*, meaning "cloth, piece of cloth."  This evolved to refer to a piece or section of something, eventually including glass in a window.  The suffix "-ed" forms adjectives, meaning "having" or "characterized by."
