deciduate:/dɪˈsɪdʒuət/ /dəˈsɪdʒuˌeɪt/ | adj.| 落叶的；脱落的；暂时的 |Shedding foliage at the end of the growing season; not persistent.  Relating to a membrane that is shed at a particular stage of development, such as after giving birth. Temporary; short-lived.|
The oak is a deciduous tree. 橡树是一种落叶树。

The decidua is the modified mucosal lining of the uterus that forms in preparation for pregnancy.  蜕膜是子宫内膜的修饰性衬里，为怀孕做准备而形成。

His enthusiasm for the project was deciduate. 他对这个项目的热情是短暂的。|
近义词: caducous, ephemeral, transient, temporary, deciduous |
反义词: persistent, evergreen, permanent |
词性: adjective
