demirelievo:/ˌdɛmi rɪˈliːvoʊ/ /ˌdɛmi rɪˈliːvəʊ/
|n.|半浮雕|Sculpture in which the figures project slightly from the background, less than half their full depth.|The artist specialized in demirelievo carvings, creating intricate scenes on marble slabs. 艺术家专门从事半浮雕雕刻，在大理石板上创作复杂的场景。|bas-relief, mezzo-rilievo|high relief, intaglio|noun
Etymology->
From French *demi-relief*, from *demi-* (“half”) + *relief* (“relief”). *Demi-* is from Latin *dīmidius* (“half”). *Relief* is ultimately from Latin *levāre* (“to raise, lighten”).
