orotundity:/ˌɔːrəˈtʌndɪti/
n.
雄浑；洪亮；（语言）夸张；（文风） pompous
Pompous or inflated speech or writing.  Characterized by fullness, richness, and clarity of sound.
The orotundity of the politician's speech impressed no one who knew the facts.  这位政客演讲的夸张言辞并没有打动任何了解事实的人。
Synonyms: bombast, grandiloquence, magniloquence, pomposity
Antonyms: simplicity, conciseness, understatement, humility
noun
