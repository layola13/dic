angelic:/ænˈdʒɛlɪk/
adjective
天使般的；似天使的
Resembling or characteristic of an angel, especially in beauty, purity, or innocence.
She has an angelic face and a sweet voice. 她有一张天使般的脸庞和甜美的嗓音。
近义词:divine, heavenly, seraphic, cherubic
反义词:diabolic, demonic, fiendish
词性:adjective
