flighty:/ˈflaɪti/| adj.|轻浮的，反复无常的，易激动的|Frivolous and irresponsible; capricious and impulsive.|例句：She has a flighty personality and is always changing her mind. 她性格轻浮，总是改变主意。

例句：He's too flighty to be trusted with important tasks. 他太反复无常了，不能把重要的任务托付给他。|近义词：fickle, frivolous, capricious, erratic, irresponsible, whimsical|反义词：stable, steady, reliable, responsible, serious|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *flighti, from Old English *flīhtig (“disposed to flee, timid”), equivalent to fly +‎ -y. Cognate with Dutch vluchtig (“volatile, fleeting”), German flüchtig (“fleeting, volatile”).  The sense of "capricious, frivolous" arose from the notion of "easily startled, skittish".
