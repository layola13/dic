imprudence:/ɪmˈpruːdəns/| n.|轻率，鲁莽，不谨慎|Lack of caution and good judgment.|His youthful imprudence led him into trouble. 他年轻时的轻率让他陷入了麻烦。|Synonyms: recklessness, foolishness, indiscretion, rashness|Antonyms: prudence, caution, wisdom, circumspection|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *imprudence*, from Latin *imprudentia*, from *imprudent-* (see imprudent).
