annalistically:/ˌænəˈlɪstɪkəli/
adv.
编年史地，按年代顺序地
in a manner of relating to annals; in the style of a chronicle; chronologically.
He documented the events annalistically, meticulously recording each occurrence in chronological order. 他按年代顺序记录了这些事件，一丝不苟地按时间顺序记录了每一次事件。
The historian approached the subject annalistically, carefully outlining the key events of each year.  历史学家以编年史的方式研究这个主题，仔细勾勒出每年的关键事件。
近义词: chronologically, historically
反义词: randomly, haphazardly
词性: 副词
