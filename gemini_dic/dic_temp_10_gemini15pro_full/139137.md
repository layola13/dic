obese:/oʊˈbiːs/| adj.|肥胖的，过胖的|Grossly fat or overweight. Characterized by excessive accumulation of body fat.|例句：He was morbidly obese. 他患有病态肥胖。

例句：Childhood obesity is a growing problem. 儿童肥胖是一个日益严重的问题。|近义词：overweight, fat, stout, plump, corpulent|反义词：thin, slim, slender, lean|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Latin *obesus*, from *ob-* (variant of *ab-* "away, from") + *esus* ("eaten"), past participle of *edere* ("to eat").  Literally, "having eaten away one's substance."  Thus, originally implying excessive eating as the cause of obesity.
