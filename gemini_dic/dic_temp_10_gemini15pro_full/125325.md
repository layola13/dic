multilamellar:/ˌmʌltiˈlæmələr/
adjective
多层的，多片层的
Composed of multiple layers or lamellae.
Multilamellar vesicles are often used in drug delivery systems.  多层囊泡常用于药物输送系统。
The researchers studied the structure of the multilamellar liposomes.  研究人员研究了多层脂质体的结构。
multilayered, laminated
unilamellar
