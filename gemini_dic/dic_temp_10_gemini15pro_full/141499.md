opisthography:/ˌɒpɪˈθɒɡrəfi/
noun
反面书写，背面书写
The practice of writing on the back of something, especially a papyrus roll.
Ancient Egyptians sometimes used opisthography to save papyrus.  古埃及人有时使用反面书写来节省莎草纸。
Some medieval manuscripts also exhibit opisthography. 一些中世纪的手稿也展现了反面书写的特征。
