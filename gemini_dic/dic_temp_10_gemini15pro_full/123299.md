monogeny:/məˈnɒdʒəni/ /məˈnɑːdʒəni/ | n. |单起源;单系起源|The theory of monogeny proposes that all humans descend from a single ancestral population. 单起源理论认为所有人类都来自一个共同的祖先群体。| Monogeny is contrasted with polygeny. 单起源与多起源相对。| 近义词: single origin | 反义词: polygeny | 词性: noun
