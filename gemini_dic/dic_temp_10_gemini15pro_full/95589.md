incommensurable:/ˌɪnkəmˈɛnʃərəbl/ |adj. | 不可通约的，不能相比的 | Not able to be judged by the same standard; having no common standard of measurement. |The two systems of belief are incommensurable. 这两个信仰体系是不可通约的。| The value of life is incommensurable with money. 生命的价值是无法用金钱来衡量的。 |近义词：incomparable, disparate, incompatible |反义词：commensurable, comparable, similar | 词性: 形容词

incommensurables:/ˌɪnkəmˈɛnʃərəblz/ |n. | 不可通约量 | Things that cannot be measured or compared using the same standard. |  Dealing with incommensurables is a challenge in philosophy. 处理不可通约量是哲学上的一个挑战。 |  The incommensurables of love and loss are difficult to reconcile. 爱与失去的不可通约性很难调和。 |近义词： disparities, inconsistencies |反义词：commensurables, comparables | 词性: 名词
