nonheuristic:/ˌnɒnˌhjʊəˈrɪstɪk/
adjective
非启发式的
Not using or involving heuristics; not based on trial and error or rules of thumb.  Characterized by a systematic and logical approach, often involving algorithms or explicit procedures.
This nonheuristic algorithm guarantees the optimal solution.  这个非启发式算法保证了最优解。
The researchers employed a nonheuristic method for data analysis. 研究人员采用了一种非启发式的数据分析方法。
近义词: algorithmic, systematic, methodical
反义词: heuristic, trial-and-error
词性: adjective
