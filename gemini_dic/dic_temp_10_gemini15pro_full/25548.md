bulrush:/ˈbʊlˌrʌʃ/ | n. | 蒲草，香蒲 | A tall marsh plant with a stiff, round stem and a brown, velvety cylindrical head of tiny flowers.  It typically grows in shallow water. | The children made little boats from bulrushes. 孩子们用蒲草做了小船。|  The marsh was thick with bulrushes. 沼泽地长满了茂密的香蒲。 |Synonyms: reed, cattail, sedge | Antonyms: N/A | noun
