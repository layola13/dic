matelote:/ˌmætəˈloʊt/| n.|红酒炖鱼 (通常指淡水鱼), 水手式炖鱼|A matelote is a French stew of fish, usually freshwater fish, cooked in wine, often red, with onions, mushrooms, and herbs.|We enjoyed a delicious matelote at the bistro by the river. 我们在河边的小酒馆享用了一顿美味的红酒炖鱼。|The restaurant's signature dish was a hearty matelote of eel and perch. 这家餐厅的招牌菜是一道丰盛的鳗鱼和鲈鱼红酒炖鱼。|近义词：fish stew, bouillabaisse|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "grilled fish" as a contrasting cooking method)|词性:noun
