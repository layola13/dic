doolally:/duːˈlɑːli/
adjective
疯狂的，精神错乱的
eccentric or crazy; mentally unstable.  Originally military slang from the British Raj, referring to a mental hospital in Deolali, India.
He went completely doolally after losing his job. 他失业后完全疯了。
近义词:crazy, mad, insane, nuts, bonkers
反义词:sane, rational, lucid
词性:adjective

doolally:/duːˈlɑːli/
noun
疯狂，精神错乱
A state of being eccentric or crazy; mental instability.
His doolally was evident in his erratic behaviour. 他的疯狂表现在他古怪的行为上。
近义词:madness, insanity, craziness
反义词:sanity, rationality, lucidity
词性:noun
