friers:/ˈfraɪərz/| n.|油炸食品；油炸锅；炸鱼薯条店|People working in friers often have to deal with hot oil.  在炸鱼薯条店工作的人经常要处理热油。|例句：The friers were busy on Friday night.  星期五晚上炸鱼薯条店很忙。

The fish and chips from the local friers are delicious. 这家炸鱼薯条店的炸鱼薯条很好吃。|近义词：fryers, deep fryers|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 ovens 烤箱)|词性:名词

词源:  From Middle English *friere, from Old French freïr (“to fry”), from Latin frīgō (“to roast, fry”). Related to fry.
