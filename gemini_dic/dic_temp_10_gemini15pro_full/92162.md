hypersomnolence:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɑːmnələns/ | n. |嗜睡症，过度睡眠 | Excessive sleepiness, a condition characterized by prolonged or excessive sleep. |  She was diagnosed with hypersomnolence after experiencing excessive daytime sleepiness for several months. 她连续几个月白天过度嗜睡后被诊断为嗜睡症。

Hypersomnolence can be a symptom of various underlying medical conditions. 嗜睡症可能是各种潜在疾病的症状。 | 近义词: somnolence, sleepiness, drowsiness | 反义词: insomnia, wakefulness | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Ancient Greek ὑπέρ (hupér, “over, excessive”) + Latin somnus (“sleep”) + -lence (suffix forming abstract nouns).
