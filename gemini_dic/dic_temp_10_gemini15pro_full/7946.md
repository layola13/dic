anthelion:/ˈæntɪˌliːən/ | n. | 反日点，幻日 | A bright spot of light sometimes appearing in the sky opposite the sun.  It is caused by the refraction of sunlight through ice crystals. |  I saw a faint anthelion while skiing in the mountains. 我在山上滑雪时看到一个微弱的反日点。

The anthelion appeared as a luminous white spot. 反日点呈现为一个发光的白色斑点。 | 近义词: sun dog, parhelion | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *anti- "opposite" + *hēlios "sun"
