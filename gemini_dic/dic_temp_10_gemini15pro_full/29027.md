cartoon: /kɑːrˈtuːn/ (US) /kɑːˈtuːn/ (UK)| n. |卡通片，动画片；漫画|A drawing, especially in a newspaper or magazine, that tells a story or jokes about a situation or event.|例句：I love watching Saturday morning cartoons. 我喜欢看周六早上的动画片。

例句：He drew a political cartoon criticizing the government. 他画了一幅讽刺政府的政治漫画。

近义词：animation, comic, drawing|反义词: photograph, reality|词性: noun

cartoon: /kɑːrˈtuːn/ (US) /kɑːˈtuːn/ (UK)| v. |把…画成卡通|To represent something in a cartoon or exaggerated way.|例句：The artist cartooned the mayor as a pig.  这位艺术家把市长画成了一头猪。

例句：The magazine cartooned the politician's hypocrisy. 这本杂志用漫画讽刺了这位政客的虚伪。

近义词：caricature, satirize|反义词: portray realistically|词性: verb
