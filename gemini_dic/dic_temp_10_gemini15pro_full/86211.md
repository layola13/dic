heathenizing:/ˈhiːðənaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使异教化，使不信教
The process of converting someone to heathenism or causing someone to abandon their religious beliefs.
Early missionaries worried about the heathenizing influence of local customs on their converts. 早期传教士担心当地习俗对其皈依者的异教化影响。
The prolonged exposure to secular ideas contributed to the gradual heathenizing of the population. 长期接触世俗思想导致了人口逐渐异教化。
近义词: paganizing, secularizing
反义词: Christianizing, evangelizing
词性: verb (动名词)
