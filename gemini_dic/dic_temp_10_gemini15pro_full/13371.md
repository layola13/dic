atmosphered:/ˈætməsfɪəd/| adj.|充满气氛的，有…气氛的|Having a particular atmosphere; imbued with a certain mood or feeling. |The dimly lit restaurant was atmosphered with romance.  昏暗的灯光使这家餐厅充满了浪漫的气氛。|The cafe was atmosphered with the aroma of coffee. 咖啡馆里弥漫着咖啡的香气。|近义词：ambient, atmospheric|反义词：bland, dull|词性:形容词

Etymology: From atmosphere + -ed (suffix forming adjectives).  "Atmosphere" itself comes from Greek atmos "vapor" + sphaira "sphere."
