deflating:/dɪˈfleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 泄气的；使人泄气的；通货紧缩的 | Reducing the size or importance of something; letting air or gas out of something; causing prices to fall. | The news of the job losses was deflating.  失业的消息令人沮丧。 | synonyms: discouraging, disheartening, dispiriting, depressing, weakening, sapping, undermining, demoralizing | antonyms: encouraging, heartening, inspiring, uplifting, inflating | adjective


deflate:/dɪˈfleɪt/ | v. | 放气；泄气；使缩小；紧缩通货 | To let air or gas out of something; to reduce the size or importance of something; to cause prices to fall. | The balloon slowly deflated. 气球慢慢地瘪了。 | synonyms: collapse, shrink, dwindle, diminish, lessen, reduce, depress | antonyms: inflate, expand, enlarge, increase | verb
