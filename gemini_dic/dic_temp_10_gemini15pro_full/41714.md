corroboratorily:/kəˈrɒbəreɪtərɪli/ | adv. | 证实地，确凿地 | In a way that confirms or supports something; in a corroborative manner. | The witness corroborated the victim's story corroboratorily, providing detailed evidence.  证人确凿地证实了受害者的说法，提供了详细的证据。| 近义词: confirmingly, supportively, validatingly | 反义词: contradictorily, disprovingly | 词性: 副词

corroborate:/kəˈrɒbəreɪt/ | vt. | 证实，确证 | To confirm or support something, typically with additional evidence. | The evidence corroborates his account of the accident. 证据证实了他对事故的描述。| 近义词: confirm, verify, substantiate | 反义词: contradict, refute, disprove | 词性: 动词

词源: From Late Latin *corroboratus, past participle of corroborare "to strengthen," from Latin com- "together" + roborare "to strengthen," from robur "strength."
