autowind:/ˌɔːtəˈwaɪnd/ | adj. n. |自动上弦的；自动上弦装置|A device, especially in a watch, that winds the mainspring automatically by the movement of the wearer.|My new watch is autowind, so I don't have to wind it manually. 我的新手表是自动上弦的，所以我不用手动上弦。|Synonyms: self-winding, automatic|Antonyms: manual-winding, hand-wound|adjective

autowind:/ˌɔːtəˈwaɪnd/ | n. |自动上弦装置|The autowind mechanism in my watch is very efficient. 我手表里的自动上弦装置非常高效。|Synonyms: self-winding mechanism, automatic winding mechanism|Antonyms: manual winding mechanism, hand-winding mechanism|noun
