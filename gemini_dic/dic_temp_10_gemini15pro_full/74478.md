frankia:/ˈfræŋkiə/| n. |弗兰克氏菌|A genus of nitrogen-fixing bacteria that live in symbiosis with certain non-leguminous plants.|Frankia bacteria form nodules on the roots of their host plants. 弗兰克氏菌在其宿主植物的根部形成根瘤。|Synonyms: actinomycete|Antonyms: None|Noun
