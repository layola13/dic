defined:/dɪˈfaɪnd/ | adj. |定义的；清晰的；明确的|Clearly characterized or delimited; having precise limits or boundaries.  Meaning "fixed, settled, determined" |例句：The terms of the contract are clearly defined.  合同条款有明确的定义。|例句：The photograph showed a sharply defined image. 照片显示出清晰的图像。|近义词：specific, precise, distinct, clear, explicit|反义词：vague, ambiguous, unclear, indefinite|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *definen*, from Old French *definer* (Modern French *définir*), from Latin *dēfīnīre*, from *dē-* (“completely, fully") + *fīnīre* (“to limit, bound, finish"), from *fīnis* (“boundary, limit, end").
