half-reclined:/ˌhæf rɪˈklaɪnd/| adj.|半躺的，斜倚的|in a position where the top half of your body is leaning back at an angle|She was half-reclined on the sofa, reading a book. 她半躺在沙发上看书。|例句：He sat half-reclined in his chair, watching the sunset. 他半躺在椅子上，看着日落。
|近义词：reclining, lounging, semi-recumbent|反义词：upright, erect|词性：形容词

half:/hæf/ | n. adj. adv.|一半，半个|one of two equal or nearly equal parts of something|例句：Half of the students are girls. 一半的学生是女孩。

|近义词：moiety, part|反义词：whole, double|词性：名词/形容词/副词


reclined:/rɪˈklaɪnd/ | adj.|斜倚的，躺卧的|leaning or lying back in a relaxed position|例句：He was reclined on a deckchair, soaking up the sun. 他斜躺在躺椅上，沐浴着阳光。

|近义词：prone, supine, horizontal|反义词：upright, erect|词性：形容词
