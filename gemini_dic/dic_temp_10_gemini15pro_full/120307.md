minicomputer:|/ˈmɪniˌkəmˌpjuːtər/ (US), /ˈmɪniˌkəmˌpjuːtə/ (UK)|n.|小型机|A type of computer that possesses most of the features and capabilities of a large computer but is smaller in physical size and processing power. Typically used by multiple users simultaneously for tasks such as scientific calculations, business data processing, and industrial control.|The university's research lab relied on a powerful minicomputer for complex simulations.|近义词:midrange computer|反义词:mainframe, microcomputer|词性:noun
