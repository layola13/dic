homoptera:/hɒˈmɒptərə/
|n.|同翅目（昆虫）|An order of insects (now generally considered to be paraphyletic) distinguished by having membranous forewings and hindwings, often held rooflike over the body.  This group includes cicadas, aphids, planthoppers, and scale insects.|Homoptera such as aphids can be serious agricultural pests. 同翅目昆虫如蚜虫会成为严重的农业害虫。|近义词: Hemiptera (some classifications)|反义词: None (as it's a taxonomic group)|词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ὁμός (homós, “same, similar”) + πτερόν (pterón, “wing”)
