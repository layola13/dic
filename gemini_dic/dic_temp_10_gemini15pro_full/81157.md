grandaunt:/ˈɡrændɑːnt/ (US), /ˈɡrɑːnt/ (UK)| n. |姑婆；姨婆|A grandaunt is the aunt of one's parent.|My grandaunt gave me a beautiful antique necklace for my birthday. 我的姑婆/姨婆在我生日时送给我一条漂亮的古董项链。|Example: She visited her grandaunt during the summer holidays. 她在暑假期间去看望了她的姑婆/姨婆。|Synonym: great-aunt|Antonym: N/A|noun
