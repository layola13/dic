EKG:/iːkeɪˈdʒiː/ /iːˈkeɪˈdʒiː/
noun
心电图； electrocardiogram
A test that records the electrical activity of the heart.
例句：The doctor ordered an EKG to check his heart rhythm. 医生要求做心电图来检查他的心律。

例句：The EKG showed an irregular heartbeat. 心电图显示心律不齐。
近义词：ECG (electrocardiogram)
反义词: 无
词性: noun
