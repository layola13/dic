balsamiferous:/ˌbɔːlsəmˈɪfərəs/
adjective
产香脂的；有香脂香味的
Producing balsam; having the fragrance of balsam.
The balsamiferous tree exuded a sweet, sticky resin.  这棵产香脂的树渗出一种甜的、粘稠的树脂。
The air was balsamiferous, filled with the scent of pine and fir. 空气中弥漫着香脂的味道，充满了松树和冷杉的香味。
fragrant, aromatic, sweet-smelling
odorless, scentless
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *balsamifer, from Latin balsamum (“balsam”) + -fer (“bearing”).  Ultimately from Hebrew  (בֹּשֶׂם, bośem, “spice, perfume”).
