anaphora:/əˈnæfərə/| n.|回指；指代|The use of a word referring to or replacing a word used earlier in a sentence, to avoid repetition; also, the word used.  指一个词语在句子中用来指代或替换前面出现的词语以避免重复的用法；也被指代的词语本身。|Example: "The dog wagged its tail." - "It" is an anaphora, referring back to "the dog."  例句： “狗摇着它的尾巴。” - “它”是一个回指，指代“狗”。

Example:  "After she arrived, Mary went straight to her room." - "She" is an anaphora referring to "Mary." 例句：玛丽到达后，径直去了她的房间。“她”是回指，指代“玛丽”。|近义词：reference, substitution|反义词:cataphora|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin anaphora, from Ancient Greek ἀναφορά (anaphorá, “a carrying back, a reference”), from ἀναφέρω (anapherō, “I carry back, I refer”), from ἀνά (aná, “up, back”) + φέρω (pherō, “I carry”). 源自后期拉丁语 anaphora，来自古希腊语 ἀναφορά (anaphorá, “带回，指代”)，来自 ἀναφέρω (anapherō, “我带回，我指代”)，来自 ἀνά (aná, “向上，向后”) + φέρω (pherō, “我携带”)。
