homonyms:/ˈhɒmənɪmz/ /ˈhɑːmənɪmz/| n. |同音异义词|Words that have the same pronunciation but different meanings, origins, or spellings.|例句：The words "write" and "right" are homonyms.  单词“write”和“right”是同音异义词。|例句：  "Sea" and "see" are homonyms. “Sea”和“see”是同音异义词。|近义词：homophones|反义词: - |词性:noun
