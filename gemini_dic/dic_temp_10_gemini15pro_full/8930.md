antihierarchical:/ˌæntiˌhaɪərˈɑːrkɪkəl/
adjective
反等级的，反对等级制度的
Opposing or rejecting hierarchy; characterized by a lack of hierarchical structure.
The antihierarchical nature of the internet allows anyone to publish their thoughts. 互联网的反等级性质允许任何人发表他们的想法。
The collective embraced an antihierarchical structure, with all members having equal say.  这个集体采用了一种反等级结构，所有成员都有平等的发言权。
Synonyms: egalitarian, nonhierarchical, democratic
Antonyms: hierarchical, authoritarian, stratified
词性: adjective


antihierarchy:/ˌæntiˈhaɪərɑːrki/
noun
反等级制度
Opposition to or rejection of hierarchy.
There is a strong sense of antihierarchy among the workers. 工人们有强烈的反等级制度意识。
The antihierarchy movement gained momentum in the 1960s. 反等级制度运动在20世纪60年代获得了动力。
Synonyms: egalitarianism
Antonyms: hierarchy, authoritarianism
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix): against, opposite of (from Greek anti-)
hierarchy (noun): a system or organization in which people or groups are ranked one above the other according to status or authority (from Greek hierarkhia)
