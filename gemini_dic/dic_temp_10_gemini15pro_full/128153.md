nettings:/ˈnɛtɪŋz/
n.
网状物；网状织物；网，罗网；[航海]防鼠网
A piece of material made of threads of rope, wire, etc., woven into a loose open pattern with spaces between the threads.

The fisherman cast his nettings into the sea.  渔夫把他的网撒入大海。

Synonyms: mesh, network, web, netting
Antonyms: solid, impenetrable

词性: 名词 (Noun)
