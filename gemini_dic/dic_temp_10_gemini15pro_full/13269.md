athanor:/ˈæθənɔːr/| n.|消化炉，炼丹炉|A furnace used by alchemists, typically for maintaining a uniform and constant temperature for a long period of time, often symbolic of spiritual purification or transformation. |The alchemist tended his athanor day and night, hoping to transmute lead into gold. 炼金术士日夜照料他的消化炉，希望能把铅变成金子。|近义词：furnace, kiln|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology:
From Old French *athanor*, from Medieval Latin *athanor*, from Arabic *al-tannūr* (التنور) meaning "oven" or "kiln."
