parvoline: /ˈpɑːrvɒliːn/ | n. |巴沃林（一种有机化合物）| An oily, aromatic base, C9H13N, of the pyridine series, obtained from coal tar or by synthesis.  It is a dimethylpyridine isomer. | Parvoline is a useful solvent in organic chemistry. 巴沃林是有机化学中一种有用的溶剂。 | Parvoline can be used in the production of certain pharmaceuticals. 巴沃林可用于生产某些药物。 | 近义词: dimethylpyridine (as it is an isomer) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
