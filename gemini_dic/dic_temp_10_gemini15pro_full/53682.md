disciplinarians:/ˌdɪsəplɪˈnɛəriənz/
n.
训练者，纪律严明的人
People who believe in and enforce strict discipline.
The school employed strict disciplinarians to maintain order. 学校雇佣了严格的训练者来维持秩序。
synonyms: enforcers, sticklers, authoritarians, martinets
antonyms:  permissivists, libertarians
noun
