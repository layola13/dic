missee:/mɪˈsiː/ | n. | 失误，过失；未击中；未得到的东西| An act of failing to hit, catch, reach, or otherwise make contact with something.  A thing that one fails to obtain, notice, or do. |  She felt terrible about the missee. 她对这次失误感到非常糟糕。|  The hunter cursed his missee. 猎人咒骂自己没有击中。|Near synonyms: mistake, error, blunder, slip-up, oversight | Antonyms: hit, success, achievement | noun
