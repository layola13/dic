cul-de-sac:/ˈkʌl də sæk/ /ˈkʊl də sæk/
n.
死胡同，死巷，困境
A street or passage closed at one end.  A situation in which further progress is impossible.
My house is located on a quiet cul-de-sac. 我的房子位于一个安静的死胡同里。
Negotiations have reached a cul-de-sac. 谈判陷入了僵局。
近义词:dead end, blind alley
反义词:through street, thoroughfare
词性:名词
