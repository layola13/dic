flexner:/ˈflɛksnər/| n.|弗莱克斯纳|Referring to Abraham Flexner (1866-1959), an American educator who had a major influence on medical and higher education in the United States and Canada.  Often used in reference to the Flexner Report of 1910, which led to significant reforms in medical education.|The Flexner Report revolutionized medical training in North America. 弗莱克斯纳报告彻底改变了北美的医学教育。|Synonym:  (depending on context) reformer, innovator|Antonym: (depending on context) traditionalist, conservative|Noun
