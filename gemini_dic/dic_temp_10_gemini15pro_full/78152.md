geoplanarian:/ˌdʒiːoʊpləˈnɛəriən/| n. |土居涡虫；地栖涡虫|A planarian that lives on land, typically in humid environments.  It's a type of free-living flatworm.|Geoplanarians are common in tropical rainforests. 土居涡虫在热带雨林中很常见。|Synonyms: land planarian, terrestrial planarian|Antonyms: aquatic planarian, freshwater planarian|noun
