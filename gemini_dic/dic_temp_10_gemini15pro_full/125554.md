multisulcate:/ˌmʌltiˈsʌlkeɪt/
adjective
多沟的，多槽的
Having multiple grooves or furrows.
The multisulcate pollen grains were easily identifiable under the microscope.  显微镜下很容易识别出多沟的花粉粒。
The archaeologist discovered a multisulcate seed from an ancient civilization. 考古学家发现了一个来自古代文明的多沟种子。
Near Antonyms: smooth, unsulcate
Etymology: multi- (many) + sulcate (furrowed)
