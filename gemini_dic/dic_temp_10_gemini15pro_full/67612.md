extraversion:/ˌɛkstrəˈvɜːrʒən/
n.
外向性，外向性格
The act, state, or habit of being predominantly concerned with obtaining gratification from without oneself. Extraversion is characterized by interest in and behavior directed toward others and the external environment rather than toward inner states and experiences.
Her extraversion makes her a natural leader.  她的外向性格使她成为天生的领导者。
近义词:outgoingness, sociability
反义词:introversion, shyness
词性:noun

extraversions:/ˌɛkstrəˈvɜːrʒənz/
n.
外向性（复数）
Plural form of extraversion.  Refers to multiple instances or types of extraversion.
The study compared the extraversions of different cultures.  这项研究比较了不同文化的外向性。
近义词:outgoingness, sociabilities
反义词:introversions, shynesses
词性:noun

词源: From Latin *extra- (outside of, beyond) + *vertere (to turn).  The term was popularized by Carl Jung in his theory of psychological types.
