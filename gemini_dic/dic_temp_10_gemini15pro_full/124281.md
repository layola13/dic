mothball:/ˈmɔːθbɔːl/ (US) /ˈmɒθbɔːl/ (UK)| n. vt.|樟脑丸；封存；搁置|A small, white ball containing a strong-smelling chemical, used to keep moths away from clothes.|She put some mothballs in her closet to protect her sweaters. 她把一些樟脑丸放在衣柜里以保护她的毛衣。|近义词：naphthalene|反义词:|名词,动词

mothball:/ˈmɔːθbɔːl/ (US) /ˈmɒθbɔːl/ (UK)| vt.|封存；搁置|To stop using something, often for a long period of time.|The project was mothballed due to lack of funding.  由于缺乏资金，该项目被搁置了。|近义词：shelve, suspend|反义词：reactivate, restart|动词
