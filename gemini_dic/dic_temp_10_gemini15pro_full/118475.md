mesina:/mɛˈsiːnə/ /məˈsiːnə/| n.|墨西拿（意大利城市）|A port city in northeastern Sicily, Italy. It is separated from mainland Italy by the Strait of Messina.|I visited Messina during my trip to Sicily. 我在西西里岛旅行期间参观了墨西拿。|Near Messina, strong currents make navigation hazardous. 在墨西拿附近，强烈的海流使航行变得危险。|近义词：city, port|反义词: (无，因为是地名)|词性:noun
