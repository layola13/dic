dramaturgist:/ˈdræmətɜːrdʒɪst/| n. | 剧作家；戏剧顾问；文学顾问 | A dramaturgist is a literary editor or advisor of a theatre, opera, or film company who researches, selects, adapts, edits, and interprets scripts, libretti, texts, and other dramatic works. | The dramaturgist helped the playwright develop the characters and storyline.  剧作家帮助编剧发展人物和故事情节。| 近义词:  literary advisor, dramatic advisor, script editor | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun
