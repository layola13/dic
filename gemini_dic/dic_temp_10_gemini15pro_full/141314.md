open-up:/ˈoʊpən ˈʌp/| phrasal verb |打开, 开放; 畅所欲言; 开始; 扩大; 出现|The store opens up at 9 am.  这家商店早上9点开门。|近义词：begin, start, commence, initiate, disclose, reveal, expand, broaden, develop|反义词:close down, shut down, conceal, hide, restrict, limit|词性:动词短语

open-up:/ˈoʊpən ˈʌp/| phrasal verb |坦诚相待; 打开心扉|He finally opened up about his feelings. 他终于坦诚了自己的感受。|近义词：confide, share, express, communicate|反义词: bottle up, withhold, suppress|词性:动词短语

ETYMOLOGY->
From "open" (Old English "open") + "up" (Old English "up").  The combination emphasizes the action of becoming open or making something open.
