morcha:/ˈmɔːrtʃə/| n.|行军，游行，示威，(印地语)阵地，营地|A large group of people walk together to show that they support or oppose something.  It can also refer to a military camp or position, especially in Indian English.|例句：The morcha against the new law attracted thousands of people.  反对新法律的示威游行吸引了成千上万的人。

例句：The army set up a morcha on the border.  军队在边境设立了营地。|近义词：march, procession, demonstration, protest, rally, picket, (military) position, encampment|反义词: retreat, withdrawal|词性: noun
