imprisons:/ɪmˈprɪzənz/
verb
监禁，关押
confines or restricts someone's freedom, typically by putting them in prison.
The government imprisons those who speak out against it. 政府监禁那些公开反对它的人。
He was imprisoned for life. 他被判处终身监禁。
Synonyms: incarcerate, jail, confine, detain, restrain
Antonyms: release, liberate, free
