nonfrigidness:/ˌnɑːnˈfrɪdʒɪdnəs/
n.
lack of frigidity; warmth
The nonfrigidness of the room was welcoming after being outside in the cold. 房间里的温暖在外面寒冷之后令人感到舒适。
nonfrigidness of the climate made it a popular vacation destination. 气候的温暖使它成为一个受欢迎的度假胜地。
Synonyms: warmth, geniality, cordiality
Antonyms: frigidity, coldness, iciness
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix meaning "not") + frigid (adjective meaning "extremely cold") + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)
