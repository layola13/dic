manderil:/ˈmændrɪl/| n.|山魈|A large, brightly colored, short-tailed monkey (Mandrillus sphinx) of western Africa, having a long muzzle with prominent ridges and vivid blue and scarlet coloration on the face and rump in adult males.|例句：The mandrill is known for its vibrant facial colors. 山魈以其鲜艳的面部颜色而闻名。

例句：We saw a mandrill at the zoo. 我们在动物园里看到了一只山魈。|近义词：baboon, drill|反义词:|n.
