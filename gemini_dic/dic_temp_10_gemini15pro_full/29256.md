cassinian:/kəˈsɪniən/
adjective|卡西尼的；与卡西尼有关的|Relating to the Italian-French astronomer Giovanni Domenico Cassini (1625-1712) or his work, especially his calculations relating to the dimensions of the solar system.|The Cassini spacecraft sent back stunning images of Saturn. 卡西尼号宇宙飞船发回了土星的惊人图像。|Synonym: Related, associated |Antonym: Unrelated, unassociated|adjective

cassinian:/kəˈsɪniən/
noun|卡西尼卵形线|A plane curve, the locus of a point the product of whose distances from two fixed points is constant.|The Cassinian oval is a fascinating mathematical concept. 卡西尼卵形线是一个引人入胜的数学概念。|Synonym: Cassini oval |Antonym: N/A|noun
