amblypoda:/æmˈblɪpədə/| n. |钝脚目|An extinct order of hoofed mammals, characterized by short, stubby feet and relatively small brains. They lived during the Paleogene period.|The amblypoda, like Coryphodon, were once common in North America.|近义词:Pantodonta|反义词: None (since it's an extinct order, there's no direct opposite)|noun
