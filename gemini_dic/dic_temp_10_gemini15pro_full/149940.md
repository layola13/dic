palliative: /ˈpæliətɪv/ | n. | 缓和剂；姑息疗法 | something that reduces the severity of something, typically pain, without removing the cause |  The hospice provides palliatives for terminally ill patients.  临终关怀医院为绝症患者提供缓和治疗。 | synonyms: remedy, relief, solace  | antonyms: irritant, aggravation | noun
