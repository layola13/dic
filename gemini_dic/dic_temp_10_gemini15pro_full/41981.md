ETYMOLOGY->
"cost" comes from Old French "cost" (expense, price), ultimately from Latin "constare" (to stand firm, cost).
"benefit" comes from Old French "benefice" (advantage, profit), from Latin "beneficium" (a kindness, favor, benefit), from "bene" (well) + "facere" (to do).
