migrational:/maɪˈɡreɪʃənəl/| adj.|迁徙的，迁移的|Relating to migration, the movement from one place to another.|例句：Migrational birds fly south for the winter. 候鸟冬天飞往南方。|例句：The scientists studied the migrational patterns of the butterflies. 科学家们研究了蝴蝶的迁徙模式。|近义词：migratory, mobile, nomadic|反义词：settled, stationary, resident|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From "migration" + "-al".  "Migration" comes from the Latin "migratio,"  meaning "a removal, change of abode," from "migrare"  "to change one's abode, depart," related to "migrare" "to move, transfer, transport."
