outcharging:/ˌaʊtˈtʃɑːrdʒɪŋ/
verb
过度收费，收费过高
The act of charging excessively or more than is fair or reasonable.
例句：The restaurant was accused of outcharging tourists. 这家餐馆被指控对游客过度收费。

例句：They were outcharging customers for basic services. 他们对客户的基本服务收费过高。

近义词：overcharging, price gouging, profiteering
反义词：undercharging, discounting

词性:v.
