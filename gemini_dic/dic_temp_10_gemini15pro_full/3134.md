aesthetes:/ˈesˌθiːts/ | n. | 审美家；唯美主义者 | A person who is particularly sensitive to and appreciative of beauty, especially in art, music, literature, etc.  |  The gallery was filled with aesthetes admiring the latest exhibition.  画廊里挤满了欣赏最新展览的审美家。| Synonyms: connoisseur, art lover, dilettante | Antonyms: philistine, barbarian | Noun


aesthete:/ˈesˌθiːt/ | n. | 审美家；唯美主义者 | A person who has or affects to have a special appreciation of art and beauty. | He postures as an aesthete but actually knows very little about art. 他摆出一副审美家的样子，但实际上对艺术知之甚少。 | Synonyms:  connoisseur, art lover | Antonyms: philistine | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek αἰσθητής (aisthētḗs, “perceiver, one who feels”), from αἰσθάνομαι (aisthánomai, “I perceive, feel”).
