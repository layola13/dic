nonalienating:/nɒnˈeɪliəneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非异化的，不疏远的
Not causing a feeling of separation or isolation; promoting a sense of belonging and connection.
例句：The company culture is nonalienating, making employees feel valued and part of a team. 公司文化是非异化的，让员工感到被重视，并成为团队的一员。

例句：The therapist created a nonalienating environment for her patients to openly share their struggles.  这位治疗师为她的病人创造了一个不疏远的环境，让他们公开分享他们的挣扎。

近义词：inclusive, welcoming, integrating, unifying
反义词：alienating, isolating, estranging, marginalizing
词性: adjective
