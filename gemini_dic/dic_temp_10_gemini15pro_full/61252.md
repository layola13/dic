emancipated: /ɪˈmænsɪpeɪtɪd/
adjective
解放了的，摆脱束缚的
Free from legal, social, or political restrictions; liberated.
例句：The emancipated slaves rejoiced in their newfound freedom.  获得解放的奴隶们为他们新获得的自由而欢欣鼓舞。

例句：She felt emancipated after moving out of her parents' house.  搬出父母家后，她感到自由了。

近义词：liberated, freed, unrestrained, independent
反义词：constrained, restricted, enslaved, dependent
词性：adjective

词源：From Latin emancipatus, past participle of emancipare (“to release from paternal power, set free”), from e (“out of, from”) + mancipium (“ownership, legal power over another person”).
