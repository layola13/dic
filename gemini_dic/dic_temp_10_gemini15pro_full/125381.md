multimodal:/ˌmʌltiˈməʊdəl/ | adj. |多模式的，多模态的 | Combining or involving several modes, especially of transportation or communication. |  This multimodal transportation system integrates road, rail, and waterway shipping.  这种多式联运系统整合了公路、铁路和水路运输。 | Example:  Multimodal learning is a hot research topic in AI. 多模态学习是人工智能中的一个热门研究课题。| Synonyms: combined, integrated, diverse | Antonyms: unimodal, single-mode | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From multi- (meaning "many") + modal (meaning "mode" or "way"). The term "mode" comes from Latin "modus," meaning "measure," "manner," or "way."
