domicile:/ˈdɒmɪsaɪl/ /ˈdɑːməsəl/| n. |住所，住处；(法律)住所|A person's fixed, permanent, and principal home for legal purposes. |He changed his domicile from New York to California. 他把住所从纽约改到了加利福尼亚。

The company's domicile is in London. 这家公司的注册地是伦敦。|近义词：residence, abode, dwelling, home|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
