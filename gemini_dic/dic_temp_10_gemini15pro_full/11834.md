arrestable:/əˈrɛstəb(ə)l/
adjective
可逮捕的；可拘留的
Liable to arrest.
例句：His actions were clearly arrestable offenses. 他的行为明显是可逮捕的罪行。

例句：The police determined that the suspect was arrestable. 警方确定嫌疑人可以被逮捕。

近义词：apprehensible, detainable
反义词：legal, lawful
词性：adjective
