disrobing:/dɪsˈroʊbɪŋ/
verb
脱衣服
the act of taking off one's clothes
She was disrobing in the privacy of her bedroom. 她正在卧室里脱衣服。
近义词: undressing, stripping
反义词: dressing, clothing
词性: verb (动名词)

disrobe:/dɪsˈroʊb/
verb
脱衣服
to remove one's clothes
He disrobed and stepped into the shower. 他脱下衣服，走进了淋浴间。
近义词: undress, strip
反义词: dress, clothe
词性: verb
