columbate:/ˈkʌləmbeɪt/ | n. | 铌酸盐 | A salt or ester of columbic acid.  |  Lithium columbate is used in some optical applications.  铌酸锂用于一些光学应用。| 近义词: niobate (often used interchangeably) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From columbium (an older name for the element niobium) + -ate (a suffix used to form names of salts or esters of acids).  Columbium was named after Columbia, a poetic name for America, where the ore from which it was first isolated was found.
