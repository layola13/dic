inflame:/ɪnˈfleɪm/ /ɪnˈfleɪm/ |v.|使燃烧；使发炎；激怒|to cause to become red, sore, and swollen, especially as a result of infection or injury; to arouse or excite feelings and passions, especially anger|The harsh criticism inflamed his temper.  严厉的批评激怒了他。|近义词: ignite, kindle, anger, enrage|反义词: soothe, calm, appease|词性:动词

inflame:/ɪnˈfleɪm/ /ɪnˈfleɪm/ |v.|煽动；加剧|to make a situation or problem worse|His inflammatory remarks only inflamed the situation further.  他煽动性的话语只会使情况进一步恶化。|近义词:aggravate, exacerbate, worsen|反义词: alleviate, mitigate, improve|词性:动词
