pasteurized:/ˈpæstʃəˌraɪzd/ | adj. | 巴氏杀菌的；经巴氏消毒的 |  Heated to a specific temperature to kill harmful bacteria but without major changes to the food's taste or nutritional value.  |  例句：We always buy pasteurized milk. 我们总是买巴氏消毒牛奶。 | 近义词：sterilized, disinfected | 反义词：unpasteurized, raw | 词性: 形容词
Etymology: From Louis Pasteur, the French scientist who developed the process.
