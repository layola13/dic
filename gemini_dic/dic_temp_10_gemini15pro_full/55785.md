diverted:/daɪˈvɜːrtɪd/ /dɪˈvɜːrtɪd/| adj.|转移的，转向的，被挪用的，娱乐的|redirected, turned aside; amused, entertained|The flight was diverted to another airport due to bad weather. 由于天气恶劣，航班改飞到另一个机场。|synonyms: redirected, rerouted, distracted, amused, entertained|antonyms: undiverted, direct, focused, bored, uninterested|adjective

divert:/daɪˈvɜːrt/ /dɪˈvɜːrt/| vt.|转移，转向，使分心，挪用，娱乐|to turn aside from a course or direction; to draw the attention of someone away from something; to entertain|The loud noise diverted my attention from my work.  巨大的噪音使我无法集中注意力工作。|synonyms: redirect, reroute, distract, amuse, entertain|antonyms: maintain, focus, bore|verb
