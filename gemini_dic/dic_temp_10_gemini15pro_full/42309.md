counter-penalty:/ˈkaʊntər ˈpɛnəlti/| n.|反惩罚; 对抗性处罚|A counter-penalty is a penalty imposed in reaction to a penalty imposed by another party.  It is essentially a retaliatory measure. |Example 1: The country imposed a counter-penalty on imported goods after facing trade restrictions from another nation.  这个国家在面临另一个国家的贸易限制后，对进口商品施加了反惩罚措施。

Example 2:  As a counter-penalty for the team's illegal substitution, they were forced to forfeit the game. 作为对该队非法换人的反惩罚，他们被迫放弃比赛。|Synonym: retaliatory penalty, countermeasure, reprisal|Antonym:  concession, compromise|noun
