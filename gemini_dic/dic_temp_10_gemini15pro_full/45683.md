curtly:/ˈkɜːrtli/ (US) /ˈkɜːtli/ (UK)| adv.|唐突地；简略地；生硬地|In a brief and abrupt manner, often seeming rude or dismissive.|“I don't want to,” she said curtly. “我不想去，”她唐突地说。|He curtly refused the invitation. 他粗暴地拒绝了邀请。|近义词：abruptly, brusquely, shortly, tersely|反义词：politely, courteously, graciously|词性:副词
