nonvegetativeness:/ˌnɒnˌvɛdʒɪˈteɪtɪvnəs/
| n. |非植物状态，无生命状态|The state of not exhibiting the characteristics of life associated with plants, especially growth and development; the state of being inanimate or lifeless.|The prolonged nonvegetativeness of the patient was a cause for concern. 病人长时间处于无生命状态令人担忧。|近义词：inanimateness, lifelessness|反义词：vegetativeness, aliveness|Noun
