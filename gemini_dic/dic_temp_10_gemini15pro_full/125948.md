musicalization:/ˌmjuːzɪkəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
音乐化;谱曲
The musicalization of poems can add another layer of depth and emotion. 将诗歌谱曲可以增添另一层深度和情感。
The play's success was due in part to the excellent musicalization. 这部戏剧的成功部分归功于优秀的音乐编排。
近义词:adaptation, arrangement, setting
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑"deconstruction" 解构)
词性:名词

Etymology: From music + -al + -ize + -ation.  Ultimately from Greek "mousikē" (art of the Muses).
