nonrevolution:/ˌnɑːnˌrɛvəˈluːʃən/ | n. | 非革命，不革命 | Absence of revolution; a situation where a revolution does not occur or is actively prevented. | The government's policies led to a period of nonrevolution. 政府的政策导致了一段非革命时期。| The country experienced a long period of nonrevolution despite the social unrest. 尽管社会动荡不安，这个国家经历了很长一段时间的非革命时期。| 近义词: stability, stagnation, quiescence | 反义词: revolution, upheaval, revolt | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
revolution (noun): from Late Latin *revolutionem "a revolving, turning around," from Latin revolvere "to roll back"
