bridgetree: /ˈbrɪdʒˌtriː/
n.
桥牌树 (一种用于桥牌比赛的图表或结构，用于展示比赛进程和结果)
A bridgetree is a diagram or structure used in bridge tournaments to display the progress and results of the matches.
The bridgetree showed that Team A was advancing to the finals. 桥牌树显示A队晋级决赛。
近义词: tournament chart, competition diagram
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用表示非比赛记录的词，例如：non-tournament record)
词性: noun


bridgetree: /ˈbrɪdʒˌtriː/
n.
桥树 (比喻意义上，指连接不同事物或群体的桥梁)
Figuratively, a bridgetree can represent a bridge connecting different things or groups.
The new community center serves as a bridgetree between different cultures.  新的社区中心充当了不同文化之间的桥梁。
近义词: link, connection, bridge
反义词: barrier, division, separation
词性: noun


词源:  "bridge" + "tree". "bridge" 指桥梁，连接；"tree" 指树，树状结构。组合在一起表示类似树状结构的比赛图表，或者比喻连接不同事物的桥梁。
