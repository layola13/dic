breccia:/ˈbrɛtʃ(i)ə/ /ˈbrɛtʃə/
| n. |角砾岩|A rock composed of sharp-angled fragments embedded in a fine-grained matrix.|The geologist identified the sample as a volcanic breccia. 地质学家将该样本鉴定为火山角砾岩。|
|例句：The museum displayed a large piece of breccia from a nearby quarry.  博物馆展出了一块来自附近采石场的大块角砾岩。|
|例句：The impact of the meteorite created a field of breccia. 陨石的撞击产生了一片角砾岩地带。|
|近义词：conglomerate, rubble|
|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a uniform, unfragmented rock)|
|词性: noun|
