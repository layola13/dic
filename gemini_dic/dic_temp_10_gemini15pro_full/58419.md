ducally:/ˈduːkəli/
adverb|像公爵一样地；以公爵的方式|In a manner befitting a duke; like a duke.
He behaved ducally, dispensing generosity and wisdom. 他举止像公爵一样，慷慨大方，充满智慧。
近义词:aristocratically, nobly, regally
反义词:basely, humbly, meanly
词性:副词

ducally:/ˈduːkəli/
adjective|公爵的；公爵领地的|Of or relating to a duke or dukedom.  (This usage is less common than the adverbial form.)
The ducally owned lands stretched for miles. 公爵拥有的土地绵延数英里。（这种用法不如副词形式常见）
近义词:ducal
反义词: (No direct antonym, depends on the context.  Could be 'common', 'plebeian' etc.)
词性:形容词
