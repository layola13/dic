bouffant:/buːˈfɒnt/ /buːˈfɑːnt/
bouffant| n. adj.|蓬松发型，蓬松的|A bouffant hairstyle is one in which the hair is styled high on the head, often using teasing or padding to create volume.  It can also be used as an adjective to describe something that is full and puffy.|She wore her hair in a bouffant. 她梳了个蓬松的发型。|近义词：puffy, full, voluminous|反义词:flat, sleek|词性:名词，形容词
bouffants:/buːˈfɒnts/ /buːˈfɑːnts/
bouffants| n.|蓬松发型（复数）|Plural form of bouffant, referring to multiple bouffant hairstyles.|The 1960s were known for their elaborate bouffants.  六十年代以其精致的蓬松发型而闻名。|近义词：puffy hairstyles, full hairstyles|反义词:flat hairstyles, sleek hairstyles|词性:名词
