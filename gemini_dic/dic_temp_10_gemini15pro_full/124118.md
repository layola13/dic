mortarless:/ˈmɔːrtərlɪs/
adjective
无砂浆的，不用砂浆的
Built without the use of mortar, a binding material used in masonry.
The Incas built magnificent mortarless stone walls. 印加人建造了宏伟的无砂浆石墙。
dry-stone, dry-laid
mortared
