barbing:/ˈbɑːrbɪŋ/| n.|倒钩，倒刺；(尤指鱼钩上的)倒钩；带倒钩的铁丝网|A barb is a sharp point that curves backwards, especially on a fish hook or a piece of wire, making it difficult to remove.|The fish hook has a barb to prevent the fish from escaping.|近义词：hook, point, spike|反义词:|n.

barbing:/ˈbɑːrbɪŋ/| v.|装倒钩；用带倒钩的铁丝网围住|To barb something means to put barbs on it or to enclose it with barbed wire.|He barbed the wire fence to keep out intruders.|近义词：hook, point, spike|反义词:|v.
