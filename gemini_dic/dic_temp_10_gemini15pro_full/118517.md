mesocephalous:/ˌmɛsoʊˈsɛfələs/ /ˌmɛzəʊˈsɛfələs/| adj.|中头型的|Having a head of medium proportions; having a cephalic index between 75 and 80.|The skull found at the archaeological site was determined to be mesocephalous.|近义词: mesocephalic|反义词: brachycephalic, dolichocephalic|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From meso- + -cephalous, from Greek kephalē "head."
