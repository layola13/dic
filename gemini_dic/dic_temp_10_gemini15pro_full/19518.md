bifaces:/ˈbaɪfeɪsiːz/
n.
双面器，两面打制石器
A biface is a stone tool that has been flaked on both sides to create a cutting edge.
Archaeologists unearthed several bifaces at the site. 考古学家在遗址 unearthed 了几个双面器。
synonyms: hand axe, handaxe
antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a specific tool type.  Could consider "unworked stone" or "natural rock" as opposites in a broad sense.)
