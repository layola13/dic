autopathic:/ˌɔːtəˈpæθɪk/
adj.
自我疗法的，自疗的
Relating to or involving autopathy, a system of alternative medicine in which a diluted dose of a patient's own bodily fluids or tissues is used to treat their illness.
Autopathic medicine is based on the principle of "like cures like".  The practitioner prepares a remedy from a sample of the patient's saliva, urine, or blood, and this is administered to stimulate the body's natural healing processes.
Autopathic treatments are sometimes used for allergies and chronic illnesses. 自我疗法有时用于治疗过敏和慢性疾病。
Synonyms:  self-healing, autotherapeutic
Antonyms: allopathic
词性: adjective
