aceldama: /əˈkɛldəmə/ | n. | 血田，凶地 | Akeldama (Aramaic: חקל דמא; ḥaqal dəmā, "field of blood"), the name given to the piece of land, the purchase of which was made with the blood money paid to Judas Iscariot for the betrayal of Jesus.
