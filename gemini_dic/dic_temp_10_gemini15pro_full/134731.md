nonmeat:/ˈnɒnmiːt/
adjective
非肉类的
Made from ingredients other than animal flesh.  Not containing meat.
Example:  She prefers nonmeat dishes. 她更喜欢素菜。
Example:  The restaurant offers a wide variety of nonmeat options. 这家餐厅提供各种各样的素食选择。
Synonym:vegetarian, meatless, plant-based
Antonym:meat, fleshy
词性:形容词
