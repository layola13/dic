innocences:/ˈɪnəsənsɪz/
n.
无辜，清白；天真烂漫；单纯
lack of guilt or wrongdoing; the state of being harmless or naive.
The lawyer argued his client's innocence. 律师辩称他的客户是无辜的。
The children played with a joyful innocence. 孩子们天真烂漫地玩耍着。
synonyms:guiltlessness, blamelessness, purity, naivety, ingenuousness
antonyms:guilt, culpability, wickedness, sophistication, worldliness
noun
