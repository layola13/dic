convicting:/kənˈvɪktɪŋ/
adjective
令人信服的;有说服力的
persuading someone that someone is guilty of a crime or that something is true
The evidence was so convicting that the jury quickly reached a guilty verdict.  证据如此确凿，陪审团很快作出了有罪判决。
Synonyms: persuasive, compelling, convincing
Antonyms: unconvincing, weak, doubtful
词性: adjective

convicting:/kənˈvɪktɪŋ/
verb
(convict的现在分词)证明…有罪;使…知罪
(of a feeling or situation) making you believe that something is true or that you are guilty of something bad
His convicting words made me realize the error of my ways. 他令人信服的话让我意识到了我的错误。
Synonyms: incriminating, damning
Antonyms: exonerating, acquitting
词性: verb
