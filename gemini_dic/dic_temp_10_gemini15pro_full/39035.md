conflicting:/kənˈflɪktɪŋ/ | adj. |冲突的，矛盾的，抵触的 | Being in opposition or disagreement; incompatible. |  The two witnesses gave conflicting accounts of the accident.  两个目击者对事故的描述相互矛盾。| synonyms: clashing, contradictory, incompatible, discordant, opposing, differing, divergent, irreconcilable | antonyms: consistent, harmonious, compatible, agreeing, concordant, similar | adjective

conflicted:/kənˈflɪktɪd/ | adj. | 矛盾的，纠结的 | Having or showing confused and mutually inconsistent feelings. | He felt conflicted about leaving his job. 他对离职感到矛盾。 | synonyms: ambivalent, torn, uncertain, doubtful, hesitant, wavering | antonyms: resolute, decisive, certain, sure | adjective
