orohippus:/ˌɔːroʊˈhɪpəs/| n. |始祖马|An extinct genus of early horse, considered a transitional form between *Eohippus* and *Epihippus*.  It lived in the Eocene epoch.|Orohippus was slightly larger than Eohippus and had four toes on its front feet and three on its hind feet. 始祖马比始马体型略大，前脚有四趾，后脚有三趾。| Orohippus fossils have been found in North America.  始祖马化石已在北美被发现。|近义词：Eohippus (although not a perfect synonym, it represents a closely related ancestor),  progenitor|反义词:descendant|词性:noun

词源:
From Greek *oros* (mountain) + *hippos* (horse).  This likely refers to the mountainous terrain where fossils were found.
