bestowment:/bɪˈstoʊmənt/
n.
赋予，授予；赠与；礼物
the act of bestowing something, or something bestowed, especially an endowment
例句：
The bestowment of the Nobel Prize is a great honor.  诺贝尔奖的授予是一项巨大的荣誉。

The university received a generous bestowment from a wealthy alumnus. 这所大学收到了一位富有校友的慷慨捐赠。

近义词：gift, donation, grant, endowment, conferment
反义词：deprivation, withholding
词性: noun
