flatulent: /ˈflætʃʊlənt/ (US), /ˈflætjʊlənt/ (UK)| adj.| 腹胀的；空虚的；夸大的 |  Affected by or producing gas in the digestive tract; full of or characterized by empty or pretentious talk or writing.|  After eating too many beans, he felt quite flatulent.  吃完太多豆子后，他感到很胀气。| synonyms: gassy, bloated, windy, pompous, pretentious | antonyms: deflated, humble, modest | adjective

flatulence: /ˈflætʃʊləns/ (US), /ˈflætjʊləns/ (UK) | n. | 腹胀；空话；夸大 | The presence of excessive gas in the digestive tract; empty or pretentious talk or writing. |  The doctor prescribed medication for his flatulence. 医生给他开了治疗腹胀的药。| synonyms: gas, bloating, wind, bombast, pomposity | antonyms: - | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *flatulentus, from Latin flatus (“a blowing, blast, breath”), past participle of flare (“to blow, puff”).
