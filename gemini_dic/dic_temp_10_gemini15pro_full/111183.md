lithographers:/lɪˈθɒɡrəfərz/
n.
平版印刷师，石版印刷工人
A person who designs, creates, or prints lithographs.  A skilled craftsperson who specializes in the process of lithography.
The lithographers carefully prepared the stones for printing.  平版印刷师仔细地准备印刷用的石头。
Modern lithographers often use metal plates instead of stones. 现代平版印刷师经常使用金属板代替石头。
近义词:printers, graphic artists
反义词: N/A
词性:noun

词源:  From French lithographe, from litho- + -graphe, from Greek lithos "stone" + graphein "to write."
