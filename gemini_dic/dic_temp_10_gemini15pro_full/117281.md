mechanizes:/ˈmɛkəˌnaɪzɪz/
verb
使机械化; 用机械装备
equips with machinery, especially to replace manual labor.
The factory mechanized its production line.  这家工厂实现了生产线的机械化。
The army mechanized its infantry divisions.  这支军队将步兵师机械化了。
Synonyms: automate, motorize
Antonyms: manualize, demechanize
词性: 动词(verb)
