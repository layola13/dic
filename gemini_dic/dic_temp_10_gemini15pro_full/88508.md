history-sheeter: /ˈhɪstriˌʃiːtər/
n.
惯犯；有犯罪记录的人；劣迹斑斑者
A person with a long criminal record, especially one who is known to the police.
The police are keeping a close watch on several known history-sheeters in the area.  警方正在密切监视该地区几个已知的惯犯。
He's a history-sheeter with multiple convictions for theft. 他是一个有多次盗窃前科的惯犯。
synonyms: habitual offender, repeat offender, hardened criminal
antonyms: law-abiding citizen, model citizen
noun
