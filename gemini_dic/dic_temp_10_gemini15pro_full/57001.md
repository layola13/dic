double-glazed:/ˌdʌbl ˈɡleɪzd/
adj.
双层玻璃的，双层玻璃窗的
Having two panes of glass in a window or door frame, separated by a space of air or gas, for insulation against heat or sound.
例句：We've just had double-glazed windows fitted. 我们刚安装了双层玻璃窗。

例句：Double-glazed windows can significantly reduce energy bills. 双层玻璃窗可以显著降低能源费用。


近义词：insulated
反义词：single-glazed
词性：adjective
