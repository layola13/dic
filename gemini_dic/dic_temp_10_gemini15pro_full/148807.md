overvary:/ˌoʊvərˈvɛri/| v. |过度变化，变化过多|To vary or change excessively or too frequently.|The temperature overvaried throughout the day, making it difficult to dress appropriately. 气温整天变化太大，让人很难穿得合适。|近义词：fluctuate, oscillate|反义词：stabilize, remain constant|词性:动词

Etymology:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "vary" (from Old French *varier*, from Latin *variāre* "to change, alter, make different").
