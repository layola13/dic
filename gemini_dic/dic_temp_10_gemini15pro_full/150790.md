paradisos:/ˈpærədaɪsɒs/ /ˌpærəˈdaɪsoʊs/| n.|天堂，乐园|A place or state of bliss, felicity, or delight.|例句：The island was a tropical paradisos. 这个岛屿是一个热带天堂。|例句：She imagined a paradisos where she could paint all day. 她想象着一个可以整天画画的天堂。|近义词：heaven, eden, utopia, bliss|反义词：hell, inferno|词性：名词
Etymology: From Ancient Greek παράδεισος (parádeisos, “park, paradise”), from Old Iranian *parādaiĵah (“walled enclosure”), borrowed into Akkadian pardesu and Aramaic pardaysa.
