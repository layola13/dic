arbitrament:/ˌɑːrbɪˈtreɪmənt/
|n.|仲裁,裁决,判决
|The matter was settled by arbitrament.|这件事通过仲裁解决了。
|The union agreed to submit the dispute to binding arbitrament.|工会同意将争议提交具有约束力的仲裁。
|近义词: arbitration, adjudication, mediation, judgment, decision
|反义词: disagreement, indecision, conflict, dispute
|词性: noun

Etymology:
From Old French *arbitrement*, from Late Latin *arbitramentum*, from Latin *arbitrari* "to judge, examine," from *arbiter* "judge, umpire."
