designing:/dɪˈzaɪnɪŋ/
verb
设计;构思;绘制;企图;打算
present participle of design
例句：She is designing a new dress. 她正在设计一件新裙子。
例句：They are designing a marketing campaign. 他们在设计一个营销活动。
近义词：planning, creating, developing, formulating, conceiving, contriving, engineering, fashioning, shaping, styling
反义词：destroying, ruining
词性：v.

Etymology:
From Middle English *designen, *desinen, from Anglo-Norman designer, from Old French designer, deigner (“to designate, appoint, assign, ordain; to condescend, deign”), from Latin dēsīgnāre (“to mark out, trace out, describe, designate, appoint”), from dē- (“down, completely, from”) + sīgnāre (“to mark”), from sīgnum (“mark, sign”).
