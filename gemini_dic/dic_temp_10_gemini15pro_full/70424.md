fightings:/ˈfaɪtɪŋz/ | n. | 打斗，战斗，争吵 |  Acts of fighting; battles or quarrels. | The fightings in the city have ceased. 城市里的战斗已经停止了。| 近义词：battles, clashes, combats, struggles, conflicts | 反义词：peace, truce, harmony | 词性: 名词

词源:  From the verb "fight" + "-ing" (present participle) + "-s" (plural).  Ultimately from Old English *feohtan*.
