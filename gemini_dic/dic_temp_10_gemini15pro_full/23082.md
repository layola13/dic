boundlessly:/ˈbaʊndləsli/
adverb
无限地，无边无际地
In a boundless manner; without limits.
Her energy seemed boundlessly optimistic. 她似乎拥有无限的乐观活力。
The prairie stretched boundlessly before them.  大草原在他们面前无限延伸。
近义词:limitlessly, infinitely, endlessly, immensely
反义词:finitely, limitedly, restrictively
词性:adverb
