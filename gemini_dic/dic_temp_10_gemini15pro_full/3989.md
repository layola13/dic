agv:/eɪdʒiːˈviː/ |n.|automated guided vehicle 自动导引车|An AGV is a portable robot that follows markers or wires in the floor, or uses vision or lasers. 自动导引车是一种便携式机器人，它沿着地板上的标记或电线移动，或使用视觉或激光导航。|The AGVs transport materials around the factory floor. 这些自动导引车在工厂车间内运输物料。|近义词:autonomous mobile robot (AMR) |反义词:manual cart|noun
