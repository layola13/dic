microbenthic:/ˌmaɪkrəʊˈbɛnθɪk/ | adj. |微底栖的|Relating to or inhabiting the microbenthos, the microscopic organisms living within or on the bottom sediments of a body of water.|The study focused on the microbenthic community structure in the intertidal zone. 这项研究的重点是潮间带微底栖生物群落结构。|近义词：benthic|反义词：pelagic|词性：形容词

Etymology:
micro- (small) + benthic (relating to the bottom of a body of water)
