distincture: /dɪˈstɪŋktʃər/
n.
区别，特性，显著的特征
A distinguishing mark or characteristic.
The distincture of her voice made her easily recognizable. 她的声音特征使她很容易被认出来。

近义词: distinction, characteristic, feature, peculiarity, attribute
反义词: similarity, resemblance, likeness

词性: noun

词源: From Late Latin *distinctura*, from Latin distinctus (distinct).
