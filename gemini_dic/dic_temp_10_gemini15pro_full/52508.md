differential:/ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃəl/ | adj. n.| 差别的，微分的；差速器；微分|  Relating to a difference; a difference in amount, value, or size.  A gear assembly that allows wheels on the same axle to rotate at different speeds, as in rounding a curve. In mathematics, an infinitesimal change in a variable.|

例句：The differential diagnosis includes several possibilities.  鉴别诊断包括几种可能性。

例句：There's a huge differential between the salaries of men and women. 男女工资差异很大。

例句：The car’s differential needs to be repaired. 这辆车的差速器需要修理。

例句：In calculus, a differential is an infinitesimal quantity. 在微积分中，微分是一个无穷小的量。

近义词：distinctive, different, varying, disparate, incremental, marginal | 反义词：similar, uniform, identical | 词性：adjective, noun


differential:/ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃəl/ | n. | 差额，差异；微分|The result of subtraction; the difference between two values. An infinitesimal change in a variable. |

例句：The pay differential between men and women is still a problem. 男女薪酬差异仍然是一个问题。

例句：The differential in their ages is significant. 他们的年龄差异很大。

近义词：difference, disparity, gap, margin | 反义词：similarity, parity | 词性：noun


词源: From Late Latin *differentiālis, from Latin differentia "difference," from differre "to differ."
