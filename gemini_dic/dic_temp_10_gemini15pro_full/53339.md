disadvantage:/ˌdɪsədˈvæntɪdʒ/ | n. |不利，劣势，缺点|A circumstance or condition that reduces the chances of success or effectiveness. |  The disadvantage of this method is its high cost.  这种方法的缺点是成本高。| synonyms: drawback, handicap, detriment | antonyms: advantage, benefit, asset | noun

disadvantage:/ˌdɪsədˈvæntɪdʒ/ | vt. |使处于不利地位，损害|To put at a disadvantage; to cause harm or loss to. | The new regulations disadvantage small businesses. 新规定使小型企业处于不利地位。| synonyms: prejudice, harm, impair | antonyms: advantage, benefit, aid | verb
