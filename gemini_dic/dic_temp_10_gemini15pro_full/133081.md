nonequitably:/ˌnɒnˈɛkwɪtəbli/
adv.
不公平地，不公正地
in a way that is not fair or equitable
The resources were distributed nonequitably among the team members.  资源在团队成员之间分配不公平。
The inheritance was divided nonequitably, leaving one sibling with significantly less. 遗产分配不公，一个兄弟姐妹得到的明显少。
近义词:unfairly, unjustly, inequitably
反义词:fairly, justly, equitably
词性:副词

词源:
nonequitably 由 "non-" (not) + "equitable" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix) 构成。 "equitable" 源自拉丁语 "aequitas" (equality, fairness).
