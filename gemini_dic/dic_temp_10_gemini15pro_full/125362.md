multiloquous:/ˌmʌltɪˈloʊkwəs/| adj.|多话的，喋喋不休的|Talking excessively; loquacious.  Characterized by excessive talkativeness; wordy.|The multiloquous professor kept the class ten minutes late.|近义词：talkative, loquacious, garrulous, voluble|反义词：taciturn, reticent, laconic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *multiloquus*, from multus (“much”) + loquī (“to speak”).
