mummification:/ˌmʌmɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/| n.|木乃伊化；干尸化；做成木乃伊|The mummification process involved elaborate rituals and techniques. 木乃伊化的过程涉及复杂的仪式和技术。|The arid climate of the desert contributed to the natural mummification of the body.  沙漠的干燥气候促成了尸体的自然木乃伊化。|近义词：embalming, preservation|反义词:decomposition, decay|词性:noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *mummificatio, from mummificare "to embalm," from mumia "mummy" + -ficare, combining form of facere "to make."
