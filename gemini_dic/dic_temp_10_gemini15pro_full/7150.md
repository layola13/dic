angelize:/ˈeɪndʒəlaɪz/ | v. |使…像天使；使圣洁；净化；宣传福音|To cause to resemble an angel; to purify; to convert to Christianity.|例句：The artist angelized the features of the Madonna in his painting.  这位艺术家把他画中的圣母玛利亚的容貌描绘得像天使一样。

例句：Missionaries traveled to remote villages to angelize the local population.  传教士前往偏远村庄向当地居民传播福音。|近义词：purify, sanctify, evangelize, Christianize|反义词：corrupt, defile, demonize|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *angelizen*, from Anglo-French *angelizer*, from Late Latin *angelizāre*, from Latin *angelus* (“angel”) + *-izāre* (suffix forming verbs).  Ultimately from Ancient Greek *ἄγγελος* (ángelos, “messenger”).
