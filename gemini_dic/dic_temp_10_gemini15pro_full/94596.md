imprecisely:/ɪm.prɪˈsaɪs.li/
adverb
不精确地；不准确地
not precisely; in an imprecise manner.
He imprecisely described the events of that night, leaving out crucial details. 他对那天晚上的事情描述得不太准确，遗漏了关键的细节。

近义词:inaccurately, vaguely, loosely
反义词:precisely, accurately, exactly
词性:adverb
