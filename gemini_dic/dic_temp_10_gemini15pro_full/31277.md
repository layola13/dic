chapellany: /ˈtʃæp.lɪn.si/ /ˈtʃæp.lən.si/
n.
牧师职位；牧师的管辖区域；牧师的职务
The position or office of a chaplain.  The provision of spiritual guidance and support.
He was offered the chapellany of the college.  他获得了学院牧师的职位。
The hospital has a multi-faith chapellany. 这家医院有一个多信仰的牧师团队。
近义词: chaplaincy, pastorate
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, perhaps secularism or atheism in a broader context.)
词性: noun
