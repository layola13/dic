monosulphide:/ˌmɒnəˈsʌlfaɪd/ /ˌmɑːnəˈsʌlfaɪd/| n.|一硫化物|A binary compound of sulfur with a more electropositive element or group, containing one sulfur atom per molecule.  Specifically, it often refers to compounds containing the S²⁻ anion.|Iron monosulphide is found in meteorites.|近义词: sulfide|反义词: None|词性: noun
