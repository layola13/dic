enlists:/ɪnˈlɪsts/ | v. | 征募；入伍；谋取（帮助或支持）| To enroll or engage in service, especially in the armed forces; to secure the support or aid of someone or something. |  He enlisted in the army at the age of 18. 他18岁参军。 The organization enlisted the help of several volunteers. 这个组织寻求了几名志愿者的帮助。 | 近义词：recruit, enroll, engage, sign up | 反义词: discharge, dismiss |词性:动词

enlists:/ɪnˈlɪsts/ | v. |登记，注册| To formally register or record something. | The hospital enlists all new patients. 医院登记所有新病人。 |近义词: register, enroll | 反义词: deregister | 词性:动词
