gooiest:/ˈɡuːiɪst/| adj.|最粘的|Superlative form of gooey: most gooey.  Having the highest degree of a thick, sticky, viscous consistency.|The gooiest chocolate cake I've ever eaten. 我吃过的最粘稠的巧克力蛋糕。|近义词：stickiest, most viscous, tackiest|反义词:driest, firmest|词性:形容词
词源:From gooey + -est (superlative suffix). Ultimately from goo.
