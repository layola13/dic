aeronautically:/ˌɛərəˈnɔːtɪkli/
adverb
航空地；飞行地
In a way that relates to aeronautics; with respect to the operation or design of aircraft.
The design was aeronautically sound.  这个设计在航空学上是合理的。
The plane was aeronautically advanced for its time. 这架飞机就其时代而言在航空技术上是先进的。
synonym:aviationally, airwise
antonym:N/A
词性:adverb
