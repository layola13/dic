antiegoist:/ˌæntiˈiːɡoʊɪst/
|n.|反利己主义者，无私的人|A person who opposes egoism or selfishness; an altruist.|He was an antiegoist, always putting others' needs before his own. 他是一个反利己主义者，总是把别人的需要放在自己的前面。|近义词: altruist, philanthropist, humanitarian|反义词: egoist, selfish person|词性:noun

antiegoistic:/ˌæntiˌiːɡoʊˈɪstɪk/
|adj.|反利己的，无私的|Opposed to egoism or selfishness; altruistic.|Her antiegoistic actions inspired many. 她无私的行为激励了很多人。|近义词: altruistic, selfless, unselfish|反义词: egoistic, selfish|词性:adjective
