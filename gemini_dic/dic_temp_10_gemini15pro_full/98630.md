instrumenting:/ˈɪnstrəˌmɛntɪŋ/ |v.|装备，检测，用仪器测量|The process of equipping something with instruments for measuring, recording, or controlling.  They are instrumenting the aircraft to collect data during the test flight. 他们正在为飞机配备仪器，以便在试飞期间收集数据。|近义词：equipping, fitting, rigging, tooling|反义词: dismantling|动词

instrumenting:/ˈɪnstrəˌmɛntɪŋ/ |adj.|用于测量的，仪器的|Relating to or used for instrumentation.  They used instrumenting amplifiers to boost the signal. 他们使用仪表放大器来增强信号。|近义词：measuring, monitoring|反义词: N/A|形容词
