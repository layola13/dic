overstrengthen:/ˌoʊvərˈstreŋθən/
verb
过度加强；过分增强
to strengthen something too much
The alloy was overstrengthened, making it brittle.  这种合金被过度强化，导致它变脆了。
The government's measures to overstrengthen the currency backfired. 政府过度加强货币的措施适得其反。
overbuild, reinforce excessively
weaken, undermine
v.
