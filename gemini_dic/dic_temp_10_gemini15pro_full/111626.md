locality:/loʊˈkælɪti/| n.|地点，位置；地区，场所|A particular place or district.|例句：The police were investigating the locality of the crime. 警方正在调查犯罪地点。|例句：The plant is found in several localities in the mountains. 这种植物生长在山区的几个地方。|近义词：area, region, place, vicinity, neighborhood, district, zone, location, site, spot|反义词：ubiquity, omnipresence|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *localite, from Old French localité, from Medieval Latin localitās (“locality”), from Latin localis (“local”), from locus (“place”).
