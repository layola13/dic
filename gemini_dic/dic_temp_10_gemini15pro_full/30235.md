cellular:/ˈseljələr/ (US), /ˈsɛljʊlə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|细胞的；蜂窝状的；多孔的|Relating to or consisting of cells, or resembling the structure of cells.|例句：Cellular respiration is the process by which organisms convert nutrients into energy. 细胞呼吸是有机体将营养物质转化为能量的过程。

例句：The new cellular phone is much smaller than the old one. 新的手机比旧的小多了。|近义词：cell-like, mobile (in the context of phones)|反义词:acellular, non-cellular|词性:adjective
