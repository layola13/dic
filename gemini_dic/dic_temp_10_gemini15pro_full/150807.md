paraes:/ˈpærəˌiːz/
noun
寄生虫,寄生物
An organism that lives in or on another organism (its host) and benefits by deriving nutrients at the other's expense.
The dog was infested with paraes. 这只狗身上有很多寄生虫。
synonyms: parasite, freeloader, leech, hanger-on
antonyms: host, symbiont
n.
