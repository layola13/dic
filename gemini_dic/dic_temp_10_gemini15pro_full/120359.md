minimizer:/ˈmɪnɪmaɪzər/| n.|最小化器，极小化器|A minimizer is an algorithm or tool that reduces something to its smallest possible amount or degree.  It can refer to a person who consistently downplays their own achievements or positive qualities. |例句：The company uses a cost minimizer to streamline its expenses. 公司使用成本最小化器来精简开支。

The minimizer helped us find the optimal solution for the problem. 这个最小化器帮助我们找到了问题的最佳解决方案。|近义词：reducer, optimizer|反义词: maximizer|词性:noun
