fumous:/ˈfjuːməs/| adj.|冒烟的；烟雾弥漫的|Producing or full of fumes; smoky.|The air was fumous with the smell of burning rubber. 空气中弥漫着燃烧橡胶的气味。|近义词：smoky, fuming|反义词:clear, airy|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *fūmōsus, from fūmus (“smoke”).
