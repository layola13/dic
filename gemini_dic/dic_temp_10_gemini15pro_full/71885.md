fleur-de-lis:/ˌflɜːr dəˈliː/ /ˌflɝːr dəˈliː/| n.|鸢尾花；百合花饰|A stylized iris or lily; a heraldic emblem of France.|The fleur-de-lis is a common symbol in French architecture. 鸢尾花是法国建筑中常见的象征。|The fleur-de-lis adorned the king's robe. 鸢尾花装饰着国王的长袍。|Synonym: iris, lily (in a heraldic context)|Antonym: (No direct antonym, as it is a symbol)|Noun

fleur-de-lis:/ˌflɜːr dəˈliː/ /ˌflɝːr dəˈliː/| n.|百合花饰; 鸢尾花(图案)|A stylized representation of a lily or iris, used as a decorative motif or heraldic emblem, especially associated with French royalty.|The fleur-de-lis is prominently displayed on the Louisiana state flag.  百合花饰在路易斯安那州的州旗上醒目地展示着。|The king's crown was studded with fleur-de-lis. 国王的王冠上镶嵌着百合花饰。|Synonym:  iris, lily (in heraldic contexts)|Antonym: (No direct antonym exists)|Noun
