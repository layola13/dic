antioxidant:/ˌæntiˈɒksɪdənt/ /ˌæntiˈɑːksɪdənt/| n. adj.|抗氧化剂；抗氧化（的）|A substance that inhibits oxidation, especially one used to counteract the deterioration of stored food products.|例句：This cream contains antioxidants to protect your skin from sun damage.  这款面霜含有抗氧化剂，可以保护你的皮肤免受阳光伤害。

例句：Antioxidants like vitamin C can help boost your immune system. 像维生素C这样的抗氧化剂可以帮助增强你的免疫系统。|近义词：preservative, inhibitor|反义词：oxidant, pro-oxidant|词性:名词, 形容词
