delving:/ˈdelvɪŋ/| v. (present participle of delve)| 探究，钻研；深入挖掘|He's been delving into local history. 他一直在钻研当地历史。|She delved into her handbag for her keys. 她在手提包里翻找钥匙。|近义词：exploring, researching, investigating, probing|反义词: skimming, ignoring, neglecting|词性:动词(verb)

词源:
From Middle English *delven*, from Old English *delfan* (“to dig, delve”), from Proto-Germanic *delbaną* (“to dig”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰelbʰ- (“to dig, bury”). Cognate with Dutch *delven* (“to dig”), German *delben* (“to dig clay”), dialectal German *tilben* (“to dig potatoes”).
