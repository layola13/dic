bestializing:/ˈbɛstiəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
|v.|使兽性化；使野蛮化；使堕落|Making something or someone behave like a beast; degrading to an animalistic level.|His actions were utterly bestializing, devoid of any human compassion. 他的行为完全是兽性的，没有任何人类的同情心。|The propaganda was aimed at bestializing the enemy, portraying them as savage and subhuman.  这种宣传旨在使敌人兽性化，将他们描绘成野蛮和非人类的。|近义词：degrading, brutalizing, dehumanizing|反义词：humanizing, civilizing, elevating|词性:动词

bestialized:/ˈbɛstiəˌlaɪzd/
|adj.|兽性化的；野蛮化的；堕落的|Having been made bestial; reduced to an animalistic state.|The bestialized prisoners were treated worse than animals.  那些兽性化的囚犯受到的待遇比动物还差。|He felt bestialized by the war, stripped of his humanity.  他觉得自己被战争兽性化了，失去了人性。|近义词：degraded, brutalized, dehumanized|反义词：humanized, civilized, elevated|词性:形容词

词源: From "bestial" + "-ize" + "-ing".  "Bestial" comes from the Old French "bestial" and ultimately from the Latin "bestialis," meaning "of a beast."  "-ize" is a suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or become like," and "-ing" is the present participle suffix.
