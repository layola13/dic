exhausted:/ɪɡˈzɔːstɪd/ /ɪɡˈzɑːstɪd/| adj.|筋疲力尽的，疲惫不堪的|Drained of all energy; extremely tired. |例句：I was exhausted after the marathon. 我跑完马拉松后筋疲力尽。|例句：She felt exhausted after a long day at work.  她在工作了一整天后感到筋疲力尽。|近义词：tired, weary, fatigued, drained, spent|反义词：energetic, refreshed, invigorated|词性:形容词

exhaust:/ɪɡˈzɔːst/ /ɪɡˈzɑːst/| vt.|使筋疲力尽；耗尽|To drain of energy or resources; to use up completely.|例句：The long journey exhausted me.  漫长的旅程使我筋疲力尽。|例句：The hikers exhausted their water supply.  徒步旅行者耗尽了他们的水供应。|近义词：tire, weary, fatigue, drain, deplete|反义词：energize, refresh, replenish|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *exhaustus*, past participle of *exhaurire*  "to draw out, drain, empty," from *ex-* "out" + *haurire* "to draw, drain."
