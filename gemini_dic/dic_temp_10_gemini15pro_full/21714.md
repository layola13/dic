boardroom:/ˈbɔːdruːm/ /ˈbɔːrdruːm/
n.
会议室；董事会会议室
A room in which the meetings of a company's board of directors are held.
The important decision was made in the boardroom.  这个重要的决定是在会议室里做出的。

The boardroom was filled with tension as the directors discussed the merger.  董事们讨论合并事宜时，会议室里充满了紧张气氛。

近义词: meeting room, conference room
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑相对的概念，例如：office, cubicle)
词性: noun
