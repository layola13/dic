nonpliantly:/nɑːnˈplaɪəntli/
adverb|不顺从地，顽固地|in a way that is not willing to obey or agree|The child nonpliantly refused to eat his vegetables.  孩子顽固地拒绝吃蔬菜。|例句：He nonpliantly resisted their attempts to control him. 他顽固地抵制他们控制他的企图。|近义词：stubbornly, obstinately, rigidly, inflexibly|反义词：obediently, compliantly, submissively|词性:副词
词源: From non- + pliant + -ly.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not." Pliant means "bending readily; flexible." -ly is a suffix that forms adverbs.
