dreadlocks:/ˈdrɛdlɒks/ (UK) /ˈdrɛdlɑːks/ (US)| n.| 缠发绺，雷鬼头| A hairstyle in which the hair is twisted into tight ropes or coils.| She wore her hair in dreadlocks. 她把头发扎成了缠发绺。| He's growing dreadlocks. 他正在留缠发绺。| synonyms: dreads, locs | antonyms: straight hair, loose hair | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "dread" + "locks,"  where "dread" refers to the awe or fear inspired by the hairstyle in some cultures, and "locks" refers to the matted strands of hair.
