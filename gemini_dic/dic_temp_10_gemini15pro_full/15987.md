bama:/ˈbæmə/ | n. | 巴马人；亚拉巴马人 | A person from Bama, a county in Guangxi, China, or from Alabama, a state in the US.  A colloquial and sometimes derogatory term for rural, uneducated white Southerners in the US. |  例句：He's a Bama, born and raised. 他是巴马人，土生土长的。

例句：Some people use "Bama" as a derogatory term. 有些人用“Bama”作为贬义词。| 近义词：Southerner, Alabaman (for Alabama residents), villager (for Bama County residents) | 反义词: Northerner, Yankee (specifically for US context) | 词性: noun
