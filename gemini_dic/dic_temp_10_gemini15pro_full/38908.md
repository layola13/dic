confessers:/kənˈfɛsərz/
noun
忏悔者；坦白者；告解者
People who confess, admit, or acknowledge something, typically a wrongdoing or sin.
The confessers were granted absolution by the priest.  神父赦免了忏悔者们的罪。
The confessers felt a sense of relief after admitting their guilt.  坦白罪行后，忏悔者们感到如释重负。
Synonyms: admitters, acknowledgers, declarers
Antonyms: deniers, concealers, liars
