clew:/kluː/
n.
线索，提示；线团
a ball of thread, yarn, or cord. Also, something that guides or directs in the solution of a mystery or problem.
The detective followed the clew to find the killer.  侦探顺着线索找到了凶手。

近义词:clue, hint, lead, indication, thread
反义词:dead end, obstacle
