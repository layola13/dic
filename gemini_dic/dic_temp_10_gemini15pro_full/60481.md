ejectment:/ɪˈdʒɛktmənt/| n.|驱逐，逐出；收回（房产）|The act of ejecting someone or something, especially from property.|The landlord sought ejectment of the tenants for non-payment of rent. 房东因租户未付房租而寻求驱逐他们。|近义词：eviction, expulsion, ouster|反义词: admittance, admission|noun

ejectment:/ɪˈdʒɛktmənt/| n.|收回（房产）|The legal process of recovering possession of real property.|The ejectment proceedings took several months. 收回房产的法律程序花了几个月的时间。|近义词：repossession|反义词: - |noun
