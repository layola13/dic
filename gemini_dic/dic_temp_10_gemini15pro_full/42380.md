counterbids:/ˈkaʊntərˌbɪdz/ | n. |还价，反建议，竞争性出价|An offer made in response to another offer, typically in a negotiation or auction. |  The company received several counterbids after making their initial offer.  该公司在提出最初报价后收到了几份还盘。 | synonyms: counteroffers, competing bids, rival bids | antonyms: offers, bids, proposals | noun

counterbid:/ˈkaʊntərˌbɪd/ | v. |还价，反建议，竞争性出价|To make a counteroffer, typically in a negotiation or auction. | They counterbid aggressively to secure the contract.  他们积极地还价以确保拿到合同。 | synonyms: counteroffer, make a counteroffer, compete, outbid | antonyms: offer, bid, propose | verb
