inexcusableness:/ɪnɪksˈkjuːzəbəlnəs/| n. |不可原谅，不可宽恕|The inexcusableness of his behavior shocked everyone. |例句：The inexcusableness of his rudeness led to his dismissal. 他的粗鲁无礼不可原谅，导致他被解雇。

例句：The judge commented on the sheer inexcusableness of the crime. 法官评论了这起罪行的极端不可饶恕性。

|近义词：unforgivableness, unpardonableness |反义词: excusability, forgivableness|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + excusable + -ness (noun suffix).  "Excusable" comes from the Old French "excusable" and ultimately from the Latin "excusare" (to excuse).
