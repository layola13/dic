orah:/ˈɔːrə/| n.|祈祷，祷告|A prayer, especially one recited in a synagogue. |Example: He recited the orah with great devotion. 他虔诚地背诵了祈祷文。|Example: The morning orah is an important part of Jewish tradition.  晨祷是犹太传统的重要组成部分。|Synonym: prayer, supplication, invocation|Antonym: blasphemy, curse|noun
