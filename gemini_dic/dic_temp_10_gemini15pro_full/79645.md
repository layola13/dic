glossitis:/ɡlɒˈsaɪtɪs/ /ɡlɑːˈsaɪtɪs/
noun
舌炎
Inflammation of the tongue.
Example: The doctor diagnosed her with glossitis after examining her swollen and painful tongue. 医生检查了她肿胀疼痛的舌头后，诊断她患有舌炎。

Example:  Glossitis can make it difficult to eat and speak. 舌炎会使进食和说话变得困难。

Synonym:  tongue inflammation
Antonym: (No direct antonym for a medical condition)
词性: 名词
