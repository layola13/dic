disavaunce: /dɪsəˈvɑːns/
verb
损害，伤害；阻碍；使处于不利地位
To harm or injure; to hinder or disadvantage.
The scandal disavaunced his reputation. 这桩丑闻损害了他的名誉。
近义词: injure, harm, damage, impair, hinder, impede, obstruct
反义词: benefit, aid, assist, promote, advance

Etymology:
From dis- (expressing negation or reversal) + avaunce (obsolete form of advance, from Old French avancer).
