nonsecludedly:/ˌnɒnsɪˈkluːdɪdli/
adverb
非隐居地，公开地
in a nonsecluded manner; openly and publicly.  Not in a secluded or private way.
The meeting was held nonsecludedly in the town square.  会议在城镇广场公开举行。
The couple dined nonsecludedly at a busy restaurant. 这对夫妇在一家繁忙的餐馆公开用餐。
近义词: openly, publicly, plainly, visibly
反义词: privately, secludedly, secretly, covertly
词性: adverb

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + secluded + -ly (adverbial suffix)
"secluded" comes from the Latin word "secludere," meaning "to shut off, separate."
