double-edgedness:/ˌdʌbəlˈɛdʒdnəs/| n. |双刃性；两面性|The quality of having two contrasting aspects, each of which can be advantageous or disadvantageous.|The double-edgedness of social media is that it can connect people but also isolate them. 社交媒体的两面性在于它既可以连接人们，也可以孤立他们。|Synonyms: duality, ambivalence, two-sidedness|Antonyms: straightforwardness, simplicity|noun

double-edged:/ˌdʌbəlˈɛdʒd/| adj. |双刃的；两面性的|Having two contrasting aspects, each of which can be advantageous or disadvantageous.|A double-edged sword can protect you but also hurt you. 双刃剑可以保护你，也可以伤害你。|Synonyms: two-sided, ambivalent|Antonyms: straightforward, simple|adjective
