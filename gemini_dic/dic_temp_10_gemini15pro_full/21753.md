boathouse:/ˈboʊthaʊs/| n. |船库，船屋|A building by the water for storing boats. |例句：We rented a boathouse on the lake for the summer. 我们夏天在湖边租了一间船屋。

例句：The boathouse was damaged in the storm. 船库在暴风雨中受损了。

|近义词：dock, pier, wharf|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From boat + house.  The word "boat" comes from Old English "bāt," and "house" comes from Old English "hūs."
