alchemically:/ˌælˈkɛmɪkli/
adv.
炼金术地; 神秘地
in a way that relates to or resembles alchemy.
The substance was alchemically transformed into gold.  这种物质被炼金术般地变成了金子。
Synonyms: magically, mysteriously, supernaturally
Antonyms: naturally, scientifically
词性: 副词
