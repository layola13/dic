countersigning:/ˌkaʊntərˈsaɪnɪŋ/
n.|副署，连署|The contract requires countersigning by both parties.  该合同需要双方签字.|近义词：co-signing, endorsing|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
countersign:/ˈkaʊntərˌsaɪn/
v.|副署，连署|The ambassador countersigned the treaty. 大使在条约上副署。|近义词：co-sign, endorse|反义词: N/A|词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- + signing, from sign.  Counter- means "against, opposite," but in this context, it signifies a reciprocal action, a second signature in addition to the first.
