counterthreats:/ˈkaʊntərθrɛts/
noun
反威胁，对抗威胁
Actions or measures taken to oppose or neutralize threats.
The military developed new counterthreats to enemy missiles.  军队发展了新的反制敌方导弹的措施。
The government implemented counterthreats to protect against cyberattacks. 政府实施了应对网络攻击的防御措施。
synonyms: countermeasures, counteractions
antonyms: threats, vulnerabilities
n.
