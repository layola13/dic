caval:/kəˈvæl/| adj.|骑士的，骑兵的；马的|Of or relating to horses or cavalry; horsemanlike.|The king reviewed his caval troops. 国王检阅了他的骑兵部队。|近义词：equestrian, mounted|反义词：pedestrian|词性:形容词
Etymology: From French *caval*, from Italian *cavall*, from Latin *caballus* "horse."
