lavishest:/ˈlævɪʃɪst/| adj.|最奢华的，最慷慨的|superlative form of lavish:  bestowed, expended, or occurring in profusion |例句：They gave her the lavishest praise. 他们给予她最高的赞扬。|例句：He threw the lavishest party I've ever seen. 他举办了我见过的最奢华的派对。|近义词：most extravagant, most luxurious, most profuse, most generous|反义词：stingiest, meagerest, most frugal|词性:形容词
词源: From Old French *lavasse (“deluge of rain”), from laver (“to wash”).
