dwell:/dwɛl/ | vi. | 居住，停留；细想，详述 | to live in a place or to stay there for a long time; to think, speak, or write about something for a long time |  They dwelt in the forest for many years. 他们在森林里住了很多年。

He dwelt on the subject for a long time. 他在这个话题上详述了很久。 | 近义词：reside, live, inhabit, linger, ponder | 反义词：depart, leave, move | 词性：动词
