nonbeauty:/nɒnˈbjuːti/ | n. | 缺乏美感的事物；不美的東西 |  Something that lacks beauty; something not considered beautiful. | The landfill was a landscape of nonbeauty.  垃圾填埋场是一片毫无美感的景象。|  The abandoned factory was a monument to nonbeauty. 这座废弃的工厂是不美的象征。| 近义词: ugliness, unattractiveness | 反义词: beauty, loveliness | 词性: 名词

词源:  "Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "beauty"
