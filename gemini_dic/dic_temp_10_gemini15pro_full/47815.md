decarburization:/diːˌkɑːrbjʊrɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |脱碳，脱碳作用|The process of removing carbon from a substance, especially from steel or iron.|The decarburization of the steel resulted in a softer material. 钢的脱碳导致材料变软。|近义词： decarbonization|反义词: carburization|名词


decarburize:/diːˈkɑːrbjʊraɪz/| v. |脱碳|To remove carbon from (something).|The blacksmith decarburized the iron to make it more malleable. 铁匠将铁脱碳以使其更具延展性。|近义词： decarbonize|反义词: carburize|动词
