ill-timed:/ˌɪlˈtaɪmd/ | adj. |不合时宜的；时机不当的 | Happening or done at a bad or inappropriate time. |  Her ill-timed joke offended everyone in the room. 她的不合时宜的笑话冒犯了房间里的每个人。

The announcement was ill-timed, coming just as the company was trying to raise capital.  这个公告的时机很不恰当，正好赶上公司试图筹集资金的时候。| 近义词: inappropriate, inopportune, unfortunate, awkward, untimely | 反义词: timely, opportune, appropriate, well-timed, fortunate | 词性: 形容词
