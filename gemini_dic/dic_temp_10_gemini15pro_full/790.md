absorbents:/əbˈzɔːrbənts/ /əbˈsɔːrbənts/| n.|吸收剂，吸收性物质|Substances that can absorb liquids or gases.  Materials that soak up something else.|The spill was quickly cleaned up with absorbents.|近义词：absorbers, sponges,  porous materials|反义词:impermeable materials|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *absorbent*, from Latin *absorbēns*, present participle of *absorbēre* (“to suck in, swallow down, devour”), from *ab* (“from, away from”) + *sorbēre* (“to suck in, swallow, absorb”).

USAGE->
These absorbents are highly effective in cleaning up oil spills. = 这些吸收剂在清理漏油方面非常有效。

The company manufactures a range of absorbents for industrial use. = 这家公司生产一系列用于工业的吸收剂。
