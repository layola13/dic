decorrelated:/ˌdiːˈkɒrəleɪtɪd/ | adj. |去相关的，不相关的 |  Having removed correlation; statistically independent.  The process of decorrelation reduces or eliminates the statistical relationship between different variables or signals. | The researchers decorrelated the data to eliminate spurious correlations. 研究人员去除了数据的相关性以消除虚假关联。 | The image processing algorithm decorrelated the color channels. 图像处理算法去除了颜色通道之间的相关性。 | independent, uncorrelated, orthogonal | correlated, related | adjective
