capsaicin:/kæpˈseɪɪsɪn/ | n. |辣椒素|A colorless, crystalline, pungent compound, C18H27NO3, found in various capsicums, especially in hot peppers.  It is the active component that makes chili peppers hot.|Capsaicin is used in pepper spray for self-defense. 辣椒素被用于自卫的胡椒喷雾中。

Capsaicin creams can be used to relieve muscle pain. 辣椒素药膏可以用来缓解肌肉疼痛。|近义词: chili pepper extract, hot pepper extract|反义词: None|词性:noun
