dissite: /dɪˈsaɪt/
n.
disseminated site, 分散的地点; 分布式站点
A dissite is a location, often geographically separate from a main office or headquarters, where a portion of a company's operations or personnel are located.  分散的地点是指一个地点，通常在地理上与主要办公室或总部分开，公司的一部分运营或人员位于该地点。
The company has several dissites across the country to better serve its customers.  该公司在全国各地设有多个分散的地点，以便更好地为客户服务。
Synonym: branch office, satellite office, remote location, outpost
Antonym: headquarters, main office
word type: noun
