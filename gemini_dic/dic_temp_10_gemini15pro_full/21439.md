blowhole:/ˈbloʊhoʊl/ (US) /ˈbləʊhəʊl/ (UK)| n.|呼吸孔；喷水孔；通风孔|A blowhole is an opening at the top of the head of a whale, dolphin, or other cetacean, through which the animal breathes air. It can also refer to a hole in the ice or rock through which air or water is forced out.|The whale surfaced and spouted a plume of water from its blowhole.  鲸鱼浮出水面，从它的呼吸孔喷出一股水柱。

The blowhole on the rocky coast emitted a loud whooshing sound.  岩石海岸上的通风孔发出巨大的呼啸声。|近义词：vent, nostril (for whales/dolphins), opening|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
