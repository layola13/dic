nux:/nʌks/| n.|坚果；毒药；要点；核心|a hard-shelled dry fruit or seed with a separable rind or shell; a poisonous substance, especially a plant extract; the central or most important part of something; the gist|The nux of the problem is a lack of communication.  这个问题的核心是缺乏沟通。|Synonyms: kernel, core, gist, essence, heart, pith, nucleus, nub, crux|Antonyms: periphery, exterior, shell|noun
