functional:/ˈfʌŋkʃənəl/| adj. n.|功能的，实用的；功能性障碍；函数|A functional object is designed to be practical and useful rather than attractive.  一个功能性物体被设计成实用而不是美观。|例句：The design is functional but not very aesthetically pleasing.  设计很实用，但不太美观。|近义词：practical, useful, operative, working|反义词:decorative, ornamental, dysfunctional|词性:形容词

functional:/ˈfʌŋkʃənəlz/| n.|泛函|In mathematics, a functional is a function whose domain is a set of functions. 在数学中，泛函是一个定义域为函数集的函数。|例句：The energy functional is used to find the minimum energy state of a system. 能量泛函用于寻找系统的最小能量状态。|近义词：(Often no direct synonym, depends on context)|反义词:(No antonym)|词性:名词
