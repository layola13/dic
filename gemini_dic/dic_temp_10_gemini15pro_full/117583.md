megalomaniacally:/ˌmɛɡələʊˈmeɪniəkli/ | adv. | 夸大地；狂妄自大地 | In a way that shows megalomania (an obsession with power and having delusions of grandeur). | He megalomaniacally declared himself emperor of the world. 他狂妄地宣布自己是世界皇帝。 |  例句：She megalomaniacally boasted about her non-existent achievements. 她夸大地吹嘘她根本不存在的成就。

例句：He spoke megalomaniacally of his plans to conquer the business world.  他狂妄地讲述了他征服商界的计划。

近义词：grandiosely, pretentiously, pompously, arrogantly | 反义词：humbly, modestly | 词性：副词
