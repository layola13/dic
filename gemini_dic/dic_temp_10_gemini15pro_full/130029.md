non-oxidizing:/ˌnɒnˈɒksɪdaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 非氧化性的 | Not causing or undergoing oxidation; inert. |  Nitrogen is a non-oxidizing gas. 氮气是一种非氧化性气体。|  This special paint is non-oxidizing and will maintain its color for years.  这种特殊的油漆是非氧化性的，可以保持颜色多年。| inert, non-reactive | oxidizing, corrosive | adjective
Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + oxidizing (from oxidize, ultimately from French oxyder and Greek oxus "sharp, acid")
