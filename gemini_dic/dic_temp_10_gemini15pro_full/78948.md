girdler:/ˈɡɜːrdlər/ | n. | 束带者；环绕者；腰带制造者 | A person or device that girdles something, often a tree.  Someone who makes or sells girdles. |  The girdler tightened the belt around the tree, eventually killing it.  束带者勒紧了树干上的带子，最终导致树木死亡。 |  binder, faster, tier | loosener, untier | noun

gird:/ɡɜːrd/ | vt. | 束缚；环绕；做好准备 | To encircle or bind with a belt or band; to prepare oneself for action. |  He girded himself for the upcoming challenge. 他为即将到来的挑战做好了准备。  A wall girds the ancient city. 一堵墙环绕着这座古城。 | surround, encircle, encompass, prepare, brace |  neglect,  ignore | verb
