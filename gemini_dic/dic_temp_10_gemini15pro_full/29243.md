casserole:/ˈkæsəˌroʊl/ (US) /ˈkæsəˌrɒl/ (UK)| n.|砂锅菜；炖锅；烤锅|A dish of meat and vegetables cooked slowly in liquid in a covered dish, often in an oven.  A deep covered dish used for cooking such meals.|She brought a tuna casserole to the potluck. 她带了一份金枪鱼砂锅菜来参加聚餐。|近义词：stew, hotdish|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "salad" as a contrasting dish)|noun
