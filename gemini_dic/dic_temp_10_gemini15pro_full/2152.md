add-in:/ˈædɪn/| n.|插件，附加组件|A software program or hardware component that adds a specific feature or capability to a larger system.|This add-in provides extra features to your word processor. 这个插件为你的文字处理器提供了额外的功能。|The finance department uses an add-in for their spreadsheet software to generate complex reports. 财务部门使用电子表格软件的插件来生成复杂的报告。|近义词：plug-in, extension, supplement|反义词: built-in, integrated|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From add + in, meaning something that is added in.

USAGE->
As a noun:

The add-in allows users to customize their experience.  这个插件允许用户自定义他们的体验。

I downloaded a useful add-in for my email client. 我为我的电子邮件客户端下载了一个有用的插件。

PHRASE->
browser add-in = 浏览器插件
Excel add-in = Excel插件
Word add-in = Word插件
add-in manager = 插件管理器
useful add-in = 有用的插件
