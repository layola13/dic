furnishing:/ˈfɜːrnɪʃɪŋ/ (US), /ˈfɜːnɪʃɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |家具，陈设；提供家具；室内陈设| the act of providing furniture or other necessary things for a room or building; furniture and other things in a room or building |  The apartment came with all the necessary furnishings. 这套公寓配备了所有必要的家具。| synonyms: furniture, fittings, décor, equipment | antonyms: bareness, emptiness | noun

furnishing:/ˈfɜːrnɪʃɪŋ/ (US), /ˈfɜːnɪʃɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. |家具的，室内陈设的| relating to furniture or the act of providing furniture | They offer a full range of furnishing fabrics. 他们提供全系列的家具面料。| synonyms: decorative, interior, household | antonyms: N/A | adjective

Etymology: From Middle English *furnishinge, from Anglo-Norman furnissement, from Old French furnissement, from furnir (“to furnish”).
