broker:/ˈbroʊkər/ (US) /ˈbrəʊkə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|经纪人，代理人；掮客；促成，安排|A person who buys and sells goods or assets for others.|例句：The real estate broker showed us several houses. 房地产经纪人带我们看了几栋房子。|近义词：agent, intermediary, negotiator|反义词: buyer, seller|noun
broker:/ˈbroʊkər/ (US) /ˈbrəʊkə(r)/ (UK)| v.|促成，安排；作为中间人|To arrange or negotiate something on behalf of others.|例句：The government brokered a peace deal between the two warring factions. 政府促成了两个交战派别之间的和平协议。|近义词：arrange, negotiate, mediate|反义词: obstruct, hinder|verb
