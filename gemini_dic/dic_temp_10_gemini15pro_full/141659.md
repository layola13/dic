optimum:/ˈɒptɪməm/ (UK), /ˈɑːptɪməm/ (US)| adj. n.|最佳的，最优的；最佳条件，最适宜条件|Most favorable or desirable; best.|The optimum temperature for growing these plants is 20°C.  这些植物生长的最佳温度是20摄氏度。|Synonyms: best, ideal, perfect, optimal|Antonyms: worst, poorest, least favorable|adjective

optimum:/ˈɒptɪməm/ (UK), /ˈɑːptɪməm/ (US)| n.|最佳条件，最适宜条件|The most favorable condition or level for growth, reproduction, or success.|We need to find the optimum balance between cost and effectiveness. 我们需要找到成本和效益之间的最佳平衡点。|Synonyms: ideal, peak, prime|Antonyms: nadir, worst|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *optimus* ("best"), superlative of *bonus* ("good").
