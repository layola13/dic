nectarlike:/ˈnɛktərˌlaɪk/| adj.|像花蜜的；甘美的|Resembling nectar in sweetness, deliciousness, or fragrance.  It describes something that is sweet, fragrant, and appealing, like the nectar of a flower.|The wine had a nectarlike flavor. 这酒有花蜜般的味道。|近义词：sweet, delicious, honeyed, ambrosial|反义词：bitter, sour, acrid|词性:形容词
Etymology: nectar + -like.  "Nectar" comes from the Greek "νέκταρ (néktar)," the drink of the gods, believed to confer immortality.  "-like" is a suffix forming adjectives and meaning "resembling, characteristic of."
