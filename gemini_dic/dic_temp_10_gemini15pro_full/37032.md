comanaged:/ˌkoʊˈmænɪdʒd/
| adj. | 共同管理的 | Managed jointly by two or more entities. | The project was comanaged by two departments.  这个项目由两个部门共同管理。| Near Synonyms: jointly managed, collaboratively managed | Antonyms: solely managed, independently managed | adjective

comanage:/ˌkoʊˈmænɪdʒ/
| v. | 共同管理 | To manage something jointly with one or more other entities. |  We decided to comanage the family business. 我们决定共同管理家族企业。| Near Synonyms: co-administer, share management | Antonyms: manage alone, manage independently | verb
