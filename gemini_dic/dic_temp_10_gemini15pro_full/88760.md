hocus-pocus:/ˌhoʊkəs ˈpoʊkəs/| n. |戏法；胡言乱语；花招；把戏| meaningless words used by magicians; sleight of hand; trickery |The magician amazed the children with his hocus-pocus. 魔术师用他的戏法使孩子们惊叹。|近义词：trickery, magic, sleight of hand, deception, mumbo jumbo|反义词:reality, truth, honesty|词性:noun

hocus-pocus:/ˌhoʊkəs ˈpoʊkəs/| interj. |胡言乱语；咒语|used to express disbelief or disapproval of something that seems to be nonsense or trickery| "Hocus-pocus!" she muttered as the salesman tried to explain the incredible deal. “胡说八道！”当销售员试图解释这笔不可思议的交易时，她低声说道。|近义词：nonsense, balderdash, rubbish|反义词:sense, truth|词性:interjection
