non-manipulative:|/ˌnɒn məˈnɪpjʊlətɪv/ (US) /ˌnɒn məˈnɪpjʊlətɪv/ (UK)|adj.|非操控性的；非操纵性的|Formed by combining "non-" (meaning "not") with "manipulative" (meaning "influencing or controlling someone or something in a clever or unscrupulous way").  The root of "manipulative" comes from the Latin "manus" meaning "hand" and implies skillful handling, which can be used for either positive or negative purposes. In this case, the "non-" prefix negates the negative connotation.|例句：The therapist employed non-manipulative techniques to help the patient discover her own solutions.  这位治疗师采用了非操控性的技术来帮助病人发现她自己的解决方案。|例句：I find his non-manipulative parenting style refreshing. 我觉得他非操控性的育儿方式令人耳目一新。|近义词:honest, straightforward, genuine, authentic|反义词:manipulative, controlling, coercive, exploitative|词性:adjective
