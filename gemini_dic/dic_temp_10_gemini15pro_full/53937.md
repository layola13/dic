discourtship: /dɪsˈkɔːrtʃɪp/ /dɪsˈkɔːrʃɪp/
n.
求爱；追求；恋爱期；(正式)求婚
The period of time during which a couple develop a romantic relationship before getting married.
例句：Their discourtship was brief, and they were married within a year. 他们的恋爱期很短，一年内就结婚了。

例句：The novel describes the hero's long and arduous discourtship of his beloved. 这部小说描述了男主人公漫长而艰难的追求他心爱的人的过程。

近义词：courtship, wooing, romance, pursuit
反义词：estrangement, separation

词性：名词
