nonconcentrated:/nɒnkɒnsəntreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 非浓缩的，未浓缩的 | Not concentrated; dilute or dispersed.  Referring to a substance that has not been made more potent or denser by reducing its volume or increasing the proportion of its active ingredient. | The juice was nonconcentrated, so it tasted watery.  这种果汁是非浓缩的，所以尝起来像水一样。| The chemist used a nonconcentrated solution for the experiment.  化学家在实验中使用了一种非浓缩溶液。 | 近义词: dilute, weak, thin | 反义词: concentrated, strong, thick | 词性: 形容词
