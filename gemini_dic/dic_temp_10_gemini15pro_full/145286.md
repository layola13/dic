overbrace:/ˈoʊvərbreɪs/| vt. n.|用括号标注，括号|To place a brace or a pair of braces over (something, such as a mathematical expression or a group of words).  Also, something that is overbraced; a brace placed over something.|The equation x + y = z was overbraced to emphasize its importance.  公式 x + y = z 被加上括号以强调其重要性。|近义词:bracket, enclose|反义词: (no direct antonym)|词性: verb, noun
