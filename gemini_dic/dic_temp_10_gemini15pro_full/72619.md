flycatcher:/ˈflaɪˌkætʃər/| n. |捕蝇鸟|A bird that catches flies and other insects in flight.|例句：The flycatcher darted out from its perch to snatch an insect.  捕蝇鸟从栖木上飞扑出去捕捉一只昆虫。

例句：Several different species of flycatchers live in this forest.  这片森林里生活着好几种不同的捕蝇鸟。|近义词：insectivore|反义词:(None -  no direct opposite for an animal that eats insects)|词性:noun
