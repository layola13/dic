insatiable:/ɪnˈseɪʃəbl/ /ɪnˈseɪʃiəbl/| adj.|贪得无厌的，不知足的|unable to be satisfied|His insatiable appetite for power led to his downfall. 他对权力永不知足的欲望导致了他的垮台。|近义词：greedy, voracious, ravenous|反义词：satisfied, content|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin insatiabilis, from in- "not" + satiabilis "able to be satisfied," from satiare "to satisfy."
