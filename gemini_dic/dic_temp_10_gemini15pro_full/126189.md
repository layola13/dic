muzzled: /ˈmʌzəld/ | v. | 使…缄默；使…噤声；给…戴口套 | to prevent (a person or animal) from speaking or making noises, especially by covering the mouth | The dog was muzzled to prevent it from biting.  这只狗被戴上了口套以防止它咬人。 | synonyms: silenced, gagged, suppressed | antonyms: unleashed, unconstrained | verb

muzzled: /ˈmʌzəld/ | adj. |  被戴上口套的；被禁言的 | having a muzzle on or as if having a muzzle on; silenced | The muzzled dog whined softly.  被戴上口套的狗轻轻地呜咽着。| synonyms: silenced, gagged, suppressed | antonyms: outspoken, vocal | adjective
