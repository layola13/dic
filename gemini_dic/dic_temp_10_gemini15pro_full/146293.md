overfattened:/ˌōvərˈfætn̩d/ | adj. | 过肥的，过度育肥的 | Excessively fattened; fattened beyond what is normal or desirable.  This adjective describes something, usually an animal, that has been fed too much and has become abnormally fat. | The farmer sold his overfattened pigs at the market.  农民在市场上卖掉了他的肥猪。| The overfattened goose was difficult to cook properly. 这只过肥的鹅很难煮好。 | 近义词: obese, overweight, plump, excessively fat | 反义词: lean, thin, underweight | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "fattened" (past participle of "fatten," meaning "to make fat or fatter")
