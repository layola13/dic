overclevernesses: /ˌoʊvərˈklɛvərnəsɪz/
noun
过分聪明;自作聪明
Excessive cleverness, often resulting in a contrived or artificial effect.
His prose style is marred by overclevernesses. 他的散文风格被过度雕琢而显得不自然。
近义词:  contrivance, artificiality, affectation, preciosity
反义词:  naturalness, simplicity, artlessness
词性: noun
