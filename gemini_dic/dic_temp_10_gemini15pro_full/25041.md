bryozoan:/ˌbraɪəˈzoʊən/ | n. |苔藓虫；苔藓动物|A small invertebrate animal that lives in colonies in the sea or in fresh water, forming moss-like growths. |Bryozoans are often mistaken for corals. 苔藓虫常被误认为是珊瑚。|  The bryozoan colony was attached to the underside of the rock.  苔藓虫群体附着在岩石的底部。 |Synonyms: moss animal, polyzoa|Antonyms: N/A| Noun

bryozoum:/ˌbraɪəˈzoʊəm/ | n. |苔藓虫群体|A colony of bryozoans. |The bryozoum formed a delicate, lace-like structure. 这个苔藓虫群体形成了一个精致的蕾丝状结构。| The diver found a large bryozoum growing on the shipwreck. 潜水员发现沉船上生长着一个巨大的苔藓虫群体。 |Synonyms: bryozoan colony, polyzoarium|Antonyms: N/A| Noun
