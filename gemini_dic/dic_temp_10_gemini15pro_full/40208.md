contester's:/kənˈtɛstərz/
noun
参赛者的，抗辩者的
belonging to or associated with a contester; possessive form of contester.  A contester is someone who challenges or disputes something.
The contester's argument was well-researched.  参赛者的论点经过了充分的研究。
synonyms: challenger's, disputant's, opponent's
antonyms: supporter's, proponent's
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From contest + -er + -'s.  Contest ultimately derives from the Latin "contestari," meaning "to call to witness."
