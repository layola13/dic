adjoins:/əˈdʒɔɪnz/| v. |毗邻，邻接|be next to and joined with (something).|例句：My house adjoins the park. 我的房子毗邻公园。

Our property adjoins theirs. 我们的房产与他们的相邻。|近义词：abut, border, neighbor,  join, connect, attach|反义词:separate, detach, disconnect|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *ajoindre, from Latin adjungere (“to join to”), from ad- (“to”) + jungere (“to join”).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
Their land adjoins ours. = 他们的土地与我们的土地相邻。
The kitchen adjoins the dining room. = 厨房与餐厅相邻。

一般过去时:
The two kingdoms adjoined each other for centuries. = 这两个王国几个世纪以来彼此相邻。
His office adjoined hers, so they often talked. = 他的办公室和她毗邻，所以他们经常交谈。

现在完成时:
The two properties have adjoined each other for many years. = 这两处房产多年来一直毗邻。
The new wing has adjoined the original building seamlessly. = 新翼楼与原建筑无缝连接。

过去完成时:
The two countries had adjoined each other before the war. = 这两个国家在战前彼此相邻。
The old house had adjoined a beautiful garden. = 老房子毗邻一个美丽的花园。

将来时:
The new extension will adjoin the existing building. = 新扩建部分将与现有建筑物相连。
Our new office will adjoin the conference center. = 我们的新办公室将与会议中心相邻。

被动语态 (较少使用, 但语法上可行):
The living room is adjoined by the kitchen. = 客厅与厨房相连。
The master bedroom is adjoined by a private balcony. = 主卧室连接着一个私人阳台。


PHRASE->
land that adjoins the river = 与河流相邻的土地
a building adjoins another = 一栋建筑物与另一栋建筑物相邻
rooms that adjoin each other = 彼此相邻的房间
the property adjoins a park = 该房产毗邻公园
a house adjoining the forest = 与森林相邻的房子
