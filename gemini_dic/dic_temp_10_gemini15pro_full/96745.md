inelligibly:/ɪnˈɛlɪdʒɪbli/
adjective
难以辨认地；模糊不清地
in a way that cannot be read or understood clearly.
Her handwriting was so messy it was almost inelligibly. 她的字迹太潦草了，几乎难以辨认。
Synonyms: unclearly, illegibly, unintelligibly
Antonyms: clearly, legibly, intelligibly
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From in- (not) + legible (readable) + -ly (adverbial suffix). Ultimately from Latin legibilis (readable).
