expenseful:/ɪkˈspɛnsfʊl/
adjective
昂贵的，花钱多的
incurring great expense; costly
例句：Running a car can be very expenseful.  开车的费用可能很高。

例句：Her expenseful tastes put a strain on the family budget. 她奢侈的品味给家庭预算带来了压力。


近义词：expensive, costly, dear, exorbitant, extravagant
反义词：cheap, inexpensive, economical, thrifty, frugal
词性: adjective
