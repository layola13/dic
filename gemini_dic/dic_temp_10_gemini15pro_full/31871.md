cheerleader:/ˈtʃɪrˌlidər/| n. |啦啦队长|A person, usually a girl or woman, who leads cheers for a sports team, especially at a school or college.  A cheerleader encourages the crowd to cheer for their team by performing organized chants, dances, and acrobatic stunts.|The cheerleaders were enthusiastic and energetic, motivating the entire crowd.|Synonyms: rooter, supporter, fan|Antonyms: critic, opponent|Noun
