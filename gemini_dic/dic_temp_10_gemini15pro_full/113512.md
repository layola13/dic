ma's:/mɑːz/| n. |妈妈的（东西）; 妈妈家| Mama's is a contraction of "mama's," which is the possessive form of "mama." It means something belongs to or is associated with one's mother.| I'm going over to ma's house for dinner. 我要去妈妈家吃晚饭。| Ma's apple pie is the best. 妈妈的苹果派是最好吃的。| 近义词: mom's, mother's | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could be something like "dad's" in specific contexts) | 词性: 名词 (Possessive noun)
