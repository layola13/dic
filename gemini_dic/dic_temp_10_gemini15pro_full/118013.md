memorize:/ˈmɛməˌraɪz/ (US) /ˈmɛməraɪz/ (UK)| v. |记住，背诵|To commit something to memory; to learn by heart.|I memorized the poem for the recital. 我为了朗诵会背诵了这首诗。

She memorized all the chemical formulas. 她记住了所有的化学公式。


|近义词：learn, remember, commit to memory, internalize|反义词: forget|词性:动词
