divulges:/daɪˈvʌldʒɪz/| v.|泄露，透露|To make known (private or sensitive information).|She divulged her secret to her best friend. 她向她最好的朋友透露了她的秘密。|The company divulged its plans for expansion. 该公司透露了其扩张计划。

|Synonyms: reveal, disclose, unveil, impart, leak, betray|Antonyms: conceal, hide, suppress, withhold|verb
