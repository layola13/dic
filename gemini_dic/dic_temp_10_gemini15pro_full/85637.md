haunchy:/ˈhɔːntʃi/ | adj. | 矮胖的，粗壮的 |  Broad and thickset, especially in the hips and thighs.  Having large haunches. |  The haunchy bulldog waddled down the street.  那只矮胖的斗牛犬摇摇摆摆地走在街上。 | The haunchy cat struggled to jump onto the counter. 那只粗壮的猫费力地跳上了柜台。 |  stocky, stout, thickset, chunky | slender, slim, thin | adjective
