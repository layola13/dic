howto:/ˈhaʊtuː/ | n. | 方法，指南 | A set of instructions or directions on how to do something. |  Example: I need a howto on fixing a leaky faucet. 我需要一个修理漏水水龙头的指南。| Synonym: guide, instructions, manual, tutorial | Antonym: N/A | noun

howto:/ˈhaʊtuː/ | adj. (informal) | 指导性的 | Relating to or providing instructions on how to do something. | Example: This is a howto video on baking a cake. 这是一个关于烘焙蛋糕的指导性视频。| Synonym: instructional, educational, informative | Antonym: N/A | adjective
