enneandrous:/ɛnˈiːændrəs/
| adj. |
| 九雄蕊的；具九枚雄蕊的 |
| Having nine stamens.  A flower is enneandrous when it possesses nine stamens. |
| The flower of the rhubarb plant is enneandrous.  大黄的花有九个雄蕊。 |

| Near synonyms: nine-stamened |
| Antonyms: N/A (Specific antonyms would depend on the number of stamens, e.g., monandrous, diandrous, etc.) |
| Word Class: Adjective |


Etymology:
From Greek *ἐννέα* (ennéa, “nine”) + *-ανδρος* (-andros, “male,” “stamen”).
