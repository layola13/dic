archegonium:/ˌɑːrkɪˈɡoʊniəm/ | n. | 颈卵器 |The female reproductive organ in mosses, ferns, and most gymnosperms.  It is a flask-shaped structure that produces a single egg. | The archegonium of the fern is located on the prothallus.  蕨类的颈卵器位于原叶体上。| The archegonium protects the developing egg. 颈卵器保护发育中的卵子。 | synonym: female sex organ (in bryophytes, pteridophytes and gymnosperms) | antonym: antheridium | noun
