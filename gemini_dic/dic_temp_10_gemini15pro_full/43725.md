creed:/kriːd/| n.|信条，教义，原则|A formal statement of religious belief or a set of principles or beliefs.|例句：The Apostles' Creed is a fundamental statement of Christian belief. 使徒信经是基督教信仰的基本陈述。

例句：His creed is to always be honest. 他的原则是永远诚实。|近义词：belief, dogma, doctrine, tenet|反义词：disbelief, doubt, skepticism|词性:noun
