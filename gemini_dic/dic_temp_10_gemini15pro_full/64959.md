estheticisms:/esˈθetɪsɪzəmz/ | n. |唯美主义；审美观|  A devotion to beauty and to the fine arts; the attitudes and beliefs of a person who values beauty and art above all else. |  His apartment was filled with art and books, reflecting his estheticisms. 他的公寓里摆满了艺术品和书籍，反映了他的审美观。| Synonyms: artistic sensibilities, refinements, tastes,  | Antonyms: philistinism, vulgarity | noun

estheticisms:/esˈθetɪsɪzəmz/ | n. | 故作风雅；矫揉造作 | Affectedly artistic or refined; artificial or superficial beauty. | Her estheticisms were evident in her elaborate clothing and exaggerated manners. 她的矫揉造作体现在她精心制作的服装和夸张的举止上。| Synonyms: affectations, pretensions, artificiality | Antonyms: naturalness, sincerity, genuineness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From aestheticism, from aesthetic + -ism.  "Aesthetic" comes from Greek *aisthētikos*, meaning "perceptive, sensitive," from *aisthēta*, "perceptible things," from *aisthanomai*, "I perceive."
