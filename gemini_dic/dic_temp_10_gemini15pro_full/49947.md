dendrolite:/ˈdɛndrəˌlaɪt/| n. |树枝石，枝状岩石|A mineral or rock resembling a plant or tree in appearance, often branching or moss-like.|The museum displayed a fascinating collection of dendrolites, including one shaped like a miniature oak tree.|近义词：pseudofossil, mineral deposit|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Greek *déndron* (tree) + *líthos* (stone).
