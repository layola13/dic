afraidness:/əˈfreɪdnəs/| n. |害怕，恐惧|The state of being afraid; fearfulness.|He confessed his afraidness of the dark. 他坦白了他对黑暗的恐惧。|近义词：fear, terror, fright, dread, anxiety, apprehension|反义词：bravery, courage, fearlessness, boldness|词性:名词

Etymology: From Middle English *afraidnes*, derived from afraid + -ness.  Afraid itself comes from Old English *afæred*, past participle of *afæran* "to frighten," from *a-* (intensive prefix) + *færan* "to frighten."
