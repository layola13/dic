n.|草地茂盛，草木繁盛|The grassiness of the field was inviting. 田野里草木繁盛，十分诱人。|English Explanation: The state or quality of being grassy; covered with grass.|例句：The sheep grazed on the lush grassiness. 羊群在茂盛的草地上吃草。|近义词：verdancy, lushness, greenery|反义词：barrenness, aridity|词性：名词

词源:
From grassy + -ness.  "grassy" comes from Old English "græs" (grass) + "-ig" (an adjective suffix meaning "having the quality of"). "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns meaning "the state or quality of being".
