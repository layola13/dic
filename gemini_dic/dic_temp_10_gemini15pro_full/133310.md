nonexploitation:/ˌnɒnˌɛksplɔɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 不剥削，非剥削 | The state of not being exploited, especially in the context of labor or resources.  The absence of the practice of taking unfair advantage of someone or something. |  The company prides itself on its policy of nonexploitation of its workers.  这家公司以其不剥削工人的政策而自豪。|  Nonexploitation of natural resources is crucial for sustainable development.  不剥削自然资源对于可持续发展至关重要。 | 近义词: fair treatment, ethical practice | 反义词: exploitation, abuse | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "exploitation" (from "exploit," from Old French "exploitier" - to utilize, accomplish, from Latin "explicitare" - to unfold, explain).
