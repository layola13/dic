manifesting:/ˈmænɪfɛstɪŋ/| v. |展现，显现；表明，证明；（思想）显化|The illness first manifested itself in severe stomach pains. 这种疾病最初表现为严重的胃痛。|近义词：displaying, showing, exhibiting, demonstrating, revealing, expressing|反义词：concealing, hiding|词性：动词

manifesting:/ˈmænɪfɛstɪŋ/| n. |显现，表明；宣言|The manifesting of his talent was a surprise to everyone. 他的才华的显现让每个人都感到惊讶。|近义词：display, show, exhibition, demonstration, revelation, expression|反义词：concealment|词性：名词

manifesting:/ˈmænɪfɛstɪŋ/| adj. |明显的，显而易见的|His manifesting anxiety made everyone uncomfortable.  他明显的焦虑让每个人都感到不舒服。|近义词：obvious, apparent, evident, clear|反义词：hidden, concealed|词性：形容词


词源: From Latin manifestus "caught in the act, palpable, evident," from manus "hand" + -festus (akin to infestus "hostile").  The original notion is of something "struck by the hand," hence "palpable."
