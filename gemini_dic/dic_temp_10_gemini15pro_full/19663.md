bilateralism:/ˌbaɪˈlætərəlɪzəm/ | n. | 双边主义 | The conduct of political, economic, or cultural relations between two sovereign states. |  Example 1: The two countries signed a treaty to promote bilateralism in trade.  两国签署了一项条约，以促进贸易双边主义。

Example 2: Bilateralism has been the cornerstone of the country's foreign policy for decades.  几十年来，双边主义一直是该国外交政策的基石。 | synonym: two-sidedness, reciprocity | antonym: multilateralism, unilateralism | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bi- (two) + lateral (sided) + -ism (doctrine, practice).  "Lateral" comes from Latin "lateralis" (of the side), from "latus" (side).
