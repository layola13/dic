bakerlike: /ˈbeɪkərˌlaɪk/
adjective
像面包师的，有面包师特点的
Resembling or characteristic of a baker, especially in appearance or behavior.
He had a bakerlike complexion, ruddy and flour-dusted. 他面色红润，沾满面粉，像个面包师。
近义词: bakerly,  bread-making
反义词:  None
