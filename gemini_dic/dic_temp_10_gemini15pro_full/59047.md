dweeb:/dwiːb/
n.
书呆子，怪胎
a person who is physically awkward and socially inept; a nerd.
例句：He was a real dweeb in high school, always studying and never going out. 他高中时是个十足的书呆子，总是学习，从不出去玩。

例句：She called him a dweeb for wearing those glasses. 她因为他戴着那副眼镜而叫他书呆子。

近义词：nerd, geek, dork
反义词：cool kid, popular person
词性：名词
