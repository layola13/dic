colonelcies: /ˈkɜːrnlsiz/ | n. | 上校的职位或任期（通常用复数形式）; 上校的职权范围;  | A term, now largely archaic, referring to the position, tenure, or jurisdiction of a colonel.  It is often used in the plural to signify multiple instances of such positions or jurisdictions. |  The colonelcies of the various regiments were filled by experienced officers. 各个团的上校职位都由经验丰富的军官担任。|  He held several colonelcies during his distinguished career. 在他杰出的职业生涯中，他担任过几个上校的职位。 | 近义词: commands, leadership (in a military context) | 反义词:  subordination (in a military context) | 词性: 名词


词源:  Derived from "colonel" + "-cy" (suffix denoting state, rank, or office). "Colonel" ultimately comes from the Italian word "colonnello," meaning "commander of a column."
