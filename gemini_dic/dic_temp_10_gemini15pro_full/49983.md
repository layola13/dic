Denisovans:/dɪˈniːsəvənz/ /dəˈniːsəvənz/
|n.|丹尼索瓦人|An extinct species or subspecies of archaic human that ranged across Asia during the Lower and Middle Paleolithic.|例句：Denisovans interbred with Neanderthals and modern humans. 丹尼索瓦人与尼安德特人和现代人杂交。

|例句：Scientists discovered Denisovans through DNA analysis of a finger bone. 科学家通过对一根指骨的DNA分析发现了丹尼索瓦人。
|近义词：hominins, archaic humans|反义词:modern humans|词性:Noun
