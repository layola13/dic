near-at-hand:/ˌnɪər ət ˈhænd/ | adj. | 在手边；在附近；即将到来 | readily available; close by; imminent | The solution was near at hand. 解决方法就在眼前。|  The festival is near at hand. 节日即将到来。 | 近义词：close, nearby, imminent, approaching, forthcoming | 反义词：distant, far, remote | 词性：形容词

Etymology:
"near" comes from Old English "near," from Proto-Germanic "*nerwaz," meaning "nearer," comparative of "*nehwaz."
"at hand" is a phrase meaning "close by" or "readily available," with "hand" referring to one's reach.
