awardees:/əˌwɔːrˈdiːz/
n.
获奖者；受奖人
A person or organization that has received an award.
The awardees were all smiles as they accepted their prizes. 获奖者们在领奖时都面带笑容。
recipients, winners, honorees
losers, non-recipients
