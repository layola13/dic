glossly:/ˈɡlɒsli/| adj.|光滑的，光彩夺目的|Having a smooth, shiny surface; superficially attractive.|The magazine was printed on glossy paper. 这本杂志印在光滑的纸上。|近义词：shiny, polished, sleek, lustrous|反义词：dull, matte, rough|词性:形容词

词源: From gloss + -ly.  "Gloss" itself comes from Old French "glose" meaning "gloss, explanation, interpretation," ultimately from Late Latin "glossa" meaning "explanation of a difficult word," originally "obsolete or foreign word needing explanation," from Greek "glossa" meaning "tongue, language, word."
