browsed:/braʊzd/| v.|浏览，随便翻阅|To look through something casually, often without a specific purpose.|I browsed the shelves of the bookstore. 我浏览了书店的书架。|近义词：skimmed, scanned, glanced at, perused|反义词：studied, scrutinized|verb

browsed:/braʊzd/| adj.|被浏览过的|Having been looked through casually.|The browsed magazines were left on the table. 被浏览过的杂志被留在桌子上。|近义词：skimmed, scanned|反义词：unopened, unread|adjective
