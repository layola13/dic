interlunary: /ˌɪntərˈluːnəri/ | adj. | 月缺的，新月之间的 | Relating to the period between two successive new moons; the dark period of the moon. |  The interlunary night was exceptionally dark. 月缺之夜异常黑暗。|  During the interlunary period, ancient cultures often held special ceremonies. 在新月之间的这段时间里，古代文化经常举行特殊的仪式。 | 近义词: dark, moonless | 反义词: lunar, bright | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin *interlunium*, from *inter-* (between) + *luna* (moon).
