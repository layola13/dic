nearby:/ˈnɪrˌbaɪ/ /ˈnɪərbaɪ/| adj. adv.|附近的，邻近的；在附近|Located a short distance away.|The nearby park is a great place for a picnic. 附近的公园是野餐的好地方。|She lives nearby. 她住在附近。|近义词：close, adjacent, neighboring|反义词:distant, far, remote|词性:形容词/副词

词源: From near + by.  "Near" comes from Old English *nēar (related to Old High German nāh "near," Gothic nehw "near"), from Proto-Germanic *nēhwaz, from PIE *negh- "near." "By" comes from Old English bī, bi "by, near, at, on, about," from Proto-Germanic *bi "around, about, near" (source also of Old Norse, Old Frisian bi, Dutch bij, German bei, Gothic bi), from PIE root *ambhi- "around," also forming words with the sense of "both."
