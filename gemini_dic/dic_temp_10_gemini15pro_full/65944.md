exactingly:/ɪɡˈzæktɪŋli/| adv.|严格地，精确地，一丝不苟地|in a demanding way that expects high standards of accuracy and attention to detail|The work was carried out exactingly to ensure the highest quality. 这项工作一丝不苟地进行，以确保最高的质量。|The engineer exactingly followed the blueprints. 工程师严格按照蓝图操作。|近义词：precisely, meticulously, rigorously, carefully, strictly|反义词：carelessly, loosely, approximately, roughly|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From exacting + -ly.  Exacting comes from the late Middle English verb "exacte,"  meaning "to demand," from the Old French "exacter" and ultimately from the Latin "exigere," meaning "to drive out, demand, enforce."  The "-ly" suffix transforms the adjective "exacting" into an adverb.
