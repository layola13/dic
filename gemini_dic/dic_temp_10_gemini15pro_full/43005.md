cowardize:/ˈkaʊərdaɪz/| vt.|使胆怯，使懦弱|To cause to become cowardly; intimidate.|His threats cowardized his opponents.|近义词:intimidate, daunt, dishearten|反义词:embolden, encourage|词性:动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From coward + -ize.  "Coward" comes from Old French "coart," ultimately from Latin "cauda" (tail), referring to an animal with its tail between its legs, a sign of fear.
