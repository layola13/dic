awfulness:/ˈɔːflnəs/| n.|糟糕；恶劣；可怕|The awfulness of the situation was overwhelming.  这种情况的糟糕程度令人难以承受。|The film was of such awfulness that I walked out after 20 minutes. 这部电影糟糕透了，20分钟后我就走了。|近义词：dreadfulness, terribleness, horror, frightfulness|反义词：wonderfulness, pleasantness, beauty|词性:名词
词源: From awful + -ness.  来自awful(糟糕的) + -ness(名词后缀，表示状态或性质).
