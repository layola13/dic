outblunder: /ˌaʊtˈblʌndər/
verb
犯更大的错误，在失策方面胜过
to surpass in blundering; to make more or bigger mistakes than someone else.
He outblundered his opponent in the chess match, making three crucial errors. 他在棋赛中比对手犯了更大的错误，犯了三个关键性的失误。
The government outblundered the opposition party with its series of policy failures. 政府在一系列的政策失败中比反对党犯了更大的错误。
近义词: outdo, surpass, eclipse, outstrip
反义词: be outdone, be surpassed
词性: verb
