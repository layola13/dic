nonallegiance:/ˌnɒnəˈliːdʒəns/
n.|不效忠；不效力；不效顺|The state of not owing allegiance to a particular country or ruler.  A formal renunciation of allegiance.|He declared his nonallegiance to the king.|近义词：disloyalty, unfaithfulness, treachery|反义词: loyalty, allegiance, fidelity|词性:名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
allegiance (noun): from Old French alegiance "loyalty, homage," from aleger "to bind by oath"
