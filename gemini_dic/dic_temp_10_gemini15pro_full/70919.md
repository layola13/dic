fireboard:/ˈfaɪərˌbɔːrd/
n.|防火板; 壁炉挡板; 壁炉前的地板|A board or panel in front of a fireplace, often decorative or protective.|The ornate fireboard shielded the carpet from stray embers.  壁炉上华丽的挡板保护地毯免受飞溅的余烬的伤害。|The fireboard was scorched from years of intense heat.  防火板因多年的高温而烧焦了。|mantelpiece, fire screen|hearth|noun
