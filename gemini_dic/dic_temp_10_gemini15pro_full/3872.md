agraphia:/əˈɡræfiə/| n.|失写症|Loss of the ability to write, due to injury to the brain.|Due to her agraphia after the stroke, she could no longer sign her name. 由于中风后的失写症，她再也无法签名了。|Near synonyms: dysgraphia (partial loss of writing ability), alexia (inability to read), aphasia (loss of ability to understand or express speech)|Antonyms: literacy, fluency in writing|noun
