noncultivated:/ˌnɒnˈkʌltɪveɪtɪd/ /ˌnɑːnˈkʌltɪveɪtɪd/
adjective
未开垦的，未耕种的
Not cultivated; not tilled or prepared for raising crops; undeveloped or wild.
The hikers trekked through the noncultivated wilderness.  徒步旅行者穿越了未开垦的荒野。
Wildflowers blossomed in the noncultivated field. 野花在未耕种的田野里盛开。
近义词:uncultivated, wild, untamed, fallow
反义词:cultivated, farmed, domesticated, tilled
词性:adjective
词源:From non- + cultivated.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not." Cultivated comes from the Latin word "cultivare" meaning "to till, cultivate."
