excommunicate: /ˌɛks.kəˈmjuː.nɪ.keɪt/
verb
逐出教会，开除教籍
To officially exclude someone from participation in the sacraments and services of the Christian Church.
The priest excommunicated the thief from the church.  神父把小偷逐出了教会。
近义词: ostracize, banish, expel
反义词: admit, include, welcome
词性: verb

excommunicate: /ˌɛks.kəˈmjuː.nɪ.kət/
noun
被逐出教会的人
A person who has been officially excluded from participation in the sacraments and services of the Christian Church.
He lived as an excommunicate for many years. 他作为一个被逐出教会的人生活了很多年。
近义词: outcast, pariah
反义词: member, believer
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin excommunicatus, past participle of excommunicare "to exclude from communion," from ex- "out of" + communicare "to share, communicate."
