nonprecedent:/nɒnˈprɛsɪdənt/ | adj. | 无先例的 | Not having or constituting a precedent. |  This ruling is nonprecedent and applies only to this specific case.  这项裁决没有先例可循，只适用于这个具体案例。 | non-binding, novel | precedented | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + precedent.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "precedent" comes from the Latin "praecedens," meaning "going before."
