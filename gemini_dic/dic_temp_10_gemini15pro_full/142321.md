orthodoxness:/ˈɔːrθədɑːksnɪs/
n.
正统性，正统观念
The quality or state of being orthodox; adherence to established or traditional beliefs or practices.
His orthodoxness in religious matters was well known. 他在宗教事务上的正统性是众所周知的。
conformity, conventionality, traditionalism
heterodoxy, unorthodoxy, heresy
noun
