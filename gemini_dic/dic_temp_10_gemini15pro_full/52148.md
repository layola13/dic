diaphanometer:/ˌdaɪəfəˈnɒmɪtər/ |n.|透光计，透明度计|An instrument for measuring the transparency of the atmosphere, especially as affected by fog, smoke, or dust.|例句：The diaphanometer showed a significant decrease in atmospheric transparency due to the heavy smog. 透光计显示由于浓雾，大气透明度显著下降。

例句：Researchers used a diaphanometer to measure the clarity of the lake water. 研究人员使用透光计来测量湖水的清澈度。|近义词：transparency meter, clarity meter|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *dia* ("through") + *phaneros* ("visible, apparent") + *-meter* ("instrument for measuring")
