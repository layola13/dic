minuet:/ˌmɪnjuˈɛt/ | n. |小步舞；小步舞曲|A slow, stately dance in triple time, popular in the 17th and 18th centuries.  A piece of music for such a dance, typically in triple time and having a moderate tempo. |The dancers moved gracefully through the minuet. 舞者们优雅地跳着小步舞。| The composer wrote a beautiful minuet for the queen. 作曲家为女王写了一首优美的小步舞曲。 | synonyms: dance, waltz, gavotte | antonyms: jig, reel | noun
