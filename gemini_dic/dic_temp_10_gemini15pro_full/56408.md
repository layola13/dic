dolichocephalism:/ˌdɒlɪkoʊˈsɛfəlɪzəm/ /ˌdɒlɪkəʊˈsɛfəlɪzəm/| n. | 长头畸形 |  A condition where the head is longer than average, relative to its width.  It's characterized by a cephalic index (the ratio of the maximum width of the head to its maximum length) of less than 75. |  Dolichocephalism is a characteristic of some breeds of dogs, like Greyhounds.  长头畸形是一些犬种的特征，比如灰狗。| synonyms: long-headedness | antonyms: brachycephalism | noun
