bassiest:/ˈbeɪsiɪst/| adj.|最低音的；最深沉的| having the lowest pitch or range; deepest.|His voice was the bassiest in the choir. 他的声音是唱诗班里最低沉的。|The instrument produced the bassiest notes I'd ever heard. 这乐器发出我从未听过的最低音。|近义词：deepest, lowest|反义词：highest, shrillest|词性:形容词
词源: From bass + -iest (superlative suffix). Ultimately from Late Latin bassus "low".
