Etymology: From fork + -ed + -ness. "Fork" comes from Old English *forca, from Proto-Germanic *furko, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *perk- "pointed."  "-ed" is a suffix forming adjectives from nouns. "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, denoting a state or quality.
