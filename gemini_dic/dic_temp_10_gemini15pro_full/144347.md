outstartle: /ˌaʊtˈstɑːrtl/
verb
惊吓过度，比…更令人吃惊
to surprise or frighten someone more than someone or something else has already done
例句：The sudden noise outstartled even the seasoned hunters. 突然的噪音甚至把经验丰富的猎人都吓了一跳。

例句：Her scream outstartled the birds in the nearby trees. 她的尖叫声把附近树上的鸟都吓飞了。

近义词：astound, amaze, surprise, shock, startle
反义词：comfort, soothe, reassure
词性：v.
