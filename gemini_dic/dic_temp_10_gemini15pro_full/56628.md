donies:/ˈdoʊniz/
noun
传闻；小道消息；内幕消息
rumors; gossip; inside information
The donies from the meeting suggest a major restructuring is planned.  会议上传来的小道消息表明，一项重大重组计划正在酝酿中。
synonyms: rumors, gossip, reports, hearsay, whispers
antonyms: facts, truth, certainty, confirmation
词性: 名词 (n.)
