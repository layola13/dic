diaphragming:/ˌdaɪəˈfræmɪŋ/
n.|横膈膜运动，光阑调节|The act of controlling the diaphragm, especially in breathing or singing.  This refers to the movement and control of the diaphragm muscle, which plays a crucial role in respiration. It can also refer to the adjustment of a camera's diaphragm to control the amount of light entering the lens.|Diaphragming is essential for proper breathing technique in singers.|近义词:breathing control, respiration control|反义词:(No direct antonym)
词性:名词

diaphragming:/ˌdaɪəˈfræmɪŋ/
n.|光阑调节，光圈调节|The act of adjusting the diaphragm of a camera lens to control the amount of light reaching the film or sensor.|Proper diaphragming is crucial for achieving the desired depth of field in photography.|近义词:aperture adjustment|反义词:(No direct antonym)
词性:名词
