hairbrained:/ˈhɛərbreɪnd/| adj.|轻率的；鲁莽的；不切实际的|Impractical; foolish and reckless.|例句：That hairbrained scheme of yours is never going to work. 你那轻率的计划永远不会成功。

例句：Investing all your savings in a single, volatile stock is a hairbrained idea. 把你所有的积蓄都投资于一只单一、波动性大的股票是一个不切实际的想法。|近义词：reckless, foolish, harebrained, crazy, insane, ill-conceived, ill-advised|反义词：sensible, prudent, wise, judicious, well-thought-out|词性:形容词
