consumers:/kənˈsuːmərz/| n.|消费者，用户|People who buy and use goods and services. |Consumers are becoming more price-conscious. 消费者越来越注重价格。|customers, buyers, users|producers, sellers|noun

Etymology->
From Late Middle English *consumere*, from Latin *consumere* (“to consume, devour, waste, spend, use up”), from *con-* (“together”) + *sumere* (“to take”).
