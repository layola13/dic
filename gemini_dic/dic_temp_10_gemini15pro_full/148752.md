overtruthfulnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈtruːθfəlnəsɪz/
n.
过分诚实，过分坦率
The state or quality of being excessively truthful, often to the point of being blunt or tactless.
His overtruthfulnesses sometimes offended people, even though he meant no harm. 他的过分诚实有时会冒犯别人，即使他并无恶意。
Synonyms:  candor, frankness, honesty, outspokenness
Antonyms: deceptiveness, dishonesty, evasiveness, prevarication
