falteringly:/ˈfɔːltərɪŋli/ (US) /ˈfɔːltərɪŋli/ (UK)
adverb
犹豫地；吞吞吐吐地；结结巴巴地
in a hesitant or unsteady manner.
She spoke falteringly, her voice barely above a whisper. 她结结巴巴地说着，声音几乎听不见。
He walked falteringly towards the door. 他步履蹒跚地走向门口。
近义词: hesitantly, uncertainly, unsteadily, waveringly
反义词: confidently, firmly, steadily, resolutely
词性: adverb

词源: From the verb "falter" + "-ing" + "-ly". "Falter" comes from the Middle English "falteren,"  frequentative of "fallen" (to fall), from Old English "feallan."
