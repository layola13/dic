indecision's:/ˌɪndɪˈsɪʒənz/| n.|犹豫不决，优柔寡断|The possessive form of "indecision," referring to the state of being unable to decide.  It signifies that something belongs to or is caused by indecision. |Her indecision's consequences were significant. 她的犹豫不决导致了严重的后果。|近义词：hesitancy's, wavering's|反义词：determination's, decisiveness's|词性: 名词所有格
