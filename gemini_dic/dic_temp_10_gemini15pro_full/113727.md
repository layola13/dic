macrodactylic: /ˌmækroʊdækˈtɪlɪk/ | adj. | 巨指的，巨趾的 | Having abnormally large fingers or toes. |  Example:  The child was born with a macrodactylic hand. 这个孩子出生时一只手患有巨指症。

Example: Macrodactylic limbs can sometimes be associated with other genetic conditions. 巨指（趾）肢有时可能与其他遗传疾病有关。

ETYMOLOGY->
From macro- (large) + -dactyly (condition of the fingers/toes). Ultimately from Greek *makros* (large) and *daktylos* (finger).
