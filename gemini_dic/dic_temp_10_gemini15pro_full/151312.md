parillin:/ˈpærɪlɪn/ | n. |菝葜皂苷,菝葜素|A glycoside obtained from various species of smilax, formerly used as an antisyphilitic.|Sarsaparilla contains parillin, a natural compound believed to have medicinal properties.|近义词:sarsaponin, smilacin|反义词: None|词性:noun

Etymology: From Spanish *parrilla*, diminutive of *parra* "vine", referring to the plant's climbing habit. Ultimately from Latin *pergula* "vine arbor".
