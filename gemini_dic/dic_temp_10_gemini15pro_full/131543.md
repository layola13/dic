noncommercials:/ˌnɑːnkəˈmɜːrʃəlz/
|n.|公益广告; 非商业广告|Advertisements or announcements broadcast on radio or television that promote a public service or cause, rather than a product or service for sale.|The television station aired several noncommercials about the importance of voting.|Synonyms: public service announcements (PSAs), community service announcements|Antonyms: commercials, advertisements
|noun
