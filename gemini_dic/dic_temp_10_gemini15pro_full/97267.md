inflowing:/ˈɪnfloʊɪŋ/
adjective
流入的；注入的
flowing in
The inflowing river provided water for the town. 流入的河流为小镇提供了水源。
inflowing current 流入电流  The inflowing current charged the battery. 流入的电流给电池充电。
synonyms: incoming, entering, inward
antonyms: outflowing, outgoing, exiting
adjective
