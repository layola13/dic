honeysuckled:/ˈhʌniˌsʌk(ə)ld/| adj.| 缠绕忍冬的；充满甜蜜的| Covered with or scented by honeysuckle.|  The honeysuckled cottage was charming. 缠绕忍冬的农舍很迷人。| The air was honeysuckled and warm. 空气中弥漫着忍冬的香味，温暖宜人。 |近义词：fragrant, sweet-smelling|反义词: unscented|词性:形容词

Etymology:  From honeysuckle + -ed.  "Honeysuckle" itself comes from "honey" + "suck" due to the sweetness of the flower's nectar and the way it can be sucked out.
