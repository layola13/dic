passageway:/ˈpæsɪdʒweɪ/ | n. |通道，走廊|A narrow way, typically indoors, that allows passage from one place to another.|We walked down a long passageway to reach the main hall. 我们沿着一条长长的走廊走到大厅。|近义词：corridor, hallway, aisle, passage|反义词: barrier, obstacle | noun

passageway:/ˈpæsɪdʒweɪ/ | n. |航道，海峡|A navigable route, especially a narrow channel of water.|The ship carefully navigated the narrow passageway between the islands.  这艘船小心地航行于岛屿间的狭窄航道。|近义词：channel, strait, waterway|反义词: blockage | noun
