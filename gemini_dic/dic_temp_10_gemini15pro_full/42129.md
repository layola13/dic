cottonmouth:/ˈkɑːtnˌmaʊθ/ (US), /ˈkɒtnˌmaʊθ/ (UK)| n.|水蝮蛇，棉口蛇；（非正式）口干|A venomous semiaquatic pit viper ( *Agkistrodon piscivorus*) found in the southeastern United States, characterized by its white mouth lining. Also refers to the condition of having a dry mouth.|

例句：
The cottonmouth is a dangerous snake.
棉口蛇是一种危险的蛇。

He woke up with a terrible cottonmouth after a night of drinking.
他喝了一晚上酒后醒来，感到口干舌燥。

近义词：water moccasin (for the snake), dry mouth, parched mouth|反义词：(for dry mouth) hydrated, moist mouth|词性: noun
