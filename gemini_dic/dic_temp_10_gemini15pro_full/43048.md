cowinner:/ˈkoʊˌwɪnər/| n.|共同获奖者；共同获胜者|A person or entity that shares a prize or victory with another.|Marie Curie and her husband, Pierre, were cowinners of the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1903. 玛丽·居里和她的丈夫皮埃尔是1903年诺贝尔物理学奖的共同获奖者。|Synonym: joint winner, co-recipient|Antonym: loser, runner-up|noun
