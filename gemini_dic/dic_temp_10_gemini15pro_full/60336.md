egolessness:/ˈiːɡəʊlɪsnəs/| n.|无私，忘我|The state of being without ego; lack of egotism or self-centeredness. |Her egolessness allowed her to truly connect with others. 她无私的精神让她能真正地与他人联系起来。|近义词：selflessness, humility, modesty|反义词: egotism, narcissism, self-centeredness|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From ego + -less + -ness.  "Ego" comes from the Latin word for "I". "-less" is a suffix indicating lack or absence of something.  "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
