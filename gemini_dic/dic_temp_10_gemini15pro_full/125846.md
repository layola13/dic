musaceous:/ˌmjuːˈseɪʃəs/
adjective
芭蕉科的；像芭蕉的
Relating to or resembling plants of the Musaceae family (banana family), typically having large, oblong leaves.
The musaceous plants thrived in the humid climate.  这些芭蕉科植物在潮湿的气候中茁壮成长。
The large, paddle-like leaves were indicative of a musaceous species.  这些巨大的桨状叶子表明这是一个芭蕉科物种。
Synonyms: banana-like
