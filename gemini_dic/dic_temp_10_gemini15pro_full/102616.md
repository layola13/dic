jainas:/ˈdʒaɪnəz/
noun
耆那教徒（复数）
Followers of Jainism, an ancient Indian religion.
The Jainas are known for their strict adherence to non-violence. 耆那教徒以严格遵守非暴力而闻名。
Jainas often wear white clothes. 耆那教徒经常穿白色衣服。
