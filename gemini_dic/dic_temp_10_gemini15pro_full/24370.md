bring-to-pass:/ˌbrɪŋ tə ˈpæs/ | v. | 实现；使发生 | To cause something to happen; to accomplish. |  He worked hard to bring his dreams to pass. 他努力工作以实现他的梦想。

The new CEO brought to pass many important changes. 新任首席执行官实现了许多重要的变革。 | 近义词: accomplish, achieve, realize, effect, execute | 反义词: prevent, obstruct, hinder | 词性: 动词 (verb)


词源:
From Middle English *bringen to passe*, equivalent to *bring* + *to* + *pass*.  The phrase *to pass* originally meant "to come about" or "to happen".
