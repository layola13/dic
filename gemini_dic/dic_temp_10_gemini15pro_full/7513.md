anneal:/əˈniːl/| vt. & vi.|退火，使（金属、玻璃等）退火；使变软；使缓和|To heat (metal or glass) and allow it to cool slowly, in order to remove internal stresses and toughen it.  To make less intense; to soften.|The glass is annealed to make it stronger.|近义词：temper, toughen, soften, mitigate|反义词：harden, strengthen|verb
