nibblingly:/ˈnɪblɪŋli/| adv.|一点一点地，慢慢地|in a nibbling manner; by taking small bites or amounts.  Derived from "nibble" which means to bite or chew something gently and repeatedly.|She nibblingly ate the cookie, savoring each small bite. 她一小口一小口地吃着饼干，细细品味着每一口。|近义词：gingerly, cautiously, delicately|反义词：voraciously, greedily|词性:副词
