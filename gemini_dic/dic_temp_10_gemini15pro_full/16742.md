barterers:/ˈbɑːrtərərz/
n.
物物交换者, 以物易物的人
People who exchange goods or services without using money.
The barterers met at the edge of town to trade their wares.  物物交换者聚集在城镇边缘交换他们的货物。
The barterers agreed on a fair exchange of tools for food.  物物交换者同意公平地交换工具和食物。
近义词: traders, exchangers, swappers
反义词: buyers, sellers, consumers
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From barter (v.) + -er (suffix forming nouns denoting agents or persons performing an action).  Barter itself comes from Old French barater "to cheat, deceive, barter," of uncertain origin.
