enclaved:/ɪnˈkleɪvd/ | adj. |被包围的，被封锁的 | Surrounded or enclosed by another territory, often of a different nature or culture; forming an enclave. | The small village was enclaved within the enemy's territory.  这个小村庄被包围在敌人的领土内。 |  The Vatican City is enclaved within Rome. 梵蒂冈城被包围在罗马境内。 | synonyms: enclosed, surrounded, landlocked, embedded, inserted | antonyms: open, exposed, bordering | adjective


enclave: /ˈɛnkleɪv/ | n. |飞地，被包围的领土| A portion of territory within or surrounded by a larger territory whose inhabitants are culturally or ethnically distinct. |  The Spanish enclaves in Morocco have been a source of tension for years.  位于摩洛哥的西班牙飞地多年来一直是紧张局势的根源。 | Campione d'Italia is an Italian enclave within Switzerland.  坎皮奥内迪塔利亚是瑞士境内的意大利飞地。| synonyms: territory, region, area, district, zone | antonyms: surrounding area, mainland | noun
