dipper's:/ˈdɪpərz/
noun
汲水者之物；北斗七星的
belonging to or associated with a dipper (a person who dips or a bird that dips into water for food) or the constellation Ursa Major (also known as the Big Dipper or the Plough).
The dipper's ladle hung on the wall.  汲水者的勺子挂在墙上。
The dipper's stars shone brightly. 北斗七星闪耀着光芒。
Synonyms:  scooper's, ladler's, Ursa Major's, Plough's
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, could be contextually dependent, like "non-dipper's")
