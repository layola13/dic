dreadlessness:/ˈdrɛdləsnəs/
n.
无畏; 大胆
absence of dread; fearlessness.
He faced the challenge with dreadlessness. 他无所畏惧地面对挑战。
近义词: fearlessness, intrepidity, courage, boldness
反义词: dread, fear, anxiety, apprehension
词性: 名词

词源:
From dread + -less + -ness.  Dread comes from Old English *drǣdan, from Proto-Germanic *drōzaną (“to respect, have religious awe, fear”).  The suffix -less indicates absence, and -ness forms a noun from an adjective.
