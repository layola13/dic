awkwardish:/ˈɔːkwərdɪʃ/| adj.|略显尴尬的，有点笨拙的|Slightly awkward; somewhat clumsy.|例句：He made an awkwardish attempt at a joke. 他讲了个有点尴尬的笑话。|例句：She felt awkwardish in her new dress. 她穿着新裙子感到有点不自在。|近义词：clumsy, ungainly, gawky|反义词：graceful, elegant|词性:形容词
词源: awkward + -ish (suffix forming adjectives meaning 'somewhat', 'rather')  From awkward (meaning "clumsy, ungainly") + the suffix "-ish" (meaning "somewhat" or "having the quality of").
