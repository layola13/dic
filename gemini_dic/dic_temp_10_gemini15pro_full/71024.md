firmitude:/ˈfɜːrmɪtjuːd/| n. |坚定；坚固；坚决|The quality of being firm; resoluteness, steadfastness, and strength of character.  It implies a determined and unwavering adherence to one's principles or beliefs.|She faced the challenges with remarkable firmitude. 她以非凡的坚定面对挑战。|近义词：resolution, steadfastness, resolve, determination, constancy|反义词：irresolution, weakness, vacillation|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *firmitudo*, from Latin firmus ("firm, strong").
