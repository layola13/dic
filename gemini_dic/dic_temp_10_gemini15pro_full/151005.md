paranoeas:/ˌpærəˈnɔɪəz/| n. |偏执狂；妄想症（paranoia 的复数形式）|A mental condition characterized by delusions of persecution, unwarranted jealousy, or exaggerated self-importance, typically elaborated into an organized system. It may be an aspect of chronic personality disorder, or a symptom of another psychiatric illness.|He suffers from paranoeas about being followed. 他患有被跟踪的妄想症。|近义词：delusions, suspicions, fixations|反义词：trust, sanity|词性:名词
