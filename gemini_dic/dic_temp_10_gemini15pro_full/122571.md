moderation:/ˌmɒdəˈreɪʃn/ (US) /ˌmɑːdəˈreɪʃn/| n. |适度，节制，缓和|The key to a healthy lifestyle is moderation in all things. 健康的生活方式的关键在于凡事适度。|例句：He preached moderation in all things. 他宣扬凡事都要适度。|近义词：temperance, restraint, control|反义词：excess, extravagance|词性:名词
moderation:| | |A policy of moderation was adopted. 采取了温和的政策。|例句：Moderation in eating and drinking is the way to preserve health.  饮食适度是保持健康的方法。|近义词：balance, equilibrium|反义词：immoderation, extremism|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *moderatio, from Latin moderari "to regulate, restrain," from modus "measure, limit."
