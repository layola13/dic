exobiology:/ˌɛksoʊbaɪˈɒlədʒi/ | n. | 外空生物学；地外生物学 | The study of life beyond Earth, encompassing the search for extraterrestrial life and the effects of extraterrestrial environments on living organisms. |  Exobiology research focuses on the possibility of life on Mars. 外空生物学研究的重点是火星上存在生命的可能性。|  astrobiology |  None | noun
