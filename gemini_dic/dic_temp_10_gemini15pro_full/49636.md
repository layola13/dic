demilance:/ˈdɛmɪlæns/ /ˈdɛmiˌlæns/ | n. | 轻骑枪, 半身甲 | A light lance about five or six feet long, formerly used by light cavalry.  A piece of armor protecting the front of the body from the waist to the thighs. | The knight couched his demilance and charged. 骑士举起轻骑枪冲锋陷阵。 | The demilance deflected the blow, saving the knight from serious injury.  半身甲挡开了攻击，使骑士免受重伤。| 近义词: lance, spear, corselet, breastplate | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly "disarmament" in a broader sense) | 词性: noun

demilance:/ˈdɛmɪlæns/ /ˈdɛmiˌlæns/ | n. | 轻骑枪, 半身甲 |  Used figuratively to refer to something small but effective,  or a partial defense.  | His witty retort was a demilance against their criticism. 他的机智反驳是对他们批评的有效回击。|  The new policy offered only a demilance against the growing economic crisis. 新政策仅对日益严重的经济危机提供部分保护。| 近义词: defense, protection, shield | 反义词: vulnerability, weakness  | 词性: noun
