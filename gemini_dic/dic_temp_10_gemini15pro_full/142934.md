out-trick: /ˌaʊtˈtrɪk/
verb
以计胜过，智胜
to trick someone by being more clever or skillful
He out-tricked his opponents and won the game. 他智胜了对手，赢得了比赛。
近义词: outsmart, outwit, outmaneuver
反义词: be outwitted, be outsmarted
词性: verb

out-trick: /ˌaʊtˈtrɪk/
noun
高明的计谋，骗术
a clever trick that is used to deceive someone
His out-trick surprised everyone. 他的计谋让每个人都感到惊讶。
近义词: ruse, stratagem, ploy
反义词: honesty, forthrightness
词性: noun
