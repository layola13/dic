hypersecrete:/ˌhaɪpərˈsiːkriːt/| v. |过度分泌，分泌过多|To secrete at an abnormally high rate.|The adrenal glands are hypersecreting cortisol. 肾上腺皮质正在过度分泌皮质醇。|Synonyms: oversecrete|Antonyms: hyposecrete|verb
hypersecretion:/ˌhaɪpərˈsiːkriːʃn/| n. |过度分泌，分泌过多|The excessive secretion of a substance, such as a hormone, by a gland.|Hypersecretion of growth hormone can lead to gigantism. 生长激素分泌过多会导致巨人症。|Synonyms: oversecretion|Antonyms: hyposecretion|noun
