bilinguals:/baɪˈlɪŋɡwəlz/ | n. | 双语者 | People who can speak two languages fluently. | Many bilinguals find it easier to learn a third language. 许多双语者发现学习第三种语言更容易。| multilinguals, polyglots | monolinguals | noun


bilingual:/baɪˈlɪŋɡwəl/ | adj. | 双语的 | Able to speak two languages fluently. |  She grew up in a bilingual household, speaking both English and Spanish. 她在一个双语家庭长大，会说英语和西班牙语。| multilingual, polyglot | monolingual | adjective


Etymology: From bi- (two) + lingual (relating to language).  Ultimately from Latin *bi- (two) and lingua (tongue, language).
