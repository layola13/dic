finalises:/ˈfaɪnəlaɪzɪz/
verb
完成；最终确定；定案
puts the finishing touches to something; completes all the arrangements for something.
The lawyer finalised the contract. 律师最终确定了合同。
Synonyms: complete, conclude, finish, settle
Antonyms: begin, start, commence
词性: 动词 (verb)
