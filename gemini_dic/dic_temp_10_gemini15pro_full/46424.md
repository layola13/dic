dagger:/ˈdæɡər/| n.|匕首；短剑；\|\
A short, pointed knife used as a weapon.\
He drew a dagger from beneath his cloak. 他从斗篷下抽出一把匕首。\|\
近义词：knife, blade, stiletto|\
反义词:shield, armor|\
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *dague*, diminutive of *dag*; akin to Old Norse *dagg* “dew,” so called from its resemblance to a dewdrop.
