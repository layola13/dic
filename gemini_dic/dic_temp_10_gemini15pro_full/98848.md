intelsat:/ˈɪnˌtɛlˌsæt/ | n. | 国际通信卫星组织；Intelsat卫星 |  A private company that provides satellite-based communications services globally. It was originally an intergovernmental consortium. | Intelsat operates a fleet of satellites providing global coverage. 国际通信卫星组织运营着一支覆盖全球的卫星舰队。| synonyms: satellite operator, communications provider | antonyms: terrestrial network | noun
