extrageneous: /ˌekstrəˈdʒiːniəs/
adjective
外来的；无关的；非必要的
introduced or coming from outside; not forming a necessary part of something
例句：The discussion was hampered by extraneous considerations.  讨论受到无关因素的阻碍。

例句：We must ignore extraneous information and focus on the core issue. 我们必须忽略无关信息，专注于核心问题。

近义词：irrelevant, extrinsic, external, foreign, adventitious
反义词：intrinsic, inherent, relevant, essential, pertinent
词性: adjective
