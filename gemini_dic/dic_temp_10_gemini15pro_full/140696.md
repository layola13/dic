omitting: /əˈmɪtɪŋ/ | v. |  省略；遗漏；删除；疏忽；未做 |  The report is omitting some important facts.  该报告遗漏了一些重要的事实。 | Example: He was omitting key details from his story. 他在故事中省略了关键细节。| Synonyms: excluding, leaving out, neglecting | Antonyms: including, adding, incorporating | Verb

Etymology: From Late Middle English omitten, from Latin omittere (“to let go, send away; leave undone, neglect”), from o- (“away”) + mittere (“to send, let go”).
