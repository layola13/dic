oxalic acid:/ɒkˈsælɪk ˈæsɪd/ (US) /ɒkˈsælɪk ˈæsɪd/ (UK) | n. | 草酸 | A poisonous crystalline carboxylic acid found in many plants, especially rhubarb and sorrel.|Oxalic acid can be used as a bleaching agent. 草酸可以用作漂白剂。 | synonyms: ethanedioic acid | antonyms: None specific to the chemical nature of oxalic acid | noun
