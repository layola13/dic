intakes:/ˈɪnteɪks/| n. |摄入量；吸入量；通风口；（尤指一年内）招生人数；纳入,引进|An act of taking something in, especially breath or food. The amount of something taken in. |例句：The daily intake of calories should be controlled.  每日卡路里摄入量应该控制。

例句：The university has increased its intake of overseas students. 这所大学增加了海外学生的招生人数。|近义词：consumption, ingestion, admission|反义词：output, discharge|词性:noun
