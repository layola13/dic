nontransitives:/ˌnɒnˈtrænzɪtɪvz/ | adj. | 非及物动词 | Not taking a direct object; designating a verb or verb construction that does not require or cannot take a direct object to complete its meaning. | The verb "sleep" is intransitive.  睡觉这个动词是非及物的。|  The child slept soundly. 孩子睡得很香。 | synonyms: complete, independent | antonyms: transitive | word type: adjective

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Intransitive verbs function normally in the simple present.
The sun rises in the east.  太阳从东方升起。
Birds sing beautifully. 鸟儿歌唱得很动听。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
The children are playing in the park. 孩子们正在公园里玩耍。
She is sleeping peacefully. 她正在安详地睡觉。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He has arrived. 他已经到了。
The flowers have bloomed. 花儿已经开了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
They have been working all day. 他们工作了一整天。
She has been sleeping for hours. 她已经睡了几个小时了。
