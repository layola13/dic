etheostomoid:/ɪˌθiːəˈstɒmɔɪd/
| adj. n.| 镖鲈科的；镖鲈科鱼类 |Relating to or denoting a large family (Etheostomidae) of small freshwater ray-finned fishes that are native to North America.  Many species are brightly coloured.| Many etheostomoid species inhabit fast-flowing streams. 许多镖鲈科鱼类栖息于快速流动的溪流中。| The etheostomoid darter is a beautiful fish.  镖鲈科的镖鲈是一种美丽的鱼。|近义词: darter (some species), perch-like (general appearance)|反义词: None (specific to a family of fish)|词性: Adjective, Noun


Etymology:
From Greek *etheo* (to strain) + *stoma* (mouth) + *-oid* (resembling).  Refers to the small, upturned mouths of many species in this family.
