outdriven:/ˌaʊtˈdrɪvən/
verb |超越，胜过（在驾驶方面）|  Surpassed or defeated in driving; driven better or further than. | He was outdriven by his rival in the final lap of the race. 他在比赛的最后一圈被对手超越了。|Synonyms: surpassed, outpaced, outrun, defeated | Antonyms:  defeated, lost to, fallen behind |v.

outdriven:/ˌaʊtˈdrɪvən/
adjective |被超越的，被胜过的（在驾驶方面）| Surpassed or defeated in driving. | The outdriven driver parked his car in frustration.  被超越的车手沮丧地停下了他的车。 | Synonyms: surpassed, outpaced, outrun | Antonyms:  victorious, winning |adj.
