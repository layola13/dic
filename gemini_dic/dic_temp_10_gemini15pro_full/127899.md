neoliberal:/ˌniːoʊˈlɪbərəl/ | adj. n. | 新自由主义的；新自由主义者 | Relating to or denoting a modified form of liberalism tending to favor free-market capitalism. |  The government adopted neoliberal policies. 政府采取了新自由主义政策。|  She's a staunch neoliberal. 她是一个坚定的新自由主义者。 | 近义词: free-market, laissez-faire | 反义词: socialist, interventionist | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:  "neo-" (new) + "liberal" (favoring individual liberty).  The term gained prominence in the late 20th century, referring to a resurgence of 19th-century liberal ideas about free markets and minimal government intervention.
