felspar:/ˈfɛlspɑːr/ /ˈfɛldˌspɑr/ | n. |长石|A group of rock-forming minerals, the most abundant minerals in the Earth's crust, composed of aluminum silicates of potassium, sodium, and calcium.|Feldspar is used in the manufacture of glass and ceramics.|近义词：albite, anorthite, orthoclase|反义词: None|noun

felspath:/ˈfɛlspæθ/ /ˈfɛldˌspæθ/ | n. |长石|Alternative spelling of feldspar, a group of rock-forming minerals.|Felspath is a common component of many igneous rocks.|近义词：feldspar, albite, anorthite, orthoclase|反义词: None|noun
