overhastily:/ˌoʊvərˈheɪstɪli/| adv.|过于匆忙地，草率地|Done or acting too quickly and without enough thought.  Formed from "over" (excessively) + "hastily" (in a hurried manner).  Ultimately derived from the Proto-Germanic *haifstiz. |He overhastily agreed to the deal and later regretted it. 他过于匆忙地同意了这笔交易，后来后悔了。|She overhastily packed her bags and forgot her passport. 她匆忙收拾行李，忘记了护照。|近义词：hurriedly, rashly, precipitately, prematurely|反义词：carefully, deliberately, thoughtfully|词性:副词
