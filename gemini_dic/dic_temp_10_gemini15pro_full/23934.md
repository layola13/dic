bream:/briːm/| n.|鲤科鱼；欧洲鲫鱼|A freshwater fish of the carp family, common in Europe and Asia.  It has a deep body and a forked tail.|We caught several bream while fishing in the lake. 我们在湖里钓到了几条鲤科鱼。|近义词：carp, roach|反义词:|noun
