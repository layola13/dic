outsitting:/ˌaʊtˈsɪtɪŋ/
verb
熬过；比…坐得久；在户外静坐
staying outside longer than someone else
例句：He outsat his rivals in the negotiations. 他在谈判中比他的对手们坚持的时间更长。

例句：They outsat the storm in the shelter. 他们在避难所里熬过了风暴。

近义词：outlast, survive, endure
反义词：succumb, yield
词性：v.
