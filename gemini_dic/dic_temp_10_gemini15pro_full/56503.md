domesticating:/dəˈmestɪkeɪtɪŋ/
v.|驯化，使习惯于家庭生活|The process of taming an animal and making it suitable for living with humans.|例句：They are domesticating wild horses. 他们在驯化野马。| 近义词: taming, civilizing, training| 反义词: wilding, releasing| 词性: 动词现在分词

domesticate:/dəˈmestɪkeɪt/
v.|驯养，驯化；使习惯于家庭生活|To tame an animal and make it suitable for living with humans, or to adapt a plant for cultivation.|例句：Humans domesticated dogs thousands of years ago.  人类在几千年前驯化了狗。|近义词:tame, train, cultivate |反义词: release, wild |词性: 动词
