complexities:/kəmˈpleksɪtiz/ /kəmˈpleksətiz/
n.
复杂性，复杂的事物
the state of being complex or intricate; the features of something that make it complex or intricate.
例句：The complexities of the legal system can be overwhelming.  法律制度的复杂性可能让人难以承受。

例句：We need to consider the complexities of the situation before making a decision. 我们需要在做决定之前考虑情况的复杂性。

近义词：intricacies, complications, difficulties, subtleties
反义词：simplicities, easiness
词性: 名词(plural noun)
