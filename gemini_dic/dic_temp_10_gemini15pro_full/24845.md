brownier:/ˈbraʊniər/| adj.|更巧克力的，更浓厚的 (指布朗尼蛋糕) |More chocolatey and rich (referring to brownies).|These brownies are brownier than the ones I baked last week. 这些布朗尼比我上周烤的更浓厚。|The recipe calls for extra chocolate to make the brownies brownier.  这个食谱要求额外的巧克力来使布朗尼更浓郁。|近义词：richer, chocolatier, denser|反义词：less chocolatey, less rich, lighter|词性:形容词

词源:
From brownie + -er (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from brown, referring to the color of the cake.
