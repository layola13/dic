in-question:/ˌɪn ˈkwɛstʃən/ | adj.|讨论中的；有疑问的；不确定的|being talked about or examined; uncertain or subject to doubt| The matter in question is the company's future.  讨论中的事情是公司的未来。|Synonyms: under discussion, disputed, doubtful, uncertain, at issue| Antonyms: certain, definite, resolved, undisputed| adjective

in-question:/ˌɪn ˈkwɛstʃən/ | pronoun |正在谈论的；上述的；有关的|the thing or person that is being talked about | The car in question was bright red.  上述的汽车是鲜红色的。|Synonyms: aforementioned, aforesaid, the subject under discussion|Antonyms: N/A|pronoun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *in quaestione* "in question," from *quaestio* "a seeking, inquiry, investigation," from *quaerere* "to seek, look for."
