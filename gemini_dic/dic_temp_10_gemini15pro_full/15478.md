badging:/ˈbædʒɪŋ/| n.| 授予徽章，配戴徽章，徽章系统|The act of providing or wearing badges, often as a form of identification or to indicate membership, achievement, or authorization.  It can also refer to the system used to manage and control access based on badges. |例句：The company's badging system ensures only authorized personnel can enter restricted areas.  这家公司的徽章系统确保只有授权人员才能进入限制区域。

例句：Badging all staff is a vital security measure. 为所有员工配戴徽章是一项重要的安全措施。|近义词：identification, credentialing, accreditation|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "anonymity" in a broader context)|noun
