nonavoidances:/nɒnəˈvɔɪdənsɪz/ | n. | 不可避免性；无法回避之事 | The state or quality of being unavoidable; things that cannot be avoided. |  The nonavoidances of aging include wrinkles and declining health.  衰老的不可避免性包括皱纹和健康衰退。| synonyms: inevitabilities, necessities | antonyms: avoidances, preventables | noun
