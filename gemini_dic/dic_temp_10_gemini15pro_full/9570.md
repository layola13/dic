antisleep:/ˌæntiˈsliːp/
adjective
抗睡眠的，防止睡眠的
Preventing or counteracting sleep.
Drivers often use antisleep medication to stay awake on long journeys. 司机经常使用抗睡眠药物以保持在长途旅行中清醒。
The antisleep effects of caffeine are well-known. 咖啡因的抗睡眠作用是众所周知的。
近义词：wakefulness-promoting, stimulant
反义词：sleep-inducing, soporific
词性: adjective

词源:
From anti- + sleep.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to".  "Sleep" refers to the natural state of rest.
