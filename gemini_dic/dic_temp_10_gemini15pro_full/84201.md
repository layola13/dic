half-reluctantly:/ˌhɑːf rɪˈlʌktəntli/
half-reluctantly| adv.|半不情愿地，勉强地|With some reluctance; showing a degree of unwillingness. It implies a mixture of willingness and unwillingness.|She half-reluctantly agreed to go to the party. 她半推半就地同意去参加聚会。|Near synonyms: hesitantly, unwillingly, grudgingly|Near antonyms: eagerly, willingly, readily
