hemitropous:/ˌhɛmɪˈtrɒpəs/
adj.
半倒生的，半转生的
(of a plant ovule) having the micropyle and chalaza at opposite ends, but with the funicle more or less on the same horizontal level as the micropyle, as a result of the ovule turning back on itself through about half a circle.
例句：The hemitropous ovule is a common type in flowering plants. 半倒生胚珠是开花植物中常见的一种类型。

例句：The flower's ovary contained numerous hemitropous ovules.  这朵花的花房里有很多半倒生胚珠。
近义词：campylotropous (somewhat similar ovule orientation)
反义词：orthotropous, anatropous
词性: adjective
