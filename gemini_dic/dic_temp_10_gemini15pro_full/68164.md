faineancy:/ˈfeɪniənsi/ | n. |懒惰，无所事事 |Laziness; aversion to work; indolence. |He was fired for faineancy. 他因为懒惰被解雇了。|  His faineancy prevented him from achieving his goals. 他的懒惰阻碍了他实现目标。 | 近义词: laziness, indolence, sloth, idleness | 反义词: diligence, industry, assiduity | 词性: noun


Etymology: From French *faineance*, from *fainéant* "idle," literally "do-nothing."
