corruptingly:/kəˈrʌptɪŋli/
| adv. |腐败地，堕落地|In a way that encourages or causes someone to behave dishonestly or immorally. | The officials were corruptingly influenced by the wealthy businessman. 官员们被这位富商腐蚀了。|Synonyms: dishonestly, deceitfully, wickedly, sinfully | Antonyms: honestly, ethically, righteously |词性:副词

corrupt:/kəˈrʌpt/
| adj. |腐败的，堕落的|Having or showing a willingness to act dishonestly in return for money or personal gain. | The corrupt politician accepted bribes in exchange for favors.  这个腐败的政客收受贿赂以换取好处。|Synonyms: dishonest, crooked, unscrupulous | Antonyms: honest, upright, ethical |词性:形容词

corrupt:/kəˈrʌpt/
| v. |使腐败，使堕落|Cause to act dishonestly in return for money or personal gain. | The desire for power can corrupt even the most virtuous individuals. 对权力的渴望甚至可以腐蚀最有道德的人。|Synonyms: bribe, suborn, pervert | Antonyms: purify, cleanse, reform |词性:动词
