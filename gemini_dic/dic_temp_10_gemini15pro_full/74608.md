freckle:/ˈfrɛkəl/ (US), /ˈfrɛkl/ (UK)| n.|雀斑， freckles(复数)|A small, light-brown spot on the skin, especially on the face, caused by exposure to sunlight.|She has a scattering of freckles across her nose. 她鼻子上散布着一些雀斑。|近义词：speck, spot, blemish|反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑 clear skin 乾淨的皮膚)|noun
