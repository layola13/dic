glutting:/ˈɡlʌtɪŋ/
v.
使…充满；使…过量；狼吞虎咽
overfilling; supplying or feeding to excess.
He is glutting himself on chocolate. 他正狂吃巧克力。
近义词：overfilling, satiating, gorging, stuffing
反义词：starving, depriving
词性：动词的现在分词形式

glut:/ɡlʌt/
n.
供过于求；过量
an excessively abundant supply of something.
There's a glut of cheap DVDs on the market. 市场上廉价DVD供过于求。
近义词：surplus, excess, overabundance
反义词：shortage, scarcity, dearth
词性：名词

词源：From the Old French *glout "glutton," from Latin glutire "to swallow."
