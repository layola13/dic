downsamples:/ˌdaʊnˈsæmpəlz/ | v. | 下采样，降低采样率 | Reduces the number of samples in a signal, typically an audio or image signal. | The software downsamples the audio to reduce file size.  该软件对音频进行下采样以减小文件大小。 | decimates, reduces resolution | upsamples, increases resolution | verb
downsample:/ˈdaʊnˌsæmpəl/ | n. | 下采样样本 | A sample taken at a lower rate than the original. | The downsample showed the same trend as the original data. 下采样样本显示出与原始数据相同的趋势。| reduced sample, subsample | upsample | noun

downsampling:/ˌdaʊnˈsæmplɪŋ/ | n. | 下采样 | The process of reducing the number of samples in a signal. | Downsampling can lead to loss of information. 下采样会导致信息丢失。| decimation, reduction of resolution | upsampling | noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From "down" + "sample".  "Down" indicates reduction, and "sample" refers to a data point or element taken from a larger set.
