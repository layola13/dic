glenn:/ɡlɛn/| n.|峡谷; 格伦(男子名)|A narrow valley, especially one with steep sides. Also used as a male given name.|例句：We hiked through the glenn, admiring the towering cliffs. 我们徒步穿越峡谷，欣赏着高耸的悬崖。

例句：Glenn is a common name in Scotland. 格伦在苏格兰是一个常见的名字。|近义词：valley, ravine, gorge, canyon|反义词:mountain, peak|Noun
