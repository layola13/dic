gazette:/ɡəˈzɛt/ | n. | 公报，报纸 | An official journal or newspaper, especially one publishing legal notices. | The results of the election were published in the government gazette.  选举结果刊登在政府公报上。| synonyms: journal, newspaper, periodical, bulletin, publication | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "unpublished material" or "private communication") | noun

gazette:/ɡəˈzɛt/ | v. | (在公报上)公布，宣布 | To announce or publish something officially in a gazette. |  The company was gazetted bankrupt. 该公司被宣布破产。| synonyms: announce, publish, proclaim, declare | antonyms: conceal, suppress, withhold | verb
