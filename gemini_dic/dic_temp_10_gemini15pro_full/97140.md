infinite:/ˈɪnfɪnɪt/ | adj. |无限的，无穷的|limitless or endless in space, extent, or size; impossible to measure or calculate.|例句：The universe seems infinite. 宇宙似乎是无限的。|近义词：boundless, endless, limitless, immeasurable|反义词:finite, limited|词性:形容词

infinite:/ˈɪnfɪnɪt/ | n. |无限，无穷大|something that is infinite or endless, especially in space or extent.|例句：She gazed into the infinite. 她凝视着无垠的远方。|近义词：infinity, eternity, vastness|反义词:finitude, limit|词性:名词
