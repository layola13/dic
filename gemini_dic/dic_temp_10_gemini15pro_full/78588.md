giantize:/ˈdʒaɪənˌtaɪz/
verb
使巨大化；使变成巨人；夸大
to make something gigantic or to cause something to appear gigantic; to exaggerate
They giantized the image of the president on the billboard. 他们在广告牌上放大了总统的形象。
The story giantized the actor's role in the event.  这个故事夸大了演员在事件中的作用。
近义词: enlarge, magnify, amplify, exaggerate, overstate
反义词: miniaturize, shrink, reduce, diminish, understate
词性: verb
