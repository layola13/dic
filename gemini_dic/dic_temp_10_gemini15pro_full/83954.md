half-consciousness:/ˌhɑːfˈkɒnʃəsnəs/ /ˌhæfˈkɑːnʃəsnəs/| n. |半清醒；半昏迷|A state of being only partially conscious; a state between consciousness and unconsciousness. |The patient drifted in and out of half-consciousness after the surgery.  手术后，病人时而清醒时而昏迷。|Near synonyms: semiconsciousness, stupor, daze|Near antonyms: consciousness, alertness, awareness|noun

half-consciousness:/ˌhɑːfˈkɒnʃəsnəs/ /ˌhæfˈkɑːnʃəsnəs/| adj. |半清醒的；半昏迷的|(Rarely used as an adjective)  Characterized by being only partially conscious. |He lay there in a half-conscious state, unable to fully comprehend what was happening. 他半昏迷地躺在那儿，无法完全理解发生了什么。|Near synonyms: semiconscious, dazed, stuporous|Near antonyms: conscious, alert, aware|adjective
