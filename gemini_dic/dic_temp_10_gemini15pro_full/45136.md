cuddleable:/ˈkʌdələbl/| adj.|可爱的，令人想拥抱的|Suitable for or inviting cuddling.|The kitten was so small and cuddleable.|近义词：huggable, cuddly, adorable, sweet|反义词：unpleasant, repulsive|词性:形容词
Etymology: cuddle + -able.  "Cuddle" comes from the early 19th century, possibly a frequentative of "cud," meaning to hold something close.
