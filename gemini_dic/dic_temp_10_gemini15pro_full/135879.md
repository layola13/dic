nonprovidently:/ˌnɒnˈprɒvɪdəntli/
adverb
不谨慎地，不节俭地，挥霍无度地
in a way that does not show foresight or careful planning; improvidently.
He had nonprovidently spent his inheritance. 他挥霍掉了他的遗产。
Synonyms: improvidently, recklessly, rashly, carelessly, thoughtlessly, imprudently, unwisely, foolishly
Antonyms: providently, prudently, wisely, carefully
词性: adverb
