dilucidate:/dɪˈluːsɪdeɪt/| v. |阐明，说明|to make something clear and easy to understand|例句：The report elucidated the complex issues surrounding climate change. 这份报告阐明了围绕气候变化的复杂问题。

例句：Could you please elucidate your point further? 你能进一步阐明你的观点吗？|近义词：clarify, explain, expound, illuminate|反义词：obfuscate, confuse, obscure|词性：动词

dilucid:/dɪˈluːsɪd/| adj. |清晰的，明了的|expressed clearly; easy to understand|例句：She gave a lucid explanation of the scientific theory. 她对这个科学理论作出了清晰的解释。

例句：His writing style is remarkably lucid and engaging. 他的写作风格非常清晰，引人入胜。|近义词：clear, coherent, intelligible, perspicuous|反义词：unclear, confusing, obscure, ambiguous|词性：形容词
