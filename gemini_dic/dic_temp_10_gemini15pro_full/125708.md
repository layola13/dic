mung:/mʌŋ/ | v. |  (通常不用作动词)  挑拣出 (通常指绿豆) |  (This usage is rare and often considered dialectal.)  She munged the bad beans from the good ones. 她把坏豆子从好豆子里挑拣出来。 | synonyms: sort, separate | antonyms: mix, combine | verb
