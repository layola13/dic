external:/ɪkˈstɜːrnl/ /ɛkˈstɜːrnəl/| adj.|外部的，外面的；外界的；表面的；外用的；[医] 外部的|Relating to or occurring on the outside; outer. |The external walls of the house are painted white. 房子的外墙被漆成白色。|近义词：outer, outside, exterior, outward|反义词：internal, inner, inside, interior|词性:形容词

external:/ɪkˈstɜːrnl/ /ɛkˈstɜːrnəl/| n.|外部；外观；外面；外形|The outward features or aspect of something. |The externals of the building are impressive. 这栋建筑的外观令人印象深刻。|近义词：exterior, outside, appearance|反义词：interior, inside|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *externālis, from Latin externus (“outward, external”), from exter (“outside, without”).
