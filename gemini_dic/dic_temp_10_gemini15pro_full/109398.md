lentisk: /ˈlɛntɪsk/ | n. | 乳香黄连木；乳香脂 | An evergreen shrub or small tree (Pistacia lentiscus) of the cashew family, native to the Mediterranean region, yielding a fragrant resin (mastic). | The lentisk tree is common in dry, rocky areas. 乳香黄连木常见于干燥的岩石地区。| The resin from the lentisk is used in varnishes. 乳香黄连木的树脂用于制作清漆。 | synonyms: mastic tree, Pistacia lentiscus | antonyms: N/A | noun


lentisk: /ˈlɛntɪsk/ | adj. | 乳香黄连木的 | Made from or relating to the lentisk tree. |  Lentisk resin has a distinctive aroma. 乳香黄连木树脂有一种独特的香味。|  The lentisk leaves are small and leathery. 乳香黄连木的叶子小而坚韧。| synonyms: mastic | antonyms: N/A | adjective
