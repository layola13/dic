catches:/ˈkætʃɪz/| n. v.|抓住，捕获物，捕捉;理解,明白|The cat catches mice. 猫抓老鼠。|近义词：grasps, seizes, apprehends, understands|反义词:misses, releases, loses|词性:名词(复数),动词(第三人称单数)

Etymology: From Middle English *cacchen*, from Old North French *cachier*, from Vulgar Latin *captiāre*, frequentative of Latin *captāre* "to seize, strive to seize," from *captus*, past participle of *capere* "to take, seize."
