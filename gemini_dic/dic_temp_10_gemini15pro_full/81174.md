grandeur:/ˈɡrændʒər/| n.|宏伟，壮丽，伟大|Splendor and impressiveness, especially of appearance or style.|The palace was restored to its original grandeur. 这座宫殿恢复了昔日的宏伟壮丽。|近义词：magnificence, majesty, splendor, glory|反义词：simplicity, modesty, insignificance|noun

Etymology:
From Old French "grandeur" (12c.), from Late Latin "grandor," from Latin "grandis" ("great").
