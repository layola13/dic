declaws:/diːˈklɔːz/
verb
去除爪子; 剪掉爪子
to remove the claws of (an animal), especially a cat.
The vet declawed the kitten.  兽医给小猫做了去爪手术。
近义词:  trim, clip
反义词:  none (The opposite action isn't a single word but a phrase like "let claws grow".)
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- + claw.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "remove" or "off."  "Claw" refers to the sharp, curved nail on the foot of an animal.
