debonnaire:/ˌdebəˈnɛr/ | adj. | 温文尔雅的；风度翩翩的 | Having a sophisticated charm; suave; urbane; affable and courteous. | He greeted everyone with a debonair smile. 他笑容满面地迎接每一个人。 |  He strolled into the room with a debonair air. 他神态自若地走进房间。 | 近义词:  suave, urbane, charming, elegant, sophisticated, gracious | 反义词:  awkward, clumsy, gauche, boorish, uncouth | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Old French *de bon aire "of good disposition," literally "of good air/manner."  The French phrase de bonne aire referred to someone who had the easy manners associated with a courtly upbringing, literally someone who "breathed good air."
