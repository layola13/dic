gyrates:/ˈdʒaɪˌreɪts/
gyrate: v.
旋转，转动
To move or cause to move in a circle or spiral, especially quickly.
The dancers gyrated wildly to the music. 舞者随着音乐疯狂地旋转。
gyrated:/ˈdʒaɪˌreɪtɪd/
gyrate: v.
旋转，转动
Moved or caused to move in a circle or spiral, especially quickly.
The leaves gyrated in the wind. 树叶在风中旋转。
gyrating:/ˈdʒaɪˌreɪtɪŋ/
gyrate: v.
旋转，转动
Moving or causing to move in a circle or spiral, especially quickly.
The spinning top was gyrating rapidly on the table. 旋转的陀螺在桌子上快速旋转。
gyration:/dʒaɪˈreɪʃn/
gyration: n.
旋转，转动
The act of gyrating; a circular or spiral motion.
The gyration of the planets around the sun. 行星绕太阳的旋转。
gyrations:/dʒaɪˈreɪʃnz/
gyration: n.
旋转，转动
The acts of gyrating; circular or spiral motions.
The complex gyrations of the dancers. 舞者复杂的旋转动作。



Etymology: From Latin *gyratus*, past participle of *gyrare* "to turn round in a circle," from Greek *gyros* "circle, ring."

近义词: rotate, spin, whirl, twirl
反义词: still, stationary
