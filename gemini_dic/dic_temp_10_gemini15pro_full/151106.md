parasite:/ˈpærəsaɪt/ | n. | 寄生虫；寄生生物；食客；靠他人为生的人 | An organism that lives in or on another organism (its host) and benefits by deriving nutrients at the other's expense.  A person who habitually relies on or exploits others. |  The dog has intestinal parasites. 这只狗有肠道寄生虫。 |  He's a parasite, always living off other people. 他是个寄生虫，总是靠别人生活。 | symbiont, freeloader | host | noun
