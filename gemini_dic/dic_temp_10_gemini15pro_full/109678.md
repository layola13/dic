leucoplastid: /ˌluːkəˈplæstɪd/ | n. | 白色体 | A colorless plastid in plant cells, typically found in roots and storage tissues. Leucoplastids store starch, oils, or proteins. |  Leucoplastids are essential for storing starches in potato tubers. 白色体对于马铃薯块茎中淀粉的储存至关重要。| synonym: amyloplast, proteinoplast, elaioplast | antonym: chloroplast, chromoplast | noun
