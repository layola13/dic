detasseled:/diːˈtæsəld/
Verb|去雄; 除去(玉米等植物的)雄穗|The corn plants were detasseled to prevent self-pollination. 玉米植株被去雄以防止自花授粉。| The researchers detasseled the plants by hand. 研究人员手工去除了植物的雄穗。| emasculate, top | pollinate |v.
Etymology:  'de-' (prefix meaning "remove") + 'tassel' (referring to the male flower of the corn plant).
