co-exist:/ˌkoʊɪɡˈzɪst/| v.|共存，共处|To live in peace or harmony together, often despite differences.|例句：Different cultures can co-exist peacefully. 不同的文化可以和平共处。|例句：Predators and prey often co-exist in a delicate balance. 捕食者和猎物经常在微妙的平衡中共存。|近义词：cohabit, coexist, live together|反义词:clash, conflict|词性:动词
Etymology: From Latin co- (together) + existere (to exist).
