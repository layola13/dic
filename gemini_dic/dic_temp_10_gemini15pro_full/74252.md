foxier:/ˈfɒksiə(r) (UK), ˈfɑːksiər (US)| adj.|更狡猾的，更漂亮的，更迷人的|Comparative form of "foxy": more cunning, attractive, or sly.|She's gotten foxier with age. 她随着年龄的增长变得更迷人了。|近义词：craftier, slyer, more attractive, sexier|反义词：naive, innocent, plain|词性:形容词

词源:
From "foxy" + "-er" (comparative suffix).  "Foxy" ultimately derives from "fox," referring to the animal's reputation for cunning.
