incorporeity:/ˌɪnkɔːrˈpɔːriəti/
n.
无实体;非物质性;无形性
The state or quality of being incorporeal; lack of material body or form.
Spirits, by their nature, possess incorporeity.  精灵，就其本质而言，拥有无形性。
The philosopher discussed the concept of incorporeity of the soul. 这位哲学家讨论了灵魂的非物质性概念。
近义词: immateriality, intangibility, bodilessness, spirituality
反义词: corporeality, materiality, physicality
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *incorporeitas, from incorporeus (“incorporeal”) + -itas (“-ity”).  Incorporeus is from in- (“not”) + corporeus (“bodily, corporeal”), from corpus (“body”).
