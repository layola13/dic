crustily:/ˈkrʌstɪli/| adv.|粗暴地，暴躁地，唐突地|In a crusty or surly manner; gruffly.  Showing irritation.|He crustily replied, "Leave me alone."  他粗暴地回答：“让我一个人待着。”|Synonyms: gruffly, surlily, churlishly, irascibly|Antonyms: politely, kindly, gently|adverb

crusty:/ˈkrʌsti/| adj.|易怒的，暴躁的；（食物）有硬壳的|1. (of a person) bad-tempered and irritable. 2. (of food) having a hard or crisp surface.|1. He gave me a crusty look. 他给了我一个恼怒的眼神。 2. The bread had a delicious crusty crust.  这面包有一层美味的硬皮。|Synonyms:  (for person) irritable, grumpy, cantankerous; (for food) crisp, crunchy|Antonyms:  (for person) good-natured, pleasant; (for food) soft, soggy|adjective
