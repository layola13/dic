impressionistic:/ɪmˈprɛʃənˈɪstɪk/
adjective
印象派的；给人印象的
relating to or characteristic of Impressionism, an artistic style that seeks to capture a feeling or experience rather than to achieve accurate depiction.  Also, based on a general feeling rather than on specific facts.
Her painting style is very impressionistic. 她的绘画风格非常印象派。
The report was rather impressionistic, lacking hard data. 这份报告相当印象主义，缺乏确凿的数据。
近义词: evocative, suggestive, atmospheric
反义词: realistic, precise, detailed
词性: adjective
