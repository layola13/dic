ineffability:/ˌɪnɛfəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可言喻，难以表达
The quality of being too great or extreme to be expressed or described in words.
The ineffability of religious experience. 宗教体验的不可言喻性。

The artist tried to capture the ineffability of the sunset. 这位艺术家试图捕捉日落的不可言喻之美。


inexpressibility, unspeakableness, indescribability
communicability, expressiveness, describability
