excommunications:/ˌɛks kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃənz/
n.
逐出教会；绝罚；开除教籍
The act of officially excluding someone from participation in the sacraments and services of the Christian Church.
The Pope threatened the King with excommunication. 教皇威胁要将国王逐出教会。
近义词：exclusion, expulsion, banishment
反义词：inclusion, admittance, readmission
词性: 名词 (plural)
