inactive:/ɪnˈæktɪv/| adj.|不活动的，闲置的，惰性的|Not active or in use; not working, operating, or progressing.|The volcano has been inactive for centuries. 这座火山已经沉寂了几个世纪了。|近义词：idle, dormant, inert, passive|反义词：active, dynamic, energetic|词性:形容词
词源:  Late Middle English: from in- (expressing negation) + active.
