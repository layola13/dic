overpayments:/ˌoʊvərˈpeɪmənts/
n.
多付款；超额付款
The act of paying more than is due or required.
The company issued refunds for all overpayments.  公司为所有多付款项发放了退款。
synonyms: excess payments, surplus payments, reimbursements
antonyms: underpayments, short payments, debts
noun
