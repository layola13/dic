dhuti:/ˈduːti/ /ˈdʊti/| n.|男士缠腰布，印度传统服饰|A long piece of cloth worn by men wrapped around the lower body, traditionally in India. |He wore a simple white dhuti. 他穿着一件简单的白色缠腰布。|近义词：dhoti, lunghi|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps Western clothing)|noun
