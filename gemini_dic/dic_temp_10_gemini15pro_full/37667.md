compactible:/kəmˈpæktɪbl/ | adj. | 可压缩的，可压实的，可精简的 | Able to be made smaller by being pressed or packed together; capable of being reduced in size or volume. | This sleeping bag is highly compactible, making it easy to carry in a backpack.  这个睡袋可以高度压缩，便于放在背包里携带。

The data is compactible into a smaller file size.  这些数据可以压缩成更小的文件大小。 | 近义词：compressible, condensable, reducible | 反义词：incompressible, noncompactible, uncompressible | 词性: 形容词


词源: From compact + -ible.  由compact（紧凑的） + -ible（可...的）组成。
