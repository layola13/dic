excesses:/ˈɛksɛsɪz/
n.
过度；过量；无节制；放肆行为；暴行
an instance of going beyond limits
例句：His excesses at the party caused a scandal. 他在派对上的放肆行为引起了丑闻。
例句：The excesses of the regime led to its downfall. 该政权的暴行导致了它的垮台。
近义词：surpluses, immoderations, extravagances, overindulgences
反义词：shortages, deficiencies, moderations

词性: 名词 (plural of excess)
词源: From Old French exces, from Latin excessus “a going out, departure, digression; excess,” past participle of excedere “to go out, depart, withdraw; exceed, surpass,” from ex- “out” + cedere “to go, yield.”
