incumber:/ɪnˈkʌmbər/| vt.| 阻碍，妨碍，拖累|To burden or weigh down; to hinder or impede the function or activity of.|The company is incumbered with debt. 这家公司负债累累。|近义词：hinder, impede, obstruct, burden|反义词:aid, assist, help|verb

incumbrance:/ɪnˈkʌmbrəns/| n.|累赘，妨碍，阻碍物|Something that burdens, hinders, or obstructs; a burden or impediment.|The high taxes are an incumbrance on the business.  高额税收是企业的负担。|近义词：burden, obstacle, impediment, hindrance|反义词:advantage, aid, asset, help|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *encombrer*, from Late Latin *incombrare* "to block up," from *in-* "in" + *combrus* "barrier, obstacle."
