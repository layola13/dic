archbutler: /ˈɑːrtʃˌbʌtlər/ | n. | 总管，大管家 | A chief butler; the head butler in a large household. |  The archbutler oversaw all the staff and ensured the smooth running of the estate.  总管负责监督所有员工并确保庄园的顺利运转。

The archbutler announced the arrival of the distinguished guests.  总管宣布贵宾的到来。 | 近义词: head butler, major-domo, steward | 反义词: servant, underling | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  "arch-" (from Greek *arkhos "leader, ruler, chief") + "butler" (from Old French *buteillier, meaning "bottle-keeper," ultimately from Latin *buticula "bottle").  The prefix "arch-" intensifies the meaning of "butler" to signify the chief or principal butler.
