communicant:/kəˈmjuːnɪkənt/| n.|领圣餐者，交流者|A person who receives Holy Communion.  Also, more generally, a person who communicates.|例句：The communicants knelt at the altar rail. 领圣餐者跪在祭坛栏杆前。

例句：The communicants shared their experiences with each other. 交流者彼此分享了他们的经历。|近义词：partaker, participant, communicator|反义词:non-communicant|noun
