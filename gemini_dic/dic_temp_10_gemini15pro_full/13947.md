aunts:/ɑːnts/ | n. |阿姨们；姑妈们；舅妈们|The plural form of "aunt," referring to more than one female relative of a parent.  It encompasses the sisters of one's parents, as well as the wives of their brothers. |My aunts are coming to visit next week. 我的阿姨们下周要来看我。|
