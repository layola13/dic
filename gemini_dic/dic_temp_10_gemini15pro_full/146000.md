overdominated:/ˌoʊvərˈdɒmɪneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过度支配的，被压倒的 | Being excessively controlled or influenced by a particular person or thing. |  The market is overdominated by a few large companies.  市场被几家大公司过度支配。| 近义词: controlled, dominated, overpowered | 反义词: independent, free, autonomous | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "dominate" (from Latin "dominari" - to rule, control) + "-ed" (past participle suffix)
