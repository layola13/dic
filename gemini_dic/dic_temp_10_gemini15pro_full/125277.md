multifeatured:/ˌmʌltiˈfiːtʃərd/
adjective
多功能的，多特色的
having many features
This multifeatured software can handle various tasks, from photo editing to video processing.  这款多功能软件可以处理各种任务，从照片编辑到视频处理。
例句：The new phone is multifeatured and includes a camera, GPS, and internet access. 这款新手机功能多样，包括相机、GPS和互联网接入。
近义词：versatile, multifaceted, comprehensive
反义词：simple, basic, limited
词性：adjective
