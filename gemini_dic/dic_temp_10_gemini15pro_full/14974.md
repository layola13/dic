baba:/ˈbɑːbɑː/| n.|爸爸；(儿语)父亲；|A child's word for father.|例句：The baby called out "baba". 婴儿叫着“爸爸”。|近义词：dad, father, daddy, papa, pop|反义词:mother, mom|noun

baba:/ˈbɑːbɑː/| n.|巴巴，东正教长老；|A title of respect for an elderly man, especially a holy man or hermit, in certain Eastern Christian churches.|例句：The baba offered wise counsel. 巴巴长老提供了明智的建议。|近义词：elder, sage, monk|反义词: (No direct antonym, depends on context.  Perhaps "youth" or "novice")|noun

baba:/ˈbɑːbɑː/| n.|巴巴(一种甜点)；|A type of yeast cake, especially popular in Eastern Europe.|例句：She baked a delicious baba for dessert. 她烤了一个美味的巴巴蛋糕作为甜点。|近义词：cake, yeast cake, brioche (somewhat similar)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
