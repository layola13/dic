denied: /dɪˈnaɪd/ | v. | 否认，拒绝 | to state that something is not true; to refuse to admit something | He denied stealing the money. 他否认偷了钱。|  近义词: refuted, rejected, contradicted | 反义词: admitted, confessed, acknowledged | verb

denied: /dɪˈnaɪd/ | adj. | 被拒绝的 | refused or not allowed | He was denied access to the building. 他被拒绝进入大楼。 | 近义词: refused, rejected | 反义词: granted, allowed, permitted | adjective
