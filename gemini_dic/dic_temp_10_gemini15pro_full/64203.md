equicrural:/ˌiːkwɪˈkrʊərəl/
adjective
等边的；等腰的
Having equal legs or sides, especially (of a triangle) having two equal sides.
An equicrural triangle has two sides of equal length.  一个等腰三角形有两条边长度相等。
The building features an equicrural triangular facade.  该建筑的特点是等腰三角形的正面。
近义词:isosceles
反义词:scalene
词性:adjective
