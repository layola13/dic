crystal-like:/ˈkrɪstəlˌlaɪk/| adj.|水晶般的，像水晶一样的|Resembling crystal in transparency, clarity, or brilliance.|The lake had crystal-like waters, allowing us to see the bottom clearly.  湖水清澈如水晶，我们可以清楚地看到湖底。|The ice sculpture was crystal-like in its delicate beauty. 冰雕精致美丽，晶莹剔透。|Synonyms: crystalline, glassy, transparent, clear, limpid|Antonyms: opaque, cloudy, murky|adjective
