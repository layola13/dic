nonprogrammatic:/ˌnɒnˌprəʊɡrəˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 非程序化的，非编程的 | Not relating to or determined by computer programming; not following a strict, predetermined set of instructions. |  Example: Nonprogrammatic advertising relies on manual insertion and negotiation rather than automated systems.  非程序化广告依赖于手动插入和协商，而不是自动化系统。 | Example:  They took a nonprogrammatic approach to problem-solving, brainstorming freely instead of following a rigid protocol.  他们采用非程序化的方法来解决问题，自由地进行头脑风暴，而不是遵循僵化的协议。| Synonyms: manual, bespoke, custom, non-automated | Antonyms: programmatic, automated, algorithmic | word type: adjective
