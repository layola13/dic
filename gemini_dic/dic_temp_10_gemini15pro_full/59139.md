dynamization:/ˌdaɪnəmaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |活力化；激活；赋能|The process of making something more dynamic or energetic.  The dynamization of the classroom through interactive activities engaged the students. | Example: The dynamization of the local economy brought new jobs and opportunities.  当地经济的活力化带来了新的就业机会。

Example: The coach's dynamization of the team's strategy led to a winning streak. 教练对球队战略的激活带来了连胜。| Near Synonyms:  invigoration, stimulation, energizing, activation | Antonyms: stagnation, suppression, weakening | noun
