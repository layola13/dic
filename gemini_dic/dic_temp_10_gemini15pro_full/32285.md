chiefest:/ˈtʃiːfɪst/| adj.|最主要的，最重要的 (古语，现已很少使用)|Most important; principal (archaic and now rarely used).|Of all his works, this is considered the chiefest. 在他所有的作品中，这被认为是最重要的。|Synonyms: principal, foremost, leading, paramount, preeminent|Antonyms: least important, minor, insignificant|adjective
