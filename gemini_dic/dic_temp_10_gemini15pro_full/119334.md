microbrewers:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌbruːərz/
n.
小型酿酒商；精酿啤酒厂
People who own or operate microbreweries, small-scale breweries that produce limited quantities of beer.
Many microbrewers focus on using local ingredients and experimenting with unique flavors. 许多小型酿酒商专注于使用当地原料和尝试独特的口味。
近义词: craft brewers, independent brewers
反义词: macrobrewers, large-scale breweries
词性: noun (plural)

microbrewer:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌbruːər/
n.
小型酿酒商；精酿啤酒厂
