overprizing: /ˌoʊvərˈpraɪzɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
高估；过分重视
The act of valuing something more than it is actually worth.
He was overprizing his old car, thinking it was a classic.  他高估了他的旧车，以为它是辆经典款。
They were overprizing the importance of the competition. 他们过分重视了这场比赛的重要性。
近义词: overvaluing, overestimating
反义词: undervaluing, underestimating
词性: v. (present participle)


overprize: /ˌoʊvərˈpraɪz/
verb (transitive)
高估；过分重视
To value something more than it is actually worth.
Don't overprize your abilities; stay humble. 不要高估你的能力；保持谦虚。
The market is overprizing these tech stocks. 市场高估了这些科技股。
近义词: overvalue, overestimate
反义词: undervalue, underestimate
词性: v. (transitive)


词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "prize" (verb meaning "to value highly")
