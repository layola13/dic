bombast:/ˈbɒmbæst/ (US) /ˈbɒmbɑːst/ (UK)| n.|夸大其词，浮夸的言辞|High-sounding language with little meaning, used to impress people.|His speech was full of bombast. 他的演讲充满了夸夸其谈。|近义词：bluster, rhetoric, hyperbole|反义词:understatement, modesty|noun

bombastic:/bɒmˈbæstɪk/ (US) /bɒmˈbæstɪk/ (UK)| adj.|夸大的，言过其实的|Using high-sounding but meaningless language.|The CEO delivered a bombastic speech about the company's future. 首席执行官就公司的未来发表了一番夸夸其谈的演讲。|近义词：inflated, pompous, overblown|反义词:simple, understated, modest|adjective
