flabby:/ˈflæbi/| adj.|松弛的，肥胖的，软弱的|Lacking firmness or elasticity; soft and loose.  Characterized by weakness or lack of determination.|His flabby arms jiggled as he waved.  他的胳膊挥舞时松弛地晃动着。|Synonyms: soft, loose, flaccid, limp, weak, feeble|Antonyms: firm, toned, strong, muscular, resolute|adjective

flabby:/ˈflæbi/| adj.|软弱无力的|Lacking force, vigor, or effectiveness.|His flabby handshake conveyed a lack of confidence.  他软弱无力的握手表明他缺乏自信。|Synonyms: weak, feeble, ineffective, spineless|Antonyms: strong, forceful, effective, resolute|adjective
