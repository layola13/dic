nonpermeable:/ˌnɒnpɜːrˈmiːəbl/
adjective
不可渗透的
Not allowing fluids or gases to pass through.
This special fabric is nonpermeable to water.  这种特殊织物不透水。
The membrane is nonpermeable to certain ions.  这种膜对某些离子是不渗透的。

Synonym:
impermeable, impervious

Antonym:
permeable, porous

Etymology:
From non- + permeable, from Latin permeabilis, from permeare "to pass through," from per- "through" + meare "to pass, go."
