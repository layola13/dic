macarize: /ˈmækəˌraɪz/
verb
使幸福；使有福；赐福
to cause to be blessed or blissful; to make supremely happy.
She felt macarized by the birth of her child. 她因孩子的出生而感到无比幸福。
近义词: bless, beatify, felicitate
反义词: curse, condemn, afflict
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *macariser*, from Provençal *macarar*, from *macar*, meaning "blessed, happy, fortunate". Ultimately from Late Latin *macar(ius)* “blessed, happy," from Greek *μάκαρ (makar)* "blessed, happy."
