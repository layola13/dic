bluets:/ˈbluːɪts/| n. |矢车菊；蓝铃花；淡蓝色|A small plant with delicate blue flowers, typically of the genus Houstonia.  Often used in the plural to refer to a group or patch of these flowers. |She found a patch of bluets blooming by the stream. 她发现小溪边有一片盛开的矢车菊。|Synonyms: Quaker ladies, innocence, azure bluet|Antonyms: N/A|noun

bluets:/ˈbluːɪts/| adj. |淡蓝色的|Having a pale blue color.|Her dress was a delicate shade of bluets. 她的连衣裙是淡蓝色的。|Synonyms: baby blue, powder blue, light blue|Antonyms: dark blue, navy blue|adjective
