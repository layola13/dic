bassness:/ˈbeɪsnəs/| n.|低音的程度；低沉的音质|The bassness of his voice was surprising. 他的嗓音低沉得令人惊讶。|例句：The bassness of the music vibrated through the floor.  音乐的低音震动着地板。

The speaker lacked bassness, making the music sound tinny.  扬声器缺乏低音，使音乐听起来很单薄。|近义词：depth, richness, resonance, low-end|反义词:trebleness, highness, shrillness|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From bass + -ness.  "Bass" comes from the Italian "basso" meaning "low". "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
