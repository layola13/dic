merganser:/mərˈɡænsər/ | n. | 潜水鸭，秋沙鸭 | A fish-eating duck that dives underwater. |  例句：The merganser dived swiftly to catch a fish. 秋沙鸭迅速潜入水中捕鱼。

例句：We observed a merganser preening its feathers by the riverbank. 我们观察到一只秋沙鸭在河岸边梳理羽毛。 | 近义词：sawbill, fish duck | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific type of bird) | 词性: noun
