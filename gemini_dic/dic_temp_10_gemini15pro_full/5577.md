altarage:/ˈɔːltərɪdʒ/
n.
祭坛收入，祭坛供奉
Emoluments or offerings due to a priest for services at the altar.
The altarage from the village church was barely enough to support the priest.  村里教堂的祭坛收入几乎不足以养活牧师。
近义词: offerings, oblations, tithes
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "desecration" in a contextual sense)
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English *alterage*, from Medieval Latin *altaragium*, from Latin *altare* (“altar”) + *-agium* (suffix forming nouns denoting things connected with or produced by what is denoted by the base).
