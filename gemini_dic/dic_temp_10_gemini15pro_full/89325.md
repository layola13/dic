homogenised:/həˈmɒdʒənaɪzd/ /həˈmɑːdʒənaɪzd/| adj.|均质化的，同质化的|Made uniform or consistent throughout.|The milk is homogenised to prevent cream from separating.|Synonyms: uniform, consistent, standardized, similar|Antonyms: heterogeneous, varied, diverse|adjective

homogenize:/həˈmɒdʒənaɪz/ /həˈmɑːdʒənaɪzd/| v.|使均匀，使均质|To make something uniform or consistent throughout.|The company aims to homogenize its products across all markets.|Synonyms: standardize, blend, mix, unify|Antonyms: diversify, vary, differentiate|verb
