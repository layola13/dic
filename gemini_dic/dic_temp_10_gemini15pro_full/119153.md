mewled:/mjuːld/ | v. |  低声哭泣，发出喵叫声 | to cry weakly, like a baby or kitten; to make a sound like a cat | The newborn baby mewled softly in its mother's arms. 新生儿在母亲的怀里轻轻地低声哭泣。| The kitten mewled pitifully, lost and alone. 小猫可怜地喵喵叫着，孤单无助。 | synonyms: whimpered, cried, meowed, wailed | antonyms: roared, shouted, bellowed | verb
