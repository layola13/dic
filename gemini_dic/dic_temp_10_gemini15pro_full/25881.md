burlesqued: /bərˈlɛskt/ | v. (past tense)|滑稽地模仿；嘲弄地模仿|To imitate in a humorous or satirical way, often by exaggerating the original's characteristics.|The comedian burlesqued the president's mannerisms. 这位喜剧演员滑稽地模仿了总统的举止。|The play burlesqued the pompous conventions of grand opera. 这部戏滑稽地模仿了歌剧的浮夸传统。| 近义词: mocked, satirized, parodied, lampooned | 反义词: imitated, honored, respected | 词性: verb

词源: From French *burlesquer*, from Italian *burlesco*, from *burla* "joke, mockery."
