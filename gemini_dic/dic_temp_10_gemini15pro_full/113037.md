luminescences:/ˌluːmɪˈnɛsənsɪz/
|n.|冷光，发光|The emission of light by a substance that has not been heated, as in fluorescence and phosphorescence.  物质未经加热而发光，如荧光和磷光。|The scientists studied the luminescences of the deep-sea creatures. 科学家们研究了深海生物的发光现象。|近义词：glow, shimmer, shine, radiance|反义词：darkness, blackness|词性:名词

luminescence:/ˌluːmɪˈnɛsəns/
|n.|冷光，发光|The emission of light by a substance that has not been heated, as in fluorescence and phosphorescence.  物质未经加热而发光，如荧光和磷光。|The fireflies' luminescence lit up the night. 萤火虫的发光照亮了夜晚。|近义词：glow, shimmer, shine, radiance|反义词：darkness, blackness|词性:名词
