observers:/əbˈzɜːrvərz/ | n. |观察者，观察员|A person who watches or notices something carefully. |例句：The observers noted the bird's unusual behavior. 观察员注意到鸟儿不寻常的行为。

例句：United Nations observers monitored the elections. 联合国观察员监督了选举。|近义词：onlookers, spectators, witnesses, viewers, monitors|反义词：participants, actors|Noun
