gravelled:/ˈɡrævəld/ | adj. | 铺满碎石的；沙哑的(嗓音) | Covered with gravel; (of a voice) husky or rough-sounding. | The driveway was gravelled.  车道上铺满了碎石。|  The singer's gravelled voice added to the song's bluesy feel. 歌手沙哑的嗓音增添了歌曲的蓝调感。 | 近义词: gravelly, stony, hoarse, raspy | 反义词: smooth, paved, clear | 词性: 形容词

gravelled:/ˈɡrævəld/ | v. | 铺碎石；使困惑 | Covered with gravel; To puzzle or confuse |  They gravelled the path to the garden. 他们在通往花园的小路上铺了碎石。 | The complex question gravelled even the experts.  这个复杂的问题甚至让专家们都感到困惑。 | 近义词: confuse, bewilder, perplex, confound | 反义词: clarify, enlighten | 词性: 动词
