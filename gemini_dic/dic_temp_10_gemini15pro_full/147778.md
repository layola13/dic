overpromotes:/ˌoʊvərprəˈmoʊts/
verb
过度晋升，过快提拔
To promote (someone) to a position for which they are not adequately qualified or experienced.
The company overpromoted several young employees who were not ready for the responsibility. 这家公司过度提拔了几名年轻员工，他们还没有准备好承担责任。
近义词: advance prematurely, elevate too quickly
反义词: demote, downgrade
