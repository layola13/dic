infringe:/ɪnˈfrɪndʒ/ | v. | 侵犯，违反 | to actively break the terms of (a law, agreement, etc.); violate | Copying copyrighted material is infringing on the copyright holder's rights.  复制受版权保护的材料侵犯了版权所有者的权利。| They infringed on the neighbor's property by building a fence that extended beyond the property line. 他们通过建造一个超出房产线的栅栏侵犯了邻居的财产。 | synonyms: violate, breach, encroach, trespass | antonyms: comply with, uphold, respect | verb
