microseme: /ˈmaɪkroʊsiːm/ | n. | 微义; 微素 | A microseme is a minimal unit of meaning, often used in componential analysis to describe the smallest semantic features that distinguish words. It's a theoretical concept used in linguistics. |  The microsemes "animate" and "inanimate" distinguish "dog" and "rock". 狗和石头的区别在于微义“有生命”和“无生命”。 |  The linguist analyzed the word using microsemes to understand its core meaning. 语言学家使用微义来分析这个词，以理解它的核心意义。 | 近义词: semantic feature, seme | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists as it's a theoretical unit of meaning.) | 词性: noun
