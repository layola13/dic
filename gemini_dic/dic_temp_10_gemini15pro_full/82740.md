guild: /ɡɪld/ | n. | 行会，协会，同业公会 | An association of people with similar interests or pursuits, especially merchants or craftspeople in the Middle Ages. |  The carpenter's guild was very powerful in the town. 木匠行会在镇上很有势力。

The writer's guild negotiated a new contract. 作家协会协商了一份新合同。

近义词: association, society, union, league | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 non-member group 或 independent individuals) | 词性: 名词
