depravers:/dɪˈpreɪvərz/| n.|堕落者，败坏者|People who corrupt or make others worse morally. Those who cause a decline in moral standards. |The depravers of youth were brought to justice.  败坏青少年的那些人被绳之以法。| Synonyms: corrupters, perverters, degraders| Antonyms: reformers, improvers, elevators| Noun


deprave:/dɪˈpreɪv/| vt.|使堕落，败坏|To make someone morally bad; to corrupt. To reduce the quality or strength of something. |Exposure to violence can deprave young minds. 接触暴力会败坏年轻人的思想。 The dictator depraved the legal system for his own benefit. 独裁者为了自己的利益败坏了法律制度。| Synonyms: corrupt, pervert, degrade, debauch| Antonyms: improve, elevate, uplift, purify| Verb
