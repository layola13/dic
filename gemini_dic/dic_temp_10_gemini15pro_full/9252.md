antipathy:/ænˈtɪpəθi/
n.|反感，厌恶|A deep-seated feeling of aversion or dislike.|She felt a strong antipathy towards her boss. 她对她的老板感到强烈的反感。|近义词：aversion, dislike, hostility, animosity, antagonism|反义词：affinity, liking, fondness, sympathy|词性:名词

Etymology: From Late Latin *antipathia*, from Ancient Greek ἀντιπάθεια (antipátheia, “mutual dislike, aversion”), from ἀντί (antí, “against, opposite”) + πάθος (páthos, “suffering, feeling”).
