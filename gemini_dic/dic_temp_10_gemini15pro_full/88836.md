hogmanay: /ˌhɒɡməˈneɪ/ (UK), /ˌhɑːɡməˈneɪ/ (US)| n. |除夕，新年庆祝活动|A Scottish celebration of the New Year.|例句：We always have a big party for Hogmanay. 我们总是为除夕举办一个盛大的派对。|例句：Hogmanay celebrations in Scotland are famous worldwide. 苏格兰的除夕庆祝活动世界闻名。|近义词：New Year's Eve, New Year celebration|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology:
From Scots <i>Hogmanay</i>, possibly from Anglo-Norman <i>*aguillanneuf</i>, from Old French <i>aguillanneuf</i> ("gift given on New Year's Day"), of uncertain origin.
