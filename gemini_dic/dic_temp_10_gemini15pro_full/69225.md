fearmongers: /ˈfɪəmʌŋɡərz/
noun
散布恐慌的人；危言耸听者
People who deliberately spread fear and anxiety; alarmists.
The fearmongers are predicting economic collapse. 这些散布恐慌的人预测经济会崩溃。
近义词: alarmists, scaremongers, doomsayers
反义词: reassurers, optimists
词性: noun (plural)

fearmonger: /ˈfɪəmʌŋɡər/
noun
散布恐慌的人；危言耸听者
A person who deliberately spreads fear and anxiety; an alarmist.
He's just a fearmonger trying to scare people. 他只是一个危言耸听的人，试图吓唬别人。
近义词: alarmist, scaremonger, doomsayer
反义词: reassurer, optimist
词性: noun

Etymology:
From fear + monger (from Middle English *mongere, from Old English mangere "merchant, trader," from Proto-Germanic *mangari, from Latin mango "dealer, trader")
