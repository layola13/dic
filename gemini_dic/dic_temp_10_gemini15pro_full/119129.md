metroscope:/ˈmɛtrəˌskoʊp/| n.|都市万花镜；城市风光影片；（尤指）城市风貌的电影|A device or film presenting a rapid succession of views of city life, often used for entertainment. |Example: The metroscope offered a thrilling glimpse into the bustling streets of 1920s New York. 都市万花镜让人们得以一窥20世纪20年代纽约繁华街道的激动人心的景象。

Example:  The old metroscope film captured the energy and vibrancy of a bygone era.  这部古老的城市风貌影片捕捉到了过去时代的活力和生机。|近义词：kaleidoscope, cityscape film|反义词: None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From metro- (relating to a metropolis or large city) + -scope (an instrument for viewing).
