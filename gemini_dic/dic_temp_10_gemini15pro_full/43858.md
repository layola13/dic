crescent:/ˈkrɛsənt/| n. adj.|新月，月牙形，新月状的|A curved shape wider in the middle and tapering to points at the ends, resembling the moon in its first or last quarter.  The symbol of Islam. |例句：The crescent moon shone brightly in the night sky. 新月在夜空中闪耀。

例句：The bakery makes delicious crescent rolls. 这家面包店制作美味的新月形面包卷。|近义词：curve, arc, half-moon, meniscus|反义词：full moon, gibbous|词性:名词, 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *cressant*, present participle of *creistre* "to grow," from Latin *crescere* "to grow, increase".  The connection to the moon is through its waxing phase.
