condolences:/kənˈdoʊlənsɪz/
n.
慰问，吊唁
an expression of sympathy, especially on the occasion of a death.
Please accept my sincere condolences on the death of your father. 请接受我对您父亲去世的诚挚慰问。
近义词：sympathy, commiserations
反义词:congratulations
词性:名词(plural)
