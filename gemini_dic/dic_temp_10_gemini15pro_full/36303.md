cohesion:/koʊˈhiːʒən/ /kəʊˈhiːʒən/| n.|凝聚力，结合力，内聚力|The cohesion of the group was remarkable, despite their different backgrounds.  这个群体的凝聚力很强，尽管他们的背景各不相同。|The molecules are held together by cohesive forces. 分子通过内聚力结合在一起。|近义词：unity, coherence, adhesion, solidarity|反义词：division, disunity, fragmentation|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin cohaesio, from cohaerere "to cleave together," from co- "together" + haerere "to stick, cling".
