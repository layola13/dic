fibrillary:/ˈfɪbrɪləri/ /faɪˈbrɪləri/ | adj. | 纤维状的；由纤维组成的 | Composed of or resembling fibrils; characterized by fibrils. |  Amyloid plaques and fibrillary tangles are hallmarks of Alzheimer's disease.  淀粉样斑块和纤维缠结是阿尔茨海默病的标志。 | synonymous: fibrous, filamentous, threadlike | antonymous: nonfibrous, amorphous | adjective

fibril:/ˈfaɪbrəl/ /ˈfɪbrɪl/ | n. | 细纤维；原纤维 | A small or slender fiber; a fine thread-like structure. | Collagen fibrils provide tensile strength to tissues. 胶原纤维为组织提供抗拉强度。| synonymous: fiber, filament, thread | antonymous: N/A | noun
