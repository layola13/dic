horse-like:/ˈhɔːrslaɪk/| adj.|像马的，马似的|Resembling a horse in appearance, behavior, or qualities. |The creature had a horse-like head and a lion's body.  这个生物长着像马的头和狮子的身体。|The powerful, horse-like legs of the ostrich allow it to run at high speeds. 鸵鸟强壮的、像马一样的腿使它能高速奔跑。|近义词：equine, equestrian|反义词: unlike a horse|词性:形容词
