husher:/ˈhʌʃər/| n.|压制者；使安静的人或物|A person or thing that hushes or silences. |The librarian acted as a husher, quieting the noisy students. 图书管理员充当了压制者的角色，让吵闹的学生安静下来。| The thick curtains acted as a husher, blocking out the street noise. 厚厚的窗帘起到隔音的作用，阻挡了街上的噪音。|Synonyms: silencer, quieter, suppressor|Antonyms: amplifier, encourager|noun
