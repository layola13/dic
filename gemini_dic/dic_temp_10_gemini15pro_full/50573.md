deracinate:/ˌdiːˈræsɪneɪt/ | n. | 被迫离开家园的人；| A person who has been deracinated. | The deracinates longed for their homeland. 这些被迫离开家园的人渴望回到故土。| synonyms: exile, refugee, displaced person | antonyms: native, resident, citizen | noun
