have-no-words-for:/hæv no wɜːrdz fɔːr/ | idiom |  难以言表；无以言表；无法形容 |  This idiom is used to express the inability to adequately describe something, often due to its intensity or complexity. | The beauty of the sunset was such that I have no words for it.  夕阳的美丽让我难以言表。 | synonyms: speechless, dumbfounded, at a loss for words, beyond words | antonyms: articulate, eloquent | idiom
