imagists:/ˈɪmɪdʒɪsts/| n.|意象派诗人|Members of a  movement in early 20th-century Anglo-American poetry that favored precision of imagery and clear, sharp language.|Ezra Pound was a key figure in the Imagist movement.  埃兹拉·庞德是意象派运动的关键人物。|Synonyms: Modernists, avant-garde poets|Antonyms: Traditionalists|Noun

imagism:/ˈɪmɪdʒɪzəm/| n.|意象主义|A literary movement that flourished between 1912 and 1917, emphasizing precision and clarity of image and language over traditional rhyme and rhythm.|Imagism significantly influenced the development of Modernist poetry. 意象主义极大地影响了现代主义诗歌的发展。|Synonyms: Modernism (in poetry)|Antonyms: Traditionalism (in poetry)|Noun
