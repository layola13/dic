effacer:/e.fa.se/ (French)| v. |擦掉，抹去，清除|To erase, rub out, or obliterate something, often a mark, writing, or memory.|The artist effaced the unwanted lines from his sketch. 艺术家把他草图中不需要的线条擦掉了。|Synonyms: erase, obliterate, expunge, delete, remove|Antonyms: preserve, retain, maintain|verb
