mislocating:/ˌmɪsləʊˈkeɪtɪŋ/ /ˌmɪsloʊˈkeɪtɪŋ/
verb
放错地方；错误定位
putting something in the wrong place or identifying its location incorrectly
I seem to have mislocated my keys. 我好像把钥匙放错地方了。
misplace, lose
find, locate
