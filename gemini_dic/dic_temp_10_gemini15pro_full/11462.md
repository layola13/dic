argumentative: /ˌɑːrɡjʊˈmentətɪv/ | adj. | 好争辩的，爱争论的 | Given to expressing divergent opinions; inclined to dispute or disagree. |  He's so argumentative; it's impossible to have a peaceful conversation with him. 他太好争辩了，和他根本没法好好说话。|  She became argumentative when challenged on her views. 当她的观点受到质疑时，她变得好争辩起来。|  Synonyms: quarrelsome, contentious, disputatious, combative | Antonyms: agreeable, amenable, conciliatory, cooperative | adjective
