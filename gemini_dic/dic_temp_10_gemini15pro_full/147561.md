overpeopled:/ˌoʊvərˈpiːpəld/ | adj. |人口过剩的，人口稠密的 | Having too many people; excessively populated. | The overpeopled city struggled with providing adequate housing and resources. 人口过剩的城市难以提供足够的住房和资源。 |  The small island was becoming increasingly overpeopled.  这个小岛人口越来越稠密。 | 近义词: overcrowded, densely populated | 反义词: underpopulated, sparsely populated | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From over- + peopled, past participle of people.  "Over-" signifies "excessively" or "too much".  "People" (verb) means to populate or inhabit.
