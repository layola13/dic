overhastiness:/ˌoʊvərˈheɪstɪnəs/| n.| 过于匆忙，草率|The overhastiness of his decision led to disastrous consequences. 他的草率决定导致了灾难性的后果。|例句：His overhastiness in selling the stock cost him a fortune. 他匆忙出售股票让他损失了一大笔钱。|近义词：rashness, impetuosity, precipitateness|反义词：caution, deliberation, prudence|词性:名词
词源: From over- + hastiness, from hasty + -ness. Ultimately from the Proto-Germanic *haifstiz.
