identifiers:/aɪˈdɛntɪfaɪərz/ | n. | 标识符 | A name that identifies (that is, labels the identity of) a variable, function, class, module, or other entity in a computer program. | Example:  In the equation "x = 5", "x" is the identifier. 在等式“x = 5”中，“x”是标识符。

Example:  Choosing descriptive identifiers makes your code easier to understand.  选择描述性的标识符使你的代码更容易理解。 | Synonyms: names, labels, tags | Antonyms:  N/A | noun
