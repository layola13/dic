gnathotheca:/ˌnæθəˈθiːkə/
noun
颚鞘；颚膜；包覆颚的鞘
A covering or sheath enclosing the jaws, especially in insects and birds.
The gnatotheca of the beetle protects its delicate mouthparts. 甲壳虫的颚鞘保护着它脆弱的口器。
近义词:jaw sheath,  mandible covering
反义词: (No direct antonym exists, possibly "exposed mandibles")
