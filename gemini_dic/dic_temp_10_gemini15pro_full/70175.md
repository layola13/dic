fibreglass:/ˈfaɪbərɡlæs/ (US) /ˈfaɪbəɡlɑːs/ (UK)| n.|玻璃纤维|A material consisting of extremely fine filaments of glass embedded in various resins, used as a reinforcing agent in plastics, etc., and in thermal and sound insulation.|The boat hull is made of fibreglass.|近义词：glass fiber, fiberglass-reinforced plastic (FRP)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps natural fibers like cotton or wool depending on the context)|noun
