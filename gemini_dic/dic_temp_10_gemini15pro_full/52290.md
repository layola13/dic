dichromatic:/ˌdaɪkrəˈmætɪk/
adjective
二色的；二色性的
Having or exhibiting two colors.  Relating to or characterized by dichromacy (a form of color blindness in which only two of the three primary colors can be distinguished).
The dichromatic bird flashed its red and blue wings.  这只二色鸟闪动着它的红蓝翅膀。
近义词:bicolor, two-colored
反义词:monochromatic, polychromatic
词性:adjective

dichromatic:/ˌdaɪkrəˈmætɪk/
noun
二色视者
A person or animal having dichromacy (a form of color blindness).
Tests revealed that the patient was a dichromatic.  测试显示病人是二色视者。
近义词:bicolor, two-colored
反义词:monochromatic, polychromatic
词性:noun
