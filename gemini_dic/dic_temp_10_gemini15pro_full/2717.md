adsorbers:/ædˈzɔːrbərz/ /ædˈsɔːrbərz/| n. |吸附器；吸附剂|A substance that collects molecules of a gas or liquid on its surface. |Activated carbon is a common adsorber used in water filters.  活性炭是滤水器中常用的吸附剂。

The adsorbers in this system remove impurities from the air.  该系统中的吸附器去除空气中的杂质。|近义词：absorbent, adsorption medium|反义词:desorber|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From *adsorb* + *-er*. *Adsorb* is from *ad-* (to, toward) + *sorb* (to suck in, swallow). *Sorb* ultimately comes from the Latin *sorbēre*.
