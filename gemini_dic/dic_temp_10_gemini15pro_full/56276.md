dogged:/ˈdɒɡɪd/ (UK) /ˈdɔːɡɪd/ (US)| adj.|顽强的，坚持不懈的，锲而不舍的|Having or showing tenacity and grim persistence.|Despite numerous setbacks, her dogged determination finally paid off. 尽管屡次受挫，但她顽强的决心最终得到了回报。|Example: The detective's dogged pursuit of the suspect led to an arrest.  警探对嫌疑人锲而不舍的追捕最终导致了逮捕。

Example:  He showed a dogged refusal to give up. 他表现出坚决不放弃的态度。|Synonyms: persistent, tenacious, determined, resolute, persevering|Antonyms: wavering, hesitant, irresolute, yielding|adjective
