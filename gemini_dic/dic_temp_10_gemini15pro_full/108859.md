lecherously:/ˈlɛtʃərəsli/| adv.|好色地，淫荡地|Showing excessive or offensive sexual desire.|He lecherously eyed the young woman across the bar. 他好色地盯着酒吧对面的年轻女子。|Synonyms: lustfully, lasciviously, lewdly|Antonyms: chastely, purely, respectfully|adverb

Etymology:
From Middle English *lecherousli, from Old French lecheros, from lechier ("to lick"), from Latin lingere ("to lick").  The sense of "licking" evolved to "lustful" over time.
