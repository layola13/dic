counterpetitions:/ˌkaʊntərˈpətɪʃənz/
|n.| 反诉，对立的请愿 |A petition that opposes another petition.|  The community submitted counterpetitions arguing against the proposed zoning changes. 社区提交了反对拟议的分区变更的反诉。| synonyms: counterclaims, counterpleas, protests | antonyms: petitions, appeals, requests | noun


counterpetition:/ˌkaʊntərˈpətɪʃən/
|n.| 反诉，对立的请愿 |A petition that opposes another petition.| The company filed a counterpetition against the lawsuit.  该公司对诉讼提出了反诉。| synonyms: counterclaim, counterplea, protest | antonyms: petition, appeal, request | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- (prefix meaning "against, opposite") + petition (from Old French peticion, from Latin petitio "a seeking, request," from petere "to seek, aim at, go to, attack").
