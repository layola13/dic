overmercifully: /ˌoʊvərˈmɜːrsɪfəli/
| adv. | 过分仁慈地，过度宽容地 |  in an excessively merciful manner; too leniently. | He treated his enemies overmercifully, which some saw as a weakness. 他对敌人过于仁慈，有些人认为这是一种弱点。| The judge was accused of acting overmercifully towards the convicted criminal.  法官被指责对被定罪的罪犯过于仁慈。 | 近义词: excessively mercifully, too leniently, unduly leniently | 反义词: mercilessly, cruelly, harshly | 词性: 副词

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "merciful" (adjective meaning "showing compassion or forgiveness") + "-ly" (adverb suffix)
