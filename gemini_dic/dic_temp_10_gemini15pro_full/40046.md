consummation:/ˌkɑːnsəˈmeɪʃən/ |/ˌkɒnsəˈmeɪʃən/| n. | 完成，实现；圆满；婚姻的圆房|The consummation of the deal took months of negotiation. 这笔交易的完成耗费了数月的谈判。|Example: The signing of the treaty marked the consummation of years of diplomatic effort.  签署条约标志着多年外交努力的圆满成功。

Example:  For many couples, the consummation of their marriage is a significant event. 对许多夫妇来说，婚姻的圆房是一件意义重大的事件。|Synonyms: completion, fulfillment, achievement, realization|Antonyms:  inception, beginning, commencement|noun
