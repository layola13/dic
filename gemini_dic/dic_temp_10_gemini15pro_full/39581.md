conscription:/kənˈskrɪpʃən/| n.|征兵，强制征募|The act of enrolling people compulsorily, typically into the armed services.|Many young men tried to avoid conscription during the war. 战争期间，许多年轻人试图逃避征兵。|Synonyms: draft, enlistment, levy|Antonyms: volunteering, discharge|noun

conscription:/kənˈskrɪpʃən/| n.|征兵，强制征募|Compulsory enrollment of persons especially for military service.|The government introduced conscription to bolster its armed forces. 政府为了加强军队实力，实行了征兵制。|Synonyms: draft, recruitment|Antonyms: voluntary enlistment|noun
