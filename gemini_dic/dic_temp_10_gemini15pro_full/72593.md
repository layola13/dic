fly-by-night:/ˈflaɪ baɪ naɪt/| adj. n.|不可靠的，不稳定的；夜间飞行的；只做一次生意就逃走的商人（或公司）|A fly-by-night operation is one that is unreliable and likely to disappear or cheat people, especially to avoid paying debts.|例句：Be careful with that fly-by-night car dealer; they might disappear with your money. 小心那个不靠谱的汽车经销商，他们可能会卷款潜逃。|例句：They set up a fly-by-night operation selling fake designer handbags. 他们建立了一个出售假名牌手袋的皮包公司。|近义词：unreliable, untrustworthy, shady, disreputable|反义词：reliable, trustworthy, reputable|词性:形容词，名词

fly-by-night:/ˈflaɪ baɪ naɪt/| n.|只做一次生意就逃走的商人（或公司）|A person or business that is dishonest and only stays in a place for a short time.|例句：That fly-by-night took our money and disappeared.  那个骗子拿了我们的钱就消失了。|例句：Many fly-by-nights operate online, making them difficult to track down. 许多皮包公司在网上运作，这使得他们难以追踪。|近义词：fraud, con artist, swindler|反义词：established business|词性:名词
