owner-occupier:/ˈəʊnər ˈɒkjʊpaɪər/ (UK) /ˈoʊnər ˈɑːkjəpaɪər/ (US)| n.|自住业主|A person who owns and lives in a property, as opposed to renting it out. |Example:  The government offered incentives to encourage more owner-occupiers. 政府提供激励措施以鼓励更多自住业主。

Example:  Owner-occupiers often take more pride in maintaining their properties. 自住业主通常更注重维护他们的房产。


|Synonym: homeowner, resident owner|Antonym: tenant, landlord, renter|noun|
