concussional:/kənˈkʌʃənl/
adjective
脑震荡的，由脑震荡引起的
Relating to or resulting from a concussion.
He suffered concussional symptoms after the accident.  他事故后出现了脑震荡症状。
The doctor checked for concussional injuries. 医生检查了脑震荡造成的损伤。
近义词:post-concussive, traumatic
反义词: healthy, uninjured
词性: adjective
