eye-catching:/ˈaɪˌkætʃɪŋ/
adjective
引人注目的；抢眼的；醒目的
Attracting attention; noticeable.
The dress had an eye-catching design. 这件连衣裙有一个引人注目的设计。
synonyms:attractive, noticeable, conspicuous, striking, arresting
antonyms:unnoticeable, dull, drab, inconspicuous
词性:形容词
