canticoy: /kænˈtiːkɔɪ/ | n. | 欢快的歌曲；欢乐的歌唱 | A lively or cheerful song; a joyful singing. | The canticoy of the birds filled the morning air. 鸟儿欢快的歌声充满了早晨的空气。| 近义词: song, melody, tune, carol | 反义词: dirge, lament | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Spanish *cantico* (song, canticle) + *-oy* (suffix indicating abundance or intensity).  Ultimately from Latin *canticum* (song).
