anthropomorphize: /ˌænθrəpəˈmɔːrfaɪz/ | v. | 赋予人性，人格化 | To give human characteristics to something that is not human, such as an animal or an object. | Example 1:  Children often anthropomorphize their toys, giving them names and personalities.  孩子们经常将他们的玩具拟人化，赋予它们名字和个性。

Example 2: The author anthropomorphizes the animals in her stories, making them talk and think like humans.  作者将故事中的动物拟人化，使它们像人类一样说话和思考。 | 近义词: personify, humanize | 反义词: objectify, dehumanize | 词性: 动词
