hydrozoal:/ˌhaɪdrəˈzoʊəl/
adjective
水螅的
Relating to or characteristic of Hydrozoa, a class of aquatic invertebrates.
Hydrozoal colonies can be found attached to rocks and seaweed.  水螅群体可以在附着在岩石和海藻上找到。
The Portuguese man-of-war is a complex hydrozoal organism. 葡萄牙战舰是一种复杂的水螅生物。
近义词: cnidarian 
反义词: (No specific antonym)
词性: adjective

hydrozoan:/ˌhaɪdrəˈzoʊən/
noun
水螅动物
An invertebrate animal belonging to the class Hydrozoa.
Many hydrozoans form colonies. 许多水螅动物会形成群体。
Hydra is a common freshwater hydrozoan. 水螅是一种常见的淡水水螅动物。
近义词: cnidarian
反义词: (No specific antonym)
词性: noun
