doled:/doʊld/| v.|施舍，少量发放|to give out sparingly or reluctantly, often money or food to the needy. |The government doled out meager rations to the refugees. 政府向难民分发了少量口粮。|Synonyms: distributed, dispensed, apportioned|Antonyms: hoarded, withheld|verb
doled:/doʊld/| v.|悲叹，哀悼 (古语，罕用) | to grieve; lament |  They doled their misfortunes. 他们悲叹自己的不幸。 | Synonyms: mourned, lamented, grieved | Antonyms: rejoiced, celebrated | verb
