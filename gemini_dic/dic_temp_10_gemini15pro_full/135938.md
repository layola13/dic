nonpurchase:/ˌnɑːnˈpɜːrtʃəs/ /ˌnɒnˈpɜːtʃəs/ | n. |非购买，未购买|The act of not buying something; a product or service that a consumer chooses not to buy. |  The survey tracked consumer nonpurchases as well as purchases. 这项调查追踪了消费者的未购买以及购买情况。| synonyms: abstention from buying, refusal to buy | antonyms: purchase, acquisition | noun

nonpurchase:/ˌnɑːnˈpɜːrtʃəs/ /ˌnɒnˈpɜːtʃəs/ | adj. |非购买的|Relating to or characterized by not buying.  |  The nonpurchase rate for this product is surprisingly high. 这个产品的未购买率高得惊人。 | synonyms: unbought, unpurchased | antonyms: purchased, bought | adjective
