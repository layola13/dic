graining:/ˈɡreɪnɪŋ/| n.|纹理，木纹，颗粒感|The graining on the fake wood was surprisingly realistic.  人造木头的纹理出奇地逼真。|Example: The painter skillfully imitated the graining of oak. 画家巧妙地模仿了橡木的纹理。|Synonyms: texture, grain, finish|Antonyms: smoothness, flatness|noun
graining:/ˈɡreɪnɪŋ/| n.|磨光，磨粒|The graining process removes imperfections from the surface. 磨光过程可以去除表面瑕疵。|Example:  The fine graining of the sandpaper produced a smooth finish.  细砂纸的磨粒产生了光滑的表面。|Synonyms: polishing, sanding, grinding|Antonyms: roughening|noun
graining:/ˈɡreɪnɪŋ/| n.|（摄影）颗粒，噪点|The old photo had a lot of graining.  这张老照片有很多颗粒。|Example: Excessive graining can ruin a photograph. 过多的颗粒会毁掉一张照片。|Synonyms: graininess, noise, artifacts|Antonyms: clarity, sharpness|noun
