missuccess: /ˌmɪsɪkˈsɛs/
n.
lack of success; failure
The project was a missuccess due to poor planning.  这个项目由于计划不周而失败了。
He attributed his missuccess to a lack of focus. 他将自己的失败归咎于缺乏专注力。
近义词: failure, flop, debacle, fiasco
反义词: success, triumph, victory
词性: 名词


词源:  "missuccess" is a blend of "mis-" (meaning "bad, wrong," from Old French and ultimately Latin) and "success."  It's a relatively modern formation and less common than "failure."
