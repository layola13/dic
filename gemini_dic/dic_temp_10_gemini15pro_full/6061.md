amenorrhoea (British English): /əˌmenəˈriːə/ | n. | 闭经，无月经 | Absence of menstruation. |  The doctor diagnosed her with secondary amenorrhoea. 医生诊断她患有继发性闭经。| 近义词:  menstrual cessation,  menostasis | 反义词: menstruation | 词性: 名词
