disestablishes:/ˌdɪsɪˈstæblɪʃɪz/
verb
废除；取消…的建制；解除…的正式地位
To officially end a system, practice, or institution.
The Church of England was disestablished in Wales in 1920.  1920年，威尔士教会脱离了英国国教。
The act disestablishes the city's housing authority.  该法案解除了该市住房管理局的正式地位。
synonyms: abolish, annul, revoke, repeal, dismantle
antonyms: establish, found, institute, set up
词性: 动词
