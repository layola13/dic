denominators:/dɪˈnɒmɪneɪtəz/ | n. |分母|The number below the line in a common fraction; a divisor.|
The denominator of the fraction ¾ is 4. |分数 ¾ 的分母是 4。|
Common multiples can help you add fractions with different denominators. |公倍数可以帮助你将不同分母的分数相加。|
Synonyms: divisor |
Antonyms: numerator |
