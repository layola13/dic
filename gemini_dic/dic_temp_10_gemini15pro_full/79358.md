glenlike:/ˈɡlɛnlaɪk/
adjective
像峡谷的; 峡谷似的
Resembling a glen; narrow and secluded.
The path led through a glenlike valley. 这条小路穿过一个像峡谷的山谷。
近义词: canyonlike, valleylike, ravinelike
反义词: open, exposed
词性: adjective
