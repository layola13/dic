pastries:/ˈpeɪstrɪz/| n. |糕点，甜点|Small, sweet baked goods often made of flour, butter, sugar, and eggs, such as cakes, tarts, and pies.|例句：The bakery displayed a tempting array of pastries.  这家面包店展示了一系列诱人的糕点。

例句：She has a weakness for cream-filled pastries. 她偏爱奶油馅的糕点。|近义词：baked goods, sweets, treats, confections|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider savory dishes or main courses) |词性:noun


Etymology: From Old French *pastrie* (Modern French *pâtisserie*), from paste. Ultimately from Latin *pasta* "dough, paste".
