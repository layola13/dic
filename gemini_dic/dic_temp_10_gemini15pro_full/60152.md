adjective
有效的，奏效的
Producing a desired effect; effective.
例句：The medicine was effectuous in relieving her pain.  这种药有效地缓解了她的疼痛。

例句：His effectuous leadership led the team to victory. 他有效的领导带领团队走向了胜利。

近义词：effective, efficient, potent, powerful
反义词：ineffective, inefficient, weak, futile
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From Late Latin *effectuosus, from Latin effectus (“effect, accomplishment, result”) + -osus (suffix forming adjectives meaning ‘full of, abounding in’).  Effectuous is related to the more common word "effective".
