overwasted:/ˌoʊvərˈweɪstɪd/ | adj. | 过度浪费的，过度挥霍的 |  Excessively wasted; more wasted than necessary or desirable.  This word blends "over" (meaning excessively) with "wasted" (meaning consumed or used inefficiently). It's often used informally to describe a state of extreme intoxication from alcohol or drugs, beyond simply being drunk or high. |  He was completely overwasted at the party and couldn't even stand up straight. 他在派对上喝得烂醉，甚至站都站不直。 |  The company's resources were overwasted on that failed project. 公司的资源在那个失败的项目上被过度浪费了。| 近义词: excessively drunk, extremely intoxicated, squandering, profligate | 反义词: sober, thrifty, economical | 词性: 形容词
