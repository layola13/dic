overglamorizing: /ˌoʊvərˈɡlæməˌraɪzɪŋ/
verb
过分美化；过分渲染
Making something seem more glamorous or exciting than it actually is.
The advertisement was overglamorizing the effects of the diet pills.  这则广告过分美化了减肥药的效果。
Hollywood often overglamorizes the lives of celebrities. 好莱坞经常过分渲染名人的生活。
近义词: exaggerating, romanticizing, idealizing
反义词: deglamorizing, downplaying, understating
词性: verb (present participle)

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "glamorize" (verb meaning "to make something seem glamorous") + "-ing" (present participle suffix)
