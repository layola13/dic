finales:/fɪˈnɑːliːz/ /fɪˈnæliːz/| n.|决赛；结局；终曲；尾声|The Olympic finales were exciting to watch. 奥运会决赛非常精彩。|近义词：conclusions, endings, culminations, ends|反义词:beginnings, commencements|名词

finales:/fɪˈnɑːliːz/ /fɪˈnæliːz/| n.|（尤指音乐的）终曲；末乐章|The symphony's finale was powerful and moving. 这部交响曲的末乐章气势磅礴，感人至深。|近义词：coda, conclusion|反义词:prelude, introduction|名词

Etymology: From French *final*, from Latin *finālis*, from *fīnis* "end, boundary, limit".
