bumbags:/ˈbʌmˌbæɡz/ (US) /ˈbʌmˌbæɡz/ (UK)| n.|腰包，挎包|A small bag attached to a belt and worn around the waist, used for carrying personal items.|例句：She wore her bumbag across her chest. 她把腰包斜挎在胸前。

例句：He kept his passport and money in his bumbag. 他把护照和钱放在腰包里。

近义词：fanny pack (US), waist bag, belt bag|反义词:backpack, handbag|词性:noun
