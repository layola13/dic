depreciate:/dɪˈpriːʃieɪt/ /dəˈpriːʃieɪt/
verb
贬值；贬低；轻视
to decrease in value over time; to belittle or undervalue.
例句：
The value of the car depreciated quickly. 这辆车的价值迅速贬值。

New cars depreciate as soon as you drive them off the lot. 新车一旦你开出停车场就会贬值。


近义词：devalue, lessen, diminish, belittle
反义词：appreciate, increase, enhance, value
词性：verb
