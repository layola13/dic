disgorge:/dɪsˈɡɔːrdʒ/ /dɪsˈɡɔːrdʒ/
|v.|吐出；排放；倾泻|to pour something out; to empty something from a place; to vomit|The volcano disgorged molten lava. 火山喷出了熔岩。|
|v.|被迫交出，吐露（尤指不情愿地）|to unwillingly give something up, especially information or money|The company was forced to disgorge its ill-gotten gains.  该公司被迫交出非法所得。|
近义词:emit, expel, spew, vomit, regurgitate, relinquish, surrender
反义词:absorb, retain, withhold, conceal
词性:verb (动词)
