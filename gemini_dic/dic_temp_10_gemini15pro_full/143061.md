outbought:/ˌaʊtˈbɔːt/
verb
买过，胜过(在购买方面)
To have bought more than someone else, especially of a particular commodity or stock.
Warren Buffet outbought his rivals during the market crash.  沃伦·巴菲特在股市崩盘期间比他的竞争对手买入更多股票。
outbid, outspend
- (No clear single antonym, could be outsold, underspent depending on context)
词性:v.
