derivational:/ˌdɛrɪˈveɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 衍生的；派生的 |  Relating to or resulting from derivation; formed by derivation.  Used in linguistics to describe words formed from other words, often by adding prefixes or suffixes. |  "Happiness" is a derivational word formed from "happy." “幸福”是一个从“快乐”派生出来的词。 |  derivative, derived |  root, base | adjective
