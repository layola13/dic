elba:/ˈelbə/
proper noun
厄尔巴岛
An island off the west coast of Italy, famous as the place of Napoleon's first exile (1814-15).
Napoleon was exiled to Elba.  拿破仑被流放到厄尔巴岛。
Synonyms: isle, island
Antonyms: mainland, continent
noun
