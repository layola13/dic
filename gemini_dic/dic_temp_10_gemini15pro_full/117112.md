mealworms:/ˈmiːlwɜːrmz/| n. |黄粉虫|The larvae of the darkling beetle, Tenebrio molitor, often used as food for pets and increasingly for human consumption due to their high protein content.|例句：I feed my pet lizard mealworms. 我用黄粉虫喂我的宠物蜥蜴。

例句：Mealworms are becoming a popular sustainable food source. 黄粉虫正成为一种受欢迎的可持续食物来源。|近义词：grubs, larvae|反义词: None|词性:noun
