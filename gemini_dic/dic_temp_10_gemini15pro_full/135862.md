nonprotective:/ˌnɑːnprəˈtɛktɪv/ | adj. |无保护的，不提供保护的 | Not providing protection or shielding from harm. |  The workers were provided with nonprotective clothing.  工人们得到了不具备保护功能的衣服。

The thin material offered nonprotective cover from the sun.  这种薄的材料不能遮挡阳光。| 近义词: unprotected, vulnerable, exposed | 反义词: protective, shielding, safeguarding | 词性: adjective

Etymology: non- + protective. "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "protective" comes from the Latin word "protectus," meaning "covered, sheltered."
