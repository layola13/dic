caesar:/ˈsiːzər/| n.|凯撒；皇帝；独裁者|A title of Roman emperors, and later a general title for a supreme ruler.|Julius Caesar was a Roman general and statesman.  尤利乌斯·凯撒是罗马的将军和政治家。|Synonym: emperor, ruler, dictator|Antonym: subject, citizen|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *Caesar*, the cognomen of Julius Caesar, later adopted as a title by Roman emperors.  源自拉丁语 *Caesar*，尤利乌斯·凯撒的姓氏，后来被罗马皇帝用作头衔。
