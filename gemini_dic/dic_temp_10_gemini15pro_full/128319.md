neurotoxicity:/ˌnʊərəʊtɒkˈsɪsɪti/ /ˌnʊroʊtɑːkˈsɪsəti/| n. |神经毒性|The quality of being neurotoxic; the ability of a substance to exert a destructive or poisonous effect on nerve tissue.|Exposure to high levels of mercury can cause neurotoxicity.  暴露于高浓度的汞会导致神经毒性。|Synonyms: nerve poison, neurotoxin|Antonyms: neuroprotective|noun
