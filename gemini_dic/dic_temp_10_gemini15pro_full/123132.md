monisms:/ˈmɒnɪzəmz/
n.
一元论；单一主义
The doctrine that only one supreme being exists.  A theory or doctrine that reduces all phenomena to one principle.
Monisms are often contrasted with dualisms. 一元论常与二元论对比。
Philosophical monisms include idealism and materialism. 哲学一元论包括唯心主义和唯物主义。
Synonym: Unitarianism, nondualism
Antonym: Dualism, pluralism
noun
