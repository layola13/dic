oklahoman:/ˌoʊkləˈhoʊmən/| n. adj.|俄克拉荷马州人；俄克拉荷马州的|A person from or residing in the state of Oklahoma, USA. Also used as an adjective to describe something related to Oklahoma.|例句：He's an Oklahoman, born and raised. 他是土生土长的俄克拉荷马人。|例句：The Oklahoman landscape is diverse, ranging from plains to mountains. 俄克拉荷马州的地貌多种多样，从平原到山地都有。|近义词：Okie (informal, sometimes derogatory)|反义词:None (specific antonym doesn't exist)|词性:Noun, Adjective
