goad:/ɡəʊd/| n. vt.| 刺激；激励；尖头棒；驱使|Something that encourages, urges, or drives someone to do something.|The constant goading from his friends finally persuaded him to enter the competition. 他朋友不断地激励最终说服他参加了比赛。|Synonyms: stimulus, spur, incentive, motivation, provocation|Antonyms: deterrent, discouragement|noun

goad:/ɡəʊd/| vt.| 刺激；激励；用尖头棒驱赶；驱使|To provoke or urge someone into action.|He was goaded into a fight by the bully's taunts. 他被恶霸的嘲讽激怒而打了起来。|Synonyms: provoke, incite, stimulate, spur, prod|Antonyms: deter, discourage, dissuade|verb
