chippable:/ˈtʃɪpəbl/
adjective
可切削的;易碎的
Easily chipped or broken into small pieces.
The paint on the old chair was chippable.  这把旧椅子的油漆很容易剥落。
The archaeologist discovered a chippable flint. 考古学家发现了一块易碎的燧石。
近义词:brittle, fragile, friable
反义词:durable, tough, resilient
词性:adjective

词源:
From "chip" (verb, meaning to break off a small piece) + "-able" (suffix meaning capable of being).  来自于动词"chip"（意思是切削一小块） + 后缀"-able"（意思是能够...的）。
