eskimo:/ˈeskɪmoʊ/ (US) /ˈɛskɪməʊ/ (UK)| n.|爱斯基摩人 (现被认为带有贬义，建议使用Inuk 或 Inuit)|A term formerly used to refer to the indigenous peoples inhabiting Arctic regions of North America (Alaska, Canada, and Greenland), and parts of Siberia. Now generally considered offensive, especially in Canada, and preferred terms like Inuk (singular) and Inuit (plural) should be used instead.  |例句：The documentary explored the traditional life of the Eskimo people. (这部纪录片探索了爱斯基摩人的传统生活。)
