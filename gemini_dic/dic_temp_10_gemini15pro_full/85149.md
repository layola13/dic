hardscapes:/ˈhɑːrdskeɪps/ | n. |硬景观，硬质景观|  The non-living elements of landscaping, such as paved areas, retaining walls, fences, and rocks. |  The hardscapes of the garden, including the stone patio and brick walkways, provided a beautiful contrast to the lush greenery.  花园的硬景观，包括石制庭院和砖砌走道，与茂密的绿色植物形成了美丽的对比。 | synonyms: hard landscaping, paved areas, structures | antonyms: softscapes, plantings, vegetation | noun
