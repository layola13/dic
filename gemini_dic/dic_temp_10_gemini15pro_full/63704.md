ephemeralism: /ɪˈfɛmərəlɪzəm/ | n. | 短暂性；朝生暮死 | The belief in or emphasis on the ephemeral nature of life or things.  The doctrine that all things are short-lived. |  The artist’s work explored themes of ephemeralism, focusing on the fleeting beauty of nature. 艺术家的作品探索了短暂性的主题，着重于自然稍纵即逝的美。| synonyms: transience, brevity, impermanence | antonyms: permanence, eternity, immortality | noun

ephemeral: /ɪˈfɛmərəl/ | adj. | 短暂的；朝生暮死的 | Lasting for a very short time. |  The beauty of the cherry blossoms is ephemeral.  樱花的美丽是短暂的。| synonyms: fleeting, transient, momentary | antonyms: permanent, lasting, enduring | adjective
