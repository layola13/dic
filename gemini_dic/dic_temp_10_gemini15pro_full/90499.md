huckstering:/ˈhʌkstərɪŋ/
n.
叫卖；兜售；不择手段地推销
the aggressive selling of goods, especially by exaggerating their value or usefulness
The huckstering of miracle cures preys on vulnerable people.  兜售灵丹妙药的行为是在利用弱势群体。
peddling, hawking, touting
-
noun

huckster:/ˈhʌkstər/
n.
叫卖小贩；兜售者；不择手段的推销员
a person who sells goods, especially by shouting in the street or by exaggerating their value or usefulness
The huckster promised that his product would cure all ills.  这个叫卖小贩承诺他的产品能包治百病。
peddler, hawker, salesman, vendor
merchant
