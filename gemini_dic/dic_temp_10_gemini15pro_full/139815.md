octodentate: /ˌɒktəʊˈdɛnteɪt/ | adj. | 八齿的，八配位的 | Having eight teeth or toothlike projections. In chemistry, referring to a ligand that can bond to a central metal ion at eight points. |  The octodentate ligand effectively encapsulates the metal ion.  八齿配体有效地包裹了金属离子。| EDTA is a well-known hexadentate ligand, while less common are octodentate ligands. EDTA是一种众所周知的六齿配体，而八齿配体则不太常见。 | multidentate, polydentate | bidentate, tridentate | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *octo* (eight) + *dentatus* (toothed), from *dens* (tooth).
