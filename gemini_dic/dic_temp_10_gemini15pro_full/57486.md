drag-and-drop:/ˌdræɡ ən ˈdrɒp/ (British), /ˌdræɡ ən ˈdrɑːp/ (American)| adj. n.|拖放|Relating to or involving the action of selecting an item on a computer screen and moving it to another location by dragging it with a mouse or other pointing device.  Also used as a noun to refer to this action itself.|例句：The drag-and-drop interface makes it easy to move files.  拖放界面使移动文件变得很容易。

例句：Drag-and-drop functionality simplifies website building for beginners.  拖放功能简化了初学者的网站建设。|近义词：click and drag, point and click|反义词: (No direct antonym - perhaps "manual input", "keyboard entry")|词性: adjective, noun
