maturing:/məˈtʃʊərɪŋ/ /məˈtʊrɪŋ/
adjective
成熟的，正在成熟的
becoming ripe or fully developed
The maturing wine develops a complex bouquet. 正在成熟的葡萄酒会散发出复杂的香味。
近义词: ripening, developing, aging, growing
反义词: immature, undeveloped, young, green
词性: adjective


maturing:/məˈtʃʊərɪŋ/ /məˈtʊrɪŋ/
verb
(使)成熟，(使)发育
to become or cause to become ripe or fully developed
The cheese is maturing nicely.  奶酪正在很好地成熟。
近义词: ripen, develop, age, grow
反义词:  decay, rot, spoil, wither
词性: verb
