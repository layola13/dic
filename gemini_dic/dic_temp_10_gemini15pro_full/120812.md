misassigning:/ˌmɪsəˈsaɪnɪŋ/ | v. | 错误分配，分配错误 |  The act of assigning something to the wrong category, place, or person.  |  The clerk misassigned the package, sending it to the wrong address.  职员把包裹分配错了，寄到了错误的地址。


The company misassigned the blame for the failure to the junior employees. 公司错误地将失败的责任归咎于初级员工。 | 近义词: misallocating, misplacing, misdirecting | 反义词: assigning correctly, allocating correctly | 词性: 动词 (Verb)
