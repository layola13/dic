imbecility:/ˌɪmbəˈsɪləti/
n.|愚蠢，低能|The imbecility of his actions was astounding. 他的行为愚蠢得令人震惊。|例句：His argument was pure imbecility. 他的论点纯粹是愚蠢。|例句：The sheer imbecility of the plan shocked everyone. 这个计划的愚蠢程度震惊了所有人。|近义词：stupidity, foolishness, idiocy, silliness|反义词：intelligence, wisdom, brilliance|词性: 名词
Etymology: From Late Latin *imbecillitas, from Latin imbecillus (“weak”), from in- (“not”) + baculum (“staff”).  Essentially meaning "without a staff" hence weak and then mentally weak.
