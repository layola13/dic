diaphanousnesses:/ˌdaɪəˈfænəsnɪsɪz/| n. |透明度，薄纱感|The quality of being diaphanous; transparency, sheerness.|The diaphanousnesses of the fabrics created an ethereal atmosphere. 这些织物的透明度营造了一种空灵的氛围。|近义词：transparencies, sheernesses, thinnesses|反义词：opaqueness, thicknesses, densities|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From diaphanous + -ness.  Diaphanous comes from the Greek "diaphanēs" (transparent, translucent), from "dia-" (through) + "phainein" (to show).
