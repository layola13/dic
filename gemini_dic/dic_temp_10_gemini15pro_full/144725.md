ovenlike:/ˈʌvənˌlaɪk/
adjective
像烤箱的;闷热的
Resembling an oven, especially in heat.  Characterized by intense, stifling heat.
The small room was ovenlike in the summer heat.  夏天，小房间里热得像烤箱一样。
The car became ovenlike after sitting in the sun all day.  这辆车在太阳下晒了一整天，变得像烤箱一样热。
近义词:stifling, stuffy, airless, sweltering
反义词:cool, airy, breezy, ventilated
词性:adjective
词源:A compound of "oven" and "-like," meaning "resembling an oven."  "Oven" comes from Old English "ofen."
