centrist:/ˈsɛntrɪst/| n. adj.|中间派，中间派的|A person who holds moderate political views, especially one who avoids extreme political positions.|The candidate positioned herself as a centrist to appeal to a wider range of voters. 这位候选人将自己定位为中间派，以吸引更广泛的选民。|近义词：moderate, neutral|反义词：extremist, radical|noun, adjective
