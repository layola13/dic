autopilot:/ˈɔːtəʊpaɪlət/ (UK) /ˈɔːtəˌpaɪlət/ (US)| n. |自动驾驶仪；自动驾驶状态|A device that automatically steers ships, aircraft, or spacecraft.|The plane was on autopilot for most of the flight.  飞机在大部分飞行时间里都处于自动驾驶状态。|Synonyms: automatic pilot, self-steering, automatic control|Antonyms: manual control|noun

autopilot:/ˈɔːtəʊpaɪlət/ (UK) /ˈɔːtəˌpaɪlət/ (US)| adj. |自动的；无意识的|Operating automatically or without conscious thought.|He seemed to be living his life on autopilot. 他似乎在无意识地过着他的生活。|Synonyms: automatic, involuntary, mechanical|Antonyms: manual, conscious, deliberate|adjective
