mountainless:/ˈmaʊntɪnləs/
adjective
无山的；没有山的
lacking mountains; having no mountains
The vast, mountainless plains stretched as far as the eye could see.  广阔无垠的平原一望无际。
The island is relatively flat and mountainless.  这个岛屿相对平坦，没有山脉。
Synonyms: flat, level, plain
Antonyms: mountainous, hilly
word type: adjective
