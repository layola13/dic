agonising:/ˈæɡənaɪzɪŋ/ /ˈæɡəˌnaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 痛苦的，折磨人的 | Extremely painful, either physically or mentally. |例句：She described the agonizing process of coming to terms with her illness. 她描述了逐渐接受自己患病的痛苦过程。

例句：He suffered an agonizing defeat in the final. 他在决赛中遭受了痛苦的失败。

近义词：excruciating, tormenting, harrowing | 反义词：pleasant, soothing, comforting | 词性：形容词
