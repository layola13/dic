parablastic:/ˌpærəˈblæstɪk/
adjective
副囊的，部分卵裂的
Relating to or denoting a type of egg cleavage in which the early cell divisions do not completely separate the blastomeres, resulting in a superficial layer of cells around a central yolk mass.
The parablastic cleavage pattern is common in insect eggs.  副囊卵裂模式常见于昆虫卵。
Many arthropods exhibit parablastic cleavage during early development. 许多节肢动物在早期发育过程中表现出副囊卵裂。
近义词: meroblastic (部分卵裂的)
反义词: holoblastic (完全卵裂的)
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek para- (beside, alongside) + blastos (germ, sprout) + -ic (adjectival suffix).
