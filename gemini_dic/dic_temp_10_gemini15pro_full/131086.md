nonbeneficent: /ˌnɒnbɪˈnɛfɪs(ə)nt/
adjective
无益的；不仁慈的
Not doing good or causing good to be done; kindly.  Lacking in kindness, generosity, or charity.
The nonbeneficent policy had a detrimental effect on the community.  这项无益的政策对社区产生了不利影响。
His nonbeneficent actions led to much suffering. 他的不仁慈行为导致了许多苦难。
近义词: harmful, detrimental, unkind, malevolent, malicious
反义词: beneficent, benevolent, kind, charitable, altruistic
词性: adjective

词源:
From non- (not) + beneficent (doing good).  Beneficent comes from Latin beneficus, from bene (well) + facere (to do).
