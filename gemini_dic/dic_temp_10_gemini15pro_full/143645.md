outkilled: /ˌaʊtˈkɪld/
verb
超过…的杀敌数; 比…杀死的更多
to kill more enemies than (someone or something else)
例句：Team A outkilled Team B by a significant margin.  A队以显著优势超过了B队的杀敌数。

例句：The veteran soldier outkilled all the recruits.  这位老兵比所有新兵杀死的敌人都多。
近义词：outmatch, surpass, defeat
反义词：be outkilled by
词性: verb
