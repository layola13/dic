macadamization: /məˌkædəmaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 碎石路铺设；用碎石铺路 | The process of making a road by laying and compacting layers of crushed stone, especially as developed by John Loudon McAdam. | The macadamization of the road greatly improved travel conditions.  碎石路铺设极大地改善了出行条件。 | Synonyms: road surfacing, paving | Antonyms: N/A | Noun

macadamize: /məˈkædəˌmaɪz/ | vt. | 用碎石铺（路） | To cover (a road) with small broken stones. |  They macadamized the dirt road to make it more durable.  他们用碎石铺设了土路，使其更耐用。 | Synonyms: pave, surface | Antonyms: N/A | Verb
