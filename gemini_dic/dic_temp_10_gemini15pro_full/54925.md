disposingly:/dɪˈspəʊzɪŋli/
adverb
倾向于...地；有...倾向地
Inclined or willing in a particular way; favorably.
She smiled disposingly at him. 她对他报以好感地笑了笑。
Synonyms: favorably, kindly, graciously, sympathetically, benignly, well-disposedly
Antonyms: unfavorably, unkindly, ungraciously, unsympathetically, hostilely


ETYMOLOGY->
From dispose + -ing + -ly.  Dispose comes from Old French *disposer (modern French disposer), from Latin dispōnere "put in different places, arrange, distribute, regulate," from dis- "apart, asunder" + pōnere "to put, place."
