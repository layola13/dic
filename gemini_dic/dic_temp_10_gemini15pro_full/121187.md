misdividing:/ˌmɪsdɪˈvaɪdɪŋ/
verb
错误划分，不正确地划分
Dividing something incorrectly, usually in a mathematical context.
He misdivided the property among his heirs, leading to a family feud. 他错误地划分了继承人的财产，导致了家族纷争。
The student misdivided the number by two, resulting in a wrong answer.  这个学生把数字除以二除错了，导致答案错误。
