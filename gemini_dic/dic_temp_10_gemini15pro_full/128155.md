nettle-like:/ˈnetlˌlaɪk/
adjective
像荨麻的；有刺的
Resembling a nettle in appearance or character, especially having stinging hairs or causing a stinging sensation.
The plant had nettle-like leaves that irritated my skin. 这种植物有类似荨麻的叶子，刺激了我的皮肤。
The criticism had a nettle-like effect, stinging his pride.  这批评像荨麻一样刺痛了他的自尊心。
近义词:stinging, prickly, irritating, urticating
反义词:smooth, soft, soothing
