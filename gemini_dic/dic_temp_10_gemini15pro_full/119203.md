mhm:/mˈhʌm/ /ˈmhm/ | interjection | 嗯，是的，表示同意或理解 | An interjection used to express agreement, understanding, or acknowledgment. | Example: "Mhm, I understand." 嗯，我明白了. | Example: "Mhm, that makes sense." 嗯，那很有道理。| Synonym: yes, uh-huh, yeah, okay | Antonym: no, uh-uh | interjection

mhm:/mˈhʌm/ /ˈmhm/ | noun | 肯定的回应 | A sound or utterance used to express affirmation or agreement.  | Example:  She responded with a quiet "mhm." 她轻轻地“嗯”了一声作为回应。| Example:  His "mhm" indicated he was listening. 他的“嗯”表示他在听。 | Synonym: agreement, affirmation, assent | Antonym: disagreement, dissent, refusal | noun
