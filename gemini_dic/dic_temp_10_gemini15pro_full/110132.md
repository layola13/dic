lidocaines:/ˈlaɪdəkeɪnz/| n.|利多卡因（复数）|A local anesthetic used to numb tissue in a specific area.  It is also used to treat certain types of irregular heartbeats.|The dentist used lidocaines before extracting the tooth. 牙医在拔牙前使用了利多卡因。|Synonyms:  xylocaine, lignocaine|Antonyms:  N/A (Anesthetic drugs don't have direct antonyms, their opposing effect would be stimulation, not a single drug.)|noun
