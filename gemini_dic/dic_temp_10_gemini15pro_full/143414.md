outflares:/ˌaʊtˈflɛəz/
verb
突然爆发; 闪耀; 比...更闪耀
burns more brightly than something else
The fire outflared briefly, then died down. 火焰短暂地闪耀了一下，然后熄灭了。
近义词: blaze, flare, erupt
反义词: dwindle, fade
词性:v.

outflare:/ˌaʊtˈflɛər/
noun
爆发; 闪耀
a sudden burst of flame or light
a sudden outburst of anger or other strong emotion
The sudden outflare of violence shocked the community. 突如其来的暴力爆发震惊了社区。
近义词: burst, eruption, flash
反义词: decline, decrease
词性:n.



词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding; surpassing") + "flare" (verb meaning "burn with a sudden unsteady flame")
