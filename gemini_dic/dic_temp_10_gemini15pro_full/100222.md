USAGE->
Simple Present:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他靠什么谋生？

Present Continuous:
What are you cooking for dinner tonight? = 你今晚做什么晚餐？
What is she wearing to the party? = 她穿什么去参加聚会？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she been up to lately? = 她最近在忙些什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on all week? = 你整个星期都在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do last weekend? = 你上周末做了什么？
What happened at the meeting? = 会议上发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? = 我看到她的时候她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you eaten before you got sick? = 你生病前吃了什么？
What had they discussed before the decision was made? = 在做决定之前他们讨论了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

Simple Future:
What will you do after you graduate? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气怎么样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be presenting at the conference? = 他们将在会议上展示什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将取得什么成就？
What will she have finished by the deadline? = 她将在截止日期前完成什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been studying for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经学习五年什么了？
What will they have been working on for so long? = 到那时他们将已经做了那么长时间的什么了？
