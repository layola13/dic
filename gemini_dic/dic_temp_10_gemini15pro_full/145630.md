overconstructed:/ˌoʊvərˈkənˈstrʌktɪd/
adjective
过度构建的；过于复杂的
Having been constructed or designed in an excessively elaborate or complicated way, often to the detriment of functionality or aesthetics.
The plot of the movie was overconstructed, with too many twists and turns that ultimately made it confusing. 这部电影的情节过于复杂，有太多的曲折，最终让人感到困惑。
The bridge was overconstructed and unnecessarily expensive. 这座桥建造得过于复杂，而且不必要地昂贵。

近义词:overengineered, overwrought, convoluted, baroque
反义词:simple, straightforward, minimalist, streamlined
词性:adjective
