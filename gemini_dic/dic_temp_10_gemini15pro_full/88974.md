holier-than-thou:/ˈhoʊliər ðæn ˈðaʊ/
adjective
假装圣洁的，道貌岸然的
Behaving as if one is more moral or religious than others, often in a way that is annoying or offensive.
She always acts holier-than-thou, judging everyone else's behavior. 她总是表现出一副道貌岸然的样子，评判别人的行为。
He gave me a holier-than-thou lecture on the evils of gambling. 他对我进行了一番关于赌博罪恶的道貌岸然的教训。
近义词:sanctimonious, self-righteous, pious
反义词:humble, modest, unpretentious
词性:adjective
