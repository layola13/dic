amidst:/əˈmɪdst/| prep.|在…中间，在…之中，被…围绕|surrounded by; in the middle of.|例句：He stood amidst the crowd. 他站在人群中。|例句：The house stood amidst tall trees. 这所房子坐落在高大的树木之中。|近义词：among, amongst, in the middle of, surrounded by|反义词：outside, beyond|词性:介词

词源:
From Middle English *amiddes, equivalent to amid +‎ -s. Cognate with Dutch te midden, German inmitten.  The final -t was added by analogy with amongst.
