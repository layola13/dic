emulsifiers:/ɪˈmʌlsɪfaɪərz/| n. |乳化剂|A substance that stabilizes an emulsion, preventing liquids that would normally separate from combining.  It works by reducing the surface tension between the liquids.|Mayonnaise contains emulsifiers to keep the oil and water from separating.|synonyms: stabilizer, binding agent|antonyms: demulsifier|noun
