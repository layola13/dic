outjuggle: /ˌaʊtˈdʒʌɡəl/
verb
胜过；以更熟练的杂耍胜过
to surpass in juggling; to outdo in manipulative skill
He outjuggled his opponent with a dazzling display of three balls and two flaming torches. 他用三个球和两个燃烧的火把的炫目表演胜过了他的对手。
outmaneuver, outperform, outsmart
outshine, surpass, eclipse
verb
