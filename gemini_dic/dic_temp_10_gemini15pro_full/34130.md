cladophyll:/ˈklæd.əˌfɪl/ | n. |叶状茎；扁平的枝|A flattened stem that resembles and functions as a leaf. |Example 1: The cactus's cladophyll stores water, allowing it to survive in arid environments. 仙人掌的叶状茎储存水分，使其能够在干旱的环境中生存。

Example 2: Some orchids have prominent cladophylls that perform photosynthesis. 一些兰花有明显的叶状茎进行光合作用。|Synonym: phylloclade|Antonym: leaf (in the traditional sense)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *klado- (branch) + -phyll (leaf).
