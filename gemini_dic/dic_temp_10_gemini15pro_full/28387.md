carbohydrate:/ˌkɑːrboʊˈhaɪdreɪt/ /ˌkɑːbəˈhaɪdreɪt/| n. |碳水化合物；糖类|A class of organic compounds containing carbon, hydrogen, and oxygen, typically with a hydrogen-oxygen atom ratio of 2:1 (as in water), and thus with the empirical formula Cm(H2O)n (where m could be different from n). They are the primary source of energy for the body.|  Bread, rice, and pasta are good sources of carbohydrates. 面包、米饭和意大利面都是碳水化合物的重要来源。

Carbohydrates are essential for providing the body with energy. 碳水化合物对为身体提供能量至关重要。

|近义词：saccharide|反义词: None (No direct antonym for a biological molecule)|词性: noun
