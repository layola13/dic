counter-questioning:/ˈkaʊntərˌkwɛstʃənɪŋ/
|n.|反诘；反问|The lawyer's counter-questioning exposed inconsistencies in the witness's testimony.|律师的反问揭露了证人证词中的矛盾之处。|
|Example 1: The defendant's counter-questioning of the witness was very effective. 被告对证人的反问非常有效。|
|Example 2:  Her skillful counter-questioning revealed the truth. 她熟练的反问揭示了真相。|
|Synonym: cross-examination, interrogation, questioning|
|Antonym: answering, responding|
|Etymology:  From counter- + questioning,  "counter-" meaning "against" or "in opposition to".|
