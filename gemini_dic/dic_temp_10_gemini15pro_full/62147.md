enclitic:/ɪnˈklɪtɪk/| n. adj.|粘附词，附着词|A word or part of a word that is unstressed and attached phonologically to the preceding word.  It forms a single phonological unit with the preceding word.|例句：The word "n't" in "didn't" is an enclitic.  单词 "didn't" 中的 "n't" 是一个粘附词。|近义词：clitic|反义词:proclitic|词性:noun, adjective

enclitic:/ɪnˈklɪtɪk/| adj.|粘附的，附着的|Pronounced with very little emphasis and joined to the preceding word.|例句：The possessive marker -'s is enclitic in English.  英语中的所有格标记 -'s 是粘附的。|近义词：clitic|反义词:proclitic|词性:adjective
