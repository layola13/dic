diaphoretic:/ˌdaɪəfəˈrɛtɪk/| adj. n.|发汗的，促使发汗的；发汗剂|Inducing perspiration.  Also, a medicine that induces perspiration.|The doctor prescribed a diaphoretic for his fever. 医生给他开了发汗药来治疗他的发烧。|近义词：sudorific, perspiratory|反义词：antiperspirant|词性:adjective, noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *diaphoreticus*, from Greek *diaphorētikós* (“carrying through”), from *diaphorein* (“to carry through”), from *dia-* (“through”) + *phorein* (“to carry”).
