etcetera:/etˈsɛtərə/ | adv. | 等等，及其他 | Used to indicate that more similar items or examples could be listed. |  The shop sells pens, pencils, notebooks, etcetera. 这家商店出售钢笔、铅笔、笔记本等等。

The report discussed costs, benefits, risks, etcetera. 报告讨论了成本、收益、风险等等。

| 近义词: and so on, and so forth, and the like | 反义词: (无) | 词性: 副词
