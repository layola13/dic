dyspepsia:/dɪsˈpepsɪə/ | n. | 消化不良，消化障碍 | Indigestion; impaired digestion. |  He suffered from dyspepsia after eating too much rich food. 他吃了太多油腻的食物后消化不良。 | Synonyms: indigestion, upset stomach, heartburn | Antonyms: digestion, eupepsia | noun

dyspepsies:/dɪsˈpepsɪəz/ | n. |  消化不良（dyspepsia的复数形式，较少使用）| Plural form of dyspepsia, referring to multiple instances or types of indigestion. |  The doctor diagnosed various dyspepsies stemming from different dietary habits. 医生诊断出由不同饮食习惯引起的各种消化不良。| Synonyms: indigestions | Antonyms: digestions | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dyspepsia*, from Ancient Greek δυσπεψία (duspepsía, “indigestion”), from δυσ- (dus-, “bad, difficult”) + πέψις (pépsis, “digestion”).
