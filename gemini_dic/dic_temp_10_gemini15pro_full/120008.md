millimetric:/ˌmɪlɪˈmɛtrɪk/| adj.|毫米的，精确到毫米的|Relating to or measured in millimeters; extremely precise or accurate.|The components require millimetric precision in their assembly. 这些组件的组装需要毫米级的精度。|例句：The surgeon made a millimetric incision.  外科医生做了一个毫米级的切口。|近义词：precise, accurate, exact|反义词：imprecise, inaccurate, approximate|词性:形容词
millimetre:/ˈmɪlɪmiːtər/| n.|毫米|A unit of length equal to one thousandth of a meter.|The rain measured five millimetres.  降雨量为五毫米。|例句：The screw was only a few millimetres long.  螺丝只有几毫米长。|近义词：mm|反义词:kilometre, metre|词性:名词
