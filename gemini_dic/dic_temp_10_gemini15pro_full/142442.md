oscillational:/ˌɒsɪˈleɪʃənəl/ |adj.| 振荡的，振动的| Relating to or characterized by oscillation; swinging back and forth. |The oscillational motion of the pendulum was mesmerizing. 钟摆的振荡运动令人着迷。|The bridge exhibited oscillational behavior under the heavy load.  在大负荷下，桥梁表现出振荡行为。|近义词:vibratory, oscillatory, fluctuating|反义词:static, stable, fixed|词性:形容词
Etymology: From oscillation + -al.  Oscillation comes from Latin oscillatio, meaning "a swinging."
