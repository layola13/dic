ETYMOLOGY->
From good-hearted + -ness.  "Good" comes from Old English "gōd," related to Dutch "goed" and German "gut." "Heart" comes from Old English "heorte," related to Dutch "hart" and German "Herz."  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, meaning "the state or quality of being."
