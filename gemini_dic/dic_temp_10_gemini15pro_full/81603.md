greaten:/ˈɡreɪtn/| v. |使变大; 使变伟大; 增大; |To make greater; increase.|The king hoped to greaten his realm through conquest. 国王希望通过征服来扩大他的王国。|近义词：enlarge, expand, augment, enhance, increase, boost|反义词:diminish, reduce, lessen, decrease|词性:动词

Etymology:  From Middle English *greten*, from Old English *grēatnian* (“to make great, enlarge”), from *grēat* (“great”). Cognate with Scots *greaten* (“to become great, grow”), West Frisian *greaten*, *graten* (“to make great, enlarge”). Related to great.
