communicability:/kəˌmjuːnɪkəˈbɪləti/ | n. |可传达性，传播性，感染力|The communicability of an idea refers to how easily it can be understood and shared.  一个想法的可传达性指的是它被理解和分享的容易程度。|例句：The communicability of the disease is a major concern for public health officials. 这种疾病的传染性是公共卫生官员关注的主要问题。

例句：The communicability of scientific findings is crucial for their impact on society.  科学发现的可传达性对于它们对社会的影响至关重要。|近义词：transmissibility, contagiousness, spreadability |反义词：incommunicability, non-contagiousness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin communicabilis, from communicare "to share, communicate," from communis "common."  来自晚期拉丁语 communicabilis，来自 communicare “分享，交流”，来自 communis “共同的”。
