manichaeism:/ˌmænɪˈkiːɪzəm/ | n. |摩尼教;二元论|A dualistic religious system with Christian, Gnostic, and pagan elements, founded in Persia in the 3rd century by Mani. It emphasized a cosmic struggle between good and evil, light and darkness. |例句：Manichaeism spread widely throughout the Roman Empire. 摩尼教在罗马帝国广泛传播。

例句：His philosophy reflects a strain of Manichaeism. 他的哲学反映出摩尼教的色彩。|近义词：dualism, gnosticism |反义词:monism|词性:noun
