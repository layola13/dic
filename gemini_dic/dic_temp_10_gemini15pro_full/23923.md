breakpoints:/ˈbreɪkˌpɔɪnts/ | n. |断点，中断点，临界点|A point in a program where execution can be stopped for debugging.  A critical moment or turning point.|例句：The debugger allows you to set breakpoints in your code. 调试器允许你在代码中设置断点。

例句：The company reached a breakpoint in its development.  公司发展到了一个临界点。|近义词：stopping point, checkpoint, milestone, turning point|反义词：continuation, progression|词性：名词
