herbals:/ˈɜːrbəlz/
n.
草药; 草本植物（制品）
Substances made from plants, especially those used for medicinal purposes.
She takes several herbals to help with her arthritis. 她服用几种草药来治疗关节炎。
近义词：botanicals, medicinal plants
反义词: pharmaceuticals, synthetics
词性: 名词

词源:
From Medieval Latin *herbālis, from Latin herba (“herb”).
