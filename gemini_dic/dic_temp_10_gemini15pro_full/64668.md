escalatory: /ˈɛskəleɪtəri/ | adj. | 升级的，逐步增强的 | Tending to escalate or increase in intensity or seriousness. |  The escalatory rhetoric between the two countries increased fears of war. 两国之间不断升级的言辞加剧了人们对战争的恐惧。|  The general ordered escalatory measures in response to the enemy's attack. 将军下令采取逐步升级的措施来应对敌人的攻击。 | 近义词: intensifying, aggravating, heightening | 反义词: de-escalatory, mitigating, calming | 词性: adjective

词源: From "escalate" + "-ory." "Escalate" comes from the Latin "scala" meaning "ladder" or "stairs."
