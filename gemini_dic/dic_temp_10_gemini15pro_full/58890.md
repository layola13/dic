词源:  "dureless" comes from the word "durable" with the addition of the negative prefix "-less."  "Durable" itself originates from the Old French word "durable" and the Latin "durabilis," meaning "able to last." The suffix "-less" is of Old English origin and indicates a lack or absence of something.
