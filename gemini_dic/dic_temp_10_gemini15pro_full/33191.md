chromophotography:/ˌkroʊmoʊfəˈtɑːɡrəfi/
n.
彩色照相术，彩色摄影术
The art or process of producing photographs in color.
Chromophotography was a significant advancement in the history of photography. 彩色照相术是摄影史上的一项重大进步。
Early forms of chromophotography involved complex processes. 早期的彩色照相术涉及复杂的过程。
近义词: color photography
反义词: monochrome photography
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From  chromo- (color) + -photography (process of producing images by the action of light).  "Chromo-" comes from Greek "chrōma" meaning "color". "-photography" comes from Greek "phōs" (light) and "graphē" (drawing, writing).
