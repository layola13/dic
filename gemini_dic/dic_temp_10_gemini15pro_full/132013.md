noncorrectives:/ˌnɒnkəˈrɛktɪvz/
noun
非矫正措施；非纠正措施
Actions or measures that do not aim to correct a problem or deficiency, but rather focus on other aspects like prevention or mitigation.
The report highlighted the importance of noncorrectives, such as preventive maintenance, in ensuring system reliability.  这份报告强调了非矫正措施，例如预防性维护，在确保系统可靠性方面的重要性。
The company invested in noncorrectives to minimize future disruptions. 该公司投资于非矫正措施以最大限度地减少未来的中断。
Synonyms: preventative measures, preventive actions, mitigating actions
Antonyms: correctives, remedies, solutions
词性: 名词
