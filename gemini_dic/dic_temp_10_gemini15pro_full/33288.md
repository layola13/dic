chthonic:/ˈkθɑːnɪk/ /ˈθɑːnɪk/| adj.|冥界的，地下的，阴森的|Relating to or inhabiting the underworld; dwelling in or under the earth; pertaining to the deities of the underworld.  Derived from the Greek word "khthōn," meaning "earth."|The ancient Greeks believed in chthonic deities who ruled the underworld. 古希腊人相信统治冥界的冥府神灵。

The archaeologist unearthed chthonic artifacts from the burial chamber. 考古学家从墓室中挖掘出冥界文物。


|近义词：infernal, subterranean, underworld|反义词:celestial, heavenly|词性:adjective
