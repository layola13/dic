nonrevenge:/ˌnɑːn rɪˈvɛndʒ/ | n. |不报复，不复仇| The act of choosing not to seek revenge.  It emphasizes a conscious decision to forgo retaliation. |  Example: He chose nonrevenge, believing it was the more mature path. 他选择了不报复，相信这是更成熟的做法。

Example:  Her commitment to nonrevenge was tested when her car was vandalized. 当她的车被破坏时，她不报复的决心受到了考验。 | 近义词:forgiveness, pardon, mercy| 反义词:revenge, retaliation, vengeance | 词性: 名词
