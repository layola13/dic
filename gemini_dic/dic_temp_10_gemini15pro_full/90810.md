humpty:/ˈhʌmpti/| n.|矮胖子，笨手笨脚的人，易碎的东西|A short, dumpy, clumsy person; something likely to break easily.  Originally, a short, dumpy person or thing.  Later associated with Humpty Dumpty, the character in the nursery rhyme.|All the king's horses and all the king's men couldn't put Humpty together again. 所有的国王的马和所有国王的人都没能把矮胖子重新拼起来。|近义词：clumsy, awkward, dumpy|反义词:graceful, agile|词性:noun
