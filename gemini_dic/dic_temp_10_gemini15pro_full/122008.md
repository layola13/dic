misstamps:/ˌmɪsˈstæmps/| n.| 邮票错贴；贴错邮票|An instance of putting the wrong stamp or stamps on a letter or package.  The package was returned due to misstamps.  包裹由于贴错邮票被退回。|例句：There were several misstamps on the envelopes, causing delays in delivery. 信封上贴错了好几张邮票，导致投递延误。

例句：The misstamps cost him extra postage. 贴错的邮票让他额外付了邮资。|近义词：error in postage, incorrect stamping|反义词:correct stamping|词性:noun
