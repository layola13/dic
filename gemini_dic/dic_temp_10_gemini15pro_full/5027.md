all-in:/ˌɔːl ˈɪn/ | adj. adv.| 全押的，孤注一掷的；包含一切的 |  Completely committed to something, risking everything; including everything. | He went all-in with his poker hand. 他在扑克游戏中全押了。 | 近义词：committed, invested, all-out | 反义词: hesitant, cautious | 词性: 形容词，副词

all-in:/ˌɔːl ˈɪn/ |  n.|  总共，全部 | The all-in price includes flights and accommodation. 总价包括机票和住宿。 | 近义词：total, everything, inclusive | 反义词: partial, exclusion | 词性: 名词

词源: From the gambling term meaning to bet all of one's chips.  源于赌博术语，意思是押上所有筹码。
