exert:/ɪɡˈzɜːrt/ (British), /ɪɡˈzɜːrt/ (American)| v. |运用，发挥，施加|to apply or bring to bear (a force, influence, or quality)|He exerted all his strength to move the rock. 他使出浑身力气去搬那块石头。|近义词：apply, employ, utilize, wield, exercise|反义词：relax, restrain, withhold|词性:动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *exertus*, past participle of *exserere* "to put forth," from *ex-* "out" + *serere* "to join, link, bind."
