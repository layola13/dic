nonsocialness:/ˌnɒnˈsəʊʃəlnəs/
n.
不善社交；孤僻
Lack of sociability; aversion to social interaction.
Her nonsocialness made it difficult for her to make friends. 她不善交际，很难交到朋友。
近义词：unsociability, introversion, shyness, reclusiveness
反义词：sociability, gregariousness, extroversion
词性：名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + social + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)
词源：non-（前缀，意为“不”）+ social + -ness（后缀，由形容词构成名词）
