harpsichord:/ˈhɑːrpsɪkɔːrd/| n.|大键琴|A keyboard instrument that looks like a small grand piano but produces sound by plucking the strings.|She played a piece of Bach on the harpsichord. 她用大键琴演奏了一首巴赫的曲子。|近义词：clavichord, virginal|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑现代钢琴 piano)|词性:noun
