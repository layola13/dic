cassoulet:/ˌkæsʊˈleɪ/ (US), /ˈkæsʊleɪ/ (UK)| n. | 法式砂锅菜（通常用白豆、各种肉类和香肠炖制而成）| A rich, slow-cooked casserole containing meat (typically pork sausages, goose, duck and sometimes mutton), pork skin and white beans. |例句：The cassoulet was rich and flavorful, a perfect dish for a cold winter evening.  法式砂锅菜味道浓郁，是一道完美的冬夜菜肴。
