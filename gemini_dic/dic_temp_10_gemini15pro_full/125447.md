multiplexer:/ˈmʌltɪˌpleksər/ (US) /ˈmʌltɪˌpleksə/ (UK)| n.|多路复用器, 多路转换器|A device that selects one of several analog or digital input signals and forwards the selected input into a single line.|The multiplexer allows multiple devices to share a single communication line.  多路复用器允许多个设备共享一条通信线路。|synonyms: mux, data selector|antonyms: demultiplexer, demux|noun


multiplex:/ˈmʌltɪˌpleks/ (US) /ˈmʌltɪˌpleks/ (UK)| v. n. adj.|多路传输，多路复用，多重的，复合的| (v.) To combine multiple signals into one; (n.) A building with multiple, often separate, movie theaters; (adj.) Having multiple parts or aspects. |(v.)  The system multiplexes the data streams onto a single fiber optic cable.  该系统将数据流多路复用到一根光纤电缆上。|(n.) We saw the new movie at the local multiplex. 我们在当地的多厅影院看了那部新电影。 |(adj.)  The company faced multiplex challenges in the current market. 该公司在当前市场面临多重挑战。|synonyms: combine, merge, compound (v.); cinema, movie theater (n.); multifaceted, complex (adj.)|antonyms: separate, divide (v.); single, simple (adj.)|verb, noun, adjective
