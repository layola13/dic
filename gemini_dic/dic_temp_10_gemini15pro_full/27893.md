cantankerous:/kænˈtæŋkərəs/ | adj. |脾气坏的，爱争吵的 | Bad-tempered, argumentative, and uncooperative. |例句：The cantankerous old man yelled at the children for stepping on his lawn.  脾气暴躁的老人对踩到他草坪的孩子们大吼大叫。|例句：My neighbor is a cantankerous old woman who complains about everything. 我的邻居是一个爱抱怨一切的坏脾气老妇人。|近义词：bad-tempered, grumpy, irascible, irritable, curmudgeonly |反义词：good-natured, agreeable, amiable |词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain, possibly from a dialectal word meaning "to talk incessantly," perhaps influenced by "rancorous."
