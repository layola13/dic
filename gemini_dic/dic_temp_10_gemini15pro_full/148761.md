overturner:/ˌoʊvərˈtɜːrnər/ | n. | 推翻者，颠覆者 |Someone or something that overturns something else, especially a legal decision or established practice. | The Supreme Court decision was hailed by some as a landmark overturning of precedent. 最高法院的裁决被一些人誉为具有里程碑意义的先例推翻。| The new evidence could be a complete overturner for the case. 新的证据可能会彻底颠覆这个案件。 | synonyms: subverter, revolutionary, overthrower, invalidator | antonyms: upholder, supporter, maintainer | noun


overturn:/ˌoʊvərˈtɜːrn/ | v. | 推翻，颠覆；倾覆 | 1. To cause something to turn over or capsize. 2. To abolish, invalidate, or reverse (a decision, law, etc.). | The car overturned on the icy road. 汽车在结冰的道路上翻车了。 | The court overturned the previous ruling. 法院推翻了先前的裁决。| synonyms:  capsize, upset, overthrow, invalidate, reverse | antonyms: uphold, support, maintain, establish, confirm | verb
