diverter:/daɪˈvɜːrtər/ (US), /daɪˈvɜːtə/ (UK)| n.| 转向器，分流器，导流器|A device that diverts something, such as water, air, or electrical current, from its usual course.|The dam has diverters to control the flow of water.  大坝上有分流器来控制水流。|synonyms: deflector, shunt, bypass|antonyms: concentrator, collector|noun

diverter:/daɪˈvɜːrtər/ (US), /daɪˈvɜːtə/ (UK)| n.| 分散注意力的事物|Something that distracts or amuses.|The comedian was a great diverter, keeping the audience entertained during the long wait.  这位喜剧演员很会分散注意力，在漫长的等待中让观众保持愉悦。|synonyms: distraction, amusement, entertainment|antonyms: bore, annoyance|noun
