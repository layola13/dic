dioxin: /ˈdaɪˌɒksɪn/ (UK) /daɪˈɑːksɪn/ (US) | n. | 二噁英 |  A highly toxic chemical compound produced as a byproduct in some manufacturing processes, especially those involving chlorine. It is a known carcinogen and can cause a variety of health problems. |  Dioxin contamination is a serious environmental problem. 二噁英污染是一个严重的环境问题。|  Dioxins are persistent organic pollutants. 二噁英是持久性有机污染物。 | synonyms: pollutant, contaminant, toxin | antonyms:  purifier (not a direct antonym, but represents the opposite concept) | noun
