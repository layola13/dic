influenceable:/ɪnˈfluːənsəbl/
adjective
易受影响的;可被左右的
Susceptible to influence; easily persuaded or swayed.
He's too influenceable and tends to follow the crowd. 他很容易受影响，总是随波逐流。
influenceable by others 受他人影响
近义词:impressionable, susceptible, persuadable
反义词:uninfluenceable, resistant, impervious

Etymology:
From influence + -able.  The suffix "-able" forms adjectives meaning "capable of being" or "worthy of being". "Influence" comes from Old French influence "flowing in; influx," from Medieval Latin influentia "a flowing in," especially of celestial bodies, from Latin influere "to flow into," from in- "into" + fluere "to flow."
