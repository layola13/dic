abating:/əˈbeɪtɪŋ/
verb
减轻，减少
becoming less intense or widespread.
The storm is abating. = 风暴正在减弱。
The pain in his leg was slowly abating. = 他腿上的疼痛正在慢慢减轻。
subside, diminish, lessen, decrease, decline, wane, dwindle
increase, intensify, heighten, worsen, aggravate
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *abaten*, from Anglo-Norman *abater* (to beat down, diminish, cast down, humble, lower, abate), from Late Latin *abbatere* (to beat down), from Latin *ad* (to) + *battuere* (to beat).

USAGE->
Present Participle:
The wind is abating, making it safer to go outside. = 风正在减弱，出去更安全了。
The floodwaters are finally abating after days of heavy rain. = 几天的大雨过后，洪水终于开始消退了。

Past Participle:
With the storm abated, they were able to continue their journey. = 暴风雨平息后，他们得以继续旅程。
Having abated somewhat, the noise was no longer unbearable. = 噪音减轻了一些，不再难以忍受了。

Present Perfect Continuous:
The traffic has been abating since the rush hour ended. = 交通高峰期结束后，交通拥堵情况有所缓解。
The fever has been abating, which is a good sign. = 高烧一直在消退，这是一个好兆头。
