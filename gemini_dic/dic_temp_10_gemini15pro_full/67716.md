exuperation:/ɪɡˌzuːpəˈreɪʃn/
n.
超越，胜过，优越性
the act of exceeding or surpassing; superiority.
His exuperation of all his rivals was evident in the final race. 他在决赛中明显胜过所有对手。
近义词:surpassing, exceeding, transcendence, outdoing
反义词: inferiority, subordination
词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *exuperatio, from Latin exuperare "to surpass," from ex- "out" + superare "to overcome, surpass," from super "above."
