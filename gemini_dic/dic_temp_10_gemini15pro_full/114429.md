make up for: /meɪk ˈʌp fɔːr/
verb phrase
补偿，弥补
to compensate for something; to do something good to show that you are sorry about something bad that you have done
例句：I worked extra hours to make up for the time I had missed. 我加班是为了弥补我错过的時間。

例句：He bought her flowers to make up for forgetting her birthday. 他买了花送给她以弥补忘记了她生日。
近义词：compensate for, atone for, redeem
反义词：worsen, aggravate
词性：动词短语


make up for: /meɪk ˈʌp fɔːr/
verb phrase
弥补（缺陷、不足）
to supply a deficiency
例句：Her enthusiasm makes up for her lack of experience. 她的热情弥补了经验的不足。

例句：What he lacks in skill, he makes up for in enthusiasm. 他在技能上的不足，用热情来弥补。
近义词：compensate for, offset
反义词：exacerbate, detract from
词性：动词短语
