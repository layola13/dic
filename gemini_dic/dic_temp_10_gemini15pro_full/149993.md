palming-off:/ˈpɑːmɪŋ ˈɒf/| n.|假冒；冒充；以假乱真|The act of selling goods as if they are something else, usually something more expensive or of better quality.|The company was accused of palming off cheap imitations as genuine designer products. 这家公司被指控将廉价仿制品冒充为真正的名牌产品。|近义词：passing off, misrepresentation, fraud, deception|反义词:authenticity, genuineness|noun

palming-off:/ˈpɑːmɪŋ ˈɒf/| v.|假冒；冒充；以假乱真|To sell goods as if they are something else, usually something more expensive or of better quality.|They tried to palm off fake watches to unsuspecting tourists. 他们试图把假表卖给毫无戒心的游客。|近义词：pass off, misrepresent|反义词:authenticate, verify|verb
