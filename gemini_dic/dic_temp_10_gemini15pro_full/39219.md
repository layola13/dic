conglomeration:/kənˌɡlɒməˈreɪʃn/ /kənˌɡlɑːməˈreɪʃn/
n.
混合物，聚集体，企业集团
A mixed mass; a collection of diverse things.  A large corporation formed by merging several smaller and different firms.
The rock is a conglomerate of various minerals. 这块岩石是由各种矿物混合而成的。
The company is a large conglomerate with holdings in various industries. 这家公司是一个大型企业集团，在各行各业都有控股。
近义词：mixture, aggregate, combination, cluster, corporation, multinational
反义词：separation, division, component
词性：noun
