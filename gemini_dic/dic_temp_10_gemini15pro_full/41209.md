coquina:/koʊˈkiːnə/ | n. | 贝壳碎屑 | Loose fragments of shells. | The beach was covered in coquina. 海滩上覆盖着贝壳碎屑。 | Synonyms: shell fragments, shell hash | Antonyms: solid rock | noun
