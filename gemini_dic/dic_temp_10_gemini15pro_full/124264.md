motacil: /ˈmoʊtəsɪl/ | n. |  Wagtail (a type of bird) | A small, slender songbird with a long, constantly wagging tail.  It belongs to the family Motacillidae. | Example:  I saw a pied wagtail bobbing its tail by the river. 我看到一只白鶺鴒在河边摆动着尾巴。| Example: The yellow wagtail is a migratory bird. 黄鶺鴒是一种候鸟。 | Synonym:  None (wagtail is the common name) | Antonym:  N/A | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *motacilla, from motacilla,  from motare "to move, shake" + -cilla, diminutive suffix.  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *meue- "to push away."
