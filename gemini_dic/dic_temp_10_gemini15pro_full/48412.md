dedition: /dɪˈdɪʃən/
n.
奉献，献身；赠送，捐赠
the act of dedicating something to a cause, activity, or person
例句：His dedition to his work is admirable. 他对工作的奉献令人钦佩。

例句：The dedition of the new hospital wing was a momentous occasion. 新医院侧楼的落成是一个重要的时刻。
近义词：devotion, commitment, dedication
反义词：apathy, indifference
词性: 名词
