caterwaulings:/ˌkætərˈwɔːlɪŋz/
n.
猫叫声，刺耳的叫声
A loud, harsh, and unpleasant cry, like that of a cat in heat.
The caterwaulings of the alley cats kept me awake all night.
整夜巷子里猫的叫声让我无法入睡。

近义词:shrieks, howls, wails, screams
反义词:purrs, meows, whispers, murmurs
词性:名词(plural)
