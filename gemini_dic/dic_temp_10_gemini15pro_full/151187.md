parcheesi:/pɑːrˈtʃiːzi/ | n. |飞行棋 |A board game in which players move pieces around a cross-shaped track by throwing dice. |例句：We played parcheesi all afternoon. 我们一下午都在玩飞行棋。|例句：Parcheesi is a game of strategy and luck. 飞行棋是一个策略和运气的游戏。|近义词：Sorry!, Ludo, Trouble |反义词: None |词性:noun

Etymology->
From Hindi pāćīs "twenty-five," the highest score obtainable with the cowrie shells originally used as dice.
