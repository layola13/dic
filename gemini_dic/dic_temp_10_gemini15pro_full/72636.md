flyover:/ˈflaɪˌoʊvər/ (US) /ˈflaɪˌəʊvə(r)/ (UK)| n.|立交桥；天桥；飞越|A bridge that carries a road or railway over another road or railway.  A flight over a place, especially in a plane.|例句：We drove over the flyover to avoid the traffic. 我们开车过立交桥以避开交通拥堵。

例句：The Air Force performed a flyover at the football game.  空军在橄榄球比赛中进行了飞行表演。|近义词：overpass, bridge, aerial view|反义词:underpass|词性:名词
