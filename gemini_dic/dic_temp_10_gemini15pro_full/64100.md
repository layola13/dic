epoophoron:/ˌɛpoʊˈɒfərɒn/ | n. | 附睾管 | A rudimentary ductal system in the mesosalpinx between the ovary and the uterine tube, representing the remains of the Wolffian duct.  It is homologous to the epididymis in the male. | The epoophoron is a remnant of the mesonephric duct in females. 附睾管是女性中肾管的残余。| The doctor explained that the epoophoron can sometimes form cysts. 医生解释说，附睾管有时会形成囊肿。 |Synonyms: parovarium, organ of Rosenmüller | Antonyms: None (anatomical structure) | Noun
