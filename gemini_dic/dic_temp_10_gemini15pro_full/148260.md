overslip:/ˈəʊvəslɪp/ /ˈoʊvərslɪp/| n. |衬裙；覆盖物；飞机失速时无意中增大的滑移角|A thin protective covering, often of paper or plastic. A small piece of paper placed over a letter or document before signing it, to prevent smudging or blotting.  In aviation, an unintentional increase in sideslip during a stall. |例句：She carefully placed the overslip over the freshly signed document. 她小心翼翼地把衬纸放在刚签好的文件上。

例句：The pilot struggled to control the aircraft after the overslip during the stall. 飞行员在失速期间滑移角增大后，努力控制飞机。|近义词：covering, sheet, slip, overlay|反义词: (No direct antonym, depends on specific meaning)|n.
