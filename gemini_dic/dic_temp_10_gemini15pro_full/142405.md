orvet:/ˈɔːrvɪt/| n. |无足蜥蜴;蛇蜥|A small, legless lizard of the family Anguidae, resembling a snake.  It is also known as a slow worm.|例句：The orvet, despite looking like a snake, is actually a lizard. 无足蜥蜴虽然看起来像蛇，但实际上是蜥蜴。

例句：Orvets are known for their ability to shed their tails when threatened.  无足蜥蜴以在受到威胁时能够断尾而闻名。|近义词：slow worm, blindworm, glass lizard|反义词:snake (loosely, as a contrasting reptile with legs)|词性:noun
