coalitionists:/ˌkoʊ.əˈlɪʃ.ən.ɪsts/
n.
联盟成员，联合主义者
Members of a coalition; supporters of a coalition.
The coalitionists agreed on a common platform despite their differing ideologies. 联盟成员尽管意识形态不同，但在一个共同的平台上达成了一致。
The coalitionists worked together to achieve their shared goals. 联合主义者为了共同的目标一起努力。
synonyms: allies, partners, collaborators
antonyms: opponents, adversaries
noun
