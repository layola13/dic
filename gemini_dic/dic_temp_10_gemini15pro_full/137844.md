nonviewer:/ˌnɒnˈvjuːər/ | n. | 非观众；不观看者 | Someone who does not watch a particular television program or type of program. | Example 1: The marketing campaign aimed to reach not only viewers but also nonviewers. 这项营销活动的目标不仅是吸引观众，还包括非观众。

Example 2:  The survey also included a sample of nonviewers to understand their media consumption habits.  该调查还包括一部分非观众样本，以了解他们的媒体消费习惯。 | Near Synonyms: non-watcher,  non-subscriber (if referring to streaming services) | Antonyms: viewer, watcher, audience, subscriber | Noun
