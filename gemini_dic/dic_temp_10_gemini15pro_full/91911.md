hyperlustrously:/ˌhaɪpərˈlʌstrəsli/
adverb
极度光亮地，极度闪耀地
With an extreme luster; very brightly or shiningly.
The newly polished car shone hyperlustrously in the sunlight.  新抛光的汽车在阳光下闪耀着极亮的光芒。
The gem hyperlustrously reflected the light of the candles. 宝石极度闪耀地反射着烛光。
近义词: brilliantly, dazzlingly, radiantly, resplendently
反义词: dimly, dully
词性: adverb

词源:
"Hyper-" (Greek):  meaning "over, beyond, excessive"
"Lustrous" (Latin "lustrum"): meaning "light, brilliance, splendor"
"-ly" (Old English "-lice"): adverbial suffix
