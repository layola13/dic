overestimation:/ˌoʊvərˌɛstɪˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 高估，过高估计 | An appraisal that assigns too high a value.  An act of judging something to be better, larger, or more important than it really is. |  He admitted to a slight overestimation of his own abilities. 他承认稍微高估了自己的能力。|  The company's overestimation of demand led to a surplus of inventory.  公司对需求的高估导致了库存过剩。 | overvaluation, exaggeration | underestimation | noun
