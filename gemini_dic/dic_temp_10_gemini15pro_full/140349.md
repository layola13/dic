oho:/oʊˈhoʊ/| int.|哦嗬（表示惊讶、理解或得意）| An exclamation expressing surprise, comprehension, or triumph. |例句：Oho! I see what you mean now. 哦嗬！现在我明白你的意思了。

例句：Oho! You've won the lottery! 哦嗬！你中彩票了！

近义词：aha, oh, well, I see|反义词: (No direct antonym for an interjection)|词性: interjection
