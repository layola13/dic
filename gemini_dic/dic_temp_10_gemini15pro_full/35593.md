coarsely:/ˈkɔːrsli/ | adv. | 粗糙地；粗俗地；粗略地 | In a rough or unrefined manner; roughly; crudely. | The fabric was coarsely woven.  这块布料织得很粗糙。| roughly, crudely, coarsely, harshly,  | finely, delicately, smoothly, gently | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *corsly, from Old French cors, gros (“coarse”), from Late Latin *corpulentus, from Latin corpus (“body”).  More at corse, gross.
