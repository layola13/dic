leakers:/ˈliːkərz/| n.|泄密者；泄露者|People who disclose confidential information. |The leakers were eventually caught and prosecuted. 泄密者最终被抓获并被起诉。|Synonyms: whistleblowers, informants, divulgers|Antonyms: keepers, concealers|Noun

leak:/liːk/| n. v.|泄漏；漏洞；泄露|1.(n.) a hole or crack that lets something such as liquid or gas escape. 2. (v.) to allow liquid or gas to escape. |1. (n.) The roof had a leak. 屋顶上有个漏洞。 2. (v.) The pipe is leaking. 管道正在漏水。|Synonyms: drip, seep, disclose, reveal|Antonyms: contain, seal, withhold|Noun, Verb
