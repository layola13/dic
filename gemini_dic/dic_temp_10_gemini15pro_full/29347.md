castleward:/ˈkɑːsəlwərd/
adjective
朝向城堡的；位于城堡方向的
Situated in the direction of a castle.
The castleward slope offered a stunning view. 朝向城堡的山坡视野极佳。

adverb
朝向城堡
Toward a castle.
They rode castleward as the sun began to set.  随着太阳开始落山，他们朝着城堡的方向骑马而去。

noun
城堡方向
The direction of a castle.
The castleward was clearly marked on the map.  城堡的方向在地图上清晰地标明了。


Etymology:
From 'castle' + 'ward' (Old English 'weard', meaning "towards").  由“castle”（城堡）和“ward”（朝向）组成，“ward”来自古英语“weard”，意为“朝向”。


Synonyms:
(adjective/adverb) towards the castle, castle-facing, in the direction of the castle
(noun) direction of the castle


Antonyms:
(adjective/adverb) away from the castle, castle-backing
(noun)  opposite direction of the castle
