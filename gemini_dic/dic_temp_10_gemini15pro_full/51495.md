detractiveness:/dɪˈtræktɪvnəs/
n.
unattractiveness; the quality of being unpleasant or unattractive
The detractiveness of the rundown building lowered the property values in the neighborhood.  这栋破旧建筑的难看降低了附近地区的房产价值。
The detractiveness of the smell made it difficult to enjoy the meal.  难闻的气味让人难以享受这顿饭。
unattractiveness, ugliness, repulsiveness
attractiveness, beauty, pleasantness
noun

detract:/dɪˈtrækt/
v.
reduce the worth or value of something
The constant interruptions detracted from her ability to concentrate.  不断的打扰降低了她集中注意力的能力。
The negative reviews detracted from the movie's success. 负面评论有损于这部电影的成功。
diminish, reduce, lessen
enhance, increase, improve
verb
