homespun:/ˈhoʊmˌspʌn/| adj. n.|家织的，朴素的，简朴的|Made at home, typically using simple materials and methods; simple and unsophisticated.|例句：She wore a homespun dress. 她穿着一件家织的连衣裙。|例句：His homespun philosophy was refreshing. 他朴素的哲学令人耳目一新。|近义词：homemade, rustic, simple, unpretentious|反义词：sophisticated, elaborate, refined|词性:形容词, 名词

homespun:/ˈhoʊmˌspʌn/| n.|家织布，粗布|Cloth made at home.|例句：The tablecloth was made of homespun. 桌布是用家织布做的。|例句：She learned to weave homespun from her grandmother. 她跟祖母学会了织粗布。|近义词：homespun cloth, handwoven cloth|反义词：manufactured cloth|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From home + spun (past participle of spin).  Meaning "made at home" is from the late 16th century, and the figurative sense of "simple, unsophisticated" is from the mid 18th century.
