一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你觉得怎么样？
What does she want for her birthday? = 她生日想要什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing over there? = 他在那里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she eaten for lunch? = 她午饭吃了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading all this time? = 他一直都在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the wedding? = 她参加婚礼穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 我来之前你做了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting? = 开会之前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the accident? = 事故发生前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about? = 他们一直在争论什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by then? = 到那时会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将学习什么多久了？
