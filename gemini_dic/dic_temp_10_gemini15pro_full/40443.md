contrasting:/kənˈtrɑːstɪŋ/ | adj. | 对比的，截然不同的 | Showing differences when compared | The contrasting colors of black and white create a striking visual effect.  黑白对比鲜明的颜色营造出惊人的视觉效果。 | synonyms: different, dissimilar, divergent, distinct | antonyms: similar, alike, identical, uniform | adjective

contrasting:/kənˈtrɑːstɪŋ/ | n. | 对比，对照 | The act of comparing in order to show differences | The contrasting of the two proposals revealed their strengths and weaknesses. 对两项提案的对比揭示了它们的优缺点。 | synonyms: comparison, juxtaposition, differentiation | antonyms: similarity, resemblance, likeness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "contrast," which comes from the French "contraster" (to contrast, oppose), ultimately from the Latin "contrastare" (to stand against), formed from "contra" (against) and "stare" (to stand).
