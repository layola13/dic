lurky:/ˈlɜːrki/| adj.|鬼鬼祟祟的，偷偷摸摸的，潜伏的|Given to lurking; stealthy. Characterized by lurking; furtive.|The lurky figure in the shadows made me uneasy.  阴影中鬼鬼祟祟的身影让我感到不安。|近义词：furtive, stealthy, sneaky, shifty|反义词：open, forthright, honest|词性:形容词
词源: From *lurk* + *-y*.  The adjective is first attested 1844.
