antirationalists:/ˌæntiˈræʃənəlɪsts/
n.
反理性主义者
People who oppose or distrust reason and logic, especially in favor of intuition, emotion, or tradition.
Anti-rationalists often prioritize feelings and beliefs over evidence and logical reasoning.  反理性主义者通常将感觉和信仰置于证据和逻辑推理之上。
Many Romantic poets could be described as antirationalists, embracing emotion and imagination over strict adherence to logic. 许多浪漫主义诗人可以被描述为反理性主义者，他们崇尚情感和想象力，而不是严格遵守逻辑。
近义词: irrationalists, mystics, skeptics
反义词: rationalists, logicians, empiricists
词性: Noun
