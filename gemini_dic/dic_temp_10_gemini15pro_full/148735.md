overtrim:/ˌoʊvərˈtrɪm/| v.|过度修剪，过度调整|To trim or adjust something excessively, beyond the desired or optimal point.|The gardener overtrimmed the hedge, making it look too short and stubby. 园丁把树篱修剪得过度了，使它看起来又矮又粗短。|The pilot overtrimmed the aircraft, causing it to stall. 飞行员过度调整了飞机，导致它失速。|overadjust, overcrop|undertrim|verb
overtrim:/ˌoʊvərˈtrɪm/| n.|过度修剪，过度调整（的状态或结果）|The act of trimming or adjusting something excessively.|The overtrim of the sails caused the boat to capsize. 帆的过度调整导致船倾覆。|The overtrim of the hedge ruined its appearance. 树篱的过度修剪破坏了它的外观。|overadjustment, overcropping|undertrim|noun
