n.
书柜，书橱

I have two large bookcases in my study.  我的书房里有两个大书柜。

bookcase:/ˈbʊkkeɪs/
n.
书柜，书橱
A piece of furniture with shelves for storing books.
She bought a new bookcase for her living room. 她为她的客厅买了一个新书柜。

Etymology:  book + case (from Old French casse, from Latin capsa "box")

Synonyms: bookshelf, bookrack, cabinet

Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical object.  Could consider concepts like "digital library" or "e-reader" as representing opposing ways of storing books.)
