eliminate:/ɪˈlɪmɪneɪt/
verb
消除，淘汰
to remove or get rid of something
Example: The company eliminated several positions to save money. 公司裁撤了几个职位以节省资金。
Example: We need to eliminate the possibility of error. 我们需要消除出错的可能性。
Synonyms: remove, eradicate, get rid of, abolish, exterminate
Antonyms: keep, retain, include, add
词性: 动词
