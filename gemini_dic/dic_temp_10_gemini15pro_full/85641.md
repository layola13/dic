haunter:/ˈhɔːntər/ (US) /ˈhɔːntə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 经常出没的人；鬼魂；缠扰者|Someone who visits a place often; a ghost; someone who persistently bothers another person.|例句：The café is a popular haunter of artists and writers. 这家咖啡馆是艺术家和作家经常光顾的地方。

例句：The old house is said to be haunted by the ghost of its previous owner. 据说这栋老房子闹鬼，是以前房东的鬼魂。

近义词：frequent visitor, regular, ghost, specter, stalker, pursuer|反义词:avoider|词性:noun


haunt:/hɔːnt/ (US) /hɔːnt/ (UK)| v.|  (鬼魂)出没；(思想、回忆等)萦绕；经常去；缠扰| (of a ghost) to appear in a place repeatedly; (of a thought, memory, etc.) to stay in someone's mind continually; to visit a place often; to persistently bother someone.|例句：The memory of the accident still haunts him. 事故的记忆仍然萦绕着他。

例句：He haunts the local bars. 他经常光顾当地的酒吧。

近义词：pursue, stalk, frequent, visit regularly, plague, torment|反义词:avoid, shun|词性:verb
