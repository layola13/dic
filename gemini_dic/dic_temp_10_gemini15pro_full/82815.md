guitarlike: /ɡɪˈtɑːrlaɪk/
adjective
像吉他一样的，类似吉他的
Resembling a guitar in shape, sound, or playing technique.
The instrument had a guitarlike body but only four strings.  这件乐器有着像吉他一样的琴身，但只有四根弦。
He played a guitarlike instrument made from a cigar box. 他演奏了一个用雪茄盒做成的类似吉他的乐器。
近义词: guitar-shaped, guitar-esque
反义词: (No clear antonym, consider using dissimilar to a guitar)
词性: adjective
