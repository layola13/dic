bleak:/blik/ /blik/| adj.|荒凉的，凄凉的，阴冷的，无望的|lacking vegetation and exposed to the elements; depressing; cold and miserable; offering little or no hope|The future looked bleak for the company. 这家公司的前景看起来很 bleak。|近义词：desolate, dreary, grim, barren, cold, cheerless|反义词：bright, cheerful, promising, hopeful, warm|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *bleikr "pale, wan," from Proto-Germanic *blaikaz "shining, white," from PIE root *bhel- (1) "to shine, flash, burn."  The sense development in Old Norse is perhaps from the pale color of exposed, barren landscapes.
