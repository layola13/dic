nonpictorially:/ˌnɒnpɪkˈtɔːriəli/
adverb
非图像地；不用图画地
Not using or involving pictures or other visual representations.
She described the scene nonpictorially, focusing on sounds and smells. 她没有用图像的方式描述场景，而是专注于声音和气味。
non-pictorially, abstractly, verbally
pictorially, visually, graphically
adverb
