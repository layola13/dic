inseams:/ˈɪnˌsiːmz/ | n. | 内缝；裤裆长 | The length of the seam on the inside of a trouser leg, from the crotch to the bottom of the hem. |  My inseam is 32 inches. 我的内缝是32英寸。| synonyms: inside leg (UK), leg length | antonyms: outseam | noun
