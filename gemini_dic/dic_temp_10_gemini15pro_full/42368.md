counterattacked:/ˌkaʊntərəˈtækt/ | v. |反攻，反击|Launched a counterattack against the enemy. 对敌人发动了反击。|例句：The army counterattacked after the initial assault. 军队在最初的攻击之后进行了反击。

例句：They counterattacked with a new marketing campaign. 他们用新的营销活动进行反击。

近义词：retaliated, responded, struck back|反义词: retreated, withdrew |词性: 动词

词源: counter- (against) + attack (to begin fighting against)
