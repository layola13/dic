lilo:/ˈlaɪloʊ/| n.|救生圈，橡皮艇|An inflatable mattress used as a flotation device or a small raft.|We floated on lilos in the pool. 我们在泳池里用救生圈漂浮。|近义词: inflatable raft, air mattress|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "sink") |noun
lilo:/ˈlaɪloʊ/| n.|Linux Loader 的缩写 (LILO)|A boot loader for Linux operating systems.|LILO was the default boot loader for many Linux distributions.  LILO曾是许多Linux发行版的默认引导加载程序。|近义词: GRUB (another boot loader)|反义词: (No direct antonym) |noun
