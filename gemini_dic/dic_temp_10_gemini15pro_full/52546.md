difflation:/dɪˈfleɪʃən/ /diːˈfleɪʃən/| n.|通货紧缩，物价下跌|A decrease in the general price level of goods and services in an economy.|The country experienced a period of deflation after the housing bubble burst.  这个国家在房地产泡沫破裂后经历了一段时期的通货紧缩。|Deflation can lead to lower wages and decreased economic growth. 通货紧缩会导致工资降低和经济增长放缓。|synonyms: price deflation, falling prices, disinflation (mild deflation)|antonyms: inflation, reflation|noun
