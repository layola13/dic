bipennate:/baɪˈpɛneɪt/| adj.|双羽状的|Having the fibers arranged obliquely and converging to two sides of a central tendon, resembling a feather.  Relating to a muscle in which the fibers are attached obliquely to both sides of a central tendon.|The rectus femoris is a bipennate muscle.|Synonym: bi-pennate|Antonym: unipennate|adjective
