hyperproducers:/ˈhaɪpərprəˈdusərz/| n.|超级生产者，高产者|Individuals or entities that produce an exceptionally large amount of something, often content or information, in a given period.  They are characterized by their high output and efficiency.|The rise of social media has created a new breed of hyperproducers who constantly churn out content. 社交媒体的兴起造就了一批不断生产内容的超级生产者。

Hyperproducers of misinformation can significantly impact public discourse.   misinformation的超级生产者会严重影响公众讨论。|近义词：prolific producers, high-output producers, power users|反义词:low producers, infrequent producers|词性:noun
