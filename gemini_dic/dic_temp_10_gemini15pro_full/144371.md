outsting:/ˌaʊtˈstɪŋ/
|v.|胜过，超过|surpasses, exceeds, especially in a painful or unpleasant way|Her performance outstung all the others. 她的表演胜过所有其他人。|近义词：surpasses, exceeds, betters, trumps|反义词：loses to, falls short of
|词性：动词

词源: "outsting" comes from "out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "sting" (originally referring to a sharp, pointed object or the act of piercing with such an object).  The sense of surpassing in pain or unpleasantness developed later.
