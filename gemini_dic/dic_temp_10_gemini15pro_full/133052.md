nonepicureans:/ˌnɒnˌɛpɪkjʊˈriːənz/
|n.|非享乐主义者|People who are not devoted to the pursuit of pleasure; those who do not subscribe to the philosophy of Epicureanism.|例句：The nonepicureans at the feast focused on the conversation, not the food.  宴会上那些非享乐主义者专注于交谈，而不是食物。

例句：The nonepicureans found the lavish spending on luxury goods wasteful. 非享乐主义者认为在奢侈品上的 lavish 开销是浪费的。|近义词：ascetics, stoics|反义词：epicureans, hedonists|词性:名词
