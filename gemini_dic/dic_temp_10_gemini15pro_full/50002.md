denmark:/ˈdɛnmɑːrk/ | n. | 丹麦 | A country in northern Europe, a member of the European Union. | Denmark is known for its high quality of life. 丹麦以其高品质的生活而闻名。|  Denmark is a Scandinavian country. 丹麦是一个斯堪的纳维亚国家。 | 近义词: (无，因为是专有名词) | 反义词: (无，因为是专有名词) | 词性: noun

词源: From Old Norse *Danmǫrk, meaning "the march of the Danes".  The "Danes" refers to the Germanic tribe known as the Dani, and "mark" signifies a border region or march.
