dismount:/dɪsˈmaʊnt/
v.
下马，下车；卸下
To get off of something, typically a horse or bicycle.
The knight dismounted his warhorse. 骑士从他的战马上下来。
alight, get off
mount, board
verb


dismounted:/dɪsˈmaʊn.tɪd/
adj.
已下马的，已下车的
Having gotten off of something, typically a horse or bicycle.
The dismounted cavalrymen formed a defensive line. 下马的骑兵们组成了一道防线。
unmounted
mounted
adjective
