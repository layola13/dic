dictator:/ˈdɪkteɪtər/ (US), /dɪkˈteɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. |独裁者，专制者|A ruler with total power over a country, typically one who has obtained power by force. |例句：The dictator ruled with an iron fist.  这位独裁者实行铁腕统治。

例句：The dictator suppressed all dissent.  独裁者压制了所有异议。

近义词：autocrat, tyrant, despot |反义词: democrat|词性: noun
