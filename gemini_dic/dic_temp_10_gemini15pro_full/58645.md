dumbfounding:/dʌmˈfaʊndɪŋ/| adj.|令人惊愕的，使人目瞪口呆的|Extremely surprising and shocking, making one speechless.|The magician's sudden disappearance was a dumbfounding feat. 魔术师突然消失是一个令人惊愕的壮举。|Synonyms: astonishing, astounding, bewildering, stunning|Antonyms: unsurprising, expected, predictable|adjective

dumbfounding:/dʌmˈfaʊndɪŋ/| v.|使惊呆，使目瞪口呆|(Usually used in the passive form) To make someone speechless with amazement.|She was dumbfounded by the news of her lottery win. 她被中了彩票的消息惊呆了。|Synonyms: astonish, astound, bewilder, stun|Antonyms: bore, tire, expect|verb
