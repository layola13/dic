brickline:/ˈbrɪklaɪn/
n.
砖砌层,砖线
A line formed by bricks, often marking a boundary or edge.
The brickline of the flowerbed was uneven. 花坛的砖砌层不平整。

The brickline clearly marked the property boundary. 这条砖线清晰地标明了房产边界。
近义词: brick course, brick row
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "gap", "opening")
