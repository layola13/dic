absorbers:/əbˈzɔːrbərz/ /əbˈsɔːrbərz/
n.
吸收器；吸收体；减震器
A substance or device that absorbs energy, radiation, or sound, etc.

The car's shock absorbers need to be replaced.
这辆车的减震器需要更换。

Sound absorbers were installed in the recording studio.
录音棚里安装了吸音器。

近义词:dampers, cushions, buffers, attenuators
反义词:emitters, radiators
词性:名词(plural)

ETYMOLOGY->
From absorb (v.) + -er (suffix forming nouns denoting agents or instruments).  Absorb ultimately comes from Latin absorbere, from ab- "away" + sorbere "to suck up".

USAGE->
These are generally used as the plural of "absorber."  Here are some usage examples:

The absorbers in the acoustic panels effectively reduce echo.  这些吸音板中的吸收器有效地减少了回声。

New shock absorbers improved the car's handling.  新的减震器改善了汽车的操控性。


PHRASE->
shock absorbers = 减震器
ultraviolet absorbers = 紫外线吸收剂
sound absorbers = 吸音器
water absorbers = 吸水剂
heat absorbers = 吸热器
