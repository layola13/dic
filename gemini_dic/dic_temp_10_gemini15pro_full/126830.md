naphthalic:/næfˈθælɪk/ | adj. |萘的，萘二甲酸的 | Relating to or derived from naphthalene, especially naphthalene-1,8-dicarboxylic acid. | Naphthalic anhydride is an important intermediate in the production of certain dyes.  萘酐是生产某些染料的重要中间体。 |  Naphthalic acid is a dicarboxylic acid derived from naphthalene. 萘二甲酸是一种由萘衍生的二元羧酸。 | synonyms: naphthalene-based | antonyms:  (No common antonyms) | adjective
