hammer-out:/ˈhæmər aʊt/| phrasal verb |推敲出；敲定；解决|To discuss or negotiate something thoroughly, especially over a period of time, until a solution or agreement is reached.  It often implies arduous work and compromise.|例句：The negotiators hammered out a deal after weeks of talks.  谈判代表们经过数周的会谈终于敲定了一项协议。|例句：They finally hammered out a compromise that satisfied both sides. 他们最终达成了双方都满意的妥协方案。|近义词：work out, thrash out, iron out, negotiate, finalize|反义词:abandon, give up|词性:动词短语
