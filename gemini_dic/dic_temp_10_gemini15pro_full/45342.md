cumulativeness:/ˈkjuːmjʊlətɪvnəs/
n.|累积性，累加性|The property of increasing or growing by successive additions.|The cumulativeness of knowledge allows us to build upon past discoveries. 知识的累积性使我们能够在过去的发现基础上继续发展。|
cumulativeness:/ˌkjuːmjəˈleɪtɪvnəs/
n.|累积性，累加性|The quality of accumulating or being cumulative; the state of being cumulative.|The report highlighted the cumulativeness of the evidence against the defendant.  报告强调了针对被告的证据的累积性。|synonyms: accumulation, aggregation, accrual|antonyms: decrease, reduction, diminution|noun
