droplet:/ˈdrɑːplɪt/ (US) /ˈdrɒplɪt/ (UK)| n. |小滴，微滴|A small drop of liquid.|例句：A single droplet of rain splashed on the window. 一滴雨水溅在窗户上。|例句：The chemist carefully measured out the droplets of the solution.  化学家小心翼翼地量取溶液的小滴。|近义词：drop, bead, globule|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Middle English *dropelette*, diminutive of *drope* (drop), from Old English *dropa*
