cots:/kɑːts/| n.|简易床; 婴儿床|A cot is a small bed, especially one for a baby or a young child. |The baby slept soundly in its cot.  婴儿在婴儿床里睡得很香。|synonyms: crib, cradle, bassinet|antonyms: bed, bunk|noun

cots:/kɑːts/| n.|帆布床; 吊床|A cot can also refer to a lightweight, portable bed, often made of canvas and folding frame.|We set up our cots under the stars. 我们在星空下搭起了帆布床。|synonyms: camp bed, folding bed|antonyms: bed, bunk|noun
