decene: /ˈdiːsiːn/ /ˈdɛsiːn/ | n. |癸烯|An alkene hydrocarbon with the formula C10H20. It is a colorless liquid. Decene refers to a class of isomers, not a single molecule, distinguished by the location of the double bond. |1-Decene is used in the production of epoxides and synthetic lubricants.  癸烯用于生产环氧化物和合成润滑剂。

Decene is an important component in some fuels. 癸烯是某些燃料的重要成分。|近义词: alkene, olefin |反义词: alkane |词性: noun
