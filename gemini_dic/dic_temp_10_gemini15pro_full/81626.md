greco:/ˈɡreɪkoʊ/ /ˈɡriːkəʊ/ | adj. n. | 希腊式的，希腊风格的；希腊人 | Of or relating to Greece, its people, or their language.  A Greek person. | The restaurant serves Greco-Roman cuisine. 这家餐厅供应希腊罗马菜肴。 | He's a Greco living in America. 他是一位住在美国的希腊人。| 近义词:Greek, Hellenic | 反义词:Roman, Latin | 词性:形容词, 名词

词源: From Italian *greco*, from Late Latin *Graecus*, from Latin *Graecus*, ultimately from Ancient Greek *Γραικός (Graikós)*.
