localed: /ˈloʊkəˌlaɪzd/
adjective
本地化的; 局部的
adapted to a particular place or market.  Specifically referring to software or other products that have been translated and adapted for use in a specific region or country.
Example: The software has been localized for the Chinese market.  这个软件已经本地化以适应中国市场。
Example: The pain was localized to his lower back.  疼痛局限于他的下背部。
Synonyms: adapted, regionalized, customized
Antonyms: globalized, internationalized
词性: adjective
