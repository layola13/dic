harbingering:/ˈhɑːrbɪndʒərɪŋ/ | v. |预示，预兆 |Acting as a harbinger; foreshadowing. |The robin is harbingering the arrival of spring. 知更鸟预示着春天的到来。|近义词：foreshadowing, presaging, portending, auguring |反义词：concealing, hiding |词性:动词

Etymology: From *harbinger* + *-ing*. *Harbinger* comes from Middle English *herbergeour*, meaning "one who provides lodging," later "one who goes ahead to arrange lodgings," from Old French *herbergeor*. This sense evolved to "forerunner" and then to "something that foreshadows."
