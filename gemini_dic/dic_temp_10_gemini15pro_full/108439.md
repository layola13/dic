laxative:/ˈlæksətɪv/| n. adj.|泻药，通便的|A medicine or food that loosens the bowels and encourages bowel movements.  A laxative effect.|例句：The doctor prescribed a mild laxative to relieve her constipation. 医生给她开了一种温和的泻药来缓解便秘。

例句：Prunes have a laxative effect. 西梅有通便的作用。|近义词：aperient, cathartic, purgative|反义词：constipating, astringent|词性:名词, 形容词
Etymology->
From Late Latin *laxativus, from Latin laxare "to loosen, open, relax," from laxus "loose, wide, spacious."
