lilywort:/ˈlɪliwɜːrt/ |n.|百合; 水仙|A plant of the genus Lilium; a lily.  Also sometimes applied to other lily-like plants, especially the daffodil.|The lilywort bloomed beautifully in the spring. 百合花在春天开得很漂亮。|Synonym: lily, daffodil (sometimes)|Antonym: N/A|noun
