effusively:/ɪˈfjuːsɪvli/
adverb
热情洋溢地；奔放地
In a manner overflowing with enthusiasm or emotion.
She greeted him effusively, showering him with hugs and kisses. 她热情洋溢地迎接他，拥抱亲吻了他一番。
He thanked her effusively for her help. 他热情洋溢地感谢她的帮助。

enthusiastically, warmly, exuberantly, passionately
apathetically, coldly, indifferently, reservedly
词性: 副词
