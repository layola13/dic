indecomposable:/ˌɪndɪkəmˈpoʊzəbl/
adj.
不可分解的，不可拆分的
not able to be separated into simpler parts or elements.
This protein is an indecomposable molecule.  这种蛋白质是一个不可分解的分子。
The argument presents an indecomposable whole.  这个论证呈现出一个不可分割的整体。
近义词: indivisible, inseparable, irreducible
反义词: decomposable, divisible, separable
词性: 形容词


Etymology:
From in- "not" + decomposable, from decompose + -able.  Decompose ultimately comes from Latin *decomponere, from de- "down, away" + componere "to put together," from com- "together" + ponere "to put, place."
