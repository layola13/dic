bootlicker:/ˈbuːtlɪkər/| n.|拍马屁的人，谄媚者|A person who behaves obsequiously to those in power, especially in order to gain favor.|He's such a bootlicker, always praising the boss even when she's wrong. 他真是个马屁精，即使老板错了也总是赞扬她。|Synonyms: sycophant, flatterer, yes-man, brown-noser|Antonyms: rebel, nonconformist|Noun
