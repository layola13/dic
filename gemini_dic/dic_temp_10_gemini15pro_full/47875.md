decelerating: /diːˈsɛləreɪtɪŋ/
adjective
减速的，变慢的
losing speed; slowing down.
The car is decelerating as it approaches the traffic light. 汽车在接近交通灯时正在减速。
The decelerating economy is a cause for concern. 经济增长放缓令人担忧。

近义词: slowing, reducing speed, braking
反义词: accelerating, speeding up
词性: adjective (形容词)

词源:  From the verb "decelerate," which comes from the Latin prefix "de-" (down, away from) + "celer" (swift) + "-ate" (verb suffix).
