melancholist: /ˈmɛləŋkɒlɪst/ (UK) /ˈmɛlənˌkɑːlɪst/ (US)| n.| 忧郁症患者; 悲观主义者| A person who is habitually melancholic; a person prone to or affected by melancholy. | He was a melancholist, always dwelling on the negative aspects of life. 他是个忧郁症患者，总是沉湎于生活的消极面。|Synonyms: pessimist, cynic, depressive | Antonyms: optimist, cheerful person | noun

melancholy: /ˈmɛləŋkɒli/ (UK) /ˈmɛlənˌkɑːli/ (US) | n. adj.| 忧郁; 忧伤 | A feeling of pensive sadness, typically with no obvious cause. / Sad and pensive. | A deep melancholy settled over him.  一种深深的忧郁笼罩着他。| Synonyms: sadness, dejection, gloom | Antonyms: happiness, joy, cheerfulness | noun, adjective
