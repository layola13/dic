melodeon:/məˈloʊdiən/ | n. |簧风琴，手风琴 |A small portable reed organ, played by pumping bellows and pressing keys or buttons. | He played a lively tune on his melodeon. 他用他的簧风琴演奏了一首轻快的曲子。| The street musician's melodeon filled the air with cheerful music. 街头音乐家的簧风琴让空气中充满了欢快的音乐。| 近义词: accordion, concertina | 反义词: None | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *mélodèon*, from Greek *μελῳδία* (melōidia, “singing, song, melody”) + *-on* (suffix forming instrumental nouns).
