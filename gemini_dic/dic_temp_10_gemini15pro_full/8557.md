anticigarette:/ˌæntiˈsɪɡəˌrɛt/
adjective
反吸烟的; 反对吸烟的
Opposed to the smoking of cigarettes.
例句：
The anticigarette campaign was successful in reducing the number of smokers.
反吸烟运动成功地减少了吸烟者的数量。

He has a strong anticigarette stance.
他有一个强烈的反吸烟立场。

近义词：antismoking, nonsmoking
反义词：pro-smoking
