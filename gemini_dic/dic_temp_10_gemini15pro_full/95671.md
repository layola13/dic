inconceivablenesses:/ɪnkənˈsiːvəbəlnəsɪz/
n.
不可想象性；不可思议的事物
the state or quality of being impossible to imagine or conceive.
The vastness of space and the inconceivablenesses of the universe filled her with awe.  浩瀚的宇宙和不可思议的宇宙现象让她充满了敬畏。
Near synonyms:  unimaginability, incredibility, impossibility, implausibility
Antonyms: conceivability, possibility, plausibility, credibility
noun
