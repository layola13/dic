carrageen: /ˈkærəɡiːn/ | n. | 角叉菜；角叉菜胶 | A type of red seaweed (specifically *Chondrus crispus*) and the gelatinous substance extracted from it, used as a thickener and stabilizer in food and other products. | The pudding uses carrageen as a thickener.  这布丁用角叉菜胶做增稠剂。| synonym: Irish moss, seaweed | antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider substances with opposite effects, such as thinning agents) | noun
