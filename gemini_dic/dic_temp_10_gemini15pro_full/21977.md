bohemian:/boʊˈhiːmiən/ | n. adj. | 波西米亚人，放荡不羁的艺术家 | A person, especially an artist, writer, or musician, who lives an unconventional life, often in a colony with others, and disregards conventional rules of behavior.  |  She led a bohemian lifestyle, traveling the world and writing poetry. 她过着波西米亚式的生活，周游世界，写诗。 |  nonconformist, unconventional, free-spirited | conventional, traditional, bourgeois | noun, adjective

bohemia:/boʊˈhiːmiə/ | n. | 波西米亚 | A historical region of central Europe, now part of the Czech Republic.  Also refers to a place or community inhabited by bohemians.  | Prague is the capital of Bohemia. 布拉格是波西米亚的首都。 |  - | - | noun
