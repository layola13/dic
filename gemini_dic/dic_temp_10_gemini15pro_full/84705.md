handicapped's:/ˈhændɪkæptz/ | n. (possessive form, now largely considered offensive) | 残疾人的（所有格形式，现在很大程度上被认为具有冒犯性） |  This term refers to something belonging to or associated with a person with a disability. However, it is now generally considered outdated and offensive due to its implication of limitation and dependence.  Using "person with a disability" and focusing on their abilities rather than their limitations is preferred. |  (Example usage is discouraged due to the offensive nature of the term.  Avoid using this term.) | 近义词: disabled person's (use with caution), person with a disability's | 反义词:  able-bodied person's | 词性: noun (possessive)
