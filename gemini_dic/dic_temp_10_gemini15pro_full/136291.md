nonrepresentative:/ˌnɒnˌreprɪˈzentətɪv/ | adj. | 非代表性的，不典型的 | Not representative; not typical of a particular group or thing. | This small group is nonrepresentative of the population as a whole.  这个小组并不能代表整体人口。|  The survey results are nonrepresentative because the sample size was too small.  由于样本量太小，调查结果不具有代表性。 | atypical, uncharacteristic, biased | representative, typical | adjective
