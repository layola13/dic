misreflected:/ˌmɪsrɪˈflektɪd/
adjective
反射错误的，曲解的
incompletely, inaccurately, or falsely reflected
The curved mirror misreflected her image, making her look much taller and thinner. 扭曲的镜子错误地反射了她的形象，使她看起来更高更瘦。
misrepresented, distorted
accurately reflected, faithfully represented
