grief:/ɡriːf/| n.|悲痛，悲伤，忧伤|Deep sorrow, especially that caused by someone's death.|She was overcome with grief when her husband died. 她丈夫去世后，她悲痛欲绝。|Synonyms: sorrow, sadness, anguish, distress|Antonyms: joy, happiness|noun

grief:/ɡriːf/| v.|使悲伤，使苦恼|To cause someone to feel grief.|It grieved her to see her son so unhappy. 看到儿子如此不开心，她感到很悲伤。|Synonyms: sadden, distress, upset|Antonyms: gladden, please|verb
