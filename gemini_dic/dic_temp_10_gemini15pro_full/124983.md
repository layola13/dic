mugging's:/ˈmʌɡɪŋz/
|n.|抢劫(行为),行凶(行为)|The act of attacking and robbing someone in a public place.|The mugging's impact on the victim was severe, leaving them traumatized. 这起抢劫对受害者的影响非常严重，给他们留下了心理创伤。|近义词:attack, robbery, assault|反义词:defense, protection|词性:名词

词源: From the verb "mug," meaning to attack and rob someone, with the "-ing" suffix forming a gerund (verbal noun) and the "-'s" indicating possession. The word "mug" itself likely originated from the idea of attacking someone's face, which was slang for "mouth" or "face."
