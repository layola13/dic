overimaginativenesses:/ˌoʊvərɪˌmædʒɪˈnətɪvnəsɪz/
|n.|过度想象力（复数）|The state of having an excessively or unrealistically active imagination.  This refers to instances where someone's imagination runs wild, creating scenarios or beliefs that are detached from reality.|His overimaginativenesses often led him to believe in conspiracies.|近义词：fantasies, delusions, flights of fancy|反义词：realism, practicality|词性: Noun

Etymology:
From "over-" (excessive) + "imaginative" (having or showing creativity or inventiveness) + "-ness" (forming nouns expressing a state or quality) + "-es" (plural suffix). The root word "imagine" comes from the Latin "imaginari," meaning "to form a mental image."
