antiscientific:/ˌæntiˌsaɪənˈtɪfɪk/
adjective
反科学的
Opposed to or contradicting science; rejecting the methods or principles of science.
His antiscientific views made it difficult for him to accept the evidence. 他反科学的观点使他难以接受证据。
Near Antonyms: scientific, pro-science, rational, empirical
Antonyms: proscientific
