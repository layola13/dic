desserts:/dɪˈzɜːrts/
noun
甜点
A usually sweet course or dish (as of pastry or ice cream) served at the end of a meal.
After dinner, we had delicious chocolate desserts.  晚餐后，我们吃了美味的巧克力甜点。
synonyms: sweets, treats, pudding, afters
antonyms: appetizers, starters
n.

Etymology: From Old French *desservir "to clear the table," from des- "un-, off" + servir "to serve."  Originally referred to the clearing of the table after the main meal, hence the course served after the main course is cleared.
