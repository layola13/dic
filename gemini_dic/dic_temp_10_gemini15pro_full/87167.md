herby:/ˈhɜːrbi/ | adj. | 草本的；香草味的 | Containing or tasting of herbs. |  The soup had a lovely herby flavour. 这汤有一股可爱的草本香味。| aromatic, savory, fragrant | bland, tasteless | adjective

herby:/ˈɜːrbi/ | adj. | 草本的；香草味的 | Resembling or characteristic of herbs. | The garden had a distinctly herby smell. 花园里有一股明显的草药味。 | grassy, leafy | barren, desert | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From herb + -y.  "Herb" comes from Old French "herbe" and ultimately from Latin "herba" meaning "grass," "green crops," or "plant." The suffix "-y" forms adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "full of."
