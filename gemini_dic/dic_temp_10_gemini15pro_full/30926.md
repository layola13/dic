chainwork:/ˈtʃeɪnwɜːrk/
noun
链上工作量，累计工作量
The total amount of computational work exerted on a blockchain, typically measured in hashes or similar units.  It represents the cumulative difficulty of all blocks in the chain.
The Bitcoin network's chainwork has steadily increased over time. 比特币网络的链上工作量随着时间的推移稳步增长。
The chain with the highest chainwork is considered the valid blockchain in Proof-of-Work systems. 在工作量证明系统中，拥有最高链上工作量的链被认为是有效的区块链。
cumulative work, total work, accumulated difficulty
