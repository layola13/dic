desertlike:/ˈdɛzətˌlaɪk/
adjective
像沙漠的，荒凉的
Resembling a desert; barren, arid.
The landscape was desertlike, with only sparse vegetation. 这片景象如同沙漠一般，只有稀疏的植被。
The surface of Mars is desertlike. 火星表面像沙漠一样。
Synonyms: barren, arid, desolate, deserted, infertile
Antonyms: fertile, lush, verdant, productive
word type: adjective
