boughed:/baʊd/
adjective
枝繁叶茂的；低垂的；弯曲的
having branches that curve downwards; bent over
The apple tree, boughed down with fruit, was a welcome sight. 那棵果实累累、枝条低垂的苹果树，真是令人赏心悦目。
近义词: arched, drooping, laden
反义词: straight, upright
词性: adjective

bough:/baʊ/
noun
大树枝;粗枝
a main branch of a tree
The monkey swung from bough to bough. 猴子在树枝间荡来荡去。
近义词: branch, limb
反义词: trunk, root
词性: noun

词源: From Middle English *bow, *bogh, from Old English bōg (“bough, branch, shoulder, bow”), from Proto-Germanic *bōgz (“bow, shoulder”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰewǵ- (“to bend, curve”). Cognate with Scots bou (“bough”), West Frisian boech (“bow, bend”), Dutch boog (“bow, bend, arch, vault”), German Bug (“bow, bend, curve, shoulder, haunch”), Danish bug (“bow, bend, curve, shoulder, haunch”), Swedish bug (“bow, bend, curve, shoulder, haunch”), Icelandic bogi (“bow”). Related to bow (v.).
