eider: /ˈaɪ.dər/ | n. | 绒鸭; 绒鸭绒; | A large sea duck that lives in northern regions and has soft, warm down. |  例句：The eider duck is known for its exceptionally warm down. 绒鸭以其异常温暖的绒毛而闻名。

例句：Eiderdown is a luxurious and insulating material. 绒鸭绒是一种奢华且保暖的材料。| 近义词：duck, seaduck | 反义词:  (无明确反义词) | 词性: 名词
