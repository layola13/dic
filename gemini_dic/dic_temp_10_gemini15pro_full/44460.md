cross-vault:/ˈkrɒs vɔːlt/ (US) /ˈkrɔːs vɔːlt/ (UK)| n.|交叉拱顶|A vault formed by the intersection of two barrel vaults of the same diameter which cross at right angles.  The groins thus formed are elliptical curves.|The cathedral boasted a magnificent cross-vault.  这座大教堂拥有宏伟的交叉拱顶。|Synonyms: groin vault, quadripartite vault|Antonyms: N/A|noun
cross-vault:/ˈkrɒs vɔːlt/ (US) /ˈkrɔːs vɔːlt/ (UK)| v.|建造交叉拱顶|To construct a cross-vault.|They cross-vaulted the ceiling of the chapel.  他们用交叉拱顶建造了小教堂的天花板。|Synonyms: construct a groin vault|Antonyms: N/A|verb
