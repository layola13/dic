inscrutableness:/ˌɪnˈskruːtəbəlnəs/
n.
神秘莫测；难以理解；高深莫测
the quality of being impossible to understand or interpret.
His face maintained its usual inscrutableness. 他的脸上保持着一如既往的神秘莫测。
inscrutability, impenetrability, unfathomability, incomprehensibility
transparency, clarity, obviousness
noun
