cite:/saɪt/ | v. | 引用，引证，提及 | to mention something as proof for a statement or as a reason why something should be done |  The lawyer cited two similar cases. 律师引用了两个类似的案例。| quote, refer to, mention | omit, ignore | verb


cite:/saɪt/ | n. | 引用，引证 | an act of quoting or mentioning something as an example or proof |  The article contained several citations from the Bible. 这篇文章包含了几个来自圣经的引用。| quotation, reference, mention | omission | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *citāre*, frequentative of ciēre (“to move, rouse, summon”).
