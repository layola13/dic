nonlugubrious:/ˌnɒnlʊˈɡuːbriəs/
adjective
不悲伤的，不忧郁的
Not lugubrious; cheerful; not mournful.
Her nonlugubrious demeanor at the funeral surprised everyone. 她葬礼上并不悲伤的态度让每个人都很惊讶。
Synonyms: cheerful, merry, joyful, happy, lighthearted, jovial, upbeat, positive, optimistic, bright, sunny, buoyant, chirpy, perky, blithe, carefree, untroubled, serene, tranquil, peaceful, content, satisfied, glad, pleased, delighted, elated, ecstatic, jubilant, joyous, rapturous
Antonyms: lugubrious, mournful, sad, sorrowful, gloomy, depressed, dejected, despondent, melancholy, miserable, unhappy, downcast, glum, morose, doleful, somber, grim, dour, sullen, surly, grumpy, bad-tempered, irritable, crotchety, cantankerous, churlish
Etymology:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + lugubrious (from Latin *lūgubris, from lūgēre "to mourn")
