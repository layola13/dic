aeroplanist:/ˈɛəːrəʊpleɪnɪst/| n.|飞行员，飞机驾驶员|A person who flies or operates an airplane.|Amelia Earhart was a famous aeroplanist. 艾米莉亚·埃尔哈特是一位著名的飞行员。

The Wright brothers were the first successful aeroplanists. 莱特兄弟是第一批成功的飞行员。|近义词：pilot, aviator, flyer|反义词:passenger|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From aero- (relating to air) + planist (one who operates a plane).  "Plane" in this context is short for "aeroplane," an older term for "airplane."
