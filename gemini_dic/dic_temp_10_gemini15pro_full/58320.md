dry land:/ˈdraɪ lænd/ | adj. |(比喻)枯燥无味的；无趣味的 | (Figurative) Lacking excitement or interest. | The lecture was a bit dry land.  这个讲座有点枯燥无味。| synonyms: dull, boring, tedious | antonyms: exciting, interesting, stimulating | adjective
