adjudicatory: /əˈdʒuːdɪkətɔːri/ /əˈdʒuːdɪkeɪtɔːri/
adjective
审判的；裁决的
Relating to the process of making a formal judgment or decision, especially in a court of law or other official setting.
The adjudicatory hearing lasted for three days. = 听证会持续了三天。
The committee has an adjudicatory function in disputes between members. = 该委员会在成员之间的争议中具有裁决职能。

近义词: judicial, juridic, legal
反义词:  non-judicial, extrajudicial
词性: adjective
