ganglion:/ˈɡæŋɡlɪən/ | n. |神经节；(植物)节 |A structure containing a number of nerve cell bodies, typically linked by synapses, and often forming a swelling on a nerve fibre.|The doctor discovered a ganglion on his wrist. 医生在他的手腕上发现了一个神经节。|近义词:nerve center, node|反义词: N/A | noun

ganglia:/ˈɡæŋɡliə/ | n. |神经节（ganglion的复数形式）|The plural form of ganglion, referring to multiple structures containing nerve cell bodies.|The surgeon carefully removed the ganglia. 外科医生小心翼翼地切除了这些神经节。|近义词: nerve centers, nodes|反义词: N/A | noun

词源:  From Late Latin *ganglion, from Ancient Greek γάγγλιον (gánglion, “tumor, nerve ganglion”), diminutive of γάγγλιο (gánglio).
