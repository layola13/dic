nonexistential:/ˌnɑːnɪɡzɪˈstɛnʃəl/
adjective
非存在的; 不存在的
Relating to something that does not exist.  Something that is hypothetical or lacking in reality.
His fears about the monster were completely nonexistential. 他对怪物的恐惧完全是不存在的。
The threat was nonexistential, a mere figment of his imagination.  这种威胁是不存在的，仅仅是他想象的产物。
近义词: nonexistent, imaginary, unreal, hypothetical, fictitious
反义词: real, existent, actual, factual, concrete
词性: adjective

nonexistent:/ˌnɑːnɪɡˈzɪstənt/
adjective
不存在的
Not existing; not real or actual.
The problem is nonexistent. 这个问题不存在。
Proof of the nonexistent creature was impossible to find.  不可能找到这种不存在的生物的证据。
近义词: nonexistential, imaginary, unreal, hypothetical, fictitious
反义词: real, existent, actual, factual, concrete
词性: adjective
