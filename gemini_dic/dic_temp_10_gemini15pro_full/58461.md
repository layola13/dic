ductilities: /ˌdʌkˈtɪlətiz/
| n. |延展性，韧性；可塑性|The ductilities of various metals are tested under stress. 各种金属的延展性都在压力下进行了测试。|Ductility is the ability of a material to be drawn out into a wire without breaking.  延展性是指一种材料在不断裂的情况下被拉成丝的能力。|例句：The ductilities of copper allow it to be easily shaped into wires. 铜的延展性使其易于塑造成电线。

例句：The alloy's high ductilities make it ideal for intricate designs.  这种合金的高延展性使其成为复杂设计的理想选择。


|近义词：malleability, flexibility, plasticity, pliability|反义词: brittleness, rigidity, stiffness|词性: 名词(plural)


词源:
From Late Middle English: from Old French *ductilité or late Latin ductilitas, from Latin ductilis ‘able to be led’, from ducere ‘to lead’.
