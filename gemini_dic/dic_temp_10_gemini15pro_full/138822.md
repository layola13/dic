nuncupate:/ˈnʌŋkjʊˌpeɪt/| vt.|指定；口头宣称；口头遗赠|To declare or name something orally; especially to make a will orally.|He nuncupated his wishes on his deathbed. 他在临终前口头宣布了他的遗愿。|近义词：declare, pronounce, nominate, designate|反义词: rescind, revoke|verb

Etymology:
From Latin *nuncupatus*, past participle of *nuncupare* "to name, call, declare," from *nomen* "name" + *capere* "to take."
