backbeat:/ˈbækbiːt/| n.|反拍，重拍|A steady, prominent accent on the second and fourth beats of a four-beat measure, characteristic of rock and roll and other popular music genres.  It's often emphasized by the snare drum or other percussion instruments.|The drummer laid down a solid backbeat that drove the song forward.  鼓手打出了一个坚实的反拍，推动了歌曲的前进。|Synonyms:  accent, stress, pulse|Antonyms:  offbeat|noun
