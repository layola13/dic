defame:/dɪˈfeɪm/ | vt. | 诽谤，中伤 | to harm someone's reputation by saying or writing bad things about them that are not true | The article defamed him by claiming he was corrupt. 这篇文章声称他腐败，对他进行了诽谤。 | synonyms: slander, libel, malign, vilify | antonyms: praise, commend, extol | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *defamer, from Latin diffamare (“to spread abroad a bad report”), from dis- (“apart, away”) + fama (“fame, reputation”).
