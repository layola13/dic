gormlessly: /ˈɡɔːmləsli/ | adv. | 愚蠢地，呆头呆脑地 | In a stupid and vacuous manner; without showing thought or intelligence. | He stared gormlessly at the television. 他呆呆地盯着电视。|  She gormlessly smiled and waved. 她傻笑着挥手。 | 近义词: stupidly, foolishly, vacantly, blankly, idiotically | 反义词: intelligently, shrewdly, wisely, astutely | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From gormless + -ly.  Gormless itself is from gorm (meaning "sense, attention, understanding") + -less.  The word "gorm" is now obsolete.  It likely comes from Old Norse *gaumr, meaning "heed, attention".
