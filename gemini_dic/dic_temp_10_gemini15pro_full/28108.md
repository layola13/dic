capitalizable:/ˈkæpɪtəlaɪzəbl/| adj.|可大写的，可资本化的|Something that is capitalizable can be written with a capital letter, often referring to proper nouns or the first word in a sentence.  It can also refer to costs that can be included as an asset on a balance sheet rather than being expensed immediately.|This acronym is capitalizable.  这个首字母缩略词可以大写。| The cost of the new machinery is capitalizable. 新机器的成本可以资本化。|近义词: uppercaseable|反义词: non-capitalizable|词性:形容词
