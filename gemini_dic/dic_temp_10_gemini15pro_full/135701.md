nonpressure:/ˈnɒnˈprɛʃə(r)/ (UK) /ˈnɑːnˈprɛʃər/ (US)| adj.|非压力性的，非加压的|Relating to or denoting a system or process that does not involve or require pressure.|This is a nonpressure process, so it's relatively safe. 这是一个非压力过程，所以相对安全。|The pipes are designed for nonpressure applications. 这些管道设计用于非压力应用。|近义词:low-pressure, unpressurized|反义词:pressure, pressurized|词性:形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non*, meaning "not"
pressure (noun): from Old French *presser*, from Latin *pressare* "to press"
