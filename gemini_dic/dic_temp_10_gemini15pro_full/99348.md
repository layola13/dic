interdenominational:/ˌɪntərdiˌnɑːməˈneɪʃənl/ /ˌɪntərdiˌnɑːmɪˈneɪʃənl/
adjective
跨教派的；不分教派的
Involving or relating to different Christian denominations.
The interdenominational service was attended by people from many different churches.  跨教派的礼拜仪式有来自许多不同教堂的人参加。
An interdenominational prayer group meets weekly at the community center.  一个跨教派的祈祷小组每周在社区中心聚会。
interfaith, ecumenical, nondenominational
sectarian, denominational
adjective
