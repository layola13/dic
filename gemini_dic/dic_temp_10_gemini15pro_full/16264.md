banzai:/bænˈzaɪ/| interj. n.| 万岁；进攻的呼喊 | A Japanese exclamation, originally used to wish the emperor long life, but later adopted as a war cry by attacking Japanese troops. |例句：The soldiers charged screaming "Banzai!". 士兵们喊着“万岁！”冲锋陷阵。

例句：The crowd shouted "Banzai!" as the team won. 球队获胜时，人群高呼“万岁！”。|近义词：hurrah, cheers|反义词: (No direct antonym for a celebratory exclamation. Perhaps words expressing defeat or surrender could be considered loosely antonymous, such as "retreat" or "surrender")|词性: interjection, noun
