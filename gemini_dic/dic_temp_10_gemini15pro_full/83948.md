half-confederate:/ˌhæf kənˈfɛdərɪt/ | adj. | 半联盟的；半同谋的；不完全支持的 | Partly or incompletely allied or supportive; sharing some but not all of the characteristics of a confederate. | He played a half-confederate role, supporting the cause publicly but privately expressing doubts. 他扮演了一个半联盟的角色，公开支持这项事业，但私下表示怀疑。 |  She gave him a half-confederate glance, acknowledging his presence but not engaging in conversation. 她给了他一个半联盟的一瞥，承认他的存在，但没有参与谈话。 | 近义词: half-hearted, lukewarm, ambivalent | 反义词: fully supportive, committed, dedicated | 词性: adjective

词源: "half-" (Old English *healfu) + "confederate" (from Latin confœderātus, past participle of confœderāre "to unite by league")
