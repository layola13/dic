afflux:/ˈæflʌks/| n.| 流入；汇集；涌入| a flowing or pouring in; a sudden arrival of large numbers of people or things|例句：The afflux of tourists during the summer months strains the resources of the small town. 夏季几个月中游客的涌入给小镇的资源带来了压力。

例句：The country experienced a huge afflux of refugees fleeing the war.  这个国家经历了大量逃离战争的难民涌入。|近义词：influx, inflow, inrush, surge, flood|反义词: outflow, efflux, exodus|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *affluxus, from Latin ad- (“to, towards”) + fluere (“to flow”).  *Affluxus is the past participle of affluere (“to flow to”).
