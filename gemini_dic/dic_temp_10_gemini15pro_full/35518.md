coagent:/ˈkoʊˌeɪdʒənt/| n.|合作者，共同代理人|A person or thing that works with another.  A coagent is someone who shares responsibility and authority with another person in carrying out a particular task or duty.|The two lawyers acted as coagents in the complex negotiation. 这两名律师在复杂的谈判中担任共同代理人。|Synonyms: collaborator, partner, associate, confederate|Antonyms: opponent, adversary, rival|noun

coagent:/ˈkoʊˌeɪdʒənt/| adj.|共同作用的|Acting together with another agent.|The coagent forces brought about the desired outcome. 共同作用的力量带来了预期的结果。|Synonyms: joint, combined, collaborative|Antonyms: separate, individual, independent|adjective
