mellowing:/ˈmɛloʊɪŋ/
adjective
变得柔和，变得成熟，变得芳醇
becoming softer and sweeter; becoming more relaxed and less intense
The cheese is mellowing nicely.  这种奶酪正在变得芳醇。
近义词:softening, maturing, ripening, aging
反义词:hardening, worsening, deteriorating
词性:adjective (形容词)

mellow:/ˈmɛloʊ/
adjective
柔和的，成熟的，芳醇的
soft, sweet, and ripe; smooth and pleasant; relaxed and pleasant
The wine has a mellow flavor.  这酒味道醇厚。
近义词:soft, smooth, rich, gentle
反义词:harsh, sharp, bitter
词性:adjective (形容词)

mellow:/ˈmɛloʊ/
verb
使柔和，使成熟，使芳醇
to become or make something softer and sweeter; to become or make someone more relaxed and less intense
The years have mellowed him. 岁月使他变得成熟了。
近义词:soften, mature, ripen, age
反义词:harden, worsen, deteriorate
词性:verb (动词)
