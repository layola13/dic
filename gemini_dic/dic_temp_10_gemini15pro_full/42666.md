counterstrategy:/ˈkaʊntərˌstrætədʒi/| n. |反策略，应对策略|A plan of action designed to counteract or oppose another strategy.|The company developed a counterstrategy to compete with their rival's new product.  这家公司制定了一个反策略来与竞争对手的新产品竞争。|例句：Their counterstrategy involved a surprise attack. 他们的反策略包括一次突袭。|近义词：countermeasure, counterplan, countertactic|反义词:strategy|noun
