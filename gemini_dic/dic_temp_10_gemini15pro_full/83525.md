hades:/ˈheɪdiːz/| n.|冥府，地狱|The ancient Greeks believed that souls went to Hades after death. 古希腊人相信灵魂在死后会去冥府。|Hades is often depicted as a dark and gloomy underworld. 冥府通常被描绘成一个黑暗阴沉的地下世界。|近义词：underworld, hell, inferno|反义词：heaven, paradise|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ᾍδης (Háidēs), the name of the god of the underworld.  Originally an euphemism, perhaps meaning "the unseen one".
