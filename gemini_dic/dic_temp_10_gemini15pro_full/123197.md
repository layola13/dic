monochlamydeous:/ˌmɒnəʊkləˈmɪdiəs/
adjective
单被花的，单层被的
Having a perianth composed of a single whorl of tepals, rather than distinct sepals and petals.  The perianth is undifferentiated and consists of only one series of segments.
Example: The amaryllis family is known for its monochlamydeous flowers, showcasing a single whorl of tepals.  石蒜科以其单被花而闻名，展示了单轮花被片。

Example: Monochlamydeous flowers, like those of the elm tree, have a perianth that isn't clearly divided into sepals and petals. 像榆树这样的单被花，其花被没有明显地分成萼片和花瓣。

synonym: apetalous (sometimes, but this primarily refers to lacking petals), haplochlamydeous
antonym: dichlamydeous
