easements:/ˈiːzmənts/
n.
地役权；通行权
A right to cross or otherwise use someone else's land for a specified purpose.
The farmer granted an easement to the power company so they could run lines across his fields. 农民授予电力公司地役权，以便他们可以在他的田地上架设线路。
right of way, access
restriction, prohibition
noun
