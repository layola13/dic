ensoul:/ɪnˈsoʊl/| vt.|赋予灵魂；使充满活力；鼓舞|To endow with a soul or spirit; to make lively or spirited.|He wrote poems that ensouled the landscape of his childhood. 他写的诗歌赋予了他童年时代的风景以灵魂。|近义词：animate, inspire, enliven, vitalize|反义词：dead, devitalize|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From en- (to put into or on) + soul (spirit).  The prefix "en-" adds the meaning of "to cause to be" or "to provide with."
