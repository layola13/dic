derogate:/ˈdɛrəɡeɪt/ | v. | 贬低，损害，毁损 | to lessen the value or importance of; detract |  The scandal derogated from his reputation.  这桩丑闻损害了他的名誉。 | synonyms: belittle, disparage, depreciate | antonyms: enhance, elevate, praise | verb

derogate:/ˈdɛrəɡeɪt/ | adj. |  贬损的；减损的 (rare) |  Used to describe something that derogates from something else, usually in legal contexts.  |  The derogate clause in the contract limited his liability. 合同中的减损条款限制了他的责任。| synonyms: detracting, diminishing | antonyms: enhancing, augmenting | adjective
