outdragging:/ˌaʊtˈdræɡɪŋ/
verb
胜过；在拖曳中胜过
surpassing someone in dragging something; beating someone in a contest of dragging
Example: He was outdragging his opponent in the tug-of-war. 他在拔河比赛中胜过了他的对手。
Example: The team was outdragging the other in the rope pulling contest.  这个队在拉绳比赛中胜过了另一个队。

近义词: outpulling, outdoing, surpassing
反义词: losing to, being outdone by
词性: verb
