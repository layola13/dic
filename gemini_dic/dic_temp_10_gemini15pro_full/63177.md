entailment:/ɪnˈteɪlmənt/| n.|蕴涵，包含；必然结果|Something that is entailed is a necessary part or consequence of something else.  A situation that entails something involves it or makes it necessary.|Building a new airport entails relocating several families. 建设新机场需要搬迁几个家庭。|synonyms: consequence, result, implication, necessity|antonyms: option, choice, irrelevance|noun

entail:/ɪnˈteɪl/| vt.|牵涉；需要；使必要|If one thing entails another, it involves it or causes it to happen.|Such a large investment inevitably entails some risk. 如此巨大的投资必然会带来一些风险。|synonyms: involve, necessitate, require, demand|antonyms: exclude, prevent, preclude|verb
