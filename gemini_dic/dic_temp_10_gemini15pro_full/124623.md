moussing:/ˈmaʊsɪŋ/ | n. |起泡沫，冒泡|The act of forming or producing a mousse-like foam or froth. |The beer was moussing over the top of the glass.  啤酒的泡沫溢出了杯子。|近义词：foaming, frothing, bubbling|反义词：flattening, settling|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French "mousser," meaning "to foam," from "mousse," meaning "foam." Ultimately from Latin "mulsa," meaning "honey-sweet wine."
