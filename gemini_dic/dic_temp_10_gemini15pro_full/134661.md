nonmanipulative:/ˌnɒn.məˈnɪpjʊ.lə.tɪv/
adjective
非操控性的,非操纵性的
Not influencing or attempting to influence others by devious or indirect means.
She appreciated his nonmanipulative approach to their relationship. 她很欣赏他在他们关系中不操控的方式。
He gave her nonmanipulative advice, allowing her to make her own decisions. 他给了她非操控性的建议，让她自己做决定。
近义词: straightforward, honest, direct, frank, open, genuine
反义词: manipulative, controlling, scheming, devious, cunning
词性: adjective
