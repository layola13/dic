besiegements:/bɪˈsiːdʒmənts/ | n. |包围，围攻，围困 | The prolonged besiegements of the city caused widespread famine.  The act or process of besieging a place. | 例句：The besiegements of the city lasted for months. 这座城市的围困持续了数月。

例句：The castle withstood numerous besiegements throughout history. 这座城堡在历史上抵御了无数次围攻。


近义词：sieges, blockades | 反义词：relief, liberation | 词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *besegement, from Old French besegement, from besegier (“to besiege”), from be- (“thoroughly”) + siege (“seat, throne; siege”).
