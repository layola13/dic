nonsensical:/nɒnˈsɛnsɪk(ə)l/ /nɑːnˈsɛnsɪk(ə)l/
adjective
荒谬的；无意义的
lacking in sense; absurd
Her story was completely nonsensical. 她的故事完全是胡说八道。
The lyrics to that song are nonsensical. 那首歌的歌词毫无意义。
近义词: absurd, illogical, meaningless, foolish, silly, preposterous
反义词: sensible, logical, meaningful, rational
词性: adjective
