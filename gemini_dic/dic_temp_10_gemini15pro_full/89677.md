hoopoe:/ˈhuːpuː/| n.|戴胜鸟|A brightly colored bird with a long, downcurved bill and an erectile crest.|例句：The hoopoe is known for its distinctive crown of feathers. 戴胜鸟以其独特的羽毛冠而闻名。

例句：We spotted a hoopoe foraging for insects in the grass. 我们看到一只戴胜鸟在草地上觅食昆虫。|近义词：None (Specific bird)|反义词:None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *huppe*, from Latin *upupa*, imitative of the bird's cry.
