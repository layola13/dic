nonflagrance:/nɒnˈfleɪɡrəns/| n.|无现行犯的状态，非现行犯罪|The suspect was arrested hours after the crime, due to the nonflagrance of the offense.  由于不是现行犯罪，嫌疑人在犯罪几个小时后才被逮捕。|English explanation: The state of not being caught in the act of committing a crime.  |例句：The police couldn't arrest him for nonflagrance.  由于没有当场抓住他犯罪，警察无法逮捕他。

Because of the nonflagrance of the act, the police had to build a stronger case before making an arrest. 由于该行为并非现行犯，警方在逮捕前必须建立更强有力的案件。 |近义词：absence of flagrancy, non-flagrancy|反义词:flagrance, in flagrante delicto|词性:noun
