leafing: /ˈliːfɪŋ/ | n. | 发芽；翻页 | The process of producing leaves; turning pages of a book | The trees are leafing out in spring. 树木在春天发芽。 She was leafing through a magazine. 她正在翻阅一本杂志。 | synonyms: sprouting, burgeoning, paging, browsing | antonyms: defoliating, wilting | noun

leafing: /ˈliːfɪŋ/ | adj. | 长出叶子的；翻书页的 | Producing leaves; turning pages of a book |  The leafing trees looked beautiful. 长出叶子的树木看起来很美。  Her leafing hand paused on a photograph. 她翻页的手在一张照片上停了下来。 | synonyms: budding, foliating, perusing, skimming | antonyms: bare, leafless | adjective
