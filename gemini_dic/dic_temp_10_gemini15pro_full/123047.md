monerula:/məˈnɪərʊlə/ | n. |桑椹胚|A solid ball of cells resulting from the cleavage of a fertilized ovum, and from which a blastula is formed.|The monerula stage is critical for the development of the embryo. 桑椹胚阶段对胚胎发育至关重要。|Synonyms: morula | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but could consider later developmental stages like blastula, gastrula) | Noun
