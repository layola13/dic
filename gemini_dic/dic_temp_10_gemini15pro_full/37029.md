comae:/ˈkəʊmiː/ (British) /ˈkoʊmiː/ (American)| n. (plural)|彗发; 昏迷|The coma of a comet is the nebulous envelope around the nucleus of a comet. 彗星的彗发是彗核周围的星云状包层。|The patient slipped into a coma after the accident. 事故发生后，病人陷入了昏迷。|近义词: (for cometary coma) halo, atmosphere; (for medical coma) unconsciousness, stupor|反义词: (for medical coma) consciousness, awareness|Noun

comae:/ˈkəʊmiː/ (British) /ˈkoʊmiː/ (American)| n. (plural)|毛; (植物)簇毛|Certain seeds have comaes that aid in their dispersal.  某些种子有簇毛，有助于它们的传播。|The comae on the plant's leaves help to trap moisture.  植物叶子上的毛有助于吸收水分。|近义词: hair, bristles, trichomes|反义词: (Often no direct opposite)|Noun
