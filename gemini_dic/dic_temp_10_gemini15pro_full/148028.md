overromanticize: /ˌoʊvərroʊˈmæntɪsaɪz/ | v. | 过度浪漫化；过分理想化 | To interpret or view something in an excessively romantic or idealized way, often ignoring negative or realistic aspects. |  She tends to overromanticize her past relationships, remembering only the good times and forgetting the bad. 她倾向于过度美化她过去的关系，只记得美好的时光而忘记了不好的。

|例句：He overromanticizes the idea of living in the countryside, ignoring the practical difficulties. 他过分浪漫化了住在乡下的想法，忽略了实际的困难。

|例句：The movie overromanticizes war, portraying it as a glorious adventure. 这部电影过度浪漫化了战争，将其描绘成一次光荣的冒险。

|近义词：idealize, glamorize, sentimentalize |反义词:  be realistic, be practical |词性: verb
