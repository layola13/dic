nonadmirers:/ˌnɒnədˈmaɪərərz/ (US) /ˌnɒnədˈmaɪərəz/ (UK)
n.
非崇拜者，不欣赏者
People who do not admire someone or something.
The book was panned by critics and nonadmirers alike.  这本书受到了评论家和不欣赏它的人的一致批评。
non-fans, detractors, critics
admirers, fans, devotees
noun
