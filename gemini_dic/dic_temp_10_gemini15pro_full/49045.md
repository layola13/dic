dehisce:/dɪˈhɪs/| vi.|裂开，开裂|To gape or burst open, as the pods of some plants or the anthers of flowers. |The ripe seed pods dehisced, scattering seeds everywhere. 成熟的种子荚裂开了，种子散落各处。|近义词：split, burst, open, gape|反义词:close, seal|verb

dehiscence:/dɪˈhɪsəns/| n.|裂开，开裂|The process of gaping or bursting open, typically of a plant structure, such as a fruit, anther, or sporangium, to release its contents.|The dehiscence of the fruit allows the seeds to disperse. 果实的开裂使种子得以传播。|近义词：splitting, bursting, opening|反义词:closure, sealing|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin dēhiscere, from dē- (apart, away from) + hīscere (to gape, yawn).
