/wɒt/ (UK), /wʌt/ (US) |pron. det. int. adv.|什么，多么，多少|Used to ask for information about something.  It can also be used to express surprise or disbelief.|
What is your name? 你叫什么名字？
What time is it? 现在几点了？

/wɑːt/ (UK), /wɑːt/ (US) |pron. det. int. adv.|什么，多么，多少|What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！
What do you think? 你怎么想？


近义词: which, how
反义词: (What doesn't have a direct antonym as it's a question word)
词性: pronoun, determiner, interjection, adverb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you usually eat for breakfast? 你早餐通常吃什么？
What does she do for a living? 她做什么工作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? 他晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned so far? 到目前为止你学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? 她在商店买了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? 你整个星期都在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? 派会上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the wedding? 她婚礼上穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you eaten before you got sick? 你生病前吃了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting started? 会议开始前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been studying before you changed your major? 你换专业之前一直在学什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by 2030? 到2030年会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by then? 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
