overintellectualize: /ˌoʊvərˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəˌlaɪz/ | v. | 过度理性化，过度思考 | To think about or analyze something in an excessively intellectual way, often to the point of making it more complicated than it needs to be. | He tends to overintellectualize everything, which makes simple decisions take forever. 他倾向于过度思考一切，这使得简单的决定也要花很长时间。| She overintellectualized the movie and missed the simple enjoyment of the story. 她对这部电影过度分析，错过了故事本身的简单乐趣。 |  近义词: overanalyze, overthink | 反义词: simplify, intuit | 词性: 动词

Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "intellectualize" (from "intellectual" + "-ize," a suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or render"). The word "intellectual" ultimately derives from Latin "intellectus," meaning "understanding" or "perception."
