legitimator:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmeɪtər/
n.|合法化者，赋予合法性的人（或事物）|Someone or something that makes something legitimate. |The United Nations acted as a legitimator for the new government.|近义词:validator, ratifier, authorizer|反义词:invalidator, denier|词性:名词

legitimate:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmət/
adj.|合法的；正当的；合理的|conforming to the law or to rules. |His claim to the throne was legitimate. |近义词:lawful, legal, valid|反义词:illegal, illegitimate, invalid|词性:形容词

legitimate:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmeɪt/
v.|使合法；证明…为合法|to make something legal or acceptable. |The government has legitimized the use of force.|近义词:validate, legalize, justify|反义词:invalidate, criminalize, condemn|词性:动词
