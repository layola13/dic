deconsecrations: /ˌdiːˌkɒnsɪˈkreɪʃənz/
noun
撤销祝圣，解除神圣性
The act of depriving something of its sacred character; the act of removing a religious blessing from a place or object.
The deconsecration of the church saddened the community.  教堂的撤销祝圣令社区感到悲伤。
The museum acquired artifacts from the deconsecration of several temples.  博物馆收藏了来自几座寺庙撤销祝圣的文物。
secularization, desacralization
consecration, sanctification
