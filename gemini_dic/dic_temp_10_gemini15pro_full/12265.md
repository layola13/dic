ascertainable:/ˌæsərˈteɪnəbl/
adjective
可查明的，可确定的
able to be ascertained or discovered.
The cause of the fire was ascertainable. 火灾原因是可以查明的。
近义词:discoverable, verifiable, determinable
反义词:uncertain, indeterminable, unknowable
词性:adjective

词源: From ascertain + -able.  Ascertain comes from Old French acertener, from a- (from Latin ad 'to') + certain 'certain'.
