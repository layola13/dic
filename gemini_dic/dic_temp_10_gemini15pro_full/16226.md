banqueteers:/ˌbæŋkwɪˈtɪərz/ | n. | 宴会宾客；参加宴会的人 |  People attending a banquet; those who feast at a banquet. | The banqueteers were enjoying the lavish spread of food and wine. 宴会宾客们正在享用丰盛的食物和美酒。| The banqueteers were dressed in their finest attire. 宴会宾客们穿着他们最华丽的服装。 |  guests, diners, attendees, revelers | hosts | noun

Etymology:
From banquet + -eer (a suffix forming nouns denoting persons engaged in or connected with something).  Banquet itself comes from Old French "banquet" (a small bench, a feast), from Old Italian "banco" (bench).
