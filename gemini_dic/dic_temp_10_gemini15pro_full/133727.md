nonfugitive:/nɒnˈfjuːdʒɪtɪv/
adjective
非逃亡的，非挥发的
Not fleeing from justice; not volatile or evaporative.
The suspect is considered nonfugitive and is expected to appear in court. 这名嫌疑人被认为是非逃亡的，预计将出庭。
The chemical is nonfugitive and will remain on the surface for an extended period.  这种化学物质是非挥发的，会在表面停留较长时间。
near synonyms: permanent, stable, fixed, nonvolatile
antonyms: fugitive, volatile, evaporative
词性: 形容词
