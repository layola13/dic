friendship:/ˈfrɛndʃɪp/ | n. |友谊，友情 |The state of being friends; a relationship between friends. |  She values her friendships above all else. 她珍视友谊胜过一切。

|synonyms: companionship, camaraderie, amity, fellowship, closeness, intimacy, rapport, accord, harmony, understanding, affection, love | antonyms: enmity, hostility, animosity, antagonism, antipathy, hatred | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *frēondscipe, from frēond (“friend”) +‎ -scipe (“-ship”). Cognate with Old Frisian frīendscip (“friendship”), Old Saxon friundscapi, Old High German frīuntschaft (“friendship”), Old Norse vinátta (Danish, Norwegian, Swedish venskap).
