brindled:/ˈbrɪnd(ə)ld/| adj.|有斑纹的，斑驳的|Having dark streaks or flecks on a lighter background.|The farmer owned a brindled cow.|近义词：streaked, mottled, spotted, tabby|反义词:solid, plain|词性:形容词
Etymology: From brindled, past participle of brindle, from brinded, from obsolete brander "to be brown," from Old Norse brandr "a piece of burning wood."
