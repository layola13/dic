infomercial: /ˌɪnfəˈmɜːrʃl/ (US), /ˌɪnfəˈmɜːʃəl/ (UK)| n. | 商业信息片，专题广告片 | A television program that promotes a product in an informative and entertaining way, often resembling a documentary or talk show. |  Example: I saw an infomercial for a new type of blender last night.  我昨晚看到一个新型搅拌机的专题广告片。

Example:  That infomercial for the miracle mop was surprisingly persuasive.  那个神奇拖把的专题广告片出奇地有说服力。
