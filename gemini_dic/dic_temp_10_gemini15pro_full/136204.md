nonreliance:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈlaɪ.əns/ | n. |不依赖，自立，自恃|The country's policy of nonreliance on foreign aid has fostered domestic innovation. 这个国家不依赖外国援助的政策促进了国内创新。|例句：Their nonreliance on traditional marketing methods led to a unique campaign. 他们不依赖传统的营销方式，从而发起了一场独特的营销活动。

例句：The community's nonreliance on government funding is a testament to their resourcefulness.  这个社区不依赖政府资金，证明了他们的足智多谋。|近义词：independence, self-sufficiency, autonomy|反义词:dependence, reliance|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + reliance.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "reliance" comes from the Old French "relier" meaning "to rally, assemble," later evolving to mean "to depend on."
