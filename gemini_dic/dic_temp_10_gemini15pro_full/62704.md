engineeringly:/ˌɛndʒɪˈnɪərɪŋli/
adverb|工程上地；工程学地；像工程师一样地|In a manner characteristic of engineering or engineers.  It implies a practical, methodical, and systematic approach, often focused on efficiency and problem-solving.|He approached the problem engineeringly, breaking it down into smaller, manageable parts. 他像工程师一样处理这个问题，把它分解成更小、更易于管理的部分。|近义词: methodically, systematically, practically|反义词: haphazardly, randomly|词性:副词

词源:
From engineering + -ly.  "Engineering" ultimately derives from the Latin word "ingenium," meaning "inborn quality, talent, intelligence, clever invention."
