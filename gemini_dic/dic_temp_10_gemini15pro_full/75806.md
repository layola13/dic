fumer:/fyme/ (French)| v. |抽烟，冒烟|To inhale and exhale the smoke of tobacco or other substances.| Il fume une cigarette.  He smokes a cigarette.| 近义词：smoke, puff |反义词: abstain |词性: verb

fumer (as a noun - rare): /fyme/ (French) | n. |吸烟者| A person who smokes (archaic and rarely used).|  C'est un grand fumer. He is a heavy smoker (archaic). | 近义词：smoker |反义词: non-smoker |词性: noun (rare)

词源: From Old French *fumer, from Vulgar Latin *fūmāre, from Latin fūmāre (“to smoke, steam, fume”), frequentative of fūmus (“smoke”).

注意事项:  The verb "fumer" is primarily used in French. The English equivalent is "to smoke".  While "fumer" can sometimes be found in English texts, it is generally in a French context or as a borrowed term.
