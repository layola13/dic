cubisms:/ˈkjuːbɪzəmz/
noun
立体主义
Cubism is an early-20th-century avant-garde art movement that revolutionized European painting and sculpture, and inspired related movements in music, literature and architecture.
Picasso's painting "Les Demoiselles d'Avignon" is considered a proto-Cubist work. 毕加索的画作《亚威农少女》被认为是原始立体主义作品。
Cubism fragmented objects and reassembled them in abstract forms. 立体主义将物体碎片化，然后以抽象的形式重新组合。
modernism, avant-garde
realism, classicism
