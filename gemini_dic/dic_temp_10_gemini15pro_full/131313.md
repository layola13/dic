nonchalant:/ˌnɑːnʃəˈlɑːnt/ | adj. | 冷淡的，漠不关心的，若无其事的 |  (of a person or manner) feeling or appearing casually calm and relaxed; not displaying anxiety, interest, or enthusiasm. | He gave a nonchalant shrug. 他漫不经心地耸了耸肩。 |  例句：She was surprisingly nonchalant about winning the lottery. 她对赢得彩票表现得异常冷淡。 | 近义词：calm, cool, collected, composed, unconcerned, indifferent, apathetic | 反义词：anxious, eager, enthusiastic, concerned | 词性: 形容词
