abuse: /əˈbjuːs/ (US) /əˈbjuːz/ (UK)| n. vt.|滥用，虐待，辱骂|The improper or excessive use of something; cruel and violent treatment of a person or animal.|She suffered years of physical and emotional abuse. 她遭受了多年的身心虐待。

He abused his position as manager by giving jobs to his friends. 他滥用经理的职权，把工作给了他的朋友们。|近义词：misuse, mistreat, exploit, insult|反义词：cherish, protect, respect|词性：名词，动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *abuser*, from Latin *abusus*, past participle of *abuti* "to misuse, abuse," from *ab* "away, from" + *uti* "to use."


USAGE->
名词用法(Noun Usage):
The child suffered terrible abuse. = 这个孩子遭受了可怕的虐待。
Drug and alcohol abuse is a serious problem. = 毒品和酒精滥用是一个严重的问题。

动词用法(Verb Usage):
She abused her power as a teacher. = 她滥用了她作为一名教师的权力。
He verbally abused the referee. = 他辱骂了裁判。

一般现在时:
He abuses his privileges. = 他滥用他的特权。
She often abuses alcohol. = 她经常酗酒。


一般过去时:
He abused his position for personal gain. = 他为了个人利益滥用了他的职位。
They abused the animals on the farm. = 他们在农场虐待动物。


现在完成时:
He has abused his wife for years. = 他虐待他的妻子多年了。
She has abused her authority several times. = 她已经滥用职权好几次了。

过去完成时:
He had abused his position before he was caught. = 他在被抓之前就已经滥用职权了。
She had abused drugs for many years before getting help. = 她在获得帮助之前已经吸毒多年了。

将来时:
He will abuse his power if he is elected. = 如果他当选，他将会滥用权力。
They will abuse the system if they are allowed to. = 如果允许的话，他们会滥用这个系统。


被动语态:
The system was abused by corrupt officials. = 该系统被腐败官员滥用。
Children are abused every day. = 每天都有儿童受到虐待。


PHRASE->
substance abuse = 物质滥用
alcohol abuse = 酗酒
drug abuse = 滥用药物
child abuse = 虐待儿童
emotional abuse = 情感虐待
