decolorate:/diːˈkʌl.ə.reɪt/
verb
使脱色；漂白
To remove the color from something.
The harsh chemicals decolorated the fabric.  这些强效的化学物质使织物褪色。
The sun decolorated the curtains over time.  随着时间的推移，阳光使窗帘褪色。
bleach, whiten
color, dye
v.
