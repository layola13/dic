deflective:/dɪˈflɛktɪv/ | adj. | 偏斜的，偏转的，推脱的 |Causing something to change direction; diverting attention or blame away from oneself. | The car's deflective shield protected it from the impact. 汽车的偏斜护盾保护它免受撞击。 | The politician's deflective answer avoided the real issue.  这位政客的推脱性回答避开了真正的问题。 | synonyms: diverting, deviating, averting, evasive | antonyms: attractive, direct, straightforward | adjective
