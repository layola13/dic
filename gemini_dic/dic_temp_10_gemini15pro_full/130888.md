nonarrival:/ˌnɒnəˈraɪvəl/
n.
未到达，未抵达
The nonarrival of the shipment caused a delay in production.  这批货物的未抵达导致了生产的延误。
The nonarrival of the guests was a disappointment. 客人们没有到达令人失望。
Synonyms: absence, failure to arrive, no-show
Antonyms: arrival, presence
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + arrival.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not". "Arrival" comes from the Old French "arriver", meaning "to arrive at a shore".
