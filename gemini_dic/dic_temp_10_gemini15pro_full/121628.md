misparaphrases:/ˌmɪspærəˈfreɪzɪz/ | n. | 误述; 释义错误 | An act or instance of paraphrasing something incorrectly.  The student's essay was full of misparaphrases, showing a lack of understanding of the original text. | 例句：His misparaphrases of the poem completely changed its meaning. 他对这首诗的错误释义完全改变了它的意思。

例句：The witness's misparaphrases of the conversation led to confusion in the courtroom.  证人对对话的错误转述导致了法庭上的混乱。 | 近义词：misinterpretations, misrepresentations, distortions | 反义词：accurate paraphrases, correct interpretations | 词性: 名词


词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrong" or "bad") + "paraphrase" (from Greek "para-" meaning "beside" + "phrazein" meaning "to tell").
