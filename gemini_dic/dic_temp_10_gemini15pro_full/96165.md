independentism:/ˌɪndɪˈpɛndənˌtɪzəm/
n.
独立主义；分离主义
The advocacy of or support for the political independence of a nation or territory.
Catalonia's independentism movement has gained momentum in recent years.  加泰罗尼亚的独立主义运动近年来势头强劲。
The government cracked down on Basque independentism. 政府镇压了巴斯克的独立主义运动。

近义词:separatism, autonomy, self-determination
反义词:unionism, integration, dependence
词性:noun
