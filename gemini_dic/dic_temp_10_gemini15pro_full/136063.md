nonreceptive:/ˌnɒnrɪˈseptɪv/ /ˌnɑːnrɪˈseptɪv/| adj.|不接受的，不感受的，无反应的|Not receptive; unresponsive or unwilling to accept or consider something.|The audience was nonreceptive to his new ideas.  观众对他的新想法不接受。|The patient was nonreceptive to the medication. 病人对药物没有反应。|Synonyms: unreceptive, unresponsive, resistant, impervious, closed-minded|Antonyms: receptive, responsive, open-minded, accepting|词性:形容词
