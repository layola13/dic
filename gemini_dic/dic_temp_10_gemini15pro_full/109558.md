let's:/lets/ | contraction | 让我们 | Contraction of "let us," used to make suggestions or proposals. | Let's go to the movies tonight. 今晚我们去看电影吧。 | Let's grab some dinner. 我们去吃点晚餐吧。 | suggest, propose | forbid, refuse | contraction

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Let's eat dinner now.  现在我们吃晚饭吧。
Let's walk to the park. 我们走去公园吧。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Let's not be wasting time. 我们别浪费时间了。 (Technically, this uses "let's" with the continuous form of "be" and a participle.)
This doesn't naturally fit with the continuous tense.  "Let's" inherently implies a future action.

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
This doesn't naturally fit with the perfect tenses.  "Let's" initiates a suggestion for a future action, not a completed one.

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
This doesn't naturally fit with the perfect continuous tenses. "Let's" initiates a suggestion for a future action.

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
This tense doesn't fit. You wouldn't say "Let's went..."  The suggestion/proposal is made in the present.

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
This tense doesn't fit.  The suggestion/proposal is made in the present.

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
This tense doesn't fit. The suggestion/proposal is made in the present.

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
This tense doesn't fit. The suggestion/proposal is made in the present.

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Technically, "let's" implies the near future. The simple future can be used for emphasis:  Let's go to the beach tomorrow. 明天我们去海滩吧。
Let's meet next week.  下周我们见面吧。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
This doesn't naturally fit with the future continuous tense.

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
This doesn't naturally fit with the future perfect tense.

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
This doesn't naturally fit with the future perfect continuous tense.


虚拟语气:
Let's pretend we're pirates!  让我们假装我们是海盗！
Let's assume he's telling the truth. 让我们假设他说的是实话。

被动语态:
"Let's" is inherently active and cannot be used in the passive voice.


疑问句:
Let's go, shall we? 我们走吧，好吗？ (Technically, this is a tag question added to "let's")
"Let's" itself cannot form a question.


否定句:
Let's not argue. 我们别争论了。
Let's not go there. 我们别去那里。


PHRASE->
Let's get this straight.  我们把话说清楚。
Let's face it.  面对现实吧。
Let's get started.  我们开始吧。
Let's see.  让我想想 / 看看。
Let's just say...  这么说吧...

词源:  "Let's" is a contraction of "let us," with "let" coming from Old English *lætan "to allow" and "us" from Old English ūs, the objective case of we.
