imperiling:/ɪmˈpɛrəlɪŋ/| v.|危及，使陷于危险|Putting oneself in harm's way, endangering.  The rising floodwaters were imperiling the entire town. 上涨的洪水正危及整个城镇。|Synonyms: endangering, jeopardizing, compromising, threatening|Antonyms: protecting, safeguarding, securing|Verb

Etymology->
From Middle English *imperilen, from Anglo-Norman emperiler, emperer, from Late Latin impericulāre (“to put in peril”), from Latin periculum (“danger”).
