conceited:/kənˈsiːtɪd/| adj.|自负的，骄傲自满的|Having an excessively high opinion of oneself; vain.|He was so conceited that he thought he was the best player on the team. 他太自负了，认为自己是队里最好的球员。|近义词：arrogant, narcissistic, vain, egotistical, self-important|反义词：humble, modest, unassuming|词性:形容词
词源:From Late Latin *concipitus, past participle of concipere (“to conceive”).  Originally meaning "clever, intelligent", it later developed negative connotations of being overly proud of one's abilities.
