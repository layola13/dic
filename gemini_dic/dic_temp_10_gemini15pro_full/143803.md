outorganizing: /ˌaʊtˈɔːrɡəˌnaɪzɪŋ/
verb
胜过组织; 组织得比...更好
surpassing in organizing; organizing better than.
Example:  They outorganized their competitors and gained a larger market share. 他们比竞争对手组织得更好，获得了更大的市场份额。

Example:  She outorganized the entire department, streamlining workflows and improving efficiency. 她把整个部门组织得井井有条，简化了工作流程，提高了效率。

Near Synonyms: outmaneuver, outperform, outsmart
Antonyms: disorganize, mismanage
