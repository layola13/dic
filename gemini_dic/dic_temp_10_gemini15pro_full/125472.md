multiplitarian:/ˌmʌltɪplɪˈtɛəriən/

A person who believes in or advocates for multiplicity, especially in cultural or religious contexts.|例句：The multiplitarian nature of modern society is undeniable. 现代社会的多元性是不可否认的。
