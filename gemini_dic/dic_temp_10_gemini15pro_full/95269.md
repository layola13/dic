incapables:/ɪnˈkeɪpəbəlz/
n.
无能者；不能胜任者
Those deemed "incapables" were often excluded from positions of responsibility.  那些被认为“无能”的人经常被排除在负责的职位之外。
People who lack the necessary skills or abilities for a particular task or role are sometimes referred to as incapables.  缺乏完成特定任务或角色所需技能或能力的人有时被称为无能者。
Synonym: incompetents, inefficients
Antonym:  experts, professionals
词性: 名词
