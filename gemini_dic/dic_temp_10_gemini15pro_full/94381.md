impleach:/ɪmˈpiːtʃ/
|vt.|弹劾；控告；责难|To charge (a public official) with misconduct in office before a proper tribunal.|The governor was impeached for corruption. 州长因腐败被弹劾。|Synonyms: accuse, indict, charge|Antonyms: exonerate, acquit|Verb

impleach:/ɪmˈpiːtʃ/
|n.|弹劾；控告|The act of impeaching someone.|The impeachment of the president led to a lengthy trial. 总统的弹劾导致了漫长的审判。|Synonyms: accusation, indictment, charge|Antonyms: exoneration, acquittal|Noun
