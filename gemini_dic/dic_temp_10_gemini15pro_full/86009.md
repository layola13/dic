headwind:/ˈhedwɪnd/| n.|逆风，顶头风|A wind blowing from directly in front, opposing forward motion.|The cyclists faced a strong headwind.  自行车手们面临着强劲的逆风。|Synonyms: adverse wind, contrary wind|Antonyms: tailwind, following wind|noun
