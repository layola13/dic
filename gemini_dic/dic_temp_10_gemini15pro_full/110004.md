librar:/ˈlaɪbrər/ (US), /ˈlaɪbrəri/ (UK)| n.|图书馆；藏书；书房|A building or room containing collections of books, periodicals, and sometimes films and recorded music for people to read, borrow, or refer to.|例句：I often go to the library to study. 我经常去图书馆学习。

例句：He has a large library of books at home. 他家里有很多藏书。|近义词：book collection, archive, atheneum|反义词：N/A|词性：名词
