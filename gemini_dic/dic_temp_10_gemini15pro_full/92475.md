hypotarsus:/ˌhaɪpəˈtɑːrsəs/
n.
跗蹠骨; 下跗骨
A plate of cartilage or bone that supports the back of the tarsus in birds.  It serves as an attachment point for tendons that control the toes.
The hypotarsus of the eagle is well-developed, aiding in its powerful grip.  鹰的跗蹠骨非常发达，有助于其强有力的抓握。
metatarsus, tarsus
