noxious:/ˈnɑːkʃəs/ | adj. | 有害的；有毒的；讨厌的 | Harmful, poisonous, or very unpleasant. |  The factory emitted noxious fumes. 工厂排放出有毒的烟雾。 | synonyms: harmful, poisonous, toxic, baneful, pernicious | antonyms: harmless, safe, beneficial, healthy | adjective
