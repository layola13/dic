nonprejudicial:/ˌnɑːnˌpredʒʊˈdɪʃəl/
adjective
无损害的；不损害…的
Not harmful or detrimental to someone or something.  Not creating bias or prejudice against someone or something.
The judge ruled that the pre-trial publicity was nonprejudicial to the defendant's case. 法官裁定，审判前的宣传对被告的案件没有损害。
The agreement was structured to be nonprejudicial to the rights of either party.  该协议的结构不会损害任何一方的权利。
Synonyms: harmless, innocuous, inoffensive, impartial, unbiased, neutral
Antonyms: prejudicial, harmful, detrimental, biased, partisan
词性: 形容词
