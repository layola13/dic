emollience:/ɪˈmɒliəns/ | n. |柔软；润肤剂；缓和 |The quality of being soft and smooth, especially that of skin; a substance that softens or soothes the skin. | This cream has excellent emollience and keeps my skin hydrated all day.  这款面霜柔润效果极佳，让我的皮肤一整天都保持水润。| 近义词：softness, smoothness, suppleness, lotion, ointment | 反义词：roughness, hardness, dryness | 词性: noun

emollient:/ɪˈmɒliənt/ | adj. | 柔软的；润肤的；缓和的 |Having the quality of softening or soothing the skin. | The emollient cream soothed her chapped hands. 这款润肤霜舒缓了她皲裂的双手。| 近义词：soothing, softening, moisturizing | 反义词：irritating, drying | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *emollient- (stem of emollientem), present participle of emollire "to soften," from e- "out" + mollire "to soften," from mollis "soft."
