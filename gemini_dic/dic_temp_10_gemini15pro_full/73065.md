footplate:/ˈfʊtplɪt/| n.|脚板，踏板，机车司机室|A flat plate or platform for the foot to rest on, especially the platform on which the driver and fireman stand in a steam locomotive.|The driver stood on the footplate, watching the track ahead.  司机站在机车司机室里，注视着前方的轨道。|Synonyms: platform, tread, pedal, floorboard|Antonyms: N/A|noun

footplate:/ˈfʊtplɪt/| n.|脚踏板|A pedal or similar device operated by the foot.|He pressed down on the footplate to activate the machine. 他踩下脚踏板启动机器。|Synonyms: pedal, treadle|Antonyms: N/A|noun
