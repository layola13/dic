conducted:/kənˈdʌktɪd/ |v. |进行；指挥；引导；传导|Past participle of conduct|The orchestra was conducted by a renowned maestro. 这支管弦乐队由一位著名的指挥家指挥。|Synonyms: performed, directed, managed, led, transmitted |Antonyms: neglected, abandoned|verb

Etymology:
From Latin conductus, past participle of conducere "to lead or bring together," from com- "together" + ducere "to lead."
