deedful:/ˈdiːdfʊl/
adjective
充满行动的；有效率的；活跃的
Active and effective; characterized by deeds.
He led a deedful life, always helping others and contributing to his community. 他过着充满行动的生活，总是帮助别人，为他的社区做出贡献。
She was a deedful woman, never idle and always accomplishing something. 她是一位充满行动力的女性，从不闲着，总是在完成一些事情。
近义词:active, effective, energetic, industrious, productive
反义词:inactive, ineffective, lazy, idle, unproductive
词性:adjective

词源:
From Middle English *dedful, equivalent to deed + -ful.
