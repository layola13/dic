blunderbuss:/ˈblʌndərˌbʌs/| n.| blunderbuss; old type of gun, archaic; clumsy person|A short-barreled firearm with a flared muzzle, used for scattering shot at close range.  Figuratively, a clumsy or awkward person.|The pirate leveled his blunderbuss at the captain.  海盗把他的短枪对准了船长。|He's such a blunderbuss; he always knocks things over. 他真是个笨蛋，总是把东西弄倒。|Synonyms: musket, shotgun (for the weapon); oaf, klutz (for the person)|Antonyms: rifle, sniper rifle (for the weapon); graceful, adept (for the person)|noun
