beset:/bɪˈsɛt/| v. |困扰，围攻|To trouble persistently; harass. To surround; hem in.|The expedition was beset by bad weather and dwindling supplies. 探险队受到恶劣天气和日益减少的补给的困扰。|近义词：trouble, harass, plague, beleaguer, besiege|反义词:aid, assist, help|动词

beset:/bɪˈsɛt/| adj. |充满...的|Studded or covered with.|A path beset with difficulties. 一条充满困难的道路。|近义词：covered, studded, filled|反义词:empty, clear|形容词
