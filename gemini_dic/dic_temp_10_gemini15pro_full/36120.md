coerciveness:/kəʊˈɜːsɪvnəs/
n.
强制性，胁迫性
The quality of being coercive; the use of force or threats to persuade someone to do something.
The coerciveness of the government's policy sparked widespread protests. 政府政策的强制性引发了广泛的抗议。
coercion, force, compulsion, oppression, pressure
persuasion, freedom, liberty, voluntariness
noun
