ictic:/ˈɪktɪk/| adj.|抽搐的，痉挛的|Relating to or characterized by a tic; a sudden, involuntary, repetitive muscle movement or vocalization.|He exhibited ictic symptoms, including eye blinking and throat clearing. 他表现出抽搐的症状，包括眨眼和清嗓子。|近义词：spasmodic, convulsive|反义词: relaxed, controlled|词性:形容词
Etymology: From French *ictique*, from Greek *iktikos* "fit for striking, of a blow," from *iktos* "stroke, blow."
