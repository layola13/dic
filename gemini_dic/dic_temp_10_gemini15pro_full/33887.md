circumstantial:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈstænʃəl/ | adj. |  依情况而定的；详细的；间接的；(证据)旁证的 | pointing indirectly toward someone's guilt but not conclusively proving it; containing many details | The case against her was circumstantial. 对她的指控是间接的。| synonyms: indirect, inferential, presumptive, conjectural | antonyms: direct, conclusive, positive | adjective

circumstantial:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈstænʃəl/ | adj. |  (指描述)详细的，详尽的 | including all the details |  She gave a long, circumstantial account of the incident. 她对这起事件作了冗长而详细的描述。| synonyms: detailed, comprehensive, thorough, minute | antonyms: brief, concise, succinct | adjective
