clamours:/ˈklæmərz/ (US), /ˈklæməz/ (UK)| n. |喧闹声；吵闹声；大声的要求|A loud and persistent outcry from many people.|The newspapers were full of clamours for his resignation.  报纸上到处都是要求他辞职的呼声。|Synonyms: uproar, outcry, commotion, hubbub|Antonyms: silence, quiet, hush|noun

clamor:/ˈklæmər/ (US), /ˈklæmə(r)/ (UK)| v. |喧闹；吵闹；大声要求|To make a loud and persistent outcry.|The crowd clamored for an encore.  人群大声要求再来一个节目。|Synonyms: demand, insist, shout, yell|Antonyms: whisper, murmur, be silent|verb
