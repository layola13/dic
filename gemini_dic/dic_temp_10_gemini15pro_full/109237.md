leisureless: /ˈliːʒələs/ | adj. | 无闲暇的，忙碌的 | Without leisure; constantly occupied. |  He lived a leisureless life, always working. 他过着没有闲暇的生活，总是工作。|  She felt trapped in a leisureless existence. 她感觉被困在没有闲暇的生活中。 | 近义词: busy, occupied, industrious, tireless | 反义词: leisurely, relaxed, idle, free | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From leisure + -less.  "Leisure" comes from Old French "leisir" meaning "permission, opportunity," ultimately from Latin "licēre" meaning "to be permitted."
