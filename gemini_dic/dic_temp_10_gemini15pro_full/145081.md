overannotated:/ˌoʊvərˈænəˌteɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过度注释的 | Provided with too many annotations; excessively explained or commented upon. | The textbook was so overannotated that it was difficult to focus on the main text. 这本教科书注释太多了，很难集中注意力看正文。 | The code was overannotated, making it harder to read than necessary.  这段代码注释过度，使其比必要的更难阅读。 | 近义词：over-documented, over-explained | 反义词：under-documented, sparsely annotated | 词性: adjective
