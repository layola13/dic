chignoned:/ʃiːnɒnd/ | adj. | 盘着发髻的，梳着高髻的 | Having hair arranged in a chignon (a knot or coil of hair worn at the back of the head). |  She looked elegant with her hair chignoned for the gala. 她盘着发髻，为晚会盛装打扮，看起来很优雅。 | Her chignoned hair made her look taller. 她盘起的头发使她看起来更高了。 | 近义词：coiffed, styled | 反义词：unstyled, loose | 词性: adjective
词源: From French *chignonné*, past participle of *chignonner* "to tie up the hair in a chignon," from *chignon* "nape of the neck, chignon."
