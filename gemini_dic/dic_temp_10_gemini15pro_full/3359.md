affliction:/əˈflɪkʃən/| n. |痛苦，折磨，苦难|Something that causes pain or suffering, either physical or mental.|例句：She bore her affliction with courage. 她勇敢地承受着自己的苦难。

例句：The affliction of poverty can be devastating. 贫困的折磨可能是毁灭性的。

近义词：suffering, distress, hardship, tribulation, ordeal, torment|反义词:comfort, solace, relief, ease|词性:名词
