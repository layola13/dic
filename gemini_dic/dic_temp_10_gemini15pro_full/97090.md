infertility's:/ˌɪnfərˈtɪlətiz/ | n. |不孕症，不育症|The possessive form of "infertility," referring to the state or condition of being unable to conceive children. It signifies that something belongs to or is associated with infertility. |例句：Infertility's impact on a couple can be devastating. 不孕症对夫妻的影响可能是毁灭性的。

例句：The study focused on infertility's emotional toll. 这项研究的重点是不孕症造成的情感伤害。|近义词：barrenness, sterility|反义词：fertility, fecundity|词性: 名词


词源:

Late Middle English: from Old French infertilité, from late Latin infertilitas, from infertilis ‘infertile’.
