forlorn:/fərˈlɔːrn/ | adj. | 孤獨的，凄涼的，被遺棄的，绝望的 |  pitifully sad and abandoned or lonely; (of an aim or endeavor) unlikely to succeed or be fulfilled; hopeless. |  A forlorn figure stood on the empty platform. 一个孤獨的身影站在空旷的站台上。| The team made a forlorn attempt to win the game. 该队为赢得比赛做出了绝望的尝试。| 近义词: desolate, miserable, wretched, abandoned, lonely, hopeless | 反义词: cheerful, happy, hopeful, optimistic | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From Middle English *forloren, past participle of forlesen (“to lose completely”), from Old English forlēosan (“to lose entirely, forfeit, destroy”), from Proto-Germanic *fralausianą (“to lose”), equivalent to for- + lose. Cognate with Dutch verliezen (“to lose”), German verlieren (“to lose”).
