cadetships:/kəˈdɛtʃɪps/
n.
学员身份；见习军官职位；见习官员职位
A cadetship is a period of training for someone who wants to become an officer in the armed forces or the merchant navy, or a trainee position in a profession like engineering or journalism.
He was awarded a cadetship in the Royal Navy. 他被授予了皇家海军学员的职位。
Synonym: traineeship, apprenticeship, internship
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider:  seniority, veteran status, mastery)
noun
