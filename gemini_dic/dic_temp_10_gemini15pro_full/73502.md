foreseeably:/fɔːrˈsiːəbli/
adverb
可预见地
in a way that can be predicted or expected
例句：Foreseeably, the traffic will be heavy during rush hour.  可预见的是，交通在高峰时段会很拥堵。

例句：The company is foreseeably going to expand its business in Asia.  该公司可预见地将在亚洲扩展业务。

近义词：predictably, expectedly, likely
反义词：unexpectedly, surprisingly
词性：副词

词源：From "foreseeable" + "-ly."  "Foreseeable" comes from "foresee" (to see or know beforehand), from "fore-" (before) + "see."
