cold-store:/ˈkəʊld stɔːr/ (UK) /ˈkoʊld stɔːr/ (US)| n.|冷藏库，冷库|A large refrigerated room or building for storing perishable goods.|We keep all our produce in the cold-store to maintain its freshness. 我们把所有的产品都存放在冷库里以保持新鲜。|Synonyms: cold storage, freezer, refrigerator|Antonyms: oven, furnace|noun

cold-store:/ˈkəʊld stɔːr/ (UK) /ˈkoʊld stɔːr/ (US)| v.|冷藏，冷冻|To store something in a refrigerated environment.|They cold-store the meat to prevent spoilage.  他们把肉冷藏起来以防止腐败变质。|Synonyms: refrigerate, freeze|Antonyms: heat, warm|verb
