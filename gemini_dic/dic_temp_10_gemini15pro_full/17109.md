battalia:/bəˈtæliə/ /bæˈteɪljə/| n.|军队，战斗队形，战斗，战争|A large body of troops arrayed for battle; an army; a battle array; a battle.  The word has a sense of large-scale, organized combat.|The battalia clashed on the open field.  两军在开阔地带交锋。|近义词：army, host, legion, force, battle, combat, engagement|反义词:peace, truce|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *battaglia* "battle," from Late Latin *battalia* "fighting, fencing," from *battuere* "to beat, fight."  Ultimately from Latin *batuere* "to beat, strike."
