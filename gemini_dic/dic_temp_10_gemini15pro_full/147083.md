overliberalizing: /ˌōvərˈlɪbərəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度自由化
Making something excessively liberal, often in reference to economic or political policies, by removing too many restrictions or controls.
The government was accused of overliberalizing the banking sector, leading to instability. 政府被指责过度放开银行业，导致了不稳定。
Overliberalizing trade can harm domestic industries.  过度开放贸易会损害国内产业。
Synonyms: deregulating excessively, loosening controls too much
Antonyms: tightening controls, restricting, regulating
词性: 动词
