neighbour:/ˈneɪbər/ (US) /ˈneɪbə(r)/ (UK)| n.|邻居；邻国；邻近的事物|A person who lives next to or near another.| My neighbour is a doctor. 我的邻居是一位医生.| synonyms:  next-door neighbor,  adjacent resident,  abutter | antonyms: stranger, foreigner | noun

neighbour:/ˈneɪbər/ (US) /ˈneɪbə(r)/ (UK)| v.|邻接；毗邻|To live or be situated next to or near another.| Our countries neighbour each other. 我们的国家彼此相邻。| synonyms: adjoin, border, abut | antonyms: separate, divide | verb
