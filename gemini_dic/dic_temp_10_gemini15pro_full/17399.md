beakiron: /ˈbiːkˌaɪərn/
noun
喙铁; 鸟嘴铁（一种用于修补或加固鸟喙的工具或材料）
A tool or material used to repair or strengthen a bird's beak.
The falconer used a beakiron to mend the hawk's broken beak.  猎鹰人用喙铁修补鹰隼断裂的喙。
Near synonyms: beak repair tool, beak support
Antonyms: N/A (No direct antonym)
