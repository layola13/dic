overcoach:/ˌoʊvərˈkoʊtʃ/| v. |过度指导，过分执教|To coach excessively, often to the detriment of the athlete or team.  This can involve overly rigorous training, excessive focus on details, or creating a stressful and demanding environment.|The team was overcoached and burned out by the end of the season.  这个队伍被过度训练，在赛季末已经筋疲力尽了。

He overcoached his son, pushing him too hard in little league. 他过度指导他的儿子，在少年棒球联盟中给他施加了过大的压力。|近义词：overtrain, overmanage|反义词: undercoach, neglect|词性:动词
