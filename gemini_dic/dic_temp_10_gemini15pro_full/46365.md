dada:/ˈdɑːdɑː/ /ˈdædɑː/| n. |达达主义；（婴儿语）马|A movement in art and literature based on deliberate irrationality and negation of traditional artistic values.|例句：Dadaism emerged as a reaction to the horrors of World War I. 达达主义是对第一次世界大战恐怖的一种反应。

例句：The baby pointed at the horse and said, "Dada!" 婴儿指着马说：“达达！”|近义词：nonsense, absurdity|反义词:rationality, sense|词性:noun
