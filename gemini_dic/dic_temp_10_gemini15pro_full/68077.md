faddists:/ˈfædɪsts/
n.
追随时尚的人;赶时髦的人
People who are enthusiastic about a particular thing, especially a new idea or fashion, for a short time only.
Faddists are quick to pick up the latest trends, but their interest fades just as fast.  赶时髦的人很快就会接受最新的潮流，但他们的兴趣也消失得很快。
The latest diet fad attracts countless faddists.  最新的节食时尚吸引了无数赶时髦的人。
近义词:trend-followers, fashionistas
反义词:traditionalists, conservatives
词性:noun


fad:/fæd/
n.
一时流行的狂热;时尚;风尚
An intense and widely shared enthusiasm for something, especially one that is short-lived and without basis in the object's qualities; a craze.
The latest fad amongst teenagers is a new style of dance. 青少年中最新的时尚是一种新的舞蹈风格。
There was a fad for wearing ripped jeans a few years ago.  几年前流行穿破洞牛仔裤。
近义词:trend, craze, vogue
反义词:tradition, custom
词性:noun
