intercoordinating:/ˌɪntərkoʊˈɔːrdɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
相互协调的
acting or working together in a smooth, efficient way, especially in a system of organization.
The intercoordinating departments ensured a smooth product launch.  相互协调的部门确保了产品顺利发布。
Different teams are intercoordinating to achieve a common goal. 不同的团队正在相互协调以实现共同目标。
近义词: collaborating, cooperating, integrating, meshing
反义词: conflicting, clashing
