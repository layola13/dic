fubbery:/ˈfʌbəri/
n.
胡说八道；愚蠢的行为；欺骗；哄骗
nonsense; foolish behavior; deception; trickery
例句：His explanation was complete fubbery. 他的解释完全是胡说八道。
例句：The politician's promises were just more fubbery to get votes.  这位政客的承诺只不过是为了获得选票的更多谎言。
近义词：nonsense, baloney, claptrap, hogwash
反义词：truth, honesty, fact
词性：名词

词源:  Possibly a blend of "fumble" and "blubbery," suggesting clumsiness and incoherence.  Its origin is uncertain.
