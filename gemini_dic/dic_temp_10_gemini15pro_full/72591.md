fly-blind:/flaɪ blaɪnd/| adv. | 盲目飞行，无仪表飞行；冒险行事，摸着石头过河 | To operate an aircraft without the aid of instruments or external visual references; to proceed without sufficient information or planning. |  He had to fly blind through the thick fog. 他不得不盲目地在浓雾中飞行。 |  The company decided to fly blind and launch the product without market research.  这家公司决定冒险行事，在没有市场调查的情况下推出产品。 |近义词: improvise, wing it, take a chance, gamble |反义词: plan meticulously, proceed cautiously, be well-informed |词性: 副词
