partlet:/ˈpɑːtlɪt/| n.|围裙，（母鸡或母鸟的）颈羽，围巾|A covering for the neck and upper breast, worn by women, or the feathers on the neck of a hen or other bird.|The old woman wore a colorful partlet over her dress. 老妇人穿着一件色彩鲜艳的围裙套在她的连衣裙外面。|The hen puffed out its partlet feathers. 母鸡抖起了它的颈羽。|Synonyms: neckerchief, bib, ruff|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, possibly 'bare neck/chest')|noun
