inflamingly:/ɪnˈfleɪmɪŋli/
adverb
煽动地；激怒地；使人激愤地
in a way that causes someone to become very angry or excited, especially about a political or social matter.
His inflamingly racist remarks caused outrage. 他煽动性的种族主义言论引起了众怒。
近义词:provocatively, incendiary, inflammatory
反义词:soothingly, calmingly
词性:副词
