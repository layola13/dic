lawyerly:/ˈlɔɪərli/| adj.|律师般的；精明谨慎的；像律师的|Resembling, characteristic of, or suitable to a lawyer, especially in shrewdness, carefulness, or formality.|He gave a lawyerly response, carefully avoiding any direct admission of guilt. 他给出了一个律师式的回答，小心翼翼地避免任何直接承认罪行。|近义词:legalistic, attorney-like, professional, shrewd, astute, meticulous, cautious|反义词:naive, careless, artless, guileless|词性:形容词
