mauresque:/ˌmɔːˈrɛsk/ | adj. | 摩尔式的，摩尔风格的 | Relating to the Moors, their culture, or their artistic style, especially the style prevalent in Islamic Spain between the 8th and 15th centuries.  Characterized by intricate ornamentation, arches, and domes. | The Alhambra palace in Granada is a stunning example of Mauresque architecture. 格拉纳达的阿尔罕布拉宫是摩尔式建筑的绝佳典范。|  Moorish, Islamic (in reference to art and architecture) |  Gothic, Renaissance | adjective
