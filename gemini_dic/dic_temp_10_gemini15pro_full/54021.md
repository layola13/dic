discurrent:/dɪsˈkɜːrənt/
adjective
不连续的，散乱的，岔开的
straying from the main subject; rambling; digressive.  Not following a regular or continuous course.
The speaker's discurrent remarks made it difficult to follow his main point. 演讲者散漫的言辞让人难以抓住他的主要观点。

Antonyms: coherent, focused, connected, continuous, linear
