pastiest:/ˈpeɪstiɪst/| adj.|最苍白的，面色最差的|Having the palest or most unhealthy-looking complexion. |She looked the pastiest of all the girls in the room. 她看起来是房间里所有女孩中最苍白的。|近义词：palest, wan, ashen|反义词：ruddy, healthy, glowing|词性:形容词
词源: From 'pasty' + '-est' (superlative suffix).  Ultimately from Old French 'paste' meaning 'dough, paste'.  The word 'pasty' originally referred to the pale color of dough and then came to describe a pale complexion.
