mangonist: /ˈmæŋɡonɪst/ | n. |芒果爱好者；特别喜欢吃芒果的人 | A person who loves mangoes. |  She's a real mangonist and can eat several mangoes in one sitting. 她是一个真正的芒果爱好者，可以一次吃掉几个芒果。

| Example: He brought a whole box of mangoes, a true delight for the mangonist in him. 他带了一整箱芒果，对他这个芒果爱好者来说真是太高兴了。

| Synonym: mango lover, mango enthusiast | Antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider someone who dislikes mangoes) | Noun
