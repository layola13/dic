overdecorativeness:/ˌoʊvərˌdɛkərətɪvnəs/
n.
过度装饰; 过分装饰
the state or quality of being excessively decorated.
Her overdecorativeness made the room feel cluttered. 她过度的装饰让房间感觉很杂乱。
近义词: ornateness, gaudiness, flamboyance
反义词: simplicity, minimalism, austerity
词性: noun

overdecorate:/ˌoʊvərˈdɛkəˌreɪt/
v.
过度装饰; 过分装饰
to decorate excessively.
Be careful not to overdecorate the Christmas tree. 小心不要把圣诞树装饰得太过火。
近义词: overdo, embellish excessively
反义词: understate, simplify
词性: verb

decorative:/ˈdɛkərətɪv/
adj.
装饰性的; 作装饰用的
serving or intended to be ornament.
The decorative pillows added a touch of color to the room. 装饰性的枕头为房间增添了一抹色彩。
近义词: ornamental, embellishing
反义词: functional, plain
词性: adjective
