oceans:/ˈoʊʃənz/| n.|海洋|The vast body of salt water that covers most of the Earth's surface.|We sailed across the oceans. 我们航行穿越了海洋。|近义词：seas, waters|反义词:land|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *occeans (plural), from Latin ōceanus, from Ancient Greek ὠκεανός (ōkeanós, “ocean; the great river or sea surrounding the earth”), probably from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ōḱi̯eh₂nós.
