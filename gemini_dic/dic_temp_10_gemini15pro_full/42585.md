counterpoint:/ˈkaʊntərˌpɔɪnt/ | n. |对位法；对比；对照物|A contrasting but complementary element, idea, or sound.|The spicy peppers were a perfect counterpoint to the sweet mangoes. 辣椒与甜芒果形成了完美的对比。|近义词:contrast, antithesis, complement|反义词:similarity, harmony|noun


counterpoint:/ˈkaʊntərˌpɔɪnt/ | v. |构成对照；与…形成对比|To set in contrast or opposition; to oppose with contrasting arguments.|The author counterpointed the benefits of technology with its potential dangers. 作者将科技的益处与其潜在的危险进行了对比。|近义词:contrast, juxtapose, oppose|反义词:harmonize, agree|verb
