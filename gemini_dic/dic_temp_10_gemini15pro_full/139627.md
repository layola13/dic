occipitoaxial:/ɒkˌsɪpɪtəʊˈæksiəl/
adjective

Relating to the occiput (back part of the skull) and the axis (second cervical vertebra).
The occipitoaxial joint allows for a significant range of head movement. 枕寰关节允许头部进行大范围的运动。
