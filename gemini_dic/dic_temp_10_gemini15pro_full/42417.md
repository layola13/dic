countercountermeasures:/ˌkaʊntərˈkaʊntərˌmeʒərz/| n. |反反措施，对抗反制措施| Actions taken to counteract an adversary's countermeasures.  These are measures taken in response to measures taken against initial measures. | The general developed countercountermeasures to the enemy's anticipated countermeasures to their initial attack. 将军针对敌人预期的反制措施制定了反反措施，以应对他们的初步攻击。|  The development of advanced radar systems necessitated countercountermeasures to jamming techniques. 先进雷达系统的开发需要对抗干扰技术的反反措施。 |Synonyms: counter-countermeasures, counterstrategies| Antonyms: measures, countermeasures | noun
