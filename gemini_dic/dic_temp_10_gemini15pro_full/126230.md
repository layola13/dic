Mycenaean: /ˌmaɪsəˈniːən/ | adj. n. |迈锡尼的；迈锡尼人| Relating to or characteristic of Mycenae, a major center of Greek civilization in the late Bronze Age (c. 1600-1100 BC).  Also refers to the people or culture of Mycenae.| The Mycenaean civilization preceded the classical Greek era. 迈锡尼文明先于古典希腊时代。 |The museum houses a fascinating collection of Mycenaean artifacts.  博物馆收藏了大量迷人的迈锡尼文物。|  近义词: Achaean, Argive | 反义词: (No direct antonym, could use post-Mycenaean or referring to a different ancient culture, e.g., Minoan) |词性: adjective, noun
