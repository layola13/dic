antivaccinist:/ˌæntiˈvæksɪnɪst/
n.
反疫苗者，疫苗接种反对者
A person who opposes vaccination or is against the practice of vaccinating.
The antivaccinist spread misinformation about the safety of vaccines.  反疫苗者散布关于疫苗安全性的错误信息。

The politician, known for his antivaccinist views, faced criticism from the medical community.  这位以反疫苗观点闻名的政治家面临医学界的批评。

近义词:vaccine opponent
反义词:pro-vaccinist, vaccine advocate
词性:名词
