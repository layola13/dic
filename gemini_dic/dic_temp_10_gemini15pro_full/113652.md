machined:/məˈʃiːnd/| adj.|机加工的，用机器制造的|Past participle of "machine," meaning worked or processed by a machine.  It describes something that has been made or shaped using a machine.|The part was precisely machined to fit perfectly.  这个零件经过精密加工，可以完美地装配。|Synonyms: manufactured, fabricated, engineered,  tooled |Antonyms: handmade, handcrafted, crude,  unprocessed|verb (past participle)

machine:/məˈʃiːn/| n. v.|机器，机械; 用机器加工|A device that makes work easier by changing the size or direction of a force.  As a verb, it means to make or shape something using a machine.|The sewing machine is old but still works. 这台缝纫机很旧但还能用。  They machined the metal into a complex shape.  他们用机器把金属加工成复杂的形状。|Synonyms:(n.) apparatus, device, mechanism, appliance; (v.)  mill, tool, process |Antonyms:(n.)  nothing specific; (v.) handcraft|noun, verb
