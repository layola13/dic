concordance:/kənˈkɔːrdəns/| n.|一致，和谐，索引；词汇索引|An alphabetical list of the principal words used in a book or body of work, listing every instance of each word with its immediate context.  一个按字母顺序排列的书籍或作品中主要单词的列表，列出了每个单词的每个实例及其直接上下文。|The concordance shows that the word "love" appears over 200 times in Shakespeare's sonnets.|莎士比亚十四行诗的索引显示，“爱”这个词出现了200多次。|近义词：agreement, harmony, index|反义词：discord, disagreement|词性:noun

concordances:/kənˈkɔːrdənsɪz/| n.|一致，和谐，索引；词汇索引（复数）|Plural form of concordance.  词汇索引的复数形式。|The concordances to the Bible are invaluable tools for theological research.|圣经索引是神学研究的宝贵工具。|近义词：agreements, harmonies, indexes|反义词：discords, disagreements|词性:noun
