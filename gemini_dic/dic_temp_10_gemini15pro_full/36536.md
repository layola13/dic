collaborative: /kəˈlæbərətɪv/
collaborative: adj.|合作的，协作的|Produced or conducted by two or more parties working together. |例句：The project was a collaborative effort between several departments.  这个项目是几个部门共同努力的结果。|例句：We adopted a collaborative approach to problem-solving. 我们采取了一种合作的方式来解决问题。|近义词：cooperative, joint, combined, shared|反义词：independent, solo, individual|词性:形容词

collaborative: /kəˈlæbərətɪv/
collaborative: n.|合作，协作|The action of working with someone to produce or create something. |例句：This collaborative brought together artists and scientists. 这次合作汇集了艺术家和科学家。|例句：The collaborative between the two companies was very successful. 两家公司的合作非常成功。|近义词：cooperation, partnership, joint venture|反义词：competition, rivalry|词性:名词
