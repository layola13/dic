artoid:/ˈɑːrtɔɪd/| adj.| 似艺术的，艺术品的仿制品 |Resembling art, but lacking originality or aesthetic value; imitative of genuine art.  Something that appears to be art but isn't considered true art.|The museum shop was full of cheap, artoid souvenirs.  博物馆的商店里到处都是廉价的、仿造艺术品的纪念品。|Near synonyms: pseudo-art, imitation art, faux art|Antonym: genuine art|Adjective
