hard-bitten:/ˈhɑːrdˌbɪtn/ | adj. |顽强的；坚韧不拔的；饱经风霜的|Toughened by experience; cynical.|The hard-bitten detective had seen it all before.  饱经风霜的侦探以前见过这一切。|近义词：tough, resilient, cynical, jaded, world-weary|反义词：naive, innocent, idealistic, tenderhearted|词性:形容词
词源:  Compound of hard and bitten.  "Bitten" here likely refers to the metaphorical sense of being "bitten" by experience, hardship, or disappointment, leaving one tough and cynical.
