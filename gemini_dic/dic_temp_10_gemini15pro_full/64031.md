ETYMOLOGY->
From French épithélial, from Modern Latin epithelium, from Ancient Greek ἐπί (epí, “upon, on”) + θηλή (thēlḗ, “nipple”). The term was originally used to describe the tissue covering the nipple, later generalized to refer to any lining or covering tissue.
