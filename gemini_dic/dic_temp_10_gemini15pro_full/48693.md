defiant:/dɪˈfaɪənt/| adj.|挑衅的，反抗的，不服从的|Showing resistance or disobedience; willing to openly resist authority.|The defiant child refused to go to bed. 那个不服从的孩子拒绝上床睡觉。|近义词：rebellious, resistant, disobedient, insubordinate, unyielding|反义词：obedient, submissive, compliant, yielding|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *defiant*, present participle of *defier* "to challenge, defy," from Latin *dēfidāre* "to renounce one's faith, distrust, defy," from *dē-* "away" + *fidāre* "to trust," from *fidēs* "faith, trust."
