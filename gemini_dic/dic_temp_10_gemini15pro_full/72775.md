fohist:/ˈfoʊhɪst/| n.|福希斯特（人名）;  精通中国古代哲学的人 (尤指道家)|A person versed in ancient Chinese philosophy, especially Taoism.  Sometimes used as a surname.|例句：He is a renowned fohist, known for his deep understanding of Tao Te Ching. 他是一位著名的道家学者，以其对《道德经》的深刻理解而闻名。

例句：Dr. Fohist published a new translation of the I Ching. 福希斯特博士出版了一本新的《易经》译本。

近义词：Taoist scholar, Sinologist|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider someone ignorant of or opposed to Taoism)|词性:noun
