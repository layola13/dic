misorganizing:/ˌmɪsˈɔːrɡənaɪzɪŋ/
verb
组织不善，整理混乱
The act of organizing something badly or incorrectly.
He was reprimanded for misorganizing the company's files. 他因公司文件整理混乱而受到斥责。
mismanaging, messing up
organizing, arranging
