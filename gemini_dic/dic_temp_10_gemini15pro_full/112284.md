lopsidedly:/ˌlɒpˈsaɪdɪdli/ /ˌlɑːpˈsaɪdɪdli/| adv.| 不平衡地，倾斜地 |In a lopsided manner; unevenly.  Describes actions or states that are unbalanced or uneven.| The picture hung lopsidedly on the wall.  这幅画歪歪斜斜地挂在墙上。| unevenly, asymmetrically, crookedly | evenly, symmetrically, straight | adverb

Etymology: From lopsided + -ly. Lopsided itself comes from lop (meaning to hang down limply) + sided.
