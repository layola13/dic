headcheese:/ˈhɛdˌtʃiːz/| n. |猪头冻|A cold cut made from the flesh of a pig's head, often set in aspic. It does *not* contain cheese.|例句：My grandfather loves headcheese. 我祖父喜欢吃猪头冻。

例句：Headcheese is a traditional dish in many European countries.  猪头冻是许多欧洲国家的传统菜肴。|近义词：brawn, souse|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "hot dish" as a contrast in temperature)|词性:noun
