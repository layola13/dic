internecion: /ˌɪntərˈniːʃən/ | n. | internecine conflict; mutual destruction; massacre |  Mutual or reciprocal slaughter; carnage. A conflict within a group or organization that is destructive to all involved.  It often implies a struggle so intense that it threatens to destroy both sides. |  The internecion within the political party threatened to tear it apart.  党内的相互残杀几乎要把它撕裂。| The internecion of the two armies resulted in heavy losses on both sides.  两军之间的相互残杀导致双方损失惨重。 | synonyms:  bloodbath, carnage, slaughter, fratricide, mutual destruction | antonyms: peace, harmony, reconciliation, truce | noun
