nematodes:/ˈnɛməˌtoʊdz/ (US) /ˈnɛməˌtəʊdz/ (UK)| n. |线虫|Roundworms, a phylum of unsegmented worms with a cylindrical body, typically pointed at both ends. They are found in a wide variety of habitats, including soil, water, and as parasites of plants and animals.|Example:  Root-knot nematodes can damage crops. 根结线虫会损害农作物。

Example: Many nematodes are beneficial to the environment. 许多线虫对环境有益。|近义词：roundworms, eelworms|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


Etymology: From Modern Latin Nematoda, from Ancient Greek νῆμα (nêma, “thread”) + εἶδος (eîdos, “form, shape, appearance”).
