bewigged:/bɪˈwɪɡd/
adjective
戴假发的
Wearing a wig; having a wig on.
The bewigged judge entered the courtroom.  戴着假发的法官走进了法庭。
The actor, bewigged and costumed, looked nothing like himself.  戴着假发，穿着戏服的演员看起来一点也不像他自己。
Synonyms: wigged
Antonyms: wigless
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + wigged, from wig + -ed.  "Wig" comes from periwig, shortened in the 17th century.  Ultimately from French perruque.
