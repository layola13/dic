pacinian:/pəˈsɪniən/| adj.|帕西尼的，环层小体的|Relating to or denoting a Pacinian corpuscle, a type of nerve ending in the skin sensitive to pressure and vibration.|Pacinian corpuscles are responsible for our sense of deep touch. 环层小体负责我们的深层触觉。|近义词:None (highly specific term)|反义词:None|词性:adjective
