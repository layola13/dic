demonetized: /diːˈmʌnɪtaɪzd/
| adj. | 废除货币的，使货币失效的 |  Officially deprived of its status as money.  No longer recognized as legal tender. |  The 500 and 1000 rupee notes were demonetized in India in 2016.  2016年，印度废除了500和1000卢比的纸币。 | invalidated, discontinued, withdrawn | legitimized, authorized | adjective
