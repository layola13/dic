hungrinesses:/ˈhʌŋɡrinɪsɪz/
noun
饥饿感（复数）
The state of being very hungry; multiple instances of the feeling of hunger.  This word emphasizes the intensity or frequency of the hunger pangs.
He suffered from recurring hungrinesses throughout the day due to his medication. 由于药物作用，他整天都反复感到饥饿。
hunger, ravenousness, starvation, famishment
satiety, fullness
