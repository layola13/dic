etesian:/ɪˈtiːʒən/| adj. n.|地中海地区的夏季北风；地中海夏季的|Relating to or denoting the dry north wind that blows in the Aegean Sea during the summer.|The etesian winds provide a welcome relief from the summer heat. 地中海夏季的北风带来了令人愉快的清凉。|The etesian was a strong wind that aided sailors in ancient times. 夏季北风是一种强风，在古代帮助了水手航行。|近义词:meltemi|反义词:(no direct antonym, perhaps southerly winds)|词性:adjective, noun


词源: From Ancient Greek ἐτήσιος (etḗsios, “yearly, annual”), from ἔτος (étos, “year”).
