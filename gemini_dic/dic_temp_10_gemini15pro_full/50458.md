depredation:/ˌdɛprɪˈdeɪʃən/ | n. |掠夺；破坏；损害|The act of attacking and damaging something; the act of preying upon or plundering. |例句：The depredations of the invaders left the city in ruins. 入侵者的掠夺使这座城市沦为废墟。

例句：The farmer was concerned about the depredations of wolves on his livestock. 农民担心狼群会捕食他的牲畜。|近义词：destruction, devastation, pillage, plunder, looting |反义词：construction, preservation, protection |词性: 名词
