overlubrication: /ˌoʊvərˌluːbrɪˈkeɪʃən/
| n. |过度润滑|The act of applying too much lubricant, which can be detrimental to machinery. |Excessive overlubrication can cause seals to fail. 过度润滑会导致密封件失效。|近义词：excess lubrication|反义词: underlubrication|词性:名词

overlubricate: /ˌoʊvərˈluːbrɪkeɪt/
| vt. |过度润滑|To apply too much lubricant to something. |Be careful not to overlubricate the bearings. 注意不要给轴承过度润滑。|近义词：grease excessively|反义词: underlubricate|词性:动词
