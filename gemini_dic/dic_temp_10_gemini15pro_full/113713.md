macrobenthic:/ˌmækrəʊˈbɛnθɪk/| adj.|大型底栖生物的，大型底栖动物的|Relating to or denoting the flora and fauna inhabiting the bottom of a body of water and large enough to be visible to the naked eye.|Macrobenthic invertebrates play a crucial role in the ecosystem. 大型底栖无脊椎动物在生态系统中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：benthic|反义词:|形容词
