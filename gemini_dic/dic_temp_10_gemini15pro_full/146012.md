overdoubting: /ˌoʊvərˈdaʊtɪŋ/
verb
过度怀疑
Doubting something excessively or to an unreasonable degree.  Experiencing excessive doubt.
She was overdoubting her abilities, convinced she would fail the exam. 她过度怀疑自己的能力，确信自己考试会不及格。
He was overdoubting his decision to move, second-guessing every aspect of it. 他过度怀疑自己搬家的决定，对每一个方面都反复推敲。
近义词: overthinking, agonizing, second-guessing
反义词: trusting, confident, certain
词性: verb
