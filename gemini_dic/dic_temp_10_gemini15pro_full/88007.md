high-speed:/ˌhaɪˈspiːd/| adj.|高速的|Moving or operating at high speed.  Relating to or denoting a fast rate of transfer of electronic data.|例句：The high-speed train arrived on time. 高速列车准时到达。|例句：High-speed internet access is essential these days.  如今，高速互联网接入至关重要。|近义词：fast, rapid, quick, swift|反义词:slow, low-speed|词性:形容词
Etymology: 'high' (Old English 'hēah') + 'speed' (Old English 'spēd', meaning 'success, prosperity, wealth, speed, quickness') -  The compound 'high-speed' emerged with the advent of technologies requiring greater velocities.
