nonvertically:/ˌnɒnˈvɜːtɪkli/ | adv. | 非垂直地，不垂直地 | Not in a vertical direction or position; not upright or perpendicular. | The picture was hung nonvertically, at a slight angle. 这幅画没有垂直悬挂，而是略微倾斜。 | The leaning tower of Pisa is nonvertically positioned. 比萨斜塔并非垂直放置。| 近义词: slantingly, obliquely, diagonally | 反义词: vertically, upright, perpendicular | 词性: 副词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "vertical" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Vertical" comes from Old French "vertical," ultimately from Latin "vertex" (top, summit).
