déjà vu: /ˌdeɪʒɑː ˈvuː/ | n. | 似曾相识 |The feeling that one has already lived through the present situation.  It is a French phrase that literally translates to "already seen."| I had a strong sense of déjà vu when I walked into the room. 我走进房间时有一种强烈的似曾相识的感觉。

I experienced déjà vu when I visited that town, even though I'd never been there before.  当我访问那个小镇时，我经历了似曾相识的感觉，即使我以前从未去过那里。| near synonyms:  paramnesia,  familiarity | antonym:  N/A (The opposite of déjà vu is not a single word, but rather the absence of the feeling.) | Noun
