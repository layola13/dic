errantia:/ɛˈrænʃɪə/
noun
漫游生物；游走性动物
Wandering or mobile animals, especially those that move about actively in search of food.  Specifically refers to a class of polychaete worms (marine bristle worms) characterized by their active lifestyle.
The errantia include many common seashore worms. 漫游生物包括许多常见的海岸蠕虫。
The errantia, unlike their sedentary counterparts, actively forage for food. 与不活动的同类不同，漫游生物会主动觅食。
synonyms: mobile organisms, roving creatures, free-living animals (in the context of polychaetes)
antonyms: sedentary organisms, sessile organisms (in the context of polychaetes)
词性:n.
