ordinator:/ˈɔːrdɪneɪtər/ | n. | 坐标;纵坐标;任命者;授予圣职者 |  A value on the y-axis of a graph; a person who coordinates or organizes something, especially an event or activity; a person who ordains someone into religious service. | The ordinator of point A is 5.  A点的纵坐标是5。 | synonyms: coordinate, y-value, organizer, planner | antonyms: abscissa (x-value) | noun
