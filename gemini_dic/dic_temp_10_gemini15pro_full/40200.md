contestable:/kənˈtɛstəbl/ | adj. | 可争辩的，可质疑的，可竞争的 | Open to challenge or dispute; able to be competed for. |  The election results were contestable due to irregularities in the voting process. 由于投票过程中存在违规行为，选举结果受到质疑。| synonyms: disputable, debatable, challengeable, open to competition | antonyms: indisputable, undeniable, irrefutable, unchallengeable | adjective

contested:/kənˈtɛstɪd/ | adj. | 有争议的，竞争激烈的 | Disputed or challenged; involving competition. | The contested territory has been the source of conflict for decades. 这片有争议的领土几十年来一直是冲突的根源。| synonyms: disputed, challenged, contended, competitive | antonyms: undisputed, unchallenged, uncontended, noncompetitive | adjective
