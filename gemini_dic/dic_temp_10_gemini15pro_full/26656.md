cacophony: /kəˈkɒfəni/ (British) /kəˈkɑːfəni/ (American)| n.| 刺耳的声音，不和谐的声音| A harsh, discordant mixture of sounds.| The cacophony of the city streets was overwhelming. 城市街道上刺耳的声音震耳欲聋。| Synonyms: din, racket, noise, clamor, discord | Antonyms: harmony, melody, euphony, silence, quiet | noun

Etymology: From Greek *kakophōnia*, from *kakos* ("bad") + *phōnē* ("sound").
