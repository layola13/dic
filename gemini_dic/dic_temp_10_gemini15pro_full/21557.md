blueprinted:/ˈbluːprɪntɪd/
adjective|蓝图化的，按计划进行的|Planned or designed in detail, often with a blueprint.|The project was carefully blueprinted before construction began.  这个项目在开工前经过了精心的规划。|近义词：planned, designed, outlined, charted, mapped out|反义词：improvised, unplanned, ad-hoc|词性: adjective
Etymology:  From "blueprint" + "-ed." "Blueprint" itself comes from the process of creating copies of technical drawings using a photosensitive paper that turns blue where exposed to light, leaving white lines where the original drawing was.
