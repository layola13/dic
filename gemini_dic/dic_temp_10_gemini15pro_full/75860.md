functionalisms:/ˈfʌŋkʃənəlɪzəmz/
n.
功能主义（复数）
A theory that all aspects of a society serve a function and are necessary for the survival of that society.  A belief that the value of a thing depends on its usefulness.
Functionalisms in sociology and anthropology emphasize the interconnectedness of social institutions and their contribution to social stability.
社会学和人类学中的功能主义强调社会制度的相互联系及其对社会稳定的贡献。

functionalisms:/ˈfʌŋkʃənəlɪzəmz/
n.
实用主义（复数）
The doctrine that the design of an object should be determined solely by its function, rather than by aesthetic considerations.
The architect's functionalisms resulted in a building that was efficient but unattractive.
这位建筑师的实用主义导致了一座高效但缺乏吸引力的建筑。


近义词:

反义词:

词性:
