hyperalkalinity:/ˌhaɪpərˌælkəˈlɪnɪti/ | n. | 超碱性 | The condition of having a pH considerably above 7, typically above 10.  A high alkalinity. | The lake's hyperalkalinity supports a unique ecosystem.  这个湖的超碱性支撑着一个独特的生态系统。 | 近义词: extreme alkalinity, high alkalinity | 反义词: acidity, hyperacidity | 词性: noun
Etymology: hyper- (Greek: 'over, beyond') + alkaline + -ity (suffix forming nouns of quality or state)  来自希腊语"hyper-"（超过，超出） + alkaline（碱性的）+ "-ity"（构成性质或状态名词的后缀）
