overlap:/ˌoʊvərˈlæp/ /ˌəʊvəˈlæp/| v. n.|重叠，交叠；部分相同；重复；|To extend over and cover a part of something else; to have elements in common.|The shingles on the roof overlap each other. 屋顶上的瓦片相互重叠。|synonyms: coincide, overlay, intersect|antonyms: diverge, separate|verb

overlap:/ˈoʊvərˌlæp/ /ˈəʊvəˌlæp/| n.|重叠部分；相同之处；重复|A part or amount which overlaps another.|There is considerable overlap between the two subjects. 这两个学科之间有很多重叠之处。|synonyms: intersection, convergence, concurrence|antonyms: divergence, separation|noun
