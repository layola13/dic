fumingly:/ˈfjuːmɪŋli/| adv.|愤怒地，生气地|In a manner filled with anger and resentment, often visibly so.  Showing or expressing anger or annoyance.|He stomped out of the room fumingly after the argument.  他争吵后愤怒地跺着脚走出了房间。|近义词：angrily, furiously, wrathfully, indignantly|反义词：calmly, peacefully, placidly|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From fume + -ing + -ly.  Fume comes from Old French fumer, from Latin fumare "to smoke," figuratively "to be angry."
