liquoring:/ˈlɪkə.rɪŋ/
n.
浸渍，湿润处理
The process of applying a liquid to a material, often textile, to improve its properties.
The wool underwent a liquoring process to make it softer.  羊毛经过了浸渍处理，使其更加柔软。
saturation, wetting, steeping, soaking
drying
noun
