dci:/ˈdiː siː aɪ/ | n. | 数据中心互连，数据中心内部互连 | Data Center Interconnect or Data Center Interconnection refers to the network links between two or more data centers.  | The company invested heavily in dci to improve application performance. 这家公司在数据中心互连上投入巨资以提高应用程序性能。 | Example: We need a robust DCI solution to connect our data centers across the country. 我们需要一个强大的DCI解决方案来连接我们全国的数据中心。| 近义词: data center connectivity, inter-data center network | 反义词: (No direct antonym.  Perhaps "isolated data center" could be considered a conceptual opposite.) | 词性: noun
