crownland:/ˈkraʊnlænd/| n. |王室领地；国有土地|Land owned by the monarch or the state.|例句：The government sold some crownland to developers. 政府将一些国有土地出售给了开发商。

例句：Access to crownland for recreational purposes is usually permitted.  出于娱乐目的进入国有土地通常是被允许的。|近义词：public land, state land|反义词：private land|词性:noun

Etymology: From "crown" (referring to the monarch) + "land."  The term signifies land belonging to the reigning monarch or, in modern usage, land held by the state.
