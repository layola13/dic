nondedicated:/ˌnɒnˈdedɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
非专用的；未指定的
Not specifically allocated or assigned for a particular use or purpose.
The server is nondedicated, meaning it's shared with other users. 这台服务器是非专用的，意味着它与其他用户共享。
We use a nondedicated phone line for internet access. 我们使用非专用电话线连接互联网。
近义词:shared, general-purpose, common
反义词:dedicated, exclusive, specific
词性:adjective

non-:/nɒn/
prefix
非；不
Expressing negation or absence.
"Non" in "nondedicated" means "not."  “nondedicated”中的“non”表示“不”。
This is a non-smoking area. 这是一个禁烟区。
近义词:un-, in-, a-
反义词:pro-, co-
词性:prefix

dedicated:/ˈdedɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
专用的；献身的
Exclusively allocated or assigned for a particular use or purpose; devoted to a task or purpose.
This is a dedicated server for our website. 这是一个我们网站的专用服务器。
She's a dedicated teacher who always puts her students first. 她是一位敬业的教师，总是把学生放在第一位。
近义词:committed, devoted, exclusive
反义词:nondedicated, shared, general-purpose
词性:adjective
