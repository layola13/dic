antitotalitarian:/ˌæntiˌtoʊtælɪˈtɛəriən/ | adj. | 反极权主义的 | Opposed to totalitarianism, a political system in which the state holds total authority over all aspects of public and private life. |  The antitotalitarian movement gained momentum after the war. 战后，反极权主义运动势头强劲。 | The author's antitotalitarian stance is evident throughout the novel. 作者的反极权主义立场在整部小说中都很明显。 | 近义词: democratic, libertarian, liberal  | 反义词: totalitarian, authoritarian, fascist | 词性: adjective

antitotalitarian:/ˌæntiˌtəʊˌtælɪˈteəriən/ | n. | 反极权主义者 | A person who opposes totalitarianism. | He was a staunch antitotalitarian, fighting for freedom of speech and assembly. 他是一位坚定的反极权主义者，为言论自由和集会自由而战。 |  Many antitotalitarians were exiled during the regime. 许多反极权主义者在该政权统治期间被流放。 | 近义词: dissident, rebel, freedom fighter | 反义词: totalitarian, conformist | 词性: noun
