cocksure:/ˌkɒkˈʃʊə(r)/ | /ˌkɑːkˈʃʊr/ | adj. | 自信的；过于自信的；武断的 | Too confident, especially in a way that is annoying. |  He's a cocksure young man. 他是一个过于自信的年轻人。 |例句：Her cocksure attitude made her unpopular. 她自以为是的态度使她不受欢迎。| 近义词：confident, arrogant, presumptuous | 反义词：hesitant, timid, modest | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "cock" (as in a rooster, known for its confidence) + "sure".  The image is of a rooster's confident strut.
