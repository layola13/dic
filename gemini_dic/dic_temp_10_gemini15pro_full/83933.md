half-civilly:/ˌhɑːfˈsɪvɪlli/
|adv.|半礼貌地；勉强客气地；|Acting or speaking in a way that is only partially polite or courteous.  Showing a limited degree of civility.|He half-civilly acknowledged their presence with a curt nod. 他勉强客气地点了点头，算是承认了他们的存在。|Near synonyms:  half-heartedly, perfunctorily, grudgingly|Antonyms: politely, courteously, graciously
|adverb|
