injudiciousnesses:/ˌɪndʒʊˈdɪʃəsnəsɪz/
|n.|不明智；缺乏判断力|The state or quality of being injudicious; lack of judgment or discretion.|His injudiciousnesses led to a series of poor decisions that ultimately cost him his job. 他的不明智导致了一系列糟糕的决定，最终让他丢了工作。|近义词：folly, imprudence, indiscretion|反义词：wisdom, prudence, discretion|词性:名词

词源:  From injudicious + -ness. Injudicious itself comes from in- (not) + judicious (wise, showing good judgment), which ultimately derives from Latin judicium (judgment).
