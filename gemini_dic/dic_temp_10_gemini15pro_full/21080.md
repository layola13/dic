bling:/blɪŋ/
n.| 闪光饰品，珠宝|Expensive, ostentatious jewelry or other decorative items. |She was covered in bling. 她浑身戴满了闪光饰品。|近义词:jewelry, ice, sparkle, glitter|反义词:plainness, simplicity|词性:名词

bling:/blɪŋ/
vi.| 闪耀，闪光|To shine or glitter ostentatiously. |Her diamond necklace blinged under the lights. 她的钻石项链在灯光下闪闪发光。|近义词:sparkle, glitter, shimmer, flash|反义词:dull, fade|词性:动词
