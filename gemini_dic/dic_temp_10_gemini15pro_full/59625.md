ecclesiarch: /ɪˌkliːziɑːrk/ | n. | 教会统治者，教会领袖 | A ruler or leader of a church or religious organization. | The ecclesiarch decreed a new set of religious laws. 教会统治者颁布了一套新的宗教法律。 |  The ecclesiarch presided over the grand ceremony.  教会领袖主持了盛大的仪式。 | synonyms: prelate, primate, patriarch, metropolitan | antonyms: layman, follower, parishioner | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ecclesiarchēs*, from Greek *ekklēsiarkhēs*, from *ekklēsia* (church, assembly) + *arkhein* (to rule).
