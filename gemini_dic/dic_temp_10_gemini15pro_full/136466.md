nonrevival:/ˌnɑːnriˈvaɪvəl/
n.|未复兴，未恢复|The nonrevival of the play disappointed many theatregoers.  这出戏没有重新上演，让很多戏迷失望了。|The nonrevival of the economy led to further hardship. 经济未能复苏导致了进一步的困境。|近义词：decline, stagnation, dormancy|反义词: revival, resurgence, renaissance|词性:名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
revival (noun): from re- "again" + vive "to live" (from Latin vivere)
