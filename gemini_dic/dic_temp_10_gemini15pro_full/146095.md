overeffusively: /ˌoʊvərɪˈfjuːsɪvli/
adverb
过于热情地，过分热情地
Expressing feelings of gratitude, pleasure, or approval in an unrestrained or excessive way.
She greeted him overeffusively, embarrassing him in front of his colleagues. 她过分热情地和他打招呼，让他在同事面前很尴尬。
Near Synonyms:  excessively, extravagantly, immoderately, profusely, too enthusiastically
Antonyms:  reservedly, restrainedly, subtly, understatedly
词性: adverb (副词)
