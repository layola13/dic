contrarinesses: /ˌkɒntrəˈrɪnɪsɪz/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈrɪnɪsɪz/
| n. | 反抗，逆反，固执 | The state of being contrary; a disposition to oppose or contradict; perverseness. | Her contrarinesses made it difficult to work with her. 她的固执使得很难与她共事。 | 近义词: perverseness, obstinacy, stubbornness, willfulness | 反义词: agreeableness, compliance, obedience, docility | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *contrariousnes, from contrarious +‎ -ness.  Ultimately from Latin contrārius (“opposite, contrary”) + -ness.
