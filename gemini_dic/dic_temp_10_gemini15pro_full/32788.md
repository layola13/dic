choiceless:/ˈtʃɔɪslɪs/| adj.|无选择的，别无选择的，没有选择余地的|Having no choice or alternative; involuntary.|She felt choiceless in the matter. 她觉得在这件事上别无选择。|近义词：unavoidable, compulsory, inevitable, obligatory|反义词：optional, voluntary|词性:形容词
Etymology: choice + -less.  From *choice* (noun, meaning "act of choosing, selection") + *-less* (suffix forming adjectives meaning "without, lacking").
