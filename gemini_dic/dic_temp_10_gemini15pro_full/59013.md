dvergr:/ˈdvɛrɡr/| n.|矮人,侏儒|(from Old Norse *dvergr, meaning "dwarf") A mythical being from Norse mythology, typically short in stature, skilled in crafting and metalworking, and often associated with mountains and the earth.  Dvergr are known for their wisdom, magical abilities, and sometimes, their greed.|The dvergr fashioned a magnificent hammer for Thor.|近义词：dwarf, gnome|反义词:giant|词性:noun
