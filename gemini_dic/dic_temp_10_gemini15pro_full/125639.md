mumbo-jumbo:/ˈmʌmboʊ ˈdʒʌmboʊ/
|n.|胡言乱语；莫名其妙的话；繁文缛节|Meaningless or unintelligible speech; ritualistic or esoteric language often intended to obscure or impress.|例句：The politician's speech was full of mumbo-jumbo that no one understood. 这位政客的演讲充满了没人听得懂的胡言乱语。

例句：The ancient ritual involved a lot of mumbo-jumbo and chanting.  这个古老的仪式包含了大量的繁文缛节和吟唱。|近义词：nonsense, gibberish, jargon, claptrap, balderdash|反义词:sense, clarity, truth|词性:名词
