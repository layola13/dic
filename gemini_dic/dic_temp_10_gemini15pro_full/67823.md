eyewitnessed:/ˌaɪˈwɪtnɪst/ | v. | 目睹，亲眼看见 |  To have personally seen something happen; to be an eyewitness to something. | She eyewitnessed the robbery. 她亲眼目睹了抢劫。|  saw, observed, witnessed | overlooked, missed | verb

eyewitnessed:/ˌaɪˈwɪtnɪst/ | adj. (less common) |  亲眼目睹的 | Seen personally; witnessed firsthand. |  The eyewitnessed account of the crash was compelling.  亲眼目睹的撞车描述令人信服。| observed, witnessed | unobserved, unverified | adjective
