folliculous:/fəˈlɪkjʊləs/
adjective
多毛的，多囊的; covered with, or having, follicles
Having numerous small cavities or sacs.
The plant's stem was folliculous, covered in tiny bumps.  这种植物的茎多囊，覆盖着微小的突起。
The folliculous ovarian tissue appeared abnormal in the ultrasound. 超声波显示多囊卵巢组织异常。
近义词: hairy, bristly, pilose
反义词: glabrous, smooth
