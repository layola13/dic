The nonpliant branches wouldn't bend to form an archway. 这些倔强的树枝无法弯曲形成拱门。 | 近义词:inflexible, rigid, unyielding, stubborn, obstinate | 反义词:pliant, flexible, yielding, compliant, obedient | 词性:形容词
