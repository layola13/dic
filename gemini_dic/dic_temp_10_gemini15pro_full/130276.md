non-subsidized:/ˌnɒnˈsʌbsɪdaɪzd/
adjective
未补贴的，无津贴的
Not receiving financial assistance or support from a government or other organization.
Students with non-subsidized loans have to start paying interest immediately.  持有未补贴贷款的学生必须立即开始支付利息。
The company operates on a non-subsidized basis. 这家公司在没有补贴的情况下运营。
近义词：unsupported, unaided
反义词：subsidized, supported
词性：adjective
