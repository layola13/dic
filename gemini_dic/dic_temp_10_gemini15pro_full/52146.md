diaphanic:/ˌdaɪəˈfænɪk/| adj.|透明的，半透明的|Allowing light to pass through so that objects behind can be distinctly seen.  Characterized by such fineness of texture as to permit seeing through; translucent; transparent; pellucid. | The artist used diaphanic fabrics to create a sense of ethereal beauty.  艺术家使用透明的织物来营造一种空灵的美感。|Synonyms: translucent, transparent, sheer, gossamer, pellucid |Antonyms: opaque, dense, thick, impenetrable|adjective
