gradualistic:/ˌɡrædʒuəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
逐渐的;渐进的
Happening or developing slowly and by degrees.
The museum's gradualistic approach to acquiring new pieces has built a well-rounded collection over time. 博物馆逐步获得新藏品的做法，随着时间的推移，建立了一个全面的收藏。
Evolution is a gradualistic process. 进化是一个渐进的过程。
近义词:gradual, incremental, progressive, step-by-step
反义词:sudden, abrupt, rapid, immediate
词性:adjective
