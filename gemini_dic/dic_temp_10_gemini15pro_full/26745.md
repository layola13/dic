cadres:/ˈkɑːdrəz/ (US), /ˈkɑːdrəɪz/ (UK)| n.| 骨干，干部，核心小组|A group of trained or otherwise qualified personnel capable of forming, training, or leading an expanded organization, movement, or the like.  A cadre of scientists were working on the project.| The party cadres were responsible for disseminating propaganda.  党的干部负责宣传。|synonyms: core, nucleus, team, group, personnel|antonyms:  rank and file, masses, multitude|noun
