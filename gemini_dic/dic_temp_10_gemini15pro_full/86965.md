hendecagonal:/ˌhɛnˈdɛkəˈɡɒnəl/ /ˌhɛnˈdɛkəˈɡɑːnəl/
adjective
十一邊形的；十一角形的
Having eleven sides and eleven angles.
A hendecagonal prism has two hendecagonal bases and eleven rectangular sides.  一个十一棱柱有两个十一边形的底面和十一个矩形侧面。
The architect incorporated a hendecagonal window into the design.  建筑师在设计中加入了一个十一边形的窗户。
Near Synonyms: eleven-sided, eleven-angled
