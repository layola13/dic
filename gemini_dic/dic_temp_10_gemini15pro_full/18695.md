berating:/bɪˈreɪtɪŋ/
verb
严厉斥责，痛骂
Criticizing or scolding someone angrily and at length.
The coach was berating the players for their poor performance. 教练因球员表现不佳而严厉斥责他们。
He berated her for being late. 他因她迟到而严厉斥责她。
reprimanding, scolding, chastising, rebuking
praising, commending, complimenting
