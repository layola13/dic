parabronchus:/ˈpærəbrɒŋkəs/ (US) /ˈpærəbrɒŋkəs/ (UK)| n. | 支气管 |A parabronchus is one of the many small air passages in the lungs of birds, which allow air to flow unidirectionally through the lungs during both inspiration and expiration, unlike the tidal flow in mammalian lungs.|The intricate network of parabronchi facilitates efficient gas exchange in birds. 鸟类错综复杂的支气管网络促进了有效的气体交换。|The structure of the parabronchus is crucial for the high metabolic demands of flight.  支气管的结构对于飞行的高代谢需求至关重要。|Synonym: air capillary |Antonym: N/A|noun
