mannequin:/ˈmænɪkɪn/ /ˈmænəˌkɪn/ | n. |人体模型；时装模特儿|A life-sized dummy used to display clothes in shop windows.  A person employed to model clothes.|例句：The mannequins in the window display were dressed in the latest fashions.  橱窗里的人体模型穿着最新款式的服装。

例句：She worked as a mannequin before becoming an actress.  在成为演员之前，她做过时装模特儿。|近义词：dummy, model, form, figure|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
