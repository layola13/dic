nonpracticality:/ˌnɒnpræk.tɪˈkæl.ɪ.ti/ /ˌnɑːnpræk.tɪˈkæl.ə.ti/
noun
不实用性，非实用性
The nonpracticality of the design made it unsuitable for mass production.  这个设计的不实用性使其不适合大规模生产。
The committee rejected the proposal due to its nonpracticality. 委员会因其不切实际而拒绝了该提议。
Synonyms: impracticality, unfeasibility, uselessness
Antonyms: practicality, feasibility, usefulness
n.


Etymology:
From non- + practicality.  "Practicality" comes from the Late Middle English "practical", derived from the Medieval Latin "practicus" and ultimately from the Greek "praktikos", meaning "fit for action".
