例句：The leporous condition of the building was evident in its crumbling facade. 这栋建筑的破败状况从其摇摇欲坠的正面就能明显看出。|近义词：leprous, diseased, infected, contaminated, tainted|反义词：healthy, clean, pure, unblemished|词性:adjective
