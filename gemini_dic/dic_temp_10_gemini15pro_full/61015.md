elinguid: /iˈlɪŋɡwɪd/
adjective
无舌的; 不会说话的
lacking a tongue; speechless.
He stood there, elinguid and trembling, unable to utter a single word. 他站在那里，无舌且颤抖，说不出一个字。
近义词: speechless, mute, dumb, silent
反义词: eloquent, articulate, talkative
词性: adjective

词源:  From e- (meaning "without") + lingu (meaning "tongue") + -id (adjectival suffix).  The "lingu" part ultimately comes from Latin "lingua" meaning tongue.
