negotiate: /nɪˈɡoʊʃieɪt/ | v. | 谈判，协商 | To discuss something formally in order to make an agreement. |  We successfully negotiated a new contract. 我们成功地协商了一份新合同。 | Synonyms: bargain, discuss, confer, mediate | Antonyms: dictate, impose, refuse | verb
