gynoecium:/ɡaɪˈniːsiəm/
n.
雌蕊群
The female reproductive organ of a flower, consisting of one or more carpels.
The gynoecium of this flower has three carpels.  这朵花的雌蕊群有三个心皮。
近义词:pistil
反义词:androecium
