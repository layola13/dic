connaturality:/ˌkɒnætʃəˈrælɪti/ /ˌkɑːnætʃəˈrælɪti/
|n.|同质性，共性，天然联系|The state of being connatural; participation in the same nature.  A close affinity or connection.|His connaturality with the land was evident in his paintings. 他对这片土地的天然联系在他的绘画中显而易见。|Near synonyms: affinity, kinship, consanguinity, congeniality|Antonyms:  disparity, difference, dissimilarity|noun
