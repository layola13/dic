ethnologies:/ˌɛθˈnɒlədʒiz/
n.
民族志学，人种志学
The study of the characteristics of various peoples and the differences and relationships between them.
Ethnologies from around the world offer valuable insights into human cultures.  世界各地的民族志为了解人类文化提供了宝贵的见解。
Ethnologies often compare and contrast different societies. 民族志经常比较和对比不同的社会。
synonyms: anthropology, cultural studies
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but potentially: uniformity, homogeneity)
noun
