decriers:/dɪˈkraɪərz/
n.
批评者，诋毁者
People who express strong disapproval or criticism of someone or something.
The president's policies have many detractors and decriers. 总统的政策有很多批评者和诋毁者。
critics, detractors, attackers, denouncers
supporters, advocates, defenders, praisers
noun
