byssine:/ˈbɪsaɪn/| adj.|亚麻布的，细麻布制的，极细的|Made of or resembling byssus; very fine. |The princess wore a byssine gown. 公主穿着一件细麻布长袍。|近义词：fine, delicate, sheer, diaphanous|反义词：coarse, rough, thick|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin *byssinus, from Latin byssus (“fine linen, cotton”), from Ancient Greek βύσσος (bússos, “fine flax, linen”).
