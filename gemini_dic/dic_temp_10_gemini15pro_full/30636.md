ceremoniousnesses:/ˌserɪˈmoʊniəsnəsəz/
n.
礼节；仪式；拘谨；正式
The state of being ceremonious; formality; adherence to ceremony.
Her ceremoniousnesses made everyone feel uncomfortable. 她的拘谨让每个人都感到不舒服。
近义词:formalities, rituals, stiffness
反义词:informality, casualness
词性:名词

Etymology:
From ceremonious + -ness.  Ceremonious derives from ceremony, which comes from Old French ceremonie, ultimately from Latin caerimonia "sacred rite, religious observance."
