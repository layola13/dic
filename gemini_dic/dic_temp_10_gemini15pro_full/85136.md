hardland:/ˈhɑːrdlənd/| n.|旱地，贫瘠的土地|Arid and unproductive land, typically with thin, rocky soil.|例句：The settlers struggled to farm the hardland. 定居者们努力在贫瘠的土地上耕作。

例句：The hardland stretched for miles, a testament to the harshness of the climate. 贫瘠的土地绵延数英里，证明了气候的恶劣。|近义词：badlands, wasteland, barren land|反义词: fertile land, rich soil|词性:名词

词源:  Compound of 'hard' (meaning firm and resistant, also implying difficulty in cultivation) and 'land'.
