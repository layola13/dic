infra-orbital:/ˌɪnfrəˈɔːrbɪtl/
adjective
眶下的
situated beneath the orbit of the eye.
The infra-orbital nerve supplies sensation to the lower eyelid, cheek, and upper lip.  眶下神经支配下眼睑、脸颊和上唇的感觉。
The infra-orbital artery supplies blood to the area below the eye. 眶下动脉为眼睛下方的区域供血。
inferior to the orbit, suborbital
