|adj.|下放的，移交的|transferred or delegated to a lower level of authority|The king devolved some of his power to the local governors. 国王将部分权力下放给地方官员。|近义词:delegate, transfer, devolve|反义词:centralize, retain|词性:形容词
devolute:/ˌdiːvəˈluːt/
|v.|下放，移交（权力、责任等）|to transfer or delegate (power, responsibility, etc.) to a lower level of authority|The government devolved responsibility for education to local authorities. 政府将教育责任下放给地方当局。|近义词:delegate, transfer, devolve|反义词:centralize, retain|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin devolutus, past participle of devolvere "to roll down," from de- "down" + volvere "to roll."
