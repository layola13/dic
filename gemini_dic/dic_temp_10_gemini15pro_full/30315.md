censorial:/senˈsɔːriəl/
adjective
审查的；检查的
Relating to censorship or to a censor.  Expressing or involving censorship.
例句：The government imposed censorial restrictions on the press. 政府对新闻界施加了审查限制。

例句：The censorial board cut several scenes from the film. 审查委员会从电影中剪掉了几个场景。

近义词： restrictive, suppressive, controlling, expurgatory
反义词： permissive, liberal, unrestricted
词性：adjective


censor:/ˈsensər/
noun
审查员；检查员
An official who examines books, films, news, etc. to remove anything considered offensive or harmful.
例句：The censor banned the book because of its political content.  审查员因为这本书的政治内容而禁了它。

例句：The film was passed by the censor without any cuts. 这部电影未经任何删减就通过了审查。

近义词： examiner, inspector, reviewer, editor
反义词：N/A
词性：noun


censor:/ˈsensər/
verb
审查；检查
To examine books, films, news, etc. to remove anything considered offensive or harmful.
例句：The government censored all news reports about the protests. 政府审查了所有关于抗议活动的新闻报道。

例句：Many websites are censored in some countries.  在一些国家，许多网站都受到审查。

近义词：  suppress, restrict, control, expurgate
反义词： permit, allow, publish
词性：verb
