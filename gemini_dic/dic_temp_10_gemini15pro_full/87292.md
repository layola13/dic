hermit:/ˈhɜːmɪt/ (US) /ˈhɜːmɪt/ (UK)| n.|隐士，遁世者|A person who lives in solitude, often for religious reasons, secluded from society.|The old hermit lived in a cave in the mountains. 这位老隐士住在山里的一个洞穴里。|Synonyms: recluse, anchorite, solitary|Antonyms: socialite, extrovert|noun

hermit:/ˈhɜːmɪt/ (US) /ˈhɜːmɪt/ (UK)| adj.|隐居的，遁世的|(of a person) Living in solitude; (of a place) secluded or suitable for a hermit.|He led a hermit existence in the forest. 他在森林里过着隐居的生活。|Synonyms: reclusive, secluded, solitary|Antonyms: social, gregarious|adjective
