osmund:/ˈɒzmənd/| n.|紫萁|A fern of the genus Osmunda, having large, pinnately compound fronds and sporangia borne in clusters on modified fronds.|The royal osmund is a particularly striking fern. 紫萁蕨是一种特别引人注目的蕨类植物。|Synonyms: fern, brake |Antonyms: N/A|Noun
