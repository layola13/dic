amorosities:/ˌæməˈrɒsətiz/
noun
爱情, 恋爱, 暧昧关系
love affairs, especially of a playful or casual nature.
Her early diaries are filled with accounts of her various amorosities. 她早期的日记里充满了对她各种爱情的描述。
近义词:love affairs, romances, flirtations, dalliances
反义词:aversions, hostilities
词性:noun (plural)
