hygrometric:/ˌhaɪɡrəˈmɛtrɪk/
adjective
测湿的;湿度测定的
relating to hygrometry; measuring the humidity of the air or a gas.
The hygrometric properties of the material were carefully considered in the design of the sensor.  这种材料的测湿特性在传感器设计中被仔细考虑。
The researchers used a hygrometric sensor to monitor the moisture levels in the air. 研究人员使用湿度传感器来监测空气中的水分含量。
synonyms: hygroscopic, moisture-sensitive
antonyms: non-hygroscopic, moisture-resistant
词性: adjective
