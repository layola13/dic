demesnial:/dɪˈmiːniəl/
adjective
领地的；属于领主的
Of or relating to a lord's demesne.  Pertaining to or comprising the land attached to a manor and retained by the lord for his own use.
The demesnial lands were extensive and productive.  领主的土地广阔而肥沃。
Synonym: manorial, seigneurial, baronial
Antonym: public, common, communal
词性: adjective

demesne:/dɪˈmeɪn/
noun
领地；庄园；私人财产
Land attached to a manor and retained by the lord for his own use. A region or domain over which dominion is exercised.  Personal estate or property.
The king's demesne included forests and hunting grounds. 国王的领地包括森林和狩猎场。
Synonym: estate, domain, territory, property
Antonym: public land, common land
词性: noun
