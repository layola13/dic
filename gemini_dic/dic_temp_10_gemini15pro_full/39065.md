conformers:/kənˈfɔːrmərz/
n.
遵从者；顺应者
People or things that conform to rules, standards, or customs.
Deep-sea fish are often conformers, their internal body temperature matching the surrounding water. 深海鱼通常是顺应者，它们的体内温度与周围的水温相匹配。

Conformers adapt to their environment. 顺应者适应他们的环境。

近义词:adapters, compliers, followers
反义词:nonconformists, rebels, dissenters
词性:noun (plural)


conform:/kənˈfɔːrm/
v.
遵从；顺应；符合
To comply with rules, standards, or customs.
He conformed to the local customs. 他遵守了当地的习俗。

She refused to conform to the stereotype. 她拒绝顺从刻板印象。


近义词: comply, obey, adapt
反义词: disobey, rebel, defy
词性:verb


conforming:/kənˈfɔːrmɪŋ/
adj.
一致的；顺从的；符合的
Adhering to established rules or customs.
The conforming behavior of the students pleased the teacher. 学生们顺从的行为使老师很高兴。

His conforming attitude stifled his creativity. 他墨守成规的态度扼杀了他的创造力。

近义词: compliant, obedient, conventional
反义词: nonconforming, rebellious, unconventional
词性:adjective



词源:
From Late Latin conformāre "to form in harmony with," from com- "together" + formāre "to form."
