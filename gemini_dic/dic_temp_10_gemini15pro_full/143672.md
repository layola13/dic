outlasting:/ˌaʊtˈlæstɪŋ/
verb
比...持久；比...长久
survive or last longer than; live or exist longer than
The pyramids have outlasted many empires. 金字塔比许多帝国存在的时间都长。
近义词: outlive, survive, endure
反义词: succumb, perish
词性: verb (present participle)


outlasting:/ˌaʊtˈlæstɪŋ/
adjective
持久的；耐久的
lasting longer than; surviving longer than
The company's outlasting success is due to its innovative products. 该公司持久的成功归功于其创新产品。
近义词: enduring, long-lasting, persistent
反义词: short-lived, ephemeral, fleeting
词性: adjective


词源:  "out-" (meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + "last" (meaning "to continue in existence")
