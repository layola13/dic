nonassonances:/ˌnɒnˈæsənənsɪz/
|n.| 非谐音；不谐和的声音|The poem was full of nonassonances, making it jarring to the ear.|这首诗充满了不和谐的声音，听起来很刺耳。|
|英文解析: The absence of assonance, or the lack of repeated vowel sounds in words close to each other within a phrase or sentence.|
|例句：The poet deliberately used nonassonances to create a sense of unease. 诗人故意使用不和谐音来营造一种不安的感觉。|

|例句：The nonassonances in the piece made it sound cacophonous rather than melodious.  这首作品中的不和谐音使它听起来刺耳而不是悦耳。|

|近义词：dissonance, discord, cacophony|
|反义词：assonance, consonance, harmony|
|词性：noun|
