espinel:/ˌɛspɪˈnɛl/ | n. |尖晶石；埃斯皮内尔诗体|A hard, glassy mineral consisting of magnesium aluminate, typically occurring as variously colored octahedral crystals.  A type of décima poem named after Vicente Espinel. |The ring was set with a large red espinel. 这枚戒指上镶嵌着一颗大的红色尖晶石。|Near synonyms: spinel, ruby, garnet|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

espinel:/ˌɛspɪˈnɛl/ | adj. |埃斯皮内尔的|Relating to or characteristic of Vicente Espinel or his poetry. |He wrote an espinel poem in the traditional style. 他用传统风格写了一首埃斯皮内尔诗。|Near synonyms: Espinelian|Antonyms: N/A|Adjective
