conform:/kənˈfɔːrm/ (US) /kənˈfɔːm/ (UK)| v. |遵守，遵从，符合；顺应|To act in accordance with prevailing standards, attitudes, or practices.  To be similar or identical; to correspond.|例句：He refused to conform to the social norms. 他拒绝遵从社会规范。|近义词：comply, obey, adapt, adjust|反义词：rebel, disobey, deviate, differ|verb

conform:/kənˈfɔːrm/ (US) /kənˈfɔːm/ (UK)| adj. |一致的，顺应的|Similar or identical; corresponding.|例句：The building's design is conform to safety regulations.  这座建筑的设计符合安全规范。|近义词：compliant, obedient, similar, consistent|反义词：nonconformist, rebellious, dissimilar, inconsistent|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *conformier, from Latin conformare (“to form, shape, fashion, arrange”), from con- (“together”) + formare (“to form”).
