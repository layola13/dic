atomician:/ˌætəˈmɪʃən/ | n. |原子论者;原子学家| A believer in or supporter of atomism; a person who studies atoms. | Democritus was a prominent atomician in ancient Greece. 德谟克利特是古希腊著名的原子论者。| The atomician explained the properties of matter in terms of atoms and their interactions. 原子学家用原子及其相互作用来解释物质的性质。 |  Synonyms: atomist | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "continuist" philosophically) | noun
