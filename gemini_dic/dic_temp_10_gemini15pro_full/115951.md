marriages:/ˈmærɪdʒɪz/
n.
婚姻（marriage的复数形式）
The state of being united to a person of the opposite sex as husband or wife in a consensual and contractual relationship recognized by law.
Many marriages end in divorce these days.  如今很多婚姻以离婚告终。
近义词：weddings, unions
反义词：divorces, separations
词性：名词

词源：Middle English: from Old French mariage, from marier ‘marry’, from Latin maritare, from maritus ‘husband’.
