hypertechnicality:/ˌhaɪpərˌtɛknɪˈkælɪti/ | n. | 过于技术性的细节；钻技术牛角尖 | An excessive focus on or adherence to technical details, often to the point of obscuring the larger issue or becoming pedantic. | The lawyer's argument was bogged down in hypertechnicalities, losing the jury's attention. 律师的论点陷入了过于技术性的细节，失去了陪审团的注意力。 | synonyms: minutiae, technicalities, hairsplitting, nitpicking | antonyms: generality, simplification, broad strokes | noun

hypertechnical:/ˌhaɪpərˈtɛknɪkəl/ | adj. | 过于技术性的；钻技术牛角尖的 | Excessively concerned with or focused on technical details. | The instructions were so hypertechnical that even the experts had trouble understanding them.  这些说明过于技术性，即使是专家也很难理解。| synonyms: overly technical, excessively detailed, pedantic, nitpicky | antonyms: general, simplified, broad | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (meaning "excessive, over") + technicality.  "Hyper-" comes from the Greek "hyper" meaning "over, beyond." "Technicality" comes from "technical" + "-ity." "Technical" derives from the Greek "technikos" meaning "pertaining to art."
