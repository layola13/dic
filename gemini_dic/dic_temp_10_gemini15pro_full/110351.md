lightness:/ˈlaɪtnəs/| n.|轻盈，亮度，轻率|The lightness of the fabric made it perfect for summer dresses. 这种面料的轻盈使它非常适合做夏装。|Synonyms: airiness, brightness, levity|Antonyms: heaviness, darkness, gravity|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lightnesse*, from Old English *lēohtnes*, from *lēoht* ("light") + *-nes* (suffix forming abstract nouns).
