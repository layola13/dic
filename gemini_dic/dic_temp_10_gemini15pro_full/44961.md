crystal's: /ˈkrɪstlz/ | n. | 水晶的; 晶体的 |  Belonging to or associated with a crystal or crystals.  |  Example: The crystal's facets reflected the light beautifully.  水晶的刻面将光线反射得非常漂亮。| Example:  I admired the crystal's clarity and sparkle. 我欣赏水晶的清澈和闪耀。 | Synonyms: crystalline, glassy, transparent | Antonyms: opaque, cloudy, dull | Possessive noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English cristal, from Old French cristal, from Latin crystallum, from Ancient Greek κρύσταλλος (krústallos, “ice, crystal”), from κρύος (krúos, “ice-cold, frost”).
