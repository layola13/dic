v.|使失效，解除，停用|To make something inactive or no longer functioning; to disable or disengage a mechanism. |The pilot deactuated the autopilot before landing. 飞行员在着陆前关闭了自动驾驶仪。|deactivate, disable, disengage|activate, enable, engage|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove") + actuate (from Latin actuatus, past participle of actuare "to put into action," from actus "a doing, a driving," from agere "to do, drive, lead").
