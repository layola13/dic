misdread:/mɪsˈdred/| v. | 误恐; 害怕不应害怕的事; 对…怀有无端的恐惧 | To fear something that is not a real threat or to have an unfounded fear of something. | She misdreaded the upcoming exam, even though she was well-prepared. 她对即将到来的考试感到莫名的恐惧，即使她已经做好了充分的准备。

He misdreaded the trip, only to find it was quite enjoyable. 他对这次旅行感到担忧，结果却发现它非常愉快。| 近义词: fear, apprehend, worry, dread | 反义词:  be unafraid, have no fear | 词性: 动词
