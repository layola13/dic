grateless:/ˈɡreɪtləs/| adj.|无炉栅的，无格栅的|Lacking a grate, especially referring to a fireplace or stove.|The grateless fireplace made it difficult to control the fire.|近义词:ungrated|反义词:grated|词性:形容词
词源: grate + -less.  "Grate" comes from Old French "grate," meaning "grating," from Medieval Latin "crata." "-less" is a suffix meaning "without."
