cartouche:/kɑːrˈtuːʃ/ /kɑrˈtuːʃ/
noun
涡卷形装饰;  装饰框;  (古埃及象形文字的)椭圆形框;  子弹带;  火药筒;
An oval or oblong figure, as on ancient Egyptian monuments, enclosing hieroglyphs of a royal name. A decorative tablet or panel in the shape of a scroll with rolled-up ends, used to display an inscription or coats of arms.
The pharaoh's name was written inside a cartouche. 法老的名字写在椭圆形框内。
The building's facade was adorned with cartouches bearing intricate designs.  建筑物的正面装饰着带有复杂设计的涡卷形装饰。
Synonyms:  ornament, decoration, emblem, insignia, escutcheon
Antonyms:  plainness, simplicity
n.

cartouche:/kɑːrˈtuːʃ/ /kɑrˈtuːʃ/
noun
(尤指旧时士兵的)子弹带
A cartridge belt, especially one worn over the shoulder.
The soldier adjusted his cartouche, ensuring his ammunition was readily accessible. 士兵调整了他的子弹带，确保他的弹药很容易拿到。
Old Western movies often depict cowboys wearing cartouches across their chests.  老西部电影经常描绘牛仔胸前挎着子弹带。
Synonyms:  bandolier, ammunition belt
Antonyms: N/A
