haling:/ˈheɪlɪŋ/| v.|拖，拉，拽；大声招呼；强迫…去某处|The fishermen were haling in their nets. 渔民们正在收网。|近义词：pulling, dragging, hauling,  yelling, shouting|反义词: pushing, shoving|verb

Etymology->
From Middle English *halen, *halien, from Old English *halian, *hōlian (“to fetch, pull, draw”), from Proto-Germanic *halōną, *haljaną (“to pull, fetch, bring”), from Proto-Indo-European *kel- (“to drive, set in motion”). Cognate with Dutch halen (“to fetch, bring”), German holen (“to fetch, bring”), Swedish hala (“to haul”), Danish hale (“to haul”).
