dehyphening:/diːˈhaɪfənɪŋ/
noun
去连字符号化
The act of removing hyphens from a word or phrase.
Dehyphening is common in modern writing. 去连字符号化在现代写作中很常见。
The software performed automatic dehyphening.  该软件执行了自动去连字符号化。
近义词: hyphen removal, unhyphenation
反义词: hyphenation
词性: n.
