antifemininely:/ˌæntiˈfɛmɪnɪnli/
| adv. |
| 不像女性地，缺乏女性气质地 |
| In a way that is not considered typical of or appropriate for a woman; unwomanly. |
| She dressed antifemininely in loose-fitting trousers and a plain shirt. 她穿着不像女性的宽松裤子和朴素的衬衫。|
| She behaved antifemininely, refusing to conform to traditional gender roles. 她表现得不像女性，拒绝遵守传统的性别角色。|
| 近义词: unwomanly, mannishly, masculinely |
| 反义词: femininely, womanly |
| 词性: 副词(adverb) |
