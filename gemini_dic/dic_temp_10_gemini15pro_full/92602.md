iambical:/aɪˈæmbɪk(ə)l/
adjective
抑扬格的，由抑扬格组成的
Of or relating to iambs; composed of iambs.
The poem is written in iambical pentameter. 这首诗是用抑扬格五音步写成的。

The play's dialogue is characterized by its elegant iambical rhythm.  这出戏的对话以其优雅的抑扬格节奏为特征。
synonym:iambic
antonym:n/a
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin iambicus, from Greek iambikos, from iambos (“iamb”) + -ikos (“-ic”).
