blinds:/blaɪndz/| n.| 百叶窗；窗帘；遮光物|A covering for a window, especially one made of slats.|She closed the blinds to block out the sun. 她拉上百叶窗以遮挡阳光。|近义词：shades, shutters, curtains|反义词: none (blinds block light, the opposite would be something that allows light in, not a specific single word)|noun

blinds:/blaɪndz/| v.| 使失明；使目眩；使思维不清| Deprive of sight; dazzle; deprive of judgment or understanding.|The sudden bright light blinded me momentarily. 突然的强光使我暂时失明。|近义词：dazzle, obscure|反义词: enlighten, illuminate|verb
