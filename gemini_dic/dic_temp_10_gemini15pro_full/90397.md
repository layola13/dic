howler:/ˈhaʊlər/| n.| 咆哮; 嚎叫; 滑稽可笑的人(或事物);  大错; |A loud cry expressing pain, anger, or amusement.  A ridiculously funny person or thing. A blunder.|The hyenas let out a series of bloodcurdling howlers.  鬣狗发出了一连串令人毛骨悚然的嚎叫。

That joke was a real howler. 那个笑话真是滑稽。

Missing the deadline was a major howler. 错过最后期限是一个大错。|近义词：yell, scream, roar, shriek, blunder, gaffe, mistake|反义词: whisper, murmur|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English houlen, from Old French huller, of Germanic origin.
