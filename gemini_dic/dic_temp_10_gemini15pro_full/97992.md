inoffensive:/ˌɪnəˈfɛnsɪv/| adj.|无害的，不冒犯的，不讨厌的|Not likely to give offense or to arouse strong feelings or hostility; harmless, unobjectionable.|Her remarks were entirely inoffensive. 她的言论完全没有冒犯之意。|Synonyms: harmless, innocuous, unobjectionable, benign|Antonyms: offensive, harmful, obnoxious, objectionable|adjective

inoffensive:/ˌɪnəˈfɛnsɪv/| adj.|平淡无奇的，乏味的|Lacking interest or excitement; bland.|The decor was inoffensive but rather dull.  这装潢并不难看，但相当乏味。|Synonyms: bland, dull, uninteresting|Antonyms: interesting, exciting, stimulating|adjective
