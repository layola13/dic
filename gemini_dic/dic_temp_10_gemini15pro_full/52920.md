dimpling:/ˈdɪmplɪŋ/ | n. | 酒窝; 浅凹; 波纹 | a small hollow place, especially one that appears on a person's cheek when they smile; a slight hollow in a surface |  Her dimpling cheeks made her look even more charming. 她的酒窝让她看起来更加迷人。 | synonyms: indent, hollow, depression | antonyms: bulge, protrusion | noun

dimple:/ˈdɪmpl/ | n. | 酒窝; 浅凹 | a small hollow place, especially one that appears on a person's cheek when they smile | He has a dimple in his chin. 他的下巴上有个酒窝。| synonyms: indent, hollow, depression | antonyms: bulge, protrusion | noun

dimple:/ˈdɪmpl/ | v. |  (使)出现酒窝; (使)出现涟漪 | to form a dimple or dimples | The surface of the water dimpled in the breeze. 微风吹拂着水面，泛起涟漪。| synonyms: indent, hollow | antonyms: bulge, protrude | verb
