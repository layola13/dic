mesotartaric:/ˌmiːzoʊtɑːrˈtærɪk/ |adj.|内消旋酒石酸的；中酒石酸的| An optically inactive form of tartaric acid that is internally compensated. It has two chiral centers with opposite configurations, canceling each other's optical activity. |Mesotartaric acid is not commonly encountered in nature. 内消旋酒石酸在自然界中并不常见。|The synthesis of mesotartaric acid involves specific reaction conditions. 内消旋酒石酸的合成需要特定的反应条件。|近义词: internally compensated tartaric acid |反义词: (+)-tartaric acid, (-)-tartaric acid |词性: adjective

Etymology:
meso- (Greek): middle, intermediate + tartaric (from Late Latin tartarum, "tartar," a deposit in wine casks).
