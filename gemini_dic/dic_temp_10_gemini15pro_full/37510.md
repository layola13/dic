commodities:/kəˈmɒdətiz/ /kəˈmɑːdətiz/| n.|商品，货物|a raw material or primary agricultural product that can be bought and sold, such as coffee or gold.|例句：The prices of commodities like oil and gold have been fluctuating.  像石油和黄金这样的商品价格一直在波动。

例句：The country's economy is heavily reliant on the export of commodities.  这个国家的经济严重依赖商品出口。|近义词：goods, merchandise, products, wares|反义词:services|词性:noun
