nonraiseable:/ˌnɒnˈreɪzəbl/
adjective
不可加薪的；无法提高的
Not able to be raised or increased, especially in reference to wages or salaries.
The contract stated that his salary was nonraiseable for the first two years.  合同规定他的工资在头两年不可加薪。
The company imposed a nonraiseable salary freeze due to the economic downturn. 由于经济低迷，公司强制实行工资冻结，不得加薪。
near synonyms: fixed, unchangeable, static, inflexible
antonyms: raiseable, adjustable, variable, negotiable
词性: adjective
