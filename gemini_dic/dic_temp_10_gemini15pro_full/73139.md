foraminifer:/fəˌræmɪˈnɪfər/ |n.|有孔虫|A single-celled planktonic animal with a perforated chalky shell through which slender protrusions of protoplasm extend.  These shells form deposits on the ocean floor.|例句：Foraminifers are important indicators of past climates. 有孔虫是过去气候的重要指标。

例句：The study of foraminifers helps in oil exploration. 对有孔虫的研究有助于石油勘探。|近义词：protozoan, amoeboid|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


foraminifera:/fəˌræmɪˈnɪfərə/ |n.|有孔虫类|Plural form of foraminifer, referring to the group of these organisms.|例句：Foraminifera are abundant in marine environments. 有孔虫在海洋环境中很丰富。

例句：The shells of foraminifera contribute to the formation of limestone. 有孔虫的壳有助于石灰岩的形成。|近义词：protozoa|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


词源: From Modern Latin *foraminifera, neuter plural of *foraminifer,  from foramen "opening, hole" + -fer "bearing."
