calypsoed:/kəˈlɪpsoʊd/
verb
用卡里普索歌曲演唱；以卡里普索风格表演
performed or sang in the style of calypso music.
He calypsoed his way through the Caribbean festival. 他在加勒比海节日上以卡里普索风格演唱。
近义词:sang, performed
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用  remained silent 保持沉默)
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From calypso (n.) + -ed (verb suffix).  Calypso music originated in Trinidad and Tobago.
