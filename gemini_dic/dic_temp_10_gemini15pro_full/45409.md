cupolas:/ˈkjuːpələz/ (US), /ˈkjuːpələs/ (UK)| n. |穹顶，圆屋顶；【冶】冲天炉|A small dome, especially a roof over a circular or polygonal base.  A cupola can also refer to a cylindrical furnace used in foundries for melting metals.|The church has a beautiful copper cupola. 教堂有一个美丽的铜制穹顶。

The foundry uses a cupola to melt iron.  铸造厂用冲天炉熔化铁。|Synonyms: dome, turret, rotunda|Antonyms: base, foundation|noun


cupolas:/ˈkjuːpələz/ (US), /ˈkjuːpələs/ (UK)| n. |冲天炉|A tall cylindrical furnace used in foundries for melting metals, especially iron.|The molten iron poured from the cupola into molds.  熔化的铁水从冲天炉倒入模具中。|Synonyms: furnace, kiln|Antonyms: N/A|noun
