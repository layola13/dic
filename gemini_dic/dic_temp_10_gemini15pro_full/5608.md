alternant:/ˈɔːltərnənt/ | adj. n. | 交替的，轮流的；交替的事物 | Occurring in or forming a repeated series of two different things. | The leaves of the plant are alternant. 这种植物的叶子是交替生长的。 | The hospital has an alternant system for nurses, with day and night shifts. 这家医院对护士实行轮班制，有白班和夜班。 | 近义词：alternating, alternative, rotational | 反义词：continuous, constant | 词性: adjective, noun | 词源: From Latin alternans, present participle of alternare ("to alternate"), from alter ("other").

alternant:/ˈɔːltərnənt/ | n. | 交替的事物，轮流工作的人 |  A person who alternates between two different jobs or activities. |  He's an alternant in the engineering department, spending half his time in design and half in production. 他是工程部门的轮岗人员，一半时间在设计，一半时间在生产。 |  The company offers an alternant program for recent graduates. 这家公司为应届毕业生提供轮岗项目。 | 近义词：trainee, apprentice | 反义词：permanent employee | 词性: noun | 词源: From Latin alternans, present participle of alternare ("to alternate"), from alter ("other").
