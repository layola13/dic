implicated:/ˈɪmplɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
牵连的，涉及的
involved in or connected to something, especially something bad
例句：Several officials were implicated in the scandal.  几位官员被牵连进这桩丑闻。

例句：He was implicated in the robbery. 他和抢劫案有牵连。

近义词：involved, connected, incriminated
反义词：exonerated, cleared

词性: adjective
