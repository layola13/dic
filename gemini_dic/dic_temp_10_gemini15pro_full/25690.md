bunked:/bʌŋkt/
verb|逃课，躲避，睡在简易床上|To have slept, especially in a makeshift bed or a place not normally used for sleeping; to have avoided something, often school or work.|He bunked school to go to the football game. 他逃学去看足球比赛了。|近义词：skipped, dodged, avoided|反义词:attended, faced|v.

bunked:/bʌŋkt/
adjective|配备床铺的|Furnished with bunks or beds.|The cabin was small but bunked. 小屋很小，但有床铺。|近义词：bedded, furnished|反义词:unfurnished|adj.
