metrologists: /ˌmɛtrɒlədʒɪsts/ /ˌmiːtrɒlədʒɪsts/
n.
计量学家；度量衡学家
Experts in metrology, the science of measurement.
例句：The metrologists calibrated the instruments to ensure accurate readings.  计量学家校准了仪器以确保读数准确。

例句：A team of metrologists is working on developing new measurement standards. 一组计量学家正在致力于开发新的测量标准。

近义词：measurement specialists, calibration experts
反义词: None (No direct antonym for a profession)
词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *métrologiste*, from *métrol-* (from Greek *métron* "measure") + *-logiste* (from Greek *-logistēs* "one who studies (a certain field)").
