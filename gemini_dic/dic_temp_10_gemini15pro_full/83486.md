hackbuss:/ˈhækˌbʌs/ | n. | 火绳枪 | An early firearm, a type of arquebus, fired from the shoulder and ignited by a match. It was a precursor to the musket. | The soldiers marched into battle, their hackbutts ready. 士兵们带着准备好的火绳枪进入战场。

The king's guard was armed with hackbutts and pikes. 国王的卫队配备了火绳枪和长矛。 | 近义词:arquebus, handgonne | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps modern firearms) | 词性:noun
