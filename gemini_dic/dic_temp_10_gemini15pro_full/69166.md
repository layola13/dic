fawns:/fɔːnz/ | n. | 幼鹿（复数）, 奉承（第三人称单数） |  A young deer, less than a year old.  Also, the third-person singular present tense form of the verb "fawn," meaning to exhibit affection or attempt to gain favor by servile demeanor. | The fawns stayed close to their mother. 幼鹿紧紧地跟着它们的母亲。| 近义词: (for young deer) yearlings, calves; (for the verb) flatter, grovel | 反义词: (for the verb) criticize, defy | 词性: noun (primarily), verb (third person singular present tense)
fawn:/fɔːn/ | n. | 幼鹿 | A young deer, less than a year old. | The fawn hid in the tall grass. 幼鹿躲在高高的草丛里。| 近义词: yearling, calf | 反义词: (no direct antonym for the animal) | 词性: noun
fawn:/fɔːn/ | v. | 奉承，讨好 | To exhibit affection or attempt to gain favor by servile demeanor. | The courtiers fawned over the king. 朝臣们对国王阿谀奉承。| 近义词: flatter, grovel, kowtow | 反义词: criticize, defy, challenge | 词性: verb

词源: From Old English *fegen "glad, rejoicing," of Germanic origin; related to Dutch veulen "foal," and German fegen "to sweep."  The sense of "to show affection" developed from the frisky behavior of young animals.
