overrigidness:/ˌoʊvərˈrɪdʒɪdnəs/
n.
过度僵硬；过分刻板
Excessive rigidity; inflexibility.
Her overrigidness in applying the rules alienated her colleagues. 她对规则的过度僵化疏远了她的同事。
近义词：inflexibility, rigidity, stiffness
反义词：flexibility, adaptability, pliability
词性：名词

overrigid:/ˌoʊvərˈrɪdʒɪd/
adj.
过度僵硬的；过分刻板的
Excessively rigid or inflexible.
His overrigid parenting style stifled his children's creativity. 他过分刻板的育儿方式扼杀了孩子们的创造力。
近义词：inflexible, strict, unyielding
反义词：flexible, adaptable, lenient
词性：形容词
