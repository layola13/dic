dissenter:/dɪˈsɛntər/| n.|持异议者，反对者|A person who disagrees with the accepted opinion, especially in matters of religion or politics. |例句：The dissenter was ostracized by the community for his unorthodox views.  这个持异议者因为他的非正统观点而被社区排斥。

例句：Several dissenters voiced their objections to the proposed plan.  几位反对者表达了他们对拟议计划的反对意见。|近义词：objector, protestor, nonconformist, rebel, heretic|反义词：conformist, supporter, adherent|Noun
