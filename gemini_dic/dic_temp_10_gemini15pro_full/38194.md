compulsive:/kəmˈpʌlsɪv/| adj.|强迫性的，难以控制的|resulting from or relating to an irresistible urge, especially one that is against one's conscious wishes.|例句：He's a compulsive gambler. 他是个嗜赌成瘾的人。|例句：She has a compulsive need to be in control. 她有一种难以控制的掌控欲。|近义词：obsessive, uncontrollable, involuntary|反义词：controlled, voluntary, restrained|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin compuls- ‘driven, compelled,’ stem of compellere (see compel).
