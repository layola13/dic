franchisers:/ˈfræntʃaɪzərz/
n.
特许经营人，授予特许经营权者
A person or company that grants a franchise to another.
McDonald's is one of the biggest franchisers in the world. 麦当劳是世界上最大的特许经营商之一。
Many franchisers offer training and support to their franchisees. 许多特许经营商向其特许经营者提供培训和支持。
