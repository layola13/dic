equiaxe:/ˌiːkwiˈæks/ | adj. | 等轴的，等粒状的 | Having axes of equal length; composed of equidimensional crystals. | The equiaxe microstructure is typical of castings.  铸件的典型结构是等轴晶组织。| The solidified metal showed an equiaxe grain structure. 凝固后的金属呈现等轴晶粒结构。| 近义词: equidimensional, equigranular | 反义词: columnar, elongated | 词性: adjective

equiaxed:/ˌiːkwiˈækst/ | adj. | 等轴的，等粒状的 | Having axes of approximately equal length; said of crystals or grains in a metal or rock. | The equiaxed grains are randomly oriented. 等轴晶粒的方向是随机的。 | The microstructure revealed equiaxed ferrite grains. 显微组织显示了等轴状铁素体晶粒。 | 近义词: equiaxe, equidimensional, equigranular | 反义词: columnar, elongated | 词性: adjective


词源: From equi- + axis.  "Equi-" 表示“相等”，"axis" 表示“轴”。
