napping:/ˈnæpɪŋ/| n.|小睡，打盹|The act of taking a short sleep, typically during the day.|例句：I enjoy napping in the hammock on warm afternoons. 我喜欢在温暖的午后在吊床上小睡一会儿。

例句：Napping can improve alertness and performance.  小睡可以提高警觉性和表现。|近义词：dozing, resting, sleeping|反义词:staying awake, working|词性:noun
