axinomancy:/ˈæksɪnəˌmænsi/| n.|占卜，斧占卜|Divination by means of an axe or hatchet, observing its movements or the chips of wood it produces when struck against a hard surface.  It was practiced in ancient Greece and Rome.|The villagers resorted to axinomancy to determine the guilty party. 村民们 resorted to axinomancy to determine the guilty party. |近义词：divination, augury, prophecy|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "scientific analysis")|noun
