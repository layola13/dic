empyreumatical: /ˌɛmpaɪruːˈmætɪkl/ /ˌɛmpaɪruːˈmætɪkəl/
adjective
焦油的；烧焦的；有焦油味的
Relating to or having the smell or taste of burnt animal or vegetable matter.
The empyreumatical odor filled the air after the fire. 火灾过后，空气中弥漫着焦油味。
The steak had a slightly empyreumatical taste. 牛排有点焦味。
近义词:burnt, charred, smoky
反义词:fresh, unburnt, unscented
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *empyreumaticus, from Greek empyreuma "a live coal covered with ashes," from en- "in" + pyr "fire" + -ema (noun suffix).
