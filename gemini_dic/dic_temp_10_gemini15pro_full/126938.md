narratable:/ˈnærətəbl/ /ˈnɛrətəbl/| adj.|可讲述的，可以叙述的|Suitable to be narrated; capable of being narrated.|Her life story is narratable and full of interesting anecdotes.  她的人生故事很适合讲述，充满了有趣的轶事。|The events of that day are hardly narratable due to their sensitive nature. 那天发生的事件由于其敏感性几乎无法叙述。|近义词：recountable, tellable, describable|反义词：untellable, indescribable|词性: adjective
