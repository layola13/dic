inconstancy: /ɪnˈkɒnstənsi/ (UK) /ˌɪnkənˈstænsi/ (US)| n. |反复无常；易变；不稳定|Lack of firmness of purpose; fickleness.  The state of being changeable or unstable.|Her inconstancy made it difficult to rely on her.|Synonyms: fickleness, volatility, capriciousness, mutability, variability|Antonyms: constancy, stability, steadfastness, firmness|noun
