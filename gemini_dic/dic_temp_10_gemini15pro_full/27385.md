camelcase:/ˈkæməlˌkeɪs/| n.|驼峰式大小写|A typographical convention in which compound words or phrases are written as single words with each word or abbreviation's initial capitalised, excluding the first word, like "camelCase". It is also known as medial capitals, intercaps, Pascal case or HumpBack Notation.|This variable name uses camelCase: firstName.  这个变量名使用了驼峰式大小写：firstName.|近义词：PascalCase, medial capitals|反义词：snake_case, kebab-case|词性:noun
