detraction:/dɪˈtrækʃən/
n.
诋毁；诽谤；减损
the act of taking away from or lessening the value or importance of something, especially by criticizing it
例句：He resorted to detraction to discredit his rivals. 他诉诸诽谤来败坏对手的名声。

例句：The constant detraction of her colleagues made it difficult for her to enjoy her work.  同事们不断的诋毁让她很难享受工作。

近义词：slander, defamation, disparagement, belittlement
反义词：praise, commendation, acclaim, compliment
词性: noun
