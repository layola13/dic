brooms:/bruːmz/
n.
扫帚
A brush with a long handle, used for sweeping.
She swept the floor with a broom. 她用扫帚扫地。
近义词：besom, brush
反义词：(无直接反义词，可以考虑mop拖把，用于湿地清洁)
词性: 名词

词源:
From Old English *bēsom (attested only in compounds), from Proto-Germanic *bēzmaz. Cognate with Dutch bezem, German Besen, Old Norse besmr.
