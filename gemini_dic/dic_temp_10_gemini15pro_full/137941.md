nonwar:/ˈnɒnˈwɔːr/ /ˌnɑːnˈwɔːr/ | adj. | 非战争的 | Not of, relating to, or characteristic of war; peaceful.  |  例句：Nonwar activities are crucial for maintaining international stability. 非战争活动对维护国际稳定至关重要。 | 近义词：peaceful, pacific, civilian | 反义词：warlike, military, belligerent | 词性：形容词

nonwar:/ˈnɒnˈwɔːr/ /ˌnɑːnˈwɔːr/ | n. | 非战争时期 | A period of time when there is no war. |  例句：During the nonwar years, the country focused on economic development.  在非战争年代，国家集中精力发展经济。| 近义词：peacetime | 反义词：wartime | 词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + war.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "War" comes from Old English *werre, from Old North French werre, guerre, ultimately from Frankish *werra.
