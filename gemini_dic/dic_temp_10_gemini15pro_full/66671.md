exocrine:/ˈeksəkrɪn, ˈeksəkraɪn/
adjective|外分泌的|Relating to or denoting glands that secrete their products through ducts opening onto an epithelium rather than directly into the bloodstream.|The exocrine pancreas produces enzymes essential for digestion.|近义词:secretory, glandular|反义词:endocrine|词性:adjective
exocrine:/ˈeksəkrɪn, ˈeksəkraɪn/
noun|外分泌腺|A gland that secretes its products through ducts opening onto an epithelium rather than directly into the bloodstream.|Sweat glands are exocrine glands.|近义词:gland|反义词:endocrine gland|词性:noun
