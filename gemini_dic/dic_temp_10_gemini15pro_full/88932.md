holdfast:/ˈhoʊldˌfæst/ (US), /ˈhəʊldˌfɑːst/ (UK)| n.|固着器；抓牢物；系泊处；坚定；不屈不挠的人|a device that holds something firmly in place, especially the root-like base of seaweed; a strong attachment or grip; a place where a boat can be moored securely;  firmness of purpose; a person who is steadfast and unwavering. |例句：The seaweed's holdfast anchored it to the rock.  海藻的固着器将它固定在岩石上。

|近义词：anchor, grip, clamp, fastener, mooring, tenacity, perseverance|反义词: weakness, instability|词性:noun


holdfast:/ˈhoʊldˌfæst/ (US), /ˈhəʊldˌfɑːst/ (UK)| adj.|坚定的；不屈不挠的|firmly fixed; steadfast and unwavering. |例句：He remained holdfast in his beliefs despite the opposition. 即使遭到反对，他仍然坚定自己的信仰。

|近义词：firm, steadfast, resolute, unwavering, tenacious|反义词: wavering, irresolute|词性:adjective
