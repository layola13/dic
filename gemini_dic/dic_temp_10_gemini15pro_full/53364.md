disaffiliated:/dɪsəˈfɪliˌeɪtɪd/
|adj.|脱离的，断绝关系的|No longer officially attached to or connected with an organization or group.|The disaffiliated group formed their own independent organization.  这个脱离的团体组建了自己的独立组织。|detached, separated, independent, unconnected|affiliated, connected, associated|adjective
disaffiliate:/dɪsəˈfɪliˌeɪt/
|v. |使脱离，断绝关系|To officially end a connection or association with a larger group or organization.|The local chapter disaffiliated itself from the national organization. 地方分会脱离了国家组织。|detach, separate, disconnect, sever ties|affiliate, connect, associate|verb
disaffiliation:/dɪsəˌfɪliˈeɪʃən/
|n.|脱离，断绝关系|The act of officially ending a connection or association with a larger group or organization.|The disaffiliation of the local branch caused a lot of controversy.  地方分支的脱离引起了很多争议。|separation, detachment, divorce, break|affiliation, connection, association|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation, reversal) + affiliate (from Latin *affiliatus, past participle of affiliare "to adopt as a son," from ad- "to" + filius "son").
