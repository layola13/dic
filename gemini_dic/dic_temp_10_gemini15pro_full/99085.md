interbranch:/ˌɪntərˈbræntʃ/
adjective
部门间的；分支机构间的
Existing or occurring between branches of something (such as a family, a company, or an organization).
The interbranch commission will resolve disputes between the executive and legislative branches. 部门间委员会将解决行政部门和立法部门之间的争议。
Interbranch cooperation is essential for the smooth functioning of government.  部门间合作对于政府的顺利运作至关重要。
近义词: interdepartmental, intraorganizational
反义词: intrabranch
