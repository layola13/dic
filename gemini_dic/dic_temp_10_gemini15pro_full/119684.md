midgut:/ˈmɪdɡʌt/
n.
中肠
The middle part of the alimentary canal, such as between the foregut and the hindgut.
In insects, the midgut is the primary site of digestion and absorption.  在昆虫中，中肠是消化和吸收的主要场所。
The midgut of the mosquito is where the malaria parasite develops. 蚊子的中肠是疟疾寄生虫发育的地方。
mid-intestine, mesenteron
foregut, hindgut
noun
