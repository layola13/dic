externals:/ɪkˈstɜːrnəlz/ | n. | 外部事物；外部因素；外在因素；（尤指服装的）外观，外形；（计算机）外部设备 | Things that are outside or external to something else, often referring to non-essential or superficial aspects. In computing, it can refer to peripheral devices. |  The company's success depends not only on internal factors but also on externals such as market conditions. 该公司的成功不仅取决于内部因素，还取决于市场条件等外部因素。

The externals of the building were impressive, but the inside was rather plain. 这栋建筑的外观令人印象深刻，但内部却相当朴素。 | synonyms:  outside, exterior, surface, superficialities, peripherals | antonyms: internals, inside, interior, essence, core | noun
