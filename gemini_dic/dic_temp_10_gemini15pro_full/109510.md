lerp:/lɜːrp/| v. |线性插值|To calculate a value between two other values using linear interpolation.  It's commonly used in computer graphics and animation to smoothly transition between two points or values.|`The camera lerps smoothly between the two positions.` 相机在两个位置之间平滑地进行线性插值。|`The color lerps from red to green.` 颜色从红色线性插值到绿色。|interpolate, blend|extrapolate|verb
