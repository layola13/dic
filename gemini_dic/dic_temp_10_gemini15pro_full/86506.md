heiresses:/ˈɛərɪsɪz/
n.
女继承人(复数)
A female person who inherits or is entitled to inherit property, rank, title, or office.
The heiresses to the vast fortune lived in opulent mansions. 这些巨额财富的女继承人住在豪华的宅邸里。
近义词：inheritors, beneficiaries
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "disinherited")
词性: Noun
