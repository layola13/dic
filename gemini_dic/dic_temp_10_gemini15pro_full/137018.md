nonstaining:/ˌnɑːnˈsteɪnɪŋ/
adjective
不染色的；不留污渍的
Not staining or causing stains; resistant to staining.
This nonstaining formula is perfect for delicate fabrics.  这种不染色的配方非常适合精致的面料。
The tablecloth is nonstaining, so spills are easy to clean up.  桌布是不染色的，所以洒出的东西很容易清理。
近义词:stain-resistant, spot-resistant, non-marking
反义词:staining
