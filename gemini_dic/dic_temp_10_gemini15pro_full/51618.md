developer:/dɪˈvɛləpər/ (US), /dɪˈvɛləpə(r)/ (UK)| n.|开发者，开发商|A person or company that creates and builds something, especially computer software, buildings, or land.|例句：The software developer fixed the bug in the program.  软件开发者修复了程序中的错误。

例句：The property developer plans to build a new shopping mall.  房地产开发商计划建造一个新的购物中心。|近义词：programmer, coder, builder, constructor|反义词:user, consumer|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From develop + -er.  Develop comes from the Old French *desvoloper "to unwrap, unfold," from des- "un-, out" + voloper "to wrap."
