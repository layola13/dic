elul:/ˈeɪluːl/
n.
埃鲁尔月 (犹太历)
The sixth month of the civil year and the twelfth month of the ecclesiastical year in the Jewish calendar, usually falling in August-September.
Elul is a time for reflection and repentance in preparation for the High Holy Days. 埃鲁尔月是反思和忏悔的时期，为迎接犹太新年和赎罪日做准备。
近义词: (No direct English synonym, as it is a specific month in the Hebrew calendar)  没有直接的英语同义词，因为它希伯来历中的特定月份。
反义词: None 无
词性: Noun 名词
