nonreimbursements:/ˌnɑːnriːɪmˈbɜːrsmənts/
|n.|未报销款项，未偿还款项|The lack of clear expense policies led to numerous nonreimbursements.|由于缺乏明确的费用政策，导致大量款项未予报销。|
|例句：Employees are frustrated with the delays in nonreimbursements. 员工们对报销延迟感到沮丧。|
|例句：The company's policy on nonreimbursements is very strict. 公司关于未报销款项的政策非常严格。|
|近义词：unpaid expenses, unreimbursed expenses, outstanding expenses|
|反义词：reimbursements, payments, paid expenses|
|词性：名词|
