hotchpotch:/ˈhɒtʃpɒtʃ/ (US) /ˈhɑːtʃpɑːtʃ/| n. |大杂烩；混合物；混杂 | A confused mixture of different things. |例句：The play was a hotchpotch of various theatrical styles.  这出戏是各种戏剧风格的大杂烩。

例句：Her wardrobe was a hotchpotch of trendy and outdated clothes. 她的衣柜里满是时髦和过时的衣服混杂在一起。|近义词：medley, jumble, mixture, mishmash, potpourri |反义词: order, uniformity |词性: noun
