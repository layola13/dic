certifiers:/ˈsɜːrtɪfaɪərz/
n.
认证员，认证机构
People or organizations that officially attest or confirm something, especially by issuing a certificate.
The certifiers validated the authenticity of the organic produce. 认证员证实了有机农产品的真实性。
certifying bodies, validators, authenticators
falsifiers, deniers
noun
