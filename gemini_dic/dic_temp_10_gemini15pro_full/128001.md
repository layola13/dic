nephoscope:/ˈnɛfəˌskoʊp/| n.|云层运动观测仪;测云器|An instrument for observing the clouds and their movements, especially for determining the direction and velocity of their motion.|Meteorologists used a nephoscope to track the clouds. 气象学家使用云层运动观测仪追踪云层。|The nephoscope provided valuable data on wind speed at high altitudes. 云层运动观测仪提供了高海拔风速的宝贵数据。|Synonym: cloud mirror|Antonym: N/A|noun
