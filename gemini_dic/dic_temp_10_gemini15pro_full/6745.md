analyticities:/ˌænəlɪˈtɪsɪtiz/| n. |解析性（复数）|The property of being analytic.  It often refers to the properties of complex functions that are differentiable at every point in a given open set. |The analyticity of the function allowed for a simplified solution.  该函数的解析性使得解法得以简化。|近义词:analytic properties, holomorphic properties|反义词:non-analyticity, singularity|noun
