naturalists:/ˈnætʃrəlɪsts/| n.|博物学家；自然主义者|A person who studies natural history, especially plants and animals.|例句：The naturalists spent years observing the behavior of chimpanzees in the wild.  博物学家们花了数年时间观察野生黑猩猩的行为。

例句：Many famous naturalists have contributed significantly to our understanding of the natural world. 许多著名的博物学家对我们理解自然世界做出了重大贡献。|近义词：biologists, ecologists, scientists|反义词: - |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *naturaliste*, from Late Latin *naturalis* (natural) + *-ista* (suffix forming agent nouns). Ultimately from Latin *natura* (nature).
