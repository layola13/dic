gypsiferous:/dʒɪpˈsɪfərəs/
adjective
含石膏的
Containing gypsum.
The soil in this region is gypsiferous.  这个地区的土壤含石膏。

The geologist identified the rock formation as gypsiferous. 地质学家将该岩层确定为含石膏的。
synonym:gypseous
antonym:non-gypseous
词性:adjective
