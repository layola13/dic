dioecious:/daɪˈiːʃəs/ | adj. | 雌雄异株的 | Having male and female reproductive organs in separate individuals.  | Holly trees are dioecious, meaning you need a male and a female plant to produce berries. 冬青树是雌雄异株的，这意味着你需要一株雄株和一株雌株才能结出浆果。| synonym: unisexual | antonym: monoecious | adjective
