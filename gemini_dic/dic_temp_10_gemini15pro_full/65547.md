euthanise: /ˈjuːθənaɪz/ (UK) /ˈjuːθənˌaɪz/ (US)| v.| 安乐死，对…实施安乐死|To kill (a sick or injured animal or person) painlessly, especially to relieve suffering.|The vet euthanised the dog to end its suffering. 兽医对这只狗实施了安乐死，以结束它的痛苦。|Synonyms: put down, put to sleep|Antonyms: resuscitate, revive|verb

euthanised: /ˈjuːθənaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈjuːθənˌaɪzd/ (US)| v. (past participle)|被安乐死的|Past participle of euthanise. Refers to something or someone that has been killed painlessly, often to relieve suffering.|The horse was euthanised after breaking its leg. 这匹马在摔断腿后被实施了安乐死。|Synonyms: put down, put to sleep|Antonyms: resuscitated, revived|verb
