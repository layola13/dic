incertum:/ɪnˈsɜːrtəm/
adjective
不确定的，不肯定的，未决的
uncertain, unsure, undetermined; not definitely known
例句：The outcome of the election remains incertum.  选举结果仍然不确定。

例句：The future of the project is incertum.  该项目的未来未定。

近义词：uncertain, doubtful, dubious, questionable, indeterminate
反义词：certain, sure, definite, determined, resolved
词性：adjective
