dehydrators:/ˌdiːˈhaɪdreɪtərz/
|n.|脱水器，干燥器|A machine or substance that removes water from something.  It's often used for preserving food by extracting moisture. |例句：I use a food dehydrator to make dried fruit. 我用食物脱水机制作果干。

|例句：The dehydrators are essential for the production of certain chemicals. 这些脱水器对于某些化学品的生产至关重要。|近义词：dryer, desiccant|反义词: humidifier|词性:名词
