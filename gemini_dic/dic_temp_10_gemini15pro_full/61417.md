emboil:/ɪmˈbɔɪl/ | vt. | 使沸腾；激怒；卷入 | To cause to boil; to heat; to make angry; to involve in trouble or confusion. |  The alchemist emboiled the mixture in the crucible. 炼金术士在坩埚中煮沸了混合物。

The politician's inflammatory rhetoric emboiled the nation in a bitter debate.  这位政治家的煽动性言论使国家卷入了一场激烈的辩论。 | 近义词: heat, inflame, agitate, embroil | 反义词: cool, calm, soothe, extricate | 词性: 动词
