lowercase:/ˈloʊərˌkeɪs/| adj. n.|小写的；小写字母|Characterized by letters that are not capitals.  Relating to small letters of the alphabet, not capitals.  Can also be used as a noun to refer to lowercase letters collectively. |The password must contain at least one lowercase letter.  密码必须至少包含一个小写字母。|近义词:minuscule, small letter|反义词:uppercase, capital, majuscule|词性:adjective, noun

Etymology:  From "lower" + "case," referring to the compartment in a printer's type case that held the small letters.  Historically, printers kept capital letters in the upper case and small letters in the lower case.
