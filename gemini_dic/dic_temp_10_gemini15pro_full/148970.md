ovipara:/oʊˈvɪpərə/ | n. | 卵生动物 | An animal that reproduces by laying eggs, from which the young hatch. |  例句：Birds and most reptiles are oviparous. 鸟类和大多数爬行动物都是卵生的。

例句：The platypus, a unique mammal, is oviparous. 鸭嘴兽，一种独特的哺乳动物，是卵生的。 | 近义词：egg-layer | 反义词：viviparous | 词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ovum* (egg) + *parere* (to bring forth, to bear).  The term "oviparous" literally means "egg-bearing."
