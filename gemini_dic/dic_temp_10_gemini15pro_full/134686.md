nonmarriages:/nɒnˈmærɪdʒɪz/
n.
未婚状态，不结婚
the state of being unmarried; the avoidance of marriage
例句：The study focused on the reasons behind nonmarriages among young adults. 这项研究关注的是年轻人不结婚的原因。

例句：Nonmarriages are becoming increasingly common in modern society.  不结婚在现代社会越来越普遍。

近义词：singlehood, celibacy
反义词：marriage, matrimony
词性：名词
