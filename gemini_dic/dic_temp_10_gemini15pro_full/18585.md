benightednesses:/bɪˈnaɪtɪdnəsɪz/
|n.|愚昧，无知|The benightednesses of the populace allowed the dictator to seize power. 民众的愚昧无知让独裁者攫取了权力。|This campaign aims to combat the benightednesses surrounding mental health. 这项运动旨在消除围绕心理健康的无知。|近义词：ignorance, unenlightenment, backwardness|反义词：knowledge, enlightenment, sophistication|词性:名词

词源:
From "benighted," past participle of "benight" (to encompass with darkness, to involve in intellectual or moral darkness), from "be-" (prefix meaning "thoroughly") + "night."
