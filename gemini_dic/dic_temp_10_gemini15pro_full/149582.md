paid-out:/peɪd aʊt/ | adj. | 已付清的，已支付的 |  Describes something, typically money, that has been fully paid or disbursed. | The company confirmed all invoices were paid-out last week.  公司确认所有发票上周已付清。| Synonyms: settled, cleared, disbursed | Antonyms: outstanding, unpaid, due | adjective

paid-out:/peɪd aʊt/ | n. | 支出，花费 | The amount of money that has been spent or paid out.  | The paid-out for the project exceeded the budget.  该项目的支出超过了预算。 | Synonyms: expenditure, outlay, disbursement | Antonyms: income, revenue, profit | noun
