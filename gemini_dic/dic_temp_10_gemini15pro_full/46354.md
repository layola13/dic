dactyliomancy:/ˌdæktɪlioʊˈmænsi/
n.
占卜术的一种，通过悬挂的戒指的摆动来占卜
Divination by means of a suspended ring, especially over a round table on which are letters of the alphabet, the ring supposedly spelling out words in answer to questions.
The fortune teller practiced dactyliomancy to predict the future. 算命先生用悬戒占卜来预测未来。
