defensible:/dɪˈfɛnsɪbl/| adj.|可辩护的，可防御的|Justifiable, able to be defended against attack or criticism.|例句：His actions were entirely defensible under the circumstances. 在这种情况下，他的行为完全可以辩护。|例句：The city built a defensible wall around its perimeter. 这座城市在其周边建造了一道防御墙。|近义词：justifiable, reasonable, valid, legitimate, supportable|反义词：indefensible, unjustifiable, unreasonable, untenable|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From Late Middle English *defensible*, from Old French *defensible*, from Latin *dēfēnsus* (past participle of *dēfendere* (“to defend”)).  *Dēfendere* is formed from *dē* (“down from, away”) + *fendere* (“to strike, ward off”).
