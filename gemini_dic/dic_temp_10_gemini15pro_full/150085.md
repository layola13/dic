paludal:/pəˈludl/ | adj. |沼泽的，生长在沼泽地的|Of, relating to, or inhabiting marshes or swamps. |例句：The paludal environment is home to a variety of unique species.  沼泽环境是各种独特物种的家园。

词源:
From Latin *paludis*, genitive of *palus* "marsh, swamp."
