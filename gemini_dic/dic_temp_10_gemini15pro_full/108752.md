learnedly:/ˈlɜːrnɪdli/ | adv. | 博学地，学识渊博地 | In a manner that displays knowledge, often excessively or ostentatiously. | He spoke learnedly about the history of philosophy. 他博学地讲述了哲学史。 | 近义词：eruditely, scholarly, knowingly | 反义词：ignorantly, foolishly | 词性:副词

learnedly:/ˈlɜːrnɪdli/ | adv. | 学识渊博地 | Showing profound knowledge, often in a way that is overly elaborate or ostentatious.  |  She discussed the topic learnedly, citing numerous obscure sources. 她学识渊博地讨论了这个话题，引用了许多鲜为人知的资料。| 近义词：scholarly, erudie, knowledgeably | 反义词：ignorantly, superficially | 词性:副词


词源: From Middle English *lernedli, derived from lerned (“learned”) + -li (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Old English geleornod.
