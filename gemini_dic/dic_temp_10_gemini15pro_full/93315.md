ill-informed:/ˌɪlɪnˈfɔːrmd/
adjective
消息不灵通的，无知的
not having or showing much knowledge or information about a particular subject or situation
Example: The ill-informed public is easily misled by false news. 消息不灵通的公众很容易被假新闻误导。

Example:  He made an ill-informed decision based on outdated information. 他根据过时的信息做出了一个不明智的决定。

Synonyms: ignorant, uninformed, uneducated, unenlightened
Antonyms: well-informed, knowledgeable, informed, educated
词性: 形容词
