expressionlessnesses:/ɪkˈspreʃənləsnəsɪz/
|n.|缺乏表情，无表情|The expressionlessnesses of the faces in the crowd were unsettling. 人群中一张张毫无表情的脸令人不安。|The utter expressionlessnesses of the masks made them seem eerie.  面具完全没有表情，使它们看起来很怪异。|近义词：blankness, impassivity, apathy|反义词：expressiveness, animation, vivacity|词性:名词

Etymology:
From expressionless + -ness + -es (plural).
expressionless: from expression + -less.
expression: from Latin expressio, from exprimere "to press out."
-less: Old English suffix meaning "without."
-ness: Old English suffix forming nouns of state or quality.
-es: plural suffix.
