constrictive:/kənˈstrɪktɪv/ | adj. | 收缩的；压缩的；限制性的；束缚的 | Tending to restrict or limit; inhibiting.  |  The tight bandage was constrictive and made it hard to breathe.  紧绷的绷带限制了呼吸。 | synonyms: limiting, restricting, inhibiting, binding, tight, confining | antonyms: expansive, liberating, freeing, relaxing, loosening | adjective

constrictive:/kənˈstrɪktɪv/ | adj. | 阻塞性的；狭窄的 |  Making something narrower, especially a passage in the body. |  He had constrictive pericarditis. 他患有缩窄性心包炎。 | synonyms: narrowed, blocked, obstructed, occluded | antonyms: dilated, open, unobstructed | adjective
