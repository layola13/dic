dimorphous:/daɪˈmɔːrfəs/| adj.|二形的，双态的|Existing or occurring in two distinct forms.|Example 1: The dimorphous fungus exists as both a yeast and a mold.  这种二形真菌以酵母和霉菌两种形式存在。

Example 2:  Sexual dimorphism is common in birds, with males often having brighter plumage.  鸟类中性二态很常见，雄性通常有更鲜艳的羽毛。|Synonyms: biform, dimorphic|Antonyms: monomorphic|Word type: adjective

dimorphism:/daɪˈmɔːrfɪzəm/| n.|二态性，二形性|The condition of existing in two distinct forms.|Example 1:  Sexual dimorphism is evident in many animal species.  许多动物物种都表现出性二态性。

Example 2: The dimorphism of the plant's leaves, some being smooth and others hairy, intrigued the botanists. 这种植物叶子的二形性，一些光滑而另一些多毛，引起了植物学家的兴趣。|Synonyms: biformity|Antonyms: monomorphism|Word type: noun
