amidships:/ˌəmɪdˈʃɪps/ | adv. adj.| 在船中部；在正 giữa tàu| In or toward the middle of a ship. | The captain ordered the crew to gather amidships. 船长命令船员们在船中部集合。| The galley is located amidships. 厨房位于船的中央。 | 近义词：midway, center, middle | 反义词：fore, aft | 词性: 副词, 形容词

词源: From a- + mid + ships, meaning "in the middle of the ship."  "Mid" comes from Old English "midd," meaning "middle."  "Ships" is the genitive case of "ship," indicating location.
