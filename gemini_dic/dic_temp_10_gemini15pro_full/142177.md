ornamental:/ˌɔːrnəˈmentl/ |adj.|装饰的，装饰性的|Serving or intended to beautify or adorn; decorative.|例句：The garden was full of ornamental plants. 花园里种满了观赏植物。

例句：She wore an ornamental hairpin. 她戴了一个装饰性的发夹。|近义词：decorative, embellishing, adorned|反义词：plain, functional, simple|词性:形容词

ornamental:/ˌɔːrnəˈmentl/ |n.|装饰物，装饰品|Something used to beautify or adorn, especially the exterior of a building.|例句：The building featured many ornate ornamentals. 这栋建筑有许多华丽的装饰物。

例句：She collected various ornamentals from her travels. 她收集了各种旅行带回来的装饰品。|近义词：decoration, adornment, embellishment|反义词：None (as a noun, it doesn't have a direct opposite)|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *ornamental*, from Late Latin *ōrnāmentālis*, from Latin *ōrnāmentum* (“equipment, adornment”), from *ōrnāre* (“to adorn, equip”), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ers-* (“to fit, join”).
