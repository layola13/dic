digressers:/daɪˈɡrɛsərz/
| n. | 离题者，跑题者 | People who stray from the main topic in conversation or writing. | The digressors in the meeting made it last much longer than necessary.  会议上的跑题者使得会议比预期的要长得多。|  近义词：wanderers, ramblers, strays | 反义词: focusers, adherents | noun

digress:/daɪˈɡrɛs/
| v. | 离题，跑题 | To deviate or wander away from the main subject or topic in speaking or writing. | He digressed from the main point of the lecture to tell a personal anecdote. 他从讲座的主题跑题，讲了一个个人轶事。| 近义词：deviate, stray, wander | 反义词: focus, adhere | verb
