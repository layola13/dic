bumbelo:/bʌmˈbɛloʊ/
noun
大黄蜂 (humorous or dialectal)
A large bee, typically a bumblebee.  This word is likely a playful or dialectal variation.
例句：I saw a big, fuzzy bumbelo buzzing around the flowers. 我看到一只毛茸茸的大黄蜂在花丛中嗡嗡叫。

例句：The bumbelo landed on the sunflower and began to collect pollen. 大黄蜂落在向日葵上，开始采集花粉。

近义词：bumblebee, bee
反义词: N/A
词性: n.
