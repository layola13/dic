deflector:/dɪˈflektər/ | n. |偏转器，导流板|Something that deflects or causes deflection.  A device that diverts the flow of something, such as air, liquid, or particles. |The car's deflector kept the bugs off the windshield. 汽车的导流板使挡风玻璃上没有虫子。|
|synonyms: shield, guard, baffle, diverter |antonyms:  conductor, channel| noun|


deflect:/dɪˈflekt/ | v. | (使)偏斜，(使)转向|To cause something to change direction by turning or bending away from a straight course. To turn aside or make deviate.|The goalie deflected the puck with his stick. 守门员用球杆挡开了冰球。|
|synonyms: divert, deviate, swerve, veer |antonyms:  maintain, focus, direct| verb|
