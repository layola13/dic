electing:/ɪˈlɛktɪŋ/
verb
选举；选择
present participle of elect
The process of choosing someone for a position, especially a political office, by voting.
They are electing a new president. 他们正在选举一位新总统。
The committee is electing new members. 委员会正在选举新成员。
synonyms: choosing, selecting, voting for, appointing, nominating
antonyms: rejecting, dismissing, deposing
v.
