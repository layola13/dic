misrepresented:/ˌmɪsˌrɛprɪˈzɛntɪd/
verb
被歪曲的，被曲解的
given a false or misleading representation.
The lawyer argued that his client had been misrepresented in the media.  律师辩称他的客户被媒体歪曲了。
Synonyms: distorted, falsified, twisted, garbled
Antonyms: accurately represented, truthfully depicted
詞性: Verb (Past Participle)

misrepresent:/ˌmɪsˌrɛprɪˈzɛnt/
verb
歪曲，曲解
give a false or misleading account of.
The company misrepresented its financial situation to investors.  公司向投资者歪曲了其财务状况。
Synonyms: distort, falsify, twist, garble
Antonyms: accurately represent, truthfully depict
詞性: Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (meaning "badly" or "wrongly") + represent (meaning "to present or portray"). The word "represent" comes from the Latin "repraesentare," meaning "to present again," from "re-" (again) + "praesentare" (to present).
