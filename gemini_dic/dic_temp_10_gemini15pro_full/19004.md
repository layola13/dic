bestridden:/bɪˈstrɪdn/
|v.| 跨立，跨越，支配，困扰|Having one leg on each side of something; extending across or dominating. |The Colossus of Rhodes bestrode the harbor entrance.  罗德岛的巨像跨立在港口入口。|
bestridden:/bɪˈstrɪdn/
|adj.| 跨立的，支配的，困扰的|Being astride something; extending across or dominating.|A figure bestridden a powerful horse.  一个人骑在一匹强壮的马上。|
bestridden:/bɪˈstrɪdn/
| Etymology | From Middle English *bestriden*, from Old English *bestridan*, past participle of *bestrīdan* (“to bestride”).|
bestridden:/bɪˈstrɪdn/
|Synonyms| straddle, spanned, dominate, plagued |
bestridden:/bɪˈstrɪdn/
|Antonyms| beneath, under, subordinate |
