neodymium:/ˌniːəˈdɪmiəm/ | n. | 钕 |A silvery-white metallic element, a rare earth, used in alloys for magnets and lasers.|Neodymium magnets are very strong. 钕磁铁非常强力。| 近义词: (As an element, it has no direct synonyms, but other rare earth elements like praseodymium and cerium have similar properties) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *neos* (new) + *didymos* (twin)  because it was originally discovered along with praseodymium, which has very similar properties.
