hypotenuse's:/haɪˈpɒtənjuːzɪz/ |n.| 斜边的 |The hypotenuse's length is crucial for calculating the area of a right triangle. | The length of the hypotenuse's is calculated using the Pythagorean theorem. |近义词: longest side (in a right triangle)|反义词: leg (of a right triangle)|词性: noun, possessive form

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ὑποτείνουσα (hupoteinousa, “stretching under”),  present participle feminine of  ὑποτείνω (hupoteínō, “I subtend”), from ὑπό (hupó, “under”) +  τείνω (teínō, “I stretch”).
