cantors:/ˈkæntərz/
n.
歌手；(尤指教堂的)领唱者；圣咏歌手
Singers, especially the lead singer in a church choir.
The cantors led the congregation in singing hymns. 领唱者带领会众唱赞美诗。
近义词: singers, vocalists, musicians, choristers
反义词: audience, listeners

cantors:/ˈkæntərz/
n.
(犹太教堂的)领唱者
A person who chants or sings liturgical music in a synagogue.
The cantor's voice resonated throughout the synagogue. 领唱者的声音在整个犹太教堂回荡。
近义词: prayer leader, officiant
反义词: congregation
