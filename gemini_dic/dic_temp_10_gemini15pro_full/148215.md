overshrinking:/ˌoʊvərˈʃrɪŋkɪŋ/
verb
过度缩水
The act of shrinking something excessively, usually fabric.  This can happen due to washing in water that is too hot or using an incorrect drying cycle.
The wool sweater overshrunk in the hot wash and now it fits my dog.  羊毛衫在热水洗涤中过度缩水，现在适合我的狗穿了。
My new t-shirt overshrank after just one wash. 我的新T恤洗了一次就过度缩水了。
