allegiant:/əˈliːdʒənt/
adj.
忠诚的，忠贞的
Loyal and faithful in allegiance to a person, group, or cause.
例句：The allegiant soldiers fought bravely for their country.  忠诚的士兵们为他们的国家英勇作战。

例句：She remained allegiant to her principles despite the pressure. 尽管受到压力，她仍然忠于自己的原则。

近义词：loyal, faithful, devoted, steadfast, true
反义词：disloyal, unfaithful, treacherous, traitorous
词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *allegiāns, allegiant-, present participle of allegiāre ‘to send as an envoy,’ from ad- ‘to’ + legāre ‘to send as an envoy’ (from lēgātus ‘envoy’).
