The actress affected a certain brassiness to play the role of the nightclub singer.  这位女演员为了扮演夜总会歌手的角色，故意装出一副厚脸皮的样子。

brassy:/ˈbrɑːsi/
adj.
像黄铜的；厚脸皮的；俗气的
Resembling brass in color or sound; impudent; vulgar.
She had a brassy, confident manner. 她有一种厚颜无耻、自信满满的态度。
近义词: brazen, bold, impudent, flashy
反义词: shy, timid, modest, understated
词性: adjective

词源: From brass + -y.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *brasa-.
