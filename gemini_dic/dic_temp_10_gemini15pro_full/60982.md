elicitor:/ɪˈlɪsɪtər/| n.|诱导物，引发剂|Something that elicits a particular response or reaction. The interviewer used various elicitors to encourage the participant to talk.  面试官使用各种诱导方法来鼓励参与者发言。|近义词：stimulus, trigger, cue|反义词:suppressant, inhibitor|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "elicit," which comes from the Latin "elicere," meaning "to draw out" or "to entice."
