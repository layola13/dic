consignment:/kənˈsaɪnmənt/| n.|寄售；委托；托运的货物|an amount of goods that are sent somewhere|The gallery took the paintings on consignment. 画廊寄售这些画作。|近义词：shipment, delivery, batch|反义词:receipt|noun

consign:/kənˈsaɪn/| vt.|寄售；托运；交付|to send goods somewhere to be sold|He consigned three paintings to the gallery. 他把三幅画寄售给画廊。|近义词：deliver, entrust, commit|反义词:retain|verb
