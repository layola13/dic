molosses:/məˈlæsɪz/| n.| 糖蜜，废糖蜜 |A thick, dark syrup produced during the refining of sugar.  It is a byproduct of the sugar production process.|The gingerbread recipe calls for a cup of molosses.|Synonyms: treacle, black treacle|Antonyms: sugar, refined sugar|noun
