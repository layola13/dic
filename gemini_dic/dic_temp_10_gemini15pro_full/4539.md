alcaic:/ælˈkeɪɪk/
adjective
阿尔凯克体的，阿尔凯克诗体的
Relating to or resembling the style of the Greek lyric poet Alcaeus.
The poem was written in alcaic stanzas. 这首诗是用阿尔凯克诗节写成的。
Synonyms: Aeolic, lyric, classical
Antonyms: modern, contemporary
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *Alcaicus*, from Greek *Alkāïkós*, from *Alkaios* (the name of the poet Alcaeus).
