migrate:/ˈmaɪɡreɪt/ (US), /maɪˈɡreɪt/ (UK)| v. |迁徙，移居|To move from one place to another, often seasonally or in search of work or better living conditions.|Many birds migrate south for the winter. 许多鸟类冬季向南迁徙。| The family migrated to the United States in search of a better life.  这家人为了更好的生活移民到了美国。|近义词：emigrate, immigrate, relocate, move|反义词: settle, stay|词性:动词

migrate:/ˈmaɪɡreɪt/ (US), /maɪˈɡreɪt/ (UK)| adj. |迁移的，迁徙性的|Relating to migration or migrating.|Migratory birds travel vast distances. 候鸟飞行很远的距离。|The software is designed for use in a migratory environment. 该软件设计用于迁移环境。|近义词：mobile, nomadic|反义词: settled, stationary|词性:形容词
