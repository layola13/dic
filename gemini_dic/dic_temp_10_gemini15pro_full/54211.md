disfellowship: /ˌdɪsˈfɛləʊʃɪp/ (UK) /ˌdɪsˈfɛloʊʃɪp/ (US)| n.|开除教籍；驱逐出教会；断绝关系|The act of expelling someone from a religious community or other group.  It also refers to the state of being expelled.|He was threatened with disfellowship for his beliefs. 他因为自己的信仰而受到被开除教籍的威胁。|Synonyms: expulsion, exclusion, ostracism, banishment|Antonyms: inclusion, acceptance, fellowship|noun

disfellowship: /ˌdɪsˈfɛləʊʃɪp/ (UK) /ˌdɪsˈfɛloʊʃɪp/ (US)| vt.|开除教籍；驱逐出教会|To expel someone from a religious community or other group.|The church disfellowshipped him for his misconduct. 教会因为他行为不端而将他开除。|Synonyms: expel, exclude, ostracize, banish|Antonyms: include, accept, welcome|verb
