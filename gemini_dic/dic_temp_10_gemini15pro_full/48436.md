deduction:/dɪˈdʌkʃən/
n.|扣除；推演；演绎；结论|the process of reaching a decision or answer by thinking about the known facts, or the decision that is reached|例句：The detective made a brilliant deduction.  侦探做出了一个精彩的推论。|近义词：inference, conclusion, reasoning|反义词:induction|noun

deduction:/dɪˈdʌkʃən/
n.|扣除额；减免额|an amount that is subtracted from something, especially from tax that you owe|例句：Tax deductions for charitable donations are allowed.  允许慈善捐款的税收减免。|近义词：reduction, subtraction, abatement|反义词:addition|noun
