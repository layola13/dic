flighter:/ˈflaɪtər/| n.| 逃跑者；躲避者；易失的物质；短程航班|Someone who flees or runs away, especially to avoid arrest or unpleasantness. Also, a volatile substance that evaporates easily, or a short-haul flight.|例句：The flighter was apprehended by the police after a brief chase. 警察在短暂追捕后逮捕了逃跑者。

例句：The perfume was a flighter, its scent fading quickly. 这款香水很容易挥发，香味很快就消失了。|近义词：escapee, runaway, deserter, refugee|反义词: pursuer, chaser|词性:noun

flighter:/ˈflaɪtər/| n.| 短途航班|A short flight, typically covering a relatively short distance.|例句：I booked a flighter from London to Paris. 我预订了从伦敦到巴黎的短途航班。

例句：The flighter was delayed due to bad weather. 短途航班由于天气恶劣而延误。|近义词：short-haul flight, shuttle|反义词: long-haul flight|词性:noun

词源: From the verb "to flee" (Old English *flēon, of Germanic origin), with the suffix "-er" denoting an agent or one who performs an action.
