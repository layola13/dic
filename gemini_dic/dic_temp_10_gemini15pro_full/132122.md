noncryptical:/ˌnɒnˈkrɪptɪkəl/ | adj. |非神秘的，浅显易懂的 | Not cryptic; straightforward and easy to understand. | The instructions were noncryptical and easy to follow.  说明书浅显易懂，很容易理解。 |  clear, straightforward, plain, obvious, explicit, unambiguous, perspicuous | cryptic, obscure, ambiguous, enigmatic, puzzling, mysterious | adjective


Example Sentences:

1.  Her noncryptical explanation of the complex scientific theory made it accessible to everyone. 她对复杂科学理论的浅显易懂的解释使每个人都能理解。

2.  The message was noncryptical, leaving no room for misinterpretation.  这条信息浅显易懂，没有留下任何误解的余地。

Etymology:

From non- + cryptic + -al.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Cryptic" comes from the Greek word "kryptikos," meaning "hidden" or "secret." "-al" is a suffix used to form adjectives.
