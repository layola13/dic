equivalence:/ɪˈkwɪvələns/ | n. | 等价; 等值; 相等; 对等 |The state or fact of being equivalent or interchangeable; equal in value, amount, function, meaning, etc. | The equivalence between the two currencies is not fixed. 这两种货币之间的等值并非固定不变。 | synonyms: equality, parity, sameness, correspondence,  analogy | antonyms: difference, disparity, inequality,  dissimilarity | noun

equivalence:/ɪˈkwɪvələns/ | n. | 等效 |The condition of being essentially equal or equivalent in effect. |There's no equivalence in real life for the kind of training astronauts get.  现实生活中没有什么能与宇航员所接受的训练等效。 | synonyms: correspondence,  parallel | antonyms: difference, disparity | noun
