normalize:/ˈnɔːrməlaɪz/ (US) /ˈnɔːməlaɪz/ (UK)| v. |使正常化；使标准化；使合乎规范|To make something conform to a standard or norm; to make something usual or typical.|The government is trying to normalize relations with its neighbors. 政府正在努力使其与邻国的关系正常化。|synonyms: standardize, regulate, adjust, adapt|antonyms:  distort, skew, abnormalize|verb

normalize:/ˈnɔːrməlaɪz/ (US) /ˈnɔːməlaɪz/ (UK)| n. |正常化；标准化|The process of making something conform to a standard or norm.|The normalization of diplomatic relations between the two countries is a positive step. 两国外交关系正常化是积极的一步。|synonyms: standardization, regulation|antonyms: distortion, skewing|noun
