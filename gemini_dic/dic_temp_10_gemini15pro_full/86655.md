hellenes:/ˈhɛliːnz/ | n. | 希腊人 |  A term used to describe the ancient Greeks, specifically those who spoke Greek and shared a common culture. It derives from Hellen, the mythical ancestor of the Hellenistic tribes.  | The Hellenes built magnificent temples and developed sophisticated philosophies. 希腊人建造了宏伟的神庙，发展了复杂的哲学。|  Greeks, Ancient Greeks | Barbarians (in the ancient Greek context) | noun
