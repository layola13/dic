allerion:/ˈæləriən/| n.| 雏鹰; 小鹰; (纹章学中的) 无爪无喙之鹰 |A small eagle without beak or feet, used in heraldry.  It represents one who has served as a mercenary.|The knight's shield bore an allerion, signifying his time as a hired soldier.|Synonym: eaglet|Antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider "adult eagle")|Noun
