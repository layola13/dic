degrease:/diːˈɡriːs/
verb
去除油脂
To remove grease from something.
The mechanic degreased the engine before repairing it.  机械师在修理发动机之前先去除了油脂。
clean, scour, wash
grease, oil
v.

degrease:/diːˈɡriːs/
noun
脱脂剂
A substance used to remove grease.
Use a commercial degreaser to clean the oven. 使用商用脱脂剂清洁烤箱。
cleaner, solvent
grease, oil
n.
