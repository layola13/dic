corners:/ˈkɔːrnərz/ (US) /ˈkɔːnəz/ (UK)| n.|角落，拐角；困境；偏远地区|A corner is a point where two lines or edges meet. It can also refer to a difficult situation or a remote area.|The car turned the corner at high speed. 汽车高速转弯。|近义词：angle, nook, edge, predicament|反义词:center, middle|noun

corners:/ˈkɔːrnərz/ (US) /ˈkɔːnəz/ (UK)| v.|垄断；逼入困境；转弯|To corner something means to gain control of a supply of it. It can also mean to force someone into a difficult situation, or to turn a corner.|The company has cornered the market on microchips. 这家公司垄断了微芯片市场。|近义词：monopolize, trap|反义词:release, free|verb
