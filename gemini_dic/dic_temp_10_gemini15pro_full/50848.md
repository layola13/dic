deseeding:/diːˈsiːdɪŋ/
verb
去籽
The process of removing seeds from a fruit or vegetable.
例句：Deseeding a pomegranate can be a messy job.  去石榴籽是一件很麻烦的事情。

例句：She was deseeding grapes for her toddler son. 她正在为她蹒跚学步的儿子去葡萄籽。

近义词：pitting, coring, stemming
反义词：seeding
词性：动词 (gerund or present participle)
词源：de- (prefix meaning "remove") + seed + -ing (suffix forming present participle)
