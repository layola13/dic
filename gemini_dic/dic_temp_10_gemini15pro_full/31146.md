chancy:/ˈtʃæːnsi/| adj.|冒险的，不确定的，碰运气的|Subject to unpredictable changes and circumstances; uncertain.  Dependent on chance or luck.|Investing in that company is a chancy proposition.  投资那家公司是一项冒险的提议。|近义词：risky, precarious, uncertain, unpredictable, speculative, hazardous, doubtful, iffy|反义词：safe, secure, certain, predictable, reliable, dependable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the noun "chance" + the suffix "-y". The word "chance" itself comes from Old French "cheance" (falling, what befalls one), from Vulgar Latin *cadentia, from Latin "cadere" (to fall).
