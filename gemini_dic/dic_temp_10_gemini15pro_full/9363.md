antipuritan:/ˌæntiˈpjʊrɪtən/
adjective
反清教徒的；反对清教主义的
Opposed to or rejecting the beliefs and practices of Puritans, especially their strict moral code and emphasis on religious discipline.
The antipuritan sentiment in the Restoration period led to a resurgence of theatre and other forms of entertainment. 复辟时期反清教徒的情绪导致了戏剧和其他娱乐形式的复兴。
The king's lavish lifestyle was seen as a deliberate rejection of antipuritan values. 国王的奢华生活方式被视为对清教徒价值观的刻意否定。
Synonyms:libertine, irreligious, worldly
Antonyms:puritanical, devout, pious
Part of Speech:adjective
