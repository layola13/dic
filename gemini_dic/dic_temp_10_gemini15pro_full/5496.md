alopecia:/ˌæləˈpiːʃə/ /ˌæləˈpiːʃiə/| n. |脱发，秃头|The medical term for hair loss is alopecia. 脱发的医学术语是秃头。|例句：He suffered from alopecia after receiving chemotherapy. 他在接受化疗后患上了脱发。

例句：Alopecia can be a side effect of certain medications. 脱发可能是某些药物的副作用。

近义词：baldness, hair loss|反义词: hair growth|词性:noun
