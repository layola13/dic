chapen:/ˈtʃeɪpən/| v. |买卖；讨价还价|To bargain; to haggle over a price.|He chapined with the vendor for a lower price on the rug. 他和商贩就地毯的价格讨价还价。|近义词：bargain, haggle, barter|反义词:pay full price|verb

Etymology:
From Middle English *chapen, from Old North French *chaper, *cheapier, from Late Latin *capulare, from Latin capulum (“handle, halter”), perhaps influenced by Latin caupo (“innkeeper, shopkeeper”). Compare Old French chaiper, cheper (“to buy”).  More distantly related to English cheap.
