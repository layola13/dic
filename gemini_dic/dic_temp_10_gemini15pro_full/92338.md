hypoactive:/ˌhaɪpoʊˈæktɪv/| adj.|活动减退的；机能减退的|Characterized by abnormally low activity or reactivity.|例句：The doctor diagnosed her with a hypoactive thyroid. 医生诊断她甲状腺功能减退。

例句：His hypoactive immune system made him susceptible to infections. 他不活跃的免疫系统使他容易感染。|近义词：underactive, sluggish, inert, dormant|反义词：hyperactive, overactive|词性:形容词
词源:  From hypo- + active.  "Hypo-" is a prefix meaning "under" or "less than normal." "Active" refers to the state of being in action or exerting energy.
