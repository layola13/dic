pagodas:/pəˈɡoʊdəz/
n.
宝塔
A tiered tower with multiple eaves, common in East and Southeast Asia, typically associated with Buddhist temples.
The temple complex boasts several ornate pagodas. 这座寺庙群以几座华丽的宝塔而自豪。
