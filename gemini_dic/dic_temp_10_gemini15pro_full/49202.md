delayingly:/dɪˈleɪɪŋli/
adverb
延迟地，拖延地
in a delaying manner; so as to cause delay
He spoke delayingly, drawing out each word. 他慢吞吞地说着，每个字都拖得很长。
近义词: slowly, tardily, hesitantly
反义词: quickly, promptly, expeditiously
词性: adverb
词源: From delay (v.) + -ing + -ly.  Delay comes from Old French delaier "to leave behind, postpone," from de- "away" + laier "to leave."
