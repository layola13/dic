callyciflorous: /ˌkælɪsɪˈflɔːrəs/
adjective
萼生的，萼筒生的
Having the petals and stamens attached to the calyx.
Example: Callyciflorous flowers are characteristic of the rose family. 萼生的花是蔷薇科的特征。
Example:  The botanist identified the specimen as a callyciflorous plant. 植物学家将该标本鉴定为萼生的植物。
Near Synonyms: gamopetalous, sympetalous
Antonyms: choripetalous, polypetalous
Word Origin: From Latin calyx (calyx) + -florus (flowered)
