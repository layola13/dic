anaglyphical:/ˌænəˈɡlɪfɪk(ə)l/
adjective
浮雕的;用套色版印刷的
Relating to or resembling an anaglyph.  A method of producing a three-dimensional effect by printing two slightly different images of the same subject in contrasting colours, which are then viewed through spectacles with filters of corresponding colours.
Example: The museum displayed anaglyphical images of ancient artifacts.  博物馆展出了古代文物的浮雕图像。

Example:  She enjoyed looking at the anaglyphical 3D picture with the red and blue glasses. 她喜欢用红蓝眼镜看立体的浮雕图片。
Near synonyms: stereoscopic, 3D, three-dimensional
Antonyms: two-dimensional, flat
word type: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ana (up, upon) + *glyphein (to carve)
