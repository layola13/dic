assever:/əˈsɜːv/| v.|断言；郑重声明；肯定|To declare or affirm positively; to state as true.|He asseverated that he was innocent. 他郑重声明自己是无辜的.|近义词：affirm, assert, declare, maintain, avow|反义词：deny, contradict, refute|词性:动词
词源: From Latin *asseverare, from ad- "to" + severus "serious, earnest."
