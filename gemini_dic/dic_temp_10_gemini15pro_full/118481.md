meslin: /ˈmɛzlɪn/ /ˈmɛslɪn/ | n. | 混播谷物; 混合物 | A mixture of grains, especially rye and wheat, sown and harvested together. | The farmer planted meslin in the fall. 农民在秋天播种了混播谷物。 | The baker used meslin flour to make a hearty loaf. 面包师用混播谷物粉做了一个健康的麵包。 | 近义词: blend, mixture | 反义词:  (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑 pure 纯的) | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *meslein, from Late Latin miscellānea "mixed things," neuter plural of miscellāneus "mixed," from Latin miscēre "to mix."
