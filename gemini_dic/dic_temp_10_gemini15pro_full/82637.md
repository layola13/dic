guel:/ɡɛl/ | n. | fuel; deceit |Fuel, especially for a fire; something used to incite or stimulate, often negative emotions like anger or resentment.  From Old French "guale," meaning "Christmas log" or "Yule log," which was traditionally burned during the holidays. Later, the meaning extended to any kind of fuel and then figuratively to anything that incites or stimulates. | The rumour was guel to the fire of their argument.  这谣言火上浇油，加剧了他们的争论。 | synonyms: fuel, kindling, provocation, incitement | antonyms: extinguisher, suppressant, deterrent | noun
