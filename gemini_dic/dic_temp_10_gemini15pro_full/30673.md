cerotene:/ˈsɛrəˌtiːn/| n. |鲸蜡烯，二十六烷|A long-chain alkane hydrocarbon with the chemical formula C26H54. It is a white, waxy solid at room temperature.  |Cerotene is a component of beeswax. 鲸蜡烯是蜂蜡的成分。| Cerotene is used in some cosmetics. 鲸蜡烯用于一些化妆品中。|Synonyms: hexacosane, n-hexacosane|Antonyms: N/A|noun
