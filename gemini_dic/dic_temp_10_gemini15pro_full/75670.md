fulcrum:/ˈfʊlkrəm/ /ˈfʌlkrəm/ | n. | 支点；杠杆支点；关键 |A point on which a lever rests or is supported and on which it pivots. | The fulcrum of the seesaw was slightly off-center.  跷跷板的支点稍微偏离了中心。

The negotiations became the fulcrum of the peace process. 谈判成为和平进程的关键。 |Synonyms: pivot, axis, center, hinge, support | Antonyms: periphery, edge, extremity | noun

fulcrums:/ˈfʊlkrəmz/ /ˈfʌlkrəmz/ | n. | 支点（复数）| Plural form of fulcrum. | The engineer adjusted the fulcrums of the bridge to distribute the weight evenly. 工程师调整了桥梁的支点，以均匀分布重量。

Several key events served as fulcrums for social change.  几个关键事件成为社会变革的支点。| Synonyms: pivots, axes, centers, hinges, supports | Antonyms: peripheries, edges, extremities | noun
