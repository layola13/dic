outreigns:/ˌaʊtˈreɪnz/
verb
统治时间超过；比…活得长；比…更重要
to reign longer than; to outlive; to surpass in importance
Queen Victoria outreigned all previous British monarchs. 维多利亚女王比以往任何一位英国君主在位时间都长。
His influence outreigned his actual time in office. 他的影响力比他实际的任期更持久。
近义词: outlast, survive, surpass
反义词: precede, predate
