marmoration:/ˌmɑːrməˈreɪʃən/| n.|大理石花纹，斑驳状，(尤指褐斑病)|The process of becoming marbled; a marbled appearance or pattern, especially the mottled discoloration characteristic of brown marmorated stink bug damage.|The marmoration on the fruit was a sign of stink bug infestation. 水果上的斑驳状是椿象侵扰的迹象。|近义词：mottling, variegation, discoloration|反义词: uniformity, solid color|词性:名词

Etymology: From Latin *marmoratus*, past participle of *marmorare* "to marble," from *marmor* "marble."
