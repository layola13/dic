absorbed: /əbˈzɔːrbd/ /əbˈsɔːrbd/
adjective
全神贯注的；一心一意的；被…吸引住的
Completely engrossed or occupied; deeply interested in something.

The children were absorbed in playing their video game.
孩子们全神贯注地玩着电子游戏。


He was so absorbed in his book that he didn't hear me come in.
他看书看得太入神了，没听到我进来。


SYNONYM
engrossed, preoccupied, captivated, immersed, riveted

ANTONYM
distracted, uninterested, bored

词性
adjective


ETYMOLOGY
From Latin absorbere (“to suck in, swallow up”), from ab- (“from, away”) + sorbere (“to suck in”).


USAGE->
Attributive usage (before a noun):
The absorbed student didn't even notice the bell ring. =  全神贯注的学生甚至没有注意到铃声响了。
She sat with an absorbed expression on her face, lost in the music. = 她脸上带着全神贯注的表情坐着，沉浸在音乐中。


Predicative usage (after a verb):
He became completely absorbed in the intricate details of the painting. = 他完全沉浸于这幅画错综复杂的细节中。
They were so absorbed by the movie that they forgot to eat their popcorn. = 他们如此沉迷于电影以至于忘记吃爆米花了。


With prepositions:
She was absorbed in her work. = 她全神贯注于工作。
He was absorbed by the beauty of the sunset. =  他被日落的美丽所吸引。


PHRASE->
absorbed in = 全神贯注于
completely absorbed = 完全沉浸
become absorbed = 变得全神贯注
totally absorbed = 完全被…吸引
absorbed by = 被…吸引
