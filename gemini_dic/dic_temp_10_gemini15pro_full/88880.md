hokeday:/ˈhoʊkˌdeɪ/| n.|胡凯日，二番日|A traditional English holiday, celebrated on the second Tuesday after Easter.  It involved playful pranks and mock battles between men and women.|Hoketide was a popular event in medieval England, especially among the lower classes. 胡凯日是中世纪英格兰的流行活动，尤其是在下层阶级中。|近义词：holiday, festival|反义词: workday|词性:noun
