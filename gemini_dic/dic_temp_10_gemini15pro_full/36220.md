cognate:/ˈkɒɡneɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːɡneɪt/ (US)| adj. n.|同源的；同类的；同族语；同源词|related by birth; descended from the same language or form; related in origin, nature, or function|例句：The English word "father" and the Latin word "pater" are cognates. 英文单词“father”和拉丁文单词“pater”是同源词。|近义词：related, similar, analogous, corresponding|反义词：unrelated, dissimilar, different|词性：adjective, noun

cognate:/ˈkɒɡneɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːɡneɪt/ (US)| n.|同源词；同类物；同族语|a word related to another word in a different language, both deriving from the same source; something related or analogous|例句：Spanish and French have many cognates. 西班牙语和法语有很多同源词。|近义词：related word, counterpart|反义词：unrelated word|词性：noun
