gasohols: /ˈɡæsəhɔːlz/ | n. |汽油醇；乙醇汽油|A blend of gasoline and alcohol (usually ethanol), used as an alternative fuel for internal combustion engines.| Example: Brazil is a major producer and consumer of gasohols. 巴西是汽油醇的主要生产国和消费国。| Example:  The use of gasohols can help reduce dependence on fossil fuels. 使用汽油醇有助于减少对化石燃料的依赖。|Synonym: ethanol fuel, alcohol fuel|Antonym: gasoline, petrol|noun
