finagler:/fɪˈneɪɡlər/| n.|欺骗者，骗子|A person who obtains something by trickery or devious methods. |He's a real finagler, always finding a way to get what he wants. 他真是个骗子，总能想办法得到他想要的东西。|Synonyms: swindler, cheat, con artist, trickster|Antonyms: honest person, straight shooter|noun

finagle:/fɪˈneɪɡl/| v.|欺骗，哄骗; (用计谋)获得|To obtain something by trickery or devious methods. |She managed to finagle a free upgrade to first class. 她设法骗取了免费升舱到头等舱的机会。|Synonyms: wangle, wheedle, coax, manipulate, scheme|Antonyms: earn, deserve|verb
