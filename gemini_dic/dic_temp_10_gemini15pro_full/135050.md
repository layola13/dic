nonnominations:/ˌnɒnˌnɒmɪˈneɪʃənz/
n.
未提名；未获提名
The nonnominations were a surprise to many. 许多人对未提名感到惊讶。
nonnominations are rare for incumbents.  对于现任者来说，未获提名的情况很少见。
Near Antonyms: nominations
Etymology:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + nomination (from Latin nominatio, meaning "naming, appointment")
