firstest:/ˈfɜːrstɪst/ | adj. | 最第一的 |  This word is a non-standard and humorous superlative form of "first". While grammatically incorrect, it is sometimes used for emphasis or playful exaggeration. It implies being even more first than first, which is logically impossible. |  "He was the firstest one to cross the finish line!" (humorous) 他是第一个冲过终点线的！（幽默用法） |  first, earliest | last, latest | adjective
