intendant:/ɪnˈtɛndənt/ | n. |监督官，管理者，负责人| An official appointed to oversee a district or carry out a specific duty. | The king appointed an intendant to manage the royal estates. 国王任命了一名监督官来管理皇家财产。| The intendant oversaw the construction of the new canal. 监督官监督了新运河的建设。| synonyms: administrator, manager, supervisor, overseer, director | antonyms: subordinate, employee, worker | noun
