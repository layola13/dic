nonrepressions:/ˌnɑːnriˈprɛʃənz/ | n. | 非压抑，无压抑 | The state of not being repressed; lack of repression.  This refers to the absence of suppression of thoughts, feelings, or desires.  It can also describe a social or political environment where free expression and action are encouraged. | The era of nonrepressions led to a flourishing of art and culture.  非压抑的时代导致了艺术和文化的繁荣。 |  The government's policy of nonrepressions allowed for open protest. 政府的非压抑政策允许公开抗议。 | synonyms: freedom, liberation, unrestraint, openness | antonyms: repression, suppression, constraint, inhibition | noun
