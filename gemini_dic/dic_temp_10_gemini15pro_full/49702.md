demobilising:/diːˈməʊbɪlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
复员，遣散（尤指战后）
The process of discharging soldiers from military service, especially after a war.
The government is demobilising thousands of soldiers. 政府正在遣散数千名士兵。
discharging, releasing
conscripting, enlisting
verb
