fairly:/ˈferli/| adv.|相当地，公平地，完全地|Moderately; to a reasonable or moderate extent; justly; impartially; completely; absolutely.|The house is fairly new. 这房子还很新。|近义词：quite, rather, reasonably, moderately|反义词：unfairly, very, extremely|词性:副词
词源:From Middle English *fairely, derived from fair + -ly. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *fagraz.
