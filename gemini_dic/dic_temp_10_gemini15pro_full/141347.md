operand:/ˈɒpərænd/ (UK) /ˈɑːpərand/ (US)| n.|操作数|A quantity on which an operation is performed.  It's the input to an operator.|The operands in the equation 2 + 3 = 5 are 2 and 3.|近义词：argument, parameter, input|反义词: (No direct antonym, but could be considered the opposite of the result or output)|noun
