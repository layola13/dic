dupes:/djuːps/ | n. | 复制品，赝品；受骗者 |  A copy or imitation, especially one that is intended to deceive; a person who is easily deceived. |  The market was flooded with dupes of designer handbags.  市场上充斥着名牌手袋的仿冒品。| synonyms:  copies, imitations, fakes, replicas, counterfeits, phonies,  impostors,  frauds; victims, suckers, gulls | antonyms: originals, authentics | noun

dupe: /djuːp/ | vt. | 欺骗，愚弄 | To deceive or fool someone. |  He was duped into investing in a fraudulent scheme. 他被骗投资了一个欺诈性的计划。| synonyms: deceive, trick, fool, cheat, con, swindle, hoodwink, defraud | antonyms: enlighten, inform, educate | verb
