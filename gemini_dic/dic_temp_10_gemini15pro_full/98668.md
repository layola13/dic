insulary:/ˈɪnsjʊləri/ | adj.|岛屿的；偏狭的；孤立的|Relating to or characteristic of an island; also, narrow-minded or illiberal.  Having a limited outlook or scope, often as a result of geographic isolation.|The islanders had a rather insulary view of the world, unaware of the changes happening elsewhere. 岛民们对世界持有相当狭隘的看法，不知道其他地方发生的变化。|Synonyms: narrow-minded, provincial, parochial, isolated, detached|Antonyms: cosmopolitan, broad-minded, liberal, open-minded|adjective

insularity:/ˌɪnsjʊˈlærəti/ | n.|岛国状态；偏狭；孤立|The state or quality of being insular, especially the narrowness of viewpoint resulting from isolation.|The insularity of the community made it difficult for new ideas to take hold.  这个社区的封闭性使得新思想难以扎根。|Synonyms: narrow-mindedness, provincialism, parochialism, isolation, detachment|Antonyms: cosmopolitanism, broad-mindedness, liberalism, open-mindedness|noun
