gynaecium:/ɡaɪˈniːsiəm/ /dʒɪˈniːsiəm/
n.
雌蕊群
The female reproductive organ of a flower, consisting of one or more carpels.
The gynaecium of this orchid is highly specialized.  这种兰花的雌蕊群高度特化。

近义词:pistil
反义词:androecium
