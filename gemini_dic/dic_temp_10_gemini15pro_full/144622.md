outwinged:/ˌaʊtˈwɪŋd/
verb
胜过，以更优异的翅膀超越, 在飞行中超越
surpass in flight; outstrip, especially in a competition involving aircraft
The new aircraft easily outwinged its older rivals.  这架新飞机轻易地超越了它的老对手。
The smaller, more agile fighter outwinged the larger bomber.  更小、更灵活的战斗机在飞行中胜过了更大的轰炸机。
Synonyms: surpass, outstrip, excel, outdo, outmaneuver
Antonyms: lag behind, fall behind
v.
