mistral:/ˈmɪstrəl, miˈstrɑːl/| n. |密史脱拉风;西北风|A strong, cold, dry, northwesterly wind that blows from the Alps over southern France to the Mediterranean coast, especially in winter.|The mistral whipped through the valley, carrying the scent of pine and snow. 密史脱拉风吹过山谷，带着松树和雪的味道。|Synonyms: northerly, northwesterly, gale, windstorm|Antonyms: southerly, southeasterly|noun
