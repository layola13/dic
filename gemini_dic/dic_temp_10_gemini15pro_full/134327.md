noninstrumental:/ˌnɒnɪnstrʊˈmɛntəl/ | adj. | 非工具性的，非手段的 | Not serving as an instrument or means to an end; having intrinsic value or being an end in itself. |  Many philosophers argue that happiness is noninstrumental, meaning it's desirable for its own sake, not just as a means to something else. 许多哲学家认为，幸福是非工具性的，这意味着幸福本身就是可取的，而不仅仅是达到其他目的的手段。

| Synonym: inherent, intrinsic, essential | Antonym: instrumental, extrinsic, utilitarian | adjective
