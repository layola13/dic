filmmakers:/ˈfɪlmˌmeɪkərz/
noun
电影制作人
People who make films, including directors, producers, screenwriters, and others involved in the creative process.
The filmmakers were praised for their innovative storytelling. 电影制作人因其创新的叙事方式而受到赞扬。
The documentary was a collaboration between several talented filmmakers. 这部纪录片是几位才华横溢的电影制作人合作的成果。
synonyms: directors, producers, moviemakers
antonyms: audience, viewers
