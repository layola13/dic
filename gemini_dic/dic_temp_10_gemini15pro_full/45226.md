culminating:/ˈkʌlmɪneɪtɪŋ/
verb form (present participle)
达到顶点;达到高潮
reaching the highest point of development; ending in a final, decisive event.
Her career culminated in her appointment as CEO. 她的职业生涯最终达到了担任首席执行官的顶峰。
The festival culminated with a spectacular fireworks display.  节日以一场壮观的烟花表演达到高潮。
近义词: climaxing, peaking, concluding
反义词: beginning, starting, initiating
词性: Verb (present participle of "culminate")


culminate:/ˈkʌlmɪneɪt/
verb
达到顶点;达到高潮
reach a climax or point of highest development.
The celebration culminated in a grand parade. 庆典以盛大的游行达到高潮。
His efforts culminated in success. 他的努力最终获得了成功。
近义词: climax, peak, conclude
反义词: begin, start, initiate
词性: Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin culminatus, past participle of culminare "to top, crown," from culmen "top, summit."
