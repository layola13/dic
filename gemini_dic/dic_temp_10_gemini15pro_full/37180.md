comedia: /kəˈmiːdiə/ /kəˈmidiə/ | n. | 喜剧 | A play, movie, or other performance that is intended to be funny.  Derived from the Greek κωμῳδία (kōmōidía), meaning "revel, merrymaking," ultimately from κῶμος (kômos, “revel”) + ᾠδή (ōidḗ, “song”). | We went to see a comedia last night and laughed a lot.  我们昨晚去看了一部喜剧，笑了很多。|  The comedia was a huge success, selling out every night. 这部喜剧非常成功，每晚都座无虚席。 | Synonyms: comedy, farce, play | Antonyms: tragedy, drama | Noun
