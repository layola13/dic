nobbled:/ˈnɒbəld/ (UK) /ˈnɑːbəld/ (US)| vt.|使无效；搞定；暗中破坏；买通；逮捕|To disable or incapacitate, often by underhand means; to get hold of; to secure by dubious means; to arrest. |The horse was nobbled before the race.  这匹马在比赛前被做了手脚。|Synonyms:  tamper with, fix, sabotage, bribe, capture|Antonyms: repair, assist, protect|verb

nobbled:/ˈnɒbəld/ (UK) /ˈnɑːbəld/ (US)| adj.|(Australian slang) Arrested; caught.|被逮捕的；被抓到的|He got nobbled for speeding. 他因超速被捕了。|Synonyms:  arrested, apprehended, caught|Antonyms: released, freed|adjective
