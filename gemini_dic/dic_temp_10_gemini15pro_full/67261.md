expurgatorial:/ˌekspərˈɡətɔːriəl/
adjective
净化（的）；删除不当内容（的）
Relating to or intended to expurgate. Removing or intended to remove objectionable passages from a text.
The editor made several expurgatorial changes to the manuscript before publication.  编辑在出版前对手稿进行了几处净化修改。
The expurgatorial version of the novel omitted several sexually explicit scenes.  这部小说的净化版本删除了几个露骨的性爱场景。
近义词: censoring, bowdlerizing, purifying, cleansing
反义词: including, adding, uncensored, unexpurgated
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *expurgatorius, from Latin expurgat-, past participle stem of expurgare to purge thoroughly, from ex- out + purgare to cleanse.
