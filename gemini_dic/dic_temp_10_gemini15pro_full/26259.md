button-down:/ˈbʌtn daʊn/ (US), /ˈbʌtn daʊn/ (UK)| n. adj.|纽扣领衬衫；保守的，传统的|A shirt, typically with a collar having buttoned-down points, or a person who is conventional and somewhat conservative in attitude and dress.|He always wore button-downs to work.  他总是穿着纽扣衬衫去上班。|近义词：shirt, conservative, traditional|反义词:radical, unconventional|词性:名词，形容词

button-downs:/ˈbʌtn daʊnz/ (US), /ˈbʌtn daʊnz/ (UK)| n.|纽扣领衬衫（复数）|Plural form of button-down, referring to multiple button-down shirts.|He has a collection of button-downs in various colors. 他收藏了各种颜色的纽扣衬衫。|近义词：shirts|反义词: (No direct antonym for the plural noun)|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the phrase "button down," describing the collar points that are buttoned down to the shirt.  The term later broadened to describe a person with a conservative style.
