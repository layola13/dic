overbrow:/ˈoʊvərˌbraʊ/| adj.|夸张的，做作的，矫揉造作的|Excessively refined or cultured in a manner perceived as affected or pretentious.|Her overbrow poetry was difficult for most people to understand. 她那矫揉造作的诗歌大多数人都难以理解。|例句：His overbrow pronouncements alienated his colleagues. 他那装腔作势的声明疏远了他的同事们。|近义词：pretentious, affected, mannered, artificial|反义词：natural, genuine, unaffected, sincere|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From over + brow.  "Brow" in this sense refers to the forehead, often associated with intellectual or artistic expression.  "Over" implies an excessive or exaggerated degree.  Therefore, "overbrow" suggests an excessive display of intellectualism or artistic refinement, often perceived as pretentious.
