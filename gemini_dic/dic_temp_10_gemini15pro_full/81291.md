grants-in-aid: /ɡrænts ɪn eɪd/ | n. | 补助金；援助金 |  A sum of money given by a government or other organization to a person or institution for a particular purpose, especially to help fund a project or cover costs.  | The university received several grants-in-aid to support its research programs. 大学收到几笔补助金来支持其研究项目。 | synonyms: subsidies, subventions, appropriations | antonyms:  loans, debts | noun
