cacodylic:/ˌkækəˈdɪlɪk/| adj.|二甲胂酸的;  含二甲胂基的|Relating to or containing the cacodyl radical, (CH3)2As.  Cacodylic acid is an organoarsenic compound. It is a colorless, odorless solid that is soluble in water.|Cacodylic acid is used as a herbicide. 二甲胂酸用作除草剂。|Synonyms: dimethylarsinic, |Antonyms: N/A|adjective
