folily:/ˈfoʊlɪʃli/ | adv. | 愚蠢地 | In a foolish manner; unwisely. |  He folily invested all his money in a single stock. 他愚蠢地把所有的钱都投资在一支股票上。

He folily believed everything the salesman told him. 他愚蠢地相信了推销员所说的一切。 | 近义词: foolishly, stupidly, unwisely, imprudently, rashly | 反义词: wisely, prudently, sensibly, judiciously | 词性: 副词
