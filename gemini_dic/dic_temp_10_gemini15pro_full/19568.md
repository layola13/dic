bigamistically:/ˌbɪɡəˈmɪstɪkli/| adv.|重婚地，犯重婚罪地|In a manner that constitutes bigamy; illegally having two spouses at the same time.|He married bigamistically, unaware that his first wife was still alive. 他犯了重婚罪，因为他不知道他的第一任妻子仍然活着。|Synonym: illegally, unlawfully|Antonym: legally, lawfully|词性:副词

bigamistically:/ˌbɪɡəˈmɪstɪkli/| adv.|重婚地，犯重婚罪地|The act of entering into a marriage while already legally married to another person.|She was charged with bigamistically marrying two men. 她被指控犯重婚罪，同时嫁给了两个男人。|Synonym: doubly married, polygamously (in a specific sense)|Antonym: monogamously, singly married|词性:副词
