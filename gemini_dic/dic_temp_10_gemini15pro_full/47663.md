debriefed:/ˌdiːˈbriːft/
verb
汇报；向…询问情况；听取（某人）的汇报
question (someone, typically a soldier or spy) about a completed mission or undertaking.

例句：
The pilots were debriefed after every mission.
每次任务结束后，飞行员都会进行汇报。

The agent was debriefed upon his return.
特工返回后进行了汇报。


近义词：
questioned, interviewed, interrogated, examined

反义词：
briefed  (Although not a direct opposite, it represents the reverse process)

词性
verb
