expressionist: /ɪkˈspreʃənɪst/ | n. adj. | 表现主义者；表现主义的 |  A person who advocates or practices expressionism, especially in art, literature, or music. Characterized by the free and subjective expression of emotion. |  The painter was a leading expressionist, known for his bold use of color and distorted figures. 这位画家是一位重要的表现主义者，以大胆运用色彩和扭曲的人物而闻名。 | Near synonyms: artist, painter, writer, composer, creative, avant-garde | Antonyms: traditionalist, realist, classicist | Noun, Adjective

expressionistic: /ɪkˈspreʃənˈɪstɪk/ | adj. | 表现主义的 | Relating to or characteristic of expressionism.  |  The film's expressionistic lighting and set design created a nightmarish atmosphere.  这部电影的表现主义灯光和布景设计营造了一种噩梦般的氛围。 | Near synonyms: dramatic, intense, emotional, evocative, stylized | Antonyms: realistic, natural, objective | Adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Expressionismus*, from *Expression* (expression), from Late Latin *expressio* (expression), from Latin *expressus* (pressed out, clear), past participle of *exprimere* (to press out, express).
