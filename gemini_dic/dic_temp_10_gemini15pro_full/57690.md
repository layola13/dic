drawbridge:/ˈdrɔːbrɪdʒ/| n. |吊桥|A bridge that can be raised or lowered to allow ships or boats to pass underneath or to prevent access.|The knight crossed the drawbridge to enter the castle. 骑士穿过吊桥进入城堡。|The drawbridge was raised to prevent the enemy from entering. 吊桥升起以阻止敌人进入。|近义词：bridge, bascule bridge|反义词: None |noun


drawlink:/ˈdrɔːlɪŋk/| n. |吊桥的活动桥板|One of the sections of a drawbridge that can be raised or lowered. It is also sometimes used interchangeably with "drawbridge."|One of the drawlinks was damaged in the storm.  暴风雨中吊桥的一块活动桥板损坏了。|The engineers inspected the drawlinks to ensure they were functioning properly. 工程师检查了活动桥板以确保它们正常工作。|近义词：bridge section, bascule leaf|反义词: None |noun
