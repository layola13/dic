incomparable:/ɪnˈkɒmpərəbl/ | adj. | 无与伦比的，不可比拟的 | Not able to be compared; peerless. | Her beauty is incomparable. 她的美貌无与伦比。 | The view from the mountaintop was incomparable. 从山顶看到的景色无与伦比。 | 近义词：matchless, peerless, unrivaled, unsurpassed | 反义词：comparable, ordinary, mediocre | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French incomparable, from Late Latin incomparābilis, from in- "not" + comparabilis "comparable," from comparare "to compare"
