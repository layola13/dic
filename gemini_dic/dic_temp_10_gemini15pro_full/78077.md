geodetic:/ˌdʒiːəˈdɛtɪk/
adjective
大地测量的
Relating to the shape and size of the earth, and the precise location of points on its surface.
The geodetic survey accurately mapped the mountainous terrain.  大地测量精确地绘制了山区的地形。

近义词:  geodesical, surveying
反义词: None

词性: adjective
词源:  From French *géodésique*, from Greek *geōdaitēs* 'divider of the earth', from *gē* 'earth' + *daiein* 'to divide'.
