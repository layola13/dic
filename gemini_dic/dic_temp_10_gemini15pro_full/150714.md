paraboloid:/pəˈræbəˌlɔɪd/| n. |抛物面|A surface formed by rotating a parabola around its axis.  It resembles a bowl or a dish shape.|The satellite dish is shaped like a paraboloid to focus the signals. 卫星天线形状像一个抛物面，用来聚焦信号。|The headlight reflector is a paraboloid.  车前灯反射镜是一个抛物面。|近义词：parabolic surface|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a plane or a sphere, depending on context)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek "parabole" (parabola) + "-oid" (like, resembling).  "Parabole" itself comes from "para-" (beside, alongside) + "ballein" (to throw).
