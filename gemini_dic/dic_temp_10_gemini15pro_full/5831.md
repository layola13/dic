amativeness:/ˈæmətɪvnɪs/
n.
喜爱；性欲；好色
A propensity for sexual love; lustfulness.
He was a man of strong amativeness. 他是一个性欲很强的人。

近义词:lust, passion, desire, libido
反义词:frigidity, asexuality
词性:noun


Etymology: From Late Latin *amativus, from Latin amare (“to love”) + -ivus.  First recorded in 1822–32.
