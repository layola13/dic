ecliptic:/ɪˈklɪptɪk/ | n. | 黄道 |The apparent path of the Sun across the celestial sphere over the course of a year, as seen from Earth.  It is the plane of Earth's orbit around the Sun.| The planets in our solar system all orbit roughly on the ecliptic.  我们的太阳系中的行星都大致在黄道上运行。| synonym:  celestial path, sun's path | antonym: N/A | noun
