fluxility:/flʌkˈsɪləti/
n.
易变性，不稳定性
The quality or state of being fluxible; instability; mutability.
The fluxility of the stock market makes it a risky investment. 股票市场的波动性使其成为一项高风险投资。
近义词：instability, volatility, mutability, fluidity
反义词：stability, constancy, permanence
词性: 名词


fluxility:/flʌkˈsɪləti/
词源: From Latin fluxus (“a flowing”), past participle of fluere (“to flow”).
词性: 名词
