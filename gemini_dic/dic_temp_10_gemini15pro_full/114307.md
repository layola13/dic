maintain:/meɪnˈteɪn/ /mənˈteɪn/| vt.|维持，保持；维修；保养；坚持；断言|To continue to have; to keep in existence or not allow to become less.  To keep in good condition by checking or repairing it regularly. To express firmly your belief that something is true.|例句：The company has maintained a steady growth rate.  这家公司一直保持着稳定的增长率。|近义词：preserve, sustain, uphold, assert|反义词: neglect, abandon|词性:动词
