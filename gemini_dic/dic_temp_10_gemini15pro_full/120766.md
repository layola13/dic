disappointing:/ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪntɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人失望的 | Causing disappointment; failing to fulfill expectations. | The movie was disappointing. 这部电影令人失望。| The concert was very disappointing. 这场音乐会非常令人失望。 | 近义词: unsatisfactory, underwhelming, frustrating | 反义词: satisfying, pleasing, gratifying | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From disappoint + -ing.  Disappoint comes from the Old French desapointer, meaning "to undo an appointment," which in turn comes from des- (meaning "un-") + apointer (meaning "to appoint").
