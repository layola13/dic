exemplarist:/ɪɡˈzɛmplərɪst/ | n. | 模范主义者；以榜样为导向的人 | Someone who believes in or advocates exemplarism (the theory that moral knowledge comes from identifying and emulating moral exemplars). |  Mother Teresa is often cited as an exemplarist figure.  特蕾莎修女经常被引用为模范主义者。 | synonym: role model, ideal, paragon | antonym: nonconformist, rebel, iconoclast | noun

exemplarist:/ɪɡˈzɛmplərɪst/ | adj. | 模范主义的；以榜样为导向的 | Relating to or characteristic of exemplarism. |  He adopted an exemplarist approach to moral education. 他采用了以榜样为导向的道德教育方法。 | synonym: exemplary, model | antonym: unconventional, unorthodox | adjective
