ecmascript:/ˈɛkməskrɪpt/| n. |ECMAScript|A standardized scripting language specification, most commonly used for client-side scripting on the World Wide Web. It is best known as the language of JavaScript.|Example: Modern web browsers fully support ECMAScript 6 features. 现代网页浏览器完全支持 ECMAScript 6 的特性。|Example:  ECMAScript is the foundation for languages like JavaScript and ActionScript. ECMAScript 是 JavaScript 和 ActionScript 等语言的基础。|近义词:JavaScript (often used interchangeably), JScript|反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's a language specification)|词性:noun
