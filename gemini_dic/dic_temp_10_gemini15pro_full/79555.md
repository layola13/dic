glomerulus:/ɡləˈmɛrʊləs/ (US), /ɡləˈmɛrʊləs/ (UK)| n.|肾小球|A cluster of capillaries around the end of a kidney tubule, where waste products are filtered from the blood.|The glomerulus is the primary filtering unit of the kidney. 肾小球是肾脏的主要过滤单位。|The glomerulus filters waste products out of the blood. 肾小球过滤血液中的废物。|Synonyms: renal corpuscle|Antonyms: N/A|noun
