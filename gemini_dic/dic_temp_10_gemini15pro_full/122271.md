mitigation:/ˌmɪtɪˈɡeɪʃn/| n.| 缓和，减轻，缓解|The act of lessening the impact or force of something unpleasant.|The government announced new policies for climate change mitigation. 政府宣布了新的气候变化缓解政策。|近义词：amelioration, easement|反义词:intensification|词性:名词
