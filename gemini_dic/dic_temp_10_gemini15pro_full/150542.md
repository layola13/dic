panzoism:/ˈpænzoʊɪzəm/
n.
泛心论
The doctrine that mind or a mind-like principle pervades the universe and is the source of both material phenomena and consciousness.
Panzoism suggests that consciousness is inherent in the universe. 泛心论认为意识是宇宙固有的。
近义词：panpsychism, hylozoism
反义词：materialism, physicalism
词性：noun

Etymology:
From Greek *pan* (all) + *zoe* (life) + *-ismos* (forming abstract nouns of action or condition).
