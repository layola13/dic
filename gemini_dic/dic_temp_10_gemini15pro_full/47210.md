daymare:/ˈdeɪmɛər/ (US), /ˈdeɪmeə(r)/ (UK)| n. |噩梦，白日梦|A frightening or unpleasant dreamlike experience occurring while awake.  A state of anxiety or extreme apprehension.|例句：She suffered from daymares after the accident. 事故发生后，她一直遭受噩梦的困扰。

例句：The thought of losing his job was a constant daymare.  想到失去工作，他一直处于极度焦虑中。|近义词：nightmare, anxiety, apprehension, dread|反义词:calm, peace, tranquility|词性:noun
