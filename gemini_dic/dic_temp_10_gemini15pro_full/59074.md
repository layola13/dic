dwindle:/ˈdwɪndl/| v. |减少，缩小，衰落|To become smaller and smaller; to decrease gradually.|The town's population has dwindled to a mere few hundred.  这个镇的人口已经减少到只有几百人。

The snow dwindled away as the temperature rose. 随着温度升高，雪逐渐融化消失了。|近义词：decrease, diminish, shrink, decline, lessen|反义词：increase, grow, expand, rise|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dwindlen, frequentative of *dwine (“to waste away”), from Old English dwīnan (“to vanish, disappear, perish, waste away”), from Proto-Germanic *dwīnaną (“to disappear, fade, vanish”), from Proto-Indo-European *dwey- (“to disappear, vanish”). Cognate with Dutch verdwijnen (“to disappear”), German schwinden (“to disappear, vanish, dwindle”).
