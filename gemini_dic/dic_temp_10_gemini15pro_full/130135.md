non-recoverable:/ˌnɒn rɪˈkʌvərəbl/ (US) /ˌnɑːn rɪˈkʌvərəbl/ (UK)| adj.|不可恢复的，无法挽回的|Not able to be recovered, regained, or restored.|The data loss was non-recoverable, and the company lost years of research. 数据丢失是不可恢复的，公司损失了多年的研究成果。|例句：The damage to the ecosystem is non-recoverable.  对生态系统的破坏是不可恢复的。|近义词：irreparable, irreversible, irretrievable|反义词：recoverable, retrievable, reversible|词性:形容词

non-recoverable:/ˌnɒn rɪˈkʌvərəbl/ (US) /ˌnɑːn rɪˈkʌvərəbl/ (UK)| n.|不可恢复的事物|Something that cannot be recovered, regained, or restored (used as a noun, though less common).|The non-recoverables from the fire included family heirlooms. 火灾中无法挽回的损失包括传家宝。|例句：The company wrote off the non-recoverables after the bankruptcy. 公司在破产后注销了无法收回的款项。|近义词：loss, damage|反义词：asset, gain|词性:名词
