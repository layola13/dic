non-technical:/ˌnɒnˈtɛknɪkəl/ /ˌnɑːnˈtɛknɪkəl/| adj.|非技术性的；通俗的|Not related to or requiring specialized knowledge of a technical subject; easy to understand for people without specialized training.|This book provides a non-technical explanation of quantum physics. 这本书对量子物理学进行了通俗易懂的解释。|Example: The manual is written in non-technical language so that anyone can understand it.  这本手册是用非技术性的语言写的，所以任何人都能理解。

Example:  He gave a non-technical summary of the complex scientific findings. 他对复杂的科学发现做了通俗的总结。

|Synonyms: simple, easy-to-understand, layman's, plain, popular, accessible|Antonyms: technical, specialized, complex, esoteric, jargon-filled|adjective
