chondroma:/kɒnˈdrəʊmə/ (US: /kɑːnˈdroʊmə/) | n. | 软骨瘤 | A benign tumor composed of mature cartilage cells.  It is a common type of bone tumor. | Example 1: The doctor discovered a chondroma in the patient's knee. 医生在病人的膝盖里发现了一个软骨瘤。

Example 2:  Chondromas are usually painless and slow-growing. 软骨瘤通常是无痛且生长缓慢的。| Synonym:  cartilage tumor | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific type of tumor.  One could consider malignant bone tumors as a contrasting concept.) | noun
