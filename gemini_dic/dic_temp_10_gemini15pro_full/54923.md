dispose:/dɪˈspoʊz/
| v. |处理，处置；使倾向于；安排|To get rid of something, especially by throwing it away; to make someone feel a particular way towards someone or something; to arrange things or people in a particular way or position.|The company disposed of its old computers. 这家公司处理掉了它的旧电脑。|近义词：arrange, incline, settle, discard|反义词: retain, keep, gather|词性:动词


词源:
From Old French *disposer (Modern French disposer), from Latin dispōnere (“to put in different places, distribute, arrange”), from dis- (“apart, asunder, in different directions”) + pōnere (“to put, place”).
