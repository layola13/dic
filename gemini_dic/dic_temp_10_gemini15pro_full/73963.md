fortifier:/ˈfɔːrtɪfaɪər/ | n. |  укрепляющее средство, фортификатор |Something that fortifies or strengthens, especially something added to food to increase its nutritional value. | Orange juice is often fortified with vitamin C. 橙汁通常添加维生素C。| synonyms: strengthener, invigorator, supplement | antonyms:  weaken, deplete | noun

fortify:/ˈfɔːrtɪfaɪ/ | vt. | укреплять, усиливать | To strengthen (something, such as a place or thing) by building military defenses or adding fortifications.  To make (someone or something) physically or mentally stronger. | The city walls were fortified against attack. 城墙被加固以抵御攻击。 | synonyms: reinforce, bolster, strengthen, enrich | antonyms: weaken, undermine, deplete | verb
