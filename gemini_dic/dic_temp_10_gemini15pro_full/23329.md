boyishness:/ˈbɔɪɪʃnəs/| n. |男孩气；孩子气|The quality of behaving in a way that is typical of a young boy.  A youthful, playful, and sometimes mischievous quality often associated with males, but not exclusively.| He retained a certain boyishness even in middle age. 他即使到了中年仍然保留着某种孩子气。|近义词：youthfulness, childishness, playfulness, immaturity, naiveté|反义词：maturity, sophistication, worldliness|词性:noun
