一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does he do for a living? 他靠什么谋生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? 她晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has he done to help? 他做了些什么来帮忙？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has she been studying at university?  她在大学里一直在学什么？

What happened at the party? 聚会上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

What will happen next? 接下来会发生什么？
