cross-breed:/ˈkrɒs briːd/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːs briːd/ (US)| n. v. adj.|杂交品种，杂交，杂交的|A cross-breed is an animal or plant that has parents of different breeds or varieties.  To cross-breed means to breed (animals or plants) using parents of different breeds or varieties.  Cross-bred is used as an adjective to describe something that is a product of cross-breeding.|例句：The dog is a cross-breed between a Labrador and a Collie. 这只狗是拉布拉多犬和柯利牧羊犬的杂交品种。|近义词：hybrid, mongrel (n.),  hybridize (v.)|反义词:purebred, pedigree|词性: noun, verb, adjective


cross-breed:/ˈkrɒs briːd/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːs briːd/ (US)| v. |杂交|To breed (animals or plants) using parents of different breeds or varieties.|例句：Farmers often cross-breed different types of cattle to improve milk production.  农民经常杂交不同品种的牛以提高产奶量。|近义词：hybridize, interbreed|反义词:purebreed|词性: verb
