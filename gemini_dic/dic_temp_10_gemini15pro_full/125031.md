mukene:/muˈkɛneɪ/ | n. | 银汉鱼 |A small, silvery freshwater fish found in Lake Victoria, Africa, often dried and eaten whole.  It's a crucial food source in the region. |  Mukene are a staple food in many communities around Lake Victoria.  银汉鱼是维多利亚湖周围许多社区的主食。|  Fishermen catch tons of mukene every year. 渔民每年捕捞数吨的银汉鱼。 | synonyms: silver cyprinid (a more general term), dagaa (another name for mukene) | antonyms: N/A (no direct opposite for a specific fish species) | noun
