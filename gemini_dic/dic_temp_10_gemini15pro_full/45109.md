cuckooed:/ˈkʊkuːd/| v.|杜鹃叫；鸠占鹊巢；<非正式>傻笑；<非正式>把…送去寄养；<非正式>强加于；<非正式>插嘴|to lay one's eggs in another bird's nest, or to use another's resources or property as one's own without permission. It can also mean to make a sound like a cuckoo, or informally, to giggle or to deposit someone (especially a child) somewhere temporarily.|The cuckoo cuckooed loudly in the early morning. 杜鹃在清晨大声地叫。|近义词：intrude, usurp, impose, giggle, deposit|反义词: vacate, relinquish, withdraw|词性:动词
