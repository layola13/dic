nullified: /ˈnʌlɪfaɪd/
verb
使无效；取消；废除
To make legally null and void; invalidate.
The contract was nullified due to a breach of terms.  由于违反条款，合同被废除了。
The judge nullified the verdict. 法官宣布判决无效。
invalidated, voided, negated, annulled
ratified, validated, confirmed
v.
