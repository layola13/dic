matsu:/ˈmɑːtsuː/ | n. | 松树 |  A type of pine tree, especially the Japanese red pine (Pinus densiflora) and the Japanese black pine (Pinus thunbergii), which are highly valued in Japanese culture and gardens. |  The matsu is a symbol of longevity and perseverance in Japan.  松树在日本象征着长寿和毅力。| synonym: pine | antonym: deciduous tree | noun
