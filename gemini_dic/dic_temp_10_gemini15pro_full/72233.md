floriculture:/ˈflɔːrɪkʌltʃər/ /ˈflɔrəˌkʌltʃər/| n. |花卉栽培；花卉园艺|The cultivation of flowers or flowering plants.|例句：The floriculture industry in the Netherlands is world-renowned. 荷兰的花卉栽培业闻名世界。

例句：She took a course in floriculture to learn more about growing orchids. 她参加了花卉栽培课程，以了解更多关于种植兰花的知识。|近义词：flower gardening, horticulture|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology->
From French *floriculture*, from Latin *flōs* (“flower”) + *cultūra* (“cultivation”).
