jabbing:/ˈdʒæbɪŋ/ | adj.  v. (present participle of jab) | 刺戳的，戳刺的 |  The sharp, jabbing pain in his side made him gasp. 他侧身一阵剧烈的刺痛让他倒吸一口气。|  例句：He felt a jabbing pain in his chest. 他感到胸口一阵刺痛。 | 近义词：poking, prodding, stabbing | 反义词：caressing, stroking | 词性：形容词，动词(现在分词)
jabbing:/ˈdʒæbɪŋ/ | n. | 刺，戳 | The boxer's jabbing was quick and precise.  拳击手的刺拳又快又准。 | 例句：The constant jabbing of her finger was annoying. 她不停地戳着手指，真烦人。 | 近义词：poking, thrusting | 反义词： withdrawing | 词性：名词

Etymology:  From *gib,*  a variant of *job,* meaning "to peck, strike with a beak."  This evolved to "poke sharply" and then to the modern meaning. 词源：源于*gib*，是*job*的变体，意思是“啄，用喙击”。 这演变成“猛地戳”，然后演变成现代的含义。
