miseducations:/ˌmɪsˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃənz/| n. |错误的教育，误导|The book "Miseducations" explores the ways in which societal structures contribute to the misdirection of young minds. 这本书《错误的教育》探讨了社会结构如何导致年轻人思想的误导。|例句：Her miseducations about personal finance led to poor financial decisions. 她对个人理财的错误理解导致了糟糕的财务决策。

例句：The film highlighted the miseducations surrounding race and class. 这部电影突出了围绕种族和阶级的错误教育。|近义词：misinformation, misguidance, indoctrination|反义词:education, enlightenment|词性:名词
