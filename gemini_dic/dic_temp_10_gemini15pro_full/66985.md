experting:/ˈɛkspɜːtɪŋ/
verb
充当专家；提供专家意见
Acting as an expert; providing expert advice.

例句：She's been experting in the field of artificial intelligence for over a decade.  她在人工智能领域担任专家已超过十年。

近义词：consulting, advising, specializing
反义词：inexperienced, amateur
词性：v.
