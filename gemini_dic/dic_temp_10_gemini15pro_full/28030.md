capacitive:/kəˈpæsɪtɪv/| adj.|电容的，电容性的|Relating to or having capacitance; capable of storing an electrical charge.|例句：A capacitive touchscreen responds to the electrical properties of your finger.  电容式触摸屏会响应你手指的电特性。

例句：Capacitive sensors are commonly used in proximity detection.  电容式传感器常用于接近检测。|近义词：electrostatic, dielectric|反义词: resistive, inductive|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From capacity + -ive.  Capacity ultimately derives from Latin "capācitās" (capacity, capability), from "capāx" (able to hold). The suffix "-ive" forms adjectives meaning "tending to; having the nature or quality of."
