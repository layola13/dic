outblush:/aʊtˈblʌʃ/
verb
使脸红得胜过；使羞愧；使相形见绌
to surpass in blushing; to cause to blush by comparison
She outblushed the rose. 她的脸比玫瑰花还要红。
近义词:surpass, exceed, outdo, eclipse
反义词:pale, fade
词性:v.
