miscontent:/ˌmɪskənˈtɛnt/
n.|不满，不满足|A feeling of dissatisfaction or discontent.|The new policy caused widespread miscontent among the workers. 新政策在工人中引起了广泛的不满。
近义词:discontent, dissatisfaction, unhappiness
反义词:contentment, satisfaction, happiness
词性:名词

miscontent:/ˌmɪskənˈtɛnt/
vt.|使不满，使不满足|To make discontented or dissatisfied.|The constant delays miscontented the passengers.  不断的延误让乘客感到不满。
近义词:displease, annoy, irritate
反义词:please, satisfy, delight
词性:动词


词源:
From mis- (expressing wrongness or error) + content (satisfied).  由表示错误或失误的前缀 mis- 和 content（满足的）组成。
