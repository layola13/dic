cubbing:/ˈkʌbɪŋ/| n.|幼崽训练；幼童军活动；解魔方|The activity of raising or training young animals, especially wolf cubs.  Also refers to the activity of solving Rubik's Cubes.|He enjoys cubbing and often takes his wolf cubs out for training. 他喜欢幼崽训练，经常带他的小狼出去训练。

She's really into cubbing and can solve a Rubik's Cube in under a minute. 她真的很喜欢玩魔方，可以在一分钟内解出一个魔方。|近义词：training, rearing (for animals); puzzle-solving (for Rubik's Cube)|反义词: neglect (for animals)|词性:名词
