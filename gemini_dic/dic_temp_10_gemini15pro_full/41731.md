corrodible:/kəˈroʊdəbl/
adjective
可腐蚀的
Susceptible to corrosion; able to be corroded or eaten away by chemical action.
Iron is a corrodible metal, especially when exposed to moisture.  铁是一种易腐蚀的金属，尤其是在潮湿环境下。
The corrodible pipes needed to be replaced. 这些易腐蚀的管道需要更换。
近义词:rustable, oxidizable, erodable
反义词:corrosion-resistant, inert, non-corrosive
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *corrodere, from Latin corrodere (“to gnaw to pieces”), from com- (“together, with”) + rodere (“to gnaw”).
