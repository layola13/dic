maathai: /mɑːˈtaɪ/ | n. |  旺加里·马塔伊 (Wangari Maathai)|  Wangari Maathai (1940-2011) was a Kenyan environmental and political activist. She founded the Green Belt Movement, an environmental non-governmental organization focused on planting trees, environmental conservation, and women's rights.  She was awarded the Nobel Peace Prize in 2004. | Wangari Maathai was the first African woman to receive the Nobel Peace Prize. 旺加里·马塔伊是第一位获得诺贝尔和平奖的非洲女性。 |  近义词: environmentalist, activist | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps opponent of environmentalism) | 词性: noun
