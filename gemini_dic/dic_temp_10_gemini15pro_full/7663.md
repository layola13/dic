anode:/ˈænəʊd/ (UK) /ˈænˌoʊd/ (US)| n.|阳极，正极|The positive electrode in an electrolytic cell, toward which the negatively charged ions (anions) migrate.|Electrons flow from the anode to the cathode. 电子从阳极流向阴极。|The anode corroded over time.  阳极随着时间的推移被腐蚀了。|Synonyms: positive electrode, positive pole|Antonyms: cathode, negative electrode|noun
