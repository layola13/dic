paraphrasability:/ˌpærəˈfreɪzəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可释义性，可意译性 | The quality of being able to be paraphrased; the extent to which something can be expressed in different words while retaining its original meaning. | The paraphrasability of the poem is high, meaning it can be expressed in various ways without losing its core message. 这首诗的可释义性很高，意味着它可以用不同的方式表达而不会失去其核心信息。 | synonyms: rewordability, rephrasability | antonyms:  incommunicability, untranslatability | noun

paraphrasable:/ˌpærəˈfreɪzəbl/ | adj. | 可释义的，可意译的 |  Able to be paraphrased; capable of being expressed in different words while retaining its original meaning. |  The sentence is paraphrasable, so you can reword it for clarity.  这个句子是可以释义的，所以你可以为了清晰起见改写它。| synonyms: rewordable, rephrasable | antonyms: incommunicable, untranslatable | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From paraphrase + -ability.  Paraphrase comes from Late Latin paraphrasis, from Greek paraphrasis, from paraphrazein "to tell in other words," from para- "beside" + phrazein "to tell, declare."
