biradiated:/baɪˈreɪdieɪtɪd/
adj.
双辐射的，有两条辐射线的
Having two rays or radiating parts.
The biradiate structure of the antenna allows for optimal signal reception.  天线的双辐射结构可以实现最佳信号接收。
adj.
双射线的，有两条射线的
Having two divergent rays or branches.
The biradiate markings on the insect's wings are a key identifying feature.  昆虫翅膀上的双射线标记是关键的识别特征。

近义词:two-rayed, double-rayed
反义词:uniradiate

词性:adjective
词源:From bi- + radiate.  "Bi-" meaning two and "radiate" meaning to emit rays.
