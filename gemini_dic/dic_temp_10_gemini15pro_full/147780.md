overprompt:/ˈoʊvərprɒmpt/| adj.|过度提示的；过度引导的|Providing excessive prompts or guidance, often to the point of hindering independent thought or action.  It implies that the prompting is more than necessary and may be counterproductive.|The teacher overprompted the student, giving away the answer before they had a chance to think.|近义词：oversuggest, overguide, overdirect|反义词:underprompt, undersuggest|adjective
