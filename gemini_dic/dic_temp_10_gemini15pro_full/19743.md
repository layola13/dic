billed:/bɪld/| v. |开账单；宣传；把…列为（节目单等）| to charge someone an amount of money for goods or services; to advertise or describe someone or something in a particular way, especially in order to attract people's attention |  The company billed me $100 for the service.  公司向我收取了100美元的服务费。| synonyms: charged, invoiced, advertised | antonyms: paid, compensated | verb

billed:/bɪld/| adj. |（鸟）有喙的| having a beak | The billed bird pecked at the seeds.  这只长着喙的鸟啄食着种子。 | synonyms: beaked | antonyms: beakless | adjective
