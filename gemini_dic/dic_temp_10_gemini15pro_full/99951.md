internuncius:/ɪntərˈnʌnsiəs/
noun
使者，信使，中间人
a messenger between two parties; a go-between
The internuncius delivered the king's message to the neighboring kingdom.  这位使者将国王的信送到了邻国。
synonyms: messenger, envoy, emissary, legate, mediator, go-between
antonyms: receiver, recipient
