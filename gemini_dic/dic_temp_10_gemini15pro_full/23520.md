brain-drain:/ˈbreɪn dreɪn/
noun
人才流失
The emigration of highly trained or educated people from a particular country.
Many developing countries suffer from brain drain, as their best and brightest leave for better opportunities elsewhere. 许多发展中国家都遭受人才流失的困扰，因为他们最优秀、最聪明的人才都为了更好的机会去了别的地方。
The government introduced new policies to combat brain drain. 政府出台了新的政策来应对人才流失。
近义词:  human capital flight, talent migration, loss of skilled workers
反义词:  brain gain, talent influx
