originates:/əˈrɪdʒɪneɪts/
verb
起源于，发源于
comes from a particular place, time, situation, etc.
The disease is thought to have originated in the tropics.  这种疾病被认为起源于热带地区。

The custom originates from an ancient ritual.  这个习俗起源于一个古老的仪式。

近义词:derive, stem, spring, emanate, proceed
反义词:terminate, end, finish, conclude
词性:v.
