effectuously:/ɪˈfɛktʃuəsli/
adjective
有效地；有力地；显著地
Producing a desired effect; effective.  Successfully fulfilling a purpose or achieving a result.
例句：The medicine worked effectuously to reduce the fever.  这药有效地降低了体温。

例句：She effectuously managed the crisis, preventing further damage. 她有效地控制了危机，防止了进一步的损害。

近义词：effectively, efficiently, potently, successfully
反义词：ineffectually, unsuccessfully, inefficiently
词性：adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *effectuosus, from Latin effectus (“effect, result, accomplishment, performance, execution”) (from efficio (“I bring about, accomplish”), from ex (“out, from”) + facio (“I do, make”)) + -osus (suffix forming adjectives meaning “full of, abounding in, prone to, given to”).
