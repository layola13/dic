bowden:/ˈboʊdən/| n.|鲍登线，鲍登钢丝， Bowden cable|A flexible cable consisting of a closely coiled spiral of high-tensile steel wire running inside a close-fitting flexible outer tube. Used for transmitting motion, especially in control mechanisms.|The Bowden cable connects the gear lever to the transmission.|近义词：cable, wire, control cable|反义词:|名词
