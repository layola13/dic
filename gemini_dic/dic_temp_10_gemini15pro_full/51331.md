detectability:/dɪˌtɛktəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可探测性，可检测性 | The quality of being detectable; the extent to which something can be detected.  |  The detectability of the signal is low due to background noise.  由于背景噪音，信号的可探测性很低。 | synonyms:  discoverability, perceptibility, observability | antonyms:  invisibility, imperceptibility, undetectability | noun

detect:/dɪˈtɛkt/ | v. | 探测，检测，发现 | To discover or identify the presence or existence of something. |  The sensor can detect even small changes in temperature.  传感器可以检测到即使是很小的温度变化。 | synonyms: discover, find, identify, notice, sense, perceive | antonyms: miss, overlook, ignore | verb

detectable:/dɪˈtɛktəbl/ | adj. | 可探测的，可检测的 | Able to be discovered or identified. | The change in color is barely detectable to the naked eye.  肉眼几乎无法察觉到颜色的变化。 | synonyms:  perceptible, noticeable, observable, discernible | antonyms:  imperceptible, unnoticeable, invisible, undetectable | adjective
