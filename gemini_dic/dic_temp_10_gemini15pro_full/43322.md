crammable:/ˈkræməbl/| adj.| 可填塞的，可死记硬背的 | Able to be crammed; capable of being packed tightly. |  The suitcase was so crammable, I managed to fit in all my clothes.  这个手提箱容量很大，我把所有的衣服都塞进去了。| Synonyms: packable, stuffable, squeezable | Antonyms: spacious, roomy | adjective

cram:/kræm/| v. | 塞满，填满；（为考试）突击学习| To force something into a small space; to study intensively for a short time. | He crammed all his clothes into the suitcase. 他把所有的衣服都塞进了手提箱。| Synonyms: stuff, pack, jam, swot (for studying) | Antonyms: unpack, empty, unload | verb
