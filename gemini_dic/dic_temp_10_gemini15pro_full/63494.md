entwinement:/ɪnˈtwaɪnmənt/ | n. |交织，缠绕，纠缠|The act of entwining or the state of being entwined; close interweaving.|例句：The entwining of the two families' histories makes for a fascinating story.  两个家族历史的交织构成一个引人入胜的故事。

例句：The entwining of the vines created a natural archway. 藤蔓的缠绕形成了一个天然的拱门。


|近义词：intertwining, interweaving, entanglement, braiding, plaiting|反义词：separation, disentanglement, unraveling|词性:名词
