illusional:/ɪˈluːʒənəl/
adj.
幻觉的，错觉的
Of, relating to, or characterized by illusion.
例句：
The shimmering heat created an illusional oasis in the desert.
中文：闪烁的热浪在沙漠中制造出一个虚幻的海市蜃楼。

His claims of superpowers were purely illusional.
中文：他声称自己拥有超能力，这纯粹是错觉。

近义词：illusory, deceptive, misleading, fallacious
反义词：real, actual, genuine, true
词性：形容词


词源：
From illusion + -al.  Ultimately from Latin illudere "to mock."
