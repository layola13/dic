overcoated: /ˌoʊvərˈkoʊtɪd/
overcoated| adj.| 覆有外层的，上过涂层的|Covered with an overcoat or a coating.|The pills were overcoated to make them easier to swallow. 这些药丸覆有外层，以便更容易吞咽。|近义词: coated, covered|反义词: bare, uncoated|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From 'over-' (prefix meaning "above, beyond, excessively") + 'coat' (verb meaning "to cover with a layer") + '-ed' (suffix forming past participles and adjectives).
