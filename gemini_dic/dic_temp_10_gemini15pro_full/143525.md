outgrossing:/ˌaʊtˈɡroʊsɪŋ/
| v. | 超越…的票房收入 | Surpassing something else in gross revenue, especially a film surpassing another film's box office earnings. | The new superhero movie is outgrossing all previous films in the franchise.  这部新的超级英雄电影的票房收入超过了该系列之前的所有电影。| Synonyms: exceeding, surpassing, outperforming, outstripping | Antonyms: underperforming, lagging behind | Verb

outgrossing:/ˌaʊtˈɡroʊsɪŋ/
| adj. | (票房)超越的 |  That surpasses something else in gross revenue. | The outgrossing success of the sequel surprised everyone. 这部续集票房上的巨大成功让每个人都感到惊讶。| Synonyms: exceeding, surpassing, outperforming | Antonyms: underperforming | Adjective
