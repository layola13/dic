electroplate:/ɪˈlɛktroʊpleɪt/| vt.|电镀|To coat (a surface) with a metal by electrolysis.|They electroplate the jewelry with gold. 他们用黄金电镀珠宝。|近义词：plate, galvanize|反义词:strip|词性:动词
electroplating:/ɪˈlɛktroʊpleɪtɪŋ/| n.|电镀|The process of coating a metal onto a surface by electrolysis.|Electroplating is commonly used in the automotive industry. 电镀常用于汽车工业。|近义词：plating, galvanization|反义词:N/A|词性:名词
