misgraft: /mɪsˈɡræft/
n. | 移植失败；嫁接失败；不适当的结合；贪污，受贿 | An unsuccessful graft; a graft of tissue that is not accepted by the body; a combination or fusion, especially of ideas or cultures, that is incongruous or unsuccessful; dishonest use of public funds for private gain. | The skin misgraft resulted in infection. 皮肤移植失败导致感染。| 近义词: misjoin, mismatch, graft failure, corruption, bribery | 反义词: successful graft, integration, honesty | 词性: 名词

词源:  "mis-" (Old English, meaning "wrongly, badly") + "graft" (from Old French "graffe," a cutting or slip for grafting).
