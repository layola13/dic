blotchily:/ˈblɑːtʃɪli/
adverb
斑驳地，污渍斑斑地
covered with blotches; unevenly colored; in a blotchy manner.
The paint was applied blotchily, resulting in an uneven finish. 油漆涂抹得斑斑驳驳，导致表面不均匀。
Her face was blotchily red from the sunburn.  她的脸被晒得红一块白一块的。
近义词: unevenly, patchily, irregularly
反义词: evenly, smoothly, uniformly
词性: adverb

blotchy:/ˈblɑːtʃi/
adjective
有斑点的，污迹斑斑的
marked with blotches; unevenly colored.
The wall had a blotchy appearance due to dampness.  由于潮湿，墙壁呈现出斑驳的外观。
The rash on his skin was blotchy and itchy. 他皮肤上的皮疹一块一块的，很痒。
近义词: spotted, stained, mottled
反义词: clear, unblemished, spotless
词性: adjective

词源:  From blotch + -y/-ily.  Blotch itself likely originates from a blend of "bleak" and "botch".
