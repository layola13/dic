mesodermic: /ˌmezəˈdɜːrmɪk/ | adj. | 中胚层的 | Relating to or arising from the mesoderm (the middle layer of cells or tissue of an embryo in early development). | The mesodermic layer gives rise to muscles and connective tissue. 中胚层会发展成肌肉和结缔组织。|  The scientist studied the mesodermic development of the frog embryo. 科学家研究了青蛙胚胎的中胚层发育。 | synonymous: mesodermal | antonymous: ectodermic, endodermic | adjective
