crusade:/kruːˈseɪd/| n. vi.| 运动，斗争；改革运动；十字军东征|A vigorous campaign for political, social, or religious change; any of the military expeditions undertaken by European Christians in the 11th, 12th, and 13th centuries to recover the Holy Land from the Muslims.|例句：She launched a crusade against abortion. 她发起了一场反对堕胎的运动。|近义词：campaign, drive, movement, struggle|反义词：compromise, surrender|noun

crusade:/kruːˈseɪd/| vi.| 参加改革运动；从事运动；投身正义运动|To engage in a crusade.|例句：They crusaded for better schools. 他们为争取更好的学校而奋斗。|近义词：campaign, fight, strive|反义词：yield, submit|verb
