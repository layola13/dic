filigreeing:/ˈfɪlɪˌɡriːɪŋ/
| n. | 花丝;  精细的装饰 | The act of creating or decorating with filigree.  Intricate and delicate ornamentation. | The artisan spent hours filigreeing the silver locket.  工匠花了几个小时在银盒上做花丝。 | 近义词： ornamentation, decoration, embellishment, adornment | 反义词: plainness, simplicity | 词性: noun (gerund/present participle)

filigree:/ˈfɪlɪˌɡriː/
| n. | 花丝; 精细的金属丝制品 | Ornamental work of fine (typically gold or silver) wire formed into delicate tracery. |  The antique necklace was made of gold filigree. 这条古董项链是用金花丝制成的。 | 近义词： tracery, lacework | 反义词: solid, plain | 词性: noun

filigree:/ˈfɪlɪˌɡriː/
| v. | 用花丝装饰 | To decorate with filigree. | The jeweler filigreed the ring with tiny gold wires.  珠宝商用细小的金丝装饰戒指。| 近义词： adorn, decorate, embellish, ornament | 反义词: simplify, strip | 词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *filigrana*, from *filo* "thread" (from Latin *filum*) + *grana* "grain" (from Latin *granum*).
