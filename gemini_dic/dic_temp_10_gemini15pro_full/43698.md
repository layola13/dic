credited-to:/ˈkrɛdɪtɪd tuː/
|adj.|归于；记入…的帐户；|Attributed to, assigned to, or recognized as belonging to someone or something.  It indicates the source or origin of something, often used in financial contexts or to acknowledge authorship or contribution.|This invention is credited to Thomas Edison. 这项发明归功于托马斯·爱迪生。|Synonyms: attributed to, assigned to, owing to|Antonyms: debited from|adjective
