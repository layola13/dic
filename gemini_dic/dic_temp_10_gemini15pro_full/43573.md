creaked:/kriːkt/ /kriːkd/| v.|发出咯吱声，嘎吱作响|to make a harsh, high-pitched sound, typically when a wooden object rubs against another or when a hinge moves.|The old door creaked open.  旧门嘎吱一声打开了。|The stairs creaked under his weight. 楼梯在他重压下嘎吱作响。|近义词：squeaked, groaned, grated|反义词:silent, quiet|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Middle English *creken*, of imitative origin.
