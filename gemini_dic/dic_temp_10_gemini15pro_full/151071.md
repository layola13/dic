parapodial:/ˌpærəˈpoʊdiəl/
adjective|疣足的，侧足的|Relating to or resembling a parapodium, a paired lateral outgrowth on each segment of many annelids and other invertebrates, used for locomotion, respiration, or other functions.|The parapodial gills of the marine worm are brightly colored.|近义词:lateral, appendicular|反义词:axial, medial|词性:adjective

parapodium:/ˌpærəˈpoʊdiəm/
noun|疣足，侧足|A paired lateral outgrowth on each segment of many annelids and other invertebrates, used for locomotion, respiration, or other functions.|The marine worm uses its parapodia to crawl along the seabed.|近义词:appendage, lateral outgrowth|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps body segment)|词性:noun
