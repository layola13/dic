parch:/pɑːrtʃ/ /pɑːrtʃ/| v. n.|烤干，烘干; (使)极渴|To make or become very dry, especially because of heat; to make or become extremely thirsty.|The sun parched the earth. 太阳烤干了大地。|Synonyms: dry, dehydrate, scorch, sear|Antonyms: soak, drench, moisturize|verb

parch:/pɑːrtʃ/ /pɑːrtʃ/| n.|焦土; 干旱|An area of extremely dry land.|The travelers crossed a vast, desolate parch. 旅行者穿过了一片广阔荒凉的焦土。|Synonyms: desert, wasteland|Antonyms: oasis, wetland|noun
