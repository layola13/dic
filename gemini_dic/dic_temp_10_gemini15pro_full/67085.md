exploiter:/ɪkˈsplɔɪtər/| n.|剥削者，利用者|Someone who takes unfair advantage of a situation or person for their own benefit.|The company was accused of being an exploiter of cheap labor.  这家公司被指控剥削廉价劳动力。|Example:  Ruthless exploiters of the poor must be brought to justice.  无情剥削穷人的人必须被绳之以法。|Synonyms: abuser, user, profiteer|Antonyms: benefactor, philanthropist|noun


exploit:/ɪkˈsplɔɪt/ | n. vt.|功绩，勋绩；利用，剥削|A bold or daring feat; to make full use of a resource or situation, sometimes in a way considered unfair or unethical.|His wartime exploits were legendary. 他的战时功绩是传奇性的。 The company exploited its workers by paying them low wages.  这家公司通过支付低工资来剥削工人。|Synonyms: accomplishment, feat, utilize, manipulate|Antonyms: neglect, ignore|noun, verb
