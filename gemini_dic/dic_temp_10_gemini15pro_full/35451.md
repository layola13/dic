co-unite:/ˌkoʊ juˈnaɪt/
verb
联合；合并
To unite together; to join in union.
The two groups co-united to form a stronger organization.  两个团体联合起来组成了一个更强大的组织。
The workers co-united to demand better wages. 工人们联合起来要求更高的工资。
Synonyms: combine, merge, amalgamate, unify
Antonyms: divide, separate, disunite
词性: 动词

词源:
From co- + unite. Co- is a prefix meaning "together," "jointly," "mutually," related to Latin *kom (source also of con-). Unite comes from Old French unit, past participle of unir "to make one, join, unite," from Latin unire "to unite, join together," from unus "one."
