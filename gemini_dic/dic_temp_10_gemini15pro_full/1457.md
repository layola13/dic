acephalocyst: /ˌeɪˌsefəloʊˈsɪst/ /ˌeɪˌsefələˈsɪst/ | n. | 无头蚴，无头囊虫 | An immature tapeworm larva, specifically of the genus *Echinococcus*, that lacks a scolex (head) and develops into a hydatid cyst.  It is the stage that infects intermediate hosts, such as humans. | The acephalocyst grows within the intermediate host and can cause serious health problems. 无头蚴在中间宿主体内生长，会导致严重的健康问题。

The doctor diagnosed an acephalocyst in the patient's liver. 医生诊断出病人肝脏内有无头蚴。| 近义词: hydatid cyst, larval cyst | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "adult tapeworm") | 词性: noun
