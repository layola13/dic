affirmativeness:/əˌfɜːrmətɪvnəs/
n.
肯定；坚定；积极性
The quality or state of being affirmative; positive assertion.
She spoke with great affirmativeness about the future of the company. 她非常肯定地谈到了公司的未来。
近义词:positivity, assertiveness, confidence
反义词:negativity, hesitancy, doubt
词性:名词

affirmative:/əˈfɜːrmətɪv/
adj.
肯定的；同意的；积极的
Expressing agreement or consent; positive.
He gave an affirmative answer to the question. 他对这个问题给出了肯定的回答。
近义词:positive, approving, consenting
反义词:negative, dissenting, opposing
词性:形容词
