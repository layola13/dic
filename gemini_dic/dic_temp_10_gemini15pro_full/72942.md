fonge:/fɒndʒ/ /fɑːndʒ/ | n. | fungus（复数）; 真菌（复数）| A general term for a large group of eukaryotic organisms that includes microorganisms such as yeasts and molds, as well as the more familiar mushrooms. | Example 1: The fonges were growing on the damp wall.  墙上长满了真菌。
Example 2:  Various fonges can cause diseases in plants and animals.  各种各样的真菌会导致动植物疾病。 |Synonyms: fungi, mushroom, mold, yeast| Antonyms:  None (There isn't a direct opposite of fungus.) | Noun
