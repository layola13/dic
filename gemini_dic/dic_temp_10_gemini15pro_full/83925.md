half-carried:/ˌhɑːf ˈkærid/ | adj. | 半扶半抱的，勉强被带走的 | Partly supported and carried, as if the person or thing being carried is only partially able to support their own weight. |  He was half-carried, half-dragged out of the burning building. 他半扶半抱地被人从燃烧的建筑物里拖了出来。|  The injured hiker was half-carried down the mountain. 受伤的徒步旅行者被半扶半抱地抬下了山。 | 近义词: partially carried, assisted | 反义词: fully carried, independent | 词性: 形容词

half:/hæf/ | n. adj. adv. | 一半，半个 | One of two equal or approximately equal parts of a divisible whole. | I ate half the pizza. 我吃了一半的披萨。 | 近义词: moiety, one-half | 反义词: whole, double | 词性: 名词，形容词，副词

carry:/ˈkæri/ | v. n. |  搬运，携带，传送 | To support and move (someone or something) from one place to another. |  She carried the baby in her arms. 她抱着婴儿。 | 近义词: transport, convey, bear | 反义词: drop, leave | 词性: 动词，名词

词源：

"half-" 源自古英语 "healf,"  意为“一半”。
"-carried" 是由动词 "carry" 的过去分词形式 "carried" 构成，"carry" 源自古北法语 "carier," 最终源自拉丁语 "carrus"，意为“车，马车”。
