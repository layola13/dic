handcuffed:/ˈhændkʌft/| adj.|戴上手铐的，被铐住的|Being restrained with handcuffs.  The suspect was handcuffed and taken into custody.|例句：The handcuffed prisoner was led into the courtroom.  戴着手铐的囚犯被带进了法庭。

例句：She felt handcuffed by the strict rules. 她感觉被严格的规定束缚住了。|近义词：manacled, shackled, restrained|反义词:freed, liberated, unrestrained|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "handcuff" (n.) + "-ed". "Handcuff" is a compound of "hand" and "cuff".  "Cuff" originally referred to a sleeve or band at the wrist. The sense of a restraining device developed later.
