domestication:/ˌdəˌmɛstɪˈkeɪʃən/ |n.|驯化，驯养|The process of taming wild animals or plants and making them suitable for human use.|The domestication of the dog began thousands of years ago. 狗的驯化始于几千年前。| 近义词: taming, cultivation|反义词: wildness, feralization| Noun

domestication:/dəˌmɛstɪˈkeɪʃən/ |n.| 教化; 驯服|The process of making someone or something more civilized or controlled.|The domestication of children is a very important part of education. 对儿童的教化是教育中非常重要的一部分。|近义词:civilizing,  taming|反义词:  wildness, savagery| Noun
