noncommunicatively:/ˌnɑːnkəˈmjuːnɪkətɪvli/ | adv. | 不善交际地，沉默寡言地 | In a manner that avoids or lacks communication; without expressing oneself verbally or nonverbally.  | He sat noncommunicatively in the corner, refusing to engage in conversation.  他沉默寡言地坐在角落里，拒绝参与谈话。| Synonym: silently, taciturnly, unresponsively | Antonym: communicatively, expressively, talkatively |  词性: 副词

noncommunicative:/ˌnɑːnkəˈmjuːnɪkətɪv/ | adj. | 不爱交际的，沉默寡言的 | Not inclined to communicate; reserved or uncommunicative in speech or manner. | He was a noncommunicative child, preferring to observe rather than participate. 他是一个不爱说话的孩子，喜欢观察而不是参与。| Synonym: reserved, reticent, taciturn | Antonym: communicative, talkative, outgoing | 词性: 形容词
