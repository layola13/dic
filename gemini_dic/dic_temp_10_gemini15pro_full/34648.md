词源:
From Cleopatra (a Greek name ultimately derived from kleos "glory" + pater "father"), the name of the famous Egyptian queen. The plural form, Cleopatras, is used to refer to multiple individuals who share qualities reminiscent of Cleopatra, or multiple representations of her in art and literature.
