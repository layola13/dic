lekgotla: /ləˈxɔtlɑː/
noun
南非的部落议会，会议；协商，讨论
a traditional Sotho/Tswana word referring to a tribal council or meeting; a discussion or consultation.
The village elders held a lekgotla to discuss the drought. 村里的长老们举行了一次部落议会来讨论干旱问题。
The government held a lekgotla with business leaders. 政府与商界领袖举行了一次协商会议。
Synonyms: meeting, council, assembly, gathering, conference, discussion, consultation
Antonyms:  disagreement, dispute
