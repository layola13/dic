incrimination:/ɪnˌkrɪmɪˈneɪʃn/
n.
控告；incriminating；归罪；罪责；牵连
The act of accusing someone of a crime or wrongdoing.
His incrimination of his neighbor in the theft led to an arrest. 他指控他的邻居偷窃导致了逮捕。
近义词：accusation, indictment, charge, imputation
反义词：exoneration, acquittal, vindication
