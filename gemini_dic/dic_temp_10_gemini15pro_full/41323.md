corers:/ˈkɔːrərz/
n.
去核器;取芯工具
Tools used to remove the cores from fruits or vegetables.
She used apple corers to prepare the pie filling. 她用苹果去核器准备馅饼馅料。
The lab technician used a special corer to extract a sample from the ice core.  实验室技术员使用一种特殊的取芯工具从冰芯中提取样本。

近义词:pitters, seed removers
反义词:(none specific - perhaps fillers, stuffers if considering the opposite action)
词性:noun
