awakener:/əˈweɪkənər/| n. |唤醒者；觉醒者|Something or someone that awakens a person or thing. |The rooster's crow is a common awakener in the countryside. 公鸡的啼叫是农村常见的唤醒者。

|The smell of coffee was my awakener this morning.  今天早上咖啡的味道唤醒了我。|近义词：alarm, rouser, stimulator|反义词：soporific, narcotic|noun
