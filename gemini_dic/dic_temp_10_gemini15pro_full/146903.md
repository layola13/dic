overinstructed: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈstrʌktɪd/
adjective
过度指导的；被给予过多指示的
Having been given too many instructions or too much guidance, often to the point of confusion or inefficiency.
The employees felt overinstructed and micromanaged, hindering their creativity and productivity.  员工们感到被过度指导和微观管理，阻碍了他们的创造力和生产力。
The overinstructed child struggled to complete the simple task, overwhelmed by the numerous steps. 这个被过度指导的孩子难以完成简单的任务，被众多的步骤弄得不知所措。

近义词: overmanaged, overscripted, overdirected
反义词: underinstructed, unguided


词性: adjective
词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "instruct" (verb meaning "to teach; to direct") + "-ed" (suffix forming past participles and adjectives)
