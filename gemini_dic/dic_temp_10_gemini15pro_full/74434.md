franciums:/ˈfrænsɪəmz/
n.
franciums（钫）
A radioactive chemical element; the most electropositive of all the elements.
Francium is a highly radioactive alkali metal. 钫是一种高放射性的碱金属。
Francium is the heaviest alkali metal and exists only in short-lived radioactive forms. 钫是最重的碱金属，并且只以寿命很短的放射性形式存在。
synonym: alkali metal, radioactive element
antonym: stable element
noun
