correlate:/ˈkɔːrəleɪt/ /ˈkɔːrəˌleɪt/ | v. n. |使…相互关联；相关联；相关物/人| To establish a mutual or reciprocal relation between. To have a mutual relationship or connection, in which one thing affects or depends on another.|  The study correlates smoking with lung cancer. 这项研究表明吸烟与肺癌有关联。| synonyms: connect, relate, link, tie in | antonyms: separate, disconnect, distinguish | verb

correlate:/ˈkɔːrəleɪt/ /ˈkɔːrəˌleɪt/ | n. |相关联的事物；相关的人|  A person or thing that is analogous or reciprocally related to another.|  He is a correlate of the CEO. 他是首席执行官的关联人士。| synonyms: counterpart, parallel, equivalent | antonyms: opposite, contrast | noun
