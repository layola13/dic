off-load:/ˌɒfˈləʊd/ /ˌɔːfˈloʊd/| v. n.|卸货，卸载；摆脱，转嫁|To remove a load from something, or to get rid of something unwanted by passing it to someone else. |The ship off-loaded its cargo at the port. 这艘船在港口卸货。|Synonyms: unload, discharge, transfer, unburden|Antonyms: load, burden|verb

off-load:/ˌɒfˈləʊd/ /ˌɔːfˈloʊd/| n.|卸货，卸载|The act of removing a load from something. |The off-load of the cargo took several hours. 卸货花了几个小时。|Synonyms: unloading, discharge|Antonyms: loading|noun
