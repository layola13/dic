nonprobability:/ˌnɑːnˌprɑːbəˈbɪləti/ | n. |非概率性；非概率抽样|  The lack of random selection makes this a nonprobability sampling method.  这种缺乏随机选择的方法使其成为非概率抽样法。 | Example: This study uses a nonprobability sampling method, specifically convenience sampling. 这项研究使用非概率抽样方法，特别是方便抽样。| Synonym: nonrandom, unscientific sampling | Antonym: probability, random sampling | noun
