amphiblastic:/ˌæmfɪˈblæstɪk/ | adj. | 两极分裂的，两端分裂的 |  Relating to or exhibiting amphiblasty, a type of cleavage in which the first few cell divisions are complete and roughly equal, but later divisions are incomplete at the vegetal pole, resulting in a mass of yolk-rich cells. |  The amphiblastic cleavage pattern is common in some mollusks.  两极分裂的卵裂模式在一些软体动物中很常见。 | holoblastic | meroblastic | adjective
