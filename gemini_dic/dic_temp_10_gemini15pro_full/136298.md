nonrepression:/ˌnɒnrɪˈprɛʃən/ | n. | 不压制，不抑制 | The absence of repression, especially of thoughts or feelings.  It implies allowing natural impulses or desires to be expressed freely. |  The therapist encouraged nonrepression as a path to emotional healing. 治疗师鼓励不压制情绪，将其作为情绪治愈的途径。

The artist's work was characterized by a spirit of nonrepression and uninhibited creativity. 这位艺术家的作品的特点是不压制和不受约束的创造力。 | 近义词: freedom, liberation, uninhibitedness,  permissiveness | 反义词: repression, suppression, inhibition, constraint | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + repression (from Latin repressio, meaning "a pressing back" or "checking").
