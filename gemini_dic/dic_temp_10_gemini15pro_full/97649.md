inheritrix: /ɪnˈherɪtrɪks/ | n. | 女继承人 | A female who inherits property or a title. |  She is the inheritrix of a vast fortune. 她继承了一大笔财产。|  The inheritrix to the throne is still a child. 王位继承人还是个孩子。| 近义词: heiress | 反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly disinherited person) | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English enheritrice, from Anglo-Norman and Old French enheretriz, from Medieval Latin inheritrix, feminine of inheritor. Ultimately from Latin in- + heres (“heir”).
