chemigraphy:/kɛˈmɪɡrəfi/
noun
化学制版法；化学刻印术
A process of etching metal printing plates chemically.
Chemigraphy allowed for finer details in the print than traditional engraving. 化学制版法使得印刷品比传统雕刻拥有更精细的细节。
The artist experimented with chemigraphy to achieve unique textures. 这位艺术家尝试用化学制版法来获得独特的纹理。
Synonyms: photoengraving, etching
Antonyms: N/A
