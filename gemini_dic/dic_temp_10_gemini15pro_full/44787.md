|近义词: crispest, brittlest|反义词: softest, chewiest|词性:形容词|

crunchy:/ˈkrʌntʃi/| adj.|嘎嘣脆的，松脆的|Pleasantly crisp or brittle. |These chips are perfectly crunchy. 这些薯片非常脆。|
|近义词: crisp, brittle|反义词: soft, chewy|词性:形容词|
