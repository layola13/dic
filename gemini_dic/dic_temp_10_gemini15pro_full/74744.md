freethinking:/ˈfriːˈθɪŋkɪŋ/
adjective
自由思考的
Characterized by independent thought; not bound by conventional or traditional beliefs.
She was known for her freethinking views on religion. 她以其对宗教的自由思考的观点而闻名。
近义词:unconventional, independent, open-minded, liberal, progressive
反义词:conventional, orthodox, dogmatic, conservative
词性:adjective


freethinkings: /ˈfriːˈθɪŋkɪŋz/
noun
自由思想
The thoughts or opinions of a freethinker.  This plural form is less common.
His freethinkings often put him at odds with the more traditional members of society. 他的自由思想经常使他与社会中更传统的人格格不入。
近义词:unconventional ideas, unorthodox beliefs, independent thoughts
反义词:conventional wisdom, orthodox doctrines, dogma
词性:noun


词源:
From free + thinking.  "Free" in this context means "free from traditional or conventional constraints."
