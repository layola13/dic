misfocuses:/ˌmɪsˈfoʊkəsɪz/
|v.|失焦，注意力不集中|To fail to focus correctly, either literally (as with a camera lens) or figuratively (as with one's attention).|The photographer misfocused the lens, resulting in a blurry picture. 摄影师的镜头失焦了，导致照片模糊不清。|The company misfocused its marketing efforts and failed to reach its target audience. 该公司营销工作重点错误，未能触及目标受众。|Synonyms: blur, confuse, distract |Antonyms: focus, concentrate, sharpen|verb
