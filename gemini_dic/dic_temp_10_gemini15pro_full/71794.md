fled:/flɛd/
verb
逃走，逃离
past tense and past participle of flee
The family fled the country during the war.  这家人在战争期间逃离了这个国家。
He fled the scene of the accident. 他逃离了事故现场。
近义词:escaped, ran away, absconded
反义词:remained, stayed, faced

词性:verb
