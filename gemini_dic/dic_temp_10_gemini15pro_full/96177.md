indescribably:/ˌɪndɪˈskraɪbəbli/
adverb
难以形容地；无法描述地
so extraordinary as to be impossible to describe
The sunset was indescribably beautiful.  日落美得难以形容。

The pain was indescribably intense. 疼痛剧烈得无法形容。

Synonym:  unutterably, inexpressibly, ineffably
Antonym: describably, expressibly

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + describable (capable of being described) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Describable ultimately comes from the Latin word "describere" meaning "to write down" or "to copy."
