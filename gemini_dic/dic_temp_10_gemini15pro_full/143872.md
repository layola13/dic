outpoise:/ˌaʊtˈpɔɪz/| v.|胜过，超过（重量或重要性）| To outweigh or surpass in weight, importance, or influence.|  Her confidence outpoised her nervousness during the presentation.  她在演讲中，自信胜过了紧张。|近义词：outweigh, surpass, exceed, eclipse|反义词:  be inferior to, be outweighed by|verb
