backboneless:/ˈbækˌboʊnlɪs/
adjective
无脊椎的；没有骨气的
Lacking a backbone; invertebrate.  Figuratively, lacking in courage or firmness.
A jellyfish is a backboneless creature. 水母是一种无脊椎动物。
He was backboneless and gave in to their demands. 他没有骨气，屈服于他们的要求。
invertebrate, spineless
vertebrate, courageous
