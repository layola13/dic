alepidote:/əˈlɛpɪdoʊt/
adjective
无鳞片的，无鳞的
Lacking scales or lepidote structures, especially referring to plants or their parts.
The alepidote rhododendron is known for its smooth leaves.  这种无鳞片的杜鹃花以其光滑的叶子而闻名。
The botanist identified the plant as an alepidote species due to the absence of scales on the buds.  植物学家根据芽上没有鳞片而将这种植物鉴定为无鳞片物种。
近义词:scaleless, smooth
反义词:lepidote, scaly
词性:adjective
