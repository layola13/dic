committed: /kəˈmɪtɪd/ | adj. | 坚定的，尽心尽力的，承诺的 | dedicated, devoted, pledged, obligated | She is a committed teacher who always puts her students first. 她是一位尽职尽责的老师，总是把学生放在第一位。 |  He's committed to finishing the project on time. 他致力于按时完成这个项目。 | 近义词: dedicated, devoted, loyal, pledged | 反义词: uncommitted, indifferent, apathetic | 词性: 形容词

committed: /kəˈmɪtɪd/ | v. | 犯（罪），做（错事），承诺，投入 | perform, perpetrate, pledge, devote | He committed a serious crime. 他犯下了严重的罪行。 |  She committed herself to helping the poor. 她承诺帮助穷人。 | 近义词: perpetrate, perform, pledge, devote | 反义词: abstain, refrain | 词性: 动词


词源:  From the Latin "committere," meaning "to join together, entrust, combine, or perpetrate." It's formed from "com-" (together) and "mittere" (to send, put).
