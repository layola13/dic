baselessness:/ˈbeɪsləsnəs/| n. |无根据，无基础|The baselessness of the accusations was clear to everyone. 指控的毫无根据对每个人来说都很清楚。|例句：The report exposed the baselessness of the rumors.  这份报告揭露了谣言的无稽之谈。

例句：His argument was based on the baselessness of her claims. 他的论点是基于她主张的毫无根据。


|近义词：groundlessness, unsubstantiated, unfounded, falsity|反义词: validity, truth, soundness, foundation|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From baseless + -ness. Baseless from base (lacking foundation) + -less (without).  Ultimately from the Latin basis (foundation).
