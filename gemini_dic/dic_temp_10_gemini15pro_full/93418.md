illogically:/ɪˈlɒdʒɪkli/ /ɪˈlɑːdʒɪkli/
adverb|不合逻辑地；无理性地|in a way that does not follow the rules of logic; unreasonably|He behaved illogically, making decisions based on emotion rather than reason. 他举止不合逻辑， Entscheidungen basierend auf Emotionen statt auf Vernunft. |Synonyms: irrationally, absurdly, senselessly, unreasonably, foolishly|Antonyms: logically, rationally, reasonably, sensibly, wisely|词性: 副词
