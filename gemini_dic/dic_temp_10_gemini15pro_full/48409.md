dedicators:/ˈdɛdɪˌkeɪtərz/
n.
奉献者；献身者；题献者
Those who dedicate something, often a book or work of art, to someone else.  Those who commit themselves to a particular purpose or cause.
This book is for all the dedicators of their lives to the pursuit of knowledge.  这本书献给所有致力于追求知识的人。
The dedicators of the new hospital worked tirelessly to raise funds. 新医院的捐赠者不知疲倦地筹集资金。

synonyms:contributors, donors, supporters, patrons
antonyms:opponents, adversaries, detractors
noun
