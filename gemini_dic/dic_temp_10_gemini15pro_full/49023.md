degravation: /ˌdiːɡrəˈveɪʃən/
n.
减轻，缓和
the lessening of the seriousness or intensity of something unpleasant
The medication provided some degravation of his symptoms.  这种药物减轻了他的症状。

近义词: alleviation, mitigation, relief, reduction
反义词: aggravation, exacerbation, intensification

词性: noun

词源:  From de- + aggravation, from Late Latin aggravationem, from aggravare "to make heavier," from Latin ad- "to" + gravis "heavy."
