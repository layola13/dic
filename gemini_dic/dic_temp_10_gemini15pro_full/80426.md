gonangium:/ɡoʊˈnændʒiəm/| n. |生殖体，芽鞘，胞囊|A gonangium is a reproductive polyp found in some hydrozoans, a type of aquatic invertebrate. It produces medusa buds, which develop into free-swimming medusae.|The gonangium releases medusae into the water column. 生殖体将水母体释放到水体中。|The researchers observed the development of medusae within the gonangium. 研究人员观察了生殖体内水母体的发育。|近义词：gonophore, reproductive polyp|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Ancient Greek γόνος (gónos, “offspring, seed, generation”) + ἄγγος (ángos, “vessel, container”).
