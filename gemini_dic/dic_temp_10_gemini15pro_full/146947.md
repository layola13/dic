overinterpretations: /ˌoʊvərɪnˌtɜːrprəˈteɪʃənz/ | n. | 过度解读，过度诠释 |  The act of assigning too much meaning or significance to something, often reading into it more than is actually there. |  His overinterpretations of her actions led to unnecessary misunderstandings. 他对她行为的过度解读导致了不必要的误解。 | synonyms: overanalyzing, misconstruing, misreading | antonyms: underestimating, undervaluing | noun
