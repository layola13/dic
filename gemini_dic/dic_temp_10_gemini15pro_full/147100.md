overlimited:/ˌoʊvərˈlɪmɪtɪd/
adj.
过度限制的
restricted excessively; limited too much.
The child's creativity was overlimited by the rigid rules of the school.  这孩子的创造力被学校死板的规定过度限制了。
Overlimited regulations stifled innovation in the industry. 过度限制的规章制度扼杀了该行业的创新。
近义词: restricted, confined, constrained, hampered
反义词: unrestricted, unconstrained, unlimited, free
词性: 形容词

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "limited" (from Latin "limitare," to bound, confine)
