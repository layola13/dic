omnipresential:/ˌɒm.nɪ.prɪˈzen.ʃəl/ | adj. | 无所不在的，遍在的 | Present everywhere at the same time; ubiquitous. | God is often considered omnipresential. 上帝常被认为是无所不在的。

The new surveillance system makes the security team feel omnipresential. 新的监控系统让安保团队感觉无处不在。 | 近义词：ubiquitous, pervasive, all-present | 反义词：localized, limited, restricted | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Latin *omnipraesentialis*, from omni- (“all”) + praesentia (“presence”) + -alis (adjective suffix).
