impassablenesses:/ɪmˈpæsəbəlnəsɪz/
|n.|不可逾越性；无法通行|The impassablenesses of the mountain range made travel difficult.  这山脉的不可逾越性使得旅行变得困难。|The heavy snow added to the impassablenesses of the roads.  大雪增加了道路的不可通行性。|近义词：inaccessibility, impermeability|反义词：accessibility, permeability|词性:名词

Etymology:
From impassable + -ness.  Impassable from im- (not) + passable (able to be passed). Passable ultimately from Latin *passare (to step, walk).
