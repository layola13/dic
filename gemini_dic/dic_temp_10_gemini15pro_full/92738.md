ichthus: /ˈɪkθəs/ | n. | 鱼，鱼形符号 | A symbol of a fish used by early Christians. It is an acronym for the Greek phrase "Ἰησοῦς Χριστός, Θεοῦ Υἱός, Σωτήρ" (Iēsous Christos, Theou Huios, Sōtēr), which translates to "Jesus Christ, God's Son, Savior". |  The ichthus symbol was often found scratched on walls or tombs in the Roman catacombs.  早期基督徒经常在罗马地下墓穴的墙壁或墓碑上刻划鱼形符号。 | synonyms: fish symbol, Christian fish | antonyms: None | noun
