mythologers:/mɪˈθɒlədʒərz/
n.
神话学家；神话研究者
Experts in mythology, or those who study and interpret myths.
The mythologers debated the meaning of the ancient symbols.  神话学家们争论着古代符号的含义。
Mythologers have offered various interpretations of this myth. 神话学家们对这个神话提出了各种各样的解释。
近义词：mythologists, folklorists
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps "literalists" or "historians" in certain contexts)
词性：noun

Etymology:
From "mythology" + "-er" (agent suffix).
"Mythology" comes from Late Latin "mythologia," from Greek "mythologia" "telling of myths," from "mythos" "myth" + "-logia" "speaking, discourse."
