americanization:/əˌmɛrɪkənɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
美国化
The process of making something or someone more American in character or culture.
The americanization of European immigrants in the early 20th century was a complex process.  20世纪初欧洲移民的美国化是一个复杂的过程。

The fast-food industry contributes to the americanization of global diets. 快餐业促进了全球饮食的美国化。

近义词:westernization, assimilation
反义词:localization, indigenization
词性:名词
