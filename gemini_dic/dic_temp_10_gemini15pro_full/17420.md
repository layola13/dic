beams:/biːmz/| n. |光束，横梁|A beam is a ray or shaft of light.|The sun's beams shone through the trees.  太阳的光束穿过树林。|近义词：rays, shafts, girders|反义词: darkness, shadow |noun
beams:/biːmz/| v. |照射，发射，眉开眼笑|To beam is to emit light or to smile radiantly.|Her face beamed with joy. 她的脸上洋溢着喜悦。|近义词：shine, radiate, grin|反义词: frown, scowl |verb
