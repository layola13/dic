cellulite: /ˈsɛljʊlaɪt/| n.|脂肪团，橘皮组织|A localized uneven dimpling of the skin, especially on women's thighs and buttocks, caused by fat deposits.  It refers to the puckered appearance of skin when fatty tissue pushes through the connective tissue beneath it.|She's been exercising to try to reduce the cellulite on her thighs.|Synonyms: orange peel skin, cottage cheese skin|Antonyms: smooth skin|Noun
