baulk:/bɔːk/| v. | 畏缩；犹豫；阻碍；(马等)不肯前进 | He baulked at the idea of going to the dentist. 他对去看牙医的想法感到畏缩。 | 近义词：hesitate, refuse, resist, balk | 反义词: accept, embrace | verb
