oligotokous:/ˌɒlɪɡəˈtəʊkəs/| adj.|寡营养的；贫营养的|Relating to or denoting a lake or other body of water that is relatively low in plant nutrients and containing abundant oxygen in the deeper parts.|The oligotokous lake was pristine and clear.  这个寡营养湖泊原始而清澈。|Lake Superior is an example of an oligotokous lake. 苏必利尔湖是寡营养湖泊的一个例子。|oligotrophic|eutrophic|adjective
