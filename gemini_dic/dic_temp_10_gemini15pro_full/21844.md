bodhisattva:/ˌboʊdiˈsʌtvə/ |n.|菩萨|A person who is able to reach nirvana but delays doing so out of compassion in order to save suffering beings.|The bodhisattva vowed to help all beings escape suffering. 菩萨发愿帮助所有众生脱离苦海。|Synonyms: enlightened being, compassionate one|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
