dauphine:/ˈdɔːfiːn/ (US), /ˈdɔːfiːn/ (UK)| n.|王太子妃; 长太子妃|A dauphine is the wife of the dauphin of France (the heir apparent to the French throne).|Marie Antoinette was the dauphine before she became queen. 玛丽·安托瓦内特在成为王后之前是王太子妃。|Synonyms: crown princess, princess royal|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

dauphin:/ˈdɔːfɪn/ (US), /ˈdɔːfɪn/ (UK)| n.|法国太子|The Dauphin was the eldest son of the King of France and the heir to the throne.|Louis, the Dauphin of France, died before his father. 法国太子路易在他父亲之前去世了。|Synonyms: crown prince, heir apparent|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
