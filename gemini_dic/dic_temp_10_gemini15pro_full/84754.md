handloom:/ˈhændˌluːm/| n.| 手织布|Cloth woven on a handloom.|The market stall was filled with colourful handloom fabrics.  市场摊位上摆满了五颜六色的手织布料。|Synonym: handwoven cloth, handwoven textile |Antonym: machine-made cloth, power loom fabric|noun
