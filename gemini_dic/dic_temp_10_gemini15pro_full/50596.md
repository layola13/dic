deray:/dɪˈreɪ/| v. n.| 耽搁，延迟；骚乱，混乱|To make someone or something late; to delay. A state of disorder or confusion; commotion.|例句：The train was derayed by the heavy snow. 火车因为大雪而延误了。|近义词：delay, postpone, hinder, obstruct, disruption, turmoil|反义词：expedite, hasten, advance, order, calm|词性:动词/名词
