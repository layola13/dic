foramen:/fəˈreɪmən/ | n. |孔， foramen (复数 foramina)|An opening, especially in a bone or covering an organ, through which nerves or vessels pass.|The optic nerve passes through the optic foramen.| aperture, opening, hole, orifice | - | noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *forāmen*, from *forō* ("I bore, pierce").
