navel-gazings:/ˈneɪvəlˌɡeɪzɪŋz/
noun
沉思；内省；自省；顾影自怜
Excessive contemplation of one's own feelings and experiences, especially considered as excessive or unproductive.
He indulged in navel-gazings about his own emotional state. 他沉溺于对自身情绪状态的内省。
近义词：introspection, self-absorption, rumination, contemplation
反义词：extroversion, action, engagement
