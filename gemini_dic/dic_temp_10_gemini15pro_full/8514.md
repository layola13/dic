anticapitalisms:/ˌæntiˈkæpɪtəlɪzəmz/
n.
反资本主义（复数）
Doctrines or movements opposed to capitalism.  These can range from individual criticisms of capitalism's perceived flaws to fully developed systems like socialism and communism.
Many anticapitalisms emerged during the Industrial Revolution.  许多反资本主义思想在工业革命期间出现。
The conference featured speakers representing various anticapitalisms.  这次会议邀请了代表各种反资本主义思想的发言人。
Synonyms:  anti-capitalist movements, socialist movements, communist movements
Antonyms:  pro-capitalism, capitalism, free market ideologies
