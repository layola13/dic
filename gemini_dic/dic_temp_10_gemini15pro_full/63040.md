enravishingly:/ɪnˈrævɪʃɪŋli/
adverb
令人陶醉地，迷人地
in a way that fills you with delight
Her voice was enravishingly beautiful. 她的声音美得令人陶醉。
近义词: captivatingly, charmingly, delightfully, alluringly
反义词: repulsively, unattractively, displeasingly
词性: adverb

词源: From enravish + -ing + -ly.  Enravish comes from the Old French word *enraviss-, present participle of enravir "to seize, snatch away, ravish," from en- "in" + ravir "to seize, snatch," from Latin rapere "to seize."
