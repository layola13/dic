attorney:/əˈtɜːrni/
attorney|n.|律师；代理人|A person legally appointed to act on behalf of another person in business or legal matters.|My attorney will be contacting you shortly. 我的律师很快会联系你。|近义词:lawyer, solicitor, counsel, advocate|反义词:defendant, plaintiff (These aren't true opposites, but represent opposing sides in legal proceedings)
attorney|n.|（尤指美国的）检察官，检查官|A legal officer who represents the state or federal government in legal proceedings.|The district attorney is prosecuting the case. 地区检察官正在起诉此案。|近义词:prosecutor, district attorney|反义词:defense attorney
