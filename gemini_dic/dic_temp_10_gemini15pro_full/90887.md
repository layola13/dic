hunlike 不是一个标准的英文单词。 你可能是想问 unlike。 

unlike: /ʌnˈlaɪk/ | prep. adj. |不像，和...不同 |Different from; not similar to. |例句：Unlike his brother, he is very quiet.  不像他的兄弟，他很安静。|例句：It's unlike her to be late. 她迟到很不寻常。|近义词：dissimilar, different, contrasting |反义词: like, similar, alike |词性: 介词, 形容词
