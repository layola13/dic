focalization:/ˌfoʊkələˈzeɪʃən/| n.|聚焦; 中心化;  |The act or process of focusing or concentrating.  The state of being focused or concentrated. In narrative theory, the perspective through which a story is told. |The focalization of the narrative shifts between the two main characters.  叙事焦点在两个主要人物之间转换。|近义词：focus, concentration, perspective, viewpoint|反义词: diffusion, dispersal |noun
