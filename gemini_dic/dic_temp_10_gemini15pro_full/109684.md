leucosphere: /ˈljuːkəsfɪər/ | n. | 白层；白圈; 白界 | The leucosphere is the inner layer of the Earth's atmosphere, extending from the surface to about 50 kilometers (31 miles) in altitude and characterized by a decrease in temperature with height. |  The leucosphere is where most of Earth's weather occurs.  地球上的大部分天气都发生在白层。| The troposphere is part of the leucosphere. 对流层是白层的一部分。 | 近义词: troposphere, atmosphere | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

词源:  From Greek *leukós* (white) + *sphaîra* (sphere).
