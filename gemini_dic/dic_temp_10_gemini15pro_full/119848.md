mildnesses:/ˈmaɪldnɪsɪz/
n.
温和；轻微；和善；慈祥
the quality of being kind and gentle; the fact of not being severe or extreme
Her mildness and patience with the children were admirable. 她对孩子们温和而有耐心，令人钦佩。
Synonyms: gentleness, leniency, clemency, tenderness
Antonyms: harshness, severity, strictness, cruelty
词性: 名词
