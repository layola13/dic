forced-fed:/fɔːrstˈfɛd/ /fɔːrstˈfɛd/
adjective|强行喂食的；填鸭式的|fed by force, especially excessively or unwillingly|The geese were forced-fed to produce foie gras.|近义词：force-fed, overfed|反义词:underfed|词性:adjective
verb (past participle)|被强行喂食|having been fed by force|The prisoner was forced-fed during the hunger strike.|近义词：force-fed, overfed|反义词:starved|词性:verb
