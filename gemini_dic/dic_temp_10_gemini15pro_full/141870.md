orchidlike:/ˈɔːrkɪdlaɪk/
adjective
像兰花的, 蘭花似的
Resembling an orchid in some way, such as in shape, color, or fragrance.
The delicate, orchidlike flowers bloomed in the spring.  这些精致的、像兰花一样的花朵在春天盛开了。
Her dress was a vibrant purple, almost orchidlike in its richness. 她的裙子是鲜艳的紫色，其 richness 几乎像兰花一样。
近义词:  orchid-shaped, orchidaceous, floral, flowery
反义词: plain, simple, unadorned
词性: adjective
