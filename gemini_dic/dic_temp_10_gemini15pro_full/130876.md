nonarguments:/ˌnɒnˈɑːɡjʊmənts/ | n. | 非论据; 无关论点 | Statements or points that do not serve as valid arguments, lacking logical connection to the conclusion or supporting evidence.  These might be irrelevant statements, emotional appeals, or personal attacks. |  Many of the points raised in the discussion were nonarguments, simply distracting from the main issue. 讨论中提出的许多观点都是非论证，只是偏离了主要问题。

The politician's speech was full of nonarguments and lacked any substantial policy proposals.  这位政治家的演讲充满了无关紧要的论点，缺乏实质性的政策建议。 | 近义词: fallacies, irrelevancies, distractions | 反义词: arguments, evidence, proof | 词性: noun
