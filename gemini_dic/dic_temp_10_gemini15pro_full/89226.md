homeynesses:/ˈhoʊminɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
亲切感；舒适感；温馨感
The quality of being homey; a feeling of comfort, coziness, and familiarity.
例句：
The homeynesses of the small town made her feel welcome.
这个小镇的温馨感让她感到宾至如归。

例句：
She missed the homeynesses of her grandmother's house.
她想念奶奶家的舒适感。

近义词：coziness, comfort, warmth, familiarity
反义词：unfamiliarity, coldness, discomfort, strangeness
词性:名词(plural noun)


ETYMOLOGY->
From homey + -ness. Homey itself comes from home + -y.  Ultimately from Old English *hām meaning "house, dwelling."
