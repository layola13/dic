carpes: /ˈkɑːrpiːz/ | n. |鲤鱼（复数）| Carp (plural).  A freshwater fish of the family Cyprinidae, a very large group of fish that also includes the minnows and barbels. Carp are popular as food fish and are also often kept in ponds and aquariums. |The carpes in the pond were large and colorful. 池塘里的鲤鱼又大又鲜艳。| Carp are a popular game fish. 鲤鱼是一种受欢迎的游钓鱼。| 近义词:fish | 反义词: (无直接反义词) | 词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *carpe, from Old French carpe, from Late Latin carpa, from a Germanic source, from Proto-Germanic *karpō, from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ker- (“to turn, bend”). Cognate with Dutch karper, German Karpfen, Swedish karp.
