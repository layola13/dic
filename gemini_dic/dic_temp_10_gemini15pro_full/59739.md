ecocidal: /ˌiːkəˈsaɪdəl/ | adj. | 生态灭绝的，破坏生态的 | Destructive to the environment, especially in a way that threatens widespread damage or extinction. |  The company's ecocidal practices led to the destruction of the rainforest. 该公司的生态灭绝行为导致了雨林的破坏。

The government was accused of ecocidal policies that prioritized short-term economic gains over environmental protection. 政府被指责实行优先考虑短期经济收益而不是环境保护的生态灭绝政策。 | 近义词：environmentally destructive, ecologically damaging | 反义词：eco-friendly, environmentally sustainable | 词性：形容词

ecocide: /ˈiːkəsaɪd/ | n. | 生态灭绝 | The destruction of the natural environment, especially when deliberate. | The war resulted in widespread ecocide. 战争导致了广泛的生态灭绝。

Rampant deforestation is a form of ecocide.  猖獗的森林砍伐是一种生态灭绝的形式。 | 近义词：environmental destruction, ecological devastation | 反义词：environmental protection, conservation | 词性：名词


词源: eco- (from Greek oikos, meaning "house, dwelling, environment") + -cidal (from Latin -cida, meaning "killer," related to caedere, "to kill").
