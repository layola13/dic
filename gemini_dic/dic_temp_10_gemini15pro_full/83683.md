haggard:/ˈhæɡərd/| adj.|憔悴的，形容枯槁的|Looking exhausted and unwell, especially from fatigue, worry, or suffering.|例句：He looked haggard after his long journey.  他长途旅行后显得憔悴不堪。|例句：The refugees arrived looking tired and haggard.  难民们到达时看起来疲惫不堪，形容枯槁。|近义词：gaunt, drawn, exhausted, worn|反义词：healthy, robust, vibrant|词性:形容词|词源:From Old French *hagard, of unknown origin.|
