brooks:/brʊks/ | v. | 容忍; (brook的第三人称单数) | To tolerate or allow (something). | He will not brook any interference. 他不会容忍任何干涉。| synonyms: tolerate, allow, permit, endure | antonyms: oppose, resist, disallow | verb
