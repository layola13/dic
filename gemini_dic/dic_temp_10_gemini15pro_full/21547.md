bluegrasses:/ˈbluːˌɡræsɪz/| n.|蓝草|A group of perennial grasses of the genus Poa, native to Europe and North America, several species of which are important pasture and lawn grasses.|Kentucky bluegrass is a popular choice for lawns. 肯塔基蓝草是草坪的热门选择。|近义词：fescue, ryegrass|反义词: (No direct antonym for a type of grass)|noun
