nonformulations:/ˌnɒnˌfɔːrmjʊˈleɪʃənz/ | n. | 非公式化; 非制剂化; 未明确表达的想法 |  Ideas or concepts that have not been precisely articulated or developed into formal statements or products. This often refers to plans, theories, or arguments that are still in a nascent stage and lack clear structure. | The meeting consisted largely of nonformulations, with everyone brainstorming but no concrete plans emerging.  会议主要是非正式的讨论，每个人都在集思广益，但没有形成具体的计划。 | synonyms: outlines, drafts, sketches, nascent ideas, inchoate concepts | antonyms: formulations, established theories, defined principles | noun
