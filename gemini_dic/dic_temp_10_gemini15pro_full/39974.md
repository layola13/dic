consubstantiate:/ˌkɒnsəbˈstænʃɪeɪt/ /ˌkɑːnsəbˈstænʃieɪt/| vt.| 同质化，使成为同一物质，(尤指在圣餐中)使(面包和酒)与基督的身体和血液合一 |To unite into one common substance; to become of one and the same substance.|The doctrine of transubstantiation says that the bread and wine are consubstantiated with the body and blood of Christ.  圣餐变体论认为面包和酒与基督的身体和血液同质化了。|Synonyms: combine, unite, merge, fuse|Antonyms: separate, divide, disunite|verb
