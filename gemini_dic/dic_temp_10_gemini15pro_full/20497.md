bivalved:/ˌbaɪˈvælvd/
adjective
双壳的；有两瓣壳的
Having two valves or shells hinged together.
The bivalved mollusk closed its shell tightly.  这只双壳软体动物紧紧地合上了壳。
The oyster is a bivalved creature. 牡蛎是一种双壳类生物。
近义词: two-shelled
反义词: univalve


词性: adjective
