cessant:/ˈsesənt/| adj.|不停的，持续不断的|Characterized by constant or continued action or existence without pause or interruption.|The incessant noise of the city kept him awake all night. 城市不停的噪音让他彻夜未眠。|近义词：continuous, constant, uninterrupted, perpetual, unremitting|反义词：intermittent, occasional, sporadic, ceasing, stopping|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin cessant- ‘ceasing, stopping,’ present participle of cessare ‘to cease.’
