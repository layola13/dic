bargain:/ˈbɑːrɡən/ (US) /ˈbɑːɡɪn/ (UK)| n. vi. |交易；便宜货；协议| something bought or offered for sale much cheaper than its usual price or value; an agreement between two or more people or groups as to what each will do for the other | I got a real bargain on this dress. 我买这条裙子真是捡了个大便宜。| synonyms: deal, agreement, contract | antonyms: rip-off, swindle | noun

bargain:/ˈbɑːrɡən/ (US) /ˈbɑːɡɪn/ (UK)| vi. |讨价还价| to discuss the conditions of a sale, agreement, etc., for example to try and get a lower price | We bargained with the street vendor for a lower price. 我们和街边小贩讨价还价，想让他便宜点。| synonyms: haggle, negotiate | antonyms: accept, concede | verb
