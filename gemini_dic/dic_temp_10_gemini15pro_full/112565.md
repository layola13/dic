lovelock: /ˈlʌvlɒk/ (UK) /ˈlʌvlɑːk/ (US) | n. | 爱情锁，定情锁 | A padlock fastened to a bridge, fence, or similar public fixture by sweethearts to symbolize their everlasting love. |  Example: They fastened a lovelock to the Pont des Arts bridge in Paris. 他们在巴黎艺术桥上挂了一把爱情锁。

Example:  The city had to remove thousands of lovelocks because of the damage they caused to the bridge. 由于爱情锁对桥梁造成的损害，该市不得不拆除了数千把爱情锁。| 近义词: love token, keepsake | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider symbols of broken relationships) | 词性: noun
