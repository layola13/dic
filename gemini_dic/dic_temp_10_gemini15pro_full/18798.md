berry-like:/ˈberiˌlaɪk/| adj.|像浆果的，类似浆果的|Resembling or characteristic of a berry, typically small, round, and juicy.|The plant produces small, berry-like fruits that are edible.  这种植物会结出小的、类似浆果的可食用果实。|The tiny, berry-like formations on the cactus are actually modified stems. 仙人掌上微小的、类似浆果的结构实际上是变态茎。|Synonyms: berry-shaped, bacciform|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, consider using descriptions like "unlike a berry," "not resembling a berry")|adjective
