outpursue:/ˌaʊtpərˈsuː/
verb
超越；在追逐中胜过
to pursue someone or something and overtake or catch them
The police outpursued the escaping criminals. 警察追上了逃跑的罪犯。
近义词：outrun, surpass, overtake
反义词：lag behind, fall behind
词性:v.

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "beyond, exceeding") + "pursue" (from Old French "porsuivre," ultimately from Latin "prosequi" - "to follow").
