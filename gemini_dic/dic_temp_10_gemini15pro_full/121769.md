misquoting:/ˌmɪsˈkwoʊtɪŋ/ | gerund, present participle of misquote | 错误引用，歪曲原意 | The act of repeating something someone said inaccurately, often to change the original meaning. | He's always misquoting Shakespeare, twisting his words to fit his own arguments. 他总是错误地引用莎士比亚，歪曲他的话来适应自己的论点。 |  She was accused of misquoting the scientist in her article. 她被指控在文章中错误引用了这位科学家的话。 | misrepresenting, distorting, falsifying | quoting accurately, representing faithfully | verb (in present participle form)
