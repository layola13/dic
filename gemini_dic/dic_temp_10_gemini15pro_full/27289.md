calvaria: /kælˈvɛəriə/ /kælˈværiə/ | n. | 头盖骨，颅顶 | The skullcap or upper domelike portion of the cranium, which protects the brain. |  The surgeon carefully removed a section of the calvaria to access the brain.  外科医生小心地切除了一块颅顶骨以便进入大脑。

The calvaria is composed of several bones fused together. 颅顶由几块融合在一起的骨头组成。 | 近义词: skullcap, cranium | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Late Latin *calvāria, from Latin calvus (“bald, hairless”).  The term likely arose from the resemblance of a bare skull to a bald head.
