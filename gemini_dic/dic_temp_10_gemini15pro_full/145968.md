overdiverseness:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈvɜːrsnəs/ | n. | 过度多样性；物种过度丰富 |  The state of having too much diversity, especially of species in an ecosystem, which can sometimes lead to instability. | The overdiverseness of the rainforest makes it a challenging environment to study.  雨林的过度多样性使其成为一个具有挑战性的研究环境。| 近义词：hyperdiversity, superrichness | 反义词: lack of diversity, homogeneity | 词性: noun

Etymology: "over" (Old English "ofer," meaning "above, beyond") + "diverse" (from Latin "diversus," past participle of "divertere," meaning "to turn in different directions") + "-ness" (Old English "-nes," forming nouns of state or quality)
