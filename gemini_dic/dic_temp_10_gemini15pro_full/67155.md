exposition:/ˌɛkspəˈzɪʃən/ | n. | 展览；阐述；讲解；暴露 | a comprehensive description and explanation of an idea or theory. | The museum's exposition on ancient Egypt was fascinating.  博物馆关于古埃及的展览令人着迷。| synonyms: exhibition, display, presentation, explanation, elucidation | antonyms: concealment, suppression | noun

exposition:/ˌɛkspəˈzɪʃən/ | n. | 展览；阐述；讲解；暴露 | a clear and detailed explanation. |  His exposition of the theory was both concise and insightful. 他对该理论的阐述既简洁又有见地。| synonyms: explanation, elucidation, clarification | antonyms: obfuscation, confusion | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *expositionem (nominative expositio), noun of action from past-participle stem of exponere "to set forth, expound," from ex- "out" + ponere "to put, place".
