parbuckle:/ˈpɑːrbʌkəl/| n. v. |套拉索；用套拉索吊起|A rope arrangement for raising or lowering cylindrical objects, especially casks.|They parbuckled the heavy barrels onto the ship. 他们用套拉索把沉重的桶吊上了船。|Synonyms: hoist, lift, raise|Antonyms: lower, drop|noun
parbuckle:/ˈpɑːrbʌkəl/| n. v. |套拉索；用套拉索吊起|To raise or lower (something) by means of a parbuckle.|They parbuckled the wrecked car up the embankment.  他们用套拉索把失事的汽车吊上了堤岸。|Synonyms: hoist, lift, raise|Antonyms: lower, drop|verb
