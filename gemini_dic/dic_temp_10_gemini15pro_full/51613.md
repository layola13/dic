develop:/dɪˈvɛləp/ (US), /dɪˈvɛləp/ (UK)| v. vt. vi.|发展，开发，研制，冲洗（照片）|to grow or cause to grow and become more mature, advanced, or elaborate.|例句：The company is developing a new software.  这家公司正在开发一款新软件。|近义词：grow, evolve, progress, expand, advance, mature|反义词:stagnate, decline, regress, shrink, diminish|verb

develop:/dɪˈvɛləp/ (US), /dɪˈvɛləp/ (UK)| n.|发展，开发，研制|an event constituting a new stage in a changing situation.|例句：The latest developments in the case are worrying.  案件的最新进展令人担忧。|近义词：evolution, progress, growth, advancement|反义词:stagnation, decline, regression|noun
