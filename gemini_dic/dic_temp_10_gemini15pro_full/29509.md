catasterism: /kætəˈstɛrɪzəm/ /kætəˈstɛrɪzəm/
n.|星化；星辰化；星座故事|The transformation of a person or object into a constellation.  The telling of myths and legends associated with constellations.|例句：The catasterism of Orion tells of a great hunter placed among the stars.  猎户座的星化讲述了一位伟大的猎人被置于群星之中。

例句：Ancient cultures explained the constellations through elaborate catasterisms. 古代文化通过精心设计的星座故事来解释星座。|近义词：constellation, myth, legend, apotheosis|反义词:|noun
