ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *morcellen, from Old French morceler (“to break into pieces”), from morsel (“a small piece, a bit”), from Vulgar Latin *morsicellus, diminutive of Latin morsus (“bite”), past participle of mordēre (“to bite”).  Doublet of morsel.
