inopportune:/ˌɪnɒpəˈtjuːn/ /ˌɪnɑːpərˈtuːn/| adj.|不合时宜的，不适当的|Occurring at an inconvenient or inappropriate time.|例句：She arrived at an inopportune moment. 她来的不是时候。|例句：He made an inopportune remark that offended everyone. 他说了一句不合时宜的话，冒犯了所有人。|近义词：untimely, inappropriate, awkward, inconvenient|反义词: opportune, timely, appropriate, convenient|词性:形容词

词源:
From Latin *inopportunus*, from in- (“not”) + opportunus (“convenient, suitable”).
