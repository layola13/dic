convallaria: /ˌkɒnvəˈleəriə/ (US) /ˌkɒnvəˈlɛːriə/ (UK) | n. | 铃兰; 山谷百合 | A genus of flowering plants in the family Asparagaceae, containing the single species Convallaria majalis, commonly known as lily of the valley. It is a sweetly scented, highly poisonous woodland flowering plant that is native throughout the cool temperate Northern Hemisphere in Asia, and Europe. | The convallaria blooms in spring and has small, white, bell-shaped flowers. 铃兰在春天开花，有白色的小钟形花朵。

The delicate fragrance of convallaria filled the air. 空气中弥漫着铃兰的清香。 | 近义词: lily of the valley | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
