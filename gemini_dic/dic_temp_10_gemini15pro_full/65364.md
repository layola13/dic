eulogy: /ˈjuːlədʒi/ (US), /ˈjuːlədʒi/ (UK)
n.
赞颂；颂词；悼词
a speech or piece of writing that praises someone or something highly, typically someone who has just died
例句：He delivered a moving eulogy at his father's funeral.  他在父亲的葬礼上发表了感人的悼词。

例句：The book is a eulogy to the natural beauty of the countryside. 这本书是对乡村自然美景的赞歌。

近义词：tribute, praise, encomium, panegyric
反义词：criticism, condemnation, denunciation
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin eulogia "praise," from Greek eulogia "praise, blessing, a speaking well of," from eu- "well" + -logia "speaking," related to legein "to speak."
