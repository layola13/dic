abducted: /æbˈdʌktɪd/
verb
绑架的，劫持的
Taken away illegally by force or deception; kidnapped.
The millionaire's son was abducted from his boarding school.  这位百万富翁的儿子从寄宿学校被绑架了。

The rebels abducted several foreign aid workers. 叛军绑架了几名外国援助人员。

近义词: kidnapped, snatched, seized, captured
反义词: released, freed
词性: verb (past participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *abductus*, past participle of *abducere*  "to lead away," from *ab* "away" + *ducere* "to lead."


USAGE->
一般现在时:
He abducts people for ransom. = 他绑架人勒索赎金。
She abducts children from the playground. = 她从操场上绑架孩子。

一般过去时:
The police investigated who abducted the child. = 警察调查了谁绑架了孩子。
They abducted the businessman and demanded a large sum of money. = 他们绑架了商人并索要一大笔钱。

现在完成时:
The authorities have abducted several political dissidents. = 当局绑架了几名政治异议人士。
Someone has abducted the witness before the trial. = 有人在审判前绑架了证人。

过去完成时:
The kidnappers had abducted the girl before the police arrived. = 绑匪在警察到达之前绑架了女孩。
They had abducted the diplomat and held him hostage for weeks. = 他们绑架了外交官并将其扣为人质数周。

将来时:
If you don't pay, they will abduct your daughter. = 如果你不付钱，他们会绑架你的女儿。
The terrorists will abduct more people if their demands are not met. = 如果他们的要求得不到满足，恐怖分子将绑架更多的人。

被动语态:
The child was abducted from the park yesterday. = 昨天孩子从公园被绑架了。
Several hostages have been abducted by the terrorist group. = 几名人质被恐怖组织绑架。

疑问句:
Was he abducted by aliens? = 他是被外星人绑架了吗？
Has anyone been abducted from this town before? = 以前有人从这个镇上被绑架过吗？

否定句:
They didn't abduct the mayor, but his daughter. = 他们没有绑架市长，而是绑架了他的女儿。
She wasn't abducted, she ran away. = 她没有被绑架，她逃跑了。
