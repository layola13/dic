declamator:/ˈdɛkləmeɪtər/| n.|雄辩家，朗诵家，高谈阔论者|A person who speaks or recites something in a theatrical or rhetorical style.|The declamator captivated the audience with his booming voice and dramatic gestures.  这位雄辩家以其洪亮的声音和戏剧性的姿态吸引了观众。|例句：He was a renowned declamator, known for his powerful delivery of speeches. 他是一位著名的演说家，以其强有力的演讲而闻名。

例句：The declamator's performance was met with thunderous applause. 这位朗诵家的表演赢得了雷鸣般的掌声。|近义词：orator, speaker, rhetorician|反义词:listener, audience|词性:noun
