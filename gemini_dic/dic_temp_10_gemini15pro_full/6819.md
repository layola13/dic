anarthropoda:/ænˌɑːrˈθrɒpədə/ | n. | 无节肢动物 | Animals without jointed appendages.  A taxonomic group characterized by the absence of jointed limbs, distinct from the arthropods. | Example:  Anarthropoda includes creatures like velvet worms. 无节肢动物包括像天鹅绒虫这样的生物。 | Example:  Some scientists debate the classification of anarthropoda. 一些科学家对无节肢动物的分类存在争议。 | 近义词:  non-arthropods | 反义词: arthropoda | noun
