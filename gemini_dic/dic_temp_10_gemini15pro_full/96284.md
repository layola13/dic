indifference:/ɪnˈdɪfərəns/ | n. |冷漠，漠不关心|Lack of interest, concern, or sympathy.|He showed complete indifference to her feelings. 他对她的感受完全漠不关心。|Synonyms: apathy, unconcern, aloofness|Antonyms: interest, concern, care|noun

indifferences:/ɪnˈdɪfərənsɪz/ | n. | 冷漠（复数）|Plural form of indifference, referring to multiple instances of lack of interest, concern, or sympathy. |His indifferences towards his studies led to poor grades.  他对学习的漠不关心导致成绩很差。|Synonyms: apathies, unconcerns|Antonyms: interests, concerns, cares|noun
