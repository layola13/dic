词源:
From Late Latin *episcopalis*, from Latin episcopus (“bishop”) + -alis (adjective suffix).  The Latin episcopus comes from Greek episkopos (“overlooker, guardian, bishop”), from epi (“over”) + skopos (“watcher, guard”).
