comfortingly:/ˈkʌmfərtɪŋli/
adverb|令人欣慰地，安慰地|in a way that makes someone feel less worried, unhappy, or upset.|She smiled comfortingly at him. 她安慰地对他笑了笑。|Synonyms: soothingly, reassuringly, calmingly|Antonyms: distressingly, alarmingly, upsettingly|词性: 副词

comfortingly:/ˈkʌmfərtɪŋli/
Etymology: From comfort + -ing + -ly.  "Comfort" comes from Old French "confort", ultimately from Latin "confortare" (to strengthen).|词源: 由comfort + -ing + -ly构成。“Comfort”来自古法语“confort”，最终源于拉丁语“confortare”（加强）。
