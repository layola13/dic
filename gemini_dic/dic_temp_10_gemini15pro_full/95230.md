inburning:/ɪnˈbɜːrnɪŋ/
adjective
内燃的; 内在燃烧的
Internal burning or combustion; burning within.  Relating to or denoting combustion that takes place within a confined space, such as the cylinder of an engine.
The inburning process generates intense heat. 内燃过程会产生大量的热量。
The inburning engine is more efficient than external combustion engines. 内燃机比外燃机效率更高。
近义词: internal combustion, endoergic
反义词: exothermic
词性: adjective
