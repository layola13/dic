mortalities:/mɔːrˈtælɪtiz/
n.
死亡人数;死亡率
the number of deaths, or the number of deaths in a particular group, or the state of being human and not living forever
例句：Infant mortalities are much higher in the poorest areas of the city. 该市最贫困地区的婴儿死亡率要高得多。

例句：The mortality rate for heart disease is higher in men than in women. 心脏病的死亡率男性高于女性。

近义词：deaths, fatalities, losses
反义词：births, survivals
词性: 名词(plural noun)
