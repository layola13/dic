attuned:/əˈtuːnd/ /əˈtjuːnd/| adj.|协调的，相合的，适应的|Made receptive or aware; in harmony.|She is attuned to the needs of her students. 她很了解学生的需要。|近义词：harmonized, synchronized, adjusted, adapted, sensitive, receptive|反义词：unaware, insensitive, discordant, out of sync|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From at- + tune.  Originally meaning "brought into musical accord," later broadened to encompass other forms of harmony or receptiveness.
