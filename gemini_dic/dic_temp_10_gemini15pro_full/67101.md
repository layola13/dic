explored: /ɪkˈsplɔːrd/ /ɪkˈsplɔːrd/
verb
已探索的，已探险的
having been travelled over or through for the purpose of discovery.
The Amazon rainforest has been explored by many scientists.  亚马逊雨林已经被许多科学家探索过。
近义词: investigated, examined, researched, surveyed
反义词: unexplored, uncharted, unknown
词性: past participle of explore

explore: /ɪkˈsplɔːr/ /ɪkˈsplɔːrd/
verb
探索，探险
to travel in or through (a place) for the purpose of discovery.
We explored the caves with a guide. 我们在导游的带领下探索了洞穴。
近义词: investigate, examine, research, survey
反义词: ignore, neglect
词性: verb

词源: From Late Latin *explorare, from Latin explorare (“to search out, investigate, examine”), from ex- (“out”) + plorare (“to cry out, weep”).
