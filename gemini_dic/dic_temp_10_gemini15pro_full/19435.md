bicolligate:/baɪˈkɒlɪɡeɪt/
verb
双系，双联结
to unite; combine.  Specifically in botany, to yoke together; to connect by a tendril or other natural tie.
The vines bicolligate the two trees.  藤蔓将两棵树连在一起。
近义词: connect, link, join, unite, combine
反义词: separate, divide, disconnect
词性: verb
