glost:/ɡlɒst/ /ɡlɑːst/| n. |釉，釉料；光泽表面；虚饰|A glassy coating applied to pottery or porcelain to give it a smooth, shiny surface.  The final firing of pottery in a kiln to fuse the glaze.|The potter applied a beautiful blue glost to the vase.  陶工在花瓶上涂了一层美丽的蓝色釉料。|Synonyms: glaze, enamel, varnish|Antonyms: matte, dull|noun
