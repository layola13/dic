dickered:/ˈdɪkərd/| v.|讨价还价；做小生意；|To bargain or haggle over a price, usually on small or relatively inexpensive items.|They dickered over the price of the used car for hours. 他们为了这辆二手车的价格讨价还价了几个小时。|Synonym: bargained, haggled, negotiated|Antonym: agreed, conceded|verb
