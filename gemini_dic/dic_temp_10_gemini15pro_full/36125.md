coessential:/ˌkoʊɪˈsɛnʃəl/| adj.|同质的，同体的，共存的|Sharing the same essence or nature; of equal importance or necessity.|The Father, Son, and Holy Spirit are considered coessential in Christian theology.  圣父、圣子和圣灵在基督教神学中被认为是同体的。|例句：Love and compassion are coessential to a fulfilling life. 爱和同情对充实的生活至关重要。|近义词：consubstantial, identical, inherent|反义词：different, separate, distinct|词性:adjective
词源:Late Middle English: from late Latin coessentialis, from co- ‘together’ + essentialis (see essential).
