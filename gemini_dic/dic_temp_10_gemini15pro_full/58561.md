dukes:/duːks/
n.
公爵（复数）;  (duke的复数); 手; 拳头;
Plural form of duke.  A duke is a male holding the highest hereditary title in the British and certain other peerages.  Informally, "dukes" can refer to hands or fists.
The dukes of the realm attended the coronation.  这个王国的公爵们参加了加冕典礼。
He put up his dukes, ready to fight. 他举起拳头，准备战斗。
近义词:noblemen, lords, fists, hands
反义词:commoners
词性:名词


duke:/duːk/
n.
公爵;  <非正式>家伙; 老兄;
A male holding the highest hereditary title in the British and certain other peerages. Informally, a term of address to a man.
He was the Duke of York. 他是约克公爵。
Hey, duke, how's it going? 嘿，老兄，你好吗？
近义词:lord, nobleman, peer, guy, fellow
反义词:commoner
词性:名词
