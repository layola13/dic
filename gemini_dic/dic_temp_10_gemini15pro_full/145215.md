overbattle:/ˈoʊvərˌbætl/| v. |过度战斗，战斗过度|To fight or contend excessively or beyond what is necessary or reasonable.  It implies a prolonged or overly aggressive approach to conflict.|The two armies overbattled the territory, leaving it scorched and barren. 两支军队过度争夺这片领土，使其焦土一片，寸草不生。

The lawyers overbattled the case, driving up costs for both sides. 律师们在这个案件上过度争论，导致双方费用飙升。|近义词：overfight, contend excessively|反义词:compromise, negotiate, concede|词性:动词
