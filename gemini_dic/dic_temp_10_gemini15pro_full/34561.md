clear-up:/ˈklɪər ʌp/ (US), /ˈklɪər ʌp/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|清理；放晴；澄清；解释；痊愈|The misunderstanding cleared up after they talked. 误会在他们交谈后澄清了。|近义词：tidy up, clean up, resolve, explain, improve|反义词:mess up, worsen, confuse|noun, verb

词源: From the verb phrase "clear up," composed of the verb "clear" (from Old French "cler," ultimately from Latin "clarus" meaning "clear, bright") and the adverb/particle "up."
