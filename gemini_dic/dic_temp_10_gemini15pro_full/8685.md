anticorporately:/ˌæntiˈkɔːrpərɪtli/
adverb
反公司地，反对公司地
Acting in a way that opposes or challenges the power and influence of large corporations.  This word describes actions or stances taken against corporate interests.
The activist group protested anticorporately, challenging the company's environmental record.  这个激进组织以反公司的方式抗议，挑战该公司的环境记录。
He voted anticorporately, supporting the small businesses over the multinational corporation. 他以反公司的立场投票，支持小型企业而不是跨国公司。
Near Synonyms: anti-business, anti-establishment, anti-corporate
Opposite: pro-corporate, pro-business
词性: 副词
