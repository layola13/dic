archimedean:/ˌɑːrkɪˈmiːdiən/ |adj.|阿基米德的; 阿基米德原理的; 浮力的; 螺旋形的|Relating to Archimedes or his work, especially his principle of buoyancy.  Also, relating to the Archimedean spiral.|The bath toy demonstrated the Archimedean principle.  这个洗澡玩具演示了阿基米德原理。|Near synonyms: buoyant, floating, helical. |Antonyms: sinking, non-buoyant.|adjective


archimedean:/ˌɑːrkɪˈmiːdiən/ |n.|阿基米德的追随者;  坚持阿基米德原理的人|A follower of Archimedes or someone who adheres to his principles.|He considered himself an archimedean, always seeking practical solutions. 他认为自己是一个阿基米德学派的人，总是寻求实际的解决方案。|Near synonyms: follower, disciple. |Antonyms: opponent, critic.|noun
