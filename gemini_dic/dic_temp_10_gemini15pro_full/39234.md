congratulant:/kənˈɡrætʃʊlənt/ | adj. n.| 祝贺的，表示祝贺的；祝贺者 |Expressing or offering congratulations.  A person offering congratulations. |例句：He sent a congratulant message to the winning team.  他向获胜的队伍发送了祝贺信息。

例句：She was among the congratulants at the graduation ceremony. 她在毕业典礼上是众多祝贺者之一。 |近义词：congratulatory, well-wishing, felicitous |反义词: unsympathetic, uncaring |词性: adjective, noun

Etymology:
From Latin congratulans, present participle of congratulari "to wish joy, congratulate,"  from com- "together" + gratulari "to show joy, congratulate," from gratus "pleasing, thankful, agreeable."
