branlike: /ˈbrænlaɪk/
adjective
像麸皮的；麸皮状的
Resembling bran in texture, appearance, or consistency.
The bread had a dense, branlike texture.  这面包口感很扎实，像麸皮一样。
The substance had a dry, branlike consistency. 这种物质干燥得像麸皮一样。
近义词: coarse, grainy, fibrous
反义词: smooth, fine
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From bran +‎ -like.
