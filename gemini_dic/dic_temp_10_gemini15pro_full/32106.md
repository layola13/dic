chestiest:/ˈtʃɛstiɪst/
adjective
最胸部发达的，胸肌最突出的
Having the most prominent or well-developed chest.
He was the chestiest guy in the gym, always puffing out his pecs. 他是健身房里胸肌最发达的家伙，总是鼓着胸肌。
近义词:broad-chested, muscular
反义词:narrow-chested, slight
