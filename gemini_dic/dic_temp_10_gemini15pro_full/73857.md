formulate:/ˈfɔːrmjəleɪt/ (US) /ˈfɔːmjʊleɪt/ (UK)| v. |规划；明确表达；构想；确切地阐述|to create or devise methodically (a strategy or a proposal)|The committee will formulate a plan. 委员会将制定一个计划。|Synonyms: devise, conceive, articulate, frame, define|Antonyms: improvise, disregard, neglect|verb
