osteoclasia: /ˌɒstiːəʊˈkleɪʒə/ /ˌɑːstiːoʊˈkleɪʒə/ | n. | 骨折术；碎骨术；外科手术骨折 | The surgical breaking of a bone, especially to correct a deformity.  | Example: Osteoclasia was performed to correct the malformation of his tibia.  胫骨畸形矫正术是用碎骨术进行的。

Example:  The doctor considered osteoclasia as a treatment option for the patient's bowed legs. 医生考虑将碎骨术作为治疗病人罗圈腿的一种选择。 | 近义词: osteotomy, fracture (in a medical context) | 反义词: bone healing, bone grafting | 词性: noun
