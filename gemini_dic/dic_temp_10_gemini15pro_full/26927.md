ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *calamitosite, from Latin calamitōsus (“miserable, wretched, disastrous, destructive”), from calamitās (“damage, loss, misfortune, disaster, calamity”), from calamus (“reed, stalk”), of uncertain origin.
