cobblestone:/ˈkɒblˌstəʊn/ (UK) /ˈkɑːblˌstoʊn/ (US)| n.|鹅卵石，铺路石|A small, rounded stone used for paving streets.|The streets of the old town were paved with cobblestones. 老城区的街道是用鹅卵石铺成的。|Synonyms: paving stone, sett, flagstone|Antonyms: asphalt, concrete|Noun

cobbled:/ˈkɒbld/ (UK) /ˈkɑːbld/ (US)| adj.|铺以鹅卵石的|Paved with cobblestones.|We walked along the cobbled streets of Prague. 我们沿着布拉格铺满鹅卵石的街道走着。|Synonyms: stone-paved, paved|Antonyms: unpaved, asphalt|Adjective
