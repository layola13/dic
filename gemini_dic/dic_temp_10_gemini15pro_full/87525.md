heterogeneous:/ˌhetərəˈdʒiːniəs/ | adj. |异质的，由不同成分组成的|Consisting of dissimilar or diverse ingredients or constituents.|例句：The population of the city is quite heterogeneous, with people from all over the world. 这个城市的人口非常多样化，来自世界各地。

例句：The mixture was heterogeneous, containing both solids and liquids. 混合物是异质的，既包含固体也包含液体。

|近义词：diverse, varied, mixed, dissimilar, miscellaneous|反义词：homogeneous, uniform, similar|词性:形容词
