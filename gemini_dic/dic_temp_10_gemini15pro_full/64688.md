escapement:/ɪˈskeɪpmənt/
n.|擒纵机构，擒纵装置|A mechanical device in a clock or watch that transfers energy to the timekeeping element and regulates its oscillations.|The watchmaker carefully adjusted the escapement.|Synonyms: regulator, controller|Antonyms: N/A|noun

escapement:/ɪˈskeɪpmənt/
n.|（气体、液体或情感的）泄漏，逸出|The act or process of escaping or releasing something.|The escapement of gas from the pipe caused a fire.|Synonyms: leak, release, discharge|Antonyms: containment, retention|noun
