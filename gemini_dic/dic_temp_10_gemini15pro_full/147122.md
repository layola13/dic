overloaded:/ˌoʊvərˈloʊdəd/
overloaded| adj.|超载的，过载的，负担过重的|Having too much of something; loaded beyond capacity or limit.  |The server is overloaded and can't handle any more requests. 服务器超载，无法处理更多请求。|overburdened, overwhelmed, swamped|underloaded|adjective


overloaded:/ˌoʊvərˈloʊdɪd/
overloaded| v.|超载，使负担过重|(past tense and past participle of overload)  To load with too great a burden or cargo; to burden excessively. |The truck was overloaded with goods. 卡车货物超载。|overburden, overwhelm, swamp|underload|verb
