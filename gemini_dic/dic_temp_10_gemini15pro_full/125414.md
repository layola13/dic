multiparty:/ˈmʌltiˌpɑːrti/ | adj. |多方的，多党制的| Involving several or many parties. |  例句：The multiparty system allows for a wider range of political views. 多党制允许更广泛的政治观点。

例句：They engaged in multiparty negotiations to resolve the conflict. 他们进行了多方谈判来解决冲突。 | 近义词：multilateral, pluralistic | 反义词：bilateral, two-party | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From multi- (many) + party. "Multi-" comes from Latin "multus" meaning "much, many". "Party" comes from Old French "partie" meaning "part, side, share."
