avert:/əˈvɜːrt/| v. |避免，防止，转移（目光、注意力等）|To prevent something bad from happening; to turn away (your eyes or thoughts).|The company took steps to avert a financial crisis. 公司采取措施避免了一场金融危机。|He averted his gaze from the gruesome scene. 他把目光从可怕的景象中移开。|近义词：prevent, avoid, preclude, obviate, forestall|反义词：cause, incite, provoke|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *avertere*  "to turn away," from *ab-* "away, from" (see ab-) + *vertere* "to turn" (see versus).
