Mayans: /ˈmaɪənz/ | n. | 玛雅人 |  A member of an indigenous people of Yucatan and parts of Guatemala and Belize, whose civilization reached its height around AD 300–900.  The Mayan civilization is known for its advancements in mathematics, astronomy, and art. | The Mayans built impressive pyramids and temples. 玛雅人建造了令人印象深刻的金字塔和寺庙。|  The Mayans developed a complex writing system. 玛雅人发展出了一套复杂的文字系统。 | Synonyms: Maya people | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
