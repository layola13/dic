enduement:/ɪnˈdjuːmənt/
n.
赋予，授予；授权；加持
The act of enduing or the state of being endued; investment with some quality or power.
The knight received the enduement of magical powers from the ancient sword.  这位骑士从古老的剑中获得了魔法力量的加持。
investment, bestowal, empowerment
divestiture, deprivation
noun
