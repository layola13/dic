indulging:/ɪnˈdʌldʒɪŋ/ | v. | 纵容；沉溺；享受 | Allowing oneself to enjoy something, especially something considered pleasurable or wrong. |  She was indulging in a long, hot bath. 她正在享受一个长时间的热水澡。

He indulged his passion for fast cars. 他沉溺于他对跑车的热情。 | 近义词：pampering, gratifying, indulging in, reveling in, wallowing in | 反义词：abstaining, resisting, denying | 词性: Verb (present participle)

indulge:/ɪnˈdʌldʒ/ | v. | 纵容；沉溺；享受 |  Allow oneself to enjoy the pleasure of. |  I indulged in a piece of chocolate cake. 我享用了一块巧克力蛋糕。

They indulged themselves with a trip to the spa. 他们用一趟水疗之旅犒劳自己。 | 近义词：pamper, gratify, treat, spoil | 反义词：abstain, resist, deny | 词性: Verb


词源:  From Late Latin *indulgentia, from Latin indulgere "to be kind or tender to, yield to," from in- "in, toward" + dulgere "be kind to"
