overhanging:/ˌoʊvərˈhæŋɪŋ/
adjective
悬垂的，突出，伸出的
Projecting or extending beyond a supporting base or edge.
The overhanging cliff threatened to collapse. 悬崖峭壁悬在空中，随时可能崩塌。
The overhanging branches provided shade. 突出的树枝提供了阴凉。
近义词: projecting, protruding, jutting, beetling
反义词: recessed, sunken
词性: adjective


overhang:/ˈoʊvərhæŋ/
noun
突出部分，悬垂物
Something that overhangs.
The overhang of the roof protected the doorway from the rain. 屋檐挡住了雨水，保护了门口。
The rock overhang provided shelter for climbers. 岩石的突出部分为登山者提供了庇护所。
近义词: projection, protrusion, overhang
反义词:undercut
词性: noun


overhang:/ˌoʊvərˈhæŋ/
verb
悬垂，突出，逼近
To project or extend beyond a supporting base or edge.
Dark clouds overhung the city.  乌云笼罩着城市。
The threat of war overhung the nation. 战争的威胁笼罩着这个国家。
近义词: project, protrude, jut, beetle
反义词: recede
词性: verb
