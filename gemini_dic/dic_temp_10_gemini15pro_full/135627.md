nonpractically:/ˌnɒnˈpræktɪkli/
adverb|不切实际地|Not in a practical or feasible manner; impractically.|His ideas, while interesting, were nonpractically applied. 他的想法虽然有趣，但不切实际。|
近义词:impractically, unrealistically, unfeasibly
反义词:practically, realistically, feasibly
词性:副词
词源:non- (not) + practically
