infantilizes:/ˈɪnfəntɪlaɪzɪz/
verb
使幼稚化；把…当作幼儿对待
treats (someone) as a child or in a way that denies their maturity.
He infantilizes her by constantly making decisions for her. 他总是替她做决定，把她当孩子看待。
patronize, condescend, diminish
empower, respect, validate
v.
