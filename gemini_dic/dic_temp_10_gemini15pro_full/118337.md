mericarp:/ˈmɛrɪˌkɑːrp/ | n. | 分果爿；小坚果 | One of the separate carpels of a schizocarp. A dry, indehiscent fruit formed from one carpel of a compound ovary. | The mericarps of the plant split apart when ripe.  这种植物的分果爿成熟时会裂开。| Synonym: carpel, segment | Antonym: aggregate fruit | noun

/ˈmɛrɪˌkɑːrp/ | n. | 分果爿；小坚果 | One of the separate carpels of a schizocarp. A dry indehiscent fruit formed from one carpel of a compound ovary. |  The fennel plant produces mericarps containing its seeds. 茴香植物会结出含有种子的分果爿。 | Synonym: achene, nutlet | Antonym: aggregate fruit | noun
