maraschino:/ˌmærəˈskiːnoʊ/ /ˌmærəˈʃiːnoʊ/
|n.|
|马拉斯基诺樱桃（一种用糖和食用色素腌制的樱桃，通常用于鸡尾酒和甜点）|
|A maraschino cherry is a preserved, sweetened cherry, typically made from light-colored varieties such as the Royal Ann, Rainier, or Gold varieties. They are dyed bright red, given a sugar syrup bath, then packed in a brine solution usually containing sulfur dioxide and calcium chloride to preserve their texture. Maraschinos are frequently used as a garnish in cocktails, such as the Manhattan, and desserts.|

词源：来源于意大利语maraschino, 指一种用Marasca樱桃酿制的利口酒。后来这个词被用来指用这种利口酒浸泡的樱桃，最终泛指所有这种类型的加工樱桃，即使它们不再用Marasca樱桃或利口酒制作。
