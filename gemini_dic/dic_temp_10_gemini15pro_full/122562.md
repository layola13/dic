moderable:/ˈmɒdərəbl/ (US) /ˈmɒdrəbəl/ (UK)| adj.|可调节的，可缓和的|Able to be moderated; capable of being made less extreme, severe, or intense.  This allows for a more nuanced and controlled approach, ensuring the outcome is more manageable and less drastic.|The temperature in the room is moderable with the new thermostat. 房间里的温度可以用新的恒温器调节。|The pain was moderable with medication. 药物可以减轻疼痛。|近义词：adjustable, controllable, manageable, tempereate|反义词：immoderate, extreme, uncontrollable, unmanageable|词性:形容词
词源: From moderate + -able.  "Moderate" comes from Latin moderatus, past participle of moderari "to regulate, restrain," from modus "measure, manner."
