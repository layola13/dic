nondeliberately:/ˌnɒndɪˈlɪbərətli/
adverb
非故意地，无意地
Not done consciously or intentionally; accidentally.
She nondeliberately bumped into him, causing him to spill his coffee. 她无意中撞到了他，使他洒了咖啡。
Synonyms: unintentionally, accidentally, inadvertently, unconsciously
Antonyms: deliberately, intentionally, purposely, consciously
词性: 副词
