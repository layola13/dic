metal:/ˈmetl/ (US) /ˈmetəl/ (UK)| n.|金属|A solid material that is typically hard, shiny, malleable, fusible, and ductile, with good electrical and thermal conductivity.|例句：Iron is a common metal. 铁是一种常见的金属。

|例句：Gold is a precious metal. 黄金是一种贵金属。

|近义词：mineral, element|反义词:nonmetal|noun
