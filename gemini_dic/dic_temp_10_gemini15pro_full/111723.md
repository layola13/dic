locomotives:/ˌloʊkəˈmoʊtɪvz/ | n. | 火车头，机车（复数）| Plural form of locomotive. | The museum displayed several vintage locomotives. 博物馆展出了几辆老式火车头。 | synonyms: engines, trains, railcars | antonyms: (No direct antonym) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "loco" (place) + "motivus" (moving), meaning "moving from place to place".
