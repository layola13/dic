drake:/dreɪk/ | n. |公鸭；龙|A male duck. Also, a mythical creature like a large lizard or serpent, typically depicted with wings or fire-breathing ability.|例句：The drake preened its iridescent feathers. 公鸭梳理着它彩虹般的羽毛。

例句：Legends speak of a fearsome drake guarding the treasure. 传说有一条可怕的龙守护着宝藏。|近义词：mallard (for duck), dragon, wyvern, wyrm (for mythical creature)|反义词：duck (female), hen (female duck) |词性:noun
