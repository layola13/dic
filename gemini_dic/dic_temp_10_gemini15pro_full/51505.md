detrainment:/diːˈtreɪnmənt/
n.|卸货，下火车；（气象学）空气从云中移出|The process by which air is removed from a cloud.  空气从云中移出的过程。|例句：Detrainment of drier air from the environment can suppress convection.  来自周围环境的较干燥空气的移出会抑制对流。|近义词：removal, discharge, unloading|反义词：entrainment|词性:名词

detrainment:/diːˈtreɪnmənt/
n.|（乘客）下火车|The action of passengers leaving a train. 乘客离开火车的动作。|例句：Detrainment at this station is prohibited.  禁止在此站下车。|近义词：disembarkation, alighting|反义词：embarkation, boarding|词性:名词
