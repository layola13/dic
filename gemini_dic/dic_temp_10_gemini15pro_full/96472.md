individualizes:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使个性化；个别处理；区别对待
To make something suitable for particular people or things, or to make someone or something different from others.
The program individualizes learning by allowing students to progress at their own pace.  这个课程通过允许学生按照自己的进度学习来实现个性化学习。
The coach individualizes the training program for each athlete. 教练为每位运动员制定个性化的训练计划。

近义词: personalize, customize, tailor, differentiate
反义词: standardize, generalize, homogenize
词性: verb
