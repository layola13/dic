connectively:/kəˈnɛktɪvli/
adverb
连接地，连贯地
in a way that connects things or events
The chapters are connectively themed. 章节主题相互关联。
近义词: jointly, together, unitedly
反义词: separately, disjointedly
词性: adverb

词源: From connect + -ive + -ly.  "Connect" traces back through French to Latin *connectere, from con- 'together' + nectere 'to bind'.
