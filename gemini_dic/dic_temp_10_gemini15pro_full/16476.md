bargainable:/ˈbɑːrɡɪnəbl/| adj.|可讨价还价的，可商量的|Open to negotiation or discussion, especially with regard to price or terms.|The price of the car is bargainable. 这辆车的价格是可以商量的。|近义词：negotiable, discussable|反义词：non-negotiable, fixed, firm|词性:形容词
词源: From bargain + -able.
