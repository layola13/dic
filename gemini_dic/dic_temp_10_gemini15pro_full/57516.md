dragonnade:/ˌdræɡəˈneɪd/| n.|龙骑兵的暴行；迫害|The persecution of French Protestants by Louis XIV's dragoons, who were quartered in their houses to force conversions to Catholicism. 指路易十四时期龙骑兵对法国新教徒的迫害，龙骑兵驻扎在新教徒家中，强迫他们皈依天主教。|The dragonnade was a brutal period in French history. 龙骑兵的暴行是法国历史上残酷的时期。|近义词：persecution, oppression|反义词:tolerance, acceptance|词性:名词

Etymology:
From French *dragonnade*, from *dragon*, dragoon.
