clunch:/klʌntʃ/ | v. | (非正式)猛击，重击| (informal) To hit or strike heavily. |He clunched his opponent on the jaw. 他猛击对手的下巴。|She clunched the table in frustration. 她沮丧地捶打桌子。|近义词：bash, thump, wallop |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "caress") | 词性: verb
