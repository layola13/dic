orbate:/ˈɔːrbeɪt/ | n. | 轨道; 眼窝; 球|An orbit is the curved path in space that is followed by a planet, moon, or artificial satellite as it goes round and round a much larger object, such as a star. |The Earth is in orbit around the sun. 地球绕太阳运行。|Synonyms: path, trajectory, course, circuit, circle, revolution|Antonyms: N/A | noun

orbate:/ˈɔːrbeɪt/ | v. | 绕轨道运行; 使进入轨道|If a spacecraft, planet, or other object orbits something larger, it moves around it in space.|The satellite orbits the Earth every 90 minutes. 这颗卫星每90分钟绕地球运行一周。|Synonyms: circle, revolve, rotate, go around, circumnavigate|Antonyms: N/A | verb
