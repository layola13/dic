ophiomorpha:/ˌɒfiəˈmɔːrfə/ | n. | 蛇形管迹 |A trace fossil, typically a burrow, characterized by a distinctive, often lined, meandering or spiral form, attributed to crustacean activity.  It represents the dwelling structures of these organisms. | Example: Ophiomorpha burrows are common in Cretaceous shallow marine sandstones.  蛇形管迹常见于白垩纪浅海砂岩中。

Example: The discovery of ophiomorpha helped geologists understand the paleoenvironment. 蛇形管迹的发现帮助地质学家了解古环境。| synonym: burrow, trace fossil | antonym: body fossil | noun
