interactivities:/ˌɪntərækˈtɪvətiz/ | n. |互動性；交互性|The plural form of interactivity, referring to multiple instances or ways in which something is interactive.  It describes the processes or qualities that allow users to engage with and influence something, typically a computer system or piece of software.|The museum exhibit offered several interactivities, including touchscreens and virtual reality experiences. 博物館展覽提供了幾種互動方式，包括觸控螢幕和虛擬實境體驗。|Synonyms: engagements, exchanges, reciprocities |Antonyms: passivity, inactivity | noun
