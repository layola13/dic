overeasily:/ˌoʊvərˈiːzɪli/
adv.|太容易地，过于轻易地|Too easily; without enough effort or difficulty.|He won the match overeasily, which made his opponent suspicious. 他赢得比赛太容易了，这使他的对手产生了怀疑。|The task was completed overeasily, raising concerns about its thoroughness.  这项任务完成得太容易了，引发了对其彻底性的担忧。|近义词: too easily, effortlessly, without difficulty|反义词: with difficulty, laboriously, strenuously|词性:副词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "easily" (adverb)
