outlaboring:/ˌaʊtˈleɪbərɪŋ/
verb
胜过工作; 通过更辛勤的工作胜过
working harder than
例句：They outlabored their opponents and won the game. 他们比对手更努力，赢得了比赛。

例句：She outlabored everyone in the office to get the promotion.  她在办公室里比任何人都努力工作以获得晋升。

近义词：outworking, surpassing, excelling
反义词：underperforming, slacking

词性:v.
