bastile:/bæˈstiːl/ /bæˈstiːl/ | n. |巴士底狱；堡垒；监狱|A fortress in Paris, used as a state prison and armory, and destroyed at the beginning of the French Revolution. |The storming of the Bastille marked the beginning of the French Revolution. 巴士底狱的攻陷标志着法国大革命的开始。|例句：The Bastille was a symbol of royal oppression. 巴士底狱是皇权压迫的象征。

例句：Many political prisoners were held in the Bastille. 许多政治犯被关押在巴士底狱。

近义词：fortress, prison, jail |反义词:freedom, liberty|词性:noun
