granitoid: /ˈɡrænɪtɔɪd/
n.|花岗岩类；似花岗岩的|A coarse-grained igneous rock with a composition similar to granite.|The geologist identified the sample as a granitoid. 地质学家将该样品鉴定为花岗岩类。|Synonyms: granite-like, plutonic|Antonyms: N/A|Noun

granitoid: /ˈɡrænɪtɔɪd/
adj.|花岗岩状的，似花岗岩的|Resembling granite in texture or composition.|The granitoid rocks formed the core of the mountain range.  花岗岩状的岩石形成了山脉的核心。|Synonyms: granitic|Antonyms: N/A|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From granite + -oid.  "Granite" comes from the Italian word "granito," meaning "grained," ultimately from the Latin "granum," meaning "grain." The suffix "-oid" means "resembling" or "like."
