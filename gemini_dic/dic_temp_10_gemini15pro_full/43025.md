cowedly:/ˈkaʊədli/| adj.|胆怯的，懦弱的|Exhibiting or characteristic of cowardice; timid.|He gave a cowedly apology after being confronted.  他被质问后，胆怯地道歉了。|近义词：timid, craven, pusillanimous|反义词：brave, courageous, valiant|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *cuwardli, from Old English *cūheardlīce (“cowardly”), equivalent to coward +‎ -ly.
