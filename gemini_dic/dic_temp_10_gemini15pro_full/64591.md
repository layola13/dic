erudite:/ˈɛrʊdaɪt/| adj.|博学的，有学问的|Having or showing great knowledge or learning.|例句：She is an erudite scholar in the field of ancient history. 她是一位古代史领域的博学学者。|例句：His erudite commentary on the text illuminated its complexities. 他对文本的博学评论阐明了其复杂性。|近义词：learned, scholarly, knowledgeable, well-read, educated|反义词：ignorant, uneducated, unlearned, illiterate|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *ērudītus*, past participle of *ērudīre* (“to educate, instruct, polish”), from *ē* (“out, from”) + *rudis* (“rough, untrained”).
