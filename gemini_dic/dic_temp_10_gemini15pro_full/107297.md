lamaite:/ˈlɑːmaɪt/
n.
块状硅镁石
A mineral, basic magnesium silicate, occurring in compact masses.
Lamaite is a rare mineral found in volcanic rocks. 块状硅镁石是一种罕见的矿物，发现于火山岩中。
Lamaite has been found in association with other magnesium silicates. 块状硅镁石与其他硅酸镁一起被发现。
近义词:  magnesium silicate, forsterite (similar composition but different crystal structure)
反义词: N/A (Minerals don't typically have antonyms)
词性: Noun
