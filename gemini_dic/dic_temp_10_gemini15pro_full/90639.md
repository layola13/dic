humanize:/ˈhjuːmənaɪz/ |v.|使人性化；赋予人性|They tried to humanize the factory environment. 他们试图使工厂环境人性化。|  The author humanizes the historical figures by portraying their personal struggles. 作者通过描绘历史人物的个人挣扎来使他们人性化。|近义词: personalize, individualize |反义词: dehumanize, mechanize |verb
