partook:/pɑːrˈtʊk/
verb
参与；分享；吃，喝
to have eaten or drunk something.  To have taken part in or shared something.
She partook of the communion bread and wine. 她领了圣餐的面包和酒。
He partook in the festivities. 他参加了庆祝活动。
shared, participated, consumed
abstained, refused, refrained
