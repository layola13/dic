nonassertive:/ˌnɒnəˈsɜːtɪv/ /ˌnɑːnəˈsɜːrtɪv/ | adj. |不自信的，不果断的，顺从的|Not confidently or forcefully expressing one's views or desires; tending to be submissive or passive.|She was too nonassertive to demand a refund. 她太不自信，不敢要求退款。|Synonyms: timid, shy, unassertive, passive, submissive|Antonyms: assertive, confident, aggressive, forceful|Adjective
