bustard:/ˈbʌstərd/ (US), /ˈbʌstɑːd/ (UK)| n. |鸨|A large, heavily built, ground-dwelling bird found in open country, typically having cryptic brownish plumage.|We saw a great bustard displaying its impressive plumage during our safari. 我们在旅行中看到一只大鸨展示它令人印象深刻的羽毛。|
|近义词: (no direct single synonym, but consider) large bird, ground bird, game bird|反义词: (no antonym)|词性: noun
