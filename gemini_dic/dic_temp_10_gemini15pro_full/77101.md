garrulity:/ɡəˈruːləti/ /ɡæˈruːləti/| n.|饶舌，多言，喋喋不休|Excessive talkativeness, especially on trivial matters.|Her garrulity made it difficult to get a word in edgewise. 她太啰嗦了，我根本插不上话。|The garrulity of the old man kept us entertained for hours. 老人的健谈让我们开心了好几个小时。|近义词：loquacity, talkativeness, volubility|反义词：reticence, taciturnity|词性:noun

词源:
From Late Latin *garrulitas, from Latin garrulus (“talkative”) + -itas.  The Latin garrulus is related to the verb garrire (“to chatter”).
