muniment:/ˈmjuːnɪmənt/
n.
防御工事；保障；凭据；（尤指）契据
a document, especially a legal one, that gives official information about something, or that proves that something is true
The family kept the ancient muniments in a strongbox. 这家人把古代契据保存在一个保险箱里。
document, record, evidence, deed, title
lack, absence
