copywriting:/ˈkɑːpiˌraɪtɪŋ/ | n. |文案写作；广告文字撰写|The act or process of writing text for the purpose of advertising or other forms of marketing. It aims to increase brand awareness and ultimately persuade a person or group to take a particular action.|The company hired a new team for copywriting their latest product. 这家公司聘请了一个新团队来撰写他们最新产品的广告文案。|synonyms: advertising copy, promotional writing, marketing text | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider: silence, censorship) | noun
