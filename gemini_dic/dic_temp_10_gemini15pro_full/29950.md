cavelike:/ˈkeɪvˌlaɪk/| adj.|像洞穴的，洞穴状的|Resembling a cave; dark, hollow, or suggestive of a cave. |The room was dark and cavelike.  这个房间又黑又像洞穴。|The tunnel had a cavelike atmosphere. 这条隧道有一种像洞穴般的气氛。|近义词：cavernous, dark, hollow|反义词：bright, open, airy|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From cave + -like.  "Cave" comes from Old French "cave" and Latin "cava," meaning "hollow place." "-like" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling, characteristic of."
