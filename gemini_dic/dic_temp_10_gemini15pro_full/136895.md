nonsocietal:/ˌnɒn.səˈsaɪ.ə.təl/
adjective
非社会的
Not relating to or involving society as a whole; not connected with the organization of society.
The artist's work explored nonsocietal themes of personal identity and the subconscious.  这位艺术家的作品探索了个人身份和潜意识等非社会主题。
The hermit lived a nonsocietal existence, far removed from the pressures of modern life.  这位隐士过着非社会化的生活，远离现代生活的压力。
近义词:asocial, nonsocial
反义词:social, societal
词性:adjective
