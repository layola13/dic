bullfist: /ˈbʊlfɪst/ | n. | 拳头; 暴力; 强权 |  A fist, especially a large or powerful one, often used figuratively to represent strength, aggression, or coercion.  This word is often associated with violence or the threat of violence. | He ruled with a bullfist. 他用强权统治。 | 近义词: fist, iron fist, clenched fist | 反义词: gentleness, kindness | 词性: 名词

词源:  A compound word combining "bull," suggesting powerful or aggressive like a bull, and "fist," referring to the closed hand.
