foundationary:/faʊnˈdeɪʃəneri/ | adj. | 基础的，根本的 | Relating to or forming the foundation; fundamental. |  This foundationary principle underpins our entire legal system.  这个基本原则支撑着我们整个法律体系。| The foundationary work for the new building is complete. 新大楼的基础工程已经完成。 |  fundamental, basic, essential, primary |  superficial, secondary | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From foundation + -ary.  "Foundation" comes from the Old French "fondacion,"  ultimately from the Latin "fundare" (to found, lay the basis for).  The suffix "-ary" derives from the Latin "-arius," forming adjectives with the sense "pertaining to" or "connected with."
