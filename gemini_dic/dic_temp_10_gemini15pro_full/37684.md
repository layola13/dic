comparator:/kəmˈpærətər/| n.|比较器，比较仪|A device or circuit that compares two input signals and produces an output signal indicating the relationship between them.  It's often used in electronics and computer science.|The comparator flagged an error when the two data streams didn't match. 比较器在两个数据流不匹配时标记了一个错误。|Synonyms: comparer, differentiator|Antonyms: integrator|noun
