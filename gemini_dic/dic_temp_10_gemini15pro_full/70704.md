finances:/ˈfaɪnænsɪz/
n.
财务状况；资金
the money or other liquid resources of a government, business, group, or individual.
The company's finances are in a healthy state. 这家公司的财务状况良好。

finances:/faɪˈnæns/
vt.
提供资金
provide funding for (a person or enterprise).
The museum is financed by the local council.  这家博物馆是由当地议会资助的。

Etymology:  From Old French *finer ("to end, settle a debt, pay a ransom"), from fin ("end") ultimately from Latin finis ("boundary, limit, end"). Doublet of fine. The verb sense ("to provide funding for") arose in the 19th century.

Synonyms:  funds, resources, capital, assets, wealth, money, backing, investment (for noun); fund, support, back, subsidize, underwrite (for verb)

Antonyms: debts, liabilities (for noun)
