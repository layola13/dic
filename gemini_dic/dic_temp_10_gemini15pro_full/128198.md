neurenteric:/ˌnjʊərənˈtɛrɪk/ | adj. |神经肠的|Relating to the neural tube and the gut. It describes a congenital abnormality resulting from incomplete separation of the notochord from the foregut during embryonic development.|Neurenteric cysts are rare congenital lesions that are thought to arise from remnants of the neurenteric canal. 神经肠囊肿是一种罕见的先天性病变，被认为起源于神经肠管的残余。|Synonyms: neuroenteric (variant spelling) | Antonyms: N/A | Word type: Adjective
