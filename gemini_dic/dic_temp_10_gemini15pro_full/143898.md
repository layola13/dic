outpowers:/ˌaʊtˈpaʊərz/
verb
胜过，压倒
to defeat or overcome someone or something by having more power or strength
例句：The larger army easily outpowered the smaller one.  较大的军队轻易地击败了较小的军队。

例句：Her opponent completely outpowered her in the debate. 她的对手在辩论中完全压倒了她。

近义词：overpowers, overwhelms, defeats, conquers, surpasses
反义词：loses to, succumbs to, is defeated by
词性:v.
