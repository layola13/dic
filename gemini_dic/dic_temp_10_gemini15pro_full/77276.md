gastrovascular: /ˌɡæstrəʊˈvæskjʊlə(r)/ | adj. | 消化循环的；胃管的 | Relating to a gastrovascular cavity.  A gastrovascular cavity is a structure found in primitive animal phyla. It is responsible for both digestion and the transport of nutrients throughout the organism's body, often functioning as a simple circulatory system as well. |  The jellyfish has a gastrovascular cavity. 水母有一个消化循环腔。| synonymous: digestive circulatory | antonymous: N/A | adjective


gastrovascular: /ˌɡæstrəʊˈvæskjʊlə(r)/ | n. | 消化循环腔；胃管 | A gastrovascular cavity. | The gastrovascular cavity of the hydra allows it to digest relatively large prey.  水螅的消化循环腔使其能够消化相对较大的猎物。 | synonymous: digestive circulatory cavity | antonymous: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From gastro- (combining form meaning "stomach") + vascular (relating to vessels or ducts).  Gastro- comes from the Greek "gastēr" (stomach), and vascular comes from the Latin "vasculum" (small vessel).
