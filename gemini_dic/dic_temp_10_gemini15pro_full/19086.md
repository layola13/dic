betrothal:/bɪˈtrəʊθəl/ /bɪˈtrɒθəl/| n.|订婚，婚约|A mutual promise or contract for a future marriage.|The betrothal was announced at a family gathering.  这个婚约是在一个家庭聚会上宣布的。|Synonyms: engagement, promise, pledge|Antonyms: divorce, separation|noun

betrothed:/bɪˈtrəʊðd/ /bɪˈtrɒθd/| adj.  n.|已订婚的，订了婚的；未婚夫，未婚妻|Engaged to be married; the person to whom one is engaged.|The betrothed couple exchanged rings. 已订婚的夫妇交换了戒指。  Her betrothed is a doctor. 她的未婚夫是一位医生。|Synonyms: engaged, promised, pledged|Antonyms: divorced, separated|adjective, noun
