dulled:/dʌld/| adj.|钝的，迟钝的，无光泽的，暗淡的|Made less sharp, bright, sensitive, or keen.|The rain dulled the shine on my car. 雨水使我的车失去了光泽。|近义词：blunted, weakened, dimmed, subdued|反义词:sharpened, brightened, heightened, intensified|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *dullen, from Old English *dyllan (“to make dull, dim”), from Proto-Germanic *duljaną (“to be or become dull”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰul- (“muddy, cloudy”). Cognate with Dutch dullen (“to be lazy, be slow”), German verdullen (“to stop up, clog”), dialectal German dudeln (“to be lazy, to be dull”).
