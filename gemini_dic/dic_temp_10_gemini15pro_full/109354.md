lenity:/ˈlenɪti/| n.|宽大，仁慈，温和|Mildness or gentleness, especially in punishing someone.|The judge treated the defendant with lenity because of his young age. 法官因为被告年轻而对他从轻发落。|Near Synonyms: mercy, clemency, compassion, forbearance, tolerance|Antonyms: severity, harshness, strictness, rigor|noun
