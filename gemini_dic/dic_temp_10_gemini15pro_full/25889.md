burliest:/ˈbɜːrliɪst/| adj.|最强壮的，最粗壮的|Superlative form of burly: most burly.  Meaning exceptionally strong and large in physique.|例句：He was the burliest man in the village, capable of lifting incredible weights. 他是村里最强壮的人，能够举起惊人的重量。

例句：The burliest of the bouncers stood guard at the entrance to the club.  保镖中最强壮的那位守在俱乐部的入口处。|近义词：strongest, sturdiest, heftiest, most muscular|反义词：weakest, frailest, slimmest, thinnest|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *burly*, from Old English *borlice* (“splendidly, nobly”), from *bor* (“noble”) + *-lice* (adverbial suffix).  The meaning shifted over time from "noble" and "stately" to "large and strong."
