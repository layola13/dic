discage:/dɪsˈkeɪdʒ/
verb
释放，解雇
To release from a cage or confinement; to discharge from service or obligation.
The knight discaged the prisoner from the dungeon. 骑士把囚犯从地牢里释放出来。
The company discaged him from his duties. 公司解除了他的职务。
liberate, release, free, discharge
confine, imprison, detain
