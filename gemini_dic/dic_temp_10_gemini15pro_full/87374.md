heronshaw:/ˈhɛrənˌʃɔː/
n.
苍鹭
A large wading bird with long legs, a long neck, and a long, pointed bill.  It is a type of heron.
The heronshaw stood motionless in the shallows, waiting for an unsuspecting fish.  苍鹭一动不动地站在浅滩里，等待着毫无戒心的鱼。
近义词：heron, egret
反义词：(None -  No direct opposite for a type of bird)
词性：noun


Etymology:
From Middle English *herounshawe, equivalent to heron + shaw (“wood”).  The word literally means "heron of the woods."
