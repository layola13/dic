automatists:/ɔːˈtɒmətɪsts/
n.
自动机; 自动主义者; 机械般行动的人
People who act in a mechanical or involuntary way, or artists who create art spontaneously without conscious control.
The gallery showcased the works of several prominent automatists.  这家画廊展出了几位著名自动主义者的作品。
Many factory workers felt like automatists, repeating the same motions day after day. 许多工厂工人感觉自己像个机器人，日复一日地重复着相同的动作。
Synonyms: robots, machines, automatons
Antonyms: conscious actors, deliberate artists
Noun
