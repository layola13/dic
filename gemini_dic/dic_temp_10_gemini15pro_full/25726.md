bunts:/bʌnts/
n.|触击（棒球），轻击球|A bunt is a batting technique in baseball where the batter lightly taps the ball instead of swinging. |The batter surprised everyone with a bunt down the third base line.  击球员以沿着三垒线的触击球 sorprendió 了所有人。|Synonyms: tap, push bunt|Antonyms:  home run, grand slam
v.|触击，轻击（球）|To bunt is to lightly tap the ball in baseball. |He bunted the ball to advance the runner. 他触击球以推进跑垒员。|Synonyms: tap, push bunt|Antonyms: slug, swing for the fences
词性:名词/动词
