overdiversity:/ˌoʊvərdaɪˈvɜːrsɪti/ | n. | 过度多样性，过度分化 |  The condition of having too much diversity, often leading to inefficiency or instability.  Excessive biodiversity, potentially leading to ecological imbalances. | The overdiversity of products offered by the company made it difficult for customers to choose.  这家公司提供的产品种类过多，让顾客难以选择。

The overdiversity of species in this small area is putting a strain on the limited resources.  这个小区域物种的过度多样性给有限的资源带来了压力。 |  hyperdiversity, excessive diversity | lack of diversity, homogeneity, uniformity | noun
