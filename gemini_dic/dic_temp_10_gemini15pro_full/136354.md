nonresistive:/ˌnɒnrɪˈzɪstɪv/
adjective
无电阻的;非电阻的
Not having or exhibiting electrical resistance.
The material is nonresistive, allowing electricity to flow freely.  这种材料是无电阻的，允许电流自由流动。
The superconducting material exhibited nonresistive behavior at low temperatures.  这种超导材料在低温下表现出无电阻特性。

synonym:conductive, nonresistant
antonym:resistive, resistant
词性:adjective
