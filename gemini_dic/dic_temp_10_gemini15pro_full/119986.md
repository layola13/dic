million:/ˈmɪljən/ |n. |百万；无数|A very large number (a thousand thousand).|There are millions of stars in the sky. 天空中有数百万颗星星。| Example: Millions of people watched the World Cup final. 数百万人观看了世界杯决赛。|Synonyms: myriad, countless, innumerable|Antonyms: few, little|Noun

million:/ˈmɪljən/ |adj. |百万的；无数的|Amounting to one million in number.| The city has a population of several million. 这座城市有几百万人口。| Example: The lottery winner received a million-dollar prize. 彩票中奖者获得了百万美元的奖金。|Synonyms: numerous, innumerable|Antonyms: few, little|Adjective
