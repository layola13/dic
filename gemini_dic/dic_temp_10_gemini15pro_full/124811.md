mucific:/mjuːˈsɪfɪk/
adjective
产生粘液的; 粘液性的
forming or secreting mucus; of, relating to, or resembling mucus.
The mucific glands secrete a thick, sticky substance. 这些粘液腺分泌一种粘稠的物质。
近义词: mucous, mucilaginous
反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "dry" or "non-secretory" in specific contexts)
词性: adjective
