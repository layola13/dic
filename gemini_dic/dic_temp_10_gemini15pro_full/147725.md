overprint:/ˌoʊvərˈprɪnt/ | n. v. |套印；叠印；重印|An additional layer of printing over an existing one.  The act of printing over something already printed. | The stamp was an overprint on an earlier issue. 这枚邮票是在早先发行的邮票上套印的。|  The red text was an overprint on the original black and white image.  红色的文字是叠印在原始黑白图像上的。 | synonym: superimpose, surprint | antonym: underprint | n. v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + print.  "Over-" signifies "above" or "in addition to." "Print" refers to the process of reproducing text or images.
