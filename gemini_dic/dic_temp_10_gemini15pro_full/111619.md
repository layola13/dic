localisable:/ˌləʊkəˈlaɪzəbl/ /ˌloʊkəˈlaɪzəbl/| adj.|可本地化的，可局部化的|Able to be adapted to suit the language, culture, or other specific requirements of a particular locality.  The software is localisable into multiple languages.|The software is easily localisable, making it suitable for international markets.  该软件易于本地化，使其适合国际市场。

The game's text is fully localisable, allowing for translations into many different languages.  游戏的文本完全可以本地化，可以翻译成许多不同的语言。|近义词：adaptable, translatable|反义词: non-localisable, fixed|词性: adjective
