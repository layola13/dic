nonnutritiously:/ˌnɒnnuˈtrɪʃəsli/
adverb
没有营养地；不提供营养地
in a way that does not provide the body with the substances it needs to grow and stay healthy.
The child was eating nonnutritiously, mostly candy and chips.  这个孩子吃得很没有营养，大部分是糖果和薯片。
They often eat nonnutritiously due to their busy schedules.  由于他们忙碌的日程安排，他们经常吃得没有营养。
近义词: unhealthily, poorly
反义词: nutritiously, healthily
词性: adverb

词源:
non- (prefix):  not
nutritious (adjective): nourishing, providing nutrients
-ly (suffix):  forms adverbs
