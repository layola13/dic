ferre:/ˈfer.e/ /ˈfɛr.eɪ/ | v. |to bear, carry, bring| To carry or bring something.  Derived from the Proto-Indo-European root *bʰer- (to carry, bear).  Forms the basis for many English words related to carrying or enduring, such as "transfer," "offer," "infer," and "refer."| She had to ferre the heavy box across the room. 她不得不把沉重的箱子搬到房间的另一边。|  He offered to ferre the groceries for her. 他提出帮她提杂货。| synonyms: carry, bear, transport, convey | antonyms: leave, abandon | verb
