confectory:/kənˈfɛktəri/ | n. | 糖果店；甜食店；（修道院或大学的）零食店 | A place where sweets or confections are made or sold, especially in a monastery or college. |  The monks sold their homemade jams and honey in the confectory. 修道士们在糖果店里出售他们自制的果酱和蜂蜜。

The college confectory was a popular spot for students to grab a snack between classes.  大学的零食店是学生课间吃零食的热门场所。 | 近义词：sweet shop, candy store, confectioner's | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


confectory:/kənˈfɛktəri/ | adj. |  糖果的；甜食的 (较少用) | Relating to confections or sweetmeats. | The confectory aromas filled the air.  空气中弥漫着糖果的香味。(较少用这种用法) | 近义词： confectionary | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *confectorium, from Latin confectus (“prepared, preserved”) + -orium (suffix forming nouns indicating a place for something).  Confectus is the past participle of conficere (“to make ready, complete, finish, perform”).
