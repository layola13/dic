anticompetitivenesses:/ˌæntiˌkəmˈpɛtɪtɪvnɪsɪz/
|n.|反竞争行为；反竞争性|The state of being anticompetitive; actions or practices that discourage or prevent competition.  It refers to behaviors or situations that stifle competition in a market, often to the detriment of consumers.|The company was accused of engaging in a range of anticompetitivenesses, including price fixing and exclusive dealing.|近义词:monopolistic practices, unfair competition, restrictive practices|反义词:fair competition, open market, competitiveness|词性:名词
