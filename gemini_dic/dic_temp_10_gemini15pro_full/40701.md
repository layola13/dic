conversos:/kənˈvɛərsəʊz/ (British), /kənˈvɜːrsoʊz/ (American)| n. |皈依者，改宗者（尤指中世纪在西班牙和葡萄牙改信基督教的犹太人）|People who have converted to another religion, specifically used to refer to Jewish people who converted to Christianity in medieval Spain and Portugal. | Many conversos continued to practice Judaism in secret. 许多改宗者仍然秘密地信奉犹太教。| The conversos faced persecution from both the Christian and Jewish communities.  改宗者面临着来自基督教和犹太教社区的迫害。| synonyms: converts, proselytes | antonyms: apostates | noun
