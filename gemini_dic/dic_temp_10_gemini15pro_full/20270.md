birthright:/ˈbɜːθraɪt/ | n. |与生俱来的权利；天赋的权利 |A right or privilege that you have from birth, especially one that is given to you because of your family or social position. |  Everyone has a birthright to education. 每个人都有受教育的与生俱来的权利。

She believes access to healthcare is a birthright. 她认为获得医疗保健是一项与生俱来的权利。 | 近义词：privilege, prerogative, entitlement | 反义词：acquired right | 词性：名词
