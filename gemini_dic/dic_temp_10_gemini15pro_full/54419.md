disincorporation:/ˌdɪsɪnkɔːrpəˈreɪʃən/
n.
解散，撤销法人资格
The formal act of dissolving a corporation.  A company undergoes disincorporation when it ceases to exist as a legal entity.
The disincorporation of the company resulted in job losses. 公司的解散导致了失业。
The board voted for the disincorporation of the subsidiary. 董事会投票决定解散子公司。

近义词:dissolution, liquidation, winding-up
反义词:incorporation, formation, establishment
词性:名词
