earnestful:/ˈɜːrnɪstfʊl/| adj.|认真的，热心的，真诚的|Characterized by or showing deep sincerity and seriousness.|She gave an earnestful plea for help. 她恳切地请求帮助。|近义词：sincere, serious, fervent, heartfelt, passionate|反义词：frivolous, insincere, apathetic|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "earnest" (Old English *eornust, "seriousness, zeal") + "-ful" (suffix meaning "full of").
