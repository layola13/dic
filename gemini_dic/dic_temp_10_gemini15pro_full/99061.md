interassured:/ˌɪntərəˈʃʊərd/ | adj. | 相互确定的，彼此放心的 |  Feeling mutually assured or confident; having a shared sense of certainty or trust.  This word is not common in modern English and might sound archaic or formal. It's constructed from "inter-" (between) and "assured" (confident). |  "The two parties, interassured of their common goal, signed the treaty." 双方对共同目标确信无疑，签署了条约。 | Near synonyms: mutually assured, confident, trusting, certain | Antonyms: uncertain, doubtful, distrustful | adjective
