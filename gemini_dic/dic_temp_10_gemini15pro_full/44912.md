cryptic:/ˈkrɪptɪk/| adj.|神秘的，隐秘的，含义模糊的|Having a meaning that is mysterious or obscure.|The detective was puzzled by the cryptic message left by the killer.  侦探对凶手留下的神秘信息感到困惑。|例句：Her cryptic remarks left me wondering what she really meant. 她隐晦的话让我琢磨不透她到底是什么意思。|近义词：enigmatic, mysterious, obscure, ambiguous|反义词：clear, obvious, explicit, straightforward|词性:形容词
cryptically:/ˈkrɪptɪkli/| adv.|神秘地，隐秘地，含义模糊地|In a cryptic manner; mysteriously or obscurely.|She smiled cryptically and refused to answer the question. 她神秘地笑了笑，拒绝回答问题。|例句：He answered cryptically, leaving me more confused than before. 他含糊其辞地回答，让我比以前更困惑了。|近义词：enigmatically, mysteriously, obscurely, ambiguously|反义词：clearly, obviously, explicitly, straightforwardly|词性:副词
