aftermath:/ˈæftərmæθ/ /ˈɑːftərmɑːθ/| n. |后果，余波，灾后|the consequences or after-effects of a significant event, especially a catastrophe|例句：The aftermath of the hurricane was devastating. 飓风过后一片狼藉。
