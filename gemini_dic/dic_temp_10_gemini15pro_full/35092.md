PHRASE->
airing on the clothesline (比喻公开暴露):  His dirty laundry was aired on the clothesline for all to see. 他的丑事被公之于众。
taken off the clothesline (比喻收回，撤回): The controversial proposal was taken off the clothesline after public outcry.  这项有争议的提案在公众的强烈抗议后被撤回了。
put on the clothesline (比喻公开展示):  She put her skills on the clothesline during the competition.  她在比赛中充分展示了自己的技能。
right off the clothesline (比喻新鲜的，刚出炉的):  This news is right off the clothesline!  这个消息是最新鲜的！
hang out to dry (比喻置之不理，不管不顾):  He felt like his friends hung him out to dry when he needed their support. 当他需要朋友的支持时，他感到被他们抛弃了。
