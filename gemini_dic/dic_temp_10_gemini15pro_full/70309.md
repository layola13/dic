fiducially:/fɪˈdjuːʃəli/ | adv. | 受托地，信托地; 基于信用的 | In a fiduciary capacity; as a trustee.  Relating to or involving a confidence or trust. | The lawyer acted fiducially for his client.  这位律师以受托人的身份为其客户行事。 |  trustworthily, responsibly | irresponsibly, negligently | adverb

fiduciary:/fɪˈdjuːʃəri/ | adj. n. | 信托的；受托的；信用的 |  (adj.) Holding something in trust for another; (n.) A person who holds assets in trust for a beneficiary. | (adj.) The company has a fiduciary duty to its shareholders. 这家公司对其股东负有信托责任。 (n.)  He is the fiduciary of the estate. 他是该遗产的受托人。 | (adj.) trustee, confidential | (adj.) irresponsible, untrustworthy | adjective, noun
