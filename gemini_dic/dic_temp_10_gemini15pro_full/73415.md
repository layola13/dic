foreland:/ˈfɔːrlənd/ | n. | 前沿地带，海角，岬角 | A piece of land that projects into the sea or other body of water; an area of land lying in front of something else. | The ship rounded the foreland and entered the bay. 这艘船绕过海角进入了海湾。 | Synonyms: headland, promontory, cape, peninsula | Antonyms: hinterland | noun

foreland:/ˈfɔːrlənd/ | n. |  前沿，领先地位 | An advanced or leading position in a particular field or activity. | The company is at the foreland of technological innovation.  这家公司处于技术创新的前沿。 | Synonyms: vanguard, forefront, leading edge | Antonyms: rear, backwater | noun
