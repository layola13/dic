nonfervidly:/ˌnɒnˈfɜːrvɪdli/ | adv. | 冷淡地，不热情地 |  In a manner lacking fervor or passion; without enthusiasm. | He nonfervidly agreed to help with the project, clearly lacking any real interest. 他冷淡地同意帮忙做这个项目，显然缺乏真正的兴趣。|  apathetically, indifferently, listlessly | enthusiastically, passionately, fervently | adverb

Etymology->
From non- + fervid + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," "fervid" comes from the Latin "fervidus," meaning "glowing," "burning," or "passionate," and "-ly" is a suffix forming adverbs.
