ababa:/əˈbɑːbə/| n. |阿巴巴（一种埃塞俄比亚的披肩）|A handwoven cotton cloth, often brightly colored and patterned, made in Ethiopia and used as a shawl or scarf.|She wore a beautiful ababa over her shoulders. 她肩上披着一条漂亮的阿巴巴披肩。|近义词：shawl, scarf|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun

词源: From Amharic አባባ (ʾäbaba).
