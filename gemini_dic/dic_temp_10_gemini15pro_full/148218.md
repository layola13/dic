overshrunk:/ˌoʊvərˈʃrʌŋk/ | adj. | 过度缩水，缩水过度的 |  Having shrunk excessively, usually due to washing or drying at too high a temperature.  |  My favorite sweater is now overshrunk and unwearable. 我最喜欢的毛衣现在缩水过度，没法穿了。 | synonyms: shrunken, undersized, too small | antonyms: oversized, too big | adjective
