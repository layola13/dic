hyperingenuity:/ˌhaɪpərˌɪndʒɪˈnjuːɪti/
|n.|超级精巧; 过度精巧|Extreme ingenuity; excessive cleverness.  It often implies an overcomplicated or unnecessarily elaborate solution to a problem.|His hyperingenuity led him to design a needlessly complex system.|近义词: over-ingenuity, sophistication, elaborateness|反义词: simplicity, artlessness|词性:名词

词源:
"hyper-" (Greek): over, above, beyond
"-ingenuity" (Latin): ingenuitas "natural capacity, talent, cleverness"
