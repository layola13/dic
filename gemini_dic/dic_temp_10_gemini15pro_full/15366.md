backsword:/ˈbæksɔːrd/
n.
单手刀，反手刀
A type of single-edged sword, typically straight and used for cutting and thrusting, with a hilt designed to protect the hand.
He skillfully wielded his backsword, deflecting his opponent's blows. 他熟练地挥舞着他的单手刀，挡开了对手的攻击。
The museum displayed several antique backswords from different eras. 博物馆展出了几把不同时期的古董单手刀。
近义词:sword, cutlass
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'shield' as a defensive counterpoint)
词性:noun
