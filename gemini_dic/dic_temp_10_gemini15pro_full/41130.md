copse:/kɒps/ (British) /kɑːps/ (American)| n. |小树林，灌木丛|A small group of trees growing close together.|We walked through the copse to the river. 我们穿过小树林走到河边。|Example: The rabbits darted into the copse for safety. 兔子冲进小树林寻求安全。|Synonyms: thicket, grove, wood, spinney|Antonyms: field, meadow|Noun
