chillily:/ˈtʃɪlɪli/ | adv. | 冷淡地，寒气逼人地 | In a chilly manner; coldly.  | She greeted him chillily, offering only a curt nod. 她冷淡地问候他，只草草地点了点头。|  She spoke chillily to the unwelcome visitor. 她对这位不速之客说话冷冰冰的。 | coldly, icily, frostily | warmly, kindly | adverb

Etymology: From chilly + -ly.  "Chilly" comes from "chill," which ultimately derives from Old English "ciele" (cold).
