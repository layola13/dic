outspeeding:/ˌaʊtˈspiːdɪŋ/
verb
超越；比…速度快
Moving faster than something or someone else.
The newer cars easily outsped the older models on the track.  这些新车在赛道上轻松超越了旧款车型。
The cheetah outsped the gazelle. 猎豹比瞪羚跑得快。
Synonyms: outrun, overtake, surpass
Antonyms: lag behind, trail
