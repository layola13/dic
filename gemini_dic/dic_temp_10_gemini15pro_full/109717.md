leveled-off:/ˈlɛvəld ˈɒf/ (British), /ˈlɛvəld ˈɔːf/ (American)| v. |趋于平稳，稳定下来|To cease rising or falling and become level or steady.|After a period of rapid growth, sales leveled off.  经过一段时间的快速增长后，销售额趋于平稳。|The plane leveled off at 30,000 feet.  飞机在3万英尺的高空保持平稳飞行。| 近义词:stabilized, plateaued, evened out|反义词:increased, decreased, fluctuated|词性:动词

leveled-off:/ˈlɛvəld ˈɒf/ (British), /ˈlɛvəld ˈɔːf/ (American)| adj. |平稳的，稳定的|Having become level or steady after a period of change.|The graph shows a leveled-off trend in unemployment.  图表显示失业率呈平稳趋势。|The company experienced a leveled-off period after years of expansion.  经过多年的扩张后，公司经历了一段平稳期。| 近义词:stable, steady, flat|反义词:increasing, decreasing, fluctuating|词性:形容词
