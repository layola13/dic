Lübeck: /ˈlyːbɛk/ | n. | 吕贝克 | A historic port city in northern Germany on the Baltic Sea. |  Lübeck is a UNESCO World Heritage site. 吕贝克是联合国教科文组织世界遗产地。| synonyms: Hanseatic city, port city | antonyms: inland city | noun

Etymology: From Middle Low German *Lubeke, from Old Saxon *Liubiki, meaning "lovely" or "pleasant place".
