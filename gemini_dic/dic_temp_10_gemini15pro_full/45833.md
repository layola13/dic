customize:/ˈkʌstəˌmaɪz/| v. |定制，个性化|To modify something to suit a particular individual or task. |
She customized her car with new paint and rims. 她用新的油漆和轮毂定制了她的汽车。

The software can be customized to fit the needs of any business. 这个软件可以定制以满足任何企业的需要。

|近义词：personalize, tailor, adapt, modify|反义词: standardize, generalize|词性:动词|
