hydrophore:/ˈhaɪdrəfɔːr/
noun
输水管;提水器;贮水器
A device for raising water, especially for supplying a cistern or boiler.
The old hydrophore system needs to be replaced.  旧的提水系统需要更换。

近义词:water pump, water lifter
反义词:N/A
词性:n.


Etymology:
From French *hydrophore*, from Ancient Greek *ὕδωρ* (*húdōr*, “water”) + *φέρω* (*phérō*, “I carry”).
