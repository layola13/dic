ascending:/əˈsɛndɪŋ/ | adj. | 上升的，向上的 | going up; increasing in size or importance | The hikers followed the ascending path up the mountain.  徒步旅行者沿着上升的小路爬上山。| ascending order: The numbers are arranged in ascending order. 数字按升序排列。 | synonyms: rising, climbing, increasing, mounting | antonyms: descending, falling, decreasing, declining | adjective

ascending:/əˈsɛndɪŋ/ | n. | 上升，登高 | the act of going up | The ascending of the hot air balloon was a breathtaking sight. 热气球的上升是一个令人叹为观止的景象。 | synonyms: ascent, rise, climb | antonyms: descent, fall, decline | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ascendens*, present participle of *ascendere* ("to climb up, mount, ascend"), from *ad* ("to, towards") + *scandere* ("to climb").
