ectobronchium:/ˌɛktoʊˈbrɒŋkiəm/
n.
外鳃
a gill developing outside the usual gill area, as in certain larval amphibians.
Larval salamanders possess ectobronchia. 幼年蝾螈拥有外鳃。
ectobronchus, external gill
internal gill
noun
