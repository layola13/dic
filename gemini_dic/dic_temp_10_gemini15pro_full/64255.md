equipollency:/ˌiːkwɪˈpɒlənsɪ/ /ˌiːkwɪˈpɑːlənsɪ/
n.
均势，同等效力，等价
The state of being equipollent; equality of power or force.
The treaty aimed to create a military equipollency between the two nations. 该条约旨在在两国之间建立军事均势。
equivalence, parity, equality
inequality, disparity, imbalance
