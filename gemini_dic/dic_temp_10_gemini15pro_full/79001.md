gismondite:/ˈɡɪzməndaɪt/
noun
菱沸石
A zeolite mineral, a hydrated calcium aluminium silicate.
Gismondite crystals were found in the volcanic rock. 在火山岩中发现了菱沸石晶体。
近义词:zeolite, phillipsite, chabazite (all zeolites)
反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific mineral)
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
Named after Italian mineralogist Carlo Giuseppe Gismondi (1762-1826).
