blossom: /ˈblɒsəm/ (UK) /ˈblɑːsəm/ (US)| n. vi. |花朵；开花；兴旺；发展|The state of flowering, especially of fruit trees.  A flower, especially one on a fruit tree. To produce blossoms; to flourish or thrive.|例句：The cherry trees are in full blossom. 樱花盛开了。|近义词：bloom, flower, flourish, thrive|反义词：wither, fade, decline|词性:noun, verb

blossom: /ˈblɒsəm/ (UK) /ˈblɑːsəm/ (US)| vi. |开花；兴旺；发展|To develop or come to a promising stage. To mature or develop in a promising or healthy way.|例句：Their friendship blossomed into love. 他们的友谊发展成了爱情。|近义词：bloom, flower, flourish, thrive|反义词：wither, fade, decline|词性:verb
