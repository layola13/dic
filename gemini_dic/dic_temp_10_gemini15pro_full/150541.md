panzers:/ˈpæn.zərz/| n.|坦克（尤指德国坦克）|German tanks, especially those used in World War II.  It comes from the German word "Panzer," short for "Panzerkampfwagen," meaning "armored fighting vehicle."|The panzers rolled across the Polish border, marking the beginning of World War II. 德军的坦克越过波兰边界，标志着第二次世界大战的开始。

The general ordered the panzers to advance. 将军命令坦克前进。


|近义词：tanks, armored vehicles|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly infantry or artillery)|词性:noun
