asafetida:/ˌæsəˈfɛtɪdə/ | n. | 阿魏，阿魏胶 | A hard, resinous gum with a pungent, fetid odor obtained from the roots of various plants of the genus *Ferula*, used as a flavoring, especially in Indian cuisine. |  Asafoetida is a common ingredient in many Indian dishes. 阿魏是许多印度菜肴的常见成分。

| The strong smell of asafetida can be off-putting to some.  阿魏的强烈气味可能会让一些人反感。 | 近义词: hing, devil's dung, stinking gum | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific spice) | 词性: noun
