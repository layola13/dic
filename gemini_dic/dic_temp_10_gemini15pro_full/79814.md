glycolide: /ˈɡlaɪkəˌlaɪd/ | n. | 乙交酯; 羟基乙酸内酯 |A cyclic diester of glycolic acid. It is used as a monomer for the production of polyglycolic acid, a biodegradable polymer used in surgical sutures. | Glycolide is a key component in the production of absorbable sutures. 乙交酯是可吸收缝合线生产中的关键成分。| Glycolide readily undergoes ring-opening polymerization to form polyglycolic acid. 乙交酯很容易进行开环聚合形成聚乙醇酸。| synonyms: glycolic acid lactone | antonyms: N/A | noun
