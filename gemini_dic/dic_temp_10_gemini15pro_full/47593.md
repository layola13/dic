debatefully:/dɪˈbeɪtfʊli/
adv.
充满争议地，可争辩地
in a way that is open to debate or argument.
The issue was debatefully discussed in the parliament.  这个问题在议会中引起了激烈的辩论。
The effectiveness of the new policy is debatefully considered. 这项新政策的有效性有待商榷。
近义词: arguably, disputably, contentiously
反义词: indisputably, undoubtedly, unequivocally
词性: 副词
