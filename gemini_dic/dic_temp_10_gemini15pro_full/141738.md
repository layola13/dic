orange:/ˈɔːrɪndʒ/ (US) /ˈɒrɪndʒ/ (UK)| n. adj.|橙子，橙色|A round citrus fruit with a thick reddish-yellow rind and a sweet juicy pulp. Also, the color of this fruit.|例句：I ate an orange for breakfast. 我早餐吃了一个橙子。

例句：She wore an orange dress. 她穿了一件橙色的连衣裙。

近义词：tangerine, clementine (for the fruit); gold, amber (for the color)|反义词: (No direct antonym for the fruit) blue, green, purple (for the color) |词性: noun, adjective

orange:/ˈɔːrɪndʒ/ (US) /ˈɒrɪndʒ/ (UK)| adj.|橙色的|Of the color orange.|例句：The sunset was a vibrant orange. 日落是鲜艳的橙色。

例句：He painted the wall bright orange. 他把墙漆成了亮橙色。

近义词：gold, amber, reddish-yellow|反义词: blue, green, purple |词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *orenge (modern French orange), from Arabic nāranj, from Persian nārang, from Sanskrit nāranga ("orange tree"). The initial n- was lost in French because of the following vowel; it was reintroduced in English in the 16th century.
