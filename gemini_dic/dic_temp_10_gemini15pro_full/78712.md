gigantomachy:/ˌdʒaɪɡæntoʊˈmæki/ | n. | 巨人之战，巨人族之战 | A struggle or conflict of great scope or violence, especially one in which the protagonists are of immense size or power. | The movie depicted a dramatic gigantomachy between colossal robots and alien invaders.  这部电影描绘了巨型机器人和外星入侵者之间一场惊天动地的巨人之战。|  Example: The epic poem described a gigantomachy that shook the heavens. 这首史诗描述了一场震动天地的巨人之战。| 近义词: battle, war, struggle, clash, conflict | 反义词: peace, truce, harmony, concord | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Ancient Greek Γιγαντομαχία (Gigantomakhía), from γίγας (gígas, “giant”) + μάχη (mákhē, “battle”).
