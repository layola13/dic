cotidal:/koʊˈtaɪdəl/| adj.|同潮的，等潮的|Having the same tides or occurring at the same time as the tides.|例句：Cotidal lines on a map connect points where high tide occurs simultaneously. 地图上的等潮线连接了同时发生涨潮的点。

例句：The cotidal chart shows the progression of the tide across the ocean. 等潮图显示了潮汐在海洋中的进展。|近义词：synchronous, concurrent|反义词：asynchronous|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "co-" (together) + "tidal" (relating to tides).  "Co-" comes from Latin "com-" (together, with). "Tidal" comes from Old English "tid" (time, season).
