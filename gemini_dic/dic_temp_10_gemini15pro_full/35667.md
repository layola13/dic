cobbers:/ˈkɒbəz/ (UK), /ˈkɑːbərz/ (US)| n.|朋友，伙伴，哥们儿|A close friend or companion, often used in Australian and British English.  It carries a connotation of mateship and camaraderie.|例句：I'm going to the pub with my cobbers tonight. 今晚我要和我的朋友们去酒吧。

例句：He's one of my oldest cobbers. 他是我最老的朋友之一。

近义词：friends, mates, pals, buddies|反义词:enemies, foes|词性:noun
