nott:/nɒt/ | adv. | 不，不是 | Not is the negative particle, used to form the negative of verbs, adjectives, and other words. | It's not raining today. 今天不下雨。| 近义词: no | 反义词: yes | 词性: 副词

nott:/nɑːt/ | n. | (古语) 理发，剪发 | An archaic term for haircut or the act of cutting hair.  | He went to the barber for a nott. 他去理发店理发了。| 近义词: haircut, trim | 反义词: (无) | 词性: 名词
