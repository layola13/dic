anecdotal:/ˌænɪkˈdəʊtl/ (British), /ˌænɪkˈdoʊtl/ (American)| adj.|轶事的，趣闻的，传闻的|Based on personal accounts or stories rather than on systematic research or scientific evidence.|例句：The evidence he presented was largely anecdotal. 他提供的证据大多是轶事传闻。|例句：Anecdotal evidence suggests that the new drug is effective. 传闻证据表明这种新药很有效。|近义词：informal, unscientific, hearsay|反义词：empirical, scientific, statistical|词性: adjective
