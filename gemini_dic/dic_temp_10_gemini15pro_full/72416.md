fluffier:/ˈflʌfiər/ | adj. | 更蓬松的，更柔软的 |  The comparative form of "fluffy," meaning having a soft, light, and airy texture.  It indicates a greater degree of fluffiness than something else. | The kitten's fur is fluffier than the older cat's. 小猫的毛比老猫的毛更蓬松。|  softer, downier, plusher | coarser, flatter, smoother | adjective

fluffier:/ˈflʌfiər/ | adj. | 更轻浮的，更肤浅的 | In informal contexts, "fluffier" can also describe something as being more lighthearted, less serious, or even superficial.  | The new sitcom is fluffier than their previous drama. 这部新的情景喜剧比他们之前的电视剧更轻松愉快。| lighter, frothier, airier | heavier, more serious, more profound | adjective
