dentilave:/ˈdɛntɪˌleɪv/
n.
齿状装饰，锯齿形花边
A series of small, tooth-like projections used as an architectural ornament or as a decorative border.
The dentilave along the roofline added a touch of elegance to the building.  屋顶线上的齿状装饰为建筑增添了一丝优雅。
The cake was decorated with a dentilave of white icing.  蛋糕上装饰着白色的糖霜锯齿花边。

smoothness
