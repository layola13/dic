mysterioso:/ˌmɪstɪriˈoʊsoʊ/ | adj. | 神秘的，诡秘的 |  Used in music to indicate a passage should be played in a mysterious and secretive manner.  It derives from the Italian word "misterioso," meaning "mysterious." | The composer marked the passage *mysterioso*, creating a haunting effect. 作曲家将这段标为*mysterioso*，营造出一种令人难忘的效果。 | mysterious, secretive, enigmatic | obvious, clear, straightforward | adjective
