indecisively:/ˌɪndɪˈsaɪsɪvli/| adv.|优柔寡断地，犹豫不决地|In a manner that shows an inability to make decisions quickly and effectively.  Characterized by hesitation and uncertainty.|He paced indecisively back and forth. 他犹豫不决地来回踱步。|Synonyms: hesitantly, tentatively, uncertainly, irresolutely|Antonyms: decisively, resolutely, firmly, confidently|Adverb
