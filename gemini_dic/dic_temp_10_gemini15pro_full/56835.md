dornick:/ˈdɔːrnɪk/| n.|多尼克粗麻布；一种粗麻布|A coarse linen cloth, originally made in Dornick (now Tournai, Belgium).|The curtains were made of a heavy dornick.|近义词:linen, burlap, sackcloth|反义词:silk, satin|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Middle Dutch *dornek, from Dornick, the Flemish name for Tournai, Belgium, where the cloth was originally made.
