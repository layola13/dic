cespitous:/ˈsespɪtəs/| adj.|丛生的，簇生的，草皮状的|Growing in dense tufts or mats; forming a turf or sod.|例句：The cespitous growth of the grass made it difficult to walk through. 丛生的草使得很难穿过。|例句：Cespitose plants are often found in harsh environments.  簇生植物常出现在恶劣的环境中。|近义词：tufted, matted, clumped|反义词：scattered, dispersed|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *caespitosus, from caespes (“turf, sod”).
