crossover:/ˈkrɒsˌəʊvə(r)/ (UK), /ˈkrɑːsˌoʊvər/ (US)| n. | 交叉, 交接, 跨界, 转场, 跨界作品 | a place where two roads, lines, etc. cross each other, or a bridge that carries one road over another; something that combines the qualities of two different things | The pedestrian crossover is clearly marked. 行人交叉路口有明确的标记。 | synonyms: intersection, junction, interchange, bridge, blend, hybrid | antonyms: separation, divergence | noun

crossover:/ˈkrɒsˌəʊvə(r)/ (UK), /ˈkrɑːsˌoʊvər/ (US)| adj. | 交叉的, 跨界的 | relating to or involving a combination of two different things | The film is a crossover hit, appealing to both children and adults. 这部电影是一部跨界热门影片，对儿童和成人都有吸引力。 | synonyms: interdisciplinary, hybrid, blended | antonyms: specialized, separate | adjective
