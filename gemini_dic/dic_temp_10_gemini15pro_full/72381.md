flubbing:/ˈflʌbɪŋ/
verb (present participle of flub)
笨拙地做；搞砸；弄糟
performing poorly or making mistakes, especially in a performance or presentation
He kept flubbing his lines during the rehearsal. 他在排练中总是念错台词。
近义词: messing up, botching, bungling, muffing
反义词: succeeding, excelling, mastering
词性: verb (present participle)


flub:/flʌb/
noun
失误；失败
a blunder; a mistake
Her first attempt at baking a cake was a complete flub. 她第一次尝试烤蛋糕完全失败了。
近义词: blunder, mistake, error, gaffe
反义词: success, triumph
词性: noun
