hydrate:/ˈhaɪdreɪt/ (US), /ˈhaɪdreɪt/ (UK)| n. v.|水合物，使成水合物|A compound formed by the combination of water with some other substance.|The doctor told me to keep hydrated. 医生告诉我保持水分。|Synonyms: moisturize, dampen|Antonyms: dehydrate, desiccate|noun, verb

hydrate:/ˈhaɪdreɪt/ (US), /ˈhaɪdreɪt/ (UK)| v.|使成水合物，补充水分|To cause to take up or combine with water or the elements of water.|I need to hydrate after that workout.  在那次锻炼后我需要补充水分。|Synonyms:  rehydrate, water|Antonyms: dehydrate, dry|verb
