obesity:/oʊˈbiːsəti/ /əʊˈbiːsɪti/| n.|肥胖，肥胖症|The increasing rates of obesity are a serious public health concern. 肥胖率的上升是一个严重的公共卫生问题。| Obesity can lead to a number of health problems, including heart disease and diabetes. 肥胖会导致许多健康问题，包括心脏病和糖尿病。|近义词：adiposity, corpulence|反义词:thinness, leanness|词性:名词

obese:/oʊˈbiːs/ /əʊˈbiːs/| adj.|肥胖的|Obese individuals are at higher risk for certain health conditions. 肥胖的人患某些疾病的风险更高。|He was morbidly obese and struggled to walk. 他病态肥胖，走路都很吃力。|近义词：overweight, fat|反义词:thin, slim|词性:形容词
