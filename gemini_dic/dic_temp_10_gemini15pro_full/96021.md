indaba:/ɪnˈdɑːbə/
noun
会议，讨论，特别指南非的部落会议
a conference or discussion, especially a traditional Zulu assembly
The tribal elders held an indaba to discuss the drought. 部落长老们举行了一次indaba来讨论旱灾。
conference, meeting, assembly, council, gathering
argument, disagreement
n.
