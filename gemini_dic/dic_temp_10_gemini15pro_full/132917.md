nonelliptic:/ˌnɒnɪˈlɪptɪk/
adjective
非椭圆的
Not elliptic; not having the shape of an ellipse.
The trajectory of the spacecraft was nonelliptic, following a more complex path.  这艘宇宙飞船的轨迹是非椭圆形的，遵循一条更复杂的路径。
The shape of the amoeba was constantly changing, often appearing nonelliptic.  变形虫的形状不断变化，经常呈现非椭圆形。
近义词:non-elliptical,anisotropic(在某些情况下)
反义词:elliptic, elliptical
词性:adjective
