holidaying:/ˈhɒlədeɪɪŋ/ /ˈhɑːlədeɪɪŋ/
verb
度假，休假
spending a holiday
例句：They are holidaying in Spain this summer. 他们今年夏天在西班牙度假。

例句：We were holidaying in the mountains when we heard the news. 我们在山上度假时听到了这个消息。

近义词：vacationing, taking a break, traveling
反义词：working

词性：verb (present participle)
