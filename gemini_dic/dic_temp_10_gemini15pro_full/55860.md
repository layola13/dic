divining:/dɪˈvaɪnɪŋ/
adj. |占卜的，预言的| Relating to or used in the practice of divination; prophetic.|  The old woman had divining powers, it was said. 据说这位老妇人有占卜的能力。|近义词：prophetic, oracular, mantic|反义词:mundane, ordinary|词性:adjective
divining:/dɪˈvaɪnɪŋ/
n. |占卜，预言| The practice of seeking knowledge of the future or the unknown by supernatural means.| She practiced divining with tarot cards. 她用塔罗牌占卜。|近义词：foretelling, soothsaying, prophecy|反义词: - |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English divinen, from Old French deviner, from Latin divinare (“to foresee, foretell, predict, prophesy, divine”), from divinus (“divine”), from divus (“god”).
