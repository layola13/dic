mannered:/ˈmænərd/| adj.|有礼貌的，矫揉造作的，做作的|Having manners, especially good manners; behaving in a polite way.  Can also mean artificial or affected in behaviour.|She was a very mannered young lady.|近义词：polite, courteous, refined, affected, artificial|反义词：rude, impolite, natural, unaffected|词性:形容词

词源: From the noun "manner" + "-ed".  "Manner" comes from Old French "maniere" meaning "fashion, way, kind".
