battlements: /ˈbætəlmənts/ | n. | 城垛；雉堞 | A parapet wall on a castle or fortification, with alternating high sections (merlons) and low sections (embrasures) for defense. |  The castle's battlements offered a commanding view of the surrounding countryside. 城堡的城垛可以俯瞰周围的乡村。| synonyms: parapets, ramparts, fortifications | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider: openings, gaps) | noun

battlements: /ˈbætəlmənts/ | n. | (比喻)防御工事；保护物 | Anything resembling a battlement in form or function. | The CEO saw his team as the company’s battlements against the competition. 首席执行官将他的团队视为公司对抗竞争的防御工事。| synonyms: defenses, safeguards, protections | antonyms: vulnerabilities, weaknesses | noun
