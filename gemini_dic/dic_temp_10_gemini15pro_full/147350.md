overmystifies:/ˌoʊvərˈmɪstɪfaɪz/
verb
使过度神秘化，使过于玄妙
to make something seem more mysterious or complicated than it actually is, often deliberately
The magician overmystified his tricks, refusing to reveal even the simplest secrets. 魔术师故弄玄虚，即使是最简单的秘密也不肯透露。
近义词:obfuscate, mystify, confuse
反义词:clarify, explain, simplify
词性:v.


overmystified:/ˌoʊvərˈmɪstɪfaɪd/
adjective
被过度神秘化的，被过于玄妙化的
having been made to seem more mysterious or complicated than necessary
The audience felt overmystified by the lecturer's complex jargon and obscure references. 观众觉得演讲者复杂的术语和晦涩的参考文献让他们一头雾水。
近义词:confused, bewildered, perplexed
反义词:clear, understandable, lucid
词性:adj.


词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "mystify" (verb meaning "to make obscure or mysterious")  "mystify" ultimately comes from the Greek "mystes" (one initiated into mysteries).
