guideboard:/ˈɡaɪdbɔːrd/| n. |路标；指导方针；准则|A sign that gives directions or information to travelers or users of a particular place or system. | The guideboards pointed us toward the scenic overlook. 路标指引我们前往风景优美的观景台。| The company's values serve as a guideboard for all its employees. 公司的价值观是所有员工的指导方针。| synonym: signpost, marker, direction, guideline | antonym: misdirection,  obfuscation | noun
