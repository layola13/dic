monophthongal:/ˌmɑːnəfˈθɑːŋɡəl/ /ˌmɒnəfˈθɒŋɡəl/| adj.|单元音的|Of or relating to a monophthong; consisting of a single vowel sound; (of a language) having monophthongs.|The word "cat" has a monophthongal vowel sound. |近义词: pure (vowel)|反义词: diphthongal|词性:adjective
