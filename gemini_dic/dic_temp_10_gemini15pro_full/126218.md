myalgias:/maɪˈældʒəz/
noun
肌肉疼痛
muscle aches and pains
例句：She complained of myalgias after her strenuous workout.  她剧烈运动后抱怨肌肉疼痛。

例句：The patient presented with fever and myalgias. 病人出现发烧和肌肉疼痛的症状。

近义词: muscle pain, muscle aches, myodynia
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用表示健康的词) well-being, comfort

词性: noun (名词)
