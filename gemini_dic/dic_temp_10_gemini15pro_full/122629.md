modifiability:/ˌmɒdɪfaɪəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可修改性，可变性 | The modifiability of the software is a key advantage, allowing it to adapt to changing user needs.  软件的可修改性是一个关键优势，使其能够适应不断变化的用户需求。 | Example:  The system's modifiability allows for easy updates and improvements.  该系统的可修改性允许轻松更新和改进。
The high modifiability of the clay makes it ideal for sculpting. 这种粘土的高可塑性使它非常适合雕刻。 | Synonyms: alterability, adaptability, flexibility | Antonyms: immutability, inflexibility, rigidity | noun
