busboys:/ˈbʌsbɔɪz/
n.
餐馆勤杂工，餐厅侍应生助手
A person who assists waiters and waitresses in a restaurant by clearing tables, setting places, and doing other chores.
The busboys quickly cleared the tables after the lunch rush.  餐馆勤杂工在午餐高峰后迅速清理了桌子。
The busboy refilled the water glasses.  餐厅侍应生助手给水杯加满了水。
近义词：busser, dining room attendant
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps manager/chef as they supervise busboys)
词性: noun
