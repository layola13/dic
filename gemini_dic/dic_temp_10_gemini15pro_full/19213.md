beyondnesses:/biˈjɒndnɪsɪz/
noun
超越性；超越的境界（复数）
The state or quality of being beyond something, often referring to multiple instances of surpassing limits or expectations.  This word emphasizes the plural nature of these instances.
He explored the beyondnesses of human experience through meditation. 他通过冥想探索了人类经验的超越性。
transcendencies, sublimities
limitations, confinements
