co-assume:/koʊəˈsuːm/
verb
共同承担，共同假定
To assume something jointly with another or others. To undertake jointly; share.
例句：They co-assumed responsibility for the project. 他们共同承担了这个项目的责任。

例句：The two parties co-assumed the risk of the investment. 双方共同承担了投资的风险。
近义词：share, jointly assume
反义词：renounce, disavow
词性:v.
