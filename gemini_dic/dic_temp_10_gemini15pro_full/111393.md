lividness:/ˈlɪvɪdnəs/| n.|青紫; 铅灰色;  lividity;  铁青;  惨白|The lividness of the bruise was alarming.  瘀伤的青紫色令人担忧。|近义词：paleness, wanness, discoloration, ashenness|反义词: ruddiness, flush, rosiness|词性:noun
Etymology: From "livid" + "-ness". "Livid" comes from the Old French "livide" and Latin "lividus", meaning "bluish, lead-colored".
