de-seed:/diːˈsiːd/
verb
去除种子
remove the seeds from (a fruit or vegetable)
The chef de-seeded the peppers before stuffing them with rice.  厨师在把辣椒塞满米饭之前去除了辣椒籽。
The recipe says to de-seed the tomatoes before blending them. 食谱上说要把西红柿去籽后再混合。
近义词: seed, pit, core
反义词: sow, plant
