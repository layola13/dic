cacklers:/ˈkæklərz/| n.|咯咯叫的人/动物（尤指鹅、母鸡）; 喋喋不休的人|People or animals, especially geese or hens, that cackle. Also, people who talk incessantly and foolishly. |The cacklers in the bar were making it difficult to hear the music. 酒吧里喋喋不休的人使得很难听到音乐。|近义词：gossips, chatterboxes|反义词：quiet people|词性:名词

cackle:/ˈkækl/| v. n.|咯咯叫；喋喋不休|To make a loud, broken sound, like a hen or goose. To talk rapidly and foolishly. |The hens cackled excitedly when they laid an egg. 母鸡下蛋时兴奋地咯咯叫。|近义词：chuckle, gabble|反义词：whisper|词性:动词/名词
