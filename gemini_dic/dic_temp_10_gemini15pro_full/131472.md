noncohesively:/ˌnɒnkəʊˈhiːsɪvli/ /ˌnɑːnkəʊˈhiːsɪvli/
adverb
不连贯地，不凝聚地
in a way that does not stick together or form a united whole.
The essay was written noncohesively, jumping from topic to topic without a clear connection. 这篇文章写得缺乏连贯性，在各个主题之间跳来跳去，没有清晰的联系。
The sand on the beach was dry and noncohesive, shifting with every gust of wind.  海滩上的沙子干燥且不具凝聚力，随着阵阵风吹而移动。
incoherently, disjointedly, loosely
cohesively, unitedly
adverb
