nonstick:/ˌnɑːnˈstɪk/ /ˌnɒnˈstɪk/| adj.|不粘的，防粘的|Covered with a substance that prevents food from sticking to it.|例句：I always use a nonstick pan for frying eggs. 我煎鸡蛋总是用不粘锅。

例句：This nonstick cookware is easy to clean.  这套不粘炊具很容易清洗。


近义词：non-adhesive|反义词:sticky|词性:形容词
