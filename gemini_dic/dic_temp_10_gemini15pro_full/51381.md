determinant:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪnənt/| n. |行列式；决定因素；决定物|A quantity obtained from a square matrix of numbers and used in solving systems of linear equations.  A factor which decisively affects the nature or outcome of something.|The determinant of this matrix is zero. 这个矩阵的行列式为零。|synonyms: decisive factor, crucial factor, key factor|antonyms: insignificant factor, minor factor|noun
rank:/ræŋk/| n. v. |等级；排名；排列；位列|A position in a hierarchy.  Arrange in order according to quality or importance.  Assign a rank or rating to.|His rank in the army is captain. 他的军衔是上尉。 He ranked the wines in order of preference. 他按照喜好对葡萄酒进行了排名。|synonyms: position, status, order, arrange, classify|antonyms: disorder, disorganize|noun, verb

ranks:/ræŋks/ |n. |队伍，行列；阶层|The members of a group or organization, especially the armed forces.  A social class or group.|He joined the ranks of the unemployed. 他加入了失业者的行列。|synonyms: rows, lines, tiers, echelons|antonyms: individuals, singularity|noun
