mawworm:/ˈmɔːwɜːrm/| n. |伪善者，假虔诚的人|A person who displays exaggerated or insincere religious piety, often for personal gain.  A hypocrite who feigns religious devotion.|例句：He was considered a mawworm by his colleagues, always preaching morality but engaging in unethical practices.  他的同事认为他是个伪君子，总是宣扬道德，却从事不道德的行为。

例句：The mawworm donated a large sum of money to the church, but it was later revealed that the money was stolen.  这个伪君子向教堂捐了一大笔钱，但后来发现这笔钱是偷来的。|近义词：hypocrite, sanctimonious person, pharisee|反义词:sincere believer, genuine person|词性:noun
