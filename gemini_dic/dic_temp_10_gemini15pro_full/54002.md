discriminational: /dɪˌskrɪmɪˈneɪʃənəl/
|adj.|歧视性的，差别待遇的|Relating to or involving discrimination. Characterized by or exhibiting prejudice or bias.|例句：Discriminational practices in hiring are illegal.  招聘中的歧视性做法是非法的。|例句：The report highlighted the discriminational treatment of minorities.  该报告强调了少数群体受到的歧视性待遇。|近义词：prejudiced, biased, unfair, inequitable|反义词：fair, just, equitable, impartial|词性: 形容词
词源: From discrimination + -al.  Discrimination comes from Late Latin discriminātiōn- (stem of discriminātiō ), from discrimināt- , past participle stem of discrimināre  ‘to distinguish’, from dis-  ‘apart’ + crimināre  ‘to accuse, distinguish’.
