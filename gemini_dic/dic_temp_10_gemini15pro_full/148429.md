overstiffening:/ˌoʊvərˈstɪfənɪŋ/ | n. | 过度硬化，过度增强 |  The act of making something too stiff or rigid. overstiffen:/ˌoʊvərˈstɪfən/ | v. |  (使)过度硬化，(使)过度增强  | To make something too stiff or rigid.  | He overstiffened the batter, resulting in a dense cake. 他把面糊弄得太硬了，导致蛋糕很硬。| over-tighten, make too rigid | loosen, relax | verb
