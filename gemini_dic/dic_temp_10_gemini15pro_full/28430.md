carbonometer:/ˌkɑːrˌbɒˈnɒmɪtər/ | n. |碳量计，二氧化碳测量仪 | An instrument for measuring the amount of carbon dioxide in a gas or liquid. |  The scientist used a carbonometer to measure the CO2 levels in the atmosphere. 科学家用碳量计测量大气中二氧化碳的含量。

The carbonometer showed a significant increase in carbon dioxide emissions from the factory. 碳量计显示工厂的二氧化碳排放量显著增加。


|近义词: CO2 meter, carbon dioxide meter |反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps devices measuring other gases) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "carbo-" (combining form meaning carbon) + "-meter" (suffix meaning measuring instrument).
