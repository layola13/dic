libelled: /ˈlaɪbəld/ (US), /ˈlaɪbəld/ (UK)| v. |诽谤，中伤|To make a false and damaging statement about someone in writing or printing, or in some other permanent form (like a broadcast).  It's considered a legal term.|例句：The newspaper libelled the politician by claiming he had accepted bribes. 这家报纸声称这位政治家接受了贿赂，从而对他进行了诽谤。

例句：He was libelled in the press. 他在新闻界被诽谤。

近义词：defamed, slandered, maligned, calumniated|反义词：praised, commended, lauded|词性：动词
