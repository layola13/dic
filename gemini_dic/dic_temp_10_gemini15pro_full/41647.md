corrector:/kəˈrɛktər/ (US), /kəˈrektə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 校正者，修正者，修改者|A person or device that corrects something, especially mistakes in written or printed material.|The spelling corrector on my computer is very helpful. 我的电脑上的拼写校正器非常有用。|Synonyms: editor, proofreader, reviser, amender|Antonyms: distorter, falsifier|noun
