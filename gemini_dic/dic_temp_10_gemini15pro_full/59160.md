dyno:/ˈdaɪnoʊ/ (US), /ˈdaɪnəʊ/ (UK)| n.|测功机，功率计；(俚语)强壮的人或物|A device for measuring the power output of an engine.  (Informal) A strong person or thing. |The mechanic put the car on the dyno to test its horsepower. 技工把车放在测功机上测试它的马力。|
|synonyms: dynamometer, power meter (formal); powerhouse, stud (informal, for people)|antonyms: weakling (for people)|noun
