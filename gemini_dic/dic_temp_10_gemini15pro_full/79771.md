glutinate:/ˈɡluːtɪneɪt/| v.  adj.|粘合，胶合；使凝集|To unite or cause to adhere with or as if with glue; to stick together.|The proteins in the egg glutinate when heated. 蛋白质受热会凝结。|近义词：adhere, bond, stick, cling, cohere|反义词:separate, detach, divide|verb
glutinate:/ˈɡluːtɪneɪt/| adj.|粘性的；胶状的|Resembling glue; sticky; viscid.|The glutinate substance sealed the envelope.  这种粘稠的物质封住了信封。|近义词：sticky, adhesive, viscous|反义词:non-sticky, dry|adjective
