dreamed:/driːmd/ /drɛmt/| v.|做梦，梦见；梦想，渴望|To experience images, thoughts, and emotions during sleep; to have hopes or ambitions.|I dreamed I was flying last night. 我昨晚梦见我在飞。|近义词：envisioned, fantasized, imagined|反义词：-|verb

dreamed:/driːmd/ /drɛmt/| adj. (less common) |理想的，梦寐以求的|Used to describe something considered perfect or ideal.|She had the dreamed-of opportunity to travel the world. 她拥有梦寐以求的环游世界的机会。|近义词：ideal, perfect, desired|反义词：undesirable, flawed|adjective
