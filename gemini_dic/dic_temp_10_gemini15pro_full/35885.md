cocobolo:/ˌkoʊkoʊˈboʊloʊ/| n.|黄檀木，可可波罗木|A tropical American hardwood tree (Dalbergia retusa) yielding a valuable timber, also known as Nicaragua rosewood.  It is highly prized for its beautiful figure, colors ranging from orange-red to deep brown, and its ability to take a high polish. |The guitar was made of beautiful cocobolo.|近义词：rosewood, granadillo|反义词:|noun
