haplogroup:/ˈhæpləˌɡruːp/| n. |单倍群|A genetic population group of people who share a common ancestor on the patrilineal or matrilineal line.| Scientists used haplogroups to trace the migration patterns of ancient humans. 科学家利用单倍群来追踪古代人类的迁徙模式。|Synonym: lineage, clade|Antonym: N/A|noun
