inexpiableness:/ˌɪnɪkˈspaɪəbəlnəs/
n.
不可饶恕, 不可原谅,  无法辩解
The quality or state of being inexpiable; inability to be expiated or atoned for.
His inexpiable crime led to a life sentence.  他不可饶恕的罪行导致了无期徒刑。

The inexpiable betrayal shattered their friendship.  这不可原谅的背叛粉碎了他们的友谊。


Synonyms: unpardonableness, unforgivableness, irremissibleness
Antonyms: forgivableness, pardonableness, remissibleness
