anointing:/əˈnɔɪntɪŋ/
n.
涂油礼，恩膏，任命
The ceremonial act of applying oil to someone or something, often as a religious rite or symbol of consecration or appointment.
The king's anointing was a sacred ceremony. 国王的涂油礼是一项神圣的仪式。

近义词: consecration, blessing, ordination, inauguration
反义词: desecration, cursing
