canapes:/ˈkænəˌpeɪz/ (US), /ˈkænəˌpiːz/ (UK)| n. |小点心，开胃小菜|Small, bite-sized pieces of food, often served as appetizers at parties or receptions.  These are often decorative and served on small pieces of bread or toast.|We had a selection of delicious canapes at the wedding reception. 我们在婚礼招待会上享用了一系列美味的小点心。|Synonyms: appetizers, hors d'oeuvres, snacks|Antonyms: main course, full meal|noun
