lengthiest:/ˈlɛŋθiɪst/| adj.|最长的，持续时间最久的|Having the greatest length in space or time.  The lengthiest movie I've ever seen was over four hours long.|例句：This is the lengthiest book I've ever read. 这是我读过的最长的书。|例句：The lengthiest debate in the Senate lasted for weeks.  参议院最长的辩论持续了数周。|近义词：longest, protracted, extended|反义词：shortest, briefest|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From lengthy + -est (superlative suffix). Lengthy comes from length + -y (adjective suffix). Length ultimately derives from Old English *lenþu.
