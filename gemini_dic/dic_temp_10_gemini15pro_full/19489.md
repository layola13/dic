bided:/ˈbaɪdɪd/| v.|等待，停留， abiding 的过去式和过去分词| The spy bided his time, waiting for the right moment to strike.  间谍等待时机，等待合适的时机发动袭击。|例句：He bided his time until the market improved. 他等待时机，直到市场好转。|近义词：waited, stayed, remained|反义词：left, departed|词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *bīdan, from Proto-Germanic *bīdaną (“to wait, abide, remain”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰeydʰ- (“to wait, expect”). Cognate with Dutch bijten (“to bite”), German beissen (“to bite”), Latin fovēre (“to warm, cherish”). The sense of "to wait" developed from the original sense of "to remain, dwell."
