lifespan:/ˈlaɪfspæn/| n. |寿命；预期使用期限|The average lifespan of a human has increased significantly over the past century.  人类的平均寿命在上个世纪显著提高。|The lifespan of a butterfly is very short. 蝴蝶的寿命很短。|近义词：lifetime, duration, life expectancy|反义词: - |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From life + span.  "Span" originally referred to the distance between the thumb and little finger of an outstretched hand, hence a measure or extent.
