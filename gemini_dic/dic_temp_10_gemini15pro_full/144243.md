outsmelled:/ˌaʊtˈsmɛld/
|v.|胜过嗅觉；嗅觉更灵敏| to surpass in smelling; to have a keener sense of smell than another|The bloodhound outsmelled the other dogs and quickly found the lost child. 警犬嗅觉胜过其他狗，很快找到了走失的孩子。|Synonyms: outsniff, outnose|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider "unable to smell", "anosmic")
|Verb|


outsmelled:/ˌaʊtˈsmɛld/
|v.|（气味上）压倒，盖过（另一气味）| to overpower or mask another smell|The fragrant flowers outsmelled the faint odor of mildew. 芬芳的花朵盖过了淡淡的霉味。|Synonyms: overpower, overcome|Antonyms: be overpowered by
