outgabble: /ˌaʊtˈɡæbəl/
verb
胜过（某人）的饶舌；比…更健谈；喧宾夺主
to talk more loudly or rapidly than (someone); to outdo in talkativeness.
He outgabbled his opponent in the debate. 他在辩论中以口才压倒了对手。
synonym: outtalk, outspeak
antonym: be quiet, listen
