bad-tempered:/ˌbædˈtempəd/ | adj. | 脾气暴躁的，易怒的 | Easily annoyed and irritated; having a bad temper. | He's so bad-tempered in the mornings. 他早上脾气很暴躁。 |  She’s been very bad-tempered lately. 她最近脾气一直很坏. | Synonyms: irritable, grumpy, irascible, cantankerous, cross, surly | Antonyms: good-natured, affable, amiable, pleasant | adjective

Etymology:
From "bad" + "tempered." "Temper" in this context refers to one's state of mind or disposition.
