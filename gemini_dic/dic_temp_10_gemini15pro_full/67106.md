exploring:/ɪkˈsplɔːrɪŋ/
v.|探索，探险，探究|The act of searching, investigating, or traveling through an unfamiliar area or domain to learn more about it.|例句：They are exploring new ways to treat cancer. 他们在探索治疗癌症的新方法。|例句：The team is exploring the Amazon rainforest.  这个团队正在探索亚马逊雨林。|近义词：investigating, researching, searching, traversing|反义词：ignoring, neglecting|词性：动词 (present participle of explore)
explore:/ɪkˈsplɔːr/
v.|探索，探险，探究|To travel through an unfamiliar area or domain in order to learn about it; to investigate or examine something systematically.|例句：He explored the possibilities of opening a new business. 他探索了开办新业务的可能性。|例句：Scientists are exploring the depths of the ocean. 科学家们正在探索海洋深处。|近义词：investigate, research, search, traverse|反义词：ignore, neglect|词性：动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *explorare*, from Latin *explorare* ("to search out, investigate, examine"), from *ex- ("out") + *plorare* ("to cry out, wail, weep"). The original sense was of scouts reconnoitering enemy territory and crying out or weeping if danger was found.
