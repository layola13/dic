interdebated:/ˌɪntərdiˈbeɪtɪd/
adjective
相互辩论的，相互争论的
Having been debated or argued between or among different parties.
The interdebated topic of climate change continues to divide opinions.  气候变化这个相互争论的话题继续使意见分歧。
The merits of the different proposals were interdebated for hours.  不同提案的优点被相互辩论了几个小时。
近义词:discussed, argued, contested
反义词:agreed upon, uncontested
词性:adjective

词源:
"inter-" (between) + "debate" (to argue) + "-ed" (past participle suffix)
