alehoof:/ˈeɪlˌhuːf/| n.|地钱，活血丹|A European aromatic plant (Glechoma hederacea) of the mint family, with rounded, scalloped leaves and small purplish flowers.  It was formerly used medicinally and to clarify ale.|The alehoof grew rampantly in the shady corner of the garden.|近义词：ground ivy, gill-over-the-ground|反义词: N/A|noun
