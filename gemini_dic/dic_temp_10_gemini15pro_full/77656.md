gelder:/ˈɡɛldər/ | n. | 阉割者；阉割牲畜的人 |A person who castrates animals, especially farm animals. |The gelder skillfully castrated the young bull.  阉割者熟练地阉割了小公牛。| 近义词: castrator | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

gelder:/ˈɡɛldər/ | vt. | 阉割；使丧失活力 |To castrate (an animal); to deprive of vitality or vigor. |They gelded the colt to make him more manageable. 他们阉割了小马驹，使它更容易驾驭。  The oppressive regime gelded the spirit of the people.  压迫性的政权使人民丧失了活力。| 近义词: castrate, emasculate | 反义词: None | 词性: verb

Etymology: From Middle English *gelden, from Old Norse gelda (“to castrate”), from Proto-Germanic *geldaną (“to castrate”), from Proto-Indo-European *gʰel- (“to cut”).  Related to geld (n.).
