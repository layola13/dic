charybdis:/kəˈrɪbdɪs/| n. |大漩涡，险境，毁灭性的力量|A dangerous or destructive situation; a whirlpool.  It alludes to the mythical whirlpool faced by Odysseus in Homer's Odyssey. |例句：He found himself between Scylla and Charybdis, forced to choose between two equally dangerous options. 他发现自己进退两难，被迫在两个同样危险的选择之间做出抉择。

例句：The failing economy became a Charybdis, swallowing small businesses whole. 衰退的经济变成了一个巨大的漩涡，将小企业全部吞噬。|近义词：maelstrom, vortex, whirlpool, hazard, peril|反义词:safe harbor, haven|词性:noun
