chlorofluorocarbon:/ˌklɔːrəʊˈflʊərəʊˌkɑːbən/ /ˌklɔːroʊˈflʊrəʊˌkɑːrbən/| n. |氟氯化碳|A compound containing carbon, chlorine, and fluorine, belonging to the larger group of compounds called halocarbons.  These were commonly used as refrigerants, propellants in aerosol cans, and in other industrial applications, but have been phased out due to their damaging effect on the ozone layer.| Chlorofluorocarbons were widely used in refrigerators before their harmful effects were discovered. 氟氯化碳在发现其有害影响之前被广泛用于冰箱。

Chlorofluorocarbons contribute to the depletion of the ozone layer. 氟氯化碳导致臭氧层损耗。|近义词：CFC, freon|反义词: None|词性: noun
