hamama:/ˌhɑːˈmɑːmə/ | n. | 室内农场，哈玛玛 | An indoor farming system that uses automated technology to grow produce.  It typically involves stacked trays, LED lighting, and precise climate control. Hamama offers pre-seeded mats for consumers to grow microgreens at home. |  I'm growing kale and arugula in my hamama. 我在我的哈玛玛里种植羽衣甘蓝和芝麻菜。|  Indoor garden, vertical farm, hydroponic system |  Outdoor garden, traditional farming | noun
