gainsay:/ˌɡeɪnˈseɪ/ /ˌɡeɪnˈseɪ/
verb
否认；反驳；否定
To deny or contradict; to dispute or oppose.
Nobody can gainsay his claims. 没有人能够反驳他的说法。
近义词：deny, contradict, dispute, challenge, controvert
反义词：confirm, acknowledge, admit, concede, accept
词性:v.

gainsay:/ˌɡeɪnˈseɪ/ /ˌɡeɪnˈseɪ/
词源：From Old English *gēansecgan (unattested), from gēan- "against" + secgan "to say". Cognate with Dutch tegenspreken, German gegensprechen.
词性:v.
