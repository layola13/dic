hydroponics:/ˌhaɪdrəˈpɒnɪks/ | n. | 水培法 | The science of growing plants in a nutrient-rich water solution, instead of soil. | Example: Hydroponics allows for year-round crop production, regardless of weather conditions.  水培法可以实现全年作物生产，不受天气条件的影响。

Example:  He set up a hydroponics system in his basement to grow fresh herbs. 他在地下室建立了一个水培系统来种植新鲜香草。 | 近义词: soilless culture, water culture | 反义词: geoponics (soil-based agriculture), traditional farming | 词性: Noun
