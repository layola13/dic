funnier:/ˈfʌniər/| adj.|更滑稽的，更有趣的|Comparative form of "funny". Used to describe something that is more humorous or amusing than something else.|He told a funnier joke than his brother. 他讲的笑话比他兄弟的更有趣。|
|近义词：more amusing, more humorous, wittier|反义词：less funny, more serious, less amusing|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *funnien, from Old English *fynig. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *funjają (“to become moldy, rot”). Related to Danish funke (“spark”). The sense development is from "strange, peculiar, odd" to "amusing".
