paperiness:/ˈpeɪpərɪnɪs/
n.
纸质；像纸一样；薄而易碎
The paperiness of the old documents made them difficult to handle.  这些古老文件的纸质使它们难以处理。
The paperiness of the skin is a sign of aging. 皮肤像纸一样薄是衰老的迹象。
例句：The antique map’s paperiness made it extremely fragile. 这张古董地图因为纸质易碎而非常脆弱。
例句：He noted the paperiness of her skin. 他注意到她皮肤薄如纸。
近义词：flimsiness, thinness, fragility
反义词：thickness, sturdiness, robustness
词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From paper + -i- + -ness.  来自 paper（纸）+ -i-（连接字母）+ -ness（名词后缀，表示状态或性质）。
