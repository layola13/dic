embrasured: /ɪmˈbreɪʒərd/
adjective
有枪眼的；有射击孔的；有拥抱的
Provided with an embrasure (an opening in a wall or parapet with sides flaring outward).  Figuratively, it can also suggest being enclosed or embraced.
The embrasured windows of the castle offered archers a protected position to fire from. 城堡有枪眼的窗户为弓箭手提供了一个受保护的射击位置。
The lovers sat embrasured in a quiet corner of the cafe. 恋人们在咖啡馆一个安静的角落里相拥而坐。
近义词: indented, recessed, windowed, sheltered
反义词: exposed, unprotected
词性: adjective

词源: From French "embraser" (to set on fire), from "en-" (in) + "braise" (burning coals).  Later, it came to mean to widen an opening in a fortification for cannons, and then more generally, any such opening.
