homogenize:/həˈmɑːdʒənaɪz/ /həˈmɒdʒənaɪz/| v. |使均匀，使均质化|To make something uniform or consistent throughout.|The milk is homogenized to prevent the cream from separating.|Synonyms: blend, mix, standardize, unify|Antonyms: separate, differentiate, diversify|Verb

homogenization:/həˌmɑːdʒənɪˈzeɪʃən/ /həˌmɒdʒənɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |均匀化，均质化|The process of making something uniform or consistent throughout.|The homogenization of milk is a common practice in the dairy industry.|Synonyms: standardization, unification|Antonyms: differentiation, diversification|Noun
