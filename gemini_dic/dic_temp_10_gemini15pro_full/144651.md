outwrangling:/ˌaʊtˈræŋɡlɪŋ/
verb
胜过诡辩; 通过更巧妙的辩论击败
To outdo or surpass someone in wrangling, arguing, or disputing, especially through superior skill or cleverness.
The lawyer outwrangled his opponent in court and won the case.  这位律师在法庭上通过辩论战胜了他的对手，赢得了这场官司。
Near synonyms: outmaneuver, outsmart, outargue, defeat
Antonyms: lose to, be defeated by
词性: 动词
