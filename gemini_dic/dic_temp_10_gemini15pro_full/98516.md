instar:/ˈɪnstɑːr/
n.
龄期，幼虫期
A phase between two periods of molting in the development of an insect larva or other invertebrate animal.
The caterpillar is in its third instar. 这条毛毛虫处于第三龄期。

近义词:stage, phase
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用adult 成虫)
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin īnstar ("form, likeness, image, resemblance, equality"), from Proto-Italic *enstere, from Proto-Indo-European *h₁én-stero-, from *h₁en ("in"). Cognate with Sanskrit अस्ति (ásti, “is, exists”).
