misperform:/ˌmɪspərˈfɔːrm/ /ˌmɪspərˈfɔːrm/| v. |表现不佳，执行不力|To perform poorly or inadequately; to fail to meet expectations.|The company misperformed last quarter due to supply chain issues.  由于供应链问题，该公司上季度业绩不佳。|Synonyms: underperform, fail, falter|Antonyms: succeed, excel, outperform|verb

misperformance:/ˌmɪspərˈfɔːrməns/ /ˌmɪspərˈfɔːrməns/| n. |表现不佳，执行不力|The act or instance of performing poorly or inadequately.|The misperformance of the team led to their elimination from the tournament. 该团队的表现不佳导致他们被淘汰出局。|Synonyms: underperformance, failure, lapse|Antonyms: success, achievement, triumph|noun
