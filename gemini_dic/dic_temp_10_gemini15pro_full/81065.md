grainering: /ˈɡreɪnərɪŋ/ | n. | 木纹装饰; 仿木纹涂装 | The process of painting wood to resemble the grain of a more expensive or desirable wood.  It can also refer to the finished effect of this process. | The grainering on the door made it look like solid oak.  门上的木纹装饰让它看起来像实橡木做的。 | 近义词: wood graining, faux bois | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "natural wood finish") | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "grain" (referring to the texture of wood) + "-er" (agent suffix, indicating someone who performs an action) + "-ing" (suffix forming a noun from a verb).
