hypersexuality:/ˌhaɪpərˌsɛkʃuˈælɪti/ | n. |性欲亢进，性欲过盛| Excessive sexual desire or activity.  It is characterized by an abnormally increased interest in sex and sexual activity, which can lead to significant distress or impairment in one's life. | Example 1: His hypersexuality caused significant strain on his marriage. 他的性欲亢进给他的婚姻带来了很大的压力。

Example 2:  The patient exhibited signs of hypersexuality after starting the new medication.  病人开始服用新药后表现出性欲亢进的迹象。 | Near synonyms: nymphomania (female), satyriasis (male), excessive libido, compulsive sexual behavior | Antonyms:  hypoactive sexual desire disorder, low libido, sexual aversion | noun
