hypotrochoid:/ˈhaɪpəˌtrɒkɔɪd/| n. |次摆线|A curve traced by a point attached to a circle rolling along the inside of a fixed circle.|

The spirograph creates beautiful hypotrochoids.  螺旋描图仪可以画出美丽的次摆线。

The design of the gear incorporated a hypotrochoid shape for smoother operation.  齿轮的设计采用了次摆线形状，以实现更平滑的运转。


|近义词: roulette (general term for curves generated by rolling)|反义词: epicycloid (curve generated by a circle rolling outside another circle), epitrochoid|词性: noun
