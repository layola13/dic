n. | 名词
liturgist | 礼拜仪式主持人；司礼者
A person who leads or performs a liturgy. |  指引或执行礼拜仪式的人。
The liturgist prepared the altar for the service. | 司礼者为仪式准备祭坛。
The experienced liturgist guided the congregation through the complex ceremony. | 经验丰富的司礼者引导会众完成了复杂的仪式。
Synonyms: officiant, celebrant, minister, priest, pastor | 近义词: 主祭, 主持人, 牧师, 神父,  教士
Antonyms: None (The concept doesn't have a direct opposite.) | 反义词: 无 (这个概念没有直接的反义词)
