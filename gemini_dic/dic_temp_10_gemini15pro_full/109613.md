letter-size:/ˈlɛtərˌsaɪz/| adj.|信纸大小的，标准尺寸的|Of, relating to, or being a standard size of paper, 8 1/2 by 11 inches in the U.S.  在美国指标准尺寸的纸张，8.5 x 11 英寸。|I need some letter-size paper for my printer. 我需要一些信纸大小的纸张用于我的打印机。|Synonyms: standard-sized, A4 (outside US), 8.5x11|Antonyms: oversized, legal-size|adjective

letter-size:/ˈlɛtərˌsaɪz/| n.|信纸大小|The dimensions of standard paper, 8 1/2 by 11 inches in the U.S. 在美国指标准纸张的尺寸，8.5 x 11 英寸。|The document must be printed on letter-size.  该文件必须打印在信纸大小的纸张上。|Synonyms: standard size, A4 (outside US)|Antonyms: oversized, legal size|noun
