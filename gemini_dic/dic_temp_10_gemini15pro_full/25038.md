bryophyta:/ˌbraɪˈɒfɪtə/ /ˌbraɪˈɑːfɪtə/| n. |苔藓植物|A phylum of nonvascular plants comprising the mosses, liverworts, and hornworts. These plants lack true roots, stems, and leaves, and reproduce by spores.|Mosses, liverworts, and hornworts are all bryophyta. 苔藓、地钱和角苔都是苔藓植物。|Synonyms: moss plants, |Antonyms: tracheophyta (维管植物)|noun
