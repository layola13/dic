ornithodelphia:/ˌɔːrnɪθəˈdɛlfiə/
n.
原兽亚纲/单孔目动物
A subclass of mammals, the Prototheria, characterized by laying eggs, having a pouch, and a cloaca.  It includes the echidnas and the platypus.
例句：Ornithodelphia are unique among mammals because they lay eggs.  原兽亚纲在哺乳动物中是独一无二的，因为它们会下蛋。

例句：The platypus and echidnas are the only surviving members of the Ornithodelphia.  鸭嘴兽和针鼹是原兽亚纲仅存的成员。
近义词：Prototheria, Monotremata
反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's a taxonomic classification)  没有直接的反义词，因为它是一个分类学分类。
词性: noun
