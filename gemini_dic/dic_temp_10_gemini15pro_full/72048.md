flirtatiously:/flɜːrˈteɪʃəsli/ /flɝːˈteɪʃəsli/
adverb
轻佻地，调情地
In a way that suggests playful romantic or sexual attraction.
She smiled flirtatiously at him across the room. 她隔着房间对他轻佻地笑了笑。
He winked flirtatiously. 他轻佻地眨了眨眼。
近义词: playfully, teasingly, coquettishly
反义词: seriously, formally, coldly
词性: adverb
