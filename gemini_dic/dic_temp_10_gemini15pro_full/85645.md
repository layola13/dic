hauntingly:/ˈhɔːntɪŋli/ | adv. | 萦绕心头的，令人难忘的，(尤指)带有一丝恐惧或悲伤的 | In a way that is difficult to forget, often in a slightly frightening or sad way. | The music was hauntingly beautiful.  这音乐美得令人难忘。|  Synonyms: memorably, unforgettable, poignantly | Antonyms: forgettably, ordinarily | adverb

haunt:/hɔːnt/ | v. |  (鬼魂)出没；(不愉快的事)萦绕心头；经常去(某地) | 1. (of a ghost) to appear in a place repeatedly. 2. (of a memory, image, feeling, etc.) to continue to appear in your mind. 3. to visit a place often. | The old house is said to be haunted.  据说那栋老房子闹鬼。| Synonyms: plague, torment, frequent | Antonyms: avoid, shun | verb
