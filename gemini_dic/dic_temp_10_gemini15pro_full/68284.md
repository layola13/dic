falafel:/fəˈlɑːfəl/ /ˈfɑːləfəl/ | n. |炸鹰嘴豆丸子，沙拉三明治|A Middle Eastern dish of spiced mashed chickpeas or fava beans formed into balls or patties and deep-fried, usually eaten with or in pita bread.| I had a delicious falafel for lunch. 我午餐吃了一个美味的炸鹰嘴豆丸子。| 近义词:felafel (variant spelling) | 反义词: N/A | 词性:noun

falafels:/fəˈlɑːfəlz/ /ˈfɑːləfəlz/ | n. |炸鹰嘴豆丸子（复数）|Plural form of falafel.| I ordered two falafels to go. 我点了两个炸鹰嘴豆丸子带走。| 近义词:felafels (variant spelling) | 反义词: N/A | 词性:noun


词源: From Arabic فَلَافِل (falāfil), plural of فَلَافِلَة (falāfila), from Coptic *pha la phel (literally, “of many beans”).
