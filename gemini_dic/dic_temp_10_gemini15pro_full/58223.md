druggist:/ˈdrʌɡɪst/| n.|药剂师；药商|A person who is licensed to prepare and dispense drugs and medicines; a pharmacist.|例句：The druggist prepared the prescription. 药剂师配好了处方药。

例句：I went to the druggist to get my medication refilled. 我去药店续药了。|近义词：pharmacist, chemist, apothecary|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
