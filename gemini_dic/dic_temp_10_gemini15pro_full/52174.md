The shaft of a long bone.
The diaphysis is the main or midsection (shaft) of a long bone. It is made up mostly of compact bone and contains bone marrow in the medullary cavity.  The diaphysis is essential for providing structural support and strength to the bone.
例句：A fracture in the diaphysis of the femur can be a serious injury.  股骨骨干骨折可能是严重的损伤。

例句：The diaphysis of the humerus is relatively long and slender. 肱骨的骨干相对较长且细长。
近义词：shaft
反义词:epiphysis
词性:noun
