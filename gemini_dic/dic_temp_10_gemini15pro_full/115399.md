manstealer:/ˈmænˌstiːlər/
n.|拐卖人口者；诱拐者|Someone who abducts or kidnaps people, often for forced labor or sexual exploitation.  This term can also be used figuratively to describe someone who entices another person away from a relationship or commitment.|The pirates were notorious manstealers, preying on coastal villages.  这些海盗是臭名昭著的拐卖人口者，专门掠夺沿海村庄。|Synonyms: kidnapper, abductor, trafficker|Antonyms: rescuer, liberator|noun
