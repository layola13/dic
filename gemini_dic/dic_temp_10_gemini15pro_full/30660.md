cerographic: /ˌsɪərəˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. |蜡版的；蜡画的| Relating to cerography, the art of engraving or writing on wax. |  The museum displayed several cerographic portraits of historical figures. 博物馆展出了几幅历史人物的蜡像。|  The artist used a heated stylus to create the cerographic image. 艺术家用加热的铁笔创作了这幅蜡画。 |Synonyms: waxen, wax, encaustic | Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps "non-wax," "etched," "painted" depending on context) | adjective
