divinely:/dɪˈvaɪnli/| adv.|神圣地；极其地；非常好地|In a godlike manner; superbly; extremely well|例句：The music was divinely inspired. 这音乐受到了神的启示。

例句：The cake was divinely rich and delicious.  这蛋糕美味极了，浓郁极了。

近义词：wonderfully, excellently, superbly, beautifully, heavenly, marvelously|反义词：poorly, badly, terribly, awfully|词性:副词

词源: From Middle English *divinely, from Old French devinement, from Late Latin dīvīnāmentum, from Latin dīvīnus (“divine”) + -mentum.
