parergon:/ˈpærərɡɒn/ (US) /ˈpærərɡən/ (UK)| n.| 副业，附属物，次要作品|Something subsidiary or accessory to a main work or activity; a subordinate activity or work.| The intricate carvings on the frame were a beautiful parergon to the painting itself.  画框上精细的雕刻是这幅画作的精美附属物。|Near synonyms: accessory, adjunct, supplement, appendage|Antonyms: core, center, main, primary|Noun

parergon:/ˈpærərɡɒn/ (US) /ˈpærərɡən/ (UK)| n.| 附加物，附属品|Something added to a main work, but not essential to it.|  The footnotes and appendices are a useful parergon to the main text. 脚注和附录是对正文的有用补充。|Near synonyms:  addendum, appendix, supplement|Antonyms: essential, integral, necessary|Noun
