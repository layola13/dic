gastromalacia: /ˌɡæstrəməˈleɪʃə/ /ˌɡæstrəməˈleɪʒə/
noun
胃软化; 胃软化症
Softening of the walls of the stomach.  This condition can be caused by various factors, including severe inflammation, ischemia (lack of blood flow), and certain medications.

Gastromalacia can lead to serious complications if left untreated.

近义词: stomach softening
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider the opposite state) healthy stomach, normal stomach function
