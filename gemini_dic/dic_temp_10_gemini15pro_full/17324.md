be-bopper's:/ˈbiːˌbɑːpərz/ |n. (possessive form)|比波普爱好者的（东西）|Something that belongs to or is associated with a be-bopper. A be-bopper is someone who enjoys or performs bebop, a style of jazz music characterized by fast tempos, complex harmonies, and improvisation.|The be-bopper's hat sat perched jauntily on his head.|近义词:jazz musician's, improviser's|反义词:traditionalist's|词性:名词 (possessive)


Etymology:

be-bopper (n.):  From "bebop" (n.), imitative of the characteristic rhythmic phrasing of the music + -er (suffix forming nouns denoting a person or thing that performs a specified action).
's (clitic):  From Old English "es," genitive suffix. Marks possession.
