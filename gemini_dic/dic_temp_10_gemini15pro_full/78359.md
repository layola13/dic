gestating:/ˈdʒɛsteɪtɪŋ/
verb
孕育，酝酿，(想法、计划等)逐渐形成
developing or maturing slowly in the mind
The idea for the novel had been gestating in her mind for years.  这部小说的想法在她脑海中酝酿多年。
近义词: developing, maturing, incubating, brewing
反义词: fading, disappearing, diminishing
词性: verb (present participle)
