brucite:/ˈbruːsaɪt/| n.|水镁石|A white, pearly mineral consisting of magnesium hydroxide, Mg(OH)₂. It is used as a fire retardant and as a source of magnesium.|Brucite is often found in metamorphic rocks. 水镁石常发现于变质岩中。| Brucite is a common alteration product of periclase. 水镁石是方镁石常见的蚀变产物。|Synonyms: magnesium hydroxide|Antonyms: N/A|noun
