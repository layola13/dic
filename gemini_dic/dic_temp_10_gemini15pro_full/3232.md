affeer:/əˈfɪər/
|v.|确定（罚款数额），估价，评估|The magistrate affeered the fine at $50. 地方法官将罚款定为50美元。|To assess the value of something, especially a fine.|The council affeered the damages caused by the flood. 理事会评估了洪水造成的损失。|
|近义词：assess, estimate, appraise|
|反义词：ignore, disregard|
