directed:/dəˈrɛktɪd/ /daɪˈrɛktɪd/| adj. |定向的，指导的，受指挥的|  guided or aimed toward a particular goal or destination; managed or controlled by someone. |The directed study program allows students to pursue their specific interests.  定向学习项目允许学生追求他们的特定兴趣。

The missile was directed at the enemy base. 导弹瞄准了敌人的基地。|近义词：guided, aimed, focused, supervised, controlled, managed|反义词:undirected, aimless, random, uncontrolled|词性:形容词

Etymology: From the Latin word "dirigere," meaning "to direct" or "to guide,"  past participle of "direct."  The word entered Middle English as "directen."
