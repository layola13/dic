overhydrated:/ˌoʊvərˈhaɪdreɪtɪd/| adj.|水分过多的，过度水合的|Being overhydrated means having consumed too much water, which can disrupt the body's electrolyte balance.|The marathon runner was overhydrated after drinking excessive amounts of water. 马拉松运动员喝了过量的水后水分过多。|She felt nauseous and dizzy, symptoms of being overhydrated. 她感到恶心和头晕，这是水分过多的症状。|近义词: waterlogged, over-hydrated|反义词: dehydrated, underhydrated|词性:形容词

overhydrate:/ˌoʊvərˈhaɪdreɪt/| v.|过度水合，喝水过多|To overhydrate means to drink an excessive amount of water, potentially leading to health problems.|Don't overhydrate before a workout; it can lead to discomfort.  锻炼前不要喝太多水；这会导致不适。|He overhydrated during the race and had to stop. 他在比赛中喝水过多，不得不停下来。|近义词: drink too much water|反义词: dehydrate|词性:动词

词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessive") + hydrate (verb meaning "to combine with water")
