misesteem:/ˌmɪsɪˈstiːm/
verb
低估，轻视，看轻
to have too low an opinion of (someone or something); underestimate
He misesteemed his opponent and lost the match. 他低估了他的对手，输掉了比赛。
undervalue, underestimate, depreciate
overestimate, overvalue, exaggerate
v.

misesteem:/ˌmɪsɪˈstiːm/
noun
低估，轻视
a wrong or unfair opinion about someone or something that shows you do not think they are very good
She suffered from a deep sense of misesteem. 她深受低估之苦。
underestimation, undervaluation, depreciation
overestimation, overvaluation, exaggeration
n.
