bulbless:/ˈbʌlb.ləs/| adj.|无灯泡的，没有灯泡的|Lacking a bulb; without a bulb.  It describes something that is designed to function or operate without the need for a traditional light bulb, often utilizing alternative light sources like LEDs.|The bulbless flashlight uses LEDs.  这个无灯泡手电筒使用LED。|The streetlight was bulbless after the storm.  暴风雨过后路灯没有灯泡了。|近义词：lightless, lampless|反义词：bulbed|词性:形容词
