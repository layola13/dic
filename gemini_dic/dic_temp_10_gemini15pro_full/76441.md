gainfulnesses:/ˈɡeɪnfəlnəsɪz/
noun
获利性；有利可图性；有益性
The quality or state of being gainful or profitable.
The gainfulnesses of various occupations were compared.  各种职业的获利性进行了比较。
The report analyzed the gainfulnesses of different investment strategies. 报告分析了不同投资策略的获利性。
Synonyms: profitability, lucrativeness, advantageousness
Antonyms: unprofitability, loss, disadvantage
词性: 名词
