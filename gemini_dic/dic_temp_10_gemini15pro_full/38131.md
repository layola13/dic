comprehensive:/ˌkɑːmprɪˈhensɪv/| adj.|全面的，综合的，广泛的，理解的|Including or dealing with all or nearly all elements or aspects of something.|例句：We offer a comprehensive range of services. 我们提供全方位的服务。|近义词：complete, thorough, extensive, full, inclusive|反义词：limited, narrow, restricted|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin comprehensivus, from comprehendere ‘grasp mentally’ (see comprehend).
