androphagi:/ænˈdrɒfədʒaɪ/
n.
食人族，食人者
People who eat human flesh.
Herodotus described the Androphagi as a savage tribe.  希罗多德将安德罗帕吉人描述为野蛮的部落。
The explorers feared encountering the legendary androphagi. 探险家们害怕遇到传说中的食人族。
Synonyms: cannibals, man-eaters
Antonyms: vegetarians, vegans
