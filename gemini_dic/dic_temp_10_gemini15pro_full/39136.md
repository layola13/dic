confuses:/kənˈfjuːzɪz/ | v. | 使困惑，使糊涂 |  To make someone uncertain or unable to understand something clearly. | The complicated instructions confused me. 复杂的说明书把我搞糊涂了。|近义词: perplexes, bewilder, disorients |反义词: clarifies, explains, elucidates |词性:动词

confuses:/kənˈfjuːzɪz/ | v. | 混淆，弄错 | To mistake one thing for another. | She often confuses the twins' names. 她经常把这对双胞胎的名字搞混。 |近义词: mixes up, muddles, mistakes |反义词: distinguishes, differentiates |词性:动词
