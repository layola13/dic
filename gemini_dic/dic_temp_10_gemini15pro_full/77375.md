gaunt:/ɡɔːnt/| adj.|憔悴的，瘦削的，荒凉的|Lean and haggard, especially because of suffering, hunger, or age; (of a building or place) grim or desolate in appearance.|例句：The refugees arrived at the camp looking gaunt and exhausted.  难民们到达营地时，一个个面容憔悴，精疲力竭。

例句：The gaunt, grey building loomed over the street.  那栋荒凉的灰色建筑耸立在街道上。|近义词：haggard, thin, emaciated, lean, bony, skeletal, grim, desolate, bleak|反义词：plump, robust, healthy, lush, fertile|词性:形容词
