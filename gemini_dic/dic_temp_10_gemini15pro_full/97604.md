inhalants:/ɪnˈheɪlənts/ | n. | 吸入剂 | Substances that are inhaled to produce a mind-altering effect. |  Example: Inhalant abuse is a serious problem among teenagers. 吸入剂滥用在青少年中是一个严重的问题。

Example:  Some common inhalants include glue, paint thinner, and aerosols. 一些常见的吸入剂包括胶水、油漆稀释剂和气雾剂。| 近义词:  volatile substances,  inhaled drugs | 反义词:  None | 词性: noun


inhalant:/ɪnˈheɪlənt/ | adj. | 吸入的 | Relating to or involving inhalation. | Example: The inhalant anesthetic quickly put the patient to sleep. 吸入麻醉剂很快使病人入睡。


Example:  Inhalant toxins can cause severe lung damage. 吸入性毒素会导致严重的肺损伤。| 近义词:  respiratory, inhaled  | 反义词:  None | 词性: adjective
