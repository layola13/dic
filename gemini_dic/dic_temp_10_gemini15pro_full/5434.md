almondy: /ˈɑːməndi/ | adj. | 杏仁味的；像杏仁的 | Having a flavor or texture reminiscent of almonds. | The cake had a delicately almondy aroma.  这蛋糕有淡淡的杏仁香味。 |  The biscuits were crisp and almondy.  饼干酥脆，有杏仁味。| 近义词: nutty, almond-flavored | 反义词: (无明确反义词) | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From almond + -y.  来自 almond (杏仁) + -y (形容词后缀，表示“具有...性质的”)
