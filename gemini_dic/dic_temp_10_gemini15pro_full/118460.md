mesenteron: /ˌmezənˈtɪərən, ˌmezənˈtɛrən/| n. | 中肠 | The portion of the alimentary canal of an embryo that develops into the small intestine and part of the large intestine.  The midgut, or the main part of the digestive tract, especially in invertebrates. | The mesenteron is responsible for most of the digestion and absorption of nutrients in insects.  中肠负责昆虫的大部分消化和营养吸收。|  midgut, gut |  foregut, hindgut | noun

mesentery: /ˈmezənˌtɛri/| n. | 肠系膜 | A fold of the peritoneum that attaches the stomach, small intestine, pancreas, spleen, and other organs to the posterior wall of the abdomen.  A membrane that supports the intestines. | The surgeon carefully examined the mesentery for any signs of damage.  外科医生仔细检查肠系膜是否有任何损伤迹象。|  peritoneum |  None | noun
