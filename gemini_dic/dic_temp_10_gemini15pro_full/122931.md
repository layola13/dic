moment:/ˈmoʊmənt/ (US) /ˈməʊmənt/ (UK)| n.| 片刻，瞬间；时刻；重要性；时机|a very short period of time; a particular point in time; importance; the right time|
She'll be back in a moment. 她一会儿就回来。

He enjoyed every moment of the holiday. 他享受假期的每一刻。

The discovery was a moment of great importance. 这一发现是一个非常重要的时刻。

This is not the moment to argue. 现在不是争论的时候。
|近义词：instant, second, minute, importance, significance|反义词:eternity|词性:名词
