hobbing:/ˈhɒbɪŋ/ (UK), /ˈhɑːbɪŋ/ (US)| n. |滚齿，滚齿加工|A machining process for generating gears, splines, and sprockets using a hobbing machine. The hob, a specialized cutting tool, progressively shapes the workpiece through a series of rotary and linear motions.|The gears were manufactured using a hobbing process.|Synonyms: gear cutting, gear generating|Antonyms: N/A|noun

hobbing:/ˈhɒbɪŋ/ (UK), /ˈhɑːbɪŋ/ (US)| v. |滚齿加工|To cut or shape (gears, splines, etc.) with a hob.|They are hobbing the gears for the new transmission.|Synonyms: gear cutting, gear generating|Antonyms: N/A|verb
