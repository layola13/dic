dwarfling:/ˈdwɔːflɪŋ/ | n. | 小矮人，侏儒 |A creature resembling a dwarf, especially a small one; a diminutive dwarf.  A child of a dwarf. |The dwarfling hid amongst the tree roots. 小矮人藏在树根间。| Near synonyms: dwarf, midget, pygmy, gnome | Antonyms: giant | noun
