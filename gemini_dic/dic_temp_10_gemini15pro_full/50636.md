deride:/dɪˈraɪd/| v.|嘲笑，讥讽|To laugh at or make fun of someone or something in a way that shows contempt or ridicule.|例句：He derided their efforts as childish. 他嘲笑他们的努力幼稚。|例句：The critics derided the movie as predictable and clichéd.  评论家嘲笑这部电影老套且充满陈词滥调。|近义词：mock, ridicule, scorn, taunt|反义词: praise, admire, commend|verb
