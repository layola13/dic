hermaphroditical:/hɜːrˌmæfrəˈdɪtɪkl/ | adj. |雌雄同体的；阴阳人的|Having both male and female reproductive organs.|例句：Some species of fish are hermaphroditical.  一些鱼类是雌雄同体的。

例句：The snail is a hermaphroditical creature.  蜗牛是雌雄同体的生物。|近义词：monoecious, bisexual, androgynous|反义词：dioecious, unisexual|词性：形容词

hermaphrodite: /hɜːrˈmæfrəˌdaɪt/ | n. | 雌雄同体的人或生物 | An organism or individual having both male and female reproductive organs. | 例句：In Greek mythology, Hermaphroditus was a hermaphrodite. 在希腊神话中，赫马佛洛狄忒斯是一位雌雄同体的神。

例句：The earthworm is a hermaphrodite. 蚯蚓是雌雄同体的。|近义词：bisexual, androgynous|反义词：unisex|词性：名词
