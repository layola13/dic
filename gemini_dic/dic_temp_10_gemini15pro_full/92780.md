icicles:/ˈaɪsɪklz/| n. |冰柱，冰锥|A pointed piece of ice formed by the freezing of dripping water.|The icicles hanging from the roof sparkled in the sunlight. 屋顶悬挂的冰柱在阳光下闪闪发光。|近义词：ice formation, ice pendant|反义词: (no direct antonym, perhaps) puddle, meltwater|noun
