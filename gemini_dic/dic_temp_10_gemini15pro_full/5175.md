alleviative:/əˈliːviˌeɪtɪv/
adjective
缓解的，减轻的
Making something less severe or intense; relieving.
The doctor prescribed alleviative medication for her back pain. 医生给她开了缓解背痛的药物。
The cool compress had an alleviative effect on the swelling.  冷敷对肿胀有缓解作用。
近义词:  soothing, palliative, relieving, mitigating, assuaging
反义词:  aggravating, exacerbating, worsening
词性:  adjective

词源:  From Late Latin *alleviativus, from Latin alleviatus, past participle of allevare  "to lighten, relieve," from ad- "to" + levis  "light."
