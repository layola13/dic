nonignitibility:/ˌnɒnɪɡˈnɪtɪbɪləti/
n.
不可燃性，不燃性
The quality or state of being nonignitible; resistance to ignition.
The nonignitibility of the material makes it safe for use in high-temperature environments.  这种材料的不可燃性使其可以安全地用于高温环境。
non-flammability, incombustibility
flammability, ignitability
noun
