crutchlike:/ˈkrʌtʃlaɪk/
adjective
拐杖似的；像拐杖一样支撑的
Resembling or functioning as a crutch; providing support or assistance, often suggesting dependence.
Her crutchlike cane helped her navigate the uneven sidewalk. 她那根拐杖似的的手杖帮助她在不平坦的人行道上行走。
The government's bailout served as a crutchlike support for the failing bank. 政府的救助措施对这家濒临倒闭的银行起到了拐杖般的支撑作用。
近义词:supportive, aiding, assisting, dependent
反义词:independent, unaided
