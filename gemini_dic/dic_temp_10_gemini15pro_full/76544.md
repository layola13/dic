gallantries: /ˈɡæləntriz/ | n. |殷勤，勇敢的行为；(尤指)对女性献殷勤；英勇事迹| acts of polite attention to women; acts of bravery | He performed many gallantries for the ladies present. 他对在场女士献殷勤。| 近义词: courtesies, chivalry, valor, heroism | 反义词: rudeness, cowardice | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Old French *galanterie, from galant (“courteous, gallant”), from galer (“to rejoice”). Ultimately from Frankish *galan.
