bacilluria:/ˌbæsɪˈljʊəriə/
noun
杆菌尿
The presence of bacilli (rod-shaped bacteria) in the urine.
Bacilluria can be a sign of a urinary tract infection. 杆菌尿可能是尿路感染的迹象。
The lab results showed significant bacilluria. 实验室结果显示明显的杆菌尿。
Synonyms: bacteriuria (more general term)
Antonyms: sterile urine (no bacteria present)
