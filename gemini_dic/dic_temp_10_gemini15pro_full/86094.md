heart-searcher:/ˈhɑːrtˌsɜːrtʃər/ | n. |  探求内心者；追求真爱的人；认真思考的人 | Someone who introspects deeply, often seeking self-understanding, spiritual truth, or romantic love. |  She was a heart-searcher, always questioning her motives and desires. 她是一个探求内心的人，总是质疑自己的动机和愿望。|  He embarked on a spiritual journey as a heart-searcher, hoping to find enlightenment. 他作为一个探求内心的人踏上了精神之旅，希望能找到启迪。 |Synonyms: soul-searcher, seeker, inquirer | Antonyms: conformist, follower | Noun
