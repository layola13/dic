garterless:/ˈɡɑːrtərlɪs/
adjective
无吊袜带的
Without garters (elastic bands worn to hold up stockings).
She wore a garterless stocking. 她穿着一只没系吊袜带的长袜。
Synonyms: stockingless, bare-legged
Antonyms: gartered
词性: 形容词
