discretively:/dɪˈskriːtɪvli/ | adv. | 谨慎地，小心地，分别地 |  In a manner that is careful not to attract attention or give offense; separately or individually. | The detective discretively followed the suspect.  侦探谨慎地跟踪嫌疑人。| He handled the sensitive documents discretively. 他谨慎地处理了敏感文件。 | 近义词：carefully, cautiously, prudently, separately, individually | 反义词：openly, publicly, carelessly, together | 词性: 副词

词源: From the adjective "discrete" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Discrete" comes from Latin "discretus," past participle of "discernere" (to separate, distinguish).
