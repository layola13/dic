oathable:/ˈoʊðəbl/| adj.|可宣誓的，可发誓的|Suitable for swearing an oath on; able to be sworn.|例句：The document was oathable and legally binding.  这份文件是可以宣誓的，具有法律约束力。

例句：Is this testimony oathable in a court of law? 这份证词可以在法庭上宣誓吗？|近义词：swearable, attestable|反义词:unswearable, unattestable|词性:adjective

Etymology: From "oath" + "-able".  "Oath" comes from Old English "āð," from Proto-Germanic "*aiþaz," meaning "oath, curse."  "-able" is a suffix of Latin origin meaning "capable of being," "worthy of being," or "tending to."
