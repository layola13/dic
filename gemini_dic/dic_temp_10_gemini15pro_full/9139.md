antinational:/ˌæntiˈnæʃ(ə)nəl/
adjective
反国家的，反对国家利益的
Opposing one's own nation or its interests.
His antinational sentiments led him to betray his country. 他的反国家情绪导致他背叛了自己的国家。
近义词: unpatriotic, disloyal, treasonous, subversive
反义词: patriotic, nationalistic, loyal
词性: adjective
