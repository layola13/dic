ligamentous:/ˌlɪɡəˈmɛntəs/
adjective
韧带的；由韧带构成的
Relating to or consisting of ligaments.
Example: The ligamentous structures of the knee provide stability.  膝盖的韧带结构提供稳定性。

Example:  Ligamentous laxity can lead to joint instability. 韧带松弛会导致关节不稳定。

Synonym:  ligamental
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider terms like "muscular" or "tendinous" to refer to other connective tissues)
词性: adjective
