crispin:/ˈkrɪspɪn/| n.|克里斯平，鞋匠，圣克里斯平节|A shoemaker; St. Crispin's Day (October 25th), the patron saint of shoemakers.|例句：They celebrated Crispin with a feast. 他们以盛宴庆祝圣克里斯平节。

|近义词：shoemaker, cobbler|反义词:| noun

crispin:/ˈkrɪspɪn/| adj.|脆的，易碎的|Crisp and brittle.|例句：The crispin leaves crunched underfoot.  易碎的叶子在脚下嘎吱作响。|近义词：brittle, fragile, crisp|反义词:flexible, pliable| adjective
