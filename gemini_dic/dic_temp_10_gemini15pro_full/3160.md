aetiology: /ˌiːtiˈɒlədʒi/ (UK) /ˌiːtiˈɑːlədʒi/ (US)| n.|病原学；病因学|The study of the causes or origins of diseases or abnormal conditions.|The aetiology of the disease is still unknown.  这种疾病的病因仍然未知。|synonyms: causation, origin, genesis|antonyms: outcome, result|noun

aetiological: /ˌiːtiəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ (UK) /ˌiːtiəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/ (US)| adj.|病原学的；病因学的|Relating to the cause of a disease.|Aetiological factors for heart disease include smoking and high cholesterol. 心脏病的病因包括吸烟和高胆固醇。|synonyms: causal, causative|antonyms: resultant|adjective
