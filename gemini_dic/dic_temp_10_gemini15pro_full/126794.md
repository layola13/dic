nanostructures:/ˈnænoʊˌstrʌktʃərz/
n.
纳米结构
Structures with at least one dimension in the nanometer range (typically 1-100 nm).
Researchers are exploring the unique properties of nanostructures. 研究人员正在探索纳米结构的独特性质。
