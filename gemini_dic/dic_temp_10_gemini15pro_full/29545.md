catch:/kætʃ/ (present) /kɔːt/ (past & past participle)| v.|抓住，接住，赶上，染上(疾病)|To seize or grasp, especially after pursuit; to intercept and hold; to become infected with (a disease).|
The dog caught the ball in mid-air. 狗在半空中接住了球。

He caught a cold last week. 他上周感冒了。

近义词：grab, seize, capture, contract|反义词：miss, release, lose|词性：动词

caught:/kɔːt/| v. (past tense & past participle of catch)|抓住，接住，赶上，染上(疾病) (catch的过去式和过去分词)|Past tense and past participle form of "catch".|
She caught the train just in time. 她及时赶上了火车。

I caught the flu from my colleague. 我被同事传染了流感。
