cheliferous:/kɪˈlɪfərəs/
adj.
有螯的，具螯的
Bearing chelae; having chelae or pincers.
The cheliferous scorpion grabbed its prey. 有螯的蝎子抓住了它的猎物。
近义词:chelate, chelated
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "clawless" or "pincerless" in specific contexts)
词性: adjective
