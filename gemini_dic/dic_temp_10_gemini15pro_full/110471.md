lilliputianize:/ˌlɪlɪˈpjuːʃənaɪz/
|v.|使…变得极小；使…矮小化|to cause to seem small or insignificant; reduce to a Lilliputian scale or status.  It refers to making something or someone appear small, often by comparison to something much larger, or by reducing their importance or power.|The company's new policies lilliputianized the role of middle management.  这家公司的新政策削弱了中层管理人员的作用。|
|The author lilliputianized the protagonist's achievements to emphasize the vastness of the universe. 作者将主人公的成就缩小，以强调宇宙的浩瀚。|
|minimize, diminish, reduce, belittle|
|magnify, enlarge, amplify, enhance|
|verb|
