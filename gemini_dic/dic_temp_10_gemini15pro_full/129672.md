non-demise:/ˌnɒn dɪˈmaɪz/ | adj. | 非终止的，非失效的 |  Relating to a lease or agreement that does not terminate upon the death of a party. It signifies continuity or perpetuity, often used in legal contexts concerning property or contracts. | The non-demise clause in the contract ensures the agreement remains valid even after the owner's death.  合同中的非终止条款确保即使在业主死后协议仍然有效。 | Near Synonyms: perpetual, enduring, continuing, ongoing | Antonyms:  terminable, expiring, limited | Adjective

non-demise:/ˌnɒn dɪˈmaɪz/ | n. | 非终止，非失效 | The state or quality of not terminating or expiring, especially in legal contexts relating to leases or agreements. | The non-demise of the contract protected the tenant's rights. 合同的非终止性保护了租户的权利。| Near Synonyms:  perpetuity, continuance,  persistence | Antonyms: termination, expiration, cessation | Noun
