dynamite:/ˈdaɪnəˌmaɪt/ | n. | 炸药；具有爆炸性的事物 | An explosive made of nitroglycerin absorbed in a porous material.  A powerful or exciting person or thing. |  The workers used dynamite to blast through the rock. 工人们用炸药炸穿岩石。| synonyms: explosive, TNT, bomb, bombshell | antonyms: peace, tranquility | noun
