financialist:/faɪˈnænʃəlɪst/ | n. |金融家；财务专家|A person who is expert in or occupied with finance.|例句：The financialist predicted a market downturn.  这位金融家预测市场会下滑。

例句：She consulted a financialist about her investments. 她向一位金融专家咨询了她的投资事宜。

近义词：financier, investor, banker|反义词: debtor, borrower|词性:noun
