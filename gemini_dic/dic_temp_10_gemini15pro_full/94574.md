impoverisher:/ɪmˈpɒvərɪʃər/ | n. | 使人贫穷的人或物；使…贫瘠的事物 | Something that makes a person or area poor or infertile. | The war was an impoverisher of the nation, draining its resources and leaving its people destitute. 这场战争使国家变得贫穷，耗尽了资源，使人民陷入赤贫。|  synonyms: debilitator, depleter, exhauster, weakener | antonyms: enricher, benefactor, patron | noun

impoverish:/ɪmˈpɒvərɪʃ/ | v. | 使贫穷；使贫瘠；使枯竭 | To make poor; to deprive of strength, richness, or fertility. | The drought impoverished the farmers, leaving them with no crops to sell. 干旱使农民陷入贫困，他们没有庄稼可卖。| synonyms: deplete, exhaust, drain, bankrupt | antonyms: enrich, enhance, augment | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *empoverir, from em- (in, into) + povre (poor), from Latin pauper (poor).
