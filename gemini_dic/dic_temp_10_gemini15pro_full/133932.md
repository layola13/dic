nonhardy:/nɒnˈhɑːdi/ | adj. | 非耐寒的，不耐寒的 | Not able to survive exposure to winter conditions, especially cold temperatures.  | Example:  Many tropical plants are nonhardy and cannot be grown outdoors in colder climates. 许多热带植物不耐寒，不能在较冷的气候下露天种植。

Example:  Nonhardy annuals must be replanted each year.  一年生不耐寒植物必须每年重新种植。 | 近义词: tender, delicate | 反义词: hardy, robust | 词性: adjective
