gurglet:/ˈɡɜːrɡlət/| n. vi.|汩汩声，潺潺声；|A gurglet is a bubbling sound, like the sound of water flowing from a bottle. 汩汩声是指像水从瓶子里流出来的声音一样的气泡声。|The baby gurgled happily. 婴儿高兴地发出咯咯声。|近义词：bubble, gurgle, ripple|反义词：splash, roar|名词
gurglet:/ˈɡɜːrɡlət/| vi.|发出汩汩声，潺潺流动|To gurglet means to make a bubbling sound, like the sound of water flowing from a bottle.  发出汩汩声的意思是发出像水从瓶子里流出来的声音一样的气泡声。|The stream gurgled over the rocks. 小溪在岩石上潺潺流过。|近义词：bubble, gurgle, ripple|反义词：splash, roar|动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "gurgle," which is imitative of the sound. 源于动词 "gurgle"，是对声音的模仿。
