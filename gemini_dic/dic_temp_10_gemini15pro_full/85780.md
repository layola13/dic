haymaker:/ˈheɪmeɪkər/| n.|重拳；强有力的一击；<非正式>烈性酒|A powerful blow, typically a swinging punch.  A strong alcoholic drink (informal).|The boxer landed a haymaker that knocked his opponent out.  拳击手使出一记重拳，把对手击倒了。 He ordered a haymaker and downed it in one gulp. 他点了一杯烈酒，一口就喝了下去。|Synonyms: knockout punch, wallop, roundhouse, uppercut,  (for drink) potent potable|Antonyms: jab, weak blow, light punch|noun
