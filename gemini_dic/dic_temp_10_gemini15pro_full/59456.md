earworm:/ˈɪəwɜːrm/ (US), /ˈɪəwɜːm/ (UK)| n.|耳虫; 洗脑歌; 脑海挥之不去的旋律|A catchy tune or song that continues to run through one's mind even after it has stopped playing.|The advertising jingle became a real earworm – I couldn't stop humming it all day. 这广告歌真是洗脑，我一整天都在哼它。|Synonyms: brainworm, stuck song syndrome|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms exist, but one could consider "silence" or "peace and quiet" as contrasting states.)|Noun
