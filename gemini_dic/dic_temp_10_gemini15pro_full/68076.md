faddisms:/ˈfædɪzəmz/
noun
一时流行的东西；时尚；风尚
Trends, often short-lived, that gain popularity quickly and then fade away.
Platform shoes were one of the many faddisms of the 1970s.  20世纪70年代，松糕鞋是众多一时流行的时尚之一。
synonyms: trends, crazes, fashions, vogues
antonyms: traditions, classics, staples
n.
