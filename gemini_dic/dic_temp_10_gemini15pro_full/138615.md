nuclear-free:/ˈnjuːklɪər friː/
adj.
无核的；非核的；禁止使用核武器的
Free from nuclear weapons; advocating the abolition of nuclear weapons.
New Zealand is a nuclear-free country.  新西兰是一个无核国家。
The group is campaigning for a nuclear-free world.  该组织正在为一个无核世界而开展运动。
近义词:non-nuclear, antinuclear
反义词:nuclear, nuclearized
词性:形容词
