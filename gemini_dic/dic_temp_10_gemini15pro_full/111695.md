lockjaw: /ˈlɒkˌdʒɔː/ (British), /ˈlɑːkˌdʒɔː/ (American)
n.|破伤风; 牙关紧闭|A bacterial infection that causes painful muscle spasms, especially in the jaw and neck.  It is also called tetanus.|The patient exhibited symptoms of lockjaw after stepping on a rusty nail.  病人踩到生锈的钉子后出现了破伤风的症状。|近义词:tetanus|反义词:(None -  Lockjaw is a medical condition, not a descriptive adjective with a direct opposite)|词性:名词
