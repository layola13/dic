paleontology:/ˌpeɪˌliːɒnˈtɒlədʒi/ /ˌpeɪˌliːənˈtɑːlədʒi/ | n. | 古生物学 | The study of fossils as a way of getting information about the history of life on Earth and the structure of rocks that contain them. |  She's studying paleontology at university. 她在大学学习古生物学。 |  Paleontology provides crucial evidence for evolution. 古生物学为进化提供了关键证据。|  近义词: fossil science | 反义词:  None | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *paléontologie*, from Greek *palaios* "ancient" + *onta* "beings" + *-logia* "study of".
