immaterialism:/ɪməˈtɪəriəlɪzəm/
n.
唯心主义; 非物质主义
The doctrine that material things have no objective existence but exist only as ideas in the mind.
He studied the philosophy of immaterialism. 他研究了唯心主义哲学。
