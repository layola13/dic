multarticulate:/ˌmʌltɪɑːrˈtɪkjʊlət/
| adj. |多关节的；多音节的；表达清晰的|Having many joints or articulations; having many syllables; expressed clearly and distinctly.|The multarticulate robot arm could reach into tight spaces.  这个多关节的机械臂可以伸到狭窄的空间。| The multarticulate word "unconstitutionality" is difficult to pronounce. 多音节词“违宪”很难发音。  She delivered a multarticulate and persuasive speech. 她发表了一场表达清晰、令人信服的演讲。|近义词：articulate, polysyllabic, multijointed|反义词: inarticulate, monosyllabic|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Latin *multus* ("many") + *articulatus* ("jointed," past participle of *articulare*, "to joint"), from *articulus* ("small joint"), diminutive of *artus* ("joint").
