intelligentsia: /ɪnˌtɛlɪˈɡɛntsɪə/ | n. |知识分子，知识阶层 | A group of intellectuals and educated people who form an influential, often critical, segment of a society. | The intelligentsia often criticized the government's policies. 知识分子经常批评政府的政策。|  The intelligentsia debated the merits of the new economic plan. 知识分子们就新经济计划的优点进行了辩论。| synonyms: intellectuals, literati, academics | antonyms: masses, commoners | noun
