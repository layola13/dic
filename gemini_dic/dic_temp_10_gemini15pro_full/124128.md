mortgagers:/ˈmɔːrɡɪdʒərz/
n.
抵押人；按揭人
A person who mortgages property.  Someone who borrows money and gives the lender a mortgage on property as security.
例句：The mortgagers were struggling to keep up with their monthly payments. 抵押人正在努力跟上每月的还款进度。

例句：The bank contacted the mortgagers about the overdue payments. 银行就逾期付款联系了抵押人。

近义词：borrower, debtor
反义词：lender, mortgagee
词性：noun
