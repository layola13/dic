let-down:/ˈletdaʊn/| n.|失望；令人失望的人或事|a disappointment; something that fails to meet expectations|The party was a bit of a let-down.  这个派对有点令人失望。|synonyms: disappointment, setback, anticlimax|antonyms: success, triumph|noun

let-down:/ˈletdaʊn/| adj.|失望的；沮丧的|(feeling) disappointed or depressed, especially because something has not been as good, successful, exciting, etc. as you had hoped or expected|She felt very let down when her parents didn't come to see her play.  当她的父母没有来看她的演出时，她感到非常失望。|synonyms: disappointed, disheartened, discouraged|antonyms: elated, thrilled, encouraged|adjective
