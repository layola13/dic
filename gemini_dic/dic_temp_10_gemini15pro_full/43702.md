creditless:/ˈkrɛdɪtləs/
adjective
无片头字幕的，无制作人员名单的
Lacking the usual screen credits listing the names of the people involved in making a film or television program.
The fans circulated a creditless version of the movie trailer online. 影迷们在网上流传了一个无片头字幕的电影预告片版本。
Near Synonyms: uncredited, anonymous
Antonyms: credited, acknowledged
word type: adjective
