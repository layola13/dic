millets:/ˈmɪlɪts/| n.|小米（总称）；粟类|Small-seeded grasses that are grown as cereal crops, primarily in Asia and Africa.  Millets are highly tolerant of drought and other extreme weather conditions.|Millets are a staple food in many parts of the world. 小米是世界许多地方的主食。|India is the largest producer of millets. 印度是小米的最大产地。|近义词：grains, cereals|反义词: None|Noun
