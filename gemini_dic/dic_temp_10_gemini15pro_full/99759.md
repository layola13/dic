intermediae:/ˌɪntərˈmiːdieɪ/
adjective
中间的，中级的
lying or occurring between two extremes or in a middle position or state.
The disease is in its intermediate stage. 这种疾病处于中期阶段。
intermediate, median, middle, mid
extreme, ultimate
adjective
