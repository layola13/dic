brothership: /ˈbrʌðəʃɪp/ | n. | 兄弟情谊；手足之情；同志情谊 | The feeling of friendship and loyalty between brothers or people who share similar interests or goals. |  Example: There is a strong brothership between the members of the team.  团队成员之间有着深厚的兄弟情谊。| Example:  The brothership of soldiers who fought together in the war was undeniable.  一起在战争中战斗过的士兵之间的同志情谊是不可否认的.| 近义词: camaraderie, fellowship, fraternity | 反义词: animosity, hostility | 词性: 名词

词源:  From "brother" + "-ship" (suffix denoting state, quality, or condition).  来源于"brother"(兄弟) + "-ship"(后缀，表示状态、性质或条件).
