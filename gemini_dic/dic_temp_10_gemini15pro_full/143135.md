outcasts: /ˈaʊtˌkæsts/ | n. |被抛弃者，被驱逐者，流浪者|  A person who has been rejected by society or a group. | The outcasts lived on the fringes of society, shunned by everyone. 这些被抛弃者生活在社会的边缘，被所有人排斥。| synonyms: pariahs, exiles,  refugees | antonyms: insiders, members,  belongers | noun

outcast: /ˈaʊtˌkæst/ | adj. |被抛弃的，被驱逐的| Rejected and cast out. |  He felt outcast and alone after losing his job and family.  失去工作和家庭后，他感到被抛弃和孤独。| synonyms: rejected, excluded, shunned | antonyms: accepted, included, welcomed | adjective
