attainableness:/əˈteɪnəb(ə)lnəs/
n.
可达到性；可获得性
The attainableness of his goals motivated him to work harder.  他目标的可实现性激励他更加努力工作。
The attainableness of a college education depends on various factors.  大学教育的可获得性取决于多种因素。
近义词:achievability, accessibility, feasibility
反义词:unattainability, inaccessibility, impossibility
词性:noun

attain:/əˈteɪn/
v.
达到；获得
He attained his goal of becoming a doctor. 他实现了成为医生的目标。
She attained the highest score in the class. 她获得了班级最高分。
近义词:achieve, reach, accomplish
反义词:fail, miss, lose
词性:verb

attainable:/əˈteɪnəb(ə)l/
adj.
可达到的；可获得的
His goals are attainable with hard work. 他的目标通过努力是可以实现的。
A college education is attainable for most people.  对大多数人来说，大学教育是可以获得的。
近义词:achievable, accessible, feasible
反义词:unattainable, inaccessible, impossible
词性:adjective
