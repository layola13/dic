imperforable:/ɪmˈpɜːrfərəbəl/ | adj. | 不可穿透的；不能渗透的 | Not able to be perforated or penetrated. |  The material is imperforable to water. 这种材料不透水。

The shield was imperforable to their arrows.  这盾牌他们的箭射不穿。

| 近义词: impenetrable, impervious, impermeable | 反义词: permeable, porous, penetrable | 词性: adjective
