impolitical:/ɪmpəˈlɪtɪkl/
adjective
不关心政治的；与政治无关的
Not interested or involved in politics.
He always tried to remain impoltical, preferring to focus on his business. 他总是试图保持不关心政治，更喜欢专注于他的事业。
Her impoltical stance made her an unpopular choice for student body president. 她对政治不感兴趣的立场使她成为学生会主席的不受欢迎人选。
Synonyms: non-political, apolitical, unpolitical
Antonyms: political, partisan
