hernshaw:/ˈhɜːrnʃɔː/
n.
苍鹭
A large wading bird with long legs, a long neck, and a long beak.
例句：The hernshaw stood motionless in the shallows, waiting for an unsuspecting fish.  苍鹭一动不动地站在浅滩里，等待着毫无戒心的鱼。

例句：A hernshaw took flight, its broad wings carrying it effortlessly across the sky. 一只苍鹭飞了起来，它宽阔的翅膀毫不费力地带着它飞过天空。

近义词：heron, egret
