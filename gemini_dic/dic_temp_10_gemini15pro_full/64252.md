equipoise:/ˈekwɪpɔɪz/ /ˈiːkwɪpɔɪz/
noun
平衡，均衡
A state of equilibrium or balance.
The painting achieves a perfect equipoise of light and shadow. 这幅画的光影达到了完美的平衡。

equipoise:/ˈekwɪpɔɪz/ /ˈiːkwɪpɔɪz/
verb
使平衡，使均衡
To balance or counterbalance.
He equipoised the risks against the potential rewards. 他权衡了风险和潜在的回报。


Etymology:
From Middle French equipoise, from Late Latin aequipois-, from Latin aequi- (equal) + pondus (weight).

Synonyms:
balance, equilibrium, counterpoise, stability, symmetry

Antonyms:
imbalance, instability, asymmetry, disequilibrium
