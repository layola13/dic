araneiform:/əˈreɪnɪfɔːrm/| adj.|蜘蛛形的，蛛网状的|Resembling a spider or its web; cobweb-like.|例句：The frost on the window formed araneiform patterns. 窗户上的霜形成了蛛网状的图案。|例句：The delicate, araneiform sculpture added an ethereal touch to the artwork. 精致的蛛网状雕塑为艺术品增添了空灵的气息。|近义词：arachnoid, web-like, gossamer|反义词:solid, dense|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *arāneiformis*, from *arānea* (spider) + *-formis* (having the form of).
