martiality:/mɑːrʃiˈælɪti/
n.
好战性，尚武精神，武术
The state or quality of being martial or warlike; warrior-like character.
The small nation, despite its size, maintained a fierce martiality.  这个小国虽然面积不大，但却保持着强烈的尚武精神。
His martiality was evident in his stiff posture and booming voice. 他的好战性从他僵硬的姿势和洪亮的声音中显露无疑。
近义词: belligerence, aggressiveness, militarism
反义词: pacifism, peacefulness, gentleness
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *martialitas, from Latin martialis ("martial").
