detour:/ˈdiːtʊr/ (US), /ˈdiːtʊə(r)/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|绕道，迂回；绕行的路|A deviation from a direct course or the usual route.|We had to make a detour around the flooded area. 我们不得不绕过洪水区。|synonyms: bypass, diversion, roundabout|antonyms: direct route, straight path|noun
detour:/ˈdiːtʊr/ (US), /ˈdiːtʊə(r)/ (UK)| vi. vt.|绕道行驶，绕路|To travel by a detour.|The road was closed, so we had to detour through the backroads. 道路封闭了，所以我们不得不绕道穿过小路。|synonyms: bypass, divert, go around|antonyms: go direct, go straight|verb
