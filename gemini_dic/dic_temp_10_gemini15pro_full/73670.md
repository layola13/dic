forges:/fɔːrdʒɪz/| v. (third-person singular simple present forges, present participle forging, simple past and past participle forged)|伪造；锻造；缔造；前进|creates something strong, enduring, or fraudulent; moves ahead steadily, often with determination|He forged a new career in software development.  他开创了软件开发的新事业。

The blacksmith forged a beautiful sword. 铁匠锻造了一把漂亮的剑。|近义词：creates, builds, fabricates, fashions, forms, makes, produces, shapes, constructs, establishes, molds, develops, strengthens, advances, proceeds, progresses|反义词：destroys, dismantles, ruins, weakens, retreats, withdraws|词性:动词

forge:/fɔːrdʒ/| n.|锻造车间；熔炉|a workshop or furnace where metal is heated and shaped|The blacksmith works in his forge. 铁匠在他的锻造车间工作。

He built a forge in his backyard. 他在后院建了一个熔炉。|近义词：furnace, smithy, workshop, foundry|反义词：N/A|词性:名词
