coetaneous:/ˌkoʊɪˈteɪniəs/| adj.|同龄的|Of the same age or date.|The twins are coetaneous, born only minutes apart. 这对双胞胎同年同月同日生，只相差几分钟。|Synonyms:  contemporary, coeval|Antonyms:  non-contemporary|adjective
