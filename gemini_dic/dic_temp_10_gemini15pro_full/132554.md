nondiagnostic:/ˌnɒndaɪəɡˈnɒstɪk/ | adj. | 非诊断性的 | Not leading to or establishing a diagnosis; inconclusive.  | The test results were nondiagnostic, so further investigation is needed.  测试结果是非诊断性的，因此需要进一步调查。 | The scan proved nondiagnostic, leaving the doctors puzzled.  扫描结果没有诊断价值，让医生们感到困惑。 |  Synonyms: inconclusive, unhelpful, uninformative | Antonyms: diagnostic, conclusive, revealing | adjective
