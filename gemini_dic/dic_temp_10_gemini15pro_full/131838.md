nonconspirator:/ˌnɒnkənˈspɪrətər/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈspɪrətər/
noun
非共谋者; 非阴谋者
A person who is not involved in a conspiracy.
The jury found him to be a nonconspirator and acquitted him. 陪审团认定他不是共谋者，并宣判他无罪。

The judge released the nonconspirators after the trial.  法官在审判后释放了非共谋者。

Synonym: innocent, bystander
Antonym: conspirator, accomplice
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not". This prefix negates the word it is attached to.
conspirator: from Late Latin conspirator, from Latin conspirare "to agree, unite, plot," from com- "together" + spirare "to breathe."  This refers to the idea of people whispering together secretly.
