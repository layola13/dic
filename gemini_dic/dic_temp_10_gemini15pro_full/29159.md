casework:/ˈkeɪswɜːrk/
noun
个案工作，个案研究，社会工作
The study and treatment of an individual's social problems by a social worker.
The social worker was assigned a heavy casework load.  这个社会工作者被分配了繁重的个案工作。
synonyms: social work, case management, counseling
antonyms: N/A
n.


casework:/ˈkeɪswɜːrk/
noun
（尤指定制的）家具；细木工制品
Cabinets and other fitted woodwork, especially of the better quality.
The kitchen featured beautiful cherrywood casework. 厨房里漂亮的樱桃木细木工制品引人注目。
synonyms: cabinetry, woodwork, joinery
antonyms: N/A
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From "case" + "work". "Case" referring to an instance of something or a person being studied or treated, and "work" referring to the activity or process involved.
