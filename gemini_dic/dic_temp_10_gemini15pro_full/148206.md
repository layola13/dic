overshortened:/ˌoʊvərˈʃɔːrtnd/| adj. |过度缩短的|Having been shortened excessively.|The overshortened dress was inappropriate for the occasion. 这条过短的裙子不适合这个场合。|The overshortened cable wouldn't reach the outlet. 这条过短的电缆够不到插座。|short, curtailed, truncated|long, extended|adjective
