discharging:/dɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒɪŋ/
verb
排出；卸货；释放；解雇；履行（职责）；清偿（债务）
releasing, emitting, unloading, firing, dismissing, performing (duties), paying off (debt)
The factory is discharging pollutants into the river. 这家工厂正在向河里排放污染物。
The ship is discharging its cargo at the dock.  这艘船正在码头卸货。

synonym
release, emit, unload, fire, dismiss, acquit
antonym
charge, load, hire, retain,  accumulate

词性
verb
