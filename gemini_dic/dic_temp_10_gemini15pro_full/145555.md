overcompensated: /ˌoʊvərˈkɑːmpənˌseɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过度补偿的，矫枉过正的 | Having or showing a tendency to compensate excessively for a perceived inadequacy or insecurity.  |  He bought a huge, flashy car, which seemed overcompensated for his small stature. 他买了一辆巨大的、炫耀的汽车，这似乎是为了弥补他矮小的身材而矫枉过正。 | Example:  Feeling insecure about his lack of education, he overcompensated by boasting about his wealth.  由于对缺乏教育感到不安全，他通过吹嘘自己的财富来过度补偿。 | Synonyms: overreacting, overdoing, going overboard | Antonyms: undercompensated, understated, restrained | word type: adjective
