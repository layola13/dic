knickerless:/ˈnɪkərləs/
adjective
没穿灯笼裤的；不穿女用短衬裤的
Not wearing knickers (women's underwear).
She went knickerless under her skirt. 她裙子里面没穿内裤。
Near Synonyms: underwearless, pantyless
Antonyms:  (wearing underwear, but no single perfect antonym)
word type: adjective
