counterprove:/ˌkaʊntərˈpruːv/
verb
反驳，证明…不成立
To prove to be false; disprove.
The lawyer counterproved the witness's testimony. 律师反驳了证人的证词。
Synonyms: disprove, refute, rebut
Antonyms: prove, confirm, verify
词性: 动词
