nonassertions:/ˌnɒnəˈsɜːʃənz/ | n. | 非断言，非声明 | Statements that do not assert or claim something definitively.  These can include questions, commands, expressions of emotion, etc. | Example: Her response was a series of nonassertions, leaving me unsure of her actual opinion. 她的回答是一系列非断言，让我不确定她的真实想法。 | Example:  Questions and commands are classic examples of nonassertions.  问题和命令是非断言的典型例子。| Synonym: implications, insinuations | Antonym: assertions, declarations | noun

nonassertion:/ˌnɒnəˈsɜːʃən/ | n. | 非断言，非声明 | The act of not asserting or claiming something definitively. | Example: His nonassertion of guilt frustrated the investigators.  他不声明自己有罪，这让调查人员感到沮丧。 | Example: Her nonassertion in the meeting allowed others to dominate the conversation. 她在会议上的非断言让其他人主导了谈话。| Synonym: reticence, silence | Antonym: assertion, declaration | noun
