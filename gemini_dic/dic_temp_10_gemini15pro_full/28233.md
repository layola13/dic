capstone:/ˈkæpˌstoʊn/ (US) /ˈkæpˌstəʊn/ (UK)| n.|顶点；顶峰；毕业项目；压顶石|A final project in a student’s academic program, typically involving research, design, or production.  A crowning achievement or final touch. A stone fixed on top of a structure.|例句：Her capstone project was a sophisticated marketing plan for a new product. 她的毕业项目是一个针对新产品的复杂营销计划。

例句：Winning the championship was the capstone to a remarkable season. 赢得冠军是这个辉煌赛季的顶峰。|近义词：culmination, climax, pinnacle, summit, keystone|反义词:base, foundation, beginning|noun
