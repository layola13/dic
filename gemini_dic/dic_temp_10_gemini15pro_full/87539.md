heteroglossic:/ˌhɛtərəˈɡlɒsɪk/ /ˌhɛtəroʊˈɡlɑːsɪk/
adjective
多语体的；包含多种语言变体的
Characterized by a diversity of voices, styles, or viewpoints.  A heteroglossic text acknowledges the existence of multiple perspectives and avoids presenting a single, monolithic truth.
The novel is a heteroglossic work, incorporating dialects, slang, and multiple narrative voices. 这部小说是一部多语体作品，包含了方言、俚语和多种叙事声音。
Bakhtin's theory of heteroglossic discourse emphasizes the interplay of different languages and social voices. 巴赫金的多语体话语理论强调了不同语言和社会声音的相互作用。
