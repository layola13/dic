artiodactylous:/ˌɑːrtiəˈdæktɪləs/
| adj. |偶蹄的；偶蹄类动物的|Having an even number of toes or digits on each foot, typically two or four.|Cows, sheep, and deer are artiodactylous mammals. 牛、羊和鹿都是偶蹄哺乳动物。|Synonyms: even-toed, cloven-hoofed|Antonyms: perissodactyl (odd-toed)|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἄρτιος (ártios, “even”) + δάκτυλος (dáktulos, “finger, toe”).
