gawns:/ɡɔːnz/ | n. | gowns（gown的复数形式） |  A long, loose robe, typically worn on formal occasions or for a particular purpose. | The graduates wore their gowns with pride. 毕业生们自豪地穿着他们的长袍。| The queen wore a beautiful, flowing gown. 女王穿着一件漂亮飘逸的长袍。| 近义词：robes, dresses, frocks | 反义词: everyday clothes, casual wear | 词性: noun

词源: From Middle English *goune*, from Anglo-Norman *gune, goune*, Old North French *gone* (compare Old French *gune*), from Frankish **gunna**, from Proto-Germanic **gunnō** (compare West Frisian *gone*, Dutch *goon*, German *Gonne* (“gown, chasuble”)).
