dipterous:/ˈdɪptərəs/| adj.|双翅目的；双翅类的|Having two wings or two wing-like appendages.  Relating to or characteristic of the order Diptera, which includes flies, mosquitoes, gnats, and midges.|Dipterous insects, like houseflies, are known for their ability to transmit diseases.|Synonyms: two-winged|Antonyms: wingless
词性: 形容词
