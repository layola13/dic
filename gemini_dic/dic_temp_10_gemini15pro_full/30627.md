ceremonialist:/ˌserɪˈmoʊniəlɪst/
n.
仪式专家；司仪
A person who performs or leads ceremonies, especially religious or traditional ones.
The ceremonialist led the bride and groom through the ancient wedding rituals.  仪式专家带领新娘和新郎完成了古老的婚礼仪式。

The ceremonialist chanted the prayers in a low, resonant voice. 司仪用低沉而洪亮的声音吟诵着祈祷文。


近义词:master of ceremonies, officiant, ritualist
反义词:None
词性:Noun
