Etymology:
From Middle English *overweningnesse*, from Old English *oferwenignys*, from *oferwenian* ("to be arrogant, presume")  + *-ness* (suffix forming nouns expressing a state, condition, or quality). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *ubarwinjaną* (“to presume, despair”).
