calicoback:/ˈkælɪkoʊˌbæk/ | n. |印花棉布背面; (尤指)廉价粗糙的布料背面; 粗糙的布料; 廉价而粗糙的东西 | The underside of calico fabric, often rough and inexpensive; by extension, something cheap and rough. |  The curtains were made of cheap calicoback.  窗帘是用廉价的印花棉布背面做的。| Synonym: cheap cloth, shoddy material | Antonym: fine fabric, high-quality material | noun

calicoback:/ˈkælɪkoʊˌbæk/ | adj. |(尤指布料)廉价粗糙的 | Cheap and rough, especially of fabric. | The calicoback dress felt scratchy against her skin. 这件印花棉布背面的裙子在她皮肤上感觉很粗糙。| Synonym: coarse, rough, cheap | Antonym: fine, smooth, expensive | adjective
