frills:/frɪlz/
noun
装饰;褶边;不必要的东西
decorative or unnecessary features or additions
The dress was simple, without any frills. 这件衣服很朴素，没有任何装饰。
ornaments, decorations, embellishments
basics, essentials, necessities
n.

frill:/frɪl/
noun
装饰;褶边;不必要的东西
a decorative pleated or gathered edge of cloth
The curtains had a pretty frill along the bottom. 窗帘底部有一圈漂亮的褶边。
ruffle, flounce, furbelow
plainness, simplicity
n.

frill:/frɪl/
verb
给…加上褶边
to decorate with a frill
She frilled the edges of the tablecloth. 她给桌布的边缘加了褶边。
decorate, adorn, ornament
strip, bare
v.
