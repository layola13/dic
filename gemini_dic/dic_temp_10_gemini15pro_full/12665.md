assembles:/əˈsɛmbəlz/
| v. |集合，聚集，装配|To gather or bring together (parts) into a whole; to fit together the components of (something, such as a machine).|The workers assembled the car in the factory. 工人们在工厂里组装汽车。|
| v. |集合，聚集|To come together in a group. |The students assembled in the auditorium for the lecture. 学生们聚集在礼堂里听讲座。|
|近义词：gather, collect, congregate, construct, build|
|反义词：disassemble, disperse, scatter|
|词性：动词|
