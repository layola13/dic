familias:/fəˈmiːliəs/ | adj. | family (used as an adjective, though less common than "familial")  | Relating to or characteristic of a family or families. |  The familias bonds were strong in that community.  那个社区的家族纽带很强。 | familial, kin, related | unrelated | adjective
