bolger:/ˈboʊldʒər/| n.|吹牛大王；大人物；大块头|A boastful, pompous, or self-important person; a large or bulky person or thing.|He's a bit of a bolger, always talking about his achievements.|近义词：braggart, blowhard, big shot|反义词：humble person, nobody|词性:noun

Etymology:
Likely from dialectal English *bolge "to swell," related to bulge.  The sense evolved from "something swollen or puffed up" to "a pompous person."
