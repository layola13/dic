effected: /əˈfɛktɪd/
verb
产生；实现；引起
to produce; to bring about; to cause
The new policy effected significant changes in the economy. 新政策对经济产生了重大变化。
The lawyer effected a compromise between the two parties. 律师促成了双方之间的和解。

synonym: achieved, accomplished, executed, realized, performed, implemented
antonym: prevented, hindered, obstructed, thwarted

词性: 动词 (verb)
