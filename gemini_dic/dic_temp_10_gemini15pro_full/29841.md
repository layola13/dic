causal:/ˈkɔːzl/| adj.|因果的，原因的|Relating to or acting as a cause; involving cause and effect.|The study showed a causal link between smoking and lung cancer. 这项研究表明吸烟和肺癌之间存在因果关系。|Synonyms: causative, generative, productive|Antonyms: resultant, resulting, consequent|adjective
