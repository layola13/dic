cocoonery:/kəˈkuːnəri/
noun
茧房，安乐窝
A state of being enclosed or wrapped up comfortably, like a cocoon; a place of seclusion or retreat.
He retreated into his cocoonery of books and blankets.  他躲进他由书籍和毯子构成的安乐窝里。
cocooning, sanctuary, refuge, retreat
exposure, vulnerability

词性: n.
