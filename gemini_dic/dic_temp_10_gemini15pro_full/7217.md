Anglicanism emphasizes both scripture and tradition. 圣公会主义强调圣经和传统。 | 近义词: Episcopalianism | 反义词: Catholicism, Protestantism (Note: These are broad generalizations, as Anglicanism shares aspects with both) | 词性: noun
