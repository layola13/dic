alabastrum:/ˌæləˈbæstrəm/| n.|雪花石膏盒；雪花石膏小瓶；(尤指用于盛香油的)细颈瓶|A small container, typically made of alabaster, used to hold ointments or perfumes, especially in ancient times.  It often had a narrow neck.|The archaeologist unearthed a delicate alabastrum filled with remnants of ancient perfume. 考古学家出土了一个盛有古代香水残留物的精致雪花石膏小瓶。|Synonym: vial, flask, phial|Antonym: N/A (lack of a direct opposite for a specific container type)|noun
