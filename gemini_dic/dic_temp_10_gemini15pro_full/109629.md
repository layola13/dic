letters:/ˈlɛtərz/ | n. |字母；信件|Characters that represent sounds in writing; messages communicated in written form.|例句：The child is learning her letters.  这个孩子正在学习字母。

例句：She writes letters to her pen pal every month. 她每个月都给她的笔友写信。|近义词：characters, alphabet (for letters of the alphabet), mail, correspondence (for letters as messages)|反义词: None (no direct opposite for letters) |词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French lettre, from Latin littera (“letter of the alphabet, literature, epistle”), related to legere (“to read”).
