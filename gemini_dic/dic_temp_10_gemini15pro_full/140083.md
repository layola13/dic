ofan:/ˈʌv ən/ /əv ən/ | preposition | (表示所属) …的 | Of a/an is used to show possession or belonging, similar to the possessive apostrophe ('s). It's used before nouns referring to a part or characteristic of something.  |  例句：She is a friend of an acquaintance. 她是一个熟人的朋友。

例句：He is a man of an interesting character.  他是一个性格有趣的人。 | 近义词: belonging to,  | 反义词: N/A | 词性: preposition
