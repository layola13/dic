adhort:/ədˈhɔːrt/ | v. | 劝告；鼓励；敦促 | To urge strongly; advise earnestly. | The general exhorted his troops to fight bravely. 将军激励他的部队勇敢作战。

The coach adhorred the team to give their all in the final minutes. 教练敦促队员在最后几分钟全力以赴。 | 近义词：urge, encourage, exhort, advise, counsel | 反义词：dissuade, discourage | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adhortārī*, from *ad-* (“to, towards”) + *hortārī* (“to urge, encourage”), frequentative of *horrēre* (“to bristle, stand on end”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰers-* (“to bristle”).


USAGE->

I adhort my students to study hard. = 我劝我的学生努力学习。
