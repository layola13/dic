bullionless:/ˈbʊljənˌləs/
adjective
无金条的；没有金银的
Without bullion; lacking gold or silver in bulk.
The museum's vault was surprisingly bullionless after the robbery.  博物馆的保险库在抢劫后竟然空空如也，没有金条。
The once wealthy nation became bullionless after years of economic mismanagement.  这个曾经富裕的国家由于多年的经济管理不善而变得没有金银储备。
synonym:  goldless, silverless, without precious metals
antonym:  bullion-rich, wealthy in gold/silver
词性: adjective
