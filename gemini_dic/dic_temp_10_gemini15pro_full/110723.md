liner:/ˈlaɪnər/| n.|班轮，班机；衬垫，衬里|A large ship or aircraft used for carrying passengers on regular routes.  Something used as a lining, such as a piece of material placed inside a garment or other object.|The ocean liner docked at the pier. 这艘远洋班轮停靠在码头。|She used a liner to keep her lipstick from smudging. 她用唇线笔防止口红晕染。|Synonyms: ship, vessel, aircraft, lining, padding|Antonyms:  (For ship/aircraft) none; (For lining) covering, exterior|noun
