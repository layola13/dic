hornlessness:/ˈhɔːnlɪsnəs/| n.|无角；无角性|The state or quality of being without horns.  The absence of horns in an animal that would normally have them.|The farmer bred polled cattle for their hornlessness, as it made them easier to manage.|近义词: polledness, absence of horns|反义词: hornedness, presence of horns|词性:noun
