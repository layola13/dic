foisty:/ˈfɔɪsti/ (US), /ˈfɔɪsti/ (UK)| adj.|发霉的；有霉味的；闷浊的|Having a stale, damp, and musty smell, as from mildew.  Implies a confined, airless atmosphere.|例句：The old books in the attic had a foisty smell.  阁楼里的旧书散发出一股霉味。

例句：The foisty air in the basement made me cough.  地下室里霉浊的空气让我咳嗽。|近义词：musty, mildewy, damp, stale, fusty|反义词：fresh, airy, clean, fragrant|词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From obsolete *foist* (probably a variant of *fist*), meaning 'stench', of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to Middle Dutch *vuist* ('fist'), referring to the unpleasant smell of a closed fist.
