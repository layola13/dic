eradicable:/ɪˈrædɪkəbl/
adjective
可根除的，可消灭的
Able to be eradicated; destroy completely.
Malaria is not eradicable in many tropical countries.  疟疾在许多热带国家无法根除。
The government is trying to eradicate corruption. 政府正试图根除腐败。
近义词:removable, eliminable, extinguishable
反义词:ineradicable, incurable
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin eradicabilis, from eradicare ‘to root out,’ from e- (variant of ex-) ‘out’ + radix, radic- ‘root.’
