left-winger: /ˌleftˈwɪŋər/ | n. | 左翼人士；左翼分子 | A person with left-wing political views, typically advocating social equality and government intervention. | Example: The left-winger advocated for higher taxes on the wealthy.  左翼人士主张对富人征收更高的税。

Example:  She's considered a left-winger because of her support for universal healthcare.  由于她支持全民医保，她被认为是左翼人士。| 近义词: socialist, progressive, liberal | 反义词: right-winger, conservative | 词性: 名词
