insolubly:/ɪnˈsɒljʊbli/ | adv. | 不可溶地；无法解决地 | In a way that cannot be solved or explained. |  The two countries are insolubly linked by history. 这两个国家有着无法解开的历史渊源。| 近义词: inextricably, permanently, hopelessly | 反义词: solubly, resolvably | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From Late Latin *insolubilis, from in- "not" + solubilis "that can be loosened, dissolved," from solvere "to loosen, dissolve."
