ccps:/ˈsiː siː piː ɛs/ | n. | 中央对手方清算机制 (Central Counterparty Clearing System) | A system that interposes itself between counterparties to contracts traded in one or more financial markets, becoming the buyer to every seller and the seller to every buyer. | Example: The CCPS guarantees the settlement of trades, even if one party defaults. 中央对手方清算机制保证交易的结算，即使一方违约。| Example: CCPS mitigates systemic risk by managing the exposure of its members. 中央对手方清算机制通过管理其成员的风险敞口来降低系统性风险。|Synonym: clearing house, central counterparty| Antonym: N/A | noun
