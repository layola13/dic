cannula:/ˈkæn.jʊ.lə/ (US), /ˈkæn.jə.lə/ (UK)| n.|套管;插管;导管|A thin tube inserted into a vein or body cavity to administer medicine, drain off fluid, or insert surgical instruments.|The surgeon inserted a cannula into the patient's vein to administer fluids. 外科医生将套管插入患者的静脉以输送液体。|The doctor used a cannula to drain fluid from the patient's knee. 医生用套管排出病人膝盖里的积液。|近义词：catheter, tube|反义词: None|noun
