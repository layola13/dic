delftware:/ˈdɛlftˌwɛər/
noun
代尔夫特陶器
Tin-glazed earthenware, typically decorated in blue on a white ground, originally made in Delft, Netherlands.
My grandmother has a beautiful collection of delftware.  我祖母收藏了一套漂亮的代尔夫特陶器。
synonyms: Delft pottery, Delft, earthenware
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a specific type of pottery)
