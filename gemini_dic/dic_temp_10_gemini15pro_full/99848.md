intermixt:/ˌɪntərˈmɪkst/| v. adj.|混合，混杂|To mix together; to blend.|The poem intermixed humor and pathos. 这首诗幽默和伤感交织在一起。|近义词：mix, blend, mingle, combine, intermingle|反义词:separate, divide, sort|词性:动词/形容词
词源: From Middle English *intermyxten, from Late Latin intermixtus, past participle of Latin intermiscēre (“to mix among, intermingle”), from inter- (“between, among”) + miscēre (“to mix”).
