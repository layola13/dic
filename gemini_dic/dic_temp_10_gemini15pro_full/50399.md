depositary:/dɪˈpɒzɪtəri/ /dɪˈpɑːzɪtɔːri/
n.|保管人，受托人，存放处|a person or institution to whom something is entrusted for safekeeping or management|The bank acted as a depositary for the client's valuable documents.  这家银行充当客户贵重文件的保管人。|
synonyms: custodian, trustee, guardian, keeper
antonyms: depositor, owner
noun

depositary:/dɪˈpɒzɪtəri/ /dɪˈpɑːzɪtɔːri/
adj.|保管的，存放的|relating to or characteristic of a depositary|The depositary institution is responsible for the safety of the assets. 该保管机构负责资产的安全。|
synonyms: custodial
antonyms: N/A
adjective
