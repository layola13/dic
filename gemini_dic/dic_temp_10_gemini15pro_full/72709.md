focused:/ˈfoʊkəst/ (US) /ˈfəʊkəst/ (UK)| adj.|专注的，集中的|Paying particular attention to something.|She had a focused expression on her face as she worked on the problem. 她专注地处理问题，脸上带着专注的表情。|The laser beam is focused on the target. 激光束聚焦在目标上。|近义词：concentrated, attentive, intent|反义词：distracted, unfocused, scattered|词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "focus," which comes from the Latin "focus" meaning "hearth, fireplace."  The meaning shifted to the point where light rays converge and then to a center of activity or attention.
