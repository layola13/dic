embodies:/ɪmˈbɑːdiz/ | v. | 体现，使具体化，包含 | represent in a physical form; include or contain as a constituent part |  This painting embodies the spirit of the Renaissance. 这幅画体现了文艺复兴的精神。| synonyms:  represent, personify, epitomize, incorporate, exemplify | antonyms: exclude, omit | verb


embodies:/ɪmˈbɑːdiz/ | v. | 表现，象征 | be an expression of or give a tangible or visible form to (an idea, quality, or feeling) | He embodies the qualities of a good leader. 他展现了一位优秀领导者的品质。| synonyms: express, manifest, demonstrate | antonyms: conceal, hide | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English *embodien*, from Anglo-Norman *embodier*, from *en-* "in" + *bodi* "body".  The modern spelling emerged in the 16th century.
