batch:/bætʃ/ | n. | 一批；一炉；一组；一批生产量 | A group of things or people that are treated or dealt with together. |  The baker made a batch of cookies. 面包师烤了一炉饼干。

The next batch of students will arrive in September. 下一批学生将于九月到来。 | group, set, lot | individual, single | noun

batch:/bætʃ/ | vt. | 分批处理 | To group things or people together for treatment or processing. |  The emails were batched and sent out every hour. 电子邮件被分批处理，每小时发送一次。

We batched the orders together to save on shipping costs. 我们把订单合并在一起以节省运费。 | group, bundle, process | separate, individualize | verb
