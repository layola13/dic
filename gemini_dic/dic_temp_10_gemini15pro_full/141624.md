optician:/ɒpˈtɪʃən/ (UK) /ɑːpˈtɪʃən/ (US)| n. |眼镜商；验光师|A person who makes or sells eyeglasses and contact lenses.|例句：I went to the optician to get my eyes tested. 我去验光师那里检查了眼睛。

例句：The optician adjusted the frames of my glasses. 眼镜商调整了我的眼镜框。

近义词：optometrist|反义词:|词性:noun

opticians:/ɒpˈtɪʃənz/ (UK) /ɑːpˈtɪʃənz/ (US)| n. |眼镜商（复数）；验光师（复数）|Plural form of optician.|例句：Many opticians now offer a wide range of designer frames. 许多眼镜商现在提供各种各样的名牌镜框。


例句：Opticians are qualified to examine eyes and prescribe corrective lenses. 验光师有资格检查眼睛并配矫正镜片。
