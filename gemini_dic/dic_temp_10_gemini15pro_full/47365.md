deaconships:/ˈdiːkənʃɪps/
n.
执事职务；执事身份
The office or ministry of a deacon.  The period of time served as a deacon.
He held several deaconships during his time with the church.  他在这间教堂任职期间担任过几个执事职务。
Her deaconship lasted for five years. 她的执事任期持续了五年。
