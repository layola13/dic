icterus:/ˈɪktərəs/ | n. | 黄疸 |A medical condition in which the skin, whites of the eyes, and mucous membranes turn yellow because of a high level of bilirubin in the blood.| The doctor diagnosed the patient with icterus.  医生诊断病人患有黄疸。| Jaundice |  | noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin icterus, from Greek ἴκτερος (íkteros), jaundice. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ǵhel- (“yellow, green”).
