cornucopia:/ˌkɔːrnjəˈkoʊpiə/ /ˌkɔːnjuˈkəʊpiə/| n.|丰饶之角，大量，丰富|A symbol of abundance and nourishment, depicted as a goat's horn overflowing with fruits, flowers, and other desirable things.  A large amount or supply of something.|The Thanksgiving table was a cornucopia of delicious food. 感恩节的餐桌上摆满了各种美味佳肴。|Synonyms: abundance, profusion, bounty, plethora|Antonyms: scarcity, dearth, lack, paucity|noun
