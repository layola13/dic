dissemblingly:/dɪˈsɛmblɪŋli/
adverb
虚伪地，假装地
in a dissembling manner; so as to hide one's true feelings or intentions.
She smiled dissemblingly, hiding her true anger. 她虚伪地笑着，掩饰着她真实的愤怒。
He dissemblingly praised his rival's work, while secretly plotting to undermine him. 他假惺惺地称赞对手的作品，暗地里却计划着要破坏他。
近义词: deceptively, falsely, hypocritically
反义词: honestly, sincerely, truthfully
词性: adverb

词源:
From dissemble + -ing + -ly.  Dissemble comes from Old French *dessembler, from des- "away from" + sembler "to seem, appear."
