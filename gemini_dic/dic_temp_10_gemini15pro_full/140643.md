奥马哈（美国内布拉斯加州的最大城市）
Omaha is the largest city in Nebraska, USA.
例句：Omaha is located on the Missouri River. 奥马哈位于密苏里河畔。

例句：My family is from Omaha, Nebraska. 我的家人来自内布拉斯加州的奥马哈。

近义词：(As a city, it has no direct synonyms.  One could use "city," "metropolis," or "urban center" in a general sense.)
反义词：(As a proper noun, it has no antonyms.)
词性: noun


词源: From the Omaha people, a Native American tribe.  The name likely derives from an Omaha word meaning "upstream people."
