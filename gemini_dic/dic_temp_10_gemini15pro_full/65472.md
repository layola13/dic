euromaidan:/ˌjʊərəʊˈmaɪdɑːn/ | n. | 欧洲广场（乌克兰基辅独立广场的别称，2013-2014年乌克兰亲欧盟示威运动的中心）| A large wave of protests that started in Ukraine in November 2013, initially against the government's decision not to sign an agreement with the European Union, but which later expanded into wider anti-government demonstrations. | The Euromaidan protests led to the ousting of President Yanukovych. 欧洲广场抗议导致亚努科维奇总统下台。|  synonyms: Maidan,  Ukrainian Revolution of Dignity | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps counter-protest or pro-Russian movement in Ukraine) | noun
