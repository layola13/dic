overanimations: /ˌoʊvərˌænɪˈmeɪʃənz/ | n. | 过度动画，过度活跃 | Excessive or exaggerated animations, often appearing unnatural or frantic.  This can refer to both physical movements and emotional expressions. | The character's overanimations made the scene feel cartoonish rather than realistic.  人物的过度动画让场景感觉像卡通而不是现实。| The overanimations of the user interface were distracting and made the app difficult to use. 用户界面的过度动画分散了注意力，使应用程序难以使用。| synonyms:  exaggerations, overacting, hyperactivity | antonyms:  understatement, subtlety, stillness | noun
