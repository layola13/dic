foveate:/ˈfoʊvieɪt/ | v. | 注视；集中注意力于；形成中央凹| To look at or concentrate on something with the fovea, the central part of the retina responsible for sharpest vision.  To form a fovea or central pit. |  The eagle foveated its prey before swooping down.  老鹰在俯冲之前注视着它的猎物。| 近义词: focus, concentrate, fixate | 反义词:  ignore, neglect | 词性: 动词

foveate:/ˈfoʊvieɪt/ | adj. | 中央凹的；具中央凹的| Having a fovea or central pit. |  The foveate structure of the eye allows for sharp vision.  眼睛的中央凹结构可以提供清晰的视力。 | 近义词: central, focal | 反义词: peripheral | 词性: 形容词
