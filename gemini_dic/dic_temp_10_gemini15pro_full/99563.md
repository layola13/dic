interictal:/ˌɪntərˈɪktəl/
adjective
发作间歇的；痫间期的

The patient's interictal EEG showed no epileptiform activity. 病人的发作间歇期脑电图没有显示癫痫样放电。
Synonyms:  between seizures, between attacks
Antonyms: ictal
