disanchor:/dɪsˈæŋkər/
verb
脱离锚；使解锚；使不再固定
To release from an anchor; to loosen from a fixed position.
The ship was disanchored by the storm.  暴风雨使船脱离了锚。
The strong winds disanchored the boat.  强风使船漂走了。
近义词: detach, unmoor, loose, free
反义词: anchor, moor, secure, fasten
词性: v.

disanchor:/dɪsˈæŋkər/
verb
使失去稳定；使不安定
To deprive of stability or security; unsettle.
The news disanchored her from her usual calm. 这消息让她失去了往日的平静。
The sudden change disanchored him emotionally. 突如其来的变化让他情绪不稳定。
近义词: unsettle, disturb, disorient, destabilize
反义词: stabilize, secure, ground, anchor
词性: v.
