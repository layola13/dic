orthostade: /ˈɔːrθəˌsteɪd/ | n. | 正投影图；正射投影 | A drawing or diagram made by orthographic projection. | Example 1: The architect presented an orthostade of the building's facade. 建筑师展示了建筑物正面的正投影图。

Example 2: The orthostade clearly showed the dimensions of the object. 正投影图清晰地显示了物体的尺寸。 | Synonym: orthographic projection, elevation, plan | Antonym: perspective drawing | noun
