fainty:/ˈfeɪnti/| adj.|微弱的，昏厥的，虚弱的|Feeling weak and dizzy, likely to faint.  Characterized by a lack of strength or intensity.|She felt faint after standing in the sun for too long.  她在太阳下站得太久，感到头晕。|Near synonyms: weak, dizzy, lightheaded, feeble|Opposite: strong, robust, vigorous|adjective

faintly:/ˈfeɪntli/| adv.|微弱地，模糊地，隐约地|In a faint manner; weakly; indistinctly.|She smiled faintly. 她微微一笑。|Near synonyms: weakly, slightly, dimly, vaguely|Opposite: strongly, clearly, distinctly|adverb
