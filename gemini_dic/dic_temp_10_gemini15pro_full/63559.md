enviably:/ˈɛnviəbli/| adv.|令人羡慕地，值得嫉妒地|In a manner that arouses envy; in a way that is desirable and enviable.|She lives an enviably glamorous life. 她过着令人羡慕的迷人生活。|近义词：desirably, admirably, fortunately|反义词：undesirably, unfortunately|词性:副词
enviable:/ˈɛnviəbl/| adj.|令人羡慕的，值得嫉妒的|Worthy of envy; desirable.|She has an enviable job. 她有一份令人羡慕的工作。|近义词：desirable, admirable, coveted|反义词：undesirable, undesirable|词性:形容词

词源:  From envy + -able.  Ultimately from Latin invidere "to look askance at, regard with ill-will."
