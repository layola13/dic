brotherly:/ˈbrʌðərli/| adj.|兄弟般的，亲切的|Characteristic of or befitting a brother; kind and affectionate.|He gave me a brotherly hug. 他给了我一个兄弟般的拥抱。|近义词：fraternal, kind, affectionate|反义词：unbrotherly, hostile, unkind|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *brotherlich, equivalent to brother +‎ -ly.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *brōþer, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰréh₂tēr.
