anaerobia:/ˌænəˈroʊbiə/ | n. |厌氧菌；厌氧生物|An organism, especially a bacterium, that does not require free oxygen to live.|Example:  Anaerobia thrive in environments lacking oxygen. 厌氧菌在缺氧环境中 thrive。

Example: Certain anaerobia can cause infections in humans.  某些厌氧菌会导致人体感染。 |Synonym: anaerobe |Antonym: aerobe |noun
