oralists:/ˈɔːrəlɪsts/ | n. | 口述史学家；口述传统拥护者 |  A person who uses or advocates oral methods of instruction or preservation of history, especially one who uses oral interviews to gather historical information. | Example 1: The oralists interviewed many survivors of the war to document their experiences. 口述史学家采访了许多战争幸存者，记录了他们的经历。

Example 2:  Many oralists believe that oral traditions are a valuable source of historical information. 许多口述史学家认为口述传统是宝贵的历史信息来源。 | 近义词: storytellers, narrators, historians | 反义词: scribes, writers | 词性: noun
