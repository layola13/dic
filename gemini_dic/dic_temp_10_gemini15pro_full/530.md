abominated: /əˈbɒmɪneɪtɪd/
verb
憎恶的，厌恶的
past participle of abominate, meaning to feel hatred or disgust for something.
He abominated cruelty to animals. 他憎恶虐待动物。
She abominated the idea of leaving her family. 她厌恶离开家人的想法。
近义词: detested, loathed, abhorred, despised
反义词: adored, cherished, loved, revered
词性: verb (past participle)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin abominari "to deprecate as an ill omen," from ab- "away" + ominari "to forebode, prophesy," related to omen.  The original sense was thus "to turn away from as from something ominous."
