/ɪkˈspektɪd/ /ɛkˈspɛktɪd/ | adj. | 预期的，预料的 | Something that is expected is thought to be going to happen or arrive. |  The expected announcement did not come. 预计的公告没有发布。| anticipated, foreseen, predicted | unexpected, unforeseen | adjective

/ɪkˈspektɪd/ /ɛkˈspɛktɪd/ | n. | 预期的事物 | Something expected; an expectation. | The results were not quite as expected. 结果与预期不太一样。| anticipation, prediction | surprise, shock | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *expectatus, past participle of expectare (“to look out for, await, expect”), from ex- (“out”) + spectare (“to look”).
