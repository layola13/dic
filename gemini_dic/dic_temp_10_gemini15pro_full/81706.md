greener:/ˈɡriːnər/| adj.|更绿的，更环保的|Comparative form of "green".  Means having more of the quality of green, either in color or environmentally.|The grass is greener on the other side.  这山望着那山高。|近义词：more eco-friendly, more sustainable|反义词：browner, less green|词性: 形容词
