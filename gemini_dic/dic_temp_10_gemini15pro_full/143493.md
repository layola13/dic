outglitter:/ˌaʊtˈɡlɪtər/
verb
胜过…的光辉；比…更闪耀
to shine more brightly than; surpass in brilliance or glitter.
Her diamond necklace outglittered all the others at the gala. 她在晚会上的钻石项链比所有其他项链都闪耀。
Synonyms: outshine, eclipse, overshadow
Antonyms:  be outshone, be eclipsed
词性:动词

outglitter:/ˌaʊtˈɡlɪtər/
verb
胜过…的华丽；比…更炫目
to surpass in showiness or ostentation.
The new casino outglittered all the other buildings on the strip. 新赌场比商业街上的所有其他建筑都更炫目。
Synonyms: outdo, surpass, exceed
Antonyms: be outdone, be surpassed
词性:动词
