adorns:/əˈdɔːrnz/
verb
装饰，装扮
Makes something more beautiful or attractive.
The queen's gown was adorned with jewels. 女王的礼服上饰有珠宝。
Flowers adorned the table. 桌子上装饰着鲜花。
decorate, embellish, ornament
strip, bare
v.
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French adorner, from a- (ad-) + orner "to adorn," from Latin ornare "to equip, adorn, furnish," related to ordo "order, rank."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
She adorns her hair with flowers. = 她用鲜花装饰她的头发。
He adorns his house with paintings. = 他用画装饰他的房子。

一般过去时:
They adorned the Christmas tree with ornaments. = 他们用饰品装饰了圣诞树。
She adorned herself with jewelry for the party. = 她为派对戴上了珠宝。

现在完成时:
The artist has adorned the walls with murals. = 艺术家在墙上装饰了壁画。
They have adorned the cake with fresh fruit. = 他们在蛋糕上装饰了新鲜水果。

过去完成时:
The bride had adorned her veil with lace. = 新娘用蕾丝装饰了她的面纱。
He had adorned the room with flowers before she arrived. = 在她到达之前，他已经用鲜花装饰了房间。

将来时:
We will adorn the hall with balloons for the celebration. = 我们将用气球装饰大厅以庆祝。
She will adorn her dress with a beautiful brooch. = 她将在她的连衣裙上装饰一枚漂亮的胸针。


被动语态:
The Christmas tree was adorned with lights and ornaments. = 圣诞树上装饰着彩灯和饰品。
The walls were adorned with tapestries. = 墙上装饰着挂毯。


PHRASE->
