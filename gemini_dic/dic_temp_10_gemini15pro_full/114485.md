malacolite:/ˈmæləˌklaɪt/| n. |透辉石，绿透辉石|A variety of diopside, a mineral of the pyroxene group, typically pale green to dark green in color and sometimes used as a gemstone. |例句：The malacolite displayed a beautiful deep green hue. 这块透辉石展现出美丽的深绿色色调。

例句：Malacolite is sometimes confused with emerald due to its similar color. 透辉石有时因其相似的颜色而与祖母绿混淆。|近义词：diopside, pyroxene|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
