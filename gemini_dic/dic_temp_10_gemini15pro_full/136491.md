nonriparian:/ˌnɒnraɪˈpɛəriən/
adjective
非河岸的，不依河岸的
Not situated on the banks of a river or other body of water; not relating to or dependent on riparian rights.
The nonriparian vegetation was sparse and dry due to lack of water.  非河岸植被由于缺水而稀疏干燥。
The developer planned to build on the nonriparian land, sparking controversy. 开发商计划在非河岸土地上建造房屋，引发了争议。
Synonyms: upland, inland
Antonyms: riparian
词性: adjective
