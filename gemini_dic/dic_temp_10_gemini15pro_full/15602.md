bailee:/beɪˈliː/
n.
受托人；保管人
A person or party to whom goods are delivered for a purpose, such as custody or repair, without transfer of ownership.
The bailee is responsible for the safekeeping of the property. 受托人有保管财产的责任。

The museum is the bailee of the artifacts.  博物馆是这些文物的受托人。

custodian, trustee, consignee
bailor
noun
