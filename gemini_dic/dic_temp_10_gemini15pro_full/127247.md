nautically:/ˈnɔːtɪkli/ | adv. | 航海地，海上的 | In a nautical manner; with respect to navigation or seamen. |  The ship was nautically sound. 这艘船在航海方面是可靠的。|  The captain dressed nautically. 船长穿着航海服。 | 近义词:maritime, marine | 反义词:landward | 词性:副词

词源:
From nautical + -ly. Nautical comes from Late Latin nauticus, from Latin nauta (“sailor”), from Ancient Greek ναύτης (naútēs, “sailor”), from ναῦς (naûs, “ship”).
