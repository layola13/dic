misspends:/ˌmɪsˈspɛndz/
verb
浪费，滥用，虚度
To spend (time or money) unwisely or wastefully.
He misspent his youth playing video games. 他把青春都浪费在玩电子游戏上了。
The government misspent millions on the project. 政府在这个项目上浪费了数百万美元。
squander, waste, fritter away
use well, manage effectively
v.
