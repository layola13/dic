overstockings:/ˈoʊvərˌstɑːkɪŋz/
n.
长筒袜（尤指女用）
Stockings that reach above the knee or higher.
She wore a pair of sheer overstockings with her evening gown. 她穿着晚礼服，搭配了一双透明的长筒袜。
近义词：thigh-highs, stockings
反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially socks or ankle socks if contrasting length)
词性: noun
