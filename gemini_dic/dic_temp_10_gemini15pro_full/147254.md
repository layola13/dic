overmerited: /ˌoʊvərˈmɛrɪtɪd/
adjective
过分奖赏的，不应得的
Having received more reward or recognition than is deserved.
He received an overmerited promotion due to his connections, not his skills. 他因为关系而不是技能得到了一个不应得的晋升。
The overmerited praise made him uncomfortable. 过分的赞扬让他感到不安。
Synonyms: undeserved, unearned, unwarranted
Antonyms: deserved, earned, merited
词性: 形容词
