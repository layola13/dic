gingered:/ˈdʒɪndʒərd/
adjective
姜味的；加了姜的；(脸色)苍白的；(头发)姜黄色的
Flavored or colored with ginger; having pale or ginger-colored hair.
The cake was gingery and delicious.  这蛋糕有姜味，非常好吃。
The old man had a gingery complexion. 那位老人面色苍白。
She had a mass of gingery hair. 她有一头浓密的姜黄色头发。
近义词:ginger, ginger-flavored, ginger-colored, pale, reddish-yellow
反义词:(For complexion)ruddy, flushed
