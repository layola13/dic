ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *mæþ, from Proto-Germanic *maiþō (“measure, size”), from Proto-Indo-European *mey- (“to measure, to cut, to allot”). Cognate with Old Frisian meth (“measure, quantity”), Middle Dutch mate (“measure, quantity”), Old High German maza (“measure, size”), German Maß (“measure”), Gothic 𐌼𐌴𐌻 (mēl, “hour”). More at mete.
