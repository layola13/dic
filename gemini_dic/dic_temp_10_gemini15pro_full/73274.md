n.
前梁，车辕横木
The forebeam of the wagon was cracked. 马车的车辕横木裂开了。
The heavy forebeam provided stability for the carriage.  厚重的车辕横木为马车提供了稳定性。
fore part of a beam; specifically : the beam in a wagon or carriage to which the horses are attached

forebeam:/ˈfɔːrbiːm/
n.
(织机)经轴前梁
The forebeam of the loom held the warp threads taut. 织机的经轴前梁拉紧了经线。
Adjusting the forebeam can alter the tension of the warp. 调整经轴前梁可以改变经线的张力。
The beam at the front of a loom over which the warp threads pass
近义词: breast beam, warp beam
反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially back beam or cloth beam depending on context)
词性: noun


词源：fore- + beam.  "Fore-" signifies "front" or "anterior."  "Beam" refers to a long, sturdy piece of timber or metal.
