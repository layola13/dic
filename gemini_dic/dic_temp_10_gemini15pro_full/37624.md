communist:/ˈkɒmjənɪst/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmjənɪst/ (US)| n. adj.|共产主义者；共产党员；共产主义的|A person who supports or believes in the principles of communism; relating to or characteristic of communism.|例句：He was a committed communist. 他是一位坚定的共产主义者。|近义词：Marxist, socialist, leftist, radical|反义词：capitalist, conservative, right-winger|词性:名词/形容词

communist:/ˈkɒmjənɪst/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmjənɪst/ (US)| adj.|共产主义的|Relating to or characteristic of communism.|例句：The communist party held a congress. 共产党召开了一次代表大会。|近义词：Marxist, socialist|反义词：capitalist|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *communiste*, from *commune* ("commune") + *-iste* (suffix forming agent nouns and adjectives).  *Commune* ultimately derives from Late Latin *communia* (“community”),  from Latin *communis* (“common”).
