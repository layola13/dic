chit-chat:/ˈtʃɪt tʃæt/
n.|闲聊，聊天|Light informal conversation.|We spent the afternoon chit-chatting about nothing in particular. 我们一下午都在闲聊些无关紧要的事情。
|Synonyms: small talk, gossip, chatter|Antonyms: debate, discussion, argument
|noun

chit-chat:/ˈtʃɪt tʃæt/
vi.|闲聊，聊天|To engage in light informal conversation.|They were chit-chatting by the water cooler. 他们在饮水机旁闲聊。
|Synonyms: gab, gossip, chatter|Antonyms: debate, discuss, argue
|verb
