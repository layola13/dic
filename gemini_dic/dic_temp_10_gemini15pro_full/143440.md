outflowing:/ˈaʊtfloʊɪŋ/
adjective
流出的，外流的
Flowing out from a source or container.
例句：The outflowing river carried debris downstream. 流出的河流将碎屑带到下游。

例句：The outflowing tide revealed hidden rocks. 退潮露出了隐藏的岩石。

近义词：exiting, emanating, discharging, issuing
反义词：inflowing, incoming
词性：adjective
