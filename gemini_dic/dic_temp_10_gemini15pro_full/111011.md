ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian lira, from Latin libra (“pound”), from Proto-Italic *librā (“balance, scales”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)lei- (“to bend, incline”).  Ultimately related to English lean. 来自意大利语lira，源自拉丁语libra（“磅”），源自原始意大利语*librā（“平衡，秤”），源自原始印欧语*(s)lei-（“弯曲，倾斜”）。最终与英语lean有关。
