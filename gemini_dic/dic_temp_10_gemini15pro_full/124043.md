morpheming: /ˈmɔːrfiːmɪŋ/ | n. | 词素分析; 形态分析 | The process of breaking down words into their constituent morphemes.  |  Linguists use morpheming to analyze the structure of words. 语言学家使用词素分析来分析单词的结构。 | Example: Morpheming the word "unbreakable" reveals the morphemes "un-," "break," and "-able."  对单词"unbreakable"进行词素分析揭示了词素"un-," "break," 和 "-able." | synonyms: morphological analysis, decomposition | antonyms: synthesis, compounding | noun
