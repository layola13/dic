concertgoing:/ˈkɑːnsərtˌɡoʊɪŋ/ /ˈkɒnsətˌɡəʊɪŋ/
| adj. n.| 参加音乐会的，常去听音乐会的；去听音乐会这件事|Attending concerts; the activity of attending concerts|例句：She's a regular concertgoing enthusiast. 她是一位经常参加音乐会的爱好者。
|例句：Concertgoing is one of her favorite pastimes. 去听音乐会是她最喜欢的消遣之一。
|近义词：concert-loving, music-loving, concert attendee |反义词:  non-concertgoing, stay-at-home|
词性: 形容词, 名词


Etymology->
concert + going.  "Concert" comes from the Italian "concerto," ultimately from Latin "concertare" meaning "to contend, strive together." "Going" is the present participle of the verb "go."
