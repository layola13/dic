belts:/bɛlts/| n.|腰带，传送带|A strip of flexible material, typically leather, used to fasten or secure something around the waist. Also, a continuous band of material used for transmitting power or conveying objects.|He tightened his belt. 他勒紧了腰带。|The conveyor belt carries the products to the packaging area.  传送带将产品运送到包装区。|synonyms: straps, bands, sashes|antonyms: looseness|noun
