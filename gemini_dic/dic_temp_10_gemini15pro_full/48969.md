The discussion degenerated into a shouting match. 讨论演变成了互相叫喊。| 近义词: deteriorate, decline, worsen | 反义词: improve, enhance, progress |词性: 动词

degenerate:/dɪˈdʒɛnərət/ | n. | 堕落的人 | A person who has declined, especially morally. |  Some people considered him a degenerate because of his lifestyle.  有些人因为他生活方式糜烂而认为他是一个堕落的人。
