banshee:/ˈbænʃiː/
noun
女妖，报丧女妖
A female spirit in Irish folklore whose wailing warns of an impending death in a family.
The banshee's cries echoed through the valley. 女妖的哭声回荡在山谷中。
synonyms: spirit, ghost, phantom, specter
antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a supernatural entity.  One could consider "living being" or "mortal" as a contrasting concept.)
