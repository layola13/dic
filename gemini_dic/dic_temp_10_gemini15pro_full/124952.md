muffled:/ˈmʌfəld/ (US), /ˈmʌfld/ (UK)| adj.|低沉的，压抑的，模糊不清的|made quieter or less clear|例句：Her voice was muffled by the thick scarf. 她的声音被厚厚的围巾压低了。

例句：The muffled sound of drums came from the distance. 远处传来低沉的鼓声。|近义词：muted, subdued, hushed, indistinct|反义词：clear, loud, distinct|词性:形容词

muffle:/ˈmʌfəl/ (US), /ˈmʌfl/ (UK)| vt.|使低沉，压抑，裹住|to make a sound quieter or less clear|例句：She muffled her cough with her hand. 她用手捂住嘴咳嗽。

例句：The heavy curtains muffled the street noise. 厚重的窗帘减弱了街道的噪音。|近义词：mute, subdue, hush, deaden|反义词：amplify, increase|词性:动词
