cartel: /kɑːrˈtɛl/ | n. | 卡特尔；同业联盟；垄断联盟 | An association of manufacturers or suppliers with the purpose of maintaining prices at a high level and restricting competition. |  Example 1: The drug cartel controlled the flow of cocaine into the country.  这个贩毒集团控制着可卡因流入该国。

Example 2: The OPEC cartel is a group of oil-producing nations that often influences global oil prices.  欧佩克是一个石油生产国集团，经常影响全球油价。 | Synonyms: syndicate, combine, trust, monopoly | Antonyms: competition, free market | noun
