ancient:/ˈeɪnʃənt/| adj.|古老的，古代的|Belonging to the very distant past and no longer in existence.|例句：Ancient civilizations fascinate me. 古代文明使我着迷。|例句：They discovered an ancient city buried beneath the sand. 他们在沙子下面发现了一座古城。|近义词：old, antique, archaic, prehistoric|反义词：modern, contemporary, recent|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *auncient*, *ancien*, from Old French *ancien* (Modern French *ancien*), from Latin *antiquus* (“old, aged, former, ancient”), from *ante* (“before”).
