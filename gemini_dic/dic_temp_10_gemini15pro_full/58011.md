drive:/draɪv/ | v. | 驾驶，驱动，迫使 | To operate and control the movement of a vehicle or animal; to force someone or something to move in a particular direction; to motivate or urge someone. | He drives a truck. 他驾驶一辆卡车。| She drives her children to school every morning. 她每天早上开车送孩子上学。| 近义词: steer, operate, propel, compel | 反义词: park, stop | 词性: verb
词源: From Old English *drīfan (attested only in past participle gedrifen), "to push, shove, drive," from Proto-Germanic *dreibaną ("to drive, push").
