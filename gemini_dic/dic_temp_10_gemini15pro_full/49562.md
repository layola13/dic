demedicated:/diːˈmɛdɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
停止用药的；未服药的
No longer taking medication; not medicated.
The patient was demedicated after showing signs of improvement.  这位病人在出现好转迹象后停止了用药。
He entered a demedicated state after leaving the hospital. 他出院后就处于未服药的状态。
近义词:drug-free, off medication, unmedicated
反义词:medicated, on medication
词性:adjective
