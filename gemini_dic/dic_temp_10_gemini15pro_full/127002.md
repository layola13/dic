nasalizes:/ˈneɪzəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使鼻音化; 用鼻音发音
To make a sound nasal; to speak through the nose.
She nasalizes her vowels when she sings. 她唱歌时会用鼻音发音。
近义词: nasalize
反义词: (No clear single antonym; perhaps "oralize")
词性: verb

nasalizes:/ˈneɪzəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使鼻音化; 用鼻音发音
To pronounce a sound with air passing through the nose, either partly or completely.
The singer nasalizes certain notes for stylistic effect.  这位歌手为了达到风格效果，用鼻音唱某些音符。
近义词: nasalize
反义词: (No clear single antonym)
词性: verb
