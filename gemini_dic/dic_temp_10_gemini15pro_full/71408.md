flagstones:/ˈflæɡˌstoʊnz/
n.
石板，铺路石
Flat stones, typically rectangular or square, used for paving.
The patio was paved with large flagstones. 露台是用大块石板铺成的。

近义词: paving stones, slabs
反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑 gravel 碎石, asphalt 沥青 等不同铺路材料)
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "flag" (a flat stone) + "stones".  The "flag" part comes from Old Norse "flaga" meaning "slab of stone".
