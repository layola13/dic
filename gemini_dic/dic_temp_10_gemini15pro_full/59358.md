earned: /ɜːrnd/ /ɝːnd/ | v. | 挣得，赢得，获得 | Past participle of "earn".  Means to receive something (usually money or a reward) in return for work or effort. |  He earned a promotion after working hard all year. 他努力工作了一整年，最终获得了晋升。| synonyms: gained, obtained, acquired, achieved, attained | antonyms: lost, forfeited, squandered | verb

hard-earned:  辛苦挣来的  She deserved the award after years of hard-earned success. 多年辛苦的成功后，她理应获得这个奖项。
well-earned:  应得的；名副其实的  After a long day's work, a cold beer is well-earned.  一天辛苦工作之后，喝杯冰啤酒是应得的。
earned income:  劳动所得  His earned income increased significantly after he got promoted. 他升职后，劳动所得显著增加。
earned run average (ERA) (棒球):  自责分率  The pitcher had an impressive earned run average. 投手的自责分率令人印象深刻。
earned value (项目管理):  挣值  The project manager tracked the earned value to assess the project's progress. 项目经理跟踪挣值来评估项目进度.
