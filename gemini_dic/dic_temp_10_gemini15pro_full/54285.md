disguises:/dɪsˈɡaɪzɪz/
| n. |伪装，掩饰|Something that hides the usual appearance of something or someone, making it difficult to recognize. |He wore a disguise to avoid being recognized. 他乔装打扮以避免被人认出。|近义词：camouflages, masks, covers, cloaks |反义词：reveals, exposes|Noun
disguise:/dɪsˈɡaɪz/
| vt. |伪装，掩饰|To give a different appearance to something or someone, especially in order to hide its true form. |She disguised her voice so that no one would recognize her. 她伪装了她的声音，这样就没有人能认出她了。|近义词：camouflage, mask, cover, cloak|反义词：reveal, expose|Verb
