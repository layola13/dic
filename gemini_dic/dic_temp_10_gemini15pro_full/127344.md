nazis:/ˈnɑːtsiːz/
noun

The Nazis persecuted Jews, Roma, and other minorities. 纳粹迫害犹太人、罗姆人和其他少数民族。
The Nazis' rise to power was a dark chapter in history. 纳粹上台是历史上黑暗的一章。
synonyms: fascists, Nazis
antonyms: resistance fighters, anti-fascists
