morosity:/məˈrɒsəti/ (US) /məˈrɒsɪti/ (UK)| n. | 闷闷不乐，郁闷，阴郁 | A state of being sullen, gloomy, or ill-humored. |  He was known for his morosity and pessimistic outlook. 他以郁闷和悲观的 outlook 而闻名。

The rainy weather only added to his morosity.  阴雨天气更加重了他的郁闷。

近义词：sullenness, gloominess, dejection, melancholy, glumness | 反义词：cheerfulness, happiness, optimism, joviality | 词性: noun

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin *morositas, from Latin morosus ‘peevish’ (see morose).
