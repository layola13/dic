complementary-angles:/ˌkɒmplɪˈmentəri ˈæŋɡlz/ |n.| 互补角 |Two angles are complementary if the sum of their measures is 90 degrees. |Example: The two angles of 30° and 60° are complementary angles. 这两个30°和60°的角是互补角。| Example: In a right-angled triangle, the two non-right angles are complementary. 在直角三角形中，两个非直角是互补角。|Synonym: supplementary angles (when referring to angles adding up to 180°)| Antonym: N/A (There isn't a direct opposite of complementary angles.) | noun
