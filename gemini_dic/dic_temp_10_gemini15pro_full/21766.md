boatsman:/ˈboʊtsmən/ | n. | 船夫；水手 | A person who operates or manages a boat, especially a small one. | The boatsman ferried us across the river. 船夫把我们渡过了河。|  The experienced boatsman navigated the treacherous rapids with skill.  经验丰富的船夫熟练地驾驶着船只 melewati 了危险的急流。 | 近义词: boatman, ferryman, oarsman, waterman | 反义词: passenger | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *bātsmann (“boatman”), from bāt (“boat”) + mann (“man”). Cognate with Dutch bootsman, German Bootsmann.
