incension:/ɪnˈsɛnʃən/| n.|上升，升天，登基|The act of rising or ascending; elevation.  A ceremony or rite of installation in an office or position, especially a high one.|The book described the king's incention to the throne. 这本书描述了国王的登基仪式。|近义词：ascension, ascent, rise, elevation|反义词：descent, fall, decline|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *incensiōn-, incensiō, from Latin incendō (“to kindle, set fire to, inflame”), from in- + candō (“to glow, shine”).
