exquisiteness:/ɪkˈskwɪzɪtnəs/| n. |精致，优美，精妙|The exquisiteness of the carving was breathtaking.  雕刻的精美令人叹为观止。|近义词：delicacy, refinement, elegance|反义词：coarseness, crudeness, vulgarity|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From exquisite + -ness.  Exquisite from Late Middle English, from Latin exquisitus, past participle of exquirere “to search out,” from ex- “out” + quaerere “to seek.”
