luge:/luːʒ/| n. vi.|雪橇，平底雪橇；|A racing sled for one or two people, ridden in a supine position with the feet first. |例句：He competed in the luge at the Winter Olympics. 他参加了冬奥会的雪橇比赛。|近义词：sled, toboggan|反义词: None|词性:noun, verb


luge:/luːʒ/| vi.|滑行，乘坐雪橇滑行|To slide down a track on a luge. |例句：She loves to luge down the icy slopes. 她喜欢坐着雪橇滑下冰冷的斜坡。|近义词：sled, toboggan|反义词:climb, ascend|词性:verb

词源: French, from Franco-Provençal *luge, from Vulgar Latin *slēdica, alteration of Latin slēdō, sled.
