disarticulation:/dɪsˌɑːrˌtɪkjʊˈleɪʃən/
n.|脱臼，关节离断|The surgical disarticulation of a limb.  肢体的外科关节离断。|例句：The surgeon performed a disarticulation of the shoulder.  外科医生进行了肩关节离断手术。

例句：Disarticulation of the hip joint is a complex procedure. 髋关节离断是一个复杂的手术。

近义词：amputation, separation
反义词：articulation, connection
词性: noun

disarticulate:/dɪsɑːrˈtɪkjʊleɪt/
v.|使脱臼，使关节分离；不连贯地说|To disarticulate a bone from its socket. 使骨头从关节窝中脱臼。|例句：The fall disarticulated his shoulder. 摔倒使他的肩膀脱臼了。

例句：He disarticulated his arguments, making them difficult to follow. 他表达观点时断断续续，使人难以理解。

近义词：disconnect, separate, disjoin
反义词：connect, join, articulate
词性: verb


词源:  From Late Latin *disarticulātus, past participle of disarticulāre (“to disjoin”), from dis- (“apart, asunder”) + articulātus, past participle of articulāre (“to articulate”). Equivalent to dis- +‎ articulate.
