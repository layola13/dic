nonstop:/ˈnɑːnstɑːp/ (US) /ˈnɒnstɒp/ (UK)| adj. adv.|不停的，不断的|Without stopping; continuously.|The band played nonstop for three hours. 乐队连续演奏了三个小时。|近义词：continuous, incessant, constant|反义词：intermittent, occasional|词性:形容词/副词
nonstop:/ˈnɑːnstɑːp/ (US) /ˈnɒnstɒp/ (UK)| n.|直达航班/车次|A flight or journey that does not stop at any intermediate points.|We took a nonstop flight from New York to London. 我们乘坐直达航班从纽约飞往伦敦。|近义词：direct|反义词：connecting|词性:名词
