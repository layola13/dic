murre:/ˈmɜːr/ (US), /mɜː/ (UK)| n.|海雀，一种海鸟|A seabird of northern and Arctic waters, typically with a black head and body and white underparts.  It breeds in colonies on cliffs. |例句：The cliffs were teeming with murres.  悬崖上挤满了海雀。

例句：A lone murre bobbed on the waves. 一只孤独的海雀在波浪上漂浮。|近义词：guillemot, auk|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
etymology->From Old Norse *murr.
