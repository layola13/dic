brachypinacoid:/brækɪˈpɪnəkɔɪd/| n. |短柱面|A crystal form consisting of two parallel faces symmetrically placed about a plane of symmetry and perpendicular to a pinacoid.|The brachypinacoid is a defining characteristic of some monoclinic crystals.|近义词:pinacoid|反义词:N/A|词性:noun


Etymology:  From Greek *brakhys* "short" + *pinax* "board, tablet" + *-oid* "like".
