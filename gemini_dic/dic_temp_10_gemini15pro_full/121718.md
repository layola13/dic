misprize:/mɪsˈpraɪz/
verb
轻视，蔑视；低估；看不起
to undervalue or underestimate someone or something; to regard with contempt or disdain.
He misprized her kindness, mistaking it for weakness. 他误解了她的好意，错把它当成了软弱。
She felt misprized by her colleagues who ignored her suggestions. 她觉得被同事们轻视了，因为他们无视她的建议。
synonyms: underestimate, undervalue, disdain, scorn, despise, belittle
antonyms: appreciate, value, esteem, admire, respect
词性: 动词
