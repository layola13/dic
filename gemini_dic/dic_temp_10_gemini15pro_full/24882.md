brucellosis:/bruːsəˈloʊsɪs/| n. |布鲁氏菌病|An infectious bacterial disease caught from infected cattle, pigs, goats or sheep, causing fever, sweating, weakness, pains and aches.  It can be transmitted to humans through consumption of unpasteurized milk or undercooked meat from infected animals, or through direct contact with their secretions.|  Brucellosis can be a debilitating disease if left untreated. 布鲁氏菌病如果不及时治疗可能会使人衰弱。

Brucellosis is a significant problem for farmers in developing countries. 布鲁氏菌病是发展中国家农民面临的一个重大问题。|近义词：Malta fever, Mediterranean fever, undulant fever|反义词: (No direct antonym for a disease, could consider "health" in a broad sense) health|词性:noun
