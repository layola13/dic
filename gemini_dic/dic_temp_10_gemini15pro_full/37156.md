come to one's senses:/kʌm tə wʌnz ˈsɛnsɪz/
|phrasal verb|
|苏醒过来；恢复理智；醒悟|
|To regain one's ability to think and behave reasonably after a period of confusion or irrationality.|
|He finally came to his senses and realized he was making a huge mistake.|他终于醒悟过来，意识到自己犯了一个大错。

|After her initial panic, she came to her senses and called for help.|她最初的恐慌过后，恢复了理智并打电话求助。

|近义词： regain consciousness, come around, sober up, see reason, wise up|
|反义词： lose one's mind, go crazy, act irrationally|
|词性：动词短语|
