glyoxime: /ɡlaɪˈɒksɪm/ | n. |乙二肟|An organic compound, specifically a diketone dioxime, often used in analytical chemistry for the detection and quantification of nickel. |Nickel glyoxime is a bright red precipitate. 镍乙二肟是一种鲜红色的沉淀物。| Glyoxime reacts with nickel ions to form a characteristic red precipitate. 乙二肟与镍离子反应形成特征性的红色沉淀。 | synonym: diketone dioxime | antonym: N/A | noun
