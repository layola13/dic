inductric:/ɪnˈdʌktrɪk/ | adj. | 感应的；电感的 | Relating to inductance or induction, especially electromagnetic induction. | The inductric effect of the coil caused a surge in voltage. 线圈的电感效应导致电压激增。 | 近义词: inductive | 反义词: non-inductive | 词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *inductus*, past participle of *inducere* "to lead in," from *in-* "in" + *ducere* "to lead."  The suffix *-ic* forms adjectives.
