anthropophagite:/ˌænθrəˈpɒfədʒaɪt/
n.
食人者，食人族
A person who eats human flesh; a cannibal.
The isolated tribe was rumored to be anthropophagites, shunning all contact with the outside world.  这个与世隔绝的部落被传言是食人族，他们避开与外界的任何接触。
The explorers feared the anthropophagites who were said to inhabit the island. 探险家们害怕据说居住在这个岛上的食人族。
近义词:cannibal, man-eater
反义词:(None -  difficult to have a true opposite of cannibal)
词性:noun

anthropophagous:/ˌænθrəˈpɒfəɡəs/
adj.
食人的
Practicing cannibalism; relating to cannibalism.
Anthropophagous rituals were practiced by some ancient tribes.  一些古代部落实行食人仪式。
Evidence suggests that anthropophagous behavior existed in this region centuries ago. 有证据表明，几个世纪以前，这个地区存在食人行为。
近义词:cannibalistic
反义词:(None - difficult to have a true opposite of cannibalistic)
词性:adjective

词源: From Greek *anthropophagos*, from *anthrōpos* "human being" + *phagein* "to eat."
