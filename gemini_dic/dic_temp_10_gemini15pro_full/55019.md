disputing:/dɪˈspjuːtɪŋ/
verb
争论；辩论；质疑
The act of arguing or debating about something.
They are disputing the ownership of the land. 他们在争论这块土地的所有权。

The two countries are disputing the border. 这两个国家正在争论边界问题。

arguing, debating, contesting, questioning, challenging
agreeing, accepting, conceding

verb
(present participle of dispute)
The lawyers are disputing the evidence. 律师们正在对证据提出异议。

Scientists are disputing the cause of global warming. 科学家们正在争论全球变暖的原因。


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *disputer* (Modern French *disputer*), from Latin *disputare* "to reckon, discuss, examine," from *dis-* "apart, thoroughly" + *putare* "to think, reckon, believe, suppose."
