creatrix:/kriˈeɪtrɪks/
n.
女创造者，女性创始人
A female creator; a woman who creates or founds something.
例句：She is the creatrix of this innovative design. 她是这项创新设计的创始人。

例句：The company recognizes her as the creatrix of its most successful product. 公司认为她是最成功产品的创始人。

近义词：creator, founder, originator, maker, author
反义词：destroyer

词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *creatrix*, feminine form of *creator*, from *creare* "to create."  The -trix suffix is a feminine agentive suffix.
