harriers:/ˈhæriərz/
n.
猎兔犬，鹞(harrier的复数)；掠夺者
A slender long-legged hawk of the genus Circus, that flies low over fields and marshes hunting small mammals and birds.
The harriers circled overhead, searching for prey. 猎兔犬在头顶盘旋，寻找猎物。

harrier:/ˈhæriər/
n.
猎兔犬，鹞；掠夺者
A slender long-legged hawk of the genus Circus, that flies low over fields and marshes hunting small mammals and birds.  A person or group that harasses or causes trouble for another.
The farmer used harriers to control the rabbit population. 农民用猎兔犬来控制兔子的数量。
The company was targeted by corporate harriers. 这家公司成为企业掠夺者的目标。

近义词:hawk, raptor, predator
反义词:prey
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hariere, from Anglo-Norman harier, from Old French harier (“to harry, to set a dog upon”), of Germanic origin.
