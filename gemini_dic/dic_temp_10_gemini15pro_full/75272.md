ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *front, *fruns, from Vulgar Latin *frontem, accusative singular of Latin frōns (“forehead, front”), from Proto-Italic *frōns, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰr̥Hs-nós (“eyebrow, forehead”). Cognate with Old Irish brú (“eyebrow”), Welsh bryn (“hill”).
