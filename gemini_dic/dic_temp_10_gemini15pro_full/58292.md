ETYMOLOGY->
From German *drusig*, from *Druse* (“druse, geode”), borrowed from French *druse*, from German *Druse*. Ultimately from Ancient Greek *δρύος* (*drýos*, “tree, oak”).  The connection likely refers to the resemblance of some crystal formations to tree moss or lichen.
