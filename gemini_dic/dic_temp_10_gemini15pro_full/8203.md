anti-egoist:/ˌæntiˈiːɡoʊɪst/ | n. | 反利己主义者；非利己主义者 | A person who opposes egoism or selfishness; someone who believes in altruism. |  She was known as an anti-egoist, always putting the needs of others before her own. 她以反利己主义者而闻名，总是把别人的需要放在自己的需要之前。

| Example: He consistently demonstrated anti-egoist behavior, donating a significant portion of his income to charity. 他一直表现出反利己主义的行为，将他的大部分收入捐给了慈善机构。 | Synonym: altruist, humanitarian | Antonym: egoist, narcissist | Noun
