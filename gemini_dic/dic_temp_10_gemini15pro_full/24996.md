brutality:/bruːˈtælɪti/
n.
野蛮；残暴；暴行
Savage physical violence; cruel and inhumane treatment.
The brutality of the attack shocked the nation. 这次袭击的残暴震惊了全国。
近义词：savagery, cruelty, barbarity, ferocity
反义词：gentleness, kindness, compassion, humanity
词性:名词

brutal:/ˈbruːtl/
adj.
残忍的；野蛮的；无情的
Savagely violent; cruel and inhumane.
The winter was brutal. 冬天十分严酷。
近义词：cruel, savage, vicious, ruthless
反义词：gentle, kind, compassionate, humane
词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle French brutalité, from Old French, from brutal + -ité -ity.  Ultimately from Late Latin brutalis "of beasts," from Latin brutus "dull, irrational, brutish."
