digitalize:/ˈdɪdʒɪtəlaɪz/ /ˈdɪdʒɪtlaɪz/ | vt. |数字化，数位化|To convert (analog information) to digital form.|例句：Many companies are now digitalizing their archives. 许多公司现在正在将其档案数字化。

例句：The library is digitalizing its collection of old manuscripts. 图书馆正在将其收藏的旧手稿数字化。|近义词：digitize, computerize, automate|反义词: analogize |词性：动词

digitalize:/ˈdɪdʒɪtəlaɪz/ /ˈdɪdʒɪtlaɪz/ | n. |数字化，数位化|The action or process of converting something to digital form.|例句：The digitalization of the banking sector has made transactions faster and more efficient.  银行业的数字化使交易更快、更高效。

例句：The digitalization of medical records has improved patient care.  医疗记录的数字化改善了病人护理。|近义词：digitization, computerization, automation|反义词: - |词性：名词
