ozonize:/ˈoʊzəˌnaɪz/ | v. | 臭氧化，用臭氧处理 | To treat or impregnate with ozone. |  The water is ozonized to kill bacteria.  用臭氧处理水来杀死细菌。 | The air was ozonized to remove odors. 用臭氧净化空气以去除异味。 | synonyms: oxidize, sterilize | antonyms: reduce, deodorize | verb

ozonation:/ˌoʊzəˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 臭氧化作用 | The process of treating or impregnating with ozone. | The ozonation of the water supply improved its quality.  对供水进行臭氧化提高了水质。|  Ozonation is a common method of water purification. 臭氧化是一种常用的水净化方法。 | synonyms: oxidation, sterilization | antonyms: reduction, deodorization | noun
