matanza:/mɑːˈtɑːnsə/ /mæˈtænzə/
n.
屠杀，杀戮
A large-scale slaughter of animals or people.
The matanza was a brutal act of violence.  这场屠杀是一次残暴的暴力行为。
The annual matanza of pigs is a tradition in some villages.  在一些村庄，一年一度的杀猪是一项传统。
slaughter, massacre, carnage, butchery
preservation, protection

词性: 名词
