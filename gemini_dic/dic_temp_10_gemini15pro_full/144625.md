outwish:/ˌaʊtˈwɪʃ/ | v. | 超越…的愿望; 比…更希望; | To wish more intensely than.  She outwished him for the prize. 她比他更渴望得到那个奖品。 | 近义词: outdesire, surpass in wishing | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "yield" in the context of wishing) | 词性: verb

词源:  A combination of "out-" (meaning exceeding, surpassing) and "wish".  It's a relatively modern formation.
