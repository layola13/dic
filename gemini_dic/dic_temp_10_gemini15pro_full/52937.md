dinars:/dɪˈnɑːrz/
noun
第纳尔（货币单位）
The basic monetary unit of several countries, including Algeria, Bahrain, Iraq, Jordan, Kuwait, Libya, Serbia, and Tunisia.
He exchanged his dollars for dinars. 他把美元兑换成了第纳尔。
