determinator:/dɪˈtɜːrmɪneɪtər/ | n. |限定词|A word or phrase that modifies a noun or noun phrase and specifies its reference, such as "the," "a," "this," "that," "my," "your," "some," "many," "each," "every," etc. |The determinator "the" specifies a particular book.|近义词:modifier, specifier |反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider words like "indefinite" as conceptually opposite to the function of a determinator) |词性:noun
