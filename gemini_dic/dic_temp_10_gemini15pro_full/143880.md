outpolitick: /ˌaʊtˈpɑːlətɪk/ | v. | 以更精明的政治手段胜过 | To defeat or outmaneuver someone by using more clever or effective political tactics. |  The young senator outpoliticked his more experienced opponent.  年轻的参议员以更精明的政治手腕击败了他经验更丰富的对手。| 近义词: outmaneuver,outsmart | 反义词: be outmaneuvered, be outsmarted | 词性: 动词

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "to surpass") + "politick" (verb meaning "to engage in political activity").  "Out-" 前缀表示“超越”，“politick” 动词意为“从事政治活动”。
