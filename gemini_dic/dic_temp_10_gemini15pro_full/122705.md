moguled:/ˈmoʊɡəld/ | v. |  成为大亨，称霸，主宰 | To dominate or become a powerful figure in a particular field, often related to business or entertainment. mogul:/ˈmoʊɡəl/ | n. | 大亨，巨头 | An important or powerful person, especially in the film or news industry. Originally, a Mogul was a member of the Muslim dynasty that ruled India from the 16th to the 19th centuries. |  He's a media mogul with a vast empire. 他是一位媒体大亨，拥有庞大的帝国。 | magnate, tycoon, baron, lord, leader | underling, follower, subordinate | noun
