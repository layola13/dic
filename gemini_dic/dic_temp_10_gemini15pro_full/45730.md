curvilinear:/ˌkɜːrvɪˈlɪniər/| adj.|曲线形的，弯曲的|Having or represented by curved lines.  Characterized by curved lines.|例句：The architect incorporated curvilinear designs into the building's facade. 建筑师将曲线设计融入建筑的立面。|例句：The artist's curvilinear brushstrokes gave the painting a sense of movement. 艺术家弯曲的笔触赋予了这幅画动感。|近义词：curved, bending, sinuous|反义词:straight, linear|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *curvilineus, from Latin curvus (“curved”) + linea (“line”).
