misclosure:/mɪsˈkloʊʒər/| n. |闭合差，闭合误差|The difference between a theoretically correct sum of a set of quantities and their actual sum, especially as a result of errors in measurement or calculation.  It indicates the inconsistency or error in a closed traverse or loop.|The misclosure in the survey was larger than acceptable.  测量中的闭合差大于可接受值。|近义词：discrepancy, error, imbalance|反义词：closure, agreement|词性:noun
