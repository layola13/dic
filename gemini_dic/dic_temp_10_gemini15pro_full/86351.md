hecticness:/ˈhɛktɪknəs/
n.
忙碌；狂乱
The state of being hectic; a busy and frantic activity.
The hecticness of modern life can be overwhelming.  现代生活的忙碌可能会让人难以承受。
She thrived in the hecticness of the newsroom. 她很适应新闻编辑室里忙碌的工作。
近义词: frenzy, bustle, chaos, flurry
反义词: calmness, tranquility, peacefulness
词性: 名词

Etymology: From hectic + -ness.  Hectic ultimately comes from the Greek word "hektikos," meaning "habitual," referring to a type of fever.  The modern sense of "busy and frantic" developed later.
