fatiguesome:/fəˈtiːɡsəm/| adj.|令人疲倦的，使人疲劳的|Causing tiredness or exhaustion; tiring.|The long journey was fatiguesome.  漫长的旅程令人疲倦。|近义词：tiring, exhausting, wearying, draining|反义词：refreshing, invigorating, energizing|词性:形容词
Etymology: From French fatiguer (to tire) + -some (suffix indicating a tendency to cause something).  Ultimately from Latin fatigare.
