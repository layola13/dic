outpitching:/ˌaʊtˈpɪtʃɪŋ/
verb
胜过投球，投球比…更好
performing better than another pitcher in a baseball game.
例句：The rookie was outpitching the veteran ace.  这位新秀的投球表现胜过了经验丰富的老王牌。

例句：He was consistently outpitching his opponents throughout the playoffs. 在整个季后赛中，他的投球一直比对手更好。
近义词：outperforming, surpassing, exceeding
反义词：being outpitched, underperforming
词性：v.
