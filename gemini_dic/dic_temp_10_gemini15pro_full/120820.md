misattributed:/ˌmɪsəˈtrɪbjʊtɪd/ | adj. | 错误归属的，被误认为是…创作（或说）的 | Incorrectly attributed; assigned to the wrong author, source, or cause. |  The painting was misattributed to Rembrandt for many years. 这幅画多年来一直被误认为是伦勃朗的作品。| Synonyms: miscredited, wrongly ascribed, falsely attributed | Antonyms: correctly attributed, accurately ascribed | adjective

misattribute:/ˌmɪsəˈtrɪbjuːt/ | vt. | 错误地归于，把…错误地归因于 | To wrongly attribute something; to credit something to the wrong person, source, or cause. |  The scholar misattributed the poem to a lesser-known poet.  这位学者把这首诗错误地归于一位不太知名的诗人。| Synonyms: miscredit, falsely ascribe | Antonyms: correctly attribute, accurately ascribe | verb
