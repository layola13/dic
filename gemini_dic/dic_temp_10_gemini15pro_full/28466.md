carburizer:/ˈkɑːrbəˌraɪzər/ | n. |渗碳剂；增碳剂|A substance used to introduce carbon into the surface of steel in order to harden it.| The carburizer was added to the furnace to increase the carbon content of the steel.  渗碳剂被添加到熔炉中以增加钢的碳含量。| synonyms:  carbonizing agent,  cementing agent | antonyms: decarburizer | noun


carburize: /ˈkɑːrbəˌraɪz/ | v. |渗碳；使增碳|To introduce carbon into the surface of steel in order to harden it.|  The gears were carburized to improve their wear resistance. 齿轮经过渗碳处理以提高其耐磨性。| synonyms: carbonize, cement | antonyms: decarburize | verb
