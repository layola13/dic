freebooted:/ˌfriːˈbuːtɪd/
verb|掠夺的，以海盗方式获得的|Having engaged in freebooting; acquired by freebooting.|The freebooted software was distributed online.  这款盗版软件在网上被传播。|Synonyms: pirated, plundered, stolen|Antonyms: legitimate, earned, purchased|v.

freebooter:/ˈfriːbuːtər/
noun|海盗，掠夺者|A pirate or plunderer, especially one who preys on shipping.|The freebooter terrorized the coastal towns.  这个海盗恐吓着沿海城镇。|Synonyms: pirate, buccaneer, corsair, privateer|Antonyms: lawman, officer, authority|n.
