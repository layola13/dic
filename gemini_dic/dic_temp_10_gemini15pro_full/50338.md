deployed:/dɪˈplɔɪd/ | adj. | 已部署的，已展开的 |  spread out strategically or for a particular purpose. |  The deployed forces awaited further orders.  已部署的部队等待进一步的命令。| synonyms: positioned, stationed, arranged | antonyms: withdrawn, retracted, removed | adjective
