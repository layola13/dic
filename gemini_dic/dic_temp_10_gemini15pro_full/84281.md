half-timbered:/ˌhɑːfˈtɪmbərd/ | adj. | 半木结构的 | A building style where the wooden frame is exposed on the exterior walls, with the spaces between the timbers filled with plaster, brick, or other materials. |  The town is famous for its charming half-timbered houses.  这个小镇以其迷人的半木结构房屋而闻名。 | Synonyms: timber-framed, Tudor-style | Antonyms: modern, concrete | adjective

half-timbered:/ˌhɑːfˈtɪmbərd/ | adj. | 半木结构的 |  Characterized by a framework of timber with the intervening spaces filled with masonry or plaster. | We stayed in a quaint half-timbered inn. 我们住在一个古色古香的半木结构的旅店。 | Synonyms: timber-framed | Antonyms: brick, stone | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "half" + "timbered".  "Half" refers to the timbers being visible, and "timbered" refers to the construction method using timbers.
