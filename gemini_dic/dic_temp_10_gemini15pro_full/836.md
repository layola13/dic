abstractable: /æbˈstræktəbl/
adjective
可抽象的，可提取的
Capable of being abstracted or considered separately from material objects or practical matters.  Something that can be summarized or extracted.
The core principles of his philosophy are easily abstractable. 他的哲学核心原则很容易被抽象出来。
The data is abstractable into a simpler form.  这些数据可以被提取成更简单的形式。
synonyms: conceptual, theoretical, generalizable, extractable, summarizable
antonyms: concrete, specific, tangible
词性: adjective
