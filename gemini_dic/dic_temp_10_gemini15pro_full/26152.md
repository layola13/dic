busybodied:/ˈbɪziˌbɒdid/ (US) /ˈbɪziˌbɒdid/ (UK)| adj.|爱管闲事的，好事的|Excessively interested in the affairs of others; meddling.|She's a busybodied old woman who always has to know everyone's business. 她是一个爱管闲事的老太太，总是想知道每个人的事。|近义词：nosy, intrusive, meddlesome, interfering|反义词：unconcerned, indifferent|词性：形容词
