concrescible:/kənˈkrɛsɪbl/ /kɑːnˈkrɛsɪbl/
adjective
可凝固的; 可变硬的
Capable of becoming concrete or solid; capable of solidifying.
The scientist hypothesized that the unusual liquid was concrescible under specific conditions.  这位科学家假设这种不寻常的液体在特定条件下是可凝固的。
The molten lava, initially a fiery liquid, was concrescible as it cooled.  最初是火热液体的熔岩在冷却时凝固了。
近义词:coagulable, solidifiable, hardenable
反义词:insoluble, non-coagulating, fluid
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *concrescibilis, from concrescere (“to grow together, solidify”), from com- (“together”) + crescere (“to grow”).
