implorable:/ɪmˈplɔːrəbl/
adjective
值得恳求的；可怜的；令人同情
Deserving of imploring; pitiable; evoking sympathy.
Her implorable situation moved everyone to tears. 她可怜的处境使每个人都感动得流下了眼泪。
Synonyms: pitiable, pathetic, piteous, wretched
Antonyms: enviable, fortunate, blessed
Word Origin: From Late Latin implorabilis, from Latin implorare "to implore"
