card:/kɑːrd/ (US) /kɑːd/ (UK)| n. |卡片；名片；纸牌；明信片；信用卡；（电子）卡|A piece of stiff paper that is usually rectangular and serves various purposes, such as identification, playing games, or sending greetings.|She handed me her business card. 她递给我她的名片。|synonyms: ticket, pass, badge|antonyms: none|noun

card:/kɑːrd/ (US) /kɑːd/ (UK)| v. |梳理（羊毛、棉花等）|To comb or disentangle fibers, especially wool or cotton, using a card.|They carded the wool before spinning it into yarn. 他们在把羊毛纺成纱线之前先梳理了它。|synonyms: comb, disentangle|antonyms: tangle, mat|verb
