obtainabilities:/əbˌteɪnəˈbɪlətiz/
n.
可获得性；可得到性
The obtainabilities of these rare resources are questionable. 这些稀有资源的可获得性值得怀疑。
The report analyzed the obtainabilities of different energy sources. 该报告分析了不同能源的可获得性。
近义词:availability, accessibility
反义词:unavailability, inaccessibility
词性:名词


obtainable:/əbˈteɪnəbl/
adj.
可获得的；可得到的
Information on the subject is readily obtainable. 关于这个主题的信息很容易得到。
The necessary documents are obtainable from the office.  必要的证件可以从办公室获取。
近义词:available, accessible, acquirable
反义词:unobtainable, unavailable, inaccessible
词性:形容词

obtain:/əbˈteɪn/
v.
获得；得到
He obtained his degree in 1995. 他于1995年获得学位。
I finally managed to obtain a copy of the report. 我终于设法得到了一份报告的副本。
近义词:get, acquire, procure, gain
反义词:lose, forfeit
词性:动词

词源: From Late Latin *ōbtēnēbilis, from Latin obtinēre (“to hold, possess, maintain, acquire, obtain”) +‎ -bilis.  Equivalent to obtain +‎ -able.
