custodial:/kʌˈstoʊdiəl/
adjective
保管的，监护的
relating to or involving the legal right to keep and care for someone, especially a child after a separation or divorce
例句：The court awarded custodial rights to the mother. 法院将监护权判给了母亲。

例句：He works in a custodial role at the museum, ensuring the safety and preservation of the artifacts. 他在博物馆担任保管员的角色，确保文物的安全和保存。
近义词：protective, guardian, supervisory
反义词：non-custodial, neglectful
词性: adjective
