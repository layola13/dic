mushier:/ˈmʌʃiər/ | adj. | 更糊状的，更感伤的 | Softer and more pulpy than before; excessively sentimental. | The overripe bananas were too mushier for the bread.  过熟的香蕉太糊状了，不适合做面包。|  The movie was much mushier than I expected. 这部电影比我想象的要感伤得多。 | 近义词：softer, pulpier, sentimental, mawkish | 反义词：firmer, harder, unsentimental | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From mush + -ier (comparative suffix).  "Mush" originates from Middle English "mos," meaning soft, pulpy matter.
