deservers:/dɪˈzɜːrvərz/
|n. |应得者；值得拥有者|People who deserve something are deservers.  那些应得某物的人是应得者。| Those who work hard are the true deservers of success. 努力工作的人才是真正的成功应得者。|recipients, beneficiaries|undeserving|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From deserve (v.) + -er (suffix forming nouns denoting persons who perform an action or are associated with something).  Deserve comes from Old French deservir "to deserve, merit," from des- "fully" (from Latin dis-) + servir "to serve."
