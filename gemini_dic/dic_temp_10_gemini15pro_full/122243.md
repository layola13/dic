miszoned:/ˌmɪsˈzoʊnd/
adjective
错误分区的
incorrectly zoned; assigned to the wrong zone.
The miszoned property caused a lot of confusion for the developers.  这块被错误分区的土地给开发商造成了很多困惑。
The warehouse was miszoned for residential use.  这个仓库被错误地划分为住宅用地。
近义词: incorrectly zoned, wrongly classified
反义词: correctly zoned, properly classified
词性: adjective
