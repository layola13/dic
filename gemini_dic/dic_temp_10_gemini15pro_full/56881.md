doses:/ˈdoʊsɪz/ (US), /ˈdəʊsɪz/ (UK)| n.|剂量; 一服, 一剂|A measured quantity of medicine, drug, or radiation administered or recommended to be taken at one time.|The doctor prescribed two doses of antibiotics per day.|近义词: amount, quantity, portion, dosage|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "whole" or "entirety" in certain contexts)|noun
