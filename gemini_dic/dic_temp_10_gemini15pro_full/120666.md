misadapt:/ˌmɪsəˈdæpt/ | v. | 误适应，适应不良 | To adjust poorly to a new environment or situation, often resulting in negative consequences. | The polar bear is misadapted to a warmer climate. 北极熊不适应温暖的气候。|  The company's marketing strategy was misadapted to the local culture. 该公司的营销策略不适应当地文化。| 近义词: maladapt, fail to adapt | 反义词: adapt, adjust | 词性: 动词

misadapt:/ˌmɪsəˈdæpt/ | adj. | 适应不良的 | Poorly adjusted to a new environment or situation. | The misadapted species struggled to survive. 这个适应不良的物种难以生存。| The misadapted software caused numerous problems.  这个适应不良的软件导致了许多问题。| 近义词: maladapted, ill-adapted | 反义词: adapted, well-adapted | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (badly, wrongly) + adapt (to adjust).  The prefix "mis-" indicates a negative or incorrect action or state. The root "adapt" comes from the Latin word "adaptare," meaning "to fit."
