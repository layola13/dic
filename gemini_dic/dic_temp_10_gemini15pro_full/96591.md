inductility:/ˌɪndʌkˈtɪləti/ | n. |延展性，韧性 |The property of a material that allows it to be drawn into wires or hammered into thin sheets without breaking.  | Copper has high inductility, making it ideal for wiring. 铜具有很高的延展性，使其成为理想的电线材料。|  近义词：malleability, ductility, plasticity | 反义词：brittleness, fragility | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *inductilitas, from inductus, past participle of inducere (“to lead in, bring in, introduce”), equivalent to induct + -ility.  Related to induce.
