homefield:/ˈhoʊmˌfiːld/ (US) /ˈhəʊmˌfiːld/ (UK)| n.|主场；主场优势|A team's home field is the stadium or field where they regularly play their games. It often implies an advantage due to familiarity with the field and support from local fans.|The team has a strong homefield advantage.  这支队伍有很强的的主场优势。|Synonyms: home ground, home stadium, home turf|Antonyms: away field, opponent's field|noun

homefield advantage:/ˌhoʊmˈfiːld ədˈvæntɪdʒ/ (US) /ˌhəʊmˈfiːld ədˈvɑːntɪdʒ/ (UK)| n.|主场优势|The advantage a team has when playing in its home stadium or field, typically due to familiarity with the environment and support from local fans.|The homefield advantage really helped them win the game. 主场优势真的帮助他们赢得了比赛。|Synonyms: home ground advantage, home advantage|Antonyms: away disadvantage|noun
