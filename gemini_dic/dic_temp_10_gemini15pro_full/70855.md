finking:/ˈfɪŋkɪŋ/
| n. |告密；背叛；(尤指在罢工期间)破坏罢工|The act of informing on someone, especially a fellow worker or member of a group, to the authorities or those in power. Often used in the context of labor strikes or criminal activity. |He was accused of finking on his fellow union members. 他被指控向工会会员告密。|近义词：informing, snitching, ratting, betraying|反义词：loyalty, solidarity|词性:名词

| n. |告密者；叛徒；破坏罢工者|A person who informs on someone, especially a fellow worker or member of a group, to the authorities or those in power.
