dispeople:/dɪsˈpiːpəl/
verb
使无人居住；减少人口
To deprive of inhabitants; depopulate.
例句：The government's policies have dispeopled the rural areas. 政府的政策使得农村地区人口减少。

例句：War and famine dispeopled the region. 战争和饥荒使这个地区人口减少。

近义词：depopulate, evacuate
反义词：populate, inhabit
词性：v.
