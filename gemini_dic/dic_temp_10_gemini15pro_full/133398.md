nonfactor:/ˈnɒnˌfæktə(r)/ (US) /ˈnɑːnˌfæktər/| n. |无关紧要的因素；不起作用的因素|Something that is not a factor or does not have any influence on a situation.|The weather was a nonfactor in the game; both teams played equally well in the rain. 天气对比赛没有影响；两队在雨中都表现得同样出色。| The coach's decision to bench the star player was a nonfactor in their loss; the team simply didn't play well. 教练让明星球员坐冷板凳的决定对他们的失利没有影响；球队根本就没有打好。 |Synonym: irrelevancy, insignificance|Antonym: factor, influence|noun
