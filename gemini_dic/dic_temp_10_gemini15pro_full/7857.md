ante-up:/ˌænti ˈʌp/| phrasal verb|下注；付款；交钱；拿出（份额）|To pay or contribute (a required sum of money).  Origin: From poker, where players must "ante up" before the hand is dealt.|例句：It's time to ante up for the charity auction.  是时候为慈善拍卖捐款了。|近义词：pay, contribute, chip in|反义词:withdraw, withhold|词性:动词短语
