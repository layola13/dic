gelidity:/dʒəˈlɪdɪti/
n.
极寒，冰冻状态
Extreme coldness; iciness.
The gelidity of the Antarctic air was breathtaking. 南极空气的极度寒冷令人窒息。
近义词: coldness, frigidity, iciness
反义词: warmth, heat
词性: 名词

gelid:/ˈdʒɛlɪd/
adj.
极冷的，冰冻的
Extremely cold; icy.
The explorer shivered in the gelid wind. 探险家在冰冷的寒风中瑟瑟发抖。
近义词: icy, freezing, frigid
反义词: warm, hot
词性: 形容词
