flinting:/ˈflɪntɪŋ/| n. |打火石，燧石击火|The act of striking flint to produce sparks, or the sparks themselves.|The early humans discovered flinting as a way to make fire.|近义词：striking, sparking|反义词: extinguishing|词性:noun
flinting:/ˈflɪntɪŋ/| adj. |坚硬的，冷酷无情的|Resembling flint in hardness or coldness.|He gave her a flinting stare.|近义词：hard, cold, unyielding|反义词:soft, warm, compassionate|词性:adjective
