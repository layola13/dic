functional:/ˈfʌŋkʃənəl/| adj.|功能性的，实用的|Designed for or adapted to a particular function or use; practical and useful rather than decorative.|This building has a functional design. 这栋楼的设计很实用。|近义词:practical, useful, operative, working|反义词:decorative, ornamental, nonfunctional, useless|词性:形容词

functional:/ˈfʌŋkʃənəl/| n.|功能|An activity or purpose natural to or intended for a person or thing.|The functional of this machine is to cut wood.  这台机器的功能是切割木材。|近义词:purpose, role, use|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
