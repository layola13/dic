mewler:/ˈmjuːlər/ | n. | 哀鸣者；喵喵叫的人/动物 |  A person or animal that mews.  | The kitten was a persistent mewler, crying until it was fed.  这只小猫不停地喵喵叫，直到被喂食才停止。| 近义词: crier, whiner, complainer | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From the verb "mew" (to cry like a cat) + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting a person or thing that performs the action of the verb).  "Mew" itself is imitative of the sound a cat makes.
