feinting:/ˈfeɪntɪŋ/
n.
佯攻; 假动作
An act of making a deceptive or distracting movement, typically during a fight or game.
The boxer's feinting confused his opponent. 拳击手的佯攻迷惑了他的对手。
synonyms: deception, subterfuge, trickery
antonyms: honesty, openness, sincerity
noun


feint:/feɪnt/
v.
佯攻; 做假动作
Make a deceptive or distracting movement, typically during a fight or game.
He feinted left and then threw a right hook. 他佯攻向左，然后打了一个右勾拳。
synonyms: pretend, simulate, sham
antonyms: be honest, be truthful, be sincere
verb
