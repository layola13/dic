aphasias: /əˈfeɪʒəz/ /əˈfeɪziəz/ | n. | 失语症 |Loss of ability to understand or express speech, caused by brain damage.| After the stroke, he suffered from aphasias and struggled to communicate. 中风后，他患上了失语症，难以沟通。 | synonyms: dysphasia, speech impediment | antonyms: fluency, eloquence | noun
