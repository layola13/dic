baptisms:/ˈbæptɪzəmz/
n.
洗礼，浸礼
The act of baptizing someone, usually with water, as a religious rite of initiation.
例句：The baptisms were conducted in the river.  这些洗礼是在河里进行的。

例句：There were several baptisms at the church last Sunday. 上个星期天教堂举行了好几场洗礼仪式。

近义词：christenings, initiations
反义词: None

词性: noun (plural)

词源: From Late Latin *baptismus, from Greek baptīsma, from baptīzein ‘baptize.’
