chickweed:/ˈtʃɪkwiːd/| n. |繁缕；鸡草|A low-growing weedy plant of the carnation family, with small white flowers and ovate leaves. It is common in gardens and waste ground and is used as food by some birds, especially chicks. |Chickweed is a common weed in my garden. 我的花园里有很多繁缕。|近义词：stitchwort, mouse-ear chickweed|反义词: N/A|noun


chickweed:/ˈtʃɪkwiːd/| n. |繁缕；鸡草|Chickweed is a nutritious plant often eaten by chickens and other birds, hence the name.  It's also used in some herbal remedies. |The chickens pecked at the chickweed in the yard.  鸡在院子里啄食繁缕。|近义词：Stellaria media (scientific name)|反义词: N/A|noun
