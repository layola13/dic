hirling:/ˈhɜːrlɪŋ/ | n. |雇工，雇佣兵；受雇于不光彩工作的人 |A person who is hired, especially for unskilled labor; a mercenary. |He dismissed his lazy hirling. 他解雇了他懒惰的雇工。| 近义词: employee, worker, laborer, mercenary | 反义词: employer, boss | noun

hirling:/ˈhɜːrlɪŋ/ | adj. (古语，罕用) | 受雇的，雇佣的 | Hired; mercenary. |  The hirling shepherd cared little for the flock. 受雇的牧羊人对羊群漠不关心。| 近义词: hired, employed | 反义词:  unemployed | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hirlinge, from Old English *hyrling, from hyrian (“to hire”).
