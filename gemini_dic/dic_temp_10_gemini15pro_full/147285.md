overmobilizes: /ˌoʊvərˈmoʊbəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
过度动员
To mobilize (troops, resources, etc.) in excessive numbers or to an excessive degree.
The country overmobilized for the war, leaving its economy severely weakened.  这个国家为战争过度动员，导致经济严重衰弱。
The general overmobilized his troops, exhausting their supplies before the battle even began.  将军过度动员了他的部队，在战斗开始之前就耗尽了他们的补给。
Synonyms: overdeploy, overextend
Antonyms: undermobilize, underdeploy
词性: 动词
