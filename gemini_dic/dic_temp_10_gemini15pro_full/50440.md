deprecation:/ˌdɛprɪˈkeɪʃən/| n. |反对，不赞成；贬低；弃用|The act of expressing disapproval of something.  The official act of reducing the value of something.  The act of officially stopping the use of something.|The software documentation contains many deprecations, warning programmers about functions that may be removed in future versions. 该软件文档包含许多弃用警告，提醒程序员注意将来版本中可能删除的功能。

The government’s deprecation of violence was welcomed by all. 政府对暴力的谴责受到所有人的欢迎。

The deprecation of the currency caused economic hardship. 货币贬值造成了经济困难。|近义词：disapproval, criticism, condemnation, devaluation, obsolescence|反义词：approval, praise, appreciation, valuation|词性:名词
