consumptively:/kənˈsʌmp.tɪv.li/
| adv. | 消耗地；肺病地；浪费地 |  In a way that relates to or is characterized by consumption, especially of resources or by a wasting disease (like tuberculosis). | He coughed consumptively, clutching his chest. 他捂着胸口，咳嗽得像是肺痨病人。 | 近义词: wastefully, extravagantly, destructively | 反义词: productively, conservatively | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From "consumptive" + "-ly". "Consumptive" comes from the Late Latin "consumptivus", from Latin "consumere" (to consume).
