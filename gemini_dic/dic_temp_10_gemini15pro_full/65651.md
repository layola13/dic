evaporating:/ɪˈvæpəˌreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 蒸发; 消失 | The water is evaporating quickly in the sun.  水在阳光下迅速蒸发。| 近义词: vaporizing,  disappearing, vanishing | 反义词: condensing, materializing | 词性: 动词 (present participle)

Etymology: From Latin *evaporatus*, past participle of *evaporare* "to disperse in vapor," from *ex-* "out" + *vapor* "vapor, steam."
