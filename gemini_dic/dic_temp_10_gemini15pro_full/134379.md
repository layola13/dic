noninterpretative:/ˌnɒnɪnˈtɜːprɪtətɪv/
adjective
非解释性的，不带解释的
Not involving or providing interpretation; based on facts without giving an opinion or explanation.
The museum offers noninterpretative exhibits, allowing visitors to draw their own conclusions.  这家博物馆提供非解释性的展览，让参观者得出自己的结论。
The data presented was purely noninterpretative, simply showing the raw figures.  提供的数据纯粹是非解释性的，仅仅显示了原始数据。
近义词:objective, factual, descriptive, literal
反义词:interpretative, subjective, analytical, explanatory
词性:adjective

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + interpretative (adjective from "interpret")
