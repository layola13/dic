outbitch:/ˌaʊtˈbɪtʃ/
|v.|比…更bitchy; 胜过|To surpass someone in bitchiness or spiteful behavior.|She tried to outbitch her rival by spreading rumors, but it backfired. 她试图通过散布谣言来胜过她的对手，但事与愿违。|Synonyms: outmaneuver, outsmart, surpass in spitefulness|Antonyms: be kind, be compassionate|verb

outbitch:/ˌaʊtˈbɪtʃ/
|n.|更bitchy的人|A person who is more bitchy or spiteful than another.|She's the ultimate outbitch, always starting drama. 她是最bitchy的人，总是挑起事端。|Synonyms: shrew, harpy, viper|Antonyms: sweetheart, angel|noun
