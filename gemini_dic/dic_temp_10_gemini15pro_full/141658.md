optimizing: /ˈɒptɪmaɪzɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈɑːptɪmaɪzɪŋ/ (US)
verb, present participle
优化，最佳化
the act of making something as good as possible
例句：They are optimizing the website for mobile devices. 他们正在优化网站以适应移动设备。

例句：The company is optimizing its production processes to reduce costs.  公司正在优化其生产流程以降低成本。

近义词: improving, enhancing, perfecting, refining
反义词: worsening, degrading
词性: verb (present participle)
