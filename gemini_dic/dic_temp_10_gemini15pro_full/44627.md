crowfoot:/ˈkrəʊfʊt/ (UK) /ˈkroʊfʊt/ (US)| n.|毛茛；鸦脚；鱼尾纹；星形轮|A type of plant with divided leaves resembling a bird's foot, especially the buttercup; a wrinkle at the outer corner of the eye; a metal device used for securing ropes or chains.|例句：The field was covered in yellow crowfoot. 这田野上开满了黄色的毛茛。
