lobule:/ˈlɒbjuːl/ (US) /ˈlɑːbjuːl/| n. |小叶; 小裂片|A small lobe or subdivision of a lobe, as of the lung, liver, or brain. |The doctor identified a shadow on the lower lobule of his left lung. 医生在他左肺下叶发现了一个阴影。|The surgeon carefully removed the affected lobule of the liver.  外科医生小心地切除了受影响的肝小叶。|Synonym: segment, subdivision|Antonym: lobe (in a broader sense)|noun
