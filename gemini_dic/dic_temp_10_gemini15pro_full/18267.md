belittle: /bɪˈlɪt(ə)l/
verb
贬低，轻视
to make someone or something seem unimportant
He belittled her efforts to get a promotion. 他贬低了她为升职所做的努力。

近义词: disparage, denigrate, minimize, underestimate, undervalue, depreciate
反义词: praise, commend, extol, laud, magnify, overestimate
词性: verb
