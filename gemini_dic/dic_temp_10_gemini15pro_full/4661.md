aled: /əˈlɛd/ | adj. | 点燃的；燃烧的；发光的 |  Lit up; burning; glowing.  Derived from the verb "ale" (to kindle, to set on fire), which is now archaic. | The street was aled with festive lights. 节日彩灯照亮了街道。|  Synonyms: illuminated, ablaze, lighted | Antonyms: dark, extinguished | adjective

aled: /əˈlɛd/ | v. (archaic) | 点燃；使燃烧 | To kindle; to set on fire.  This usage is now archaic and rarely encountered. |  He aled the fire with a single match.  他用一根火柴点燃了火。 (archaic usage) | Synonyms: ignite, kindle | Antonyms: extinguish, quench | verb (archaic)
