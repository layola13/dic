inspire:/ɪnˈspaɪər/ (US), /ɪnˈspaɪə(r)/ (UK)| v. | 激励，鼓舞；赋予灵感；启迪 | to fill (someone) with the urge or ability to do or feel something, especially to do something creative |  Her bravery inspired us all. 她的勇气鼓舞了我们所有人。| motivate, encourage, stimulate | discourage, depress | verb

inspire:/ɪnˈspaɪər/ (US), /ɪnˈspaɪə(r)/ (UK)| n. |  (吸气) | an act of breathing in |  He took a deep inspire. 他深深地吸了一口气。| inhalation | exhalation | noun (This usage is rare and archaic)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *inspirer*, from Latin inspirare "to breathe or blow into," figuratively "to inspire, inflame," from in- "in" + spirare "to breathe."
