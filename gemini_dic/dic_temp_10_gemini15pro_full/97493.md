ingerminate:/ɪnˈdʒɜːrmɪneɪt/
adjective
未萌发的，未发芽的
not germinated; not having sprouted or developed
The ingerminate seeds remained dormant in the dry soil.  未萌发的种子在干燥的土壤中保持休眠状态。
The farmer was concerned about the ingerminate seeds. 农民担心种子没有发芽.
Synonyms: dormant, undeveloped, quiescent
Antonyms: germinated, sprouted, developed
adjective
