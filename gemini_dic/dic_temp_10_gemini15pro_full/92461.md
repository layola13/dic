hypostasize: /haɪˈpɒstəsaɪz/ /haɪˈpɑːstəsaɪz/
verb
假设；赋予实体；具体化
to attribute real existence to (a concept or idea)
例句：Philosophers hypostasize abstract concepts like justice and truth. 哲学家将正义和真理等抽象概念具体化。

例句：Some people hypostasize their fears, imagining them as real entities. 有些人将他们的恐惧具体化，想象它们是真实存在的实体。

近义词：reify, personify, embody, objectify
反义词：abstract
词性：verb
