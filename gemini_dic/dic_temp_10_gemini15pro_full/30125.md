ceding:/ˈsiːdɪŋ/
verb
割让；放弃；交出
The act of surrendering or yielding something, such as territory, rights, or possessions.
He is ceding his position as CEO. 他将辞去首席执行官的职位。
synonyms: surrendering, relinquishing, yielding, granting
antonyms: retaining, keeping, acquiring, seizing
词性: 动词
