bologna:/bəˈloʊni/ (US), /bəˈlɒni/ (UK)| n.|博洛尼亚香肠，大香肠|A large seasoned sausage, typically made of mixed meats.  It is often served sliced in sandwiches.|例句：I had a bologna sandwich for lunch. 我午餐吃了个博洛尼亚香肠三明治。

例句：He prefers bologna to salami. 他比起意大利腊肠更喜欢博洛尼亚香肠。|近义词：sausage, frankfurter, baloney|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

bologna:/bəˈloʊni/ (US), /bəˈlɒni/ (UK)| n. (informal)|胡说，废话|Nonsense; lies.|例句：That's a load of bologna! 那是一堆废话！

例句：Don't give me that bologna! 别跟我胡扯！|近义词：nonsense, rubbish, baloney|反义词:truth, fact|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *bologna*, from *Bologna*, the city in Italy where the sausage originated.
