amazonic:/ˌæməˈzɒnɪk/ | adj. |亚马逊的，巨大的，强壮的 |Of or relating to the Amazon River or its basin; resembling an Amazon; (figurative) powerful and formidable. | The amazonic rainforest is teeming with life. 亚马逊雨林充满了生机。|  The athlete possessed an amazonic physique. 这位运动员拥有健壮的体格。 | 近义词: huge, immense, gigantic, powerful, strong | 反义词: small, tiny, weak | 词性: 形容词

詞源: From Amazon + -ic.  Ultimately from Greek Ἀμαζών (Amazōn).
