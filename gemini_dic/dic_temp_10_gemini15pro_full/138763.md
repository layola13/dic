numerically:/nuːˈmerɪkli/| adv.|数字地，用数字表示地|Expressed or considered in terms of numbers; quantifiable.  From Latin "numerus" (number) + "-ical" (forming adjectives).|The results were presented numerically in the report. 结果在报告中以数字形式呈现。|The population has increased numerically over the past decade.  过去十年人口数量有所增加。| 近义词: quantitatively, mathematically|反义词: qualitatively, descriptively|词性: 副词
