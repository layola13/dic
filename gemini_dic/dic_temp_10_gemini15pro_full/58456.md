ductile:/ˈdʌktaɪl/ /ˈdʌktəl/| adj.|易延展的；可塑的；柔韧的|Able to be deformed permanently without breaking or fracturing; pliable, malleable.  Able to be drawn into wires or hammered thin.|Copper is a very ductile metal, which is why it's used in wiring.  铜是一种非常有延展性的金属，所以它被用于电线。|近义词：malleable, pliable, flexible, plastic|反义词：brittle, rigid, inflexible, stiff|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *ductilis*, from *ductus* "a leading, drawing," past participle of *ducere* "to lead, draw."
