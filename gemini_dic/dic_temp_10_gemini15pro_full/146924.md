overintellectualness:/ˌoʊvərˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəlˌnɛs/ | n. | 过度理智，过度思考 | The state of being excessively intellectual or relying too much on intellect.  An overemphasis on abstract thought and analysis, often at the expense of practical considerations or emotional intelligence. |  His overintellectualness made it difficult for him to connect with people on an emotional level. 他过度理智，以至于难以与人在情感层面上建立联系。 |  Overthinking, intellectualism, cerebrality | practicality, pragmatism | noun
