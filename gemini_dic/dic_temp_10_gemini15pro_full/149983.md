palmettos:/pælˈmetoʊz/
noun
棕榈树（尤指矮小的扇形叶棕榈）
Any of various usually small palms with fan-shaped leaves.
The wind rustled through the palmettos. 风吹过棕榈树发出沙沙声。
palms,  sabal palms
(通常指高大的树)
