bombing:/ˈbɒmɪŋ/ (UK), /ˈbɑːmɪŋ/ (US)| n.|轰炸，爆炸，失败|An act of dropping bombs from an aircraft.  The bombing caused widespread destruction. 轰炸造成了大范围的破坏。|例句：The bombing of the city resulted in many casualties.  城市遭到轰炸，造成许多人员伤亡。

例句：The bombing raid destroyed the factory. 轰炸袭击摧毁了工厂。

近义词：attack, assault, raid, airstrike|反义词:defense, protection|词性:noun


bombing:/ˈbɒmɪŋ/ (UK), /ˈbɑːmɪŋ/ (US)| adj.(informal)|糟糕的，极差的|Extremely bad or unsuccessful.  The movie was a bombing at the box office. 这部电影票房惨败。|例句：The play was a bombing and closed after only a week.  这部戏很失败，只演了一周就停演了。

例句：He had a bombing time at the party and left early. 他在派对上玩得很糟糕，很早就离开了。

近义词：terrible, awful, disastrous|反义词:successful, excellent|词性:adjective
