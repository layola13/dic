angustate:/æŋˈɡʌsˌteɪt/| vt.|使狭窄；使收缩；使痛苦|To make narrow or constricted; to cause distress or anguish.|The drought angustated the river, reducing it to a mere trickle. 干旱使河流变窄，减少到涓涓细流。|近义词：constrict, narrow, contract, distress, afflict|反义词：expand, widen, broaden, comfort, soothe|Verb

词源:
From Latin angustatus, past participle of angustare (“to make narrow, confine, compress”), from angustus (“narrow, strait, limited”), from ango (“I press tightly, squeeze, strangle”).
