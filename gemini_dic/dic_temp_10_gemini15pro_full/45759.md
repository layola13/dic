cushioned:/ˈkʊʃənd/ /ˈkʊʃnd/| adj.|缓冲的，垫衬的|Provided with soft padding or cushioning for comfort or protection.|The shoes have cushioned insoles for added comfort. 这双鞋有缓冲鞋垫，穿着更舒适。|Synonyms: padded, soft, comfortable, supportive|Antonyms: hard, unyielding, rigid|adjective

cushioned:/ˈkʊʃənd/ /ˈkʊʃnd/| v.|缓冲，缓和|To protect or soften the impact of something.|The thick carpet cushioned his fall. 厚厚的地毯缓冲了他的跌落。|Synonyms: soften, mitigate, absorb, lessen|Antonyms: worsen, aggravate, intensify|verb
