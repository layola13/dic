biogeography:/ˌbaɪoʊdʒiˈɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 生物地理学 | The study of the distribution of species and ecosystems in geographic space and through geological time. |  Example 1: Biogeography helps us understand why certain animals are found in specific regions. 生物地理学帮助我们理解为什么某些动物在特定区域被发现。

Example 2:  Island biogeography is a fascinating field of study. 岛屿生物地理学是一个引人入胜的研究领域。 | Near synonyms:  phytogeography, zoogeography | Antonym: N/A | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bio- + geography.  "Bio-" comes from the Greek "bios" meaning "life". "Geography" comes from the Greek "gē"  meaning "earth" and "graphein" meaning "to write" or "describe".
