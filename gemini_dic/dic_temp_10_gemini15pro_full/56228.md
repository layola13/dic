doeskin: /ˈdoʊskɪn/ (US), /ˈdəʊskɪn/ (UK)| n. adj.| 鹿皮；鹿皮似的|A soft, smooth, durable leather originally made from the skin of a deer, now often from sheepskin or other leather treated to resemble it. Also used as an adjective to describe something resembling doeskin.|
She wore a pair of doeskin gloves. 她戴着一副鹿皮手套。

The fabric felt like doeskin. 这块布料摸起来像鹿皮。

|近义词：buckskin, chamois, suede|反义词: - |名词, 形容词|
