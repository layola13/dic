contentednesses:/kənˈtɛntɪdnɪsɪz/
n.

She radiated a quiet contentedness. 她散发着一种静静的满足感。
satisfaction, contentment, happiness, pleasure
discontent, dissatisfaction, unhappiness, displeasure
