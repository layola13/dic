imploration:/ˌɪmplɔːˈreɪʃn/
n.
恳求，哀求
an earnest or humble request
The king ignored her imploration for mercy. 国王无视了她恳求宽恕的请求。

近义词:plea, entreaty, supplication, beseechment
反义词:demand, command, order
词性:noun
