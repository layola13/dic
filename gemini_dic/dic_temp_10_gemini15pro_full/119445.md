microlithic:/ˌmaɪkrəˈlɪθɪk/
adjective
微石器的；由微石器制成的
relating to or denoting tools made from small pieces of flint or other stone, characteristic of the Mesolithic period.
Microlithic blades were hafted onto wooden shafts to create composite tools. 微石器刀片被装在木柄上，制成复合工具。
Near East, microlithic industries continued in use. 在近东，微石器工业继续使用。
Synonyms: miniature, tiny, minute
Antonyms: macrolithic, large, huge
word type: adjective
