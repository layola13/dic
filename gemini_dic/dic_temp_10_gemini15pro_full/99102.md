intercapillary:/ˌɪntəkəˈpɪləri/
adjective
毛细血管间的，毛细管间的
Situated or occurring between capillaries.
例句：Intercapillary distance is an important factor in tissue oxygenation. 毛细血管间距是组织氧合的重要因素。

例句：The intercapillary space contains interstitial fluid. 毛细血管间隙包含组织液。
近义词：intervascular
反义词：(none specific, perhaps intracapillary - within a capillary)
词性：adjective
