nondemonstrably:/ˌnɒndɪˈmɒnstrəbli/
| adv. |不可证明地，无法证明地|Not in a way that can be clearly shown or proved. |His claims were nondemonstrably false. 他的说法无法证明是假的。|The benefits of this new policy are nondemonstrably positive. 这项新政策的好处无法证明是积极的。|近义词：unprovably, indisputably (arguably - depending on context)|反义词：provably, demonstrably|词性:副词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
demonstrably (adverb): From demonstrable + -ly.  Demonstrable comes from Late Latin demonstrabilis "that can be shown or proved," from Latin demonstrare "to point out, demonstrate," from de- "fully" + monstrare "to show."
