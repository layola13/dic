hospitality:/ˌhɒspɪˈtælɪti/ (US) /ˌhɑːspɪˈtælɪti/| n.|好客，热情，款待|The friendly and generous reception and entertainment of guests, visitors, or strangers.|例句：We were overwhelmed by the hospitality of the local people. 我们被当地人的热情好客所感动。

例句：The hotel is renowned for its excellent hospitality. 这家酒店以其优质的款待而闻名。


近义词：welcoming, warmth, generosity, friendliness, cordiality|反义词:hostility, unfriendliness, coldness, indifference|词性:名词
