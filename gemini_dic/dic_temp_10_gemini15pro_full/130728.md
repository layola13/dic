nonalluvial:/ˌnɒnəˈluːvɪəl/
adjective
非冲积的
Not deposited by running water; not alluvial.
The nonalluvial soils of the region are less fertile.  该地区的非冲积土壤肥力较差。
The geologist studied the nonalluvial formations in the mountains. 地质学家研究了山区的非冲积地层。
Synonyms: non-fluvial, upland
Antonyms: alluvial, fluvial
词性: adjective
