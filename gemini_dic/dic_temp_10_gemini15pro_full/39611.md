consensuses:/kənˈsɛnsəsiz/
n.
一致意见，共识
general agreement among a group of people
The committee reached a consensus on the proposal. 委员会就该提案达成了一致意见。
synonyms: agreement, accord, harmony, unanimity
antonyms: disagreement, discord, conflict
noun
