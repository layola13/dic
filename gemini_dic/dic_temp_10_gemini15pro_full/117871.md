melodiousness:/məˈləʊdiəsnəs/ /məˈloʊdiəsnəs/| n. |优美；悦耳；旋律美|The quality of being melodious; sweetness of sound.|Her voice was filled with a captivating melodiousness. 她的声音充满了迷人的旋律美。|近义词：euphony, harmony, musicality|反义词：cacophony, dissonance, harshness|词性:noun

Etymology:
From melodious + -ness.  Melodious comes from Late Middle English: from late Latin melodiosus, from Latin melodia (see melody) + -osus (English -ous).
