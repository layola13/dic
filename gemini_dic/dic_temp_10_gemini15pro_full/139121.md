obedientness:/əˈbiːdiəntnəs/| n.|服从；顺从|The quality of being obedient; compliance with an order, request, or law.|Her obedientness to her parents was remarkable. 她对父母的顺从令人瞩目。|Synonyms: compliance, submissiveness, ductility, tractability|Antonyms: disobedience, defiance, rebelliousness, recalcitrance|noun
