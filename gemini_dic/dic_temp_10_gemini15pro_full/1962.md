activate: /ˈæktɪveɪt/ (UK) /ˈæktɪˌveɪt/ (US)| v. | 激活；启动；使活化|To make something active or operational, or to make someone more active or enthusiastic.|The alarm is activated by movement.  这个警报器是由移动激活的。

The enzyme is activated by the presence of calcium ions.  这种酶被钙离子的存在激活。|近义词：trigger, start, initiate, switch on, turn on, enable|反义词：deactivate, disable|词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *activatus*, past participle of *activare*, from Latin activus (“active”).  Ultimately from Latin actus (“act, drive”).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
Stress activates the sympathetic nervous system. = 压力会激活交感神经系统。
He activates the alarm system every night. = 他每晚都启动警报系统。

一般过去时:

The researchers have activated the gene in the lab. = 研究人员已经在实验室里激活了该基因。

过去完成时:
