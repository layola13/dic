nonsatiric:/ˌnɒnsəˈtɪrɪk/ /ˌnɑːnsəˈtɪrɪk/
adjective
非讽刺的；非挖苦的
Not satirical; not intended to satirize.
Her nonsatiric commentary offered a refreshing perspective on the current political climate. 她非讽刺性的评论为当前的政治气候提供了一个令人耳目一新的视角。
The documentary presented a nonsatiric, straightforward account of the events. 这部纪录片对事件进行了直截了当的非讽刺性描述。
synonyms: serious, earnest, sincere, genuine, straightforward
antonyms: satirical, ironic, sarcastic, mocking
词性: adjective
