mollusc:/ˈmɒləsk/ (UK) /ˈmɑːləsk/ (US)| n. |软体动物|Any invertebrate of the phylum Mollusca, typically having a soft unsegmented body, a mantle, and a hard shell made of calcium carbonate.  This phylum includes snails, slugs, mussels, and octopuses.|Snails and slugs are common garden molluscs.  蜗牛和蛞蝓是花园里常见的软体动物。|近义词：invertebrate, shellfish (for some molluscs)|反义词: vertebrate|词性: noun

mollusk:/ˈmɑːləsk/ (US)| n. |软体动物| American spelling of mollusc. |Many mollusks, like clams and oysters, are edible. 许多软体动物，如蛤蜊和牡蛎，是可以食用的。|近义词：invertebrate, shellfish (for some mollusks)|反义词: vertebrate|词性: noun
