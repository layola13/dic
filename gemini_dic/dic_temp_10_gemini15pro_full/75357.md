frothiest:/ˈfrɒθiɪst/ | adj. | 最多泡沫的；最轻浮的 |  Superlative form of frothy: most frothy. Covered with or resembling froth; foamy. (Figuratively) frivolous and superficial. | The barista made the frothiest cappuccino I've ever had.  咖啡师做出了我喝过泡沫最多的卡布奇诺。| The frothiest gossip filled the tabloids.  小报上充斥着最轻浮的八卦。 | 近义词：foamiest, airiest, flimsiest | 反义词：flattest, densest | 词性: 形容词

词源：From froth + -y + -est.  Froth comes from Old Norse *froða.
