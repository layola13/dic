nonperishing:/ˌnɒnˈpɛrɪʃɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːnˈpɛrɪʃɪŋ/
adjective
不易腐烂的；耐久的
not subject to decay or spoilage.
例句：Nonperishing food items like canned goods are essential for emergencies.  不易腐烂的食品，例如罐头食品，在紧急情况下是必不可少的。

例句：Investing in gold is considered a safe bet as it's a nonperishing asset.  投资黄金被认为是一种安全的赌注，因为它是一种不会腐烂的资产。

近义词：imperishable, durable, lasting, enduring
反义词：perishable, decaying, decomposing
词性：adjective
