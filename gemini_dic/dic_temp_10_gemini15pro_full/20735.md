blameworthinesses:/ˈbleɪmwɜːrðinəsɪz/
n.
可责备性；应受谴责的程度；罪责
The degree to which someone deserves blame or censure for an action or omission.
The judge considered the blameworthinesses of each defendant before passing sentence. 法官在宣判前考虑了每个被告的罪责。
The blameworthinesses of the two parties involved were vastly different.  双方当事人的罪责程度大相径庭。
culpability, responsibility, accountability
innocence, blamelessness
noun
