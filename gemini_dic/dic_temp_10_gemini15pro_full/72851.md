folkething: /ˈfɔːlkəˌtɪŋ/ | n. | 国民议会，丹麦议会 |The lower house of the Danish Parliament.  It is one of the two houses of the Danish Parliament (Folketinget), the other being the Landsting (upper house which was abolished in 1953). | Denmark's Folkething passed a law banning the sale of new petrol and diesel cars by 2030. 丹麦国民议会通过了一项法律，禁止在2030年前销售新的汽油和柴油汽车。

The Folkething is responsible for passing legislation in Denmark. 丹麦国民议会负责通过丹麦的立法。 | synonym: parliament, legislature, congress | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "executive branch" or "judiciary" in a broad sense) | noun
