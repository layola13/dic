-mycetes:/maɪˈsiːtiːz/
noun
真菌纲
a taxonomic class within the kingdom Fungi; suffix used in naming classes of fungi
Example: Ascomycetes include yeasts and morels.  子囊菌纲包括酵母菌和羊肚菌。
Example: Basidiomycetes are a large and diverse class of fungi. 担子菌纲是一个庞大而多样的真菌纲。
Synonym: fungal class
Antonym: N/A (This is a taxonomic classification, not a concept with a direct opposite.)
词性: noun
