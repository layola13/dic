mesovarium:/ˌmɛzoʊˈvɛəriəm/ | n. | 卵巢系膜 | The fold of peritoneum that connects the ovary to the broad ligament of the uterus.  It carries blood vessels and nerves to the ovary. | The surgeon carefully dissected the mesovarium to avoid damaging the ovarian blood supply.  外科医生仔细地解剖了卵巢系膜，以避免损伤卵巢的血液供应。| synonym:  mesentery (in a broader sense), ligament (in a broader sense) | antonym:  None (anatomical structures don't typically have direct antonyms) | noun
