hansom:/ˈhænsəm/ | n. | 轻便双轮马车 | A two-wheeled horse-drawn carriage, especially one in which the driver's seat is elevated behind the passengers. |  The hansom cab rattled down the cobbled street.  轻便双轮马车沿着鹅卵石街道发出嘎啦嘎啦的声音。| synonyms: cab, carriage | antonyms: N/A | noun

Etymology: Named after its designer, Joseph Aloysius Hansom (1803-1882), a York architect.
