commiserations:/kəˌmɪzəˈreɪʃənz/
|n.|慰问，同情，吊唁|An expression of sympathy, especially on the occasion of a misfortune.|例句：My commiserations on your loss.  我对你的损失表示慰问。|例句：Please accept my commiserations on the death of your father. 请接受我对您父亲去世的慰问。|近义词：condolences, sympathy, empathy|反义词:congratulations|词性:名词
