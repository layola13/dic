linguists:/ˈlɪŋɡwɪsts/| n.|语言学家|Experts in the field of linguistics, studying the structure, history, and social context of language.|Linguists from around the world gathered for the conference. 全世界的语言学家聚集在一起参加会议。|synonyms: language scholars, philologists|antonyms: (no direct antonym, perhaps layman)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the French "linguiste" and ultimately from Latin "lingua" (tongue, language) + "-ist" (denoting a person skilled in or occupied with something).
