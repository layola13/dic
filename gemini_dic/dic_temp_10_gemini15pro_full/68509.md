fanaticalness:/fəˈnætɪkəlnəs/| n.|狂热，极端|The fanaticalness of his belief was frightening. 他的信仰的狂热程度令人恐惧。|例句：The fanaticalness of their devotion to the cause was inspiring to some, alarming to others.  他们对事业的狂热奉献鼓舞了一些人，也惊吓了另一些人。

例句：Her fanaticalness about cleanliness bordered on obsessive-compulsive disorder. 她对清洁的狂热近乎强迫症。|近义词：zealotry, extremism, fanaticism|反义词: moderation, apathy, indifference|词性:名词

fanatic (n.) + -al (adj.) + -ness (n.)  From the Latin *fanaticus*, "inspired by a deity, frenzied," also "of a temple," later "insane."
