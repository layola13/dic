opted:/ˈɒptɪd/ /ˈɑːptɪd/
verb
选择，挑选
chose or decided to do something
She opted to study abroad. 她选择出国留学。
He opted for early retirement. 他选择了提前退休。
近义词:chose, selected, decided, picked
反义词:rejected, declined, refused
词性:verb

词源:
From Latin *optāre, frequentative of optāre (“to wish, desire, choose”).
