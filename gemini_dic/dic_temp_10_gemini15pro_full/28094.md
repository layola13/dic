capita:/ˈkæpɪtə/| n.|人均，每人|Latin, from caput "head." Originally meaning "head," it evolved to represent individuals in calculations, thus "per head" or "per person."|The GDP per capita is a measure of a country's economic output per person. 人均GDP衡量一个国家的经济产出。|The library has 50 books per capita. 图书馆人均拥有50本书。|synonyms: per head, per person|antonyms: N/A (It's a unit of measurement, not a concept with an opposite)|noun
