endorses:/ɪnˈdɔːrsɪz/| n.| 赞同，支持；认可；背书；签署（姓名）|(This is the third-person singular present tense form of the verb "endorse.")  这是动词“endorse”的第三人称单数现在时形式。|近义词：support, approval, backing, sanction, recommendation|反义词：opposition, rejection, condemnation, denunciation, criticism|词性:  (动词的第三人称单数形式)

词源: From Old French *endorser, from en- (“in, on”) + dos (“back”), hence literally "to write on the back of".  源自古法语 *endorser，由 en-（“在...上”）+ dos（“背面”）组成，因此字面意思是“在背面书写”。
