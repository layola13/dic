dethrone:/diːˈθroʊn/| v. | 废黜，罢免 | To remove a king or queen from power; to remove someone from a position of authority or dominance. | The king was dethroned and exiled. 国王被废黜并流放了。

The champion was dethroned by the young challenger. 冠军被年轻的挑战者击败了。| 近义词：oust, depose, unseat, remove | 反义词：crown, enthrone, install | 词性：动词
