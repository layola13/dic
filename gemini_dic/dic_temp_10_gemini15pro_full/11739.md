aromatically:/ˌærəˈmætɪkli/
adverb
芳香地，香喷喷地
In a way that has a pleasant and distinctive smell.
The kitchen was filled aromatically with the smell of baking bread.  厨房里充满了烤面包的香味。
The flowers scented the room aromatically.  花朵使房间充满了芳香。
近义词: fragrantly, sweetly, pleasurably
反义词: odorlessly, unpleasantly
词性: adverb

aromatic:/ˌærəˈmætɪk/
adjective
芳香的；芬芳的
Having a pleasant and distinctive smell.
The aromatic herbs filled the garden with a delightful fragrance.  芳香的草本植物使花园充满了宜人的香味。
The coffee was rich and aromatic.  咖啡浓郁而芳香。
近义词: fragrant, sweet-smelling, perfumed
反义词: odorless, unscented
词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *aromaticus, from Latin aroma (“spice, sweet herb”), from Ancient Greek ἄρωμα (árōma, “spice, perfume”).
