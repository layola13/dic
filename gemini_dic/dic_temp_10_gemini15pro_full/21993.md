boil-down:/ˈbɔɪl daʊn/ | phrasal verb | 归结为；浓缩；简化 | To reduce something, usually a liquid, by boiling; to summarize or simplify something. | The chef boiled down the sauce to thicken it.  厨师将酱汁煮浓。|  The whole problem boils down to a lack of communication. 整个问题归结为缺乏沟通。 | synonyms: condense, summarize, reduce, abridge | antonyms: expand, elaborate, lengthen | verb phrase

boil-down:/ˈbɔɪl daʊn/ | noun | 浓缩物；概要；精华 |  The boil-down of the report was that the project was over budget. 报告的概要是项目超出了预算。 |  This is the boil-down of years of research. 这是多年研究的精华。 | synonyms: summary, gist, essence | antonyms: details, full version, unexpurgated version | noun
