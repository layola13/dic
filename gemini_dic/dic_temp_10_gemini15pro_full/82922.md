gumminess:/ˈɡʌminəs/| n.|胶粘性；粘性|The gumminess of the candy made it difficult to chew.  这种糖果的粘性使它很难咀嚼。|例句：The gumminess of the dough was perfect for bread making.  面团的粘性非常适合做面包。

The gumminess of the sealant ensures a tight waterproof seal.  密封胶的粘性确保了紧密的防水密封。|近义词：stickiness, tackiness, adhesiveness|反义词：smoothness, slipperiness|词性:noun

Etymology: From "gummy" + "-ness".  "Gummy" comes from "gum" (referring to a sticky substance), which ultimately derives from the Late Latin "gummi".
