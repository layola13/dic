chaplaincy:/ˈtʃæplɪnsi/
n.
教士的职位;牧师的职位;随军牧师的职位;附属教堂
The office or position of a chaplain.
He accepted the chaplaincy of the college. 他接受了学院牧师的职位。
近义词: chaplainhood
反义词: (无直接反义词)
词性: 名词

chaplaincy:/ˈtʃæplɪnsi/
n.
附属教堂;附属牧师的辖区;附属牧师的职务
A chapel or an institution served by a chaplain.
The chaplaincy serves the spiritual needs of the students.  这附属教堂服务学生的精神需求。
近义词: chapel
反义词: (无直接反义词)
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *chapelainerie*, from Old French *chapelainerie*, from *chapelain* (“chaplain”) + *-erie*. Ultimately from Late Latin *capella* (“chapel”).
