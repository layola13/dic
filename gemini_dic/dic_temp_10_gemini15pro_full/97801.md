injuring: /ˈɪndʒərɪŋ/ | v. | 伤害，损害 |  The act of causing harm or damage to someone or something. |  He was injuring his health by smoking. 他因吸烟损害了自己的健康。 | synonyms: harming, damaging, hurting, wounding, impairing | antonyms: healing, curing, repairing, benefiting | verb (present participle)

injuring: /ˈɪndʒərɪŋ/ | adj. | 造成伤害的 | Causing harm or damage. | The injuring words made her cry. 那些伤人的话让她哭了。| synonyms: harmful, damaging, hurtful | antonyms: helpful, beneficial, healing | adjective (present participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *injuren, from Anglo-Norman enjurer, Old French injurier (“to insult, offend, injure”), from Latin injūriāre (“to act unjustly towards, injure”), from injūria (“injustice, injury, wrong, insult”), from in- (“not”) + jūs (“right, law”) + -ia.
