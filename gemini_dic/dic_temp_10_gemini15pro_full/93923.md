immunises:/ˈɪmjʊnaɪzɪz/ | v. | 使免除，使不受影响 | Protects someone or something from an undesirable influence or effect. |  The company tried to immunise itself against takeover bids.  这家公司试图避免被收购。| Synonyms: shields, protects | Antonyms:  endangers, jeopardizes | verb
