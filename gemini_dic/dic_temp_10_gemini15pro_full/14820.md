axile:/ˈæksaɪl/| adj.|轴生的，与轴相关的|Situated or growing in the angle formed by a leaf or branch with the stem; relating to an axis.|The axile placentation is a characteristic feature of certain plant families. 轴生胎座是某些植物科的特征。|Synonyms: axial, central|Antonyms: peripheral, terminal|adjective
