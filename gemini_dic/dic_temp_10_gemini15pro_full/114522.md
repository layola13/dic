malaise:/məˈleɪz/ /mæˈleɪz/| n. |不适， malaise;不舒服; malaise;心神不安|A general feeling of discomfort, illness, or unease whose exact cause is difficult to identify.|例句：A sense of malaise settled over the crowd. 一种不安的感觉笼罩着人群。

例句：The economic malaise affected the entire country. 经济不景气影响了整个国家。|近义词：uneasiness, discomfort, sickness, illness, lethargy|反义词:well-being, comfort, energy|词性:noun
