critique:/krɪˈtiːk/ | n. vt. |评论，批评；评论文章| A detailed analysis and assessment of something, especially a literary, philosophical, or political theory. | The professor wrote a critique of the student's essay. 教授写了一篇对学生论文的评论。|
critique:/krɪˈtiːk/ | n. vt. |评论，批评；评论文章|  An act of criticizing. |  She offered a critique of the new policy. 她对新政策提出了批评。 | synonyms: analysis, assessment, evaluation, review, commentary, appraisal, judgment, criticism | antonyms: praise, commendation, acclaim | noun, verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *critique*, from Ancient Greek *κριτικός (kritikós)*, meaning "able to discern, judge".
