draki:/ˈdrɑːki/| n.|龙形风筝; (东非民间传说中的)恶龙, 邪灵|A large, often elaborately decorated kite flown in competitions, especially in Indonesia. Also, a mythical dragon or evil spirit in East African folklore.|Example 1: The draki soared high above the beach, its vibrant colours a spectacle against the blue sky.  多彩的龙形风筝在蓝天上翱翔，与蓝天形成鲜明对比，蔚为壮观。

Example 2:  In Swahili folklore, the draki is a fearsome creature of the night. 在斯瓦希里民间传说中，恶龙是一种可怕的夜间生物。|近义词：kite, dragon, monster|反义词: angel, god |词性:noun
