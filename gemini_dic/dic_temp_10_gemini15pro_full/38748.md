condoler:/kənˈdoʊlər/ | n. | 哀悼者，慰问者 |  A person who expresses sympathy or condolences to another person, especially on the death of someone close to them. |  She was a true condoler, offering comfort and support to the grieving family. 她是一位真正的慰问者，为悲痛的家庭提供安慰和支持。 |  sympathizer, consoler |  None | Noun

condole:/kənˈdoʊl/ | v. | 哀悼，慰问 | To express sympathy or sorrow to someone who is grieving, typically for a death. | I condoled with her on the death of her father. 我对她父亲的去世表示哀悼。 |  sympathize, commiserate |  None | Verb
