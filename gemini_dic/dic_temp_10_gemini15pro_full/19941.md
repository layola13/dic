biodegradation:/ˌbaɪoʊˌdɛɡrəˈdeɪʃən/| n. |生物降解|The process by which organic substances are broken down into simpler substances by the action of living organisms, such as bacteria, fungi, or insects.|例句：Biodegradation of plastic is a slow process. 塑料的生物降解是一个缓慢的过程。

例句：The biodegradation of pollutants helps clean up the environment. 污染物的生物降解有助于净化环境。|近义词：decomposition, biodecomposition|反义词:biosynthesis|noun
