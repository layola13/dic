USAGE->

Hoorah! The sun is shining. 好哇！阳光明媚。

Hoorah!  The birds are singing. 好哇！鸟儿在歌唱。

Hoorah! She is graduating! 好哇！她要毕业了！

PHRASE->

(Since "hoorah" is a standalone interjection, it doesn't typically form phrases.  However, it can be used in conjunction with other phrases.)

Hoorah for the weekend!  周末万岁！
Hoorah and a hearty congratulations! 好哇，衷心祝贺！
A big hoorah to the winners! 为获胜者们热烈欢呼！
Three cheers and a hoorah!  三声欢呼和一声好哇！
Let's give them a big hoorah! 让我们为他们大声欢呼！
