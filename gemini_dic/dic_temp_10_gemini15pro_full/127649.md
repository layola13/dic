needs-must:/ˌniːdz ˈmʌst/ | adv. | 必需；不得不 | Expressing the compelling force of necessity; indicating that something must be done, however unwelcome or inconvenient. | Example: Needs must when the devil drives.  魔鬼逼人，不得不做。| Synonym: necessarily, inevitably, unavoidably, perforce | Antonym: optionally, unnecessarily | adverb

needs-must:/ˌniːdz ˈmʌst/ | n. | 必然性；不可避免性 | The state or fact of being necessary or unavoidable. | Example:  It was a matter of needs-must; there was no other choice.  这是必然的，没有其他选择。 | Synonym: necessity, inevitability, compulsion | Antonym: option, choice | noun
