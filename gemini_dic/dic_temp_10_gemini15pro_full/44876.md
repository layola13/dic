cry wolf:/kraɪ wʊlf/| v.| 谎报军情；狼来了；发出假警报|To raise a false alarm; to ask for help when you don't need it, with the result that when you really do need help, people don't believe you.|The boy cried wolf so many times that no one believed him when the wolf really came.  这个男孩多次谎报狼来了，以至于当狼真的来的时候没有人相信他。|近义词: raise a false alarm, exaggerate, lie|反义词: tell the truth, be honest|动词短语

cry wolf:/kraɪ wʊlf/| n.| 谎报军情；狼来了（的行为）|The act of raising a false alarm.|His constant crying wolf made everyone distrust him. 他不断地谎报军情让每个人都不信任他。|近义词: false alarm, hoax|反义词: truth, honesty|名词短语
