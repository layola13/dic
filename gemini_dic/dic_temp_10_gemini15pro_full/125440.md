multiple:/ˈmʌltɪpl/| adj. n.|多样的，多重的；倍数|A multiple is a number that can be divided by another number a certain number of times without a remainder.|She suffered multiple injuries in the accident. 她在事故中多处受伤。|近义词：numerous, many, several, various|反义词：single, sole, individual|词性:形容词

multiple:/ˈmʌltɪpl/| n.|倍数|A multiple is a product of a quantity and an integer.|12 is a multiple of 3. 12是3的倍数。|近义词：product|反义词：factor, divisor|词性:名词
