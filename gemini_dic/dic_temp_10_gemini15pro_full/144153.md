outshine:/ˌaʊtˈʃaɪn/
verb
胜过，优于
To be much more impressive, skillful, or successful than someone or something else.
例句：
He easily outshone his rivals in the competition.
他在比赛中轻松胜过对手。

例句：
Her talent outshines all the others.
她的才华胜过所有其他人。

近义词：surpass, eclipse, excel, outdo, outstrip, overshadow
反义词：pale in comparison, be inferior to
词性：verb
