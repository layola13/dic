fistula:/ˈfɪstʃələ/ (US), /ˈfɪstjʊlə/ (UK)| n.|瘘管；瘭管|An abnormal passage or tube-like opening that connects two organs or cavities, or connects an organ or cavity to the surface of the body.|The patient developed a fistula after the surgery.  这个病人在手术后形成了一个瘘管。|Synonyms: sinus, tract|Antonyms: None|noun

fistulous:/ˈfɪstʃələs/ (US), /ˈfɪstjʊləs/ (UK)| adj.|瘘管的；有瘘管的|Characterized by or having a fistula.|The wound became fistulous and required further treatment.  伤口形成了瘘管，需要进一步治疗。|Synonyms: None|Antonyms: None|adjective
