abayas: /əˈbaɪəz/ /æˈbaɪəz/
noun
阿拉伯罩袍
A long, loose-fitting robe worn by some Muslim women, particularly in the Arabian Peninsula and some parts of North Africa.
Many women choose to wear abayas as a form of modest dress. 许多女性选择穿罩袍作为一种端庄的着装方式。
She wore a beautiful black abaya embroidered with gold thread. 她穿着一件绣着金线的漂亮黑色罩袍。
近义词: jilbab, chador
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) Western clothing
词性: noun
