dotty:/ˈdɒti/ (UK) /ˈdɑːti/ (US)| adj.|古怪的；疯狂的；过分喜爱的；有点精神错乱的|eccentric or slightly mad; excessively fond of someone or something|例句：She's become quite dotty in her old age. 她年纪大了，变得有点古怪。|例句：He's dotty about his granddaughter. 他非常疼爱他的孙女。|近义词：eccentric, crazy, senile, besotted|反义词：sane, lucid|词性:形容词

dotty:/ˈdɒti/ (UK) /ˈdɑːti/ (US)| n.|傻瓜；古怪的人|a silly or eccentric person (informal)|例句：He's a bit of a dotty, but harmless. 他有点傻，但无害。|例句：She's a dotty old woman who lives down the street. 她是一个住在街尾的古怪老太太。|近义词：eccentric, fool|反义词：genius|词性:名词
