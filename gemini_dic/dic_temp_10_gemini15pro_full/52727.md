dijudicant:/daɪˈdʒuːdɪkənt/
adjective
判决的；裁定的
Relating to or serving as a judgment or adjudication, especially in a legal context.
The dijudicant power of the court is essential for a fair trial. 法院的判决权对于公平审判至关重要。
The dijudicant process took several months to complete. 判决过程花了几个月的时间才完成。
近义词: judicial, decretal, determinative
反义词: indecisive, inconclusive
词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin *dijudicant-, present participle stem of dijudicare "to judge between," from di- "apart, between" + judicare "to judge."
