free-standing:/ˌfriːˈstændɪŋ/ | adj. | 独立式的，自立的 | Not attached to another structure or supported by another object; able to stand alone. | The sculpture was free-standing in the middle of the plaza.  这尊雕塑独立地矗立在广场中央。|  independent, self-contained, separate | attached, connected, dependent | adjective

free-standing:/ˌfriːˈstændɪŋ/ | adj. | 独立的，单独存在的（指组织或机构） | Existing independently; not part of a larger organization. | The company operates as a free-standing entity. 这家公司作为一个独立的实体运营。 | independent, autonomous, separate | subsidiary, affiliated, dependent | adjective
