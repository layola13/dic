outfling:/ˈaʊtflɪŋ/ | n. |  迸发；激烈言辞；爆发 | A sudden and forceful outburst or expulsion, especially of emotion or words. | He launched into a passionate outfling against the injustice of the system. 他对体制的不公正发表了激烈的抨击。 | 近义词: outburst, eruption, explosion | 反义词: suppression, restraint | 词性: 名词

outfling:/ˈaʊtflɪŋ/ | v. |  猛掷；用力抛出；迸发；激烈地表达 | To fling or throw out forcefully; to express with sudden vehemence. | She outflung her arms in a gesture of despair. 她绝望地挥舞着手臂。| 近义词: hurl, fling, cast, expel | 反义词: restrain, suppress | 词性: 动词
