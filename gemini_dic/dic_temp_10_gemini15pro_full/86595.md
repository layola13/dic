heliograph:/ˈhiːliəɡrɑːf/ | n. |日光反射信号机|An apparatus for signaling by flashes of sunlight reflected by a mirror. |Example 1: The heliograph was used extensively in the Boer War. 日光反射信号机在布尔战争中被广泛使用。
