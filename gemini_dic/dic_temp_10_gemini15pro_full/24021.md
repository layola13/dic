breechloader:/ˈbriːtʃˌloʊdər/| n.|后膛装填枪|A firearm loaded from the breech (the rear end of the barrel) as opposed to the muzzle.|The breechloader quickly replaced muzzle-loading rifles in the late 19th century.|Synonyms: breech-loading rifle, breech-loading gun|Antonyms: muzzleloader|noun
breech-loading:/ˈbriːtʃ ˈloʊdɪŋ/| adj.|后膛装填的|Describing a firearm that is loaded from the breech.|Breech-loading artillery became the standard for modern armies.|Synonyms: breech-loaded|Antonyms: muzzle-loading|adjective
