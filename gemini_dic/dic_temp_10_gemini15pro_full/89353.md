homology:/hɒˈmɒlədʒi/ /həˈmɑːlədʒi/| n.|同源性，相同，相似|A degree of resemblance or correspondence.|The homology between the forelimbs of mammals and birds suggests a common ancestor. 哺乳动物和鸟类前肢的同源性表明它们有共同的祖先。|近义词：similarity, resemblance, analogy|反义词：heterology, difference|noun

homology:/hɒˈmɒlədʒi/ /həˈmɑːlədʒi/| n.|【数学】同调|A fundamental concept in algebraic topology.|The homology groups of a space capture information about its holes and higher-dimensional voids.  一个空间的同调群捕捉了关于它的孔洞和高维空隙的信息。|近义词：--|反义词：--|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *homologie*, from Greek *homologos* "agreeing, corresponding," from *homos* "same" + *logos* "relation, proportion, ratio; speech, word, reason."
