blue-collars:/ˈbluː ˈkɒlərz/ (UK) /ˈbluː ˈkɑːlərz/ (US)| n.|蓝领工人（复数）|Workers, especially in industry, who do manual work.|例句：Blue-collars are essential to the economy. 蓝领工人对经济至关重要。|近义词：workers, laborers, tradespeople|反义词：white-collar workers|词性：名词

词源：This term originated in the early 20th century, referring to the typically blue denim or chambray shirts worn by manual laborers.  这个词起源于20世纪初，指的是体力劳动者通常穿的蓝色牛仔布或钱布雷衬衫。
