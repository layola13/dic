copyism:/ˈkɒpiɪzəm/| n.|抄袭主义，复制主义|The act or practice of copying, especially in an unoriginal or unauthorized way.  The philosophy that all information should be freely copied and distributed.|The rise of the internet has led to an increase in copyism, with many people sharing copyrighted material without permission. 互联网的兴起导致了抄袭主义的增加，许多人未经许可就分享受版权保护的资料。|近义词：plagiarism, imitation, duplication|反义词：originality, innovation|词性:noun
