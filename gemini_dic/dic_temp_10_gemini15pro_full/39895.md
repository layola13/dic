constitutionalist:/ˌkɒnstɪˈtjuːʃənəlɪst/ /ˌkɑːnstɪˈtuːʃənəlɪst/| n. adj.|宪政主义者；拥护宪法者|A person who strongly supports or advocates for a constitution, especially a particular interpretation of it.|The constitutionalist argued that the new law violated the fundamental principles of the nation.  这位宪政主义者认为，新法律违反了国家的根本原则。|Synonym: constitutional adherent, supporter of the constitution|Antonym:  authoritarian, dictator|noun

constitutionalist:/ˌkɒnstɪˈtjuːʃənəlɪst/ /ˌkɑːnstɪˈtuːʃənəlɪst/| adj.|宪法的；拥护宪法的|Relating to or supporting a constitution or constitutional government.|The constitutionalist movement aimed to limit the power of the monarchy. 宪政运动旨在限制君主的权力。|Synonym:  constitutional|Antonym:  unconstitutional, anti-constitutional|adjective
