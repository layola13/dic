afresh:/əˈfreʃ/| adv.|重新；再一次|Starting afresh after the failure, she was determined to succeed.|She decided to paint the room afresh. 她决定重新粉刷房间。|近义词：anew, again, newly|反义词:old, before|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From a- (Old English) + fresh (Old English fersc).  The prefix "a-" here means "on" or "in," giving the sense of "in a fresh manner."
