nonhedonistically:/ˌnɒnˌhiːdənˈɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 非享乐主义地 |  In a way that does not prioritize pleasure or self-indulgence.  It emphasizes actions motivated by factors other than the pursuit of pleasure. |  She lived nonhedonistically, focusing on service to others. 她过着非享乐主义的生活，专注于服务他人。|  He approached his work nonhedonistically, driven by a sense of duty. 他以非享乐主义的态度对待工作，受责任感驱使。 | 近义词: ascetically, selflessly | 反义词: hedonistically, selfishly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Latin, meaning "not"
hedonistic (adjective): From Greek "hēdonē" (pleasure)
-ally (suffix): Forms adverbs from adjectives
