argument's:/ˈɑːrɡjʊmənts/ | n. | 论点，论据 |  The possessive form of "argument," indicating ownership or association. It refers to something belonging to or associated with an argument. |  The argument's main points were clearly stated.  论证的要点表达得很清楚。| synonyms:  premise, assertion, claim, contention | antonyms: counterargument, rebuttal |  noun (possessive)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English argument, from Old French argument, from Latin argumentum (“proof, evidence, token, argument”), from arguere (“to make clear, prove, declare, argue”).
