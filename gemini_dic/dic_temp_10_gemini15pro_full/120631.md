mis-editing:/ˌmɪsˈɛdɪtɪŋ/
|n.|错误编辑；编辑失误|The article was full of mis-editings and had to be revised. 这篇文章充满了编辑错误，必须修改。|The numerous mis-editings made the document look unprofessional.  大量的编辑错误使文件看起来很不专业。|misprint, typo, error|correct editing, accurate editing|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
mis- (prefix): from Old English "mis-" meaning "wrongly, badly, astray."  表示“错误地，糟糕地，误入歧途地”。
edit (verb): from Latin "editus," past participle of "edere" meaning "to put forth, publish."  源自拉丁语“editus”，是“edere”的过去分词，意思是“提出，出版”。
-ing (suffix): forms the present participle of verbs and also creates nouns from verbs.  构成动词的现在分词，也由动词构成名词。
