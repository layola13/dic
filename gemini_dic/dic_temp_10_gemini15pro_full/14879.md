如果你是想了解疑问词 "what" 的用法，我可以提供相关信息：

what: /wɒt/ (British) /wʌt/ (American) | pronoun, determiner, adverb | 什么 | used to ask for information about something |

例句：
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you want for dinner? = 你晚餐想吃什么？

近义词: which (在有限选项中选择)

反义词:  没有直接的反义词，因为 what 是一个疑问词。


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you doing right now? = 你现在正在做什么？
What is he working on? = 他正在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you done today? = 你今天做了什么？
What has she learned in this class? = 她在这门课上学到了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been doing all afternoon? = 你整个下午都在做什么？
What has he been working on all week? = 他整个星期都在做什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? = 她对这部电影说了些什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when I called? = 我打电话的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 开会的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? = 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? = 她生病前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the power went out? = 停电前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她换专业前一直在学习什么？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天要做什么？
What will she bring to the party? = 她会带什么来参加派对？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be studying next semester? = 她下学期会学习什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have done by the end of the year? = 到年底你将会完成什么？
What will she have learned by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业的时候，她将会学到什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past ten years by 2033? = 到2033年，你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she gets her PhD? = 到她获得博士学位时，她将已经学习了什么多久了？
