nonaccommodable:/ˌnɒnəkɒməˈdeɪtəbl/
adjective
不可调和的，不妥协的
Not willing to compromise or make concessions; inflexible.
The two parties held nonaccommodable positions, making a resolution impossible.  双方立场不可调和，使得解决问题成为不可能。
The union and management remained nonaccommodable, leading to a prolonged strike. 工会和管理层仍然互不让步，导致了长时间的罢工。
synonyms:inflexible, unyielding, intractable, uncompromising, rigid
antonyms:flexible, accommodating, yielding, compromising
词性:adjective
