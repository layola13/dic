nonchargeable:/ˌnɒnˈtʃɑːrʤəbəl/
adjective
不可收费的，免费的
Not subject to a charge or fee; free.
The hotel provides nonchargeable Wi-Fi access to all guests.  酒店向所有客人提供免费的Wi-Fi接入。
Conference calls within the company are nonchargeable. 公司内部的电话会议是免费的。
近义词: free, complimentary, gratis
反义词: chargeable, payable, fee-based
词性: adjective
词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + chargeable (adjective meaning "subject to a charge")
