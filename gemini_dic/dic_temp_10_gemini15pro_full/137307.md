nontabulated:/nɒnˈtæbjʊleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 未列入表格的；未制成表的 | Not arranged or recorded in a table or list. | The raw data was nontabulated, making it difficult to analyze. 原始数据未制成表格，难以分析。 | The survey results were nontabulated and presented as a narrative.  调查结果未制成表格，而是以叙述方式呈现。 | unlisted, unrecorded, untabulated | tabulated, listed, recorded | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + tabulated.  "Tabulated" comes from the Latin "tabula" meaning "table" or "tablet".
