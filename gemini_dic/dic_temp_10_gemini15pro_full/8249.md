词源:
"anti-" (prefix): from Greek "anti" meaning "against," "opposite of"
"inductive": from Latin "inducere" meaning "to lead in," referring to the phenomenon of electromagnetic induction.
"-ness" (suffix): forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
