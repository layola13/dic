interocean:/ˌɪntərˈoʊʃən/ | adj. | 海洋间的，大洋间的 | Situated between oceans; connecting oceans. | Example: Interocean canals facilitate trade and travel. 海洋间运河促进了贸易和旅行。| Example: The interocean currents affect global climate patterns. 洋流影响全球气候模式。| 近义词: transoceanic | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "coastal" or "continental") | 词性: adjective
词源: From inter- + ocean.  "Inter-" is a Latin prefix meaning "between" or "among."
