hailers:/ˈheɪlərz/| n.|欢呼者，高呼者，招呼者|People who hail someone or something, often expressing approval or greeting.  Those who shout a greeting or call to attract attention.|The hailers cheered as the ship returned to port.|近义词：greeters, cheerers, callers, summoners|反义词:critics, dissenters|noun
