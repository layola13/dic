almendrón:/ˌalmenˈdron/ | n. | 古董车，老爷车（尤指古巴的美国老式汽车） | A vintage car, especially an old American car in Cuba. |例句：We took a ride in a colorful almendrón through Havana. 我们乘坐一辆色彩鲜艳的古董车游览了哈瓦那。

例句：The almendrones are a symbol of Cuba's resilience. 这些老爷车是古巴人民坚韧不拔的象征。|近义词：classic car, vintage car, antique car |反义词: modern car, new car |词性:noun

Etymology: From Spanish *almendrón*, augmentative of *almendra* ("almond"), likely referring to the car's shape.
