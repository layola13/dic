nonscoring:/ˌnɒnˈskɔːrɪŋ/ | adj. | 非评分的，不计分的 | Not contributing to or affecting a score.  Relating to a test, activity, or competition where performance is not evaluated with points or grades. | The students took a nonscoring practice test to familiarize themselves with the format. 学生们参加了一次不计分的练习测试，以熟悉考试形式。

The coach emphasized the importance of nonscoring contributions like passing and defense. 教练强调了传球和防守等不计分贡献的重要性。 | synonyms: non-competitive, practice, informal | antonyms: scoring, competitive, graded | adjective
