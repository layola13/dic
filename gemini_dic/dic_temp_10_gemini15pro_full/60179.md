effetely:/ɪˈfɛktli/| adv.|无力地，衰弱地，颓废地|In a manner that is depleted of vitality, strength, or effectiveness; marked by weakness, decay, or decadence.  Derived from "effete."|He effetely waved his hand, unable to summon the strength to gesture more forcefully. 他无力地挥了挥手，无法鼓起力气做出更有力的姿势。|Synonyms: weakly, feebly, languidly, powerlessly|Antonyms: strongly, vigorously, powerfully|Adverb
