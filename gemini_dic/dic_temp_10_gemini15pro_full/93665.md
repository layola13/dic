imbuements:/ɪmˈbjuːmənts/
n.
灌输；充满；浸染
The act of imbuing or the state of being imbued; permeation or impregnation with qualities or feelings.
His writings are filled with imbuements of ancient wisdom. 他的著作充满了古老智慧的浸染。
近义词:infusions, instillations, impregnations, permeations
反义词:depletions, drainings
