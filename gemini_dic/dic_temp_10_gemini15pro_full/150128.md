pamphleteer:/ˌpæmfləˈtɪr/| n.|小册子作者；抨击者|A writer of pamphlets, especially one who attacks established beliefs or institutions. |The political pamphleteer distributed his work on street corners. 这位政治小册子作者在街角分发他的作品。|Example 2:  The anonymous pamphleteer stirred up controversy with accusations of corruption.  这位匿名的小册子作者以腐败指控引发了争议。|近义词：polemicist, propagandist, agitator|反义词:apologist, defender|noun
