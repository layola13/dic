elmont: /ˈɛlmɒnt/ (US) /ˈɛlmɒnt/ (UK)| n. |埃尔蒙特（地名）| Elmont is a hamlet and census-designated place (CDP) located in Nassau County, New York, United States.  It is considered part of the Town of Hempstead. | Elmont is famous for the Belmont Stakes horse race. 埃尔蒙特以贝尔蒙特锦标赛赛马而闻名。| Elmont is a suburban community. 埃尔蒙特是一个郊区社区。|synonyms: village, town, suburb, community | antonyms: city, metropolis | noun
