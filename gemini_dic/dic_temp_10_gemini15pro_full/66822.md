expansively:/ɪkˈspænsɪvli/
adverb
广泛地，广阔地；开朗地，豪爽地
in a way that covers a large area; in a way that shows generous and unrestrained enthusiasm, esp. by spending freely.
He smiled expansively and invited us to sit. 他笑容满面地邀请我们坐下。
The city sprawls expansively across the plain. 这座城市向平原延伸开来，非常辽阔。
近义词: broadly, widely, generously, lavishly, extensively
反义词: narrowly, restrictively, stingily, meanly
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From Late Latin *expansīvus, from Latin expansus (past participle of expandere, "to spread out") + -īvus.
