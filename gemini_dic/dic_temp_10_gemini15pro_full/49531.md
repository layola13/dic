demarcating:/diːˈmɑːrkeɪtɪŋ/
Verb
划界；标明界限
Marking the boundary or limits of something.
The surveyor is demarcating the property lines. 测量员正在标明地产界线。
Synonyms: delimiting, separating, distinguishing
Antonyms: merging, combining, uniting
词性: 动词

demarcate:/diːˈmɑːrkeɪt/
Verb
划界；标明界限
To set the boundaries or limits of something.
The new fence demarcates the boundary between the two properties.  新的栅栏标明了两处房产之间的界限。
Synonyms: delimit, separate, distinguish
Antonyms: merge, combine, unite
词性: 动词

demarcation:/ˌdiːmɑːrˈkeɪʃn/
Noun
划界；界限
The action of fixing the boundary or limits of something.
The demarcation of the new territories led to several disputes. 新领土的划界导致了几起争端。
Synonyms: delimitation, separation, distinction
Antonyms: merger, combination, union
词性: 名词
