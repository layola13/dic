flimsy:/ˈflɪmzi/ | adj. | 薄弱的，脆弱的；不牢靠的；站不住脚的；轻薄的 | Easily damaged or broken; not strongly made; weak and thin.  A flimsy excuse or argument is weak and unconvincing. | The evidence against him was pretty flimsy.  针对他的证据相当薄弱。| synonyms:fragile, weak, thin, slight, insubstantial, feeble | antonyms:strong, sturdy, robust, solid, substantial | adjective

flimsy:/ˈflɪmzi/ | n. | 薄纸；复写纸 | A thin sheet of paper, especially one used for making a carbon copy. |  She scribbled a note on a flimsy. 她在一张薄纸上草草地写了一张便条。| synonyms:tissue paper, thin paper | antonyms:cardboard, thick paper | noun
