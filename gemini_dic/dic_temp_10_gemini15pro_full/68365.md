fallibility:/ˌfælɪˈbɪləti/| n. |易错性，出错性|The quality of being fallible; the possibility of making mistakes or being wrong. |例句：Human fallibility is something we all have to accept. 我们都必须接受人类的易错性。

例句：The scientist acknowledged the fallibility of his theory.  这位科学家承认了他的理论的易错性。


近义词：imperfection, weakness, flaw, error-prone nature|反义词:infallibility, impeccability, perfection|词性:noun
