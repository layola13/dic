mirabilis:/ˌmɪrəˈbɪlɪs/| adj.|奇妙的，不可思议的|Of a remarkable nature; wonderful, extraordinary, marvelous.  Derived from Latin mīrābilis, from mīror "to wonder at."  Often used in botanical contexts.|The Mirabilis jalapa, also known as the four o'clock flower, displays a mirabilis array of colors.  四点钟花，也被称为紫茉莉，展现出一系列奇妙的色彩。|Synonyms: wondrous, amazing, astonishing, marvelous, extraordinary|Antonyms: ordinary, commonplace, mundane|adjective
