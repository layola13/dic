defenseman:/dɪˈfɛnsmən/| n. |防守员|A player on a sports team, especially in ice hockey, whose primary role is to prevent the opposing team from scoring.|例句：The defenseman blocked the shot and saved the game.  防守员挡住了射门，挽救了比赛。

例句：He's a skilled defenseman known for his powerful slapshot. 他是一位技术娴熟的防守员，以其强力射门而闻名。|近义词：defender, back|反义词:forward, attacker|词性:noun
