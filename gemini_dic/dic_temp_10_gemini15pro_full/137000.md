nonspiritualnesses:/nɒnˈspɪrɪtʃuəlnəsɪz/
noun
非精神性；缺乏灵性
The state or quality of being nonspiritual; lack of spirituality.
His nonspiritualnesses surprised those who thought him a devout man. 他缺乏灵性让那些认为他虔诚的人感到惊讶。
Near Synonyms: worldliness, secularism, materialism
Antonyms: spirituality, religiousness
