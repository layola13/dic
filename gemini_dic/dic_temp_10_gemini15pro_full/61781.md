emperished:/ɪmˈpɛrɪʃt/
adjective
衰败的，破败的；贫困的
damaged, deteriorated, or decayed; especially of fabric. Also impoverished or reduced to poverty.
The emperished building was deemed unsafe. 这栋破败的建筑被认为不安全。
近义词: dilapidated, ruined, deteriorated, decayed, impoverished
反义词:  prosperous, thriving, flourishing, intact

词性: adjective
