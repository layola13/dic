champer's:/ˈtʃeɪmbərz/ | n. | chambers |  A judge's private room; a lawyer's or barrister's office; a legislative or judicial body.  Also, (archaic) a room, especially a bedroom. | The barrister returned to his chambers after the trial.  律师在审判后回到了他的办公室。 |  The king summoned the advisors to his chambers. 国王召见顾问到他的房间。| synonyms: office, room, compartment, chamber | antonyms: hall, corridor | noun
