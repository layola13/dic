noncommodious:/ˌnɒnkəˈməʊdiəs/
adjective
不宽敞的，狭小的
Not spacious; lacking ample space or room.
The noncommodious apartment felt cramped with all of our furniture.  这套不宽敞的公寓摆满我们的家具后感觉很拥挤。
The attic room was dark and noncommodious. 阁楼房间又黑又窄。
synonym: cramped, confined, small, restricted, tight
antonym: spacious, roomy, commodious, large, ample
词性: 形容词
