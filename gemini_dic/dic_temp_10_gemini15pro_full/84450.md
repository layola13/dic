halogen:/ˈhælədʒən/ /ˈheɪlədʒən/| n.|卤素|A chemical element that forms a salt by direct union with a metal.  A nonmetallic univalent element (fluorine, chlorine, bromine, iodine, and astatine) that readily forms negative ions.|Fluorine, chlorine, and bromine are halogens. 氟、氯和溴都是卤素。|近义词:none (as a class of elements)|反义词:none (as a class of elements)|词性:noun
