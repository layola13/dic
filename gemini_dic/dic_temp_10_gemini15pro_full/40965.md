cooperators:/kəˈɒpəreɪtəz/ (UK), /koʊˈɑːpəreɪtərz/ (US)| n. |合作者，合作人|People who work together to achieve a common goal. |The cooperators in the project shared the workload and the credit.|近义词：collaborators, partners, allies|反义词：competitors, rivals|词性:名词

Etymology:
From the verb "cooperate" + "-or" (suffix forming agent nouns).
"Cooperate" comes from Late Latin "cooperari," from "co-" (together) + "operari" (to work).
"Operari" is related to "opus" (work).
