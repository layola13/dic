nonhereditarily:/ˌnɑːnhəˈrɛdɪtɛrɪli/
|adv.| 非遗传地；非世袭地|Not in a hereditary manner; not passed down from one generation to the next.| This trait appears nonhereditarily, suggesting environmental factors are at play.  这种特征似乎是非遗传性的，表明环境因素在起作用。| Example: The disease manifests nonhereditarily, meaning it's not passed down through genes.  这种疾病是非遗传性的，意味着它不是通过基因遗传的。 |Synonym: non-inheritable, acquired|Antonym: hereditarily, genetically|adverb
