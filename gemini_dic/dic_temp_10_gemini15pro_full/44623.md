crowed:/kraʊd/| v. | 挤满；拥挤；聚集；涌入；逼近；催促；（想法）涌上心头；自吹自擂 |To fill a space with too many people or things; to gather together in large numbers.|The fans crowed around the stage door.  粉丝们挤在舞台门口。|近义词：throng, swarm, pack, cram, crowd |反义词: disperse, scatter |词性:动词


crowed:/kraʊdɪd/| adj. | 拥挤的；挤满的；充满的 |Full of people or things.|The streets were crowded with shoppers. 街上挤满了购物者。|近义词：packed, jammed, congested |反义词: empty, deserted |词性:形容词


词源:
From Old English *crūdan, from Proto-Germanic *krōdōną (“to press, urge”). Cognate with Old Norse krydda, Old High German krūdon, Middle Dutch crooden (“to press, load”), Dutch kruien (“to wheelbarrow, push”), Old Frisian krūda (“to push”), German kräuden (“to scratch, pick, stir”).
