outflank:/ˌaʊtˈflæŋk/| vt.|包抄，迂回，智胜|To go around the side of an enemy or opponent, especially in order to attack them from behind; to gain an advantage over someone by being cleverer or more skillful.|The army outflanked the enemy and attacked them from the rear.  军队包抄了敌人，从后方发动了攻击。|近义词：bypass, circumvent, outmaneuver, surpass|反义词:confront, face|动词

outflank:/ˌaʊtˈflæŋk/| vt.|包抄，迂回，智胜|To surpass or get the better of by superior tactics or strategy.|The company outflanked its competitors by offering a better product at a lower price.  这家公司通过以更低的价格提供更好的产品来智胜竞争对手。|近义词：outwit, outsmart|反义词:be outmaneuvered, be outsmarted|动词
