ETYMOLOGY->
From abnormal + -ness.  "abnormal" comes from Late Latin abnormalis "irregular," from Latin ab- "away from" (see ab-) + normalis "in accordance with rule."  "-ness" is an Old English suffix forming nouns meaning "state or quality of being."
