break-even:/breɪkˈiːvən/ | n. | 盈亏平衡点；保本点 | The point at which total cost and total revenue are equal, so there is no profit or loss. | The company reached break-even in the second quarter.  这家公司在第二季度达到了盈亏平衡。| synonyms:  cost recovery point, profitability threshold | antonyms: loss, profit | noun

break-even:/breɪkˈiːvən/ | adj. | 盈亏平衡的；保本的 | Relating to the point at which revenue equals costs. |  The project is expected to be break-even within two years.  预计该项目将在两年内实现盈亏平衡。| synonyms: cost-neutral, no-profit-no-loss | antonyms: profitable, loss-making | adjective
