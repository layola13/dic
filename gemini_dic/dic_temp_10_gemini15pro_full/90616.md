humanness:/ˈhjuːmənɪs/
n.
人性, 人类, 人道
The quality of being human; kindness, compassion.
He showed his humanness by helping the injured stranger.  他通过帮助受伤的陌生人展现了他的人性。
Synonyms: humanity, compassion, empathy, kindness, benevolence
Antonyms: inhumanity, cruelty, barbarity
