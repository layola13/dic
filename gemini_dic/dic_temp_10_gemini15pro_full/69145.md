favouredly:/ˈfeɪvərdli/ | adv. | 受欢迎地；有利地；赞许地 | In a way that shows approval or support; advantageously. |  He spoke favouredly of the proposal. 他赞许地说起这个提议。| 近义词: approvingly, favorably, kindly | 反义词: unfavorably, disapprovingly | 词性: 副词

favouredly:/ˈfeɪvərdli/ | adv. | 偏袒地；偏爱地 |  Showing or giving preference to one person or thing over another. | The judge was accused of acting favouredly towards the defendant.  法官被指控偏袒被告。| 近义词: partially, biasedly | 反义词: impartially, fairly | 词性: 副词


词源:  From "favoured" (past participle of "favour") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Favour" comes from Old French "favor," ultimately from Latin "favor" (goodwill, kindness).
