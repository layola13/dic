argy-bargy:/ˈɑːrdʒi ˈbɑːrdʒi/
noun
争吵，口角
A noisy quarrel or disagreement about something unimportant.
例句：The children were having a bit of an argy-bargy over who got the last cookie. 孩子们正在为谁拿到最后一块饼干而争吵。

例句：They had a little argy-bargy about the bill. 他们就账单发生了一点口角。

synonym
quarrel, argument, squabble, dispute, tiff, wrangle, spat
antonym
agreement, harmony, concord, peace
