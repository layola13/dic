esotericist:/ˌɛsəˈtɛrɪsɪst/
n.
神秘主义者；奥秘学者
A person who studies or is devoted to esoteric matters.
例句：The esotericist delved into ancient texts seeking hidden knowledge.  这位神秘主义者钻研古代文献，寻求隐藏的知识。

例句：Only a true esotericist could understand the complex symbolism. 只有真正的奥秘学者才能理解这复杂的象征意义。

近义词：mystic, occultist, gnostic
反义词：exotericist, pragmatist
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From esoteric + -ist.  Esoteric from Greek esōterikos "inner," from esōterō "further in" (comparative of esō "within"), related to en "in."
