halftones:/ˈhɑːftoʊnz/ (US), /ˈhɑːftoʊnz/ (UK)| n. |半色调|A printing technique that simulates continuous tone imagery through the use of dots, varying either in size or in spacing.|The newspaper photo was printed in halftones.|Synonyms: screen tints, mezzotints|Antonyms: continuous tones|Noun

halftone:/ˈhɑːftoʊn/ (US), /ˈhɑːftoʊn/ (UK)| adj. |半色调的|Relating to or using halftones.|The halftone image was grainy but recognizable.|Synonyms: screened|Antonyms: continuous tone|Adjective
