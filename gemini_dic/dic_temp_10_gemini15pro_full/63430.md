entreat:/ɪnˈtriːt/ /ɛnˈtriːt/
verb
恳求；乞求
To ask someone earnestly or anxiously to do something.
He entreated her to stay, but she was determined to leave. 他恳求她留下，但她决心要离开。

She entreated the judge for mercy. 她乞求法官开恩。

近义词: beg, implore, beseech, plead, supplicate
反义词: demand, command, order
词性: verb
