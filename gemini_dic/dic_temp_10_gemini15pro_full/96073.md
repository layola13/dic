indects: /ɪnˈdɛkts/ | v. |  指示；表明；象征 |  (Usually "indicates") Shows, signifies, points to.  This word appears to be a misspelling of "indicates," the third-person singular present tense form of the verb "indicate." |  The data indects (indicates) a strong correlation between the two variables. 数据表明这两个变量之间有很强的相关性。 | 近义词: indicates, shows, signifies, suggests, implies | 反义词: hides, obscures, conceals | 词性: verb (likely misspelled)


词源: From Late Latin *indicātus, past participle of indicāre (“to point out, show”), from in- (“in, at, on”) + dicāre (“to proclaim, say”).
