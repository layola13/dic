deauthorizing:/diːˈɔːθəˌraɪzɪŋ/
verb
取消授权，撤销授权
The act of revoking or removing someone's authorization or permission to do something.
He is deauthorizing all third-party apps from accessing his account.  他正在取消所有第三方应用访问他帐户的授权。
The company is deauthorizing the use of its software on certain devices. 该公司正在撤销在某些设备上使用其软件的授权。
Synonyms: revoking, rescinding, canceling, withdrawing
Antonyms: authorizing, granting, permitting
