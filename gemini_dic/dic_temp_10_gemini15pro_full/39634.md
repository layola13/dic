consequentialities: /ˌkɒnsɪˌkwɛnʃiˈælɪtiz/ /ˌkɑːnsɪˌkwɛnʃiˈælɪtiz/ | n. |后果，重要性| the importance or relevance of something, especially its implications.|The consequentialities of such a decision could be far-reaching.  这样的决定的后果可能是深远的。|Synonyms: implications, ramifications, repercussions, outcomes|Antonyms: insignificance, triviality|noun


consequence: /ˈkɒnsɪkwəns/ /ˈkɑːnsɪkwɛns/ | n. |结果，后果| a result or effect of an action or condition.|The consequence of her actions was a loss of trust. 她行为的后果是失去了信任。|Synonyms: result, outcome, effect, aftermath|Antonyms: cause, origin|noun
