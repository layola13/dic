immediately:/ɪˈmiːdiətli/ | adv. |立即，马上|at once; without delay|例句：I need to see you immediately. 我需要马上见你。|近义词：instantly, directly, promptly, at once|反义词: later, eventually|词性:副词
词源: From Latin *immediatus*, from *in-* (not) + *mediatus* (mediated, being in the middle).
