gushing:/ˈɡʌʃɪŋ/| adj.|涌出的；喷涌的；滔滔不绝的；过分热情的|Flowing forth suddenly and in great quantity; expressing oneself effusively.|The pipe was gushing water. 管道里喷涌而出水。|The wound was gushing blood.  伤口血流如注。|She was gushing about her new boyfriend. 她滔滔不绝地谈论着她的新男友。|近义词：flowing, spouting, effusive, enthusiastic|反义词：trickling, restrained, reserved|词性:形容词

gushing:/ˈɡʌʃɪŋ/| n.|涌出；喷射|A sudden outpouring.|There was a gushing of blood from the wound. 伤口处涌出鲜血。|The gushing of the oil well was spectacular.  油井的喷涌景象十分壮观。|近义词：outpouring, eruption, surge|反义词：drip, trickle|词性:名词


词源: From the verb "gush," which comes from the Middle English *guschen*, probably of imitative origin.
