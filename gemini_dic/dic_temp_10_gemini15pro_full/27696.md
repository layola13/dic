candlewickings: /ˈkændəlˌwɪkɪŋz/
noun
烛芯
The threads of cotton or other fibrous material that form the wick of a candle.
The candlewickings were too short, causing the candle to burn out quickly. 烛芯太短了，导致蜡烛很快就烧完了。
