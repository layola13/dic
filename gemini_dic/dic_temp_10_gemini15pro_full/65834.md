evincing:/ɪˈvɪnsɪŋ/
verb, adjective
表明; 显示
revealing clearly; indicating
Her smile was evincing genuine happiness. 她的笑容表明她是真的开心。
synonyms: demonstrating, exhibiting, manifesting, displaying, showing
antonyms: concealing, hiding, masking
verb
