clamberers:/ˈklæmərərz/
n.
攀登者；攀缘植物
People or plants that clamber.
The clamberers reached the summit after a challenging climb. 攀登者们在一次具有挑战性的攀登后到达了顶峰。
The roses, avid clamberers, quickly covered the trellis. 这些玫瑰，是热衷的攀缘植物，迅速覆盖了格子架。
近义词: climbers, scramblers, vines
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 descenders 下降者)
词性: 名词

clamber:/ˈklæmbər/
v.
攀登，爬
To climb with difficulty, using both hands and feet.
We clambered over the rocks to reach the beach. 我们爬过岩石到达海滩。
The children clambered up the tree. 孩子们爬上了树。
近义词: climb, scramble, scale
反义词: descend
词性: 动词
