goldilocks: /ˈɡoʊldɪlɑːks/ | n. adj.|金发姑娘;  恰到好处的事物 |A girl with golden hair; something that is just right, not too much or too little.|例句：The porridge was goldilocks, not too hot and not too cold.  这粥的温度刚刚好，不冷不热。|近义词：perfect, ideal, just right|反义词：extreme, excessive, insufficient|词性: 名词, 形容词
Etymology: From the fairy tale "Goldilocks and the Three Bears," where the main character, a young girl with golden hair, finds the bears' porridge, chairs, and beds, choosing the ones that are "just right."
