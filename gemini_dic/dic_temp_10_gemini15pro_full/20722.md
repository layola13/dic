blame:/bleɪm/| n. vt.|责备，责怪；责任，过失| assigning responsibility for a fault or wrong |例句：He blamed me for the accident. 他把事故的责任归咎于我。

|近义词：reproach, censure, condemn, fault, criticize |反义词: praise, credit, commend |noun, verb

blame:/bleɪm/| n. vt.|责备，责怪；责任，过失| the act of attributing responsibility for a fault or wrong |例句：She accepted the blame for the mistake. 她承担了错误的责任。


|近义词：responsibility, guilt, culpability|反义词: innocence, merit |noun, verb
