hyperscale:/ˈhaɪpərˌskeɪl/| adj. n.|超大规模的，超大规模|Relating to or denoting computing and data storage systems of very large or massive scale, typically achieved by the use of cloud computing.|Many companies are now investing in hyperscale data centers. 许多公司现在都在投资超大规模数据中心。|近义词：massive, large-scale, enormous, immense|反义词:small-scale, limited|词性:形容词, 名词


Etymology:
From hyper- + scale.  "Hyper-" as a prefix means "over, beyond, excessive," coming from Greek "hyper" (over, above). "Scale" refers to size or extent.
