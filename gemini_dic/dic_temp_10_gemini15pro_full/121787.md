|n.|错误的回忆；记错|An incorrect memory; a false recollection.|My misrecollection of the event caused some confusion. 我对这件事的错误回忆引起了一些混乱。|Synonyms: misremembering, lapse of memory, inaccuracy|Antonyms: remembrance, recollection, accuracy|Noun
