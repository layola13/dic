engorgement:/ɪnˈɡɔːrdʒmənt/
n.|充血，肿胀；充满|The engorgement of the blood vessels caused the area to swell. 血管充血导致该区域肿胀。|例句：Breast engorgement is common in new mothers. 新妈妈的乳房充血很常见。

例句：The river was engorged with floodwater. 河水因洪水而暴涨。|近义词：swelling, distension, congestion|反义词:shrinking, contraction|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: engorgement, from Old French engorgement, from engorger ‘to gorge, cram, stuff’, from en- ‘in’ + gorge ‘throat’.
