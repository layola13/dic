lingala: /lɪŋˈɡɑːlə/ | n. | 林加拉语 | A Bantu language spoken in the northwestern part of the Democratic Republic of the Congo and a large part of the Republic of the Congo, as well as some parts of Angola and Central African Republic. It is one of the four national languages of the Democratic Republic of the Congo. |  Many people in Kinshasa speak Lingala.  金沙萨的许多人说林加拉语。| synonyms: None (as it's a specific language) | antonyms: None (as it's a specific language) | noun
