emulsions:/ɪˈmʌlʃənz/
n.
乳状液; 乳浊液
A mixture of two or more liquids that are normally immiscible (unmixable or unblendable).
例句：The salad dressing was a simple emulsion of oil and vinegar. 沙拉酱是一种简单的油和醋的乳浊液。

例句：Milk is an emulsion of fat in water. 牛奶是脂肪在水中的乳浊液。


近义词：mixture, suspension, colloid
反义词:solution
词性:名词

例句：Photographic film is coated with a light-sensitive emulsion. 摄影胶片上涂有一层感光乳剂。

例句：The pharmacist prepared an emulsion of cod liver oil. 药剂师配制了鳕鱼肝油乳剂。
