maccabees:/ˈmækəˌbiz/| n.|马加比家族，马加比战士|Jewish family, also known as the Hasmoneans, who led a rebellion against the Seleucid Empire in the 2nd century BCE, resulting in the rededication of the Second Temple in Jerusalem, an event commemorated by the festival of Hanukkah.|The Maccabees fought valiantly for their religious freedom. 马加比家族为他们的宗教自由英勇奋战。|The story of the Maccabees is one of courage and resilience. 马加比家族的故事是一个关于勇气和韧性的故事。|Synonyms: Hasmoneans|Antonyms: N/A|noun

maccabean:/ˌmækəˈbiːən/| adj. n.|马加比的；马加比家族成员|Relating to the Maccabees, the Jewish family who led a rebellion in the 2nd century BCE.  Also can refer to a member of the Maccabees.|The Maccabean revolt is a significant event in Jewish history. 马加比起义是犹太历史上的一件大事。|Judas Maccabaeus was a great Maccabean leader.  犹大·马加比是一位伟大的马加比领袖。|Synonyms: Hasmonean|Antonyms: N/A|adjective, noun
