ideates:/ˈaɪdieɪts/
verb
形成思想；构思；设想
form ideas or concepts.
例句：The team ideates new marketing strategies every quarter.  团队每个季度都会构思新的营销策略。

例句：She ideates best in a quiet environment.  她在安静的环境中更容易想出好点子。

近义词：brainstorm, conceive, generate, imagine, innovate, invent
反义词：plagiarize, copy, imitate
词性：v.
