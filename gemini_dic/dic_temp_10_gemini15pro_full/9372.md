antiquarian:/ˌæn.tɪˈkweə.ri.ən/
n.|古董收藏家；古物研究者|A person who studies or collects antiques or antiquities.|He was a renowned antiquarian with a vast collection of Roman artifacts. 他是一位著名的古董收藏家，收藏了大量的罗马文物。|近义词: collector, connoisseur, archaeologist |反义词: modernist |词性: 名词

antiquarian:/ˌæn.tɪˈkweə.ri.ən/
adj.|古董的；古文物研究的|Relating to or dealing in antiques or antiquities.|She had an antiquarian interest in medieval manuscripts. 她对中世纪的手稿有研究的兴趣。|近义词: ancient, antique, old |反义词: modern, contemporary |词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *antiquarius*, from Latin antiquus (“old, ancient”).
