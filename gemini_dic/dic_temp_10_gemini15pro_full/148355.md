overspeculative: /ˌoʊvərˈspɛkjʊlətɪv/ | adj. | 过度投机的；过度推测的 | Characterized by excessive speculation or conjecture, often without sufficient evidence or justification. |  The overspeculative nature of the market led to the crash.  市场过度投机的性质导致了崩盘。| 近义词：reckless, rash, imprudent, speculative | 反义词：cautious, prudent, conservative | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "speculative" (from Late Latin speculativus, from speculare "to observe," from specula "watchtower").
