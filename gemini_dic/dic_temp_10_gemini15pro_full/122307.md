mittened:/ˈmɪtənd/| adj.|戴着手套的，像手套的|Wearing mittens; resembling a mitten.  Covered or protected as if by a mitten.|例句：Her mittened hands were warm despite the cold. 即使天气寒冷，她戴着手套的双手也很温暖。

例句：The cat's paws looked mittened because of their white fur.  这只猫的爪子因为白色的毛看起来像戴着手套一样。|近义词：gloved|反义词:bare-handed, ungloved|词性:形容词
词源: From mitten + -ed.  Ultimately from Old French mitaine.
