bloating:/ˈbloʊtɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbləʊtɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|腹胀，肿胀|A feeling of swelling in the abdomen, often caused by gas or fluid retention.|After eating too much, I experienced uncomfortable bloating. 吃太多后，我感到不舒服的腹胀。|近义词：swelling, distension, inflation|反义词: deflation, shrinking|noun

bloated:/ˈbloʊtɪd/ (US) /ˈbləʊtɪd/ (UK)| adj.|肿胀的，臃肿的|Swollen with fluid or gas.|My stomach felt bloated after the large meal.  吃完大餐后，我的肚子感觉胀胀的。|近义词：swollen, puffy, distended|反义词: deflated, shrunken|adjective
