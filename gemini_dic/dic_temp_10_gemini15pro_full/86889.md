hemisphere:/ˈhemɪsfɪr/ (US) /ˈhemɪsfɪə/ (UK)| n.|半球|One half of the earth, sphere, or brain.|例句：The Northern Hemisphere is tilted towards the sun in summer. 北半球在夏天向太阳倾斜。

|例句：The left hemisphere of the brain controls the right side of the body. 左脑控制身体的右侧。|近义词：side, half|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

hemisphere:/ˈhemɪsfɪr/ (US) /ˈhemɪsfɪə/ (UK)| n.|范围，领域|A part or division of a field or sphere of activity.|例句：The company is a dominant force in the financial hemisphere. 该公司在金融领域占据主导地位。

|例句：The conference attracted experts from all hemispheres of academia. 会议吸引了来自学术界各个领域的专家。|近义词：domain, area, field|反义词: N/A|词性:noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *hēmisphaerium, from Ancient Greek ἡμισφαίριον (hēmisphaírion, “hemisphere”), from ἡμι- (hēmi-, “half”) + σφαῖρα (sphaîra, “sphere, ball”).
