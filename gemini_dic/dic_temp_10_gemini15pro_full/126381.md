myrmidon:/ˈmɜːrmɪdən/ | n. | 军蚁; 喽啰; 忠实的追随者; 唯命是从的人 | A loyal follower, especially one who executes orders without question or moral qualms.  A soldier of Achilles in the Trojan War. |  Example: The dictator surrounded himself with myrmidons who carried out his every whim.  独裁者身边都是唯命是从的喽啰，为他执行各种奇思妙想。

Example:  Like myrmidons, the workers toiled tirelessly in the factory. 工人们像蚂蚁一样不知疲倦地在工厂里辛苦劳作。 | Synonyms: follower, minion, henchman, lackey, subordinate | Antonyms: leader, rebel, dissenter | noun
