crustaceous:/krʌˈsteɪʃəs/
| adj. |甲壳类的；有甲壳的|Relating to or belonging to the class Crustacea, a large group of mainly aquatic arthropods with hard, segmented bodies, including crabs, lobsters, shrimps, and barnacles. |例句：Lobsters are crustaceous animals. 龙虾是甲壳类动物。|例句：The beach was littered with crustaceous debris. 海滩上到处是甲壳类动物的残骸。|近义词：shellfish, shelled |反义词: (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑用 non-crustacean 非甲壳类的) |词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "crustaceus" (having a crust or shell), from "crusta" (crust, shell). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European "*kreus-" (hard covering).
