dextrously:/ˈdɛkstrəsli/| adv.|灵巧地，熟练地|In a skillful and clever way, especially in handling things.|She dextrously maneuvered the car through the narrow streets. 她熟练地驾驶着汽车穿过狭窄的街道。|近义词：skillfully, adroitly, adeptly, nimbly|反义词：clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly|词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *dexter* (right, right-handed, skillful) + *-osus* (forming adjectives meaning "full of") + *-ly* (adverbial suffix). The connection with "right" comes from the observation that most people are right-handed and thus more skillful with their right hand.
