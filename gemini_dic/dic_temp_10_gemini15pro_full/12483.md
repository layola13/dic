aspen:/ˈæspən/ (US), /ˈæspɪn/ (UK)| n.|白杨，山杨|A type of poplar tree with leaves that tremble in the slightest breeze.|The aspen leaves quivered in the gentle wind. 白杨树叶在微风中颤抖。|The slopes were covered with aspens. 山坡上长满了白杨树。|Synonyms: poplar, cottonwood|Antonyms: N/A|noun

aspen:/ˈæspən/ (US), /ˈæspɪn/ (UK)| adj.|白杨的，山杨的 (Less common usage)|Made of aspen wood, or relating to aspen trees.|She pointed to a grove of aspen trees. 她指着一片白杨树林。|He crafted a beautiful bowl from aspen wood. 他用白杨木做了一个漂亮的碗。|Synonyms: poplar, cottonwood (as nouns referring to the tree)|Antonyms: N/A|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *æspe, from Proto-Germanic *aspo, from Proto-Indo-European *ap-. Cognate with Old High German aspa, Old Norse osp.
