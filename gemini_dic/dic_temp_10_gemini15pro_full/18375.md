bellyband: /ˈbɛliˌbænd/ | n. | 腹带；束腹带；书腰；| A band of fabric worn around the belly, for support, warmth, or to smooth the silhouette.  A paper strip wrapped around a book, often containing promotional information. | The pregnant woman wore a bellyband to support her growing belly. 孕妇戴着腹带以支撑她日益增大的肚子。

The publisher added a bellyband to the book highlighting its awards. 出版商在书上加了一个书腰，突出了它的奖项。 | 近义词：abdominal binder, girdle, cummerbund (for formal wear), book jacket | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
