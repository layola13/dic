bedevilment:/bɪˈdɛvəl mənt/
n.
折磨；困扰；纠缠
The state of being bedeviled; harassment or torment.
The project was plagued by constant bedevilments, delaying its completion.  这个项目一直受到持续不断的困扰，导致其完成时间被推迟。
harassment, torment, affliction, plague, vexation
blessing, peace, tranquility
noun
