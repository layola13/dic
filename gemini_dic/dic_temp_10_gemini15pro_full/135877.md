nonprovidential:/ˌnɒnˌprɒvɪˈdɛnʃəl/
adjective
非天意的，非神助的，偶然的
Not attributed to or characterized by divine providence; due to chance or human agency.
The nonprovidential nature of the accident led investigators to focus on mechanical failure rather than divine intervention.  这起事故的非天意性质导致调查人员将重点放在机械故障上，而不是神圣干预。

His success was due to hard work and talent, a decidedly nonprovidential explanation. 他的成功归功于努力和天赋，这是一个明确的非天意解释。
近义词:accidental, fortuitous, random, chance
反义词:providential, destined, fated
词性:adjective
