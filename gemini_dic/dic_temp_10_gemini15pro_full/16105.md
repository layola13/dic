bandy:/ˈbændi/ (US), /ˈbændɪ/ (UK)| adj. v. n.|弯曲的，交换(尤指言语)，用球棍打(曲棍球)，(腿)向外弯曲的|
(adj.) Having a slight outward curve; (v.) Pass on or discuss (an idea or rumor) in a casual or uninformed way; exchange (words, especially insults); (n.) A game resembling hockey, played on ice, especially in Russia and other northern European countries. |
(adj.) He had bandy legs.  他双腿向外弯曲。
(v.) Accusations were bandied about.  互相指责。
(n.) He enjoys playing bandy. 他喜欢打班迪球。
|近义词：(adj.) bowed, curved; (v.) exchange, swap, trade;  (n.) hockey |反义词：(adj.) straight |词性：adjective, verb, noun

bandy:/ˈbændi/ (US), /ˈbændɪ/ (UK)|v.|交换(尤指言语), 争论，来回抛球|To discuss or argue something, especially an idea, rumor, or insult, in a casual or informal way; to hit a ball back and forth.|
The two candidates bandied accusations of corruption. 两位候选人互相指责对方腐败。
They were bandying words about the best course of action.  他们就最佳行动方案争论不休。|近义词：exchange, trade, swap,  argue, discuss, debate|反义词：withhold, conceal|词性：verb
