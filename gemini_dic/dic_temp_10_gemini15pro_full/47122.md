dauntlessly:/ˈdɔːntləsli/| adv.|无畏地，勇敢地|In a fearless and determined manner; bravely.|She dauntlessly faced the challenges ahead. 她无畏地面对未来的挑战。|近义词：bravely, courageously, fearlessly, intrepidly, boldly|反义词：fearfully, timidly, cowardly|词性:副词
词源: From dauntless + -ly.  Dauntless itself comes from the Old French *danter (to tame, subdue) + -less (without).
