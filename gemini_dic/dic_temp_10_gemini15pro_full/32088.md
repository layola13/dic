chisel:/ˈtʃɪzl/| n. vt. |凿子；雕刻；|A chisel is a tool with a sharp, beveled edge used for carving or shaping wood, stone, or metal.  |He used a chisel to shape the wood. 他用凿子来塑形木头。|The sculptor skillfully used a chisel to create the delicate features of the statue. 雕刻家巧妙地运用凿子塑造了雕像精致的特征。|近义词：gouge, burin|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑与其作用相反的工具，例如：file - 锉刀)|n. v.
