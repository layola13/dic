ditzy:/ˈdɪtsi/| adj.|愚蠢的，傻乎乎的|Scatterbrained and silly; frivolous and flighty.|例句：She's a bit ditzy and often forgets things. 她有点傻乎乎的，经常忘事。|近义词：scatterbrained, flighty, silly, dizzy, airheaded|反义词：sensible, intelligent, smart, serious|词性:形容词
Etymology:  Likely a blend of "dizzy" and "witless," reflecting a sense of lightheadedness and lack of intelligence.
