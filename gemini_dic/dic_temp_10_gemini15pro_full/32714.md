chlorinations:/ˌklɔːrɪˈneɪʃənz/
|n.|氯化（作用）；加氯（处理）|The process of adding chlorine to something, typically water, to disinfect it or to participate in a chemical reaction.|The chlorinations of the pool occur regularly to maintain hygiene.  游泳池定期进行氯化以保持卫生。| synonyms: disinfection, sterilization | antonyms: decontamination, purification
