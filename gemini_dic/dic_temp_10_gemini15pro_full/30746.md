cesaro:/ˈtʃɛzɑːroʊ/ /ˈsɛzɑːroʊ/| adj. n.|切萨罗的，切萨罗求和|Relating to or denoting a method of assigning a value to the limit of a sequence by taking the average of the first n terms and then the limit of that average as n tends to infinity.  Cesàro also refers to Ernesto Cesàro (1859-1906), an Italian mathematician who studied divergent series.|The Cesàro sum of the Grandi series (1 - 1 + 1 - 1 + ...) is 1/2.  格兰迪级数 (1 - 1 + 1 - 1 + ...) 的切萨罗和是 1/2。|The Cesàro mean is used in Fourier analysis. 切萨罗平均值用于傅里叶分析。|近义词: (for sum) Riesz, Hölder|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun
