nonabsolutistic:/ˌnɒnˌæbsəluːˈtɪstɪk/
adjective
非绝对主义的
Not absolutist; not characterized by the belief in absolute principles or the exercise of unrestricted power.
The philosopher advocated for a nonabsolutistic approach to morality, arguing that context and individual circumstances should always be considered.  哲学家提倡一种非绝对主义的道德方法，认为应该始终考虑背景和个人情况。
Their government adopted a nonabsolutistic stance on freedom of speech, acknowledging certain limitations in the interest of public safety.  他们的政府对言论自由采取了非绝对主义的立场，承认为了公共安全需要有一定的限制。
Near Synonyms: relative, contextual, contingent, qualified, nuanced
Antonyms: absolute, absolutist, unconditional, unrestricted, dogmatic
词性: adjective
