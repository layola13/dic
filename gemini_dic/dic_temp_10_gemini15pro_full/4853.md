alienating: /ˈeɪliəneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 疏远的，使人疏远的，离间的 | Causing someone to feel isolated or estranged. |  His constant criticism was alienating his friends. 他不断的批评使他的朋友们疏远了他。

The new policy is alienating many voters. 新政策使许多选民感到疏远。


|近义词：isolating, estranging, distancing, off-putting |反义词：welcoming, endearing, engaging, uniting | 词性: 形容词
