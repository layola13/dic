inhibition:/ˌɪnhɪˈbɪʃən/ | n. | 抑制，拘束，顾虑 | a feeling that makes one self-conscious and unable to act in a relaxed and natural way |  She overcame her inhibitions and spoke confidently. 她克服了拘束，自信地发言。 | synonyms: restraint, reserve, shyness | antonyms: boldness, confidence, extroversion | noun

inhibition:/ˌɪnhɪˈbɪʃən/ | n. | 禁止，阻止 | the act of inhibiting or the state of being inhibited | The government imposed inhibitions on trade. 政府对贸易施加了限制。 | synonyms: prohibition, restriction, suppression | antonyms: allowance, permission, promotion | noun
