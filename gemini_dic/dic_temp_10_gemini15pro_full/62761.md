engraft:/ɪnˈɡræft/ /ɛnˈɡræft/| vt. |嫁接；移植；灌输|To insert or implant something (like a graft) into something else; to fix or establish firmly, as if by grafting.|The surgeon skillfully engrafted skin from the patient's leg onto the burn area.  外科医生熟练地将病人腿上的皮肤移植到烧伤部位。|近义词：implant, insert, embed, instill|反义词:excise, remove, uproot|v.

engraft:/ɪnˈɡræft/ /ɛnˈɡræft/| n. |嫁接；移植；灌输|The act of engrafting something.|The engraftment of the new tissue was successful. 新组织的移植成功了。|近义词：implantation, insertion|反义词:removal, excision|n.
