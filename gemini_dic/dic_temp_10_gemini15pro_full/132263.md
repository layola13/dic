nondedications:/ˌnɒndɛdɪˈkeɪʃənz/
n.
lack of dedications; absences of formal dedicatory acts or inscriptions
The museum's collection suffered from nondedications, making it difficult to trace the provenance of many items.  博物馆的藏品缺乏捐赠记录，因此很难追溯许多物品的来源。
The nondedications of the new park left many wondering who had funded it. 新公园没有落成仪式，许多人想知道是谁资助了它。
近义词: omissions, absences
反义词: dedications, consecrations
词性: 名词

词源:
Prefix "non-" (not, without) + "dedication" (from Latin "dedicatio," meaning "a setting apart, devotion")
