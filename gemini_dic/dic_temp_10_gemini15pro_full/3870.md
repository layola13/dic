agraffe:/əˈɡræf/ | n. |搭扣，扣环，钩子|A clasp or hook for fastening a garment, especially a jeweled one.|The antique gown was fastened with a delicate agraffe encrusted with diamonds.|近义词：clasp, fastener, hook, buckle|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From French *agrafe*, from *agrafer* "to fasten with a hook," from *a-* (from Latin *ad-*) + *grafe, graffe* "hook," of Germanic origin.
