baniya: /ˈbɑːniːə/ (UK) /bəˈniːə/ (US)| n.| 商人，店主 (尤指印度的)| A merchant, trader, or shopkeeper, especially in India.  This term is often associated with the Bania caste, traditionally involved in commerce and trade.  Due to its association with caste, the term can be considered outdated and potentially offensive in some contexts. It's important to use it with caution and sensitivity.| Example:  His grandfather was a baniya who ran a successful textile business.  他的祖父是一位经营着成功纺织品生意的商人。

Example:  The baniya's shop was well-stocked with spices and grains.  这个商人的店铺里备满了香料和谷物。| 近义词: merchant, trader, shopkeeper, businessman| 反义词: customer, buyer| 词性: noun
