hidey:/ˈhaɪdi/| int.|你好；嗨|An informal greeting, similar to "hi" or "hey."|Hidey, neighbor! How are you doing today? 你好，邻居！你今天过得怎么样？|近义词：hi, hey, hello|反义词:goodbye, farewell|词性:感叹词

hidey:/ˈhaɪdi/| n.|藏身之处|A place of concealment; a hiding place. (Less common usage)|The children found a hidey in the woods. 孩子们在树林里找到了一个藏身之处。|近义词：hiding place, hideout|反义词:open space, exposed area|词性:名词
