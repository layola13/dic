enthrallment:/ɪnˈθrɔːlmənt/| n. |着迷，迷住，吸引|The state of being enthralled; captivated, fascinated.  A feeling of great fascination or excitement.|The enthrallment of the audience was obvious as they listened to the captivating storyteller.  观众们听着引人入胜的故事讲述者，显然都听得入了迷。|Synonyms: captivation, fascination, enchantment, spellbound, mesmerism|Antonyms: boredom, disinterest, apathy|noun

enthrall:/ɪnˈθrɔːl/| v. |迷住，吸引|To hold spellbound; captivate.|The magician's tricks enthralled the children. 魔术师的戏法迷住了孩子们。|Synonyms: captivate, fascinate, enchant, mesmerize, charm|Antonyms: bore, repel, disgust|verb
