cruciforms:/ˈkruːsɪfɔːrmz/
noun
十字形物
objects shaped like a cross
The church was decorated with numerous cruciforms. 教堂装饰着许多十字形饰物。

近义词:cross-shaped, cruciform
反义词:(No direct antonym, could consider shapes like "circular," "square," etc.)
词性:n.(noun)

Etymology: From Latin "crux" (cross) + "-formis" (shaped like).
