metaphysician:/ˌmɛtəfɪˈzɪʃən/ | n. | 形而上学家；玄学家 | A metaphysician is a philosopher who studies metaphysics, which is concerned with the fundamental nature of reality, including the relationship between mind and matter, between substance and attribute, and between potentiality and actuality. | He considered himself a metaphysician, exploring the nature of existence. 他认为自己是一位形而上学家，探索存在的本质。 |  Synonyms: philosopher, theorist, ontologist | Antonyms: physicist, materialist | noun
