avengeful:/əˈven(d)ʒfəl/
adjective
复仇的；报复心重的
disposed to revenge; vindictive
例句：He gave her an vengeful look. 他带着报复性的眼神看着她。

例句：She plotted an vengeful campaign against her rivals. 她策划了一场针对竞争对手的报复行动。

近义词：vindictive, revengeful, retaliatory
反义词：forgiving, merciful, compassionate
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *avengeful, equivalent to avenge + -ful.
