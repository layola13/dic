ETYMOLOGY->
From the German *Österreich*, meaning "eastern realm" or "eastern march".  The term ultimately derives from the Old High German *Ostarrîchi*, which first appeared in a document from 996.  The *Ost- * part means "east", related to the English "east". The *-rîchi* part means "realm, kingdom, empire", related to the English "rich" (in the sense of powerful).
