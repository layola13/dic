instillator:/ɪnˈstɪleɪtər/ | n. |灌输者；谆谆教诲者|A person who instills something, such as a quality, idea, or feeling, into another person. |The teacher was a true instillator of knowledge and a love of learning in her students. 这位老师真正是一位知识和学习热爱的灌输者，影响了她的学生。|  |Synonyms: inculcator, implanter, infuser | Antonyms: extractor, remover | Noun

instil:/ɪnˈstɪl/ | v. |慢慢灌输；逐渐培养|To gradually establish an idea, feeling, or attitude in a person's mind. |She instilled a love of reading in her children. 她逐渐培养了孩子们对阅读的热爱。| | Synonyms: implant, infuse, inculcate | Antonyms: eradicate, remove | Verb
