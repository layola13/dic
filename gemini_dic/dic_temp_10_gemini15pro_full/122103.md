mistitle:/ˌmɪsˈtaɪtl/
verb
错误地命名；给…加错标题
To give a wrong or inappropriate title to.
The book was mistitled "A History of the World."  这本书被错误地命名为“世界史”。
The newspaper mistitled the article. 报纸给文章加了错误的标题。
misname, mislabel
correctly title
