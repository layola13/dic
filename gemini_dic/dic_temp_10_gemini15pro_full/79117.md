glades:/ɡleɪdz/| n. | 树林中的空地；沼泽地 |An open space in a wood or forest; a marshy tract of land. |例句：We picnicked in a sunny glade. 我们在阳光明媚的林间空地野餐。

例句：The deer grazed peacefully in the glade. 鹿群在空地上平静地吃草。|近义词：clearing, meadow, pasture |反义词:forest, thicket |词性:名词

词源: From Old English *glǣd.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *glaidaz (“bright, shining”). Related to glad.
