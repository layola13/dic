mugwort:/ˈmʌɡˌwɜːrt/ (US), /ˈmʌɡwɜːt/ (UK)| n.| 艾草，洋艾|A strongly aromatic plant (Artemisia vulgaris) of the composite family, having grayish-green leaves and small yellowish flowers, formerly used medicinally and in brewing. It is considered a weed in some areas.|Mugwort tea is said to have medicinal properties. 艾草茶据说有药用价值。

|Synonyms: common mugwort, felon herb, chrysanthemum weed|Antonyms: N/A|noun
