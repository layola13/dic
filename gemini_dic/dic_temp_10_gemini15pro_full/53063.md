dioramas:/ˌdaɪəˈrɑːməz/ |n. |立体模型；透视画；西洋景|A model representing a scene with three-dimensional figures, either in miniature or as a large-scale exhibit.|The museum had several fascinating dioramas depicting prehistoric life.  博物馆里有几个描绘史前生物的精彩立体模型。|例句：The children were captivated by the diorama of the rainforest. 孩子们被热带雨林的立体模型迷住了。

例句：The diorama vividly portrayed a scene from the American Civil War. 立体模型生动地描绘了美国内战的一个场景。|近义词：model, exhibit, display, miniature|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|词性: 名词
