buoyage:/ˈbɔɪɪdʒ/ | n. |航标系统; 浮标; |A system of buoys; the buoys themselves.  The positioning and maintenance of buoys to aid navigation.|The ship relied on the buoyage system to navigate the treacherous waters. 这艘船依靠航标系统在危险的水域航行。| 近义词: navigation aids, beacon system | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "lack of navigational aids") | 词性: noun
