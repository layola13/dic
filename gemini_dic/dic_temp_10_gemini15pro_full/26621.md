cachunde:/kəˈtʃʊndeɪ/ | n. | 卡春德（一种巴西炖菜）| A Brazilian stew, typically made with various meats, vegetables, and spices. | Example: We had a delicious cachunde for dinner last night. 昨晚我们吃了一顿美味的卡春德。| Example: Cachunde is a popular dish in the Northeast region of Brazil. 卡春德是巴西东北地区的一道流行菜。| 近义词: stew, feijoada | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Portuguese *caçunda*, meaning "rump" or "hindquarters", likely referring to the tougher cuts of meat used in the dish.
