nonexponentially:/ˌnɒnˌɛkspəˈnɛnʃəli/
| adv. |非指数地| Not increasing or decreasing at an exponential rate; in a manner that is not exponential. |The population grew nonexponentially due to limited resources. 由于资源有限，人口呈非指数增长。|
|The algorithm performs nonexponentially, making it suitable for large datasets. 该算法执行起来是非指数级的，因此适用于大型数据集。|
|近义词: linearly, gradually, steadily|
|反义词: exponentially|
|词性: 副词|
