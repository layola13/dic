chancer:/ˈtʃɑːnsər/ (UK), /ˈtʃænsər/ (US)| n. |投机分子；冒险家；机会主义者|Someone who takes risks, especially in business or financial matters, hoping to gain an advantage, often without caring about the consequences for others.  A chancer may also be someone who exploits situations or tries to achieve something by deceitful or unscrupulous methods.|He's a bit of a chancer, always looking for a quick way to make money. 他有点投机取巧，总想找快速赚钱的方法。|近义词：gambler, risk-taker, opportunist, rogue, swindler|反义词: cautious person, conservative|noun
