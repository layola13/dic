ETYMOLOGY->
CBSA (Canada Border Services Agency) is a relatively recent term, coming into use in 2003 when the agency was created.  It's formed from the initial letters of the full name.
CBSA (Census Bureau Statistical Area), specifically Census Metropolitan Area (CMA) in Canada,  uses "census" relating to population count and "metropolitan" denoting a large urban area.
