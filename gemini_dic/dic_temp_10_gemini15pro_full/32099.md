chesterfield:/ˈtʃɛstə(r)fiːld/| n.|切斯特菲尔德沙发；一种大型舒适的沙发，通常是皮革或天鹅绒的，带有高靠背和扶手，通常与座位齐高。|A large, comfortable sofa, typically upholstered in leather or velvet, with high arms and a back of the same height. |例句：He sank into the plush chesterfield. 他沉陷在豪华的切斯特菲尔德沙发里。

例句：The antique chesterfield was the centerpiece of the living room.  这件古董切斯特菲尔德沙发是客厅的中心摆设。

近义词：sofa, couch, settee|反义词:|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Named after Philip Stanhope, 4th Earl of Chesterfield (1694-1773), who is believed to have commissioned the first such sofa.
