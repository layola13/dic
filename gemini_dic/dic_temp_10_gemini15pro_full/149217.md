oxymora:/ˌɒksɪˈmɔːrə/ | n. |矛盾修饰法，逆喻 |A figure of speech in which apparently contradictory terms appear in conjunction. |例句： "Jumbo shrimp" is a classic oxymoron.  “巨型虾”是一个经典的矛盾修饰法。

例句：Bittersweet is an oxymoron. 苦乐参半是一种矛盾修饰法。|近义词：paradox, contradiction |反义词:tautology |词性:noun
