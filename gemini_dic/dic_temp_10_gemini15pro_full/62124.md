enchoric:/ɛnˈkɔːrɪk/ | adj.| 本土的，地方性的；(尤指古埃及)通俗文字的 |  Relating to or being the common language of a particular region or people; native or indigenous. Specifically referring to the demotic form of ancient Egyptian writing. | The Rosetta Stone contained hieroglyphic, demotic, and enchoric scripts, allowing scholars to decipher the ancient language. 罗塞塔石碑包含象形文字、通俗文字和地方文字，使学者们能够破译这种古老的语言。|  indigenous, native, local, vernacular | foreign, exotic | adjective

enchoric:/ɛnˈkɔːrɪk/ | n.| 本土语言；地方方言；(尤指古埃及)通俗文字 | The common language of a particular region or people; a vernacular. Specifically, the demotic form of ancient Egyptian writing. | Enchoric was the everyday script used in ancient Egypt, unlike the more formal hieroglyphs.  古埃及的日常文字是通俗文字，不像更正式的象形文字。 | vernacular, dialect, local language | formal language | noun
