chewable:/ˈtʃuːəbl/| adj.|可咀嚼的，耐嚼的|Something that is chewable can be chewed. chewable:/ˈtʃuːəbl/| n.|可咀嚼物，咀嚼片|A chewable substance, typically a tablet or candy.|The doctor prescribed chewable tablets for the child. 医生给孩子开了咀嚼片。|Synonyms: chew, gum, tablet|Antonyms: N/A|noun

Etymology:
