morn:/mɔːrn/| n.|早晨，清晨|The morn was bright and sunny. 清晨阳光明媚。|近义词：morning, dawn, sunrise|反义词:evening, night|noun
Etymology->From Middle English *morwen, from Old English morgen (“morning”), from Proto-Germanic *murgana (“morning”), from Proto-Indo-European *merǵ- (“short; small; to blink; to twinkle”). Cognate with Scots morn (“morning”), Saterland Frisian Moarn (“morning”), West Frisian moarn (“morning”), Dutch morgen (“morning”), German Morgen (“morning”), Danish morgen (“morning”), Swedish morgon (“morning”), Icelandic morgunn (“morning”). Related to morrow.

注意事项:

morn 虽然是名词，但由于其古老的性质，现在主要用于诗歌或文学作品中，日常生活中很少使用。  更常见的是使用 morning。
