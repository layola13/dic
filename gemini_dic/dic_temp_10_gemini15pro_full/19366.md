biblioteca:/ˌbiːbli.oʊˈteɪkə/ /ˌbɪbliəˈteɪkə/| n. |图书馆；藏书；图书室|A building or room containing collections of books, periodicals, and sometimes films and recorded music for people to read, borrow, or refer to.|例句：The town has a good public biblioteca. 这个镇有一个很好的公共图书馆。|例句：His personal biblioteca contains thousands of volumes. 他的私人藏书有数千册。|近义词：library, archive|反义词: None|词性:noun

词源: From Latin *bibliothēca*, from Ancient Greek βιβλιοθήκη (bibliothḗkē, “book repository”), from βιβλίον (biblíon, “book”) + θήκη (thḗkē, “receptacle”).
