cerosin:/ˈsɪərəsɪn/ | n. | 蜡状烃; 白地蜡 | A waxy hydrocarbon mixture found in certain plants and insects.  It is a type of long-chain alkane. |  Cerosin is used in some candles and polishes. 白地蜡用在一些蜡烛和抛光剂中. | synonyms: paraffin wax, mineral wax | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but could consider substances that dissolve waxes, like solvents) | noun
