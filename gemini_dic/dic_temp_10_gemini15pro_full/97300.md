infoliate: /ɪnˈfoʊliˌeɪt/
verb
覆盖树叶;使…长满叶子;用叶子装饰
to cover with leaves; to cause to become covered with leaves; to decorate with leaves.
The ancient ruins were infoliated with vines and creepers. 古老的废墟上覆盖着藤蔓和爬山虎。
近义词: leaf, cover with foliage
反义词: defoliate
词性: verb
