maybe:/ˈmeɪbi/ (US), /ˈmeɪbi/ (UK)| adv.|也许，可能|Perhaps; possibly.  It indicates uncertainty or possibility.|例句：Maybe I'll go to the party. 也许我会去参加聚会。

例句：Maybe she's right. 也许她是对的。

近义词：perhaps, possibly, perchance|反义词:certainly, definitely, absolutely|词性:adverb
