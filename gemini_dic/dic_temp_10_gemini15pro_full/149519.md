paga:/ˈpɑːɡə/ (UK), /ˈpeɪɡə/ (US)| n.|工资，薪水|A fixed regular payment, typically paid on a monthly or biweekly basis, made by an employer to an employee, especially a professional or white-collar worker.|例句：Her paga has increased significantly over the past year. 她的薪水在过去一年里大幅增长了。

例句：He was unhappy with his current paga and started looking for a new job.  他对目前的薪水不满意，开始寻找新工作。

近义词：salary, wages, pay, remuneration, compensation|反义词: deductions|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *paga*, from pagar "to pay",  from Old French *paier*, from Latin *pacāre* "to pacify, appease," from *pax* "peace."  The idea is that payment brings peace or settles a debt.
