nonadvocacy:/nɒnˈædvəkəsi/
n.|非倡导，不提倡|The organization's nonadvocacy stance prevents it from taking sides on political issues.  该组织的不提倡立场使其无法在政治问题上选边站队。|
例句：The museum maintained a policy of strict nonadvocacy on all religious matters. 博物馆在所有宗教事务上都保持严格的不提倡政策。
例句：Their nonadvocacy position on the issue disappointed many supporters. 他们在该问题上的不提倡立场令许多支持者感到失望。
近义词：neutrality, impartiality, objectivity|
反义词：advocacy, support, promotion|
词性：名词

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "advocacy" (from Late Latin "advocatia," meaning "the office or function of an advocate")
