The nonhabitabilities of Mars pose significant challenges for human colonization.  火星的非宜居性对人类殖民构成了重大挑战。

Synonym: uninhabitability, inhospitability

noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + habitability.  Habitability comes from Late Latin habitabilis, from habitare "to inhabit," from Latin habitus "condition, appearance, dress, posture," past participle of habēre "to have, hold."
