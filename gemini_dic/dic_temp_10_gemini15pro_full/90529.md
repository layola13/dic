huffcap: /ˈhʌfkæp/
n.| 怒气冲冲的人;爱生气的人|A person who is easily angered or offended; a bad-tempered person.|He's such a huffcap; he gets angry over the smallest things. 他真是个爱生气的人，一点小事就发火。|近义词: hothead, grump, crank, curmudgeon|反义词: easygoing person, pacifist|词性: 名词

词源:
huff (n.) meaning a fit of anger or resentment + cap probably in the sense of "head"
