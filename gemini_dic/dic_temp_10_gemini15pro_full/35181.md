cloy:/klɔɪ/
verb
使腻烦；使过饱；使倒胃口
to make someone feel that they have had too much of something pleasant, especially something sweet
例句：The sweetness of the dessert began to cloy.  这道甜点的甜味开始让人腻了。

例句：After a while, the constant praise began to cloy.  过了一会儿，不断的赞扬开始让人感到厌烦。

近义词：satiate, surfeit, glut
反义词：stimulate, excite
词性：v.
