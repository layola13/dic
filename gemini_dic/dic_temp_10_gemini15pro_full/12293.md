ascigerous:/æsɪˈdʒɪrəs/
adjective
产生子囊的; 有子囊的
Containing asci; bearing asci (the sacs in which sexual spores are formed in ascomycete fungi).
The morel is an ascigerous fungus. 羊肚菌是一种有子囊的真菌。
The ascigerous fruiting body is small and inconspicuous.  这种产生子囊的子实体很小且不显眼。
synonyms: ascus-bearing
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, possibly "asexual" in the context of fungal reproduction)
词性: adjective
