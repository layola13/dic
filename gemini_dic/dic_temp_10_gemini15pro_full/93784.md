immensity:/ɪˈmensɪti/| n. |巨大；广阔；无限|The immensity of the universe is difficult to grasp. 宇宙的浩瀚难以理解。|例句：She was overwhelmed by the sheer immensity of the task ahead. 她被摆在面前的艰巨任务的巨大规模所震撼。

例句：The immensity of the ocean always fills me with awe. 海洋的浩瀚总是让我充满敬畏。

近义词：vastness, hugeness, enormity, boundlessness|反义词:smallness, tininess|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *immensite, from Old French immensité, from Latin immensitās (“immeasurableness, vastness”), from immensus (“immeasurable, vast”), from in- (“not”) + mensus (“measured”), past participle of metiri (“to measure”).
