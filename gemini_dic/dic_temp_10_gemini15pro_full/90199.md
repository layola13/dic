houlet:/ˈhuːlɪt/ | n. | 猫头鹰 (一种旧的或方言的拼写) | An old or dialect spelling of "owl," a nocturnal bird of prey with large eyes, a hooked beak, and typically a hooting call. | He saw a houlet perched on the barn roof. 他看到一只猫头鹰栖息在谷仓的屋顶上。| 近义词: owl | 反义词:  (无直接反义词) | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *houlet, howlet, from Old English ūlet, from Proto-Germanic *ūwwalǭ, *ūwilǭ (“owl”). Cognate with Scots howlet (“owl”), West Frisian ûle (“owl”), Dutch uil (“owl”), German Eule (“owl”).
