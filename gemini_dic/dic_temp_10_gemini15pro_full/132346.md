nondegradation:/ˌnɒndɛɡrəˈdeɪʃən/ |n.| 非退化，不降低 |The principle of nondegradation ensures that environmental quality does not deteriorate.  非退化原则确保环境质量不恶化。|  The policy focuses on nondegradation of water resources. 这项政策侧重于水资源的非退化。 |近义词: preservation, conservation, maintenance | 反义词: degradation, deterioration, decline | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non, meaning "not"
degradation (noun): from Late Latin dēgradātiōn- (stem of dēgradātiō), meaning "a reducing in rank," from dēgradāt- (past participle stem of dēgradāre) meaning "to reduce in rank," from Latin dē- "down" + gradus "step, rank"
