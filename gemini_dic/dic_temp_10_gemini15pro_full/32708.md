chlorimetry:/ˌklɔːˈrɪmɪtri/
noun
氯量法; 氯量测定法
The process of quantitatively determining the amount of chlorine in a substance.
Chlorimetry is used to measure the chlorine content in drinking water.  氯量法用于测量饮用水中氯的含量。
This method of chlorimetry is widely used in water treatment plants. 这种氯量法广泛应用于水处理厂。
Synonyms: chlorine analysis, chlorine titration
Antonyms: N/A
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *chlorométrie*, from *chloro-* (from Greek *khlōros* "pale green, greenish-yellow," referring to the color of chlorine gas) + *-métrie* (from Greek *-metria* "measurement").
