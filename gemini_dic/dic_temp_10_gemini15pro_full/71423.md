flakelike:/ˈfleɪklaɪk/| adj.|薄片状的，像鳞片一样的|Resembling flakes; flaky.  Having a thin, flat, and easily detached form, like a flake.|The pastry had a delicate, flakelike texture.  这糕点有着精致的、薄片状的质地。|The paint was peeling off in flakelike pieces. 油漆呈薄片状剥落。|近义词：flaky, scaly, laminar|反义词: solid, compact|词性:adjective

词源: flake + like.  "Flake" comes from Old Norse "flaka" (to flake, peel off).
