annotationist:/ˌænəˈteɪʃənɪst/
n.
注释者，注解者
A person who adds annotations or notes to a text, image, or other piece of information.
The annotationist meticulously documented the historical context of each photograph.  注释者一丝不苟地记录了每张照片的历史背景。
The annotationist provided helpful explanations of the complex legal jargon. 注释者对复杂的法律术语提供了有益的解释。
近义词: commentator, annotator, expounder, explainer
反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly obfuscator - someone who makes things unclear)
词性: noun
