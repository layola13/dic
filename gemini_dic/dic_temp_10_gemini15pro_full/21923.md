bog-in:/ˈbɒɡ ɪn/ (UK), /ˈbɑːɡ ɪn/ (US)| phrasal verb| 开始认真工作；大吃特吃 |To start working seriously on something, or to start eating enthusiastically.|例句：Let's bog in and get this project finished. 让我们开始认真工作，完成这个项目。|近义词：get started, get down to it, tuck in, dig in|反义词:procrastinate, delay|词性:动词短语

bog-in:/ˈbɒɡ ɪn/ (UK), /ˈbɑːɡ ɪn/ (US)| n.|困境，僵局|A difficult or awkward situation.|例句：The negotiations reached a bog-in and no progress was made.  谈判陷入了僵局，没有取得任何进展。|近义词：stalemate, impasse, deadlock|反义词:breakthrough, solution|词性:名词
