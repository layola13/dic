inquisiturient:/ɪnˌkwɪzɪˈtʊəriənt/ | adj. | 好奇的，爱打听的 | Given to inquiry; eager to learn; curious, especially in a searching or prying way.  Characterized by a desire to investigate and learn, often excessively or intrusively. |  She gave him an inquisiturient look, trying to figure out his secret. 她用好奇的眼光打量着他，试图找出他的秘密。| Synonym: curious, inquisitive, prying, nosy | Antonym: apathetic, indifferent, incurious | word property: Adjective
