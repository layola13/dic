outdespatching:/ˌaʊtdɪˈspætʃɪŋ/
verb
超越派遣；胜过派遣
The company's efficiency in outdespatching goods surpassed all its competitors.  这家公司在货物发送效率方面超过了所有竞争对手。
Etymology:  'out-' (prefix meaning surpassing or exceeding) + 'despatching' (present participle of 'despatch', meaning to send off with speed). 源于out-（前缀，表示超越或胜过）+ despatching（despatch的现在分词，意为快速发送）。
Example: Their logistics team is known for outdespatching orders faster than anyone else in the industry. 他们的物流团队以比业内任何人都更快地发出订单而闻名。
Synonyms: outdoing, excelling, surpassing, exceeding
Antonyms: lagging, falling behind
part of speech: verb
