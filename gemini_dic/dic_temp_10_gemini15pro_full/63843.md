epiglottiditis: /ˌɛpɪˌɡlɑːtɪˈdaɪtɪs/
noun
会厌炎
Inflammation of the epiglottis, a flap of cartilage at the root of the tongue, which can be life-threatening due to airway obstruction.
The child was diagnosed with epiglottiditis and required immediate hospitalization.  这个孩子被诊断出患有会厌炎，需要立即住院。
Epiglottitis can cause difficulty breathing and swallowing. 会厌炎会导致呼吸和吞咽困难。
synonyms: supraglottitis, epiglottic inflammation
antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a disease condition.  One could consider "epiglottic health" as a contrasting concept.)
词性: noun
