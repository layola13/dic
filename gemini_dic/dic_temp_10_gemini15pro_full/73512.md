foreshadowers:/fɔːrˈʃædəʊərz/ (UK) /fɔːrˈʃædoʊərz/ (US)
noun
预示者；预兆
people or things that foreshadow something; harbingers
The dark clouds were foreshadowers of the coming storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨即将到来。
synonyms: harbingers, precursors, portents, omens
antonyms: aftereffects, consequences, results
n.

foreshadow:/fɔːrˈʃædəʊ/ (UK) /fɔːrˈʃædoʊ/ (US)
verb
预示；预兆
be a warning or indication of (a future event).
His nightmares foreshadowed the terrible accident. 他的噩梦预示了那场可怕的事故。
synonyms: prefigure, presage, portend, augur
antonyms: conceal, hide
