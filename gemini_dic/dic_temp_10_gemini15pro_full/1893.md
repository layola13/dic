acs:/æks/ | n. |  access control system的缩写，访问控制系统 | Access control systems (ACS) manage who can enter a building or access specific resources. 访问控制系统 (ACS) 管理谁可以进入建筑物或访问特定资源。 |  The building has a new ACS installed.  这栋楼安装了一个新的访问控制系统。  The ACS logs all entries and exits.  访问控制系统记录所有进出。 | synonyms: security system, entry system | antonyms: open access | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
ACS is an acronym derived from "Access Control System".  It is formed by taking the first letter of each word.
