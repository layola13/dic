interminable:/ɪnˈtɜːrmɪnəbl/ | adj. | 无休止的，没完没了的 | Seemingly endless; tediously long. | The wait for the bus seemed interminable. 公共汽车似乎等了很久。|  The interminable lecture bored the students.  没完没了的讲座让学生们感到厌烦。 | 近义词: endless, incessant, ceaseless, perpetual | 反义词: short, brief, finite, concise | 词性: 形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French, or from medieval Latin interminabilis, from in- ‘not’ + terminabilis (from terminare ‘to limit’).
