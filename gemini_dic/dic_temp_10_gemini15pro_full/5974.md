ambulation: /ˌæmbjəˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 步行；行走能力 | The act of walking or moving about. |  The patient's ambulation improved significantly after physical therapy. 病人经过物理治疗后，行走能力显著提高。

After the surgery, his ambulation was limited. 手术后，他的行走能力受限。

| 近义词: walking, locomotion, mobility | 反义词: immobility, paralysis | 词性: noun
