what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US) | pron. det. adv.|什么，多么| What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|Synonyms: which, whatever | Antonyms:  (What doesn't have a direct antonym as it's an interrogative word) | Pronoun/Determiner/Adverb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你怎么想？
What does she want for her birthday? = 她生日想要什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? = 他晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? = 她在商店买了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading this week? = 他这周一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃了什么？
What happened at the party last night? = 昨晚的派对发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the wedding? = 她婚礼上穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they discussed before the meeting started? = 会议开始前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the phone rang? = 电话响之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be presenting at the conference? = 他们在会议上将要展示什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will they have built by next summer? = 到明年夏天他们将建成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been studying for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经学习什么五年了？
What will she have been working on by the time she retires? = 到她退休时她将一直在做什么？


虚拟语气:
If I knew what to say, I would tell you. = 如果我知道该说什么，我会告诉你的。
I wish I knew what she was thinking. = 我希望我知道她在想什么。

被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
He doesn't understand what I'm saying. = 他不明白我在说什么。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What the heck? = 搞什么鬼？（语气比 what the hell 轻）
what if = 如果...会怎么样？
what for = 为了什么？
no matter what = 无论如何
