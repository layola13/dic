manifestly:/ˈmænɪfɛstli/ | adv. |明显地，显然地| readily perceived by the eye or the understanding; obviously. | It was manifestly unfair to blame him for everything.  很明显，把一切都怪到他头上是不公平的。|  The claim was manifestly false. 这一说法显然是假的。|Synonyms: obviously, clearly, evidently, plainly, patently, visibly, unmistakably, undeniably, indisputably | Antonyms: subtly, obscurely, vaguely, doubtfully, questionably |词性:副词
