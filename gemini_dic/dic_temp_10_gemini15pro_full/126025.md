musses:/ˈmʌsɪz/| n. |混乱，脏乱|A state of disorder or untidiness.|Her hair was in a complete muss after the windy bike ride.  她骑完自行车后，头发完全乱了。|近义词：messes, disarray, clutter|反义词：order, neatness|名词

muss:/ˈmʌs/| vt. |弄乱，弄脏|To make untidy or dishevelled.|The wind mussed her carefully styled hair. 风吹乱了她精心做的发型。|近义词：dishevel, rumple, tousle|反义词：neaten, tidy|动词

词源: From Middle Dutch *musen "to disorder," related to Old English *mūsian "to soil"
