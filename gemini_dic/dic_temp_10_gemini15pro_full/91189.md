hydrator:/ˈhaɪdreɪtər/| n.|补水器，保湿器|A device or substance used to add or restore water to something, especially to the body.|例句：She always carries a portable hydrator when hiking. 她远足时总是带着一个便携式补水器。

例句：This face cream is a powerful hydrator. 这款面霜是一款强效保湿霜。|近义词：moisturizer, humidifier|反义词:dehydrator|词性:noun
