comptrollerships:/kənˈtroʊlərʃɪps/
n.
主计长职位；主计长任期；主计长管理
The position or office of a comptroller; the term during which a comptroller holds office; management by a comptroller.
The comptrollerships of the two organizations were merged. 这两个组织的主计长职位合并了。
The new comptrollerships brought significant changes to the financial department. 新的主计长管理给财务部门带来了重大变化。
Synonyms: controllerships, financial management
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but possibly lack of oversight, mismanagement)
