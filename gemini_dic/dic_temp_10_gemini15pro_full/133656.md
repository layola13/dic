nonformulation:/ˌnɒnˌfɔːmjʊˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 未公式化，未系统阐述 | The idea is still in a state of nonformulation.  这个想法还处于未系统阐述的状态。|  His nonformulation of a clear plan led to confusion. 他没有制定明确的计划导致了混乱。 | 近义词:  lack of formulation, absence of definition,  | 反义词: formulation, definition, articulation | 词性: 名词

Etymology: non- + formulation. "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "formulation" refers to the act of creating something, especially a plan or policy.  词源: non- + formulation。“Non-”是一个表示“不”的前缀，“formulation”指的是创造某事物的行为，尤指计划或政策。
