cystocele: /ˈsɪstəˌsiːl/ | n. |膀胱膨出，膀胱脱垂|A cystocele occurs when the wall between a woman's bladder and her vagina weakens and allows the bladder to droop into the vagina. | Example 1:  The doctor diagnosed her with a cystocele after she complained of urinary incontinence.  医生在她抱怨尿失禁后诊断她患有膀胱膨出。

Example 2:  Surgery is sometimes necessary to repair a cystocele.  有时需要手术来修复膀胱膨出。| 近义词: bladder prolapse | 反义词:  None (This is a medical condition, not a descriptive term with a direct opposite) | 词性: noun
