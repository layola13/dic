ETYMOLOGY->
From Azerbaijan + -i/-is, denoting nationality or language.  Azerbaijan itself derives from Atropates, a Persian satrap who ruled the region in the 4th century BC. The name evolved through Middle Persian and Arabic before reaching its modern form.
