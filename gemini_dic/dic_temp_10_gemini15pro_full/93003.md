idiophanous:/ɪˌdiˈɒfənəs/| adj.|半透明的，显晶质的| (of minerals) transparent or translucent.  Relating to or denoting igneous rock whose crystals are large enough to be identified with the naked eye.|例句：The mineral sample was idiophanous, allowing light to pass through and revealing its internal structure.  矿物样本是半透明的，光线可以穿透并显示其内部结构。

例句：The geologist identified the rock as an idiophanous granite due to its visible crystals.  地质学家根据其可见的晶体将岩石鉴定为显晶质花岗岩。|近义词：translucent, transparent, crystalline|反义词：opaque|词性:形容词
