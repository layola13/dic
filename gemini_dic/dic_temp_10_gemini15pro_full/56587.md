donable:/ˈdoʊnəbl/
adjective
可捐赠的
able to be donated.
例句：The old clothes are donable to charity. 这些旧衣服可以捐给慈善机构。
例句：Is this furniture still donable?  这件家具还能捐赠吗？
近义词：giftable, contributable
反义词：nondonable, unusable
词性: adjective

词源:
From "donate" + "-able". "Donate" comes from Latin "donare" (to give, present). "-able" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being," "fit to be," or "worthy of being."
