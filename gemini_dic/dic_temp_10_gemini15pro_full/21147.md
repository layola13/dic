blitzing:/ˈblɪtsɪŋ/| n. v.|闪电战，突击，密集轰炸|An intensive campaign, attack, or effort.|The coach sent in the linebacker on a blitzing play. 教练派了线卫队员进行突击。|近义词：assault, attack, onslaught, barrage|反义词:defense, withdrawal|词性:名词, 动词

blitzing:/ˈblɪtsɪŋ/| v.|突击，闪电战，快速完成|To attack suddenly and intensely; to do something rapidly and intensively.|She blitzed through her homework so she could go out with her friends. 她快速完成了作业，以便能和朋友出去玩。|近义词：rush, speed through, hurry|反义词:dawdle, procrastinate|词性:动词
