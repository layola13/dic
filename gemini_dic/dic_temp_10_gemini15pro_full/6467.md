ampule:/ˈæmpjuːl/ (US), /ˈæmpuːl/ (UK)| n.|安瓿；小药瓶|A small sealed glass container that holds a single dose of a sterile injectable medication.|The nurse carefully snapped the neck of the ampule to withdraw the medication.|Synonym: vial, ampoule|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps bulk container)|noun

ampules:/ˈæmpjuːlz/ (US), /ˈæmpuːlz/ (UK)| n.|安瓿（复数）；小药瓶（复数）|Plural form of ampule, referring to multiple small sealed glass containers holding single doses of sterile injectable medication.|The box contained several ampules of the vaccine.|Synonym: vials, ampoules|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps bulk containers)|noun
