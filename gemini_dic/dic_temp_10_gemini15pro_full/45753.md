cushily:/ˈkʊʃɪli/
adverb
舒适地，安逸地
in a comfortable and relaxed way
She was sitting cushily on the sofa, reading a book. 她舒适地坐在沙发上看书。
comfortably, snugly, cosily
uncomfortably, awkwardly
词性: 副词
