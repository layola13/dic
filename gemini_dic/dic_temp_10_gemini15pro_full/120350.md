minimis:/də ˈmɪnɪmɪs/ | adj. | 最小的；微不足道的；最低限度的 | Of minimal importance; negligible.  Relating to things so small or insignificant as to merit disregard. Typically used in the phrase *de minimis*. | The de minimis rule allows taxpayers to deduct small amounts of miscellaneous expenses.  这条最低限度规则允许纳税人扣除少量杂项费用。 | negligible, insignificant, trifling, trivial | significant, substantial | adjective
