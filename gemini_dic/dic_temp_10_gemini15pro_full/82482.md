grungier:/ˈɡrʌndʒiər/ | adj. |更邋遢的，更脏的，更颓废的 |Comparative form of grungy: more grungy.  It describes something that exhibits the characteristics of grunge, a subculture characterized by unkempt appearances, often including torn clothing, flannel shirts, and a general disregard for conventional fashion.  It signifies a greater degree of this style or attitude than simply "grungy". | The band's new album is even grungier than their last.  这支乐队的新专辑比上一张更邋遢了。 |  The basement club was dark and grungier than any place I'd ever been.  地下室俱乐部又黑又脏，比我去过的任何地方都脏。 |  shabbier, dirtier, messier,  scruffier | cleaner, tidier, smarter, neater | adjective
