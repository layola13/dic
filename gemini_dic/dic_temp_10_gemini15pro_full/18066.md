beguile: /bɪˈɡaɪl/ /biˈɡaɪl/
verb
欺骗；哄骗；使着迷
To charm or enchant (someone), sometimes in a deceptive way.
例句：
He beguiled the audience with his smooth talking.
他用流畅的谈吐哄骗了观众。

The beautiful scenery beguiled us into staying longer than we planned.
美丽的景色让我们流连忘返，待得比计划的更久。

近义词：charm, enchant, captivate, deceive, mislead
反义词：repulse, bore, disenchant
词性：v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English bigilen, from be- + gilen (“to deceive, beguile”), of uncertain origin.
