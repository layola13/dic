overdilute:/ˌōvərdaɪˈl(j)uːt/| v. | 过度稀释 | To dilute something excessively, beyond what is desired or effective. |  The chemist overdiluted the solution, rendering it useless for the experiment. 化学家过度稀释了溶液，导致它对实验毫无用处。

The instructions warned against overdiluting the paint, as it would affect the coverage. 说明书警告不要过度稀释油漆，因为它会影响覆盖率。 | 近义词: water down, thin out | 反义词: concentrate, strengthen | 词性: 动词
