nonenlightened:/ˌnɒnɪnˈlaɪtnd/
adjective
未受启迪的，无知的
Not spiritually or intellectually enlightened; ignorant or unaware.
The nonenlightened masses were easily swayed by the charismatic leader's rhetoric.  未受启蒙的群众很容易被这位富有魅力的领导人的华丽辞藻所左右。
Many nonenlightened people still cling to outdated beliefs. 许多无知的人仍然坚持过时的信仰。
synonym:unenlightened, ignorant, unaware, benighted
antonym: enlightened, aware, informed, knowledgeable
词性:adjective
