loadstone:/ˈloʊdˌstoʊn/| n. |天然磁石，磁铁矿；吸引人的东西|A piece of magnetite or other naturally magnetized mineral, able to attract iron.  Something that attracts strongly. |例句：The loadstone attracted the iron filings. 天然磁石吸引了铁屑。

例句：Her charisma is a loadstone for new followers. 她的魅力吸引了新的追随者。|近义词：magnet, attraction|反义词: repellent |词性: noun
