annexationists:/ˌænɛkˈseɪʃənɪsts/
noun
兼并主义者；合并主义者
People who advocate or support annexation, the act of adding territory to one's own territory by appropriation.
The annexationists believed that acquiring new territories would strengthen the nation's economy and political power.  兼并主义者认为，获得新的领土将增强国家的经济和政治实力。
The annexationists' arguments were met with fierce resistance from those who opposed expansionism. 兼并主义者的论点遭到了那些反对扩张主义者的强烈抵制。
Synonyms: expansionists, imperialists
Antonyms: anti-imperialists, secessionists
