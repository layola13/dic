dotingly:/ˈdoʊtɪŋli/ | adv. | 溺爱地，宠爱地 | In a way that shows excessive fondness or love. | She looked at her grandson dotingly. 她宠溺地看着她的孙子。|  He patted his dog dotingly. 他宠溺地拍了拍他的狗。 | 近义词: fondly, lovingly, affectionately | 反义词: coldly, indifferently | 词性: 副词

詞源: From the verb "dote" + "-ing" + "-ly". "Dote" comes from Middle English *doten*, of uncertain origin, possibly from a Scandinavian source.
