overawing:/ˌoʊvərˈɔːɪŋ/| adj.|令人敬畏的，使人畏惧的|Inspiring awe or wonder; very impressive and often intimidating.|例句：The sheer size of the mountain was overawing.  大山巍峨的体积令人敬畏。|例句：His overawing presence made it difficult to relax. 他威严的仪态让人难以放松。|近义词：impressive, awesome, daunting, intimidating|反义词：unimpressive, underwhelming, insignificant|词性：形容词

词源：From "overawe" + "-ing". "Overawe" means to impress so much as to subdue or silence.  来源于 "overawe" + "-ing".  "Overawe" 的意思是留下深刻印象以至于征服或沉默。
