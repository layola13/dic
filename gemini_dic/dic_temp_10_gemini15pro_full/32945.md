choppier:/ˈtʃɒpiər (UK) /ˈtʃɑːpiər (US)| adj.|更波涛汹涌的，更断断续续的|Comparative form of choppy: more choppy.  Characterized by short, irregular waves or movements. Also used to describe something that is not smooth or continuous, like a jerky film or a halting speech.|The sea became choppier as the wind picked up. 海面随着风势增强而变得更加波涛汹涌。|The film's editing was choppier than I expected, making it hard to follow. 这部电影的剪辑比我预想的更断断续续，让人难以理解。|近义词：rough, turbulent, uneven, jerky|反义词：smooth, calm, steady, continuous|词性:形容词
