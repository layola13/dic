desaturated:/diːˈsætʃəreɪtɪd/
adjective
不饱和的; 减色的;  褪色的
Reduced in saturation; having had some or all of the saturation removed.
The photo was desaturated to give it a vintage feel. 这张照片被降低了饱和度，使其具有复古感。
近义词: muted, dull, faded, washed-out
反义词: saturated, vibrant, vivid
词性: adjective

desaturate:/diːˈsætʃəreɪt/
verb
降低饱和度; 使…不饱和; 使…褪色
To reduce the saturation of; to make less saturated.
The artist desaturated the colors in the painting to create a somber mood.  这位艺术家降低了画作中色彩的饱和度，以营造一种忧郁的氛围。
近义词: mute, dull, fade, wash out
反义词: saturate, brighten, intensify
词性: verb

Etymology:
From de- (prefix meaning 'reduce' or 'remove') + saturated. The word "saturated" comes from the Latin "saturatus," past participle of "saturare" (to fill, saturate), from "satis" (enough).
