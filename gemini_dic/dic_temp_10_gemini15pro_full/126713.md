namers:/ˈneɪmərz/| n.|命名者；取名者|People who give names to things or people.  Those who assign names.|The namers of the new product wanted something catchy and memorable.  新产品的命名者想要一个朗朗上口且令人难忘的名字。|nominators, christeners| --- |noun
Etymology:  From the verb "name" + "-er" (agent suffix).  Ultimately from Old English "nama" and Proto-Germanic "*namō".
