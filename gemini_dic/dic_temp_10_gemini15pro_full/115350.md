manometric:/ˌmænəˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. | 压力计的；测压的 | Relating to, or measured by a manometer (an instrument for measuring the pressure of fluids). | The manometric pressure was significantly higher than expected. 压力计上的压力读数明显高于预期。| The experiment required precise manometric readings. 实验需要精确的压力计读数。 |  近义词: pressure-measuring | 反义词:  (No common antonym) | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *manométrique*, from *manomètre* ("manometer") + *-ique* (suffix forming adjectives). *Manomètre* is from Ancient Greek *μανός* (manós, “thin, rare”) + *μέτρον* (métron, “measure”).
