antihunger:/ˌæntiˈhʌŋɡər/ | adj. | 抗饥饿的；抑制饥饿的 | Designed to prevent or relieve hunger. |  The government launched an antihunger campaign to address food insecurity. 政府发起了一项抗饥饿运动以解决粮食安全问题。 | The charity distributed antihunger packages to refugees.  慈善机构向难民分发了抗饥饿物资。 | 近义词:  anti-starvation, famine-relief | 反义词: hunger-inducing, appetite-stimulating | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
"anti-" (prefix) : from Greek "anti" meaning "against, opposite"
"hunger" : from Old English "hungor", from Proto-Germanic "*hungraz"
