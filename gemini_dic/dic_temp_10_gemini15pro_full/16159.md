banjos:/ˈbændʒoʊz/ (US), /ˈbændʒəʊz/ (UK)| n. |班卓琴（复数）|A stringed instrument with a long neck and a round body like a tambourine, played by plucking or strumming the strings.|例句：The banjos were the heart of the bluegrass band. 班卓琴是蓝草乐队的核心。

例句：He collects antique banjos. 他收藏古董班卓琴。|近义词：guitar, ukulele, mandolin|反义词:None|词性:noun
