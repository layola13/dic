orle:/ɔːrl/ | n. |  边饰，带状物 | A narrow band or stripe, typically of a different color, along the edge of something, especially in heraldry. | The shield has an orle of gold. 盾牌上有一圈金色的边饰。| 近义词: border, rim, edge | 反义词: center, middle | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Old French *orle, from Vulgar Latin *ōrla, from Latin ōra (“border, edge”).
