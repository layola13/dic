bulimus:/ˈbjuːlɪməs/| n. |非洲大蝸牛 (尤指在美國南部發現的)|A large land snail, especially one of a kind found in the southern US. It's considered an invasive species.|例句：The bulimus snail is a voracious eater and can cause significant damage to crops. 非洲大蝸牛食量很大，会对农作物造成重大损害。

例句：The introduction of the bulimus snail to Florida has had devastating consequences for local ecosystems.  非洲大蝸牛被引入佛罗里达州对当地生态系统造成了破坏性后果。|近义词：Giant African land snail, Achatina fulica|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

词源: From Latin *būlimus*, from Ancient Greek βουλῖμος (*boulimos*), meaning "ravenous hunger".  The name refers to the snail's voracious appetite.
