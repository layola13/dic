fiendlike:/ˈfiːndlaɪk/| adj.|恶魔般的；极其残忍的；极恶的|Resembling or characteristic of a fiend; diabolically cruel or wicked.|He had a fiendlike grin on his face as he plotted his revenge. 他在策划复仇时脸上露出了恶魔般的狞笑。|The dictator's fiendlike regime terrorized the population.  这位独裁者的恶魔般政权恐吓着人民。| 近义词:diabolical, demonic, hellish, satanic, evil, wicked, cruel, inhuman, monstrous|反义词:angelic, benevolent, kind, compassionate, humane|词性:形容词

词源:
From fiend +‎ -like.  "Fiend" itself comes from Old English *fēond (attested as gefēon, gefā), meaning "enemy," related to Old High German fiant "enemy" and Gothic fijands.
