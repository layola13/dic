overaccumulate:/ˌoʊvərəˈkjuːmjəleɪt/
verb
过度积累
to accumulate excessively; to gather or amass more than is needed or desirable
The company overaccumulated inventory, leading to storage problems and losses due to spoilage.  这家公司过度积累库存，导致储存问题和由于腐败造成的损失。

The squirrel overaccumulated nuts for the winter, burying more than it could ever eat.  这只松鼠为冬天过度储存了坚果，埋藏的数量超过了它所能吃掉的。

近义词:overstock, hoard, amass
反义词:deplete, exhaust, squander
词性:verb
