enucleation:/ɪˌnjuːkliˈeɪʃən/ | n. |去核; 摘除 |The surgical removal of something, especially an eye, a tumor, or an organ.  The process of removing the nucleus from a cell. |例句：The enucleation of the tumor was successful. 肿瘤的摘除手术很成功。

例句：Enucleation of the eye was necessary due to the advanced stage of the cancer. 由于癌症晚期，必须摘除眼球。|近义词：removal, excision, extraction |反义词: implantation, insertion | noun
