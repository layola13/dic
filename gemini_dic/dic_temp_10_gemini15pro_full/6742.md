analytica: /ˌænəˈlɪtɪkə/ | adj. | 分析的，解析的 | Relating to or using analysis or logic. |  Example:  An analytical approach to problem-solving.  分析性的问题解决方法。

Example: She has a very analytical mind. 她有一个非常善于分析的头脑。 | 近义词: logical, rational, diagnostic | 反义词: intuitive, emotional, synthetic | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *analyticus, from Ancient Greek ἀναλυτικός (analytikós, “dissolving, resolving”), from ἀναλύω (analúō, “I dissolve, resolve”).
