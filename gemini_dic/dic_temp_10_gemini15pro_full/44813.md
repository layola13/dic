crusado:/kruːˈzɑːduː/ /kruˈzeɪduː/| n.|克鲁萨多（葡萄牙和巴西旧货币单位）|A former monetary unit of Portugal and Brazil.|He paid 1000 cruzados for the antique chair. 他花了1000克鲁萨多买了那把古董椅子.|Synonyms: Portuguese currency, Brazilian currency|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific currency unit)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Portuguese *cruzado* "marked with a cross," from *cruz* "cross," from Latin *crux* "cross."  The name originally referred to a gold coin bearing a cross, first minted in Portugal in 1435.
