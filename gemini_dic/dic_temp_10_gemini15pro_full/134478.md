nonlabelings:/ˌnɒnˈleɪbəlɪŋz/ | n. | 非标记，无标签 | Absence of labels or classifications.  The process or state of not applying labels or categories to something.  It often implies a deliberate avoidance of categorization for various reasons, such as promoting inclusivity or acknowledging complexity. |  The researcher advocated for nonlabelings in the study to avoid bias. 研究人员提倡在研究中不贴标签以避免偏见。

The nonlabelings of individuals in the group fostered a sense of unity.  小组中个人没有标签促进了团结感。 | synonyms:  uncategorized, unclassified, undefined, unspecified  | antonyms: labeling, classifying, categorizing, defining, specifying | noun
