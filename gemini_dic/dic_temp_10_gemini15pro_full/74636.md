free-based:/ˈfriːbeɪst/ | adj. |游离碱的；(毒品)提纯的|Containing pure cocaine that has been separated from its hydrochloride salt, typically by heating it with baking soda.  This process makes the drug smokable.|He was arrested for possession of free-based cocaine.|近义词:crack cocaine, pure cocaine|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "cocaine hydrochloride" as the unprocessed form)|adjective
