darwinius:/dɑːrˈwɪniəs/| n. |达尔文猴|An extinct primate genus from the Eocene epoch, considered a significant transitional fossil in primate evolution.  It is named after Charles Darwin.|The discovery of Darwinius masillae provided valuable insights into early primate evolution. 达尔文猴的发现为早期灵长类动物的进化提供了宝贵的见解。|Synonym: Adapid|Antonym: N/A|noun
