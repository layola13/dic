homogenized:/həˈmɑːdʒənaɪzd/ /həˈmɒdʒənaɪzd/| adj.|均质化的，同质化的|Made uniform or consistent throughout a substance or mixture by breaking down and blending its components.|The milk we buy from the supermarket is homogenized. 我们从超市买的牛奶是均质化的。|
Homogenized milk is easier to digest. 均质牛奶更容易消化。|
Synonyms: uniform, consistent, standardized, blended|Antonyms: heterogeneous, varied, diverse|adjective
