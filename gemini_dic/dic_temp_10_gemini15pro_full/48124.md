decoction:/dɪˈkɒkʃən/
n.
煎煮液，汤剂
A liquid extract obtained by boiling a substance, especially a plant material, in water.
The herbalist prescribed a decoction of chamomile and lavender to soothe her nerves. 草药师开了一种洋甘菊和薰衣草的煎剂来舒缓她的神经。
extract, infusion, tea
solution, dilution
noun
