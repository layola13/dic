fossicker:/ˈfɒsɪkə(r)/ (UK), /ˈfɑːsɪkər/ (US)| n.| 淘金者；翻找者；搜寻者|Someone who searches for something, especially by rummaging or digging; often used for someone who searches for gold or precious stones in old diggings or riverbeds.|He's been fossicking around in the attic all morning. 他整个上午都在阁楼里翻找东西。|
|The old fossicker found a small nugget of gold.  这位老淘金者发现了一小块金子。|
|Synonyms: prospector, searcher, hunter, rummager, scavenger|
|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider someone who discards or neglects things)|
|noun|
