indomitably:/ɪnˈdɒmɪtəbli/| adv.|不屈不挠地，顽强地|In an indomitable manner; unyieldingly; stubbornly; resolutely.  Showing an unwillingness to be subdued or defeated.|She fought indomitably against the disease. 她不屈不挠地与病魔作斗争。|近义词: resolutely, stubbornly, persistently, tenaciously|反义词: submissively, weakly, yieldingly|词性:副词

词源: From Latin *indomitabilis*, from in- "not" + domitabilis "tamable," from domitare "to tame," frequentative of domare "to tame, subdue."
