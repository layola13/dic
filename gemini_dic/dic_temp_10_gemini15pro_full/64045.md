epithet:/ˈɛpɪθɛt/ | n. | 绰号，称号，修饰语 | An adjective or descriptive phrase expressing a quality characteristic of the person or thing mentioned. |  Homer's epithet for the dawn is "rosy-fingered." 荷马对黎明的称号是“玫瑰指的”。

Achilles is often referred to by the epithet "swift-footed."  阿喀琉斯常被称为“神行太保”。| synonym: nickname, sobriquet, appellation, designation | antonym: anonymity,  namelessness | noun
