cryptogamian: /ˌkrɪptəˈɡeɪmiən/ | adj. n. | 隐花植物的；隐花植物 |  Relating to or denoting a plant that reproduces by spores, without flowers or seeds.  Also, a cryptogam is a plant of this type. |  Ferns and mosses are cryptogamian plants. 蕨类植物和苔藓是隐花植物。 | Cryptogamic, spore-bearing |  Phanerogam, flowering plant | adjective, noun
