coinstantaneous:/ˌkoʊɪnstənˈteɪniəs/| adj.|同时发生的，同时存在的|Occurring or existing at the same instant.|The two events were coinstantaneous, happening at precisely the same moment.|近义词：simultaneous, concurrent, synchronous|反义词：non-simultaneous, asynchronous|词性:形容词
Etymology: co- (together) + instantaneous (happening in an instant) From Latin 'con-' (with, together) + 'instantanea' (present moment).
