imagesetters:/ˈɪmɪdʒˌsɛtərz/| n.|图像输出机，照排机|A person or device that produces high-quality images, typically for printing.  This often refers to a high-resolution output device used in the prepress stage of printing.  It can also refer to someone skilled in creating and preparing images for print.|The imagesetters produced crisp, clear film for the printing plates.|近义词：output device, typesetter, printer|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "scanner" as it inputs rather than outputs)|noun
