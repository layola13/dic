circulate: /ˈsɜːrkjəleɪt/ (US) /ˈsɜːkjʊleɪt/ (UK)
verb
循环；流通；传播
to move around or flow in a circle or circuit; to pass from person to person or place to place
Blood circulates throughout the body. 血液在全身循环。
The news circulated quickly through the office.  这个消息在办公室里迅速传播开来。
近义词: spread, distribute, disseminate, flow, move, travel
反义词: stagnate, localize, restrict
词性: verb

circulate: /ˈsɜːrkjəleɪt/ (US) /ˈsɜːkjʊleɪt/ (UK)
noun
印刷品；传单；期刊
a printed notice, leaflet, or periodical, especially one that is distributed to a large number of people
The library circulates a monthly newsletter. 图书馆每月发行一份通讯。
This is a confidential circulate for staff only. 这是一份仅供员工阅读的机密传单。
近义词: publication, leaflet, pamphlet, periodical
反义词: N/A (Noun form is less common and doesn't have a direct antonym)
词性: noun
