mismated:/ˌmɪsˈmeɪtɪd/
adjective
不相配的，不合适的
ill-matched, unsuitably partnered
The mismated couple argued constantly. 这对不相配的夫妇经常争吵。
近义词: ill-matched, incompatible, unsuitable, uncongenial
反义词: well-matched, compatible, suitable, congenial
词性: adjective

mismated:/ˌmɪsˈmeɪtɪd/
verb
错误地配对
to match or pair wrongly
The breeder mismated the dogs, resulting in unhealthy puppies.  饲养员错误地给狗配对，导致幼犬不健康。
近义词: mismatch
反义词: match
词性: verb (past participle)


词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + "mated" (past participle of "mate," meaning "to join together as a pair")
