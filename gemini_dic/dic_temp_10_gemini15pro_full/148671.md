overtimid: /ˌoʊvərˈtɪmɪd/ | adj. | 过于羞怯的，胆小的 | Excessively timid; shy to an excessive degree. | She was so overtimid that she barely spoke to anyone at the party. 她太胆小了，在派对上几乎没和任何人说话。|  He was overtimid about asking for a raise. 他不敢要求加薪。 | 近义词：shy, timid, bashful, diffident, apprehensive | 反义词：bold, confident, audacious, extroverted | 词性: 形容词

词源：
From over- + timid.  "Over-" acts as an intensifier, meaning "excessively" or "too much." "Timid" comes from the Latin timidus, meaning "fearful" or "timorous."
