continues:/kənˈtɪnjuːz/ /kənˈtɪnjuːs/| v. |继续，持续|  To persist in an activity or process. |The rain continues to fall. 雨继续下。|Synonyms:  persist, proceed, carry on, go on, last, endure|Antonyms: stop, cease, halt, pause, discontinue|verb

continues:/kənˈtɪnjuːz/ /kənˈtɪnjuːs/| adj. |连续的|  Marked by uninterrupted extension in space, time, or sequence.  (Less common usage)| This is a continues line. 这是一条连续的线。|Synonyms:  uninterrupted, unbroken, continuous, constant|Antonyms: intermittent, broken, discontinuous|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *continuen*, from Old French continuer, from Latin continuāre (“to join together, connect, make continuous”), from continuus (“continuous”).
