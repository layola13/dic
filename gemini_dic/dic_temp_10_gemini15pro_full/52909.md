dimnesses:/ˈdɪmnɪsɪz/
noun
昏暗，朦胧，模糊不清
The state of being dim; lack of brightness or clarity.
The dimnesses of the old photographs made it hard to identify the people. 这些老照片的昏暗使得很难辨认出照片中的人。
近义词: darkness, obscurity, faintness, dullness
反义词: brightness, clarity, vividness, brilliance
词性:n.


dimness:/ˈdɪmnɪs/
noun
昏暗，朦胧，模糊不清
The state of being dim; lack of brightness or clarity.
The dimness of the room made it difficult to read. 房间的昏暗使阅读变得困难。
近义词: darkness, obscurity, faintness, dullness
反义词: brightness, clarity, vividness, brilliance
词性:n.


dim:/dɪm/
adjective
昏暗的，朦胧的，模糊不清的
Not bright or clear; faint.
The dim light made it hard to see. 微弱的光线使人看不清。
近义词: faint, weak, obscure, dull
反义词: bright, clear, vivid, brilliant
词性:adj.

dim:/dɪm/
verb
使变暗，使变模糊
To make or become less bright or clear.
The fog dimmed the headlights. 雾使车头灯变暗了。
近义词: darken, obscure, cloud, dull
反义词: brighten, illuminate, clarify
词性:v.
