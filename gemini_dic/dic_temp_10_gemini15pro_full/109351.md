lenitive:/ˈlɛnɪtɪv/| adj. n.|安慰的；缓和的；止痛的；润肤剂；镇痛剂|soothing or mitigating, especially to pain or sorrow.  Can be used as both an adjective and a noun.|The doctor prescribed a lenitive balm for her sunburn. 医生给她开了舒缓晒伤的药膏。|Synonyms: soothing, calming, alleviating, palliative, assuaging, mollifying|Antonyms: irritating, aggravating, exacerbating|adjective

lenitive:/ˈlɛnɪtɪv/| n.|润肤剂；镇痛剂|a lenitive substance or agent.|She applied a lenitive to the wound. 她给伤口涂了镇痛药。|Synonyms: balm, ointment, salve|Antonyms: irritant|noun
