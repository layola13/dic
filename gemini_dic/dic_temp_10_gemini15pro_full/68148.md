failer:/ˈfeɪlər/ | n. | 失败者 |  A person or thing that fails to succeed or meet expectations. | He felt like a failer after losing the competition. 他在比赛失利后感觉自己像个失败者。|  She considered the project a failer due to its poor performance.  由于项目表现不佳，她认为该项目失败了。| 近义词: loser, non-starter, flop | 反义词: winner, success, achiever | 词性: noun
