indecorum:/ˌɪndɪˈkɔːrəm/ | n. |失礼，不得体|Lack of decorum; impropriety.  A violation of etiquette or propriety. |Her indecorum at the funeral shocked the mourners. 她的不雅行为在葬礼上震惊了哀悼者。| The king was shocked by the visitor's indecorum. 国王对来访者的失礼感到震惊。 | 近义词: impropriety, indecency, unseemliness | 反义词: decorum, propriety, decency | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *indecorus* "unbecoming, disgraceful," from *in-* "not" + *decorus* "becoming, proper, graceful," related to *decor* "beauty, grace."
