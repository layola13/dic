concisely:/kənˈsaɪsli/ /kənˈsaɪsli/ | adv. | 简洁地，简明地 | In a brief and to-the-point manner; without unnecessary words or details. | She concisely explained the complex issue. 她简洁地解释了这个复杂的问题。| briefly, succinctly, shortly | verbosely, lengthily | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *concisus*, past participle of *concidere* "to cut off, cut down, cut short," from *com-* "together" (see com-) + *caedere* "to cut" (see -cide).
