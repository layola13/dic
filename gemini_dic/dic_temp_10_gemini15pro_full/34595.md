cleated:/ˈkliːtɪd/ | adj. |带防滑钉的，有鞋钉的 |Fitted with cleats, especially for grip.  | The football player wore cleated shoes. 足球运动员穿着带钉的鞋。|  近义词：studded, spiked | 反义词：smooth, flat | 词性：形容词

cleat:/kliːt/ | n. |防滑钉，楔子，夹板 | A piece of metal or wood fixed to something, or a projection on something, designed to give grip or prevent slipping. Also, a wedge or block fastened to something to give strength or act as a support. |  He secured the rope to the cleat on the deck. 他把绳子固定在甲板上的夹板上。 | 近义词：stud, spike, wedge | 反义词: none | 词性：名词
