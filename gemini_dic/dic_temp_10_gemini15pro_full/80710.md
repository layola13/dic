gorin:/ˈɡɔːrɪn/| n. |五人小组，五人委员会| A group or council of five people, especially in Japanese contexts. | The gorin oversaw the distribution of resources. 五人小组监督资源的分配。| The gorin made the final decision. 五人委员会做出了最终决定。|近义词：council, committee, board, panel|反义词: individual|词性: 名词


词源: From Japanese 五輪 (gorin), meaning "five rings."  This often refers to the five Olympic rings, but can also more generally refer to a group of five.
