imminentness:/ˈɪmɪnəntnəs/| n. |即将发生；迫近；逼近|The imminentness of the storm prompted us to evacuate.  暴风雨即将到来，促使我们撤离。|例句：The imminentness of the danger was palpable. 危险迫在眉睫，显而易见。|近义词：imminence, nearness, proximity|反义词：remoteness, distance|词性:名词

词源:  From Late Latin imminent- (stem of imminens), present participle of imminēre 'to overhang, impend, be at hand,' from in- 'into, upon, towards' + minēre 'to project, threaten.'
