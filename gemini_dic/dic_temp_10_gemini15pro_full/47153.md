dawdle:/ˈdɔːdl/ (US), /ˈdɔːd(ə)l/ (UK)| v. |闲逛，磨蹭，拖延|To waste time; to spend time idly; to move slowly and lackadaisically.|He dawdled over his breakfast, reading the newspaper. 他慢吞吞地吃着早餐，读着报纸。|She dawdled on her way to school, stopping to look at the flowers.  她在上学的路上磨磨蹭蹭，停下来看花。|Synonyms: linger, loiter, tarry, delay, procrastinate|Antonyms: hasten, hurry, rush|verb
