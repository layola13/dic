biodiversity:/ˌbaɪoʊdaɪˈvɜːrsəti/ | n. | 生物多样性 |  The variety of life in the world or in a particular habitat or ecosystem. |例句：The Amazon rainforest is known for its incredible biodiversity. 亚马逊雨林以其令人难以置信的生物多样性而闻名。

例句：Protecting biodiversity is crucial for the health of our planet. 保护生物多样性对我们星球的健康至关重要。|近义词：biological diversity |反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用monoculture单一栽培，或homogeneity同质性表达相反的概念) |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From bio- (life) + diversity.  The term was coined by Walter G. Rosen in 1985.
