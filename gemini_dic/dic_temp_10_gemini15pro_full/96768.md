inequality:/ˌɪnɪˈkwɒləti/ (US) /ˌɪnɪˈkwɑːləti/ (UK)| n. |不平等，不均等；差异；不等式|lack of equality; difference in size, degree, circumstances, etc.; a statement that two quantities are not equal|The growing inequality between rich and poor is a serious social problem. 贫富差距日益扩大是一个严重的社会问题。|synonyms: disparity, imbalance, unevenness, disproportion, asymmetry|antonyms: equality, parity, uniformity, balance|noun
