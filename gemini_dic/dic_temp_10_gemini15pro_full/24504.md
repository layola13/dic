broad-based:/ˌbrɔːdˈbeɪst/ | adj. | 基础广泛的，涉及面广的 | Having a wide range or involving many different groups of people or things. |  The government introduced a broad-based tax cut to stimulate the economy. 政府推出了基础广泛的减税政策以刺激经济。| synonyms: wide-ranging, comprehensive, extensive, diversified | antonyms: narrow, limited, restricted, specialized | adjective

broad-based:/ˌbrɔːdˈbeɪst/ | adj. | 分布广泛的，根基牢固的 |  Having a wide distribution or a solid foundation. |  The company has a broad-based customer base across the country. 这家公司在全国拥有广泛的客户群。| synonyms: widespread, pervasive, established, entrenched | antonyms: localized, concentrated, unstable, precarious | adjective
