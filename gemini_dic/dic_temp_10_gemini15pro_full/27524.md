camporated:/ˈkæmpəreɪtɪd/
adjective
樟脑处理过的; 含有樟脑的
treated with or containing camphor.
Camphorated oil can be used to relieve muscle aches. 樟脑油可以用来缓解肌肉酸痛。
近义词: camphoric
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "uncamphorated" though not commonly used)
词性: adjective
