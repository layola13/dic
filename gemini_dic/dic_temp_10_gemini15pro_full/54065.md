diseased:/dɪˈziːzd/
adjective
患病的；有病的
Affected by a disease; unhealthy or morbid.
The diseased tree had to be cut down.  这棵病树必须砍掉。
diseased lungs 患病的肺


近义词:sick, ill, ailing, unhealthy, infected
反义词:healthy, well, sound

词性:adjective

词源:From Middle English *diseased, *dised, from Anglo-Norman diseasé, disaisé, past participle of diseaser, desaiser (“to dispossess, seize, trouble, make uneasy, discomfort”), from des- + aise (“ease”). Equivalent to dis- + ease + -ed.
