fun-loving:/ˈfʌnˌlʌvɪŋ/| adj.|爱好娱乐的，喜欢玩乐的|Someone who is fun-loving enjoys having fun and doing enjoyable things.  This adjective is formed by combining the noun "fun" with the present participle of the verb "love," indicating a characteristic or disposition.|例句：She's a fun-loving person who always enjoys a good party. 她是一个喜欢玩乐的人，总是喜欢参加派对。

例句：My fun-loving uncle is always the life of the party. 我那爱玩的叔叔总是派对的中心人物。

|近义词：lighthearted, jovial, playful, easygoing|反义词:serious, dour, austere|词性:adjective
