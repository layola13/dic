passive:/ˈpæsɪv/ | adj. n.|被动的，消极的；被动语态；被动式| subjected to or receptive of an action or influence without active participation; not acting or participating; acted upon| The dog was passive during the grooming session.  这只狗在美容过程中很顺从。| synonyms: inactive, inert, submissive, receptive | antonyms: active, energetic, assertive, resistant | adjective

passive:/ˈpæsɪv/ | n.|被动语态；被动式|the grammatical voice used to indicate that the grammatical subject is the recipient of the action denoted by the verb|  We use the passive voice when the doer of the action is unknown or unimportant. 当我们不知道或不认为动作的执行者重要时，我们会使用被动语态。| synonyms: passive voice | antonyms: active voice | noun
