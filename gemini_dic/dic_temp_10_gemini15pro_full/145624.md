overconstant:/ˈoʊvərˈkɑːnstənt/| adj.| 过分恒定的，过于稳定的|Excessively constant; too unwavering or unchanging.|The overconstant temperature in the lab made the experiment unreliable. 实验室里过分恒定的温度使得实验结果不可靠。|The overconstant pressure from his parents made him feel suffocated.  父母持续不断的压力让他感到窒息。|近义词：invariable, unchanging, unvarying, uniform, consistent, stable, steady, fixed, unwavering, resolute, steadfast, firm|反义词：variable, fluctuating, erratic, inconsistent, unstable, unsteady|词性:形容词

Etymology:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive," "too much") + "constant" (from Latin "constans," present participle of "constare" meaning "to stand firm," from "com-" (together) + "stare" (to stand))
