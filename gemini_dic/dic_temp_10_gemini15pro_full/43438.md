crapshoot:/ˈkræpʃuːt/
noun
掷骰子赌博; 碰运气的事;  冒险的事情
a situation in which the result is very uncertain; a gamble
例句：Starting a new business can be a real crapshoot.  创业就像一场赌博。

例句：The election is a crapshoot; no one knows who will win.  这次选举结果难料，没人知道谁会赢。
synonym: gamble, risk, chance, lottery
antonym: sure thing, certainty, guarantee
词性: 名词
