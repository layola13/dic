hardship:/ˈhɑːrdʃɪp/| n.|艰难，困苦|Something that is hard to bear; difficulty.  A condition that causes suffering or privation.|例句：They faced many hardships during the war. 他们在战争期间面临许多苦难。

例句：The loss of her job caused her great hardship.  失去工作给她带来了巨大的困难。

近义词：difficulty, adversity, tribulation, struggle, suffering, privation, burden, affliction, trial, ordeal|反义词:comfort, ease, luxury, prosperity|词性:名词
