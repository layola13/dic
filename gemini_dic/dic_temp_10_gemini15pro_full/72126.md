floodable:/ˈflʌdəbl/| adj.|可淹没的，可泛滥的|Susceptible to being flooded or inundated with water.|The basement is floodable during heavy rains. 地下室在下大雨时容易被淹。|近义词：inundable, submersible, submergible|反义词:floodproof, impervious|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From flood (n.) + -able.  "Flood" comes from Old English *flōd, from Proto-Germanic *floduz, related to flow.  "-able" is a suffix of Latin origin, meaning "capable of being," "worthy of being," or "tending to."
