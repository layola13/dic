maharanees:/ˌmɑːhəˈrɑːniz/
noun
王妃；女王；女土邦主
A Hindu queen or princess, especially a wife or widow of a maharaja.
The maharanees of India were known for their opulent jewelry.  印度的王妃们以其华丽的珠宝而闻名。
synonyms: queen, princess, rani
antonyms: commoner, peasant
