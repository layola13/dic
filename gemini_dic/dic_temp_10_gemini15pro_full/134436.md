nonirrevocableness:/ˌnɒnɪrɪˈvɒkəbəlnəs/
|n.| 不可撤销性|The state or quality of not being irrevocable; the possibility of being reversed or undone.|The contract emphasized the nonirrevocableness of the agreement, assuring both parties that changes could be made.|Synonyms: revocability, reversibility, alterability|Antonyms: irrevocability, irreversibility, finality|Noun
