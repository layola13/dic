bargeboards: /ˈbɑːrdʒbɔːrdz/
n.
挡风板，封檐板
Boards, often ornately carved, fixed to the projecting gables of a roof to conceal the rafters.
The bargeboards of the Victorian house were intricately carved with flowers and leaves.  维多利亚时代房屋的封檐板雕刻着复杂的花卉和树叶。
近义词:fascia boards, gable boards
反义词:(No direct antonym,  perhaps "exposed rafters")
词性:noun


bargeboard: /ˈbɑːrdʒbɔːrd/
n.
挡风板，封檐板
A board, often ornately carved, fixed to the projecting gable of a roof to conceal the rafters.
The bargeboard was damaged in the storm. 挡风板在暴风雨中受损了。
近义词:fascia board, gable board
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "exposed rafters")
词性:noun


词源:  "barge" (originally a small ship, later a flat-bottomed boat) + "board".  The original meaning referred to a board used on a barge. Over time, the term came to refer to the boards used on gables, perhaps due to their resemblance to the shape of a barge.
