impersonification:/ɪmˌpɜːrsənɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ | n. |  扮演；模仿；拟人化；化身 | The act of pretending to be another person, especially to deceive or defraud someone.  The attribution of human qualities to inanimate objects or abstract concepts. |  His impersonification of the president was remarkably accurate. 他对总统的模仿非常逼真。  The poem uses impersonification to give the trees human emotions. 这首诗用拟人化赋予树木人类的情感。 | synonyms: imitation, mimicking, portrayal, embodiment, personification | antonyms: N/A | noun
