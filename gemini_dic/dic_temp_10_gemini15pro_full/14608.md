avision:/əˈvɪʒən/ | n. | 预感；幻象；幻觉；神示 | A strong feeling that something is going to happen, especially something unpleasant.  A sight or image that appears to a person in a dream, trance, or religious experience. |  She had an avision of her own death. 她预感到自己快要死了。 | 近义词: premonition, foreboding, vision, apparition | 反义词:  None | 词性: 名词
