flysheet:/ˈflaɪʃiːt/
n.
防水布; 天幕; 帐篷外罩
A piece of waterproof material used as a secondary outer cover for a tent, protecting it from rain.
We used a flysheet to keep our tent dry during the storm. 我们在暴风雨中用防水布保持帐篷干燥。
tarpaulin, cover
