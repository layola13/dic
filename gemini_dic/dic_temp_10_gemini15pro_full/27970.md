cants:/kænts/ | n. | 黑话；行话；伪善之言 |  Slang; jargon; hypocritical talk, especially about moral or religious issues. |  He spoke in pious cants, but his actions betrayed him. 他满口仁义道德，但他的行为却出卖了他。 | synonyms: hypocrisy, jargon, slang, argot, patter | antonyms: sincerity, honesty, genuineness | noun

cants:/kænts/ | v. |  倾斜；用斜角；(使)倾斜；(使)翘起 | To tilt or lean; to bevel; to set at an oblique angle. | The ship cants to one side in heavy seas. 船在汹涌的海浪中向一侧倾斜。 | synonyms: tilt, lean, slope, slant, incline | antonyms: straighten, level | verb
