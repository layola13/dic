harvesting:/ˈhɑːrvɪstɪŋ/| n. | 收获；收割 | The process of gathering a ripe crop from the fields. | The harvesting of the wheat crop is now complete. 小麦的收割现已完成。 | 近义词: gathering, reaping, collecting | 反义词: sowing, planting | noun

harvesting:/ˈhɑːrvɪstɪŋ/| adj. | 收获的 | Related to or involved in the process of harvesting.  | The harvesting season is busy for farmers. 收获季节对农民来说很忙碌。 | 近义词: reaping | 反义词: sowing | adjective
