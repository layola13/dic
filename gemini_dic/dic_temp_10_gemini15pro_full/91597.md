hyperaggregates:/ˌhaɪpərˈæɡrɪɡeɪts/ | n. | 超聚集体；过度聚集体 |  Clumps of platelets, white blood cells, and red blood cells that form within the blood vessels, particularly in small vessels, and contribute to blockage of blood flow.  These are larger than typical platelet aggregates. | Platelet hyperaggregates were observed in patients with acute coronary syndrome. 在急性冠脉综合征患者中观察到血小板超聚集体。| synonyms: clumps, clusters, masses | antonyms: dispersed cells, individual cells | noun
