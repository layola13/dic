orpiment:/ˈɔːrpɪmənt/ | n. |雄黄，雌黄 |A bright yellow mineral, arsenic trisulfide (As₂S₃), formerly used as a pigment. |Orpiment was used as a pigment in ancient Egyptian art. 雄黄在古埃及艺术中被用作颜料。|  Orpiment is a toxic mineral that should be handled with care. 雄黄是一种有毒矿物，应小心处理。|Synonyms: arsenic yellow, King's yellow | Antonyms: N/A | noun
