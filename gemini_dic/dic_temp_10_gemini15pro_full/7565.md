The correct spelling is *annotated*.  "Annodated" isn't a recognized English word.

annotated: /ˈænəteɪtɪd/
| adj. | 注解的，带注释的 | Provided with explanatory notes or comments. | The annotated bibliography provided valuable context for the research.  带注释的参考书目为研究提供了宝贵的背景资料。 |  The professor assigned an annotated edition of Shakespeare's Hamlet. 教授布置了莎士比亚《哈姆雷特》的注释版。 | commented, explained, glossed | unannotated | adjective

词源: From the verb "annotate," which comes from the Latin "annotatus," the past participle of "annotare" (to mark, note), from "ad-" (to) + "notare" (to note).
