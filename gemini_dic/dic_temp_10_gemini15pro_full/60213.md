effluent:/ˈɛfluːənt/ | n. | 流出物，污水，废水 | Liquid waste or sewage discharged into a river or the sea. | The factory's effluent polluted the river. 工厂的废水污染了河流。| The chemical effluent contaminated the soil. 化学废水污染了土壤。 |  synonyms: discharge, outflow, waste, sewage | antonyms: inflow, influx | noun
