dirt:/dɜːrt/ (UK), /dɝːt/ (US)| n. |污垢，泥土，灰尘|Loose earth or soil.|Example: The children were covered in dirt after playing in the garden. 孩子们在花园里玩耍后浑身是泥土。|Example:  Wash the dirt off your hands before dinner.  饭前把手上的脏东西洗掉。 |Synonyms: grime, filth, soil, dust|Antonyms: cleanliness, purity|Noun

dirt:/dɜːrt/ (UK), /dɝːt/ (US)| n. |丑闻，流言蜚语|Information about someone, especially a celebrity, that could damage their reputation.|Example:  They published all the dirt they could find on the politician. 他们发表了所有能找到的关于这位政治家的丑闻。|Example:  She threatened to spread dirt about him if he didn't pay up. 她威胁说如果他不付钱，她就散布关于他的流言蜚语。 |Synonyms: gossip, scandal, rumors|Antonyms: praise, acclaim|Noun
