diphycercal:/daɪˈfɪsɜːrkl/| adj.|双尾的，原尾的|Having two lobes to the tail fin, symmetrically arranged above and below the vertebral column which extends to the tip of the tail.  A tail fin in which the vertebral column extends to the tip, dividing the fin into dorsal and ventral lobes. |The coelacanth has a distinctive diphycercal tail.腔棘鱼有一个独特的双尾。|The ancient fish fossil displayed a diphycercal tail structure. 这块古代鱼类化石展现了双尾结构。|synonym:homocercal (in some usages, though not strictly synonymous)|antonym:heterocercal|adjective
