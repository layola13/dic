anticathode:/ˌæntiˈkæθoʊd/| n. |对阴极，靶|The anticathode is the target in an X-ray tube, which electrons strike to produce X-rays.|Electrons striking the anticathode produce X-rays. 电子撞击对阴极产生X射线。|Synonym: target|Antonym: cathode|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + cathode.  "Anti-" prefix meaning "opposite" or "against". "Cathode" refers to the negative electrode.
