decomplex:/ˌdiːkəmˈplɛks/
verb
简化；化繁为简
to make something less complicated or easier to understand
The new software decomplexes the process of filing taxes.  这个新软件简化了报税的流程。
The government is trying to decomplex the tax system. 政府正在努力简化税收制度。
simplify, streamline
complicate, confuse
词性: 动词
