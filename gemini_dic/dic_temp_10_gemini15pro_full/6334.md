ampelite:/ˈæmpɪlaɪt/ | n. | 闪岩,  |A type of metamorphic rock, typically dark grey or black, containing aluminosilicates, especially andalusite, chiastolite, or kyanite. | The geologist identified the sample as ampelite due to its dark color and the presence of chiastolite.  地质学家根据样品的深色和红柱石的存在将其鉴定为闪岩。| 近义词:metamorphic rock, schist, slate | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific rock type) | 词性:noun
