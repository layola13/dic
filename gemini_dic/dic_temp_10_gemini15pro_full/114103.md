magnitude:/ˈmæɡnɪtjuːd/| n.|大小；量级；震级；重要性；巨大|Greatness of size, extent, or importance.|例句：They don’t seem to grasp the magnitude of the problem. 他们似乎没有意识到这个问题的严重性。

例句：The earthquake was of magnitude 8.2 on the Richter scale. 这次地震的里氏震级为8.2级。|近义词：size, extent, scale, degree, importance, significance|反义词:insignificance, unimportance|词性:noun
