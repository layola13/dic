gunship: /ˈɡʌnʃɪp/ | n. |武装直升机，炮艇|A heavily armed aircraft, helicopter or boat, equipped with guns and other weapons, used for attacking ground targets.| The gunship circled overhead, providing cover for the ground troops. 武装直升机在头顶盘旋，为地面部队提供掩护。| The navy deployed gunships to patrol the coastal waters. 海军部署了炮艇来巡逻沿海水域。|Synonyms: attack helicopter, warplane, warship| Antonyms: unarmed aircraft, passenger plane | noun
