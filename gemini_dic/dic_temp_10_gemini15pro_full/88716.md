hobbyists:/ˈhɒbiɪsts/ (UK) /ˈhɑːbiɪsts/ (US)| n.|业余爱好者；沉迷于某嗜好的人|People who engage in a hobby for pleasure and not as a profession.  A person who enjoys pursuing a particular activity in their spare time.|例句：The club is open to hobbyists of all levels.  这个俱乐部对所有级别的爱好者开放。

例句：Many hobbyists collect stamps or coins. 许多爱好者收集邮票或硬币。|近义词：enthusiasts, amateurs, devotees, aficionados|反义词：professionals|词性: 名词(plural)
