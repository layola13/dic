aborigines: /ˌæbəˈrɪdʒəniz/ | n. | 土著居民，原住民 |  A person, animal, or plant that has been in a country or region from earliest times. | The aborigines of Australia have a rich and complex culture. 澳大利亚的土著居民有着丰富而复杂的文化。

The government is working to protect the rights of aborigines. 政府正在努力保护土著居民的权利。 | 近义词: natives, indigenous people, first peoples | 反义词: immigrants, newcomers, settlers | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ab origine* "from the beginning," from *ab* "from" + *origo, originis* "origin, source, beginning."
