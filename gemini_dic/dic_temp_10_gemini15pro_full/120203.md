mineralization:/ˌmɪnərəlɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 矿化，矿化作用 | The process by which organic matter is converted into inorganic matter during decomposition, resulting in the release of nutrients back into the soil.  The process of converting something into a mineral or a mineral-like substance. | 例句1：The mineralization of the bone makes it strong and rigid. 骨骼的矿化作用使其坚固而僵硬。

例句2：The rate of mineralization in the soil is affected by temperature and moisture. 土壤中矿化作用的速率受温度和水分的影响。 | 近义词：petrification, fossilization | 反义词：decomposition, decay | 词性：名词
