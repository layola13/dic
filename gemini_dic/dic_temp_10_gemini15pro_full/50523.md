depthen:/ˈdɛpθən/| v. |加深，使变深|To make something deeper or more profound. |例句：The crisis deepened their bond.  这场危机加深了他们的联系。|近义词：deepen, intensify, strengthen|反义词:shallow, weaken|词性:动词

Etymology: From "depth" + "-en" (suffix forming verbs meaning "to make or become"). "Depth" comes from Old English "dēopþu," related to "dēop" (deep).
