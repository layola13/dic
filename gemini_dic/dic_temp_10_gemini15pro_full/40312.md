contortionist:/kənˈtɔːrʃənɪst/ | n. |柔术演员，扭曲主义者| A person who can twist their body into unusual positions.  A performer who specializes in extreme flexibility feats. | The contortionist bent her body into an unbelievable shape. 柔术演员将她的身体弯曲成一个令人难以置信的形状。|  The circus contortionist amazed the audience with her flexibility.  马戏团的柔术演员以其柔韧性令观众惊叹。 | synonyms: acrobat, gymnast, flexible person | antonyms: stiff person, inflexible person | noun

contort:/kənˈtɔːrt/ | vt. | 扭曲，扭弯 | To twist or bend out of its normal shape. |  His face was contorted in pain. 他的脸因疼痛而扭曲。 | Anger contorted his features. 愤怒使他的面容扭曲。 | synonyms: twist, deform, warp, distort | antonyms: straighten, smooth | verb
