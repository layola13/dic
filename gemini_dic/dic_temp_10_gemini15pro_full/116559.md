matildes:/mɑːˈtildɛz/ | n. | 波浪号; 玛蒂尔德（女子名） |  A diacritical mark (˜) placed over a letter to indicate a particular sound value, especially in Spanish, Portuguese, and some other languages, as in señor, São Paulo. Also, a female given name. |  The word "cañon" uses a tilde over the 'n'.  这个单词“cañon”在字母'n'上使用了一个波浪号。 |  She was named Matildes after her grandmother. 她以祖母的名字命名为玛蒂尔德。 | synonyms: tildes (plural), diacritical mark, accent mark (for the symbol); Matilda, Mathilde (for the name) | antonyms: N/A (no direct opposite for a diacritical mark or a name) | noun
