cyclopaedia:/ˌsaɪkləˈpiːdiə/ /ˌsaɪkloʊˈpiːdiə/| n. |百科全书|A book or set of books giving information on many subjects or on many aspects of one subject and typically arranged alphabetically.|例句：He consulted the cyclopaedia for information on ancient Greece. 他查阅百科全书以获取有关古希腊的信息。

例句：The cyclopaedia provided a comprehensive overview of the topic. 这部百科全书对该主题进行了全面的概述。

|近义词：encyclopedia, lexicon |反义词:|n.
