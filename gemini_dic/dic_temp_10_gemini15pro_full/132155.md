noncuriosities:/ˌnɒnˌkjʊəriˈɒsətiz/ | n. | 缺乏好奇心; 冷漠 | Lack of curiosity; things that do not arouse interest. |  The museum curator was surprised by the students' noncuriosities about the ancient artifacts.  博物馆馆长对学生们对古代文物的漠不关心感到惊讶。| apathy, indifference, unconcern | curiosity, interest | noun


Etymology:
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + curiosity (from curious, from Latin curiosus "careful, inquisitive," from cura "care").
