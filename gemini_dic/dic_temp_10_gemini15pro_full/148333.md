overspaciousness:/ˌoʊvərˈspeɪʃəsnəs/
n.|过度宽敞|The overspaciousness of the apartment made it feel empty and impersonal.  这套公寓过于宽敞，让人感觉空旷而没有人情味。|
Etymology:  "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, beyond") + "spacious" (from Latin spatium "space, room") + "-ness" (Old English -nes(s), suffix forming nouns expressing state, quality, or condition).|
Example: The overspaciousness of the new office was initially exciting, but soon proved impractical. 新办公室的过度宽敞最初令人兴奋，但很快就被证明不切实际。|
Synonym: vastness, immensity, expanse|
Antonym: crampedness, confinement, smallness|
词性: noun
