classically:/ˈklæsɪkli/| adv.|古典地，经典地，传统地|In a way that is traditional or characteristic of the classical period or style.  It relates to the traditional standards and principles of ancient Greek or Roman literature, art, or architecture.|The building was classically designed with Doric columns. 这座建筑采用多立克柱式，设计古典。|近义词：traditionally, conventionally, typically|反义词:modernly, contemporarily|词性:副词
Etymology: From classical + -ly.  "Classical" ultimately derives from Latin "classicus," meaning "belonging to the highest class of citizens," later used to refer to writers of the highest rank.
