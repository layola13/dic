berate:/bɪˈreɪt/ | v. | 严厉责备，痛斥 | To scold or criticize someone angrily. |  The coach berated the team for their poor performance. 教练严厉斥责了球队糟糕的表现。

|Synonyms: scold, rebuke, reprimand, castigate, censure, lambaste, upbraid|Antonyms: praise, commend, compliment, laud|verb|
