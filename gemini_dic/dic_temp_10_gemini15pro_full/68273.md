fakes:/feɪks/
n.|赝品；骗子；假货；欺骗|Something that is not genuine; a fraud or forgery. |The painting was later discovered to be a fake. 这幅画后来被发现是赝品。| synonyms: forgeries, counterfeits, imitations, frauds, shams| antonyms: originals, authentics, genuine articles|noun
fakes:/feɪks/
v.|伪造；假装；捏造；模拟；|To make something appear genuine when it is not; to pretend or feign.|He faked his own death to escape his debts. 他伪造了自己的死亡以逃避债务。|synonyms: forge, counterfeit, fabricate, simulate, feign|antonyms: verify, authenticate, confirm|verb
