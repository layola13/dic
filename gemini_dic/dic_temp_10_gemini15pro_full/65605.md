evanesce:/ˌɛvəˈnɛs/| v. |消失，消散|To disappear gradually; vanish; fade away.|例句：The mist evanesced as the sun rose. 雾气随着太阳升起而消散。

例句：His youthful dreams of glory gradually evanesced. 他年轻时对荣耀的梦想逐渐消失了。|近义词：disappear, vanish, fade, dissolve|反义词：appear, materialize, emerge|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin evanescere "to vanish, pass away," from ex- "out" (see ex-) + vanescere "to vanish," inchoative of vanere "to vanish," related to vanus "empty, vain" (see vain).
