boscabel:/ˈbɒskəbəl/ | n. | 隐蔽处，藏身处 | A place of concealment; a hiding place.  Derived from Boscobel House in Shropshire, England, where Charles II hid after the Battle of Worcester in 1651. |  After the defeat, the king found refuge in the boscabel of a dense forest.  战败后，国王在一个茂密的森林里找到了藏身之处。 | synonyms: hideout, refuge, sanctuary, retreat | antonyms: exposure, vulnerability | noun
