demephitize:/diːˈmɛfɪtaɪz/
verb
去除瘴气；净化空气
To purify or rid of miasma or foul air.
The engineers worked to demephitize the swampy area. 工程师们致力于净化沼泽地区的空气。
The new ventilation system helped to demephitize the mine. 新的通风系统有助于净化矿井中的空气。
近义词:purify, cleanse, sanitize, disinfect
反义词:pollute, contaminate, taint
