edginesses:/ˈɛdʒɪnɪsɪz/
n.
锐利; 尖锐; 锋利; 急躁; 紧张
sharpness or nervousness
Her edginess was apparent in her quick, jerky movements. 她的急躁在她快速、急促的动作中显而易见。

近义词: sharpness, nervousness, anxiety, irritability, tension
反义词: calmness, composure, tranquility, relaxation

词性: 名词 (plural of edginess)

词源:  From edgy + -ness.  Edgy itself comes from edge, referring to a sharp or pointed border. The figurative sense of "nervous" developed later.
