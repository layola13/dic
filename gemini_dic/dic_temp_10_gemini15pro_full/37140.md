come-ons:/ˈkʌm ɑːnz/ (US), /ˈkʌm ɒnz/ (UK)
n.|诱惑；挑逗；引诱|Actions or words intended to attract or seduce someone.
例句：His cheesy come-ons made her uncomfortable. 他俗气的挑逗让她感到不舒服。

例句：She wasn't fooled by his smooth come-ons. 她没有被他油嘴滑舌的引诱所迷惑。

近义词：advances, flirtations, enticements, seductions
反义词：rejections, rebuffs
词性：名词(plural)


come-on:/ˈkʌm ɑːn/ (US), /ˈkʌm ɒn/ (UK)
n.|劝诱；鼓励|An encouragement to do something, often in a competitive situation.
例句：The coach yelled a come-on to his team. 教练向他的团队大声喊叫鼓励。

例句：The crowd's come-ons energized the players. 观众的鼓励激励了球员。

近义词：encouragement, exhortation
反义词：discouragement
词性：名词(singular)


come-on:/ˈkʌm ɑːn/ (US), /ˈkʌm ɒn/ (UK)
int.|得了吧；算了吧；别这样|An expression of disbelief, impatience, or protest.
例句："Come on, you don't really expect me to believe that!" “得了吧，你不会真的指望我会相信吧！”

例句："Come on, let's get going!" “快点，我们走吧！”

近义词：oh, please, really
反义词：(No direct antonym, depends on context)
词性：感叹词
