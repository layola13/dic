pamplona:/pæmˈploʊnə/ /pæmˈpluːnə/
noun
潘普洛纳 (西班牙城市)
Pamplona is the capital city of the Navarre region in Spain, famous for the San Fermín festival, particularly the Running of the Bulls.
He visited Pamplona during the San Fermín festival.  他去潘普洛纳参加了圣费尔明节。
Pamplona is a vibrant city with a rich history.  潘普洛纳是一座充满活力、历史悠久的城市。
