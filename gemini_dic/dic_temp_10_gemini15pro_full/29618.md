categorizing:/ˈkætəɡəˌraɪzɪŋ/
|v.|分类，归类|The process of placing items or concepts into different groups or categories based on shared characteristics or criteria.  It involves identifying similarities and differences to create a structured system of organization.|She is categorizing the books by genre. 她正在按体裁对书籍进行分类。| classifying, sorting, grouping, pigeonholing | differentiating, separating, distinguishing |Verb
