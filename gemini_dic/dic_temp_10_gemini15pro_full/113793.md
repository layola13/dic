macruran:/məˈkrʊərən/ | n. |长尾类动物（尤指十足目中的长尾亚目）|A macruran is a type of crustacean, typically a decapod, with a long tail, such as a lobster, shrimp, or prawn. It belongs to the suborder Macrura. |例句：The lobster, a well-known macruran, is highly prized for its delicate flavor.  龙虾，一种著名的长尾类动物，因其鲜美的味道而备受推崇。

例句：Many macrurans are important commercially fished species. 许多长尾类动物都是重要的商业捕捞品种。|近义词：decapod, crustacean |反义词: brachyuran (短尾类动物)|词性:noun
