cymballike:/ˈsɪmbəlˌlaɪk/
adjective
像铙钹一样的
Resembling a cymbal, especially in sound or shape.
The crashing sound was cymballike.  撞击声像铙钹一样。
The large, flat leaves of the water lily are almost cymballike in appearance.  睡莲宽大扁平的叶子外观几乎像铙钹一样。
近义词: resonant, clangorous, metallic
反义词: soft, muffled, muted
词性: adjective

词源:  cymbal + like.  Cymbal comes from Old French *cimbale, from Late Latin cymbalum, from Greek kymbalon "cymbal," from kymbe "bowl, cup."
