Etymology:
From disconnect + -ive + -ness + -es.  "Disconnect" comes from the prefix "dis-" (meaning "not" or "apart") and "connect" (from Latin "connectere," meaning "to bind together").  The suffix "-ive" forms adjectives, "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, and "-es" creates the plural form.
