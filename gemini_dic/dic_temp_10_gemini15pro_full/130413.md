non-worker:/ˌnɒnˈwɜːkər/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈwɜːrkər/ (US)| n. |非工作者，失业者|A person who does not have a job or is not part of the workforce.  It can refer to people who are unemployed, retired, or not actively seeking employment.|The government is providing support for non-workers during the pandemic.| 近义词: unemployed person, jobless person, retiree (in some cases)| 反义词: worker, employee| 词性: Noun

non-worker:/ˌnɒnˈwɜːkər/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈwɜːrkər/ (US)| adj. |非工作的，不工作的|Not related to work or employment.|The survey included questions about non-worker activities.| 近义词: non-working, unemployed| 反义词: working, employed| 词性: Adjective (less common usage)
