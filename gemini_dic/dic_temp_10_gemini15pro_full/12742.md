assibilate: /əˈsɪbɪleɪt/ /əˈsɪbəˌleɪt/
verb
使成咝音; 发出咝咝声
to pronounce with a sibilant sound, or to cause to make a hissing sound

She assibilated the "s" in "sun." 她把“sun”中的“s”发成了咝音。
