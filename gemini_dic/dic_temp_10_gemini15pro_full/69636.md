feminizes:/ˈfɛmɪnaɪzɪz/
verb
使女性化，赋予女性特征
to make or become feminine in qualities or appearance.
The hormone therapy feminizes the male body.  荷尔蒙疗法使男性身体女性化。
The movie industry often feminizes male characters. 电影业经常将男性角色女性化。
synonyms: effeminate, emasculate
antonyms: masculinize, virilize
word type: verb
