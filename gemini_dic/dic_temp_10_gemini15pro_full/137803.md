nonveritable:/ˌnɒnvɛrɪtəbl/
adjective
非真实的，不真实的
Not genuinely or truly so; not factual.
His account of the incident was nonveritable and full of exaggerations. 他对事件的描述不真实，充满了夸张。
non-factual, untrue, fabricated, spurious, fictitious
veritable, true, genuine, real, authentic
词性: 形容词
