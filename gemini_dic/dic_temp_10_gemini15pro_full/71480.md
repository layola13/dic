flammable:/ˈflæməbl/| adj.|易燃的，可燃的|Easily set on fire; readily burning.|例句：This material is highly flammable. 这种材料高度易燃。|近义词：inflammable, combustible|反义词：nonflammable, fireproof|词性:形容词
flammable:/ˈflæməbl/| 词源:From Late Latin *flammabilis, from Latin flamma (“flame”) + -bilis. Equivalent to flame +‎ -able.|
