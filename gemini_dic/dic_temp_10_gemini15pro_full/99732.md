intermarine:/ˌɪntərˈməˈriːn/| adj.| 海间的，海洋之间的|Existing or occurring between seas or oceans.|例句：Intermarine commerce thrived during the Roman Empire. 罗马帝国时期，海间贸易繁荣。

例句：The intermarine ecosystem is delicate and easily disrupted. 海洋间的生态系统很脆弱，容易被破坏。|近义词：maritime, oceanic, pelagic|反义词：terrestrial, landlocked|词性:形容词

Etymology:  From Latin *intermarinus*, from *inter* (between) + *marinus* (marine, of the sea).  Ultimately from *mare* (sea).
