monocardian: /ˌmɒnəʊˈkɑːdiən/ | adj. n. | 单心室的；单心室病人 |  Relating to or having a single ventricle (chamber) in the heart. | This rare condition, known as monocardian circulation, involves a single functional ventricle. 这种罕见的疾病，被称为单心室循环，涉及单个功能性心室。|  The infant was diagnosed with a monocardian heart defect.  这个婴儿被诊断出患有单心室心脏缺陷。|  synonym: single-ventricle | antonym: two-ventricle, biventricular | adjective, noun
