locellate:/ˈlɒsəleɪt/ /ˈlɑːsəleɪt/| adj.|具小眼的；有单眼的|Having or resembling small eyes; having simple eyes or ocelli. |Some jellyfish are locellate, possessing primitive light-sensing organs.  一些水母有单眼，拥有原始的光感器官。|The insect has a locellate marking on its wings. 这种昆虫的翅膀上有类似小眼睛的斑点。|近义词: ocellated, eyed|反义词: eyeless, blind|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *locellus, diminutive of locus ("place, spot") + -ate (suffix forming adjectives).  源自拉丁语 *locellus，locus（“地方，地点”）的指小词 + -ate（构成形容词的后缀）。
