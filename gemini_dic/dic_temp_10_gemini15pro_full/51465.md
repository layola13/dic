detonization:/diːˌtɒnɪˈzeɪʃən/ |diːˌtɑːnɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.|去爆轰; 失爆|The process of ceasing to detonate; the failure of an explosive to detonate.|The detonization of the explosive charge prevented a catastrophic explosion.炸药的失爆阻止了一场灾难性的爆炸。|failure to detonate, misfire|detonation|noun
