deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbərət/ | adj. | 故意的，蓄意的；慎重的，深思熟虑的 | Done consciously and intentionally; characterized by careful thought or planning. |  He made a deliberate attempt to provoke a reaction. 他故意试图激起反应。|  She spoke in a slow and deliberate manner. 她说话的方式缓慢而慎重。 | 近义词: intentional, purposeful, conscious, calculated, careful, thoughtful | 反义词: accidental, unintentional, impulsive, careless, hasty | 词性: adjective

deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪt/ | v. | 仔细考虑，深思熟虑；商议，讨论 | Engage in long and careful consideration; discuss the pros and cons of an issue. | The jury deliberated for three days before reaching a verdict. 陪审团商议了三天，才做出裁决。| We need to deliberate carefully before making a decision. 我们在做决定之前需要仔细考虑。 | 近义词: consider, ponder, contemplate, discuss, debate | 反义词:  act impulsively, rush | 词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin  _dēlīberātus_, past participle of _dēlīberāre_  "to weigh in mind, consider, deliberate," from _de_ "thoroughly" + _lībra_ "balance, scales."
