barfish:/ˈbɑːrfɪʃ/| n. |梭鱼; 犬牙鱼; 海狼;|A predatory marine fish with an elongated body and prominent teeth. It is found in tropical and subtropical waters worldwide.|例句：The barfish darted through the coral reef, snatching up smaller fish. 梭鱼在珊瑚礁中飞快地游动，捕食小鱼。

例句：Barfish are known for their aggressive behavior and sharp teeth. 梭鱼以其侵略性的行为和锋利的牙齿而闻名。|近义词：gar, needlefish|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific fish. Could use "prey" if discussing food chain.)|词性:noun
