agritourist:/ˈæɡriˌtʊərɪst/| n.|农业旅游者；乡村旅游者|A tourist who visits agricultural areas for recreational and educational purposes, often engaging in activities like fruit picking, wine tasting, or farm stays.|例句：The agritourists enjoyed picking strawberries at the local farm. 农业游客们喜欢在当地农场采摘草莓。

例句：Many agritourists are interested in learning about sustainable farming practices. 许多农业游客对学习可持续农业实践感兴趣。|近义词：farm tourist, rural tourist|反义词: urban tourist|词性:noun
