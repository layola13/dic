broiling:/ˈbrɔɪlɪŋ/ | adj. v. |  炙热的，酷热的；烧烤 | extremely hot; cooking by direct exposure to radiant heat |  The broiling sun beat down on the desert.  炙热的太阳照射着沙漠。| synonyms: scorching, sweltering, grilling | antonyms: freezing, cold | adjective

broiling:/ˈbrɔɪlɪŋ/ | v. | 烧烤 | to cook by direct exposure to radiant heat | I'm broiling the steaks for dinner. 我正在烤牛排作为晚餐。| synonyms: grilling, barbecuing | antonyms: freezing, chilling | verb


Etymology: From Middle English *broilen, from Old French bruillir "to roast," of uncertain origin.
