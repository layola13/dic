herum:/heˈrʊm/ (German)| adv.| around, about, in all directions, messing around | This word is primarily used in German and doesn't have a direct English equivalent. It implies a sense of movement or activity that is not directed towards a specific goal. | Er läuft im Park herum. (He's walking around in the park.) | 近义词: (in German) umher, ringsum, herum, | 反义词: (This depends heavily on context; there's no direct opposite), | 词性: adverb
