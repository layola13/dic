outvalue:/ˌaʊtˈvæljuː/| vt.| 比…更有价值；胜过| To exceed in value. |例句：This new phone outvalues all the others on the market. 这款新手机比市面上所有其他手机都更有价值。|例句：His experience outvalues his lack of formal education. 他的经验比他缺乏正规教育更有价值。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outstrip, outweigh|反义词：undervalue, underestimate| verb
