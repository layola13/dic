calculatedly:/ˈkælkjuleɪtɪdli/
adverb
故意地；蓄意地；精打细算地
done deliberately and for a particular purpose, often in order to gain an advantage.
She smiled calculatedly. 她故意笑了笑。
近义词:deliberately, intentionally, purposefully, knowingly
反义词:accidentally, unintentionally, unknowingly, innocently
词性:副词

calculated:/ˈkælkjuleɪtɪd/
adjective
故意的；蓄意的；精打细算的
planned or arranged in order to produce a particular effect.
His speech was a calculated attempt to win votes. 他的演讲是为了赢得选票而精心策划的。
近义词:deliberate, intentional, purposeful, knowing
反义词:accidental, unintentional, unknowing, innocent
词性:形容词

calculate:/ˈkælkjuleɪt/
verb
计算；估计；预测；打算；计划；认为
determine (the amount or number of something) mathematically.
Have you calculated the cost of the holiday? 你计算过假期的费用吗？
近义词:compute, determine, estimate, reckon, figure
反义词:guess, estimate (sometimes)
词性:动词


词源: Late Middle English: from Latin calculatus ‘counted, reckoned’, past participle of calculare, from calculus ‘pebble’ (used in reckoning).
