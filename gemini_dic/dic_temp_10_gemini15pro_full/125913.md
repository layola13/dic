museo:/muˈzeɪoʊ/ (US), /mjuːˈzeɪoʊ/ (UK)| n. |博物馆|A building or place where works of art, scientific specimens, or other objects of interest are kept and displayed.|例句：We visited the natural history museo. 我们参观了自然历史博物馆。

例句：The city has a wonderful museo of modern art. 这座城市有一座很棒的现代艺术博物馆。|近义词：museum, gallery|反义词: None|词性: Noun
