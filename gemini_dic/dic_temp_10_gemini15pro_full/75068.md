frightment:/ˈfraɪtmənt/
n.
惊吓，恐惧
The state of being frightened; fear.
The child experienced a moment of frightment when the dog barked.  孩子在狗叫的时候经历了一刻的恐惧。
近义词：fear, terror, dread, alarm, panic
反义词：calm, composure, tranquility, peace
词性：名词

词源：From the verb "frighten" + "-ment" (suffix forming nouns of action or result).  源自动词 "frighten" + "-ment" （构成动作或结果名词的后缀）。
