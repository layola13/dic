orangutan:/ɔːˈræŋuːtæn/ (US) /ɔːˈræŋʊtæn/ (UK)| n.|猩猩|A large ape with reddish-brown hair, long arms, and no tail, found in the forests of Borneo and Sumatra.|例句：Orangutans are highly intelligent primates. 猩猩是高度聪明的灵长类动物。

例句：The orangutan swung effortlessly through the trees.  这只猩猩毫不费力地在树间荡来荡去。


近义词：ape, primate|反义词: None (no direct opposite for a specific animal)|词性:noun
