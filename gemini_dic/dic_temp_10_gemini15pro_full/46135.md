cymene:/ˈsaɪmiːn/ | n. |对伞花烃;异丙基甲苯|A liquid aromatic hydrocarbon, C10H14, present in some essential oils. It exists as three isomers, the most common being p-cymene.|Cymene is a component of several essential oils, including cumin oil. 对伞花烃是几种精油的成分，包括孜然油。| Cymene can be synthesized from toluene. 对伞花烃可以从甲苯合成。| isomers, hydrocarbon |  | noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cymène, from Latin cuminum (“cumin”). Ultimately from Akkadian *kamūnu.
