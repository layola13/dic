overshrunken:/ˌoʊvərˈʃrʌŋkən/
adjective
过度缩水的；缩水过度的
Having shrunk excessively, usually due to washing or drying at too high a temperature.
The sweater was overshrunken and no longer fit.  这件毛衣缩水过度，再也穿不上了。
The overshrunken shirt looked like it belonged to a child. 这件缩水过度的衬衫看起来像是小孩的。
Near Synonyms: shrunken, too small, undersized, diminutive, miniaturized
Antonyms: oversized, baggy, loose, large
词性: 形容词
