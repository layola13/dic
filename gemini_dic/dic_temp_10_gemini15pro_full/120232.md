minger:/ˈmɪŋə(r)/| n.|令人讨厌的人；卑鄙的人；丑陋的人 (British slang, derogatory)|A person considered unpleasant, contemptible, or unattractive.  This term is primarily used in British English and is considered offensive.|例句：He's such a minger, always trying to cheat people. 他真是个卑鄙小人，总是试图欺骗别人。

例句：Don't go out with him, he's a right minger!  别和他出去，他是个丑八怪！|近义词：creep, jerk, loser, ugly person|反义词:  attractive person, beauty|词性:noun
