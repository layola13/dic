mettle:/ˈmetl/| n.|勇气；毅力；气质；精神|A person's ability to cope well with difficulties or to face a demanding situation in a spirited and resilient way. |例句：The team showed their true mettle in the final game.  这支队伍在决赛中展现了他们的真正实力。

例句：He was a man of mettle and determination. 他是一个有勇气和决心的人。|近义词：courage, spirit, fortitude, resolve, grit, stamina |反义词：cowardice, timidity, fear, weakness|词性:noun
