declassifications:/diːˌklæsɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n.|解密；撤销保密级别|The declassifications of these documents shed new light on the historical event. 这些文件的解密为这一历史事件提供了新的线索。|例句：The recent declassifications have revealed some shocking truths.  最近的解密揭露了一些令人震惊的真相。

The declassifications of the war documents were controversial.  战争文件的解密引发了争议。


近义词：releases, disclosures|反义词:classifications, concealments|词性:名词(复数)

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove") + classification (from classify, from class + -ify).  Ultimately from Latin *classis (a division of the people, fleet, army) and facere (to make).
