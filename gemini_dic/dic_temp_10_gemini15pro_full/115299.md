manna:/ˈmænə/| n.|吗哪；天赐之物；意外的收获|Something that comes unexpectedly and is very helpful or valuable.  It originally referred to the food miraculously supplied to the Israelites in the wilderness.|例句：The grant was manna from heaven for the struggling artist. 这笔拨款对于这位苦苦挣扎的艺术家来说简直是天赐之物。

例句：The unexpected bonus was like manna from heaven. 这意外的奖金就像天赐之物。|近义词：windfall, godsend, boon, blessing|反义词：curse, misfortune|词性:noun
