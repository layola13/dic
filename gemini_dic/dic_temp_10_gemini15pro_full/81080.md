grackle:/ˈɡrækl̩/ (US), /ˈɡreɪkl̩/ (UK)| n.|鹩哥，美洲黑鸟|A blackbird of the family Icteridae, found in North and Central America, typically with iridescent black plumage, a long tail, and a loud, harsh call.|例句：A flock of grackles descended on the bird feeder. 一群鹩哥飞落到喂鸟器上。

例句：The grackle's song is a series of raucous clicks and whistles. 鹩哥的歌声是一连串沙哑的咔哒声和口哨声。|近义词：blackbird, starling|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the obsolete French 'gracul', from Latin 'graculus', diminutive of 'gracus', a kind of jackdaw.
