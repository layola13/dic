indigenous-to:/ɪnˈdɪdʒənəs tuː/
adjective
原产于，土生土长的
originating or naturally occurring in a particular place; native.
Koalas are indigenous to Australia.  考拉原产于澳大利亚。
The plant is indigenous to the Amazon rainforest.  这种植物是亚马逊雨林的原生植物。
Synonyms: native, aboriginal, endemic
Antonyms: foreign, alien, exotic, introduced
adjective
