intermarry:/ˌɪntərˈmærɪ/ /ˌɪntərˈmæri/
verb
通婚；与…异族通婚
(of people of different races, religions, etc.) marry each other.
例句：The two communities have intermarried for centuries. 这两个社群已经通婚了几百年。

例句：Some families encourage their children to intermarry with wealthy families. 一些家庭鼓励子女与富裕家庭通婚。

近义词：intermix, blend, integrate
反义词：segregate
词性：v.
