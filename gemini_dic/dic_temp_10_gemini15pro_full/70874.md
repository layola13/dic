finishes:/ˈfɪnɪʃɪz/| v.| 完成，结束，终结| This finishes the job. 这就完成了工作。| synonyms: completes, concludes, ends | antonyms: begins, starts, commences | verb (third person singular simple present)

finishes:/ˈfɪnɪʃɪz/| n.| 结束，结尾，最后阶段，表面处理| The finishes on the furniture were exquisite. 家具的表面处理非常精美。| synonyms: completions, endings, conclusions, surfaces | antonyms: beginnings, starts, commencements | noun (plural)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *finischen, from Old French fenir, finir (“to finish, end”), from Latin fīnīre (“to limit, bound, end”), from fīnis (“boundary, limit, end”).
