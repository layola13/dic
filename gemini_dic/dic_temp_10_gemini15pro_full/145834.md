overdeliberations:/ˌoʊvərdiˌlɪbəˈreɪʃənz/
|n.|过度审议；过分考虑|Excessive deliberation; considering something for too long or too deeply.|The committee's overdeliberations led to the project missing its deadline. 委员会的过度审议导致项目错过了最后期限。|The endless overdeliberations stalled the decision-making process. 无休止的过度审议使决策过程停滞不前。|近义词: overthinking, overanalyzing, rumination|反义词: impulsiveness, decisiveness|词性:名词

Etymology:
"Over-" (prefix) + "deliberation"
"Over-" from Old English "ofer," meaning "above, across, beyond."  It indicates excess or going too far.
"Deliberation" from Latin "deliberationem" (nominative deliberatio), noun of action from past-participle stem of deliberare "to weigh in mind, consider, consult," from de- "thoroughly" (see de-) + librare "to weigh, balance," from libra "balance, scales."
