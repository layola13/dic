organized:/ˈɔːrɡənaɪzd/ | adj. | 有条理的，有组织的 | Arranged in a systematic way, especially so as to be efficient or easy to understand or use. |  The event was very well organized.  这个活动组织得非常好。|  structured, ordered, systematic, methodical | disorganized, chaotic, messy | adjective

organized:/ˈɔːrɡənaɪzd/ | v. (past tense and past participle organized, present participle organizing, third person singular present tense organizes) | 组织，安排 | Arrange systematically; order.  | She organized her desk. 她整理了她的桌子。 | arrange, plan, coordinate, manage | disorganize, disrupt | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English: from Old French organiser, from organisme ‘organism’, from medieval Latin organismus, from Latin organum ‘instrument, organ’ (see organ).
