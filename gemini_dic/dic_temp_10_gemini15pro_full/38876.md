conferenced:/ˈkɒnfərənst/ /ˈkɑːnfərənst/
verb
举行会议；商讨；协商
past tense and past participle of conference
They conferenced with the president about the issue. 他们与总统就这个问题举行了会议。
近义词: discussed, deliberated, consulted
反义词: disagreed, argued
