cacher:/kæˈʃeɪ/ (US), /ˈkæʃeɪ/ (UK)| v. |隐藏，贮藏|To hide or store something in a concealed place.|He cached the treasure under a loose floorboard. 他把财宝藏在一块松动的地板下。|
例句：
The squirrels cached nuts for the winter. 松鼠为冬天储存坚果。
She cached the important documents in a safe place. 她把重要文件藏在一个安全的地方。

近义词：hide, conceal, stash, store|反义词:reveal, expose|词性:verb


cacher (noun):/kæˈʃeɪ/ (US), /ˈkæʃeɪ/ (UK)| n. |隐藏处，贮藏处|A hiding place or store.|The police found the thief's cache of stolen goods. 警察发现了小偷藏赃物的地方。|
例句：
The hikers discovered a cache of food left by previous explorers.  徒步旅行者发现了一处先前探险者留下的食物储藏处。
The spy used a secret cache to store his equipment. 间谍用一个秘密的藏匿处来存放他的设备。

近义词：hiding place, store, stockpile|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
