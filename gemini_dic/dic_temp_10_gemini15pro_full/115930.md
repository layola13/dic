marquetries:/ˈmɑːrkɪtriz/
noun
镶嵌工艺；镶嵌细工
The art of decorating a surface with inlaid pieces of wood, shell, ivory, or other materials, often forming decorative patterns or pictures.
The antique table featured intricate marquetries of exotic woods. 这张古董桌子以其精美的异国木材镶嵌工艺为特色。
