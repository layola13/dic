hemimorphic:/ˌhɛmiˈmɔːrfɪk/| adj.|半形的，异极的|Having dissimilar ends or poles; developing differently at opposite ends of an axis.|Crystals of tourmaline are hemimorphic. 电气石晶体是半形的。|近义词:anisotropic, asymmetric|反义词:isomorphic, symmetric|adjective
