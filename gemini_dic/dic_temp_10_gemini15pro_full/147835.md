overpunishing: /ˌoʊvərˈpʌnɪʃɪŋ/
| adj. | 过度惩罚的；惩罚过度的 | Punishing someone too severely or excessively for a particular offense. | The school's policy on plagiarism was seen as overpunishing, often leading to expulsion for minor infractions.  学校关于剽窃的政策被认为是惩罚过度的，经常因为轻微的违规行为而导致开除。| 近义词: excessive, draconian, harsh, undue | 反义词: lenient, merciful, moderate | 词性: 形容词

overpunish: /ˌoʊvərˈpʌnɪʃ/
| v. | 过度惩罚；惩罚过度 | To punish someone too severely or excessively for a particular offense. | Don't overpunish the child for a small mistake; it might damage their self-esteem. 不要因为一个小错误就过度惩罚孩子；这可能会损害他们的自尊。 | 近义词: overpenalize, chastise excessively | 反义词: underpunish, pardon, excuse | 词性: 动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "punish" (from Old French "puniss-" and ultimately from Latin "punire," meaning "to punish").
