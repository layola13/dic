nonimmanence:/ˌnɒnˈɪmənəns/
n.|非内在性，非固有性|The state or quality of not being immanent; transcendence.|The concept of nonimmanence suggests a power or being that exists outside of and independent from the material world.  非内在性的概念暗示了一种存在于物质世界之外并独立于物质世界的力量或存在。|
