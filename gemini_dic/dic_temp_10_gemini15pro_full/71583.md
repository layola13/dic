flashmobbed:/ˈflæʃmɒbd/ (UK) /ˈflæʃmɑːbd/ (US)| v.|快闪突袭；快闪围观|To have been the target of a flash mob. A flash mob is a large public gathering at which people perform an unusual or seemingly random act and then disperse, often having coordinated beforehand using social media.|例句：The singer was flashmobbed by fans while shopping at the mall. 歌手在商场购物时被粉丝快闪突袭。|近义词：ambushed, surprised, swarmed|反义词:avoided, ignored|Verb (past participle)

flashmobbed:/ˈflæʃmɒbd/ (UK) /ˈflæʃmɑːbd/ (US)| adj.|被快闪的|Having been subjected to a flash mob.|例句：The flashmobbed celebrity was overwhelmed by the sudden attention. 被快闪的名人被突如其来的关注淹没了。|近义词：besieged, overwhelmed|反义词:unnoticed, ignored|Adjective
