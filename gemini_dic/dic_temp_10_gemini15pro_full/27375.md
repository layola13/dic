came-back:/keɪm bæk/ | n. | 回来，恢复，反弹 | A return to a former condition, place, or activity. | He made a remarkable came-back after his injury. 他在受伤后取得了惊人的恢复。 |  近义词: return, recovery, comeback, rebound | 反义词: decline, relapse, deterioration | 词性: 名词

came-back:/keɪm bæk/ | adj. | 回来的，恢复的 | Having returned to a former condition, place, or activity. | The came-back champion was greeted with cheers.  这位复出的冠军受到了欢呼。| 近义词: returned, recovered, revived | 反义词: gone, lost, departed | 词性: 形容词


词源:  "Came-back" is formed from the simple past tense of the verb "come" (came) and the adverb "back."  It originated as a noun phrase, but can also function adjectivally.  "Come back" as a phrasal verb dates back to Old English.
