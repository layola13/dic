absconder: /æbˈskɒndər/ (UK) /æbˈskɑːndər/ (US)
n.
潜逃者；逃匿者
a person who leaves hurriedly and secretly, typically to avoid detection of or arrest for an unlawful action such as theft.
The absconder was apprehended at the border.  潜逃者在边境被逮捕了。

The company is looking for the absconder who embezzled millions of dollars. 公司正在寻找卷款潜逃的百万美元的逃犯。

近义词: fugitive, runaway, escapee
反义词: returnee
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin abscondere "to hide away," from ab- "away" + condere "to put together, store, conceal."
