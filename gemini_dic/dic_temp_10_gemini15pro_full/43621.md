create:/kriˈeɪt/ | vt. |创造，创作；产生，造成|To bring into existence; to cause something new to exist. | God created the world. 上帝创造了世界。|The artist created a beautiful sculpture. 艺术家创作了一座美丽的雕塑。|近义词：make, produce, generate, originate, invent, design, form, compose, establish, found, institute|反义词：destroy, demolish, ruin, annihilate|词性:动词

词源: From Latin *creātus*, past participle of *creāre* "to create," from *cerō* "I separate, distinguish, decide."
