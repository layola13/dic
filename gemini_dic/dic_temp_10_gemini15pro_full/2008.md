actualized: /ˈæktʃuəlaɪzd/
adjective
实现的，已实现的
Made real or brought into effect.
She finally actualized her dream of opening a bakery. 她终于实现了开面包店的梦想。

His potential was actualized through hard work and dedication. 他的潜力通过努力和奉献实现了。

近义词: realized, achieved, fulfilled, accomplished
反义词: potential, unrealized, dormant
词性: adjective

He actualizes his vision through his art. = 他通过他的艺术实现了愿景。
