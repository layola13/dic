cop-a-plea: /ˈkɒp ə ˈpliː/ (UK), /ˈkɑːp ə ˈpliː/ (US)
cop-a-plea: n.
cop-a-plea: 认罪协议，辩诉交易
cop-a-plea: A plea bargain, or negotiated plea, is an agreement between a defendant and the prosecution in a criminal case whereby the defendant agrees to plead guilty to a particular charge in return for some concession from the prosecution.
cop-a-plea:  He took a cop-a-plea to avoid a longer sentence. 他接受了辩诉交易以避免更长的刑期。
cop-a-plea: plea bargain, guilty plea
cop-a-plea: not guilty plea, trial
