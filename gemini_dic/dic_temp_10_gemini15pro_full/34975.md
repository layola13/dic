clod:/klɑːd/ (US) /klɒd/ (UK)| n.|土块，笨蛋|A compact mass of earth, clay, etc.; a stupid person.|The farmer broke up the clods of earth with a hoe. 农民用锄头把土块打碎。| The boy was called a clod for his clumsy behavior.  这男孩因为笨手笨脚的行为被称为笨蛋。|近义词：lump, chunk, dolt, oaf|反义词:genius, intellectual|名词

clod:/klɑːd/ (US) /klɒd/ (UK)| v.|结块，覆盖|To form clods; to cover with clods.|The heavy rain caused the soil to clod.  大雨使土壤结块。|近义词：clump, coagulate|反义词:separate, disperse|动词
