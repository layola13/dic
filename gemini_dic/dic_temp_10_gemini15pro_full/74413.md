franchisal: /frænˈtʃaɪzəl/
n. |特许经营的,特许经营权|A franchisal is relating to or involving a franchise. |The franchisal agreement outlines the terms and conditions.  特许经营协议概述了条款和条件。|近义词：franchise, licensed|反义词: independent, proprietary|词性: adjective (primarily), noun (rarely)

franchisal: /frænˈtʃaɪzəl/
n. |特许经营权|A franchisal is a right granted to an individual or group to market a company's goods or services in a particular territory. |He purchased the franchisal for the popular fast-food chain. 他购买了这家热门快餐连锁店的经营权。|近义词：franchise, license|反义词:  N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "franchise" + "-al" (suffix forming adjectives). "Franchise" itself comes from Old French "franchise" meaning "freedom, liberty," derived from "franc" meaning "free," ultimately from Frankish "*frank" meaning "free."
