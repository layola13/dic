araneida:/əˈreɪniɪdə/
n.
蜘蛛目；蜘蛛类
A taxonomic order of arachnids that includes spiders.
Spiders, which spin webs to catch prey, belong to the order Araneida. 蜘蛛，结网捕食猎物，属于蜘蛛目。
The Araneida are known for their eight legs and ability to produce silk.  蜘蛛目以其八条腿和产丝能力而闻名。
synonym:Araneae, spiders
antonym:None
noun
