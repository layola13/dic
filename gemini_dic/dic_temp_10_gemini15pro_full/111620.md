localised:/ˈləʊkəlaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈloʊkəˌlaɪzd/ (US)| adj.|局部的，地方性的，本地化的|Restricted or limited to a particular area or part.  It describes something that is confined to a specific location and doesn't spread widely.|The pain was localized to his lower back. 疼痛局限于他的下背部。|localized burning sensation 局部烧灼感|synonyms: confined, limited, restricted, focused, concentrated, pinpoint|antonyms: widespread, general, global, universal, diffuse, disseminated|adjective
