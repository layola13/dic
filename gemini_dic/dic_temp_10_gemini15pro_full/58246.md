drumbeats:/ˈdrʌmˌbiːts/ | n. |鼓声，敲击声|The steady drumbeats filled the air. 稳定的鼓声弥漫在空中。|例句：The drumbeats grew louder as the parade approached. 随着游行的临近，鼓声越来越响亮。|例句：The rhythmic drumbeats accompanied the dancers.  有节奏的鼓声伴随着舞者。|近义词：drumming, percussion, rhythm|反义词: silence, quiet|词性: 名词

词源:  Compound word formed from "drum" + "beats." "Drum" comes from Middle Dutch "tromme" and Old French "trompe." "Beat" comes from Old English "bēatan" meaning "to strike."
