exculpate:/ˈɛksˌkʌlpeɪt/| vt.| 开脱；使无罪；证明…无罪|To clear from alleged fault or guilt; to prove innocent.|The lawyer worked tirelessly to exculpate his client from the murder charges. 律师孜孜不倦地工作，以使其委托人摆脱谋杀指控。|近义词：absolve, acquit, exonerate, vindicate|反义词：incriminate, condemn, convict|词性:动词
