hymns:/hɪmz/
n.
赞美诗，圣歌
songs of praise, especially Christian ones
The congregation joined in singing hymns.  会众一起唱赞美诗。

近义词:
psalms, anthems, chants
反义词:
blasphemy, profanity

词性:
noun (plural)

词源:
From Old French *ymne, from Late Latin hymnus, from Greek hymnos "festive song, ode sung to a god," originally "song, lay, ode," from hy- "sing, celebrate."
