dullard:/ˈdʌlərd/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A slow or stupid person; someone who is not very intelligent. |He called the other drivers dullards for not understanding the rules of the road. 他称其他司机为笨蛋，因为他们不懂交通规则。|近义词：idiot, moron, imbecile, fool, simpleton|反义词:genius, intellectual, savant|名词
