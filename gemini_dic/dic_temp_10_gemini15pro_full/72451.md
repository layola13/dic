flume:/fluːm/| n.| 水槽，斜槽，峡谷|A narrow channel or passage with steep sides, especially one carrying water.|The miners built a flume to carry water to the gold fields. 矿工们建造了一个水槽，将水引到金矿区。|近义词：channel, chute, trough, conduit|反义词:ridge, peak|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *flum, from Old French flum (“river”), from Latin flūmen (“river, stream, flow”), from fluō (“to flow”).
