fraternization:/ˌfrætərnɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 友好交往，兄弟般的交往，联谊 |  The act of associating or mingling with others in a friendly way, especially when such behavior is discouraged or prohibited. | The general fraternization between officers and enlisted men is against regulations. 军官和士兵之间的普遍 fraternization 是违反规定的。 | synonyms: association, mingling, socializing, consorting, hobnobbing | antonyms: segregation, isolation, avoidance | noun


fraternize:/ˈfrætərnʌɪz/ | v. | 友好交往，亲如兄弟 | To associate or mingle with others in a friendly way, especially when such behavior is discouraged or prohibited. |  The soldiers were warned not to fraternize with the enemy. 士兵们被警告不要与敌人 fraternize。| synonyms: associate, mingle, socialize, consort, hobnob | antonyms: segregate, isolate, avoid | verb
