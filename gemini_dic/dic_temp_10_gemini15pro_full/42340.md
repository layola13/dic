counteracting:/ˌkaʊntərˈæktɪŋ/
verb
抵消；中和；对抗
acting in opposition to something in order to reduce its force or effect.
The government is trying to counteract the effects of inflation. 政府正在努力抵消通货膨胀的影响。
synonyms: offsetting, neutralizing, counterbalancing, opposing, resisting
antonyms:  reinforcing, strengthening, intensifying
