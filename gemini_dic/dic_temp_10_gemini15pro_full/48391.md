dedentition:/ˌdiːdɛnˈtɪʃən/
n.
脱牙；换牙
the shedding or loss of teeth, either naturally (as in human children and in many animals whose teeth are replaced throughout their lives) or as a result of accident or disease.
The child's dedentition was a painful process.  这个孩子的换牙过程很痛苦。

近义词: teeth shedding, tooth loss
反义词: teething
