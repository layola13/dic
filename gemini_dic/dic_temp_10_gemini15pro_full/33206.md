chronic:/ˈkrɑːnɪk/ (US) /ˈkrɒnɪk/ (UK)| adj.|慢性的，长期的|Lasting for a long time or recurring often. |例句：He suffers from a chronic back problem. 他患有慢性背痛。|近义词：persistent, long-standing, lingering, inveterate, habitual|反义词：acute, temporary, transient|词性:形容词

chronic:/ˈkrɑːnɪk/ (US) /ˈkrɒnɪk/ (UK)| adj.|（非正式）严重的，糟糕的| (informal) very bad; of a serious nature. |例句：The film was absolutely chronic. 这部电影烂透了。|近义词：severe, dire, dreadful, terrible|反义词：mild, slight|词性:形容词
