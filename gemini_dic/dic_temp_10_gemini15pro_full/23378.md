brachiopod:/ˈbrækɪəˌpɒd/| n. |腕足动物|A marine invertebrate animal of the phylum Brachiopoda, which comprises the lamp shells. They have a hinged shell and live attached to the seabed.|Brachiopods are often mistaken for clams, but their internal anatomy is quite different. 腕足动物经常被误认为是蛤蜊，但它们的内部解剖结构却大不相同。|Synonym: lamp shell|Antonym: None|noun
