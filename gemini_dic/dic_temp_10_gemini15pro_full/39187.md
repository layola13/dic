congenial:/kənˈdʒiːniəl/
adjective
友好的；志趣相投的；适宜的；宜人的
(of a person) pleasant because of a personality, qualities, or interests that are similar to one's own.  (of a thing) pleasant or agreeable because suited to one's taste or inclination.
She found him a congenial companion. 她觉得他是一个志趣相投的伙伴。
The department provides a congenial atmosphere for research. 这个系提供了一个适宜研究的氛围。
近义词:agreeable, pleasant, amiable, compatible, sympathetic, kindred
反义词:uncongenial, disagreeable, incompatible, hostile
词性:adjective
