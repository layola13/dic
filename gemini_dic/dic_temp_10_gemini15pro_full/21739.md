boastingly: /ˈboʊstɪŋli/ | adv. | 吹嘘地，自夸地 | In a way that shows excessive pride and self-satisfaction in one's achievements, possessions, or abilities. | He boastingly announced his victory to everyone in the room. 他吹嘘地向房间里的每个人宣布了他的胜利。|  He spoke boastingly about his new car.  他吹嘘着自己的新车。 | 近义词: proudly, arrogantly, vaingloriously | 反义词: humbly, modestly | 词性: 副词

词源: From the verb "boast" + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverb suffix). "Boast" ultimately comes from the Old French word "boster" meaning "to brag."
