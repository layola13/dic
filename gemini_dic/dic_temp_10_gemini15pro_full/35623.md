coated:/ˈkoʊtɪd/| adj.|涂层的，包覆的|Covered with a layer of something.|例句：The pills are coated with sugar. 药片上裹了一层糖。|例句：The car was coated in dust. 汽车上覆盖了一层灰尘。|近义词：covered, layered, filmed|反义词：bare, uncoated|词性:形容词
Etymology: From coat (n.) + -ed (suffix forming past participles and adjectives). The noun "coat" comes from Old French "cote" (tunic, coat of mail), ultimately from Frankish or another West Germanic source related to Old High German "kozzo" (coarse cloth). The suffix "-ed" derives from Old English "-ed," "-od," or "-ad."
