crabeater:/ˈkræbiːtər/| n.|食蟹动物，食蟹海豹|An animal that eats crabs. Specifically, it usually refers to the crabeater seal, which, despite its name, primarily eats krill.  It can also refer to any animal that consumes crabs.|The crabeater seal is a common sight in Antarctica. 食蟹海豹在南极洲很常见。|The leopard seal is another example of a crabeater.  豹海豹是食蟹动物的另一个例子。|Synonyms: krill-eater (specifically for the crabeater seal), predator |Antonyms:  prey (in a broader sense)|noun
