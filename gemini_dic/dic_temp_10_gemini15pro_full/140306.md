oghams:/ˈɒɡəmz/ | n. | 欧甘字母 | An early form of writing used in Ireland and Britain, consisting of a series of notches cut into a stick or stone. |  Example: The ancient oghams carved on the standing stone are difficult to decipher. 这块立石上雕刻的古老欧甘字母很难解读。

Example: Researchers are studying the oghams to learn more about the early Celtic languages. 研究人员正在研究欧甘字母，以了解更多关于早期凯尔特语言的信息。 | 近义词: runes, inscriptions | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "modern alphabet") | 词性: noun
