coolness:/ˈkuːlnəs/| n.|冷静；凉爽；沉着|The state of being cool in temperature or demeanor.  A calmness or composure, especially in a difficult situation.|He handled the crisis with remarkable coolness. 他以惊人的冷静处理了这场危机。|Synonyms: composure, calmness, equanimity, self-possession, poise, aplomb, sangfroid|Antonyms: anxiety, agitation, nervousness, excitability|noun
