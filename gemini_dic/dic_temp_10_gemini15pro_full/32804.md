choiring: /ˈkwaɪərɪŋ/
noun
合唱; 合唱表演
the act of singing in a choir
The school's choiring was magnificent at the Christmas concert.  学校的合唱在圣诞音乐会上非常精彩。
Near synonyms: singing, choral singing, harmonizing
Antonyms: solo performance

verb (present participle of 'choir')
合唱; 参加合唱
singing in a choir
They were choiring beautiful hymns in the church. 他们在教堂里合唱着优美的赞美诗。
Near synonyms: singing, performing, harmonizing
Antonyms: soloing


Etymology:  From the noun "choir" + "-ing" suffix, indicating the action or process of.  "Choir" comes from Old French "cuer" (choir), from Latin "chorus," ultimately from Greek "khoros" (dance, chorus).
