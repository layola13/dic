outplodded:/ˌaʊtˈplɒdɪd/
verb
艰难地跋涉，吃力地走完
walked or moved laboriously or tediously
The weary hikers outplodded the last mile of their journey.  疲惫的徒步旅行者艰难地走完了最后一英里路程。

The old horse outplodded the muddy field. 老马吃力地走过泥泞的田野。

trudged, slogged
sprinted, raced
