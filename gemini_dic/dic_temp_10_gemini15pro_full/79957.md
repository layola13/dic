go-aheadativeness: /ˌɡoʊ əˈhɛdətɪvnəs/
n.
积极进取；主动性；果断
The quality of being willing and eager to proceed with something, often without hesitation.  A blend of "go-ahead" (adjective meaning enterprising and progressive) and "-ativeness" (a suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality).
She demonstrated remarkable go-aheadativeness in pursuing her goals. 她追求目标时展现出非凡的进取心。
His go-aheadativeness led him to start his own business. 他的积极进取使他创办了自己的公司。
近义词: initiative, proactiveness, enterprise, boldness, dynamism
反义词: hesitation, reluctance, passivity, timidity, procrastination
