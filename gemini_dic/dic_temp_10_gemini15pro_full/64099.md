eponymy:/ɪˈpɒnɪmi/ /əˈpɑːnɪmi/ | n. |命名法；名字的由来；同名异物|The act of naming something after a person or thing, or the name so given.  The term "sandwich" is an eponymy, derived from the Earl of Sandwich. | This disease is an eponymy, named after the doctor who first described it.  这种疾病是以第一个描述它的医生命名的。| synonym: name derivation, appellation | antonym: anonymity | noun

eponymous:/ɪˈpɒnɪməs/ /əˈpɑːnɪməs/ | adj. |同名的；以...命名的|Being named after a person or thing; giving one's name to something.  The eponymous hero of the novel is a complex character. | The eponymous album was their most successful. 这张同名专辑是他们最成功的。| synonym: namesake, titled | antonym: anonymous | adjective
