nonerrantly:/ˌnɒnˈɛrəntlɪ/
adverb
无误地，正确无误地
without error; infallibly.
The scripture speaks nonerrantly about the nature of God. 圣经无误地讲述了神的本质。

近义词:infallibly, accurately, correctly, perfectly
反义词:errantly, inaccurately, incorrectly, faultily
词性:adverb


词源:
From non- + errant + -ly.  Non- meaning "not," errant meaning "wandering or straying from the proper course or standards," and -ly forming the adverb.
