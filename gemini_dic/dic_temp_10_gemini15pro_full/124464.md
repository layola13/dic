mottos:/ˈmɒtəʊz/ (UK) /ˈmɑːtoʊz/ (US)| n. |座右铭，格言，箴言|A short sentence or phrase that expresses a belief or ideal.|The school's motto is "Knowledge is power."  学校的座右铭是“知识就是力量”。|例句：Their company motto is "Quality First". 他们的公司座右铭是“质量第一”。

例句：He chose "Never give up" as his motto. 他选择“永不放弃”作为他的座右铭。|近义词：slogan, maxim, creed, precept|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "contradiction" in a philosophical sense) |词性:noun


Etymology: From Italian *motto* "word; sentence," from Late Latin *muttum* "grunt, murmur; word," which is of imitative origin.
