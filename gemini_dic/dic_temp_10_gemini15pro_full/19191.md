bewitches:/bɪˈwɪtʃɪz/
|v.|使着迷，迷惑|Casts a spell on someone or something, causing them to be fascinated or enchanted.|Her beauty bewitches everyone she meets. 她美丽的容貌使每个见到她的人都为之倾倒。|enchants, captivates, charms|repels, disgusts|verb
bewitched:/bɪˈwɪtʃt/
|adj.|被迷住的，着迷的|Fascinated, enchanted, or under a spell.|He was completely bewitched by her singing. 他完全被她的歌声迷住了。|captivated, enchanted, fascinated|disinterested, uninterested|adjective

词源:
From Middle English *biwicchen*, from Old English *bewiccian* (“to bewitch, charm, fascinate, enchant”), from *be-* + *wiccian* (“to practice witchcraft, use magic arts, fascinate, enchant”), from *wicca* (“wizard, witch, sorcerer”).  Related to witch.
