bewildering:/bɪˈwɪldərɪŋ/| adj.|令人困惑的，令人迷惑的|Extremely confusing and difficult to understand.|The city was a bewildering maze of narrow streets. 这座城市是一个令人困惑的狭窄街道的迷宫。|The instructions were so bewildering that I couldn't assemble the furniture.  说明书太让人困惑了，我根本组装不了家具。|近义词：confusing, perplexing, baffling|反义词：clear, straightforward|词性:形容词

bewilder:/bɪˈwɪldər/| vt.|使迷惑，使不知所措|To confuse or puzzle someone very much.|The sudden change in policy bewildered everyone. 政策的突然改变使每个人都感到困惑。|The complex plot of the movie completely bewildered me.  这部电影复杂的情节让我完全迷惑了。|近义词：confuse, puzzle, perplex|反义词：clarify, enlighten|词性:动词

词源: From be- + wilder (to lead astray, from wild + -er)
