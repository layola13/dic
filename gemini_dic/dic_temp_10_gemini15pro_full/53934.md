discourtesies:/dɪsˈkɜːrtəsiːz/
n.
失礼，无礼
acts that show a lack of good manners or respect
His discourtesies to the staff were unacceptable. 他对员工的无礼行为是不可接受的。
近义词：rudeness, impoliteness, incivility
反义词：courtesy, politeness, civility
词性:名词 (plural noun)

词源:
From dis- (expressing negation) + courtesy.  The word "courtesy" comes from Old French "cortoisie," meaning "courtly behavior," ultimately derived from Latin "cohors," meaning "enclosure" or "company."
