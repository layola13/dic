clairvoyance:/klɛərˈvɔɪəns/
noun
透视力；千里眼
The ability to see things beyond the range of normal perception, especially future events or things happening at a distance.
She claims to have clairvoyance and can predict the future. 她声称拥有透视力，可以预知未来。
Near Synonyms:  prescience, second sight, foreknowledge, prophecy
Antonyms: blindness, myopia
