fatling:/ˈfætlɪŋ/| n.| 肥畜，肥美的动物（尤指供食用的）|A young animal fattened for slaughter.|The farmer sold his fatlings at the market. 农民把他的肥畜拿到市场上去卖了。|Synonyms:  well-fed animal, plump animal, livestock|Antonyms: lean animal, undernourished animal|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fættling, from fætt (fat) + -ling (suffix forming nouns denoting small or young animals).
