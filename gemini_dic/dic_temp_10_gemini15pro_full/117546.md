megabit:/ˈmɛɡəbɪt/| n. |兆位|A unit of information equal to one million bits, abbreviated as Mb or Mbit.  Used to measure data transfer rates and storage capacity.|A high-speed internet connection might offer download speeds of 100 megabits per second.  高速互联网连接的下载速度可能为每秒 100 兆位。|Synonyms: Mbit, Mb|Antonyms: kilobit, byte|noun
