overcriticisms: /ˌoʊvərˈkrɪtɪsɪzəmz/
n.
过度批评；过分苛责
Excessive criticisms; finding fault too readily or severely.
The constant overcriticisms from her parents made her lose confidence. 她父母不断的过度批评让她失去了信心。
His overcriticisms of the project discouraged the team. 他对项目的过度批评使团队灰心丧气。
近义词: carping, nitpicking, faultfinding, caviling
反义词: praise, commendation, approval
