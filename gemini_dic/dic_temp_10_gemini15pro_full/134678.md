nonmarketability:/ˌnɒnˌmɑːkɪtəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可销售性，非市场性，缺乏销路
The state or quality of being difficult or impossible to sell or trade in a market.
The nonmarketability of the property made it difficult to sell.  这处房产由于缺乏销路，难以出售。
The company's nonmarketability stemmed from its poor reputation. 该公司缺乏销路源于其糟糕的声誉。
illiquidity, unmarketability
marketability, salability
noun
