nonchauvinists:/ˌnɒnˈʃoʊvɪnɪsts/
|n.|非沙文主义者|People who do not hold chauvinistic beliefs, meaning they do not believe in the superiority of their own group (e.g., gender, race, nationality) over others.| Nonchauvinists advocate for equal rights and respect for all individuals, regardless of their background.  非沙文主义者提倡人人平等，尊重所有个人，不论其背景如何。|Near synonyms: egalitarians, feminists (in the context of gender chauvinism),  equalitarian|Antonyms: chauvinists, bigots, sexists (in the context of gender chauvinism)|noun
