messiness:/ˈmesɪnəs/| n.|杂乱，凌乱，脏乱|The state of being messy; disorderliness; untidiness.|Her room was in a state of utter messiness. 她的房间一片狼藉。|近义词：disorder, untidiness, chaos, clutter|反义词：tidiness, neatness, order, cleanliness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From messy + -ness.  Messy comes from Old French messie "dirty, filthy," perhaps related to Old Norse *myki "dung, manure."
