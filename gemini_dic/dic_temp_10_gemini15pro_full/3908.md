agrichemicals:/ˈæɡrɪˌkɛmɪkəlz/ | n. | 农业化学品; 农药 | Chemical products used in agriculture. These include fertilizers, pesticides, herbicides, and soil conditioners. | Farmers use various agrichemicals to improve crop yields. 农民使用各种农业化学品来提高作物产量。| The overuse of agrichemicals can contaminate groundwater. 过度使用农业化学品会污染地下水。| synonyms: agrochemicals, pesticides, fertilizers, farm chemicals | antonyms: organic fertilizers, biopesticides | noun
