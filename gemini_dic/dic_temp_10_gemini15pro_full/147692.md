overpraise: /ˌoʊvərˈpreɪz/ | vt. | 过度赞扬，过分夸奖 | To praise excessively or beyond what is deserved. | He overpraised his son's mediocre performance in the school play. 他过分夸奖了他儿子在学校戏剧中平庸的表演。

The media overpraised the new phone, leading to disappointment among consumers. 媒体过分夸赞了这款新手机，导致消费者失望。

近义词: exaggerate, overrate, laud excessively | 反义词: underpraise, criticize, downplay | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + praise.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much." "Praise" comes from the Old French word "preis" meaning "price, worth, merit, praise."
