habitator:/ˈhæbɪteɪtər/
noun
居住者，栖息者
One that inhabits a place; a resident.
例句：The forest is a habitator for various species of birds. 这片森林是各种鸟类的栖息地。

例句：The city's habitators faced a housing shortage. 城市居民面临住房短缺。
近义词：resident, inhabitant, dweller, occupant
反义词：migrant, nomad
词性：n.
