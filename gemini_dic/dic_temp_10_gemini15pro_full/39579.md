conscriptional:/kənˈskrɪpʃənəl/
adjective
征兵的；与征兵有关的
relating to conscription (compulsory enlistment for military service).
The government introduced conscriptional measures during the war. 政府在战争期间实行了征兵措施。
The conscriptional age was lowered to eighteen.  征兵年龄降低到十八岁。
近义词: draft, compulsory
反义词: voluntary
词性: adjective
