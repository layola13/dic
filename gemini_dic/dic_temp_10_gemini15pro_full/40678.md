conversationalists: /ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃənəlɪsts/ (US) /ˌkɒnvəˈseɪʃənəlɪsts/ (UK)
n.
健谈者，爱说话的人
People who enjoy and frequently engage in conversations.
例句：The two conversationalists spent hours discussing their favorite books.  两位健谈者花了几个小时讨论他们最喜欢的书。

例句：The party was full of lively conversationalists, making it a very enjoyable evening.  派对上充满了活泼健谈的人，让这个夜晚非常愉快。

近义词：talkers, chatterboxes, communicators
反义词：quiet people, introverts
词性：noun
