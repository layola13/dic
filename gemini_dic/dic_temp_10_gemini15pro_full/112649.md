lowbred:/ˈloʊˌbrɛd/| adj.|粗野的，低级的，出身卑贱的|Of low breeding; vulgar; coarse; unrefined.|He displayed lowbred behavior at the dinner party, embarrassing his wife. 他在晚宴上表现粗俗，让他的妻子很尴尬。|近义词：vulgar, coarse, unrefined, ill-bred, baseborn|反义词：well-bred, refined, aristocratic, noble|词性:adjective

词源:  From low + bred (past participle of breed).  Meaning "of low or inferior breed" is from the late 14c.  The figurative sense of "vulgar, unrefined" is from the mid-15c.
