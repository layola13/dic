hosier:/ˈhoʊʒər/| n.|袜商；针织品商人|A person who sells stockings, socks, and other hosiery.|例句：The hosier had a wide selection of colorful socks.  这家袜商有各种各样的彩色袜子。

例句：He apprenticed with a hosier in his youth. 他年轻时在一位袜商那里当学徒。|近义词：haberdasher, clothier|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *hosier, from hose (meaning "leggings, stockings") + -ier (denoting an agent or occupation).  Ultimately from Frankish *hosa.
