computist:/kəmˈpjuːtɪst/| n.|计算员，计算机专家|A person skilled in computation or the use of computers.  Someone who performs calculations or computations, especially with a computer.|Early computists were essential for scientific advancements before the widespread availability of electronic computers. 早期的计算员在电子计算机普及之前对科学进步至关重要。|Synonyms: computer scientist, programmer, calculator, mathematician|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps Luddite in a specific context)|noun
