fishhook:/ˈfɪʃˌhʊk/| n.|鱼钩|A usually barbed hook for catching fish.|He baited the fishhook with a worm. 他用蠕虫作鱼饵挂在鱼钩上。|近义词：hook, angle|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *fishhok*, equivalent to fish + hook.
