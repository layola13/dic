nonrespondent:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈspɒn.dənt/ | /ˌnɑːn.rɪˈspɑːn.dənt/ | n. | 未答复者；无反应者 | A person who does not respond to a survey, questionnaire, or request for information. |  The survey had a high nonrespondent rate, making the results less reliable. 这项调查的无回应率很高，使得结果不太可靠。

The researchers tried to contact the nonrespondents by phone to understand why they didn't participate. 研究人员试图通过电话联系未答复者，以了解他们没有参与的原因。

| 近义词：non-replier, defaulter | 反义词: respondent | 词性: noun
