monocotyledon:/ˌmɒnəʊkɒtɪˈliːdən/ | n. |单子叶植物|A flowering plant with an embryo that bears a single cotyledon (seed leaf). Monocots also typically have leaves with parallel veins, scattered vascular bundles in the stem, the absence of a typical cambium, and an adventitious root system.|例句：Corn is a monocotyledon. 玉米是一种单子叶植物。

例句：Monocotyledons are one of the two major groups of flowering plants. 单子叶植物是开花植物的两大类群之一。|近义词：monocot|反义词：dicotyledon, dicot|词性: noun

monocot:/ˈmɒnəkɒt/ | n. |单子叶植物|Short for monocotyledon.|例句：Grasses are a common type of monocot. 草是一种常见的单子叶植物。

例句：Monocots typically have fibrous root systems. 单子叶植物通常有须根系。|近义词：monocotyledon|反义词：dicot, dicotyledon|词性: noun
