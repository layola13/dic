farse:/fɑːrs/| n.|闹剧；滑稽剧；胡扯；谎言|A ludicrous or extravagant act or situation; a humorous play or film based on unlikely situations and improbable coincidences; something that is untrue and intended to deceive somebody|The whole trial was a farce. 整个审判就是一场闹剧。|近义词:comedy, burlesque, mockery, sham, hoax|反义词:tragedy, drama, reality, truth|noun
