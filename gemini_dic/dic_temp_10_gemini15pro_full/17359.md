beacon:/ˈbiːkən/ (US), /ˈbiːkən/ (UK)| n.|灯塔，信号灯，烽火; 指路明灯|A fire or light set up in a high or prominent position as a warning, signal, or celebration. Also, something that serves as a guide or inspiration.|The lighthouse served as a beacon for ships lost in the fog. 灯塔为在雾中迷路的船只指明了方向。|synonyms: signal, warning, guide, landmark|antonyms: obscurity, darkness|noun
