equivalent:/ɪˈkwɪvələnt/ | adj. n. | 相等的，等价的；等价物；对应物 |Something that is equivalent is equal in value, amount, function, meaning, etc. |This amount of exercise is equivalent to walking about three miles.  这个运动量相当于步行大约三英里。| synonyms: equal, identical, same, similar, comparable, corresponding, analogous | antonyms: different, unequal, dissimilar, disparate | adjective


equivalent:/ɪˈkwɪvələnt/ | n. | 相等的，等价的；等价物；对应物 |Something that is of equal value, amount, function, meaning, etc. |There is no English equivalent for "bon appétit".  英语中没有与“bon appétit”（祝您好胃口）相等的表达。| synonyms: counterpart, parallel, match | antonyms: opposite, contrast | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *equivalente*, from Late Latin *aequivalentem* (nominative *aequivalens*), present participle of *aequivalēre* (“to be equivalent”), from Latin *aequus* (“equal, level, fair, just”) + *valēre* (“to be strong, be well, be worth”).
