cycad:/ˈsaɪkæd/| n. |苏铁|A palmlike plant of tropical and subtropical regions, bearing large male or female cones.  Cycads are gymnosperms, meaning their seeds are not enclosed in an ovary.|The botanical garden has a fascinating collection of cycads.|Synonyms: sago palm, fern palm|Antonyms: N/A|noun
