brumous:/ˈbruːməs/| adj.|雾蒙蒙的；烟雾弥漫的|Filled with or resembling mist or fog; misty.|The brumous air made it difficult to see the mountains. 雾蒙蒙的空气使人们难以看到群山。|近义词：misty, foggy, hazy, murky|反义词：clear, bright|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin brūmōsus (“full of winter”), from Latin brūma (“winter solstice; winter”).
