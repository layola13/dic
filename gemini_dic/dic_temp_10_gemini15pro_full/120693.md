misadvice:/ˌmɪsədˈvaɪs/ | n. |  错误的建议；不当的忠告 | Bad or incorrect advice. |  He followed his friend's misadvice and lost a lot of money. 他听从了朋友的错误建议，损失了很多钱。| Example:  The misadvice she received about her investments proved costly. 她收到的关于投资的错误建议证明代价高昂。|Synonyms: bad advice, poor advice, ill advice, wrong advice | Antonyms: good advice, sound advice, wise counsel | noun

misadvice:/ˌmɪsədˈvaɪs/ | vt. |  给…错误的建议；误导 | To give bad or incorrect advice to. |  He was misadvised about the legal implications of his actions. 他在行为的法律后果方面受到了误导。 | Example: I believe she was misadvised by her lawyer. 我相信她的律师给了她错误的建议。 | Synonyms: mislead, misguide, misdirect | Antonyms: advise correctly, guide well, counsel wisely | verb
