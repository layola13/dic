hatteria:/hætəˈriːə/ | n. | 喙头蜥；喙头目 | A reptile of the order Rhynchocephalia, found only in New Zealand. It is the only surviving member of its order, which flourished during the Triassic period. |  The tuatara (hatteria) is a remarkable example of a "living fossil".  喙头蜥是一个活化石的显著例子。 |  synonym: tuatara, sphenodon | antonym: N/A | noun
