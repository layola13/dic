impermeable:/ɪmˈpɜːrmiəbl/ /ɪmˈpɜːrmiəbəl/| adj.|不可渗透的，不透水的|Not allowing liquids or gases to pass through.|The membrane is impermeable to water. 这层膜不透水。|近义词：impenetrable, impervious, waterproof|反义词:permeable, porous|adjective

impermeable:/ɪmˈpɜːrmiəbl/ /ɪmˈpɜːrmiəbəl/| n.|不透水物，防渗材料|A material that does not allow liquids or gases to pass through.|They used an impermeable material to line the pond. 他们用一种不透水的材料衬在池塘里。|近义词：barrier, sealant|反义词:filter|noun
