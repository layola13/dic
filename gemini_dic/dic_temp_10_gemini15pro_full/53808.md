disconfirmation:/ˌdɪskɑːnfərˈmeɪʃən/
n.|不确认，证伪|The disconfirmation of his theory left him disappointed. 他的理论被证伪让他很失望。|
例句：
The experiment resulted in the disconfirmation of the hypothesis.  这个实验导致了假设的证伪。

His claims were met with strong disconfirmation from other researchers.  他的说法遭到了其他研究人员的强烈反驳。

近义词：refutation, invalidation, contradiction
反义词：confirmation, validation, support
词性：名词

disconfirm:/ˌdɪskənˈfɜːrm/
vt.|不确认，证伪，否定|The evidence disconfirmed his alibi.  证据否定了他的不在场证明。|
例句：
The new data disconfirmed the initial findings. 新数据否定了最初的发现。

Further research disconfirmed the popular belief about the effectiveness of the drug.  进一步的研究否定了关于这种药物有效性的普遍看法。

近义词：refute, invalidate, contradict
反义词：confirm, validate, support
词性：动词


词源：dis- (not) + confirm (to establish the truth of)  来自拉丁语 confirmare, con- (together) + firmare (to strengthen).
