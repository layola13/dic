herne:/hɜːrn/ | n. |苍鹭，(尤指)灰鹭 |A large wading bird with long legs, a long neck, and a long bill. It is typically grey or white. |例句：The herne stood motionless in the shallows, waiting for a fish. 苍鹭一动不动地站在浅滩上，等待着鱼儿上钩。

例句：The herne spread its wings and took flight. 苍鹭展开翅膀飞了起来。 |近义词：heron |反义词: N/A |词性: 名词

词源:
From Middle English *herne, from Old English *hearn, from Proto-Germanic *kraunaz, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱerh₂- (“bird”). Cognate with Middle Dutch herne, hern (“heron”), German Reiher (“heron”), Danish hejre (“heron”), Swedish häger (“heron”).
