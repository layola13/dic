beamless:/ˈbiːmlɪs/| adj.|无梁的，无光束的|Lacking beams, especially of light; dark.  Without visible beams of light.|The cave was beamless and eerily silent.  这个洞穴没有光线，安静得可怕。|近义词：dark, unlit, rayless|反义词:bright, radiant, luminous|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From beam + -less.  "Beam" comes from Old English "bēam," meaning "tree, wood, ray of light."  "-less" is a suffix indicating absence or lack of something.
