guru's:/ˈɡʊruːz/
noun
上师的；大师的；专家；权威的；
Possessive form of guru.  Belonging to a guru or gurus.  It signifies ownership or close relationship.
The guru's teachings were profound.  上师的教诲意义深远。
mentor's, teacher's, master's
student's, disciple's
n.


Etymology:
From Hindi गुरु (guru), from Sanskrit गुरु (guru, “heavy, weighty, venerable, respectable”).
