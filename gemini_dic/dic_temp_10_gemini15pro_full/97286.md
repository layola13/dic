ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *influenza*, literally “influence,” from Medieval Latin *influentia* "influence (of the stars)," from Latin *influentem* (nominative *influens*), present participle of *influere* "to flow into, to influence."  Borrowed in the mid-18th century, originally referring to diseases thought to be caused by the influence of the stars.
