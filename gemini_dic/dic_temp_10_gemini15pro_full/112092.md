longirostres:/ˌlɒŋɪˈrɒstriːz/ | n. |长吻类 |Long-beaked or long-snouted animals, especially a group of extinct crocodiles.  It's derived from Latin "longus" (long) and "rostrum" (beak/snout).| The longirostres, with their elongated snouts, were well-adapted for catching fish. 长吻类动物，凭借其细长的鼻子，非常适应捕鱼。 |Near synonyms: long-snouted, long-beaked | Antonyms:  short-snouted, short-beaked | noun
