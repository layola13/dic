mangroves:/ˈmæŋɡroʊvz/ (US), /ˈmæŋɡrəʊvz/ (UK)| n. |红树林|A tree or shrub that grows in chiefly tropical coastal swamps that are flooded at high tide. Mangroves typically have numerous tangled roots above ground and form dense thickets.|The mangroves protect the coastline from erosion.| 近义词: mangrove swamp, tidal forest| 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑内陆森林inland forest)| noun
