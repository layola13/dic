indorse:/ɪnˈdɔːrs/ /ɪnˈdɔːrs/ | vt.|背书；赞同；认可|To sign (a check or bill of exchange) on the back, usually to assign payment to someone else; to approve or support something.|She indorsed the check and deposited it. 她背书了支票并存入了银行。|近义词：endorse, approve, support, sanction, ratify|反义词：oppose, reject, disapprove, condemn|verb

indorse:/ɪnˈdɔːrs/ /ɪnˈdɔːrs/ | n.|背书；赞同|An act of indorsing; approval or support.|He gave his indorse to the project. 他对这个项目表示赞同。|近义词：endorsement, approval, support, sanction, ratification|反义词：opposition, rejection, disapproval, condemnation|noun
