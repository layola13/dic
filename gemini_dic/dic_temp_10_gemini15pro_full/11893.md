arrogator:/ˈærəɡeɪtər/ | n. | 僭越者；篡夺者；擅取者 | Someone who arrogates, or appropriates something to themselves without justification.  |  He acted as an arrogator of power, seizing control without any legitimate authority. 他像个权力篡夺者，没有任何合法授权就夺取了控制权。 |  usurper, appropriator |  renouncer, abdicator | noun

arrogate:/ˈærəɡeɪt/ | v. | 僭取；擅取；霸占 | To claim or seize something without justification. | The king arrogated the right to dissolve parliament. 国王僭取了解散议会的权利。 |  usurp, seize, appropriate | renounce, relinquish | verb
