aphemia:/əˈfiːmiə/| n. |失语症（一种运动性失语症，患者理解语言但不能说话或写字）|Loss of the power of speech or of articulate speech, while the understanding remains and the vocal organs are unimpaired; motor aphasia.|She suffers from aphemia after the stroke, understanding everything but unable to speak. 她中风后患上了失语症，能理解一切却无法说话.|Synonym: motor aphasia, anarthria|Antonym: fluency|Noun
