meditate:/ˈmedɪteɪt/ (US) /ˈmedɪteɪt/ (UK)| v. | 冥想，沉思，思考 | To think deeply and quietly, often for religious or spiritual purposes, or to consider something carefully. | He meditated on the meaning of life. 他沉思着生命的意义。

| He was meditating in the garden. 他在花园里冥想。| 近义词：contemplate, ponder, reflect, muse | 反义词：ignore, neglect | Verb
