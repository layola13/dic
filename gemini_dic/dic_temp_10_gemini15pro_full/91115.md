hyalophane:/ˈhaɪəloʊfeɪn/ | n. |透明长石|A barium-rich feldspar mineral, (Ba,K)Al(Si,Al)3O8, that forms transparent to translucent, white, gray, or yellowish crystals. It is typically found in metamorphic rocks. | Hyalophane is a relatively rare mineral found in certain metamorphic deposits. 透明长石是一种相对稀有的矿物，发现于某些变质矿床中。| Synonym: feldspar | Antonym: None | Noun
