bunchberry:/ˈbʌntʃˌberi/ (US), /ˈbʌntʃˌbɛri/ (UK)| n. |Bunchberry,  鹿蹄草|A low, creeping, evergreen plant (Cornus canadensis) of the dogwood family, bearing small greenish flowers surrounded by four white bracts and producing a cluster of bright red berries.|We found bunchberry growing in the shade of the pine trees. 我们在松树的荫凉处发现了鹿蹄草。|Near synonyms: dwarf cornel, Canadian dwarf cornel, crackerberry | Antonyms: N/A (It's a plant; direct antonyms don't apply.) | Noun
