incapacity:/ˌɪnkəˈpæsəti/ | n. |无能;无力;没有能力|Lack of ability or power to do something; inability.|He was let go from his job due to his incapacity to perform the required tasks. 他因为无法完成所需任务而被解雇了。|She suffered from an incapacity to speak after the accident. 事故发生后，她丧失了说话的能力。|近义词: inability, incompetence, disability, powerlessness |反义词: ability, competence, capability, power |词性: 名词

incapacity:/ˌɪnkəˈpæsəti/ | n. |丧失行为能力;不适合|The state of being legally incapable of doing something, such as making decisions or signing contracts.|His mental incapacity prevented him from managing his own finances. 他的精神失能使他无法管理自己的财务。|Due to his incapacity, a guardian was appointed to manage his affairs. 由于他丧失了行为能力，一位监护人被任命来管理他的事务。|近义词: incompetence, disqualification, ineligibility |反义词: competence, eligibility, qualification |词性: 名词
