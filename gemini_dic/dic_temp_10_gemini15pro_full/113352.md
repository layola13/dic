luxury:/ˈlʌkʃəri/ (US), /ˈlʌkʃəri/ (UK)| n. adj.| 奢侈，奢侈品；享受，奢华|Something that is expensive and enjoyable but not essential.|She lives a life of luxury. 她过着奢侈的生活。|近义词：opulence, extravagance, indulgence|反义词：necessity, austerity, poverty|noun

luxury:/ˈlʌkʃəri/ (US), /ˈlʌkʃəri/ (UK)| adj.| 奢华的，豪华的|Providing great comfort and pleasure, especially at great expense.|a luxury hotel  一家豪华酒店|近义词：luxurious, opulent, lavish|反义词：basic, simple, modest|adjective
