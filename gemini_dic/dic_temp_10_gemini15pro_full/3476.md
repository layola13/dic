aftercare:/ˈæftərˌkɛr/ | n. | 善后护理；后续照料；后期保养 |  The continuing care of a person after they have left a hospital, prison, or other institution. | The hospital provides excellent aftercare for its patients. 这家医院为病人提供优秀的后续照料。 | 近义词: follow-up care, post-treatment care, support | 反义词: neglect | 词性: 名词
