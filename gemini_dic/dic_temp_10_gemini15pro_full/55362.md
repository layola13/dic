distant:/ˈdɪstənt/ (US), /ˈdɪstənt/ (UK)| adj.|遥远的；疏远的；冷淡的|Far away in space or time; not friendly; reserved.|例句：The distant mountains were capped with snow. 遥远的山峰覆盖着积雪。|例句：He seemed distant and preoccupied. 他显得冷漠而心事重重。|近义词：remote, far, aloof, cold|反义词：near, close, friendly, warm|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Old French *distant* (unattested), from Latin *distantem*, accusative singular masculine of *distans*, present participle of *distare* "to stand apart, be distant," from *dis-* "apart" + *stare* "to stand."
