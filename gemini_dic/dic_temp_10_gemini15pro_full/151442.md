paroles:/pəˈroʊlz/ | n. | lyrics; words (especially of a song) | Words of a song. | The paroles of the song were moving and poetic. 这首歌的歌词感人至深，富有诗意。 | synonyms: lyrics, words, text | antonyms: music, melody | noun

Etymology: From Old French "parole" (word, speech), from Latin "parabola" (comparison, parable, word). Ultimately from Greek "parabole" (a placing beside; comparison, parable).
