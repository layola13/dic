inantherate:/ɪnˈænθəˌreɪt/ | adj. | 花药着生的，花药内生的 | Having anthers borne within the corolla tube or throat. | Example: The inantherate flowers of the foxglove are a striking feature.  毛地黄的花药内生是一个显著的特征。| Example:  The botanist identified the plant by its inantherate stamens.  植物学家通过其内生的雄蕊识别了这种植物。 | Synonym: included | Antonym: exserted | Adjective
