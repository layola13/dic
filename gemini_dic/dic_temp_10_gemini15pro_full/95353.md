inception:/ɪnˈsɛpʃən/| n.|开始，开端，成立，(尤指想法、计划、组织等的)创始|The inception of the project was in 2010.  这个项目的开始是在2010年。|Beginning, commencement, outset, origin| Cessation, termination| noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *inceptionem, from Latin incipere (“to begin”), from in (“in, on”) + capere (“to take, seize”).
