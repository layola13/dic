misbelieve:/ˌmɪsbɪˈliːv/
verb
不信；怀疑；误信
To not believe something, or to believe something that is not true.
He misbelieved the doctor's diagnosis and sought a second opinion. 他不相信医生的诊断，于是又去找了另一个医生。
She misbelieved his promises and was deeply hurt when he broke them. 她误信了他的承诺，当他违背承诺时，她深受伤害。

近义词:disbelieve, doubt
反义词:believe, trust
词性:verb
