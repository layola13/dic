nudicaul:/njuːdɪˈkɔːl/| adj.|裸茎的；无茎叶的|Having the stem apparently leafless; having leaves only at the base of the stem, close to the ground. |Example 1: The nudicaul dandelion displays a bright yellow flower atop a long, leafless stalk.  裸茎蒲公英在长而无叶的茎上开着一朵鲜黄色的花。

Example 2:  Many desert plants are nudicaul, reducing water loss through leaves. 很多沙漠植物都是裸茎的，以减少水分通过叶子的流失。|Synonym: acaulescent, stemless|Antonym: leafy, foliose|word property: adjective
