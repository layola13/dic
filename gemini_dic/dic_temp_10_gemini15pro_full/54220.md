disfiguring:/dɪsˈfɪɡərɪŋ/
adjective
损毁容貌的；毁容的；丑陋的
Spoiling the appearance of something or someone, especially their face.
The accident left him with a disfiguring scar.  这场事故给他留下了一个毁容的疤痕。
The disease can cause disfiguring skin lesions. 这种疾病会导致毁容的皮肤损伤。

近义词:deforming, scarring, mutilating, marring
反义词:beautifying, enhancing, improving

词性:adjective
词源:From disfigure + -ing.  Disfigure comes from Old French desfigurer, from des- (expressing reversal) + figurer (to fashion, represent).
