let-sleeping-dogs-lie:/lɛt ˈsliːpɪŋ dɒɡz laɪ/ |idiom| 不要惹麻烦；别多事；别自找麻烦 | To avoid interfering in a situation that is currently stable and could potentially cause problems if disturbed.  |Example: It's best to let sleeping dogs lie and not bring up that sensitive topic again. 最好不要惹麻烦，不要再提起那个敏感话题了。

Example:  He decided to let sleeping dogs lie and not question his boss's decision. 他决定不要自找麻烦，不去质疑老板的决定。|近义词: leave well enough alone, don't rock the boat |反义词: stir the pot, open a can of worms | idiom
