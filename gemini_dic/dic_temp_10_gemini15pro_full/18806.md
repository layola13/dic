berserker:/bərˈsɜːrkər/ /bərˈzɜːrkər/| n.|狂战士；暴怒的人|A fierce, wild warrior or one behaving like a maniac.|He fought like a berserker, fueled by rage and adrenaline. 他像个狂战士一样战斗，被愤怒和肾上腺素所驱使。|Synonyms: maniac, wildman, madman, fanatic|Antonyms: pacifist, calm person|Noun

berserker:/bərˈsɜːrkər/ /bərˈzɜːrkər/| adj.|狂暴的；暴怒的|Resembling or characteristic of a berserker; frenzied.|His berserker rage terrified everyone around him. 他狂暴的愤怒吓坏了周围的每个人。|Synonyms: frenzied, furious, raging, berserk|Antonyms: calm, peaceful, tranquil|Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *berserkr, from *ber- "bear" + *serkr "shirt, coat of mail".  Originally referred to Norse warriors who fought in a frenzied state, possibly wearing bearskins.
