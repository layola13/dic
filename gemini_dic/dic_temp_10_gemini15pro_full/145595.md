overcondensed:/ˌōvərˈkənˈdenst/ |adj.| 过于浓缩的，过度压缩的 | excessively condensed; reduced to an extremely dense state | The overcondensed milk was thick and difficult to pour.  过于浓缩的牛奶很稠，难以倒出。| The report was overcondensed and lacked crucial details. 报告过于简略，缺少关键细节。 | 近义词: dense, compact, concentrated | 反义词: dilute, thin, expanded | 词性: 形容词

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "condense" (from Latin "condensare," meaning "to thicken")
