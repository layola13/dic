causativity:/ˌkɔːzəˈtɪvɪti/
n.
因果关系; 使役性
The property of a verb or construction that indicates that a subject causes someone or something else to do or be something.
The teacher encouraged the students' creativity. 老师鼓励学生的创造力。（并非老师直接创造，而是使学生创造）
Causativity plays a crucial role in understanding the relationship between events. 因果关系在理解事件之间的关系方面起着至关重要的作用。
近义词:causation, agency
反义词:inertness, passivity
词性:noun
