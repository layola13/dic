masorete:/ˈmæsəˌraɪt/| n.|马所拉文本|The traditional Hebrew text of the Jewish Bible, meticulously copied and annotated by the Masoretes between the 6th and 10th centuries CE.  马所拉文本是犹太圣经的传统希伯来文本，由马所拉学者在公元6世纪到10世纪之间精心抄写和注释。|例句：Scholars study the Masorete text to understand the history of the Hebrew Bible.  学者们研究马所拉文本以了解希伯来圣经的历史。

|近义词：Masoretic Text|反义词:|词性:noun
