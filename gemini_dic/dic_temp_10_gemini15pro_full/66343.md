exculpatory:/ɪkˈskʌlpətɔːri/
adjective
辩解的，开脱的
Tending to clear from a charge of fault or guilt.
The lawyer presented exculpatory evidence that proved his client's innocence.  律师出示了证明其委托人无罪的辩解性证据。

The defendant offered an exculpatory statement to the police. 被告向警方提供了一份辩解声明。

近义词:absolving, clearing, vindicating, exonerating
反义词:incriminating, accusing, blaming
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *exculpātōrius, from Latin exculpāre (to excuse, clear from blame).  Ex- (out of, from) + culpāre (to blame).
