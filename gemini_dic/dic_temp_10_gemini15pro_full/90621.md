humanify:/ˌhjuːmənɪfaɪ/ |v. |使人性化；赋予人性|To make something more human or humane; to attribute human qualities to something non-human. |例句：The company tried to humanify its brand by featuring employees in its advertisements.  公司试图通过在广告中突出员工来使其品牌人性化。

例句：They've humanified the robot by giving it a friendly voice and expressive eyes.  他们通过赋予机器人友好的声音和富有表情的眼睛来使其人性化。 |近义词：anthropomorphize, personalize|反义词:dehumanize, objectify |词性:动词
