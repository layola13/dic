inadmissibility:/ˌɪnədˌmɪsəˈbɪləti/| n. |不可受理性，不许可入境|The inadmissibility of the evidence led to the case being dismissed.  证据不可采纳导致案件被驳回。|例句：His inadmissibility to the country was due to a previous criminal conviction.  他因先前的犯罪记录而不得入境该国。

例句：The inadmissibility of hearsay evidence is a cornerstone of the legal system. 传闻证据的不可采纳性是法律体系的基石。|近义词：unacceptability, ineligibility|反义词: admissibility, eligibility|词性:noun

inadmissible:/ˌɪnədˈmɪsəbl/| adj. |不可接受的，不许可入境的|Inadmissible evidence cannot be used in court. 不可接受的证据不能在法庭上使用。|例句：The judge ruled the testimony inadmissible because it was hearsay. 法官裁定该证词不可采纳，因为它是传闻。

例句：His visa application was rejected due to inadmissible documentation. 他的签证申请因文件不合格而被拒绝。|近义词：unacceptable, ineligible|反义词: admissible, eligible|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + admissible.  "Admissible" comes from Latin "admittere" (to admit), from "ad-" (to) + "mittere" (to send, let go).
