nonparallel:/ˌnɑːnˈpærəlel/ | adj. | 不平行的，不一致的 | Not parallel; not having the same direction, tendency, or course; not corresponding. | The two lines are nonparallel, eventually they will intersect. 这两条线不平行，最终它们会相交。|  The two arguments are nonparallel, making comparison difficult. 这两个论点不一致，使得比较变得困难。| 近义词: divergent, disparate, dissimilar, incongruous | 反义词: parallel, similar, congruent | 词性:形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + parallel (from Greek parallelos, meaning "alongside one another")
