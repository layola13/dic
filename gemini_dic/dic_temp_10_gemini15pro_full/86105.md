heartache:/ˈhɑːrteɪk/ | n. | 心痛，悲痛 | A feeling of sadness and emotional pain. |  She suffered terrible heartache after the death of her husband. 她丈夫去世后，她遭受了巨大的悲痛。

|  He experienced a deep heartache when his girlfriend left him.  当他的女朋友离开他时，他经历了深深的痛苦。 | synonyms: grief, sorrow, anguish, agony | antonyms: joy, happiness | noun
