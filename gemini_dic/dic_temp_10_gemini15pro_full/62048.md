encapsulated:/ɪnˈkæpsjʊleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 被封装的，被包裹的 | Enclosed in a capsule or other protective covering.  | The radioactive material was encapsulated in concrete. 放射性物质被封装在混凝土中。 |  The virus is encapsulated within a protein coat. 病毒被包裹在蛋白质外壳内。 | 近义词: enclosed, encased, sealed, covered | 反义词: exposed, open, uncovered | 词性: adjective

encapsulate:/ɪnˈkæpsjʊleɪt/ | vt. |  封装，概括 | To enclose something in or as if in a capsule. To express the essential features of (something) succinctly.  | The company's mission is encapsulated in its motto. 公司的使命在其座右铭中得到了概括。 | The new drug encapsulates the active ingredient in a biodegradable coating. 新药将活性成分封装在可生物降解的涂层中。 | 近义词: summarize, condense, epitomize, enclose | 反义词: expand, elaborate | 词性: verb

词源: From Late Latin "capsula," meaning "small box, chest," diminutive of "capsa."  "En-" is a prefix meaning "in" or "within."
