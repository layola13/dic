misrecord:/ˌmɪsrɪˈkɔːrd/ | v. | 错误记录；记错 | To record something incorrectly or inaccurately. |  The clerk misrecorded the sale and charged me twice. 店员错误地记录了销售情况，向我收了两次费用。

misrecord:/ˌmɪsrɪˈkɔːrd/ | n. | 错误记录 | An incorrect or inaccurate record. | The misrecord in the database caused a lot of confusion. 数据库中的错误记录造成了很多混乱。


Etymology:
From mis- + record.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Old English and Germanic origin, meaning "wrong," "bad," or "incorrect." "Record" comes from the Old French "recorder," and ultimately from the Latin "recordari" meaning "to remember," "to call to mind."

Synonyms (v.): misreport, misstate, misrepresent, err, blunder, fumble
Synonyms (n.): error, mistake, inaccuracy, blunder, slip, fault
Antonyms (v.): record correctly, document accurately, report truthfully
Antonyms (n.): accuracy, correctness, precision, truth
