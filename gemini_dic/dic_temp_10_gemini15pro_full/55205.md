dissertationists:/ˌdɪsəˈteɪʃənɪsts/
n.
论文作者；学位论文撰写人
A person who writes a dissertation, especially one submitted for a higher degree.
Many dissertationists struggle with writer's block. 许多论文作者都与写作障碍作斗争。
The library provides resources for dissertationists. 图书馆为学位论文撰写人提供资源。
Synonyms: doctoral candidates, graduate students, researchers, scholars
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps consider:  undergraduates, non-academics)
noun
