frostlike:/ˈfrɔːstlaɪk/| adj.|霜状的，像霜的|Resembling frost in appearance or properties; frosty.|The windows were covered in a frostlike pattern. 窗户上覆盖着霜状的图案。|近义词：frosty, icy, glacial|反义词:warm, thawing|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "frost" + "-like".  "Frost" comes from Old English "forst," meaning "frost," and "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling" or "similar to."
