chanterships:/ˈtʃæntərʃɪps/
n.
吟唱者身份；吟唱的职位；领唱的职位
The position or office of a chanter, especially in a cathedral or church choir.  A chanter is a lead singer, particularly in religious settings.
He held the chantership at the cathedral for over two decades.  他在大教堂担任领唱职位超过二十年。
近义词: lead singer, precentorship
反义词: None.
词性: noun
