confection:/kənˈfekʃən/ | n. | 甜食，糖果；精心制作的东西 | Something, especially food, that is elaborately prepared or decorated. |  The bakery displayed a dazzling array of confections.  这家面包店展示了一系列令人眼花缭乱的甜点。

The dress was a confection of lace and silk. 这件连衣裙是用蕾丝和丝绸精心制作的。 | 近义词：sweet, treat, delicacy, dessert | 反义词: staple, basic | 词性: 名词

词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French confection, from Latin confectio(n-), from conficere ‘put together, complete,’ from con- ‘together’ + facere ‘do, make.’
