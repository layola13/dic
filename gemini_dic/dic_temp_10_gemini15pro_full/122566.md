moderately:/ˈmɒdərətli/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdərətli/ (US)| adv.|适度地，中等地|In a moderate manner; to a moderate degree; not excessively or extremely.  It describes something that is within reasonable limits, not extreme.|The pain was moderately severe. 疼痛程度中等。|She is a moderately successful businesswoman. 她是一位中等成功的 businesswoman。|近义词：reasonably, fairly, averagely, mildly|反义词：extremely, excessively, intensely, severely|词性:副词
