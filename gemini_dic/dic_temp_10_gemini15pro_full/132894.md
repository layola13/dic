nonelectrical:/ˌnɒnɪˈlɛktrɪk(ə)l/
adjective
非电的；不使用电的
Not operated by electricity; not producing or using electricity.
例句：
They use a nonelectrical can opener.
他们使用一个非电动的开罐器。

This building has a nonelectrical heating system.
这栋楼有一个非电力的供暖系统。

近义词：manual, mechanical
反义词：electrical, electric

词性: adjective
词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "electrical" (adjective relating to electricity). "Electrical" ultimately derives from the Greek word "elektron" meaning "amber," as amber was one of the first substances observed to exhibit electrical properties.
