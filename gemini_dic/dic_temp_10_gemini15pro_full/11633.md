armchair: /ˈɑːrmtʃeə(r)/ (UK) /ˈɑːrmtʃer/ (US)| n.|扶手椅；单人沙发|A comfortable chair with supports for the arms.|She sat in her armchair by the fire, reading a book. 她坐在壁炉旁的扶手椅上看书。|Synonyms: easy chair, lounge chair|Antonyms: stool, bench|noun

armchair: /ˈɑːrmtʃeə(r)/ (UK) /ˈɑːrmtʃer/ (US)| adj.|不切实际的；空谈的；纸上谈兵的|Not based on practical experience; theoretical.|He's an armchair traveler who reads about exotic places but never goes to them. 他是一个纸上谈兵的旅行家，只阅读关于异国风情的地方，却从不去那里。|Synonyms: theoretical, speculative|Antonyms: practical, hands-on|adjective
