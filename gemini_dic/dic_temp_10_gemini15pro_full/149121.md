oxidability:/ˌɒksɪdəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 氧化性；可氧化性 | The capacity of a substance to be oxidized.  |  The oxidability of iron is higher than that of gold. 铁的氧化性比金高。 | Synonyms: oxidation potential, oxidizability | Antonyms: reducibility, resistance to oxidation | noun
