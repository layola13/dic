myriads:/ˈmɪriədz/| n.|无数，极大数量|A vast, indefinite number; countless.  |例句：Myriads of stars twinkled in the night sky. 无数星星在夜空中闪烁。

|例句：There are myriads of insects in the rainforest.  雨林中有无数的昆虫。

|近义词：countless, innumerable, numerous, a multitude of, a host of|反义词:few, scarce|词性:名词


myriad:/ˈmɪriəd/| adj.|无数的，大量的|Countless or extremely great in number.|例句：The city offers myriad opportunities for entertainment. 这座城市提供了无数的娱乐机会。

|例句：She faced myriad problems when she started her own business. 她创业时面临着无数的问题。

|近义词：countless, innumerable, numerous, a multitude of, a host of|反义词:few, scarce|词性:形容词
