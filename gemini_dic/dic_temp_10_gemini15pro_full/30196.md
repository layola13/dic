celiac: /ˈsiːliæk/ (US), /ˈsiːliæk/ (UK)| adj. n.| 腹腔的；乳糜泻|Relating to the abdomen, particularly the part of the small intestine where nutrients are absorbed.  Also refers to a disease, celiac disease, in which the small intestine is hypersensitive to gluten, leading to difficulty in digesting food.|She has celiac disease and must follow a strict gluten-free diet. 她患有乳糜泻，必须严格遵守无麸质饮食。|Synonyms: abdominal, intestinal (for anatomical sense);  gluten-sensitive, gluten-intolerant (for disease sense)|Antonyms: None (for anatomical sense); gluten-tolerant (for disease sense)|adjective, noun


celiac: /ˈsiːliæk/ (US), /ˈsiːliæk/ (UK)| n.| 乳糜泻患者|A person who has celiac disease.|The restaurant catered to celiacs by offering a separate gluten-free menu. 这家餐厅通过提供单独的无麸质菜单来满足乳糜泻患者的需求。|Synonyms:  gluten-sensitive individual, person with celiac disease|Antonyms: None|noun
