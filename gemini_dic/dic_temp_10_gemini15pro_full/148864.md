overweary:/ˌoʊvərˈwɪri/| adj.|过度疲倦的，极其疲惫的|Exhausted to the point of weariness; extremely tired. |The overweary travelers finally reached their destination.  过度疲惫的旅行者终于到达了目的地。|Synonyms: exhausted, fatigued, worn out, tired, spent, drained, jaded|Antonyms: energetic, refreshed, invigorated|adjective

overweary:/ˌoʊvərˈwɪri/| verb (rarely used) |使过度疲劳|To make excessively weary; to exhaust. |The long journey overwearied the horses.  漫长的旅程使马匹过度疲劳。|Synonyms: exhaust, fatigue, tire, wear out|Antonyms: refresh, invigorate|verb
