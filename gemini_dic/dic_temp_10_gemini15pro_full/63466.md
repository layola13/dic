entrick:/ɪnˈtrɪk/ | v. | 欺骗；诱骗；使...感兴趣 | To deceive or trick someone, often by cunning or flattery.  Also, to fascinate or captivate. | He was entricked by her beauty and charm. 他被她的美貌和魅力所迷惑。 |Synonyms: beguile, charm, captivate, enchant, lure, tempt | Antonyms: repel, disgust, disenchant | Verb

entrick:/ɪnˈtrɪk/ | n. | 诡计；阴谋 | A trick or scheme, typically one designed to deceive someone. |  The whole affair was an entrick to get him out of the country. 整个事件是一个把他弄出国的诡计。 | Synonyms: ruse, ploy, scheme, trick, wile | Antonyms: honesty, truthfulness | Noun
