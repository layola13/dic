criticiser:/ˈkrɪtɪsaɪzər/| n.|批评家，批判者|Someone who expresses disapproval of someone or something. |例句：The film criticiser panned the movie for its poor acting.  这位影评家批评这部电影的演技拙劣。

例句：He's a harsh criticiser of the government's policies. 他是政府政策的严厉批评者。|近义词：critic, detractor, faultfinder, attacker|反义词:supporter, advocate, proponent|词性:noun
