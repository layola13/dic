mulish:/ˈmjuːlɪʃ/| adj.|固执的，倔强的|Like a mule, unreasonably and stubbornly unwilling to change one's opinion or behavior.|He was mulish and refused to listen to anyone's advice. 他很固执，不肯听任何人的劝告。|The child was being mulish about eating his vegetables.  这个孩子在吃蔬菜方面很固执。|stubborn, obstinate, headstrong, inflexible, intractable|compliant, amenable, docile|adjective
