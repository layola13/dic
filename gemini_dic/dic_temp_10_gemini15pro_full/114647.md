malformed:/ˌmælˈfɔːrmd/ /ˌmælˈfɔːrnd/ | adj. |畸形的，错误格式的|Faulty in structure, formation, or growth; badly formed.  Incorrectly structured or formatted, especially of data or code.|The program crashed because of a malformed data file. 程序因为一个格式错误的数据文件而崩溃。|Synonyms: misshapen, deformed, distorted, flawed, invalid, corrupted|Antonyms: well-formed, perfect, correct|adjective
