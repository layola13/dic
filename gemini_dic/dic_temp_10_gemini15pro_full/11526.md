ariman:/ˈærɪmən/ | n. | 阿里曼 | In Zoroastrianism, Ariman is the evil spirit of destruction and the source of all evil in the world.  He is the opponent of Ahura Mazda, the good spirit. | The ancient Persians believed that Ariman was responsible for all the suffering and misfortune in the world.  古代波斯人认为阿里曼是世界上所有苦难和不幸的根源。 |  Ariman brought disease and death upon the land. 阿里曼给大地带来了疾病和死亡。 | synonyms: devil, satan, demon, Angra Mainyu | antonyms: Ahura Mazda, god, good | noun
