baroscope:/ˈbærəˌskəʊp/| n.|气压计，气压秤|An instrument that indicates changes in atmospheric pressure, often by the rising or sinking of a float in a liquid. |The baroscope indicated a drop in pressure, suggesting an approaching storm. 气压计显示压力下降，预示着暴风雨即将来临。|Synonyms: barometer, aneroid barometer|Antonyms: N/A|noun
