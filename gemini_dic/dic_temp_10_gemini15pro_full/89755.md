hopscotch:/ˈhɑːpskɑːtʃ/ /ˈhɒpskɒtʃ/| n. |跳房子游戏；跳房子格子|A children's game in which a player hops through a series of marked squares on the ground. |例句：The children were playing hopscotch in the playground. 孩子们在操场上玩跳房子游戏。

例句：She drew a hopscotch grid on the sidewalk with chalk. 她用粉笔在人行道上画了一个跳房子格子。|近义词：None (as a game)|反义词:None|noun
