opiferous:/əˈpɪfərəs/
adjective
多产的，丰富的
producing or yielding much; abundant.
The opiferous land produced a bountiful harvest. 这片肥沃的土地带来了丰收。
近义词:productive, fertile, fruitful, abundant, copious
反义词:barren, infertile, unproductive, scarce
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *opifer* "aid-bringing," from *ops* "wealth, resources, power" + *-fer* "bearing, carrying."
