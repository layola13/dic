luxuries:/ˈlʌkʃəriːz/
n.
奢侈品；奢华；享受；乐趣
things that are expensive and not necessary but that provide comfort and pleasure
例句：They live a life of luxury. 他们过着奢侈的生活。

例句：She enjoys the simple luxuries of life, like a hot bath and a good book. 她享受生活中简单的奢侈，比如热水澡和一本好书。

近义词：comforts, amenities, indulgences, extravagances
反义词：necessities, essentials

词性:noun (plural)
词源:From Old French *luxurie, from Latin luxuria (“excess, abundance, profusion”), from luxus (“dislocation, extravagance, excess”), related to lucere (“to shine, be light”), from Proto-Italic *louksō, from Proto-Indo-European *lewk- (“light, brightness”).
