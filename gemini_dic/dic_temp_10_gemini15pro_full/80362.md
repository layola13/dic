goldstone:/ˈɡoʊldˌstoʊn/| n.|金光石，人造宝石，仿宝石|A type of glittering glass that resembles aventurine.  It is made by including metallic particles in molten glass.|The vase was made of a beautiful blue goldstone.|Synonyms: aventurine glass, imitation gemstone, simulated gemstone|Antonyms: natural gemstone, precious stone|noun

goldstone:/ˈɡoʊldˌstoʊn/| n.|戈德斯通玻色子|A massless particle predicted to exist in some theories of particle physics.|The existence of goldstone bosons is a consequence of spontaneous symmetry breaking.|Synonyms: Nambu-Goldstone boson|Antonyms: N/A|noun
