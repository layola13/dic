accoil:/əˈkɔɪl/| vt.|缠绕，卷绕|To wind something into rings or coils.|He ac coiled the rope neatly. 他把绳子整齐地盘绕起来.|近义词：coil, wind, twine, twist|反义词:uncoil, unwind|动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *acoiller (unattested), from a- (prefix indicating motion towards) + coillir "to collect, gather," from Latin colligere "to gather together, collect," from com- "together" + legere "to gather, select."
