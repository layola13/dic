nonforbearing:/ˌnɑːnfɔːrˈbɛrɪŋ/
adjective
不宽容的，不忍耐的
Unwilling to tolerate or be patient with something or someone.
She cast a nonforbearing look at the noisy children. 她不耐烦地瞪了一眼吵闹的孩子们。
近义词: impatient, intolerant, unforgiving
反义词: patient, tolerant, forgiving
词性: adjective

词源:
From non- (not) + forbearing (patient, tolerant). "Forbearing" comes from the Middle English "forberen," from Old English "forberan," meaning "to endure, restrain oneself."
