disterminate:/dɪˈstɜːrmɪneɪt/
|v.|消灭，灭绝，根除|To exterminate or destroy completely.|The disease was disterminated through a rigorous vaccination program. 通过严格的疫苗接种计划，这种疾病被根除了。|例句：The government launched a campaign to disterminate the rats infesting the city. 政府发起了一场消灭城市老鼠的运动。|近义词：exterminate, eradicate, annihilate, destroy|反义词：create, establish, propagate|词性:动词

distermination:/dɪˌstɜːrmɪˈneɪʃən/
|n.|消灭，灭绝，根除|The act of exterminating or destroying completely.|The distermination of the insect pest was successful.  对害虫的灭绝是成功的。|例句：The distermination of smallpox was a major triumph of modern medicine. 天花的根除是现代医学的一项重大胜利。|近义词：extermination, eradication, annihilation, destruction|反义词：creation, establishment, propagation|词性:名词
