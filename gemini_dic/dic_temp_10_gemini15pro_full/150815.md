parafiscal:/ˌpærəˈfɪskəl/| adj.|准财政的；类似财政的|Relating to or denoting a tax or levy that is similar to a tax but is imposed for a specific purpose.|The government imposed a parafiscal charge on imported goods to fund worker retraining programs. 政府对进口商品征收准财政费用，以资助工人再培训项目。|Synonyms: quasi-fiscal|Antonyms: N/A|Adjective
