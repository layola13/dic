deadlatch:/ˈdɛdlætʃ/| n.|插销；自动锁；|A deadlatch is a type of door lock that automatically latches when the door is closed.|The front door has a deadlatch for added security. 前门有一个插销以增加安全性。|Synonyms: latch, bolt, lock|Antonyms: unlock, open|noun

deadlatch:/ˈdɛdlætʃ/| vt.|用插销锁上|To secure with a deadlatch.|He deadlatched the door behind him. 他用插销锁上了身后的门。|Synonyms: lock, bolt, fasten|Antonyms: unlock, open|verb
