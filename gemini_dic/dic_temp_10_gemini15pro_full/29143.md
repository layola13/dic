casemate:/ˈkeɪsmeɪt/ | n. |（尤指军舰上）炮位，炮廓|A protected structure, especially in a warship, for housing a gun.|The battleship's casemates housed powerful cannons.  这艘战舰的炮廓里装有威力强大的火炮。|The casemate provided a secure firing position for the ship's artillery. 这个炮位为舰炮提供了一个安全的射击位置。|近义词：gun emplacement, battery|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|名词


词源:
From French casemate, from Italian casamatta "covered place, casemate," from casa "house" + matta "mat, dull" (perhaps implying a darkened space).
