canalize:/ˈkænəlaɪz/ | v. | 开凿运河，引导，限制 | To make into or like a canal; to direct along a specific path. |  The government canalized the river to prevent flooding. 政府开凿运河以防止洪水。 | channel, direct, guide, restrict | obstruct, free, release | verb
