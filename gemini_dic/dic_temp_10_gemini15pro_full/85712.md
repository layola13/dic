havockers:/ˈhævəkərz/| n. |破坏者，制造混乱的人|People who cause chaos or destruction; those who wreak havoc. |The havockers spread through the city, leaving a trail of destruction in their wake. 破坏者席卷了整个城市，留下一片狼藉。| The havockers disrupted the peaceful protest, turning it into a riot. 破坏者扰乱了和平抗议，将其变成了暴动。|Synonyms: vandals, destroyers, saboteurs, disrupters|Antonyms: protectors, guardians, builders, creators|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the noun "havoc" + "-er" (agent suffix). "Havoc" comes from the Anglo-Norman *havoc, meaning "pillage, devastation," of uncertain origin.
