exocrines:/ˈɛksəkrɪnz/ /ˈɛksoʊkraɪnz/| n.|外分泌腺|Glands that secrete their products through ducts onto epithelial surfaces, such as the skin or the lining of the digestive tract.  These secretions include sweat, tears, saliva, and digestive enzymes.|The pancreas has both endocrine and exocrine functions. 胰腺兼有内分泌和外分泌功能。|
近义词:glandular epithelium|反义词:endocrines|词性:noun

exocrine:/ˈɛksəkrɪn/ /ˈɛksoʊkraɪn/| adj.|外分泌的|Relating to or denoting glands that secrete their products through ducts opening onto an epithelial surface.|The exocrine pancreas produces digestive enzymes. 外分泌胰腺产生消化酶。|近义词:secretory|反义词:endocrine|词性:adjective
