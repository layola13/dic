indeprecable:/ˌɪndɪˈprɛkəbl/ | adj. | 不可轻视的，极宝贵的，无法估价的 | Not subject to depreciation; not losing value; invaluable.  | This antique watch is indeprecable due to its historical significance. 这块古董手表因其历史意义而极具价值，不可轻视。 |  The family photos are indeprecable to me.  这些家庭照片对我来说无比珍贵。 | Synonyms: invaluable, priceless, precious, irreplaceable | Antonyms: worthless, valueless, cheap, common | word type: Adjective

Etymology:
From in- (not) + deprecable (capable of being depreciated), from Late Latin deprecabilis, from deprecari (to pray against, deprecate). Ultimately from Latin precari (to pray).
