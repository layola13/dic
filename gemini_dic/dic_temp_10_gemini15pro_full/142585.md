osteodystrophic:/ˌɒstiəʊdɪˈstrɒfɪk/ | adj. | 骨营养不良的 | Relating to or characterized by osteodystrophy, a disorder of bone metabolism. |  Osteodystrophic changes are common in patients with chronic kidney disease.  慢性肾病患者常见骨营养不良改变。|  Synonym: osteopathic, osteomalacic | Antonym: healthy bone | Adjective

osteodystrophy:/ˌɒstiəʊdɪˈstrɒfi/ | n. | 骨营养不良 | A disorder of bone metabolism, often associated with chronic kidney disease. | Renal osteodystrophy can lead to bone pain and fractures. 肾性骨营养不良会导致骨痛和骨折。| Synonym: osteopathy, osteomalacia | Antonym: healthy bone | Noun
