kvetching:/ˈkvɛtʃɪŋ/
n.|抱怨，牢骚|The constant kvetching about the weather is getting tiresome.  不断抱怨天气让人厌烦。|Complaining, whining, griping|Praising, commending
词性:名词

kvetch:/kvɛtʃ/
v.|抱怨，发牢骚|He's always kvetching about something. 他总是抱怨一些事情。|Complain, whine, gripe|Praise, commend
词性:动词


kvetchy:/ˈkvɛtʃi/
adj.|爱抱怨的，牢骚满腹的|She's become quite kvetchy in her old age. 她老了以后变得很爱抱怨。|Complaining, whiny, griping|Content, satisfied
词性:形容词

词源: From Yiddish קוועטשן (kvetsn), from Middle High German quetschen (“to squeeze, pinch, oppress”).
