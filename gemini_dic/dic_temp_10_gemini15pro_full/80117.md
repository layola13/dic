goblet:/ˈɡɒblət/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːblət/ (US)| n.|高脚杯，酒杯|A drinking vessel, especially one with a foot and stem, often used for wine.|He raised the jeweled goblet and proposed a toast. 他举起镶满宝石的高脚杯，提议敬酒。|近义词：cup, chalice, glass|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology:
From Old French *gobelet*, diminutive of *gobe*, *coupe*, cup, of Germanic origin.
