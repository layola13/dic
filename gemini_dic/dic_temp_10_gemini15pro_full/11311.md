ardours:/ˈɑːrdərz/
n.
热情，激情，热心
enthusiasm or passion
She pursued her artistic ardours with unwavering dedication. 她以坚定不移的奉献精神追求着她的艺术热情。
His ardours cooled as he grew older.  随着年龄的增长，他的热情冷却了下来。
synonyms: passions, fervors, zeal, eagerness
antonyms: apathy, indifference, coldness
noun
