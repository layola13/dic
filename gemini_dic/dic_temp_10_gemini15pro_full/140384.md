oillet:/ˈɔɪlɪt/ |n.|油壶；油孔；油杯|A small hole or tube through which oil is supplied to a machine or engine.  A small container for oil.|The oillet on the engine needs to be refilled.  发动机上的油孔需要重新加注油。|Synonyms: oil cup, lubricator|Antonyms: None|Noun
