cateran:/ˈkætərən/| n.|劫匪，强盗；(尤指苏格兰高地的)牛贼|A cateran is a robber or bandit, especially one who steals cattle (particularly in the Scottish Highlands).|例句：The caterans raided the village and stole all their livestock.  那些劫匪袭击了村庄，偷走了他们所有的牲畜。

例句：Stories of Highland caterans are common in Scottish folklore.  关于苏格兰高地强盗的故事在苏格兰民间传说中很常见。|近义词：bandit, robber, thief, outlaw, brigand|反义词:lawman, police|词性:noun
