nonabstractedly:/nɒnæbˈstræktɪdli/ | adv. | 非抽象地，具体地 | In a non-abstract manner; concretely. 词源:  "Non-" (not) + "abstract" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  The word "abstract" comes from the Latin "abstractus," past participle of "abstrahere" (to draw away).  Therefore, "nonabstractedly" means "not in a way that draws away from the concrete or specific."
