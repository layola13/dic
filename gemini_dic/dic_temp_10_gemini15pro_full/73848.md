formulas:/ˈfɔːrmjʊləz/ (more common in American English)
noun|公式; 配方|A mathematical relationship or rule expressed in symbols.|The chemical formula for water is H₂O. 水的化学式是H₂O。|synonyms: equation, rule, principle|antonyms: (No direct antonym for a formula exists, perhaps "exception" in some contexts)|n.
