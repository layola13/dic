burn-off:/ˈbɜːrn ɒf/ (US) /ˈbɜːn ɒf/ (UK)| n. |燃尽，烧除，清除|The controlled burning of something, especially waste material.|例句：The burn-off of excess gas was carefully monitored. 过量气体的燃烧受到了严密的监控。
