dowitcher:/ˈdaʊɪtʃər/| n. | 长嘴鹬，矶鹬 | A long-billed wading shorebird of the genus *Limnodromus*, having a mottled brownish back and long legs. It probes the mud for food. |  We saw a dowitcher probing the mudflats for invertebrates. 我们看到一只长嘴鹬在泥滩上探寻无脊椎动物。 |  Synonym: snipe, sandpiper | Antonym: (No direct antonym for a specific bird species.
