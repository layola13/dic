idleness:/ˈaɪdl̩nəs/
n.
懒惰，无所事事
The state of being idle; inactivity.
He spent his days in idleness. 他整天无所事事。
近义词：laziness, inactivity, sloth
反义词：industry, diligence, activity
词性：名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *idilnes*, from idil +‎ -nes.  Idil comes from Old English īdel "empty, void, vain, useless," from Proto-Germanic *idala- "empty."
