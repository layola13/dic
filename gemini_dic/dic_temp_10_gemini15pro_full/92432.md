hyponitrous:/ˌhaɪpəˈnaɪtrəs/| adj.|次硝酸的；亚硝酸的|Relating to or derived from hyponitrous acid (H2N2O2), an unstable acid that readily decomposes.|Hyponitrous acid is a weak acid. 次硝酸是一种弱酸。|Hyponitrous compounds are often unstable. 次硝酸盐化合物通常不稳定。|近义词:nitrous (in a broader sense)|反义词: (No direct chemical antonym)|词性:adjective
