antiphonal: /ænˈtɪfənəl/ | adj. | 对唱的，轮唱的 | Sung, recited, or played alternately by two groups, especially in church services or musical performances. | The choir sang an antiphonal psalm, with the two halves of the group taking turns.  唱诗班唱了一首对唱的赞美诗，两组轮流演唱。| The antiphonal chanting resonated through the cathedral. 对唱的圣歌在教堂里回荡。 |  Synonyms: responsive, alternating, reciprocal | Antonyms: unison, simultaneous | adjective


antiphony: /ænˈtɪfəni/ | n. | 对唱，轮唱 | Music or chanting performed alternately by two groups. | The antiphony between the two choirs created a beautiful, echoing effect.  两个唱诗班之间的对唱营造出一种美丽的回声效果。|  The service featured a moving antiphony of hymns.  礼拜仪式以一首感人的对唱圣歌为特色。 | Synonyms: response, alternation | Antonyms: unison, monotony | noun
