carburetant:/kɑːrbjʊˈrɛtənt/ /kɑːrbjʊˈrɛtənt/
n.|碳化剂; 燃料|A substance used as fuel, especially in internal combustion engines.|The carburetant used in this engine is a mixture of gasoline and ethanol.  这种发动机的燃料是汽油和乙醇的混合物。|fuel, gasoline, petrol| oxidant
词性|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *carburant*, present participle of *carburer* "to carburize," from *carbure* "carbide," from Latin *carbo* "coal, charcoal."
