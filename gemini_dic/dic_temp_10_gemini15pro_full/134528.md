nonlicentiates: /nɒnˈlaɪsənʃiɪts/ | n. | 未获得许可证者；未持有执照者 |  A person who does not hold a license or qualification for a particular profession or activity.  |  The conference was open to both licentiates and nonlicentiates in the field of engineering.  该会议对工程领域的持照者和未持照者都开放。 | Synonyms: unqualified individuals, unlicensed practitioners | Antonyms:  licentiates, qualified professionals | noun

Etymology: "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "licentiate" (from Latin "licentiatus," past participle of "licentiare," meaning "to permit").
