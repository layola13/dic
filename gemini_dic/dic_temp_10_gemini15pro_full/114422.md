make-the-fur-fly:/meɪk ðə fɜːr flaɪ/ | idiom | 使场面火爆，引起激烈争吵 | To cause a commotion, a heated argument, or a fight. |  The political debate really made the fur fly. 这场政治辩论真的让场面火爆起来。| 近义词: cause a stir, spark a controversy, raise a ruckus | 反义词: calm things down, smooth things over | idiom

make-the-fur-fly:/meɪk ðə fɜːr flaɪ/ | idiom | 使毛发飞扬 |  To cause animal fur or hair to be dislodged and fly around, often due to a fight or rough play. (Literal, less common)  | The two cats were wrestling and making the fur fly. 两只猫在扭打，毛发乱飞。 | 近义词: (in literal sense) shed, molt | 反义词: (in literal sense) none | idiom
