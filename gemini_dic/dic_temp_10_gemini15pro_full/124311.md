mothering-sunday:/ˈmʌðərɪŋ ˈsʌndeɪ/ |n.|母亲节（英国）|Mothering Sunday is a celebration held on the fourth Sunday in Lent, traditionally a day on which one visited one's mother church.|例句：I bought my mum flowers for Mothering Sunday. 我为母亲节给我妈妈买了花。

例句：Mothering Sunday is a special day for families in the UK. 母亲节是英国家庭的特殊日子。|近义词：Mother's Day|反义词: None|词性: noun
