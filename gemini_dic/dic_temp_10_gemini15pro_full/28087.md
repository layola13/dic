capillation:/ˌkæpɪˈleɪʃən/ | n. | 按人头收费，人头费 | A system of payment based on a fixed amount per patient or client, regardless of the services provided. |  Example 1: Capitation payments are common in managed care organizations. 按人头收费在管理式医疗机构中很常见。

Example 2: The doctor receives a capitation fee for each patient enrolled in the health plan. 这位医生为每个参加健康计划的病人收取人头费。 | Synonyms: per capita payment, head fee | Antonyms: fee-for-service | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *capillatio, from Latin capillus (“hair”) + -ation.  Originally referred to a poll tax levied "by the head".
