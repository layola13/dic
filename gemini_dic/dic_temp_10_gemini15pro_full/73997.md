fortuity:/fɔrˈtuːɪti/ /fɔrˈtʃuːɪti/
n.
偶然事件；意外；偶然性
the state of being fortuitous; chance or luck
例句：By sheer fortuity, I ran into my old friend at the airport.  纯粹出于偶然，我在机场遇到了我的老朋友。

例句：The discovery was a happy fortuity. 这个发现是一个幸运的偶然。

近义词：chance, accident, coincidence, serendipity
反义词：intention, plan, design, purpose
词性: 名词
