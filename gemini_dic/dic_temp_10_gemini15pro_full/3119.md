aeruginous:/ˌɛrʊˈdʒɪnəs/| adj.|铜绿色的；绿青色的|Resembling the color of verdigris (a green or bluish deposit on copper, brass, or bronze).|例句：The old statue had an aeruginous patina.  这尊古老的雕像有一层铜绿色的光泽。

例句：The pond water had an aeruginous tinge, suggesting the presence of copper. 池水略带铜绿色，表明其中含有铜。|近义词：verdigris, bluish-green, greenish-blue|反义词: (No direct antonym for a color, could use terms like bright, clear, etc. depending on context)|词性: adjective
