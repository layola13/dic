brocken:/ˈbrɒkən/ (UK), /ˈbroʊkən/ (US)| adj.|破碎的，残缺的；被打败的；不完整的；（土地）未开垦的|Broken describes something that is separated into pieces or no longer functioning correctly. brocken:/ˈbrɒkən/ (UK), /ˈbroʊkən/ (US)| n.| (literary)  rough terrain; a mountain or hill|Brocken refers to a type of rough or mountainous terrain, often associated with a specific mountain in Germany called the Brocken.|The hikers struggled across the brocken.  徒步旅行者艰难地穿过崎岖的山路。|synonyms:  mountain, hill, upland, moorland|antonyms: valley, plain, lowland|noun
