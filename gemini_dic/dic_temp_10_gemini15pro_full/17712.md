bedchair: /ˈbɛdtʃɛː(r)/ | n. | 床椅，折叠床 | A piece of furniture that can be used as both a bed and a chair, typically folding out from a chair-like form. |  I bought a bedchair for guests to sleep on. 我买了一张床椅供客人睡觉。|  The bedchair is handy for small apartments. 这张床椅对于小公寓来说很方便。|Synonyms:  sofa bed, futon, convertible sofa, pull-out sofa, daybed | Antonyms:  chair, bed | Noun
