disaffiliation's:/dɪsˌəˌfɪliˈeɪʃənz/
noun
脱离；不隶属；断绝关系
The act of formally withdrawing from an organization, group, or affiliation.
The union voted for disaffiliation from the national organization. 工会投票决定脱离国家组织。
近义词:separation, detachment, divorce, severance
反义词:affiliation, association, connection, union
词性:名词


Etymology:
From dis- (expressing reversal) + affiliation.  Ultimately from Latin *affiliare, from ad- "to" + filius "son".
