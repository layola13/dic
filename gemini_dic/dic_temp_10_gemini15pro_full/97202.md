inflammableness:/ɪnˌflæməbəlnəs/| n. |易燃性，可燃性|The quality of being easily set on fire.  The inflammableness of the material made it dangerous to store near heat sources.|例句：The inflammableness of dry leaves makes them a fire hazard. 干树叶的易燃性使它们成为火灾隐患。

例句：The inflammableness of the gas was a major concern for the engineers.  这种气体的易燃性是工程师们主要关心的问题。|近义词：combustibility, flammability|反义词：nonflammability, fire resistance|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *inflammablenesse, derived from inflammable + -ness.  "Inflammable" itself comes from Late Latin inflammabilis, from Latin inflammare "to set on fire," from in- "in" + flamma "flame."
