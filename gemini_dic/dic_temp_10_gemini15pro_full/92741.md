ichthyocolla:/ˌɪkθiəˈkɒlə/ | n. | 鱼胶，鱼鳔胶 | A form of isinglass or gelatin obtained from the dried swim bladders of certain fish, especially sturgeons.  It's used as a clarifying agent, in adhesives, and historically in medicine. |  The winemaker used ichthyocolla to clarify the wine.  酿酒师用鱼胶来澄清葡萄酒。 | Synonyms: isinglass, gelatin, collagen | Antonyms: None | Noun
