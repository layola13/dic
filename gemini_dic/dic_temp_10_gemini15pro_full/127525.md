necrologist: /nəˈkrɒlədʒɪst/ /nɛˈkrɑːlədʒɪst/
n.
讣告作者；撰写讣告的人
A person who writes obituaries, especially as a profession.
The necrologist wrote a moving tribute to the late actor.  讣告作者为已故演员写了一篇感人的悼词。
Synonyms: obituarist, obituary writer
Antonyms:  None (This word doesn't have a direct opposite.)
词性: noun
