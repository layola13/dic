devalues:/diːˈvæljuːz/
verb
贬值；降低…的价值；贬低
To reduce the value or worth of something.
The new currency devalues the savings of the people. 新货币使人们的储蓄贬值。
synonyms:depreciates, diminishes, cheapens, undermines
antonyms:appreciates, enhances, increases, values
词性:动词
