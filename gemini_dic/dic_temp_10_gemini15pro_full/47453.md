deal-breaker:/ˈdiːlˌbreɪkər/| n. | 阻碍交易的因素；致命的缺点；无法妥协的关键点 | Something that is considered unacceptable in a negotiation or relationship and would cause it to end. |  His refusal to relocate was the deal-breaker in the job offer. 他拒绝搬迁是这份工作机会的致命因素。

| Example:  Her smoking habit was a deal-breaker for him.  她吸烟的习惯对他来说是无法接受的。


| Synonym: sticking point, obstacle, impediment, non-starter, no-go | Antonym:  advantage, benefit, plus, selling point | noun
