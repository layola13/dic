lead-time:/ˈliːd taɪm/ | n. |  交付周期;准备时间;提前期 | The time between initiating a process and its completion.  | The lead time for this project is six months. 这个项目的交付周期是六个月。| synonyms: turnaround time, waiting time, processing time | antonyms:  delay, lag time | noun


lead-time:/ˈliːd taɪm/ | n. | 前置时间;提前量 | The time allowed or required between the initiation of an activity and a specified point in its completion, such as delivery of materials. | We need a two-week lead time to process your order. 我们需要两周的前置时间来处理您的订单。| synonyms: preparation time, advance notice | antonyms: last minute | noun
