looming:/ˈluːmɪŋ/ (US), /ˈluːmɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.| 隐约地出现，逼近的，即将发生的|appearing large and ominous; about to happen, especially in a threatening way|例句：Dark clouds loomed on the horizon.  乌云在地平线上隐约出现。

例句：The looming deadline added to the pressure. 逼近的截止日期增加了压力。

近义词：impending, approaching, threatening, menacing|反义词：receding, diminishing, disappearing|词性:形容词
