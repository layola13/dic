embosser: /ɪmˈbɒsər/ | n. | 压印机；压花机；浮雕工人 |  A person or machine that creates raised designs or lettering on a surface, such as paper, leather, or metal. |  He used an embosser to personalize his business cards. 他用压印机将自己的名片个性化。|  Near synonyms:  stamper, imprinter, die cutter | Antonyms: flattener | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French embosser, from en- (“in, on”) + bosser (“to strike, to emboss”), from Old French boce (“swelling, lump”).
