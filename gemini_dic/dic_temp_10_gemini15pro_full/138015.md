noon:/nuːn/
n.
中午，正午
midday; twelve o'clock in the daytime.
The wedding ceremony will be held at noon. 婚礼将在中午举行。
近义词:midday, twelve o'clock
反义词:midnight
词性:名词

词源: From Old English *nōn "the ninth hour (3 p.m.)," later "midday," from Latin nona (hora) "(the) ninth (hour)," from nonus "ninth." The shift in meaning from 3 p.m. to midday occurred c. 1300.
