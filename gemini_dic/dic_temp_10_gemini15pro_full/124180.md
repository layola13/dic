mosaicing:/ˌmoʊˈzeɪɪkɪŋ/ |n. |镶嵌; 镶嵌工艺; 镶嵌图案; 马赛克 |The process of creating a mosaic, or a mosaic itself. |The mosaicing on the floor was intricate and beautiful. 地板上的马赛克图案精美绝伦。|Synonyms: tessellation, inlay, |Antonyms: fragmentation, separation |noun

mosaicing:/ˌmoʊˈzeɪɪkɪŋ/ |adj. |镶嵌的 |Relating to or resembling a mosaic. |The mosaicing effect of the different colored tiles was stunning. 不同颜色瓷砖的镶嵌效果令人惊叹。|Synonyms: tessellated, inlaid |Antonyms: fragmented, separated |adjective
