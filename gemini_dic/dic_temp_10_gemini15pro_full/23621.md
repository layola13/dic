branchiest:/ˈbræntʃiɪst/
adjective
最多枝杈的；分支最多的
Having the most branches.
The branchiest tree in the forest provided ample shade for the weary travelers.  森林里枝杈最多的树为疲惫的旅行者提供了充足的阴凉。
近义词: bushiest, most ramified
反义词: least branched, sparsest
词性: adjective
词源: From branch + -y + -est (superlative suffix). Ultimately from Vulgar Latin *branca, of uncertain origin.
