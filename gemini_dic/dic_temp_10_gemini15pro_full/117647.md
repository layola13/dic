megawatt:/ˈmɛɡəwɒt/ (US) /ˈmɛɡəwɑːt/ (UK)| n. |兆瓦|A unit of power equal to one million watts.|The power plant generates 1000 megawatts of electricity.  该发电厂发电量为1000兆瓦。|近义词:gigawatt |反义词:microwatt, milliwatt |词性:名词

Etymology:  From mega- + watt.  "Mega-" comes from Greek μέγας (megas) meaning "great". "Watt" is named after James Watt, a Scottish inventor.
