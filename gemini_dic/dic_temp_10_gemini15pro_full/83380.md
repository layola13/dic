gyrostatics:/ˌdʒaɪərəˈstætɪks/ | n. | 陀螺静力学 | The branch of mechanics that deals with rotating bodies and their tendency to maintain their orientation.  |  Example: The principles of gyrostatics are crucial for understanding the stability of spinning tops and gyroscopes.  陀螺静力学的原理对于理解旋转陀螺和陀螺仪的稳定性至关重要。 | Example:  He studied gyrostatics to understand the behavior of the spacecraft. 他学习陀螺静力学以了解航天器的行为。 | Synonym:  rotational dynamics, gyroscopic theory | Antonym: N/A | noun
