harmonist:/ˈhɑːrmənɪst/ | n. | 和声歌手；合唱队员 | A singer in a chorus or harmony group.  |  The harmonist blended her voice seamlessly with the other singers.  这位和声歌手的声音与其他歌手完美融合。| Synonyms:  chorister, singer, vocalist | Antonyms: soloist | noun
