catachresis: /ˌkætəˈkriːsɪs/
n.
滥用，比喻不当
The use of a word in a way that is not correct, for example using the wrong word or using a word with the wrong meaning.
例句：The poet's catachresis of "blind mouths" created a striking image.  诗人对“盲目之口”的滥用创造了一个引人注目的意象。

例句：Using "literally" to mean "figuratively" is a common catachresis these days.  如今，用“literally”表示“figuratively”是一种常见的滥用。

近义词：misuse, misapplication, malapropism, solecism
反义词: correct usage, proper usage
词性: noun
