deeming:/ˈdiːmɪŋ/
verb
认为；视为
Considering or judging something to be true or appropriate.
The government deemed the building unsafe. 政府认为该建筑不安全。
He deemed it necessary to apologize. 他认为有必要道歉。
synonyms: considering, regarding, judging, believing, holding
antonyms: disregarding, ignoring, overlooking
