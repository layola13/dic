nonservile:/nɒnˈsəːvaɪl/
adjective
非奴隶的，不卑躬屈膝的，不屈从的
Not servile; not behaving like a slave; not showing excessive subservience.
He maintained a nonservile attitude even when facing the powerful CEO.  他即使面对强大的首席执行官也保持着不卑不亢的态度。
The ambassador delivered a nonservile speech, defending his country's interests.  大使发表了不卑不亢的讲话，捍卫了国家的利益。

近义词: independent, autonomous, self-reliant, assertive
反义词: servile, subservient, obsequious, submissive
词性: adjective
