chased:/tʃeɪst/| v. |追逐，追赶|The police chased the thief through the streets. 警察在街上追赶小偷。|例句：The dog chased the ball across the park. 狗追着球穿过公园。|近义词：pursue, follow, hunt|反义词：flee, escape|动词

词源: From Middle English *chasen*, from Old French *chacier* (to chase, hunt), from Vulgar Latin *captiāre* (to try to seize), from Latin *captāre* (frequentative of *capere* to take, seize).

PHRASE->
chase after: 追逐  He's chasing after a promotion. 他在追求晋升。
chase down: 追捕，追查  The police chased down the suspect. 警察追捕了嫌疑犯。
chase away: 赶走 The dog chased away the birds. 狗把鸟赶走了。
chase up:  跟进，催促 I need to chase up the client about the payment. 我需要跟进客户付款事宜。
give chase:  开始追逐  When the thief ran, the police gave chase. 当小偷逃跑时，警察开始追捕。
