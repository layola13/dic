friablenesses:/ˌfraɪəbəlˈnɛsɪz/
n.
易碎性；脆性；脆弱性
The quality of being easily crumbled or broken.
The friablenesses of the ancient manuscript made it difficult to handle.  这古老手稿的脆弱性使得它难以处理。
The soil's friablenesses allowed for easy planting.  土壤的疏松使得很容易种植。
Synonym: fragility, crumbliness, brittleness
Antonym: strength, toughness, resilience

friable:/ˈfraɪəbl/
adj.
易碎的；脆的
Easily crumbled or broken.
The friable rock crumbled in my hand.  这易碎的岩石在我手中碎裂了。
The cake was too friable and fell apart when I tried to frost it.  蛋糕太酥了，我试图给它加糖霜时，它碎了。
Synonym: crumbly, brittle, fragile
Antonym: strong, tough, resilient
adjective

词源：
From Late Latin *friabilis, from Latin friāre (“to rub, crumble”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰreyH- (“to rub, grind”).
