coadjutrix:/ˌkoʊəˈdʒuːtrɪks/
n.
女助手，女性协理
a female assistant; a woman who assists a person in a position of authority, especially a bishop.
She served as the bishop's coadjutrix for many years. 她多年来一直担任主教的助理。
近义词:assistant, helper, aide
反义词:opponent, adversary
词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *coadjutrix*, feminine of *coadjutor*, from Latin *coadiutor*, from *coadiuvare* "to assist," from *com-* "together" + *adiuvare* "to help."
