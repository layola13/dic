carcassing:/ˈkɑːrkəsɪŋ/ | n. |屠宰，去骨，（尤指鸟类的）剥皮|The process of butchering an animal and removing its internal organs, especially in preparation for consumption; also, the removal of feathers or skin, particularly from birds.| Example: The carcassing of the chickens was done quickly and efficiently.  鸡的屠宰工作快速高效地完成了。

Example:  The carcassing of the pheasant was a messy job. 剥野鸡的皮是一项脏活。| 近义词: butchering, dressing, slaughtering | 反义词:  None (no direct opposite for the process itself) |词性: 名词
