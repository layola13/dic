hit-the-road:/ˌhɪt ðə ˈroʊd/| idiom|出发，上路，启程|To begin a journey, especially by car.|例句：We packed our bags and hit the road early in the morning. 我们打包好行李，一大早就上路了。|近义词：depart, leave, set off, get going|反义词：arrive, stay, remain|phrase

hit-the-road:/ˌhɪt ðə ˈroʊd/| idiom|离开，滚蛋（较粗鲁）|To leave, often abruptly or angrily.|例句：He told me to hit the road, so I left. 他叫我滚蛋，所以我离开了。|近义词：get lost, scram, clear out|反义词：stay, welcome|phrase
