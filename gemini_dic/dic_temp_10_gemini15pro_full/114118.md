maguari:/ˌmæɡwəˈriː/ | n. |Maguari Stork, 马瓜里鹳 |A large South American stork (Ciconia maguari) with predominantly white plumage, black flight feathers, and a reddish facial patch. It inhabits wetlands and feeds on fish, amphibians, and other aquatic animals. |例句：The maguari stalked patiently through the shallows, searching for prey.  马瓜里鹳耐心地在浅滩中搜寻猎物。

例句：We observed a maguari feeding on a large frog in the marsh. 我们观察到一只马瓜里鹳在沼泽地里捕食一只大青蛙。|近义词：stork, jabiru |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
