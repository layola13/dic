gamifies: /ˈɡeɪmɪfaɪz/
verb
游戏化
Applies game-design elements and game principles in non-game contexts to engage users and solve problems.
The company gamifies its employee training program to make it more engaging.  公司将游戏元素融入员工培训项目，使其更具吸引力。
Duolingo gamifies language learning, making it fun and addictive. 多邻国将语言学习游戏化，使其变得有趣且容易上瘾。
近义词:  makes a game of, ludifies, incentivizes
反义词: makes tedious, makes boring
