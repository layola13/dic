interfulgent:/ˌɪntərˈfʌldʒənt/
adjective
闪耀的，灿烂的
Shining brightly between; gleaming intermittently.  Characterized by intermittent flashes of brilliance.
The interfulgent lightning illuminated the dark sky.  间歇闪耀的闪电照亮了黑暗的天空。
近义词：bright, radiant, luminous, gleaming, shimmering
反义词：dull, dim, dark, obscure
