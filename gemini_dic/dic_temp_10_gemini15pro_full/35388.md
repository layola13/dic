co-operation:/kəʊˌɒpəˈreɪʃn/ (UK) /koʊˌɑːpəˈreɪʃn/ (US)| n.|合作，协作|The project requires co-operation between several departments.|这个项目需要几个部门之间的合作。|
The success of the venture depends on co-operation between the two companies. |这项事业的成功取决于两家公司之间的合作。|
近义词：collaboration, teamwork, partnership|反义词：opposition, antagonism|词性:名词

co-operate:/kəʊˈɒpəreɪt/ (UK) /koʊˈɑːpəreɪt/ (US)| v. |合作，协作|The two companies agreed to co-operate on the project.|两家公司同意在这个项目上合作。|
We need to co-operate with each other to achieve our goals. |我们需要互相合作以达成目标。|
近义词：collaborate, work together|反义词：obstruct, hinder|词性:动词

词源: Late Middle English: from Latin cooperatio(n-), from cooperari ‘work together’, from co- ‘together’ + operari ‘work’.
