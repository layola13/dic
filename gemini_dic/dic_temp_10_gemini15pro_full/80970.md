gracility:/ɡrəˈsɪləti/| n. |优雅，优美|Slender and graceful, especially in movement or form. |Her gracility on the dance floor captivated everyone. 她在舞池中的优雅迷住了所有人。|The swan moved with effortless gracility across the lake. 天鹅以毫不费力的优雅姿态游过湖面。|近义词：elegance, grace, refinement, delicacy|反义词：clumsiness, awkwardness, gaucheness|词性: noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *gracilitas, from Latin gracilis (“slender, slim”).
