detractress:/dɪˈtræktərɪs/
noun
诽谤者（女性）; 诋毁者（女性）
a woman who detracts, especially from the reputation of another; a female detractor.
Her political rivals were quick to seize on the scandal, with one particularly vocal detractress labeling her a "disgrace to the office." 她的政敌很快就抓住了这个丑闻，一个特别高调的诽谤者给她贴上了“给办公室蒙羞”的标签。
The book was praised by most critics, but one notable detractress called it "derivative and uninspired." 这本书受到了大多数评论家的赞扬，但一位著名的诋毁者称它“缺乏创意且毫无灵感”。
近义词: critic, slanderer, backbiter, defamer
反义词: supporter, admirer, advocate, champion
词性: noun

detract:/dɪˈtrækt/
verb
贬低; 减损; 降低
reduce or take away from the worth or value of.
The noisy traffic detracted from the peacefulness of the park.  喧闹的交通降低了公园的宁静。
These minor flaws do not detract from the overall quality of the product. 这些小瑕疵不会影响产品的整体质量。
近义词: diminish, lessen, reduce, impair
反义词: enhance, improve, augment, increase
词性: verb
