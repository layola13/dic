illegally:/ɪˈliːɡəli/
adv.
非法地，违法地
in a way that is against the law
He entered the country illegally. 他非法入境。
Synonyms: unlawfully, illicitly, wrongly
Antonyms: legally, lawfully, rightfully
词性: 副词
