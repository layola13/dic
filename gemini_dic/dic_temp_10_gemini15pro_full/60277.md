egest:/ɪˈdʒɛst/ | v. | 排泄；排出（废物）| To discharge or excrete from the body, especially undigested food as feces. | The owl egests indigestible parts of its prey in pellets. 猫头鹰将猎物的不可消化部分以团块的形式排出。 |  The caterpillar egested a small, dark pellet. 毛毛虫排出了一个小的黑色颗粒。 | synonyms: excrete, defecate, void, eliminate | antonyms: ingest, consume, absorb | verb
