abattoir:/ˈæbətwɑːr/ (US) /ˈæbətɔːr/ (UK)| n. |屠宰场|A place where animals are slaughtered for food.|The meat comes from a local abattoir. 这肉来自当地一家屠宰场。|近义词：slaughterhouse|反义词: None|词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *abatre, to beat down, fell (from Latin *battuere, to beat). The original meaning was a place where animals were knocked down before being slaughtered.

USAGE->
The abattoir processes thousands of animals each week.  这家屠宰场每周处理数千只动物。

The documentary exposed the unsanitary conditions in the abattoir.  这部纪录片揭露了屠宰场里不卫生的环境。

modern abattoir = 现代屠宰场
illegal abattoir = 非法屠宰场
mobile abattoir = 移动屠宰场
abattoir waste = 屠宰场废物
