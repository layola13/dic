discounters:/ˈdɪskaʊntərz/
n.
折扣店；廉价商店
Retailers who sell goods at prices lower than those typically charged.
Many discounters are opening new stores to compete with established retailers. 许多折扣店正在开设新店，与老牌零售商竞争。
Walmart and Target are two of the largest discounters in the United States.  沃尔玛和塔吉特是美国最大的两家折扣店。
synonyms: bargain stores, cheap shops, budget retailers, off-price retailers
antonyms:  luxury retailers, high-end stores, upscale boutiques
