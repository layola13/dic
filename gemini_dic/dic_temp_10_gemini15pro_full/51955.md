diachronically:/ˌdaɪəˈkrɒnɪkli/ | adv. | 跨时间地，历时地 |  In a way that considers development over a period of time, as opposed to at a single point in time (synchronically). |  The evolution of language can be studied diachronically, tracing changes in pronunciation and meaning over centuries. 语言的演变可以历时地研究，追溯几个世纪以来发音和意义的变化。| 近义词: historically, over time | 反义词: synchronically | 词性: 副词
