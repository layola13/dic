cosmologer:/kɒzˈmɒlədʒər/ |/kɑːzˈmɑːlədʒər/| n. |宇宙学家|A scientist who studies the origin, evolution, and structure of the universe.|例句：The cosmologer presented her theory about the Big Bang.  这位宇宙学家提出了她关于宇宙大爆炸的理论。

例句：Cosmologers are still trying to understand dark matter. 宇宙学家们仍在试图理解暗物质。|近义词：astronomer, astrophysicist|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


Etymology:  From  "cosmo-" + "-logy" + "-er". "Cosmo-" comes from the Greek "kosmos" meaning "order, world, universe". "-logy" comes from the Greek "-logia"  meaning "study of". "-er" denotes a person who performs an action or has a specific occupation.
