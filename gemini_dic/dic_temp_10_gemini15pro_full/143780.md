outmouthed:/ˌaʊtˈmaʊðd/
adjective
大声疾呼的，直言不讳的
Speaking or expressing oneself freely and openly, often in a loud or bold manner.
He was outmouthed in his criticism of the government. 他直言不讳地批评了政府。
outspoken, frank, candid, forthright
reticent, reserved, inhibited, taciturn
