pasturer:/ˈpæstʃərər/| n.|牧场主，牧人|A person who owns or manages livestock on pasture land.  Someone who grazes animals on pasture. |The pasturer rotated his cattle through different fields. 牧场主轮流将他的牛放到不同的田里吃草。|Synonym: herdsman, rancher, stockman, grazier|Antonym: farmer (not always a direct antonym, but often implies cultivation of crops rather than grazing)|noun

pasture:/ˈpæstʃər/| n. v.|牧场；牧草；放牧|n. Land covered with grass and other low plants suitable for grazing animals. v. To put animals in a pasture to graze.|The cows were put out to pasture. 牛被放出去吃草。 The farmer pastured his sheep on the hillside. 农夫在山坡上放牧他的羊。|Synonym: grassland, meadow, range, field (n.); graze (v.)|Antonym: barren land (n.)|noun, verb
