nondepository:/ˌnɑːndɪˈpɑːzɪtɔːri/
adjective
非存款的
Not accepting or holding deposits of money, especially referring to financial institutions that do not accept traditional deposits like checking or savings accounts.
Nondepository institutions, such as insurance companies and investment firms, play a vital role in the financial system.  非存款机构，例如保险公司和投资公司，在金融体系中扮演着至关重要的角色。
These nondepository financial institutions provide essential services to businesses and individuals. 这些非存款金融机构为企业和个人提供必要的服务。
nonbank, nondeposit-taking
depository

词性: adjective
