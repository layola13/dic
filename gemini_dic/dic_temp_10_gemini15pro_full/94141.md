impeding:/ɪmˈpiːdɪŋ/
adjective
阻碍的，妨碍的
delaying or preventing (someone or something) by obstructing them; hindering.
The heavy traffic was impeding our progress.  交通拥堵阻碍了我们的前进。
近义词: hindering, obstructing, hampering, blocking, thwarting
反义词: facilitating, aiding, assisting, helping, expediting
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin impedire "to shackle the feet of," from in- "in, into" + pes (genitive pedis) "foot."
