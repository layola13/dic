catastrophize: /kəˈtæstrəfaɪz/ | v. | 使灾难化；小题大做；夸大其词 | To imagine or describe something as considerably worse than it actually is; to exaggerate the negative aspects of a situation. |  She tends to catastrophize every minor setback, turning a small problem into a major crisis. 她总是把每一个小挫折都灾难化，把一个小问题变成一个大危机。

He catastrophized the situation, convinced he would lose his job and end up homeless.  他把情况灾难化了，确信他会丢掉工作，最终无家可归。

| 近义词: exaggerate, overreact, dramatize, magnify | 反义词: downplay, minimize, understate | 词性: verb (动词)
