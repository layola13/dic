cowbane:/ˈkaʊbeɪn/ | n. |毒芹，水毒芹|A highly poisonous plant of the parsley family, also known as water hemlock.  It grows in wet places and is considered one of the most toxic plants in North America. |The cowbane near the river is a serious danger to livestock. 河边的毒芹对牲畜是严重的威胁。| 近义词: water hemlock, poison hemlock | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific plant, could consider general terms like "harmless plant", "edible plant") | 词性: noun
