nonmanufactures:/ˌnɒnˌmænjuˈfæktʃəz/
| n. |非制成品；非制造业产品|Goods that are not manufactured; products of non-manufacturing industries.  These often include agricultural products, minerals, and services.|例句：The country's economy relies heavily on nonmanufactures, such as agricultural exports and tourism. 这个国家的经济严重依赖非制成品，例如农业出口和旅游业。

例句：The trade deficit was offset by a surplus in nonmanufactures.  贸易逆差被非制成品的顺差所抵消。|近义词：commodities, raw materials, services|反义词:manufactures, manufactured goods|词性:Noun (plural)
