llc:/ˌɛl ɛl ˈsiː/ | n. | 有限责任公司 | Limited Liability Company, a business structure in the United States that protects its owners from personal responsibility for its debts or liabilities. | Example: My friend started an LLC to sell handmade jewelry. 我的朋友开了一家有限责任公司来销售手工珠宝。| Synonym: corporation, company | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps sole proprietorship or partnership) | noun
