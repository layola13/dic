delectable:/dɪˈlɛktəbl/ | adj. | 美味的；令人愉悦的 | Extremely pleasing to the senses, especially to the taste; delicious. | The restaurant served a delectable chocolate mousse. 这家餐厅供应美味的巧克力慕斯。 |  Synonyms: delicious, delightful, luscious, savory, palatable, exquisite | Antonyms: unappetizing, disgusting, repulsive, unpleasant, distasteful | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *delectible, *delectable, from Late Latin dēlectabilis (“delightful”), from Latin dēlectō (“I delight, charm, allure”) + -bilis.
