effendi:/ɪˈfɛndi/| n. |先生，阁下 (对男性的尊称)|A title of respect or courtesy in Turkey and some other parts of the Middle East, formerly used after the name of a government official or educated man.|Example: The effendi greeted his guests warmly.  这位先生热情地迎接了他的客人。|Example:  The letter was addressed to "Ahmed Bey Effendi". 这封信是寄给“艾哈迈德·贝伊先生”的。|Synonym: sir, mister, gentleman|Antonym: N/A (no direct antonym for a title of respect)|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Turkish *efendi*, from Modern Greek *αφέντης* (aféntis, “master, lord”), from Byzantine Greek *αὐθέντης* (authéntēs, “master”), from *αὐτός* (autós, “self”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\sew-*.
