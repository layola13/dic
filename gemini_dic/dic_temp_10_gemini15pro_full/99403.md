interdisciplinary:/ˌɪntərˈdɪsəplɪneri/| adj.|跨学科的，交叉学科的|Involving two or more academic disciplines.|例句：The university offers an interdisciplinary program in environmental studies. 这所大学提供环境研究的跨学科课程。

例句：Interdisciplinary research is becoming increasingly important in solving complex problems. 跨学科研究在解决复杂问题方面变得越来越重要。|近义词：cross-disciplinary, multidisciplinary|反义词：specialized, disciplinary|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- (between) + disciplinary (relating to a particular field of study).  The prefix "inter-" comes from Latin, meaning "between, among, in the midst of." "Disciplinary" comes from the Latin word "disciplina" meaning "instruction, knowledge".
