nonghetto:/nɒnˈɡɛtəʊ/ (US) /nɒnˈɡɛtəʊ/ (UK)| adj.|非贫民区的；非少数民族聚居区的|Not relating to or characteristic of a ghetto, especially not impoverished or disadvantaged.|例句：The nonghetto areas of the city are generally safer. 城市的非贫民区通常更安全。|例句：They moved to a nonghetto neighborhood with better schools. 他们搬到了一个有更好学校的非贫民区。|近义词：affluent, prosperous, upscale|反义词：ghetto, impoverished, disadvantaged|词性:形容词

词源:  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "ghetto" refers to a part of a city, especially a slum area, occupied by a minority group or groups.  Therefore, "nonghetto" signifies "not a ghetto."
