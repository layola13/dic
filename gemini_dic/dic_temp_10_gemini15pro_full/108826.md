leavening:/ˈlɛvənɪŋ/ | n. | 发酵；发酵剂；使发酵 |The process of making something rise, especially bread, by adding a substance such as yeast or baking powder. | The leavening in the bread wasn't working properly, so it didn't rise.  面包里的发酵剂没有正常工作，所以它没有发起来。| synonyms: fermentation, raising, expanding | antonyms: deflation, shrinking | noun

leaven:/ˈlɛvən/ | n. vt. | 酵母；使发酵；影响 |A substance, typically yeast, that is added to dough to make it ferment and rise. To permeate and transform. |  The baker added leaven to the dough. 面包师在面团里加了酵母。 | synonyms: yeast, ferment, catalyst | antonyms: inhibitor | noun, verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *levine, from lever "to raise," from Latin levare "to lift, lighten, raise," related to levis "light."
