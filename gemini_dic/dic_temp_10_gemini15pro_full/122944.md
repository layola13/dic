momentousness:/moʊˈmɛntəsnəs/| n.|重大，重要性|The momentousness of the occasion was not lost on anyone. 没有人没有意识到这个时刻的重要性。|The signing of the peace treaty was an event of great momentousness. 和平条约的签署是一件具有重大意义的事件。|近义词：importance, significance, weight, gravity|反义词：insignificance, triviality|词性:noun

Etymology:
From momentous + -ness.  Momentous itself comes from Late Latin *momentōsus, from Latin momentum "movement, moving power; importance, weight, influence" + -osus "-ous."
