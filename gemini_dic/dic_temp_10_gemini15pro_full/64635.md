erythrism:/ˈɛrɪθrɪzəm/
noun
赤化，红化
Abnormal redness in animals caused by overproduction of red pigments.
Erythrism is a rare pigmentation condition. 赤化是一种罕见的色素沉着病症。
The fox's erythrism made it stand out in the forest. 狐狸的赤化使它在森林中格外显眼。
近义词:rufus coloration, red pigmentation
反义词:albinism, melanism
词性:n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *erythrós* (red) + *-ismos* (forming nouns of condition).
