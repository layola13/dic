chicaner:/ʃɪˈkeɪnər/| n.|诡辩者，刁难者|Someone who uses tricks, especially legal or political ones, to achieve something.|The lawyer was a master chicaner, using every loophole to delay the trial. 这位律师是个刁难高手，利用每一个漏洞来拖延审判。|Synonyms: trickster, deceiver, manipulator|Antonyms: honest person, straightforward person|noun
chicanery:/ʃɪˈkeɪnəri/| n.|诡辩，欺骗|The use of tricks to deceive someone (usually in legal, political or financial matters).|The company resorted to chicanery to avoid paying taxes.  这家公司 resorted to chicanery to avoid paying taxes.|Synonyms: trickery, deception, subterfuge|Antonyms: honesty, forthrightness|noun
