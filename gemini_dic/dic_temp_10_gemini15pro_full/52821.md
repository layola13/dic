diluteness:/daɪˈluːtnəs/
n.|稀释度，淡薄，稀薄|The diluteness of the solution made it ineffective.  溶液太稀了，所以失效了。|近义词：thinness, weakness, rarity|反义词：concentration, strength, thickness|词性:名词
Etymology: From dilute + -ness.  Dilute comes from the Latin "dilutus," the past participle of "diluere," meaning "to wash away" or "to dissolve."
例句：The diluteness of the coffee made it taste watery.  咖啡太淡了，喝起来像水一样。
例句：The diluteness of the evidence meant the case was dismissed.  证据不足，案件被驳回了。
