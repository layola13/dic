pare-down:/pɛərˈdaʊn/
|adj.|削减的，精简的|Reduced to its essential elements; simplified.|The pared-down version of the software is much easier to use.  精简版的软件更容易使用。|reduced, simplified, minimalist|elaborate, complex, detailed|adjective
