overblame:/ˌoʊvərˈbleɪm/ | vt. | 过分责备，过度指责 | To blame someone excessively or unfairly for something. |  She tended to overblame herself for the problems in their relationship. 她倾向于过度责备自己在他们关系中存在的问题。

| Example: He overblamed the driver for the accident, even though the icy roads were a major factor.  他过度责备了事故中的司机，即使结冰的路面是一个主要因素。 | Synonym: overcriticize, overcondemn | Antonym: understate, excuse, condone | Verb
