imagine:/ɪˈmædʒɪn/ | v. | 想象，设想 | To form a mental image or concept of something. | I can't imagine life without you. 我无法想象没有你的生活。

| Example:  She imagined herself winning the lottery. 她想象自己中了彩票。| Synonym:  envision, visualize, conceive, fancy | Antonym:  disbelieve, doubt | verb
