cordoning:/ˈkɔːrdənɪŋ/
n.
封锁；隔离；警戒线
The act of enclosing an area with a cordon, often to prevent access or to mark off a dangerous or contaminated zone.
Police are cordoning off the area. 警察正在封锁该地区。
The area was cordoned off after the explosion. 爆炸发生后，该地区被封锁了。
closure, barrier, blockade
opening, access
noun
