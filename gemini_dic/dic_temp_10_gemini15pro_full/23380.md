brachiopodous:/ˌbrækiˈɒpədəs/
adjective
有腕足的，腕足动物的
Relating to or belonging to the brachiopods, a phylum of marine invertebrate animals.
Brachiopodous shells are common fossils in Paleozoic rocks. 腕足动物的贝壳是古生代岩石中常见的化石。
Synonyms: brachiopod, brachiopodan
Antonyms: None
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *brachiopoda*, from Ancient Greek βραχίων (brakhíōn, “arm”) + πούς (poús, “foot”) + -ous (English adjectival suffix).
