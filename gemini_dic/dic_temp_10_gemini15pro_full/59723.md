eclipsers:/ɪˈklɪpsərz/| n.|日食/月食观察者；使黯然失色的人或物|People who observe eclipses or things/people that outshine others.|例句：The eclipsers gathered on the hilltop to witness the rare celestial event.  日食/月食观察者聚集在山顶，见证这罕见的天文事件。

例句：The new technology eclipsed all its predecessors, making them obsolete. 这项新技术使所有以前的版本都黯然失色，使它们过时了。|近义词：observers, viewers (for people observing eclipses); overshadowers, surpassers (for things that outshine others)|反义词: None (for observers);  predecessors, inferiors (for things that are outshone)|词性: Noun
