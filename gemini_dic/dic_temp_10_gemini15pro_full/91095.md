一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she do for a living?  她以什么为生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? 他晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? 她用这笔钱做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been talking about all this time? 他一直都在说什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last weekend? 你上周末做了什么？
What happened at the party? 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

What had they discussed before I arrived? 在我到达之前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the power went out? 停电前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? 他们一直在争论什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will happen if we don't finish on time? 如果我们没有按时完成会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):

What will they be discussing at the next meeting? 他们下次会议将讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):

What will have changed by the end of the century? 到本世纪末会发生什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):

虚拟语气:

If I were you, what would I do in this situation? 如果我是你，在这种情况下我会做什么？


被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? 会议上说了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the summer? 你暑假的计划是什么？

否定句:

There's nothing what I want. 没有我想要的东西。
