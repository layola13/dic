gravitate:/ˈɡrævɪteɪt/| vi.|被吸引；受吸引移动；倾向；沉淀|To move or be drawn toward something, especially by natural or physical forces (like gravity). Also, to be drawn toward something or someone due to shared interests, values, etc.|例句：Young people tend to gravitate toward the big cities in search of opportunities. 年轻人往往被大城市吸引，去寻找机会。

例句：Dust particles gravitate downwards. 灰尘颗粒向下沉淀。|近义词：be drawn to, be attracted to, incline towards, tend towards|反义词：avoid, repel, shun|词性:动词

gravitate (towards/to):/ˈɡrævɪteɪt (tɔːrdz/tuː)/| vi.|被…所吸引|To be drawn to something or someone.|例句：She naturally gravitates towards people who share her interests. 她自然而然地被和她有共同兴趣的人所吸引。

例句：He gravitated to the older, more experienced members of the team. 他被团队中年龄较大、经验更丰富的成员所吸引。|近义词：be drawn to, be attracted to|反义词：avoid, repel|词性:动词短语
