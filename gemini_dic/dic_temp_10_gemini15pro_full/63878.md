epilogism: /ɪˈpɪləˌdʒɪzəm/ /ɛˈpɪləˌdʒɪzəm/
noun
推理; 推论; 演绎
A conclusion, inference, or deduction.  An argument that concludes or infers something from preceding arguments.
The detective's epilogism that the murderer was left-handed was based on the angle of the knife wounds.  侦探推断凶手是左撇子，这是基于刀伤的角度。

He presented a convincing epilogism based on the available evidence. 他根据现有证据提出了一个令人信服的推论。

近义词: deduction, inference, conclusion, reasoning
反义词: premise, assumption

词性: n.
