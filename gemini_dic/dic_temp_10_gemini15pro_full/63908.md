epipharynx:/ˌɛpɪˈfærɪŋks/| n. |鼻咽，上咽|The part of the pharynx above the soft palate, connecting with the nasal cavity.  It is the uppermost part of the throat behind the nasal cavity.| The doctor examined his epipharynx for signs of infection. 医生检查了他的鼻咽部是否有感染迹象。|Synonyms: nasopharynx, postnasal space|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider considering structures below it like oropharynx or laryngopharynx)|noun
