hectostere:/ˈhɛktəstɪər/ |n.|一百立方米|A unit of volume equal to 100 cubic meters.  It is primarily used for measuring things like timber or firewood.|A hectostere of wood was delivered to the sawmill.  一百立方米木材被运送到锯木厂。|Synonyms: cubic meter (though not a direct equivalent), stere|Antonyms: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *hectostère*, from *hecto-* (hundred) + *stère* (cubic meter). Ultimately from Greek *stereos* (solid).
