boi:/bɔɪ/| n.|男孩，伙计 (非正式，通常用于网络)|A term of address for a male, often used online. It can be seen as a more casual and playful version of "boy" or "guy."|例句：Hey boi, what's up? 嘿，伙计，怎么了？

|例句：That boi got some serious skills. 那家伙真有两把刷子。|近义词：guy, dude, fellow, man|反义词:girl, woman|Noun
