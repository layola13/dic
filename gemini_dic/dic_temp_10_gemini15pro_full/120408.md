ministrations:/ˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃənz/
n.
服务; 照料; 援助
the act of helping or caring for someone, often in a religious or medical context.

例句：
The nurse's gentle ministrations helped ease the patient's pain.
护士温柔的照料帮助减轻了病人的疼痛。


The priest offered spiritual ministrations to the grieving family.
牧师向悲痛的家庭提供了精神上的慰藉。


近义词：care, assistance, aid, help, support, attention
反义词：neglect, indifference, abandonment
词性: 名词(plural noun)


词源:
From Late Middle English: ministracioun, from Old French: ministracion, from Latin: ministrātiō, from ministrāre ‘serve, attend’, from minister ‘servant’.
