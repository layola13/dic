antikickback:/ˈænˌtiːˈkɪkˌbæk/| adjective, noun|回扣防止,反回扣|Relating to or denoting measures taken to prevent kickbacks (payments made illegally in return for favorable treatment or services).  As a noun, it refers to the measures themselves or the relevant legislation. |例句：The company has a strict antikickback policy.  这家公司有严格的反回扣政策。

例句：Antikickback laws are designed to prevent corruption. 反回扣法旨在防止腐败。|近义词：anticorruption, antibribery|反义词: bribery, kickback|词性:adjective, noun
