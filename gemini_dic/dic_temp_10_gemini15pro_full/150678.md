papyrus:/pəˈpaɪrəs/ | n. | 纸莎草纸；莎草纸文献 | A material prepared in ancient Egypt from the pithy stem of a water plant, used in sheets throughout the ancient world for writing or painting on and also for making rope, sandals, and boats.  |  Example 1: The ancient Egyptians wrote on papyrus. 古埃及人在纸莎草纸上写字。

Example 2:  Many papyri from ancient Egypt have survived to this day. 许多古埃及的莎草纸文献保存至今。 | 近义词: parchment, scroll | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps digital document) | 词性: noun


papyruses:/pəˈpaɪraɪ/ | n. | 纸莎草纸（复数）；莎草纸文献（复数）| The plural form of papyrus. | Example 1:  The museum houses a collection of ancient papyri.  这家博物馆收藏了一批古代莎草纸文献。
