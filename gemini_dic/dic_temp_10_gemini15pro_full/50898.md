desertization:/dɪˌzɜːtɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
沙漠化
The process by which fertile land becomes desert, typically as a result of drought, deforestation, or inappropriate agriculture.
例句：Desertization is a serious environmental problem in many parts of the world.  世界许多地区的沙漠化是一个严重的环境问题。

例句：The ongoing desertization threatens the livelihoods of millions.  持续的沙漠化威胁着数百万人的生计。

近义词：aridification, desolation
反义词：forestation, reclamation
词性：noun
