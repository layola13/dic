oompah:/ˈuːmpɑː/ /ˈʊmpɑː/| n. |低音铜管乐器的声音；(尤指)奥姆帕乐|A loud, rhythmic sound made by a tuba or similar brass instrument, especially in oompah music.|The oompah music filled the beer tent.|近义词：thump, beat|反义词: (No direct antonym for a sound. Could consider "silence" or "quiet")|noun
