confuters:/kənˈfjuːtərz/ | n. | 驳斥者；反驳；混乱（confuter 的复数）| People who disprove or refute something.  It can also refer to a state of confusion. |  The confuters of the theory presented strong evidence. 理论的驳斥者提出了强有力的证据。| synonyms: refuters, disputants, contradictors | antonyms: supporters, proponents, advocates | noun
