metamer:/ˈmɛtəmər/| n.|同分异构体；异构体；等价刺激；分节|A metamer is one of several similar segments into which the body of an animal is divided.  A metamer is also a chemical compound having the same composition and molecular weight as another compound but a different structure.  In color science, a metamer is one of two colors that appear to match under some lighting conditions but not others.|The earthworm's body is composed of many metamers.|Synonyms: isomer, segment, homologue|Antonyms: monomer|noun


metamer:/ˈmɛtəmər/| adj.|分节的|Divided into metamers; segmented.|The earthworm is a metameric animal.|Synonyms: segmented, articulate|Antonyms: unsegmented|adjective
