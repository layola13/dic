curcumin:/ˈkɜːrkjʊmɪn/ | n. | 姜黄素 |A bright yellow chemical produced by some plants, most notably turmeric. It is responsible for the yellow color of turmeric and is used as a spice and food coloring. It also has some medicinal properties.|  Curcumin is being studied for its potential anti-inflammatory effects. 姜黄素因其潜在的抗炎作用而正在被研究。

Curcumin gives curry powder its distinctive yellow color. 姜黄素赋予咖喱粉独特的黄色。| 近义词:diferuloylmethane (chemical name)| 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
