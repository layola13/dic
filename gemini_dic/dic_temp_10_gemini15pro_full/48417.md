deducement:/dɪˈdjuːsmənt/
n.
推论；推断；推导出的结论
The detective's deduction that the killer knew the victim was based on circumstantial evidence.  侦探推断凶手认识受害者，这是基于间接证据的。
His deducement from the facts was brilliant. 他从事实中推断出的结论非常精彩。
Synonyms: deduction, inference, conclusion, reasoning
Antonyms:  induction, premise, assumption
词性: 名词
