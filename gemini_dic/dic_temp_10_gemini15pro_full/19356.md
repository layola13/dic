bibliophilists: /ˌbɪbliəˈfaɪlɪsts/
n.
藏书家，爱书人
A person who collects or has a great love of books.
He was a renowned bibliophile, with a library containing thousands of first editions. 他是一位著名的藏书家，他的图书馆藏有数千本初版书籍。
Bibliophiles often spend hours browsing in secondhand bookstores. 爱书的人经常花几个小时在二手书店里浏览。
Synonyms: bookworm, book lover, bibliomaniac
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym; one might consider "illiterate" or "technophile" as contrasting terms)
