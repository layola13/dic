nonuprightly:/nɒnˈʌpraɪtli/
adverb
不正直地，不诚实地
Not in an upright, honest, or just manner.
He acted nonuprightly in his business dealings, cheating his partners out of their fair share. 他在生意上行事不诚实，骗取了合伙人应得的份额。
Synonyms: dishonestly, unjustly, unfairly, crookedly, wrongly
Antonyms: uprightly, honestly, justly, fairly, righteously
词性: 副词
