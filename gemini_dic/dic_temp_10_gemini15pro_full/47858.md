deceitless:/dɪˈsiːtlɪs/
adjective
无欺诈的;真诚的;诚实的
Free from deceit; honest and sincere.
She had a deceitless heart, always speaking the truth. 她有一颗真诚的心，总是说实话。
近义词:honest, sincere, truthful, upright, guileless
反义词:deceitful, dishonest, lying, untruthful
词性:adjective

词源:
From deceit + -less.  "Deceit" comes from Old French *deceite*, from the verb deceivre "to deceive," ultimately from Latin decipere "to ensnare, beguile."  The suffix "-less" is an Old English suffix meaning "without."
