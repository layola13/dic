palmatisect:/ˌpælˈmætɪˌsɛkt/ | adj. | 掌状深裂的 | Divided into lobes like a hand with fingers spread; palmately cleft. | Example: The leaves of the Japanese maple are palmatisect, giving it a delicate and intricate appearance. 日本枫树的叶子是掌状深裂的，这使它看起来精致而复杂。

Example:  The palmatisect leaves of the fig tree are easily recognizable.  无花果树的掌状深裂叶很容易辨认。 | Near Synonyms: palmately lobed, palmately divided, dissected | Antonyms: entire, undivided | word type: adjective
