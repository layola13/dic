effeminately:/ɪˈfɛmɪnɪtli/| adv.|女人似地，柔弱地|In an effeminate manner; like a woman.|He walked effeminately, swaying his hips. 他走路扭着屁股，女人味十足。|近义词：weakly, softly, gently|反义词：masculinely, strongly, firmly|词性:副词

effeminate:/ɪˈfɛmɪnɪt/| adj.|柔弱的，女人气的（指男子）|Having or showing characteristics regarded as typical of a woman; unmanly.|His effeminate mannerisms made him the target of bullies.  他女人气的举止使他成为被欺负的对象。|近义词：womanish, unmanly|反义词：masculine, manly|词性:形容词

词源:
From Latin *effeminātus*, past participle of effēmināre (“to make womanish, weaken, enervate”), from ex (“out of, from”) + fēmina (“woman”).
