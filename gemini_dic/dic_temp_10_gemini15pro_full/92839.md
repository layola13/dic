icosandrian:/ˌaɪkəˈsændriən/
adjective
二十雄蕊的；具二十雄蕊的
Having twenty stamens, or about twenty.
The icosandrian flower has numerous stamens attached to the calyx.  这种二十雄蕊的花有许多雄蕊附着在花萼上。
The botanist identified the flower as icosandrian due to its stamen count.  植物学家根据它的雄蕊数量将这朵花鉴定为二十雄蕊的。
Synonyms: polyandrous
Antonyms:  N/A (Lack of a direct antonym.  One could consider flowers with fewer or a significantly different number of stamens, but no single word encapsulates that.)
