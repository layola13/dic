nonenumerated:/nɒnɪˈnjuːməreɪtɪd/
adjective
未列举的；未计数的
not listed or counted among others of a similar kind.
The Constitution reserves to the states all powers not specifically nonenumerated to the federal government. 宪法将所有未明确列举授予联邦政府的权力保留给各州。

The report details numerous nonenumerated expenses. 这份报告详细列出了许多未列举的费用。

近义词:unlisted, unspecified, unmentioned
反义词:enumerated, listed, specified
