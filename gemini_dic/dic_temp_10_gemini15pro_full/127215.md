naughtinesses:/ˈnɔːtinɪsɪz/
| n. |顽皮，淘气（naughtiness的复数形式） |Instances of mischievous or disobedient behavior. |The children's naughtinesses kept their parents on their toes. 孩子们的淘气让他们的父母一刻不得闲。|近义词：mischiefs, pranks, shenanigans|反义词：obedience, goodness|词性:名词

词源:
From Middle English *nauti, from naught (“nothing, worthless”) +‎ -y (“characterized by”).  The suffix -ness forms nouns from adjectives.
