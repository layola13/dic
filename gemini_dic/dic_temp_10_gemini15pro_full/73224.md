forcible:/ˈfɔːrsəbl/ (US) /ˈfɔːsəbəl/ (UK)| adj.|强行的；强迫的；有说服力的|Done by force; involving the use of physical power or violence. Also, compelling or persuasive.|

The police made a forcible entry into the building. 警方强行进入了大楼。

He made a forcible argument for his proposal. 他提出了一个有说服力的提案论证。


近义词:powerful, strong, vigorous, compelling, coercive|
反义词:gentle, weak, voluntary|
词性:adjective|

词源:
From Late Middle English *forcible*, from Anglo-Norman *forceable*, from Old French *forcible* "done by force," from Latin *forcibilis* "that can be forced, strong," from *forcis* "force, strength."
