excisional:/ɪkˈsɪʒənl/
adjective|切除的，切除术的|Relating to or involving excision; characterized by removal by cutting.|The doctor recommended an excisional biopsy of the suspicious mole. 医生建议对可疑的痣进行切除活检。|近义词:surgical, ablative|反义词:additive, constructive|词性:adjective

excision:/ɪkˈsɪʒən/
noun|切除，切除术|The act or process of excising; surgical removal or cutting out.|The excision of the tumor was successful. 肿瘤切除手术很成功。|近义词:removal, resection|反义词:addition, insertion|词性:noun

词源: From Latin excisio (“a cutting out”), from excīsus, past participle of excīdō (“cut out, cut off”), from ex (“out”) + caedō (“cut, kill”).
