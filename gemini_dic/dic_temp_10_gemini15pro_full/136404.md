nonretainment:/ˌnɒn rɪˈteɪnmənt/
noun
未保留，不保留
lack of retention; failure to retain.
The nonretainment of staff is a serious issue for the company.  公司员工的流失是一个严重的问题。
The nonretainment of important information led to the project's failure.  重要信息的未保留导致了项目的失败。
nonretention, loss
retention, keeping
