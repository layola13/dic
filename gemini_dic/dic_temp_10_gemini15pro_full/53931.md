discourteously:/dɪsˈkɜːrtiəsli/
adv.
无礼地，粗鲁地
in a rude and impolite manner.
He discourteously interrupted the speaker. 他粗鲁地打断了演讲者。
近义词:rudely, impolitely, uncivilly
反义词:politely, courteously, respectfully
词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation) + courteous + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Courteous" ultimately derives from the Old French "corteis," meaning "courtly" or "having courtly manners," from "cort" meaning "court."
