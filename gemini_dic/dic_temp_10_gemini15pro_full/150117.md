pamperedness:/ˈpæmpərdnɪs/
n.
娇生惯养；溺爱；被宠坏的状态
the state of being pampered; having been treated with excessive indulgence.
She grew up in pamperedness, never having to lift a finger. 她娇生惯养地长大，从不需要做任何家务。
The hotel caters to the pamperedness of its wealthy clientele. 这家酒店迎合了其富裕客户的娇生惯养。
近义词:indulgence, luxury, coddling
反义词:hardship, austerity, neglect
词性:名词

pamper:/ˈpæmpər/
v.
纵容；娇惯；溺爱
indulge with every attention, comfort, and kindness; spoil.
She pampered her dog with gourmet treats and a luxurious bed. 她用美味的食物和豪华的床娇惯她的狗。
He was pampered throughout his childhood. 他整个童年都过着娇生惯养的生活。
近义词:indulge, spoil, coddle
反义词:neglect, deprive
词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "pamper," which comes from Middle Dutch "pampelen" (to cram, stuff).  Originally, the word implied overfeeding, but the meaning evolved to include excessive indulgence and attention in general.
