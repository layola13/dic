nonpliabilities: /ˌnɒnplaɪəˈbɪlɪtiz/ | n. | 无责任，无义务，无债务 |  Absence of liabilities or obligations.  It refers to the state of not being liable or responsible for something, or not having debts or obligations. |  The company's nonpliabilities were clearly stated in the contract.  该公司在合同中明确声明了其无责任。 | Synonyms:  immunity, exemption, freedom from obligation | Antonyms: liabilities, obligations, debts | noun
