durian:/ˈdʊəriən/ | n. | 榴莲 |A large, oval, spiny tropical fruit containing a creamy, strong-smelling pulp. |例句：The durian is known as the "king of fruits" in Southeast Asia. 榴莲在东南亚被称为“水果之王”。

例句：Some people love the taste of durian, while others find it overpowering. 有些人喜欢榴莲的味道，而另一些人觉得它太强烈了。| 近义词: (As a fruit, it doesn't have a direct synonym, perhaps jackfruit in terms of size and texture, though the flavor is vastly different) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
