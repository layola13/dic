edibles:/ˈedəblz/
n.
可食用物；食物
food that can be eaten
例句：The forest offers a variety of edibles, including berries, mushrooms, and nuts.  森林里提供各种各样的可食用物，包括浆果、蘑菇和坚果。

例句：She brought a basket of fresh edibles from her garden. 她从她的花园里带来了一篮子新鲜的食材。
近义词：food, foodstuffs, provisions
反义词：inedibles, poisons, toxins
词性：名词
