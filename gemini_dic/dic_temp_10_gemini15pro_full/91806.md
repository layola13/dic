hyperfemininity:/ˌhaɪpərˌfɛmɪˈnɪnɪti/ | n. | 超级女性化，极端女性化 | An exaggerated adherence to stereotypically feminine gender roles, often emphasized through appearance, behavior, and interests. |  She embraced hyperfemininity, always dressed in frilly dresses and high heels. 她崇尚极致的女性化，总是穿着荷叶边连衣裙和高跟鞋。 |  ultra-femininity, exaggerated femininity | antifemininity, masculinity | noun
