crinkliness:/ˈkrɪŋklɪnɪs/| n. |皱巴巴; 皱纹;|The state or quality of being crinkled or wrinkled.|The crinkliness of the old map made it difficult to read. 这张旧地图皱巴巴的，很难读。|Synonyms: wrinkledness, rumpledness, corrugatedness|Antonyms: smoothness, flatness|noun


crinkle:/ˈkrɪŋkl/| n. v. |皱纹;  使起皱;|A small wrinkle or crease; to become wrinkled or creased.|The crinkle in her nose showed she was amused. 她鼻子上的皱纹表明她很开心。 / The heat crinkled the leaves.  高温使树叶卷曲。|Synonyms: wrinkle, crease, furrow (n.); rumple, crumple (v.)|Antonyms: smooth, flatten (v.)|noun, verb
