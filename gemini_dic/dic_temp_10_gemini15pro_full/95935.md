incubate:/ˈɪŋkjubeɪt/ (US), /ˈɪŋkjʊbeɪt/ (UK)| v.  adj.|孵化，培养，潜伏，酝酿|to keep (something, such as eggs) warm so that it can hatch; to create a situation that allows something to develop|The eggs were incubated at a constant temperature. 这些蛋在恒温下孵化。|近义词：develop, foster, nurture, hatch|反义词:suppress, stifle|verb

incubated:/ˈɪŋkjubeɪtɪd/ (US), /ˈɪŋkjʊbeɪtɪd/ (UK)| adj.|已孵化的，已培养的，潜伏的|having been incubated; maintained under conditions favorable for hatching or development|The incubated eggs hatched after a few weeks.  已孵化的蛋几周后就孵出来了。|近义词：developed, fostered, nurtured, hatched|反义词:undeveloped, suppressed|adjective
