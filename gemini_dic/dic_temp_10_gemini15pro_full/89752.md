hopplebush:/ˈhɑːpəlˌbʊʃ/| n. |美洲毒漆树，毒漆木|A shrub or small tree (Viburnum alnifolium syn. Viburnum lantanoides) of eastern North America, having broad leaves, white flowers, and red berries that turn purple-black. It is also known as witch-hobble and moosewood.|The hopplebush grew thick along the trail.|Synonyms: witch-hobble, moosewood|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
