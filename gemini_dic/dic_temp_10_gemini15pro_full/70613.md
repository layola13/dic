filmless:/ˈfɪlm.ləs/
adjective
无胶片的，不用胶片的
Without film; not using film.
例句：
Filmless cameras are becoming increasingly popular.
无胶片相机越来越受欢迎。

The filmless process saves time and money.
无胶片工艺节省时间和金钱。

近义词：digital, electronic
反义词：film-based

词性: adjective
