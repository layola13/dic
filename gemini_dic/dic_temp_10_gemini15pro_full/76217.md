futilely:/ˈfjuːtaɪlli/ /ˈfjuːtəlli/
adverb
徒劳地，无效地
in a futile manner; unsuccessfully.
She tried futilely to reason with him. 她试图和他讲道理，但徒劳无功。
Synonyms: vainly, unsuccessfully, pointlessly, fruitlessly, ineffectually
Antonyms: effectively, successfully, productively
词性: 副词
