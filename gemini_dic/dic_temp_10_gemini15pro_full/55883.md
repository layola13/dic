divisions: /dɪˈvɪʒənz/ | n. | 分歧，划分，部门 | The act or process of dividing or separating something into parts; a disagreement or difference of opinion between people or groups; a separate part of a large organization, army, etc. | The company has several divisions, each specializing in a different area.  这家公司有几个部门，每个部门都专注于不同的领域。| synonyms: sections, departments, branches, partitions, segments, schisms, rifts, disagreements | antonyms: unity, agreement, harmony, accord, wholeness | noun


Etymology:  Late Middle English: from Old French division or late Latin divisio(n-), from dividere ‘to divide’.
