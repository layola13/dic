bor:/bɔːr/ | n. | 硼 | A metalloid chemical element with atomic number 5.  It is a trivalent nonmetal that occurs abundantly in the evaporite deposits of borax and kernite. | Boron is used in pyrotechnics and flares to produce a green color. 硼被用于烟火和照明弹中以产生绿色。| Boron is essential for plant growth. 硼对植物生长至关重要。 | synonym: element | antonym: (No direct antonym for a chemical element) | noun
