outlengthens:/ˌaʊtˈlɛŋθənz/
verb
超过…的长度；比…长得多
To exceed in length; to become longer than something else.
The new bridge outlengthens the old one by several meters. 新桥比旧桥长几米。
outstrip, surpass, exceed
fall short of, lag behind
词性:v.
