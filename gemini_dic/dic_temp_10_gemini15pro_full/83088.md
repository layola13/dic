gusher:/ˈɡʌʃər/| n.|喷油井；涌出；滔滔不绝的人（或事物）|A gusher is an oil well from which oil flows profusely without being pumped. It can also refer to anything that flows out suddenly and in large quantities, or a person who talks effusively. |The new oil well proved to be a gusher. 这口新油井证明是一口喷油井。

The speaker was a gusher of information. 演讲者滔滔不绝地提供信息。|近义词：spout, surge, outpouring, torrent|反义词:trickle, drip|词性:noun
