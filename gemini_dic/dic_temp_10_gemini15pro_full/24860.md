brownshirt: /ˈbraʊnʃɜːrt/ | n. | 褐衫队员；纳粹冲锋队员 | A member of the Nazi SA (Sturmabteilung), a paramilitary organization known for their brown uniforms and violent tactics. |  The brownshirts intimidated political opponents during the rise of the Nazi party.  褐衫队员在纳粹党崛起期间恐吓政治对手。| synonym: Nazi stormtrooper | antonym:  resistance fighter | noun

brownshirt: /ˈbraʊnʃɜːrt/ | adj. | 褐衫党的；纳粹冲锋队的 | Relating to or characteristic of the Nazi SA. |  Brownshirt violence was a common occurrence in pre-war Germany. 褐衫党的暴力行为在战前德国很常见。| synonym: Nazi, fascist | antonym: anti-fascist | adjective
