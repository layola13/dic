anesthesias:/ˌænɪsˈθiːʒəz/ | n. | 麻醉（复数）| Loss of feeling or sensation, especially the loss of the sensation of pain, induced by drugs or other medical interventions. | The patient received several anesthesias during the course of treatment. 病人在治疗过程中接受了几次麻醉。|  The surgeon administered several anesthesias during the long operation. 外科医生在漫长的手术中进行了多次麻醉。 | synonyms: narcoses, numbnesses | antonyms: sensitivities, consciousnesses | noun
