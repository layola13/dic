misadvised:/ˌmɪsədˈvaɪzd/
adjective
不明智的，失策的，接受了错误建议的
Having been given bad advice; unwise.
He made a misadvised decision to invest all his savings in the stock market. 他做了一个不明智的决定，把所有积蓄都投资了股市。
Synonyms: ill-advised, imprudent, unwise, foolish, misguided
Antonyms: well-advised, prudent, wise, sensible, judicious
词性: adjective
