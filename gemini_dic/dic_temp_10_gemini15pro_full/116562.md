matinal:/ˈmætɪnəl/| adj.|早晨的，清晨的|Of or occurring in the morning; early.|例句：Matinal birds sing at dawn. 早晨的鸟儿在黎明歌唱。

例句：She enjoys a matinal cup of coffee. 她喜欢早上喝一杯咖啡。|近义词：early, morning, dawn, sunrise|反义词：evening, nocturnal, night|词性:形容词
词源:From French *matinal*, from Latin *matinalis* (“of the morning”), from *matutinus* (“of the morning”), from *Matuta*, goddess of the dawn. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\*meh₂t-*.
