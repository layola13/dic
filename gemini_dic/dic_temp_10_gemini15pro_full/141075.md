The use of onomatopoeias, such as "buzz" and "hiss," adds to the poem's vibrancy. 使用像“buzz”和“hiss”这样的拟声词增加了诗歌的活力。
Onomatopoeias like "meow" and "woof" are commonly used to represent animal sounds.  像“meow”和“woof”这样的拟声词通常用来代表动物的声音。
The writer's skillful use of onomatopoeias, like "crackle" and "pop," makes the scene come alive. 作家巧妙地使用了像“crackle”和“pop”这样的拟声词，使场景栩栩如生。
Words like "bang" and "crash" are examples of onomatopoeias that describe sudden loud noises. 像“bang”和“crash”这样的词是描述突然巨响的拟声词的例子。
Onomatopoeias such as "drip," "drop," and "gurgle" evoke the sound of water. 像 "drip"、"drop" 和 "gurgle" 这样的拟声词让人联想到水的声音。

词源：From late Latin onomatopoeia, from Greek onomatopoiia, from onomatopoios 'word-making,' from onoma, onomat- 'name' + poiein 'to make.'
