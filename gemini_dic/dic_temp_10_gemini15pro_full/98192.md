insect:/ˈɪnsekt/ (US), /ˈɪnˌsekt/ (UK)| n. |昆虫|A small arthropod animal that has six legs and generally one or two pairs of wings.|例句：The bee is a flying insect. 蜜蜂是一种会飞的昆虫。|例句：Insects are crucial to the ecosystem. 昆虫对生态系统至关重要。|近义词：bug, creepy-crawly|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑 large animal 大型动物)|词性:noun
