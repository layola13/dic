ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (indicating error or wrongness) + alter (to change).  The prefix "mis-" comes from Old English "mis-", meaning "wrongly, badly, amiss," from Proto-Germanic "*missa-", "wrong, different." The word "alter" comes from Old French "alterer," from Latin "alterare," meaning "to change, modify."
