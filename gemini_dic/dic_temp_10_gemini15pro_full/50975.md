desipient:/dɪˈsɪpiənt/
adjective
愚蠢的，不明智的
Lacking good judgment or wisdom; foolish.
He made a desipient decision to invest all his money in a single stock. 他做了一个愚蠢的决定，把所有的钱都投资在一支股票上。
近义词: foolish, unwise, silly, imprudent, injudicious
反义词: wise, prudent, judicious, sagacious, intelligent
词性: adjective

desipient:/dɪˈsɪpiənt/
noun
愚蠢的人，不明智的人
A foolish or unwise person.
The desipient fell for the obvious scam.  这个愚蠢的人上了明显的骗局的当。
近义词: fool, idiot, simpleton, dunce
反义词: sage, genius, intellectual
