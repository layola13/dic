gross-outs:/ˈɡroʊs aʊts/| n.|令人作呕的东西；恶心的事物|Something (such as a film scene or story) designed to disgust or horrify.|The movie was full of gross-outs, like someone vomiting. 这部电影充满了令人作呕的场景，比如有人呕吐。|近义词：disgusting things, repulsive things|反义词: pleasant things, appealing things|词性:名词

Etymology:
Compound of "gross" (meaning unpleasant and offensive) and "out" (used here to suggest something that is overtly displayed or emphasized).  The term likely emerged in informal language in the late 20th century.
