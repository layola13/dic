chelate:/ˈkēˌlāt/ /ˈkiːleɪt/ | n. v. adj. |螯合物；螯合；螯合的 |A chelate is a chemical compound in which a metal ion is bonded to a ligand in a ring structure.  As a verb, it means to combine with a metal ion to form a chelate. As an adjective, it describes a ligand or complex that forms a chelate.|

例句：
The plant absorbs iron more efficiently when it is chelated.
这种植物在铁螯合后吸收效率更高。

EDTA is a common chelating agent.
EDTA是一种常见的螯合剂。

The chelate complex is more stable than the non-chelated form.
螯合物比非螯合形式更稳定。


近义词：complex, coordinate, bind |
反义词: release, dissociate |
词性: noun, verb, adjective |
