impertinent:/ɪmˈpɜːrtnənt/ (US), /ɪmˈpɜːtɪnənt/ (UK)| adj.|无礼的，鲁莽的，不相关的|Not showing proper respect; rude.  Also, not pertinent to a particular matter; irrelevant.|Example 1:  His impertinent remarks offended the guests.  他的无礼言论冒犯了客人。

Example 2: The lawyer objected to the witness's impertinent testimony, as it was irrelevant to the case.  律师反对证人无关紧要的证词，因为它与案件无关。|近义词：rude, insolent, impudent, disrespectful, irrelevant|反义词：polite, respectful, courteous, relevant|词性:形容词
