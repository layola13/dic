binky:/ˈbɪŋki/| n.|安抚奶嘴；特别指婴儿用的安抚奶嘴|A pacifier, especially one for a baby. |The baby wouldn't go to sleep without his binky.  这个婴儿没有安抚奶嘴就睡不着。|Synonym: pacifier, dummy (chiefly British)|Antonym: None|Noun

binky:/ˈbɪŋki/| v. (informal) |(兔子)跳跃；欢跳|(of a rabbit) to hop or jump playfully. |The rabbit binkied across the lawn. 兔子在草坪上欢快地跳跃。|Synonym: hop, frisk, frolic|Antonym: None|Verb
