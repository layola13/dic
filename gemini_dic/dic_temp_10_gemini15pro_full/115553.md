maps:/mæps/ | n. |地图; 图表; (基因的)图谱; 映射 |A symbolic depiction, usually on a flat surface, of a selection of material or abstract features on or of the earth or another celestial body. A diagrammatic representation of an area.| She unfolded the map and traced the route with her finger. 她展开地图，用手指描绘路线。 | synonyms: chart, plan, diagram, atlas, cartogram | antonyms: globe (a three-dimensional representation) | noun
