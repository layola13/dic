nondictatorialnesses:/nɒndɪktəˈtɔːriəlnəsɪz/| n. |非独裁性；非专制性|The quality or state of being nondictatorial, characterized by a lack of authoritarianism or tyranny.  It refers to systems or approaches where power isn't concentrated in a single entity and allows for diverse opinions and participation. | The country's transition to nondictatorialness was marked by free and fair elections.  这个国家向非独裁性的过渡以自由公正的选举为标志。


The constitution guarantees the nondictatorialness of the government. 宪法保障了政府的非独裁性。
