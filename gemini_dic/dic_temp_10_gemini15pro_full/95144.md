inappropriate:/ˌɪnəˈproʊpriət/| adj.|不合适的，不恰当的|Not suitable or proper in the circumstances.|His casual clothes were inappropriate for such a formal occasion. 他的便服在这种正式场合不合适。|近义词：unsuitable, improper, unfit|反义词：appropriate, suitable, proper|adjective

inappropriateness:/ˌɪnəˈproʊpriətnəs/| n.|不恰当，不适合|The state or quality of being inappropriate.|The inappropriateness of his remarks shocked everyone. 他言辞的不恰当震惊了所有人。|近义词：unsuitability, impropriety|反义词：appropriateness, suitability|noun
