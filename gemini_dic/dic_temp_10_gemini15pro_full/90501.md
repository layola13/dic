hucksterisms: /ˈhʌkstərɪzəmz/ | n. | 叫卖；兜售；夸大其词的广告宣传；江湖郎中的言辞 |  Aggressive, exaggerated sales methods or advertising, often involving dubious claims.  | The politician's speech was full of hucksterisms, promising impossible solutions to complex problems. 政治家的演讲充满了夸大其词的宣传，承诺对复杂问题提出不可能的解决方案。 |  Example: The infomercial was filled with hucksterisms about the product's miraculous abilities.  这部电视购物广告充满了关于该产品神奇功效的夸大宣传。 | Synonyms:  hype, ballyhoo, puffery, sales pitch | Antonyms:  understatement, modesty, truthfulness | Noun
