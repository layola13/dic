nonfundamentals:/nɒnˌfʌndəˈmentəlz/ | n. | 非基本面因素 | Factors other than economic and financial fundamentals that affect market prices, such as investor sentiment, technical analysis, or news events. | Example: The recent surge in stock prices is attributed to nonfundamentals, as the underlying economic data remain weak. 最近股价的飙升归因于非基本面因素，因为潜在的经济数据仍然疲软。 | Example:  Market volatility can be driven by nonfundamentals like rumors and speculation. 市场波动可能是由谣言和投机等非基本面因素驱动的。 | Synonym:  technical factors, market sentiment, speculative factors | Antonym: fundamentals, economic data, financial data | Noun
