feuar:/ˈfjuːər/| n.|封地，采邑|A feuar is someone who holds land under a feudal tenure, especially in Scotland.  封建制度下拥有土地的人，尤指在苏格兰。|The feuar paid an annual rent to the laird.  采邑持有者每年向领主缴纳地租。|近义词：fief, vassal|反义词:lord, superior|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *feüer, from fief (“feud, fief”), from Medieval Latin feudum, of Germanic origin.  源自古法语 *feüer，来自 fief（“封地、采邑”），来自中世纪拉丁语 feudum，源自日耳曼语。
