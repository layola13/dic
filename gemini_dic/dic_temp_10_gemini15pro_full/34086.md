civilizations: /ˌsɪvələˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 文明（复数） | The stage of human social development and organization that is considered most advanced.  The process by which a society or place reaches an advanced stage of social development and organization. |  Many ancient civilizations flourished along rivers. 许多古代文明沿着河流繁荣发展。|  Ancient civilizations left behind impressive monuments. 古代文明留下了令人印象深刻的遗迹。 | 近义词: cultures, societies | 反义词: barbarity, savagery | 词性: 名词

词源: From Old French civilisation, from Latin civilis “relating to a citizen, civil, courteous,” from civis “citizen.”
