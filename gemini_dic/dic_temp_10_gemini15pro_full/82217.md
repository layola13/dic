grottier:/ˈɡrɒtiər/ (US) /ˈɡrɒtɪər/ (UK)| adj.|更丑陋的，更怪诞的，更粗俗的|Comparative form of "grotty," meaning more unpleasant, ugly, or dirty.|The abandoned house was even grottier than I remembered. 这栋废弃的房子比我记忆中更加破败不堪。|The bathroom in the hostel was grottier than any I'd ever seen.  这家旅馆的浴室比我见过的任何一间都更脏乱差。|近义词：uglier, nastier, dirtier, shabbier, more repulsive|反义词：prettier, nicer, cleaner, more attractive|词性:形容词

词源:
From "grotty" + "-er" (comparative suffix). "Grotty" itself is likely a shortened form of "grotesque," with a change in vowel sound and the addition of the "-y" suffix. "Grotesque" ultimately derives from Italian "grottesca," related to "grotta" (grotto).
