beadroll:/ˈbiːdˌroʊl/| n. |念珠；祷告者名单；一长串|A list of people or things, especially one recited in prayer; a rosary.|The priest read from the beadroll of the deceased. 神父宣读了逝者的名单。|近义词：list, roster, catalogue, inventory|反义词: None|词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bede-rolle*, from Old English *bed*, prayer + *rolle*, roll.  The original meaning referred to a list of those to be prayed for.
