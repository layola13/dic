overawareness:/ˌoʊvərəˈwɛrnəs/| n. |过度意识；过度警觉|An excessive consciousness or sensitivity to something, often to the point of anxiety or discomfort.|She suffers from overawareness of her own flaws. 她过度在意自己的缺点。|近义词：hypersensitivity, self-consciousness|反义词：obliviousness, unawareness|词性:名词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "awareness" (n.)
