disciflorous:/dɪˈskɪˈflɔːrəs/
adj.
盘状花的，头状花序中央盘状的
Having disc florets, as in the central part of a composite flower head.
The sunflower exhibits a disciflorous structure, with numerous small florets packed tightly together in the central disc. 向日葵呈现盘状花结构，在中心盘中紧密地排列着许多小花。
Some species of aster are disciflorous, while others have ray florets. 一些紫菀种类是盘状花的，而其他种类有舌状花。
近义词:discoid
反义词:radiate
词性:adjective
