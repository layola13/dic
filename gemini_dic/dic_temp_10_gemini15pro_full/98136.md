insanitariness:/ɪnˌsænɪˈtɛrɪnɪs/
|n.|不卫生；不健康|The state of being insanitary; lack of sanitation.  A condition characterized by filth, dirt, and a lack of hygiene, posing a risk to health.|The insanitariness of the living conditions led to an outbreak of disease.  生活条件的不卫生导致了疾病的爆发。|insalubrity, unhealthiness, filthiness, uncleanliness|cleanliness, hygiene, salubrity
