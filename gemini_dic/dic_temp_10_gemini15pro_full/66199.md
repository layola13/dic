excision:/ɪkˈsɪʒən/ /ɛkˈsɪʒən/
noun
切除; 删除; 删节
The surgical removal of a tissue or organ.
The excision of a tumor.  肿瘤的切除。
removal, deletion, extraction
inclusion, insertion, addition
