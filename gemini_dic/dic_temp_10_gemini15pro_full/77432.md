gawby:/ˈɡɔːbi/| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋；容易上当的人|A naive or foolish person; someone easily duped. |He was such a gawby, he believed everything she told him. 他真是个傻瓜，她说什么他都信。|Synonym: simpleton, fool, dupe, gull, chump |Antonym: genius, sage, sharpie| noun

gawby:/ˈɡɔːbi/| adj.|傻的，笨的|Naive or foolish.|That was a gawby thing to do. 那样做真是傻。|Synonym: foolish, silly, stupid, naive, gullible |Antonym: smart, intelligent, wise, astute| adjective
