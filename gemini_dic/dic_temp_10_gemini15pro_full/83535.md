USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you do for a living?  你是做什么工作的？
What does she like to eat? 她喜欢吃什么？


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has she cooked for dinner? 她晚饭做了什么？


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? 他最近一直在读什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last night? 你昨晚做了什么？
What happened at the party? 派对上发生了什么？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had they discussed before I arrived? 在我到达之前他们讨论了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called?  在我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? 在她换专业之前她一直在学什么？



一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will happen next? 接下来会发生什么？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? 他们下次会议会讨论什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by 2030? 到2030年会发生什么变化？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by then? 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？



虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
If I knew what to say, I would tell you. 如果我知道该说什么，我会告诉你的。


被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？


疑问句:
What is the capital of France? 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? 你周末有什么计划？


否定句:
I don't know what to do. 我不知道该怎么办。
There's nothing what I can do. 我什么也做不了。(更常见的表达是: There's nothing I can do.)


PHRASE->
What's up?  怎么了？/你好吗？
What about you? 你呢？
What for? 为了什么？
What if it rains? 如果下雨怎么办？
So what? 那又怎样？
