muscadine:/ˈmʌskəˌdaɪn/ | n. |  麝香葡萄 | A grape of the southern US, with a thick, tough skin and a sweet, musky flavor. |  例句：We made muscadine wine last fall. 我们去年秋天酿了麝香葡萄酒。

例句：Muscadine jelly is a Southern delicacy. 麝香葡萄果冻是南方的一种美味佳肴。| 近义词：scuppernong (a type of muscadine), fox grape | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Powhatan (an Algonquian language) *meskútaten, from *mesk- “musky” + *-aten “vine”.
