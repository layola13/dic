compacture:/kəmˈpæktʃər/ |n.|紧密, 压缩, 密实; 简洁, 紧凑|The compactization of the soil makes it difficult for plants to grow. 土壤的密实使得植物难以生长。|近义词: compactness, density, solidity, compression|反义词: looseness, expansion, porosity|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *compactura, from Latin compactus (past participle of compingere, "to fasten together"), from com- ("together") + pangere ("to fix, fasten").
