farcing:/ˈfɑːrsɪŋ/
v.
填塞; 填馅; 虚伪地表现
stuffing; filling something with force; behaving insincerely
He was farcing a smile, clearly unhappy with the situation. 他强颜欢笑，显然对情况很不满意。
近义词: stuffing, cramming, packing, feigning, faking
反义词: emptying, removing

词性: v. (动词)
