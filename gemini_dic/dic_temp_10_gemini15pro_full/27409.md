cameramen: /ˈkæmərəmən/ (US), /ˈkæmərəmən/ (UK)
noun
摄影师（复数）
A person who operates a movie or television camera.
The cameramen were positioned to capture the best shots of the race. 摄影师们被安排在能够捕捉到比赛最佳镜面的位置。
cameraman (singular), photographers, cinematographers

ETYMOLOGY->
From camera + man.  "Camera" ultimately comes from the Latin "camera obscura," meaning "dark chamber," an early device used to project images. "Man" comes from Old English "mann," meaning "human being, person."
