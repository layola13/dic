gravid:/ˈɡrævɪd/| adj.|怀孕的，妊娠的|Carrying developing young or eggs within the body; pregnant.|例句：The gravid mare was carefully led to the stable. 怀孕的母马被小心翼翼地带到马厩。|例句：The scientist studied the gravid female fish to understand their reproductive cycle.  科学家研究了怀孕的雌鱼以了解它们的繁殖周期。|近义词：pregnant, expectant|反义词：non-pregnant, barren|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin gravidus (“loaded, heavy, full”), from gravis (“heavy”).
