overcharger:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃɑːrdʒər/| n.|过度收费者；漫天要价者|A person or business that charges excessively high prices.|The mechanic was an overcharger and I won't be going back to him.  这个机修工漫天要价，我不会再去找他了。|Example: The restaurant was known as an overcharger, often adding hidden fees to the bill. 这家餐厅以漫天要价而闻名，经常在账单上添加隐藏费用。|Synonyms: profiteer, gouger|Antonyms: discounter, bargainer|noun


overcharge:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃɑːrdʒ/| vt.  vi.|过度充电；过度收费；装药过多；过分强调；使…负担过重|To charge too much for goods or services; to load or fill to excess.|The taxi driver overcharged me for the ride to the airport. 出租车司机向我多收了去机场的车费。|Example: Be careful not to overcharge your phone, it can damage the battery. 小心不要给你的手机过度充电，这会损坏电池。|Synonyms: overprice, gouge, inflate|Antonyms: undercharge, discount|verb
