niggardly: /ˈnɪɡərdli/ | adj. | 吝啬的，小气的 | Stingy; unwilling to spend or give; miserly. | He was niggardly with his praise, offering only a grudging compliment. 他对表扬很吝啬，只勉强地说了几句好话。

|  He gave a niggardly donation to the charity. 他给慈善机构捐了很少的钱。 | 近义词: stingy, miserly, parsimonious, frugal, penny-pinching | 反义词: generous, liberal, lavish, munificent, prodigal | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *nigardly, alteration of *niggardly influenced by niggard (“miser”), equivalent to niggard +‎ -ly.  From Old Norse *hnǫggr (“stingy, miserly”) (compare Icelandic hnöggr (“close-fisted, niggardly”), Norwegian dialectal hnøgg (“miser, skinflint”)), from Proto-Germanic *hnaggiaz (“stingy, niggardly”).  More at niggard.

注意事项:

由于该词与一个具有冒犯性的种族歧视词语发音相似，尽管词源不同，但在使用时仍需谨慎，以免造成误解和冒犯。  在许多情况下，使用其他近义词例如 stingy, miserly 或 parsimonious 更为安全。
