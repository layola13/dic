cruels:/ˈkruːəlz/| n.|残忍行为；残酷行径|Plural form of cruel, meaning acts of cruelty or instances of inhumane behavior.|The cruels inflicted upon the prisoners of war were unspeakable.  战俘遭受的残酷行径难以言喻。|近义词：atrocities, barbarities, brutalities|反义词: kindnesses, mercies|词性:名词

cruel:/ˈkruːəl/| adj.|残酷的；残忍的；无情的|Deliberately causing pain or suffering to others, or showing no concern about it.|It was a cruel and unusual punishment. 这是一种残酷且不寻常的惩罚。|近义词：brutal, callous, heartless|反义词: kind, compassionate|词性:形容词
