gastropoda:/ɡæˈstrɒpədə/ | n. | 腹足纲；腹足动物 |  A large class of mollusks, typically having a single, usually spirally coiled shell and a broad, muscular foot for locomotion.  They include snails, slugs, and limpets. | Snails and slugs are both gastropoda.  蜗牛和蛞蝓都是腹足纲动物。 | synonyms: univalves, gastropod | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a taxonomic class) | noun
