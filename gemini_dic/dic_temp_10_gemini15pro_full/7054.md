anemographic:/ˌænɪməˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
风速记录的；风力描记的
Of or relating to the measurement of wind speed and direction or the automatic recording of wind data by means of an anemograph.
The anemographic data showed a significant increase in wind speed during the storm.  风速记录数据显示，暴风雨期间风速显著增加。
The researchers used anemographic instruments to study the wind patterns in the region.  研究人员使用风速记录仪器来研究该地区的风型。
近义词:wind-related,  wind-measuring
反义词: None
