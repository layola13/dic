nonmonetarists:/ˌnɒnˈmʌnɪtərɪsts/ | n. | 非货币主义者 |  Those who disagree with or do not adhere to the principles of monetarism, an economic theory that emphasizes the role of the money supply in controlling inflation and economic growth. | The nonmonetarists argued that government spending could also stimulate economic growth.  非货币主义者认为政府支出也可以刺激经济增长。| Synonyms: Keynesians, fiscalists | Antonyms: monetarists | noun
