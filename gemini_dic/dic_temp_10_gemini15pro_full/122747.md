moistless:/ˈmɔɪstləs/| adj.|无水分的，干燥的|Lacking moisture; dry.  It describes something that is completely dry, devoid of any dampness or moisture.|The desert air was moistless and hot. 沙漠的空气干燥而炎热。|近义词：arid, dry, parched, desiccated|反义词:moist, damp, humid|词性:形容词

词源: moist + -less. "Moist" comes from Middle English *moiste*, from Old French *moiste*, ultimately from Latin *musteus* "new, fresh, young."  "-less" is a suffix meaning "without" or "lacking."
