niched:/nɪtʃt/ | adj. | 利基的，针对特定市场的 | Occupying a niche, specialized. |  This company has a very niched product, appealing to a small but dedicated group of customers.  这家公司拥有一款非常利基的产品，吸引了一小群但专注的客户。 | Example: The shop sells niched perfumes for people with specific allergies. 这家商店出售针对特定过敏人群的小众香水。 | Synonyms: specialized, targeted, specific, niche | Antonyms: general, broad, mass-market | adjective


niched:/nɪtʃt/ | v. | 放入壁龛 | Placed in a recess, alcove, or niche. | They niched the statue in the wall of the garden. 他们在花园的墙上放置了雕像。 | Example: The builder niched the shelves into the wall. 建筑工人把架子嵌入墙里。 | Synonyms: inset, embed, place, insert | Antonyms: remove, extract | verb
