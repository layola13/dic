pantsuited:/ˈpæntsˌsuːtɪd/
adjective
穿裤装的
Wearing a pantsuit; dressed in a pantsuit.
Hillary Clinton is often seen pantsuited at public events. 希拉里·克林顿经常在公共场合穿裤装。
The pantsuited woman strode confidently into the boardroom.  这位穿裤装的女士自信地大步走进会议室。
近义词: suited, dressed up
反义词: casually dressed, informally dressed
词性: adjective

pantsuit:/ˈpæntsˌsuːt/
noun
裤套装
A woman's outfit consisting of a matching jacket and trousers.
She chose a stylish pantsuit for the interview. 她为面试选择了一套时尚的裤装。
Power dressing often involves a well-tailored pantsuit.  权力着装通常包括一套剪裁合体的裤装。
近义词: trouser suit
反义词: dress, skirt and blouse
词性: noun

词源:
pantsuit (n.) 1960s, from pants + suit (n.).  "Pants" is short for "pantaloons," which came from Italian "pantalone," a comic character in Italian commedia dell'arte who wore tight trousers.
