imparity:/ɪmˈpærəti/| n. |不平等，不均等，奇数|Lack of equality or equivalence; difference.  Also, the state of being odd (in mathematics).|The disparity in wealth between the rich and the poor is shocking.  富人和穷人之间的财富差距令人震惊。|The parity bit checks for errors by ensuring the number of bits is odd, thus exploiting the principle of imparity. 奇偶校验位通过确保位数为奇数来检查错误，从而利用了奇数原理。|近义词：inequality, disparity, asymmetry, disproportion, oddness|反义词: equality, parity, evenness, uniformity|词性:noun
