homoiothermal: /ˌhoʊmiːoʊˈθɜːrməl/ | adj. | 温血的，恒温的 |  Maintaining a relatively constant body temperature despite changes in the surrounding temperature.  An animal that maintains a stable internal body temperature regardless of external influence. | Birds and mammals are homoiothermal animals. 鸟类和哺乳动物是恒温动物。|  Homoiothermal animals can maintain their body temperature even in cold environments. 恒温动物即使在寒冷的环境中也能保持体温。 |  endothermic, warm-blooded | poikilothermic, cold-blooded | adjective
