neurotics:/nʊˈrɒtɪks/ | n. | 神经症患者；神经质的人 | People suffering from a relatively mild mental illness that is characterized by excessive anxiety, compulsions, obsessions, and unreasonable fears. |  Many neurotics exhibit obsessive-compulsive behaviors. 许多神经症患者表现出强迫性行为。|  Neurotics often worry excessively about minor details. 神经症患者经常过度担心一些小细节。 | synonyms: worriers, sufferers,  anxiety-ridden individuals | antonyms:  stable individuals, well-adjusted people | noun
