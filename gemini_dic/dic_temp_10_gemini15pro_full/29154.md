cassettes:/kəˈsɛts/
n.
盒式磁带；卡式磁带
A sealed plastic unit containing a length of magnetic tape, used for recording sound or pictures.
例句：I found a box of old cassettes in the attic. 我在阁楼里发现了一箱旧磁带。

例句：He used to record his favorite songs onto cassettes. 他过去常常把喜欢的歌曲录到磁带上。

近义词：tapes, cartridges
反义词：(No direct antonym, but could consider digital files, streaming)
词性：noun
