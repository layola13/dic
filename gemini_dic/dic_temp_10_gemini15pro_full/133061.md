nonequable:/ˌnɒnˈiːkwəb(ə)l/
adjective
不稳定的，易变的
Not uniform or consistent; changeable.
The nonequable climate made farming difficult.  这变化无常的气候使得农耕变得困难。
Her nonequable temper made it hard to be her friend. 她反复无常的脾气使人难以和她做朋友。
近义词: variable, changeable, unstable, fluctuating, inconsistent, erratic, uneven, irregular
反义词: stable, constant, steady, uniform, consistent, equable
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From non- + equable.  "Non-" meaning "not," and "equable" meaning "even or uniform." Ultimately from Latin "aequabilis."
