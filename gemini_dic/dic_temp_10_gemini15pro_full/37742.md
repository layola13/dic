compartmented:/kəmˈpɑːrtməntɪd/ | adj. | 分隔的，划分成各自独立部分的 | Divided into separate compartments or sections, either physically or metaphorically. |  The ship had a compartmented hull for safety.  这艘船的船体为了安全起见被分隔开来。|  segmented, partitioned, subdivided | unified, integrated | adjective

compartmented:/kəmˈpɑːrtməntɪd/ | adj. | (指思想或生活)分隔的，条块分割的 | (of a person's mind or life) divided into separate sections. | He led a very compartmented life, with his work and family life strictly separate. 他的生活划分得很清楚，工作和家庭生活截然分开。 |  categorized, classified |  intermingled | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From compartment + -ed.  Compartment itself comes from the French word "compartiment," meaning a division or compartment, derived from "compartir," meaning to divide or share. Ultimately, the roots trace back to the Latin "compartere," meaning to share.
