non-stop:/ˌnɒnˈstɒp/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈstɑːp/ (US)| adj. adv.|不停的，不断的|Without stopping; continuously.|We took a non-stop flight from New York to London. 我们从纽约直飞伦敦。|近义词：continuous, incessant, uninterrupted|反义词：intermittent, occasional|词性:形容词/副词
non-stop:/ˌnɒnˈstɒp/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈstɑːp/ (US)| n.|直达航班/车次|A non-stop flight or train journey.|The non-stop to Chicago was delayed.  直达芝加哥的航班延误了。|近义词：direct, through|反义词：connecting|词性:名词

词源:  "Non-" (Latin, meaning "not") + "stop".  A relatively modern word, first appearing in the late 19th century.
