overharvested:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrvɪstɪd/| adj.|过度捕捞的，过度采伐的|Harvested more than is sustainable or replenishable, leading to depletion or damage to a resource.  It describes a situation where a natural resource has been exploited excessively, beyond its capacity to recover.|The ocean's fish stocks are severely overharvested. 海洋的鱼类资源被过度捕捞。|The Amazon rainforest has been overharvested for timber. 亚马逊雨林的木材被过度采伐。|近义词：overexploited, depleted, exhausted|反义词:sustainable, conserved|词性:形容词

overharvest:/ˌoʊvərˈhɑːrvɪst/| vt.|过度捕捞，过度采伐|To harvest more than is sustainable or replenishable, leading to depletion or damage to a resource.|Fishermen overharvest the cod, leading to a decline in the fish population. 渔民过度捕捞鳕鱼，导致鱼类数量下降。|Logging companies sometimes overharvest forests, causing environmental damage.  伐木公司有时过度采伐森林，造成环境破坏。|近义词：overexploit, deplete, exhaust|反义词:conserve, sustain|词性:动词

Etymology:
From "over-" (meaning "excessive") + "harvest" (from Old English *hærfest, meaning "autumn")
