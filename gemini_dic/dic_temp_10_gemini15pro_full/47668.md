debriefs:/diːˈbriːfs/
verb
汇报；听取汇报；事后情况汇报
To question (someone, typically a pilot or astronaut) about a completed mission or undertaking.
The pilots were debriefed after every mission. 飞行员每次任务结束后都要进行汇报。
synonyms: question, interview, interrogate, examine
antonyms: brief, instruct

debrief:/diːˈbriːf/
noun
汇报；事后情况汇报
A series of questions about a completed mission or undertaking.
The debrief after the simulation was intense. 模拟结束后的事后汇报非常紧张。
synonyms: interview, interrogation, examination
antonyms: briefing, instruction

词性:
verb
noun
