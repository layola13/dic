cornloft:/ˈkɔːrnˌlɒft/ | /ˈkɔːrnˌlɑːft/ | n. | 谷仓，玉米仓 | A room or space, typically located above a barn or stable, used for storing corn (and often other grains). | The farmer stored the harvest in the cornloft. 农民把收获的粮食储存在玉米仓里。 | The old cornloft was filled to the brim with dried corn.  旧玉米仓里装满了晒干的玉米。 | 近义词：granary, silo | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "field" as the origin of the corn) | 词性: noun
