basicerite:/beɪsɪˈsɪraɪt/
noun
基节
The second segment of the crustacean antenna.  It's the second segment (counting outwards from the body) of the antenna in crustaceans.
The basicerite of the shrimp is particularly large.  虾的基节特别大。
近义词:  antennal segment,  basis
反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific anatomical term)
词性: n.


Etymology:  From the Greek  "basis" (base) +  "-cerite" (relating to the antennae/horns).
