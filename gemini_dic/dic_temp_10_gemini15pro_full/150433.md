pansyish:/ˈpænsiɪʃ/| adj.|女性化的，娘娘腔的|Effeminate or homosexual in a derogatory sense; lacking in masculinity or suggestive of stereotypical feminine traits.|He was teased for his pansyish walk and high-pitched voice. 他因为他女性化的步态和尖细的嗓音而被嘲笑。|近义词：effeminate, sissy, unmanly|反义词：masculine, manly, macho|词性:形容词

词源:
From "pansy" (a violet flower, often associated with effeminacy) + "-ish" (suffix indicating resemblance or characteristic).  "Pansy" itself acquired its derogatory connotation in the early 20th century.
