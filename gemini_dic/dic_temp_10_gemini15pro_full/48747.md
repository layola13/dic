definitely:/ˈdefɪnɪtli/| adv.|肯定地，当然，明确地|Without doubt; certainly.|I will definitely be there. 我一定会去的。|近义词：certainly, absolutely, surely, undoubtedly|反义词：possibly, perhaps, maybe|词性:副词
词源: From Late Latin *definitive, from Latin definitus (“defined, bounded, limited”), past participle of definire (“to define, limit, bound”), from de- (“down, completely”) + finis (“end, limit, boundary”).
