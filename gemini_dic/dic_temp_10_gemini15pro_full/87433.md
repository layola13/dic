hesiod:/ˈhiːsiəd, ˈhɛsiəd/| n.|赫西俄德|An ancient Greek poet, generally thought to have flourished around 700 BC, slightly later than Homer. He is best known for his *Works and Days*, a didactic poem on farming, and *Theogony*, which describes the origins and genealogies of the Greek gods.|Hesiod's *Works and Days* offers a fascinating glimpse into ancient Greek agricultural practices. 赫西俄德的《工作与时日》让我们得以一窥古希腊的农业实践。|近义词:Homer (in terms of being an early Greek poet)|反义词:N/A|noun
