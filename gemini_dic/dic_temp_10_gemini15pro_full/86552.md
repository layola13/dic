helia:/ˈhiːliə/ | n. | 太阳, 日轮; 向日葵 |  From Greek *hēlios* "sun".  Refers to the sun or, by extension, sunflowers which turn towards the sun. | The ancient Greeks worshipped Helia as the sun god. 古希腊人崇拜赫利俄斯为太阳神。|  Helia, the sunflower, followed the sun across the sky. 向日葵赫利娅随着太阳划过天空。 | synonyms: sun, sol | antonyms: darkness, night | noun
