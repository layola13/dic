causticily:/kɔːˈstɪsɪli/
adverb
刻薄地；挖苦地
in a sarcastic and bitter way that is intended to hurt someone's feelings.
His remarks about her appearance were delivered caustically. 他对她的外貌的评论说得非常刻薄。
Synonyms:  bitterly, acrimoniously, sarcastically, mordantly, cuttingly, sharply,  vitriolically
Antonyms: kindly, gently, sweetly, pleasantly
词性: 副词
