mismanaging: /ˌmɪsˈmænɪdʒɪŋ/
verb
管理不善，处置失当
Failing to manage or control something effectively or efficiently.
The company was mismanaging its resources and losing money.  公司对资源管理不善，正在亏损。
The government was accused of mismanaging the economy. 政府被指责管理经济不当。
近义词: mishandling, misgoverning, bungling
反义词: managing well, administering effectively
