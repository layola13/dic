monogrammic: /ˌmɒnəˈɡræmɪk/ | adj. | 单字母的，用字母组合图案的 | Consisting of a single letter or a monogram.  A design formed by the combination of two or more letters, typically the initials of a person or company, used as a logo or emblem. |  The company used a monogrammic logo on their stationery.  这家公司在他们的信纸上使用了单字母组合的标志。 |  The artist created a beautiful monogrammic design for the wedding invitations. 艺术家为婚礼请柬设计了一个漂亮的字母组合图案。 | synonymous: initialed, ciphered | antonymous: polygrammic | adjective
