mulls:/mʌlz/
| v. | 仔细考虑，反复思考 | To think about something carefully and for a long time. | She mulled over the offer for several days before making a decision. 她仔细考虑了几天才做出决定。|  I mulled the idea over in my mind. 我在心里反复思考这个主意。| consider, ponder, contemplate | dismiss, ignore | verb

mulls:/mʌlz/
| n. |  (通常指加热加糖的)热饮，香料酒 | A hot drink made with wine or beer, sweetened and spiced. | He sipped a hot mulled wine. 他啜饮了一口热乎乎的香料酒。 | We enjoyed mulled cider on Christmas Eve.  我们在圣诞夜享用了热苹果酒。 | drink, beverage | food, solid | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mullen, from Anglo-Norman muler, moller “to grind, mill,” from Latin molō, molere “to grind.” The original sense referred to grinding spices for mulled wine. The sense of "ponder" arose from the image of grinding something over in one's mind.
