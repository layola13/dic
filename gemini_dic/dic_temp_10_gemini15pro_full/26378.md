by-lined:/ˈbaɪlaɪnd/
adjective
署名的，附有作者署名的
Having a byline; credited to a named author.
The article was by-lined by a famous journalist. 这篇文章是一位著名记者署名的。
credited, signed, attributed
anonymous, uncredited
词性: 形容词

by-line: /ˈbaɪlaɪn/
noun
署名；作者署名行
A line at the beginning of a news story, magazine article, or book giving the writer's name.
The by-line read "John Smith." 署名写着“约翰·史密斯”。
credit, signature, attribution
anonymity
词性: 名词
