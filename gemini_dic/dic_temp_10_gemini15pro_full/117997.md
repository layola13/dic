memorializes:/məˈmɔːriəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
纪念，使人怀念
serves to commemorate or keep alive the memory of someone or something.
The monument memorializes the soldiers who died in the war. 这座纪念碑是为了纪念在战争中牺牲的士兵。
commemorates, remembers, honors
forgets, ignores
词性:动词
