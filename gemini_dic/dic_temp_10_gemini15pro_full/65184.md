ethological:/ˌiːθəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ /ˌiːθəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
adjective
动物行为学的；生态学的
Relating to the study of animal behavior in natural conditions.
Ethological studies have shown that some animals exhibit altruistic behavior. 动物行为学研究表明，一些动物表现出利他行为。
Ethological research often involves observing animals in their natural habitats.  动物行为学研究通常包括观察动物在其自然栖息地的情况。
synonyms: behavioral, instinctive
antonyms: learned, conditioned
词性: adjective
