annellata:/ˌænəˈleɪtə/ | adj. |环节动物门的；有环纹的 | Having rings or ring-like markings; belonging to the Annelida phylum. | The earthworm is an annellata. 蚯蚓是环节动物。| The annellata specimen showed distinct segmentation.  这个环节动物标本显示出明显的节段。|近义词：segmented, ringed |反义词: unsegmented | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *annellatus*, past participle of *annellare* "to furnish with rings," from *anellus* "little ring," diminutive of *anus* "ring."
