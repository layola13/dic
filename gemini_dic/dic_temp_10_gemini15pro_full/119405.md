microfiche:/ˈmaɪkrəˌfiːʃ/| n.|缩微胶片|A flat piece of film containing microphotographs of the pages of a newspaper, catalogue, or other document.|The library has back issues of the newspaper on microfiche. 图书馆有报纸的缩微胶片合订本。|近义词：microfilm|反义词:hard copy|noun
