fall-under:/ˈfɔːl ˈʌndər/ | phrasal verb | 属于；归入；被归类为 | To belong to a particular group, category, or heading. | This issue falls under the jurisdiction of the federal government.  这个问题属于联邦政府的管辖范围。| Synonyms: belong to, be classified as, be included in, come under | Antonyms: be excluded from, be separate from | verb phrase

fall-under:/ˈfɔːl ˈʌndər/ | phrasal verb | 受…管辖 | To be subject to a particular rule, law, or authority. |  These activities fall under the new regulations. 这些活动受新规章的管辖。| Synonyms: be subject to, be governed by, be controlled by | Antonyms: be exempt from, be independent of | verb phrase
