gorbach:/ˈɡɔːrbætʃ/| n.|戈尔巴乔夫（Mikhail Gorbachev，苏联最后一位领导人）|A Soviet statesman who served as General Secretary of the Communist Party of the Soviet Union from 1985 until 1991.  He is best known for his policies of glasnost (openness) and perestroika (restructuring).|例句：Gorbachev played a key role in ending the Cold War. 戈尔巴乔夫在结束冷战中发挥了关键作用。

例句：Gorbachev's reforms ultimately led to the dissolution of the Soviet Union. 戈尔巴乔夫的改革最终导致了苏联的解体。|近义词：Soviet leader, reformer|反义词: hardliner, conservative|词性:noun
