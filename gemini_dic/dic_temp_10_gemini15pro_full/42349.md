counteradaptation:/ˌkaʊntərˌædæpˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 反适应，对抗适应 | An adaptation in one lineage that occurs in response to an adaptation in another lineage with which it interacts.  It's an evolutionary arms race. | Example 1: The development of thicker shells in snails is a counteradaptation to the stronger beaks of their bird predators.  蜗牛壳变厚是对其鸟类捕食者喙变强的反适应。

Example 2:  The evolution of plant toxins is a counteradaptation to herbivory. 植物毒素的进化是对食草动物的反适应。 | Near Synonyms: countermeasure, response, reaction | Antonyms: adaptation, maladaptation | noun
