facadist:/fəˈsɑːdɪst/| n.|外观设计师；注重外观的人；|A person who designs or specializes in facades of buildings; someone preoccupied with outward appearances.|The architect hired a specialist facadist to design the building's intricate front.  这位建筑师聘请了一位专业的外观设计师来设计建筑复杂的正面。|Synonym: facade designer, exterior architect|Antonym: structural engineer|noun

facadist:/fəˈsɑːdɪst/| n.|虚伪的人；伪君子|Someone who presents a false or superficial appearance to conceal their true nature.|He was a facadist, pretending to be charitable while secretly motivated by greed. 他是个伪君子，假装慈善，暗地里却受贪婪的驱使。|Synonym: hypocrite, pretender, poser|Antonym: genuine person, authentic individual|noun
