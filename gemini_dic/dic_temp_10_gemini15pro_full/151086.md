parakeet:/ˈpærəkiːt/| n. |鹦鹉，长尾小鹦鹉|A small, slender parrot with a long, graduated tail.  These birds are often kept as pets and are known for their vibrant colors and ability to mimic sounds.|例句：My parakeet learned to say "hello." 我的鹦鹉学会说“你好”。|例句：The parakeet flew around the room, chirping happily. 鹦鹉绕着房间飞，快乐地啁啾着。|近义词：parrot, budgerigar (specifically for a type of parakeet)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *perroquet*, ultimately from Spanish *periquito* (meaning "small parrot"), a diminutive of *perico*, probably of imitative origin.
