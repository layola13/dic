importunely:/ˌɪmpɔːrˈtjuːnli/ | adv. |纠缠不休地，再三恳求地|In a persistently annoying or troublesome way; urgently or persistently. |He importunely begged for another chance. 他再三恳求再给他一次机会。|近义词:persistently, incessantly, pressingly|反义词:patiently, respectfully, discreetly|词性:副词
词源: From Late Latin importunus "unfit, inconvenient, troublesome," from in- "not" + portus "harbor, port" (figuratively "rest, refuge"). The original sense was "having no safe harbor," hence "unseasonable, inconvenient," later "troublesome, annoying."
