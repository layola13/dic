hourglass:/ˈaʊərɡlæs/| n.|沙漏；沙漏状物|A device for measuring time, consisting of two glass bulbs connected by a narrow neck, through which sand flows from the upper bulb to the lower one in a fixed amount of time.  |The hourglass on the table indicated that only five minutes remained. 桌上的沙漏显示只剩下五分钟了。|Synonyms: sandglass, sand timer, timer|Antonyms: clock, watch|noun
