influx:/ˈɪnflʌks/| n. |涌入；汇集|An arrival or entry of large numbers of people or things.|例句：The influx of tourists during the summer months strains the town's resources. 夏季大量游客涌入，使该镇的资源紧张。|例句：There has been a large influx of immigrants into the city.  该市涌入了大量移民。|近义词：inflow, inrush, arrival,涌入|反义词: outflow, exodus 流出|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin influxus, from Latin influere "to flow in," from in- "in" + fluere "to flow."
