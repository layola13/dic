dippy:/ˈdɪpi/| adj.|傻的，古怪的，轻微疯狂的|Slightly eccentric or foolish; mildly insane.  Originating from "dip" suggesting a slight mental instability.|She has a dippy sense of humour. 她有一种傻傻的幽默感。|Synonyms: silly, foolish, wacky, eccentric, daft|Antonyms: sensible, wise, serious|adjective

dippy:/ˈdɪpi/| n.|傻瓜，笨蛋|A silly or foolish person.|He's such a dippy sometimes! 他有时候真是个傻瓜！|Synonyms: fool, idiot, nitwit|Antonyms: genius, sage|noun
