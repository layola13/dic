overtediousnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈtiːdiəsnəsɪz/
|n.| 过分冗长乏味; 过于繁琐 |The overtediousnesses of the legal proceedings frustrated everyone involved.  法律程序的冗长乏味让所有参与的人都感到沮丧。|
|English Explanation: The state of being excessively tedious; extreme tediousness.  It refers to an excessive amount of tediousness, often to the point of being unbearable or frustrating.|
|例句：The overtediousnesses of his lectures caused many students to lose interest. 他的讲座过于冗长乏味，导致许多学生失去了兴趣。|
|例句：She complained about the overtediousnesses of the bureaucratic process. 她抱怨官僚程序的繁琐冗长。|
|近义词：tediousness, prolixity, verbosity, dullness|
|反义词：conciseness, brevity, succinctness|
|词性: noun (plural)|
