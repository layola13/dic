electrobiological:/ɪˌlɛktroʊbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
电生物学的
Relating to the electrical activity of living organisms.
Electrobiological studies have shown how electrical signals control muscle contractions. 电生物学研究表明电信号如何控制肌肉收缩。
Electrobiological sensors can detect minute changes in brain activity. 电生物学传感器可以检测到大脑活动的微小变化。
近义词:bioelectrical
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-biological" in some contexts)
词性: adjective
