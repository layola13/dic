bobbin:/ˈbɒbɪn/ (UK) /ˈbɑːbɪn/ (US)| n.|线轴，梭心|A cylindrical device around which thread, yarn, or wire is wound, especially one used in sewing machines or weaving.|The bobbin on my sewing machine ran out of thread. 我的缝纫机上的线轴用完了线。|Synonyms: spool, reel, coil|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps something unwound like a tangle or mess)|noun

bobbin:/ˈbɒbɪn/ (UK) /ˈbɑːbɪn/ (US)| v.|缠绕在线轴上|To wind thread, yarn, or wire onto a bobbin.|She bobbined the thread onto the spool. 她把线缠绕在线轴上。|Synonyms: wind, spool, reel|Antonyms: unwind, unravel|verb
