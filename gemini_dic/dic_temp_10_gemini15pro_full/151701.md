parton:/ˈpɑːrtɒn/ (UK) /ˈpɑːrtɑːn/ (US)| n. |部分子，组分|A parton is a point-like constituent of a hadron, such as a quark or a gluon.  Partons are theorized to be the fundamental components of hadrons, and their interactions are described by quantum chromodynamics (QCD).|例句：The proton is composed of three partons: two up quarks and one down quark. 质子由三个部分子组成：两个上夸克和一个下夸克。

例句：Parton distribution functions (PDFs) describe the probability of finding a parton with a certain momentum fraction inside a hadron. 部分子分布函数 (PDF) 描述了在强子内部找到具有特定动量分数的部分子的概率。|近义词：constituent, component|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
