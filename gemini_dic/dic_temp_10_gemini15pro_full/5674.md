altruist: /ˈæltruɪst/ | n. | 利他主义者；无私的人 | A person who shows unselfish concern for the welfare of others. |  Mother Teresa was known as a great altruist, dedicating her life to helping the poor. 特蕾莎修女是一位伟大的利他主义者，她一生致力于帮助穷人。

例句：
He is an altruist and always puts others before himself. 他是一个利他主义者，总是把别人放在自己前面。

例句：
The altruist donated a large sum of money to the charity.  这位利他主义者向慈善机构捐赠了大笔资金。


近义词: humanitarian, philanthropist, benefactor | 反义词: egoist, narcissist | 词性: noun
