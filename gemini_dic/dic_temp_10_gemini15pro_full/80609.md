goondas:/ˈɡuːndəz/| n. |暴徒，恶棍，歹徒|A hired thug or gangster, especially in India.|The goondas threatened the villagers. 这些暴徒威胁村民。|近义词：thugs, hooligans, gangsters, criminals|反义词:law-enforcers, police|noun

词源:
From Hindi गुंडा (guṇḍā), from Sanskrit गण्ड (gaṇḍa, “lump, swelling”).  Originally referred to a wrestler or club-wielding ruffian.
