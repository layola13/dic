nonreputable:/ˌnɑːnˈrɛpjətəbəl/
adjective
声誉不好的，不值得信赖的
Not reputable; having a bad reputation.
例句：He got his degree from a nonreputable online university. 他从一所声誉不好的网络大学获得了学位。

例句：It's a nonreputable company, known for its shady business practices. 这是一家声誉不好的公司，以其不正当的商业行为而闻名。

近义词：disreputable, shady, questionable, dubious
反义词：reputable, respectable, trustworthy

词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "reputable" (from Latin "reputare" - to think over, consider).  Essentially, "not considered well."
