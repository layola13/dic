interfamilial:/ˌɪntər.fəˈmɪliəl/
adjective
家庭内部的；发生在家庭成员之间的
Occurring within a family; between members of a family.
Interfamilial disputes can be particularly damaging.  家庭内部的纠纷可能特别具有破坏性。
The interfamilial dynamics were complex. 这个家庭内部的动态很复杂。
近义词: intrafamilial
反义词: extrafamilial
词性: adjective
词源: From inter- + familial, from Latin familia "family."
