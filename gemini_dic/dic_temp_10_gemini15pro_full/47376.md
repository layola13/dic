deactuates:/diːˈæktʃueɪts/
verb
使失效，使停止运作
to make something stop working; to disable
The pilot deactuated the autopilot before landing. 飞行员在着陆前关闭了自动驾驶仪。
The system automatically deactivates after a period of inactivity.  系统在一段时间不活动后会自动停用。
synonyms: deactivate, disable, shut down, turn off
antonyms: activate, enable, turn on
词性: 动词
