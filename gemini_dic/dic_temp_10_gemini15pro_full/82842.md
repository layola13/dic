gulfiest:/ˈɡʌlfiɪst/
adjective
最轻信的，最容易上当的
Most gullible; most easily deceived.
That Nigerian prince email scam is so obvious, only the gulfiest person would fall for it.  那个尼日利亚王子邮件骗局太明显了，只有最容易上当受骗的人才会上当。
He's so gulfiest, he'd believe anything you tell him. 他太容易上当了，你说什么他都信。
近义词:naive, credulous, unsuspecting
反义词:skeptical, cynical, discerning
词性:adjective

词源:
From "gullible" + "-est" (superlative suffix).  "Gullible" comes from "gull" (to cheat or fool) + "-ible" (suffix meaning "capable of being"). "Gull," in the sense of "to cheat,"  likely originates from a now obsolete word meaning "young bird," referencing the vulnerability of young birds.
