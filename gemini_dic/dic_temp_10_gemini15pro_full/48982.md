degerminator:/diːˈdʒɜːrmɪneɪtər/
noun
去胚芽器，去芽器
A machine or device used to remove germs, especially the germ from a grain of cereal.
The degerminator efficiently removes the germ from the corn kernels.  去胚芽器有效地去除了玉米粒中的胚芽。
The wheat degerminator is a crucial part of the flour milling process. 小麦去胚芽器是面粉研磨过程中的关键部分。
Synonyms: germ separator, huller (for some applications)
Antonyms: germinator (opposite function)
n.
