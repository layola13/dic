aspire-to:/əˈspaɪər tuː/| phrasal verb | 渴望，追求，立志 | to have a strong desire to achieve or become something |  例句：She aspires to be a doctor. 她渴望成为一名医生。 | 近义词：aim for, strive for, desire, yearn for | 反义词：disregard, neglect | 词性：动词短语

Etymology: From Old French *aspirer, from Latin aspirare "to breathe upon, breathe on, blow gently, pant for," figuratively "to strive after, long for," from ad- "to" (see ad-) + spirare "to breathe" (see spirit (n.)).
