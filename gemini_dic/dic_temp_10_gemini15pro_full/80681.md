gorgelet: /ˈɡɔːrdʒlət/ | n. |小峡谷，峡谷小溪|A small gorge or ravine, often containing a stream. |The hikers discovered a picturesque gorgelet hidden in the mountains.  徒步旅行者在山中发现了一条风景如画的小峡谷。|近义词：ravine, gully, dell |反义词: plain, plateau |词性: noun
Etymology: From French *gorgelette, diminutive of gorge ("throat, gorge").
