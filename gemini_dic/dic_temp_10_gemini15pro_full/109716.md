PHRASE->
level with someone:  对某人坦诚  She leveled with him about her feelings. 她向他坦白了她的感受。
level the playing field: 创造公平竞争的环境 The new rules are designed to level the playing field for smaller businesses. 新规则旨在为小型企业创造公平的竞争环境。
level a charge/accusation/criticism at someone:  对某人提出指控/批评  He leveled a charge of theft against his neighbor. 他指控他的邻居盗窃。
level off/out:  趋于平稳  After a period of rapid growth, sales have leveled off. 经过一段时间的快速增长后，销售额趋于平稳。
be leveled (at someone): 指向某人(通常指武器或批评)  Her criticism was leveled at the management team. 她的批评是针对管理团队的。
