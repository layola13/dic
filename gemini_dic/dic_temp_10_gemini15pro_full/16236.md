banshee:/ˈbænʃiː/| n.|女妖，报丧女妖|A female spirit in Irish folklore whose wailing is believed to foretell death.|The villagers heard the banshee's wail and knew someone would soon die. 村民们听到了女妖的哀号，知道很快就会有人死去。|Synonyms: wraith, phantom, specter|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
