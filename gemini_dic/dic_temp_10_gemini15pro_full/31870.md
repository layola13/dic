cheerlead:/ˈtʃɪrliːd/| v. |为…加油；给…打气|To shout encouragement and approval, typically to support a team or performer.|The crowd cheerleaded their team to victory.  人群为他们的队伍加油助威，最终队伍取得了胜利。|近义词：encourage, support, root for|反义词: discourage, boo|词性:动词
cheerleader:/ˈtʃɪrˌliːdər/| n. |啦啦队长；支持者；鼓吹者|A person, typically a girl or woman, who leads the cheers for a sports team, especially at a high school or college game. Also, someone who actively supports or promotes something or someone.|The cheerleaders performed an impressive routine during halftime. 啦啦队长们在中场休息时表演了一套令人印象深刻的节目。  The CEO is a cheerleader for sustainable business practices. 首席执行官是可持续商业实践的支持者。|近义词：supporter, advocate, booster|反义词: critic, opponent|词性:名词
