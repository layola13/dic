decline:/dɪˈklaɪn/ | v. n. |下降，衰退；拒绝，谢绝|To decrease in quantity, strength, or importance; to politely refuse an invitation or offer.|The company's profits declined by 10% last year.  该公司利润去年下降了10%。|近义词：decrease, refuse, reject, diminish, wane |反义词: increase, accept, rise, grow|verb
decline:/dɪˈklaɪn/ | n. |下降，衰退；斜坡，倾斜|A gradual decrease in quantity, strength, or importance; a downward slope.|The decline in the housing market has affected the entire economy. 房地产市场的衰退影响了整个经济。|近义词：decrease, reduction, downturn, descent |反义词: increase, rise, ascent, incline|noun
