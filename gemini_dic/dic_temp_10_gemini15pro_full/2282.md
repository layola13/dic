adenological: /ˌædɪnəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
腺体的；腺学上的
Relating to the study of glands, or adenology.  Specifically, it pertains to the structure, function, and diseases of glands.
The adenological findings were consistent with a benign tumor.  腺体学检查结果与良性肿瘤一致。

The doctor ordered an adenological examination to assess the patient's thyroid. 医生要求进行腺体学检查以评估患者的甲状腺。

近义词: glandular
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists. One might consider terms related to non-glandular tissues or systems, but these would be context-specific.)
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From adeno- (relating to glands) + -logical (relating to the study of). Adeno- comes from the Greek word 'aden' meaning 'gland'.
