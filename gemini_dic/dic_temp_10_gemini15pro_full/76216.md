futile:/ˈfjuːtaɪl/ /ˈfjuːtəl/| adj.|无用的，徒劳的，无效的|incapable of producing any useful result; pointless|例句：All our efforts proved futile. 我们所有的努力都证明是徒劳的。

例句：It's futile to argue with him.  和他争论是徒劳的。|近义词：useless, pointless, vain, ineffective|反义词：useful, effective, productive|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *futile*, from Latin *fūtīlis* “that easily pours out, leaky,” hence “brittle, fragile, failing easily,” figuratively “vain, empty, futile,” from *fundere* "to pour, melt."
