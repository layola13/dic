castled: /ˈkɑːsəld/ | adj. | 城堡式的，像城堡的；受城堡保护的 | Resembling a castle; fortified like a castle; protected by a castle. |  The castled town stood on a hill overlooking the valley. 城堡小镇坐落在俯瞰山谷的小山上。|  The king retreated to his castled stronghold. 国王撤退到他城堡般的要塞。 | 近义词: fortified, castellated | 反义词: unfortified, undefended | 词性: 形容词

词源: From "castle" + "-ed" suffix, meaning "having the characteristics of". 源于“castle” + “-ed”后缀，意思是“具有……特征的”。
