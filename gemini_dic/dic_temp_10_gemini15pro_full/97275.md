influencers:/ˈɪnfluənsərz/| n.|影响者，有影响力的人|People who have the power to affect the purchasing decisions or opinions of others, typically through social media or other online platforms.|Social media influencers can have a huge impact on their followers. 社交媒体影响者可以对他们的粉丝产生巨大的影响。|Influencers often partner with brands to promote products. 影响者经常与品牌合作推广产品。|synonyms: leaders, trendsetters, opinion leaders, key players|antonyms: followers, subordinates|noun
