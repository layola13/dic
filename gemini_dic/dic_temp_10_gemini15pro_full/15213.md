backdown:/ˈbækˌdaʊn/| n.|让步，放弃，撤回|The government's backdown on the proposed tax increase was welcomed by the public. 政府对拟议增税的让步受到了公众的欢迎。|例句：His backdown avoided a major confrontation. 他的让步避免了一场大的冲突。

|近义词：withdrawal, retreat, concession, surrender|反义词:insistence, persistence|词性:名词

back down:/bæk daʊn/| vi.|让步，放弃，撤回|He refused to back down from his position. 他拒绝放弃自己的立场。|例句：She eventually backed down and apologized. 她最终还是让步并道歉了。|近义词：yield, give in, concede|反义词:persist, insist|词性:动词
