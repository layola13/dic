cadging:/ˈkædʒɪŋ/
verb
乞讨，索取
the act of asking people for things, especially food or money
He was always cadging cigarettes from his friends. 他总是向他的朋友们索要香烟。
synonyms: begging, scrounging, sponging
antonyms: giving, donating, providing
v.
