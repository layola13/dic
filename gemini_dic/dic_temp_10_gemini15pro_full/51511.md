detrimentalities: /ˌdɛtrɪmɛnˈtælɪtiz/ | n. | 损害，不利因素 |  The plural form of "detrimentality," referring to multiple factors or qualities that cause harm or disadvantage.  It emphasizes the various aspects or instances of something being detrimental. |  The report highlighted the detrimentalities of smoking on public health.  该报告强调了吸烟对公众健康的不利影响。 | The various detrimentalities of the new policy quickly became apparent. 新政策的各种不利之处很快就显现出来了。 | Synonyms: disadvantages, drawbacks, harms, downsides | Antonyms: advantages, benefits, assets | noun
