frostbird:/ˈfrɒstbɜːd/ (UK) /ˈfrɔːstbɜːrd/ (US)| n.|冬候鸟；耐寒鸟|A bird that overwinters in a cold climate, or a bird that is adapted to cold environments.|例句：The frostbird flew south for the winter. 这只冬候鸟飞往南方过冬。

例句：Frostbirds are well adapted to survive harsh winters. 耐寒鸟很适应在严冬中生存。

近义词：winter bird, hardy bird|反义词: summer bird, migratory bird|词性:noun
