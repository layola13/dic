overpester: /ˌoʊvərˈpɛstər/ | v. | 过分纠缠，过度烦扰 | To bother or annoy someone excessively or persistently. |  He overpestered his neighbor with requests to borrow tools. 他不停地向邻居借工具，把邻居烦透了。

| The children overpestered their parents for a new video game. 孩子们不断纠缠父母要买新的电子游戏。

| 近义词: badger, harass, hound, pester | 反义词: soothe, comfort |词性: 动词
