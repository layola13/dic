forweary:/fɔːrˈwɪəri/
adjective
极其疲惫的；筋疲力尽的
extremely tired; exhausted
The forweary travelers finally reached their destination. 筋疲力尽的旅行者终于到达了目的地。
After a long day's work, I felt forweary.  一天漫长的工作后，我感到筋疲力尽。
近义词: exhausted, worn out, fatigued, spent
反义词: energetic, refreshed, invigorated
词性: adjective
词源:  From Middle English *forweri, from Old English forwērig (“very tired, exhausted”), from for- (“thoroughly, excessively”) + wērig (“tired”). Cognate with German verwirrt (“confused, bewildered”).
