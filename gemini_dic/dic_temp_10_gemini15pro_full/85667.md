hauynite:/ˈhɔɪˌnaɪt/ | n. | 蓝方石 | A rare mineral, a tectosilicate of sodium, calcium, and aluminum with sulfate and sulfide, found in igneous rocks.  | Hauynite is a vibrant blue mineral prized by collectors. 蓝方石是一种充满活力的蓝色矿物，受到收藏家的珍视。 | Hauynite crystals can be found embedded in volcanic rock. 蓝方石晶体可以在火山岩中找到。| 近义词: nosean, lazurite | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: Named after French crystallographer René Just Haüy (1743-1822).
