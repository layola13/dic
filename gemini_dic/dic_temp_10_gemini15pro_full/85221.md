harl:/hɑːrl/ | v. | 拉, 拖, 拽 | To drag or pull with effort. | He harled the heavy box across the floor. 他吃力地把沉重的箱子拖过地板。 | The fisherman harled the net onto the boat. 渔夫把网拖到船上。 | 近义词: drag, haul, pull, tug | 反义词: push | 词性: 动词

词源: From Middle English *harlen, from Old Norse *harla "to drag, pull," related to Old English herian "to praise, glorify," from Proto-Germanic *harjaz "army."  The semantic shift likely occurred through the notion of "drawing along" a crowd or army.
