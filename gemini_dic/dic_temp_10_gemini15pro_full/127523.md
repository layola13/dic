necrologic:/ˌnɛkrəˈlɒdʒɪk/ | adj. | 讣告的，死亡的 | Relating to or constituting an obituary. |  The necrologic tone of the announcement made it clear the company was in serious trouble.  该公告的讣告语气清楚地表明该公司陷入了严重的困境。 |  obituary, mortuary | celebratory, vital | adjective

necrology:/nɪˈkrɒlədʒi/ | n. | 讣告；死亡名单；死亡统计 | A list of people who have died, especially in a particular period or place; an obituary. |  The church published a necrology of its members who had died that year. 教堂公布了当年去世的成员的讣告。 | obituary, death list, mortality statistics | birth list, register of births | noun
