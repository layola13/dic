facading:/fəˈseɪdɪŋ/
n.|正面装饰，虚假的外表|presenting a false appearance; concealing something unpleasant behind a superficial respectability or attractiveness|The company's commitment to environmental protection was just facading, as they continued to pollute the river.  这家公司对环境保护的承诺只是表面文章，因为他们继续污染河流。|synonyms: dissembling, masquerading, posturing, feigning|antonyms: revealing, exposing, unmasking|noun

facading:/fəˈseɪdɪŋ/
v.|用…作正面; 给…装正面|covering with a facade; giving a false or misleading appearance to|They were facading their true intentions with promises of reform.  他们用改革的承诺掩盖他们真正的意图。|synonyms: disguising, camouflaging, cloaking|antonyms: revealing, exposing, unmasking|verb
