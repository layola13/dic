evangelical:/ˌiːvænˈdʒɛlɪk(ə)l/ /ˌivænˈdʒɛlɪkəl/
adjective|福音派的；热衷于传播福音的|Of or relating to the Christian gospel, especially the four Gospels of the New Testament.  Characterized by a belief in the Bible as the inspired word of God, the importance of personal conversion and faith, and the duty of spreading the Christian message.| Many evangelical Christians believe in the importance of missionary work. 许多福音派基督徒相信传教工作的重要性。|近义词：fundamentalist, Bible-believing|反义词:liberal, secular|词性:adjective

evangelical:/ˌiːvænˈdʒɛlɪk(ə)l/ /ˌivænˈdʒɛlɪkəl/
noun|福音派信徒；福音派基督徒|A person who believes in the Christian gospel, especially one who emphasizes the importance of personal conversion and faith, and the duty of spreading the Christian message.|He is a well-known evangelical in his community. 他是社区里著名的福音派信徒。|近义词：fundamentalist, born-again Christian|反义词: none (no direct antonym for a person)|词性:noun
