overconsiderate:/ˌoʊvərˌkənˈsɪdərɪt/| adj.| 过分体贴的，过分周到的| Showing excessive consideration for the needs and feelings of others; too careful not to hurt or inconvenience others. |  She was so overconsiderate that she ended up neglecting her own needs. 她太过于体贴别人，以至于最终忽略了自己的需求。|Synonyms: excessively thoughtful, overly solicitous, too careful, overattentive| Antonyms: inconsiderate, thoughtless, selfish, insensitive|adjective
