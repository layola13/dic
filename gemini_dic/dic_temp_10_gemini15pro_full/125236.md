multicuspid:/ˌmʌltɪˈkʌspɪd/| adj.|多尖的，多瓣的|Having multiple cusps or points, particularly referring to teeth or heart valves.|The tricuspid valve is a multicuspid valve located in the heart.  三尖瓣是位于心脏的多尖瓣。|The dentist identified the tooth as multicuspid. 牙医确认这颗牙齿是多尖齿。|Synonym: multipointed, polycuspid|Antonym: bicuspid, tricuspid|adjective
