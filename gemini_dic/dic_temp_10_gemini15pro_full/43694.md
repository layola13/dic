creditableness:/ˈkrɛdɪtəblnəs/
n.
可信度，可靠性
The quality of being believable or trustworthy.
His creditableness was enhanced by his honesty and integrity.  他的诚实和正直增强了他的可信度。
credibility, reliability, trustworthiness
dishonesty, unreliability, untrustworthiness
