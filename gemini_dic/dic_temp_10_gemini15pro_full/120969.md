mischief:/ˈmɪstʃɪf/| n. |恶作剧，淘气，损害，伤害| Playful but often annoying behavior; trouble or damage caused by something.| The children were full of mischief. 孩子们很淘气。| synonyms: naughtiness, prank, trouble, harm | antonyms: obedience, goodness | noun

mischievous:/ˈmɪstʃɪvəs/| adj. |淘气的，恶作剧的，有害的| Showing a fondness for causing trouble in a playful way; causing or likely to cause harm.|  He has a mischievous grin. 他露出了顽皮的笑容。| synonyms: naughty, playful, troublesome, harmful | antonyms: well-behaved, harmless | adjective
