declutter:/diːˈklʌtər/
verb
清理，整理（尤指清除不需要的东西）
To remove unnecessary items from (a place).
I spent the weekend decluttering the house. 我周末都在清理房子。
近义词：tidy, organize, clean, neaten
反义词：clutter, mess up
词性：verb

declutter:/diːˈklʌtər/
noun
清理，整理
The act of removing unnecessary items from a place.
The declutter of my office took longer than I expected.  清理我的办公室比我预想的要久。
近义词：tidying, organizing, cleaning
反义词：cluttering, messing up
词性：noun
