brokenness:/ˈbroʊkənnɪs/| n.|破碎，破损；（精神上的）崩溃，颓丧；贫困|The state of being broken, either literally or figuratively, often referring to a state of emotional or spiritual distress, or to poverty.|The earthquake caused widespread brokenness and devastation. 地震造成了大范围的破坏和毁灭。|近义词：fracture, rupture, fragmentation, ruin, despair, poverty|反义词：wholeness, completeness, integrity, healing, prosperity|词性:名词

词源: From "broken" + "-ness".  "Broken" comes from the Old English "brocen," past participle of "brecan" (to break).  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
