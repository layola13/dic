overstrives:/ˌoʊvərˈstraɪvz/| v.|过度努力，力求过度|To try too hard, often to the point of exhaustion or counterproductivity.|He overstrives to impress his boss, often working late into the night and making mistakes due to fatigue.|近义词：overexert, overwork, strain, labor|反义词: relax, underachieve|词性:动词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "strive" (verb meaning "to make great efforts to achieve or obtain something")
