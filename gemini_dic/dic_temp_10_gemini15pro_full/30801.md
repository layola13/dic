cetyl:/ˈsiːtɪl/| adj.|鲸蜡基的；十六烷基的|Relating to or derived from spermaceti, a waxy substance found in the head of the sperm whale, specifically referring to a 16-carbon chain.|Cetyl alcohol is a common ingredient in cosmetics.  鲸蜡醇是化妆品中的常见成分。|Synonyms: hexadecyl|Antonyms: None|Adjective
