fruticose:/ˈfruːtɪkoʊs/| adj.|灌木状的，像灌木的|Resembling a shrub; shrubby.  Having a branched, bushy structure.|Example: The lichen had a fruticose growth form, branching out like a miniature tree.  地衣呈灌木状，像一棵微型树一样分支生长。

Example: Fruticose lichens are often found in pristine environments. 灌木状地衣通常出现在原始环境中。|近义词：shrubby, bushy, branching|反义词:crustose, foliose|词性:adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fruticōsus*, from *frutex* ("shrub") + *-ōsus* (suffix forming adjectives of abundance).
