glary: /ˈɡlɛəri/ (US), /ˈɡlɛːri/ (UK)| adj.|耀眼的，刺眼的|Having or causing a strong, unpleasant glare.  Characterized by excessive or dazzling light.|The glary headlights of the oncoming car blinded me momentarily.  迎面而来的汽车耀眼的车灯使我一时失明。|Synonyms: dazzling, blinding, glaring, harsh, intense|Antonyms: soft, dim, subdued|adjective
