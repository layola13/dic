convertite:/ˈkɒnvɜːtaɪt/ (UK), /ˈkɑːnvərtaɪt/ (US)| n.|皈依者，改变信仰者|A person who has been converted to a particular religion or belief.|She was a convertite to Buddhism after years of studying Eastern philosophy. 她研究了多年的东方哲学后皈依了佛教。|Example: He was a convertite to Christianity after a life-changing experience. 他在一次改变人生的经历后皈依了基督教。|Synonyms: proselyte, neophyte|Antonyms: apostate, heretic|noun
