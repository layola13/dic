flare-up:/ˈfleər ʌp/ (US) /ˈfleə(r) ʌp/ (UK)| n.|爆发，突发，加剧|A sudden increase in intensity or activity, especially of a disease or conflict.|The patient experienced a flare-up of his arthritis after exercising. 病人在运动后关节炎发作了。|近义词：outbreak, eruption, resurgence, aggravation|反义词: remission, abatement|词性:名词

flare-up:/ˈfleər ʌp/ (US) /ˈfleə(r) ʌp/ (UK)| vi.|突然爆发，突然加剧|To suddenly increase in intensity or activity, especially of a disease or conflict.|Her eczema flared up after she used a new detergent. 她用了新的洗涤剂后湿疹发作了。|近义词：erupt, worsen, intensify|反义词: subside, improve|词性:动词
