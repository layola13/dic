leitmotifs:/ˈlaɪtmoʊˌtiːfs/
noun
主导动机;  leitmotif的复数形式
A recurrent theme throughout a musical or literary composition, associated with a particular person, idea, or situation.
Wagner's operas are full of leitmotifs. 瓦格纳的歌剧充满了主导动机。
synonyms: theme, motif, subject, melody, strain, tune, refrain, chorus, air, phrase, figure, idea, motive, recurring theme, recurring image
antonyms: N/A
