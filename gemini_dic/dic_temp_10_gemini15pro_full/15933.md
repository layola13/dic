balmify: /bɑːmɪfaɪ/ /ˈbɑːməˌfaɪ/ | vt. |  embalming, preserving a dead body | To preserve a corpse from decay, especially by treating it with spices and drugs. |  The ancient Egyptians were experts at balmifying their pharaohs. 古埃及人非常擅长将他们的法老木乃伊化。

The body was balmified and placed in a sarcophagus.  尸体经过防腐处理后被放置在石棺中。 | 近义词: embalm, preserve, mummify | 反义词: decompose, rot, decay | 词性: verb
词源: From Late Latin balsamificare, from balsamum "balsam" + -ificare "-ify."
