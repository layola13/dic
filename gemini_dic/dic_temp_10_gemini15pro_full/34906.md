cliquishness:/ˈkliːkɪʃnəs/| n.|小集团主义，排外性|The tendency to form or associate with exclusive groups; snobbishness.|The cliquishness of the art world can be intimidating for newcomers. 艺术圈的小集团主义可能会让新人感到害怕。|近义词：exclusivity, insularity, clannishness|反义词：inclusiveness, openness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From clique + -ishness.  Clique (meaning a small, exclusive group of people) comes from French, where it originally meant a "latch" or "snap" (referring to the close-knit nature of the group).
