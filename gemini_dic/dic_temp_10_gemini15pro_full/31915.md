chef-d'oeuvre:/ˌʃef ˈdɜːvrə/ (US), /ˌʃef ˈdɜːvʁə/ (UK)| n.|杰作；代表作|A masterpiece; a supreme achievement, especially in art or literature.|"This painting is considered the artist's chef-d'oeuvre." 这幅画被认为是这位艺术家的杰作。|Synonyms: masterpiece, magnum opus, tour de force, classic|Antonyms: failure, flop, dud|noun

chef-d'oeuvre:/ˌʃef ˈdɜːvrə/ (US), /ˌʃef ˈdɜːvʁə/ (UK)| n.|代表作；最佳作品|A work of outstanding skill or artistry.|"Her latest novel is a true chef-d'oeuvre." 她最新的小说是真正的杰作。|Synonyms: masterwork, pièce de résistance|Antonyms: mediocrity, failure|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French, literally "chief of work." "Chef" means "head" or "chief," and "d'oeuvre" means "of work" or "work."
