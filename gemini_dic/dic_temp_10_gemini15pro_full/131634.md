noncomprehension:/ˌnɒnkɒmprɪˈhenʃn/ | n. | 不理解，缺乏理解力 | Lack of understanding; inability to comprehend. |  Her noncomprehension of the situation led to further problems. 她对情况的不理解导致了进一步的问题。|  He stared at the equation with utter noncomprehension. 他茫然地看着这个方程式，完全无法理解。 | 近义词: incomprehension, misunderstanding, bewilderment | 反义词: comprehension, understanding, grasp | 词性: 名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + comprehension (from Latin comprehensio, meaning "a grasping, seizing, or understanding")
