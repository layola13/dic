nonclericals:/ˌnɒnˈklɛrɪkəlz/
noun
非神职人员；俗人
People who are not members of the clergy or other religious orders.
The nonclericals in the town outnumbered the clergy.  这个镇上的非神职人员比神职人员多。
The union represented both clericals and nonclericals.  该工会同时代表神职人员和非神职人员。
synonyms: laypeople, laity
antonyms: clerics, clergy
