inlard:/ɪnˈlɑːrd/
verb
使充满脂肪; 肥育 |To fill or saturate with fat; make rich or fatty.|The chef inlarded the roast with strips of bacon. 厨师用培根条肥育烤肉。|
近义词:fatten, enrich
反义词:lean, trim
词性:v.

inlard:/ɪnˈlɑːrd/
noun
动物油脂, lard |Animal fat, especially hog fat; lard.|The recipe called for inlard, but I substituted butter.  食谱要求用猪油，但我用黄油代替了。|
近义词:lard, fat
反义词:N/A
词性:n.
