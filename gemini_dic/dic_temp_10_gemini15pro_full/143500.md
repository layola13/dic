outglooms:/aʊtˈɡluːmz/
verb
使...黯然失色；胜过...的忧郁
to be gloomier than; to surpass in gloominess.
The storm clouds outgloomed the already gray landscape.  暴风云使原本灰暗的景色更加黯淡。
The poet's later works outgloomed his earlier, more joyful poems.  这位诗人后期的作品比他早期更快乐的诗歌更显忧郁。
近义词:overshadow, eclipse, darken
反义词:brighten, cheer, illuminate
词性:v.
