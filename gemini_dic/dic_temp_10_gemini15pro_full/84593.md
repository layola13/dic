hampering:/ˈhæmpərɪŋ/
verb
阻碍，妨碍
hindering or impeding the movement, progress, or action of something or someone.
The heavy rain was hampering rescue efforts. 大雨阻碍了救援工作的进行。
hampering:/ˈhæmpərɪŋ/
adjective
阻碍的，妨碍的
hindering or impeding the movement, progress, or action of something or someone.
The hampering effect of the bureaucracy was felt by everyone. 官僚主义的阻碍作用每个人都感受到了。


Etymology:  From hamper (n.), from Anglo-French hanaper "case for a goblet," from Old North French hanapier, from hanap "goblet, drinking vessel."  The original meaning of hamper (n.) was a large basket, often with a cover, used for carrying food or other goods. This sense evolved to mean "to impede or restrain" as if by entanglement in such a basket.
