indemnity:/ɪnˈdɛmnɪti/ | n. |赔偿，保障，豁免 |  Protection against loss or financial burden, typically in the form of a promise of payment or reimbursement; security against legal responsibility for one's actions.| The insurance company paid him an indemnity for his losses in the fire. 保险公司赔偿了他在火灾中的损失。 |synonyms: compensation, restitution, reparation, reimbursement | antonyms: penalty, forfeiture, loss | noun
