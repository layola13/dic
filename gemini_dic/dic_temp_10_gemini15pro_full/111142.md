lithaemia: /lɪˈθiːmiə/ | n. | 锂血症 |  A condition characterized by an abnormally high level of lithium in the blood.  This typically occurs as a side effect of lithium medication, which is used to treat bipolar disorder. |  The patient's lithaemia was closely monitored during his treatment. 病人在治疗期间密切监测锂血症。 |  Hyperlithaemia | Hypothermia | noun
