hyri:/ˈhaɪraɪ/
noun
hyri (复数 hyris)
a freshwater mussel of the family Hyriidae, found in South America, Australia, and New Zealand.  These mussels are often large and some species produce pearls.
Hyris are found in rivers and lakes across Australia.  They play a vital role in the ecosystem.
近义词:freshwater mussel, bivalve
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 terrestrial animal 陆生动物)
词性:n.


词源:
From the Indigenous Australian language (Wiradjuri) word *hyri* meaning mussel.
