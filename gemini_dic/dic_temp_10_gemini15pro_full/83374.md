gyroscope: /ˈdʒaɪrəˌskoʊp/ (US) /ˈdʒaɪrəskəʊp/ (UK)| n. |陀螺仪，回转仪|An instrument containing a wheel mounted so that its axis can turn freely in any direction, used to maintain equilibrium or to detect and measure angular motion.|The gyroscope in the airplane helps maintain stability during flight. 飞机上的陀螺仪有助于在飞行中保持稳定。|Synonyms: gyro, stabilizer|Antonyms: N/A|noun

gyroscopes: /ˈdʒaɪrəˌskoʊps/ (US) /ˈdʒaɪrəskəʊps/ (UK)| n. |陀螺仪（复数）|Plural form of gyroscope.|The ship uses multiple gyroscopes for navigation.  这艘船使用多个陀螺仪进行导航。|Synonyms: gyros, stabilizers|Antonyms: N/A|noun
