endolymph: /ˈɛndəʊlɪmf/ (UK) /ˈɛndoʊlɪmf/ (US) | n. | 内淋巴 | The fluid contained in the membranous labyrinth of the inner ear.  It is important for hearing and balance. | The endolymph plays a crucial role in transmitting sound vibrations to the brain. 内淋巴在将声音振动传递到大脑中起着至关重要的作用。

The composition of the endolymph is carefully regulated by the inner ear. 内耳仔细地调节内淋巴的成分。 | 近义词: None (highly specialized term) | 反义词: perilymph | 词性: noun
