nonofficials:/ˌnɒnəˈfɪʃəlz/ /ˌnɑːnəˈfɪʃəlz/
noun
非官方人士；非官员
People who do not hold an official position in a government or organization.
The nonofficials on the committee voiced their concerns. 委员会中的非官方人士表达了他们的担忧。
Synonyms: civilians, laypeople, private citizens
Antonyms: officials, authorities, office-bearers
词性: 名词
