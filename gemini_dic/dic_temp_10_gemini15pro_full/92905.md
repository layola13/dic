ideals:/aɪˈdiːəlz/| n.|理想，理念；典范|a principle or standard of perfection or high moral value; a person or thing regarded as perfect or close to perfect|例句：He has high ideals. 他有崇高的理想。

例句：She is a woman of strong ideals. 她是一个有着坚定理想的女性。

近义词：principles, values, standards, beliefs, aspirations, goals, objectives|反义词：realities, practicalities|词性：名词

Etymology:
From Late Middle English ideal, from Late Latin ideālis (“existing in idea”), from Latin idea (“idea, form, kind”).  Ultimately from Ancient Greek ἰδέα (idéa, “form, pattern, idea”), from εἶδον (eîdon, “I saw”).
