countercomplaints:/ˌkaʊntər kəmˈpleɪnts/
noun
反诉，反抱怨
An accusation brought by a defendant against a plaintiff in a legal action.
The company filed countercomplaints against its former employees.  这家公司对其前雇员提起了反诉。
synonyms: counterclaim, countersuit
antonyms: complaint, accusation
n.
