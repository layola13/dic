gearing-up:/ˈɡɪərɪŋ ʌp/ | n. | 准备；预备；加速；加紧；增加 | The process of preparing something or increasing its activity or intensity. | The company is gearing up for a major expansion into new markets.  公司正在为大举进军新市场做准备。 | synonyms: preparing, readying, ramping up, stepping up, accelerating | antonyms: slowing down, winding down, scaling back | noun
