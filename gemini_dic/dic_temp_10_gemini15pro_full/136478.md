nonrevolving:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈvɒl.vɪŋ/
adjective
非循环的，非周转的
Not revolving; not subject to periodic renewal or replacement.
The bank offers nonrevolving credit for specific purchases, meaning you borrow a fixed amount and repay it over a set period.  这家银行为特定购买提供非循环信贷，这意味着你借入固定金额并在设定期限内偿还。
The museum received a nonrevolving grant for its new exhibition.  博物馆收到了用于新展览的非循环赠款。
near synonyms: fixed, non-recurring, one-time
antonyms: revolving, recurring, renewable
词性: 形容词
