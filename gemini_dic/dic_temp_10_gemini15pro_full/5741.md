always: /ˈɔːlweɪz/ | adv. | 总是，一直，永远 | An adverb indicating something happens or is true at all times or on every occasion. | He always arrives early for meetings. 他总是早到开会。

I always eat breakfast. 我总是吃早餐。
She always goes to bed early. 她总是早睡。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
He is always complaining. 他总是抱怨。
She is always asking questions. 她总是问问题。(This usage often implies annoyance.)

She has always been kind to me. 她一直对我很好。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
