misplanning:/ˌmɪsˈplænɪŋ/
noun
计划失误，筹划不周
the act or process of planning badly or incorrectly
The project's failure was due to misplanning and a lack of communication.  这个项目的失败是由于计划不周和缺乏沟通造成的。
miscalculation, mismanagement, bad planning
foresight, good planning
n.
