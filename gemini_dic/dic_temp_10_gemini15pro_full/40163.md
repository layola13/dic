contendingly:/kənˈtɛndɪŋli/
adverb
争辩地，竞争地
In a manner characterized by contention; argumentatively or competitively.
The two teams battled contendingly for the championship.  两支队伍为冠军而激烈竞争。
He argued contendingly that his solution was the best. 他争辩说他的解决方案是最好的。
近义词: competitively, argumentatively, pugnaciously, aggressively
反义词: cooperatively, agreeably, harmoniously, peacefully
词性: adverb
