nonskaters:/ˈnɒnˈskeɪtəz/ (British), /ˈnɑːnˈskeɪtərz/ (American)
noun
非溜冰者；不会溜冰的人
People who do not skate or are not skilled at skating.
The nonskaters were huddled together at the edge of the rink, watching the others glide effortlessly across the ice.  非溜冰者们挤在溜冰场的边缘，看着其他人毫不费力地在冰面上滑行。
The rink offered special sessions for nonskaters to learn the basics.  溜冰场为非溜冰者提供学习基本技巧的特别课程。
Synonym: nonskating people
Antonym: skaters
词性: 名词
