nonhomogeneously:/ˌnɒnhɒməˈdʒiːniəsli/ | adv. | 非均匀地 | In a manner that is not uniform throughout; having different properties or composition in different parts. | The particles were distributed nonhomogeneously throughout the solution. 颗粒在整个溶液中分布不均匀。| The temperature is nonhomogeneously distributed across the surface. 温度在表面上分布不均匀。 | 近义词: unevenly, heterogeneously | 反义词: homogeneously, uniformly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From non- (not) + homogeneous + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Homogeneous" comes from the Greek "homogenes," meaning "of the same kind."
