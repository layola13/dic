backyard:/ˌbækˈjɑːrd/ | n. | 后院；后庭 | The children were playing in the backyard. 孩子们在后院玩耍。|  A private area behind a house, often enclosed.  房屋后面的私人区域，通常是封闭的。 |  We had a barbecue in our backyard last weekend. 上周末我们在后院烧烤了。| garden, yard | frontyard | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From back + yard.  Attested since the late 14th century.  Originally referred to a yard behind a building, often used for domestic purposes.  The metaphorical sense of "an area of expertise or influence" developed later.
