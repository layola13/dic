cherry-picked: /ˈtʃɛri pɪkt/
cherry-picked: adj.
cherry-picked: 精心挑选的；优选的
cherry-picked: Selected carefully to appear better than the average; selectively chosen.
cherry-picked: The company was accused of using cherry-picked data to support its claims.  这家公司被指控使用精心挑选的数据来支持其主张。
cherry-picked:  He cherry-picked examples to suit his argument. 他精心挑选了一些例子来支持他的论点。
cherry-picked: selective, hand-picked, chosen
cherry-picked: random, unselected, unbiased
cherry-picked: adjective
