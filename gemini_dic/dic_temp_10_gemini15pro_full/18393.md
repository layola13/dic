belonged:/bɪˈlɔːŋd/
verb
属于(过去式)
past tense of "belong"; used to indicate past possession, association, or fitting in.
The car belonged to my grandfather. 这辆车曾经属于我的祖父。
That book belonged on the top shelf. 那本书本来应该放在最上层的书架上。
近义词:possessed, owned, pertained
反义词:lacked
