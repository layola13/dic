foolish:/ˈfuːlɪʃ/| adj.|愚蠢的；傻的|Lacking good sense or judgment.|It was foolish of him to trust that stranger. 他相信那个陌生人真是太愚蠢了。|Synonyms: silly, unwise, imprudent, senseless, absurd, ridiculous|Antonyms: wise, sensible, prudent, intelligent, clever|adjective
