domeless:/ˈdoʊmlɪs/| adj.|无圆顶的，无穹顶的|Lacking a dome.  It refers to something that typically would have a dome, but doesn't.|The domeless building looked strange against the skyline.  这座无圆顶的建筑在天际线下显得格外奇怪。|The observatory was rendered domeless after the storm.  暴风雨过后，天文台的圆顶不见了。|roofless, open-topped|domed|adjective
