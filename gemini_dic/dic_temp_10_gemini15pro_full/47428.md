deadpanners:/ˈdedˌpænərz/
noun
冷面笑匠; 不为所动的人
Performers who maintain a deadpan expression, especially while delivering comedic lines or performing absurd actions.  People who remain expressionless, especially in humorous or surprising situations.
The deadpanners expertly delivered their lines without cracking a smile.  这些冷面笑匠熟练地说着台词，却连一丝笑容都没有。
He's a natural deadpanner; nothing seems to faze him. 他天生就是个冷面笑匠，似乎没有什么能让他动容。
synonyms: poker face, stoic, impassive
antonyms: expressive, emotional, demonstrative
