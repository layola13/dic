paradoxist:/ˈpærəˌdɑːksɪst/
n.
悖论者；诡辩家
Someone who uses or believes in paradoxes.
He's a bit of a paradoxist, always arguing seemingly contradictory points. 他有点像个悖论者，总是争论看似矛盾的观点。
Synonyms:  contradictor,  enigmatist
Antonyms:  conformist,  dogmatist
