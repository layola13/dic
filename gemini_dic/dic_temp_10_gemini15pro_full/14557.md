avertissement:/a.vɛʁ.tis.mɑ̃/ |n.|警告，预告，通知|A warning or notice given beforehand. |The government issued an avertissement about the dangers of smoking. 政府发布了关于吸烟危害的警告。|Near synonyms: warning, caution, notice|Antonyms:  encouragement, all-clear|noun
Etymology-> From Old French *avertiss-, present participle stem of avertir "to warn," from Vulgar Latin *adverterire, from Latin advertere "to turn toward, direct one's attention to," from ad- "to" + vertere "to turn."
