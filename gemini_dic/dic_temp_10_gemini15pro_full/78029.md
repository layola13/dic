gentrifying:/ˈdʒɛntrɪfaɪɪŋ/
verb
仕绅化；贵族化；中产阶级化
The process of renovating and improving a house or district so that it conforms to middle-class taste.
The area is gentrifying rapidly, with new cafes and boutiques opening up.  这个地区正在快速地仕绅化，新的咖啡馆和精品店纷纷开业。
gentrification, urban renewal, upgrading, improving
displacement, deterioration, decline
v.
