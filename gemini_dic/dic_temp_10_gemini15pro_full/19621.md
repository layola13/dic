bigwiggedness:/ˌbɪɡˈwɪɡɪdnəs/| n. |自命不凡；摆架子|The state or quality of being a bigwig; pompous or self-important behavior.|His bigwiggedness was evident in the way he addressed the staff.|Synonyms: pomposity, arrogance, haughtiness, self-importance, superciliousness|Antonyms: humility, modesty, humbleness|noun


Etymology:  From bigwig + -ed + -ness.  "Bigwig" itself dates back to the 17th century, referring to important people who wore large wigs.
