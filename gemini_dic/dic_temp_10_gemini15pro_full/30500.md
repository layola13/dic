centuple:/ˈsɛntjʊpəl/ /ˈsɛntuːpəl/
verb|使成一百倍，增加到一百倍|To multiply or increase by one hundred.|The investment centupled his initial capital. 这项投资使他的初始资本增加了一百倍。|近义词:multiply by one hundred|反义词:divide by one hundred|词性:verb
centuple:/ˈsɛntjʊpəl/ /ˈsɛntuːpəl/
noun|一百倍的数或量|A number or amount one hundred times greater than another.|The centuple of 10 is 1000. 10的一百倍是1000。|近义词:hundredfold|反义词:one hundredth|词性:noun
