inconvertibility:/ˌɪnkənˌvɜːrtɪˈbɪləti/
n.|不可兑换性，不可转变性|The state or quality of not being convertible, especially of currency or assets, into another form, such as another currency, gold, or goods.|Due to the inconvertibility of the currency, it was difficult for travelers to exchange their money.|non-convertibility, non-exchangeability|convertibility, exchangeability|noun
