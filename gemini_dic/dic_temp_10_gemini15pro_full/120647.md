mis-routes:/ˌmɪsˈruːts/ | v. | 误送，错误路由 | To send (something, especially a shipment or communication) to the wrong destination or along the wrong path. | The package was mis-routed and ended up in Alaska. 包裹被误送，最终到了阿拉斯加。| redirect incorrectly, send to the wrong address, misdirect | route correctly, deliver correctly | verb
mis-routes:/ˌmɪsˈruːts/ | n. | 错误路由，误送 | An instance of something being sent to the wrong destination or along the wrong path. | The mis-routes caused significant delays in delivery.  这些错误路由导致了交货的严重延误。 | wrong routing, incorrect delivery, misdirection | correct routing, accurate delivery | noun
