non-exemplary:/ˌnɑːnɪɡˈzɛmpləri/ | adj. | 非典范的；非示范性的；不值得效仿的 | Not serving as a good example; not typical or representative. |  This is a non-exemplary case and should not be followed.  这是一个非典范的案例，不应效仿。 | synonyms: atypical, unusual, irregular, aberrant, deviant | antonyms: exemplary, typical, representative, model, standard | adjective

Etymology -> From non- + exemplary.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "exemplary" comes from the Latin "exemplum," meaning "example."
