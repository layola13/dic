orogen:/ˈɔːrədʒən/ | n. | 造山带 | A belt of the earth's crust involved in mountain formation, usually along the margins of tectonic plates. | The Himalayas are a classic example of an orogen. 喜马拉雅山脉是造山带的典型例子。| synonym: mountain belt, orogenic belt | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps 'stable craton') | noun

orogeny:/ɔːˈrɒdʒəni/ | n. | 造山运动 | The process of mountain formation, especially by the folding and faulting of the earth's crust. | The Alpine orogeny created the Alps.  阿尔卑斯造山运动形成了阿尔卑斯山脉。 | synonym: mountain building | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'erosion') | noun
