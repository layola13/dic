The impartment of his wisdom to his followers was his greatest legacy.  将他智慧传授给追随者是他最大的遗产。|近义词：communication, conveyance, transmission, bestowal|反义词: withholding, concealment|词性:名词

Etymology: From the verb "impart," from late Middle English *emparten*, from Old French empartir "to share, divide," from Latin *impartire* "to share, bestow," from *in-* "into" + *partire* "to divide, share."
