nonsibilances:/ˌnɒnsɪbɪlənts/ |n. |非咝擦音|Sounds that are not sibilants, meaning they are produced without forcing air through a narrow channel in the mouth.  These are all the consonants except /s/, /z/, /ʃ/, /ʒ/, /tʃ/, and /dʒ/. |The word "plot" contains only nonsibilances.  这个词“plot”只包含非咝擦音。|Near Synonyms: non-sibilant consonants|Antonyms: sibilances|noun
