cyamelide:/saɪˈæməlaɪd/ | n. |氰氨化物，三聚氰酸|A white amorphous powder, (HNCO)3, a polymer of cyanic acid.  It is formed by the spontaneous polymerization of liquid cyanic acid, and is also obtained by other reactions involving cyanic acid derivatives. It is insoluble in water and other common solvents, and is used as a stabilizer for explosives.|Cyamelide is formed by the trimerization of cyanic acid.|近义词: cyanuric acid |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
