glasnost:/ˈɡlɑːznɒst/ (US) /ˈɡlæznɒst/ (UK)| n.|公开性，透明度|A policy of openness and transparency in the activities of government institutions and freedom of information.|例句：
Glasnost led to greater freedom of expression in the Soviet Union.
公开性导致苏联更大的言论自由。

例句：
The era of glasnost brought about significant political and social changes.
公开化时代带来了重大的政治和社会变革。

|近义词：openness, transparency, candor|反义词：secrecy, censorship, repression|词性:noun
