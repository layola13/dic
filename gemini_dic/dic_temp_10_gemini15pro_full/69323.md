fecklessly:/ˈfɛklɪsli/| adv.|不负责任地，无能地，无效地|acting in an irresponsible or ineffective way|He fecklessly spent all his money on gambling. 他不负责任地把所有的钱都花在了赌博上。|She fecklessly ignored the doctor's advice. 她漫不经心地忽视了医生的建议。|近义词：irresponsibly, carelessly, negligently, inefficiently|反义词：responsibly, carefully, diligently, effectively|词性: 副词

feckless:/ˈfɛklɪs/| adj.|无能的，不负责任的，软弱的|lacking initiative or strength of character; irresponsible|He was a feckless youth with no direction in life. 他是一个没有生活方向的无能青年。|The feckless government failed to address the economic crisis.  无能的政府未能解决经济危机。|近义词：irresponsible, incompetent, inept, weak|反义词：responsible, competent, capable, strong|词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Scots *feckless, from feck (“effect, value, majority”) + -less. Ultimately from Old English *feoh (“cattle, property”)
