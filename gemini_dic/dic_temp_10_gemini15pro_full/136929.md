nonsovereigns:/nɒnˈsɒvrɪnz/ | n. | 非主权实体；非独立国家 | Entities that are not sovereign states, meaning they don't have full self-governance and are often dependent on or part of another country. |  Example: Many nonsovereigns participate in international organizations as observers. 许多非主权实体以观察员身份参与国际组织。 | Example: The legal status of these nonsovereigns is complex and varied. 这些非主权实体的法律地位复杂且多样。 | Synonyms: dependencies, territories, protectorates | Antonyms: sovereign states, independent nations | noun
