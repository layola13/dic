decistere:/ˈdɛsɪstɪər/| n.|立方米|A unit of volume equal to one-tenth of a cubic meter, or 100 liters. It is primarily used for measuring volumes of stacked firewood.|The forester measured the woodpile in decisteres.|近义词:cubic meter, stere (1 stere = 1 cubic meter)|反义词:N/A|noun
