grandific:/ˈɡrændɪfaɪ/ | adj. | 夸大的，堂皇的，宏伟的 | Making something appear grand or imposing; pompous or bombastic. |  He delivered a grandific speech about the company's future, filled with exaggerated promises. 他就公司的未来发表了一篇夸夸其谈的演讲，充满了夸张的承诺。

The mayor's grandific plans for the city were met with skepticism by the council. 市长关于城市的宏伟计划遭到了委员会的质疑。

| 近义词：magnificent, grandiose, pompous, bombastic, ostentatious | 反义词：humble, modest, simple, understated | 词性：形容词
