nonrealist:/ˌnɒnˈriːəlɪst/ | adj. | 非现实主义的；非写实主义的 | Not adhering to or based on realism, especially in art or literature.  Characterized by a preference for the abstract, the imaginative, or the fantastic. |  The play was a nonrealist portrayal of war, focusing on the psychological impact rather than the physical battles.  这出戏是对战争的非现实主义刻画，侧重于心理影响而不是实际战斗。| Synonyms: unrealistic, fanciful, idealistic, romanticized, expressionistic, surreal, abstract | Antonyms: realist, realistic, naturalistic, pragmatic | adjective

nonrealist:/ˌnɑːnˈriːəlɪst/ | n. | 非现实主义者 | A person who advocates or practices nonrealism, especially in art or literature. |  The nonrealist artist preferred to paint dreams and visions rather than scenes from everyday life.  这位非现实主义艺术家更喜欢画梦境和幻象，而不是日常生活场景。 | Synonyms: idealist, romantic, visionary | Antonyms: realist, pragmatist | noun
