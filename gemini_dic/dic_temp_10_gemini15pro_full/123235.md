monocrotism:/ˌmɒnəˈkrɒtɪzəm/ | n. |单搏脉|A single beat or pulsation, especially of the pulse; a condition characterized by a single pulse wave for each cardiac cycle.|The patient exhibited monocrotism, which concerned the doctor.|Near Synonyms: single pulse, uniform pulse|Antonyms: dicrotism, polycrotism|noun
