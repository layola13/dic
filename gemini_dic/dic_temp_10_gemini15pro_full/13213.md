at-top-speed:/æt ˌtɒp ˈspiːd/ /æt ˌtɑːp ˈspiːd/|adv.|以最高速度，全速|At top speed means at the fastest possible speed.|The car raced down the track at top speed. 汽车以最高速度沿着赛道飞驰。|Synonyms: full speed, maximum speed, flat out|Antonyms: slowly, gradually|adverb

at-top-speed:/æt ˌtɒp ˈspiːd/ /æt ˌtɑːp ˈspiːd/|n.|最高速度|At top speed can also refer to the fastest possible speed itself.|The at-top-speed of the cheetah is incredible. 猎豹的最高速度令人难以置信。|Synonyms: maximum speed, top velocity|Antonyms: minimum speed, lowest speed|noun
