almightyship:/ɔːlˈmaɪtiʃɪp/ | n. | 全能,上帝的万能 |The state of being almighty; omnipotence.  |He spoke of God's almightyship. 他谈到了上帝的万能。| Synonyms: omnipotence, supremacy, sovereignty | Antonyms: powerlessness, weakness, impotence | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From almighty +‎ -ship.  Almighty from all +‎ mighty. Mighty from Middle English *myghty, from Old English mihtig (“mighty, powerful, strong, great, important”), from miht (“might, power, strength”).
