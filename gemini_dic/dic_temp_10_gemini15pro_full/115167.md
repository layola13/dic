maniacal:/məˈnaɪək(ə)l/
adjective
疯狂的；狂热的；着魔的
Exhibiting or suggestive of extreme enthusiasm or obsession.
He had a maniacal laugh that made everyone uncomfortable. 他疯狂的笑声让每个人都感到不舒服。

The fans displayed a maniacal devotion to their team.  球迷们对他们的球队表现出狂热的奉献。

近义词:insane, crazy, deranged, frenzied, rabid, wild
反义词:sane, rational, calm, composed

词性:adjective
