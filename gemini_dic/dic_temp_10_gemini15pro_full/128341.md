neutralises:/ˈnjuːtrəlaɪzɪz/
verb
抵消；使无效；中和
To make something neutral, especially to counteract something acidic or alkaline; to make something ineffective.
Baking soda neutralises stomach acid. 小苏打能中和胃酸。
Synonyms: counteract, offset, balance, negate
Antonyms: activate, enhance, intensify
词性:动词
