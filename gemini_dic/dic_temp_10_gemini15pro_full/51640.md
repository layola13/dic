deviable:/ˌdiːˈvaɪəbl/
adj.
可 desviación 的，可偏离的
Capable of deviating or being deviated.
The missile's trajectory is highly deviable, allowing it to evade enemy defenses.  导弹的轨迹高度可偏离，使其能够避开敌人的防御。
The river's course is deviable through canals. 这条河的河道可以通过运河改变。
近义词: alterable, changeable, modifiable, variable
反义词: fixed, inflexible, rigid, unchangeable
词性: adjective


词源:
From deviate + -able. Deviate comes from Late Latin deviatus, past participle of deviare "to turn aside," from de- "off, away from" + via "way, road."
