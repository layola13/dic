achiever:/əˈtʃiːvər/| n.|获得者，成功者，有成就的人|A person who achieves a lot, especially in their work.  Someone who sets goals and successfully accomplishes them.|She's a high achiever and expects a lot from her employees. 她是一个成就很高的人，对员工期望很高。

He was an achiever in every aspect of his life. 他在他人生的各个方面都是一个成功者。|近义词:winner, successor, high-flier|反义词:failure, underachiever|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "achieve" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting persons who perform or are associated with an action). "Achieve" comes from Old French "achever" (to finish, complete), ultimately from Latin "ad caput venire" (to come to a head, be finished).
