carbon-dates:/ˈkɑːrbən deɪts/ | v. | 碳定年，放射性碳测定年代 | Determines the age of something using the carbon-14 dating method. |  The archaeologist carbon-dates the ancient pottery shards. 考古学家用碳14测定了古代陶器碎片的年代。 | 近义词: radiocarbon dates | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "estimates age" could be considered a loose opposite) | 词性: 动词 (verb)


carbon-dating:/ˈkɑːrbən ˈdeɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 碳定年法，放射性碳测年 | The process of determining the age of organic material by measuring the amount of carbon-14 remaining. | Carbon-dating helps us understand the chronology of past events.  碳定年法帮助我们了解过去事件的年代顺序。| 近义词: radiocarbon dating | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: 名词 (noun)
