browed:/braʊd/| adj.|有…眉毛的；眉毛浓密的；眉毛…的|Having eyebrows of a specified kind. |例句：He was a dark-browed, intense young man. 他是一个眉毛浓密、眼神热烈的年轻人。

例句：She raised a perfectly browed eyebrow in question. 她完美地挑起了一边的眉毛表示疑问。|近义词：bushy-browed, heavy-browed|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑thin-browed 眉毛稀疏的)|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Old English *brūw-ed, past participle of brūwan 'to brew,' originally referring to the color or shape of eyebrows resembling the froth on brewed beverages. Later, it simply became an adjective for describing eyebrows.
