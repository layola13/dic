mollification:/ˌmɒlɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/ /ˌmɑːlɪfɪˈkeɪʃn/| n. | 平息；缓和；抚慰；变软|The mollification of her anger took several hours.  她花了几个小时才平息怒火。|The act of mollifying something or someone.  使某事物或某人缓和的行为。|例句：His apology was a mollification of her anger. 他的道歉平息了她的怒气。

例句：The government attempted mollification of the protesters through concessions. 政府试图通过让步来安抚抗议者。

|近义词： appeasement, conciliation, pacification, mitigation |反义词: aggravation, exacerbation, provocation|词性:noun
