cliquism: /ˈkliːkɪzəm/ | n. | 小集团主义，派系主义 |  The practice of forming small, exclusive groups within a larger group, often with the intention of promoting the interests of the clique's members. | The cliquism in the office made it difficult for newcomers to integrate. 办公室的小集团主义让新来的人很难融入。| synonyms: exclusivity, factionalism, sectarianism | antonyms: inclusivity, integration, open-mindedness | noun
