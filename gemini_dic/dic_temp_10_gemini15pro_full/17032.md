batfish:/ˈbætˌfɪʃ/| n. |蝙蝠鱼|A fish of the order Ogcocephaliformes, typically having a flattened body and a projecting snout.|The batfish uses its modified fins to "walk" along the ocean floor. 蝙蝠鱼用它特化的鳍在海底“行走”。|Synonyms: anglerfish, sea toad|Antonyms: None|Noun


batfish:/ˈbætˌfɪʃ/| n. |蝙蝠鱼|Any of various fishes having a batlike appearance, especially those of the family Ogcocephalidae (shortnose batfishes) which have a flattened, roughly triangular body and a long, slender tail.|Researchers observed the batfish attracting prey with a bioluminescent lure. 研究人员观察到蝙蝠鱼用生物发光的诱饵吸引猎物。|Synonyms: deep-sea anglerfish, handfish|Antonyms: None|Noun
