non-teutonic:/nɒn-tjuːˈtɒnɪk/
adjective
非条顿的，非日耳曼的
Not of or relating to the Teutons, a group of northern European peoples who spoke Germanic languages and included the ancestors of the English, Germans, Dutch, Scandinavians, etc.
The architecture of the building was distinctly non-Teutonic, showcasing Mediterranean influences. 这座建筑的建筑风格明显不是条顿式的，展现了地中海的影响。
The cultural practices of this tribe were non-Teutonic, differing significantly from their northern neighbors.  这个部落的文化习俗不是条顿式的，与他们北方的邻居大相径庭。
近义词:non-Germanic
反义词:Teutonic, Germanic
词性:adjective
