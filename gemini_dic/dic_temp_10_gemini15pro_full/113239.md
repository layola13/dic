lustfully:/ˈlʌstfəli/| adv.|好色地，贪欲地，渴望地|In a manner filled with or expressing lust; with strong sexual desire or covetousness.|He looked at her lustfully.| 近义词： lasciviously, lewdly, lecherously, desirously, covetously|反义词：purely, chastely, innocently|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lustfulli, equivalent to lustful +‎ -ly.  Lust itself comes from Old English *lust, meaning "pleasure, delight," with later development into the sense of "inordinate sexual desire."  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *lustuz.
