companionable:/kəmˈpænjənəbl/ | adj. | 友善的；好交谊的；适于作伴的 | Friendly and sociable; agreeable to be with. |  We spent a companionable evening chatting by the fire. 我们在炉火旁聊了一个晚上，气氛很融洽。 | Synonyms: sociable, friendly, affable, amiable, congenial, gregarious | Antonyms: unsociable, unfriendly, aloof, reserved | adjective

Etymology:
From late Middle English *companionable*, from companion + -able.  Ultimately from Late Latin *compāniō* "one who shares bread," from *com-* "with" + *pānis* "bread".
