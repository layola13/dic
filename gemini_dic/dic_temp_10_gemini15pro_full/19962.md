bioengineered:/ˌbaɪoʊˌɛndʒɪˈnɪrd/| adj.|生物工程的，基因工程的|Genetically modified or altered for specific purposes.|The bioengineered tomatoes had a longer shelf life. 这些经过生物工程改造的西红柿保质期更长。|Synonyms: genetically modified, genetically engineered, transgenic|Antonyms: natural, unmodified|adjective
