immerited:/ˌɪmˈɛrɪtɪd/ | adj. | 不应得的；不配的 | Not deserved; not earned or merited. | He received immerited praise for the project. 他因这个项目受到了不应得的赞扬。|  The immerited criticism demoralized the team.  不应得的批评使团队士气低落。| 近义词: undeserved, unmerited, unwarranted | 反义词: deserved, merited, earned | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From in- (not) + merited (deserved). The prefix "in-" negates the meaning of "merited." "Merited" comes from the Latin "meritus," past participle of "merēre" (to deserve, earn).
