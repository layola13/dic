odorosities:/ˌoʊdəˈrɑːsətiz/
n.
气味，香味（通常指难闻的气味）
The odorosities emanating from the garbage dump were overwhelming.
垃圾堆散发出的气味令人难以忍受。
The odorosities of the fish market were overpowering.
鱼市场的气味非常强烈。
Synonym: smells, aromas, scents, stenches (especially for unpleasant smells)
Antonym: fragrances, perfumes (especially for pleasant smells)
词性: 名词 (Noun)
