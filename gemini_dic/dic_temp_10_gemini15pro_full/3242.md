affile:/əˈfaɪl/
verb
折磨，使苦恼
to trouble or distress mentally or physically
He was afflicted by a terrible disease. 他被一种可怕的疾病折磨着。
近义词:trouble, distress, worry, burden, plague, torment
反义词:comfort, soothe, relieve, ease, console
词性:v.

affile:/əˈfaɪl/
verb
(古)使卑下；降低…的身份
(archaic) To lower in rank or dignity; humble
The king was affiled by the loss of his kingdom. 国王的王国沦陷，他的身份也随之降低。
近义词:humble, abase, degrade, debase, humiliate
反义词:exalt, elevate, promote, raise
词性:v.
