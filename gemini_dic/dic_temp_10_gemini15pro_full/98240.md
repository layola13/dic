insensibly:/ɪnˈsɛnsəbli/| adv.|不知不觉地，无意识地，逐渐地|Not aware of something; without feeling. Gradually and subtly. |例句：The days grew shorter insensibly. 白天不知不觉地变短了。|近义词：gradually, imperceptibly, subtly, unconsciously|反义词：consciously, noticeably, obviously, perceptibly|词性：副词
词源：Late Middle English: from Old French insensible, from Latin insensibilis, from in- ‘not’ + sensibilis (see sensible).
