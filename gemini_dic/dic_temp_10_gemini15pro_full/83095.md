gusseted:/ˈɡʌsɪtɪd/
adjective
有角撑板的，有三角形衬布的
Having a gusset or gussets (a piece of fabric inserted into a garment to add width or reduce stress from tight-fitting clothing).
The gusset allows for greater freedom of movement. 这块三角形衬布可以提供更大的活动自由。
近义词:expanded, widened, reinforced
反义词:narrow, tight, constricted
词性:adjective

gusseted:/ˈɡʌsɪtɪd/
verb (past participle of "gusset")
装角撑板，装三角形衬布
To add a gusset to something.
The bag is gusseted for extra capacity.  这个包装有角撑板以增加容量。
近义词:reinforced, strengthened, expanded
反义词:narrowed, constricted
词性:verb (past participle)
