half-annoying:/ˌhæf əˈnɔɪɪŋ/
adjective
有点烦人的；略微恼人的
Partially annoying; somewhat irritating, not fully or extremely annoying. It suggests a mild level of irritation.  "Half-" acts as an adverb, modifying the adjective "annoying."
Her habit of tapping her pen is half-annoying when I'm trying to concentrate. 她敲笔的习惯在我努力集中注意力时有点烦人。
近义词:somewhat annoying, a little irritating, slightly bothersome
反义词:pleasant, enjoyable, not at all bothersome
词性:adjective

词源:
"Half-" comes from Old English "healf," meaning "side," "part," or "one of two equal parts."  "Annoying" comes from the verb "annoy," which has roots in Old French "enoier," meaning "to injure" or "to vex."
