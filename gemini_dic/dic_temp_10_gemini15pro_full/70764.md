finesse:/fɪˈnɛs/ /fɪˈnɛs/| n. v.|技巧，策略；精细；灵巧；vt. 巧妙地处理|She handled the situation with finesse. 她巧妙地处理了这个局面。|近义词：skill, delicacy, tact, subtlety, diplomacy|反义词：clumsiness, awkwardness, gaucheness|noun, verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *finesse*, from Old French *fin* (fine, thin, delicate) ultimately from Latin *finis* (end, limit, boundary).  The sense of "skillful maneuvering" developed in the 18th century.
