endeavored:/ɪnˈdɛvərd/| v. |努力，尽力，尝试|tried hard to do or achieve something|例句：They endeavored to create a welcoming environment. 他们努力营造一个友好的环境。|近义词：tried, attempted, strived, sought|反义词：neglected, ignored|词性:动词

endeavor:/ɪnˈdɛvər/| n. |努力，尽力，尝试|an attempt to achieve a goal|例句：His endeavor to climb Mount Everest was successful. 他攀登珠穆朗玛峰的努力成功了。|近义词：attempt, effort, try|反义词：neglect, inaction|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *endeveren, from Anglo-Norman *endeverer, from Old French *s’esforcier (“to exert oneself”), itself from Late Latin *exfortiō (“strengthening”).  Ultimately from Latin ex (“out of, from”) + fortis (“strong”).
