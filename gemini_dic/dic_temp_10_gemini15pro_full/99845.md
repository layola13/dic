intermixer:/ˌɪntərˈmɪksər/| n.| 混合器，搅拌器 |A device or substance that mixes things together, especially thoroughly.|The baker used an intermixer to combine the wet and dry ingredients for the cake.  面包师用搅拌器混合蛋糕的干湿配料。|近义词：mixer, blender, combiner|反义词:separator, divider| noun


intermix:/ˌɪntərˈmɪks/| v. |混合，混杂|To mix together thoroughly; blend.|The different cultures intermixed in the melting pot of the city.  不同的文化在城市的熔炉中融合在一起。|近义词：mix, blend, mingle, combine|反义词:separate, divide, segregate|verb
