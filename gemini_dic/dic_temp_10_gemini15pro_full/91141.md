hybridizer:/ˈhaɪbrɪdaɪzər/| n. |杂交者，杂交培育者|A person who produces hybrids, especially of plants or animals.| Luther Burbank was a famous hybridizer of plants.  路德·伯班克是一位著名的植物杂交专家。|Synonym: crossbreeder, hybridizing|Antonym: N/A|noun

hybridize:/ˈhaɪbrɪdaɪz/| v. |杂交，使杂交|To crossbreed (two plants or animals of different species or varieties) to produce a hybrid.|Scientists have hybridized different species of corn to create a more disease-resistant variety. 科学家们将不同种类的玉米杂交，以创造出更抗病的品种。|Synonym: cross, interbreed, crossbreed|Antonym: N/A|verb
