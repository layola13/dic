词源：
Over- (prefix): excessive; beyond normal limits.
Joy (noun): a feeling of great pleasure and happiness.
-ous (suffix): having the quality of; characterized by.
-ness (suffix): forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
Therefore, "overjoyousness" literally means the state of being excessively joyful.
