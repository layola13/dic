coachnik: /ˈkoʊtʃnɪk/ | n. | 教练，指导者（尤指体育或技能方面，有时略带贬义） | A coachnik is a coach, especially in sports or skills training. Sometimes the term carries a slightly negative connotation, suggesting an overbearing or overly enthusiastic coach. | He acted like a real coachnik, yelling instructions constantly.  他表现得像个十足的教练狂，不停地大声喊着指示。 |  The coachnik pushed the team too hard and several players got injured.  这个教练把队员逼得太紧，导致几个队员受伤。|Synonyms: trainer, instructor, mentor (sometimes) | Antonyms:  trainee, student, pupil | noun
