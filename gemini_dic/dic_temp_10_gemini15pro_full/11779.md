arpine:/ɑːrˈpiːn/ | n. | 阿尔平，一种计量单位 (通常指土地面积) | A unit of land area used in some regions, particularly in Switzerland and France.  It's a measure based on the amount of land a single person could plow in a day with a yoke of oxen. |  The farmer owned 10 arpines of land.  这位农民拥有10阿尔平的土地。 | synonyms:  (no direct English equivalent, varies regionally; consider acre, hectare depending on region)  | antonyms: N/A | noun
