limblike:/ˈlɪmlaɪk/
adjective
像肢体的，肢体状的
Resembling a limb in form or function.
The robot had long, limblike appendages that allowed it to manipulate objects with precision.  这个机器人有长长的、像肢体一样的附属物，使它能够精确地操纵物体。
The branches of the old oak tree were gnarled and limblike.  这棵老橡树的树枝扭曲纠结，像肢体一样。
similar:  branchlike, appendage-like, armlike, leglike
opposite: shapeless, formless
词性: 形容词


词源:
From limb +‎ -like.  由 limb (肢体) + -like (像...的) 组成.
