gentrifies:/ˈdʒɛntrɪˌfaɪz/
verb
使贵族化；中产阶级化；高档化
to change a place from being a poor area to a richer area, by people of a higher social class moving to live there, improving housing, and opening businesses such as restaurants and stores.
The area is rapidly gentrifying, with new cafes and boutiques opening up.  这个地区正在迅速高档化，新的咖啡馆和精品店纷纷开业。
Developers are gentrifying the old industrial area.  开发商正在将旧工业区高档化。
synonyms: upgrade, improve, regenerate, revitalize, renovate, restore, rehabilitate, revamp, modernize, develop, enhance, enrich, elevate
antonyms: degrade, decline, deteriorate, decay, worsen, impoverish
词性:v.
