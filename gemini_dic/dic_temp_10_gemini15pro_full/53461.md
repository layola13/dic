disapprovingly:/ˌdɪsəˈpruːvɪŋli/| adv.|不赞成地，反对地|In a manner that expresses disapproval; with a look or expression of disapproval.|She looked at him disapprovingly when he lit a cigarette. 当他点燃香烟时，她不赞成地看着他。|近义词：critically, censoriously, scornfully|反义词：approvingly, supportively|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From disapprove + -ing + -ly.  "Disapprove" comes from the prefix "dis-" (expressing negation) + "approve" (from Latin "approbare," meaning "to prove, test, or find good").  The suffix "-ing" forms the present participle, and "-ly" turns it into an adverb.
