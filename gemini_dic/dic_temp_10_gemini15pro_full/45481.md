curbstone:/ˈkɜːrbˌstoʊn/ (US) /ˈkɜːbˌstəʊn/ (UK)| n. |路缘石，马路牙子|A row of stones bordering a street, separating the sidewalk from the roadway. |例句：The car scraped against the curbstone as it parked. 汽车停车时刮到了路缘石。

例句：She tripped over a loose curbstone. 她被一块松动的路缘石绊倒了。

近义词：kerbstone (UK), curb, kerb|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "roadway", "street")|词性:noun
