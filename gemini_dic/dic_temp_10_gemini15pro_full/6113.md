ametabola:/ˌeɪmɛtəˈboʊlə/
|n.|无变态类，无变态昆虫|Insects of the subclass Ametabola undergo little or no metamorphosis.  无变态亚纲的昆虫几乎没有或根本没有变态。|Ametabola includes insects like silverfish. 无变态类昆虫包括蠹虫。|近义词: Apterygota (in older classifications)|反义词: Holometabola, Hemimetabola|词性: Noun


ametabolous:/ˌeɪmɛtəˈbɒləs/
|adj.|无变态的|Apterygotes are ametabolous insects.  无翅亚纲昆虫是无变态昆虫。|The ametabolous life cycle is the simplest type of insect development. 无变态的生命周期是昆虫发育的最简单类型。|近义词: Apterygote (in older classifications)|反义词: Holometabolous, Hemimetabolous|词性: Adjective
