noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + habitable + -ness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not."  "Habitable" comes from the Late Latin "habitabilis," from "habitare" "to inhabit,"  ultimately from "habere" "to have, hold." "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
