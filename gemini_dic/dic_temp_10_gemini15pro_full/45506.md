cure-all:/ˈkjʊrˌɔːl/
noun
万能药; 万灵药
a medicine or other remedy for all ills or difficulties; a panacea.
Many people consider vitamin C to be a cure-all. 很多人认为维生素C是万能药。
synonyms: panacea, elixir, nostrum, remedy, solution
antonyms: placebo, poison, toxin
词性: 名词


cure-all:/ˈkjʊrˌɔːl/
adjective
包治百病的
(of a medicine or other remedy) purporting to cure all ills or difficulties.
There is no such thing as a cure-all solution to poverty.  不存在包治百病的解决贫困的方案。
synonyms: all-healing, all-purpose, universal, general, comprehensive
antonyms: specialized, specific, targeted, limited, ineffective
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From cure + all. First recorded in the 1540s.
