disestablishment:/dɪsɪˈstæblɪʃmənt/
|n.| 解除建制，废除制度 |The disestablishment of the Church of England is a recurring topic of debate.  英国教会的解除建制是一个反复出现的辩论话题。|
| |The disestablishment of the state-sponsored religion led to greater religious freedom. 解除国家支持的宗教制度带来了更大的宗教自由。|
|近义词: abolition, annulment, revocation|
|反义词: establishment, founding, institution|
|词性: noun|


ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- + establishment.  "Dis-" is a prefix meaning "reversal or removal of," while "establishment" refers to the act of establishing something, especially a formal organization.  Therefore, "disestablishment" literally means the reversal or removal of an established institution.
