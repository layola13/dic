eozoonal:/ˌiːəˈzoʊnəl/
n.|伊奥珠藻石（一种被误认为是最早生命证据的岩石）|A banded mineral structure, once thought to be the fossilized remains of a Precambrian marine organism, but now known to be an inorganic product of metamorphism.
例句：Eozoonal was once hailed as a crucial discovery in the search for early life. 伊奥珠藻石曾被誉为寻找早期生命的重要发现。

例句：Further research revealed that eozoonal was not a fossil at all.  进一步的研究表明，伊奥珠藻石根本不是化石。
近义词：fossil (in earlier usage), pseudofossil|
