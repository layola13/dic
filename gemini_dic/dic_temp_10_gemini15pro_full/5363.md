alluringnesses:/əˈlʊərɪŋnɪsɪz/ | n. | 魅力，诱惑力（复数） | The quality of being alluring; attractiveness or temptingness.  This word is not commonly used in its plural form as the singular "alluringness" already conveys the general concept. |  The alluringnesses of the tropics drew him in. (This sentence is grammatically correct but sounds unnatural. It's better to use "allure" or "alluring qualities".) | 近义词: attractiveness, charm, appeal, temptation, enticement | 反义词: repulsiveness, unattractiveness, ugliness | 词性: 名词

词源: From alluring + -ness.  Alluring comes from the Old French "alurer" meaning "to entice, lure."
