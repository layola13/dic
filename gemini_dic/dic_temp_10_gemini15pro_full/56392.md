doleful: /ˈdoulfəl/ (US) /ˈdəʊlfʊl/ (UK)| adj. | 悲哀的，忧郁的 | Expressing sorrow; mournful. |  She gave a doleful sigh. 她悲伤地叹了口气。

The dog gave a doleful whine when its owner left.  当主人离开时，狗发出了一声悲哀的呜咽。


近义词: mournful, sorrowful, melancholy, sad, gloomy, dejected, downcast

反义词: cheerful, happy, joyful, merry, upbeat

词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *dolful, from Old French dolful, doelful (“sorrowful, painful”), from doel (“grief, sorrow, pain”) + -ful.  Further etymology uncertain, possibly from Vulgar Latin *dolus, from Latin dolor (“pain, grief”).
