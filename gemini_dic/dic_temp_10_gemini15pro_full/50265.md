depersonalized:/diːˈpɜːrsənəlaɪzd/
adj.
失去人情味的；客观的；非个人的
Divorced from personal feelings or characteristics; made impersonal or objective.
The hospital felt cold and depersonalized.  这家医院感觉很冷淡，没有人情味。
She found the large lecture classes depersonalized and difficult to connect with. 她觉得大型讲座课没有人情味，很难融入。
近义词: impersonal, objective, detached, anonymous
反义词: personal, subjective, intimate
词性: 形容词


depersonalize:/diːˈpɜːrsənəlaɪzd/
vt.
使失去个性；使客观化；使非人格化
To make impersonal or objective; to deprive of personal identity or individuality.
Bureaucracy can depersonalize the relationship between doctors and patients. 官僚主义会使医生和病人之间的关系变得没有人情味。
Modern technology sometimes depersonalizes customer service. 现代技术有时会使客户服务失去人情味。
近义词: objectify, alienate, dehumanize
反义词: personalize, humanize
词性: 动词


词源:
From de- + personalize, from person + -al + -ize.  "De-" prefix signifies removal or reversal.  "Personalize" means to make something personal. Therefore, "depersonalize" means to remove the personal aspect.
