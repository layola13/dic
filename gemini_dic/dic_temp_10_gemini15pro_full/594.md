above-water:/əˈbʌvˈwɔːtər/
adjective
水面上的；未沉没的；没有负债的
Being above water means not submerged or afloat. Figuratively, it means financially solvent or not in debt.
The ship remained above water despite the damage.  这艘船尽管受损，但仍然漂浮着。

After years of struggle, their business is finally above water.  经过多年的奋斗，他们的生意终于扭亏为盈了。

近义词:afloat, solvent, viable
反义词:underwater, submerged, insolvent, bankrupt
词性:adjective
