intergossipping:/ˌɪntərˈɡɑːsɪpɪŋ/ | n. | 私下闲聊，互相八卦 | The act of gossiping amongst a group of people, especially in a low voice or secretive manner. | Example 1: There was a lot of intergossiping going on at the water cooler.  办公室饮水机旁有很多私下闲聊。

Example 2: The intergossiping among the neighbors led to a lot of misunderstandings. 邻居间的互相八卦导致了很多误解。 | 近义词: whispering, chattering, gossiping, backbiting | 反义词: silence, discretion | 词性: 名词
