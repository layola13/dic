endorse:/ɪnˈdɔːrs/ /ɪnˈdɔːrs/| v. |认可，支持，背书，签署（支票等）|To give support or approval to something, especially publicly. Also, to sign one's name on the back of a check, etc. to make it payable to someone else.|The president endorsed the candidate for mayor. 总统公开支持这位市长候选人。|Synonyms: support, approve, back, champion, ratify|Antonyms: oppose, reject, disapprove, condemn|verb

endorsed:/ɪnˈdɔːrst/ /ɪnˈdɔːrst/| adj. |被认可的，被支持的|Having received support or approval.|The endorsed candidate went on to win the election. 这位获得支持的候选人继续赢得了选举。|Synonyms: supported, approved, backed|Antonyms: opposed, rejected, disapproved|adjective
