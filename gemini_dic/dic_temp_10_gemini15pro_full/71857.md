fleshhood:/ˈflɛʃhʊd/ | n. | 肉体状态，肉身存在 | The concept of fleshhood explores the physical and embodied experience of being human.  肉体状态的概念探讨了作为人类的生理和肉身体验。|  He embraced his fleshhood and all its limitations. 他接受了他的肉体状态及其所有局限性。| 近义词: physicality, corporeality, mortality | 反义词: spirituality, immortality | 词性: noun

词源: flesh (肉体) + -hood (状态，时期)  Old English *flæschad (recorded only as flæschaman, "incarnation"), from flæsc "flesh" + -had.  古英语 *flæschad（仅记录为 flæschaman，“化身”），来自 flæsc “肉体” + -had。
