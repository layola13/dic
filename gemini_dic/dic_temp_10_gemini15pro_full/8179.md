anti-democrat:/ˈæntiˈdɛməˌkræt/| n. |反民主主义者；反对民主的人|Someone who is opposed to democracy.|He was labeled an anti-democrat for his authoritarian views. 他因其专制观点而被称为反民主主义者。|The anti-democrat sought to undermine the election process.  这个反民主主义者试图破坏选举进程。|近义词：authoritarian, totalitarian, despot|反义词: democrat, liberal, egalitarian|词性:noun


anti-democratic:/ˈæntiˌdɛməˈkrætɪk/| adj. |反民主的；非民主的|Opposed to or violating the principles of democracy.|The government implemented anti-democratic policies that restricted free speech. 政府实施了限制言论自由的反民主政策。|The new law was seen as an anti-democratic measure. 这项新法律被视为一项反民主措施。|近义词：authoritarian, totalitarian, oppressive|反义词: democratic, liberal, egalitarian|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix): from Greek anti- "against, opposite of, instead of, in return"
democrat: from French démocrate, from Greek dēmokratēs "democrat," from dēmos "people" + -kratēs "member of a specified group," from kratos "power, rule"
