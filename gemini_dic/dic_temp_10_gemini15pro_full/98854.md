intemperance:/ɪnˈtɛmpərəns/| n.|放纵；无节制；过度|Lack of moderation or restraint, especially in consuming alcohol; excessive indulgence.|His intemperance ultimately led to his downfall. 他的无节制最终导致了他的垮台。| 近义词: excess, immoderation, overindulgence, dissipation, profligacy| 反义词: temperance, moderation, abstinence, self-control| noun

intemperate:/ɪnˈtɛmpərət/| adj.|放纵的；无节制的；过度的|Given to or characterized by excessive indulgence, especially in alcohol; not moderate or restrained.|He was known for his intemperate outbursts of anger.  他以无节制的愤怒爆发而闻名。| 近义词: excessive, immoderate, unrestrained, extreme, inordinate| 反义词: temperate, moderate, restrained, controlled, mild| adjective
