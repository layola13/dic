inflammability:/ɪnˌflæməˈbɪləti/| n. |易燃性，可燃性|The quality of being easily set on fire.  The ease with which something catches fire and burns.|  The flammability of dry leaves makes them a fire hazard. 干树叶的易燃性使它们成为火灾隐患。| synonyms: combustibility, ignitability | antonyms: nonflammability, fire resistance | noun
