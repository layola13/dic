entrustment:/ɪnˈtrʌstmənt/ | n. | 委托，托付 | The act of entrusting something to someone.  The state of being entrusted with something. |  She was given the entrustment of a large sum of money. 她被委托管理一大笔钱。|近义词: commission, delegation, assignment | 反义词:  appropriation, usurpation | noun

entrust:/ɪnˈtrʌst/ | vt. | 委托，托付 |  To give something to someone for safekeeping or for a particular purpose. | I entrusted my savings to my brother. 我把我的积蓄托付给了我哥哥。| 近义词: commit, confide, delegate | 反义词: withhold, keep, retain | verb
