ordinate:/ˈɔːrdɪnət/ | n. | 纵坐标；坐标 |The ordinate is the vertical coordinate in a two-dimensional system. | 纵坐标是二维系统中的垂直坐标。| synonyms: y-coordinate, vertical coordinate | antonyms: abscissa, x-coordinate | noun

ordinates:/ˈɔːrdɪnəts/ | n. | 纵坐标（复数）；坐标（复数）| The ordinates represent the values of the dependent variable. | 纵坐标表示因变量的值。| synonyms: y-coordinates, vertical coordinates | antonyms: abscissas, x-coordinates | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ōrdināta (līnea) "(ordered line)", feminine past participle of ōrdināre "to order, arrange," from Latin ōrdō "order, row, rank."
