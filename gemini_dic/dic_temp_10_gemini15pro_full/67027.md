explainable:/ɪkˈspleɪnəbl/
adjective
可解释的
capable of being explained or accounted for
The sudden increase in sales is easily explainable. 销售额的突然增长很容易解释。

The phenomenon is explainable by scientific principles. 这种现象可以用科学原理来解释。

近义词: understandable, interpretable, justifiable
反义词: inexplicable, mysterious, incomprehensible
词性: adjective
