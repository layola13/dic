corrumpable:/kəˈrʌptəbl/
adj.
可腐败的，易堕落的，可以收买的
Susceptible to corruption, capable of being morally corrupted or bribed.
The official was corruptible and accepted bribes. 这个官员可以收买，接受了贿赂。
近义词:venal, dishonest, bribable, susceptible
反义词:incorruptible, honest, unbribable, impervious
词性:adjective
词源:From Late Latin *corruptibilis, from Latin corrumpere (to corrupt) + -ibilis (capable of being).

corrupt:/kəˈrʌpt/
vt. adj. n.
腐败，堕落，贿赂；腐败的，堕落的；腐败，贪污
(v.) To cause to become dishonest, immoral, or impure; to alter or debase; (adj.) Dishonest, immoral, or impure; altered or debased; (n.) Decayed or rotting matter.
The corrupt politician was eventually jailed.  这个腐败的政客最终被判入狱。(adj.)
Power tends to corrupt. 权力容易使人腐败。(v.)
The corrupt files could not be opened. 这些损坏的文件打不开。(adj.)
