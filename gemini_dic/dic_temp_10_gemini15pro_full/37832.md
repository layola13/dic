competency:/ˈkɒmpɪtənsi/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmpɪtənsi/ (US)| n. |能力，胜任，称职|(Often interchangeable with competence) The ability to do something successfully or efficiently, often referring to a specific skill or area of expertise.|Key competencies for this role include communication and problem-solving.  这个职位的主要能力要求包括沟通和解决问题的能力。|近义词：ability, capability, skill, proficiency, expertise, aptitude|反义词：incompetence, inability, inadequacy|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English: from late Latin competentia ‘meeting, agreement’, from competent- ‘competing, suitable’, from the verb competere (see compete).
