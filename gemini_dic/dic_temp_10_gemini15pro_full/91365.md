hydrosphere:/ˈhaɪdrəsfɪr/| n. |水圈|All the waters on the earth's surface, such as lakes and seas, and sometimes including water over the earth's surface, such as clouds.|例句：The hydrosphere is essential for life on Earth. 水圈对地球上的生命至关重要。

例句：Pollution of the hydrosphere is a serious environmental problem. 水圈污染是一个严重的环境问题。|近义词：water sphere|反义词:lithosphere (岩石圈), atmosphere (大气圈)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From hydro- + sphere.  Hydro- comes from Greek *hydōr* meaning "water." Sphere comes from Greek *sphaira* meaning "ball" or "globe."
