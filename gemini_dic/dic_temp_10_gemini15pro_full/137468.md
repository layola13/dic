nontolerable:/nɒnˈtɒlərəbl/ | adj. | 不可容忍的 | Not able to be tolerated; unbearable. | The noise from the construction site was nontolerable, so I had to move.  建筑工地的噪音令人无法忍受，所以我不得不搬家。|  The heat was nontolerable, forcing us to seek shelter indoors.  酷热难耐，迫使我们到室内寻找遮蔽处。| 近义词: unacceptable, insufferable, unbearable, intolerable | 反义词: tolerable, acceptable, bearable, sufferable | 词性: 形容词

non- + tolerable

non-: prefix meaning "not"
tolerable: from Latin tolerabilis, from tolerare "to endure"
