anesthetised:/əˈnɛsθətaɪzd/ | adj. | 麻醉的，失去知觉的 |  Deprived of feeling or awareness, typically by a drug that causes temporary loss of sensation.  | The patient was anesthetised before the surgery. 病人在手术前被麻醉了。|  The anesthetised dog lay still on the operating table.  麻醉的狗静静地躺在手术台上。 |  numb, insensible, unconscious | conscious, aware, sensitive | adjective


anesthetize:/əˈnɛsθətaɪz/ | v. | 麻醉 | To administer an anesthetic to (a person or animal), especially so as to induce a loss of consciousness. | The vet anesthetized the cat before the operation. 兽医在手术前麻醉了猫。 | They anesthetized the area before inserting the needle. 他们在插入针头之前麻醉了该区域。| numb, sedate | stimulate, awaken | verb



Etymology:
From Greek "anaisthesia,"  from "an-" (without) + "aisthesis" (sensation).
