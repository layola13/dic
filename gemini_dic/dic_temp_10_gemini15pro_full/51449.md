dethronization:/diːˌθroʊnɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
废黜，罢免，推翻
The act of removing a monarch from their throne.  The dethronization of the king led to a period of instability.
The dethronization of the old software by the new version was swift and complete. 国王的废黜导致了一段时期的不稳定。 新版本软件迅速而彻底地取代了旧软件。
近义词：removal, ousting, deposition, overthrow
反义词：enthronement, coronation, accession
词性: noun


dethrone:/diːˈθroʊn/
vt.
废黜，罢免，推翻
To remove a monarch from power. The rebels dethroned the king and established a republic.
The new phone dethroned the previous model in terms of popularity.  叛军废黜了国王，建立了共和国。  这款新手机在受欢迎程度上取代了之前的型号。
近义词：remove, oust, depose, overthrow
反义词：enthrone, crown
词性: verb
