murex: /ˈmjʊərɛks/ (US), /ˈmjʊərɪks/ (UK) | n. | 海螺，骨螺 | A marine gastropod mollusk of the family Muricidae, typically having a rough, elaborately spined shell.  Many species are carnivorous and some yield a purple dye. |  The ancient Romans used the murex to produce a valuable purple dye. 古罗马人用骨螺来制作珍贵的紫色染料。

The murex is a common sight in tide pools.  海螺在潮池中很常见。 | 近义词:  whelk, rock snail | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin *murex*, from Ancient Greek *μούρεξ (moúrex)*.
