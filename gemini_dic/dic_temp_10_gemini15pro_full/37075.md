combbroach:/ˈkoʊmˌbroʊtʃ/ /ˈkəʊmˌbrəʊtʃ/ | n. |梳子蟑螂，栉尾蟑螂|A type of cockroach, typically *Supella longipalpa*, characterized by its small size and preference for warm, dry environments.  It's known for its distinctive two-toned appearance, often light brown with two darker bands across its wings. |例句：The combbroach is a common pest in apartments. 梳子蟑螂是公寓里常见的害虫。

例句：Combroaches are smaller than American cockroaches. 梳子蟑螂比美洲大蠊小。|近义词：German cockroach (often confused due to size), brown-banded cockroach|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
