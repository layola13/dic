gra:/ɡrɑː/ /ɡræ/ |  prefix |  灰色的，灰白的 |  From the Old English "græg," meaning "gray." It is used as a prefix to indicate a shade of gray or a mixture of black and white. |  Example:  A **grayscale** image is composed of shades of gray. 灰度图像由不同深浅的灰色组成。

Example: **Graupel** is a form of precipitation that looks like soft hail.霰是一种看起来像软冰雹的降水形式。 |  近义词: grizzled, hoary (for hair) | 反义词: colorful, bright | 词性: prefix
