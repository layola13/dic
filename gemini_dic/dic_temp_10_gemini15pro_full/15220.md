backed:/bækt/
adjective
有背衬的，得到支持的
supported; having a back or backing
The chair is backed with soft cushions. 这把椅子有柔软的靠垫。
supported, reinforced
opposed, unsupported
adjective


backed:/bækt/
verb
支持，援助（back的过去式和过去分词）
gave support to
The community backed the new playground project. 社区支持了新的游乐场项目。
supported, championed
opposed, hindered
verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bakked*, past participle of *bakken*, from Old English *bacian* (“to bake”), from Proto-Germanic *bakōną (“to bake”).  The sense of "support" developed later, likely metaphorically from the idea of providing a strong foundation or "backing."
