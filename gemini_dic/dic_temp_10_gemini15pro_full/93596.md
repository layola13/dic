imbargo:/ɪmˈbɑːrɡoʊ/ (US), /ɪmˈbɑːɡəʊ/ (UK)| n. vt.|禁运，禁止；封港|An official ban on trade or other commercial activity with a particular country.|The government imposed an embargo on arms sales to the region. 政府对该地区的武器销售实施了禁运。|Synonyms: ban, prohibition, restriction, sanction|Antonyms: allowance, permission|Noun, Verb

imbargo:/ɪmˈbɑːrɡoʊ/ (US), /ɪmˈbɑːɡəʊ/ (UK)| vt.|禁止；阻止|To officially ban.|They embargoed all shipments of coffee from that country. 他们禁止了从那个国家的所有咖啡运输。|Synonyms: prohibit, forbid, ban, restrict|Antonyms: permit, allow|Verb
