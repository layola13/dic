cantor:/ˈkæntər/ (US), /ˈkæntɔːr/ (UK)| n.|歌手；领唱者；圣咏团领唱人|A cantor is a person who sings liturgical music and leads congregational singing, especially in Jewish synagogues or Christian churches. |The cantor's voice resonated through the cathedral. 领唱者的声音在教堂里回荡。| The cantor led the congregation in prayer.  领唱者带领会众祈祷。|Synonyms: singer, vocalist, chorister, leader of song|Antonyms: listener, audience|noun
