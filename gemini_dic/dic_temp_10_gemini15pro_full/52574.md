diffusibleness:/dɪˌfjuːzɪbəlˈnɛs/ | n. |扩散性，弥散性|The quality of being diffusible; capability of being spread or dispersed.|The diffusibleness of the scent filled the entire room.  香味的弥散性让整个房间都充满了香味。|Synonyms: dispersibility, spreadability, pervasion |Antonyms: impermeability, impenetrability|noun

diffusible:/dɪˈfjuːzɪbl/ | adj. |可扩散的，可弥散的|Capable of being diffused or spread out; capable of passing through or into surrounding substances.|The diffusible nature of gases allows them to mix readily. 气体的可扩散性使它们容易混合。|Synonyms: dispersible, spreadable, permeable|Antonyms: impermeable, impenetrabile|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin diffusibilis, from Latin diffusus (past participle of diffundere "to pour out, spread out") + -ibilis (suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being").
