lead-out:/ˈliːdaʊt/| n.|引出，导出；(尤指电视或广播节目最后的)片尾音乐，结束曲|A section of music or other material played at the end of a radio or television broadcast, or at the end of a piece of recorded music.  The lead-out from the news program was a catchy jingle.|例句：The lead-out music signaled the end of the show. 片尾音乐标志着节目的结束。

例句：The DJ played a short lead-out after the final song. DJ在最后一首歌之后播放了一段简短的结束曲。|近义词：outro, coda, ending, closing|反义词: intro, beginning, prelude|词性:noun


lead-out:/ˈliːdaʊt/| adj.|引出的，导出的|Serving to lead something out. This is less common than the noun form.|例句：The lead-out groove on the vinyl record signaled the end. 黑胶唱片上的导出槽标志着结束。

例句：They designed a lead-out strategy for their marketing campaign. 他们为他们的营销活动设计了一个导出策略。|近义词：exit, concluding, final|反义词: introductory, initial, leading-in|词性:adjective
