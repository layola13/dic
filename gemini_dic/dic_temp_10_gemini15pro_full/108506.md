laymen:/ˈleɪmən/| n.|门外汉，俗人；非专业人士|A layman is a person who is not trained or ordained as a member of the clergy. A layman is also someone who is not a member of a particular profession, especially medicine or law.|The medical jargon was incomprehensible to the layman.|Synonyms: nonprofessional, amateur, novice, outsider|Antonyms: professional, expert, specialist|Noun
