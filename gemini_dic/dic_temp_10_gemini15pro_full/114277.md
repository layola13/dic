mainframes:/ˈmeɪnfreɪmz/| n. |大型机，主机|A large, powerful computer system, typically used by large organizations for critical applications, bulk data processing, transaction processing, and enterprise resource planning.|The bank relies on its mainframes to process millions of transactions daily. 这家银行依靠其大型机每天处理数百万笔交易。|近义词：mainframe computers, supercomputers|反义词:minicomputers, microcomputers|noun
