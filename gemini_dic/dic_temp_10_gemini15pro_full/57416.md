drabbed:/dræbd/| adj.|单调的，乏味的，灰暗的|Dull and without color; lacking brightness or interest.  It describes something that appears washed out, faded, or lacking vibrancy. It can also refer to something monotonous or dreary.|The drabbed curtains made the room feel depressing.  单调的窗帘使房间感觉很压抑。|Synonyms: dull, dreary, monotonous, colorless, lackluster, dingy|Antonyms: bright, vibrant, colorful, cheerful, lively|adjective
