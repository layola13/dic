madrilenian: /ˌmædrɪˈleɪniən/ | n. adj. |马德里人，马德里的| A native or inhabitant of Madrid, Spain. Also used as an adjective to describe something related to Madrid. |例句：She is a proud Madrilenian. 她是一位自豪的马德里人。

例句：The Madrilenian dialect is quite distinct. 马德里方言很独特。

近义词：Madrileño, inhabitant of Madrid |反义词: (No direct antonym - perhaps someone not from Madrid)  | 词性: Noun, Adjective

Etymology: From Spanish *madrileño/a*, derived from *Madrid* + suffix *-eño/a* (denoting origin or belonging).  Ultimately from Arabic *Maǧrīṭ* (مَجْرِيط).
