monkeying:/ˈmʌŋkiɪŋ/ | v. | 捣乱，胡闹，玩弄 |  playing or fiddling with something, usually in a mischievous or careless way; also, mimicking or imitating someone playfully. |  He was monkeying around with the radio and broke it. 他玩弄收音机，把它弄坏了。| synonyms: tampering, fiddling, playing, fooling, mimicking | antonyms: fixing, repairing, leaving alone | verb

monkeying:/ˈmʌŋkiɪŋ/ | n. | 玩耍，捣蛋 | The act of playing or fiddling with something, usually in a mischievous or careless way. |  Their monkeying around caused a delay in the project.  他们的胡闹导致项目延期。| synonyms:  mischief, horseplay, clowning | antonyms:  seriousness, diligence | noun
