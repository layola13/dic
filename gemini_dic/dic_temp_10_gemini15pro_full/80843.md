gould:/ɡuːld/
n.
金翅雀，古尔德(姓氏)
A small, brightly coloured finch, native to Australia. / A surname.
John Gould was a renowned ornithologist.  约翰·古尔德是一位著名的鸟类学家。
The Gouldian finch is a popular pet bird.  七彩文鸟是一种受欢迎的宠物鸟。
近义词:finch (for the bird)
反义词: N/A
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the surname of John Gould (1804-1881), a British ornithologist.  The bird species were named in his honour by his wife, Elizabeth Gould. 源于英国鸟类学家约翰·古尔德（1804-1881）的姓氏。这些鸟类物种是由他的妻子伊丽莎白·古尔德以他的名字命名的。
