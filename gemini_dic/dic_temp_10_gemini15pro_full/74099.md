fosters:/ˈfɒstəz/ (UK) /ˈfɑːstərz/ (US)| v. |促进，培养，鼓励，收养，代养|encourages or promotes the development or growth of something; to bring up a child that is not legally one's own |例句：
The program fosters creativity in young children.  这个项目培养幼儿的创造力。

A warm, sunny climate fosters plant growth.  温暖、阳光充足的气候有利于植物生长。

They fostered three children.  他们收养了三个孩子。

The organization fostered a sense of community among its members.  该组织在其成员之间培养了社群意识。

The teacher fostered a love of reading in her students.  这位老师培养了学生对阅读的热爱。|近义词：encourages, promotes, cultivates, nurtures, supports, adopts|反义词：hinders, discourages, neglects, abandons|词性:动词
