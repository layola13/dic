commiserable:/ˈmɪzərəbl/ (US), /ˈmɪzrəbl/ (UK)| adj.|悲惨的，痛苦的，可怜的|Feeling or causing great physical or mental distress.  Characterized by or causing misery or discomfort; wretched.|例句：The refugees were living in commiserable conditions.  难民们生活在悲惨的境况中。|例句：I felt commiserable after failing the exam. 我考试不及格后感到很痛苦。|近义词：wretched, pathetic, miserable, deplorable, pitiful|反义词：happy, cheerful, comfortable, fortunate|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French *commiserable, from Latin miserabilis ‘pitiable’, from miserari ‘pity’, from miser ‘wretched’.
