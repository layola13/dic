affiliative:/əˈfɪliətɪv/ | adj. | 亲和的，寻求联系的 | Relating to or promoting affiliation or connection.  Having a tendency to affiliate with others. |例句：Her affiliative nature made her a popular member of the team. 她的亲和力使她成为团队中受欢迎的成员。

|例句：Affiliative behaviors, such as grooming and huddling, strengthen social bonds.  诸如梳理毛发和拥抱之类的亲和行为可以加强社会联系。| 近义词：social, gregarious, sociable, companionable | 反义词：antisocial, withdrawn, aloof | 词性: adjective

词源: From the verb 'affiliate' + '-ive'.  'Affiliate' comes from the Latin 'affiliare', meaning 'to adopt as a son'.
