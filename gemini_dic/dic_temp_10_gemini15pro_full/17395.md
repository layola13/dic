beakful:/ˈbiːkfʊl/
noun
一喙之量；鸟喙所能容下的食物量
The amount of food a bird can hold in its beak.
The robin flew back to its nest with a beakful of worms. 知更鸟带着一喙的虫子飞回了巢穴。

The pelican scooped up a beakful of fish. 鹈鹕用喙舀起满满一口鱼。
近义词: mouthful
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "empty beak")
词性: n.
