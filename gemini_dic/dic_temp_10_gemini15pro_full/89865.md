ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *horologium, from Ancient Greek ὡρολόγιον (hōrológion, “instrument for telling the hour”), from ὥρα (hṓra, “hour, time”) + -λόγιον (-lógion, suffix forming nouns denoting instruments or devices).
