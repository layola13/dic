buffable:/ˈbʌfəbl/
adjective
可抛光的，可擦亮的
able to be buffed (polished by rubbing).
The car's paint is buffable to a high sheen. 这辆车的油漆可以抛光到很高的光泽度。
This leather is buffable and will look great with a little polish.  这种皮革可以抛光，稍微打磨一下就会看起来很棒。
synonyms: polishable, burnishable
antonyms: unpolishable, dull, matte
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From buff (v.) + -able.  "Buff" meaning "to polish" originates from Old French "buffer" or "buffeter" meaning "to strike, to puff."
