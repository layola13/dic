left-hander:/ˌleftˈhændər/| n.|左撇子|A person who uses their left hand more naturally than their right hand for writing and other tasks.|例句：My brother is a left-hander. 我的兄弟是个左撇子。

例句：Many famous artists were left-handers. 很多著名的艺术家都是左撇子。


近义词：southpaw|反义词:right-hander|词性:noun
