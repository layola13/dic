dodecahedron:/ˌdoʊdekəˈhiːdrən/ /ˌdəʊdekəˈhiːdrən/ | n. |十二面体|A three-dimensional shape with twelve faces, each of which is a pentagon. |例句：The ancient Greeks were fascinated by the dodecahedron. 古希腊人对十二面体很着迷。

例句：He held up a shimmering dodecahedron made of glass. 他举起一个闪闪发光的玻璃十二面体。|近义词：polyhedron |反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider shapes with different numbers of faces like tetrahedron, cube, octahedron, etc.)|词性:noun
