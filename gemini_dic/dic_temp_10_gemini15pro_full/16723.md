barrulet:/ˈbærʊlət/ | n. | 横条, 小横杆 | A narrow bar or stripe, especially one forming part of a heraldic field. | The shield features three barrulets argent on a field azure. 盾牌的特征是在蔚蓝的底色上有三条银色的横条。| synonym: bar, stripe, band | antonym: field (in heraldry) | noun
barrulet:/ˈbærʊlət/ | n. | 小栅, 障板 | A small bar or barrier.  (Less common usage) |  The barrulets on the window prevented the child from falling out.  窗户上的小栅栏防止了孩子掉出去。| synonym: barrier, railing, guard | antonym: opening | noun
