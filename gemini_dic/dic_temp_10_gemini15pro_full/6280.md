amorette:/ˌæməˈrɛt/| n.|小爱神，丘比特，恋爱，调情|A cherubic figure, often depicted as a small, winged child symbolizing love; a brief love affair or flirtation.|She felt a surge of amorette when he smiled at her. 她在他对她微笑时感到一阵心动。|近义词：cupid, cherub, flirtation, romance|反义词：hatred, animosity|词性:noun

词源:
From French *amorette*, diminutive of *amour* "love", from Latin *amor*.
