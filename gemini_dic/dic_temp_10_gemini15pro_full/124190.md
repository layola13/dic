moschatel:/ˈmɒskəˌtɛl/ | n. |麝香草|A small perennial plant (Adoxa moschatellina) of the family Adoxaceae, having small, yellowish-green flowers with a musky scent. It is native to Europe, Asia, and North America.|The moschatel bloomed early this spring in the shady part of the garden.|近义词:townhall clock, muskroot|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *moschatel, from Old French moscatel, muscatel (“musk”), from Late Latin muscatellus (“musky”), diminutive of Latin muscatus (“musky”), from muscus (“musk”). Ultimately from Ancient Greek μόσχος (móskhos, “musk”).
