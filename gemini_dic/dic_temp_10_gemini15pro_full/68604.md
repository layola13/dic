fantasizes:/ˈfæntəˌsaɪzɪz/
verb
幻想，想像
indulges in fantasies; daydreams; creates imaginary scenarios.
She often fantasizes about winning the lottery. 她经常幻想中彩票。
近义词:daydreams, imagines, muses, dreams
反义词: reasons, analyzes, thinks logically
