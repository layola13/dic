antiracists:/ˌæntiˈreɪsɪsts/
n.|反种族主义者|People who actively oppose racism and discrimination based on race.|Many antiracists marched in the protest against racial inequality. 许多反种族主义者参加了反对种族不平等的抗议游行。|Synonyms: egalitarians, integrationists|Antonyms: racists, bigots|Noun
antiracist:/ˌæntiˈreɪsɪst/
adj.|反种族主义的|Opposing or combating racism.|The organization promotes antiracist policies and education. 该组织倡导反种族主义政策和教育。|Synonyms: anti-discrimination, anti-bigotry|Antonyms: racist, discriminatory|Adjective

词源:
"anti-" (prefix) + "racist"
"anti-" comes from Greek, meaning "against" or "opposed to".
"racist" comes from "race" + "-ist" (suffix indicating a person who practices or believes in something).
