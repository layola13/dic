clinoid: /ˈklaɪnɔɪd/ | adj. n. | 蝶骨床突的，蝶鞍状的 |  Resembling a bedpost; specifically, relating to any of several bony processes of the sphenoid bone.  These processes form part of the sella turcica, which houses the pituitary gland. | The anterior clinoid processes are prominent bony landmarks.  前床突是重要的骨性标志。 |  The surgeon carefully identified the clinoid processes during the procedure. 手术过程中，外科医生仔细地识别了床突。 | synonymous:  sphenoidal | antonymous: (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-sphenoidal" could be considered) | adjective, noun
