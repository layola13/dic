outrigs: /ˈaʊtrɪɡz/ | n. | 支腿，舷外支架，平衡杆 |  A framework extending outward from the side of a boat or canoe to prevent it from capsizing, or a similar structure on a crane, aircraft, or other vehicle.  | The outriggers on the canoe provided stability in the rough water. 独木舟上的支腿在汹涌的水面上提供了稳定性。 | synonyms: stabilizers, supports | antonyms: N/A | noun

outrigger: /ˈaʊtrɪɡər/ | n. | 支腿，舷外支架，平衡杆 | A beam, spar, or framework projecting from the side of a boat to give it stability. Also, a canoe or boat stabilized by such a structure. | The outrigger helped the small boat navigate the choppy waters.  舷外支架帮助小船在波涛汹涌的水面上航行。 | synonyms: stabilizer, support | antonyms: N/A | noun
