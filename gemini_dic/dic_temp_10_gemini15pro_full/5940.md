amblotic: /æmˈblɒtɪk/
adjective
流产的，导致流产的
Causing abortion; abortifacient.
例句：Some herbs have amblotic properties and should be avoided during pregnancy.  一些草药具有导致流产的特性，怀孕期间应避免使用。

例句：The doctor warned her about the amblotic effects of certain medications. 医生警告她某些药物会导致流产。

近义词：abortifacient, aborticide
反义词：None (The opposite concept would be something that *prevents* abortion, but there isn't a single established word for this.)
词性：adjective
