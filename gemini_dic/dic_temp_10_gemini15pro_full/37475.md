commissively:/kəˈmɪsɪvli/
adverb
承诺地，带有承诺意味地
In a manner that expresses or implies a commitment or promise.
She spoke commissively about supporting the project. 她承诺支持这个项目。
近义词:promisingly, obligingly
反义词:noncommittally, evasively
词性:adverb

commissive:/kəˈmɪsɪv/
