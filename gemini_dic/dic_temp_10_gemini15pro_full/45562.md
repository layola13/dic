curlew:/ˈkɜːljuː/ (US) /ˈkɜːljuː/ (UK)| n. |麻鹬，杓鹬|A large wading bird with a long, downcurved bill, brownish plumage, and a distinctive call.|例句：The curlew's mournful cry echoed across the moor. 麻鹬的哀鸣声回荡在荒野上。

例句：We spotted a curlew probing the mudflats for food. 我们看到一只麻鹬在泥滩上觅食。

|近义词：sandpiper, snipe, godwit|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *corliu (unattested), from corl "to cry out", of imitative origin.
