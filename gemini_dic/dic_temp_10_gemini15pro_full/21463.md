blowth:/bloʊθ/
n.
开花, 盛开; 花, 花期
The act or process of blossoming; the state of being in flower; bloom.
The apple trees are in full blowth. 苹果树正盛开。
近义词:blossom, bloom, flowering
反义词:decay, decline, withering
词性:名词

Etymology: From Middle English *blōthe, from Old English *blōwþu, from *blōwan (“to blow, bloom”). Cognate with Dutch bloei (“bloom”), German Blüte (“blossom”), Icelandic blómi (“blossom”).
