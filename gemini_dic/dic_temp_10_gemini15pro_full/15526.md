bag-of-tricks:/ˌbæɡ əv ˈtrɪks/ | n. | 花招；诡计 | A set of deceptive or manipulative tactics. |  The con man used a bag of tricks to swindle his victims. 骗子用各种花招来诈骗他的受害者。 | synonyms:  schemes, ruses, ploys, subterfuges, machinations | antonyms: honesty, straightforwardness, sincerity | noun
