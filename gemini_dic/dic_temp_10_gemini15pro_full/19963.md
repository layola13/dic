bioengineering:/ˌbaɪoʊˌɛndʒɪˈnɪrɪŋ/
n.
生物工程；生物工程学
The application of engineering principles to biological systems.  This often involves the design and development of new medical devices, artificial organs, and other technologies to improve human health.
Bioengineering is a rapidly growing field with the potential to revolutionize medicine. 生物工程是一个快速发展的领域，有可能彻底改变医学。
Bioengineers are working on developing new ways to treat cancer. 生物工程师正在研究治疗癌症的新方法。
近义词:biomedical engineering, genetic engineering
反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially "natural biology")
词性:noun
