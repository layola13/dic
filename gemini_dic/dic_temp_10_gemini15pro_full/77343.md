gaud:/ɡɔːd/| n.| 华丽的装饰；俗丽的炫耀；热闹的庆祝活动|An ostentatious display or ornament; a showy trinket. A festive celebration or entertainment, often of a boisterous or vulgar nature.|The carnival was a gaudy gaud, full of flashing lights and loud music.  嘉年华是一个俗丽的庆祝活动，充满了闪烁的灯光和喧闹的音乐。|Synonyms: finery, bauble, trinket, festivity, revelry|Antonyms: simplicity, austerity|noun
