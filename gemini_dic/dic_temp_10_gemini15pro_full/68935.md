fatals:/ˈfeɪtlz/
noun
致命错误，致命事故，致命弱点 (通常复数)
errors or weaknesses that cause failure or disaster.
The project was plagued by a series of fatals. 这个项目被一系列致命错误困扰。
synonyms: disasters, catastrophes, critical flaws, fatal flaws, fatal errors
antonyms: advantages, strengths, assets, benefits
词性: 名词

fatals:/ˈfeɪtlz/
adjective (rarely used)
致命的，毁灭性的 (极少使用，通常使用fatal)
causing death or destruction.  (It's more common to use "fatal" in the singular form.)
This proved a fatals blow to their plans. (Less common usage) 这证明是对他们计划的致命打击。(较少见的用法)
synonyms: deadly, lethal, destructive, catastrophic
antonyms: harmless, benign, beneficial, life-saving
词性: 形容词
