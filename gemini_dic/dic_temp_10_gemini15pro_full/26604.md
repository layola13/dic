cached:/kæʃt/ | adj. |缓存的，高速缓存的 |Stored in a cache, especially a computer's memory, so that it can be quickly retrieved.| The webpage was cached so it loaded quickly. |网页被缓存了，所以加载很快。| 近义词: stored, saved, memoized | 反义词: uncached, live | 词性:形容词

词源:  From "cache" (n.) + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives from nouns).  "Cache" itself comes from French "cache" (hiding place), from "cacher" (to hide), ultimately from Vulgar Latin "*coacticare" (to press together, pile up).
