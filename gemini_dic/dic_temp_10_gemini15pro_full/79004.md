gist:/dʒɪst/| n.|主旨，要点，梗概|The gist of his argument was that we needed to invest more in education. 他论证的要点是我们需要在教育上加大投资。|Example 1: I didn't catch every word, but I got the gist of the conversation. 我没有听懂每个字，但我理解了谈话的要点。

Example 2: Can you give me the gist of the report? 你能告诉我报告的要点吗？|Synonyms: essence, core, substance, crux|Antonyms: details, specifics, trivialities|noun
