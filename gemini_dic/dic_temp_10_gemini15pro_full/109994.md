libidinousness:/lɪˌbɪdɪnəsnəs/
|n.|好色，淫荡|The state of being libidinous; excessive sexual desire or lustfulness.  Characterized by strong sexual urges or interests.|His libidinousness was apparent in his constant flirtations. 他的好色表现在他不停地调情。|Synonyms: lustfulness, lasciviousness, lechery, salaciousness|Antonyms: chastity, purity, abstinence|Noun


Etymology:
From Late Latin *libidinōsus*, from Latin libido (“desire, lust, pleasure”) + -ōsus (suffix forming adjectives of quality).
