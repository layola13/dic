hothousing:/ˈhɑːthaʊzɪŋ/ /ˈhɒthaʊzɪŋ/
n.|强行培养，温室培养|The practice of educating a child at an accelerated pace, often from a very young age, to achieve exceptional levels of academic or artistic skill.|Many parents resort to hothousing their children in the hope of securing them a place at a prestigious university.|近义词：intensive training, accelerated learning, cramming|反义词:laid-back parenting, natural development|词性:名词
