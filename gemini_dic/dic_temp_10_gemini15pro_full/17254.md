bayed:/beɪd/
verb
吠叫；大声叫喊
gave a deep, prolonged howl, typically of a dog or wolf.
The hounds bayed at the moon.  猎犬对着月亮吠叫。
近义词:howled, barked, yelped
反义词:whimpered, whined
词性:v.

bayed:/beɪd/
adjective
(of a color) reddish-brown
红棕色的
a bayed horse. 一匹红棕色的马。
近义词:chestnut, reddish-brown, maroon
反义词:n/a
词性:adj.

PHRASE->
bay for blood:  强烈要求惩罚或报复  The crowd bayed for blood after the team lost the championship. 球队输掉冠军后，人群强烈要求惩罚。
bay the moon:  做无用功，徒劳  He's baying the moon trying to convince her to change her mind. 他试图说服她改变主意，简直是徒劳无功。
bay at someone: 对某人咆哮或大声斥责  The boss bayed at his employees for their poor performance.  老板对员工糟糕的表现大声斥责。
keep someone/something at bay:  阻止某人或某事靠近或造成伤害 The high fence kept the wolves at bay. 高高的栅栏阻止了狼的靠近。
hold/keep something at bay:  控制住某事物（尤指令人不快的事物） She struggled to keep her fears at bay. 她努力控制住自己的恐惧。
