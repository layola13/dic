despot:/ˈdespɒt/ (UK), /ˈdespɑːt/ (US)| n. |暴君，专制君主|A ruler or other person who holds absolute power, typically one who exercises it in a cruel or oppressive way.|例句：The despot ruled the country with an iron fist. 这位暴君以铁腕统治着这个国家。

例句：The people lived in fear of the despot's wrath. 人民生活在对暴君暴怒的恐惧之中。

近义词：tyrant, dictator, autocrat|反义词：democrat|词性：noun
