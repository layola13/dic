noninsect:/nɒnˈɪnsɛkt/ | n. |非昆虫|Any creature that is not an insect, especially small invertebrates.  A non-insect arthropod, like a spider or a centipede.|例句：Spiders are noninsects. 蜘蛛是非昆虫。|例句：Many people mistakenly think spiders are insects, but they are actually noninsects. 很多人误以为蜘蛛是昆虫，但它们实际上是非昆虫。|近义词：invertebrate, non-arthropod (in some contexts)|反义词：insect|词性：名词
