hypokinetic:/ˌhaɪpoʊkɪˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
运动不足的，运动机能减退的
Characterized by or resulting from decreased bodily movement.  Relating to diminished or abnormally slow movement.
Hypokinetic diseases are conditions caused by a lack of physical activity. 运动不足的疾病是由缺乏身体活动引起的疾病。
A hypokinetic lifestyle can lead to various health problems.  运动不足的生活方式会导致各种健康问题。
近义词:sedentary, inactive
反义词:hyperkinetic, active
词性:adjective

Etymology:  From Greek hypo- (under, less) + kinetic (of or relating to motion).
