| adj. |

| Craniometrical data can be useful in forensic anthropology.  颅骨测量数据在法医人类学中可能很有用。 |

craniometry: /ˌkreɪniˈɒmɪtri/
| n. |
| 颅骨测量法，头骨测量学 |
| The scientific measurement of skulls, especially to determine their characteristic relationship to sex, body type, or genetic population. |
| Craniometry was a popular field of study in the 19th century.  颅骨测量法是19世纪的一个热门研究领域。 |

| Modern techniques have largely replaced craniometry in anthropological research.  现代技术在很大程度上取代了人类学研究中的颅骨测量法。 |
| 近义词: cephalometry |
| 反义词: N/A |
| 词性: noun |


词源:
cranio- (prefix):  from Greek "kranion" meaning "skull"
-metrical (suffix): from Greek "metron" meaning "measure"
