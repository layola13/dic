hyperaggregated:/ˌhaɪpərˈæɡrɪɡeɪtɪd/
adjective|过度聚集的，过度凝集的|Characterized by excessive aggregation or clumping, typically of cells like platelets.|Platelets became hyperaggregated in response to the stimulus.|近义词：overagglutinated, excessively clumped|反义词:dispersed, disaggregated|词性:adjective
词源: "Hyper-" (Greek: ὑπέρ, hupér, 'over, above') + "aggregated" (Latin: aggregatus, past participle of aggregare, 'to add to a flock').
