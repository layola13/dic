dishabit:/dɪsˈhæbɪt/
verb
使不习惯，使不再居住
to cause to give up a habit; to drive away from a place of habitation
The noise of the construction dishabited the family from their home.  建筑施工的噪音使这家人无法继续住在家里。

The war dishabited the village. 战争使这个村庄荒无人烟。

近义词:dislodge, displace, evict, expel
反义词:inhabit, settle, populate
词性:v.
