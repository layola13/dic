concuss:/kənˈkʌs/| vt.|震荡，使脑震荡|To injure the brain by a violent blow, causing temporary unconsciousness or confusion.|The fall concussed him badly. 这次摔倒让他脑震荡很严重。|近义词：stun, daze, shock|反义词: heal, cure|verb

concussion:/kənˈkʌʃən/| n.|脑震荡|A violent jarring or shaking that results in injury; especially, a jarring injury of the brain resulting in disturbance of cerebral function.|He suffered a concussion in the accident. 他在事故中脑震荡。|近义词：shock, trauma, injury|反义词: recovery, healing|noun
