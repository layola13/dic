ETYMOLOGY->
From blameless + -ly.  Blameless from blame + -less. Blame ultimately from Old French *blasmer (modern French blâmer), from Late Latin blasphemare "to blaspheme," from Greek blasphēmein "to speak evil of, revile, slander."
