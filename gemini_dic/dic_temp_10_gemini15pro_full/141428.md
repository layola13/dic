ophthalmite:/ˌɒfθælˈmaɪtɪs/ | n. |眼炎|Inflammation of the eye, especially of the eyeball. |  The veterinarian diagnosed the cat with ophthalmite. 兽医诊断这只猫患有眼炎。|  The ophthalmite caused the dog considerable discomfort. 眼炎使这只狗感到非常不舒服。| 近义词: eye inflammation, uveitis | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 healthy eyes 健康的眼睛) | 词性: 名词
