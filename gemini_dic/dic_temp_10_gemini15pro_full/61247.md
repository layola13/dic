emanatively:/ˈemənətɪvli/
adverb
散发地，流露地
in a manner that emanates; by emanating.  Expressing something powerfully or effectively.
The love emanated emanatively from her heart.  爱从她的心中散发出来。
Near Antonyms: reservedly, restrainedly
