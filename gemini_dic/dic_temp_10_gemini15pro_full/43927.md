crewet:/ˈkruːɪt/ | n. |  小瓶, 油壶, 调味瓶 | A small vessel or bottle for holding sauces, oils, or condiments. | The chef filled the crewet with olive oil.  厨师把橄榄油装满了小瓶。 | synonyms: cruet, vial, flask | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a container) | noun


Etymology:  From Old French *creue*, diminutive of *crue* "pot, earthen pot," from Old Norse *krukka* "pitcher, jug".
