hypermeter:/ˌhaɪpərˈmiːtər/| n.|超量，长音步，超节拍|A metrical unit composed of two or more feet, or a rhythmic unit larger than a measure.  A rhythmic unit perceived as a single unit but encompassing smaller rhythmic units.|The poem's complex hypermeter creates a sense of unease. 这首诗复杂的超节律营造了一种不安的感觉。|Synonyms: rhythmic unit, metrical unit|Antonyms: N/A|noun

Etymology: From Greek *hyper* (over, beyond) + *metron* (measure).
