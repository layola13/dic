deputised:/ˈdɛpjʊtaɪzd/
verb (past tense)|代理的，委任的|Having been appointed to act as a substitute for someone else.|She deputised for the manager while he was on holiday. 她在他休假期间代理经理。|Synonyms: substituted, acted for, stood in for|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider "primary" or "principal")|v.

deputise:/ˈdɛpjʊtaɪz/
