meddlingly:/ˈmedlɪŋli/
adverb
爱管闲事地，干预地
In a way that involves interfering in something that is not one's concern.
She meddled meddingly in her sister's marriage. 她过分干涉她妹妹的婚姻。
Synonyms: interfering, intrusive, officious, nosy
Antonyms: respectful, unintrusive, discreet
词性: 副词

meddle:/ˈmedl/
verb
干涉，干预，管闲事
Interfere in or busy oneself unduly with something that is not one's concern.
Don't meddle in my affairs. 不要干涉我的事情。
Synonyms: interfere, intervene, intrude
Antonyms: ignore, neglect, disregard
词性: 动词
