dopamine:/ˈdōpəˌmēn/ | n. | 多巴胺 | A neurotransmitter associated with feelings of pleasure, reward, and motivation. It plays a crucial role in movement, memory, and learning. |  The release of dopamine in the brain is associated with pleasurable activities like eating and exercise. 大脑中多巴胺的释放与进食和运动等愉悦的活动有关。

| Synonyms: neurochemical, transmitter | Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but dopamine *inhibitors* exist) | noun
