excreta:/ɪkˈskriːtə/
n.
排泄物，粪便
Waste matter discharged from the body, especially feces and urine.
The farmer used animal excreta as fertilizer.  农民用动物粪便作肥料。

excreta:/ɪkˈskriːtə/
n.
排泄物
Bodily waste, especially feces and urine.
The analysis of the excreta revealed traces of the poison. 对排泄物的分析显示出毒药的痕迹。

近义词:feces, droppings, waste, dung, stool
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑"intake"摄入)
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *excrēta*, neuter plural of *excrētus*, past participle of *excernere* "to sift out, separate," from *ex-* "out" + *cernere* "to distinguish, separate, sift."
