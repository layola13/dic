nutraceuticals:/ˌnuːtrəˈsuːtɪkəlz/| n. |营养保健品；保健食品|A food containing health-giving additives and having medicinal benefit.|Example: Many consumers are turning to nutraceuticals to improve their health. 许多消费者正在转向营养保健品来改善他们的健康。|Example: The company specializes in the development and marketing of new nutraceuticals. 这家公司专门从事新型营养保健品的开发和营销。|Synonyms: functional foods, dietary supplements, health supplements|Antonyms: junk food, processed food|Noun
