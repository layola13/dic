comitiva:/kəʊmɪˈtiːvə/ | n. |  随行人员，随从；代表团 | A group of people accompanying an important person, especially on a formal or official visit; a retinue or delegation. | The president's comitiva included several advisors and security personnel. 总统的随行人员包括几位顾问和安保人员。

The trade comitiva traveled to Asia to explore new markets.  贸易代表团前往亚洲探索新市场。 | synonyms: entourage, retinue, cortege, escort, suite,  delegation, mission | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider using "individual," "loner," or describing a lack of accompaniment) | noun
