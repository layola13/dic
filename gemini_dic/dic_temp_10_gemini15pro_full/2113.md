adaptability:/əˌdæptəˈbɪləti/ | n. |适应性，适应能力 |The ability to adjust to new conditions.  |The chameleon's adaptability allows it to survive in various environments. 变色龙的适应能力使它能够在各种环境中生存。

The company's adaptability to market changes is key to its success.  公司对市场变化的适应能力是其成功的关键。|近义词：flexibility, versatility, adjustability |反义词：inflexibility, rigidity |词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From adapt + -ability. Adapt comes from Late Latin *adaptare, from Latin ad- 'to' + aptare 'to fit'.

USAGE->
She showed great adaptability in her new role. = 她在新角色中表现出极大的适应能力。
His adaptability was crucial to his success in a foreign country. = 他的适应能力对于他在异国的成功至关重要。

PHRASE->
adaptability to change = 适应变化的能力
adaptability skills = 适应技能
cognitive adaptability = 认知适应性
cultural adaptability = 文化适应性
environmental adaptability = 环境适应性
