metoche:/ˈmɛtəki/ | n. | 分租房; 合租房; (尤指)合租公寓 | A metoche is a shared apartment, especially one occupied by students or young professionals. |  Example: They found a cheap metoche near the university. 他们在大学附近找到了一个便宜的合租公寓。 | Example: Living in a metoche can be a great way to save money and meet new people. 住在合租公寓里是省钱和结识新朋友的好方法。 | Synonym: shared apartment, communal apartment, co-living space | Antonym: private apartment, single-family home | noun
