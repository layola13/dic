overbreathing:/ˌoʊvərˈbriːðɪŋ/
n.
过度换气
The act of breathing too deeply or too rapidly, causing an imbalance of oxygen and carbon dioxide in the blood.
She experienced dizziness and tingling in her fingers, symptoms of overbreathing. 她感到头晕和手指刺痛，这是过度换气的症状。
hyperventilation
hypoventilation
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + breathing, from breathe + -ing.  "Over-" prefix signifies "excessive" or "too much". "Breathe" comes from Old English "brēthan," meaning "to smell, exhale, breathe."
