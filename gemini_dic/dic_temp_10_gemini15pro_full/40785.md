|n.|欢乐；欢宴；热闹的聚会|The holiday season is a time for conviviality with family and friends. 假期是与家人和朋友欢聚的时刻。|Example: He was known for his conviviality and good humor. 他以热情好客和幽默著称。
Example:  The conviviality of the gathering made everyone feel welcome.  聚会的欢乐气氛让每个人都感到宾至如归。
|近义词：festivity, merrymaking, celebration, revelry|反义词：somberness, gloom, seriousness|词性:Noun (Singular)


Etymology: From Late Latin *convivialitas, from Latin convivialis ("of or belonging to a feast"), from conviva ("a guest at a feast"), from com- ("with") + vivere ("to live").  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *gʷih₃wós ("alive").
