brazenface: /ˈbreɪzənˌfeɪs/ | n. | 厚颜无耻的人；无赖 | A person who is brazenly impudent or shameless. | He had the brazenface to ask for a raise after being late every day this week.  他竟然有脸要求加薪，这周他每天都迟到。| 近义词: scoundrel, rogue, impudent person | 反义词: shy person, modest person | 词性: 名词

brazenfaced: /ˈbreɪzənˌfeɪst/ | adj. | 厚颜无耻的；无赖的 | Shamelessly impudent or bold. |  She gave him a brazenfaced stare. 她厚颜无耻地盯着他。| 近义词: impudent, shameless, bold | 反义词: shy, modest, ashamed | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From "brazen" (made of brass) + "face".  The original sense was probably related to having a face as hard and unyielding as brass.  Over time, this evolved to the current meaning of shameless boldness.
