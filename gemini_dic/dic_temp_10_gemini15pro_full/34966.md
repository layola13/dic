cloddishnesses:/ˈklɑːdɪʃnɪsɪz/
noun
笨拙；粗俗；愚钝
The plural form of cloddishness, referring to multiple instances or aspects of being cloddish.  It describes a lack of social grace, refinement, or intelligence.
His cloddishnesses embarrassed his wife at the party. 他的笨拙在派对上让他的妻子感到尴尬。
Her repeated cloddishnesses made her seem unsuited for the diplomatic post. 她屡次的粗俗行为使她看起来不适合担任外交职位。
Synonyms: awkwardnesses, gaucheries, clumsinesses, boorishnesses
Antonyms: refinements, elegances, sophistications
