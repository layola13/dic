infixes:/ˈɪnfɪksɪz/| n. |中缀|An infix is an affix that is inserted within a word stem (an existing word).|Example:  "abso-freakin'-lutely" where "freakin'" is an infix inserted into "absolutely".|近义词: affix, morpheme|反义词: prefix, suffix|noun
