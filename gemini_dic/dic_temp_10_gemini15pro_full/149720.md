palaestric:/ˌpælɪˈstrɪk/| adj.|角力的，摔跤的，体育场的|Of or relating to wrestling; pertaining to the palaestra (a wrestling school or gymnasium in ancient Greece).|The ancient Greeks held palaestric contests as part of their athletic competitions.|Synonyms: wrestling, athletic, gymnastic|Antonyms: sedentary, inactive|adjective

palaestra:/pəˈliːstrə/| n.|角力学校，体育馆，摔跤场|In ancient Greece, a public place for exercise in wrestling and athletics.|The palaestra was a central part of Greek social and athletic life.|Synonyms: gymnasium, wrestling school, training ground|Antonyms:  None (a specific place, hard to have a direct opposite)|noun
