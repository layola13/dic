lovely: /ˈlʌvli/ (US), /ˈlʌvli/ (UK) | adj. | 可爱的，美丽的，令人愉快的 | Pleasing, delightful, beautiful; inspiring love or affection. |  The hotel had a lovely view of the ocean.  这家酒店的海景很美。
