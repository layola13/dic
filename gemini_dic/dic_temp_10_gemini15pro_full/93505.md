ilvaite:/ˈɪlvaɪt/| n. |黑柱石，硅灰石|A black sorosilicate mineral of iron and calcium, occurring as prismatic crystals in metamorphic rocks.  A sorosilicate of calcium and iron.|例句：Ilvaite is a relatively rare mineral. 黑柱石是一种相对稀有的矿物。

例句：The geologist identified the black crystal as ilvaite. 地质学家将黑色晶体鉴定为黑柱石。|近义词：lievrite (another name for ilvaite),  andradite (similar in appearance but a different mineral) |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
