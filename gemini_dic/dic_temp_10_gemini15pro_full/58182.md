drowsed: /draʊzd/ | adj. | 昏昏欲睡的；困倦的 | Feeling sleepy and lethargic. |  I felt drowsy after taking the medication.  我服药后感到昏昏欲睡。| sleepy, lethargic, sluggish | alert, awake, energetic | adjective

drowse: /draʊz/ | v. |  打瞌睡；昏昏欲睡 | To sleep lightly; doze. | The warm sun made me drowse. 温暖的阳光让我昏昏欲睡。| doze, nap, slumber |  awaken, energize | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *drousen, from Old English *drūsian, of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to Old Norse drausa (“to fall, sink”).
