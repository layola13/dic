dyspeptically:/dɪsˈpɛptɪkli/ | adv. | 消化不良地，胃不舒服地 |  In a manner relating to or suffering from dyspepsia (indigestion). | He complained dyspeptically about the rich food. 他抱怨油腻的食物让他消化不良。 | Near Synonyms:  indigestibly, queasily | Antonyms: comfortably, easily | Word Type: Adverb

dyspepsia: /dɪsˈpɛpsiə/ | n. | 消化不良 | Indigestion |  He suffered from dyspepsia after eating too much. 他吃太多后消化不良。 | Near Synonyms: indigestion, upset stomach | Antonyms: good digestion | Word Type: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek δυσ- (dus-)  "bad, difficult" + πέψις (pepsis) "digestion," from πέσσειν (pessin) "to cook, to digest."
