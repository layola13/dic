monument:/ˈmɒnjumənt/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnjumənt/ (US)| n. |纪念碑，纪念物；遗迹，遗址；丰碑|A structure, such as a building or sculpture, erected as a memorial.  Something that serves as a lasting reminder of a person, event, or achievement.|例句：The Washington Monument is a towering obelisk. 华盛顿纪念碑是一座高耸的方尖碑。

例句：This building is a monument to Victorian architecture. 这座建筑是维多利亚时代建筑的典范。|近义词：memorial, tribute, testament|反义词: N/A |词性: Noun
