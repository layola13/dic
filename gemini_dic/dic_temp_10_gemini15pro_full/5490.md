aloofness:/əˈluːfnəs/| n.|冷漠，疏远，超然|The state of being distant, reserved, and uninterested in other people.|His aloofness made it difficult to get to know him. 他的冷漠让人难以了解他。|She maintained an aloofness from the other students. 她与其他学生保持着距离。|近义词：remoteness, detachment, indifference, reserve|反义词：warmth, friendliness, sociability, involvement|词性:noun

aloof:/əˈluːf/| adj.|冷漠的，疏远的，超然的|Not friendly or forthcoming; cool and distant.|He remained aloof despite their efforts to be friendly. 尽管他们努力表示友好，他仍然保持冷漠。|She stood aloof from the crowd. 她远离人群站着。|近义词：distant, remote, detached, reserved|反义词：warm, friendly, sociable, involved|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From a-(on) + loof (windward side). Originally a nautical term referring to keeping a ship's sails close to the wind, hence keeping a distance.  The figurative sense of emotional distance developed later.
