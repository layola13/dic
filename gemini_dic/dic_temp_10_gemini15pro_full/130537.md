nonaccusing:/ˌnɒnəˈkjuːzɪŋ/
adjective
非指责的，不指责的
Not blaming or critical; conveying no accusation.
Her nonaccusing tone made him feel more comfortable. 她不指责的语气让他感觉更舒服。
近义词: understanding, sympathetic, forgiving, nonjudgmental
反义词: accusing, blaming, critical, judgmental
词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "accusing" (present participle of the verb "accuse")
