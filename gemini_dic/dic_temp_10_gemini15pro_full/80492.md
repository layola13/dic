goober:/ˈɡuːbər/| n.|花生，傻瓜，乡巴佬|A peanut, especially one grown in the southeastern US. Also used informally to refer to a foolish or unsophisticated person. |例句：He's such a goober, he wore mismatched socks to the prom. 他真是个傻瓜，竟然穿着不搭的袜子去参加舞会。

例句：Pass me a handful of those goobers.  递给我一把花生。|近义词：peanut, dolt, simpleton, yokel|反义词:genius, intellectual|词性:noun
