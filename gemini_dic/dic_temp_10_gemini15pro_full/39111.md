confronting:/kənˈfrʌntɪŋ/
adj.
对抗的，使人不安的
Presenting a challenge; difficult to deal with.
The confronting reality of climate change requires immediate action.  气候变化的严峻现实需要立即采取行动。
She found the confronting images of the war deeply disturbing. 她发现战争中那些直面的画面令人深感不安。
近义词: challenging, daunting, disturbing, unsettling
反义词: comforting, reassuring, soothing
词性: adjective

confront:/kənˈfrʌnt/
vt.
面对，对抗
To face or deal with a difficult situation or person.
He decided to confront his fears and try public speaking. 他决定面对自己的恐惧，尝试公开演讲。
The police confronted the suspect with the evidence. 警方用证据与嫌疑人对峙。
近义词: face, encounter, tackle, address
反义词: avoid, evade, ignore
词性: verb

词源:
From Old French confronter "to compare," from Latin confrontare "to compare, bring face to face," from com "together" + frontem (nominative frons) "forehead, brow, front." The notion is of bringing foreheads together, like animals about to fight.
