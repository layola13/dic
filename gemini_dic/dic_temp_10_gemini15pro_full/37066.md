combative:/kəmˈbætɪv/
| adj. | 好斗的，好战的|  Ready or inclined to fight or argue; aggressive. |  He had a combative personality. 他性格好斗。| aggressive, belligerent, pugnacious, antagonistic | passive, peaceful, cooperative | 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *combativus, from combātere (to fight together), from com- (together) + batere (to fight). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *bhau- (to strike).
