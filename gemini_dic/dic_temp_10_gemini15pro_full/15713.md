balanced:/ˈbælənst/| adj.|平衡的，均衡的，稳定的|Being in a state of equilibrium; having different parts or elements arranged in the correct proportions.|例句：She has a balanced diet. 她饮食均衡。|近义词：stable, steady, even, impartial, unbiased|反义词:unstable, uneven, biased, prejudiced|adjective

balanced:/ˈbælənst/| v.|（使）平衡，（使）均衡|To bring into or maintain in a state of equilibrium.|例句：He balanced the book on his head. 他把书顶在头上保持平衡。|近义词：stabilize, steady, equalize|反义词:destabilize, unbalance|verb
