noncurative:/ˌnɒnˈkjʊərətɪv/
adjective
非治愈性的，无法治愈的
Not able to cure a disease or medical condition.
The doctor informed him that his condition was noncurative. 医生告诉他，他的病情是无法治愈的。
His illness is considered noncurative at this stage.  他的病在现阶段被认为是无法治愈的。
近义词:incurable, irremediable, terminal
反义词:curable, remedial
词性:adjective
