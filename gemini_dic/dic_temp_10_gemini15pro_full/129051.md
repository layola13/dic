nitrified:/ˈnaɪtrɪfaɪd/
adjective
硝化的
converted into nitrates; treated with nitric acid.
The nitrified soil is rich in nutrients. 硝化的土壤富含营养。
近义词: oxidized, nitrate-rich
反义词: unnitrified, denitrified
词性: adjective

nitrify:/ˈnaɪtrɪfaɪ/
verb
硝化
treat or combine with nitric acid; convert (ammonia) into nitrates, especially with the help of bacteria.
Bacteria nitrify ammonia in the soil.  细菌在土壤中硝化氨。
近义词: oxidize
反义词: denitrify
词性: verb

词源: From nitrify + -ed/-ied.  "nitrify" comes from French *nitrifier,  from nitre (niter, saltpeter) + -fier (from Latin -ficare, to make).  Ultimately from Greek nitron + -fy.
