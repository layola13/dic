damneder:/ˈdæmədər/ | adj. | 更该死的，更可恶的 |  A non-standard and intensified form of "damned," meaning more thoroughly cursed or deserving of condemnation. Used to express stronger disapproval or anger than "damned." | "That damneder fool went and did it again!"  他那个更该死的傻瓜又去做了！ |  Near Synonyms: more damned, accursed, execrable | Opposite: blessed, sacred |  adjective
