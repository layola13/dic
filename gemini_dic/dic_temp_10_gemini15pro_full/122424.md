moanful:/ˈmoʊnfəl/ (US) /ˈməʊnfʊl/ (UK)| adj.| 悲叹的，哀伤的|Expressing sorrow or suffering through moans or complaints.| The dog gave a moanful howl when its owner left.  这只狗在主人离开时发出一声悲鸣。|The wind made a moanful sound as it blew through the trees. 风吹过树林，发出呜咽的声音。|近义词：mournful, plaintive, doleful, sorrowful|反义词：cheerful, joyful, happy|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From moan + -ful.
