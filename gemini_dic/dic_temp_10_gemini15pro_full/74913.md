fret-away:/frɛt əˈweɪ/
verb
消磨；烦躁不安地度过
to gradually wear something away by rubbing or gnawing, or to cause someone to become thin or weak, especially through worry
例句：He fretted away the hours worrying about the exam. 他为考试焦虑不安地度过了几个小时。

例句：The constant dripping water fretted away the stone. 持续滴落的水逐渐侵蚀了石头。
近义词：worry, erode, wear away
反义词：relax, build up, strengthen
词性：verb (动词)
