clamorer:/ˈklæmərər/| n. |喧闹者；大声疾呼者；强烈要求者|A person who shouts loudly or makes a vehement protest or demand.|The clamorers outside the government building demanded justice.  政府大楼外的抗议者强烈要求正义。|synonyms: protester, shouter, objector, complainer|antonyms: supporter, approver, conformist|noun


clamor:/ˈklæmər/| n. vi. |喧闹； clamor; 吵闹；大声疾呼|A loud and confused noise, especially that of people shouting vehemently.|The clamor of the crowd grew louder as the team scored. 球队得分后，人群的欢呼声越来越大。|synonyms: uproar, din, hubbub, outcry|antonyms: silence, quiet, stillness|noun, intransitive verb
