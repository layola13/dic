layettes:/leɪˈɛts/
noun
婴儿的全套服装及用品
A complete set of clothing and accessories for a newborn baby.
She carefully folded the tiny garments and placed them in the layette. 她仔细地叠好小衣服，放进婴儿的全套服装里。
synonyms: baby clothes, newborn outfit, infant wear
antonyms: adult clothing
