astringency:/əˈstrɪndʒənsi/| n.|涩味，收敛性|Astringency is the quality in a substance, typically one containing tannin, that causes the mouth to pucker.|The wine's astringency complemented the rich flavors of the cheese.  这种葡萄酒的涩味与奶酪的浓郁味道相得益彰。|近义词：tartness, dryness, sharpness|反义词:sweetness, smoothness|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *astringentia, from astringentem (nominative astringens), present participle of astringere "to bind fast," from ad- "to" + stringere "to draw tight."
