balsamous:/bɔːlˈseɪməs/| adj.|香脂的，有香脂香味的，像香脂的|Having the qualities of balsam; fragrant, resinous, healing.|The air was thick with the balsamous scent of pine needles. 空气中弥漫着松针的香味。|近义词：fragrant, aromatic, resinous|反义词:odorless, scentless|词性:形容词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from Old French balsamin, via late Latin from Greek balsamon ‘balsam’.
