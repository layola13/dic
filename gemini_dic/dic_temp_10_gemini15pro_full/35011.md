cloner:/ˈkloʊnər/ (US), /ˈkləʊnə(r)/ (UK)| n.|克隆人，克隆器|A person or device that makes clones.  Someone or something that creates a copy or duplicate. |The scientist was known as a master cloner, creating genetically identical mice for research.  这位科学家被称为克隆大师，他为研究创造了基因相同的老鼠。|Synonyms: duplicator, copier, replicator|Antonyms: originator, inventor|noun
