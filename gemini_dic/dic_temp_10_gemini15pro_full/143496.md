outglitters:/ˌaʊtˈɡlɪtərz/
verb
胜过…的光辉；比…更闪耀
surpasses in glitter or brilliance; shines more brightly than
Her dress outglittered everyone else's at the party. 她的礼服在派对上比其他任何人的都闪耀。
Synonyms: outshines, eclipses, surpasses
Antonyms: pales, dims, fades
词性: 动词
