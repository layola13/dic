h:/eɪtʃ/ | n. | the eighth letter of the alphabet |  H is between G and I in the alphabet. | H 在字母表中位于 G 和 I 之间。 | 近义词: (无，字母没有近义词) | 反义词: (无，字母没有反义词) | 词性: noun


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What is his name? = 他叫什么名字？
What does he do for a living? = 他以什么为生？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What is he doing now? = 他现在在做什么？
What is she having for lunch? = 她午饭吃什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What has he eaten today? = 他今天吃了什么？
What has she learned in school? = 她在学校学了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What has he been working on all week? = 他整个星期都在忙什么？
What has she been reading lately? = 她最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did he say to you? = 他对你说了什么？
What happened at the party? = 聚会上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was he thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间他在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had he done before he left? = 他离开之前做了什么？
What had she eaten before dinner? = 晚饭前她吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I arrived? = 我来之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她转专业之前一直在学什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will he do after graduation? = 毕业后他要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting tomorrow? = 明天的会议上他们会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will he have accomplished by the end of the year? = 到年底他将完成什么？
What will she have learned by then? = 到那时她将学到什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么专业多久了？
