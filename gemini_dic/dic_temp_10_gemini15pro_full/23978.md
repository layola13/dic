breathable:/ˈbriːðəbl/| adj.|透气的，可呼吸的|Allowing air to pass through; permeable to air.|例句：This jacket is made of a breathable fabric.  这件夹克是由透气面料制成的。|例句：Breathable bandages are essential for wound healing. 透气绷带对伤口愈合至关重要。|近义词：porous, permeable, airy|反义词：airtight, impermeable, non-porous|词性: 形容词


Etymology->
From breathe (v.) + -able.  "breathe" comes from Old English "brēthan," from Proto-Germanic "*brēthanan."  "-able" is a suffix of Latin origin, meaning "capable of being."
