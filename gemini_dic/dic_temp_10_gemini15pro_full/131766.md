nonconjugate:/nɒnˈkɒndʒʊɡɪt/ /nɑːnˈkɑːndʒəɡət/| adj.|非共轭的，不结合的|Not undergoing or marked by conjugation.  Relating to a verb that does not change its form according to person, number, or tense.|The virus remains nonconjugate and therefore highly infectious. 这种病毒保持非共轭状态，因此具有高度传染性。|近义词:unconjugated, unbound|反义词:conjugated, bound|词性:形容词

nonconjugate:/nɒnˈkɒndʒʊɡɪt/ /nɑːnˈkɑːndʒəɡət/| n.|非共轭物|A substance that does not undergo conjugation.|The experiment involved the use of a nonconjugate.  这个实验涉及使用一种非共轭物。|近义词:unconjugated substance|反义词:conjugate|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + conjugate.  "Conjugate" comes from Latin conjugatus, past participle of conjugare "to yoke together," from con- "together" + jugare "to yoke," related to jugum "yoke."
