funebrial:/fjuːˈnɪəriəl/
adjective
丧葬的；适合葬礼的
Relating to or suggestive of a funeral; mournful.
The procession had a somber, funebrial atmosphere. 这支队伍有一种忧郁的、适合葬礼的氛围。
The music was slow and funebrial.  这音乐缓慢而悲伤。
Synonyms: mournful, somber, funereal, doleful, lugubrious
Antonyms: joyful, celebratory, festive
adjective


Etymology->
From Latin *fūnebriālis*, from fūnebris (“of a funeral”), from fūnus (“funeral rites; death, corpse”).
