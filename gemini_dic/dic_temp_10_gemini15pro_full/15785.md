balik:/ˈbɑːlɪk/| n.|鱼; 回程; 返回; (尤指从国外)回家; 回到…; 恢复; 复原|A return, especially from abroad.  Also, a fish, often used in Southeast Asia. |例句：
The balik kampung rush during festive seasons can be chaotic.  节日期间返乡的人潮可能会很混乱。

My uncle brought back some dried balik from his trip to Malaysia. 我叔叔从马来西亚旅行回来带了一些鱼干。

|近义词：return, homecoming, fish|反义词:departure, going|词性:名词
