|n.|修改，改变，变更|There's been an alteration in our plans. 我们的计划有所改变。|
| |The tailor made some alterations to the suit. 裁缝对这套西装做了一些修改。|
| |Synonyms: change, modification, adjustment, amendment|
| |Antonyms: consistency, stability, uniformity|
|noun|


Etymology: From Late Middle English *alteracion*, from Late Latin *alterātiō*, from Latin *alterātus*, past participle of *alterāre* (“to change”), from *alter* (“other”).
