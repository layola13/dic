outblunder:/ˌaʊtˈblʌndər/
verb
犯更大的错误，在失误方面胜过
To surpass in blunders; to make more or bigger mistakes than.
He outblundered his opponent in the chess match, making three crucial errors. 他在棋赛中犯的错误比对手更多，犯了三个关键性的错误。
outblundered (past tense)
outblunders (3rd person singular present)
outblundering (present participle)
outblunder (base form)
Synonym: outmistake, outdo (in errors)
Antonym:  outsmart, outmaneuver (in a positive sense - avoiding errors)
