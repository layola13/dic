authenticator:/ɔːˈθɛntɪkeɪtər/ | n. | 身份验证器，认证器 | A device or program that establishes the identity of a user or device. |  Example:  I use a two-factor authenticator app on my phone for added security. 我在手机上使用双因素身份验证应用程序来增强安全性。

Example: The website requires an authenticator for login.  该网站要求使用身份验证器登录。| 近义词: verifier, validator | 反义词:  N/A | noun
