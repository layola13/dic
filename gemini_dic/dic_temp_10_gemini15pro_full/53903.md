discounted:/dɪˈskaʊntɪd/ | adj. | 打折的，贴现的 | Reduced in price. | The store offered discounted prices on all winter clothing.  这家商店所有冬季服装都打折出售。| Example: I bought a discounted book. 我买了一本打折的书。| Synonyms: reduced, cheap, sale, bargain | Antonyms: full-price, premium, expensive | adjective

discounted:/dɪˈskaʊntɪd/ | v. |  打折，贴现（discount的过去式和过去分词）| To reduce the price of something. |  The retailer discounted all items by 20% for the holiday weekend.  零售商在假日周末将所有商品打折20%。| Example:  They discounted the price because of a small defect. 因为有点小瑕疵，他们给打了折。| Synonyms: reduce, rebate, cut | Antonyms: increase, raise, mark up | verb
