inflectors:/ɪnˈflɛktərz/| n.| 变形者，屈折变化词 |A word or affix that indicates grammatical relations, such as tense, person, number, gender, mood, voice, or case.  |Example: The "-ed" in "walked" is an inflector indicating past tense.  "-ed" 在 "walked" 中是一个表示过去时的屈折词。|Synonyms: morphemes, affixes, endings|Antonyms: roots, stems|noun
