bubos:/ˈbjuːboʊz/| n. |腹股沟淋巴结肿大|Swollen lymph nodes in the groin, characteristic of bubonic plague. |The doctor examined the patient and found bubos, confirming the diagnosis of bubonic plague. 医生检查了病人，发现了腹股沟淋巴结肿大，证实了鼠疫的诊断。|近义词:swollen glands (肿大的腺体)|反义词: healthy lymph nodes (健康的淋巴结)|词性:noun (名词)
Etymology: From Late Latin *būbō, from Greek boubṓn ‘groin, swelling in the groin’.
