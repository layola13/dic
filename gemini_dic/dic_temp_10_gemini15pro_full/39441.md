connecting:/kəˈnɛktɪŋ/ | adj. |连接的，联系的 |Joining two or more things together; relating two or more things. | The connecting flight was delayed.  转机航班延误了。 | synonyms: linking, joining, associating, relating | antonyms: disconnecting, separating, isolating | adjective

connecting:/kəˈnɛktɪŋ/ | n. |连接，联系 |The act of joining or the state of being joined. | The connecting rods in the engine are crucial.  发动机中的连杆至关重要。 | synonyms: link, junction, connection | antonyms: disconnection, separation | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *connecten*, from Latin *connectere* ("to bind together"), from *con-* ("together") + *nectere* ("to bind").
