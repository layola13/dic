equipping:/ɪˈkwɪpɪŋ/
verb (present participle of equip)
装备;配备
The process of providing someone or something with the necessary tools, skills, or knowledge.
The army is equipping its soldiers with the latest weapons.  军队正在为士兵配备最新的武器。
They are equipping the school with new computers. 他们正在为学校配备新的电脑。
synonyms: furnishing, providing, supplying, outfitting, preparing
antonyms: stripping, depriving, divesting
