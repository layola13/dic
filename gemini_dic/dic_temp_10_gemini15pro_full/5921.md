ambitionist: /æmˈbɪʃənɪst/
n.|野心家，抱负过高的人|A person who is excessively ambitious, often to the point of ruthlessness or unscrupulously.| He was known as a ruthless ambitionist who would stop at nothing to achieve his goals. 他被称为一个冷酷无情的野心家，为了达到目的不择手段。|近义词: careerist, go-getter, social climber|反义词: underachiever, slacker|词性: 名词

Etymology: From ambition + -ist.  The suffix "-ist" forms nouns that refer to a person who practices or is concerned with something, or who holds certain principles, beliefs, or doctrines. In this case, it refers to a person who is characterized by ambition, often excessively so.
