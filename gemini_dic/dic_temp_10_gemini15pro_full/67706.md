exulting:/ɪɡˈzʌltɪŋ/ | adj. | 欣喜若狂的，欢腾的 | Feeling or expressing great joy or triumph. |  She raised her hands in an exulting gesture. 她举起双手，欣喜若狂。| 近义词: jubilant, triumphant, rejoicing | 反义词: despondent, dejected, miserable | 词性:形容词

exult:/ɪɡˈzʌlt/ | v. |  欢欣鼓舞，兴高采烈 | To show or feel a lively or triumphant joy; rejoice exceedingly. |  They exulted at their victory. 他们为自己的胜利而欢欣鼓舞。 | 近义词: rejoice, revel, triumph | 反义词: mourn, grieve, lament | 词性:动词

词源:  From Latin exsultare "to leap up, jump for joy," from ex- "out" + saltare "to leap, dance"
