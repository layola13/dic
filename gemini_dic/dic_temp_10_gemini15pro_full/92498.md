hypothermias:/ˌhaɪpəˈθɜːrmiəz/ | n. | 低体温（症）, 体温过低 |  A medical emergency that occurs when your body loses heat faster than it can produce heat, causing a dangerously low body temperature. | The hikers suffered from hypothermias after being lost in the blizzard.  这些徒步旅行者在暴风雪中迷路后患上了低体温症。 |  hypothermia (singular), low body temperature, coldness | fever, hyperthermia | noun
