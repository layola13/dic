outsell: /ˌaʊtˈsɛl/ /ˌaʊtˈsel/ | v. | 胜过，比...卖得好 | To sell more than something or someone else. | Toyota outsells all other car manufacturers in the world. 丰田汽车的销量超过了世界上所有其他汽车制造商。

Our new product is outselling the competition by a wide margin. 我们的新产品销量远远超过竞争对手。| 近义词: surpass, exceed, outstrip | 反义词: be outsold by | 词性: 动词
