PHRASE->
lock up:  锁好；关起来  Lock up your valuables before you leave. 离开前锁好你的贵重物品。
lock down: 封锁；禁闭  The school was on lock down after the threat.  受到威胁后，学校被封锁了。
lock in:  锁住；困住  The rising tide locked us in the cave.  上涨的潮水把我们困在了山洞里。
lock out:  把…关在外面  I locked myself out of my apartment. 我把自己锁在了公寓外面。
lock onto:  锁定目标  The missile locked onto the enemy aircraft. 导弹锁定了敌机。
