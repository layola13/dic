brewing:/ˈbruːɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbruːɪŋ/ (UK)| v.|酿造，冲泡，酝酿|To make beer or other alcoholic drinks. Also used figuratively to describe something developing or about to happen.|
Example 1:  They are brewing a new type of beer. 他们正在酿造一种新型啤酒。

Example 2: A storm is brewing on the horizon.  暴风雨正在地平线上酝酿。

brewing:/ˈbruːɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbruːɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|即将发生的，酝酿中的|Used to describe something that is developing or about to happen.|
Example 1: There's a brewing crisis in the financial markets. 金融市场正酝酿着一场危机。

Example 2:  He sensed a brewing conflict between the two groups. 他感觉到两组之间即将发生冲突。
近义词: impending, imminent, developing, gathering|
反义词: subsiding, dissipating, resolving|
词性: adjective|




词源: From Old English "breowan" meaning "to brew."  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*breuwan-" meaning "to boil, brew."
