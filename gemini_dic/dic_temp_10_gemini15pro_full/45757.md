cushion:/ˈkʊʃən/ (US), /ˈkʊʃn/ (UK)| n. v.|垫子，缓冲物；缓和，减轻|A soft bag filled with air, feathers, or other material, used for sitting, kneeling, or reclining on.  To make something softer or less harsh.|例句：She plumped up the cushions on the sofa. 她拍松了沙发上的垫子。|近义词：pillow, pad, bolster, buffer|反义词:aggravate, worsen|noun

cushion:/ˈkʊʃən/ (US), /ˈkʊʃn/ (UK)| v.|缓和，减轻|To make the effect of something less severe.|例句：The government took steps to cushion the economy against recession. 政府采取措施以缓和经济衰退。|近义词：soften, mitigate, alleviate, lessen|反义词:aggravate, worsen|verb
