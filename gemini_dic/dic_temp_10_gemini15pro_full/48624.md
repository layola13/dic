/dɪˈfensləsli/| adv.|无防备地，无力抵抗地|Without the ability to defend oneself; in a vulnerable manner.|The deer stood defencelessly in the headlights.  这只鹿无助地站在车前灯的光束里。|The child clung defencelessly to his mother as the stranger approached.  当陌生人靠近时，孩子无助地依偎在母亲身边。| 近义词: vulnerably, helplessly, weakly|反义词: powerfully, strongly, securely|词性: 副词
词源:  From "defenceless" + "-ly". "Defenceless" comes from "defence" (from Old French "defense") + "-less". Ultimately from Latin "defendere" (to ward off).
