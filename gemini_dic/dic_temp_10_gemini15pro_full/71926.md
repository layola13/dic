flibbertigibbet:/ˌflɪbərtiˈdʒɪbɪt/
| n. |轻浮的人；爱饶舌的人；反复无常的人|A frivolous, flighty, or excessively talkative person. Someone who is restless and irresponsible.|She's such a flibbertigibbet, always flitting from one thing to another.|近义词：scatterbrain, lightweight, featherbrain|反义词：serious person, responsible person|词性:noun

Etymology:
Likely a fanciful coinage, possibly imitating the sound of chattering or frivolous talk.  The first recorded use is from the 16th century.
