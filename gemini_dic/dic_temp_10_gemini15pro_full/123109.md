mongrelized: /ˈmʌŋɡrəlaɪzd/ /ˈmɑːŋɡrəlaɪzd/
| adj. | 混杂的，杂种的 |  Mixed or crossbred; having diverse elements or origins often of questionable value.  Derived from "mongrel," referring to a dog of mixed breed. Implies a loss of purity or original character. | The city's architecture is a mongrelized mix of old and new styles.  这座城市的建筑是新旧风格的混合体。

The neighborhood had become increasingly mongrelized over the years, with people from all backgrounds moving in. 多年来，随着来自不同背景的人们迁入，这个社区变得越来越混杂。 | 近义词: hybrid, mixed, crossbred, adulterated | 反义词: pure, unmixed, homogeneous | 词性: adjective
