dabchick:/ˈdæbtʃɪk/| n.|小䴙|A small grebe (aquatic bird) of the genus *Tachybaptus*, especially the Little Grebe (*Tachybaptus ruficollis*).  These birds are expert divers and swimmers, known for their quick dives underwater. |例句：The dabchick disappeared beneath the surface of the pond in a flash. 小䴙一瞬间消失在池塘水面下。

|例句：We watched the dabchick skillfully catch a small fish. 我们看着小䴙熟练地捉到一条小鱼。|近义词：Little Grebe, dabbler|反义词: (No specific antonym for a type of bird) |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *dab* (meaning to strike lightly, referring to the bird's bobbing motion) + *chick* (a young bird).
