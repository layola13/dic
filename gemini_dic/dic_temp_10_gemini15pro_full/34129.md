cladonia:/kləˈdoʊniə/ | n. |石蕊属|A genus of lichens commonly known as reindeer moss, cup lichen, or pixie-cup lichens. They are found in various habitats, including arctic and alpine tundra, boreal forests, and temperate woodlands. |The ground was covered with cladonia.|Synonyms: reindeer moss, cup lichen, pixie-cup lichen |Antonyms: N/A |noun

cladonia:/kləˈdoʊniə/ | n. |石蕊|A type of lichen that serves as a food source for reindeer and other animals. It is also used in some traditional medicines and as a decorative element in crafts.|Reindeer rely on cladonia during the winter months.|Synonyms: reindeer moss |Antonyms: N/A |noun
