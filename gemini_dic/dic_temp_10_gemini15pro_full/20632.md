blacklisting:/ˈblækˌlɪstɪŋ/
n.|列入黑名单，抵制|The act of putting someone or something on a blacklist.  This means excluding them from a privilege, service, mobility, or recognition. |The company faced blacklisting after the scandal. 这家公司在丑闻曝光后遭到抵制。|近义词：banning, boycotting, excluding|反义词:  approving, accepting, welcoming|词性:名词

blacklisted:/ˈblækˌlɪstɪd/
adj.|被列入黑名单的，被抵制的|Put on a blacklist; barred or excluded from some activity, privilege, or group.|The actor was blacklisted for his political views. 这位演员因其政治观点而被列入黑名单。|近义词：banned, boycotted, excluded|反义词: approved, accepted, welcomed|词性:形容词
