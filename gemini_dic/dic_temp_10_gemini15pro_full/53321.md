disabuse:/ˌdɪsəˈbjuːz/
verb
纠正，使醒悟，打消…的错误念头
to free someone from a misconception or deception
例句：I was quickly disabused of the notion that I could burn the candle at both ends. 我很快就打消了可以过度消耗精力的念头。

例句：He disabused her of the idea that she could get a job without any experience. 他让她明白没有经验是找不到工作的。

近义词：undeceive, enlighten, correct, set straight
反义词：deceive, mislead, misinform
词性：v.
