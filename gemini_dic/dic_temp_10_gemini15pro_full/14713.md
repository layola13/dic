awarders:/əˈwɔːrdərz/
n.
颁奖者，授予者
People or organizations who give awards.
The awarders chose the winner based on merit. 颁奖者根据成绩选出获胜者。

The Academy Awarders selected the best film of the year. 奥斯卡颁奖者评选出了年度最佳影片。

近义词:judges, givers, donors, presenters
反义词:recipients, receivers

词性:noun (plural)
词源:From "award" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting a person or thing that performs a specified action). "Award" comes from Old North French "awarder" (to adjudge, assign, allot).
