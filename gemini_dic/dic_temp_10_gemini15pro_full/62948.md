enneahedron:/ˌɛniəˈhiːdrən/
|n.|九面体|A solid figure with nine plane faces.|The enneahedron is a complex shape rarely seen in everyday objects.  九面体是一种复杂的形状，在日常物品中很少见到。|synonym: polyhedron|antonym: N/A|noun
