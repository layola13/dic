gdc:/dʒiː diː siː/ |abbr.| 最大公约数 (greatest common divisor) |Abbreviation for greatest common divisor, a mathematical concept representing the largest number that divides two or more numbers without leaving a remainder.  It is also sometimes referred to as the highest common factor (HCF). | The GDC of 12 and 18 is 6. 12和18的最大公约数是6。 | synonym: HCF (highest common factor) | antonym: LCM (least common multiple) | abbreviation
