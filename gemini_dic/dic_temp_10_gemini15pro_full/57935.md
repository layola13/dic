drinkableness:/ˈdrɪŋkəbəlnəs/
n.
可饮用性,适饮性
The quality of being drinkable; potability.
The drinkableness of the tap water was questionable after the flood. 洪水过后，自来水的可饮用性令人怀疑。
potability, palatability
undrinkableness
noun
