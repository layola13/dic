calderas:/kɔːlˈdɛrəz/ /kælˈdɪrəz/
n.
破火山口；火山臼；凹陷处
A large volcanic crater, typically one formed by a major eruption leading to the collapse of the mouth of the volcano.
The Yellowstone caldera is one of the largest volcanic features on Earth. 黄石火山臼是地球上最大的火山特征之一。

Mount St. Helens has a prominent caldera formed after its 1980 eruption. 圣海伦斯火山在1980年喷发后形成了一个明显的破火山口。


近义词: crater, basin
反义词: dome, peak
词性: noun
