mockup:/ˈmɑːkʌp/ (US) /ˈmɒkʌp/ (UK)| n.| 模型，样机，原型 |A full-size model of something, especially a machine or structure, used for testing or display.| We built a mockup of the new website to test its functionality. 我们构建了一个新网站的模型来测试它的功能。| synonyms: prototype, model, sample, dummy, mock-up| antonyms: finished product, final version | noun

mockup:/ˈmɑːkʌp/ (US) /ˈmɒkʌp/ (UK)| v.| 制作模型 |To create a mockup of something.| The design team mocked up several versions of the logo. 设计团队制作了几个版本的标志模型。| synonyms: model, prototype, simulate, draft | antonyms: finalize, complete | verb
