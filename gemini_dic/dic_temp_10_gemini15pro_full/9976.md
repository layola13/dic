aphonous:/ˈæfənəs/ | adj. |无声的，失声的|Voiceless; speechless; unable to produce sound.|The aphonic patient communicated through writing.  这位失声的病人通过书写进行交流。|例句：After the surgery, he was temporarily aphonous. 手术后，他暂时失声了。|近义词：mute, silent, voiceless|反义词：vocal, articulate|词性:形容词
词源: From Greek *ἄφωνος (áphōnos, “voiceless, mute”), from *ἀ- (a-, “not, without”) + *φωνή (phōnḗ, “voice, sound”).
