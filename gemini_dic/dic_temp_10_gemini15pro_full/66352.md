excursion:/ɪkˈskɜːrʒən/ | n. | 短途旅行，远足；游览；偏离 | A short journey or trip, especially one engaged in for pleasure or education; a deviation or digression. |  We took a day excursion to the coast. 我们去海边进行了一日游。

The book contains many interesting excursions into the history of science. 这本书包含了许多有趣的科学史的探讨。| synonyms: trip, outing, journey, expedition, tour, jaunt, junket | antonyms: stay, sojourn, residence | noun
