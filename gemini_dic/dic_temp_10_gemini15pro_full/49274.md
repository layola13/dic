deliberates:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪts/| v.| 仔细考虑，审议 |  considers carefully; thinks about deeply and seriously  | The jury deliberated for three days before reaching a verdict. 陪审团审议了三天，才做出裁决。|  contemplates, ponders, reflects, weighs, mulls over | rushes, hastens, acts impulsively | verb

deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪt/| adj.| 故意的，深思熟虑的 | done consciously and intentionally; carefully thought out |  The attack was a deliberate attempt to provoke a war. 这次袭击是蓄意挑起战争。 | intentional, planned, conscious, purposeful | accidental, unintentional, unplanned, impulsive | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *deliberatus*, past participle of *deliberare* "to weigh in the mind, consider, deliberate," from *de-* "thoroughly" + *librare* "to weigh," from *libra* "balance, scales."
