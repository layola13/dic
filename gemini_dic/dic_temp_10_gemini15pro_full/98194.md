insect-like:/ˈɪnˌsɛktˌlaɪk/ |adj.|昆虫似的，像昆虫的|Resembling or characteristic of an insect. | The alien had insect-like antennae.  这个外星人有像昆虫一样的触角。|The robot moved with insect-like precision.  这个机器人以昆虫般的精确度移动。|近义词:insectoid, insectan|反义词:mammalian, vertebrate|词性:形容词
词源: insect + like.  "Insect" comes from the Latin "insectum," meaning "cut into," referring to the segmented bodies of insects.  "Like" comes from Old English "gelic," meaning "similar."
