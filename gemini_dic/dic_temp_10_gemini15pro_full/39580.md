conscriptionist:/kənˈskrɪpʃənɪst/
noun
强制征兵的支持者
A person who advocates for conscription, the compulsory enlistment of people in a national service, most often a military service.
The conscriptionist argued that a draft was necessary to maintain a strong national defense.  强制征兵的支持者认为，征兵是维持强大国防的必要手段。
The newspaper editorial was written by a known conscriptionist. 这篇报纸社论是由一位著名的强制征兵支持者撰写的。
Synonyms: draft advocate, pro-conscription, jingoist (often used pejoratively)
Antonyms: pacifist, anti-conscriptionist, dove
