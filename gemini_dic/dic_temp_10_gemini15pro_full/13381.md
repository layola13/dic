atms:/ˌeɪ tiː ˈɛmz/ | n. | 自动取款机（复数） | Automated Teller Machines (plural).  A machine that allows bank customers to access their accounts and perform transactions such as withdrawals, deposits, and balance inquiries. |  I stopped by the ATMs to get some cash. 我在自动取款机那里取了些现金。| synonym: cash machines, cashpoints | antonym: bank teller | noun
