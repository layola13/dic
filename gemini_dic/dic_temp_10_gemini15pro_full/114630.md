maleo:/ˈmɑːleɪoʊ/ /ˈmeɪlioʊ/
| n. |冢雉|A large, ground-dwelling bird (Macrocephalon maleo) found in Sulawesi, Indonesia, known for its unique nesting habits. It incubates its eggs using geothermal heat or solar radiation.|The maleo buries its eggs in the sand. 冢雉把蛋埋在沙子里。|
