attest:/əˈtɛst/| v.|证明，证实|To declare or affirm something as true or genuine, often based on personal knowledge or observation.|The numerous awards attest to her talent. 众多奖项证明了她的才华。|Synonyms: certify, confirm, corroborate, verify|Antonyms: deny, contradict, refute|verb

attest:/əˈtɛst/| n.|证明，证据 (罕见)|A declaration or affirmation that something is true or genuine. |His impeccable record serves as an attest to his honesty.  他无可挑剔的记录证明了他的诚实。|Synonyms: testimony, evidence, proof|Antonyms: disproof, refutation|noun
