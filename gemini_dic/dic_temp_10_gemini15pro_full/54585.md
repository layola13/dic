dismalness:/ˈdɪzməlnəs/| n.| 凄凉，阴沉，惨淡|The dismalness of the weather matched his mood. 天气的阴沉和他低落的心情相符。|例句：The dismalness of the economic forecast worried investors. 经济预测的惨淡令投资者担忧。

The pervasive dismalness of the abandoned town was unsettling.  这个废弃小镇弥漫的凄凉气氛令人不安。|近义词：gloom, dreariness, bleakness|反义词：brightness, cheerfulness, joy|词性: 名词

词源: From dismal + -ness.  Dismal comes from the Old French *dismal ("unlucky days"), from Latin dies mali ("evil days").
