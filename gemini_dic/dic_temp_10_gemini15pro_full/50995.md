desiringly:/dɪˈzaɪərɪŋli/ | adv. | 渴望地，希望地 | In a manner expressing desire or longing. | She looked at the cake desiringly. 她渴望地看着蛋糕。|She gazed desiringly at the handsome stranger. 她渴望地注视着那位英俊的陌生人。| 近义词：longingly, yearningly, wistfully, covetously | 反义词：indifferently, apathetically | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From desire (v.) + -ing + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin *dēsīderāre* "to long for, demand," perhaps from *de-* "from" + *sīdus* "star, constellation," suggesting the notion of missing a guiding star.
