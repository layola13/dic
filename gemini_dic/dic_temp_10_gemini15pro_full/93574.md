imagoes:/ɪˈmeɪɡoʊz/ /ɪˈmɑːɡoʊz/
| n. | 成虫（复数）; 精神上的完美形象（复数） | The final, adult, sexually mature, and typically winged state of an insect that undergoes metamorphosis. Also, a mental image of a person, especially a parent, that influences behavior. |  The imagoes of butterflies are strikingly different from their larval forms. 蝴蝶的成虫形态与它们的幼虫形态截然不同。| synonyms: adult, mature form | antonyms: larva, pupa, nymph | noun
