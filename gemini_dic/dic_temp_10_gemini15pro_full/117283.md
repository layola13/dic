mechanograph:/ˈmɛkənəɡrɑːf/
noun
机械记录器；机械描记器
An instrument that records physical movements or forces mechanically.
The mechanograph recorded the patient's pulse rate and breathing. 机械记录器记录了病人的脉搏和呼吸。
synonyms: recording instrument, measuring device
antonyms: None.
n.
