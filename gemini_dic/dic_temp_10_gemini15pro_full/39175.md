congealer:/kənˈdʒiːlər/ | n. | 凝固剂，凝结剂 |A substance that causes a liquid to congeal or solidify.|  The recipe called for a congealer to set the dessert.  这道甜点需要凝固剂来定型。| synonyms: coagulant, thickener, setting agent | antonyms: thinner, solvent | noun

congealers:/kənˈdʒiːlərz/ | n. | 凝固剂，凝结剂（复数）| Substances that cause liquids to congeal or solidify. |  Several different congealers were tested in the lab. 实验室里测试了几种不同的凝固剂。| synonyms: coagulants, thickeners, setting agents | antonyms: thinners, solvents | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From congeal + -er.  Congeal comes from the Old French congeler, from Latin congelare "to freeze, thicken, curdle," from com- "together" + gelare "to freeze."
