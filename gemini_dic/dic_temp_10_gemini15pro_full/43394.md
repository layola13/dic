crankiest:/ˈkræŋkiɪst/| adj.|最暴躁的，脾气最坏的|Superlative form of cranky: most cranky.  It means extremely irritable and bad-tempered.|例句：He was the crankiest old man in the village. 他是村里脾气最坏的老头。|例句：She's always crankiest first thing in the morning.  她早上起来脾气总是最坏。|近义词：grumpiest, grouchiest, most irritable|反义词：cheeriest, happiest, most pleasant|词性:形容词
词源:From crank (n.) + -y + -est.  "Crank" originally referred to a part of a machine that could be turned, and came to mean "irritable" figuratively, perhaps because a cranky machine is difficult to operate smoothly.
