nonpurposive:/ˌnɑːnˈpɜːrpəsɪv/
adjective
无目的的，非目的性的
Lacking a definite purpose; not directed towards a specific goal.
Her nonpurposive wanderings through the city led her to discover a hidden gem. 她漫无目的地在城市里闲逛，让她发现了一个隐藏的宝石。
nonpurposive sampling is a sampling technique where the samples are gathered in a process that does not give all the individuals in the population equal chances of being selected. 非目的性抽样是一种抽样技术，样本的收集过程不会让所有个体有平等的被选中机会。
Synonyms: aimless, undirected, random, haphazard
Antonyms: purposeful, directed, intentional, deliberate
词性: adjective
