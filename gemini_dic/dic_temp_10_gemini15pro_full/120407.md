ministration:/ˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən/
n.
管理；执行；服务；帮助
the process of giving something such as a medicine or medical help to someone
例句：
The ministration of the sacraments  圣礼的执行


The timely ministration of first aid can save lives.  及时实施急救可以挽救生命。

近义词：administration, management, execution, service, assistance
反义词：neglect, disregard


词性: noun
