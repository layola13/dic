doctorless:/ˈdɑːktərlɪs/| adj.|无医生的，缺医少的|Lacking doctors; without medical care.  Formed by combining "doctor" with the suffix "-less," which indicates a lack or absence of something.|The remote village was doctorless for months after the only physician moved away.  这个偏远的村庄在唯一的医生搬走后数月都没有医生。|近义词：medically underserved, without medical attention|反义词:doctor-staffed, medically well-served|词性:形容词
