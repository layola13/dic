overbakes:/ˌoʊvərˈbeɪks/ /ˌəʊvəˈbeɪks/| v. |烤过头，烘过头|To bake (something) for too long.|例句：I overbaked the cookies, and they were hard as rocks. 我把饼干烤过头了，它们硬得像石头一样。

例句：Be careful not to overbake the cake, or it will be dry. 小心不要把蛋糕烤过头，否则会很干。|近义词：burn, scorch|反义词：underbake|词性：动词

overbaked:/ˌoʊvərˈbeɪkt/ /ˌəʊvəˈbeɪkt/| adj. |烤过头的，烘过头的|Baked for too long.|例句：The overbaked bread was hard to chew. 烤过头的面包很难嚼。

例句：The crust of the pie was perfectly golden, but the filling was slightly overbaked. 派的皮烤得金黄完美，但馅料稍微烤过头了。|近义词：burnt, scorched|反义词：underbaked|词性：形容词
