n.
细菌溶解
The destruction or dissolution of bacteria cells.
Bacteriolysis occurred after the introduction of the antibiotic.  抗生素引入后发生了细菌溶解。
The bacteriolysis of the harmful bacteria was crucial to the patient's recovery.  有害细菌的溶解对病人的康复至关重要。
近义词: bacterial destruction, bacterial dissolution
反义词: bacterial growth, bacterial proliferation
词性: noun

词源:  bacterio- (bacteria) + -lysis (dissolution) From Greek *bakterion* (small rod) + *lusis* (loosening).
