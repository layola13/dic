boule:/buːl/| n.|法式圆面包；滚球|A round loaf of bread, typically sourdough, with a crisp crust and chewy interior. Also, a metal ball used in the game of pétanque.|例句：The bakery is known for its delicious boule. 这家面包店以其美味的法式圆面包而闻名。

例句：He threw the boule with precision, landing it close to the jack. 他精准地投掷了滚球，使其落在目标球附近。|近义词：loaf (for bread), ball (for pétanque)|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:noun
