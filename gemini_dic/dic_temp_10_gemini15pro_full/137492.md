nontoxic:/ˌnɑːnˈtɑːksɪk/ | adj. | 无毒的 | Not poisonous or toxic; harmless. | This cleaning product is nontoxic and safe for use around children.  这种清洁产品无毒，可安全用于儿童周围。|  The paint is nontoxic and environmentally friendly.  这种油漆无毒且环保。 | 近义词：harmless, safe, innocuous | 反义词：toxic, poisonous, venomous | 词性: 形容词
词源: non- + toxic.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "toxic" comes from the Latin word "toxicum," meaning "poison."
