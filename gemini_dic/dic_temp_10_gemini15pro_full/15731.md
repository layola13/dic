balbutiate:/bælˈbuːʃieɪt/ /bɔːlˈbʌtieɪt/| v. |结结巴巴地说，口吃|To speak haltingly or stammer, often due to nervousness or excitement.|He balbutiated an apology, his face flushed with embarrassment. 他结结巴巴地道歉，脸涨得通红，十分尴尬。|She balbutiated a few words, too overcome with emotion to speak clearly.  她断断续续地说了几句话，激动得说不清话。|近义词：stammer, stutter, falter|反义词:articulate, enunciate|词性:动词
词源: From Latin *balbutire*, frequentative of *balbus* ("stammering").
