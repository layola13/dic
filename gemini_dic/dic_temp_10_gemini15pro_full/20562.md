blackball:/ˈblækˌbɔːl/ | n. vt. | 黑球; 否决票; 排斥; 投反对票 | A small black ball used in voting, especially to exclude someone from a club or society.  To vote against someone or something, especially to prevent them from joining a club or society. |  He was blackballed from the club for his controversial views.  他因为有争议的观点而被俱乐部拒之门外。 | Synonyms: reject, veto, exclude, ostracize | Antonyms: accept, admit, include, welcome | noun, verb
