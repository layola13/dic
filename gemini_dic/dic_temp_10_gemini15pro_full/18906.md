besmut:/bɪˈsmʌt/
|vt.|玷污，弄脏|To stain or soil something, especially with something dirty or unpleasant.|He besmutted his good name with the scandal. 他因丑闻玷污了自己的好名声。|近义词：soil, stain, tarnish, sully, defile|反义词：clean, cleanse, purify|词性:动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + smut.  "Be-" is a prefix meaning "thoroughly," and "smut" refers to soot or dirt. Thus, besmut means to thoroughly cover in dirt.
