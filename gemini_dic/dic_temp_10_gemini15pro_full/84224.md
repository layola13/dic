half-seas-over:/ˌhɑːf ˈsiːz ˈoʊvər/ | adj. | 半醉的，微醺的 |  Drunk, intoxicated, but not completely inebriated.  A nautical term that likened a drunken person's swaying to a ship rolling at sea. |  He was half-seas-over by the time the party started.  他聚会开始时就已经半醉了。 |  例句：The captain was half-seas-over and singing sea shanties. 船长喝得半醉，唱着海歌。 | 近义词: tipssy, merry, intoxicated | 反义词: sober, clear-headed | 词性: 形容词
