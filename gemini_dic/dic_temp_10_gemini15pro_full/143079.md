outbrawl:/ˌaʊtˈbrɔːl/
verb
打败；在争吵或斗殴中胜过
to defeat or surpass in brawling or quarreling
He outbrawled his opponent in the tavern. 他在酒馆里打败了他的对手。
The two rivals outbrawled each other for hours. 这两个对手互相争吵了几个小时。
近义词: outfight, overpower, subdue, conquer
反义词: lose to, surrender, yield
词性: verb
