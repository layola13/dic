falsettos:/fɔːlˈsɛtoʊz/ (US), /fɔːlˈsetəʊz/ (UK)
n.
假声；假声男高音；用假声唱出的歌曲
A male singing voice in a higher register than usual, or a song sung in this register.  The plural form often refers to the musical "Falsettos".
He sang the high notes in falsetto.  他用假声唱高音。
The falsettos in the opera were particularly moving.  歌剧中的假声部分特别感人。
synonyms: head voice, high register
antonyms: chest voice, low register
noun
