buffer:/ˈbʌfər/| v. |缓冲，减轻；保护|To buffer is to protect from impact or change.| The government is trying to buffer the economy against a recession. 政府正在努力缓冲经济衰退的影响。 | synonym: protect, shield, cushion | antonym: expose, worsen | verb
