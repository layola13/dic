devotement:/dɪˈvoʊtmənt/
|n.|奉献，献身；热爱|The artist's devotement to his craft was evident in every brushstroke.  这位艺术家对他的工艺的奉献体现在每一笔触中。|例句：His devotement to his family was admirable. 他对家庭的奉献令人钦佩。

例句：Her devotement to her work often led to long hours in the office. 她对工作的投入常常导致她在办公室长时间工作。|近义词：dedication, commitment, devotion|反义词：neglect, indifference|词性: 名词

词源:  From French *dévotement*, from *dévoter* "to devote," from Latin *dēvōtus* "set apart, dedicated," past participle of *dēvovēre* "to vow, dedicate, curse," from *dē-* "down, away" + *vovēre* "to vow."
