nonincitement:/ˌnɒnɪnˈsaɪtmənt/
n.
不煽动；不起哄；不刺激
The lack of incitement; a state of not inciting.
The judge stressed the importance of nonincitement in his closing remarks. 法官在结束语中强调了不煽动的重要性。
The policy of nonincitement helped to de-escalate the situation. 不煽动的政策有助于缓和局势。
近义词:pacification, appeasement, conciliation
反义词:incitement, provocation, agitation
词性:名词

词源:
Prefix "non-" (not) + "incitement" (the act of inciting)
