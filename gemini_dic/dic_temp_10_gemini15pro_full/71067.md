firsthand:/ˈfɜːrstˈhænd/ | adj. adv.| 第一手的，直接的 | Experienced or learned directly, not through another person. | I heard the news firsthand from the president. 我直接从总统那里听到了这个消息。| 近义词: direct, immediate, personal | 反义词: indirect, secondhand | 词性: 形容词/副词

firsthand:/ˌfɜːrstˈhænd/ | adv.| 第一手的，直接地 | In a direct way, from the original source. | I experienced the culture firsthand by living there for a year. 我通过在那里生活一年亲身经历了这种文化。| 近义词: directly, personally | 反义词: indirectly, secondhand | 词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "first" + "hand."  "First" meaning primary or original, and "hand" referring to direct experience, as if receiving something directly from the source's hand.
