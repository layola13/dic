azimuth:/ˈæzɪməθ/| n.|方位角，方位|The azimuth of the sun at noon is 180 degrees. 中午太阳的方位角是180度。|An azimuth is the angular measurement in a spherical coordinate system.  方位角是在球坐标系中的角度测量。|例句：The ship's azimuth was 270 degrees. 船的方位角是270度。

例句：The surveyor used a compass to determine the azimuth of the property line. 测量员用指南针确定了地产线的方位角。

近义词：bearing, direction|反义词:|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French azimut, from Arabic السموت (as-sumūt, “the directions”), plural of السمت (as-samt, “the direction, way, path”).
