dulcinea:/dʌlˈsɪniə/ |n.|梦中情人，理想中的女子；心上人|A woman idealized by a lover, a sweetheart.  Don Quixote's dulcinea was Dulcinea del Toboso. 堂吉诃德的梦中情人是托博索的杜尔西内娅。| 近义词: sweetheart, beloved, ladylove | 反义词: enemy, rival | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Spanish *Dulcinea*, from *dulce* "sweet," ultimately from Latin *dulcis*.
