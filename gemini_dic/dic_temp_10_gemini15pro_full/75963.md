funkily:/ˈfʌŋkɪli/
adverb|古怪地；时髦地；有灵魂乐韵味地|in a funky way; with a funky style or rhythm|She danced funkily to the music. 她随着音乐古怪地跳舞。|近义词：groovily, stylishly, eccentrically|反义词：conventionally, plainly, dully|词性: 副词

funkily:/ˈfʌŋkɪli/
adjective|时髦的，古怪的|having or showing a characteristic funky quality|Her funkily decorated apartment was full of vintage finds. 她那间装饰得很时髦的公寓里摆满了古董。|近义词：funky, groovy, stylish, eccentric|反义词：conventional, plain, dull|词性: 形容词 (rarely used)
