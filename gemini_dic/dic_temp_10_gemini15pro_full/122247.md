mita:/ˈmiːtə/| n.|米塔（一种强制劳役制度）|A mandatory public service system in the Inca Empire.|The mita system required communities to contribute labor for public works projects. 米塔制度要求社区为公共工程项目贡献劳动力。|The Spanish colonial authorities adapted the mita system for their own purposes. 西班牙殖民当局为了自己的目的调整了米塔制度。|近义词: corvée, forced labor|反义词: voluntary work|词性:noun
