balk:/bɔːk/ | v. n. | 阻碍，不愿做，突然停止 | To refuse to do something or to stop doing something because it is difficult or unpleasant.  | The horse balked at the jump.  这匹马在跳跃前突然止步。 | synonyms: hesitate, resist, refuse, shy, recoil | antonyms: accept, embrace, comply, continue, proceed | verb

balk:/bɔːk/ | n. | 障碍，梁木 | A roughly squared timber beam. An obstacle or hindrance. |  His plans hit a balk. 他的计划遇到了障碍。 | synonyms: obstacle, hindrance, impediment, obstruction | antonyms: aid, assistance, help | noun
