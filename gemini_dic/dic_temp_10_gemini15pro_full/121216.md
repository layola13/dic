misemphasize:/ˌmɪsˈɛmfəsaɪz/ | v. | 不予重视；轻描淡写；低估 | To emphasize inadequately; understate; underemphasize. | The report misemphasized the severity of the problem.  这份报告低估了问题的严重性。 | The government has consistently misemphasized the importance of education. 政府一直低估教育的重要性。 | Synonyms: underemphasize, understate, downplay, de-emphasize | Antonyms: emphasize, overemphasize, exaggerate | verb
