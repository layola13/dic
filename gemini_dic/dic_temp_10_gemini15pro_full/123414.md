monoplane:/ˈmɑnəˌpleɪn/ | n. | 单翼飞机 | An airplane with only one set of wings. |  The Wright brothers' first successful airplane was a monoplane.  莱特兄弟的第一架成功的飞机是单翼飞机。

The Red Baron flew a Fokker Dr.I, a famous triplane, not a monoplane.  红色男爵驾驶的是福克Dr.I，一架著名的三翼飞机，而不是单翼飞机。 | synonym: single-wing aircraft | antonym: biplane, multiplane | noun
