legibility:/ˌledʒəˈbɪləti/ | n. |易读性，清晰度|The legibility of her handwriting is remarkable. 她的字迹非常清晰易读。|例句：The legibility of the document was poor due to the faded ink.  由于墨水褪色，这份文件的易读性很差。

词源:  From Late Middle English *legibilite, from Late Latin legibilitās (“legibility”), from Latin legibilis (“legible”).  来自中世纪晚期英语 *legibilite，源自晚期拉丁语 legibilitās（“易读性”），来自拉丁语 legibilis（“清晰易读的”）。
