nonsegmental:/ˌnɒnsegˈmentl/ /ˌnɑːnsegˈmentl/
adjective
非音段的
Relating to or being a feature of speech, such as stress or pitch or juncture, that accompanies or is superimposed upon the phonemes of an utterance rather than constituting one of them.  Pertaining to features of pronunciation (such as pitch, stress, and juncture) that are not limited to single sounds.
Example: Intonation is a nonsegmental feature of language. 语调是语言的非音段特征。

Example: Nonsegmental features like rhythm and stress can greatly affect the meaning of a sentence.  像节奏和重音这样的非音段特征会极大地影响句子的意思。

Synonym: suprasegmental
Antonym: segmental
词性: 形容词
