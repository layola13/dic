cardioid:/ˈkɑːrdɪɔɪd/| n.| 心形线，心脏线|A heart-shaped curve traced by a point on the circumference of a circle that rolls around a fixed circle of the same radius.  It is a type of epicycloid with one cusp.|The microphone has a cardioid pickup pattern, so it primarily records sound from the front.|近义词: epicycloid (in a specific case)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
