nonaccommodably:/ˌnɒnəkɒməˈdeɪbli/
| adv. |不宽容地，不迁就地|in a way that is not accommodating; inflexibly.  Not willing to fit in with someone's wishes or needs.|He nonaccommodably refused to change his plans.|近义词：inflexibly, rigidly, unyieldingly|反义词: accommodatingly, flexibly, yieldingly|词性:副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): not
accommodate (verb): to adjust or adapt to
-able (suffix): capable of being
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives
