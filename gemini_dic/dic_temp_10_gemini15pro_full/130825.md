nonappealingnesses:/ˌnɒnəˈpiːlɪŋnɪsɪz/
| n. |无吸引力；不吸引人的地方|The nonappealingnesses of the food turned many customers away.  食物的诸多不吸引人的地方让很多顾客望而却步。|The many nonappealingnesses of the apartment, such as the leaky roof and the noisy neighbors, made it difficult to rent. 这套公寓的许多缺点，比如漏水的屋顶和吵闹的邻居，使得它很难出租。|近义词：unattractiveness, unpleasantness, undesirability|反义词：attractiveness, appeal, desirability|词性:名词

Etymology:
From non- + appealing + -ness + -es.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not". "Appealing" comes from the Old French "apeler" meaning "to call, appeal". "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality. "-es" is a plural suffix.
