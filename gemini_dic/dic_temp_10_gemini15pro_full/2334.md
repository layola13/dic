adherer:/ədˈhɪərər/
n.
拥护者；追随者；信徒
Someone who adheres to a particular belief, cause, or leader.
She is a strict adherer to the rules. 她严格遵守规则。
The king's adherers remained loyal even after his defeat. 国王的拥护者即使在他战败后也保持忠诚。
近义词:follower, supporter, devotee, advocate
反义词:opponent, dissenter, critic
词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *adherer*, from Old French *adherer*, from Latin *adhaerēre* "to stick to," from *ad-* "to" + *haerēre* "to stick."

USAGE->
The word "adherer" is typically used in formal contexts.
