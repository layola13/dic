fogy:/ˈfoʊɡi/ (US) /ˈfəʊɡi/ (UK)| n.|守旧的人；老顽固|A person with old-fashioned ideas, especially one who is resistant to change.|He's a bit of a fogy and doesn't approve of modern music. 他有点守旧，不喜欢现代音乐。|
|近义词：conservative, traditionalist, diehard, fuddy-duddy|反义词：modernist, progressive, radical|词性：名词


fogyish:/ˈfoʊɡɪʃ/ (US) /ˈfəʊɡɪʃ/ (UK)| adj.|守旧的；老顽固的|Having old-fashioned or conservative ideas.|His fogyish attitudes made him unpopular with the younger generation. 他守旧的态度让他不受年轻一代的欢迎。|
|近义词：conservative, traditionalist, old-fashioned, outdated|反义词：modern, progressive, forward-thinking|词性：形容词
