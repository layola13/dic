enragements:/ɪnˈreɪdʒmənts/| n. |愤怒，激怒，暴怒|The repeated enragements finally led to war.  反复的激怒最终导致了战争。|例句：His enragements were legendary. 他的暴怒是传奇性的。

The political enragements of the past few years have left the country deeply divided.  过去几年的政治愤怒使国家严重分裂。|近义词：fury, rage, wrath, indignation|反义词:calm, peace, tranquility|词性:名词

词源: From Old French *enrager ("to enrage"), from en- ("in, into") + rager ("to rage").  Ultimately from Latin rabies ("rage, madness").
