misnomers:/ˌmɪsˈnoʊmərz/ | n. | 误称; 用词不当 | An incorrect or misleading name or designation. | Many flowers have misnomers, like "morning glory," which blooms all day. 许多花的名字都用词不当，比如“牵牛花”，它整天都开花。| synonyms: misnaming, inaccurate naming, incorrect designation | antonyms: correct name, accurate designation | noun

misnomer:/ˌmɪsˈnoʊmər/ | n. | 误称; 用词不当 | An incorrect or misleading name or designation. |  "Shooting star" is a misnomer because they are actually meteors. “流星”这个名称是用词不当的，因为它们实际上是陨石。 | synonyms: misnaming, inaccurate naming, incorrect designation | antonyms: correct name, accurate designation | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *misnomer*, from *mesnomer* "to misname," from *mes-* "badly, wrongly" (from Latin *minus* "less") + *nomer* "to name" (from Latin *nominare* "to name").
