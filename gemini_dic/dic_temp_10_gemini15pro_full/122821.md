molecules:/ˈmɒlɪkjuːlz/ (UK), /ˈmɑːləˌkjuːlz/ (US)| n.|分子|The smallest particle of a substance that retains the chemical and physical properties of that substance and is composed of two or more atoms; a group of like or different atoms held together by chemical forces.|

例句：Water molecules are made up of two hydrogen atoms and one oxygen atom. 水分子由两个氢原子和一个氧原子组成。

例句：The molecules of air are constantly in motion. 空气分子不断运动。

近义词：particles, atoms (in some contexts)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
