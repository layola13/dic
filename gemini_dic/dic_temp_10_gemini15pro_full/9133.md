antimysticism:/ˌæntiˈmɪstɪsɪzəm/
|n.|
|反神秘主义|
|Opposition to mysticism or mystical beliefs and practices.|
|The rise of scientific rationalism fueled a wave of antimysticism in the 18th century.|
|18世纪科学理性主义的兴起，助长了反神秘主义的浪潮。|

|Her antimysticism led her to reject all forms of spiritual experience as delusion.|
|她的反神秘主义使她将所有形式的精神体验都视为错觉。|

|近义词: rationalism, skepticism, secularism|
|反义词: mysticism, spiritualism|
|词性: noun|
