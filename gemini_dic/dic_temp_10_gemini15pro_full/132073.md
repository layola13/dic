noncreatively:/ˌnɑːnkriˈeɪtɪvli/
adverb|不创造性地，缺乏创造力地|in a way that lacks creativity or originality.
He solved the problem noncreatively, simply following the established procedure.| 他不创造性地解决了问题，只是简单地遵循了既定的程序。
non-creatively
unimaginatively, conventionally, routinely, dully, predictably
