nonsacrificing:/nɒnˈsækrɪfaɪsɪŋ/
adjective
非牺牲的; 不需牺牲的
Not involving or requiring sacrifice.
The company adopted a nonsacrificing approach to expansion, managing to grow without laying off any employees.  该公司采取了一种非牺牲性的扩张方式，在不裁员的情况下实现了增长。
They found a nonsacrificing solution that benefited everyone involved. 他们找到了一个对所有相关人员都有利的、无需牺牲的解决方案。
近义词:  nondestructive, conservative
反义词:  sacrificial, costly
词性: adjective
