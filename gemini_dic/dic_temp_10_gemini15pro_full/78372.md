gesticulators:/ˌdʒɛsˈtɪkjʊleɪtərz/
|n.|做手势的人；用手势表达的人|A person who makes gestures, especially while speaking.|The enthusiastic gesticulators made their point clear even without words. 热情地做手势的人即使不用语言也清楚地表达了他们的观点。|communicators, speakers, orators|listeners, audience|noun
