overprocessing:/ˌoʊvərˈprɑːsesɪŋ/ | n. | 过度加工，过度处理 | The act of processing something excessively, often to the point of diminishing returns or negative consequences.  This can apply to food, data, information, or even emotions. |  Overprocessing the image made it look blurry. 过度处理图像使其看起来模糊。 | Overprocessing of food can strip away nutrients. 食品过度加工会流失营养。 |  overdoing, excessive processing | underprocessing, minimal processing | noun
