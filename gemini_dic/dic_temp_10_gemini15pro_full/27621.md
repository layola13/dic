cancericidal: /ˈkænsərɪˈsaɪdəl/
adjective
杀癌的; 抗癌的
Destructive to cancer cells.
Example 1:  Researchers are developing new cancericidal drugs. 研究人员正在开发新的抗癌药物。
Example 2: The cancericidal properties of this substance are still under investigation.  这种物质的抗癌特性仍在研究中。
Synonym: anticancer, antineoplastic
Antonym: carcinogenic
词性: adjective
