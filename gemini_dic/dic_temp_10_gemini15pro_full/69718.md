fenugreek: /ˈfɛnjuːɡriːk/ | n. | 胡芦巴; 胡芦巴籽; 胡芦巴叶 | An annual clover-like herb (Trigonella foenum-graecum) native to the Mediterranean region, cultivated for its aromatic seeds and leaves, used as a herb and spice and also medicinally. |  Fenugreek is a common ingredient in Indian cuisine. 胡芦巴是印度菜肴中常见的成分。| Synonyms: methi, bird's foot, Greek hay | Antonyms: N/A | Noun

fenugreek: /ˈfɛnjuːɡriːk/ | adj. | 胡芦巴的 | Made with or containing fenugreek. | The fenugreek tea has a slightly bitter taste.  胡芦巴茶略带苦味。 | Synonyms: methi | Antonyms: N/A | Adjective
