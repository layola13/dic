Derived from the Yiddish word "geyrishe," meaning "non-Jewish" and often used to describe clothing or customs perceived as flashy or overly elaborate. |  She wore a garish outfit to the party, covered in sequins and feathers. 她穿了一套俗丽的衣服去参加派对，上面满是亮片和羽毛。

The interior design was garish, with clashing colors and an abundance of gold trim.  室内设计很俗气，颜色冲突，金边装饰过多。 | 近义词：gaudy, flashy, ostentatious, flamboyant | 反义词：understated, subtle, simple, elegant | 词性: 形容词
