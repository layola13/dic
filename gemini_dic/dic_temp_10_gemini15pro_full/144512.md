outtravelled:/ˌaʊtˈtrævəld/
verb
旅行比...多；去过比...更多的地方
Having travelled more extensively than.
例句：He has outtravelled most of his friends. 他去过的地方比他的大多数朋友都多。

例句：She's outtravelled everyone in her family.  她去过的地方比她家里的任何人都多。


近义词：outdo, surpass, eclipse
反义词：lag behind


词性
v.


词源：
From out- + travelled, past participle of travel.  Out- prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing.
