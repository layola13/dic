miscopies:/ˌmɪsˈkɒpiz/ | n. | 抄写错误；误写 | An inaccurate copy of something, typically a document or piece of writing. |  There were several miscopies in the student's essay.  学生的论文中有几处抄写错误。 | synonyms: errors, mistakes, inaccuracies, slips | antonyms: accurate copies, correct versions | noun

miscopy: /ˌmɪsˈkɒpi/ | vt. | 抄写错误；误写 | To make an inaccurate copy of something, typically a document or piece of writing. | He miscopied the phone number and couldn't reach his friend. 他抄错了电话号码，联系不上他的朋友。 | synonyms: miswrite, transcribe incorrectly, reproduce inaccurately | antonyms: copy correctly, transcribe accurately | verb
