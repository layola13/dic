consonancy:/ˈkɒnsənənsi/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsənənsi/ (US)| n. |一致，和谐，调和；辅音（的）和谐 |Agreement or compatibility between opinions or actions.  The recurrence of similar sounds, especially consonants, in close proximity (chiefly as used in prosody).|The consonancy between the two reports suggests they were based on the same data.  两份报告的一致性表明它们基于相同的数据。 The poet used consonancy to create a sense of rhythm and flow. 诗人用辅音和谐来营造节奏感和流畅感。|近义词：harmony, agreement, accord, concord, unity, consistency |反义词：dissonance, disagreement, discord, conflict, inconsistency|noun
