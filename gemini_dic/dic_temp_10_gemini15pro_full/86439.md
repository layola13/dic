heeltap:/ˈhiːltæp/| n.|酒底,杯底剩酒|The small amount of wine left in a glass after drinking.  It can also refer to the act of drinking this last bit.|He finished his wine with a satisfied sigh and a final heeltap. 他满意地叹了口气，喝完了杯底的酒。|Synonyms: dregs, lees, bottom of the glass|Antonyms: full glass|noun

heeltap:/ˈhiːltæp/| v.|喝掉杯底剩酒|To drink the last bit of wine left in a glass.|He heeltapped his glass and ordered another. 他喝完了杯底的酒，又点了一杯。|Synonyms: finish off, drain|Antonyms: pour, fill|verb
