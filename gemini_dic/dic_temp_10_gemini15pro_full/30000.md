cavort:/kəˈvɔːrt/| v. |雀跃；嬉戏；欢蹦乱跳 |To leap or dance about in a lively or playful way; to prance; to frolic.|The children cavorted in the park, enjoying the sunshine. 孩子们在公园里嬉戏玩耍，享受着阳光。|The lambs cavorted in the meadow. 羊羔在草地上欢蹦乱跳。|近义词：frolic, gambol, prance, romp, caper|反义词：mope, sulk|词性:动词

词源: From Italian *cavalcare* "to ride," from Late Latin *caballaricare*, from Latin *caballus* "horse."  The meaning shifted from riding a horse to prancing or leaping like a horse, and then generalized to any playful leaping or dancing.
