on-the-alert:/ˌɒn ði əˈlɜːt/ | adj. | 警惕的，警觉的 | Watchful and prepared for danger or something unexpected. | The security guards were on-the-alert for any suspicious activity. 保安人员对任何可疑活动都保持警惕。| The soldiers were on-the-alert for any signs of the enemy. 士兵们警惕着敌人的任何迹象。 | 近义词: vigilant, watchful, wary, attentive | 反义词: inattentive, careless, negligent | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From "on" (preposition indicating a state or condition) + "the" (definite article) + "alert" (adjective from the French "alerte," meaning "watchful").  The phrase functions as a compound adjective.
