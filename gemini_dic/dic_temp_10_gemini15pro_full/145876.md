overdesire: /ˌoʊvər dɪˈzaɪər/
v.|过度渴望，过分想要|To desire something excessively or inordinately. |She overdesired the approval of her peers. 她过分渴望得到同龄人的认可。|Synonyms: crave, covet, yearn for, lust after|Antonyms: be content with, be satisfied with|verb
