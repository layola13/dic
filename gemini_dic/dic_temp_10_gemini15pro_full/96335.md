indignly:/ɪnˈdɪɡnəntli/| adv.|愤慨地，义愤填膺地|In a manner expressing strong displeasure at something considered unjust, offensive, insulting, or base.|She indignantly denied the accusations. 她愤慨地否认了这些指控。|近义词：angrily, furiously, resentfully|反义词：calmly, patiently, submissively|词性:副词
词源: From Latin *indignus* "unworthy," from *in-* "not" + *dignus* "worthy."
