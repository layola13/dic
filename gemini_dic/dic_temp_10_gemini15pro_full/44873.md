cry-babies:/ˈkraɪˌbeɪbiz/| n.|爱哭鬼，娇气的人|A person, especially a child, who cries easily or complains a lot.|例句：Stop being such a cry-baby and deal with it! 别再像个爱哭鬼一样，去面对它！

例句：The coach told the cry-babies to toughen up. 教练告诉那些爱哭鬼要坚强起来。|近义词：whiner, complainer, sook (Australian)|反义词：stoic, tough guy/girl|Noun


cry-baby:/ˈkraɪˌbeɪbi/| adj.|爱哭的，娇气的|Used to describe someone who cries or complains a lot, especially a child.|例句：He's got a cry-baby attitude. 他有一种爱哭鬼的态度。

例句：She's being cry-baby about the whole thing. 她对整件事都表现得很娇气。|近义词：whiny, complaining|反义词：stoic, tough|Adjective
