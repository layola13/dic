bejewel:/bɪˈdʒuːəl/
verb
用珠宝装饰;镶嵌珠宝
To decorate or adorn something with jewels.
The queen's crown was bejeweled with diamonds and rubies. 女王的王冠上镶嵌着钻石和红宝石。

The ornate box was bejeweled with precious stones.  这个华丽的盒子镶嵌着宝石。

近义词:adorn, decorate, embellish, ornament
反义词:strip, denude
词性:v.
