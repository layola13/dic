cross-fertilize: /ˌkrɒs ˈfɜːtɪlaɪz/ (British) /ˌkrɔːs ˈfɜːrtɪlaɪz/ (American)
|v.|交叉施肥，异花授粉; (指思想、方法等)相互影响，相互促进|To fertilize a plant or animal with pollen or sperm from a different variety; to cause (ideas, methods, etc.) to influence each other beneficially.|Scientists are cross-fertilizing different varieties of corn to develop a more drought-resistant strain. 科学家们正在对不同品种的玉米进行交叉授粉，以培育出更耐旱的品种。|The exchange program allowed students from different universities to cross-fertilize ideas and approaches.  这个交换项目让来自不同大学的学生能够相互交流思想和方法。|synonyms: crossbreed, hybridize, interbreed, mingle, blend|antonyms: isolate, separate|verb
