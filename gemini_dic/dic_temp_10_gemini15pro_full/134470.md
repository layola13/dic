nonjurorism:/ˌnɒnˈdʒʊərɪzəm/ | n. | 非宣誓主义；拒誓效忠主义 | The refusal to swear allegiance to the government, especially to the English monarchs William and Mary and their successors.  |  Nonjurorism was a significant movement in the late 17th and early 18th centuries in England.  非宣誓主义是17世纪晚期和18世纪早期在英格兰的一个重要运动。 |  The study of nonjurorism offers a window into the political and religious tensions of the time.  对非宣誓主义的研究为了解当时的政治和宗教紧张局势提供了一个窗口。 | synonyms:  recusancy, dissent, nonconformity | antonyms:  conformity, loyalty, allegiance | noun
