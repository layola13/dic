impotent:/ˈɪmpətənt/ | adj. | 无力的；虚弱的；阳痿的 | Lacking power or ability to act or succeed; unable to have an erection. |  He felt impotent in the face of such overwhelming odds. 他在如此巨大的困难面前感到无能为力。

The medication can cause impotent. 这种药物会导致阳痿。 | 近义词：powerless, weak, ineffective, infertile (in males) | 反义词：potent, powerful, strong, virile | 词性：adjective

impotence:/ˈɪmpətəns/ | n. | 无力；虚弱；阳痿 | The state of being impotent. |  He raged against his own impotence. 他对自己无能为力感到愤怒。

His impotence made him deeply insecure. 他的阳痿让他非常没有安全感。| 近义词：powerlessness, weakness, inability | 反义词：potency, power, strength, virility | 词性：noun

词源：Late Middle English: from Old French, or from late Latin impotent- ‘powerless’, from in- ‘not’ + potent- ‘powerful’ (see potent).
