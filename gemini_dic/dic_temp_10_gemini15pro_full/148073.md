oversaturation:/ˌoʊvərˌsætʃəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 过饱和，过度饱和 | The state of being oversaturated; an excessive supply of something. | The market suffered from oversaturation of cheap products. 市场充斥着廉价产品。|近义词：glut, surplus, excess |反义词: scarcity, shortage, undersupply | noun

oversaturated:/ˌoʊvərˈsætʃəreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过饱和的，过度饱和的 | Containing more of a substance than can be absorbed or dissolved; excessively supplied. | The oversaturated market made it difficult for new businesses to thrive. 过度饱和的市场使新企业难以发展。|近义词：glutted, saturated, overflowing |反义词: undersupplied, scarce | adjective
