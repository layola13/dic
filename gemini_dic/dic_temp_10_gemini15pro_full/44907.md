cryopreserved:/ˌkraɪəʊprɪˈzɜːvd/ | adj. | 冷冻保存的 | Preserved by freezing at very low temperatures. | The cryopreserved embryos were thawed and implanted.  冷冻保存的胚胎被解冻并植入。|  The hospital cryopreserved the patient's stem cells. 医院冷冻保存了病人的干细胞。 | 近义词: frozen, cryoconserved | 反义词: thawed | 词性:形容词

cryopreserve:/ˌkraɪəʊprɪˈzɜːv/ | v. | 冷冻保存 | To preserve (cells, tissues, etc.) by freezing at very low temperatures. |  Scientists can cryopreserve embryos for future use. 科学家可以冷冻保存胚胎以备将来使用。|  The sperm were cryopreserved in liquid nitrogen. 精子被冷冻保存在液氮中。| 近义词: freeze, deep-freeze | 反义词: thaw | 词性:动词

词源: cryo- (from Greek kryos, meaning "frost" or "ice") + preserve
