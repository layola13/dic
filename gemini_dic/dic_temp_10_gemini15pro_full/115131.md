mangold:/ˈmæŋɡoʊld/ (US), /ˈmæŋɡəʊld/ (UK)| n.|甜菜，牛皮菜，叶用甜菜|A leafy vegetable related to the beet, cultivated for its leaves and stalks, which are used as a vegetable. |例句：I added some chopped mangold to the stew for extra flavor. 我在炖菜里加了一些切碎的牛皮菜来增加味道。

例句：Mangold is a nutritious and versatile green. 牛皮菜是一种营养丰富、用途广泛的绿色蔬菜。|近义词：chard, beet greens, spinach|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
