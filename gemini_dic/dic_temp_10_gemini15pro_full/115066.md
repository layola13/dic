manequin:/ˈmænɪkɪn/ /ˈmænəkɪn/| n. |人体模型；时装模特儿|A life-sized dummy used to display clothes.  |The dress looked stunning on the mannequin. 这条裙子在人体模型上看起来美极了。|Synonyms: dummy, model, lay figure|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "person," "human")|noun
manikin:/ˈmænɪkɪn/|n.|人体模型；侏儒|A small model of the human body, used especially for teaching anatomy. Also, a person of abnormally small stature.|Medical students study anatomy using manikins.  医学生用人体模型学习解剖学。|Synonyms: dummy, model, anatomical model|Antonyms: giant|noun
