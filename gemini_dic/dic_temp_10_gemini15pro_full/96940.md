inextirpable:/ˌɪnɪkˈstɜːrpəbl/ | adj. | 不能根除的；无法消灭的 | Not capable of being extirpated or destroyed; ineradicable. |  The weeds in my garden are seemingly inextirpable. 我花园里的杂草似乎无法根除。

| The memory of the trauma was inextirpable.  创伤的记忆无法磨灭。 | 近义词: ineradicable, indelible, ingrained | 反义词: eradicable, removable, extinguishable | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Late Latin *inextirpabilis, from in- "not" + extirpare "to root out, destroy," from ex- "out" + stirps "root, stock."
