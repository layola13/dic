例句：She acts like a diva, demanding special treatment wherever she goes. 她表现得像个天后，无论走到哪里都要求特殊待遇。|近义词：prima donna, star, celebrity|反义词: nobody, unknown|词性:noun

词源:
From Italian diva "goddess," from Latin divus "divine." Originally used to describe a leading female singer in an opera. Over time, it has expanded to refer to any female celebrity, particularly in the entertainment industry, often with connotations of being demanding or temperamental.
