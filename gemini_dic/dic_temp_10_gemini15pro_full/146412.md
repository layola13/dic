overfond:/ˈoʊvərˌfɑːnd/ | adj. | 过于喜爱，溺爱 | Excessively fond; too affectionate. | She's overfond of her cat, letting it sleep in her bed. 她太溺爱她的猫了，让它睡在自己的床上。 | He is overfond of sweets, which is bad for his teeth. 他太喜欢吃甜食了，这对他的牙齿不好。 | 近义词: doting, excessively fond | 反义词: indifferent, uncaring | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (excessive) + "fond" (having an affection or liking for). "Fond" comes from the Middle English "fonnen," meaning "to be foolish or act foolishly," later developing the sense of "to be foolishly in love."
