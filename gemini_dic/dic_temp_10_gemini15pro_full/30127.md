cedrene:/ˈsiːdriːn/| n. |柏木烯|A sesquiterpene hydrocarbon, C15H24, found in cedarwood oil.|The characteristic aroma of cedarwood is largely due to cedrene. 柏木的独特香气主要归功于柏木烯。|Synonyms: sesquiterpene|Antonyms: None|Noun
