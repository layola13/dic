moris:/ˈmɔːrɪs/ /ˈmɒrɪs/| n. |摩尔人舞; 摩里斯舞; |A traditional English folk dance, usually performed by men, involving stylized movements, costumes, and often the use of sticks, swords, or handkerchiefs.|Morris dancers often wear bells on their legs. 摩里斯舞者经常在腿上系铃铛。|Synonyms: folk dance, traditional dance|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific type of dance)|noun
