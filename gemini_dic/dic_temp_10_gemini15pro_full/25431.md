bulkinesses:/ˈbʌlkinɪsɪz/
n.
庞大，笨重，体积大
The state or quality of being bulky; large size or volume.
The bulkinesses of the packages made them difficult to transport. 这些包裹的体积过大，难以运输。
近义词: sizes, volumes, masses, largenesses
反义词: smallness, compactness, slightness
词性: 名词 (plural)

bulkiness:/ˈbʌlkinɪs/
n.

The state or quality of being bulky; large size or volume.

词源:  From bulky + -ness.  Bulky itself comes from Middle English *bulky*,  of uncertain origin, possibly related to  Norwegian *bulka* "lump, swelling".
