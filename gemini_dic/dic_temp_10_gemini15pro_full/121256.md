misestimating:/ˌmɪsˈɛstɪmeɪtɪŋ/
verb
错误估计；估错
Incorrectly estimating or judging something.
They are misestimating the cost of the project. 他们错误地估计了该项目的成本。
misjudging, undervaluing, overvaluing
correctly estimating, accurately assessing
