crick:/krɪk/| n.| 痉挛; 抽筋; 落枕 |A painful stiffness in a muscle, especially in the neck or back.| I woke up with a crick in my neck. 我醒来时脖子落枕了。|近义词: spasm, cramp, stiffness, twinge|反义词: flexibility, looseness|noun

crick:/krɪk/| v.| 使…抽筋; 使…扭伤 |To cause a painful stiffness in (a muscle).| I cricked my neck while sleeping. 我睡觉时扭伤了脖子。|近义词: strain, pull, wrench|反义词: relax, loosen|verb
