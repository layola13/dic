heartlessnesses:/ˈhɑːrtlɪsnɪsɪz/
| n. |无情；冷酷；残酷|The plural form of heartlessness, referring to multiple instances or acts of lacking compassion or empathy.  It describes a state of being uncaring, unkind, and indifferent to the suffering of others.|His heartlessnesses toward his family shocked everyone. 他对家人的冷酷无情震惊了所有人。|近义词：cruelties, inhumanities, mercilessness|反义词：kindnesses, compassions, mercies|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From heartlessness + -es (plural suffix).  Heartlessness is derived from heartless (lacking compassion) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality). Heartless comes from heart (the organ of love and compassion) + -less (suffix meaning "without").  Ultimately from Old English "heorte" (heart).
