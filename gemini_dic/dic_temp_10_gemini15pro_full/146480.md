overfurnish: /ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrnɪʃ/ | v. |  过度布置（家具）| To furnish (a room, house, etc.) with too much furniture. |  They had overfurnished the living room, making it feel cramped.  他们把客厅布置得太多家具了，感觉很拥挤。| Example 2:  The small apartment was overfurnished and felt cluttered.  这间小公寓家具太多，感觉很杂乱。|  overdecorate, overcrowd | underfurnish | verb
