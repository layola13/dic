ovulum:/ˈɑːvjuːləm/ /ˈɒvjʊləm/| n. |胚珠; 卵子|A small egg-like structure in a seed plant that develops into a seed after fertilization.  In animals, it refers to a small egg or ovum.|

例句：
The ovulum develops into a seed after fertilization.
胚珠受精后发育成种子。

The ovulum is located within the ovary of the flower.
胚珠位于花的子房内。

近义词：ovule, egg cell|反义词: seed (in the context of plant reproduction) |词性: noun
