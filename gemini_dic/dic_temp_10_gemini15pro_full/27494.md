camped:/kæmpt/ | v. | 露营；扎营 | past tense and past participle of "camp" which means to stay in a tent or other temporary shelter, usually outdoors. | We camped by the lake for a week. 我们在湖边露营了一个星期。|  We camped out in the backyard last night. 昨晚我们在后院露营。 | synonyms: bivouacked, tented | antonyms: lodged, resided | verb
