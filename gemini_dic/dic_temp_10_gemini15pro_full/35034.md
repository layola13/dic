close-harmonies: /kloʊs ˈhɑːrməniz/ | n. | 紧密的和声；密切的和声 |Close harmonies are created when the notes sung by different voices are very close together, creating a rich and blended sound.  紧密的和声是指不同声部演唱的音符非常接近，从而创造出丰富而融合的声音。 | The barbershop quartet sang in close harmonies.  这个理发店四重唱组合以紧密的和声演唱。 | The choir's performance was characterized by its beautiful close harmonies.  合唱团的表演以其优美的紧密和声为特色。| synonyms: tight harmonies, blended harmonies | antonyms: open harmonies, dissonant harmonies | noun
