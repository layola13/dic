bicolored:/baɪˈkʌlərd/| adj.|双色的，两色的|Having two colors.  Formed from "bi-" (meaning two) and "colored."|The bicolored flag symbolized the unity of the two nations. 这面双色旗象征着两个国家的团结。|bicolor, two-toned, dichromatic|monochromatic, одноцветный (odnotsvetny - Russian for one-colored)|adjective
