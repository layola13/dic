bailer:/ˈbeɪlər/| n.|保释人; 舀水工具; 农业中用于捆扎干草或稻草的机器|A person who provides bail for someone; A device for removing water from a boat; A machine used in agriculture for bundling hay or straw.|The bailer posted a $10,000 bond. 保释人提交了一万美元的保证金。

The farmer used a bailer to bundle the hay. 农民用打包机把干草捆起来。

We used a bailer to remove the water from the flooded basement. 我们用舀水器把淹水地下室里的水舀出去。|近义词：(for person) bondsman, surety; (for device) scoop, dipper; (for machine) baler|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
