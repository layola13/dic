capillary: /ˈkæpɪˌlɛri/ (US), /kəˈpɪləri/ (UK) | n. adj. | 毛细血管；毛细管；毛细作用 | a tiny blood vessel connecting arteries and veins; a tube with a very small internal diameter; relating to capillarity |  Blood flows from the arteries to the capillaries. 血液从动脉流向毛细血管。| synonyms:  capillary tube, capillary vessel, arteriole, venule | antonyms: artery, vein | noun

capillary: /ˈkæpɪˌlɛri/ (US), /kəˈpɪləri/ (UK) | adj. | 毛细管的；毛细作用的| relating to or involving capillaries or capillarity | Capillary action causes the water to rise in the tube. 毛细作用使水在管中上升。 | synonyms: hairlike, fine, thin | antonyms: thick, wide | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *capillaris "hair-like," from Latin capillus "hair".
