dropbox:/ˈdrɒpˌbɒks/ (UK) /ˈdrɑːpˌbɑːks/ (US)| n.|投递箱；文件存储与共享服务|A service that allows users to store files online and synchronize them across multiple devices.  It also refers to a physical box for depositing items.|I backed up all my important documents to Dropbox. 我把所有重要文件都备份到Dropbox了。|近义词：Google Drive, OneDrive, iCloud|反义词: (For physical box) collection point |noun
