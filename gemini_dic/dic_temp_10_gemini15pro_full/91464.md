hylicist: /ˈhaɪlɪsɪst/
noun
森林学家；树木学家
A specialist in the study of forests or woodland ecosystems.
例句：The hylicist studied the impact of deforestation on local wildlife.  森林学家研究了森林砍伐对当地野生动物的影响。

例句：The hylicist recommended planting a variety of tree species to improve biodiversity.  树木学家建议种植各种树种以提高生物多样性。
近义词：forester, arborist, dendrologist
反义词: N/A
词性: n.

Etymology: From Greek 'hylē' (wood, forest) + '-ist' (one who studies or practices).
