militarize:/ˈmɪlɪtəraɪz/| v. |使军事化，军事化|To equip or organize for war; to give a military character to.|例句：The government decided to militarize the border region. 政府决定将边境地区军事化。|例句：The increasing militarization of the island nation caused concern among its neighbors. 岛国的日益军事化引起了邻国的担忧。|近义词：arm, fortify, strengthen|反义词：demilitarize, disarm|词性:动词
