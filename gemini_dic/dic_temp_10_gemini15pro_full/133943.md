nonhazardousnesses:/ˌnɑːnˈhæzərdəsnəsɪz/
n.
无害性；非危险性
The nonhazardousnesses of the materials used in the toy were confirmed by the lab.  该实验室确认了玩具所用材料的无害性。
The report emphasized the nonhazardousnesses of the new cleaning product. 报告强调了这种新型清洁剂的无害性。
近义词：safety, harmlessness, innocuousness
反义词：hazardousness, dangerousness, riskiness
词性：名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + hazardous (adjective meaning "risky; dangerous") + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)  来自non-（前缀，意思是“不”）+ hazardous（形容词，意思是“有风险的；危险的”）+ -ness（后缀，由形容词构成名词）
