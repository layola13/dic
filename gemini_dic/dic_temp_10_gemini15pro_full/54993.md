present participle of disprove
词性: 动词 (present participle)

disprove:/ˌdɪsˈpruːv/
verb
反驳；证明…是错误的
The lawyer disproved the witness's testimony. 律师反驳了证人的证词。
近义词: refute, debunk, invalidate
反义词: prove, confirm, validate
词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dis- (expressing negation) + prove (from Old French prover, from Latin probare "to test, to prove").
