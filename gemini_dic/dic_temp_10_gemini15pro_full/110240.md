lifely:/ˈlaɪfli/| adj.|活泼的，充满活力的，生动的|Full of life and energy; animated or spirited.|The performance was lively and engaging.  演出活泼又吸引人。|近义词：animated, energetic, vibrant, spirited|反义词：dull, lifeless, listless|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *lifli, *liifli, from Old English līflīc (“living, vital, lively”), equivalent to life +‎ -ly. Cognate with Scots lifely, leefly (“lively”), West Frisian liiflik (“lively, lovely, amiable”), Dutch liefelijk (“lovely, charming, sweet”), German lieblich (“lovely, charming, sweet”), Swedish livlig (“lively”).
