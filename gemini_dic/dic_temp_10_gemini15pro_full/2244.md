adductive:/əˈdʌktɪv/
adjective
附加的；引证的
Bringing something forward as evidence or argument; leading or tending to adduce.  Relating to adduction (the movement of a body part toward the body's midline).
His adductive reasoning convinced the jury. 他的引证推理说服了陪审团。
The lawyer presented adductive evidence to support his client's claim. 律师提供了引证证据来支持其客户的主张。

近义词: inferential, deductive,  supporting
反义词:  unsubstantiated, unproven
词性: adjective
