mariachi: /ˌmɑːriˈɑːtʃi/ | n. | 墨西哥流浪乐队；墨西哥流浪乐队音乐 | A traditional Mexican style of music played by a band of musicians typically using trumpets, violins, guitars, and a vihuela. |  Example: The mariachi band played a lively tune for the fiesta. 墨西哥流浪乐队为节日演奏了一首欢快的曲子。

Example:  We hired a mariachi band for our wedding reception. 我们为我们的婚礼招待会聘请了一支墨西哥流浪乐队。 | Synonym: banda, conjunto | Antonym: N/A | Noun
