hagiographies:/ˌhæɡiˈɒɡrəfiz/ | n. |圣徒传记；(理想化、溢美之词的)传记| An idealized biography, especially of a saint or religious figure. A biography that treats its subject with undue reverence. |例句：Modern hagiographies tend to gloss over the flaws of their subjects. 现代圣徒传记往往掩盖主人公的缺点。

|例句：The campaign biography was pure hagiography, ignoring all of the candidate's past scandals. 这本竞选传记完全是溢美之词，忽略了候选人过去所有的丑闻。|近义词：biography, life story, memoir |反义词：exposé, debunking |词性:名词
