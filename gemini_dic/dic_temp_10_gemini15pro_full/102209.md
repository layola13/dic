一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she think about the new policy? 她对新政策有什么看法？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done to deserve this award? 她做了什么值得获得这个奖项？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has she been reading all afternoon? 她整个下午都在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say to him? 你对他说了什么？
What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么事？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? 我看到她的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you came here? 你来这里之前做了什么？
What had they decided before the meeting? 会议前他们决定了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major?  她转专业之前一直在学什么？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will happen next? 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你会完成什么？
What will they have built by next summer? 到明年夏天他们会建成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2030? 到2030年你将已经做什么十年了？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
What would she say if she knew the truth? 如果她知道真相她会说什么？

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? 会议上说了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what you mean. 她不明白你的意思。
