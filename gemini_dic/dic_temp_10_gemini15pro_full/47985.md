decimalize:/ˈdɛsɪməlaɪz/| v. |十进制化；改用十进制|To convert to a decimal system, especially to convert a system of weights and measures to one based on units of ten.|The government decided to decimalize the currency. 政府决定将货币十进制化。|The UK decimalized its currency in 1971. 英国在1971年将货币十进制化。|synonyms: metrify|antonyms: - |verb
decimalization:/ˌdɛsɪməlaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |十进制化；改用十进制|The process of converting to a decimal system, especially a system of weights and measures.|The decimalization of the currency made calculations easier. 货币的十进制化使计算更容易。|The decimalization process took several years to complete. 十进制化的过程需要几年时间才能完成。|synonyms: metrication|antonyms: - |noun
