contraindicative:/ˌkɒntrəˈɪndɪkətɪv/ | adj. |禁忌的，不适宜的 | Suggesting that something should not be done or used in a particular situation, especially in a medical context because it is likely to be harmful. |  This medication is contraindicated in patients with kidney disease. 这种药物在肾病患者中是禁忌的。|  Smoking is contraindicated for people with respiratory problems. 吸烟对呼吸系统疾病患者是禁忌的。| 近义词: inadvisable, unsuitable, inappropriate, detrimental, harmful | 反义词: advisable, suitable, appropriate, beneficial | 词性: adjective
词源: contra- (against) + indicate (to show)
