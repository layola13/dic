indecorous: /ɪnˈdekərəs/ | adj. | 不合礼仪的，不得体的 | Not in keeping with good taste and propriety; improper. |  Her indecorous behavior at the funeral shocked everyone. 她葬礼上的失礼行为震惊了所有人。

Her indecorous laughter during the serious ceremony was embarrassing.  在严肃的仪式上她不合时宜的笑声令人尴尬。


近义词: improper, unseemly, unbecoming, inappropriate, indelicate | 反义词: decorous, proper, appropriate, seemly | 词性: adjective

词源:
From Latin *indecorus*, from in- ‘not’ + decorus ‘becoming, proper’ (from decor ‘beauty, grace’).
