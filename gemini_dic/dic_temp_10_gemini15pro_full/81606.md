greatest: /ˈɡreɪtɪst/ | adj. | 最伟大的；最大的 | superlative form of great |  He was the greatest boxer of all time. 他是有史以来最伟大的拳击手。 | best, finest, top | worst, least | adjective

Etymology: From Middle English *greatest*, *grestest*, from Old English *grēatost* (superlative of *grēat* (“great”)), from Proto-Germanic *grautastaz* (superlative of *grautaz* (“great”)), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵrewh₂tós* (superlative of *ǵrewh₂- (“great, big”)).
