neography:/niˈɒɡrəfi/ | n. |新文字，新书写系统，新书法|The invention of the printing press discouraged further development of neography. 印刷机的发明阻碍了新文字的进一步发展。| Neography can refer to a new writing system, or a new way of writing using an existing system. 新文字可以指一种新的书写系统，也可以指使用现有系统的一种新的书写方式。|例句：His neography made his writing difficult to decipher. 他的新书法使他的作品难以辨认。

The development of neography was crucial for the evolution of language. 新文字的发展对语言的进化至关重要.| synonyms: new writing, calligraphy, orthography | antonyms: traditional writing | noun
