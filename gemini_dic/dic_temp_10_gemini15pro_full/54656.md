disobeisant:/dɪsˌəˈbeɪsɒnt/ /dɪsˌəˈbeɪsənt/ | adj. |不服从的，不顺从的 | Not obedient; refusing to obey.  Characterized by a lack of obedience. |  The disobeisant child refused to go to bed.  不服从的孩子拒绝上床睡觉。| The disobeisant soldier was court-martialed. 不服从命令的士兵被送上了军事法庭。 |Synonyms: disobedient, rebellious, defiant, insubordinate, unruly, wayward, recalcitrant, contumacious | Antonyms: obedient, compliant, submissive, docile, tractable | Word Origin: From Old French *desobeissant*, present participle of desobeir "to disobey," from des- "dis-" + obeir "to obey."
