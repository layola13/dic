driftpin:/ˈdrɪft pɪn/| n.| 销钉，定位销，漂移销 |A pin, often tapered, used for aligning parts or securing them in place.  It is sometimes used in contexts where parts might need to shift slightly, hence "drift".|The mechanic used a driftpin to align the holes before inserting the bolt. 机械师在插入螺栓之前用销钉对准孔。|The bridge builder used driftpins to temporarily hold the steel beams in place. 桥梁建造者使用销钉将钢梁暂时固定到位。|Synonyms: dowel, pin, peg|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "adhesive" in some contexts)|noun
