holostraca:/ˌhɒləˈstrɑːkə/ /ˌhoʊləˈstrɑːkə/
n.
全甲类（动物）
A subclass of Crustacea, typically having a carapace covering the thorax and abdomen.  These include shrimps, crabs, lobsters, etc.
The holostraca are a commercially important group of crustaceans. 全甲类是具有重要商业价值的甲壳类动物群。
Holostraca such as crabs and lobsters are popular seafood choices.  像螃蟹和龙虾这样的全甲类是受欢迎的海鲜选择。
近义词:Malacostraca (软甲纲, 更广泛的分类)
反义词:无 (没有生物学上直接对应的反义词)
词性:名词
