cineraceous:/ˌsɪnəˈreɪʃəs/| adj.|灰的；灰白色的|Of the color of ashes.  Resembling ashes, especially in color.|The cineraceous owl blended seamlessly with the bark of the tree. 灰林鸮与树皮完美地融合在一起。|Synonyms: ashen, ash-colored, ash-grey, grey, silver-grey|Antonyms: colorful, bright, vibrant|adjective
