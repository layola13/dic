nonqualifications:/ˌnɒnˌkwɒlɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/
|n.|不合格；无资格；不胜任|Lack of necessary skills, experience, or credentials for a particular job or task.  The state of being unqualified.|His lack of formal qualifications was a barrier to his career advancement. 他缺乏正式的资格是他职业发展的障碍。|近义词：inadequacies, shortcomings, deficiencies, disabilities|反义词:qualifications, aptitudes, competencies|词性:名词
