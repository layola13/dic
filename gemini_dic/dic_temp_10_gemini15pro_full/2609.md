adolescent: /ˌædəˈles(ə)nt/ | n. adj.|青少年，青春期的|A young person who is developing into an adult.|例句：The adolescent years can be a challenging time. 青少年时期可能是一个充满挑战的时期。

The book deals with the emotional problems of adolescents. 这本书论述青少年的情感问题。|近义词：teenager, youth, juvenile|反义词：adult, grown-up|词性：名词，形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adolescens*, present participle of *adolescere* "to grow up," from *ad-* "to" + *alescere* "to grow," from PIE root *al-* "to grow, nourish."

USAGE->
名词用法:
Many adolescents face peer pressure. 许多青少年面临同龄人压力。
The group consisted mainly of adolescents. 这个群体主要由青少年组成。


形容词用法:
Adolescent behavior can be unpredictable. 青少年的行为可能难以预测。
He experienced the typical anxieties of adolescent life. 他经历了青少年生活中典型的焦虑。

PHRASE->
adolescent development = 青少年发展
adolescent health = 青少年健康
adolescent psychology = 青少年心理学
adolescent behavior = 青少年行为
