freiherr: /ˈfraɪˌhɛːr/ | n. | 自由领主（特指拥有自己领地的贵族）|  Historically, a freiherr held lands directly from the emperor, signifying a degree of independence.  历史上，男爵直接从皇帝那里获得土地，这标志着一定程度的独立性。| synonyms: free lord, independent lord | antonyms: vassal, serf | noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From German Freiherr, literally "free lord," from frei "free" + Herr "lord."  The title originated in the Holy Roman Empire.
