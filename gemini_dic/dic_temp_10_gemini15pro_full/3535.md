against the grain:/əˈɡenst ðə ɡreɪn/
|idiomatic expression|违背意愿；格格不入|Going against the grain means doing something that is difficult or unpleasant because it contradicts one's natural inclinations or beliefs.|例句：Asking for help goes against the grain for him.  对他来说，寻求帮助是违背意愿的。|例句：It goes against the grain to have to work on Sundays.  星期天还要工作，真是违反我的意愿。|近义词：against one's nature, against one's will, reluctantly, unwillingly|反义词：naturally, willingly, readily, easily|phrase
