aced-it:/eɪst ɪt/ | verb phrase | 成功地完成了；完美地做到了 |  It is a colloquial expression meaning to have succeeded brilliantly in something, particularly a test or exam. It combines the verb "ace" meaning to excel and "it" referring to the task or challenge. | I aced it on the math test. 我在数学考试中取得了优异的成绩。

She aced it in the job interview and got the offer. 她在面试中表现出色，并得到了这份工作。 | 近义词: nailed it, excelled, triumphed, succeeded | 反义词: failed, flunked, bombed, messed up | 词性: verb phrase

ETYMOLOGY->
From "ace," meaning "one," the highest playing card, later extended to mean "excellent" or "to achieve a perfect score," and "it," a pronoun referring to the task or challenge.
