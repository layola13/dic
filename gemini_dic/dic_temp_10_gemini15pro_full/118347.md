merils: /ˈmɛrɪlz/ | n. |九柱戏 |A game played with nine pins set up in a square, similar to skittles or bowling. The pins are knocked down with a ball or disc.  |He enjoyed a game of merils in the tavern. 他在酒馆里玩了一场九柱戏。 |Near synonyms: skittles, ninepins, bowling |Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a game) | noun
