multitasking:/ˌmʌltiˈtɑːskɪŋ/ /ˌmʌltiˈtæskɪŋ/ | n. | 多任务处理 | The simultaneous execution of multiple tasks or processes by a single person or system.  |  She's a master of multitasking, handling phone calls, emails, and client meetings all at once. 她精于多任务处理，可以同时处理电话、电子邮件和客户会议。 | Synonyms:  parallel processing, concurrent processing | Antonyms: single-tasking, serial processing | noun
