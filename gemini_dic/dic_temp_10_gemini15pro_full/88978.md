holiness:/ˈhoʊlinəs/| n.|神圣, 圣洁, 虔诚|The holiness of the place was palpable. 这个地方的神圣感显而易见。|例句：The Pope is regarded as a symbol of holiness. 教皇被视为神圣的象征。  The holiness of the cathedral inspired awe. 大教堂的神圣激发了敬畏之情。|近义词：sanctity, piety, sacredness|反义词：profaneity, impiety, wickedness|词性:名词

词源: From Old English *hālignes  (unattested), from hālig "holy" + -nes, a suffix forming abstract nouns from adjectives. Cognate with German Heiligkeit.
