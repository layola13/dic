monopteral: /məˈnɒptərəl/ (UK) /məˈnɑːptərəl/ (US)
| adj. | 单列柱式的；单排柱的 | Having a single row of columns supporting a roof, typically circular, and without a cella (enclosed inner chamber). | The temple was a small, monopteral structure, open to the sky. 这座寺庙是一个小型单列柱式建筑，露天开放。 |  The park features a charming monopteral pavilion. 公园里有一个迷人的单排柱亭子。 | 近义词: circular, columnar | 反义词: peripteral | 词性: adjective
