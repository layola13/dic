numbered:/ˈnʌmbərd/ | adj. | 有编号的，标有号码的 | Having a number or numbers assigned to it or them; counted. | The numbered streets made it easy to find her house.  有编号的街道使找到她的房子很容易。 | numbered streets 有编号的街道 | Synonyms: counted, enumerated, listed, ordered | Antonyms: unnumbered, countless, innumerable | adjective

/ˈnʌmbərd/ | v. (past tense and past participle of number) | 编号，计数 |  To give a number to something, typically to indicate its position in a series. |  We numbered the pages of the report. 我们给报告的页面编了号。|  numbered the pages 给页面编号 | Synonyms: counted, totaled, tallied, calculated | Antonyms: uncounted, estimated | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *noumbren, from Old French nombrer, from Latin numerāre (“to count, number”), from numerus (“number”).
