feudatory:/ˈfjuːdəˌtɔːri/ (US) /ˈfjuːdətri/ (UK)| n. adj.|封臣；封建附庸的|A person or state that holds land on condition of homage and allegiance to a feudal lord; relating to or characteristic of a feudatory.|例句：The duke had several feudatories who owed him military service. 公爵有几个欠他兵役的封臣。

例句：The small kingdom was a feudatory of the larger empire. 这个小王国是大帝国的附庸国。|近义词：vassal, liegeman, tributary, subordinate|反义词：lord, sovereign, overlord|词性: 名词, 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *feudataire*, from Medieval Latin *feudatarius*, from *feudum* (“fief”).
