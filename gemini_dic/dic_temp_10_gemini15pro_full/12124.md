artsy:/ˈɑːrtsi/ | adj. | 艺术气的，附庸风雅的 |  Pretending or trying to be artistic or interested in the arts, often in a way that seems affected or superficial.  |  She's become so artsy lately, always talking about obscure films and galleries. 她最近变得很有艺术气息，总是谈论一些晦涩的电影和画廊。 | 近义词: artistic, pretentious, affected, bohemian | 反义词: down-to-earth, unpretentious, practical | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  Derived from "art" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "somewhat").
