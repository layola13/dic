floodlighted:/flʌdˈlaɪtɪd/
adjective
泛光灯照明的
illuminated with floodlights.
The football field was floodlighted for the night game. 足球场被泛光灯照亮以进行夜间比赛。
The stage was floodlighted, revealing the actors in all their glory. 舞台被泛光灯照亮，展现了演员们的全部光彩。
近义词:illuminated, lit, bright
反义词:dark, unlit, dimmed
词性:adjective

floodlight:/ˈflʌdˌlaɪt/
noun
泛光灯
a large, powerful lamp that produces a wide beam of light, typically used to illuminate outdoor areas.
The floodlights illuminated the construction site. 泛光灯照亮了建筑工地。
Security floodlights deterred intruders. 安全泛光灯阻止了入侵者。
近义词:spotlight, searchlight
反义词:dim light
词性:noun

floodlight:/ˈflʌdˌlaɪt/
verb
用泛光灯照亮
to illuminate with floodlights.
They floodlighted the stadium for the evening concert. 他们用泛光灯照亮了体育场，为晚上的音乐会做准备。
The police floodlighted the area searching for the escaped convict. 警察用泛光灯照亮该地区，寻找逃犯。
近义词:illuminate, light up
反义词:dim, darken
词性:verb


词源: flood + light.  "Flood" implies a large amount, similar to a flood of water, and here refers to a large amount of light.
