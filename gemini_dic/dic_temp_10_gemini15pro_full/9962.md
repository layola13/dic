appetize: /ˈæpɪtaɪz/ | v. |  刺激食欲，增进食欲 | To make food more appealing or to stimulate one's appetite. | The smell of freshly baked bread always appetize me.  新鲜出炉的面包的香味总是让我胃口大开。 | synonyms: tempt, whet, entice | antonyms: satiate, disgust | verb

appetizer: /ˈæpɪtaɪzər/ | n. | 开胃菜，开胃食品 | A small dish of food or drink taken before a meal or the main course of a meal to stimulate the appetite. | We ordered some appetizers to share before the main course. 我们点了一些开胃菜在主菜之前一起吃。| synonyms: starter, hors d'oeuvre, antipasto | antonyms: dessert, main course | noun
