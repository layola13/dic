indoctrinate:/ɪnˈdɑːktrɪneɪt/ | v. | 灌输；教导；向…反复灌输 | to teach someone to fully accept the ideas, opinions, and beliefs of a particular group and not consider other ideas, opinions, and beliefs |  The army indoctrinates its soldiers to obey orders without question.  军队反复灌输士兵要无条件服从命令。| synonyms: brainwash, propagandize, inculcate | antonyms: enlighten, educate, free | verb

indoctrinated:/ɪnˈdɑːktrɪneɪtɪd/ | adj. | 被灌输的；被教导的 | having been taught a particular set of beliefs, often uncritically |  He was indoctrinated from an early age with the family's political beliefs. 他从小就被灌输了家庭的政治信仰。 | synonyms: brainwashed, programmed, conditioned | antonyms: open-minded, unbiased, critical | adjective
