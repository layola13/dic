dimpliest:/ˈdɪmpliɪst/| adj.|酒窝最多的，最可爱的|Superlative form of "dimply," meaning having the most dimples.|She had the dimpliest smile I'd ever seen. 她拥有我见过的最可爱的酒窝笑容。|Synonyms: cutest, most charming, most adorable|Antonyms: plainest, least attractive|adjective

dimply:/ˈdɪmpli/| adj.|有酒窝的，可爱的|Having dimples.|The dimply child giggled.  这个有酒窝的孩子咯咯地笑了。|Synonyms: cute, charming, adorable|Antonyms: plain, unattractive|adjective

dimple:/ˈdɪmpl/| n.|酒窝|A small natural indentation in the flesh, especially in the cheek or chin.|He has a dimple in his left cheek. 他的左脸颊上有一个酒窝。|Synonyms: indent, hollow|Antonyms: protrusion, bump|noun
