hodgepodge:/ˈhɑːdʒpɑːdʒ/ /ˈhɒdʒpɒdʒ/| n.|大杂烩；混合物；混杂|A confused mixture or collection of different things.|The room was decorated with a hodgepodge of furniture from different eras.|近义词: mixture, medley, jumble, assortment, miscellany|反义词: uniformity, homogeneity|词性:名词

Etymology: From obsolete French *hochepot, from hocher "to shake" + pot "pot". Originally referred to a stew of mixed ingredients.
