anacardic:/ˌænəˈkɑːrdɪk/| adj.| 腰果的；与腰果有关的；漆树科的|Relating to, or obtained from, the cashew tree (Anacardium occidentale) or its nut.  It can also refer more broadly to the Anacardiaceae family, which includes cashew, mango, poison ivy, and sumac.| Cashews are known for their anacardic acid content. 腰果以其腰果酸含量而闻名。|The anacardic oil caused a skin irritation. 腰果油引起皮肤刺激。|Synonyms: cashew (when referring to the nut itself), anacardiaceous|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym.  Could use terms like "non-anacardic" in specific contexts)|adjective
