influenced:/ˈɪnfluənst/| v. adj.|影响，受影响的|To have an effect on someone or something, or to cause them to change.  The past participle of "influence".|Her work has been greatly influenced by her travels. 她的作品深受她旅行经历的影响。|Synonyms: affected, swayed, impressed, shaped, determined|Antonyms: unaffected, untouched|verb, adjective

influence:/ˈɪnfluəns/| n. vt.|影响，影响力，施加影响|The power to have an effect on people or things, or a person or thing that has such power.  The act of influencing someone or something.|His father had a great influence on his life. 他的父亲对他的一生影响很大。|Synonyms: impact, effect, sway, control, power|Antonyms: weakness, impotence|noun, verb
