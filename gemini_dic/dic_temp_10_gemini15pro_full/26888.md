cakier:/ˈkeɪkiər/ | adj. | 更像蛋糕的；更甜的；更松软的 | More resembling cake, especially in being sweeter and softer. | The second batch of brownies was cakier than the first.  第二批布朗尼比第一批更像蛋糕。|  sweeter, softer, spongier | denser, chewier, drier | adjective

Etymology->
From cake + -y + -er (comparative suffix).  The word "cake" has a long and complex history, ultimately deriving from Old Norse "kaka."
