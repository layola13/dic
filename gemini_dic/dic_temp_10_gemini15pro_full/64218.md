equilibrious:/ˌiːkwɪˈlɪbriəs/
adjective
平衡的，均衡的
Being in a state of equilibrium; balanced.
The tightrope walker maintained an equilibrious stance despite the strong winds.  走钢丝的人尽管风很大，仍然保持着平衡的姿势。
The ecosystem is equilibrious, with predators and prey maintaining a stable population.  生态系统是平衡的，捕食者和猎物维持着稳定的种群数量。
近义词:balanced, stable, steady, poised, even
反义词:unbalanced, unstable, unsteady, precarious
词性:adjective

词源:
From Latin *aequilibrium*  "level, balance," from *aequi-* (see equi-) + *libra* "balance, scales."  源自拉丁语 *aequilibrium* “水平，平衡”，来自 *aequi-*（参见 equi-）+ *libra* “平衡，天平”。
