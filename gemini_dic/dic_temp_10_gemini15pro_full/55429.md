distinct:/dɪˈstɪŋkt/ | adj. |清晰的，明显的；不同的，独特的|Clearly different or of a different kind.|例句：The two flavors are quite distinct. 这两种味道截然不同。|例句：There's a distinct smell of gas. 有一股明显的煤气味。|近义词：different, separate, clear, obvious, marked|反义词：similar, alike, indistinct, vague|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin distinctus, past participle of distinguere "to separate, distinguish," from dis- "apart" + -stinguere "to prick, mark."
