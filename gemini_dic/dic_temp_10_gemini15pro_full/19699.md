biliferous:/bɪˈlɪfərəs/
adjective
产胆汁的；含胆汁的
Containing or producing bile.
Bile is a bitter greenish-brown alkaline fluid which aids digestion and is secreted by the liver and stored in the gallbladder.  A biliferous duct carries bile.
The liver is a biliferous organ. 肝脏是一个产胆汁的器官。
The gallbladder stores biliferous fluid until it is needed for digestion. 胆囊储存胆汁直到消化需要它。
近义词:bile-producing, cholagogue
反义词: None
