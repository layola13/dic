incuriousness:/ˌɪnˈkjʊəriəsnəs/| n.|缺乏好奇心，漠不关心|Lack of curiosity; indifference.|He showed a surprising incuriousness about the world around him. 他对周围的世界表现出令人惊讶的漠不关心。|She displayed an utter incuriousness about his personal life. 她对他个人生活表现出完全的漠不关心。|近义词：apathy, indifference, unconcern|反义词：curiosity, inquisitiveness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From in- (not) + curious + -ness.  "Curious" comes from the Old French "curios" (careful, inquisitive), ultimately from the Latin "cura" (care, concern).
