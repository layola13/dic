helping:/ˈhɛlpɪŋ/| adj. n.| 帮助的；有帮助的；一份（食物）|A helping hand is always appreciated.  伸出援手总是值得赞赏的。  She gave me a large helping of mashed potatoes. 她给了我一大份土豆泥。|近义词：assisting, aiding, supportive, a portion, a serving|反义词:hindering, obstructing, a small amount|词性:形容词,名词

Etymology->
From Middle English *helpinge, from Old English helpend, present participle of helpan (“to help”).
