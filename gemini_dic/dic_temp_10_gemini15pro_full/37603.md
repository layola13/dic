communication:/kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 沟通，交流，通讯 | the process by which information is exchanged between individuals through a common system of symbols, signs, or behavior. | Example 1: Effective communication is essential in any relationship.  有效的沟通在任何关系中都至关重要。

Example 2: The company improved its internal communication. 公司改进了内部沟通。 | synonyms: interaction, exchange, dialogue, correspondence, intercourse | antonyms: silence, isolation, secrecy, non-communication | noun
