geminate:/ˈdʒɛmɪneɪt/ | n.|成对的东西；双生子|Something that is a twin or double of another.|The geminate crystals were identical in shape and size. 这对孪生晶体在形状和大小上完全相同。|Synonyms: twin, double, duplicate|Antonyms: single, individual|noun
