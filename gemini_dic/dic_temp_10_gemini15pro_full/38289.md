concavation:/ˌkɒn.kəˈveɪ.ʃən/ /ˌkɑːn.kəˈveɪ.ʃən/| n. |凹陷，凹面|A hollowed-out area or surface; a concavity.|The concavation on the underside of the leaf collected rainwater. 叶子下面的凹陷处积满了雨水。|近义词：cavity, hollow, depression, indentation|反义词：convexity, protuberance|noun


concave:/ˈkɒŋ.keɪv/ /ˈkɑːn.keɪv/| adj. |凹的|Curving inwards.|The mirror was concave, making his face look smaller. 镜子是凹的，使他的脸看起来更小。|近义词：hollowed, sunken, indented|反义词：convex|adjective
