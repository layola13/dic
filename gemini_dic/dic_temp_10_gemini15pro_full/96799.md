inerrably:/ɪˈnɛrəbli/
adjective
必然地，无误地
in a manner that is impossible to stop or prevent; inevitably.
His arguments pointed inerrably to this conclusion. 他的论点不可避免地指向了这个结论。
Synonyms: inevitably, unavoidably, certainly, surely, necessarily, inescapably
Antonyms: avoidably, possibly, uncertainly

词性: adverb
中文翻译: 必然地，无误地
英文解析: in a manner that is impossible to stop or prevent; inevitably.
例句：The events of that day led inerrably to war. 那一天的事件不可避免地导致了战争。
例句：The logic of his argument led inerrably to that conclusion. 他的论证逻辑必然导向那个结论。
近义词：inevitably, unavoidably, certainly
反义词：avoidably, uncertainly
