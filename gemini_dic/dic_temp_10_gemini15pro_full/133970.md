nonheroically:/ˌnɑːnˌhɪˈroʊɪkli/ | adv. | 非英雄地，不英勇地 | In a manner lacking courage or the qualities of a hero. | He nonheroically hid under the table during the earthquake.  他在地震时很不英勇地躲在桌子底下。| Examples:  The soldier nonheroically abandoned his post.  这名士兵不光彩地放弃了他的岗位。He nonheroically admitted he was scared. 他不英勇地承认他害怕了。 | Synonyms: cowardly, cravenly, timidly, pusillanimously | Antonyms: heroically, bravely, courageously | Word Type: Adverb
