migrations:/maɪˈɡreɪʃənz/| n.|迁移，移居，移动|The annual migrations of birds are amazing. 鸟类的年度迁徙令人惊叹。|例句：The migrations of wildebeest across the Serengeti are a spectacular sight.  角马穿越塞伦盖蒂的迁徙是一场壮观的景象。

The salmon migrations up the river are essential for their reproduction. 鲑鱼溯河洄游对它们的繁殖至关重要。|近义词：movements, displacements, relocations|反义词: settlements, immigrations|词性:noun


Etymology: From Late Middle English *migracion*, from Late Latin *migrātionem* (nominative *migrātiō*), noun of action from past-participle stem of *migrāre* (“to migrate”).
