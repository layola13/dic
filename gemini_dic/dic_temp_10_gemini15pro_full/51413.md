deterred:/dɪˈtɜːrd/
verb|阻止;威慑;使不敢|discouraged someone from doing something, typically by instilling doubt or fear of the consequences.|The high price of the tickets deterred many fans from attending the concert.  高昂的票价使许多歌迷无法参加音乐会。|dissuaded, prevented, discouraged|encouraged, emboldened
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin deterrere, from de- + terrere "to frighten."
