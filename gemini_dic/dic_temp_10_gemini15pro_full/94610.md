impregnant:/ɪmˈprɛɡnənt/| adj.|怀孕的；充满的；饱和的|Saturated or permeated with something; filled or imbued with a quality, feeling, or idea.  Specifically, containing a developing fetus within the womb.|例句：The air was impregnant with the smell of pine needles. 空气中充满了松针的气味。

The queen bee is impregnant.  蜂王怀孕了。|近义词：pregnant, saturated, permeated, imbued, filled, laden|反义词：empty, devoid, barren|词性:形容词

impregnant:/ɪmˈprɛɡneɪt/| vt.|使怀孕；使充满；使饱和|To make pregnant; to fill or saturate something with a substance or quality.|例句：The cloth was impregnated with a fire-retardant chemical. 布料浸透了防火化学物质。

He impregnated his wife. 他使他的妻子怀孕了。|近义词：saturate, permeate, imbue, infuse, fill|反义词：empty, drain, deplete|词性:动词
