coroner:/ˈkɔːrənər/ (US) /ˈkɒrənə(r)/ (UK)| n. |死因裁判官 (某些司法管辖区)|A public official who inquires into the causes and circumstances of certain kinds of deaths (especially sudden, violent, or unexplained deaths).|The coroner's inquest concluded that the death was accidental.|Synonyms:  inquisitor (in some jurisdictions)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
