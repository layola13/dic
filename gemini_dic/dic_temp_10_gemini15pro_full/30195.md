celestite: /ˈsɛləstaɪt/ /səˈlɛstaɪt/ | n. | 天青石 | A mineral form of strontium sulfate (SrSO4), typically pale blue in color, used as a source of strontium and in fireworks. | Celestite is a popular mineral among collectors. 天青石是收藏家们喜爱的矿物。|  Celestite's delicate blue color is often compared to the sky. 天青石柔和的蓝色常被比作天空。 | synonyms: celestine | antonyms: N/A | noun
