endosperm:/ˈendəʊspɜːrm/| n. |胚乳|The nutritive tissue surrounding the embryo within the seeds of flowering plants.|The endosperm of the coconut is the white fleshy part we eat. 椰子的胚乳就是我们吃的白色肉质部分。|近义词: kernel, albumen (in older texts)|反义词: embryo|noun
