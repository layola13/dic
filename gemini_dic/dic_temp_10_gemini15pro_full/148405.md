overstatement:/ˌoʊvərˈsteɪtmənt/| n.|夸大；夸张；言过其实|An exaggeration; a statement that represents something as greater or worse than it really is.|Her claims about the product's effectiveness were a gross overstatement. 她关于产品功效的说法过于夸大了。|近义词：exaggeration, hyperbole|反义词: understatement, minimization|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + statement.  "Over-" signifies "too much" or "excessive," while "statement" refers to an assertion or declaration.
