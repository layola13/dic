overrule: /ˌoʊvərˈruːl/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈruːl/ (UK)| vt.| 驳回；否决；宣布…无效；统治；支配|to reject or disallow a decision, opinion, etc.; to have more power or influence than someone or something|The judge overruled the objection. 法官驳回了异议。|近义词：override, reject, disallow, overturn|反义词： uphold, sustain, approve|verb

overrule: /ˌoʊvərˈruːl/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈruːl/ (UK)| n.| 驳回；否决|The act of overruling.|The chairman’s overrule of the committee’s decision caused an uproar. 主席否决了委员会的决定，引起了轩然大波。|近义词：rejection, disapproval|反义词：approval, acceptance|noun
