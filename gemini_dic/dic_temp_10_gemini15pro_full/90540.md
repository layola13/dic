huffish:/ˈhʌfɪʃ/ | adj. | 生气的，愠怒的，发怒的 | Easily offended and quick to show resentment; sulky. |  He gave a huffish reply and stormed off. 他怒气冲冲地回答后，气呼呼地走开了。| synonyms: sulky, sullen, petulant, grumpy, irritable, cross, churlish, surly, bad-tempered, ill-tempered, ill-humored, irascible, cantankerous, testy, touchy, tetchy, prickly, peevish, querulous, crabbed, crabby, waspish | antonyms: cheerful, good-natured, amiable, pleasant | word origin: From huff (n.) + -ish.
