palaeo:/ˈpeɪliəʊ/ (UK) /ˈpeɪlioʊ/ (US)| adj. |古生物的，古代的|Relating to a geological era or the organisms that existed during it; ancient.|例句：Palaeo-climatic data can provide insights into past environmental conditions. 古气候数据可以提供对过去环境条件的了解。

例句：Palaeo-botanical studies focus on fossilized plants. 古植物学研究的重点是植物化石。|近义词：ancient, prehistoric, primeval |反义词:modern, recent|词性:adjective
