hieroglyphics: /ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪks/ | n. | 象形文字 | A system of writing using picture-like symbols, especially that of ancient Egypt. |  Example: The Rosetta Stone helped scholars decipher Egyptian hieroglyphics.  罗塞塔石碑帮助学者们破译了埃及象形文字。

| Example:  Learning to read hieroglyphics is a challenging but rewarding task. 学习阅读象形文字是一项具有挑战性但回报丰厚的任务。 |  Synonyms: pictographs, ideograms | Antonyms: alphabet, letters | noun


hieroglyphic: /ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪk/ | adj. | 象形文字的 | Relating to or written in hieroglyphics. | Example: The walls of the tomb were covered in hieroglyphic inscriptions.  墓穴的墙壁上覆盖着象形文字铭文。

| Example:  The hieroglyphic script tells the story of the pharaoh's reign. 象形文字的文字讲述了法老统治的故事。 | Synonyms: pictographic, ideographic | Antonyms: alphabetic | adjective
