delousers:/diːˈlaʊzərz/

delouser:/diːˈlaʊzər/
noun
除虱的人
a person who removes lice.
The delouser carefully combed through the child's hair. 除虱员仔细地梳理了孩子的头发。
近义词:nitpicker
反义词: (None -  no direct opposite for a person with this specific purpose)
词性:n.


词源:  "de-" (prefix meaning "remove" or "separate") + "louse" (a parasitic insect) + "-er" (suffix indicating a person or thing that performs an action).
