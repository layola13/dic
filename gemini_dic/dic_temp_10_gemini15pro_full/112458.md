loudmouths:/ˈlaʊdmaʊðz/
noun
大声喧哗的人; 多嘴多舌的人; 夸夸其谈的人
A person who talks too much, especially in a loud and boastful manner.
He's a real loudmouth and can't keep a secret. 他真是个大嘴巴，守不住秘密。
近义词: gossips, braggarts, windbags
反义词: introvert, quiet person
词性: noun (plural)
