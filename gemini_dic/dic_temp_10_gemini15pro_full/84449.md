haloesque:/ˌheɪloʊˈɛsk/
adj.
像光环的；神圣的；崇高的
Resembling or suggestive of a halo; saintly or ethereal.
The painting had a haloesque quality, with light seemingly emanating from the figure's head. 这幅画有一种光环般的感觉，光线似乎从人物的头部发出。
近义词:saintly, ethereal, angelic, divine, heavenly
反义词:mundane, earthly, profane, secular, demonic
词性:形容词
