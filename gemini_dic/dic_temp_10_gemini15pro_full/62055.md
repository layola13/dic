encarpus:/ɛnˈkɑːrpəs/
adjective
内果皮的;包在果皮内的
Enclosed within a matured ovary or fruit.  The seeds are encarpus within the fleshy fruit.
The encarpus seeds were protected by the thick outer layer of the fruit. 种子被果实厚厚的外层保护着。
近义词:endocarp, pericarp
反义词:exocarp, epicarp
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Greek *endon* (within) + *karpos* (fruit).
