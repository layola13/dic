fixed-term:/ˌfɪkstˈtɜːrm/| adj.|固定期限的；定期限的|lasting for a fixed period of time|例句：He has a fixed-term contract. 他有一份固定期限的合同。|例句：She's looking for a fixed-term deposit account. 她正在寻找一个定期存款账户。|近义词：short-term, temporary, limited-term|反义词:long-term, permanent, indefinite|词性:adjective

fixed-term:/ˌfɪkstˈtɜːrm/| n.|固定期限；定期|a period of time that is fixed|例句：The loan is for a fixed-term of five years. 这笔贷款期限为五年。|例句：The project will be completed within a fixed-term. 该项目将在规定期限内完成。|近义词：period, term, duration|反义词:indefinite period|词性:noun
