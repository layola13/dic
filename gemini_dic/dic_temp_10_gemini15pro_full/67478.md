extortioner:/ɪkˈstɔːrʃənər/ /ɪkˈstɔrʃənɚ/
noun
勒索者；敲诈者
A person who obtains something, especially money, through extortion.  The act of obtaining something, especially money, through force, threats, or intimidation.
The extortioner demanded $10,000 from the businessman.  这个勒索者向商人索要1万美元。
synonyms: blackmailer, racketeer, shakedown artist
antonyms: benefactor, philanthropist
