mockup:/ˈmɑːkʌp/ (US), /ˈmɒkʌp/ (UK)| n.|模型，样机|A mockup is a model or replica of a design or device, used for demonstration, evaluation, or testing.|We created a mockup of the website to show the client. 我们创建了一个网站的模型来展示给客户。|Synonyms: prototype, model, sample, dummy|Antonyms: final product, finished version|noun

mockup:/ˈmɑːkʌp/ (US), /ˈmɒkʌp/ (UK)| vt.|制作模型|To create a mockup of something.|They mocked up a new design for the car's dashboard. 他们为汽车仪表盘制作了一个新的设计模型。|Synonyms: model, prototype, simulate|Antonyms: finalize, complete, finish|verb
