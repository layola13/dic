ciao:/ˈtʃaʊ/| interj. n.|你好，再见，| An informal Italian greeting or farewell.| Example: "Ciao, Bella!" 你好，美女！| Example:  He gave a quick "ciao" and left the cafe. 他快速地说了声“ciao”就离开了咖啡馆。|Synonym: hello, goodbye, farewell, so long|Antonym: (No direct antonym for a greeting/farewell)|interjection, noun

词源 Etymology:
From Italian *ciao*, from Venetian *s'ciao* "I am your slave" (literally "slave I"), from *schiavo*, from Medieval Latin *sclavus*. Related to slave.
