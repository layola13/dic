gunmetal:/ˈɡʌnˌmetl/| n. adj.|炮铜色，青铜色；炮铜，青铜|A dark grey metallic colour, resembling the colour of gunmetal.  A type of bronze formerly used for cannons.|The car was painted a sleek gunmetal grey.|近义词：bronze, dark grey, charcoal|反义词:bright, light|词性:名词，形容词

Etymology:
From gun + metal.  First attested in the late 18th century, originally referring to a type of bronze used in cannons.  The color sense emerged later, based on the appearance of this alloy.
