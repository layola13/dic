colloquium:/kəˈləʊkwiəm/ /kəˈloʊkwiəm/| n.|学术讨论会，研讨会|An academic conference or seminar.|例句：The colloquium on artificial intelligence attracted experts from around the world.  人工智能研讨会吸引了来自世界各地的专家。

例句：She presented her research findings at the physics colloquium.  她在物理学研讨会上展示了她的研究成果。

近义词：seminar, conference, symposium, workshop|反义词:|词性:noun
