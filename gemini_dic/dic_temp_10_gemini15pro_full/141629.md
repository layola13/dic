optima:/ˈɒptɪmə/ (UK) /ˈɑːptɪmə/ (US)| n. | 最优条件，最佳结果，最适宜条件 | The plural of optimum.  It refers to the most favorable conditions or level for growth, reproduction, or success. | The researchers are trying to determine the optima for bacterial growth. 研究人员正试图确定细菌生长的最佳条件。| 近义词: best, ideal, optimal conditions | 反义词: worst, pessimal, least favorable conditions | noun
