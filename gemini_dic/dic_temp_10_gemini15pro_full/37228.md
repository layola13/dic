comfort:/ˈkʌmfərt/ (US), /ˈkʌmfət/ (UK)| n. vt.|安慰，舒适，慰藉；安慰，使舒适|A feeling of ease and contentment.  She found comfort in her family during the difficult time. 她在这段艰难的时期从家人那里得到了安慰。|近义词：solace, consolation, ease, relief|反义词:discomfort, distress, misery|noun
comfort:/ˈkʌmfərt/ (US), /ˈkʌmfət/ (UK)| vt.|安慰，使舒适|To soothe or console someone. He comforted her with a hug. 他用拥抱安慰她。|近义词：soothe, console, reassure|反义词:distress, upset|verb
