mazdoor: /məzˈdʊər/ | n. | 劳动者，工人 | A laborer or worker, especially an unskilled one.  From Urdu مزدور (mazdūr), from Persian مزدور (mazdūr), meaning "laborer, worker". Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *mizdʰ- (“wages, reward”). |  The mazdoors worked tirelessly in the fields.  这些工人在田里不知疲倦地工作。| Synonyms: laborer, worker, workman, hand | Antonyms: employer, boss, manager | noun
