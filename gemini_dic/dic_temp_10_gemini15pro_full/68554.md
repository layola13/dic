fanfoot:/ˈfænˌfʊt/| n. |扇足；瓣足动物|An animal, typically a bird, with webbed or lobed feet, adapted for swimming or wading.|The grebe is a fanfoot that dives for fish.  鸊鷉是一种瓣足动物，潜入水中捕鱼。|近义词：webfoot, lobefoot|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-webbed foot")|词性: noun
