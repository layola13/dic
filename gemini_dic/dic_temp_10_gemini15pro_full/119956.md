millenarian:/mɪˌlɛˈnɛəriən/ | adj. n. | 千禧年的；千禧年主义的；信奉千禧年主义者 | Relating to a millennium, especially to a belief in a future golden age of peace and prosperity.  A person who believes in the millennium. |  例句：The preacher's millenarian prophecies attracted a large following.  这位传教士的千禧年预言吸引了大批追随者。

例句：Many millenarian groups emerged around the turn of the millennium.  许多千禧年主义团体在世纪之交出现。 | 近义词：chiliastic, apocalyptic | 反义词: -- | 词性: adjective, noun
