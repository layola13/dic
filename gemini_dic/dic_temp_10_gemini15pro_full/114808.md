mamalia:/məˈmeɪliə/ | n. | 哺乳动物 | Mammals are a class of vertebrate animals characterized by the presence of mammary glands which in females produce milk to nourish their young. They are also distinguished by hair or fur, three middle ear bones, and a neocortex (a region of the brain). |  Blue whales are the largest mammalia. 蓝鲸是最大的哺乳动物。 | synonyms: mammals | antonyms: non-mammals | noun
