mundation:/mʌnˈdeɪʃən/
n.|清洗, 净化, 世界|The act of cleansing or purifying, especially the world.|The mundation of the polluted river is a long-term project. 这条污染河流的净化是一个长期项目。|近义词：purification, cleansing, ablution|反义词：pollution, contamination|词性:名词

词源:
From Latin *mundātiō*, from mundātus, past participle of mundāre (“to cleanse”), from mundus (“clean, neat, elegant”).
