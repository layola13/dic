outswore:/ˌaʊtˈswɔːr/
verb
胜过（某人）的誓言；发誓比（某人）厉害
To outdo in swearing; to surpass in taking oaths.
He outswore his opponent, claiming innocence with greater vehemence. 他发誓比他的对手更厉害，更强烈地声称自己是无辜的。
