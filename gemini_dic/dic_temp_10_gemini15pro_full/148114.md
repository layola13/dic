oversecure:/ˌoʊvər sɪˈkjʊr/| adj.|过分安全的，过分担心的|Excessively secure or overly cautious, often to the point of being counterproductive or paranoid.|She was so oversecure about her belongings that she carried them all with her everywhere, making travel a nightmare. 她对自己的财物过于担心，以至于随身携带所有东西，使得旅行成了一场噩梦。|近义词:overcautious, overprotective, anxious, paranoid|反义词:careless, reckless, negligent|词性:形容词
