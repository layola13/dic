barry:/ˈbæri/ | n. | 巴里（男子名）| A male given name, a diminutive of Bartholomew. | Barry is a common name in Ireland. 巴里在爱尔兰是个常见的名字。| Example: My uncle's name is Barry.  我的叔叔叫巴里。 | 近义词: (无，因为是专有名词) | 反义词: (无，因为是专有名词) | Noun


barry:/ˈbæri/ | v. | 埋葬;掩埋 | To bury or cover something completely.  (Less common usage) |  The dog barried its bone in the garden.  狗把骨头埋在了花园里。| Example: The avalanche barried the village. 雪崩掩埋了村庄。 | 近义词: bury, inter, conceal | 反义词: unearth, exhume, uncover | Verb
