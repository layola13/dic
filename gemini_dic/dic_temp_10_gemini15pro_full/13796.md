audiometrically:/ˌɔːdiəˈmɛtrɪkli/| adv.|听力测定地，通过听力测试|By measuring hearing ability using an audiometer.  The patient's hearing loss was assessed audiometrically. 病人的听力损失是通过听力测试评估的。|例句：His hearing was tested audiometrically. 他的听力是用听力计测试的。  The researchers audiometrically evaluated the participants' hearing thresholds. 研究人员用听力计评估了参与者的听阈。|近义词：by audiometry|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps subjectively)|词性:副词

词源: From "audiometric" + "-ally".  "Audiometric" comes from "audiometry", from "audio-" (hearing) + "-metry" (measurement).  Ultimately from Greek "akouein" (to hear) and "metron" (measure).
