mothery:/ˈmʌðəri/| adj.|母性的，慈母般的，溺爱孩子的|Having or showing qualities characteristic of a mother, especially in a kind, gentle, and loving way.  Characterized by excessive indulgence or protectiveness.|She had a very mothery attitude towards her students. 她对她的学生有一种非常慈母般的态度。|近义词：maternal, protective, nurturing|反义词：uncaring, neglectful|词性:形容词
词源: From mother + -y.
