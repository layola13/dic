grenades:/ɡrəˈneɪdz/ | n. | 手榴弹 | Small bombs thrown by hand or launched mechanically, designed to explode on impact or after a short delay. | The soldiers used grenades to clear the enemy bunker. 士兵们用手榴弹清除敌人的碉堡。 |  The soldier pulled the pin and hurled the grenade. 士兵拔掉保险销，投掷了手榴弹。 | synonyms: bombs, explosives, projectiles | antonyms: peacekeepers (this is a loose antonym, representing the opposite concept) | noun
