nonemployment:/ˌnɒnɪmˈplɔɪmənt/ | n. |失业，非就业|The state of not being employed.  A situation where individuals are actively seeking employment but are unable to find a job.|  High nonemployment rates can indicate a struggling economy.  高失业率可能表明经济不景气。| synonyms: unemployment, joblessness | antonyms: employment, full employment | noun
