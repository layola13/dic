lionizing:/ˈlaɪənaɪzɪŋ/
verb
捧为名流；把…捧成名人；吹捧
treating (someone) as a celebrity.
The media is lionizing the young athlete after his Olympic victory. 媒体在年轻运动员奥运夺冠后把他捧成了名流。
近义词: celebrating, glorifying, exalting, idolizing
反义词: criticizing, condemning, denouncing, vilifying
词性: verb (present participle)

lionize:/ˈlaɪənaɪz/
verb
捧为名流；把…捧成名人；吹捧
treat (someone) as a celebrity.
The press lionized the actress after her Oscar win.  媒体在女演员获得奥斯卡奖后把她捧成了名流。
近义词: celebrate, glorify, exalt, idolize
反义词: criticize, condemn, denounce, vilify
词性: verb


词源: From lion + -ize.  The original meaning was "to see or show lions," later evolving to mean "treat as an object of great interest."
