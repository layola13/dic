buffaloes:/ˈbʌfəloʊz/ (US) /ˈbʌfələʊz/ (UK)
n.
水牛；美洲野牛
Large, shaggy-haired mammals native to North America and parts of Europe and Asia, often found in herds.
The buffaloes roamed the plains. 水牛在平原上漫游。

The herd of buffaloes crossed the river.  一群水牛渡过了河。

ox, bison
cow, bull (if specifying gender)
