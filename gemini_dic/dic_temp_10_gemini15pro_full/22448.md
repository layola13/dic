booking-up:/ˈbʊkɪŋ ʌp/
n.
预订满; 订完
the act of reserving all available seats, rooms, etc.
The booking-up of all the flights for the holiday weekend meant we had to drive. 假期周末所有航班都订满了，这意味着我们不得不开车。
reservation, selling out
availability
