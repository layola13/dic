dialogic:/ˌdaɪəˈlɒdʒɪk/ | adj. |对话的，对话式的| Relating to or characterized by dialogue; characterized by reciprocal communication between two or more parties.|  The novel employs a dialogic structure, presenting multiple perspectives on the same events. 这部小说采用了对话式的结构，从多个角度呈现同一事件。

The classroom became a dialogic space where students could exchange ideas freely. 教室变成了一个对话空间，学生可以自由地交流想法。| 近义词: conversational, interactive, communicative | 反义词:monologic, unidirectional | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Greek *dialogikos*, from *dialogos* "conversation," from *dialegesthai* "to converse," from *dia-* "through, across" + *legein* "to speak."
