oscillaria:/ˌɒsɪˈleɪrɪə/ | n. |颤藻属|A genus of filamentous cyanobacteria (blue-green algae) characterized by their unbranched filaments and oscillating movement. |Example 1: Oscillaria is commonly found in freshwater environments. 颤藻属常见于淡水环境中。

Example 2:  The bloom of Oscillaria can sometimes be toxic to animals. 颤藻的大量繁殖有时会对动物有毒。| 近义词: blue-green algae | 反义词: N/A | Noun

oscillating:/ˈɒsɪleɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |振荡的，摆动的|Moving back and forth or swinging from side to side.  Relating to or resembling oscillation. | Example 1: The oscillating fan provided a welcome breeze on a hot day.  摆动的风扇在炎热的天气里带来了一阵令人愉悦的微风。

Example 2:  The oscillating movement of the pendulum was mesmerizing.  钟摆的摆动令人着迷。| 近义词: swinging, vibrating, fluctuating | 反义词: static, fixed, stationary | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *oscillārius*, from oscillāre (“to swing”), from oscillum (“swing”).
