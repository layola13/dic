braes:/breɪz/| n. |山坡，斜坡 (尤指苏格兰诗歌中)|Slopes or hillsides, especially in Scottish poetry. |The poet wrote of the bonny braes of Scotland. 诗人写了苏格兰美丽的山坡。|近义词：hillsides, slopes, banks|反义词:valleys, plains|名词

词源: From Scottish Gaelic *bràigh* (“upper part of a hillside”).  Ultimately from Proto-Celtic *brigā* (“hill, height”).
