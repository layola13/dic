bogof:/ˈbɒɡɒf/ (UK) /ˈbɑːɡɔːf/ (US)| adj. n.|买一送一|Abbreviation of "buy one, get one free".  A sales promotion offering two items for the price of one.| The supermarket has a bogof offer on selected wines this week. 这家超市本周精选葡萄酒买一送一。|Synonyms: two for one, buy one get one free|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider "full price", "no discount")|Abbreviation
