humblenesses:/ˈhʌmbəlnəsɪz/
n.
谦逊，谦卑；谦虚的行为
the quality or state of not thinking you are better than other people : the quality or state of being humble
Her humbleness is admirable. 她的谦逊令人钦佩。
Synonyms: humility, modesty, meekness
Antonyms: arrogance, pride, haughtiness
词性: 名词 (noun)
