nonimperiously:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpɪəriəsli/
adverb
非专横地，不傲慢地
In a manner that is not domineering or arrogant.
She spoke nonimperiously, requesting rather than demanding assistance. 她说话并非专横跋扈，而是以请求而非要求的方式寻求帮助。
He corrected his student nonimperiously, offering guidance rather than criticism. 他并没有以专横的方式纠正他的学生，而是给予指导而不是批评。
近义词: humbly, modestly, unassumingly
反义词: imperiously, arrogantly, domineeringly
词性: adverb

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not" or "opposite of"  源自拉丁语 non
imperious (adjective):  meaning "domineering or overbearing" 源自拉丁语 imperiosus,  意思是 "powerful," "commanding."
-ly (suffix):  forms adverbs from adjectives.
