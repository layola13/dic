chain-smoked:/ˈtʃeɪn sməʊkt/ | v. |  连抽; 一支接一支地抽 | He chain-smoked all through the meeting. 他在整个会议期间一支接一支地抽烟。|例句：She chain-smoked nervously while waiting for the results. 她紧张地一支接一支地抽烟，等待结果。| 近义词:smoked heavily, smoked continuously | 反义词:abstained from smoking|词性:动词

chain-smoked:/ˈtʃeɪn sməʊkt/ | adj. | (指香烟)一支接一支抽的 | the chain-smoked cigarettes  一支接一支抽的香烟 | 例句：The ashtray was full of chain-smoked cigarette butts. 烟灰缸里装满了连抽的烟蒂。 | 近义词: heavily smoked | 反义词:unsmoked|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "chain" (meaning a series of connected things) + "smoked" (past participle of "smoke").  The term evokes the image of cigarettes being smoked one after another, linked like the links of a chain.
