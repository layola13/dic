discoursed:/dɪˈskɔːrst/
verb
讲述；论述；探讨
talked or wrote about a subject formally and in detail
He discoursed at length on the history of the region. 他详细地讲述了该地区的历史。
近义词：discussed, spoke, lectured, expounded
反义词：was silent, listened
词性：verb (past tense of discourse)


discourse:/ˈdɪskɔːrs/
noun
演讲；论述；探讨
written or spoken communication or debate
The discourse on climate change has become increasingly urgent.  关于气候变化的讨论变得越来越紧迫。
近义词：discussion, conversation, debate, dialogue
反义词：silence
词性：noun


discourse:/dɪˈskɔːrs/
verb
讲述；论述；探讨
speak or write authoritatively about a topic
She discoursed eloquently on the subject of art history. 她雄辩地论述了艺术史这个主题。
近义词：discuss, speak, lecture, expound
反义词：be silent, listen
词性：verb
