granniest:/ˈɡræniɪst/| adj.|最像祖母的；最慈祥的；最絮叨的|Most resembling a grandmother in character; most kind, gentle, or fussy.|例句：She has the granniest smile I've ever seen. 她有着我见过的最慈祥的笑容。|近义词：kindest, gentlest, most motherly|反义词：meanest, cruelest, youngest|词性:形容词
词源: "granny" (grandmother) + "-est" (superlative suffix).  The word "granny" itself comes from the Old French word "granny," meaning "grandmother."
