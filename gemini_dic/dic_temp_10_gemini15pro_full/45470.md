curatrix:/kjʊəˈreɪtrɪks/
noun
女监护人；女管理人
A woman appointed by a court to manage the affairs of another person legally deemed incapable of managing their own affairs.
She was appointed curatrix of her elderly mother's estate. 她被任命为她年迈母亲的遗产管理人。
The curatrix oversaw the finances of the incapacitated individual. 女监护人监管了丧失行为能力者的财务。
Synonyms: guardian, trustee, conservator
Antonyms: ward
