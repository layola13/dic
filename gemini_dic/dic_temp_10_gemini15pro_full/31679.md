chauffeur:/ʃəʊˈfɜː(r)/ (UK) /ʃoʊˈfɜːr/ (US)| n. v.|司机；驾驶；|A person employed to drive a private or rented automobile.  A person who chauffeurs someone.|例句：He works as a chauffeur for a wealthy family. 他为一个富裕的家庭当司机。|例句：She chauffeured her boss to the airport. 她开车送老板去机场。|近义词：driver, operator|反义词:passenger|词性:名词, 动词
