credence:/ˈkriːdəns/| n. |信任，相信；凭证|belief, acceptance, trust, faith|例句：The scientist's theory gained credence after it was supported by new evidence.  这位科学家的理论在得到新证据的支持后获得了信任。

例句：His claims lacked credence, as he had a history of dishonesty. 由于他有不诚实的历史，他的说法缺乏可信度。|近义词：belief, trust, faith, confidence, acceptance|反义词：disbelief, distrust, skepticism, doubt|词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Middle English: from Old French credence, from creire ‘believe’, from Latin credere.
