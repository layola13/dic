logined:/ˈlɒɡɪnd/ | v. | 已登录（非标准用法） | This word is not standard English. The correct past tense and past participle of "log in" is "logged in".  It's formed by combining the verb "log" with the adverb "in".  | *Incorrect:* He logined to his account.  *Correct:* He logged in to his account. 他登录了他的帐户。 | 近义词: logged in | 反义词: logged out | 词性: 动词
