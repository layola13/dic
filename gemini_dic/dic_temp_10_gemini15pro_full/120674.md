misadding:/ˌmɪsˈædɪŋ/
verb
错误地加，加错
Adding incorrectly; making a mistake in addition.
She misadded the numbers in the column and got the wrong total. 她把这一列的数字加错了，得出了错误的总数。
miscalculate, miscompute
calculate correctly, add correctly
