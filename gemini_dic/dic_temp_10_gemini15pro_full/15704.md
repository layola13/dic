balaji:/bɑːləˈdʒiː/| n.|巴拉吉,  (印度教神名)|A Hindu deity, often considered an incarnation of Vishnu. Balaji is particularly venerated at the Tirupati temple in Andhra Pradesh, India. |例句：Millions of pilgrims visit the Balaji temple every year. 数百万朝圣者每年都会参观巴拉吉神庙。

例句：Balaji is a popular deity in South India. 巴拉吉是南印度很受欢迎的神。|近义词：Venkateswara, Govinda|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
