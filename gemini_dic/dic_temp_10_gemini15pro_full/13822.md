auditioning:/ɔːˈdɪʃənɪŋ/
| n. |试镜，试演；试音 | the process of performing or presenting oneself for assessment, typically in order to be considered for a role or position. | She was nervous about her auditioning for the lead role. 她很紧张自己要试演主角。| 近义词: trying out, screening | 反义词:  N/A | noun

auditioning:/ɔːˈdɪʃənɪŋ/
| adj. |试演的，试镜的 (Less common as an adjective) | related to the act of auditioning |  The auditioning actors waited nervously.  参加试镜的演员们紧张地等待着。| 近义词:  N/A | 反义词:  N/A | adjective
