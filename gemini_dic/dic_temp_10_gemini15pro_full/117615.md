megapixel:/ˈmeɡəˌpɪksəl/ |n.| 百万像素 |A unit of graphic resolution equivalent to one million pixels.  It is commonly used to describe the resolution of digital cameras and image sensors.|This camera has 16 megapixels.  这个相机有1600万像素。|Synonyms: resolution, definition|Antonyms: N/A|noun

megapixel:/ˈmeɡəˌpɪksəl/ |adj.| 百万像素的 |Having or relating to a resolution of one million or more pixels.|The new phone boasts a megapixel camera. 这款新手机拥有百万像素的摄像头。|Synonyms: high-resolution|Antonyms: low-resolution|adjective
