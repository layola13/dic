illicit:/ɪˈlɪsɪt/| adj.|非法的，违禁的|Forbidden by law, rules, or custom.|例句：The police discovered an illicit drug operation. 警方发现了一起非法毒品交易。|例句：He was involved in illicit activities. 他参与了非法活动。|近义词：illegal, unlawful, illegitimate, prohibited, forbidden|反义词：legal, lawful, legitimate, permitted, allowed|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *illicitus*, from in- “not” + licitus “lawful”, from licēre “to be permitted”.
