nonessential:/ˌnɒnɪˈsenʃəl/ /ˌnɑːnɪˈsenʃəl/| adj. n.|非必要的，不重要的；非必需品|Not absolutely necessary; not essential.  Something that is not essential.|例句：The government has announced cuts in nonessential spending. 政府宣布削减非必要支出。|例句：During a crisis, it's important to differentiate between essential and nonessential purchases. 在危机期间，区分必需品和非必需品的购买至关重要。|近义词：unnecessary, superfluous, inessential, dispensable|反义词:essential, necessary, crucial, vital|词性:形容词, 名词

词源:
From non- + essential.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Essential" comes from Late Middle English, from late Latin *essentialis, from essentia 'being, essence'.
