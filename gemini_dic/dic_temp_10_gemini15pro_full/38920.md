confessionary: /kənˈfɛʃənəri/ (UK) /kənˈfɛʃəˌnɛri/ (US)| n. |忏悔室; 告解室; (尤指罗马天主教的)忏悔;  |a small enclosed structure, booth, or stall in a church where a priest sits to hear confessions. Also refers to the act of confessing sins.|例句：He entered the confessionary to seek forgiveness. 他进入忏悔室寻求宽恕。

例句：The confessionary was dimly lit and smelled of incense. 忏悔室光线昏暗，散发着熏香的气味。|近义词：confessional|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


confessionary: /kənˈfɛʃənəri/ (UK) /kənˈfɛʃəˌnɛri/ (US)| adj. |忏悔的;告解的|relating to confession, especially of sins.|例句：Confessionary practices vary across different religions. 忏悔的做法因宗教而异。

例句：He wrote a confessionary letter to his friend. 他给朋友写了一封忏悔信。|近义词：penitential|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective
