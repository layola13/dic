mistrustfully:/mɪsˈtrʌstfəli/
adverb
不信任地，怀疑地
With suspicion; showing a lack of trust.
She looked at him mistrustfully, unsure if she could believe his story. 她怀疑地看着他，不确定是否能相信他的故事。
He eyed the stranger mistrustfully. 他不信任地打量着那个陌生人。

近义词:suspiciously, doubtfully, skeptically
反义词:trustingly, confidently, believingly
词性:adverb

词源:
From mistrust + -fully. Mistrust from mis- + trust.
