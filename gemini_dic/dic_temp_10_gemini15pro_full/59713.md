eclecticism: /ɪˈklɛktɪsɪzəm/
noun
折衷主义; 博采众长
The practice of deriving ideas, style, or taste from a broad and diverse range of sources.
Her home décor is a fascinating blend of Victorian furniture and modern art, a true example of eclecticism.  她家的装潢是维多利亚时代家具和现代艺术的迷人融合，是折衷主义的真实体现。
The museum's collection is an eclectic mix of ancient artifacts and contemporary paintings.  博物馆的藏品是古代文物和当代绘画的折衷组合。
synonyms:  diversity, variety, heterogeneity, pluralism
antonyms:  uniformity, homogeneity, consistency, sameness
