meet-and-greet:/ˈmiːt ən ɡriːt/
noun
见面会；与…见面打招呼
An informal gathering at which people, especially celebrities, meet and talk to other people.
例句：The band did a meet-and-greet before the concert. 乐队在演唱会前举办了见面会。

例句：The president attended a meet-and-greet with local business owners. 总统出席了与当地企业主的见面会。

近义词：reception, gathering, social
反义词：farewell
