overrepress:/ˌoʊvərɪˈprɛs/| v. |过度抑制，过度压制|To repress beyond what is normal or necessary; to excessively suppress or control.|The government overrepressed dissent, leading to widespread unrest. 政府过度压制异议，导致了广泛的动荡。|The negative feedback loop in this system tends to overrepress the target gene. 该系统中的负反馈回路倾向于过度抑制目标基因。|近义词：suppress, stifle, repress, inhibit|反义词：express, encourage, promote|词性:动词

词源:
From over- + repress.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much." "Repress" comes from the Latin "repremere," meaning "to press back" or "to check."
