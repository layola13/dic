arched:/ɑːrtʃt/ /ɑːrʧt/| adj.|拱形的，弓形的，弯曲的|Having the curved shape of an arch.  A structure that spans a space and supports weight, formed from pieces of wedge-shaped blocks, called voussoirs.|The cat arched its back. 猫弓起了背。|Synonyms: curved, bowed, vaulted, rounded, humped|Antonyms: straight, flat|adjective


arched:/ɑːrtʃt/ /ɑːrʧt/| v.|（使）成弓形，（使）拱起|past tense and past participle of arch|He arched his eyebrows in surprise. 他惊讶地扬起了眉毛。|Synonyms: curve, bend, bow, arc|Antonyms: straighten, flatten|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *arched*, *archede*, from Old French *arché*, past participle of *archer*, from Latin *arcāre*, from *arcus* (“bow, arch”).
