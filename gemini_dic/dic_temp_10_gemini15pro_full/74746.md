freeware:/ˈfriːwɛər/
n.
免费软件
Software that is available for use at no monetary cost.  It is often distributed over the Internet or on CDs bundled with magazines.
I downloaded some useful freeware for editing photos.  我下载了一些用于编辑照片的有用的免费软件。
Many companies offer freeware versions of their software to attract new customers. 许多公司提供其软件的免费版本以吸引新客户。
近义词：shareware, gratis software
反义词：commercial software, payware
词性：名词
