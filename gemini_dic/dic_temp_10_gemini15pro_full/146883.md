overingeniously: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʒiːniəsli/
adverb
过于巧妙地；过分精巧地
in a way that is too clever or complicated
Her argument was overingeniously constructed, making it difficult to follow. 她的论证构建得过于巧妙，让人难以理解。

The plot of the movie was overingeniously contrived. 这部电影的情节设计得过于复杂。

Synonym: overelaborately, too cleverly, excessively ingeniously
Antonym: simply, plainly, artlessly
词性: 副词
