doormats:/ˈdɔːrmæts/ /ˈdɔːrˌmæts/| n. |门垫|A mat placed before a door, typically outside, for wiping one's shoes on.|She wiped her muddy boots on the doormat. 她把沾满泥的靴子在门垫上擦了擦。|近义词：mat, rug|反义词: (No direct opposite, perhaps "bare floor")|noun
