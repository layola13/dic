facetiousness:/fəˈsiːʃəsnəs/
n.
滑稽；戏谑；爱开玩笑
The tendency to be facetious; joking inappropriateness.
His facetiousness was inappropriate at the funeral. 他的戏谑在葬礼上很不合适。
近义词：levity, jocularity, wittiness, flippancy
反义词：seriousness, solemnity, gravity
词性：名词

facetious:/fəˈsiːʃəs/
adj.
爱开玩笑的；滑稽的；戏谑的
Treating serious issues with deliberately inappropriate humor; flippant.
He made a facetious remark about the boss. 他对老板说了一句戏谑的话。
近义词：jocular, witty, humorous, playful, flippant
反义词：serious, solemn, grave
词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *facetiosus*, from Latin facetiae ("witticisms, jests"), plural of facetia, from facetus ("witty, elegant, fine, courteous").  Ultimately from Latin facere ("to do, make").
