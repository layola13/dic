entreative:/ɪnˈtriːtɪv/ | adj. | 恳求的，哀求的 | Of the nature of or expressing entreaty; pleading; beseeching. |  She cast an entreative glance at her father, hoping he'd change his mind. 她恳求地看了父亲一眼，希望他能改变主意。 |  例句：His entreative letters went unanswered. 他恳求的信件没有得到回复。 | 近义词：pleading, beseeching, imploring, suppliant | 反义词：demanding, assertive, imperious | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Late Middle English: from Old French *entreatif, from entreater ‘to entreat’.
