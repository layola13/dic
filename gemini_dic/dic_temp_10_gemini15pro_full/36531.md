collaborateur:/kəˌlæbəˈrɑːtər/ |kəˌlæbəˈreɪtər/
n.|合作者，协作者|A person who collaborates with another or others, especially in literary or scientific work.|例句：He was a close collaborateur of the prime minister. 他是首相的亲密合作者。|例句：She found a willing collaborateur in the project.  她在项目中找到了一个乐于合作的伙伴。|近义词：associate, partner, colleague, confederate|反义词：opponent, adversary, rival|Noun
