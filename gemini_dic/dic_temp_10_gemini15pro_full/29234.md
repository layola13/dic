cassareep:/ˈkæsəriːp/
n.
木薯汁，苦木薯浓缩汁
a dark, thick syrup made by boiling down the juice of the bitter cassava root, used as a flavoring and coloring agent in Caribbean cuisine.
Cassareep is a key ingredient in pepperpot stew.  木薯汁是胡椒罐炖菜的关键成分。
Cassareep adds a rich, smoky flavor to dishes. 木薯汁为菜肴增添了浓郁的烟熏味。
近义词:  molasses (in terms of consistency and color) 就浓稠度和颜色而言，类似于糖蜜
反义词:  (No direct antonym) 没有直接的反义词
词性: noun
