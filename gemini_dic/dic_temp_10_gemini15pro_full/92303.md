hypnocyst: /ˈhɪpnoʊˌsɪst/
n.
催眠囊;催眠状态
A state resembling sleep, induced by hypnosis.  A quiescent state resembling suspended animation.
例句：The therapist guided the patient into a deep hypnocyst. 治疗师引导病人进入深度催眠状态。

例句：The creature retreated into its hypnocyst for the winter.  这种生物在冬天会退回到它的催眠囊中。
近义词：trance, hypnotic state, dormancy
反义词：wakefulness, alertness
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From hypno- (Greek *hypnos* "sleep") + cyst (Greek *kustis* "bladder, pouch").  Essentially meaning a "sleep pouch" or "state of sleep."
