misappropriate:/ˌmɪsəˈprəʊprieɪt/ /ˌmɪs əˈproʊpriˌeɪt/| vt.| 盗用，挪用，滥用|To dishonestly or unfairly take something, especially money, for one's own use that belongs to someone else.| The former CEO was charged with misappropriating company funds.  这位前首席执行官被指控挪用公司资金。| misuse, embezzle, divert| appropriate, allocate| verb

misappropriation:/ˌmɪsəˌprəʊpriˈeɪʃn/ /ˌmɪs əˌproʊpriˈeɪʃn/| n.| 盗用，挪用，滥用|The dishonest or unfair act of taking something, especially money, for one's own use that belongs to someone else.| The investigation revealed a significant misappropriation of public funds.  调查揭露了大量的公款被挪用。| misuse, embezzlement, diversion| allocation, appropriation| noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (meaning "wrong" or "bad") + appropriate (meaning "to take for one's own use"). The word "appropriate" comes from the Latin appropriare, meaning "to make one's own."
