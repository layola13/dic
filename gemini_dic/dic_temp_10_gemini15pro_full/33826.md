| | | | The eagle soared in circumgyre patterns above the canyon.  老鹰在峡谷上空盘旋翱翔。 | 近义词：rotating, revolving, circling, orbiting | 反义词: static, stationary | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin circum- (around) + gyrare (to turn, revolve)
