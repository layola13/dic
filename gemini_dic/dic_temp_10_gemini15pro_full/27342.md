camberkeeled:/ˈkæmbərˌkiːld/
adjective
翘龙骨的，弓形龙骨的
Having a curved keel, like a camber, or arch.  This shape is designed to reduce drag and improve performance in the water.
The yacht's camberkeeled hull sliced through the waves effortlessly. 这艘游艇的弓形龙骨轻松地划过海浪。
近义词: arched keel, curved keel
反义词: flat-bottomed, straight keel
词性: adjective
