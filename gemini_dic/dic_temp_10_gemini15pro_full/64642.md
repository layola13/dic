erythrodextrin:/ɪˌrɪθroʊˈdɛkstrɪn/
noun
红糊精
A soluble starch formed by the hydrolysis of starch. It gives a red color with iodine.
Erythrodextrin is formed during the breakdown of starch by amylase. 红糊精是在淀粉酶分解淀粉的过程中形成的。
Starch is progressively hydrolyzed to erythrodextrin, then achrodextrin, and finally maltose. 淀粉逐步水解成红糊精，然后是无色糊精，最后是麦芽糖。
Synonym:dextrin
Antonym:N/A
