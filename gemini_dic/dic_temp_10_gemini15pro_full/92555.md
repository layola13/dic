hysteresis:/ˌhɪstəˈriːsɪs/
n.|滞后(现象),迟滞现象|The tendency of a system to remain in its present state and resist change, or the lag between a cause and its effect.|The hysteresis of the thermostat caused the temperature to fluctuate.|lag, delay|advance, anticipation|noun
