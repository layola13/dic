intracranial:/ˌɪntrəˈkreɪniəl/
adjective
颅内的
Located or occurring within the skull.
Example: Intracranial pressure can cause headaches. 颅内压会引起头痛。
Example: The surgeon performed an intracranial operation. 外科医生进行了一次颅内手术。
Synonym: endocranial
Antonym: extracranial
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From intra- (within) + cranial (relating to the skull).  "Cranial" comes from the Latin "cranium" (skull), which in turn comes from the Greek "kranion" (skull).
