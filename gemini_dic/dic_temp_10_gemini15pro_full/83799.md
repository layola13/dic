hairpin:/ˈhɛərˌpɪn/ (US), /ˈheəˌpɪn/ (UK)| n. | 发夹；U形弯；发卡弯 | A small, U-shaped piece of metal or plastic used to hold hair in place. A sharp bend in a road, shaped like a hairpin. | She used a hairpin to keep her hair out of her face. 她用发夹把头发别在脸后。 The car struggled to navigate the hairpin bends in the mountain road. 这辆车艰难地在山路的U形弯道上行驶。 |近义词：bobby pin, hair grip, hairpin bend (for the road meaning)|反义词: N/A | noun

hairpin:/ˈhɛərˌpɪn/ (US), /ˈheəˌpɪn/ (UK)| adj. |  急转弯的；U形的 |  Describing a sharp bend in a road, resembling the shape of a hairpin. | The hairpin turn was particularly treacherous in the icy conditions. 在结冰的路况下，这个急转弯尤其危险。  The cyclists slowed down for the hairpin curve.  骑自行车的人在U形弯道处减速。 | 近义词：sharp, tight, acute (for bends) | 反义词: straight, gentle | adjective
