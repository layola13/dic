experientialist:/ɪkˌspɪəriˈɛnʃəlɪst/
n.|经验主义者|A person who emphasizes the importance of experience, especially sensory experience, in the formation of ideas and knowledge.|She's an experientialist who believes that learning comes through doing, not just reading.|近义词:empiricist, pragmatist|反义词:rationalist, theorist
词性:名词
