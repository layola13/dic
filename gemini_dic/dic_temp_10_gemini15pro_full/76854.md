gangetic:/ɡænˈdʒɛtɪk/
adjective
恒河的；与恒河有关的
Relating to the Ganges River in India.
The Gangetic dolphin is an endangered species. 恒河豚是一种濒危物种。
The Gangetic plain is a fertile region. 恒河平原是一片肥沃的地区。
Near synonyms:  fluvial, riparian
Antonyms:  none (antonyms would be unrelated to a specific river)
