jadelike:/ˈdʒeɪdlaɪk/
adjective
像玉的; 玉一般的
Resembling jade in appearance or quality.
The vase had a smooth, jadelike finish.  这个花瓶表面光滑，像玉一样。
Her eyes were a striking, jadelike green. 她的眼睛是醒目的翡翠绿。
近义词: jade-green, jade-colored, emerald
反义词: dull, opaque
词性: adjective
