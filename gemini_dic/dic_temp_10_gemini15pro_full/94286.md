impersuadable:/ˌɪmpərˈsweɪdəbl/
adjective
不可说服的；固执的
not persuadable; stubbornly persistent in one's opinion.
He was utterly impersuadable on the subject. 他在这个问题上完全无法被说服。
Synonyms:inflexible, obstinate, unyielding, adamant, intractable, immovable, fixed, resolute, determined, stubborn, headstrong, willful, pigheaded, mulish
Antonyms:persuadable, flexible, amenable
词性:adjective
