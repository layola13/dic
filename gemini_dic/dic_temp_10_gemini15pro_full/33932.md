cirrhus:/ˈsɪrəs/ (US), /ˈsɪrəs/ (UK)| n.|卷云；触须；卷须|A high-altitude cloud characterized by thin, wispy strands, often resembling a horse's tail. It is composed of ice crystals.  Also, a tendril-like appendage of some animals.|例句：The cirrus clouds painted the sunset sky. 卷云装点了日落的天空。

例句：The octopus uses its cirri to grasp prey. 章鱼用它的触须抓住猎物。|近义词：tendril, filament, wisp (for cloud: mare's tail) |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
