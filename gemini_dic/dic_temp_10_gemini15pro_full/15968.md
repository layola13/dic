balsas:/ˈbɑːlsəz/ | n. | 木筏；轻木筏（尤指南美洲印第安人使用的） | A raft made of bundles of reeds or light wood, especially one used by South American Indians. | The indigenous people used balsas to navigate the river.  土著居民使用木筏在河上航行。| raft, float | boat, ship | noun

balsas:/ˈbɑːlsəz/ | n. | 轻木；轻木木材 | The wood of the balsa tree, used for making models and rafts because it is very light. |  He built a model airplane out of balsa wood. 他用轻木做了一个飞机模型。 | lightweight wood, corkwood | hardwood, heavy wood | noun
