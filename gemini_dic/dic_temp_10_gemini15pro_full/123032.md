monde:/mɔ̃d/ (French pronunciation)| n.| 世界; 世俗; 社会 |The world; the universe; the earth with its inhabitants and all things upon it. Also refers to secular society, as opposed to religious life.|  She wants to travel the monde and see different cultures. 她想周游世界，看看不同的文化。| The political monde was buzzing with the news. 政界对这条消息议论纷纷。| synonyms: world, universe, cosmos, society, secular world | antonyms: hereafter, heaven, otherworld | noun

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Le monde change constamment. = The world is constantly changing.
Le monde est plein de merveilles. = The world is full of wonders.

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Le monde est en train de changer rapidement. = The world is changing rapidly.  (This tense is less common with "monde" as it refers to a generally existing state.)
Les scientifiques étudient comment le monde est en train d'évoluer. = Scientists are studying how the world is evolving.

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Le monde a beaucoup changé ces dernières années. = The world has changed a lot in recent years.
Le monde a connu de nombreuses guerres. = The world has known many wars.

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
(This tense is rarely used with "monde".)

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Le monde était différent autrefois. = The world was different in the past.
Le monde a traversé de nombreuses crises. = The world went through many crises.


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
(This tense is rarely used with "monde".)

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Le monde avait déjà changé avant mon arrivée. = The world had already changed before my arrival.
Le monde avait connu de grandes transformations. = The world had undergone great transformations.

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
(This tense is rarely used with "monde".)

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Le monde changera encore. = The world will change again.
Le monde sera différent dans le futur. = The world will be different in the future.

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
(This tense is rarely used with "monde".)

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
Le monde aura beaucoup changé d'ici 2100. = The world will have changed a lot by 2100.
Le monde aura connu de nouveaux défis. = The world will have faced new challenges.

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
(This tense is rarely used with "monde".)


虚拟语气:
Si je pouvais changer le monde, je le ferais. = If I could change the world, I would.
J'aimerais que le monde soit un meilleur endroit. = I wish the world were a better place.

被动语态:
(Less common with "monde" as the subject.)

疑问句:
Comment est le monde aujourd'hui ? = How is the world today?
Quel est l'avenir du monde ? = What is the future of the world?

否定句:
Ce n'est pas la fin du monde. = It's not the end of the world.
Le monde n'est pas parfait. = The world is not perfect.



PHRASE->

tout le monde = everyone  所有人
le monde entier = the whole world 全世界
du monde = some, any (used with uncountable nouns) 一些
le beau monde = high society 上流社会
le monde des affaires = the business world 商界
