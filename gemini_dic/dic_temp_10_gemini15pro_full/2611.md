adolescents: /ˌædəˈlesənts/ | n. | 青少年 |  A young person developing into an adult.  This period is generally considered to be between puberty and adulthood. | Many adolescents struggle with issues of identity. 许多青少年都在为身份认同问题而苦恼。

Adolescents are often influenced by peer pressure. 青少年经常受到同侪压力的影响。 | synonyms: teenagers, youths, juveniles | antonyms: adults, children, elders | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adolescenstem (nominative adolescens), present participle of adolescēre ("to grow up, mature"), from ad- ("to, towards") + alēscere ("to grow, be nourished").
