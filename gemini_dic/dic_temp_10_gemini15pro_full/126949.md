narratives:/ˈnærətɪvz/ | n. |故事，叙述，记叙文| a spoken or written account of connected events; a story. | The novel weaves together several different narratives. 这部小说交织了几个不同的故事。| synonyms: story, tale, account, recital, chronicle | antonyms:  None (Narratives are inherently neutral; the opposite might be "facts," "data," or "silence," but these aren't direct antonyms.) | noun


narratives:/ˈnærətɪvz/ | adj. |叙事的，叙述的| relating to the process of narration |  He has a highly narrative style of writing. 他的写作风格非常具有叙事性。| synonyms: storytelling, descriptive, recounting | antonyms:  None (Similar to the noun form, a direct antonym is difficult to define. Perhaps "lyrical," "poetic," or "abstract" could be considered contrasting styles.) | adjective
