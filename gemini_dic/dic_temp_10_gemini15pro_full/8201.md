anti-educational:/ˌæntiˌɛdʒʊˈkeɪʃənəl/
adjective
反教育的，不利于教育的
Hindering or opposing education; not promoting or conducive to learning.
That TV show is anti-educational; it promotes violence and stereotypes.  那个电视节目是反教育的；它宣扬暴力和刻板印象。
The government's decision to cut funding for schools was seen as an anti-educational move. 政府削减学校资金的决定被视为一项反教育的举措。
Synonyms: counter-educational, uneducational, miseducative
Antonyms: educational, pro-educational, instructive
