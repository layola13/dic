mushily:/ˈmʌʃɪli/ | adv. | 糊状地，软绵绵地 | In a soft, pulpy, or mushy manner. | The overripe bananas turned mushily in the heat.  过熟的香蕉在高温下变得软烂。| 近义词：softly, squidgily | 反义词：firmly, solidly | 词性: 副词

mushily:/ˈmʌʃɪli/ | adv. | 感情脆弱地，多愁善感地 | In a sentimental or overly emotional way. | He spoke mushily about his childhood. 他多愁善感地谈论他的童年。| 近义词：sentimentally, mawkishly | 反义词：stoically, unemotionally | 词性: 副词


词源: From mushy + -ly. "Mushy" originates from "mush," which likely comes from a blend of "mash" and "slush."
