nights:/naɪts/
n.
夜晚；晚上
Plural form of night.  The period between sunset and sunrise.
Many people find it hard to sleep during hot summer nights.
炎热的夏夜，许多人发现很难入睡。

近义词: evenings
反义词: days
词性: 名词(plural)
