neurolinguists: /ˌnjuːroʊˈlɪŋɡwɪsts/ | n. | 神经语言学家 |  Experts who study the relationship between the brain and language, specifically how the brain processes and produces language. | Neurolinguists are investigating how bilingualism affects brain structure. 神经语言学家正在研究双语能力如何影响大脑结构。 | Neurolinguists are exploring the neural networks involved in language comprehension. 神经语言学家正在探索参与语言理解的神经网络。 |  Synonyms: language scientists, cognitive neuroscientists (related) | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
