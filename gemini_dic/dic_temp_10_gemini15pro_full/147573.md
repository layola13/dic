overpersecuted: /ˌoʊvərˈpɜːrsɪkjuːtɪd/
adjective
过度迫害的
Having been persecuted excessively or to an unreasonable degree.
The overpersecuted religious minority finally found refuge in a neighboring country.  这个被过度迫害的宗教少数群体最终在邻国找到了避难所。
The overpersecuted journalist fled the country.  这位受尽迫害的记者逃离了该国。
近义词: oppressed, victimized, hounded, harassed
反义词: protected, sheltered, defended
词性: adjective

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "persecute" (from Latin persecutus, past participle of persequi "to follow persistently, pursue, hunt down")
