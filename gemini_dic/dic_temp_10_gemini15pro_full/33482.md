chyme:/kaɪm/| n. |食糜|The pulpy acidic fluid which passes from the stomach to the small intestine, consisting of gastric juices and partly digested food.|The chyme moves slowly through the intestines as nutrients are absorbed. 食糜在肠道中缓慢移动，同时营养物质被吸收。|Synonyms: pulp, paste|Antonyms: None|Noun
