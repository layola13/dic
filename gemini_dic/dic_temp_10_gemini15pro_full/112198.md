loon:/luːn/
n.|潜鸟；愚人，疯子|A large aquatic diving bird with a loud, haunting cry. Also, an informal term for a crazy or foolish person.|例句：We saw a loon diving for fish on the lake. 我们看到一只潜鸟在湖中潜水捕鱼。|例句：He acted like such a loon at the party. 他在派对上表现得像个疯子。|近义词：fool, idiot, madman (for person); diver (for bird)|反义词：genius, sage (for person)|词性:名词

loons:/luːnz/
n.|潜鸟（复数）；愚人，疯子（复数）|Plural form of "loon," referring to multiple aquatic diving birds or multiple crazy or foolish people.|例句：Several loons were calling across the misty lake. 几只潜鸟在雾气弥漫的湖面上鸣叫。|例句：Those loons are always getting into trouble. 那些疯子总是惹麻烦。|近义词：fools, idiots, madmen (for people); divers (for birds)|反义词：geniuses, sages (for people)|词性:名词
