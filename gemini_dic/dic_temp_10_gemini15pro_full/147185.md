overluxuriantly: /ˌoʊvərˌlʌɡˈʒʊəriəntli/
adverb
过度奢华地，过于繁茂地
in an excessively luxurious or profuse manner.
The gardens were overluxuriantly planted, with flowers spilling onto the paths.  花园里种植得过于繁茂，花朵都蔓延到小路上来了。
The banquet was decorated overluxuriantly, with gold and jewels everywhere. 宴会装饰得过于奢华，到处都是金银珠宝。
近义词: excessively, profusely, immoderately, exorbitantly
反义词: sparsely, modestly, simply, plainly
词性: adverb

词源:
From over- + luxuriantly.  Over- 表示“过度”，luxuriantly 来自 luxuriant (繁茂的，奢华的)，最终源自拉丁语 luxus (奢侈，放纵).
