concertina:/ˌkɒnsərˈtiːnə/ (UK), /ˌkɑːnsərˈtiːnə/ (US)| n.|六角手风琴；六角形|A musical instrument of the free-reed aerophone family, like a small accordion, but with buttons instead of a keyboard, and with hexagonal ends.|He played a lively tune on his concertina. 他用他的六角手风琴演奏了一首欢快的曲子。|近义词：accordion, squeezebox|反义词: (No direct antonym)|名词

concertina:/ˌkɒnsərˈtiːnə/ (UK), /ˌkɑːnsərˈtiːnə/ (US)| v.|（使）折叠成六角形；（使）像六角手风琴那样折叠|To collapse or fold something into a concertina shape.|The bus concertinaed in the crash. 公共汽车在车祸中折叠成了六角形。|近义词：fold, collapse, compress|反义词: expand, unfold|动词
