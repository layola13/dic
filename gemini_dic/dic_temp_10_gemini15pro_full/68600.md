fantasize:/ˈfæntəsaɪz/ /ˈfæntəˌsaɪz/ | v. | 幻想，想象 | To indulge in fantasies or daydreams; to imagine something pleasant or exciting. |  She fantasized about winning the lottery. 她幻想中了彩票。

He fantasized about traveling the world. 他幻想着环游世界。

| 近义词：imagine, daydream, dream, envision, muse | 反义词：face reality | 词性: 动词
