overlaying:/ˌoʊvərˈleɪɪŋ/
verb
覆盖，重叠
the act of placing one thing over another
例句：Overlaying one image onto another can create interesting effects. 将一个图像覆盖在另一个图像上可以产生有趣的效果。

例句：The artist was overlaying the sketch with thin washes of color.  艺术家正在用薄薄的彩色颜料覆盖素描。
近义词：covering, superimposing, overlapping
反义词：revealing, exposing
词性: v.


overlay:/ˈoʊvərleɪ/
noun
覆盖物，重叠物
a layer or covering that is placed over something else
例句：A transparent overlay protected the map from damage.  一张透明的覆盖层保护地图免受损坏。

例句：The software allows you to add text overlays to your photos.  该软件允许您在照片上添加文本覆盖。

近义词：covering, layer, film
反义词：underlay
词性: n.


overlay:/ˈoʊvərleɪ/
adjective
覆盖的，重叠的
placed over something else
例句：The overlaying textures created a rich visual effect. 重叠的纹理创造了丰富的视觉效果。

例句：The overlaying music added to the emotional impact of the scene. 覆盖的音乐增加了场景的情感冲击力。
近义词：covering, overlapping, superimposed
反义词：underlying
词性: adj.

词源: From Old English *oferlecgan, from ofer (over) + lecgan (to lay).
