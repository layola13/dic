burgeon:/ˈbɜːrdʒən/
verb
开始生长; 发芽; 迅速发展
begin to grow or increase rapidly; flourish
例句：The city’s population has burgeoned in recent years.  近年来，该市人口迅速增长。

例句：New suburbs have burgeoned all around the city. 城市周围涌现出新的郊区。

近义词：flourish, thrive, prosper, expand, boom, mushroom
反义词：wither, decline, shrink, contract, diminish
词性：v.
