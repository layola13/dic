hasidism:/ˈhɑːsɪdɪzəm/ /ˈhæsɪdɪzəm/
n.
哈西德主义，哈西德教派
A Jewish religious movement characterized by its emphasis on mysticism, piety, and joy.
Hasidism originated in Eastern Europe in the 18th century. 哈西德主义起源于 18 世纪的东欧。

The Hasidism community is known for its distinctive dress and customs. 哈西德教派以其独特的服饰和习俗而闻名。

近义词:Hasidic Judaism
反义词: (No direct antonym, but potentially opposing philosophies within Judaism could be considered)
词性:noun
