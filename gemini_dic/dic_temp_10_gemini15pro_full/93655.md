imbroglio:/ɪmˈbroʊljoʊ/ /ɛmˈbroʊljoʊ/
noun
混乱；纠纷；阴谋
a confused or complicated situation or affair, typically one involving a disagreement or misunderstanding.
The inheritance became an imbroglio when the deceased's will couldn't be found.  遗产继承变成了一场纠纷，因为死者的遗嘱找不到了。

The political imbroglio threatened to destabilize the entire region. 政治纠纷有可能破坏整个地区的稳定。

synonym: entanglement, predicament, quandary, muddle, confusion, complication
antonym: solution, resolution, clarity, order, simplicity
n.
