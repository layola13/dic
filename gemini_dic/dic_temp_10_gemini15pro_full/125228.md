multiculturalisms:/ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəlɪzəmz/
n.
多元文化主义（复数）
The existence or promotion of multiple cultural traditions within a single jurisdiction.
Many sociologists study the benefits and challenges of multiculturalisms in urban environments.  许多社会学家研究城市环境中多元文化主义的益处和挑战。
Multiculturalisms offer a variety of perspectives. 多元文化主义提供了各种各样的视角。
Near Synonyms: cultural diversity, pluralism, interculturalism
Antonyms: monoculturalism, homogeneity
noun
