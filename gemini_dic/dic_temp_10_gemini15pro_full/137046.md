nonstickiest:/ˌnɑːnˈstɪkiɪst/ | adj. | 最不粘的 |  Superlative form of nonsticky: most nonsticky.  It describes something that has the least sticky quality compared to other items in a group. | This is the nonstickiest pan I've ever used.  这是我用过的最不粘的锅。 | The nonstickiest surface repelled even honey.  最不粘的表面甚至能排斥蜂蜜。| 近义词: least sticky | 反义词: stickiest | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "sticky" + "-est" (suffix indicating superlative degree).  The word "sticky" comes from Old English "stician" meaning "to adhere."
