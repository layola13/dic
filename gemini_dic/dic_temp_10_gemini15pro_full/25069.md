bub:/bʌb/
noun
兄弟；伙计；小伙子；(尤指男)宝贝儿
A term of endearment, often used for a boy or young man.
Example: Hey there, bub, how's it going? 嘿，伙计，最近怎么样？
Example: He called his younger brother "bub". 他叫他弟弟“小伙子”。
Synonyms: buddy, pal, dude, chap, fella, guy
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but could consider terms of disrespect) jerk, creep
