chargeableness:/ˌtʃɑːrdʒəbəlˈnɛs/ |n.|乐于助人，随和 |The quality of being willing and eager to help others; agreeable and easy to deal with. |Her chargeableness made her a popular colleague. 她的乐于助人使她成为受欢迎的同事。|Synonyms: affability, amiability, agreeableness, good-naturedness|Antonyms: disagreeableness, unhelpfulness, coldness, hostility|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From chargeable + -ness.  Chargeable meaning "liable to be charged or accused" comes from Old French chargable. The sense shifted to "ready to take on a task or responsibility," and eventually to the positive connotation of "willing and helpful."
