nonsententiousness:/ˌnɒnsɛnˈtɛnʃəsnəs/

A lack of  moralizing or dogmatism. |Her nonsententiousness was refreshing in a world full of self-righteous pronouncements. 在一个充满自以为是的宣言的世界里，她的不武断令人耳目一新。 |objectivity, impartiality, open-mindedness, fairness, neutrality|sententiousness, dogmatism, preachiness, moralizing, didacticism|noun
