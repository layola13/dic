palaced:/ˈpælɪst/| adj.|宫殿般的，宏伟的|Resembling a palace, especially in being spacious and splendid; magnificent.|例句：They lived in a palaced mansion overlooking the sea. 他们住在一座俯瞰大海的宫殿般的豪宅里。|近义词：grand, majestic, stately, opulent, luxurious|反义词：humble, modest, simple, plain|词性:形容词
词源: From palace + -ed, meaning "having the characteristics of a palace."  Ultimately derived from Latin *Palātium*, the name of one of the seven hills of Rome.
