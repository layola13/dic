maxillary:/ˈmæksɪləri/ (US), /mækˈsɪləri/ (UK)| adj. n.|上颌的，上颌骨|Relating to the jaw or jawbone, specifically the upper jaw.  The maxillary bones are the two bones that form the upper jaw.|The maxillary sinus is located above the upper teeth. 上颌窦位于上牙上方。|The patient suffered a maxillary fracture. 病人上颌骨骨折。|近义词：upper jaw, jawbone|反义词:mandibular|词性:adjective, noun

maxilla:/mækˈsɪlə/ (US), /mækˈsɪlə/ (UK)| n.|上颌骨|The bone that forms the upper jaw in vertebrates.|The maxilla forms the major portion of the face below the eyes. 上颌骨构成了眼睛下方脸部的大部分。|Damage to the maxilla can affect both breathing and eating. 对上颌骨的损伤会影响呼吸和进食。|近义词：upper jawbone|反义词:mandible|词性:noun
