nondisparity:/ˌnɒndɪˈspærəti/ |n.|无差异，平等|The report emphasized the nondisparity in access to healthcare between different groups. 该报告强调了不同群体之间在获得医疗保健方面不存在差异。|例句：The government is working towards nondisparity in education. 政府正在努力实现教育平等。

The company's policy promotes nondisparity in pay for equal work. 公司的政策提倡同工同酬。|近义词：equality, parity, equivalence|反义词：disparity, inequality, difference|词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "disparity" (from Latin "disparitas," meaning "inequality").
