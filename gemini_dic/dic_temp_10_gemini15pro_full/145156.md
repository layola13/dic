overassisted:/ˌoʊvərəˈsɪstɪd/
adjective
过度辅助的；过度帮助的
Provided with too much assistance; helped to an excessive degree, often to the point of hindering development or self-reliance.
The overassisted child struggled to tie his shoes on his own.  这个被过度帮助的孩子很难自己系鞋带。
Overassisted startups often fail to develop crucial problem-solving skills.  被过度帮助的初创公司往往无法发展出关键的解决问题的能力。
Synonyms: overprotected, coddled, overhelped
Antonyms: neglected, unsupported, independent
词性: 形容词
