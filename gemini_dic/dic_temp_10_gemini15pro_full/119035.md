methylal:/ˈmɛθəlæl/ (US) /ˈmɛθɪlæl/ (UK)| n. |甲缩醛;二甲氧基甲烷|A colorless flammable liquid with a low boiling point, used as a solvent and in organic synthesis.  It is formally the dimethyl acetal of formaldehyde.|Methylal is used as a solvent in the production of resins and plastics. 甲缩醛在树脂和塑料生产中用作溶剂。|Methylal is also used as a fuel additive. 甲缩醛也用作燃料添加剂。|synonyms: dimethoxymethane, formaldehyde dimethyl acetal|antonyms: N/A|noun
