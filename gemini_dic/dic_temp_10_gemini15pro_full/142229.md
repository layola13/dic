orogastric: /ˌɔːrəˈɡæstrɪk/ /ˌɔːroʊˈɡæstrɪk/
adjective
经口胃的；口胃的
Relating to or involving passage through the mouth and stomach.
The doctor inserted an orogastric tube to feed the patient. 医生插入经口胃管给病人喂食。
The nurse administered medication via the orogastric route.  护士通过口胃途径给药。
近义词:  oral-gastric
反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly parenteral)
词性: adjective
