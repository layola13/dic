mahwa:/ˈmɑːxwɑː/| n. |马花树，马花果|An evergreen tree (Madhuca longifolia) native to India, whose flowers are used to make an alcoholic drink, and whose seeds yield an edible oil.|The mahwa tree is highly valued in its native regions. 马花树在其原产地非常珍贵。|The oil from the mahwa seeds is used in cooking. 马花籽油用于烹饪。|Synonyms: mowrah, mahua, butter tree|Antonyms: N/A|noun


mahwa:/ˈmɑːxwɑː/| n. |马花酒|An alcoholic beverage made from the flowers of the mahwa tree.|Mahwa is a popular drink in some parts of India. 马花酒在印度某些地区是一种很受欢迎的饮料。|The mahwa drink has a sweet and slightly intoxicating effect. 马花酒味道香甜，略带醉意。|Synonyms: mowrah, mahua|Antonyms: N/A|noun
