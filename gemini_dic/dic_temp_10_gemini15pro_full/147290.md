overmodernized: /ˌoʊvərˈmɒdərnɪzd/ | adj. | 过度现代化的 | Excessively modernized; modernized to an unnecessary or excessive degree. | The city felt sterile and overmodernized, losing its original charm. 这座城市感觉毫无生气，过度现代化，失去了原有的魅力。 | The newly renovated apartment was sleek and overmodernized, lacking any sense of warmth.  新装修的公寓光鲜亮丽，过度现代化，缺乏任何温暖的感觉。| 近义词: overdeveloped, excessively modernized | 反义词: underdeveloped, traditional, antiquated | 词性: 形容词

词源: over- + modernized.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much."  "Modernized" is the past participle of the verb "modernize," which means "to make modern."  The root "modern" ultimately comes from Latin "modernus."
