minicyclone:/ˈmɪniˌsaɪkloʊn/| n. |迷你旋风，小型气旋|A small-scale atmospheric vortex resembling a cyclone, characterized by rotating winds and often associated with dust devils or waterspouts. It's smaller and less intense than a true cyclone.|The minicyclone whipped up dust and leaves in the park. 迷你旋风在公园里卷起了灰尘和树叶。|近义词：dust devil, whirlwind|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps calm) |名词
