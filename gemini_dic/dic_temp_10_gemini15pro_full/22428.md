bookcrossing:/ˈbʊkˌkrɒsɪŋ/ (US) /ˈbʊkˌkrɒsɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|图书漂流；漂书|The practice of leaving a book in a public place for someone else to find and read, and then releasing it back into the wild.|例句：I found a great book through bookcrossing in the park. 我在公园里通过图书漂流找到了一本很棒的书。

例句：Bookcrossing is a fun way to share your love of reading. 图书漂流是一种分享你阅读爱好的有趣方式。|近义词：book sharing, book swapping|反义词:book hoarding|词性:noun
