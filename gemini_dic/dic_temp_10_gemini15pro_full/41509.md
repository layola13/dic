corollary:/ˈkɔːrəˌlɛri/ /ˈkɔːrəˌlɛri/| n. |推论，必然结果|A proposition that follows from (and is often appended to) one already proved.  A direct or natural consequence or result.|例句：A corollary of increased unemployment is increased crime. 失业率上升的一个必然结果是犯罪率上升。

例句：Loss of habitat is a corollary of deforestation. 栖息地丧失是森林砍伐的必然结果。|近义词：consequence, result, outcome, upshot|反义词:cause, origin|词性:noun
