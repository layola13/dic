nonprosperity:/ˌnɒnprɒˈsperɪti/
n.
lack of prosperity; the state of not being prosperous
The region's nonprosperity is largely due to the ongoing drought.  该地区的经济不景气很大程度上是由于持续的干旱造成的。
The nonprosperity of the company led to many layoffs.  公司的经济不景气导致许多人被解雇。
近义词: adversity, hardship, poverty, destitution
反义词: prosperity, affluence, wealth


词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + prosperity (from Old French prosperite, from Latin prosperitas)
