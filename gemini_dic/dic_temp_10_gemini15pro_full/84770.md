handrail:/ˈhændˌreɪl/ | n. |扶手，栏杆|A rail fixed to posts or a wall for people to hold on to for support, especially on stairs or ramps. |例句：She gripped the handrail tightly as she descended the steep staircase. 她紧紧抓住扶手，走下陡峭的楼梯。

例句：The handrail was smooth and polished from years of use.  多年的使用使扶手光滑而锃亮。|近义词：banister, railing, balustrade |反义词: None |词性: noun
