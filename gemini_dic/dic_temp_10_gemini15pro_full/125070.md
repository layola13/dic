mull:/mʌl/| v. n.| 仔细考虑，思考；研磨，磨碎；加热并加香料于（酒等）| To think about something carefully; to grind or crush something into a powder; to heat (wine, beer, etc.) with sugar and spices | She mulled over the offer for a few days before making a decision. 她仔细考虑了几天才做出决定。| 近义词: consider, ponder, contemplate, reflect, ruminate, meditate, deliberate, chew over, think over | 反义词: ignore, dismiss, disregard | 词性: 动词

mull:/mʌl/| n.| 混乱，困境| A state of confusion or disarray | The project was in a mull. 这个项目陷入了混乱。| 近义词: mess, muddle, confusion, disarray, disorder | 反义词: order, organization | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mullen*, from Old Norse *mylja* (“to crush, grind”), from Proto-Germanic *muljaną* (“to mill, grind”), from Proto-Indo-European *melh₂- (“to grind, crush”).  Related to English *meal* (ground grain).
