impenetrable:/ɪmˈpɛnɪtrəbl/| adj.|不能穿透的；不可理解的；难以进入的|Not able to be penetrated, pierced, entered, or passed through; also, not able to be understood or grasped.|例句：The jungle was so dense and impenetrable that we had to turn back.  这片丛林如此茂密，我们不得不原路返回。

例句：His motives remain impenetrable to me. 我仍然无法理解他的动机。


近义词：impervious, impermeable, impassable, unfathomable, incomprehensible|反义词：penetrable, permeable, passable, understandable, comprehensible|词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin impenetrabilis, from in- "not" + penetrabilis "that can be penetrated," from the verb penetrare "to penetrate."
