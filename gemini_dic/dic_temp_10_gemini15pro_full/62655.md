engagedly:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒɪdli/
adverb|专注地，投入地|In a manner showing deep involvement or concentration.|She listened engagedly to the speaker, her eyes never leaving his face. 她专注地听着演讲者讲话，眼睛一刻也没有离开过他的脸。|例句：He worked engagedly on the project, determined to finish it on time.  他全神贯注地投入到项目中，决心按时完成。|近义词：attentively, earnestly, intently|反义词:inattentively, distractedly
词性:副词
