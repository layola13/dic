degassing:/diːˈɡæsɪŋ/
n.|除气,脱气|The process of removing dissolved gases from a liquid or solid.|例句：Degassing the molten metal is crucial for preventing defects in the final product. 将熔融金属脱气对于防止最终产品中的缺陷至关重要。|例句：Degassing of the ocean contributes to atmospheric carbon dioxide levels. 海洋的脱气作用导致大气中二氧化碳含量的增加。|近义词：outgassing, venting|反义词：absorption, dissolution|词性：名词

词源:  de- (prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse") + gassing (from gas)
