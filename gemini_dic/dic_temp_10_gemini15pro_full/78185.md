geosphere:/ˈdʒiːəsfɪər/
noun
地圈；岩石圈；固体地球
The solid portion of the earth (distinguished from atmosphere, hydrosphere, and biosphere).  The geosphere includes the rocks, minerals, and landforms of the surface and interior of the Earth.
The earthquake originated deep within the geosphere. 地震起源于地圈深处。
近义词:lithosphere
反义词:atmosphere, hydrosphere, biosphere
词性:n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From geo- (earth) + -sphere (sphere).  "Geo" comes from the Greek "gē" meaning "earth."  "-sphere" comes from the Greek "sphaira" meaning "ball" or "globe."
