duena:/duˈeɪnjə/ /d(j)uˈeɪnə/ | n. | 女伴；陪伴人；女监护人 | A woman who serves as a companion and chaperone to other women, especially younger women. |  She acted as duena to her younger sister at the party. 她在派对上担任她妹妹的陪伴人。

The elderly woman served as a duena for the young heiress.  这位老妇人担任年轻女继承人的监护人。
