overimitatively: /ˈoʊvərˌɪmɪtətɪvli/
adverb
过度模仿地
in a way that imitates excessively, copying actions or behaviors beyond what is necessary or appropriate.
The child overimitatively copied every gesture the adult made, even the unnecessary ones.  这个孩子过度模仿了成年人做的每一个动作，即使是不必要的动作。
The toddler overimitatively repeated the phrase, not understanding its meaning.  这个幼儿过度模仿地重复了这句话，并不理解它的意思。
近义词: excessively mimetically, slavishly, uncritically
反义词: independently, originally, creatively
词性: adverb

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "imitative" (adjective meaning "inclined to imitate") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix)
