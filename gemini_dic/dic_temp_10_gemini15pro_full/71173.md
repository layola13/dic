fissirostral:/fɪsɪˈrɒstrəl/
adjective
深裂喙的; 裂喙的
Having a deeply cleft beak, as some birds.
The fissirostral swallows are adept at catching insects on the wing.  裂喙的燕子擅长在飞行中捕捉昆虫。

The black skimmer is a fissirostral bird.  黑剪嘴鸥是一种裂喙的鸟。
近义词: cleft-beaked
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) uncleft, entire
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *fissus* (cleft, split) + *rostrum* (beak, snout)
