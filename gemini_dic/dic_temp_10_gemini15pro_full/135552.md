nonpoisonousnesses:/ˌnɑːnˈpɔɪzənəsnəsɪz/
noun
无毒性（复数）
The state or quality of being nonpoisonous.  This word is rarely used in practical applications.
例句：The nonpoisonousnesses of these mushrooms were confirmed by the expert.  这些蘑菇的无毒性已由专家确认。
例句：He studied the nonpoisonousnesses of various berries in the forest. 他研究了森林里各种浆果的无毒性。
近义词：harmlessness, innocuousness
反义词：toxicity, poisonousness
词性:noun

nonpoisonous:/ˌnɑːnˈpɔɪzənəs/
adjective
无毒的
Not poisonous; harmless.
例句：These berries are nonpoisonous and can be eaten safely.  这些浆果无毒，可以安全食用。
例句：The snake is nonpoisonous, so there's no need to worry. 这条蛇无毒，所以不用担心。
近义词：harmless, innocuous, safe
反义词：poisonous, toxic, venomous
词性:adjective

poisonous:/ˈpɔɪzənəs/
adjective
有毒的
Containing or producing poison; toxic.
例句：Some mushrooms are poisonous and can cause serious illness.  有些蘑菇有毒，会导致严重疾病。
例句：The fumes from that chemical are poisonous.  那种化学物质产生的烟雾有毒。
近义词：toxic, venomous, noxious
反义词：nonpoisonous, harmless, innocuous
词性:adjective
