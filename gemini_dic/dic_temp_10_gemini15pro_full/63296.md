enthusiast:/ɪnˈθuːziæst/ | n. | 爱好者；热心家；狂热者 | A person who is highly interested in a particular activity or subject. | She is a real gardening enthusiast. 她是一位真正的园艺爱好者。 |  He's a model train enthusiast. 他是一位火车模型爱好者。 | 近义词: fan, buff, aficionado, devotee | 反义词: critic, detractor, skeptic | 词性: 名词

enthusiastic:/ɪnˌθuːziˈæstɪk/ | adj. | 热情的；热心的；狂热的 | Having or showing intense and eager enjoyment, interest, or approval. |  She was enthusiastic about the new project. 她对这个新项目充满热情。 | The audience gave an enthusiastic response. 观众的反应非常热烈。 | 近义词: eager, ardent, zealous, passionate | 反义词: apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic | 词性: 形容词

enthusiastically:/ɪnˌθuːziˈæstɪkli/ | adv. | 热情地；热心地；狂热地 | In a way that shows intense and eager enjoyment, interest, or approval. | They greeted the news enthusiastically.  他们热情地回应了这个消息。 |  The children cheered enthusiastically for their team. 孩子们热情地为他们的队伍欢呼。| 近义词: eagerly, ardently, zealously, passionately | 反义词: apathetically, indifferently | 词性: 副词

词源：
From French *enthousiaste*, from Ancient Greek ἐνθουσιαστής (enthousiastḗs, “one inspired by a god”), from ἐνθουσιάζω (enthousiázō, “I am inspired or possessed by a god”), from ἐν (en, “in”) + θεός (theós, “god”).
