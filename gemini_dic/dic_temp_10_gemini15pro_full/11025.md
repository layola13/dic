arcade's:/ɑːrˈkeɪdz/ |n.| 街机厅的;游戏厅的 | Possesses something belonging to an arcade. This word demonstrates ownership or association with an arcade.  | Example: The arcade's games were all classics from the 80s.  游戏厅里的游戏都是80年代的经典款。 | Example: The arcade's neon sign buzzed all night.  游戏厅的霓虹灯招牌整夜嗡嗡作响。 | Synonym: game center's, video arcade's | Antonym: (No direct antonym, but consider) library's, museum's |  noun (possessive form)


Etymology:
From "arcade" + "'s" (possessive suffix). "Arcade" comes from the Old French "arcade," meaning "arch," ultimately from the Latin "arcus."  The meaning shifted to a covered passageway with shops, and later to a place with amusement games.
