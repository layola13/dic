marginality's:/ˌmɑːrdʒɪˈnælɪtiz/ | n. | 边缘性，边缘化 | The state or quality of being marginal; the process of becoming marginal.  It often refers to social exclusion, economic disadvantage, or a lack of power and influence. |  The marginality's impact on the community was devastating, leading to increased poverty and crime. 边缘化对社区的影响是毁灭性的，导致贫困和犯罪增加。|Synonyms: periphery, fringe, margin, outsiderness | Antonyms: centrality, mainstream, inclusion, core | noun
