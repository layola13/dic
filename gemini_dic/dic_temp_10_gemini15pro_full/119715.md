midshipman: /ˈmɪdʃɪpmən/ (US), /ˈmɪdʃɪpmən/ (UK)| n. |海军学员；见习军官|A student at a naval academy or college, training to become an officer. |例句：The midshipmen were learning navigation. 海军学员们正在学习航海。

例句：Several midshipmen were promoted to ensign after graduation. 几名海军学员毕业后被提升为少尉。

近义词：naval cadet, officer trainee|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) civilian, veteran|词性: noun
