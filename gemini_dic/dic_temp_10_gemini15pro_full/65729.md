eventuating:/ɪˈvɛntʃueɪtɪŋ/
verb
最终发生；结果是
culminating in a specified result.
The investigation eventually eventuated in the arrest of the suspect.  调查最终导致了嫌疑人的逮捕。
近义词: result, culminate, end, transpire
反义词: commence, begin, initiate
词性: verb
