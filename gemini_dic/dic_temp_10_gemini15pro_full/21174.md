blobber:/ˈblɒbə(r)/| v.| 哭诉；抽泣；发牢骚 |To cry noisily and uncontrollably; to weep and complain. |He blobbered about how unfair it all was. 他哭诉着这一切多么不公平。| 近义词：sob, wail, weep, whine | 反义词: laugh, rejoice | 词性: 动词
