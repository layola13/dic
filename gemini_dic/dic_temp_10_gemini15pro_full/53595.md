disburthen:/dɪsˈbɜːrdən/
verb
卸下负担，解除…的负担
To relieve of a burden; unload.
The ship was disburthened of its cargo. 这艘船卸下了货物。
近义词:unload, unburden, relieve, discharge
反义词:burden, load, encumber
词性:v.

disburthen:/dɪsˈbɜːrðən/
verb
吐露（心事等），透露
To express or reveal (thoughts, feelings, etc.).
He disburthened himself of his worries to his friend. 他向朋友倾诉了他的烦恼。
近义词:unburden, confide, divulge, reveal
反义词:conceal, suppress, hide
词性:v.
