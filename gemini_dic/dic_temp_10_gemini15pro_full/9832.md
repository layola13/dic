anytown:/ˈɛniˌtaʊn/
noun
任何城镇；典型城镇
A fictional or hypothetical town, used to represent a typical or ordinary small town.
Life in Anytown, USA can be quite peaceful.  在美国任何一个小镇，生活都可以很平静。
synonyms:  everytown, typical town, ordinary town
antonyms: metropolis, megacity
n.
