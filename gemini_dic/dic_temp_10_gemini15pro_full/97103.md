infestuous:/ɪnˈfɛstʃuəs/ | adj. | 充满害虫的；猖獗的；大批滋生的 | Overrun with or contaminated by something harmful or unwanted; teeming or swarming (especially with pests or vermin).  | 例句：The barn was infestuous with rats. 谷仓里老鼠成灾。| 例句：Corruption is infestuous in some political systems. 腐败在一些政治体系中猖獗。 | 近义词：ridden, overrun, swarming, teeming, rampant | 反义词：clean, pure, uncontaminated | 词性: 形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French enfesté ‘infected’, past participle of enfester, based on Latin infestus ‘hostile’.
