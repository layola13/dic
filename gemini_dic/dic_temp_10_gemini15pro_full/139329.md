obolo:/ˈɒbələʊ/ (UK), /ˈoʊbəloʊ/ (US)| n.|奥波尔（古代希腊货币单位）, 小额捐款|A small coin or sum of money, especially one given as an offering or contribution.  Originally a small silver coin of ancient Greece.|He offered an obolo to the beggar.| 近义词：mite, pittance, trifle, donation, contribution|反义词: fortune, wealth|noun
