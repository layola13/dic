indiscernibly:/ˌɪndɪˈsɜːrnəbli/ /ˌɪndɪˈsɜːrnɪbli/| adv.|难以察觉地，不易察觉地|In a way that is impossible or very difficult to see or notice.|The object moved indiscernibly slowly.  这物体移动得慢得难以察觉。|The lines on the map were so faint as to be almost indiscernibly. 地图上的线条太细了，几乎看不出来。|Synonyms: imperceptibly, unnoticeably, subtly|Antonyms: noticeably, obviously, visibly|Adverb
