corticose:/kɔːrtɪˌkoʊs/
adjective
多皮质的；具有厚皮层的
Having a thick cortex, bark, or rind.
The corticose stem of the plant provided a strong structure.  这种植物的多皮质茎秆提供了强壮的结构。
The ancient tree had a remarkably corticose trunk. 这棵古树有着异常厚实的树皮。
近义词: thick-barked, rindlike
反义词: thin-barked
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *corticōsus, from cortex (“bark, rind”) + -ōsus.
