chipboard:/ˈtʃɪpbɔːrd/| n. | 纸板，刨花板 |A type of board made from wood chips and resin, used in furniture and building. | The bookcase is made of chipboard.  这个书柜是用刨花板做的。| The back of the wardrobe is cheap chipboard. 衣柜的背面是廉价的刨花板。|Synonyms: particleboard, fiberboard | Antonyms: solid wood, hardwood | noun
