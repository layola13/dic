fast-trackers:/ˈfæstˌtrækərz/ (US), /ˈfɑːstˌtrækəz/ (UK)| n.| 快速晋升者，速成班学员| People who are identified as having potential for rapid promotion within an organization or those taking an accelerated course of study.  They are often given special training and opportunities. |The company identified several fast-trackers early on and provided them with mentoring and leadership development. 这家公司很早就确定了几位快速晋升者，并为他们提供指导和领导力发展。

The fast-trackers in the coding bootcamp were able to build a functional app in just a few weeks.  速成班的学员们在短短几周内就能够构建一个功能性应用程序。|近义词：high-flyers, go-getters, rising stars|反义词: laggards, underachievers|词性:Noun
