anti-italian:/ˌæntiɪˈtæliən/
adjective
反意大利的；仇视意大利的
Opposed to, prejudiced against, or hostile towards Italy, its people, or its culture.
There was a surge of anti-Italian sentiment after the war. 战后反意大利的情绪高涨。
Synonyms:  Italy-hating, anti-Italianism
Antonyms: pro-Italian, Italophile
adjective


anti-italian:/ˌæntiɪˈtæliən/
noun
反意大利主义者；仇视意大利的人
A person who is opposed to, prejudiced against, or hostile towards Italy, its people, or its culture.
The anti-Italians spread their hateful propaganda. 这些反意大利主义者散布他们可恶的宣传。
Synonyms: Italy-hater
Antonyms: Italophile, pro-Italian
noun
