intentness:/ɪnˈtɛntnɪs/| n. |专注，专心，一心一意|The state of being intent; concentration of attention; fixedness of purpose.|She listened with intentness. 她专心致志地听着。|近义词：concentration, focus, attention, absorption|反义词：distraction, inattention, carelessness|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French entente ‘intention’ (see intent) + -ness.
