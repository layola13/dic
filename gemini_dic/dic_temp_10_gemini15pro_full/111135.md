literatures:/ˈlɪtrətʃərz/ /ˈlɪtrəˌtʃʊrz/ | n. | 文学；文献；著作；印刷品 |  The body of written works of a language, period, or culture.  Also, printed material about a particular subject. | Example 1:  He studied English literature at university. 他在大学学习英国文学。

Example 2: The company produces a large amount of sales literature.  这家公司制作了大量的销售印刷品。| 近义词: writings, publications, books, articles | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "illiteracy" in a broad sense) | 词性: noun
