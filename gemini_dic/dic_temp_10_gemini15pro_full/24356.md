brined:/braɪnd/ | adj. | 盐水浸泡的; 用盐水腌制的 | Preserved or treated in brine. | The brined olives were delicious.  盐水橄榄很美味。| The turkey was brined for 24 hours before roasting. 这只火鸡在烤之前用盐水腌了24小时。 | 近义词: pickled, marinated, salted | 反义词: fresh, unsalted | 词性:形容词

brine:/braɪn/ | n. | 盐水; 海水 | Water saturated or strongly impregnated with salt. |  The chef soaked the cucumbers in brine.  厨师将黄瓜浸泡在盐水中。| The smell of the brine filled the air near the sea. 海水的气味弥漫在海边的空气中。 | 近义词: saltwater, seawater | 反义词: freshwater | 词性:名词

brine:/braɪn/ | vt. | 用盐水腌制; 用盐水浸泡 | To treat or preserve with brine. | He brined the pork before smoking it. 他在熏猪肉之前先用盐水腌制。|  We brined the chicken overnight to make it more tender. 我们把鸡肉浸泡在盐水中过夜，以使其更嫩。 | 近义词: pickle, marinate, salt | 反义词: --- | 词性:动词

词源:  From Old English *brýne (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *brunjo, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰer- (to boil, bubble, effervesce). Cognate with Dutch brijn, German Brine. The original meaning referred to bubbling or foaming, as salt water does when heated.
