nonblockaded:/ˌnɒnblɒˈkeɪdɪd/ /ˌnɑːnblɑːˈkeɪdɪd/| adj.|未被封锁的|Not subjected to a blockade; open to passage or trade.|The nonblockaded port allowed for a steady flow of supplies. 未被封锁的港口允许物资稳定流通。|The nonblockaded city thrived despite the ongoing conflict.  尽管冲突持续不断，这座未被封锁的城市依然繁荣昌盛。|近义词：open, accessible, unobstructed|反义词：blockaded, besieged, closed|词性:形容词

词源:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non.
blockade (noun/verb): From French blocus, from Dutch blokhuis "blockhouse."  The sense of "blockade" developed from the idea of a fort blocking a passage.
-ed (suffix):  Forms the past participle of verbs and also adjectives from nouns, indicating a state or condition.
