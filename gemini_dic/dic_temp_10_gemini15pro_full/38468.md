conchal:/ˈkɒŋkəl/ (UK), /ˈkɑːŋkəl/ (US)| adj.|贝壳状的；耳廓的|Relating to the concha of the ear.  Specifically, it refers to a structure shaped like a shell, particularly the concave shell of the external ear.|The conchal cartilage provides structural support to the ear. 耳廓软骨为耳朵提供结构支撑。|The surgeon performed a conchal resection to address the patient's prominent ears.  外科医生进行了耳廓切除术来处理患者的招风耳。|Synonyms: shell-like, auricular|Antonyms: None|adjective


concha:/ˈkɒŋkə/ (UK), /ˈkɑːŋkə/ (US)| n.|耳甲；贝壳|The concave shell of the external ear; a structure resembling a shell.|The concha helps to collect and funnel sound waves into the ear canal. 耳甲有助于收集声波并将其导入耳道。|Seashells, like the conch, have been used as musical instruments for centuries.  像海螺这样的贝壳几个世纪以来一直被用作乐器。|Synonyms: auricle, shell|Antonyms: None|noun
