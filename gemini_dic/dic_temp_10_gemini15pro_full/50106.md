dentiloquist:/ˌvɛnˈtrɪləkwɪst/ | n. | 腹语师 | A person who can speak or utter sounds so that they seem to come from somewhere else, especially a dummy. |  The ventriloquist made the dummy appear to be talking. 腹语师使木偶看起来像是在说话。|  Synonyms:  puppeteer | Antonyms: listener | noun
