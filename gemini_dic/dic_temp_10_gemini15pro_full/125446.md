multiplexed:/ˈmʌltɪplekst/| adj.|多路复用的，多路传输的|Combined or transmitted through a single channel or system.|The multiplexed signal carries data from multiple sources. 多路复用信号携带来自多个来源的数据。|combined, simultaneous|separate, individual|adjective


multiplex:/ˈmʌltɪpleks/| v. n.|多路传输，多路复用，多功能的|To combine multiple signals into one, or to transmit multiple signals simultaneously over a single channel; a building with multiple movie theaters.|The cable company multiplexes hundreds of channels onto a single fiber optic cable. 有线电视公司将数百个频道多路复用到一根光纤电缆上。|combine, merge, integrate|separate, divide|verb, noun
