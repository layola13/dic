dorgon:/ˈdɔːrɡɔn/| n. |多尔衮|Dorgon (1612–1650) was a Manchu prince and regent of the early Qing dynasty. He was the fourteenth son of Nurhaci, the founder of the Later Jin dynasty which preceded the Qing.  He played a crucial role in the Manchu conquest of China and the establishment of the Qing dynasty.|Dorgon was instrumental in the Qing conquest of China.|近义词：prince, regent|反义词:commoner|词性:noun
