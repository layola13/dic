overcooling:/ˌoʊvərˈkuːlɪŋ/
noun
过度冷却，过冷
The process of cooling something below its normal operating temperature or freezing point.
The engine suffered damage due to overcooling.  发动机由于过度冷却而受损。
The overcooling of the liquid led to its solidification.  液体的过度冷却导致其凝固。
supercooling, overchilling
overheating
n.
