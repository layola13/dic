conducive:/kənˈd(j)uːsɪv/| adj.|有益的，有助于…的|Making a place conducive to learning is very important.  创造一个有利于学习的环境是非常重要的。|例句：Regular exercise is conducive to good health.  经常锻炼有利于身体健康。

例句：A quiet atmosphere is conducive to concentration. 安静的环境有助于集中注意力。

近义词：favorable, helpful, beneficial, advantageous|反义词：unfavorable, detrimental, harmful, hindering|词性:adjective
