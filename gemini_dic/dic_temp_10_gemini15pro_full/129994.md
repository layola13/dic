non-negligent:/ˌnɑːn ˈnɛɡlɪdʒənt/
| adj. |无过失的，无疏忽的|Not negligent; characterized by the absence of negligence or carelessness.  Taking proper care and attention.|The court found the driver non-negligent in the accident. 法庭认定司机在此次事故中没有过失。|Synonyms: careful, responsible, conscientious, attentive, diligent |Antonyms: negligent, careless, irresponsible, reckless, inattentive|adjective
