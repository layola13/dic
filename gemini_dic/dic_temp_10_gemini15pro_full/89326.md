homogenization:/həˌmɒdʒənɪˈzeɪʃən/ /həˌmɑːdʒənɪˈzeɪʃən/| n.|均质化，同质化|The homogenization of culture is a complex issue with both benefits and drawbacks. 文化的同质化是一个复杂的问题，既有好处也有坏处。|例句：The homogenization of milk prevents cream from separating. 牛奶的均质化防止了奶油分离。|近义词：uniformity, standardization, blending|反义词:heterogenization, diversification|noun

homogenize:/həˈmɒdʒənʌɪz/ /həˈmɑːdʒənʌɪz/| v.|使均匀，使均质|The company homogenizes the milk before bottling it.  这家公司在装瓶前将牛奶均质化。|例句：Globalization has homogenized many aspects of popular culture. 全球化使流行文化的许多方面同质化。|近义词：standardize, blend, mix, unify|反义词:diversify, vary|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *homogénéisation*, from *homogénéiser* "to make homogeneous," from *homogène* "homogeneous," from Greek *homogenes* "of the same kind," from *homos* "same" + *genos* "kind, race, stock."
