antimusically:/ˌæntiˈmjuːzɪkli/
adv.
反音乐地；与音乐相反地；缺乏音乐性地
In a way that is not musical or harmonious; discordantly.
The band played antimusicically, creating a cacophony of sound.  这乐队演奏得毫无音乐性，制造出一片刺耳的声音。
The piece was performed antimusicically, lacking any sense of rhythm or melody. 这首曲子演奏得缺乏音乐性，没有任何节奏感或旋律感。
Near Antonyms: musically, harmoniously, melodically
Antonyms: musically
