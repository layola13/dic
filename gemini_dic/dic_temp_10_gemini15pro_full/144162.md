outshouldering: /ˌaʊtˈʃoʊldərɪŋ/
|v. |胜过，超越，比…更强壮|surpassing in strength or dominance, especially by having broader shoulders.  It can also refer to the act of pushing someone or something aside with one's shoulder.|The new player outshouldered the veteran for a spot on the starting lineup. 这名新队员凭借更强壮的体格，取代了老队员在首发阵容中的位置。|Synonyms: outdo, surpass, eclipse, outmaneuver, outstrip|Antonyms:  be inferior to, lose to, be surpassed by|verb

outshouldering: /ˌaʊtˈʃoʊldərɪŋ/
|n. |超越，胜过|The act of outshouldering someone or something.|His outshouldering of the competition led to his victory. 他对竞争对手的超越导致了他的胜利。|Synonyms: surpassing, outdoing, eclipsing|Antonyms:  failure, defeat, loss|noun
