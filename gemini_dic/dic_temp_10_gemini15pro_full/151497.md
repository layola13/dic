parries:/ˈpæriz/ | v. | 挡开，避开; parry的第三人称单数形式 | To ward off, especially with a countermove.  挡开，尤指用反击动作。 | Example: She skillfully parries all the journalist's awkward questions. 她巧妙地避开了所有记者的尴尬问题。 | Synonyms: avoids, evades, deflects | Antonyms: accepts, receives | verb
