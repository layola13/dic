cinnamonic:/ˌsɪnəˈmɒnɪk/ | adj. | 肉桂的，肉桂色的，肉桂酸的 | Relating to, derived from, or having the flavor or fragrance of cinnamon; containing cinnamic acid. | The cinnamonic scent filled the bakery. 肉桂的香味弥漫着整个面包店。 |  The tea had a warm, cinnamonic flavor.  这茶有一种温暖的肉桂味。| 近义词: cinnamony, aromatic, spicy | 反义词: bland, tasteless | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cinnamique*, from *cinnam(ome)* (cinnamon) + *-ique* (-ic). Ultimately from Latin *cinnamomum*, from Greek *κιννάμωμον* (kinnámōmon).
