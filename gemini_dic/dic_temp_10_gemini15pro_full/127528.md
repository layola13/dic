necromancer: /ˈnɛkrəˌmænsər/ | n. | 死灵法师；巫师 | A person who practices necromancy; a sorcerer or wizard who is believed to be able to communicate with the dead, especially in order to predict the future or to use their power. | The necromancer summoned a skeleton warrior from the depths of the earth. 死灵法师从地底深处召唤出一个骷髅战士。

The old necromancer lived in a crumbling tower on the edge of the forbidden forest.  老死灵法师住在禁林边缘一座摇摇欲坠的塔里。| 近义词: sorcerer, wizard, magician | 反义词:  None (no direct opposite for a profession) | 词性: noun
