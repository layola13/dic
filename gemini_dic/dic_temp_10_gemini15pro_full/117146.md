meaningless:/ˈmiːnɪŋləs/ | adj. | 无意义的，无价值的 | Having no meaning or significance; pointless; senseless. |例句：The meeting was meaningless; nothing was accomplished. 会议毫无意义，什么也没有完成。

例句：His words were meaningless; they lacked sincerity. 他的话毫无意义，缺乏诚意。

近义词：insignificant, pointless, futile, senseless, trivial, worthless | 反义词：meaningful, significant, important, valuable | 词性：形容词
