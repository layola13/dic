becalmed:/bɪˈkɑːmd/
adjective
静止的；停滞的；(尤指帆船)因无风而停航的
(especially of a sailing ship) unable to move through lack of wind; motionless
The ship was becalmed in the middle of the ocean. 这艘船在茫茫大洋中因无风而停滞不前。
stranded, stuck, motionless
moving, active
adjective
