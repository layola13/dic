hydrotherapic:/ˌhaɪdrəʊθəˈræpɪk/ | adj. |水疗法的，用水治疗的 | Relating to hydrotherapy; using water to treat illness or injury. |  Hydrotherapic treatments can be very beneficial for muscle soreness. 水疗法对肌肉酸痛非常有益。| Synonym: balneotherapeutic, hydropathic | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, consider:  medication-based, pharmaceutical) | adjective
