illusionists: /ɪˈluːʒənɪsts/
noun
魔术师；幻术师
A person who performs illusions to entertain an audience.
The illusionists captivated the audience with their dazzling tricks. 魔术师们用他们眼花缭乱的戏法吸引了观众。
The illusionists made the rabbit disappear into thin air. 魔术师们让兔子消失得无影无踪。
Synonyms: magician, conjurer, prestidigitator
Antonyms: realist
