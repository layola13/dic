botcheries:/ˈbɒtʃəriːz/
n.
拙劣的工作；笨手笨脚的修补；粗制滥造的东西
a piece of work that is done badly or clumsily; something that is poorly made
The dress she made was a complete botchery, with uneven seams and a crooked hem. 她做的裙子完全是粗制滥造，接缝不均匀，下摆也不直。

近义词:mess, bungle, hash
反义词:masterpiece, triumph
