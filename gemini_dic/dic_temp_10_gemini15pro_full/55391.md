distended:/dɪˈstɛndɪd/
adjective
肿胀的；膨胀的
Enlarged or swollen from internal pressure.
His stomach was distended from overeating. 他的胃因为吃得太多而胀起来了。

近义词:swollen, inflated, bloated, expanded
反义词:shrunken, contracted, deflated, compressed
词性:adjective


distended:/dɪˈstɛnd/
verb
（使）膨胀，肿胀
To swell or cause to swell by pressure from inside.
The balloon distended as it was filled with air.  气球充气后膨胀起来。

近义词:expand, swell, inflate, bloat
反义词:contract, shrink, deflate, compress
词性:verb
