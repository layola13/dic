friablest:/ˈfraɪəbələst/ |adj.|最易碎的；最易粉碎的|Most easily crumbled or pulverized; most friable.|The friablest biscuits are often the tastiest, but they crumble easily.  最易碎的饼干通常最好吃，但它们很容易碎。

The archaeologist carefully handled the friablest pottery shards. 考古学家小心翼翼地处理着最易碎的陶器碎片。|近义词: crumbliest, brittle, fragile|反义词: tough, strong, durable|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *friabilis, from friare (“to rub, crumble”), of uncertain origin.
