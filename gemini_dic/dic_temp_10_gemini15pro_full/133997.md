nonhomogeneities:/ˌnɒnhɒmədʒɪˈniːɪtiz/ | n. | 非均匀性；不均匀体；异质性 | The presence of nonhomogeneities in the material can affect its strength.  材料中非均匀性的存在会影响其强度。| The researchers studied the effects of nonhomogeneities on the propagation of light through the crystal. 研究人员研究了非均匀性对光在晶体中传播的影响。 | synonyms: heterogeneities, irregularities, variations, disparities | antonyms: homogeneities, uniformities, similarities | noun

Etymology:
From non- + homogeneity + -ities.  Homogeneity comes from the Greek "homogenes", meaning "of the same kind".
