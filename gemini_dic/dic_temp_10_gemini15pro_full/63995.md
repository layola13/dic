epistolary: /ɪˈpɪstəˌleri/ /ɪˈpɪstəˌlɛri/
adjective
书信体的；用书信写成的
Relating to or written in the form of letters.
Example:  Mary Shelley's Frankenstein is an epistolary novel. 玛丽·雪莱的《弗兰肯斯坦》是一部书信体小说。

Example:  He maintained an epistolary relationship with his sister while he was abroad. 他在国外期间与妹妹保持着书信往来。
synonym: letter-based, correspondence-based
antonym: non-epistolary
词性: adjective
