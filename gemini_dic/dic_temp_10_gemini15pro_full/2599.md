what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US) |pron. det. adv.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information about people or things.|例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

What color is her hair?  她的头发是什么颜色？|近义词：which|反义词: (no single antonym) |pronoun, determiner, adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hwæt, neuter of hwā (“who”), from Proto-Germanic *hwat, from Proto-Indo-European *kʷod. Cognate with Scots what (“what”), West Frisian wat (“what”), Dutch wat (“what”), German was (“what”), Danish hvad (“what”), Swedish vad (“what”), Icelandic hvað (“what”), Latin quod (“what, which”), Sanskrit कत् (kat, “who, which”).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you want for dinner? = 你晚饭想吃什么？

一般过去时:
What did he say? = 他说了什么？
What happened yesterday? = 昨天发生了什么事？

现在完成时:
What have you done? = 你做了什么？
What has she achieved so far? = 到目前为止她取得了什么成就？

过去完成时:
What had happened before I arrived? = 在我到达之前发生了什么事？
What had they discussed before the meeting? = 会议前他们讨论了什么？

将来时:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天要做什么？
What will the weather be like next week? = 下周天气会怎样？


疑问句:
What time is it? = 现在几点了？
What are you talking about? = 你在说什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to say. = 我不知道该说什么。
She didn't tell me what she wanted. = 她没有告诉我她想要什么。

感叹句:
What a beautiful day! = 多么美好的一天！
What a shame! = 真可惜！

关系从句:
I know what you mean. = 我知道你的意思。
That's what I'm talking about. = 这就是我所说的。

PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/你好吗？
What about...? = ...怎么样？/ ...怎么办？
What if...? = 如果...会怎么样？
What for? = 为了什么？/做什么用？
So what? = 那又怎样？
