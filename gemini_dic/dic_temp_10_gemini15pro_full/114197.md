maidenhairs:/ˈmeɪdnˌhɛərz/| n. |孔雀草，铁线蕨|A fern of the genus *Adiantum*, having delicate, often fan-shaped fronds and wiry black or purplish stalks.|例句：The maidenhairs thrived in the humid environment of the greenhouse.  铁线蕨在温室潮湿的环境中茁壮成长。

例句：She admired the delicate fronds of the maidenhairs growing along the stream. 她欣赏着沿着溪流生长的铁线蕨精致的叶子。|近义词：fern|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific plant) |词性:noun
