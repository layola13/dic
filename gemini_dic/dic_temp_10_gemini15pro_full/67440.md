extinguishments:/ɪkˌstɪŋɡwɪʃmənts/
n.
熄灭，扑灭；消灭；偿清
The act of extinguishing something, especially fire.  The extinguishments of the flames took several hours.  几小时才扑灭了火焰。
The fire brigade’s swift action resulted in the quick extinguishment of the blaze. 消防队行动迅速，很快扑灭了大火。
近义词:suppressions, quenchings
反义词:ignitions, kindlings
词性:名词
