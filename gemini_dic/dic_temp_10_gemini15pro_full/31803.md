checkmating:/ˈtʃɛkmeɪtɪŋ/
n.|将死; 逼入绝境|The act of placing an opponent's king in checkmate, thereby winning the game of chess.|He achieved checkmating his opponent in just five moves. 他只用了五步就将死了对手。|近义词:defeating, conquering|反义词:losing, surrendering|词性:名词 (derived from the verb "checkmate")

checkmate:/ˈtʃɛkmeɪt/
vt.|将死; 使彻底失败|To place (a king) in checkmate; to defeat completely and decisively.|The new evidence checkmated the defense lawyer's argument. 新的证据彻底驳倒了辩护律师的论点。|近义词:defeat, conquer, vanquish|反义词:lose, surrender, fail|词性:动词

词源: From Old French *eschec mat* "checkmate," borrowed from Arabic *šāh māt*  meaning "the king is dead/helpless/unable to escape."
