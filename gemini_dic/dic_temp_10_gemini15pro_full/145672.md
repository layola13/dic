overcook:/ˌoʊvərˈkʊk/ | v. | 煮过头，烹煮过久 | To cook food for too long, resulting in it becoming dry, tough, or burnt. | I overcooked the chicken and it was dry. 我把鸡肉煮过头了，它很干。| The steak was overcooked and tough. 牛排煮过头了，很硬。 | 近义词: burn, scorch | 反义词: undercook | 词性: 动词
