harmonizers:/ˈhɑːrmənaɪzərz/ /ˈhɑːrməˌnaɪzərz/| n.|协调者，和声歌手|People who create harmony, either in music (by singing or playing harmonizing notes) or in other areas (by mediating between different groups or ideas).|The harmonizers in the choir blended their voices beautifully. 合唱团里的和声歌手们优美地融合了他们的声音。

The UN acts as a harmonizer in international disputes. 联合国在国际争端中充当协调者的角色。|近义词：mediators, conciliators, singers, musicians|反义词:disruptors, antagonists|词性:Noun
