franchise: /ˈfrænˌtʃaɪz/ | n. | 特许经营权；加盟店；公民权，选举权 | A business arrangement in which a company gives another company or individual the right to sell its goods or services. | The company expanded rapidly by selling franchises.  这家公司通过出售特许经营权迅速扩张。| synonyms: license, permit, authorization | antonyms: prohibition, restriction | noun

franchise: /ˈfrænˌtʃaɪz/ | vt. | 授予特许经营权；给予公民权 | To grant a franchise to someone. | They franchised the restaurant chain across the country. 他们在全国范围内授予了这家连锁餐厅的特许经营权。| synonyms: license, authorize, empower | antonyms: revoke, withdraw | verb
