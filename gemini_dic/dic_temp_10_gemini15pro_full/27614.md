cancelling:/ˈkænsəlɪŋ/| v.|取消，撤销|The act of making something void or ineffective.  It refers to the process of stopping something that was planned or scheduled.|例句：He is cancelling his trip due to the storm.  他因为暴风雨取消了旅行。|近义词：aborting, revoking, withdrawing|反义词:confirming, approving|词性:动词

cancelling:/ˈkænsəlɪŋ/| adj.|取消的，撤销的 |Used to describe something that has been cancelled.  It can also describe something that has the effect of neutralizing or offsetting something else.|例句：The cancelling effect of the two forces resulted in no movement. 这两个力的抵消作用导致没有移动。|近义词：nullifying, invalidating|反义词:validating, confirming|词性:形容词
