back-down:/ˌbæk ˈdaʊn/ | phrasal verb | 退让，放弃，撤回 | To withdraw from a position in a dispute; to concede defeat. |  The union refused to back down on its demands for higher wages. 工会拒绝在提高工资的要求上让步。

The company backed down after threats of a lawsuit.  公司在受到诉讼威胁后做出了让步。 | 近义词：retreat, withdraw, concede, yield | 反义词：insist, persist, stand firm | 词性：动词短语
