misfiles:/ˌmɪsˈfaɪlz/ | n. | 错档；归错档案的文件 | Files that have been incorrectly filed or categorized. |  The clerk apologized for the misfiles and promised to rectify the issue.  办事员为归错档案的文件道歉，并承诺会纠正这个问题。 | synonyms: misfiled documents, wrongly filed papers | antonyms: correctly filed documents, properly organized files | noun
misfile:/ˌmɪsˈfaɪl/ | vt. | 把…归错档案 | To file something incorrectly. | I accidentally misfiled an important document and now I can't find it. 我不小心把一份重要文件归错了档案，现在找不到了。 | synonyms: file incorrectly, misplace, miscategorize | antonyms: file correctly, categorize properly | verb
