brewhouses:/ˈbruːhaʊzɪz/
noun
啤酒厂；酿酒厂
A building where beer is brewed commercially.
例句：The town boasts several traditional brewhouses.  这个小镇拥有几家传统的啤酒厂。

例句：The tour included a visit to a historic brewhouse.  这次旅行包括参观一家历史悠久的啤酒厂。

近义词：brewery, distillery
反义词：None (No direct opposite for a place of production)
词性：n. (noun)
