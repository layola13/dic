hexapla:/ˈhɛksəplə/| n.|六列对照圣经|A manuscript of the Hebrew Bible with six versions of the text in parallel columns (usually the Hebrew text, a Greek transliteration of the Hebrew, the Aquila, Symmachus, and Theodotion Greek versions, and Origen's edition of the Septuagint).|Origen prepared a massive work, the Hexapla, comparing six different versions of the Old Testament. 奥利金编写了一部巨著《六列对照圣经》，比较了六个不同版本的旧约。|近义词:polyglot bible|反义词: (None, as it's a unique work)|词性:noun
