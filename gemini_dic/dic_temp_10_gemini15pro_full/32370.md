chile:/ˈtʃɪli/ (US), /ˈtʃɪleɪ/ (UK)| n.|辣椒; 智利|A pungent hot-tasting pod of a variety of capsicum peppers, used in cooking; the Republic of Chile, a country in western South America.|例句：I like to add chile to my tacos. 我喜欢在我的墨西哥卷饼里加辣椒。

例句：Chile is a long, narrow country. 智利是一个狭长的国家。|近义词：(for the pepper) chili, chili pepper, cayenne pepper (for the country) none|反义词: (for the pepper) sweet pepper (for the country) none|词性:noun
