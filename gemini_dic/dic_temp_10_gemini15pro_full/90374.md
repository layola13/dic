howadji:/hoʊˈɑːdʒi/| n. | 豪杰，要人；(尤指)在东方国家旅行或居住过的欧美人 | A European or American who has traveled or lived in the Orient, especially one who has adopted local customs and dress. | He returned from his travels a veritable howadji, sporting flowing robes and a long beard. 他旅行归来，俨然一位豪杰，穿着飘逸的长袍，留着长长的胡须。| 近义词:effendi, notable, dignitary | 反义词: commoner, nobody | 词性: 名词

词源: From Turkish *hoca* (teacher, master) + Persian *-ji* (suffix indicating profession or activity).
