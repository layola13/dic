chore:/tʃɔːr/ /tʃɔr/| n.|家务杂事，讨厌的或令人不愉快的工作|A routine task, especially a household one; an unpleasant task.|例句：Washing dishes is my least favorite chore. 洗碗是我最不喜欢的家务。

例句：Mowing the lawn is a weekly chore. 修剪草坪是每周的家务。|近义词：task, duty, job, assignment|反义词:fun, entertainment, pleasure|词性:noun

词源:  From Old French *chore, from Late Latin *cora (unattested) "service, compulsory labor," from Greek khōra "what is provided, care, service," from khōreō "I make room, give way, withdraw."  Related to yard (n.1). Meaning narrowed to "small, routine task" by 1843.
