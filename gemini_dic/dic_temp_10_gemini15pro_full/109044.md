legales:/leɪˈɡɑːleɪz/ | adj. | 合法的，法律上的 |  Relating to or permitted by law. | Example: The contract is legally binding.  合同具有法律约束力。 | Example:  It is not legally permissible to drive without a license.  无证驾驶在法律上是不允许的。 | Synonyms: lawful, legitimate, licit | Antonyms: illegal, unlawful, illicit | adjective


legales (plural form): /leɪˈɡɑːleɪz/ | n. | 法律文件，法律事务 (Often used in plural form in Spanish and French, less common in English) | Legal documents or matters. | Example:  Los legales del contrato son complejos. (Spanish) The legalities of the contract are complex. 合同的法律条文很复杂。 |  Example: Il faut vérifier les légales. (French)  It is necessary to check the legalities. 有必要检查一下法律方面的事宜。| Synonyms: legal documents, legal matters, legalities (in English when used) | Antonyms: N/A (The opposite would be the absence of legal documents or matters, not a specific word.) | noun


词源:  From Late Latin *legalis, from Latin lex, legis "law."
