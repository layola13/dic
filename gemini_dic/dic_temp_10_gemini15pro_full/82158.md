groove:/ɡruːv/| n. vt.| 凹槽，沟；最佳状态；惯例|a long narrow cut or depression, especially one made to guide the movement of something; a settled routine or habit; a pronounced enjoyable rhythm in music|The wheels of the train run along grooves in the rails. 火车的轮子沿着铁轨上的凹槽行驶。|近义词：furrow, channel, rut, routine, habit|反义词：ridge, projection|noun

groove:/ɡruːv/| vt.|开槽于|make a groove or grooves in|He grooved the wood for the inlay. 他在木头上刻出凹槽以便镶嵌。|近义词：furrow, channel|反义词：smooth|verb
