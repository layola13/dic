overinhibiting: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈhɪbɪtɪŋ/
| adj. | 过度抑制的 | Inhibiting excessively; suppressing natural impulses or behavior to an excessive degree. |  He was so overinhibiting that he rarely spoke up in class, even when he knew the answer. 他过度抑制自己，即使知道答案也很少在课堂上发言。 | overcontrolling, repressive, restrictive | uninhibited, expressive, free | adjective

overinhibit: /ˌoʊvərɪnˈhɪbɪt/
| vt. | 过度抑制 | To inhibit excessively; to suppress natural impulses or behavior to an excessive degree. |  Her strict upbringing overinhibited her and made it difficult to form close relationships. 严格的家庭教育过度抑制了她，使她难以建立亲密关系。 | overcontrol, repress, restrict | liberate, encourage, free | verb
