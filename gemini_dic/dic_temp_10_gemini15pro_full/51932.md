diabetes mellitus:/ˌdaɪəˈbiːtiːz məˈlaɪtəs/| n.|糖尿病|A group of metabolic disorders characterized by hyperglycemia resulting from defects in insulin secretion, insulin action, or both.|My grandfather has diabetes mellitus and has to monitor his blood sugar levels regularly. 我的祖父患有糖尿病，必须定期监测血糖水平。|Diabetes is a serious health concern worldwide. 全球糖尿病是一个严重的健康问题。|synonyms: hyperglycemia, sugar diabetes|antonyms: hypoglycemia|noun
