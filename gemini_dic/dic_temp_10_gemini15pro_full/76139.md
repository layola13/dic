fuscation:/fʌsˈkeɪʃən/| n.|混淆，模糊，昏暗|The fuscation of the issue made it difficult to understand.|这个问题的模糊性使它难以理解。|例句：The lawyer's fuscation of the facts confused the jury. 律师对事实的混淆使陪审团感到困惑。

例句：The dense fog caused a fuscation of the landscape. 浓雾使景色变得模糊。|近义词：obfuscation, confusion, darkening, obscurity|反义词：clarification, elucidation, illumination|词性：名词

词源: From Latin *fuscatus*, past participle of *fuscare* "to darken," from *fuscus* "dark, dusky."
