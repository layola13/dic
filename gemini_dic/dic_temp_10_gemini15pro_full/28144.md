capitulate: /kəˈpɪtʃəleɪt/ | vi. | 投降，屈服，停止抵抗 | cease to resist an opponent or an unwelcome demand; surrender. |  The rebels were eventually forced to capitulate. 叛军最终被迫投降。| synonyms: surrender, yield, submit, give in, concede, succumb | antonyms: resist, fight, oppose | verb

capitulate: /kəˈpɪtʃəleɪt/ | vi. |  (因持续压力而) 让步，妥协| to stop trying to fight or resist something : to agree to do or accept something that you have been resisting or opposing | After a long argument, he capitulated and let her have her way.  经过长时间的争论，他妥协了，让她随心所欲。| synonyms: give in, yield, concede, succumb | antonyms: resist, fight, oppose | verb
