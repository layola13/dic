enjoin:/ɪnˈdʒɔɪn/ | vt. | 命令；禁止；嘱咐 | To direct or order with authority; to prohibit or forbid; to urge earnestly. | The judge enjoined the defendant from contacting the victim. 法官命令被告不得联系受害者。

The doctor enjoined him to rest. 医生嘱咐他休息。 | 近义词：command, order, prohibit, forbid, urge, admonish | 反义词：allow, permit | 词性：动词
