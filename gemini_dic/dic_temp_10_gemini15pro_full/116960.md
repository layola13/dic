mazurkas:/məˈzɜːrkəz/
noun
玛祖卡舞曲（波兰民间舞曲）
A lively Polish folk dance in triple time, characterized by accents on the second or third beat.  The term also refers to the music for this dance.
Chopin composed many beautiful mazurkas. 肖邦创作了许多优美的玛祖卡舞曲。
synonyms: mazurka,  mazurkas (plural form)
antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific dance.  Could consider contrasting dances like waltz or tango.)
