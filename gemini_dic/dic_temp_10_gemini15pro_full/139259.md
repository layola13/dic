obliger:/əˈblaɪdʒər/| n.|施恩者，帮别人忙的人|A person who confers a favor or does a kind act.|He was a great obliger of his friends, always ready to lend a helping hand. 他是一位乐于助人的朋友，总是乐于伸出援手。|近义词：benefactor, patron|反义词：ingrate|noun

oblige:/əˈblaɪdʒ/| vt.|迫使，强制；施恩惠于|To compel or require someone to do something; to do a favor or service for someone.|The law obliges parents to send their children to school. 法律规定父母必须送孩子上学。

The store owner obliged me by staying open late. 店主帮了我的忙，很晚才关门。|近义词：compel, force, favor, accommodate|反义词：exempt, excuse|verb
