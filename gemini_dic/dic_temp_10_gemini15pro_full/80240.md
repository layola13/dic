goes-around:/ɡoʊz əˈraʊnd/ | n. | 传播，谣言，流传 | something that is passed from person to person, often a rumour or illness |  The flu goes around quickly in schools. 流感在学校里传播得很快。| synonyms: circulates, spreads | antonyms: stays put, remains localized | noun

goes-around:/ɡoʊz əˈraʊnd/ | idiom |  报应；因果报应；一报还一报 |  what you do to others will eventually happen to you | What goes around comes around. / You reap what you sow. 种瓜得瓜，种豆得豆。| synonyms: karma, retribution | antonyms:  (no direct antonym, perhaps "undeserved consequence") | idiom
