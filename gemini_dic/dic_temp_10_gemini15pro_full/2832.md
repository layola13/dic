adventures:/ədˈventʃərz/| n.|冒险，冒险经历|An adventure is an exciting or unusual experience. It can also be a bold, usually risky undertaking.|She had many adventures during her travels in Africa.  她在非洲旅行期间经历了许多冒险。|synonyms: exploits, escapades, feats, experiences|antonyms: boredom, monotony|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *aventure* "chance, happening, event," from Latin *adventura* "a thing about to happen," from *advenire* "to arrive," from *ad-* "to" + *venire* "to come."  The sense of "exciting or unusual experience" developed in the late 14th century.


USAGE->
Plural Form:
The book detailed his many adventures in the Amazon. = 这本书详细描述了他在亚马逊的多次冒险。
Her childhood adventures shaped her into the person she is today. = 她童年的冒险经历塑造了她今天的样子。

Possessive Form:
The adventurer's spirit never left him. = 冒险家的精神从未离开过他。
The adventures' outcomes were uncertain. = 这些冒险的结果是不确定的。

As part of a compound noun:
Adventure travel is becoming increasingly popular. = 冒险旅行越来越受欢迎。
He joined an adventure club. = 他加入了一个冒险俱乐部。

Following a preposition:
He went on an adventure to find the lost city. = 他去冒险寻找失落的城市。
She told stories about her adventures in the jungle. = 她讲述了她在丛林中的冒险故事。

As the object of a verb:
They sought adventure and excitement. = 他们寻求冒险和刺激。
She recounted her adventures with great enthusiasm. = 她热情地讲述了她的冒险经历。
