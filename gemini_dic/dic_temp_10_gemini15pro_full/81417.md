grasswards:/ˈɡræs.wədz/| adv.|向着草地，朝向草地|Toward or in the direction of grass. |The dog ran grasswards, chasing a butterfly.  狗朝着草地跑去，追逐一只蝴蝶。|Synonyms: toward the grass, in the direction of the grass|Antonyms: away from the grass|Adverb
Etymology: grass + -wards (suffix indicating direction)
