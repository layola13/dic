aureoling:/ɔːriˈoʊlɪŋ/
aureoling|v.|环绕以光环，赋予光环|Surrounding with or as if with an aureole; giving a halo to.
The setting sun was aureoling the mountain peaks.|落日为山峰增添了光环。
aureoling|adj.|赋予光环的|Giving a halo to.
The aureoling effect of the mist made the trees look magical.|薄雾的光环效应使树木看起来很神奇。
aureoling|n.|光环，光轮|An aureole; a halo.
The aureoling of the saint in the painting was very bright.|画中圣徒的光环非常明亮。
近义词:haloing, glorifying, illuminating
反义词:obscuring, darkening
词性:v., adj., n.

词源: From aureole + -ing.  Aureole comes from Old French aureole, from Medieval Latin aureola "golden crown," diminutive of Latin aureus "golden."
