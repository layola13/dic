chiromancy: /ˈkaɪrəmænsi/ | n. | 手相术，掌纹术 | The art or practice of supposedly telling fortunes by examining the lines and other features of a person's hand. | She learned chiromancy from her grandmother. 她从祖母那里学习了手相术。 | He doesn't believe in chiromancy. 他不相信手相术。 | 近义词: palmistry | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
