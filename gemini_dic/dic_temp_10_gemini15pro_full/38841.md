confated:/kənˈfeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 混合的，混淆的 |  Blended together, especially in a way that creates confusion or makes it difficult to distinguish individual elements.  Derived from the Latin word "conflare," meaning "to blow together." | The historical accounts of the battle were so confated that it was impossible to determine what actually happened. 战斗的历史记载是如此混乱，以至于无法确定实际发生了什么。 | Synonyms:  amalgamated, blended, commingled, fused, intermingled, merged, mixed | Antonyms: separated, distinct, individual | Adjective
