implode:/ɪmˈploʊd/ | v. | 内爆；崩溃；瓦解 | To collapse or burst inward violently. | The old building imploded during demolition. 老建筑在拆除过程中内爆了。

The submarine imploded under the immense pressure of the deep ocean. 潜水艇在深海巨大的压力下内爆了。| 近义词：collapse, burst, crumble | 反义词: explode, expand | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin implodere (to clap the hands, drive in by clapping), from in- (in, into) + plaudere (to clap, applaud).  The modern sense of collapsing inward developed later.
