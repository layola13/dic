mirror-image:/ˈmɪrər ˌɪmɪdʒ/| n. |镜像; 镜中之像|An image that is seen in a mirror or other reflecting surface, appearing reversed from left to right.|The building's mirror image was reflected in the still water of the lake. 建筑物的镜像倒映在平静的湖水中。|synonyms: reflection, likeness, counterpart|antonyms: original, opposite, inverse|noun

mirror-image:/ˈmɪrər ˌɪmɪdʒ/| adj. |镜像的|Being a mirror image; reversed.|The two molecules are mirror-images of each other. 这两个分子互为镜像。|synonyms: reflected, reversed, inverted|antonyms: original, direct, unreversed|adjective
