paranoiac:/ˌpærəˈnɔɪæk/ | adj. n. | 偏执的；患妄想狂的；妄想狂患者 | Suffering from or exhibiting paranoia; characterized by or exhibiting extreme and irrational fear or distrust of others. | He had a paranoiac fear of being followed. 他偏执地害怕被人跟踪。 |  近义词: paranoid, delusional, suspicious, distrustful | 反义词: trusting, confiding | 词性: adjective, noun

paranoiac:/ˌpærəˈnɔɪæk/ | n. | 妄想狂患者 | A person who suffers from paranoia. | The paranoiac saw enemies everywhere.  这个妄想症患者到处都看到敌人。| 近义词: paranoid | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a person) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *paranoicus, from Ancient Greek παράνοος (paránoos, “deranged, insane”), from παρά (pará, “beside, amiss”) + νοῦς (noûs, “mind”).
