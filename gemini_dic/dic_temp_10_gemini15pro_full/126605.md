nagyagite:/ˈnɑːdjɑːɡɪtaɪt/| n. |叶碲铋矿|A rare telluride mineral containing gold, lead, tellurium, and bismuth. It is named after Nagyág, Romania, where it was first discovered. |Nagyagite is a valuable ore of gold and tellurium. 叶碲铋矿是金和碲的宝贵矿石。|Synonyms: sylvanite, krennerite|Antonyms: N/A|noun
