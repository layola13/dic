nonequivalent:/ˌnɒnɪˈkwɪvələnt/
adjective
不相等的，不等价的
not equivalent or equal, especially in value, amount, or meaning.
The two versions of the software are nonequivalent; the newer version has many more features. 这两个版本的软件不等价；新版本有更多功能。
synonyms: unequal, disparate, different, dissimilar, distinct
antonyms: equivalent, equal, identical, same
词性:形容词
