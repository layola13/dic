muffish:/ˈmʌfɪʃ/| adj.|闷闷不乐的；生气的；不高兴的|Annoyed and sullen; sulky.  Derived from "muffle," suggesting a suppressed or stifled feeling of resentment.|He gave me a muffish look when I interrupted his game. 他在我打断他游戏时给了我一个闷闷不乐的眼神。|近义词：sullen, sulky, grumpy, petulant|反义词：cheerful, happy, jovial|词性:形容词
