enschedule:/ɛnˈskɛdʒuːl/
verb
安排；列入计划；预定
To place something in a schedule; to schedule.
The meeting is enscheduled for next Tuesday.  会议安排在下周二举行。
The surgery was enscheduled for 2 pm.  手术安排在下午2点进行。
近义词: schedule, plan, arrange
反义词: cancel, postpone
词性: verb
