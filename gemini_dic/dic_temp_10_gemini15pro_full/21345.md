bloomingnesses:/ˈbluːmɪŋnəsnɪsɪz/
noun
盛开的状态；繁荣的状态
The state or quality of being in bloom, flourishing, or thriving.
The bloomingnesses of the flowers in spring are breathtaking. 春天花朵盛开的状态令人叹为观止。
Near Synonyms: efflorescences, blossomings, flourishings
Antonyms: declines, decays, witherings
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From blooming + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality).  Ultimately from bloom (n.) + -ing (present participle suffix) + -ness.
