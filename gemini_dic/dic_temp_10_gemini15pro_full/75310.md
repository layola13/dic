frontlet:/ˈfrʌntlət/| n.|额饰，前额带，护额|A band worn on the forehead, especially as an ornament or part of a headdress; a phylactery.|The high priest wore a golden frontlet inscribed with "Holiness to the Lord". 大祭司戴着刻有“归耶和华为圣”的金额饰。|近义词：headband, diadem, fillet, tiara|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

frontlet:/ˈfrʌntlət/| n.|（鸟类的）额甲|A colored patch of feathers on the forehead of a bird.|The bird's frontlet was a vibrant shade of blue. 这只鸟的额甲是鲜艳的蓝色。|近义词：forehead patch|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *frontelet, diminutive of front "forehead," from Latin frons (genitive frontis) "forehead, front."
