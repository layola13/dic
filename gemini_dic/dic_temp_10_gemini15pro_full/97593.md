inhabited:/ɪnˈhæbɪtɪd/ | adj. |有人居住的， обитаемый|Lived in; occupied. |例句：The island is inhabited by a small community of fishermen.  这个岛上居住着一个小渔民社区。

例句：The house appeared abandoned and uninhabited. 这房子看起来像是被遗弃了，无人居住。|近义词：occupied, populated, settled |反义词:uninhabited, deserted, vacant |词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: past participle of *inhabiten, from Latin inhabitare ‘dwell in’, from in- ‘in’ + habitare ‘dwell’.
