cements:/səˈments/ | n. v. | 水泥；胶结材料；巩固，加强 | A powdery substance made with calcined lime and clay. It is mixed with water to form mortar or mixed with sand, gravel, and water to make concrete.  As a verb, it means to bind together firmly. |  The builder used cement to make the concrete.  建筑工人用水泥制作混凝土。 | Synonyms: concrete, mortar, adhesive, glue, bind, consolidate, strengthen | Antonyms: weaken, loosen, separate | noun

cements:/səˈments/ | v. | 巩固，加强 | To make something stronger, more certain, etc. | The agreement cemented the relationship between the two countries.  这项协议巩固了两国之间的关系。 | Synonyms: solidify, strengthen, fortify, reinforce | Antonyms: weaken, undermine, dissolve | verb
