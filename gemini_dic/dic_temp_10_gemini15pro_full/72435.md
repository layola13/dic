fluidlike:/ˈfluːɪdlaɪk/
adjective
像流体一样的，似液体的
Resembling a fluid in its properties or behavior.
The molten lava had a fluidlike consistency. 熔岩具有类似流体的稠度。
liquid-like, fluid, watery
solid, viscous
adjective
