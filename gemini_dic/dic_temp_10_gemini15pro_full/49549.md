dematerializing: /ˌdiːməˈtɪəriəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
非物质化，消失
The process of ceasing to exist in physical form; converting assets into cash.
The magician made the rabbit dematerialize before our very eyes.  魔术师让兔子在我们眼前消失了。
近义词: disappearing, vanishing, fading, dissolving
反义词: materializing, appearing, solidifying
词性: verb (present participle)

dematerialize: /ˌdiːməˈtɪəriəlaɪz/
verb
非物质化，消失
To cease to exist in physical form; to convert assets into cash.
The spaceship dematerialized into thin air. 宇宙飞船消失在稀薄的空气中。
近义词: disappear, vanish, fade, dissolve
反义词: materialize, appear, solidify
词性: verb

词源:  "de-" (Latin prefix meaning "away from, down, off, thoroughly") + "materialize" (from "material" + "-ize").  "Material" ultimately derives from Latin "materia" (matter).
