devoids:/dɪˈvɔɪdz/| v.| 使…缺乏；剥夺 |devoids is the third-person singular present tense form of the verb "devoid," meaning to make something empty or lacking in something. It signifies the action of stripping something away or leaving it completely without a particular quality or attribute.|He **devoids** the room of all furniture. 他把房间里的所有家具都搬走了。|近义词：strips, empties, deprives|反义词:fills, provides, endows|词性: verb

Etymology:
From Old French *desvoidier*, from *des-* (expressing removal, separation) + *voidier* "to empty," from *voide* "empty."
