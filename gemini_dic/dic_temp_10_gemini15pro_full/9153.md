antinaturalists:/ˌæntiˈnætʃrəlɪsts/
n.|反自然主义者|People who oppose the philosophical view of naturalism, which emphasizes the natural world and scientific explanations.  They often believe in supernatural or spiritual forces beyond the natural realm.|The antinaturalists argued that morality cannot be reduced to simply biological drives. 反自然主义者认为道德不能简化为单纯的生物驱动力。|近义词:supernaturalists, spiritualists|反义词:naturalists|词性:noun

antinaturalistic:/ˌæntiˌnætʃrəˈlɪs.tɪk/
adj.|反自然主义的|Opposing or disagreeing with the principles of naturalism. This can refer to beliefs, actions, or artistic styles that seem to go against the natural world or scientific explanations.|His antinaturalistic philosophy emphasized the importance of human will and divine intervention. 他的反自然主义哲学强调了人类意志和神圣干预的重要性。|近义词:artificial, contrived, non-natural|反义词:naturalistic, natural|词性:adjective
