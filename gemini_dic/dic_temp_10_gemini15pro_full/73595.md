forethink:/fɔːrˈθɪŋk/
verb
预先考虑；预想；提前思考
to think about or consider something in advance
She had forethought to book a table in advance. 她提前预订了桌子。
foresee, anticipate, plan
fail to anticipate, overlook, neglect
