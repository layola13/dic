n.
公民身份；居民身份
The state of being a denizen; the status of an inhabitant; the fact of belonging to a particular place.
The artist explored themes of belonging and denizenship in her latest exhibition.  这位艺术家在她的最新展览中探讨了归属感和公民身份的主题。

The penguins' denizenship of the island is undisputed.  企鹅是该岛无可争议的居民。

近义词: citizenship, residency, inhabitancy, membership
反义词: alienage, foreignness, exile
