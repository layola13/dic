mesopodial:/ˌmɛzəˈpoʊdiəl/
adjective
中足的，中伪足的
Relating to the middle part of the foot, or, in amoeboid cells, relating to pseudopodia (temporary projections of the cytoplasm) formed from this region.
Amoebae extend their *mesopodial* regions to move and engulf food.  变形虫伸展其中足区域来移动和吞噬食物。
近义词:  pseudopodial
反义词:  (No direct antonym; possibly anterior podia or posterior podia depending on context)
词性:  adjective
