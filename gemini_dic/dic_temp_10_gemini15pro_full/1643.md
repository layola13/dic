acinus: /ˈæsɪnəs/ | n. | 腺泡 |  A small sac-like cavity in a gland, surrounded by secretory cells.  A terminal part of a gland with secretory cells. | The pancreas is composed of numerous acini that produce digestive enzymes. 胰腺由许多产生消化酶的腺泡组成。

The liver acinus is the functional unit of the liver. 肝腺泡是肝脏的功能单位。 | 近义词: alveolus, saccule | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *acinus*, meaning "grape" or "berry," from Greek *ákīnos* meaning "grape stone".  The term reflects the grape-like clustering of these secretory units.
