bromic:/ˈbroʊmɪk/| adj.|溴的，含溴的|Of or relating to bromine, especially with a valence of 5.|Potassium bromate is a powerful oxidizing agent. 溴酸钾是一种强氧化剂。|近义词:bromate (as in "bromate salts")|反义词: N/A|词性:形容词

词源:
From French *bromique*, from *brome* (“bromine”) + *-ique* (English *-ic*).
