gaugeable:/ˈɡeɪdʒəbl/
adjective
可测量的；可估计的
Able to be gauged or measured.
The impact of the new policy is not easily gaugeable.  这项新政策的影响不容易评估。
The depth of the water was gaugeable with a stick. 水的深度可以用一根棍子测量。
近义词:measurable, assessable, estimable
反义词:immeasurable, incalculable, indeterminable
词性:adjective
