damocleses:/ˈdæməkliːziːz/
n.
达摩克利斯之剑（的复数形式）;  随时可能降临的危险或灾祸（的复数形式）
Multiple instances of an impending doom or threat, like the Sword of Damocles.
The ambitious general lived with several damocleses hanging over his head: potential coups, assassination attempts, and the ever-present threat of foreign invasion.
近义词: threats, dangers, perils, menaces
反义词: securities, safeguards, protections
词性: 名词

damocles:/ˈdæməkliːz/
n.
达摩克利斯之剑; 随时可能降临的危险或灾祸
An impending doom or threat, like the Sword of Damocles.
The threat of a trade war hung over the nation like a damocles.
近义词: threat, danger, peril, menace
反义词: security, safeguard, protection
词性: 名词

词源: From the name of Damocles, a courtier of Dionysius II of Syracuse, over whose head a sword was hung by a single hair to illustrate the precariousness of power and happiness.
