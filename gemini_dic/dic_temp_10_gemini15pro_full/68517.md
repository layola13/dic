fanboy:/ˈfænˌbɔɪ/| n. | 狂热粉丝，迷弟 | An enthusiastic and devoted fan, especially of comics, science fiction, fantasy, or technology.  Typically used with a slightly derogatory connotation, suggesting excessive enthusiasm and uncritical admiration. | He's such a fanboy for that band, he buys all their merchandise. 他是那个乐队的狂热粉丝，他们的周边商品他都买。| 近义词：enthusiast, devotee, aficionado | 反义词: critic, detractor | noun
