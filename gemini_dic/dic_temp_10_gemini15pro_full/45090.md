cuboidal:/kjuːˈbɔɪd(ə)l/
adjective
立方形的，立方体状的
shaped like a cube; approximately cubic
The cells are small and cuboidal. 这些细胞小而呈立方体状。
近义词:cube-shaped, cubic
反义词:spherical, round
词性:adjective

cuboidal:/kjuːˈbɔɪd(ə)l/
noun
立方体
a cuboidal object
The protein forms cuboidals under certain conditions. 在特定条件下，这种蛋白质会形成立方体。
近义词:cube
反义词:sphere
词性:noun (rare usage)
