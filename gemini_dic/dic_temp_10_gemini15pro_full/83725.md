haidingerite:/ˈhaɪdɪŋəriːt/
n.
海丁格石(一种含水砷酸钙矿物)
A hydrated calcium arsenate mineral, Ca(AsO3OH)·H2O, occurring as colorless to white orthorhombic crystals.
Haidingerite is a relatively rare mineral found in some arsenic-rich hydrothermal veins. 海丁格石是一种相对稀有的矿物，存在于一些富含砷的热液矿脉中。
Haidingerite can be confused with pharmacolite. 海丁格石容易与毒石混淆。
Synonym: none (as it's a specific mineral name)
Antonym: none
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Named after Wilhelm Karl Ritter von Haidinger (1795-1871), Austrian mineralogist. 以奥地利矿物学家威廉·卡尔·里特·冯·海丁格(1795-1871)的名字命名。
