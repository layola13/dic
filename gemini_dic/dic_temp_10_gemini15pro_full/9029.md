antimarket:/ˌæntiˈmɑːrkɪt/ | n. | 反市场者，反市场行为 | A person or action that opposes the principles of a free market economy. | The protesters' antimarket actions disrupted the trade negotiations. 抗议者的反市场行为扰乱了贸易谈判。 | synonyms:  interventionism, protectionism | antonyms: free market, deregulation | noun
