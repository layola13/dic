hafting:/ˈhæftɪŋ/ | n. | 装柄，装把；柄，把；[机械工程] 柄固，夹紧 | The process of attaching a handle or hilt to a tool or weapon.  The secure fixing of a cutting tool to a machine tool. |  The hafting of the axe head was done with great care.  斧头的装柄做得非常仔细。 |  synonyms: attaching, fixing, securing, fitting, mounting | antonyms: detaching, removing, loosening | noun

hafting:/ˈhæftɪŋ/ | v. |  装柄，装把 |  The act of attaching a handle or hilt to a tool or weapon. |  He carefully hafted the new axe head. 他仔细地给新的斧头装上柄。 | synonyms: attach, fix, secure, fit, mount | antonyms: detach, remove, loosen | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hæft, from Proto-Germanic *haftō, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *keh₂p- ("to seize, hold"). Related to heave.
