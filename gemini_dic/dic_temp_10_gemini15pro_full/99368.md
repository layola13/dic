interdestructive:/ˌɪntərdiˈstrʌktɪv/
adjective
相互破坏的，互相摧毁的
Characterized by mutual destruction; destroying one another.
The interdestructive nature of their relationship left both parties emotionally scarred. 他们之间互相摧毁的感情让他们双方都留下了情感伤疤。
The two chemicals reacted in an interdestructive manner, neutralizing each other.  这两种化学物质以相互破坏的方式发生反应，相互中和。
近义词: mutually destructive, reciprocally destructive
反义词: constructive, beneficial
词性: adjective

词源:
From inter- (prefix meaning "between, among, mutually") + destructive.
