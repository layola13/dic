bonnilass: /ˈbɒnɪˌlæs/ (UK) /ˈbɑːnɪˌlæs/ (US)| n.| 美女，漂亮姑娘 | A term of endearment for a pretty girl or woman, often used in Scottish or Northern English dialects.| She's a bonny lass, isn't she? 她真是个漂亮姑娘，不是吗？ | 近义词: beauty, belle, pretty girl | 反义词: hag, crone | 词性: noun

词源:
From Scottish and Northern English dialect, from Scots *bonny* "beautiful, pleasant" (from Old French *bon*, *bone* "good") + *lass* "girl, young woman".
