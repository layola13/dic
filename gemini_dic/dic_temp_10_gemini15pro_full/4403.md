aland: /ˈeɪlənd/ | n. | 旱地；岛屿；水中高地 | A piece of land surrounded by water, or a high and dry area within a marsh or similar wet area. |  The boat approached the aland, a small patch of dry ground in the vast swamp.  船驶近了旱地，那是广阔沼泽中的一小块干燥地带。

The aland provided a safe haven for the migrating birds. 这块旱地为迁徙的鸟类提供了一个安全的避难所。| 近义词: island, isle, dry land | 反义词: water, sea, ocean | 词性: 名词

词源: From Old Norse *al-land "land not belonging to the sea," from al- "all" + land "land."  源自古诺尔斯语 *al-land “不属于海洋的土地”，由 al- “全部” + land “土地” 组成。
