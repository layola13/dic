especial:/ɪˈspɛʃəl/ |adj.| 特别的，尤其的| Notably distinct from others; exceptional.  Used especially in formal or literary contexts. | This is an especial occasion. 这是一个特别的场合。| The government has promised to take especial care of the elderly. 政府承诺要特别照顾老年人。| 近义词: special, particular, specific | 反义词: ordinary, common, general | 词性: adjective

词源: From Middle English *especial, borrowed from Old French especial, from Latin speciālis (“pertaining to a species, special”), from speciēs (“appearance, form, kind, sort”).
