basketing:/ˈbɑːskɪtɪŋ/
n.|装篮; 装入篮内; 投篮|The act of putting things into baskets, especially fruits or vegetables during harvesting. Also refers to the sport of basketball.|The farmers were basketing the apples.  农民们正在把苹果装进篮子里。|近义词:gathering, collecting, harvesting|反义词:unloading, emptying|词性:名词
