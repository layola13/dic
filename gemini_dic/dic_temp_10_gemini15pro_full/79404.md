glimpse:/ɡlɪmps/ | v. | 瞥见 | to see something or someone for a very short time | He glimpsed her through the window as he drove past. 他开车经过时从窗户瞥见了她。 | glance, peek, spot | miss, overlook | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Low German glimsen, perhaps of imitative origin, related to glīm "gleam."
