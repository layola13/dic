de-integration:/diːˌɪntɪˈɡreɪʃən/
n.|去整合，分解，拆分|The process of separating previously integrated parts of a system or organization.|The de-integration of the company led to the formation of several smaller, independent businesses. 公司的去整合导致了几个更小、独立的企业的形成。|disintegration, separation, breakup|integration, unification|noun
de-integration:/diːˌɪntɪˈɡreɪʃən/
n.|反向整合，逆向整合|The process of reversing the integration of different parts of a business or system.|The company's de-integration strategy involved selling off its manufacturing division.  公司的反向整合战略包括出售其制造部门。|decentralization, divestiture|centralization, consolidation|noun
