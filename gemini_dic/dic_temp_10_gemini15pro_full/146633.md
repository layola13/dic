overhappy:/ˌoʊvərˈhæpi/| adj.|过于高兴的，欣喜若狂的|Excessively happy; too happy.|She was overhappy about winning the lottery. 她中了彩票欣喜若狂。|近义词：elated, overjoyed, ecstatic|反义词：sad, unhappy, miserable|词性:形容词

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubar "over, above") + "happy" (Middle English happi, from Old Norse happ "luck, chance," related to Old English hæp "fortunate")
