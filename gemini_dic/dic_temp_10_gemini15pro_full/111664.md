lochs: /lɒks/ (UK) /lɑːks/ (US)| n. |湖泊，海湾 (尤指苏格兰的)|A loch is a large area of water surrounded by land, especially in Scotland.|例句：We visited several lochs during our trip to Scotland. 我们在苏格兰旅行期间参观了几个湖泊。

例句：The Loch Ness Monster is said to live in a loch in Scotland.  据说尼斯湖水怪生活在苏格兰的一个湖泊里。


近义词：lakes, inlets, bays|反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 land, continent)|词性: 名词
