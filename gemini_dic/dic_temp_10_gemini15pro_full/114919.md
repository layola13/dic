manageablenesses:/ˌmænɪdʒəˈbɪlnəsɪz/
|n.|可管理性；易处理性|The manageableness of the project depends on the team's efficiency.  这个项目的可管理性取决于团队的效率。|English Explanation: The quality or state of being manageable; ease of being managed or handled.|例句：The small size of the class makes it more manageable for the teacher.  小班授课让老师更容易管理。|近义词：controllability, tractability|反义词：unmanageability, intractability|词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From manageable + -ness. Manageable from manage + -able. Manage from French *ménager "to handle, manage a household," from Latin manu agere "to do something by hand," from manus "hand" + agere "to do, drive, lead, act."
