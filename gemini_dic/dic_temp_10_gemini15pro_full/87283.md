hermeneutically:/ˌhɜːrməˈnjuːtɪkli/ | adv. | 诠释地，解释地 | In a way that relates to the interpretation of something, typically a text or work of art. | The poem can be interpreted hermeneutically in several different ways. 这首诗可以从诠释学的角度进行多种不同的解读。 |  例句：The scholar approached the text hermeneutically, seeking to understand the author's intent.  这位学者从诠释学的角度来研究文本，试图理解作者的意图。

例句：The philosopher hermeneutically analyzed the ancient scriptures. 这位哲学家对古代经文进行了诠释学分析。 | 近义词：interpretively, exegetically | 反义词：literally | 词性: 副词
