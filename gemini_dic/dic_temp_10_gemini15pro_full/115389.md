manslaughter:/ˈmænˌslɔːtər/| n. |过失杀人|The unlawful killing of a human being without malice aforethought.|He was convicted of manslaughter. 他被判过失杀人罪。|Synonyms: homicide, killing, murder (in some contexts)|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider:  justifiable homicide, self-defense)|noun
