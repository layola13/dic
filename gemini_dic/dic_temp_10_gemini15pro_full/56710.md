doomscrolling:/ˈduːmˌskroʊlɪŋ/
noun
末日刷屏，不停地浏览负面新闻
The act of spending an excessive amount of screen time devoted to absorbing negative news, typically about current events, on social media.
She found herself doomscrolling for hours, unable to tear herself away from the constant stream of bad news. 她发现自己连续几个小时都在末日刷屏，无法摆脱源源不断的坏消息。
constant scrolling, obsessive news checking, information overload
mindful internet use, digital detox, taking a break from social media
n.
