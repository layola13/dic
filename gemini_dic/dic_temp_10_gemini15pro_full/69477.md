felafels:/fəˈlɑːfəlz/| n.|沙拉三明治（复数）|The plural form of falafel, referring to multiple deep-fried balls or patties made from ground chickpeas or fava beans and spices.|I ordered two felafels to go. 我点了两个沙拉三明治带走。|近义词:falafels (variant spelling), chickpea fritters|反义词:None|词性:名词
