eejit:/ˈiːdʒɪt/
noun
笨蛋，傻瓜
A foolish or inept person.
"Those eejits left the door unlocked." 那些笨蛋没锁门。
synonyms: idiot, fool, moron, imbecile, dolt, nitwit, simpleton
antonyms: genius, intellectual, savant
n.
