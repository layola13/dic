oversmoothly:/ˌoʊvərˈsmuːðli/
| adv. | 过于平滑地，过度平滑地 |  In an excessively smooth manner; too smoothly, often implying a lack of authenticity or genuineness. |  He delivered his lines oversmoothly, making the character seem insincere. 他念台词念得太顺溜了，显得角色不真诚。| 近义词: slickly, glibly | 反义词: roughly, jerkily | 词性: 副词

oversmooth:/ˌoʊvərˈsmuːð/
| adj. | 过于平滑的 | Too smooth, often in a way that seems artificial or insincere. | The oversmooth surface of the sculpture lacked texture and interest.  这座雕塑过于光滑的表面缺乏质感和趣味。| 近义词: slick, glib | 反义词: rough, textured | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "oversmoothly" is derived from the prefix "over-" (meaning "excessively" or "too much") and the adjective "smooth," combined with the adverbial suffix "-ly."  "oversmooth" is simply the adjective form without the adverbial "-ly."
