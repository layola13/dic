ataraxia:/ˌætəˈræksiə/ | n. | 平静，心神安宁 | A state of freedom from emotional disturbance and anxiety. |  He achieved a state of ataraxia through meditation. 他通过冥想达到了心神安宁的状态。

| 近义词：tranquility, serenity, composure, calmness, peace of mind | 反义词：anxiety, worry, distress, agitation, turmoil | 词性: noun

词源: From Greek ἀταραξία (ataraxia), from ἀ- (a-) "without" + τάραξις (taraxis) "disturbance," from ταράσσω (tarassō) "to disturb."
