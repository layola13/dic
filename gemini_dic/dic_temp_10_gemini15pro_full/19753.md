billets:/ˈbɪlɪts/ | n. | 军用票据；入场券；(尤指剧院、音乐会等的)预售票；(短程旅行的)客票；(金属锭)坯 | A piece of semi-finished metal, ready for further processing. Also, a ticket or pass, especially one for travel or a performance. |  Example 1: The factory received a shipment of steel billets. 工厂收到了一批钢坯。

Example 2:  I managed to get two billets for the concert. 我设法弄到了两张音乐会的票。 | Near synonyms: tickets, passes, slabs (for metal), ingots | Antonyms: none (for tickets); finished product (for metal) | noun


billets:/ˈbɪlɪts/ | v. | 为…提供住宿; 安排…住宿 | To provide lodging for, especially soldiers. | Example 1: The troops were billeted in local homes. 部队被安排住在当地居民家中。

Example 2: The hotel was billeted with tourists. 这家旅馆住满了游客。 | Near synonyms: lodge, quarter, accommodate | Antonyms: evict | verb
