frieze:/friːz/
n.
雕带，饰带，横条
A decorative band, usually carved or painted, along a wall, especially near the ceiling.
The Parthenon frieze depicts a procession of people and animals.  帕特农神庙的雕带描绘了一队人马的游行。
band, decoration, ornament
plainness, bareness
noun
