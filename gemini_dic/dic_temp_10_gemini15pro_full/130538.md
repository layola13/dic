nonachievement:/ˌnɒnəˈtʃiːvmənt/ | n. | 未完成；未成就；缺乏成就 |Lack of achievement; failure to achieve something. | His nonachievement in school worried his parents. 他在学校的未成就让他的父母很担心。|  The team's nonachievement this season was a huge disappointment. 这个赛季球队的未取得成就是巨大的失望。 | 近义词:failure, lack of success, underachievement | 反义词: achievement, success, accomplishment | 词性: 名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + achievement (from Old French achever "to finish, complete")
