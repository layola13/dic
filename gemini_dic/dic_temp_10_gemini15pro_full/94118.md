impecuniosities:/ɪmˌpɛkjuːniˈɒsɪtiz/
|n.| 贫困，拮据 |Lack of money; poverty. |His impecuniosities prevented him from pursuing his education further. 他的贫困使他无法继续深造。|Synonyms: poverty, indigence, penury, destitution |Antonyms: wealth, affluence, prosperity |noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *impecuniositas, from Latin impecuniosus (“without money”), from in- (“not”) + pecunia (“money”).
