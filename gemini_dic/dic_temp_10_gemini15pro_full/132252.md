It often refers to memory or learning that is not consciously recalled or expressed, such as skills, habits, and conditioning.|He learned the route nondeclaratively, simply by driving it every day.|Synonym: implicitly, tacitly, unconsciously|Antonym: declaratively, explicitly, consciously|adverb
