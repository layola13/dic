articling:/ˈɑːrtɪkəlɪŋ/
n.
实习期；实习律师的身份
the period of time when a law graduate works for a law firm before being admitted to the bar
She completed her articling at a prestigious law firm in Toronto. 她在多伦多一家著名的律师事务所完成了实习期。

近义词:internship, apprenticeship, training
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "qualified lawyer" or "professional practice")
词性:noun
