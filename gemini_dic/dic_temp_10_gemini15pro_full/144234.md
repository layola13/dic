outslinked:/ˌaʊtsˈlɪŋkt/ | adj. | 超链接的，外部链接的 |  Having more outgoing links than incoming links.  A website is outslinked if it links to many other websites.  | Example: The outslinked nature of the website helped improve its SEO ranking.  这个网站外链多的特性帮助提升了它的SEO排名。

| Example:  The page was heavily outslinked, pointing to resources all over the web.  这个页面有大量的外部链接，指向网络上的各种资源。 | Synonym: outbound linked, externally linked | Antonym: inlinked, internally linked | adjective
