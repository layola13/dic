dustpan:/ˈdʌstˌpæn/| n.|簸箕|A flat container with a handle, used for collecting dust and dirt swept up with a brush.|She swept the broken glass into the dustpan. 她把碎玻璃扫进了簸箕里。|近义词: dust shovel,  scoop|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps broom as it performs the opposite function)|noun

dustpans:/ˈdʌstˌpænz/| n.|簸箕（复数）|Plural form of dustpan, referring to more than one dustpan.|I need to buy new dustpans for both floors of the house. 我需要为房子的两层楼都买新的簸箕。|近义词: dust shovels, scoops|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
