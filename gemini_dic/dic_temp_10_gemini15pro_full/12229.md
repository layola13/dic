asbestos:/æsˈbɛstəs/ /əsˈbɛstəs/
noun
石棉
a fibrous silicate mineral with fireproof properties, formerly used as a building material, but now known to cause cancer on inhalation.
例句：Exposure to asbestos can cause mesothelioma.  接触石棉会导致间皮瘤。

例句：The old building was full of asbestos insulation.  这栋老建筑里充满了石棉隔热材料。

近义词：amphibole, chrysotile
反义词：(No direct antonym, as it's a specific material)
词性:n.
