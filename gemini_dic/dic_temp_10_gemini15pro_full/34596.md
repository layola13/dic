cleating: /ˈkliːtɪŋ/
n.
装夹板;  防滑钉;  楔形物;  夹板固定; (用夹板)固定;
The act of attaching cleats to something, or the cleats themselves.  It can also refer to the action of securing something with cleats.
The workers were busy cleating the new deck.  工人们正忙着给新甲板装夹板。
The cleating on the shoes provided excellent traction on the muddy field.  鞋子上的防滑钉在泥泞的场地提供了极好的抓地力。
Synonyms: fastening, securing, anchoring
Antonyms: loosening, detaching, unfastening
noun


cleat: /kliːt/
n.
夹板;  防滑钉;  楔形物;
A piece of wood or metal fastened to something to strengthen it or to provide a grip.
He tripped over a loose cleat on the dock.  他在码头上被一块松动的夹板绊倒了。
The climbing boots had sharp cleats for gripping the ice.  登山靴上有锋利的防滑钉，可以抓住冰面。
Synonyms:  grip, spike, stud
Antonyms: smooth surface
noun

cleat: /kliːt/
vt.
(用夹板)固定;
To fasten or secure with a cleat.
They cleated the ropes to the deck.  他们用夹板把绳子固定在甲板上。
The carpenter cleated the boards together.  木匠用夹板把木板固定在一起。
Synonyms: fasten, secure, anchor
Antonyms: loosen, detach, unfasten
verb
