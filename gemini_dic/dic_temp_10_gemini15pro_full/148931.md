overwithholding:/ˌoʊvərwɪθˈhoʊldɪŋ/| n.|预扣过多，多扣税款|The act of deducting too much tax from an employee's paycheck.  It results in a larger tax refund but less money in each paycheck.| Example:  Overwithholding can tie up your money unnecessarily throughout the year.  预扣过多会导致你的钱在一年中不必要地被占用。

Example: He realized he had significant overwithholding and adjusted his W-4. 他意识到他的预扣税款过多，并调整了他的W-4表格。|近义词：excess withholding, overpayment|反义词: underwithholding, underpayment|词性:noun
