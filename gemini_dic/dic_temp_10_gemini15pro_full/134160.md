nonimputable:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpjuːtəbl/
| adj. | 不可归咎的，不可归罪的 | Not attributable to someone or something; not blameworthy. |  The accident was deemed nonimputable to the driver, as the brakes failed unexpectedly.  这起事故被认为不能归咎于司机，因为刹车意外失灵了。|Synonyms: unblameable, unexceptionable, irreproachable, inculpable | Antonyms: blameworthy, culpable, guilty, reproachable | adjective
