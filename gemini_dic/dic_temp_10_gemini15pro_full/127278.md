navelwort:/ˈneɪvəlˌwɜːrt/| n. |脐景天，脐草；破壁草|A succulent plant of the genus *Umbilicus*, especially *Umbilicus rupestris*, with rounded, fleshy leaves and a spike of greenish flowers.  It is traditionally used in herbal medicine.|The navelwort is a common sight on old walls.|近义词：pennywort, wall pennywort|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Middle English *navelwort*, from Old English *nafela* (navel) + *wyrt* (wort, plant).  The name refers to the shape of the leaves, which resemble a navel.
