manila:/məˈnɪlə/ | n. | 马尼拉麻, 马尼拉纸, 马尼拉雪茄 | A fiber obtained from the leaves of a Philippine plant (Musa textilis), used for rope, paper, etc. Also, paper or cardboard made from this fiber. A type of cigar originally made in Manila. |  The sailors used strong manila rope to secure the ship. 水手们用结实的马尼拉麻绳固定船只。 | synonyms: abaca, hemp | antonyms: N/A | noun

manila:/məˈnɪlə/ | adj. | 马尼拉制的，马尼拉产的 | Made from or originating in Manila. | These manila envelopes are quite sturdy.  这些马尼拉纸信封很结实。 | synonyms: Philippine | antonyms: N/A | adjective
