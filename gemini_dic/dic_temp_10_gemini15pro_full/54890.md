dispondee:/dɪˈspɒndiː/ /dɪˈspɑːndiː/
noun
长短短格，双长音步
A metrical foot consisting of two long syllables or stresses.
The poet used several spondees to create a sense of gravity and weight in the line.  诗人使用了几个长短短格来营造诗句的庄重感。
Spondees are less common in English than in classical Greek or Latin poetry.  长短短格在英语中不如在古典希腊语或拉丁语诗歌中常见。
近义词:molossus (though not a perfect synonym, it refers to three long syllables)
反义词:pyrrhic (two short syllables)
