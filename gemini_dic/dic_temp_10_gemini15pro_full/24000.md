breathingly:/ˈbreθɪŋli/| adv.| 气喘吁吁地，屏息地|In a manner suggestive of catching one's breath, often due to excitement, awe, or fear.  表示因兴奋、敬畏或恐惧而屏住呼吸的方式。|She watched breathingly as the tightrope walker crossed the chasm. 她屏息凝神地看着走钢丝的人走过深渊。|近义词：breathlessly, excitedly, eagerly|反义词: calmly, indifferently|词性:副词
