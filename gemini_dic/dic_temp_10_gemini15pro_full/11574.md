aristotype:/ˈærɪstəˌtaɪp/
n.
原型；原始型；标准型
The original type or form of a species from which others are derived; a prototype.
The museum displayed an aristotype of the earliest known printing press.  博物馆展出了已知最早印刷机的原型。
synonyms: prototype, archetype, original, model
antonyms: variant, derivative, copy
noun
