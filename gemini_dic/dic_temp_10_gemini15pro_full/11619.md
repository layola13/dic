armado:/ɑːrˈmɑːdoʊ/ (US), /ɑːˈmɑːdəʊ/ (UK)| adj. | 武装的；配备的 | Equipped with weapons; armored. | The soldiers were heavily armado. 士兵们全副武装。|  The ship was armado with powerful cannons. 这艘船配备了强大的火炮。 | 近义词: armed, equipped, fortified | 反义词: unarmed, defenseless | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Spanish armado, past participle of armar "to arm," from Latin armare "to arm," from arma "arms, weapons."
