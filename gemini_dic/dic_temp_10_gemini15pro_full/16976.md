bastardizes:/ˈbæstərdaɪzɪz/
verb
使…不正宗；窜改；贬低；降低…的价值
corrupts, debased, or makes impure, especially by mixing with something inferior; to adapt (something) in a simplified or distorted form.
The movie bastardizes the classic novel. 这部电影歪曲了这部经典小说。
synonyms:debase, corrupt, degrade, contaminate, adulterate, alloy, dilute
antonyms:purify, refine, enhance, improve
