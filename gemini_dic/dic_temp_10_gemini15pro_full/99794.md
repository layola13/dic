intermeshes:/ˌɪntərˈmɛʃɪz/
verb
互相啮合，紧密交织
become or make something become entangled or intertwined together.
The gears intermesh smoothly. 齿轮平稳啮合。
interlocks, intertwines, interweaves
disengages, separates
