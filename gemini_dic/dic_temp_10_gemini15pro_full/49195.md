delay:/dɪˈleɪ/| n. v.| 延迟，延误，延期|a period of time by which something is late or postponed|The flight was delayed due to bad weather. 由于天气恶劣，航班延误了。|近义词：postponement, deferral, procrastination|反义词:advancement, expedition|n.
delay:/dɪˈleɪ/| n. v.| 延迟，延误，延期|to make something late or slow|Heavy traffic delayed my arrival. 交通拥堵耽误了我的到达。|近义词：postpone, defer, procrastinate|反义词:advance, expedite|v.
