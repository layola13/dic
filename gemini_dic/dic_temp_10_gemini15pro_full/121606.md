misorganization:/ˌmɪsɔːrɡənəˈzeɪʃn/
n.
混乱；组织不善
the condition of being badly organized
The misorganization of the event led to chaos. 活动的组织混乱导致一片混乱。

The office was in a state of complete misorganization. 办公室完全处于混乱状态。

近义词:disorganization, disorder, mess, chaos
反义词:organization, order, tidiness
