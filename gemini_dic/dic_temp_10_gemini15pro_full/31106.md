champlevé:/ʃɑːmˈləˈveɪ/ /ʃæ̃ləˈveɪ/
n.
嵌丝珐琅; 掐丝珐琅
Enamelling in which hollows are carved out of a metal surface and filled with colored enamel.
The artist created a beautiful champlevé brooch with intricate designs.  这位艺术家创作了一枚带有复杂设计的美丽的掐丝珐琅胸针。
The museum's collection includes several exquisite examples of champlevé from the 12th century.  博物馆的藏品中包括几件12世纪的精美掐丝珐琅作品。
近义词:cloisonné 景泰蓝
