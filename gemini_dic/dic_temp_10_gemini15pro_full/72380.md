flubbers:/ˈflʌbərz/ | n. | 笨拙的错误，失误；胡乱说的话 | An act of flubbing; a blunder; a mistake, especially in speaking. |  He made a series of flubbers during his presentation, completely losing his train of thought. 他在演讲中犯了一系列错误，完全失去了思路。| synonyms: blunders, mistakes, goofs, errors, slips, mishaps | antonyms: successes, achievements, triumphs | noun

flub:/flʌb/ | v. | 搞砸，弄糟；支吾其词；胡乱说 | To perform or execute poorly; to bungle; to mishandle.  To speak indistinctly or haltingly; to misspeak. | I completely flubbed the interview. 我完全搞砸了面试。 | synonyms: bungle, botch, mess up, screw up, muff | antonyms: succeed, achieve, triumph, excel | verb
