bilaterals: /baɪˈlætərəlz/ | n. | 双边关系，双边协议 |  Agreements or discussions between two parties, typically countries. |  The two countries signed several bilaterals during the summit.  两国在峰会期间签署了几项双边协议。 | synonyms: agreements, treaties, pacts | antonyms: multilateral, unilateral | noun

bilateral: /baɪˈlætərəl/ | adj. | 双边的，双方的 | Involving two parties, typically countries. | The bilateral trade agreement boosted the economies of both nations. 双边贸易协定促进了两国经济的发展。 | synonyms: two-sided, mutual, reciprocal | antonyms: unilateral, multilateral | adjective
