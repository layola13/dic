andaran:/ˌɑːnˈdɑːrən/ | n. | 安达兰人（居住在西班牙安达卢西亚地区的人） | A person from Andalusia, a region of Spain. |  Example: The andaran was known for his flamenco dancing.  这位安达兰人以他的弗拉门戈舞而闻名。| Example:  Many andarans are proud of their Moorish heritage. 许多安达兰人以他们的摩尔人血统为荣。 | Synonym: Andalusian | Antonym: N/A | Noun


Etymology: From Spanish *Andaluz*, meaning "Andalusian".  Ultimately from Arabic *al-Andalus*, the historical Arabic name for the Iberian Peninsula.
