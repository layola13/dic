caldera:/kælˈdɛrə/ /kɔːlˈdɪərə/ | n. | 火山口，破火山口 | A large, basin-shaped volcanic depression, typically formed by the collapse of a volcano's summit after a massive eruption. |  Example 1: The Yellowstone caldera is a massive supervolcano. 黄石火山口是一个巨大的超级火山。

Example 2:  The hikers marveled at the vastness of the caldera.  徒步旅行者对火山口的广阔感到惊叹。 | synonym:  crater, volcanic crater | antonym:  volcanic cone, peak | noun
