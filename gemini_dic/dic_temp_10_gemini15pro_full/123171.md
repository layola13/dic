monkishnesses:/ˈmʌŋkɪʃnəsɪz/
noun
僧侣习性；修道士作风；禁欲主义
The quality or state of being monkish; excessive devotion to religious observances or ascetic practices.
His monkishnesses included fasting and long hours of prayer. 他的僧侣习性包括禁食和长时间祈祷。
Near synonyms: asceticism, austerity, religiosity
Near antonyms: worldliness, hedonism
词性: 名词 (plural noun)
