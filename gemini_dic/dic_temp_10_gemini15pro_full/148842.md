overward:/ˈoʊvərwərd/| adj. adv.|向外的，在外部的；向外地，在外边地|Situated on the outside; directed or moving outward.|The overward voyage was smooth and uneventful. 向外的航行顺利而平静。|近义词：outward, external, outer|反义词: inward, internal, inner|词性:形容词/副词
Etymology: From Old English *oferweard, from ofer (over) + -weard (toward).
