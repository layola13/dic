hdr:/ˈeɪtʃˌdiːˈɑːr/ |abbr.|高动态范围（High Dynamic Range）|High Dynamic Range, a technique used in imaging and photography to reproduce a greater dynamic range of luminosity than is possible with standard digital imaging or photographic techniques.  It allows for a wider range of light and dark tones in an image, resulting in more realistic and detailed visuals.|This camera supports HDR photography. 这款相机支持HDR摄影。|近义词:wide dynamic range|反义词:standard dynamic range|n.
