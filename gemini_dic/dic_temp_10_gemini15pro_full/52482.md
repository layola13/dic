dietic:/daɪˈɛtɪk/
adjective
饮食的；与饮食相关的

The dietic advice she received helped her manage her diabetes. 她接受的饮食建议帮助她控制了糖尿病。

He followed a strict dietic plan to lose weight. 他遵循严格的饮食计划来减肥。
