enervating:/ˈenərveɪtɪŋ/
adjective
使人衰弱的；使人丧失活力的
causing one to feel drained of energy or vitality.
The enervating humidity made it difficult to concentrate.  闷热潮湿的天气让人难以集中精力。
近义词:weakening, debilitating, exhausting, draining
反义词:invigorating, energizing, strengthening
词性:adjective

enervate:/ˈenərveɪt/
verb
使衰弱；使丧失活力
to weaken or drain of energy.
The long illness had enervated him.  长期的疾病使他身体虚弱。
近义词:weaken, debilitate, exhaust, drain
反义词:invigorate, energize, strengthen
词性:verb
