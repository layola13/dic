alignments:/əˈlaɪnmənts/| n.|对准，校准；联盟，联合；路线，方位| the process of adjusting something so that it is in the correct position or matches something else, or the result of this; an agreement between countries, political parties, or people to work together because they have similar aims; the position or direction in which something is placed|例句：The mechanic checked the wheel alignments. 机械师检查了车轮定位。|近义词：arrangement, positioning, association, alliance, partnership|反义词:misalignment, discord, disagreement|词性:noun

alignments:/əˈlaɪnmənts/| n.|（复数）政治立场| someone's political positions or opinions|例句：The candidate's alignments were clear from his voting record.  候选人的政治立场从他的投票记录中可以清楚地看出。|近义词：views, beliefs, positions, stances|反义词: - |词性:noun
