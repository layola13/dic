braises:/ˈbreɪzɪz/
v.
炖; 焖; 红烧
cooks (food) by browning it in fat and then simmering it in a closed pot with a small amount of liquid.
The chef braises the short ribs for hours until they are melt-in-your-mouth tender.  厨师将牛小排炖了几个小时，直到它们入口即化。

braises:/ˈbreɪzɪz/
n.
(braise的第三人称单数形式)
Third-person singular simple present indicative form of braise.
He braises the beef with red wine and herbs. 他用红酒和香草炖牛肉。

braise:/breɪz/
v.
炖; 焖; 红烧
To cook (meat or vegetables) by browning in fat and then simmering slowly in a closed container with a small amount of liquid.
She braised the pork shoulder in apple cider. 她用苹果酒焖猪肩肉。

braise:/breɪz/
n.
炖; 焖; 红烧
The act or process of braising.
The pot roast needs a long, slow braise. 这锅烤肉需要长时间的慢炖。


词源: From Old French *braiser, from braise (“embers, burning charcoal”), from Frankish *brasa.

近义词: stew, simmer, pot-roast
反义词: fry, roast, grill
词性: verb (v.), noun (n.)
