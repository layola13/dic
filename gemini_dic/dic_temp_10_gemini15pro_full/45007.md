crystallurgy:/ˌkrɪstəˈlɜːrdʒi/
n.
结晶学，晶体生长学
The science of the formation, structure, and properties of crystals.
Crystallurgy plays a vital role in material science. 结晶学在材料科学中起着至关重要的作用。
The advancements in crystallurgy have led to the development of new materials with improved properties. 结晶学的进步导致了具有改进性能的新材料的开发。
近义词: crystallography, crystallogenesis
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider amorphous state studies)
词性: noun
