delighting:/dɪˈlaɪtɪŋ/
adjective
令人愉悦的；使人快乐的
Giving great pleasure or delight.
例句：
She found delighting in the simple things in life.
她发现自己能从生活中简单的事情中获得快乐。

例句：
The children were delighting in their new toys.
孩子们非常喜欢他们的新玩具。

近义词：pleasing, enjoyable, charming, delightful
反义词：displeasing, unpleasant, boring, tedious
词性: adjective (形容词)

词源：
From delight (v.) + -ing.  Delight itself comes from the Old French *deleitier, from de- "completely" + leitier "to gladden," from Latin laetus "glad."
