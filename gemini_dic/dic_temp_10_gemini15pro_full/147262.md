overmerry:/ˌoʊvərˈmɛri/| adj.|过于欢乐的，过分高兴的|Excessively merry; too jovial.  Originating from "over" (excessive) + "merry" (happy).|He became overmerry after a few drinks and started singing loudly. 他几杯酒下肚就变得过于兴奋，开始大声唱歌。|例句：The overmerry crowd became boisterous and difficult to manage. 过于欢乐的人群变得喧闹，难以管理。|近义词：exuberant, boisterous, jovial, elated|反义词:somber, gloomy, morose|adjective
