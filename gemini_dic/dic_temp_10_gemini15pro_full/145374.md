overcapable:/ˌoʊvərˈkeɪpəbəl/
adjective
能力过剩的，过于能干的
having more than enough ability for a particular task or situation.
She was overcapable for the job, and quickly became bored. 她对于这份工作来说能力过剩，很快就感到厌倦了。
The team was overcapable, leading to some internal conflicts as members competed for more challenging tasks.  这个团队能力过剩，导致一些内部冲突，因为成员们都在竞争更有挑战性的任务。
近义词:overqualified, overcompetent
反义词:incapable, incompetent
词性:adjective

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "capable" (adjective meaning "having the ability, fitness, or quality necessary to do or achieve a specified thing")
