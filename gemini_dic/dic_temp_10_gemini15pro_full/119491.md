micropantograph: /ˈmaɪkroʊˈpæntoʊɡræf/ | n. | 微缩雕刻机，微型缩放仪 | A device used for engraving or copying drawings, designs, etc., on a reduced scale.  It works on the principle of a pantograph, but is much smaller and more precise. | The watchmaker used a micropantograph to engrave the intricate design on the watch face.  钟表匠用微缩雕刻机在表盘上雕刻复杂的图案。| synonym:  reducing machine,  engraving tool | antonym: enlarging machine | noun
