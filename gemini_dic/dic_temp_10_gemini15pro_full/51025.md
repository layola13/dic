deskwork: /ˈdɛskwɜːrk/ (US), /ˈdɛskwɜːk/ (UK)
noun
案头工作；文书工作
Work that is done at a desk, such as writing, paperwork, or using a computer.
I have a lot of deskwork to catch up on this week.  这周我有很多案头工作要补上。
She prefers fieldwork to deskwork. 她喜欢外勤工作胜过案头工作。
近义词: paperwork, office work, administrative work
反义词: fieldwork, manual labor
词性: noun
