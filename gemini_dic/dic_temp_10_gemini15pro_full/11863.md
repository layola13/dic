arrivals:/əˈraɪvəlz/
noun
到达；到达的人（或物）
the act of arriving somewhere, or the people or things that have arrived
例句：The arrivals lounge was crowded. 到达大厅里挤满了人。
例句：We waited for the arrivals from the delayed flight. 我们等待着延误航班的旅客到达。
近义词：arriving, advent, coming
反义词：departures, leaving
