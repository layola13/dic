ecclesiastes:/ɪˌkliːziˈæstiːz/ | n. |传道书; 召集人| A book of the Bible that discusses the meaning of life.  A preacher or convener of an assembly. | The book of Ecclesiastes explores themes of vanity and the pursuit of meaning.  He served as the ecclesiastes for the local church, bringing people together for worship. | synonym: preacher, teacher, convener | antonym: follower, student, disperser | noun
