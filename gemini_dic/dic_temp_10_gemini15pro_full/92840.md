icosandrous:/ˌaɪkəˈsændrəs/
adj.
二十雄蕊的；具有二十个或更多雄蕊附着于花萼的
Having twenty or more stamens attached to the calyx.
The flower is icosandrous, with numerous stamens arising from the calyx tube.  这朵花是二十雄蕊的，有许多雄蕊从花萼筒中长出。
Near Antonyms: polyandrous, oligandrous (though these don't specifically refer to 20)
Etymology:  From Greek *eikosi* (twenty) + *andros* (male, stamen).
