heartstricken:/ˈhɑːrtˌstrɪkən/ | adj. | 悲痛欲绝的，极度伤心的 | Overwhelmed by grief or sorrow.  Deeply affected by emotional distress, especially due to loss or tragedy. | She was heartstricken when she learned of her brother's death. 她得知哥哥去世的消息后悲痛欲绝。| heartbroken, devastated, grief-stricken, inconsolable | cheerful, happy | adjective
