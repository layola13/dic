amyotrophic:/ˌeɪmaɪəˈtrɒfɪk/ (US) /ˌeɪmaɪəˈtrɒfɪk/ (UK)| adj.|肌萎缩的|Relating to or denoting amyotrophy, the wasting away of muscles, typically due to nerve damage.  It describes a condition where muscles progressively weaken and waste away due to the degeneration of motor neurons.|Amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS) is a progressive neurodegenerative disease. 肌萎缩性侧索硬化症 (ALS) 是一种进行性神经退行性疾病。|Synonym:  neurodegenerative, atrophic|Antonym:  healthy, robust|Word Type: Adjective
