here-or-there:/ˌhɪər ɔːr ˈðɛər/
| adv.| 无所谓; 不论这里还是那里; 无论如何 |  Used to indicate that something is unimportant or makes no difference which of two or more alternatives is chosen. |  "He'll get a job here or there" 他会在这里或那里找到一份工作 |  anywhere, in any case,  | specific location | adverb

here-or-there:/ˌhɪər ɔːr ˈðɛər/
| adj.| 不重要的; 无关紧要的 | Not important or relevant. | "Whether they win or lose is here or there" 他们输赢都无所谓 | unimportant, irrelevant, insignificant | important, relevant, significant | adjective
