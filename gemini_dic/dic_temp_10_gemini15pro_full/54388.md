disillusionize:/ˌdɪsɪˈluːʒənaɪz/
v.
使幻想破灭，使不再抱有幻想
To cause someone to stop believing in something that is not true or not as good as they thought it was.
The harsh realities of life soon disillusionized her.  生活的残酷现实很快使她不再抱有幻想。
The experience disillusionized him about politics. 这次经历使他对政治不再抱有幻想。
disenchant, undeceive
enchant, delude
