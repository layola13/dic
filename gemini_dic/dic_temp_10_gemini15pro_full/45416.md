cuppings:/ˈkʌpɪŋz/
n.
拔罐; 杯测
the practice of placing heated glass cups on the skin to create suction. Also, the practice of tasting and evaluating coffee or tea.
例句：Cupping is a traditional Chinese medicine therapy. 拔罐是一种传统的中医疗法。

例句：The coffee cuppings were held to select the best beans.  咖啡杯测是为了挑选最好的咖啡豆。
近义词：cupping therapy (for medical use), tasting (for beverages)
反义词: None
词性: Noun
