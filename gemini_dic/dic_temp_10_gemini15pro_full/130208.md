non-selection:/ˌnɒn səˈlɛkʃ(ə)n/ | n. | 未被选中；未被选择；不选；淘汰 | The non-selection of his proposal was a huge disappointment.  他的提案未被选中是一个巨大的失望。|  His non-selection for the team came as a shock. 他落选了球队，这让他很震惊。 | 近义词: rejection, exclusion, omission | 反义词: selection, choice, inclusion | 词性: 名词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + selection (from Latin *selectio, from selectus, past participle of seligere "to choose")
