empyreuma:/ˌɛmpaɪˈruːmə/ | n. |焦糊味，烧焦味|The characteristic smell of burning or charring organic matter, especially animal or vegetable matter.|例句：The empyreuma of burnt toast filled the kitchen. 烤焦面包的焦糊味弥漫了整个厨房。

例句：The steak had a distinct empyreuma, suggesting it had been overcooked. 牛排有一股明显的焦糊味，表明它煮过头了。|近义词：burnt smell, char, scorch |反义词: fragrance, aroma |词性:noun
