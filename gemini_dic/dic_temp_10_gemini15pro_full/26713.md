cadet:/kəˈdɛt/| n.|学员，军校学生|A young person who is training to become an officer in the armed forces or police.|例句：The cadet was eager to begin his training at the military academy.  这位学员渴望在军事学院开始他的训练。

例句：The police cadet spent the day observing experienced officers.  这位警校学员花了一天时间观察经验丰富的警官。|近义词：trainee, student, apprentice|反义词:veteran, graduate|Noun
