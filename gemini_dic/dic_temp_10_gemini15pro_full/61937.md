emulous: /ˈɛmjʊləs/ | adj. | 渴望竞争的；好胜的；有竞争心的 | Seeking to emulate someone or something; motivated by a desire to equal or surpass another. |  She was emulous of her sister's success. 她嫉妒她姐姐的成功。 |  The young writer was emulous of Hemingway's terse prose. 这位年轻作家渴望模仿海明威简洁的散文风格。 | 近义词: competitive, ambitious, aspiring, envious | 反义词: complacent, indifferent, unambitious | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin aemulus "rivaling," from aemulari "to rival," of unknown origin.
