full-size:/ˈfʊlˈsaɪz/ | adj. | 全尺寸的，标准尺寸的 | Of the usual or standard size.  |  例句：She bought a full-size refrigerator for her new apartment. 她为她的新公寓买了一个全尺寸的冰箱。 | 近义词：standard-size, regular, normal | 反义词：compact, miniature, small, downsized | 词性: 形容词

full-size:/ˈfʊlˈsaɪz/ | n. | 标准尺寸 | The usual or standard size. | 例句：The truck is available in a full-size only. 这辆卡车只有标准尺寸。 | 近义词：standard size, regular size | 反义词：compact size, miniature size | 词性: 名词


词源: "full" (OE full) + "size" (from Old French sise, ultimately from Latin sedere "to sit")
