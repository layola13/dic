nowadays:/ˈnaʊədeɪz/| adv.|如今，现在|at the present time; in these times|例句：Nowadays, people are more health-conscious. 如今，人们更加注重健康。|例句：Nowadays, it's common to see people working from home. 现在，在家工作很常见。|近义词：currently, presently, these days|反义词：formerly, previously|词性:副词

Etymology->
From "now" + "a days," with "a" being a reduced form of "on."  "Now" comes from Old English "nu,"  meaning "now." "Days" comes from Old English "dagas," the plural of "dæg," meaning "day." The phrase evolved to its current form, "nowadays."
