flattish:/ˈflætɪʃ/| adj.|略平的，有点平的|Relatively flat; somewhat flat, but not completely so.|The table was a bit flattish, making it difficult to write on.  桌子有点平，使得在上面写字很困难。|The ground is flattish in this area.  这一带的地面比较平坦。|近义词:level, even, almost flat|反义词:sloping, hilly, bumpy|词性:形容词
词源:From flat + -ish (suffix indicating a moderate degree of a quality). 由flat加上后缀-ish（表示某种性质程度适中）构成。
