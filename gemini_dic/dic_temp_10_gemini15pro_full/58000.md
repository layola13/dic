driverless:/ˈdraɪvərlɪs/
adjective
无人驾驶的
Without a driver; operating without a human driver.
例句：Driverless cars are becoming increasingly common on our roads. 无人驾驶汽车在我们的道路上越来越常见。

例句：The company is developing driverless technology for trucks. 该公司正在开发卡车的无人驾驶技术。

近义词：autonomous, self-driving, robotic
反义词：manned, driven
词性：adjective
