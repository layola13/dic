nonlubricant:/ˌnɒnˈluːbrɪkənt/ | adj. | 非润滑的；非润滑剂的 | Not lubricating; not serving as a lubricant. | The nonlubricant coating on the pan prevents sticking.  锅上的非润滑涂层可以防止粘连。| Near Synonyms:  non-lubricating, dry, friction-increasing | Antonyms: lubricant, lubricating, oily, greasy | adjective

nonlubricant:/ˌnɒnˈluːbrɪkənt/ | n. | 非润滑剂 | A substance that does not lubricate. |  They used a nonlubricant sealant to prevent leaks. 他们使用了一种非润滑密封剂来防止泄漏。 | Near Synonyms: desiccant, drying agent | Antonyms: lubricant, oil, grease | noun
