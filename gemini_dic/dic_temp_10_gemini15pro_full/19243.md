bhaji:/ˈbɑːdʒiː/ (UK), /ˈbʌdʒiː/ (US)| n. |油炸蔬菜丸子；蔬菜咖喱|A spicy Indian snack or side dish made of vegetables, often onions, coated in chickpea flour batter and deep-fried.|例句：I ordered some bhajis as a starter. 我点了一些油炸蔬菜丸子作为开胃菜。

例句：She made delicious onion bhajis for the party. 她为聚会做了美味的洋葱油炸丸子。|近义词：pakora, fritter|反义词: None|词性: Noun

bhajis:/ˈbɑːdʒiːz/ (UK), /ˈbʌdʒiːz/ (US)| n. |油炸蔬菜丸子；蔬菜咖喱（bhaji的复数形式）|Plural form of bhaji, referring to multiple spicy Indian snacks or side dishes.|例句：The bhajis were crispy and flavorful. 这些油炸蔬菜丸子酥脆可口。

例句：We enjoyed a variety of bhajis with our meal. 我们在用餐时享用了各种各样的油炸蔬菜丸子。|近义词：pakoras, fritters|反义词: None|词性: Noun
