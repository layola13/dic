例句：He has always been my cornerman, supporting me through thick and thin. 他一直是我的支持者，无论顺境逆境都支持着我。|近义词：assistant, supporter, second, confidant|反义词: opponent, adversary|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "corner" (referring to the corner of the boxing ring) + "man."
