heckles:/ˈhɛkəlz/| n.|奚落，嘲笑，起哄|Sharp, interrupting shouts or jeers, typically from a hostile audience.|The comedian faced a barrage of heckles from the unruly crowd.  喜剧演员面对着不守规矩的观众一连串的奚落。|Synonyms: jeers, taunts, gibes|Antonyms: cheers, applause|Noun

heckle:/ˈhɛkəl/| v.|奚落，嘲笑，起哄|To interrupt (a public speaker) with derisive or aggressive comments or abuse.|The audience member heckled the politician throughout his speech.  那位观众在政治家演讲的整个过程中不断地奚落他。|Synonyms: jeer, taunt, gibe|Antonyms: cheer, applaud|Verb
