charted:/ˈtʃɑːrtɪd/ | v. | 绘制…的图表；详细计划 | Past participle of "chart," meaning to make a map or plan of something. |  They charted a course for the new company. 他们为新公司制定了发展路线。| Synonyms: map, plan, outline, plot | Antonyms: wander, drift | verb
