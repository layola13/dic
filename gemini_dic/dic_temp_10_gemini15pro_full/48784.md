deflect:/dɪˈflekt/ | v. | 转向，偏斜，使避开| To cause something to change direction by hitting or touching it; to turn aside or prevent (something) from happening or succeeding. | The ball deflected off the wall. 球偏离了墙。| synonyms: divert, deviate, avert | antonyms: attract, focus, maintain | verb

deflection:/dɪˈflekʃən/ | n. | 转向，偏斜| The action of deflecting or the state of being deflected. | The deflection of the missile prevented a disaster. 导弹的转向避免了一场灾难。| synonyms: deviation, diversion, digression | antonyms: attraction, convergence | noun
