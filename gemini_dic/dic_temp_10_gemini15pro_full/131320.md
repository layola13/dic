nonchallengers:/ˌnɒnˈtʃælɪndʒəz/
noun
不挑战者；不反对者；顺从者
Those who do not challenge or oppose something; those who conform.
The nonchallengers accepted the new policy without a word.  不挑战者们一言不发地接受了新政策。
The government's decision was met with silence from the nonchallengers. 政府的决定遭到了不挑战者的沉默对待。
Synonyms: conformists, acquiescers, followers
Antonyms: challengers, dissenters, rebels
