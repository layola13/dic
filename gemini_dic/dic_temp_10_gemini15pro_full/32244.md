chickenshit:/ˈtʃɪkɪnˌʃɪt/| adj. n.|胆小的，懦弱的；胆小鬼|A cowardly or contemptible person.  Displaying a lack of courage.  |He called me chickenshit for not jumping off the cliff with him. 他因为我没有和他一起跳下悬崖就骂我胆小鬼。|近义词：cowardly, timid, craven, pusillanimous|反义词：brave, courageous, bold|词性: 形容词/名词

chickenshit:/ˈtʃɪkɪnˌʃɪt/| n.|懦弱的行为；胆怯|An act of cowardice.  Something considered insignificant or worthless. |He pulled a real chickenshit move by telling the teacher on me. 他向老师告我的状，真是个懦夫行为。|近义词：cowardice, timidity|反义词：bravery, courage|词性: 名词
