omphalos: /ˈɒmfələs/ (UK) /ˈɑːmfəˌlɔːs/ (US) | n. | 中心点，肚脐，世界中心 |  A central point, hub, or navel.  Often used figuratively to refer to a place believed to be the center of the world or a very important place.  Derived from the Greek word "ὀμφαλός" (omphalos), meaning "navel".  In ancient Greece, the omphalos at Delphi was considered the center of the world. |  Delphi was considered the omphalos of the ancient Greek world. 德尔斐被认为是古希腊世界的中心。

The archaeologist discovered an omphalos stone, marking a sacred site. 考古学家发现了一块脐石，标志着一个圣地。| 近义词: center, hub, core, nucleus | 反义词: periphery, edge, margin | 词性: noun
