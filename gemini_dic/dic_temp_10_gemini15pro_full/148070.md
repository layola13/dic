oversaturated:/ˌoʊvərˈsætʃəreɪtɪd/
adjective
过度饱和的；过分饱和的
Excessively saturated; containing more of a substance than can be absorbed or dissolved.  Filled to excess; oversupplied.
The market is oversaturated with cheap clothing.  市场上充斥着廉价服装。
The colors in the photograph were oversaturated, making it look unnatural. 照片中的颜色过度饱和，看起来不自然。
近义词: saturated, glutted, overflowing, inundated
反义词: unsaturated, undersupplied, deficient
