overfaithful: /ˌoʊvərˈfeɪθfʊl/ | adj. | 过分忠诚的，过分虔诚的 | Excessively or overly faithful; loyal to an excessive degree.  This word often implies a negative connotation, suggesting blind loyalty or adherence even when it's unwarranted or detrimental. |  She was overfaithful to her husband, even when he was clearly wrong. 她对她的丈夫过分忠诚，即使他明显错了。|  His overfaithful adherence to the company's outdated policies hindered progress. 他对公司过时政策的过分忠诚阻碍了进步。 |  devoted, loyal, steadfast |  disloyal, unfaithful, treacherous | adjective
