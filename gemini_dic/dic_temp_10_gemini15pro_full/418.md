abjurers:/əbˈdʒʊrərz/
noun
放弃者，发誓放弃者
People who formally renounce or reject a belief, cause, or claim.
The abjurers of violence gathered to protest the war.  放弃暴力的人聚集在一起抗议战争。
Many former cult members became abjurers of its teachings. 许多以前的邪教成员放弃了它的教义。
renouncers, repudiators, forsakers
advocates, supporters, adherents
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English abjuren, from Old French abjurer, from Latin abjurare (“to deny on oath”), from ab- (“away from”) + jurare (“to swear”).
