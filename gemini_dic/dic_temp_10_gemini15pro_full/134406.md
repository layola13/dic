noninvasive:/ˌnɒnɪnˈveɪsɪv/ (US) /ˌnɒnɪnˈveɪsɪv/ (UK)| adj.|非侵入性的|Not involving penetration of the skin or body. |Doctors opted for a noninvasive procedure to diagnose the problem. 医生们选择了一种非侵入性程序来诊断这个问题。|The surgery was thankfully noninvasive.  谢天谢地，手术是非侵入性的。|近义词：nonsurgical, atraumatic|反义词:invasive, surgical|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From non- + invasive.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "invasive" comes from the Latin "invadere," meaning "to go into, enter, invade."
