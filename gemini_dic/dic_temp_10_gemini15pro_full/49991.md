denizen:/ˈdenɪzən/ /ˈdɛnɪzən/| n.|居民，常客，外来物种|an inhabitant or occupant of a particular place; one who frequents a place|例句：The denizens of the forest looked up as the strange bird flew overhead. 森林里的居民抬头看着那只奇怪的鸟从头顶飞过。

例句：He was a denizen of the local bar, known to everyone there. 他是当地酒吧的常客，在那里每个人都认识他。|近义词：inhabitant, resident, occupant, frequenter, regular|反义词:alien, foreigner, stranger|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *denzein* "from within, of the house," from *deinz* "within," from Latin *de intus* "from within."
