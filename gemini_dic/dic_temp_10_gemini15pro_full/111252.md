litigates: /ˈlɪtɪɡeɪts/
verb
提起诉讼；打官司
To engage in a legal dispute; to prosecute or defend a case in court.
The company litigates patent infringements aggressively.  这家公司积极地对专利侵权行为提起诉讼。
She litigates cases involving medical malpractice. 她处理涉及医疗事故的案件。
synonyms: sues, prosecutes, contests, disputes
antonyms: settles, mediates, compromises, arbitrates
词性: 动词 (v.)
