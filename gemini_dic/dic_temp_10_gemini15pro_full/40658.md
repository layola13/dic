converge:/kənˈvɜːrdʒ/ (US) /kənˈvɜːdʒ/ (UK)| v. |汇聚，聚集，集中; (意见或观点)趋于一致|To tend to meet at a point or line; to come together and unite in a common interest or focus.|The two roads converge just north of the city. 这两条路就在城市的北部汇合。|synonyms: meet, join, intersect, merge, unite, coalesce|antonyms: diverge, separate, disperse|verb

converge:/kənˈvɜːrdʒ/ (US) /kənˈvɜːdʒ/ (UK)| n. |(尤指道路的)会合点|The point at which lines, roads, or paths converge.|The converge of the two rivers created a fertile delta. 两条河流的交汇处形成了一个肥沃的三角洲。|synonyms: junction, intersection, confluence, meeting point|antonyms: divergence, separation|noun
