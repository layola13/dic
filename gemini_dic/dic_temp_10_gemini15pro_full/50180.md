deoppilate:/diːˈɒpɪleɪt/
v.
疏通，清除阻塞
To clear of obstruction; to open a passage through anything obstructed.
The doctor deoppilated the patient's blocked artery. 医生疏通了病人阻塞的动脉。
Synonyms: unclog, unblock, clear, open
Antonyms: obstruct, block, clog
