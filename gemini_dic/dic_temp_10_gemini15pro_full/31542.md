charting:/ˈtʃɑːtɪŋ/
n./v./
制图；图表绘制；跟踪；记录；规划
the activity of making charts of areas of the sea or land
例句：The charting of the ocean floor is a complex process.  绘制海底地图是一个复杂的过程。

例句：They are charting their progress on a graph. 他们在图表上记录他们的进展。

近义词：mapping, graphing, plotting, tracking, recording, planning
反义词：Ignoring, neglecting
词性: 动名词/现在分词


charting:/ˈtʃɑːtɪŋ/
n./v./
描绘；记述；详细记录
making a detailed record of something over a period of time
例句：The book charts the rise and fall of the Roman Empire. 这本书记述了罗马帝国的兴衰。

例句：She has been charting her family's history. 她一直在记录她的家族史。

近义词：describing, documenting, chronicling
反义词：Ignoring, neglecting
词性: 动名词/现在分词
