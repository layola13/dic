parapophysis:/pærəˈpɒfɪsɪs/
n.
横突；副突
A bony process projecting laterally or ventrally from the centrum of a vertebra.
The parapophysis of the lumbar vertebrae is well-developed.  腰椎的横突很发达。
Synonyms: transverse process, lateral process
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a skeletal structure)
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek para- (beside, alongside) + apophysis (offshoot, outgrowth).
