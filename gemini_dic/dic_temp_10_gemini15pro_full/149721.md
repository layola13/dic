palaetiologist:/ˌpeɪliːəˈtɒlədʒɪst/ | n. | 古生物学家 | A scientist who studies paleontology, the science of the forms of life existing in former geologic periods, as represented by their fossils. |  Mary Anning was a renowned palaetiologist who made significant discoveries in the Jurassic period. 玛丽·安宁是一位著名的古生物学家，在侏罗纪时期做出了重大发现。

A team of palaetiologists unearthed a complete dinosaur skeleton. 一组古生物学家挖掘出一个完整的恐龙骨架。 |  Synonym: paleontologist | Antonym: N/A | noun
