n.
艾伊尔人（Aiel）
A fictional group of people in Robert Jordan's "The Wheel of Time" fantasy series. They are a nomadic, warrior culture inhabiting a harsh desert land.
The Aiel are fierce warriors, known for their honor and adherence to a strict code.
近义词：clansmen, tribespeople (in a general sense)
反义词: None (specific to the fictional world)
词性: Noun
