dummy:/ˈdʌmi/| n. adj.| 仿制品；笨蛋；傀儡|A figure designed to resemble a human being, typically used for displaying clothes or other items; a stupid person; a figurehead.|She used a dummy to display her latest designs. 她用一个仿制品来展示她的最新设计。| 近义词:mannequin, idiot, fool, puppet |反义词:genius, intelligent person, real person|词性:名词,形容词

dummies:/ˈdʌmiz/| n.| 笨蛋们；仿制品；傀儡们|Plural form of "dummy".|Those dummies fell for the trick. 那些笨蛋上当了。|近义词:idiots, fools, puppets|反义词:geniuses, intelligent people, real people|词性:名词
