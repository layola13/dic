homomallous:/ˌhɒməˈmeɪləs/| adj.|同形的，同型的|Having the same form or structure; isomorphic. Referring to mosses, having the antheridia and archegonia on the same plant.|The homomallous moss species exhibited both male and female reproductive structures on a single plant.|近义词:monoicous, autoicous|反义词:dioicous, heteroicous|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Greek *homo-* (same) + *mallos* (wool, fleece), referring to the similar appearance of the male and female reproductive structures in some mosses.
