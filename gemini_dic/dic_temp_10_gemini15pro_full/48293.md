decree: /dɪˈkriː/ /dɪˈkri/ | n. vt. | 法令，判决；颁布，裁定 | An official order issued by a legal authority. | The court decreed that the company must pay compensation. 法院裁定该公司必须支付赔偿金. |  The king decreed that everyone should pay a new tax. 国王颁布法令，每个人都要缴纳新税. | 近义词：order, edict, ruling, judgment, proclamation | 反义词：appeal, petition | 词性: noun, verb
词源: From Old French *decreer, from Latin decrētum "something decided," neuter past participle of decernere "to decide, determine," from de- "down, completely" + cernere "to separate, distinguish, perceive."
