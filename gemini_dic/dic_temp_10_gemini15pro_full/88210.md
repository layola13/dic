hindbrain:/ˈhaɪndbreɪn/| n. |后脑|The hindbrain is the lower part of the brainstem, comprising the cerebellum, pons, and medulla oblongata.  It controls basic life functions such as breathing and heart rate.|The hindbrain is responsible for vital autonomic functions. 后脑负责重要的自主功能。|The doctor explained that damage to the hindbrain can be life-threatening. 医生解释说，后脑的损伤可能会危及生命。|Synonyms: rhombencephalon|Antonyms: forebrain|noun
