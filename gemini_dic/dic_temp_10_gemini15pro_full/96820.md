inescapableness:/ˌɪnɪˈskeɪpəbəlɪs/
n.|不可避免性; 不可逃避性|The inescapableness of death is a difficult concept to grasp. 死亡的不可避免性是一个难以理解的概念。|The inescapableness of his fate weighed heavily on him.  他命运的不可避免性沉重地压在他身上。|inevitability, unavoidability|escapability, avoidability|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + escapable (able to be escaped) + -ness (noun suffix forming abstract nouns from adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin ex- (out of) + capere (to seize, take).
