effluvial:/ɪˈfluːviəl/| adj.|恶臭的；污浊的；由流出物引起的|Relating to or arising from outflow or effluence, especially noxious or foul-smelling outflow.|The effluvial smell from the sewage plant permeated the neighborhood.  污水处理厂散发出的恶臭弥漫在整个社区。|Synonyms: noisome, fetid, mephitic, foul|Antonyms: fragrant, aromatic, sweet-smelling|Adjective
