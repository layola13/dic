hackest:/ˈhækɪst/| v.| 你非法入侵 (第二人称单数现在时)|This archaic form is rarely used.  It's the second-person singular present indicative form of "hack."  It means "you hack" in a rather old and formal way. | Thou hackest the king's computer! 你非法入侵了国王的电脑！(古英语用法，非常罕见)| 近义词: intrude, breach, crack |反义词: protect, secure |词性: 动词
