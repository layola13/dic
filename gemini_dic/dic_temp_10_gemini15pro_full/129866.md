non-inflammable:/ˌnɒnɪnˈflæməbl/ | adj. |不易燃的，非易燃的 |Not easily set on fire; not flammable.  Although literally meaning "not flammable," non-inflammable is often misinterpreted as "flammable." Therefore, "nonflammable" is generally preferred. |This material is non-inflammable and safe to use near heat sources. 这种材料不易燃，可在热源附近安全使用。|The outer layer of the spacecraft is made of non-inflammable materials. 宇宙飞船的外层由不易燃材料制成。| 近义词:nonflammable, fire-resistant, fireproof | 反义词: flammable, inflammable | 词性:形容词
