monodimetric:/ˌmɒnəʊdaɪˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. |单向二聚体的，单向二量体的 |  Relating to or characterized by monodimorphism; having two crystal forms, one of which is derived from the other by halving the parameters along one of the crystallographic axes. |  Monodimetric crystals are relatively rare, and their formation is not fully understood. 单向二聚体晶体相对罕见，它们的形成尚未完全了解。 |  The compound exhibits monodimetric crystal growth. 该化合物呈现单向二聚体晶体生长。 | Near synonyms:  dimorphous, dimorphic | Antonym:  monomorphic | Adjective
