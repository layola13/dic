nuisance:/ˈnjuːsns/ /ˈnuːsəns/| n.|讨厌的人（或事物）；麻烦事|Something or someone that causes trouble, annoyance, or inconvenience.|The dog next door is a real nuisance, barking all night. 隔壁的狗真是个麻烦，整夜吠叫。|Synonyms: annoyance, bother, pest, inconvenience, problem|Antonyms:  pleasure, delight, joy, comfort|noun

nuisance:/ˈnjuːsns/ /ˈnuːsəns/| n.|损害；妨害行为|An act or omission which causes inconvenience or damage to the public.|Leaving your garbage cans out on the street all week is a public nuisance. 把你的垃圾桶整周放在街上是一种妨害公共利益的行为。|Synonyms:  offence, violation, infringement|Antonyms:  benefit, advantage|noun
