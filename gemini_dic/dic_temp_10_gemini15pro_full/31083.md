champe:/tʃæmp/| n.|咀嚼，嚼碎，咬|The act of chewing or biting something, especially noisily.|The horse champed on its bit.|近义词：chew, munch, gnaw, bite|反义词: swallow|词性:noun

词源: From Old French *champer, frequentative of champoir "to chew," ultimately of imitative origin.
