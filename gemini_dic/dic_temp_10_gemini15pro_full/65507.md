euroscepticism:/ˌjʊərəʊˈskɛptɪsɪzəm/ /ˌʊrəˈskɛptɪsɪzəm/
noun
欧洲怀疑主义
Criticism of or opposition to the European Union and European integration.
The party's euroscepticism has been growing in recent years.  该党的欧洲怀疑主义近年来一直在增长。
Euroscepticism is a significant factor in British politics. 欧洲怀疑主义是英国政治中的一个重要因素。
synonyms: anti-EU sentiment,  EU-phobia
antonyms:  pro-Europeanism,  Euro-enthusiasm
n.
