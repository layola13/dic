cancelled /ˈkænsəld/ (US), /ˈkænsld/ (UK)| adj. v. |取消的，被取消的；撤销的| officially ended or stopped before it has been completed. |The flight was cancelled due to bad weather. 由于天气恶劣，航班被取消了。|synonyms: aborted, abandoned, withdrawn| antonyms:  approved, confirmed, continued|verb

cancelled /ˈkænsəld/ (US), /ˈkænsld/ (UK)| v. |取消；撤销| to decide that an arranged event will not take place. |I had to cancel my appointment because I was sick. 我不得不取消我的预约，因为我病了。|synonyms:  abort, abandon, withdraw| antonyms:  approve, confirm, continue|adjective
