gerundive:/ˈdʒɛrənˌdaɪv/
adjective|动名词的；将来被动分词的|A gerundive is a verbal adjective that indicates necessity or obligation. It is formed from the present stem of a verb plus -ndus, -nda, -ndum (Latin) or -τέος, -τέα, -τέον (Greek).|The agenda included several matters requiring immediate attention.|近义词:obligatory, necessary, required|反义词:optional, unnecessary|词性:adjective
