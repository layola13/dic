coppicing:/ˈkɒpɪsɪŋ/ (US: /ˈkɑːpɪsɪŋ/)
n.
矮林作业; 萌芽更新
The practice of cutting back trees or shrubs to ground level periodically to stimulate growth and produce a crop of small poles or branches.
Coppicing is a traditional method of woodland management. 矮林作业是一种传统的林地管理方法。
The farmer practiced coppicing to produce firewood. 农民采用矮林作业来生产柴火。

近义词:pollarding, cutting back
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps allowing trees to grow naturally)
词性:noun
