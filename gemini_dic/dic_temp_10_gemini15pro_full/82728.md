guider:/ˈɡaɪdər/|n.|指导者，引路人，导游|Someone who shows or tells people the way to a place, especially someone employed to show tourists around interesting places. |例句：The museum hired a guider to lead tours through the new exhibit.  博物馆聘请了一名导游带领游客参观新的展览。|例句：Our tour guider was very knowledgeable about the local history. 我们的导游对当地历史非常了解。|近义词：guide, leader, director, instructor, mentor|反义词：follower|词性：名词
