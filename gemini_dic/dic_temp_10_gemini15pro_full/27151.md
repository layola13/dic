call-it-a-day:/ˈkɔːl ɪt ə ˈdeɪ/
verb phrase
结束一天的工作；到此为止；收工
to stop working on something
例句：After working for ten hours, they decided to call it a day.  工作了十个小时后，他们决定收工。

例句：Let's call it a day and go home. 我们今天就到这里吧，回家吧。

近义词：knock off, pack it in, wrap it up
反义词: start, begin, continue
词性: verb phrase
