arecoline:/əˈriːkəliːn/ | n. |槟榔碱| An alkaloid found in the areca nut (betel nut), with parasympathomimetic properties. It stimulates the central and autonomic nervous systems. | Arecoline is known to increase saliva production. 槟榔碱已知会增加唾液分泌。| Arecoline is used in veterinary medicine. 槟榔碱用于兽医学。 | Synonyms:  Arecaidine methyl ester | Antonyms: None | noun
