conduct:/kənˈdʌkt/ (US) /ˈkɒndʌkt/ (UK)| v.| 行为，举止；管理，引导；传导|  To behave in a particular way, or to organize and/or do a particular activity. |例句：The experiments were conducted by scientists. 这些实验是由科学家进行的。|近义词：manage, direct, lead, guide |反义词:mismanage|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin conductus "escort, guide," from Latin conduct-, past participle stem of conducere "to lead together, bring, collect, conduct," from com- "together" (see com-) + ducere "to lead" (see duke (n.)).
