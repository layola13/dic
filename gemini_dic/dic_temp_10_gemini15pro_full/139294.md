obliviously:/əˈblɪviəsli/ | adv. | 显然地；没注意到地；健忘地 | in a way that shows you are not aware of something | He walked obliviously into the wall. 他毫无察觉地走进了墙里。 | synonym: unmindfully, unknowingly, unconsciously | antonym: mindfully, consciously, knowingly | adverb

obliviously:/əˈblɪviəsli/ | adv. | 显然地；没注意到地；健忘地 |  Clearly, without awareness | She obliviously stepped into a puddle. 她显然地踩进了一个水坑。 | synonym: plainly, evidently, patently | antonym: subtly, discreetly, covertly | adverb
