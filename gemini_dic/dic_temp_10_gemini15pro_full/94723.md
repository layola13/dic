impropriatrix: /ɪmˌproʊpriˈeɪtrɪks/
n.
女业主；女性侵占者
a female impropriator (one who seizes something wrongly, especially church property)
She was denounced as an impropriatrix of the abbey's lands. 她被谴责为侵占修道院土地的人。

近义词: usurper, encroacher, confiscator
反义词: donor, benefactor

词性: noun
