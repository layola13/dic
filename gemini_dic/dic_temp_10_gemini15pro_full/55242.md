dissimilate:/dɪˈsɪməleɪt/
verb
吸收；同化；消化（食物）; (使)变得不同,不像;
To break down (nutrients) into a form that can be absorbed and used by the body.  To cause to appear unlike or dissimilar.
Plants dissimilate organic matter by respiration. 植物通过呼吸作用分解有机物。
The chameleon can dissimilate itself to match its surroundings. 变色龙可以改变自身颜色以匹配周围环境。
近义词: digest, metabolize, absorb, differentiate
反义词: assimilate, homogenize
词性: verb

dissimilation:/dɪˌsɪməˈleɪʃn/
noun
异化作用；分解代谢; 分化,差异化;
The process by which living organisms break down complex organic molecules into simpler substances, often releasing energy. The process of making things appear different or dissimilar.
Dissimilation of fats provides the body with energy. 脂肪的分解代谢为身体提供能量。
The dissimilation of regional dialects can lead to communication difficulties. 地区方言的差异化会导致沟通困难。
近义词: catabolism, breakdown, differentiation
反义词: assimilation, synthesis
词性: noun
