overnursing:/ˈoʊvərˌnɜːrsɪŋ/ | n. |过度护理；护理过度|The act of providing excessive or unnecessary nursing care, often leading to dependence and hindering recovery.  This can manifest as doing things for a patient that they are capable of doing themselves.| The elderly woman suffered from overnursing, her children doing everything for her even though she was still quite capable.  过度照顾这位老妇人，即使她仍然很能干，她的孩子们也为她做了一切。 |近义词: overcaring, overprotection |反义词: neglect, undercaring |词性:名词

overnurse:/ˌoʊvərˈnɜːrs/ | v. |过度护理；护理过度|To provide excessive or unnecessary nursing care, often leading to dependence and hindering recovery. | He overnursed his wife after her surgery, preventing her from regaining her strength as quickly as she could have. 他在妻子手术后过度护理她，阻止她尽快恢复体力。 |近义词: overcare, overprotect |反义词: neglect, undercare |词性:动词
