hipps:/hɪps/
n.
臀部，屁股
The part of the body that curves outward below the waist and above the thighs.
She swayed her hips as she walked. 她走路时扭动着臀部。

近义词:buttocks, rear, bottom
反义词: (没有直接的反义词)
