de-litters:/diːˈlɪtərz/
verb
清除垃圾
Removes litter from (a place).
The volunteers de-littered the park after the concert.  志愿者们在音乐会后清理了公园的垃圾。
The city council has launched a campaign to de-litter the streets. 市议会发起了一项清除街道垃圾的运动。
clean up, tidy up
litter, pollute
