clang:/klæŋ/| n. vi.|铿锵声，叮当声|A loud, resonant, metallic sound, like that of a large bell. |The clang of the blacksmith's hammer echoed through the valley. 铁匠的锤子铿锵声回荡在山谷中。|近义词：ring, chime, jangle|反义词:silence, hush|noun
clang:/klæŋ/| vi.|发出铿锵声|To make a loud, resonant, metallic sound. |The trolley clang as it went down the street.  电车发出铿锵声沿着街道驶去。|近义词：ring, chime, jangle|反义词:silence, hush|verb
