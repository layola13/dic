mackle:/ˈmækl/| n.| 模糊的印痕，重影，双晶|a blurred impression; a double impression, as in printing; a twin crystal|The old book had a mackle on several pages, making the text difficult to read.|近义词：blur, smudge, double, twin|反义词:clear, distinct|名词

mackle:/ˈmækl/| v.| 模糊地印刷，形成重影|to blur or double, as in printing; to produce a twin crystal|The printer mackled the image, ruining the photograph.|近义词：blur, smudge, double|反义词:clarify, sharpen|动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *macle* (unattested), from Old French *mascle* "spot, mesh of a net," from Latin *macula* "spot, stain, mesh of a net."  The sense of "twin crystal" arose from the resemblance of such crystals to a blurred or doubled image.
