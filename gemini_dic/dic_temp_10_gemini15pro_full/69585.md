felt:/fɛlt/ | n. | 毡；毡制品 | From Middle English felt, from Old English felt (“felt, fleece”), from Proto-Germanic *feltaz (“felt”), from Proto-Indo-European *pel- (“skin, hide; cloth”).  | The hat was made of felt. 这顶帽子是用毡做的。 | 近义词：fabric, material | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词
