parcheesi: /pɑːrˈtʃiːzi/ /pɑːrˈʃiːzi/ | n. |帕切西;  一种棋盘游戏| A board game of Indian origin, played by two, three, or four persons, each with four pawns, which are moved around the board according to throws of dice, the object being to get all one's pawns "home" first.| We played parcheesi all afternoon. 我们一下午都在玩帕切西。| 近义词: board game,  ludo | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific game) | 词性: 名词
