festschrift:/ˈfɛstʃrɪft/ | n. |  纪念文集； Festschrift | A collection of essays or articles written by colleagues and admirers to honor a scholar, often on a special anniversary such as retirement. |  A festschrift was published in honor of Professor Smith's 70th birthday.  为庆祝史密斯教授70岁生日，出版了一本纪念文集。 |  testimonial, commemoration volume, tribute | None | noun
