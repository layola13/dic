grecian:/ˈɡriːʃən/| adj. n.|希腊的；希腊人的；希腊艺术（或文化）的；希腊式建筑的|Relating to ancient or modern Greece, its people, or its culture.  A native or inhabitant of Greece.|例句：The grecian urn was a beautiful piece of art.  希腊瓮是一个美丽的艺术品。|例句：The grecian islands are a popular tourist destination.  希腊群岛是一个受欢迎的旅游目的地。|近义词：Greek, Hellenic|反义词：Roman, Barbarian|词性:形容词, 名词|词源：From Middle French *grecien*, from Latin *Graecianus*, from *Graecus* ("Greek").
