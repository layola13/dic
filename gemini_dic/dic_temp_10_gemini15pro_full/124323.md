motherling:/ˈmʌðərliŋ/
n.| 母语者；用母语表达的人|A person who speaks a language as their native language. |She is a motherling of English. 她是一位英语母语者。| native speaker, first-language speaker | non-native speaker| noun
Etymology-> From mother + -ling, a diminutive suffix.  The word suggests a close connection to one's mother tongue.
