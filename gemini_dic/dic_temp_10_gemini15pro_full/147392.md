overnicely:/ˌoʊvərˈnaɪsli/| adv.|过分殷勤地，过分客气地|Overnicely combines "over" (meaning excessively) and "nicely" (meaning in a pleasant and agreeable manner). It describes an action performed with excessive niceness, often to the point of being insincere or bothersome.|She greeted him overnicely, which made him suspicious. 她过分殷勤地和他打招呼，这让他起了疑心。|近义词：excessively politely, too kindly|反义词：rudely, curtly|词性:副词
