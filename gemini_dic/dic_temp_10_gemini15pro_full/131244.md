noncarbon:/nɑːnˈkɑːrbən/
adjective
非碳的;无碳的
Not composed of carbon; not containing carbon.
Electric cars are promoted as a noncarbon alternative to gasoline-powered vehicles. 电动汽车被推广为汽油动力汽车的非碳替代品。
The company is investing in noncarbon energy sources. 该公司正在投资非碳能源。
non-carbonaceous, carbon-free
