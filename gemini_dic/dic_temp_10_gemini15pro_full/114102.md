magniloquous: /ˌmæɡˈnɪləkwəs/ | adj. | 浮夸的，夸大的 | Using high-flown or bombastic language; pompous. |  He delivered a magniloquous speech, full of long words and elaborate metaphors. 他发表了一篇浮夸的演讲，充满了长长的单词和复杂的比喻。

|例句：Her magniloquous style of writing was not appreciated by the editor. 她浮夸的写作风格不被编辑欣赏。|近义词：bombastic, pompous, grandiose, inflated |反义词：simple, plain, understated, humble |词性: adjective

Etymology:  From Latin *magniloquus*, from *magnus* ("great") + *loquor* ("to speak").
