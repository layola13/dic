identities:/aɪˈdɛntɪtiz/| n. |身份，特性，同一性|The state or fact of being the same one as described.  The condition of being oneself or itself, and not another. |He struggled to reconcile his various identities. 他努力调和自己不同的身份。|Synonyms: personalities, characters, individualities|Antonyms: anonymity, differences|noun
