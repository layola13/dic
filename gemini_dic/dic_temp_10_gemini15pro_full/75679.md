fulfilment:/fʊlˈfɪlmənt/ /fʊlˈfɪlmənt/| n. |满足；履行；实现；完成|the act of doing or achieving what was hoped for or expected|例句：The company failed in its fulfilment of the contract.  该公司未能履行合同。

例句：She found fulfilment in her work as a teacher.  她作为一名教师在工作中找到了满足感。|近义词：completion, accomplishment, realization, achievement, satisfaction|反义词：failure, lack, deficiency|词性:名词

fulfill:/fʊlˈfɪl/ /fʊlˈfɪl/| vt. |履行；实现；完成；满足|to do or achieve what was hoped for or expected|例句：He fulfilled his promise to visit her. 他履行了去看望她的承诺。

例句：The company failed to fulfill its obligations. 公司未能履行其义务。|近义词：accomplish, achieve, complete, realize, satisfy|反义词：fail, neglect|词性:动词
