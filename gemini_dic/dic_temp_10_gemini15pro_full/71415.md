flain:/fleɪn/
adj.
剥皮的，去皮的; 扒皮的；去壳的
skinned, having the skin removed; having the shell or husk removed
The chef prepared the flain tomatoes for the sauce.  厨师准备了去皮的西红柿做酱汁。
The workers quickly flain the logs before processing them. 工人们在加工原木之前快速地剥了皮。
近义词:skinned, peeled, husked
反义词:unskinned, unpeeled
