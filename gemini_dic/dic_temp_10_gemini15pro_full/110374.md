lignify:/ˈlɪɡnɪfaɪ/| v. |木质化，木化|To become wood or woody; to convert into wood.  The plant cells began to lignify, making the stems stronger. 植物细胞开始木质化，使茎变得更强壮。|例句：The tree's branches began to lignify as it matured.  树枝随着成熟开始木质化。

例句：The process of lignification strengthens the plant cell walls. 木质化过程增强了植物细胞壁。|近义词：harden, solidify, petrify|反义词:soften, decay|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin lignum ("wood") + -ify.
