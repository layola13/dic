immortalises:/ɪˈmɔːtəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使永垂不朽，使不朽
To preserve the memory of someone or something forever, often through art, literature, or other forms of expression.
The poet's work immortalises the beauty of the natural world. 这位诗人的作品使自然世界的美丽永垂不朽。
The museum immortalises the history of the town.  博物馆使小镇的历史永垂不朽。
synonyms: commemorates, celebrates, remembers, preserves, eternalizes
antonyms: forgets, ignores, neglects, obliterates
词性: 动词(verb)
