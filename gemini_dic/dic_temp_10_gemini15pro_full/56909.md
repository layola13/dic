dotage:/ˈdoʊtɪdʒ/| n. |老年昏聩；溺爱|A state of mental decline or senility associated with old age; excessive fondness or foolish affection.|His grandfather, in his dotage, often repeated the same stories. 他的祖父年老昏聩，经常重复讲同样的故事。|Synonyms: senility, feebleness, imbecility, second childhood, infatuation|Antonyms: youth, lucidity, sharpness|noun
