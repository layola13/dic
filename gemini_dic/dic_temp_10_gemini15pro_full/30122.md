cedes:/siːdz/
verb
放弃，割让
to surrender possession of, especially by treaty
Mexico ceded California to the US in 1848. 墨西哥在1848年把加利福尼亚割让给了美国。
synonyms: relinquish, yield, surrender
antonyms: retain, keep, acquire
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin cedere "to go, yield, withdraw," from PIE root *ked- "to go, yield."
