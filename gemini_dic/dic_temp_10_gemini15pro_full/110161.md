liegeless:/ˈlaɪdʒləs/
adj.
无臣民的；无封地的；无家可归的
Without liegemen; without a fief; homeless.
The deposed king wandered the countryside, liegeless and alone.  被废黜的国王在乡下流浪，无依无靠，孤苦伶仃。
The war left many knights liegeless and without purpose. 战争使许多骑士失去了封地和目标。
近义词: landless, homeless, unlanded, dispossessed
反义词: landed, entitled
