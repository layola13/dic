nonconfining:/ˌnɒnkənˈfaɪnɪŋ/ | adj. | 不限制的，不拘束的 | Not limiting or restricting freedom of movement or action. |  The horses roamed in a nonconfining pasture. 马儿在一片无拘无束的牧场上漫步。|  Open-plan offices offer a nonconfining work environment. 开放式办公室提供了一个不受拘束的工作环境。 | 近义词: unrestricted, open, free, liberating | 反义词: confining, restrictive, limiting, imprisoning | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + confining (from confine, meaning "to keep within limits")
