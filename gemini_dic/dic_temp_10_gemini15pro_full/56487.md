domanial:/dəˈmeɪniəl/
adjective
领地的；土地的；国土的
Of or relating to the private landed property of a sovereign or feudal lord.  Pertaining to a domain.
The king retained domanial rights over the forest. 国王保留了对森林的领地权。
Synonyms: territorial, proprietary, feudal, seigniorial
Antonyms: public, communal
