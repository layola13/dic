infuriate:/ɪnˈfjʊərieɪt/| v. |激怒，使愤怒|To make someone extremely angry.|Her condescending tone infuriated him. 她居高临下的语气激怒了他。|Near synonyms: enrage, anger, provoke, incense|Near antonyms: calm, soothe, placate|verb
