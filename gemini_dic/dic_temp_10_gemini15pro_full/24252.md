briefest:/ˈbriːfɪst/| adj.|最简短的|Superlative form of brief.  Meaning the shortest in duration, length, or conciseness.|The briefest explanation is often the best. 最简短的解释通常是最好的。|Shortest, most concise, tersest|Longest, most verbose, prolix|adjective
Etymology->
From Middle English *bref, from Old French bref, from Latin brevis (“short”).  The -est suffix is used to form the superlative degree of adjectives.
