donations:/doʊˈneɪʃənz/ | n. | 捐赠，捐款 | something that is given to a charity, especially a sum of money |  The hospital relies heavily on donations to fund its research.  这家医院的研究资金很大程度上依赖于捐款。| gifts, contributions, offerings | withholdings, deductions | noun

donation:/doʊˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 捐赠，捐款 | the act of giving something, especially money, to a charity or other organization | He made a generous donation to the Red Cross. 他向红十字会慷慨解囊。 | gift, contribution, offering | withholding, deduction | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *donacion, from Latin donationem (nominative donatio) "a giving, gift, present," noun of action from past-participle stem of donare "to give, present," from donum "gift," related to dare "to give"
