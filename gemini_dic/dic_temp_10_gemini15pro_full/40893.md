例句：The cookshop offers a variety of takeaway meals. 这家熟食店提供各种外卖餐点。

近义词：eatery, cafe, deli, snack bar | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "restaurant" for a larger, more formal establishment) | 词性: noun
