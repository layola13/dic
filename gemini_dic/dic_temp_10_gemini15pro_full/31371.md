charbroiler:/ˈtʃɑːrˌbrɔɪlər/| n.|炭火烤肉架，烤肉机|A charbroiler is a kitchen appliance used for broiling food over or under a heat source.  It typically uses charcoal or gas as a fuel, creating high heat that sears the food, giving it a distinctive charred flavor.|We cooked the steaks on the charbroiler for a smoky flavor. 我们用炭火烤肉架烤牛排，使其带有烟熏味。|Synonyms: grill, broiler, barbecue|Antonyms: oven, microwave|noun


charbroilers:/ˈtʃɑːrˌbrɔɪlərz/| n.|炭火烤肉架（复数）|Plural form of charbroiler, referring to more than one charbroiler.|The restaurant has two charbroilers to handle the dinner rush.  这家餐厅有两个炭火烤肉架来应对晚餐高峰。|Synonyms: grills, broilers, barbecues|Antonyms: ovens, microwaves|noun
