hanaper:/ˈhænəpər/| n.| (古)文件盒；(尤指英国旧时)财政部印章保管盒；(古)国库|A container, originally a basket or hamper, for documents, especially one for writs in the English Chancery, or for the Great Seal.  Later, by extension, the fees collected for the sealing of documents.|The hanaper held the royal seal and important documents. 文件盒里保存着皇家印章和重要文件。|Synonyms: coffer, repository, treasury|Antonyms: None|noun
