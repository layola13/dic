etymon:/ˈɛtɪmən/ | n. |词源；原始词|A word or morpheme from which a later word is derived. |例句：The etymon of the word "nice" is the Latin word "nescius," meaning "ignorant."  “nice”一词的词源是拉丁语单词“nescius”，意思是“无知的”。

例句：Tracing the etymon of a word can reveal fascinating insights into its historical and cultural context. 追溯一个词的词源可以揭示其历史和文化背景的迷人见解。|近义词：origin, root, source, derivation|反义词: derivative|词性:noun
