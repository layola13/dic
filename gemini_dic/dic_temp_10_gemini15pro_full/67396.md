exterminatory:/ɪkˈstɜːmɪnətɔːri/ /ɪkˈstɜːrmɪnətɔːri/
adjective
灭绝的；毁灭性的；根除的
Relating to or involving extermination; tending to exterminate.
The government launched an exterminatory campaign against the rebels. 政府对叛乱分子发动了灭绝性的打击。
The exterminatory measures employed against the insects proved effective.  用来消灭昆虫的根除措施证明是有效的。

近义词:destructive, eradicative, deadly, lethal
反义词:preservative, protective, constructive

词性:adjective
词源:From Late Latin exterminātōrius, from Latin extermināre ‘drive out, banish, destroy’.
