blink:/blɪŋk/| v. n.|眨眼，闪光|To close and open the eyes quickly.  A quick flash of light.|She blinked rapidly, trying to clear her vision. 她快速地眨了眨眼，试图使自己的视线清晰起来。|The light blinked on and off. 灯一闪一闪的。|synonyms: wink, flicker, flash|antonyms: stare, gaze|verb, noun

blink:/blɪŋk/| n.|眨眼，瞬间，一瞬间|A very short period of time; an instant.|In the blink of an eye, the bird had flown away.  转眼间，鸟儿就飞走了。|It all happened in a blink. 一切都发生在一瞬间。|synonyms: instant, moment, jiffy|antonyms: eternity, ages|noun
