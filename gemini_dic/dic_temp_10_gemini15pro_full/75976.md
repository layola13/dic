funnel:/ˈfʌn(ə)l/| n. vt.|漏斗；漏斗状物；烟囱|a cone-shaped utensil with a tube at the apex for conducting liquid or other substances through a small opening, or for pouring something into a small-mouthed container.|The bartender used a funnel to pour the liquor into the bottle. 酒保用漏斗把酒倒进瓶子里。|近义词：channel, cone, tube|反义词:  (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑广口容器 wide-mouthed container)|n.

funnel:/ˈfʌn(ə)l/| vt.|（使）通过漏斗；像漏斗般集中；汇集|to guide or move (something) through or as if through a funnel|They funneled the donations into a special account. 他们把捐款集中到一个专门的账户。|近义词：channel, direct, concentrate|反义词: disperse, scatter|v.
