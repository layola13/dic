five-star:/ˌfaɪv ˈstɑːr/ (US) /ˌfaɪv ˈstɑː(r)/ (UK)
adjective
五星级的；一流的
Relating to or denoting a hotel, restaurant, etc., of the highest quality, typically one officially rated as having five stars.
This is a five-star hotel with excellent service.  这是一家五星级酒店，服务一流。
The restaurant offers a five-star dining experience. 这家餐厅提供五星级的用餐体验。
Synonyms: top-notch, first-class, deluxe, luxury, premium
Antonyms: low-quality, poor, substandard
词性: 形容词
