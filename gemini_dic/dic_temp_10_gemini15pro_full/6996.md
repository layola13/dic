androecium:/ænˈdriːsiəm/
n.
雄蕊群
The male reproductive part of a flower, consisting of the stamens.
The androecium of this flower has numerous stamens.  这朵花的雄蕊群有很多雄蕊。
Synonyms: staminate whorl
Antonyms: gynoecium
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀνήρ (anḗr, “man”) + οἶκος (oîkos, “house”).  The "house of man" referring to the male reproductive part of the flower.
