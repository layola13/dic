blastoidea:/blæstɔɪdiə/
n.
海蕾纲(已灭绝)
An extinct class of stemmed echinoderms, common in the Paleozoic era, superficially resembling crinoids but having respiratory structures in the form of thin folds.
The Blastoidea are an extinct class of stemmed echinoderms. 海蕾纲是一类已灭绝的有柄棘皮动物。
Blastoids, like crinoids, were stalked echinoderms that lived attached to the seafloor.  像海百合一样，海蕾也是有柄的棘皮动物，附着在海底生活。
近义词: cystoids (囊状动物)
反义词: (无明显的反义词，因为是一个已灭绝的生物分类)
词性: 名词


Etymology:
From Greek *blastos* ("bud") + *-oidea* (suffix forming class names of animals).
