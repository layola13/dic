impost:/ˈɪmpəʊst/ (US) /ˈɪmpoʊst/| n.|税;  (尤指)进口税;  负担;  强加;|A tax, especially one imposed on imported goods; a burden or something that is imposed.|The government imposed a new impost on fuel. 政府对燃料征收了新的税。|Synonyms: tax, duty, levy, tariff, burden, charge|Antonyms: exemption, deduction|noun


impost:/ˈɪmpəʊst/ (US) /ˈɪmpoʊst/| v.|征(税);  强加;  施加;  把…强加于;|To impose (something, such as a tax) officially; to levy.|The government has imposed a new tax on cigarettes. 政府对香烟征收了新的税。|Synonyms: levy, impose, charge, inflict|Antonyms: remit, remove|verb
