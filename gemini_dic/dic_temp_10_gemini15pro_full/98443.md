insperse:/ɪnˈspɜːrs/ /ɪnˈspɝːs/
verb
散布，点缀
scatter something among or between other things; diversify something with something scattered or interspersed.
The text is interspersed with illustrations.  正文中穿插着插图。
He interspersed his speech with anecdotes. 他在演讲中穿插了一些轶事。
近义词: scatter, sprinkle, distribute, dot, pepper, stud
反义词: gather, collect, concentrate
词性: verb

词源: From Latin inspersus, past participle of inspergere (“to sprinkle upon, scatter”), from in- (“in, on, upon”) + spargere (“to scatter”).
