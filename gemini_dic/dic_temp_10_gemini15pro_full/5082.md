allanite:/ˈæləˌnaɪt/| n. |褐帘石|A sorosilicate mineral containing cerium, lanthanum, and other rare earths, calcium, iron, and aluminum.  It is a common accessory mineral in igneous and metamorphic rocks.|Allanite is a relatively common mineral in some granites.|近义词:orthite (a synonym)|反义词: None (Minerals don't typically have antonyms)|noun
