collate: /kəˈleɪt/ (US), /kɒˈleɪt/ (UK) | vt. | 校对，整理，核对 | To gather, arrange, or compare critically, especially texts or information. |  例句：The researcher collated data from several different studies. 研究人员整理了来自几项不同研究的数据。 |  例句：Please collate these documents in alphabetical order. 请按字母顺序整理这些文件。 | 近义词: assemble, gather, organize, compare, compile | 反义词: disperse, scatter, disorganize | 词性: verb

Etymology: From Latin "collatus," past participle of "conferre" (to bring together).
