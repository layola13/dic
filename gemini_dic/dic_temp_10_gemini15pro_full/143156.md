outcheat:/ˌaʊtˈtʃiːt/
verb
胜过；以欺骗取胜
to cheat more or better than someone else
He outcheated his opponent in the poker game. 他在扑克游戏中以欺骗的手段胜过了对手。
近义词: outsmart, outwit, defraud
反义词: be cheated, lose to


Etymology:
"out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding, surpassing") + "cheat" (verb meaning "to act dishonestly or unfairly in order to gain an advantage")
