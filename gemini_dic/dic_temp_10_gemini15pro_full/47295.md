de-iced:/diːˈaɪst/
verb
除冰，除去…上的冰
To remove ice from something, typically an aircraft or a road.
The plane was de-iced before takeoff.  飞机起飞前已除冰。
The roads were de-iced after the snowstorm.  暴风雪后道路上的冰被清除了。
Synonyms: defrost, thaw
Antonyms: freeze, ice over
verb

de-iced:/diːˈaɪst/
adjective
已除冰的
Having had ice removed.
The de-iced runway was ready for use. 已除冰的跑道可以使用了。
The car's windshield was de-iced and clear.  汽车的挡风玻璃已除冰，清晰可见。
Synonyms:  defrosted, thawed
Antonyms: frozen, iced, icy
adjective
