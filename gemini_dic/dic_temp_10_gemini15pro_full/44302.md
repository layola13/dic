crookest:/ˈkrʊkɪst/ | adj. | 最弯曲的；最不诚实的；最狡猾的 |  Superlative form of crooked: most crooked.  It describes something that is bent, dishonest, or corrupt to the greatest degree. |  That's the crookest politician I've ever seen. 那是我见过的最腐败的政客。| The path through the woods was the crookest I'd ever walked.  穿过树林的小路是我走过的最弯曲的。 | synonyms: most bent, most twisted, most corrupt, most dishonest, most deceitful | antonyms: straightest, most honest, most upright | adjective
