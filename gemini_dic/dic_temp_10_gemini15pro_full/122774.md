moksha:/ˈmoʊkʃə/ | n. | 解脱，涅槃 | Liberation from the cycle of death and rebirth (samsara) in Hinduism, Buddhism, Jainism, and Sikhism.  Release from earthly existence. |  His ultimate goal was moksha, a release from the endless cycle of reincarnation. 他最终的目标是解脱，从无尽的轮回中解脱出来。

She believed that through meditation she could achieve moksha. 她相信通过冥想可以获得解脱。 | 近义词: liberation, release, enlightenment, nirvana | 反义词: bondage, samsara, imprisonment | 词性: noun
