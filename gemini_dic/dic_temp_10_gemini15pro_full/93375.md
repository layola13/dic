illest:/ˈɪləst/| adj.|最棒的；最酷的；最厉害的 (slang)|Superlative form of "ill" (in its slang sense meaning "excellent," "cool," or "awesome").  Used to express strong approval or admiration.  Originated in African American Vernacular English (AAVE).|Example 1: That new song is the illest! 那首新歌太棒了！

Example 2: He's got the illest sneakers I've ever seen. 他有我见过最酷的运动鞋。|近义词：best, coolest, dopest, sickest, tightest|反义词:worst, lamest, corniest|词性:形容词
