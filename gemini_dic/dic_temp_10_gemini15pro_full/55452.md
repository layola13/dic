distinguishably:/dɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃəbli/| adv.|可区分地，明显地|In a way that is clearly noticeable or different from something else.|The twins are distinguishably different in height. 这对双胞胎的身高明显不同。|Example: The two versions of the software are distinguishably different in performance. 这两个版本的软件在性能上明显不同。|Synonyms: noticeably, perceptibly, clearly, markedly, evidently|Antonyms: indistinguishably, imperceptibly|Word Type: Adverb


Etymology:
From distinguish + -able + -ly.  "Distinguish" comes from Old French "distinguiss-" (stem of distinguer), from Latin "distinguere"  meaning "to separate by pricking, to distinguish, to mark," from "dis-" (apart) + "stinguere" (to prick, extinguish).
