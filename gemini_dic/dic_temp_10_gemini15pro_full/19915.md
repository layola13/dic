bio-organic:/ˌbaɪoʊɔːrˈɡænɪk/ | adj. | 生物有机的 | Relating to or derived from living organisms and containing carbon.  Pertaining to the branch of chemistry that deals with organic compounds found in living organisms. | Bio-organic chemistry plays a crucial role in understanding life processes. 生物有机化学在理解生命过程中起着至关重要的作用。|  Bio-organic fertilizers improve soil health. 生物有机肥料改善土壤健康。 |  biological, organic | inorganic, synthetic | adjective
