flexion:/ˈflekʃən/ (US), /ˈflekʃn/ (UK)| n.|弯曲，屈曲；变形；词尾变化|The flexion of the knee allows us to walk and run. 膝盖的弯曲使我们能够行走和奔跑。  The word "books" demonstrates the flexion of adding "-s" to indicate plurality.  单词“books”展示了添加“-s”来表示复数的词尾变化。|近义词：bending, curvature, inflection|反义词:extension, straightening|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *flexioun, from Late Latin flexiōnem, accusative singular of flexiō (“a bending”), from Latin flexus, past participle of flectere (“to bend”).
