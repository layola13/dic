mindfulnesses:/ˈmaɪndfəlnəsɪz/
n.
正念的时刻; 正念练习
The state of being mindful or the practice of mindfulness.  Multiple instances or types of mindfulness.
She practices several mindfulnesses, including meditation and mindful walking. 她练习几种正念，包括冥想和正念行走。
Synonyms: awareness, attention, presence, concentration
Antonyms: distraction, inattention, unawareness
词性: 名词(plural)


Etymology:
From "mindfulness" + "-es" (plural suffix). "Mindfulness" is derived from "mindful" + "-ness".  "Mindful" comes from "mind" + "-ful".  Ultimately from Old English "gemynd" (memory).
