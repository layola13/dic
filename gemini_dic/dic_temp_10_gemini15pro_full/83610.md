haematosis:/ˌhiːməˈtoʊsɪs/ /ˌheməˈtoʊsɪs/| n. | 血液氧化；氧合作用 | The process of oxygenation of the blood. The process by which oxygen is taken into the blood and carbon dioxide is removed from it, especially in the lungs. | Example 1: Haematosis occurs in the alveoli of the lungs. 血液氧化作用发生在肺泡中。

Example 2: Efficient haematosis is essential for healthy bodily function.  有效的血液氧化作用对于健康的身体机能至关重要。 | Synonym: oxygenation, aeration | Antonym: deoxygenation | noun
