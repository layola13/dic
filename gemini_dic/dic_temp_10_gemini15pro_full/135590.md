nonporousness:/ˌnɒnˌpɔːrəsnəs/
n.
无孔性，不透气性
The nonporousness of the material makes it ideal for containing liquids.  这种材料的无孔性使其成为盛装液体的理想选择。
The nonporousness of the rock prevented water from seeping through.  岩石的无孔性阻止了水的渗透。
impenetrability, impermeability
porosity, permeability
