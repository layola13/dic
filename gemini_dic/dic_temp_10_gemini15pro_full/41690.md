corridored: /ˈkɒrɪdɔːrd/ (UK) /ˈkɔːrɪdɔːrd/ (US)
adjective
有走廊的，走廊式的
Having or resembling a corridor or corridors; lined with corridors.
The hotel was a vast, corridored building.  这家酒店是一座巨大的、走廊式的建筑。
The office is in a long, corridored wing of the building. 办公室位于大楼一个长长的、走廊式的侧翼。
近义词:  hallwayed, galleried
反义词:  open-plan
