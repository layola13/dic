attests:/əˈtɛsts/
|v.|证明，证实|provides or serves as clear evidence of.|His success attests to his hard work. 他的成功证明了他的努力。|certifies, confirms, verifies|denies, refutes|verb

attests:/əˈtɛsts/
|v.|表明，显示|shows something to be true|The high quality of the product attests to the manufacturer's commitment to excellence.  这产品的优良品质证明了制造商对卓越的承诺。 |demonstrates, indicates, reveals|conceals, hides|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French atester, from Latin attestari "to bear witness, confirm," from ad- "to" (see ad-) + testari "to bear witness," from testis "witness" (see testament).
