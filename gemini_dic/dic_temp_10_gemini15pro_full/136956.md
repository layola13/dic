nonspecifics:/ˌnɑːnspɪˈsɪfɪks/ | n. | 非特异性事物；非特异性症状 | things or matters that are not clearly defined or specified; symptoms that are not characteristic of a particular disease |  The doctor said my symptoms were nonspecifics and ordered more tests. 医生说我的症状是非特异性的，并要求进行更多检查。 |  The report was full of nonspecifics and lacked concrete details.  这份报告充满了非特异性的内容，缺乏具体的细节。| synonyms: generalities, vagaries, ambiguities | antonyms: specifics, particulars | noun

Etymology:  From non- (not) + specific. "Specific" traces back to Latin *species "kind, sort"
