outthrow:/ˌaʊtˈθroʊ/
verb
投得比…远；超过…的投掷距离
to throw something further than someone else throws something
The quarterback outthrew all the other players. 四分卫投球的距离超过了所有其他球员。
outdistance, surpass, exceed
