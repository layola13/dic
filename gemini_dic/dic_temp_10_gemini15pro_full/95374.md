incestuous:/ɪnˈsɛs.tʃu.əs/| adj.|乱伦的；近亲相奸的；|Characterized by or involving sexual relations between people too closely related to marry legally.  | The play explored the incestuous relationship between a brother and sister.  这出戏探讨了兄妹之间乱伦的关系。| The family had a dark, incestuous secret. 这个家庭有一个黑暗的、乱伦的秘密。 | 近义词：forbidden, taboo, improper, immoral| 反义词：proper, moral, acceptable|词性:形容词

incestuous:/ɪnˈsɛs.tʃu.əs/| adj.|（尤指排斥其他人的）封闭的，小圈子的|Exclusive and closed, often implying a negative connotation of exclusivity and self-serving behavior.  | The academic department was criticized for its incestuous hiring practices, favoring internal candidates over more qualified outsiders.  这个学术部门因其封闭的招聘做法而受到批评，他们偏袒内部候选人而不是更有资格的外部人士。| The fashion industry can be quite incestuous, with the same models and designers working together constantly. 时装业可能相当封闭，同样的模特和设计师不断地在一起工作。| 近义词：cliquey, clannish, exclusive| 反义词：open, inclusive, welcoming|词性:形容词
