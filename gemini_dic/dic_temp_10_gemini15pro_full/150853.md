paragram:/ˈpærəɡræm/| n.|改写后的段落，释义|A restatement of a text or passage giving the meaning in another form, as for clearness; a paraphrase.|The teacher asked the students to write a paragram of the poem. 老师要求学生们改写这首诗。|近义词：paraphrase, rewording, restatement|反义词: quotation, verbatim|noun
