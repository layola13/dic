nonschematic:/ˌnɒnskiːˈmætɪk/ /ˌnɑːnskiːˈmætɪk/
adjective
非图解的；非概要的；非模式化的
Not relating to or based on a schematic diagram or representation; not simplified or formulaic.
This research uses a nonschematic approach to understanding memory. 这项研究使用非图解的方法来理解记忆。
The artist's nonschematic paintings capture the essence of the natural world.  这位艺术家的非图解绘画捕捉了自然世界的精髓。

近义词:nonformulaic, unstructured, atypical, irregular
反义词:schematic, formulaic, structured, typical, regular
词性:adjective
