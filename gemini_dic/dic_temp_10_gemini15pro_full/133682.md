nonfraudulencies:/ˌnɒnfrɔːdʒʊˈlɛnsiz/ | n. | 无欺诈行为;诚实行为 |  The auditor confirmed the nonfraudulencies of the transactions. |  例句：The company's record of nonfraudulencies gave investors confidence.  这家公司的无欺诈记录给了投资者信心。

例句：  His nonfraudulencies in business dealings are well known.  他诚实的商业行为是众所周知的。| 近义词：honesty, integrity, probity | 反义词：fraud, deceit, dishonesty | 词性: 名词

词源: non- (not) + fraudulent (deceitful, dishonest) + -ency (state or quality)  来自拉丁语 fraus, fraudis, 意思是“欺骗，欺诈”。
