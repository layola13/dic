miscognize:/ˌmɪskɒɡˈnaɪz/ /ˌmɪskɑːɡˈnaɪz/| vt.|误认，认错|To fail to recognize correctly; mistake for something or someone else.|例句：I miscognized her at first, thinking she was her sister. 我一开始认错人了，把她当成了她姐姐。

例句：He miscognized the symptoms and thought he just had a cold. 他误判了症状，以为自己只是感冒了。|近义词：misidentify, mistake, misconstrue|反义词：recognize, identify, know|词性:动词
