berthage: /ˈbɜːθɪdʒ/ (UK) /ˈbɜːrθɪdʒ/ (US)
n.
停泊处; 泊位;  码头费
A place where a ship can be moored or docked; the charge for mooring a ship.
The ship paid a hefty fee for its berthage at the busy port. 这艘船为在繁忙的港口停泊支付了高额的码头费。
dockage, mooring, wharfage
anchorage
noun
