chamoising:/ˈʃæmwɑːzɪŋ/| n. |麂皮擦拭；用麂皮擦拭|The act of cleaning or polishing something with a chamois leather.  |He gave his car a thorough chamoising. 他用麂皮把他的车彻底擦了一遍。|Synonyms: polishing, buffing, cleaning, wiping |Antonyms: soiling, dirtying|noun
chamois:/ˈʃæmwɑː/| n. |麂皮|A soft, pliable leather originally made from the skin of the chamois antelope, now often from sheepskin or other animal hide.  |I use a chamois to dry my car. 我用麂皮擦干我的车。|Synonyms:  shammy, leather cloth |Antonyms: N/A|noun
