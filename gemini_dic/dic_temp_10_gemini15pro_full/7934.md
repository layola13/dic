anteriorly:/ænˈtɪəriərli/
|adv.|
|向前地，在前面|
|Situated or directed toward the front.  It refers to the front part of the body, or a position closer to the head in animals.|
|The sternum is located anteriorly in the chest.  胸骨位于胸腔的前部。|

|The tumor was located anteriorly to the heart. 肿瘤位于心脏前方。|
|frontwards, to the front, ahead|
|posteriorly|
|adverb|
