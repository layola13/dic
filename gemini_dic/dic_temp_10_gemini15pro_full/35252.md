clunker:/ˈklʌŋkər/| n.|破车，老爷车；失败的作品|A dilapidated or poorly functioning vehicle; something that is old, worn-out, or of poor quality. |例句：He drives around in an old clunker. 他开着一辆老爷车到处跑。

|例句：The movie was a real clunker. 这部电影真是个败笔。|近义词：lemon, jalopy, dud|反义词：gem, masterpiece|词性:noun
