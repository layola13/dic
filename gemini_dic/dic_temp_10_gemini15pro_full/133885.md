nongrieving:/ˌnɑnˈɡriːvɪŋ/
adjective
未感到悲伤的，不悲伤的
Not experiencing or expressing grief.
She appeared remarkably nongrieving after her husband's death, surprising everyone with her stoicism. 她丈夫去世后，她显得异常平静，她的坚忍震惊了所有人。
近义词: unmoved, impassive, unemotional, stoic
反义词: grieving, sorrowful, mourning, bereaved
词性: adjective
