englishnesses:/ˈɪŋɡlɪʃnɪsɪz/
n.
英语的特性；英国人的特性
The qualities or characteristics that are considered typically English.
He embodied all the stereotypical englishnesses, from his love of tea to his stiff upper lip. 他体现了所有典型的英国人特征，从他对茶的热爱到他坚毅的性格。
Synonym: Englishness (singular form is more common), English traits, English characteristics
Antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider traits of other nationalities, e.g., Frenchness)
