gutsy:/ˈɡʌtsi/| adj.|勇敢的，有胆量的，坚韧的|Showing courage, determination, and spirit.|She made a gutsy decision to start her own business. 她做了一个勇敢的决定，开始自己的事业。|近义词:brave, courageous, bold, daring, spirited|反义词:timid, cowardly, fearful|词性:形容词
词源:From gut (n.) + -y (adj. suffix).  Originally referred to having "lots of guts," implying fortitude and resilience.
