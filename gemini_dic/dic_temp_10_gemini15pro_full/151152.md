paravail:/ˈpærəveɪl/
n.
最低级地产保有权; 最低级保有条件
The lowest or most immediate estate in land, held by the tenant who actually occupies and uses the land.
The tenant held the land in paravail.  该佃户拥有这块土地的最低级保有权。
近义词: tenancy, fee tail
反义词: freehold

Etymology:
From Anglo-French *par avail, literally "by the avowal."  "Avowal" referred to the acknowledgment of tenancy by a subtenant to the superior lord of the fee.
