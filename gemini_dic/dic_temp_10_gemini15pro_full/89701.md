hoovering:/ˈhuːvərɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈhuːvərɪŋ/ (US)| n. v.|吸尘,盘旋,吵闹地打扫|The act of cleaning with a vacuum cleaner, often used metaphorically to describe someone's persistent and intrusive attention or attempts to regain control or influence over another person.  Derived from the brand name "Hoover," which became synonymous with vacuum cleaners.|例句：She was hoovering the carpet when the phone rang. 她正在用吸尘器打扫地毯时，电话响了。

例句：He's been hoovering around her ever since she broke up with him. 自从她和他分手后，他就一直缠着她。|近义词：cleaning, vacuuming, pestering, harassing|反义词: ignoring, neglecting|词性: noun, verb (present participle)
