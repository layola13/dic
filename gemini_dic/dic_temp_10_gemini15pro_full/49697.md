demobbed:/ˌdiːˈmɒbd/ (UK), /ˌdiːˈmɑːbd/ (US)| adj.|复员的，退伍的|Discharged from military service.|例句：He was demobbed after the war.  他战后复员了。

例句：Many demobbed soldiers struggled to find work. 许多退伍军人难以找到工作。

近义词：discharged, mustered out|反义词：enlisted, drafted|词性:形容词


词源:  Shortened from "demobilized," from "de-" (meaning "down from" or "removal") + "mobilize" (to prepare for war).
