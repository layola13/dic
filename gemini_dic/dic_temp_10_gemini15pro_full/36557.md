collapsables:/kəˈlæpsəbəlz/ | n. | 可折叠物，可折叠式 |  Items that are designed to be collapsed or folded for storage or transport.  | The campervan had collapsables like chairs and a table. 露营车里有可折叠的椅子和桌子。 | Folding items, retractable items | Fixed items, rigid items | Noun

collapsable:/kəˈlæpsəbəl/ | adj. | 可折叠的 | Able to be folded or collapsed into a smaller form. |  The stroller is collapsable, making it easy to store.  婴儿车是可折叠的，便于存放。| Folding, retractable, portable | Fixed, rigid, bulky | Adjective
