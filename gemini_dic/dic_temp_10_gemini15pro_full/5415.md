almery: /ˈɑːlməri/ | n. | 衣橱，碗橱 | A cupboard or wardrobe, especially one used for storing food or clothing.  A chiefly archaic word. | The almery held linens and blankets.  衣橱里放着亚麻布和毯子。 |  cupboard, wardrobe, closet |  N/A | noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *almarie, from Latin armarium (“closet, chest, cupboard, bookcase”), neuter of armarius (“of or belonging to arms or tools”), from arma (“tools, implements, arms, armor”).
