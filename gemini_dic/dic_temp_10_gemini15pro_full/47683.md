debugged:/diːˈbʌɡd/
v. | 调试过的，除错的 | To identify and remove errors from (a computer program) or other complex system. | The software engineer debugged the program until it ran smoothly. 软件工程师调试程序直到它顺利运行。 | fixed, corrected, repaired | broken, faulty | verb (past tense)

debugged:/diːˈbʌɡd/
adj. | 调试过的，除错的 | Free of errors or bugs. |  The debugged version of the software was released to the public.  调试后的软件版本向公众发布。 | fixed, corrected, repaired | broken, faulty | adjective
