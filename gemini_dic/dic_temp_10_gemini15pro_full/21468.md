blowtube:/ˈbloʊˌtjuːb/ | n. | 吹管，吹火筒 | A tube through which air is blown, often used to propel small projectiles or to fuel a fire. |  He used a blowtube to shoot a dart at the target. 他用吹管向目标射出一支飞镖。

The tribesmen used blowtubes to hunt small animals. 部落成员用吹管捕猎小动物。 | synonyms: blowpipe, pea shooter | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps bellows for creating a larger airflow) | noun
