ferruginated:/fəˈruːdʒɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
铁锈色的，锈色的，含铁的
covered with or containing rust; rust-colored.
The old, ferruginated lock refused to budge.  那把老旧的、锈迹斑斑的锁怎么也打不开。
The geologist discovered a ferruginated layer of rock. 地质学家发现了一层含铁的岩石层。
近义词:rusty, rust-colored, iron-stained
反义词:stainless, unrusted, clean


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ferruginatus*, past participle of *ferruginare* "to make rusty," from ferrugo "rust," related to ferrum "iron."
