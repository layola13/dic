doornails:/ˈdɔːrneɪlz/
n.
(figurative, informal) Absolutely, completely.
This idiom is always used in the phrase "dead as a doornail."
He's dead as a doornail. 他已经死透了。
近义词: deceased, lifeless, defunct
反义词: alive, living
词性: noun (plural)
