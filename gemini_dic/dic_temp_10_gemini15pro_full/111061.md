listenability:/ˌlɪsənəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可听性，易听度 | The quality of being easy and enjoyable to listen to.  How easily and pleasantly something can be listened to, often referring to audio content like podcasts or audiobooks. | The podcast's high listenability is due to the host's engaging storytelling style.  这个播客的高可听性源于主持人引人入胜的叙事风格。 |  近义词：audibility, accessibility, enjoyability | 反义词：unlistenability, tediousness | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From "listen" + "-able" (suffix meaning "capable of being") + "-ity" (suffix forming nouns of quality or state).  源自 "listen" + "-able" (表示“能够被”的后缀) + "-ity" (构成表示性质或状态的名词的后缀)。
