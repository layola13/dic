chimneys:/ˈtʃɪm.niz/| n.|烟囱|A vertical channel or pipe made of brick or metal that carries smoke and gases up from a fireplace, furnace, or industrial process.|Smoke billowed from the factory chimneys.  工厂的烟囱里冒出浓烟。|近义词：stacks, flues|反义词:None|noun
