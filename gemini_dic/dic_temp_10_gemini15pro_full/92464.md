hypostatically:/ˌhaɪpəˈstætɪkli/
adv.
本质上地，实体地
in a hypostatic manner; by union of natures in one person.  Existing in a distinct and separate state.
The divine and human natures of Christ are hypostatically united. 基督的神性和人性是本质上统一的。
近义词:essentially, substantially, inherently
反义词:accidentally, superficially
词性:adverb

词源: From Late Latin *hypostaticus, from Ancient Greek ὑποστατικός (hupo statikos, “of or pertaining to substance or subsistence; constitutive”), from ὑπόστασις (hypostasis, “sediment, foundation, substance, subsistence”).
