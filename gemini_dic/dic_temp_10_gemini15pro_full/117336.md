medevacing:/ˈmɛdəvækɪŋ/
verb
医疗后送；医疗撤离
The act of evacuating someone who is injured or ill, especially from a remote area, to a place where they can receive medical care.
例句：They were medevacing the injured climber from the mountain. 他们正在用直升机把受伤的登山者从山上送走。

例句：The soldier was medevaced after being wounded in combat.  这名士兵在战斗中受伤后被医疗后送。

近义词：evacuating, airlifting
反义词: None (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑"leaving behind" 放弃，留下)
词性: verb (动词) - present participle


medevac:/ˈmɛdəvæk/
noun
医疗后送；医疗撤离；医疗后送的飞机（或车辆）
The evacuation of someone who is injured or ill, especially from a remote area, to a place where they can receive medical care. Also refers to the aircraft or vehicle used in such an evacuation.
例句：The medevac arrived quickly and transported the patient to the hospital. 医疗后送飞机迅速到达，并将病人送往医院。

例句：The team requested a medevac for their injured teammate. 团队请求为他们受伤的队友进行医疗后送。


近义词：evacuation, airlift
反义词: None
词性: noun (名词)


Etymology (词源):
A blend of medical and evacuation.  "Medical evacuation" 的缩写。
