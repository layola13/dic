fictitious:/fɪkˈtɪʃəs/
adjective
虚构的；假的
created or imagined, not real
例句：The characters in the novel are entirely fictitious. 小说中的人物完全是虚构的。

例句：He gave a fictitious account of his movements. 他编造了关于他的行踪的谎言。

近义词：fabricated, invented, imaginary, unreal, made-up, spurious
反义词：real, actual, genuine, true, factual
词性: adjective
