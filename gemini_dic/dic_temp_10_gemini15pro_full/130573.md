nonactivists:/ˌnɒnˈæktɪvɪsts/ | n. | 非活动人士；不积极分子 |  People who do not participate in activism or social movements.  A person who is not involved in political or social activism. |  Many nonactivists still hold strong political opinions. 许多非活动人士仍然持有强烈的政治观点。 | The nonactivists in the community rarely voiced their concerns. 社区里的非活动人士很少表达他们的担忧。 | synonyms: bystanders, observers, onlookers | antonyms: activists, campaigners, protesters | noun
