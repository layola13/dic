cmet:/ˈsiːmɛt/ | n. |  C-MET (Mesenchymal-Epithelial Transition factor) |  A receptor tyrosine kinase that plays a role in cell growth, motility, and survival. It is often overexpressed or mutated in cancer. |  CMET is a key driver of metastasis in many types of cancer. | 近义词: (No direct synonym, but related terms include: tyrosine kinase receptor, growth factor receptor, oncogene) | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
