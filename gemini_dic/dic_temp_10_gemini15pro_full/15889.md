balloonlike:/bəˈluːnˌlaɪk/
adjective
像气球的，气球状的
Resembling a balloon in shape, lightness, or inflation.
The cloud formations were balloonlike and fluffy.  这些云层像气球一样蓬松。
The child carried a balloonlike toy. 孩子拿着一个像气球一样的玩具。
近义词:inflated, puffy, buoyant
反义词:deflated, flat, solid
词性:adjective
