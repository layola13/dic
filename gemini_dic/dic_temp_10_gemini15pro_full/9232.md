antiparasitics:/ˌæntiˌpærəˈsɪtɪks/| n. |抗寄生虫药|Medications used to treat parasitic infections.|例句：The veterinarian prescribed antiparasitics for the dog's fleas. 兽医给狗开了治疗跳蚤的抗寄生虫药。

例句：Antiparasitics are important for maintaining the health of livestock. 抗寄生虫药对维护牲畜的健康很重要。|近义词：antihelminthics, parasiticides|反义词: None|词性: Noun


antiparasitic:/ˌæntiˌpærəˈsɪtɪk/| adj. |抗寄生虫的|Having the property of destroying or inhibiting the growth of parasites.|例句：The shampoo has antiparasitic properties that help control lice.  这种洗发水具有抗寄生虫的特性，有助于控制虱子。

例句：He was prescribed an antiparasitic cream for his skin infection. 他被开了抗寄生虫药膏治疗皮肤感染。|近义词：antihelminthic, parasiticidal|反义词: None|词性: Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + parasitic.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to," derived from Greek. "Parasitic" comes from the word "parasite," which ultimately derives from the Greek "parasitos," meaning "one who eats at the table of another."
