lubber:/ˈlʌbər/ | n. | 笨拙的人，旱鸭子，新手 | a clumsy, awkward person, especially one unskilled at seamanship |  The lubber tripped over the rope and fell into the sea. 这个旱鸭子被绳子绊倒，掉进了海里。| synonyms: clumsy, awkward, oaf, landlubber | antonyms: expert, skilled, adept | noun

lubber:/ˈlʌbər/ | adj. | 笨拙的，不熟练的 | clumsy, awkward, unskilled |  His lubber movements made it clear he was no sailor. 他笨拙的动作表明他不是水手。| synonyms: clumsy, awkward, ungainly | antonyms: graceful, agile, deft | adjective
