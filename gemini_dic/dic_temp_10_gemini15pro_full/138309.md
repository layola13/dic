noted:/ˈnoʊtɪd/| adj.|著名的，显著的；记录下来的|Well-known; recognized; recorded.|例句：He is a noted historian. 他是一位著名的历史学家。|近义词：famous, renowned, celebrated, distinguished, recognized, recorded|反义词：unknown, obscure, anonymous|adjective

noted:/ˈnoʊtɪd/| v.|注意，留意；记录；特别提到|To observe or pay attention to something; to make a written record of something; to mention something specifically.|例句：I noted her careful attention to detail. 我注意到她对细节的认真关注。|近义词：observe, perceive, remark, record, mention|反义词：ignore, overlook, disregard|verb
