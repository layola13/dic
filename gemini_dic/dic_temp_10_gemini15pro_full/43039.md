cowhearted:/ˈkaʊˌhɑːrtɪd/| adj.|胆小的，懦弱的|Lacking courage; timid.  Derived from "cow" suggesting a timid nature like a cow, and "hearted" referring to one's disposition.|He was too cowhearted to stand up to the bully. 他太胆小了，不敢反抗那个恶霸。|Synonyms: timid, cowardly, fearful, pusillanimous|Antonyms: brave, courageous, bold, valiant|adjective
