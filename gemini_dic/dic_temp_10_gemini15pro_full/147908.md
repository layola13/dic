overreflectively:/ˌoʊvərɪˈflektɪvli/
adv. |过度反思地|Characterized by excessive reflection or introspection.  Thinking too much about past actions or possible future outcomes, often to the point of anxiety or paralysis.| She analyzed the situation overreflectively, second-guessing every decision she had made. 她过度反思地分析了情况，对她做出的每一个决定都进行了事后诸葛亮。 |近义词：excessively introspective, ruminatively, broodingly| 反义词: impulsively, thoughtlessly |词性: 副词

overreflectively:/ˌoʊvərɪˈflektɪvli/
词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "reflective" (adjective meaning "characterized by reflection") + "-ly" (adverb suffix)
