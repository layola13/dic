burgeoning:/ˈbɜːrdʒənɪŋ/| adj.|迅速发展的，快速增长的|Beginning to grow or increase rapidly; flourishing.|The city's burgeoning population is putting a strain on resources. 城市迅速增长的人口给资源带来了压力。|The company experienced burgeoning sales in the first quarter.  该公司在第一季度销售额迅速增长。|近义词：expanding, growing, flourishing, thriving, booming, developing|反义词：declining, shrinking, dwindling, contracting, stagnating|词性:形容词
词源: From the Middle English word *burjonen*, meaning "to bud, sprout," from Old French *burjoner*. Ultimately from Latin *burjo*, a variant of *surculus* "twig, shoot."
