aerodynamic:/ˌɛroʊdaɪˈnæmɪk/ /ˌeərəʊdaɪˈnæmɪk/| adj.|空气动力学的|Relating to the movement of air and the forces acting on objects moving through it.  Designed to reduce air resistance.|The car's aerodynamic design improves fuel efficiency. 这款车的空气动力学设计提高了燃油效率。

The plane's aerodynamic properties allow it to fly at high speeds. 这架飞机的空气动力学特性使其能够高速飞行。|近义词:streamlined, sleek|反义词:clumsy, unwieldy|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From aero- (Greek ἀήρ, aer, "air") + dynamic (Greek δύναμις, dynamis, "power").

USAGE->
The aerodynamic design of the car reduces drag. 这辆车的空气动力学设计减少了阻力。

The new aircraft has improved aerodynamic performance.  这架新飞机的空气动力性能得到了提升。

PHRASE->
aerodynamic drag = 空气阻力
aerodynamic lift = 空气升力
aerodynamic shape = 流线型
aerodynamic efficiency = 空气动力学效率
aerodynamic properties =  空气动力学特性
