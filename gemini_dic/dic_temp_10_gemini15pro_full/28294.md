capuchin:/ˈkæpjutʃɪn/| n.|卷尾猴； капуцин猴；卡布奇诺咖啡；卡布奇诺修士|A New World monkey of the genus Cebus, having prehensile tails and inhabiting tropical forests. Also, a member of a branch of the Franciscan order, wearing a brown habit with a pointed hood.  A type of coffee beverage flavored with steamed and frothed milk.|例句：The capuchin monkey deftly peeled the banana. 卷尾猴灵巧地剥开了香蕉。

例句：I ordered a capuchin with extra foam. 我点了一杯加了额外奶泡的卡布奇诺。|近义词：monkey, friar (for the religious order), cappuccino (for the coffee)|反义词: (No direct antonym for the animal or religious order. For the coffee, perhaps "espresso" as a simpler alternative.)|词性:noun
