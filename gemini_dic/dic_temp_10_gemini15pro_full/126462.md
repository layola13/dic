mythically:/ˈmɪθɪkəli/
adverb
神话般地；传说的；虚构地
In a manner relating to or characteristic of myths or legends; in a way that is not based on fact.
The hero was mythically described as having the strength of ten men.  这位英雄被神话般地描述为拥有十个人的力量。
The land was mythically associated with dragons and other fantastical creatures. 这片土地在神话中与龙和其他奇幻生物联系在一起。

近义词: legendarily, fabulously, fabledly
反义词: realistically, factually, historically
词性: adverb
