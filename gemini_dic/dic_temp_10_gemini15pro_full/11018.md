arbuscular:/ɑːrˈbʌskjʊlər/
adj.
丛枝状的
Relating to or resembling an arbuscule, a branched tree-like structure formed by arbuscular mycorrhizal fungi inside the cells of plant roots.
Arbuscular mycorrhizal fungi form symbiotic relationships with plant roots. 丛枝菌根真菌与植物根系形成共生关系。
The arbuscular structures are essential for nutrient exchange between the fungus and the plant. 丛枝状结构对于真菌和植物之间的营养交换至关重要。
近义词:  mycorrhizal
反义词:  non-mycorrhizal
词性:  adjective
