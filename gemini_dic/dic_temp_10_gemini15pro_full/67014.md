expire:/ɪkˈspaɪər/ /ɛkˈspaɪr/| v. |到期，失效，终止；死亡| to come to an end; to cease to be valid; to die |My passport expires next month. 我的护照下个月到期。| synonyms: terminate, lapse, run out, cease, die | antonyms: renew, extend, begin, start | verb

expire:/ɪkˈspaɪər/ /ɛkˈspaɪr/| n. |到期，截止| the date or time at which something ends or ceases to be valid |What is the expiry date of this product?  这个产品的有效期是什么时候？| synonyms: termination, expiry, deadline | antonyms:  renewal, extension, commencement | noun
