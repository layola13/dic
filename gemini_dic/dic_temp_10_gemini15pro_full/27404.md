camerade:/ˌkæməˈrɑːd/ /ˌkæməˈræd/ | n. |同志，战友，同事|A comrade is a companion who shares one's activities or is a fellow member of an organization.|例句：He fought alongside his comrades in the war. 他和他的战友们并肩作战。|例句：She valued the support of her comrades in the struggle for equal rights.  她在争取平等权利的斗争中珍视同志们的支持。 |近义词：companion, friend, colleague, associate, peer|反义词:enemy, foe, opponent|词性:noun

词源:
From French *camarade*, from Spanish *camarada*, from *cámara* (“chamber, room”). Ultimately from Late Latin *camera*. Doublet of *chamber*.
