cameoing:/ˈkæmioʊɪŋ/
| v. |客串；短暂亮相|Making a brief appearance, especially as a famous person in a film, play, or television programme in which they do not have a major part.|例句：The famous actor cameoed in the new movie.  这位著名演员在新电影中客串了一下。|例句：She cameoed as a waitress in the play. 她在剧中客串了一个服务员的角色。|近义词：appearing, featuring, guest starring|反义词: starring, leading|词性:动词

cameo:/ˈkæmioʊ/
| n. |客串；（尤指名演员在电影、戏剧中）短暂露面；浮雕宝石|A brief appearance of a well-known person in a film, play, or television programme in which they do not have a major part; a piece of jewellery, typically oval in shape, consisting of a portrait in profile carved in relief on a background of a different colour.|例句：He made a cameo in the film. 他在电影中客串了一下。|例句：She wore a beautiful cameo brooch. 她戴着一个漂亮的浮雕宝石胸针。|近义词：appearance, bit part, walk-on|反义词: lead role|词性:名词
