amorous: /ˈæmərəs/ | adj. | 多情的，恋爱的，怀春的 | Showing, feeling, or relating to sexual desire. | She gave him an amorous glance. 她多情地瞥了他一眼。 |  He cast an amorous look at the waitress. 他对女服务员投以爱慕的目光。| 近义词: loving, affectionate, passionate, ardent, romantic | 反义词: frigid, platonic, indifferent | 词性: 形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French amorous, from amors ‘love’ (from Latin amor), influenced by Latin amorosus.
