middleclass:/ˈmɪdl ˌklæs/| adj. n.|中产阶级的，中产阶级|Relating to or characteristic of the social class between the working class and the upper class, typically with comfortable incomes, good education, and some property.|例句：They live in a middleclass neighborhood. 他们住在中产阶级社区。|近义词：bourgeois, middle-income|反义词：working-class, upper-class|词性:形容词,名词
Etymology: From Middle English *middel classe*, from middel + classe.  Ultimately from Latin *medius* (middle) and *classis* (class).
