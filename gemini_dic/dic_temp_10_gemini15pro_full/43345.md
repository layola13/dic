crampons: /ˈkræmpɒnz/ (UK) /ˈkræmpɑːnz/ (US) | n. | 登山 crampons; 冰爪 | A pair of metal frames with spikes, attached to boots for walking on ice or snow. |  He strapped on his crampons before tackling the icy slope. 他在攀登冰坡前系上了冰爪。|  Mountaineers use crampons to climb icy mountains. 登山者使用冰爪攀登冰山。 | synonyms: ice cleats, spikes | antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "regular boots") | noun
