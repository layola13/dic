categorizes:/ˈkætəɡəˌraɪzɪz/
verb
分类，归类
divides into categories; classifies.
The librarian categorizes the books by genre. 图书管理员按体裁对书籍进行分类。
The system automatically categorizes incoming emails. 系统自动对收到的电子邮件进行分类。
classify, sort, group, arrange, pigeonhole
decategorize, randomize
v.
