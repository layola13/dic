nicknackery: /ˈnɪknækəri/ (US), /ˈnɪknækəri/ (UK)| n. | 小摆设，小玩意儿；廉价的装饰品 | A collection of small, cheap ornaments or trinkets. |  Her shelves were filled with nicknackery from her travels. 她的架子上摆满了她旅行时收集的小玩意儿。

| Example: The shop sold a variety of nicknackery, from miniature porcelain dolls to brightly colored keychains.  这家商店出售各种各样的小玩意儿，从微型瓷娃娃到色彩鲜艳的钥匙链。| Synonym: trinkets, knick-knacks, baubles, gewgaws, bibelots | Antonym: valuables, treasures | noun
