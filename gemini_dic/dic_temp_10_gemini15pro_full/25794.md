burette:/bjuːˈret/ | n. |滴定管|A graduated glass tube with a tap at one end, for delivering known volumes of a liquid, especially in titrations. |The chemist filled the burette with the titrant. 化学家将滴定剂装入滴定管。|Synonyms:  graduated tube, measuring tube|Antonyms:  N/A (It's a specific piece of equipment, not a concept with an opposite)|noun
