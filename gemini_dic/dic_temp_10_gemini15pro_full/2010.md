actualizing: /ˈæktʃuəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
实现；使现实化；使潜能得以发挥
The process of making something real or giving it practical form.  Turning a potential into reality.
She is actualizing her dream of becoming a doctor. 她正在实现她成为一名医生的梦想。

He is finally actualizing his potential as a writer. 他终于发挥了他作为一名作家的潜力。

近义词: realizing, fulfilling, achieving, manifesting
反义词: neglecting, ignoring, hindering
词性: verb (present participle)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin actualis "active, practical" (from actus "act," past participle of agere "to do, drive, lead, act;" see act (n.)) + -ize.  The meaning "make real, turn into fact" is from 1889.
