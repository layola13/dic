blogosphere:/ˈblɒɡəsfɪə(r)/ /ˈblɑːɡəsfɪr/| n. |博客圈；博客世界|The blogosphere has been buzzing about the latest celebrity scandal.  博客圈一直在议论最新的名人丑闻。|The news spread quickly through the blogosphere. 这条消息迅速传遍了整个博客圈。|近义词：blogging community, online community|反义词:  (没有明确的反义词)|词性:名词

Etymology->
blog + sphere.  "Blog" is a shortened form of "weblog," a website containing a writer's or group of writers' own experiences, observations, opinions, etc., and often having images and links to other websites. "Sphere" refers to a particular environment or walk of life.
