fabulist:/ˈfæbjəlɪst/| n.|寓言家；说谎者|A person who writes or tells fables; a liar, especially a habitual liar. |He was known as a fabulist, his stories often blurring the line between truth and fiction. 他被称为寓言家，他的故事常常模糊了真相和虚构的界限。|近义词：liar, storyteller, fibber, prevaricator|反义词：truth-teller, honest person|词性:noun

Etymology: From French *fabuliste*, from Latin *fabula* ("story, tale").
