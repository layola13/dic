nontraceability:/ˌnɒntreɪsəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可追溯性 | The state or quality of being impossible to trace or track. |  The cryptocurrency's nontraceability makes it attractive to criminals. 这种加密货币的不可追溯性使其对犯罪分子很有吸引力。 |  anonymity, untraceability | traceability, identifiability | noun

Etymology:
non- (prefix): not
traceable (adjective): able to be traced
-ity (suffix): forms nouns denoting quality or condition
