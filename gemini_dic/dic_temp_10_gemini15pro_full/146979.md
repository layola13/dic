overjoyously:/ˌoʊvərˈdʒɔɪəsli/| adv.|欣喜若狂地，非常高兴地|In a manner expressing extreme joy and happiness.|She laughed overjoyously at the news. 她听到这个消息欣喜若狂地大笑起来。|Synonyms: ecstatically, rapturously, jubilantly|Antonyms: sadly, sorrowfully, dejectedly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From over- + joyously, from joyous + -ly.  Ultimately from Old French joie.
