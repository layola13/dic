misarticulated: /ˌmɪsɑːrˈtɪkjʊleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 发音不清的，吐字不清的 | Pronounced incorrectly, usually due to a speech impediment or lack of clarity. |  The child misarticulated several words, making it difficult to understand him.  这个孩子几个词发音不清，很难听懂他的话。 | Synonyms: slurred, mumbled, garbled, inarticulate | Antonyms: articulate, clear, distinct | adjective

misarticulate: /ˌmɪsɑːrˈtɪkjʊleɪt/ | v. | 发音不清，吐字不清 | To pronounce (a word or words) incorrectly. | He misarticulated the word "sixth," pronouncing it "sixt." 他把“sixth”这个词发音发错了，发成了“sixt”。 | Synonyms: slur, mumble, garble | Antonyms: articulate, enunciate | verb
