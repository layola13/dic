burstwort: /ˈbɜːrstwɜːrt/
noun
爆果植物; 弹射种子植物
A plant whose ripe seed pods burst open suddenly, scattering the seeds.
The burstwort scattered its seeds across the meadow. 爆果植物把种子撒满草地。

近义词: touch-me-not, squirting cucumber
反义词: N/A
词性: n.

Etymology:
From 'burst' + 'wort' (a plant).  'Burst' refers to the explosive opening of the seed pods, and 'wort' is an Old English word meaning 'plant' or 'herb.'
