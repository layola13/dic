befriendment:/bɪˈfrɛndmənt/
n.
友善；友好；交友
The act of becoming friends with someone.
例句：Her befriendment of the lonely child was a kind gesture. 她和那个孤独的孩子交朋友是一个善意的举动。

例句：The befriendment program at the senior center helps combat isolation.  老年中心的交友项目有助于对抗孤独。

近义词：friendship, companionship, amity
反义词：estrangement, alienation, hostility
词性：名词
