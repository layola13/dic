overdebated:/ˌoʊvərdiˈbeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过度辩论的，被过度讨论的 |  Having been debated or discussed excessively, often to the point of exhaustion or futility. | The issue of school uniforms is overdebated and frankly, quite tiresome. 校服的问题已经被过度讨论了，坦白说，很烦人。 | The merits of online learning have been overdebated during the pandemic.  在线学习的优点在疫情期间被过度讨论了。 | 近义词: overdiscussed, overworked, exhausted | 反义词: underexamined, unexplored, neglected | 词性: adjective

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "debated" (past participle of "debate")  "over-"（表示“过度”的前缀）+ "debated"（"debate"的过去分词）
