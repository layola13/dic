consentive:/kənˈsɛntɪv/ | adj. | 同意的，一致的 |  Relating to or expressing consent; agreed upon. |  The consentive nature of the agreement ensured a smooth transaction.  该协议的同意性质确保了交易顺利进行。| 近义词: consensual, accordant, agreeable | 反义词: dissenting, objecting, opposing | 词性: adjective

consentive:/kənˈsɛntɪv/ | n. | 同意者 | One who consents. | The consentives signed the document. 同意者在文件上签了字。 | 近义词: approver, assenter | 反义词: dissenter, objector | 词性: noun


Etymology: From Late Latin *consentivus, from Latin consentire "to agree," from com- "together" + sentire "to feel."
