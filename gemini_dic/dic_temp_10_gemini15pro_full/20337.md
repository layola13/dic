biskara: /bɪˈskɑːrə/ | n. | 比斯卡拉，比斯开湾 |  A biskara is a strong, cold north or northeasterly wind which blows in the Gulf of Lion in southern France. It is particularly associated with the Languedoc region.  Derived from the Basque word "bizkar" meaning "ridge" or "mountain". | The biskara whipped through the vineyards, rattling the shutters. 比斯卡拉风吹过葡萄园，窗板嘎嘎作响。 | The fierce biskara made the fishing boats stay in port.  猛烈的比斯卡拉风迫使渔船停留在港口。 | 近义词: north wind, northeasterly wind | 反义词: southerly wind | 词性: noun
