buccinum:/bʌˈkaɪnəm/
noun
whelk; a type of sea snail
A large, edible marine gastropod mollusk with a strong, spiral shell. Buccinum is a genus of sea snails, commonly known as whelks, within the family Buccinidae.
The beachcomber found a beautiful buccinum shell amongst the seaweed.  海滩拾荒者在海藻中发现了一个美丽的蛾螺壳。
synonyms:whelk, conch
