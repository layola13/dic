fascia:/ˈfæʃə/ (US), /ˈfæʃɪə/ (UK)| n. |筋膜；饰带；招牌|A thin sheath of fibrous tissue enclosing a muscle or other organ.|The surgeon cut through the fascia to reach the muscle.  外科医生切开筋膜以到达肌肉。|The fascia of the building was decorated with intricate carvings.  这栋建筑的饰带装饰着复杂的雕刻。|Synonyms: covering, membrane, band, strip|Antonyms: core, interior|noun


fascias:/ˈfæʃiːiːz/ (plural of fascia)| n. |筋膜（复数）；饰带（复数）；招牌（复数）|Plural form of fascia, referring to multiple thin sheaths of fibrous tissue or multiple decorative bands.|The fascias of the muscles were inflamed. 肌肉的筋膜发炎了。|The building's fascias were repainted.  建筑物的饰带重新粉刷了。|Synonyms: coverings, membranes, bands, strips|Antonyms: cores, interiors|noun
