fenian:/ˈfiːniən/ | n. | 芬尼党人; (蔑称)爱尔兰天主教徒 | A member of a 19th-century Irish nationalist organization committed to achieving Irish independence from Britain, often by violent means. Also used as a derogatory term for Irish Catholics. | He was branded a Fenian by the British authorities. 他被英国当局称为芬尼党人。| synonyms: Irish nationalist, rebel, revolutionary | antonyms: loyalist | noun

fenian:/ˈfiːniən/ | adj. | 芬尼党人的; 爱尔兰民族主义的 | Relating to the Fenian Brotherhood or its aims. | The Fenian uprising was quickly suppressed. 芬尼党人的起义很快就被镇压了。| synonyms: Irish republican, nationalist | antonyms: loyalist, unionist | adjective
