glories:/ˈɡlɔːriz/ /ˈɡlɔːris/| n.|荣耀，光荣；壮丽，辉煌|Something that inspires admiration or pride; magnificence.|The setting sun cast its glories across the sky. 落日余晖洒满了天空。|Synonyms: splendors, magnificence, grandeur, brilliance|Antonyms: shame, disgrace|noun

glories:/ˈɡlɔːriz/ /ˈɡlɔːris/| v. (third-person singular simple present glories, present participle glorying, simple past and past participle gloried)|为...而自豪，得意，荣耀|To take great pride or pleasure in.|He gloried in his victory. 他为自己的胜利而自豪。|Synonyms: revel, delight, exult|Antonyms: mourn, grieve|verb
