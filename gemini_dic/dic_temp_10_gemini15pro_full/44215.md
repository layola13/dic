crocidolite:/kroʊˈsɪdəˌlaɪt/| n. |青石棉，蓝石棉|A fibrous mineral of the amphibole group, a form of asbestos, typically blue in color and considered particularly dangerous. It is a type of asbestos known for its high tensile strength and resistance to heat and chemicals, but it is also highly carcinogenic.|例句：Exposure to crocidolite is a major risk factor for mesothelioma.  接触青石棉是间皮瘤的主要风险因素。

例句：Crocidolite is a type of asbestos that is now banned in many countries. 青石棉是一种现在在许多国家被禁止的石棉。|近义词：blue asbestos,riebeckite asbestos|反义词: None (no direct antonym for a specific mineral)|词性:noun
