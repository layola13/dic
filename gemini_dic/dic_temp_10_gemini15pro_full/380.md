abime:/əˈbiːm/| n.|深渊，深坑，无底洞|A seemingly bottomless chasm or pit.  A profound depth or void.|The climber peered into the abime. 攀登者凝视着深渊。|近义词：abyss, chasm, gulf, void|反义词:summit, peak|noun

USAGE->
Example Sentences:

The hiker fell into a deep abime. =  这名徒步旅行者掉进了一个深深的深渊。

His heart was an abime of despair. = 他的内心是绝望的深渊。

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *abisme*, from Latin abyssus, from Greek abyssos "bottomless," from a- "without" + byssos "bottom, depth."  Ultimately from the same Proto-Indo-European root that gives us "bottom."
