nonvictories:/ˌnɒnˈvɪktəriːz/
noun
非胜利，未取得的胜利
lack of victories; failures or draws where a victory was hoped for or expected.
The team's string of nonvictories led to the coach's dismissal.  这支队伍一连串的非胜利导致了教练的解雇。
Near synonyms: losses, defeats, failures, draws, setbacks
Antonyms: victories, wins, triumphs
