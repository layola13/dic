multicorporation: /ˌmʌltiˌkɔːrpəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 多公司; 多法人公司 | A large business entity consisting of multiple incorporated companies, often operating in diverse industries or geographical regions.  It may refer to a conglomerate or a holding company structure. |  Example 1: The multicorporation expanded its reach by acquiring smaller companies in emerging markets. 这家多公司通过收购新兴市场的较小公司扩大了其业务范围。

Example 2:  Managing a multicorporation requires complex strategies and coordination across various subsidiaries. 管理一家多公司需要复杂的战略和跨各个子公司的协调。 | 近义词: conglomerate, multinational corporation, holding company | 反义词: small business, single-entity company | 词性: noun
