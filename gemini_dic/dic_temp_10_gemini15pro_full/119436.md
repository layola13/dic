microlepidoptera:/ˌmaɪkroʊˌlɛpɪˈdɑːptərə/ | n. | 小蛾类，微蛾类 | Small moths, typically having a wingspan of less than 20 mm, belonging to several superfamilies formerly considered a single group. | Microlepidoptera are often overlooked due to their small size. 小蛾类由于体型小，经常被忽视。| synonyms: micro-moths | antonyms: macrolepidoptera | noun
