interadaptation:/ˌɪntərˌædæpˈteɪʃən/ | n. |相互适应|The mutual adaptation of two or more species, organs, or functions to one another.|例句：The interadaptation of organisms within an ecosystem is essential for its stability.  生态系统内生物的相互适应对其稳定性至关重要。

例句：The interadaptation of the software and hardware allowed for optimal performance. 软件和硬件的相互适应实现了最佳性能。|近义词：coadaptation, coevolution, symbiosis|反义词:incompatibility, conflict|词性:名词
