nonarrogances: /nɒnˈærəɡənsɪz/
noun
非傲慢；谦逊
lack of arrogance; humility
Her nonarrogance and willingness to learn made her a beloved colleague. 她谦逊好学的品质使她成为一位受人爱戴的同事。
Synonyms: humility, modesty, humbleness
Antonyms: arrogance, haughtiness, conceit
