honed:/hoʊnd/| v. |磨练，提高，使…更完美|Made by or adapted for a particular purpose. Her skills were honed over years of practice. 她的技能是经过多年练习磨练出来的。|例句：He honed his skills as a writer.  他磨练了自己作为作家的技能。

例句：The blade was honed to razor sharpness.  刀刃被磨得锋利无比。|近义词：sharpened, refined, perfected, improved|反义词:dulled, blunted|词性:动词

词源: From Old English *hān "stone," related to Old Norse hein "whetstone." Originally meant "to sharpen with a whetstone."
