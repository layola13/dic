disintegratory:/dɪsˈɪntɪɡrətɔːri/
adjective
崩解的；瓦解的
Causing or tending to cause disintegration.  Breaking something down into its component parts, destroying its unity or cohesion.
The disintegratory forces of the explosion shattered the building. 爆炸的崩解力震碎了大楼。
The disintegratory effects of the disease were devastating. 这种疾病的瓦解性影响是毁灭性的。

近义词:destructive, disruptive, decomposing
反义词: constructive, unifying, cohesive
词性:adjective

词源: From disintegrate + -ory.  Disintegrate comes from dis- (expressing negation, reversal) + integrate (from Latin integratus, past participle of integrare "to make whole").
