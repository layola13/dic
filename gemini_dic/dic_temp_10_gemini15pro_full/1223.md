accompanied: /əˈkʌmpənid/
verb
伴随，陪同；与…同时发生；为…伴奏
to go somewhere with someone or something; to happen or exist at the same time as something else; to play a musical instrument with someone else who is singing or playing the main tune
She accompanied me to the doctor. 她陪我去看了医生。
The storm was accompanied by heavy rain. 暴风雨伴随着大雨。
近义词: attend, escort, follow, go with
反义词: leave, abandon
词性: v.

accompanied: /əˈkʌmpənid/
adjective
伴随的，附带的
existing or happening together with something else
The letter was accompanied by a photograph. 这封信附有一张照片。
His speech was accompanied by loud applause. 他的演讲伴随着热烈的掌声。
近义词: associated, connected, related
反义词: separate, detached
词性: adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *acompaignier, from compaignon "companion," ultimately from Late Latin companio "one who shares bread," from Latin com- "with" + panis "bread."


USAGE->

过去完成时:
