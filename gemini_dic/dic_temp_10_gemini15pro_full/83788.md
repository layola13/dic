hairinesses:/ˈhɛəriˌnɪsɪz/
noun
多毛；毛茸茸的状态
The state or quality of being hairy; excessive hair growth.
The hairinesses of the caterpillars made them unpleasant to touch. 这些毛毛虫的毛茸茸的让人摸起来很不舒服。
The dog's hairinesses increased during the winter months. 这只狗在冬季的毛发变得更浓密了。
近义词: hirsuteness, villosity, shagginess
反义词: smoothness, baldness
词性: noun

Etymology:
From hairy + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  "Hairy" comes from Old English *hǣrig, from *hǣr (hair).
