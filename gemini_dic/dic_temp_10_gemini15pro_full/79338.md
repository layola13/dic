gleed:/ɡliːd/
n.
炽热的煤块; 余烬
A live coal; an ember.
例句：The blacksmith pulled a gleed from the forge. 铁匠从锻炉里拉出一块燃烧的煤。

例句：The dying embers, or gleeds, glowed softly in the hearth.  将熄的余烬，或称炽热的煤块，在壁炉里发出柔和的光芒。

近义词：ember, coal, cinder
反义词：ash

词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English *glede, from Old English glēd (“glowing coal, ember”), from Proto-Germanic *glōdiz (“glowing coal”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰel- (“to shine, burn, glow”). Cognate with Scots gled (“kite; live coal”), West Frisian gleed (“ember”), Dutch gloed (“glow, ardour”), German Glut (“glow, embers”), Danish glød (“glow, embers”), Swedish glöd (“glow, embers”), Icelandic glóð (“glow, embers”).
