iatrochemistry:/ˌaɪˌætroʊˈkɛmɪstri/ | n. | 医药化学；医疗化学 |  A historical approach to medicine that emphasized chemical remedies and explanations for disease, especially prevalent in the 16th and 17th centuries. | Paracelsus is considered a major figure in the development of iatrochemistry.  帕拉塞尔苏斯被认为是医药化学发展的重要人物。 |  Iatrochemistry played a significant role in the transition from alchemy to modern chemistry. 医药化学在从炼金术到现代化学的过渡中发挥了重要作用。 | synonyms:  chemiatry, medical chemistry | antonyms:  natural medicine, herbalism | noun
