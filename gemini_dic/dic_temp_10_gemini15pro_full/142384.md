orthosilicic:/ˌɔːrθəʊsɪˈlɪsɪk/ |adj.|原硅酸的|Relating to or containing silicon with a valence of four, specifically in the form of Si(OH)₄.  It refers to the unstable, unpolymerized form of silicic acid. |Orthosilicic acid is the predominant form of silicon found in natural waters. 原硅酸是天然水中硅的主要存在形式。|  Dietary supplements often contain orthosilicic acid as a source of bioavailable silicon. 膳食补充剂通常含有原硅酸作为生物可利用硅的来源。|Near synonyms: silicic, tetrahydroxylsilane |Antonyms: N/A | Word type: adjective
