formalizes:/ˈfɔːməlaɪzɪz/
verb
使正式化；正式确定；使…形式化
Makes something official or definite; gives something a fixed structure or form.
The agreement formalizes the relationship between the two companies. 该协议使两家公司之间的关系正式化。
The new rules formalize the procedures for handling complaints. 新规则正式确定了处理投诉的程序。
synonyms: codify, systematize, regularize, standardize, institutionalize
antonyms: informalize, loosen, relax
词性: 动词
