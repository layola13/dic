overlearnednesses:/ˌoʊvərˈlɜːrndnɪsɪz/
n.
过度学习
The quality or state of being overlearned.  A high degree of proficiency or automaticity resulting from extensive practice beyond the point of initial mastery.
His overlearnednesses in mathematics allowed him to solve complex equations effortlessly. 他对数学的过度学习使他能够毫不费力地解决复杂的方程式。
近义词：automaticity, mastery, proficiency
反义词：unfamiliarity, inexperience
