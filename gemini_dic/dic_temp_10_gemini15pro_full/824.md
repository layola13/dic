abstersion:/æbˈstɜːrʃən/
noun
清洗，擦净
the act of cleansing or wiping away
The abstersion of the wound was crucial to prevent infection.  伤口的清洗对于防止感染至关重要。
Surgical abstersion is the first step in many medical procedures.  外科清洗是许多医疗程序的第一步。
synonym: cleansing, purification, washing, wiping
antonym: contamination, soiling
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin abstersiōn-, abstersiō, from abstersus, past participle of abstergēre "to wipe off," from ab- "off, away from" + tergēre "to wipe."


USAGE->
The abstersion of the dirt from the shoes was a necessary task.  从鞋子上擦掉污垢是必要的任务。

The surgeon performed a thorough abstersion of the area before the operation.  外科医生在手术前对该区域进行了彻底的清洁。
