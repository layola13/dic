overexpansion:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspænʃən/ | n. | 过度扩张，过度膨胀 | An expansion that is excessive or beyond reasonable limits.  The act of expanding something too much. | The company's overexpansion into new markets led to significant financial losses.  这家公司过度扩张到新市场导致了巨大的经济损失。 | overextension, inflation, surplus, glut | contraction, underdevelopment, recession | noun
