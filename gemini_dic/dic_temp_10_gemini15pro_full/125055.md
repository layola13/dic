muleteers:/ˌmjuːləˈtɪərz/
noun
赶骡人；骡夫
A person who drives mules.
The muleteers led their animals along the narrow mountain path. 赶骡人沿着狭窄的山路赶着他们的骡子。
mule drivers, teamsters
(No clear antonym)
