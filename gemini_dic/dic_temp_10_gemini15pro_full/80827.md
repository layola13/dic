gouaches:/ˈɡwɑːʃɪz/ /ɡuˈɑːʃɪz/
noun
不透明水彩颜料; 水粉画;
Opaque watercolors; paintings done with opaque watercolors.
The artist used vibrant gouaches to create a stunning landscape.  这位艺术家用鲜艳的水粉颜料创作了一幅令人惊叹的风景画。
The exhibition showcased a series of breathtaking gouaches.  展览展示了一系列令人叹为观止的水粉画。
synonyms:opaque watercolors, poster paints
antonyms:watercolors, acrylics
n.
