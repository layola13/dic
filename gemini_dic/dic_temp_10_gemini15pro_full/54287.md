disgulf:/dɪsˈɡʌlf/ | v. |  使脱离困境；解救；释放 | To free from engulfment; to release from a difficult or overwhelming situation. | The lifeboat disgulfed the sailors from the sinking ship. 救生艇将水手们从沉没的船上解救出来。 | The tow truck disgulfed the car from the mud. 拖车把汽车从泥里拉了出来。 | 近义词: extricate, rescue, liberate, deliver | 反义词: engulf, submerge, overwhelm | 词性: 动词

词源:  dis- (prefix meaning "reversal or removal") + gulf (noun meaning "a deep inlet of the sea," figuratively "a wide gap or chasm")
