overgraciousness:/ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪʃəsnəs/| n. | 过分殷勤，过分客气 | Excessive graciousness; being too polite or kind, often to the point of insincerity. |  Her overgraciousness made me suspicious of her motives. 她的过分殷勤让我怀疑她的动机。|  He displayed an almost embarrassing overgraciousness to the visiting dignitaries. 他对来访的贵宾表现出近乎令人尴尬的过分殷勤。 | 近义词: obsequiousness, fawning,  | 反义词: rudeness, impoliteness | 词性: 名词
