commending:/kəˈmɛndɪŋ/ | adj. | 值得称赞的；推荐的 | Present participle of *commend* meaning praising or recommending something or someone. |  The teacher wrote a commending letter of recommendation for the student. 老师为学生写了一封推荐信。| Synonyms:  laudatory, praiseworthy, approving | Antonyms: critical, disapproving, censuring | Verb (present participle)

commend:/kəˈmɛnd/ | vt. |  称赞；推荐；委托；把…托付给 | To praise formally or officially; to present as worthy of attention or notice; to entrust for care or preservation. | I commend your bravery. 我赞扬你的勇气。 The waiter commended the steak to us. 服务员向我们推荐了牛排。 | Synonyms: praise, applaud, endorse, entrust | Antonyms: criticize, condemn, denounce | Verb
