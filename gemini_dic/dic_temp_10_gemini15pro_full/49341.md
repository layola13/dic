delineate:/dɪˈlɪnieɪt/
verb
描绘，描述，勾勒
To describe or portray something precisely, often by drawing or writing.
The report clearly delineates the steps involved in the process.  这份报告清晰地描述了这个过程中的步骤。
The artist delineated the woman's features with great skill.  这位艺术家非常熟练地勾勒了这位女性的面部特征。

近义词:describe, portray, depict, outline, sketch, define
反义词:obfuscate, confuse, obscure
词性:v.
