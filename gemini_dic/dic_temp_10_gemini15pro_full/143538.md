outguns:/ˌaʊtˈɡʌn/
verb
胜过，以火力压制
defeat or overcome by having better weapons or greater firepower than.
The rebels outgunned the government troops. 叛军以强大的火力压制了政府军。
outmatch, surpass, exceed, outdo, outstrip, outclass
be outgunned by, be inferior to
v.
