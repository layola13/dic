guise: /ɡaɪz/ | n. | 外观，伪装，幌子 | An external form, appearance, or manner of presentation, typically concealing the true nature of something. | She attended the party in the guise of a pirate. 她打扮成海盗的样子参加了聚会。 |  He entered the country under the guise of a tourist. 他以游客的身份进入了这个国家。

| 近义词: appearance, semblance, pretense, disguise, mask | 反义词: reality, truth | 词性: noun
