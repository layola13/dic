conversed:/kənˈvɜːrst/| v.|交谈，谈话|engaged in conversation.|We conversed for hours about our shared interests. 我们就共同的兴趣聊了几个小时。|近义词：talked, chatted, discussed, spoke|反义词：remained silent, listened|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *conversen*, from Old French *converser*, from Latin *conversārī* ("to live with, keep company with, dwell with, be busy with, associate with, frequent, practice"), present active infinitive of *conversor* ("I dwell, live, I am engaged in, I am busy with"), frequentative of *convertō* ("I turn around, turn back, return, convert, transform"), from *com-* ("together, with") + *vertō* ("I turn").
