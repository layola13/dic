compacting:/kəmˈpæktɪŋ/ | v. | 压实；压缩；使紧密 | making something smaller or denser by pressing it together | The machine is compacting the soil.  这台机器正在压实土壤。 |  The workers were compacting the trash in the landfill. 工人们正在垃圾填埋场压实垃圾。 | synonyms: compressing, condensing, consolidating | antonyms: expanding, loosening, separating | verb

compacting:/kəmˈpæktɪŋ/ | adj. | 压实的；紧凑的；简洁的 | designed to be small or take up little space |  a compacting garbage truck  一辆压缩式垃圾车。 | This new laptop is compacting and easy to carry. 这款新笔记本电脑小巧易携带。 | synonyms: compact, dense, compressed | antonyms: large, bulky, expansive | adjective
