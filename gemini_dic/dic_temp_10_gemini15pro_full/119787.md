migrainous:/maɪˈɡreɪnəs/ /maɪˈɡreɪnɪs/| adj.|偏头痛的，患偏头痛的|Relating to or resembling migraine; suffering from or subject to migraines.|She had a migrainous headache that lasted for days. 她偏头痛持续了好几天。|近义词：migraine-related, migraine-prone|反义词: healthy, well|词性:形容词

migrainous:/maɪˈɡreɪnəs/ /maɪˈɡreɪnɪs/| n.|偏头痛患者|A person who suffers from migraines. (This usage is less common.)|He's a migrainous and has to avoid certain foods. 他是偏头痛患者，必须避免某些食物。|近义词：migraine sufferer|反义词: healthy individual|词性:名词 (较少使用)
