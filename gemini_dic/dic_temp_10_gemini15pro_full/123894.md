mordacious:/mɔːrˈdeɪʃəs/ | adj. | 尖刻的；讽刺的；辛辣的 | Given to biting or sarcastic remarks; incisive, cutting.  Characterized by a sharp or biting wit. | Her mordacious wit made her a feared opponent in debates. 她尖刻的机智使她成为辩论中令人畏惧的对手。 | Synonyms: biting, sarcastic, caustic, trenchant, cutting, acrimonious, sharp, incisive, pungent, virulent | Antonyms: kind, gentle, complimentary, flattering, mild, bland | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin *mordāx* (stem *mordāc-), from *mordēre* "to bite."
