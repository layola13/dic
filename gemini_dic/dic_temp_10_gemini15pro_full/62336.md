endemical: /ɛnˈdɛmɪk(ə)l/ | adj. | 地方性的；特有的；流行的 | Native or restricted to a certain country or area; regularly found among particular people or in a certain area. | Malaria is endemic in many hot countries. 疟疾在许多炎热的国家流行。 | Synonyms: native, indigenous, aboriginal, local | Antonyms: exotic, foreign, alien | adjective

endemicity: /ˌɛndɛˈmɪsɪti/ | n. | 地方性；特有性；流行程度 | The fact or condition of being endemic. | The endemicity of malaria in the region is a major public health concern. 该地区疟疾的流行是一个主要的公共卫生问题。 | Synonyms: prevalence, incidence, occurrence | Antonyms: rarity, absence | noun
