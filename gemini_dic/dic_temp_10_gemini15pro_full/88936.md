holding-point:/ˈhəʊldɪŋ pɔɪnt/| n.|集结待命点；临时停留点；暂停点|A designated place where troops, vehicles, or supplies are assembled and held ready for further movement or action.  A point in a process or activity where progress is temporarily halted.|The convoy reached the holding-point and awaited further instructions.  车队到达了集结待命点，等待进一步指示。 The project reached a holding-point due to funding issues.  由于资金问题，该项目暂停了。|近义词：rendezvous, assembly point, staging area, waystation, stop, pause|反义词:destination, terminal|词性:noun
