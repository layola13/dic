corrosively:/kəˈroʊsɪvli/
adverb
腐蚀性地；侵蚀性地
in a corrosively destructive manner.
Acid rain corrosively affects stone buildings. 酸雨对石头建筑物有腐蚀作用。
Synonyms: destructively, harmfully, damagingly
Antonyms: beneficially, harmlessly, constructively
词性: 副词
