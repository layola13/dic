awestruck: /ˈɔːstrʌk/ | adj. | 充满敬畏的，惊叹的 | Filled with awe; deeply impressed and often reverential. |  The tourists were awestruck by the grandeur of the Grand Canyon. 游客们被大峡谷的壮丽景色所震撼。

| Example:  The children were awestruck by the magician's performance. 孩子们被魔术师的表演惊呆了。

| Example:  She stood awestruck before the majestic waterfall. 她敬畏地站在雄伟的瀑布前。


| Synonym: amazed, astonished, wonderstruck, dumbfounded, awe-inspired

| Antonym: unimpressed, indifferent, apathetic

| Word type: adjective
