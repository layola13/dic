blamers:/ˈbleɪmərz/| n.|指责者，责备者|People who assign blame or responsibility for something negative.  Those who find fault or criticize others.|The blamers were quick to point fingers after the project failed.  项目失败后，指责者们迅速开始指责别人。|critics, faultfinders, accusers|supporters, defenders|noun
