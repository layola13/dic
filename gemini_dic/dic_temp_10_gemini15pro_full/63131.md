ensnare:/ɪnˈsneər/ /ɛnˈsneər/
verb
诱捕；使陷入圈套；使入迷
to catch or trap something, especially an animal, in a snare or net; to trap someone by tricking them
The fox was ensnared in the hunter's trap. 狐狸被猎人的陷阱困住了。
Her beauty ensnared many a young man. 她的美貌迷住了许多年轻人。
近义词:trap, catch, tangle, entrap, enmesh
反义词:release, free, liberate
词性:verb

词源:
From en- (meaning "in," "into," or "on") + snare (a trap for catching small animals).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *snarōną.
