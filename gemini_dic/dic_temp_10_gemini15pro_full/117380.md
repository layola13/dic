mediatorships:/ˌmiːdiˈeɪtərʃɪps/
n.
调解；调停；斡旋
The act of serving as a mediator; the process of mediating a dispute.
The UN played a key role in the mediatorships between the warring factions. 联合国在交战各派之间的调解中发挥了关键作用。

The success of the mediatorships depends on the willingness of both sides to compromise.  调解的成功取决于双方妥协的意愿。

近义词: mediation, arbitration, conciliation, intervention
反义词: conflict, dispute, disagreement
词性: noun
