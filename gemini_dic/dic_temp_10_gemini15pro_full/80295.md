goitrous:/ˈɡɔɪtrəs/| adj.|甲状腺肿的；患甲状腺肿的|Affected with or characterized by goiter; swollen.|例句：The doctor diagnosed her as having a goitrous thyroid. 医生诊断她患有甲状腺肿。|例句：The region was known for its high incidence of goitrous individuals. 这个地区以甲状腺肿患者的高发病率而闻名。|近义词：thyroidal, hyperthyroid|反义词: healthy, normal|词性: adjective

词源:
Late Middle English: from Old French goitreux, from goitre ‘goiter’.
