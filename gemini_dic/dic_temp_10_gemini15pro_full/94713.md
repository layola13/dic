improperly:/ɪmˈprɒpəli/ (US) /ɪmˈprɑːpərli/ (UK)| adv.|不正确地，不适当地|Not correctly; in a way that is not suitable or appropriate.|He used the tool improperly and broke it. 他不正确地使用了工具，把它弄坏了。|The funds were improperly managed. 资金管理不当。|近义词：incorrectly, wrongly, inappropriately, unsuitably|反义词：correctly, properly, appropriately, suitably|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + proper + -ly (adverb suffix).  "Proper" comes from Old French "propre" meaning "one's own, special, particular," ultimately from Latin "proprius."
