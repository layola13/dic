hokey:/ˈhoʊki/| adj.|做作的，不自然的，老套的|Something that is hokey is overly sentimental, mawkish, or obviously contrived.|The plot of the movie was so hokey, I could predict the ending within the first ten minutes. 这部电影的情节太老套了，我十分钟内就能猜到结局。|近义词：corny, cheesy, clichéd, sentimental, contrived|反义词：genuine, sincere, authentic|词性:形容词

hokey:/ˈhoʊki/| n.|骗人的东西，廉价的玩意儿 (较少用)|A hokey is something cheap or falsely attractive, often used to deceive.|The carnival games were all hokeys designed to take your money.  嘉年华上的游戏都是些骗钱的玩意儿。|近义词：gimmick, trick, sham|反义词：genuine article|词性:名词
