ostreaculture:/ˈɒstriəkʌltʃər/ (US) /ˈɔːstriəkʌltʃər/ (UK)| n. |牡蛎养殖；牡蛎养殖业|The cultivation of oysters, especially for commercial purposes.  It involves various techniques for breeding, raising, and harvesting oysters.|Oyster farming is a form of ostreaculture. 牡蛎养殖是牡蛎养殖的一种形式。| 近义词: oyster farming, shellfish farming | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

Etymology:
From Latin *ostrea* (oyster) +  -culture (cultivation).
