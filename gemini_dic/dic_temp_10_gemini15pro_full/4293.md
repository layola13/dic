airy-fairy:/ˈɛəri ˈfɛəri/ | adj. | 不切实际的，异想天开的，不着边际的 | Not practical or realistic; whimsical, vague, impractical. |  She has all these airy-fairy ideas about changing the world, but no concrete plans. 她有一堆关于改变世界的不切实际的想法，却没有具体的计划。| Example: He dismissed her suggestions as airy-fairy nonsense. 他认为她的建议是异想天开的废话。| Synonyms: whimsical, fanciful, impractical, unrealistic, dreamy, vague | Antonyms: practical, realistic, down-to-earth, pragmatic | adjective
