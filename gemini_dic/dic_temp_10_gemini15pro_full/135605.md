nonpossessively:/ˌnɑːnpəˈzɛsɪvli/
adv.
非占有地，不带所属关系地
In a way that does not indicate possession or ownership.
She used the car nonpossessively, understanding it belonged to her parents. 她以非占有的方式使用这辆车，明白它属于她的父母。
近义词: neutrally, objectively
反义词: possessively

词性: 副词
