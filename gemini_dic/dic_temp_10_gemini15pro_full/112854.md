lucking:/ˈlʌkɪŋ/ | adj. | 幸运的；侥幸的；带来好运的 | Having good luck; fortunate. | He was lucking out when he found a $20 bill on the sidewalk. 他在人行道上发现一张 20 美元的钞票，真是走运。 | 近义词: fortunate, lucky, blessed, charmed | 反义词: unlucky, unfortunate, cursed, jinxed | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From the verb "luck" (meaning "to have good fortune"), which comes from Middle Dutch *luc* (luck, fortune), possibly from Proto-Germanic *luk- (luck, fortune, happiness). The "-ing" suffix transforms the verb into a present participle, which can function as an adjective.

注意事项:

"lucking" 虽然可以作为形容词使用，但相对少见，通常用作现在分词，表示一种正在进行的状态或动作。
