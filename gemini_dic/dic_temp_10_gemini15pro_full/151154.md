paraventricular: /ˌpærəvɛnˈtrɪkjʊlər/
adjective
室旁的，傍室的
Situated or occurring near a ventricle, especially of the brain.
The paraventricular nucleus of the hypothalamus plays a crucial role in regulating stress response.  下丘脑室旁核在调节应激反应中起着至关重要的作用。
The paraventricular nucleus is involved in controlling appetite. 室旁核参与控制食欲。
近义词: juxtaventricular
反义词: None
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From para- (beside, near) + ventricular (relating to a ventricle).  "Para-" comes from Greek, and "ventricular" comes from Latin "ventriculus" (little belly).
