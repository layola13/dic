freemasonry:/ˈfriːˈmeɪsənri/| n.|共济会，兄弟会|A fraternal organization with secret rituals and symbols.|例句：He joined freemasonry for the camaraderie and networking opportunities. 他加入共济会是为了友谊和人脉。

例句：Freemasonry has a long and complex history, shrouded in mystery. 共济会历史悠久而复杂，笼罩在神秘之中。|近义词：fraternity, brotherhood, lodge|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用表示公开透明的词) transparency, openness|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *freemasson*, from *franc maçon* "free mason." A "free mason" was originally a mason of superior skill, particularly one who could travel freely to work.
