exterritorial:/ˌekstɛrɪˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
治外法权的
(of a person or place) outside the jurisdiction of local law.
例句：Exterritorial rights were granted to foreign diplomats. 外交官被授予治外法权。

例句：The embassy is considered exterritorial. 大使馆被认为是治外法权区域。

近义词：immune, exempt
反义词：subject (to local laws)
词性: adjective
