idols: /ˈaɪdlz/ (US), /ˈaɪdəlz/ (UK)| n.|偶像; 神像|A person or thing that is greatly admired, loved, or revered.  A representation of a god used as an object of worship.|Many teenagers have pop idols. 许多青少年都有流行偶像。|Synonyms: heroes, icons, stars|Antonyms: foes, enemies|Noun


idols: /ˈaɪdlz/ (US), /ˈaɪdəlz/ (UK)| n.|偶像; 神像|Images or representations of deities used in religious worship.|The temple was filled with idols. 寺庙里摆满了神像。|Synonyms: deities, icons, effigies|Antonyms: none|Noun
