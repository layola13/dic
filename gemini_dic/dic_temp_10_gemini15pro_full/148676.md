overtimidnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈtɪmɪdnɪsɪz/| n. | 过分羞怯，过分胆小 | Excessive timidity; extreme shyness. |  Her overtimidnesses prevented her from speaking up in class. 她的过分胆怯让她无法在课堂上发言。| Synonyms: shynesses, diffidences, hesitancy | Antonyms: boldness, confidence, assertiveness | Noun

Etymology:
From "over-" (excessive) + "timid" (shy, fearful) + "-ness" (noun suffix indicating a state or quality).  "Timid" comes from the Latin "timidus," meaning "fearful."
