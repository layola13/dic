oversensitize: /ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsɪˌtaɪz/ /ˌəʊvəˈsɛnsɪtaɪz/ | vt. | 过度敏感化；使过敏 | To make someone or something too sensitive or easily upset. |  The media has oversensitized the public to the dangers of air travel. 媒体使公众对航空旅行的危险过度敏感。|  He's been oversensitized to criticism after the incident. 那件事之后，他变得对批评过度敏感。 | sensitize, heighten sensitivity | desensitize, numb | verb

oversensitive: /ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsɪtɪv/ /ˌəʊvəˈsɛnsɪtɪv/ | adj. | 过度敏感的 | Too easily upset or offended. | She's oversensitive about her weight. 她对自己的体重过度敏感。 | He's oversensitive to criticism. 他对批评过度敏感。 | sensitive, touchy | insensitive, thick-skinned | adjective


词源:
over- (prefix) + sensitive + -ize (suffix)
"over-" 表示“过度”、“过分”
"sensitive" 表示“敏感的”
"-ize"  动词后缀，表示“使…化”
