embrave:/ɪmˈbreɪv/
verb
使勇敢，使有胆量；鼓舞
To inspire with courage; to make brave.
She emboldened her team to take on the new project. 她鼓励她的团队接受这个新项目。

近义词: encourage, hearten, inspire, fortify, bolster
反义词: discourage, dishearten, cow, intimidate
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From en- + brave.  The prefix "en-" means "to put into" or "to cause to be."  "Brave" comes from the Italian word "bravo" meaning "wild, courageous."
