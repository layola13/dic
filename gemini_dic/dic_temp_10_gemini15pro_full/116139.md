marzipanned:/ˌmɑːrzɪˈpænd/ /ˌmɑːrˈzɪˌpænd/| adj.|杏仁糖衣的；用杏仁糖覆盖的|Covered with or containing marzipan (a sweet paste made of almonds and sugar).|The cake was marzipanned and decorated with sugared fruits.  这个蛋糕上覆盖着杏仁糖，并用蜜饯水果装饰。|The Christmas cake was beautifully marzipanned and iced.  圣诞蛋糕上覆盖着漂亮的杏仁糖衣，并且结了冰。|近义词：iced, frosted|反义词：unfrosted, bare|词性:形容词
Etymology: From marzipan + -ed. Marzipan comes from Italian marzapane, which may be derived from Arabic mawthābān.
