mensurate:/ˈmenʃərət/| adj.|相称的，相当的，相等的|Corresponding in size or degree; in proportion.|Her salary is commensurate with her experience. 她的薪水与她的经验相称。|Example: The reward was commensurate with the effort involved.  回报与所付出的努力相称。|Synonyms: proportionate, comparable, equivalent, corresponding|Antonyms: disproportionate, unequal, incommensurate|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *mensuratus, past participle of mensurare "to measure," from Latin mensura "measure," from mensus, past participle of metiri "to measure."
