damndest:/ˈdæmdɪst/| adj.|最奇特的；最古怪的；最令人惊讶的|This word is a superlative adjective, formed by adding "-est" to "damned."  While "damned" itself can be used as an intensifier, "damndest" specifically emphasizes something surprising, strange, or remarkable.  It often carries a slightly humorous or ironic tone.|He told the damndest story about meeting a unicorn in his backyard. 他讲了一个最古怪的故事，说他在后院遇到了一只独角兽。|近义词：strangest, oddest, weirdest, most surprising|反义词:normal, ordinary|词性:形容词

词源: From "damnedest," a superlative form of "damned," which is a past participle of "damn."  The word ultimately derives from the Latin "damnare," meaning "to condemn."
