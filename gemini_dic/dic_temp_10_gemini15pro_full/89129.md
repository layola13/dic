homebound:/ˈhoʊmbaʊnd/ (US) /ˈhəʊmbaʊnd/ (UK)| adj.|在家的，被限制在家中的，不能外出的|Confined to one's home, especially by illness or old age. Unable to leave home.|例句：She became homebound after breaking her hip. 她摔断臀部后就只能待在家里了。

例句：The heavy snowstorm left many people homebound. 暴风雪使许多人只能待在家中。

近义词：housebound, confined, restricted, shut-in|反义词：mobile, outgoing|词性：形容词
