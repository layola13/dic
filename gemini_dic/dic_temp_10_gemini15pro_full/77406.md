gave:/ɡeɪv/| v.|给予，赠送|Past tense of "give".  It means to transfer possession of something to someone else. |She gave me a book for my birthday. 她送给我一本书作为生日礼物。|He gave his seat to an elderly woman on the bus. 他在公交车上把座位让给了一位老妇人。|近义词：granted, bestowed, presented, offered|反义词:received, took, accepted|动词
