ditheistic:/ˌdaɪˈθiːɪstɪk/
adjective
二神论的
believing in two gods, especially two equal and opposite gods, such as good and evil.
Ditheism posits two equally powerful gods, often in conflict. 二神论假设存在两个同等强大的神，经常处于冲突之中。
Many ancient religions had ditheistic elements within their belief systems. 许多古代宗教的信仰体系中都有二神论的元素。
近义词:dualistic
反义词:monotheistic, polytheistic
词性:adjective
