delegated: /ˈdɛlɪɡeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 委派的；授权的 |  Given authority or responsibility by someone in a higher position. | The manager delegated the task of scheduling to his assistant. 经理将安排时间表的任务委派给了他的助手。|  authorized, entrusted, assigned | retained, withheld | adjective

delegated: /ˈdɛlɪɡeɪtɪd/ | v. | 委派；授权 | Past participle of "delegate," meaning to entrust a task or responsibility to another person. |  He delegated his voting rights to his son. 他把他的投票权委托给了他的儿子。 | entrusted, assigned, commissioned | retained, withheld | verb
