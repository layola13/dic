moody: /ˈmuːdi/ (UK) /ˈmudi/ (US) | adj. | 心情多变的；喜怒无常的；闷闷不乐的 | Subject to unpredictable changes of mood, especially sudden bouts of gloominess or sullenness. |  She's been very moody lately, snapping at everyone. 她最近心情很不好，对每个人都厉声斥责。

He tends to get moody when he's tired. 他累了的时候往往会变得喜怒无常。| 近义词: temperamental, changeable, volatile, sullen, gloomy | 反义词: cheerful, stable, even-tempered | 词性: adjective
