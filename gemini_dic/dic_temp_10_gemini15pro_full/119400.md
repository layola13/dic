microfabrication:/ˌmaɪkroʊˌfæbrɪˈkeɪʃən/| n.|微加工，微制造|The process of fabricating miniature structures and devices, typically with dimensions of micrometers or smaller.  It involves various techniques like photolithography, etching, and deposition.|Microfabrication techniques are crucial for producing microchips. 微加工技术对于生产微芯片至关重要。|近义词:micromanufacturing, nanofabrication|反义词:macrofabrication|noun
