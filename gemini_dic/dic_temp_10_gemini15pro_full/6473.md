ampullated:/æmˈpʌleɪtɪd/
|adj.|瓶状的；膨大的|Having a flask-like swelling or enlargement at one end.|The ampullated ends of the tubes contained the reactive chemicals.|近义词：swollen, inflated, dilated, bulbous|反义词：constricted, narrow, slender|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *ampullatus*, past participle of *ampullare* "to swell out", from Latin *ampulla* "flask, bottle".
