outcoaching:/ˌaʊtˈkoʊtʃɪŋ/
verb
在教练方面胜过
surpassing another coach in tactical skill and decision-making during a game or season.
Example: The Warriors outcoached the Cavaliers in the NBA finals. 勇士队在NBA总决赛中教练水平胜过骑士队。
Example:  Despite having less talented players, the underdog team outcoached their opponents and secured a surprising victory. 尽管球员天赋不如对手，但这支弱旅在教练方面更胜一筹，取得了惊人的胜利。

近义词: outmaneuvering, outsmartiing
反义词: being outcoached

词性:v.
