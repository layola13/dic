decampment:/dɪˈkæmpmənt/
n.
撤营; 搬家; 突然离去
The act of decamping; departure.  A breaking up of camp; moving away.
The refugees made a hasty decampment from the war-torn city.  难民们匆忙地撤离了饱受战争蹂躏的城市。
Synonyms: departure, evacuation, exodus, flight
Antonyms: arrival, entrance, settling, stay
noun
