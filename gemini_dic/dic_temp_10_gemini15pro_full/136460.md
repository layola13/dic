nonrevertive:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈvɜː.sɪv/ | adj. | 非恢复性的，非回返的 | Not returning to the original state or condition after a disruption or change. |  The system employs a nonrevertive switching mechanism, meaning it won't automatically switch back to the primary power source after a power outage.  该系统采用非恢复性切换机制，这意味着它在断电后不会自动切换回主电源。 | Example 2:  A nonrevertive failover system ensures that the backup remains active even after the primary system recovers.  非恢复性故障转移系统确保即使主系统恢复后，备份系统仍然保持活动状态。 | Synonyms: non-returning, non-restorative,  | Antonyms:  revertive, returning, restorative | word property: adjective
