echinoderm:/ɪˈkaɪnəˌdɜːrm/ /ɪˈkɪnəˌdɜːrm/ | n. | 棘皮动物 | Any of a phylum (Echinodermata) of radially symmetrical coelomate marine animals including the starfishes, sea urchins, and sea cucumbers. |  Starfish are echinoderms that live on the ocean floor. 海星是生活在海底的棘皮动物。 | synonym: invertebrate | antonym: vertebrate | noun
