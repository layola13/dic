dissing:/ˈdɪsɪŋ/| v.| 贬低，诋毁，轻视；说唱歌手之间的较量|To speak disrespectfully about; to criticize or put down, especially in a public or competitive context, often associated with hip-hop culture. | He's always dissing his opponents' skills on the basketball court. 他总是在篮球场上贬低对手的技术。|Synonyms: criticizing, belittling, disparaging, slamming, disrespecting|Antonyms: praising, complimenting, respecting, honoring|verb

dissing:/ˈdɪsɪŋ/| n.| 贬低，诋毁，轻视|The act of speaking disrespectfully about someone or something. | The constant dissing from her peers made her feel insecure.  来自同龄人的不断诋毁让她感到没有安全感。|Synonyms: criticism, belittlement, disparagement, disrespect|Antonyms: praise, compliment, respect, honor|noun
