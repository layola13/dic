nares:/ˈnɛəriːz/ /ˈneɪriːz/
n.
鼻孔
The nostrils; the external openings of the nose.
The vet examined the horse's nares. 兽医检查了马的鼻孔。

近义词:nostrils
反义词:None

词性:名词(plural noun)

词源:From Latin *nāris*, from *nāsum* "nose."
