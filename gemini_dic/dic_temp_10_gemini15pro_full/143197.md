outcourt:/ˈaʊtkɔːrt/
adjective
外场的；场外的
Relating to or taking place off the court, especially in basketball or tennis.
The outcourt areas were crowded with spectators. 外场区域挤满了观众。
synonyms: off-court
antonyms: on-court
词性: adjective

outcourt:/ˈaʊtkɔːrt/
noun
外场
The area outside the boundaries of a court, especially in basketball or tennis.
The coach called the team together in the outcourt for a pep talk. 教练把队伍叫到外场打气。
synonyms: sidelines
antonyms: court
词性: noun
