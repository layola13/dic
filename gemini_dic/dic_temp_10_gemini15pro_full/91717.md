hypercritic:/ˈhaɪpərˌkrɪtɪk/ | n. | 超级批评家；苛评家 | A person who judges with excessive severity; a captious critic. |  He's such a hypercritic, finding fault with every single detail. 他真是个超级批评家，对每个细节都吹毛求疵。 |  The hypercritic dismissed the novel as derivative and uninspired.  这位苛评家认为这部小说缺乏创意，毫无灵感。|  nitpicker, faultfinder, carper |  supporter, admirer | noun
