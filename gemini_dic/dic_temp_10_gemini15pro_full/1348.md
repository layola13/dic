acculturating: /əˈkʌltʃəreɪtɪŋ/
verb
逐渐适应文化，文化适应
The process of adapting or adjusting to a new culture.
Many immigrants face the challenge of acculturating to a new society. 许多移民面临着适应新社会的挑战。

They are acculturating to life in the city. 他们正在适应城市生活。

近义词: adapting, adjusting, assimilating, integrating
反义词: alienating, isolating
词性: verb (present participle)

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "acculturate," which comes from the Latin "ad" (to) + "cultura" (culture).  Meaning "to adapt to a new culture."
