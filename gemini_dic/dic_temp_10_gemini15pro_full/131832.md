nonconsolidated:/ˌnɑːnkənˈsɑːlɪdeɪtɪd/
adjective
未合并的，未加固的
Not combined into a single whole; not consolidated.
Example: The company issued nonconsolidated financial statements for its subsidiaries.  这家公司发布了其子公司的未合并财务报表。

Example: The gravel road remained nonconsolidated and difficult to drive on.  那条砾石路仍然没有加固，很难开车。
synonym:unconsolidated, separate, independent
antonym:consolidated, combined, merged
