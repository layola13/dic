choric: /ˈkɔːrɪk/ /ˈkɔːrɪk/ | adj. | 合唱的，歌队的 | Relating to or resembling a chorus or choir; performed by a chorus or choir. | The play incorporated choric elements, with a group of actors commenting on the action.  这出戏融入了合唱元素，由一组演员对剧情进行评论。 | The choric odes in Greek tragedies provide insight into the characters' motivations.  希腊悲剧中的合唱颂歌可以洞察人物的动机。| 近义词: choral | 反义词: solo | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Greek *khorikos*, from *khoros* "chorus, dance"
