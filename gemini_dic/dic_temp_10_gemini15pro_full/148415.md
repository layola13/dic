oversteadfastness:/ˌoʊvərˈstɛdfæstnəs/| n. |过度坚定；顽固；过分执着|The state of being excessively steadfast, often to the point of being stubborn or inflexible.  It implies an unwillingness to change one's mind or course of action even when it is advisable to do so.|His oversteadfastness in sticking to the original plan, despite clear evidence it wouldn't work, ultimately led to the project's failure. 他过于执着地坚持原计划，尽管有明确的证据表明它行不通，最终导致了项目的失败。|近义词: obstinacy, stubbornness, rigidity, inflexibility|反义词: flexibility, adaptability, open-mindedness|词性:名词
