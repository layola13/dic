dehiscence:/diˈhɪsəns/| n.|开裂，裂开|The dehiscence of the wound caused further complications.  伤口的开裂导致了进一步的并发症。| The dehiscence of the seed pod allowed the seeds to disperse. 种荚的开裂使种子得以散播。|近义词：splitting, rupture, opening|反义词: closure, sealing|词性:noun
Etymology: From Latin *dēhiscens*, present participle of *dēhīscere* "to gape, yawn, open," from *dē* "from, away from" + *hīscere* "to gape."
