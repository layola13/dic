USAGE->
Simple Present:
What do you think? = 你怎么想？
What does she want for her birthday? = 她生日想要什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing over there? = 他在那里做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she eaten for lunch? = 她午餐吃了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the event? = 她参加活动时穿了什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had she said before the meeting started? = 会议开始前她说了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I called? = 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about? = 他们一直在争论什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will happen if I press this button? = 如果我按下这个按钮会发生什么？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing at 8 pm tomorrow? = 明天晚上8点你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have finished by Friday? = 到星期五你会完成什么？
What will have changed by the end of the year? = 到年底会有什么变化？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been studying for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经学习五年什么了？
What will she have been working on for so long? = 到那时她将一直致力于什么那么长时间了？
