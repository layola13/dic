cantoris:/kænˈtɔːrɪs/
adjective
唱诗班的，领唱的
Of or relating to a cantor, precentor, or choir; specifically designating the side of the choir, usually the north side, where the precentor sits.
The cantoris side of the choir usually has stronger singers. 唱诗班的领唱侧通常有更强的歌手。
The new hymnals were placed on the cantoris side of the chancel. 新的赞美诗被放在了唱诗台的领唱侧。
Near Synonyms: choral, choir, precentor
Antonyms: congregation, audience
词性: adjective
