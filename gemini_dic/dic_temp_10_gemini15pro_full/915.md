abusively:/əˈbjuːsɪvli/
adverb
滥用地，辱骂地
in an abusive manner; using harsh and insulting language.
He spoke abusively to the referee. = 他对裁判说话很粗鲁。

She was abusively treated by her employer. = 她被雇主虐待。

近义词:insultingly, offensively, rudely, cruelly, harshly
反义词:kindly, respectfully, politely
词性:adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From abuse (n.) + -ive (suffix forming adjectives) + -ly (suffix forming adverbs).  The word "abuse" ultimately comes from the Latin "abusus," past participle of "abuti," meaning "to misuse" or "to use up."

USAGE->
She abusively rejected his offer of help. = 她粗暴地拒绝了他的帮助。
The customer treated the waiter abusively. = 顾客辱骂了服务员。

He shouted abusively at the children playing in the street. = 他对在街上玩耍的孩子们大声辱骂。
The manager spoke abusively to his staff, causing widespread resentment. = 经理对他的员工说话粗暴，引起了普遍的怨恨。

PHRASE->
speak abusively to someone = 对某人恶语相向
treat someone abusively = 虐待某人
behave abusively = 行为粗暴
act abusively towards someone = 对待某人粗暴
use language abusively = 滥用语言
