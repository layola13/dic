owner-occupied:/ˈoʊnər ˈɑːkjəpaɪd/
| adj. |自住的；业主自用的|A property that is lived in by the person who owns it.  The owner lives in the property, as opposed to renting it out.|Example:  Owner-occupied housing is generally better maintained than rental properties.  业主自住的房屋通常比出租房屋维护得更好。
|Example: The mortgage rates for owner-occupied properties are often lower than those for investment properties.  自住房产的抵押贷款利率通常低于投资房产。|Near Synonyms: owner-resident, self-occupied|Antonyms: rented, leased, tenant-occupied|adjective
