emissary: /ˈemɪseri/ (US), /ˈemɪsəri/ (UK)| n.|使者，特使|a person sent on a special mission, usually as a diplomatic representative.|The king sent an emissary to negotiate a peace treaty. 国王派了一名使者去谈判和平条约。|synonyms: envoy, ambassador, delegate, agent, representative|antonyms: receiver, recipient|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *emissarius, from Latin ēmissus, past participle of ēmittō (“send forth”), from ē- (“out”) + mittō (“send”).
