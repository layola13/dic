deerlet:/ˈdɪərlɪt/
n.
小鹿，幼鹿
A young deer, especially a fawn.
The deerlet hid in the tall grass, safe from predators. 小鹿藏在高高的草丛里，免受捕食者的伤害。

The deerlet, still unsteady on its legs, followed its mother. 小鹿腿脚还不稳，跟着妈妈。
近义词：fawn, young deer
反义词:adult deer, stag (雄鹿), hind (雌鹿)
词性:noun

Etymology:
deer + -let (diminutive suffix)
