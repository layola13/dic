outspied:/ˌaʊtˈspaɪd/
verb
胜过…的侦察；在侦察方面超过
To surpass in spying; to outdo in espionage.
CIA agents outspied the enemy and uncovered their secret plans. 中央情报局特工在侦察方面胜过敌人，并发现了他们的秘密计划。
近义词:outmaneuvered, outwitted, surpassed
反义词:outdone, outsmarted
