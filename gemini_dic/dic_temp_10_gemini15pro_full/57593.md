dramshop:/ˈdræmˌʃɒp/ (US), /ˈdræmˌʃɒp/ (UK)| n. |酒馆；酒吧| A place where alcoholic drinks are sold to be drunk on the premises. Specifically, one that can be held legally liable for the actions of intoxicated patrons. | The dramshop was sued after a patron caused an accident after leaving drunk.  一个顾客在离开酒吧后醉酒肇事，酒吧因此被起诉。|近义词: bar, pub, tavern|反义词: None| noun
