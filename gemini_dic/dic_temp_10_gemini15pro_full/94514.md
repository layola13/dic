importunes:/ɪmˈpɔːrtjuːnz/ | v. | 恳求，不断请求；强求 | to ask (someone) for something or to do something in a repeated or annoying way | He importuned the teacher for a better grade. 他不断恳求老师给他更高的分数。| synonyms: begs, pleads, pesters, bothers | antonyms:  desists, refrains | verb

importune:/ɪmˈpɔːrtjuːn/ | adj. | 纠缠不休的；厌烦的 | making repeated or annoying requests |  His importunate requests for money finally wore her down. 他不断纠缠要钱，最终让她屈服了。 | synonyms: persistent, insistent, pressing | antonyms:  unobtrusive, discreet | adjective
