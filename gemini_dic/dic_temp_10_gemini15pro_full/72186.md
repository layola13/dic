floppy:/ˈflɒpi/ (US) /ˈflɒpi/ (UK)| adj.|软的，松软的，不成功的|Something that is floppy is soft and hangs loosely rather than being stiff.|My hat has a wide, floppy brim. 我的帽子有一个宽大松软的帽檐。|His handshake was floppy and weak. 他的握手软弱无力。|The play was a complete flop.  这出戏完全失败了。|近义词：soft, loose, limp, unsuccessful|反义词：stiff, firm, rigid, successful|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "flop" + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives meaning 'having the quality of', 'tending to').  "Flop" originates from a Scandinavian source, related to Old Norse *flappa ("to strike, flap").
