cupric:/ˈkjuːprɪk/
adjective
铜的；含铜的；二价铜的
Of or relating to copper, especially in its divalent state.
Cupric sulfate is a common compound. 硫酸铜是一种常见的化合物。

Cupric chloride is used as a catalyst. 氯化铜用作催化剂。
近义词: copper
反义词: cuprous
词性: adjective
