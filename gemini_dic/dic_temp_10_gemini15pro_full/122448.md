mobile:/ˈmoʊbəl/ (US) /ˈməʊbaɪl/ (UK)| adj. n.|可移动的，活动的；手机|Able to move or be moved freely or easily; a cell phone.|She has a very mobile face. 她有一张表情非常丰富的脸。|He uses his mobile to stay in touch with his family. 他用手机与家人保持联系。|近义词：movable, portable, cellphone (n.)|反义词:immobile, fixed, stationary|词性:adjective, noun

mobile:/ˈmoʊbəl/ (US) /ˈməʊbaɪl/ (UK)| n.|手机|A cell phone.|I lost my mobile yesterday. 我昨天丢了手机。|Can I borrow your mobile for a minute? 我可以借用一下你的手机吗？|近义词：cellphone, cellular phone, smartphone|反义词:landline|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *mobil*, from Old French *mobile*, from Latin *mōbilis* (“movable, flexible, changeable, nimble”), from *movēre* (“to move”).
