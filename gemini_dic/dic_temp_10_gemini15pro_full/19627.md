bijou:/ˈbiːʒuː/ (US), /ˈbiːʒuː/ (UK)| n. |小巧精致的物品；珍品；小饰品|A small, exquisitely wrought trinket or piece of jewelry.|She wore a bijou brooch on her lapel. 她衣翻领上戴着一枚精致的小胸针。|Synonyms: trinket, bauble, gem, jewel, ornament|Antonyms: clunky, gaudy|noun

bijou:/ˈbiːʒuː/ (US), /ˈbiːʒuː/ (UK)| adj. |小巧精致的|Small and elegant.|The hotel had a bijou bar. 这家酒店有一个小巧精致的酒吧。|Synonyms: dainty, delicate, exquisite, miniature|Antonyms: large, bulky, clumsy|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *bijou*, meaning "jewel, trinket". Ultimately from Breton *bizou*, meaning "finger ring".
