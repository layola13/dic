constructor's:/kənˈstrʌktərz/| n.|建造者的，建设者的|The constructor's blueprint was highly detailed. 建造者的蓝图非常详细。|例句：The constructor's hard hat protected him from falling debris.  建造者的安全帽保护他免受坠落碎片的伤害。
|例句：The constructor's experience was crucial to the project's success. 建造者的经验对项目的成功至关重要。|近义词：builder's, maker's, architect's|反义词:destroyer's, demolisher's|词性:名词所有格

Etymology:  From "constructor" + "'s" (possessive suffix). "Constructor" comes from the Latin "constructus," the past participle of "construere," meaning "to heap up, build."  The "-or" suffix denotes an agent or one who performs the action.
