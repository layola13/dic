bludgeoners:/ˈblʌdʒənərz/ | n. | 施暴者；用棍棒打的人； | People who use a bludgeon (a short, heavy club) as a weapon.  Those who attack others, either physically or verbally, in a forceful and relentless manner. | The bludgeoners attacked the peaceful protesters. 施暴者袭击了和平抗议者。|  The senator's opponents bludgeoned him with accusations of corruption. 参议员的反对者用腐败指控猛烈抨击他。 | Synonyms: assailants, attackers, thugs | Antonyms: protectors, defenders | noun
