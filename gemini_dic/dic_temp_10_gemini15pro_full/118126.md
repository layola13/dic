menostasis: /ˌmɛnəˈsteɪsɪs/
noun
月经停止；闭经
Cessation of menstruation.  The physiological cessation of menstruation.
She experienced menostasis after chemotherapy. 她化疗后经历了闭经。
The doctor diagnosed her with premature menostasis. 医生诊断她患有早发性闭经。
近义词: amenorrhea
反义词: menorrhagia (月经过多)
词性: n.
