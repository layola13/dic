bessies:/ˈbɛsiz/| n.|贝西（女孩名 Bessie 的复数形式）;奶牛（非正式）|Bessies is a plural form of the name Bessie, a diminutive of Elizabeth. It can also be used informally to refer to cows.|We saw three bessies grazing in the field. 我们看到三头奶牛在田野里吃草。|近义词：cows, cattle (奶牛，牛)|反义词:bulls (公牛)|noun
