challenger:/ˈtʃælɪndʒər/| n.|挑战者; 竞争者;  претендент (Russian)|A person who engages in a contest or challenge; a competitor.|例句：The challenger surprised everyone by defeating the champion.  挑战者击败了冠军，让所有人大吃一惊。

例句：Several challengers have emerged in the race for mayor.  几位挑战者出现在了市长竞选中。|近义词：competitor, rival, contestant, opponent|反义词：incumbent, defender, champion|词性:noun
