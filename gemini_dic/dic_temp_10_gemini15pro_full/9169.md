antineutral:/ˌæntiˈnjuːtrəl/
adjective
反中性的；反对中立的
Opposing neutrality; taking sides in a conflict.
The antineutral stance of the country led to its involvement in the war.  这个国家反对中立的立场导致其卷入了战争。
The newspaper took an antineutral position on the conflict. 这家报纸在冲突中采取了不中立的立场。
近义词:biased, partisan, unneutral
反义词:neutral, impartial, objective
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + neutral.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to." "Neutral" refers to not taking sides in a conflict.
