nags:/næɡz/| v. (third-person singular simple present nags, present participle nagging, simple past and past participle nagged)| 纠缠，唠叨；不断烦扰| to annoy someone by repeatedly asking them to do something or by repeatedly complaining about something| My mom keeps nagging me about cleaning my room. 我的妈妈一直唠叨我打扫房间。|近义词：pester, badger, harass, annoy|反义词: praise, commend |词性: verb

nags:/næɡz/| n. (plural noun)| 唠叨的人；爱抱怨的人| a person who nags someone| He's such a nag about everything! 他对每件事都这么唠叨！|近义词：pest, nuisance|反义词: supporter, encourager |词性: noun
