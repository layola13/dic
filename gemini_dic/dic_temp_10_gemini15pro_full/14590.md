avidity:/əˈvɪdɪti/
n.
贪婪；渴望；热望
An eager desire or enthusiasm for something.
She had an avidity for knowledge. 她对知识有着强烈的渴望。
eagerness, keenness, enthusiasm, zeal
apathy, indifference, reluctance
noun

avidity:/əˈvɪdɪti/
n.
贪婪；渴望；热望
A strong desire for something.
He showed an avidity for power. 他表现出对权力的渴望。
greed, hunger, thirst
indifference, apathy
noun
