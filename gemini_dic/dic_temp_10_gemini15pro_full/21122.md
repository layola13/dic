blistered:/ˈblɪstərd/ /ˈblɪstərd/ | adj. |起水泡的；起了泡的；（油漆等）起泡的；严厉批评的 |Covered in or full of blisters. |  My feet were blistered after the long hike. 我的双脚在长途跋涉后起了水泡。| synonyms: blistered, vesicated | antonyms: smooth, unblemished | adjective
blistered:/ˈblɪstərd/ /ˈblɪstərd/ | v. |（使）起水泡；严厉批评 |To cause blisters to form on. | The hot sun blistered the paint on the car.  炎热的太阳晒得车上的油漆起了泡。| synonyms:  scald, scorch | antonyms: soothe, heal | verb
blister:/ˈblɪstər/ /ˈblɪstər/ | n. |水泡；（油漆等）气泡；缺点；令人不快的人 |A small bubble on the skin filled with serum and caused by friction, burning, or other damage. |  I got a blister on my heel from my new shoes.  我的新鞋子把我的脚后跟磨出了水泡。| synonyms:  bubble, vesicle | antonyms: none | noun

词源: From Middle English *blister, *blester, from Old French blestre, from Frankish *blistra (“blister”), from Proto-Germanic *blis-trō (“blister”), from Proto-Indo-European *bʰley- (“to swell, inflate”).
