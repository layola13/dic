compete:/kəmˈpiːt/ (US), /kəmˈpiːt/ (UK)| v. |竞争，比赛|To strive to gain or win something by defeating or establishing superiority over others.|例句：Several companies are competing for the contract.  几家公司正在竞争这份合同。

例句：He's competing in the 100-meter race. 他将参加100米赛跑。

近义词：contend, vie, rival, contest|反义词：cooperate, yield, forfeit|词性：动词

compete:/kəmˈpiːt/ (US), /kəmˈpiːt/ (UK)| adj. |(仅限于构词，例如"competitive")| Having or displaying a strong desire to win.|例句：This is a highly competitive market. 这是一个竞争激烈的市场。(competitive)|反义词：cooperative, noncompetitive|词性：形容词 (构词成分)
