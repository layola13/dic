indubitable:/ɪnˈdjuːbɪtəbl/ /ɪnˈduːbɪtəbl/| adj.|不容置疑的；无可置疑的|Beyond doubt or question; unquestionable.|Her talent is indubitable. 她的才华无可置疑。|近义词：unquestionable, undeniable, certain, absolute, incontestable|反义词：doubtful, questionable, debatable, disputable|词性:形容词

indubitable:/ɪnˈdjuːbɪtəbl/ /ɪnˈduːbɪtəbl/| adj.|千真万确的；确凿的|Absolutely true; certain.|The evidence presented in court was indubitable. 在法庭上提出的证据确凿无疑。|近义词：unmistakable, irrefutable, conclusive|反义词：dubious, uncertain|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *indubitabilis, from in- "not" + dubitabilis "doubtful," from dubitare "to doubt," from dubius "doubtful."
