blissed:/blɪst/ | adj. | 极乐的，幸福的 | Extremely happy; in a state of perfect happiness. | She looked utterly blissed after the wedding. 她婚礼后看起来无比幸福。|  He felt blissed out after the massage. 按摩后他感到飘飘欲仙。| 近义词：ecstatic, blissful, joyful, happy | 反义词：miserable, unhappy, wretched | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From bliss + -ed.  Bliss itself comes from Old English *bliþs, from Proto-Germanic *bliþiz ("gentle, kind, friendly, merciful, gracious").
