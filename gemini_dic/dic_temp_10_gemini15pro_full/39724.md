consimilitude:/ˌkɒnsɪˈmɪlɪtjuːd/ /ˌkɑːnsɪˈmɪlɪtuːd/
noun
相似;类似
similarity, resemblance, likeness, alikeness, similitude, analogy.  A consimilitude is a resemblance or similarity.
The consimilitude between the two paintings suggests they were done by the same artist.  这两幅画的相似之处表明它们出自同一位艺术家之手。
近义词: similarity, resemblance, likeness
反义词: difference, dissimilarity, unlikeness
词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *consimilitūdō, from Latin consimilis (“very similar”) + -tūdō (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality).  Consimilis is from con- (prefix meaning “with, together”) + similis (“like, similar, resembling”). Similis is of uncertain origin.
