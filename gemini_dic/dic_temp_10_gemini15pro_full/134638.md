nonmalicious:/ˌnɒn.məˈlɪʃ.əs/
adjective
无恶意
not having or showing malice; harmless
例句：The email appeared to be nonmalicious, simply a misdirected message. 这封邮件似乎没有恶意，只是发送错了。

例句：It was a nonmalicious prank that went too far.  这是一个没有恶意的玩笑，玩得太过火了。

近义词：benign, harmless, innocent, innocuous
反义词：malicious, harmful, malevolent, spiteful
词性：adjective
