cymometer:/saɪˈmɒmɪtər/ | /saɪˈmɑːmɪtər/ | n. | 波长计，频率计 | An instrument for measuring the wavelength or frequency of electromagnetic waves, especially radio waves. |  A cymometer was used to calibrate the radio transmitter.  波长计被用来校准无线电发射机。

| Example: The engineer used a cymometer to determine the frequency of the signal. 工程师使用波长计来确定信号的频率。 | Synonym: wavemeter, frequency meter | Antonym: N/A | Noun
