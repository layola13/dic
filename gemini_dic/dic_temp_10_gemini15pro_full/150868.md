paragraph:/ˈpærəɡrɑːf/ | n. | 段落，章节 | A distinct section of a piece of writing, usually dealing with a single theme and indicated by a new line, indentation, or numbering. | The book is divided into short, easy-to-read paragraphs. 这本书被分成简短易读的段落。

|  Example:  Please read the following paragraph carefully. 请仔细阅读以下段落。

| Synonym: section, passage, segment | Antonym: (No direct antonym for a structural unit) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Old French *paragraphe* (modern French *paragraphe*), from Medieval Latin *paragraphus*, from Greek *paragraphos* "written beside" or "marginal mark," from *para-* "beside" + *graphein* "to write."
