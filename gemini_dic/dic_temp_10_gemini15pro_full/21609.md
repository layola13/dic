blunging:/ˈblʌndʒɪŋ/
verb
混合，搅拌（尤指陶瓷原料）
The process of mixing clay and water to a creamy consistency in a blunger.
The workers were blunging the clay for the pottery. 工人们正在为陶器搅拌粘土。
近义词：mixing, blending, stirring
反义词：separating
词性：v.

Etymology: From blunge + -ing.  Blunge itself likely originated as a variant of plunge, influenced by words like blend.
