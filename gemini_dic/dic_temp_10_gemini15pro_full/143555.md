outhears:/ˌaʊtˈhɪrz/| v. |偷听到，无意中听到|to hear something that someone else did not intend you to hear| I overheard them talking about the surprise party. 我无意中听到他们在谈论惊喜派对。|近义词：eavesdrop, overhear|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑miss - 错过)|词性:动词
