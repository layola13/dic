decaploid:/ˈdɛkəˌplɔɪd/| adj. n.|十倍体|Having ten times the basic number of chromosomes in a cell.  A cell or organism that is decaploid.|例句：Decaploid strains of certain plants have been developed.  某些植物的十倍体品系已经被开发出来。

例句：The researchers discovered a decaploid frog in the Amazon. 研究人员在亚马逊发现了一只十倍体青蛙。|近义词：polyploid|反义词：haploid, diploid|词性：adjective, noun


Etymology:  deca- (ten) + -ploid (fold, referring to chromosome sets) from Greek "deka" (ten) + "-ploos" (fold)
