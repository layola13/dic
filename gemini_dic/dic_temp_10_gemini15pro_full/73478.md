foreran:/fɔːrˈræn/
verb
预示，预兆，先于…发生
acted as a warning or sign of a future event.
The dark clouds foreran the storm.  乌云预示着暴风雨的来临。
Synonyms: presaged, foreshadowed, portended, preceded, heralded
Antonyms: followed, succeeded
词性：动词
