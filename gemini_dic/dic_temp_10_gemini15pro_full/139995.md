odyle:/ˈəʊdaɪl/ | n. | 乙二醇醚 | A colorless, oily liquid used as a solvent and plasticizer. |  Ethylene glycol monoethyl ether is an odyle used in paints. 乙二醇单乙醚是一种用于油漆中的乙二醇醚。| synonym: glycol ether | antonym: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Greek 'hodós' (way, path) + 'hýlē' (matter, wood). This refers to its use as a solvent, which helps other substances dissolve.  It's a coined term specifically for the chemical compound.
