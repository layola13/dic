diffraction:/dɪˈfrækʃən/ | n. | 衍射 | The spreading of waves, such as light or sound, as they pass through an aperture or around an obstacle. |  Light waves undergo diffraction when they pass through a narrow slit. 光波穿过狭缝时会发生衍射。| synonyms: scattering, dispersion | antonyms: convergence, focusing | noun
