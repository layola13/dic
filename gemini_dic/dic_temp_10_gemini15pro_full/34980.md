cloggers:/ˈklɑːɡərz/| n.| 木屐舞者；木底鞋|Wooden shoes, often worn for dancing, especially clog dancing. |The cloggers entertained the crowd with their rhythmic steps. 木屐舞者用他们有节奏的舞步娱乐了观众。| 近义词: dancers, performers | 反义词: audience, spectators | noun

clogs:/klɑːɡz/| n.| 木底鞋；障碍；阻塞物|Wooden shoes, or something that blocks or obstructs.|The farmer wore clogs in the muddy field.  农民在泥泞的田里穿着木底鞋。
The drain was clogged with hair. 下水道被头发堵塞了。| 近义词: shoes, obstructions, blockages | 反义词: openings, clearings | noun
