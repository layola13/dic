defencing:/dɪˈfɛnsɪŋ/
n.
防御;防护;辩护
The act of defending oneself or something else.
The team's defencing was weak, leading to their defeat.
该队的防守很弱，导致了他们的失败。

近义词: protecting, guarding, shielding
反义词: attacking, assaulting
