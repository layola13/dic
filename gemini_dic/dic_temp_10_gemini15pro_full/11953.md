artfulness:/ˈɑːrtfəlnəs/
n.
技巧；狡猾；熟练
The quality of being artful or cunning, especially in a clever or deceptive way.
Her artfulness was evident in the way she manipulated the conversation. 她的技巧体现在她操纵谈话的方式上。
近义词: cunning, craftiness, guile, slyness, deviousness
反义词: artlessness, ingenuousness, naivety, sincerity, honesty
词性: 名词

artful:/ˈɑːrtfʊl/
adj.
巧妙的；狡猾的；熟练的
Clever or skillful, especially in a crafty or cunning way.
He's an artful dodger. 他是一个狡猾的骗子。
近义词: cunning, crafty, wily, sly, devious
反义词: artless, ingenuous, naive, sincere, honest
词性: 形容词

词源:
From art + -ful.  "Art" in this older sense means "skill" or "craft," derived from the Latin "ars" (skill).  The suffix "-ful" adds the meaning "full of" or "characterized by."
