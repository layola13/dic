deraign:/dɪˈreɪn/
verb
证明…有罪；控告；责难；提出
to prove (something) to be true; to accuse; to censure; to submit (something) to proof
The lawyer deraigned the witness's testimony, exposing inconsistencies and lies. 律师驳斥了证人的证词，揭露了其中的前后矛盾和谎言。
He was deraigned for his mismanagement of public funds. 他因对公共资金管理不善而受到谴责。
synonyms: accuse, indict, impeach, censure, denounce, condemn
antonyms: exonerate, acquit, absolve, vindicate
v.
