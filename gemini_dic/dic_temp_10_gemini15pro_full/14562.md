avesta:/əˈvɛstə/
noun
阿维斯陀，琐罗亚斯德教的圣典
The sacred scriptures of Zoroastrianism.
The Avesta is a collection of sacred texts. 阿维斯陀是一部圣典合集。
The Avesta contains hymns, prayers, and laws. 阿维斯陀包含赞美诗、祈祷文和律法。
synonyms: scripture, holy book, sacred text
antonyms: None (lack of a single antonym for a religious text)
