ballo:/ˈbæloʊ/| n.| 舞会，大型正式舞会 |A large formal dance.|  She wore a beautiful gown to the ballo. 她穿着一件漂亮的礼服去参加舞会。| synonym: ball, formal dance | antonym: casual gathering | noun

Etymology: From Italian *ballo*, from Old French *bal*, from Late Latin *ballāre* "to dance," of Germanic origin.
