bluegown:/ˈbluːɡaʊn/
noun
蓝袍学者; 蓝袍律师; 苏格兰爱丁堡大学的毕业生
a graduate of the University of Edinburgh, traditionally wearing a blue gown.
例句：He's a bluegown from Edinburgh. 他是爱丁堡大学的毕业生。

例句：The bluegowns gathered for the graduation ceremony.  蓝袍学者们聚集在一起参加毕业典礼。

近义词：graduate, alumnus, alumna
反义词：undergraduate
