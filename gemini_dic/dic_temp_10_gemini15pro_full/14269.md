autogenesis:/ˌɔːtəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/ | n. | 自生，自然发生 | The theory of autogenesis, now disproven, held that life could arise spontaneously from non-living matter.  自生论，现在已被证伪，认为生命可以从无生命物质中自发产生。 | Autogenesis is a concept central to many creation myths. 自生是许多创世神话的核心概念。 | 近义词：abiogenesis, spontaneous generation | 反义词：biogenesis | 词性: noun
