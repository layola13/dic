manhunt:/ˈmænˌhʌnt/| n. |搜捕，追捕|An organized search for a person, especially a criminal.|Police launched a massive manhunt after the bank robbery. 警方在银行抢劫案后发起了大规模搜捕。|近义词：search, pursuit, chase|反义词:rescue, release|名词
