misemploys:/ˌmɪsɪmˈplɔɪz/
|v.| 误用，滥用；使人失业|To employ wrongly or improperly; to use inefficiently or inappropriately.|The company misemploys its resources by focusing on short-term profits.  这家公司因注重短期利润而滥用资源。|
The manager misemployed John in a role that didn't utilize his skills.  经理让约翰担任了一个不能发挥他技能的职位，对他用非所长。|
Synonyms: misuse, abuse, misapply|
Antonyms: utilize, employ correctly, apply properly|
