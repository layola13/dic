crammer:/ˈkræmər/| n.| 填鸭式学习者；死记硬背的人|A person who studies intensively for a short time, especially for an examination.|He's a real crammer, spending the week before the test memorizing everything. 他是一个真正的填鸭式学习者，考试前一周都在死记硬背。|近义词：swimmer, grinder|反义词: scholar|词性: 名词

cram:/kræm/| v.| 塞满；填塞；死记硬背|To force a large amount of something into a small space; to study intensively for a short time.|I crammed all my clothes into the suitcase. 我把所有的衣服都塞进了手提箱。
He crammed for the exam all night. 他整晚都在为考试突击。|近义词：stuff, pack, cram, swot (British)|反义词: browse, skim|词性: 动词
