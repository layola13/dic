ahistorical:/ˌeɪhɪˈstɔːrɪkl/| adj.|非历史的；不顾历史的；与历史无关的|Lacking historical perspective or context; unconcerned with or unrelated to history.|例句：An ahistorical approach to art ignores the cultural context in which it was created.  非历史的艺术方法忽略了艺术创作的文化背景。

例句：His ahistorical interpretation of the events led to many misunderstandings. 他对事件的非历史性解释导致了许多误解。|近义词：unhistorical, antihistorical|反义词：historical|词性:形容词
