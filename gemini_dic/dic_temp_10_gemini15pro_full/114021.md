magnanimous:/mæɡˈnænɪməs/| adj.|宽宏大量的；慷慨的|Very generous or forgiving, especially toward a rival or someone less powerful than oneself.|例句：He was magnanimous in victory, praising his opponent's skill. 他在胜利中表现得很大度，称赞对手的技术高超。

例句：Despite the insults, she responded with magnanimous silence. 尽管受到了侮辱，她还是以宽宏大量的沉默回应。|近义词：generous, forgiving, noble, charitable|反义词：petty, mean, vindictive, ungenerous|词性:形容词

词源:
From Latin magnanimus (“great-souled, high-minded”), from magnus (“great, large, big, important”) + animus (“mind, soul, spirit, life”).
