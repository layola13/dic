dirndls: /ˈdɜːrndlz/ (US), /ˈdɪəndlz/ (UK)| n. |紧身连衣裙，背心裙（通常指巴伐利亚或奥地利传统女性服饰）|A traditional dress worn by women and girls in the Alps, consisting of a bodice, blouse, and full skirt. |例句：The women wore colorful dirndls at the festival.  妇女们在节日上穿着色彩鲜艳的紧身连衣裙。|近义词：dress, frock, gown|反义词: N/A | Noun
