eteostic: /ˌiːtiˈɒstɪk/
adjective
离合诗体的
Relating to or designating a kind of acrostic in which the final letters of the lines, when taken in order, form a word or phrase.
Eteostic poems are less common than acrostics that use the first letter of each line.  This eteostic spells out "HOPE".
Example:

DreaminG
HopinG
LivinG
PrayinG

近义词: acrostic, telestich
反义词: None
词性: adjective
