hearsay:/ˈhɪrseɪ/ | n. |传闻，道听途说|Information received from other people that one cannot adequately substantiate; rumor.|例句：The court cannot accept evidence based on hearsay. 法庭不能接受基于传闻的证据。

例句：The rumor was nothing more than malicious hearsay. 这谣言不过是恶意传闻。|近义词：rumor, gossip, report|反义词：evidence, proof, fact|词性: 名词
