cordialness:/ˈkɔːrdʒəl.nəs/ /ˈkɔːrdiəl.nəs/| n.|热情, 真挚, 诚恳|The cordialness of their welcome made us feel at home.  他们的热情欢迎让我们感到宾至如归。|Friendliness, warmth, geniality|Coldness, hostility, indifference|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From cordial + -ness.  Cordial itself comes from Late Middle English *cordial, from Old French cordial, from Medieval Latin cordiālis (“of the heart, heartfelt”), from Latin cor (“heart”).
