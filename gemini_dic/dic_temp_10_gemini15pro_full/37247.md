comfrey:/ˈkʌmfri/ | n. | 紫草科植物;聚合草;康復力;康富利| A perennial Eurasian herb (Symphytum officinale of the family Boraginaceae) widely grown for its hairy leaves and pinkish-purple or white flowers and formerly much used in folk medicine.  | Comfrey is known for its healing properties.  聚合草以其愈合特性而闻名。 |  synonyms: boneset, knitbone, bruisewort | antonyms: N/A | noun
