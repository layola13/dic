faddier: /ˈfædiər/
adjective
更流行的;更时尚的;更一时狂热的
more faddish; given to fads.
Example: Platform shoes are faddier this year than last year.  今年厚底鞋比去年更流行。
Example:  Teenagers are often faddier than adults.  青少年通常比成年人更赶时髦。
Synonym: trendier, more stylish, more fashionable
Antonym: less fashionable, outdated, classic
词性: adjective
