borealis:/bɔːriˈælɪs/| adj.|北方的，北极光的|Relating to the north or the north wind.  It is often used in the context of the aurora borealis (Northern Lights).|The aurora borealis illuminated the night sky with vibrant colors. 北极光用鲜艳的色彩照亮了夜空。|The borealis wind brought a chill to the air. 北风给空气带来了一丝寒意。|近义词：northern, arctic|反义词：southern, austral|词性:adjective
词源:From Latin *boreālis, from Boreās (“north wind, north”).
