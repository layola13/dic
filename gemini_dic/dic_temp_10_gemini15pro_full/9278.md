antiphilosophic:/ˌæntiˌfɪləˈsɑːfɪk/
adjective
反哲学的; 敌视哲学的
opposed to or mistrustful of philosophy or philosophers.
He held an antiphilosophic stance, preferring practical experience to abstract thought. 他持有一种反哲学的立场，更喜欢实践经验而不是抽象思维。
anti-philosophical
philosophical, pro-philosophical
adjective
