flauntiest:/ˈflɔːntiɪst/
adjective
最炫耀的；最招摇的
(superlative of) Displaying ostentatiously, especially in a cheerful, confident way.
She wore her flauntiest dress to the party. 她穿着她最招摇的裙子去参加了派对。
Near Synonyms: showiest, flashiest, ostentatious, flamboyant
Antonyms: modest, unassuming, understated
