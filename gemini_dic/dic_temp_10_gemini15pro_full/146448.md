overfreight:/ˌoʊvərˈfreɪt/ | n. vt.| 过度装载，超载；超额运费 |The act of loading a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle with more cargo than it can safely carry, or the excess cargo itself.  It can also refer to the extra charge for carrying this excess cargo.| The ship was overfreighted and sank in the storm. 这艘船超载了，在暴风雨中沉没了。 | The company had to pay an overfreight charge for the extra goods.  这家公司不得不为额外的货物支付超额运费。| synonyms: overload, surcharge, excess cargo | antonyms: underload, underfreight | noun, verb
