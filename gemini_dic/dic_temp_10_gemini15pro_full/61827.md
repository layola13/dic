empiricist:/ɪmˈpɪrɪsɪst/ | n. | 经验主义者 | A person who believes that knowledge comes primarily from sensory experience. |  John Locke was a prominent empiricist philosopher.  约翰·洛克是一位杰出的经验主义哲学家。

Empiricists conduct experiments to test their theories. 经验主义者进行实验来检验他们的理论。 | 近义词: experimentalist, pragmatist | 反义词: rationalist, theorist | 词性: noun
