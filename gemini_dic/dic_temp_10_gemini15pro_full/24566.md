brocaded:/brəˈkeɪdɪd/
adjective
织锦的；用织锦装饰的
Woven with a raised design, or decorated with brocade.
The queen wore a brocaded gown. 女王穿着一件织锦长袍。
近义词: embroidered, figured, tapestry, woven
反义词: plain, simple
词性: adjective

brocade:/brəˈkeɪd/
noun
织锦
a rich fabric woven with a raised pattern, typically with gold or silver thread.
The cushions were made of a beautiful silk brocade.  这些垫子是用漂亮的丝绸织锦做的。
近义词: tapestry, damask
反义词: None
词性: noun

Etymology: From Italian *broccato*, past participle of *broccare* "to decorate with studs," from *brocco* "small nail, sprout, spike," ultimately from Vulgar Latin *brocca* "pointed tool."
