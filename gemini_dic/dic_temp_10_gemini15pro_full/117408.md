medicinally:/məˈdɪsɪnəli/
adverb
药用地；医学上地
In a medicinal way; for medical purposes.
This plant is used medicinally to treat fevers.  这种植物药用上用来治疗发烧。
The herb is medicinally valuable. 这种草药具有药用价值。
Synonyms: therapeutically, remedially, curatively
Antonyms: harmfully, detrimentally
词性: 副词
