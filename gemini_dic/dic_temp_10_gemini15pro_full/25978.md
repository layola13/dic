bursar: /ˈbɜːrsər/ (UK), /ˈbɜːrsər/ (US) | n. | 财务主管，会计 | A person who manages the financial affairs of a college or university. | The bursar handles all the tuition payments and student account inquiries.  财务主管处理所有的学费缴纳和学生账户查询。| Synonyms: treasurer, cashier | Antonyms: debtor | noun


bursar: /ˈbɜːrsər/ (UK), /ˈbɜːrsər/ (US) | n. | （尤指英国私立学校的）奖学金获得者 | A student who receives financial aid to attend a school, especially a private school in the UK. | He was a bursar at Eton College. 他是伊顿公学的奖学金获得者。| Synonyms: scholar, recipient | Antonyms: donor, benefactor | noun
