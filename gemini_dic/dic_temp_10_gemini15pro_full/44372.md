cross-court:/ˌkrɒs ˈkɔːrt/ /ˌkrɑːs ˈkɔːrt/ | adj. adv.|斜对角的，斜对角地|Hitting the ball diagonally across the court, typically in racquet sports.  在球场上斜对角击球，通常指在球拍运动中。|例句：He played a brilliant cross-court shot. 他打了一个漂亮的斜线球。|近义词：diagonal, angled|反义词:down-the-line|词性:形容词, 副词

cross-court:/ˌkrɒs ˈkɔːrt/ /ˌkrɑːs ˈkɔːrt/ | n.|斜线球|A shot hit diagonally across the court. 斜对角的击球|例句：Her cross-court was too powerful for her opponent. 她的斜线球对于她的对手来说太强大了。|近义词：diagonal shot|反义词:down-the-line shot|词性:名词
