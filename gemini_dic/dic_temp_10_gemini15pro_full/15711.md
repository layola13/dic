balance:/ˈbæləns/| n. v.|平衡，均衡；余额；结余；使平衡；使均衡|A state of equilibrium or equipoise; equality of distribution.|例句：The gymnast maintained her balance on the beam. 体操运动员在平衡木上保持平衡。|近义词：equilibrium, stability, symmetry, harmony|反义词：imbalance, instability, asymmetry, discord|noun
balance:/ˈbæləns/| v.|平衡，均衡；权衡；抵消|To bring into or maintain in a state of equilibrium; to counteract or offset.|例句：She balanced the book on her head. 她把书顶在头上保持平衡。|近义词：equalize, stabilize, counterbalance, offset|反义词：destabilize, unbalance, upset|verb
