mahomentanize: /məˈhoʊmɪntənaɪz/  /məˈhɒmɪntənaɪz/
verb
使...短暂化；使...转瞬即逝
To make something last only a moment; to render ephemeral.
The fireworks mahomentanized the night sky with bursts of color.  烟花使夜空短暂地绽放出绚丽的色彩。
The artist attempted to mahomentanize the feeling of joy in his painting.  艺术家试图在他的画作中捕捉稍纵即逝的喜悦之情。
近义词: ephemerize, fleetingize (these are less common)
反义词: eternalize, perpetuate
词性: verb
