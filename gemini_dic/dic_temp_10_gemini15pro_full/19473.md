biddabilities:/ˌbɪdəˈbɪlətiz/
|n.|可投标性；可竞标性|The capacity or suitability of something to be bid on.  The various factors that influence whether or not something is likely to attract bids.|The biddabilities of the government contracts were assessed by the independent committee. 政府合同的可投标性由独立委员会评估。|synonyms: attractiveness, desirability, viability|antonyms: unattractiveness, undesirability, unviability|noun

biddability:/ˌbɪdəˈbɪləti/
|n.|可投标性；可竞标性|The capacity or suitability of something to be bid on.|The biddability of the project was questioned due to its complexity.  由于项目的复杂性，其可投标性受到了质疑。|synonyms: offerability, tenderability|antonyms: unattractiveness, undesirability|noun

词源:  "biddability" comes from "biddable" + "-ity".  "Biddable" itself comes from "bid" (meaning "to offer a price") + "-able" (a suffix meaning "capable of being").  The suffix "-ity" turns an adjective into a noun denoting a state or quality.
