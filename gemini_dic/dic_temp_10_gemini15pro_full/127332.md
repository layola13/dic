nazaritic:/ˌnæzəˈrɪtɪk/| n.|拿细耳人|A person who took a vow of abstinence, especially from alcohol, as described in the Book of Numbers in the Bible.|例句：Samson was a famous Nazaritic. 参孙是一位著名的拿细耳人。|例句：The Nazaritic  vowed not to cut his hair. 拿细耳人发誓不剪头发。|近义词：ascetic|反义词：hedonist|词性:名词
