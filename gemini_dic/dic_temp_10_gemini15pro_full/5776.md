amalgam:/əˈmælɡəm/| n.|汞合金，混合物|A mixture or blend of different elements.|The band's music is an amalgam of jazz, rock, and blues. 这个乐队的音乐融合了爵士、摇滚和蓝调。|Example: The city is an amalgam of old and new buildings. 这座城市是新旧建筑的混合体。|Synonyms: mixture, blend, combination, fusion, compound|Antonyms: separation, division, segregation|noun

amalgamate:/əˈmælɡəˌmeɪt/| v.|合并，混合|To combine or unite to form one organization or structure.|The two companies amalgamated to form a larger corporation. 这两家公司合并成了一家更大的公司。|Example:  Several small businesses amalgamated to form a single strong competitor. 几家小企业合并成一个强大的竞争对手。|Synonyms: merge, combine, unite, consolidate, integrate|Antonyms: separate, divide, split, disintegrate|verb
