militated:/ˈmɪlɪteɪtɪd/
verb
发生作用；产生影响；构成不利因素
to have a powerful effect, especially against something
His poor health militated against his chances of success. 他糟糕的健康状况对他成功的机会产生了不利影响。
近义词: count against, work against, go against, argue against, operate against, tell against, weigh against
反义词: contribute to, support, help, aid, assist, promote, encourage
