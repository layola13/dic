ETYMOLOGY->
From Marathon (n.), a plain in Greece, where the Greeks defeated the Persians in 490 BC. A messenger ran from Marathon to Athens (about 26 miles) to announce the victory and died upon arrival.  The modern marathon race commemorates this event.
