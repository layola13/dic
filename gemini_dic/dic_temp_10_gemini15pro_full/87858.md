hideously:/ˈhɪdiəsli/ | adv. | 可怕地，丑陋地 | in a hideous manner; dreadfully; extremely unpleasantly. |  The building was hideously ugly. 这栋建筑丑得可怕。 |  horribly, awfully, dreadfully, frightfully, terribly | beautifully, attractively, pleasantly | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hideousli, from Old French hideusement, from hideus (“hideous”) + -ment (“-ly”).  hideus from Late Latin hideosus, from Latin hispidosus (“rough, bristly, shaggy”), from hispidus (“rough, bristly, shaggy”).
