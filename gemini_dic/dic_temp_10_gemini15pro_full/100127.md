interprets:/ɪnˈtɜːrprɪts/| v. |解释，理解，诠释|explains the meaning of something; understands something in a particular way|例句：He interprets the poem as a metaphor for life. 他把这首诗理解为人生的隐喻。|近义词：explains, understands, construes, elucidates|反义词：misinterprets, misunderstands|词性：动词
Etymology: From Old French interpreter, from Latin interpretari "to explain, expound, interpret," from interpres "agent, broker, interpreter,"
