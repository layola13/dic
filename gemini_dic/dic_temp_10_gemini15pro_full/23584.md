braked:/breɪkt/
verb
刹车；制动
past tense and past participle of *brake* - slowed or stopped by using a brake.
The car braked suddenly to avoid hitting the deer. 汽车突然刹车以避免撞到鹿。
The driver braked hard at the red light. 司机在红灯前猛踩刹车。
