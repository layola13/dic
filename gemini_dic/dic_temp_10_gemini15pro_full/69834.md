ferretto:/fəˈrɛtoʊ/
noun
a small, domesticated ferret, especially one kept as a pet.
雪貂，通常指被当作宠物饲养的小型驯养雪貂。
She bought a ferretto from the pet store. 她从宠物店买了一只雪貂。
synonyms: ferret
antonyms: N/A
n.


ferretto:/fəˈrɛtoʊ/
adjective
(of an animal) resembling a ferret in appearance or behavior.
像雪貂的，指动物在外貌或行为上像雪貂。
The small, ferretto-like creature darted into the bushes.  这个像雪貂一样的小动物冲进了灌木丛。
synonyms: ferret-like
antonyms: N/A
adj.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *ferretto*, diminutive of *ferro* ("iron"), from Latin *ferrum* ("iron").  This likely refers to the ferret's iron-grey fur or its use in hunting rabbits from their burrows, perhaps involving iron tools or traps.
