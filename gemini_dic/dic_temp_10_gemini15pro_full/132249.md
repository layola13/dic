nondeclaration:/ˌnɒndɛkləˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 未申报，不声明 |  The act of not declaring something, especially income or goods for tax purposes or customs.  It can also refer to a lack of a formal statement or announcement. |  His nondeclaration of income led to a hefty fine. 他的未申报收入导致了巨额罚款。

The government's nondeclaration of war was criticized by the opposition. 政府不宣战的做法受到了反对派的批评。|  nondisclosure, concealment, omission | declaration, announcement, statement | noun
