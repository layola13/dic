detainable:/dɪˈteɪnəbl/
adjective
可拘留的
Able to be detained; liable to be held in custody.
The suspect was detainable due to the strong evidence against him.  这名嫌疑人由于有强烈的证据指控他而可以被拘留。
The illegal immigrants were detainable pending deportation.  非法移民在等待遣返期间可以被拘留。
Synonym:arrestable, imprisonable
Antonym:releasable
词性:adjective
