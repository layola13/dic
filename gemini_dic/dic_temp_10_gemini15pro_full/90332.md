housing-estates:/ˈhaʊzɪŋ ɪˈsteɪts/| n.| 住宅区，房屋建筑群|A group of houses and other buildings, especially those built at the same time by the same developer.  It often implies a planned residential development.| Many families live in housing estates on the outskirts of the city. 许多家庭住在城市郊区的住宅区。|synonyms: housing developments, residential areas, subdivisions, housing projects|antonyms: slums, ghettos|noun


Etymology:
"housing" from Old English "hūsian" (to house)
"estate" from Old French "estat" (state, condition)
