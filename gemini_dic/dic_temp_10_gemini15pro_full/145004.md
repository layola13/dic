overadjust:/ˌoʊvərəˈdʒʌst/
verb
过度调整；矫枉过正
To adjust something too much, often resulting in a worse situation than before the adjustment.
The thermostat was overadjusted, making the room too cold.  恒温器被过度调整，导致房间太冷。
He overadjusted the brakes, causing the car to skid. 他过度调整了刹车，导致汽车打滑。
近义词: overcompensate, overcorrect
反义词: underadjust
词性: verb

overadjust:/ˌoʊvərəˈdʒʌst/
noun
过度调整
The act of adjusting something too much.
The overadjust of the settings caused the machine to malfunction. 设置的过度调整导致机器故障。
An overadjust in interest rates can destabilize the economy. 利率的过度调整会破坏经济的稳定。
近义词: overcompensation, overcorrection
反义词: underadjustment
词性: noun
