conceiving:/kənˈsiːvɪŋ/
verb
构思，设想，怀孕
forming an idea, plan, or design in the mind; becoming pregnant
She was conceiving a plan to escape. 她正在构思一个逃跑计划。
The architect was conceiving the design for a new museum.  这位建筑师正在构思一个新博物馆的设计。
synonyms: imagining, envisioning, formulating, devising, ideating, gestating
antonyms: neglecting, ignoring, dismissing
verb

conceiving:/kənˈsiːvɪŋ/
verb
怀孕
becoming pregnant
They were having trouble conceiving a child. 他们很难怀上孩子。
She was conceiving her first child. 她怀上了她的第一个孩子。
synonyms: gestating, pregnant
antonyms: infertile, barren
