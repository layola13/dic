parboil:/ˈpɑːrˌbɔɪl/ | v. | 部分煮熟，预煮 | To boil food briefly, usually until partially cooked, as a preliminary cooking step.  | I parboiled the potatoes before roasting them. 我在烤土豆之前先把它们煮了一下。

The chef parboiled the green beans to brighten their color.  厨师将青豆焯水以使其颜色更鲜亮。

| 近义词: blanch, precook | 反义词: overcook | 词性: 动词
