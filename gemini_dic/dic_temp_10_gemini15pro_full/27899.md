cantata:/kənˈtɑːtə/ | n. |清唱剧；康塔塔|A medium-length narrative piece of music for voices with instrumental accompaniment, typically with solos, chorus, and recitative sections.|Bach wrote many beautiful cantatas. 巴赫写了很多优美的清唱剧。|The choir performed a cantata for Easter.  唱诗班为复活节表演了一首清唱剧。|近义词：oratorio, choral work|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps a short, instrumental piece)|名词
