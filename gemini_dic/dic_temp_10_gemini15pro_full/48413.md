dedolent:/ˈdɛdələnt/| adj.| 懒惰的，不勤奋的；不痛的，无感觉的|Lacking diligence; indolent. Also, insensitive to pain.|He displayed a dedolent attitude towards his studies, preferring to play video games all day. 他对学习表现出懒惰的态度，宁愿整天玩电子游戏。|近义词：indolent, lazy, slothful, apathetic|反义词：diligent, industrious, assiduous|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin dēdolēns (“free from pain, insensitive”), present participle of dēdolēre (“to cease feeling pain”), from dē (“from, away”) + dolēre (“to feel pain”).
