papyrograph: /pəˈpaɪrəɡræf/ /pəˈpaɪroʊɡræf/
noun
纸烙画，烙画器
A method of decorating wood or leather by burning designs into the surface with a heated point.  Also, the design or picture produced by this method, or the instrument used to produce it.
She created a beautiful papyrograph of a bird on a piece of wood. 她在一块木头上创作了一幅美丽的鸟的烙画。
synonyms:  pyrography, woodburning
antonyms:  N/A
词性: 名词

papyrograph: /pəˈpaɪrəɡræf/ /pəˈpaɪroʊɡræf/
verb
用烙画装饰
To decorate wood or leather by burning designs into the surface with a heated point.
He papyrographed a delicate floral pattern onto the leather cover of the book. 他在书的皮革封面上烙上了精致的花卉图案。
synonyms:  pyrograph, woodburn
antonyms:  N/A
词性: 动词
