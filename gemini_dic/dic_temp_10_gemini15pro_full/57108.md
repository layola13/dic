doura:/ˈdʊərə/| n. |高粱|A type of grain sorghum, cultivated for its edible seeds and stalks, primarily in Africa and Asia.  It is a drought-tolerant crop and a staple food in many regions.|The farmers planted doura to withstand the dry season. 农民们种植高粱以抵御旱季。|Synonyms: sorghum, milo maize|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a specific crop.
