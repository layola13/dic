metaleptic:/ˌmɛtəˈlɛptɪk/ | adj. | 转喻的; 超越常规的; (修辞)远隔的 | A figure of speech consisting in the description of one thing by reference to another thing associated with it.  A narrative technique in which the boundary between the fictional world and the real world is breached. | The author uses a metaleptic device where the narrator comments on the reader's potential reaction to the story. 作者运用了一种转喻手法，叙述者评论了读者对故事的潜在反应。 |  transumptive, far-fetched, unconventional | conventional, ordinary | adjective
