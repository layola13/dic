instigate:/ˈɪnstɪɡeɪt/| v. |煽动，唆使，激起|To urge forward; provoke or incite.|The rebels instigated a riot in the city. 叛乱分子在城市里煽动了一场暴乱。|Synonyms: incite, provoke, goad, abet, foment|Antonyms: prevent, deter, discourage, suppress|verb

instigate:/ˈɪnstɪɡeɪt/| v. |教唆，怂恿|To bring about or initiate (an action or event).|He instigated a campaign to raise awareness about environmental issues. 他发起了一场提高环境意识的运动。|Synonyms: initiate, launch, start, commence|Antonyms: halt, stop, end, finish|verb
