asthenic:/æsˈθɛnɪk/
adjective
无力气的；虚弱的；衰弱的
Characterized by asthenia; weak.  Lacking energy or strength.
例句：The asthenic patient was too weak to sit up in bed. 虚弱的病人太虚弱了，无法在床上坐起来。

例句：His asthenic condition made it difficult for him to perform even simple tasks.  他虚弱的身体状况使他连简单的任务都难以完成。

近义词：weak, feeble, frail, debilitated, enervated
反义词：strong, robust, vigorous, energetic
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *asthenēs* (weak), from *a-* (without) + *sthenos* (strength).
