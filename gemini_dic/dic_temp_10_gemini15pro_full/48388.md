dedecorate: /diːˈdɛkəˌreɪt/
verb
去除装饰
to remove decorations from something
They decided to dedecorate the Christmas tree in January. 他们决定在一月份拆除圣诞树上的装饰。
The landlord asked us to dedecorate the apartment before moving out.  房东要求我们在搬出去之前把公寓里的装饰都拆掉。
strip, dismantle, undecorate
decorate, adorn, embellish
v.
