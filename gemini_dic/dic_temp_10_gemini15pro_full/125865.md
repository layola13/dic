muscats:/ˈmʌskəts/ | n. |麝香葡萄(通常指白葡萄品种); 麝香葡萄酒|A sweet white wine made from muscat grapes.  A type of grape used to make sweet wine. |例句：I enjoy a glass of chilled muscats on a warm evening. 我喜欢在温暖的夜晚享用一杯冰镇麝香葡萄酒。

|例句：These muscats are incredibly fragrant and juicy. 这些麝香葡萄香气浓郁，汁水饱满。 |近义词：Muscatel, Moscato |反义词: N/A (No direct antonym for a type of grape or wine) |词性: noun


Etymology: From Old French *muscat, from Medieval Latin muscatum,  ultimately from Late Latin muscus (“musk”), due to the musky aroma of the grapes.
