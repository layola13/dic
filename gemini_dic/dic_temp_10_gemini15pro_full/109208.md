lei:/ˈleɪ/| n.|花环，花圈|A wreath of flowers and leaves, worn around the neck, especially in Hawaii as a symbol of welcome or affection. |例句：She placed a fragrant lei around his neck. 她在他脖子上戴了一个芬芳的花环。

例句：The dancers wore colorful leis made of orchids.  舞者们戴着五彩缤纷的兰花花环。|近义词：garland, wreath|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
