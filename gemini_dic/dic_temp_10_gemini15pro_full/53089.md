diphtheritic:/ˌdɪfθəˈrɪtɪk/
adjective
白喉的；患白喉的
Relating to or suffering from diphtheria, a serious bacterial infection affecting the mucous membranes of the nose and throat.
The child exhibited diphtheritic symptoms, including a sore throat and difficulty breathing.  这个孩子表现出白喉症状，包括喉咙痛和呼吸困难。
The doctor diagnosed a diphtheritic infection. 医生诊断出白喉感染。
Synonyms: diphtherial, diphtheroid
Antonyms: healthy, well
词性: adjective
