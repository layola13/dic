broken:/ˈbroʊkən/| adj.|破碎的，坏掉的；破产的；不完整的；（精神上）崩溃的|Something that is broken is damaged and separated into pieces. It can also refer to a state of being bankrupt, incomplete, or emotionally shattered.|The window is broken. 窗户破了。|近义词：damaged, fractured, bankrupt, ruined, shattered|反义词：intact, whole, repaired, fixed, functional
词性:形容词
