mismanages:/ˌmɪsˈmænɪdʒɪz/
verb
管理不善，处理不当
fails to manage or handle something effectively
The company mismanaged its resources and went bankrupt.  这家公司管理不善，最终破产。
mishandles, mishandles
manages well, handles efficiently
