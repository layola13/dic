clamlike:/ˈklæmlaɪk/| adj.|像蛤蜊的，蛤蜊似的|Resembling a clam, especially in being closed or quiet.  指类似蛤蜊，尤指在外形上紧闭或性格上沉默寡言。|The child sat clamlike, refusing to speak.  那孩子像蛤蜊一样坐着，拒绝说话。|近义词：tight-lipped, reticent, uncommunicative|反义词：talkative, loquacious, open|词性:形容词

Etymology: clam +‎ -like.  From *clam* (meaning the shellfish) and *-like* (a suffix meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of").
