friedel-crafts:/ˈfriːdəl kræfts/| adj.| 傅-克反应的；傅-克酰基化的 |Relating to or denoting a class of reactions in which an alkyl or acyl group is added to an aromatic ring by the action of a Lewis acid catalyst.| Friedel-Crafts alkylation is a common method for introducing alkyl groups onto aromatic rings. 傅-克烷基化是将烷基引入芳香环的常用方法。|  Friedel-Crafts acylation is used to synthesize aromatic ketones. 傅-克酰基化用于合成芳香酮。|近义词:electrophilic aromatic substitution (更广义)|反义词: 无|词性:形容词

friedel:/ˈfriːdəl/| n.| 傅列德尔 (人名；指 Charles Friedel)|The surname of Charles Friedel, a French chemist who co-discovered the Friedel-Crafts reaction.| Charles Friedel was a prominent 19th-century chemist. 查尔斯·傅列德尔是 19 世纪杰出的化学家。| He collaborated with James Crafts on the Friedel-Crafts reaction. 他与詹姆斯·克拉夫茨合作研究了傅-克反应。| 近义词: 无| 反义词: 无| 词性: 名词
