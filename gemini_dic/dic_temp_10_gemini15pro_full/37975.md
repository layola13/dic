compliantly:/kəmˈplaɪəntli/
adverb
顺从地，服从地
in a way that obeys rules or requests
The company compliantly followed the new regulations.  这家公司顺从地遵守了新的规章制度。
She compliantly agreed to his request. 她顺从地同意了他的请求。
近义词:obediently, submissively, dutifully
反义词:rebelliously, defiantly, resistant

词性:adverb
