crustiness:/ˈkrʌstinɪs/
n.

The state or quality of being crusty.
The crustiness of the old man's demeanor was off-putting. 老人的举止粗暴无礼，令人反感。

crusty:/ˈkrʌsti/
adj.
硬皮的; 脾气坏的; 执拗的
Having a hard crust; bad-tempered and uncooperative.
The crusty bread was perfect for dipping in soup.  这块硬皮面包非常适合蘸汤吃。
He gave a crusty reply to the journalist's question. 他粗暴地回答了记者的提问。
近义词:hard, crisp, gruff, surly
反义词:soft, tender, polite, amiable
词性:形容词


词源: From crust + -y.  Ultimately from Latin crusta "crust, rind, shell."
