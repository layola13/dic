administering: /ədˈmɪnɪstərɪŋ/
verb
管理，执行；给予，施用
The nurse is administering the medicine to the patient.  护士正在给病人用药。
Giving or applying something, such as medication or a test.  给予或应用某些东西，例如药物或测试。
The manager is administering the company's finances. 经理正在管理公司的财务。
Supervising or managing something. 监督或管理某些东西。

近义词: managing, directing, applying, dispensing, conducting, implementing
反义词: neglecting, ignoring

词性: v. (动词)
词源: From Latin *administrāre, from ad- + ministrāre (“to serve, attend, manage”).

USAGE->

The government administers social welfare programs. = 政府管理社会福利项目。
He administers the school's computer network. = 他管理学校的计算机网络。
