moundy:/ˈmaʊndi/| adj.|土墩状的，隆起的，多土堆的|Covered with mounds; rising in mounds.  |The landscape was moundy, dotted with small hills.  这片景观是土丘状的，点缀着小山。|The trail led through a moundy field. 这条小路穿过一片满是土堆的田野。|近义词：hilly, bumpy, uneven|反义词:flat, level, smooth|词性:形容词
Etymology: From *mound* + *-y*. *Mound* comes from Middle English *mount*, from Old French *mont*, from Latin *montem*, the accusative of *mons* "mountain." The suffix *-y* forms adjectives meaning "full of" or "characterized by."
