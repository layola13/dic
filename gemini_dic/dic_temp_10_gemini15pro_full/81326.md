grapheme:/ˈɡræfiːm/ | n. | 字素 | A written symbol representing a phoneme; the smallest meaningful contrastive unit in a writing system. |  The grapheme "ph" represents the phoneme /f/ in English. 字素"ph"在英语中代表音素/f/。 | synonym: character, letter, symbol | antonym: phoneme | noun
