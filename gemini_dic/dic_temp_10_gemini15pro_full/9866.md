apagogic:/ˌæpəˈɡɒdʒɪk/ |adj.|  推理的，反证的，间接证明的 | Relating to a method of proof by showing that the denial of the proposition to be proved leads to a contradiction.| The apagogic argument demonstrated that the initial assumption was false.  反证法论证表明最初的假设是错误的。|  deductive, indirect | direct | adjective

apagogic:/ˌæpəˈɡɒdʒɪk/ |n.| 反证法，间接证明 | A method of proof by showing that the denial of the proposition to be proved leads to a contradiction.|  He used apagogic reasoning to prove his point. 他用反证法来证明他的观点。| reductio ad absurdum, proof by contradiction | direct proof | noun
