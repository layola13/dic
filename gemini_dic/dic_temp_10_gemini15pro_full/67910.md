face-up-to:/feɪs ˈʌp tuː/| phrasal verb |面对，正视，承担|to confront or deal with a difficult situation or person|例句：He finally faced up to his responsibilities. 他最终承担起了自己的责任。

例句：She had to face up to the fact that she was no longer young. 她不得不正视自己不再年轻的事实。

近义词：confront, deal with, tackle, address|反义词：avoid, evade, ignore, shirk|词性：动词短语
