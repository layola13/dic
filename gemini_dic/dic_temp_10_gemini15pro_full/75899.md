fundholding:/ˈfʌndˌhəʊldɪŋ/
noun
基金持有；（尤指英国）医疗预算持有
The practice of allocating funds to organizations or individuals, especially in the context of healthcare, giving them responsibility for managing those funds.
GP fundholding was introduced in the UK in the 1990s.  在20世纪90年代，英国引入了全科医生基金持有制度。
Fundholding allowed hospitals to manage their own budgets. 基金持有允许医院管理自己的预算。
Synonym: budget management, resource allocation
Antonym: centralized funding
