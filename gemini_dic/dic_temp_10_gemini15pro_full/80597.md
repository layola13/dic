googol:/ˈɡuːɡɒl/ (US) /ˈɡuːɡɔːl/ (UK)| n. |古戈尔；10的100次方|A googol is the large number 10 to the power of 100 (1 followed by 100 zeros).  It's a number larger than the estimated number of atoms in the observable universe.|The number of possible chess games is estimated to be less than a googol.  古戈尔数小于国际象棋可能棋局的总数。

There are more stars in the universe than grains of sand on all the beaches on Earth, but even that number is less than a googol. 宇宙中的星星比地球上所有沙滩上的沙粒还多，但即使是这个数字也小于古戈尔。|近义词: (none - it's a unique number) |反义词: (none - it's a unique number)|noun
