heatedly:/ˈhiːtɪdli/ /ˈhitɪdli/
adverb
激烈地，热烈地
in a heated manner; with passion and excitement.
The two candidates debated the issue heatedly.  两位候选人激烈地辩论了这个问题。
They argued heatedly about the new policy. 他们就新政策进行了激烈的争论。

近义词: fiercely, passionately, vehemently, angrily, furiously
反义词: calmly, coolly, peacefully, quietly
词性: adverb

词源: From the adjective "heated" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Heated" comes from the verb "heat," which ultimately derives from Proto-Germanic *haitaną.
