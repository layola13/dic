bcc:/ˌbiː siː ˈsiː/ | n. | 密送，副本抄送 | Blind Carbon Copy, an email feature that allows the sender to send a copy of the message to additional recipients without the primary recipients knowing. | Example: I sent the email to John and bcc'd Mary. 我把邮件发给了约翰，并密送给了玛丽。| Synonym: carbon copy (cc, though not secret), blind copy | Antonym: to (primary recipient) | noun

bcc:/ˌbiː siː ˈsiː/ | v. |  密送，副本抄送 | To send a blind carbon copy of an email or other message. | Example:  I bcc'd my boss on the email to the client. 我把发给客户的邮件密送给了我的老板。| Synonym: carbon copy (cc, though not secret), blind copy | Antonym: send directly | verb
