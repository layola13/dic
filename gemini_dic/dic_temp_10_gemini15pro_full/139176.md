object:/ˈɒbdʒɪkt/ (UK) /ˈɑːbdʒekt/ (US)| n. vt.| 物体；目标；宾语；反对|Something physical that can be seen or touched; a goal or purpose; the thing or person that is affected by the action of a verb; to disagree with or oppose something.|

例句：
The object of the game is to score the most points.
这个游戏的目标是获得最高分。

The shiny object caught my eye.
那个闪亮的物体吸引了我的目光。


近义词：thing, item, article, target, aim, goal, oppose, disagree|
反义词: subject, support, agree|
词性: noun, verb


object:/əbˈdʒekt/ (UK) /əbˈdʒekt/ (US)| v. | 反对；不赞成|To express or feel disapproval or opposition to something.|

例句：
He objected to the proposal.
他反对这项提议。

I object to being treated like a child.
我反对被当作孩子对待。


近义词：oppose, disagree, protest, challenge|
反义词: support, agree, approve|
