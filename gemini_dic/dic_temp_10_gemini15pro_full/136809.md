nonshipper:/ˌnɒnˈʃɪpər/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈʃɪpə(r)/ (UK)| n. |非CP粉，不嗑CP的人|A person who does not engage in or support the romantic pairing of fictional characters (shipping).|例句：She's a nonshipper and doesn't understand the appeal of fanfiction. 她是一个非CP粉，不理解同人小说的吸引力。|近义词：antishipper (sometimes), casual fan|反义词:shipper|词性:名词
