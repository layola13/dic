meth:/mɛθ/ | n. | 冰毒; 甲基苯丙胺 |Crystallized methamphetamine, a powerful and highly addictive stimulant drug. |He was arrested for possession of meth. 他因持有冰毒被捕。|近义词：crystal meth, methamphetamine, speed, crank |反义词: N/A |词性:名词

词源: Shortened from methamphetamine. Methamphetamine itself is a clipped compound of N-methylamphetamine, which refers to the chemical structure of the drug.
