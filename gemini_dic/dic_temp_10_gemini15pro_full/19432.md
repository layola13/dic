bicoastal:/ˌbaɪˈkoʊstl/
adjective
双海岸的；来往于两岸的
Living or working on both the East and West Coasts of the United States, especially between New York and California.
She has a bicoastal life, splitting her time between New York and Los Angeles. 她过着双海岸的生活，时间分别分配在纽约和洛杉矶。
synonyms: transcontinental
antonyms: regional, local
词性: adjective
