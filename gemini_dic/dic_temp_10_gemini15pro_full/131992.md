nonconvivialities: /ˌnɒnkənˌvɪviˈælɪtiz/
noun
不友好；不欢快；不 convivial 的状态或场合
Lack of conviviality; the state or quality of being unconvivial; an instance of this.  Absence of cheerfulness or good company.

The nonconvivialities of the meeting made everyone uncomfortable.
这次会议的不友好气氛让每个人都不舒服。

His sullen silence added to the general nonconvivialities.
他阴沉的沉默加剧了整体的不愉快气氛。

Synonym:  unsociability, dourness, sullenness, gloom, unfriendliness
Antonym: conviviality, sociability, friendliness, cheerfulness, warmth
词性: noun
