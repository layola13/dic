methadone:/ˈmɛθəˌdoʊn/ | n. | 美沙酮 | A synthetic opioid drug used as a pain reliever and as a substitute for heroin or other opioid drugs in the treatment of addiction. |  The doctor prescribed methadone to help him overcome his heroin addiction. 医生给他开了美沙酮来帮助他戒除海洛因毒瘾。 | synonyms: dolophine, amidone | antonyms:  naloxone, naltrexone | noun


methadones: /ˈmɛθəˌdoʊnz/ | n. | 美沙酮（复数）| Plural form of methadone, referring to multiple doses or types of methadone. | The clinic dispenses methadones to patients daily.  这家诊所每天向病人分发美沙酮。| synonyms: dolophines, amidones | antonyms: N/A (plural of an antonym doesn't necessarily apply) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From German _Methadon_, a contraction of _Methylaminopropionoxyphenylheptanon_ (methylamino-propion-oxy-phenyl-heptanone), describing its chemical structure.
