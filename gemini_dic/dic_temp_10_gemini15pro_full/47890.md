decence:/ˈdiːsənsi/| n.|得体；体面；合宜|behavior that conforms to accepted standards of morality or respectability|She behaved with utmost decence throughout the difficult situation.  她在困境中始终保持着最大的得体。|近义词：propriety, decorum, modesty|反义词：indecency, impropriety|词性:名词
decency:/ˈdiːsənsi/| n.|礼貌；规矩；合乎礼仪|conformity to the recognized standards of propriety, good taste, modesty, etc.|He had the decency to apologize. 他很有礼貌地道歉了。|近义词：courtesy, politeness|反义词：rudeness, impoliteness|词性:名词
