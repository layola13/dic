nda:/ˈɛn diː ˈeɪ/ | abbreviation | 保密协议; 不披露协议 | Non-Disclosure Agreement. A legally binding contract that establishes a confidential relationship between parties, typically to protect sensitive information shared during business negotiations or professional collaborations. |  We signed an NDA before discussing the terms of the merger. 我们在讨论合并条款之前签署了保密协议。 | synonyms: confidentiality agreement, secrecy agreement | antonyms: disclosure agreement | n.
