dignifying: /ˈdɪɡnɪfaɪɪŋ/
adjective
使高贵的；使有尊严的
Making something or someone seem worthy and impressive.
She handled the difficult situation with a dignifying grace. 她以一种高贵优雅的方式处理了这个困难的局面。
dignified, ennobling, elevating
demeaning, degrading
adjective
