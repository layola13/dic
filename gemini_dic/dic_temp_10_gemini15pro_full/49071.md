dehydrated:/diːˈhaɪdreɪtɪd/
adjective
脱水的；干燥的
Removed of water; dried.
The dehydrated hiker was suffering from severe thirst.  脱水的徒步旅行者口渴得厉害。
dehydrated fruit  水果干
synonyms: dried, parched, desiccated
antonyms: hydrated, moist, saturated
词性:形容词


dehydrate:/diːˈhaɪdreɪt/
verb
脱水；使干燥
To remove water from; to dry.
The sun can dehydrate your skin very quickly.  太阳会很快使你的皮肤脱水。
You need to dehydrate these herbs for storage. 你需要将这些草药脱水以进行储存。
synonyms: dry, desiccate, parch
antonyms: hydrate, moisten, saturate
词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "remove") + hydrate (to combine with water).  Ultimately from Greek hydor (water).
词源: 由de-（前缀，意为“去除”）+ hydrate（与水结合）组成。最终源自希腊语 hydor（水）。
