correspondingly:/ˌkɔːrəˈspɑːndɪŋli/ /ˌkɔːrɪˈspɑːndɪŋli/
adverb
相应地，对应地
In a way that relates to or matches something else.
As the cost of living rises, salaries correspondingly increase.  随着生活成本的上升，工资也相应增加。
Demand has increased, and correspondingly, prices have risen.  需求增加了，相应地，价格也上涨了。
近义词: accordingly, consequently, therefore, thus
反义词:  independently, unrelatedly
词性: adverb

词源:
From correspond + -ing + -ly.  Correspond ultimately derives from Latin *com-respondēre 'to answer together,' from com- 'together' + respondēre 'to answer.'
