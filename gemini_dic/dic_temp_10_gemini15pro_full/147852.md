overran:/ˌoʊvərˈræn/ | v. | 蔓延，泛滥；超过（预算、时间等）；攻占，侵占 | To spread over and occupy or overrun, as a flood, plant, or animal does; to exceed a limit, such as a budget or schedule; to invade and occupy (a place) by force. |  The weeds overran the garden. 杂草蔓过了花园。| synonyms:  overwhelm, overrun, swamp, infest, invade, conquer | antonyms: retreat, withdraw, surrender, abandon | verb

overran:/ˌoʊvərˈræn/ | v. | 超过（预算、时间等）| To exceed a limit, such as a budget or schedule. | The project overran its budget by 20%.  这个项目超支了20%。| synonyms: exceed, surpass, overstep | antonyms: underestimate, underspend | verb
