anlaut:/ˈanˌlaʊt/
noun
首音，词首音
The initial sound of a word.
The anlaut of "cat" is /k/.  “cat”的首音是/k/。
近义词:onset, initial sound
反义词:auslaut (final sound)
词性:n.
