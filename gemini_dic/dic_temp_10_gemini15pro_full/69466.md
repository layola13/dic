feistier:/ˈfaɪstiər/ | adj. | 更活跃的，更有精神的，更爱争吵的 | More lively, spirited, and quarrelsome than before; more likely to argue or fight. |  She's become much feistier since her promotion.  她升职后变得更有活力了。

The negotiations became feistier as the deadline approached. 随着截止日期的临近，谈判变得更加激烈了。| 近义词:  spirited, lively, pugnacious, belligerent, combative | 反义词:  meek, mild, submissive, docile | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "feisty" + "-er" (comparative suffix).  "Feisty" is of uncertain origin, possibly related to "fist" suggesting a readiness to fight.
