intermeddle:/ˌɪntərˈmedl/| v. |干涉，干预，插手|To interfere in something that is not one's concern.|例句：He was always intermeddling in other people's affairs. 他总是干涉别人的事情。

例句：She intermeddled in the dispute between her neighbors. 她插手了她邻居之间的纠纷。


|近义词：interfere, meddle, intrude|反义词:ignore, disregard|词性:动词
