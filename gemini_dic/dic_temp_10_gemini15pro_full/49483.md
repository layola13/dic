n.

delusive:/dɪˈluːsɪv/
adj.
欺骗性的；虚妄的；迷惑的
Giving a false or misleading impression.
The promise of easy money proved delusive. 轻松赚钱的承诺被证明是骗人的。
She had a delusive hope that he would change his mind. 她抱有他会改变主意的虚妄的希望。
近义词:deceptive, misleading, fallacious, illusory
反义词:true, real, accurate, genuine
词性:形容词

delude:/dɪˈluːd/
v.
欺骗；迷惑
Impose a misleading belief upon (someone); deceive.
He deluded himself into thinking he could win. 他欺骗自己，以为自己能赢。
She was deluded by his charm. 她被他的魅力所迷惑。
近义词:deceive, mislead, trick, fool
反义词:enlighten, inform, tell the truth
词性:动词

词源: From Latin *dēlūs-, from dē- ‘down, away’ + lūdō ‘I play, mock’.
