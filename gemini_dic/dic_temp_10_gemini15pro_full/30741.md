cesarean:/sɪˈzɛəriən/ /sɪˈzær.i.ən/ | n. |剖腹产|A surgical operation for delivering a child by cutting through the wall of the abdomen and the uterus. |例句：She had a cesarean section for her first child. 她第一个孩子是剖腹产的。

例句：The doctor recommended a cesarean delivery due to complications. 由于并发症，医生建议剖腹产。|近义词：C-section |反义词:vaginal birth|名词
