chouka: /ˈtʃuːkə/ | n. | 中华料理，中华菜肴 | A Japanese term derived from the Chinese word "中華" (zhōnghuá), meaning Chinese cuisine. It generally refers to Japanese-style Chinese cuisine, which has evolved separately from authentic Chinese food. | Example: I ate chuka for lunch today. 我今天中午吃了中华料理。| Example: This restaurant specializes in chuka. 这家餐厅专营中华料理。| Synonym: Chinese food (in a Japanese context), | Antonym: Washoku (Japanese cuisine) | noun
