macrons:/ˈmeɪkrɒnz/ (US), /ˈmækrɒnz/ (UK)| n.|长音符号|A diacritical mark placed above a vowel to indicate a long vowel sound, especially in Latin and Greek.|The word "rēs" uses a macron to show that the 'e' is long.|近义词:long vowel mark|反义词:breve|noun
