membranous:/ˈmɛmbrənəs/ | adj. | 膜的；膜状的 | Relating to, resembling, or consisting of a membrane or membranes. |  The lungs are covered by a thin, membranous sac called the pleura.  肺被一层薄薄的膜状囊，称为胸膜所覆盖。

The baby's skull is still membranous in places. 婴儿的头骨有些地方仍然是膜状的。 |  Near synonyms: filmy, membrane-like, membranaceous | Near antonyms: solid, thick | word type: adjective
