mathemagicians:/ˌmæθəməˈdʒɪʃənz/| n. |数学魔术师|A person who is exceptionally skilled in mathematics, often combining mathematical ability with a flair for presentation or teaching, making mathematics seem like magic.  This is a playful and informal term.|例句：The mathemagicians wowed the audience with their rapid calculations. 这些数学魔术师以其快速的计算能力让观众惊叹不已。

例句：Her professor was a true mathemagician, making complex concepts seem simple. 她的教授是一位真正的数学魔术师，能把复杂的概念变得简单易懂。|近义词：math whiz, math genius, number cruncher|反义词:mathematically challenged|词性:名词
