differentia:/ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃiə/ |n.|区别，差异，特征|A distinguishing mark or characteristic.  It is something that makes an entity or concept distinct from others.|The differentia of humans is their capacity for abstract thought. 人类的特征是他们具有抽象思考的能力。|synonyms: distinction, characteristic, feature, peculiarity, mark, property|antonyms: similarity, sameness, resemblance|noun

differentia:/ˌdɪfəˈrɛnʃiə/ |n.|差分|The amount by which one thing differs from another.  This meaning is primarily used in mathematics and philosophy.|The differentia between the two values is 5. 这两个值之间的差分是5。|synonyms: difference, variance, discrepancy|antonyms: similarity, sameness, equivalence|noun
