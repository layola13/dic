iive: /aɪv/
adjective
活的; 生动的; 实况转播的; 精力充沛的
alive; living; active; lively


English Explanation: "Iive" is likely a misspelling of "live" used as an adjective.  As an adjective, "live" can mean:
1. Alive: living, not dead.
2. (of a broadcast) transmitted at the time of occurrence, not from a recording.
3. (of a performance) given in front of an audience.
4. (of a wire or device) carrying electric current.
5. Full of energy; active.

Example 1: The band played a live concert. 乐队举办了一场现场音乐会。

Example 2: We watched a live broadcast of the football game. 我们观看了足球比赛的现场直播。

Example 3: He caught a live fish in the river. 他在河里抓到一条活鱼。

Example 4: She gave a live demonstration of the new product. 她对新产品进行了现场演示。

Example 5: The wire is live, so be careful! 这根电线带电，所以要小心！

Synonyms: alive, living, active, energetic, lively, vibrant

Antonyms: dead, inactive, dull, lifeless

Word type: adjective
