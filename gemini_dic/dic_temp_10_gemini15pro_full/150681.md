par:/pɑːr/ | n. | 标准；平均水平；票面价值 |  A standard or average level, especially in sports (golf) or finances.  Also, the face value of a stock, bond, etc. | The golfer finished the round two strokes under par.  高尔夫球手完成这一轮比标准杆少两杆。| synonyms: standard, norm, average | antonyms:  exceptional, extraordinary | noun

par:/pɑːr/ | adj. | 平价的；平均的 | Equal to the standard or average. | The company's stock is trading at par. 这家公司的股票以平价交易。 | synonyms: average, normal, standard | antonyms: exceptional, extraordinary | adjective
