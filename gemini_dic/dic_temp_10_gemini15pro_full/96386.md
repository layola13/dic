indiscretionary:/ˌɪndɪˈskreʃəneri/
adjective
无须判断的；无需斟酌的；不需自行决定的
Not requiring or involving discretion; not left to individual judgment.
The fund manager has indiscretionary power over the investments.  基金经理对投资拥有无须判断的权力。
His role was largely indiscretionary, simply carrying out orders. 他的角色很大程度上是不需要自行决定的，只是执行命令。
近义词: non-discretionary, mandatory, obligatory, compulsory
反义词: discretionary, optional
词性: adjective
词源:  From in- (not) + discretionary (requiring discretion).  Discretionary comes from Latin discretionem (discernment, separation), from discernere (to distinguish).
