accessive:/əkˈsɛsɪv/| adj.|过度的，过分的，极端的|Going to extremes; exceeding the usual or proper limits.|His accessive drinking led to health problems. = 他过度饮酒导致了健康问题。

Her accessive spending put her in debt. = 她过度消费使她负债累累。|近义词：excessive, immoderate, inordinate, extreme|反义词：moderate, reasonable, temperate|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *accessus, past participle of accedere (“to approach, to be added to”), from ad (“to”) + cedere (“to go, yield”).
USAGE->
This word is typically used to describe something that is beyond a normal or acceptable limit, often with negative connotations.

She showed accessive zeal in completing the project. =  她在完成项目时表现出过分的热情。

His accessive pride led him to make some poor decisions. = 他过度的骄傲使他做出了一些糟糕的决定。
