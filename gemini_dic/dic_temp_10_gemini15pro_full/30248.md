cellulose:/ˈseljʊloʊs/ (US) /ˈseljʊləʊs/ (UK)| n. |纤维素|A complex carbohydrate that is the main constituent of the cell walls of plants.|例句：Cotton is almost pure cellulose. 棉花几乎是纯纤维素。

例句：Cellulose is the most abundant organic polymer on Earth. 纤维素是地球上最丰富的有机聚合物。


近义词：fiber, roughage|反义词: None|词性: noun
