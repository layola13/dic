goal-lines:/ˈɡoʊl laɪnz/ (US), /ˈɡəʊl laɪnz/ (UK)| n. |球门线|The lines marking the ends of a playing field or court, specifically in sports like soccer, rugby, and American football, beyond which points are scored.|The ball crossed the goal-lines, resulting in a touchdown.  球越过了球门线，达阵得分。|Synonym: goal line, end line|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps sideline)|Noun
