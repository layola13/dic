blushingly:/ˈblʌʃɪŋli/| adv.|脸红地，羞涩地|In a way that shows embarrassment or shyness by blushing.|She blushingly accepted the compliment. 她羞涩地接受了赞美。|近义词：shyly, timidly, coyly|反义词：boldly, confidently|词性:副词
Etymology: From blush (v.) + -ing (present participle suffix) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  The verb "blush" comes from Old English *blyscan, related to Old Norse *blysja "to blaze, glow," referring to the reddening of the face.
