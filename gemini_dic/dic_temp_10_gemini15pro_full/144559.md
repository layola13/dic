outwalking:/ˌaʊtˈwɔːkɪŋ/
v.
胜过…的步行；比…走得更远；比…走得更久
Surpassing someone in walking; walking farther or longer than someone else.
He outwalked his companions on the hiking trip. 他在徒步旅行中比同伴走得更远。
近义词: outpace, outhike, outstride
反义词: lag behind
词性: 动词

outwalking:/ˌaʊtˈwɔːkɪŋ/
n.
胜过…的步行；比…走得更远；比…走得更久(的行为)
The act of surpassing someone in walking; walking farther or longer than someone else.
His outwalking of the others demonstrated his superior stamina. 他比其他人走得更远，证明了他的耐力 superior。
近义词: -
反义词: -
词性: 名词
