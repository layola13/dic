galenite:/ɡəˈliːnaɪt/ | n. | 方铅矿 | A common lead sulfide mineral (PbS) and the most important ore of lead. It is a soft, heavy, metallic mineral that typically occurs as cubic crystals. | Galena is the primary ore from which lead is extracted. 方铅矿是提取铅的主要矿石。|  Galenite often occurs with other sulfide minerals like sphalerite and pyrite. 方铅矿经常与其他硫化物矿物，如闪锌矿和黄铁矿一起出现。|  lead ore, lead glance |  | noun
