broadspreading:/brɔːdˈsprɛdɪŋ/
adjective
广泛传播的，散布的
spreading widely or over a large area.
例句：The broadspreading branches of the oak tree provided ample shade. 橡树宽阔的枝条提供了充足的阴凉。

例句：The broadspreading rumor caused widespread panic. 广泛传播的谣言引起了普遍的恐慌。

近义词：widespread, extensive, far-reaching
反义词：limited, localized, confined
词性：adjective


词源:
From broad + spreading, the present participle of spread.
