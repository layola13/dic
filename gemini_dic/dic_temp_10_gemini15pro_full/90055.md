hospitalizes:/ˈhɒspɪtəlaɪzɪz/ (UK), /ˈhɑːspɪtəlaɪzɪz/ (US)
verb
住院治疗
admits or causes someone to be admitted to a hospital for treatment.
The accident victim was hospitalized for several weeks.  事故受害者住院治疗了几个星期。
He was hospitalized with a broken leg. 他因为腿骨折住院了。
synonyms: admit, treat, intern
antonyms: discharge, release
v.
