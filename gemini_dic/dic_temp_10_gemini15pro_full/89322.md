homogenesis:/ˌhɒməˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/ /ˌhoʊmoʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/ | n. |同源发生，同型生殖|The development of different organs from the same embryonic tissues.  The production of offspring that are genetically identical to the parent. |例句：Homogenesis is a common reproductive strategy in some single-celled organisms.  一些单细胞生物中，同型生殖是一种常见的繁殖策略。

例句：The scientist studied homogenesis in plants.  这位科学家研究了植物的同型生殖。|近义词：homogeny, asexual reproduction|反义词：heterogenesis|词性：名词
