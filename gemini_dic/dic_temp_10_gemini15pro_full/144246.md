outsmelt:/ˌaʊtˈsmelt/
verb
熔化胜过; 熔化速度超过
to extract (metal) by smelting; to smelt more or better than another.
The new furnace outsmelted the old one by a significant margin.  新的熔炉的熔炼速度明显超过了旧熔炉。
outsmelted (past tense)
outsmelted (past participle)
outsmelting (present participle)
outsmelts (3rd person singular present)
近义词: outproduce, surpass, exceed
反义词: underperform
词性: verb
