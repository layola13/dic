beefed:/biːft/ | adj. | 强壮的，肌肉发达的 |  (Slang)  Muscular, physically strong. | He's been working out and is looking pretty beefed up these days. 他一直在健身，这些天看起来肌肉很发达。| Synonyms: muscular, brawny, strong, buff | Antonyms: weak, frail, skinny | adjective
