crease:/kriːs/| n. v.|折痕，皱纹；使起皱褶|A line or ridge produced on paper or cloth by folding, pressing, or crushing.|The paper has a crease down the middle. 这张纸中间有一条折痕。|近义词：wrinkle, fold, furrow|反义词:smoothness, flatness|词性:名词,动词
crease:/kriːs/| v. |使起皱褶，弄皱；变皱，起皱褶|To make a line or ridge in (something, typically paper or cloth) by folding, pressing, or crushing.|He carefully creased the paper in half. 他小心地把纸对折，弄出一条折痕。|近义词：wrinkle, crinkle, rumple|反义词:smooth, iron, flatten|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *krása (“to wrinkle, gather”), from Proto-Germanic *krasōną (“to wrinkle”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ker- (“to turn, bend”).
