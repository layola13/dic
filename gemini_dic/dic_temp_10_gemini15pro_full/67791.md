eyepieces:/ˈaɪˌpiːsɪz/
n.
目镜
A small magnifying lens that you look through at the top of a telescope, microscope, or other optical instrument.
I need to clean the eyepieces of my telescope. 我需要清洁我的望远镜的目镜。

近义词:ocular, lens
反义词:None (No direct antonym for a physical object like an eyepiece)
词性:noun
