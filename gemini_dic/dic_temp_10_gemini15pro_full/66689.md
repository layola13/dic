exogenously:/ɛkˈsɒdʒɪnəsli/
adverb
外生地，外源性地
From outside the system being considered; externally.
例句：The price of oil is determined exogenously. 石油价格是由外部因素决定的。

例句：The variable was introduced exogenously into the model.  这个变量是从外部引入到模型中的。

近义词：externally, outwardly
反义词：endogenously, internally
词性：adverb

词源：From exo- (outside) + -genous (producing or originating) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Greek exo- (outside) + -genes (born, produced).
