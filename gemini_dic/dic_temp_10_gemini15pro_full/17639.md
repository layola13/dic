beccafico:/ˌbɛkəˈfiːkoʊ/
n.
beccafico; fig pecker
A small bird, typically a warbler, that feeds on figs and other small fruits, especially one considered a delicacy in some European cuisines.
The beccafico is a small, olive-brown bird with a melodious song.  这种莺科小鸟体型小，呈橄榄褐色，歌声悠扬。
Beccaficos are considered a delicacy in some parts of Italy.  在意大利某些地区，这种莺被认为是一种美味佳肴。
近义词:garden warbler, fig-eater

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *beccafico*, from *beccare* (to peck) + *fico* (fig).  Ultimately from Latin *beccus* (beak) and *ficus* (fig).
