humanises:/ˈhjuːmənaɪzɪz/ /ˈhjuːmənʌɪzɪz/
verb
使人性化;使通人情;赋予人性
Makes something more humane or relatable to humans.
The company tried to humanise its image by featuring employees in its advertisements.  该公司试图通过在广告中突出员工来使其形象更人性化。
The author humanises the historical figures by portraying their personal struggles. 作者通过描绘历史人物的个人奋斗，使他们更具人性。
humanize, personalize, civilize
dehumanize, objectify
词性:动词
