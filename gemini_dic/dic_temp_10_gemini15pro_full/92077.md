hyperrational:/ˌhaɪpərˈræʃənəl/| adj.|过度理性的|Excessively rational; overly reliant on logic and reason, often to the exclusion of emotion or intuition.|例句：His hyperrational approach to problem-solving sometimes made him seem cold and unfeeling. 他过度理性的解决问题的方法有时让他看起来冷漠无情。

例句：Her hyperrational analysis of the situation neglected the human element. 她对情况的过度理性分析忽略了人为因素。|近义词：overly rational, excessively logical, unemotional, analytical|反义词：irrational, emotional, intuitive, impulsive|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- (meaning "over, excessive") + rational. The prefix "hyper-" comes from the Greek "hyper" meaning "over, above, beyond." "Rational" comes from the Latin "rationalis" meaning "reasonable."
