baguer:/baɡe/ |v.| 戏弄，嘲笑，愚弄 | to tease, mock, or fool someone | He baguered his little sister until she cried. 他戏弄他的妹妹直到她哭了起来。|  He's always baguering the new employees. 他总是戏弄新员工。 | synonyms: tease, mock, ridicule, taunt, banter,  | antonyms:  comfort, console, support | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *baguer, perhaps related to bague ("ring"), suggesting the idea of running circles around someone.  This etymology is uncertain. The word is primarily used in Canadian French.
