over-powerful:/ˌoʊvərˈpaʊərfl/
adjective
过强的，过于强大的
having too much power; excessively powerful
The over-powerful central government stifled local initiatives. 过于强大的中央政府扼杀了地方的积极性。
The king became over-powerful and began to abuse his authority. 国王变得权力过大，开始滥用职权。

近义词:excessive, overwhelming, dominant, overpowering
反义词:weak, powerless, feeble, impotent
词性:adjective

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "powerful" (adjective)
