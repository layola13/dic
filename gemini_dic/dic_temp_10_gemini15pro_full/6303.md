amortise:/ˈæmətaɪz/ (US) /əˈmɔːtaɪz/ (UK)| v. |摊销，分期偿还|To gradually reduce or write off the cost of an asset over a period of time.|The company is amortising the cost of new equipment over five years.  该公司正在将新设备的成本在五年内摊销。|
The loan will be amortised over 25 years. 这笔贷款将在 25 年内分期偿还。|
Synonyms: depreciate, write off, expensed|Antonyms: appreciate, increase|verb
