angioplasty: /ˈændʒiːoʊˌplæsti/ | n. | 血管成形术 | A surgical procedure to widen narrowed or obstructed blood vessels, especially coronary arteries. |  The doctor recommended angioplasty to open up the blocked artery. 医生建议进行血管成形术以疏通阻塞的动脉。| synonyms: balloon angioplasty, percutaneous transluminal coronary angioplasty (PTCA) | antonyms:  N/A | noun

Etymology:  From *angio- + -plasty. *angio-  "vessel" (from Greek *angeîon* "vessel") + *-plasty* "surgical repair or reconstruction" (from Greek *plastós* "molded, formed")
