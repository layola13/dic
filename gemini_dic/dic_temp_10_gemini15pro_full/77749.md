gendered:/ˈdʒɛndəd/| adj.|性别化的，有性别的|Having characteristics that are traditionally associated with a particular gender.|例句：Gendered expectations can limit people's choices. 性别期望会限制人们的选择。|近义词：sexist, biased|反义词：ungendered, neutral|词性:形容词

gendered:/ˈdʒɛndəd/| adj.|赋予性别的，按性别区分的|Assigned or restricted to one sex or the other.|例句：Toys are often highly gendered, marketed specifically to boys or girls. 玩具通常高度性别化，专门针对男孩或女孩销售。|近义词：categorized, classified|反义词：universal, general|词性:形容词
