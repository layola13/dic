noninterfering: /ˌnɒnɪntəˈfɪərɪŋ/ (US) /ˌnɒnɪntəˈfɪərɪŋ/ (UK) | adj. | 非干扰的，互不干扰的 | Not interfering or causing interference; independent.  Relating to systems or processes that do not affect each other's operation. | The noninterfering nature of the two programs allows them to run simultaneously. 这两个程序互不干扰的特性允许它们同时运行。|  independent, autonomous, separate | interfering, interacting, dependent | adjective

noninterference: /ˌnɒnɪntəˈfɪərəns/ (US) /ˌnɒnɪntəˈfɪərəns/ (UK) | n. |  不干涉，非干扰 |  The act or policy of not interfering; abstention from interference. The state of not interfering or causing interference. |  The principle of noninterference is crucial in international relations. 不干涉原则是国际关系中的关键。 | independence, autonomy | interference, intervention | noun
