gunocracy:/ɡʌˈnɒkrəsi/ | n. |枪支统治；枪支政治|A government or society ruled or controlled by force of arms, especially by the use of guns.  It implies a state where gun ownership and violence heavily influence political power and social order.|The escalating violence suggests the country is descending into gunocracy.| 近义词:stratocracy,  rule by force | 反义词: democracy, pacifism | 词性: noun
