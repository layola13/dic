macrameing: /ˈmækrəˌmeɪɪŋ/
| n.|花边工艺; 用绳结编织;|The act or process of creating decorative or functional items by knotting cord or string in patterns. |She spent the afternoon macrameing a plant hanger. 她下午用绳结编织了一个植物吊架。|近义词：knotting, weaving, braiding|反义词:|noun
