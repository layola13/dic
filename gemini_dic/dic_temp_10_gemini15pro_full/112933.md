luggaged:/ˈlʌɡɪdʒd/
adjective
带行李的，有行李的
Carrying or having luggage.
She arrived, luggaged and tired, after a long journey. 她带着行李，疲惫不堪地结束了漫长的旅程。
近义词:burdened, laden
反义词:unburdened, unladen
词性:adjective

luggage:/ˈlʌɡɪdʒ/
noun
行李
Suitcases, bags, and other items travelers carry containing their belongings.
Please collect your luggage from the carousel. 请从行李传送带取走行李。
近义词:baggage, suitcases, belongings
反义词:None (lack of luggage doesn't have a single word opposite)
词性:noun

词源:
From the verb "lug" (to carry or pull with effort) + "-age" (a suffix forming nouns from verbs).  The original meaning was "things that are lugged."
