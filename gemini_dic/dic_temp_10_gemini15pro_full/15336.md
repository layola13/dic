backspin:/ˈbækˌspɪn/| n.|回旋，倒旋|A backward rotation of a ball, especially in golf, tennis, or table tennis, that causes it to bounce backwards after landing.|The golfer put backspin on the ball to stop it quickly on the green. 高尔夫球手给球加了回旋，使其落在果岭上后迅速停止。|近义词：reverse spin, underspin|反义词: topspin|noun
