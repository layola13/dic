befogging:/bɪˈfɒɡɪŋ/
verb
使迷惑，使模糊不清
Confusing or obscuring something, making it difficult to understand.
The dense fog was befogging the road ahead, making it difficult to see. 浓雾笼罩着前方的道路，使人难以看清。
The politician's rhetoric was befogging the real issues.  这位政客的华丽辞藻混淆了真正的问题。
近义词: confusing, obscuring, bewildering, mystifying
反义词: clarifying, illuminating, elucidating
词性: Verb (present participle of befog)

befogged:/bɪˈfɒɡd/
adjective
感到困惑的，模糊不清的
Confused, bewildered, or unclear.
I was completely befogged by the complex instructions.  我完全被复杂的指令弄糊涂了。
His mind was befogged with grief. 他的思绪被悲伤笼罩。
近义词: confused, bewildered, muddled, perplexed
反义词: clear, lucid, coherent
词性: Adjective (past participle of befog)
