hewable:/ˈhjuːəbl/| adj.|可砍伐的，可凿的|Suitable for hewing or shaping by cutting, chopping, or other similar methods.|The sculptor searched for a hewable block of marble for his next project.  雕塑家正在寻找一块可雕刻的大理石块用于他的下一个项目。|The ancient trees were massive and hewable.  这些古树巨大且可砍伐。|近义词：workable, malleable, shapable|反义词:unworkable, intractable|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Old English *hēawian "to cut, chop," related to Old High German houwan.  The "-able" suffix indicates capability or suitability.
