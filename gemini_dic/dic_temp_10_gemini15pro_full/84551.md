hammer-up:/ˈhæmər ʌp/
verb phrasal verb
抬高（价格）; 通过反复宣传来提高
To artificially inflate or increase (a price or value) through exaggerated claims or publicity.
The auctioneer hammered up the price of the antique vase. 拍卖师抬高了古董花瓶的价格。
hammer up the price of shares  炒高股价
inflate, boost, hike, jack up
lower, reduce, decrease
