nonmarketable: /ˌnɒnˈmɑːkɪtəbəl/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈmɑːrkɪtəbəl/ (US)
adjective
不可销售的，无销路的
Not fit to be offered for sale; difficult or impossible to sell.
The house proved nonmarketable due to its remote location and dilapidated condition.  这房子由于地处偏远且破旧不堪，证明无法出售。
The company's outdated technology made their products nonmarketable.  这家公司过时的技术使得他们的产品毫无销路。
Synonyms: unsalable, unsellable, unvendible
Antonyms: marketable, salable, sellable
词性: adjective
