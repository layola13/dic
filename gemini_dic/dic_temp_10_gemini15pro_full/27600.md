canceled: /ˈkænsəld/ (US) /ˈkænsld/ (UK)| v. adj.|取消，已取消的|To stop something that has already been arranged from happening; no longer planned or scheduled.|The flight was canceled due to bad weather. 由于天气恶劣，航班被取消了。|近义词：aborted, called off, withdrawn|反义词：approved, confirmed|词性:动词/形容词
cancel: /ˈkænsəl/ (US) /ˈkænsl/ (UK)| v. n.|取消，删除|To decide that an arranged event will not take place; to order that something should not be done or made.|I had to cancel my trip because I got sick. 我不得不取消我的旅行，因为我生病了。|近义词：abort, revoke, annul|反义词：approve, confirm, ratify|词性:动词/名词

词源: From the Old French canceller, from Late Latin cancellare "to cross out with lines," from Latin cancelli "lattice work."
