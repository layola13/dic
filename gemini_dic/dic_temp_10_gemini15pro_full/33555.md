cigarette:/ˌsɪɡəˈret/ | n. |香烟；卷烟|A small roll of finely cut tobacco enclosed in a thin paper cylinder for smoking. | He lit a cigarette. 他点燃了一支香烟。| synonym:cigar, fag (informal, offensive in some contexts) | antonym: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French cigarette, diminutive of cigare (“cigar”), from Spanish cigarro, ultimately of Mayan origin.
