holistic:/hoʊˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 全面的；整体的；功能整体的 | Characterized by the belief that the parts of something are intimately interconnected and explicable only by reference to the whole. |  例句：
A holistic approach to health considers the whole person, not just their physical symptoms.
全面/整体的健康方法会考虑整个人，而不仅仅是他们的身体症状。

例句：
Holistic medicine attempts to treat the whole person, including mind, body, and spirit.
整体医学试图治疗整个人，包括思想、身体和精神。| 近义词：comprehensive, integrated, whole | 反义词：partial, fragmented, segmented | 词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *holos* (whole) + *-istic* (suffix forming adjectives).
