dissolubly:/dɪˈsɒljʊbli/
adv.
可溶地，可分解地
In a manner that can be dissolved or broken down.
Their fates are dissolubly linked. 他们的命运紧密相连。
近义词: separably, disjointedly
反义词: indissolubly
词性: 副词


indissolubly:/ˌɪndɪˈsɒljʊbli/
adv.
不可分解地，牢不可分地
In a manner that cannot be dissolved or separated.
These two concepts are indissolubly linked. 这两个概念是密不可分的。
近义词: inseparably, permanently
反义词: dissolubly
词性: 副词
