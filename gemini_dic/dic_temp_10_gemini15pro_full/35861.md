cockshy:/ˈkɒkʃaɪ/ /ˈkɑːkʃaɪ/
n.
投掷游戏；靶子；试探；试验
A game in which objects are thrown at a target, especially a live cock tied to a stake.  Also, a shy at something; an attempt; an experiment.
The local fair featured a cockshy, much to the dismay of animal rights activists.  当地集市以投掷活鸡为特色，这令动物权利活动家非常沮丧。

He took a cockshy at writing a novel. 他尝试写了一部小说。
近义词：target, butt, test, trial, experiment
反义词：None
词性：noun
