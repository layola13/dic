half-drunk:/ˌhɑːf ˈdrʌŋk/ | adj. | 微醉的，半醉的 | Slightly drunk; intoxicated but not completely drunk. | He was half-drunk and started singing loudly. 他喝得半醉，开始大声唱歌。|  She stumbled home, half-drunk and giggling. 她踉踉跄跄地回到家，半醉半醒地傻笑着。 | tipsy, merry, slightly intoxicated | sober, clear-headed | adjective
