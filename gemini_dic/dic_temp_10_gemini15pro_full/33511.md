cicatrix:/ˈsɪkətrɪks/ (US), /ˈsɪkətrɪks/ (UK)| n. |疤痕，瘢痕|A mark left on the skin or other tissue after a wound or injury has healed.|The surgery left a small cicatrix on her abdomen. 手术在她腹部留下了一个小疤痕。|Synonyms: scar, blemish, mark|Antonyms: clear skin, unblemished skin|Noun


cicatrices:/ˌsɪkəˈtraɪsiːz/ (US), /ˌsɪkəˈtraɪsiːz/ (UK)| n. |疤痕（复数）|Plural form of cicatrix.|The soldier bore the cicatrices of many battles.  这位士兵身上留下了许多战斗的伤疤。|Synonyms: scars, blemishes, marks|Antonyms: clear skin, unblemished skin|Noun
