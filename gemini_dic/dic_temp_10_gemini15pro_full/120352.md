minimised:/ˈmɪnɪmaɪzd/ | v. | 使最小化；使减少到最低限度 |  Reduced to the smallest possible amount or degree. | The company minimised its losses by selling off some assets. 公司通过出售一些资产将损失降到最低。| synonyms: reduced, decreased, lessened, curtailed | antonyms: maximized, increased, amplified | verb

minimised:/ˈmɪnɪmaɪzd/ | adj. | 最小化的；被减少到最低限度的 | Having been reduced to the smallest possible amount or degree. | The impact of the storm was minimised by the timely evacuation. 由于及时的疏散，风暴的影响被降到了最低。 | synonyms: minimal, reduced, smallest | antonyms: maximal, maximized, largest | adjective
