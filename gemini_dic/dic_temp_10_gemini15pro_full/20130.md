bipartitely:/baɪˈpɑːrtaɪtli/
| adv. |二分地，两部分地|In a bipartite manner; involving two separate parts or groups.|The data was bipartitely analyzed, considering both the control and experimental groups separately.  数据进行了二分分析，分别考虑了对照组和实验组。|Synonym: separately, distinctly|Antonym: jointly, together|词性:副词

bipartitely:/baɪˈpɑːrtaɪtli/
| adj. |二分的，两部分的 (较少使用，通常用bipartite)|Having two parts; divided into two.|Although less common, "bipartitely arranged" can describe something organized into two sections. 尽管不太常见，“bipartitely arranged”可以描述被组织成两部分的事物。|Synonym: two-part, dual|Antonym: unified, whole|词性:形容词
