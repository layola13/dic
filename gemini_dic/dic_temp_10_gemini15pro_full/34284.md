clareteer:/ˌklɛərəˈtɪər/| n.|葡萄酒生产者，葡萄酒商|A person who makes or sells claret (a type of red wine).|例句：The clareteer boasted of his vineyard's excellent terroir.  这位葡萄酒生产者吹嘘他的葡萄园有着极佳的风土。

例句：The clareteer offered a tasting of his newest vintage.  这位葡萄酒商提供了他最新年份葡萄酒的品尝。|近义词：winemaker, vintner|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From French "clairet" (meaning clear, light-colored wine), which evolved into the English term "claret" for a type of red Bordeaux wine.  The suffix "-eer" denotes a person engaged in a particular activity or trade.
