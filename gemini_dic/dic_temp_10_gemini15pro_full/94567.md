impounded:/ɪmˈpaʊndɪd/| adj.|被扣押的，被没收的|Seized and held in the custody of the law; confiscated.|The impounded vehicle was taken to the police station. 被扣押的车辆被带到了警察局。|Synonyms: confiscated, seized|Antonyms: released, returned|adjective
