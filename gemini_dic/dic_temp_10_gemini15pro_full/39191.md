congenially:/kənˈdʒiːniəli/ | adv. | 融洽地，合得来地，愉快地 | In a pleasant, friendly, and sociable way, getting along well with others. |  We chatted congenially about our shared interests. 我们愉快地聊着共同的兴趣。| synonym: agreeably, amiably, pleasantly, genially, sociably | antonym: disagreeably, unpleasantly, coldly, hostilely | 词性: 副词

congenial:/kənˈdʒiːniəl/ | adj. | 意气相投的，合得来的，宜人的 | Pleasant because of a personality, qualities, or interests that are similar to one's own; agreeable, suitable, or pleasing in nature or character. | The congenial atmosphere of the cafe made it a popular gathering spot. 咖啡馆融洽的氛围使它成为一个受欢迎的聚会场所。| synonym: agreeable, amiable, pleasant, genial, sociable | antonym: disagreeable, unpleasant, uncongenial, inhospitable | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *congeniālis*, from con- 'with' + geniālis 'of the same genius'.
