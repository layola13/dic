It can also refer to a person riding a horse at this gait, or to something developing quickly. |The horse broke into a gallop.  这匹马飞奔起来。|synonyms: sprint, dash, race, bolt, canter (slower gallop)|antonyms: crawl, creep, plod, walk, trot|noun, verb
