motive's:/ˈmoʊtɪvz/| n.|动机，目的|The detective was trying to understand the killer's motives.  侦探试图理解凶手的动机。|近义词：reasons, intentions, aims, goals, objectives|反义词:  deterrents|词性:名词复数


Etymology->
From Late Middle English motiv, from Late Latin motivus (“moving, impelling”), from Latin motus (“motion”),  ultimately from PIE *meue- (“to push away”).  The spelling with -ive was influenced by French motif.
