nonpertinences:/ˌnɒnpɜːrtnənsɪz/
n.
不相干的事物；无关紧要；不恰当
lack of relevance or appropriateness.
His speech was filled with nonpertinences,  boring the audience. 他的演讲充满了不相干的内容，使观众感到厌烦。
irrelevancies, impertinences, extraneousness
relevance, pertinence
noun
