haemin:/ˈhiːmɪn/ | n. | 血红素 |  A red-colored, iron-containing pigment that occurs in hemoglobin and myoglobin. It is responsible for transporting oxygen in the blood. |  Haemin is essential for oxygen transport in the body. 血红素对于体内氧气的运输至关重要。|  heme, hematin | deoxyhaemin | noun
