cotter:/ˈkɒtə(r)/ (UK), /ˈkɑːtər/ (US)| n.|楔子，销，键|A cotter is a metal fastener, typically a split pin or wedge, used to secure two parts together.|The cotter securing the wheel axle had broken.  车轮轴上的楔子断了。|The cotter pin kept the nut from coming loose.  开口销防止螺母松动。|Synonyms: pin, wedge, key, fastener|Antonyms: N/A|noun

cotter:/ˈkɒtə(r)/ (UK), /ˈkɑːtər/ (US)| v.|用楔子固定|To fasten or secure with a cotter.|He cottered the joint securely. 他用楔子牢牢地固定住了接头。|They cottered the two pieces of wood together.  他们用楔子把两块木头固定在一起。|Synonyms: fasten, secure, pin, wedge|Antonyms: loosen, unfasten, detach|verb
