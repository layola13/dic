laut:/laʊt/ (German)
adjective|响亮的，大声的；吵闹的|loud; noisy|The music was too laut.  音乐太吵了。|Synonyms: loud, noisy, boisterous|Antonyms: quiet, silent|adjective

laut:/laʊt/ (German)
adverb|大声地，响亮地|loudly; aloud|He spoke laut and clear. 他说得响亮而清晰。|Synonyms: loudly, clearly, audibly|Antonyms: quietly, softly|adverb
