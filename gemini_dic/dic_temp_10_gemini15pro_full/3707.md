aggressed:/əˈɡrɛst/ | v. | 攻击，侵犯 | To have attacked or initiated hostilities against someone or something. |  The country aggressed against its smaller neighbor.  这个国家侵略了它较小的邻国。 | Synonyms: attacked, assaulted, invaded | Antonyms: defended, protected, retreated | verb

aggressed:/əˈɡrɛst/ | adj. (rare) |  侵略的, 攻击性的 (较少用作形容词, 通常用 aggressive) | Showing or characterized by aggression. | While the term "aggressed" is grammatically correct as an adjective, "aggressive" is more commonly used. For example, "He displayed aggressive behavior" is more natural than "He displayed aggressed behavior." | Synonyms: aggressive, hostile, belligerent | Antonyms: peaceful, passive, non-violent | adjective
