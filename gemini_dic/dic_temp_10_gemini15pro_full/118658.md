Measurements of length, width, etc. |The government is taking mesures to combat inflation. 政府正在采取措施打击通货膨胀。 The dressmaker took her mesures for a new gown.  裁缝给她量尺寸做一件新礼服。|近义词：steps, actions, provisions, dimensions, sizes|反义词：inaction, neglect (for measures meaning actions), |词性:noun
