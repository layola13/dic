cornice:/ˈkɔːrnɪs/ (US) /ˈkɔːnɪs/ (UK)| n.|檐口，飞檐，雪檐|A horizontal molded projection that crowns or completes a building or wall.  It can also refer to a mass of snow projecting over a ridge.|The building features a decorative cornice along the roofline. 这栋建筑的屋顶线沿着装饰性的檐口。 The climbers avoided the dangerous cornice overhanging the cliff. 登山者避开了悬崖上危险的雪檐。|近义词：molding, eaves, overhang, ledge (for snow cornice)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
