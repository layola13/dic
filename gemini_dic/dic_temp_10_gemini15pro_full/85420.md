harvestman:/ˈhɑːrvɪstmən/ | n. | 长腿蜘蛛，盲蛛 | An arachnid of the order Opiliones, with a small rounded body and very long thin legs. Unlike spiders, they have only one body section and do not produce silk.  |  Example: The harvestman scurried across the leaf litter.  长腿蜘蛛匆匆穿过落叶层。

Example:  We found a harvestman in the garden shed. 我们在花园棚子里发现了一只长腿蜘蛛。| 近义词: daddy longlegs | 反义词:  N/A | noun
