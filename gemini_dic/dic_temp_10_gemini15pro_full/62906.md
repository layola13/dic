enlisters:/ɪnˈlɪstərz/| n. |征募者，登记员，入伍者|People who enlist others, typically for military service or a cause.  Those who sign up or register someone.|The army enlisters were busy signing up new recruits. 陆军征募员正忙着招募新兵。|Synonyms: recruiters, registrars, enrollees|Antonyms: dischargers, releasers|noun
