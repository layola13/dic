encroacher:/ɪnˈkroʊtʃər/| n. |侵入者，侵占者|Someone who intrudes on the rights or property of another. |例句：The encroacher was ordered to remove the fence he had built on his neighbor's land.  侵入者被勒令拆除他在邻居土地上建造的围栏。

例句：The city council is taking steps to prevent further encroachment by developers. 市议会正在采取措施，防止开发商进一步侵占土地。|近义词：intruder, trespasser, invader|反义词:defender, protector|词性:noun
