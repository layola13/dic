meltabilities:/ˌmɛltəˈbɪlətiz/
| n. |可熔性；熔化性；熔解性|The meltabilities of different metals vary greatly. 不同金属的可熔性差异很大。| Meltabilities refer to the ease with which a substance can be melted or the ability of a substance to melt.  It is often expressed as a temperature range or a specific melting point. |The meltabilities of these alloys are crucial for the manufacturing process. 这些合金的可熔性对于制造过程至关重要。|近义词：fusibility, liquefaction|反义词:infusibility, resistance to melting|词性:名词
