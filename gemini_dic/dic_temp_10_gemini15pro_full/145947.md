overdiscriminating:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈskrɪmɪneɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 过分歧视的，过度挑剔的 |  Making excessively fine distinctions; discriminating too much or too carefully.  It suggests a negative connotation, implying that the person is being overly critical or judgmental, often to an unreasonable degree. | She was so overdiscriminating about food that she barely ate anything. 她对食物太挑剔了，几乎什么都不吃。| The company was accused of overdiscriminating against older applicants. 这家公司被指控过度歧视年长的申请者。 | fastidious, picky, hypercritical, choosy, finicky | undiscriminating, uncritical, accepting | adjective
