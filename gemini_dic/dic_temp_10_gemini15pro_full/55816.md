divestments:/daɪˈvɛstmənts/| n.|撤资，剥离|The company announced a series of divestments to refocus on its core business.  该公司宣布了一系列撤资，以重新专注于其核心业务。|例句：The company is considering divestments in some of its non-core businesses.  该公司正在考虑剥离一些非核心业务。

例句：The divestments will allow the company to reduce its debt.  这些撤资将使公司能够减少债务。|近义词：sale, disposal, divestiture|反义词：acquisition, investment|词性:名词
