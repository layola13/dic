equalizations:/ˌiːkwəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 平衡，均等化，同等化 |The process of making things equal or uniform. |例句：The equalizations of resources across the different departments ensured fairness.  不同部门之间资源的均等化确保了公平。

例句：The new tax policy aims at the equalization of wealth distribution. 新的税收政策旨在平衡财富分配。|近义词：balancing, leveling, adjustment, standardization |反义词: imbalance, disparity, inequality | 词性: noun
