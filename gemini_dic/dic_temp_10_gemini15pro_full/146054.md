overdubbed:/ˌoʊvərˈdʌbd/ | adj. | 配音的，叠录的 |  Recorded over an original recording, typically to add or replace sounds.  The original audio or video has had new audio recorded on top of it. | The film was poorly overdubbed, and the actors' lip movements didn't match the dialogue. 这部电影的配音很差，演员的嘴型和对白对不上。

The singer overdubbed the chorus to improve the harmony. 歌手为改进和声叠录了合唱部分。| Synonyms: rerecorded, dubbed, postsynched | Antonyms: original, live | Word type: Adjective

overdub:/ˌoʊvərˈdʌb/ | v. | 配音，叠录 | To record (sound) over an existing recording. To add (sound) to a recording by recording over it. | They overdubbed the dialogue in the studio. 他们在录音棚里为对白配音。

The band overdubbed several layers of guitar to create a richer sound. 乐队叠录了几层吉他来创造更丰富的音效。| Synonyms: rerecord, dub, postsynchronize | Antonyms: record live | Word type: Verb

Etymology:  "over-" + "dub" .  "Dub" comes from the word "double," referencing the process of copying or adding a second layer of sound.
