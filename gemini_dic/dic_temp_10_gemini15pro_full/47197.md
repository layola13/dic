daydreamlike:/ˈdeɪdriːmlaɪk/| adj.|如梦似幻的，白日梦般的|Resembling or characteristic of a daydream; dreamy, fanciful, vague.|She had a daydreamlike smile on her face. 她脸上带着梦幻般的微笑。|The music created a daydreamlike atmosphere.  音乐营造了一种梦幻般的氛围。|近义词：dreamy, fanciful, ethereal, surreal, otherworldly|反义词：realistic, practical, down-to-earth|词性:形容词
词源: daydream (n.) + -like (suffix).  "Daydream" comes from "day" and "dream."  "-like" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling," "characteristic of."
