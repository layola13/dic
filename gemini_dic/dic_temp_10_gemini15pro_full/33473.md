chyliferous:/kaɪˈlɪfərəs/
adjective
乳糜的；含乳糜的
Conveying chyle; containing chyle.
The chyliferous vessels absorb fats from the small intestine. 乳糜管从小肠吸收脂肪。
Synonyms: lacteal
Antonyms: None
adjective
