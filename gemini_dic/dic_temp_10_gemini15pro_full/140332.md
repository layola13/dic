oh's:/oʊz/
|n.| 惊叹声，感叹词 | An exclamation expressing surprise, pain, or other strong emotion, often used in the plural.  It is a shortened form of "ohs". | Example:  The acrobat's daring feat was met with a chorus of oh's and ah's from the audience.  观众对杂技演员大胆的技艺报以一阵阵的惊叹声。| Synonym: exclamations, gasps | Antonym: silence | noun
