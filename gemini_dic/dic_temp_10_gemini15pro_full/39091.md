confraternities:/ˌkɒnfrəˈtɜːnɪtiz/
n.
宗教团体；兄弟会；联谊会
A confraternity is a lay society of persons associated for some pious purpose or common object.
The confraternity organized a pilgrimage to the holy site.  这个宗教团体组织了一次到圣地的朝圣。
The local confraternity raised funds for the new church. 当地的宗教团体为新教堂筹集资金。
synonyms: brotherhood, fellowship, society, association, guild, league
antonyms:  isolation, solitude
noun
