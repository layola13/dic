informatic:/ˌɪnfərˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 信息的；信息学的 | Relating to or involving information, especially in or as computer data. |  Informatic analysis of the data revealed hidden patterns. 信息分析揭示了隐藏的模式。|  Information-based, computational | Analog | adjective


informatic:/ˌɪnfərˈmætɪk/ | n. | 信息学 | The science of information, usually as computer data. |  He studied informatics in college. 他在大学学习信息学。|  Information science, computing | None | noun


Etymology: From French *informatique*, a portmanteau of *information* and *automatique*, coined in 1962 by Philippe Dreyfus.
