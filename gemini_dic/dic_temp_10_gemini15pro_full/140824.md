on one hand:/ɒn wʌn hænd/
|phrase|一方面|Used to introduce one side of an argument or dilemma.|On one hand, I'd like to travel, but on the other hand, I don't have much money. 一方面，我想去旅行，但另一方面，我没有多少钱。|on the one hand|on the other hand|phrase

on the other hand:/ɒn ði ˈʌðə hænd/
|phrase|另一方面|Used to introduce a contrasting point of view or argument.|On the other hand, perhaps it's better to wait until we have saved more money. 另一方面，也许等到我们存了更多钱再去旅行比较好。|on one hand| |phrase
