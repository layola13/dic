multiaxial:/ˌmʌltiˈæksiəl/
adjective
多轴的; 多维的
Having or involving several axes or aspects.
例句：The diagnosis of personality disorders is multiaxial, taking into account various aspects of an individual's functioning. 人格障碍的诊断是多轴的，考虑到个体功能的各个方面。

例句：Engineers designed a multiaxial testing machine to simulate real-world stress conditions on materials. 工程师设计了一种多轴试验机来模拟材料在现实世界中的应力条件。

近义词：multidimensional, multifaceted
反义词：uniaxial, single-factor
词性: adjective
