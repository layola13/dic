brakier:/ˈbreɪkiər/ | adj. | 更棘手的，更困难的；更需要技巧的 | More difficult, requiring more skill or effort; more intricate or complex. | The puzzle is much brakier than I expected.  这个谜题比我预期的要棘手得多。| The brakier the terrain, the more experienced the climber needs to be.  地形越复杂，攀登者就需要越有经验。 |  近义词：trickier, thornier, more challenging, more demanding, more difficult  | 反义词：easier, simpler, less demanding | 词性: 形容词

brakier:/ˈbreɪkiər/ | adj. | （刹车）更灵敏的，更容易刹住的 | (of brakes) More responsive, easier to activate and effective in stopping. | The new brakes are much brakier than the old ones. 新刹车比旧刹车灵敏得多。|  The car felt brakier after the mechanic adjusted them.  修理工调整后，汽车的刹车感觉更灵敏了。| 近义词：responsive, sensitive, sharp | 反义词：sluggish, unresponsive, weak | 词性: 形容词

词源：
Brakier 是 brake 的比较级形式。Brake 源于古挪威语 *breka，意为“打破，粉碎”。  在汽车语境中，“brakier”的含义是更易于使车辆停止或减速，即刹车更灵敏。
