nonintroverted:/ˌnɒnˈɪntrəvɜːtɪd/
adjective
非内向的，外向的
Not introverted; extroverted or sociable.
She has a nonintroverted personality and enjoys meeting new people. 她性格外向，喜欢结识新朋友。
Synonyms: extroverted, outgoing, sociable, gregarious
Antonyms: introverted, shy, reserved, withdrawn
词性: 形容词

词源：
non- (prefix) + introverted (adjective)

"non-" comes from Latin "non" meaning "not".
"introverted" comes from Latin "introvertere" meaning "to turn inwards".  Therefore, "nonintroverted" literally means "not turned inwards".
