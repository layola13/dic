mahayanistic:/ˌmɑːhɑːjəˈnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 大乘佛教的 | Relating to or characteristic of Mahayana Buddhism, one of the two major traditions of Buddhism. |  Example 1: The mahayanistic concept of bodhisattva emphasizes compassion and helping others achieve enlightenment.  大乘佛教的菩萨概念强调慈悲和帮助他人获得觉悟。

Example 2:  Many mahayanistic scriptures were translated into Chinese during the Tang Dynasty.  许多大乘佛教的经文在唐代被翻译成中文。 | Synonyms: Mahayana | Antonyms: Theravada (although not a direct opposite, it represents the other major branch of Buddhism) | adjective
