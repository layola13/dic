embroidering:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdərɪŋ/
|v.|刺绣；装饰；渲染|The act of decorating fabric with needlework.  It can also be used figuratively to describe embellishing a story or account.|She was embroidering a beautiful floral pattern onto the cushion. 她正在给垫子绣一个漂亮的花卉图案。|近义词：stitching, embellishing, adorning, ornamenting|反义词：simplifying, stripping|Verb (present participle)

embroider:/ɪmˈbrɔɪdər/
|v.|刺绣；装饰；渲染|To decorate fabric with needlework. It can also be used figuratively to describe embellishing a story or account.|She embroidered the tablecloth with intricate designs.  她用复杂的设计绣了桌布。|近义词：stitch, embellish, adorn, ornament|反义词：simplify, strip|Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *embrouder, from en- "in, on" + brouder "to border, edge," from Vulgar Latin *bordare, from Late Latin bordus "border."
