anti-oxygenating:/ˌæntiˈɒksɪdʒəneɪtɪŋ/
adj.
抗氧化的，防氧化的
Preventing oxidation or the effects of oxygen.
The cream boasts anti-oxygenating properties that protect skin from free radicals.  这款面霜宣称具有抗氧化特性，可以保护皮肤免受自由基的侵害。
近义词: antioxidant, anti-oxidative
反义词: oxidizing, pro-oxidant
词性: adjective
词源:  "anti-" (against) + "oxygen" + "-ating" (present participle suffix indicating action or process)
