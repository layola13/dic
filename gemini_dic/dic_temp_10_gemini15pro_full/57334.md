downwardness:/ˈdaʊnwərdnəs/
n.
向下; 下降趋势; 颓势
A state of moving or tending downwards; a descending tendency.
The economy is in a state of downwardness. 经济处于下滑趋势。
The graph shows a marked downwardness in sales. 图表显示销售额明显下降。
decline, descent, fall
rise, ascent, increase
