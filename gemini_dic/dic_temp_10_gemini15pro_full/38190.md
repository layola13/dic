compulsively:/kəmˈpʌlsɪvli/
adverb|强迫地，不由自主地|In an irresistible and repetitive manner, driven by an inner compulsion.|He compulsively washes his hands every few minutes. 他每隔几分钟就强迫性地洗手。|例句：She compulsively checks her phone for messages. 她强迫性地查看手机上的信息。|近义词：obsessively, uncontrollably|反义词:willingly, voluntarily|词性:副词
词源: From the adjective compulsive + -ly. Compulsive derives from the Late Latin compulsīvus (“obligatory”), from the past participle of compellere (“to compel”).
