nonpertinency:/ˌnɑːnpɜːrtnənsi/
n.|不相关性；不切题；无关紧要|The nonpertinency of his remarks to the topic at hand was evident. 他说的话与当前主题无关，这是显而易见的。|例句：The lawyer objected to the nonpertinency of the witness's testimony. 律师反对证人提供的不相关证词。|近义词：irrelevance, immateriality, extraneousness|反义词：relevance, pertinence, germaneness|词性:名词

Etymology:
From non- + pertinency.  Pertinency comes from Late Latin *pertinentia, from Latin pertinēns, present participle of pertinēre “to pertain,” from per- “thoroughly” + tenēre “to hold.”
