clad:/klæd/
adjective
穿衣的，覆盖的
covered
例句：The knight was clad in shining armor. 骑士身穿闪亮的盔甲。

例句：The mountains were clad in snow. 山脉覆盖着白雪。
近义词：dressed, clothed, covered, arrayed
反义词：naked, bare, uncovered
词性: adjective


clad:/klæd/
verb
给…穿衣（clad的过去式和过去分词）
(past tense and past participle of clothe) dressed, covered
例句：He was clad in a simple tunic.  他穿着一件简单的束腰外衣。

例句：The hills were clad in mist. 山丘笼罩在薄雾之中。
近义词：dressed, clothed, covered
反义词：undressed, unclothed, uncovered
词性: verb (past tense and past participle)



ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *clad, *cled, from Old English clāþod (past participle of clāþian (“to clothe”)), from Proto-Germanic *klæþijaną (“to clothe”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱel- (“to cover, conceal”). Cognate with Old Norse klæða (“to clothe”), Dutch kleden (“to dress”), German kleiden (“to dress”).  Related to cloth.
