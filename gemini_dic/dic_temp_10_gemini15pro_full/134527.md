nonlicentiate:/ˌnɒnlaɪˈsɛnʃɪɪt/
noun
未获许可者；没有执照的人
a person who does not have a license or qualification.
He was working as a nonlicentiate electrician, which is illegal and dangerous. 他作为一名无证电工工作，这是违法且危险的。
The nonlicentiate doctor was arrested for practicing medicine without a license.  这位无证医生因无证行医而被捕。
unlicensed, unqualified
licensed, qualified
