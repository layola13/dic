overlicentious: /ˌoʊvərlaɪˈsɛnʃəs/ | adj. | 放荡的，淫荡的；过度纵欲的 | Excessively unrestrained by convention or morality, especially in sexual matters. Characterized by excessive freedom or indulgence in sensual pleasures. |  His overlicentious behavior led to his expulsion from the university. 他放荡的行为导致他被大学开除。 |  The overlicentious lyrics of the song caused controversy. 这首歌放荡的歌词引起了争议。 | synonyms: licentious, dissolute, debauched, promiscuous, wanton, lascivious, lewd, immoral, profligate | antonyms: chaste, virtuous, moral, upright, puritanical | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + licentious.  "Licentious" comes from the Latin "licentiosus" meaning "unrestrained, wanton," derived from "licentia" meaning "freedom, license."
