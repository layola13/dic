over-pollinated:/ˌoʊvərˈpɑːləneɪtɪd/
adjective
过度授粉的
Having received excessive pollen, often resulting in reduced fruit or seed set.
The over-pollinated apple tree produced small, misshapen fruit.  过度授粉的苹果树结出了又小又畸形的果实。
The farmer worried that his squash plants were over-pollinated due to the abundance of bees.  农民担心由于蜜蜂过多，他的南瓜植株授粉过度。
Synonyms: over-fertilized
Antonyms: under-pollinated
adjective
