embryology:/ˌɛmbrɪˈɒlədʒi/ (US) /ˌembriˈɒlədʒi/ (UK)| n. |胚胎学|The branch of biology that studies the development of gametes (sex cells), fertilization, and development of embryos and fetuses.|Human embryology is a complex and fascinating field. 人类胚胎学是一个复杂而迷人的领域.|Synonyms: developmental biology|Antonyms: None|Noun

embryology:/ˌɛmbrɪˈɒlədʒi/ (US) /ˌembriˈɒlədʒi/ (UK)| n. |胚胎发生|The formation and development of an embryo.|The embryology of the frog is often studied in biology classes.  青蛙的胚胎发育常在生物课上学习。|Synonyms: embryogenesis|Antonyms: None|Noun
