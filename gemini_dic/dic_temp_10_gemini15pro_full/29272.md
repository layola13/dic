cassowary:/ˈkæsəˌwɛri/ | n. |食火鸡|A large flightless bird native to New Guinea and northeastern Australia, with a distinctive bony crest on its head and powerful legs.|例句：The cassowary is a dangerous bird with sharp claws. 食火鸡是一种有锋利爪子的危险鸟类。

例句：We saw a cassowary while hiking in the rainforest. 我们在雨林徒步旅行时看到了一只食火鸡。|近义词：rhea, emu, ostrich|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific animal) |词性:noun
