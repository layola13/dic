catagmatic: /ˌkætəˈɡmætɪk/ | adj. |  范畴的，属于范畴的 |  Relating to or belonging to a category; predicable.  Of or relating to that which can be predicated of a subject.  |  Example: Color is a catagmatic term, unlike "all," "some," or "none." 颜色是一个范畴词，不像“所有”、“一些”或“没有”。 | Example: Catagmatic words are those that can be used as predicates of subjects.  范畴词是可以用来作主语谓语的词。 |  Synonyms: predicable, categorical | Antonyms: syncategorematic | Word type: adjective
