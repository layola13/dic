construer:/kənˈstruːər/ | n. | 解释者，分析者，理解者 |  Someone who interprets or analyzes something, especially a text or piece of writing. | The lawyer carefully construer the contract. 律师仔细解释了合同。| 近义词: interpreter, analyst, commentator | 反义词: misinterpreter, distorter | 词性: noun
Etymology: From Old French *construere*, from Latin *construere* "to construct, build, pile up, heap together, arrange," from *com-* "together" (see com-) + *struere* "to pile, build." The meaning shifted from the literal building of something to the building up of meaning through interpretation.
