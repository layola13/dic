fretworked:/ˈfrɛtwɜːkt/
adjective
镂空的，格子细工的
Decorated with fretwork; having a design of interlaced patterns.
The antique chair back was fretworked in a delicate floral pattern.  古董椅背上装饰着精致的花卉图案镂空雕刻。
The balcony was fretworked and offered a view of the garden. 阳台是镂空的，可以俯瞰花园。
近义词:filigreed, latticed, openwork
反义词:solid, plain
词性:adjective

fretwork:/ˈfrɛtwɜːk/
noun
镂空细工，格子细工
Ornamental openwork, typically in wood or metal, consisting of interlaced patterns.
The fretwork on the window screens was intricate and beautiful. 窗纱上的镂空细工复杂而美丽。
The artisan specialized in creating delicate fretwork designs.  这位工匠专门制作精美的镂空设计。
近义词:latticework, tracery, filigree
反义词:solid, plain
词性:noun


词源：
From fret (verb) + -work. The verb "fret" originally meant "to adorn with interlaced work," from Old French "fretes" (lattice).
