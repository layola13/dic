asymmetral: /ˌeɪsɪˈmɛtrɪkl/ | adj. | 不对称的，非对称的 |  Not symmetrical; having unequal or dissimilar parts. | The artist intentionally created an asymmetral design, breaking the traditional rules of balance.  这位艺术家特意创作了一个不对称的设计，打破了传统的平衡规则。| The building's asymmetral facade made it stand out from the surrounding architecture.  这座建筑不对称的正面使其在周围的建筑中脱颖而出。|  近义词: asymmetrical, dissymmetrical, unbalanced, irregular | 反义词: symmetrical, balanced, regular, even | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  From a- (not) + symmetral (symmetrical).  "Symmetral" comes from Greek "symmetros" (of like measure), from "syn-" (together) + "metron" (measure).
