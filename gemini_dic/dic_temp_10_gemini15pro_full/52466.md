diesinker:/ˈdaɪˌsɪŋkər/
noun
模具制造工，雕模工
A skilled worker who makes dies, especially for stamping or shaping metal.
The diesinker carefully crafted the mold for the new coin.  模具制造工仔细地为新硬币制作了模具。
The experienced diesinker was known for his precision and attention to detail.  这位经验丰富的雕模工以他的精确度和对细节的关注而闻名。
synonyms: die maker, toolmaker, mold maker
antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词
