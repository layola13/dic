paraquat: /ˈpærəkwɒt/ (UK) /ˈpærəkwɑːt/ (US)| n. |百草枯|A highly toxic herbicide and contact poison.  It is rapidly absorbed after ingestion and causes severe damage to the lungs and other organs. |Paraquat is banned in many countries due to its toxicity. 百草枯因其毒性在许多国家被禁用。

Paraquat poisoning can be fatal. 百草枯中毒可能是致命的。|近义词: herbicide, weedkiller |反义词: None (no direct antonym for a specific herbicide) |词性:noun
