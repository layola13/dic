disparately:/dɪˈspærɪtli/ | adv. | 不同地，相异地 | in a way that is very different and unequal | The two groups were treated disparately. 这两组人受到了不同的对待。| Examples:  The wealth is distributed disparately across the region.  财富在该地区分布不均。

The twins, though identical, performed disparately in school.  这对双胞胎虽然长相一样，但在学校的表现却大相径庭。 |Synonyms: differently, unequally, dissimilarly, distinctly | Antonyms: similarly, equally, uniformly, identically |词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *disparatus, from Latin dis- + paratus (“prepared”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *pere- (“to produce, procure”).
