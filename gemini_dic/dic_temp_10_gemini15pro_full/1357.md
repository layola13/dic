accumulates: /əˈkjuːmjəleɪts/
verb
积累，积聚
(of things) to gradually increase in number or amount.
Dust accumulates quickly if you don't clean regularly. = 如果你不经常打扫，灰尘会很快积聚。
The evidence against him is accumulating. = 对他不利的证据越来越多。
近义词: gather, collect, amass, accrue
反义词: dissipate, disperse, dwindle, decrease
词性: verb

USAGE->
一般现在时:
Dust accumulates quickly in this room. = 这个房间灰尘积聚得很快。
He accumulates wealth through wise investments. = 他通过明智的投资积累财富。

一般过去时:
Over the years, he accumulated a vast fortune. = 多年来，他积累了巨额财富。
The snow accumulated quickly overnight. = 一夜之间雪积聚得很快。

现在完成时:
She has accumulated a lot of experience in her field. = 她在她的领域积累了很多经验。
They have accumulated a significant amount of debt. = 他们已经积累了大量的债务。

过去完成时:
By the time he retired, he had accumulated enough wealth to live comfortably. = 到他退休时，他已经积累了足够的财富过上舒适的生活。
The company had accumulated substantial losses before it went bankrupt. =  该公司在破产前已经积累了巨额亏损。

将来时:
The interest on your savings will accumulate over time. =  你的储蓄利息会随着时间的推移而累积。
If we don't address this issue, problems will accumulate. = 如果我们不解决这个问题，问题将会累积。

虚拟语气:
If he had saved more money, he would have accumulated a larger fortune. = 如果他多存点钱，他会积累更大的财富。
I wish I had accumulated more frequent flyer miles. = 我希望我积累了更多飞行常客里程。


被动语态:

疑问句:

否定句:
