mess:/mɛs/| n. v. |杂乱，混乱；困境；一份食物；弄乱，弄脏|A state of untidiness or disorder.  To make something untidy or dirty.|The room was a complete mess. 房间里乱七八糟。 He messed up his shirt with ketchup. 他把番茄酱弄到衬衫上了。|近义词：clutter, disorder, muddle, jumble, confuse, spoil |反义词：order, tidiness, neatness, organize, clean|词性:名词，动词
