hyloism:/ˈhaɪloʊɪzəm/
n.
物质活力论，泛心论
The philosophical doctrine that matter is imbued with life.
Hyloism proposes that all matter, even seemingly inanimate objects, possesses a kind of life or consciousness.  物质活力论认为，所有物质，即使是看似无生命的物体，也拥有一种生命或意识。
The philosopher argued for hyloism, claiming consciousness is inherent in all physical substance. 这位哲学家主张物质活力论，声称意识是所有物理实体固有的。
近义词：panpsychism, animism
反义词：materialism, mechanism
词性：noun
