buttery:/ˈbʌtəri/ (US), /ˈbʌtəri/ (UK)| adj.|黄油味的；像黄油的；含大量黄油的；奉承的，油嘴滑舌的|Resembling or containing a large amount of butter; rich and smooth in texture or taste; also, excessively flattering or insincere.|The croissants were light and buttery.  羊角面包松软，带着黄油的香味。|近义词：rich, creamy, smooth, unctuous, flattering|反义词：dry, bland, austere|词性:形容词

buttery:/ˈbʌtəri/ (US), /ˈbʌtəri/ (UK)| n.|食品储藏室；餐具室|A room, typically near a kitchen, for storing food, dishes, and silverware.|The butler retrieved the silver platter from the buttery. 男管家从餐具室取回了银盘。|近义词：pantry, larder|反义词: None|词性:名词
