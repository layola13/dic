begin:/bɪˈɡɪn/| v. |开始，着手|to start something|例句：The meeting will begin at 9 am. 会议将于上午9点开始。|近义词：start, commence, initiate|反义词：end, finish, stop|词性:动词

began:/bɪˈɡæn/| v. |（begin的过去式）开始，着手|past tense of begin|例句：They began working on the project last month.  他们上个月开始做这个项目。|近义词：started, commenced, initiated|反义词：ended, finished, stopped|词性:动词

begun:/bɪˈɡʌn/| v. |（begin的过去分词）开始，着手|past participle of begin|例句：The movie had begun by the time we arrived.  我们到达时电影已经开始了。|近义词：started, commenced, initiated|反义词：ended, finished, stopped|词性:动词


词源: From Middle English *beginnen, from Old English beginnan (“to begin, undertake, enter upon”), from Proto-Germanic *duginnaną (“to begin, conceal”), equivalent to be- +‎ gin. Cognate with Dutch beginnen (“to begin”), German beginnen (“to begin”), Swedish begynna (“to begin”).
