crossfish: /ˈkrɒsfɪʃ/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsfɪʃ/ (US)| n. |海星|A starfish, a marine invertebrate with five or more arms radiating from a central disc.|We saw many colorful starfish in the tide pool. 我们在潮池里看到了许多色彩斑斓的海星。|The starfish clung to the rock. 海星紧紧地贴在岩石上。|近义词：starfish|反义词: None|词性: noun

Etymology: From 'cross' + 'fish', referring to the star-like shape with radiating arms.  The term 'starfish' is becoming increasingly common, especially in scientific contexts, as the animal is not a fish.
