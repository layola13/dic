nonlibidinous:/ˌnɒnlɪˈbɪdɪnəs/
adjective
无性欲的; 没有性欲的
Lacking sexual desire or interest.
She described herself as nonlibidinous due to a medical condition. 她由于某种疾病而自称没有性欲。
The medication had a nonlibidinous side effect. 这种药物有抑制性欲的副作用。

synonym: asexual, non-sexual, frigid (for women), impotent (for men - though this primarily refers to erectile dysfunction), passionless
antonym: libidinous, lustful, passionate, sexual

词性: adjective
