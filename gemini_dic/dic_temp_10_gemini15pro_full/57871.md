dribber:/ˈdrɪbər/| n. |口水滴落者；运球者；胡扯的人|Someone who dribbles saliva, a ball in sports, or talks nonsense. |The baby is a dribber, always with a wet bib.  这个婴儿总是流口水，围嘴总是湿的。

The basketball player is a skilled dribbler. 这位篮球运动员是个运球高手。

He's such a dribber, always going on about nothing. 他真是个胡扯的人，总是说些不着边际的话。|近义词：(for saliva) drooler; (for ball) ball-handler; (for talk) babbler, rambler|反义词: (for ball)  none; (for talk)  concise speaker|词性:noun
