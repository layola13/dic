-ology:/ɑːˈlɒdʒi/
n.
学问；学科；研究
The study of a particular subject.
例句：Biology is the study of living organisms. 生物学是对生物体的研究。

例句：Zoology is the study of animals. 动物学是对动物的研究。

近义词：study, discipline
反义词: (No direct antonym for a suffix)
词性: suffix (后缀)

Etymology:
From Greek -λογία (-logia), from λόγος (lógos, “word, speech, reason”).
