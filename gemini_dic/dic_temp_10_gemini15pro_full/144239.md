outsmarted:/ˌaʊtˈsmɑːrtɪd/
verb
智胜，比...更聪明
defeated someone by being more clever or cunning than them.
He outsmarted his opponents in the negotiation and secured a favorable deal. 他在谈判中智胜了对手，达成了有利的协议。
synonyms: outwitted, outfoxed, outmaneuvered, tricked
antonyms: outwitted, outfoxed, outmaneuvered, tricked (These can be antonyms depending on the context - if someone *is* outsmarted, they *are not* outwitting.)
