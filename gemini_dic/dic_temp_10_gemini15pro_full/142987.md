outbargains:/ˌaʊtˈbɑːrɡənz/
verb
胜过…的讲价能力; 比…更会讨价还价
outbargain someone into doing something: to persuade someone to give you something or do something for you by discussing it with them for a long time and trying to get the best possible result for yourself.
He outbargained the salesman and got a lower price. 他和售货员讨价还价，最终以更低的价格买到了东西。
近义词: outtrade, outsmart, haggle better than
反义词: be outbargained, be outsmarted in bargaining
词性: verb
