devastatingly:/ˈdevəsteɪtɪŋli/
adverb
毁灭性地；极具破坏力地
in a way that causes a lot of damage or destruction
The hurricane devastated the island devastatingly.  飓风以毁灭性的方式摧毁了这个岛屿。
The fire spread devastatingly quickly. 火势蔓延得惊人地快。
近义词: disastrously, catastrophically, ruinously
反义词: beneficially, constructively, helpfully
词性: adverb
词源: From devastate (from Latin devastatus, past participle of devastare ‘lay waste’) + -ing + -ly.

devastate:/ˈdevəsteɪt/
verb
摧毁；毁灭
to destroy or ruin something
The earthquake devastated the city. 地震摧毁了这座城市。
The news devastated her. 这个消息让她崩溃。
近义词: destroy, ruin, wreck
反义词: build, construct, create
词性: verb
