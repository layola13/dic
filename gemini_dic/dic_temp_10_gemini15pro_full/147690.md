overpractices:/ˌoʊvərˈpræktɪsɪz/ | v. | 过度练习，练习过量 |  To practice something excessively, often to the point of detriment.  The pianist overpractices, sometimes playing for eight hours a day, which has led to repetitive strain injuries.  钢琴家过度练习，有时一天弹八个小时，这导致了重复性劳损。 | overtrains, overworks | underpractices | verb
overpractice:/ˌoʊvərˈpræktɪs/ | n. | 过度练习 |  Excessive practice of something, often leading to negative consequences.  His overpractice resulted in burnout and a decline in his performance. 他的过度练习导致了倦怠和表现下降。 | overtraining, overwork | underpractice | noun
