devulgarize:/diːˈvʌlɡəraɪz/
verb
使不再庸俗；使文雅；净化
to remove vulgar or offensive elements from; to refine or purify
The editor devulgarized the manuscript before publication. 编辑在出版前净化了稿件。
近义词: refine, purify, elevate, civilize
反义词: vulgarize, coarsen, debase
词性: verb

devulgarization:/diːˌvʌlɡərɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
去庸俗化；净化
the act or process of devulgarizing something
The devulgarization of language is an ongoing process.  语言的净化是一个持续的过程。
近义词: refinement, purification, elevation
反义词: vulgarization, coarsening
词性: noun
