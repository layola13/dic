dowagerly:/ˈdaʊədʒərli/| adj.|寡妇似的，像贵妇人的，威严的，专横的|Behaving in a way that is typical of a woman of high social rank who is no longer young, especially a widow; dignified and somewhat authoritarian.|例句：She swept into the room with a dowagerly air. 她以贵妇人的气势走进了房间。|例句：Her dowagerly demeanor commanded respect. 她那威严的举止令人肃然起敬。|近义词：stately, dignified, imperious, matronly|反义词：youthful, unassuming, humble|词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dowager + -ly.  "Dowager" comes from Old French *douagiere, from douage (dower), ultimately from Latin dotare (to endow).
