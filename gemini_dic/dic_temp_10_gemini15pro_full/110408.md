likably:/ˈlaɪkəbli/| adv.|讨人喜欢地，可爱地|in a likable or pleasant manner|She spoke likably about her childhood. 她愉快地谈起了她的童年。|近义词：pleasantly, agreeably, charmingly|反义词：disagreeably, unpleasantly, offensively|词性:副词
词源:From likable + -ly.  Likable comes from like + -able.  The word "like" ultimately traces back to Proto-Germanic *likam, meaning "body, form."
