eurocommunist:/ˈjʊrəʊˈkɒmjʊnɪst/ | n. | 欧共主义者 | A member of a Western European communist party that attempted to reconcile Marxist theory with liberal democratic principles, rejecting the Soviet model of centralized state control.  |  The Italian Communist Party, under Enrico Berlinguer, became a prominent example of Eurocommunism.  意大利共产党在恩里科·贝林格的领导下成为了欧共主义的一个突出例子。 | Synonyms:  revisionist, reformist | Antonyms:  Stalinist, orthodox communist | Noun
