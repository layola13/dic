corniced:/ˈkɔːrnɪst/| adj.|有飞檐的，有檐口的|Having cornices; decorated with cornices.|The grand hotel featured a corniced roofline, adding to its majestic appearance. 这家豪华酒店的屋顶有飞檐，更显雄伟。|近义词：ornamented, decorated, embellished|反义词:plain, simple|adjective

cornice: /ˈkɔːrnɪs/ |n.|飞檐，檐口|A horizontal molded projection crowning a building or structure, especially the uppermost part of a classical facade.|The cornice of the building was intricately carved.  这栋建筑的檐口雕刻精美。|近义词:molding, eaves|反义词:(没有直接的反义词，可用absence of cornice表示无檐口)|noun
