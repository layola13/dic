batteler:/ˈbætlər/| n.|斗争者，战士，争论者|A person who battles; a fighter or contender.|He was a fierce batteler in the courtroom. 他在法庭上是一个激烈的斗争者。|近义词：fighter, combatant, contender, warrior|反义词：pacifist, peacemaker|noun

词源：From Middle English *batelour, *bateler, from Anglo-Norman *batelour, from Old French *bataleor (“fighter”), from *bataillier (“to fight”), from Late Latin *battulāre, from Latin battuō (“I beat, strike, fence”).
