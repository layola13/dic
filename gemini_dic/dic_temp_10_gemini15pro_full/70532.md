filibustered:/ˈfɪlɪbʌstərd/
verb
阻挠议事，冗长辩论
to obstruct or delay legislative action, especially by making long speeches
The senator filibustered the bill for over 24 hours.  这位参议员用冗长的发言阻挠了这项法案超过24小时。
Synonyms: obstruct, delay, stall, stonewall
Antonyms: expedite, facilitate, hasten

filibustered:/ˈfɪlɪbʌstərd/
adjective
被冗长辩论阻挠的
having been subjected to a filibuster
The filibustered bill eventually failed to pass. 这项被冗长辩论阻挠的法案最终未能通过。
Synonyms: obstructed, delayed, stalled
Antonyms: expedited, facilitated, hastened
