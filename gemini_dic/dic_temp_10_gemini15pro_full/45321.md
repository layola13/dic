cumidine:/ˈkjuːmɪdiːn/
n.
枯茗胺;对异丙基甲苯胺
An aromatic amine, specifically 4-isopropylaniline.  It is used in the production of some dyes and pharmaceuticals.
Cumidine is an important intermediate in the synthesis of certain herbicides. 枯茗胺是合成某些除草剂的重要中间体。
Cumidine derivatives are being investigated for their potential use in cancer therapy.  枯茗胺衍生物正在被研究用于癌症治疗的潜力。
近义词:4-isopropylaniline, p-isopropylaniline
反义词: None (no direct antonym for a specific chemical compound)
词性: noun
