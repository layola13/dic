denationalises:/diːˌnæʃnəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使私有化;取消国有化
To transfer (a business or industry) from public to private ownership.
The government denationalised the railways in the 1990s. 政府在20世纪90年代将铁路私有化了。
The government is planning to denationalise several key industries. 政府正计划将几个关键行业私有化。
Synonyms: privatize, deregulate
Antonyms: nationalize, socialize
