ochlocratical: /ˌɒklɒˈkrætɪkəl/ /ˌɑːkləˈkrætɪkəl/
adjective
暴民政治的；群众统治的
Relating to or characterized by ochlocracy (mob rule).  Government by the mob; mob rule.  Characterized by or suggestive of such rule; demagogic.
The speaker warned against the dangers of ochlocratical tendencies in modern democracies. 演讲者警告了现代民主社会中暴民政治倾向的危险。
The rise of social media has fueled concerns about ochlocratical influences on political discourse. 社交媒体的兴起加剧了人们对暴民政治影响政治言论的担忧。
近义词: demagogic, populist, mobocratic
反义词: aristocratic, oligarchic, meritocratic
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Greek *okhlos* (mob, crowd) + *-kratia* (rule, power)  ->  ochlocracy + -ical (suffix forming adjectives)
