oversensibly:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsɪbli/| adv.| 过于明智地，过分理性地|In an excessively sensible or practical manner, often to the point of being unimaginative or unfeeling.| He reacted oversensibly to the news, showing no emotion whatsoever. 他对这个消息反应过分理性，丝毫没有表现出任何情绪。| She packed oversensibly for the trip, bringing only practical items and no fun souvenirs. 她为这次旅行打包得过于理性，只带了实用物品，没有带任何有趣的纪念品。 | 近义词：pragmatically, practically, rationally| 反义词：emotionally, irrationally, foolishly| 词性: 副词
