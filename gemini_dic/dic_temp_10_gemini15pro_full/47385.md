deadbolt:/ˈdɛdbəʊlt/ (US) /ˈdɛdbɒlt/ (UK)| n.|门闩，插销锁|A lock bolt that is moved by turning a knob or key and that slides into a socket to fasten a door.|I always make sure to lock the deadbolt when I leave the house. 我出门时总是确保锁好插销锁。|Synonym: lock, latch, bolt|Antonym: unlock|Noun

deadbolt:/ˈdɛdbəʊlt/ (US) /ˈdɛdbɒlt/ (UK)| vt.|用插销锁锁住|To lock with a deadbolt.|He deadbolted the door before going to bed. 他睡觉前用插销锁锁好了门。|Synonym: lock, secure, fasten|Antonym: unlock|Verb
