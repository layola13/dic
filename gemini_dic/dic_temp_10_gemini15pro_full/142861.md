out-in-the-cold:/aʊt ɪn ðə koʊld/
adjective
被冷落；被排斥；被忽视
excluded, neglected, or ignored, especially by those in power or by a group one would like to belong to.
He felt out in the cold after his colleagues were promoted and he was not.  他感到被冷落了，因为他的同事都升职了，而他没有。
She was left out in the cold when the company restructured and her department was eliminated. 公司重组后，她的部门被裁撤，她被排除在外。
近义词: neglected, ignored, excluded, ostracized, sidelined
反义词: included, accepted, involved, welcomed
