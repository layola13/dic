gunfighters:/ˈɡʌnˌfaɪtərz/
n.
持枪歹徒，枪手
People skilled with guns, especially in the American Old West.
The movie featured a showdown between two legendary gunfighters.  这部电影以两位传奇枪手的对决为特色。
Synonyms: gunmen, shootists, pistoleers
Antonyms: pacifists, peacemakers
noun
