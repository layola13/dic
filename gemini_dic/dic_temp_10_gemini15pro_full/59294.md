ealdorman:/ˈɔːldərmən/| n.| 地方官，郡长，市议员 |A high-ranking official in Anglo-Saxon England, similar to a governor or magistrate. |The ealdorman presided over the shire court. 郡长主持郡法院。|The ealdorman collected taxes for the king. 郡长为国王收税。|Synonym: magistrate, governor, official|Antonym: commoner, peasant|noun
