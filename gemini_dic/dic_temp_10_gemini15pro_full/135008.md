nonnegligences:/nɒnˈnɛɡlɪdʒənsɪz/
n.
lack of negligence; diligence; carefulness
The state of not being negligent; the act of taking care or paying attention.
His nonnegligence in checking the equipment prevented a serious accident. 他的认真检查设备避免了一场严重的事故。
近义词: diligence, carefulness, attentiveness
反义词: negligence, carelessness, inattention
词性: noun


Etymology:
From non- (not) + negligence.  Negligence comes from Old French negligence, from Latin negligentia "neglect," from negligent-, present participle stem of negligere "to neglect," from nec "not" + legere "to gather, choose, read."
