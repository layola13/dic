bibulously:/ˈbɪbjʊləsli/
adjective
嗜酒地，海量地
Given to or marked by the consumption of alcoholic beverages.  Inclined to drink; absorbent.
He led a bibulous life, frequenting bars and parties. 他过着嗜酒的生活，经常出入酒吧和派对。
The bibulous man stumbled out of the pub.  这个嗜酒的男人跌跌撞撞地走出酒吧。
近义词：alcoholic, drunken, intemperate, sottish
反义词：abstemious, temperate, sober
词性：adjective
词源：From Latin bibulus (“drinking, fond of drink”), from bibō (“I drink”).
