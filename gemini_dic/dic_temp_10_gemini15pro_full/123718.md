moonlighting:/ˈmuːnˌlaɪtɪŋ/ | n. |  兼职；夜间活动 | The practice of working a second job, especially secretly, in addition to one's regular employment. |  He was discovered moonlighting as a security guard.  他被发现兼职做保安。| synonyms: side hustle, second job, working two jobs | antonyms: main job, primary employment | noun

moonlight:/ˈmuːnˌlaɪt/ | v. | 兼职；在月光下活动 | To work at a second job, typically secretly, in addition to one's regular employment. | He moonlights as a DJ on weekends. 他周末兼职做DJ。| synonyms: work a second job, side hustle | antonyms:  main job, primary employment | verb
