conquistadors:/kɒŋˈkɪstədɔːz/ (UK), /kɑːŋˈkɪstədɔːrz/ (US)| n. |征服者，（尤指16世纪征服美洲的）西班牙征服者|Spanish soldiers, explorers, and fortune hunters who took part in the conquest of the Americas in the 16th century.|Example: The conquistadors brought disease and destruction to the native populations. 西班牙征服者给当地居民带来了疾病和毁灭。|Example: The conquistadors were driven by a thirst for gold and glory. 西班牙征服者被对黄金和荣耀的渴望所驱使。|近义词：conquerors, invaders|反义词: defenders, protectors|Noun

conquistador:/kɒŋˈkɪstədɔː/ (UK), /kɑːŋˈkɪstədɔːr/ (US)| n. |征服者，（尤指16世纪征服美洲的）西班牙征服者|A Spanish soldier, explorer, and fortune hunter who took part in the conquest of the Americas in the 16th century.|Example: Hernán Cortés was a famous conquistador. 埃尔南·科尔特斯是一位著名的征服者。|Example:  The conquistador Pizarro conquered the Inca Empire. 征服者皮萨罗征服了印加帝国。|近义词：conqueror, invader|反义词: defender, protector|Noun
