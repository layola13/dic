contralto: /kənˈtræltoʊ/ (US), /kənˈtrɑːltəʊ/ (UK)| n. |女低音；女低音歌手|A contralto is the lowest female singing voice.|The contralto's rich voice filled the hall. 女低音浑厚的嗓音响彻整个大厅。|Synonyms: alto, low voice|Antonyms: soprano, high voice|Noun


contraltos: /kənˈtræltoʊz/ (US), /kənˈtrɑːltəʊz/ (UK)| n. |女低音们；女低音歌手们|Plural form of contralto, referring to multiple singers with the lowest female singing voice.|The contraltos harmonized beautifully in the chorus.  女低音们在合唱中配合得非常和谐。|Synonyms: altos, low voices|Antonyms: sopranos, high voices|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *contralto*, from *contra* (against, opposite) + *alto* (high).  Originally referred to the voice part opposite the highest male voice (tenor).
