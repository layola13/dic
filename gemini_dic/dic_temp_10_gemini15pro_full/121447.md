misinterpreted:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɜːrprɪtɪd/
adjective
曲解的，误解的
incorrectly interpreted or understood.
His silence was misinterpreted as arrogance. 他的沉默被误解为傲慢。
近义词:misunderstood, misconstrued
反义词:understood, correctly interpreted
词性:adjective


misinterpret:/ˌmɪsɪnˈtɜːrprɪt/
verb
曲解，误解
to understand (something or someone) incorrectly.
She misinterpreted his actions as a sign of affection. 她误解了他的行为，以为是爱的表示。
近义词:misunderstand, misconstrued
反义词:understand, correctly interpret
词性:verb


词源:
From mis- + interpret.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "wrong," "bad," or "wrongly." "Interpret" comes from the Latin "interpretari," meaning "to explain, expound, understand."
