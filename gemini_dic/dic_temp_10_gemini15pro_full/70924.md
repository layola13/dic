firebombs:/ˈfaɪərbɒmz/ (US) /ˈfaɪərbɒm/ (UK)| n. |燃烧弹；燃烧瓶|A bomb designed to cause fires, often containing napalm or other flammable substances. |Rioters threw firebombs at police. 暴徒向警察投掷燃烧弹。|Synonyms: incendiary bomb, Molotov cocktail, petrol bomb|Antonyms: extinguisher, fire suppressant|Noun


firebombs:/ˈfaɪərbɒmz/ (US) /ˈfaɪərbɒm/ (UK)| v. |用燃烧弹轰炸|To attack with firebombs.|The factory was firebombed during the riots. 工厂在暴乱期间遭到燃烧弹袭击。|Synonyms: bomb, attack|Antonyms: defend, protect|Verb
