blackspot:/ˈblækspɒt/ (UK) /ˈblækspɑːt/ (US)| n.|黑点；污点；事故多发地点；（广播电视的）信号盲区|A blackspot is a place on a road where accidents frequently occur. |This road is a notorious blackspot for accidents. 这条路是一个臭名昭著的事故多发地段。|Synonyms: trouble spot, danger zone, hazard, accident area |Antonyms: safe zone, secure area|noun
blackspot:/ˈblækspɒt/ (UK) /ˈblækspɑːt/ (US)| n.|（植物的）黑斑病|Blackspot is a fungal disease that affects roses and other plants, causing black spots on the leaves. |The roses in my garden are suffering from blackspot. 我花园里的玫瑰得了黑斑病。|Synonyms: leaf spot, fungal disease|Antonyms: healthy foliage|noun
