over-animated: /ˌoʊvərˈænɪmeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过于活跃的，过于兴奋的 | Excessively animated; showing too much excitement or enthusiasm in one's movements and gestures.  Derived from "over-" (excessively) + "animated" (full of life, lively).  |  The children were over-animated after eating too much candy. 孩子们吃了太多糖果后过于兴奋。 | synonyms:  hyperactive, boisterous, exuberant, frenetic | antonyms: calm, subdued, restrained, quiet | adjective
