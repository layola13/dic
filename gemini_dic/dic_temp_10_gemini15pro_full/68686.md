farcilite:/fɑːrˈsiːlaɪt/ | n. |  法西石（一种岩石）| A rare, light-colored, intrusive igneous rock composed primarily of alkali feldspar.  It is a type of syenite rich in alkali feldspar and containing small amounts of mafic minerals. |  Farcilite is found in Montana. 法西石在蒙大拿州被发现。| 近义词: syenite（正长岩）| 反义词: (No direct antonym as it's a specific rock type) | 词性: noun

Etymology->
From Farci, the name of a locality in Montana, USA, where it was first discovered, + -lite (suffix indicating rock or mineral).
