deerstalking: /ˈdɪərstɔːkɪŋ/
n.
猎鹿; 潜行猎鹿
The act of hunting deer by stalking, which involves careful observation and quiet movement to approach the animal undetected.
He enjoys deerstalking in the Scottish Highlands. 他喜欢在苏格兰高地猎鹿。
近义词：hunting, still-hunting
反义词：(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑animal rights activism 动物权利保护主义)
词性：名词

deerstalking: /ˈdɪərstɔːkɪŋ/
词源: deer + stalking.  "deer" 指的是鹿，"stalking" 指的是潜行追踪。
词性: 名词
