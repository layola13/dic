curtailed:/kərˈteɪld/
|adj.|缩短的，削减的|Reduced in extent or quantity; shortened.|The trip was curtailed due to bad weather. 因为天气不好，旅行缩短了。|近义词：reduced, shortened, abridged, decreased|反义词:extended, lengthened, increased, expanded|词性:形容词
词源:From the Old French "curtailler," meaning "to dock," from "curtil," meaning "short," ultimately from Latin "curtus," meaning "short."
