infralapsarian:/ˌɪnfrəˈlæpsəriən/ | adj. n. |次堕落论的；次堕落论者| Relating to or characteristic of infralapsarianism; a believer in infralapsarianism.  Infralapsarianism is a Calvinistic view of predestination that holds that God's decree to permit the fall occurred logically prior to his decree of election and reprobation. |  Example: His infralapsarian views were a subject of much debate among his fellow theologians. 他的次堕落论观点在他神学家同伴中引起了很多争论。 | Example:  He was considered an infralapsarian, believing God decreed election after the fall. 他被认为是一个次堕落论者，相信上帝是在堕落之后才颁布拣选的。| Synonym: sublapsarian | Antonym: supralapsarian | adjective, noun
