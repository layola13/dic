corporation: /ˌkɔːrpəˈreɪʃn/ (US) /ˌkɔːpəˈreɪʃn/ (UK)| n. |公司，企业|A large company or group of companies authorized to act as a single entity and recognized as such in law.|  Example:  She works for a large multinational corporation. 她为一家大型跨国公司工作。

Example: The corporation announced record profits this year. 这家公司今年宣布了创纪录的利润。


近义词: company, firm, business, enterprise, conglomerate
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider: sole proprietorship, partnership)
词性: noun
