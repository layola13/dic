impedimenta:/ɪmˌpedɪˈmentə/
| n. |辎重，累赘，妨碍物| baggage or other things that slow movement or make a task more difficult | The army's progress was slowed by the heavy impedimenta. 部队的行进被沉重的辎重拖慢了。|
|Synonyms: baggage, luggage, encumbrance, obstacle, hindrance|
|Antonyms: aid, assistance, advantage |
|Noun|

impedimenta:/ɪmˌpedɪˈmentə/
| n. |障碍，阻碍| something that makes progress, movement, or achieving something difficult or impossible |  Lack of funding has been a major impediment to the project's success. 缺乏资金是该项目成功的主要障碍。|
|Synonyms: obstruction, barrier, hurdle, snag |
|Antonyms:  help, support |
|Noun|
