outsparkling:/ˌaʊtˈspɑːrkəlɪŋ/
verb
胜过…的光辉；比…更闪耀
surpassing in brilliance or sparkle; outshining.
Her dress outsparkled all the others at the gala. 她的礼服在晚会上比其他所有人的都闪耀。
outshine, eclipse, outdazzle
dim, dull
v.
