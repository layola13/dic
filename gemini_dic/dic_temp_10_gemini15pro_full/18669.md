bepraise:/bɪˈpreɪz/| vt.|赞扬，称赞|To express warm approval or admiration of someone or something.|He bepraised her for her courage and determination. 他称赞她的勇气和决心。|近义词：praise, laud, extol, commend|反义词：criticize, condemn, denounce|词性:动词

词源:  "be-" (prefix meaning "thoroughly" or "all over") + "praise" (from Old French "preisier," ultimately from Latin "pretium" meaning "price, value").  So, to "bepraise" is to thoroughly praise or value someone or something.
