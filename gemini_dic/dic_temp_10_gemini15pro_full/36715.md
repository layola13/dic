collo:/ˈkɒləʊ/ (UK) /ˈkɑːloʊ/ (US)
noun
脖子，衣领，解剖学术语：颈
the part of the body between the head and the shoulders
例句：He grabbed him by the collo.  他抓住他的脖子。

例句：The giraffe's long collo allows it to reach high branches. 长颈鹿的长脖子使它能够够到高处的树枝。

近义词：neck, scruff (especially nape of the neck)
反义词:  (No direct antonym for a body part)

词性:  n. (noun)
