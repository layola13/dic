hematite:/ˈhiːməˌtaɪt/ (US), /ˈhemətaɪt/ (UK)| n. |赤铁矿|A common iron oxide mineral (Fe2O3) that is a major ore of iron, typically black or dark red in color.|The hematite deposits in this region are extensive. 这个地区的赤铁矿矿藏非常丰富。|Synonyms: iron ore, red iron ore, ferric oxide|Antonyms: N/A|noun

hematite:/ˈhiːməˌtaɪt/ (US), /ˈhemətaɪt/ (UK)| n. |赤铁矿|From the Greek word *haimatitēs lithos* meaning "blood-like stone" because of its reddish-brown streak when powdered, ultimately from *haima* meaning "blood".|The ancient Greeks used hematite as a pigment. 古希腊人用赤铁矿作颜料。|Synonyms: iron ore, red iron ore, ferric oxide|Antonyms: N/A|noun
