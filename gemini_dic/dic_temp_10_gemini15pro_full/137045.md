nonstickier:/ˌnɑːnˈstɪkiər/ | adj. |更不粘的；粘性较小的| Less sticky than something else.  It's the comparative form of "nonsticky".| This new pan is nonstickier than my old one.  这个新锅比我的旧锅更不粘。| The updated formula makes the tape nonstickier and easier to remove.  更新后的配方使胶带粘性更小，更容易去除。| 近义词：less sticky, less adhesive | 反义词：stickier, more adhesive |词性: 形容词

nonsticky:/ˌnɑːnˈstɪki/ | adj. |不粘的；不粘锅的| Having a surface to which things will not stick.| I prefer to use a nonstick pan for cooking eggs. 我更喜欢用不粘锅煎鸡蛋。|  The nonstick coating on the pan makes cleanup a breeze.  锅上的不粘涂层使清洁变得轻而易举。| 近义词：non-adhesive | 反义词：sticky, adhesive |词性: 形容词
