forlornness:/fərˈlɔːrnɪs/
n.
凄凉；孤寂；绝望
A state of sadness, loneliness, and hopelessness.
The forlornness of the empty house was palpable.  空荡荡的房子里弥漫着 palpable 的凄凉。
She felt a deep sense of forlornness after her husband died.  丈夫去世后，她感到深深的孤寂。
近义词：desolation, loneliness, despair, hopelessness
反义词：hope, happiness, cheerfulness
词性: 名词

词源：
From forlorn + -ness.  Forlorn itself comes from Middle English forloren, from Old English forloren "lost, ruined, depraved," past participle of forlēosan "to lose utterly."
