anti-catholic:/ˌæntiˈkæθlɪk/
adjective
反天主教的
Opposed to or prejudiced against the Roman Catholic Church, its beliefs, or its followers.
His anti-Catholic sentiments were evident in his writings. 他的反天主教情绪在他的作品中很明显。
anti-religious, sectarian, bigoted
pro-Catholic, Catholic, accepting
adjective


anti-catholic:/ˌæntiˈkæθlɪk/
noun
反天主教者
A person who is opposed to or prejudiced against the Roman Catholic Church, its beliefs, or its followers.
The anti-Catholics protested the Pope's visit. 反天主教者抗议教皇的访问。
bigot, sectarian
Catholic, supporter
noun
