gymnodont:/ˈdʒɪmnəˌdɒnt/ (US) /ˈdʒɪmnəʊˌdɒnt/ (UK)| n. |鲀形目鱼类；硬鳞鱼|A fish of the order Gymnodontes, characterized by having a tough skin, often with spines or plates, and the ability to inflate their bodies with water or air when threatened.
