burkini: /bɜːrˈkiːni/ /bərˈkiːni/
| n. |布基尼（一种覆盖全身的女性泳衣）|A swimsuit that covers the whole body except the face, hands and feet, worn by some Muslim women.|She wore a burkini at the beach.|近义词: swimsuit, bathing suit|反义词: bikini|词性:名词

Etymology:
A portmanteau word formed from burqa and bikini.  The word was coined by Aheda Zanetti, a Lebanese-Australian designer.
