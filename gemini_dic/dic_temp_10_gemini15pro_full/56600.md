donatist:/ˈdɒnətɪst/ /ˈdoʊnətɪst/ | n. adj.|多纳图斯派教徒，多纳图斯派的|A member of a schismatic Christian group in North Africa in the 4th–6th centuries ad, who believed that the sacraments required a morally pure minister in order to be efficacious and thus rejected the validity of sacraments administered by those who had lapsed during the Diocletianic Persecution.|例句：The Donatists believed that the church should be a church of saints, not sinners. 多纳图斯派相信教会应该是圣人的教会，而不是罪人的教会。

例句：The Donatist schism weakened the church in North Africa. 多纳图斯派的分裂削弱了北非教会的力量。|近义词：schismatic, heretic|反义词：orthodox|词性：名词，形容词

donatist:/ˈdɒnətɪst/ /ˈdoʊnətɪst/ | adj.|多纳图斯派的|Relating to or characteristic of the Donatists.|例句：Donatist beliefs were condemned by the Council of Arles. 多纳图斯派的信仰被阿尔勒会议谴责。

例句：The Donatist controversy lasted for centuries. 多纳图斯派的争议持续了几个世纪。|近义词：schismatic, heretical|反义词：orthodox|词性：形容词
