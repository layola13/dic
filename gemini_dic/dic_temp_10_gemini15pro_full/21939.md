boggiest:/ˈbɒɡiɪst/ (US) /ˈbɒɡɪɪst/ (UK)| adj.|最沼泽的，最泥泞的|Superlative form of boggy: having many bogs; wet and spongy.|The boggiest part of the trail was nearly impassable.  这条小径中最泥泞的部分几乎无法通行。| The boggiest ground made hiking difficult. 最泥泞的地面使得徒步旅行很困难。|近义词：marshiest, swampiest, soggiest|反义词：driest, firmest|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From bog + -y + -est.  "Bog" comes from Irish or Scottish Gaelic *bogach* meaning "soft, wet ground."  The suffix "-y" forms adjectives meaning "having the quality of," and "-est" is the superlative suffix.
