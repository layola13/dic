crimeless: /ˈkraɪmləs/
adjective
无罪的，没有犯罪的
Free from crime; innocent.
The small town prides itself on being a crimeless utopia.  这个小镇以其无犯罪的乌托邦而自豪。
He led a relatively crimeless life until he fell in with the wrong crowd. 在与坏人交往之前，他过着相对没有犯罪的生活。
近义词: innocent, law-abiding, blameless
反义词: criminal, guilty, lawless
词性: adjective

Etymology: crime + -less.  From "crime" (meaning an illegal act) and the suffix "-less" (meaning without).
