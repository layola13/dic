insinuate: /ɪnˈsɪn.ju.eɪt/ /ɪnˈsɪn.jʊ.eɪt/
verb
暗示；含沙射影地说；巧妙地取得…的信任(或好感)
To suggest or hint something, especially something unpleasant or critical, in an indirect and subtle way; to subtly introduce oneself into a situation or someone's favor.
He insinuated that she had lied. 他暗示她撒了谎。

She gradually insinuated herself into his confidence. 她逐渐巧妙地取得了他的信任。

Synonyms: imply, suggest, intimate, hint, allude
Antonyms: declare, state, assert, pronounce
词性: 动词
