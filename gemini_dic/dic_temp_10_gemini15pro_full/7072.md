anencephalic:/ˌænənˌsɛˈfælɪk/ | adj. | 无脑的；无脑畸形的 | Born without a major portion of the brain, skull, and scalp. | The anencephalic baby tragically died shortly after birth.  这个无脑婴儿出生后不久就悲惨地死去了。|  Synonyms: anencephalous | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider "neurologically intact" or "normal brain development") | adjective


anencephaly: /ˌænənˈsɛfəli/ | n. | 无脑畸形 | A birth defect in which a baby is born without parts of the brain and skull. | Anencephaly is a serious neural tube defect. 无脑畸形是一种严重的神经管缺陷。 | Synonyms: anencephalia | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, as it is a birth defect.) | noun
