noncriminally:/ˌnɑːnˈkrɪmɪnəli/
adverb
非犯罪地，非刑事地
In a way that is not a crime.
He settled the dispute noncriminally.  他以非刑事的方式解决了纠纷。
The issue was handled noncriminally.  这个问题得到了非刑事处理。
近义词: legally, lawfully
反义词: criminally
词性: adverb
