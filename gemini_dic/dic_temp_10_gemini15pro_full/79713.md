glucoside: /ˈɡluːkəsaɪd/ | n. | 糖苷 | A compound formed from a simple sugar and another compound by replacement of a hydroxyl group in the sugar molecule.  A glycoside is any molecule in which a sugar group is bonded through its anomeric carbon to another group via a glycosidic bond. |  Many drugs are administered as glycosides to increase their water solubility. 许多药物以糖苷的形式给药以增加其水溶性。 | Salicin is a glucoside found in willow bark. 水杨苷是一种存在于柳树皮中的糖苷。 |  glycoside | None | noun
