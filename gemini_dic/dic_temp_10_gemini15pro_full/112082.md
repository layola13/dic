longingnesses:/ˈlɒŋɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
渴望，憧憬， yearning;  strong desires
Longingnesses for home filled his heart as he traveled abroad.  当他在国外旅行时，对家乡的渴望充满了他的心。
Synonyms: yearnings, hankerings, cravings, desires
Antonyms: apathy, indifference, contentment
noun

Etymology: From "longing" (from Old English "langung," meaning "a longing, yearning") + "-ness" (a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, denoting a state, quality, or condition).
