acephalan: /eɪˈsɛfələn/
n.
无头畸胎
An acephalan is a fetus or infant born without a head.
The acephalan was a tragic result of a genetic mutation.  这个无头畸胎是基因突变的悲剧结果。

近义词: anencephalic
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider terms like 'normal fetus', 'healthy infant')
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From a- (without) + cephale (head) from Greek kephalē.
