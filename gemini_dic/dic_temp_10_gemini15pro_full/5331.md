alloxantin:/ˌælɒˈzænθɪn/ | n. | 别嘌呤醇 | A purine derivative related to uric acid. It is a competitive inhibitor of xanthine oxidase. | Alloxantin can be used in some biochemical assays.  别嘌呤醇可用于某些生化分析。| Alloxantin is an inhibitor of xanthine oxidase. 别嘌呤醇是黄嘌呤氧化酶的抑制剂。 | synonym: xanthine oxidase inhibitor | antonym:  xanthine oxidase activator | noun
