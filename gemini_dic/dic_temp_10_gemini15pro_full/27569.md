canalize:/ˈkænəlaɪz/| v.|引导；疏导；形成管道；开凿运河|To direct into a particular course or channel; to provide with a canal or canals.|The government is canalizing the river to prevent flooding. 政府正在疏浚河流以防止洪水泛滥。|direct, channel, guide, constrain|divert, disperse, scatter|verb
canalize:/ˈkænəlaɪz/| v.|（指本能或情感）被引导，得到宣泄|To provide an outlet for (instincts or emotions) so that they are directed into socially acceptable activities.|He canalized his aggression into sports. 他把他的攻击性转化为体育运动。|channel, direct, sublimate|repress, suppress, inhibit|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French canaliser, from canal (“canal”).  Ultimately from Latin canalis (“pipe, channel”).
