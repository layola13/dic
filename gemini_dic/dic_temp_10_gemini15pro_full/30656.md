cernuous:/ˈsɜːrnjuːəs/| adj.|低垂的，下垂的|Drooping; hanging down, typically of a plant's leaves or flowers.|The cernuous blossoms of the weeping willow trailed in the breeze. 垂柳低垂的花朵在微风中摇曳。|近义词：drooping, pendulous, nodding|反义词：erect, upright|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *cernuus*, from *cernere* "to separate, distinguish, sift, decide, decree".  The sense development is likely from the idea of something separated or sifted out, thus hanging down.
