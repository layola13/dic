interconfounded: /ˌɪntərˌkənˈfaʊndɪd/
adjective
相互混淆的，混杂的
mixed up or confused in such a way that the individual elements are difficult to distinguish.
The study's results were interconfounded by a number of variables, making it difficult to draw clear conclusions.  这项研究的结果被许多变量相互混淆，难以得出明确的结论。
The various factors were so interconfounded that it was impossible to determine the cause of the problem. 各种因素相互混杂，以致于无法确定问题的根源。
近义词:  intermingled, intertwined, confused, entangled
反义词: distinct, separate, clear
