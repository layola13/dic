narcissists:/ˈnɑːrsɪsɪsts/| n. |自恋者|A person who has an excessive admiration of themselves.  A person who is overly self-involved, and often vain and selfish.|例句： Narcissists often have difficulty maintaining healthy relationships. 自恋者通常难以维持健康的人际关系。

例句：Many narcissists crave attention and admiration. 很多自恋者渴望得到关注和赞赏。|近义词：egotists, self-centered individuals|反义词：altruists, humble individuals|词性:Noun (plural)
