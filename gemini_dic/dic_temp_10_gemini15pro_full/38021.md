compluvium:/kɒmˈpluːviəm/ /kɑːmˈpluːviəm/ | n. |雨水池，天井 |An opening in the roof of a Roman house, through which rainwater fell into a cistern below.  It was the main source of water for domestic use.| The compluvium was a key feature of Roman architecture, providing light and water.  罗马房屋的天井是其建筑的关键特征，提供光线和水源。| synonyms: atrium, courtyard, lightwell | antonyms: roof, ceiling | noun
