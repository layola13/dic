chaperoning:/ˈʃæpərəʊnɪŋ/ |n./v. |陪伴；护送|The act of accompanying someone, especially a young person, to ensure their safety and proper behavior.|She was chaperoning a group of teenagers at the concert. 她在一个音乐会上陪伴着一群青少年。|近义词: escorting, accompanying, supervising, attending |反义词: neglecting, abandoning |词性:  noun (gerund form of the verb "chaperone")

chaperone:/ˈʃæpərəʊn/ |n./v.|陪伴；护送|A person who accompanies and looks after another person or group of people, especially a young person or group of young people.|My aunt chaperoned me to the school dance. 我阿姨陪我去参加学校舞会。|近义词: escort, attendant, guardian, supervisor |反义词: - |词性: noun/verb
