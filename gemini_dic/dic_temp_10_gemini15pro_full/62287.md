endable:/ˈɛndəbl/
adjective
可结束的，可终止的
capable of being ended or terminated.
例句：The contract is endable after three years.  合同三年后可终止。

例句：This is an endable cycle of violence. 这是一个可以结束的暴力循环。

近义词：terminable, finishable, concludable
反义词：interminable, endless, perpetual
词性: adjective
