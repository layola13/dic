adulterating: /əˈdʌltəreɪtɪŋ/
verb
掺杂，搀假
making something poorer in quality by adding another substance
The company was accused of adulterating its products with cheap ingredients.  该公司被指控在产品中掺杂廉价成分。

The milk was adulterated with water. 牛奶里掺了水。


近义词: contaminating, debasing, polluting, tainting
反义词: purifying, refining
词性: verb (present participle)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adulteratus*, past participle of *adulterare*  "to corrupt, falsify," from *ad* "to" + *alter* "other"


USAGE->
一般现在时:
The company adulterates its products to increase profits. = 这家公司为了增加利润而在产品中掺假。
He adulterates the wine with cheaper alcohol. = 他用便宜的酒精掺假葡萄酒。

一般过去时:
The manufacturer adulterated the food with harmful chemicals. = 制造商在食品中掺入了有害化学物质。
They adulterated the olive oil with a cheaper substitute. = 他们用更便宜的替代品掺假橄榄油。

现在完成时:
The company has adulterated its products for years. = 这家公司多年来一直在产品中掺假。
Someone has adulterated the milk with water. = 有人往牛奶里掺了水。

过去完成时:
The company had adulterated the product before it was discovered. = 在被发现之前，公司就已经在产品中掺假了。
They had adulterated the meat with horsemeat before it reached the market. = 在肉类上市之前，他们已经在其中掺入了马肉。


将来时:
The authorities will prosecute anyone who adulterates food products. = 当局将起诉任何掺假食品的人。
If they adulterate the medicine, it will be dangerous. = 如果他们掺假药品，那将会很危险。

被动语态:
The product was adulterated with a cheaper ingredient. = 该产品被掺入了更便宜的成分。
The olive oil is being adulterated with a substitute. = 橄榄油正被掺入替代品。


PHRASE->
adulterating food = 掺假食物
adulterating drugs = 掺假药物
adulterating with chemicals = 用化学物质掺假
adulterating for profit = 为了利润掺假
adulterating products = 掺假产品
