biscutate:/bɪˈskʌteɪt/
adj.
饼干状的；两裂的
Having the form of a biscuit; divided into two lobes.
The biscutate leaf of the plant was a unique characteristic.  这种植物的两裂叶子是一个独特的特征。

The kidney is biscutate in shape. 肾脏的形状是两裂的。
synonym: bifurcate, bilobed, two-lobed
antonym: entire, undivided
词性: adjective
