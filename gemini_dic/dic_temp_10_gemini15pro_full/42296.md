counter-intuitive:/ˌkaʊntər ɪnˈtuːɪtɪv/ | adj. | 反直觉的 | Contrary to what one would intuitively expect; not easily understood. |  It seems counter-intuitive to eat more to lose weight, but it can be true in some cases. 看似反直觉的是吃得更多反而能减肥，但在某些情况下这可能是真的。

It's counter-intuitive, but sometimes silence is the best response.  这似乎违反直觉，但有时沉默是最好的回应。 | Synonyms: illogical, unreasonable, unexpected, surprising | Antonyms: intuitive, logical, reasonable, expected, predictable | adjective
