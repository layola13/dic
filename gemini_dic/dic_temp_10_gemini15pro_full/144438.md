outswear:/ˌaʊtˈswɛr/
verb
咒骂得比...厉害
to swear more or more effectively than someone else
He could outswear any sailor. 他骂起人来比任何水手都厉害。
近义词: outcurse, surpass in swearing
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider "bless," "praise," or "speak kindly of")
词性: verb

Etymology:
From out- + swear.  The prefix "out-" adds the sense of surpassing or exceeding.
