ancestral:/ænˈsɛstrəl/| adj.|祖先的，祖传的|Of, relating to, or inherited from an ancestor or ancestors.|例句：She has an ancestral home in Scotland. 她祖籍苏格兰，在那里有一套祖传的房子。

例句：The family has passed down their ancestral recipes for generations.  这个家族世代相传他们的祖传食谱。|近义词：inherited, hereditary, familial, genealogical|反义词：modern, contemporary|词性:形容词
