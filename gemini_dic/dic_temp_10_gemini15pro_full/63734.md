epibolic:/ˌɛpɪˈbɒlɪk/ | adj. | 上包的，外包的 | Relating to epiboly, a process in embryonic development where one layer of cells spreads over and encloses another layer. |  The epibolic movement of the ectoderm is crucial for gastrulation.  外胚层的包覆运动对原肠胚形成至关重要。

The researchers studied the epibolic closure of the neural tube. 研究人员研究了神经管的上包闭合。 | 近义词: enveloping, overgrowing | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective
