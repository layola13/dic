nondecorous:/nɒnˈdɛkərəs/ | adj. | 不雅的，不得体的 | Lacking decorum; improper or unseemly. | Her nondecorous behavior at the funeral shocked the mourners. 她不合时宜的葬礼举止震惊了哀悼者。|  His nondecorous laughter during the serious presentation was inappropriate.  在严肃的演讲中，他不得体的笑声是不合适的。 | 近义词：indecorous, unseemly, improper, unbecoming | 反义词：decorous, proper, seemly, appropriate | 词性: adjective

词源: non- (Latin prefix meaning "not") + decorous (from Latin decōrus, meaning "becoming, proper, graceful")
