ankylose:/ˈæŋkɪˌloʊs/ (US), /ˈæŋkɪˌləʊs/ (UK)| v.  adj.|强直，僵硬|To become stiff or fixed; to cause a joint to become stiff or fixed.|The arthritis caused his knee to ankylose.  关节炎导致他的膝盖僵硬。| 近义词:stiffen, solidify, fuse|反义词:loosen, free|词性:动词，形容词
ankylosis:/ˌæŋkɪˈloʊsɪs/ (US), /ˌæŋkɪˈləʊsɪs/ (UK)| n.|强直，僵硬|The stiffening or fixation of a joint.|The patient suffered from ankylosis of the spine. 病人患有脊柱强直。|近义词:stiffening, rigidity|反义词:flexibility|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ankylosis, from Greek ankylosis "stiffening of a joint,"  from ankylos "bent, crooked, stiff," from ankyle "bend, angle."
