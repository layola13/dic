chastisement:/ˈtʃæstɪzmənt/ (US) /ˈtʃæstɪzmənt/ (UK)| n. |惩罚；责罚；磨炼|The act of punishing someone, often physically, as a disciplinary measure.  It can also refer to a severe reprimand or criticism.|He received a severe chastisement for his disobedience. 他因不服从而受到了严厉的惩罚。|Synonyms: punishment, discipline, correction, reprimand, rebuke, admonition|Antonyms: reward, praise, commendation|Noun
