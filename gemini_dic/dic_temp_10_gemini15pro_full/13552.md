attemptible:/əˈtɛmptɪbl/
adjective
可尝试的，可攻击的
Open to attempts; capable of being attempted. Also, vulnerable or open to attack.
The climb, though challenging, seemed attemptible with the right equipment. 攀登虽然具有挑战性，但如果装备合适，似乎还是可以尝试的。
The enemy's weak defenses made them attemptible. 敌人的薄弱防御使他们容易受到攻击。
近义词: achievable, assailable, vulnerable
反义词: impossible, unattainable, invincible
词性: adjective

词源: From attempt + -ible.  "Attempt" comes from the Old French "attempter" and ultimately from the Latin "attentare," meaning "to try."  "-ible" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being," from the Latin "-ibilis."
