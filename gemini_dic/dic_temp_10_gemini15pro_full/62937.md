enmove: /ɛnˈmuːv/
|v.|使感动，使行动|To inspire or prompt to action; to move emotionally.|Her plea enmoved the jury to tears. 她的恳求使陪审团感动落泪。|近义词: move, touch, stir, affect|反义词: leave cold, unmoved|词性:动词

词源: "En-" (prefix meaning "to put into" or "to cause to be") + "move".  The prefix "en-" adds intensity to the verb "move".
