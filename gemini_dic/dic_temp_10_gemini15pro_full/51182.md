destabilises:/diːˈsteɪbəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使不稳定，破坏稳定
To make something less stable or secure, to undermine the stability of something.
The sudden change in leadership destabilised the country. 突然的领导层变动使该国变得不稳定。
The rumors of a coup destabilised the government. 政变的谣言动摇了政府的统治。
Synonyms: undermine, weaken, unsettle, disrupt
Antonyms: stabilize, strengthen, secure
词性: 动词


destabilise:/diːˈsteɪbəlaɪz/
verb
使不稳定，破坏稳定
To make something less stable or secure, to undermine the stability of something.
The economic crisis destabilised the entire region. 经济危机使整个地区动荡不安。
The new policy destabilised the housing market. 新政策导致房地产市场不稳定。
Synonyms:  undermine, weaken, unsettle, disrupt
Antonyms: stabilize, strengthen, secure
词性: 动词


词源:  de- (prefix meaning "reverse" or "remove") + stable + -ise (verb suffix)  来自拉丁语 stabilis, 意思是"stable, steadfast".
