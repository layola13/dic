overmortgaged:/ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrɡɪdʒd/
adjective
过度抵押的
Having a mortgage or mortgages whose total value is greater than the value of the property.
The overmortgaged couple struggled to make their monthly payments. 这对过度抵押的夫妇难以支付每月的还款。
overleveraged, debt-ridden
