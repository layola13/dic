gilgalad:/ˈɡɪlɡælæd/| n. |吉尔加拉德|A fictional character, the High King of the Ñoldor in Tolkien's legendarium, particularly known from The Lord of the Rings and The Silmarillion.  He is renowned for his wisdom, bravery, and skill in both war and peace.  His name means "Starlight" in Sindarin.|例句：Gilgalad was a mighty king. 吉尔加拉德是一位强大的国王。

例句：The ships of Gilgalad sailed to Valinor. 吉尔加拉德的船只航向了维林诺。|近义词：king, ruler, sovereign|反义词:servant, subject|词性:noun
