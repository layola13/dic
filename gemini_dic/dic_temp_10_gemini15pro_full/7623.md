annuitize:/əˈnuːɪtaɪz/ |v. |  年金化; 将(资本)转化为年金| To convert (capital) into a fixed income paid annually for life or for a stated term. |Example: He decided to annuitize his savings to provide a steady income during retirement.  他决定将积蓄年金化，以便在退休期间获得稳定的收入。

|Example:  She annuitized a portion of her inheritance. 她将一部分遗产年金化了。|Synonym: amortize, capitalize |Antonym:  lump sum (n.) |verb
