deterrent:/dɪˈtɜːrənt/ /dɪˈtɝːənt/ | n. adj. | 威慑物；制止物；阻碍物 | Something that discourages or prevents a certain action or behavior. |  The high price of the tickets was a deterrent to many fans.  高昂的票价对许多球迷来说是一种威慑。| synonyms: discouragement, obstacle, impediment, curb, restraint | antonyms: incentive, encouragement, inducement, motivation | noun

deterrent:/dɪˈtɜːrənt/ /dɪˈtɝːənt/ | adj. | 威慑的；制止的；阻碍的 | Tending to deter. |  The company implemented deterrent measures to prevent theft. 公司实施了威慑措施以防止盗窃。| synonyms: preventative, dissuasive, discouraging | antonyms: encouraging, promoting, inciting | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the late 16th century, from the verb "deter" + "-ent".  "Deter" comes from the Latin "deterrēre," meaning "to frighten from," from "de-" (away) + "terrēre" (to frighten).
