antagonistic:/ænˌtæɡəˈnɪstɪk/
adjective
对抗的，敌对的
Showing or feeling active opposition or hostility towards someone or something.
He had an antagonistic relationship with his father. 他和他父亲的关系很敌对。

The two groups have always been antagonistic to each other. 这两个团体彼此一直敌对。


近义词:hostile, opposed, inimical, adversarial
反义词:friendly, supportive, cooperative, agreeable
词性:adjective
