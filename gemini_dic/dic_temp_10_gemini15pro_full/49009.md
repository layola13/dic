degraded:/dɪˈɡreɪdɪd/ | adj. | 降级的；堕落的；退化的；低落的 | Reduced in rank, quality, or character; deteriorated. | The water quality in the river has been severely degraded due to industrial pollution.  由于工业污染，河里的水质已经严重恶化。 | The old building was in a degraded state. 这栋老建筑处于破败的状态。 | synonyms: debased, lowered, diminished, impaired, deteriorated, worsened, corrupt, dishonored | antonyms: elevated, improved, enhanced, upgraded, honored, noble |词性:形容词

degrade:/dɪˈɡreɪd/ | vt. | 降低；使堕落；贬低；分解 |  To reduce in rank, quality, or character; to decompose. |  The scandal degraded him in the eyes of the public.  这起丑闻降低了他在公众心目中的形象。 |  Exposure to sunlight can degrade certain plastics. 阳光照射会使某些塑料分解。 | synonyms: debase, lower, diminish, impair, deteriorate, worsen, corrupt, dishonor | antonyms: elevate, improve, enhance, upgrade, honor, ennoble | 词性:动词
