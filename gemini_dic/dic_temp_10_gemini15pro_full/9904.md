ape:/eɪp/| n. v.|猿，模仿|A large primate that lacks a tail, including the gorilla, chimpanzee, orangutan, and gibbon.  As a verb, it means to imitate someone or something, often clumsily or ridiculously.|例句：The ape swung effortlessly through the trees.  猿猴轻松地在树间荡来荡去。|近义词：monkey, primate, mimic, imitate|反义词:human (in a broad sense)|noun, verb

ape:/eɪp/| v.|模仿|To imitate someone or something, often clumsily or ridiculously.|例句：He aped the teacher's mannerisms, much to the amusement of the class. 他模仿老师的举止，逗得全班都很开心。|近义词：mimic, imitate, copy, parrot|反义词:originate, innovate|verb
