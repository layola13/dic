fringier:/ˈfrɪndʒɪər/
adjective
更边缘的，更另类的
more unconventional or extreme than the usual or accepted; more on the fringes
Her fashion sense is getting fringier and fringier. 她的时尚感越来越另类了。
Synonyms: unconventional, eccentric, quirky, offbeat, avant-garde, bohemian, nonconformist, unorthodox, radical, extreme
Antonyms: conventional, mainstream, orthodox, traditional, conservative
词性: adjective


fringe:/frɪndʒ/
noun
边缘，流苏
the outer or less important part of an area, group, or activity; a decorative border of hanging threads, cords, or strips, often attached to a curtain, tablecloth, or piece of clothing.
They lived on the fringes of society. 他们生活在社会的边缘。
The curtains had a long fringe. 窗帘有长长的流苏。
Synonyms: edge, border, periphery, margin, rim, fringe, tassel
Antonyms: center, core, mainstream
词性: noun


fringe:/frɪndʒ/
verb
给…加穗边
to decorate (something) with a fringe
The tablecloth was fringed with gold thread. 桌布镶着金线流苏。
Synonyms: decorate, adorn, trim, edge, border
Antonyms: strip, bare
词性: noun, verb


fringy:/ˈfrɪndʒi/
adjective
有穗边的，边缘的
decorated with a fringe; on the fringes
She wore a fringy jacket. 她穿着一件流苏夹克。
Synonyms: fringed, tasseled, marginal, peripheral
Antonyms: central, core
词性: adjective
