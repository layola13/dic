bulimia:/bʊˈlɪmiə/ | n. |暴食症；贪食症|An eating disorder characterized by episodes of binge eating followed by inappropriate methods of purging, such as vomiting or the misuse of laxatives. |She struggled with bulimia for years. 她多年来一直在与贪食症作斗争。| Bulimia can have serious health consequences. 暴食症会导致严重的健康问题。 | 近义词: hyperphagia, insatiable hunger | 反义词: anorexia | 词性: noun
