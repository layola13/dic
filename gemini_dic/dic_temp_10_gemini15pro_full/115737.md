mariculturist:/ˌmærɪˈkʌltʃərɪst/| n. |海产养殖者，水产养殖家|A person who cultivates marine life, such as fish or shellfish, for food or other products.|例句：The mariculturist carefully monitored the water quality in the fish farm.  水产养殖家仔细监测养鱼场的水质。

例句：Mariculturists play a vital role in providing sustainable seafood. 海产养殖者在提供可持续海产品方面发挥着至关重要的作用。|近义词：aquaculturist, fish farmer|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *mare* (sea) + *cultura* (cultivation) + *-ist* (one who does).
