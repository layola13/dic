balustered:/ˈbæləstərd/| adj.|有栏杆的，有扶手的|Having balusters, which are small posts supporting a railing.|The balustered balcony offered a stunning view of the valley.  有栏杆的阳台提供了山谷的壮丽景色。|近义词：railed, balustraded|反义词: open, unenclosed|词性:形容词
词源：From baluster + -ed.  Baluster comes from Italian "balaustro," ultimately from Greek "balaústion" (pomegranate flower), due to the resemblance of some balusters to the flower's shape.
