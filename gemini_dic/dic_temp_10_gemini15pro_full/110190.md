lieutenant-governor:/luːˈtɛnənt ˈɡʌvərnər/
noun
副州长；副省长
A lieutenant governor is an official who is second in rank to a governor in some countries or regions.
The lieutenant governor presided over the Senate. 副州长主持了参议院会议。
The lieutenant governor will assume the governor's duties if the governor is incapacitated. 如果州长失去工作能力，副州长将承担州长的职责。
synonyms: deputy governor, vice-governor
antonyms: governor
