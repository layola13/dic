exhume:/ɪɡˈzjuːm/ /ɛksˈhjuːm/ | v. | 掘出; 发掘;  | To dig out (something buried, especially a corpse) from the earth. | The body was exhumed for further examination. 尸体被掘出以作进一步检查。 | synonyms: disinter, unearth, uncover | antonyms: bury, inter, entomb | verb

exhumation:/ˌɛkshjuːˈmeɪʃən/ /ˌɛɡzjuːˈmeɪʃən/ | n. | 掘尸; 发掘 | The act of exhuming, especially a corpse. | The exhumation of the body provided new evidence in the case. 掘尸提供了新的案件证据。 | synonyms: disinterment | antonyms: burial, interment | noun
