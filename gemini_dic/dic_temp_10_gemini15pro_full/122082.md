misthrowing:/ˌmɪsˈθroʊɪŋ/
verb
投掷失误，投错
The act of throwing something incorrectly or inaccurately.
He lost the game due to his misthrowing of the ball in the final seconds.  由于他在最后几秒钟投球失误，他输掉了比赛。
近义词:  miscasting, bungling, mishandling
反义词:  perfect throw, accurate throw
词性: verb
