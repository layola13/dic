habitable:/ˈhæbɪtəbl/| adj.|可居住的，适于居住的|Suitable for living in; offering the basic necessities for human life.|例句：The planet Mars is not currently habitable for humans. 火星目前不适合人类居住。

例句：Scientists are searching for habitable planets in other solar systems. 科学家们正在其他星系中寻找适宜居住的行星。|近义词：livable, inhabitable, residential|反义词：uninhabitable, unlivable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *habitabilis, from Latin habitāre "to inhabit," from habitus "condition, appearance," past participle of habēre "to have, hold."
