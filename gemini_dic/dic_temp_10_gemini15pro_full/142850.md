out-glows:/ˌaʊtˈɡloʊz/
verb
发出比...更强的光; 比...更辉煌
shines more brightly than something else
例句：The city out-glows the surrounding countryside at night.  这座城市在夜晚比周围的乡村更加辉煌。

例句：Her performance out-glowed everyone else's. 她的表演比其他任何人都更加出色。
近义词：outshines, surpasses, eclipses
反义词：is outshone by, is overshadowed by
词性：动词
