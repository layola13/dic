czarina:/zɑːˈriːnə/
n.
女沙皇，皇后
The female ruler of Russia, or the wife of a czar.
Catherine the Great was a powerful czarina.  叶卡捷琳娜大帝是一位强大的女沙皇。
The czarina issued a decree. 女沙皇颁布了一项法令。

近义词:empress, queen, tsarina
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps commoner, subject)
词性:noun
