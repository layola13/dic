buffeter:/ˈbʌfɪtər/| n.|自助餐者，经常吃自助餐的人|A person who frequently eats at buffets. |He's such a buffeter; he can eat mountains of food. 他真是个自助餐爱好者，他能吃下堆积如山的食物。|近义词：gourmand, glutton (贬义)|反义词：dieter, picky eater|noun

buffeter:/ˈbʌfɪtər/| n.|受自助餐折磨的人|A person who is negatively affected by buffets, perhaps due to overeating or poor quality. |After that all-you-can-eat seafood buffet, I felt like a real buffeter, bloated and uncomfortable.  在那家海鲜自助餐之后，我觉得自己像个受罪的自助餐者，肚子胀得难受。|近义词：sufferer|反义词：satisfied diner|noun
