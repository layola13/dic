bemoaning:/bɪˈmoʊnɪŋ/
verb
哀叹，悲叹
Expressing grief or disappointment about something.
She was bemoaning the loss of her favorite necklace. 她正在哀叹她最喜欢的项链的丢失。
The farmers were bemoaning the lack of rain. 农民们在哀叹缺少雨水。
近义词:lamenting, bewailing, complaining
反义词:rejoicing, celebrating
词性:v.

词源:
From Middle English *bimenen, from Old English *bēmēnan (“to mean; signify; complain; lament”), from Proto-Germanic *gamainjaną (“to have in common, share; communicate, inform; lament, complain”), from Proto-Indo-European *mey- (“to exchange, change”). Cognate with Old Frisian bimēna (“to signify, mean”), Old Saxon bimēnan (“to mean”), Old High German bimeinan (“to mean, signify”), Gothic (gamains, “common”). Related to mean.
