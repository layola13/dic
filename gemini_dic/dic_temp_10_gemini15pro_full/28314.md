caracal: /ˈkærəkæl/ (US) /ˈkærəkɑːl/ (UK)| n. |狞猫|A medium-sized wild cat with long, tufted ears, found in Africa and Asia. It is known for its ability to leap high to catch birds.|The caracal is a skilled hunter. 狞猫是一个熟练的猎手。| The zookeeper fed the caracal.  动物园管理员喂养了狞猫。 |Synonyms: lynx, desert lynx|Antonyms: N/A|noun
