continuant:/kənˈtɪnjuənt/| n. adj.|延续音，持续的|A speech sound, such as /f/ or /m/, that is produced with continuous, non-turbulent airflow.|The sounds /s/ and /z/ are fricatives, while /m/ and /n/ are continuants.|近义词：sustained, prolonged, continuous|反义词：stop, plosive|词性: noun, adjective

continuant:/kənˈtɪnjuənt/| adj.|持续的|Lasting or continuing for a long time.|The doctor recommended continuant care for the patient.|近义词：ongoing, enduring, perpetual|反义词：intermittent, sporadic|词性: adjective
