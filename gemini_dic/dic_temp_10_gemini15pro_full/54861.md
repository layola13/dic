displeasance:/dɪˈspliːzəns/
n.
不愉快，不高兴，恼怒
A feeling of annoyance or displeasure.
His displeasance at the interruption was evident. 他对被打断感到很不高兴，这很明显。
The king's displeasance was obvious to all. 国王的不悦之情显而易见。
近义词: displeasure, annoyance, irritation, dissatisfaction
反义词: pleasure, satisfaction, delight, contentment
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Old French desplaisance, from desplaisir "to displease," from des- "dis-" + plaisir "to please" (from Latin placere "to please").
