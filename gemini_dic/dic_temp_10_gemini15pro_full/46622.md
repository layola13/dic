damming:/ˈdæmɪŋ/| adj. |筑坝的；该咒骂的；十足的|  Creating a dam; extremely bad or annoying (informal).| The damming evidence led to his conviction.  这些确凿的证据导致了他的定罪。| Synonyms: condemning, incriminating, damnable (informal) | Antonyms:  exonerating, absolving | Adjective


damming:/ˈdæmɪŋ/| n. |筑坝；咒骂|  The act of building a dam; the act of condemning or cursing.| The damming of the river created a large reservoir.  这条河的筑坝形成了一个大型水库。| Synonyms:  construction (of a dam), denunciation | Antonyms: removal (of a dam), praise | Noun

damming:/ˈdæmɪŋ/| v. |筑坝；(水) 筑坝拦住；抑制；控制 (感情等)| Present participle of "to dam". | They are damming the river to create hydroelectric power. 他们正在筑坝拦河，以产生水力发电。|Synonyms:  obstructing, blocking, repressing | Antonyms: releasing, freeing | Verb
