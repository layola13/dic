apassionata:/əˌpæʃəˈnɑːtə/
n.|热情奏鸣曲；充满激情的作品|A musical piece, typically a sonata, characterized by passionate expression.|Beethoven's Appassionata is a masterpiece of the Romantic era. 贝多芬的《热情奏鸣曲》是浪漫主义时期的杰作。|近义词: fervent, impassioned, fiery|反义词: calm, serene|词性:名词

apassionato:/əˌpæʃəˈnɑːtoʊ/
adj. |充满激情的；热烈的|Performed or felt with passion.|He delivered an appassionato performance of the concerto. 他充满激情地演奏了协奏曲。|近义词: passionate, ardent, intense|反义词: apathetic, indifferent|词性:形容词

词源:
From Italian *appassionato*, past participle of *appassionare* (“to impassion”), from *passione* (“passion”). Ultimately from Latin *passio* (“suffering, passion”).
来自意大利语 *appassionato*，*appassionare*（“使充满激情”）的过去分词，源自 *passione*（“激情”）。最终源自拉丁语 *passio*（“苦难，激情”）。
