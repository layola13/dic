muffledly:/ˈmʌfldli/ | adv. | 沉闷地；低沉地；模糊不清地 |  In a way that sounds quiet or not clear because something is preventing the sound from travelling properly. |  She spoke muffledly through the thick scarf. 她透过厚厚的围巾低沉地说着。 | 近义词：indistinctly, quietly, faintly | 反义词：clearly, loudly, distinctly | 词性: 副词

muffled:/ˈmʌfld/ | adj. | 沉闷的；低沉的；模糊不清的 |  (of a sound) quiet or not clear because something is preventing the sound from travelling properly. | The muffled sound of voices came from the next room. 从隔壁房间传来低沉的声音。| 近义词：indistinct, quiet, faint | 反义词：clear, loud, distinct | 词性: 形容词

词源: From the verb "muffle" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Muffle" comes from the Old French "mouffler" meaning "to wrap up, to muffle," probably of imitative origin.
