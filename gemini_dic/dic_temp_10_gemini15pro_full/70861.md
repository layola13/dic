finless:/ˈfɪnlɪs/| adj.|无鳍的，没有鳍的|Lacking fins, the wing-like or paddle-like appendages used by fish and other aquatic animals for propulsion, balance, and steering.|例句：The finless porpoise is a unique species found in Asian waters.  长江江豚是一种独特的亚洲水域物种。

例句：Finless fish are less maneuverable in the water.  没有鳍的鱼在水中活动不便。|近义词：fin-lacking|反义词：finned|词性:形容词
