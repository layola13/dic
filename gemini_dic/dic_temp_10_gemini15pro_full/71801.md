fledglings:/ˈfledʒlɪŋz/
noun
雏鸟；新手；缺乏经验的人
Young birds that have just fledged; inexperienced people.
The fledglings were ready to leave the nest. 这些雏鸟已经准备好离开鸟巢了。
The company is hiring fledglings to train in their new program.  这家公司正在招聘新手来培训他们的新项目。
synonyms:  beginner, novice, trainee, rookie, apprentice
antonyms: expert, veteran, professional, master
