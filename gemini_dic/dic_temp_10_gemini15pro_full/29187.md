cashierer:/kæˈʃɪərər/ | n. | 收银员，出纳员 | A person who handles cash transactions in a store, bank, or other business. |  The cashierer scanned the groceries and told me the total.  收银员扫描了杂货，并告诉我总价。| Synonyms: teller, clerk, checkout operator | Antonyms: customer, client | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *caissier*, from caisse ("chest, box"), from Latin capsa. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *kap- (“to grab, hold”).
