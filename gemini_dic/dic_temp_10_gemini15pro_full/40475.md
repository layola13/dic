contribution:/ˌkɒntrɪˈbjuːʃn/ /ˌkɑːntrɪˈbjuːʃn/| n. |贡献，捐款，捐赠；稿件| something that you contribute or do to help produce or achieve something with other people;  an amount of money that you give in order to help pay for something |例句：His contribution to the project was invaluable. 他对这个项目的贡献是不可估量的。|近义词：donation, gift, offering, input |反义词:detriment, hindrance |词性:名词

词源: From Latin *contributus*, past participle of contribuere ("to bring together, contribute"), from con- ("together") + tribuere ("to grant, bestow").
