catafalque: /ˈkætəfɔːlk/ | n. | 棺架，灵柩台 | A decorated platform, often raised, on which a coffin is placed for a funeral service or lying-in-state. | The king's coffin rested on a magnificent catafalque in the cathedral. 国王的棺材停放在大教堂里一个华丽的棺架上。

The catafalque was draped in black velvet. 棺架上覆盖着黑色天鹅绒。 | 近义词: bier, hearse | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
