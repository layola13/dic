mousquetaire:/ˌmʌskɪˈtɛər/| n.|火枪手；火枪队士兵；（旧时）佩剑者；高筒女靴|A soldier armed with a musket, especially a member of the former French royal household troops.|例句：The Three Musketeers is a classic adventure novel.  《三个火枪手》是一部经典的冒险小说。

例句：She wore a pair of stylish mousquetaire boots. 她穿着一双时髦的高筒女靴。|近义词：guardsman, soldier, trooper|反义词:civilian|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *mousquetaire*, from *mousquet* ("musket"). Ultimately from Italian *moschetto* ("small hawk").
