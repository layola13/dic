mailing's:/ˈmeɪlɪŋz/
noun
邮件，邮寄, 邮寄物
The possessive form of "mailing," referring to something that belongs to or is associated with a mailing.  It can refer to the content, the process, or the materials related to a mailing.
Example: The mailing's contents were damaged in transit.  邮件的内容在运输过程中损坏了。
Example: The mailing's success depended on the accuracy of the address list.  邮件投递的成功取决于地址列表的准确性。
Synonyms:  mail, correspondence, post
Antonyms:  receiving, delivery (These are antonyms in the sense of the opposite action, not the opposite of the word itself.)
