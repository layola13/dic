paraoxon:/ˈpærəɒksɒn/ | n. | 对氧磷 |A highly toxic organophosphate insecticide and acaricide. It is the active metabolite of parathion. | Paraoxon is much more toxic than parathion. 对氧磷比对硫磷毒性更大。|  Synonyms: diethyl 4-nitrophenyl phosphate (IUPAC name) | Antonyms: None (lack of direct antonymous chemical compound) | Noun
