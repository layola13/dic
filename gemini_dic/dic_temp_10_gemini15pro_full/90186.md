hotspur:/ˈhɒtspɜːr/ (UK) /ˈhɑːtspɜːr/ (US)| n.|性急的人；暴躁的人|A person who is hot-tempered and impetuous.|Harry Hotspur was known for his fiery temper and impulsive actions. 哈利·霍茨珀以其暴躁的脾气和冲动的行为而闻名。|例句：He acted like a hotspur, rushing into battle without a plan. 他表现得像个莽撞鬼，不假思索地冲进战场。|近义词：firebrand, maverick, daredevil|反义词：pacifist, peacemaker|词性:名词

hotspur:| adj.|性急的；暴躁的|Impetuous; hot-tempered.|His hotspur nature often led him into trouble. 他暴躁的性格经常使他陷入困境。|例句：She gave him a hotspur reply, which she immediately regretted.  她冲动地回答了他，随即就后悔了。|近义词：fiery, impetuous, rash|反义词：calm, patient, level-headed|词性:形容词

词源: From "hot" (meaning heated, passionate) + "spur" (a device used to urge a horse forward).  由“hot”（意为热的，充满激情的）和“spur”（一种用来催促马前进的装置）组成。最初指用马刺策马狂奔的人，后来引申为指性情暴躁，容易冲动的人。
