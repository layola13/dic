dandie: /ˈdændi/ | n. | 矮腿长身梗, 花花公子 | A small long-bodied terrier with short legs, or a man unduly devoted to style and fashion. |  The dandie dinmont terrier is a unique breed. 矮腿长身梗是一种独特的品种。 He was quite the dandie in his youth, always impeccably dressed. 他年轻时是个十足的花花公子，总是衣着 impeccable。 | 近义词：fop, dandy, dude, coxcomb | 反义词：sloven, slob | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From the character "Dandie Dinmont" in Sir Walter Scott's novel *Guy Mannering* (1815), who owned terriers of this breed.
