enshelter:/ɪnˈʃɛltər/| v. n.|庇护，保护；躲避|To provide shelter or protection for someone or something; to take shelter or find refuge.|The organization enshelters homeless families during the winter.  这个组织在冬天为无家可归的家庭提供庇护。|The hikers ensheltered themselves in a cave during the storm.  徒步旅行者在暴风雨中躲在一个山洞里。|近义词：shelter, protect, harbor, house|反义词：expose, endanger|词性:动词, 名词

Etymology:
From en- + shelter.  "En-" is a prefix meaning "to put into or onto," thus "enshelter" literally means "to put into shelter."
