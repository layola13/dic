baste:/beɪst/| v.|涂油；狠揍；粗缝；公开责骂|To moisten (meat) with pan drippings, butter, or other fat during cooking; to sew with long, loose stitches; to criticize harshly.|例句：She basted the turkey with butter. 她用黄油给火鸡涂油。

|近义词：sear, roast, grill (for cooking); tack, stitch (for sewing); criticize, lambaste (for criticizing)|反义词: praise, commend (for criticizing)|词性:动词
baster:/ˈbeɪstər/| n.|烤肉刷；私生子；粗缝针|A tool used to baste meat; an illegitimate child; a needle used for basting.|例句：She used a baster to keep the chicken moist. 她用烤肉刷保持鸡肉湿润。

|近义词：brush (for cooking); bastard (for illegitimate child)|反义词: legitimate child (for illegitimate child)|词性:名词
