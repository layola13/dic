corinthianesque: /ˌkɔːrɪnˈθiːənɛsk/ | adj. | 科林斯式的；华丽的；奢华的 |  Resembling or characteristic of the elaborate Corinthian order of classical architecture, often used figuratively to describe something ornate or luxurious. | The hotel lobby was corinthianesque in its grandeur, with marble pillars and gilded chandeliers.  酒店大堂富丽堂皇，科林斯式风格，有大理石柱子和镀金吊灯。 | The new opera house is a corinthianesque masterpiece.  新的歌剧院是一座科林斯式的杰作。 | 近义词: ornate, elaborate, luxurious, opulent | 反义词: simple, plain, austere | 词性: adjective | 词源: From Corinthian + -esque, ultimately from Corinth (Κόρινθος), a city in ancient Greece.
