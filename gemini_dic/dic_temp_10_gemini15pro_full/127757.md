negritude:/ˈnɛɡrɪtjuːd/ /ˈnɛɡrɪtuːd/| n.|黑人性; 黑人文化|The concept of negritude emphasizes the cultural identity and pride of people of African descent.  黑人性概念强调非洲裔人的文化认同和自豪感。|Negritude is a literary and ideological movement that celebrates Black consciousness and culture. 黑人性是一种文学和意识形态运动，它颂扬黑人意识和文化。|近义词:Black consciousness, Afrocentrism|反义词:Eurocentrism|词性:noun

Etymology:
From French négritude, from nègre (“Negro”) + -itude.  Ultimately from Latin niger (“black”). 源自法语 négritude，由 nègre（“黑人”）+ -itude 组成。最终源自拉丁语 niger（“黑色”）。
