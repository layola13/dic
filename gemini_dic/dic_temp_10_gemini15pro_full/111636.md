localizes:/ˈloʊkəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
本地化；局限；确定…的位置
restricts something to a particular place.
The infection is localized in the throat. 感染局限于喉咙。
localize:/ˈloʊkəˌlaɪz/
verb
本地化；局限；确定…的位置
To adapt something to a local environment or market, particularly in terms of language and culture.
The company decided to localize its products for the Asian market.  公司决定将其产品本地化以适应亚洲市场。
synonym: adapt, adjust, modify, tailor, pinpoint, situate, confine, restrict
antonym: globalize, internationalize, spread, disperse
