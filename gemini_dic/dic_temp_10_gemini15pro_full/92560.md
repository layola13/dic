hysterical:/hɪˈstɛrɪkəl/
adjective
歇斯底里的，情绪异常激动的
Marked by excessive or uncontrollable emotion, such as fear or laughter.
She burst into hysterical laughter when she heard the news. 她听到这个消息时，爆发出歇斯底里的笑声。
近义词: frantic, frenzied, uncontrolled
反义词: calm, composed, serene
词性: adjective

hysterical:/hɪˈstɛrɪkəl/
noun
歇斯底里的人; (尤指) 歇斯底里的女人
a person, especially a woman, who is prone to hysteria
The nurse tried to calm the hysterical.  护士试图让这个歇斯底里的人平静下来。
近义词: drama queen
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
