infinitesimal: /ˌɪnfɪnɪˈtɛsɪməl/ | adj. n. | 极小的；无限小的；无穷小；无穷小量 | Extremely small; too small to be measured |  The effect on sales was infinitesimal. 对销售额的影响微乎其微。 | synonyms: minute, tiny, microscopic, negligible | antonyms: huge, enormous, vast, immense | adjective

infinitesimal: /ˌɪnfɪnɪˈtɛsɪməl/ | n. | 无穷小；无穷小量 | An indefinitely small quantity; a value approaching zero |  Calculus deals with infinitesimals. 微积分处理无穷小量。 | synonyms:  differential | antonyms: infinity | noun
