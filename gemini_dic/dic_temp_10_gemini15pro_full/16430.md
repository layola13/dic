bare-off:/ˈbeər ɒf/| v.| 拿掉棋子; 离场; (航海)偏离|To remove all of one's checkers from the board in backgammon, thereby winning the game. Also, to steer a vessel further from the wind. |Example 1: He bare-offed all his checkers and won the game. 他拿掉了所有的棋子并赢得了比赛。

Example 2: The captain ordered the helmsman to bare off. 船长命令舵手偏离航线。|近义词：remove, clear, depart|反义词:enter, approach|词性:动词
