furzeling: /ˈfɜːzəlɪŋ/ | n. | 小玩意儿；小装饰；小摆设 | A small, often decorative, object of little value or use; a trinket. |  She covered her shelves with furzelings she'd collected on her travels. 她把她旅行时收集的小玩意儿摆满了架子。

|  The shop sold all sorts of furzelings and knick-knacks. 这家商店出售各种各样的小玩意儿和小摆设。 | 近义词: trinket, knick-knack, bauble, gewgaw, gimcrack | 反义词: necessity, essential | 词性: 名词


词源:  Likely a playful coinage, possibly related to German "Fusel" (bad liquor) suggesting something frivolous or of little value.  The ending "-ing" might suggest a diminutive or a process, adding to the sense of something small and insignificant being created or collected.
