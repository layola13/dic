forehand:/ˈfɔːrˌhænd/| n. adj. adv.|正手击球；正手的；用正手的|A stroke in tennis and other racket sports made with the palm of the hand facing in the direction of the stroke.  (在网球和其他球拍运动中，手掌朝向击球方向的击球方式)|He hit a powerful forehand down the line. 他沿着边线打了一个强有力的正手球。|Synonyms: front hand, overhand|Antonyms: backhand|noun

forehand:/ˈfɔːrˌhænd/| adj.|正手的|Made or done with the palm of the hand facing forward. (用手掌向前做的或完成的)|His forehand volley was a thing of beauty. 他的正手截击非常漂亮。|Synonyms: front-handed|Antonyms: backhand|adjective

forehand:/ˈfɔːrˌhænd/| adv.|用正手|With the palm of the hand facing forward. (手掌向前)|He returned the serve forehand. 他用正手回击了发球。|Synonyms: front-handedly|Antonyms: backhand|adverb
