consonance:/ˈkɒnsənəns/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsənəns/ (US)| n.|一致，和谐，调和；辅音韵|Agreement or compatibility between opinions or actions.  The repetition of consonant sounds, especially at the ends of words, as in blank and think.|The consonance between the two leaders on the issue of trade was surprising.  两位领导人在贸易问题上意见一致，令人惊讶。|近义词：harmony, agreement, accord, concord, unity|反义词：dissonance, discord, disagreement, conflict|noun

consonances:/ˈkɒnsənənsɪz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsənənsɪz/ (US)| n.|一致，和谐，调和；辅音韵（复数）|The plural form of consonance, referring to multiple instances of agreement or compatibility, or multiple instances of consonant sound repetition.|The consonances between their political platforms were few and far between.  他们的政治纲领之间很少有一致之处。|近义词：harmonies, agreements, accords, concords|反义词：dissonances, discords, disagreements, conflicts|noun
