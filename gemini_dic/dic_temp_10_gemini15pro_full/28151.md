capitulum:/kəˈpɪtʃʊləm/| n. |小头状花序; 头状花; 骨小头|A dense cluster of sessile flowers or foliage, as in a composite plant or the head of a rib.  A small head or rounded extremity of a bone or body part.|The capitulum of the dandelion is bright yellow. 蒲公英的头状花序是亮黄色的。|Synonym: head, cluster|Antonym: base, stem|noun


capitulum:/kəˈpɪtʃʊləm/| n. |章; 回; 篇|A chapter or section of a book or other writing.|The second capitulum of the book deals with the history of the region. 书的第二章讲述了该地区的历史。|Synonym: chapter, section|Antonym: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin capitulum, diminutive of caput (“head”).
