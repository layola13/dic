dreary:/ˈdrɪəri/| adj.|沉闷的，阴郁的，单调乏味的|The weather has been dreary for days.  天气已经阴沉好几天了。|Synonyms: gloomy, dull, bleak|Antonyms: bright, cheerful, lively|adjective


Etymology:
From Middle English *dreri, from Old English *drēorig "sad, sorrowful, grieved; bloody, gory," from Proto-Germanic *drauziją "mournful."  Related to Old English dreor "gore, blood."  The meaning shifted from "sorrowful" to "gloomy" and then to "dull, tedious."
