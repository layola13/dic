hairen:/ˈheɪrən/ | n. | 海人; 采珠人; 潜水者 | A person who dives for pearls or other valuable objects in the sea.  This word seems to be a transliteration of the Japanese word "海人" (ama) which refers to female free divers, traditionally known for collecting pearls and seaweed. |  例句：The hairen surfaced with a handful of pearls. 海人浮出水面，手里拿着几颗珍珠。

例句：The hairen dived deep to collect seaweed. 海人潜入深海采集海藻。| 近义词：diver, pearl diver, free diver | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词
