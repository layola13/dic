gules:/ˈɡjuːlz/| adj. n.|红色，朱红色|Of the colour gules (red) in heraldry.|The shield was gules, charged with a silver lion rampant.|近义词：red, crimson, scarlet|反义词:azure, vert|词性:形容词,名词
Etymology: From Old French *goules, plural of goule "throat, gullet", from Latin gula.  The connection may be the red color of the throat, or from the red dyes originally imported from the East via the Gulf of Aqaba (referred to as the *Gula*).
