nonegocentric:/ˌnɒnˌiːɡoʊˈsɛntrɪk/ | adj. | 非自我中心的；不以自我为中心的 | Not egocentric; considering the needs and perspectives of others.  |  Example: Her nonegocentric approach to problem-solving made her a valuable team member. 她不以自我为中心的问题解决方法使她成为一名宝贵的团队成员。 | Example:  He took a nonegocentric view of the situation, considering how his actions would affect everyone involved. 他对这种情况采取了非自我中心的观点，考虑了他的行为将如何影响所有相关人员。 | Synonyms: altruistic, selfless, considerate, empathetic | Antonyms: egocentric, selfish, self-centered, narcissistic | adjective
