consisted:/kənˈsɪstɪd/
verb
由…组成；由…构成；在于；存在于
be composed or made up of.
The team consisted of four doctors and six nurses.  这个团队由四名医生和六名护士组成。
The problem consisted in the lack of communication. 问题在于缺乏沟通。
Synonyms: comprise, be composed of, be made up of
Antonyms: differ, vary
词性: 动词
