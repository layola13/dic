chunk-out:/tʃʌŋkˈaʊt/ | phrasal verb | 大块切出；大量生产；快速完成 | To cut or remove a large piece or portion of something; to produce something quickly and in large quantities. |  He chunked out a large piece of the wood with his axe. 他用斧头砍下了一大块木头。

The factory chunked out thousands of cars each week. 这家工厂每周生产数千辆汽车。 | 近义词: mass-produce, churn out, produce quickly | 反义词: craft, meticulously create | 词性: 短语动词

chunk-out:/tʃʌŋkˈaʊt/ | n. | 大块; 大量生产的物品 | A large piece of something; something produced quickly and in large quantities. |  He gave me a chunk-out of the profits. 他给了我很大一部分利润。

The latest chunk-out from the factory is a new kind of electric car. 工厂最新的大量生产产品是一种新型电动汽车。 | 近义词: chunk, portion, output | 反义词: sliver, bit, trifle | 词性: 名词
