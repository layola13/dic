hallowmas:/ˈhæləʊmæs/ (US) /ˈhæləʊmæs/ (UK)| n.|万圣节前夕; 诸圣节;|A Christian festival, also known as All Hallows' Eve, celebrated on the night of October 31st.  It is the eve of All Saints' Day (All Hallows' Day).|例句：Children often dress up in costumes on Hallowmas.  孩子们经常在万圣节前夕穿上戏服。

例句：Hallowmas is a time for spooky stories and trick-or-treating. 万圣节前夕是讲鬼故事和“不给糖就捣蛋”的时候。|近义词：All Hallows' Eve, Halloween|反义词: None|词性:noun
