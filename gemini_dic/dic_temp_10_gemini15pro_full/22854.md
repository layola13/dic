bosun:/ˈboʊsən/ (US), /ˈbəʊsən/ (UK)| n. | 水手长；甲板长 | A ship's officer in charge of equipment and the crew who work on the deck. | The bosun ordered the crew to raise the anchor. 水手长命令船员起锚。|  Example 2: The bosun was responsible for maintaining the ship's rigging. 水手长负责维护船上的索具。 | synonyms: boatswain, deck officer | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a job title) | noun
