hymnology:/hɪmˈnɒlədʒi/ | n. |赞美诗学；赞美诗集|The study of hymns, their origin, use, and significance.  It can also refer to a collection of hymns.|例句：Her research in hymnology led her to discover a forgotten composer. 她的赞美诗学研究使她发现了一位被遗忘的作曲家。

例句：The church's hymnology reflected the changing social values of the time.  教堂的赞美诗集反映了当时不断变化的社会价值观。|近义词：hymnody|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
