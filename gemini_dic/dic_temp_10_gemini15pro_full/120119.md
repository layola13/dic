mimicry: /ˈmɪmɪkri/ | n. | 模仿; 拟态 | The act of imitating someone or something, typically in order to entertain or deceive.  The viceroy butterfly uses mimicry to protect itself from predators. | Example 1: The comedian's mimicry of the president was hilarious.  这个喜剧演员对总统的模仿滑稽极了。

Example 2: The insect's mimicry of a leaf was so perfect that it was almost invisible to birds.  这只昆虫对叶子的模仿如此完美，以至于鸟类几乎看不见它。 | Synonyms: imitation, impersonation, simulation, emulation | Antonyms: originality, authenticity | noun
