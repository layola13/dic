disir:/ˈdiːsɪr/| n. | 女神; 女精灵 (复数) | Norse mythology, female guardian spirits, somewhat like Valkyries but less directly associated with warfare. They could be benevolent or malevolent.  They are often associated with fate and prophecy. | The disir watched over the family and guided their destinies. 女精灵守护着这个家庭，指引着他们的命运。 | Near synonyms: Valkyrie, Norns, Fylgja | Near antonyms:  Demon | Noun
