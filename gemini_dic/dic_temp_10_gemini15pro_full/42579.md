counterplayers:/ˈkaʊntərˌpleɪərz/
noun
对手，竞争者
Those two chess counterplayers are highly skilled. 那两位棋手都是高手。
Counterplayers in the market are vying for the same customers.  市场上的竞争对手都在争夺相同的客户。
Synonyms: rivals, competitors, opponents, adversaries
Antonyms: allies, partners, collaborators
