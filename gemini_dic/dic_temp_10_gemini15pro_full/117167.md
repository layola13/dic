meanwhiles:/ˈmiːnwaɪlz/| adv.|与此同时，在此期间|Meanwhile, back at the ranch, the horses were getting restless. 与此同时，在牧场，马儿们变得焦躁不安。|例句：The storm raged outside; meanwhile, inside, the family played board games.  外面暴风雨肆虐；与此同时，在屋内，一家人玩着棋盘游戏。|近义词：meantime, in the meantime, at the same time, simultaneously|反义词:afterwards, subsequently|词性:副词

词源: From Middle English *menewhile, equivalent to mean + while.  Mean in this context is an archaic word meaning "intermediate, intervening."
