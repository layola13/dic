limited-companies:/ˌlɪmɪtɪd ˈkʌmpəniz/| n.| 有限公司 |A private company with limited liability for its shareholders.  A limited company's owners are legally distinct from the business. | Many small businesses operate as limited companies. 许多小企业以有限公司的形式运营。 | Synonyms: Ltd., corporation, incorporated company | Antonyms: sole proprietorship, partnership | noun
