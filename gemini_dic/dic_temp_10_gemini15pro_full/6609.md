anacoluthon:/ˌænəkəˈluːθɒn/ (UK), /ˌænəkəˈluːθɑːn/ (US)| n. | 语法中断，句法不连贯 | A sudden break in a sentence's grammatical structure, leaving it incomplete or illogical.  It occurs when the speaker or writer abruptly shifts from one construction to another without completing the first. | I met this morning—I mean, yesterday, I saw her. 我今天早上见到——我的意思是，昨天，我见到她了。| Example: While walking to the store – oh look, a cute dog!  在去商店的路上——哦，看，一只可爱的狗！ |Synonyms:  syntactic discontinuity, grammatical interruption, sentence fragment (when the first part is left entirely dangling), derailment| Antonyms:  syntactic coherence, grammatical consistency, fluency | noun
