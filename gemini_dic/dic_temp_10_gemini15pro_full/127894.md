neogrammarian:/ˌniːoʊˈɡræməriən/ | n. |新语法学家|A scholar of the Neogrammarian school of linguistics, which emphasized the regularity of sound change in language. | The neogrammarians revolutionized the study of historical linguistics. 新语法学家彻底改变了历史语言学的研究。| The neogrammarians posited that sound laws operate without exception. 新语法学家假设语音规律的运作没有例外。|Synonyms: Young Grammarian |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "traditional grammarian") | noun
