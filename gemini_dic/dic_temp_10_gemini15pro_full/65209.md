etical:/ˈɛθɪkəl/| adj.|伦理的，道德的|Relating to moral principles or the branch of knowledge dealing with these.|例句：The ethical implications of this research are significant. 这项研究的伦理意义重大。|例句：It's not ethical to lie to your customers. 对你的客户撒谎是不道德的。|近义词：moral, principled, righteous|反义词：unethical, immoral, unscrupulous|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *ethicus, from Greek ēthikós, from ēthos ‘character, custom’.
