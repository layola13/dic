overbrim:/ˌoʊvərˈbrɪm/ | vi. vt.| 溢出，充满 | To fill or be filled beyond the brim; overflow. | The cup overbrimmed with milk. 杯子里的牛奶溢出来了。|  The river overbrimmed its banks. 河水漫过了河岸。| 近义词：overflow, brim over, run over, spill over | 反义词: drain, empty | 动词

overbrim:/ˌoʊvərˈbrɪm/ | adj.| 盈满的，洋溢的 | Filled to overflowing; brimming. |  She was overbrim with joy. 她心中充满了喜悦。| His heart was overbrim with gratitude. 他的内心充满了感激。| 近义词：brimming, overflowing, full | 反义词: empty, lacking | 形容词

词源:

From over- + brim.  "Over-" signifies "across," "above," or "excessive," while "brim" refers to the top edge of a container.  Thus, "overbrim" literally means to go over the brim.
