belemnite:/ˈbɛləmˌnaɪt/| n.|箭石|An extinct cephalopod mollusk of the order Belemnitida, which existed during the Jurassic and Cretaceous periods, having a squid-like body and an internal, cone-shaped calcareous shell called the guard.  These guards are often fossilized.|Belemnites are common fossils found in Jurassic and Cretaceous rocks.  箭石是侏罗纪和白垩纪岩石中常见的化石。|近义词:cephalopod, fossil|反义词: (No direct antonym as it's an extinct organism) |词性:noun
