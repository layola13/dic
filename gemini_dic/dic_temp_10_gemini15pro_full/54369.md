dishrag:/ˈdɪʃræɡ/ | n. |抹布；洗碗布|A cloth used for washing dishes.|She wrung out the dirty dishrag and threw it in the sink. 她拧干脏抹布，扔进了水池里。|I use a dishrag to wipe down the kitchen counters after cooking. 我用抹布擦拭做饭后的厨房台面。| 近义词：dishcloth, tea towel, washcloth | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑干净的新抹布 a clean, new dishcloth) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From dish + rag.  "Dish" refers to dishes, and "rag" refers to a piece of cloth, often old or torn.  The word simply describes a rag used for dishes.
