nonauthoritatively:/ˌnɒnɔːˈθɒrɪtətɪvli/ /ˌnɑːnəˈθɔːrəteɪtivli/
adverb|非权威地，非正式地|In a way that does not claim to be authoritative or definitive; informally.|He spoke nonauthoritatively on the subject, admitting he was not an expert.|informally, unofficially, casually|authoritatively, officially, formally
词性:副词
