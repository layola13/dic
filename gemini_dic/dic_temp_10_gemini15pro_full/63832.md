epigeal:/ˌepɪˈdʒiːəl/
adjective
地上生的，子叶出土的
Growing or occurring above the ground; (of cotyledons) appearing above ground during germination as opposed to remaining below ground.
The bean plant is epigeal, its cotyledons rising above the ground during germination.  豆类植物是地上生的，它的子叶在萌发过程中会露出地面。
The seedlings exhibited epigeal germination. 这些幼苗表现出地上萌发。
synonyms: aboveground
antonyms: hypogeal
词性: adjective
