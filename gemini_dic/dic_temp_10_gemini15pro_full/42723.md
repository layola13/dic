counterweigh:/ˌkaʊntərˈweɪ/| vt.|平衡；抵消；抗衡|To act against with equal weight or force; offset.|The advantages counterweigh the disadvantages. 优点抵消了缺点。|Synonyms: offset, balance, compensate, neutralize|Antonyms: enhance, aggravate, exacerbate|verb

counterweigh:/ˈkaʊntərˌweɪ/| n.|平衡物；抗衡力|Something that counterbalances something else; an opposing weight or force.|The government's spending cuts acted as a counterweigh to inflation. 政府削减开支对通货膨胀起到抗衡作用。|Synonyms: counterbalance, offset, balance|Antonyms: supplement, addition|noun
