espn:/iː-ɛs-piː-ɛn/| n. |娱乐与体育节目电视网|Entertainment and Sports Programming Network. A cable and satellite television channel dedicated to broadcasting sports news, events, and analysis.|I watched the NBA game on ESPN last night. 我昨晚在ESPN上看了NBA比赛。|Synonyms: sports channel, sports network|Antonyms: N/A|noun
