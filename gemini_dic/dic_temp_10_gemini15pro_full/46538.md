ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *dæl (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *dalą (“valley”), from Proto-Indo-European *dʰolh₁- (“valley, hollow”). Cognate with Old Saxon dal (“valley”), Old Frisian dele, Old High German tal (“valley”), Old Norse dalr (“valley”), Gothic 𐌳𐌰𐌻𐍃 (dals, “valley”).
