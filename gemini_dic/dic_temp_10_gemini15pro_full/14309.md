automate-out:/ˈɔːtəˌmeɪt aʊt/ | v. | 自动化输出; 自动化淘汰 | To automate a process so that it produces output without manual intervention, or to automate a process that removes or eliminates something.  | Example 1: The system automates-out reports every morning. 系统每天早上自动生成报告。

Example 2:  The new software automates-out inefficient employees. 新软件自动淘汰了效率低下的员工。 | Synonyms: automate, generate, produce, eliminate, phase out | Antonyms: manual, input, retain | verb
