idumean:/ˌaɪˈdjuːˌmiːn/ | interj. | 我的意思是；我是说 |  Used to clarify or correct a previous statement.  | I said I liked the blue one, Idumean, the darker blue one. 我说我喜欢蓝色的那个，我的意思是，深蓝色的那个。 | synonyms: I mean, that is to say, in other words, rather, to be precise | antonyms: N/A | interjection
