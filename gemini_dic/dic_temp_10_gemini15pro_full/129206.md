noblesse:/noʊˈblɛs/ | n. | 高贵，贵族气质，贵族阶级 | The quality of being noble in character, mind, or rank.  |  She carried herself with a quiet noblesse. 她举止优雅高贵。 | synonyms: nobility, aristocracy, dignity, grandeur | antonyms: baseness, vulgarity, commonness | noun


noblesse oblige:/noʊˈblɛs əˈbliːʒ/ | n. | 贵族义务，地位高则责任重 | The inferred responsibility of privileged people to act with generosity and nobility toward those less privileged. |  Noblesse oblige requires that we offer our assistance.  地位高则责任重，我们应该提供帮助。 | synonyms: social responsibility, duty, moral obligation | antonyms:  selfishness, irresponsibility | noun
