antidomestically:/ˌæntiˌdəˈmɛstɪkli/
| adv. | 反家庭式地，反对家庭生活地 |  Acting or existing in a way that opposes or rejects traditional domestic values or lifestyles. |  She lived antidomestically, prioritizing her career over marriage and children. 她反家庭式地生活，将事业置于婚姻和孩子之上。|  unconventionally, non-traditionally | domestically, traditionally | adverb

antidomestic:/ˌænti dəˈmɛstɪk/
