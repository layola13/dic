antiodontalgic: /ˌæntiˌoʊdɑːnˈtældʒɪk/
antiodontalgic: adj.
antiodontalgic: 止牙痛的
antiodontalgic: Relieving toothache.  Formed from anti- (against) + odontalgia (toothache), which itself comes from Greek odous (tooth) + algos (pain).
antiodontalgic: The dentist prescribed an antiodontalgic balm to soothe the patient's throbbing toothache.  牙医给病人开了一种止痛膏来缓解他剧烈的牙痛。
antiodontalgic: This medication has antiodontalgic properties and should provide relief within minutes. 这种药物具有止牙痛的特性，应该在几分钟内就能缓解疼痛。
antiodontalgic: analgesic, pain-relieving
antiodontalgic: pain-inducing
antiodontalgic: adjective
