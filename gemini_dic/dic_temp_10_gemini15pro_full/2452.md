adjure:/əˈdʒʊr/| vt.|恳求；命令；要求|To solemnly urge or command someone to do something, often under oath or threat of penalty.|The judge adjured the witness to tell the truth. 法官要求证人说出真相。

I adjure you to reconsider your decision. 我恳求你重新考虑你的决定。|近义词： beseech, implore, entreat, urge, command|反义词: allow, permit|词性:动词


USAGE->
一般现在时:
I adjure you to tell the truth. = 我要求你说实话。
They adjure him to remain silent. = 他们要求他保持沉默。

一般过去时:
He adjured his followers to remain loyal. = 他要求他的追随者保持忠诚。
The king adjured his knights to defend the realm. = 国王命令他的骑士保卫王国。

现在完成时:
The court has adjured the witness to cooperate. = 法庭已责令证人合作。
I have adjured him to reconsider his actions. = 我已经劝告他重新考虑他的行为。

过去完成时:
She had adjured him to leave before the police arrived. = 她在他到达之前就劝他离开了。
They had adjured her to keep the secret. = 他们之前就要求她保守秘密。

将来时:
I will adjure him to stop spreading rumors. = 我会要求他停止散布谣言。
The judge will adjure the jury to consider all the evidence. = 法官将要求陪审团考虑所有证据。

虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would adjure him to apologize. = 如果我是你，我会要求他道歉。
I wish he would adjure his supporters to remain peaceful. = 我希望他会劝他的支持者保持和平。

被动语态:
He was adjured to tell the truth by the judge. = 法官要求他说实话。
The witnesses were adjured to remain silent until called upon. = 证人被要求保持沉默，直到被传唤。

疑问句:
Why did you adjure him to leave? = 你为什么要求他离开？
Will you adjure them to reconsider their decision? = 你会要求他们重新考虑他们的决定吗？

否定句:
I did not adjure him to do anything illegal. = 我没有要求他做任何违法的事情。
She will not adjure them to break the law. = 她不会要求他们违反法律。


PHRASE->
adjure someone to do something = 要求某人做某事
adjure someone by something = 以...的名义要求某人
adjure someone on oath = 宣誓要求某人
adjure someone to secrecy = 要求某人保密
adjure someone to silence = 要求某人保持沉默
