divinities:/dɪˈvɪnɪtiz/
n.
神性; 神圣; 神;
The state or quality of being divine, or a divine being; a god or goddess.
Ancient Egyptians believed in a complex system of divinities. 古埃及人信仰一个复杂的神系。
synonyms: gods, goddesses, deities, immortals, celestials
antonyms: mortals, humans


divinities:/dɪˈvɪnɪtiz/
n.
神学; 神性论;
Theology; the study of divine things.
He studied the divinities of ancient Greece. 他研究古希腊神学。
synonyms: theology, religion, divine studies
antonyms: atheism, secularism
