coronium:/kəˈroʊniəm/
noun
日冕元素（一种假设的元素，曾被认为是日冕光谱中某些不明谱线的来源）
A hypothetical element once thought to exist in the Sun's corona and to be responsible for certain unidentified spectral lines.
Scientists once believed coronium was a new element, but it was later determined to be highly ionized iron. 科学家曾认为日冕元素是一种新元素，但后来确定它是高度电离的铁。
Synonyms: None (as it's a disproven element)
Antonyms: None
