dankish:/ˈdæŋkɪʃ/
adjective
潮湿的，阴冷的
Slightly damp and chilly; unpleasantly humid.
The basement had a dankish smell, like mildew and old earth.  地下室有一股潮湿的气味，像是霉菌和陈土的味道。
The air in the cave was dankish and cold.  洞穴里的空气潮湿阴冷。
近义词:damp, clammy, humid
反义词:dry, arid
词性:adjective
