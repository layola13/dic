ouroscopy:/ˌjʊərˈɒskəpi/ /ˌʊrˈɑːskəpi/ | n. | 尿液检查 | Examination of urine, typically by visual inspection or chemical analysis, for diagnostic purposes. | Example 1: The doctor ordered an ouroscopy to check for kidney problems. 医生要求进行尿液检查以检查肾脏问题。

Example 2: Ouroscopy can reveal a lot about a patient's health. 尿液检查可以揭示很多关于病人健康的信息。 | Synonym: urinalysis | Antonym:  (No direct antonym) | noun
