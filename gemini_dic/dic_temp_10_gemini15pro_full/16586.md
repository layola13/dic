词源:  "barnstorm" 最初指的是 itinerant theatrical troupes performing in barns, often in rural areas. The term later broadened to include political campaigning in a similar style, and eventually came to describe anything energetic and exciting.  "barnstorming" 最初指的是在谷仓（通常在农村地区）演出的巡回剧团。这个词后来扩展到包括类似风格的政治竞选活动，最终用来描述任何充满活力和令人兴奋的事情。
