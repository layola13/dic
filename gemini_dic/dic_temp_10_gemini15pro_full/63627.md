eocene:/ˈiːəsiːn/ | n. adj.| 始新世 |  Relating to or denoting the second epoch of the Tertiary period, between the Paleocene and Oligocene epochs. | The Eocene epoch saw the diversification of many mammal groups. 始新世见证了许多哺乳动物群体的多样化。|  The Eocene climate was much warmer than today's. 始新世的气候比今天温暖得多。| 近义词: Early Tertiary | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: 名词，形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *Éocène*, from Greek *ēṓs* (“dawn”) + *kainós* (“new, recent”).  Referencing the "dawn" of recent life.
