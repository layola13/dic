forgivingness:/fərˈɡɪvnəs/| n. |宽恕，原谅|The quality of being forgiving; readiness to stop feeling angry or resentful towards someone for an offense, flaw, or mistake.|She showed great forgivingness towards her husband after his infidelity. 她对丈夫不忠的行为表现出了极大的宽恕。|Synonyms: mercy, clemency, pardon, absolution|Antonyms: resentment, rancor, bitterness, unforgivingness|Noun
