nonexploitive:/ˌnɒnɪkˈsplɔɪtətɪv/
adjective
非剥削性的
Not exploiting others, especially not making profit from their work unfairly.
例句：They promote nonexploitive trade practices. 他们提倡非剥削性的贸易行为。

例句：The company prides itself on its nonexploitive labor practices.  这家公司以其非剥削性的劳工实践为荣。

近义词：ethical, fair, just, equitable
反义词：exploitative, abusive, predatory
词性: adjective
