nonreducibilities:/ˌnɒnˌrɪˌdjuːsɪˈbɪlɪtiz/ | n. | 不可约性(复数) | The state or quality of being nonreducible; the inability to be reduced or simplified further.  It often appears in mathematical or computational contexts, referring to problems or expressions that cannot be simplified to a more basic form. | The nonreducibilities of certain computational problems make them challenging to solve efficiently. 某些计算问题的不可约性使得难以有效地解决它们。|  irreducibility, incompressibility | reducibility, simplicity | noun
