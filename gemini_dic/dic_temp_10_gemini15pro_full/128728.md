Niger River:/ˈnaɪdʒər ˈrɪvər/ | n. | 尼日尔河 | A major river in West Africa, the third longest in Africa, extending about 4,180 km (2,600 mi). It rises in the Guinea Highlands of southeastern Guinea and flows northeast through Mali and Niger, then southeast through Nigeria to its delta in the Gulf of Guinea. | The Niger River is a vital source of water for agriculture in the region. 尼日尔河是该地区农业的重要水源。| The Niger River delta is a biodiversity hotspot. 尼日尔河三角洲是生物多样性热点地区。 |Synonyms:  River Niger | Antonyms: N/A | noun
