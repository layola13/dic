antae:/ˈæntiː/
adjective
前面的；在前的
situated in front, or at the front part
The antae of the temple were adorned with carvings.  寺庙的前柱装饰着雕刻。
近义词: anterior, frontal
反义词: posterior, rear
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *antae, plural of *anta "front pillar, pilaster," perhaps related to ante "before, in front of."
