daisy-like:/ˈdeɪziˌlaɪk/
adj.
像雏菊的, 似雏菊的
Resembling a daisy in form or appearance.
The small, daisy-like flowers dotted the meadow.  小而像雏菊的花朵点缀着草地。
The plant had daisy-like petals.  这种植物有类似雏菊的花瓣。
近义词:daisy-shaped, flower-like
反义词: (No clear antonym, perhaps something like "unflowerlike")
词性: adjective
