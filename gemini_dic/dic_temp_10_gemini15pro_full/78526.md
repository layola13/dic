ghole:/ɡoʊl/| n.|洞穴，地洞；（尤指）老鼠洞；偏僻处；隐蔽处|A ghole is a burrow or hole in the ground, especially one made by a small animal such as a rat.|The rat disappeared into its ghole. 老鼠消失在它的洞穴里。|近义词：burrow, hole, den, lair|反义词: mound, hill|词性:名词

Etymology: From Marathi घोळ (ghoḷ) meaning "hole", "burrow".
