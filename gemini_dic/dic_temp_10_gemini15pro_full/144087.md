outsail:/ˌaʊtˈseɪl/
verb
胜过；比…航行得快
to sail faster than another ship or boat
The newer yacht easily outsailed the older vessels.  这艘新游艇轻松地超过了那些旧船。

outsail:/ˌaʊtˈseɪl/
verb

to be better at sailing than someone else
