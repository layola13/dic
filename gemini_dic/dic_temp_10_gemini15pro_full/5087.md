allantois:/əˈlæntɔɪs/| n.| 尿囊|A membranous sac that develops from the posterior part of the alimentary canal in embryos of reptiles, birds, and mammals. In mammals, it forms part of the placenta.|The allantois plays a crucial role in the development of the embryo. 尿囊在胚胎发育中起着至关重要的作用。|The allantois helps to remove waste products from the developing fetus. 尿囊帮助清除发育中胎儿的废物。|近义词:chorion (绒毛膜)|反义词: None|noun
