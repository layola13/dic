accreditation: /əˌkrɛdɪˈteɪʃən/
n.
认证；鉴定；认可
The process of officially recognizing someone or something as having a particular status or being qualified to perform a particular activity.
The college was given full accreditation in 1925.  这所学院在1925年获得了完全认证。

The laboratory has received accreditation to carry out these tests. 该实验室已获得进行这些测试的认证。

近义词: certification, approval, recognition, endorsement
反义词: disapproval, rejection
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin accrēditāre, from Latin ad- ‘to’ + crēditāre ‘entrust’.
