overprinting:/ˈoʊvərˌprɪntɪŋ/| n.|套印，叠印|The process of printing one color on top of another, often to create a richer or darker color, or a special effect.|The designer used overprinting to create a subtle textured effect on the business cards. 设计师在名片上使用套印技术，创造出一种微妙的纹理效果。|superimposition, surprinting|underprinting|noun
overprint:/ˌoʊvərˈprɪnt/| v.|套印，叠印|To print one color on top of another.|They overprinted the logo in gold on the cover of the book. 他们在书的封面上套印了金色的商标。|superimpose, surprint|underprint|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + printing, from print (v.).  The word "print" ultimately comes from the Latin "premere" meaning "to press."
