hello:/həˈloʊ/ (US), /hɛˈloʊ/ (UK) | int. n. | 你好；问候语 | Used as a greeting or to begin a phone conversation. | Hello, how are you? 你好，你好吗？| Hi, hey | Goodbye | Interjection, Noun


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What is your name? 你叫什么名字？
What do you like to eat? 你喜欢吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you doing now? 你现在在做什么？
What is she cooking in the kitchen? 她在厨房里做什么菜？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you done today? 你今天做了什么？
What has she learned in this class? 她在这节课学到了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been doing all afternoon? 你整个下午都在做什么？
What has he been working on recently? 他最近一直在做什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃了什么？
What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 开会的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had they discussed before I arrived? 在我到达之前他们讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the storm started?  暴风雨开始前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? 他们一直在争论什么这么久？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do this weekend? 你这周末要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气会怎样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? 他们下次会议将讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have accomplished by the end of the year? 到今年年底你将完成什么？
What will they have finished by tomorrow afternoon? 明天下午他们将完成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业的时候，她将学习什么专业多久了？


虚拟语气:
If I knew what to say, I would say it. 如果我知道该说什么，我就会说。
I wish I knew what she was thinking. 我希望我知道她在想什么。

被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What is your favorite color? 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What are your plans for the summer? 你暑假的计划是什么？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what he's saying. 她不明白他在说什么。
