awned:/ɔːnd/
adjective
有遮篷的；有雨篷的
furnished with an awning
例句：
The awned windows provided shade from the afternoon sun.
带遮阳篷的窗户遮挡了下午的阳光。

The cafe had awned tables outside.
咖啡馆外面有带遮阳篷的桌子。

近义词：
covered, sheltered, shaded
反义词：
uncovered, exposed

词性
adjective
