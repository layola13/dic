built-in:/ˌbɪltˈɪn/| n.|内置装置，嵌入式设备|A built-in is a cupboard, wardrobe, or other fitting that forms part of a room or building.|The kitchen has all the latest built-ins.|fixture, appliance|separate, standalone|noun
