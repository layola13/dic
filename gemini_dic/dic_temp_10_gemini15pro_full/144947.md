overabstemious: /ˌoʊvəræbˈstiːmiəs/ | adj. | 过度节制的，过分禁欲的 | Excessively abstinent or temperate, especially in eating and drinking.  Characterized by restraint taken to an extreme. | He was so overabstemious that he wouldn't even allow himself a small piece of birthday cake. 他过度节制，甚至连一小块生日蛋糕都不允许自己吃。|  She is overabstemious in her diet, avoiding all sugar and fat. 她的饮食过分节制，避免所有糖和脂肪。 | 近义词: ascetic, austere, abstemious | 反义词: gluttonous, indulgent, intemperate | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From "over-" (excessive) + "abstemious" (moderate, especially in eating and drinking).  "Abstemious" comes from Latin "abstemius," from "ab-" (from, away from) + "temetum" (strong drink).
