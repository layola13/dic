nodosity:/nɑːˈdɑːsɪti/ /nəʊˈdɒsɪti/ | n. |多节性；节状| the state or quality of having many nodes or knots | The nodosity of the ginger root made it difficult to peel.  生姜根的多节性使它难以去皮。| synonyms: knottiness, lumpiness, unevenness | antonyms: smoothness, evenness | noun
