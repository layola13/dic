mongrelly: /ˈmʌŋɡrəli/ /ˈmɑːŋɡrəli/
| adj. | 杂种的，混血的；低劣的，卑贱的 | Of mixed breed or origin; of inferior quality; base or mean. |  The mongrelly dog wandered the streets looking for food. 这只杂种狗在街上游荡寻找食物。|  He spoke in a mongrelly accent, a mixture of several regional dialects. 他说话的口音很杂，混合了好几种方言。 | Near Synonyms: hybrid, mixed, impure, base, degenerate | Antonyms: purebred, thoroughbred, noble | word type: adjective
