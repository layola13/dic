clean-and-jerk: /ˌkliːn ən ˈdʒɜːrk/ (British) /ˌkliːn ən ˈdʒɜːrk/ (American)| n.|挺举|A weightlifting exercise in which the barbell is lifted from the floor to the shoulders in one movement (the clean), and then from the shoulders to an overhead position in another movement (the jerk).|The clean-and-jerk is a highly technical lift.|近义词: weightlifting exercise, Olympic lift|反义词: None|词性: noun

clean-and-jerked: /ˌkliːnd ən ˈdʒɜːrkt/ (British) /ˌkliːnd ən ˈdʒɜːrkt/ (American)| v. (past tense and past participle)|挺举（过去式和过去分词）|Past tense and past participle of "clean-and-jerk." Refers to the action of performing the clean-and-jerk lift.|She clean-and-jerked 100kg.|近义词: lifted, hoisted|反义词: lowered, dropped|词性: verb

词源：clean (v.) from Old English clæne "free from dirt or defilement;" and jerk (v.) from perhaps imitative; perhaps related to Low German and Dutch jukken.  The compound "clean-and-jerk" refers to the two distinct movements of the lift.
