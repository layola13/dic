mahayana:/ˌmɑːhəˈjɑːnə/ | n. | 大乘佛教 | One of the two major traditions of Buddhism, characterized by its emphasis on the Bodhisattva ideal and the potential for all beings to achieve enlightenment. |  例句：Mahayana Buddhism is prevalent in East Asia. 大乘佛教在东亚盛行。

例句：The Mahayana sutras emphasize compassion and wisdom.  大乘佛经强调慈悲和智慧。

| 近义词: Greater Vehicle | 反义词: Hinayana (now often considered pejorative and replaced by Theravada) | 词性: noun
