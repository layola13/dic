aerolith: /ˈɛə.rə.lɪθ/
noun
陨石
A stony meteorite. A meteoric stone.  A stone that falls to earth from outer space.
The aerolith blazed across the sky before landing in a farmer's field. 这块陨石划过天空，最后落在了一块农田里。
The museum displayed a large aerolith found in the desert.  博物馆展出了一块在沙漠中发现的大陨石。

synonyms: meteorite, meteoroid, falling star, shooting star
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but perhaps terrestrial rock, earth rock)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀερόλιθος (aerolíthos, “air-stone”), from ἀήρ (aḗr, “air”) + λίθος (líthos, “stone”).
