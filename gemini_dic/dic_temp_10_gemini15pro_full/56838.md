dorp: /dɔrp/ (South African English)
n.
village, small town
A small rural community, typically smaller than a town.
Example: We visited a charming dorp nestled in the valley. 我们参观了坐落在山谷中的一个迷人的村庄。
Example:  The dorp held its annual market day. 这个村庄举行了年度集市日。
Synonym: hamlet, village, settlement
Antonym: city, metropolis
Noun
