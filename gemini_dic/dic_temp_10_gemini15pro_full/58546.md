dugout: /ˈdʌɡaʊt/ (US) /ˈdʌɡaʊt/ (UK)
n.
防空洞，掩体; (尤指棒球场边)球员休息区; 独木舟
A shelter dug in the ground, especially one used by troops; a low shelter at the side of a baseball field where players sit when they are not on the field; a canoe made by hollowing out a log.
The soldiers huddled in the dugout during the bombardment.  士兵们在轰炸期间挤在防空洞里。
The manager yelled instructions from the dugout.  经理从球员休息区大声喊着指示。
The indigenous people fished from their dugout canoes.  原住民用他们的独木舟捕鱼。
synonyms: shelter, trench, bunker, dugout canoe, canoe
antonyms:  (For shelter meaning) exposure, open ground


词性: noun
