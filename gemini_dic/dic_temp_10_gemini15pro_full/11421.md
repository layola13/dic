argilliferous:/ˌɑːrdʒɪˈlɪfərəs/
| adj. |
含粘土的，富含粘土的 |
Containing clay; consisting of or abounding in clay. |
The argilliferous soil was ideal for making pottery.  这种富含粘土的土壤非常适合制作陶器。
The geologist examined the argilliferous rocks. 地质学家检查了这些含粘土的岩石。
|
近义词：clayey, argillaceous |
反义词：sandy, gravelly |
词性：adjective


Etymology:
From Latin *argillifer* (clay-bearing), from *argilla* (clay) + *-fer* (bearing).  The *-fer* suffix is related to the verb *ferre* (to bear, carry).
