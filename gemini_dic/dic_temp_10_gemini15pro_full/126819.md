naperian:/nəˈpɪəriən/
adjective|纳皮尔的，自然对数的|Relating to or involving natural logarithms, especially with base e (approximately 2.71828).  This term honors John Napier, a Scottish mathematician who pioneered the study of logarithms.|The naperian logarithm of 10 is approximately 2.30259.  纳皮尔对数10约等于2.30259.|Synonym: natural (in the context of logarithms)|Antonym: common (in the context of logarithms)
word type: adjective
