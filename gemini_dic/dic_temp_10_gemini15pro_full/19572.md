bigaroon:/ˌbɪɡəˈruːn/ | n. | 流浪汉，游民 | A person who wanders from place to place without a settled home or occupation; a vagrant. | He lived like a bigaroon, drifting from town to town. 他像个流浪汉一样，从一个城镇漂泊到另一个城镇。| 近义词：vagrant, hobo, tramp, drifter | 反义词：resident, settler | 词性: 名词
