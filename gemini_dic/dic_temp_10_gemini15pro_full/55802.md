divertise:/daɪˈvɜːtaɪz/ | v. | 转移；使娱乐；使消遣 | To turn (something) aside from its intended course; to amuse or entertain. |  The magician diverted the audience's attention with a sleight of hand. 魔术师用巧妙的手法转移了观众的注意力。

The colorful toys diverted the baby.  色彩鲜艳的玩具逗乐了宝宝。 | 近义词：amuse, entertain, distract | 反义词：bore, tire, focus | 词性: 动词

词源: From Late Latin *divertire, from Latin divertere (“to turn aside, divert”), from dis- (“apart, away”) + vertere (“to turn”).
