nonferventness:/ˌnɒnˈfɜːrvəntnəs/ | n. | 冷淡，缺乏热情 | Lack of fervor or enthusiasm; apathy. |  Her nonferventness towards the project worried the team. 她对这个项目的冷淡让团队感到担忧。 |  The artist's nonferventness was reflected in his recent, uninspired work.  这位艺术家的冷淡反映在他最近缺乏灵感的创作中。| 近义词：apathy, indifference, coldness, lukewarmness | 反义词：fervor, enthusiasm, passion, zeal | 词性: 名词

词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + fervent (adjective meaning "having or displaying a passionate intensity") + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)
