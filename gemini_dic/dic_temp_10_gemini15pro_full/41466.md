cornily:/ˈkɔːrnili/ | adv. | 庸俗地，老套地 | In a sentimental, overly emotional, or cliché way.  | He cornily declared his love for her under the moonlight. 他在月光下老套地对她表白了爱意。| Example: The movie ended with a cornily romantic kiss.  这部电影以一个老套的浪漫之吻结束。 | Near Synonyms: sentimentally, mawkishly, cheesily, sappily | Antonyms: unsentimentally, sincerely, genuinely | 词性: 副词

cornily:/ˈkɔːrnɪli/ | adv. | 愚蠢地;可笑地 | In a foolish or silly way, often unintentionally. | He cornily tripped over his own feet while trying to impress her. 他为了给她留下好印象，结果笨拙地绊倒了自己。| Example:  He wore a cornily oversized hat to the party. 他戴着一顶愚蠢的大帽子去参加派对。| Near Synonyms: foolishly, ridiculously, absurdly, clumsily | Antonyms: gracefully, skillfully, elegantly | 词性: 副词
