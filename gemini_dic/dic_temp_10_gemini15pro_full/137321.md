nontalkers:/ˈnɒnˌtɔːkəz/ (British), /ˈnɑːnˌtɔːkərz/ (American)
n.
不说话的人，沉默寡言的人
People who do not talk, or who talk very little.
The nontalkers in the group often have valuable insights.  小组里不说话的人通常有宝贵的见解。
The meeting was dominated by the talkers, while the nontalkers were largely ignored.  会议由健谈的人主导，而沉默寡言的人则基本上被忽略了。
synonyms: silent types, quiet ones, introverts
antonyms: talkers, chatterboxes, extroverts
noun
