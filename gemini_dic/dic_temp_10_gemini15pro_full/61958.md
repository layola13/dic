emulsioned:/ɪˈmʌlʃənd/
adjective
乳化的; 乳状的
made into or resembling an emulsion.
The recipe called for emulsioned butter and sugar.  这食谱需要乳化黄油和糖。
The paint was emulsioned before applying it to the wall.  油漆在涂到墙上之前被乳化了。
近义词: emulsified, homogenized
反义词: separated, immiscible
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "emulsify," which comes from the Latin "emulsus," the past participle of "emulgere" (to milk out, drain out), from "ex" (out) + "mulgere" (to milk).
