nontrailing:/ˌnɒnˈtreɪlɪŋ/ | adj. | 非尾随的，非拖尾的 | Not trailing; not extending or hanging loosely behind.  | The nontrailing edge of the wing is crucial for lift. 机翼的非尾随边缘对升力至关重要。 | The car's nontrailing suspension improved its handling. 这辆车的非拖尾悬架改善了它的操控性。 | 近义词: leading, forward | 反义词: trailing, following | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "trailing" (present participle of the verb "trail," from Old French "trailler," meaning "to drag").
