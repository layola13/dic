equiparate:/ɪˈkwɪpəreɪt/
verb
使相等；同等看待
to regard or treat as equal
例句：The law should equiparate men and women.  法律应该平等对待男性和女性。

例句：Some people believe that artificial intelligence should not be equiparated with human intelligence. 有些人认为人工智能不应该与人类智能相提并论。

近义词：equalize, equate, level, match
反义词：differentiate, distinguish
词性：v.
