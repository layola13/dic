magnum:/ˈmæɡnəm/| n.|大酒瓶；大容量容器；大型号商品；大事件；（左轮）大口径手枪|A large bottle, typically of wine, holding 1.5 liters (equivalent to two standard bottles); something large and impressive.|例句：He ordered a magnum of champagne to celebrate.  他点了一大瓶香槟来庆祝。

例句：The museum showcased a magnum opus of the renowned artist.  博物馆展出了这位著名艺术家的一部巨作。|近义词：large bottle, double bottle, jeroboam (for wine), masterpiece (for a great work)|反义词：miniature, small bottle|词性：名词
