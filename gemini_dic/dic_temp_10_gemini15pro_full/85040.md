harborough:/ˈhɑːrbərə/ | n. | 港口；海港；避难所 | A place on the coast where ships may moor in shelter, especially one protected from rough water by piers, jetties, and other artificial structures. |  例句：The fishing boats returned to the harborough at sunset. 渔船在日落时回到了港口。

例句：The town grew up around the natural harborough.  这个城镇围绕着天然港口发展起来。 | 近义词：port, haven, harbor | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 open sea 公海) | 词性: noun
