detachedness:/dɪˈtætʃt.nəs/| n.|超然；冷漠；客观|The state of being objective or emotionally removed.|She observed the events with a certain detachedness, as if they didn't concern her. 她以某种超然的态度观察着这些事件，仿佛它们与她无关。|Example: He spoke about the tragedy with a strange detachedness. 他以一种奇怪的超然态度谈论这场悲剧。|Synonyms: objectivity, neutrality, indifference, aloofness|Antonyms: involvement, engagement, passion, fervor|noun

detachedness:/dɪˈtætʃt.nəs/| n.|分离；脱离|The state of being separated or disconnected.|The detachedness of the garage from the house made it feel like a separate world. 车库与房屋的分离使它感觉像一个独立的世界。|Example: The detachedness of the island community fostered a unique culture. 岛屿社区的隔离促成了一种独特的文化。|Synonyms: separation, disconnection, isolation|Antonyms: connection, attachment, union|noun
