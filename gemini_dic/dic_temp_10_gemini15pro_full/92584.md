i-spy:/ˈaɪ spaɪ/ | n. | 我发现，一种儿童游戏 | A children's guessing game in which one player chooses an object within sight and challenges the others to guess its identity by describing it only by its first letter.  |  例句：Let's play I-spy on our road trip. 我们在公路旅行中玩“我发现”游戏吧。 | 近义词：guessing game, hidden object game | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

i-spy:/ˈaɪ spaɪ/ | v. |  发现，侦察，看到 | To catch sight of something or someone, often unexpectedly or surreptitiously. | 例句：I spied a rare bird in the tree. 我在树上发现了一只珍稀的鸟。 | 近义词：spot, see, notice, observe, detect, espy | 反义词: miss, overlook | 词性: verb
