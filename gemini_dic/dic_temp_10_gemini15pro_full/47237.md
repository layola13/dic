dazzlingly:/ˈdæzəlɪŋli/| adv.|令人眼花缭乱地，耀眼地|In a way that is dazzling; brilliantly.  Shining intensely.|The sun shone dazzlingly on the water.  阳光耀眼地照在水面上。|近义词：brightly, brilliantly, radiantly, gloriously|反义词：dully, dimly|词性:副词
Etymology: From dazzling + -ly. Dazzling from dazzle + -ing. Dazzle from Middle English dasen, from Old Norse dasa.
