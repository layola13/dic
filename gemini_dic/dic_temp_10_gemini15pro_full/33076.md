chria:/ˈkraɪə/ | n. | 格言；箴言；警句；练习 | A concise saying or maxim; an aphorism. Also, an exercise in which students elaborate on a brief narrative, often focusing on moral or ethical implications. |  The teacher assigned a chria on the importance of honesty. 老师布置了一篇关于诚实重要性的格言练习。| synonym: maxim, aphorism, adage, proverb | antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider: falsehood, lie, fallacy) | noun
