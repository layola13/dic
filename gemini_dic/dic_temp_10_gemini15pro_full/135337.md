nonpenetrabilities:/ˌnɒnpɛnɪtrəˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. |不可渗透性；不透性|The quality or state of being nonpenetrable; impenetrability.  The inability of something to be penetrated or passed through.|The nonpenetrabilities of certain materials make them ideal for protective clothing.|近义词：impenetrabilities, impermeabilities|反义词: permeabilities, porosities|词性:名词
