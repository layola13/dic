nucleolus:/njuːˈkliːələs/, /njuːˈkliːoʊləs/ | n. | 核仁 | A small, dense, spherical structure in the nucleus of a cell during interphase. It is the site of ribosomal RNA (rRNA) synthesis and ribosomal subunit assembly. | The nucleolus is clearly visible under a microscope.  核仁在显微镜下清晰可见。

The nucleolus plays a crucial role in protein synthesis. 核仁在蛋白质合成中起着至关重要的作用。 | 近义词: None (It's a specific cellular component) | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *nucleolus*, diminutive of *nucleus* (kernel, little nut).
