albification:/ælbɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
白化，变白
The process of becoming white or whiter.
The albification of the coral reefs is a serious environmental concern. 珊瑚礁的白化是一个严重的环境问题。
whitening, bleaching
darkening, pigmentation
noun
