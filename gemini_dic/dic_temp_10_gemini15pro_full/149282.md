ozonification:/ˌoʊzənɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 臭氧化，臭氧处理 | The process of treating something with ozone, typically for the purposes of disinfection or purification. |  Ozone is a powerful oxidizing agent and ozonification is effective in removing a wide range of contaminants from water. 臭氧是一种强氧化剂，臭氧化能有效去除水中的各种污染物。| synonyms: ozone treatment, oxidation | antonyms: reduction, deozonification | noun
