indenting:/ɪnˈdɛntɪŋ/ | n. | 缩进；缩排；锯齿状 | The process of setting a block of text in from the margin. | Example:  The indenting of the first line of each paragraph makes the text easier to read. 每段第一行的缩进使文本更易于阅读。

Example:  Consistent indenting is important for clear code.  一致的缩进对于清晰的代码很重要。|Synonyms: indentation, formatting, offsetting | Antonyms: protruding, outdenting | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From indent + -ing.  Ultimately from Latin *in- + dens "tooth".
