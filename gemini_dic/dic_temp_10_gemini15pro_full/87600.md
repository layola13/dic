heterothermic:/ˌhetərəˈθɜːrmɪk/ |adj.|变温的，异温的|An animal whose body temperature varies with its surroundings, rather than being kept constant.|Bats are heterothermic animals, meaning their body temperature changes depending on the environment.|近义词:poikilothermic|反义词:homeothermic, endothermic|adjective
