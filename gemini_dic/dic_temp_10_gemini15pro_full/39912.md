constrain:/kənˈstreɪn/ | v. |限制，约束，强迫|to limit or restrict someone or something|例句：Financial constraints forced the company to lay off workers. 财政限制迫使公司解雇工人。|近义词：restrict, limit, confine, compel, oblige|反义词：liberate, free, encourage|词性:动词

constrain:/kənˈstreɪn/ | n. |限制，约束|something that limits or restricts someone or something|例句：The constraints of time and budget limited the scope of the project. 时间和预算的限制缩小了项目的范围。|近义词：restriction, limitation, confinement|反义词：freedom, liberty|词性:名词
