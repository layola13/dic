bracteolate: /ˈbræktiəˌlət/ | adj. | 具小苞片的；有小苞片状附属物的 | Having small bracts or bract-like appendages. |  Example: The bracteolate flowers of this plant are quite distinctive.  这种植物具小苞片的花朵非常独特。| Example:  The botanist identified the species by its bracteolate stem.  植物学家通过其具小苞片状附属物的茎干识别了该物种。 | Synonym: bracteate | Antonym: ebracteate | Adjective

bracteole: /ˈbræktiˌoʊl/ | n. | 小苞片 | A small bract, especially one on a flower stalk. | Example: The bracteole is located just below the flower. 小苞片位于花朵的正下方。| Example: Upon closer inspection, he noticed the tiny bracteole.  仔细观察后，他注意到了那个小苞片。| Synonym: bractlet | Antonym: (No direct antonym, the absence of a bracteole might be described) | Noun


Etymology:
From Latin *bracteola, diminutive of bractea (“thin plate of metal, gold leaf, bract”), from Late Latin bractea (“bract”), from Latin bractea (“thin plate of metal, gold leaf”)
