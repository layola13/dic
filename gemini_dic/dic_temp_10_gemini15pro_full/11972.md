arteriole:/ɑːrˈtɪəriˌoʊl/ | n. |小动脉|A small branch of an artery leading into capillaries.|The arterioles constrict to reduce blood flow to the skin in cold weather.  小动脉收缩以减少寒冷天气流向皮肤的血液。|Synonyms: capillary, venule |Antonyms: artery, vein | noun
