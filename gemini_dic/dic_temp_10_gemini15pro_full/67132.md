exportations:/ˌɛkspɔːrˈteɪʃənz/
n.
出口(商品)；输出(物)
The act of exporting goods or services to another country.
The government is trying to boost exportations. 政府正在努力推动出口。
synonyms: exports, shipments, outward bound goods
antonyms: importations, imports

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English exportation, from Late Latin exportātiō (“a carrying out, exportation”), from Latin exportātus, past participle of exportō (“I carry out, export”), from ex (“out of, from”) + portō (“I carry”).
