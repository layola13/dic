multipurpose:/ˌmʌltiˈpɜːrpəs/| adj.|多用途的，多功能的|Designed or intended to serve more than one purpose.|This is a multipurpose tool that can be used for a variety of tasks. 这是一种多用途工具，可以用于各种任务。|Example:  A multipurpose room can be used for meetings, presentations, and parties. 一个多功能房间可以用于会议、演示和聚会。
This multipurpose cleaner is great for kitchen and bathroom surfaces.  这款多用途清洁剂非常适合厨房和浴室表面。|Synonyms: versatile, adaptable, flexible, all-purpose|Antonyms: specialized, single-purpose|Adjective
