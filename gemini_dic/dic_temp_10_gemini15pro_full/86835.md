hematothermal:/ˌhɛmətoʊˈθɜːrməl/ | adj. | 温血的；恒温的 | Relating to or characterized by warm-bloodedness; homeothermic.  Maintained at a constant temperature by internal physiological mechanisms, as in warm-blooded animals. | Birds and mammals are hematothermal animals. 鸟类和哺乳动物是恒温动物。 |  The hematothermal nature of mammals allows them to thrive in diverse climates. 哺乳动物的恒温特性使它们能够在不同的气候中茁壮成长。| synonyms: homeothermic, endothermic, warm-blooded | antonyms: poikilothermic, ectothermic, cold-blooded | word type: adjective
