carbine:/ˈkɑːrbaɪn/ /ˈkɑːrbɪn/ | n. |卡宾枪; 短步枪|A lightweight rifle, shorter than a standard rifle, often used by cavalry or special forces.  It is characterized by its shorter barrel, making it easier to handle in close quarters or on horseback.|The soldier carried a carbine into battle.  这个士兵带着卡宾枪上战场。|Synonyms: rifle, short rifle, musket|Antonyms: long rifle|Noun
