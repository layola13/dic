detonating:/ˈdɛtəˌneɪtɪŋ/
verb, adjective|爆炸的，引爆的|exploding, causing to explode|
The detonating cord ignited the explosives. 引爆线点燃了炸药。
近义词:exploding, blasting, igniting
反义词:defusing, neutralizing
词性:verb (present participle), adjective

detonate:/ˈdɛtəˌneɪt/
verb|引爆，爆炸|to explode or cause to explode|
The bomb detonated at 6:00 a.m. 炸弹在早上6点钟爆炸。
近义词:explode, blast, blow up
反义词:defuse, neutralize
词性:verb

词源: From Latin detonare "to thunder down," from de- "down" + tonare "to thunder."
