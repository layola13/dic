consonanted:/ˈkɑːnsənəntɪd/
adjective
辅音的；充满辅音的
Characterized by consonants; containing many consonants.
The word "strength" is very consonanted.  这个单词“strength”辅音很多。
The language is very consonanted, making it difficult to pronounce.  这门语言辅音很多，很难发音。
