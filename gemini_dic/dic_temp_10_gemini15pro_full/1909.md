actinaria:/ˌæktɪˈnɛəriə/
n.
海葵目，海葵类
An order of Anthozoa comprising the sea anemones.  These are solitary polyps that attach to rocks or other substrates.
Sea anemones, of the order Actinaria, are found in all oceans. 海葵目，即海葵，存在于所有海洋中。
The giant green anemone is a common member of the Actinaria order.  巨型绿海葵是海葵目中常见的成员。
近义词:sea anemones
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *Actinaria, from Ancient Greek ἀκτίς (aktís, “ray”) +‎ -aria.  Relates to the radial symmetry of the animals in this order.

USAGE->
The Actinaria are a fascinating group of marine invertebrates. 海葵目是一组迷人的海洋无脊椎动物。
Many species of Actinaria have symbiotic relationships with algae. 许多海葵目物种与藻类存在共生关系。
