overdescriptivenesses: /ˌoʊvər dɪˈskrɪptɪvnəsɪz/
noun
过度描述
the quality of being excessively descriptive; the use of too many details in describing something.
The overdescriptivenesses of the author's prose bogged down the narrative. 作者散文中的过度描述拖累了叙事。
近义词: verbosity, prolixity, wordiness
反义词: conciseness, brevity, succinctness
词性: noun
