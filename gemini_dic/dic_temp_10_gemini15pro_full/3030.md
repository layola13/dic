aerobaticses: /ˌɛərəˈbætɪksɪz/
noun
特技飞行；飞行表演
spectacular flying feats and maneuvers (often performed as a sport or as entertainment).
The air show featured breathtaking aerobaticses by the Blue Angels. 蓝色天使飞行表演队的特技飞行令人叹为观止。
The pilot thrilled the crowd with his daring aerobaticses.  飞行员以他大胆的特技飞行使观众激动不已。
synonyms: aerial acrobatics, stunt flying, aerial maneuvers, wingwalking
antonyms: N/A
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From aero- (relating to air) + Greek batos (walking), later influenced by acrobatics.  "Aero" comes from the Greek "aer" (air). "Batics" ultimately derives from the Greek "bainein" (to walk or step). The combination refers to “walking in the air.”
