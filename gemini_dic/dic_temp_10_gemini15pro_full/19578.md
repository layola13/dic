bigeye:/ˈbɪɡˌaɪ/| n. |大眼鱼；金枪鱼的一种|A type of tuna fish known for its large eyes.|例句：The bigeye tuna is a highly migratory species.  大眼金枪鱼是一种高度洄游的物种。

例句：We caught a bigeye on our fishing trip. 我们在钓鱼之旅中捕到了一条大眼金枪鱼。|近义词：tuna, yellowfin|反义词: (No direct antonym for a type of fish) |词性:noun
