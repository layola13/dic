nonfalsifiable:/ˌnɒnˈfɔːlsɪfaɪəbəl/ /ˌnɑːnˈfɔːlsəˌfaɪəbəl/
adjective
不可证伪的
Not capable of being proved false.  A statement is nonfalsifiable if no observation could possibly show it to be false.
例句：His theory about the origin of the universe is nonfalsifiable, making it impossible to test scientifically.  他关于宇宙起源的理论是不可证伪的，因此不可能进行科学检验。

例句：Many religious beliefs are considered nonfalsifiable, relying on faith rather than empirical evidence. 许多宗教信仰被认为是不可证伪的，依赖于信仰而不是经验证据。
近义词：untestable, unverifiable
反义词：falsifiable, testable
词性: adjective
