nongarrulous:/ˌnɒnˈɡærələs/| adj.|不健谈的，沉默寡言的|Not talkative; reserved or uncommunicative.|He was a nongarrulous man, preferring to listen rather than speak. 他是个不健谈的人，宁愿倾听也不愿说话。|近义词:reticent, taciturn, quiet, silent|反义词:garrulous, loquacious, talkative|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From non- + garrulous.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "garrulous" comes from the Latin word "garrulus," meaning "talkative" or "chattering."
