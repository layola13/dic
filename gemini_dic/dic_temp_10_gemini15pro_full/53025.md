diocese: /ˈdaɪəsɪs/ (US), /ˈdʌɪəsɪs/ (UK)| n. |主教管区；教区|A district under the jurisdiction of a bishop.| The diocese of York is very large. 约克教区很大.| 近义词: bishopric, see | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From Old French *diocèse*, from Late Latin *dioecesis*, from Greek *dioíkēsis* "administration, province," from *dioikeîn* "to manage, administer," from *dia-* "through, across" + *oikeîn* "to manage a household, inhabit."
