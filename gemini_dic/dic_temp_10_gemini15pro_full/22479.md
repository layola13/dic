books:/bʊks/ | n. |书籍，书本 | A collection of written or printed sheets of paper bound together. | I love reading books. 我喜欢读书。|  volumes, publications, texts |  None (Lack of books might be described as a scarcity or absence of reading material, but there isn't a direct antonym for the physical object.) | noun

Etymology: Old English *bōc*, from Proto-Germanic *bōkō* (meaning "beech" - runes were originally carved on beechwood tablets).  Related to German *Buch* and Dutch *boek*.
