disingenuity:/ˌdɪsɪnˌdʒɛˈnjuːɪti/
n.|不真诚，不坦率，狡猾|Lack of honesty or sincerity; deceitful cleverness.|Her disingenuity was evident in her evasive answers. 她的不坦率从她闪烁其词的回答中显而易见。|例句：He displayed considerable disingenuity in avoiding the question. 他在回避这个问题时表现出相当的狡猾。|近义词：deceit, duplicity, guile, cunning|反义词：honesty, candor, sincerity, frankness
词性:名词
