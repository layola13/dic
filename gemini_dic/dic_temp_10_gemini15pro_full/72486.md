fluoranthene:/ˈflʊərənˌθiːn/ /flɔːrˈænˌθiːn/
noun|荧蒽|A polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbon (PAH) consisting of a naphthalene unit fused to a benzene ring through two adjacent carbon atoms.|Fluoranthene is a common environmental pollutant found in combustion products.|Fluoranthene concentrations were high in the soil samples. 土壤样品中荧蒽浓度很高。|近义词: PAH, polycyclic aromatic hydrocarbon|反义词: None (lacks a direct opposite)|n.
