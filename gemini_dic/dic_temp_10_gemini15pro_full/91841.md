hypergraphs:/ˈhaɪpərˌɡræfs/ | n. | 超图 | A generalization of a graph in which an edge can connect any number of vertices.  A graph can be thought of as a special case of a hypergraph where each edge connects only two vertices. | Hypergraphs are used in machine learning to model complex relationships. 超图在机器学习中用于对复杂关系进行建模。 | hypergraph, hypernet | graph | noun
