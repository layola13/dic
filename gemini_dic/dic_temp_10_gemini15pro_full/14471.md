avalanche:/ˈævəlɑːnʃ/ (US), /ˈævəlɑːnʃ/ (UK)| n.|雪崩；大量涌现的事物|A sudden, large mass of snow, ice, and rocks sliding rapidly down a mountainside; a sudden arrival or occurrence of something in overwhelming quantities.|例句：The avalanche buried the village. 雪崩掩埋了村庄。

例句：We received an avalanche of complaints. 我们收到了大量的投诉。|近义词：landslide, flood, deluge, torrent|反义词:trickle, dribble|词性:noun
