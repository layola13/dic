equilateral:/ˌiːkwɪˈlætərəl/
adjective
等边的；等面的
Having all sides equal in length.
An equilateral triangle has three sides of equal length.  一个等边三角形有三条等长的边。
The crystal structure was equilateral.  晶体结构是等边的。
近义词:equiangular, isosceles (for triangles only)
反义词:scalene, irregular
词性:adjective
