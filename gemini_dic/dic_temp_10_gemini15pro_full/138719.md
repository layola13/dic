numb:/nʌm/| adj.|麻木的，失去感觉的|Deprived of physical sensation or the ability to feel, especially as a result of cold, pressure, or injury. Also, emotionally unresponsive.|My fingers were numb with cold. 我的手指冻僵了。|Synonyms: insensible, deadened, unfeeling|Antonyms: sensitive, responsive|adjective

numb:/nʌm/| v.|使麻木，使失去感觉|To make numb or insensitive; to deaden.|The dentist numbed my gums before the procedure. 牙医在手术前麻醉了我的牙龈。|Synonyms: desensitize, anesthetize|Antonyms: sensitize, stimulate|verb
