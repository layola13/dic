combusted:/kəmˈbʌstɪd/
|v. |燃烧的，烧毁的|burned completely; oxidized with rapid evolution of heat and usually light|The spaceship combusted upon re-entry into Earth's atmosphere.|近义词：burned, incinerated, consumed|反义词:extinguished, unburned|词性:动词(过去分词形式，用作形容词)


combustion:/kəmˈbʌʃtʃən/
|n.|燃烧|the process of burning something|The combustion of fossil fuels releases greenhouse gases.|近义词：burning, ignition, incineration|反义词:extinguishing|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *combustionem (nominative combustio) "a burning, scorching," noun of action from past-participle stem of comburere "to burn up," from com- "completely" + burere "to burn."
