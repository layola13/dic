constitutively:/kənˈstɪtjuːtɪvli/ | adv. | 组成地；本质地；结构上地 | Inherent, essential, and built-in.  Relating to the basic structure or composition of something; acting or existing in a permanent or continuous way as part of the normal functioning of something. |  These cells constitutively express the receptor. 这些细胞组成性地表达受体。 | Synonyms: inherently, intrinsically, naturally, essentially | Antonyms: conditionally, facultatively, transiently | adverb
