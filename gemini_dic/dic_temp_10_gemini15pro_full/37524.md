commonable:/ˈkɒmənəbl/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmənəbl/ (US)| adj.|可共用的，可共享的；可共有的，可公有的|Something that is commonable can be held or used in common by all members of a group or community.  Relating to land or rights that are held in common. |例句：The commonable land was used for grazing. 这片公地用于放牧。

例句：Commonable rights were an important part of village life. 公有权是乡村生活的重要组成部分。|近义词：sharable, communal, joint, public|反义词：private, exclusive|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Middle English *comunable*, from Old French *comunable*, from Latin *communis* ("common").
