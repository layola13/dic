affixation:/ˌæfɪkˈseɪʃən/ | n. | 词缀法；附加；粘着 | The process of adding affixes to a word to create a new word or change its meaning. |  Example: The word "unhappiness" is formed by affixation, using the prefix "un-" and the suffix "-ness" with the root word "happy".  汉译：单词“unhappiness”是由词缀法构成的，使用前缀“un-”和后缀“-ness”以及词根“happy”。


Example:  Affixation is a common way to create new words in English.  汉译：词缀法是英语中创造新词的常用方法。 | synonyms: derivation, word formation | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider perhaps "root word" or "base form") | noun
