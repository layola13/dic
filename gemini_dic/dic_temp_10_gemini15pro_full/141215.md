oophytic:/oʊ.əˈfɪt.ɪk/| adj.|卵生的，卵生植物的|Producing eggs that hatch outside the mother's body; relating to plants that grow on other plants, but do not derive nourishment from them.|Epiphytic orchids are oophytic, meaning their seeds germinate on other plants, but they don't parasitize them.  附生兰花是卵生的，这意味着它们的种子在其他植物上发芽，但它们不会寄生于其他植物。|Near Synonyms: oviparous, epiphytic|Antonyms: viviparous, parasitic|adjective
