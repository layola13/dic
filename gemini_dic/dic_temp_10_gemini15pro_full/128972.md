niobium:/naɪˈoʊbiəm/ | n. |铌|A chemical element with the symbol Nb and atomic number 41. It is a soft, grey, ductile transition metal. |Niobium is used in alloys, particularly in stainless steels where it improves corrosion resistance. 铌用于合金，尤其是不锈钢，以提高其耐腐蚀性。 | 近义词: (As an element, it has no direct synonyms, but in specific applications, other metals might offer similar properties) | 反义词: None | noun
