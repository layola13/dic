marginicidal:/ˌmɑːrdʒɪnɪˈsaɪdəl/
adjective
边缘杀伤的; 仅能杀死少量细菌的
Killing only a small proportion of bacteria; having only a slight bactericidal effect.
The antibiotic proved to be marginicidal at best, failing to significantly reduce the bacterial load. 这种抗生素最多只能起到轻微的杀菌作用，未能显著减少细菌负荷。

Marginicidal concentrations of the disinfectant were used.  使用了仅能杀死少量细菌的消毒剂浓度。
Synonym: weakly bactericidal, slightly bactericidal
Antonym: bactericidal, strongly bactericidal
词性: adjective
