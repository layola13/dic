bopster:/ˈbɒpstər/ (UK) /ˈbɑːpstər/ (US)| n.| 爵士乐爱好者，比波普音乐迷 |A person who is enthusiastic about or plays bop music, a style of jazz characterized by fast tempos, complex harmonies, and virtuosic improvisation. | He's a real bopster, always listening to Charlie Parker and Dizzy Gillespie. 他是个真正的爵士乐迷，总是听查理·帕克和迪兹·吉莱斯皮的音乐。|  She's a talented bopster, her saxophone playing is incredible. 她是一位才华横溢的比波普音乐家，她的萨克斯演奏令人难以置信。 |Synonym: jazz aficionado, bebop enthusiast | Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps someone who dislikes bop music) | noun
