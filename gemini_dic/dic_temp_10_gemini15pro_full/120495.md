minuted:/ˈmɪnɪtɪd/
| adj. | 记录在案的；详细的 | Recorded in or as minutes; detailed. | The meeting was minuted in detail. 会议记录非常详细。 |  | detailed, recorded | concise, summarized | adjective

minuted:/ˈmɪnɪtɪd/
| v. |  记录；将…写入会议记录 | To record something in the minutes of a meeting. | The secretary minuted the decisions taken at the meeting. 秘书记录了会议上做出的决定。 | record, document | omit, ignore | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From *minute* (n.), ultimately from Latin *minuta* ("small part"). The verb sense developed from the noun, referring to the practice of recording things in minutes.
