loafing:/ˈloʊfɪŋ/ (US), /ˈləʊfɪŋ/ (UK)| n. v.|闲逛，游荡；懒散|The act of spending time idly or lazily.  Relates to avoiding work or other duties.|He was fired for loafing on the job. 他因为工作时偷懒而被解雇了。|Synonyms: idling, lazing, lounging, slacking|Antonyms: working, toiling, striving|noun/verb

loaf:/loʊf/ (US), /ləʊf/ (UK)| n. v.|一条面包；一块面包；游荡|A shaped mass of bread; to spend time idly or lazily.|He bought a loaf of bread at the bakery. 他在面包店买了一条面包.  They were loafing around the mall. 他们在商场里闲逛。|Synonyms: (n.) boule, baguette, (v.) loafing, idling|Antonyms: (v.) working, toiling|noun/verb
