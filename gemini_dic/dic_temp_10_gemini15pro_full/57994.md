drivenness:/ˈdrɪvənnəs/
noun
强烈的进取心，内驱力
The state of being driven; a powerful inner motivation to achieve something.
Her drivenness led her to start her own company.  她的进取心促使她创办了自己的公司。
近义词: ambition, drive, motivation, determination
反义词: apathy, laziness, indifference
