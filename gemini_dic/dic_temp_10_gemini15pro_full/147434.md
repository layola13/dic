overobeseness:/ˌoʊvərəʊˈbiːsnəs/
n.|过度肥胖|The state of being extremely obese, beyond clinically severe obesity.|His overobeseness led to a variety of health problems. 他的过度肥胖导致了各种健康问题。|Extreme obesity, morbid obesity|Underweight, healthy weight|noun

Etymology:
"Over-" (prefix): Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi (meaning "above, over, beyond").
"Obese": Latin obesus "fat, stout," past participle of obedere "to eat away, devour," from ob "away" + edere "to eat."
"-ness" (suffix): Old English -nes(s), forming nouns of state or quality.
