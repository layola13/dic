bouldering:/ˈboʊldərɪŋ/
n.
攀石; 抱石攀岩
The sport of climbing on large rocks or artificial rock walls without ropes or harnesses, typically close to the ground.
She enjoys bouldering in her free time. 她空闲时间喜欢抱石攀岩。
rock climbing, free climbing
rappelling, abseiling
noun

boulderings:/ˈboʊldərɪŋz/
n.
攀石活动; 多次抱石攀岩 (通常指多次攀石的经历或活动)
Multiple instances or sessions of bouldering.
His weekend boulderings were always challenging and rewarding. 他周末的攀石活动总是充满挑战和收获。
rock climbing sessions, free climbing practices
