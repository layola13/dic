dibble:/ˈdɪbəl/ | n. v. |  掘穴器，移植用的铲子; 用掘穴器栽植 | A small, hand-held tool used for making holes in the ground for planting seeds or seedlings. | She used a dibble to plant the lettuce seedlings. 她用掘穴器种莴苣苗。| Synonyms: dibber, trowel, planter | Antonyms: N/A | noun
dibble:/ˈdɪbəl/ | v. | 用掘穴器栽植; 戳洞 | To plant seeds or seedlings using a dibble. | He dibbled the seeds into the prepared soil. 他用掘穴器把种子点播在准备好的土壤里。 | Synonyms: plant, sow, insert | Antonyms: uproot, remove | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dibelen, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to dab.
