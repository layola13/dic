indebtment:/ɪnˈdɛtmənt/ | n. | 负债，债务; 受恩惠; |The state of being in debt; the condition of owing something, especially money, to someone.  A feeling of gratitude or obligation. |  The company's indebtment to the bank was substantial.  这家公司欠银行的债务相当多。

The museum's indebtment to its patrons is immense.  博物馆对赞助人的感激之情是巨大的。 | 近义词：debt, obligation, liability | 反义词：credit, affluence | 词性：名词
