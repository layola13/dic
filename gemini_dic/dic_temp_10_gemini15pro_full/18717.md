bereaving: /bɪˈriːvɪŋ/
verb
使…丧失（亲人）,使…感到悲痛
depriving someone of a loved one through death, especially a close relative or friend.
The accident bereaved them of their only child. 这场事故使他们失去了唯一的孩子。
synonyms: depriving, robbing, stripping, divesting
antonyms: giving, providing, granting
词性: 动词

bereaved: /bɪˈriːvd/
adjective
丧失亲人的；痛失亲友的
(of a person) deprived of a close relation or friend through their death.
The bereaved family received many condolences.  这个痛失亲人的家庭收到了许多慰问。
synonyms: grieving, mourning, sorrowful, heartbroken
antonyms: joyful, happy, celebrating
词性: 形容词

bereavement: /bɪˈriːvmənt/
noun
丧亲之痛；丧失
the action or condition of being bereaved.
She never fully recovered from her husband's bereavement. 她从未从丧夫之痛中完全恢复过来。
synonyms: loss, grief, mourning, sorrow
antonyms: joy, happiness, celebration
词性: 名词
