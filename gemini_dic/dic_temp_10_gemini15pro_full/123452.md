monoptote:/ˈmɒnəptəʊt/ | n. |单数不变名词|A noun that has only one form for all cases, regardless of its grammatical function in a sentence.  It does not inflect for number or case.|例句：The word "sheep" is a monoptote.  “sheep”这个词是单数不变名词。

例句： "Measles" is a common example of a monoptote in English. “麻疹”是英语中单数不变名词的一个常见例子。|近义词：indeclinable noun|反义词：declensional noun, inflected noun|词性:noun
