nonshedding:/ˌnɑːnˈʃɛdɪŋ/ /ˌnɒnˈʃɛdɪŋ/
adjective
不掉毛的，不脱落的
Not shedding hair or fur; hypoallergenic.  This term is often used to describe pets, fabrics, or other materials that do not release hair or fibers easily.
例句：This breed of dog is nonshedding, making it ideal for people with allergies.  这种狗不掉毛，非常适合对过敏的人。

例句：We chose nonshedding carpet for the bedroom to minimize allergens. 我们为卧室选择了不掉毛的地毯以减少过敏原。
近义词：hypoallergenic, low-shedding
反义词：shedding
词性：adjective
