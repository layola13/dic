over-hunt:/ˌoʊvərˈhʌnt/ | v. | 过度捕猎 | To hunt a species excessively, to the point of endangering its population or causing significant ecological imbalance. | The over-hunting of whales in the 19th century nearly drove them to extinction. 19世纪对鲸鱼的过度捕猎几乎使它们灭绝。|  The indigenous people were careful not to over-hunt the deer in their territory.  土著居民小心翼翼地不在他们的领地过度捕猎鹿。 | overexploit, overfish | conserve, protect | verb
