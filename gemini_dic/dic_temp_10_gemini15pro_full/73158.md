forbearant:/fɔːrˈbɛərənt/| adj.|忍耐的，宽容的|Patient and tolerant, especially in the face of provocation or difficulty.|例句：She was forbearant with her noisy neighbors. 她对吵闹的邻居很宽容。

例句：He showed great forbearance in dealing with the difficult customer.  他在应付难缠的顾客时表现出了极大的耐心。

近义词：patient, tolerant, understanding, forgiving, indulgent, lenient|反义词：impatient, intolerant, unforgiving, vindictive|词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *forberand*, present participle of *forberen*, from Old English *forberan* (“to forbear, refrain, abstain”), from *for-* (prefix indicating prohibition or abstention) + *beran* (“to bear, endure”). Cognate with Dutch *verberen*, German *verbehren*.
