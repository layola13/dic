inhearse:/ɪnˈhɜːrs/ | vt. | 安葬；埋葬； | To place (a corpse) in a coffin. |  They inhearsed the king in a magnificent tomb. 他们将国王安葬在一座宏伟的陵墓中。

They inhearsed the body with great ceremony.  他们以隆重的仪式埋葬了遗体。 | 近义词: bury, inter, entomb | 反义词: exhume, disinter | 词性: 动词

词源: From Middle English *inhercen, from Old French enhercier (“to enclose in a chest, coffin, or shrine”), from en- + herce (“harrow”).  The original meaning referred to enclosing a body within a coffin. Over time, the meaning shifted to include the entire burial process.
