amens:/ˈɑːˈmɛnz/ /ˈeɪˈmɛnz/ | n. | 阿门；诚心所愿；祈祷的结束语 | Used after a prayer, hymn, or other religious statement, expressing solemn ratification or agreement.  It is a Hebrew word meaning "certainly," "truly," or "so be it." |  The pastor ended the service with a resounding "Amen." 牧师以响亮的“阿门”结束了仪式。

The congregation responded with a chorus of "Amens."  会众齐声回应“阿门”。 | 近义词: so be it | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, as it's an affirmation) | 词性: noun
