incorruptibilities:/ˌɪnkəˌrʌptɪˈbɪlɪtiz/
noun
不可腐败性；廉洁性；正直
The quality of being incorruptible; inability to be corrupted, especially by bribery.
His incorruptibility made him an ideal candidate for the position. 他的廉洁使他成为该职位的理想人选。
Synonyms: integrity, honesty, probity, uprightness, righteousness
Antonyms: corruptibility, dishonesty, venality, depravity
