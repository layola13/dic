huggingly:/ˈhʌɡɪŋli/| adv.|拥抱地，亲昵地|In a way that involves hugging; affectionately.|She held the teddy bear huggingly. 她亲昵地抱着泰迪熊。|近义词：affectionately, tenderly, warmly, lovingly|反义词：coldly, distantly|词性:副词

词源: From hug (v.) + -ing + -ly.  "Hug" comes from the Old Norse word "hugga" meaning to comfort, soothe.
