overgovern:/ˌoʊvərˈɡʌvərn/
verb
过度管理，管制过严
to govern excessively or too strictly
The city is overgoverned, with too many regulations stifling businesses.  这个城市管理过度，过多的规章制度扼杀了商业。
The school was overgoverned, with strict rules about everything from dress code to lunch breaks.  这所学校管理过严，从着装到午休，所有事情都有严格的规定。
synonyms: overregulate, overcontrol
antonyms: undergovern, deregulate
v.
