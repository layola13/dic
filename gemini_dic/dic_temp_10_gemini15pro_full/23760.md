braving:/ˈbreɪvɪŋ/| v.|勇敢面对，冒（风险），经受（风雨）|Facing or enduring something difficult or dangerous with courage.|例句：They were braving the blizzard to get home.  他们冒着暴风雪回家。|近义词：enduring, facing, withstanding, confronting|反义词:avoiding, shrinking from, fearing|词性:动词(present participle of "brave")

词源: From Middle English *braven, from Old French braver (“to boast, to defy”), of uncertain origin. Perhaps ultimately of Germanic origin, from Proto-Germanic *brōaną (“to swell, to sprout”).
