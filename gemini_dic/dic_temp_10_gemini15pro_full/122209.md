misvalue:/ˌmɪsˈvæljuː/
verb|错误估价；低估；看轻|To assess something incorrectly, especially to underestimate its value or importance.|The company misvalued the property, leading to a significant financial loss.  公司对房产估价错误，导致了严重的经济损失。|The painting was misvalued and sold for far less than it was worth. 这幅画被低估了，售价远低于其实际价值。|undervalue, underestimate|overvalue, overestimate|v.

misvalue:/ˌmɪsˈvæljuː/
noun|估价错误；低估|An incorrect assessment of something's value, especially an underestimation.|The misvalue of the company's assets contributed to its downfall.  公司资产的错误估值导致了它的垮台。|There was a significant misvalue in the initial appraisal of the jewelry.  最初对珠宝的评估存在严重的估价错误。|undervaluation, underestimation|overvaluation, overestimation|n.
