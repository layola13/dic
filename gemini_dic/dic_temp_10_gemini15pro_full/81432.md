gratelike: /ˈɡreɪtˌlaɪk/
adjective
像格栅的；类似格栅的
Resembling a grate; having the form or appearance of a grate.
The building featured a gratelike façade, with narrow vertical windows separated by metal bars.  这栋建筑的正面像格栅一样，狭窄的垂直窗户被金属条隔开。
The spider spun a gratelike web between the branches. 蜘蛛在树枝间结了一张像格栅一样的网。
近义词: gridlike, latticelike, meshlike
反义词: solid, unbroken, continuous
词性: adjective

词源:  "grate" (n.) + "-like" (suffix).  "Grate" comes from Old French "grate" meaning "grating," ultimately from Latin "craticula," diminutive of "crates" meaning "wickerwork, hurdle."  "-like" is a native English suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
