fervency:/ˈfɜːvənsi/ /ˈfɜːrvənsi/| n. |热情；热烈；炽热 |The state of being fervent; warmth of feeling; ardor; zeal. |She spoke with a fervency that surprised everyone. 她说话的热情令所有人惊讶。|
例句：
The preacher delivered his sermon with great fervency. 传教士非常热情地布道。
The fervency of her belief was inspiring. 她信仰的热诚令人鼓舞。

近义词：passion, ardor, zeal, enthusiasm, eagerness
反义词：apathy, indifference, coldness, detachment
词性：noun
