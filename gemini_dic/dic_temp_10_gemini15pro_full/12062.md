artifice:/ˈɑːrtɪfɪs/| n.|技巧，诡计，人为的产物|skill in designing or making something; clever or cunning devices or expedients, especially as used to trick or deceive others; something made or produced by human beings rather than occurring naturally|例句：The magician's performance was full of artifice. 魔术师的表演充满了技巧。

例句：He won the election through political artifice. 他通过政治诡计赢得了选举。|近义词：trickery, deception, cunning, skill, craft, contrivance, ingenuity|反义词：honesty, sincerity, naturalness, artlessness|noun
