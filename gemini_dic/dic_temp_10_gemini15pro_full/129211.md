nobly:/ˈnoʊbli/| adv.|高尚地，崇高地|In a noble manner; with high moral principles and ideals.  She acted nobly in defending the weak.|近义词：honorably, virtuously, courageously, gallantly|反义词：basely, ignobly, dishonorably|词性:副词
Etymology: From Middle English *noblely*, from Old French *noblement*, from Latin *nōbilis* (“noble”) + *-ment* (suffix forming adverbs).
