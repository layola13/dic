accompliceship: /əˈkʌmplɪsʃɪp/
n.
共犯关系；合谋
The state of being an accomplice; partnership or involvement in wrongdoing.
He denied any accompliceship in the robbery. 他否认参与了这起抢劫案。

Their accompliceship in the crime was undeniable. 他们在犯罪中的共谋关系是不可否认的。


近义词:
collusion, conspiracy, complicity, partnership in crime
反义词:
innocence, non-involvement


词性:
noun


词源:
From accomplice + -ship.  Accomplice comes from French complice, from Latin complex, complicis  "partner in crime," from com- "with, together" + plic- "fold, intertwine."


USAGE->
