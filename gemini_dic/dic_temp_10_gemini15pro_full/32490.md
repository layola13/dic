chinglish:/ˈtʃɪŋlɪʃ/
noun
中式英语
A blend of Chinese and English, typically referring to English influenced by Chinese vocabulary, grammar, or pronunciation, often considered non-standard or humorous.
This sentence is full of Chinglish.  这句话充满了中式英语。
近义词:broken English, pidgin English
反义词:Standard English
