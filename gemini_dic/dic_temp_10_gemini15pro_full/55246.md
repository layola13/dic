dissimilitude's:/ˌdɪsɪˈmɪlɪtjuːdz/
noun
不同, 相异, 差异
lack of resemblance or similarity
The dissimilitude's between the twins surprised everyone. 这对双胞胎的不同之处让每个人都感到惊讶。
The dissimilitude's between the two cultures are significant.  这两种文化之间的差异很大。

近义词: difference, dissimilarity, unlikeness, divergence, disparity
反义词: similarity, resemblance, likeness, similitude

词性: noun (名词)

词源:  From Late Latin *dissimilitudo, from dissimilis (“unlike”) + -tudo (“-ness”).  Dissimilis is from dis- (“not”) + similis (“like”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *sem- (“one; as one, together with”).
