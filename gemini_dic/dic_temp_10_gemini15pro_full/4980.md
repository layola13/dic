alkalize: /ˈælkəlaɪz/ | v. | 碱化; 使成碱性 | To make something alkaline, or more alkaline; to increase its pH. |  Baking soda can be used to alkalize the soil.  小苏打可以用来碱化土壤。

| Example:  She alkalized the water with baking soda. 她用小苏打碱化了水。


| Synonym: neutralize, basify | Antonym: acidify, acidulate | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From alkali + -ize. Alkali ultimately from Arabic *al-qalīy* "ashes of the saltwort plant," used in making soap.
