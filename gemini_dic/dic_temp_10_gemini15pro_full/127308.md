navy-blue:/ˈneɪvi bluː/| adj.|海军蓝|A dark blue colour, the colour of a navy uniform.|She wore a navy-blue dress to the wedding. 她穿着一件海军蓝的连衣裙去参加婚礼。|近义词：dark blue, sapphire, indigo|反义词:light blue, pale blue|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "navy" (referring to the naval forces) + "blue".  The term became popular in the mid-19th century, after the British Royal Navy adopted the colour for its officers' uniforms.
