charmlessly:/ˈtʃɑːrmləsli/ | adv. | 缺乏魅力地，枯燥无味地 | Without charm; in a way that lacks charm or appeal. | He spoke charmlessly about his accomplishments, leaving the audience unimpressed. 他谈论自己的成就时毫无魅力，给听众留下了不好的印象。 | She delivered the speech charmlessly, failing to connect with the audience.  她演讲时缺乏魅力，没能与观众产生共鸣。 | 近义词: dully, uninterestingly, flatly, vapidly | 反义词: charmingly, appealingly, engagingly | 词性: 副词

词源:
From "charmless" + "-ly".  "Charmless" comes from "charm" (from Old French "charme," ultimately from Latin "carmen," meaning "song, incantation") + "-less" (a suffix indicating lack of something).
