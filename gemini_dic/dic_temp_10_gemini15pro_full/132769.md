nondrivable:/ˌnɒnˈdraɪvəbl/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈdraɪvəbəl/ (UK)
adjective
不可驾驶的
Not suitable or safe to be driven.
This car is nondrivable because of the flat tire. 这辆车因为爆胎而无法驾驶。
The roads are nondrivable after the blizzard. 暴风雪过后，道路无法通行。
Near Synonyms: undrivable, inoperable, unusable
Antonyms: drivable, operable, roadworthy
词性: adjective
