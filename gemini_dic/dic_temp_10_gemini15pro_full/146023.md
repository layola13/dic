overdramatically:/ˌoʊvərˌdrəmˈætɪkli/| adv.|过分戏剧化地，夸张地|In a way that is excessively dramatic or exaggerated.  It emphasizes an overly theatrical or exaggerated manner of expression or behavior.|She reacted overdramatically to the news, bursting into tears and fainting. 她对这个消息反应过度，突然大哭起来然后晕倒了。|例句：He sighed overdramatically and slumped onto the sofa.  他夸张地叹了口气，倒在沙发上。|近义词：theatrically, histrionically, exaggeratedly, melodramatically|反义词：subtly, understatedly, realistically|词性: 副词
