cumacea:/kuːˈmeɪsiə/ | n. | 囊虾类 | An order of small, shrimp-like crustaceans that are parasitic on other marine invertebrates.  They are characterized by their reduced abdomen and enlarged carapace, which often encloses the entire body. | Example: Cumacea are found in a variety of marine habitats, from shallow coastal waters to the deep sea. 囊虾类存在于各种海洋栖息地，从浅海沿岸水域到深海。

Example:  Researchers have identified several new species of cumacea in recent years. 研究人员近年来发现了几个囊虾类新物种。| 近义词:  (No direct synonyms, but related groups include isopods and amphipods) | 反义词: None | 词性: Noun
