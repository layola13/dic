inconsolable: /ˌɪnkənˈsoʊləbl/
|adj.|  不可安慰的，悲痛欲绝的 | She was inconsolable after the death of her pet.  她的宠物死后，她悲痛欲绝。 | He was inconsolable when his team lost the championship.  他的球队输掉了冠军赛，他伤心欲绝。 | 近义词: heartbroken, devastated, grief-stricken, distraught | 反义词: comforted, soothed, consoled | 词性: 形容词

console: /kənˈsoʊl/
|v.| 安慰，慰藉 | I tried to console her after her loss. 我试图在她失去亲人后安慰她。 |  He consoled himself with the thought that he had done his best. 他用尽力了的念头来安慰自己。| 近义词: comfort, soothe, solace | 反义词: distress, upset, grieve | 词性: 动词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *inconsolabilis, from in- (“not”) + consolabilis (“capable of being consoled”), from consolari (“to console”).
