geoscopy:/dʒiːˈɒskəpi/
n.
地球透视学；地球勘探学
The study of the earth by means of direct observation or by other methods such as seismic waves or radioactivity.  对地球的直接观测研究或通过其他方法如地震波或放射性进行的研究。
Geoscopy helped locate the underground oil reservoir. 地球透视学帮助定位了地下油库。
近义词: geophysics, earth science
反义词: (No direct antonym exists)
词性: noun

geoscopy:/dʒiːˈɒskəpi/
词源:  From geo- "earth" + -scopy "viewing, examination".  源于geo- “地球” + -scopy “观察，检查”。
词性: noun
