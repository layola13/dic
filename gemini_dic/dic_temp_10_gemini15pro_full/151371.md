parlay:/ˈpɑːrleɪ/ (US), /ˈpɑːleɪ/ (UK)| v. n.|使(赢来的钱)成倍增加; 连本带利再赌; 扩充，增加;  利用…获得更大的成就|To bet the winnings from one bet on a subsequent bet, or to use an initial success to achieve something greater.|He parlayed his small inheritance into a fortune. 他把他少量的遗产变成了巨额财富。|近义词：capitalize, leverage, boost, multiply, augment|反义词：squander, waste, lose|词性:动词, 名词

parlay:/ˈpɑːrleɪ/ (US), /ˈpɑːleɪ/ (UK)| n.|连本带利的赌博; 累积的赌注|A bet in which the winnings from one bet are staked on a subsequent bet.|He placed a parlay on the next three races. 他在接下来的三场比赛中下了连本带利的赌注。|近义词：accumulator, multiple bet|反义词：single bet|词性:名词
