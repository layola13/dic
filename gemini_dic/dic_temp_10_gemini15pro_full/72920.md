fonde:/fɒnd/ (UK), /fɑːnd/ (US)| adj.|喜欢的；爱好的；溺爱的|Having an affection or liking for.|例句：He's very fond of his niece. 他非常疼爱他的侄女。|近义词：affectionate, loving, doting|反义词：aloof, indifferent, uncaring|词性:形容词
词源: From Old French *fondre "to melt, dissolve," from Latin fundere "to pour, melt." The figurative sense of "having tender feelings" arose from the notion of one's heart melting with affection.
