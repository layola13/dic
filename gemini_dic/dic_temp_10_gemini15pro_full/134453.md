nonitemizers:/ˌnɒnˈaɪtəmaɪzərz/ | n. | 非逐项扣除者 |  People who choose the standard deduction instead of itemizing deductions on their tax returns.  These individuals do not list specific deductions like charitable contributions or medical expenses but opt for a fixed amount set by the government. |  Many nonitemizers find the standard deduction simpler and more beneficial than itemizing. 许多非逐项扣除者发现标准扣除比逐项扣除更简单、更有利。

Nonitemizers may miss out on potential tax savings if their itemized deductions would have exceeded the standard deduction.  如果他们的逐项扣除额超过了标准扣除额，非逐项扣除者可能会错过潜在的税收节省。 | 近义词: standard deduction filers | 反义词: itemizers | 词性: Noun
