deathliest:/ˈdedliɪst/ | adj. | 最致命的 | Causing or most likely to cause death; most deadly. |  The Black Death was one of the deathliest pandemics in history. 黑死病是历史上最致命的流行病之一。

The deathliest weapon in their arsenal was a nerve gas. 他们军火库中最致命的武器是神经毒气。| 近义词: most deadly, most lethal, fatal, pernicious | 反义词: safest, healthiest, life-giving | 词性: 形容词
