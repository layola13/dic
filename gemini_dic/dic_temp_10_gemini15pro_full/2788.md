what /wɒt; hwɒt (UK), wʌt; hwʌt (US)/ |pron. det. adv.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information about something; used to express surprise or disbelief.|

例句：
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？

What time is it? = 现在几点了？


近义词：which, how
反义词:|无|
词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English _hwæt_, neuter of _hwā_  ("who"),  from Proto-Germanic  _*hwat_, from Proto-Indo-European  _*kʷod_. Cognate with Scots _what_, West Frisian _wat_, Dutch _wat_, German _was_, Danish and Swedish _vad_, Icelandic _hvað_.


USAGE->
一般现在时:
What do you think? = 你怎么想？
What does he do for a living? = 他做什么工作？

一般过去时:
What did you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃了什么？
What time did she arrive? = 她几点到的？

现在完成时:
What have you been doing all day? = 你一整天都在做什么？
What has he learned from this experience? = 他从这次经历中学到了什么？

过去完成时:
What had you done before I called? = 在我打电话之前你做了什么？
What had they agreed upon before the meeting? = 会议前他们达成了什么协议？

将来时:
What will you do this weekend? = 你这周末要做什么？
What time will the train arrive? = 火车几点到？

虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
I wish I knew what he was thinking. = 我希望我知道他在想什么。

被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了些什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是哪里？
What are your plans for the future? = 你对未来有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
There's nothing what I want. = 没有我想要的东西。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/你好吗？
What about...? = ...怎么样？/ ...怎么办？
What if...? = 如果...怎么办？
What for? = 为了什么？/为什么？
So what? = 那又怎样？
