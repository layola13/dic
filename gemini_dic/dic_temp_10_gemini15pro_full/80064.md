goalmouths:/ˈɡoʊlˌmaʊðz/ /ˈɡəʊlˌmaʊθs/
noun
球门（复数）
The area directly in front of and between the goalposts.
The striker blasted the ball wide of the goalmouths.  这位前锋将球射偏，飞出了球门。
The goalkeeper bravely defended the goalmouths.  守门员英勇地守卫着球门。
Synonyms: goals, nets
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "field" or "pitch" in a broad sense)
词性: 名词
