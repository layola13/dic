guesswork:/ˈɡɛswɜːk/| n.|猜测，臆测|Conjecture without any sure facts or evidence.|His theory is based mostly on guesswork. 他的理论主要建立在猜测之上。|近义词：conjecture, speculation, supposition, surmise|反义词：certainty, knowledge, fact|词性:名词

词源: From guess + work.  First recorded in the late 18th century.
