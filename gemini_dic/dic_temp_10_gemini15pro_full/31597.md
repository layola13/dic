chasteness:/ˈtʃeɪst.nəs/| n.|贞洁，纯洁，朴素|The state or practice of refraining from sexual intercourse, especially outside of marriage.  A simple or restrained style of dress or behavior.|She valued her chasteness above all else. 她视自己的贞洁高于一切。|Synonyms: purity, virtue, innocence, modesty, simplicity|Antonyms: lewdness, promiscuity, immodesty, impurity|noun
