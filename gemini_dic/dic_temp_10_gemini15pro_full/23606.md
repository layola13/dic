braminic:/brəˈmɪnɪk/| adj.|婆罗门的，僧侣的|Relating to or characteristic of Brahmins or their culture; priestly.|The braminic rituals were complex and elaborate. 婆罗门仪式复杂而精细。|近义词：priestly, sacerdotal|反义词:secular, lay|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Sanskrit *brāhmaṇa-* "relating to a Brahmin," from *bráhman-* "prayer, devotion, sacred knowledge."
