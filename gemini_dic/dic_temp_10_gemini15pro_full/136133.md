nonreflectiveness:/ˌnɒnˌrɪˈflɛktɪvnəs/ /ˌnɑːnˌriːˈflektɪvnəs/
n.
无反射性；不反省
The nonreflectiveness of the surface made it difficult to see. 表面的无反射性使它难以被看到。
Lack of reflection or introspection.  The nonreflectiveness of his personality meant he never dwelled on his mistakes. 他性格的不反省意味着他从不仔细思考自己的错误。
近义词:unreflectiveness, opacity
反义词:reflectiveness, reflectivity
词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (not) + reflective + -ness (forming nouns expressing state or quality).  "Reflective" comes from Late Latin reflectīvus, from reflectere (to bend back), from re- (back) + flectere (to bend).
