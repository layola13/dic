ifa:/ˈɪfə/| n.| 伊法，一种西非的占卜系统|Ifa is a system of divination and spiritual tradition practiced by the Yoruba people of Nigeria and other parts of West Africa.| 伊法是尼日利亚和其他西非地区约鲁巴人实行的一种占卜和精神传统体系。|近义词：divination, oracle|反义词: N/A|词性: 名词

Etymology: From Yoruba *ìfá*.
