outsize:/ˈaʊtsaɪz/| adj.|特大的，超大的|Exceeding the usual or expected size.  Something that is larger than the standard or regular size.|例句：She bought an outsize sweater for extra comfort. 她买了一件特大号的毛衣，为了更加舒适。

例句：The company made an outsize profit this year.  公司今年获得了巨额利润。|近义词：oversized, enormous, huge, gigantic, massive|反义词：small, tiny, miniature, undersized|词性:形容词

词源:  "out-" (outside, beyond) + "size" (dimensions).  From the early 20th century, combining the prefix "out-" indicating exceeding or surpassing, with "size" to denote dimensions.
