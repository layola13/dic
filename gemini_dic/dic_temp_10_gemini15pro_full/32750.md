chlorosis:/kləˈroʊsɪs/
noun
萎黄病，变色病
An abnormal condition of plants in which the leaves produce insufficient chlorophyll, resulting in pale or yellow coloring.
The chlorosis of the plant was evident in its yellowing leaves.  这种植物的萎黄病在其变黄的叶子上很明显。
chlorosis:/kləˈroʊsɪs/
noun
缺绿症
A type of anemia characterized by pale yellowish-green skin.
The doctor diagnosed her with chlorosis due to iron deficiency. 医生诊断她患有缺铁性缺绿症。

近义词:blanching, yellowing, etiolation, anaemia
反义词: healthy green color,  robustness
