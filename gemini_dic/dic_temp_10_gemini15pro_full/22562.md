bootability:/ˌbuːtəˈbɪləti/
n.
可启动性
The capability of a system or device to start up or boot successfully.
The bootability of the USB drive was compromised after the virus attack.  U盘的可启动性在病毒攻击后受到了损害。

近义词:startability, boot capability
反义词:unbootable
