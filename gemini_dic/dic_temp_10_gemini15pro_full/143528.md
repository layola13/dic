outgrow: /ˌaʊtˈɡroʊ/ (US) /ˌaʊtˈɡrəʊ/ (UK)| v. | 长大不再需要；长得比…大；发展得超过 | To grow too large for (something, typically clothing); to grow taller or faster than; to mature beyond or lose interest in (something). |  My son has outgrown all his clothes. 我的儿子已经长得所有衣服都穿不上了。

She soon outgrew her childish fears. 她很快就克服了童年时的恐惧。| 近义词: surpass, exceed, outstrip | 反义词: shrink, dwindle | 词性: 动词
