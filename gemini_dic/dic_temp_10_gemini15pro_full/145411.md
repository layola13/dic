overcensors:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsərz/ | v. | 过度审查 | To censor excessively, often to the point of suppressing important or legitimate information. | The government overcensors the media, preventing citizens from accessing unbiased news. 政府过度审查媒体，阻止公民获得公正的新闻。| The platform overcensors user-generated content, stifling free speech. 该平台过度审查用户生成的内容，扼杀了言论自由。 |  近义词: overregulates, suppresses, restricts | 反义词: undercensors, permits, allows | 词性: 动词

overcensor:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsər/ | n. | 过度审查者 | A person or entity that censors excessively. | The overcensor deleted any comment that remotely criticized the government.  过度审查者删除了任何稍微批评政府的评论。 |  The news organization acted as an overcensor, withholding crucial information from the public.  这家新闻机构充当了过度审查者的角色，对公众隐瞒了关键信息。 | 近义词: censor, regulator, suppressor | 反义词: liberator, advocate | 词性: 名词
