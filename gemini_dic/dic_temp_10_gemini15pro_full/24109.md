brew-up:/ˈbruː ʌp/ | n. | 困境，麻烦 | A difficult or problematic situation. | "We've got a bit of a brew-up on our hands with the new software."  我们新的软件出了点问题。 | synonyms: problem, difficulty, mess, predicament | antonyms: solution, resolution, advantage | noun
