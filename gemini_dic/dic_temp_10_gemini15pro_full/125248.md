multidimensionally:/ˌmʌltiˌdaɪˈmɛnʃənəli/| adv.|多维地，多方面地|In a way that involves multiple dimensions or aspects.  Considering something from multiple perspectives or factors.|例句：We need to approach this problem multidimensionally, considering social, economic, and environmental factors. 我们需要多方面地处理这个问题，考虑社会、经济和环境因素。

例句：She analyzes literature multidimensionally, exploring themes, character development, and historical context. 她多维度地分析文学作品，探讨主题、人物发展和历史背景。|近义词：comprehensively, broadly, in many ways|反义词：narrowly, one-dimensionally, simply|词性:副词
