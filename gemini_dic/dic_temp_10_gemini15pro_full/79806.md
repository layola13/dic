glycocin: /ɡlaɪˈkəʊsɪn/ | n. | 糖精，甘氨酸 | A bacteriocin produced by a strain of Streptococcus lactis. It is a small protein that inhibits the growth of other bacteria, particularly closely related strains. | Glycocin A is a potent antimicrobial peptide. 糖精A是一种有效的抗菌肽。| Glycocin F is effective against Listeria monocytogenes. 糖精F对单核细胞增生李斯特菌有效。|  bacteriocin, antimicrobial peptide |  | noun
