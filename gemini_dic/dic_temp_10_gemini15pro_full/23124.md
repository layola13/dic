bourgeoisifies:/ˌbʊərʒwɑːzɪfaɪz/
verb
使资产阶级化；使中产阶级化
To cause to become bourgeois; to give a middle-class character to.
The new development bourgeoisified the formerly working-class neighborhood.  这个新的开发项目使以前工人阶级的社区变得资产阶级化了。
Rapid gentrification has bourgeoisified the area, pushing out long-term residents. 快速的贵族化使该地区变得资产阶级化，迫使长期居民搬离。
Synonym: gentrify, yuppify
Antonym: proletarianize, radicalize
词性: 动词
