diprismatic:/daɪprɪzˈmætɪk/| adj.|双棱柱的，有两棱柱的|Having the form of a diprism; relating to a diprism. A diprism is a geometric solid formed by joining two prisms together.  It is characterized by having two sets of parallel faces.|The mineral exhibited a diprismatic form.  这种矿物呈现出双棱柱状。|The crystal structure is diprismatic. 晶体结构是双棱柱形的。|prismatic, dipyramidal| - |adjective
diprismatic:/daɪprɪzˈmætɪk/| n.|双棱柱体|A geometric solid formed by joining two prisms together.|The diprismatic shape was fascinating to study.  研究这个双棱柱体形状很吸引人。|The geologist found a rare diprismatic crystal. 地质学家发现了一块罕见的双棱柱晶体。|prism, dipyramid| - |noun
