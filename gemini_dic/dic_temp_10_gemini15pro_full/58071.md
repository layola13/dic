droningly:/ˈdroʊnɪŋli/ | adv. | 单调地，沉闷地 |  In a monotonous, dull, and tedious manner, often like the sound of a drone. | He spoke droningly for an hour about the company's history. 他单调地讲了一个小时关于公司历史的事情。|  The lecturer droningly read from his notes. 演讲者沉闷地照着笔记念。| 近义词：monotonously, tediously, dully | 反义词：interestingly, engagingly, excitedly | 词性: 副词

词源: From drone + -ing + -ly.  "Drone" itself comes from Old English *drān, of imitative origin.
