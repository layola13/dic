cylindricalness:/səˌlɪnˈdrɪkəl.nəs/
n.
圆柱状，圆柱形
The state or quality of being cylindrical.
The cylindricalness of the pipe made it easy to roll. 这根管子的圆柱形状使它很容易滚动。
roundness, circularity
squareness, angularity
noun
