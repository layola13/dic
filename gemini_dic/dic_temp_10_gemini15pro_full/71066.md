first-footer:/ˈfɜːstˈfʊtər/ | n. | 第一访客；新年第一位访客 | The first-footer is traditionally a dark-haired man who brings gifts such as coal, salt, shortbread, and whisky.  新年第一位访客传统上是一位带礼物的深色头发的男士，礼物例如煤炭、盐、酥饼和威士忌。 |例句：It's considered good luck to have a dark-haired man as your first-footer.  有一个深色头发的男士作为你的新年第一位访客被认为是好运的。

例句：My uncle was our first-footer this year, bringing us a bottle of whisky. 我叔叔是今年我们的新年第一位访客，他给我们带来了一瓶威士忌。 | 近义词: first guest, initial visitor | 反义词: last guest | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From 'first' + 'foot' + '-er'. 'First' denotes the initial instance. 'Foot' refers to setting foot in a dwelling. '-er' forms the noun denoting the person who performs the action.  由“first”+“foot”+“-er”组成。“First”表示最初的。“Foot”指的是踏入住所。“-er”构成表示执行动作的人的名词。
