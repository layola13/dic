alleluia: /ˌælɪˈluːjə/ (US) /ˌælɪˈluːjə/ (UK)| n. |哈利路亚；赞美上帝|A song or shout of praise to God.|The choir sang a beautiful alleluia.  唱诗班唱了一首优美的哈利路亚。| 近义词: praise, hallelujah | 反义词: curse, blasphemy | 词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *alleluia, from Greek ἀλληλούϊα (allēlouia), from Hebrew הַלְלוּיָהּ (haleluyah), meaning "praise ye Yah" (Yah being a shortened form of Yahweh, the name of God).
