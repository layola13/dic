emmew:/ˈɛmjuː/
Verb
拥抱，搂抱 (chiefly dialectal, Scotland)
To embrace; cuddle.
例句：She emmewed her bairn tightly. 她紧紧地拥抱着她的孩子。
例句：He emmewed his wife after a long day at work.  下班后，他拥抱了他的妻子。

近义词：hug, cuddle, embrace
反义词：repel, push away
词性：v.
