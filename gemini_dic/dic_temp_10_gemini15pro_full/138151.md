northernnesses:/ˈnɔːrðərnɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
北方性，北方特质
The quality or state of being northern; the northern characteristics of something.
The painting captured the northernnesses of the landscape, the stark beauty of the icy plains. 这幅画捕捉到了这片风景的北方特质，即冰冷平原的荒凉之美。

The northernnesses of the climate are evident in the short growing season.  气候的北方性在短暂的生长季节中显而易见。

近义词:northern character, northern qualities
反义词:southernnesses, southern qualities
词性:名词


词源:
From northern + -ness (suffix forming nouns indicating a state, quality, or degree) + -es (plural suffix).  源自northern（北方的）+ -ness（后缀，构成表示状态、性质或程度的名词）+ -es（复数后缀）。
