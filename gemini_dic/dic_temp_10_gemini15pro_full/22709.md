borer:/ˈbɔːrər/ (US), /ˈbɔːrə/ (UK)| n.|钻孔者，钻孔器，蛀虫|A tool or insect that bores holes, typically in wood or other plant material.  A person or thing that causes boredom.|The carpenter used a borer to make holes for the dowels. 木匠用钻孔器钻孔以便插入销钉。

The emerald ash borer is devastating ash trees across North America.  北美翡翠灰螟正在摧毁梣树。

His lectures were so dry, he was a real borer. 他的讲座枯燥乏味，他真是个令人厌烦的人。|近义词:drill, auger, worm, pest|反义词:filler (for tools),  none (for insects/people) |Noun
