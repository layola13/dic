paraxanthine: /ˌpærəˈzænθiːn/ | n. | 仲黄嘌呤 | A dimethylxanthine, C7H8N4O2, that is a metabolite of caffeine in humans and other animals. It is a central nervous system stimulant and a weaker competitive nonselective phosphodiesterase inhibitor than caffeine. | Paraxanthine is the main metabolite of caffeine in humans. 仲黄嘌呤是人体内咖啡因的主要代谢物。| Paraxanthine contributes to the stimulating effects of caffeine. 仲黄嘌呤有助于咖啡因的刺激作用。 | synonyms: 1,7-dimethylxanthine | antonyms: None | noun
