fearlessnesses:/ˈfɪərləsnəsɪz/
n.
无畏；勇敢；大胆
The state of being fearless; multiple instances of fearlessness.  This plural form is uncommonly used.
His fearlessnesses in the face of danger were legendary.  他面对危险时的多次无畏表现已成为传奇。
近义词：braveries, intrepidities, audacities
反义词：fears, timidities, cowardices
词性: 名词


fearlessness:/ˈfɪərləsnəs/
n.
无畏；勇敢；大胆
The state of being fearless; lack of fear.
She approached the challenge with fearlessness. 她无所畏惧地迎接挑战。
近义词：bravery, intrepidity, audacity
反义词：fear, timidity, cowardice
词性: 名词


fearless:/ˈfɪərləs/
adj.
无畏的；勇敢的；大胆的
Without fear; brave.
He was a fearless warrior. 他是一位无畏的战士。
近义词：brave, intrepid, audacious
反义词：fearful, timid, cowardly
词性: 形容词

词源:
From Old English *fǣrlēas (unattested), from fǣr (“fear”) +‎ -lēas (“-less”). Cognate with Dutch *vaarloos (Middle Dutch vareloos), German *farlos (Middle High German varlôs).  More literally, "without fear."
