confessing:/kənˈfɛsɪŋ/
v.|忏悔; 承认; 坦白|The act of admitting or acknowledging something, typically a fault, wrongdoing, or secret.|He confessed to stealing the money. 他承认偷了钱。|admitting, acknowledging, owning up, divulging|denying, concealing, hiding|verb

confessing:/kənˈfɛsɪŋ/
adj.|忏悔的；坦白的|Used to describe someone in the act of confessing.|With a confessing look, she admitted her mistake. 她带着忏悔的表情承认了自己的错误。|admitting, repentant, contrite|unrepentant, unapologetic|adjective

词源：confessing 源自拉丁语 confessus，是 confiteri 的过去分词，意思是“承认，坦白”。  Confiteri 由 con-（一起，完全）和 fateri（承认，宣称）组成。
