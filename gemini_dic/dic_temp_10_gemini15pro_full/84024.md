half-elizabethan:/ˌhɑːf ɪˌlɪzəˈbiːθən/
adj.
半伊丽莎白时代的
Relating to or characteristic of the latter half of the reign of Queen Elizabeth I (1558-1603), often implying a transitional style or period between the high Renaissance and the early Baroque.
The architecture of that building has a distinct half-Elizabethan feel to it. 那座建筑的结构有一种明显的半伊丽莎白时代的感觉。
Near synonyms: Late Elizabethan, Jacobean
Antonyms: Early Elizabethan, Tudor
