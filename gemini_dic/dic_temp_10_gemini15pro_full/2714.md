adsorbent:/ædˈsɔːrbənt/ | n. adj. | 吸附剂；吸附的 | A substance that absorbs another substance.  | Activated charcoal is a common adsorbent used in water filters.  活性炭是水过滤器中常用的吸附剂。

The adsorbent material effectively removed the pollutants from the water.  这种吸附材料有效地去除了水中的污染物。 | 近义词: absorber, sorbent | 反义词:  desorbent | 词性: 名词, 形容词
