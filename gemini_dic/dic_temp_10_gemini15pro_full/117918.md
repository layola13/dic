melting:/ˈmɛltɪŋ/ | adj. n.| 融化的；熔化的；感人的 |The process of changing from a solid to a liquid state, typically by heating. Also, causing strong emotional feelings, typically of tenderness or sadness.  | The melting snow caused flooding. 融化的雪造成了洪水。| synonyms: thawing, dissolving, liquefying, softening, moving | antonyms: freezing, solidifying, hardening | adjective

melting:/ˈmɛltɪŋ/ | n.| 融化；熔化物 |The process of becoming liquid or the product of this process.| The melting of the glaciers is a serious concern. 冰川的融化是一个严重的问题。| synonyms: liquefaction, fusion, thawing | antonyms: freezing, solidification | noun
