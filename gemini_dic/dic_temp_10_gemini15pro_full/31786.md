checkering:/ˈtʃɛkərɪŋ/ | n. | 格子花纹，方格图案 | The act or process of marking with checkers or squares.  A pattern of squares or rectangles, often used for decoration or grip. | The gunstock features fine checkering for a secure grip.  枪托上有精细的格子花纹，可以确保握持牢固。 | synonyms: crosshatching, grid, lattice | antonyms: plainness, smoothness | noun

checkered:/ˈtʃɛkərd/ | adj. | 格子花纹的，多变的，盛衰荣辱交替的 | Marked with checkers or squares. Varied; marked by changes in fortune. | He wore a checkered shirt. 他穿了一件格子衬衫。 | She had a checkered past. 她有着坎坷的过去。 | synonyms: variegated, mottled, varied, irregular | antonyms: uniform, consistent, plain | adjective
