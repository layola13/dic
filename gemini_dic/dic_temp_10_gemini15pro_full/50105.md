dentilingual:/ˌdɛntiˈlɪŋɡwəl/
adjective
齿舌的；与齿舌有关的
Relating to the radula, a ribbon-like structure covered with teeth that is found in the mouths of most mollusks, used for scraping or cutting food.
Mollusks use their dentilingual apparatus for feeding.  软体动物用它们的齿舌器官进食。
The dentilingual structure varies greatly among different mollusk species. 不同种类软体动物的齿舌结构差异很大。
近义词: radular
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-radular")
词性: adjective
