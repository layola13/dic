bibracteate: /ˌbaɪˈbræktiət/
adjective
苞叶状的; 有苞片的
Having or bearing bracts.
The bibracteate flower has two bracts.  这朵苞叶状的花有两片苞片。
Near Synonyms: bracteate, bracted
Antonyms: ebracteate
adjective
