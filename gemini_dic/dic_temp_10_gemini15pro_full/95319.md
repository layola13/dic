incaved:/ɪnˈkeɪvd/| adj.|凹陷的，内凹的|Curved inward; hollowed.|The weathered rock face was incaved, creating small shelters.|近义词：concave, hollowed, indented, recessed|反义词：convex, protruding, bulging|词性:形容词
Etymology: From in- + cave + -ed.  "In-" indicates "inward" or "into," while "cave" refers to a hollow space. The suffix "-ed" transforms the noun "cave" into an adjective.
