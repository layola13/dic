herd:/hɜːrd/ (British) /hɝːd/ (American)| n. v. |兽群，人群；放牧|A large group of animals, especially hoofed mammals, that live together or are kept together as livestock; to gather or move (animals or people) as a group. |The shepherd herded the sheep into the pen. 牧羊人把羊群赶进羊圈。|Synonyms: flock, drove, pack, crowd, throng |Antonyms: individual, scatter, disperse| noun, verb
