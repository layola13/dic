nombles:/ˈnʌmbəlz/ | n. | 内脏，尤指鹿的内脏 | The nombles were considered a delicacy in medieval times.  内脏在中世纪被认为是一种美味佳肴。 |  The chef prepared a dish of seasoned nombles.  厨师准备了一道调味内脏的菜。 | synonyms: entrails, viscera, innards | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "exterior" in a broad sense) | noun

Etymology: From Old French *nombles, plural of nomble "entrails, especially of a deer",  from Latin *lumulus, diminutive of lumbus "loin".  Over time, the initial "l" changed to "n".
