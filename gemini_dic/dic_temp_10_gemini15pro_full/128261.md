neuronal:/nʊˈrəʊnəl/ /nʊˈroʊnl/| adj.|神经元的，神经系统的|Relating to a neuron or neurons.|The neuronal activity in the brain increases during REM sleep. 大脑中的神经元活动在快速眼动睡眠期间增加。|近义词:nerve, neural|反义词:non-neuronal|词性:形容词
Etymology: From neuron + -al.  "Neuron" comes from Greek "neuron" meaning "sinew, tendon, nerve."
