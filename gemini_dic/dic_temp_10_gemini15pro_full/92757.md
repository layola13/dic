ichthyomorphous:/ˌɪkθiəˈmɔːrfəs/| adj.|鱼形的，鱼类的|Having a form resembling that of a fish.  Resembling or characteristic of a fish, especially in having a streamlined body.|The extinct ichthyosaur had an ichthyomorphous body, perfectly adapted to life in the sea.  这种已灭绝的鱼龙拥有鱼形的身体，非常适应海洋生活。|Near synonyms:  fish-shaped, pisciform, piscine|Antonyms: terrestrial, aerial|adjective
