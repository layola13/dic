hypophosphate:/ˌhaɪpoʊˈfɒsfeɪt/ | n. |次磷酸盐; 联磷酸盐|A salt or ester of hypophosphorous acid.  A compound containing the H2PO2− anion.|Sodium hypophosphite is used as a reducing agent in electroless nickel plating. 次磷酸钠在化学镀镍中用作还原剂。|近义词:phosphite (in some contexts, though chemically distinct)|反义词:phosphate (in some contexts, though chemically distinct)|noun
