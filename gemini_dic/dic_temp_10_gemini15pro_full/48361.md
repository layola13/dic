decumbent:/dɪˈkʌmbənt/ | adj. | 躺着的；伏卧的；斜倚的；(植物)匍匐茎的 | Lying down; reclining; (of a plant) having stems that trail along the ground but have tips that turn upward. | The decumbent figure of a sleeping man lay on the park bench. 公园长椅上躺着一个熟睡的男人的身影。|  The decumbent stems of the creeping phlox spread quickly across the garden. 匍匐福禄考的匍匐茎很快蔓延到整个花园。 | 近义词: recumbent, prostrate, procumbent | 反义词: erect, upright | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *decumbentem (nominative decumbens), present participle of decumbere "to lie down," from de- "down" + -cumbere "to lie."
