cephalocercal: /ˌsefələˈsɜːrkəl/ | adj. | 头尾的，从头到尾的 | Relating to or extending from the head to the tail.  An anatomical term referring to a body orientation where the head and tail are aligned along the primary axis. | The cephalocercal axis in vertebrates extends from the snout to the tip of the tail. 脊椎动物的头尾轴从鼻子延伸到尾巴尖。|  The nervous system develops along the cephalocercal axis. 神经系统沿着头尾轴发育。 |近义词：longitudinal, axial | 反义词： transverse, lateral | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
cephalo- : from Greek kephalē, meaning "head"
-cercal: from Latin  *cercus (unattested), from Greek kerkos "tail"
