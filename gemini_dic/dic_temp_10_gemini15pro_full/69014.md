fattening:/ˈfætənɪŋ/ | adj. | 使人发胖的；容易使人长胖的 | (of food) causing an increase in body fat. |  This cake is delicious, but it's so fattening!  这个蛋糕很好吃，但是很容易让人发胖！| synonyms: rich, calorific, high-calorie | antonyms: slimming, diet, low-calorie | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From fat (n.) + -en (verb suffix) + -ing (present participle suffix).  The "-en" suffix turns the noun "fat" into a verb meaning "to make fat," and the "-ing" suffix creates the present participle adjective, which describes something that causes fattening.
