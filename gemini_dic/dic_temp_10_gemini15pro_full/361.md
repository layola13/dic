abiding-by:/əˈbaɪdɪŋ baɪ/| phrasal verb |遵守，遵循|The company is abiding by the terms of the contract.  该公司正在遵守合同条款。| The citizens are expected to be abiding by the law. 公民应该遵守法律。|近义词：complying with, adhering to, observing, following|反义词：breaking, violating, ignoring, disregarding|词性:动词短语

ETYMOLOGY->
From "abide" (Old English "abidan," meaning "to remain, continue, endure") + "by" (Old English "bi," meaning "near, by, beside"). The combination signifies remaining or continuing in a state of adherence to something.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He abides by the rules. = 他遵守规则。
She always abides by her promises. = 她总是信守承诺。

一般过去时:
They abided by the agreement. = 他们遵守了协议。
We abided by the judge's decision. = 我们遵守了法官的判决。

现在完成时:
The company has always abided by the regulations. = 这家公司一直遵守规定。
He has abided by his principles throughout his career. = 在他的整个职业生涯中，他一直坚持自己的原则。

过去完成时:
They had abided by the treaty for many years before the conflict erupted. = 在冲突爆发前，他们遵守了该条约很多年。
She had abided by the school's dress code until she graduated. = 她直到毕业都遵守学校的着装规定。

将来时:
We will abide by the decision of the court. = 我们将遵守法院的判决。
The new employees will be expected to abide by the company's policies. = 新员工将被要求遵守公司的政策。

虚拟语气:
If they were to abide by the agreement, the problem could be resolved. = 如果他们遵守协议，问题就可以得到解决。
I wish they would abide by the rules. = 我希望他们能遵守规则。

被动语态:
The rules are being abided by. = 规则正在被遵守。
The agreement was abided by by both parties. = 双方都遵守了协议。


疑问句:
Are they abiding by the law? = 他们遵守法律吗？
Did you abide by the terms of the contract? = 你遵守了合同条款吗？

否定句:
They did not abide by the agreement. = 他们没有遵守协议。
She does not always abide by the rules. = 她并不总是遵守规则。
