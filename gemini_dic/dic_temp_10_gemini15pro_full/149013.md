PHRASE->

owing to = 由于；因为
I was late owing to the traffic. 由于交通拥堵，我迟到了。

owe it to oneself to do something = 应该做某事 (为了自己)
You owe it to yourself to take a break. 你应该休息一下 (为了你自己好)。

owe someone an apology = 应该向某人道歉
I owe you an apology for my behavior last night. 我应该为我昨晚的行为向你道歉。

owe someone a debt of gratitude = 非常感激某人
I owe you a debt of gratitude for your help. 我非常感激你的帮助。

owe someone one = 欠某人一个人情
I owe you one for this. 这件事我欠你一个人情。
