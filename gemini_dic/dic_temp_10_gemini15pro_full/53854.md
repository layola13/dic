discontentive:/dɪskənˈtɛntɪv/| adj.|不满的，不满足的|Causing or showing discontent; dissatisfied.|例句：The discontentive workers went on strike. 不满的工人们举行了罢工。|近义词：dissatisfied, malcontent, disgruntled|反义词：content, satisfied|词性:形容词
Etymology: From dis- (expressing negation) + content (satisfied) + -ive (forming adjectives).
