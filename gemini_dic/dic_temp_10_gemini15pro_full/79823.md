glycose:/ˈɡluːkoʊs/| n. |葡萄糖|A simple sugar (a monosaccharide); a crystalline dextrose, less sweet than sucrose and found in fruits, honey, etc.|Glucose is the main source of energy for the body. 葡萄糖是人体主要的能量来源。|Synonyms: dextrose, grape sugar|Antonyms: None|Noun

glycose:/ˈɡluːkoʊs/| n. |葡萄糖|A simple sugar, specifically a monosaccharide. It's an important carbohydrate in biology and is a primary source of energy for living organisms.|Glycose plays a vital role in cellular respiration. 葡萄糖在细胞呼吸中起着至关重要的作用。|Synonyms: dextrose|Antonyms: None|Noun
