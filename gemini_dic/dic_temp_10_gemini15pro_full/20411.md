biternate:/baɪˈtɜːrneɪt/ | adj. | 二回三出的；双三出的 | Having bipinnate leaves, where each leaflet is itself divided into three parts.  It refers to a leaf structure where the leaf is divided three times in a pinnate (feather-like) fashion. | The biternate leaves of the plant were a striking feature.  这种植物的二回三出叶是一个显著特征。 | synonym: tripinnate (sometimes used interchangeably, but tripinnate strictly refers to thrice pinnate) | antonym: simple (referring to a leaf that is not divided) | word type: adjective

biternate:/baɪˈtɜːrneɪt/ | n. | 二回三出的叶子 | A biternate leaf. | The botanist examined the biternate carefully. 植物学家仔细检查了这片二回三出的叶子。 | synonym: tripinnate leaf (sometimes used interchangeably) | antonym: simple leaf | word type: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bi- (twice) + ternatus (arranged in threes), past participle of ternare (to arrange in threes), from terni (three each).
