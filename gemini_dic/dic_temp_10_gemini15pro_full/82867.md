gullible:/ˈɡʌləbl/ (US), /ˈɡʌləbəl/ (UK)| adj.|易受骗的，轻信的|Easily persuaded to believe something; credulous.|He's so gullible, he'll believe anything you tell him. 他太容易上当了，你说什么他都信。|近义词：credulous, naive, trusting|反义词：skeptical, cynical, suspicious|词性:形容词
Etymology: From late 18th century: from gull ‘dupe’ + -ible.
