deejaying:/ˈdiːdʒeɪɪŋ/
n.|播放DJ音乐;担任DJ|The art of selecting and playing recorded music for an audience, typically in a club or at a party. | He enjoys deejaying at local clubs. 他喜欢在当地俱乐部担任DJ。|近义词:DJing, spinning, mixing|反义词: None|词性:名词
词源: From "disc jockeying," shortened to "DJing" and then respelled as "deejaying."
