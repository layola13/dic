doctor: /ˈdɑːktər/ (US) /ˈdɒktə(r)/ (UK) | n. | 医生；博士 | A person licensed to practice medicine, especially one who examines, treats, and advises patients.  A person who has earned the highest degree awarded by a university in a particular subject. |  例句：The doctor prescribed me some medicine. 医生给我开了一些药。

例句：She's a doctor of philosophy. 她是一位哲学博士。| 近义词：physician, surgeon, medic | 反义词: patient | 词性: noun
