pageant: /ˈpædʒənt/ (US) /ˈpædʒənt/ (UK)| n. | 盛会，庆典；选美比赛 | A public entertainment consisting of a procession of people in elaborate costumes, or an outdoor performance of a historical scene. Also, a competition in which women are judged on their beauty and other qualities.|  The town held a pageant to celebrate its bicentennial.  小镇举行了一场盛会来庆祝其二百周年纪念日。| synonyms: spectacle, celebration, festival, parade, competition | antonyms:  informality, simplicity | noun


pageant: /ˈpædʒənt/ (US) /ˈpædʒənt/ (UK)| adj. (literary) | 壮观的，华丽的|  Resembling a pageant; stately or showy. (This usage is less common.) | The autumn leaves created a pageant of color. 秋天的树叶构成了一幅壮观的彩色景象。 | synonyms: spectacular, magnificent, grand | antonyms:  dull, plain | adjective
