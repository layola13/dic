hyperalgic:/ˌhaɪpərˈældʒɪk/ | adj. | 疼痛过敏的；感觉过敏的 | Characterized by increased sensitivity to pain; excessively sensitive to pain. | Example 1:  The patient exhibited hyperalgic responses to even light touch.  病人即使是轻微的触碰也表现出疼痛过敏反应。

Example 2: Hyperalgic skin can be a symptom of certain neurological conditions. 疼痛过敏的皮肤可能是某些神经系统疾病的症状。 | Near Synonyms:  hypersensitive, hypersensitive to pain, allodynic | Antonyms:  analgesic, pain-free, numb | Word Type: Adjective

Etymology:
From *hyper- "over, excessive" + -algia "pain," from Greek algos "pain."
