musicological:/ˌmjuːzɪkəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ / ˌmjuːzɪkəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
adjective
音乐学的
Relating to the scholarly study of music, including its history, theory, and forms.
The book provides a musicological analysis of Beethoven's symphonies. 这本书对贝多芬的交响曲进行了音乐学分析。
Synonyms: musical, academic, scholarly
Antonyms: unscholarly, amateur
词性: adjective
