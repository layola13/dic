loans:/ləʊnz/ (UK) /loʊnz/ (US)| n.|贷款，借款|A sum of money that is borrowed, especially from a bank or other financial institution, and is expected to be paid back with interest.|She took out a loan to buy a car. 她贷款买了一辆车。|Synonyms: advances, credits, borrowings|Antonyms: repayments, deposits|noun
