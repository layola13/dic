inflows:/ˈɪnfloʊz/
noun
流入；流入物；汇入
the arrival or movement of large amounts of money, goods, people etc into a place, organization, activity etc
例句：The country has seen large inflows of foreign investment.  这个国家已经见证了大量外国投资的流入。

例句：Heavy inflows of river water caused the dam to overflow. 大量河水流入导致大坝溢流。

近义词：receipts, intake, influx
反义词：outflows

词性:n.
