humic:/ˈhjuːmɪk/ | adj. |腐殖质的；腐殖酸的 | Relating to or derived from humus. | The soil was rich in humic substances. 土壤富含腐殖质。|The researchers analyzed the humic content of the compost. 研究人员分析了堆肥中的腐殖酸含量。|近义词：organic, earthy |反义词: inorganic |词性: adjective
Etymology: From Latin *humicus, from humus (“earth, soil”)
