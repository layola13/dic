gluelike:/ˈɡluːlaɪk/
adjective
胶状的；像胶水的
resembling glue in consistency; sticky.
The substance was gluelike and difficult to remove.  这种物质像胶水一样，很难去除。
The sap from the tree was gluelike.  树上的树液像胶水一样黏。
sticky, adhesive, gummy, viscous
watery, runny, fluid
adjective
