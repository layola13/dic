anagrammatic:/ˌænəɡrəˈmætɪk/
adjective
字谜的；由字谜构成的
formed by rearranging the letters of a word or phrase to produce a different word or phrase.
The sentence "Eleven plus two" is anagrammatic of "Twelve plus one". “Eleven plus two”这句话是“Twelve plus one”的字谜。
近义词: transposable, rearranged
反义词: original, unchanged
词性: adjective
