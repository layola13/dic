cloddiest:/ˈklɒdiɪst/| adj.|最土块的，最结块的|Superlative form of cloddy: most cloddy.  Covered in or full of clods of earth.|The farmer struggled to plow the cloddiest field he had ever seen.|近义词：lumpy, clumpy, chunky|反义词：smooth, fine, powdery|词性:形容词

词源: From clod + -y + -est.  "Clod" comes from Old English *clod-, related to clot.
