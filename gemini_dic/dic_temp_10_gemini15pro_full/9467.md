antirepublican:/ˌænti rɪˈpʌblɪkən/
adjective
反对共和政体的；反共和主义的
Opposed to republican government; opposed to republican principles.
The antirepublican sentiment was strong among some factions.  这些派系中反共和情绪强烈。
He gave an antirepublican speech. 他发表了一场反共和的演讲。
近义词: monarchist, antidemocratic
反义词: republican, pro-republican
词性: adjective


antirepublican:/ˌænti rɪˈpʌblɪkən/
noun
反共和主义者
A person who is opposed to republican government.
The antirepublicans plotted to overthrow the government.  这些反共和主义者密谋推翻政府。
He was known as a staunch antirepublican. 他以坚定反共和主义者而闻名。
近义词: monarchist
反义词: republican
词性: noun
