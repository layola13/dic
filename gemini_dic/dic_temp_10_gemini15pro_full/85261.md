harmonical:/hɑːrˈmɒnɪkəl/ /hɑːrˈmɑːnɪkl/
adjective
和谐的；和声的；协调的
Relating to harmony in music or other fields; pleasingly ordered or arranged.
The painting's harmonical colors created a sense of peace. 这幅画和谐的色彩营造了一种平和的感觉。
The harmonical arrangement of the furniture made the room feel spacious. 家具和谐的布置使房间感觉很宽敞。
近义词: harmonious, consonant, concordant, tuneful
反义词: disharmonious, discordant, inharmonious, cacophonous
词性: adjective
词源: From Late Latin *harmonicus, from Ancient Greek ἁρμονικός (harmonikós, “harmonic”), from ἁρμονία (harmonía, “harmony, agreement”).
