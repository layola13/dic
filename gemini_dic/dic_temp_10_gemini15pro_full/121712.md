misprinted:/ˌmɪsˈprɪntɪd/ | adj. | 印错的，印刷错误的 | printed incorrectly; containing a mistake in printing | The book was misprinted, and several pages were missing. 这本书印错了，缺了好几页。| synonyms: mispelled, typographical, erroneous | antonyms: correctly printed, accurate, flawless | adjective

misprinted:/ˌmɪsˈprɪntɪd/ | v. | 印错 | to print (something) incorrectly | The publisher had to recall the books because the cover was misprinted. 出版商不得不召回这些书，因为封面印错了。| synonyms: misspell, typo, err | antonyms: print correctly, spell correctly | verb


ETYMOLOGY->

From mis- + printed, past participle of print.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Old English origin meaning "wrong," "badly," or "wrongly." "Print" comes from the Old French "printer," meaning "to press, imprint."
