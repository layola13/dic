bureaucracies: /bjʊˈrɒkrəsiːz/ (UK) /bjʊˈrɑːkrəsiːz/ (US) | n. |官僚机构；官僚作风；繁文缛节| A system of government or administration marked by excessive paperwork, rigid procedures, and a hierarchy of officials.  A system of administration distinguished by its clear hierarchy, specialization of labor, adherence to fixed rules, and impersonal relationships. | The hospital's bureaucracies made it difficult to get quick treatment. 这家医院的繁文缛节使得很难得到快速治疗。|  The government bureaucracies are often criticized for being inefficient. 政府官僚机构经常因效率低下而受到批评。 | Synonyms: administration, red tape, officialdom | Antonyms:  efficiency, streamlining | noun
