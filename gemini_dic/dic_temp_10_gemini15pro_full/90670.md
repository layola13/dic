humbler:/ˈhʌmblər/ | adj. | 更谦逊的，更卑微的 |  Comparative form of humble: more humble.  It describes someone or something as being more modest, unassuming, or low in rank or status than another. |  He became a humbler man after the experience.  他经历了那件事后变得更加谦逊了。|  The new employee was humbler than the CEO. 新员工比CEO更谦逊。 | Synonyms: more modest, more unassuming, meeker | Antonyms: prouder, haughtier | adjective


humbler:/ˈhʌmblər/ | n. | 使人谦卑的事物 | Something that makes one humble.  This usage is less common than the adjective form. | The vastness of the universe is a humbler. 宇宙的浩瀚令人感到谦卑。|  Losing the competition was a real humbler for him.  输掉比赛对他来说是一次真正的教训，让他变得谦逊。 | Synonyms: humbling experience, leveller | Antonyms: ego boost | noun
