bolshevik:/ˈbɒlʃɪvɪk/ /ˈboʊlʃəvɪk/| n. adj.| 布尔什维克，激进的| A member of the majority faction of the Russian Social Democratic Party (RSDP), which was renamed the Communist Party after seizing power in the October Revolution of 1917. Also used as an adjective to describe someone with radical or revolutionary views, often in a derogatory sense. | The Bolsheviks, led by Lenin, overthrew the Provisional Government. 布尔什维克在列宁的领导下推翻了临时政府。|  The government accused the opposition party of having bolshevik tendencies. 政府指责反对党有布尔什维克倾向。 |Synonym: communist, radical, revolutionary | Antonym: moderate, conservative | noun, adjective
