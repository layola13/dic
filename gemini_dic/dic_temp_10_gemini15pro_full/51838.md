dewworm:/ˈdjuːwɜːrm/ | n. |蚯蚓；露虫|An earthworm, especially one found on the surface of the ground, typically early in the morning.|Early birds get the dewworm. 早起的鸟儿有虫吃. |近义词: earthworm, nightcrawler, angleworm |反义词: None |词性: noun

Etymology:
From dew + worm.  "Dew" refers to the common appearance of these worms on the surface after rain or heavy dew, and "worm" is a general term for a long, slender, soft-bodied invertebrate.
