belch:/bɛltʃ/| v. n.|打嗝，喷出|To expel gas noisily from the stomach through the mouth.  To emit something violently.|例句：He belched loudly after drinking the soda. 他喝完汽水后大声地打嗝。|近义词：burp, eructate|反义词：(no direct antonym, possibly suppress a belch)|verb/noun
belch:/bɛltʃ/| v. |打嗝，喷出|To emit something violently, like smoke or fire.|例句：The volcano belched smoke and ash. 火山喷出烟雾和灰烬。|近义词：spew, emit, erupt|反义词：(no direct antonym, possibly absorb)|verb
