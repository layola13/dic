dehumanizes:/ˌdiːhjuːmənaɪzɪz/
verb
使失去人性, 使物化
Deprives of human qualities, personality, or spirit; makes impersonal or mechanical.
The propaganda dehumanizes the enemy, portraying them as monsters.  这宣传使敌人失去人性，把他们描绘成怪物。
The harsh prison system dehumanizes the inmates.  这残酷的监狱系统使犯人失去人性。
synonyms: degrades, demoralizes, brutalizes, objectifies
antonyms: humanizes, personalizes, individualizes
词性: 动词
