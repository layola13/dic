anathemata:/əˈnæθəməˌtɑː/ |n.| 宗教诅咒，被诅咒的人或物; 献祭的祭品; 令人厌恶的事物|objects devoted to destruction and thus accursed; a formal ecclesiastical curse involving excommunication; something or someone that one vehemently dislikes.| The tyrant's name became anathema in the land after his cruel reign.  暴君残暴统治后，他的名字在这个国家成了诅咒。

The broken vase, a reminder of a painful argument, became anathema to her.  那个破碎的花瓶，让她想起了一场痛苦的争吵，成了她厌恶的东西。|Near Synonyms: curse, imprecation, abomination, execration |Antonyms: blessing, benediction |Noun
