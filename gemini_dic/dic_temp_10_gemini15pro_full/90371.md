how-toer:/ˈhaʊˌtuːər/
noun
实践者；操作指南爱好者； how-to 的追随者
Someone who is enthusiastic about or follows how-to guides, instructions, or tutorials.
She's a real how-toer, always looking for the next DIY project. 她是一个真正的实践者，总是寻找下一个DIY项目。
He's a how-toer when it comes to cooking; he loves trying new recipes.  说到烹饪，他就是一个实践者；他喜欢尝试新的食谱。
enthusiast, DIYer, hobbyist
non-enthusiast, professional
