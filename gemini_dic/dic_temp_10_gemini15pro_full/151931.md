pastiche: /pæˈstiːʃ/ /pɑːˈstiːʃ/ | n. |模仿作品；混合作品；集成作品|A work of art, literature, or music that imitates the style of another artist, writer, or musician, or that is made up of parts taken from various sources.|The film is a pastiche of 1940s Hollywood musicals.|近义词：imitation, parody, collage, medley |反义词：original |词性: noun

pastiches: /pæˈstiːʃɪz/ /pɑːˈstiːʃɪz/ | n. |模仿作品（pastiche的复数形式）|Plural form of pastiche, referring to multiple works of art, literature, or music that imitate the style of others or are composed of parts from various sources.|His recent paintings are pastiches of several different artistic styles.|近义词：imitations, parodies, collages, medleys |反义词：originals |词性: noun
