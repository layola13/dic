clements:/ˈklɛmənts/| n. |克莱门茨;克莱门特（姓氏）|A surname, derived from the given name Clement.  It refers to someone mild-tempered or merciful.  It can also refer to a place name.|Example:  John Clements was a renowned Shakespearean actor.  约翰·克莱门茨是一位著名的莎士比亚演员。

Example:  Many people named Clements live in the United States. 很多叫克莱门茨的人都住在美国。|近义词: (For the meaning of the name, not the surname itself) gentle, merciful, compassionate|反义词: (For the meaning of the name, not the surname itself) harsh, cruel, ruthless|词性:noun
