northwardly:/ˈnɔːrθwərdli/
adverb
向北地，朝北地
Moving or directed toward the north.
The ship sailed northwardly through the icy waters. 这艘船向北航行穿过冰冷的海水。
The wind blew northwardly across the plains. 风向北吹过平原。
近义词:northwards, northerly
反义词:southwardly, southerly
词性:adverb

Etymology:
From "north" + "-ward" (indicating direction) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  The "-ward" suffix comes from Old English "-weard," meaning "toward, in the direction of."
