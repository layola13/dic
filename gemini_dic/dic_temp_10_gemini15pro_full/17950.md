befit:/bɪˈfɪt/| vt.|适合，相称|To be suitable for or appropriate to; to be worthy of.|That dress befits the occasion. 那套衣服适合这个场合。|近义词：suit, become, behoove|反义词：misfit, unbecoming|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From be- + fit.  "Be-" is a prefix meaning "thoroughly," and "fit" meaning suitable or proper.
