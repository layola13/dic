finer:/ˈfaɪnər/| adj.|更精细的，更好的，更优质的|Comparative form of "fine."  Indicates a higher degree of quality, delicacy, or refinement.|This wine has a finer aroma than the other. 这款酒比另一款酒香气更浓郁。|synonyms: superior, better, improved, refined, exquisite|antonyms: coarser, inferior, worse, rougher|adjective

finer:/ˈfaɪnər/| n.|精炼机，精炼者|A person or thing that refines something.|The sugar finer removes impurities.  糖精炼机去除杂质。|synonyms: refiner, purifier|antonyms: N/A|noun
