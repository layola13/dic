hypertrophy:/haɪˈpɜːrtrəfi/ /haɪˈpɝːtrəfi/| n.| 肥大，过度生长|The excessive development of an organ or part; an increase in size of an organ or part, not due to tumor formation, but due to an increase in the size of its constituent cells.|  Regular exercise can lead to muscle hypertrophy.  规律的运动会导致肌肉肥大。|synonyms: enlargement, overgrowth, expansion|antonyms: atrophy, shrinking, reduction|noun


hypertrophy:/haɪˈpɜːrtrəfi/ /haɪˈpɝːtrəfi/| v. |(使)肥大，(使)过度生长|To undergo or cause to undergo hypertrophy.|The heart muscle hypertrophied due to the increased workload.  由于工作量增加，心肌肥大。|synonyms: enlarge, expand, swell|antonyms: shrink, atrophy, contract|verb
