constantly:/ˈkɑːnstəntli/ /ˈkɒnstəntli/| adv.|不断地，经常地|Happening all the time or repeatedly.|She constantly checks her phone. 她不停地查看她的手机。|近义词：continuously, perpetually, incessantly, always, frequently|反义词: rarely, seldom, occasionally, intermittently|词性:副词
Etymology: From Late Latin *constantinus, from Latin constans (present participle of constare “to stand firm, stand together”).
