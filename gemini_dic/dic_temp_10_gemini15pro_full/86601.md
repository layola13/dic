heliolite:/ˈhiːlioʊlaɪt/| n. |日光石，太阳石|A translucent reddish-brown feldspar with a golden shimmer, used as a gemstone.  A variety of oligoclase exhibiting a brilliant red aventurescence.|The heliolite pendant shimmered in the sunlight. 日光石吊坠在阳光下闪闪发光。|Synonyms: sunstone, aventurine feldspar|Antonyms: N/A|noun
