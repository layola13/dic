digester:/daɪˈdʒɛstər/ /dɪˈdʒɛstər/| n.| 消化器; 消化罐; 加压蒸煮器; 文摘; 摘要|A digester is a large container in which something is treated with heat or chemicals, especially in order to break it down. A digester is also a collection of previously published material, such as articles or reports.|The sewage sludge is treated in anaerobic digesters. 污水污泥在厌氧消化池中进行处理。|Synonyms:  autoclave, pressure cooker, reactor, vessel|Antonyms: None|Noun
