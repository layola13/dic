countertrippant:/ˌkaʊntərˈtrɪpənt/
adjective
逆行的，反向行走的 (尤指纹章中的动物)
Walking in the opposite direction, especially of animals in heraldry.
The shield depicts a lion countertrippant.  盾牌上描绘着一头逆向行走的狮子。
近义词: counter-passant
反义词: passant, trippant
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Old French "contre" (against, opposite) + "trippant" (tripping, walking). "Trippant" itself comes from the Old French verb "triper" (to trip, dance).  Ultimately derived from Frankish *trippōn, from Proto-Germanic *tripōną (“to tread, trip”).
