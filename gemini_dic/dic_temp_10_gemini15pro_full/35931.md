cocurator:/koʊˈkjʊreɪtər/| n.| 共同策展人 |A person who shares the responsibility of curating an exhibition or collection with one or more others.| The cocurators worked tirelessly to bring the exhibition to life.  共同策展人为使展览栩栩如生而不知疲倦地工作。|synonyms: joint curator, co-organizer| antonyms: sole curator| noun
