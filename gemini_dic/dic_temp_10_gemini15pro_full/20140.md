bipeds:/ˈbaɪpɛdz/| n. |两足动物|Animals that use two legs for walking.|Humans are bipeds. 人类是两足动物。|Birds are also bipeds. 鸟类也是两足动物。|近义词：two-footed animals|反义词：quadrupeds (四足动物)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bipes* "two-footed", from *bi-* "two" + *pes* "foot".
