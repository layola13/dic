gorebill:/ˈɡɔːrˌbɪl/
n.
大嘴鸟，巨嘴鸟
A large-billed bird, especially a toucan.
The rainforest echoed with the calls of the colourful gorebill.  雨林里回荡着五彩缤纷的大嘴鸟的叫声。
The gorebill perched high in the canopy.  大嘴鸟栖息在树冠的高处。
synonyms: toucan, aracari
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms for a specific bird.  Could use small-billed bird for contrast.)
