foolhardier:/ˈfuːlhɑːrdiər/ /ˈfulhɑːrdiər/| adj.|更鲁莽的，更轻率的|More foolishly bold or daring; recklessly adventurous.|例句：It was a foolhardier decision than I thought. 这比我想象的还要鲁莽。

例句：Attempting to climb the mountain without proper equipment was foolhardier than anyone expected.  试图在没有适当装备的情况下攀登这座山比任何人预想的都要鲁莽。|近义词：reckless, rash, imprudent, impetuous|反义词：cautious, prudent, careful|词性:形容词
