degreaser:/diːˈɡriːsər/
noun
除油剂；脱脂剂
A substance used to remove grease.
Example: Use a degreaser to clean the oven.  用除油剂清洁烤箱。
Example:  This degreaser works wonders on greasy engine parts.  这种除油剂对油腻的发动机部件效果奇佳。
Synonyms: cleaner, solvent, detergent
Antonyms: lubricant, oil


degreasing:/diːˈɡriːsɪŋ/
noun
除油；脱脂
The act of removing grease.
Example: Degreasing the engine is essential for maintenance.  给发动机除油是维护的必要步骤。
Example: The degreasing process took several hours.  除油过程花了几个小时。
Synonyms: cleaning, scouring
Antonyms: lubricating, oiling


degrease:/diːˈɡriːs/
verb
除油；脱脂
To remove grease from something.
Example: Degrease the pan before cooking.  烹饪前先把锅除油。
Example: I need to degrease the engine.  我需要给发动机除油。
Synonyms: clean, scour
Antonyms: lubricate, oil


ETYMOLOGY->

From de- + grease + -er/-ing/-e.  "De-" is a prefix meaning "remove" or "reverse".  "Grease" refers to oily or fatty matter. "-er" forms a noun indicating an agent or tool. "-ing" forms a noun or adjective related to the act of doing something. "-e" forms the base verb.
