intertentacular:/ˌɪntərtenˈtækjʊlər/
adjective
触手间的；触须间的
Located or occurring between tentacles.
The intertentacular membrane helps the squid to swim.  鱿鱼的触手间膜帮助它游泳。
近义词:interbrachial
反义词: None
