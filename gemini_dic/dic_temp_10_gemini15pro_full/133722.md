nonfruition:/ˌnɒnfruˈɪʃən/ |n.| 未实现，未结果 |lack of fruition; failure to produce results|  His ambitious plans ended in nonfruition. 他的雄心勃勃的计划最终未能实现。|近义词: failure, disappointment, frustration |反义词: success, achievement, fulfillment |词性: 名词

Etymology:
From non- + fruition.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "fruition" comes from the Late Latin "fruitionem," meaning "enjoyment, use, or possession of the fruits," derived from the Latin "frui," meaning "to enjoy" or "to use."
