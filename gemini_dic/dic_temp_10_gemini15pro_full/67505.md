extract:/ɪkˈstrækt/ /ɛkˈstrækt/ | n. vt. | 提取物，摘录；提取，摘录 |  A substance separated or obtained from a mixture or compound by chemical or physical processes.  To draw or pull out, often with effort or force. |  例句：The vanilla extract adds a rich flavor to the cake. 香草精为蛋糕增添了浓郁的风味。  例句：He managed to extract a promise from her. 他设法从她那里得到了一个承诺。 | 近义词：excerpt, remove, derive, obtain | 反义词: insert, inject, add | 词性: 名词, 动词
