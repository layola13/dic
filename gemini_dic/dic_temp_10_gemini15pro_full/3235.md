affettuoso:/ˌæfɛtʃuˈoʊsoʊ/ /ˌæfetʃʊˈəʊsəʊ/| adv. adj.|深情地，充满柔情的|Indicates a tender, affectionate style of performance in music.  It directs the musician to play with warmth and emotion.|Play this passage *affettuoso*.  请深情地演奏这段乐章。|Synonyms: tenderly, lovingly, warmly, affectionately|Antonyms: coldly, harshly, indifferently|adverb, adjective

affettuoso:/ˌæfɛtʃuˈoʊsoʊ/ /ˌæfetʃʊˈəʊsəʊ/| n.|（音乐）柔情地|A direction in music to play tenderly and with feeling.|The *affettuoso* marking indicated the composer's intent for a heartfelt performance. “柔情地”的标记表明了作曲家想要用心演奏的意图。|Synonyms: tenderness, affection, warmth|Antonyms: coldness, harshness, indifference|noun
