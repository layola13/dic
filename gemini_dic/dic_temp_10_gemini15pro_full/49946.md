dendroidal:/dɛnˈdrɔɪdəl/| adj.|树状的，树枝状的|Resembling a tree in form; branching like a tree.|The dendroidal pattern on the fossil suggested it was some kind of ancient plant.|近义词：arborescent, treelike, branching, dendritic|反义词:linear, straight|词性:形容词
词源: From Greek *dendron* (tree) + *-oidal* (resembling).
