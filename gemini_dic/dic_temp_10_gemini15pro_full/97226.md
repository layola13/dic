inflationist:/ɪnˈfleɪʃənɪst/ | n. adj. |通货膨胀主义者，通货膨胀的 |A person who supports or advocates inflation, or policies that cause inflation.  An inflationist policy or measure is one that is likely to cause inflation. | The economist was labeled an inflationist by his critics for his support of expansionary monetary policies.  这位经济学家因其支持扩张性货币政策而被批评者贴上了通货膨胀主义者的标签。|  The government's inflationist policies led to a sharp rise in prices. 政府的通货膨胀政策导致物价急剧上涨。 |synonyms: expansionist, reflationist | antonyms: deflationist, contractionist | noun, adjective
