cobdenic: /kɒbˈdɛnɪk/ | adj. | 科布登主义的；主张自由贸易的 | Relating to the economic and political theories of Richard Cobden, especially his advocacy of free trade. |  Example: Cobdenic policies were popular in 19th-century Britain.  19世纪的英国，科布登主义的政策很受欢迎。

Example: The government adopted a Cobdenic approach to international trade. 政府对国际贸易采取了科布登主义的做法。 | 近义词: free-trade, laissez-faire | 反义词: protectionist | 词性: adjective
