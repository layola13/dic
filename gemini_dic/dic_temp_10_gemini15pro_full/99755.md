intermeddlesome:/ˌɪntərˈmed(ə)lsəm/| adj.|爱管闲事的，好干预的|Given to meddling; interfering.  Characterized by or showing a tendency to interfere in other people's affairs without being asked.|例句：He had an intermeddlesome aunt who always had an opinion on everything. 他有一个爱管闲事的姑姑，对每件事都有自己的看法。

例句：Her intermeddlesome nature made her unpopular with her colleagues. 她爱管闲事的性格让她不受同事欢迎。|近义词：nosy, intrusive, meddling, interfering|反义词:respectful, reserved, uninvolved|词性:adjective
