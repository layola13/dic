banknote: /ˈbæŋknəʊt/ (UK) /ˈbæŋknoʊt/ (US) | n. | 钞票，纸币 | A piece of paper money issued by a bank, constituting a promise to pay a specified sum to the bearer or holder. |  I paid for the groceries with a twenty-dollar banknote. 我用一张二十美元的钞票支付了杂货费用。 | Synonyms: bill, note, currency | Antonyms: coin | noun
