bubonocele: /ˌbjuːbəˈnəʊˌsiːl/ /ˌbjuːbəˈnoʊˌsiːl/
n.
腹股沟疝,腹股沟淋巴结肿大
A hernia or rupture in the groin.  Specifically, a protrusion of intestine through the abdominal wall in the inguinal region.
The doctor diagnosed the patient with a bubonocele after a thorough examination. 医生在彻底检查后诊断病人患有腹股沟疝。
Surgery is often required to repair a bubonocele.  手术通常是修复腹股沟疝的必要手段。
近义词: inguinal hernia, hernia
