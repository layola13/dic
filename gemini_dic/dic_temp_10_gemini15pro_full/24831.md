brown-bagging:/ˈbraʊnˌbæɡɪŋ/| n.|自带午餐；自带酒水|The practice of bringing one's own lunch, typically in a brown paper bag, to work or school, often to save money or eat healthier.| Many employees are brown-bagging these days due to the rising cost of eating out. 许多员工现在都自带午餐，因为外出就餐的费用越来越高。|Example: With the recent budget cuts, more and more people are brown-bagging it.|近义词: packing a lunch|反义词: eating out|词性: noun


brown-bagging:/ˈbraʊnˌbæɡɪŋ/| v.|自带午餐；自带酒水|To bring one's own lunch, typically in a brown paper bag, to work or school.| He brown-bags it every day to save money. 他每天自带午餐以省钱。|Example: She started brown-bagging her lunch after noticing how much she was spending at restaurants.|近义词: bringing a packed lunch|反义词: dining out|词性: verb (gerund or present participle)
