blunt:/blʌnt/| adj. v.|钝的，迟钝的；直率的；使钝；使迟钝|Having a dull point or edge; not sharp.  Also, straightforward or abrupt in manner. |The knife was too blunt to cut the rope. 这把刀太钝了，切不断绳子。|Synonyms: dull, obtuse, frank, abrupt|Antonyms: sharp, keen, subtle, diplomatic|adjective
blunt:/blʌnt/| v.|使钝；使迟钝|To make less sharp or sensitive. |The constant hammering blunted the chisel.  不断的敲打使凿子变钝了。|Synonyms: dull, deaden|Antonyms: sharpen, hone|verb
