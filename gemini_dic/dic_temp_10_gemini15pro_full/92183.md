hypersthene:/ˈhaɪpərˌsθiːn/| n. | 紫苏辉石 |A rock-forming mineral belonging to the pyroxene group, containing iron and magnesium. It is typically dark greenish-brown to black in color.| Hypersthene is a common constituent of gabbro and norite. 紫苏辉石是辉长岩和苏长岩的常见成分。| Hypersthene can exhibit a shimmering effect known as schiller. 紫苏辉石可以展现一种被称为闪光的闪光效果。|近义词：enstatite, bronzite|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
