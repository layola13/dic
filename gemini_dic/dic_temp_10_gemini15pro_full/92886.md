idealization:/ˌaɪdiːələˈzeɪʃn/ |n.|理想化；理想化的事物|The idealization of rural life is a common theme in literature.  乡村生活的理想化是文学中的一个常见主题。|例句：

His account of his childhood was an idealization, omitting all the difficult parts.
他对童年的描述是理想化的，省略了所有艰难的部分。

The movie presented an idealization of family life.
这部电影呈现了理想化的家庭生活。|近义词：glorification, romanticization, exaltation|反义词：vilification, denigration, disparagement|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *idéalisé*, past participle of *idéaliser*, from *idéal* (“ideal”) + *-iser*, from Latin *-izāre*. Equivalent to *ideal* + *-ize* + *-ation*.
