damaging:/ˈdæmɪdʒɪŋ/| adj.|有害的，破坏性的|Causing or likely to cause harm or damage.|例句：The report detailed the damaging effects of pollution on the environment. 报告详细说明了污染对环境的破坏性影响。

例句：Spreading false rumors can be incredibly damaging to someone's reputation. 散布谣言会对某人的声誉造成极大的损害。|近义词：harmful, detrimental, injurious, destructive|反义词：beneficial, helpful, constructive|词性:形容词
词源: From the verb "damage" + "-ing" suffix, ultimately from the Old French "damager".
