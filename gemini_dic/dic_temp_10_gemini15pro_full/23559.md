brainstormers:/ˈbreɪnstɔːrmərz/
n.
头脑风暴者；献计献策者
People who participate in a brainstorming session to generate ideas.
The brainstormers came up with several innovative solutions to the problem. 头脑风暴者提出了几个解决问题的创新方案。
synonyms: innovators, thinkers, creators, idea generators, collaborators
antonyms: naysayers, pessimists, obstructionists
noun
