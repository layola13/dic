archive: /ˈɑːrkaɪv/ (US) /ˈɑːkaɪv/ (UK)| n. vt. adj.|档案，档案馆；存档；归档|A collection of historical documents or records providing information about the past, or the place where they are kept.  Also, to store such documents.|The library has an extensive archive of local newspapers.  图书馆有大量的当地报纸档案。|Synonyms: record, annals, chronicle, registry|Antonyms:  delete, erase|noun

archive: /ˈɑːrkaɪv/ (US) /ˈɑːkaɪv/ (UK)|vt.|存档；归档|To put or store documents in an archive.|He archived all his old emails. 他把所有旧邮件都存档了。|Synonyms: file, store, catalog|Antonyms: delete, erase|verb

archive: /ˈɑːrkaɪv/ (US) /ˈɑːkaɪv/ (UK)| adj.|档案的；存档的|Relating to or constituting an archive.|Archived documents are available upon request.  存档文件可根据要求提供。|Synonyms: stored, filed, recorded|Antonyms: current, active|adjective
