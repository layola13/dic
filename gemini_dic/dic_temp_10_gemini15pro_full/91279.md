hydrographer:/haɪˈdrɒɡrəfər/ (UK) /haɪˈdrɑːɡrəfər/ (US)| n. |水文地理学家；水道测量员|A person who studies and charts bodies of water, especially for navigation purposes. |例句：The hydrographer meticulously charted the depth of the river. 水文地理学家仔细地绘制了河流的深度。

例句：The experienced hydrographer used sonar to map the ocean floor.  经验丰富的水文地理学家使用声纳绘制海底地图。|近义词：cartographer, surveyor, chart maker|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

hydrography:/haɪˈdrɒɡrəfi/ (UK) /haɪˈdrɑːɡrəfi/ (US)| n. |水文地理学；水道测量|The science of surveying and charting bodies of water, especially for navigation purposes. |例句：Hydrography plays a vital role in safe navigation. 水文地理学在安全航行中起着至关重要的作用。

例句：Modern hydrography relies heavily on satellite technology. 现代水文地理学很大程度上依赖于卫星技术。|近义词：hydrology (partially), marine surveying|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
