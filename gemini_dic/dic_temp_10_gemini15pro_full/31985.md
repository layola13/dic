chemosmotic:/ˌkɛmoʊzˈmɑːtɪk/ | adj. | 化学渗透的 | Relating to or denoting the generation of ATP in cellular respiration by the movement of hydrogen ions across a selectively permeable membrane. | The chemiosmotic theory explains how ATP is synthesized in the mitochondria.  化学渗透理论解释了线粒体中ATP是如何合成的。| The chemiosmotic gradient is essential for energy production. 化学渗透梯度对于能量产生至关重要。 |  近义词: chemiosmosis (n.) | 反义词:  (No direct antonym exists, but processes that disrupt the chemiosmotic gradient could be considered functionally opposite.) | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "chemo-" (chemical) + "osmotic" (relating to osmosis). "Osmosis" derives from Greek "ōsmos" meaning "push, thrust."
