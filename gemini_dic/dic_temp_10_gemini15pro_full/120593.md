mirrorize: /ˈmɪrəraɪz/
verb
镜面化; 对称化
To create a mirrored image or effect; to make something symmetrical.
The software can mirrorize any image.  这个软件可以镜面化任何图像。
The artist mirrorized the pattern across the canvas. 艺术家在画布上对称绘制了图案。
近义词: reflect, duplicate, symmetrize
反义词: distort, skew, asymmetry
词性: verb

词源:  "mirror" (镜子) + "-ize" (动词后缀，表示“使成为…”, “使…化”)，是一个由名词转化而来的动词。  The word is a neologism formed from "mirror" and the suffix "-ize".  It likely emerged in the late 20th or early 21st century with the rise of digital image manipulation.
