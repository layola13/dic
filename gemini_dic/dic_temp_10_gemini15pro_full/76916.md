gantries: /ˈɡæntriz/ (US), /ˈɡæntriːz/ (UK)| n.|高架起重机；龙门架；（摄影棚等的）天桥；（发射火箭等的）钢架|A gantry is a bridge-like overhead structure with a platform supporting equipment such as a crane, camera, or other heavy machinery.|The rocket rested on the gantry, ready for launch. 火箭停在发射架上，准备发射。|Synonyms: crane, framework, scaffold, platform|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, perhaps 'ground' in some contexts)|Noun


gantries: /ˈɡæntriz/ (US), /ˈɡæntriːz/ (UK)| n.|（尤指机场的）廊桥|A gantry can also refer to a covered walkway or bridge, especially at an airport, that connects a terminal building to an aircraft.|Passengers boarded the plane via a gantry.  旅客通过廊桥登机。|Synonyms:  jet bridge, air bridge, passenger boarding bridge|Antonyms: (No direct antonym)|Noun
