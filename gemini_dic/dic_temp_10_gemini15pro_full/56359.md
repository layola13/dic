doile:/ˈdɔɪl/| n.|桌布，餐垫|A small ornamental mat, often placed under a vase or other object to protect a surface.|She placed the doile under the vase to protect the table.|近义词: placemat, mat, coaster|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps something like "bare surface")|词性:noun


词源: From French *doille*, diminutive of toile "cloth," from Latin tēla "web, cloth"
