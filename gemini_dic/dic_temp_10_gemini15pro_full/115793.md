marital: /ˈmærɪt(ə)l/ | adj. |婚姻的，夫妇间的|Relating to marriage or the relationship between a husband and wife. |例句：They are having marital problems. 他们有婚姻问题。|例句：Marital bliss is not always easy to achieve. 婚姻幸福并非总是容易获得。|近义词：matrimonial, conjugal |反义词：single, unmarried |词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *maritalis, from Latin maritus (“husband, married man”), from an Indo-European root shared by Sanskrit *jāyā́pati (“husband, lord of one’s wife”).
