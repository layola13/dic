anachronism:/əˈnækrənɪzəm/| n.|不合时宜的人或物；时代错误|Something that is out of place in time.  An error in chronology in which a person, object, or event is assigned a date or period other than the correct one. |The movie showed a car in a scene set in ancient Rome, which was a glaring anachronism.|近义词：misplacement, misdating, solecism|反义词: contemporaneity|noun
