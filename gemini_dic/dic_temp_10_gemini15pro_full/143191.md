outcompeting: /ˌaʊtkəmˈpiːtɪŋ/
v.
胜过，超过竞争对手
defeating someone or something in a competition
The company is outcompeting its rivals in the market.  这家公司在市场上胜过其竞争对手。
New, more fuel-efficient aircraft are outcompeting older models. 更省油的新型飞机正在超越旧机型。
近义词: outdoing, surpassing, beating, exceeding, triumphing over
反义词: losing to, being defeated by
词性: present participle of "outcompete"

outcompete: /ˌaʊtkəmˈpiːt/
v.
胜过，在竞争中击败
to compete more effectively than someone or something else
Smaller companies are often able to outcompete larger ones by offering more personalized service. 小公司通常能够通过提供更个性化的服务来胜过大公司。
They were outcompeted by other companies offering similar products at lower prices. 他们被其他以更低价格提供类似产品的公司击败了。
近义词: outdo, surpass, beat, exceed, triumph over
反义词: lose to, be defeated by
词性: verb


词源: "out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding," "surpassing") + "compete" (from Latin "competere" - to strive together)
