corsned:/ˈkɔːrsnɛd/ |n.|神判面包；吞咽测试|A piece of bread or cheese consecrated and given to a suspected person to swallow as a trial of guilt or innocence, the choking on which was regarded as proof of guilt.|He refused to take the corsned, fearing divine retribution.| 近义词:ordeal, trial by ordeal|反义词:None (lacks a direct antonym)|noun
