levorotation: /ˌliːvəʊrəʊˈteɪʃən/ /ˌlevəroʊˈteɪʃn/ | n. |左旋，左旋性 | The rotation of the plane of polarized light to the left, counterclockwise as viewed by an observer toward whom the light is traveling. | The substance exhibits levorotation.  这种物质呈现左旋性。|  Levorotation is the opposite of dextrorotation. 左旋性是右旋性的反义词。 | synonym: left-handed rotation | antonym: dextrorotation | noun
