owllight:/ˈaʊlˌlaɪt/ | n. | 猫头鹰光，黄昏，暮色 |The soft, dim light just after sunset, when owls become active.  黄昏后柔和昏暗的光线，此时猫头鹰开始活动。 |  We walked through the forest in the owllight. 我们在暮色中穿过森林。 | 近义词:twilight, dusk, gloaming | 反义词:dawn, daybreak | 词性:名词

词源: 
Compound word from "owl" + "light."  由“owl”（猫头鹰）和“light”（光）复合而成。  It refers to the time of day associated with the activity of owls, typically dusk. 它指的是与猫头鹰活动相关的时间段，通常是黄昏。
