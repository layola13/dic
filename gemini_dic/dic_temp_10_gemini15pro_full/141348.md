operandi:/ˌɒpəˈrændi/ /ˌɑːpəˈrændi/ | n. |  做法，手段，行动方式 |  Latin, ablative singular of *operandus*, gerundive of *operari* 'to work'.  It refers to a particular way of working or a method of operating, especially one that is characteristic of a particular person or group. | The thief's modus operandi involved disabling security cameras before entering the building.  窃贼的惯用手法是在进入大楼之前先禁用监控摄像头。

The police were able to identify the suspect based on his modus operandi.  警方能够根据嫌疑人的作案手法来识别他。 |  method, procedure, technique, approach, strategy | N/A | noun
