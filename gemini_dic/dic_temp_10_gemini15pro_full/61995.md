ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *enallagē, from Ancient Greek ἐναλλαγή (enallagḗ, “exchange, interchange”), from ἐναλλάσσω (enallássō, “I exchange, interchange”), from ἐν (en, “in, on”) + ἀλλάσσω (allássō, “I change”).
