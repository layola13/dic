nondenial:/ˌnɒndɪˈnaɪəl/ | n. | 不否认;间接承认 | An act of not denying something, often interpreted as an implicit admission of guilt or truth.  It's a statement that avoids a direct denial, often used in political or legal contexts. |  "His nondenial about the affair raised further suspicions." 他对这件事的不否认引起了进一步的怀疑。| synonyms: implicit admission, tacit acknowledgment, equivocation | antonyms: denial, refutation | noun

nondenials:/ˌnɒndɪˈnaɪəlz/ | n. | 不否认(复数) | Plural form of "nondenial," referring to multiple instances of avoiding a direct denial. | "The repeated nondenials from the spokesperson only fueled the rumors." 发言人反复的不否认只会加剧谣言。| synonyms: implicit admissions, tacit acknowledgments, equivocations | antonyms: denials, refutations | noun
