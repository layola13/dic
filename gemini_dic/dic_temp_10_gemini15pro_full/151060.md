paraphrastic:/ˌpærəˈfræstɪk/
adjective
释义的，迂回的，用解释性词语表达的
expressed in a roundabout way; circumlocutory.
She gave a paraphrastic explanation of the complex theory, making it easier for everyone to understand. 她对这个复杂的理论进行了解释性的说明，使每个人都更容易理解。
synonyms: circumlocutory, periphrastic, roundabout
antonyms: concise, succinct, terse
词性: adjective
