heteronymous:/ˌhɛtərˈɒnɪməs/ /ˌhɛtəˈrɑːnɪməs/| adj.|异名的，命名不同的|Having different names, especially applied to words having the same spelling but different pronunciations and meanings, or to organisms having different scientific names but thought to be identical.|The words "tear" (a drop of liquid from the eye) and "tear" (to rip) are heteronymous.|近义词：polysemous, homographic|反义词：homonymous, synonymous|词性:adjective
