cpms:/ˈsiːpiːɛmˈɛs/ | n. | cost per mille/thousand impressions |  Cost per mille, also called cost per thousand (CPM), is a marketing term used to denote the price of 1,000 advertisement impressions on one webpage. | The CPM for this campaign is $2.  这个活动的每千次展示成本是2美元。| synonyms: cost per thousand (CPT) | antonyms: N/A | noun
