morns:/mɔːrnz/ |n.|早晨，清晨（尤指诗歌用语）| Mornings are often peaceful and quiet. 早晨通常是宁静的。| The poet described the beauty of the morns. 诗人描绘了清晨的美景。|近义词: dawns, sunrises|反义词: evenings, nights|词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle English *morwen, from Old English morgen, from Proto-Germanic *murgananą (“morning”), from Proto-Indo-European *merǵ- (“to twinkle, blink, shimmer”). Cognate with Scots morn (“morning”), West Frisian moarn (“morning”), Dutch morgen (“morning”), German Morgen (“morning”), Danish morgen (“morning”), Swedish morgon (“morning”), Icelandic morgunn (“morning”).
