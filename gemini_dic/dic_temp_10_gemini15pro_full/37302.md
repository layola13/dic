commando:/kəˈmændəʊ/ (UK), /kəˈmændoʊ/ (US)| n.|突击队员；特种兵|A soldier specially trained for carrying out raids.|The commandoes were dropped behind enemy lines. 特种兵被空投到敌后。|Synonyms: special forces, ranger, elite troops|Antonyms: civilian, non-combatant|Noun

commando:/kəˈmændəʊ/ (UK), /kəˈmændoʊ/ (US)| adj.|突击队的；特种部队的|Relating to or characteristic of commandoes.|A commando raid. 一次突击队袭击。|Synonyms: special forces, elite|Antonyms: conventional, regular|Adjective
