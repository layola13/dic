ediacaran:/ˌiːdiˈækərən/ | n. |埃迪卡拉纪|The geological period between the Cryogenian and the Cambrian, about 635 to 541 million years ago.|The Ediacaran period witnessed the emergence of complex multicellular life. 埃迪卡拉纪见证了复杂多细胞生命的出现。|Fossils from the Ediacaran are often difficult to interpret. 来自埃迪卡拉纪的化石通常难以解释。|近义词:Vendian|反义词:Cambrian|词性:名词
