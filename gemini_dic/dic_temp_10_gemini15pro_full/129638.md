non-conveyance:/ˌnɒn kənˈveɪəns/ | n. | 不转让，不输送 | The non-conveyance of the property led to a lengthy legal battle.  该房产的不转让导致了一场漫长的法律纠纷。| The contract stipulated the non-conveyance of certain rights. 合同规定了某些权利的不转让。 |non-transfer, non-delivery | conveyance, transfer | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + conveyance (from convey, from Old French conveier, from Latin *conviare "to accompany on the way," from com- "together" + via "way").
