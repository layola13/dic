agree-to:/əˈɡriː tuː/
verb phrase
同意，答应
to consent to; to give one's approval to something
The company agreed to the terms of the contract.  公司同意了合同条款。
He agreed to help me move. 他同意帮我搬家。

近义词:accept, consent to, approve
反义词:refuse, reject, decline
词性:动词短语
