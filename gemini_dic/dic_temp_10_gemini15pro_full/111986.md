londonize:/ˈlʌndənaɪz/
verb
使伦敦化；沾染伦敦习气；模仿伦敦风格
To make or become characteristic of London, its people, or its customs.
The cafe was londonized with red telephone booths and Union Jack decorations.  这家咖啡馆用红色的电话亭和英国国旗装饰，充满了伦敦的味道。
Many small towns are becoming londonized as people move from the capital. 随着人们从首都迁出，许多小镇正在变得伦敦化。
近义词: metropolitanize, urbanize
反义词: ruralize, provincialize
词性: verb

londonization:/ˌlʌndənaɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
伦敦化；伦敦化过程
The process of becoming London-like.
The londonization of the suburbs has led to a rise in property prices. 郊区的伦敦化导致了房价上涨。
The rapid londonization of the city's culture is a concern for some residents.  城市文化的快速伦敦化让一些居民感到担忧。
近义词: urbanization, metropolitanization
反义词: ruralization, provincialization
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From London + -ize.  The suffix "-ize" forms verbs meaning "to make or become like" something.
