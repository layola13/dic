intercommunication:/ˌɪntər.kəˌmjuː.nɪˈkeɪ.ʃən/
n.|互相交流，相互通讯|The act of communicating between people or groups.|例句：Intercommunication between departments is essential for the smooth running of the company.  公司各部门之间的相互沟通对于公司的顺利运作至关重要。|例句：Effective intercommunication fostered a strong sense of community within the team.  有效的沟通促进了团队内部强烈的社区意识。|近义词：communication, interaction, exchange|反义词:isolation, separation|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- + communication.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among," and "communication" refers to the process of conveying information.
