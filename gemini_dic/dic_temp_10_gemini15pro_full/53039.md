dioecism: /daɪˈiːsɪzəm/ /daɪˈisɪzəm/ | n. | 雌雄异株 | The condition of having the male and female reproductive organs in separate individuals. | Holly trees exhibit dioecism, meaning you need both a male and a female plant to get berries. 冬青树表现出雌雄异株，这意味着你需要雄性和雌性植物才能获得浆果。 |  gonochorism | monoecism, hermaphroditism | noun
