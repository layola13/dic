blurter:/ˈblɜːrtər/| n.|脱口而出的人，快嘴的人|A person who blurts out something suddenly or indiscreetly.|He's a blurter, always saying the wrong thing at the wrong time. 他是个快嘴的人，总是在错误的时间说错话。|近义词：loudmouth, chatterbox|反义词：reticent person, clam|noun
