overhelpfulnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈhɛlpfʊlnəsəz/
|n.| 过度热心，过度帮忙|The state of being excessively helpful, often to the point of being intrusive or counterproductive.|Her overhelpfulnesses became annoying after a while, as she constantly interfered with my work.|近义词: intrusiveness, officiousness|反义词: neglect, indifference|词性: noun

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi) + "helpful" (help + -ful) + "-ness" (Old English -nes(s))
