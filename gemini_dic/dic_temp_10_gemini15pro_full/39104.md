confrontationists:/ˌkɒnfrʌnˈteɪʃənɪsts/
n.
对抗者，对峙者
People who advocate or engage in confrontation as a means of achieving their goals.  Those who favor directly challenging or opposing others, often aggressively.
The confrontationists staged a protest outside the embassy.  对抗者在使馆外举行了抗议。
The two confrontationists argued loudly in the street. 这两个对抗者在街上大声争吵。
synonyms:  agitators, militants, challengers, radicals, firebrands
antonyms:  pacifists, mediators, conciliators, negotiators, appeasers
noun
