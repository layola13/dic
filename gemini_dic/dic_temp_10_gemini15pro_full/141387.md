operetta:/ˌɒpəˈretə/ (US) /ˌɑːpəˈretə/ (UK)| n. |轻歌剧; 小型歌剧|A short amusing opera, with spoken dialogue and dancing.|例句：The operetta was a lighthearted and entertaining performance. 这部轻歌剧是一场轻松愉快的演出。

例句：She starred in several popular operettas. 她主演了几部受欢迎的轻歌剧。|近义词：musical, comic opera|反义词:tragedy, drama|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Italian *operetta*, diminutive of *opera*. Ultimately from Latin *opera* "work, effort."
