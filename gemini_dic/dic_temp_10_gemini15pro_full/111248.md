litigant: /ˈlɪtɪɡənt/ | n. | 诉讼当事人， 打官司的人 | A person involved in a lawsuit. | The judge asked the litigants to approach the bench. 法官要求诉讼当事人到法官席前。

The two litigants finally reached a settlement.  两位诉讼当事人最终达成了和解。 |  Synonym: party, litigator, suitor, claimant | Antonym: defendant (specifically), non-litigant (general lack of involvement) | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *litigant-, present participle stem of litigare "to go to law," from lis (genitive litis) "lawsuit, case, strife, dispute" + agere "to drive, lead, act, do."
