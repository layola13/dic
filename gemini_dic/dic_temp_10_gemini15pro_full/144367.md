outstepping:/ˌaʊtˈstɛpɪŋ/
|v.|超越；超出…的限度；|Going beyond the limits of what is considered appropriate or acceptable. |The company was accused of outstepping its authority by imposing new regulations.  这家公司因强制实行新规而被指控越权。|exceed, overstep, transgress| observe, respect|verb

outstepping:/ˌaʊtˈstɛpɪŋ/
|n.|超越；超出限度|The act of going beyond the limits of what is considered appropriate or acceptable.|His outstepping of his authority led to his dismissal. 他越权的行为导致了他的解雇。|excess, transgression| observance, respect|noun
