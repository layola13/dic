aikido:/aɪˈkiːdoʊ/
n.|合气道|A Japanese martial art that emphasizes blending with an attacker's movements and redirecting their energy to neutralize them without excessive force.|例句：She started practicing aikido for self-defense. 她开始练习合气道以进行自卫。|例句：Aikido is a highly disciplined martial art. 合气道是一门高度自律的武术。|近义词：martial art, judo, jujutsu|反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps aggression)|词性:noun
