avat:/ˈævɑːt/ | n. | 化身；虚拟形象；阿凡达 | An icon or figure representing a particular person in a video game, internet forum, etc., especially one customized by the user.  | Example: My avat in the game is a powerful wizard.  我在游戏中的化身是一个强大的巫师。| Example:  She customized her avat with bright pink hair and a sparkly dress. 她把自己的虚拟形象定制成亮粉色头发，穿着闪亮裙子。| Synonyms: avatar, icon, persona, character | Antonyms:  reality, real person | noun
