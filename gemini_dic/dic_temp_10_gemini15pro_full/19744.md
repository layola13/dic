biller:/ˈbɪlər/| n.|开票员，记账员，收费员|A person or company that sends bills, especially one that does so as a business.  A person who issues bills or invoices for goods or services.|The biller sent me an invoice for the electricity used last month.  上个月，收费员给我寄来了电费账单。| synonyms:  invoicer, accountant, clerk | antonyms: payer, customer | noun
