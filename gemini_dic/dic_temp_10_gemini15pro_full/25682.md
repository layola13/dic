bungling:/ˈbʌŋɡlɪŋ/
adjective
笨手笨脚的；拙劣的
clumsy and incompetent.
The bungling burglar tripped over the rug and set off the alarm. 笨手笨脚的窃贼被地毯绊倒，触发了警报。
近义词:clumsy, inept, awkward, incompetent
反义词:skillful, adept, competent, proficient
词性:adjective

bungle:/ˈbʌŋɡl/
verb
搞砸；笨手笨脚地做
carry out (a task) clumsily or incompetently, leading to failure or an unsatisfactory outcome.
He bungled the robbery and ended up getting caught. 他把抢劫搞砸了，结果被抓住了。
近义词:botch, mess up, spoil, ruin
反义词:succeed, achieve, accomplish, master
词性:verb

bungle:/ˈbʌŋɡl/
noun
失误；拙劣的工作
a mistake or clumsy performance.
The entire project was a bungle from start to finish.  整个项目从开始到结束都是一团糟。
近义词:blunder, mistake, error, mess
反义词:success, triumph, achievement, feat
词性:noun
