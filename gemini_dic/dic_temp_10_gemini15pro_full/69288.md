feature-length:/ˈfiːtʃə(r) leŋθ/
adj.
长片的，足够长的
Having the length of a full-length film, typically 90 minutes or more.
The festival showcases a variety of feature-length documentaries.  这个电影节展出了各种各样的长篇纪录片。
The director is known for his feature-length animated films.  这位导演以其长篇动画电影而闻名。
近义词:full-length
反义词:short, short-length
词性:adjective
