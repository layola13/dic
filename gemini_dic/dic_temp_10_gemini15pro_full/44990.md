crystallizers:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪzərz/
n.
结晶器；晶化器
A crystallizer is a device or vessel used for the process of crystallization, where a dissolved substance is transformed into solid crystals.
The sugar solution is cooled and concentrated in the crystallizers.  糖溶液在结晶器中被冷却和浓缩。
Crystallizers are essential for producing high-quality sugar crystals. 结晶器对于生产高质量的糖晶体至关重要。
synonyms: crystallizing tanks, crystallization equipment, crystallizing pans
antonyms: dissolvers, melters
noun
