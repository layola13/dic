factoryless:/ˈfæktərɪləs/
adjective
无工厂的；没有工厂的
Without factories; operating or existing without owning or operating manufacturing plants.
Apple is a largely factoryless company, relying on contract manufacturers to produce its products. 苹果公司基本上是一家无工厂的公司，依靠合同制造商来生产其产品。
Many tech companies adopt a factoryless model, focusing on design and marketing. 许多科技公司采用无厂模式，专注于设计和营销。
Synonyms: non-manufacturing, asset-light
Antonyms: manufacturing, factory-based
词性: 形容词
