limb:/lɪm/| n.|肢体; 大树枝|A limb is an arm or leg of a person or animal, or a large branch of a tree.|She broke a limb when she fell out of the tree. 她从树上摔下来摔断了一根肢体。|Synonym: appendage, branch, extremity|Antonym: torso, trunk|noun

limb:/lɪm/| n.|边缘;  界限|A limb is the outer edge or boundary of something, especially the sun, moon, or a star.|The sun dipped below the limb of the horizon. 太阳落到了地平线以下。|Synonym: edge, border, rim|Antonym: center, middle|noun
