carpenter:/ˈkɑːrpəntər/ (US), /ˈkɑːpɪntər/ (UK)| n.|木匠，木工|A person whose job is making and repairing wooden objects and structures.|The carpenter built a beautiful wooden deck. 木匠建造了一个漂亮的木制平台。|synonyms: woodworker, joiner|antonyms: none|noun

carpenter:/ˈkɑːrpəntər/ (US), /ˈkɑːpɪntər/ (UK)| v.|做木工活，当木匠|To work as a carpenter; to construct or repair something made of wood.|He carpented the shelves himself. 他自己做了这些架子。|synonyms: build, construct, craft|antonyms: demolish, destroy|verb
