automatically:/ˌɔːtəˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 自动地 |  In a way that happens by itself without human control.  Operating by automation. | The doors opened automatically. 门自动打开了。| The system automatically backs up your files every hour. 系统每小时自动备份你的文件。 | 近义词: spontaneously, mechanically, instinctively | 反义词: manually, deliberately | 词性: 副词

Etymology: From Late Latin automaticus + -ally.  "Automaticus" comes from Greek automatos "self-moving, self-acting," from autos "self" + matos "thinking, animated."
