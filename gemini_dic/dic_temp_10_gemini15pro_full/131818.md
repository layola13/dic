nonconsequentialness:/ˌnɒnkɒnsɪˈkwɛnʃəlɪs/| n.|无关紧要，不重要|The nonconsequentialness of the issue was apparent to everyone.  这个问题的无关紧要性对每个人来说都是显而易见的。|Example: He dismissed the matter with a wave of his hand, emphasizing its nonconsequentialness. 他挥了挥手，驳回了这件事，强调了它的无关紧要。|Synonyms: insignificance, triviality, unimportance|Antonyms: significance, importance, consequence|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + consequential + -ness.  Non- 表示否定，consequential 表示重要的，有影响的，-ness 名词后缀。
