motormouthed:/ˈmoʊtərˌmaʊðd/| adj.|多嘴的，喋喋不休的|Someone who is motormouthed talks incessantly and often indiscreetly.|He's a motormouthed idiot who can't keep a secret. 他是个多嘴的白痴，守不住秘密。|近义词：talkative, loquacious, garrulous, voluble|反义词：reticent, taciturn|词性:形容词
Etymology: motor + mouth + -ed.  "Motor" in this sense means something that drives or propels, as in a motor vehicle.  Thus, "motormouthed" describes someone whose mouth is constantly running, driven like a motor.
