oversensibleness:/ˌoʊvərˌsɛnsɪˈbɪlnəs/| n. | 过分敏感，神经质 |The state of being overly sensitive or easily offended.  Being affected by stimuli more intensely than is usual or desirable. |Her oversensibleness made it difficult for her to handle criticism. 她的过度敏感使她难以接受批评。| hypersensitivity, touchiness, sensitivity | insensitivity, indifference | noun

oversensitive:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsɪtɪv/| adj. | 过分敏感的，神经质的 | Excessively or unduly sensitive. Easily offended or upset. |He's oversensitive about his height. 他对自己的身高过于敏感。 | touchy, sensitive, thin-skinned | insensitive, thick-skinned | adjective
