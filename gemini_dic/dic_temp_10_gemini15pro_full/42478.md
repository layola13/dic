counterfiring:/ˈkaʊntərˌfaɪərɪŋ/ /ˈkaʊntərˌfaɪrɪŋ/
noun
反击火力，对抗射击
The act of returning fire, especially in artillery or other ranged weaponry, to suppress or destroy the enemy's fire.
The artillery engaged in counterfiring to silence the enemy's guns.  炮兵部队进行反击射击以压制敌人的炮火。
The counterfiring was so intense that it lit up the night sky. 反击火力如此猛烈，照亮了夜空。
近义词: counterattack, return fire, retaliatory fire
反义词: ceasefire, truce
