excommunicant: /ˌɛks kəˈmjuːnɪkənt/
n.
被绝罚者，革出教门者
A person who has been excommunicated, excluded from the communion or fellowship of a church or religious community.
He was declared an excommunicant for his heretical views.  他因为异端观点被宣布革出教门。

近义词: outcast, pariah, heretic
反义词: believer, member, adherent
词性: 名词


excommunicate: /ˌɛks kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt/
v.
把…逐出教会；开除教籍
To officially exclude someone from participation in the sacraments and services of the Christian Church.
The church excommunicated him for his blasphemy. 教会因他的亵渎神明而将他逐出教会。

近义词: banish, expel, ostracize
反义词: admit, include, welcome
词性: 动词
