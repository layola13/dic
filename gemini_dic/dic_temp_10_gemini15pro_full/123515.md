monotonously:/məˈnɒtənəsli/ /məˈnɑːtənəsli/| adv.|单调地，乏味地|In a manner that lacks variation in tone or pitch; tediously uniform or unvarying.|He spoke monotonously for an hour, and everyone was bored. 他单调地讲了一个小时，每个人都感到无聊。|The rain droned monotonously against the windowpane. 雨单调地敲打着窗玻璃。|近义词：tediously, dully, boringly|反义词：interestingly, excitingly, variedly|词性:副词
monotone:/ˈmɒnətəʊn/ /ˈmɑːnətoʊn/| n. adj.|单调，单调的|A single, unvaried tone or pitch; lacking in variety and interest.|The lecturer's monotone made it difficult to stay awake. 演讲者的单调语气让人难以保持清醒。|She delivered the news in a flat monotone. 她用平淡的单调语气宣布了这个消息。|近义词：droning, tediousness, uniformity|反义词：variety, diversity, excitement|词性:名词，形容词
