illusorily:/ɪˈluːsərɪli/
adverb
虚幻地；虚假地
in a way that appears to be real or true, but is not
He was illusorily happy. 他表面上很快乐，但实际上并非如此。
近义词: deceptively, falsely, misleadingly
反义词: genuinely, truly, realistically
词性: adverb

词源:
From illusory + -ly.  Illusory comes from the Late Latin illusorius “deceiving, mocking,” from Latin illudere "to mock, make sport of."
