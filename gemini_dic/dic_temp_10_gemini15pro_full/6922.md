ancile:/ˈænsɪl/| n.|圣物，圣盾|A sacred shield, especially one of the ancilia carried by priests of Mars in ancient Rome, believed to have fallen from heaven and to be a pledge of Roman dominion.|The ancile was kept in the temple and guarded carefully. 圣盾被保存在寺庙里，并受到严密保护。|Synonym: relic, sacred object|Antonym: profane object|noun

ancile:/ˈænsɪl/| adj.|附属的，辅助的|Serving as an aid; auxiliary.|The ancile project helped the main research. 附属项目帮助了主要研究。|Synonym: auxiliary, supplementary, subsidiary|Antonym: primary, main|adjective
