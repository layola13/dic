dehumanizing:/ˌdiːhjʊmənaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
使丧失人性的；非人化的
Depriving of human qualities or attributes; making somebody less humane.
The prisoners were subjected to dehumanizing treatment.  囚犯们受到了非人道的待遇。

The refugees described the dehumanizing conditions in the camp.  难民们描述了营地里不人道的状况。

近义词: degrading, brutalizing, inhuman, inhumane
反义词: humanizing, ennobling, uplifting
词性: adjective
