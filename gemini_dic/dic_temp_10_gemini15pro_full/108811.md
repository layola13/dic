leatherwood: /ˈleðərwʊd/ (US), /ˈleðəwʊd/ (UK)
n.|皮革木；澳洲月桂|A type of Australian tree (Eucryphia lucida) known for its fragrant flowers and the honey produced from its nectar. Also refers to the honey itself.|The leatherwood tree is native to Tasmania. 皮革木树原产于塔斯马尼亚。|The leatherwood honey has a unique, spicy flavor. 皮革木蜂蜜有一种独特的辛辣味。|Synonyms: Tasmanian leatherwood, Eucryphia lucida|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
