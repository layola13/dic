contemplatist:/ˈkɒntəmpleɪtɪst/ (US) /ˈkɑːntəmpleɪtɪst/| n.|沉思者；冥想者；静观者|A person who spends much time in contemplation; one given to contemplation.|She was a contemplatist, preferring quiet reflection to social gatherings. 她是一个沉思者，喜欢安静的思考而不是社交聚会。|近义词：meditator, thinker, ponderer, reflector|反义词：extrovert, doer|词性: 名词

contemplative:/kənˈtɛmplətɪv/ (US) /kənˈtɛmplətɪv/| adj.|沉思的；冥想的；静观的|Characterized by or given to contemplation; thoughtful.|He has a contemplative nature and enjoys spending time alone in nature. 他天性沉思，喜欢独自在大自然中度过时光。|近义词：thoughtful, meditative, reflective, pensive|反义词：rash, impulsive|词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin contemplativus, from Latin contemplatus, past participle of contemplari "to gaze attentively, observe, contemplate," from con- "together" (see con-) + templum "place for observation, consecrated place, temple" (see temple (n.)).
