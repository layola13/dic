/ɪst/ | n. | -ist |  人;主义者;…的专家 |-ist is a suffix, meaning "one who does or makes," "one who practices or is concerned with,"  "one who believes in or advocates,"  or "member of a party or sect." | A scientist is someone who studies science.  科学家是研究科学的人。|  specialist, expert, professional |  amateur, novice | suffix

/ɪst/ | adj. | -ist |  …主义的 | -ist can also be an adjective suffix meaning "relating to or characteristic of a specified practice, system, or movement." | Socialist ideas are popular among some groups.  社会主义思想在某些群体中很受欢迎。 | socialist, communist, capitalist |  - | suffix
