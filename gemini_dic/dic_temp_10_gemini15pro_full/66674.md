exode:/ˈeksəʊd/ (UK) /ˈeksoʊd/ (US)| n.|大批离去，出走; Exodus(《出埃及记》)|A mass departure or emigration. The company witnessed a mass exode of employees after the new CEO took over.  公司在新任首席执行官接任后，员工大批离职。|近义词：departure, emigration, exodus, migration|反义词：influx, arrival, immigration|noun
词源: From French exode, from Ancient Greek ἔξοδος (éxodos, “a going out”), from ἐξ (ex, “out of”) + ὁδός (hodós, “way, road”).
