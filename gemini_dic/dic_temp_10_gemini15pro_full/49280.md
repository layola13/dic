deliberativeness:/dɪˈlɪbərətɪvnəs/| n.|深思熟虑，审慎|The deliberativeness of the judge was evident in his careful consideration of all the evidence.|法官的深思熟虑体现在他对所有证据的仔细考量上。|近义词：thoughtfulness, care, caution, prudence|反义词：hastiness, impulsiveness, recklessness|noun

Etymology:
From deliberate (adjective) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  Deliberate comes from Latin *dēlīberātus*, past participle of *dēlīberāre* "to weigh in mind, consider, consult, deliberate," from *de-* "thoroughly" + *lībra* "balance, scales" (source also of English *pound*).
