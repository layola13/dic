abscissa:/æbˈsɪsə/ (US) /əbˈsɪsə/ (UK)| n.|横坐标|The horizontal (x) coordinate in a two-dimensional Cartesian coordinate system, representing the distance of a point from the y-axis.|The abscissa of the point (3,2) is 3.  该点(3,2)的横坐标是3。

The graph shows the relationship between temperature (abscissa) and pressure (ordinate).  该图显示了温度（横坐标）和压力（纵坐标）之间的关系。|近义词:x-coordinate, horizontal coordinate|反义词:ordinate|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "linea abscissa" (cut-off line), past participle of "abscindere" (to cut off), from "ab" (from) + "scindere" (to cut, cleave).
