chronology:/krəˈnɒlədʒi/ /krəˈnɑːlədʒi/| n. |年表，年代学；按时间顺序排列|The book is arranged in chronological order, starting with his birth and ending with his death. 这本书按时间顺序排列，从他的出生写到他的死亡。|例句：The chronology of events is unclear.  事件的先后顺序不清楚。|近义词：timeline, sequence, history|反义词:anachronism, randomness|noun
