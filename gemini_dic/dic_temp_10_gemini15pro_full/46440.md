dagorath:/ˈdɑːɡɔːræθ/| n.|末日决战，世界末日|The Dagorath is the final battle prophesied in Tolkien's mythology.|The prophecy speaks of the Dagorath, a final apocalyptic battle. 末日预言提到了达戈拉斯，一场最终的末世之战。|近义词：Armageddon, apocalypse, doomsday|反义词: peace, harmony|词性:noun


Etymology: From Quenya *dagor-rath "battle-end," from dagor "battle" and rath "end," related to Sindorin Dagorlad "Battle Plain."
