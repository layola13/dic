normalizing:/ˈnɔːməlaɪzɪŋ/| v.| 正常化，标准化 |The process of bringing or returning something to a normal condition or state. Making something conform to a standard. |例句：They are normalizing relations with neighbouring countries. 他们正在与邻国关系正常化。

例句：The software helps normalizing the data.  这个软件帮助标准化数据。

|近义词：standardizing, regularizing, adjusting|反义词：distorting, disrupting, exceptionalizing|词性:动词(verb)


normalizing:/ˈnɔːməlaɪzɪŋ/| adj.| 标准化的，正常化的|Relating to or involving the process of normalizing. |例句：The normalizing effect of exercise on blood pressure is well documented. 运动对血压的正常化作用已被充分证明。

例句：The company is undergoing a normalizing process after a period of rapid growth.  公司在经历一段快速增长后正在进行标准化流程。

|近义词：standard, regular, typical|反义词：abnormal, irregular, atypical|词性:形容词(adjective)
