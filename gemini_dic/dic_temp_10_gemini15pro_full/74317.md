fracturing:/ˈfræktʃərɪŋ/| n.|压裂，破裂|The fracturing of the rock created a fissure. 岩石的破裂产生了一条裂缝。|例句：Hydraulic fracturing is used to extract natural gas from shale rock. 水力压裂用于从页岩中提取天然气。|近义词：breaking, cracking, splitting, rupturing|反义词:mending, repairing, uniting, fusing|noun

fracturing:/ˈfræktʃərɪŋ/| adj.|破裂的，断裂的|The fracturing ice made it dangerous to cross the lake. 破碎的冰使得穿越湖面变得危险。|例句：The fracturing sound of the bone breaking was sickening. 骨头断裂的声音令人作呕。|近义词：broken, cracked, split, ruptured|反义词:whole, intact, solid|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin fractura ("a breach, break"), from fractus, past participle of frangere ("to break").  The -ing suffix indicates a present participle or gerund, transforming the verb "fracture" into a noun or adjective.
