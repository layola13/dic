gaining:/ˈɡeɪnɪŋ/| v.|获得，赢得；增加；(钟表)走快；|She is gaining confidence. 她正在逐渐获得自信。|近义词：acquiring, obtaining, earning, increasing|反义词：losing, decreasing|词性: 动词 (Present Participle)
Etymology: From Middle English *gainen, *gaynen, from Old North French *gaainer, *gaaigner (compare Old French gaagnier, gaaignier), from Frankish *waidanjan (“to forage, hunt, catch, profit”), from Proto-Germanic *waidjaną (“to hunt, catch, pasture, graze”), from Proto-Indo-European *weyh₁- (“to chase, pursue”).
