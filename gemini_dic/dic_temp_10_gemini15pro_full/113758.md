macrophagous:/məˈkrɒfəɡəs/| adj.| 巨噬的，食大型食物的|Feeding on relatively large food particles.|Macrophagous animals, such as lions, consume large prey. 巨噬性动物，例如狮子，会吃掉大型猎物。|Example 2:  Certain fish are macrophagous, swallowing smaller fish whole.  某些鱼类是巨噬性的，会吞下整条小鱼。|synonyms: carnivorous, predatory|antonyms: microphagous, herbivorous|adjective
