fiasco:/fiˈæskoʊ/ /fiˈæskoʊ/| n. |惨败，彻底失败|A complete failure, especially in a ludicrous or humiliating way.|The play was a fiasco; the actors forgot their lines, the scenery collapsed, and the audience booed.|Synonyms: failure, debacle, disaster, flop|Antonyms: success, triumph|noun


fiaschi:/fiˈæskoʊz/ /fiˈæskoʊz/| n. |惨败（fiasco的复数形式）|The plural form of fiasco, referring to multiple complete failures.|Several of the company's new product launches were fiaschi, costing them millions.|Synonyms: failures, debacles, disasters, flops|Antonyms: successes, triumphs|noun
