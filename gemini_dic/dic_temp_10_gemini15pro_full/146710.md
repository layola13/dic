overhighly:/ˌoʊvərˈhaɪli/| adv.|过高地，过分地| Excessively, to an extreme degree, more than is necessary or appropriate. It combines "over," meaning "excessively," with "highly," meaning "to a great degree."| He overhighly praised her performance, making it seem exaggerated. 他过分赞扬了她的表演，显得有些夸张。| The steak was overhighly seasoned, making it too salty. 牛排的调味过重，太咸了。| 近义词: excessively, unduly, exorbitantly| 反义词: moderately, reasonably, appropriately| 词性: 副词
