overurges:/ˌoʊvərˈɜːrdʒɪz/ | v. | 过度催促，过分怂恿 |  To urge excessively or too persistently.  | He overurges his employees to work overtime, leading to burnout. 他过度催促员工加班，导致他们精疲力竭。 |  pressures, badgers, coerces | dissuades, discourages | verb

Etymology:
"Over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, over, beyond") + "urge" (from Old French urgier, from Latin urgēre, meaning "to press, drive, impel").
