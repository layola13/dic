aerosol:/ˈɛrəˌsɑːl/ (US), /ˈɛərəˌsɒl/ (UK)| n.| 气溶胶；喷雾剂| A colloid of fine solid particles or liquid droplets, in air or another gas.  A substance enclosed under pressure and able to be released as a fine spray, typically by means of a propellant gas.|例句：The hairspray is an aerosol. 这款发胶是喷雾剂。

例句：Air pollution can be caused by aerosols.  空气污染可能是由气溶胶造成的。|近义词：spray, mist, fog|反义词: (没有明确的反义词)|词性:noun
