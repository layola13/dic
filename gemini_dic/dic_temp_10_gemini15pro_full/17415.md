beaming:/ˈbiːmɪŋ/| adj.|容光焕发的，喜气洋洋的|Shining brightly; radiating happiness.|She had a beaming smile after receiving the good news. 她收到好消息后笑容满面。|近义词：radiant, glowing, smiling|反义词：glum, sullen|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the verb "beam," meaning "to shine brightly" or "to emit rays of light," from Old English *bēam, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *baumaz. The sense of "smiling radiantly" developed figuratively.
