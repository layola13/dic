hyper-emotive:/ˌhaɪpərɪˈmoʊtɪv/ |adj.|过度情绪化的；极度情绪化的|Characterized by excessive emotion or readily displaying exaggerated emotional reactions.|Her hyper-emotive response to the news surprised everyone. 她对这个消息过度情绪化的反应让每个人都感到惊讶。|The child's hyper-emotive outburst disrupted the entire classroom.  这个孩子过度情绪化的爆发扰乱了整个教室。|近义词：overemotional, overly sensitive, dramatic, hysterical|反义词：calm, composed, impassive, stoic|词性: 形容词

词源:
"hyper-" (Greek): over, above, excessive
"-emotive" (Latin): relating to emotion
