ovule:/ˈoʊvjuːl/ (US), /ˈɒvjuːl/ (UK)| n. |胚珠|A small structure in seed plants, containing the female germ cell and after fertilization becoming the seed.|The ovule develops into a seed after fertilization. 胚珠受精后发育成种子。|The plant’s ovules were damaged by the frost. 植物的胚珠被霜冻损坏了。|Synonyms:  megasporangium, seed bud|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
