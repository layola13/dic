onis:/ˈoʊnɪs/| n. |负担，重担|A burden or heavy responsibility.|He carried the onis of guilt for years.  他多年来背负着罪恶感。|近义词：burden, load, weight, responsibility|反义词: relief, comfort|词性:名词

词源: From Ancient Greek ὄνις (ónis, “dung, manure”).  The connection is likely through the idea of something unpleasant or burdensome.
