graveled:/ˈɡrævəld/ | adj. | 铺满砾石的；声音沙哑的 | Covered with gravel; (of a voice) hoarse or rasping. | The road was long and graveled. 这条路又长又满是砾石。|  Her voice was graveled with exhaustion. 她因疲惫而声音沙哑。 | 近义词: gravelly, pebbly, stony, hoarse, raspy | 反义词: smooth, clear | 词性:形容词

gravel:/ˈɡrævəl/ | n. | 砾石 |  A loose aggregation of small water-worn or pounded stones. | The path was covered in gravel. 小路铺满了砾石。 | 近义词: stones, pebbles, shingle | 反义词: sand, dust | 词性:名词

gravel:/ˈɡrævəl/ | vt. | 用砾石铺；使困惑 | Cover with gravel; puzzle or irritate. |  They graveled the driveway. 他们在车道上铺了砾石。 | The complex problem graveled him.  这个复杂的问题让他很困惑。 | 近义词: cover, pave, confound, perplex | 反义词: uncover, clear | 词性:动词
