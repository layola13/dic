gravitational:/ˌɡrævɪˈteɪʃənəl/
adjective
重力的；引力的
relating to gravity or gravitation
例句：The gravitational force pulls objects towards the center of the Earth. 重力将物体拉向地球中心。

例句：Gravitational waves were predicted by Einstein's theory of general relativity. 引力波是由爱因斯坦的广义相对论预言的。

近义词：gravity-related
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective
