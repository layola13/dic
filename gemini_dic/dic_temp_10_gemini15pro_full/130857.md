nonaquatic:/ˌnɑːn əˈkwɑːtɪk/
adj.
非水生的；陆生的
Not living or occurring in water; terrestrial.
例句：Nonaquatic animals like lizards thrive in deserts.  像蜥蜴这样的非水生动物在沙漠中繁衍生息。

例句：Cacti are nonaquatic plants adapted to arid environments. 仙人掌是非水生植物，适应干旱环境。

近义词：terrestrial, land-based
反义词：aquatic, marine
词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + aquatic.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "aquatic" refers to water.
