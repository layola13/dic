cubiform:/ˈkjuːbɪfɔːrm/
adjective
立方形的，立方体的
Having the form of a cube; shaped like a cube.
The mineral formed small, cubiform crystals. 这种矿物形成了小的立方体晶体。
The child played with brightly colored, cubiform blocks. 孩子玩着色彩鲜艳的立方体积木。
近义词: cubic, cube-shaped
反义词: amorphous, shapeless
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *cubiformis, from cubus (“cube”) + -formis (“-shaped”).
