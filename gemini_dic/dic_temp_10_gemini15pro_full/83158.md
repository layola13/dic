gutterling:/ˈɡʌtərˌlɪŋ/ | n. |街头顽童; 流氓; 下贱的人|A person of low moral character, often associated with the streets.  A mischievous or troublesome child, especially one from a poor background. | The gutterlings were throwing rocks at passing cars. 那些街头顽童向过往的汽车扔石头。 |Synonyms: urchin, ragamuffin, waif, stray, rogue, scoundrel | Antonyms: gentleman, lady, noble | noun
