necessitates:/nəˈsesɪteɪts/
verb
使…成为必需，需要
to make something necessary
例句：Increased traffic necessitates widening the road. 交通量的增加使得拓宽道路成为必要。
例句：The project necessitates close collaboration between the two teams. 这个项目需要两个团队紧密合作。
近义词：requires, demands, calls for
反义词：obviates, precludes
词性:v.
