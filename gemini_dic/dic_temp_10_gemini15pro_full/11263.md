-archy:/ˈɑːrki/| n. |统治；政府；统治集团；统治体系|A combining form meaning “rule,” “government,” or “chief”|  Patriarchy is a social system in which men hold primary power. 父权制是一个男性掌握主要权力的社会体系。|  Monarchy is a form of government with a monarch at the head. 君主制是一种以君主为首的政府形式。 |Synonym: rule, dominion, government|Antonym: anarchy, disorder|Noun
