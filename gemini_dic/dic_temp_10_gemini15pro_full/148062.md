oversalty: /ˌoʊvərˈsɔːlti/ | adj. | 过咸的 | Containing too much salt. | The soup was oversalty and I couldn't finish it. 汤太咸了，我没能喝完。| The steak was a bit oversalty for my taste.  这牛排对我来说有点太咸了。| 近义词：too salty, excessively salty | 反义词: undersalted, bland | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "salty" (adjective meaning "containing salt")
