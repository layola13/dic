let-outs:/ˈlɛtˌaʊts/
noun
解脱；出租；出路；放宽的衣服
A means of escape or release from a commitment or difficulty.  A clause in a contract allowing for termination or modification under specified conditions.  Clothes that have been altered to be larger.
The contract included a let-out clause in case the project went over budget.  合同中包含一项如果项目超出预算可以终止的条款。

The tailor added some let-outs to the seams of the dress.  裁缝在连衣裙的接缝处加了一些放宽。

Her busy social life was a let-out from the pressures of work. 她忙碌的社交生活是她从工作压力中解脱出来的途径。
synonyms: escape clause, loophole, excuse, release, way out, breathing space
antonyms: commitment, obligation, restriction, confinement
词性: 名词 (n.)
