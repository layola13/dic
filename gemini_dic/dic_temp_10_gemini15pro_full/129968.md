non-migratory:/ˌnɒnˈmaɪɡrətri/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈmaɪɡrətɔːri/ (UK)| adj.|非迁徙的，不迁徙的|Not migratory; not migrating.  Referring to animals or birds that do not move from one region to another with the changing seasons.|Robins are non-migratory birds in many parts of the UK. 在英国的许多地方，知更鸟是不迁徙的鸟类。|resident, sedentary|migratory, nomadic|adjective
