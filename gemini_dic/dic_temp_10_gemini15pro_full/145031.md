overafflicted:/ˌoʊvərəˈflɪktɪd/
adjective
过度苦恼的，饱受折磨的
Suffering from excessive affliction; burdened by many troubles or hardships.
The overafflicted population needed immediate aid and support.  过度苦恼的民众需要立即的援助和支持。
The overafflicted farmer lost his crops to the drought and his home to the fire.  饱受折磨的农民在旱灾中失去了庄稼，又在火灾中失去了家园。
近义词: distressed, overwhelmed, burdened, beleaguered
反义词: fortunate, blessed, untroubled


词性: adjective

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "afflicted" (past participle of "afflict," from Latin "affligere," meaning "to strike down, weaken").
