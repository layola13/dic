manageless:/ˈmænɪdʒləs/| adj.|无管理的，无人管理的|Lacking management or managers; not subject to management.  Characterized by the absence of traditional hierarchical management structures, often associated with self-organizing teams or decentralized decision-making.|The company experimented with a manageless structure, empowering employees to take ownership of their work.|Synonyms: self-managed, autonomous, decentralized, leaderless|Antonyms: managed, hierarchical, centralized|adjective
