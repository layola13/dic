brutality: /bruːˈtælɪti/
| n. | 残暴；野蛮；残酷 |  the quality or state of being brutal; cruel and violent behavior | The brutality of the attack shocked the community.  这次袭击的残暴震惊了整个社区。 | 近义词：cruelty, savagery, barbarity, viciousness | 反义词：gentleness, kindness, compassion, humanity | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Middle French *brutalité, from Late Latin brutalitas (“brutishness”), from brutalis (“brutish, irrational, animal”), from brutus (“brute, dull, stupid, irrational”).
