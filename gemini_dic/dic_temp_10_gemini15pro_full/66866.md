expecter:/ɪkˈspɛktər/| n.|期待者，预期者|Someone who expects something to happen. |The expecters of the new technology were eager to see its release.  新技术的期待者们渴望看到它的发布。|近义词：anticipator, awaiter|反义词: doubter, skeptic|词性:noun

Etymology:  From the verb "expect," which comes from the Latin "exspectare" (to look out for, await), from "ex-" (out) + "spectare" (to look).
