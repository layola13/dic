nonbreeding:/ˌnɒnˈbriːdɪŋ/
adjective
非繁殖的，非繁殖期的
Not involved in or related to breeding.
例句：The nonbreeding plumage of the bird is less colorful.  这种鸟非繁殖期的羽毛颜色较暗淡。

例句：Nonbreeding season is a time for rest and molting.  非繁殖季节是休息和换羽的时间。

近义词：nonreproductive
反义词：breeding

词性
adjective
