homeopath:/ˈhoʊmiəpæθ/ (US), /ˈhəʊmiəpæθ/ (UK)| n.|顺势疗法医生|A practitioner of homeopathy, a system of alternative medicine.|She consulted a homeopath for her allergies. 她因过敏咨询了一位顺势疗法医生。|Synonym: homeopathic physician, homeopathic doctor|Antonym: allopath, conventional doctor|noun

homeopathy:/ˌhoʊmiˈɑːpəθi/ (US), /ˌhəʊmiˈɒpəθi/ (UK)| n.|顺势疗法|A system of alternative medicine based on the principle of "like cures like," using highly diluted substances.|Homeopathy is considered by many to be pseudoscience. 许多人认为顺势疗法是伪科学。|Synonym: homeopathic medicine|Antonym: allopathic medicine, conventional medicine|noun
