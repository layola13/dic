netsuke:/ˌnɛtˈsukeɪ/ /ˈnɛtskeɪ/ | n. |根付|A small, often intricately carved, toggle traditionally worn by Japanese men, used to secure the cords of small pouches or containers to their sashes.  |Example 1: The netsuke, carved in the shape of a grinning fox, was a family heirloom.  这枚雕刻成咧嘴笑的狐狸形状的根付是一件传家宝。

Example 2:  He collected netsuke, admiring the artistry and miniature scale of the carvings. 他收集根付，欣赏雕刻的艺术性和微型规模。|近义词：toggle, fob|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
