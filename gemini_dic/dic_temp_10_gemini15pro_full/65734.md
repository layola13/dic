everchanging:/ˈevərˌtʃeɪndʒɪŋ/
adjective
不断变化的
Constantly changing; in a state of flux.
The fashion industry is everchanging, with new trends emerging every season.  时尚行业瞬息万变，每个季节都有新的潮流出现。
The political landscape is everchanging, making it difficult to predict the future. 政治格局瞬息万变，难以预测未来。
Synonyms:  dynamic, fluid, volatile, mutable, variable
Antonyms: static, constant, unchanging, fixed, permanent
词性: 形容词
