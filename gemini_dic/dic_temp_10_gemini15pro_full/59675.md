echo:/ˈekoʊ/ (US), /ˈekəʊ/ (UK)| n. v.|回声，回音；共鸣；重复；效仿|A sound or other signal repeated by reflection.|The canyon echoed with the sound of their laughter. 峡谷回荡着他们的笑声。|synonyms: reverberation, reflection, repetition|antonyms: silence, absorption|noun

echo:/ˈekoʊ/ (US), /ˈekəʊ/ (UK)| v.|发出回声；重复；效仿|To repeat (a sound) by reflection; to repeat (someone's words or opinions), especially to express agreement.|The empty room echoed his footsteps. 空荡荡的房间回荡着他的脚步声。|synonyms: reverberate, resonate, imitate, mirror|antonyms: originate, muffle|verb
