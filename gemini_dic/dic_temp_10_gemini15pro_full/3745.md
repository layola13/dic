agilest:/ˈædʒɪlɪst/| adj.|最敏捷的|Superlative form of "agile," meaning possessing the greatest degree of agility; able to move quickly and easily.|Example: She is the agilest gymnast on the team. 她是队里最敏捷的体操运动员。|Example: The agilest monkey swung effortlessly through the trees.  最敏捷的猴子毫不费力地在树间荡来荡去。|近义词：nimblest, quickest, most flexible|反义词:clumsiest, slowest, stiffest|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *agilis, from agere "to do, drive, act."
