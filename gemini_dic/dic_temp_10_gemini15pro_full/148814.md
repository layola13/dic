overventilated:/ˌoʊvərˈvɛntɪleɪtɪd/
adjective
过度换气的
having breathed too rapidly and deeply, causing excessive loss of carbon dioxide from the blood.
The patient was overventilated and became dizzy. 病人过度换气，感到头晕。
近义词:hyperventilated
反义词:hypoventilated
词性:adjective

overventilate:/ˌoʊvərˈvɛntɪleɪt/
