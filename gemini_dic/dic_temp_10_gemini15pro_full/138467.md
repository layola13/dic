novelist: /ˈnɒvəlɪst/ (British) /ˈnɑːvəlɪst/ (American)| n. |小说家|A person who writes novels.|例句：The novelist spent years researching her historical novel.  这位小说家花了数年时间研究她的历史小说。

例句：He is a renowned novelist, known for his complex characters and intricate plots. 他是一位著名的作家，以其复杂的人物和错综复杂的情节而闻名。|近义词：author, writer, fiction writer|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
Etymology: Late Middle English: from novel + -ist.
