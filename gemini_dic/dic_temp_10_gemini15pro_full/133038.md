nonenvious:/nɒnˈɛnviəs/ | adj. | 无嫉妒心的，不羡慕的 | Not feeling or showing envy; free from envy. | She felt a nonenvious admiration for her friend's success. 她对朋友的成功感到一种没有嫉妒的钦佩。|  He cast a nonenvious glance at his neighbor's new car.  他瞥了一眼邻居的新车，没有一丝羡慕。 | 近义词: unenvious, free from envy | 反义词: envious, jealous | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- + envious.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "envious" comes from the Old French "envieus," meaning "full of envy."
