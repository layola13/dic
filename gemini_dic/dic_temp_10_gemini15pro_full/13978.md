aureoled: /ɔːriˈoʊld/ | adj. | 光环围绕的，aureoled | Surrounded by or as if by an aureole; haloed. |  The saint was depicted in a stained-glass window, aureoled in light.  圣徒被描绘在彩色玻璃窗上，周围环绕着光环。| The rising sun, aureoled in mist, looked magnificent.  冉冉升起的太阳，笼罩在薄雾中，看起来很壮观。| 近义词: haloed, nimbused, radiant, glorious | 反义词: dull, dark, dim | 词性: 形容词
词源: From the noun "aureole" + "-ed".  "Aureole" comes from the Old French "aureole", ultimately from the Latin "aureola", meaning "golden".
