inquisitionary:/ˌɪnkwɪˈzɪʃəneri/ | adj. | 审讯式的，盘问式的 |  Tending to resemble or suggesting an inquisition, especially in being harsh, relentless, or intrusive. |  The detective's inquisitionary tone made the suspect nervous.  侦探审讯式的语气让嫌疑人感到紧张。| synonyms:  interrogative, probing, scrutinizing, questioning, examining | antonyms: gentle, lenient, mild, understanding | word type: adjective

inquisitorial:/ɪnˌkwɪzɪˈtɔːriəl/ | adj. | 审讯式的，盘问式的 |  Relating to or characteristic of an inquisition. Similar to inquisitionary, but often implies a more formal or official process of inquiry. | The inquisitorial system of justice is used in some European countries.  一些欧洲国家采用审讯式司法制度。| synonyms: investigative, judicial, legal, examining, scrutinizing | antonyms:  lenient, mild, understanding | word type: adjective
