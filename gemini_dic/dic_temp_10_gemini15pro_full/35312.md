cnas:/ˈsiːnæs/ | n. | 中国合格评定国家认可委员会 (China National Accreditation Service for Conformity Assessment) | The CNAS is the national accreditation body of China.  It is responsible for accrediting certification bodies, laboratories, and inspection bodies. |  例句：The lab has received CNAS accreditation.  这家实验室已获得中国合格评定国家认可委员会的认可。

例句： CNAS accreditation enhances the credibility of testing reports. 中国合格评定国家认可委员会的认可提高了测试报告的可信度。 | 近义词：accreditation body, certification body | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "unaccredited organization") | 词性: noun
