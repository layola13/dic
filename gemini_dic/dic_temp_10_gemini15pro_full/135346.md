nonpensionable:/ˌnɒnˈpɛnʃənəbl/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈpɛnʃənəbl/ (UK)
adjective
不可领养老金的；无退休金的
Not eligible for a pension; not providing a pension.
His job was nonpensionable, so he had to save on his own for retirement.  他的工作没有养老金，所以他不得不自己存钱养老。
Part-time employees are often in nonpensionable positions. 兼职员工通常在没有养老金的职位上工作。
Synonyms: pensionless
Antonyms: pensionable
词性: adjective
