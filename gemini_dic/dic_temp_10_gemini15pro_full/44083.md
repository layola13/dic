criosphinx:/ˈkraɪoʊsfɪŋks/| n. |冰狮身人面像|A mythical creature with the body of a lion and the head of a ram, often depicted in ice or snow.  It combines elements of the Greek sphinx and arctic imagery.|例句：The artist sculpted a magnificent criosphinx from a block of ice. 艺术家从一块冰块中雕刻出一个雄伟的冰狮身人面像。

例句：Legends tell of criosphinxes guarding ancient glacial pathways. 传说冰狮身人面像守护着古老的冰川通道。|近义词：ice sculpture, snow sculpture|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) fire creature, lava creature|词性:noun
