harehound:/ˈheə(r)ˌhaʊnd/| n. |野兔; 灰白毛猎犬; 一种唇形科植物(夏至草);|A plant of the mint family, with downy leaves and small, whitish flowers. Also refers to a type of hound used for hunting hares. |The harehound thrives in dry, rocky soil. 夏至草在干燥的岩石土壤中生长茂盛。|The harehound pursued the hare relentlessly. 灰白毛猎犬无情地追赶野兔。|近义词：horehound (for the plant), greyhound (for the dog)|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
