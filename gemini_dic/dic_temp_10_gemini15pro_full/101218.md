invisibly:/ɪnˈvɪzəbli/
adv.
无形地，看不见地
in a way that cannot be seen
The thief invisibly slipped the watch into his pocket. 小偷悄悄地把手表放进了口袋。
Synonyms: imperceptibly, unnoticeably, stealthily
Antonyms: visibly, noticeably, openly
词性: 副词
