decipherable:/dɪˈsaɪfərəbl/
adjective
可破译的，可辨认的
able to be deciphered or understood.
The code was complex, but eventually decipherable. 这密码很复杂，但最终还是可以破译的。
The handwriting was messy but decipherable. 字迹很潦草，但还是可以辨认的。
synonyms: understandable, legible, readable, interpretable
antonyms: indecipherable, unreadable, illegible, incomprehensible
adjective
