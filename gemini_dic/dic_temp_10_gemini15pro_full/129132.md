ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *no*, from Old English *nā* (“no, not, not at all, not ever, never”), from Proto-Germanic *nai* (“never, not”), from Proto-Indo-European *ne* (“not”). Cognate with Scots *nae* (“no”), West Frisian *nee* (“no”), Dutch *nee* (“no”), German *nein* (“no”), Danish *nej* (“no”), Swedish *nej* (“no”), Icelandic *nei* (“no”).
