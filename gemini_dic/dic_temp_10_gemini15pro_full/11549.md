aristocracies: /ˌærɪˈstɒkrəsiːz/ (US) /ˌærɪˈstɒkrəsiːz/ (UK)
| n. |贵族统治；贵族；贵族阶级|A government or state ruled by an aristocracy, or a privileged upper class.  The aristocracy as a class.  A group or class considered to be superior, as in intellect, wealth, or social status.| The French Revolution aimed to overthrow the aristocracies. 法国大革命旨在推翻贵族统治。| Synonyms: nobility, elite, gentry, upper class| Antonyms: commoners, proletariat, masses
