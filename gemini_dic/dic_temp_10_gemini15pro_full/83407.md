habendum:/həˈbɛndəm/
n.
habendum clause (property law)
财产法中的占有条款
A clause in a deed or lease that defines the extent of the interest being granted and any conditions affecting the grant.
契约或租赁中定义授予权益的范围以及影响授予的任何条件的条款。
The habendum clause in the deed specified that the property was to be used for residential purposes only.  契约中的占有条款规定，该房产仅用于住宅用途。
The habendum clause clearly outlined the terms of the lease, including the duration and any restrictions on use.  占有条款清楚地概述了租赁条款，包括期限和任何使用限制。
近义词:granting clause, provision
反义词:None
词性:noun
