impenetrablenesses:/ɪmˌpɛnɪtrəˈbɪlnəsɪz/
|n.| 不可渗透性；难以理解|The impenetrablenesses of the jungle made navigation extremely difficult.|丛林的难以穿透性使得航行极其困难。|
|近义词:impermeability, opacity, inscrutability, incomprehensibility|反义词:permeability, transparency, clarity, comprehensibility|词性: 名词|
|英文解析:The state or quality of being impenetrable; the inability to be penetrated or understood.|
|例句 1: The impenetrablenesses of his arguments frustrated everyone in the room.  房间里每个人都对他的论点难以理解感到沮丧。|

|例句 2:  The scientist studied the impenetrablenesses of certain materials to radiation.  科学家研究了某些材料对辐射的不可渗透性。|
