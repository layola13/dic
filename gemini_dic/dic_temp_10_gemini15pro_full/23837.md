breadboarded:/ˈbredˌbɔːrdɪd/
adjective|用试验板组装的|Assembled on a breadboard.|The breadboarded circuit worked perfectly. 用试验板组装的电路工作完美。|prototyped, experimental|permanent, finalized, production|adj.
