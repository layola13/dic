mogul:/ˈmoʊɡəl/ | n. | 大亨，巨头，显要人物 | A powerful or influential person, especially in the business or entertainment industry. |  He's a media mogul, owning several newspapers and television stations. 他是一位媒体大亨，拥有几家报纸和电视台。


She's a fashion mogul with her own clothing line and perfume. 她是一位时尚巨头，拥有自己的服装系列和香水。 |  tycoon, magnate, baron, bigwig, VIP | nobody, commoner | noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From Urdu مغل (muġal), from Persian مغول (muġol), from Mongolian ᠮᠣᠩᠭᠣᠯ (mongɣol). Originally referred to a member of the Mughal dynasty that ruled India from the 16th to the 19th century.  The meaning later broadened to refer to any powerful or influential person.
