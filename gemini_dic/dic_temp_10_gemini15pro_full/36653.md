collegian:/kəˈliːdʒən/
n.
大学生，学院学生
A student in a college or university.
例句：The collegian spent long hours in the library preparing for his exams.  这个大学生在图书馆里花了很长时间准备考试。

例句：The group of collegians decided to start a study group for their challenging physics class.  这群大学生决定为他们具有挑战性的物理课成立一个学习小组。

近义词：student, undergraduate, pupil
反义词: professor, teacher, instructor
词性：noun
