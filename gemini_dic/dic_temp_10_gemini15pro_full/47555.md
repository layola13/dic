deauthorize: /diːˈɔːθəraɪz/ | v. | 取消授权，撤销授权 | To remove authorization or permission previously granted. | The company deauthorized the third-party app's access to user data.  该公司取消了第三方应用程序访问用户数据的授权。 | The administrator deauthorized the user's access to the server. 管理员取消了用户访问服务器的授权。 | Synonyms: revoke, rescind, cancel, withdraw | Antonyms: authorize, empower, grant | verb
