babylike:/ˈbeɪbiˌlaɪk/| adj.|婴儿般的，幼稚的|Resembling or characteristic of a baby, especially in being innocent, helpless, or endearing.|Her babylike features charmed everyone. 她婴儿般的容貌迷住了所有人。|近义词：childlike, infantile, innocent|反义词：mature, adult, grown-up|词性:形容词
词源: baby + like.  "Baby" comes from Middle English *babi*, of uncertain origin. "Like" comes from Old English *gelīc*.
