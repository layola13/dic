graves: /ɡreɪvz/ | n. |坟墓; 墓穴; 严重性; 重大; |A place of burial, typically a hole dug in the ground. |  They visited their ancestors' graves. 他们去拜祭了祖先的坟墓。| synonyms: tombs, sepulchers, burial grounds | antonyms:  birthplace | noun


graves: /ɡreɪvz/ | adj. |严重的; 严肃的; |Serious and solemn. | The doctor's face was grave as he delivered the news. 医生表情严肃地宣布了这个消息。| synonyms: serious, solemn, somber | antonyms:  trivial, lighthearted | adjective


Etymology: From Old English *græf, from Proto-Germanic *grabą (“grave, ditch”), from Proto-Indo-European *ghrebh- (“to dig, to scratch, to scrape”). Cognate with Dutch graf (“grave”), German Grab (“grave”), Swedish grav (“grave”).
