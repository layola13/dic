auspices:/ˈɔːspɪsɪz/
n.
支持，赞助；保护；预兆
protection or support; patronage; a sign of what will happen in the future
The event was held under the auspices of the local council.  这次活动是在地方议会的支持下举行的。
patronage, sponsorship, support, aegis, backing
opposition, hindrance, obstruction
noun
