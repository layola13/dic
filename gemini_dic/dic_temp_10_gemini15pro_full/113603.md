macerating:/ˈmæsəreɪtɪŋ/
v.
浸渍；软化
softening or breaking into pieces using liquid
The chef is macerating the fruit in wine. 厨师正在用葡萄酒浸渍水果。
近义词：soaking, steeping, softening
反义词：drying, hardening
词性：动词

macerate:/ˈmæsəreɪt/
v.
浸渍；软化
to soften or break into pieces using liquid
The fruit was macerated in wine overnight. 水果在葡萄酒中浸泡了一夜。
近义词：soak, steep, soften
反义词：dry, harden
词性：动词

maceration:/ˌmæsəˈreɪʃn/
n.
浸渍；软化
the process of softening or breaking into pieces using liquid
The maceration of the fruit in wine enhanced its flavor.  水果在葡萄酒中的浸渍增强了它的风味。
近义词：soaking, steeping, softening
反义词：drying, hardening
词性：名词


词源：From Latin *maceratus, past participle of macerare (“to make soft, to steep, to soak”), from macer (“lean, thin”).
