hypersthenic:/ˌhaɪpərˈsθɛnɪk/
adjective
体格强健的；亢进的
Relating to or exhibiting hypersthenia, characterized by excessive strength, tone, or activity.
She has a hypersthenic physique, with a broad chest and short limbs. 她体格强健，胸部宽阔，四肢短小。
The patient presented with hypersthenic muscle activity. 病人表现出亢进的肌肉活动。
近义词: robust, vigorous, athletic, energetic
反义词: asthenic, weak, frail
词性: adjective

词源:
From Greek *hyper* (over, above) + *sthenos* (strength).
