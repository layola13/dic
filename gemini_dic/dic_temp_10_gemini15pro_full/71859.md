fleshiest:/ˈflɛʃiɪst/
adjective
最肉的；最丰满的
Having the most flesh; plumpest.
The fleshiest peaches are often the ripest.  最肉的桃子通常最成熟。
The butcher recommended the fleshiest cut of beef. 肉贩推荐了最肥厚的牛肉块。
近义词: plumpest, chubbiest, meatiest
反义词: thinnest, leanest, boniest
词性: adjective
词源: From flesh + -y + -est.  来自 flesh（肉）+ -y（形容词后缀）+ -est（最高级后缀）。
