garter: /ˈɡɑːrtər (UK) /ˈɡɑːrdər (US)/
n.|吊袜带，袜带|A band worn around the leg, typically above the knee, to hold up a stocking or sock.|She adjusted her garter before going on stage. 她上台前整理了一下吊袜带。|Synonyms: stocking suspender, sock suspender|Antonyms: None|Noun

garter: /ˈɡɑːrtər (UK) /ˈɡɑːrdər (US)/
v.|用袜带系住|To fasten something with a garter.|She gartered her stockings carefully. 她仔细地用袜带系住长袜。|Synonyms: fasten, secure|Antonyms: unfasten, loosen|Verb
