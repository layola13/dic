clypeastroid:/ˌklɪpiˈæstrɔɪd/| n.|楯形海胆|A sea urchin of the order Clypeasteroida, having a flattened test and short spines.|The clypeastroid was found partially buried in the sand.|近义词:sand dollar, sea biscuit|反义词:None|词性:Noun
Etymology:  From Clypeaster + -oid.  Clypeaster comes from Greek *klupeus* "shield" + *aster* "star".  -oid means "resembling" or "having the form of".
