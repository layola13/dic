ganglike:/ˈɡæŋˌlaɪk/
adjective
像帮派的；类似黑帮的
Resembling or characteristic of a gang, especially in being threatening or violent.
The group had a ganglike mentality, always looking for trouble.  这群人有一种帮派心态，总是到处找麻烦。
The youths loitered on the corner with a ganglike swagger. 这些年轻人在街角闲逛，带着一种类似黑帮的招摇 swagger。
近义词: thuggish, hoodlum-like, gangster-like
反义词: law-abiding, respectable


词性: adjective
