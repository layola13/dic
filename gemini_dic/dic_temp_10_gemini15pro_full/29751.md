cator:/ˈkeɪtər/| v.| 迎合; 投合; 提供饮食及服务 | To provide food and drinks for an event or organization. | Example:  They catered the wedding reception. 他们为婚礼招待会提供餐饮服务。| Synonyms: provide, supply, furnish | Antonyms: deprive, withhold, refuse | verb
