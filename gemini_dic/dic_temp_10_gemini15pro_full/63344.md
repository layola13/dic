entodermic:/ˌɛntəˈdɜːrmɪk/ | adj. | 内胚层的，内胚叶的 | Relating to or derived from the entoderm (also called endoderm), the innermost of the three primary germ layers in the early embryo.  |  The entodermic cells differentiate into the lining of the digestive tract and its associated organs. 内胚层细胞分化成消化道及其相关器官的内壁。 |  The lungs develop from an entodermic outpouching of the foregut. 肺是由前肠的内胚层向外突出而发育来的。 |  Synonyms: endodermal, hypoblastic | Antonyms: ectodermic | adjective
