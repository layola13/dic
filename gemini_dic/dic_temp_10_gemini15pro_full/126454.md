mytacism:/ˈmaɪtəsɪzəm/
n.|口齿不清，m,n不分|Excessive or incorrect use of the letter M or the sound /m/.  The inability to pronounce the sounds /m/ and /n/, or confusion of these sounds with /b/ and /d/ respectively.|He suffered from mytacism and often pronounced 'name' as 'babe'. 他患有口齿不清，经常把“name”发成“babe”。|近义词：lisp,  dyslalia|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "clear articulation" or "perfect pronunciation")|词性:noun
