grappling:/ˈɡræpəlɪŋ/ /ˈɡræplɪŋ/ | n. | 格斗，扭打；努力解决（难题） | the act of struggling with another person, especially in order to throw or hold them down; the act of trying hard to deal with a difficult problem |  He was skilled in the art of grappling. 他精通格斗技巧。 | synonyms: wrestling, struggling, contending, tackling | antonyms: surrendering, yielding | noun

grapple:/ˈɡræpəl/ /ˈɡræpl/ | v. | 格斗，扭打；努力解决 | to fight, especially in order to win something; to try to deal with or understand a difficult problem or subject | The two men grappled with each other for the gun. 两个人扭打在一起抢枪。 | synonyms: wrestle, struggle, contend, tackle | antonyms: surrender, yield | verb
