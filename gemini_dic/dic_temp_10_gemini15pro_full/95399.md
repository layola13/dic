inchoateness:/ɪnˈkoʊɪtnəs/ /ɪnˈkəʊətnəs/ | n. | 初期状态，未完成状态，不成熟 | The state of being just beginning and not fully formed or developed. | The inchoateness of the plan made it difficult to implement. 计划的不成熟性使其难以实施。
