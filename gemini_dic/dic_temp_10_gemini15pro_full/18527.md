benefactress:/ˈbɛnɪfæktərɪs/| n.|女施主，女恩人|A woman who gives money or other help to a person or cause.|例句：The local hospital was built thanks to a generous benefactress. 这家当地医院的建成要感谢一位慷慨的女捐助者。

例句：She became a benefactress to the arts, supporting many struggling artists. 她成为了艺术的赞助人，支持了许多苦苦挣扎的艺术家。

|近义词：patron, donor, sponsor|反义词:malefactor|词性:noun
