hyperdelicately:/ˌhaɪpərˈdɛlɪkətli/
| adv. |极其精细地，非常微妙地 |  In an extremely delicate manner; with excessive delicacy.  | She hyperdelicately placed the antique china doll on the shelf. 她极其小心地把古董瓷娃娃放在架子上。| The surgeon hyperdelicately repaired the damaged nerve. 外科医生极其精细地修复了受损的神经。| 近义词: exquisitely, meticulously, painstakingly | 反义词: roughly, clumsily, carelessly | 词性: 副词

词源:  "hyper-" (prefix meaning "over, excessive") + "delicately" (adverb form of "delicate")
