antifur:/ˌæntiˈfɜːr/ | adj. | 反皮草的 | Opposed to the wearing of fur, especially for ethical reasons. |  Example: She joined the antifur protest outside the fashion show. 她参加了在时装秀外的反皮草抗议活动。 | Example:  He's very antifur and refuses to wear anything made from animal products. 他非常反对皮草，拒绝穿任何动物制品。 | 近义词: anti-fur (hyphenated form) | 反义词: pro-fur | 词性: adjective

antifur:/ˌæntiˈfɜːr/ | n. | 反皮草人士/反皮草运动 | A person or movement opposed to the wearing of fur.  | Example: The antifurs were protesting outside the department store. 反皮草人士在百货商店外抗议。 | Example: The antifur movement has gained momentum in recent years. 反皮草运动近年来势头强劲。 | 近义词: animal rights activist | 反义词: furrier, fur supporter | 词性: noun
