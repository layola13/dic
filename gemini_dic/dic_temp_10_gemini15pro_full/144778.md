over-exaggeration: /ˌoʊvərɪɡˌzædʒəˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 过分夸张；夸大其词 | An over-exaggeration of the facts.  对事实的夸大。 |  She told an over-exaggeration about her adventures. 她过分夸大了她的冒险经历。 | 近义词: exaggeration, hyperbole, overstatement | 反义词: understatement, downplaying | 词性: noun

Etymology: From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "exaggeration" (from Late Latin exaggeratio, from exaggerare "to heap up, increase," from ex- "out" + aggerare "to heap up," from agger "heap").
