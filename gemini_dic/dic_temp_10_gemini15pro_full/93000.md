idiopathic:/ˌɪdiəˈpæθɪk/ | adj. | 特发性的，原因不明的 |  Relating to or denoting any disease or condition that arises spontaneously or for which the cause is unknown. | Idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis is a chronic and progressive lung disease. 特发性肺纤维化是一种慢性进行性肺部疾病。 |  The doctor diagnosed the rash as idiopathic. 医生诊断皮疹为特发性皮疹。 | synonymous: essential, primary, spontaneous | antonymous: secondary, acquired | adjective
