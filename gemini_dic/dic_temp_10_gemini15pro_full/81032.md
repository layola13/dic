graduator:/ˈɡrædʒueɪtər/| n. |毕业生|A person who has completed a course of study and received a diploma or degree. |例句：The graduators threw their caps in the air at the end of the ceremony. 毕业生们在典礼结束时将帽子抛向空中。

例句：The company actively recruits new graduators each year. 这家公司每年都积极招聘应届毕业生。|近义词：graduate, alumnus, alumna |反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "dropout" or "non-graduate") |词性:noun
