hydranet:/ˈhaɪdrənɛt/| n.|水螅网，水螅群体|A colony or network of hydras.  A hydranet is formed by the budding and branching of a single hydra polyp, resulting in a connected colony of individual polyps.|The researchers observed a complex hydranet growing on the submerged rock. 研究人员观察到一块浸没的岩石上生长着一个复杂的水螅网。|近义词：hydra colony, polyp colony|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: "hydra" (from Greek ὕδρα, hydra, water serpent) + "-net" (suffix denoting a network). The word combines the name of the individual polyp, hydra, with the concept of a net-like structure.
