nonembellishing:/ˌnɑːnɪmˈbɛlɪʃɪŋ/
adjective
未加修饰的；朴实的；不加渲染的
Not embellished; plain or unadorned.
Her nonembellishing account of the events was both refreshing and believable. 她对事件未加渲染的描述既令人耳目一新，又令人信服。
He gave a nonembellishing report of the accident. 他对事故作了未加渲染的报告。

近义词:unembellished, plain, simple, unadorned, austere, stark, unvarnished
反义词: embellished, ornate, decorated, flowery, elaborate, exaggerated
词性:adjective

词源:
From "non-" (not) + "embellishing" (present participle of "embellish," from Old French "embellir," meaning "to make beautiful").  Ultimately derived from Latin "bellus" (beautiful).
