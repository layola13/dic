oarswoman: /ˈɔːrzˌwʊmən/ | n. | 女划手；女性桨手 | A woman who rows a boat, especially as a member of a crew. |  The oarswomen pulled powerfully, propelling the boat across the finish line. 女划手们用力划桨，推动着船越过终点线。

The experienced oarswoman skillfully navigated the choppy waters.  经验丰富的划桨女选手巧妙地驾驭着波涛汹涌的水面。


近义词: rower, oarsman | 反义词: coxswain (舵手 -  Technically not an opposite, but represents a different role in rowing.) | 词性: noun
