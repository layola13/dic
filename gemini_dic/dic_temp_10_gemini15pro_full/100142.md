interquarter:/ˌɪntərˈkwɔːrtər/
adjective
季度间的；跨季度的
Occurring between quarters (of a year).
The interquarter growth rate was surprisingly high. 季度间的增长率高得惊人。
近义词: between quarters
反义词: intraquarter
词性: adjective
