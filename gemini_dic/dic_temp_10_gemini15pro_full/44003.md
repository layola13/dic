criminalizes:/ˈkrɪmɪnəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
使…成为犯罪；宣布…为非法
To make something illegal or a crime.
The new law criminalizes certain types of online speech.  新的法律将某些类型的网络言论定为犯罪。
The government has criminalized the possession of certain drugs. 政府已将持有某些毒品定为非法。
Synonyms: outlaw, prohibit, ban, forbid
Antonyms: legalize, decriminalize, permit, allow
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From criminal + -ize.  来自 criminal（犯罪的） + -ize（使…）。
