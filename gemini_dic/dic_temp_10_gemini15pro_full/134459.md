nonjournalistic:/ˌnɒnˌdʒɜːrnəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
非新闻的；非新闻工作者的
Not related to or characteristic of journalism; not suitable for publication in a newspaper or magazine.
The report was criticized for its nonjournalistic tone and lack of objectivity.  这份报告因其非新闻的语气和缺乏客观性而受到批评。
His writing style is very nonjournalistic, focusing more on personal opinion than factual reporting. 他的写作风格很不新闻化，更注重个人观点而不是事实报道。
Synonyms: unjournalistic, non-news, unprofessional
Antonyms: journalistic, professional, news-related
词性: 形容词
