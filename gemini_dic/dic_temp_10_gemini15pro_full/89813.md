hornblende:/ˈhɔːrnˌblɛnd/ (US), /ˈhɔːnblɛnd/ (UK)| n. |角闪石|A common rock-forming mineral, a complex inosilicate series of the amphibole group. It is usually black or dark green in color and is a major constituent of many igneous and metamorphic rocks.|Hornblende is a common mineral found in granite. 角闪石是花岗岩中常见的矿物。|The geologist identified the black crystal as hornblende. 地质学家将黑色晶体鉴定为角闪石。|Synonyms: amphibole, inosilicate|Antonyms: None|Noun
