例句：His poetry was full of helicons, reflecting his deep connection to nature. 他的诗歌充满了灵感，反映了他与自然的深厚联系。

helical: /ˈhɛlɪkəl/ (UK) /ˈhɛlɪkl/ (US)| adj. |螺旋形的; 螺旋状的|Having the shape or form of a helix; spiral.|例句：The DNA molecule has a helical structure. DNA分子具有螺旋结构。

例句：The staircase wound upwards in a helical pattern. 楼梯以螺旋形向上盘旋。
