grumpily:/ˈɡrʌmpɪli/ | adv. | 脾气暴躁地，不高兴地 | In a bad-tempered or sullen way. | He answered grumpily, "Leave me alone." 他没好气地回答说：“让我一个人待着。” | Example: She stomped off grumpily after being told she couldn't have any more candy. 她被告知不能再吃糖果后，气呼呼地跺着脚走开了。 | Synonyms: sullenly, moodily, churlishly, peevishly | Antonyms: cheerfully, happily, pleasantly | 词性: 副词
