novae's:/ˈnoʊviːz/
noun
新星的
Possessive form of "novae," the plural of "nova."  Refers to something belonging to or associated with multiple novae.
The novae's combined brightness illuminated the nebula. 这些新星的组合亮度照亮了星云。
The astronomers studied the novae's spectra. 天文学家研究了这些新星的光谱。
Synonyms:  (for nova - singular) supernova, star
Antonyms:  (none for a celestial event)
