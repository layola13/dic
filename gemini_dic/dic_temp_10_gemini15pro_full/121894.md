missampled:/ˌmɪsˈsæmp(ə)ld/ | adj. | 错误采样的，采样不当的 |  Incorrectly sampled, usually referring to data or audio.  The process of sampling was done incorrectly, resulting in an inaccurate representation. | The missampled data skewed the results of the study.  错误采样的数据使研究结果产生了偏差。

The missampled audio track had noticeable distortion. 采样不当的音轨有明显的失真。 | 近义词: incorrectly sampled, poorly sampled | 反义词: accurately sampled, properly sampled | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "missampled" is formed by the prefix "mis-" (meaning "wrong" or "bad") and the past participle of the verb "sample" (meaning "to take a sample of").
