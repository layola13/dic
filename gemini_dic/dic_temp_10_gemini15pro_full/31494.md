charmingly:/ˈtʃɑːrmɪŋli/ /ˈtʃɑːrmɪŋli/| adv.|迷人地，可爱地|In a way that is charming; pleasantly.|She smiled charmingly at him. 她迷人地对他笑了笑。|Synonyms: attractively, delightfully, pleasantly, winningly|Antonyms: unpleasantly, offensively, disagreeably|Adverb

Etymology:
From charm + -ing + -ly.  "Charm" ultimately comes from the Latin "carmen" meaning "song, verse, incantation."
