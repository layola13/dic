brimmed:/brɪmd/| adj.|满的；溢出的；帽檐宽的|filled to the brim; having a brim (the projecting edge of a hat).|例句：Her eyes brimmed with tears. 她的眼里充满了泪水。

例句：He wore a brimmed hat to protect his face from the sun. 他戴着一顶宽边帽来保护脸部免受阳光照射。|近义词：full, overflowing, wide-brimmed|反义词：empty, unfilled, narrow-brimmed|词性:形容词
词源: From the noun "brim" (edge) + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  "Brim" comes from Old English "brymm" (edge, rim, brink).
