amorphozoa:/əˌmɔːrfoʊˈzoʊə/ | n. | 变形虫类 | A taxonomic group of amoeboid protists characterized by the absence of flagella, cilia, or a test (shell).  These organisms move and feed by extending pseudopods (temporary projections of the cytoplasm). | Amoebozoa include slime molds and other amoeboid organisms. 变形虫类包括黏菌和其他变形虫生物。|  synonyms:  amoeboid protists | antonyms:  flagellates, ciliates | noun
