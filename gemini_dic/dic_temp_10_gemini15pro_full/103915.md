judeo:/ˈdʒuːdiːoʊ/
adjective
犹太人的; 与犹太人相关的
Relating to Jews or Judaism.
Judeo-Christian values are important in Western society.  犹太教和基督教的价值观在西方社会很重要。
The Judeo-Spanish language is also known as Ladino.  犹太-西班牙语也被称为拉迪诺语。
Synonym: Jewish
Antonym: Gentile (can be considered an antonym in certain contexts)
词性: adjective
