oppositionary: /ˌɒpəˈzɪʃənəri/ | adj. | 反对的，对抗的 |Characterized by or relating to opposition; opposing.  |  His oppositionary stance on the new policy was clear. 他对新政策的反对立场很明确。 |  The senator's oppositionary tactics angered his colleagues.  这位参议员的对抗策略激怒了他的同事们。 | 近义词: antagonistic, opposing, contrary, dissident, rebellious | 反义词: supportive, compliant, agreeable, acquiescent | 词性: adjective

词源:  From the noun "opposition" + suffix "-ary" (forming adjectives meaning “relating to, connected with”). "Opposition" ultimately comes from the Latin "opponere" (to oppose, place against).
