outqueries:/ˈaʊtkwɪriz/| n.|外部查询|Requests for information or data made to external sources or systems, as opposed to internal databases or systems.|The system handles thousands of outqueries every day, fetching data from various public APIs.|近义词:external queries, outside queries|反义词:internal queries|词性:名词

Etymology:  A combination of "out" (meaning external or outside) and "queries" (requests for information).  "Query" itself comes from the Latin "quaerere," meaning "to seek" or "to ask."
