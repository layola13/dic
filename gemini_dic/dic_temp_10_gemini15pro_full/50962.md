designless:/dɪˈzaɪnlɪs/
adjective
无设计的；缺乏设计的；朴素的
lacking design or ornament; plain
The room was designless and functional, with no decorative touches.  这个房间没有设计，只有实用性，没有任何装饰性的点缀。
The furniture was simple and designless. 家具简单朴素，没有设计感。
近义词:plain, simple, unadorned, undecorated
反义词:designed, ornate, elaborate, decorated
词性:adjective
