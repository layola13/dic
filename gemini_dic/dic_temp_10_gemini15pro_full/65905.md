ewe:/juː/
noun
母羊
a female sheep
The ewe grazed peacefully in the meadow.  母羊在草地上平静地吃草。
The farmer sheared the ewe's wool. 农夫剪了母羊的毛。
Synonyms: female sheep
Antonyms: ram
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English ēowu, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch ooi and German Aue.
