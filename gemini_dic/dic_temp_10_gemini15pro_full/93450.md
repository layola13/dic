illumining:/ɪˈluːmɪnɪŋ/
verb
照亮，启发
making something clear and easier to understand.  Making a place brighter with light.
The setting sun was illumining the clouds with a fiery orange glow.  落日用火红的橙色光芒照亮了云层。

His explanation illumined the complex problem. 他的解释阐明了这个复杂的问题。

近义词: enlightening, illuminating, clarifying, brightening
反义词: obscuring, darkening, confusing

词性: Verb (Present Participle of illuminate)

词源: From Latin illuminare "to light up, brighten," from in- "in" + luminare "to light," from lumen "light."
