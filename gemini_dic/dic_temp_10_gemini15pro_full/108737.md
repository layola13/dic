leapfrogging:/ˈliːpˌfrɒɡɪŋ/ (US) /ˈliːpˌfrɒɡɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |跳蛙式发展，超越式发展|The act of surpassing or overtaking a competitor or stage of development, often by adopting a more advanced technology or strategy, bypassing intermediate steps.|Developing countries are leapfrogging older technologies and adopting the latest innovations. 发展中国家正在跨越旧技术，采用最新创新。|近义词：overtaking, bypassing, surpassing, outstripping|反义词: lagging, falling behind|noun
