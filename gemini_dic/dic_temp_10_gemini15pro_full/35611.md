coasteering:/koʊˈstɪrɪŋ/
n.
海岸攀岩; 攀岩式海岸徒步旅行
The activity of exploring a rocky coastline by climbing, jumping, and swimming.
We went coasteering in Cornwall last summer. 我们去年夏天在康沃尔郡进行了海岸攀岩。
Coasteering is an exhilarating experience. 海岸攀岩是一项令人兴奋的体验。
近义词：rock climbing, cliff jumping, sea-level traversing
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "inland hiking")
