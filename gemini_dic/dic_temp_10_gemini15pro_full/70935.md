firecracker:/ˈfaɪərkrækər/ (US), /ˈfaɪəkrækə(r)/ (UK)| n. |爆竹，鞭炮|A small explosive device, typically a paper cylinder filled with gunpowder and a fuse, used for making a loud bang, especially during celebrations.|例句：Children love to light firecrackers on the Fourth of July. 孩子们喜欢在七月四日点燃爆竹。


例句：The firecrackers made a loud bang that startled the dog. 爆竹发出一声巨响，吓了狗一跳。


近义词：banger, firework, cracker|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a physical object)|词性: noun
