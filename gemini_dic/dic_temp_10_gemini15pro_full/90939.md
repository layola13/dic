hurlbone:/ˈhɜːrlˌboʊn/ | n. | 投掷骨; 距骨; |  A small bone, specifically the talus bone in the ankle, formerly used in games of skill involving throwing.  It is also sometimes referred to as the knucklebone. |  Children used to play games with hurlbones. 孩子们过去常常玩距骨游戏。| Synonym: talus, knucklebone | Antonym: N/A | Noun
