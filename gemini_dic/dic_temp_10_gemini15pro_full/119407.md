microfiche: /ˈmaɪkrəʊfiːʃ/ (UK) /ˈmaɪkroʊfiːʃ/ (US)| n. |缩微胶片；微缩菲林|A flat piece of film containing microphotographs of the pages of a newspaper, catalogue, or other document.|The library has back issues of the newspaper on microfiche. 图书馆有报纸的缩微胶片合订本。|近义词: microfilm |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "hard copy") |noun

microfiches: /ˈmaɪkrəʊfiːʃɪz/ (UK) /ˈmaɪkroʊfiːʃɪz/ (US)| n. |缩微胶片（复数）|Plural of microfiche. Flat pieces of film containing microphotographs of the pages of newspapers, catalogues, or other documents.|The archives hold thousands of microfiches containing historical records. 档案馆保存着数千张包含历史记录的缩微胶片。|近义词: microfilms |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "hard copies") |noun
