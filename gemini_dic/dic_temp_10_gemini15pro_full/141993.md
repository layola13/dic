organisation:/ˌɔːɡənɪˈzeɪʃn/ /ˌɔːrɡənɪˈzeɪʃn/ | n. | 组织；机构；团体；安排，筹划 | A group of people with a particular purpose, such as a business or a charity. ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *organizacion, from Late Latin organizatio, from Latin organum (“instrument, tool”) + -izare (“to make”) (from Greek -ίζειν (-ízein)).
