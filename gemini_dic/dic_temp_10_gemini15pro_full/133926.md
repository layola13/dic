nonhallucinatory:/ˌnɒnhəˈluːsɪnətɔːri/ /ˌnɑːnhəˈluːsɪnətɔːri/
adjective
非幻觉的; 不引起幻觉的
Not relating to, characterized by, or causing hallucinations.
The patient experienced nonhallucinatory flashbacks of the traumatic event.  病人经历了创伤事件的非幻觉性闪回。
He had nonhallucinatory visions of his deceased grandmother. 他看到了已故祖母的非幻觉影像。
Synonyms: real, actual, genuine, true, verifiable, objective
Antonyms: hallucinatory, illusory, delusional, unreal, imagined, subjective
词性: 形容词
