camellias:/kəˈmiːliəz/| n.|山茶花|A flowering evergreen shrub or small tree of the genus Camellia, native to Asia.  Many cultivated varieties are grown for their showy flowers, which can be white, pink, red, or yellow.|The camellias in her garden are in full bloom. 她花园里的山茶花盛开了。|近义词：flower, blossom|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用weed 杂草)|noun
