overeffusiveness:/ˌoʊvərɪˈfjuːsɪvnəs/| n. | 过分热情，过分流露感情 | The state of being excessively demonstrative or enthusiastic in expressing affection or approval. | Her overeffusiveness made him uncomfortable. 她的过分热情让他感到不舒服。|  His overeffusiveness upon meeting his new boss was a bit much. 他见到新老板时表现得过于热情，有点过了。|近义词：exuberance, effusiveness, gushiness |反义词：restraint, reserve, aloofness |词性: Noun

Etymology:
From over- + effusive + -ness.  "Effusive" comes from the Latin "effusus," the past participle of "effundere," meaning "to pour out."  "Over-" indicates an excess or surpassing of a normal limit.
