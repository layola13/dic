ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *more, *moor, from Old English māra (“more, greater, larger, longer, bigger”), from Proto-Germanic *maizō (“more”), from Proto-Indo-European *meǵh₂-yōs (“great, big”). Cognate with Scots mair (“more”), West Frisian mear (“more”), Dutch meer (“more”), German mehr (“more”), Danish mere (“more”), Swedish mer (“more”), Icelandic meiri (“more”).
