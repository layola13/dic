henogeny:/hɛˈnɒdʒəni/ | n. | 单性生殖 |  Development from a single cell, such as an unfertilized egg. | Example 1: Henogeny is a form of asexual reproduction. 单性生殖是一种无性繁殖的形式。| Example 2:  Some species of lizards reproduce through henogeny.  一些蜥蜴物种通过单性生殖繁殖。 | Synonym: Parthenogenesis, agamogenesis | Antonym: Sexual reproduction | noun
