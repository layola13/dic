blogging:/ˈblɒɡɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈblɑːɡɪŋ/ (US)| n.|博客；写博客；经营博客|The act of writing or posting content on a blog.|She enjoys blogging about her travels. 她喜欢在博客上写她的旅行。| 近义词:blog writing, online journaling, microblogging|反义词: None|noun

blogging:/ˈblɒɡɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈblɑːɡɪŋ/ (US)| adj.|与写博客有关的；用于写博客的|Relating to the activity of blogging.|Blogging platforms are becoming increasingly sophisticated. 博客平台正变得越来越复杂。| 近义词:blog-related|反义词: None|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From blog + -ing.  "Blog" is a shortened form of "weblog," a term coined in 1997 by Jorn Barger.  "Weblog" combines "web" (referring to the World Wide Web) and "log" (short for logbook, a record of events).
