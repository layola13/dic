miscatalogue:/ˌmɪsˈkætəlɒɡ/ | /ˌmɪsˈkætəlɔːɡ/ | vt. n. | 错误分类，分类错误 | To catalogue incorrectly; to put into the wrong category. | The librarian miscatalogued the new biography, placing it in the fiction section. 图书管理员把这本新的传记错误地分类到了小说区。

The miscataloguing of the artifacts led to confusion among researchers.  文物的错误分类导致了研究人员的困惑。 | misclassify, misfile, mislabel | classify correctly, file correctly, label correctly | verb, noun
