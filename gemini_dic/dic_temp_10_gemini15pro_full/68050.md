factually:/ˈfæktʃuəli/
adverb
事实上地；基于事实地
Based on facts; in a way that is concerned with what is actually the case.
The article is not factually accurate. 这篇文章内容与事实不符。
Synonyms: truthfully, accurately, verifiably, empirically
Antonyms: falsely, inaccurately, untruthfully
词性: 副词
