oversoft:/ˌoʊvərˈsɒft/ /ˌoʊvərˈsɑːft/| adj.|过软的，过于柔软的|Excessively soft; softer than is desirable or usual.|The cake was oversoft and crumbled when I tried to slice it. 蛋糕太软了，我切的时候都碎了。|The pillow was oversoft and offered no support for my neck.  这个枕头太软了，我的脖子一点支撑都没有。|近义词：too soft, excessively soft, squishy, spongy|反义词：hard, firm, stiff|词性:形容词

词源:
"over-" (prefix) + "soft" (adjective)
"over-" 表示“过度”、“过于”
"soft" 表示“柔软的”
