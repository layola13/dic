guillevat:/ˈɡɪləvæt/ | n. |酒桶；大桶（尤指容量为225升的葡萄酒桶）| A large cask, especially one holding 225 liters of wine. | The winery stored its finest vintage in ancient guillevats. 酒庄将其最好的年份酒储存在古老的大桶里。|  The guillevat was carefully filled with the newly pressed grapes.  大桶里小心地装满了新压榨的葡萄。| 近义词: cask, barrel, vat | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "small container") | 词性: noun
