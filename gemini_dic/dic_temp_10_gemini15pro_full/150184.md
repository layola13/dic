pancaked:/ˈpænkeɪkt/| adj.|煎饼状的，扁平的|Resembling a pancake; flat and thin.  This verb form describes something that has been flattened or squashed, often as a result of an impact or collision.|The car was completely pancaked after the accident. 汽车在事故后被完全压扁了。|近义词：flattened, squashed, crushed|反义词:inflated, swollen|词性:形容词

Etymology: From pancake + -ed.  The word "pancake" itself comes from "pan" and "cake," referring to a cake cooked in a pan.
