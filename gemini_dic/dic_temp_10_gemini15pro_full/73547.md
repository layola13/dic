forespeech:/ˈfɔːrspiːtʃ/| n.|前言，开场白|An introductory speech; a preface; a prologue.  A speech made before the main discourse or event.|The chairman gave a brief forespeech before introducing the keynote speaker. 主席在介绍主旨发言人之前作了简短的开场白。|Synonyms: preface, prologue, introduction, preamble, prelude|Antonyms: epilogue, afterword, conclusion|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From 'fore-' (before) + 'speech'.
