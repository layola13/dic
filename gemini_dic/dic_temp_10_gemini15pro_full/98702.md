insupportability:/ˌɪnsəˌpɔːrtəˈbɪləti/
n.
无法忍受；难以支持
The insupportability of the working conditions led to a strike. 工作条件的无法忍受导致了罢工。
The insupportability of the evidence led to the case being dismissed.  证据的不可靠导致案件被驳回。
近义词:intolerability, untenability
反义词:supportability, tolerability
词性:noun

Etymology:
From in- + supportable + -ity.  "In-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of." "Supportable" comes from the verb "support," which ultimately derives from the Latin "supportare," meaning "to carry from below."  "-ity" is a suffix used to form nouns expressing a state or quality.
