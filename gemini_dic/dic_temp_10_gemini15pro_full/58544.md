dugong:/ˈduːɡɒŋ/ (UK), /ˈduːɡɑːŋ/ (US)| n.|儒艮|A large plant-eating mammal that lives in warm coastal waters, related to the manatee.|例句：The dugong is a gentle giant of the sea. 儒艮是海洋中温柔的巨人。

例句：Dugongs are vulnerable to extinction due to habitat loss. 由于栖息地丧失，儒艮很容易灭绝。|近义词：sea cow, manatee|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
