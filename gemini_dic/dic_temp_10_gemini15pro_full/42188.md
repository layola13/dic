coulomb:/ˈkuːlɒm/, /ˈkuːlɑːm/ | n. | 库仑 | The SI unit of electric charge, equal to the quantity of electricity conveyed in one second by a current of one ampere. | The battery has a capacity of 1000 coulombs.  电池容量为1000库仑。| synonyms: unit of charge | antonyms: N/A | noun

From French *coulomb*, named after French physicist Charles-Augustin de Coulomb (1736-1806).
