arrogate:/ˈærəɡeɪt/ | v. |  擅取；霸占；妄称 | To claim or seize without justification; to make undue claims to having (something). | He arrogated to himself the right to decide what everyone should do. 他擅自决定每个人应该做什么。| The government arrogated the power to control the media. 政府霸占了控制媒体的权力。 |近义词：usurp, appropriate, seize, assume |反义词：relinquish, renounce, abdicate |词性: verb

词源: From Latin *arrogatus*, past participle of *arrogare* (“to claim for oneself, adopt, add to”), from *ad-* (“to, towards”) + *rogare* (“to ask”).
