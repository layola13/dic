cookless:/ˈkʊkləs/
adjective
无须烹调的；无需厨师的
Not requiring cooking; without a cook.
The campers enjoyed cookless meals during their backpacking trip.  露营者在背包旅行期间享用了无需烹调的食物。
The invention of the microwave oven has made cookless cuisine more accessible. 微波炉的发明使无需烹调的菜肴更容易获得。
近义词:pre-cooked, ready-to-eat, instant
反义词:homemade, home-cooked
词性:adjective

Etymology:  "cook" (from Old English *cōc, from Latin coquus) + "-less" (a suffix meaning "without" or "lacking," from Old English -lēas).
