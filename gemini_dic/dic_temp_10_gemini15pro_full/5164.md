allergic: /əˈlɜːrdʒɪk/ | adj. | 过敏的；对…过敏的 | Having a negative physical reaction to a particular substance (an allergen).  |  例句：I'm allergic to peanuts. 我对花生过敏。

例句：She is allergic to pollen and dust. 她对花粉和灰尘过敏。


近义词：hypersensitive, susceptible, intolerant | 反义词: tolerant, immune | 词性: adjective

allergic: /əˈlɜːrdʒɪk/ |  adj. | 过敏的；对…过敏的 | Having an allergy.  | 例句：He gets allergic reactions to certain foods. 他对某些食物过敏。

例句：My skin becomes allergic during the springtime. 我的皮肤在春天会过敏。


近义词：sensitive, reactive | 反义词: insensitive, nonreactive | 词性: adjective


词源: Late 19th century: from German allergisch, from Allergie, from all- ‘other, different’ + ergon ‘work’.
