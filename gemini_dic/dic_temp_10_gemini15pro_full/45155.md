cudgelling:/ˈkʌdʒəlɪŋ/
n.|棒击，痛打，苦思冥想|The act of hitting someone with a cudgel; the act of thinking hard about something.|He resorted to cudgelling the donkey to make it move. 他用棍棒抽打驴子，使它移动。|近义词：beating, thrashing, pondering, racking one's brains|反义词：praising, commending|词性:名词

cudgel:/ˈkʌdʒəl/
n.|棍棒，短棍|A short, thick stick used as a weapon.|He defended himself with a cudgel. 他用一根棍棒自卫。|近义词：club, stick, bludgeon|反义词：N/A|词性:名词

cudgel:/ˈkʌdʒəl/
v.|用棍棒打|To beat with a cudgel.|He was cudgelled by a group of thugs. 他被一群暴徒用棍棒殴打。|近义词：beat, club, bludgeon|反义词：N/A|词性:动词
