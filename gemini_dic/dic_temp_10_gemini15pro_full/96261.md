indict:/ɪnˈdaɪt/| v. |控告，起诉|To formally accuse someone of a crime.|The grand jury indicted him for murder. 大陪审团控告他犯有谋杀罪。|近义词：accuse, charge, prosecute|反义词：exonerate, acquit|verb

indictment:/ɪnˈdaɪtmənt/| n. |起诉书，控告|A formal accusation of a serious crime.|The indictment charged him with theft. 起诉书指控他犯有盗窃罪。|近义词：accusation, charge|反义词：acquittal|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French enditer, endicter “to accuse, indict,” from Latin indictare "to proclaim, make known," from in- "in, on" + dictare "to say, dictate," frequentative of dicere "to say."
