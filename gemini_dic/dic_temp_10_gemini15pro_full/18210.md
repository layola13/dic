beldame:/ˈbelˌdeɪm/ | n. |老妇人；丑老太婆 |An old woman, especially one considered to be ugly or unpleasant. |例句：The beldame cackled gleefully as she stirred her potion.  丑老太婆一边搅拌着她的药水，一边高兴地咯咯笑着。

例句：The fairytale featured a wicked beldame who cursed the princess.  童话故事里有一个邪恶的老巫婆诅咒了公主。|近义词：hag, crone, witch |反义词:ingenue, maiden |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *beldam*, from Old French *bel dame* ("beautiful lady"), ironically used to refer to an old woman.  The word's meaning shifted over time from "grandmother" to its current negative connotation.
