fractionating:/ˈfrækʃəneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
分馏的，分级的
Separating something into different parts, typically by a process involving heat.
Crude oil is fractionated to produce different fuels. 原油经过分馏以生产不同的燃料。
fractional, separating, dividing
combining, mixing
adjective

fractionate:/ˈfrækʃəneɪt/
verb
分馏，分级
To separate something into different parts, typically by a process involving heat.
The refinery fractionates crude oil into gasoline, kerosene, and other products.  炼油厂将原油分馏成汽油、煤油和其他产品。
separate, divide, split
combine, mix
verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fractionatus*, past participle of *fractionare* "to break," from *fractio* "a breaking," from *frangere* "to break."
