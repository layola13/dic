connectorized: /kəˈnɛktəˌraɪzd/
adjective
连接化的，配有连接器的
equipped with a connector or connectors, allowing for easy connection and disconnection.
The server room utilizes connectorized fiber optic cables for high-speed data transfer.  服务器机房使用连接化的光纤电缆进行高速数据传输。
All equipment is connectorized for rapid deployment. 所有设备都配有连接器以便快速部署。
近义词: modular, pluggable
反义词: integrated, fixed
词性: adjective

词源:  From "connector" + "-ize" (suffix meaning "to make or become") + "-ed" (past participle suffix, indicating a state or quality).  The word "connector" itself comes from the verb "connect," ultimately derived from the Latin "connectere," meaning "to bind together."
