antipragmatic:/ˌæntipræɡˈmætɪk/
| adj. |反实用主义的，不务实的|Contrary to practical considerations or good sense; impractical.  Acting against one's own self-interest.|His antipragmatic approach to business ultimately led to its failure. 他不务实的商业方法最终导致了失败。|Synonyms: impractical, unrealistic, unwise, imprudent, foolish|Antonyms: pragmatic, practical, sensible, wise, prudent|adjective

antipragmatic:/ˌæntipræɡˈmætɪk/
| n. |反实用主义者|A person who acts against practical considerations or good sense.|He's an antipragmatic, always chasing ideals without regard for reality. 他是一个不务实的人，总是追逐理想而不考虑现实。|Synonyms: idealist, dreamer|Antonyms: pragmatist, realist|noun
