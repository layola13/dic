conjunctionally:/kənˈdʒʌŋkʃənəli/ | adv. | 连词地，连接地 | in a way that involves conjunction or connects things; jointly; in conjunction with something else | The two events occurred conjunctionally, leading many to believe they were related. 这两件事相伴发生，导致许多人认为它们之间有关联。 |  The projects will be managed conjunctionally by both departments. 这些项目将由两个部门共同管理。 | 近义词: jointly, together, concurrently, simultaneously | 反义词: separately, independently | 词性:副词

词源:  From conjunction + -al + -ly. Ultimately derived from Latin conjungere "to join together."
