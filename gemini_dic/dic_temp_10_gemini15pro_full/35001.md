cloke:/kləʊk/ /kloʊk/ | n. | 斗篷；掩饰；借口 | A garment, much like a cape, worn over outer clothes for protection from the weather.  Something that conceals or disguises something else.  |  He wore a heavy cloke to protect himself from the rain. 他穿着一件厚重的斗篷来挡雨。  The spy used his job as a journalist as a cloke for his espionage activities.  这个间谍用他记者的工作作为掩护他的间谍活动的幌子。 | 近义词: cape, mantle, cover, disguise | 反义词: revelation, exposure | 词性: 名词

词源: From Middle English cloke, from Old North French cloque (“bell”), from Old Dutch klocke, klocke (“bell, cloak”), from Late Latin clocca (“bell”).  The word originally referred to a bell-shaped garment.
