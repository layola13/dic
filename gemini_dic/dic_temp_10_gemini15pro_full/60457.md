eisenstein:/ˈaɪzənʃtaɪn/| n.|爱森斯坦|A German mathematician, Gotthold Eisenstein (1823-1852), known for his contributions to number theory and analysis, especially Eisenstein series and the Eisenstein criterion for irreducibility of polynomials.|例句：Eisenstein's work on elliptic functions was groundbreaking. 爱森斯坦在椭圆函数方面的工作是开创性的。
