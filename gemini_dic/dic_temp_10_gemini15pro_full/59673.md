echinus: /ɪˈkaɪnəs/ | n. | 海胆；刺猬；棘皮动物；建筑装饰 (圆凸形) |A sea urchin or other spiny animal.  Also, a rounded molding. | The echinus is covered in spines for protection. 海胆全身覆盖着刺以保护自己。| The architect incorporated an echinus into the column's design. 建筑师在柱子的设计中加入了一个圆凸形装饰。 | synonyms: sea urchin, urchin, spine, prickle | antonyms: smoothness, flatness | noun


echinus: /ɪˈkaɪnəs/ | n. | (古希腊建筑)柱头 | The rounded molding that forms the lower part of the capital of a Doric, Ionic, or Corinthian column. | The echinus of the Doric column was simple and undecorated.  多立克柱式的柱头简单而没有装饰。 | The elaborate carvings on the echinus of the Corinthian column were impressive. 科林斯式柱子的柱头上精美的雕刻令人印象深刻。| synonyms: molding, ovolo, torus | antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider terms like "shaft" or "base" which are other parts of the column, but not opposites) | noun
