niqab:/nɪˈkɑːb/ | n. |尼卡布，面纱 |A veil worn by some Muslim women that covers the entire face except for the eyes. |例句：She chose to wear a niqab as part of her religious observance. 她选择佩戴尼卡布作为她宗教信仰的一部分。

例句：The niqab is a common sight in some parts of the world. 在世界的一些地方，尼卡布很常见。|近义词：veil, hijab, burqa |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps Western dress or unveiled) |词性:noun
