goliard:/ˈɡoʊliɑːrd/| n.|  流浪学生；放荡不羁的诗人；玩世不恭的人|A medieval wandering scholar, especially one of a class composing satirical Latin poetry.|He lived a goliard's life, wandering from town to town, writing poems and drinking wine. 他过着流浪学生般的生活，从一个城镇流浪到另一个城镇，写诗喝酒。|Synonyms:  reveller, carouser,  bohemian,  wandering scholar|Antonyms: ascetic, hermit, recluse|Noun
