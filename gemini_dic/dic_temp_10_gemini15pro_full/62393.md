endocrinologists:/ˌɛndəʊkrɪˈnɒlədʒɪsts/
n.
内分泌学家
A medical specialist who studies and treats disorders of the endocrine system, which includes glands like the thyroid, pancreas, and adrenals.
例句：Endocrinologists treat hormonal imbalances. 内分泌学家治疗激素失衡。

例句：I have an appointment with the endocrinologist next week to discuss my thyroid levels. 我下周要和内分泌学家预约讨论我的甲状腺水平。

近义词：hormone specialist, hormone doctor
反义词: N/A
词性: Noun
