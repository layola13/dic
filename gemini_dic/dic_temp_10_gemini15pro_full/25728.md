Example 2:  The children searched the billabong, hoping to catch a glimpse of the elusive bunyip. 孩子们在水潭里搜寻，希望能瞥见那难以捉摸的班衣普。|近义词：monster, mythical creature|反义词: (No direct antonym as it's a mythical creature)|词性:noun
