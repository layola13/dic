humorous:/ˈhjuːmərəs/ (US) /ˈhjuːmərəs/ (UK)| adj.|幽默的，滑稽的|Funny or amusing; causing laughter.|例句：He told a humorous story about his childhood. 他讲了一个关于他童年的幽默故事。|例句：She has a humorous way of looking at life. 她看待生活的方式很幽默。|近义词：funny, amusing, comical, witty|反义词：serious, grave, solemn|词性:形容词
humorous:/ˈhjuːmərəs/ (US) /ˈhjuːmərəs/ (UK)|词源: Late Middle English: from Old French humoreux, from medieval Latin humorosus, from Latin humor ‘moisture’.|
