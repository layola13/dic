electro-oculography:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˌɒkjʊˈlɒɡrəfi/
noun|眼电图描记法，眼动描记法|A technique for measuring the corneo-retinal standing potential that exists between the front and the back of the human eye. This potential changes as the eye rotates, allowing eye movements to be tracked.|Electro-oculography is a useful tool for studying sleep and diagnosing certain eye conditions.|近义词:  EOG|反义词: (None - highly specific term)|词性: noun
