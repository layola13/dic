anodon:/ˈænədɒn/ (US) /əˈnəʊdɒn/ (UK)| n. |无齿蚌，河蚌|A freshwater mussel of the family Unionidae, characterized by the absence of hinge teeth.  These mussels are found in lakes and rivers throughout much of the world.|The anodon buried itself in the mud of the riverbed.|Synonyms: mussel, clam, freshwater mussel|Antonyms: None (no direct opposite for a specific type of shellfish)|noun
