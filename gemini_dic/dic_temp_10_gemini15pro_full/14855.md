axogenic:/ˌæksəˈdʒenɪk/
adjective
轴生的，轴向发生的
Relating to or stimulating the growth of an axon. An axon is the long threadlike part of a nerve cell along which impulses are conducted from the cell body to other cells.
Axogenic factors play a crucial role in nerve regeneration.  轴生因子在神经再生中起着至关重要的作用。
The study focused on the axogenic potential of certain growth factors. 这项研究集中于某些生长因子的轴生潜力。
近义词: axon-promoting, neurotrophic
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider terms like "axon-inhibiting" or "neurodegenerative")
词性: adjective
