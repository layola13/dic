gothicities: /ɡɒθɪˈsɪtiːz/
n.
哥特式风格，哥特式特征（复数）
The qualities or characteristics associated with the Gothic style or subculture.
The film explored various gothicities, from the architecture of the castle to the morbid fascinations of the characters.  这部电影探索了各种哥特式风格，从城堡的建筑到人物病态的迷恋。
Synonyms: Gothic elements, Gothic traits, Gothic features
Antonyms:  Modernity, minimalism (These are broad contrasts, not direct opposites)
