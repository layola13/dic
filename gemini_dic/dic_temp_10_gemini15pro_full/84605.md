hamstring:/ˈhæmstrɪŋ/ |v.| 使残废；使无力；使不起作用 | To severely restrict the effectiveness or efficiency of someone or something. | The bureaucracy is hamstringing our efforts to improve public services. 官僚主义阻碍了我们改善公共服务的努力。| synonyms: cripple, disable, incapacitate, impede, obstruct | antonyms: enable, empower, facilitate, aid, assist | verb

hamstring:/ˈhæmstrɪŋ/ |n.| 腿筋 | A group of tendons at the back of the knee. | He pulled a hamstring during the race. 他在比赛中拉伤了腿筋。| synonyms: hamstring muscle, hamstring tendon | antonyms: N/A | noun

hamstrings:/ˈhæmstrɪŋz/ |n.| 腿筋 | A group of tendons at the back of the knee. | Stretching your hamstrings is important for flexibility.  拉伸你的腿筋对柔韧性很重要。| synonyms: hamstring muscles, hamstring tendons | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From ham + string.  Ham refers to the back of the thigh, and string refers to the tendons.
