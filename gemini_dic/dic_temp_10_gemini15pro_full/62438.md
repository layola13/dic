endopleura:/ˈɛndoʊˈplʊərə/| n. |内果皮|The innermost layer of the pericarp, surrounding the seed(s).  It is the inside layer of the fruit wall, which can be papery, fleshy, or stony depending on the fruit.| The endopleura of a peach is the hard, stony pit that surrounds the seed. 桃子的内果皮是围绕种子的坚硬的核。| The endopleura of a grape is the fleshy part that we eat. 葡萄的内果皮是我们吃的肉质部分。|近义词:tegmen (specifically for seeds)|反义词:exocarp|词性:noun
