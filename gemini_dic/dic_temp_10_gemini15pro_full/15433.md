bactrian:/ˈbæktriən/
adjective|双峰驼的；(指骆驼)有两个驼峰的|Relating to or characteristic of Bactria, an ancient region in central Asia.|The Bactrian camel is well adapted to the harsh desert environment. 双峰驼非常适应恶劣的沙漠环境。|two-humped|one-humped (referring to Dromedary camels)
noun|双峰驼|A camel with two humps native to central Asia.|Bactrian camels are known for their ability to withstand extreme temperatures. 双峰驼以其耐受极端温度的能力而闻名。|two-humped camel, camel|Dromedary (one-humped camel)
