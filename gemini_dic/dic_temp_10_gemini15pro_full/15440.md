bad-copy:/bæd ˈkɒpi/ (US) /bæd ˈkɔːpi/ (UK)| n.| 劣质复制品; 不良副本; 赝品|A bad copy is a reproduction of something that is of poor quality, often with errors or distortions.| The pirated DVD was a bad copy, with blurry images and poor sound.  这盗版DVD是劣质复制品，图像模糊，声音也很差。| synonyms: inferior copy, poor reproduction, flawed replica | antonyms: perfect copy, original, master copy | noun


bad-copy:/bæd ˈkɒpi/ (US) /bæd ˈkɔːpi/ (UK)| adj. (informal, less common)| 劣质的; 拙劣模仿的 | Used informally to describe something that is a poor imitation or reproduction.|  His painting was a bad copy of the original masterpiece. 他的画是对原作拙劣的模仿。| synonyms: imitative, unoriginal, derivative | antonyms: original, innovative, authentic | adjective
