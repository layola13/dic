exogenetic:/ˌeksoʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk/
adjective
外生的；外成的
Relating to geological processes originating on or above the surface of the earth.
Exogenetic forces, such as weathering and erosion, shape the Earth's surface. 外生力，例如风化和侵蚀，塑造了地球表面。
The Grand Canyon is a result of exogenetic processes over millions of years.  大峡谷是数百万年来外生作用的结果。
近义词:external, exterior, surface
反义词:endogenetic, internal
词性:adjective
