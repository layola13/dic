cushiest:/ˈkʊʃiɪst/| adj.|最舒适的，最安逸的|Providing the most comfort and ease; most luxurious.  It describes something that is extremely soft and comfortable, like a very soft pillow or chair.|He landed the cushiest job in the company, with minimal work and maximum pay. 他得到了公司里最轻松的工作，工作量最小，工资最高。|Synonyms:  comfortable, soft, plush, luxurious, easy|Antonyms:  uncomfortable, hard, austere, difficult, demanding|Adjective
