abuser: /əˈbjuːzər/ (UK), /əˈbjuːzər/ (US)| n.|施虐者，滥用者|Someone who abuses someone or something.  Someone who treats another person cruelly and violently.|The child was taken away from his abuser. 这个孩子被从施虐者身边带走。

He was an abuser of alcohol and drugs. 他酗酒吸毒。|近义词：offender, maltreater, exploiter, perpetrator|反义词：protector, defender, guardian|词性：名词
