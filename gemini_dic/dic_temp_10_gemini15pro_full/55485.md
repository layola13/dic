distractful:/dɪˈstræktfəl/ | adj. | 分心的，分散注意力的 | Causing distraction; tending to take away one’s attention. | The constant notifications on my phone are very distractful. 我的手机不断弹出通知，非常让人分心。 |  The noisy environment made it distractful to study. 嘈杂的环境使学习分心。| synonyms: distracting, diverting, disturbing | antonyms: focusing, concentrating | adjective

distract:/dɪˈstrækt/ | verb | 使分心，分散(注意力) | To draw away the attention of; divert. | The loud music distracted me from my work.  喧闹的音乐使我无法专心工作。| The child's constant whining distracted her from driving. 孩子不停的哭闹让她开车分心。| synonyms: divert, sidetrack | antonyms: focus, concentrate | verb
