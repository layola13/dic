epithesis:/ɪˈpɪθɪsɪs/
n.
附加物，修补，弥补（尤指弥补身体缺陷的）
An addition, restoration, or supplying of that which is lost or wanting, especially of a bodily part.
After losing his finger in an accident, John received an epithesis that looked remarkably real.  约翰在一次事故中失去了手指，之后他接受了一个看起来非常逼真的附加物。
The dentist fitted an epithesis to replace the missing tooth. 牙医安装了一个附加物来代替缺失的牙齿。

lack, deficiency, absence
