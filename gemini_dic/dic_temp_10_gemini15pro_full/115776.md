marinids:/ˈmærɪnɪdz/ | n. | 马里尼德王朝 |  A Berber Muslim dynasty that ruled Morocco from the 13th to the 15th century.  | The Marinids, a powerful dynasty, left a lasting impact on Moroccan architecture. 马里尼德王朝是一个强大的王朝，对摩洛哥建筑产生了持久的影响。 | The Marinids constructed many madrasas and mosques. 马里尼德王朝建造了许多经学院和清真寺。 | Synonyms: Banu Marin, Marinid dynasty | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific dynasty) | Noun
