distiller:/dɪˈstɪlər/| n.|蒸馏器；酿酒者；提炼者|A person or company that distils alcoholic drinks.  A person or thing that extracts the essential elements of something.|The distiller produces a fine single malt whisky. 这家酿酒厂生产一种优质的单一麦芽威士忌。|Synonyms: refiner, purifier, extractor|Antonyms: mixer, combiner|noun

distil:/dɪˈstɪl/| v.|蒸馏；提炼；渗出；流出|Purify (a liquid) by heating it so that it vaporizes, then cooling and condensing the vapor and collecting the resulting liquid. Extract the essential meaning or most important aspects of something.|They distil the whisky in copper stills. 他们在铜蒸馏器中蒸馏威士忌。|Synonyms: extract, refine, purify|Antonyms: mix, combine, contaminate|verb
