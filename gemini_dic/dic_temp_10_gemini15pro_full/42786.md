coupé:/kuːˈpeɪ/| n.|双门跑车|A car with a fixed roof, two doors, and usually a sloping back.|He drove his sleek coupé along the coast.|近义词:sports car, two-door|反义词:sedan, four-door|词性:noun
coupé:/ˈkuːpeɪ/| n.|双座马车|A closed, horse-drawn carriage for two people inside with an outside seat for the driver.|The couple rode in a stylish coupé through Central Park.|近义词:carriage, coach|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly open carriage)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *coupé*, past participle of *couper* "to cut," from Latin *colaphus* "a blow with the fist" and *cupa* "tub, cask."  The carriage sense refers to a carriage having its forward part cut away.  The car sense refers to a closed car body style with its rear seats cut away or removed.
