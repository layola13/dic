incommoded:/ˌɪnkəˈmoʊdɪd/
adjective
感到不便的，受到妨碍的
made to feel uncomfortable or inconvenienced.
She was incommoded by the smallness of the car. 她觉得这辆车太小，很不舒服。
近义词: inconvenienced, bothered, disturbed, troubled
反义词: comfortable, convenient, unbothered, untroubled
词性: adjective

词源:
From French *incommoder*, from Latin *incommodare* "to be inconvenient to, to disturb, trouble," from *in-* "not, opposite of" (see in- (1)) + *commodus* "convenient, suitable, proper" (see commode).
