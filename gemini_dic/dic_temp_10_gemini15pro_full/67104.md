explorers:/ɪkˈsplɔːrərz/
n.
探险家；勘探者
People who travel to unknown places to discover and learn about them.
The early explorers sailed across the oceans to discover new lands. 早期的探险家们航海穿越海洋去发现新大陆。

The explorers mapped the uncharted territory. 探险家们绘制了这片未知的领域的地图。

近义词:
adventurers, pioneers, voyagers, discoverers
反义词:
settlers, inhabitants, natives
词性:
noun (plural)
