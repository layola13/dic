incomprehensible:/ˌɪnkɑːmprɪˈhensəbl/ | adj. | 不可理解的，难以理解的 | Not able to be understood; not intelligible. | The ancient text was filled with incomprehensible symbols. 这份古代文献充满了难以理解的符号。|  His motives are incomprehensible to me. 我无法理解他的动机。 | 近义词: unfathomable, unintelligible, obscure, confusing, perplexing | 反义词: comprehensible, understandable, clear, lucid | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin incomprehensibilis, from in- ‘not’ + comprehensibilis (see comprehensible).
