eti:/ˈiːtaɪ/ | n. | etiquette | 礼仪，礼节 |  Etiquette is the customary code of polite behavior in society or among members of a particular profession or group. | Observing proper dining etiquette is important in formal settings. 在正式场合遵守适当的用餐礼仪很重要。 | synonym: manners, protocol, decorum | antonym: rudeness, impoliteness | noun
