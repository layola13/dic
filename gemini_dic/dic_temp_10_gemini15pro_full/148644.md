overthrifty: /ˈoʊvərˈθrɪfti/ | adj. | 过度节俭的，过于吝啬的 | Excessively thrifty; too economical; parsimonious. |  He was so overthrifty that he reused tea bags multiple times. 他太节俭了，以至于多次重复使用茶包。|  She was overthrifty to the point of being miserly. 她节俭到吝啬的地步。 | 近义词: parsimonious, miserly, stingy, penny-pinching | 反义词: extravagant, generous, prodigal | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above") + "thrifty" (from "thrift" – Middle English, from Old Norse *þrif  "success, prosperity," related to þrífa "to thrive")
