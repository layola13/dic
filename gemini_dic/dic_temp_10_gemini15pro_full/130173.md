non-rhotic:/ˌnɒnˈrɒtɪk/ | adj. |非卷舌音的|  Relating to accents of English in which the consonant /r/ is not pronounced before consonants or at the end of a word, as in most British accents.|  British Received Pronunciation is a non-rhotic accent, so "car" sounds like "cah". |  近义词:r-less | 反义词:rhotic | 词性:adjective
