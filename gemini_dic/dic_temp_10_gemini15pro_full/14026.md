auscultation:/ˌɔːskəlˈteɪʃən/| n.|听诊|The act of listening to sounds within the body, typically with a stethoscope, for diagnostic purposes.|The doctor performed an auscultation of the patient's chest.|近义词：listening, examination, monitoring|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps ignoring, neglecting)|noun
