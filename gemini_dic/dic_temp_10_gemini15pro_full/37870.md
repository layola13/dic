compiles:/kəmˈpaɪlz/ | v. | 编译，汇编，编纂 | translates source code written in a high-level programming language into a lower-level language, such as machine code, that a computer can directly understand and execute.  Also, to collect and edit material into a book, list, or report. | The programmer compiles the code into an executable file.  程序员将代码编译成可执行文件。 | She compiled a list of names and addresses. 她编纂了一份姓名和地址的清单。 | synonyms: assemble, build, gather, amass | antonyms:  disassemble, disperse, scatter | verb
