idiocy:/ˈɪdiəsi/ | n. | 愚蠢，愚昧；极端愚蠢的行为 | Extreme foolishness or stupidity; an act of extreme foolishness. |  His decision to invest all his money in that failing company was sheer idiocy. 他把所有的钱都投资在那家快要倒闭的公司里，真是愚蠢至极。 | synonyms: stupidity, foolishness, folly, absurdity, lunacy | antonyms: wisdom, intelligence, sagacity, prudence | noun
