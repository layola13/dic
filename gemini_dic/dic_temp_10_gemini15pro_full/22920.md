botryogen:/ˈbɒtriəʊdʒɛn/| n. |葡萄石|A hydrous magnesium iron sulfate mineral, MgFe3+(SO4)2(OH)·7H2O, occurring in reddish monoclinic crystals.|Botryogen is a relatively rare mineral found in arid environments. 葡萄石是一种相对稀有的矿物，发现于干旱环境中。|Synonyms: None commonly used for the mineral itself, could use terms like "sulfate mineral", "hydrated mineral"|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
