hellgrammite:/ˈhɛlɡrəˌmaɪt/
n.
地狱火甲虫幼虫; 鱼蛉幼虫
The larval form of the dobsonfly, used as fishing bait.  It is aquatic, with feathery gills and strong pincers.
He baited his hook with a hellgrammite, hoping to catch a big catfish. 他用地狱火甲虫幼虫作鱼饵，希望能钓到大鲶鱼。
近义词: dobsonfly larva, crawler
反义词:  (无明显的反义词，可以考虑其捕食对象，例如 small fish 小鱼)
