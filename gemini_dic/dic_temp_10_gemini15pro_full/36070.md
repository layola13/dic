coefficacy:/ˌkoʊɪˈfɪkəsi/
| n. | 共同功效，协同效应 |The combined effectiveness of separate agents, especially drugs, when used in combination, producing a greater effect than the sum of the individual effects.|The coefficacy of the two drugs resulted in a significant reduction in tumor size. 这两种药物的协同效应显著缩小了肿瘤体积。| synergy, combined effect, joint action | antagonism, counteraction | noun
