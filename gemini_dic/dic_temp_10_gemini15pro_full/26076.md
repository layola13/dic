bushranging:/ˈbʊʃˌreɪndʒɪŋ/
n.
丛林抢劫；拦路抢劫；在澳大利亚丛林地区抢劫
The act of robbing people in rural areas, typically by an outlaw living in the bush, especially in 19th-century Australia.
Bushranging was a serious problem in 19th-century Australia.  19世纪的澳大利亚，丛林抢劫是一个严重的问题。
The Kelly Gang were notorious for their bushranging exploits.  凯利帮因其丛林抢劫的壮举而臭名昭著。
近义词:banditry, highway robbery, outlawry
反义词:law enforcement, policing
