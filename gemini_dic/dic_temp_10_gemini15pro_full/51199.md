destination:/ˌdɛstɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 目的地，终点 |  The place to which someone or something is going or being sent. |  Our destination is Miami. 我们的目的地是迈阿密。| synonyms: goal, target, end point | antonyms: origin, starting point, source | noun

destination:/ˌdɛstɪˈneɪʃən/ | adj. | （尤指婚礼）非常昂贵的，豪华的 | Designed for or suitable for a luxurious vacation. |  a destination wedding 一个豪华的目的地婚礼 | synonyms: luxurious, exclusive, high-end | antonyms: budget, cheap, economical | adjective
