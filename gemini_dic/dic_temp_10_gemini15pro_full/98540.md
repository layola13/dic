instigatrix:/ˈɪnstɪɡeɪtrɪks/
n.
女煽动者，女教唆犯
A woman who instigates or incites someone to do something, especially something bad.
例句：The instigatrix of the riot was never identified.  这场骚乱的煽动者从未被确认。

例句：She was the instigatrix of several charitable projects. 她是一些慈善项目的倡导者。

近义词：agitator, inciter, provocateur, ringleader
反义词：peacemaker, mediator, conciliator
词性：noun
