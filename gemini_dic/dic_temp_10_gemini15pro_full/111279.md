littered:/ˈlɪtərd/ | adj. | 散落着垃圾的；乱糟糟的 | Covered with or containing a lot of scattered objects, especially rubbish. | The park was littered with empty cans and bottles. 公园里到处都是空的罐头和瓶子。 | The floor was littered with clothes. 地板上到处都是衣服。 | 近义词：cluttered, messy, strewn, scattered | 反义词：tidy, clean, neat | 词性: 形容词

词源: From the verb "litter" (to make untidy by scattering things about), which comes from the Old Norse "liðr" (army, camp). Originally, "litter" referred to straw or other material used as bedding for animals, which often ended up scattered around.
