bulchin:/ˈbʌltʃɪn/
n.
壮汉；大块头；暴徒
A heavily built, strong man; often implies a bully or thug.
He's a real bulchin, always picking on smaller kids. 他真是个壮汉，总是欺负比他小的孩子。
Synonyms: bruiser, tough, thug, heavy, muscleman
Antonyms: weakling, wimp, pushover
