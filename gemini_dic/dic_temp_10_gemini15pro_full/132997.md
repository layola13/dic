nonenforcing:/ˌnɒnɪnˈfɔːsɪŋ/
adj.
非强制性的，不强制执行的
Not compelling adherence to or observance of (a law, rule, etc.)
The treaty is essentially nonenforcing.  这项条约基本上是不强制执行的。

The organization issued nonenforcing guidelines. 该组织发布了非强制性指导方针。
Near Synonyms:  non-binding, voluntary, optional, discretionary, permissive
Antonyms: mandatory, compulsory, obligatory, binding, enforced
词性: 形容词
