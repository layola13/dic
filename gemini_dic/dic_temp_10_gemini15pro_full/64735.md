eschynite:/ˈɛskɪnaɪt/ | n. | 钛铈铀矿，易烧石 | A complex mineral containing oxides of cerium, calcium, iron, titanium, thorium, and other elements. It is a source of rare earth elements. | Eschynite is found in igneous and metamorphic rocks.  易烧石发现于火成岩和变质岩中。| Eschynite is a radioactive mineral due to its thorium content. 由于含有钍，易烧石是一种放射性矿物。 |  Synonyms:  priorite, euxenite | Antonyms: N/A | noun
