frankensteining: /ˈfræŋkənˌstaɪnɪŋ/
frankensteining: v. | 拼凑；粗制滥造；胡乱拼凑 |  The act of creating something by combining disparate parts, often with a negative connotation implying a monstrous or unnatural result. | They were accused of frankensteining together a policy from various incompatible proposals.  他们被指责将各种不相容的提案拼凑成一项政策。 | cobbling together, patching together,  jerry-rigging | constructing, designing | verb
