mousetrap: /ˈmaʊstræp/ (US) /ˈmaʊstræp/ (UK)| n. |捕鼠器|A device designed to catch mice.|例句：The mouse was caught in the mousetrap. 老鼠被捕鼠器抓住了。

例句：He set a mousetrap in the kitchen. 他在厨房里放了一个捕鼠器。

近义词：mouse trap (two words), rodent trap|反义词: None|词性: Noun
