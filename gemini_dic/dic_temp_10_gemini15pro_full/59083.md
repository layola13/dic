dyad:/ˈdaɪæd/| n.|二元体，二分体，一对，二人组|A group of two people or things; a pair.|The mother-daughter dyad is central to the story. 母女二元关系是故事的核心。|Synonyms: pair, duo, couple|Antonyms:  monad, individual|noun

dyad:/ˈdaɪæd/| adj.|二元的，二体的|Consisting of two elements or parts.|A dyad symmetry. 二元对称。|Synonyms: binary, dual|Antonyms: singular, unitary|adjective
