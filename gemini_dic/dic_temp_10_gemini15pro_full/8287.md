anti-macho:/ˌæntiˈmɑːtʃoʊ/ /ˌæntiˈmætʃəʊ/
| adj. | 反大男子主义的；反对大男子主义的 | Opposed to or rejecting traditional masculine values and behavior, often seen as aggressive or domineering. |  He has a very anti-macho attitude towards relationships. 他对两性关系的态度非常反大男子主义。| synonyms:  non-macho, anti-sexist, feminist | antonyms: macho, sexist, patriarchal | adjective

anti-macho:/ˌæntiˈmɑːtʃoʊ/ /ˌæntiˈmætʃəʊ/
| n. | 反大男子主义者 | A person who opposes macho attitudes and behavior. | He considers himself an anti-macho. 他认为自己是一个反大男子主义者。| synonyms: feminist, egalitarian | antonyms: machoist, patriarch | noun
