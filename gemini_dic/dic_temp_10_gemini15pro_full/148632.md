overthinly:/ˌōvərˈθɪnli/ | adv. | 过于薄地，过于细地 | Too thinly; excessively thin.  It implies a negative connotation, suggesting something is spread out or distributed too sparsely, or a concept or argument is lacking substance. | The butter was spread overthinly on the toast. 黄油涂在吐司上太薄了。 | The plot was overthinly stretched across three movies.  情节过于单薄地 растянутый на три фильма。 | thinly, sparsely | thickly, densely | adverb
