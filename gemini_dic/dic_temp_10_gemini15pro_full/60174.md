effervescent:/ˌɛfərˈvɛsənt/| adj.|兴奋的；活跃的；冒泡的|Vivacious and enthusiastic; giving off bubbles of gas.|例句：Her effervescent personality made her the life of the party. 她活泼的性格使她成为派对的灵魂。|例句：The effervescent drink tickled her nose. 冒泡的饮料弄得她鼻子痒痒的。|近义词：bubbly, sparkling, lively, vivacious|反义词：flat, dull, listless|词性:形容词
effervesce:/ˌɛfərˈvɛs/| v.|冒泡；兴奋；活跃|To give off bubbles of gas; to show liveliness or exhilaration.|例句：The champagne effervesced in the glass. 香槟在杯子里冒泡。|例句：Her excitement effervesced as she opened the gift. 她打开礼物时兴奋不已。|近义词：fizz, bubble, sparkle|反义词：flatten|词性:动词

词源:
From Latin *ēffervere*, from *ex-* (out) + *fervere* (to boil, glow).
