hornpout:/ˈhɔːrnˌpaʊt/| n.|角 pout鱼，鲶鱼的一种|A freshwater catfish of North America, having fleshy barbels around the mouth.  |例句：The hornpout is a common fish in many North American rivers. 角 pout鱼是北美许多河流中常见的鱼。

例句：He caught a large hornpout while fishing in the lake. 他在湖里钓鱼时钓到了一条大角 pout鱼。|近义词：catfish, bullhead|反义词: None|词性:noun
