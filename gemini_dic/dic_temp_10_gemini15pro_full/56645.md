donnishness:/ˈdɒnɪʃnəs/ /ˈdɑːnɪʃnəs/| n. | 学究气；书呆子气；迂腐 | The quality of being donnish; resembling or characteristic of a university don, especially in being bookish and pedantic.  | He carried an air of donnishness, constantly quoting obscure authors. 他带着一股学究气，总是引用一些鲜为人知的作家。| 近义词：pedantry, bookishness, academicism, scholasticism | 反义词：worldliness, practicality, informality | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From donnish + -ness. Donnish from don + -ish. Don from Spanish don, from Latin dominus "lord, master."
