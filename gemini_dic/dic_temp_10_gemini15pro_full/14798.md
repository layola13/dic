aws:/eɪˌdʌbəlˈjuːˈɛs/ | n. | 亚马逊网络服务 (Amazon Web Services) |  An abbreviation for Amazon Web Services, a subsidiary of Amazon providing on-demand cloud computing platforms and APIs to individuals, companies, and governments, on a metered pay-as-you-go basis. |  Many startups use AWS for their server needs. 许多初创公司使用AWS来满足他们的服务器需求。 | synonyms: Amazon Web Services, cloud platform, cloud provider | antonyms: on-premise infrastructure, traditional hosting | noun
