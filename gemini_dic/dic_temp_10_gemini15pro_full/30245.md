cellulitis:/ˌsel.jʊˈlaɪ.tɪs/
noun
蜂窝织炎
Inflammation of the connective tissue cells of the skin, especially in the subcutaneous tissues, characterized by local heat, redness, pain, and swelling.
The doctor diagnosed her leg pain as cellulitis.  医生诊断她的腿疼是蜂窝织炎。

近义词:inflammation, erysipelas
反义词:(No direct antonym for a medical condition)
词性:noun


cellulitis:/ˌsel.jʊˈlaɪ.tɪs/
noun
皮炎
A spreading bacterial infection of the skin and tissues beneath the skin.
He developed cellulitis after a minor cut on his hand. 他手上的一个小伤口感染了蜂窝织炎。

近义词:dermatitis
反义词:(No direct antonym for a medical condition)
词性:noun
