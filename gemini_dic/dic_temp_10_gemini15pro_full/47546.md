deathtrap: /ˈdɛθˌtræp/ | n. | 死亡陷阱 | A situation, structure, or device that is very dangerous and likely to cause death. |  The old car was a deathtrap, with faulty brakes and bald tires. 这辆旧车刹车失灵，轮胎磨损严重，简直就是个死亡陷阱。 |  The rickety bridge was a deathtrap waiting to happen. 这座摇摇欲坠的桥是一个随时可能发生的死亡陷阱。 | synonym: hazard, peril, menace | antonym: sanctuary, haven | noun
