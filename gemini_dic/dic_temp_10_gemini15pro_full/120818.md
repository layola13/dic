misattend:/ˌmɪsəˈtɛnd/ | v. | 疏忽；未出席；未注意到 | To fail to attend something, either deliberately or accidentally. | He misattended the meeting due to a scheduling conflict. 他由于日程冲突而错过了会议。

She often misattends to her children's needs. 她经常忽略孩子的需求。


| 近义词: neglect, ignore, overlook, skip | 反义词: attend, heed | 词性: 动词
