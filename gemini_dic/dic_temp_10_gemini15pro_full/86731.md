helmsmanship:/ˈhɛlmzmənˌʃɪp/| n.|掌舵术，领导能力|The captain's helmsmanship was praised by all. 船长的掌舵技术受到了所有人的称赞。|例句：His helmsmanship in guiding the company through the crisis was admirable. 他带领公司度过危机的领导能力令人钦佩。|近义词：leadership, guidance, direction, control|反义词:mismanagement, incompetence|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From helm + -manship.  "Helm" comes from Old English "helma," meaning "rudder, tiller." "-manship" is a suffix denoting skill or art in handling something.
