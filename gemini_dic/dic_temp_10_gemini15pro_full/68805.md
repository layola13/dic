fasciated:/ˈfæʃɪeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 簇生的，扁化的，带状的 |  (of plants) developing an abnormally flattened and broadened growing tip, often with multiple growing points, resulting in a ribbon-like or crested appearance.  This can be caused by genetic mutations, bacterial infections, or environmental factors. | The cactus had a fasciated stem, giving it a unique fan-like shape.  那株仙人掌的茎簇生，使其呈现出独特的扇形。 | synonyms: crested, cristate, banded | antonyms: normal, typical | word type: adjective

fasciated:/ˈfæʃɪeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 着迷的，入迷的 | Extremely interested or captivated by something. |  He was completely fascinated by the intricate details of the painting. 他完全被这幅画复杂的细节所吸引。 | synonyms: captivated, enthralled, mesmerized, intrigued | antonyms: bored, disinterested, apathetic | word type: adjective
