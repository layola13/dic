evangelised:/ɪˈvændʒəlaɪzd/ |v. |使皈依基督教，传播福音；宣传，推广|To convert someone to Christianity; to preach the gospel; to promote or spread an idea or cause with enthusiasm.|  Missionaries evangelised the remote tribes.  传教士向偏远的部落传福音。

The company evangelised the benefits of their new product.  该公司大力宣传其新产品的好处。|近义词：preached, promoted, propagated, proselytised|反义词：concealed, suppressed|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *evangelizare, from Ancient Greek εὐαγγελίζειν (euangelizein, “to bring good news”), from εὐάγγελος (euangelos, “bringing good news”), from εὖ (eu, “well”) + ἄγγελος (angelos, “messenger”).
