contagiousness:/kənˈteɪdʒəsnəs/
n.
传染性，感染性
The contagiousness of the new virus is alarming.  这种新病毒的传染性令人担忧。
The doctor stressed the contagiousness of the disease and the importance of quarantine. 医生强调了这种疾病的传染性和隔离的重要性。

近义词:  infectivity, communicability
反义词:  non-contagiousness, resistance (to infection)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From contagious + -ness.  Contagious comes from Late Latin contagiosus "communicable by contact," from Latin contingere "to touch, reach, affect," from com- "together" (see com-) + tangere "to touch" (see tangent).
