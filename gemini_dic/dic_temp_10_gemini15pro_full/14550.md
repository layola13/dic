aversions:/əˈvɜːrʒənz/ |n.|厌恶，反感（复数）|strong dislikes or disinclinations.|She has many aversions, including loud noises and crowded places.|disgusts, distastes, repugnances| likings, fondnesses |noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *aversio*, from *aversus* (past participle of *avertere*, "to turn away"), from *ab* ("from, away from") + *vertere* ("to turn").
