forming:/ˈfɔːrmɪŋ/ | v. |形成，构成，塑造|The clouds are forming unusual shapes. 云朵正在形成不寻常的形状。|例句：  The children were forming a circle. 孩子们正在围成一个圈。|近义词：creating, shaping, developing, making, composing|反义词：destroying, dismantling, dissolving|词性:动词(present participle of form)

forming:/ˈfɔːrmɪŋ/ | adj. |形成的，构成的|The forming process is crucial.  形成过程至关重要。|例句： The forming committee will meet next week.  筹建委员会将于下周召开会议。|近义词：developing, nascent, emergent|反义词：established, complete, finished|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *formen, from Old French former, from Latin formāre (“to form, shape, build, fashion, create, conceive, imagine”), from forma (“form, shape, figure, appearance”).
