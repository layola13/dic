distilling:/dɪˈstɪlɪŋ/ | n. | 蒸馏 | The process of purifying a liquid by heating it until it becomes a gas and then cooling the gas back into a liquid. |  The distilling of whiskey is a complex process. 威士忌的蒸馏是一个复杂的过程。| synonyms: purification, refinement | antonyms: contamination, pollution | noun

distil:/dɪˈstɪl/ | v. | 蒸馏; 提炼; 渗出; 滴下 | 1. To purify a liquid by heating it until it becomes a gas and then cooling the gas back into a liquid. 2. To extract the essential meaning or most important aspects of something.  3. To fall or flow slowly in drops or a thin stream. | They distil whisky from malted barley. 他们用麦芽大麦蒸馏威士忌。| synonyms: extract, refine, purify, concentrate | antonyms: contaminate, pollute, dilute | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *distiller, from Latin distillare "to drip down," from dis- "down" + stillare "to drip," from stilla "a drop."
