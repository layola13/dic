gibbing:/ˈɡɪbɪŋ/ | n. | 去内脏；取出鱼鳃 | The process of removing the gills and guts of a fish. |  Example: The gibbing of the fish was done quickly and efficiently. 鱼的去内脏工作快速高效地完成了。

Example:  Proper gibbing is important for preserving the freshness of the catch.  适当的去内脏对于保持渔获的新鲜度很重要。 | 近义词: gutting, cleaning | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: 名词

词源:  From the verb "gib," which means to remove the gills of a fish.  Likely of Scandinavian origin.
