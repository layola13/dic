incoordinate:/ɪnkoʊˈɔːrdɪnət/ | adj. |不协调的，不配合的|Lacking coordination; not able to move different parts of your body together smoothly and easily. Not working together in a smooth and effective way. |例句：His movements were clumsy and incoordinate. 他的动作笨拙而不协调。

His incoordinate efforts to catch the ball resulted in him tripping over his own feet. 他笨手笨脚地想接住球，结果自己绊倒了。 |近义词：clumsy, awkward, ungainly, uncoordinated |反义词： coordinated, graceful, adept, skillful |词性: 形容词
