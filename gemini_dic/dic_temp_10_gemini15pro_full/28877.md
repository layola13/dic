carpological:/ˌkɑːrpəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
果实学的；果实的
Relating to the study of fruits and seeds, especially their structure and classification.
Carpological studies help us understand plant evolution. 果实学研究帮助我们了解植物进化。
The botanist presented a carpological analysis of the newly discovered species.  这位植物学家对新发现的物种进行了果实学分析。
近义词:fructarian,  fruitful
