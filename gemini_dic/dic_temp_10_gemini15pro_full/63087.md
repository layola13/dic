enseal:/ɪnˈsiːl/ | v. | 密封，盖章 | To enclose something in a sealed container or cover, often to protect it. |  例句：He ensealed the letter with wax. 他用蜡把信封起来了。

例句：The documents were ensealed in a plastic bag. 文件被密封在一个塑料袋里。 | 近义词：seal, close, secure, fasten | 反义词：open, unseal | 词性: 动词

词源:
From en- + seal (v.).  The prefix "en-" adds the sense of "in" or "into" to the verb "seal," meaning to close or fasten securely.  Ultimately, "seal" comes from Old French "seel," from Latin "sigillum," meaning "small image" or "seal."
