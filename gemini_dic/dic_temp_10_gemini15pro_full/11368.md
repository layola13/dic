areometrical:/ˌɛəriəˈmɛtrɪkəl/
adjective
面积测量的，测定面积的
Relating to the measurement of areas, especially of surfaces.
The areometrical calculations were crucial for determining the land's value.  这块土地的面积测量对于确定其价值至关重要。
The architect used areometrical methods to calculate the building's surface area. 建筑师使用面积测量方法来计算建筑物的表面积。
近义词: planimetric, area-measuring
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider volumetric for measuring volume)
词性: adjective


词源:
From Greek "ἀρεο-" (areo-), combining form of "ἄρος" (aros, "surface, area") + "-metrical" (relating to measurement).
