pastoralists:/ˈpɑːstərəlɪsts/ | n. | 牧民；游牧民族 |  People who live by herding livestock, often nomadically.  They rely on grazing animals for their livelihood and often migrate with their herds to find fresh pasture. | The pastoralists moved their herds to higher ground during the summer months. 牧民在夏季将他们的牲畜迁移到更高的地方。| Nomads, herdsmen, shepherds | Farmers, city dwellers | noun
