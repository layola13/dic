It often implies that something is so cheap that it raises suspicions about its quality or other aspects. | Example: The overcheapness of the clothes made me question their quality. 衣服的过分便宜让我怀疑它们的质量。 | Synonym: cheapness, affordability, inexpensiveness | Antonym: expensiveness, dearness, costliness | noun
