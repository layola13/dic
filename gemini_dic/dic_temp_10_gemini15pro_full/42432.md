counterculturists:/ˈkaʊntərˈkʌltʃərɪsts/| n.|反主流文化者，反文化者|People who reject mainstream culture and advocate alternative lifestyles and values.  Those who oppose the dominant values and norms of their society and seek to create alternative social structures.|The 1960s saw a rise in counterculturists who challenged traditional societal norms.  20世纪60年代反主流文化者兴起，他们挑战传统的社会规范。|synonyms: nonconformists, rebels, dissidents, bohemians|antonyms: conformists, traditionalists|noun
