glaucine:/ˈɡlɔːsiːn/| n.|光千金藤碱|An alkaloid found in several plant species, including *Glaucium flavum* (yellow horned poppy), with anti-inflammatory, antitussive, and analgesic properties. It acts as a dopamine receptor antagonist and has potential therapeutic applications.|Glaucine is being studied for its potential use in treating cough.  光千金藤碱正在被研究用于治疗咳嗽的潜力。

Glaucine is extracted from the yellow horned poppy. 光千金藤碱是从黄罂粟中提取的。|近义词:apomorphine (in some contexts), noscapine|反义词: None (specific)|词性:noun
