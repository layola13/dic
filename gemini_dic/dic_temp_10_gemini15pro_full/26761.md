caecostomy:/siːkɒˈstɒmi/ | n. |盲肠造口术|The surgical creation of an artificial opening into the cecum, the first part of the large intestine.  This allows for the drainage of fecal matter when the normal route is obstructed.|The patient required a caecostomy due to a bowel obstruction.|Synonyms: cecostomy|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
