goura:/ˈɡʊərə/| n. |冠鸠|Any of several large pigeons of the genus *Goura*, native to New Guinea and nearby islands, having a large, fan-shaped crest.|例句：The Victoria crowned pigeon (*Goura victoria*) is the largest species of goura. 维多利亚冠鸠是冠鸠中体型最大的品种。

例句：Goura pigeons are known for their elaborate courtship displays. 冠鸠以其精致的求偶仪式而闻名。|近义词：crowned pigeon, pigeon|反义词:|名词
