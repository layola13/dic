dispersal:/dɪˈspɜːrsl/
n.|分散，传播|The dispersal of seeds is essential for plant reproduction.  种子的传播对植物繁殖至关重要。|
Etymology: From Late Latin *dispersalis, from Latin dispersus (past participle of dispergere "to scatter"), from dis- "apart" + spargere "to scatter".
Synonyms: scattering, dissemination, distribution, spreading
Antonyms: concentration, gathering, collection
noun

disperse:/dɪˈspɜːrs/
v.|分散，传播|The crowd dispersed after the concert.  音乐会结束后人群散开了。|
