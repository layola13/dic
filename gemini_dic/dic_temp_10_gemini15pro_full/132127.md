noncrystallizing:/ˌnɑːnˈkrɪstəlaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
非结晶的
Not forming crystals; remaining amorphous.
The noncrystallizing polymer remained a viscous liquid even at low temperatures.  这种非结晶聚合物即使在低温下也保持粘稠的液体状态。
The scientist studied the noncrystallizing behavior of certain metallic alloys.  科学家研究了某些金属合金的非结晶行为。
近义词: amorphous, noncrystalline
反义词: crystalline, crystallizing
词性: adjective
