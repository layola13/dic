octillion:/ɒkˈtɪljən/ /ɑːkˈtɪljən/| n. |百万的三次方; 千的八次方（美式英语）; 百万的四次方; 千的十二次方（英式英语）|A number represented by 1 followed by 27 zeros (American system) or 48 zeros (British system).  一个由1后跟27个零（美式）或48个零（英式）表示的数字。|The US national debt is in the octillions. 美国国债高达数百万的三次方。|The universe is thought to contain octillions of stars.  人们认为宇宙中有数百万的四次方颗恒星（英式）。|Synonym:  (US) septillion, (UK) quadrillion|Antonym:  (none -  a specific large number has no direct opposite)|Noun
