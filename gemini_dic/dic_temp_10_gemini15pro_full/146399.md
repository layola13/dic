overfluentnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈfluːəntnɪsɪz/| n. | 过度流利; 过于流畅 | The overfluentnesses of his speech made it difficult to understand. 他说话过于流畅，让人难以理解。|  Excessive fluency, often characterized by rapid and sometimes incoherent speech.  This can be a symptom of certain neurological conditions.  |  His overfluentnesses masked a lack of real depth in his understanding of the subject. 他过于流畅的表达掩盖了他对主题缺乏真正深入的理解。 |  verbosity, volubility, loquacity |  hesitancy, reticence | noun

Etymology: "over" (Old English *ofer) + "fluent" (from Latin fluentem, present participle of fluere "to flow") + "-ness" (Old English -nes(s), suffix forming nouns of state or quality)
