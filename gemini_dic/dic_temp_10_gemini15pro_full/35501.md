coadjutive:/koʊˈæʤətɪv/| adj.|辅助的，佐剂的|Serving as or relating to a coadjutant; assisting or aiding, especially in a secondary capacity.  It can refer to something that enhances the effect of something else, often in a medical context.|The coadjutive therapy helped boost the effectiveness of the main treatment. 辅助疗法有助于提高主要治疗的有效性。|Synonyms: auxiliary, supplementary, adjuvant, ancillary|Antonyms: primary, main, principal|adjective

coadjutive:/koʊˈæʤətɪv/| n.|佐剂|A substance that enhances the body's immune response to an antigen.|The vaccine included a coadjutive to stimulate a stronger immune response. 疫苗中包含一种佐剂，以刺激更强的免疫反应。|Synonyms: adjuvant, booster|Antonyms: suppressant|noun
