manumit:/ˌmænjʊˈmɪt/| vt.|解放，释放（奴隶）|To release from slavery; to free.|例句：The plantation owner decided to manumit all of his slaves.  种植园主决定释放他所有的奴隶。|例句：The law manumitted slaves who had fought bravely in the war.  法律解放了在战争中英勇作战的奴隶。|近义词：liberate, free, release, emancipate|反义词：enslave, imprison|动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *manūmittere*, from *manu* ("from hand") + *mittere* ("to send, to let go").  Literally, "to release from one's hand (power)".
