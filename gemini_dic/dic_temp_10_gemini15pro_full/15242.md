backfriend: /ˈbækˌfrɛnd/ | n. | 背后朋友，假朋友 | Someone who pretends to be your friend but talks negatively about you behind your back. |  He thought she was his friend, but she turned out to be a backfriend, spreading rumors about him. 他原以为她是他的朋友，结果她却是一个背后朋友，到处散播关于他的谣言。 |  Synonyms: two-faced, betrayer, hypocrite, snake in the grass | Antonyms: true friend, loyal friend, confidant | noun
