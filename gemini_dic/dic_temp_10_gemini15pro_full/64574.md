ersatz:/ˈɛrzɑːts/ /ˈɜːrzɑːts/| adj. n.|代用的，ersatz 的；代用品|Made or used as a substitute, typically an inferior one, for something else.|The ersatz coffee tasted like burnt toast.  这种代用咖啡尝起来像烤焦的吐司。|The margarine was an ersatz butter.  这人造黄油是黄油的替代品。|近义词：artificial, substitute, imitation, synthetic, fake|反义词：genuine, real, authentic|词性: adjective, noun

词源: From German Ersatz, from ersetzen ‘to replace’, from er- (intensifying prefix) + setzen ‘to set’.
