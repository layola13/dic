demilitarises:/diːˈmɪlɪtəraɪzɪz/
verb
使非军事化；解除武装
to remove the military forces from an area or country; to remove weapons from an area or country
The treaty demilitarises the border region.  该条约使边境地区非军事化。
The government has pledged to demilitarise the zone. 政府已承诺将该区域非军事化。
Synonyms: disarm, neutralize
Antonyms: militarize, arm
词性: 动词
