overimported:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈpɔːrtɪd/
adjective
过度进口的
Having imported goods in excess of domestic demand or in quantities that harm domestic producers.
The market was overimported with cheap electronics, causing local manufacturers to struggle.  市场上充斥着廉价的进口电子产品，导致本地制造商举步维艰。
The country's steel industry was severely impacted by overimported products from overseas.  该国的钢铁工业受到海外过度进口产品的严重影响。
Synonyms: oversupplied, glutted
Antonyms: undersupplied, scarce
词性: 形容词
