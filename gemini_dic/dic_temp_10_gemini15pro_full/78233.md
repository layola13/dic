gerbils:/ˈdʒɜːrbɪlz/
n.
沙鼠；沙土鼠
Small, furry rodents often kept as pets. They are known for their burrowing habits and long tails.
My daughter wants two gerbils for her birthday.  我女儿生日想要两只沙鼠。
近义词: rodents, hamsters
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑predator 捕食者)
词性: noun
