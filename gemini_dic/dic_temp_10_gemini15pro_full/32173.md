chia:/ˈtʃiːə/| n.|奇亚（加密货币）|A cryptocurrency that uses a proof-of-space-and-time consensus mechanism. |Chia is designed to be more energy-efficient than Bitcoin. 奇亚币的设计比比特币更节能。|Synonyms: XCH (its ticker symbol)|Antonyms: None|Noun
