choking-off:/ˈtʃoʊkɪŋ ˈɔːf/| v.|压制，扼杀，阻止|The government is choking off the flow of information.|政府正在压制信息的流通。|例句：The sanctions are aimed at choking off the country's access to international finance.  这些制裁旨在切断该国获得国际金融的渠道。

The central bank is choking off credit to try to control inflation. 中央银行正在限制信贷以试图控制通货膨胀。|近义词：suppressing, stifling, throttling, suppressing|反义词:encouraging, promoting, fostering|词性:动词

词源: "choke" 来自古英语 *ceocian，意为“窒息”。 "off" 作为副词，表示“离开，中断”。  "choking off"  是动词短语，表示阻止某事物继续发展。
