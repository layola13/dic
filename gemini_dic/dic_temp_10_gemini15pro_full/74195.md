fourhanded:/ˌfɔːrˈhændɪd/ | adj. | 四手的；四人玩的；需要四只手的 | Played or done by four people, two on each side. |  例句：They played a fourhanded game of bridge. 他们玩了一场四人桥牌。


例句：The piano piece was fourhanded, requiring two players. 这首钢琴曲是四手联弹，需要两个演奏者。

近义词：collaborative, joint | 反义词: solo, single-handed | 词性: adjective
