out-beams:/ˌaʊtˈbiːmz/| v.|发出更强的光，胜过|To emit beams of light that are stronger or more numerous than another source.|The new lighthouse out-beamed the old one, making navigation much safer. 新灯塔的光比旧灯塔强，使航行更加安全。|Synonyms: outshine, surpass, eclipse|Antonyms: dim, darken|Verb

Etymology:
"Out-" (Old English *ūt, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *ūt) + "beam" (Old English bēam, from Proto-Germanic *baumaz)
