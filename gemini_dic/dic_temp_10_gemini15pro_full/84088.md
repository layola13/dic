half-ingeniousness:/ˌhæf ˌɪnˈdʒiːniəsnəs/
|n.| 半聪明; 不完全的聪明 | A state of being only partially ingenious; a mixture of cleverness and naiveté or impracticality. |  His plan showed a certain half-ingeniousness, clever in parts but ultimately flawed.  他的计划显示出某种半聪明，部分 clever 但最终有缺陷。 | partial ingenuity, flawed cleverness |  complete ingenuity, brilliance | noun
