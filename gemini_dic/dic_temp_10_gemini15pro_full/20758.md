blandiloquous:/ˌblændɪˈloʊkwəs/| adj.|甜言蜜语的，奉承的|Speaking or expressed in a flattering or ingratiating manner; smooth-tongued.|例句：The salesman's blandiloquous manner convinced her to buy the expensive car. 销售员奉承的态度说服了她买下那辆昂贵的车。|例句：He delivered a blandiloquous speech, full of empty promises. 他发表了一通花言巧语的演讲，充满了空洞的承诺。|近义词：flattering, ingratiating, unctuous, smooth-tongued|反义词：blunt, frank, candid, forthright|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin blandiloquus ("speaking smoothly, using flattery"), from blandus ("smooth, flattering") + loqui ("to speak").
