azerbaijans:/ˌɑːzərˌbɑɪˈdʒɑːnz/ | n. | 阿塞拜疆人 | People from Azerbaijan, a country in the Caucasus region of Eurasia. |  Many Azerbaijanis speak Azerbaijani, a Turkic language. 许多阿塞拜疆人说阿塞拜疆语，一种突厥语。| Azerbaijanis are known for their hospitality. 阿塞拜疆人以热情好客而闻名。 |  Synonyms: Azerbaijani people | Antonyms: N/A | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Azerbaijan + -s (plural marker).  "Azerbaijan" itself derives from Atropates, a Persian satrap of Media under Alexander the Great,  combined with the Persian suffix -an (place of).
