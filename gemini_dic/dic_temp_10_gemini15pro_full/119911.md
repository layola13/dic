militia: /məˈlɪʃə/ | n. | 民兵，义勇军 | A military force that is raised from the civil population to supplement a regular army in an emergency.  A body of citizens enrolled for military service, and called out periodically for drill but serving full time only in emergencies. | The governor called out the state militia to help with the flood relief efforts. 州长召集了州民兵队来协助抗洪救灾工作。| The militia was mobilized to defend the border. 民兵被动员起来保卫边境。 | synonyms:  national guard, reserves, volunteer army, irregular troops | antonyms: regular army, standing army | noun
