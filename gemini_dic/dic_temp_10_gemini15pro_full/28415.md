carboniferous:/ˌkɑːrbəˈnɪfərəs/| adj. n.|石炭纪的，含煤的；石炭纪|Formed or originating during the Carboniferous period, rich in coal deposits. |The carboniferous period is known for its vast coal swamps. 石炭纪以其广阔的煤炭沼泽而闻名。|Synonyms: coal-bearing, coal-rich|Antonyms: post-carboniferous, pre-carboniferous|adjective, noun

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The Carboniferous period lasted from about 359 to 299 million years ago. 石炭纪持续了大约3.59亿到2.99亿年。
Carboniferous rocks often contain fossils of plants and early amphibians. 石炭纪岩石通常包含植物和早期两栖动物的化石。
