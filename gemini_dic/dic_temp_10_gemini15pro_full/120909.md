miscarriage:/ˈmɪsˌkærɪdʒ/| n. |流产；失败；失误|The spontaneous or induced expulsion of a fetus from the womb before it is viable. Also, a failure to achieve a desired outcome. |She suffered a miscarriage in her first trimester. 她在妊娠的头三个月流产了。 The project ended in miscarriage. 这个项目以失败告终。|近义词：abortion (in the medical sense), failure, setback|反义词:success, birth, delivery|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *miscariage*, from mis- + cariage (“carriage, conveyance”), equivalent to mis- + carriage.
