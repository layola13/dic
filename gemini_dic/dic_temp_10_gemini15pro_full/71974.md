flimsier:/ˈflɪmziər/| adj.|更脆弱的，更薄的|Comparative form of flimsy: more flimsy.  Meaning even more weakly constructed, thin, or insubstantial.|The evidence against him was even flimsier than we thought. 对他不利的证据比我们想象的还要薄弱。|例句：The old bridge felt flimsier than ever after the storm.  暴风雨过后，那座旧桥感觉比以前更脆弱了。

例句：Her excuse for being late was pretty flimsy. 她迟到的借口很站不住脚。|近义词：frail, weak, thin, delicate, insubstantial|反义词：strong, sturdy, robust, solid, substantial|词性：形容词

flimsy:/ˈflɪmzi/| adj.|脆弱的，薄的|Weakly constructed, thin, and easily damaged; not strong or substantial; lacking force or plausibility (of an argument, excuse, etc.).|The box was made of flimsy cardboard. 这个盒子是用薄纸板做的。|例句：He tried to build a shelter from flimsy branches. 他试图用脆弱的树枝搭建一个庇护所。

例句：She gave a flimsy excuse for not attending the meeting. 她为没有参加会议找了个站不住脚的借口。|近义词：frail, weak, thin, delicate, insubstantial|反义词：strong, sturdy, robust, solid, substantial|词性：形容词
