ecclesiastically:/ɪˌkliːziˈæstɪkli/
adverb
以教会的方式，宗教上地
In a manner related to the church or clergy; from a religious standpoint.
The matter was handled ecclesiastically, with a formal ceremony conducted by the bishop. 这件事是由教会处理的，主教主持了一个正式的仪式。
The building was ecclesiastically significant, having served as a cathedral for centuries.  这座建筑具有重要的宗教意义，几个世纪以来一直是座大教堂。
近义词:religiously, piously, churchly
反义词:secularly, worldly
词性:adverb
