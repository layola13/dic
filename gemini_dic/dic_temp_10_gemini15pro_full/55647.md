ditherer:/ˈdɪðərər/ | n. | 犹豫不决的人；踌躇者 | someone who is unable to make a decision; a hesitant person |  He was a terrible ditherer and could never make up his mind. 他是个优柔寡断的人，永远做不了决定。 | synonyms: hesitator, waverer, vacillator | antonyms: decisive person, resolute person | noun

dither:/ˈdɪðər/ | v. | 犹豫不决；踌躇 | be indecisive; hesitate |  She dithered over which dress to buy.  她在买哪条裙子上犹豫不决。 | synonyms: hesitate, waver, vacillate | antonyms: decide, resolve | verb
