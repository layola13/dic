nonencyclopedical:/ˌnɒnɪnˌsaɪkləˈpiːdɪkəl/
adjective
非百科全书式的
Not suitable for inclusion in an encyclopedia, typically because of lacking depth, breadth, or verifiable factual basis.
This blog post is too nonencyclopedical for Wikipedia. 这篇博客文章太不百科全书式了，不适合维基百科。
The information is too trivial and nonencyclopedical.  这些信息太琐碎，不具百科全书性。
近义词:unencyclopedic, unsuitable, trivial, unimportant
反义词:encyclopedic, suitable, important, significant
词性:adjective
词源:non- (not) + encyclopedical (relating to an encyclopedia)
