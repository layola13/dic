amoebaeum:/əˈmiːbiəm/| n. |变形虫室，变形虫状结构|A cavity in certain protozoa, such as foraminifera, that contains granular or alveolar cytoplasm and functions in locomotion and food capture.  A structure resembling an amoeba.|The radiolarian's amoebaeum extended through the pores in its shell.|近义词:pseudopodium, cytoplasm|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider structures with rigid forms like cell wall)|noun

amoeba:/əˈmiːbə/| n. |变形虫|A single-celled animal that catches food and moves about by extending fingerlike projections of protoplasm. Amoebas are either free-living in damp environments or parasitic.|The amoeba engulfed the bacteria.|近义词:protozoan, rhizopod|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider multicellular organisms)|noun
