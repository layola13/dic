marigold: /ˈmærɪɡoʊld/ (US) /ˈmærɪɡɒld/ (UK)| n. |万寿菊；金盏花|A common garden plant with bright yellow or orange flowers.  A type of flower, typically orange or yellow, with a strong scent.|例句：The marigolds in her garden were a vibrant orange. 她花园里的万寿菊是鲜艳的橙色。

例句：She planted marigolds to deter pests. 她种植万寿菊来驱除害虫。|近义词：calendula (specifically pot marigold)|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
