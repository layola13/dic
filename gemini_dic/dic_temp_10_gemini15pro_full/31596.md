chasteners:/ˈtʃeɪsənərz/
n.
惩罚者；磨炼者；使人改正者
People who discipline or correct someone, often to improve their behavior or character.
The chasteners of his youth helped shape him into a responsible adult.  他年轻时的磨炼使他成为一个负责任的成年人。
近义词: disciplinarians, correctors, reformers
反义词: encouragers, pamperers
