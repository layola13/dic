hemidactyl:/ˌhɛmɪˈdæktɪl/| n. |壁虎科动物；攀岩 gecko|A hemidactyl is a type of gecko, often found in tropical and subtropical regions, known for its ability to climb smooth surfaces. 壁虎是一种在热带和亚热带地区常见的壁虎科动物，以其攀爬光滑表面的能力而闻名。|The hemidactyl scurried across the ceiling.  壁虎在天花板上快速爬行。|Synonym: gecko, lizard|Antonym: N/A|noun

hemidactylous:/ˌhɛmɪˈdæktɪləs/| adj. |半趾型的；半指（趾）的|Having toes or fingers partially webbed or resembling those of a hemidactyl. 指或趾部分蹼状的，或类似于壁虎科动物的指或趾。|The frog had hemidactylous feet.  这只青蛙的脚是半蹼状的。|Synonym: web-footed, semi-palmate|Antonym: non-webbed|adjective
