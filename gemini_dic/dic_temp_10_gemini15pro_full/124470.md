mouflon:/ˈmuːflɒn/ (US) /ˈmuːflɒn/ (UK)| n.|野绵羊，摩弗伦羊|A wild sheep (Ovis musimon or Ovis orientalis) native to Corsica and Sardinia, having large horns.| We saw a herd of mouflon grazing on the hillside. 我们看到一群摩弗伦羊在山坡上吃草。|Example: The mouflon is considered an ancestor of domesticated sheep. 摩弗伦羊被认为是家养绵羊的祖先.|Synonym: wild sheep, argali|Antonym: domesticated sheep|noun
