glunch:/ɡlʌntʃ/ | n. |早午餐; 介于午餐和晚餐之间的一餐 | A meal eaten between lunch and dinner; a late lunch or early dinner. |  We had a glunch at 4pm because we skipped lunch. 我们因为没吃午饭，所以在下午4点吃了一顿早午餐。

Glunch is a great option when you have a late lunch. 当你午饭吃得晚的时候，早午餐是个不错的选择。| 近义词: late lunch, early dinner | 反义词: breakfast, brunch | 词性: 名词
