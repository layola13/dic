infranchise:/ɪnˈfræntʃaɪz/ | vt. | 给予…公民权；赋予…选举权；特许，授权 | To grant the rights of citizenship, especially the right to vote; to grant a franchise to. |  The Nineteenth Amendment infranchised women in the United States.  第十九修正案赋予美国妇女选举权。

The city council infranchised the company to operate the bus system. 市议会授权该公司运营公共汽车系统。 | 近义词：empower, enfranchise, license, authorize | 反义词：disenfranchise | 词性: 动词
