congruity:/kənˈɡruːɪti/ /kɑːŋˈɡruːɪti/| n. |一致性，适合性，和谐 |The congruity between the two reports suggests they were based on the same data.  两份报告的一致性表明它们基于相同的数据。 | Example:  There's a definite congruity between her lifestyle and her values. 她的生活方式和她的价值观之间有着明确的一致性。

Example: The architect strove for congruity between the building and its surroundings.  建筑师力求建筑物与其周围环境的和谐。 | Synonym: agreement, harmony, consistency, conformity, compatibility | Antonym: incongruity, disparity, discrepancy, incompatibility, dissonance | Noun

congruent:/kənˈɡruːənt/ /kɑːŋˈɡruːənt/| adj. |一致的，适合的，全等的|The two triangles are congruent. 这两个三角形全等。 | Example: Her actions are congruent with her beliefs. 她的行为与她的信仰一致。

Example:  The company's marketing strategy is congruent with its overall business goals. 公司的营销策略与其整体业务目标一致。 | Synonym: corresponding, matching, consistent, harmonious, compatible | Antonym: incongruent, disparate, discrepant, incompatible, dissonant | Adjective
