fangleness:/ˈfæŋɡəlnəs/
noun
新奇; 新鲜玩意儿; 新奇的装置
The quality of being newfangled.  A fondness for newfangled gadgets.
He was fascinated by the fangleness of the new virtual reality headset. 他对这款新式虚拟现实头盔的新奇之处着迷不已。
The fangleness of the self-driving car intrigued him. 自动驾驶汽车的新奇性引起了他的兴趣。

近义词:novelty, newness, innovation, modernity
反义词:oldness, antiquity, staleness, tradition

词性:noun (名词)

词源:From "newfangled" + "-ness". "Newfangled" is from "new" + "fangle" (a novelty or gadget), which is of uncertain origin, possibly related to "fangen" (to catch, seize).
