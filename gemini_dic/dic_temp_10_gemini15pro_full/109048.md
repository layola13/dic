legalise:/ˈliːɡəlaɪz/ (UK) /ˈliɡəˌlaɪz/ (US)| vt.|使合法化，将…合法化|To make something legal; to give legal status to something that was previously illegal.|例句：Many countries have legalized same-sex marriage. 许多国家已经将同性婚姻合法化。

例句：The government is considering legalising cannabis for medicinal use. 政府正在考虑将大麻用于医疗用途合法化。

近义词：decriminalize, legitimize, authorize|反义词: criminalize, prohibit, ban|词性: verb
