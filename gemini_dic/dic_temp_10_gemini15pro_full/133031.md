nonenumerative:/ˌnɑːnɪˈnjuːmərətɪv/ | adj. | 非枚举的，非穷举的 | Not listing all the members of a class or category, but giving some examples or a general description. | This list of side effects is nonenumerative, meaning there may be others not listed here.  这份副作用清单并非详尽无遗，意味着可能还有其他未列出的副作用。| Synonyms: non-exhaustive, incomplete, partial | Antonyms: exhaustive, complete, comprehensive | word type: adjective
