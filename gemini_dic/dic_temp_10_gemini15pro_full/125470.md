multiplier:/ˈmʌltɪplaɪər/| n.|乘数；倍增器；乘法器；扩张器|A multiplier is a factor that amplifies or increases the base value of something.|The government spending multiplier is estimated to be 1.5. 政府支出乘数估计为 1.5。|synonyms: factor, coefficient, multiple|antonyms: divisor, divider|noun

multiplier:/ˈmʌltɪplaɪər/| n.|乘数；倍增器；乘法器；扩张器|A multiplier is something that increases something else by a specific factor.|The use of a fertilizer acts as a multiplier of crop yields. 使用肥料可以使作物产量倍增。|synonyms: amplifier, enhancer, magnifier|antonyms: reducer, diminisher|noun
