cablegram:/ˈkeɪblɡræm/| n.|电报|A message sent by cable.  A telegram sent by submarine cable.|例句：She received a cablegram informing her of her father's death. 她收到一封电报，通知她父亲去世的消息。

例句：The cablegram arrived just in time to prevent the disaster.  这封电报来得正是时候，阻止了灾难的发生。|近义词：telegram, wire|反义词: (No direct antonym, could be considered archaic communication like letter)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From cable + -gram. Cable refers to the submarine communication cables used to transmit messages, and -gram comes from Greek -gramma, meaning "something written or drawn."
