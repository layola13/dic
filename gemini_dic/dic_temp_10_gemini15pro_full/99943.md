internships:/ˈɪntɜːrnʃɪps/ (US), /ˈɪntɜːnʃɪps/ (UK)| n. |实习; 实习期|A period of work experience offered by an organization for a limited period of time, often to students or recent graduates.|She had several internships during her college years. 她大学期间做过几次实习。|Near synonyms:  placement, traineeship, apprenticeship|Antonym: permanent employment|Noun
