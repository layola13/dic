falstaffian:/fɔːlˈstæfiən/
adjective
福斯塔夫式的；豪爽快乐的；吹牛的；好色的
Of or relating to Shakespeare's character Sir John Falstaff, especially with regard to his boisterous, jovial, and boastful nature.  Marked by joviality and robust, often bawdy humor.
He has a falstaffian sense of humor. 他有福斯塔夫式的幽默感。
synonyms: jovial, boisterous,  rotund,  Rabelaisian,  Bacchanalian,  epicurean,  convivial,  merry,  jolly,  mirthful,  cheerful,  genial,  affable,  amiable,  gregarious,  sociable
antonyms: ascetic,  abstemious,  temperate,  sober,  serious,  grave,  solemn,  austere,  puritanical
