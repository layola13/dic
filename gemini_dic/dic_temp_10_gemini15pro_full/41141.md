copublished:/koʊˈpʌblɪʃt/ | adj. | 共同出版的 | Published by two or more publishers. | Example: This book was copublished by Oxford University Press and Harvard University Press. 这本书由牛津大学出版社和哈佛大学出版社共同出版。| Example: The research paper was copublished in two leading scientific journals. 这篇研究论文在两家领先的科学期刊上共同发表。 | Synonym: jointly published | Antonym: solely published | Word type: adjective
