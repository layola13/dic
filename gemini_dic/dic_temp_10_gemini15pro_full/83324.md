gypseous:/ˈdʒɪpsiəs/| adj.|含石膏的，石膏质的|Relating to or containing gypsum; resembling gypsum.|例句：The soil in this area is gypseous and therefore not suitable for most plants.  这个地区的土壤是石膏质的，因此不适合大多数植物生长。

例句：The walls of the cave were covered in gypseous formations.  洞穴的墙壁上覆盖着石膏状的构造。|近义词：chalky, calcareous|反义词:|词性:形容词

Etymology->
From French *gypseux*, from *gypse* (“gypsum”) (from Latin *gypsum*, from Ancient Greek *γύψος* (*gýpsos*)), + *-eux*.
