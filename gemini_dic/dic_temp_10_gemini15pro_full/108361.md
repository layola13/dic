lavishing:/ˈlævɪʃɪŋ/
verb
慷慨地给予，挥霍
Giving something in great amounts, often excessively or extravagantly.
He was lavishing her with gifts and attention.  他对她大肆挥霍礼物和关注。
She lavished praise on her students. 她对学生们大加赞扬。
Synonyms: showering, heaping, bestowing, squandering
Antonyms: withholding, stinting, scrimping
词性: 动词 (present participle of lavish)

lavish:/ˈlævɪʃ/
adjective
奢华的，丰富的
Characterized by extravagance and abundance.
They lived a lavish lifestyle. 他们过着奢华的生活。
The banquet included a lavish spread of food. 宴会上有丰盛的食物。
Synonyms: luxurious, opulent, extravagant, profuse
Antonyms: meager, sparse, frugal, economical
词性: 形容词
