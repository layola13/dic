abetters:/əˈbɛtərz/| n.|教唆犯；煽动者|Someone who abets a crime or wrongdoing encourages or helps someone to commit it. |The police arrested not only the thief, but also his abetters. 警察不仅逮捕了小偷，还逮捕了他的同伙。

The abetters of the rebellion were imprisoned. 叛乱的煽动者被监禁了。|近义词：accomplices, accessories, collaborators|反义词: opponent, adversary|词性:名词

词源:
From Anglo-French *abeter, from Old French abeter "to bait, urge on (an animal), incite," from a- (from Latin ad-) "to" + beter "to bait," of uncertain origin.
