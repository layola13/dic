impound:/ɪmˈpaʊnd/| vt. n.|扣押，没收；拘留，收容；把…关起来|To seize and hold in legal custody; to confine within an enclosure.|The police impounded the car after the accident. 警方在事故后扣押了这辆车。|近义词：confiscate, seize, impound, confine|反义词:release, free|verb

impound:/ˈɪmpaʊnd/| n.|扣押，没收；拘留，收容|The act of impounding; a place where seized or stray animals are kept.|The stray dog was taken to the impound. 这只流浪狗被带到了收容所。|近义词：confiscation, seizure, confinement|反义词:release, freedom|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Anglo-French *empunder, from em- 'in' + punder 'to pen, enclose,' from Latin pondus 'weight.'  The original sense was "to enclose," later developing the legal sense of "seize and hold in custody."
