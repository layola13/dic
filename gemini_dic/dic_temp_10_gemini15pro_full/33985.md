citadels:/ˈsɪtədəlz/ /ˈsɪtədɛlz/
n.
城堡；堡垒；要塞;中心
a fortress, typically on high ground, protecting or dominating a city.  A stronghold.
The citadel overlooked the city. 这座城堡俯瞰着整个城市。

The rebels captured the citadel. 叛军占领了城堡。
stronghold, fortress, fort, castle, keep
---
词性
n. (noun)

近义词
fortress, stronghold, bastion, bulwark

反义词
(No direct antonym, perhaps "vulnerable point" or "weakness")

词源
From Old French *citadelle, from Italian cittadella "small town, little city," diminutive of città "city," from Latin civitatem (nominative civitas) "citizenship, community, state, city," from civis "citizen."
