gaudery: /ˈɡɔːdəri/ (US) /ˈɡɔːdəri/ (UK)| n. | 华丽而俗气的装饰; 俗丽的服饰;  炫耀; | Showy or excessively ornamented display, often considered tasteless.  | The carnival was a riot of color and gaudery. 嘉年华上充满了艳丽俗气的装饰。 The gaudery of the Las Vegas strip can be overwhelming. 拉斯维加斯大道的俗丽景象有时会让人难以承受。 | 近义词: finery, ostentation, showiness, flashiness | 反义词: simplicity, modesty, understatement | 词性: noun

词源:  From Middle English *gauderie, from Anglo-Norman gauderie, from gaudir (“to rejoice, enjoy oneself”), from Late Latin gaudēre, from Latin gaudēre (“to rejoice”), related to gaudium (“joy”).
