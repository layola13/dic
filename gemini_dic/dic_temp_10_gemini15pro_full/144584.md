outwatered:/ˌaʊtˈwɔːtərd/
verb
胜过，在水上运动中超过
To surpass or defeat in a water-based activity or competition.
The experienced sailor easily outwatered the novice in the regatta.  经验丰富的水手在帆船赛中轻松超过了新手。
The speedboat outwatered the jetski. 快艇在水上超过了水上摩托。
近义词: outswim, outrace, surpass, defeat
反义词: lose to, be defeated by
词性: verb
