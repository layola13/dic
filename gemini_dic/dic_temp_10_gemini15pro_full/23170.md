bow:/baʊ/ (US) /baʊ/ (UK)| n.|鞠躬; 船头|An act of bending the head or upper part of the body as a sign of respect or greeting. / The front part of a ship or boat. |He gave a deep bow. 他深深地鞠了一躬。|近义词：curtsy, nod|反义词：- |词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *boga, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch boog and German Bogen.  The verb sense "to bend" developed from the noun.
