decorative:/ˈdekərətɪv/| adj.|装饰性的，装潢的|Serving an aesthetic rather than a useful purpose; ornamental.|The decorative vase added a touch of elegance to the room. 装饰性的花瓶给房间增添了一丝优雅。|近义词：ornamental, embellishing, adorning|反义词：functional, utilitarian, plain|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: via Old French from late Latin decorativus, from decorare ‘to decorate’.
