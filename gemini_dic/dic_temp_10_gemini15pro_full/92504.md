hypothesis: /haɪˈpɒθəsɪs/ (British) /haɪˈpɑːθəsɪs/ (American)| n.| 假设，假说| A supposition or proposed explanation made on the basis of limited evidence as a starting point for further investigation.| Scientists are testing the hypothesis that the universe is expanding.| Synonyms: theory, supposition, assumption, conjecture, speculation| Antonyms: fact, proof, reality| noun
