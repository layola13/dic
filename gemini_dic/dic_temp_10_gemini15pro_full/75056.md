frighteners:/ˈfraɪtnərz/| n. |恐吓，惊吓，恐怖片|Things that are designed to frighten or scare, often not very seriously.  Usually used in the plural. |The movie was full of cheap frighteners like jump scares and creepy music. 这部电影充满了廉价的惊吓手段，比如突然的惊吓和令人毛骨悚然的音乐。

|近义词：scares, chills, terrors|反义词: comforts, reassurances|词性:noun (plural)

frightener:/ˈfraɪtnər/| n. |恐吓者，令人恐惧的事物|Something that frightens or scares. |The creaking stairs were a real frightener for the children. 吱吱作响的楼梯对孩子们来说真是个吓人的东西。|近义词：bogeyman, horror, terror|反义词: comfort, reassurance|词性:noun (singular)
