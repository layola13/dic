granularity:/ˌɡrænjuˈlærəti/| n. |粒度；颗粒性|The level of detail considered in a model or analysis.  The extent to which a system is broken down into small, manageable components.|例句：The granularity of the data allows for detailed analysis of customer behavior.  数据粒度允许对客户行为进行详细分析。

例句：We need to increase the granularity of our marketing efforts to target specific demographics. 我们需要提高营销工作的粒度，以针对特定的人口统计数据。|近义词：detail, precision, specificity, fineness|反义词：coarseness, generality, vagueness|词性:noun
