anglomaniac:/ˌæŋɡləˈmeɪniæk/ | n. |亲英者，崇英狂|A person who is obsessed with English culture, customs, and people, often to the point of excessive admiration or imitation. | He's such an anglomaniac; he insists on having afternoon tea every day and only drinks imported English tea. 他真是个亲英狂，坚持每天喝下午茶，而且只喝进口的英国茶。|Synonyms: Anglophile (milder connotation), Britishophile | Antonyms: Anglophobe | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Anglo- + -maniac. Anglo- comes from Latin Angli, referring to the Angles, one of the Germanic peoples who settled in Britain. -maniac is a suffix of Greek origin, meaning "obsessed with" or "excessively enthusiastic about."
