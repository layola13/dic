marine:/məˈriːn/ /mərˈiːn/| adj. n.| 海的；海产的；海军陆战队的；海运的；海员；海军陆战队士兵 |Relating to the sea; existing in or produced by the sea. A member of a body of troops trained for service at sea as well as on land.|例句：Marine life is fascinating. 海洋生物很迷人。|近义词：nautical, maritime, oceanic|反义词：terrestrial, land|词性：形容词/名词
marine:/məˈriːn/ /mərˈiːn/| n.|海运业|The business of transporting goods and people by sea.|例句：The marine industry is vital to global trade. 海运业对全球贸易至关重要。|近义词：shipping|反义词：-|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *marin*, from Latin *marinus* "of the sea," from *mare* "sea."
