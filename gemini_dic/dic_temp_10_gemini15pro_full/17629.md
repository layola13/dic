bebop:/ˈbiːbɒp/ (US) /ˈbiːbɒp/ (UK)| n. |比波普|A style of jazz characterized by fast tempos, complex harmonies, and virtuosic improvisation.|Charlie Parker was a master of bebop. 查理·帕克是比波普大师。|The band played a bebop number that got everyone on their feet.  乐队演奏了一首比波普乐曲，让每个人都站了起来。|近义词:jazz, swing|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "traditional jazz") |词性:noun
