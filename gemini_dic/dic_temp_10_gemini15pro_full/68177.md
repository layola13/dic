faintest:/ˈfeɪntɪst/| adj.|最微弱的，最模糊的，最轻微的|The faintest sound/smell/trace:  the smallest or weakest sound, smell, or trace that can be detected.  It is the superlative form of 'faint'.|I couldn't hear the faintest sound. 我听不到一丝声音。|Synonyms: slightest, weakest, dimmest, feeblest|Antonyms: strongest, clearest, loudest, brightest|adjective
