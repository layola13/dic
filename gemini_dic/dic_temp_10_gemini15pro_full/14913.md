azimuthally:/ˌæzɪˈmʌθəli/| adv.|方位角地，沿方位角地|Relating to or measured by an azimuth.  The error varies azimuthally. 这个误差随方位角变化。|The antenna rotates azimuthally. 天线方位角旋转。|circularly, radially| - |adverb
Etymology->
From azimuth + -ally.  Azimuth comes from  the Old French azimut, from Arabic السمت (as-samt, “the way, direction”).
