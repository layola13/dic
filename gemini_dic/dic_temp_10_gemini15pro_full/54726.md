disparadised:/dɪsˈpærədaɪzd/
adj.
失去乐园般的幸福的，被逐出乐园的
Deprived of paradise; made to lose blissful happiness or innocence.
The children felt utterly disparadised when they were told they couldn't go to the theme park. 孩子们被告知不能去主题公园时，感到完全失去了快乐。
近义词:fallen, ruined, damned, lost
反义词:blessed, blissful, happy, fortunate
词性:形容词

Etymology:
From dis- (expressing negation, reversal) + paradise + -ed (forming adjectives).  The prefix "dis-" reverses the meaning of "paradise," indicating a removal or loss of paradise.
