nature:/ˈneɪtʃər/ (US), /ˈneɪtʃə/ (UK)| n.|自然，性质，本性，种类|The fundamental qualities of a person or thing; inherent characteristics.  The physical world and everything in it (such as plants, animals, mountains, oceans, stars, etc.) that is not made by people.|例句：The nature of the problem is complex. 这个问题的本质很复杂。

例句：Spending time in nature helps me relax.  在大自然中度过时光能帮助我放松。|近义词：character, essence, world, universe, cosmos|反义词：nurture, artificiality|词性：noun
