calculating:/ˈkælkjuleɪtɪŋ/| adj.|计算的；精明的；深谋远虑的|Present participle of calculate.  Meaning:  performing or involving calculation; scheming and shrewd.|He had a calculating look in his eyes. 他眼神里流露出一丝精明.|近义词：scheming, cunning, shrewd, devious, manipulative|反义词：artless, guileless, ingenuous, naive|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin *calculatus*, past participle of calculare (“to reckon, compute”), from calculus (“pebble, small stone used in reckoning”).  Originally, calculations were performed using pebbles or stones.
