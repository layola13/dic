averring:/əˈvɜːrɪŋ/
verb
断言；坚称
present participle of aver
He averred that he was innocent of the crime. 他坚称自己没有犯罪。
Synonyms: assert, declare, affirm, maintain, state, claim
Antonyms: deny, refute, contradict
