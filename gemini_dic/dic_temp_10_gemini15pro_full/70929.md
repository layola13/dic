firebreaks:/ˈfaɪərbreɪks/| n.|防火隔离带，防火带|A barrier, such as a strip of plowed land, designed to check the spread of fire.|Firebreaks had been cut through the forest to prevent the spread of wildfires.  为了防止野火蔓延，森林里开辟了防火隔离带。|synonyms: fire line, firestop, fuel break|antonyms: fuel, accelerant|noun

firebreaks:/ˈfaɪərbreɪks/| n.|阻碍，障碍|Something that checks or hinders progress or spread.|The new regulations acted as a firebreak to the company's expansion plans. 新规定阻碍了公司的扩张计划。|synonyms: obstacle, barrier, impediment|antonyms: catalyst, facilitator|noun
