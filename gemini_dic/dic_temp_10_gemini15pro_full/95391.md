inching:/ˈɪntʃɪŋ/| v. |缓慢移动，一点点地移动|To move very slowly and carefully in a particular direction, or to make something do this.|The snail was inching its way up the wall.  蜗牛正沿着墙慢慢往上爬。|近义词：creeping, edging, nudging|反义词：rushing, speeding|词性:动词
词源: From inch (n.) + -ing.  源于名词 "inch"（英寸）加上动词后缀 "-ing"。
