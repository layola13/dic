coarbiter:/koʊˈɑːrbɪtər/| n.|共同仲裁人，联合仲裁员|A person who arbitrates jointly with another.|The two nations appointed a coarbiter to resolve the border dispute.  两个国家任命了一名共同仲裁人来解决边界争端。|Synonyms: joint arbitrator, co-referee|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "disputant" or "litigant")|Noun
