implores:/ɪmˈplɔːrz/ | v. |恳求；哀求；央求 | To beg someone earnestly or desperately to do something. |  He implored her to stay. 他恳求她留下。

The stranded traveler implored the passing driver for help.  这位滞留的旅行者恳求路过的司机帮忙。

| 近义词：beg, beseech, entreat, plead, supplicate | 反义词：demand, command, order | 词性: 动词
