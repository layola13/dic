hilliness:/ˈhɪlɪnəs/
noun
丘陵起伏；多丘陵
The state of being hilly; the prevalence of hills in a region.
The hilliness of the terrain made cycling difficult.  这片地形的丘陵起伏使得骑自行车很困难。
近义词：undulation, unevenness, bumpiness
反义词：flatness, levelness
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From hilly + -ness.  "Hilly" comes from "hill" (Old English "hyll"), meaning a naturally raised area of land,  plus the suffix "-y" which forms adjectives indicating having the quality of something.  "-ness" is a suffix that forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
