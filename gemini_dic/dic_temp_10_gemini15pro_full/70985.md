firestone:/ˈfaɪərˌstoʊn/| n.|火石，打火石，燧石；（Firestone）凡士通（轮胎品牌）|A hard piece of rock such as flint or metal used for striking fire. A piece of flint used to make sparks that set fire to tinder.|He struck the firestone to make a spark. 他敲击火石产生火花。|The Firestone tires on his truck were worn. 他卡车上的凡士通轮胎磨损了。|synonyms: flint, flintstone|antonyms: tinder|noun
