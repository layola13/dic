childlikeness:/ˈtʃaɪldˌlaɪknəs/| n.|孩子气，天真烂漫|The quality of being like a child, especially in innocence, simplicity, or naivete.  It refers to having or showing the qualities or characteristics of a child.|She retained a certain childlikeness in her old age. 她晚年仍保留着某种孩子气。|近义词：innocence, naivete, simplicity, artlessness|反义词：sophistication, worldliness, maturity|词性:noun
