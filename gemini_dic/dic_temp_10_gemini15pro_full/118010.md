memorizable:/ˈmɛmərəzəbəl/ (US), /ˈmɛmrəzəbl/ (UK)| adj.|容易记住的，值得纪念的|Able to be memorized; worth remembering.|This melody is so memorizable that I can't get it out of my head. 这首旋律太容易记住了，我总是忍不住想它。|The Gettysburg Address is a memorizable speech.  葛底斯堡演说是值得纪念的演讲。|近义词：unforgettable, memorable, catchy|反义词：forgettable, unmemorable|词性:形容词

词源:  From "memorize" + "-able". "Memorize" comes from Late Latin "memorizare", from Latin "memor" (mindful).
