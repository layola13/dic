fusions:/ˈfjuːʒənz/| n.|融合，熔合，聚变|The process or result of joining two or more things together to form a single entity.|The restaurant offers a fusion of Asian and European cuisine.  这家餐厅提供亚洲和欧洲菜肴的融合。|synonyms: blends, mixtures, combinations, amalgamations, syntheses|antonyms: separations, divisions, fissions|noun


fusions:/ˈfjuːʒənz/| n.|保险丝，熔断器（复数）|A safety device that protects an electrical circuit from excessive current.|Check the fusions to see if they've blown. 检查一下保险丝是否烧断了。|synonyms: fuses, circuit breakers|antonyms: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English fusion, from Late Latin fūsiō (“a pouring out, melting”), from Latin fundere, fūsus (“to pour, melt”).
