maceraters:/ˈmæsəˌreɪtərz/| n.|浸渍器，浸软器|Machines or substances that macerate something, typically organic material.  These are used to soften or break down substances by soaking them in liquid.|The maceraters were used to prepare the plant specimens for microscopic study. 浸渍器被用来准备植物标本用于显微镜研究。|Synonyms: softeners, dissolvers|Antonyms: hardeners, dryers|noun

macerate:/ˈmæsəˌreɪt/| v.|浸渍，浸软|To soften or break down (something, especially food) by soaking it in liquid; To make or become soft or separated into constituent parts by or as if by steeping in fluid.|The chef macerated the fruit in wine overnight. 厨师将水果在葡萄酒中浸泡了一夜。|Synonyms: soak, steep, soften|Antonyms: dry, harden, dehydrate|verb
