encapsulated:/ɪnˈkæpsjʊleɪtɪd/ | adj. | 被封装的，被包裹的 | Enclosed in a capsule or as if in a capsule; summarized.  | The drug is encapsulated in a tiny bead for slow release.  这种药物被封装在一个小珠子中以缓慢释放。|  The entire story was encapsulated in the headline. 整个故事被概括在标题中。 |  Synonyms: enclosed, encased, contained, summarized, condensed | Antonyms: released, exposed, liberated | adjective

encapsulate:/ɪnˈkæpsjʊleɪt/ | v. |  封装，概括 |  To enclose in or as if in a capsule; to express the essential features of (something) succinctly. | The seeds are encapsulated in a hard shell. 种子被包裹在一个坚硬的外壳里。 |  The film encapsulates the spirit of the times. 这部电影概括了时代精神。 | Synonyms: enclose, encase, contain, summarize, condense, epitomize | Antonyms: release, expose, liberate | verb
