overcondensate:/ˌoʊvərˌkɑːndenˈseɪt/
v.|过度冷凝|To condense excessively; to form more condensate than is normal or desirable.|The humid air overcondensed on the cold pipes, causing dripping.|近义词：condense excessively|反义词:undercondense|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "condensation" (from Latin "condensare," meaning "to make dense").
