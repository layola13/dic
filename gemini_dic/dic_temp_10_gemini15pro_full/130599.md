nonaddicted: /ˌnɑːnəˈdɪktɪd/ | adj. | 未成瘾的，非上瘾的 | Not addicted to a substance or activity; free from addiction. | He is nonaddicted to nicotine. 他没有尼古丁成瘾。|  She's nonaddicted to gambling, unlike her brother.  她不像她哥哥，她对赌博不上瘾。 | 近义词: drug-free, sober, clean | 反义词: addicted, dependent, hooked | 词性: 形容词

词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + addicted (past participle of "addict," from Latin addictus, meaning "assigned to, devoted to").
