exceptionalism:/ɪkˈsɛpʃənəlɪzəm/ /ɛkˈsɛpʃənəlɪzəm/| n. |例外主义；特殊主义|The belief that something is exceptional, especially a nation, and thus not subject to the same rules or standards as others.  认为某事物，尤指一个国家，是例外的，因此不受与其他事物相同的规则或标准的约束。|American exceptionalism is a widely debated topic. 美国例外主义是一个被广泛争论的话题。|The professor challenged the notion of British exceptionalism in his lecture.  教授在他的讲座中挑战了英国例外主义的概念。|近义词：uniqueness, peculiarity, singularity|反义词：normality, typicality|词性:noun
