pap's:/pæps/ | n. |  爸爸的食物;软食; |  Baby food or any soft, bland food, often associated with what a father might prepare.  Derived from "pap," meaning soft food.  The 's indicates possessive, meaning "belonging to or associated with papa/dad." |  The baby made a mess with his pap's.  婴儿把他的爸爸做的食物弄得一团糟。|  Baby food, mush, purée | Solid food | noun
