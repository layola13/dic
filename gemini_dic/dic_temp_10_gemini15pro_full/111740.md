locust:/ˈloʊkəst/ (US), /ˈləʊkəst/ (UK)| n.|蝗虫，蚱蜢；|A locust is a large insect that lives in hot countries and flies in large groups, destroying crops.|The locusts devoured the entire field of wheat. 蝗虫吞噬了整片麦田。|Synonyms: grasshopper, cicada|Antonyms: None|Noun
