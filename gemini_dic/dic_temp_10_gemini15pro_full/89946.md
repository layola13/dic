horsemen:/ˈhɔːrsmən/| n.|骑马者；骑兵|A person who rides a horse, especially a skilled rider.  A soldier who fights on horseback.|例句：The four horsemen of the apocalypse rode across the ravaged land. 天启四骑士骑马穿越了满目疮痍的土地。

例句：The horsemen charged into battle. 骑兵冲锋陷阵。|近义词：rider, equestrian, cavalryman|反义词:pedestrian|Noun
