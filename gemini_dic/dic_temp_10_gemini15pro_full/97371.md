infralapsarianism:/ˌɪnfrəlæpˈsɛəriənɪzəm/| n.|次堕落论|The doctrine that God decreed the election of some to salvation and the reprobation of others to damnation after the fall of Adam.|Infralapsarianism is a complex theological concept debated within Reformed circles. 次堕落论是改革宗内部争论的一个复杂的神学概念。|The debate between supralapsarianism and infralapsarianism has raged for centuries. 超堕落论和次堕落论之间的争论已经持续了几个世纪。|近义词:sublapsarianism|反义词:supralapsarianism|词性:noun
