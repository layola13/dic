crossbows:/ˈkrɒsbəʊz/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsbəʊz/ (US)| n. |弩，十字弓|A medieval weapon consisting of a bow fixed transversely on a stock, having a groove for the missile and a mechanism for drawing and releasing the string.|The hunter used crossbows to silently take down the deer. 猎人用十字弓无声地猎杀了鹿。|Near synonyms: bow and arrow, ballista|Antonym: firearm|Noun
