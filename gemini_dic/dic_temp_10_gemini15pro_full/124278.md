moth:/mɒθ/ (UK) /mɔːθ/ (US)| n. |蛾|A nocturnal insect related to the butterfly, typically having a stout body, drab-coloured wings, and feathery antennae.|The moth fluttered around the light. 蛾子在灯光周围飞舞。|Moths are attracted to light. 蛾子会被光吸引。|Near synonyms:  miller, hawkmoth|Antonym: butterfly|Noun
