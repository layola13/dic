lows:/loʊz/
noun
低点；低水平；低洼地带；牛叫声
a state or situation in which something is not as good as it should be or not as high in level as it should be, or the lowest level of something
The company's profits reached a new low this year. 该公司的利润今年达到了新的低点。
synonyms: minimum, bottom, nadir
antonyms: high, peak, maximum
n.

lows:/loʊz/
noun
牛的叫声 (尤指母牛)
the mooing sound made by cattle, especially cows
The lows of the cows echoed across the valley. 母牛的叫声在山谷中回荡。
synonyms: moo, bellow
antonyms: (No direct antonym for the sound itself)
n.
