lithophagy:/lɪˈθɒfədʒi/| n.|食石癖，舔石癖|The practice of eating stones, often to aid digestion or obtain minerals.|Lithophagy is observed in various animal species, including birds and reptiles.|近义词:geophagy|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
