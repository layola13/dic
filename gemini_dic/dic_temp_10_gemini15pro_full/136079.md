nonreciprocity:/ˌnɒnˌresɪˈprɒsɪti/ | n. | 非互惠性，单向性 | Lack of reciprocity; the state of not being reciprocal.  The condition in which a relationship or interaction is not mutual or balanced. |  Example: The nonreciprocity of the trade agreement led to an imbalance in imports and exports.  贸易协定的非互惠性导致进出口失衡。

Example: The nonreciprocity of their feelings caused a rift in their friendship. 他们的感情不对等导致了友谊的破裂。 | 近义词: asymmetry, imbalance, inequality, one-sidedness | 反义词: reciprocity, mutuality, balance | 词性: noun
