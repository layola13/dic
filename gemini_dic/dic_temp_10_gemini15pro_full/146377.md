overflorid:/ˌōvərˈflɒrɪd/ /ˌoʊvərˈflɔːrɪd/| adj.| 过分华丽的，辞藻过于华丽的| Excessively ornate or flowery in style or language; too elaborate or embellished. Characterized by an overabundance of elaborate or decorative elements, often to the point of being excessive or tasteless.|  Her writing style is sometimes considered overflorid, filled with unnecessary adjectives. 她的写作风格有时被认为过于华丽，充满了不必要的形容词。

The politician's overflorid speech failed to connect with the average voter.  这位政客辞藻过于华丽的演讲未能与普通选民产生共鸣。| 近义词：ornate, flamboyant, flowery, embellished, elaborate, ostentatious | 反义词：plain, simple, understated, austere, concise | 词性: adjective
