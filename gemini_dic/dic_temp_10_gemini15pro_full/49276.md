deliberation:/dɪˌlɪbəˈreɪʃn/| n.|深思熟虑，仔细考虑，审议|Careful consideration or discussion of something.|The jury reached its verdict after hours of deliberation. 陪审团经过数小时的审议后作出了裁决。|近义词：consideration, reflection, contemplation, discussion|反义词：impulse, recklessness|noun

deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbəreɪt/| adj.|故意的，蓄意的；慎重的，从容不迫的|Done consciously and intentionally; slow and unhurried.|The attack was a deliberate attempt to destabilize the government. 这次袭击是蓄意破坏政府稳定的企图。|近义词：intentional, planned, careful, unhurried|反义词：accidental, unintentional, hasty, rushed|adjective
