circumvent:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈvent/ /ˌsɝːkəmˈvent/| vt.|规避，绕过，回避|To find a way around (an obstacle or difficult situation).  To avoid something, especially cleverly or illegally.|例句：They circumvented the problem by using a different approach. 他们通过采用不同的方法规避了这个问题。

例句：The company tried to circumvent the trade embargo by shipping goods through a third country.  这家公司试图通过经由第三国发货来规避贸易禁运。


近义词：avoid, evade, bypass, dodge, sidestep|反义词:confront, face, meet|词性:动词
