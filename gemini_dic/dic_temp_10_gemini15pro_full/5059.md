all-rounder:/ˈɔːlˌraʊndər/ | n. | 全才；多面手；全能选手 | A person who is competent in many different fields or activities. |  He's a real all-rounder – he's good at sports, music, and academics. 他是一个真正的全才——体育、音乐和学习都很好。 | synonym: versatile person, jack-of-all-trades | antonym: specialist | noun
