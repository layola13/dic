monotheistic:/ˌmɒnəʊθiːˈɪstɪk/ /ˌmɑːnəθiːˈɪstɪk/
adjective
一神论的
Relating to or characterized by the belief that there is only one God.
Christianity, Islam, and Judaism are all monotheistic religions.  基督教、伊斯兰教和犹太教都是一神论宗教。
The ancient Egyptians were not a monotheistic society, worshipping a pantheon of gods. 古代埃及人不是一个一神论的社会，他们崇拜众神。
synonyms:  one-god, single-god
antonyms: polytheistic, pantheistic
词性: adjective
