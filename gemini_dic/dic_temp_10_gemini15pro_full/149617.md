painstaking:/ˈpeɪnsteɪkɪŋ/| adj.|辛苦的， painstakingly (adv.)|done with or employing great care and thoroughness.|例句：The restoration of the ancient painting was a painstaking process. 古画的修复是一个艰苦的过程。|例句：She completed the project with painstaking attention to detail. 她一丝不苟地完成了这个项目。|近义词：meticulous, careful, thorough, diligent, assiduous|反义词：careless, sloppy, negligent, hasty|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *panes taking* "taking of pains," present participle of *panes taken*, from *pane* "a division, section, part of something" + *taken*. The current sense emerged in the 18th century.
