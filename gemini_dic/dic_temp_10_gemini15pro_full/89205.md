homespun:/ˈhoʊmspʌn/ (US), /ˈhəʊmspʌn/ (UK)| adj. n.| 家织的，朴素的；家织布|  Simple and unsophisticated, made at home. Originally referring to cloth made at home, it now also describes style, language, or philosophy.|  She wore a homespun dress. 她穿着一件家织的连衣裙。| synonyms: homemade, rustic, simple, unrefined, plain | antonyms: sophisticated, elaborate, refined, elegant| adjective

homespun:/ˈhoʊmspʌn/ (US), /ˈhəʊmspʌn/ (UK)| n.| 家织布| Cloth made at home. | The homespun was rough but warm.  这块家织布很粗糙但很暖和。| synonyms: homespun cloth, handwoven cloth | antonyms: factory-made cloth, commercial fabric | noun
