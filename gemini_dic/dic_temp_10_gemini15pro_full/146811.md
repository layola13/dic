ETYMOLOGY->
over- (prefix): from Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubari, meaning "above, over, beyond."
immunize (verb): from Late Latin immunizāre, from immunis "exempt, free," from in- "not" + munis "performing public services, obligated."  The original sense referred to exemption from public duties, later extended to freedom from disease.
