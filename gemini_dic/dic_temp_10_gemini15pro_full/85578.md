hating:/ˈheɪtɪŋ/| v.|憎恨，厌恶|The act of feeling intense dislike or hostility towards someone or something.|She couldn't hide her hating of her ex-husband. 她无法掩饰对前夫的憎恨。|近义词：detesting, loathing, abhorring, despising|反义词：loving, adoring, liking|词性:动名词
Etymology: From Middle English *hatinge*, from Old English *hatian* (to hate), from Proto-Germanic *hatōną* (to hate), from Proto-Indo-European *kad-/*ked- (to sorrow, to be anxious).
