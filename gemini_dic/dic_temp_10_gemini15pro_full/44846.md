crustier:/ˈkrʌstiər/| adj.|更硬的，更脆的，更粗鲁的|Comparative form of crusty: more crusty.  Having a hard or brittle surface or texture, especially as a result of drying or baking. Also, can refer to a grumpy or irritable person.| The bread was crustier than usual today. 今天的面包比平时更硬。|近义词：harder, crispier, grumpier, more irritable|反义词:softer, soggier, friendlier, more agreeable|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From crust + -ier (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *crusta meaning "rind, shell".
