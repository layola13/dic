decoded:/diːˈkoʊdɪd/ | v. | 解码，译解 | To convert (a coded message) into intelligible language. |  The spy decoded the secret message. 间谍解码了秘密信息。 | deciphered, interpreted, translated | encoded, encrypted | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning 'reverse, remove') + code (from Latin 'codex', meaning 'tree trunk, book') + -ed (past participle suffix).  The word essentially means "removed from code".
