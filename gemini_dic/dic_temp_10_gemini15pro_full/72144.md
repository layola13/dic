floodplains:/ˈflʌdpleɪnz/ | n. | 洪泛平原，泛滥平原 | An area of low-lying ground adjacent to a river, formed mainly of river sediments and subject to flooding. | The floodplains of the Mississippi River are known for their fertile soil. 密西西比河的洪泛平原以其肥沃的土壤而闻名。|  flood plain, alluvial plain, bottomland, riparian area, river valley |  upland, highland | noun
