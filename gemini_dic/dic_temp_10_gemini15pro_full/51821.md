dewier:/ˈdjuːɪər/ | adj. | 更湿润的；露水更重的 | More dewy; having more dew. | The grass was dewier this morning than yesterday. 今早的草比昨天更湿润。| The leaves felt dewier after the rain. 雨后叶子感觉更湿润。 | 近义词：moister, damper, wetter | 反义词：drier | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From dew + -ier (comparative adjective suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *dawwaz, meaning "dew".
