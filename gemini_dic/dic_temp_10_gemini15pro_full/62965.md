ennobling:/ɪˈnoʊblɪŋ/
adjective
使人高尚的；使人崇高的
Making someone feel or seem worthy and good.
Her work with the charity was an ennobling experience. 她与慈善机构合作的经历让她变得高尚。
The act of forgiveness can be incredibly ennobling.  宽恕的行为可以令人无比崇高。
近义词:uplifting, inspiring, elevating, dignifying
反义词:degrading, debasing, lowering
词性:adjective

词源: From ennoble + -ing.  Ennoble comes from Old French ennoblir, from en- "make" + noble "noble."
