exposal:/ɪkˈspoʊzəl/ | n. | 暴露，揭露，陈述 | the act of making something public or known, or the state of being made public or known |  The exposal of the politician's corruption shocked the nation.  这位政治家的腐败行为被揭露，震惊了全国。 | 近义词：disclosure, revelation, unveiling | 反义词：concealment, suppression | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Late Latin *exposālis, from Latin expositus (past participle of exponere ‘set forth, explain’), from ex- ‘out’ + ponere ‘to place’.
