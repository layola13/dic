diversions:/daɪˈvɜːrʒənz/ /dɪˈvɝːʒənz/
| n. |转移, 分散注意力的事物, 娱乐, 消遣|something that takes your attention away from something else, especially something unpleasant; an activity you do for entertainment|例句：The city offers many diversions for tourists. 这座城市为游客提供了许多娱乐活动。

例句：Gardening is one of my favorite diversions. 园艺是我最喜欢的消遣之一。

|近义词： distractions, amusements, entertainments, pastimes, recreations|反义词： concentration, focus|词性：名词
