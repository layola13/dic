innominable:/ɪˈnɒmɪnəbəl/
adjective
不可名状的，难以形容的
Too terrible or shocking to be spoken about; unmentionable.
The innominable horrors of war. 战争中难以名状的恐怖。
近义词:unnameable, unspeakable, unutterable, ineffable
反义词:nameable, describable
词性:adjective
词源:Late Middle English: from late Latin innominabilis, from in- ‘not’ + nominabilis (from nominare ‘to name’).
