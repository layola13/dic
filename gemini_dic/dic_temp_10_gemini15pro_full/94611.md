impregnatable:/ɪmˈprɛɡnətəbəl/
adjective
可受孕的; 可浸渍的;易受影响的
Capable of being impregnated; able to conceive; susceptible to influence or permeation.
例句：The scientist experimented with various solutions to determine if the material was impregnatable.  科学家用各种溶液做实验，以确定这种材料是否可以浸渍。

例句：The queen bee, at the height of her fertility, is highly impregnatable.  蜂王在生育力最强的时候，很容易受孕。


近义词：fertile, receptive, permeable, susceptible
反义词：infertile, barren, impermeable, resistant
词性: adjective
