coprosperity:/ˌkoʊprɒˈspɛrəti/ /ˌkəʊprɒˈspɛrɪti/| n.|共同繁荣，共荣|A political and economic policy of purported mutual benefit, often involving exploitation or coercion by the dominant power.|Japan promoted a "Greater East Asia Co-Prosperity Sphere" during World War II. 日本在二战期间推行“大东亚共荣圈”。|近义词：collaboration, partnership, alliance|反义词：conflict, antagonism, opposition|词性:名词

Etymology:
From co- + prosperity.  "Co-" is a prefix meaning "together" or "jointly."  "Prosperity" comes from Latin "prosperitas," meaning "good fortune, success."
