harebrained:/ˈhɛərbreɪnd/| adj.|轻率的；愚蠢的；不切实际的|Lacking sense or judgment; foolish.  Characterized by reckless impulsiveness.|例句：That harebrained scheme of his is bound to fail. 他的那个轻率的计划注定要失败。

例句：Investing all your savings in that company was a harebrained decision. 把你所有的积蓄都投资在那家公司是一个愚蠢的决定。|近义词：foolish, reckless, rash, ill-conceived, ill-advised, crazy, impractical|反义词：sensible, wise, prudent, judicious, practical|词性
