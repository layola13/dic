carpet-bombing:/ˈkɑːrpɪt ˌbɒmɪŋ/ | n. |地毯式轰炸|The intense bombing of a large area, intended to destroy everything within that area.  It involves dropping a large number of bombs over an area, so that everything is hit. | The city was subjected to relentless carpet-bombing during the war.  这座城市在战争期间遭受了无情的地毯式轰炸。| saturation bombing, area bombing | precision bombing, targeted airstrike | noun
