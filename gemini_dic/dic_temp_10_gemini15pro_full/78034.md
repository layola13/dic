genuflect: /ˌdʒɛnjuːˈflɛkt/ | v. | 跪拜，屈膝 | To bend one or both knees to the ground, typically in worship or as a sign of respect. |  The worshippers genuflected before the altar. 信徒们在祭坛前跪拜。| synonyms: kneel, bow, stoop | antonyms: stand, rise | verb

genuflect: /ˌdʒɛnjuːˈflɛkt/ | v. | 跪拜，屈膝 | To show deference or servility. | He genuflected to the powerful CEO. 他向这位强大的CEO屈膝示好。 | synonyms: kowtow, grovel, fawn | antonyms: defy, resist | verb
