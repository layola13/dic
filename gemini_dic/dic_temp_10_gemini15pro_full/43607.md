creance:/ˈkriːəns/ | n. | 信任; 信心;  (尤指猎鹰对驯鹰人的) 信赖;  (驯鹰用的) 长绳 |A thin, long line attached to a hawk's leash allowing it limited freedom during training, building trust and preventing escape.| The falconer used a creance to train the young bird.  驯鹰人用长绳训练幼鸟。| 近义词: trust, confidence, faith, reliance | 反义词: distrust, suspicion, mistrust | Noun
