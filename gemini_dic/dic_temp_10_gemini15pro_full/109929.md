libelluloid:/ˌlɪbəˈluːlɔɪd/ | adj. n. | 蜻蜓状的；蜻蜓状物 | Resembling a dragonfly; a substance resembling celluloid. |  The artist crafted a libelluloid sculpture. 艺术家制作了一个蜻蜓状的雕塑。|  The new material was libelluloid and surprisingly strong.  这种新材料像蜻蜓的翅膀一样，而且非常坚固。| 近义词: dragonfly-like, gossamer | 反义词: opaque, heavy | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Latin *libellula* (dragonfly) + *-oid* (resembling).
