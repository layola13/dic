houseling:/ˈhaʊzəlɪŋ/| n.| 圣餐; 圣餐仪式;临终圣事|The act of receiving or administering Holy Communion.  The sacrament or rite of Holy Communion, especially as given to the dying. |He received houseling before he died. 他在去世前领了圣餐。|Synonyms: communion, Eucharist, sacrament|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider desecration, sacrilege in certain contexts)|noun
