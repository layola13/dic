comportment:/kəmˈpɔːtmənt/
n.
行为；举止；态度
the way in which a person behaves
例句：Her elegant comportment impressed everyone at the party. 她的优雅举止给派对上的每个人都留下了深刻的印象。

例句：His comportment during the crisis was admirable. 他在危机中的举止令人钦佩。

近义词：behavior, conduct, demeanor, bearing
反义词：misbehavior, misconduct

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *comportement*, from comporter "to bear, carry, behave," from Latin comportare "to carry or bring together," from com- "together" + portare "to carry."
