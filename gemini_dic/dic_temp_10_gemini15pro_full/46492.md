daiquiri:/ˈdaɪkɪriː/ /ˈdækəriː/
n.|代基里酒|A cocktail made with rum, lime or lemon juice, and sugar. | I ordered a daiquiri. 我点了一杯代基里酒。|近义词:cocktail, margarita|反义词:None|词性:名词
Etymology: From Daiquirí, a mining town near Santiago de Cuba.
