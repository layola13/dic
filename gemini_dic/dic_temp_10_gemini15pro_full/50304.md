depleted:/dɪˈpliːtɪd/
adjective
耗尽的，减少的
reduced in quantity or quality; emptied
The soil is depleted of nutrients. 土壤中的养分耗尽了。
近义词:exhausted, drained, diminished, reduced
反义词:replenished, full, abundant
词性:adjective

deplete:/dɪˈpliːt/
verb
耗尽，减少
to reduce something in size or amount, especially supplies of energy, money, etc.
The prolonged drought has severely depleted the region's water reserves. 长期干旱严重耗尽了该地区的储水量。
近义词:exhaust, drain, diminish, reduce
反义词:replenish, increase, augment
词性:verb

词源: From Late Latin *depletus, past participle of depletare “to empty,” from Latin de- “completely” + plere “to fill.”  The sense development in Latin went from "empty out" to "reduce, diminish."
