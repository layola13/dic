/dɪˈskraɪb/ | v. | 描述，形容 | To give a detailed account of something in words. |  She described the scene in vivid detail. 她生动地描述了那个场景。

The witness described the robber to the police. 目击者向警察描述了抢劫犯。


/dɪˈskraɪb/ | adj. |  (Not commonly used as an adjective on its own) | 描述的，形容的 | (Usually used in compound forms like "describable") The feeling is difficult to describe, almost indescribable. 这种感觉很难描述，几乎难以言表。

His behavior is describable as erratic. 他的行为可以说是反复无常的。



ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *describen*, from Old French *descrivre*, from Latin *dēscrībere* ("to write down, copy, transcribe, represent, delineate, sketch, portray, describe"), from *dē* ("down, thoroughly") + *scrībere* ("to write").


SYNONYMS->
portray, depict, illustrate, characterize, define, explain, relate, recount


ANTONYMS->
obscure, confuse, misrepresent, distort
