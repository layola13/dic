capercaillie:/ˌkæpərˈkeɪli/ | n. |西方松鸡|A large, black grouse, Tetrao urogallus, with a fan-shaped tail, found in coniferous forests of Europe and Asia. |  The capercaillie strutted through the forest, displaying its impressive tail feathers. 西方松鸡昂首阔步穿过森林，展示着它令人印象深刻的尾羽。

The capercaillie is the largest member of the grouse family. 西方松鸡是松鸡科中体型最大的成员。| 近义词:wood grouse, heather cock | 反义词: N/A | 词性:noun

Etymology: From the Scottish Gaelic *capall-coille* meaning "horse of the wood."
