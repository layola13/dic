crenelled:/ˈkrɛnəld/ (US), /ˈkrɛnɛld/ (UK)| adj.| 城垛状的，有雉堞的| Having battlements or notches, like the top of a castle wall.  A crenelled wall is one with regular gaps in it, like a castle wall.| The castle walls were high and crenelled. 城墙又高又有雉堞。|  The crenelled tower offered a commanding view of the valley.  雉堞塔楼可以俯瞰整个山谷。| synonyms: battlemented, embattled, notched, indented | antonyms: smooth, flat | word type: adjective
