objectivate: /əbˈdʒɛktɪveɪt/ /əbˈdʒɛktɪˌveɪt/
verb
使客观化; 使具体化; 物化; 将…视为客体
To treat a person as an object, especially sexually.
She felt objectified by the male gaze. 她觉得被男性的目光物化了。
近义词: dehumanize, depersonalize, reify
反义词: personalize, humanize, subjectivize
词性: verb

objectification: /əbˌdʒɛktɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
noun
物化; 客体化; 具体化
The action of degrading someone to the status of a mere object.
The objectification of women in advertising is a serious issue.  广告中女性的物化是一个严重的问题。
近义词: dehumanization, depersonalization
反义词: personalization, humanization
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin objectivatus, past participle of objectivare "to present as an object," from Latin objectus (see object (n.)).
