delitigation:/diːˌlɪtɪˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 消除诉讼，诉讼外解决 | The process of resolving a dispute without going to court.  The goal of mediation is often delitigation. | Example 1:  The company preferred delitigation to a lengthy and costly court battle. 公司更倾向于庭外和解，而不是旷日持久且代价高昂的法庭斗争。

Example 2:  Delitigation through arbitration can save both time and money. 通过仲裁消除诉讼可以节省时间和金钱。 | Synonyms: alternative dispute resolution (ADR), mediation, arbitration, negotiation, settlement | Antonyms: litigation, lawsuit, prosecution | noun
