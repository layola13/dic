astringer:/əˈstrɪndʒər/
noun
收敛剂; 止血药
a substance that shrinks tissues and checks flow of blood
The doctor applied an astringent to the wound. 医生在伤口上涂了收敛剂。

近义词:styptic, hemostatic
反义词:irritant

词性:noun
