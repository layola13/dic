bounteousness:/ˈbaʊntiəsnəs/
n.
慷慨；大方；丰富
The quality of being bounteous; generosity or liberality in giving.
The king showed his bounteousness by giving gifts to all his subjects. 国王向所有臣民赠送礼物，展现了他的慷慨。
Synonyms: generosity, liberality, munificence, abundance, bounty
Antonyms: stinginess, meanness, miserliness, scarcity, lack
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bountevousnes*, from bountevous + -nes.  Ultimately from Anglo-Norman and Old French *bounte* (goodness).
