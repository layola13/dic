forecastingly:/fɔːrˈkæstɪŋli/
adverb
预言地，预测地
in a way that relates to forecasting or predicting something.
He spoke forecastingly about the future of the economy. 他预言性地谈论了经济的未来。
Forecastingly, he suggested the market would improve next quarter. 他预测市场将在下个季度有所改善。
Synonym: predictively, prophetically
Antonym:  retrospectively, historically
词性: 副词
