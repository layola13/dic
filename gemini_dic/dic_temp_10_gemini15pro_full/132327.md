nondeflations:/ˌnɒndɪˈfleɪʃənz/ | n. | 非通货紧缩；不紧缩 |  The absence of deflation; a situation where prices are not falling. | The government's policies aimed at encouraging nondeflations to stimulate the economy. 政府的政策旨在鼓励非通货紧缩以刺激经济。|  The central bank's intervention prevented nondeflations, thus averting a potential recession. 中央银行的干预阻止了非通货紧缩，从而避免了潜在的经济衰退。 | Near synonyms: price stability, economic stability | Antonyms: deflations, deflationary pressures | noun
