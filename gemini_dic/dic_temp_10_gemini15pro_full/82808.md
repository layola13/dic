guised:/ɡaɪzd/
|adj.|伪装的，假扮的|having a false appearance; disguised as something else|He entered the country guised as a priest. 他扮成神父进入了这个国家。|Synonyms: camouflaged, masked, cloaked, concealed, veiled|Antonyms: revealed, exposed, unmasked, overt|adjective

Etymology:
From Old French *guiser "to fashion, arrange," from Frankish *wīsan "to have the appearance of, seem," from Proto-Germanic *wīsōną "to visit, stay, remain, be, exist."
