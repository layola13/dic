owlet:/ˈaʊlɪt/| n.|小猫头鹰|A young owl, especially one still covered in down.|例句：The owlet peeked out of the nest. 小猫头鹰从巢里探出头来。|例句：The photographer captured a beautiful picture of an owlet perched on a branch. 摄影师捕捉到了一张美丽的照片，一只小猫头鹰栖息在树枝上。|近义词：fledgling owl, baby owl|反义词:adult owl|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *owlete*, diminutive of *oule* (owl), from Old English *ūle*, from Proto-Germanic *uwwalǭ*, from Proto-Indo-European *h₂éwh₂los* (owl).  The "-et" suffix is a diminutive, indicating smallness.
