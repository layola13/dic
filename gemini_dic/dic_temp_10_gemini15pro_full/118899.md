metathoracic:/ˌmɛtəθəˈræsɪk/| adj.|后胸的|Relating to the metathorax, the middle segment of the thorax in insects, located between the prothorax and the mesothorax.|The metathoracic legs of the grasshopper are adapted for jumping. 蝗虫的后胸足适合跳跃。|Synonym: None specific to this anatomical term|Antonym: None specific to this anatomical term|adjective
metathorax:/ˌmɛtəˈθɔːræks/| n.|后胸|The middle segment of the thorax in insects, located between the prothorax and the mesothorax, and bearing the second pair of legs and the first pair of wings (in winged insects).|The metathorax of a dragonfly is powerful for flight. 蜻蜓的后胸强壮有力，适合飞行。|Synonym: None specific to this anatomical term|Antonym: None specific to this anatomical term|noun
