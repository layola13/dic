mores:/ˈmɔːreɪz/ | n. | 习俗，道德观念 |  The customs and conventions embodying the fundamental values of a group or society. |  Example: The mores of the Victorian era were very strict. 维多利亚时代的习俗非常严格。
Example:  Challenging traditional social mores can be difficult. 挑战传统的社会习俗是很困难的。| 近义词: customs, conventions, traditions, norms | 反义词: taboos (部分反义，禁忌通常违反习俗) | 词性: noun
