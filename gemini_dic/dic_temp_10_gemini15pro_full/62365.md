endive:/ˈɛndaɪv/ (US), /ˈɛndɪv/ (UK)|n.|菊苣，苦苣|A leafy vegetable with a slightly bitter taste, belonging to the chicory family. It can be curly or broad-leaved.|

* I added endive to the salad for a bit of bitterness.
我往沙拉里加了些菊苣，增添一些苦味。

* Endive is often used in French cuisine.
菊苣经常用于法国菜。
