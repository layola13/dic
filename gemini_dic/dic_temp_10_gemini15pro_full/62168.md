encoders:/ɪnˈkoʊdərz/| n. |编码器|A device or program that converts information from one format or code to another, often for the purposes of standardization, security, or compression.|The video encoders compressed the raw footage into a manageable file size. 视频编码器将原始素材压缩成可管理的文件大小。|Synonyms: coder, converter, translator|Antonyms: decoder|Noun
