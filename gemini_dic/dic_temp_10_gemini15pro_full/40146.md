contemptibilities:/kənˌtɛmptɪˈbɪlɪtiz/
noun
轻蔑；鄙视；可鄙之处
The state or quality of being contemptible; things deserving of contempt.
His contemptibilities were numerous, including his arrogance and dishonesty. 他的可鄙之处很多，包括他的傲慢和不诚实。
Synonyms: despicable acts, vileness, baseness, worthlessness
Antonyms: virtues, merits, admirable qualities
