imprudent:/ɪmˈpruːdnt/| adj.|不明智的，不谨慎的|Not showing care for the consequences of an action; rash.|例句：It was imprudent of him to invest all his money in one stock. 他把所有的钱都投资在一支股票上，这是不明智的。

例句：Her imprudent remarks caused a great deal of offense. 她轻率的言论引起了很大的反感。|近义词：unwise, rash, foolish, reckless, injudicious|反义词：wise, prudent, cautious, circumspect|词性:形容词
