fittednesses:/ˈfɪtɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
适合性，恰当性
The state or quality of being fitted or suitable.
The fittednesses of the various tools for the job were carefully assessed.  各种工具对这项工作的适用性都经过了仔细的评估。
suitability, appropriateness, aptness
unsuitability, inappropriateness, unaptness
noun
