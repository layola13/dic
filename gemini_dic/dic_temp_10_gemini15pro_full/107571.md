landman:/ˈlændmən/| n. |土地经纪人；土地测量员；（石油公司的）土地征用员|A person whose job is to negotiate the purchase of land, especially for oil or gas exploration and production. |例句：The landman secured the mineral rights for the company.  土地经纪人为公司获得了矿产权。

例句：The landman negotiated a lease agreement with the landowner. 土地经纪人与土地所有者协商了租赁协议。|近义词：surveyor, negotiator, agent|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
