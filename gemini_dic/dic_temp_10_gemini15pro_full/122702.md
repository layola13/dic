moghuls:/ˈmoʊɡʌlz/ | n. |  莫卧儿王朝的统治者；大亨，巨头 |  A member of the Muslim dynasty of Mongol origin founded by Babur, which ruled much of India from the 16th to the 19th century.  Also used figuratively to refer to a powerful or influential person, especially in business or industry. | The Moghuls built magnificent palaces and mosques. 莫卧儿王朝建造了宏伟的宫殿和清真寺。

The media moghuls control a large part of the news.  媒体大亨控制了很大一部分新闻。 | 近义词：magnates, tycoons, mogul, Mughal | 反义词:  commoner, nobody | 词性: 名词
