dual-purpose:/ˌduːəlˈpɜːrpəs/
adjective
双用途的，两用的
Serving two purposes; having a double use.
This sofa bed is dual-purpose, serving as both a comfortable sofa and a guest bed. 这款沙发床是两用的，既可以当舒适的沙发，也可以当客房床。
This building is dual-purpose, used for both residential and commercial purposes.  这栋楼是两用的，既可用于住宅也可用于商业。
synonyms: multipurpose, versatile, adaptable, flexible
antonyms: single-purpose, specialized, dedicated
词性: 形容词
