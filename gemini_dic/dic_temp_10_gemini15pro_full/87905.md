hieroglyph:/ˈhaɪərəˌɡlɪf/ | n. | 象形文字 | A picture or symbol used in hieroglyphic writing, representing a word, syllable, or sound.  It's a form of writing used in ancient Egypt and other cultures. | The Rosetta Stone helped scholars decipher Egyptian hieroglyphs.  罗塞塔石碑帮助学者们破译了埃及象形文字。

| Synonym: symbol, ideogram, pictogram | Antonym: alphabet (arguably) | noun


hieroglyphic:/ˌhaɪərəˈɡlɪfɪk/ | adj. | 象形文字的 | Relating to or written in hieroglyphs. | The walls of the tomb were covered in hieroglyphic inscriptions. 墓穴的墙壁上覆盖着象形文字的铭文。| Synonym: pictorial, symbolic | Antonym: alphabetic | adjective
