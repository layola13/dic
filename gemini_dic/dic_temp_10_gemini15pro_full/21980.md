bohemianism:/boʊˈhiːmiənɪzəm/
n.|波西米亚主义；放荡不羁的生活方式|The artist lived a life of bohemianism, rejecting conventional values and embracing creative freedom. 这位艺术家过着波西米亚式的生活，拒绝传统价值观，拥抱创作自由。|例句：She was drawn to the bohemianism of the artistic community. 她被艺术社群的波西米亚主义所吸引。

例句：He embraced bohemianism and traveled the world, living a carefree life. 他拥抱波西米亚主义，周游世界，过着无忧无虑的生活。


近义词：unconventionality, nonconformity, counterculture
反义词：conformity, conventionalism
词性: noun
