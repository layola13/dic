catholicizes:/kəˈθɒlɪsaɪzɪz/
verb
使…天主教化，使…普遍化
to convert to Catholicism; to make more general or universal.
The missionaries catholicized the indigenous people.  传教士们使土著居民皈依天主教。
The new curriculum catholicizes the learning experience, incorporating diverse perspectives. 新课程使学习体验普遍化，融合了不同的视角。
Synonyms: universalize, broaden, generalize
Antonyms: restrict, narrow, specialize
词性: 动词
