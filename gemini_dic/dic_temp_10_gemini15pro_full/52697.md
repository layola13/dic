digram:/ˈdaɪɡræm/| n.|图表，双字母组合|A diagram is a simplified drawing showing the components of something, often using abstract, geometric symbols rather than realistic pictures.  |The teacher drew a diagram of the human heart on the board. 老师在黑板上画了一张人体心脏的图表。|synonyms: chart, graph, illustration, figure|antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a visual representation like "diagram")|noun
digram:/ˈdaɪɡræm/| n.|双字母组合|A digram is a pair of characters used together to represent a single sound or phoneme. |'Th' is a common digram in English.  'th'是英语中常见的双字母组合。|synonyms:  digraph (often interchangeable), ligature|antonyms:  monogram (single letter), trigram (three letters)|noun
