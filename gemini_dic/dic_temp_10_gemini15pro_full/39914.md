constrained:/kənˈstreɪnd/ | adj. | 受约束的，拘束的，被迫的 | Limited or restricted; forced to act or behave in a certain way. |  例句：He felt constrained to agree. 他觉得不得不表示同意。

例句：The company is constrained by budget cuts. 公司受到预算削减的限制。

近义词：restricted, limited, confined, obliged, compelled | 反义词：free, unconstrained, liberated, unrestricted | 词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *constreynen*, from Old French *constreindre*, from Latin *constringere* (to bind tightly, compress, constrict, condense), from *com-* (together) + *stringere* (to draw tight).
