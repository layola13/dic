deathwatch: /ˈdɛθˌwɒtʃ/ (UK) /ˈdɛθˌwɑːtʃ/ (US) | n. |死亡守夜，临终关怀；蛀木虫| A watch kept over a person in the last moments of life;  A vigil kept beside a dying person. Also, a kind of beetle whose larvae bore into wood, and whose ticking sound was superstitiously thought to presage death. |例句：The family kept a deathwatch over their dying grandmother. 家人们为垂危的祖母守夜。


例句：The eerie ticking of the deathwatch beetle filled the old house.  令人毛骨悚然的蛀木虫滴答声充满了老房子。


近义词：vigil, watch |反义词:  N/A |词性：名词

deathwatch: /ˈdɛθˌwɒtʃ/ (UK) /ˈdɛθˌwɑːtʃ/ (US) | adj. |垂死的，临终的| Relating to or characteristic of the period just before death. |例句：The hospital room had a deathwatch atmosphere. 医院的房间里弥漫着临终的气氛。


例句：He gave a deathwatch confession to the priest. 他在临终前向神父忏悔。


近义词：dying, terminal |反义词:  living, thriving |词性：形容词
