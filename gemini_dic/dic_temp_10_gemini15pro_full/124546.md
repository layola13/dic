mountebankery:/ˈmaʊntɪˌbæŋkəri/
n.
江湖骗术；招摇撞骗
Deceptive, fraudulent, or boastful behavior designed to impress or cheat others.
His promises were nothing but mountebankery, designed to trick unsuspecting investors. 他的承诺只不过是江湖骗术，旨在欺骗毫无戒心的投资者。
近义词:charlatanism, quackery, fraud, deception, trickery
反义词:honesty, integrity, sincerity
词性:名词

Etymology:
From Italian *montimbanco*, from *montare in banco* "to mount a bench," referring to the platform used by street vendors and performers to attract crowds.
