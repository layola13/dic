impinge:/ɪmˈpɪndʒ/| v. | 撞击，冲击；侵犯，影响 | to have a strong and often negative effect on something or someone | The noise of the traffic impinged on my concentration. 交通噪音影响了我的注意力。| Synonyms: encroach, infringe, intrude, affect, influence | Antonyms: aid, assist, help, support | verb

impinge:/ɪmˈpɪndʒ/| v. | 撞击，冲击；侵犯，影响 | to strike or dash especially with a sharp collision |  The hail impinged on the roof. 冰雹砸在屋顶上。| Synonyms: collide, crash, hit, strike | Antonyms: miss, avoid, dodge | verb
