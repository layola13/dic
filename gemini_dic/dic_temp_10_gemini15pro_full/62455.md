endorsers:/ɪnˈdɔːrsərz/ | n. | 背书人，代言人 |  A person who publicly approves or supports someone or something, especially a product or service, often for financial gain.  | Many celebrities are endorsers for various brands. 许多名星都是各种品牌的代言人。 |  Nike uses famous athletes as endorsers for their products. 耐克聘请著名运动员为其产品代言。 | synonyms:  supporters, advocates, sponsors, promoters | antonyms: critics, opponents, detractors | noun
