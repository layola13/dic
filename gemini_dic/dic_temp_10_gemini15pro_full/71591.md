flasket:/ˈflɑːskɪt/ |n.|小烧瓶；小酒瓶|A flasket is a small flask. It is often used for carrying alcoholic drinks.|He took a swig from his flasket of whiskey. 他从他的威士忌小酒瓶里喝了一口。|flask, bottle, container|jug, beaker


词源：
From Middle English *flasket, *flasket, *flasket, from Old North French flasquet (“small bottle”), diminutive of flasche (“bottle”), from Late Latin flasca, flasco (“flask; bottle”).
