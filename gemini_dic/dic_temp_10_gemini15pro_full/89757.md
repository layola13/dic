hopscotch:/ˈhɑːpskɑːtʃ/ | n. vi.|跳房子游戏；跳房子|A children's game played by hopping into and over squares marked on the ground.|The children were playing hopscotch in the schoolyard. 孩子们在校园里玩跳房子。|近义词:jump rope, tag|反义词:None|noun
hopscotches:/ˈhɑːpskɑːtʃɪz/ | v. (third-person singular simple present hopscotches, present participle hopscotching, simple past and past participle hopscotched) |跳房子；快速地从一地到另一地|To move quickly from one place or thing to another.|She hopscotched from job to job, never staying in one place for long. 她从一个工作跳到另一个工作，从不在一个地方待太久。|近义词:skip, jump, hop|反义词:stay, remain|verb
