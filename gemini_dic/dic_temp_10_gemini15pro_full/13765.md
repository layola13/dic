audible:/ˈɔːdəbl/ (US) /ˈɔːdɪbl/ (UK)| adj.|听得见的，可听见的|Able to be heard.|例句：The sound was barely audible above the noise. 声音在噪音中几乎听不见。|例句：Her whisper was scarcely audible. 她的低语几乎听不见。|近义词：hearable, perceptible, discernible|反义词：inaudible, imperceptible|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *audibilis, from audīre (“to hear”).
