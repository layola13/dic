facet:/ˈfæsɪt/ | n. |方面，侧面，刻面 |One of the many sides of a cut gem. A particular aspect or feature of something. |The diamond had many facets. 这颗钻石有很多刻面。|  The book explored every facet of the issue. 这本书探讨了这个问题的方方面面。 | synonym: aspect, side, phase, angle | antonym: whole, entirety | noun

facet:/ˈfæsɪt/ | v. | 刻面 |To cut facets on. | The jeweler carefully faceted the gemstone. 珠宝匠小心翼翼地给宝石刻面。 | synonym: cut, shape, polish | antonym: smooth, round | verb
