oystering:/ˈɔɪstərɪŋ/
n.
牡蛎采集；牡蛎养殖
the activity of collecting oysters, especially as a job or for food
例句：Oystering is a traditional occupation in some coastal areas.  牡蛎采集在一些沿海地区是传统职业。

例句：He went oystering in the bay this morning. 他今天早上到海湾去采集牡蛎了。
近义词：shellfishing, clamming
反义词: None
词性：名词
