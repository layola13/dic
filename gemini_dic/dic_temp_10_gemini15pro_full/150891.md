paralexic:/ˌpærəˈlɛksɪk/| adj.|近义的，近义词的|Relating to or being a paralexia, a condition in which a person substitutes one word for another, often similar-sounding, word.|Due to his paralexic aphasia, he often substituted "cat" for "hat." 由于他的近义失语症，他经常用“猫”代替“帽子”。|近义词:semantic, synonymous|反义词:antonymous, unrelated|词性:形容词

Etymology:  From Greek para- (beside, alongside) + lexis (word, speech).
