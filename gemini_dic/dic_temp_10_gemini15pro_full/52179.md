diarrhea:/ˌdaɪəˈriːə/ (US) /ˌdaɪəˈrɪə/ (UK)| n. |腹泻|Frequent passage of loose, watery stools.|例句：He has been suffering from diarrhea for three days.  他腹泻三天了。

例句：The contaminated water caused diarrhea among the villagers. 受污染的水导致村民腹泻。|近义词：the runs, loose bowels, the squirts|反义词: constipation|词性: noun

diarrhoea (British English spelling):/ˌdaɪəˈrɪə/ | n. |腹泻|Frequent passage of loose, watery stools.|例句：The doctor diagnosed her with diarrhoea. 医生诊断她患有腹泻。


例句：She experienced diarrhoea after eating the street food.  她在吃了街边小吃后腹泻。|近义词：the runs, loose bowels, the squirts|反义词: constipation|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From Late Latin *diarrhoia, from Ancient Greek διάρροια (diárrhoia, “diarrhea”), from διά (diá, “through”) + ῥέω (rhéō, “to flow”).
