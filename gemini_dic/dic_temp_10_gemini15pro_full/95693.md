incondensibility:/ɪnkənˌdɛnsəˈbɪləti/
n.|不可压缩性，不可冷凝性|The incondensibility of the gas made it difficult to store. 这种气体的不可压缩性使其难以储存。|The incondensibility of helium at room temperature is a well-known property. 氦在室温下的不可冷凝性是一个众所周知的特性。|incompressibility, noncondensability|condensability, compressibility|noun
