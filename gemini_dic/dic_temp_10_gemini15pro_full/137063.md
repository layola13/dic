nonstrategical: /ˌnɒnstrəˈtiːdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 非战略的 | Not strategic; not pertaining to, characterized by, or based on strategy. |  The decision to close the small branch was purely nonstrategical, based solely on short-term cost savings.  这个关闭小分店的决定纯粹是非战略性的，仅仅基于短期成本节约。

The company's nonstrategical approach to marketing led to scattered efforts and poor results.  该公司非战略性的营销方法导致了分散的努力和糟糕的结果。| 近义词: nontactical, unplanned, aimless | 反义词: strategic, tactical, planned | 词性: adjective


词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "strategic" (from Greek "strategos" meaning "general") + "-al" (suffix forming adjectives)
