downstairses:/ˌdaʊnˈsteərzɪz/ | n. | 楼下（复数，非标准用法） |  A non-standard plural form of "downstairs," referring to multiple lower floors or areas within a building.  This usage is considered informal or dialectal and generally not recommended in formal writing. |  "She went to all the downstairses to check if the windows were locked." (Informal) | Near synonyms: lower floors, downstairs (singular) | Antonym: upstairs | noun


downstairs:/ˌdaʊnˈsteərz/ | adv. adj. n. | 楼下 |  To or on a lower floor of a building, especially the ground floor. Can function as an adverb, adjective, or noun.  | Example (adv.):  "He went downstairs to get the mail." 他下楼去取邮件了。

Example (adj.): "The downstairs bathroom is being renovated."  楼下的浴室正在装修。

Example (n.): "The downstairs is flooded." 楼下被淹了。 | Near synonyms: below, downstairs (adv./adj.), lower floor(s) (n.) | Antonym: upstairs | adverb, adjective, noun
