indisputability:/ˌɪndɪspjuːtəˈbɪləti/ |n.| 无可争辩性；不容置疑 |The indisputability of the evidence led to a swift conviction. 证据确凿，迅速定罪。|Synonyms: certainty, unquestionableness, irrefutability, incontestability|Antonyms: disputability, uncertainty, questionableness|noun

indisputable:/ˌɪndɪˈspjuːtəbl/ |adj.| 无可争辩的；不容置疑的|His talent is indisputable. 他的才华无可争辩。|Synonyms: undeniable, unquestionable, irrefutable, incontestable|Antonyms: disputable, questionable, debatable|adjective
