nappinesses:/ˈnæpinəsɪz/
noun
小睡; 打盹;  瞌睡(通常指复数形式，表示多次小睡或瞌睡的状态)
The state of being inclined to sleep, especially briefly and lightly; drowsiness.  Multiple instances of brief periods of sleep.
After a long day of travel, she experienced several nappinesses on the plane.  旅途劳顿了一天后，她在飞机上打了好几次盹。
Near Synonyms: drowsiness, sleepiness, doziness
Antonyms: wakefulness, alertness
Word Family: nappy (adj.), nap (n., v.)
