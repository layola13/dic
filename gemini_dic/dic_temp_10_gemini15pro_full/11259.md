archway:/ˈɑːrtʃweɪ/| n.|拱门，拱道|A passage or covered walk under an arch or series of arches.|We walked through the archway into the courtyard. 我们穿过拱门进入庭院。|Synonyms: arcade, passage, gateway|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "opening" or "entrance" in some contexts)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *archeway*, equivalent to *arch* + *-way*.
