nonductilities:/ˌnɒndʌkˈtɪlɪtiz/
n.
非延展性；非韧性；脆性
The lack of ductility; the inability of a material to be permanently deformed without fracturing.
The nonductilities of the metal caused it to crack under pressure.  这种金属的非延展性导致它在压力下破裂。
