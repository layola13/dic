argentiferous:/ˌɑːrdʒənˈtɪfərəs/
adjective
含银的；产银的
Containing silver; yielding silver.
The Comstock Lode was an argentiferous vein discovered in Nevada.  康斯托克矿脉是在内华达州发现的一条含银矿脉。
The mine is known for its argentiferous ores.  这个矿以其含银矿石而闻名。
Synonyms: silver-bearing, silver-yielding
Antonyms: barren (in the context of mineral resources)
词性: adjective
