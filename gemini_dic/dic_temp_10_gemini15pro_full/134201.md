nonincriminatory:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈkrɪmɪnətɔːri/
adjective
无罪的；不构成犯罪的；不致入罪的
Not incriminating; not tending to establish guilt.
The lawyer advised his client to provide only nonincriminatory information to the police.  律师建议他的客户只向警方提供不构成犯罪的信息。
He gave a nonincriminatory statement to the press. 他向媒体发表了一份无罪声明。

近义词:exculpatory, exonerating, absolving
反义词:incriminatory, inculpatory
