discipliners:/ˈdɪsəplɪnərz/
noun
训练者；惩戒者；执行纪律者
People who enforce discipline or rules, or who train others in a particular field.
The school discipliners were strict but fair. 学校的纪律执行者很严格但很公平。
Synonyms: trainers, instructors, enforcers, regulators
Antonyms: lawbreakers, rebels, offenders
n.

discipline:/ˈdɪsəplɪn/
noun, verb
纪律；学科；训练；惩罚
(n.) A controlled form of behavior; a branch of knowledge or learning. (v.) To train or develop by instruction and exercise; to punish or penalize.
(n.) He lacked the discipline to study regularly. 他缺乏规律学习的自律性。
(v.) The coach disciplined the team for their poor performance. 教练因球队表现不佳而惩罚他们。
Synonyms: (n.) order, control, training, subject; (v.) train, educate, punish, correct
Antonyms: (n.) chaos, disorder, neglect; (v.) praise, reward, neglect
