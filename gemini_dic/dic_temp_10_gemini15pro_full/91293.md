hydrolyses:/haɪˈdrɒləˌsiːz/
|n.| 水解（作用）|The chemical breakdown of a compound due to reaction with water.|The hydrolysis of starch produces glucose. 淀粉的水解产生葡萄糖。| 近义词：decomposition, degradation| 反义词：synthesis, condensation|noun

hydrolyses:/haɪˈdrɒləˌsaɪzɪz/
|v.|  水解|To cause a chemical breakdown of a compound due to reaction with water.|Enzymes hydrolyze proteins into amino acids. 酶将蛋白质水解成氨基酸。|近义词：decompose, degrade|反义词：synthesize, condense|verb
