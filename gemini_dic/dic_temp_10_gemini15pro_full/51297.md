detachment:/dɪˈtætʃmənt/| n.|分离，脱离；客观，超然；冷漠；分遣队|a state of being objective or emotionally removed|例句：He spoke with detachment about the accident. 他以一种超然的态度谈论了这起事故。

例句：She felt a sense of detachment from the world around her. 她感到与周围的世界格格不入。|近义词：separation, aloofness, indifference, objectivity|反义词：attachment, involvement, connection, subjectivity|词性:名词

detachment:/dɪˈtætʃmənt/| n.|分遣队|a group of soldiers, vehicles, etc., sent away from the main group on a special mission|例句：A detachment of soldiers was sent to guard the bridge. 一支分遣队被派去守卫桥梁。

例句：The detachment camped out in the woods. 分遣队在树林里露营。|近义词：unit, squad, troop|反义词:main body, whole|词性:名词
