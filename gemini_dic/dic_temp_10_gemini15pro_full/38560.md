concoct:/kənˈkɒkt/ (UK) /kənˈkɑːkt/ (US)| v.| 捏造，编造，调制 |To create or devise something, typically a story, excuse, or plan, often by combining various elements or ingredients.| She concocted a story about missing her train. 她编造了一个错过火车的谎言。|synonyms: devise, fabricate, invent, formulate, create, make up, dream up|antonyms: discover, find, reveal, expose|verb

concoction:/kənˈkɒkʃən/ (UK) /kənˈkɑːkʃən/ (US)| n.| 混合物，调合物，捏造的故事 |A mixture of various ingredients or elements, often unusual or strange; a story or plan that has been invented.| The drink was a strange concoction of fruit juices and spices.  这饮料是一种水果汁和香料的奇怪混合物。|synonyms: mixture, blend, compound, brew, combination, invention, fabrication|antonyms: component, element, ingredient, analysis, separation|noun
