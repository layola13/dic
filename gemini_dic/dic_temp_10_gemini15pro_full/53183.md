dipsetic:/dɪpˈsɛtɪk/
adjective
口渴的; 引起口渴的
causing or suffering from thirst.
例句：The salty food made me dipsetic.  这咸的食物让我口渴。

例句：The desert traveler had a dipsetic craving for water. 沙漠旅行者渴望喝水。

近义词：thirsty, parched
反义词：quenched, satiated
词性: adjective


dipsetic:/dɪpˈsɛtɪk/
noun
口渴的人; 引起口渴的东西
a thirsty person; something that causes thirst
例句：The marathon runner was a true dipsetic after the race. 马拉松运动员赛后非常口渴。

例句：Salty snacks are a common dipsetic. 咸味零食通常会让人口渴。

近义词：N/A (as a noun, it doesn't have direct synonyms)
反义词：N/A
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *dipsētikos*  (causing thirst), from *dipsa* (thirst).
