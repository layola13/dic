chewinesses:/ˈtʃuːɪnɪsɪz/
noun
耐嚼性；嚼劲；粘性
The quality of being chewy; the plural of chewiness.
The chewinesses of these candies are different. 这些糖果的嚼劲各不相同。
近义词: textures, consistencies
反义词: tenderness, softness
词性: Noun


Etymology:
From chewy + -ness + -es.  "Chewy" comes from "chew" (Old English *ceowan) + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives). "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, and "-es" is the plural suffix.
