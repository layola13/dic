floppiest:/ˈflɒpiɪst/ /ˈflɑːpiɪst/| adj.|最软的，最松软的|Superlative form of floppy: most floppy.  It describes something that is extremely limp, flexible, and lacking firmness.|例句：That is the floppiest hat I've ever seen. 那是我见过的最软的帽子。

例句：The rabbit's ears were the floppiest part of its body.  兔子的耳朵是它身体最松软的部位。|近义词：limpest, flabbiest, loosest|反义词：stiffest, firmest, rigidest|词性:形容词
Etymology: From *flop* (imitating the sound of something soft falling) + *-y* (adjective suffix) + *-est* (superlative suffix).
