batchers:/ˈbætʃərz/| n.|批处理人员，批量处理程序|People or systems that process things in batches.  Those who divide tasks into batches for efficiency.|The batchers processed hundreds of applications in a single run.  批处理人员一次性处理了数百个应用程序。|近义词：processors, handlers|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑: individual processors)|词性:noun
