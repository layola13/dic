overindustrialism:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈdʌstriəlɪzəm/ | n. | 过工业化 | Excessive industrialization, often to the detriment of society or the environment.  The state of having developed an excessive amount of industry, typically leading to negative consequences such as pollution, social inequality, and depletion of natural resources. | The overindustrialism of the region led to severe air pollution.  该地区的过度工业化导致严重的空气污染。 | Overindustrialization has caused irreparable damage to the ecosystem. 过度工业化对生态系统造成了无法弥补的损害。 |近义词：industrial excess, overdevelopment |反义词: underdevelopment, sustainable development | 词性: noun
