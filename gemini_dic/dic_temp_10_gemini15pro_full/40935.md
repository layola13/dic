coomb:/kuːm/ | n. | 谷；小谷；山谷；量器（等于四蒲式耳） | A deep, narrow valley, especially one enclosed on three sides. Also a dry measure of volume (equal to four bushels) varying from 128 to 256 quarts. |  The farmer harvested a coomb of wheat. 农民收获了一库姆的小麦。

The sheep grazed in the coomb. 羊群在山谷里吃草。 | 近义词：valley, dell, ravine | 反义词: peak, summit | 词性: 名词

coomb:/kuːm/ | n. | （车轮的）油槽；（尤指轴承的）油槽 |  A hollow or groove in which something, especially a bearing, rotates. | The mechanic greased the coomb of the wheel. 机械师给车轮的油槽上了油。

The coomb of the axle was worn down. 轴的油槽磨损了。| 近义词：groove, channel, hollow | 反义词: ridge, protrusion | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *cumb, from Proto-Germanic *kumbaz, from Proto-Indo-European *kemb- (“to bend, crook”). Cognate with Middle Dutch com, combe (“bowl, valley”), German Kumpf (“bowl, tub”).
