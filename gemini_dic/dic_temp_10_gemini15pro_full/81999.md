grinting:/ˈɡrɪntɪŋ/ | n. | 咬牙切齿；咯吱作响 | The sound of grinting teeth filled the air. | 牙齿咯吱作响的声音弥漫在空气中. | 近义词：gnashing, grinding | 反义词: silence | 词性:名词

grinting:/ˈɡrɪntɪŋ/ | adj. |  (指声音)刺耳的，磨擦的 | He made a grinting noise as he tried to move the heavy box. | 他试图移动沉重的箱子时发出刺耳的声音。 | 近义词： grating, rasping | 反义词: smooth, melodious | 词性:形容词


词源: From the verb "to grint," which is a variant of "grind."  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *grindaną.
