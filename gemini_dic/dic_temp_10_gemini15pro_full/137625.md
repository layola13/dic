nontumultuousnesses:/ˌnɒnˌtʌmʌlˈtʃuːəsnəsɪz/
|n.| 非骚乱状态；平静；宁静
|The state of being not tumultuous; lack of tumult or disturbance.  A period of prolonged nontumultuousness followed the revolution. |
|例句：The era was marked by a surprising nontumultuousness in politics.  这个时代以政治上的惊人平静为特征。

|例句：The nontumultuousness of the countryside was a welcome change after the chaos of the city.  乡村的宁静在经历了城市的喧嚣之后，令人感到欣慰。
|近义词：calm, peace, tranquility, quiet, stillness, serenity
|反义词：tumult, chaos, disturbance, unrest, turmoil
|词性：名词
