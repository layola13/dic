novitiate:/noʊˈvɪʃɪɪt/| n.|新手，见习期，见习僧|a person who has recently joined a religious order and is preparing to take vows; a period of training and preparation for a new role or profession|例句：The novitiate spent a year in silent contemplation.  见习僧花了一年时间进行静默沉思。

例句：The company offers a two-year novitiate for new employees.  公司为新员工提供两年的见习期。|近义词：beginner, trainee, apprentice, probationer|反义词：expert, veteran, master|词性:noun
