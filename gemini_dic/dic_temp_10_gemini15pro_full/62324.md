endeavouring:/ɪnˈdɛvərɪŋ/ | v. | 努力; 尽力; 尝试 |  Making an effort to do something; trying hard. |  She is endeavouring to improve her English. 她正在努力提高她的英语水平。| Synonyms: striving, attempting, trying, working hard | Antonyms: neglecting, ignoring, abandoning | Verb

endeavour:/ɪnˈdɛvər/ | n. | 努力; 尽力; 尝试 | An attempt to achieve a goal. | His endeavour to climb Mount Everest was successful. 他攀登珠峰的努力成功了。 | Synonyms: attempt, effort, try | Antonyms:  inaction, idleness | Noun
