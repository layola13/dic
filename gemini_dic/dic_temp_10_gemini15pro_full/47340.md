de-signs:/dɪˈzaɪnz/| n. |设计; 图样; 企图; (图案)花纹|  A plan or drawing produced to show the look and function or workings of a building, garment, or other object before it is built or made. |The architect showed us the designs for the new building. 建筑师向我们展示了新建筑的设计图。| synonyms: plans, blueprints, drawings, sketches, drafts, outlines, layouts, schemes, proposals | antonyms:  implementations, realities, products | noun

de-signs:/dɪˈzaɪnz/| v. |设计; 计划; 构思; 打算; 企图|  Do or plan (something) with a specific purpose in mind.| The program is designed to help students learn English. 这个程序旨在帮助学生学习英语。 | synonyms: plan, intend, aim, mean, propose, project, contemplate | antonyms: execute, implement | verb
