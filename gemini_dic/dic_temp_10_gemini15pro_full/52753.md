dilatability:/ˌdaɪˌleɪtəˈbɪləti/
n.
可扩张性，膨胀性
The dilatability of the pupil is a key indicator of neurological function. 瞳孔的扩张性是神经功能的关键指标。
The dilatability of the balloon allows it to inflate to a large size.  气球的可膨胀性使其能够膨胀到很大的尺寸。
expandability, stretchability, extensibility
incompressibility, rigidity
noun
