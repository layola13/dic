entropium:/ɛnˈtroʊpiəm/ | n. | 熵；(医)睑内翻 |The inward turning of the eyelid, typically the lower one. |  Entropium can cause eyelashes to rub against the eye, leading to irritation and discomfort. 睑内翻会导致睫毛摩擦眼睛，引起刺激和不适。| synonym: inturned eyelid | antonym: ectropium | noun
