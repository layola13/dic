deadeningly:/ˈdɛdnɪŋli/| adv.|沉闷地，麻木地，令人沮丧地|Making something dull, lifeless, or unresponsive; in a way that reduces or stifles sound, feeling, or activity. |The silence in the room was deadeningly oppressive. 房间里的寂静令人窒息。|近义词：numbingly, dully, oppressively, stiflingly|反义词: excitingly, stimulatingly, vibrantly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From "deadening" + "-ly". "Deadening" comes from the verb "to deaden",  which means to make less strong or intense.  The Old English root is  "dēad" meaning "dead".
