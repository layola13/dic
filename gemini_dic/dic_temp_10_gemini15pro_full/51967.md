diacoustics:/ˌdaɪəˈkuːstɪks/
n.
建筑声学;建筑物声学特性
The science of sound transmission and its effects within buildings or other enclosed spaces.
The Sydney Opera House is renowned for its excellent diacoustics. 悉尼歌剧院以其优异的建筑声学而闻名。
The lecture hall's diacoustics were poor, making it difficult to hear the speaker. 演讲厅的建筑声学很差，很难听清演讲者的声音。
synonyms: architectural acoustics, building acoustics
antonyms: N/A
noun
