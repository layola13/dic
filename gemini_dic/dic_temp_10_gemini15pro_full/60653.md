electioneering:/ɪˌlɛkʃəˈnɪərɪŋ/
n.
竞选活动，拉票活动
the activity of trying to persuade people to vote for a particular political party or candidate
The candidate engaged in extensive electioneering, traveling across the state and speaking at numerous rallies.  这位候选人进行了广泛的竞选活动，走遍全州，在无数集会上发表演讲。
campaigning, canvassing
boycott, abstention
noun
