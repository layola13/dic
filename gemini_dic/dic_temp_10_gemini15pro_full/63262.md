entertains:/ˌɛntərˈteɪnz/ | v. | 娱乐，招待，使有兴趣 |  provides amusement or enjoyment for (someone).  | The comedian entertains the audience with his jokes.  喜剧演员用他的笑话娱乐观众。 |  amuses, diverts, interests | bores, wearies | verb

entertains:/ˌɛntərˈteɪnz/ | v. | 怀有，持有（想法、希望等）| considers or holds (a thought, hope, etc.) in one's mind. | She entertained the idea of moving to the countryside. 她怀有搬到乡下的想法。| considers, harbors, holds | dismisses, rejects | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *entertainen, from Old French entretenir, from entre- (“between, among”) + tenir (“to hold”).
