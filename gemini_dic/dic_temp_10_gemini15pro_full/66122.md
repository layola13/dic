eccentric:/ɪkˈsɛntrɪk/ /ɛkˈsɛntrɪk/| adj. n.|古怪的，反常的；古怪的人|Deviating from the recognized or customary character, practice, etc.; irregular; peculiar; odd;  a person of odd or unconventional habits.|例句：His eccentric behavior amused everyone. 他的古怪行为逗乐了所有人。

例句：She's known for her eccentric fashion sense. 她以其古怪的时尚感而闻名。|近义词：odd, peculiar, strange, unusual, bizarre, unconventional, idiosyncratic|反义词：normal, conventional, ordinary, regular|词性:形容词,名词
