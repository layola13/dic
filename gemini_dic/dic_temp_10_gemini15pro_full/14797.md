awry:/əˈraɪ/
adjective
错误的，出错的；歪斜的
Away from the expected or proper direction; amiss; wrong.
The carefully laid plans had gone awry. 精心制定的计划出了差错。
近义词: amiss, wrong, askew, crooked
反义词: right, straight, correct
词性: adjective

awry:/əˈraɪ/
adverb
错误地，出错地；歪斜地
In a position that is turned or twisted toward one side; askew.
Her hat was awry. 她的帽子歪了。
近义词: amiss, wrong, askew, crooked
反义词: right, straight, correct
词性: adverb
