goiter:/ˈɡɔɪtər/ (US), /ˈɡɔɪtə(r)/ (UK)| n. | 甲状腺肿，甲状腺肿大| An enlargement of the thyroid gland, located in the neck, often caused by iodine deficiency. | Example 1:  The doctor diagnosed her with a goiter after noticing the swelling in her neck. 医生注意到她脖子肿胀后，诊断她患有甲状腺肿。

Example 2:  Iodine deficiency is a common cause of goiters, especially in developing countries. 碘缺乏是甲状腺肿的常见原因，尤其是在发展中国家。| 近义词: thyroid enlargement, struma | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a physical condition) | 词性: noun
