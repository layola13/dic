motorised: /ˈməʊtəˌraɪzd/ (UK) /ˈmoʊtəˌraɪzd/ (US)
|adj.|机动的；装有发动机的|Equipped with a motor or engine to provide movement or power.|例句：Motorised transport is increasingly common in developing countries.  机动交通在发展中国家越来越普遍。|近义词：mechanized, automated|反义词：manual, non-motorised|词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "motor" + "-ised". "Motor" comes from the Latin "movere," meaning "to move."  "-ised" is a suffix indicating "provided with" or "made into."
