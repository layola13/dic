drams:/dræmz/| n.|少量液体; 一小口（尤指酒）; 少量; 极少量|A small drink of liquid, especially alcohol; a small amount; a very small quantity.|He took a few drams of whisky to warm himself. 他喝了几小口威士忌暖暖身子。|近义词:drop, sip, bit, tad|反义词:lot, abundance|词性:名词

drams:/dræmz/| n.|常衡制药剂计量单位(等于1/8液盎司或60格令); 药剂计量单位|A unit of apothecaries' weight equal to 1/8 of a fluid ounce or 60 grains; a unit of apothecaries' measure.|The prescription called for three drams of the medicine.  处方要求服用三药剂计量单位的药。|近义词:unit, measure|反义词:N/A|词性:名词
