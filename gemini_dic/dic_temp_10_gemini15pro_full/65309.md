eudiometrical:/ˌjuːdiəˈmɛtrɪk(ə)l/
adjective
测气法的；用于气体分析的
Relating to or used in eudiometry (the measurement of the volume changes of gases during chemical reactions).
The eudiometrical analysis showed a high concentration of oxygen.  测气分析显示氧气浓度很高。
近义词:  gasometric
反义词:  N/A
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *eudia* "fair weather" + metron "measure"  + -ical (suffix forming adjectives).
