busiest:/ˈbɪziɪst/ | adj. | 最忙的 | Superlative form of "busy," meaning having the most activity or work. |  This is the busiest time of year for our company. 这是我们公司一年中最忙的时候。|  The busiest day of the week is usually Friday. 一周中最忙的一天通常是星期五。| Synonyms: most active, most hectic, most occupied | Antonyms: quietest, calmest, slowest | adjective
