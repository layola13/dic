gunfights: /ˈɡʌnˌfaɪts/
noun
枪战
An exchange of gunfire between individuals or groups, typically involving firearms.
The movie climaxed in a dramatic gunfight in the dusty streets.  这部电影的高潮是尘土飞扬的街道上的一场激烈的枪战。
shootout, firefight, battle
peace, truce
