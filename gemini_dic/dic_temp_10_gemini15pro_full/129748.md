non-evaluative:/ˌnɒnɪˈvæljuːətɪv/ /ˌnɑːnɪˈvæljuːeɪtɪv/| adj.|非评价性的，不带评价的|Not making judgments or expressing opinions about the value or worth of something; objective and descriptive.|The researcher adopted a non-evaluative approach, simply observing the children's behavior without making any judgments about right or wrong.|近义词：objective, descriptive, impartial, neutral|反义词：evaluative, judgmental, subjective, biased|词性:adjective

词源:  The word "non-evaluative" is formed by combining the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") with the word "evaluative."  "Evaluative" comes from the verb "evaluate," which ultimately derives from the French "évaluer" (to assess the value of).
