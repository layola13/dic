adduce:/əˈdjuːs/
verb
引用，举出（理由、证据等）
To cite or allege as evidence or proof.
The lawyer adduced evidence that proved his client's innocence. 律师提供了证据证明他的客户无罪。

She adduced several reasons for her resignation. 她列举了几个辞职的理由。

近义词: cite, quote, present, offer, advance
反义词: suppress, withhold, conceal
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adducere* "to lead to," from *ad-* "to" + *ducere* "to lead."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The prosecution adduces new evidence in the case. = 控方在此案中提出了新的证据。
He always adduces relevant examples to support his arguments. = 他总是引用相关的例子来支持他的论点。

一般过去时:
The witness adduced evidence that contradicted the defendant's testimony. = 证人提供的证据与被告的证词相矛盾。
They adduced several studies to prove their point. = 他们引用了几项研究来证明他们的观点。


现在完成时:
The researchers have adduced compelling evidence to support their theory. = 研究人员已经提供了令人信服的证据来支持他们的理论。
She has adduced several reasons why the project should be postponed. = 她列举了几个应该推迟该项目的理由。

过去完成时:
After the defense had adduced all their evidence, the jury began deliberations. = 在辩方提供所有证据后，陪审团开始审议。
He had adduced several arguments in favor of the proposal before it was rejected. = 在提议被否决之前，他曾提出过几个支持该提议的论点。


将来时:

虚拟语气:
