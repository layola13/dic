hemmel:/ˈhɛməl/| n.|天堂，天空，苍穹|A poetic or archaic term for heaven or the sky, suggesting a blissful or idyllic place.|例句：The lark ascended into the hemmel. 云雀飞上了天空。|近义词：heaven, sky, firmament, paradise|反义词:hell, earth|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *hemel, from Old English heofon, from Proto-Germanic *hebaną (“heaven”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱep- (“to grasp, contain”). Cognate with West Frisian himel (“heaven”), Dutch hemel (“heaven”), German Himmel (“heaven, sky”), Danish himmel (“heaven, sky”), Swedish himmel (“heaven, sky”).
