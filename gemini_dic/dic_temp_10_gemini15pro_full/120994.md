ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (wrongly) + class (to categorize). The prefix "mis-" comes from Old English "mis-", meaning "wrongly, badly, astray." "Class" comes from Old French "classe" and ultimately from Latin "classis," meaning "a division of the people, army, fleet, etc."
