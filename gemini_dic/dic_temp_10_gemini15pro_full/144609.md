outwent:/ˌaʊtˈwɛnt/
verb
超过，胜过
surpassed, exceeded
Her performance outwent all expectations. 她的表现超出了所有人的预期。
The athlete outwent his previous record by a full minute.  这位运动员比他之前的记录快了整整一分钟。
近义词: surpassed, exceeded, outdid, outstripped
反义词: lost to, fell short of
词性: verb (past tense of "outgo")

词源:
From Old English *ūtgan, from ūt ("out") + gan ("to go").

注意事项: "outwent" is the past tense of the verb "outgo." While "outgo" is less commonly used in modern English, its past tense form "outwent" is still encountered, especially in formal writing.  "Outdo" and "Outstrip" are more common synonyms in modern usage.
