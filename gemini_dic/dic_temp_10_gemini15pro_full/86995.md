hennish:/ˈhɛnɪʃ/| adj.|像母鸡的；胆怯的；婆婆妈妈的|Resembling a hen, especially in being timid, fussy, or overprotective.|例句：He became rather hennish after his wife died, constantly worrying about his children. 他妻子去世后变得有些婆婆妈妈，总是担心他的孩子们。|例句：She gave a hennish cluck of disapproval. 她发出一声像母鸡一样表示不赞成的咯咯声。|近义词：timid, fussy, overprotective, cowardly, apprehensive|反义词：bold, brave, courageous|词性:形容词
