avast:/əˈvɑːst/ /əˈvæst/ | int. |停！住手！|A nautical command meaning "stop" or "cease". It's derived from the Dutch word "houd vast" meaning "hold fast".  It is now rarely used outside of fictional or historical nautical settings.|Avast, ye scurvy dogs! 停下来，你们这群坏蛋！| Example: "Avast there, matey! Drop the anchor!" 停！伙计！抛锚！ | Synonym: stop, halt, cease | Antonym:  continue, proceed | interjection

avast:/əˈvɑːst/ /əˈvæst/ | n. | (Avast Software 的产品)杀毒软件 | Avast Software, a Czech multinational cybersecurity software company, produces antivirus software under the brand name "Avast". | I use Avast antivirus on my computer. 我的电脑上使用的是Avast杀毒软件。 | Example:  My Avast antivirus blocked a malicious website. 我的Avast杀毒软件阻止了一个恶意网站。| Synonym: antivirus, anti-malware | Antonym: virus, malware | noun
