iatrochemical:/ˌaɪətroʊˈkɛmɪkəl/
adjective
医化学的;医药化学的
relating to or denoting the early application of chemistry to medicine, especially the treatment of disease by chemical means.
Iatrochemical theories were popular in the 17th century.  医化学理论在17世纪很流行。
Paracelsus was a pioneer of iatrochemistry.  帕拉塞尔苏斯是医化学的先驱。
近义词:alchemical, spagyric
反义词: (No specific antonym)
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From iatro- (from Greek iatros 'physician') + chemical.
