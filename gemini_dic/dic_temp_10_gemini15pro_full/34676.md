clericals: /ˈklɛrɪkəlz/
noun
办公室职员; 牧师
office workers, especially in the Church
The church relies on the support of its clericals. 教会依靠其办公室职员的支持。

clericals: /ˈklɛrɪkəlz/
noun
牧师的服装
clerical clothing
He wore his clericals for the service. 他穿着牧师服装参加礼拜。

Etymology: From 'clerical' + '-s', ultimately from Latin 'clericus' meaning 'clergyman'.
