non-chronological:/ˌnɒn krɒnəˈlɒdʒɪkl/ /ˌnɑːn krɑːnəˈlɑːdʒɪkl/ | adj. |非按时间顺序的 | Not arranged in the order in which events happened. | The film uses a non-chronological narrative structure, jumping between different periods of the protagonist's life.  这部电影采用了非按时间顺序的叙事结构，在主人公人生的不同时期之间跳跃。|

The photographs were displayed in a non-chronological order, creating a sense of mystery.  这些照片以非时间顺序排列，营造出一种神秘感。 | non-sequential, anachronic | chronological, sequential | adjective


Etymology:
From non- + chronological.  Chronological comes from Late Latin chronologicus, from Greek khronologikos, from khronologos "reckoner of time," from khronos "time" + -logos "one who speaks or treats of (a certain subject)."
