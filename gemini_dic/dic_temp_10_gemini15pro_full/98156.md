insatiable:/ɪnˈseɪʃəbl/ | adj. | 无法满足的，贪得无厌的 |  (of a desire or appetite) impossible to satisfy. |  His insatiable curiosity led him to explore every corner of the library. 他永不满足的好奇心驱使他探索图书馆的每一个角落。 | synonyms: unquenchable, ravenous, voracious | antonyms: satisfied, content, satiated | adjective

insatiable:/ɪnˈseɪʃəbl/ | adj. |不知足的，贪婪的 |  Always wanting more of something; not able to be satisfied.  | She had an insatiable appetite for knowledge. 她对知识有着永不满足的渴望。 | synonyms: greedy, avaricious, rapacious | antonyms: moderate, temperate, abstemious | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin insatiabilis, from in- ‘not’ + satiabilis (from satiare ‘satisfy’).
