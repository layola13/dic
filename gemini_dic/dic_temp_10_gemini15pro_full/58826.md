duped:/djuːpt/ | v. | 欺骗，愚弄 | to deceive or trick someone |  He was duped into giving them his credit card details. 他被骗提供了信用卡信息。

The con man duped her out of her life savings. 骗子骗走了她所有的积蓄。

近义词: tricked, deceived, fooled, swindled, cheated | 反义词: enlighten, undeceive | 词性: 动词
