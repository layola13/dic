frailest:/ˈfreɪlɪst/
adjective
最虚弱的；最脆弱的
superlative adjective of frail: easily damaged or broken; weak
Her grandmother was now very frail. 她的祖母现在非常虚弱。

The frailest of the kittens didn't survive.  最虚弱的小猫没有活下来。

近义词:weakest, feeblest, most delicate
反义词:strongest, toughest, most robust
词性:adjective (形容词)
