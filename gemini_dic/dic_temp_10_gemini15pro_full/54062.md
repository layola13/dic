disdiaclast:/dɪsˈdaɪəˌklæst/ | n. |斜长石双晶|A type of twinning in plagioclase feldspars where the twinning axis is perpendicular to the composition plane.  A crystal twinned in this way. |The disdiaclast was clearly visible under the microscope. 在显微镜下，斜长石双晶清晰可见。| The rock sample exhibited a prominent disdiaclast.  岩石样本呈现出一个突出的斜长石双晶。|Near synonyms: twinned crystal, twin | Antonyms: single crystal | Noun
