disburden:/dɪsˈbɜːrdən/
verb
卸下负担；解除…的负担；吐露
to relieve (someone or something) of a burden
例句：
He was glad to disburden himself of the heavy suitcase.  他很高兴卸下了沉重的行李箱。

She disburdened herself of her worries to her best friend. 她向她最好的朋友倾诉了她的烦恼。

近义词：relieve, unburden, unload, free
反义词：burden, load, encumber
词性：v.
