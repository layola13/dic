melanosperm:/ˌmɛlənoʊˈspɜːrm/ | adj. | 黑孢子的，黑色种子的 | Having dark-colored spores or seeds. | Example: The melanosperm fungus is a common cause of plant disease.  黑孢子真菌是植物病害的常见原因。| Example:  Researchers identified the plant by its melanosperm characteristics. 研究人员通过其黑色种子的特征识别了这种植物。 | 近义词: dark-spored, black-seeded | 反义词: hyalospore (referring to colorless spores) | 词性: adjective

词源:
From Greek *mélas* (μελας) "black, dark" + *spérma* (σπερμα)  "seed"
