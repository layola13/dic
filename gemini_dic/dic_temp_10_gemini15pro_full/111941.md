lollingly:/ˈlɒlɪŋli/ | adv. | 懒洋洋地，悠闲地 | In a relaxed and lazy manner, often with the tongue hanging out. | The dog lay lollingly in the sun. 狗懒洋洋地躺在阳光下。|  The cat stretched lollingly on the sofa. 猫懒洋洋地在沙发上伸展身体。 | 近义词：lazily, idly, languidly | 反义词：energetically, actively, busily | 词性: 副词

词源: From the verb "loll" + "-ing" (present participle) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Loll" comes from Middle Dutch "lollen" meaning "to hang loosely".
