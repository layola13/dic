diffuseness:/dɪˈfjuːsnəs/ | n. |扩散；冗长；弥漫性|The state of being diffuse; the property of being spread out or dispersed; lack of conciseness. |The diffuseness of the argument made it difficult to follow.  这个论证的冗长使得它难以理解。|近义词：prolixity, verbosity, wordiness, rambling, lengthiness |反义词：conciseness, brevity, succinctness |词性: noun

diffuseness:/dɪˈfjuːsnəs/ | n. |弥漫性; 漫射; 扩散性|The state of being spread out or scattered; not concentrated. |The diffuseness of the light made it hard to see clearly. 光线的弥漫使得很难看清楚。|近义词：spread, dispersion, scattering |反义词：concentration, focus |词性: noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *diffūsus (“poured forth, spread out”), from Latin diffundō (“pour forth, spread out”).  Related to diffuse (adjective).
