hypervariable:/ˌhaɪpərˈveəriəbl/| adj.|高变的，高度可变的|Exhibiting a high degree of variability or mutation.  Referring to regions of DNA or protein sequences that are highly prone to change or variation.|The hypervariable region of the antibody allows it to bind to a wide range of antigens.|近义词：mutable, labile, polymorphic|反义词：stable, constant, invariable|词性:形容词
