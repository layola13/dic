frontiered:/ˈfrʌnˌtɪrd/ | adj. |有边界的，接壤的|Having a frontier or border; bordering. | The newly frontiered territory was rich in natural resources. 这片新划定的边界地区自然资源丰富。| The frontiered states often have disputes over land ownership. 这些接壤的州经常有土地所有权纠纷。 | 近义词: bordering, bounding, adjacent | 反义词: landlocked, interior | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "frontier" + "-ed". "Frontier" comes from Old French "frontiere" meaning "front, forehead, boundary," ultimately from Late Latin "fronteria" meaning "front part of the head."
