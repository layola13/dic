globalizing:/ˈɡloʊbəˌlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 全球化；使全球化 | The world is rapidly globalizing, connecting economies and cultures. 世界正在迅速全球化，连接着经济和文化。|  Multinational corporations are globalizing their operations. 跨国公司正在将其业务全球化。 | 近义词: internationalizing, universalizing | 反义词: localizing, isolating | 词性: 动词 (present participle)

globalize:/ˈɡloʊbəˌlaɪz/ | v. | 全球化；使全球化 |  Companies are trying to globalize their products.  公司正在努力将其产品全球化。| The internet has helped to globalize communication. 互联网帮助实现了全球化沟通。| 近义词: internationalize, universalize | 反义词: localize, isolate | 词性: 动词

词源: From globe + -ize, influenced by globalization. 源自 globe（地球）+ -ize（使...化），受 globalization（全球化）的影响。
