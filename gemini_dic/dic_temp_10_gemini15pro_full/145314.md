overbrilliantly: /ˌoʊvərˈbrɪliəntli/ | adv. | 过于精彩地，过分出色地 |  In a way that is excessively brilliant; too brilliantly.  It suggests an almost artificial or exaggerated level of brilliance. | The play was overbrilliantly lit, making the actors look washed out.  这出戏的灯光过于明亮，使演员看起来面色苍白。 | The soloist performed overbrilliantly, sacrificing emotional depth for technical virtuosity.  这位独奏家表演得过于炫技，为了技术上的精湛而牺牲了情感深度。 | 近义词: excessively brilliantly, too brilliantly, dazzlingly | 反义词: dully, dimly, poorly | 词性: 副词

overbrilliantly: 词源来自 over- (过度) + brilliantly (精彩地).  "Brilliantly"  itself comes from the French "brillant," ultimately from the Latin "beryllare" meaning "to shine like a beryl."
