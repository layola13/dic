nonproducer:/ˌnɒnprəˈdjuːsər/ /ˌnɑːnprəˈduːsər/
noun
非生产者；不生产者；不生产的东西
A person, animal, or plant that does not produce anything, especially something useful or necessary.
The factory had to lay off a number of nonproducers. 这家工厂不得不解雇一些非生产人员。
Synonyms: idler, drone, loafer
Antonyms: producer, maker, creator
词性: 名词
