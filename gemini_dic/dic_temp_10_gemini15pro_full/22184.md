bombinate:/ˈbɒmbɪneɪt/ /ˈbɑːmbɪneɪt/| v. |发出嗡嗡声，发出低沉的轰鸣声|To make a humming or droning sound, especially of insects.|The bees bombinated loudly in the hive. 蜜蜂在蜂巢里嗡嗡作响。|近义词：buzz, drone, hum, whirr|反义词: be silent, be quiet|词性: 动词

词源: From Latin *bombināre, frequentative of bombitāre (“to buzz, hum”), from bombus (“a deep, hollow sound,” “a buzzing, humming sound”).
