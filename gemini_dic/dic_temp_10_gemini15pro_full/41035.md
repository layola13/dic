copelessnesses:/ˈkoʊpləsnəsnɪsɪz/
n.
无能为力；无法应对；绝望
The state of being without cope, or the inability to cope; multiple instances of this state.  This word emphasizes a profound and pervasive lack of ability to manage or deal with difficulties.
Her copelessnesses in the face of her son's illness was heartbreaking.  面对儿子的病情，她的无能为力令人心碎。
Near synonyms: helplessness, powerlessness, despair, hopelessness
Antonyms: competence, capability, resourcefulness
