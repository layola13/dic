let the cat out of the bag:/lɛt ðə kæt aʊt əv ðə bæɡ/| idiom|泄露秘密，说出真相|To reveal a secret unintentionally.|例句：I was trying to keep the party a surprise, but my brother let the cat out of the bag. 我本来想让派对成为一个惊喜，但我弟弟泄露了秘密。

例句：She let the cat out of the bag about the surprise birthday party. 她不小心说漏了嘴，透露了惊喜生日派对的消息。|近义词：reveal a secret, disclose, spill the beans, give the game away|反义词：keep a secret, conceal, hide|词性：idiom
