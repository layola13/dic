inly:/ˈɪnli/
adjective
内部地，在内心里，秘密地
Existing or occurring within; internal.
She smiled inly, a secret pleasure warming her. 她内心笑了，一种秘密的快乐温暖着她。
近义词: inwardly, internally, secretly
反义词: outwardly, externally, openly
词性: adjective

词源:
From Middle English *inly, equivalent to in +‎ -ly. Cognate with Scots inlie (“inwardly”), German innig (“intimate, heartfelt”).  More at in, -ly.
