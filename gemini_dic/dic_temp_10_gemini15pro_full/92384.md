hypocrite:/ˈhɪpəkrɪt/| n.|伪君子，伪善者|A person who pretends to have virtues, moral or religious beliefs, principles, etc., that he or she does not actually possess, especially a person whose actions belie stated beliefs.|He's such a hypocrite – he preaches about the evils of smoking, yet he smokes a pack a day. 他真是个伪君子——他宣扬吸烟的危害，自己却一天抽一包。|Synonyms: dissembler, pretender, charlatan, phony|Antonyms: saint, righteous person|noun
