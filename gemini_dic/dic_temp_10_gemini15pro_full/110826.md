linoleum:/lɪˈnoʊliəm/ | n. | 油毡，油地毡 | A floor covering made from oxidized linseed oil, rosin, and ground cork or wood flour, pressed onto a burlap or canvas backing. |  We're going to replace the linoleum in the kitchen with tile. 我们打算把厨房的油毡换成瓷砖。

|The linoleum was worn and cracked in places. 油毡磨损了，有些地方还裂开了。 | 近义词: floor covering, flooring | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps carpet for a contrasting material) | Noun
