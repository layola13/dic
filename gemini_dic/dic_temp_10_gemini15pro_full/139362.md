obscurenesses:/əbˈskjʊənɪsɪz/
|n.|隐晦; 晦涩难懂; 费解; 模糊不清|The obscurenesses in his poetry make it difficult to understand. 他的诗歌晦涩难懂。|近义词：vaguenesses, ambiguities, complexities|反义词：clarities, simplicities|词性:名词

词源: From obscure + -ness + -es.  Ultimately from Latin obscurus.

用法说明:
Obscurenesses is the plural form of obscureness, referring to multiple instances of something being difficult to understand, perceive, or define. It's less commonly used than "obscurities".

例句：
The obscurenesses of the legal jargon made it hard for the jury to follow the proceedings.
法律术语的晦涩难懂使得陪审团难以理解审理过程。
