paintability:/ˌpeɪntəˈbɪləti/
n.
可涂性，适涂性
The quality of a surface that allows it to be easily and effectively painted.
The paintability of the wall was poor due to the dampness.  由于潮湿，墙壁的可涂性很差。
paintability of the surface 表面的可涂性
coatibility, applicability
non-paintability (非正式)
noun
