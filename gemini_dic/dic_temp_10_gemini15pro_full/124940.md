muffier: /ˈmʌfiər/ (US), /ˈmʌfɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更加闷热的，更加低沉的|More muffled; quieter or less distinct.| The sound became muffier as the door closed.  随着门关上，声音变得更加低沉。| The room felt muffier after the air conditioning was turned off.  空调关掉后，房间感觉更加闷热。|近义词: quieter, duller, indistinct, stifled|反义词: louder, clearer, distinct|词性:形容词

muffle: /ˈmʌfl/ (US), /ˈmʌfl/ (UK)| v. n.|使声音低沉，裹住，消音器|To make a sound quieter or less distinct; to wrap or cover for warmth or protection; a device used to deaden the sound of a gunshot or engine.|  He muffled his cough with his hand. 他用手捂住嘴咳嗽。 |  The heavy curtains muffled the street noise. 厚重的窗帘使街道的噪音减弱了。|近义词: mute, deaden, suppress, soften, smother|反义词: amplify, enhance, increase|词性:动词, 名词
