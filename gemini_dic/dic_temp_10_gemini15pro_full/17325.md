be-hard-on:/biː hɑːrd ɒn/ | phrasal verb | 对…苛刻，对…严厉 | to criticize or treat someone severely or unkindly |  例句：Don't be too hard on him; he's just a child.  别对他太苛刻；他只是个孩子。

例句：The coach was hard on the players after their loss. 教练在他们输球后对球员们很严厉。

近义词：criticize severely, be tough on, be strict with | 反义词：be lenient with, be easy on, go easy on | 词性：动词短语
