begohm:/ˈbeɪɡoʊm/ |n.| 贝戈欧姆（电阻单位，等于一万亿欧姆） |A unit of electrical resistance equal to one quadrillion (10^15) ohms. |The resistance of the insulator was measured in begohms.  绝缘体的电阻是用贝戈欧姆来测量的。|The cable's insulation had a resistance of several begohms.  这根电缆的绝缘电阻有几个贝戈欧姆。|近义词: teraohm (although smaller) |反义词:  (No direct antonym for a unit of measurement, but conceptually:  mho/siemens - the unit of conductance)|词性:noun
