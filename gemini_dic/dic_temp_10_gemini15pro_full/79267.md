glasswort:/ˈɡlæsˌwɜːrt/ (US), /ˈɡlɑːsˌwɜːt/ (UK)| n. |碱蓬|A halophytic plant found in salt marshes or other saline habitats. It has fleshy, jointed stems and is often used as a vegetable or in glassmaking.|Glasswort is a common sight in coastal salt marshes. 碱蓬在沿海盐沼中很常见。|In the past, glasswort ash was used in the production of soda ash for glassmaking. 过去，碱蓬灰被用于生产制造玻璃用的苏打灰。|Synonyms: samphire, sea asparagus|Antonyms: N/A|noun
