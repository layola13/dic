gilt:/ɡɪlt/ | n. | 镀金；金色；金边证券（gilts）| A thin layer of gold or something resembling gold applied to a surface; gold leaf.  Also refers to British government bonds, often called gilt-edged securities. | The gilt on the picture frame was flaking off. 画框上的镀金正在脱落。| 近义词：gold leaf, gilding | 反义词：N/A | 词性：名词


Etymology: From Middle English *gilten, *gylten, from Old English *gyltan, from Proto-Germanic *gultijaną (“to gild”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰel- (“yellow, green, bright”). Cognate with Dutch vergulden (“to gild”), German vergolden (“to gild”).
