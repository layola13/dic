bakes:/beɪks/ | v. | 烘烤; 烧制; 烘干; 晒干 |  cooks food by dry heat in an oven. | She bakes bread every weekend. 她每个周末都烤面包。| bake, cook, roast | boil, fry | verb


bakes:/beɪks/ | n. | 烘烤食品(通常指糕点) | baked goods, especially sweet baked goods | The bake sale was a success. 烤点义卖很成功。 | pastries, cakes | -- | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *bacan (attested only in compounds), from Proto-Germanic *bakaną, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰeh₂g-  "to bake, dry, roast". Cognate with Old Norse baka ("to bake"), Old High German bahhan ("to bake"), German backen ("to bake"), Dutch bakken ("to bake"), Gothic 𐌱𐌰𐌺𐌰𐌽 (bakan).
