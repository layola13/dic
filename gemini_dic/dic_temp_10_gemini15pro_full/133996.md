nonhomes:/ˈnɒnˌhəʊmz/ (US) /ˈnɑːnˌhoʊmz/| n. |非家，无家可归者的住所| Places that are not conventional homes, often referring to temporary or inadequate shelters for the homeless.  It emphasizes the lack of the qualities associated with a "home," such as security, stability, and privacy.|The city is trying to address the issue of nonhomes for the homeless population. 这个城市正在努力解决无家可归者的非家问题。|Synonyms: shelters, temporary housing, makeshift accommodations |Antonyms: homes, residences, dwellings|noun
