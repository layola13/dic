adulteries: /əˈdʌlt(ə)riz/
n.
通奸行为，婚外情
Voluntary sexual intercourse between a married person and a person who is not their spouse.
The tabloids were filled with stories of the politician's adulteries. 小报上充斥着这位政客通奸的故事。
His adulteries eventually led to the breakdown of his marriage. 他的婚外情最终导致了他的婚姻破裂。

近义词: infidelity, unfaithfulness, affair(s), cheating
反义词: fidelity, faithfulness
词性: 名词 (plural noun)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *avouterie*, from *avouterer* "to commit adultery," from Latin *adulterare* "to corrupt, pollute, falsify, commit adultery," from *ad* "to" + *alter* "other."  The sense shift from "corrupt" to "commit adultery" likely arose from the idea of corrupting the marriage bond.
