bardships: /ˈbɑːrdʃɪps/
n.
艰难困苦; 艰辛
Extreme hardship and suffering.

The early settlers faced many bardships, including hunger, disease, and hostile natives.
早期的定居者面临许多艰难困苦，包括饥饿、疾病和敌对的土著。

They endured incredible bardships during their journey across the mountains.
他们在穿越山脉的旅程中忍受了难以置信的艰辛。

Synonym: hardship, adversity, tribulation, affliction, suffering, ordeal, trial, trouble, difficulty
近义词：艰难、逆境、苦难、折磨、痛苦、考验、磨难、麻烦、困难

Antonym: comfort, ease, luxury, prosperity
反义词：舒适、安逸、奢华、繁荣

词性: noun (名词)
