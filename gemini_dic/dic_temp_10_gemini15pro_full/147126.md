overlocker:/ˈəʊvəlɒkə(r)/ (UK) /ˈoʊvərˌlɑːkər/ (US)| n.|锁边机，包缝机|A sewing machine that trims and stitches over the edge of one or more pieces of cloth to prevent raveling.|She used an overlocker to finish the seams of the dress. 她用锁边机缝制了连衣裙的缝边。|近义词: serger|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "conventional sewing machine" in some contexts)|noun

overlock:/ˈəʊvəlɒk/ (UK) /ˈoʊvərˌlɑːk/ (US)| v.|锁边，包缝|To sew over the edge of a piece of cloth with an overlocker to prevent it from fraying.|I need to overlock the edges of this fabric. 我需要锁住这块布料的边缘。|近义词: serge, whipstitch|反义词: (No direct antonym)|verb
