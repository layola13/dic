bryonin:/ˈbraɪəˌnɪn/| n.| 葫芦素; 毒性蛋白|A toxic protein, found in plants of the gourd family (Cucurbitaceae), such as bryony.|Bryonin can cause severe vomiting and diarrhea if ingested.  如果摄入葫芦素会导致严重的呕吐和腹泻。|Synonyms: cucurbitacin, toxin|Antonyms: antidote, remedy|noun
