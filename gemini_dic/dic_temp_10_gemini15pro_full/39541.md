consanguined:/ˌkɒnˈsæŋɡwɪnd/ /ˌkɑːnˈsæŋɡwɪnd/| adj.|血亲的，同血统的|Related by blood; descended from the same ancestor.|例句：They were consanguined, sharing a great-grandfather. 他们是血亲，拥有同一个曾祖父。

例句：Marriage between consanguined cousins is discouraged in many cultures.  许多文化都不鼓励近亲表兄妹结婚。

近义词：related, kindred, cognate|反义词:unrelated|词性:形容词
