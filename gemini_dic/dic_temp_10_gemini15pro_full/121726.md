ETYMOLOGY->
mis- (prefix):  From Old English *miss-, from Proto-Germanic *missa-, meaning "wrongly," "badly," "astray."
programmed (past participle of program): From program (n.), ultimately from Greek programma "a public notice," from pro- "forth" + graphein "to write."
