hyperconfident:/ˌhaɪpərˈkɒnfɪdənt/ | adj. | 过于自信的，自负的 | Excessively confident; showing too much confidence.  Characterized by excessive confidence, often to the point of arrogance. | He was hyperconfident, bordering on arrogant, in his presentation. 他在演讲中表现得过于自信，近乎傲慢。|  He strode into the room, hyperconfident that he would ace the interview. 他大步走进房间，过于自信地认为自己能顺利通过面试。 | Synonyms: overconfident, cocky, arrogant, presumptuous | Antonyms: insecure, diffident, hesitant, self-effacing | adjective
