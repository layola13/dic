nonavoidable:/ˌnɒnəˈvɔɪdəbl/ | adj. |不可避免的| Not able to be avoided; inevitable. |例句：The consequences of his actions were nonavoidable. 他的行为的后果是不可避免的。|例句：A nonavoidable delay occurred due to the storm. 由于暴风雨，发生了不可避免的延误。|近义词：inevitable, unavoidable, inescapable|反义词：avoidable, preventable|词性:adjective

Etymology: non- (prefix meaning "not") + avoidable (from avoid + -able)
