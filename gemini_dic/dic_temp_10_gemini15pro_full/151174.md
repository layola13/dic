parcelize:/ˈpɑːrsəlaɪz/| v. |打包;  (Android) 将对象分解成可打包形式|To divide into portions or parcels; In Android development, to decompose an object into a form that can be passed between activities or fragments. |例句：The app uses parcelize to pass complex data between activities.  这个应用程序使用 parcelize 在活动之间传递复杂数据。

例句：The developer needed to parcelize the custom object to send it through an intent. 开发人员需要打包自定义对象以通过意图发送。|近义词：serialize, marshal, bundle|反义词:  unparcel, deserialize|词性:动词
