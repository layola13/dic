denigrative: /dɪˈnɪɡrətɪv/ | adj. | 贬低的，诋毁的 | Showing a critical or disrespectful attitude; tending to belittle or diminish the importance of something. |  His denigrative remarks about her work made her feel discouraged. 他对她工作的贬低性评论让她感到沮丧。|  The article was full of denigrative comments about the politician. 这篇文章充满了对这位政治家的贬损性评论。|  derogatory, disparaging, belittling, insulting |  complimentary, laudatory, praising


词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *denigrativus, from Latin denigrare (“to blacken”), from de- (“down, completely”) + nigrare (“to blacken”), from niger (“black”).
