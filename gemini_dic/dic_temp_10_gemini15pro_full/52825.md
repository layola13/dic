dilute:/daɪˈluːt/ /dɪˈluːt/
verb
稀释，冲淡
to make a liquid weaker by adding water or another liquid to it
The paint can be diluted with water to make it go further.  油漆可以用水稀释，使其用量更广。
thin, weaken
concentrate, strengthen
v.

dilute:/daɪˈluːt/ /dɪˈluːt/
adjective
稀释的，冲淡的
(of a liquid) made weaker by adding water or another solvent
a dilute solution of bleach  稀释的漂白剂溶液
thin, weak, watered-down
concentrated, strong
adj.
