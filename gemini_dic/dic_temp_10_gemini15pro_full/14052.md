austerity:/ɔːˈsterəti/| n.| 紧缩；朴素；严厉；苦行 |A state of reduced spending and increased saving, typically enforced by governments.|  The government has introduced a new era of austerity. 政府已开启了新的紧缩时代。|  The wartime austerity meant that many families went without basic necessities. 战时的紧缩政策意味着许多家庭连基本生活必需品都无法满足。|  frugality, severity, rigor, asceticism |  luxury, extravagance, lavishness | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *austerite*, from Latin austeritas ("severity, strictness, harshness"), from austerus ("severe, strict, harsh").
