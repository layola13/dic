adenous:/ˈædɪnəs/
adjective
腺的；腺状的
Relating to glands, or resembling a gland in appearance.
The adenous tissue was inflamed.  腺体组织发炎了。
The biopsy revealed adenous structures. 活检显示腺体结构。
近义词:glandular
反义词:aglandular
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *adénieux, from Latin aden, gland + -ous.
