demonstrablenesses:/dɪˌmɒnstrəbləlnɪsɪz/ /dɪˌmɑːnstrəbləlnɪsɪz/
noun
可论证性；可证明性；可示范性
The quality or state of being demonstrable; demonstrability.  The fact or condition of being demonstrable. The multiple instances or ways in which something can be demonstrably proven.
His argument lacked demonstrablenesses, making it unconvincing. 他的论点缺乏可证明性，让人难以信服。
The demonstrablenesses of the scientific theory were rigorously tested.  这个科学理论的可论证性经过了严格的测试。
Synonym: demonstrability, provability, verifiability
Antonym:  unprovability, unverifiability
词性: noun
