overvulcanize: /ˌōvərˈvʌlkəˌnīz/ | v. | 过硫化，过度硫化 | To vulcanize (rubber) for too long a time or at too high a temperature, making it hard and brittle. | The tires were overvulcanized, making them prone to cracking. 轮胎过度硫化，容易开裂。 | overcure | undercure | verb

overvulcanized: /ˌōvərˈvʌlkəˌnīzd/ | adj. | 过硫化的，过度硫化的 |  Having been vulcanized for too long or at too high a temperature. | The overvulcanized rubber lost its elasticity. 过度硫化的橡胶失去了弹性。 | overcured | undercured | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + vulcanize, from Vulcan + -ize.  Vulcan refers to the Roman god of fire and metalworking, and the process of vulcanization involves heating rubber with sulfur.
