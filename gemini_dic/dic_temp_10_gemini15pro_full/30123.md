cedilla:/səˈdɪlə/| n.| 分音符 |A diacritic mark (¸) placed under the letter c, especially in French and Portuguese, to indicate that it is pronounced as an /s/ before a, o, or u.|The word "façade" uses a cedilla under the c.|近义词: diacritical mark, diacritic|反义词:  (No direct antonym for a punctuation mark)|词性:noun
