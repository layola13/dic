broody:/ˈbruːdi/| adj.|沉思的，阴郁的；(母鸡等)孵蛋的|inclined to sit on eggs; (of a person) appearing moody and bad-tempered;|例句：The hen has gone broody. 母鸡开始孵蛋了。|例句：He's been very broody ever since he lost his job. 他自从失业后就一直很闷闷不乐。|近义词：moody, sullen, thoughtful, contemplative|反义词：cheerful, happy, sociable|词性:形容词
broody:/ˈbruːdi/| n.|孵蛋的母鸡|a broody hen|例句：The farmer collected the eggs from the broody. 农夫从孵蛋的母鸡那里收集了鸡蛋。|例句：We need a broody to hatch these chicks. 我们需要一只孵蛋的母鸡来孵化这些小鸡。|近义词：sitting hen|反义词:|词性:名词

词源: From Old English *brōdig, from brōd "brood".
