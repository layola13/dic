describe:/dɪˈskraɪb/ | v. | 描述，形容 | To give a detailed account or picture of something in words. |  She described the scene in vivid detail. 她生动地描述了那个场景。

He described his childhood as a time of great happiness. 他形容他的童年是一段非常快乐的时光。


近义词：portray, depict, illustrate, characterize, explain, relate | 反义词：misrepresent, distort, obscure | 词性: 动词

PHRASE->
describe as  描述为...  He described the experience as life-changing. 他将这段经历描述为改变人生的。
describe in detail  详细描述  Please describe the incident in detail. 请详细描述一下这起事件。
describe to  向...描述  Can you describe the process to me? 你能向我描述一下这个过程吗？
describe oneself as  自称是...  She describes herself as a free spirit. 她自称是一个自由奔放的人。
describe something to someone  向某人描述某事  I tried to describe the painting to her. 我试图向她描述这幅画。
