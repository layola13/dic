airiness:/ˈɛrɪnɪs/| n.|轻盈，通风良好，空旷|The airiness of the room made it feel spacious and bright. 房间通风良好，感觉宽敞明亮。|例句：The curtains added to the airiness of the room. 窗帘增加了房间的通风。 The airiness of the fabric made it perfect for summer. 这种布料轻盈透气，非常适合夏天。|近义词：lightness, breeziness, spaciousness|反义词:stuffiness, closeness, density|词性:名词

词源: From airy + -ness.  "airy" 源于古英语 *ærig, 来自于ær (air) + -ig (形容词后缀). "-ness"是名词后缀，表示状态或性质。
