hydrastine:/ˈhaɪdrəstiːn/
noun
黄连素
A white crystalline alkaloid, C21H21NO6, obtained from hydrastis and used medicinally as a uterine hemostatic and vasoconstrictor.
Hydrastine is sometimes used to stop bleeding. 黄连素有时用来止血。
近义词:berberine, canadine
反义词:(No direct antonym for a specific chemical compound)
词性:n.
