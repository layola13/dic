naturalness:/ˈnætʃrəlnəs/| n.|自然；自然性；不做作|The naturalness of her performance was praised by the critics. 她的表演自然流畅，受到了评论家的赞扬。|近义词：spontaneity, simplicity, authenticity, genuineness|反义词：artificiality, affectation, contrivedness|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From natural + -ness.  "natural" comes from Old French "naturel". Ultimately derived from Latin "naturalis" (natural, in accordance with nature) related to "natus" (born). "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
