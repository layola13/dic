essayer:/ɛseje/ (French), /ɛˈseɪ/ (Anglicized)| v. |尝试，试验|To attempt or try something; to try on (clothing).|例句：
J'ai essayé de réparer la voiture. = I tried to repair the car.
Elle a essayé la robe rouge. = She tried on the red dress.


近义词：attempt, try, test, experiment (for actions), try on (for clothing)
反义词:forgo, neglect, avoid, ignore
词性:verb
