npu:/ɛn piː juː/ |abbr.|神经处理单元 (Neural Processing Unit)|A specialized electronic circuit designed to accelerate machine learning algorithms.  It is optimized for matrix operations and often used in artificial intelligence applications.|Example: The new smartphone uses an NPU for faster image recognition. 这款新的智能手机使用神经处理单元来加快图像识别速度。|Synonym: AI accelerator, TPU|Antonym: CPU, GPU|Abbreviation
