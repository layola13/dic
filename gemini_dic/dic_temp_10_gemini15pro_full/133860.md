nongraphitic:/ˌnɒnɡræˈfɪtɪk/
adjective
非石墨的
Not consisting of or resembling graphite.
The electrode material is made of a nongraphitic carbon.  这种电极材料由非石墨碳制成。
Amorphous carbon is a nongraphitic form of carbon. 无定形碳是一种非石墨形式的碳。
non-graphitic, amorphous
graphitic, crystalline
