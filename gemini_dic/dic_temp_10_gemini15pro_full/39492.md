PHRASE->
There are no commonly used phrases with "connotes" in the same way as there are with "what". "Connotes" is typically used on its own within a sentence to describe the implied meaning of a word.  Since it's a verb describing a relationship between a word and its implied meaning, it doesn't form fixed phrases in the same way nouns or question words do.
