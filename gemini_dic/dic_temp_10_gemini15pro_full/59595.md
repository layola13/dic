例句：His ebullition of anger frightened the children. 他勃然大怒，吓坏了孩子们。|近义词：boiling, effervescence, excitement, fervor|反义词：calmness, tranquility|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ēbullitiō, from Latin ēbullīre (“to boil over”), from ex- (“out of, from”) + bullīre (“to boil, bubble”).
