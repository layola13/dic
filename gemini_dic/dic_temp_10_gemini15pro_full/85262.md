harmonically:/hɑːrˈmɒnɪkli/ | adv. |和谐地，协调地 |In a harmonious manner; in a way that is musically pleasing or that produces a pleasing combination of elements.| The colors blended harmonically, creating a soothing effect. 颜色和谐地融合在一起，营造出舒缓的效果。| The instruments played harmonically together. 乐器和谐地一起演奏。| synonym: concordantly, agreeably, melodiously | antonym: discordantly, disharmoniously | adverb

词源:
From Late Latin *harmonicus, from Ancient Greek ἁρμονικός (harmonikos, “of or belonging to harmony, musical”), from ἁρμονία (harmonia, “harmony; concord; a fitting together”), ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ser- (“to join, bind”).
