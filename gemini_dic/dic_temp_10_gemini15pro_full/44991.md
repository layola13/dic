crystallizes:/ˈkrɪstəlaɪzɪz/
verb
结晶；明确；具体化
becomes definite or clear
The plan crystallized in her mind.  这个计划在她的脑海中逐渐清晰起来。
solidifies into crystals
The solution crystallized overnight. 溶液一夜之间结晶了。
Synonyms: solidify, harden, concretize, materialize, take shape
Antonyms: dissolve, melt, liquefy
v.
