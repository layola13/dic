overmodesty:/ˌoʊvərˈmɒdəsti/| n.|过度谦虚，过分谦逊|An excessive or exaggerated sense of modesty, often manifested as downplaying one's accomplishments or abilities.|She dismissed her achievements with characteristic overmodesty. 她以一贯的过分谦虚淡化了自己的成就。|近义词：humility, self-deprecation|反义词: arrogance, boastfulness|词性:名词

overmodest:/ˌoʊvərˈmɒdəst/| adj.|过度谦虚的，过分谦逊的|Excessively or exaggeratedly modest, often downplaying one's accomplishments or abilities.|He was overmodest about his contribution to the project. 他对自己在项目中的贡献过于谦虚。|近义词：humble, self-effacing|反义词: arrogant, boastful|词性:形容词
