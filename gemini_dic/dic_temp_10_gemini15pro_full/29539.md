catch-all:/ˈkætʃˌɔːl/| adj. n.|包罗万象的，万能的；包罗万象的事物|A category or description that includes a wide variety of different things, often those that do not fit easily into other categories.  A catch-all term or solution is intended to cover all possibilities or to solve all problems.|This drawer is a catch-all for everything I don't have a specific place for.  这个抽屉是用来放所有我没有特定地方放的东西的。|Synonyms: general, inclusive, broad, sweeping|Antonyms: specific, narrow, limited|adjective, noun


catch-all:/ˈkætʃˌɔːl/| n.|包罗万象的事物|A thing that holds or includes a variety of miscellaneous items or encompasses a wide range of situations. |The new law is a catch-all for various minor offenses. 这项新法律涵盖了各种轻微违法行为。|Synonyms: miscellaneous collection, hodgepodge, omnium-gatherum|Antonyms: specific item, individual component|noun
