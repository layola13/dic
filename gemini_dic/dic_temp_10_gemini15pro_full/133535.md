nonfigurativeness:/ˌnɒnˈfɪɡjərətɪvnəs/| n.|非形象性，非具象性|The quality of being nonfigurative; abstractness.  The absence of recognizable figures or objects.|The painting was striking in its nonfigurativeness, a swirl of colors and textures. 这幅画以其非具象性引人注目，是一团色彩和纹理的漩涡。|近义词：abstractness, nonrepresentation|反义词: figurativeness, representationalism|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From nonfigurative + -ness.  Nonfigurative from non- + figurative. Figurative from Latin *figurativus, from figura ("figure, shape, form").
