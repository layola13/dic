barcoding:/ˈbɑːrˌkoʊdɪŋ/| n.|条形码编码，条码化|The process of representing data with a barcode. It involves assigning a unique barcode to an item and storing relevant information associated with that barcode in a database.|Supermarkets use barcoding to track inventory and speed up checkout. 超市使用条形码来跟踪库存并加快结账速度。|Near synonyms: barcode scanning, barcode technology|Antonyms: None|Noun

barcoding:/ˈbɑːrˌkoʊdɪŋ/| n.|条形码技术|The technology or system of using barcodes for identification and data collection.|Barcoding has revolutionized retail and logistics. 条形码技术彻底改变了零售业和物流业。|Near synonyms: barcode system, automatic identification|Antonyms: None|Noun
