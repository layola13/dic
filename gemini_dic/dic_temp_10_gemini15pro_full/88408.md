hirsuteness:/hɜːrˈsuːtnəs/ | n. | 毛发茂盛，多毛 | The state of being hairy or covered with hair. | His hirsuteness was remarkable, with hair covering most of his body.  他的毛发茂盛，令人瞩目，毛发覆盖了他身体的大部分。| synonyms: hairiness, shagginess, villosity | antonyms: hairlessness, smoothness, glabrousness | noun
