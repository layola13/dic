ceremonious:/ˌserɪˈməʊniəs/ (US) /ˌserɪˈməʊniəs/ (UK)| adj.|隆重的，正式的，讲究仪式的|Marked by or given to ceremony; formal; ritualistic.  Characterized by elaborate and often excessively polite formalities.|The ambassador made a ceremonious entrance into the hall. 大使隆重地步入了大厅。|Synonyms: formal, ritualistic, solemn, stately, conventional|Antonyms: informal, casual, relaxed, simple|adjective

ceremoniously:/ˌserɪˈməʊniəsli/ (US) /ˌserɪˈməʊniəsli/ (UK)| adv.|隆重地，正式地|In a ceremonious manner; formally.|He ceremoniously presented her with the award. 他隆重地把奖项颁给了她。|Synonyms: formally, officially, ritually, solemnly|Antonyms: informally, casually|adverb
