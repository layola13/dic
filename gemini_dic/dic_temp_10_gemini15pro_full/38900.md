confess:/kənˈfɛs/ (US) /kənˈfes/ (UK)| v. |承认，坦白，忏悔|To admit that you have done something wrong or something that you feel guilty or ashamed about.|He confessed to stealing the money. 他承认偷了钱。|She confessed her love for him. 她坦白爱他。|近义词：admit, acknowledge, own up, reveal, divulge|反义词：deny, conceal, hide|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French confesser, from Latin confessus, past participle of confiteri "to confess," from com- "together" (see com-) + fateri "to acknowledge, confess."
