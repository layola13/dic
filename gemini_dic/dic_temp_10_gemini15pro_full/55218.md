dissever:/dɪˈsɛvər/
verb
切断，割裂，分离
To separate or divide, especially by cutting or breaking.
The rope was dissevered by the sharp knife.  这根绳子被锋利的刀子割断了。
The two countries were dissevered by war. 这两个国家因战争而分离。
近义词：separate, divide, sever, detach, disunite, sunder
反义词：unite, join, connect, combine
词性：v.

dissever:/dɪˈsɛvər/
词源：From Late Latin *disseparare, from Latin dis- "apart" + separare "to separate."
用法说明：Dissever is a rather formal and literary word, less commonly used than synonyms like "separate" or "divide." It often implies a more forceful or violent separation.
