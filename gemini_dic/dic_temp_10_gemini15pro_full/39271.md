congressionally:/kənˈɡrɛʃənəli/
adjective
由国会地；国会地；按照国会地
In a manner relating to or enacted by a congress.
The issue was congressionally mandated.  这个问题是由国会授权的。
The funds were congressionally appropriated. 这些资金是由国会拨款的。
近义词: legislatively
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "executively")
词性: adjective
