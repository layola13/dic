extremisms: /ɪkˈstriːmɪzəmz/
| n. | 极端主义，极端行为 | The rise of political extremisms is a worrying trend. 政治极端主义的兴起是一个令人担忧的趋势。|  Extremisms can manifest in various forms, including religious, political, and ideological. 极端主义可以以各种形式表现出来，包括宗教、政治和意识形态。 | 例句：
The extremisms of the political fringes are a threat to democracy.  政治边缘的极端主义是对民主的威胁。

The government is taking steps to combat violent extremisms. 政府正在采取措施打击暴力极端主义。
| 近义词：radicalism, fanaticism, fundamentalism | 反义词：moderation, tolerance, centrism | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From the adjective "extreme" + "-ism," a suffix forming nouns denoting action, practice, or adherence to a principle, doctrine, etc.  "Extreme" ultimately derives from the Latin "extremus," meaning "outermost, furthest, last, utmost."
