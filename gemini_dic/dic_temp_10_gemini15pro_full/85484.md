hassles:/ˈhæsəlz/ (US), /ˈhæsəlz/ (UK)| n.|麻烦，困难，争论|minor annoyances or difficulties that cause stress or frustration|例句：Dealing with customer complaints is one of the biggest hassles of the job. 处理客户投诉是这份工作最大的麻烦之一。

例句：I don't want to go through the hassle of getting a visa. 我不想经历获得签证的麻烦。

近义词：problems, difficulties, troubles, annoyances, inconveniences|反义词:ease, convenience|词性:noun
