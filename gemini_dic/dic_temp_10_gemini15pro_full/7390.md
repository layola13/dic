animastic:/ˌænɪˈmæstɪk/
adjective
动画风格的，像动画的
Relating to or resembling animation, especially in style or appearance.  Often used to describe something that looks like it belongs in a cartoon or animated film.
The animastic style of the commercial made it appealing to children.  这支广告的动画风格使它吸引了孩子们。
The video game featured animastic characters and vibrant colors.  这款电子游戏以动画风格的人物和鲜艳的色彩为特色。
近义词: cartoonish, animated
反义词: realistic, lifelike
词性: adjective

animastic:/ˌænɪˈmæstɪk/
noun
动画样片，动态故事板
A preliminary animated version of a film, television program, or commercial, typically used to visualize the story and plan the shots.
The director reviewed the animastic before starting principal photography. 导演在开始主要拍摄之前审查了动画样片。
The animastic helped the team refine the timing and pacing of the action sequences.  动画样片帮助团队完善了动作场面的时间和节奏。
近义词: storyboard, previsualization
反义词: final cut, finished product
词性: noun
