overexaggerations: /ˌoʊvərɪɡˌzædʒəˈreɪʃənz/ | n. | 过分夸大；夸张 |  The statement was filled with overexaggerations, making it difficult to discern the truth.  这个陈述充满了夸张，很难辨别真相。 | Example: His overexaggerations about his achievements made him seem untrustworthy. 他对自己成就的过分夸大让他显得不值得信任。 | Synonym: hyperbole, embellishment, exaggeration | Antonym: understatement, minimization | Noun

overexaggerate: /ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzædʒəreɪt/ | v. | 过分夸大；夸张 |  Don't overexaggerate the importance of this event; it's not as significant as you make it out to be. 不要过分夸大这件事的重要性；它不像你说的那么重要。 | Example: She tends to overexaggerate her symptoms when she's feeling unwell. 她不舒服的时候往往会夸大她的症状。 | Synonym: exaggerate, embellish, overstate | Antonym: understate, downplay | Verb
