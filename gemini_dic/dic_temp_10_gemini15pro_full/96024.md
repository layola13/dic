indagation:/ˌɪndəˈɡeɪʃən/| n.|调查，探究|A careful investigation or inquiry into a matter, especially one that is difficult or complex. |The police launched a thorough indagation into the robbery. 警方对这起抢劫案展开了彻底的调查。|近义词：investigation, inquiry, probe, examination|反义词: disregard, neglect|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin indagationem, accusative singular of indagatio (“a searching into, investigation”), from indagare (“to search into, investigate”), from in- (“in, into”) + dagare (“to prick, stab”).
