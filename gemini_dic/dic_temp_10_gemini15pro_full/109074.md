legated:/ˈleɡeɪtɪd/
verb
遗赠；委托；交付
transferred (property) to someone by will; passed on or handed down
He had legated his entire estate to his daughter. 他已将全部财产遗赠给女儿。
bequeath, will, bestow
revoke, withhold
v.
