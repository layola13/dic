homotaxia:/ˌhɒməˈtæksiə/ | n. |同源异型，平行进化|The similar body shape of sharks and dolphins is an example of homotaxia, where unrelated species evolve similar traits due to similar environmental pressures.|Dolphins and sharks exhibit homotaxia in their streamlined bodies, despite not being closely related.  These similarities arise from adapting to a shared aquatic environment.|近义词:convergence, parallel evolution|反义词:divergence|词性:noun
