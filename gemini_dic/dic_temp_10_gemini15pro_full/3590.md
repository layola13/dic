agatoid:/ˈæɡətɔɪd/
adjective
玛瑙状的;像玛瑙的
Resembling agate.  Having the appearance or texture of agate.
The polished stone had an agatoid appearance. 这块抛光的石头有玛瑙般的外观。
The mineral exhibited an agatoid texture. 这种矿物呈现出玛瑙状的质地。
Synonyms: agate-like, agatine
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, perhaps something like "amorphous," "shapeless," depending on context)
