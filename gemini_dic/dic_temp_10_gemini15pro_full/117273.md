mechanistically:/ˌmɛkəˈnɪstɪkli/
adverb
机械地，机制上地
Explaining something in terms of the mechanism or process by which it works, without considering other factors like purpose or meaning.  Focusing on the physical or chemical processes involved.
The scientist explained mechanistically how the drug interacted with the body's receptors.  科学家从机制上解释了药物如何与身体受体相互作用。

The report described mechanistically how the machine functioned, but failed to address its overall purpose.  报告从机制上描述了机器是如何工作的，但没有说明它的总体用途。

Synonym:  materially, physically
Antonym: teleologically, purposefully
词性: 副词
