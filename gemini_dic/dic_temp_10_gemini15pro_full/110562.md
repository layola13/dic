limicoline: /ˌlɪmɪˈkɒlaɪn/ (UK), /ˌlɪmɪˈkoʊlaɪn/ (US)
| adj. n.| 涉禽类的；涉禽 |Relating to or denoting birds of the suborder Limicolae (or Charadrii), which includes sandpipers, plovers, and snipes, typically having long legs and bills adapted for wading and probing in mud or shallow water. Also used as a noun to refer to a bird of this type. |例句：The limicoline bird probed the mud for food.  涉禽在泥里探寻食物。

例句：Limicolines are often found near shorelines.  涉禽通常在海岸线附近被发现。 |近义词：shorebird, wader |反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "land bird") |词性: adjective, noun
