nontransient:/nɒnˈtrænziənt/ |adj.|非瞬态的，持久的，永久的|Not transient; lasting; permanent.  Relating to data or memory that persists beyond the immediate execution of a program or the current session of a device. |The nontransient nature of the data ensures it's available even after a system reboot.|近义词：permanent, persistent, enduring, lasting|反义词:transient, temporary, ephemeral|形容词
