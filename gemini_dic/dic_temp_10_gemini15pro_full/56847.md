dorsally:/ˈdɔːrsəli/| adv.| 背侧地，向背侧地|  Relating to the back or posterior of an organism.  The shark's fin is located dorsally. 鲨鱼的鳍位于背部。|例句：The feather is attached dorsally to the bird's wing. 这根羽毛连接在鸟翅膀的背面。

例句：The nerve runs dorsally along the spine. 这条神经沿着脊柱背面延伸。|近义词：posteriorly, at the back|反义词:ventrally|词性:副词
