meritmonger:/ˈmɛrɪtˌmʌŋɡər/| n. |沽名钓誉者，炫耀功绩的人|Someone who boasts about their merits or accomplishments, often excessively.  A person who makes a show of their good deeds or qualities, especially in order to gain praise or recognition.|He's such a meritmonger, always bragging about his charity work.|Synonyms: braggart, show-off, egotist, boaster|Antonyms: humble person, self-effacing person|noun
