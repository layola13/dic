cyans:/ˈsaɪænz/| n.|青色，蓝绿色；氰化物（复数）|A deep greenish-blue colour.|The artist used several shades of cyans in the painting.|近义词：blues, azures, teals|反义词:reds, yellows|noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek κύανος (kúanos, “dark-blue enamel, lapis lazuli”). Doublet of cyan.
