foretoken:/ˈfɔːrˌtoʊkən/ | n. |预兆，征兆 |A sign or warning of something to come. |The raven was seen as a foretoken of death.  乌鸦被视为死亡的预兆。

The unusual quiet was a foretoken of the coming storm.  不同寻常的寂静是即将到来的暴风雨的预兆。 | synonym: omen, portent, presage | antonym:  aftermath, consequence | noun
