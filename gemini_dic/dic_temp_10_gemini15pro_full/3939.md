agro-ecosystem:/ˈæɡroʊˈiːkoʊˌsɪstəm/ |n.|农业生态系统|An ecosystem largely created and maintained to satisfy a human want or need; it is usually of low diversity. It is an ecosystem under agricultural management.|Example: The agro-ecosystem of the farm includes the crops, livestock, soil, water, and climate. 这个农场的农业生态系统包括农作物、牲畜、土壤、水和气候。

Example: Sustainable practices are crucial for maintaining the health of the agro-ecosystem. 可持续的做法对于维护农业生态系统的健康至关重要。|近义词:agroecological system, farming system, agricultural system|反义词:natural ecosystem|词性:noun
