erodibilities:/ɪˌroʊdəˈbɪlətiz/ /ɪˌrəʊdəˈbɪlətiz/
| n. |可蚀性；易蚀性|The susceptibility of soil or other material to erosion.  The plural form of erodibility.|The erodibilities of different soil types vary greatly depending on their composition and structure. 不同土壤类型的可蚀性因其组成和结构而差异很大。|Synonym: vulnerability, susceptibility |Antonym: resistance, imperviousness|noun
