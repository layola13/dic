bual: /buˈɑːl/ | n. | 闲聊，聊天; (尤指东南亚)棚屋，小屋 | A casual conversation; a hut or shed (especially in Southeast Asia). |  We had a long bual about our families. 我们聊了很久家常。 | 近义词：chat, talk, gossip, shed, hut | 反义词: silence, argument, house | 词性: 名词

词源:  Likely derived from Malay "bual" meaning "to chat" or "to talk idly."
