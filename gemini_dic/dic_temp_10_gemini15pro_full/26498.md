caballer:/ˌkæbəˈlɪər/ | n. | 骑士；武士；绅士 | A horseman, especially a knight or gentleman trained in horsemanship. |  He rode like a caballer of old. 他骑马像古代的骑士。| 近义词: knight, horseman, cavalier | 反义词: knave, villain | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cavalier* "horseman," from Old French *chevaler* "knight, horseman" (11c.), from Late Latin *caballarius* "horseman," from Latin *caballus* "horse."
