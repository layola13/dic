ennoblement:/ɪˈnoʊblmənt/
n.
赋予贵族身份，提高；使高贵
The act of ennobling someone or something.  The state of being ennobled.
His ennoblement as a Lord was a great honor.  他被封为勋爵是一种莫大的荣誉。
elevation, dignification, exaltation
degradation,abasement
noun
