dichroism:/ˈdaɪkroʊɪzəm/| n.|二色性|The property of exhibiting two different colors, especially when viewed in polarized light due to the difference in absorption of light vibrating in different planes.|The tourmaline crystal shows strong dichroism.  电气石晶体表现出强烈的二色性。|Synonym: pleochroism|Antonym: monochroism|noun

dichroic:/daɪˈkroʊɪk/| adj.|二色的|Exhibiting dichroism; having the property of presenting different colors by transmitted light when viewed in different directions, especially by polarized light.|This mineral is dichroic, appearing green or brown depending on the angle of light. 这种矿物是二色的，根据光线的角度呈现绿色或棕色。|Synonym: pleochroic|Antonym: monochromatic|adjective
