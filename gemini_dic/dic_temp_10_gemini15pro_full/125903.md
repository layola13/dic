musculocutaneous: /ˌmʌskjʊloʊkjuˈteɪniəs/
adjective
肌皮的
Relating to or involving both muscles and skin.  Pertaining to both muscle and skin; specifically designating a nerve supplying muscles of the arm and skin of the forearm.
The musculocutaneous nerve supplies the biceps muscle. 肌皮神经支配肱二头肌。
近义词: myodermic
反义词: None
