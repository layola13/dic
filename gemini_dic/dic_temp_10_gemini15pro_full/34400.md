classman: /ˈklɑːsmən/ (US) /ˈklɑːsmən/ (UK)| n. |同班同学；低年级学生|A male student in the same class as another, or in a lower class at a school or college.|例句：He was my classman in high school. 他是我高中的同班同学。

例句：The upperclassmen often mentor the classmen.  高年级学生经常指导低年级学生。

近义词：classmate, schoolmate|反义词: upperclassman|词性:noun
