globalized:/ˈɡloʊbəlaɪzd/ (US) /ˈɡləʊbəlaɪzd/ (UK)| adj.|全球化的|Made or adapted for international use or appeal; worldwide in scope.|例句：Many companies are now operating in a globalized marketplace. 许多公司现在都在全球化的市场中运营。|近义词：international, worldwide, global|反义词:local, regional, isolated|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From globe + -ize + -ed.  "Globe" ultimately comes from the Latin "globus," meaning "sphere" or "ball." "-ize" is a suffix used to form verbs meaning "to make or become like" or "to cause to be." "-ed" is a suffix that creates the past participle form of verbs, often used to form adjectives.
