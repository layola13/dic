half-brother:/ˈhæfˌbrʌðər/ | n. |同父异母或同母异父的兄弟|A male sibling who shares one parent with another person. | My father remarried, and I now have a half-brother. 我父亲再婚了，我现在有一个同父异母的兄弟。|  My mother's son from her previous marriage is my half-brother. 我母亲和她前夫生的儿子是我的同母异父的兄弟。| 近义词:stepbrother (if the shared parent is the one who remarried) | 反义词:full brother |词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
"half" (Old English healf) + "brother" (Old English broþor)
