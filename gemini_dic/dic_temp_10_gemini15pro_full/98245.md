insensitivity:/ɪnˌsɛn səˈtɪv əti/ /ɪnˌsɛn sɪˈtɪv əti/| n.|不敏感，麻木不仁，感觉迟钝|Lack of sensitivity or responsiveness; inability to perceive or react to something.|Her insensitivity to his feelings hurt him deeply. 她对他感情的麻木不仁深深地伤害了他。|近义词：apathy, indifference, callousness, obtuseness|反义词：sensitivity, empathy, compassion, responsiveness|词性:名词

insensitivity:/ɪnˌsɛn səˈtɪv əti/ /ɪnˌsɛn sɪˈtɪv əti/| n.|漠视，不关心|Lack of concern or consideration for the feelings of others.|His insensitivity to the plight of the refugees was appalling. 他对难民困境的漠视令人震惊。|近义词：unconcern, disregard, neglect|反义词：solicitude, concern, care|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + sensitive + -ity (noun suffix).  The word "sensitive" ultimately derives from Latin "sentire" (to feel).
