contributorial:/ˌkɒntrɪˈbjuːtəriəl/ /ˌkɑːntrɪˈbjuːtɔːriəl/
adjective
促成的；贡献的；起作用的
tending to contribute to a result; helping to cause something
例句：Smoking is a contributorial factor in lung cancer. 吸烟是导致肺癌的一个因素。

例句：His contributorial negligence led to the accident. 他的过失导致了这起事故。
近义词：contributing, causative, instrumental
反义词：inhibiting, hindering, preventative
词性：adjective

contributory:/kənˈtrɪbjʊtəri/ /kənˈtrɪbjətɔːri/
adjective
贡献的；促成的；起作用的
playing a part in bringing something about
例句：Stress is a major contributory factor in many illnesses. 压力是许多疾病的主要诱因。

例句：He made a contributory pension scheme payment. 他支付了一笔缴费型养老金计划款项。
近义词：contributing, causative, instrumental
反义词：inhibiting, hindering, preventative
词性：adjective

contributory:/kənˈtrɪbjʊtəri/ /kənˈtrɪbjətɔːri/
noun
贡献者；促成因素
a person or thing that contributes something
例句：She was a major contributory to the project's success. 她是该项目成功的主要贡献者。

例句：All contributories to the fund benefit from the interest earned. 所有向基金捐款的人都将从所得利息中受益。
近义词：contributor, donor, supporter
反义词：detractor, opponent
词性：noun


词源:  From contribute + -ory.  "Contribute" comes from Latin "contribuere," from "com-" (together) + "tribuere" (to grant, bestow).
