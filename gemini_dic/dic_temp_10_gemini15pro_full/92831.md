iconomania: /ˌaɪkɒnəˈmeɪniə/ (US) /ˌaɪkɒnəˈmeɪniə/ (UK)| n. |肖像崇拜；圣像崇拜|Excessive love or admiration of icons or images.|Her iconomania led her to collect hundreds of religious paintings.|近义词：idolatry, iconolatry|反义词：iconoclasm|词性: noun

Etymology:  From Greek εἰκών (eikōn, “image, icon”) + -mania.
