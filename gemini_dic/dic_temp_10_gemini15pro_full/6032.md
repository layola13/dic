amenability:/əˌmiːnəˈbɪləti/
n.
顺从；服从；通情达理
the quality of being open to suggestion or persuasion; willingness to cooperate.
The government's amenability to the proposal surprised many. 政府对这项提议的接受程度让很多人感到惊讶。
Synonyms: receptiveness, responsiveness, willingness, compliance, tractability
Antonyms: intransigence, resistance, unwillingness, stubbornness, inflexibility
noun


amenabilities:/əˌmiːnəˈbɪlətiz/
n.
顺从；服从；通情达理（amenability的复数形式）
plural form of amenability, referring to multiple instances or aspects of being open to suggestion or persuasion.
The amenabilities of the different parties involved made the negotiation process smoother.  各方参与者的通情达理使得谈判过程更加顺利。
Synonyms: receptivenesses, responsivenesses, willingnesses, compliances, tractabilities
Antonyms: intransigencies, resistances, unwillingnesses, stubbornnesses, inflexibilities
noun
