noxious: /ˈnɑːkʃəs/ | adj. | 有毒的；有害的；令人厌恶的 | Harmful, poisonous, or very unpleasant. |  The factory emitted noxious fumes. 工厂排放出有毒的烟雾。

The noxious smell of burning rubber filled the air. 燃烧橡胶的难闻气味弥漫在空气中。

近义词: harmful, poisonous, toxic, detrimental, damaging, injurious, pernicious, baneful, unhealthy, unwholesome, insalubrious, foul, nasty, offensive, disgusting, repellent, repugnant, loathsome, abhorrent, detestable, odious

反义词: harmless, safe, healthy, beneficial, pleasant

词性: 形容词

词源:  Late Middle English: from Latin noxius ‘hurtful’, from noxa ‘harm, injury’.
