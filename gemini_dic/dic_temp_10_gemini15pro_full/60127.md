effecting:/ɪˈfɛktɪŋ/
v.
实现；引起；产生
bringing about; causing; producing
例句：They are effecting changes in the company structure. 他们在改变公司结构。
例句：The new CEO is effecting a major turnaround in the company's fortunes. 新任首席执行官正在扭转公司的命运。
近义词：achieving, accomplishing, realizing, executing, performing, implementing
反义词：hindering, obstructing, preventing
词性：动词
