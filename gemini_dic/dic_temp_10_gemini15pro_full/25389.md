bulbul:/ˈbʊlbʊl/ | n. | 噪鹛，夜莺 | A songbird found in Africa and Asia, known for its melodious voice. |  The bulbul's song filled the air. 噪鹛的歌声充满了空气。| synonyms: songbird, nightingale | antonyms: crow, raven | noun

bulbul:/ˈbʊlbʊl/ | n. | 傻瓜，笨蛋 (informal, less common) | A foolish or naive person. | Don't be such a bulbul! 别这么傻！| synonyms: fool, idiot, simpleton | antonyms: genius, intellectual | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Persian بلبل (bolbol), ultimately from Arabic بلبل (bulbul), imitative of the bird's call.
