centralness:/ˈsɛntrəlnɪs/| n.|中心性，处于中心地位|The centralness of the issue made it a topic of national debate.  这个问题的核心地位使其成为全国辩论的话题。|例句：The centralness of its location makes it ideal for a distribution center. 它的中心位置使其成为配送中心的理想选择。

The centralness of this theme in his work cannot be overstated.  这个主题在他作品中的中心地位怎么强调都不为过。|近义词：centrality, importance, significance|反义词:peripheralness, insignificance, marginality|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From central + -ness.  Central derives from Late Latin centralis, from centrum "center," which ultimately comes from Greek kentron "sharp point, stationary point of a pair of compasses, center."  The suffix -ness forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
