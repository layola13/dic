myzontes:/maɪˈzɒntiːz/| n. |米佐特人（古希腊色雷斯部落）|An ancient Thracian tribe known for their brigandage and inhabiting the mountainous regions near the Propontis (Sea of Marmara). They were notorious for demanding tribute and engaging in piracy.|The myzontes posed a constant threat to travelers and merchants in the region. 米佐特人对该地区的旅行者和商人构成了持续的威胁。| The myzontes extracted heavy tolls from ships passing through the Propontis. 米佐特人向通过普罗蓬蒂斯的船只征收高额通行费。|近义词:brigands, pirates, raiders|反义词:citizens, settlers|词性:noun
