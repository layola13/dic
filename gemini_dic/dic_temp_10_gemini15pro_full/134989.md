nonnavigable:/nɒnˈnævɪɡəbl/ /nɑːnˈnævɪɡəbl/| adj.|不可航行的，不能通航的|Not navigable; not capable of being navigated.  Unable to be sailed on or through by ships or boats.|The river is nonnavigable due to low water levels. 这条河由于水位低而无法通航。|Synonyms: impassable, unnavigable|Antonyms: navigable, passable|Adjective
