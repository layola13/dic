folded:/ˈfoʊldɪd/ (US), /ˈfəʊldɪd/ (UK)| adj.|折叠的；合拢的|Folded means bent over on itself so that one part covers another.|She carefully folded the letter and put it in an envelope. 她小心翼翼地把信折好，放进信封里。|The bird sat with its wings folded. 鸟儿坐着，翅膀合拢着。|Synonyms: creased, pleated, doubled|Antonyms: unfolded, open, spread|adjective
