overweigh:/ˌəʊvəˈweɪ/| v.| 比…重；过于重视；超过；胜过| To be heavier than; to give too much importance to.|  The advantages overweigh the disadvantages. 利大于弊。 |Synonyms: outweigh, outbalance, preponderate, overbalance |Antonyms: underweigh, undervalue| verb

overweigh:/ˌəʊvəˈweɪ/| adj. (rare)| 过重的；过胖的| Excessively heavy or fat.| His overweigh luggage caused him problems at the airport. 他超重的行李在机场给他带来了麻烦。 |Synonyms: overweight, obese, corpulent |Antonyms: underweight, slim, thin| adjective
