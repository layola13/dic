dishearteningly:/dɪsˈhɑːrtnɪŋli/
adverb
令人沮丧地，令人灰心地
in a way that causes someone to lose confidence, hope, and enthusiasm.
The team played dishearteningly in the final, losing by a large margin.
该队在决赛中表现令人沮丧，惨败。
近义词:discouragingly, depressingly, disappointingly
反义词:encouragingly, inspiringly
