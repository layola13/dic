martyring:/ˈmɑːrtərɪŋ/| v.|折磨；殉难；牺牲|The act of making someone a martyr, often involving suffering or sacrifice.|She felt she was martyring herself for her children.|近义词：sacrificing, immolating|反义词: sparing, preserving|动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *martyren*, from Old French *martirer*, from Late Latin *martyrizāre*, from Greek *μαρτυρίζειν* (martyrizein, “to bear witness, give testimony”), from *μάρτυς* (martys, “witness”).
