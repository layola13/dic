contrasters:/kənˈtræstərz/
n.
对比者；形成对照的人或事物
People or things that contrast with each other.
The artist used light and shadow as contrasters to create a dramatic effect.  艺术家用光影作为对比，创造出戏剧性的效果。
近义词:foils, opposites
反义词:similarities, parallels
词性:名词
