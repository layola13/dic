circuitously:/sɜːrˈkjuːɪtəsli/
adv.
迂回地，绕圈子地
in a roundabout way; indirectly.
He answered the question circuitously, never coming to the point. 他绕圈子地回答了这个问题，始终没有说到点子上。

circuitously:/sɜːrˈkjuːɪtəsli/
adverb
迂回地，绕圈子地
in a roundabout way; indirectly.
She explained the situation circuitously, avoiding the main issue. 她拐弯抹角地解释了情况，避开了主要问题。


circuitously:/sɜːrˈkjuːɪtəsli/
副词
迂回地，绕圈子地
in a roundabout way; indirectly.
The path to the summit was circuitous, winding through dense forest. 通往山顶的小路迂回曲折，蜿蜒穿过茂密的森林。


近义词:indirectly, tortuously, windingly
反义词:directly, straightforwardly
词性:adverb

词源:
From Latin *circuitus*, past participle of circuire (“to go around”), from circum (“around”) + ire (“to go”).
