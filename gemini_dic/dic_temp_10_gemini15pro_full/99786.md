intermention: /ˌɪntərˈvɛnʃən/
n.|干预，介入，调停|The government's intervention in the market stabilized prices. 政府对市场的干预稳定了价格。|intervention is the act of intervening, especially to prevent something from happening.|例句：Her intervention saved the business from failing. 她的介入挽救了这家企业免于倒闭。

例句：The doctor decided that surgical intervention was necessary. 医生认定需要进行外科手术干预。

近义词：interference, mediation, involvement
反义词：nonintervention, noninterference
词性：名词


词源：From Late Middle English: from Old French intervencion, from Latin interventio(n-), from the verb intervenire ‘intervene’.
