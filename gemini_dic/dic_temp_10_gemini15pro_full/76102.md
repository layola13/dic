furs:/fɜːrz/
n.
皮毛，毛皮；毛皮衣服
the hairy coats of animals, especially when used for clothing

She wore a luxurious coat of furs. 她穿着一件奢华的皮草大衣。

coats, pelts, hides
nakedness, bareness
noun
