horsewhip:/ˈhɔːrsˌwɪp/ | n. v. | 马鞭；鞭打 | A whip for controlling horses. To flog with a horsewhip. | He cracked the horsewhip across the horse's back. 他用马鞭抽打马背。| 近义词：whip, flog, lash | 反义词: caress, soothe | 词性: 名词, 动词
Etymology: From horse + whip.  The word 'whip' itself comes from Old English 'wippen' meaning "to move quickly, swing."
