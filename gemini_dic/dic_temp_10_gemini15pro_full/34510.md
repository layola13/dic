cleanability:/ˌkliːnəˈbɪləti/
n.
可清洁性；清洁的容易程度
The ease with which a material or surface can be cleaned.
This new countertop material boasts superior cleanability.  这种新的台面材料具有优异的清洁性能。
The cleanability of the fabric is a major selling point.  这种织物的易清洁性是一个主要的卖点。
cleanableness, washability
uncleanability, dirtiness
noun
