nongarrulously:/ˌnɒnˈɡærələsli/ | adv. | 不饶舌地，简洁地 | In a manner that is not talkative or excessively wordy; concisely and to the point. | He nongarrulously explained the complex situation. 他简洁地解释了复杂的情况。| Near Synonyms: concisely, succinctly, briefly, tersely | Antonyms: garrulously, loquaciously, verbosely | 词性: 副词
