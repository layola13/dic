hemicardia:/ˌhɛmɪˈkɑːrdiə/
n.
半心
A developmental anomaly characterized by the presence of only half a heart.
Hemicardia is a rare and fatal congenital defect.  半心是一种罕见且致命的先天性缺陷。
The fetus presented with hemicardia, a condition incompatible with life. 胎儿表现出半心，这是一种与生命不相容的疾病。
近义词: (No direct synonyms, but related terms include) cardiac anomaly, congenital heart defect
