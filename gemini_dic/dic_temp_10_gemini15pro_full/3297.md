afflicted:/əˈflɪktɪd/
adjective
痛苦的，受折磨的
Severely affected by some unpleasant feeling or disease.
She was afflicted with arthritis. 她患有关节炎。
近义词: troubled, distressed, suffering, tormented
反义词: healthy, well, sound, untroubled
词性: adjective

afflict:/əˈflɪkt/
verb
使痛苦，折磨
(of a problem or illness) cause pain or suffering to; affect or trouble.
The drought afflicts the northern part of the country most severely. 干旱对该国北部地区的影响最为严重。
近义词: trouble, burden, distress, plague
反义词: comfort, soothe, relieve, aid
词性: verb

词源:
From Latin afflictus, past participle of affligere “to dash to the ground, strike down, damage, injure, weaken, trouble, afflict,” from ad- “to” + fligere “to strike.”
