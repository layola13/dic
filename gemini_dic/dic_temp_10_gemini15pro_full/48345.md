decrustation:/diːkrʌˈsteɪʃən/
n.
除垢，清除外壳
The removal of a crust or coating, especially from a surface like teeth or a boiler.
The dentist performed a decrustation procedure to remove the hardened plaque from the patient's teeth. 牙医进行了除垢手术，去除了病人牙齿上硬化的牙菌斑。

The decrustation of the old kettle took hours. 清除旧水壶上的水垢花了几个小时。
近义词:cleaning, descaling, removal
反义词:incrustation, coating, accretion
词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *décrustation*, from *décruster* "to remove the crust", from *de-* (expressing removal) + *crouter* "to form a crust", from *croute* "crust". Ultimately from Latin *crusta* "crust, shell".
