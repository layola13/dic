dpp:/diː piː piː/ |abbr.| decentralized physical power, distributed power production 分布式发电|Decentralized physical power (DPP) refers to the generation of electricity from many small power sources, typically located close to where the electricity is used. 分布式发电(DPP)是指从许多小型电源发电，这些电源通常位于用电地点附近。|DPP systems are becoming increasingly popular as a way to reduce reliance on the traditional grid. 分布式发电系统正变得越来越流行，以此来减少对传统电网的依赖。|近义词: distributed generation (DG)|反义词: centralized power generation|n.
