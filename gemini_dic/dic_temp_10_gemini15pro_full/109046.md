legalese:/ˌliːɡəˈliːz/ | n. |法律术语;法律措辞| Specialized language of the legal profession, often considered obscure and difficult for laypeople to understand. |  The contract was filled with legalese that I couldn't understand.  合同里充满了法律术语，我看不懂。| synonyms: jargon, cant, argot, technicality | antonyms: plain English, simple language | noun


Etymology:  From legal + -ese (a suffix forming nouns meaning "language of" or "style characteristic of," as in Chinese, journalese).
