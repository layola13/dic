fedoras:/fɪˈdɔːrəz/| n. |软呢帽，费多拉帽|A soft felt hat with a curled brim and a lengthwise crease in the crown.  It is often associated with 1920s gangsters and later with film noir detectives.|He wore a gray fedora pulled low over his eyes. 他戴着一顶灰色的软呢帽，帽檐拉得很低，遮住了眼睛。|近义词：hat, trilby|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific type of hat)|noun
