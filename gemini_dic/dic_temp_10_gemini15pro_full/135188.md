nonordinations:/ˌnɒnɔːrdɪˈneɪʃənz/
n.
未任命；未授予圣职
The nonordinations of women in certain religious denominations remain a contentious issue.  某些宗教派别对女性不授予圣职仍然是一个有争议的问题。
The nonordinations caused a schism within the church. 未任命在教会内部造成了分裂。
Etymology: non- (not) + ordination (the act of ordaining)
Synonym: non-appointment
Antonym: ordination
词性: noun
