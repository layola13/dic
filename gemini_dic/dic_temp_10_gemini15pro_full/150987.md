paramo:/ˈpɑːrəmoʊ/ (US), /ˈpærəməʊ/ (UK)| n.| 高山稀疏草地；荒野|A high treeless plateau in the Andes, especially in Colombia, Ecuador, and northern Peru.  A bleak and desolate landscape.|例句：The hikers struggled across the windswept paramo.  徒步旅行者艰难地穿过风吹swept的荒野。

例句：The paramo is home to unique plant and animal life.  高山稀疏草地是独特动植物的家园。|近义词：moor, heath, highland, plateau|反义词:lowland, valley|词性:noun
词源:From Spanish páramo, from Late Latin paramus (“desert”), of unknown origin.
