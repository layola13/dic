nonfraternity:/ˌnɑːnfrəˈtɜːrnəti/
adjective
非兄弟会的；不属于兄弟会的
Not belonging to or connected with a fraternity.
He lived in nonfraternity housing. 他住在非兄弟会宿舍。
近义词: independent, unaffiliated
反义词: fraternity
词性: adjective

Etymology:
non- + fraternity
"non-"  is a prefix meaning "not"
"fraternity" comes from Late Latin fraternitatem (nominative fraternitas) "brotherhood," from Latin frater (genitive fratris) "brother".
