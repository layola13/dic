birban:/ˈbɜːrbən/| n. |流氓，恶棍; 暴徒; 恶棍|A ruffian; a villain; a rogue. |He was known as a birban and a troublemaker in his youth. 他年轻时被称为流氓和捣乱分子。| The birban accosted the travelers on the road. 这个恶棍在路上拦住了旅行者。|近义词：ruffian, villain, rogue, scoundrel, hooligan|反义词:gentleman, law-abider|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Urdu word  "birbān," meaning "wild," "uncivilized," or "lawless."  Ultimately from Persian "bi-ravān" (بیروان) "without reins," "uncontrolled."
