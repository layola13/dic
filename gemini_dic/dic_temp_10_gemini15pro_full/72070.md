flittiness:/ˈflɪtɪnɪs/
n.
轻浮；轻佻；轻率；反复无常
The quality or state of being flitting; frivolousness.
Her flittiness made it difficult to have a serious conversation with her. 她的轻浮使得很难与她进行严肃的对话。
近义词：frivolity, levity, flightiness, capriciousness, fickleness
反义词：seriousness, stability, constancy, steadfastness
词性: 名词

flitting:/ˈflɪtɪŋ/
adj.
轻快的；飞掠的；一闪而过的
Moving swiftly and lightly.
The flitting butterflies in the garden were a beautiful sight. 花园里飞舞的蝴蝶构成了一道美丽的风景。
近义词：darting, flashing, fleeting, ephemeral
反义词：slow, lingering, permanent
词性: 形容词

flit:/flɪt/
v.
轻快地移动；掠过；迁徙
To move swiftly and lightly.
Birds flitted from branch to branch. 鸟儿在枝头间飞来飞去。
近义词：dart, flash, flutter, flit
反义词：plod, trudge
词性: 动词

词源:  From Middle English flitten, from Old Norse flytja (“to convey, remove, transport”), from Proto-Germanic *flutjaną (“to float, swim, fly”). Related to float.
