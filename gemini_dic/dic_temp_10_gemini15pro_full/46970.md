dartoic:/dɑːrˈtɔɪk/ | adj. |  镖毒的;  | Relating to or containing dart poison, a poisonous substance used on darts or arrows, typically by indigenous peoples for hunting or warfare.  Derived from "dart" + "-oic" (a suffix forming adjectives relating to chemical compounds). |  The dartoic effects of the frog's skin secretions were potent enough to paralyze small animals.  镖蛙皮肤分泌物的镖毒作用足以麻痹小动物。|  poisonous, toxic, venomous | harmless, innocuous | adjective
