barrowful:/ˈbæroʊfʊl/ | n. | 一独轮车（的量）| The amount that a barrow can hold. | He wheeled a barrowful of earth to the garden. 他把一独轮车土运到花园里。|  The gardener brought a barrowful of compost. 园丁带来了一独轮车的堆肥。 | load, cartload | handful | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "barrow" + "-ful". "Barrow" comes from Old English "bearwe" (a carrying frame or litter). "-ful" is a suffix meaning "full of" or "having the qualities of," derived from Old English "-ful."
