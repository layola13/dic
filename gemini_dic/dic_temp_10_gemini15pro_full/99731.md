intermarginal:/ˌɪntərˈmɑːrdʒɪnəl/
adjective
边缘间的，页边空白间的
Located or written between the margins, especially of a book page.
The intermarginal notes were barely legible due to their small size. 页边空白间的注释因为字迹太小几乎难以辨认。
Near synonyms: marginal, interlinear
Antonyms: central, middle
词性: adjective
