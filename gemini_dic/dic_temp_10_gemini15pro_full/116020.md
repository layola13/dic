marshy:/ˈmɑːrʃi/ /ˈmɑːrʃi/| adj.|沼泽的，湿软的|Characterized by swamps or marshes; boggy.  Covered with marsh; swampy.|The path led through a marshy area. 这条小路穿过一片沼泽地带。|The bird built its nest in the marshy ground near the river. 这只鸟在河边沼泽地上筑巢。|近义词：swampy, boggy, fenny, miry|反义词：dry, arid|词性:形容词
Etymology: From marsh + -y.  "Marsh" comes from Old English *mersc, from Proto-Germanic *mariską, from Proto-Indo-European *mōri- "sea, lake, marsh". The suffix "-y" forms adjectives meaning "characterized by" or "full of".
