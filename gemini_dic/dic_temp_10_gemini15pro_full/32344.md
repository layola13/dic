childhoods:/ˈtʃaɪldhʊdz/| n. |童年（复数）|The state or period of being a child; the time from birth to puberty.  It can also refer to the experiences and memories of that period, often used in the plural to emphasize the diverse nature of childhood experiences across different individuals or cultures.|

例句：
He had a happy childhood filled with adventures.
他有一个充满冒险的快乐童年。

例句：
Their childhoods were very different, shaped by contrasting circumstances.
他们的童年截然不同，是由不同的环境塑造的。

近义词：youth, boyhood, girlhood, infancy |反义词:adulthood, maturity |词性:noun
