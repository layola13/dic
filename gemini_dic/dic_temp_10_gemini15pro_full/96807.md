inertiae:/ɪˈnɜːʃiːiː/ |n.|惯性（复数）|The plural of inertia, a property of matter by which it continues in its existing state of rest or uniform motion in a straight line, unless that state is changed by an external force.|The *inertiae* of the two objects contributed to the impact.|近义词: (Singular form) inertia, inactivity, momentum, resistance|反义词: (Singular form) activity, change, energy|词性:Noun
