de-licensing:/diːˈlaɪs(ə)nsɪŋ/
n.
取消许可，撤销许可证
The act of removing or abolishing licensing requirements for a particular industry or activity.
The government is considering de-licensing certain sectors of the economy. 政府正在考虑取消某些经济部门的许可证制度。
Deregulation, liberalization
Licensing, regulation
noun
