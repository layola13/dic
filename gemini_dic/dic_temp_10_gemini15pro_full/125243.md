multidialectalism:/ˌmʌltiˌdaɪəˈlɛktəlɪzəm/ | n. | 多方言性；多方言使用 | The use or presence of multiple dialects within a particular region, community, or individual's speech. |  Her multidialectalism allows her to communicate effectively with people from various regions. 她掌握多种方言，可以和来自不同地区的人有效沟通。 | multilingualism, polydialectalism | monolingualism | noun
