galvanopuncture:/ɡælˌvænəʊˈpʌŋktʃər/| n.|电针疗法；电针穿刺术|A technique of acupuncture in which a small electric current is passed between pairs of acupuncture needles.  It stimulates the acupoints with electric current.|Galvanopuncture was used to treat his chronic pain. 电针疗法被用来治疗他的慢性疼痛。|Synonym: electroacupuncture|Antonym: None|noun
