doomer:/ˈduːmər/| n.|末日论者；悲观主义者|A person who believes that the world is doomed and that societal collapse or some other catastrophic event is inevitable.  A doomer often expresses negativity and pessimism about the future, sometimes to the point of fatalism.|例句：He's become a real doomer lately, constantly talking about the end of the world. 他最近变成了一个真正的末日论者，总是谈论世界末日。

例句：Many young people are becoming doomers due to climate change anxieties. 许多年轻人因为对气候变化的焦虑而变成了末日论者。|近义词：pessimist, defeatist, cynic|反义词: optimist, idealist|词性:noun
