desecrating:/ˈdɛsɪˌkreɪtɪŋ/
v.
亵渎，玷污
treating (a sacred place or thing) with violent disrespect; violating.
The vandals were desecrating the cemetery by spray-painting graffiti on the tombstones. 那些故意破坏公物者在墓碑上喷涂涂鸦，亵渎了墓地。
despoiling, profaning, violating, defiling
consecrating, sanctifying, revering
verb
