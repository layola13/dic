magnanimity:/ˌmæɡnəˈnɪməti/| n. |宽宏大量；慷慨|The quality of being generous and forgiving, especially toward a rival or someone less powerful than oneself. |  He showed magnanimity in defeat, praising his opponent's skill. 他在失败中展现了宽宏大量，称赞对手的技术。

She accepted her brother's apology with magnanimity. 她宽宏大量地接受了她哥哥的道歉。|近义词：generosity, forgiveness, forbearance, benevolence, charity |反义词：pettiness, meanness, spitefulness, vindictiveness|词性:名词

词源:
From Latin *magnanimitās*, from magnanimus (“great-souled, high-minded”) (from magnus (“great”) + animus (“mind, spirit”)) + -itās (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality).
