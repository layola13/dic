enchanting:/ɪnˈtʃæntɪŋ/ | adj. | 迷人的，令人陶醉的 | Delightfully charming or attractive; captivating. | The sunset over the ocean was an enchanting sight.  海上日落景色迷人。| 近义词: captivating, charming, fascinating, alluring, magical | 反义词: repulsive, unappealing, dull, boring | 词性: 形容词
词源:  From Old French enchanter (“to sing a charm upon, bewitch”), from in- (“in, into”) + cantare (“to sing”).
