intercirculated:/ˌɪntərˈsɜːrkjəleɪtɪd/ | adj. |相互循环的，互相流通的 |  Distributed or circulated among each other.  Referring to something, like information, currency, or goods, that moves and is shared within a closed system or group. | The intercirculated rumors caused confusion among the team members.  相互传播的谣言在队员之间引起了混乱。|  The intercirculated funds within the organization helped different departments support each other's projects. 机构内部相互流通的资金帮助不同部门支持彼此的项目。 | 近义词: exchanged, shared, distributed | 反义词: restricted, confined, isolated | 词性: adjective

词源:
"inter-" (prefix): between, among; from Latin "inter"
"circulate": to move around; from Latin "circulatus," past participle of "circulare," to make round, from "circulus," a little circle, diminutive of "circus," circle
