clypeus: /ˈklɪpiəs/ | n. | （昆虫的）唇基；（蜘蛛的）头胸甲 | A plate or shield-like structure forming part of the face below the front of the epicranium in insects and some other arthropods. In spiders, it is the anterior portion of the cephalothorax. | The clypeus of the grasshopper is particularly prominent. 蚱蜢的唇基特别突出. | Synonyms:  (No direct synonym, can use descriptive phrases like "facial shield," "head plate") | Antonyms: (No direct antonym) | noun
