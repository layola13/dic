gemmiparous:/dʒɛˈmɪpərəs/
adjective
(生物)发芽繁殖的，通过芽体繁殖的
Producing buds; reproducing by buds.
Example:  Some species of yeast are gemmiparous, reproducing through budding.  一些酵母菌种是通过芽殖繁殖的。
Example: Gemmiparous plants often spread quickly in favorable conditions. 发芽繁殖的植物在有利条件下通常会迅速蔓延。
Synonyms: budding, proliferous
Antonyms: seed-bearing, spore-forming
词性:adjective
