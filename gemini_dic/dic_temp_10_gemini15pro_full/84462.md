halop:/ˈheɪlɒp/ | n. | 海市蜃楼; 幻景; 幻觉; 晕; 光环 | An optical illusion of a hazy, luminous ring or arc surrounding a celestial body, especially the sun or moon. |  A halop appeared around the moon. 月亮周围出现了一个光晕。| synonyms: halo, nimbus, aureole | antonyms: reality | noun
