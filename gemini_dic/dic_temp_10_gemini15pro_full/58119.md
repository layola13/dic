ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *droppian, from Proto-Germanic *druppōną, frequentative of *drūpaną (“to drip”). Cognate with Dutch druppen (“to drip”), German tropfen (“to drip”), Icelandic drjúpa (“to drip”).
