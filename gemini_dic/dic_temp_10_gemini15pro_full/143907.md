outpray:/ˌaʊtˈpreɪ/
verb
胜过…的祈祷
To pray longer or more fervently than.
He outprayed his rivals in the monastery. 他在修道院里比他的竞争对手祈祷得更久更虔诚。
近义词: outdo, surpass
反义词: be outdone by, be surpassed by
词性: verb
