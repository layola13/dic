healths:/hɛlθs/
n.
健康状况（尤指祝酒时）
A toast to everyone's healths.  为每个人的健康干杯。
He drank to their healths. 他为他们的健康干杯。
近义词:well-being, fitness
反义词:illnesses, sicknesses
词性:名词

词源:
From Middle English *helthes, plural of helthe, from Old English hǣlþ (“health, wholeness, salvation, happiness, prosperity”). More at health.  来自中古英语 *helthes，是 helthe 的复数形式，源自古英语 hǣlþ（“健康、完整、救赎、幸福、繁荣”）。更多信息请参见 health。
