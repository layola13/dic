ethics: /ˈeθɪks/ | n. | 道德，伦理；伦理学 |  A system of moral principles that govern a person's behavior or the conducting of an activity. | Medical ethics prevent doctors from taking bribes.  医学伦理阻止医生受贿。| synonyms: morals, morality, principles, values | antonyms: immorality, dishonesty, corruption | noun

ethics: /ˈeθɪks/ | n. | 道德规范，行为准则 | The moral principles or rules of conduct recognized in respect to a particular class of human actions or a particular group or culture. |  Journalistic ethics require reporters to protect their sources.  新闻职业道德要求记者保护其消息来源。| synonyms: code of conduct, rules of behavior, standards | antonyms: lawlessness, anarchy | noun
