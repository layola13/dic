brahma:/ˈbrɑːmə/ | n. |梵天，婆罗门，创造之神|The Hindu god of creation.  The first member of the Hindu trinity (trimurti), consisting of Brahma the creator, Vishnu the preserver, and Shiva the destroyer.|例句：Brahma is often depicted with four heads. 梵天通常被描绘成有四个头。

例句：In Hinduism, Brahma is considered the creator of the universe. 在印度教中，梵天被认为是宇宙的创造者。|近义词：creator, God, the Creator |反义词:destroyer, Shiva| noun
