pantheologist:/ˌpænθiˈɒlədʒɪst/
n.
泛神论者
Someone who believes in pantheism (the doctrine that God is everything and everywhere, or that God is the universe).
Pantheologists see the divine in all aspects of nature. 泛神论者在自然界的各个方面都看到了神性。
Einstein was sometimes described as a pantheologist because of his awe of the universe. 爱因斯坦有时因其对宇宙的敬畏而被描述为泛神论者。
近义词:panentheist,  deist
反义词:atheist, agnostic
词性:noun


pantheistic:/ˌpænθiˈɪstɪk/
adj.
泛神论的
Relating to or characteristic of pantheism.
Her poetry expressed a pantheistic view of the world. 她的诗歌表达了一种泛神论的世界观。
Indigenous cultures often hold pantheistic beliefs, seeing spirits in nature. 许多土著文化持有泛神论的信仰，认为自然界中存在着神灵。
近义词:panentheistic
反义词:atheistic, agnostic
词性:adjective


pantheism:/ˈpænθiˌɪzəm/
n.
泛神论
The doctrine that God is everything and everywhere, or that God is the universe.
Pantheism offers a spiritual connection to the natural world. 泛神论提供了一种与自然世界的精神联系。
Some philosophers argue that pantheism is a more logical explanation for the existence of the universe than traditional theism. 一些哲学家认为，泛神论比传统的 theism 更能逻辑地解释宇宙的存在。
近义词:panentheism
反义词:atheism, theism
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek  pan (all) + theos (god) + -ism.
