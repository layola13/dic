pascal:/ˈpæskəl/ | n. adj.|帕斯卡（压强单位）、帕斯卡（人名）|A unit of pressure equal to one newton per square meter. Also used as a person's name, referring to Blaise Pascal, a French mathematician, physicist, inventor, writer and Catholic theologian. |The pressure was measured in pascals.  压力以帕斯卡为单位测量。|Blaise Pascal was a brilliant mathematician. 布莱兹·帕斯卡是一位杰出的数学家。|近义词：(for unit) newton per square meter |反义词: (no antonym for a unit of measurement or a person's name)|词性:noun, adjective
