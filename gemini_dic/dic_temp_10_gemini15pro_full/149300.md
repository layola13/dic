pa:/pɑː/ (UK), /pɑː/ (US)| determiner, pronoun|哪一个，哪一些|Used to ask about one or more people or things from a limited range of possibilities.|例句：Pa, which car do you prefer? 爸爸，你更喜欢哪辆车？|近义词：which|反义词:none, neither|词性:determiner, pronoun

pa:/pɑː/ (UK), /pɑː/ (US)| n.|爸爸 (Informal, childish term for father)|A term of endearment used by children for their father.|例句：Can I have a cookie, Pa? 爸爸，我可以吃块饼干吗？|近义词：dad, daddy, father, pop|反义词:mom, mommy, mother|词性:noun
