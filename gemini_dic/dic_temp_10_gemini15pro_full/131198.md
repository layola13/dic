nonburdensomenesses:/ˌnɒnbɜːrdənsəmˈnɛsɪz/
n.
无负担；轻松；不麻烦
The state or quality of not being burdensome; lack of burdensomeness.
The nonburdensomenesses of the new regulations were appreciated by small businesses.  新规定的轻松性受到了小企业的赞赏。

近义词:easiness, lightness, simplicity, effortlessness
反义词:burdensomeness, onerousness, difficulty

词性:noun (plural)
词源:  From non- + burdensome + -ness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Burdensome" comes from "burden" (a load or weight) + "-some" (a suffix meaning "characterized by"). "-Ness" is a suffix that forms nouns denoting a state or quality.
