vengeance:/ˈven.dʒəns/ (US) /ˈven.dʒəns/ (UK)| n. |复仇，报仇|Punishment inflicted or retribution exacted for an injury or wrong. |例句：He swore vengeance on his enemies. 他发誓要向他的敌人复仇。

例句：The family sought vengeance for the murder of their son. 这家人为儿子被谋杀寻求报复。|近义词：revenge, retribution, retaliation|反义词：forgiveness, pardon|词性:名词
