euthanize:/ˈjuːθənaɪz/ | v. | 安乐死；使安乐死；对…实施安乐死 | to kill (a person or animal) painlessly, usually by giving a drug, especially to end suffering from an incurable illness | The vet euthanized the dog to end its suffering. 兽医对这只狗实施了安乐死以结束它的痛苦。| synonyms: put down, put to sleep | antonyms: resuscitate, revive | verb

euthanized:/ˈjuːθənaɪzd/ | v. | 安乐死的（过去式和过去分词）| past tense and past participle of euthanize | The cat was euthanized after the accident. 这只猫在事故后被安乐死了。| synonyms: put down, put to sleep | antonyms: resuscitated, revived | verb
