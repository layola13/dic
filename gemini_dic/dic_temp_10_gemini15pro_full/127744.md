negotiator:/nɪˈɡoʊʃiˌeɪtər/ /nɪˈɡəʊʃieɪtə(r)/| n.|谈判者，协商者|A person who negotiates, especially in business or politics.|例句：The skilled negotiator was able to bring both sides to an agreement.  这位熟练的谈判者成功地使双方达成了协议。

例句：The company sent a negotiator to discuss the terms of the contract. 公司派了一名谈判者去商讨合同条款。|近义词：mediator, arbitrator, intermediary, representative|反义词：opponent, adversary|noun
