greened:/ɡriːnd/ | v. | 使…变绿; 绿化;  | to cause something to become green or covered with green plants |  The warm spring rains greened the hillsides. 春雨使山坡绿了。| synonyms:  vegetated, leafy, verdant | antonyms: barren, brown, withered | verb

greened:/ɡriːnd/ | adj. | 绿色的; 被绿化的 | covered with green plants or grass | The greened fields looked beautiful in the sunlight. 绿油油的田野在阳光下看起来很美。| synonyms: verdant, grassy, leafy | antonyms: barren, brown, withered | adjective
