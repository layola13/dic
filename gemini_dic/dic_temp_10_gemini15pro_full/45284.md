culturation:/ˌkʌltʃəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 文化适应; 文化陶冶; 文化修养 | The process of adapting to or acquiring the culture of a particular group or society.  The process of cultivating or refining someone's tastes, manners, and intellectual pursuits. |  Example 1: The culturation of immigrants into a new society can be a challenging process.  移民融入新社会的过程可能是一个充满挑战的过程。

Example 2:  Her travels abroad contributed to her culturation and broadened her perspective.  她的海外旅行有助于提高她的文化修养，开阔了她的视野。 |  Synonyms: acculturation, assimilation, socialization, refinement | Antonyms: deculturation, alienation | noun
