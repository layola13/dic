haemoconcentration:/ˌhiːmoʊˌkɒnsənˈtreɪʃən/ /ˌhiːməʊˌkɒnsənˈtreɪʃən/| n. |血液浓缩|An increase in the concentration of blood cells and other constituents due to a loss of plasma fluid.|Dehydration can lead to haemoconcentration. 脱水会导致血液浓缩。|Near synonyms: hemoconcentration, dehydration, inspissation|Near antonyms: hemodilution|noun
