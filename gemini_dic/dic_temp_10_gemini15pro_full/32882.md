chondrite:/ˈkɒndraɪt/ (US) /ˈkɒndrʌɪt/ (UK)| n. |球粒陨石|A stony meteorite containing small mineral granules called chondrules.|例句：The museum acquired a rare chondrite from the recent meteor shower.  博物馆获得了一块来自最近流星雨的稀有球粒陨石。

例句：Scientists study chondrites to learn about the early solar system. 科学家研究球粒陨石以了解早期太阳系。|近义词：stony meteorite|反义词: None (No direct antonym for a specific type of meteorite)|词性: Noun
