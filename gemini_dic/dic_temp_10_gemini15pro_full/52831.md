diluvian:/dɪˈluːviən/| adj.|大洪水的；像大洪水的；滔滔不绝的|Relating to a flood, especially the biblical Flood; also, figuratively, resembling a flood in force or abundance.|The diluvian rains caused widespread flooding.  暴雨造成了大范围的洪水。|Synonyms: torrential, flooding, overwhelming, cataclysmic|Antonyms: meager, scanty, trickling|adjective

diluvian:/dɪˈluːviən/| n.|大洪水时代的人或动物|A survivor of the Deluge (Noah's flood).  (Rarely used)|The museum displayed fossils of diluvian creatures.  博物馆展出了大洪水时代生物的化石。|Synonyms: antediluvian|Antonyms: modern|noun
