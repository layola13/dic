aetiologies: /ˌiːtiˈɒlədʒiz/ | n. | 病因学，病原学 |  The study of the causes or origins of diseases.  It can refer to the cause of a single disease or the study of disease causation in general. | The aetiologies of many cancers are still unknown. 许多癌症的病因仍然未知。|  The doctor investigated the aetiology of the patient's condition. 医生调查了病人病情的病因。 | synonyms: causes, origins, reasons, sources | antonyms:  effects, results, outcomes | noun
