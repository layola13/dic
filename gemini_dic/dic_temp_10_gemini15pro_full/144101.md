outscented:/ˌaʊtˈsɛntɪd/
verb|胜过香味，以更强的香味盖过...的气味|to surpass in scent; to overcome or mask the scent of something with a stronger scent|The roses outscent the lilies in the garden.  花园里玫瑰花的香味胜过百合花。| The strong perfume outscent the natural smell of the room.  浓烈的香水盖过了房间里的自然气味。|overpower, outperfume| - |verb
outscented:/ˌaʊtˈsɛntɪd/
adjective|香味被盖过的|having its scent masked or overcome by a stronger scent|The outscent flowers wilted quickly. 香味被盖过的花很快就枯萎了。|The outscent room felt stuffy. 香味被盖过的房间感觉很闷。|-|-|adjective
