dunkleosteus: /ˌdʌŋklɪˈɒstiəs/ (US) /ˌdʌŋklɪˈɒstiəs/ (UK)| n. |邓氏鱼|A genus of extinct placoderm fish that lived during the Late Devonian period, known for its massive size and powerful bite.|The Dunkleosteus was a fearsome predator of the ancient seas. 邓氏鱼是古代海洋中可怕的掠食者。| The Dunkleosteus terrelli could grow up to ten meters long.  邓氏鱼泰雷利种可以长到十米长。 |近义词: placoderm (板皮鱼), prehistoric fish (史前鱼)|反义词:  N/A (extinct, no living counterparts)|词性: noun
