bap's:/bæps/ | n. | 浸礼; 洗礼; (尤指)婴儿洗礼; 严峻考验; 磨炼; | A Christian ceremony in which a person is dipped in water or water is poured over their head, to welcome them into the Christian Church, or (especially) a baby's baptism. A difficult experience that tests someone's character and makes them stronger. | 例句：The baby's baps was a joyous occasion for the family.  婴儿的洗礼对这个家庭来说是一个快乐的时刻。

例句：Going through bankruptcy was a real baps for him.  经历破产对他来说是一次真正的磨练。 | 近义词：baptism, ordeal, trial | 反义词:  (For baptism) None; (For ordeal) ease, comfort | 词性: noun
