mutually:/ˈmjuːtʃuəli/ /ˈmjuːtʃʊəli/| adv.|互相地，共同地|in a shared manner; reciprocally|例句：They agreed mutually to end the contract. 他们双方同意终止合同。|例句：The feeling was mutually understood between them. 他们彼此心照不宣。|近义词：reciprocally, jointly, commonly|反义词：separately, individually|词性:副词

词源:
From Late Middle English *mutualli, from Old French mutuālement, from Latin mūtuālis (“done in exchange, reciprocal, mutual”) + -ment.
