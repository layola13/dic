bluebreast:/ˈbluːbrɛst/| n.|蓝胸鸟，蓝喉歌鸲|A small Old World songbird with a bright blue breast and throat.  A type of thrush.|例句：The bluebreast sang a melodious tune from the branch. 蓝胸鸟在树枝上唱着悠扬的曲调。

例句：We spotted a bluebreast flitting amongst the bushes. 我们看到一只蓝胸鸟在灌木丛中飞来飞去。|近义词：robin, redbreast (specifically for the European Robin)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "blue" + "breast," referring to the bird's distinctive plumage.  "Blue" traces back to Old French "bleu," and ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*blæwaz." "Breast" comes from Old English "brēost," related to Old High German "brust."
