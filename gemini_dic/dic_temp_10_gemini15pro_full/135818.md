nonpromiscuousnesses:/ˌnɒnprəmɪsˈkjuːəsnəsɪz/ | n. | 非滥交，非混杂 |  The state or quality of not being promiscuous; avoidance of promiscuity.  This refers to refraining from engaging in casual sexual encounters with multiple partners.  It can also refer to a lack of indiscriminate mixing or mingling, in a broader sense. |  The nonpromiscuousnesses of their relationships was remarkable given the social climate. 鉴于当时的社会风气，他们之间关系的非滥交性是很了不起的。|  The scientist was impressed by the nonpromiscuousnesses of the bacteria in the sample, each type keeping to its own niche.  科学家对样本中细菌的非混杂性印象深刻，每种细菌都待在自己的生态位。 | 近义词: chastity, fidelity, monogamy | 反义词: promiscuity | 词性: 名词

词源:
non- (prefix):  not
promiscuous (adjective): from Latin promiscuus "mixed, indiscriminate," from pro- "forward" + miscere "to mix."
-ness (suffix):  forms nouns indicating state or quality.
