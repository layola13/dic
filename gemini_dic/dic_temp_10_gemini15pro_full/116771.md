mauve:/ˈmoʊv/ (US), /ˈmɔːv/ (UK)| adjective, noun|淡紫色，紫红色；淡紫色的染料|A pale purple colour.  As a noun, it refers to this colour or a dye that produces it.|例句：She wore a mauve dress to the wedding. 她穿着一件淡紫色的连衣裙去参加婚礼。

例句：Mauve was a very popular colour in Victorian times. 淡紫色在维多利亚时代非常流行。

近义词：lavender, lilac, violet|反义词: (No direct antonym for a color.  Consider complementary colors like yellow-green)|词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From French *mauve* "mallow," from Latin *malva*.  The colour is named after the mallow flower.
