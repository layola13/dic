consult:/kənˈsʌlt/ (US), /kənˈsʌlt/ (UK)| v. n.|咨询，商量，查阅|To seek information or advice from someone with expertise or experience in a particular area. Also, to refer to a book or other source of information.|I consulted my doctor about the pain in my back. 我向我的医生咨询了背痛的问题。|synonyms: confer, discuss, advise, refer to|antonyms: disregard, ignore|verb

consult:/ˈkɒnsʌlt/ (UK)| n.|咨询，商议|An act of consulting or seeking advice.|The architect held a consult with the builders. 建筑师与建筑工人进行了磋商。|synonyms: conference, discussion, meeting|antonyms: N/A|noun
