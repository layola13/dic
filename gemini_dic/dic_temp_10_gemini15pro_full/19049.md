bethlehemite:/ˈbɛθlɪhɛˌmaɪt/
noun
伯利恒人；伯利恒居民
A person from Bethlehem.
Example: The bethlehemite welcomed the visitors.  伯利恒人欢迎来访者。

Example:  Many bethlehemites traveled to Jerusalem for the festival. 许多伯利恒人前往耶路撒冷参加节日。
Synonym: resident of Bethlehem, native of Bethlehem
Antonym: N/A (No direct antonym for a person from a specific place)
词性: n.
