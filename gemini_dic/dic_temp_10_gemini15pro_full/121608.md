misorganize:/ˌmɪsˈɔːrɡənaɪz/
verb
组织混乱；组织不善
to organize something badly or inefficiently
The event was completely misorganized – nobody knew where to go or what to do.  这场活动组织得一塌糊涂——没人知道该去哪里或做什么。
The office files were misorganized, making it difficult to find anything. 办公室文件组织混乱，很难找到任何东西。
Synonyms: disorganize, muddle, mess up
Antonyms: organize, arrange, systematize
词性: 动词
