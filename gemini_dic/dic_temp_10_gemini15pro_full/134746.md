nonmeeting:/nɒnˈmiːtɪŋ/
noun
非会议，非会面；不开会
A scheduled time when people intentionally do *not* meet, often to encourage focused work.  It's a period of time set aside for individual work, avoiding interruptions from meetings.
Many companies are implementing nonmeeting days to improve productivity. 许多公司正在实施无会议日以提高生产力。
non-meeting, no-meeting time, focus time, deep work time
