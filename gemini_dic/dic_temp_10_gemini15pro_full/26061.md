bushka:/ˈbʊʃkə/ | n. | 巴布什卡, 头巾 | A headscarf tied under the chin, typically worn by Russian peasant women. |  She wore a bright red bushka. 她戴着一个鲜红色的头巾。 | synonyms: headscarf, babushka | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps uncovered head/hair) | noun
