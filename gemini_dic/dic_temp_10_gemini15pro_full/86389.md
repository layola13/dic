hedgeless:/ˈhedʒləs/| adj.|无避险的，无对冲的，不受保护的|Lacking protection or a hedge against risk.  Referring to investments or situations where there are no measures taken to mitigate potential losses.|The hedgeless fund suffered massive losses during the market downturn.  这个无避险基金在市场低迷时期遭受了巨额损失。|unprotected, exposed, uninsured|protected, hedged, insured|adjective
