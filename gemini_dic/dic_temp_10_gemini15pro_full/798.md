absorption:/əbˈzɔːrpʃn/ /əbˈsɔːrpʃən/
n.
吸收; 专注; 合并
The process by which a substance is taken in by another, either physically or chemically.  The complete attention of someone on something. The merging of one thing into another.
Plants obtain nutrients through absorption from the soil.  植物通过从土壤中吸收养分来获取营养。

His absorption in the book made him oblivious to his surroundings. 他对这本书的专注使他对周围的环境浑然不觉。

The absorption of the smaller company into the larger one led to job losses.  小公司并入大公司导致了失业。
synonyms: assimilation, intake, incorporation, preoccupation, immersion
antonyms:  release, emission, excretion, distraction, inattention
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin absorptionem,  from absorbere "to swallow up," from ab- "from, away" + sorbere "to suck in."
