orthogonal:/ɔːrˈθɒɡənəl/ (US) /ɔːrˈθɑːɡənəl/ (UK)| adj.|正交的，垂直的，无关的|Relating to or involving right angles; statistically independent;  not pertinent to the matter under consideration.|The x and y axes are orthogonal. x轴和y轴是正交的。|The two issues are completely orthogonal. 这两个问题完全无关。|近义词：perpendicular, unrelated, independent|反义词：related, dependent, parallel|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *orthogonalis*, from Ancient Greek ὀρθός (orthós, “straight, right”) + γωνία (gōnía, “angle”).
