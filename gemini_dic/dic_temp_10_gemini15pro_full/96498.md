indo-british:/ˌɪndoʊˈbrɪtɪʃ/
adjective
印英的；英印关系的
Relating to both India and Britain, or to the relations between the two countries.  This term often refers to people of mixed Indian and British ancestry, or cultural aspects reflecting both heritages.
The indo-british relationship has a complex history. 英印关系有着复杂的历史。
The museum showcased indo-british art from the colonial period.  博物馆展出了殖民时期的印英艺术。
Near Synonyms: Anglo-Indian
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider purely Indian or purely British)
词性: adjective
