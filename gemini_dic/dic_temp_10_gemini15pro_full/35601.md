coarsest:/ˈkɔːrsɪst/| adj.|最粗糙的，最粗俗的|Superlative adjective of coarse.  Having a rough texture, lacking refinement or delicacy.|The coarsest sandpaper is used for stripping paint.|近义词：roughest, crudest, vulgar, unrefined|反义词: finest, smoothest, softest, refined, delicate|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *cors*, from Old French *cors*, from Late Latin *cursus* (“course”), from Latin *currere* (“to run”).  The sense development is from "running straight" to "ordinary, common" to "rough, coarse."
