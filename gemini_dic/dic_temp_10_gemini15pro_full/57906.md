driftweed:/ˈdrɪftˌwiːd/
noun
漂流的杂草，漂浮的 seaweed
detached seaweed floating on the surface of the ocean or washed ashore.
例句：The beach was covered in driftweed after the storm.  暴风雨过后，海滩上布满了漂流的杂草。

例句：The birds used driftweed to build their nests. 鸟类用漂浮的杂草筑巢。

近义词：seaweed, kelp, wrack
反义词: (No direct antonym exists, could consider rooted aquatic plants)


Etymology:
drift (Old Norse *drifa* "to drive") + weed (Old English *wēod* "plant, herb")
