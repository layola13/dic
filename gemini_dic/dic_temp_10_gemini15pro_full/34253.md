clape:/klæp/
n.
拍手声，掌声
The sound of clapping hands.
The audience erupted in claps. 观众爆发出掌声。
近义词：applause, ovation
反义词:silence, hush
词性:名词

词源:
From Middle English *clappen, from Old English *clappan, of imitative origin. Cognate with Middle Low German klappen (“to clap”), Middle Dutch clappen (“to clap, strike”), Old Norse klappa (“to pat, stroke”), Swedish klappa (“to pat, stroke, clap”).
