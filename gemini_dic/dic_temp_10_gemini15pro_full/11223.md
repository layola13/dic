archived:/ˈɑːrkaɪvd/| adj.|已归档的，存档的|Stored or filed away, typically for future reference.|The archived documents were retrieved from the storage room.  已归档的文件从储藏室中被取回。|近义词:filed, stored, preserved|反义词:current, active|词性:形容词

Etymology: From "archive" + "-ed".  "Archive" comes from the Old French "archif", ultimately from the Greek "arkheion" meaning "public building," later "public records."
