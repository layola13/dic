parroting:/ˈpærətɪŋ/ | n. | 模仿;  鹦鹉学舌;  机械重复 |Repeating something without understanding or thinking about it.  |He was just parroting what he'd heard on the news. 他只是在重复他在新闻里听到的内容。| mimicry, imitation, echoing | originality, creativity | noun

parroting:/ˈpærətɪŋ/ | v. | 模仿;  鹦鹉学舌;  机械重复 |To repeat something without understanding or thinking about it.  |The students were just parroting the teacher's words. 学生们只是在机械地重复老师的话。| mimic, imitate, echo | originate, create | verb

Etymology: From parrot (n.), from Old French *parot (modern French perroquet), ultimately from Spanish periquito "parakeet." The "-ing" suffix transforms the noun into a present participle/gerund, indicating the action of behaving like a parrot.
