cheaper:/ˈtʃiːpər/| adj.|更便宜的|Comparative form of "cheap."  Indicates a lower price or cost than something else being compared.|This new phone is cheaper than my old one. 这款新手机比我的旧手机便宜。|近义词：less expensive, more affordable, lower-priced|反义词:more expensive, pricier, costlier|词性:adjective
词源:From Middle English *chep*, from Old English *cēap* (“cheap, low-priced, of little value”), from Proto-Germanic *kaupō* (“bargain, price, business, trade”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)kʷop-/* (“to watch, observe; to buy”).
