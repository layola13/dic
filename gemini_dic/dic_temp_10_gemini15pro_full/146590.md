overgreatly:/ˌoʊvərˈɡreɪtli/| adv.| 过度地，过分地| excessively; to an excessive degree | He was overgreatly concerned about his appearance. 他过分在意自己的外表。|  The victory was overgreatly celebrated. 胜利的庆祝活动过分了。| 近义词: excessively, unduly, inordinately | 反义词: moderately, slightly, reasonably | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi "over, above") + "greatly" (from "great" (Old English grēat "big, large, tall; grown up; pregnant, heavy with child; important, chief; main; excellent; splendid, magnificent," from Proto-Germanic *grautaz) + "-ly" (suffix forming adverbs from adjectives))
