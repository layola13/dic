muss:/mʌs/| vt.| 弄乱，弄脏 |To make something untidy or messy.| Don't muss my hair! 别弄乱我的头发！|近义词：disarrange, rumple, dishevel, mess up|反义词：tidy, straighten, neaten|词性:v.
