montro:/ˈmɒnstroʊ/ (US) /ˈmɒnstrəʊ/ (UK)| n. |怪物，怪兽|A large, frightening imaginary creature.|例句：The children were scared by the montro in the movie. 孩子们被电影里的怪兽吓坏了。|例句：Legends tell of a montro that lived in the deep forest. 传说森林深处住着一个怪兽。|近义词：monster, beast, creature|反义词:hero, beauty|词性:名词

词源: From Italian *mostro*, ultimately from Latin *monstrum* ("divine omen, portent, monster, prodigy").
