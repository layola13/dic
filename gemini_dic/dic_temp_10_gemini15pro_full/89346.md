homologinic:/ˌhɒməlɒˈdʒɪnɪk/| adj.|同源的|Of or relating to homology; characterized by homology.  Specifically relating to structures or features in different species that are derived from a common ancestor.|The biologist pointed out the homologic relationship between the bat's wing and the human hand.|近义词：homologous, cognate|反义词:analogous|词性:adjective
