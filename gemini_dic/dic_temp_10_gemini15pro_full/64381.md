erectnesses:/ɪˈrɛktnɪsɪz/
n.
直立, 竖立; 挺拔
The state of being erect or upright.  The quality of being firm and unwavering in principle or action.
His erectness of posture conveyed an air of authority. 他的挺拔姿态流露出一种权威感。
The erectnesses of the soldiers during the parade were impressive.  阅兵式上士兵们的挺拔姿态令人印象深刻。
近义词: uprightness, straightness, firmness, rigidity
反义词: flaccidity, limpness, droop
