moondog:/ˈmuːnˌdɒɡ/ (US) /ˈmuːnˌdɒɡ/ (UK)| n. |幻日, 月虹旁的淡光|A bright spot of light sometimes seen near the moon, caused by the refraction of moonlight by ice crystals in the atmosphere.  It's a relatively rare atmospheric phenomenon.|We saw a moondog last night, it was quite beautiful. 昨晚我们看到了一个幻日，非常漂亮。|Synonyms: paraselene, mock moon|Antonyms: N/A|noun


moondog:/ˈmuːnˌdɒɡ/ (US) /ˈmuːnˌdɒɡ/ (UK)| n. |(俚语)流浪汉, 无业游民|A homeless person, especially one who wanders the streets.|He lived as a moondog for years, sleeping under bridges. 他多年来像个流浪汉一样生活，睡在桥下。|Synonyms: vagrant, tramp, hobo|Antonyms: N/A|noun
