bookishness:/ˈbʊkɪʃnəs/
n.
书呆子气，学究气
The quality of being excessively fond of reading and studying; scholarly or pedantic in appearance, manner, or interest.
He was known for his bookishness and quiet demeanor. 他以书呆子气和安静的举止闻名。
近义词：studiousness, scholarly, pedantry, academic, intellectualism
反义词：worldliness, practicality, ignorance, illiteracy
词性：noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From bookish + -ness.  "Bookish" comes from "book" (Old English "boc") + "-ish" (Old English "-isc," a suffix forming adjectives).
