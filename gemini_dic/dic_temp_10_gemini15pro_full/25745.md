buprestidan: /ˌbjuːprɪˈstaɪdən/ /ˌbjuːprɪˈstiːdən/
adjective |吉丁虫科的；属于吉丁虫科的| Relating to or belonging to the family Buprestidae, a family of beetles known as jewel beetles or metallic wood-boring beetles.
The buprestidan collection at the museum is quite extensive.  博物馆的吉丁虫收藏非常丰富。
The iridescent colors of the buprestidan elytra are mesmerizing. 吉丁虫鞘翅的彩虹色令人着迷。
近义词: (No single perfect synonym, but could use descriptive phrases) metallic wood-boring beetle, jewel beetle
反义词: None
词性: adjective
