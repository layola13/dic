contributes:/kənˈtrɪbjuːts/ /ˈkɑːntrɪˌbjuːts/
verb
贡献，捐献；促成，有助于
gives something, especially money or goods, to help achieve or provide something; helps to cause or bring about
Her family has contributed $50,000 to the fund. 她的家人向该基金捐赠了 5 万美元。
Regular exercise contributes to good health. 经常锻炼有助于身体健康。
contribute, donate, give, provide
neglect, ignore
v.
