outdistrict:/ˌaʊtˈdɪstrɪkt/
adjective
外围区域的；郊区的；偏远地区的
Located outside of the main district or central area; peripheral.
People in the outdistricts complained about the lack of public transportation.  郊区的人们抱怨缺乏公共交通。
The outdistrict schools often have fewer resources than those in the city.  偏远地区的学校往往比城市里的学校资源少。
synonyms: outlying, peripheral, remote, suburban, rural
antonyms: central, urban, metropolitan
词性: 形容词
