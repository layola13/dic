melanin: /ˈmɛlənɪn/ (US), /ˈmɛlənɪn/ (UK)| n.|黑色素|A dark brown to black pigment occurring in the hair, skin, and iris of the eye in people and animals. It is responsible for tanning of skin exposed to sunlight.| People with darker skin have more melanin.  皮肤较黑的人有更多的黑色素。| pigment, coloring | N/A | noun
