briefs:/briːfs/
n.|内裤， briefs; 短裤， briefs; 摘要， briefs; 案件摘要, briefs|Underwear covering the groin and buttocks, often worn under other clothing.  A concise summary of a legal case or other document. |He forgot to pack his briefs. 他忘了带内裤。 The lawyer reviewed the briefs before the trial. 律师在庭审前审阅了案件摘要。|近义词:underpants, panties, summary, abstract|反义词:(For underwear) outerwear, (for legal briefs) full document|词性:noun

briefs:/briːfs/
v.|向...介绍基本情况， briefs; 给...指示, briefs|To instruct or inform someone thoroughly about a task or situation. |The general briefed his troops before the battle. 将军在战斗前向他的部队作了指示。  The team was briefed on the new project. 团队听取了关于新项目的简报。|近义词:inform, instruct, advise|反义词: withhold information|词性:verb
