condensation:/ˌkɒndenˈseɪʃn/ (UK), /ˌkɑːndenˈseɪʃn/ (US)| n.|冷凝，凝结，浓缩，缩写|The process by which a gas or vapor changes to a liquid.|The condensation on the window was caused by the difference in temperature inside and out. 窗户上的凝结是由于内外温差造成的。|synonyms: liquefaction, precipitation|antonyms: evaporation, vaporization|noun

condensation:/ˌkɒndenˈseɪʃn/ (UK), /ˌkɑːndenˈseɪʃn/ (US)| n.|浓缩，精简，缩写|A shortened version of something, such as a book or play.|The condensation of the original novel lost much of the author's subtle nuances.  这部小说的缩写版失去了原作者许多微妙的细节。|synonyms: abridgment, summary|antonyms: expansion, elaboration|noun
