cage:/keɪdʒ/| n. vt.|笼子，囚禁|A structure of bars or wires in which birds or other animals are confined.  A cage can also be used figuratively to describe a restrictive situation or feeling.|The bird sang sweetly in its cage.  这只鸟在笼子里甜美地歌唱。|The office felt like a cage. 这办公室感觉像个牢笼。|近义词：pen, enclosure, jail, prison|反义词:freedom, liberty|noun
cage:/keɪdʒ/| vt.|把…关进笼子，限制|To confine in or as if in a cage.|He caged the lion. 他把狮子关进笼子里。|Her fears caged her spirit. 她的恐惧禁锢了她的精神。|近义词：confine, imprison, restrict|反义词:liberate, free|verb
