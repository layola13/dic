Simple Present:
Heavy rain causes flooding.  暴雨会导致洪水。
Stress causes many health problems. 压力会导致许多健康问题。

Present Continuous:
The construction noise is causing a disturbance. 施工噪音正在造成干扰。
His behavior is causing concern among his friends. 他的行为正在引起朋友们的担忧。

Present Perfect:
The pandemic has caused significant economic disruption.  疫情已经造成了严重的经济混乱。
His carelessness has caused several accidents. 他的粗心已经导致了几起事故。

Present Perfect Continuous:
The leak has been causing problems for weeks. 泄漏已经造成数周的问题。
The traffic has been causing delays all morning.  交通拥堵导致了整个上午的延误。

Simple Past:
The fire caused extensive damage. 火灾造成了广泛的损害。
The earthquake caused widespread panic. 地震引起了广泛的恐慌。

Past Continuous:
The road closure was causing traffic jams. 道路封闭造成了交通堵塞。
Her constant complaining was causing tension in the office. 她不断的抱怨在办公室里造成了紧张气氛。

Past Perfect:
The storm had caused significant damage before we arrived. 在我们到达之前，暴风雨已经造成了严重的破坏。
The power outage had caused chaos in the city.  停电在城市中造成了混乱。

Past Perfect Continuous:
The dripping faucet had been causing a stain on the sink. 滴水的水龙头一直在水槽上造成污渍。
His negative attitude had been causing problems for the team. 他的消极态度一直给团队带来问题。

Simple Future:
The new law will cause some controversy. 新法律将引起一些争议。
The rising sea levels will cause coastal flooding. 海平面上升将导致沿海洪水。

Future Continuous:
The roadworks will be causing delays next week.  下周道路施工将造成延误。
The demonstration will be causing disruption to traffic. 示威活动将扰乱交通。

Future Perfect:
By next year, the project will have caused a significant shift in the market. 到明年，该项目将引起市场上的重大转变。
By the time you arrive, the medication will have caused the pain to subside.  到你到达时，药物将使疼痛消退。

Future Perfect Continuous:
By 2030, climate change will have been causing more extreme weather events. 到2030年，气候变化将导致更多极端天气事件。
By the end of the decade, the new technology will have been causing a revolution in communication. 到本世纪末，新技术将在通信领域引发一场革命。


Passive Voice:
The accident was caused by a mechanical failure. 事故是由机械故障引起的。
The damage was caused by the storm.  损害是由暴风雨造成的。


Interrogative:
What caused the accident?  是什么导致了这起事故？
What is causing the delay? 是什么导致了延误？


Negative:
The rain didn't cause the flood. 雨水没有造成洪水。
The new policy won't cause any problems. 新政策不会造成任何问题。
