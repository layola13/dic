overcommercialization: /ˌoʊvər kəˈmɜːrʃələˌzeɪʃən/ /ˌəʊvə kəˈmɜːʃəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|过度商业化|The excessive use of commercial strategies, often to the detriment of other values.|The overcommercialization of Christmas has led to a focus on buying gifts rather than the religious significance of the holiday. 圣诞节的过度商业化导致人们关注购买礼物，而不是这个节日的宗教意义。|commercialism, marketization| restraint, moderation|noun
