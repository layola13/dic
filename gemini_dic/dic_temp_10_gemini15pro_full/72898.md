follows: /ˈfɒləʊz/ (British) /ˈfɑːloʊz/ (American)
| v. (follows, following, followed) | 跟随，接着，理解，密切关注 |  Comes after something in time, order, or position; acts in accordance with something; understands something; pays close attention to something. | He follows his father's footsteps and became a doctor. 他追随父亲的脚步，成为了一名医生。|近义词：ensues, succeeds, pursues, obeys, comprehends |反义词：precedes, leads, disobeys, ignores |词性:动词

follows: /ˈfɒləʊz/ (British) /ˈfɑːloʊz/ (American)
| n. |  追随者，信徒 | People who follow a particular person, belief, or activity.  | The guru had many devoted follows.  这位大师有很多忠实的追随者。|近义词：supporters, adherents, disciples |反义词：opponents, detractors |词性:名词


词源:  From Middle English *folowen*, from Old English *folgian* (“to follow”), from Proto-Germanic *fulgijaną* (“to follow”), from Proto-Indo-European *pleḱ- (“to plait, braid, turn, twist”).
