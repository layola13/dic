glossolalia:/ˌɡlɒsəˈleɪliə/ (US) /ˌɡlɒsəˈleɪlɪə/ (UK)| n. |  说方言；语言混乱|The unintelligible speech in an ecstatic religious experience, sometimes interpreted as the language of angels or of God.  It is also known as speaking in tongues.|She experienced glossolalia during the religious service.  她在宗教仪式上经历了说方言。|Near synonyms:  speaking in tongues, xenoglossy|Antonyms: articulate speech, clear communication|noun
