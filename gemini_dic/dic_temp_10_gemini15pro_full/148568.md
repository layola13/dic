overtaker: /ˌəʊvərˈteɪkər/ (UK), /ˌoʊvərˈteɪkər/ (US)
| n. | 超车者；赶超者 | Someone who overtakes another vehicle or person. | The overtaker was driving recklessly. 超车者鲁莽驾驶。| Near Synonyms: passer, outstripper | Antonyms: laggard, straggler | noun
overtake: /ˌəʊvərˈteɪk/ (UK), /ˌoʊvərˈteɪk/ (US)
| vt. | 超车；赶上；超过 | To catch up with and pass another vehicle or person. | He overtook the truck on the highway. 他在高速公路上超过了卡车。 | Near Synonyms: pass, outstrip | Antonyms: lag behind, fall behind | verb
