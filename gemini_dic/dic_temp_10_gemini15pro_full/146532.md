overglamorize: /ˌoʊvərˈɡlæməraɪz/ | v. | 过分美化；过分渲染；| To make something seem more glamorous or exciting than it actually is. | The movie overglamorized the life of a rock star, showing only the parties and fame, not the hard work and struggles.  这部电影过分美化了摇滚明星的生活，只展示了派对和名声，没有展现努力和奋斗。

The advertisement overglamorized the effects of the diet pills.  这则广告夸大了减肥药的效果。 | 近义词：exaggerate, romanticize, idealize | 反义词：downplay, understate, minimize | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "glamorize" (from "glamour" + "-ize," meaning "to make glamorous")
