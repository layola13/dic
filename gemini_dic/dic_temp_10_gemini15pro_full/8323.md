anti-patriot:/ˌæntiˈpeɪtriət/ |n.|反爱国者，不爱国的人|A person who does not love or support their country, or who actively opposes it. | He was branded an anti-patriot for speaking out against the war. 他因公开反对战争而被贴上不爱国者的标签。|The anti-patriot actively protested against the government's policies.  这个不爱国者积极抗议政府的政策。|Synonym: traitor, dissident, rebel |Antonym: patriot, nationalist| noun

anti-patriot:/ˌæntiˈpeɪtriət/ |adj.|不爱国的，反爱国的|Opposed to or showing a lack of patriotism. |His anti-patriot sentiments shocked many. 他不爱国的言论让许多人感到震惊。|The group spread anti-patriot propaganda. 这个团体散布不爱国的宣传。|Synonym: unpatriotic, disloyal, seditious |Antonym: patriotic, loyal|adjective
