centin:/ˈsɛntɪn/ | n. | 分 |A monetary unit equal to one hundredth of a basic monetary unit.  Derived from the Latin word "centum" meaning hundred.  It is used in various currencies around the world, such as the euro and the US dollar. | I found a centin on the ground. 我在地上发现了一分钱。| synonyms: penny, cent | antonyms: N/A (Units of currency don't typically have antonyms) | noun
