inamiable:/ɪˈneɪmiəbl/ | adj. | 不友好的，不和蔼的 | Not amiable; unfriendly; unpleasant. |  He gave me an inamiable look, clearly displeased with my question. 他不友善地看了我一眼，显然对我的问题很不满。

| Example:  The inamiable shopkeeper refused to accept my return.  那个不友好的店主拒绝接受我的退货。

| Synonyms: unfriendly, disagreeable, hostile, unpleasant, unsociable | Antonyms: amiable, friendly, agreeable, pleasant, sociable | Word type: Adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *inamiabilis, from in- "not" + amiabilis "lovable, amiable."  The root "amare" means "to love" in Latin.
