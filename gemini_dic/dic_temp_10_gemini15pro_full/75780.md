fumacious:/fjuːˈmeɪʃəs/| adj.|冒烟的；烟雾弥漫的|Producing or full of smoke or fumes; smoky.|The fumacious factory polluted the air. 这家冒烟的工厂污染了空气。|例句：The volcano emitted a fumacious plume. 火山喷发出烟雾弥漫的羽流。|近义词：smoky, fumy, smoldering|反义词：clear, clean|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Latin *fumaceus (“smoky”), from fumus (“smoke”).
