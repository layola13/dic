crankish: /ˈkræŋkɪʃ/ | adj. | 脾气古怪的；易怒的；古怪的 |  Having strange or eccentric ideas or habits; irritable.  Derived from "crank" (n.), referring to a person with eccentric or unconventional ideas. | He's always been a bit crankish, but he seems even more irritable lately. 他一直有点脾气古怪，但最近似乎更加易怒了。| The old woman had some crankish ideas about health and refused to see a doctor.  这位老妇人对健康有一些古怪的想法，拒绝去看医生。| synonyms: eccentric, crotchety, grumpy, quirky, peculiar | antonyms: easygoing, affable, amiable, pleasant | adjective
