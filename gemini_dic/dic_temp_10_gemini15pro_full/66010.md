examiners:/ɪɡˈzæmɪnərz/| n. |审查员，考官|People who are officially appointed to test students or candidates in an examination. |The examiners were very strict with the marking. 考官的评分很严格。| The examiners will assess the candidates' knowledge and skills. 考官将评估候选人的知识和技能。| assessors, testers, invigilators |  | Noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *examinour*, from Anglo-Norman *examinur, examinour*, from Old French *examinour*, agent noun from *examiner* (“to examine”). Equivalent to *examine* + *-er*.
