mussulmanish:/mʌˈsʌlmənɪʃ/
adjective
穆斯林式的；像穆斯林的
Having qualities or characteristics perceived as typical of Muslims, often used in a derogatory or condescending way.
He spoke in a mussulmanish tone, which irritated some of the listeners. 他说话的语气像穆斯林，这激怒了一些听众。
Near Synonyms: Muslim-like, Islamic-like, Moorish
Antonyms: Non-Muslim, Westernized
