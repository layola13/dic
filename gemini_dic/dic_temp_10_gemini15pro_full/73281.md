foreboder:/fɔːrˈboʊdər/ | n. |预兆者，预言者|Someone or something that forebodes or foreshadows a future event, usually something unpleasant.|The raven is a classic foreboder of doom in literature.  乌鸦在文学作品中是厄运的经典预兆。|近义词：harbinger, omen, portent|反义词: None (lack of a direct antonym, could be considered as 'sign of good fortune')|词性: noun

forebode:/fɔːrˈboʊd/ | v. |预示，预兆|To predict or foreshadow a future event, usually something unpleasant.|The dark clouds forebode a storm. 乌云预示着暴风雨。|近义词：presage, augur, portend|反义词: None (lack of a direct antonym)|词性: verb
