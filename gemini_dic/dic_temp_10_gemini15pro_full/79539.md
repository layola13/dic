glochidiate:/ˌɡloʊˈkɪdiət/| adj.|具钩毛的，有芒刺的|Covered with barbed bristles or hairs; having glochids.  Referring to the small, barbed bristles found on cacti, especially Opuntia species.|例句：The glochidiate spines of the prickly pear cactus can be quite irritating to the skin. 仙人掌的钩毛刺会刺激皮肤。

例句：Handle cholla cacti with care, as their glochidiate spines are easily detached and difficult to remove. 小心处理cholla仙人掌，因为它们的钩毛刺很容易脱落且难以去除。|近义词：barbed, bristly, prickly|反义词:smooth, soft|词性:adjective

词源: From Latin *glochidium,* diminutive of Greek *glōkhís* "point of an arrow, barb of a hook," from *glōssā* "tongue."
