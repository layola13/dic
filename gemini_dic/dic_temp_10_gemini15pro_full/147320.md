overmoralizing: /ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrəˌlaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 过分道德化的，过分说教的 |  Engaging in or characterized by excessive moralizing; imposing or tending to impose moral judgments or pronouncements to an excessive degree.  |  He's always overmoralizing about everything, it's so exhausting.  他总是对每件事都过分道德化，真让人筋疲力尽。|  sanctimonious, preachy, moralistic | permissive, lenient | adjective


overmoralize: /ˌoʊvərˈmɔːrəˌlaɪz/ | v. | 过分道德化，过分说教 |  To moralize excessively; to preach or pronounce moral judgments to an excessive degree. | She tends to overmoralize about people's choices. 她倾向于对人们的选择进行过分的说教。| preach, sermonize, lecture |  tolerate, condone | verb

词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessive") + moralize (from moral + -ize, meaning "to make moral")
