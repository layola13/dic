misfields:/ˌmɪsˈfiːldz/
| n. |失误，漏球，防守失误|An act of misfielding a ball in a sport such as baseball or cricket.  The act of incorrectly fielding a ball. |The shortstop's misfield cost them the game. |近义词：error, blunder, fumble|反义词：perfect catch, clean play|noun
misfield:/ˌmɪsˈfiːld/
| v. |失误，漏接（球）|To field (a ball) incorrectly. |He misfielded the grounder and the runner reached first base. 他漏接了地滚球，跑垒员到达了一垒。|近义词：miss, fumble, drop|反义词：catch, field cleanly|verb
