mannitol:/ˈmænɪtɒl/ (US) /ˈmænɪtɔːl/ (UK)| n. |甘露醇|A white crystalline alcohol found in many plants and used as a diuretic and a sweetener.  It is a sugar alcohol derived from mannose.|Mannitol is used to reduce swelling in the brain. 甘露醇用于减轻脑肿胀。|Mannitol is often used as a sweetener in sugar-free candies. 甘露醇常被用作无糖糖果的甜味剂。|Synonyms:  osmitrol, diuretic|Antonyms:  None|noun

mannose:/ˈmænoʊs/ (US) /ˈmænoʊs/ (UK)| n. |甘露糖|A simple sugar that occurs naturally in some fruits and is a constituent of many polysaccharides.|Mannose is a type of sugar found in cranberries.  甘露糖是一种存在于蔓越莓中的糖。|Mannose can be used as a dietary supplement. 甘露糖可以用作膳食补充剂。|Synonyms:  hexose|Antonyms:  None|noun
