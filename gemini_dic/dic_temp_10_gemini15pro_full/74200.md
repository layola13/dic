fourneau:/fuːrˈnoʊ/ (US), /fʊrˈnəʊ/ (UK)| n.|炉子，火炉，熔炉|A structure or appliance for cooking or heating, typically using solid fuel.  A furnace, stove, or oven.|例句：The baker put the bread in the fourneau. 面包师把面包放进炉子里。|例句：The old fourneau heated the entire house. 老式火炉温暖了整栋房子。|近义词：oven, stove, furnace, kiln|反义词:refrigerator, freezer|词性:noun
Etymology: From Old French *fornel, diminutive of forn "oven", from Latin furnus.
