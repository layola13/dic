intended:/ɪnˈtɛndɪd/ /ɪnˈtɛndəd/ | adj. | 预定的；打算的；计划的 | Planned or meant. |  
She married her intended husband. 她嫁给了她预定的丈夫。
The book is intended for children. 这本书是为孩子们准备的。

近义词: planned, meant, designed, proposed, aimed
反义词: unintended, accidental, unplanned

词性: adjective
