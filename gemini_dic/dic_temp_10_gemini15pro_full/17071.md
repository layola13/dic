batik:/ˈbætɪk/ | n. |蜡染; 蜡染布|A method of dyeing fabric by covering parts of it with a coat of wax which prevents the dye from reaching the cloth, typically creating patterns.| The museum has a fine collection of Indonesian batiks.  这家博物馆收藏了精美的印度尼西亚蜡染作品。|synonyms: tie-dye, resist dyeing| antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific dyeing technique) | noun


batik:/ˈbætɪk/ | v. |用蜡染法染色|To dye (fabric) using batik.|  She batiked a beautiful scarf with a floral design. 她用花卉图案蜡染了一条漂亮的围巾。|synonyms: tie-dye, resist dye| antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific dyeing technique) | verb
