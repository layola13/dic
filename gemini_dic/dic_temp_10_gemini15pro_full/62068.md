Example 2: The museum's collection boasts several exquisite encaumas from the 18th century.  这家博物馆的收藏品中有多幅18世纪的精美搪瓷画。 | Synonym: enamel painting, porcelain painting | Antonym: (No direct antonym, could consider fresco, mural for different painting techniques) | Noun
