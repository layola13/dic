arthen:/ˈɑːrθən/ | adj. | 土制的，陶制的 | Made of earth or clay.  | The arthen pot cracked in the fire. 土制罐在火中裂开了。 | 近义词: earthenware, ceramic, clay | 反义词: metal, plastic, wooden | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Middle English *erthen, from Old English *eorþen, from Proto-Germanic *erþīnaz, from Proto-Indo-European *erh₂- (“earth, ground”).  Cognate with Dutch aarden (“earthen”), German irden (“earthen”), Icelandic jarðinn (“earthen”).
