duetted:/duːˈɛtɪd/
verb
二重唱，二重奏
performed a duet, sang or played a duet
例句：They duetted beautifully at the concert. 他们在音乐会上进行了精彩的二重唱。

例句：The two singers duetted on a classic love song. 两位歌手二重唱了一首经典的爱情歌曲。

近义词：harmonized, collaborated, performed together
反义词：soloed, performed alone
词性：v.
