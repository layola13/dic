hypnotised:/ˈhɪpnətaɪzd/ /ˈhɪp.nə.taɪzd/ | adj. |被催眠的；着迷的|Influenced or controlled by hypnosis; captivated or fascinated.|例句：The audience sat hypnotised by the magician's performance. 观众被魔术师的表演迷住了。

例句：She was hypnotised by the flickering flames in the fireplace. 她被壁炉里闪烁的火焰迷住了。|近义词：mesmerized, entranced, captivated, spellbound, fascinated|反义词：alert, aware, conscious, uninterested|词性:形容词

hypnotize:/ˈhɪpnətaɪz/ /ˈhɪp.nə.taɪz/ | vt. |催眠；使着迷|To induce a state of hypnosis; to fascinate or captivate.|例句：The therapist hypnotized the patient to help her overcome her fear of flying. 治疗师对病人进行催眠，以帮助她克服飞行恐惧症。

例句：The beauty of the sunset hypnotized us. 日落的美丽使我们着迷。|近义词：mesmerize, entrance, captivate, spellbind, fascinate|反义词：bore, disenchant, repulse|词性:动词
