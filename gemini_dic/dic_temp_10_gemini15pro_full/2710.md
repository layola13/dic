adsorbabilities: /ædˌsɔːrbəˈbɪlɪtiz/ | n. | 吸附能力，吸附性 | The adsorbabilities of different materials vary greatly. 不同材料的吸附能力差异很大。 |  The researchers compared the adsorbabilities of activated carbon and zeolites for removing pollutants from water. 研究人员比较了活性炭和沸石去除水中污染物的吸附能力。 | 近义词：adsorption capacities, uptake capacities | 反义词：desorbabilities | 词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From adsorb + -ability + -ities.  Adsorb comes from ad- (to) + sorbere (to suck in).
