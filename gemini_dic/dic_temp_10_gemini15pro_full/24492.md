brize:/braɪz/| v. |烤，炙烤|To cook meat by grilling or roasting over coals or an open fire.|The chef brized the chicken over an open flame.  厨师在明火上烤鸡肉。|例句：They brized the lamb for the feast. 他们为宴会烤了羊肉。|近义词：grill, roast, broil|反义词: freeze, chill|词性:动词
词源: From Middle English *brisen, from Old French *brisier (modern French briser), meaning "to break, shatter," from Frankish *brīsjan. The semantic shift likely occurred because the cooking method can cause the meat to become tender and easily broken apart.
