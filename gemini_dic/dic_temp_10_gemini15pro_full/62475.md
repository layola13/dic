endosmometric:/ˌɛndɒzˈmɒtrɪk/
adjective
内渗的
Relating to or exhibiting endosmosis; characterized by the inward flow of a solvent through a semipermeable membrane towards a solution of higher concentration.
The endosmometric pressure caused the cell to swell. 内渗压导致细胞膨胀。

近义词:  inward osmotic
反义词: exosmometric

词性: adjective
