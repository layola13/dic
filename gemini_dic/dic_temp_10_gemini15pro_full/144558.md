outwalked:/ˌaʊtˈwɔːkt/
verb
胜过…的步行速度；比…走得更远；比…走得更久
to walk faster, farther, or longer than someone else.
I easily outwalked my friends on the hike. 我在徒步旅行中轻松地比我的朋友们走得更远。
outshine, outrun, outswim
be outwalked by
