herborization:/ˌhɜːrbərɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.| 植物采集; 植物标本制作|The herborization of medicinal plants requires careful selection and preservation.  药用植物的采集需要仔细的选择和保存。|The class went on a herborization trip to the nearby forest. 这个班级去附近的森林进行了一次植物采集之旅。|近义词: plant collecting, botanical collecting|反义词: None|词性: noun


Etymology: From French *herborisation*, from *herboriser* "to collect herbs," from herbe "herb" (from Latin *herba*) + -iser (from Latin -izare).
