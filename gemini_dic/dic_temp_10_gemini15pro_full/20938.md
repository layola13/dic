bleck:/blɛk/
interjection|呕，呸；表示厌恶|An exclamation of disgust.|Bleck, this milk is sour! 呸，这牛奶馊了！
近义词：yuck, ugh, ew
反义词:yum, delicious


词性:interjection
英文解析:An exclamation used to express disgust or aversion.
例句：Bleck, this spinach tastes awful! 呸，这菠菜难吃死了！

例句：Seeing the rotten food made him say, "Bleck!" 看到腐烂的食物，他说道：“呸！”


词源:Likely imitative of the sound of vomiting.  It's a relatively recent addition to the English lexicon, becoming popular in the late 20th century.
