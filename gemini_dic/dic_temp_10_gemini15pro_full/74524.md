fraternity:/frəˈtɜːrnəti/
n.
兄弟会，博爱，友爱
a group of people sharing a common profession or interests.  A male student society in a university or college.
例句：He joined a fraternity in his first year of college.  他大学一年级时加入了一个兄弟会。

例句：There's a strong sense of fraternity among the firefighters. 消防队员之间有一种强烈的兄弟情谊。

近义词：brotherhood, fellowship, sodality
反义词：sorority, hostility, enmity
词性: noun


fraternal:/frəˈtɜːrnl/
adj.
兄弟般的，友好的
of or like a brother or brothers.
例句：He felt a fraternal bond with his teammates. 他和队友之间有一种兄弟般的情谊。

例句：The two organizations have a close fraternal relationship. 这两个组织有着密切的兄弟般的关系。

近义词：brotherly, friendly, amicable
反义词：unbrotherly, unfriendly, hostile
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fraternitas, from Latin fraternus (“brotherly”), from frater (“brother”).
