cognitive:/ˈkɒɡnɪtɪv/ (UK) /ˈkɑːɡnɪtɪv/ (US)| adj.|认知的，认识的|Relating to the mental processes of perception, memory, judgment, and reasoning, as contrasted with emotional or volitional processes.|例句：Cognitive psychology is the study of mental processes. 认知心理学是对心理过程的研究。|近义词：mental, intellectual, rational|反义词：emotional, instinctive|词性: adjective

cognitive:/ˈkɒɡnɪtɪv/ (UK) /ˈkɑːɡnɪtɪv/ (US)| n.|认知能力|The mental action or process of acquiring knowledge and understanding through thought, experience, and the senses.|例句：Her cognitive abilities are impressive. 她的认知能力令人印象深刻。|近义词：intellect, mind, understanding|反义词：ignorance|词性: noun
