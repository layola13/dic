carpophyll: /ˈkɑːrpəfɪl/ | n. | 心皮; 果叶 | A modified leaf that forms the whole or part of the pistil of a flower. | The carpophyll encloses the ovules which develop into seeds after fertilization. 心皮包裹着胚珠，受精后胚珠发育成种子。| The lily has three carpophylls fused together to form a single pistil. 百合花有三片心皮融合在一起形成一个雌蕊。| synonym: megasporophyll | antonym:  N/A | noun
