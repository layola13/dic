anticlassicism:/ˌæntiˈklæsɪsɪzəm/
n.|反古典主义|Opposition to or rejection of the principles of classicism, especially in art, literature, and music.|The rise of Romanticism in the 19th century marked a strong wave of anticlassicism. 19世纪浪漫主义的兴起标志着反古典主义浪潮的强劲来袭。|
|Anticlassicism rejected the strict rules and formal elegance of classicism, favoring more emotional and expressive forms. 反古典主义反对古典主义严格的规则和形式上的优雅，更倾向于更具情感和表现力的形式。|synonyms: romanticism, modernism|antonyms: classicism|noun
