ingluvial:/ɪnˈɡluːviəl/
adjective
嗉囊的; 食管的
Relating to, or situated in, the crop or craw of a bird or other animal.
The ingluvial ganglion is a nerve center located near the bird's crop.  鸟类的嗉囊神经节位于嗉囊附近。
Birds soften seeds in their ingluvial pouch before digestion. 鸟类在消化之前会在嗉囊中软化种子。
近义词:crop, craw
反义词:None
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin ingluviēs (“crop, craw”), from in- (“in, into”) + gluvire (“to swallow”).
