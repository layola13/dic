dissentiate:/dɪˈsɛnʃɪˌeɪt/
verb
持异议；不同意
to disagree with or oppose an opinion, belief, or decision
Several board members dissented from the chairman's proposal. 几位董事会成员不同意主席的提议。
近义词: disagree, dissent, object, oppose
反义词: agree, concur, assent
词性: verb
词源: From Latin dissentiāre, present active infinitive of dissentiō (“I disagree”), from dis- (“apart, away”) + sentiō (“I feel, perceive, think”).
