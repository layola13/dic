hinder:/ˈhɪndər/ (US), /ˈhɪndə(r)/ (UK)| v. |阻碍，妨碍|To make it more difficult for something to happen or for someone to do something.|The heavy rain hindered our progress. 大雨阻碍了我们的前进。|近义词：obstruct, impede, hamper, inhibit|反义词：help, aid, assist, facilitate|词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *hindren, from Old English hindrian (“to hinder, restrain, prevent, check, hold back, keep back, withhold”), from Proto-Germanic *hindrōną (“to hinder, restrain”). Cognate with Old Saxon hindaron, Old Norse hindra, Old High German hintarōn, Gothic  hindran.
