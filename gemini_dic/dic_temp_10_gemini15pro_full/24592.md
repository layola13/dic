brogan:/ˈbroʊɡən/| n.|粗革皮鞋，高帮工作靴|A heavy, sturdy work shoe, typically made of leather and reaching to the ankle or above.|例句：He wore a pair of well-worn brogans. 他穿着一双很旧的粗革皮鞋。

例句：The miners all wore brogans to protect their feet. 矿工们都穿着高帮工作靴来保护他们的脚。|近义词：boot, work shoe, work boot|反义词:slipper, sandal|noun
