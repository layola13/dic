flirtatious:/flɜːrˈteɪʃəs/
adjective
轻浮的；调情的；爱调情的
Behaving in such a way as to suggest a playful sexual attraction to someone.
She gave him a flirtatious smile. 她给了他一个调情的微笑。
Synonyms: coquettish, playful, teasing, seductive, alluring
Antonyms: serious, reserved, modest, shy, unapproachable
词性: 形容词
