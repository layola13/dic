cognoscence:/ˌkɒɡˈnɒsəns/ /ˌkɑːɡˈnɑːsəns/| n. |认知；认识；知识|The act or process of knowing; perception; knowledge.|He demonstrated a keen cognoscence of the political landscape. 他展现出对政治局势敏锐的认知。|近义词：knowledge, awareness, comprehension, understanding, perception, apprehension, discernment|反义词：ignorance, unawareness, incomprehension|词性:名词

Etymology: From Latin *cognoscenti*, from *cognoscere* "to know," from *co-* "together" + *gnoscere* "to know," related to *noscere* "to come to know."
