arrivistes:/ˌærɪˈviːsts/ | n. |暴发户，新贵，野心家|  A person who has recently reached a position of power or success, especially in a social group, but is considered to lack the experience, knowledge, or refinement appropriate to it.  Someone who is considered an upstart, often seen as pushy or ostentatious. | The city was filled with arrivistes eager to flaunt their newfound wealth.  这座城市里到处都是渴望炫耀自己新财富的暴发户。

The old money families looked down on the arrivistes who had recently joined their social circle.  那些老牌富豪家族看不起最近加入他们社交圈的暴发户。 | 近义词：upstart, parvenu, social climber, nouveau riche | 反义词：established member, old money, aristocrat | 词性：名词
