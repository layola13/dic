hightail:/ˈhaɪteɪl/| v. | 迅速离开，逃之夭夭 | To leave somewhere quickly, often because you are frightened or embarrassed. |  Example 1: When they saw the police, they hightailed it out of there. 当他们看到警察时，他们迅速逃离了那里。

Example 2: He hightailed it back to his apartment after the awkward encounter.  尴尬的相遇后，他迅速跑回了自己的公寓。 | 近义词: flee, run away, escape, bolt | 反义词: approach, arrive, stay | 词性: 动词
