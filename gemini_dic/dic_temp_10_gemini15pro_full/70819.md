finical:/fɪˈnɪk(ə)l/ /fəˈnɪk(ə)l/
adjective
过分讲究的，挑剔的，苛求的
Excessively particular or fastidious, especially about details of dress, food, or other material things.
She's so finical about her food that she drives everyone crazy. 她对食物过于挑剔，让每个人都抓狂。

He was finical about his appearance, always adjusting his tie and smoothing his hair.  他非常在意自己的外表，总是整理领带和抚平头发。

近义词: fussy, particular, fastidious, picky, choosy, selective, discriminating, dainty, squeamish
反义词: unfussy, easygoing, undemanding, tolerant

词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From fine (adj.) + -ical. Originally in the sense of "refined, elegant," but by the late 16th century, it had developed the negative connotation of "overly refined, fastidious."
