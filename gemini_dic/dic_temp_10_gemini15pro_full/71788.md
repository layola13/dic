flecky:/ˈfleki/ (US), /ˈfleki/ (UK)| adj.|有斑点的，斑驳的|Covered with small marks or spots of a different color or texture.|例句：The horse had a flecky coat. 这匹马的毛皮有斑点。|例句：The walls were painted a flecky grey. 墙壁被漆成了斑驳的灰色。|近义词：spotted, mottled, speckled, dappled|反义词：plain, solid, uniform|词性:形容词

flecky: 词源来自古诺尔斯语 *flekkr，意为“斑点”。
