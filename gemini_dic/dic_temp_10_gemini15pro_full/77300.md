gatekeeping:/ˈɡeɪtˌkiːpɪŋ/ | n. |把关，看门，控制信息或资源的获取|The act of controlling, and usually limiting, general access to something.|  Social media platforms are increasingly focused on gatekeeping to combat misinformation. 社交媒体平台越来越注重把关，以打击虚假信息。|  The prestigious university's stringent admissions process is a form of academic gatekeeping.  这所著名大学严格的录取程序是一种学术把关的形式。 | synonyms: guarding, controlling, restricting, vetting | antonyms:  opening, sharing, democratizing | noun

gatekeepings: /ˈɡeɪtˌkiːpɪŋz/ | n. | 把关行为（复数）| Multiple instances of controlling access to something. | The various gatekeepings in the industry prevent newcomers from gaining traction.  该行业的各种把关行为阻止了新人的发展。 |  The conference addressed the gatekeepings that exist within the scientific community.  会议讨论了科学界内部存在的各种把关行为。 | synonyms: controls, restrictions, vettings | antonyms: openings, sharings | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "gate" (a barrier) + "keeping" (the act of maintaining or guarding).  源自“gate”（大门）+“keeping”（维持或守护的行为）。
