defaulters:/dɪˈfɔːltərz/| n.|违约者，拖欠者|A person or entity that fails to fulfill a financial obligation or meet the terms of a loan, contract, or agreement.|Many mortgage defaulters face foreclosure. 许多抵押贷款违约者面临丧失抵押品赎回权。|The company is taking legal action against the defaulters.  这家公司正在对违约者采取法律行动。|Synonyms: non-payers, delinquents, offenders|Antonyms: payers, adherents|noun
