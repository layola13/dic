faint-hearted:/ˈfeɪntˈhɑːrtɪd/| adj.|胆小的，怯懦的|Lacking courage; timid.|例句：He was too faint-hearted to climb the mountain. 他太胆小了，不敢爬山。

例句：She was faint-hearted about speaking in public.  她怯于在公共场合发言。

近义词：timid, cowardly, fearful, apprehensive|反义词：brave, courageous, bold, daring|词性:形容词
