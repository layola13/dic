microbicidal: /ˌmaɪkroʊbɪˈsaɪdəl/
adjective
杀菌的，灭菌的
Destroying microbes.
This disinfectant is microbicidal and effective against a broad range of bacteria. 这种消毒剂具有杀菌作用，对多种细菌有效。
Microbicidal agents are essential in preventing infections. 杀菌剂在预防感染方面至关重要。
近义词:bactericidal, germicidal, disinfectant
反义词:microbistatic
