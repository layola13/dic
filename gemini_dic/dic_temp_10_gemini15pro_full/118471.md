meshy:/ˈmɛʃi/| adj.|网状的，多孔的|Resembling a mesh; having many small holes or openings.|The fisherman cast his meshy net into the sea. 渔夫把他的网状渔网撒入大海。|近义词：netlike, reticulated, laced, open-weave|反义词:solid, dense, compact|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From mesh + -y.  "Mesh" comes from the Old English *masc or *measc, meaning "loop" or "space in a net," related to Dutch maesche and German Masche. The suffix "-y" forms adjectives meaning "full of" or "characterized by."
