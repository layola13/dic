misrewards:/ˌmɪsrɪˈwɔːrdz/
|v.|错误地奖励；给予不当的奖励|To give rewards or incentives in a way that is inappropriate, ineffective, or counterproductive, often reinforcing undesired behavior.|The current system misrewards mediocrity and punishes excellence.  目前的制度错误地奖励了平庸，惩罚了卓越。|The company's bonus structure misrewards short-term gains over long-term growth.  公司的奖金结构错误地奖励了短期收益，而不是长期增长。|misallocate, mismanage|properly reward, incentivize|verb
