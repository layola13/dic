overslight:/ˌoʊvərˈslaɪt/| v.| 轻视，怠慢，忽略 |To treat with too little attention or respect; to neglect. | She felt overslighted when her contributions to the project were not acknowledged. 她觉得自己的贡献没有得到认可，受到了轻视。| He overslighted his duties and was subsequently fired. 他玩忽职守，后来被解雇了。 | 近义词: neglect, ignore, disregard, snub | 反义词: acknowledge, appreciate, recognize | 词性: 动词

词源:
From over- + slight (verb).  The verb "slight" itself comes from Old Norse *sletta, meaning "to slap, strike lightly."  The sense of "treat with disrespect" developed from the idea of treating someone as if they were insignificant enough to be brushed aside with a light blow.
