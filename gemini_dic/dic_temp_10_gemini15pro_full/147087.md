overlicentiousness:/ˌoʊvərlaɪˈsɛnʃəsnəs/
n.
过度放荡，放纵无度
Excessive indulgence in sensual pleasures; lack of restraint, especially in sexual matters.
His overlicentiousness ultimately led to his downfall.  他过度放荡最终导致了他的毁灭。
The Puritans condemned the overlicentiousness of the court. 清教徒谴责了宫廷的放纵。
Synonyms: debauchery, dissoluteness, immodesty, lasciviousness, lewdness, licentiousness, profligacy, wantonness
Antonyms: chastity, decency, moderation, restraint, self-control
noun
