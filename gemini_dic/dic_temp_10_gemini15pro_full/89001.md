hollerer:/ˈhɒlərər/ (US), /ˈhɒlərə(r)/ (UK)| n.|叫喊者；大声喊叫的人|Someone who shouts or yells loudly.|The hollerer at the game was so loud he was almost deafening.  比赛中的那个叫喊者声音太大了，几乎震耳欲聋。|Example: The street hollerer was trying to sell his wares.  那个在街上叫卖的小贩试图卖掉他的商品。|Synonyms: shouter, yeller, screamer|Antonyms: whisperer, murmurer|noun
