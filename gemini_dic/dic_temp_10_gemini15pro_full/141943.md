ordonnance:/ˈɔːrdɪnəns/ /ˈɔːrdnəns/| n.|条例，法令；指令；规定；(尤指旧时的)大炮|An ordinance is an authoritative decree, direction, or law issued by a government, especially a municipal regulation.  It can also refer to a piece of artillery, particularly one used in the past.|The city passed an ordinance banning smoking in public parks.  该市通过了一项禁止在公园吸烟的法令。|The battery of ordnance was positioned on the hill overlooking the valley.  炮兵阵地位于可以俯瞰山谷的山坡上。|Synonyms: decree, law, statute, regulation, edict, canon, injunction; (for artillery) cannon, gun, artillery|Antonyms: suggestion, request, appeal|noun
