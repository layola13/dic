lutheranisms:/ˈluːθərənɪzəmz/
n.
路德教义；路德宗的教义和实践
The doctrines and practices of Lutheranism.
Lutheranisms emphasize the importance of scripture and faith. 路德教义强调圣经和信仰的重要性。
The various Lutheranisms around the world share core beliefs. 世界各地的各种路德教派都有一些共同的核心信仰。
近义词: Lutheran doctrines, Lutheran beliefs
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider other Christian denominations like Catholicism, Calvinism)
词性: noun
