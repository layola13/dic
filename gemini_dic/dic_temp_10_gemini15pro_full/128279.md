neuropodium:/ˈnʊərəˈpoʊdiəm/| n. |神经足|The lateral, fleshy, lobed, or fin-like appendages on each side of a parapodium in many polychaete worms.  It is primarily involved in respiration and locomotion.|The neuropodium of this species of worm is particularly well-developed for swimming.|Synonyms: parapodial lobe, lateral appendage|Antonyms: notopodium|noun
