archenteron:/ˌɑːrkˈɛntərɒn/ (US) /ˌɑːrkˈɛntərən/ (UK)| n. |原肠腔|The central cavity of the gastrula, which ultimately becomes the gut.|The archenteron forms during gastrulation. 原肠腔在原肠胚形成过程中形成。|The archenteron eventually develops into the digestive tract. 原肠腔最终发育成消化道。|Synonyms: gastrocoel|Antonyms: N/A|noun
