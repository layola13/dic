paranoids:/ˈpærənɔɪdz/| n.|偏执狂患者；妄想狂|People suffering from paranoia are called paranoids. 患有偏执狂的人被称为偏执狂患者。|例句：He’s convinced they’re all out to get him, he’s becoming a real paranoid. 他确信他们都想陷害他，他变得越来越偏执。

paranoid:/ˈpærənɔɪd/| adj.|偏执的；妄想的|Excessively or unreasonably suspicious and distrustful. 过度或不合理地怀疑和不信任。|例句：She's getting really paranoid about what other people think of her. 她越来越担心别人对她的看法。|近义词：suspicious, distrustful, wary|反义词：trusting, confiding|词性:形容词

词源: From Greek paranoia "derangement", literally "beside the mind". 来自希腊语 paranoia “精神错乱”，字面意思是“在心灵之外”。
