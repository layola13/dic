guilelessly:/ˈɡaɪllɪsli/| adv.|天真地；坦率地；诚实地|Without guile; innocently. Showing a lack of cunning or deceit; in an open and honest way.|She guilelessly admitted she had eaten the last cookie. 她坦率地承认她吃了最后一块饼干。|例句：He guilelessly believed everything she told him. 他天真地相信她所说的一切。|近义词：innocently, naively, artlessly, candidly, frankly, honestly|反义词：craftily, deceitfully, slyly, cunningly|词性:副词
