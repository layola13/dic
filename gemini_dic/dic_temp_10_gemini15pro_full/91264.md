hydrogalvanic:/ˌhaɪdrəʊɡælˈvænɪk/
adjective
水电的；流电水的；由水流产生的电流的
Relating to or denoting electricity generated by the action of water, especially on dissimilar metals.
Hydrogalvanic corrosion can be a serious problem in marine environments.  水电腐蚀在海洋环境中可能是一个严重的问题。
The hydrogalvanic effect can be used to generate small amounts of electricity.  水电效应可以用来产生少量电力。
近义词: electrochemical
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-electrochemical)
词性: adjective
