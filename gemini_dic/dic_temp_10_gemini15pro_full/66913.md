expellable:/ɪkˈspɛləbl/
adjective
可驱逐的，可开除的
Able to be expelled or dismissed from a school, organization, or country.
The student's behavior was so disruptive that he was deemed expellable.  这个学生的行为如此具有破坏性，以至于他被认为应该开除。
His repeated violations of school rules made him expellable. 他屡次违反校规，使他可以被开除。
近义词: dismissable, removable, ejectable
反义词: admissible, acceptable
