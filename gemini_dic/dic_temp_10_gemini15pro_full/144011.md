outrebounds:/ˌaʊtˈriːbaʊndz/
|n.|篮板球（尤指进攻篮板）|The act of gaining possession of the basketball after a missed shot, especially on the offensive end. |The Lakers outrebounded the Celtics, grabbing 15 offensive rebounds.|近义词: rebounds, boards|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "giving up rebounds")|词性:名词


outrebound:/ˌaʊtˈriːbaʊnd/
|vt.|在篮板球方面胜过|To gain possession of more rebounds than the opposing team. |The center outrebounded everyone on the court.|近义词: out-rebound, dominate the boards|反义词: be outrebounded|词性:动词


Etymology: "Out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "rebound" (from the Old French "rebondir," meaning "to bounce back").
