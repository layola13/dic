omelet:/ˈɑːmlɪt/ (US) /ˈɒmlɪt/ (UK)| n.|煎蛋卷，蛋饼|A dish of beaten eggs cooked in a frying pan until firm, often with a filling of cheese, vegetables, meat, etc.|例句：I had a cheese omelet for breakfast. 我早餐吃了一个奶酪煎蛋卷。

例句：She made a delicious omelet with mushrooms and onions. 她做了一个美味的蘑菇洋葱煎蛋卷。|近义词：fritatta|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

Etymology: From French *omelette*, alteration (influenced by *alumelle*, "knife blade") of *alemelle*, diminutive of *aleme*, from Old French *alume*, *aleume*, "knife blade," from Latin *lamina*.  The dish was so called from its shape.
