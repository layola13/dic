overcharitable:/ˌōvərˈtʃærɪtəbl/
adjective
过度慈善的；过分慷慨的
Excessively charitable or generous, often to the point of naivety or foolishness.
例句：His overcharitable nature often left him vulnerable to being taken advantage of. 他过分慷慨的天性常常让他容易被利用。

例句：It was overcharitable of her to assume everyone had good intentions.  她认为每个人都有好意，这也太过仁慈了。

近义词：overgenerous, too kind, excessively lenient
反义词：stingy, miserly, ungenerous
词性: adjective
