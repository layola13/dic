derricks:/ˈdɛrɪks/ | n. | 起重机，井架 | A tall framework or tower over a deep drill hole, such as an oil well, that supports the drilling equipment. |  The derricks dotted the oil field, pumping black gold from the earth.  井架星罗棋布地散布在油田里，从地下抽取黑色的黄金。

The derricks were silhouetted against the setting sun. 井架在落日余晖的映衬下显得格外醒目。 | synonyms: crane, hoist, rig | antonyms: N/A | noun
