aslake:/əˈsleɪk/
verb
消解；平息；缓和；解（渴）
to lessen the force of (something, such as thirst); satisfy or appease.
The deer aslake their thirst at the stream. 鹿在小溪边解渴。
近义词: quench, satisfy, slake, allay, appease, mitigate
反义词: aggravate, intensify, increase
词性: verb

词源:
From Middle English *aslaken, from Old English āslæccan (“to slacken, loosen, relax, abate, mitigate”), equivalent to a- +‎ slacken. Cognate with Old High German aslāhan (“to become slack, abate, grow weak”). More at a-, slacken.
