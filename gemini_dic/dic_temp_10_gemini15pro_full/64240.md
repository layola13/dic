equipable:/ɪˈkwɪpəbl/
adjective
可装备的，可配备的
able to be equipped or furnished; suitable for being equipped or furnished
The character has several equipable items, including swords, shields, and armor.  这个角色有几件可装备的物品，包括剑、盾和盔甲。
All rooms are equipable with air conditioning units. 所有房间都可以配备空调。
近义词: usable, fittable, attachable
反义词: unequipable, unusable
