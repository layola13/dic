confound:/kənˈfaʊnd/| v. |使困惑；使惊讶；挫败；混淆|To surprise and confuse someone so much that they are unable to react.|The sudden rise in prices confounded economists. 价格的突然上涨让经济学家们感到困惑。|Synonyms: confuse, bewilder, puzzle, baffle|Antonyms: clarify, enlighten|verb

confounds:/kənˈfaʊndz/| v. |(confound的第三人称单数形式) 使困惑；使惊讶；挫败；混淆|Third person singular present tense form of "confound".|This evidence confounds our previous theories. 这个证据推翻了我们之前的理论。|Synonyms: confuses, bewilders, puzzles, baffles|Antonyms: clarifies, enlightens|verb
