gasmen:/ˈɡæsmən/| n. |煤气工人，燃气公司员工| Workers employed by a gas company, typically responsible for installing, maintaining, or repairing gas lines and appliances. | The gasmen came to fix the leak in our stove. 煤气工人来修理我们炉子上的漏气了。| The gasmen are on strike. 燃气公司的员工在罢工。 | 近义词: gas fitters, gas engineers | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "customers" in a business context) | 词性: Noun (plural)

Etymology:
gas + men
"gas" (from a hypothetical word *ghast, coined by the Flemish chemist J. B. van Helmont, c. 1630) + "men" (Old English mann, meaning "person")
