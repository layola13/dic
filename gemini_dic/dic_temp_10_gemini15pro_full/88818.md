hogchain:/ˈhɒɡtʃeɪn/ | n. | 大桶（尤指容量为63加仑的葡萄酒或白兰地桶）| A large cask, especially one holding 63 gallons of wine or brandy. |  例句：The hogchain of brandy was stored in the cellar.  大桶白兰地存放在地窖里。

例句：The winery produced several hogchains of their finest vintage.  这家酿酒厂生产了几大桶他们最好的年份葡萄酒。

近义词：cask, barrel | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "small container") | 词性: 名词
