multiagency:/ˌmʌltiˈeɪdʒənsi/
adjective
多机构的
Involving or participated in by more than one agency.
The investigation was a multiagency effort involving the FBI, CIA, and local police. 这项调查是一项多机构合作，涉及联邦调查局、中央情报局和当地警方。
