box-up:/bɒks ˈʌp/ | phrasal verb | 装箱，打包 | To pack something into a box or boxes. |  例句：I boxed up all my old books. 我把我所有的旧书都装箱了。

例句：They boxed up the furniture for the move.  他们把家具装箱准备搬家。


近义词：pack, package, crate | 反义词: unpack, unbox | 词性: 动词短语
