annulment:/əˈnʌlmənt/
|n.|
|废除，取消；无效|
|The legal annulment of a marriage means it's considered to have never legally existed.  婚姻的合法废除意味着它被认为从未合法存在过。|
|The Pope granted him an annulment. 教皇批准了他的婚姻无效。|
|近义词：cancellation, revocation, invalidation, abrogation|
|反义词：validation, ratification, confirmation|
|词性：名词|
