bingo:/ˈbɪŋɡoʊ/ (US) /ˈbɪŋɡəʊ/ (UK)| n. int. |宾果游戏；（因猜中而喊的）宾果！|A game of chance in which each player has a card printed with different numbers on which to place markers when the numbers are called out. The winner is the first person to mark off a complete row of numbers. Also used as an exclamation upon winning the game. | I shouted "Bingo!" when I completed the row. 当我完成一行时，我喊道“宾果！” |近义词：lotto, housey-housey (UK)|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun, interjection
