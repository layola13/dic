examples:/ɪɡˈzɑːmplz/ | n. | 例子，样本 |  Something that is typical of the group or set which it belongs to; a representative instance.  Derived from the Latin "exemplum." |  These examples illustrate the point perfectly. 这些例子完美地说明了这一点。| synonyms: instances, illustrations, specimens, samples | antonyms: counter-examples, exceptions | noun
