harmfulnesses:/ˈhɑːrmfəlnəsɪz/
n.
有害性，危害性
The various harmfulnesses of different pollutants were assessed.  对不同污染物的各种危害性进行了评估。
The report detailed the harmfulnesses of smoking. 报告详细说明了吸烟的危害性。
Synonym: dangers, hazards, risks, detriments
Antonym: benefits, advantages, safety, harmlessness
noun
