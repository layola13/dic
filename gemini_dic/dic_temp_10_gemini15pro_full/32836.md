cholecystis:/ˌkoʊləsɪˈstaɪtɪs/| n. |胆囊炎|Inflammation of the gallbladder, a small organ located beneath the liver that stores bile.|She was diagnosed with cholecystis after experiencing severe abdominal pain. 她被诊断出患有胆囊炎，此前她经历了严重的腹痛。|近义词:gallbladder inflammation|反义词:None|词性:noun
Etymology: From Greek *kholé* (bile) + *kustis* (bladder) + *-itis* (inflammation).
