nonpartially:/ˌnɒnˈpɑːʃəli/ | adv. | 公正地，不偏不倚地 |  In a way that is not biased or prejudiced; fairly.  It combines the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") with "partially" (meaning "in part" or "with bias").  Thus, it signifies a lack of partiality. | The judge ruled nonpartially, considering only the facts presented. 法官公正地裁决，只考虑了提出的事实。 | Synonyms: impartially, fairly, justly, equitably, objectively | Antonyms: partially, biasedly, unfairly, unjustly | 词性: 副词
