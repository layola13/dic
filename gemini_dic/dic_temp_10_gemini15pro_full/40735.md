conveyance:/kənˈveɪəns/| n.|运输, 运送; 运输工具; 财产转让; 财产让渡书|The act of conveying something from one place to another; a means of transport; the legal process of transferring property. |例句：The stagecoach was a popular conveyance in the 19th century.  驿马车是19世纪一种流行的交通工具。

例句：He signed the conveyance for the house. 他签署了房屋转让书。|近义词：carriage, delivery, transmission|反义词:None|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English conveiaunce, from Anglo-French conveiance, from conveier, from Latin convēhere (to bring together, carry, convey), from com- (together) + vēhere (to carry).
