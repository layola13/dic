overprescriptions:/ˌoʊvərprɪˈskrɪpʃənz/| n.|过度处方，过度开药|The act of prescribing too much medication, often leading to dependence or other health problems.  It stems from "over" (meaning excessive) and "prescription" (a doctor's written order for medicine).|The overprescription of antibiotics has led to the development of resistant strains of bacteria. 过度使用抗生素导致了耐药菌株的出现。

The doctor was accused of overprescriptions of painkillers.  这位医生被指控过度开具止痛药。|近义词：overmedication, polypharmacy|反义词:underprescription|noun
