antialien:/ˌæntiˈeɪliən/
adjective
反对外星人的；排外的
opposed to or hostile toward aliens or foreigners.

例句：The antialien sentiment in the community made it difficult for newcomers to feel welcome.  社区中排外的氛围让新来者很难感到受欢迎。

近义词：xenophobic, nativist, anti-immigrant
反义词：pro-alien, pro-immigrant, welcoming
