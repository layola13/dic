bullfighters:/ˈbʊlˌfaɪtərz/
n.
斗牛士（复数）
People who fight bulls, especially as a public spectacle.
The bullfighters entered the arena to the cheers of the crowd.  斗牛士在人群的欢呼声中进入竞技场。
The bullfighters were incredibly brave. 斗牛士们非常勇敢。
近义词：matadors, toreadors
反义词: None
词性：noun


bullfighter:/ˈbʊlˌfaɪtər/
n.
斗牛士
A person who fights bulls, especially as a public spectacle.
The bullfighter skillfully dodged the charging bull. 斗牛士巧妙地躲过了冲锋的公牛。
The bullfighter raised his sword for the final blow. 斗牛士举起剑进行最后一击。
近义词：matador, toreador
反义词: None
词性：noun

词源:
From bull + fighter.  "Bull" comes from Old English "bula." "Fighter" comes from the verb "fight," which has Proto-Germanic roots.
