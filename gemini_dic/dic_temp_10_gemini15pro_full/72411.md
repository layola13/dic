fluework:/ˈfluːwɜːrk/
n.
烟道工程，烟道系统
The system of flues in a building.
The fluework needs to be inspected regularly to ensure safe operation.  烟道工程需要定期检查以确保安全运行。
The old house had intricate fluework that needed extensive repairs.  这栋老房子有复杂的烟道系统，需要大修。
Synonym: flue system, chimney system, venting system
Antonym: None (lack of a fluework implies no system, not an opposite system)
noun
