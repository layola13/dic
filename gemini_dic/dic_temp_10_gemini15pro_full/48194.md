decompresses:/ˌdiːkəmˈprɛsɪz/| v.|减压，解压|To release from pressure; to expand or return to original size after being compressed.|After a long day at work, I like to decompress by reading a book. 下班后我喜欢通过读书来减压。|Synonyms: relax, unwind, expand|Antonyms: compress, condense, constrict|verb

decompression:/ˌdiːkəmˈprɛʃən/| n.|减压，解压|The act or process of decompressing.|Divers must undergo slow decompression after deep dives. 潜水员深潜后必须进行缓慢减压。|Synonyms: relaxation, release|Antonyms: compression, condensation|noun

词源:  From de- + compress, from Latin compressus, past participle of comprimere "to press together."
