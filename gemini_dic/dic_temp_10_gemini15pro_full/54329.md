disheartening:/dɪsˈhɑːrtnɪŋ/
adj.
令人沮丧的，使人灰心的
Causing someone to lose determination or enthusiasm; discouraging.
The team's loss was disheartening. 球队的失利令人沮丧。
近义词: discouraging, demoralizing, dispiriting
反义词: encouraging, heartening, inspiring
词性: adjective


disheartening:/dɪsˈhɑːrtnɪŋ/
词源: From dis- + heartening, from hearten + -ing.  "Dis-" prefixes the word "hearten" which means to give courage or confidence to.  Therefore, "disheartening" means the opposite – to take away courage or confidence.
中文翻译: 令人沮丧的，使人灰心的
英文解析: Causing someone to lose determination or enthusiasm; discouraging.
