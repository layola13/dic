incident-to:/ˈɪnsɪdənt tuː/ | adj. | 附带的，伴随发生的，从属的 |  Relating to, dependent on, or occurring as a consequence of something else, especially a main event or activity. Typically used in a medical context to describe services provided by non-physician staff under the direct supervision of a physician. | The physician billed for the surgery, and the incident-to services provided by the physician assistant. 医生对外科手术进行了收费，也对医生助理提供的附带服务进行了收费。 |  The nurse's visit was incident-to the doctor's appointment.  护士的探访是从属于医生的预约。 |  associated, ancillary, attendant, subordinate | primary, main, principal | adjective
