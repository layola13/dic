freegan:/ˈfriːɡən/| n. |拾荒者；免费食物主义者|A person who rejects consumerism and seeks to help the environment by reducing waste, especially by retrieving and using discarded food and other goods.|Example: The freegan retrieved edible food from the supermarket's dumpster.  拾荒者从超市的垃圾箱里找到了可食用的食物。

Example:  Freegans often share resources and knowledge about where to find discarded items. 免费食物主义者经常分享资源和关于在哪里找到被丢弃物品的知识。|近义词：scavenger, dumpster diver|反义词: consumerist|词性:noun
