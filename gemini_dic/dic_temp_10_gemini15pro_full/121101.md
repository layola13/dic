miscreates:/ˌmɪskrɪˈeɪts/
verb
错误地创造；歪曲；败坏
to create in a deformed or perverted way; to make or form badly or improperly.
He miscreated the character in his adaptation, turning a hero into a villain. 他在他的改编作品中歪曲了这个角色，把一个英雄变成了一个恶棍。
misrepresent, distort, deform, pervert
create, form, shape
v.
