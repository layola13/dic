cruet:/ˈkruːɪt/| n. |调味瓶；油醋瓶|A small bottle for holding vinegar, oil, or other condiments, typically with a stopper or lid.|She reached for the silver cruet and drizzled oil over her salad. 她伸手拿起银质调味瓶，在沙拉上淋了一些油。|Synonyms: vial, phial, flask, decanter|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, perhaps "container" in a very broad sense)|noun

cruet:/ˈkruːɪt/| n. |(古)小瓶，细颈瓶|A small bottle or vial, especially one used for holy water or wine in religious ceremonies (archaic usage).|The priest filled the cruet with holy water.  神父在小瓶里装满了圣水。|Synonyms: vial, phial, flask|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms)|noun
