hibernate:/ˈhaɪbərneɪt/ (US), /ˈhaɪbəneɪt/ (UK)| v. i.|冬眠，蛰伏，停止活动|To spend the winter in a dormant state; to remain inactive or unused for an extended period.|Bears hibernate during the winter. 熊在冬天冬眠。|The computer system has been hibernating since the company closed down. 自公司倒闭以来，计算机系统一直处于休眠状态。|synonyms: overwinter, sleep, lie dormant, slumber|antonyms: awaken, revive, activate|verb

hibernate:/ˈhaɪbərneɪt/ (US), /ˈhaɪbəneɪt/ (UK)| n.|冬眠，蛰伏|The state of being dormant or inactive, especially during the winter.|The bear's hibernation lasted for several months.  熊的冬眠持续了几个月。|The old house was in a state of hibernation. 这栋老房子处于休眠状态。|synonyms: dormancy, inactivity, sleep|antonyms: activity, wakefulness|noun
