filmi:/ˈfɪlmi/| adj.|电影的；像电影的；虚假的；做作的|Relating to films; cinematic. Also, resembling the characteristics of a film, often in a dramatic or unrealistic way.|She had a very filmi reaction to the news, bursting into tears and fainting dramatically. 她对这个消息的反应非常戏剧化，突然大哭起来，然后戏剧性地晕倒了。|近义词：cinematic, theatrical, dramatic, artificial, affected|反义词：genuine, authentic, realistic|词性:形容词
词源: From Hindi/Urdu फ़िल्मी (filmī), derived from film.
