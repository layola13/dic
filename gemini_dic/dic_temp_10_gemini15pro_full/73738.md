forks:/fɔːrks/| n.|叉子；岔路；分叉；餐叉|A utensil with two or more prongs used for eating, lifting, or digging.|She used a fork to eat her salad. 她用叉子吃沙拉。|近义词：utensil, cutlery|反义词:spoon|名词

forks:/fɔːrks/| v.|分叉；叉起；走岔路(fork的第三人称单数)|To divide into two or more branches or prongs.|The road forks just ahead.  路就在前面分叉了。|近义词：divide, branch, split|反义词:merge, join|动词
