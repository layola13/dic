autotoxic:/ˌɔːtəʊˈtɒksɪk/
adjective
自体中毒的
Poisonous to itself; inhibiting one's own growth or reproduction.
Autotoxic substances released by the plant can inhibit its own growth.  这种植物释放的自体毒性物质会抑制自身的生长。
The autotoxic effects of walnut trees are well-documented. 胡桃树的自毒效应已被充分证明。
synonyms: self-poisoning, self-inhibiting
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider using terms like "growth-promoting" or "beneficial")
词性: adjective
