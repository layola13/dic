doorkeeper:/ˈdɔːrˌkiːpər/ (US), /ˈdɔːˌkiːpə(r)/ (UK)| n.|门卫，守门人|A person who guards or controls access to a building or other place.|The doorkeeper wouldn't let anyone in without a ticket.  门卫不让任何人没有票进入。|synonyms: gatekeeper, guardian, watchman, janitor, custodian|antonyms: intruder, trespasser|noun
