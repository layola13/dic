carbonation:/ˌkɑːrbəˈneɪʃn/ | n. |碳酸化作用；充气；碳酸饮料 |The process of dissolving carbon dioxide in a liquid, especially to make it fizzy. Also refers to the fizzy quality of a carbonated drink.| The carbonation in the soda made it refreshing.  汽水中的碳酸使它令人耳目一新。|Synonyms: fizz, effervescence |Antonyms: flatness |noun
