empassionate:/ɪmˈpæʃənət/| adj.|充满激情的，热烈的|Filled with or showing intense emotion or zeal.|Her empassionate plea for help moved everyone in the room. 她充满激情的求助感动了房间里的每一个人。|近义词：passionate, ardent, fervent, zealous|反义词：apathetic, indifferent, unemotional|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin *empassionatus*, past participle of *empassionare* "to inflame with passion," from *in-* "in" + *passio* "passion".
