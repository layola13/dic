earthshot:/ˈɜːrθʃɒt/ (UK) /ˈɜːrθʃɑːt/ (US)| n.|地球目标，地球计划|An ambitious but specific goal for improving the environment.  It is often used in the context of the Earthshot Prize, which awards five £1 million prizes each year for solutions to the world's greatest environmental problems. |例句：The Earthshot Prize aims to incentivize innovation and problem-solving for the planet. 地球目标奖旨在激励地球的创新和问题解决。

例句：Several Earthshots focus on restoring ocean health.  几个地球目标专注于恢复海洋健康。|近义词：environmental goal, sustainability target, green initiative|反义词:environmental degradation, unsustainable practice|词性:noun
