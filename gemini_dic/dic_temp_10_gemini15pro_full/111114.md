literalizer:/ˈlɪtərəlaɪzər/| n.|字面化者，逐字翻译者|Someone who adheres strictly to the literal meaning of words or texts, often without considering context or nuance.  A person who interprets something literally. |例句：The lawyer was a literalizer, focusing on the precise wording of the contract. 这位律师是一位字面主义者，专注于合同的精确措辞。

例句：He's such a literalizer that he missed the irony in her statement. 他是一个如此字面化的人，以至于他错过了她声明中的讽刺意味。|近义词：literalist, precisian|反义词: figurative speaker, interpreter|词性:noun
