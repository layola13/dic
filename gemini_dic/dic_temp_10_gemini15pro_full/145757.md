overcultured: /ˌoʊvərˈkʌltʃərd/ | adj. | 过分追求文化的；矫揉造作的；过度文明的 | Excessively refined or cultured, often to the point of artificiality or affectation.  Characterized by an excessive interest in and pursuit of high culture, sometimes to the point of being pretentious or losing sight of genuine appreciation. |  She found the gallery opening a bit overcultured, with everyone speaking in hushed tones about obscure artists. 她觉得画廊的开幕有点矫揉造作，每个人都在低声谈论一些不知名的艺术家。

The overcultured atmosphere of the party made him feel uncomfortable.  聚会上过分追求文化的氛围让他感到不舒服。 | 近义词：pretentious, affected, sophisticated, refined | 反义词：uncultured, unsophisticated, natural, genuine | 词性：形容词
