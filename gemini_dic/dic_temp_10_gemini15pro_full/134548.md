nonliquids:/nɑːnˈlɪkwɪdz/
|n.| 非液体|Substances that are not liquid; solids and gases.| Many nonliquids, such as sand and flour, can behave like liquids under certain conditions. 许多非液体，例如沙子和面粉，在某些条件下可以像液体一样运动。|
|近义词：solids, gases|反义词:liquids|词性:名词
