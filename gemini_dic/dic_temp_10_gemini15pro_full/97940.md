innovationist:/ˌɪnəˈveɪʃənɪst/
n.|创新者，革新者|A person who introduces new methods, ideas, or products.|例句：The innovationist was awarded for his groundbreaking work in renewable energy.  这位创新者因其在可再生能源领域的突破性工作而获奖。

例句：She is a renowned innovationist in the tech industry. 她在科技行业是一位著名的创新者。|近义词：innovator, pioneer, inventor, reformer|反义词：traditionalist, conservative|词性:名词


词源:
From innovation + -ist.  来自innovation（创新）+ -ist（表示人的后缀）。
