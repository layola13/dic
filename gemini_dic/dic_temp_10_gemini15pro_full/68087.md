fadedly:/ˈfeɪdɪdli/
adverb
暗淡地，褪色地，微弱地
In a faded or indistinct manner; faintly.  The memory fadedly lingered in his mind.
The light fadedly illuminated the room.
近义词：dimly, faintly, palely, weakly
反义词：brightly, vividly, strongly
词性: adverb

fade:/feɪd/
verb
褪色，消失，衰退
To lose freshness, brightness, or strength; to disappear gradually. The sun faded the curtains. His hopes faded as the days went by.
近义词：wane, diminish, disappear
反义词：brighten, intensify, appear
词性: verb

-ly:/-li/
suffix
构成副词
A suffix that forms adverbs from adjectives.  Quick becomes quickly.  Sad becomes sadly.
词性: suffix

词源:  "fadedly" comes from the verb "fade" + the adjective suffix "-ed" + the adverb suffix "-ly". "Fade" itself has roots in the Old French word "fader," meaning "to grow weak or faint."
