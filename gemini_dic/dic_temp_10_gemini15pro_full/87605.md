heterotropal:/ˌhɛtərɒˈtrɒpəl/ | adj. | 倒生的，横生的 |(of an ovule) inverted; having the micropyle close to the hilum. | Example: The heterotropal ovule is common in many flowering plants.  倒生胚珠在许多开花植物中很常见。 | Example:  Heterotropal ovules are characteristic of the family Asteraceae. 倒生胚珠是菊科植物的特征。| Synonym: inverted, anatropous (in some contexts) | Antonym: orthotropous, atropous |adjective
