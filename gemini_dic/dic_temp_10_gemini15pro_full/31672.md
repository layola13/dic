chatty:/ˈtʃæti/ | adj. | 爱说话的，健谈的 | Given to conversation; talkative. | She's a very chatty person and can talk for hours. 她是一个非常健谈的人，可以连续说上几个小时。| My neighbor is quite chatty; we often have long conversations over the fence. 我的邻居很健谈；我们经常隔着篱笆长时间聊天。 | 近义词: talkative, loquacious, garrulous | 反义词: quiet, reserved, taciturn | 词性: 形容词
