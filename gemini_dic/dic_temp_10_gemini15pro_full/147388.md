overneutralizers:/ˌōvərˈn(y)o͞otrəˌlīzərz/ | n. | 过度中和剂 | Substances that neutralize an acid or base to a pH beyond the neutral point (7). |  The overneutralizers caused the solution to become overly alkaline.  过度中和剂导致溶液变得过碱性。| synonyms: excess neutralizers, over-alkalizers (if resulting pH is basic), over-acidifiers (if resulting pH is acidic) | antonyms: insufficient neutralizers | noun


overneutralize:/ˌōvərˈn(y)o͞otrəˌlīz/ | v. | 过度中和 | To neutralize an acid or base to a pH beyond the neutral point (7). | Adding too much baking soda overneutralized the vinegar solution.  加入过多的烘焙苏打过度中和了醋溶液。| synonyms: over-alkalize (if resulting pH is basic), over-acidify (if resulting pH is acidic) | antonyms: underneutralize | verb
