deritualized:/diːˈrɪtʃʊəlaɪzd/
adj.
去仪式化的，非仪式化的
Deprived of ritual; made secular or informal.  Removed of the symbolic meaning or traditional practices associated with a ritual.
The wedding ceremony was deritualized, focusing on personal vows rather than traditional religious practices.  婚礼仪式去除了仪式化，侧重于个人誓言，而不是传统的宗教习俗。
secularized, informal, non-ritualistic
ritualized, formal, ceremonial
adjective
