frescoists:/ˈfrɛskoʊɪsts/
n.
壁画画家
Artists who paint frescoes.
The frescoists of the Renaissance were masters of their craft. 文艺复兴时期的壁画画家都是技艺精湛的大师。
muralists, painters
sculptors, architects
