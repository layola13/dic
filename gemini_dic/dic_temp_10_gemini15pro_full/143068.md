outboxes:/ˈaʊtbɒksɪz/
n.
发件箱；已发送邮件文件夹
A folder on a computer or other electronic device where copies of emails or other messages that have been sent are stored.
I always check my outboxes to make sure my emails have been sent successfully. 我总是检查我的发件箱以确保我的邮件已成功发送。
He deleted all the messages in his outboxes. 他删除了发件箱里的所有邮件。
Near Synonyms: sent folders, sent items, sent mail
Antonyms: inboxes, received mail
noun
