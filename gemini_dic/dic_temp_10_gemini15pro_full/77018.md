gargle: /ˈɡɑːrɡl/ (US) /ˈɡɑːɡl/ (UK)| n. v.|漱口，漱口液|To wash one's throat and mouth with a liquid kept in motion by exhaling through it.|
例句：
The doctor told me to gargle with salt water.
医生让我用盐水漱口。

He used a medicated gargle for his sore throat.
他用药水漱口来治疗喉咙痛。


近义词：rinse, wash (out)|
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "swallow")|
词性: noun, verb
