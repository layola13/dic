egyptianization:/ɪˌdʒɪpʃənɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 埃及化 | The process of making something Egyptian in character or style; the adoption of Egyptian customs, beliefs, or practices. | The museum exhibit explored the egyptianization of Roman art and architecture.  博物馆的展览探索了罗马艺术和建筑的埃及化。 |  Near synonyms:  Egyptianizing, cultural assimilation, acculturation | Antonyms: Westernization, Hellenization | Noun
