metamorphic:/ˌmet̬əˈmɔːrfɪk/| adj.|变质的，变形的|Relating to metamorphosis, the process of transformation or change in the form and structure of rock (or of an animal).  Characterized by metamorphosis or change.|Marble is a metamorphic rock. 大理石是一种变质岩。|The caterpillar undergoes a metamorphic process to become a butterfly. 毛毛虫经历一个变质过程变成蝴蝶。|Synonyms: transformed, altered, changed, transmuted|Antonyms: unchanged, static, unaltered|adjective
