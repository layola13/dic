acquired: /əˈkwaɪərd/
adjective
已获得的，后天习得的
obtained possession of something; developed or learned something
She has acquired a good knowledge of French. 她法语学得很好。
He had acquired a reputation for dishonesty. 他落了个不诚实的名声。
近义词: obtained, gained, learned, developed
反义词: lost, innate, inherent
词性: adjective

acquired: /əˈkwaɪərd/
verb (past tense and past participle of acquire)
已获得，后天习得（动词acquire的过去式和过去分词）
obtained possession of something; developed or learned something
He acquired the firm in 2008. 他在2008年收购了这家公司。
I've acquired a taste for olives. 我渐渐喜欢上了橄榄。
近义词: obtained, gained, earned
反义词: lost, forfeited
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *aquyren*, from Old French *aquierre*, from Latin *acquirere* ("to acquire, get, obtain, gain, procure"), from *ad* ("to") + *quaerere* ("to seek, ask").

USAGE->
Present Simple:
He acquires new skills quickly. = 他很快就能学会新技能。
She acquires knowledge through reading. = 她通过阅读获取知识。

Present Continuous:
They are acquiring a new company. = 他们正在收购一家新公司。
He is acquiring a taste for spicy food. = 他正在逐渐喜欢上辛辣食物。

Present Perfect:
We have acquired a valuable painting. = 我们获得了一幅珍贵的画作。
She has acquired a reputation as a talented musician. = 她赢得了天才音乐家的声誉。

Past Simple:
He acquired his wealth through hard work. = 他通过努力工作获得了财富。
She acquired a new car last week. = 她上周买了一辆新车。

Past Continuous:
They were acquiring new customers rapidly. = 他们那时正快速地获得新客户。
He was acquiring valuable experience in the field. = 他当时正在这个领域获得宝贵的经验。

Future Simple:
The company will acquire new assets next year. = 公司明年将获得新的资产。
She will acquire the necessary skills for the job. = 她将获得这份工作所需的技能。

Conditional:
If I acquired a million dollars, I would travel the world. = 如果我得到一百万美元，我就会环游世界。
If she acquired the property, she would develop it. = 如果她获得了这块地，她就会开发它。

Passive Voice:
The company was acquired by a larger corporation. = 这家公司被一家更大的公司收购了。
The painting was acquired by the museum. = 这幅画被博物馆收藏了。
