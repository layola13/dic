dorsalmost:/ˈdɔːrsəlˌmoʊst/| adj.|最背侧的，最靠近背部的|Situated nearest to the back or dorsum.  Relating to the dorsal side and furthest from the ventral side.|The dorsalmost fin on the fish was bright blue.  这条鱼最背部的鳍是亮蓝色的。|近义词：dorsal, posterior|反义词：ventral, anterior|词性:形容词
Etymology: From 'dorsal' + '-most'. 'Dorsal' comes from Late Latin 'dorsalis', from Latin 'dorsum' (back). '-most' is a suffix forming superlative adjectives.
