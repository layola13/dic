boilerplate:/ˈbɔɪlərpleɪt/| n. adj.|样板文件，公式化文件；（引申义）乏味的，陈腐的|Standardized text, often legal or technical, that can be reused in multiple documents with little or no modification. It can also refer to something unoriginal or clichéd.|The lawyer quickly skimmed the boilerplate in the contract. 律师快速浏览了合同中的样板条款。|Synonyms: template, formula, cliché, stereotype|Antonyms: original, innovative, unique|noun

boilerplate:/ˈbɔɪlərpleɪt/| adj.|样板的，公式化的，陈腐的|Unoriginal, formulaic, and clichéd.|The movie's plot was disappointingly boilerplate. 这部电影的情节令人失望地老套。|Synonyms: clichéd, formulaic, stereotyped, unoriginal|Antonyms: original, innovative, fresh, unique|adjective
