levelnesses: /ˈlɛvəlnəsɪz/ | n. | 平坦；水平；稳定；均衡 | The levelness of the playing field is crucial for a fair game.  比赛场地的平坦对于公平比赛至关重要。 |  The levelness of his voice betrayed no emotion. 他平静的声音没有流露出任何情绪。 | 近义词: flatness, evenness, stability, equilibrium | 反义词: unevenness, instability, imbalance | 词性: 名词

词源:  From "level" (meaning flat or even) + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns indicating a state or quality).  Ultimately from Old English "læfel" and Proto-Germanic "*labalaz".
