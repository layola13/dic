controversialists:/ˌkɒntrəˈvɜːʃəlɪsts/
n.
爭論者，爭議者
A person who engages in controversies or disputes; a disputant.
The two controversialists debated the issue for hours.  这两位争论者就这个问题辩论了几个小时。

The controversialists continued their heated debate long into the night. 这些争论者继续他们激烈的辩论直到深夜。

Synonyms: disputant, arguer, debater, polemicist
Antonyms: peacemaker, mediator, conciliator
noun


Etymology:
From controversial + -ist.  Controversial itself comes from controversy, from Late Latin controversia, from Latin controversus "turned against, disputed," from contra "against" + versus, past participle of vertere "to turn."
