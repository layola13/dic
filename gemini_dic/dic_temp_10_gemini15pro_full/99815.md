interminableness:/ɪnˌtɜːrmɪnəbəlnəs/ |ɪnˌtɜːrmɪnəbəlnɪs/
n.| 无休止，没完没了 |The quality or state of being interminable; endlessness.|The interminableness of the journey was starting to wear on everyone's nerves. 旅途的漫长无休止开始让每个人都感到紧张。|近义词：endlessness, perpetuity, infinity|反义词：brevity, transience, finiteness|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From interminable + -ness.  Interminable from Late Middle English: from Old French, or from medieval Latin interminabilis, from in- ‘not’ + terminabilis (from terminare ‘to limit’).
