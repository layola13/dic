overloudness:/ˌoʊvərˈlaʊdnəs/| n.|过响度，过度响度|Excessive loudness; a sound level perceived as too loud, often causing discomfort or potential hearing damage.  It's often used in the context of audio engineering and acoustics.|The overloudness of the music gave me a headache.  这音乐太吵了，让我头疼。|Synonyms: excessive loudness, excessive volume, blasting, deafening sound|Antonyms: quietness, softness, faintness|noun

overloud:/ˌoʊvərˈlaʊd/| adj.|过响的，过度响亮的|Excessively loud; having a volume that is too high, often causing discomfort or potential hearing damage. |The overloud music from the concert could be heard blocks away.  音乐会的音量过大，几个街区外都能听到。|Synonyms: excessively loud, deafening, blasting, earsplitting|Antonyms: quiet, soft, subdued|adjective
