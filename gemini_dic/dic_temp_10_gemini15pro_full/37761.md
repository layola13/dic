compassionating:/kəmˈpæʃənˌeɪtɪŋ/| adj.|表示同情心的，富有同情心的|Feeling or showing sympathy and concern for others.|She gave him a compassionating look. 她给了他一个充满同情心的眼神。|近义词：sympathetic, empathetic, caring, kind|反义词：uncaring, cruel, heartless|adjective


compassionate:/kəmˈpæʃənɪt/| adj.|有同情心的，表示怜悯的|Feeling or showing sympathy and concern for others.|He was a compassionate man who always tried to help those in need. 他是一个富有同情心的人，总是试图帮助那些需要帮助的人。|近义词：sympathetic, empathetic, caring, kind|反义词：uncaring, cruel, heartless|adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French compassionat, from late Latin compassionatus ‘having compassion’, from compassio (see compassion).
