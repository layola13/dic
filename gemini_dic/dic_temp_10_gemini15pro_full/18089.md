behaviorist: /bɪˈheɪvjərɪst/ (US) /bɪˈheɪvjərɪst/ (UK)| n. |行为主义者|A psychologist who believes that human or animal psychology can be accurately studied only through the examination and analysis of objectively observable and quantifiable behavioral events, in contrast with subjective mental states.|例句：The behaviorist observed the rat's actions in the maze. 行为主义者观察了老鼠在迷宫中的行为。|近义词：learning theorist, behavior modifier|反义词：cognitivist, mentalist|词性: noun

behaviorist: /bɪˈheɪvjərɪst/ (US) /bɪˈheɪvjərɪst/ (UK)| adj. |行为主义的|Relating to behaviorism.|例句：The behaviorist approach focuses on observable actions. 行为主义方法注重可观察的行为。|近义词：behavioral|反义词：cognitive, mentalistic|词性: adjective
