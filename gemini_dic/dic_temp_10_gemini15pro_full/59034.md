dwarfism: /ˈdwɔːrfɪzəm/ (US) /ˈdwɔːfɪzəm/ (UK)| n. |侏儒症；矮小；|A medical or genetic condition resulting in abnormally short stature.  A person with dwarfism is significantly shorter than an average person of the same age and sex.|Achondroplasia is the most common type of dwarfism. 软骨发育不全是最常见的侏儒症。|Short stature, restricted growth|Gigantism, overgrowth|Noun
