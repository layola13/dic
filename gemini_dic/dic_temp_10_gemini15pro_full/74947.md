freya:/ˈfreɪə/| n. |芙蕾雅,弗蕾亚|A Norse goddess of love, beauty, fertility, war, and death.  In some accounts, she is associated with cats and is depicted riding in a chariot pulled by them.| Freya is a popular name for girls in Scandinavian countries.  芙蕾雅在斯堪的纳维亚国家是一个很受欢迎的女孩名字。|近义词: Frigg (another Norse goddess of love and marriage), Aphrodite (Greek goddess of love), Venus (Roman goddess of love)|反义词: None (deities typically don't have direct opposites)|词性:noun
