obloquial:/əˈbloʊkwiəl/
adjective
口语的，通俗的
Relating to or characteristic of spoken language or conversation; informal.
Her writing style is quite obloquial, making it easy to read. 她的写作风格非常口语化，很容易阅读。
The politician adopted an obloquial tone in his speech to connect with the working class.  这位政治家在他的演讲中采用了口语化的语气，以与工人阶级建立联系。
近义词: colloquial, informal, conversational, chatty
反义词: formal, literary, written
词性: adjective
