name-droppings:/ˈneɪmˌdrɒpɪŋz/ | n. | 炫耀交游；抬出名人 | The act of mentioning famous people you have met or know, usually in order to impress other people. | He's always name-dropping – it's so embarrassing! 他总是炫耀自己认识的名人——真让人尴尬！ |  synonyms: boasting, bragging | antonyms: humility, modesty | noun
