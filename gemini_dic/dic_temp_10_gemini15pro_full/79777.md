glutinously:/ˈɡluːtɪnəsli/| adv.|粘稠地；胶着地|in a glutinous manner; stickily.  Characterized by a sticky or gluey consistency.|The rice was cooked glutinously, sticking together in a thick mass.  米饭煮得黏糊糊的，粘成一团。|Synonyms: stickily, adhesively, viscously|Antonyms: thinly, loosely, dryly|词性:副词

glutinous:/ˈɡluːtɪnəs/| adj.|粘的；胶状的|having the quality of glue; sticky.|The glutinous rice made excellent mochi.  糯米做的麻糬很好吃。|Synonyms: sticky, gummy, adhesive, viscous|Antonyms: thin, watery, dry|词性:形容词
