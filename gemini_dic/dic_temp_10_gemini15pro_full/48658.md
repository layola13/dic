defensibleness:/dɪˌfɛnsɪblənəs/| n. |可辩护性，正当性|The quality or state of being defensible; capability of being defended or justified.|His actions, while unconventional, had a certain defensibleness. 虽然他的行为不合常规，但也有一定的正当性。|近义词：justifiability, vindicability, excusability|反义词：indefensibleness, unjustifiability|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From defensible + -ness.  Defensible comes from Old French defensible, from defendre "to defend," from Latin defendere "to ward off, protect."
