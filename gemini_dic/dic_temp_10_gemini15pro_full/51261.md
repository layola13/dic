desubsidizes:/ˌdiːˈsʌbsɪdaɪzɪz/
verb
取消补贴，停止资助
to remove a subsidy from (something)
The government desubsidizes farming. 政府取消了对农业的补贴。
近义词:defund, withdraw funding
反义词:subsidize, fund, support
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
de- (prefix): meaning "reverse, remove, reduce" from Latin
subsidize (verb): from subsidy + -ize. Subsidy comes from Late Latin subsidiarium "reserve troops, aid, support," from Latin subsidium "assistance, aid, relief," from sub "under, near" + sedere "to sit" (meaning "troops stationed in reserve").
