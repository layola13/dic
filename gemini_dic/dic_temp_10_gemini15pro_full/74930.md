frettingly:/ˈfrɛtɪŋli/ | adv. | 烦躁地，焦急地 | In a way that shows worry and anxiety. | She paced the room frettingly, waiting for the phone to ring. 她焦急地在房间里踱来踱去，等着电话铃响。| 近义词: anxiously, nervously, worriedly | 反义词: calmly, peacefully | 词性: 副词

frettingly:/ˈfrɛtɪŋli/ | adv. | 烦躁地，焦急地 | Showing signs of worry and agitation. | He frettingly chewed his fingernails during the exam.  考试期间，他焦躁地咬着指甲。| 近义词: agitatedly, restlessly, uneasily | 反义词: serenely, tranquilly | 词性: 副词

词源: From the verb "fret" (to worry) + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverb suffix).  "Fret" comes from Old English "fretan" (to devour, consume), related to Old High German "frezzan" (to eat greedily).  The meaning shifted from physical consumption to mental agitation and worry.
