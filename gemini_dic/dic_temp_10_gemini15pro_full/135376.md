nonperformance:/ˌnɒnpəˈfɔːməns/ /ˌnɑːnpərˈfɔːrməns/| n. |不履行，不执行，未履行|Failure or omission to perform a duty, contract, or obligation.|The nonperformance of the contract led to a lawsuit.  合同的不履行导致了一场诉讼。|Synonyms: default, breach, failure, omission|Antonyms: performance, fulfillment, execution|noun

nonperformance:/ˌnɒnpəˈfɔːməns/ /ˌnɑːnpərˈfɔːrməns/| n. |未履行，未执行|The nonperformance of his duties resulted in his dismissal. 他未能履行职责导致了他的解雇。|Synonyms:  dereliction, negligence|Antonyms:  achievement, accomplishment|noun
