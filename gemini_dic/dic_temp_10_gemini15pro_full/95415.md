incidental-to:/ˌɪnsɪˈdɛntl tuː/ | adj. | 附带的，次要的，伴随发生的 |Happening as a natural or expected result of something else.  Relating to something else but less important than it.  In US healthcare, referring to services that are necessary for the primary procedure.  |The benefits are incidental to the scheme. 这些好处是该计划的附带利益。| Example: The travel expenses were incidental to the business trip.  出差的旅行费用是附带发生的。
