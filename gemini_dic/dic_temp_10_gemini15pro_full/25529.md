bulls:/bʊlz/
noun
公牛（复数）
Male cattle, especially those kept for breeding or for bullfighting.
The bulls grazed peacefully in the pasture. 公牛在牧场上平静地吃草。

bulls:/bʊlz/
noun
(股市)上涨；牛市
A rising stock market. (Informal)
The bulls are running on Wall Street today. 今天华尔街股市上涨。

bulls:/bʊlz/
noun
(游戏)全中；红心
(In darts or other target games) a shot that hits the center of the target.
He scored three bulls in a row. 他连续三次击中红心。
