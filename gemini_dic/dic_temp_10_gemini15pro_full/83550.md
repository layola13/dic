haecceity:/ˌhekˈsiːɪti/ /ˌhɛkˈsiːɪti/ | n. | 此性；特异性；个体性 | The property or quality of being a particular thing; that which makes something the kind of thing that it is and different from anything else.  | This painting captures the haecceity of the moment. 这幅画捕捉到了这一刻的特异性。|  synonym: thisness, quiddity, individuality | antonym: universality, generality | noun
