harmonics:/hɑːrˈmɑːnɪks/ /hɑːrˈmɑːnɪks/| n.|谐波，泛音|A component frequency of an oscillation or wave.  It is a multiple of the fundamental frequency.|The guitarist played the harmonics beautifully, creating a shimmering effect. 吉他手优美地演奏了泛音，营造出一种闪亮的效果。|synonyms: overtones, partials|antonyms: fundamental, base|noun

harmonics:/hɑːrˈmɑːnɪks/ /hɑːrˈmɑːnɪks/| adj.|和声的，泛音的|Relating to or characterized by harmony or harmonics.|The harmonic series is a sequence of frequencies that are integer multiples of a fundamental frequency. 谐波序列是由基频的整数倍构成的一系列频率。|synonyms: harmonious, consonant|antonyms: inharmonic, dissonant|adjective
