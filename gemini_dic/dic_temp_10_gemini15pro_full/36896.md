coloring's:/ˈkʌlərɪŋz/ |n.|着色，上色；着色剂，颜料；（尤指儿童用的）涂色书；色彩，色调|The process or result of adding color to something, or the materials used for this. Also, a book with line drawings for children to color.|例句：The children's coloring's were vibrant and full of life. 孩子们的涂色作品色彩鲜艳，充满活力。

例句：The coloring's of the sunset were breathtaking. 日落时的色彩令人叹为观止。|近义词：colouring, coloration, pigmentation, tint, hue|反义词：bleaching, fading|词性:名词

词源:  From "coloring,"  which comes from the verb "to color" + "-ing" suffix to form a noun.  Ultimately from the Old French "colur" (modern French "couleur"), derived from Latin "color."
