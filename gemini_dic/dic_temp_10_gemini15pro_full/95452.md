incisive:/ɪnˈsaɪsɪv/| adj.|深刻的；敏锐的；尖锐的；果断的|Marked by sharpness, clarity, and effectiveness; penetrating.|His incisive wit made him a popular dinner guest. 他敏锐的机智使他成为受欢迎的晚宴嘉宾。|Synonyms: insightful, perceptive, discerning, clever, brilliant|Antonyms: unperceptive, dull-witted, slow|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *incīsīvus*, from Latin incīdere (“to cut into”), from in (“in, into”) + caedere (“to cut”).
