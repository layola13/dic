anglerfish:/ˈæŋɡlərˌfɪʃ/| n. |鮟鱇鱼|A fish of the order Lophiiformes, typically having a large head and mouth and an elongated dorsal fin ray that resembles a fishing lure.|The anglerfish uses its bioluminescent lure to attract prey in the dark depths of the ocean. 鮟鱇鱼利用其生物发光的诱饵在黑暗的海洋深处吸引猎物。|Synonyms: frogfish, monkfish|Antonyms: N/A|noun

anglerfish:/ˈæŋɡlərˌfɪʃ/| n. |鮟鱇|A bottom-dwelling deep-sea fish with a large head and mouth and a fleshy, luminous growth that projects from its head and acts as a lure to other fish.|The anglerfish lurks on the seabed, waiting to ambush its unsuspecting prey. 鮟鱇潜伏在海底，等待伏击毫无戒心的猎物。|Synonyms: sea devil, fishing frog|Antonyms: N/A|noun
