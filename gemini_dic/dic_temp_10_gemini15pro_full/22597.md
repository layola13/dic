bootstrapped:/ˈbuːtˌstræpt/
adjective
自举的，用自己的资源发展起来的
started with limited resources and built up by oneself, without external help.
The company was bootstrapped from the ground up. 这家公司白手起家。
synonyms: self-funded, self-made, self-starting, independent
antonyms: dependent, subsidized, externally funded
词性: adjective

bootstrapped:/ˈbuːtˌstræpt/
verb
自举，用自己的资源发展
to start and build something with limited resources and without external help.
They bootstrapped their business by reinvesting all their profits. 他们通过将所有利润再投资来发展业务。
synonyms: self-fund, self-start
antonyms: outsource, seek external funding
词性: verb (past participle)
