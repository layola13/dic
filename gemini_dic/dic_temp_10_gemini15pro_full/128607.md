niching:/ˈnɪtʃɪŋ/
n.|利基化，利基市场|The process of finding a specialized but profitable corner of a market. | Many small businesses succeed by niching down and focusing on a specific customer group. 许多小型企业通过利基化并专注于特定的客户群体而获得成功。| specialization, targeting | generalization, mass marketing | noun

niching:/ˈnɪtʃɪŋ/
v.|找到利基市场，开拓利基市场|To establish a niche. |The company niched itself in the luxury travel market. 这家公司在豪华旅游市场找到了自己的利基市场。| specializing, targeting | generalizing, broadening | verb
