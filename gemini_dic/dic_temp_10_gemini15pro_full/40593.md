convalescently: /ˌkɒnvəˈlɛsəntli/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈlɛsəntli/ | adv. | 康复地，渐愈地 | in a way that relates to recovering health after an illness or operation |  He was convalescing convalescently after the surgery.  他术后正在康复中。| Synonym: recuperatively, restfully | Antonym: sickly, ailing | Adverb

convalescent: /ˌkɒnvəˈlɛsənt/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈlɛsənt/ | adj. | 康复的，渐愈的 | recovering health after an illness or operation | The convalescent patient was allowed to sit in the garden.  正在康复的病人被允许坐在花园里。| Synonym: recuperating, recovering | Antonym: sick, ill | Adjective

convalesce: /ˌkɒnvəˈlɛs/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈlɛs/ | v. | 康复，渐愈 | recover one's health and strength over a period of time after an illness or operation | She is convalescing at home after a major operation.  大手术后她正在家康复。| Synonym: recuperate, recover | Antonym: deteriorate, worsen | Verb

Etymology: From Late Latin *convalescere, from Latin convalescere "to grow strong, regain health," from com- "together" (see com-) + valescere "to grow strong" (from PIE root *wal- "to be strong").
