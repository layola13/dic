cringes:/ˈkrɪndʒɪz/| v. (third-person singular simple present)| 畏缩，感到尴尬|  Shrinks physically or recoils mentally, typically in disgust or embarrassment.  It's the third-person singular present tense form of the verb "cringe."|He cringes every time he hears that song. 他每次听到那首歌都感到尴尬。|近义词：winces, recoils, flinches|反义词:embraces, welcomes|词性:verb

cringe:/krɪndʒ/| v. n.| 畏缩，尴尬，奉承| To shrink back in fear or embarrassment; an act of cringing. | The movie was so bad it made me cringe. 这部电影太糟糕了，让我感到尴尬。| Seeing the accident made him cringe. 目睹这场事故让他感到畏缩。|近义词：wince, recoil, flinch, fawn|反义词:embrace, welcome, confront|词性:verb, noun
