adenoid:/ˈædɪnɔɪd/| n. |腺样体;增殖体|A mass of lymphoid tissue at the back of the throat, often removed in childhood because of enlargement. |The doctor recommended removing the child's adenoids. 医生建议切除孩子的腺样体。

|近义词: tonsil |反义词: None|名词

adenoidal:/ˌædɪˈnɔɪdəl/| adj. |腺样体肥大的|Relating to or resembling adenoids, especially in having a nasal voice or blocked nose.|He had an adenoidal voice, a common symptom of enlarged adenoids. 他说话带鼻音，这是腺样体肥大的常见症状。|近义词:nasal|反义词: None|形容词
