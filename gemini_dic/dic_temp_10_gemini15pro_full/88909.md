hold forth:/hoʊld fɔːrθ (US), həʊld fɔːθ (UK)| vi.|滔滔不绝地讲；长篇大论|to speak at length, often in a pompous or tedious way|例句：He held forth for over an hour on the subject of his latest research. 他就他最新的研究滔滔不绝地讲了一个多小时。

例句：She held forth on the importance of a healthy diet. 她大谈健康饮食的重要性。

近义词：expatiate, declaim, lecture, sermonize|反义词: be quiet, listen|词性:动词
