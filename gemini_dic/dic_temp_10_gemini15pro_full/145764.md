overcured:/ˌoʊvərˈkjʊərd/
adj.
过度固化的，过度硫化的
cured beyond the desired or optimal point; excessively hardened or vulcanized.
The resin was overcured, making it brittle and difficult to work with.  树脂过度固化，使其变脆且难以加工。
The tires were overcured and lost their grip. 轮胎过度硫化，失去了抓地力。
近义词: overbaked, overdone, overprocessed
反义词: undercured
词性: adjective
