nondelegates:/nɒnˈdɛlɪɡɪts/ | n. | 非代表; 未委派的代表 | People who are not delegates, or responsibilities that cannot be delegated.  Those who haven't been officially chosen or appointed to represent a group or organization, or tasks and duties that cannot be transferred to another person. | The nondelegates were not allowed to vote at the convention. 非代表不允许在大会上投票。| The CEO made it clear that certain key responsibilities were nondelegates. 首席执行官明确表示某些关键责任是不可转授的。 | near synonyms: non-representatives, unauthorized individuals | antonyms: delegates, representatives | noun
