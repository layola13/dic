opisthocoelous: /ˌɒpɪsˈθəʊsɪləs/ /ˌɑːpɪsˈθoʊsələs/
adjective
后凹的
Having centra (the central part of a vertebra) that are concave posteriorly (on the back side) and convex anteriorly (on the front side).  This refers to the shape of the vertebrae in certain animals.
The vertebrae of some salamanders are opisthocoelous. 一些蝾螈的椎骨是后凹的。
近义词: concave posteriorly
反义词: procoelous
词性: adjective
