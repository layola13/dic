oke:/oʊˈkeɪ/ | adj. |好的，可以的| An informal spelling of "okay." It signifies agreement, acceptance, or that something is satisfactory.  | Example: "Oke, I'll meet you there." 好的，我会在那里见你。 | Example: "Everything's oke." 一切都好。 | 近义词: okay, alright, fine, good | 反义词: bad, awful, terrible | 词性: adjective

词源:  "Oke" is a variant spelling of "okay," which has a somewhat disputed etymology.  A popular theory suggests it originated in the 1830s as an abbreviation of "oll korrect," a humorous misspelling of "all correct."
