oneidas:/oʊˈnaɪdəz/ | n. | 奥奈达人（Oneida people） |  A member of an Iroquoian people originally inhabiting the region around Oneida Lake, New York. Now, members of the Oneida Nation of Wisconsin, the Oneida Indian Nation of New York, and the Six Nations of the Grand River. |  The Oneidas were one of the original Five Nations of the Iroquois Confederacy. 奥奈达人是易洛魁联盟最初的五个国家之一。| The Oneida language is still spoken by some members of the tribe.  一些部落成员仍在使用奥奈达语。 | synonyms: Oneida people, Oneida tribe | antonyms: N/A | noun
