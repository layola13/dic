bougainvillea:/ˌbuːɡənˈvɪliə/ | n. | 九重葛，叶子花 |  A tropical climbing plant with brightly colored bracts, often mistaken for the flower itself.  The actual flowers are small and white, surrounded by these colorful bracts. | The bougainvillea climbed up the wall, adding a splash of color to the garden. 九重葛爬上了墙，为花园增添了一抹色彩。| Example:  My grandmother's garden is full of vibrant bougainvilleas. 我祖母的花园里种满了鲜艳的九重葛。 | Synonyms: paperflower | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
