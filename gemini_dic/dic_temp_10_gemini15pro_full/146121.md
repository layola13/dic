overemphasis:/ˌoʊvərˈɛmfəsɪs/| n.|过分强调，过分重视|An overemphasis on any one aspect can lead to an unbalanced perspective.  对任何一个方面的过分强调都会导致视角失衡。|The media's overemphasis on violence desensitizes viewers. 媒体对暴力的过分强调降低了观众的敏感度。|近义词：overstress, exaggeration|反义词:underemphasis, understatement|词性:noun

Etymology:
From over- + emphasis.  "Over-" signifies "too much" or "excessive." "Emphasis" comes from the Greek word "emphasis" meaning "significance" or "stress."
