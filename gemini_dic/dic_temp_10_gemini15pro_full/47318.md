de-mobbing:/ˌdiːˈmɒbɪŋ/
n.|复员，遣散|The process of discharging soldiers from military service, especially after a war or conflict, and returning them to civilian life.|The de-mobbing of the troops was a slow and complex process. 部队的复员是一个缓慢而复杂的过程。|demobilization, discharge|mobilization, enlistment|noun
de-mobbing:/ˌdiːˈmɒbɪŋ/
n.|裁员，精简机构|The process of reducing the number of employees in a company or organization.|The company announced a de-mobbing exercise due to declining profits.  由于利润下降，公司宣布进行裁员。|downsizing, redundancy, layoff|hiring, recruitment|noun
