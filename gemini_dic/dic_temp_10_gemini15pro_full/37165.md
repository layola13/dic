come-up:/ˈkʌm ʌp/ (US) /ˈkʌm ʌp/ (UK)| n. |出现，发生；成功；晋升；（计划、想法等）被提出；临时住宿；<非正式>令人不快的经历|An unexpected problem came up. 一个意想不到的问题出现了。|近义词：arise, emerge, occur, happen, transpire, materialize, surface, develop, present itself, crop up, turn up, pop up|反义词:go down, disappear, vanish|词性:名词

come-up:/ˈkʌm ʌp/ (US) /ˈkʌm ʌp/ (UK)| phrasal verb |走近；发生；出现；被提出；即将到来；从社会底层崛起；<非正式>提供；拿出|He came up to me and asked for directions. 他走到我跟前问路。|近义词：approach, arrive, appear, arise, emerge|反义词:go down, recede|词性:动词短语

词源：From the Old English cuman "to come" + up "upward, to a higher position".
