finochio:/fɪˈnoʊkioʊ/
n.
fennel, Florence fennel
A cultivated variety of fennel, *Foeniculum vulgare* var. *azoricum*, having a bulbous, overlapping leaf base that is eaten as a vegetable.
Florence fennel, also known as finocchio, has a crisp, anise-flavored bulb.
