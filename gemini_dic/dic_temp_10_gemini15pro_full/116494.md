materials:/məˈtɪəriəlz/ /məˈtɪriəlz/ | n. | 材料，原料 |  The physical components or substances used for construction or manufacturing something.  Derived from Old French "materie" and ultimately from Latin "materia" (matter, timber, stuff). | The building materials were delivered on time. 建筑材料按时交付了。 |  supplies, resources, components, ingredients | immaterial, intangible | noun
