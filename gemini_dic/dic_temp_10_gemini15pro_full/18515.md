benedictional:/ˌbɛnɪˈdɪkʃənəl/
n.
祝福祷告书; 祝福仪式书
A book containing the forms of blessing used in church services.
The bishop raised the benedictional and blessed the congregation. 主教举起祝福祷告书，为会众祝福。
近义词：prayer book, missal
反义词：curse, malediction
词性：名词


benedictional:/ˌbɛnɪˈdɪkʃənəl/
adj.
祝福的
Relating to or expressing a blessing.
The benedictional service was beautiful and moving. 祝福仪式既美丽又感人。
近义词：blessing, consecratory
反义词：cursing, maledictory
词性：形容词

词源:
Late Middle English: from late Latin *benedictionale, from benedictio(n-) ‘blessing’ (see benediction).
