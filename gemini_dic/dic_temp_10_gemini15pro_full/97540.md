ingrateful:/ɪnˈɡreɪtfəl/ | adj. | 忘恩负义的，不领情的 | Not feeling or showing gratitude; unthankful. | She was ungrateful for all the help her friends had given her. 她对朋友们给予的所有帮助都忘恩负义。| ungrateful wretch 忘恩负义的家伙 |  Example: He seemed ungrateful for the gift, barely acknowledging it. 他似乎对这份礼物不领情，几乎没有表示感谢。|Synonyms: thankless, unappreciative, unthankful | Antonyms: grateful, thankful, appreciative | Adjective
