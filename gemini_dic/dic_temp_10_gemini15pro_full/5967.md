ambulacriform:/ˌæmbjʊˈlækrɪfɔːrm/
adjective
走廊状的，柱廊状的
Resembling a cloister or covered walkway, typically with a series of arches or columns.
The ancient Roman bath featured ambulacriform halls lined with statues. 古罗马浴场以走廊状的大厅为特色，两旁排列着雕像。
The monastery's ambulacriform courtyard provided a peaceful space for contemplation. 修道院的柱廊式庭院提供了一个宁静的沉思空间。
Near synonyms:  arcade-like, cloistered, colonnaded, porticoed
Antonyms: open, unconfined
词性: adjective
