bambocciade:/ˌbæmbɒˈtʃɑːdeɪ/ |n.|低俗场景|A scene of low life. | The artist captured the energy and chaos of the marketplace in his bambocciade.  这位艺术家在他的风俗画中捕捉到了市场的活力和混乱。| synonyms: low-life scene, genre scene | antonyms: idealized scene, formal portrait| noun
