nixons:/ˈnɪksənz/
n.
尼克松一家/尼克松的人/尼克松的支持者
Referring to people associated with or supporters of Richard Nixon, the 37th President of the United States.
The Nixons left the White House after the resignation. 尼克松一家在辞职后离开了白宫。
Nixon loyalists, often referred to as Nixons, still defend his legacy. 尼克松的忠实拥护者，通常被称为尼克松的人，仍然捍卫他的政治遗产。
synonyms: Nixonites, Nixon supporters
antonyms: Nixon's opponents, Nixon's critics
