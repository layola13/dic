blessing:/ˈblesɪŋ/| n.|祝福，恩赐，幸事|Something that is good and helpful; approval from someone in authority|例句：May God's blessing be upon you.愿上帝保佑你。

例句：Children are a blessing. 孩子们是上天的恩赐。|近义词：boon, gift, advantage, benefit|反义词：curse, misfortune, affliction|noun
