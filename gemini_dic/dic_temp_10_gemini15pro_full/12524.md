asphaltus:/æsˈfɔːltəs/
n.
沥青
A dark, sticky substance used for paving roads.
The asphaltus on the road was melting in the hot sun.  路上的沥青在烈日下融化了。
synonyms: bitumen, tar, pitch
antonyms:  None (lack of a direct opposite for a substance)
noun
