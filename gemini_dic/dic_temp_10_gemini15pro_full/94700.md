improficiency:/ˌɪmprəˈfɪʃənsi/| n.|不熟练，不精通|The student's improficiency in math was a concern for his teacher.  这个学生数学不熟练让他的老师很担心。|例句：His improficiency in French made it difficult for him to communicate in Paris. 他法语不熟练，这让他在巴黎很难沟通。|近义词：incompetence, inability, inadequacy, lack of skill|反义词：proficiency, skill, expertise, mastery|词性:名词

词源:  Late 16th century from in- ‘not’ + proficiency.
