overcommunicate:/ˌoʊvər kəˈmjuːnɪˌkeɪt/
verb
过度沟通；过分沟通
To communicate excessively, often to the point of redundancy or inefficiency.  The act of sharing too much information or repeating information unnecessarily, sometimes leading to confusion or annoyance.
The manager overcommunicated the new policy, sending multiple emails and holding several meetings on the same topic.  经理过度沟通了新政策，发送了多封电子邮件，并就同一主题举行了多次会议。

She overcommunicates her feelings, which can be overwhelming for some people. 她过度表达自己的感受，这可能会让一些人难以承受。

近义词:overexplain, overdo, belabor
反义词:undercommunicate, withhold information
词性:verb
