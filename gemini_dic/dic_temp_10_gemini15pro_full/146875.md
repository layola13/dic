overinfluencing:/ˌoʊvərˈɪnfluənsɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度影响; 过分影响
Exerting excessive influence on someone or something.
The marketing campaign was criticized for overinfluencing young consumers. 这场营销活动因过度影响年轻消费者而受到批评。
Social media influencers are sometimes accused of overinfluencing their followers' purchasing decisions. 社交媒体影响者有时被指责过度影响其粉丝的购买决定。
近义词: manipulating, dominating, controlling
反义词:  underinfluencing, empowering
