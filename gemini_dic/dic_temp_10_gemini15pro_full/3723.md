aggrievedly:/əˈɡriːvdli/
adverb
委屈地，愤愤不平地
In a manner expressing or suggesting a sense of injury, injustice, or offense.
She looked at him aggrievedly when he suggested she hadn't worked hard enough. 她在他暗示她工作不够努力时，委屈地看了他一眼。
He shook his head aggrievedly, muttering about the unfairness of it all.  他愤愤不平地摇了摇头，嘴里嘀咕着这一切的不公平。
近义词:resentfully, indignantly, bitterly
反义词: cheerfully, happily, contentedly
词性:adverb

词源: From aggrieved + -ly. Aggrieved comes from the Old French agrever, meaning "to aggravate, make worse."
