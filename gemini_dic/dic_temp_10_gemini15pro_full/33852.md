circumpolar:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈpoʊlər/ (US) /ˌsɜːkəmˈpəʊlə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|环极的，绕极的|Situated around or near one of the earth's poles.  Relating to the regions around the North or South Pole.|例句：Circumpolar stars are visible all year round. 环极星终年可见。

例句：Penguins are a circumpolar species in the Southern Hemisphere.企鹅是南半球的环极物种。|近义词：polar|反义词：equatorial|词性: adjective
