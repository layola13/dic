lockful:/ˈlɒkfʊl/ (UK) /ˈlɑːkfʊl/ (US)| n.|一锁之量; 足够装满一把锁的量|A quantity that fills a lock, especially in canals.  It refers to the amount of water needed to operate a canal lock. |The canal lock required two lockfuls of water to raise the boat.  运河船闸需要两锁之量的水才能抬起船。|近义词: lockage|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "trickle" in a figurative sense regarding water volume.)|词性:noun
