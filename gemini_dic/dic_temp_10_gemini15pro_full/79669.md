glottological:/ˌɡlɑːtəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 语言学的，语言学上的 | Relating to the study of language and languages, especially their structure, history, and relationships. |  Glottological research helps us understand how languages evolve. 语言学研究帮助我们理解语言是如何演变的。 | Example: The professor presented a glottological analysis of the ancient text.  教授对古代文本进行了语言学分析。 | Synonyms: linguistic, philological | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps non-linguistic) | word type: adjective

glottologically:/ˌɡlɑːtəˈlɑːdʒɪkli/ | adv. | 从语言学角度 | In a way that relates to the study of language and languages. | The similarities between the two languages were glottologically significant. 这两种语言之间的相似性在语言学上意义重大。 | Example: He examined the text glottologically, focusing on its grammatical structure. 他从语言学角度审视了文本，重点关注其语法结构。 | Synonyms: linguistically | Antonyms: (No direct antonym) | word type: adverb
