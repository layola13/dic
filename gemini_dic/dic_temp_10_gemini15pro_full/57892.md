drifted: /ˈdrɪftɪd/
verb
漂流，飘移；漂泊，游荡
moved slowly, carried by air or water currents; wandered aimlessly
The boat drifted down the river. 小船顺着河流漂流而下。
The snow drifted against the walls. 雪堆积在墙边。
He drifted from job to job. 他从一个工作换到另一个工作，居无定所。
drift, float, wander
be anchored, be fixed
v.
