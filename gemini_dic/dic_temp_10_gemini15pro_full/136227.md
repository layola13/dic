nonremonstrance:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈmɒn.strəns/
n.
lack of protest or objection; acquiescence
absence of protest or complaint.
His nonremonstrance to the new policy surprised everyone. 他对新政策的无异议让每个人都感到惊讶。
The government interpreted their nonremonstrance as consent. 政府将他们的不抗议解释为同意。
近义词: acquiescence, compliance, acceptance, resignation, submissiveness
反义词: protest, objection, resistance, opposition, dissent
词性: noun

Etymology:
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + remonstrance (from Old French remonstrance, from remonstrer "to show," from Late Latin remonstrare "to demonstrate, point out," from re- "again" + monstrare "to show").
