overgenerosity:/ˌoʊvərˌdʒɛnəˈrɑːsəti/
n.
过度慷慨，过分大方
Excessive generosity; giving more than is necessary or wise.
His overgenerosity led him into financial trouble.  他的过分慷慨使他陷入了财务困境。
The charity benefited from the businessman's overgenerosity. 这个慈善机构受益于这位商人的过度慷慨。
近义词: extravagance, prodigality, lavishness
反义词: stinginess, miserliness, parsimony
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + generosity.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much". "Generosity" comes from the Latin "generositas", meaning "nobility of birth or character".
