consistently:/kənˈsɪstəntlɪ/
adverb
始终如一地，一致地
in a way that does not change and continues for a period of time
She has consistently ranked among the top students in her class. 她一直名列班级前茅。
The company has consistently delivered high-quality products. 这家公司一直提供高质量的产品。
近义词: regularly, steadily, uniformly, invariably, constantly
反义词: erratically, inconsistently, irregularly, sporadically
词性: 副词

consistently:/kənˈsɪstəntlɪ/
adverb
一贯地，坚持不懈地
in a manner that is faithful to a particular idea or principle
He consistently argued for a more balanced approach. 他始终如一地主张采取更平衡的方法。
The coach consistently emphasized the importance of teamwork. 教练一直强调团队合作的重要性。
近义词: persistently, steadfastly, resolutely
反义词: waveringly, hesitantly
词性: 副词

词源: From the adjective consistent + -ly, from Latin consistentem (nominative consistens), present participle of consistere "to stand firm, stand still, take a stand; be consistent, subsist," from com- "together" (see com-) + sistere "to cause to stand, place, set, stand still," from PIE root *sta- "to stand, make or be firm."
