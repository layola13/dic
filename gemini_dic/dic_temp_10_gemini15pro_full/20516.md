bivvy:/ˈbɪvi/| n.|帐篷，临时住宿|A temporary shelter, usually a small tent, used especially by soldiers or climbers.|例句：He spent the night in a bivvy. 他在帐篷里过了一夜。

例句：The climbers set up their bivvies on a ledge. 登山者在岩壁上搭起了帐篷。


近义词：tent, shelter, camp|反义词:|词性:noun

bivouac:/ˈbɪvuæk/ |n. vi.|露营，临时营地|A temporary camp without tents or cover, used especially by soldiers or mountaineers. / To stay in such a camp.|例句：The soldiers made bivouac for the night. 士兵们露宿过夜。

例句：They bivouacked at the foot of the mountain. 他们在山脚下露营。
