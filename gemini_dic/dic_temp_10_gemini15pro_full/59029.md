dwarfing:/ˈdwɔːrfɪŋ/
verb|使显得矮小，使相形见绌|Making something appear small or insignificant by comparison.|The skyscrapers dwarfed the surrounding buildings. 摩天大楼使周围的建筑物显得矮小。|overshadowing, eclipsing|accentuating, highlighting|v.
dwarfing:/ˈdwɔːrfɪŋ/
adjective|使显得矮小的，使相形见绌的|Having the effect of making something appear small or insignificant.|The dwarfing effect of the mountains made us feel tiny. 山脉的矮化效应使我们感到渺小。|diminishing, minimizing|magnifying, enlarging|adj.
