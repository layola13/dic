imperishability:/ˌɪmpɛrɪʃəˈbɪləti/
n.
不朽性；永恒性；持久性
The quality or state of being imperishable; inability to decay or be destroyed.
The imperishability of gold makes it a valuable investment.  黄金的不朽性使其成为一项有价值的投资。
近义词：indestructibility, permanence, durability, eternity
反义词：perishability, mortality, transience, ephemerality
词性: noun
