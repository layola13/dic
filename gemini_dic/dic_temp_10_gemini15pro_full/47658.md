debouch:/dɪˈbaʊtʃ/ /diˈbaʊʃ/| vi. n.|涌出，流出；（军队）走出，出动|To emerge from a narrow or confined space into a wider or more open area.  (指从狭窄或受限的空间进入更宽阔或更开放的区域)|The river debouches into the sea. 这条河流入大海。|近义词：emerge, issue, exit, flow|反义词:enter, converge|verb

debouch:/dɪˈbaʊtʃ/ /diˈbaʊʃ/| n.|出口，河口|The place where a river or stream flows into a larger body of water or an open area. (指河流或溪流流入较大水域或开阔地区的出口)|The debouch of the river is a popular fishing spot.  这条河的河口是一个受欢迎的钓鱼地点。|近义词：outlet, mouth, exit|反义词:inlet, source|noun
