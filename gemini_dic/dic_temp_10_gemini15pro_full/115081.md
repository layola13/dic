maneuvering:/məˈnuːvərɪŋ/ | n. | 策略，调遣；操纵； maneuvering (美式) /məˈnuːvərɪŋ/  |The complex maneuvering required to get the large truck into the tight parking space was impressive.  将大型卡车开进狭窄的停车位所需的复杂操作令人印象深刻。|近义词：manipulation, strategy, tactic |反义词: inaction, stillness |词性: noun (名词)
maneuver:/məˈnuːvər/ | v. | 操纵，调遣； maneuvering (美式) /məˈnuːvər/ |She maneuvered the car expertly through the heavy traffic. 她熟练地驾驶汽车穿过拥挤的交通。|近义词：manipulate, steer, navigate |反义词: neglect, abandon |词性: verb (动词)

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *manœuvrer*, from Italian *manovrare*, from *mano* (“hand”) and *opera* (“work, task”). Ultimately from Latin *manus* (“hand”) and *opera* (“work”).
