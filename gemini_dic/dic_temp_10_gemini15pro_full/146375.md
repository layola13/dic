overflooding:/ˌoʊvərˈflʌdɪŋ/
|n.|泛滥，过多，过量
|The act of flooding excessively; an overwhelming amount or influx.|
|The river burst its banks, causing widespread overflooding in the surrounding areas. 这条河决堤了，导致周边地区大面积泛滥。|
|flood, deluge, inundation, overflow|
|drought, scarcity, lack|
|noun|


overflooding:/ˌoʊvərˈflʌdɪŋ/
|v.|（使）泛滥，过多，过量（通常用于被动语态）
|To flood excessively; to overwhelm with an excessive amount or influx.|
|The market was overflooding with cheap imports. 市场充斥着廉价进口商品。|
|flood, deluge, inundate, overwhelm, saturate|
|drain, dry, deplete|
|verb|
