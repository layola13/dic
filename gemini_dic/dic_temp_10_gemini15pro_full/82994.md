gunnies:/ˈɡʌniz/
n.
粗麻布袋；装粗麻布袋的货物
large sacks made of coarse material (such as burlap)
The farmer loaded the gunnies of potatoes onto the truck.  农民把一袋袋的土豆装上了卡车。

近义词:sacks, bags
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps containers made of finer material)
词性:noun
