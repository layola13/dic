hepatocystic:/ˌhɛpətəˈsɪstɪk/
adjective
肝囊的；与肝和胆囊有关的
Relating to the liver and gallbladder or the hepatic duct and cystic duct.
The hepatocystic triangle is an important anatomical landmark during gallbladder surgery.  肝囊三角是胆囊手术中的一个重要解剖标志。
