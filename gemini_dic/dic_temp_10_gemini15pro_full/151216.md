pardoning:/ˈpɑːrdnɪŋ/ /ˈpɑːrdənɪŋ/
verb
原谅，赦免
The act of forgiving or excusing a fault or offense.  Officially absolving someone from the consequences of a crime.
The president is considering pardoning the convicted spy. 总统正在考虑赦免被定罪的间谍。
synonyms: forgiving, excusing, absolving, remitting, reprieving
antonyms: condemning, punishing, convicting
