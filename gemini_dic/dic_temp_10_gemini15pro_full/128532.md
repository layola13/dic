nextly:/ˈnɛkstli/ | adv. | 其次地，接下来 | Subsequently, following in order or time.  |  Nextly, we will discuss the budget for the next quarter. 其次，我们将讨论下个季度的预算。 |  Nextly, I'd like to thank all the volunteers who helped make this event possible. 接下来，我要感谢所有帮助促成此次活动的志愿者们。 | 近义词: subsequently, then, afterward, next | 反义词: previously, beforehand, firstly | 词性: 副词

词源:  Formed by adding the adverbial suffix "-ly" to the adjective "next".
