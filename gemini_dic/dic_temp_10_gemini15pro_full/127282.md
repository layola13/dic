navies:/ˈneɪviz/| n. |海军，海军力量|A nation's warships; a fleet of warships. The combined strength of a country's warships.  |例句：The navies of several countries participated in the joint exercise.  几个国家的海军参加了联合演习。

例句：The country invested heavily in modernizing its navies.  这个国家大力投资现代化海军。|近义词：fleets, armadas|反义词: None (lack of navies implies vulnerability, not an opposing force)|词性:名词
