fuelers:/ˈfjuːələrz/
noun
加油员；燃料添加者
People who supply fuel to vehicles or machinery.
The fuelers quickly filled the airplane's tanks. 加油员迅速加满了飞机的油箱。
Refuelers, gas attendants
