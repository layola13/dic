miserable:/ˈmɪzrəbl/| adj.|悲惨的，痛苦的，可怜的|Wretchedly unhappy or uncomfortable.|The weather was cold, wet, and miserable. 天气寒冷、潮湿、令人难受。|Synonyms: unhappy, wretched, sorrowful, dejected, depressed|Antonyms: happy, cheerful, joyful, content|adjective
