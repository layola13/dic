generator:/ˈdʒɛnəˌreɪtər/| n.|发电机；发生器；生成器|A machine that converts one form of energy into another, especially mechanical energy into electrical energy; a person or thing that generates something.|The hospital has a backup generator in case of power outages. 医院有一台备用发电机，以防停电。|synonyms: alternator, dynamo, producer, creator|antonyms: consumer, destroyer|noun

generator:/ˈdʒɛnəˌreɪtər/| n.|发电机；发生器；生成器|A person or thing that originates or produces something.|He is a generator of innovative ideas. 他是一个创新思想的创造者。|synonyms: originator, initiator, inventor|antonyms: imitator, follower|noun
