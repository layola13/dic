misrepresentation:/ˌmɪsˌrɛprɪzɛnˈteɪʃən/| n. |失实陈述|He claimed that the company's financial reports contained several serious misrepresentations. 他声称该公司的财务报告中包含几处严重的失实陈述。|synonyms: inaccuracy, falsehood, lie|antonyms: accuracy, truthfulness, veracity|noun
