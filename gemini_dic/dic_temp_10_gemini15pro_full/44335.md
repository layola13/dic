croquette:/kroʊˈkɛt/| n. |炸丸子，可乐饼|A small roll of minced meat, fish, or vegetables, coated in breadcrumbs and deep-fried.|例句：I love potato croquettes. 我喜欢土豆可乐饼。

例句：These salmon croquettes are delicious. 这些鲑鱼炸丸子真好吃。

近义词：patty, fritter, rissole|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
