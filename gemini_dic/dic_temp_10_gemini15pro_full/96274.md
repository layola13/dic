indictment:/ɪnˈdaɪtmənt/| n. |起诉书，控告，谴责|a formal accusation that someone has committed a crime|The grand jury handed down an indictment against the mayor. 大陪审团对市长做出了起诉。|synonyms: accusation, charge, prosecution|antonyms: acquittal, exoneration|noun

indictment:/ɪnˈdaɪtmənt/| n. |谴责，控诉|a thing that serves to illustrate that a system or situation is bad and deserves to be condemned|The poverty in the city is a stark indictment of the government's policies. 城市的贫困是对政府政策的严厉谴责。|synonyms: condemnation, criticism, denunciation|antonyms: praise, commendation|noun
