experimentors:/ɪkˈspɛrɪməntərz/
n.
实验者,试验者
People who conduct experiments are called experimentors.
进行实验的人被称为实验者。
The experimentors carefully recorded the results.
实验者仔细地记录了结果。
近义词:researchers, scientists, investigators
反义词:subjects, participants
词性:名词
