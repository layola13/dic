compassless:/ˈkʌmpəsləs/| adj.|无指南针的，迷失方向的|Lacking a compass, either literally or figuratively; lost and without direction. |例句：The compassless ship drifted aimlessly in the fog. 那艘没有指南针的船在雾中漫无目的地漂流。|例句：He felt compassless in his new career, unsure of which direction to take.  他在新的职业生涯中感到迷茫，不知道该往哪个方向发展。|近义词：lost, disoriented, bewildered, adrift|反义词:guided, oriented, directed|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From "compass" (navigational instrument) + "-less" (suffix indicating absence). The word "compass" ultimately comes from the Old French "compas" meaning "circle, circuit, pair of compasses," from the Late Latin "compassus," past participle of "compassare" meaning "to measure by steps, pace off."
