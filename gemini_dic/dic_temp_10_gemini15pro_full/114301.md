mainstays:/ˈmeɪnsteɪz/ | n. | 支柱，中流砥柱 |A mainstay is a key element or part that provides essential support or strength to something.  It can refer to a person, thing, or idea that is central to the existence or success of something else. | Tourism is a mainstay of the local economy.  旅游业是当地经济的支柱。| synonyms:  pillar, cornerstone, linchpin, support, foundation | antonyms: periphery, minor element | noun

Etymology:
From "main" (principal) + "stay" (support).  Originally a nautical term referring to a large rope supporting the mast of a ship.
