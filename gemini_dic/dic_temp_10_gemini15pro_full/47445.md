deafest:/ˈdɛfɪst/ (US), /ˈdɛfɪst/ (UK)| adj.|最聋的|Superlative form of deaf; most unable to hear.|例句：He's the deafest person I've ever met. 他是我见过的最聋的人。

例句：Even with the hearing aid, she remained the deafest in the group. 即使戴着助听器，她仍然是小组中最聋的。

近义词：hardest of hearing, most impaired hearing|反义词:hearing, sharpest hearing|词性:形容词
