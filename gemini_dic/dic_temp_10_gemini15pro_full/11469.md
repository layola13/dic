argutation:/ˌɑːrɡjuˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 争论，辩论，论证 | The process of reasoning systematically in support of an idea, action, or theory. |  His argutation was well-reasoned and persuasive. 他的论证很有道理，也很有说服力。

|  The argutation continued late into the night. 辩论一直持续到深夜。 | 近义词：argument, debate, reasoning, disputation | 反义词：agreement, concord, harmony | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *argūtātiōn-, from Latin argūtāre  "to prate, chatter, argue," frequentative of arguere "to make clear, prove, accuse."
