gospels:/ˈɡɑːspəlz/ | n. | 福音，福音书 | The four Gospels are Matthew, Mark, Luke, and John. 四福音书是马太福音、马可福音、路加福音和约翰福音。| The Gospels tell the story of Jesus Christ's life. 福音书讲述了耶稣基督的生平。 | synonyms: scriptures, testament | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a religious text) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *gōdspel (unattested), from gōd "good" + spel "story, message".  Cognate with Old Saxon godspell, Old Frisian godspell, Old High German gotspel, all from Proto-Germanic *gōda-spellą. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ghdʰ- "good" + *spel- "to tell".
