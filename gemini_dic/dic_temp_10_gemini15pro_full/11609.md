arm-twisted:/ˈɑːrmˌtwɪstɪd/
adjective
受到胁迫的，被迫的
persuaded to do something against one's will by the use of threats or other forms of pressure.
例句：I felt arm-twisted into agreeing to help. 我感觉是被迫同意帮忙的。

例句：He was arm-twisted by his boss to work overtime. 他被老板强迫加班。

近义词：coerced, pressured, forced
反义词：willing, voluntary
词性：adjective
