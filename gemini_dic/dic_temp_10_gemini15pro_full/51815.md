dewclaw:/ˈdjuːklɔː/ /ˈduːklɔː/
n.
悬爪，上爪，假指甲
A dewclaw is a small claw on the inside of a dog's, cat's, or other animal's leg, slightly higher up than the other claws. It does not usually touch the ground when the animal walks.

The dog's dewclaws were removed shortly after birth.
这只狗的悬爪在出生后不久就被去除了。

The cat sharpened its claws, including the dewclaws, on the scratching post.
这只猫在猫抓柱上磨它的爪子，包括上爪。


近义词: rudimentary claw, accessory claw
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "main claw")
词性: noun
