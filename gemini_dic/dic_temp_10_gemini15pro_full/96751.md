ineluctably:/ˌɪnɪˈlʌktəbli/ | adv. | 不可避免地，必然地 | Unable to be resisted or avoided; inescapable. |  The events of that fateful day led ineluctably to war. 那灾难性的一天发生的事件不可避免地导致了战争。|  His arguments led ineluctably to the conclusion that change was needed. 他的论证不可避免地得出了需要改变的结论。| 近义词: inevitably, unavoidably, necessarily | 反义词: avoidably, preventably | 词性: 副词

词源:  From Latin *inelutabilis*, from in- 'not' + eluctari 'struggle out of'.
