noncausalities:/ˌnɒnˌkɔːˈzælɪtiz/ | n. | 非因果关系；非因果性 |  The absence of a cause-and-effect relationship between two or more phenomena.  It implies that the events are not linked by a direct causal chain. | The study explored the noncausalities between economic growth and social progress in developing countries. 这项研究探讨了发展中国家经济增长与社会进步之间的非因果关系。|  non-relationships, correlations without causation | causalities | noun
