depascent:/dɪˈpæsənt/
adjective
放牧的；吃草的
Feeding on growing herbage; grazing.
例句：The depascent sheep dotted the hillside.  吃草的羊群点缀着山坡。

例句：Depascent cattle grazed peacefully in the meadow.  吃草的牛群在草地上平静地吃草。

近义词：grazing, pasturing, browsing
反义词：predatory, carnivorous
词性：adjective

词源：From Latin *dēpāscent- (stem of *dēpāscēns), present participle of dēpāscī (“to feed upon, graze down”), from dē (“down, off”) + pāscō (“to feed, nourish, cherish”).
