gullet:/ˈɡʌlɪt/| n. |食道，咽喉；海峡|The food goes down the gullet into the stomach. 食物通过食道进入胃。|例句：A piece of steak got stuck in his gullet. 一块牛排卡在他的食道里。

例句：The narrow gullet between the islands was difficult to navigate. 岛屿之间的狭窄海峡难以航行。|近义词：esophagus, throat|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

Etymology: From Old French *goulet "throat, opening," diminutive of goule "throat," from Latin gula "throat."
