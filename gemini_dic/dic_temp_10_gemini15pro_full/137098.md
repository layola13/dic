nonsubconsciousnesses:/nɒnˌsʌbˈkɒnʃəsnəsɪz/
|n.| 非潜意识状态 | The state or quality of not being subconscious; awareness.  This word highlights the aspect of being fully conscious and aware, as opposed to thoughts or feelings that are repressed or not fully understood. |His nonsubconsciousness of the danger was alarming. 他对危险的非潜意识状态令人担忧。| Near synonyms: awareness, consciousness, mindfulness | Antonyms: subconsciousness, unconsciousness | Noun
