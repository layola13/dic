burrel:/ˈbʌrəl/ | n. |粗麻布; 粗麻布衣服; 粗糙的披风 | A coarse, rough fabric, typically made from hemp or jute, used for clothing or coverings.  It can also refer to a garment made from this fabric, especially a rough cloak. | He wore a simple burrel to protect himself from the wind and rain. 他穿着一件简单的粗麻布衣服来遮风挡雨。| The monk's burrel was worn and patched.  和尚的粗麻布披风又旧又补丁.| 近义词: sackcloth, hessian | 反义词: silk, satin | 词性: noun

词源: From Old French *burel, from bure "dark brown cloth". Ultimately from Latin *burrus "reddish-brown".
