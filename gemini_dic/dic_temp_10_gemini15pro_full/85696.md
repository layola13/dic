havens:/ˈheɪvənz/| n.|避难所，安全港|A place of safety or refuge.|例句：These islands have become havens for wildlife. 这些岛屿已成为野生动物的避难所。|例句：The library was a haven of peace and quiet. 图书馆是一个宁静的避难所。|近义词：sanctuaries, refuges, shelters|反义词：dangers, threats, perils|词性:名词

词源: From Old English *hæfen "haven, port," from Proto-Germanic *hafno (source also of Old Norse höfn "haven," Old Frisian hafene, Middle Dutch havene, Dutch haven, German Hafen), from PIE root *kap- "to grasp, hold, contain."
