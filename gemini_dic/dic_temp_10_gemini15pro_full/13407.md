atomization:/ˌætəmaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. |  雾化；原子化；分裂 | The process of reducing something to fine particles or spray, or the state of being reduced to fine particles or spray. Also, the process of breaking something down into smaller, independent units. | The atomization of the liquid fuel is crucial for efficient combustion. 液体燃料的雾化对于有效燃烧至关重要。

The atomization of society into individuals can lead to feelings of isolation. 社会原子化成个体可能导致孤立感。 | synonyms: spraying, nebulization, disintegration, fragmentation | antonyms: unification, consolidation, integration | noun
