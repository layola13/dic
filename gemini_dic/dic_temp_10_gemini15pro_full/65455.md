eurhythmicses: /juːˈrɪðmɪksɪz/
noun
优律学，节奏体操
A system of coordinated exercises, often set to music, designed to develop bodily rhythm and grace.
The school offers classes in eurhythmics for children aged 3-5.  这所学校为3-5岁的孩子提供优律学课程。
Near synonyms: rhythmic movement, rhythmic gymnastics, dance therapy
Antonyms: arrhythmia, discoordination
词性: noun
