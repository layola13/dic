enshield:/ɪnˈʃiːld/
verb
保护，掩护
to protect or shield someone or something
The knight enshielded the princess from the dragon's fiery breath. 骑士保护公主免受火龙的火焰气息。
The soldiers enshielded themselves from enemy fire. 士兵们掩护自己免受敌人的炮火。
近义词: protect, shield, defend, safeguard, guard
反义词: expose, endanger
