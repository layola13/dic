maximises:/ˈmæksɪmaɪzɪz/
verb
最大化
Makes as large or great as possible; increases to the greatest possible degree.
The company maximises profits by keeping costs low. 公司通过保持低成本将利润最大化。
synonyms: optimize, boost, amplify, enhance, augment
antonyms: minimize, reduce, decrease, diminish
词性: 动词(第三人称单数)
