germinate:/ˈdʒɜːrmɪneɪt/| v. |发芽，萌芽，开始发展|to begin to grow or develop|例句：The seeds will germinate in a few days. 种子几天后就会发芽。

例句：His ideas germinated into a successful business. 他的想法发展成了一项成功的生意。|近义词：sprout, develop, grow, burgeon|反义词: wither, die, stagnate|词性:动词

germination:/ˌdʒɜːrmɪˈneɪʃn/| n. |发芽，萌芽|the process of germinating|例句：The germination of the seeds takes about a week.  种子的发芽大约需要一周时间。

例句：Warmth and moisture are essential for germination. 温暖和水分对发芽至关重要。|近义词：sprouting, development, growth|反义词: decay, withering|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin germinatus, past participle of germinare "to sprout," from Latin germen "bud, sprout."
