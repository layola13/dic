deactuating:/diːˈæktʃueɪtɪŋ/
verb
使失效，解除，停止运作
The process of stopping a mechanism or device from operating, typically by releasing a control or removing power.
The pilot deactuated the autopilot system before landing.  飞行员在着陆前关闭了自动驾驶系统。
The safety system automatically deactivates the engine in case of overheating.  安全系统会在发动机过热时自动使其停止运作。
deactivate, disable, disengage
activate, engage, enable
