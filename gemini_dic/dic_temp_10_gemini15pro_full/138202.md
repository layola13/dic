noselessly:/ˈnoʊzləsli/| adv.|无鼻地，没有鼻子地|Without a nose; in a manner suggesting the absence of a nose.  Figuratively, it can imply a lack of discernment or sensitivity.|He sniffed noselessly at the flower, unable to appreciate its fragrance. 他无鼻地嗅着花，无法欣赏它的芬芳。|Synonyms: insensitively, unperceptively|Antonyms: sensitively, perceptively|adverb
