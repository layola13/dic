entrepreneur: /ˌɒntrəprəˈnɜːr/ (UK) /ˌɑːntrəprəˈnɜːr/ (US)| n.| 企业家，创业者|A person who organizes and operates a business or businesses, taking on greater than normal financial risks in order to do so.|  Elon Musk is a well-known entrepreneur.  埃隆·马斯克是一位著名的企业家。

Mark Zuckerberg, the founder of Facebook, is a successful entrepreneur.  脸书的创始人马克·扎克伯格是一位成功的企业家。|近义词：businessman, businesswoman, founder, initiator|反义词: employee, worker|词性:noun

Etymology: From French *entrepreneur*, from *entreprendre* "to undertake," from *entre-* "between" + *prendre* "to take."
