deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbərət/ /dɪˈlɪbəreɪt/ | adj.  v. | 故意的，深思熟虑的；仔细考虑 | done intentionally; carefully thought out |  He made a deliberate attempt to provoke a fight. 他故意挑起争端。| synonyms: intentional, planned, conscious, purposeful | antonyms: unintentional, accidental, spontaneous, impulsive | adjective

deliberate:/dɪˈlɪbərət/ /dɪˈlɪbəreɪt/ | v. | 仔细考虑，商议 | think about or discuss something very carefully | The jury deliberated for two days before reaching a verdict. 陪审团商议了两天才作出裁决。 | synonyms: consider, ponder, contemplate, discuss | antonyms: disregard, ignore, neglect | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin  *dēlīberātus*, past participle of *dēlīberāre*  "to weigh in one's mind, consider,"  from *de-* "from" + *lībra* "scales, balance."
