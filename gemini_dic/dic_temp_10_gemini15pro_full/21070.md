blindly:/ˈblaɪndli/| adv.|盲目地，轻率地|Without thinking or paying attention to the consequences; in a way that shows poor judgment.|She blindly followed the instructions without understanding them. 她盲目地遵照指示，却不明白其中的含义。|例句：He blindly accepted her explanation. 他盲目地接受了她的解释。|近义词：recklessly, thoughtlessly, foolishly, unthinkingly|反义词：carefully, cautiously, thoughtfully, prudently|词性:副词
blindly:/ˈblaɪndli/| adv.|不加思考地|Without using one's judgment or considering the possible consequences.|He blindly followed the crowd. 他不假思索地跟着人群走。|例句：She blindly trusted him with her money. 她不加思考地把钱托付给了他。|近义词：implicitly, unquestioningly|反义词：critically, skeptically|词性:副词
