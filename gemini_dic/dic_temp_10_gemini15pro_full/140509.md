oleaceous:/ˌoʊliˈeɪʃəs/
adjective
油橄榄的；似油的；产油的
of, relating to, or resembling the olive or the olive family.
The oleaceous scent of the lotion reminded her of the Mediterranean.  这种乳液的橄榄香味让她想起了地中海。
synonyms: olive-like, oily
antonyms: non-oily, dry
Etymology:  From Latin *oleaceus*, from olea (“olive”) + -aceus.
