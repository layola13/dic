misstarts:/ˌmɪsˈstɑːrts/
noun
错误的开始；不顺利的开端
A misstart is a beginning that is not successful or goes wrong.
The project suffered from a series of misstarts and delays. 这个项目遭受了一系列的错误开端和延误。
synonyms: false starts, bad starts, unsuccessful beginnings
antonyms: good starts, successful beginnings
n.
