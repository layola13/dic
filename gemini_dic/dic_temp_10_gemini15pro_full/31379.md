charcoaly: /ˈtʃɑːrkoʊli/ | adj. | 炭色的，像木炭的 | Resembling charcoal in color; dark gray or black. | The artist used a charcoaly shade to depict the shadows. 艺术家用炭黑色描绘阴影。 |  The dress was a charcoaly grey, almost black. 这件连衣裙是炭灰色，几乎是黑色的。 | 近义词: dark gray, blackish, sooty, carbonous | 反义词: light, bright, colorful | 词性: 形容词

词源: From charcoal + -y.  "charcoal" comes from Old English *ċearcol (attested only in compounds), from ċeorfan "to carve" + col "coal." The suffix "-y" is used to form adjectives meaning "having the quality of" or "full of."
