disinterest's:/dɪsˈɪntrɪsts/
noun
lack of interest; indifference; impartiality
The judge's disinterest in the case ensured a fair trial.  法官在此案中不偏不倚，确保了审判的公正。
His disinterest in politics is well known. 他对政治不感兴趣是众所周知的。
Synonyms: apathy, detachment, neutrality
Antonyms: interest, enthusiasm, bias, partiality
n.
