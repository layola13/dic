morphined:/ˈmɔːrfiːnd/ | v. |  用吗啡治疗 | To treat with morphine. | The doctor morphedined the patient to alleviate the pain. 医生用吗啡给病人止痛。| Synonym:  medicate (with morphine), dose (with morphine) | Antonym:  stimulate | verb
