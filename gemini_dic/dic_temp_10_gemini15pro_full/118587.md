mesotrochal:/ˈmɛzəˌtrɒkəl/ | adj. | 中环轮幼虫的 | Having a band of cilia around the middle of the body, as in certain larval forms of invertebrates, especially mollusks and annelids. |  The mesotrochal larva swims freely in the water column.  中环轮幼虫在水体中自由游动。| Synonym:  mid-ringed (less common) | Antonym:  telo-trochal, prototrochal | adjective
