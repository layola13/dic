deepening:/ˈdiːpənɪŋ/| n. adj. v.|加深，深化；变深的，加深的；加深的行为|The deepening crisis requires immediate attention.  不断加深的危机需要立即关注。|近义词：intensifying, increasing, strengthening|反义词: lessening, weakening, reducing|词性:名词, 形容词, 动名词

Etymology: From "deep" + "-en" (to make) + "-ing" (present participle/gerund suffix).  "Deep" comes from Old English "deop".
