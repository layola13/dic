inflect:/ɪnˈflɛkt/ (US), /ɪnˈflɛkt/ (UK)| v.|使变形；使屈折；改变音调|Change the form of (a word) to express a grammatical function or attribute, typically tense, mood, person, number, case, and gender.|You need to inflect the verb to agree with the subject.|近义词：modulate, vary, conjugate, decline|反义词: maintain, preserve|词性: verb
