gamekeeping:/ˈɡeɪmˌkiːpɪŋ/
n.
狩猎看守；猎场管理
the activity of taking care of wild animals and birds that are kept especially for hunting
The estate employs two gamekeepers.  这个庄园雇佣了两名猎场看守。

近义词：wildlife management, hunting management
反义词：poaching

词性: noun
