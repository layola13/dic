commissive:/kəˈmɪsɪv/
adjective
承诺的; 承担义务的
Creating a binding obligation or commitment.  Expressing a promise or undertaking.
例句：
A commissive utterance, such as "I promise to pay you back," creates an obligation.
一句承诺的话语，例如“我保证还你钱”，就产生了一种义务。

The contract contains several commissive clauses, guaranteeing specific actions by both parties.
该合同包含若干承诺条款，保证双方采取具体行动。

近义词：obligatory, binding, pledging
反义词：noncommittal, unobliging

词性: adjective
