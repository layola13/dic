misawarded:/ˌmɪsəˈwɔːrdɪd/
adjective
错误地授予的；不当授予的
incorrectly awarded or given
The prize was misawarded to the wrong contestant.  这个奖项被错误地颁发给了错误的选手。
The medal was misawarded to the runner-up instead of the winner. 这枚奖牌被错误地颁发给了亚军而不是冠军。
Synonyms: wrongly awarded, improperly given, mistakenly bestowed
Antonyms: correctly awarded, properly given, deservedly bestowed
word class: adjective

Etymology:
"mis-" (prefix meaning "wrongly" or "badly") + "award" (verb meaning "to give or grant something as a prize or reward") + "-ed" (past participle suffix)
