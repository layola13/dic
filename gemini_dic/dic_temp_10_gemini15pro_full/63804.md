epidemiology: /ˌɛpɪˌdiːmiˈɒlədʒi/ (UK) /ˌɛpɪˌdiːmiˈɑːlədʒi/ (US)| n.| 流行病学 |The study of the distribution and determinants of health-related states or events in specified populations, and the application of this study to the control of health problems. | Epidemiology plays a crucial role in understanding and controlling the spread of diseases. 流行病学在理解和控制疾病传播方面起着至关重要的作用。| synonyms: study of disease, study of epidemics | antonyms: N/A | noun
