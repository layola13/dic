caribou:/ˈkærɪbuː/ (NAmE) /ˈkærɪbʊ/ (BrE)| n. |驯鹿；北美驯鹿|A large deer with long, branching antlers, found in the northern regions of North America, Europe, and Asia.  It is closely related to the reindeer of Europe.|The caribou migrate south every winter. 驯鹿每年冬天都会向南迁徙。|近义词：reindeer|反义词:|noun
