compatibly:/kəmˈpætɪbli/ | adv. | 兼容地，和谐地 | In a way that is compatible; in harmony. | The two systems work compatibly. 这两个系统兼容地工作。|近义词：harmoniously, consistently, appropriately |反义词：incompatibly, discordantly |词性:副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *compatibilis*, from Latin com- "together" + pati "to suffer, endure."  The sense evolution is from "able to exist together" to "harmonious."
