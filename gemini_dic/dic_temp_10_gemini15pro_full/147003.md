overlade:/ˌoʊvərˈleɪd/| v. |超载，负担过重，过度使用|To overload or overburden someone or something with work, responsibility, or information.  To cover something with too many layers or too much of something.|例句：The camel was overladed with baggage.  骆驼被行李超载了。

例句：He was overladed with work and felt stressed. 他工作负担过重，感到压力很大。|近义词：overload, overburden, overtax|反义词:underload|词性：动词
