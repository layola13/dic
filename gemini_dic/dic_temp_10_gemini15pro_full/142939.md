out-vote:/ˌaʊtˈvoʊt/
verb
以票数胜过,投票击败
defeat (someone) by winning more votes.
The proposal was out-voted by a large margin.  这个提案以很大的优势被否决了。
The chairman was out-voted by the board. 董事长被董事会投票否决了。

近义词: defeat, overrule
反义词: lose to

词性:v.
