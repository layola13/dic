nomineeism:/ˌnɒmɪˈniːɪzəm/
n.
任命主义；被提名者的身份或状态
The practice of appointing people to positions of authority based on their loyalty or political connections rather than their qualifications or merit.  It often leads to incompetence and corruption.
The widespread nomineeism in the government agencies has led to a decline in public trust. 政府机构中普遍存在的任命主义导致了公众信任的下降。
近义词: cronyism, patronage, favoritism
反义词: meritocracy
词性: noun
