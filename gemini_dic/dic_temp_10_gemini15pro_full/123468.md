monospermous:/ˌmɒnəˈspɜːməs/ | /ˌmɑːnəˈspɜːrməs/ | adj. | 单种子的 | Containing only one seed. |  Example: The monospermous fruit of the dandelion is dispersed by the wind. 蒲公英的单种子果实靠风传播。| Example:  Some species of plants produce monospermous pods while others produce pods with multiple seeds.  一些植物物种产生单种子的豆荚，而其他物种产生多种子的豆荚。 | Synonym: single-seeded, unispermous | Antonym: polyspermous, multiseeded | adjective
