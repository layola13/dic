drossel:/ˈdrɒsəl/| vt.|扼杀，压制，限制|To strangle, suppress, or restrict.|The government tried to drossel the flow of information. 政府试图压制信息的流通。|Fear drosseled her creativity.  恐惧扼杀了她的创造力。|近义词:suppress, stifle, restrict, choke|反义词:promote, encourage, liberate|词性:verb
