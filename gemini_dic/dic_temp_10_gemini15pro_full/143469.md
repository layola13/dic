outgained:/ˌaʊtˈɡeɪnd/
verb
胜过，超过（在收益或产量方面）
To surpass in gains or production.
Our company outgained all its competitors last quarter. 我们公司上个季度超过了所有竞争对手。
The new advertising campaign outgained the previous one by a significant margin. 新的广告活动在收益上大大超过了之前的广告活动。
Synonyms: surpassed, exceeded, outstripped, outperformed
Antonyms: lagged behind, underperformed
词性：动词
