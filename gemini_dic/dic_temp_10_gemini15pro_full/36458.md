cold-turkeyed:/ˌkoʊld ˈtɜːrkiːd/
verb
突然戒掉(尤指毒瘾或坏习惯);
abruptly stopping the use of an addictive substance (such as drugs or alcohol or tobacco or food)
He cold-turkeyed his smoking habit. 他突然戒掉了烟瘾。
近义词: abruptly stopped, quit suddenly, withdrawn suddenly
反义词: gradually reduced, tapered off
词性: verb

cold-turkey:/ˌkoʊld ˈtɜːrkiː/
adverb
突然地;一下子;
suddenly and completely
She quit smoking cold turkey. 她一下子戒了烟。
近义词: abruptly, suddenly, completely
反义词: gradually, slowly
词性: adverb

cold turkey:/ˌkoʊld ˈtɜːrkiː/
noun
突然戒断(毒瘾等);
the sudden and complete cessation of taking a drug to which one is addicted
Going cold turkey can be dangerous. 突然戒断(毒瘾等)可能是危险的。
近义词: abrupt cessation, sudden withdrawal
反义词: gradual reduction, tapering off
词性: noun
