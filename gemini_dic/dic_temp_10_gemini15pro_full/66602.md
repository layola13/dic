exigencies:/ˈɛksɪdʒənsiz/ /ɪɡˈzɪdʒənsiz/
|n.|紧急情况；迫切需要；苛求|pressing needs; urgent demands; difficult situations|例句：The exigencies of the situation demanded immediate action.  情况紧急，需要立即采取行动。

例句：The government had to respond to the exigencies of the flood. 政府必须应对洪水的紧急情况。
|近义词：demands, needs, requirements, pressures, urgencies|反义词:luxuries, comforts|词性:名词
