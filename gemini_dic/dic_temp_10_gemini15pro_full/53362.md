disaffectionate:/ˌdɪsəˈfɛkʃənət/ | adj. | 不友好的，冷淡的，不亲热的 | Not showing affection; unloving. |  He gave his mother a disaffectionate glance. 他冷漠地瞥了他的母亲一眼。

The disaffectionate teenager barely acknowledged his parents.  这个冷淡的青少年几乎不理会他的父母。

近义词: aloof, cold, distant, indifferent, uncaring, unresponsive | 反义词: affectionate, loving, warm, caring, devoted | 词性: adjective

词源: Late 16th century: from dis- (expressing reversal) + affectionate.
