overliberality:/ˌoʊvərˌlɪbərˈælɪti/
n.
过度慷慨，过分大方
Excessive generosity or lavishness.
His overliberality with company funds led to his dismissal. 他对公司资金的过度慷慨导致了他的解雇。
The king's overliberality quickly depleted the royal treasury. 国王的过度慷慨很快耗尽了皇家金库。

近义词: extravagance, lavishness, prodigality, generosity
反义词: stinginess, miserliness, parsimony, frugality
词性: noun
