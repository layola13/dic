nonpessimistically:/ˌnɒnpɛsɪˈmɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 非悲观地 | In a way that is not pessimistic; with optimism or hope. |  She nonpessimistically approached the challenge, believing she could succeed. 她非悲观地迎接挑战，相信自己能够成功。|  He nonpessimistically predicted a positive outcome. 他非悲观地预测了积极的结果。 | 近义词: optimistically, hopefully | 反义词: pessimistically, hopelessly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
pessimistic (adjective): from Latin pessimus "worst" + -istic (suffix)
-ly (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives
