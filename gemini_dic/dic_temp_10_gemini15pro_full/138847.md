nuring: /ˈnɜːrɪŋ/ (This appears to be a misspelling.  Assuming the intended word is "nursing")| n.|护理；照料；哺乳；喂养|The process of providing care for someone who is ill or injured, especially in a hospital or other healthcare facility.  Can also refer to breastfeeding.|例句：She chose a career in nursing. 她选择了护理事业。

例句：The mother was nursing her baby. 这位母亲正在给她的宝宝喂奶。


近义词：care, tending,  breastfeeding (for the act of breastfeeding)|反义词: neglecting|词性: noun
