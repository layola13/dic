passthrough:/ˈpɑːs θruː/ | n. adj.| 直通，传递;  直通式,传递式的 |A feature that allows data to be transferred from one device or system to another without being processed or modified.  Allowing something to travel directly through something else.| The new software allows for passthrough of audio signals.  这个新软件允许音频信号直通。| 近义词:transfer, relay, conduit| 反义词:block, obstruct| 词性:名词，形容词

pass through:/pæs θruː/ | v.| 通过，穿过;  经历，体验 |To travel through a place or to experience a difficult period or situation.| The river passes through several towns before reaching the sea. 这条河在入海前流经几个城镇。 We're passing through a difficult time right now. 我们现在正在经历一段艰难的时期。| 近义词:traverse, undergo, experience| 反义词:avoid, bypass| 词性:动词
