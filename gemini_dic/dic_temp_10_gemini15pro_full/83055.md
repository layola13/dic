gurgly: /ˈɡɜːrɡli/ (UK) /ˈɡɜːrɡli/ (US)| adj.|汩汩作响的，发出汩汩声的|Making a bubbling sound like water flowing out of a narrow opening.  A sound like that made by liquid moving in the stomach or intestines.|例句：The baby's stomach made gurgly noises after feeding. 婴儿喂奶后肚子发出汩汩的响声。

例句：The drain made a gurgly sound as the water went down. 水流下去时，排水管发出汩汩的声音。|近义词：bubbling, burbling,sloshing|反义词:silent, quiet|词性:adjective
Etymology:  From "gurgle" + "-y". "Gurgle" likely imitates the sound it describes.
