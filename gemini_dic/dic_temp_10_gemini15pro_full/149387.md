pacing: /ˈpeɪsɪŋ/ (US) /ˈpeɪsɪŋ/ (UK)| n. v.| 步调；节奏；踱步| the speed at which someone or something moves, or with which something happens or develops.  |The pacing of the movie was too slow. 电影的节奏太慢了。| synonyms: tempo, speed, rate, rhythm | antonyms: standstill, halt | noun
pacing: /ˈpeɪsɪŋ/ (US) /ˈpeɪsɪŋ/ (UK)| v. | 踱步；调整节奏 | to walk back and forth across a small area many times, especially because you are worried or nervous | He was pacing back and forth, waiting for the news. 他来回踱步，等待着消息。| synonyms: stride, walk, prowl | antonyms: stand, sit | verb
