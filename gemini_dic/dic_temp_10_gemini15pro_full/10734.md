apteria:/eɪˈptɪəriə/
n.
aptery; winglessness; absence of wings
Absence or loss of wings, especially in insects.
The apteria of some insects is a secondary adaptation. 一些昆虫的无翅是一种次生适应。
近义词:winglessness
反义词:alation
词性:名词
