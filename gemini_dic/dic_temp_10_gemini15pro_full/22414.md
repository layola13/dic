book-in:/bʊk ˈɪn/
verb
登记；报到；预订
The act of registering or checking in, typically at a hotel, hospital, or other institution.  It can also mean making a reservation.
I booked in at the hotel and went straight to my room. 我在酒店登记入住后直接去了我的房间。
check in, register, sign in
check out, leave
词性: 动词

book-in:/bʊk ˈɪn/
noun
登记；报到；预订
The act of registering or checking in, typically at a hotel, hospital, or other institution.  It can also mean the reservation itself.
The book-in was quick and efficient. 登记过程快速高效。
check-in, registration, reservation
departure, checkout
词性: 名词
