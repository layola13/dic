burlapping: /bɜːrˈlæpɪŋ/ | n. |粗麻布覆盖；用粗麻布包装|The act of covering or wrapping something with burlap. |They used burlapping to protect the furniture during the move. 他们用粗麻布包裹家具以在搬运过程中进行保护。| The artist used burlapping to create a textured effect on the canvas.  这位艺术家用粗麻布在画布上创造了一种纹理效果。 |近义词：wrapping, covering, sacking |反义词: uncovering |词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From burlap + -ing.  Burlap itself comes from the Hindi word बरलाप (barlāp), meaning "coarse cloth."
