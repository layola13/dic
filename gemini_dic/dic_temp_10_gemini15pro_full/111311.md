liturgy: /ˈlɪtərʒi/ (US) /ˈlɪtədʒi/ (UK)| n.| 礼拜仪式; 礼拜形式 |A fixed set of ceremonies, words, and actions performed regularly as part of public worship.| The liturgy of the church includes hymns, prayers, and readings from the Bible. 教堂的礼拜仪式包括唱赞美诗、祈祷和朗读圣经。| 近义词: ritual, ceremony, service, observance | 反义词: informality | 词性: noun
