chosing: /ˈtʃuːzɪŋ/
verb
选择，挑选
present participle of choose. The act of selecting or making a decision between options.
He is chosing a new car. 他正在挑选一辆新车。
She is chosing which university to attend. 她正在选择上哪所大学。
近义词: selecting, picking, opting, deciding
反义词: rejecting, declining, dismissing
词性: verb (present participle)

Etymology:
From Middle English *chosen*, present participle of *chosen* (to choose), from Old English *cēosan* (to choose, seek out, test, taste, accept, approve), from Proto-Germanic *keusaną* (to choose, taste), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵews- (to taste, choose). Cognate with Scots *chyse* (“to choose”), West Frisian *kieze* (“to choose”), Dutch *kiezen* (“to choose”), German *kiesen* (“to choose”), Danish *kyse* (“to choose”), Swedish *kysa* (“to choose”), Icelandic *kjósa* (“to choose, elect, vote”).
