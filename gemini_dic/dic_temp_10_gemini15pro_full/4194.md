airflow:/ˈɛərˌfloʊ/| n.|气流|The movement of air.|例句：The airflow through the vent was blocked. 通风口的气流被堵住了。|例句：Proper airflow is essential for efficient ventilation. 适当的气流对于有效通风至关重要。|近义词：ventilation, current, draft|反义词:stasis, stagnation|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From air + flow.  "Air" comes from Old French "air" from Latin "aer" meaning air, atmosphere.  "Flow" comes from Old English "flowan" meaning to flow.
