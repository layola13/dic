excretive:/ɪkˈskriːtɪv/| adj.|排泄的；分泌的|Relating to or used for excretion;  serving to excrete.  Something that promotes excretion.|The excretive function of the kidneys is vital. 肾脏的排泄功能至关重要。|近义词：excretory, eliminative|反义词: retentive|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin excrētus, past participle of excernere (“to sift out, separate”), from ex- (“out”) + cernere (“to sift, separate”).
