deepener:/ˈdiːpənər/| n.|加深器，加深者，深化剂|Something that deepens.  A substance added to a liquid to increase its depth of color. A tool or process that makes something deeper.|The farmer used a deepener to increase the depth of the irrigation ditches. 农民用加深器加深灌溉沟渠的深度。|近义词：intensifier, enhancer|反义词：reducer, diminisher|词性:noun


Etymology: From the verb "deepen" + "-er" (suffix forming nouns denoting a person or thing that performs the action of the verb).  "Deepen" comes from the Old English "dēopian," meaning "to make deep," from "dēop" (deep).
