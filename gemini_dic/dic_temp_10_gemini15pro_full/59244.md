e-cigarette:/iːˈsɪɡərɛt/ | n. | 电子烟 | An electronic device that simulates smoking by heating a liquid to produce an aerosol, often containing nicotine, that is inhaled. |  Example:  He switched from traditional cigarettes to an e-cigarette. 他从传统香烟换成了电子烟。

Example:  The use of e-cigarettes is becoming increasingly popular among young people. 电子烟在年轻人中越来越流行。 | 近义词: vape, vaporizer | 反义词: cigarette, tobacco | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
e- (prefix) + cigarette.  "e-" is short for "electronic."  Cigarette comes from French, ultimately from Spanish cigarro, "cigar."
