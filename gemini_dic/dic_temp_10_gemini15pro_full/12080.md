artillerymen:/ɑːrˈtɪlərimən/ | n. | 炮兵，炮手 | Soldiers who operate artillery (large-caliber guns). |  The artillerymen fired the cannons at the enemy position. 炮兵向敌人的阵地开炮。| synonyms:  gunners, cannoneers | antonyms:  civilians, non-combatants | noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From artillery + man.  "Artillery" comes from Old French artillerie, meaning "equipment, preparation," ultimately from Latin artificium "skill, craft."
