atkins:/ˈætkɪnz/| n. |阿特金斯饮食法；阿特金斯|A high-protein, low-carbohydrate diet named after Robert Atkins, who popularized it.  It restricts carbohydrates like sugar and bread while allowing unlimited amounts of protein and fat.|She's trying the Atkins diet to lose weight. 她正在尝试阿特金斯饮食法来减肥。|Synonyms:  low-carb diet, ketogenic diet|Antonyms: high-carb diet|noun
