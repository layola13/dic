actuals:/ˈæktʃuəlz/| n.|实际数字；实际情况|The actuals for last month were better than we predicted. 上个月的实际数字比我们预测的要好。|例句：The actuals rarely match the forecast.  实际情况很少与预测相符。|近义词：realities, facts, figures|反义词：estimates, projections, forecasts|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *actualis, from Latin actus (“act, action”) + -alis (suffix forming adjectives).
