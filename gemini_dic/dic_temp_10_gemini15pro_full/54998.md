disperse:/dɪˈspɜːrs/ (US), /dɪˈspəːs/ (UK)| v. |驱散，分散，散布|To scatter or spread out in different directions.|The crowd dispersed after the concert. 音乐会结束后，人群散开了。|The seeds are dispersed by the wind. 种子被风吹散了。|近义词：scatter, distribute, disseminate|反义词：gather, collect, congregate|词性:动词


disperse:/dɪˈspɜːrs/ (US), /dɪˈspəːs/ (UK)| adj. |分散的|Spread out or scattered.|The population is quite disperse in this rural area. 这个农村地区的人口相当分散。|This is a disperse dataset. 这是一个分散的数据集。|近义词：scattered, distributed, diffused|反义词：concentrated, clustered, gathered|词性:形容词 (较少使用)


词源: From Latin *dispersus*, past participle of *dispergere* ("to scatter"), from *dis-* ("apart, asunder") + *spargere* ("to scatter, strew").
