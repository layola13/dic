overwork:/ˌoʊvərˈwɜːrk/ /ˌoʊvərˈwɝːk/| n.|过度工作|Excessive work; the state of being overworked.|He suffered a breakdown from overwork. 他因过度工作而精神崩溃。|近义词：exhaustion, strain|反义词:rest, relaxation|词性:名词


词源:  From *over- + work.  "Over-" is an Old English prefix meaning "above," "across," "beyond," or "excessive."  "Work" comes from Old English *weorc*, meaning "work," "labor," "deed," or "military work."
