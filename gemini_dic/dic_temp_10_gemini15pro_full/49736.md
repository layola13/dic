demogorgon:/ˈdɛməˌɡɔːrɡən/| n. |恶魔，魔鬼|A powerful, terrifying, and destructive demon or infernal being.  Originally a deity from Greek mythology, later popularized by D&D and Stranger Things.|例句：The demogorgon emerged from the Upside Down, wreaking havoc.  恶魔从颠倒世界出现，造成了严重破坏。

例句：In the game, the demogorgon is a fearsome opponent. 在游戏中，恶魔是一个可怕的对手。|近义词：monster, devil, demon, fiend|反义词:angel, deity (benevolent)|Noun
