dissemble:/dɪˈsɛmbəl/ | v. | 掩饰，假装；隐藏（真实感情或意图） | To conceal one's true motives, feelings, or beliefs.  | He dissembled his fear with a smile. 他用微笑掩饰他的恐惧。 |  She dissembled her anger by speaking calmly. 她强作镇定掩饰她的愤怒。 | 近义词：feign, pretend, mask, disguise | 反义词：reveal, disclose, expose | 词性：动词

dissemble:/dɪˈsɛmbəl/ | n. | 掩饰；假装 | The act of concealing one's true motives, feelings, or beliefs. |  His dissemble was so convincing that no one suspected his true intentions. 他的掩饰如此令人信服，以至于没有人怀疑他的真实意图。 | Her dissemble about her feelings made the situation worse. 她对感情的掩饰使情况变得更糟。 | 近义词：pretense, deception, camouflage | 反义词：honesty, openness, candor | 词性：名词
