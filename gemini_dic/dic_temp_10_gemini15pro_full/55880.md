divisionally:/dɪˈvɪʒənəli/| adv.|按部门地，部门性地|In a way that relates to or is organized by divisions.  指与部门有关或按部门组织的方式。|The company is structured divisionally, with each division responsible for its own profits and losses.  这家公司按部门划分，每个部门负责自己的盈亏。|Synonym: departmentally|Antonym: centrally|词性:副词

divisionally:/dɪˈvɪʒənəli/| adv.|分割地，分开地|In a way that is divided or separated. 指分开或分割的方式。|The land was divisionally fenced to keep the livestock separated. 这块地用栅栏分隔开，以将牲畜分开。|Synonym: separately|Antonym: jointly|词性:副词

词源：division + -ally
