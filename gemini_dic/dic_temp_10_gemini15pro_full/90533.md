huffiest:/ˈhʌfiɪst/ | adj. | 最生气的，最恼火的 |  superlative adjective of huffy: easily offended; touchy.  It describes someone who is extremely annoyed and quick to take offense. |  He stormed off in a huffiest mood after the argument.  他争吵后怒气冲冲地离开了。| synonyms: angriest, most indignant, most irritable, crossest | antonyms: calmest, most pleased, most content | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From *huff*, meaning a fit of anger or displeasure.  The "-y" suffix transforms the noun into an adjective, and "-est" indicates the superlative form.
