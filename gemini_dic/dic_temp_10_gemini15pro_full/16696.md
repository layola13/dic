barricader:/ˌbærɪˈkeɪdər/| n.|设路障者，用路障围堵的人|A person who builds or sets up a barricade.|The barricaders held off the police for several hours.  路障设置者阻挡了警察几个小时。|Synonyms: defender, protector, resistor|Antonyms: attacker, assailant|noun

barricade:/ˈbærɪkeɪd/| n. vt.|路障，障碍物；设置路障，阻挡|A defensive barrier hastily constructed, as in a street, to stop an enemy's advance; to block or defend with a barricade.|The protesters barricaded the street with overturned cars. 抗议者用翻倒的汽车设置路障封锁了街道。|Synonyms: barrier, obstacle, blockade|Antonyms: opening, passage|noun, verb
