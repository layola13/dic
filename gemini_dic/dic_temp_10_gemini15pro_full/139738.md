ochriest:/ˈoʊkəriɪst/| adj.|最赭色的，最土黄色的|Of a dark yellow color; the superlative form of 'ochre' or 'ochry'.  It refers to the color resembling yellow ochre, a natural clay earth pigment.|The ochriest rocks were at the top of the canyon.  峡谷顶部是颜色最赭色的岩石。|The sunset painted the sky in the ochriest hues imaginable.  日落将天空染成了可以想象到的最赭色的色调。|近义词：yellowish, brownish, golden, tawny|反义词：bluish, greenish|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From ochre + -iest.  "Ochre" comes from the Old French "ocre," and ultimately from the Greek "ōkhra," meaning "yellow ochre."  "-iest" is a superlative suffix.
