anapaest:/ˈænəpiːst/ | n. |抑抑扬格，短短长格|A metrical foot consisting of two short or unstressed syllables followed by one long or stressed syllable. |Example 1:  The word "understand" is an anapaest.  “understand”这个单词是抑抑扬格。

Example 2:  Anapaestic meter gives a poem a galloping rhythm. 抑抑扬格的韵律赋予诗歌一种飞奔的节奏。|Synonym:  tribrach (less common) |Antonym:  dactyl |noun
