hypohyal:/ˈhaɪpəˌhaɪəl/ /ˈhaɪpoʊˌhaɪəl/
noun
舌骨下的
situated or extending below the hyoid bone; pertaining to the hypohyal bones or cartilages of the hyoid arch.
The hypohyal is a small, bony element located below the hyoid bone in some vertebrates.  舌骨下是一些脊椎动物中位于舌骨下方的一个小骨骼元素。
The hypohyal plays a role in supporting the tongue and other structures in the throat.  舌骨下在支撑舌头和喉咙中的其他结构方面发挥作用。
synonym: subhyal
antonym: epihyal
