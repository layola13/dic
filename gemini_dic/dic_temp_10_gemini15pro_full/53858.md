discontinuable:/ˌdɪskənˈtɪnjuəbl/
adjective
可停止的，可中断的
able to be discontinued; that can be stopped or ended.
The service proved unpopular and was therefore discontinuable.  这项服务不受欢迎，因此可以停止。
This limited-time offer is discontinuable at any time.  这个限时优惠可以随时停止。
近义词: terminable, ceaseable, stoppable
反义词: continuous, permanent, ongoing
词性: adjective

词源:
From discontinue + -able.  Discontinue comes from the Old French discontinuer, from dis- "apart, not" + continuer "to continue," from Latin continuare "to join together, connect, make continuous."
