existentialistic:/ˌɛɡzɪˈstɛnʃəlɪstɪk/ /ˌɛksɪˈstɛnʃəlɪstɪk/
adjective
存在主义的
Relating to existentialism, a philosophical theory that emphasizes the existence of the individual person as a free and responsible agent determining their own development through acts of will.
He wrote an existentialistic novel about a man struggling to find meaning in life. 他写了一部存在主义小说，讲述一个男人努力寻找人生意义的故事。
synonyms:existential
antonyms:conformist, conventional
词性:adjective


existentialism:/ˌɛɡzɪˈstɛnʃəlɪzəm/ /ˌɛksɪˈstɛnʃəlɪzəm/
noun
存在主义
A philosophical theory or approach that emphasizes the existence of the individual person as a free and responsible agent determining their own development through acts of the will.
Existentialism emphasizes individual freedom and responsibility. 存在主义强调个人的自由和责任。
synonyms:philosophy of existence
antonyms:essentialism,determinism
词性:noun
