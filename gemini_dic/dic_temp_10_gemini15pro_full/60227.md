efforting: /ˈɛfərtɪŋ/
| n. | 努力，费力 | The act of making an effort. | The efforting to reach the top of the mountain was exhausting. 努力到达山顶令人筋疲力尽。 | 近义词: striving, struggle, exertion | 反义词: ease, relaxation | 词性: 名词


词源:  From the verb "effort," which comes from the Old French "esforcier," meaning "to force, constrain, exert oneself."  Ultimately derived from the Latin "ex-" (out) and "fortis" (strong).
