glutean:/ˈɡluːtiən/ | adj. | 谷蛋白的；含谷蛋白的 | Relating to or containing gluten, a protein composite found in wheat and other grains. | Example: This bread is glutean.  这面包含有谷蛋白。 | Example:  She avoids glutean products due to her celiac disease. 由于患有乳糜泻，她避免食用含谷蛋白的产品。 | 近义词: gluten-containing | 反义词: gluten-free | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From gluten + -ean.  "Gluten" comes from Latin "gluten" meaning "glue."  The suffix "-ean" forms adjectives meaning "relating to" or "having the characteristics of."
