elasticated:/ɪˈlæstɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
有松紧带的；弹性的
Made of or containing elastic material.
例句：She prefers elasticated waistbands for comfort. 她为了舒适喜欢穿有松紧带的裤子。

例句：The socks are elasticated for a snug fit.  这些袜子有弹性，穿着贴身。

近义词：stretchy, flexible, resilient
反义词：rigid, stiff, inflexible
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From elastic + -ated.  "Elastic" comes from the Late Latin "elasticus,"  meaning "capable of driving back," from the Greek "elastos," meaning "ductile, pliable."  The suffix "-ated" forms adjectives meaning "having, provided with, treated with," etc.
