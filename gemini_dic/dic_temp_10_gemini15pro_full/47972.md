deciles: /ˈdɛsaɪlz/ | n. |十分位数| Values that divide a data set into ten equal parts.  Each part represents 1/10th of the data set.| The income deciles show the disparity between the richest and poorest segments of the population. 收入十分位数显示了最富有和最贫穷人群之间的差距。| synonyms: quantiles, percentiles | antonyms: N/A | noun

decile: /ˈdɛsaɪl/ | n. |十分位数| Any of the nine values that divide a data set into ten equal parts.  Each part represents 1/10th of the data set.| The fifth decile represents the median of the data. 第五十分位数代表数据的中间值。| synonyms: quantile, percentile | antonyms: N/A | noun
