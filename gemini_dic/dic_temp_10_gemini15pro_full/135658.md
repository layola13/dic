nonpreferablenesses:/ˌnɑːnˈprɛfərəbəlˌnɛsɪz/ | n. | 非偏好性；不优选性 | The state or quality of not being preferable; the collection of things or options that are not preferred.  This word is rarely used.  It is much more common to express this concept using phrases like "less desirable options" or "undesirable characteristics". |  Due to the nonpreferablenesses of the proposed solutions, the committee opted for a different approach. 由于提出的解决方案不尽如人意，委员会选择了不同的方法。| Near synonyms: disadvantages, drawbacks, shortcomings | Antonyms: advantages, merits, strengths | noun
