lowboy:/ˈloʊbɔɪ/| n. |矮柜，矮床，低底盘拖车|A lowboy is a piece of furniture, typically a chest of drawers or a dressing table, that is lower than usual. It can also refer to a low-slung semi-trailer used for hauling heavy equipment.|The antique lowboy held a collection of porcelain figurines. 古董矮柜上摆放着一套瓷器小雕像。|The heavy machinery was transported on a lowboy trailer. 重型机械是用低底盘拖车运输的。|synonyms: dresser, chest of drawers (for furniture); low-loader, low-bed trailer (for trailer)|antonyms: highboy (for furniture)|noun
