croupy: /ˈkruːpi/ | adj. | 患义膜性喉炎的；声音嘶哑的 | Resembling or characteristic of croup, especially in having a harsh, barking cough. |  The child's croupy cough kept everyone awake.  这孩子患义膜性喉炎的咳嗽声让每个人都睡不着。 | synonyms: hoarse, barking, rasping, croaking | antonyms: clear, smooth, melodious | adjective


croupy: /ˈkruːpi/ | adj. | 声音嘶哑的；沙哑的 | Having a hoarse voice, as if affected with croup. | The singer's voice was croupy after the concert.  演唱会后，歌手的声音变得沙哑了。 | synonyms: husky, raspy, gravelly | antonyms: clear, smooth, melodious | adjective
