exauguration:/ɛksɔːɡjʊˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 就职典礼;登基典礼;正式落成典礼 |  A ceremony marking the beginning of a term of office, especially of a president, or the official opening of a building or institution. | The exauguration of the new library was attended by dignitaries from all over the country.  全国各地的显要人物都出席了新图书馆的落成典礼。| inauguration, commencement, dedication | abdication, resignation | noun
