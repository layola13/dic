overcanny:/ˌoʊvərˈkæni/
adjective
过于精明的；过于机敏的；诡异的
Having or showing shrewdness and cleverness to an unnatural or uncanny degree.  Too clever or shrewd; strangely insightful or perceptive, often suggesting something supernatural or unsettling.
He had an overcanny ability to predict the stock market. 他有一种不可思议的预测股市的能力。
Her overcanny sense of direction led them out of the forest. 她异乎寻常的方向感让他们走出了森林。
近义词: shrewd, astute, perceptive, uncanny, eerie
反义词: naive, gullible, obtuse
