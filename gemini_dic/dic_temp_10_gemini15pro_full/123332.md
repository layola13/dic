monogynia:/ˌmɒnəˈdʒɪniə/ /ˌmɑːnəˈdʒɪniə/| n. |单雌蕊；一雌蕊纲|A plant having only one pistil in each flower.  A Linnaean class of plants having only one pistil or style in each flower.|The *Magnolia virginiana* belongs to the class Monogynia.|近义词: (No direct synonym, as it's a taxonomic term) |反义词: polygynia|词性: noun
