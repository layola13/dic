anacanthous:/ˌænəˈkænθəs/ | adj. |无棘的，无刺的| Lacking spines or thorns; smooth.  Specifically referring to a group of fish (Anacanthini) that lack spiny fin rays. This taxonomic group is now considered obsolete. | Example: The skin of the anacanthous fish was smooth and slippery.  这条无棘鱼的皮肤光滑而黏腻。| Example:  Older texts may refer to anacanthous fish, but modern classifications no longer use this term.  较旧的文本可能会提到无棘鱼，但现代分类法不再使用这个术语。|Synonym: spineless, smooth | Antonym: spiny, thorny | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *a-* (not) + *akantha* (thorn, spine)
