farmlands:/ˈfɑːrmlændz/
n.
农田，耕地
Land used for farming.
The fertile farmlands of the valley produced abundant crops. 山谷里肥沃的农田出产了大量的农作物。
farmland, arable land, cultivated land
wasteland, barren land
noun
