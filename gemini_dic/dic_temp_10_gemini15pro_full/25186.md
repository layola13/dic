buddha:/ˈbʊdə/| n.|佛陀，佛|A person who has obtained Buddhahood.|例句：Siddhartha Gautama became the Buddha. 悉达多·乔达摩成为了佛陀。

例句：Many people visit temples to pray to the Buddha. 许多人去寺庙拜佛。|近义词：Enlightened One, Tathagata|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From the Sanskrit *buddha*, meaning "awakened" or "enlightened."  The term refers to Siddhartha Gautama, the founder of Buddhism, and also to anyone who has achieved a state of perfect enlightenment.
