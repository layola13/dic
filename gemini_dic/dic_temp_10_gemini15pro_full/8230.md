anti-heroine:/ˌæntiˈhɛrəʊɪn/ /ˌæntiˈhɛroʊɪn/
n.
反英雄;女反派
A female character in a story, film, etc. who lacks traditional heroic qualities such as idealism, courage, or morality.  She may be flawed, unconventional, or even villainous, but still sympathetic or compelling in some way.
Lady Macbeth is a classic anti-heroine, driven by ambition and willing to commit terrible acts.
麦克白夫人是一个经典的反英雄人物，她被野心驱使，愿意犯下可怕的罪行。

Fleabag, from the eponymous TV series, is a modern anti-heroine – flawed, selfish, and often unlikeable, yet still relatable and ultimately sympathetic.
电视剧《伦敦生活》中的弗里贝格是一个现代反英雄——有缺陷、自私，而且经常不讨人喜欢，但仍然让人产生共鸣，并最终获得同情。

近义词:villain, antagonist, rogue
反义词:heroine, protagonist
词性:noun
