hunting-grounds:/ˈhʌntɪŋ ɡraʊndz/
|n.|狩猎场，猎场|A place where animals are hunted.|The African savanna is a hunting-ground for lions. 非洲大草原是狮子的狩猎场。|hunting area, game preserve|urban area, city|noun


hunting-ground:/ˈhʌntɪŋ ɡraʊnd/
|n.|（比喻）有利可图的场所；活动场所|A place or situation where someone can find and exploit opportunities or victims.|The internet has become a hunting-ground for cybercriminals. 互联网已成为网络犯罪分子的活动场所。|fertile ground, happy hunting ground|restricted area, safe haven|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From hunting + ground.  "Hunting" comes from the Old English "huntung,"  a verbal noun from "huntian" meaning "to hunt." "Ground" comes from the Old English "grund" meaning "bottom, foundation, earth."
