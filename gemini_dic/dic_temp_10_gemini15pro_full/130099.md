non-proprietary:/ˌnɑːn prəˈpraɪəˌteri/
adjective
非专有的，公开的
Not protected by trademark or patent and therefore available for general use.
The software is non-proprietary and can be modified by anyone.  这个软件是非专有的，任何人都可以修改。
The committee recommended the use of non-proprietary standards. 委员会建议使用非专有标准。
Synonyms: generic, open, public, standard, open-source
Antonyms: proprietary, private, closed, copyrighted, patented
词性: 形容词
