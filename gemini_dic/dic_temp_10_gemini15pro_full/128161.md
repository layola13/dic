nettles:/ˈnetlz/
n.
荨麻（植物）；
A herbaceous plant which has jagged leaves covered with stinging hairs.
例句：Be careful of the nettles in the garden. 小心花园里的荨麻。

例句：She brushed against some nettles and her skin started to itch. 她碰到了一些荨麻，皮肤开始发痒。

近义词：stinging plants
反义词：(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用non-stinging plants表达非刺痛性植物)
词性：名词
