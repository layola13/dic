aloetic:/ˌæləˈwɪtɪk/
adjective
芦荟的；含芦荟的
Relating to or containing aloe.
Aloetic preparations are often used to soothe burns. 芦荟制剂常用于舒缓烧伤。
近义词:aloe
反义词:None
词性:adjective
