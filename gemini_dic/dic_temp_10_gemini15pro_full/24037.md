breeze-up:/ˈbriːz ʌp/ | n. | 预备赛，试训；(尤指两岁马的)公开试售 | A pre-training period and public sale, especially for two-year-old horses. |  The breeze-up sale attracted buyers from around the world.  试售会吸引了来自世界各地的买家。 | Synonyms: trial, workout, preparation | Antonyms: race, competition | noun

breeze-up:/ˈbriːz ʌp/ | vi. | (马)参加预备赛 | To participate in a breeze-up. | The colt breezed up well and fetched a high price. 这匹小马在试训中表现出色，拍出了高价。 | Synonyms: train, prepare, exercise | Antonyms: rest, retire | verb
