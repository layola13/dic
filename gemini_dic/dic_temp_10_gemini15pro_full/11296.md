ardency:/ˈɑːrdənsi/| n.|热情，热心，炽热|Great intensity of feeling; fervor; zeal.  A passionate feeling.|She spoke with an ardency that surprised everyone. 她说话的热情令所有人惊讶。|近义词：passion, fervor, zeal, enthusiasm, eagerness|反义词：apathy, indifference, coldness|词性: noun
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French ardance, from ardent ‘burning’.
