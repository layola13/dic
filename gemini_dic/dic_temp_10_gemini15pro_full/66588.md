exhortingly:/ɪɡˈzɔːrtɪŋli/ | adv. | 劝告地，勉励地 | In a manner that urges or encourages someone to do something. |  The coach spoke exhortingly to his team, urging them to give their all in the final quarter. 教练激励地对他的团队讲话，敦促他们在最后一节全力以赴。

The teacher exhortingly reminded her students to study for the upcoming exam. 老师劝勉学生们为即将到来的考试学习。| 近义词: encouragingly, urgingly, persuasively | 反义词: discouragingly, dissuasively | 词性: 副词
