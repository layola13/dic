countervail:/ˌkaʊntərˈveɪl/| vt.|抵消，对抗，补偿|To offset the effect of something by countering it with something equally forceful.|The government will provide subsidies to countervail the rising cost of fuel. 政府将提供补贴以抵消不断上涨的燃料成本。|The company offered discounts to countervail the negative publicity. 公司提供折扣以抵消负面宣传。|synonyms: offset, neutralize, compensate, counterbalance, counteract|antonyms: enhance, augment, amplify, strengthen, intensify|verb
