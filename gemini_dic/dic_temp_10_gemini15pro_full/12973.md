asthmatic: /æzˈmætɪk/ | adj. n. | 哮喘的；患哮喘的；哮喘患者 |  Relating to or suffering from asthma.  |  She has an asthmatic son. 她有一个患哮喘的儿子。 |  My asthma makes it difficult to breathe sometimes. 我的哮喘有时让我呼吸困难。| 近义词: wheezy, breathless | 反义词: healthy | 词性: 形容词/名词

asthmatic: /æzˈmætɪk/ | n. | 哮喘患者 | A person who suffers from asthma. | The asthmatic had to use his inhaler.  这个哮喘病人不得不使用他的吸入器。 |  Many asthmatics find that their symptoms worsen in cold weather. 许多哮喘病患者发现他们的症状在寒冷的天气里会加重。 | 近义词: sufferer | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

词源: From Late Latin *asthmaticus, from Greek *asthmatikós, from asthma. Ultimately from the Indo-European root *h₂enh₁- (“to breathe”).
