octane:/ˈɒkteɪn/ (UK) /ˈɑːkteɪn/ (US)| n. |辛烷|A saturated hydrocarbon of the alkane series, C8H18, found in petroleum and used in antiknock gasoline additives.|High-octane gasoline is recommended for this car.  这辆车建议使用高辛烷值汽油。|Synonyms: alkane, hydrocarbon|Antonyms: None|Noun


octanes:/ˈɒkteɪnz/ (UK) /ˈɑːkteɪnz/ (US)| n. |辛烷|Plural form of octane, referring to more than one type or instance of octane.|The different octanes in the fuel blend affect its performance. 燃料混合物中不同的辛烷值会影响其性能。|Synonyms: alkanes, hydrocarbons|Antonyms: None|Noun
