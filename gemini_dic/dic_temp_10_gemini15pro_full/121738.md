misproportion:/ˌmɪsprəˈpɔːrʃən/ |n.| 不均衡，不成比例 |lack of proportion or equality between two things| The design flaw caused a misproportion between the base and the roof.  设计上的缺陷导致了底座和屋顶之间的比例失调。|The company spent a misproportion of its budget on advertising. 该公司在其预算中花费了过多的广告费用。| 近义词: imbalance, disparity, disproportionality | 反义词: balance, proportion, symmetry |词性: 名词

misproportion:/ˌmɪsprəˈpɔːrʃən/ |v.| 使不成比例，使不均衡 |to make something the wrong size in relation to something else|  The artist misproportioned the figures in the painting.  艺术家把画中的人物画得不成比例。|  Don't misproportion your time by spending too much on one task. 不要把时间分配得不均衡，在某一项任务上花费太多时间。| 近义词: distort, skew, imbalance | 反义词: balance, proportion, equalize |词性: 动词
