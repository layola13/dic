hypodermic:/ˌhaɪpəˈdɜːrmɪk/| adj. n.|皮下的；皮下注射的；皮下注射器| relating to the region immediately beneath the skin; (of a needle or syringe) used to inject a drug or other substance beneath the skin|The doctor gave the patient a hypodermic injection. 医生给病人进行了皮下注射。|Synonyms: subcutaneous, injected, intramuscular|Antonyms: oral, topical|adjective, noun


hypodermic:/ˌhaɪpəˈdɜːrmɪk/| n.|皮下注射器|a hypodermic needle or syringe|She filled the hypodermic with morphine. 她用吗啡注满了注射器。|Synonyms: syringe, needle|Antonyms: N/A|noun
