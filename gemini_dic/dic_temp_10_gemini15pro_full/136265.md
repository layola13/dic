nonrepentant:/ˌnɒnrɪˈpɛntənt/ /ˌnɑːnrɪˈpɛntənt/| adj.|不悔悟的，不后悔的，顽固不化的|Not feeling or showing any regret for bad behavior.|He remained nonrepentant about his crimes. 他对自己的罪行毫无悔意。|近义词：unrepentant, impenitent, remorseless, unapologetic|反义词：repentant, contrite, remorseful, apologetic|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From non- + repentant.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Repentant" comes from the Old French "repentant," from the verb "repentir," meaning "to repent."  Ultimately, the word traces back to the Latin "poenitere," meaning "to cause to repent, to punish."
