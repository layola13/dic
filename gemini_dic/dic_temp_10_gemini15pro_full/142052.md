organotrophic:/ɔːrˌɡænəˈtrɒfɪk/ | adj. | 有机营养的 | Obtaining energy from the oxidation of organic compounds. | Example 1: Many bacteria are organotrophic, meaning they break down organic matter for energy. 许多细菌是有机营养的，这意味着它们分解有机物来获取能量。

Example 2: Organotrophic organisms are essential for nutrient cycling in ecosystems.  有机营养生物对生态系统中的营养循环至关重要。 | Synonyms: chemoorganotrophic, heterotrophic | Antonyms: autotrophic, lithotrophic | Adjective
