nooses:/ˈnuːsɪz/| n.| 绞索；陷阱；束缚|A loop with a running knot, tightening as the rope or wire is pulled, typically used for hanging.  |The cowboy used a noose to catch the runaway calf. 牛仔用绳套套住逃跑的小牛。 |Synonyms: snare, trap, lasso, loop|Antonyms: release, freedom|noun
