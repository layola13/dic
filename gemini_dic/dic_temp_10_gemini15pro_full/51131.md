词性:  Incorrect usage. "Despites" is not a valid English word.

英文解析:  "Despite" is a preposition meaning "in spite of" or "regardless of."  It is never used with an "s" at the end.  The correct form is always "despite."
例句: (Using the correct word "despite")

Despite the rain, we went for a walk. 尽管下雨了，我们还是去散步了。

Despite his best efforts, he failed the exam. 尽管他尽了最大的努力，他还是考试不及格。

近义词: in spite of, notwithstanding, regardless of
反义词: because of, due to, owing to
