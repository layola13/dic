aesthetican: /ɛsˈθɛtɪkən/ | n. | 美学家，审美家 | A person who cultivates or affects an appreciation of the beautiful, especially in art. |  例句：The aesthetican praised the exquisite brushwork of the painting. 这位美学家称赞了这幅画精美的笔法。

例句：He considered himself an aesthetican, surrounding himself with objects of beauty. 他认为自己是一位美学家，周围都是美丽的物品。

近义词：connoisseur, art lover, aesthete | 反义词: philistine | 词性: noun
