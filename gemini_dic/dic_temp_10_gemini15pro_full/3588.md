agatized:/ˈæɡəˌtaɪzd/| adj. |玛瑙化的|Resembling agate; having been converted into agate.|The agatized wood was beautiful and highly polished.  这块玛瑙化的木头很漂亮，而且被高度抛光。|Synonyms: petrified, fossilized|Antonyms:  dissolved, molten|adjective
