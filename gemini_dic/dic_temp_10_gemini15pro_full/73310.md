foreclosable:/fɔːrˈkloʊzəbl/
adjective
可取消抵押赎回权的
Subject to foreclosure; capable of being foreclosed.  A mortgage or loan is foreclosable if the borrower fails to meet the terms of the loan agreement.
The bank declared the mortgage foreclosable after he missed three payments.  他连续三个月未缴纳房贷后，银行宣布可以取消他的抵押品赎回权。
The loan is foreclosable if the borrower defaults. 如果借款人违约，贷款可以被取消赎回权。

近义词:redeemable (在特定条件下可赎回的), recoverable
反义词:non-foreclosable, non-redeemable

词性:adjective
