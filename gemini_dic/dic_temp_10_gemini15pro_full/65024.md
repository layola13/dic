esurient:/ɪˈsʊəriənt/ /ɛˈsjʊəriənt/| adj.|贪婪的；渴望的；|Characterized by a greedy desire, especially for food.|例句：The esurient wolf devoured the carcass. 贪婪的狼吞虎咽地吃掉了尸体。|例句：He had an esurient appetite for knowledge.  他对知识有着贪婪的渴望。|近义词：ravenous, voracious, greedy, avid|反义词:satisfied, sated, content|词性:形容词
词源:From Latin esurient- (stem of esuriens), present participle of esurire ‘be hungry’, from edere ‘to eat’.
