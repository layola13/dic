disliken:/dɪsˈlaɪkən/
verb
不喜欢；厌恶
to regard with dislike; to not like
I disliken the taste of licorice. 我不喜欢甘草的味道。
She dislikens being told what to do. 她不喜欢被指使。
近义词: hate, detest, abhor, loathe
反义词: like, love, adore, cherish
词性: verb
