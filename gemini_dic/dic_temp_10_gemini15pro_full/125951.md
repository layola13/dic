musicalnesses:/ˈmjuːzɪkəlnəsəz/
n.
音乐性；音乐才能
The musical quality or qualities of something.
Her musicalnesses are evident in her skillful playing of the piano. 她的音乐才能在她熟练的钢琴演奏中显而易见。
musicality, artistry
lack of musicality, tone-deafness
