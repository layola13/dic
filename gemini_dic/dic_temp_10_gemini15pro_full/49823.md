demonstrating:/ˈdɛmənˌstreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 展示，演示；证明；示威；表达 |  showing something clearly by giving proof or evidence; taking part in a public meeting or march, usually as a protest |  The teacher demonstrated how to solve the equation. 老师演示了如何解这个方程。 | synonyms: exhibiting, displaying, showing, proving, illustrating | antonyms: concealing, hiding, obscuring | verb

demonstrating:/ˈdɛmənˌstreɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |  证明性的；示范性的 | serving as a demonstration or example | This is a demonstrating example of how to write a good essay. 这是一个示范性的例子，说明如何写一篇好文章。 | synonyms: illustrative, exemplary, representative | antonyms: atypical, unusual, exceptional | adjective
