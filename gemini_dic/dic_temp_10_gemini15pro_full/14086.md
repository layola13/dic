austronesian:/ˌɔːstrəˈniːʒən/ | adj. n.|南岛语系的；南岛语系民族的|Relating to or denoting a family of languages spoken in the area extending from Madagascar eastward through the Malay Peninsula and the Pacific islands.|The Austronesian languages are spoken across a vast area.|近义词:Malayo-Polynesian|反义词:|adjective, noun

austronesian:/ˌɔːstrəˈniːʒən/ | n.|南岛语系民族的人|A member of any of the peoples speaking an Austronesian language.|The Austronesians were skilled navigators.|近义词:Malayo-Polynesian|反义词:|noun
