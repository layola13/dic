clodpated:/ˈklɒdˌpeɪtɪd/| adj.|愚蠢的，笨的|Having or showing a lack of common sense; stupid.|He made a clodpated decision to invest all his money in a single stock. 他做了一个愚蠢的决定，把所有的钱都投资在一支股票上。|近义词：stupid, foolish, dense, dim-witted, doltish, simpleminded|反义词：intelligent, smart, bright, clever, astute|词性:形容词
Etymology:  From 'clod' (lump of earth) + 'pated' (having a head of a specified kind), implying a head filled with dirt or having a head like a clod of earth.
