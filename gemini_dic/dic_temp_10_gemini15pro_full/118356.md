meritable:/ˈmɛrɪtəbl/
adjective
值得称赞的；有优点的
Deserving praise, reward, or honor.
Her meritorious service to the community was recognized with an award. 她对社区的功绩得到了奖励。
synonyms: praiseworthy, laudable, commendable, deserving
antonyms: blameworthy, reprehensible, censurable, undeserving
词性: 形容词
