coauthorships:/ˌkoʊˈɔːθərʃɪps/
n.
共同作者身份；合著关系
The article discusses the benefits and challenges of coauthorships in academic research. 这篇文章讨论了学术研究中共同作者身份的益处和挑战。
This book is the product of many years of research and multiple coauthorships. 这本书是多年研究和多次合作的成果。
synonyms: joint authorships, collaborations, partnerships
antonyms: sole authorship, independent work
