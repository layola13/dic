dreys:/dreɪz/
noun
松鼠窝；巢
A drey is the nest of a squirrel, typically constructed of twigs and leaves in the branches of a tree.
The squirrel scurried back to its drey for shelter from the storm. 松鼠 scurried 回到它的巢穴躲避风暴。
nest, lair
