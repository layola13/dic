gatecrashed:/ˈɡeɪtkræʃt/
verb
闯入；不请自来
To enter (a party or other gathering) without an invitation or ticket.
He gatecrashed the wedding reception. 他不请自来地参加了婚礼招待会。
gatecrashed:/ˈɡeɪtkræʃt/
verb
擅自进入；未经允许进入
To enter a place or event without permission.
They gatecrashed the concert. 他们未经允许就进入了音乐会现场。
gatecrashed:/ˈɡeɪtkræʃt/
verb
未经允许使用；擅自使用
To use something without permission.
He gatecrashed their Wi-Fi network. 他未经允许使用了他们的Wi-Fi网络。
