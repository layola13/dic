baffled:/ˈbæfld/ | adj. | 困惑的，迷惑不解的 | Completely confused or perplexed. | I was baffled by the complex instructions. 我被复杂的说明书搞糊涂了。

The detectives were baffled by the lack of evidence.  侦探们对缺乏证据感到困惑。

| 近义词：confused, perplexed, bewildered, puzzled | 反义词：clear, certain, understanding | 词性:形容词
