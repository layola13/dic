entombs:/ɪnˈtuːmz/
verb
埋葬，掩埋
to place in or as if in a tomb; bury
例句：The avalanche entombed the skiers. 雪崩掩埋了滑雪者。

例句：The ancient city was entombed by volcanic ash.  这座古城被火山灰掩埋了。

近义词：bury, inter, sepulcher, enshroud, immure
反义词：exhume, unearth, uncover
词性：v.
