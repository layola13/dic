barefooted: /ˈbɛərfʊtɪd/ /ˈberfʊtɪd/
adjective
赤脚的
Without shoes.
She walked barefooted on the beach. 她赤脚走在沙滩上。

barefooted: /ˈbɛərfʊtɪd/ /ˈberfʊtɪd/
adverb
赤着脚地
Walking or running without shoes.
The children ran barefooted through the grass. 孩子们赤着脚跑过草地。


近义词: shoeless, unshod
反义词: shod

词性: adjective, adverb

词源: From bare + foot + -ed.
