dactylics:/dækˈtɪlɪks/| n.|强弱格, 长短短格|A metrical foot consisting of one long syllable followed by two short syllables or of one stressed syllable followed by two unstressed syllables.|The poem was written in dactylics, giving it a lilting rhythm. 这首诗是用强弱格写的，赋予它一种轻快的节奏。|dactyls| |noun|

dactylic:/dækˈtɪlɪk/| adj.|强弱格的, 长短短格的|Relating to or consisting of dactyls.|The dactylic rhythm of the poem created a sense of urgency. 这首诗的强弱格节奏营造了一种紧迫感。|rhythmic, metrical| |adjective|
