outstinks:/ˌaʊtˈstɪŋks/
verb
比...更臭，胜过...的臭味
To smell worse than something or someone else.
The durian outstinks the garbage.  榴莲比垃圾更臭。
My dog's breath outstinks a skunk. 我的狗的呼吸比臭鼬还臭。
近义词:out-smells, overpowers
反义词:smells better than, is less pungent than
词性:v.
