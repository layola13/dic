convivial:/kənˈvɪviəl/ | adj. | 欢乐的；友好的；好交际的 |  (of an atmosphere or event) friendly, lively, and enjoyable. |  The restaurant has a convivial atmosphere. 这家餐厅的氛围很欢乐。|  We spent a convivial evening with our new neighbors. 我们和新邻居度过了一个愉快的夜晚。|  sociable, genial, affable, amiable, gregarious  | unsociable, unfriendly, aloof, cold | adjective

Etymology->
From Latin *convivialis*, from convivium (“a living together, a feast, banquet”), from con- (“together”) + vīvō (“I live”).
