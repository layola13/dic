anti-Zionism: /ˌæntiˈzaɪənɪzəm/ | n. | 反犹太复国主义 | Opposition to Zionism; the political movement for the establishment and development of a Jewish nation-state in the historical Land of Israel (Palestine).  It can range from criticism of Israeli government policies to the denial of the right of Israel to exist. |  Anti-Zionism has become a controversial topic on college campuses. 反犹太复国主义已成为大学校园里的一个有争议的话题。

Anti-Zionism is sometimes confused with antisemitism. 反犹太复国主义有时与反犹太主义混淆。 | 近义词: None (due to the complex and politically charged nature of the term, direct synonyms are difficult to identify, although related terms like "anti-Israelism" are sometimes used.) | 反义词: Zionism | 词性: noun
