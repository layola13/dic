expression:/ɪkˈspreʃən/ | n. | 表达；表情；措辞 |  The process of making known one's thoughts or feelings.  A look on someone's face that conveys a particular emotion. A particular word or phrase. | Her face was pale, but her expression was calm. 她的脸色苍白，但表情镇定。 | synonyms: articulation, utterance, manifestation, declaration, look, countenance  | antonyms: repression, concealment, suppression | noun
