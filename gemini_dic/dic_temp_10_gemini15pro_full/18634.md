benzaldehyde:/ˌbenˈzældɪhaɪd/ (US), /ˌbenˈzældɪhaɪd/ (UK)| n.|苯甲醛|The simplest aromatic aldehyde, having the formula C₇H₆O, and used in the manufacture of dyes and perfumes and as a solvent.|Benzaldehyde gives almonds their characteristic smell and taste. 苯甲醛赋予杏仁特有的气味和味道。|Synonyms:  benzoic aldehyde|Antonyms: None|Noun
