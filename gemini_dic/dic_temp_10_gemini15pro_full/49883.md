demulsion:/diːˈmʌlʃən/ | n. |破乳, 解乳化|The demulsion of crude oil is a crucial step in refining. 原油的破乳是炼油过程中至关重要的一步。|例句：The chemical is designed for demulsion of water in oil.  这种化学物质是为油水分离而设计的。

The demulsion process separates the oil and water. 破乳过程将油和水分离。|近义词：separation, breaking, resolution|反义词：emulsification|词性:名词
