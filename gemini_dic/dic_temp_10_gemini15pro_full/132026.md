noncorrespondingly:/ˌnɒnkɒrɪˈspɒndɪŋli/
adverb
不相符地，不相应地
in a way that does not correspond or match.
The two sets of data noncorrespondingly reflect the market trends. 这两组数据未能相符地反映市场趋势。
The map and the terrain noncorrespondingly aligned, causing confusion for the hikers. 地图和地形不对应，给徒步旅行者带来了困惑。
Synonyms: disproportionately,  incompatibly,  divergently, dissimilarly
Antonyms: correspondingly,  congruently,  similarly,  consistently
词性: adverb
