fleecy:/ˈfliːsi/| adj.|羊毛状的，蓬松的|Covered with or resembling fleece; soft and woolly.|The lamb had soft, fleecy wool. 小羊羔有着柔软蓬松的羊毛。|近义词：fluffy, woolly, downy|反义词:coarse, rough|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *flesy, *flecy, from Old English *flēsig, flēsiġ (“fleecy, woolly”), from flēos (“fleece”) +‎ -ig (“-y”). Cognate with Scots fleecy (“fleecy”), West Frisian fleesich (“fleecy”).
