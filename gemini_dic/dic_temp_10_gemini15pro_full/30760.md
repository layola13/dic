cessations:/seˈseɪʃənz/| n.|停止，中止，中断|The cessation of hostilities brought much-needed relief to the war-torn region. 停火给饱受战争蹂躏的地区带来了亟需的缓解。|例句：The doctor recommended a complete cessation of smoking. 医生建议彻底戒烟。

例句：After the cessation of rain, the sun emerged. 雨停之后，太阳出来了。

近义词：stopping, halting, ending, discontinuation, suspension|反义词: commencement, beginning, start, continuation, resumption|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin cessatio, from cessare "to cease," from Latin cessare "to give way, yield, go slow, stop," frequentative of cedere "to go, withdraw, yield"
