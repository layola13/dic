adactyl: /əˈdæktɪl/
adjective
无指（趾）的
Lacking fingers or toes; lacking digits.
The adactyl frog couldn't climb the tree. 这只无指蛙爬不上树。

The museum displayed a fossil of an adactyl reptile. 博物馆展出了一只无指爬行动物的化石。

近义词: digitless
反义词: polydactyl, pentadactyl
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *ἀ- (a-, "without") + *δάκτυλος (dáktulos, “finger, toe”).


USAGE->
This word is primarily used in scientific or technical contexts.

The veterinarian examined the adactyl kitten. = 兽医检查了这只没有指头的小猫。

Due to a genetic mutation, the lizard was born adactyl. = 由于基因突变，这只蜥蜴生来就没有指头。

PHRASE->
While "adactyl" doesn't have common phrases associated with it like more common words, it can be used in descriptive phrases:

an adactyl limb = 无指（趾）的肢体
an adactyl forefoot = 无指的前足
born adactyl = 生来就没有指（趾）
essentially adactyl = 基本无指（趾）
completely adactyl = 完全无指（趾）
