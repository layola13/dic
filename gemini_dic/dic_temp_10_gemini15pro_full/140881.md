on-the-up-and-up:/ˌɒn ði ˈʌp ən ˈʌp/ /ˌɑːn ði ˈʌp ən ˈʌp/| adj. |正直的，诚实的；蒸蒸日上的，欣欣向荣的|Honest and straightforward; improving or becoming more successful.|例句：His business is on the up and up. 他的生意蒸蒸日上。|近义词：honest, legitimate, thriving, flourishing|反义词：dishonest, shady, declining, failing|词性:形容词
