benefiters:/ˈbɛnɪfɪtərz/
noun
受益人，受惠者
Those who receive an advantage or profit from something.
The new tax law will greatly benefit homeowners, making them the primary benefiters of the legislation. 新的税法将使房主 greatly 受益，使他们成为该立法的 primary 受益者。
beneficiaries, recipients, gainers
losers, sufferers
n.
