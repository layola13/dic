humidify:/hjuːˈmɪdɪfaɪ/ |v. |加湿，使湿润|To make (something, such as air) moist or more moist. |例句：The humidifier helps to humidify the dry air in the winter.  冬天，加湿器有助于加湿干燥的空气。

例句：You can humidify the air by placing a bowl of water near the radiator. 你可以在散热器附近放一碗水来加湿空气。|近义词：moisten, dampen |反义词：dehumidify, dry |词性:动词

词源: From Late Latin humidificare, from Latin humidus (moist) + -ificare (to make).
