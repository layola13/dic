obliterate:/əˈblɪtəreɪt/
verb
消灭，抹去，彻底摧毁
To remove or destroy all traces of; do away with completely.
The bombing raid obliterated the entire city. 轰炸袭击彻底摧毁了整个城市。
The heavy snowfall obliterated all signs of the path. 大雪掩盖了小路的所有痕迹。
synonyms:destroy, eradicate, annihilate, expunge, erase
antonyms:create, build, establish, preserve
