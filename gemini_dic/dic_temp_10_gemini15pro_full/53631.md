discernibleness:/dɪˈsɜːrnɪblənəs/
n.
可辨别性，识别性
The quality of being discernible; perceptibility.
The discernibleness of the trail in the snow made it easy to follow.  雪地上足迹清晰可见，很容易跟随。

Antonyms:  invisibility, obscurity, vagueness, indistinctness
