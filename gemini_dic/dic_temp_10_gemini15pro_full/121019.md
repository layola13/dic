miscommunicate:/ˌmɪskəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt/ | v. | 误传；沟通不畅 | To convey information inaccurately or unclearly, leading to misunderstanding. |  We miscommunicated about the meeting time, so I arrived an hour late. 我们开会的时间沟通有误，所以我迟到了一个小时。

The instructions were miscommunicated, which resulted in a lot of confusion.  说明传达不清，导致了很多混乱。

| 近义词: misunderstand, misinterpret, confuse | 反义词: communicate clearly, convey accurately | 词性: 动词
