mesodont:/ˈmɛsəˌdɒnt/ | adj. n. | 中等齿的，中等齿动物 | Having teeth of moderate size, or relating to animals with such teeth.  A mammal with molars intermediate between the bunodont and selenodont patterns, such as swine and some primates. | Example: Mesodont teeth are characteristic of certain herbivores. 中等齿是某些食草动物的特征。| Example: Pigs are considered mesodonts. 猪被认为是中等齿动物。| Synonym: medium-toothed | Antonym: bunodont, selenodont | Adjective, Noun
