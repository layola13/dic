disimplicate:/dɪsˈɪmplɪkeɪt/
verb
使免受牵连；澄清；证明…的清白
To free from implication or entanglement; to absolve from suspicion or guilt.
The lawyer worked tirelessly to disimplicate his client from the robbery charges.  律师不知疲倦地工作，以使他的客户免于抢劫指控。
The new evidence disimplicated the suspect. 新的证据证明了嫌疑人的清白。
synonyms: exonerate, acquit, absolve, clear, vindicate
antonyms: implicate, incriminate, accuse, charge
词性: 动词
