antinaturalist:/ˌæntɪˈnætʃrəlɪst/
adjective
反自然主义的
Opposed to naturalism, especially in art, literature, or philosophy.
His antinaturalist stance in theatre often led to abstract and symbolic productions. 他在戏剧中反自然主义的立场经常导致抽象和象征性的作品。
Synonyms: artificial, contrived, nonrealistic
Antonyms: naturalistic, realistic
词性: 形容词
