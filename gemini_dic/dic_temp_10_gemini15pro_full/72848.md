folk-rock:/ˈfoʊk rɑːk/ | n. | 民谣摇滚 | A genre of music that combines elements of folk music and rock music, typically characterized by acoustic instruments, lyrical themes, and vocal harmonies. |  Bob Dylan's move to electric guitar was a key moment in the development of folk-rock. 鲍勃·迪伦转向电吉他是民谣摇滚发展中的关键时刻。
