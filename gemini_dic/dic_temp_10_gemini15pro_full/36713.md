collision:/kəˈlɪʒən/ | n. | 碰撞，冲突 | an instance of one moving object or person striking violently against another. |  The two cars were involved in a head-on collision. 两辆车迎头相撞。

There was a collision of interests between the two companies. 两家公司之间存在利益冲突。 | near miss, crash, impact | avoidance, agreement | noun
