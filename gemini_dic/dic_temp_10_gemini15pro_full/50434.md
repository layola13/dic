deprecable:/ˈdɛprɪkəbəl/| adj.|应受谴责的；可非难的；令人反感的|Worthy of disapproval or censure; blameworthy.|His deprecable behavior led to his dismissal from the company. 他应受谴责的行为导致他被公司解雇。|近义词：reprehensible, objectionable, blameworthy, censurable|反义词：commendable, laudable, praiseworthy|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *deprecabilis, from deprecari (“to avert by prayer, deprecate”) + -bilis.
