linear:/ˈlɪniər/| adj.|线性的，直线的|Relating to or resembling a line; involving one dimension only.  Progressing in a single series or direction.|例句：The graph shows a linear relationship between the two variables. 这张图表显示了两个变量之间的线性关系。|近义词：straight, direct|反义词：nonlinear, curved|词性:adjective
linear:/ˈlɪniər/| n.|线性方程，线性关系|A linear equation or function.|例句：The problem can be solved using a system of linears. 这个问题可以用线性方程组来解决。|近义词：line, straight line|反义词：curve|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin linearis "belonging to a line," from Latin linea "linen thread, string, line," from linum "flax."
