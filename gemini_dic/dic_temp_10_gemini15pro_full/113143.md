lunula:/ˈluːnjʊlə/ (US), /ˈljuːnjʊlə/ (UK)| n. |甲半月，月牙|The whitish, half-moon shaped area at the base of a fingernail or toenail.|My lunula on my thumb is very prominent. 我的拇指上的甲半月很明显。|Synonyms: half-moon, lunule|Antonyms: None|Noun

lunula:/ˈluːnjʊlə/ (US), /ˈljuːnjʊlə/ (UK)| n. |小月面|A crescent-shaped structure or marking, especially in anatomy or botany. (This meaning is less common than the fingernail meaning.)|The butterfly has lunulae on its wings. 蝴蝶的翅膀上有小月面。|Synonyms: crescent|Antonyms: None|Noun
