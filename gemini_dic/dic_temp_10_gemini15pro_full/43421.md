crapaud:/kræˈpoʊd/ | n. |蟾蜍; (尤指)大蟾蜍 |A large toad, especially a marine toad. | The crapaud hopped across the damp ground.  这只蟾蜍跳过潮湿的地面。| Synonym: toad, bullfrog | Antonym: N/A | Noun

Etymology:
From French *crapaud*, from Old French *crape*, *crespe* (“toad”), of uncertain origin, probably from Vulgar Latin *crepa*, *crepare* (“to crack, burst”) because of their warty skin.
