guerrero: /ɡeˈreɾo/ (Spanish pronunciation)| n.|战士;勇士; 斗士|A soldier or fighter, especially one who is brave and skilled.|例句：El guerrero luchó valientemente en la batalla.  The warrior fought bravely in the battle.|近义词：soldier, fighter, warrior, combatant|反义词: coward, pacifist|词性:noun
Etymology: From Spanish *guerrero*, derived from Late Latin *guerra* "war," of Germanic origin.
