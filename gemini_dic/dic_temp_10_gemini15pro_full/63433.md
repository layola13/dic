entreated:/ɪnˈtriːtɪd/ | v. |恳求，乞求 |To ask earnestly or beg; implore. |She entreated him to stay. 她恳求他留下。|近义词：pleaded, begged, implored, beseeched |反义词：demanded, commanded |verb

词源:
From Middle English *entreten*, from Old French *entraiter*, from *en- "in" + *traiter "to treat, handle, deal with"*
