fluxing:/ˈflʌksɪŋ/
verb
熔化; 钎焊; 用焊剂处理
The process of treating or coating with a flux.
例句：The artist was fluxing the metal for his sculpture. 艺术家正在为他的雕塑熔化金属。

例句：Fluxing is a crucial step in preparing the metal for soldering.  用焊剂处理是准备焊接金属的关键步骤。
近义词：smelting, fusing, welding, brazing
反义词：solidifying, freezing
词性:v.


fluxing:/ˈflʌksɪŋ/
noun
熔化; 钎焊; 流动
The act of flowing or the state of being in constant change.
例句：The constant fluxing of the market makes it difficult to predict.  市场的不断波动使得难以预测。

例句：The fluxing of ideas in the brainstorming session was impressive.  头脑风暴会议中思想的流动令人印象深刻。
近义词：flow, fluctuation, change, variability
反义词：stability, constancy
词性:n.
