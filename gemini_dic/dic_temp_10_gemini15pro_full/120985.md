miscible:/ˈmɪsɪbl/ | adj. | 可混合的 | (of liquids) Forming a homogeneous mixture when added together. | Oil and water are not miscible. 油和水不能混合。 |  Ethanol and water are miscible in all proportions. 乙醇和水可以以任何比例混合。 | 近义词: mixable, blendable, combinable | 反义词: immiscible, unmixable | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *miscibilis, from Latin miscēre "to mix" + -ibilis "-ible".
