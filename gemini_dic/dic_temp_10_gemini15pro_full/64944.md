esteemer:/ɪˈstimər/ | n. | 尊重者，敬重者；评价者 | One who esteems; one who holds in high regard. | He is an esteemer of fine art. 他是一位美术鉴赏家。| 近义词: admirer, respecter, appreciator | 反义词: critic, detractor | 词性: noun

词源: From the verb "esteem" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Esteem" comes from Old French "estimer," from Latin "aestimare"  "to estimate, value, appraise."
