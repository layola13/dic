gloomiest:/ˈɡluːmiɪst/| adj.|最阴郁的，最沮丧的|Superlative form of gloomy: most gloomy.  It describes something that is extremely dark, depressing, or pessimistic.|The gloomiest day of the year is usually in December. 一年中最阴郁的日子通常在十二月。|Near Synonyms: darkest, bleakest, dreariest, most depressing|Antonyms: brightest, happiest, cheeriest|adjective
