coleading:/ˌkoʊˈliːdɪŋ/
adjective|共同领导的|sharing the leadership of something with someone else|Example: They are coleading the project. 他们共同领导这个项目。|近义词：jointly leading, co-managing, sharing leadership|反义词:solely leading, independently leading
verb (present participle)|共同领导|the act of leading something with someone else|Example: They are coleading a team of developers. 他们共同领导一个开发团队。|近义词：jointly leading, co-managing, sharing leadership|反义词:solely leading, independently leading
noun (gerund)|共同领导|the act of leading something with someone else|Example: Coleading can be challenging but rewarding. 共同领导可能充满挑战，但也回报丰厚。|近义词：joint leadership, co-management, shared leadership|反义词:sole leadership, independent leadership
