antechapel:/ˈæntiˌtʃæpəl/| n.|前殿；入口小教堂|A small chapel leading to a larger one, especially at the western end of a cathedral.|例句：The antechapel was filled with mourners.  前殿挤满了哀悼者。

例句：Visitors often pause to admire the architecture of the antechapel.  游客们常常驻足欣赏前殿的建筑。|近义词：narthex, vestibule, lobby|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
