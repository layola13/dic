exedra:/ˈɛksɪdrə/| n.|露天剧场，壁龛，凹室|A semicircular or rectangular recess, often with raised seating, typically built outdoors.|The guests gathered in the exedra to enjoy the sunset. 客人们聚集在露天剧场欣赏日落。|Synonyms: alcove, niche, recess|Antonyms: projection, protrusion|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *exedra*, from Ancient Greek ἐξέδρα (*exédra*), from ἐξ (*ex*, “out”) + ἕδρα (*hédra*, “seat, base”).
