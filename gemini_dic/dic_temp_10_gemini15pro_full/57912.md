drillabilities:/ˌdrɪləˈbɪlətiz/| n. |可钻性(复数)|The ease with which a material can be drilled.  It's influenced by factors like hardness, brittleness, and abrasiveness.|The drillabilities of different rock formations vary significantly. 不同岩层的可钻性差异很大。|Synonyms:  machinability, workability|Antonyms:  impenetrable, unworkable|noun

drillability:/ˌdrɪləˈbɪləti/| n. |可钻性|The ease with which a material can be drilled.  It's influenced by factors like hardness, brittleness, and abrasiveness.|The drillability of this rock is poor.  这种岩石的可钻性很差。|Synonyms:  machinability, workability|Antonyms:  impenetrable, unworkable|noun
