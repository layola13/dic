outreigned:/ˌaʊtˈreɪnd/
verb
统治比…更久; 比…在位时间长
ruled longer than; reigned for a longer period than
Queen Victoria outreigned all previous British monarchs. 维多利亚女王比所有以前的英国君主在位时间都长。
近义词: outlasted, survived
反义词: preceded
