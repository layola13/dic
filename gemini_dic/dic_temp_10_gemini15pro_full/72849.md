folker:/ˈfoʊkər/ (US) /ˈfəʊkə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 民谣歌手；民间音乐爱好者；人们；家伙|A person who performs or enjoys folk music; people in general (informal).|例句：The folker sang a traditional ballad.  这位民谣歌手唱了一首传统的民谣。

例句：The local folkers gather every week for a sing-along. 当地的民谣爱好者每周聚在一起唱歌。|近义词：folk musician, folksinger, people, folks|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "elitist" in certain contexts)|词性:noun
