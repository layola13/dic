overdebilitate: /ˌoʊvərdiˈbɪlɪteɪt/
verb
过度削弱，使极度衰弱
To weaken excessively; to severely impair the strength or vitality of.
The prolonged illness overdebilitated him, leaving him unable to walk without assistance.  长期患病使他极度衰弱，让他无法在没有帮助的情况下行走。
Chronic stress can overdebilitate the immune system, making individuals more susceptible to infections. 慢性压力会过度削弱免疫系统，使个体更容易受到感染。
近义词: weaken, enfeeble, debilitate, incapacitate
反义词: strengthen, invigorate, empower


词性: verb

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "debilitate" (from Latin "debilis" meaning "weak").
