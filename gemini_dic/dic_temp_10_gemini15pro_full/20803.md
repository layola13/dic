blasphemy:/ˈblæsfəmi/ /ˈblæsfəmi/| n.|亵渎，亵渎神明，|Irreverent behavior or language toward anything considered sacred. |例句：His blasphemy was met with outrage. 他的亵渎行为遭到了愤怒的回应。|例句：Burning the holy book was an act of blasphemy. 烧毁圣书是一种亵渎行为。|近义词：profanity, sacrilege, impiety|反义词：reverence, piety|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French blasfemer, from Late Latin blasphemare, from Greek blasphēmein "to speak evil of, slander, revile," from blasphemos "evil-speaking," from blaptein "to harm, injure" + phēmē "speech, utterance, rumor, report."
