devitrification:/diːˌvɪtrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
失透，脱玻化
the process of changing from a glassy or vitreous state to a crystalline state; the loss of the glassy qualities of a substance
The devitrification of the glass made it opaque.  玻璃的失透使其变得不透明。
crystallization, vitrification
