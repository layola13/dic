minute:/ˈmɪnɪt/ (US), /ˈmɪnɪt/ (UK)| n. adj.|分钟；一会儿；瞬间；备忘录；会议记录|A unit of time equal to 60 seconds.  Extremely small; tiny.|例句：The meeting lasted for ten minutes. 会议持续了十分钟。

例句：It took only a minute to solve the problem. 只花了一分钟就解决了这个问题。|近义词：moment, instant, second, short while|反义词：hour, day, eternity|词性: noun

minute:/maɪˈnjuːt/ (US), /maɪˈnjuːt/ (UK)| adj.|微小的；细微的；详细的|Extremely small; tiny; paying attention to detail.|例句：The difference between the two versions is minute. 两个版本之间的差异微乎其微。

例句：He gave a minute description of the event. 他对事件进行了详细的描述。|近义词：tiny, minuscule, microscopic, detailed|反义词：huge, enormous, massive, general|词性: adjective
