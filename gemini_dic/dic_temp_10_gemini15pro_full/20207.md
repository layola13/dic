birdie:/ˈbɜːrdi/ (US) /ˈbɜːdi/ (UK)| n.|小鸟，(高尔夫球)低于标准杆一杆|A small bird. In golf, a score of one stroke less than par for a particular hole.|I saw a little birdie in the garden. 我在花园里看到一只小鸟。|He got a birdie on the fifth hole. 他在第五洞打出了低于标准杆一杆的成绩。|synonyms: bird (general), chick (baby bird)|antonyms: N/A (no direct opposite for a small bird)|noun
