leotard: /ˈliːəˌtɑːrd/ (US), /ˈliːəˌtɑːd/ (UK)| n. |紧身衣|A close-fitting, one-piece garment, usually made of a stretchy material like spandex or lycra, that covers the torso and may have long or short sleeves and legs. It is commonly worn by dancers, gymnasts, acrobats, and athletes.|例句：The dancer wore a bright pink leotard.  舞者穿着一件亮粉色的紧身衣。

例句：She bought a new leotard for her gymnastics class. 她为体操课买了一件新的紧身衣。|近义词：unitard, bodysuit|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps loose clothing, street clothes) |词性:noun
