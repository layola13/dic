Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Old French non- and directly from Latin non "not."
vertical (adjective): From French vertical, from Late Latin verticalis "of the vertex," from Latin vertex (genitive verticis) "highest point, summit, top, crown of the head."
-ness (suffix):  Forms nouns meaning "state or quality of being."  From Old English -nes(s).
