outculling: /ˈaʊtkʌlɪŋ/ |n.|剔除；淘汰|The outculling of weak or sick animals from a herd is essential for maintaining its overall health. 从畜群中剔除虚弱或生病的动物对于维持其整体健康至关重要。|例句：Outculling of diseased trees helps prevent the spread of infection. 剔除病树有助于防止感染蔓延。

例句：The farmer practiced regular outculling to improve the genetic quality of his livestock.  这位农民定期进行剔除，以提高其牲畜的遗传质量。

近义词：culling, weeding out, elimination, removal|反义词：inclusion, introduction|词性：名词
