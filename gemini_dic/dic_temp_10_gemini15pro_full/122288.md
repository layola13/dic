mitotic:/mɪˈtɒtɪk/ (UK) /maɪˈtɑːtɪk/ (US)| adj.|有丝分裂的|Relating to or involving mitosis (cell division).|The mitotic process is crucial for cell growth and repair. 有丝分裂过程对细胞生长和修复至关重要。|The drug inhibits mitotic activity in cancer cells. 这种药物抑制癌细胞的有丝分裂活动。|Synonyms: karyokinetic|Antonyms: amitotic|adjective
