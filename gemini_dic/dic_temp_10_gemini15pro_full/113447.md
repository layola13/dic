lynchpin:/ˈlɪntʃpɪn/| n.|关键人物;核心人物;关键;核心;|A person or thing vital to an enterprise or organization.  Something that holds the various elements of a complicated structure together.|例句：She is the lynchpin of the whole team. 她 是整个团队的核心人物。

例句：The central axle is the lynchpin of the whole vehicle. 中央轴是整辆车的关键。|近义词：keystone, cornerstone, linchpin, kingpin, centerpiece|反义词:nonessential, unimportant|词性:noun
