hindlimbs:/ˈhaɪndˌlɪmz/| n. |后肢|The hindlimbs of a kangaroo are much larger and stronger than its forelimbs. 袋鼠的后肢比前肢更大更强壮。|例句：The dog injured its hindlimbs in the accident. 这只狗在事故中后腿受伤了。

The cheetah uses its powerful hindlimbs to achieve incredible speeds. 猎豹用它强有力的后肢达到惊人的速度。|近义词：rear legs, back legs|反义词:forelimbs|Noun
