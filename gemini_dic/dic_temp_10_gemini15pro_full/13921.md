auld:/ɔːld/| adj.|老的；古老的；旧时的；以前的|Old, of long standing, especially of a person or thing affectionately remembered.  Specifically a Scottish variant of "old". |例句：Auld Lang Syne is a traditional Scottish song.  友谊地久天长是一首传统的苏格兰歌曲。

例句：We met up with some auld friends. 我们和一些老朋友见了面。|近义词：old, ancient, antique, venerable|反义词：new, young, modern, recent|词性:形容词
