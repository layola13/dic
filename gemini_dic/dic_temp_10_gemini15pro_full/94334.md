impiety:/ɪmˈpaɪəti/| n.|不虔诚，不敬，亵渎|Lack of piety or reverence, especially for God or sacred things.|His impiety shocked the devout villagers. 他的不虔诚震惊了虔诚的村民们。|近义词：irreverence, blasphemy, sacrilege, profaneness|反义词：piety, reverence, devotion|词性:名词

Etymology: From Late Middle English *impiete*, from Old French *impieté*, from Latin *impiētās*, from *impius* ("impious"), from *in-* ("not") + *pius* ("pious").
