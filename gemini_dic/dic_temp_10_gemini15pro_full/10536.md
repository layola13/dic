appraisable:/əˈpreɪzəbl/
adjective
可估价的，可评价的
Capable of being appraised or evaluated; assessable.
The antique furniture was appraisable, and the owner hoped it was worth a fortune.  这件古董家具可以估价，主人希望它值一大笔钱。
The impact of the new policy is appraisable after one year. 新政策的影响一年后可以评估。
近义词: assessable, evaluable, estimable
反义词: inestimable, immeasurable, invaluable
词性: adjective

词源: From appraise + -able.  Appraise comes from Old French apreisier "to value, appreciate," from a- "to" + preisier "to prize."
