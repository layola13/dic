gipsyesque:/ˌɡɪpsiˈɛsk/ | adj. |吉普赛式的，波西米亚风格的 |  Resembling or characteristic of Gypsies or their supposed way of life; bohemian.  Evokes a sense of romantic wandering, free-spiritedness, and colorful, unconventional style. |  Her gipsyesque clothing and love of travel hinted at a restless soul. 她吉普赛式的服装和对旅行的热爱暗示着她不安分的灵魂。 | The caravan had a gipsyesque charm, with its brightly painted exterior and flowing curtains. 这辆大篷车有一种吉普赛式的魅力，有着鲜艳的油漆外墙和飘逸的窗帘。 |  bohemian, nomadic, romantic, free-spirited, unconventional | conventional, settled, conformist | adjective
