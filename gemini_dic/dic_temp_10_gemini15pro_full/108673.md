league:/liːɡ/
noun
联盟，社团，联赛
a group of people or organizations that have joined together for a particular purpose
例句：They formed a league of friends. 他们组成了一个朋友圈。
例句：The team plays in the national league.  这个队参加国家联赛。
近义词：alliance, association, federation, union
反义词：division, separation
