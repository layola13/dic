if's:/ɪfs/ | n. | 假设，设想 |  A contraction of "if" and a pluralized form indicating multiple hypothetical situations or conditions. It refers to the uncertainties or possibilities that might occur. | There are too many if's in this plan, making it unreliable.  这个计划里假设太多了，很不靠谱。|Synonyms:  hypotheticals, possibilities, uncertainties, contingencies | Antonyms: certainties, realities | noun


Etymology: From "if," which comes from Old English "gif," meaning "if," "on condition that."  The addition of -'s creates a plural noun form, referring to multiple conditions.
