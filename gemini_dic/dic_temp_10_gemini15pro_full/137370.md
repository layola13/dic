nontemperable:/ˌnɒnˈtɛmpərəbl/
adjective
不可回火的，不能回火的
Not capable of being tempered (hardened by heating and then cooling).
This alloy is nontemperable, so its hardness cannot be increased through heat treatment. 这种合金不可回火，因此无法通过热处理来提高其硬度。
The nontemperable steel was unsuitable for the project which needed high strength components.  这种不可回火的钢材不适合需要高强度部件的项目。
近义词:unhardenable, non-hardenable
反义词:temperable, hardenable
词性:adjective
词源: "non-" (not) + "temperable" (capable of being tempered), from "temper" (to moderate or harden) ultimately from Latin "temperare" (to mix, blend, moderate).
