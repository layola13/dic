bigot's:/ˈbɪɡəts/
n.
偏执者的；顽固者的（bigot的名词所有格）
belonging to or associated with a bigot; characteristic of a bigot
The bigot's views were offensive and harmful. 偏执者的观点既冒犯人又有害。
近义词: prejudiced person's, intolerant person's, fanatic's
反义词: tolerant person's, open-minded person's

词性: 名词所有格
