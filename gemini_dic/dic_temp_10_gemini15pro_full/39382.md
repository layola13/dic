conjunctively:/kənˈdʒʌŋktɪvli/
adv.
连接地，联合地
in a way that connects two or more things, or that combines two or more qualities or features
The tests are performed conjunctively, meaning all tests must pass for the system to be considered operational. 这些测试是联合进行的，意味着所有测试都必须通过才能认为系统可以运行。
The report assessed the risks both individually and conjunctively. 该报告分别和综合评估了这些风险。
近义词: jointly, together, combinedly
反义词: separately, disjunctively
