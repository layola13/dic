golds:/ɡoʊldz/| n. |金牌；金子（复数，非标准用法）|A nonstandard plural form of "gold," sometimes used to refer to gold medals or gold coins.  It's more common and grammatically correct to use "gold" as a mass noun, even when referring to multiple units.|He won three golds at the Olympics. (Nonstandard) 他在奥运会上赢得了三枚金牌。|Near Synonyms: gold medals, gold coins|Antonyms: N/A|noun

gold:/ɡoʊld/| n. adj.|金；金色的；金制的|A precious yellow metal, often used in jewelry and currency.  As an adjective, it describes something made of or colored like gold.|The ring was made of pure gold. 这枚戒指由纯金制成。 The leaves turned a beautiful gold in the autumn. 秋天，树叶变成了美丽的金色。|Near Synonyms:  (n.)  aureate, bullion, (adj.) golden, gilded|Antonyms: (adj.) silver|noun, adjective
