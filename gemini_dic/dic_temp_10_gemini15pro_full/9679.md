antitetanic: /ˌæntiˈtɛtənɪk/ | adj. | 抗破伤风的，破伤风抗毒素的 |  Relating to or effective against tetanus (lockjaw), a bacterial infection. |  The doctor administered an antitetanic injection. 医生注射了破伤风抗毒素。| Synonyms: anti-tetanus, tetanus-immune | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but susceptibility to tetanus would be the opposite condition) | adjective

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): from Greek anti- "against, opposite of, instead of, in return for"
tetanus: from Late Latin tetanus "rigid, stretched," from Greek tetanos "rigid, stretched," verbal adjective from teinein "to stretch"
