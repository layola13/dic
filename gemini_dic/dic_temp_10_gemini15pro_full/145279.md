overborrowing: /ˌoʊvərˈbɔːroʊɪŋ/ /ˌəʊvəˈbɒrəʊɪŋ/
noun
过度借贷
The act of borrowing more money than one can realistically repay.
Excessive borrowing by consumers can lead to a financial crisis.  消费者过度借贷可能导致金融危机。
overspending, excessive borrowing
underborrowing, saving
