nonwatertight:/ˌnɒnˈwɔːtətaɪt/ | adj. | 不防水的，不密封的 | Not impervious to water; allowing water to pass through. | The roof was nonwatertight and leaked during the storm. 屋顶不防水，暴风雨期间漏水了。 | The seams of the boat were nonwatertight, causing it to slowly take on water. 船的接缝不防水，导致它慢慢进水。 |  near synonyms: leaky, permeable, porous | antonyms: watertight, waterproof, impermeable | adjective
