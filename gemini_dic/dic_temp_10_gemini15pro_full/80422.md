gona:/ˈɡʌnə/ /ˈɡoʊnə/ |Informal contraction|将要，会| "Gonna" is a colloquial and informal contraction of "going to," used to express future intent or prediction. It's primarily used in spoken English and informal writing.| I'm gonna go to the store later. 我晚点要去商店。| going to, will | wouldn't, shan't | contraction

注意事项:
As demonstrated, "gonna" is inherently informal and replaces "going to" which is already a less formal way to express the future compared to "will."  Avoid using "gonna" in formal writing or speeches.  It's best suited for casual conversations, texting, and informal writing.
