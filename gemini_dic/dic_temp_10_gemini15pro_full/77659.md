geldings:/ˈɡɛldɪŋz/
noun
阉割的马；阉马（gelding 的复数）
Castrated male horses.
The geldings were put out to pasture. 阉割的马被放出去吃草了。
synonyms:  castrated horses, neutered horses
antonyms: stallions, colts
n.


gelding:/ˈɡɛldɪŋ/
noun
阉割的马；阉马
A castrated male horse.
The gelding was gentle and easy to ride. 这匹阉马性情温顺，很容易骑。
synonyms:  castrated horse, neutered horse
antonyms: stallion, colt
n.
