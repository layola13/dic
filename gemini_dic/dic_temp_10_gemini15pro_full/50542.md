depurgatory:/ˈdɛpərɡətɔːri/ /ˈdɛpərɡətəri/
noun
炼狱；净化；清除不良事物的地方
A place or state of temporary suffering or purification, especially one believed to cleanse the soul of sins before entering heaven.
The book was put through a depurgatory process to remove any offensive content. 这本书经过了净化处理，去除了所有冒犯性的内容。
近义词: purgatory, limbo
反义词: heaven, paradise
词性: n.

Etymology:
From Late Latin *depurgatorium, from depurgare "to cleanse," from de- "from, out of" + purgare "to purge."
