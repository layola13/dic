Gondwana:/ɡɒndˈwɑːnə/ /ɡɑːndˈwɑːnə/
n.
冈瓦纳大陆，冈瓦纳古陆
A vast southern supercontinent that existed from the late Precambrian to the Jurassic. It comprised present-day South America, Africa, Arabia, Madagascar, India, Australia, New Zealand, and Antarctica.
The breakup of Gondwana led to the formation of the Atlantic and Indian Oceans.  冈瓦纳大陆的分裂导致了大西洋和印度洋的形成。
Laurasia, Pangea
