nonforensically:/ˌnɑːnfəˈrɛnsɪkli/
adv.
非司法地，非正式地
Relating to or denoting something that is not related to or used in legal proceedings or forensic science.
The issue was discussed nonforensically, focusing on the social implications rather than legal ramifications.  这个问题被非正式地讨论，重点关注社会影响而不是法律后果。
The data was collected nonforensically, meaning it wasn't intended for use in a court of law.  这些数据是非司法收集的，意味着它并非用于法庭。
Synonym: informally, unofficially, casually
Antonym: forensically, legally, judicially
词性: 副词
