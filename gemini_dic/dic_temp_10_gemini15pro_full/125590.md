multitudinously:/ˌmʌltɪˈtjuːdɪnəsli/
adv.
大量地，众多地
in great numbers; in a multitude.
The stars shone multitudinously in the clear night sky.  星星在晴朗的夜空中闪耀着。
The flowers bloomed multitudinously in the spring. 春天百花盛开。
Synonyms: abundantly, copiously, profusely, numerously
Antonyms: sparsely, scarcely, meagerly
词性: 副词


Etymology:
From Latin *multitudinōsus*, from multitūdō (“multitude”) + -ōsus.
