eternities:/ɪˈtɜːrnɪtiz/
n.
永恒; 无尽的时期; 来世;
A state of endless duration; infinite time.  The afterlife.
例句：He spoke of eternities spent in blissful contemplation. 他谈到在幸福的沉思中度过的永恒时光。

例句：The thought of facing eternities in hell terrified him.  想到在地狱中度过永恒让他感到恐惧。

近义词：infinity, perpetuity, forever, timelessness
反义词：finitude, temporality, brevity

词性: 名词 (plural of eternity)
词源: From Old French *eternité, from Latin aeternitas (“eternity”), from aeternus (“eternal”).
