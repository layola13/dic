disinfective:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛktɪv/
adjective
消毒的，杀菌的
Capable of destroying disease-causing microorganisms.
The disinfectant spray is highly disinfective against bacteria and viruses.  这种消毒喷雾对细菌和病毒有很强的消毒作用。
近义词:antiseptic, bactericidal, germicidal
反义词:infective, septic
词性:adjective

disinfective:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfɛktɪv/
noun
消毒剂
An agent that destroys disease-causing microorganisms.
The hospital uses a powerful disinfective to clean operating rooms.  医院使用一种强力消毒剂来清洁手术室。
近义词:antiseptic, disinfectant
反义词:contaminant
词性:noun
