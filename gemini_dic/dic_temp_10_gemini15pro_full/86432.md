heeler:/ˈhiːlər/| n.|牧牛犬，紧随者|A dog, especially an Australian cattle dog, trained to nip at the heels of cattle to herd them.  A person who follows another closely. |例句：The heeler kept the cattle moving forward. 牧牛犬让牛群继续前进。

例句：He's such a heeler, always tagging along with the boss. 他真是个跟屁虫，总是跟着老板。|近义词：follower, supporter, fan, devotee|反义词：leader, opponent, critic|词性: 名词
