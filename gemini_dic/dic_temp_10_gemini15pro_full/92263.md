hyperventilates:/ˌhaɪpərˈvɛntɪleɪts/ | v. |  过度换气，呼吸急促 | breathes too rapidly, causing a decrease in carbon dioxide in the blood and often dizziness and tingling in the fingers and toes. | She hyperventilated when she heard the news. 她听到这个消息后呼吸急促。| Example: The runner hyperventilated after the strenuous race.  赛跑运动员在剧烈比赛后呼吸急促。| Synonyms: overbreathe, gasp, pant | Antonyms: hypoventilate | Verb
