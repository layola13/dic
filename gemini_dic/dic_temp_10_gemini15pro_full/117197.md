measurelessly:/ˈmeʒər.ləs.li/
adverb|不可估量地，无限地|Without limits or bounds; to an immeasurable degree.|Her talent was measurelessly vast. 她的才华无限宽广。|Example:  The universe is measurelessly vast. 宇宙浩瀚无垠。|Synonyms: immensely, boundlessly, infinitely, limitlessly|Antonyms: finitely, limitedly|词性: 副词

measurelessly:/ˈmeʒər.ləs.li/
adverb|无度地，过度地|Excessively; to a degree that is considered excessive.|He was measurelessly proud of his son. 他对儿子无比骄傲。|Example:  She grieved measurelessly for her lost love. 她为逝去的爱情悲痛欲绝。|Synonyms: excessively, immoderately, inordinately|Antonyms: moderately, reasonably|词性: 副词
