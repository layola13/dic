bacciferous:/ˌbækˈsɪfərəs/
adjective
结浆果的; 多浆果的
Bearing or producing berries.
The bacciferous shrub was laden with ripe, juicy berries. 结浆果的灌木上挂满了成熟多汁的浆果。
Near synonyms: berry-bearing, berry-producing
No common antonyms.
