mormon:/ˈmɔːrmən/ (US), /ˈmɔːmən/ (UK)| n. adj.| 摩门教徒；摩门教的 |A member of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints, a religion founded by Joseph Smith in the United States in the 19th century.  Also used as an adjective to describe things related to Mormonism.|例句：He is a devout Mormon. 他是一位虔诚的摩门教徒。|近义词：Latter-day Saint (preferred term), LDS|反义词:non-Mormon|词性:noun, adjective
