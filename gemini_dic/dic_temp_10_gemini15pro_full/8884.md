antifreeze:/ˈæntiˌfriːz/| n.|防冻剂|A liquid, typically containing ethylene glycol, which is added to the water in a vehicle's radiator to prevent it from freezing.| I added antifreeze to my car's radiator before winter. 我在冬天来临之前往汽车散热器里加了防冻剂。|

|Make sure you use the correct type of antifreeze for your car. 确保你使用的是适合你汽车的防冻剂类型。|近义词：coolant|反义词:|noun
