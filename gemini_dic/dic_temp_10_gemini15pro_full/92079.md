hyperrationality:/ˌhaɪpərˌræʃəˈnæləti/ | n. | 超理性 | The excessive pursuit of logical reasoning and optimization, often to the detriment of practical considerations or human values. |  He demonstrated hyperrationality in his investment strategy, meticulously calculating every possible outcome. 他在投资策略中表现出超理性，一丝不苟地计算每一种可能的结果。 | The CEO's hyperrationality led him to ignore the emotional needs of his employees. 首席执行官的超理性让他忽略了员工的情感需求。 |  近义词: overthinking, excessive rationality | 反义词: irrationality, emotionalism | 词性: noun

Etymology:  "Hyper-" (Greek: ὑπέρ, hupér) meaning "over, beyond" + "rationality" (from Latin: ratiō, "reason").
