misreforming:/ˌmɪsrɪˈfɔːrmɪŋ/
verb
错误改革；改革失败
implementing reforms incorrectly or with negative consequences.
The government's misreforming of the healthcare system led to increased costs and reduced access for many citizens. 政府对医疗体系的错误改革导致成本增加，许多公民的医疗机会减少。
The misreforming of the education system has widened the gap between rich and poor.  教育体制的改革失败加剧了贫富差距。
mismanaging, bungling, botching
successful reforming, effective reforming
v.
