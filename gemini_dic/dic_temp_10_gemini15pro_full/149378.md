pacifies:/ˈpæsɪfaɪz/| v. |平息，安抚，抚慰|to bring or restore to a state of peace or tranquility; to calm, soothe, or appease|The mother pacified her crying baby with a lullaby. 妈妈用摇篮曲安抚哭闹的婴儿。|The government tried to pacify the angry protesters. 政府试图安抚愤怒的抗议者。|近义词：calm, soothe, appease, mollify, placate|反义词：agitate, provoke, incite, inflame|词性:动词
词源: From Late Latin *pacificare, from Latin pacificus (“peace-making”), from pax (“peace”) + facere (“to make, do”).
