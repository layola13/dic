nugae:/ˈnjuːɡiː/
n.
琐事；无聊的事；无价值的东西
Trifles; unimportant things; things of no value.
His promises proved to be mere nugae. 他的承诺被证明只是空话。
Synonyms: trifles, frivolities, trivialities
Antonyms: essentials, necessities, important matters
noun


Etymology->
From Latin *nugae*, plural of *nuga* ("a trifle, jest, joke, witty saying").
