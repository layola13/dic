non-offer:/ˌnɒnˈɒfə(r)/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈɔːfər/| n.|非要约，非报价|A statement or action that resembles an offer but does not meet the legal requirements to constitute a binding offer.|The seller's statement about the price was considered a non-offer because it lacked specific terms and conditions.|近义词: invitation to treat, preliminary negotiation|反义词: offer, bid|词性:名词

non-offer: /ˌnɒnˈɒfə(r)/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈɔːfər/| adj.|非要约的|Relating to something that is not a formal offer.|The non-offer statement made it clear that the seller was not yet ready to commit to a deal. |近义词: non-binding, tentative|反义词: firm, binding|词性:形容词
