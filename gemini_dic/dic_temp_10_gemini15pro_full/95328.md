incendious:/ɪnˈsɛndiəriəs/ | adj. | 煽动性的；纵火的；引起愤怒的 | (of a speech or action) designed to cause fires or violence; tending to inflame; inflammatory |  The politician delivered an incendiary speech that roused the crowd to riot.  这位政治家发表了一场煽动性的演讲，煽动了人群暴乱。 | synonyms: inflammatory, provocative, rabble-rousing, seditious, subversive | antonyms: calming, conciliatory, peaceful, soothing | adjective

incendiary:/ɪnˈsɛndiəri/ | n. | 燃烧弹；纵火犯；煽动者 | a substance or weapon (such as a bomb) used to start fires; a person who starts fires deliberately; a person who stirs up trouble or revolt | The police arrested the incendiary who had set fire to the building. 警察逮捕了放火烧毁大楼的纵火犯。 | synonyms: arsonist, firebomber, agitator, demagogue | antonyms: peacemaker, mediator, conciliator | noun


Etymology:
From Late Latin *incendiarius, from Latin incendere "to set on fire," from in- "in" + candēre "to shine, glow."
