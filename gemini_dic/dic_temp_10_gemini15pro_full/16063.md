bandings:/ˈbændɪŋz/
n.
条带，带状物，箍带; (尤指鸟的)翅带，翼带；分级，分组
A pattern of stripes or bands. The act of putting bands on something. The act of grouping things into categories or classes.
The bird had distinctive white bandings on its wings. 这只鸟的翅膀上有明显的白色条带。
Synonyms: stripes, bands, straps, hoops, groupings, classifications
Antonyms: solid, plain, uniform, whole
noun


bandings:/ˈbændɪŋz/
n.
捆绑，绑扎
The act of tying or fastening something with bands.
The bandings on the package were loose. 包裹上的捆绑物松了。
Synonyms: tying, fastening, binding, securing
Antonyms: loosening, untying, releasing
noun
