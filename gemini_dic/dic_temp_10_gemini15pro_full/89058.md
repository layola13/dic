holometabolic:/ˌhɒləʊmɛtəˈbɒlɪk/ | adj. | 全变态的 | Undergoing complete metamorphosis, i.e., having larval and pupal stages between the egg and adult stages. | Butterflies are holometabolic insects. 蝴蝶是全变态昆虫。| The beetle undergoes holometabolic development. 甲虫经历全变态发育。 |  近义词:  endopterygote | 反义词: hemimetabolic |词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek  ὅλος (hólos, “whole”) + μεταβολή (metabolḗ, “change”).
