nitromethane:/ˌnaɪtroʊˈmeθeɪn/ | n. |硝基甲烷|A colorless, oily, flammable liquid with the chemical formula CH3NO2,  the simplest organic nitro compound. It is used as a solvent, a racing fuel, a stabilizer for explosives, and a chemical intermediate.  It can also be used to extract fats, oils, waxes, and polymers.|Nitromethane is a powerful solvent. 硝基甲烷是一种强力溶剂。|Synonyms: CH3NO2 |Antonyms: N/A |noun
