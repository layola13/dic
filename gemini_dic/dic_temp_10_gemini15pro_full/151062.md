paraphysical:/ˌpærəˈfɪzɪkəl/ | adj. | 超自然的，超物理的 | Relating to phenomena or processes that are beyond the scope of normal physical explanation, but are often claimed to have some physical basis. | Example: He was interested in paraphysical phenomena like telekinesis. 他对心灵遥感等超自然现象很感兴趣。 | Example: The book explored the paraphysical aspects of near-death experiences. 这本书探讨了濒死体验的超物理方面。 | Synonym: supernatural, paranormal, metaphysical | Antonym: physical, natural, material | word property: adjective
