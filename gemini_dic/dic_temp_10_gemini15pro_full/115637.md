marcid:/ˈmɑːrsɪd/ /ˈmɑːrsəd/| adj.|枯萎的，凋谢的，憔悴的|Withered, shriveled, and flaccid, especially as a result of decay or dehydration.  Describes something, typically organic matter like plants or flesh, that has lost its freshness and vitality due to aging, illness, or lack of moisture.|The marcid leaves fell from the dying tree. 枯萎的叶子从垂死的树上落下。|Synonyms: withered, shriveled, wilted, drooping, faded, decayed, stale|Antonyms: fresh, vibrant, healthy, flourishing, turgid|adjective
