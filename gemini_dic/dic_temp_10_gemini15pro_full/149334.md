pacha:/ˈpɑːtʃə/| n.|大地之母; 世界; 宇宙|A Quechua word referring to Mother Earth, or the cosmos, encompassing all of creation.  It represents a worldview that sees the earth as a living being and emphasizes interconnectedness and reciprocity.|例句：The Andean people have a deep respect for Pacha Mama. 安第斯人民对大地之母有着深深的敬意。

例句：The festival celebrates the Pacha and all its creations.  这个节日庆祝宇宙及其所有的创造物。|近义词：Mother Earth, Gaia, cosmos, universe|反义词:|noun
