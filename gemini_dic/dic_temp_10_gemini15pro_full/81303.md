granuliform:/ˈɡræn.jʊ.lɪ.fɔːrm/ | adj. |颗粒状的；粒状的 | Resembling or consisting of granules; granular.  | The soil sample was granuliform, composed of tiny particles. 土壤样本呈颗粒状，由微小的颗粒组成。| The rock had a granuliform texture, indicating its formation from compressed grains. 这块岩石具有颗粒状结构，表明它是从压缩颗粒形成的。 | 近义词: granular, grainy | 反义词: smooth, uniform | 词性: 形容词
Etymology: From Latin *granulum* (small grain) + -form (having the form of).  词源：来自拉丁语 *granulum*（小颗粒）+ -form（具有...的形式）。
