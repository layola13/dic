bony:/ˈboʊni/ (US) /ˈbəʊni/ (UK)| adj.|骨瘦如柴的；多骨的；似骨的|Having bones that stick out; very thin; composed of or resembling bone.|The old woman's hands were bony and gnarled.  老太太的手骨瘦如柴，疙瘩丛生。|Synonyms: gaunt, skeletal, thin, emaciated|Antonyms: plump, fleshy, robust|adjective

bony:/ˈboʊni/ (US) /ˈbəʊni/ (UK)| adj.|多刺的; 多硬骨的|Having many bones, especially fish.|We caught a bony fish that wasn't good to eat. 我们钓到一条多骨的鱼，不好吃。|Synonyms: spiny, thorny|Antonyms: boneless, fleshy|adjective
