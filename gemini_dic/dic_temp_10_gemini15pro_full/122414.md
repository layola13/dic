what:/wɒt/(UK) /wʌt/(US)| pron. det. adv.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information about something.|例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

例句：What a beautiful day! 多么美好的一天！

近义词：which|反义词: N/A|词性:pronoun, determiner, adverb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃什么？
What does she like to do in her free time? = 她空闲时间喜欢做什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在看什么电视节目？
What is he cooking for dinner tonight? = 他今晚晚餐做什么？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned recently? = 你最近学到了什么？
What has she done to help? = 她做了些什么来帮忙？


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading this week? = 他这周一直在读什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 开会的时候她在想什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you eaten before you got sick? = 你生病前吃了什么？
What had they discussed before the decision was made? = 做决定前他们讨论了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I arrived? = 我到之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will happen if we don't finish this project? = 如果我们不完成这个项目会发生什么？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们下次会议会讨论什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by the time we get back? = 我们回来的时候会有什么变化？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? = 到2033年你将已经做这件事十年了？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？
