freewheelers:/ˈfriːwiːlərz/
n.
惯性滑行者；随心所欲的人；自由放任主义者
People who coast along in life or do things without planning or restraint; those who act independently and without restrictions.
The freewheelers in the group just went with the flow and didn't worry about the details.  这群随心所欲的人只是随波逐流，并不担心细节。
Freewheelers often embrace change and are open to new experiences.  自由放任主义者通常乐于接受改变，并对新体验持开放态度。
synonyms: nonconformists, bohemians, mavericks, independents
antonyms: conformists, traditionalists, planners, followers
noun
