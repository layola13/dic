barless:/ˈbɑːrlɪs/
adj.
无栅栏的，无障碍的
Without bars; not confined or obstructed by bars.
The barless enclosure allowed the animals to roam freely.  无栅栏的围栏让动物们可以自由漫步。
The birds flew freely in the barless aviary. 鸟儿们在没有栅栏的鸟舍里自由飞翔。
近义词:open, unbarred, unobstructed
反义词:barred, caged, enclosed
词性:形容词
