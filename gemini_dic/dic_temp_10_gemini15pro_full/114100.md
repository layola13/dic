magniloquence:/mæɡˈnɪləkwəns/| n.|夸夸其谈，浮夸的言辞|The politician's speech was filled with magniloquence, but lacked substance.  这位政客的演讲充满了夸夸其谈，但缺乏实质内容。|Etymology: From Latin *magniloquentia*, from *magniloquus* "speaking grandly," from *magnus* "great" + *loqui* "to speak."|Examples:  The magniloquence of the emperor's pronouncements impressed the crowd.  皇帝的声明夸大其词，给人群留下了深刻的印象。

His magniloquence hid a deep insecurity. 他的夸夸其谈掩盖了他内心深处的不安。|Synonyms: bombast, grandiloquence, rhetoric, turgidity|Antonyms: conciseness, humbleness, modesty, simplicity|Noun
