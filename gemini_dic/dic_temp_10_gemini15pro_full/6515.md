amuse: /əˈmjuːz/ | v. | 逗乐，娱乐，消遣 | To entertain or occupy in a light, playful, or pleasant manner.| The comedian amused the audience with his jokes.  喜剧演员用他的笑话逗乐了观众。 | entertain, divert, please | bore, tire, annoy | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *amuser*, from Old French *esmuir* "to entertain, divert," from *es-* (out) + *muir* "to muse, stare fixedly," from Latin *mirari* "to wonder at."
