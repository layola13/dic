end in smoke:/ɛnd ɪn smoʊk/
verb phrase
以失败告终；化为泡影
To result in failure; to come to nothing.
All his plans ended in smoke. 他所有的计划都以失败告终。
Synonyms: fail, fall through, come to nothing, amount to nothing
Antonyms: succeed, triumph, prevail
