glycide: /ˈɡlaɪsaɪd/| n. |缩水甘油醚，环氧丙烷|An organic compound containing an epoxide group. It is a colorless liquid, slightly soluble in water and miscible with most organic solvents.  It is used in the production of epoxy resins, surfactants, and other chemicals.| Glycide is a key intermediate in the synthesis of various polymers. 缩水甘油醚是合成各种聚合物的关键中间体。|  Glycide is a reactive molecule due to its strained three-membered ring.  由于其张力很大的三元环，缩水甘油醚是一个活性分子。 |synonyms: oxirane, epoxypropane| antonyms: N/A| noun
