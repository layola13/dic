innerness:/ˈɪnərnɪs/
n.
内在性；内在；心灵深处
The quality or state of being inner or internal; a deep subjective or spiritual quality.
She explored the innerness of her emotions through meditation. 她通过冥想探索自己情感的内在。
近义词：interiority, subjectivity, inwardness
反义词：exteriority, superficiality
词性：名词

Etymology:  From "inner" + "-ness". "Inner" comes from Old English "innera" (comparative of "in"), meaning "more inward, internal."  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns from adjectives, meaning "state or quality of being."
