paludicolae:/ˌpæljuːdɪˈkoʊliː/
noun
沼泽动物；沼泽生物
Animals that live in marshes or swamps.
例句：Paludicolae, such as frogs and turtles, thrive in wetland environments.  沼泽生物，例如青蛙和乌龟，在湿地环境中繁衍生息。

例句：The study focused on the diverse paludicolae of the Amazon basin. 这项研究的重点是亚马逊河流域的各种沼泽生物。

近义词：marsh dwellers, swamp creatures, wetland inhabitants
反义词：terrestrial animals, land animals
词性：n. (plural noun)


Etymology:
From Latin *palūs*, *palūdis*  "marsh, swamp" + *-cola* "inhabitant" (from *colere* "to inhabit, cultivate").
