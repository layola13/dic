fuka:/ˈfuːkə/ | n. | 福卡（一种日本潜水服）| A type of Japanese diving suit traditionally used for gathering shellfish, seaweed, and other seafood. | Example: The ama divers wore their fukas before entering the cold water. 海女们在进入冰冷的水中之前穿上了她们的福卡。| Example:  He learned to free dive while wearing a traditional fuka. 他学会了穿着传统的福卡自由潜水。 | 近义词: diving suit, wetsuit | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "street clothes") | 词性:noun
