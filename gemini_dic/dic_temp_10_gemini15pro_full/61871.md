empowering:/ɪmˈpaʊərɪŋ/
| adj. |使人有力量的，赋能的 |Making someone stronger and more confident, especially in controlling their life and claiming their rights. |例句：Education is an empowering experience.  教育是一种赋能的经历。

例句：The workshop was empowering for the women who participated.  这个研讨会让参与的女性感到更有力量。

|近义词：encouraging, inspiring, enabling, strengthening |反义词：disempowering, weakening, debilitating |词性:形容词
