indictably:/ɪnˈdɪktəbli/ | adv. | 可起诉地，可被控告地 | In a manner that makes someone liable to be indicted (formally accused of a serious crime). |  The mayor was indictably negligent in his handling of the crisis. 市长对这场危机的处理方式是可以被控告的疏忽。 |  The evidence suggests he acted indictably. 证据表明他的行为是可以被起诉的。 |  criminally, culpably | irreproachably, blamelessly | adverb
