hercogamous:/ˌhɜːrkəˈɡæməs/
adjective
雌雄蕊异熟的
Having the male and female reproductive organs maturing at different times, thus preventing self-fertilization.
Many orchids are hercogamous, a strategy that promotes outcrossing.  许多兰花是雌雄蕊异熟的，这种策略促进了异花授粉。
近义词:dichogamous
反义词: homogamous
词性: adjective
