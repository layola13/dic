crystallology:/ˌkrɪstəˈlɒlədʒi/ | n. |晶体学| The study of crystals, their structure, and properties. | Crystallography helps us understand the arrangement of atoms in solids. 晶体学帮助我们理解固体中原子的排列。|Crystallography is essential for understanding the properties of materials. 晶体学对于理解材料的性质至关重要。| 近义词: crystal science | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From "crystal" + "-logy" (study of). "Crystal" comes from the Greek "krystallos," meaning "ice" or "rock crystal." "-logy" comes from the Greek "-logia," meaning "study of."
