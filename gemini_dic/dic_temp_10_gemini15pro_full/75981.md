funnellike:/ˈfʌn(ə)lˌlaɪk/| adj.|漏斗状的，像漏斗的|Resembling a funnel in shape; tapering toward one end.|The tornado formed a funnellike cloud in the sky. 龙卷风在天空中形成了一个漏斗状的云。|近义词：funnel-shaped, conical, tapering|反义词：cylindrical, expanding|词性:形容词
词源:  From 'funnel' + '-like'. 'Funnel' comes from the Old French 'funell' and ultimately from the Latin 'infundibulum', meaning "funnel". '-like' is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
