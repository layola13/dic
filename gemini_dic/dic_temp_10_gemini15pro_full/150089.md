Example:  Several species of paludicole frogs were identified in the nature reserve.  在自然保护区中发现了几个沼泽蛙的种类。 | 近义词：marsh-dwelling, swamp-dwelling | 反义词: terrestrial, xerophile | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *palus*, *paludis* "marsh, swamp" + *-cola* "inhabitant".
