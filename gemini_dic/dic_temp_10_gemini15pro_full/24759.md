brooking:/ˈbrʊkɪŋ/| adj.|容忍的，默许的； (尤指) 不容反对或批评的 | brooking no dissent or criticism|  The CEO, brooking no argument, announced the new policy.  首席执行官不容置疑地宣布了新政策。|  tolerant, accepting, permissive | intolerant, unaccepting, resistant | adjective

词源：
From the verb "brook," meaning "to tolerate" or "endure."  "Brook" comes from Old English *brūcan* "to use, enjoy, possess, have the use of, brook, suffer," from Proto-Germanic *brukanan.
