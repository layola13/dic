avowee:/əˌvaʊˈiː/
n.
公开承认者；坦白承认者
One who avows or acknowledges something; one to whom a declaration or avowal is made.
The avowee was granted immunity in exchange for his testimony.  公开承认者因其证词而获得豁免。
The lawyer acted as avowee for his client's statement. 律师作为委托人的陈述的公开承认者。
近义词：declarer, confessor, admitter
反义词：denier, repudiator
词性：名词

词源: From Anglo-French *avoué, past participle of avouer (“to avow”), from Latin advocare (“to call to (oneself), summon”).
