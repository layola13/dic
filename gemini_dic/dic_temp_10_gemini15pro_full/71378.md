flagginess:/ˈflæɡɪnəs/
noun
萎靡不振；衰弱；低迷
The state of being flagging; lack of energy or enthusiasm.
His recent flagginess has concerned his friends. 他最近的萎靡不振让他的朋友们很担心。
近义词: lethargy, listlessness, sluggishness, weakness, fatigue, lassitude, languor, enervation, debility
反义词: energy, vigor, vitality, liveliness
