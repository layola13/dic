impairment's:/ɪmˈpermənts/
n.
损伤，损害，障碍
the state of being damaged, weakened, or less effective than usual
The accident resulted in the impairment of her vision.  这起事故导致她视力受损。
近义词：damage, deterioration, handicap, disability
反义词：improvement, enhancement, recovery
