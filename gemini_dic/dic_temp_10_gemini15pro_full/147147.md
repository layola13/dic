overloosenesses:/ˌoʊvərˈluːsɪnɪsɪz/
n.
过度松弛；过度宽松
The state of being excessively loose.
The overloosenesses of the ropes made the swing unsafe. 绳子过度松弛使得秋千不安全。
looseness, laxity, slackness
tightness, firmness, rigidity
