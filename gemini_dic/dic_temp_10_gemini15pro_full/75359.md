frothiness:/ˈfrɒθɪnɪs/ /ˈfrɑːθɪnɪs/| n. |泡沫状；起泡沫；空洞|The frothiness of the cappuccino was perfect. 卡布奇诺的泡沫非常完美。|例句：The frothiness of the beer made it look appealing. 啤酒的泡沫使它看起来很诱人。|近义词：foaminess, bubbliness, effervescence|反义词：flatness, stillness|词性:名词

词源: From frothy + -ness.  Frothy comes from Middle English frothy, from froth + -y. Froth originates from Old Norse froða.
