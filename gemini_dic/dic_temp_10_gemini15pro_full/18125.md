beholdable:/bɪˈhəʊldəbl/
adjective
可看见的，值得一看的
able to be beheld; fit to be seen
The sunset over the mountains was a truly beholdable sight. 落日余晖下的山脉真是一幅值得一看的景象。
The intricate details of the painting were beholdable with a magnifying glass. 用放大镜可以观察到这幅画作的复杂细节。
Synonyms: visible, observable, noticeable, discernible, perceptible
Antonyms: invisible, unobservable, imperceptible, hidden
词性: 形容词
