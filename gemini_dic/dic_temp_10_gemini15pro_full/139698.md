oceanically:/ˌoʊʃiˈænɪkli/| adv.|海洋地，大洋般地|In a manner relating to or resembling the ocean; vastly, immensely.|例句：The sound resonated oceanically through the hall. 声音在大厅里回荡，如同海洋般浩瀚。|近义词：vastly, immensely, hugely, enormously|反义词: narrowly, slightly, minimally|词性:副词

Etymology:  From "oceanic" + "-ally". "Oceanic" comes from French "océanique" and ultimately from Latin "oceanus", meaning "ocean".  The suffix "-ally" transforms adjectives into adverbs.
