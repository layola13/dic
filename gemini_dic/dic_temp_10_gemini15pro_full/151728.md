parumbilical: /ˌpærʌmˈbɪlɪkəl/ | adj. | 脐周的，靠近脐的 | Situated near the umbilicus (navel). |  The patient presented with parumbilical pain and tenderness.  病人表现出脐周疼痛和触痛。|  The rash was concentrated in the parumbilical region. 皮疹集中在脐周区域。 | 近义词: periumbilical | 反义词: 无 | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:

From Latin *para- (“beside, near, alongside") + umbilical, ultimately from Latin *umbilicus* (“navel").
