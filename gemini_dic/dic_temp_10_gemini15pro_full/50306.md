depleters:/dɪˈpliːtərz/
n.
消耗者；损耗者；使减少者
Those who or that which deplete something.  Something that reduces the amount of something else.
Ozone depleters are harmful to the environment. 臭氧消耗物质对环境有害。
The increasing demand for resources makes humans resource depleters. 对资源日益增长的需求使人类成为资源消耗者。
synonyms: reducers, consumers, users, exhausters
antonyms: replenisher, restorer, enhancer
noun
