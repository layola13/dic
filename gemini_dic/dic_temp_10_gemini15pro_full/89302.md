homoeostasis:/ˌhəʊmiəˈsteɪsɪs/, /ˌhoʊmiəˈsteɪsɪs/ | n. |体内平衡，恒定性 | The tendency of a system, especially the physiological system of higher animals, to maintain internal stability, owing to the coordinated response of its parts to any situation or stimulus that would tend to disturb its normal condition or function. |  The human body maintains homoeostasis through a complex system of feedback mechanisms. 人体通过复杂的反馈机制维持体内平衡。
