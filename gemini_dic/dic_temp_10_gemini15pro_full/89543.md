honorarium:/ˌɒnəˈreəriəm/ /ˌɑːnəˈreriəm/ | n. |酬金，谢礼|A payment given for professional services that are rendered nominally without charge.|Example: The speaker received a small honorarium for her presentation. 演讲者收到了一笔小小的酬金作为她演讲的报酬。

Example:  He was given an honorarium for his work on the committee. 他因在委员会的工作而获得了一笔酬金。|近义词：fee, payment, stipend, compensation|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 debt 债务)|词性: noun
