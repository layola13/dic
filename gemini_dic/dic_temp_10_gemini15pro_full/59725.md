eclipsing:/ɪˈklɪpsɪŋ/
adjective|蚀的，遮蔽的；胜过，使黯然失色|surpassing or obscuring by excellence or fame; exceeding|例句：Her talent eclipsing all others. 她的才华胜过所有人。|近义词：overshadowing, surpassing, outshining, exceeding|反义词：inferior, subordinate|词性: adjective
词源: From eclipse (n.) + -ing. Ultimately from Latin eclipsis, from Greek ekleipsis "abandonment, a failing, eclipse," from ekleipein "to forsake, leave out, fail to appear," from ek "out" + leipein "to leave."
