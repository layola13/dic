cetacea:/sɪˈteɪʃə/ /sɪˈteɪʃɪə/
n.
鲸目动物
An order of aquatic mammals that includes whales, dolphins, and porpoises. They are characterized by their streamlined bodies, horizontal tail flukes, and blowholes for breathing.
例句：Cetacea are highly intelligent marine mammals. 鲸目动物是高度智能的海洋哺乳动物。

例句：The study of cetacea is called cetology. 对鲸类的研究被称为鲸类学。

近义词：whales, dolphins, porpoises (这些更具体，而不是同义词)
反义词: 无 (cetacea是一个生物分类，没有直接的反义词)
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cētus* "whale, sea monster," from Greek *kētos* "sea monster, whale."
