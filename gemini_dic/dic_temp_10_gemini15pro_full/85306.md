harnesslike:/ˈhɑːrnɪsˌlaɪk/| adj.|像马具的，类似挽具的|Resembling or suggestive of a harness, a piece of equipment used to control or restrain a horse or other animal.|The dog wore a harnesslike contraption around its chest. 这只狗的胸部戴着一个类似马具的装置。|The climber used a harnesslike system of ropes for safety. 攀登者使用了一个类似马具的绳索系统来确保安全。|近义词: harness-shaped, straplike|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider loose, unrestrained)|词性: 形容词

词源:
harness (n.) + -like (suffix)

"harness" from Old French "harneis" (equipment, baggage, horse's armor), from Old Norse "hernest" (warlike equipment).  "-like" is a common suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
