grit:/ɡrɪt/| n. |勇气，毅力，决心；沙砾，粗砂|firmness of character; indomitable spirit; small, loose particles of stone or sand|She showed incredible grit in overcoming her injury and returning to the competition. 她克服伤病重返赛场的勇气令人难以置信。|近义词：courage, determination, resolve, perseverance, sand, gravel|反义词：cowardice, weakness, fear|noun

grit:/ɡrɪt/| v. (gritted, gritting) |咬牙切齿；在…上撒沙砾|to clench one's teeth; to spread grit on something|He gritted his teeth and pushed through the pain. 他咬紧牙关，克服了疼痛。|近义词：clench, grind|反义词：relax|verb
