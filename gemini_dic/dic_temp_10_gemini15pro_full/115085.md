manful:/ˈmænfl/| adj.|男子气概的，勇敢的，果断的|Having qualities traditionally associated with men, such as courage, strength, and resolution.|He made a manful attempt to rescue the drowning child. 他勇敢地试图营救溺水的孩子。|近义词：brave, courageous, bold, valiant, resolute|反义词：cowardly, timid, fearful|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *manful, equivalent to man +‎ -ful.
