altruistic:/ˌæltruˈɪstɪk/
adjective
利他的；无私的
Showing a disinterested and selfless concern for the well-being of others; unselfish.
She displayed an altruistic concern for the welfare of the refugees. 她对难民的福祉表现出了无私的关怀。

Mother Teresa was known for her altruistic acts. 特蕾莎修女以其无私的行为而闻名。

synonym: selfless, benevolent, charitable, philanthropic, humanitarian
antonym: selfish, egoistic, self-centered, narcissistic
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *altruiste*, from Italian *altrui* "of others, to others," from Latin *alteri*, dative singular of *alter* "other."  Coined by French philosopher Auguste Comte.
