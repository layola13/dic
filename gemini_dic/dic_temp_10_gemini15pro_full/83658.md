haemosiderin:/ˌhiːməˈsɪdərɪn/| n. |含铁血黄素|An iron-containing pigment derived from hemoglobin, found in tissues, especially in cases of iron overload.|Example 1: The biopsy revealed deposits of haemosiderin in the liver, suggesting iron overload.  活检显示肝脏中存在含铁血黄素沉积，提示铁过载。

Example 2: Haemosiderin staining was observed in the lung tissue. 在肺组织中观察到含铁血黄素染色。|近义词: None (It's a specific biochemical term)|反义词: None|词性:noun

haemoglobin:/ˌhiːməˈɡloʊbɪn/| n. |血红蛋白|A red protein responsible for transporting oxygen in the blood of vertebrates.|Example 1: Hemoglobin carries oxygen from the lungs to the rest of the body. 血红蛋白将氧气从肺部输送到身体其他部位。
