impartialist:/ɪmˈpɑːrʃəlɪst/
n.
不偏不倚的人；公正的人
a person who is impartial
例句：The impartialist mediated the dispute between the two parties.  这位不偏不倚的人调解了双方之间的争端。

例句：She prides herself on being an impartialist in all matters.  她为自己能在所有事情上都保持公正而自豪。

近义词：neutral, unbiased, objective, fair-minded
反义词：partisan, biased, prejudiced, subjective
词性：名词
