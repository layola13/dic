cuculoid:/ˈkjuːkjʊlɔɪd/| adj.|杜鹃类的，属于杜鹃科的|Relating to or resembling the cuckoos; belonging to the Cuculidae family.|The cuculoid bird laid its eggs in another bird's nest.  这种杜鹃类的鸟把蛋下在别的鸟巢里。|The museum's ornithology exhibit showcased a variety of cuculoid species.  博物馆的鸟类学展览展示了各种杜鹃科的物种。|近义词: cuckoo-like|反义词: (No specific antonym, perhaps non-cuculoid)|词性:adjective
