nonincrimination:/ˌnɒnɪnˌkrɪmɪˈneɪʃn/ | n. | 免罪，不控告，不招供 |The act of not incriminating or the state of not being incriminated.  The protection against self-incrimination is a fundamental right. | The defendant invoked his right of nonincrimination. 被告援引了他不被控告的权利。| Synonyms: immunity, exoneration, vindication | Antonyms: accusation, indictment, incrimination | noun
