nazifications:/ˌnɑːtsɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/
n.
纳粹化（复数）
The process of imposing Nazi ideology and policies.
The historian documented the nazifications of various institutions during the Third Reich.  历史学家记录了第三帝国时期各个机构的纳粹化过程。
The nazifications of education and media were crucial for the regime's control. 教育和媒体的纳粹化对于政权的控制至关重要。
Synonym:  fascistization
Antonym:  democratization
