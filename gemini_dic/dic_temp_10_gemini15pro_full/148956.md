ovicular: /oʊˈvɪkjʊlər/
adjective
卵形的；卵状的
Shaped like an egg; ovate.
例句：The bird laid an ovicular egg.  这只鸟产下了一个卵形的蛋。

例句：The bacteria had an ovicular shape when viewed under the microscope. 在显微镜下观察，这种细菌呈卵形。


近义词：ovate, egg-shaped, oval
反义词： (No direct antonym, but consider shapes like) elongated, linear, rectangular
词性：adjective

词源：From Latin *oviculus, diminutive of ovum (“egg”)
