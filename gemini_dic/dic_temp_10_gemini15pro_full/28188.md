capreolate: /ˈkæpriəˌleɪt/ /ˈkæpriəˌlət/ | adj. | 卷须状的，有卷须的 | Having tendrils; resembling a tendril. |  The plant has a capreolate stem, allowing it to climb upwards.  这种植物的茎有卷须，可以让它向上攀爬。| synonyms: tendriled, cirrose, climbing | antonyms:  non-climbing, acaulescent | adjective
