GDPP:/ˌdʒiː diː piː piː/ | n. | 人均国内生产总值 (Gross Domestic Product per person) |  GDP per capita is a measure of a country's economic output per person. It is calculated by dividing the GDP of a country by its total population. | The GDPP of the United States is significantly higher than that of many developing countries. 美国的 人均国内生产总值 比许多发展中国家高得多。

The country's GDPP has been steadily increasing over the past decade.  这个国家的人均国内生产总值在过去十年里一直在稳步增长。 | 近义词: GDP per capita, income per capita | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
