hidey-hole's:/ˈhaɪdiˌhoʊlz/ | n. | 藏身之处; 秘密地点; 避难所 | A hidey-hole is a small, secret place where someone or something can be hidden.  It's the possessive form of "hidey-hole," indicating ownership or belonging. | The squirrel stashed its nuts in its hidey-hole's depths. 松鼠把坚果藏在它藏身处的深处。 |  The pirate's hidey-hole's location was a closely guarded secret.  海盗藏身处的位置是一个严守的秘密。| synonyms: hiding place, secret place, refuge, den, sanctuary | antonyms: exposed location, public place | noun
