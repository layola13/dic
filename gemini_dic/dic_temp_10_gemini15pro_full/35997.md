codevelops:/ˌkoʊdɪˈvɛləps/| v. |共同开发，合作开发|develops something jointly with another or others. |例句：The two companies codeveloped the new software.  两家公司共同开发了这款新软件。

例句：They codeveloped a new approach to teaching math. 他们共同开发了一种新的数学教学方法。|近义词：collaborate on, jointly develop, co-create|反义词：develop independently, develop alone|词性:动词
