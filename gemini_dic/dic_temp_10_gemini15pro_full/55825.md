divided:/dɪˈvaɪdɪd/
adjective
分裂的，有分歧的
separated into parts or groups, or having opinions that differ
The country was deeply divided on the issue of war.  这个国家在战争问题上存在严重分歧。
近义词:split, separated, fractured
反义词:united, unified, integrated
词性:adjective


divided:/dɪˈvaɪdɪd/
verb (past tense)
分，分开；分配；除
separated or shared something between several people or things; performed a mathematical calculation to find out how many times one number goes into another number
We divided the cake into six equal pieces. 我们把蛋糕分成六等份。
近义词:split, separate, distribute, share
反义词:unite, combine, join
词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin dividere "to force apart, distribute, separate," from dis- "apart" + -videre "to separate" (related to videre "to see").
