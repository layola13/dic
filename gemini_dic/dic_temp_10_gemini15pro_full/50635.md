deriders:/dɪˈraɪdərz/
|n.|嘲笑者，嘲弄者|People who mock or ridicule someone or something.  Those who express contempt or scorn through laughter or mockery.|The deriders laughed at his clumsy attempt to dance. 嘲笑者嘲笑他笨拙的舞姿。|synonyms: mockers, scoffers, jeerers|antonyms: admirers, praisers, supporters|noun
