outswum:/ˌaʊtˈswʌm/
verb
(out)游得比…远/快，(out)游泳胜过
Past participle of outswim. Having swum faster or further than.
例句：He outswum his competitors in the final race. 他在决赛中游得比他的对手们都快。
例句：She had outswum everyone in her age group.  她在她年龄组中游得比所有人都快。
近义词：outpaced, surpassed, defeated
反义词：lost to, beaten by
词性：v.
