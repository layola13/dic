dalit:/ˈdɑːlɪt/ | n. |  贱民，达利特 | A member of the lowest caste in India, formerly called untouchables.  They were traditionally considered impure and subjected to severe social discrimination. |  例句：The dalits have historically faced discrimination and oppression.  贱民在历史上一直面临歧视和压迫。

例句： Many dalits are still struggling for basic human rights. 许多贱民仍在为基本人权而斗争。 | 近义词：untouchable, outcaste | 反义词：Brahmin (highest caste) | 词性: noun
