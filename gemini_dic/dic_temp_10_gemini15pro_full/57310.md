downswing:/ˈdaʊnswɪŋ/ | n. | 下降; 下滑; 低迷时期 |A downward swing or movement; a period of decline or downturn. | The economy is in a downswing. 经济正处于下滑时期。| The golfer's downswing was powerful and precise.  高尔夫球手向下挥杆的动作强劲而精准。 | 近义词：decline, downturn, slump, recession | 反义词: upswing, upturn, recovery, boom | 词性: 名词

downswing:/ˈdaʊnswɪŋ/ | n. | (高尔夫球)下挥 | The downward swing of a golf club before hitting the ball. |  He perfected his downswing over years of practice. 他经过多年的练习完善了他的下挥动作。|  Her downswing was too fast, causing her to slice the ball. 她的下挥太快了，导致她把球打偏了。 | 近义词：backswing (反义词，但描述挥杆的另一部分) | 反义词: backswing | 词性: 名词
