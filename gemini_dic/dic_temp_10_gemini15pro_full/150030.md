palpates:/ˈpælpeɪts/ | v. | 触诊，触摸 |examines (a part of the body) by touch, especially for medical purposes. | The doctor palpates the patient's abdomen. 医生触诊了病人的腹部。|  The nurse gently palpated the lump on my neck. 护士轻轻地触诊了我脖子上的肿块。 |  examine, feel, touch, probe |  ignore, neglect | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *palpāre, present active infinitive of palpo (“I stroke, touch softly”).
