naivest:/naɪˈiːvɪst/
adjective
最天真的，最幼稚的
Showing a lack of experience, wisdom, or judgment.  Superlative form of "naive."
She was the naivest girl in the class, believing everything she was told. 她班上最天真的女孩，别人说什么都信。
Synonyms: innocent, guileless, ingenuous, artless, unsophisticated
Antonyms: sophisticated, worldly, experienced, cynical, shrewd
词性: 形容词
