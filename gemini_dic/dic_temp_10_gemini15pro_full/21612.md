blunter:/ˈblʌntər/| adj.|更钝的，更直率的|Comparative form of blunt: more blunt.  More lacking in sharpness; more direct and outspoken to the point of rudeness.|The knife was blunter than I expected, making it difficult to chop the vegetables. 这把刀比我想象的要钝，切菜很困难。|She gave a blunter assessment of the situation than her colleagues, not mincing her words. 她对局势的评估比她的同事更直率，没有拐弯抹角。|近义词：duller, less sharp, more direct, franker|反义词:sharper, more subtle, more diplomatic|词性:形容词
blunt:/blʌnt/| adj. v.|钝的，直率的；使变钝|Lacking sharpness; direct and outspoken to the point of rudeness; to make or become less sharp.|The blunt knife couldn't cut the rope. 钝刀子割不断绳子。  He was blunt about her poor performance. 他对她的糟糕表现直言不讳。 The fall blunted the tip of the sword. 跌落使剑尖变钝了。|近义词：dull, outspoken, frank|反义词:sharp, subtle, diplomatic|词性:形容词, 动词
词源:From Old English *blunt 'dull, obtuse,' of unknown origin.
