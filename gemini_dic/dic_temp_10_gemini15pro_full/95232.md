inburst:/ˈɪnbɜːrst/| n.|爆发，突然迸发|A sudden bursting in or into something; a sudden eruption or intensification.|The inburst of sunlight through the clouds was dazzling. 阳光穿过云层突然迸发出来，令人目眩。|近义词：outburst, eruption, burst, explosion|反义词: abatement, decline, decrease|词性:名词

词源:  "in-" (prefix meaning "in, into, within") + "burst" (noun meaning "a sudden eruption or outbreak")
