deglaze:/diːˈɡleɪz/| v. |煺锅，溶解锅底结块|To loosen and dissolve browned food residue from a pan by adding liquid, often wine or stock, and heating while stirring.  This creates a flavorful sauce.|After searing the steak, I deglazed the pan with red wine.  煎完牛排后，我用红酒煺了锅。|synonyms: deglaze does not have a true synonym but the process contributes to making a pan sauce. Similar cooking techniques include using fond or creating a reduction. |antonyms: N/A|verb

deglazing:/diːˈɡleɪzɪŋ/| n. |煺锅，溶解锅底结块（的动作）|The process of deglazing a pan.|Deglazing is a crucial step in making a pan sauce. 煺锅是制作锅底酱汁的关键步骤。|synonyms:  N/A|antonyms: N/A|noun
