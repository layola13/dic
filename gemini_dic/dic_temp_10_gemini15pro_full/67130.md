exportable:/ɪkˈspɔːrtəbl/ /ɪkˈspɔːrtəbəl/| adj.|可出口的；适合出口的|Suitable or able to be exported.|例句：The country's exportable surplus of grain has increased this year.  今年该国可出口的谷物盈余增加了。

例句：Software is an easily exportable commodity. 软件是一种易于出口的商品。

近义词：tradeable, marketable, shippable|反义词：unexportable, non-exportable|词性:形容词

export:/ˈekspɔːrt/ /ˈekspɔːrt/ |n. /vt.|出口；出口商品；输出|To send (goods or services) to another country for sale.|例句：The company exports coffee to Europe. 这家公司向欧洲出口咖啡。

例句：The export of live animals is strictly controlled.  活体动物的出口受到严格控制。

近义词：sell abroad, ship overseas|反义词：import|词性:名词/动词
