deceptively:/dɪˈsɛptɪvli/| adv.|欺骗性地，貌似…地|appearing to be one thing when it is really something else|The cake looked deceptively simple to make, but it actually required several complicated steps.  这个蛋糕看起来做起来很简单，但实际上需要几个复杂的步骤。|The climb looked deceptively easy, but it was actually quite challenging. 这段攀登看起来很容易，但实际上相当有挑战性。|近义词: misleadingly, falsely, deceptively|反义词: genuinely, truly, honestly|词性:副词
词源: From deceive (v.) + -ive (suffix forming adjectives) + -ly (suffix forming adverbs).  Ultimately from Latin "decipere" (to ensnare, beguile).
