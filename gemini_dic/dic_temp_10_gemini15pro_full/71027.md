firmly:/ˈfɜːrmli/ (US) /ˈfɜːmli/ (UK)| adv.|坚定地，牢固地|In a strong, steady, and resolute manner.  Holding something securely or adhering tightly.|She held onto the rope firmly. 她紧紧地抓住绳子。|近义词：strongly, securely, tightly, steadfastly|反义词：loosely, weakly, insecurely|词性:副词
词源: From Middle English *fermeliche, from Old English *feormelice, from feorm ("provision, sustenance, food, feast, refuge, shelter, protection") + -lice (suffix forming adverbs). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *firmiz ("firm, strong").
