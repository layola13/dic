derogation:/ˌdɛrəˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 减损， derogations from a rule |  The act of detracting or taking away from; lessening;  a partial repeal or abolishment of a law. |  The government granted a derogation from the environmental regulations for the construction project. 政府批准了该建设项目的环境法规减损。| synonyms: exemption, exception, allowance, dispensation | antonyms: enforcement, upholding, adherence | noun

derogation:/ˌdɛrəˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 贬低，  derogations of someone's character | The act of belittling or disparaging;  a derogatory statement. | He made several derogations about her intelligence. 他贬低了她的智力。 | synonyms: disparagement, detraction, belittling, depreciation | antonyms: praise, commendation, exaltation | noun
