forfeitable:/ˈfɔːrfɪtəbl/
adjective
可丧失的；可被没收的
Subject to being forfeited; capable of being forfeited.
The bonus is forfeitable if the employee leaves the company within a year.  如果员工在一年内离开公司，奖金将被没收。
His deposit will be forfeitable if he fails to complete the purchase. 如果他未能完成购买，他的定金将被没收。
近义词:losable, confiscable
反义词:nonforfeitable, unlosable
词性:adjective

Etymology: From forfeit + -able.  Forfeit comes from Old French *forfait, past participle of forfaire "to transgress, do amiss," from for- "outside, beyond" + faire "to do."
