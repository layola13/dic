mary:/ˈmɛəri/ (US), /ˈmɛːri/ (UK)| n.|玛丽|A female given name, derived from Maria, the Latin form of the Greek Μαρία (María), which in turn is thought to be derived from the Hebrew מִרְיָם (Miryam), meaning "bitter," "rebellious," or possibly "wished-for child."  It is often associated with the Virgin Mary in Christian tradition.|Mary had a little lamb. 玛丽有一只小羊羔。|近义词: Maria (related, not synonymous)|反义词: N/A|noun
