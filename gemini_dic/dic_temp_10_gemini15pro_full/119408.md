microfiching: /ˈmaɪkroʊˌfɪʃɪŋ/
|n.|缩微胶卷摄影；缩微胶卷制作|The process of photographing documents and storing them on microfiche.|Libraries often use microfiching to preserve old newspapers. 图书馆经常使用缩微胶卷技术来保存旧报纸。|microfilming| - |noun|
Etymology: micro- (small) + fiche (French, card index) + -ing (present participle suffix)
