incommunicado:/ˌɪnkəˌmjuːnɪˈkɑːdoʊ/ (US), /ˌɪnkəˌmjuːnɪˈkɑːdəʊ/ (UK)| adj. adv.|无法沟通的，单独监禁的|Unable to communicate with others, especially because of being imprisoned or held captive.|例句：He was held incommunicado for weeks. 他被单独监禁了几个星期。

例句：The hostages were kept incommunicado. 人质被隔离，无法与外界联系。

近义词：isolated, inaccessible, unapproachable|反义词:accessible, reachable, communicative|词性:形容词, 副词
