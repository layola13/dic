glaring:/ˈɡlerɪŋ/ | adj. | 刺眼的，明显的，怒目而视的 | shining intensely and blindingly; very conspicuous; staring fiercely and angrily | The glaring sun made it difficult to see. 刺眼的阳光使人难以看清。| synonyms: dazzling, obvious, blatant, staring | antonyms: dim, subtle, inconspicuous | adjective

glare:/ɡler/ | n. | 刺眼的光，怒视 | a harsh, bright light; a fierce, angry stare | The glare of the headlights blinded me.  车头灯的强光使我眼花缭乱。| synonyms: blaze, dazzle, scowl | antonyms: shade, gloom | noun

glare:/ɡler/ | v. | 发出刺眼的光，怒视 | to shine with a harsh, bright light; to stare fiercely and angrily | He glared at me across the table. 他隔着桌子怒视着我。| synonyms: blaze, dazzle, scowl, frown | antonyms: soften, smile | verb
