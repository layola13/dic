outblessed:/ˌaʊtˈblɛst/
verb
超越祝福; 比...更受祝福
to have received more blessings or divine favor than someone else.
He outblessed his brother by inheriting the entire estate. 他比他兄弟更受祝福，继承了全部遗产。
Synonyms: surpassed in blessings, more fortunate, more favored
Antonyms: cursed, less fortunate
词性: 动词
