nonirritating:/ˌnɑːnˈɪrɪteɪtɪŋ/
adjective
不刺激的，无刺激性的
Not causing irritation; gentle to the skin or other sensitive areas.
This lotion is nonirritating and suitable for sensitive skin.  这款乳液不刺激，适合敏感肌肤。
The doctor recommended a nonirritating shampoo for her dry scalp. 医生推荐她使用一款不刺激的头皮干燥的洗发水。

近义词:soothing, mild, gentle, non-abrasive
反义词:irritating, harsh, abrasive
词性:adjective

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + irritating (from irritate, from Latin irritare "to excite, provoke," from irritus "undecided, invalid")
