hexahedrical:/ˌhɛksəˈhiːdrɪkəl/
adjective
六面的；立方形的
Having six sides or faces; cubic.
A cube is a hexahedrical shape. 一个立方体是一个六面体形状。
Crystals of common salt have a hexahedrical form. 食盐晶体呈六面体形状。

近义词:cubic, six-sided
反义词:amorphous, irregular
词性:adjective

词源: From Greek *hexa- "six" + *hedra "seat, base, face"  + -ical (suffix forming adjectives).
