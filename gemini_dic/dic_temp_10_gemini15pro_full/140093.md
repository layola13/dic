off-duty:/ˌɒf ˈdjuːti/ (US) /ˌɒf ˈdʒuːti/ (UK)| adj.|下班的，非值班的|Not working; not on duty.  Relating to the time when someone is not working.|The off-duty police officer saw the robbery.  下班的警察看到了抢劫案。|Synonyms: off work, out of uniform, free|Antonyms: on duty, working, on the job|adjective

off-duty:/ˌɒf ˈdjuːti/ (US) /ˌɒf ˈdʒuːti/ (UK)| n.|下班时间|The time when someone is not working.|He enjoys his off-duty by playing golf. 他喜欢在下班时间打高尔夫球。|Synonyms: free time, leisure time|Antonyms: working hours, on-duty time|noun
