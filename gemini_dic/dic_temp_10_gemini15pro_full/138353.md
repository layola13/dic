notices:/ˈnoʊtɪsɪz/ | n. |通知，公告；注意，察觉|A formal or informal announcement or warning; the act of observing or paying attention to something.|The company posted notices about the upcoming layoffs. 公司发布了即将裁员的通知。| synonyms: announcements, warnings, bulletins, observations, perceptions | antonyms: ignorance, disregard | noun

notices:/ˈnoʊtɪsɪz/ | v. |注意到，察觉到|(third person singular simple present notices, present participle noticing, simple past and past participle noticed) Become aware of; observe.|He noticed a strange smell in the room. 他注意到房间里有一股奇怪的味道。| synonyms: observe, perceive, detect, spot, remark | antonyms: overlook, miss, ignore | verb
