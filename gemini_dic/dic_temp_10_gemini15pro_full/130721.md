nonallergic:/ˌnɒnəˈləːdʒɪk/ /ˌnɑːnəˈlɜːrdʒɪk/
adjective
非过敏性的
Not causing or likely to cause an allergic reaction.
例句：This detergent is nonallergic and suitable for sensitive skin.  这种洗涤剂是非过敏性的，适合敏感肌肤。

例句：She switched to a nonallergic formula for her baby. 她给宝宝换成了非过敏性配方奶粉。

近义词：hypoallergenic
反义词：allergic
词性: adjective
