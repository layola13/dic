lodge:/lɒdʒ/ (UK) /lɑːdʒ/ (US)| n. vi. vt.|小屋，旅馆；存放；提出(正式);居住，寄宿|A small house in a park or forest, often used by people hunting or fishing.  A place providing temporary accommodation.|例句：We stayed in a hunting lodge in the Scottish Highlands. 我们在苏格兰高地的一间狩猎小屋里住了一晚。|近义词：cabin, cottage, inn, hotel, file, submit|反义词: retrieve, withdraw|词性:noun, verb

lodge:/lɒdʒ/ (UK) /lɑːdʒ/ (US)| vi. vt.|居住，寄宿；存放；提出(正式)|To stay temporarily in a place. To put or deposit something somewhere for safekeeping. To make a formal request or complaint.|例句：He lodged a complaint with the manager. 他向经理提出了投诉。|近义词：stay, reside, deposit, file, submit|反义词: retrieve, withdraw|词性:verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *loge (modern French loge), from Frankish *laubja "shelter made of foliage," from Proto-Germanic *laubijō "shelter of leaves," from *laubiz "leaf."
