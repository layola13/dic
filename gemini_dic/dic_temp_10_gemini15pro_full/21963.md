bogtrotting:/ˈbɒɡˌtrɒtɪŋ/
adjective
沼泽跋涉的；在沼泽地行走的
Traversing or frequenting bogs.  Characterized by traversing or frequenting bogs.
He enjoys bogtrotting in search of rare orchids. 他喜欢在沼泽地跋涉寻找稀有的兰花。
近义词：marsh-treading, swamp-walking
反义词：mountain-climbing, desert-trekking
词性：adjective

bogtrotting:/ˈbɒɡˌtrɒtɪŋ/
noun
沼泽跋涉；在沼泽地行走
The act of traversing or frequenting bogs.
Bogtrotting is his favorite pastime. 在沼泽地跋涉是他最喜欢的消遣。
近义词：marsh-treading, swamp-walking
反义词：mountain-climbing, desert-trekking
词性：noun


词源：
"bog" + "trotting".  "Bog" refers to a wet, spongy ground, typically containing decaying plant matter. "Trotting" refers to a gait faster than walking but slower than running.  The word "bogtrotting" combines these two elements to describe the act of traversing such terrain.
