outrating:/ˌaʊtˈreɪtɪŋ/
verb
超过；胜过
Surpassing or exceeding in rating or ranking.
This new phone is outrating all others in customer satisfaction. 这款新手机在客户满意度方面超过了所有其他手机。
The challenger is outrating the incumbent in recent polls.  在最近的民意调查中，挑战者胜过现任者。
Synonyms: surpassing, exceeding, outperforming, besting, topping
Antonyms: underperforming, lagging behind, trailing
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + rating,  where "out-" acts as a prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing.  The word "rating" itself comes from the verb "rate," ultimately from Old French "rater" meaning "to assess" or "to estimate."
