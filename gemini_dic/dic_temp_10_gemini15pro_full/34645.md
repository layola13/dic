clenching:/ˈklɛntʃɪŋ/| n. v.|紧握，咬紧，攥紧|The act of gripping or holding something tightly, often with the hand or teeth. It can also refer to the tightening of muscles, particularly in the face or jaw, often due to stress, anger, or pain.|He was clenching his fists in anger. 他愤怒地握紧了拳头。|近义词：gripping, grasping, clutching|反义词:releasing, loosening|词性:noun, present participle of clench
