embayment:/ɪmˈbeɪmənt/| n. | 海湾，深入陆地或海岸线的水域|A portion of a larger body of water that extends into the land, such as a bay or cove.  An area of coast where the land curves inwards.|The boats sheltered in the embayment during the storm. 船只在风暴期间躲避在海湾里。|近义词：bay, cove, gulf, inlet, bight|反义词：headland, promontory|noun

词源:  Late 16th century: from French embaiement, from embaier ‘to enclose in a bay,’ from baie ‘bay.’
