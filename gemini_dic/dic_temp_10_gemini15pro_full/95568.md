incoherentness:/ˌɪnkəʊˈhɪərəntnəs/ /ˌɪnkoʊˈhɪrəntnəs/| n. |不连贯，语无伦次|The state of being incoherent; lack of clarity, connection, or logical order in thoughts, speech, or writing.  |His speech was marked by its incoherence, rambling from topic to topic without any clear connection. 他的演讲以语无伦次为特征，漫无边际地从一个话题跳到另一个话题，没有任何清晰的联系。

The incoherence of her writing made it difficult to understand her point. 她写作的不连贯性让人很难理解她的观点。|近义词：disjointedness, rambling, incoherency, confusion, illogicality|反义词：coherence, clarity, lucidity, order, logic|词性: 名词
