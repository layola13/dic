groundly:/ˈɡraʊndɪdli/| adv.|扎实地，有根据地，彻底地|Based on sound reasoning or evidence; thoroughly.|He argued his case groundedly, citing numerous precedents.|近义词：firmly, solidly, thoroughly, substantially, completely|反义词：superficially, flimsily, weakly, shakily|词性:副词

词源: From "ground" (meaning foundation) + "-ed" (past participle suffix) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *grunduz.
