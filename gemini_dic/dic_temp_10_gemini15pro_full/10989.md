arbitrary: /ˈɑːrbɪtrɛri/ (US), /ˈɑːbɪtrəri/ (UK)| adj.|任意的；武断的；专横的|Based on random choice or personal whim, rather than any reason or system.|例句：The choice of restaurant was arbitrary.  餐厅的选择是任意的。

例句：His decision seemed arbitrary and unfair. 他的决定显得武断且不公平。|近义词：random, capricious, whimsical, despotic, dictatorial|反义词：reasoned, logical, rational, objective, fair|词性: 形容词
