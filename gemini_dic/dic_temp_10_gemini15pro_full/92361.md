hypochlorous:/ˌhaɪpəˈklɔːrəs/| adj.|次氯酸的；含次氯的|Relating to or derived from hypochlorous acid (HClO).  Hypochlorous acid is a weak acid that forms when chlorine dissolves in water. It is a powerful oxidizing agent and is used as a disinfectant and bleach.|Hypochlorous acid is a powerful disinfectant. 次氯酸是一种强力消毒剂。

Hypochlorous solutions are commonly used in wound care. 次氯酸溶液常用于伤口护理。|近义词:chlorine-based, chlorinated|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective
