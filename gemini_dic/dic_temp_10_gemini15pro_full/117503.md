meedful:/ˈmiːdfʊl/| adj.|有益的，需要的，应得的|Necessary or deserved.|例句：He received his meedful reward. 他得到了应得的奖励。

例句：Meedful assistance was provided to the victims of the hurricane. 向飓风灾民提供了必要的援助。|近义词：necessary, required, deserved, appropriate, fitting|反义词:unnecessary, undeserved, inappropriate|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *mede-ful*, from Old English *mēd* (“reward, merit; due measure”) + *-ful*. Cognate with Dutch *middel* (“middle, means”), German *mittel* (“middle, means, remedy”), Gothic *mitteln* (“to mediate”).  The sense of "deserved reward" shifted to "necessary" over time.
