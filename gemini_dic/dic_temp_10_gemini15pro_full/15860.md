balletically:/bæˈlɛtɪkli/
adverb
芭蕾舞般地；优雅地
In a manner characteristic of ballet; gracefully.
She moved balletically across the stage. 她像芭蕾舞演员一样优雅地穿过舞台。
Synonyms: gracefully, elegantly, fluidly, lightly
Antonyms: clumsily, awkwardly, stiffly
word type: adverb
