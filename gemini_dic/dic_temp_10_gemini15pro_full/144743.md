over-assertive:/ˌoʊvər əˈsɜːrtɪv/| adj.| 过于自信的；过分武断的；咄咄逼人的| excessively assertive; too confident and aggressive in behaviour or style.  Characterized by excessive self-confidence and a forceful or aggressive manner. | She came across as over-assertive in the interview, which might have cost her the job. 她面试时表现得过于自信，这可能让她失去了这份工作。

| He was so over-assertive that he alienated his colleagues. 他过于武断，疏远了他的同事们。| 近义词：pushy, aggressive, domineering, bossy| 反义词：timid, shy, reserved, unassuming, meek|词性: 形容词
