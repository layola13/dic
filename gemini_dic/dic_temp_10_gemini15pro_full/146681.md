overheaped: /ˌoʊvərˈhiːpt/ | adj. | 堆积过多的，过度装载的 | Filled or loaded to excess; overfull. | The table was overheaped with food. 桌子上堆满了食物。 | The cart was overheaped with hay. 手推车装满了干草。 | 近义词: overloaded, overburdened, overflowing | 反义词: empty, scant, meager | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From "over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, over") + "heaped" (past participle of "heap," from Old English hēap, meaning "a pile, mass").
