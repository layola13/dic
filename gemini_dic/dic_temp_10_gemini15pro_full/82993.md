gunnie:/ˈɡʌni/ | n. |炮手，枪手|A person skilled in the use of guns, especially artillery.  A gunner can also refer to a warrant officer or non-commissioned officer in charge of a gun or group of guns. |  He was a skilled gunnie in the war. 他在战争中是一名熟练的炮手。| The gunnie adjusted the sights on his weapon. 炮手调整了他武器上的瞄准器。| synonyms: artilleryman, gunner, cannoneer | antonyms: pacifist, civilian | noun

注意事项:
“Gunnie” is an informal term.  Although less common, "gunner" is the more formal and preferred term.  While both essentially refer to someone who operates a gun, "gunner" is generally used in a military context, while "gunnie" can have a broader application, sometimes referring to someone skilled with any type of firearm.
