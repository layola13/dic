capitularly:/kəˈpɪtʃʊləli/
|adv.| 以章回体；逐章地；按章节|In a manner relating to the chapters of a book or document; chapter by chapter. |The history of the abbey was documented capitularly, with each chapter covering a different century. 修道院的历史被按章节记录，每一章都涵盖了不同的世纪。| | |adverb
