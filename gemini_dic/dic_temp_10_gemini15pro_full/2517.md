administrator:/ədˈmɪnɪstreɪtər/ | n. | 管理员，管理者 | A person who manages or directs affairs; especially, one who administers an estate. | The school administrators decided to implement a new dress code. 学校的管理人员决定实施新的着装规范。

The network administrator is responsible for maintaining the company's servers. 网络管理员负责维护公司的服务器。 | 近义词: manager, director, executive, superintendent, supervisor | 反义词: subordinate, employee | 词性: 名词

administrators:/ədˈmɪnɪstreɪtərz/ | n. | 管理员们，管理者们 | Plural form of "administrator."  Refers to a group of individuals who manage or direct affairs. | The administrators met to discuss the budget cuts. 管理员们开会讨论预算削减。

The hospital administrators are working to improve patient care. 医院的管理人员正在努力改善病人护理。| 近义词: managers, directors, executives, superintendents, supervisors | 反义词: subordinates, employees | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English administratour, from Late Latin administrator, from Latin administrare (“to manage, serve”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + ministrare (“to serve, attend, furnish”).
