andantino:/ˌɑːndɑːnˈtinoʊ/ /ˌændænˈtiːnəʊ/
| adv. n. adj.|
|稍慢的，小行板（速度比行板稍慢）|
|A musical direction indicating a moderately slow tempo, slightly faster than andante.|
|The andantino movement in the symphony was particularly beautiful. 交响曲中小行板乐章特别优美。|
|The composer marked the section as "andantino." 作曲家将这一部分标记为“小行板”。|
|近义词：moderately slow, andante|
|反义词：allegro, presto|
|词性: adverb, noun, adjective|
|词源: Italian, diminutive of andante|
