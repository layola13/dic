exosseous:/ˌɛkˈsoʊsiəs/
adjective
骨外的;骨外面的
Situated or occurring outside of a bone.
例句：The exosseous portion of the tumor was easily removed. 肿瘤的骨外部分很容易切除。

例句：Exosseous chondrosarcoma is a rare type of cancer. 骨外软骨肉瘤是一种罕见的癌症。
synonym: extraosseous, extraskeletal
antonym: intraosseous
词性: 形容词
