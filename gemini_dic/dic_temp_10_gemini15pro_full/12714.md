assessable:/əˈsesəbl/ | adj. | 可评估的，可估价的，可征税的 | Able to be assessed or evaluated. | The value of the property is easily assessable. 这房产的价值很容易评估。|  The damage to the car was assessable by the insurance company. 保险公司对汽车的损坏进行了评估。 | synonyms: evaluable, appraisable, measurable, quantifiable | antonyms: immeasurable, unquantifiable, incalculable | adjective
