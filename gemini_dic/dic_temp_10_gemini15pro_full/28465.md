carburized:/ˈkɑːrbəraɪzd/ |v. |渗碳（处理）|To introduce carbon into the surface of (iron or steel) in order to harden it. |The gears were carburized to increase their durability. 齿轮经过渗碳处理以提高其耐用性。|
carburized:/ˈkɑːrbəraɪzd/ |adj. |渗碳的|Hardened by carburization. |The carburized steel had a higher surface hardness. 渗碳钢的表面硬度更高。|


Etymology: From *carbur*, from Latin *carbo* "coal, charcoal" + *-ize*, from French *-iser*, from Latin *-izare*.

Synonyms: case-harden, surface-harden
Antonyms: decarburize, soften
