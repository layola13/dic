auditorially:/ˌɔːdɪˈtɔːriəli/| adv.|听觉上地|Relating to the sense of hearing or perceived through hearing. |She processes information auditorially, preferring to listen to lectures rather than read texts. 她通过听觉处理信息，更喜欢听讲座而不是阅读文本。|Synonyms: acoustically, by ear|Antonyms: visually|adverb
