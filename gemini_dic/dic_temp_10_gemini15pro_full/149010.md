ovulite:/ˈoʊvəˌlaɪt/| n. |卵磷脂|A phospholipid, especially lecithin, found in egg yolk and other animal and plant tissues.|Egg yolks are a good source of ovulites, which are important for cell membrane health.|近义词：lecithin, phospholipid|反义词: None|词性: noun
词源: From Latin *ovum* (egg) + *-lite* (stone, fossil).  This suffix is often used in mineralogy but can also be used more broadly to indicate a constituent part.
