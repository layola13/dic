four-strokes:/ˈfɔːr strəʊks/| n.|四冲程|An engine operating cycle that requires four strokes (intake, compression, power, and exhaust) of the piston to complete one power cycle.  It's often used in the context of internal combustion engines.|The car's engine is a powerful four-strokes. 这辆车的发动机是强大的四冲程发动机。|Near synonyms: four-cycle, Otto cycle|Antonym: two-strokes|noun

four-strokes:/ˈfɔːr strəʊks/| adj.|四冲程的|Relating to or operating on the four-stroke cycle.|This lawnmower has a four-strokes engine. 这台割草机有一个四冲程发动机。|Near synonyms: four-cycle|Antonym: two-stroke|adjective
