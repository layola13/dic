cosmetically:/kɑːzˈmetɪkli/
| adv.|表面地；化妆上地；美容上地|In a way that relates to beauty; superficially.|The car was cosmetically damaged, but the engine was fine. 这辆车表面受损，但发动机没事。|
Synonyms: superficially, outwardly, externally
|Antonyms: fundamentally, intrinsically, deeply
|词性:副词

cosmetically:/kɑːzˈmetɪkli/

|Synonyms: cosmetic
|Antonyms: N/A
