elastomer:/ɪˈlæstəmər/ | n. | 弹性体 |A natural or synthetic polymer having elastic properties, similar to rubber. | Car tires are made of a synthetic elastomer. 汽车轮胎是由合成弹性体制成的。| synonym: rubber, polymer | antonym: rigid material | noun

elastomer:/ɪˈlæstəmər/ | n. | 弹性体 |A material that can be stretched or compressed and return to its original shape. |  Silicone is a common elastomer used in seals and gaskets. 硅酮是一种常用的弹性体，用于密封件和垫圈。| synonym: elastic material, flexible material | antonym: brittle material | noun
