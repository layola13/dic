cowardliness:/ˈkaʊərdlɪnəs/| n.|怯懦，胆小|The soldier's cowardice cost him his life.  这个士兵的懦弱让他丧命。|Cowardliness is a despicable trait. 懦弱是一种卑劣的品质。|近义词: timidity, pusillanimity, fearfulness|反义词: bravery, courage, valor|词性: noun

Etymology: From Middle English *cowardlinesse*, from Old French *coardise*, from *coart*, from Vulgar Latin *cōdārius* "with a tail between the legs", from Latin *cauda* "tail".
