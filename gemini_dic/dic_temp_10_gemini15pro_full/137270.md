nonsympathetic:/ˌnɒnsɪmpəˈθetɪk/ /ˌnɑːnsɪmpəˈθetɪk/
adjective
不同情的，缺乏同情心的，不体谅的
Not feeling, showing, or expressing sympathy.
例句：He was very nonsympathetic when I told him about my problems.  当我告诉他我的问题时，他非常不同情。

例句：She gave me a nonsympathetic look. 她给了我一个毫无同情心的眼神。

近义词：unsympathetic, callous, indifferent, uncaring
反义词：sympathetic, compassionate, understanding, caring
词性: adjective
