lidding:/ˈlɪdɪŋ/ | n. | 盖子; 盖子材料; 封盖物 |A cover for a container, especially one that can be removed or opened.| The lidding on the yogurt container was difficult to peel off. 酸奶盒上的盖子很难剥掉。| 近义词: cover, top, cap, lid | 反义词: base, bottom | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From the noun "lid" + "-ing" suffix, denoting something that acts as or relates to a lid.  "Lid" comes from Old English "hlid."
