analects:/ˈænəˌlekts/| n.|语录，文选，论语|A collection of short literary or philosophical extracts.|例句：The Analects of Confucius are a collection of his sayings and teachings.  孔子的《论语》是他的言论和教诲的汇编。

例句：She's been studying the Analects for years. 她多年来一直在研究《论语》。|近义词：sayings, teachings, writings, selections, excerpts|反义词: (No direct antonym exists for a collection of writings) |词性:noun
