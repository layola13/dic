agenting:/ˈeɪdʒəntɪŋ/
n.
代理; 经纪; 代理业务
the business of acting as an agent for others, for example, writers, actors, or athletes.
Example:  She works in agenting, representing several well-known authors. 她从事代理业务，代表几位知名作家。

Example: He made his fortune in property agenting. 他靠房地产经纪发了财。

近义词: representation, agency, brokering, intermediary
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps consider)  self-representation, direct dealing
