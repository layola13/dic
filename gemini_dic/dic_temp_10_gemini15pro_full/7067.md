anemonin:/əˈnɛmənɪn/
noun
海葵毒素
A toxin, C10H8O4, found in various species of anemone and other plants, such as buttercups. It causes blistering on contact with skin and is poisonous if ingested.
Contact with the plant's sap can cause skin irritation due to the presence of anemonin.  接触这种植物的汁液会导致皮肤刺激，因为其中含有海葵毒素。
Synonyms: protoanemonin (precursor), irritant, toxin
Antonyms:  antidote, remedy
词性: noun
