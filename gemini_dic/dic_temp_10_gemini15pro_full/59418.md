earthinesses:/ˈɜːθɪnɪsɪz/
noun
世俗性，粗俗，泥土气息
The quality of being earthy; the state of being down-to-earth or unrefined.
Her paintings celebrated the earthinesses of rural life. 她的绘画歌颂了乡村生活的质朴。
earthy, groundedness, worldliness
pretentiousness, artificiality, refinement
