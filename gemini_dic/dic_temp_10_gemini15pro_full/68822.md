fascination:/ˌfæsɪˈneɪʃn/ | n. | 魅力；迷恋；入迷；令人着迷的事物 |  The state of being intensely interested in something; the power to fascinate. | Her fascination with butterflies started in childhood. 她对蝴蝶的迷恋始于童年。| synonyms: allure, captivation, enchantment, enthrallment | antonyms: apathy, boredom, disinterest | noun

fascinate:/ˈfæsɪneɪt/ | v. | 使着迷；使神魂颠倒 | To attract and hold the attention of someone by being extremely interesting, attractive, or beautiful. |  Snakes fascinate me. 蛇使我着迷。| synonyms:  captivate, enthrall, mesmerize | antonyms: bore, repel, disgust | verb
