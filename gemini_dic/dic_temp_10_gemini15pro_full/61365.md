embezzle: /ɪmˈbezəl/ | v. | 盗用，挪用（公款） | To illegally take money that you are responsible for managing. | The accountant embezzled $10,000 from the company. 会计从公司盗用了1万美元。 |  The CEO was found guilty of embezzling millions of dollars from investors. 首席执行官被判犯有挪用投资者数百万美元的罪行。 | Synonyms: misappropriate, pilfer, defraud, peculate | Antonyms: donate, contribute, reimburse | verb
