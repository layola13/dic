disorganize:/dɪsˈɔːrɡənaɪz/
verb
扰乱，打乱，使混乱
to destroy the organization or order of; to make disorganized
The strike disorganized the factory's production. 罢工扰乱了工厂的生产。
The sudden change of plans completely disorganized us. 计划的突然改变完全打乱了我们的阵脚。
synonyms: disrupt, disorder, unsettle, disturb, muddle, confuse
antonyms: organize, order, arrange, systematize, regulate
词性:v.

disorganized:/dɪsˈɔːrɡənaɪzd/
adjective
紊乱的，无组织的，杂乱无章的
lacking order or organization; messy or confused
He's a disorganized person; he always loses things. 他是一个没有条理的人，总是丢东西。
The disorganized state of the office made it difficult to find anything. 办公室杂乱无章的状态使我们很难找到任何东西。
synonyms: messy, cluttered, chaotic, disordered, jumbled, confused
antonyms: organized, orderly, tidy, neat, systematic, structured
词性:adj.

词源:  "dis-" (prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of") + "organize" (from Old French "organiser," ultimately from Latin "organum" meaning "instrument, tool").
