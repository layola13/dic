etherization:/iːθəraɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
乙醚麻醉；乙醚化
The process of anesthetizing someone with ether.
The patient underwent etherization before the surgery. 病人在手术前进行了乙醚麻醉。
anesthesia, narcosis
