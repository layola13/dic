coenenchyma:/siːˈnɛŋkɪmə/ /siːˈnɛŋkaɪmə/| n. |共肉|The common tissue or soft part of a coral, composed of mesoglea containing amoeboid cells, through which the polyps are interconnected.|The coenenchyma of the coral was bright red. 珊瑚的共肉是鲜红色的。|近义词:mesoglea (in some contexts)|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek κοινός (koinós, “common”) + ἔγχυμα (énchyma, “infusion, injection”).
