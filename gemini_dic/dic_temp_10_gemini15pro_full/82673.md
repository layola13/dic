guesstimate:/ˈɡɛstɪmət/| n. | 估算；估计；近似计算 | An approximate calculation based on incomplete data. |  The project manager gave a guesstimate of three months for completion. 项目经理粗略估计三个月就能完成。

 We can only make a guesstimate of the final cost. 我们只能粗略估计最终成本。 |近义词：estimate, approximation, rough calculation, ballpark figure |反义词: precise calculation, accurate measurement |词性: noun
