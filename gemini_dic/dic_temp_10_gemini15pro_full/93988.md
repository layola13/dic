impaired:/ɪmˈpeərd/ | adj. |受损的，削弱的|Weakened or damaged.  Having a reduced ability to function properly.|

例句：His hearing was impaired by the loud noise. 他的听力被 loud noise 损害了。

例句：The accident left him with impaired vision.  事故使他的视力受损。

近义词：damaged, weakened, diminished, hampered, compromised, debilitated |

反义词：improved, enhanced, strengthened, healthy, unimpaired, intact |

词性: adjective


词源:  Late Middle English: past participle of *impair, from Old French empeirier, based on Latin pejorare ‘make worse’.
