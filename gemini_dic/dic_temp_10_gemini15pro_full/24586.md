brodekin:/ˈbrɒdəkɪn/| n. |半统靴，短靴|A kind of heavy, laced shoe or half-boot reaching to the ankle, worn in the 15th and 16th centuries.|He wore brodekins of soft leather. 他穿了软皮短靴。|近义词：boot, half-boot, ankle boot|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly sandal or slipper as a lighter footwear option)|词性:noun
