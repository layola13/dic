afters:/ˈɑːftəz/
n.
餐后甜点
A sweet dish eaten at the end of a meal.
We had apple pie for afters. 我们餐后吃了苹果派。
近义词：dessert, pudding, sweet
反义词：(无直接反义词，可考虑appetizer, starter 开胃菜)
词性:名词
