liaised:/liˈeɪzd/ | v. | 联系，联络 | act as a link between two or more people or groups. |  The teacher liaised with parents to discuss the student's progress. 老师与家长联系，讨论学生的进步。|Synonyms: cooperated, collaborated, communicated, interacted, networked | Antonyms: ignored, neglected, disconnected, separated | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *liaiser*, from *liaison* "a binding, union," from Latin *ligatio*, from *ligare* "to bind."
