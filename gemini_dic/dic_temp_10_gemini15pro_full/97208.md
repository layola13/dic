inflammative: /ɪnˈflæməˌtɪv/ | adj. | 炎症性的，煽动性的 | Tending to inflame or excite; inflammatory. |  His inflammative rhetoric roused the crowd to anger.  他煽动性的言辞激起了群众的愤怒。| 近义词: inflammatory, provocative, incendiary | 反义词: soothing, calming, conciliatory | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Late Latin *inflammativus, from Latin inflammatus (past participle of inflammare "to set on fire"), from in- "in" + flamma "flame".
