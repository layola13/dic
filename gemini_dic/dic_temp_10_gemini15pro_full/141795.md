orbed:/ɔːrbd/
adjective
球形的，圆形的; having the shape of an orb or globe
The full, orbed moon shone brightly in the sky.  满月在天空中闪耀着光芒。
近义词: spherical, globular, round
反义词: flat, shapeless
词性: adjective

orbed:/ɔːrbd/
verb (past tense)
形成球体；使成球形; to form into an orb or sphere
The molten metal orbed into a perfect sphere. 熔化的金属形成了一个完美的球体。
近义词: sphere, globalize
反义词: flatten
词性: verb
