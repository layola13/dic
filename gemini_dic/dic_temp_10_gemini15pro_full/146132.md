overemploy:/ˌoʊvərɪmˈplɔɪ/| v. |过度雇佣，雇佣过多人员|To employ more people than are necessary or than can be used effectively.|The company overemployed during the boom years and now has to downsize. 公司在繁荣时期过度雇佣，现在不得不裁员。|近义词：overstaff, overman|反义词: underemploy, understaff|词性:动词

PHRASE->
There is no common phrase directly using "overemploy."  However, phrases describing a similar situation include:
carrying too much staff =  人员过多
having a bloated workforce = 臃肿的员工队伍
being overstaffed = 人员过剩
having more employees than necessary = 员工人数超过所需
having redundant staff =  冗员


词源：Over- (prefix meaning "excessively") + employ (verb meaning "to give work to someone and pay them for it").
