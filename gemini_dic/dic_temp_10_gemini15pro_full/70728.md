find-fault:/ˌfaɪnd ˈfɔːlt/| v. |找茬儿，吹毛求疵|To criticize or complain about someone or something, often unfairly or for trivial matters. |例句：He's always finding fault with my work. 他总是对我的工作吹毛求疵。

例句：She finds fault with everything I do.  她对我做的每件事都挑剔。

近义词：criticize, nitpick, carp, cavil|反义词: praise, compliment|词性:动词
