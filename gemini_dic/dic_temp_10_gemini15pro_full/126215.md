{my-word}: what /wɒt (UK) wʌt (US)/| pron. determiner. adv. int.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information about something.|例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|近义词：which|反义词: (No single antonym, context-dependent)|pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? = 你怎么想？
What does she want for lunch? = 她午饭想吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching? = 你在看什么？
What is he cooking? = 他在做什么饭？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? = 她用这笔钱做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on? = 你一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? = 你刚才说什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8pm yesterday? = 昨天晚上8点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? = 我看到她的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you eaten before you got sick? = 你生病之前吃了什么？
What had they decided before the meeting ended? = 会议结束前他们决定了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I called? = 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about? = 他们一直在争论什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们将在会议上讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到今年年底你将完成什么？
What will they have finished by tomorrow? = 到明天他们将完成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been studying for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经学习五年什么了？
What will they have been working on by next month? = 到下个月他们将一直在做什么？


虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? = 如果你中了彩票，你会做什么？
What would she have done if she had known the truth? = 如果她知道真相，她会怎么做？


被动语态:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在对这个问题采取什么措施？


疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？


否定句:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what I'm saying. = 她不明白我在说什么。



PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？

what if = 如果...会怎么样
what for = 为了什么
So what? = 那又怎样？
What about...? = ...怎么样？/ ...怎么办？
What's more = 而且；更重要的是
