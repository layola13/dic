consecrate:/ˈkɒnsɪkreɪt/ | vt. | 奉献，献祭，祝圣 | To dedicate something to a sacred purpose; to make something sacred. | The bishop consecrated the new altar. 主教祝圣了新祭坛。 | dedicate, sanctify, hallow | desecrate, profane | verb
