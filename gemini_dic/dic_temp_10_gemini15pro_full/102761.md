以下提供 what 的详细解析，因为题目中包含大量的what的用法示例，推测提问者可能想了解what的用法。

what: /wɒt/ (British), /wʌt/ (American) |pron. det. adv. int.| 什么，多么，|

英文解析:  "What" can function as a pronoun, determiner, adverb, and interjection.  As a pronoun, it asks for information about something.  As a determiner, it modifies a noun to ask which thing or things are meant. As an adverb, it modifies an adjective or adverb to ask about degree or extent. As an interjection, it expresses surprise or disbelief.

例句：
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？

What did you eat for lunch? = 你午饭吃了什么？


近义词：which, how

反义词:  "What" doesn't have a direct antonym.


词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection
