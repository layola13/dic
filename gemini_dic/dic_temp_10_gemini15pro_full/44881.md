crybabying:/ˈkraɪˌbeɪbiɪŋ/| n.|爱哭鬼，哭鼻子，哭哭啼啼|A person who cries easily and frequently, especially about minor problems.  A childish act of crying or complaining excessively.|He's such a crybabying; he complains about everything! 他真是个爱哭鬼，什么事都抱怨！|近义词：whining, complaining, sniveling|反义词: stoic, brave|noun
