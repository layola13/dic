estrangednesses:/ɪˈstreɪndʒɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
疏远；失和；隔阂
The state of being estranged, especially the alienation or separation resulting from hostility or indifference.
Example: The estrangednesses between the two families made family gatherings awkward. 两个家庭之间的疏远使得家庭聚会很尴尬。
Example: Years of silence had created an almost palpable estrangedness between them.  多年的沉默在他们之间造成了几乎可以感知的隔阂。
Synonyms: alienation, distance, separation, rift, schism
Antonyms: closeness, intimacy, harmony, reconciliation, rapport
词性: 名词

词源:  From estranged + -ness.  "Estranged" itself comes from the Old French "estrangier," meaning "to make strange or foreign."
