comatosities:/ˌkəʊməˈtɒsətiz/ | n. |昏迷状态（复数）| A state of deep unconsciousness for a prolonged or indefinite period, typically caused by severe injury or illness.  The plural form of "comatosity." | The hospital reported several comatosities after the bus crash.  这家医院报告了巴士车祸后有多人处于昏迷状态。|Synonyms: unconsciousness, stupor, insensibility | Antonyms: consciousness, alertness, awareness | Noun

Etymology:  From "comatose" + "-ities" (suffix forming plural nouns). "Comatose" derives from the Greek "kōma" meaning "deep sleep"
