fruited:/ˈfruːtɪd/
adjective
结果实的；果味浓郁的
bearing or having produced fruit; flavored with fruit
例句：The fruited branches of the orange tree bowed low. 橘子树结果实的枝条低垂着。
例句：She enjoyed a fruited yogurt for breakfast. 她早餐享用了果味酸奶。
近义词：fruit-bearing, fruit-laden
反义词：barren, fruitless
词性：adjective
