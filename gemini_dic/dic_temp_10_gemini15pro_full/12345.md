ashamedly:/əˈʃeɪmɪdli/| adv.|羞愧地，惭愧地|In a manner showing shame or embarrassment.  Feeling or showing guilt or embarrassment about something you have done or about a quality in your character.|She ashamedly admitted she had lied. 她羞愧地承认自己撒谎了。|He ashamedly looked away after he was caught cheating. 他被抓到作弊后，羞愧地移开了视线。|近义词：guiltily, sheepishly, apologetically|反义词：proudly, boldly, confidently|词性:副词
Etymology: From ashamed + -ly. Ashamed comes from Old English  *āscamod,  past participle of  āscamian  "to feel shame," from ā- "thoroughly" + scamian "to shame."
