microtomy:/maɪˈkrɒtəmi/| n.|显微切片术；显微切片法|The process of cutting extremely thin sections of material, typically biological samples, for examination under a microscope.  This involves embedding the sample in a medium (like paraffin wax or resin), then slicing it with a specialized instrument called a microtome.|例句：Microtomy is an essential technique in histology. 显微切片术是组织学中的一项基本技术。

例句：The student is learning the art of microtomy.  这个学生正在学习显微切片术。|近义词：sectioning, microsectioning|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "macroscopic examination")|词性:noun
