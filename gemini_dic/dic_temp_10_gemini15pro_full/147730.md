overprize: /ˌoʊvərˈpraɪz/
verb
过高评价；过高定价
To value or price something too highly.
The painting was overprized at the auction. 这幅画在拍卖会上被估价过高了。
Collectors often overprize first editions. 收藏家经常高估初版书的价值。
overvalue, overestimate
undervalue, underestimate
v.
