ensnare:/ɪnˈsneər/ | vt. | 诱捕，使陷入圈套，使落入陷阱 | To catch or trap something, often by trickery or deceit. |  The spider ensnared the fly in its web. 蜘蛛用网困住了苍蝇。

The con man ensnared several elderly people in his elaborate scheme. 骗子用他精心设计的诡计诱捕了几位老人。

| 近义词：trap, catch, entangle, enmesh | 反义词：release, free, liberate | 词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->

From en- + snare.  "En-" is a prefix meaning "to cause to be in" or "to put into." "Snare" comes from the Old English word "sner" which refers to a noose or trap for catching birds or small animals.
