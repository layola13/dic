consular: /ˈkɒnsjʊlə(r)/ (US) /ˈkɑːnsələr/ (UK)| adj.| 领事的；领事馆的| Relating to a consul or consulate.  A consul is an official appointed by a government to live in a foreign city and protect and promote the government's citizens and interests there.|  The consular officer stamped my visa. 领事官员在我的签证上盖了章。|  diplomatic, official| N/A| adjective

consular: /ˈkɒnsjʊlə(r)/ (US) /ˈkɑːnsələr/ (UK)| n.| 领事| A government official who lives in a foreign city to protect their country's citizens and interests.| She went to the consular to renew her passport. 她去领事馆更换护照。| consul, diplomat| N/A| noun
