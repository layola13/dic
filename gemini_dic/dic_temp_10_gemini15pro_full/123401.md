monophone:/ˈmɒnəfəʊn/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnəfoʊn/ (US)| n. |单音; 单音电话|A telephone that transmits only one sound at a time.  A telephone designed for use with a single earpiece.|The old monophone only allowed one person to speak at a time.  这台老式单音电话一次只能让一个人说话。|Synonym: single-channel telephone|Antonym: stereophonic telephone|Noun
