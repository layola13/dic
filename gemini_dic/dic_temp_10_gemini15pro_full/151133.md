parataxes:/ˈpærəˌtæksiːz/
n.
并列, 并置, 平行结构
The placing of clauses or phrases one after another, without words to indicate coordination or subordination, as in *Tell me, how are you*.
He used parataxes in his writing, presenting a series of short, declarative sentences. 他在写作中使用了并列结构，呈现了一系列简短的陈述句。
parataxis, coordination, juxtaposition
hypotaxis, subordination
noun
