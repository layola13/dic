debunked:/diːˈbʌŋkt/
|v. (past participle)|揭穿；驳斥；戳穿|To expose the falseness or hollowness of (a myth, idea, or belief).|The documentary debunked the myth that sharks are mindless killers. 这部纪录片揭穿了鲨鱼是无意识杀手的谬论。|Synonyms: expose, disprove, refute, invalidate|Antonyms: confirm, validate, prove, substantiate|verb

debunking:/diːˈbʌŋkɪŋ/
|n.|揭穿；驳斥；戳穿（的行为）|The act of exposing the falseness or hollowness of something.|The debunking of the myth was met with resistance. 对这个神话的揭穿遭到了抵制。|Synonyms: exposure, refutation, invalidation|Antonyms: confirmation, validation, proof, substantiation|noun
