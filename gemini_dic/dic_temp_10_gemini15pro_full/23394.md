brachydome:/ˈbrækɪdoʊm/| n. |短穹|A dome-like crystal form with low height in comparison to its diameter.  It appears flattened. It's a term used in mineralogy and crystallography. |The mineral calcite often forms brachydomes. 方解石经常形成短穹。| Calcite crystals exhibited prominent brachydomes. 方解石晶体呈现出显著的短穹。|Synonym: dome (in a broader sense) |Antonym:  (No direct antonym, potentially a prism or a long prismatic crystal depending on the context) | noun
