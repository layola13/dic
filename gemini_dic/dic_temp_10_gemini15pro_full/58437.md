duckers:/ˈdʌkərz/| n.| 躲避者；潜水者；低头弯腰的人|People who duck or crouch low, often to avoid being seen or hit.  Those who dive or swim underwater.|The duckers scurried for cover as the rocks began to fall.  岩石开始落下时，躲避者们急忙寻找掩护。|近义词：dodgers, divers, crouchers|反义词:standers, exposers|noun
