furthest:/ˈfɜːrðɪst/ /ˈfɜːrðɛst/| adj. adv.|最远的；最遥远的|Most distant in space or time; most remote.|例句：That's the furthest I can throw the ball. 那是我能把球扔得最远的地方了。|近义词：farthest, remotest, utmost|反义词：nearest, closest|词性：形容词/副词
furthest:/ˈfɜːrðɪst/ /ˈfɜːrðɛst/| adj. adv.|最大程度的；最大限度的|To the greatest degree or extent.|例句：He took the argument to its furthest extreme. 他把这场争论推向了极致。|近义词：greatest, utmost, maximum|反义词：least, minimum|词性：形容词/副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fyrrest, superlative of feorr (“far”). Cognate with Dutch verst, German fürst.
