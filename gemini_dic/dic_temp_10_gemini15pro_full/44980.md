crystallising:/ˈkrɪstəlɪzɪŋ/ /ˈkrɪstəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
结晶；使明确；使具体化
The events of that day crystallised his determination to change careers. 那一天发生的事件使他坚定了改变职业的决心。
solidify, concretize, set
dissolve, melt
v.
