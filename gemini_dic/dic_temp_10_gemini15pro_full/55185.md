dissented:/dɪˈsɛntɪd/
verb
不同意，持异议
To disagree with the majority opinion.
Two members dissented from the majority decision.  两名成员不同意多数人的决定。

dissented:/dɪˈsɛntɪd/
verb
(宗教)表达不同意见，不墨守成规
(in religion) To hold or express opinions that are different from the official beliefs of your church or religion.
Several ministers dissented from the church's teachings.  几位牧师不同意教会的教义。


近义词: disagreed, objected, opposed
反义词: agreed, concurred, assented
词性: verb
