achromatically:/ˌeɪkroʊˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 无色地；非彩色地 | Without color; in a way that relates to shades of gray or black and white. | The image was projected achromatically onto the screen.  图像被无色地投射到屏幕上。

The artist chose to represent the scene achromatically, focusing on form rather than color.  这位艺术家选择用非彩色的方式来表现这个场景，专注于形式而不是颜色。 | 近义词:monochromatically, neutrally | 反义词: chromatically, colorfully | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From a- (not) + chromatic (relating to color) + -ally (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Greek *a- (not) + chrōma (color).

USAGE->
Modifying Verbs:
The scientist achromatically rendered the microscopic image. = 科学家以无色的方式渲染了显微图像。
The photographer achromatically captured the stark landscape. = 摄影师以非彩色的方式捕捉了荒凉的景色。


Modifying Adjectives:
The achromatically designed room felt minimalist and modern. = 无色设计的房间感觉简约而现代。
The achromatically presented data was easier to interpret. = 非彩色呈现的数据更容易解读。
