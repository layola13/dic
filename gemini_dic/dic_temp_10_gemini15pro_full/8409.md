anti-stalinists:/ˈæntiˈstɑːlɪnɪsts/ | n. | 反斯大林主义者 | People who opposed Joseph Stalin, his policies, and Stalinism.  Those who disagreed with Stalin's totalitarian rule and the purges and oppression carried out under his regime. |  Many anti-stalinists were persecuted and executed during the Great Purge. 许多反斯大林主义者在大清洗中遭到迫害和处决。

Anti-stalinists within the Communist Party were often denounced as traitors.  共产党内部的反斯大林主义者经常被谴责为叛徒。 | 近义词: dissidents, opponents, critics | 反义词: Stalinists, supporters | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "anti-" (prefix meaning against) + "Stalinist" (a follower or supporter of Joseph Stalin).  "Stalinist" is derived from "Stalin," the name of the Soviet dictator,  with the suffix "-ist" indicating adherence to a doctrine or leader.
