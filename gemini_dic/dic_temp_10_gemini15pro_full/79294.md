glaze:/ɡleɪz/| n. vt. vi.|釉，光，上釉，变得光滑，眼神呆滞|A thin, glassy coating applied to pottery or food. To coat with a glaze. To become glassy or dull.|The potter glazed the vase. 陶工给花瓶上了釉。|近义词：polish, varnish, coat, gloss|反义词:dull, roughen|noun, verb

glaze:/ɡleɪz/| v. |眼神呆滞，变得无生气|To lose brightness and animation; to become dull and lifeless.|His eyes glazed over as the lecturer droned on.  演讲者喋喋不休地讲着，他的眼神变得呆滞。|近义词：stare blankly, zone out|反义词:brighten, focus|verb
