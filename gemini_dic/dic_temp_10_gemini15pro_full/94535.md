impossibility:/ɪmpɒsəˈbɪləti/ /ɪmpɑːsəˈbɪləti/
n.|不可能，不可能性|The impossibility of the task became apparent. 这项任务的不可能性变得显而易见。|例句：The impossibility of completing the project on time stressed everyone out.  项目无法按时完成给每个人都带来了压力。|近义词：unfeasibility, implausibility, impracticability|反义词：possibility, feasibility, likelihood|词性:名词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from Old French impossibilité, from late Latin impossibilitas, from impossibilis ‘impossible’.
