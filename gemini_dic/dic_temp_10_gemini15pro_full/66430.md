exegetical:/ˌɛksɪˈdʒɛtɪkəl/| adj.|释经的，解释圣经的|Relating to or characterized by exegesis; interpretative.|The professor's exegetical commentary on the biblical passage was illuminating. 教授对这段圣经的释经评论很有启发性。|Example:  His exegetical skills were highly regarded by his peers. 他的释经技巧受到同行的高度评价。|Synonyms: interpretative, explanatory, hermeneutical, analytical|Antonyms: uncritical, superficial, literal|Etymology: From Greek *exēgētikos*, from *exēgeisthai* "to interpret," from *ex-* "out" + *hēgeisthai* "to lead, guide."|Part of Speech: Adjective
