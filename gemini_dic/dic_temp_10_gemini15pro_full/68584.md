PHRASE->
fan out:  展开，散开  The police fanned out to search the area. 警察散开搜索该地区。
fan the flames:  煽风点火 His angry words only fanned the flames of the argument. 他愤怒的话语只会加剧争吵。
fan oneself:  给自己扇风 She was fanning herself with a magazine. 她正用杂志给自己扇风。
fan the embers:  煽动余烬 He fanned the embers of the fire until it blazed again. 他煽动着火的余烬，直到它再次燃烧起来。
fan out (like a fan): 像扇子一样展开 The peacock fanned out its tail feathers. 孔雀展开了它的尾羽。
