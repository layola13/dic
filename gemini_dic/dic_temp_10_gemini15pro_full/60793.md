electron-beam:/ɪˈlektrɒn biːm/ (US) /ɪˈlektrɒn biːm/ (UK)| n. | 电子束 | A stream of electrons focused into a narrow beam, often used in scientific instruments and electronic devices. | Electron-beam lithography is used to create extremely small patterns on semiconductor chips. 电子束光刻技术用于在半导体芯片上创建极其微小的图案。| synonyms: electron stream, cathode ray | antonyms: N/A | noun
