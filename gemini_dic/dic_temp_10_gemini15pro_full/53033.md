diodont:/ˈdaɪəˌdɑːnt/ (US), /ˈdaɪəˌdɒnt/ (UK)| n.| 豪猪鱼，二齿鲀|A type of fish, also called a pufferfish, that inflates its body into a ball shape when threatened.  It has two large teeth that resemble a beak.|The diver saw a diodont inflate itself on the ocean floor. 潜水员看到一条豪猪鱼在海底膨胀起来。|近义词：pufferfish, blowfish, globefish|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

diodont:/ˈdaɪəˌdɑːnt/ (US), /ˈdaɪəˌdɒnt/ (UK)| adj.| 豪猪鱼的，二齿鲀的 (rare usage)|Relating to or characteristic of diodonts/pufferfish. |  While technically correct, using "diodont" as an adjective is less common than saying "pufferfish".|例句: The diodont spines were surprisingly sharp. 豪猪鱼的刺出奇地锋利。|近义词：pufferfish, blowfish, globefish|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective
