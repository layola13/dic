broad-beam:/brɔːd biːm/| adj.|宽波束的，宽光束的|Having a beam that is wide or covers a large area.  Relating to a beam of radiation, particles, or light that has a wide cross-section.|The broad-beam sonar is effective for detecting schools of fish.|近义词：wide-beam, wide-angle|反义词：narrow-beam, focused-beam|adjective
