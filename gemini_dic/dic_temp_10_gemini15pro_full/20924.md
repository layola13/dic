bleariness:/ˈblɪərinəs/ | n. | 模糊，朦胧，视力模糊；昏昏沉沉 | The state of being bleary, especially having blurred vision or being mentally unclear due to tiredness or illness. |  I couldn't shake the bleariness from my eyes after the long flight. 长途飞行后，我无法摆脱眼睛的模糊感。| near synonyms: blurriness, haziness, dimness, fogginess, fuzziness | antonyms: clarity, sharpness, focus | noun

bleary:/ˈblɪəri/ | adj. | 模糊的，朦胧的；昏昏沉沉的 | (of the eyes) unfocused or filmy from sleep or tiredness; dim or hazy. | My eyes are still bleary from lack of sleep. 缺乏睡眠，我的眼睛仍然模糊不清。| near synonyms: blurred, unfocused, hazy, dim, misty | antonyms: clear, sharp, focused | adjective
