nonfunctionally:/ˌnɑnˈfʌŋkʃənəli/ | adv. | 非功能性地，不起作用地 | In a way that does not relate to or affect the operation or working of something.  It describes something that exists or is done without serving a practical purpose. | The decorative buttons on the coat were purely nonfunctionally.  这件外套上的装饰性纽扣纯粹是没起作用的。|  The software was installed nonfunctionally, taking up space but not working.  这个软件安装后没有发挥作用，只占用了空间却没有运行。 | synonym: uselessly, ineffectively, ornamentally | antonym: functionally, effectively, practically | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non*, meaning "not"
functional (adjective): from Late Latin *functionalis*, from *functio* "performance, execution," from *fungi* "perform, execute"
-ly (suffix): adverbial suffix
