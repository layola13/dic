molded:/ˈmoʊldɪd/ (US) /ˈməʊldɪd/ (UK)| adj.|模塑的，铸型的，塑造的|shaped or formed in a mold; influenced or formed in a particular way|例句：The plastic is molded into the shape of a car. 塑料被模塑成汽车的形状。|近义词：shaped, formed, cast, fashioned|反义词:unshaped, natural|adjective

molded:/ˈmoʊldɪd/ (US) /ˈməʊldɪd/ (UK)| v.|浇铸，塑造|past tense and past participle of "mold"|例句：He molded the clay into a beautiful sculpture. 他把粘土塑成一个美丽的雕塑。|近义词：shaped, formed, cast, fashioned|反义词:destroy, ruin|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
