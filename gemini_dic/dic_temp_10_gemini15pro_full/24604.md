broidery:/ˈbrɔɪdəri/ |n.| 刺绣；绣花；刺绣品 |Embroidery, the art or process of decorating fabric or other materials using a needle to apply thread or yarn. It can also refer to the finished product of this craft.| The broidery on her dress was exquisite.  她裙子上的刺绣精美绝伦。| synonyms: embroidery, needlework, needlepoint | antonyms: plainness, simplicity | noun
