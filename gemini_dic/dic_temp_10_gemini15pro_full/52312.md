dickering:/ˈdɪkərɪŋ/| n. vi.|讨价还价，争论不休|The process of bargaining or haggling, typically over a price.  A prolonged and often petty argument or discussion.|例句：After much dickering, they finally agreed on a price. 经过一番讨价还价，他们最终达成了价格协议。

例句：They spent hours dickering over the terms of the contract. 他们花了几个小时就合同条款争论不休。|近义词：haggling, bargaining, negotiating, bickering, quibbling|反义词：agreeing, conceding|词性: noun (gerund), verb (present participle)
