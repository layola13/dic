outgaze:/ˈaʊtɡeɪz/
n.|凝视; 注视; |The sustained outgaze of the subject suggested deep thought.  受试者持续的凝视表明他在深入思考。|例句：Her outgaze held a hint of defiance. 她的凝视中带着一丝挑衅。|近义词：stare, gaze, fixate|反义词:glance, peek|词性:名词

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "outward, exceeding") + "gaze" (noun meaning "a steady intent look").  由表示“向外，超过”的前缀 "out-" 和名词 "gaze"（意为“稳定的注视”）组成。
