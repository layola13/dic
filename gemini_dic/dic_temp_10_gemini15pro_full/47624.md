debile:/ˈdebɪl/ (US), /ˈdɛbaɪl/ (UK)| adj.| 虚弱的，衰弱的；懦弱的|Lacking strength, feeble, either physically or mentally. Characterized by weakness and often associated with illness or old age.  Can also describe someone lacking in willpower or courage.|

例句：
The debilesoldier could barely stand after the battle.
这个虚弱的士兵在战斗后几乎站不起来。

例句：
His debile attempts to resist were easily overcome.
他懦弱的抵抗尝试很容易就被克服了。

近义词：weak, frail, feeble, infirm, enfeebled, debilitated, powerless|
反义词：strong, robust, healthy, vigorous, powerful|
词性：adjective


词源：
From Old French *debile*, from Latin dēbilis “weak, crippled,” from dē- “down, away” + habilis “able, apt.”  Literally "un-able."
