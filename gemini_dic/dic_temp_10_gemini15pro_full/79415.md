glint:/ɡlɪnt/| vi.|闪光，闪烁；闪现；短暂的闪亮|A mischievous glint appeared in his eyes.  他眼中闪过一丝狡黠的光芒。|Stars glinted in the clear night sky. 星星在晴朗的夜空中闪烁。|近义词：flash, gleam, sparkle, shimmer, glitter|反义词：dullness|词性:动词

词源:  From Middle English *glenten, from Old Norse *glinta (“to shine, glitter”), from Proto-Germanic *glintaną (“to shine”). Cognate with Icelandic glinta (“to glisten, glitter”), Swedish glinta (“to gleam, glisten”), German gleißen (“to shine, glisten”).
