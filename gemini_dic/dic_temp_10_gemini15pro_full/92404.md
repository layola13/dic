hypogaeic:/ˌhaɪpəˈdʒiːɪk/| adj.|地下生的，地下萌发的|Growing or occurring beneath the earth's surface.  Specifically referring to plant parts like germination or fruit development that happens underground.|The peanut is a hypogaeic plant, its fruits developing underground. 花生是一种地下生的植物，其果实在地下发育。|近义词：subterranean, underground|反义词:epigeal, aboveground|词性:adjective
词源:From Greek *hupó* (under) + *gaia* (earth).
