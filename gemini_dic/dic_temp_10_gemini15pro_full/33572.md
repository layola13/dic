ciliary:/ˈsɪliəri/ (US), /ˈsɪliəri/ (UK)| adj.| 纤毛的，睫毛的|Relating to cilia (short, hairlike structures on the surface of some cells).|The ciliary muscles control the shape of the lens in the eye. 睫状肌控制着眼睛晶状体的形状。|近义词：ciliated|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "non-ciliated")|词性: adjective

ciliary:/ˈsɪliəri/ (US), /ˈsɪliəri/ (UK)| n.| 睫状体|The part of the eye that connects the iris to the choroid.|Damage to the ciliary body can affect the production of aqueous humor.  睫状体受损会影响房水的产生。|近义词： (No direct synonym, refers to a specific anatomical structure)|反义词: (No antonym)|词性: noun
