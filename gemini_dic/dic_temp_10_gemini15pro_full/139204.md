objective:/əbˈdʒɛktɪv/ | adj. n. |客观的；目标；|Something objective is based on facts rather than feelings or opinions.  A goal or aim.|
例句：The journalist tried to remain objective in her reporting. 记者努力在报道中保持客观。
例句：Her main objective is to get a promotion. 她的主要目标是升职。
近义词：unbiased, impartial, target, goal, aim |反义词:subjective, biased, personal |词性:adjective, noun

objective:/əbˈdʒɛktɪv/ | n. |目标；目的；物镜；|A goal or aim.  The lens in a telescope or microscope closest to the object being viewed.|
例句：The objective of the meeting was to discuss the budget. 会议的目标是讨论预算。
例句：He focused the objective of the microscope. 他调整了显微镜的物镜。
近义词：target, goal, aim, purpose |反义词: |词性:noun
