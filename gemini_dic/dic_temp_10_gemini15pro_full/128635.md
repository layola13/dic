nickelodeon:/ˌnɪkəlˈoʊdiən/ (US), /ˌnɪkəˈləʊdiən/ (UK)| n. |五分钱自动点唱机；早期电影放映机；儿童电视频道|A nickelodeon was originally a type of early movie theater where admission cost five cents (a nickel). The word later came to refer to a jukebox, and is now most commonly associated with the children's television channel. |例句：
We went to the nickelodeon to watch a short film. 我们去五分钱影院看了一部短片。

The nickelodeon played popular ragtime tunes. 自动点唱机播放了流行的拉格泰姆曲调。


近义词：movie theater, cinema, jukebox|反义词: None|词性: noun
