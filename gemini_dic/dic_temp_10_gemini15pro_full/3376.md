affronting:/əˈfrʌntɪŋ/
adjective
冒犯的；无礼的
causing offense; insulting
His affronting behavior caused a stir at the party.  他无礼的行为在聚会上引起了骚动。
The waiter's affronting tone made me want to leave. 服务员无礼的语气让我很想离开。
synonym: insulting, offensive, disrespectful, insolent, rude
antonym: respectful, polite, courteous, gracious, flattering
词性: adjective
