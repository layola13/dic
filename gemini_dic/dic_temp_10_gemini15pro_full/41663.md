correlative:/kəˈrɛlətɪv/ /ˈkɔːrələtɪv/| adj. n.|相关的，关联的；相关物，关联词|Having a mutual relationship; corresponding.  A word or phrase that grammatically corresponds to another.|例句：The rise in crime is correlative with increased unemployment. 犯罪率的上升与失业率的增加密切相关。|近义词：related, corresponding, reciprocal, interconnected|反义词:unrelated, independent, separate|词性:adjective

correlative:/kəˈrɛlətɪv/ /ˈkɔːrələtɪv/| n.|相关物，关联词|One of two related things, especially words or phrases.|例句： "Either" and "or" are correlative conjunctions. “Either”和“or”是关联连词。|近义词： counterpart, complement, parallel|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
