congeniality:/kənˌdʒiːniˈælɪti/ | n. | congeniality |  The quality of being agreeable, pleasant, and suited to one's taste or disposition. |  The congeniality of the atmosphere made it easy to relax and enjoy the evening.  轻松愉快的氛围让人很容易放松并享受夜晚。 |  agreeableness, affability, compatibility, cordiality, pleasantness  |  unpleasantness, incompatibility, hostility, coldness | noun


congenial: /kənˈdʒiːniəl/ | adj. |  合宜的；意气相投的；性格相似的 | (of a person) Pleasant because of a personality, qualities, or interests that are similar to one's own.  | We spent a relaxed evening with congenial friends. 我们与志趣相投的朋友们度过了一个轻松的夜晚。 | agreeable, pleasant, compatible, kindred, sympathetic | disagreeable, unpleasant, incompatible, hostile, uncongenial | adjective
