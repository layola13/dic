oxymel: /ˈɒksɪmɛl/ (UK) /ˈɑːksɪmɛl/ (US)| n.|蜂蜜醋|A mixture of honey and vinegar, formerly used as a medicine.|She made an oxymel to soothe her sore throat.|近义词:remedy, elixir|反义词:poison|词性:noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *oxymeli, from Greek ὀξύμελι (oxýmeli), from ὀξύς (oxús, “sharp, sour”) + μέλι (méli, “honey”).
