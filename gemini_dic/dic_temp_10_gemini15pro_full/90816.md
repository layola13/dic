humungous:/hjuːˈmʌŋɡəs/| adj.|巨大的，庞大的|Extremely large; enormous.|例句：That's a humungous spider! 那是一只巨大的蜘蛛！

例句：They live in a humungous house. 他们住在一栋巨大的房子里。|近义词：enormous, gigantic, massive, colossal|反义词：tiny, small, minuscule|词性:形容词
Etymology:  A humorous alteration of tremendous or enormous, first recorded in the 1960s.
