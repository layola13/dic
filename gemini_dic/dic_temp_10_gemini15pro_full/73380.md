forehanded:/fɔːrˈhændɪd/| adj.|有远见的；精明的；准备充分的；提前做好的；(网球等)正手的|Characterized by or done with the hand foremost, especially of a stroke in tennis or other racket sports made with the palm of the hand facing forward.  Also, prudent, provident, and characterized by foresight.|例句：She made a forehanded stroke and won the point. 她打了一个正手球赢得了这一分。

例句：His forehanded planning ensured the project's success. 他有远见的计划确保了项目的成功。|近义词：provident, prudent, thrifty, careful, prepared, anticipatory|反义词：improvident, imprudent, careless, unprepared, last-minute|词性: adjective
