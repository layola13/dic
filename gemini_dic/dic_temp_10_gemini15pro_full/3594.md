agazed:/əˈɡeɪzd/| adj.| 凝视的，注视的 | Filled with wonder and often open-mouthed admiration; staring intently. | She stood agazed at the magnificent sunset. 她凝视着壮丽的日落。 | 近义词: astonished, awestruck, bewildered, dazzled, fascinated, spellbound, startled, stunned | 反义词: indifferent, uninterested | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From the Middle English *agased*, past participle of *agasen*, "to gaze," from *a-* (an intensifying prefix) + *gasen*, "to gaze."  Ultimately from Old Norse *gaesa*.
