monolithic:/ˌmɒnəˈlɪθɪk/ | adj. | 整体的；单一的；庞大的 | Formed of a single large block of stone; (of an organization or system) large, powerful, and indivisible. |  例句：The company's monolithic structure made it slow to adapt to market changes.  这家公司庞大的结构使其难以适应市场变化。

例句：They built a monolithic concrete wall. 他们建了一堵整体的混凝土墙。| 近义词：massive, huge, gigantic, inflexible, rigid, unified | 反义词：flexible, adaptable, diverse, fragmented | 词性：形容词
