词源：
"Freaking" is a euphemism for "fucking," used to avoid saying the actual word, which is considered profane. It likely originated in the mid-20th century.  It's formed by replacing the vowel sound in "fucking" with a less offensive sound.  This type of euphemism is known as a minced oath.
