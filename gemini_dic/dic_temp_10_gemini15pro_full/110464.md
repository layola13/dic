liliest:/ˈlɪliɪst/| adj.|像百合的；最像百合的；最纯洁的|Most resembling a lily; most pure. |She wore a lilies dress, as white and pure as a bridal gown. 她穿着一件百合花般的连衣裙，像婚纱一样洁白纯净。| The lilies scent filled the room. 百合花的香味弥漫了整个房间。|近义词：pure, innocent, immaculate, pristine|反义词：sullied, tainted, impure|词性:形容词

词源：From lily + -est (superlative suffix).  来自lily（百合） + -est（最高级后缀）。
