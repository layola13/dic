hovercraft:/ˈhʌvəkrɑːft/ (US), /ˈhɒvəkrɑːft/ (UK)| n.|气垫船|A vehicle that travels just above the surface of water or land on a cushion of air.|例句：The hovercraft crossed the English Channel in just under an hour. 气垫船在不到一个小时的时间内穿越了英吉利海峡。

例句：We took a hovercraft to the Isle of Wight. 我们乘坐气垫船去了怀特岛。|近义词：air-cushion vehicle, ACV|反义词:|noun
