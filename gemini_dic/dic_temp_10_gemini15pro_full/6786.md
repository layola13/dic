anaphoric:/ˌænəˈfɒrɪk/ (US) /ˌænəˈfɔːrɪk/ (UK)| adj.|回指的，指代前文的|Referring back to something that has already been mentioned in the text or conversation.  An anaphoric expression gets its meaning from a preceding word or expression.|The word "it" in the sentence "The dog barked and then it ran away" is anaphoric, referring back to "the dog".|近义词：referential, reflexive|反义词：cataphoric|词性:形容词
