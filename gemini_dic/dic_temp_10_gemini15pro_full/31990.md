chemotherapist:/ˌkiːmoʊˈθerəpɪst/| n.| 化疗师，化学治疗师|A medical professional specializing in the administration of chemotherapy.  A doctor who specializes in chemotherapy.|The oncologist referred her to a chemotherapist for treatment. 肿瘤科医生将她转介给化疗师进行治疗。|Synonyms: chemo doctor, oncologist (sometimes)|Antonyms: None (There isn't a direct opposite professional)|Noun
