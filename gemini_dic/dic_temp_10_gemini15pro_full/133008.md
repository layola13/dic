nonenteric:/nɑːnɛnˈtɛrɪk/
adjective
非肠道的，肠外给药的
Not pertaining to, involving, or affecting the intestines.
例句：The doctor prescribed a nonenteric medication. 医生开了非肠道给药的药物。

例句：Nonenteric administration of the drug avoided potential digestive issues.  药物的肠外给药避免了潜在的消化问题。

近义词：parenteral
反义词：enteric
词性：adjective
