overfondly: /ˌoʊvərˈfɑːndli/ | adv. | 过分溺爱地，过分喜爱地 | Showing excessive fondness or affection. | She looked at him overfondly, making him uncomfortable. 她过分溺爱地看着他，让他感到不舒服。 |  He patted the dog overfondly, almost smothering it. 他过分亲昵地拍了拍那只狗，几乎要把它闷死了。 | 近义词: excessively fondly, dotingly | 反义词: coldly, indifferently | 词性: 副词

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "fondly" (adverb form of "fond," meaning "having an affection or liking for")
