affiliate: /əˈfɪlieɪt/ (US) /əˈfɪlɪeɪt/ (UK)|adj.|附属的，隶属的|Officially attached or connected to an organization.| An affiliated company. 一家附属公司。|synonyms: associated, connected, related, subsidiary|antonyms: independent, separate, unaffiliated|adjective



ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *affiliatus*, past participle of affiliare "to adopt as a son," from ad- "to" + filius "son."
