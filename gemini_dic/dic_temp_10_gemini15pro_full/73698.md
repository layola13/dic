forgivingly:/fərˈɡɪvɪŋli/
adv.
宽恕地，原谅地
in a forgiving manner; readily pardoning or overlooking offenses.
She smiled forgivingly at him. 她宽恕地对他笑了笑。

Synonyms:
mercifully, leniently, compassionately, charitably

Antonyms:
unforgivingly, relentlessly, vindictively, ruthlessly

词性: 副词
