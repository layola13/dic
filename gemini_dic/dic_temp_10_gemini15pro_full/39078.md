confounded:/kənˈfaʊndɪd/ /kɑːnˈfaʊndɪd/| adj.|困惑的，糊涂的，该死的|Confused, bewildered, or damned.  Showing or feeling confusion or bewilderment.|例句：I was confounded by the complex instructions. 我被复杂的指令搞糊涂了。|近义词：confused, bewildered, perplexed, baffled, puzzled|反义词：clear, certain, lucid|词性:形容词

confound:/kənˈfaʊnd/ /kɑːnˈfaʊnd/| vt.|使困惑，使糊涂，证明…是错误的，挫败|To cause surprise or confusion in (someone), especially by acting against their expectations. To prove (a theory, expectation, or prediction) wrong.|例句：The sudden change in weather confounded our plans. 天气的突然变化打乱了我们的计划。|近义词：confuse, bewilder, perplex, baffle, puzzle|反义词：clarify, enlighten|词性:动词
