overdominates:/ˌoʊvərˈdɑːmɪneɪts/ | v. | (基因)超显性，过显性 |  A situation in genetics where the heterozygote (having two different alleles for a trait) has a greater fitness than either homozygote (having two identical alleles for the trait). | The effect of the sickle-cell gene overdominates in malaria-prone areas, providing resistance to the disease. 镰状细胞基因在疟疾易发地区超显性，提供对疾病的抵抗力。

The heterozygote advantage overdominates the negative effects of the recessive allele. 杂合子的优势超过了隐性等位基因的负面影响。| 近义词: dominates, prevails, outweighs | 反义词: recessiveness (名词形态，指隐性) | 词性: verb
