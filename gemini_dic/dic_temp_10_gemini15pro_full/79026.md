give: /ɡɪv/ | v. | 给予，提供，赠送 | to freely transfer the possession of (something) to (someone) |  She gave me a book. 她给了我一本书。 | synonyms: offer, present, grant, donate, bestow | antonyms: take, receive, keep | verb

give: /ɡɪv/ | n. | 弹性，伸展性 | the ability of a material to stretch or bend | The fabric has a lot of give.  这种织物很有弹性。 | synonyms: elasticity, flexibility, springiness | antonyms: rigidity, stiffness | noun

Simple Present:
I give her a flower every week. 我每周都送她一朵花。
He gives his time to help others. 他奉献自己的时间去帮助别人。

Present Continuous:
I am giving a presentation tomorrow. 我明天要做一个演讲。
She is giving him a second chance. 她正在给他第二次机会。

Present Perfect:
I have given him all my money. 我已经把我所有的钱都给他了。
She has given up smoking. 她已经戒烟了。

Present Perfect Continuous:
I have been giving her advice for years. 我多年来一直在给她建议。
He has been giving his all to this project. 他一直全身心地投入到这个项目中。

Simple Past:
I gave her a gift for her birthday. 我送给她一份生日礼物。
He gave me a ride home. 他开车送我回家。

Past Continuous:
I was giving a speech when the power went out. 我正在演讲时停电了。
She was giving him a massage when the phone rang. 她正在给他按摩时电话响了。

Past Perfect:
I had given him the key before I left. 我离开前把钥匙给了他。
She had given up hope before the good news arrived. 在好消息到来之前，她已经放弃了希望。

Past Perfect Continuous:
I had been giving him lessons for months before he finally passed the test.  我给他上了几个月的课，他才最终通过了考试。
They had been giving the matter serious thought before making a decision. 在做出决定之前，他们一直在认真考虑这个问题。


Simple Future:
I will give you a call tomorrow. 我明天给你打电话。
She will give him another chance. 她会再给他一次机会。

Future Continuous:
I will be giving a lecture at this time next week. 下周这个时候我将做一个讲座。
They will be giving a concert next month. 他们下个月将举办一场音乐会。

Future Perfect:
I will have given away all my old clothes by the end of the month. 月底之前，我将把我所有的旧衣服都送出去。
She will have given birth by next spring. 到明年春天，她将已经生了孩子。

Future Perfect Continuous:
By next year, I will have been giving piano lessons for five years. 到明年，我将已经教了五年钢琴课。
By the time she retires, she will have been giving her time to charity for decades. 到她退休的时候，她将已经为慈善事业奉献了几十年。

Subjunctive Mood:
If I were you, I would give him a chance. 如果我是你，我会给他一个机会。
I wish I could give you more help. 我希望我能给你更多帮助。

Passive Voice:
The award was given to the most deserving student. 该奖项颁发给了最优秀的学生。
A second chance is being given to him. 他被给予了第二次机会。

Interrogative Sentence:
What did you give her for her birthday? 你送给她什么生日礼物？
Why did you give him so much money? 你为什么给他那么多钱？

Negative Sentence:
I didn't give him anything. 我什么都没给他。
She doesn't give up easily. 她不轻易放弃。
