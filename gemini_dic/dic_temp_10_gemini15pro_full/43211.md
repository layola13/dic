crache:/krɑːʃ/ /kræʃ/ | n. |呕吐物；呕吐（尤指婴儿）；（尤指婴儿）呕吐 | An act or instance of vomiting, especially by a baby. | The baby had a crache after feeding.  婴儿喂奶后吐了。 | 近义词: vomit, spew, puke, throw up | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *cracher*, meaning "to spit, spew." Ultimately from Vulgar Latin *cratis*, from Latin *craticula* "small hurdle, lattice," used metaphorically for the throat.
