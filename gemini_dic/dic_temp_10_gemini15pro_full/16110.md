baneful:/ˈbeɪnfəl/| adj.|有害的，恶毒的|Producing destruction or woe; seriously harmful; pernicious.|例句：The baneful effects of the drought devastated the crops. 干旱的恶劣影响摧毁了农作物。|例句：Her baneful influence corrupted the young prince. 她恶毒的影响腐蚀了年轻的王子。|近义词：pernicious, harmful, destructive, noxious, deleterious|反义词：beneficial, helpful, advantageous|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *baneful, from bane + -ful.  Bane from Old English bana "slayer, murderer, cause of death," from Proto-Germanic *banon "that which causes death" (source also of Old Norse bani "death, destruction," Old High German bano "death, destruction," Gothic banja "wound, bruise").
