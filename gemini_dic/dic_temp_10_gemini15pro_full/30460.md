centrifugal:/ˌsɛnˈtrɪfjʊɡəl/ /ˌsɛnˈtrɪfjuːɡl/| adj.|离心的|Moving or tending to move away from a center.  Relating to or produced by centrifugal force.|The centrifugal force pushes the clothes against the sides of the washing machine drum. 离心力将衣物推向洗衣机滚筒的侧面。|The cream separator works using centrifugal principles. 奶油分离器利用离心原理工作。|近义词：radiating, outward|反义词: centripetal|词性: adjective
