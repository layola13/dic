hydroponists:/ˌhaɪdrəˈpɒnɪsts/
n.
水培种植者；水耕栽培者
A person who grows plants in a nutrient solution without soil.
The hydroponists monitored the pH levels of the water carefully.  水培种植者仔细监测水的pH值。
Hydroponists can grow crops year-round, regardless of the weather.  无论天气如何，水培种植者都可以全年种植作物。
Synonyms:  hydroculture grower, soilless cultivator
Antonyms:  traditional farmer, soil-based grower
noun
