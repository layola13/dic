affixation:/ˌæfɪkˈseɪʃən/ | n. | 词缀法；附加；词缀 | The process of forming words by adding affixes (prefixes, suffixes, infixes, or circumfixes) to a root or stem. |  The word "unhappiness" is formed by affixation, using the prefix "un-" and the suffix "-ness".  词语“unhappiness”是由词缀法构成的，使用了前缀“un-”和后缀“-ness”。 | derivation, formation | - | noun
