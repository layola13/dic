neuro-effector:/ˌnʊroʊɪˈfɛktər/ | adj. | 神经效应器的 | Relating to or denoting nerve cells or fibres that stimulate or activate muscles or glands. |  The neuro-effector junction is the synapse between a motor neuron and a muscle fiber. 神经效应器连接是运动神经元和肌肉纤维之间的突触。|  The study focused on the neuro-effector mechanisms involved in the regulation of heart rate. 这项研究集中于参与调节心率的神经效应机制。 | Synonyms: neuromuscular, neuroglandular | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider terms like "neuro-inhibitory" as conceptually opposite) |  adjective
