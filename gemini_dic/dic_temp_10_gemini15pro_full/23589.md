brakiest:/ˈbreɪkiɪst/ | adj. | 最多荆棘的；最棘手的 | Most full of brambles or thorns; most difficult or prickly. |  The brakiest path led to the hidden clearing. 最多荆棘的小路通往隐蔽的空地。| Near Synonyms: thorniest, prickliest, most difficult | Antonyms: smoothest, easiest, clearest | word property: adjective

Etymology: From Old English *brǣc, ultimately from Proto-Germanic *brǣkaz. The suffix "-y" makes it an adjective "braky" meaning "full of brambles," and "-est" indicates the superlative form.
