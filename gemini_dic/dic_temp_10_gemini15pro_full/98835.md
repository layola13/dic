intelligentiary: /ɪnˌtɛlɪˈdʒɛntʃiəri/
n. |知识分子；知识阶层|The intelligentsia often plays a crucial role in social and political movements. 知识分子通常在社会和政治运动中扮演着至关重要的角色。|The intelligentsia were critical of the government's policies. 知识阶层批评政府的政策。|近义词: intellectuals, educated class|反义词: masses, common people|词性: noun

Etymology:  From Russian интеллигенция (intelligentsiya), from Latin intelligentia ("understanding, knowledge").
