epiphysial: /ˌɛpɪˈfɪziəl/ /ˌɛpɪˈfɪsiəl/ | adj. |骺的，骨骺的 | Relating to the epiphysis, the end part of a long bone, initially growing separately from the shaft. | The epiphysial plate is a hyaline cartilage plate in the metaphysis at each end of a long bone. 骨骺板是位于长骨两端干骺端的一块透明软骨板。| The doctor examined the child's epiphysial growth. 医生检查了孩子的骨骺生长。| 近义词:  growth plate, physis | 反义词: diaphysial | 词性: adjective


epiphyseal: /ˌɛpɪˈfɪziəl/ /ˌɛpɪˈfɪsiəl/ | n. |骨骺 | The end part of a long bone, initially growing separately from the shaft. | Damage to the epiphyseal can stunt growth. 对骨骺的损害会阻碍生长。| The epiphyseal fuses with the diaphysis during puberty. 骨骺在青春期与骨干融合。| 近义词:  physis, growth plate | 反义词: diaphysis | 词性: noun
