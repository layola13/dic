cratered:/ˈkreɪtərd/| adj.|布满陨石坑的；遭到严重破坏的|Covered in craters (like the surface of the moon); severely damaged or ruined.|The moon's surface is heavily cratered. 月球表面布满了陨石坑。|The company's stock price cratered after the scandal.  公司股价在丑闻曝光后暴跌。|Synonyms: pitted, scarred, ravaged, devastated|Antonyms: smooth, pristine, intact|adjective

cratered:/ˈkreɪtərd/| v.|形成坑；严重破坏|Form craters in (something); severely damage or ruin.|The asteroid cratered the planet's surface. 小行星在行星表面形成了陨石坑。|The recession cratered the housing market. 经济衰退重创了房地产市场。|Synonyms:  pit, scar, ravage, devastate|Antonyms:  repair, restore, improve|verb
