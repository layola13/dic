cowitch:/ˈkoʊwɪtʃ/| n.|共同女巫，一起施法的女巫|A woman who practices witchcraft with another woman or group of women.  It can also be used figuratively to describe close female friends who share a strong bond and engage in activities together, often with a slightly mischievous or playful connotation.|例句：The two cowitches brewed a powerful potion together. 这两个女巫一起调制了一种强大的药水。

例句：They were like cowitches, giggling and sharing secrets late into the night. 她们就像一起施法的女巫，咯咯地笑着，深夜分享秘密。|近义词：companion, friend, accomplice, collaborator|反义词:enemy, adversary|词性:名词
