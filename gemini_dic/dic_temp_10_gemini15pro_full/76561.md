galleryless:/ˈɡæləriˌləs/
adjective|无画廊的；没有展览馆的|Lacking an art gallery or galleries; without a gallery.
The small town was galleryless, much to the disappointment of the visiting artist.|这个小镇没有画廊，这令来访的艺术家很失望。
近义词: without galleries
反义词: with galleries
词性: adjective

词源: "gallery" + "-less"  "gallery" comes from Old French "galerie", ultimately from Medieval Latin "galeria". "-less" is a suffix meaning "without" or "lacking".
