paritor: /ˈpærɪtər/ | n. | 传唤员，传达员 | An officer of a court who serves summonses, warrants, etc. | Example: The paritor delivered the summons to the defendant. 传唤员将传票送达被告。

Example: The paritor was responsible for maintaining order in the courtroom.  传达员负责维持法庭秩序。


| 近义词: process server, bailiff, court officer | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun |

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *paritor, from Latin parere "to appear, obey," from Proto-Italic *pareiō, from Proto-Indo-European *pereh₁- "to produce, procure."  The sense evolution is from "one who makes someone appear (in court)" to "court officer."
