antirabies:/ˌæntiˈreɪbiːz/
adjective
抗狂犬病的
Preventing or curing rabies.
例句：The dog received antirabies injections after biting someone.  这只狗在咬了人之后注射了抗狂犬病疫苗。

例句：Antirabies serum is essential for treating a rabid animal bite. 抗狂犬病血清对于治疗被患有狂犬病的动物咬伤至关重要。

近义词：antirabic
反义词：(No direct antonym, but conceptually: rabies-promoting)
词性：adjective


Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposing (from Greek)
rabies:  (from Latin rabies, meaning "madness, rage")
