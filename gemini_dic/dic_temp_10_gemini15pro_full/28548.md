cardioinhibitory:/ˌkɑːrdioʊɪnˈhɪbɪtɔːri/
adjective
心脏抑制的
Relating to or causing the slowing or stopping of the heartbeat.
Cardioinhibitory drugs can be used to treat certain heart conditions. 心脏抑制药物可用于治疗某些心脏疾病。
Stimulation of the vagus nerve has a cardioinhibitory effect.  刺激迷走神经有抑制心脏的作用。
近义词: cardiodepressant, negative chronotropic
反义词: cardiostimulatory, positive chronotropic
词性: adjective
词源: cardio- (heart) + inhibitory (reducing or suppressing)
