coanchoring: /ˈkəʊtʃˌæŋkərɪŋ/ | n. | 联合指导; 共同指导; 协同指导 |  A collaborative approach to coaching where two or more coaches work together with a client.  | Example: Coanchoring allows for a broader perspective and more specialized support for the client. 联合指导可以为客户提供更广阔的视角和更专业的支持。

Example: The coanchoring session provided valuable insights from both coaches. 联合指导会议提供了来自两位教练的宝贵见解。 | 近义词: joint coaching, collaborative coaching, team coaching | 反义词: solo coaching, individual coaching | 词性: noun

词源:  A blend of "coach" and "anchoring." "Coach" refers to the act of guiding and supporting someone. "Anchoring" in this context refers to providing stability and support.
