briton:/ˈbrɪtn/| n.|英国人；不列颠人|A native or inhabitant of Great Britain.|例句：He is a Briton living in France. 他是一位住在法国的英国人。

例句：Many Britons enjoy a cup of tea in the afternoon. 许多英国人喜欢在下午喝杯茶。|近义词：Britisher, Englishman, Englishwoman (if referring to someone from England specifically)|反义词:foreigner|Noun
