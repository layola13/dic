nonpervasiveness:/ˌnɒnpərˈveɪsɪvnəs/
n.|非普遍性，非渗透性|The nonpervasiveness of the disease means it is contained within a specific area and not widespread.|这种疾病的非普遍性意味着它被控制在特定区域内，并未广泛传播。|
Example 1: The nonpervasiveness of internet access in rural areas is a major obstacle to development. 农村地区互联网接入的非普遍性是发展的主要障碍。

Example 2:  The study highlighted the nonpervasiveness of the belief system, showing it was limited to a small group. 这项研究强调了这种信仰体系的非普遍性，表明它仅限于一小部分人。
|近义词：localization, confinement, rarity, scarcity|反义词：pervasiveness, ubiquity, prevalence, universality|词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- (prefix meaning "not") + pervasive + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  Pervasive comes from Latin pervadere "to pass through, permeate," from per- "through" + vadere "to go."
