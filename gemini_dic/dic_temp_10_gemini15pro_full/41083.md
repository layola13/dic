coppery:/ˈkɒpəri/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpəri/ (US)| adj.|铜色的，含铜的，铜臭味的|Resembling copper in color, containing copper, or tasting of copper.|The sunset painted the sky in coppery hues.|近义词：bronze, brassy, copper-colored|反义词:silvery, golden|词性:adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From copper + -y (suffix forming adjectives meaning 'full of' or 'like'). Copper comes from Old English coper, from Late Latin cuprum, from Latin Cyprium 'metal of Cyprus', because copper was originally mined in Cyprus.
