discolith:/ˈdɪskəˌlɪθ/
n.
盘石，圆盘状结石
A small, rounded, calcareous body found in certain fossil algae.
Discoliths are found in marine sediments and can be used to reconstruct past environments. 盘石在海洋沉积物中被发现，可以用来重建过去的环境。
Discoliths are important microfossils. 盘石是重要的微化石。
Synonyms: coccolith (更常用)
Antonyms: N/A
