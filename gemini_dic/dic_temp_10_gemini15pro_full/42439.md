counterdemonstrated:/ˌkaʊntərˈdɛmənˌstreɪtɪd/
verb
举行对抗性示威；反驳
to demonstrate in opposition to another demonstration; to refute by demonstration
例句：The group counterdemonstrated against the proposed cuts to education funding.  该团体举行示威，反对提议削减教育经费。

例句：The lawyer counterdemonstrated the witness's testimony by presenting contradictory evidence.  律师通过出示矛盾的证据反驳了证人的证词。

近义词：protest, oppose, refute, rebut
反义词：support, endorse, confirm
