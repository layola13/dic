offing's:/ˈɔːfɪŋz/
n.
近海，即将发生的事情
the more distant part of the sea that can be seen from the shore, or the future
例句：There are big changes in the offing.  即将发生重大变化。

例句：We saw several ships in the offing. 我们看到几艘船在近海。
近义词：future, horizon, vista
反义词:past
词性:名词
