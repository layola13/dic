breathiest:/ˈbriːθiɪst/| adj.|最轻柔的，最气喘吁吁的|Having the quality of breath or breathing, often in a soft or gentle way; also, characterized by shortness of breath.|Her breathiest whisper sent shivers down his spine. 她轻柔的耳语让他脊背发凉。|Synonyms: airy, light, soft, whispery|Antonyms: loud, strong, heavy|adjective

breathiest:/ˈbriːθiɪst/| adj.|最喘不过气的|Characterized by labored or difficult breathing.|After running the marathon, he gave a breathiest account of the race. 跑完马拉松后，他气喘吁吁地讲述了比赛的情况。|Synonyms: gasping, panting, winded|Antonyms: calm, composed, relaxed|adjective
