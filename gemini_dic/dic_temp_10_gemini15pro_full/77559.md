geanticlinal:/ˌdʒiːˌæntiˈklaɪnəl/| adj. n.|地背斜的，地背斜|A geanticline is a large-scale, elongated upwarp in the Earth's crust.  It's essentially a very large anticline.  Think of it as a broad arch-like structure in the rock layers.|The Appalachian Mountains are a classic example of a geanticline. 阿巴拉契亚山脉是地背斜的典型例子。

The geanticlinal ridge formed a natural barrier.  地背斜山脊形成了天然屏障。|近义词：anticlinorium, upwarp, arch|反义词:syncline, downwarp|词性:adjective, noun
