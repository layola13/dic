archia:/ˈɑːrkiə/| n. |统治；支配；政府|Rule; dominion; government.  From Greek *arkhiā*, from *arkhos* "chief, leader."  Originally denoted the office or rule of an archon, a chief magistrate in ancient Greece. Later broadened to refer to rule or government in general.|The king's archia extended over the entire kingdom. 国王的统治遍及整个王国。|Synonyms:  reign, sovereignty, dominion, authority|Antonyms: anarchy, rebellion, disorder|noun
