idolizing:/ˈaɪdəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
崇拜；把…当作偶像
Adoring or revering someone or something excessively.
She was idolizing her older brother. 她非常崇拜她的哥哥。
Synonyms: worshipping, adoring, revering, venerating
Antonyms: despising, loathing, scorning
verb

idolize:/ˈaɪdəˌlaɪz/
verb
崇拜；把…当作偶像
To adore or revere someone or something excessively.
He idolized his father. 他崇拜他的父亲。
Synonyms: worship, adore, revere, venerate
Antonyms: despise, loathe, scorn
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From idol + -ize.  "Idol" comes from the Old French "idole," ultimately from the Greek "eidolon" meaning "image" or "phantom."
