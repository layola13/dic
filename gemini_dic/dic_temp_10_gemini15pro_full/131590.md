noncompeting:/ˌnɑːnkəmˈpiːtɪŋ/
| adj. |非竞争性的，非相互竞争的|Not competing; not involved in competition with another or others.|例句：Noncompeting groups often coexist peacefully. 非竞争群体通常和平共处。|近义词：noncompetitive, independent, separate|反义词：competing, rival, competitive|词性:形容词

noncompeting:/ˌnɑːnkəmˈpiːtɪŋ/
| n. |非竞争性|The state or quality of not competing.|例句：The noncompeting nature of the two businesses allowed them to thrive. 这两家企业非竞争性的性质使它们得以蓬勃发展。|近义词：noncompetition, independence|反义词：competition, rivalry|词性:名词
