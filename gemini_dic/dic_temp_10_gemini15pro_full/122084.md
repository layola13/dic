misthrows:/ˌmɪsˈθroʊz/
noun
投掷失误，错掷
An act of throwing something incorrectly or inaccurately.
The quarterback's misthrows cost them the game.  四分卫的投掷失误让他们输了比赛。
miscasts, misplays, blunders
accurate throws, successful throws
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (meaning "wrong" or "bad") + throw (meaning "to propel something through the air").
