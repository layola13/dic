dictatrix:/dɪkˈteɪtrɪks/
n.
女独裁者; 女专横者
a female dictator; a woman who behaves in a domineering or tyrannical way.
例句：The dictatrix ruled the small island nation with an iron fist.  这位女独裁者以铁腕统治着这个小岛国。

例句：Her colleagues described her as a dictatrix, micromanaging every aspect of their work.  她的同事形容她是一个女暴君，对他们工作的方方面面都进行微观管理。

近义词：autocrat, tyrant, despot
反义词：democrat
词性：noun
