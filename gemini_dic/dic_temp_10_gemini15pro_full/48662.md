defensive:/dɪˈfɛnsɪv/| adj.|防御的，防守的，保护的; 自卫的，戒备的; 辩护的，申辩的|Protecting or defending against attack; used or intended to protect someone or something against attack.  Also describes a behavior adopted to protect oneself from criticism or attack.|例句：The team adopted a defensive posture.  球队采取了守势。|近义词：protective, guarded, shielding|反义词：offensive, aggressive|词性:形容词

defensive:/dɪˈfɛnsɪv/| n.|守势，防御|The act of defending or protecting something or someone.|例句：Their defensiveness was understandable given the circumstances. 他们的戒备心理在当时的情况下是可以理解的。|近义词：protection, guard|反义词：offense, attack|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French defensif, -ive, from late Latin defensivus, from Latin defensus, past participle of defendere (see defend).
