integument:/ɪnˈtɛɡjʊmənt/| n. |外皮，覆盖物|A natural covering, such as skin, a shell, or a rind.|The insect's integument was shiny and hard. 这种昆虫的外皮又亮又硬。|Synonyms: covering, skin, rind, shell, sheath|Antonyms: interior, inside|Noun

integument:/ɪnˈtɛɡjʊmənt/| n. |（植物的）珠被|The natural outer covering of an ovule.|The integument of the ovule develops into the seed coat. 胚珠的珠被发育成种皮。|Synonyms: covering, layer|Antonyms: interior, inside|Noun
