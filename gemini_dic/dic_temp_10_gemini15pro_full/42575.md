counterpicketing:/ˌkaʊntərˈpɪkɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 反纠察，对峙纠察 | The act of picketing in opposition to another picket line.  It involves a group protesting against the initial protesters. |  The union organized a counterpicketing effort against the management's lockout. 工会组织了反纠察活动来对抗管理层的停工。 | Synonyms: counter-protest, counter-demonstration | Antonyms: picketing | noun
