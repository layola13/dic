comporting:/kəmˈpɔːrtɪŋ/
verb
表现；举止；行为
behaving; conducting oneself
He comported himself with dignity throughout the trial.  他在整个审判过程中都表现得很有尊严。
Synonyms: behaving, conducting, bearing, demeaning
Antonyms: misbehaving, acting out
