nookiest:/ˈnuːkiɪst/| adj.|最隐蔽的，最舒适的|Superlative form of nooky: most secluded, most cozy and comfortable.  It often implies a sense of privacy and intimacy.|The library has a nookiest corner where I love to read. 图书馆里有一个最隐蔽的角落，我喜欢在那里看书。|Synonyms: coziest, snuggest, most secluded, most private|Antonyms: most exposed, most public|Adjective
