antithetical:/ˌæntiˈθɛtɪk(ə)l/
adjective
对立的；相反的
Directly opposed or contrasted; mutually incompatible.
His views are antithetical to mine. 他的观点与我的观点截然相反。

antithetical:/ˌæntiˈθɛtɪk(ə)l/
adjective
正相反的
Sharply contrasted in character or purpose.
The antithetical forces of good and evil 善与恶的对立力量

antithetical:/ˌæntiˈθɛtɪk(ə)l/
adjective
对偶的
Being in antithesis; constituting or exhibiting opposition or contrast.
antithetical arguments 对偶的论点


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *antitheticus, from Ancient Greek ἀντιθετικός (antithetikós, “placed in opposition”), from ἀντίθετος (antíthetos, “opposite, opposed”), from ἀντί (antí, “against, opposite”) + τίθημι (títhēmi, “I put, place”).

SYNONYMS->
opposite, contrary, contrasting, conflicting, incompatible, irreconcilable, diametrically opposed, polar opposite

ANTONYMS->
similar, alike, identical, same, consistent, compatible, harmonious, congruent
