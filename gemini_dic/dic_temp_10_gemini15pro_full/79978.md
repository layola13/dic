go-cart: /ˈɡoʊˌkɑːrt/ (US) /ˈɡəʊˌkɑːt/ (UK)| n. |卡丁车；小型赛车|A small, low-powered racing car.|例句：He loves racing go-carts on the weekends.  他喜欢在周末开卡丁车比赛。

例句：The children were thrilled to ride the go-carts at the amusement park. 孩子们很兴奋能在游乐园里开卡丁车。

近义词：kart, mini-kart|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider larger racing vehicles like "race car", "Formula One car")|词性:noun


go-carts: /ˈɡoʊˌkɑːrts/ (US) /ˈɡəʊˌkɑːts/ (UK)| n. |卡丁车（复数）|Plural of go-cart.|例句：They bought two new go-carts for the track. 他们为赛道买了两个新的卡丁车。

例句：The go-carts were lined up ready for the race to begin.  卡丁车都排好了队，准备开始比赛。


近义词：karts, mini-karts|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun


词源:  "Go" + "cart". "Cart" comes from Old North French "cart" and ultimately from Latin "carrus". "Go" is from Old English "gān". The combination refers to a small cart that can go.
