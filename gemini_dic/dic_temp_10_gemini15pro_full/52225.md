diatomicity:/ˌdaɪətəˈmɪsɪti/
n.
二原子性
The state or quality of being diatomic.  A diatomic molecule is composed of two atoms, of the same or different chemical elements.
Oxygen exhibits diatomicity. 氧气呈现二原子性。
近义词: diatomic character
反义词: polyatomicity
词性: noun

diatomic:/ˌdaɪəˈtɒmɪk/
adj.
二原子的
Consisting of two atoms.
Oxygen is a diatomic molecule. 氧气是一个二原子分子。
近义词: biatomic
反义词: polyatomic
词性: adjective
