interneural:/ˌɪntərˈnjʊərəl/
adjective
神经间的；神经元之间的
Occurring between neurons.  Relating to the communication or interaction between nerve cells.
The interneural connections are complex and not fully understood. 神经元之间的连接很复杂，人们尚未完全理解。
Interneural communication is essential for proper brain function. 神经元之间的交流对大脑的正常功能至关重要。
近义词: interneuronal
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "intramural" within a single neuron)
词性: adjective
