inconsiderately:/ˌɪnkənˈsɪdərətli/| adv.|不体贴地，欠考虑地|In a manner that fails to take into account the feelings or needs of others; thoughtlessly. | She inconsiderately parked her car, blocking the driveway. 她停车太不体贴了，把车道堵住了。| He inconsiderately interrupted the speaker mid-sentence. 他不体贴地打断了演讲者的话。 | 近义词:thoughtlessly, rudely, selfishly, carelessly | 反义词:considerately, thoughtfully, politely, respectfully | 词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + considerate + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Considerate comes from Latin considerare "to look at closely, contemplate," from com- "together" + sidus "star, constellation."  Originally, the word implied careful observation, as if studying the stars.
