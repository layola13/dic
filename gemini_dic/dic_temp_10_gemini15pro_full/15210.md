backdate:/ˌbækˈdeɪt/ | n. | 倒填日期 | An instance of assigning an earlier date to something. | The backdate on the check raised suspicions.  支票上的倒填日期引起了怀疑。| There's evidence of a backdate on this contract. 有证据表明这份合同的日期被倒填了。| predating, antedating | postdating | noun
