coeternal:/ˌkoʊɪˈtɜːrnl/
adjective
共同永恒的；与…同永恒的
Existing or enduring from the beginning of time with something else, especially God.
例句：The doctrine of the Trinity holds that the Father, Son, and Holy Spirit are coeternal and consubstantial.  三位一体的教义认为圣父、圣子和圣灵是同永恒且同体的。

例句：Some philosophers have argued that the universe is coeternal with God. 一些哲学家认为宇宙与上帝同永恒。
近义词：everlasting, eternal, sempiternal
反义词：temporal, finite, transient
词性：adjective

coeternal:/ˌkoʊɪˈtɜːrnl/
noun
同永恒者; 与…同永恒之物
Something that is coeternal with something else.
例句: In some philosophies, the concept of a coeternal principle of evil exists alongside the good. 在某些哲学中，与善同在的永恒邪恶原则的概念是存在的。

例句:  The idea of a coeternal universe challenges the concept of creation ex nihilo. 同永恒宇宙的概念挑战了从无到有的创造概念。
近义词：coexistent
反义词：N/A
词性：noun


Etymology:
From Late Latin *coæternus, from co- + æternus (“eternal”).  来源于晚期拉丁语 *coæternus，由 co- + æternus（“永恒的”）组成。
