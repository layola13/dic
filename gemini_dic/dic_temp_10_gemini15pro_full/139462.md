obstetric:/əbˈstet.rɪk/ /ɑːbˈstet.rɪk/
adjective
产科的; 助产的
Relating to childbirth and the processes associated with it.
例句：She specialized in obstetric nursing. 她专攻产科护理。

例句：The hospital has a dedicated obstetric ward. 这家医院有一个专门的产科病房。

近义词：midwifery, parturient, childbirth
反义词：(None -  lacks a direct antonym; consider "pediatric" for contrast in age focus)
词性：adjective


obstetrics:/əbˈstet.rɪks/ /ɑːbˈstet.rɪks/
noun
产科学; 助产术
The branch of medicine and surgery concerned with childbirth and the care of women giving birth.
例句：She's studying obstetrics and gynecology. 她在学习妇产科。

例句：Advances in obstetrics have significantly reduced maternal mortality rates. 产科学的进步显著降低了产妇死亡率。


近义词：midwifery, tocology
反义词：(None - lacks a direct antonym;  doesn't have a clear opposite field of study.)
词性：noun
