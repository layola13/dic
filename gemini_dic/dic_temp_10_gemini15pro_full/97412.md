infringes:/ɪnˈfrɪndʒɪz/
verb
侵犯，违反
act so as to limit or undermine (something); encroach on.
His behavior infringes the rules of the club. 他的行为违反了俱乐部的规定。
synonyms: violate, breach, break, contravene, disobey, defy
antonyms: comply with, obey, uphold, observe
词性: 动词

infringement:/ɪnˈfrɪndʒmənt/
noun
侵犯，违反
the action of breaking the terms of a law, agreement, etc.; violation.
Copyright infringement is a serious offense.  版权侵权是一种严重的违法行为。
synonyms: violation, breach, contravention, transgression, offense
antonyms: compliance, obedience, observance
词性: 名词
