over-articulate: /ˌoʊvər ɑːrˈtɪkjʊleɪt/ (American) /ˌəʊvər ɑːˈtɪkjʊleɪt/ (British)| adj. v.|过度清晰发音的；过度阐述的|Speaking or enunciating with excessive clarity, often to an unnatural or affected degree; to express (something) too clearly or too elaborately.|She over-articulates her words when she's nervous, making her sound unnatural. 她紧张的时候会过度清晰地发音，听起来很不自然。|近义词：precise, meticulous, labored|反义词：mumbled, slurred|词性:形容词/动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "articulate" (from Latin "articulatus," past participle of "articulare" meaning "to utter distinctly, joint").
