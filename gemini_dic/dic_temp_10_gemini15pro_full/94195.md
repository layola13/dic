imperceptible:/ˌɪmpərˈseptɪbl/ | adj. | 难以察觉的；感觉不到的 | Not perceptible by a sense or by the mind : extremely slight, gradual, or subtle | The change in color was almost imperceptible.  颜色变化几乎难以察觉。|  例句：The difference in taste is imperceptible to me.  我感觉不出味道上的差别。

例句：The ship moved with an imperceptible drift. 船几乎难以察觉地漂流着。

近义词：unnoticeable, undetectable, faint, subtle | 反义词：perceptible, noticeable, obvious, apparent | 词性：形容词
