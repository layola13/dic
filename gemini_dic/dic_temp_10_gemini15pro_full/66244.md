exclamatoriness:/ɪksˌklæməˈtɔːrɪnɪs/
n.
感叹; 惊叹
the quality of being exclamatory; the use of exclamations.
Her exclamatoriness betrayed her surprise. 她的惊叹暴露出她的惊讶。
excitability, vehemence,  effusiveness
calmness, quietness, reserve
noun
