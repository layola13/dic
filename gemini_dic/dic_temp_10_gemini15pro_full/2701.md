ads:/ædz/
|n.|广告|Short for advertisements, a notice or announcement in a public medium promoting a product, service, or event.|We ran ads in the local newspaper. 我们在当地报纸上刊登了广告。|synonyms: advertisements, commercials, promotions, publicity, marketing|antonyms: N/A|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
Abbreviation of "advertisements."  The word "advertisement" comes from Old French "advertissement," from "advertir" meaning "to warn, inform," from Latin "advertere," meaning "to turn towards."

USAGE->
Plural form as a noun:

The ads on television are getting longer. = 电视上的广告越来越长了。

The company spent a lot of money on ads in magazines and newspapers. = 这家公司在杂志和报纸广告上花了很多钱。


PHRASE->
run ads = 刊登广告
place ads = 投放广告
online ads = 在线广告
targeted ads = 定向广告
pop-up ads = 弹出广告
