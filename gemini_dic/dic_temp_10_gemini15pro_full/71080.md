fiscality:/fɪsˈkælɪti/
n.

The fiscality of the country is complex and often criticized.  这个国家的财政状况复杂且经常受到批评。
The new government promised to simplify the country's fiscality. 新政府承诺要简化国家的税收制度。
近义词：taxation, revenue system, public finance
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider concepts like "tax evasion" or "privatization")
