disguised:/dɪsˈɡaɪzd/| adj.|伪装的，乔装的|Having an appearance that hides the true form or identity.|The police officer went into the building disguised as a janitor. 警察乔装成看门人进入了大楼。|近义词：camouflaged, masked, concealed, hidden|反义词：revealed, exposed, overt, obvious|词性:形容词
词源: From Old French *desguisier "to change the fashion of (clothes); to disguise," from des- "opposite of" (see dis-) + guise "manner, fashion," from Frankish *wisa "manner, fashion."
