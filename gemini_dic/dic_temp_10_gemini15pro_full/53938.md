discous:/ˈdɪskəs/| adj.|盘状的，圆形的，扁平的|Shaped like a disc; flat and circular.|The discus fish has a discous body.  七彩神仙鱼有一个圆盘状的身体。|例句：The seeds are discous and winged. 这些种子是圆盘状的，有翅的。

例句：The plant has discous leaves. 这种植物有圆盘状的叶子。|近义词：circular, round, flat|反义词：spherical, globular|词性：形容词

词源: From Latin *discus* "disk, quoit," from Greek *diskos* "disk, platter, quoit," from *dikein* "to throw."  来自拉丁语 *discus* “圆盘，铁饼”，源自希腊语 *diskos* “圆盘，盘子，铁饼”，来自 *dikein* “投掷”。
