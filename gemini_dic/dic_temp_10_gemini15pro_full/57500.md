词源:
From Middle English *draglen, frequentative of dragen (“to drag”). Cognate with Dutch dragen (“to carry, to wear”), German tragen (“to carry, to wear”), Swedish draga (“to draw, to pull”). Related to drag.
来源于中古英语 *draglen，是 dragen（“拖”）的重复形式。与荷兰语 dragen（“携带，穿着”）、德语 tragen（“携带，穿着”）、瑞典语 draga（“拉，拽”）同源。与 drag 相关。
