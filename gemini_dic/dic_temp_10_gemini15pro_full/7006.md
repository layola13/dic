androgyny:/ænˈdrɒdʒɪni/ | n. |雌雄同体，阴阳同体|The state of having both male and female characteristics, or having an appearance that does not clearly look male or female.|例句：The fashion model cultivated a look of androgyny.  这位时装模特培养了一种雌雄同体的造型。

例句：The androgynous figure in the painting was both compelling and mysterious.  画中雌雄莫辨的人物既引人注目又神秘。|近义词：unisex, epicene, hermaphroditism|反义词：masculinity, femininity|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *androgynus, from Ancient Greek ἀνδρόγυνος (andrógunos, “hermaphrodite”), from ἀνήρ (anḗr, “man”) + γυνή (gunḗ, “woman”).
