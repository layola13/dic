dewdrops:/ˈdjuːdrɒps/ /ˈduːdrɑːps/| n.|露珠|Droplets of water that condense on cool surfaces, especially plants, at night.|The dewdrops on the grass sparkled in the morning sun.  清晨的阳光下，草坪上的露珠闪闪发光。|Synonyms: droplets, beads, pearls|Antonyms: dryness, aridity|noun

dewdrops:/ˈdjuːdrɒps/ /ˈduːdrɑːps/| n.|露珠|Small drops of water formed by condensation on cool surfaces at night.|The spider web was covered in tiny dewdrops.  蜘蛛网上布满了细小的露珠。|Synonyms: water droplets, condensation|Antonyms: vapor, steam|noun
