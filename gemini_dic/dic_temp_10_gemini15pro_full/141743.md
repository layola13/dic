orangeism:/ˈɔːrɪndʒɪzəm/ | n. |橙衣主义，奥兰治主义 |A Protestant political movement, particularly strong in Northern Ireland, upholding the Protestant ascendancy and supporting the union of Great Britain and Northern Ireland. It is named after William of Orange, who became William III of England. |例句：Orangeism played a significant role in the history of Northern Ireland. 橙衣主义在北爱尔兰历史上扮演了重要角色。
