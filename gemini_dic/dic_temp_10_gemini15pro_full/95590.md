incommensurably:/ˌɪnkəmˈɛnʃərəbli/ | adv. | 不可估量地，无比地，不能比较地 |  Not capable of being measured or compared, especially because of vast difference in size or degree.  |  His contribution to the project was incommensurably greater than anyone else's.  他對這個項目的貢獻比任何人都要大得多，簡直無法估量。 | Synonyms: immensely, infinitely, vastly, beyond measure | Antonyms: measurably, comparably, slightly | Adverb

incommensurate:/ˌɪnkəmˈɛnʃərət/ | adj. | 不相称的，不相应的，不能相比的 | Not of equal size, extent, or duration; disproportionate; not commensurate.  |  Her salary was incommensurate with her experience. 她的薪水與她的經驗不相稱。 | Synonyms: disproportionate, inadequate, unequal, unsuitable | Antonyms: commensurate, proportionate, adequate, suitable | Adjective
