bowenite:/ˈboʊənaɪt/| n.|鲍文石|A massive, compact, translucent variety of serpentine, usually light to dark apple green, sometimes bluish green, and often mottled or veined, used as an ornamental stone and for carvings.  It is a type of nephrite jade.|The museum's collection boasts a beautiful bowenite carving of a dragon.|近义词：nephrite, jade|反义词: N/A|名词
