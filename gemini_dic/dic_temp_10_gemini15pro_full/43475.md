crassitude:/kræˈsɪtjuːd/
n.
愚钝；粗俗；愚蠢
The state of being crass, or the quality of crassness; grossness; stupidity; insensitivity.
His crassitude was evident in his loud, insensitive remarks. 他的愚蠢表现在他大声、麻木不仁的言论中。
近义词：crassness, insensitivity, stupidity, vulgarity, boorishness
反义词：sensitivity, refinement, intelligence, sophistication, politeness
词性：名词

Etymology:
From Latin *crassitūdō,* from crassus (“thick, fat, gross”).
