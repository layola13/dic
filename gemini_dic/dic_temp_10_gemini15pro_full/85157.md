hardtops:/ˈhɑːrdtɒps/ (US) /ˈhɑːrdtɒps/ (UK)| n.|硬顶汽车；硬顶|A car with a rigid, non-removable roof.|Example: He traded in his convertible for a hardtop. 他把敞篷车换成了一辆硬顶车。|Example:  Hardtops were popular in the 1950s and 60s.  硬顶汽车在20世纪50年代和60年代很流行。|近义词：coupe, sedan|反义词:convertible, roadster|词性:noun

hardtop:/ˈhɑːrdtɒp/ (US) /ˈhɑːrdtɒp/ (UK)| adj.|硬顶的|Having a rigid, non-removable roof.|Example:  She prefers the sleek look of a hardtop car. 她更喜欢硬顶汽车流畅的外观。|Example: The hardtop version of the sports car was more expensive. 这款跑车的硬顶版本更贵。|近义词：fixed-roof|反义词:convertible, soft-top|词性:adjective
