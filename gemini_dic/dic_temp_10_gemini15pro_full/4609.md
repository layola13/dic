alcyonium:/ælˈsaɪəʊniəm/
n.|软珊瑚属|A genus of soft corals in the family Alcyoniidae.|Divers observed various species of alcyonium on the reef. 潜水员在珊瑚礁上观察到各种各样的软珊瑚属。|Synonyms: soft coral, dead man's fingers|Antonyms: hard coral
Etymology: From Ancient Greek ἀλκυόνιον (alkuónion), diminutive of ἀλκυών (alkuṓn, “kingfisher”).  The name likely refers to the perceived resemblance of some species to a kingfisher's nest.
