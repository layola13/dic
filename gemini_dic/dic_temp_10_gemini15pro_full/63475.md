entropy: /ˈɛntrəpi/ | n. |熵，混乱度|A thermodynamic quantity representing the unavailability of a system's thermal energy for conversion into mechanical work, often interpreted as the degree of disorder or randomness in the system.|The entropy of the universe tends to a maximum. 宇宙的熵趋于最大。|Synonyms: disorder, randomness, chaos|Antonyms: order, organization, structure|noun

entropy: /ˈɛntrəpi/ | n. |(比喻)混乱，无序|A lack of order or predictability; gradual decline into disorder.|The political system is in a state of increasing entropy. 政治体系正处于日益混乱的状态。|Synonyms: disorganization, confusion, chaos|Antonyms: order, organization, structure|noun
