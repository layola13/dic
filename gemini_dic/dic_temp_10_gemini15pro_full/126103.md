mutation:/mjuːˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 突变，变异 | A change in the DNA sequence of an organism. This can lead to changes in the organism's characteristics. |  The mutation caused the plant to produce white flowers instead of red.  这种突变导致植物开出白花而不是红花。| synonym: alteration, variation, modification | antonym: stability, consistency | noun

mutation:/mjuːˈteɪʃn/ | n. | 变异，转变 | A significant change or alteration in form, nature, or quality. | The rapid mutation of the virus made it difficult to develop a vaccine. 病毒的快速变异使得开发疫苗变得困难。 | synonym: transformation, metamorphosis, evolution | antonym: stagnation, fixity | noun
