chemosynthesis:/ˌkɛmoʊˈsɪnθəsɪs/| n. |化学合成|The process by which some organisms, such as certain bacteria, use chemical energy to produce carbohydrates.|例句：Bacteria near hydrothermal vents use chemosynthesis to convert chemicals into energy.  深海热泉附近的细菌利用化学合成将化学物质转化为能量。

例句：Chemosynthesis plays a crucial role in sustaining life in deep-sea ecosystems. 化学合成在维持深海生态系统生命中起着至关重要的作用。|近义词：biosynthesis|反义词: - |词性: noun
