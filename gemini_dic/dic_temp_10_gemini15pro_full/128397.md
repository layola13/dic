newbie's:/ˈnjuːbiz/ | n. | 新手的（东西）|  Something that belongs to or is associated with a newbie (a beginner or novice). | This forum is full of newbie's questions.  这个论坛充满了新手的问题。| 近义词: beginner's, novice's | 反义词: expert's, veteran's | 词性: noun (possessive form)

Etymology:
From newbie + -'s (possessive suffix).  "Newbie" is a relatively recent term, emerging in the late 20th century, likely derived from "new boy" or "new blood."
