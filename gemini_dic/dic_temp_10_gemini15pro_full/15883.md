balloon:/bəˈluːn/ (US), /bəˈluːn/ (UK)| n. vi.|气球；热气球；（价格或数量）飞涨；|A balloon is a bag made of thin rubber or other material that is filled with air or gas so that it floats in the air.  |例句：The child was playing with a red balloon.  孩子正在玩一个红色的气球。

|例句：Housing prices ballooned in recent years. 近年来房价飞涨。|近义词：blimp, dirigible (for airships)|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "deflate" as a verb)|noun, verb
