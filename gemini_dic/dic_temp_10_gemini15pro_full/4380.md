alagan: /ˈæləɡən/ | n. |泻药，通便剂|A medicine that stimulates evacuation of the bowels.|She took an alagan to relieve her constipation. 她服用了泻药来缓解便秘。|Synonyms: laxative, purgative, cathartic|Antonyms: antidiarrheal, constipating|noun

Etymology->
From Spanish *alagaña*, meaning "eye discharge," later used to refer to purgative substances.  Ultimately from Arabic *al-ʼaḍnāʼ* (الأذناء), meaning "the earwax." The semantic shift likely occurred due to the use of earwax as a folk remedy for constipation.
