decisiveness:/dɪˈsaɪsɪvnəs/| n.|果断，决断力|The quality of being able to make decisions quickly and effectively.  Being decisive means you can choose quickly and confidently.|Her decisiveness in a crisis was admirable.  她在危机中的果断令人钦佩。|determination, resolution, firmness|indecision, hesitation, vacillation|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From decisive + -ness.  Decisive comes from the Late Latin dēcīsīvus, from dēcīdere "to decide, determine," from de- "down, off" + caedere "to cut."
