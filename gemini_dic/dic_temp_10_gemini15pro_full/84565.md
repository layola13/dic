hammerlike:/ˈhæmərlaɪk/| adj.|锤状的，像锤子的|Resembling a hammer in shape or action. |The bird had a hammerlike beak. 这只鸟有一个像锤子一样的喙。|The carpenter delivered a hammerlike blow to the nail. 木匠用锤子般的力道敲击钉子。|近义词：malleiform, hammer-shaped|反义词: 无|词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From *hammer* + *-like*.  *Hammer* comes from Old English *hamer*, from Proto-Germanic *hamaraz*, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂ḱómōr̥*  (stone). *-like* is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling," from Old English *-līc*.
