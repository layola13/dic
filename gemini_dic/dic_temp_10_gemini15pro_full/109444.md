lepadoid:/ˈlɛpədɔɪd/| adj.| 茗荷状的，茗荷形的|Resembling or characteristic of a goose barnacle (lepas).  Covered with scales overlapping like those of a goose barnacle.|The lepadoid form of the barnacle allows it to attach to various surfaces. 茗荷状的藤壶形态使其能够附着在各种表面上。|scaly, barnacle-like| - |adjective
Etymology: From Greek *lepados* (genitive of *lepas* "limpet, barnacle") + *-oid* "resembling."
