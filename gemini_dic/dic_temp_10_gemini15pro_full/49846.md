demoralized: /dɪˈmɒrəlaɪzd/ (UK) /dɪˈmɔːrəlaɪzd/ (US)
adjective
士气低落的，沮丧的
Having lost confidence or hope; disheartened.
The team was demoralized after losing the championship game. 球队在输掉冠军赛后士气低落。
The constant criticism left him feeling demoralized.  不断的批评让他感到沮丧。

近义词: discouraged, disheartened, dejected, dispirited
反义词: encouraged, heartened, optimistic, confident
词性: adjective
