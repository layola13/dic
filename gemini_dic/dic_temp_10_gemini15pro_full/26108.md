busky:/ˈbʌski/| adj.|健壮的，茂密的|Of strong or sturdy build; bushy.  A busky beard. |He had a busky frame and a booming laugh. 他身材魁梧，笑声洪亮。|近义词：sturdy, robust, thickset, brawny|反义词：frail, weak, slender|词性:形容词

词源:  Likely a variant of "bosky," from Middle English *busk, *busch, meaning "bush,"  influenced by words like "husky." Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *buskaz.
