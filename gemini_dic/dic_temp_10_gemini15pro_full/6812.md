anarchist: /ˈænəkɪst/ | n. | 无政府主义者 | A person who believes in or tries to bring about anarchy.  A person who rebels against any authority, established order, or ruling power. |  例句：The anarchist threw a rock through the window of the bank.  无政府主义者向银行的窗户扔了一块石头。

| 例句：She was labeled an anarchist because of her radical views. 由于她激进的观点，她被贴上了无政府主义者的标签。| 近义词：rebel, revolutionary, insurgent, dissident | 反义词：conformist, traditionalist, authoritarian | 词性: noun

Etymology: From French *anarchiste*, from Ancient Greek *ἀναρχία* (anarkhía, “lack of a ruler, anarchy”), from *ἀν-* (an-, “without”) + *ἀρχός* (arkhós, “leader, ruler”).
