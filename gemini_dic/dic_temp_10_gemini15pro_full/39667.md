conservator:/kənˈsɜːrvətər/ (US), /kənˈsɜːvətə(r)/ (UK)| n.|保护者，管理员，文物修复师|A person who protects, preserves, or restores something, especially artworks, buildings, or other objects of cultural value.|The conservator carefully cleaned the ancient painting.|近义词：preserver, restorer, guardian, curator|反义词：destroyer, vandal|noun

conservator:/kənˈsɜːrvətər/ (US), /kənˈsɜːvətə(r)/ (UK)| n.| (法律) 保护人，监护人|A person appointed by a court to manage the affairs of someone who is legally incapable of doing so, such as a minor or someone with mental incapacities.|The court appointed a conservator to manage the elderly woman's finances.|近义词：guardian, trustee|反义词：ward|noun
