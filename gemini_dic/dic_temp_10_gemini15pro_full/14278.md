autobiographical:/ˌɔːtəˌbaɪəˈɡræfɪkəl/
adjective
自传式的；自传体的
Relating to or based on autobiography; dealing with the writer's own life.
Her latest novel is highly autobiographical. 她最新的小说是高度自传体的。
synonyms:  autographic, self-recorded, personal
antonyms: fictional, invented, imaginary
词性: 形容词
