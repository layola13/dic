excursioning:/ɪkˈskɜːrʒənɪŋ/
| n. |  远足，短途旅行 | The act of going on an excursion; making a short journey or trip, especially for pleasure. | They spent the day excursioning in the mountains. 他们在山上远足了一天。 | 近义词：touring,  traveling,  journeying | 反义词: staying, remaining | 词性:名词/动名词
Etymology: From the late 16th century: from French excursion, from late Latin excursio(n-), from the verb excurrere ‘run out’, from ex- ‘out’ + currere ‘to run’.
