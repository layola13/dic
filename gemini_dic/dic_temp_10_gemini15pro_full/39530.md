conquers:/ˈkɒŋkəz/ (UK) /ˈkɑːŋkərz/ (US)| v.|征服，克服，战胜|overcomes and takes control of (a place or people) by use of military force. Also, to successfully overcome (a problem or weakness).|Alexander the Great conquered many lands. 亚历山大大帝征服了许多土地。

She finally conquered her fear of public speaking. 她终于克服了对公开演讲的恐惧。|近义词：defeats, overcomes, subdues, vanquishes|反义词：surrenders, submits, loses, fails|词性:Verb (Third person singular present tense)

词源: From Old French *conquerre*, from Latin *conquirere* "to seek out, procure, collect, acquire, win," from *com-* "together" (see com-) + *quaerere* "to seek, gain" (see query (n.)).
