carpetbagger:/ˈkɑːrpɪtˌbæɡər/| n. |投机分子；外来政客；背包客|A person from the northern states who went to the South after the Civil War to profit from the Reconstruction.| Many carpetbaggers were accused of exploiting the South's postwar instability for their own gain. 许多投机分子被指控利用南方战后的不稳定性谋取私利。|近义词：opportunist, profiteer, speculator|反义词: native, local|noun

carpetbagger:/ˈkɑːrpɪtˌbæɡər/| n. |地毯袋|A traveling bag made of carpet fabric.|In the 19th century, carpetbags were a common type of luggage. 在19世纪，地毯袋是一种常见的行李。|近义词：luggage, bag, suitcase|反义词: None|noun
