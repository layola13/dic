pangaeas:/pænˈdʒiːəs/
n.
盘古大陆；泛大陆
A supercontinent comprising all the continental crust of the earth, postulated to have existed in late Paleozoic and Mesozoic times before it broke into Gondwana and Laurasia.
例句：Scientists believe that the continents were once joined together in a single landmass called Pangaea. 科学家们认为，各大洲曾经连接在一起，形成一个名为盘古大陆的单一陆块。

例句：The breakup of Pangaea led to the formation of the Atlantic Ocean. 盘古大陆的分裂导致了大西洋的形成。
近义词：supercontinent
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps dispersed continents)
词性: noun
