downlinked:/ˌdaʊnˈlɪŋkt/ | v. |下行链路传输，下传|To transmit (data) from a satellite or other spacecraft to a ground station.|The data was downlinked from the satellite to the control center. 数据从卫星下传到控制中心。|Synonyms: download, transmit, relay|Antonyms: upload, uplink|Verb

downlinked:/ˌdaʊnˈlɪŋkt/ | adj. |下行链路的|Transmitted from a satellite or other spacecraft to a ground station.|The downlinked images showed the extent of the damage. 下传的图像显示了损害的程度。|Synonyms: downloaded, transmitted, relayed|Antonyms: uploaded, uplinked|Adjective
