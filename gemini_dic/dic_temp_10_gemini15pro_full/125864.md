muscatels:/ˈmʌskəˌtɛlz/ | n. |麝香葡萄干；白葡萄干|A variety of grape, often dried to produce raisins, known for its sweet, musky aroma.  These grapes are typically smaller and sweeter than regular table grapes.|例句：She enjoyed a handful of muscatels with her cheese. 她和奶酪一起吃了一把麝香葡萄干。

例句：Muscatels are often used in fruitcakes and other baked goods. 麝香葡萄干经常用于制作水果蛋糕和其他烘焙食品。|近义词：raisins, sultanas, currants|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "fresh grapes" as a contrasting term) |词性:noun
