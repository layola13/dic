disinhibited:/dɪsɪnˈhɪbɪtɪd/
adjective
无拘无束的，不受拘束的
Lacking restraint or inhibition; free from social or psychological inhibitions.
After a few drinks, he became disinhibited and started talking loudly.
几杯酒下肚后，他变得无拘无束，开始大声说话。

He displayed disinhibited behaviour at the party.
他在派对上表现得无拘无束。

近义词:uninhibited, unrestrained, unconstrained, free, loose, wild
反义词:inhibited, restrained, reserved, shy, timid
词性:adjective
