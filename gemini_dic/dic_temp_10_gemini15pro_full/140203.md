officerly: /ˈɒfɪsərli/ (UK) /ˈɔːfɪsərli/ (US)
adjective
官员般的；有军官风度的；有官僚作风的
Resembling or characteristic of an officer, especially in being authoritative or officious.
He spoke in an officerly tone. 他以一种官员般的口吻说话。
近义词: authoritative, commanding, formal, officious
反义词: informal, casual, unassuming, humble
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From officer + -ly.  "Officer" comes from Old French "officier" from Medieval Latin "officiarius," meaning "official," related to "officium" meaning "duty, service."  The suffix "-ly" transforms a noun into an adjective.
