nonsolidest:/nɒnˈsɒlɪdɪst/
adjective
最不坚固的
least solid; most fluid or gaseous.  This is a superlative form of "nonsolid," meaning not solid.
The nonsolidest part of the mixture evaporated first.  混合物中最不坚固的部分首先蒸发了。
The nonsolidest form of water is steam. 水最不坚固的形式是蒸汽。
Near synonyms: most fluid, most gaseous, least firm, least dense
Opposite: solidest, firmest, densest
词性: adjective
