gozzard:/ˈɡɒzərd/| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A foolish or inept person. |He's such a gozzard, he locked his keys in the car again.|近义词：fool, idiot, dunce, nincompoop|反义词:genius, savant|词性:名词

Etymology:  The origin of "gozzard" is uncertain.  It may be related to "gawk" or "goose," both terms associated with foolishness. It is primarily a British dialect term.
