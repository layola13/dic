mechanizer:/ˈmɛkəˌnaɪzər/| n.|机械化者，机械化推广者|A person who mechanizes something, especially farming or industry.|The farm was transformed by the mechanizer who introduced modern equipment. 这个农场被引入现代设备的机械化推广者改变了。|近义词：automaton,  machine operator, technician|反义词:laborer, manual worker|noun

mechanize:/ˈmɛkəˌnaɪz/| vt.|使机械化，用机械装备|To equip with machinery, especially to replace human or animal labor.|The company mechanized its production line to increase efficiency.  这家公司对其生产线进行了机械化改造以提高效率。
They are mechanizing the coal mine. 他们在使煤矿机械化。|近义词：automate, motorize|反义词:humanize, manualize|verb
