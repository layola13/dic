outintrigue: /ˌaʊtɪnˈtriːɡ/
verb
以智取胜，在阴谋诡计上胜过
to surpass or defeat someone in intrigue or cunning
She outintrigued her rivals and won the promotion. 她以智取胜，战胜了竞争对手，赢得了晋升。
近义词: outmaneuver, outsmart, outwit
反义词: be outmaneuvered, be outsmarted, be outwitted
词性: v.


outintrigue: /ˌaʊtɪnˈtriːɡ/
词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "surpassing, exceeding") + "intrigue" (verb meaning "to arouse the curiosity or interest of by unusual, new, or otherwise fascinating or compelling qualities")
中文翻译: 以智取胜，在阴谋诡计上胜过
