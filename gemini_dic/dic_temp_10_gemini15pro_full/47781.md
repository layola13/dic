decanter:/dɪˈkæntər/| n. |醒酒器;  倾析器;  细颈瓶|A glass container with a stopper, into which wine or port is decanted for serving.|He poured the wine into a decanter. 他把酒倒进了一个醒酒器。|近义词：carafe, bottle|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 container - 容器，表示广义上的相反)|词性: 名词

decanter:/dɪˈkæntər/| vt. |将…轻轻倒出（尤指将酒从瓶中倒入醒酒器）|To pour (a liquid, especially wine) from one container into another, typically in order to separate it from sediment.|He carefully decanted the wine into a crystal decanter. 他小心翼翼地把酒倒进了一个水晶醒酒器。|近义词：pour, transfer|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|词性: 动词
