harle:/hɑːrl/| n.|丑角；滑稽角色；小丑|A comic, often masked, character in Italian commedia dell'arte.|He played the harle in the play and his performance was hilarious. 他在剧中扮演丑角，他的表演非常滑稽。|Synonyms: clown, jester, buffoon|Antonyms: serious character, tragic figure|Noun
