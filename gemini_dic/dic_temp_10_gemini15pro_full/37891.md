complaintful: /kəmˈpleɪntfʊl/ | adj. | 爱抱怨的，充满抱怨的 | Inclined to complain; expressing complaint. |  She had a complaintful attitude about everything. 她对每件事都抱有抱怨的态度。

He was complaintful about the long hours he had to work.  他抱怨他不得不长时间工作。 |  近义词: querulous, whiny, grumbling | 反义词: contented, satisfied, cheerful | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "complaint" + "-ful." "Complaint" comes from Old French "complainte,"  from Latin "plangere" (to lament).  "-ful" is a suffix meaning "full of" or "characterized by."
