dimissory:/dɪˈmɪsəri/
adjective
解雇的；准许离开的；遣散的
Granting leave to depart; pertaining to dismissal.
The bishop gave the priest a dimissory letter, allowing him to transfer to another diocese. 主教给了神父一封解雇信，允许他调到另一个教区。
Synonyms: releasing, discharging, dismissive
Antonyms: retaining, appointing, employing
词性: 形容词
