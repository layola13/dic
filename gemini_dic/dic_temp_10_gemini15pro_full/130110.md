non-purchase:/ˌnɒnˈpɜːrtʃəs/ /ˌnɑːnˈpɜːrtʃəs/| n.|非购买，未购买|The act of not buying something.  A product or service that was not bought.|例句：The customer's non-purchase was due to the high price. 顾客因为价格高而未购买。|例句：The marketing team analyzed the reasons for non-purchases.  市场营销团队分析了未购买的原因。|近义词：non-buying, abstention from purchase|反义词:purchase, buying|词性:noun

non-purchase:/ˌnɒnˈpɜːrtʃəs/ /ˌnɑːnˈpɜːrtʃəs/| adj.|非购买的|Relating to or characteristic of not buying something.|例句：The non-purchase behavior of consumers is an important area of study. 消费者的非购买行为是一个重要的研究领域。|例句：Non-purchase intentions can be influenced by various factors. 不购买意向会受到多种因素的影响。|近义词：unbought|反义词:purchased, bought|词性:adjective
