词源: "Frampel" is likely a coined word, possibly echoing words like "framble" (to grope about clumsily) or "ramble" (to wander aimlessly), with the added "p" perhaps suggesting playfulness or a popping sound.  It does not appear in standard dictionaries and likely originates from dialect or informal usage.
