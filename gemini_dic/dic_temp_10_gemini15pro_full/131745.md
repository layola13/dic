nonconformist:/ˌnɒnkənˈfɔːmɪst/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈfɔːrmɪst/
n.
不墨守成规的人；不遵循传统规范的人
a person who does not conform to prevailing ideas or established practice in behavior or thought.
例句：She was a nonconformist in her youth, rejecting traditional values. 她年轻时是个不墨守成规的人，拒绝传统价值观。

例句：He was considered a nonconformist because he refused to dress like everyone else. 他被认为是一个不墨守成规的人，因为他拒绝穿得像其他人一样。

近义词：dissenter, rebel, maverick, individualist, freethinker
反义词：conformist, traditionalist
词性：noun
