nonresponsively:/ˌnɒnrɪˈspɒnsɪvli/ /ˌnɑːnrɪˈspɑːnsɪvli/
adverb
无反应地；不回应地
in a way that shows no reaction or response to something.
The patient lay nonresponsively on the bed. 病人毫无反应地躺在床上。

She stared nonresponsively at the wall. 她毫无反应地盯着墙。

Synonym: unresponsively, inertly, passively
Antonym: responsively, actively, reactively
词性: 副词
