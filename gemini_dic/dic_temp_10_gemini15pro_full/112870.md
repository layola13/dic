lucriferous:/luːˈkrɪfərəs/
adjective
有利可图的；赚钱的
Producing or likely to produce profit; profitable.
This new business venture appears to be quite lucriferous.  这个新的商业项目看起来相当有利可图。
The lucriferous enterprise quickly expanded into new markets.  这家赚钱的企业迅速扩展到新的市场。
近义词: profitable, gainful, remunerative
反义词: unprofitable, loss-making
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *lucrifer*, from lucrum ("profit") + -fer ("bearing").
