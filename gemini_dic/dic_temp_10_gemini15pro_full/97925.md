innocuity:/ɪˈnɒkjuɪti/ /ɪˈnɑːkjuɪti/| n. |无害，无毒，无恶意|The innocuity of the substance was confirmed by laboratory tests. 该物质的无害性已由实验室测试证实。|例句：

The doctor assured me of the medicine's innocuity.
医生向我保证了这种药的无害性。

Despite its appearance, the spider's venom has surprising innocuity.
尽管外表如此，这种蜘蛛的毒液却出奇地无害。

|近义词：harmlessness, safety, innocence|反义词：harmfulness, toxicity, virulence|词性: noun


Etymology:
From Late Latin innocuitās, from innocuus ("harmless") + -itās.  Innocuus is from in- ("not") + nocuus ("harmful").  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *nek- ("to die, perish").
