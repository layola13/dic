dema:/ˈdiːmə/ | n. | Double Exponential Moving Average (双指数移动平均线) | A type of moving average used in technical analysis that is more responsive to recent price changes than a simple moving average. |  The dema is often used by traders to identify trends and potential buy or sell signals. | 近义词: TEMA (Triple Exponential Moving Average), EMA (Exponential Moving Average) | 反义词: SMA (Simple Moving Average) | 词性: noun
