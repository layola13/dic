discourse:/ˈdɪskɔːrs/ (US), /ˈdɪskɔːs/ (UK)| n. vi. | 讨论；演讲；论文 |  A formal and often lengthy discussion of a subject, either written or spoken.  | The professor delivered a discourse on the history of philosophy. 教授做了一个关于哲学史的演讲。| synonym: discussion, lecture, treatise, speech, sermon | antonym: silence, quiet, taciturnity | noun
discourse:/ˈdɪskɔːrs/ (US), /ˈdɪskɔːs/ (UK)| vi. |  To speak or write formally and at length. |  He discoursed at length on the merits of organic farming. 他详细论述了有机农业的优点。 | synonym: speak, talk, expound, lecture, preach | antonym: be quiet, listen | verb
