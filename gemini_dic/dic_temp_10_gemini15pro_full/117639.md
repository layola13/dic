megastructures:/ˈmɛɡəˌstrʌktʃərz/| n. |巨型建筑，巨型结构|Extremely large artificial structures, often hypothetical, with dimensions comparable to planetary or stellar sizes.|Scientists theorize about possible alien megastructures. 科学家们对可能存在的外星巨型建筑进行了理论推测。|  The Dyson Sphere is a popular example of a megastructure. 戴森球是巨型建筑的一个流行例子。|Near synonyms: massive constructions, colossal structures, immense structures|Antonyms: microstructures, miniature structures|Noun
