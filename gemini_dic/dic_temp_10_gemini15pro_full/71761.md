n.

A run-down, dilapidated place; a dirty or unkempt person.
That apartment is a real fleabag; I wouldn't stay there. 那间公寓真是个破烂地方，我不会住在那里。

synonyms: dive, dump, hovel, slum, vagrant
antonyms: palace, mansion, clean person
