onlinenlan:/ˌɒnlaɪnˈlæn/ | n. |线上局域网；线上线下局域网|A local area network (LAN) that is accessible online, or that combines online and offline functionality. | Example:  Gamers often create onlinenlans to play together without lag. 玩家经常创建线上局域网以一起玩游戏而不会出现延迟。

Example: The onlinenlan allowed players to connect both locally and remotely.  这个线上线下局域网允许玩家在本地和远程连接。 | 近义词: hybrid LAN, virtual LAN | 反义词: offline LAN, physical LAN | 词性: noun
