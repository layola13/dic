euchologue:/ˈjuːkəlɒɡ/ /ˈjuːkəˌlɑːɡ/| n. |祈祷书，祷告书|A book containing the liturgical forms for the celebration of the Eucharist and other services.  A book of prayers, especially one containing the order of service for the Mass or Holy Communion. |She consulted the euchologue before the service. 她做礼拜前查阅了祈祷书。|The priest read from the ancient euchologue.  神父诵读着古老的祈祷书。| synonym: prayer book, missal, liturgical book |antonym: None (a direct opposite for a book of prayers doesn't readily exist)|noun
