epoch-making:/ˈɛpɒkˌmeɪkɪŋ/ (US) /ˈiːpɒkˌmeɪkɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|划时代的；开创性的；具有划时代意义的|Marking a new and important period in history.|The invention of the printing press was an epoch-making event. 印刷术的发明是一个划时代的事件。|

The moon landing was an epoch-making achievement. 登月是一项划时代的成就。|近义词：groundbreaking, revolutionary, pioneering, transformative|反义词：insignificant, trivial, unimportant|词性：形容词
词源:
From epoch + -making.  "Epoch" comes from the Modern Latin *epoca, from Ancient Greek ἐποχή (epokhḗ, “pause, stop”). "-making" is a suffix meaning "causing" or "creating."
