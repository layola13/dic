humanitarianism:/hjuːˌmænɪˈtɛəriənɪzəm/ /ˌhjuːmænɪˈtɛriənɪzəm/ | n. |人道主义；博爱；慈善；|A concern for human welfare, especially as manifested through philanthropy.|例句：Her humanitarianism led her to volunteer at the refugee camp. 她的人道主义精神促使她到难民营做志愿者。

例句：The organization is known for its humanitarianism and commitment to helping those in need.  这个组织以其人道主义精神和致力于帮助有需要的人而闻名。

近义词：benevolence, compassion, philanthropy, altruism|反义词：cruelty, inhumanity, barbarity|词性:noun
