february:/ˈfebruəri/ (US), /ˈfebruəri/ (UK)| n. |二月|The second month of the year, usually containing 28 days and 29 in leap years.  Derived from the Latin word "februarius," relating to purification rituals performed in this month in ancient Rome.|My birthday is in February. 我的生日在二月。|February is the shortest month of the year. 二月是一年中最短的月份。|近义词: None (as it's a specific month)|反义词: None (as it's a specific month)|词性:noun
