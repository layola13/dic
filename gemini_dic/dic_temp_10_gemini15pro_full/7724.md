anonymities:/ˌænəˈnɪmətiz/| n. |匿名；匿名者；无名之辈|The state of being anonymous; the condition of having one's identity concealed.  The quality or state of being unknown by name.|  The report protected the anonymities of the witnesses. 该报告保护了证人的匿名性。|Synonyms: anonymity, obscurity, namelessness|Antonyms: fame, renown, publicity|Noun
