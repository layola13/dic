fibrinoplastin:/ˌfaɪbrɪnoʊˈplæstɪn/| n. | 血纤蛋白原 |A globulin present in blood plasma that is converted into fibrin by the action of thrombin in the presence of calcium ions. It plays a key role in blood coagulation. |  Fibrinoplastin is essential for the formation of blood clots. 血纤蛋白原对血凝块的形成至关重要。| synonym: fibrinogen | antonym: None | noun

fibrinoplastin:/ˌfaɪbrɪnoʊˈplæstɪn/| n. | 血浆球蛋白 |An older term for fibrinogen, a soluble protein present in blood plasma that is converted to fibrin by the action of thrombin during blood clot formation. |  The term fibrinoplastin is now largely obsolete and replaced by fibrinogen.  “血浆球蛋白”这个术语现在已经过时，被“血纤蛋白原”取代。| synonym: fibrinogen | antonym: None | noun
