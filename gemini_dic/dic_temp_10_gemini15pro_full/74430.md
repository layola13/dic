franciscans:/frænˈsɪskənz/
n.
方济各会修士；方济会的修女
Members of a religious order founded by St. Francis of Assisi.
例句：The Franciscans are known for their vows of poverty. 方济各会修士以其贫困的誓言而闻名。

例句：Many Franciscans dedicate their lives to helping the poor. 许多方济各会修士致力于帮助穷人。

近义词：monks, friars
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps secularists?)
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From St. Francis of Assisi (1181/2-1226), founder of the Franciscan order + -an. Ultimately from the Germanic personal name *Frankisk "Frenchman."
