cutinization: /ˌkjutɪnaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 角质化 | The process of forming a cuticle, especially the thickening of cell walls in plants with cutin. | The cutinization of the epidermis prevents excessive water loss from the leaf.  叶片表皮的角质化防止水分过度流失。 | 近义词: lignification (木质化) | 反义词: None | noun


cutinize: /ˈkjutɪnaɪz/ | v. | 角质化 | To become covered with or converted into cutin. | The epidermal cells cutinize to form a protective layer. 表皮细胞角质化形成保护层。 | 近义词: lignify (木质化) | 反义词: None | verb
