legatees:/ˌlɛɡəˈtiːz/
n.
遗产继承人
A person or entity to whom a legacy is bequeathed; an inheritor of personal property under a will.
The legatees of the millionaire's estate will each receive a considerable sum.  百万富翁遗产的继承人每人将得到一大笔钱。

The lawyer read the will aloud, listing all the legatees and their bequests. 律师大声宣读了遗嘱，列出了所有遗产继承人和他们的遗产。


近义词:heir, beneficiary, inheritor
反义词:testator (遗嘱人), donor (赠与人)
词性:noun (plural)
