motorized:/ˈməʊtəraɪzd/ (US) /ˈməʊtəraɪzd/ (UK)| adj.|机动的，装有发动机的|Having a motor; equipped with a motor for propulsion.|例句：They sell motorized bicycles. 他们卖电动自行车。|例句：The motorized wheelchair gave her newfound independence.  电动轮椅给了她新发现的独立性。|近义词：powered, automated, mechanized|反义词：manual, unpowered|词性：形容词
词源: motor + -ize + -ed. From "motor" (meaning a source of mechanical power) + "-ize" (a suffix meaning "to equip with" or "to cause to be") + "-ed" (a suffix indicating the past participle, creating an adjective meaning "having been equipped with a motor").
