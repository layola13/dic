mislive: /mɪsˈlɪv/
verb
过错地生活；虚度光阴
To live wrongly or badly; to waste one's life.
He mislived his youth in dissipation and idleness. 他在放荡和懒惰中虚度了青春。
近义词: squander, waste, misspend
反义词: cherish, utilize, value
词性: verb


Etymology:
From mis- + live.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Old English origin meaning "wrongly," "badly," or "amiss."  "Live" comes from Old English libban, meaning "to live, be alive."
