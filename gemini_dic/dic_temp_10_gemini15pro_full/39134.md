confuser:/kənˈfjuːzər/ | n. |迷惑者，令人困惑的人或物 |Someone or something that causes confusion. |The instructions were written by a confuser; no one could understand them.  这个说明书是由一个令人困惑的人写的；没有人能理解它们。|近义词: obfuscator, bewilderer |反义词: clarifier, explainer |词性: noun

confuse:/kənˈfjuːz/ | v. |使困惑，使糊涂 |To make someone unable to think clearly; to bewilder. |The similar names confused me.  相似的名字把我弄糊涂了。|近义词: bewilder, perplex, muddle |反义词: clarify, explain |词性: verb
