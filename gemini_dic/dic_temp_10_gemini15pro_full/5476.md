alonely: /əˈloʊnli/ | adj. | 孤独的，寂寞的 | Feeling or caused to feel isolated and unhappy due to a lack of friends or companions. Experiencing emotional isolation. |  She felt alone and abandoned in the big city. 她在大城市感到孤独无助。|  The elderly man lived a lonely life after his wife passed away.  这位老先生在妻子去世后过着孤独的生活。 |  isolated, solitary, lonesome | sociable, gregarious | adjective
