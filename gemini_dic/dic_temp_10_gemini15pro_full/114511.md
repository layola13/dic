maladministrator:/ˌmælədˈmɪnɪstreɪtər/ | n. | 管理不善者，不称职的管理者 | A person who manages or administers poorly, especially a public official.  |  The maladministrator was fired after the audit revealed widespread corruption.  这位管理不善者在审计揭露了普遍腐败后被解雇了。 | Synonyms: mismanager, incompetent administrator | Antonyms: competent administrator, good manager | noun

maladministrator:/ˌmælədˈmɪnɪstreɪtər/ | n. | 滥用职权者 | A person who abuses their power in an administrative position. |  The investigation exposed the mayor as a maladministrator who used public funds for personal gain. 调查揭露了市长是一位滥用职权者，他将公共资金用于个人利益。 | Synonyms: corrupt official, dishonest administrator | Antonyms: honest official, ethical administrator | noun
