overattenuated: /ˌoʊvərəˈtɛnjuːeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 过度衰减的 | Having been reduced in strength or intensity to an excessive degree, especially of a signal. | The overattenuated signal was barely detectable.  过度衰减的信号几乎无法检测到。 | The overattenuated sound was difficult to hear.  过度衰减的声音很难听到。| 近义词: weakened, diminished, suppressed | 反义词: amplified, boosted, strengthened | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "attenuated" (from Latin "attenuare" meaning "to make thin or weak").
