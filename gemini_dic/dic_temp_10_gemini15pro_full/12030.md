artichoke:/ˈɑːrtɪtʃoʊk/ (US) /ˈɑːtɪtʃəʊk/ (UK)| n. |朝鲜蓟，洋蓟|A thistle-like plant cultivated for its large flower head, of which the fleshy base of the bracts and the receptacle are eaten as a vegetable. |例句：I love steamed artichokes with a little lemon butter. 我喜欢加一点柠檬黄油的蒸朝鲜蓟。

例句：Artichokes are a popular vegetable in Mediterranean cuisine. 朝鲜蓟是地中海美食中很受欢迎的蔬菜。|近义词：cardoon (a related plant)|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
