dudishly:/ˈduːdɪʃli/ | adv. | 花花公子般地；纨绔子弟似地；像个帅哥一样 | In a manner resembling or characteristic of a dude; like a dandy. | He strolled dudishly down the street, twirling his cane. 他沿着街道像个花花公子一样闲逛，转动着手杖。 |  He dressed dudishly in a brightly colored suit. 他穿着一套颜色鲜艳的西装，打扮得像个纨绔子弟。 | 近义词：dandily, foppishly, stylishly | 反义词：sloppily, plainly, unfashionably | 词性: 副词

词源: From dude + -ish + -ly.  "Dude" originally referred to a sharply dressed Easterner, especially one visiting the American West.
