ammonitiferous:/ˌæməˈnɪfərəs/
adjective
含菊石的；产菊石的
Containing ammonites; yielding ammonites.
The ammonitiferous layers of rock provided valuable clues to the age of the formation. 这些含菊石的岩层为地层的年龄提供了宝贵的线索。
Near synonyms: fossiliferous
Antonym: barren (in the geological sense)
adjective
