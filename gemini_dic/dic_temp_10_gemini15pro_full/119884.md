militarily:/ˌmɪlɪˈtɛrɪli/ | adv. |军事上地，从军事角度|In terms of military strategy or action.  From a military point of view.|例句：The country is militarily strong. 这个国家军事上很强大。|例句：The situation was assessed militarily.  局势是从军事角度进行评估的。|近义词：strategically, martial, in arms|反义词:peacefully, diplomatically|词性:副词

Etymology:
From military + -ly.  Military comes from Middle French militaire, from Latin mīlitāris (“of a soldier, soldierly, military”), from mīles (“soldier”).
