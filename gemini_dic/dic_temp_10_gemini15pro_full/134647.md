nonmalignantly:/ˌnɒn məˈlɪɡnəntli/ | adv. | 非恶性地 | In a way that is not harmful or cancerous. | The tumor was thankfully removed nonmalignantly.  谢天谢地，肿瘤被非恶性地切除了。| 近义词: benignly, harmlessly | 反义词: malignantly, cancerously | 词性: 副词

词源:

non- (prefix):  from Latin, meaning "not"
malignant (adjective): from Latin malignans, present participle of malignare "to do maliciously," from male "badly" + -gnus "born," related to gignere "to beget"
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives
