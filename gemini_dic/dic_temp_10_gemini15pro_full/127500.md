necklacing:/ˈnɛklɪsɪŋ/
n.
用轮胎焚烧
The extrajudicial killing of a suspected criminal by placing a burning tire around the victim's neck.
Necklacing was a common method of mob justice in South Africa during the apartheid era.  南非种族隔离时期，用轮胎焚烧是一种常见的私刑处决方式。
