liquidity:/lɪˈkwɪdɪti/ | n. | 流动性；流动资产 | The ability of an asset to be converted into cash quickly and without significant loss of value.  The ease with which something can be bought or sold. |  The company maintains a high level of liquidity to meet its short-term obligations.  这家公司保持高水平的流动性以履行其短期义务。 | fluidity, convertibility | illiquidity, frozenness | noun
