grounding:/ˈɡraʊndɪŋ/ | n. | 接地, 基础, 根基 |The act of connecting something to the earth, either physically or metaphorically.  It can refer to an electrical connection, a basis for understanding, or a sense of stability. | The electrician checked the grounding of the appliance. 电工检查了电器的接地。| Synonyms: earthing, foundation, basis, anchoring, rooting | Antonyms: ungrounded, baseless, unstable | noun

grounding:/ˈɡraʊndɪŋ/ | adj. |  扎实的，基础的 | Providing a firm foundation or basis. | Her grounding in mathematics is excellent. 她的数学基础很扎实。| Synonyms: fundamental, basic, foundational | Antonyms: superficial, shallow, unstable | adjective
