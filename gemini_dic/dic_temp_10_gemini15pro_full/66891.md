expediently:/ɪkˈspiːdiəntli/
adverb
权宜地；方便地；合算地
In a way that is convenient and practical although possibly improper or immoral.
He acted expediently to save his job, even though it meant compromising his ethics. 他为了保住工作行事权宜，即使这意味着违背他的道德。
Synonyms: practically, conveniently, pragmatically, opportunistically
Antonyms:  inconveniently, impractically, scrupulously, ethically
词性: 副词
