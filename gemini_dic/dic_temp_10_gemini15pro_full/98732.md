insurmountable:/ˌɪnsərˈmaʊntəbəl/ | adj. | 不可逾越的；不可克服的 | Too great to be overcome.  |  The challenge seemed insurmountable at first, but we persevered.  一开始这项挑战似乎不可逾越，但我们坚持了下来。 |  The team faced seemingly insurmountable odds.  团队面临着似乎不可克服的困难。 | 近义词: insuperable, unconquerable, invincible | 反义词: surmountable, conquerable, vincible | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From in- "not" + surmount + -able.  From French *insurmontable, from in- "not" + surmonter "to overcome, surpass."  Ultimately from Latin in- "not" + super "over" + mons "mountain."
