barnet: /ˈbɑːnɪt/ | n. |头发, 婴儿, 孩子 (主要指孩子)|A child's hair. Primarily used in British slang. |例句：Look at the cute barnet on that baby! 看看那个婴儿可爱的头发！

例句：He's got a thick barnet. 他头发很浓密。|近义词：hair, locks, tresses (more formal), kid, child, baby|反义词: N/A (no direct opposite for hair or child) |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From rhyming slang "Barnet Fair" (a horse fair held in Barnet, Hertfordshire, England), rhyming with "hair."
