demi-volte:/ˌdɛmi ˈvɔlt/ | n. | 半回转; 半圈 |  In ballet, a demi-volte is a half turn on one leg, often used as a connecting step.  在芭蕾舞中，半回转是用一条腿完成的半圈旋转，通常用作连接舞步。| The dancer incorporated a graceful demi-volte into her routine.  这位舞者在她的舞蹈中加入了一个优雅的半回转。 | synonyms: half-turn, pivot | antonyms: full turn, complete rotation | noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From French *demi-volte*, from *demi* ("half") + *volte* ("turn").  Ultimately from Latin *dimidius* ("half") and *volvere* ("to roll, turn").
