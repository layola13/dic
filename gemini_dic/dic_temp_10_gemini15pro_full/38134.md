comprehensiveness:/ˌkɑːmprɪˈhɛnsɪvnəs/| n. |全面性，综合性，广泛性|The comprehensiveness of the study impressed the committee. 这项研究的全面性给委员会留下了深刻的印象。|近义词：thoroughness, completeness, extensiveness|反义词：incompleteness, narrowness|noun

comprehensible:/ˌkɑːmprɪˈhɛnsəbl/| adj. |可理解的，易懂的|His explanation was clear and comprehensible. 他的解释清晰易懂。|近义词：understandable, clear, lucid|反义词：incomprehensible, obscure|adjective

comprehend:/ˌkɑːmprɪˈhɛnd/| v. |理解，领会|I couldn't comprehend the complex instructions. 我无法理解这些复杂的指令。|近义词：understand, grasp, perceive|反义词：misunderstand|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *comprehensīvus, from Latin comprehēnsus, past participle of comprehendere “to grasp, seize, comprise, include, understand,” from com- “together” + prehendere “to grasp.”
