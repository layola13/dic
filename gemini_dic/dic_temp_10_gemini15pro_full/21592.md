bluish:/ˈbluːɪʃ/| adj.|浅蓝色的，带蓝色的|Slightly blue in color.|The sky was a beautiful bluish hue. 天空呈现美丽的浅蓝色。|近义词：blue, azure, cerulean|反义词：reddish, yellowish|词性:形容词

词源:  From blue + -ish.  The suffix "-ish" adds the meaning "somewhat" or "slightly" to the base word "blue."
