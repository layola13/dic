flaccidnesses:/flæˈsɪdnəsɪz/
n.
松弛；软弱无力
The state of being flaccid; lack of firmness or stiffness.
His handshake was marked by a surprising flaccidness. 他的握手出奇地软弱无力。
近义词:limpness, softness, looseness, laxity
反义词:firmness, tightness, rigidity, hardness
