empathetic:/ˌɛmpəˈθɛtɪk/ | adj. | 同情的，有同理心的 | Showing an ability to understand and share the feelings of another. | She was very empathetic when I told her about my problems. 她当我告诉她我的问题时非常有同理心。|  He gave her an empathetic look. 他给了她一个充满同理心的眼神。 | 近义词：compassionate, understanding, sympathetic | 反义词：apathetic, uncaring, indifferent | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From empathy + -ic.  Empathy is from the German Einfühlung (literally, "feeling into"), from ein ("in") + Fühlung ("feeling").
