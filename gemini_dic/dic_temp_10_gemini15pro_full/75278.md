frontager:/ˈfrʌntɪdʒər/
n.
临街业主，临街地主，沿街地段所有人
An owner of property fronting a street or other thoroughfare.
The frontagers complained about the noise from the new nightclub. 临街业主抱怨新夜总会的噪音。
近义词:abutter, resident
反义词: (无明确的反义词)
词性: noun

frontager:/ˈfrʌntɪdʒər/
n.
(尤指战壕的)前沿，正面
The front part of something, especially a trench. (This meaning is less common in modern usage.)
The soldiers in the frontager were heavily bombarded.  (less common) 前沿的士兵遭到了猛烈的轰炸。
近义词:front, forefront
反义词:rear, back
词性: noun
