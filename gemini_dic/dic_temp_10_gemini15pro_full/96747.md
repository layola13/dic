ineloquently:/ɪnˈɛləkwəntli/
adv.
不雄辩地，表达拙劣地
in a way that is not fluent, elegant, or persuasive
He stammered ineloquently, struggling to express his feelings. 他结结巴巴地说得不清不楚，难以表达自己的感受。
Synonyms:inarticulately, clumsily, awkwardly, falteringly
Antonyms:eloquently, articulately, fluently, persuasively
词性:副词
