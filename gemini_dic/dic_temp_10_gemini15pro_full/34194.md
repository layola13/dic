clamped:/klæmpt/| v. |夹紧的，固定的| firmly fastened or secured with a clamp |The wood was clamped to the workbench. 木头被夹在工作台上。|近义词：fastened, secured, fixed, attached|反义词:loose, detached, unfastened|词性:Verb/Adjective (past participle of 'clamp')
Etymology: From the noun "clamp," which originates from Middle Low German or Middle Dutch "klampe," meaning "lump, mass, or clamp."
