nodose:/ˈnoʊdoʊs/| adj.|多节的；有结的|Having knots or swellings at intervals; knotted.|The bamboo stalks are nodose, marked by prominent nodes.|近义词：knotty, gnarled, lumpy|反义词:smooth, even|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *nōdōsus*, from nōdus (“knot”).
