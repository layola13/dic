meritocracies: /ˌmɛrɪˈtɒkrəsiːz/ (UK) /ˌmɛrɪˈtɑːkrəsiːz/ (US) | n. | 精英管理制度；贤能统治；能者为尊的社会 | a system in which people are chosen for jobs and other positions based on their ability and how well they do their work, rather than on their family background, wealth, or social position |  Many people believe that a true meritocracy is the ideal form of government. 许多人认为真正的精英管理是理想的政府形式。

The company claims to be a meritocracy, but promotions seem to be based on favoritism. 这家公司声称是精英管理，但晋升似乎是基于偏袒。| synonyms: system of merit, merit system | antonyms: aristocracy, oligarchy | noun
