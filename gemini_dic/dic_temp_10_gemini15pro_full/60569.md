elasticity:/ɪˌlæˈstɪsəti/ | n. |弹性，弹力|The ability of a material to return to its original shape after being stretched or compressed.  | The rubber band has high elasticity.  橡皮筋有很高的弹性。| 近义词: flexibility, resilience, springiness | 反义词: rigidity, stiffness | noun
