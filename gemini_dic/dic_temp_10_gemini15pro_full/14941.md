azureous:/ˈæʒərəs/| adj.|天蓝色的，蔚蓝色的|Of or having a light, purplish blue color similar to a clear sky.|The azureous waters of the Mediterranean beckoned. 地中海蔚蓝的海水令人神往。|近义词：azure, cerulean, sky-blue|反义词：dark, gloomy|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *azuré, from Medieval Latin *azureus, from Arabic لَازَوَرْد (lāzaward, “lapis lazuli”), from Persian لاجورد (lâjvard, “lapis lazuli”). Ultimately from Sanskrit राजवर्त (rājavarta, “king's portion”).
