dhows:/daʊz/
n.
单数：dhow | 复数：dhows
阿拉伯帆船，单桅三角帆船
A lateen-rigged sailing vessel used in the Indian Ocean.  These traditional boats have one or more masts with lateen sails and are often used for trading and fishing.
例句：The dhows sailed gracefully across the Arabian Sea.  阿拉伯帆船优雅地航行在阿拉伯海上。

例句：We saw several dhows anchored in the harbor. 我们看到几艘单桅三角帆船停泊在港口。

近义词：sailing vessel, sailboat, ship, boat
反义词：(无直接反义词，可以考虑现代机动船 motorized vessel)
词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Swahili *dhau*, from Arabic دَوْ (*daw*), perhaps from Persian دَغْو (*daɣw*). Ultimately from Dravidian, compare Tamil தோணி (*tōṇi*), Malayalam തോണി (*tōṇi*).
