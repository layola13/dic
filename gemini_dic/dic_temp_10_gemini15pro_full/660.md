abrogations: /ˌæbrəˈɡeɪʃənz/ | n. | 废除，撤销 | The abrogation of the treaty led to renewed hostilities. 该条约的废除导致敌意重新燃起。  The government's abrogation of civil liberties sparked widespread protests. 政府对公民自由的废除引发了广泛的抗议。 | 近义词: repeals, revocations, annulments, cancellations | 反义词: enactments, establishments, ratifications | 词性: 名词

abrogation: /ˌæbrəˈɡeɪʃən/ | n. | 废除，撤销 | The abrogation of the law was welcomed by many.  这项法律的废除受到了许多人的欢迎。 The abrogation of the agreement caused considerable confusion. 协议的废除造成了相当大的混乱。 | 近义词: repeal, revocation, annulment, cancellation | 反义词: enactment, establishment, ratification | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin abrogatio, from Latin abrogare (“to repeal”), from ab- (“away from”) + rogare (“to ask, propose a law”).

USAGE->
The word "abrogations" is a plural noun and is used in sentences similar to how its singular form "abrogation" is used.  Here are examples using both:

一般现在时:
The abrogation of old laws is a necessary part of legal reform. 旧法律的废除是法律改革的必要组成部分。
The abrogations of several trade agreements have impacted the economy.  几项贸易协定的废除影响了经济。

一般过去时:
The abrogation of the treaty occurred in 1995. 该条约于1995年被废除。
Several abrogations of outdated policies were announced last year. 去年宣布废除几项过时的政策。


现在完成时:
The government has announced the abrogation of the controversial law. 政府已宣布废除这项有争议的法律。
Recent abrogations of regulations have simplified the process.  最近的法规废除简化了流程。

过去完成时:
The treaty had been abrogated before the war began.  战争开始前，该条约已被废除。
Several key agreements had been abrogated by the time negotiations restarted. 在谈判重新开始之前，几项关键协议已被废除。


将来时:
The abrogation of this law will have serious consequences. 废除这项法律将会产生严重后果。
The upcoming abrogations of these regulations will affect businesses.  即将废除的这些法规将影响企业。


虚拟语气:
If the abrogation of the treaty were to happen, it would destabilize the region. 如果该条约被废除，将会破坏该地区的稳定。
It is imperative that the abrogations of these laws be carefully considered.  必须仔细考虑这些法律的废除。

被动语态:
The law was abrogated by the parliament last year.  该法律去年被议会废除。
Several regulations were abrogated as part of the reform.  作为改革的一部分，几项法规被废除。


否定句:
The abrogation of the agreement has not yet been confirmed.  该协议的废除尚未得到证实。
No abrogations of existing laws are planned at this time. 目前没有计划废除现有法律。
