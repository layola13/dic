curls:/kɜːrlz/ (UK) /kɝlz/ (US)| n. v. |卷发，卷曲，卷曲物；使…卷曲|A curl is a small section of hair that curves or twists.  As a verb, it means to form into a spiral or curve. |Her hair was a mass of blonde curls. 她的头发是一团金色的卷发。|Synonyms: ringlets, waves, coils, twists|Antonyms: straight hair|Noun

curls:/kɜːrlz/ (UK) /kɝlz/ (US)| v. |卷曲，使…弯曲|To bend or twist into a curved shape. |The smoke curled upwards from the chimney. 烟从烟囱里向上盘旋。|Synonyms: coil, twist, wave, bend|Antonyms: straighten, uncurl|Verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *crulle*, *curlle*, of uncertain origin, perhaps related to Old Norse *krull* (“curl”).
