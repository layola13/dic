missional:/ˈmɪʃənəl/| adj.|传教的；使命的；差传的|Relating to or characterized by mission, especially a Christian mission.|The church has a strong missional focus, sending missionaries all over the world. 教会非常注重传教，派遣传教士到世界各地。|近义词：evangelistic, missionary|反义词：secular, worldly|词性:形容词


Etymology:  From "mission" + "-al". "Mission" comes from the Latin "missio," meaning "a sending."
