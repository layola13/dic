bought:/bɔːt/
verb
买
past tense of "buy" -  acquired by paying money; obtained by exchange
I bought a new car yesterday. 我昨天买了一辆新车。
She bought him a book for his birthday. 她给他买了一本书作为生日礼物。
近义词: purchased, acquired
反义词: sold
