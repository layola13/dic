mopper:/ˈmɒpə(r) (UK), ˈmɑːpər (US)| n.|拖把; 使用拖把的人; 哭泣的人|A person who mops (floors, etc.); a crying person.  One who uses a mop, especially as an occupation.  Someone who weeps or cries frequently or readily.|例句：The mopper cleaned the floor thoroughly. 拖把工把地板彻底擦干净了。

例句：He's such a mopper; he cries at everything. 他真是个爱哭鬼，什么事都能让他哭。|近义词：cleaner, janitor, weeper, crybaby|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
