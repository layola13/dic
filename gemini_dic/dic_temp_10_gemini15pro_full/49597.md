demesne:/dɪˈmeɪn/ /dɪˈmiːn/| n.|领地，管辖范围，领域|A territory over which dominion is exercised; legal possession of land as one's own.|The king's vast demesne stretched for miles. 国王的广阔领地绵延数英里。|Synonyms: domain, estate, territory, realm|Antonyms:  public land, common land|Noun

demesne:/dɪˈmeɪn/ /dɪˈmiːn/| n.|私人财产|Private land attached to a manor and retained for the owner's own use.|The manor house and its surrounding demesne were beautifully maintained.  庄园宅邸及其周围的私人土地维护得很好。|Synonyms: property, holding, possession|Antonyms:  public property|Noun
