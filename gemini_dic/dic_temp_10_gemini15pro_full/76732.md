gambrel: /ˈɡæmbrəl/ | adj. |  (动物的腿)关节的，跗节的 | Relating to the hock or gambrel of an animal, particularly a horse. |  He examined the horse's gambrel joint. 他检查了马的跗关节。 | synonym: hock | antonym: N/A | adjective
