incorrupted: /ˌɪnkəˈrʌptɪd/
adjective
未腐败的，纯洁的，未堕落的
not morally corrupt; pure.
The incorrupted judge was known for his fairness.  这位廉洁的法官以其公正而闻名。
He maintained an incorrupted reputation throughout his career.  他在整个职业生涯中都保持着清廉的声誉。
近义词：pure, uncorrupted, untainted, undefiled
反义词：corrupted, tainted, depraved, debauched
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *incorruptus, from in- "not" + corrumpere "to corrupt, bribe, spoil"
