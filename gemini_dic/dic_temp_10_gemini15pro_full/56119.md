doctrinality:/ˌdɒktrɪˈnælɪti/ |n.| 教义性，学理性 |The doctrinality of the argument was impressive, but its practicality was questionable.  该论证的学理性令人印象深刻，但它的实用性值得怀疑。|  The high degree of doctrinality in the religious sect led to many schisms.  该教派高度的教义性导致了许多分裂。| 近义词: dogmatism, rigidity, orthodoxy | 反义词: pragmatism, flexibility, heterodoxy | 词性: noun

doctrinal:/ˈdɒktrɪn(ə)l/ |adj.| 教义的，学理的 |Doctrinal disputes often lead to divisions within a church. 教义上的争议常常导致教会内部的分裂。|  His doctrinal beliefs were very strict. 他的教义信仰非常严格。 | 近义词: dogmatic, theological | 反义词: practical, empirical | 词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *doctrīnālitās, from Latin doctrīna (“teaching, instruction”) + -ālitās, equivalent to English doctrine +‎ -ality. Ultimately from Latin docēre (“to teach”).
