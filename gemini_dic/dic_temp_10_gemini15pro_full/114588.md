malcontent:/ˌmælˈkənˌtent/ | n. adj. |不满分子；不满的|A person who is dissatisfied and rebellious.  As an adjective, it describes someone who is dissatisfied and inclined to complain. |例句：The malcontents in the group started to spread rumors.  小组里那些不满分子开始散布谣言。|近义词：dissident, rebel, complainer, disgruntled, agitator |反义词:satisfied, content, complacent, happy |词性:noun, adjective


malcontent:/ˌmælˈkənˌtent/ |adj.|不满的| Dissatisfied and inclined to complain. |例句：The malcontent workers threatened to strike. 不满的工人们威胁要罢工。|近义词：dissatisfied, disgruntled, discontented |反义词:satisfied, content, pleased |词性:adjective


Etymology:
From French *malcontent* (16th century), from *mal* ("badly") + *content* ("content").  Ultimately from Latin *male* ("badly") and *contentus* ("content").
