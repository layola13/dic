nonfatalnesses:/ˌnɑːnˈfeɪtəlnəsəz/ | n. | 非致命性 | The state or quality of being nonfatal; instances of not resulting in death. | The report highlighted the nonfatalnesses of most of the accidents.  这份报告强调了大多数事故的非致命性。|  The study focused on the nonfatalnesses of the new strain of the virus. 这项研究的重点是这种新病毒株的非致命性。 | 近义词: survivals, nonlethalities | 反义词: fatalities, mortalities | 词性: 名词

nonfatal:/ˌnɑːnˈfeɪtl/ | adj. | 非致命的 | Not causing or resulting in death. |  The injury was nonfatal, thankfully.  谢天谢地，伤势不致命。|  The virus proved to be nonfatal in most cases.  事实证明，这种病毒在大多数情况下并不致命。 | 近义词: nonlethal, survivable | 反义词: fatal, lethal, deadly | 词性: 形容词


词源: non- (prefix meaning "not") + fatal (from Latin "fatalis," meaning "destined, decreed by fate," ultimately from "fatum," meaning "fate") + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).
