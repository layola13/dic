fiddle:/ˈfɪdl/ (US), /ˈfɪd(ə)l/ (UK)| n. v. | 小提琴; 随意摆弄; 篡改; 骗取 | A violin, especially one played informally.  To play a violin, especially in a casual way. To make small movements with your hands, especially because you are nervous or bored. To dishonestly change something, such as a document or figures, in order to gain something for yourself. | He fiddled with the controls. 他摆弄着控制器。 | 近义词: violin, toy, tamper, cheat, swindle | 反义词: leave alone, be honest | n. 名词  v. 动词
