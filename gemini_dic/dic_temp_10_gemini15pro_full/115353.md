manor:/ˈmænər/ (US), /ˈmænə(r)/ (UK)| n. |庄园；领地；宅第 |A large country house with lands; the main house of a large estate. |例句：The manor house stood on a hill overlooking the village. 这座庄园大宅坐落在俯瞰村庄的山丘上。

例句：They inherited the manor from their grandparents. 他们从祖父母那里继承了这座庄园。

近义词：estate, mansion, hall, house |反义词: - |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *manoir* "dwelling, residence, mansion," from *maneir* "to dwell, remain," from Latin *manēre* "to stay, remain."
