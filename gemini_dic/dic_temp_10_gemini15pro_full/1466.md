acerbically:/əˈsɜːrbɪkli/
adverb
尖刻地，辛辣地
in a bitter, sharp, or sarcastic manner.
Her acerbically witty remarks left him speechless. 她尖刻机智的评论让他哑口无言。
The critic acerbically dismissed the novel as derivative and uninspired.  评论家尖刻地认为这部小说是抄袭的，缺乏灵感。

近义词
bitterly, sharply, caustically, sarcastically, cuttingly, mordantly, acrimoniously
反义词
sweetly, kindly, gently, mildly
词性
adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *acerbicus, from Latin acerbus (“sour, bitter, harsh”), from acer (“sharp, pointed, keen”).

USAGE->
She responded acerbically to his rude comment. = 她对他的粗鲁评论做出了尖刻的回应。

He acerbically criticized the government's handling of the crisis. = 他尖刻地批评了政府处理危机的方式。


PHRASE->
speak acerbically = 尖刻地说话
