counterblow:/ˈkaʊntərbləʊ/ (US) /ˈkaʊntəbləʊ/ (UK)| n.|反击，回击|An aggressive action taken in response to an attack or criticism.|The boxer delivered a swift counterblow to his opponent's jab. 拳击手迅速回击了对手的刺拳。|Synonyms: retaliation, riposte, comeback|Antonyms: attack, offense|noun

counterblow:/ˈkaʊntərbləʊ/ (US) /ˈkaʊntəbləʊ/ (UK)| v.|反击，回击|To strike back in response to an attack or criticism.|The army counterblowed swiftly against the enemy advance.  军队迅速反击了敌人的进攻。|Synonyms: retaliate, strike back|Antonyms: attack, initiate|verb
