internucleon:/ˌɪntərˈnjuːkliːɒn/ (UK), /ˌɪntərˈnuːkliːɑːn/ (US)| adj.|核子间的|Existing or occurring between nucleons (protons or neutrons in the atomic nucleus).|The internucleon force is responsible for holding the nucleus together. 核子间的力负责将原子核结合在一起。|The internucleon interaction is a complex phenomenon. 核子间的相互作用是一个复杂的现象。|近义词: intranuclear|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps extranuclear)|词性: adjective
