non-transferable:/ˌnɒn trænsˈfɜːrəbl/ (US) /ˌnɑːn trænsˈfɜːrəbl/| adj.| 不可转让的，不可转移的|Not able to be transferred or passed from one person to another.|This is a non-transferable ticket. 这是一张不可转让的票。|non-assignable, inalienable|transferable|adjective
non-transferable:/ˌnɒn trænsˈfɜːrəbl/ (US) /ˌnɑːn trænsˈfɜːrəbl/| n.| 不可转让的东西|Something that cannot be transferred or passed from one person to another.  (Less common usage than as an adjective)|The contract specified several non-transferables, including intellectual property rights.  合同规定了几项不可转让的内容，包括知识产权。|non-assignable item, inalienable asset|transferable item|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "transferable" (from "transfer" + "-able"). "Transfer" comes from Old French "transferrer," ultimately from Latin "transferre" (to carry across).
