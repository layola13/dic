outstartle:/ˌaʊtˈstɑːrtl/ | v. | 惊吓过度，使…大吃一惊 | To startle or surprise someone more than they were already startled. |  The sudden bang outstartled the already nervous cat. 突如其来的巨响把已经很紧张的猫吓得够呛。

The clown jumped out and outstartled the children. 小丑跳了出来，把孩子们吓得不轻。 | 近义词: surprise, frighten, shock, astound | 反义词: calm, soothe, reassure | 词性: 动词
