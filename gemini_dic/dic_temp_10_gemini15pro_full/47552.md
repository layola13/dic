deauthorization:/diːˌɔːθəraɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
取消授权，撤销授权
The act of revoking or withdrawing authorization.
The deauthorization of the software prevents further use.  软件的取消授权阻止了进一步的使用。
The company issued a deauthorization notice for all outdated access cards. 公司发布了所有过期门禁卡的取消授权通知。
revocation, withdrawal, cancellation
authorization, sanction
noun
