enquire:/ɪnˈkwaɪər/ (British English), /ɪnˈkwaɪr/ (American English)| v. |询问，打听|To ask for information about something.|例句：I enquired about train times. 我询问了火车时刻表。

例句：She enquired after his health. 她询问了他的健康状况。|近义词：ask, inquire, query|反义词: answer, reply|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Old French enquerre, based on Latin inquirere "to seek into, search for," from in- "into" + quaerere "to seek, ask."
