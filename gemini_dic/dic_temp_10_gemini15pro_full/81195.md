grandiosity:/ˌɡrændiˈɑːsəti/ | n. |宏伟，堂皇，夸大，浮夸|The state of being grandiose; an instance of this.|He was known for his grandiosity and elaborate schemes.  他以其夸大的言辞和复杂的计划而闻名。|Synonyms: magnificence, splendor, pomposity, bombast, pretension|Antonyms: humility, modesty, unpretentiousness|noun

grandiose:/ˈɡrændiˌoʊs/ | adj. |宏伟的，堂皇的，夸大的，浮夸的|Impressive and imposing in appearance or style, especially pretentiously so.|He had grandiose plans to build a huge mansion. 他有一个宏伟的计划，要建造一座巨大的豪宅。|Synonyms: magnificent, splendid, pompous, bombastic, pretentious|Antonyms: humble, modest, unpretentious|adjective
