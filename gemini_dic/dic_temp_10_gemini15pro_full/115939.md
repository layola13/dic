The marquise over the shop window protected the goods from the sun. 商店橱窗上的遮阳篷保护货物免受阳光照射。|Near synonyms: peeress, noblewoman (for the person); awning, canopy (for the object)|Antonym: commoner (for the person)|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *marquise*, feminine of *marquis* ("marquis"), from Old French *marchis*, from *marche* ("frontier, borderland"), from Frankish *marka* ("border, limit"). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *markō* ("borderland, march").
