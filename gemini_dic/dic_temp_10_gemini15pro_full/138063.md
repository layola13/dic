normalcy:/ˈnɔːrməlsi/ | n. | 常态，正常状态 |The state of being normal; a normal condition. |  After the war, the country slowly returned to normalcy. 战后，国家慢慢恢复了正常。

The government is trying to restore normalcy after the riots. 政府正试图在骚乱后恢复正常秩序。 | 近义词: normality, regularity, ordinariness | 反义词: abnormality, irregularity, anomaly | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From normal + -cy.  The word "normalcy" was popularized, though not coined, by U.S. President Warren G. Harding in a 1920 speech where he advocated a "return to normalcy" after World War I. While considered acceptable, "normality" is generally preferred in formal writing.
