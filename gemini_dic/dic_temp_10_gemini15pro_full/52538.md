diffident:/ˈdɪfɪdənt/| adj.|缺乏自信的；胆怯的；羞怯的|Lacking self-confidence; shy and hesitant.|例句：He was diffident about speaking up in class. 他在课堂上不敢发言。|近义词：shy, timid, hesitant, reserved, bashful, unassertive|反义词：confident, assertive, bold, outgoing|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *diffidentem*, present participle of *diffidere* "to distrust, lack confidence," from *dis-* "not, opposite of" + *fidere* "to trust."
