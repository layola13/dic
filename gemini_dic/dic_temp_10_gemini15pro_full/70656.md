filtrating:/ˈfɪltreɪtɪŋ/
verb
过滤中, 渗透中
The process of a liquid passing through a filter.
Rainwater is filtrating through the soil.  雨水正在渗透土壤。
The kidneys are filtrating waste products from the blood. 肾脏正在过滤血液中的废物。
synonyms: filtering, percolating, straining, sieving
antonyms: clogging, obstructing
verb

Etymology: From Latin *filtrātus, past participle of filtrāre (“to filter”), from filtrum (“filter”).
