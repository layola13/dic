carob:/ˈkærəb/| n.|角豆，角豆树，角豆荚|A sweet, edible pod from a Mediterranean tree, often used as a chocolate substitute.|例句：Carob is often used as a healthier alternative to chocolate. 角豆经常被用作巧克力的更健康替代品。

例句：The carob tree thrives in warm, dry climates. 角豆树在温暖干燥的气候中生长茂盛。|近义词：locust bean, St. John's bread|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *carobe*, from Arabic خَرُّوبٌ (*ḵarrūb*). Ultimately from Akkadian *kharubu*.
