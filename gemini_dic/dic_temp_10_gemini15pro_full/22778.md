borzoi: /ˈbɔːrzɔɪ/ (US), /ˈbɔːrzɔɪ/ (UK)| n. |俄罗斯狼犬，波索尔|A breed of tall, slender sighthound with a long, narrow head and silky coat, originally bred in Russia for hunting wolves.|例句：The borzoi gracefully chased the lure across the field.  俄罗斯狼犬优雅地在田野上追逐诱饵。

例句：Her borzoi is known for its gentle temperament. 她的俄罗斯狼犬以性情温顺而闻名。|近义词：Russian wolfhound|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


Etymology: From Russian борзой (borzój), from борзый (borzýj, “swift, fast”).
