flatworms:/ˈflætˌwɜːrmz/
noun
扁形虫
Flatworms are simple invertebrates with a flattened body shape.
Flatworms are commonly found in freshwater and marine environments.
Planarians are a type of flatworm often studied in biology labs.  涡虫是一种扁形虫，经常在生物实验室里被研究。
Some flatworms are parasitic and can cause diseases in humans and animals. 一些扁形虫是寄生虫，会导致人类和动物患病。
synonyms: platyhelminths
antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a biological classification)
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From flat + worm.  "Flat" refers to their flattened body shape, and "worm" refers to their elongated, worm-like appearance.
