overdeliberateness:/ˌoʊvərdiˈlɪbərɪtnəs/
noun
过度审慎；过分深思熟虑
The state of being excessively careful and thoughtful to the point of slowness or inaction.
His overdeliberateness resulted in a missed opportunity. 他过度审慎导致错失良机。
近义词:  hesitancy, cautiousness, indecision, procrastination
反义词:  impulsiveness, rashness, spontaneity, decisiveness
词性: noun

overdeliberate:/ˌoʊvərdiˈlɪbərɪt/
adjective
过度审慎的；过分深思熟虑的
Characterized by excessive care and thoughtfulness, often to the point of slowness or inaction.
He was overdeliberate in his decision-making.  他在做决定时过于深思熟虑。
近义词: hesitant, cautious, indecisive
反义词: impulsive, rash, spontaneous, decisive
词性: adjective


词源:
From "over-" (excessive) + "deliberate" (from Latin "deliberatus," past participle of "deliberare," meaning "to weigh in the mind, consider").
