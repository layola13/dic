hydroponicum:/ˌhaɪdrəˈpɒnɪkəm/ | adj. | 水培的，水栽的 | Relating to hydroponics; grown or cultivated in a nutrient solution rather than soil. |  Lettuce is often grown hydroponicum in greenhouses.  生菜通常在温室里水培。| 近义词: soilless, water-culture | 反义词: soil-based, earth-grown | 词性: adjective
Etymology:  From Greek 'hydro-' (water) + 'ponos' (labor) + '-icum' (Latin adjectival suffix).  词源：来自希腊语 'hydro-'（水）+ 'ponos'（劳动） + '-icum' （拉丁语形容词后缀）。
