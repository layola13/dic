debauchness:/dɪˈbɔːtʃnəs/
n.
放荡；淫乱；堕落
Excessive indulgence in pleasure or sensuality.
Her reputation was ruined by stories of debauchness. 她的名声被那些关于她放荡的故事毁了。
近义词：dissoluteness, profligacy, depravity, immorality
反义词：morality, virtue, decency, chastity
词性: 名词

debauchery:/dɪˈbɔːtʃəri/
n.
放荡；荒淫；纵情酒色
Excessive indulgence in pleasure or sensuality.
He lived a life of debauchery. 他过着放荡的生活。
近义词：dissoluteness, profligacy, depravity, immorality
反义词：morality, virtue, decency, chastity
词性: 名词

debauch:/dɪˈbɔːtʃ/
v.
使堕落；腐蚀；引诱…放荡
To corrupt morally; seduce.
He was debauched by the city's nightlife.  他被城市的夜生活腐蚀了。
近义词：corrupt, pervert, deprave
反义词：purify, uplift, elevate
词性: 动词

词源:
From Old French desbaucher "to lead astray," from des- "away" + bauche "workshop, joist." Originally meaning "to divert from work," later acquiring the sense of "to corrupt morally."
