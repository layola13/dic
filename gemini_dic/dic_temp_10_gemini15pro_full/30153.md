celebrant: /ˈsɛləbrənt/ (US), /ˈsɛlɪbrənt/ (UK)| n.| 庆祝者；(尤指) 主祭；司仪|A person who performs a rite, especially a religious rite; a person who celebrates something.|The celebrant at the wedding was a close friend of the family. 婚礼的主祭是这个家庭的密友。|近义词：officiant, minister, priest, master of ceremonies|反义词: mourner, attendee (in some contexts)|词性: noun


celebrant: /ˈsɛləbrənt/ (US), /ˈsɛlɪbrənt/ (UK)| adj.(较少使用)| 庆祝的；欢庆的|Characterized by celebration; festive.|The celebrant atmosphere filled the room with joy.  欢庆的气氛让房间充满了喜悦。|近义词：festive, celebratory, joyous|反义词:mournful, somber|词性: adjective
