Niccolò: /nikkoˈlɔ/ (Italian pronunciation)| proper n.|尼科洛|An Italian given name, the Italian form of Nicholas.  It is derived from the Greek name Nikolaos, meaning "victory of the people."|Niccolò Machiavelli was a famous Italian diplomat and writer. 尼科洛·马基雅维利是一位著名的意大利外交官和作家。| Example: My friend's name is Niccolò.  我的朋友叫尼科洛。|Synonym: Nicholas, Nicolò|Antonym: N/A|noun
