ferritic:/fəˈrɪtɪk/
adjective
铁素体的；铁素体钢的
Relating to or containing ferrite, an allotropic form of iron which is magnetic and has a body-centered cubic crystal structure, stable below 910°C.
The pipeline is made of ferritic steel.  这条管道由铁素体钢制成。

The component exhibited ferritic microstructure. 该组件呈现出铁素体微观结构。

synonym:
ferric, iron

antonym:
austenitic


词性: adjective
