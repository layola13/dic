amorpha:/əˈmɔːrfə/| n. | 无定形; 紫穗槐属; 无定形体|A genus of deciduous shrubs or small trees (family Leguminosae) of eastern North America, having spikes of small purplish flowers and bearing one-seeded pods. |例句：The amorpha can be found in eastern North America. 紫穗槐可以在北美东部找到。

例句：Amorpha shrubs are known for their unique flower spikes. 紫穗槐灌木以其独特的穗状花序而闻名。|近义词：indefinite form, formlessness|反义词: definite form |词性:noun
