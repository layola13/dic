nontruth:/ˈnɒnˌtruːθ/ /ˈnɑːnˌtruːθ/
noun
非真理；谎言；谬误
Something that is not true; a falsehood.
例句：He perpetuated the nontruth that he had been a war hero. 他一直散布着自己曾是一位战争英雄的谎言。

例句：The politician was accused of spreading nontruths during the campaign.  这位政客被指控在竞选期间散布谎言。

近义词：falsehood, lie, untruth, fib, fiction, fabrication
反义词：truth, fact, reality, verity

词性:n.
