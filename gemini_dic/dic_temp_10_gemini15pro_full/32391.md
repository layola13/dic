chilled-out:/ˌtʃɪld ˈaʊt/| adj.| 冷静的；放松的；悠闲的| Relaxed and easygoing; not stressed or anxious.  Origin: Blend of "chilled" (cooled) and "out" (relaxed). Popularized in the 1980s, likely influenced by the laid-back culture associated with reggae and hip-hop music.| I'm feeling chilled-out after that yoga class. 我在瑜伽课后感觉很放松。|Synonyms: relaxed, calm, easygoing, laid-back, mellow|Antonyms: stressed, anxious, tense, uptight|adjective

chilled-out:/ˌtʃɪld ˈaʊt/| n.| 冷静的人；放松的状态|A person who is relaxed and easygoing, or a state of relaxation. | He's a real chilled-out guy. 他真是个悠闲自在的人。|Synonyms: calmness, relaxation, tranquility|Antonyms: stress, anxiety, tension|noun
