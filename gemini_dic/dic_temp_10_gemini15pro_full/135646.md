nonpredatorily:/nɒnˈprɛdətərɪli/
adverb
非掠夺性地
in a way that does not involve predation; without preying on others.
The owl, surprisingly, fed nonpredatorily on the insects attracted to the light.  这只猫头鹰令人惊讶地以非掠夺性的方式捕食被光吸引来的昆虫。
The animals grazed nonpredatorily on the vegetation. 这些动物以非掠夺性的方式吃草。
Synonym: harmlessly, peacefully
Antonym: predatorily, aggressively
词性: 副词
