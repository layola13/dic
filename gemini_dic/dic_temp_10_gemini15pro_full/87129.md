herbarian:/hɜːrˈbeəriən/
n.
药草学家；草药专家
A person who specializes in the study of herbs.
The herbarian had an encyclopedic knowledge of medicinal plants.  这位草药专家对药用植物有着百科全书般的了解。
herbalist, botanist
None

词性: 名词
