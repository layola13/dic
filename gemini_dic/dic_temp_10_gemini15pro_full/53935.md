discourtesy:/dɪsˈkɜːrtəsi/
n.
粗鲁;失礼;无礼
Lack of courtesy; rudeness.
His discourtesy to the guests was embarrassing. 他对客人们的无礼令人尴尬。
近义词：rudeness, impoliteness, incivility, disrespect
反义词：courtesy, politeness, respect, civility
词性：名词

discourtesy:/dɪsˈkɜːrtəsi/
词源：Late Middle English: from Old French descourtoisie, from des- ‘not’ + courtoisie ‘courtesy’.
词性：名词
