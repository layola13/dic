explorer: /ɪkˈsplɔːrər/ (UK) /ɪkˈsplɔːrər/ (US)| n. |探险家；探测者；探测器|A person who travels to unknown places to discover and learn about them. Someone who investigates something new or different. Also refers to a device or program used to explore or navigate something, such as a file explorer in a computer system. |例句：
The explorer set off on a dangerous journey to the North Pole.  这位探险家开始了前往北极的危险旅程。

The Mars explorer sent back incredible images of the planet's surface. 火星探测器发回了令人难以置信的星球表面图像。

|近义词：adventurer, discoverer, investigator, pioneer, traveler|反义词: follower, settler |词性: noun
