myograph: /ˈmaɪəɡræf/ /ˈmaɪoʊɡræf/ | n. | 肌动描记器; 肌动电流描记器 | An instrument for recording the mechanical properties of muscles, such as their contractions and relaxations. | The myograph showed a significant increase in muscle activity after the stimulus was applied. 肌动描记器显示，刺激施加后肌肉活动显著增加。
