/ˈleksɪkəl/ | adj. | 词汇的；词典的 | Relating to the words or vocabulary of a language. |  Example: Lexical analysis is the first phase of a compiler. 词汇分析是编译器的第一阶段。

Example:  The lexical meaning of a word is often different from its connotative meaning. 一个词的词汇意义通常与其内涵意义不同。 | Synonyms: vocabulary, verbal, semantic | Antonyms: grammatical, syntactical | word property: adjective
