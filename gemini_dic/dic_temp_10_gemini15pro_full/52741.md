dikteon:/ˈdɪktiɒn/ | n. | dictation | the action of saying words aloud for someone to write down; a piece of writing or other work that is dictated. | The students were given a spelling test based on the dictation. 学生们根据听写进行了一次拼写测试。|  The secretary took the dictation in shorthand. 秘书用速记记录了口述内容。| synonym: transcription, recording | antonym: improvisation, extemporization | noun
