outjested:/ˌaʊtˈdʒɛstɪd/
verb
胜过…的玩笑，以玩笑胜过
surpassed in jesting; outdone in wit or humor
He outjested the comedian with a witty retort. 他以机智的反驳胜过了喜剧演员的玩笑。

近义词: outwitted, surpassed, bested
反义词: outdone, defeated

词性: verb

词源: From out- + jest, from Old French 'gester'
