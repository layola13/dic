doorga:/ˈdɔːrɡə/ | n. | 杜尔迦女神 |  Doorga (often spelled Durga) is a major Hindu goddess, often considered a principal form of the Goddess or Devi. She is a warrior goddess associated with protection, strength, motherhood, destruction, and battles. | Example: Doorga Puja is a major festival celebrated in India. 杜尔迦女神节是印度的一个重要节日。| Example:  Devotees offer prayers to Doorga for strength and protection.  信徒向杜尔迦女神祈祷以获得力量和保护。| Synonym: Devi, Shakti, Parvati | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a deity) | Noun
