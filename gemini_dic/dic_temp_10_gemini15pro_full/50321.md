deplorate:/dɪˈplɔːr/ /dɪˈplɔːreɪt/ | v. | 哀悼，痛惜，强烈反对 | To express strong disapproval of; to condemn. |  The UN deplored the violence and called for a ceasefire. 联合国谴责了暴力行为，并呼吁停火。

The president deplored the loss of life in the tragic accident. 总统对这起悲惨事故造成的生命损失表示哀悼。

| 近义词：lament, bewail, decry, condemn | 反义词：applaud, praise, commend | 词性: 动词

词源：Late 16th century (in the sense ‘weep for, lament’): from French déplorer, from Latin deplorare ‘weep for, give up for lost’, from de- ‘completely’ + plorare ‘weep’. The current sense dates from the mid 17th century.
