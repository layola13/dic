demeaning:/dɪˈmɪːnɪŋ/
adjective
贬低的，有损人格的
Causing someone to lose their dignity and self-respect.
His comments were very demeaning to the staff. 他的评论对员工来说非常贬低。
synonyms: degrading, humiliating, belittling, insulting
antonyms: uplifting, respectful, honoring, ennobling
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From demean + -ing.  Demean comes from the Old French *demener "to conduct (oneself)," and ultimately from Latin *minare "to drive (animals)." The sense shifted over time to "reduce in worth or character."
