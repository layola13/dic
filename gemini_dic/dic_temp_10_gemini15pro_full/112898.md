ludicrous:/ˈluːdɪkrəs/ | adj. | 荒谬的，可笑的 | So foolish, unreasonable, or out of place as to be amusing; ridiculous. |  His suggestion was so ludicrous that everyone burst out laughing. 他的建议太荒谬了，大家都笑了出来。

|  It's ludicrous to think that I could win the lottery. 认为我会中彩票真是可笑。

| 近义词: absurd, ridiculous, preposterous, farcical, comical | 反义词: reasonable, sensible, logical, rational | 词性: adjective
