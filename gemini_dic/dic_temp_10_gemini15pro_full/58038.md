droitzschka:/ˈdrɔɪtʃkə/ /ˈdraɪtʃkə/
noun
轻便马车；敞篷马车
A light, open horse-drawn carriage.
The count arrived in a stylish droitzschka, pulled by a pair of matching bays.  伯爵乘坐一辆时髦的轻便马车到达，由两匹相配的栗色马拉着。
The droitzschka bounced along the cobbled streets. 轻便马车在鹅卵石街道上颠簸前行。
Synonyms: carriage, buggy, phaeton
Antonyms: car, bus, train
