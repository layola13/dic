arrowheaded:/ˈærəʊˌhɛdɪd/ | adj. | 箭头形的，矢形的 | Shaped like an arrowhead; having a pointed, triangular shape. | The arrowheaded markings on the pottery were characteristic of the ancient culture.  陶器上的箭头形标记是古代文化的特征。| Near Synonyms:  sagittate, lanceolate, pointed, triangular | Antonyms: rounded, blunt, obtuse | word type: adjective

arrowheaded:/ˈærəʊˌhɛdɪd/ | n. | 箭头形的东西 | Something shaped like an arrowhead. |  The arrowheaded symbol marked the location of the treasure on the map.  地图上箭头形的符号标出了宝藏的位置。| Near Synonyms:  dart, wedge | Antonyms: circle, sphere | word type: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "arrowhead" + "-ed". "Arrowhead" comes from Old English *arowheafod (attested as areweheafod), from arwe "arrow" + heafod "head".  The "-ed" suffix transforms a noun into an adjective, indicating "having the shape or characteristics of".
