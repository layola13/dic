buller:/ˈbʊlər/| n.|矿工；矿井中用作信号的响铃|A person who works in a mine, especially one who extracts coal.  Also, a bell used as a signal in a mine.|The buller signaled the end of the shift. 矿工用铃声通知轮班结束。|Near synonyms: miner, collier|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a profession. Could use "surface worker" for contrast)|noun

buller:/ˈbʊlər/| n.|信号；响亮的铃声；大谎话|A signal, often a loud ringing sound. Can also refer to a blatant lie or exaggeration.|The buller sounded, warning of the impending danger. 铃声响起，警告即将到来的危险。|Near synonyms:  alarm, bell, clang, whopper (for lie)|Antonyms:  silence, whisper, truth|noun
