full-blood:/ˈfʊlˌblʌd/ | adj. n. |纯血统的；纯血统的动物（或人）|Of unmixed ancestry; purebred.  A person or animal of unmixed breed, especially a thoroughbred horse.|例句：He was a full-blood Arabian. 他是一匹纯血统的阿拉伯马。|近义词：purebred, thoroughbred|反义词：crossbred, hybrid|词性:形容词, 名词
Etymology: full + blood. "Full" meaning complete or entire, and "blood" referring to lineage or ancestry.  The term originated in the context of animal breeding, particularly horses.
