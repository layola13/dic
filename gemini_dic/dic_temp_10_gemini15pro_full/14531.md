averment:/əˈvɜːrmənt/
n.|断言，主张，声明|An affirmation or declaration, especially one made with confidence.  A positive assertion; a statement of fact.|The lawyer made the averment that his client was innocent. 律师声明他的客户是无辜的。|assertion, declaration, affirmation, statement, claim|denial, refutation, contradiction|noun
