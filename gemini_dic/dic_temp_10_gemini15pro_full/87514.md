heterodont:/ˈhɛtərəˌdɑːnt/| adj. n.|异齿型的，异齿动物|Having different kinds of teeth, such as incisors, canines, premolars, and molars, specialized for different functions.  Referring to an animal possessing such teeth.|Humans are heterodont mammals. 人类是异齿哺乳动物。|Most mammals are heterodont. 大多数哺乳动物都是异齿型的。|近义词:diphyodont|反义词:homodont|词性:adjective, noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *héteros* ("other, different") + *odóntos* (genitive of *odoús* "tooth")
