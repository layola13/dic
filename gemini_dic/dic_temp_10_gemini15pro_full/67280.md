exsanguinous:/ˌɛksˈsæŋɡwɪnəs/
adjective
血色全无的；苍白的；失血的
Having lost a great deal of blood; drained of blood.  Also, having a pale or bloodless appearance.
The exsanguinous patient was rushed into surgery.  这位脸色苍白的病人被紧急送往手术室。
The exsanguinous corpse was a chilling sight.  这具失血的尸体令人毛骨悚然。
synonyms: pale, pallid, ashen, bloodless, anemic
antonyms: ruddy, flushed, sanguine, healthy
词性: adjective
