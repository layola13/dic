mass-producers:/mæs prəˈdjuːsərz/| n.|大规模生产者，批量生产商|People or companies that produce goods in large quantities.|Ford was one of the first mass-producers of automobiles. 福特是第一批汽车大规模生产商之一。|Mass-producers often benefit from economies of scale. 大规模生产商通常受益于规模经济。|Synonyms: manufacturers, producers, bulk producers|Antonyms: artisans, craftspeople|noun
