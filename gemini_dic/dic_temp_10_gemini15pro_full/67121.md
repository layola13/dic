exponential: /ˌɛkspəˈnɛnʃəl/ | adj. n. | 指数的，幂的；指数，指数曲线 |A mathematical expression containing one or more exponents.  Characterized by or being an extremely rapid increase (as in size or extent). |The company experienced exponential growth in its first year.  这家公司在第一年经历了指数级增长。|近义词：rapid, accelerating, escalating |反义词: slow, gradual, linear |词性: adjective, noun

exponential: /ˌɛkspəˈnɛnʃəl/ | n. | 指数，指数曲线 | A mathematical function in which an independent variable appears as an exponent. | The exponential in the equation represents the growth rate.  这个等式中的指数代表增长率。 |近义词：power, index |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *exponentialis, from Latin exponere "to put forth, set forth, explain," from ex- "out" + ponere "to put, place".
