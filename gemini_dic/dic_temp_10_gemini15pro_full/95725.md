inconsequentially:/ˌɪnkɒnsɪˈkwɛnʃəli/ /ˌɪnkɑːnsɪˈkwɛnʃəli/| adv.|无关紧要地，无足轻重地|In a manner that is not important or significant; trivially.|He waved his hand inconsequentially, dismissing her concerns. 他漫不经心地挥了挥手，对她的担忧不屑一顾。|The conversation drifted inconsequentially from topic to topic.  对话漫无目的地从一个话题跳到另一个话题。|近义词：trivially, insignificantly, unimportantly, meaninglessly|反义词：significantly, importantly, consequentially|词性:副词
Etymology:
From inconsequential + -ly. Inconsequential from in- + consequential. Consequential from consequence + -ial. Consequence from Old French consequence, from Late Latin consequentia, from Latin consequentem (nominative consequens), present participle of consequi (“to follow”), from con- + sequi (“to follow”).
