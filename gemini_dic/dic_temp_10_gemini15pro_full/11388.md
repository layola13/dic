argali:/ˈɑːrɡəli/ | n. | 盘羊，大角羊 | A wild sheep (Ovis ammon) of the mountains of central Asia, having large, curved horns. |  The argali is the largest wild sheep in the world. 盘羊是世界上最大的野绵羊。

The argali's massive horns are prized by trophy hunters. 盘羊巨大的角受到战利品猎人的青睐。 | 近义词: wild sheep, mountain sheep | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可考虑domestic sheep 家养绵羊作为相对概念) | 词性: noun

词源: From Mongolian "argali".
