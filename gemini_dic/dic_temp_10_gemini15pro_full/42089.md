cotransduces:/ˌkoʊtrænsˈdjuːsiz/
|v.|共同转导|To transfer genetic material from one bacterium to another along with other genetic material.|Bacteriophage P1 can cotransduce markers on the E. coli chromosome.  P1噬菌体可以共同转导大肠杆菌染色体上的标记。|transduce, transfer|None|verb
cotransduction:/ˌkoʊtrænsˈdʌkʃən/
|n.|共同转导|The simultaneous transduction of two or more genes.|Cotransduction frequency can be used to map bacterial genes. 共同转导频率可用于绘制细菌基因图。|transduction, joint transduction|None|noun
