bookful: /ˈbʊkfʊl/ | adj. | 充满书的；饱读诗书的；学识渊博的 | Filled with books; well-read; knowledgeable. |  He has a bookful mind, always quoting from various authors. 他学识渊博，总是引用各种作家的名言。| 近义词: erudite, learned, scholarly | 反义词: ignorant, illiterate, unlearned | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "book" + "-ful" (suffix indicating "full of").  由"book"（书）加上后缀"-ful"（表示“充满...的”）构成。
