all intents and purposes:/ɔːl ɪnˈtɛnts ænd ˈpɜːrpəsɪz/ |phrasal preposition|实际上；事实上|This phrase means "in every practical sense" and emphasizes the completeness or effectiveness of something. While often criticized as redundant, it remains a common idiom.| For all intents and purposes, the project is complete.  这个项目实际上已经完成了。|Near synonyms: in effect, essentially, for all practical purposes|Opposite: in theory, hypothetically|Phrase

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
For all intents and purposes, he runs the show.  他实际上负责一切。
She, to all intents and purposes, lives here now.  她现在实际上住在这里。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
He is, for all intents and purposes, running the company. 他实际上正在经营这家公司。
They are, to all intents and purposes, living as a married couple. 他们实际上正以夫妻的身份生活在一起。
