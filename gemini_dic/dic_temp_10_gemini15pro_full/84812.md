handwritten:/ˈhændrɪtn/| adj.|手写的|written by hand; not typed or printed.|例句：This is a handwritten letter. 这是一封手写的信。|例句：Handwritten notes often feel more personal. 手写笔记通常感觉更亲切。|近义词：penned, script, inscribed|反义词:typed, printed|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From hand + write, with the past participle suffix -en.  Essentially, "written by hand".
