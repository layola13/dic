pachydactylous:/ˌpækiˈdæktɪləs/
adjective
厚趾的；粗指的
Having abnormally thick fingers or toes.
Example: The pachydactylous gecko clung easily to the vertical surface.  厚趾壁虎很容易地附着在垂直的表面上。

Example:  Pachydactylous limbs are a common adaptation in tree-dwelling amphibians.  厚趾的四肢是树栖两栖动物常见的适应性特征。
synonym: thick-fingered, thick-toed
antonym: slender-fingered, slender-toed
词性: adjective
