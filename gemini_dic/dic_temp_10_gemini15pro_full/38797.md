conductorless:/kənˈdʌktərlɪs/
adjective
无指挥的；没有指挥的
Without a conductor.
The orchestra gave a conductorless performance of Beethoven's Fifth Symphony.  这支管弦乐队无指挥地演奏了贝多芬的第五交响曲。
conductorless concerts are becoming increasingly popular. 无指挥音乐会正变得越来越受欢迎。
synonyms: self-conducted, unconducted
antonyms: conducted, led
