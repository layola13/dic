festivities:/feˈstɪvətiz/ (US), /fɛˈstɪvɪtiz/ (UK)| n. | 庆祝活动；节庆 |  Plural form of festivity, referring to multiple celebrations or events. | The wedding festivities lasted for three days. 婚礼庆祝活动持续了三天。 | celebrations, festivals, merrymaking | mourning, grief, sadness | noun
