anchorwomen: /ˈæŋkərˌwɪmɪn/
n.
女主播；女主持人
A woman who presents and coordinates a live television or radio program, especially a news program.
Barbara Walters was one of the most influential anchorwomen in television history.
芭芭拉·沃尔特斯是电视史上最有影响力的女主播之一。

The anchorwomen kept the audience informed about the developing crisis.
女主播不断向观众通报危机的发展情况。

近义词: anchor, newscaster, presenter, broadcaster, reporter
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps guest, interviewee)
词性: noun
