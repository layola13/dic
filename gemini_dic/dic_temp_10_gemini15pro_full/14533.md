avernian: /əˈvɜːrniən/
adjective
地狱般的；阴森的；与地狱相关的
relating to hell or the underworld; infernal; gloomy
例句：The avernian landscape of the volcano was a terrifying sight. 火山的阴森景象令人恐惧。

例句：The poet described the avernian depths of despair. 诗人描述了绝望的地狱般深渊。


近义词：infernal, hellish, stygian, chthonic
反义词：heavenly, celestial
词性: adjective


词源:
From Latin Avernus, the name of a lake near Naples believed to be the entrance to the underworld.
