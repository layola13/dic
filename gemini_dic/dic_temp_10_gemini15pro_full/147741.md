overproduced: /ˌoʊvərprəˈduːst/ | v. adj.| 生产过剩，过度生产 | to produce more of something than is needed or wanted | The factory overproduced and now they have a surplus of goods. 工厂生产过剩，现在他们有多余的货物。 | The market was overproduced with cheap electronics. 市场充斥着廉价的电子产品。| oversupply, glut | undersupply, shortage | verb, adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + produced, past participle of produce.  "Over-" signifies "excessively" or "too much." "Produce" comes from the Latin "producere," meaning "to lead forth, bring out, prolong, extend."
