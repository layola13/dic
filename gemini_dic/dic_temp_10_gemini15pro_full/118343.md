meridionality:/ˌmɛrɪdiəˈnælɪti/ | n. |南北方向性，经向性|The state or quality of extending north and south; the condition of being determined by north-south alignment.  It often refers to the influence of latitude on climate, vegetation, or culture.|The meridionality of the mountain range affects the local climate.  这座山脉的南北方向性影响了当地的气候。|Synonym: north-south orientation|Antonym: latitudinality|noun
