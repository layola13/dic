outjuggling:/ˌaʊtˈdʒʌɡlɪŋ/
verb
胜过；以更精湛的杂耍技巧胜过
surpassing in juggling skill; outdoing in trickery or deception
He outjuggled his opponent in the competition. 他在比赛中以更精湛的杂耍技巧胜过了他的对手。
outmaneuver, outsmart, outwit
outsmart
