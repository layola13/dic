divertor:/daɪˈvɜːrtər/ | n. |分流器，偏滤器|A device that redirects something, especially unwanted or hazardous material, away from its normal path.  A key component in fusion reactors, diverting exhaust plasma and impurities from the core.|The divertor successfully channeled the excess heat away from the reactor core. 分流器成功地将多余的热量从反应堆堆芯引走。|Synonyms: deflector, diverter, shunt |Antonyms: concentrator, collector|noun

divertor:/daɪˈvɜːrtər/ | n. |转向器|A device used to change the direction of something, such as a stream of water or a flow of traffic.|The divertor on the highway redirected traffic away from the accident. 高速公路上的转向器将交通从事故现场引开。|Synonyms: deflector, diverter, shunt |Antonyms: concentrator, collector|noun
