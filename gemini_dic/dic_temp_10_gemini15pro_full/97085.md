infertilely: /ɪnˈfɜːtaɪlli/ | adv. | 不育地，不结果地 | in a way that is not fertile; unproductively | The plants grew infertilely in the poor soil. 这些植物在贫瘠的土壤中生长不良。| 近义词: barrenly, unproductively, sterilely | 反义词: fertilely, productively | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From in- (not) + fertile + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Fertile" comes from Old French "fertil,"  ultimately from Latin "fertilis" (bearing, producing), from "ferre" (to bear, carry).
