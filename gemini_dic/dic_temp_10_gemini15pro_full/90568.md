huh:/hʌ/ | int. | 嗯？;啊？;什么？(表示惊讶、疑问、不理解等) | An interjection expressing surprise, confusion, or lack of understanding. | Huh? What did you say?  嗯？ 你说什么？ |  Huh? I didn't hear you. 嗯？ 我没听见你说话。| 近义词: what, eh | 反义词:  (无明确反义词) | interjection
