crescendo:/krəˈʃendoʊ/ (US), /krəˈʃɛndoʊ/ (UK)| n. adj. adv.|渐强，高潮|a gradual increase in loudness, or the moment when a sound is at its loudest;  increasing in intensity or force.|The music reached a crescendo and then faded away. 音乐声逐渐增强到最高点，然后逐渐消失。|近义词：climax, culmination, peak, surge|反义词: diminuendo, decrescendo|noun, adjective, adverb
