aghh:/ɑː/ | interj. |啊; 呀; 哎哟 | An exclamation expressing pain, disgust, or annoyance. | Example: Aghh, that hurt! 哎哟，疼！ | Example: Aghh, this milk is sour!  呀，这牛奶馊了！ | 近义词: ouch, ugh, ew | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for an interjection) | 词性: Interjection
