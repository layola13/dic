nonadmissibility:/ˌnɒnədˌmɪsəˈbɪləti/ /ˌnɑːnədˌmɪsəˈbɪləti/| n. |不可采性，不可接纳性|The state or quality of being inadmissible; inability to be admitted or accepted.|His nonadmissibility as evidence in court was a major setback for the prosecution.  他的证词在法庭上不可采纳对控方来说是一个重大的挫折。|Near Synonyms: inacceptability, ineligibility, unsuitability|Antonyms: admissibility, acceptability, eligibility|noun
