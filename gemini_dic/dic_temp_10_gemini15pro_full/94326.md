impetus:/ˈɪmpɪtəs/| n. |动力，刺激，推动|Something that makes a process or activity happen or happen more quickly.  The force or energy with which a body moves.|The discovery of gold gave a fresh impetus to the development of the town. 黄金的发现为该镇的发展注入了新的动力。|Synonyms: stimulus, incentive, motivation, drive, impulse, spur|Antonyms: hindrance, obstacle, deterrent|noun
