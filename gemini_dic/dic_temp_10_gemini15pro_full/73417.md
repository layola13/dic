forelay:/fɔːrˈleɪ/
verb
预铺；预先安排；抢先占领
to arrange or do something in advance
例句：They forelayed the ground with straw. 他们在场地预先铺上了稻草。

例句：The company forelayed plans for expansion. 公司预先安排了扩张计划。


近义词：anticipate, prepare, prearrange
反义词：improvise


ETYMOLOGY->
From fore- + lay.  Fore- means "beforehand" or "in front." Lay means "to put or place."
