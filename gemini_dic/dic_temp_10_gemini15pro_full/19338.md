bibliolatrist: /ˌbɪbliˈɒlətrɪst/ /ˌbɪbliˈɑːlətrɪst/
bibliolatrist: n.
bibliolatrist: 崇拜圣经的人；圣经至上主义者
bibliolatrist: A person who regards the Bible as inerrantly inspired and thus the ultimate source of truth.
bibliolatrist:  The bibliolatrist refused to accept any scientific findings that contradicted his interpretation of scripture.  这位圣经至上主义者拒绝接受任何与他对经文的解释相矛盾的科学发现。
bibliolatrist: synonym: fundamentalist, literalist
bibliolatrist: antonym: freethinker, skeptic
bibliolatrist: noun
