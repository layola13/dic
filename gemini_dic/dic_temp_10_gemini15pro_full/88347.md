hippolith:/ˈhɪpəˌlɪθ/
n.
马粪石，马粪化石
A concretion of fossilized horse dung.
The hippolith was discovered embedded in the ancient riverbed. 这块马粪石是在古河床中发现的。
The museum displayed a collection of hippoliths from various geological eras.  博物馆展出了一批来自不同地质年代的马粪石。
coprolite, fossil dung
 
Etymology:
From Greek *hippos* (horse) + *lithos* (stone)
