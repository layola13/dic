outwalk:/ˌaʊtˈwɔːk/
verb
胜过…的步行速度；走得比…远；走得比…久
to walk farther or faster than someone
例句：He easily outwalked his companions. 他轻松地超过了他的同伴们。

例句：She outwalked everyone on the hiking trip.  她在徒步旅行中比任何人都走得远。

近义词：outrun, outpace, surpass
反义词：lag behind, fall behind
词性：v.
