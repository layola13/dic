diseaseful:/dɪˈziːzfʊl/
adjective
疾病缠身的；引起疾病的；有害的
Causing disease; harmful; unhealthy.
例句：
The swamp was a diseaseful place, filled with mosquitoes and stagnant water.  这片沼泽是一个疾病丛生的地方，到处都是蚊子和死水。

The polluted air is diseaseful and harmful to our lungs.  污染的空气有害健康，对我们的肺部有害。

近义词：
unhealthy, insalubrious, noxious, pestilential, pathogenic
反义词：
healthy, salubrious, wholesome

词性:
adjective
