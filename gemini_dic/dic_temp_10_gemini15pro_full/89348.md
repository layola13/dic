homologon:/həˈmɑːləɡɑːn/ /ˈhɒməlɒɡɒn/| n. |同源物，对应物|Something that corresponds to something else in a different context, especially a gene, organ, or structure that has evolved from the same ancestral form but now differs in function or structure.|The human arm and a bird's wing are homologons.|近义词： counterpart, analogue, equivalent|反义词: - |名词
