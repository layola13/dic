handshaker:/ˈhændˌʃeɪkər/| n.|握手者；健谈的人；交际家|Someone who shakes hands, often to seal a deal or greet someone.  Also, a person who is outgoing and enjoys meeting new people.|例句：He's a real handshaker, always working the room at networking events. 他真是个交际家，总是在社交活动中与人寒暄。

例句：The handshaker sealed the deal with a firm grip. 握手者以坚定的握手达成了协议。|近义词：socializer, mingler, networker, extrovert|反义词：introvert, recluse, wallflower|词性:noun
