antieavesdropping:/ˌæntiˌiːvzˈdrɒpɪŋ/ | adj. | 防窃听的 | Designed to prevent eavesdropping, the secret meeting was held in a secure, soundproof room. |  The government uses antieavesdropping technology to protect sensitive information. | 近义词: secure, confidential, private | 反义词: insecure, public, open | 词性: 形容词

antieavesdropping:/ˌæntiˌiːvzˈdrɒpɪŋ/ | n. | 防窃听 | Antieavesdropping measures are crucial for maintaining privacy. | The development of antieavesdropping technology is a constantly evolving field. | 近义词: counter-surveillance, security | 反义词: surveillance, espionage | 词性: 名词


词源:  "anti-" (against) + "eavesdropping" (secretly listening to a conversation).  "Eavesdropping" comes from "eavesdrip," referring to water dripping from the eaves of a house, and the idea of someone standing under the eaves to listen to conversations inside.
