ballahou:/ˌbæləˈhuː/| n.|巴拉胡鱼，大西洋比目鱼的一种|A large flatfish (Hippoglossus hippoglossus) of the North Atlantic, valued as a food fish.  It is the largest flatfish in the world.|We caught a huge ballahou on our fishing trip. 我们在钓鱼之旅中捕获了一条巨大的巴拉胡鱼。|近义词：halibut, Atlantic halibut|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
