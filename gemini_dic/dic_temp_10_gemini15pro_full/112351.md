loriner:/ˈlɔːrɪnər/
n.
马具商；制造和修理马嚼子和其他金属马具的人
A maker or repairer of bits and other metal parts of horse harnesses.
The loriner carefully adjusted the bit in the horse's mouth.  马具商仔细地调整了马嘴里的嚼子。
近义词：saddler, harness maker
反义词:  None (No direct opposite for a profession)
词性: noun

loriner:/ˈlɔːrɪnər/
词源：From Anglo-French *lorenier, from Old French lorainier, from lorain 'bridle', from Latin lorum 'thong, strap'.
A loriner was a specialized craftsman who made and repaired bits, bridles, and other metalwork associated with horse harnesses. The word is now largely archaic, as the trade itself has declined with the decreased use of horses.
The loriner's shop was filled with the tools of his trade.  马具商的店铺里摆满了他的工具。
近义词：saddler, harness maker
反义词:  None
词性: noun
