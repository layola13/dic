paranormally:/ˌpærəˈnɔːrməli/| adv.|超自然地，异常地|In a way that is beyond the range of normal experience or scientific explanation.|She claimed to be able to communicate paranormally with her deceased grandmother. 她声称能够以超自然的方式与她已故的祖母交流。|The lights flickered paranormally, suggesting a ghostly presence. 灯光闪烁不定，暗示着幽灵的存在。|近义词：supernaturally, abnormally, mystically|反义词：normally, naturally, scientifically|词性:副词

词源:
para- (prefix): beyond, beside, near, altered, irregular (来自希腊语 παρά para)
normal (adjective): conforming to a standard; usual, typical or expected (来自拉丁语 normalis)
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives
