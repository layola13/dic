buttoned:/ˈbʌt(ə)nd/| adj.|扣纽扣的，系纽扣的| fastened with buttons; having buttons.|She wore a buttoned-up blouse. 她穿着一件扣好纽扣的衬衫。|近义词：fastened, closed|反义词：unbuttoned, open|词性:形容词
Etymology: From the verb "button" + "-ed" (suffix forming past participles and adjectives). The word "button" comes from Old French "boton," meaning "bud," referring to the resemblance of a button to a flower bud.
