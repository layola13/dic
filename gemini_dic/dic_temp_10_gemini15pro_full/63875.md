epilogation:/ɪˌpiːlɒˈɡeɪʃən/ |n.| 延长;加长;补充说明 |The epilogation of the meeting caused frustration among the attendees. 会议的延长引起了与会者的不满。 |The epilogation of his speech made it less impactful.  他演讲的加长使得它不那么有影响力了。|近义词: prolongation, extension, lengthening |反义词: shortening, abbreviation, curtailment |词性: noun
词源: From Late Latin *epilogatio, from Latin epilogare "to add a conclusion," from epi- "upon, in addition" + log- "speak," related to logos "word."
