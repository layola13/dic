ingrafter:/ɪnˈɡræftər/ | n. | 接枝者；嫁接者 |  A person who grafts plants, especially fruit trees. | The skilled ingrafter carefully joined the scion to the rootstock.  熟练的嫁接者小心翼翼地将接穗接到砧木上。 | 近义词: grafter | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps one could consider someone who *removes* grafts) | 词性: noun
