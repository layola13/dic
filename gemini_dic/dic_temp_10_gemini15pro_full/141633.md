optimality:/ˌɒptɪˈmælɪti/ | n. | 最优性，最佳状态 | The state or quality of being optimal; the best possible result or solution. |The algorithm aims for optimality in resource allocation.  该算法旨在实现资源分配的最佳状态。

Optimality in design is crucial for efficient performance.  设计的最优性对于高效的性能至关重要。 | 近义词：idealness, perfection, optimality | 反义词：suboptimality, inferiority | 词性：名词

Etymology: From optimal + -ity.  Optimal comes from Latin optimus "best," superlative of bonus "good."
