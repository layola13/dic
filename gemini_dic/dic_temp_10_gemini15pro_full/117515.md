meenie:/ˈmiːni/| n.|小人物，无足轻重的人，小人|A meenie is a person considered to be unimportant or insignificant, often used in a derogatory way.|He treated his employees like meenies, never acknowledging their hard work. 他对待他的员工像小人物一样，从不承认他们的辛勤工作。|近义词：nobody, nonentity, insignificant person|反义词: VIP, important person|名词
