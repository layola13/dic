nonanatomical:/ˌnɒnˌænəˈtɒmɪkəl/ | adj. | 非解剖的；非结构上的 | Not relating to anatomy or bodily structure; not based on anatomical considerations. | The artist took a nonanatomical approach to the human form, focusing on abstract shapes rather than realistic proportions. 这位艺术家对人体形态采取了非解剖学的方法，专注于抽象的形状而不是现实的比例。 |  The study used a nonanatomical model to simulate blood flow.  这项研究使用了一个非解剖模型来模拟血液流动。 |近义词：nonstructural, abstract, conceptual |反义词: anatomical, structural |词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
anatomical (adjective): from Late Latin anatomicus, from Greek anatomikos "of or for dissection," from anatome "dissection," from ana- "up" + temnein "to cut"
