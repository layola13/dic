ablegate: /ˈæblɪɡeɪt/
n.
代表，使节
A person sent by another as a representative; a legate.
The Pope sent an ablegate to the conference.  教皇派了一名代表参加会议。

The president sent an ablegate to the international summit. 总统派遣了一名使节参加国际峰会。

近义词: representative, delegate, envoy, emissary, legate
反义词: none (This word represents a role, not a quality, so it doesn't have a direct opposite.)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ablegatus, past participle of *ablegare (“to send away, dispatch as a legate”), from Latin a, ab (“from, away from”) + legare (“to send as a legate, depute, bequeath”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *leǵ- (“to collect, gather”).
