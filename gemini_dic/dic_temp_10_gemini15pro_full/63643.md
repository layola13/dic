eosaurus:/ˌiːoʊˈsɔːrəs/ | n. | 始蜥蜴，始祖蜥 |  An extinct reptile of the late Carboniferous and Permian periods, considered to be an early ancestor of the dinosaurs. | Example 1: The eosaurus is a fascinating subject for paleontologists. 始祖蜥是古生物学家们感兴趣的课题。

Example 2:  Museums display fossils of eosaurus to showcase early reptilian evolution.  博物馆展示始祖蜥的化石来展示早期爬行动物的进化。 | 近义词: early reptile, ancestor of dinosaurs | 反义词: descendant (in a broad sense) | 词性: noun
