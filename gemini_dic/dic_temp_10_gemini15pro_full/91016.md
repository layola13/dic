husbandship:/ˈhʌzbəndʃɪp/| n.|丈夫的身份或职责；婚姻状况；<古>节俭，精打细算|The state or condition of being a husband; the qualities or skills expected of a husband. Also, in archaic usage, thrift or good management.|He took pride in his husbandship, always ensuring his family was well cared for. 他以自己作为丈夫的职责为荣，总是确保他的家人得到很好的照顾。|Near synonyms: marriage, matrimony, wedlock|Antonyms: bachelorhood, spinsterhood|noun
