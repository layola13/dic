bare-up:/bɛər ˈʌp/ | phrasal verb | 鼓起勇气；振作起来；打起精神 | To summon one's courage or fortitude, especially in the face of adversity. | Example 1:  "Bare up, there's no need to cry. It'll be alright."  鼓起勇气来，没必要哭。一切都会好起来的。

Example 2: "He told himself to bare up and face the challenge head-on."  他告诉自己要振作起来，正面迎接挑战。 | Synonyms:  brace oneself,  stiffen one's upper lip,  pluck up one's courage, take heart,  keep one's chin up  | Antonyms:  lose heart,  give up,  despair  | verb phrase
