outedges:/ˈaʊtˌɛdʒɪz/| n.|出边，外出边，输出边|The outedges of the graph represent the connections from one node to another.  图的输出边表示从一个节点到另一个节点的连接。|This algorithm efficiently calculates the outedges of each vertex in the network.  这个算法有效地计算了网络中每个顶点的输出边。|近义词：outgoing edges,  exit edges|反义词：inedges, incoming edges|noun
