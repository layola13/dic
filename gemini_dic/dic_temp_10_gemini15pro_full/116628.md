matronymics:/ˌmætrəˈnɪmɪks/| n. |母系姓氏；取自母亲名字的姓氏|A system of naming in which an individual's surname is derived from the given name of their mother.|Many Icelandic surnames are matronymics, formed by adding "dóttir" (daughter) or "son" to the mother's given name.|Synonyms: metronymics|Antonyms: patronymics|noun
