hubristically:/ˌhjuːbrɪˈstɪkli/ | adv. | 傲慢地，狂妄自大地 | In a way that shows excessive pride or self-confidence, often leading to downfall. | He hubristically boasted about his invincibility, unaware of the impending danger. 他傲慢地吹嘘自己不可战胜，却没有意识到即将到来的危险。 | Examples: The young athlete hubristically declared he would win the championship. 年轻的运动员傲慢地宣称他会赢得冠军。  She hubristically dismissed the advice of her elders. 她傲慢地无视了长辈的建议。 | Synonyms: arrogantly, presumptuously, haughtily | Antonyms: humbly, modestly, respectfully | 词性: 副词

词源: From Greek "hybris" (ὕβρις) meaning "excessive pride" + "-istic" + "-ally".
