hanai:/hɑːˈnaɪ/| n.|收养，非正式收养（尤指在夏威夷文化中）;被收养者|A relationship like that of parent to child, where the child is raised by someone other than their biological parents, especially common in Hawaiian culture.  The word can also refer to the child being raised in this way.|例句：She considers him her hanai son, even though he's not officially adopted. 她视他为自己的hānai儿子，即使他没有被正式收养。

例句：He was raised as a hanai child in a loving family. 他在一个充满爱的家庭里作为hānai孩子被抚养长大。|近义词：adopted, foster, informal adoption|反义词: biological child |词性:noun
