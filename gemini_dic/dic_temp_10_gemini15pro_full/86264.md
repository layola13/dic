heavenward:/ˈhɛvnwərd/ | adv. adj.| 向天堂地，向上地 | Moving or directed toward heaven; upward. |  The birds flew heavenward. 鸟儿向天空飞去。|  She looked heavenward and prayed. 她仰望天空祈祷。 | 近义词: skyward, upward, Godward | 反义词: earthward, downward | 词性: 副词，形容词

Etymology:
From "heaven" + "-ward" (Old English "-weard," meaning "toward, in the direction of").  "Heaven" comes from Old English "heofon," meaning "sky, firmament."
