gourd:/ɡɔːrd/| n. |葫芦；葫芦科植物|A hard-shelled fruit with a rounded shape, the dried shell of which can be used as a container or utensil.  A plant that bears gourds.|例句：She used a dried gourd as a water bottle. 她用一个干葫芦当水壶。

例句：Gourds are commonly used for decoration during the autumn harvest festivals.  葫芦在秋季丰收节期间通常被用作装饰。|近义词：squash, pumpkin|反义词:|noun
