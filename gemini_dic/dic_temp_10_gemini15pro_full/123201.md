monochromes:/ˈmɒnəkrəʊmz/ /ˈmɑːnəkroʊmz/| n. |单色画, 单色照片; 单色印刷品|A photograph or picture developed or executed in black and white or in varying tones of one color. |The gallery featured an exhibition of monochromes, stark images that captured the essence of the desert landscape.  这个画廊举办了一个单色画展览，这些鲜明的图像捕捉到了沙漠景观的精髓。| synonyms: monochrome pictures, black and white photographs, grayscale images | antonyms: polychromes, color photographs, multicolored images| noun


monochrome:/ˈmɒnəkrəʊm/ /ˈmɑːnəkroʊmz/| adj. |单色的|Having or consisting of one color or shades of one color. |The artist preferred to work in monochrome, finding beauty in the subtle gradations of gray. 这位艺术家喜欢用单色创作，在灰色的微妙渐变中发现美。|synonyms: single-colored, one-colored, monochromatic | antonyms: polychrome, multicolored, multi-colored | adjective


monochrome:/ˈmɒnəkrəʊm/ /ˈmɑːnəkroʊmz/| v. |使成单色|To convert an image or display into monochrome. |The software can monochrome the image to reduce file size.  该软件可以将图像转换成单色以减小文件大小。|synonyms: convert to monochrome, make monochromatic, desaturate | antonyms: colorize, add color | verb
