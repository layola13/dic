daft:/dɑːft/ /dæft/| adj.|愚蠢的，傻的；疯狂的，不理智的|Silly, foolish; crazy, unwise.|He had a daft idea about climbing Mount Everest in his pyjamas. 他有一个愚蠢的想法，想穿着睡衣攀登珠穆朗玛峰。|Synonyms: silly, foolish, stupid, idiotic, insane, mad, crazy|Antonyms: sensible, wise, intelligent, rational|adjective
