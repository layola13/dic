cotyledonary:/ˌkɒtɪˈliːdənəri/| adj.|子叶的，|Relating to or resembling a cotyledon; having to do with cotyledons.  The cotyledonary stage is crucial for seedling establishment. |The bean plant has two cotyledonary leaves.|近义词：embryonic, seminal|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps 'mature' in context)|词性: adjective

cotyledon:/ˌkɒtɪˈliːdən/| n.|子叶|The embryonic leaf in seed-bearing plants, one or more of which are the first leaves to appear from a germinating seed.|The bean seed has two cotyledons.|近义词：seed leaf|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: noun
