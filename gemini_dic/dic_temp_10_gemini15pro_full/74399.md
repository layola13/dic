frampoid:/ˈfræmpɔɪd/
adjective|古怪的，难以相处的，脾气暴躁的|Unpleasant or bad-tempered; irritable.  Having a difficult and eccentric personality, often appearing irritable or grumpy.|She had a rather frampoid personality and was difficult to get along with.|近义词：cantankerous, irascible, grumpy, curmudgeonly, churlish, sullen, surly|反义词：affable, amiable, genial, pleasant, agreeable|词性:adjective
词源:  Likely a blend of "frampold" (also meaning bad-tempered) and "-oid" (a suffix indicating resemblance or likeness).  "Frampold" itself is of uncertain origin, possibly related to "frumpish."
