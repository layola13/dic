dotcom:/ˈdɑːtˌkɑːm/ /ˈdɒtˌkɒm/
n.|网络公司；互联网公司|A company that does most of its business on the internet.|例句：Many dotcoms went bankrupt in the early 2000s. 许多网络公司在21世纪初破产了。|例句：Investing in dotcoms was very popular in the late 1990s. 在20世纪90年代后期，投资网络公司非常流行。|近义词：internet company, online business, e-business|反义词:brick-and-mortar store, offline business|词性:名词

dotcom:/ˈdɑːtˌkɑːm/ /ˈdɒtˌkɒm/
adj.|互联网的；网络公司的|Relating to or characteristic of internet companies.|例句：The dotcom boom led to a surge in technology stocks. 网络热潮导致科技股飙升。|例句：He made a fortune during the dotcom era. 他在网络时代发了财。|近义词：internet, online, web-based|反义词:offline, traditional|词性:形容词
