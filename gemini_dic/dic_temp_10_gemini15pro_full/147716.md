overpresumption:/ˌoʊvərprɪˈzʌmpʃən/ | n. | 过分自信；僭越；放肆 | An act of presuming too much; excessive confidence or boldness. |  He was punished for his overpresumption in questioning the king's authority. 他因僭越质疑国王的权威而受到惩罚。 |  Example: His overpresumption led him to believe he could win the race without training. 他的过分自信让他以为不用训练就能赢得比赛。|  Synonyms: arrogance, audacity, presumption, conceitedness, hubris | Antonyms: humility, modesty, diffidence | noun

overpresume:/ˌoʊvərprɪˈzuːm/ | v. | 过于自信；僭越；放肆  | To presume too much; to be excessively confident or bold. | He overpresumed when he assumed he would be promoted. 当他认为自己会升职时，他过于自信了。 | Example:  Don't overpresume and think you can skip the line. 不要放肆地认为你可以插队。 | Synonyms:  overreach, trespass, infringe | Antonyms:  defer, respect | verb
