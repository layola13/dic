outfencing: /ˌaʊtˈfɛnsɪŋ/
verb
击败…的围栏; 在击剑中胜过
defeating someone in fencing; surpassing in fencing skill.
He outfenced his opponent in the final match. 他在决赛中击败了他的对手。
She outfenced all the other competitors and won the gold medal. 她击败了所有其他选手，赢得了金牌。
近义词: outduel, defeat, conquer, vanquish
反义词: lose to
词性: verb

outfencing: /ˌaʊtˈfɛnsɪŋ/
noun
击剑击败; 击剑优势
The act of defeating someone in fencing; superiority in fencing.
His outfencing was remarkable, leaving his opponents bewildered. 他的击剑技巧令人瞩目，让对手们不知所措。
Her outfencing secured her place on the national team. 她的击剑优势确保了她进入国家队。
近义词: victory, triumph, mastery
反义词: defeat, loss
词性: noun
