boycotter:/ˈbɔɪkɒtər/ (UK) /ˈbɔɪkɑːtər/ (US)| n. |抵制者，联合抵制者|A person who boycotts someone or something. |Example 1: The boycotters picketed the store entrance. 抵制者在商店入口处设置纠察线抗议。

Example 2:  Many boycotters refused to buy products from that company. 许多抵制者拒绝购买那家公司的产品。|近义词：protester, objector, campaigner|反义词：supporter, endorser|词性: noun
