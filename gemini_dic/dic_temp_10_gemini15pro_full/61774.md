empeach:/ɪmˈpiːtʃ/| vt.| 弹劾，控告；怀疑，指责|To charge (a public official) before a competent tribunal with misconduct in office.  To call into question; cast doubt on.|The House of Representatives voted to impeach the president. 众议院投票弹劾总统。|Synonyms: accuse, indict, charge, censure|Antonyms: exonerate, acquit, absolve|verb
