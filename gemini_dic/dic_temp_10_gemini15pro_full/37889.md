complains:/kəmˈpleɪnz/ | v. | 抱怨，诉苦 | expresses dissatisfaction or annoyance about something. | He constantly complains about the noise. 他总是抱怨噪音。| grumbles, gripes, protests | accepts, praises | verb

complaining:/kəmˈpleɪnɪŋ/ | n. | 抱怨，诉苦 | the act of expressing dissatisfaction or annoyance. |  The constant complaining was getting on everyone's nerves.  不断的抱怨让每个人都很烦躁。| grievance, criticism | praise, acceptance | noun

词源:
From Old French complaindre, from Latin plangere "to lament."
