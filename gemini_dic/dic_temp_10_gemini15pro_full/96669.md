industriousness:/ɪnˈdʌstriəsnəs/| n.|勤奋，勤勉|The quality of being industrious; diligence.|Her industriousness and dedication led to her rapid promotion. 她的勤奋和奉献精神使她迅速晋升。|近义词：diligence, assiduity, application|反义词：laziness, indolence, sloth|词性:名词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from industrious + -ness.
