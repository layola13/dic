elementation:/ˌɛlɪmɛnˈteɪʃən/
n.
要素化; 元素化; 基本要素;
The process of reducing something to its basic elements or principles. The act of providing with elements or essential features.
例句：The elementation of the design made it easier to understand.  设计的要素化使其更容易理解。

例句：The architect focused on the elementation of form and function.  建筑师专注于形式和功能的基本要素。

近义词：simplification, reduction, analysis, decomposition
反义词：complexification, elaboration, synthesis
