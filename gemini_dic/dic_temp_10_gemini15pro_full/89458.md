honestly:/ˈɑːnɪstli/| adv.|真诚地，坦率地，实在地|Used to emphasize the truth or sincerity of a statement.  It can also be used to introduce a slightly critical or negative comment.| Honestly, I don't know what to do.  坦白说，我不知道该怎么办。| truthfully, frankly, sincerely, genuinely|dishonestly, falsely|adverb
