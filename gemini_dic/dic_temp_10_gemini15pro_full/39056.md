conformances:/kənˈfɔːmənsɪz/| n.|符合，一致，遵从（conformity 的复数形式）|The company's actions are in conformance with industry standards. 该公司的行为符合行业标准。|例句：All building materials are checked for conformances to required standards. 所有建筑材料都经过检查，以确保符合规定的标准。

例句：The software's conformances with security protocols are regularly audited. 该软件与安全协议的一致性会定期审核。

近义词：compliances, agreements, observances|反义词：deviations, violations, breaches|词性：名词

conformity:/kənˈfɔːməti/| n.|符合，一致，遵从|Conformity to social norms is often expected.  人们通常期望遵守社会规范。|例句：His art is characterized by a lack of conformity. 他的艺术特点是不墨守成规。

例句：There is increasing conformity in the way people dress. 人们的穿着方式越来越趋同。


近义词：compliance, obedience, agreement|反义词：nonconformity, individuality, rebellion|词性：名词

conform:/kənˈfɔːrm/| v.| 遵守，符合，遵照|He refused to conform to the rules. 他拒绝遵守规则。|例句：We must conform to the laws of the land. 我们必须遵守这个国家的法律。

例句：The building conforms to all safety regulations. 这栋建筑符合所有安全规定。

近义词：comply, obey, adhere|反义词：deviate, disobey, rebel|词性：动词


词源：From Old French conformer, from Latin conformare (“to form, shape, fashion, mold, arrange, modify, adapt”), from con- (“together”) + formare (“to form”).
