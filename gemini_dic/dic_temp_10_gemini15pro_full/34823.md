clines:/klaɪnz/ | n. | 渐变群，连续变异 | A series of interconnected populations of a species that exhibit gradual phenotypic and/or genetic differences over a geographical area. | Example 1:  Researchers studied the clines in flower color along the mountain slope. 研究人员研究了沿着山坡的花朵颜色渐变群。

Example 2: The clines in bird size demonstrate adaptation to different climates. 鸟类大小的渐变群展示了对不同气候的适应。 | 近义词: gradients, variations | 反义词: uniformity, homogeneity | noun
