extrications:/ˌɛkstrɪˈkeɪʃənz/
n.
解救，营救；摆脱困境
The act of extricating or the state of being extricated.  The process of freeing someone or something from a difficult or dangerous situation.
The firefighters worked tirelessly on the extrications of the victims from the collapsed building. 消防队员不知疲倦地工作，从倒塌的建筑物中解救受害者。
The extrication of the company from its financial difficulties took several years.  公司花了数年时间才摆脱了财务困境。
synonyms: rescues, releases, deliverances, liberations
antonyms: entanglements, imprisonments
noun
