evolves:/ɪˈvɒlvz/ (UK) /ɪˈvɑːlvz/ (US)| v.|进化，发展，演变|develops gradually, especially from a simple to a more complex form|例句：The company has evolved from a small startup to a multinational corporation.  这家公司已经从一家小型初创企业发展成为一家跨国公司。

例句：Languages evolve over time. 语言会随着时间推移而演变。

近义词：develop, progress, advance, mature|反义词: regress, devolve, degenerate|词性:动词
