chider:/ˈtʃaɪdər/| n.|责骂者，斥责者|A person who scolds or rebukes someone.|She's such a chider, always finding fault with everyone. 她真是个爱责骂的人，总是对每个人都吹毛求疵。|Example: The teacher acted as a chider when the students misbehaved. 老师在学生行为不端时充当了斥责者的角色。|Synonyms: critic, scolder, rebuker, reprover|Antonyms: praiser, flatterer|noun
