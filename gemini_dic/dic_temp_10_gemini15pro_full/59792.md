ecotours:/ˈiːkoʊtʊərz/
noun
生态旅游
Tours designed to minimize environmental impact and often to educate travelers about the natural environment.
例句：We booked several ecotours to explore the rainforest. 我们预订了几个生态旅游项目来探索雨林。

例句：Ecotours are becoming increasingly popular as people become more aware of environmental issues. 随着人们越来越关注环境问题，生态旅游变得越来越受欢迎。

近义词：nature tours, sustainable tourism, green tourism
反义词：mass tourism
