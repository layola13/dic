agaty:/ˈæɡəti/| adj.|玛瑙状的，像玛瑙的|Resembling agate in appearance, especially in having variegated colors or a banded structure.|The rock displayed an agaty texture, with swirling bands of pink and grey. 这块岩石呈现出玛瑙般的纹理，带有粉色和灰色的漩涡状条带。|近义词：agateous, banded, variegated, marbled|反义词:uniform, homogeneous|词性:形容词

词源: From agate + -y.  Ultimately from Greek ἀχάτης (akhátēs), the name of a Sicilian river.
