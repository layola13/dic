godmothership:/ˈɡɒdmʌðəʃɪp/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːdmʌðərʃɪp/ (US)| n. |教母身份；（比喻）指导、支持、保护|A position of guidance, support, and protection, often used metaphorically in the context of a country or organization acting as a sponsor or protector of another.|The United States acted as a godmothership for the fledgling democracy.|近义词：mentorship, sponsorship, patronage, guidance|反义词: neglect, abandonment|词性: noun
