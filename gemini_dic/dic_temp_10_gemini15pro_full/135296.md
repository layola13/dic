词源:
non- (prefix):  not
passionate (adjective): having, showing, or caused by strong feelings or beliefs. From Latin *passionem* (nominative *passio*) "suffering, enduring,"  later "strong emotion, affection of the soul."
-ness (suffix):  forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
