mineralogies: /ˌmɪnərˈælədʒiz/
|n.| 矿物学; 矿物构成|The study of minerals, their formation, classification, properties, and occurrence.  The mineral content of a rock or other substance.|例句：He's studying the mineralogies of different types of volcanic rock. 他正在研究不同类型火山岩的矿物学。

例句：The mineralogies of the two samples were quite different. 这两个样本的矿物构成差异很大。|近义词：mineral composition, mineral content|反义词: N/A|词性: Noun
