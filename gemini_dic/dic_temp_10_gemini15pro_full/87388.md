herpetological:/ˌhɜːrpətəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ /ˌhɜːrpətəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/| adj.|爬虫学的|Relating to the branch of zoology concerned with reptiles and amphibians.|Herpetological research is crucial for understanding ecosystem health. 爬虫学研究对于了解生态系统健康至关重要.|近义词:batrachological (specifically for amphibians)|反义词:None|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Greek *herpetón* "creeping thing" + -logical.
