nugget:/ˈnʌɡɪt/| n.|小块；金块；珍闻；|A small lump of a precious metal, especially gold, found in its natural state.  A small piece of something, especially food. A valuable idea or fact. |He found a gold nugget. 他发现了一块金子。| synonyms: lump, chunk, piece, bit, morsel, tidbit, gem, jewel| antonyms: whole, entirety| noun
