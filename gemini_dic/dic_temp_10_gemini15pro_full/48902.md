defrayed:/dɪˈfreɪd/
verb
支付，承担（费用）
to pay the cost or expenses of something
例句：The company defrayed the travel expenses of its employees. 公司承担了员工的差旅费。

例句：The scholarship defrayed the cost of his tuition. 奖学金支付了他的学费。
近义词：paid, covered, met, settled
反义词：incurred, owed
