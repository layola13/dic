creer:/kreɪər/ (US), /kriːər/ (UK)| v. |相信；认为|To believe something is true; to have trust or confidence in someone or something.| I believe in the power of positive thinking. 我相信积极思考的力量。| synonyms: believe, trust, have faith in | antonyms: disbelieve, doubt, distrust | verb

creer:/kreɪər/ (US), /kriːər/ (UK)| n. |信条; 信念 (古用法，现已少用)|A creed or belief. |  His personal creer was based on kindness and generosity. 他的个人信条是建立在善良和慷慨之上的。| synonyms: creed, belief, doctrine | antonyms: disbelief, doubt, skepticism | noun
