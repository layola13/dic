nonphotosynthetic:/ˌnɒnˌfoʊtoʊsɪnˈθɛtɪk/
adjective
非光合作用的
Not carrying out photosynthesis; not capable of using light as a source of energy.
Some bacteria are nonphotosynthetic and obtain energy from other sources.  一些细菌是非光合作用的，从其他来源获取能量。
Parasitic plants are often nonphotosynthetic, relying on their host for nutrients. 寄生植物通常是非光合作用的，依靠宿主获取营养。
non-photosynthetic,  non-photosynthesizing
