bescummer:/bɪˈskʌmər/| v.|弄脏，玷污|To soil or defile something, often with something unpleasant or dirty.| He bescummered his new shoes by stepping in mud. 他踩到泥里把新鞋子弄脏了。|例句： The scandal bescummered the reputation of the entire family.  这桩丑闻玷污了整个家族的名誉。

例句：  His clothes were bescummered with grease and grime. 他的衣服沾满了油脂和污垢。

近义词：soil, stain, sully, defile, taint, besmirch|反义词:clean, cleanse, purify|词性:动词
