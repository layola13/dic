dewy:/ˈdjuːi/
adj.
露湿的; 带有露水的; 新鲜的
Covered with or looking like dew; fresh.
The dewy grass sparkled in the morning sun. 露湿的草地在晨曦中闪闪发光。
近义词:damp, moist, fresh
反义词:dry, arid
词性:形容词

dew:/djuː/
n.
露; 露水
Tiny drops of water that form on cool surfaces at night, when atmospheric vapor condenses.
The grass was covered in dew. 草地上覆盖着露水。
近义词:moisture, condensation
反义词:dryness
词性:名词


词源:
From Middle English *dewinesse, *dewynesse, equivalent to dewy +‎ -ness.  最终源自 Proto-Germanic *dawaz (“dew”). 同源词包括 Old English dēaw, Old Saxon dau, Old Frisian daw, Old Norse dögg, Dutch dauw, German Tau.
