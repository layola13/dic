expropriationists: /ɪkˌsproʊpriˈeɪʃənɪsts/ | n. | 剥夺者；征收者 | People who seize property, often land, from others, typically for public use but sometimes for personal gain.  | The expropriationists seized the farmer's land without proper compensation. 剥夺者在没有适当赔偿的情况下夺取了农民的土地。| The government acted as expropriationists, taking control of the oil fields. 政府充当了征收者的角色，控制了油田。 |  Synonyms: confiscators, seizers, appropriators | Antonyms:  restorers, returners | noun
