motivating:/ˈmoʊtɪveɪtɪŋ/| adj.|激励人心的，鼓舞人心的|Providing incentive or encouragement.  Giving a reason for someone to act or behave in a particular way.|The coach's speech was very motivating. 教练的讲话非常鼓舞人心。|Synonyms: inspiring, encouraging, stimulating|Antonyms: demotivating, discouraging, disheartening|adjective
