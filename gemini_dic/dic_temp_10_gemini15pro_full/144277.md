outsourcing: /ˈaʊtsɔːrsɪŋ/ | n. | 外包；外购 | The practice of obtaining goods or services from an outside or foreign supplier, especially in place of an internal source. | Many companies choose outsourcing to reduce costs. 许多公司选择外包以降低成本。| The company outsourced its IT support to India. 这家公司将其IT支持外包给了印度。| 近义词: subcontracting, offshoring | 反义词: insourcing | 词性: noun
