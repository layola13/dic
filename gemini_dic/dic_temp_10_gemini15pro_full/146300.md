overfavour: /ˌoʊvərˈfeɪvər/ | v. | 过分偏袒，过分宠爱 | To show excessive favoritism towards someone or something. | He overfavoured his youngest son, causing resentment among his other children. 他过分偏袒最小的儿子，导致其他孩子不满。| 近义词: spoil, pamper, indulge | 反义词: neglect, ignore | 词性: 动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "favour" (from Old French "favor," ultimately from Latin "favere" - to be favorable to).
