lens:/lɛnz/ | n. |透镜；镜头；晶状体 |A piece of transparent material (such as glass) with curved surfaces that refract light rays to form an image.  A structure in the eye that focuses light rays onto the retina.| The camera lens was scratched. 相机的镜头被刮花了。|  The doctor said his eye lens was damaged. 医生说他的眼晶状体受损了。| synonyms: glass, optic, objective | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a physical object.  Perhaps "opaque object" in a metaphorical sense) | noun
