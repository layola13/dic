martyrize:/ˈmɑːrtəraɪz/ | vt. |折磨；使殉难；使受苦|To cause someone to suffer greatly, especially for their beliefs; to kill someone because of their beliefs.| He was martyrized for his religious beliefs. 他因宗教信仰而殉难。| 近义词: persecute, torture, torment | 反义词: protect, comfort | 词性: verb

martyrize:/ˈmɑːtəraɪz/ | vt. |（常作 martyrize oneself）佯装受苦；装可怜|To pretend to suffer or be a victim.|She martyrized herself by working late every night, hoping to gain sympathy from her boss. 她每晚都工作到很晚，装可怜，希望得到老板的同情。| 近义词: dramatize, exaggerate | 反义词: downplay, minimize | 词性: verb
