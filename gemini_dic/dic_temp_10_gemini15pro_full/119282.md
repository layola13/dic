micrencephalous:/ˌmaɪkrənˈsɛfələs/| adj.|小头的，小脑的|Having an abnormally small head and brain, often accompanied by intellectual disability.|The child was diagnosed as micrencephalous shortly after birth.  这个孩子出生后不久就被诊断为小头畸形。|Synonyms: microcephalic, microcephalous|Antonyms: macrocephalic, macrocephalous|adjective
