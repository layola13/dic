feverless:/ˈfiːvərlɪs/| adj.|无热的，不发烧的|Without a fever; not feverish.  The patient was feverless for three days before the rash appeared.|例句：The child was feverless this morning, but his temperature spiked in the afternoon.  今天早上孩子不发烧，但下午体温飙升了。

例句：Although she felt unwell, she was feverless. 尽管她感觉不舒服，但她没有发烧。|近义词：afebrile, apyretic|反义词：feverish, febrile|词性:形容词
