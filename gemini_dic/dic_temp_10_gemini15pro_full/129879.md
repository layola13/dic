non-intercourse: /ˌnɒnˈɪntəkɔːs/ (UK) /ˌnɑːnˈɪntərˌkɔːrs/ (US)
non-intercourse: n.|不来往，中断往来；（尤指）贸易禁运|The act of refusing to communicate or do business with someone or something, especially a country.|The Embargo Act of 1807 imposed non-intercourse between the United States and Great Britain. |1807年的禁运法案强制美国和英国中断往来。|
non-intercourse: |近义词: embargo, boycott, sanctions|反义词: trade, commerce, intercourse|词性: 名词
