inclement:/ɪnˈklɛmənt/| adj.|恶劣的；严酷的；狂风暴雨的|Not clement; severe, rough, or stormy; (of the weather) unpleasantly cold or wet.|例句：The game was canceled due to inclement weather. 由于天气恶劣，比赛被取消了。|例句：We decided to stay indoors because of the inclement weather conditions. 由于恶劣的天气状况，我们决定待在室内。|近义词：severe, harsh, stormy, tempestuous, wild|反义词: mild, calm, pleasant, fair|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from Latin inclement-, from in- ‘not’ + clement- ‘mild’ (see clement).
