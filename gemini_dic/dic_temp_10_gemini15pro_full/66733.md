exorcisements:/ˈeksɔːrsaɪzmənts/ |n.|驱魔；驱邪；清除|The exorcisements performed by the priest seemed to have worked.  神父做的驱魔仪式似乎奏效了。|例句：The exorcisements were conducted in secrecy. 驱魔仪式是秘密进行的。|例句：Several exorcisements were attempted before the spirit finally left. 在灵魂最终离开之前，曾尝试过几次驱魔。|近义词：expulsions, purgations, cleansings|反义词:blessings, consecrations|词性:名词

词源:
From Late Latin *exorcismentum, from Latin exorcismus (“exorcism”), from Ancient Greek ἐξορκισμός (exorkismós).
