coal-tar:/ˈkoʊl ˌtɑːr/ /ˈkəʊl ˌtɑː(r)/| n.|煤焦油；煤焦油沥青|A thick, black, sticky liquid obtained from coal by heating it in the absence of air.|Coal-tar is a by-product of coke production. 煤焦油是焦炭生产的副产品。|The road was paved with coal-tar. 这条路是用煤焦油铺的。|Synonyms: bitumen, pitch|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but could consider cleaner alternatives like asphalt)|noun
