eulogistic:/ˌjuːləˈdʒɪstɪk/
adjective
赞美的；颂扬的
Praising someone or something very highly.
The eulogistic speech praised the deceased's kindness and generosity.  这位颂扬性的演讲赞扬了死者的善良和慷慨。
The obituary was eulogistic in its description of her life and accomplishments.  讣告用赞美的语言描述了她的一生和成就。
近义词:laudatory, complimentary, praising, commendatory
反义词:critical, derogatory, defamatory
词性:adjective
