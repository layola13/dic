consensually:/kənˈsɛnʃʊəli/
| adv.|同意地，经双方同意地|In a way that is agreed to by the people involved; by mutual consent.|The land was consensually sold. 这块地是双方同意出售的。|
|近义词：mutually, jointly, by agreement|反义词：forcibly, against one's will, nonconsensually|词性: 副词
