allurer:/əˈlʊərər/| n.|诱惑者；有魅力的人|Someone who allures; someone who tempts or attracts by the offer of something desirable.|She was a skilled allurer, drawing people in with her charm and wit. 她是一个熟练的诱惑者，用她的魅力和机智吸引着人们。|Synonyms: charmer, temptress, enticer, seducer|Antonyms: repellent, deterrent|Noun
