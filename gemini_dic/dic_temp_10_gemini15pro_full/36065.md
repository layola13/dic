coeditor:/koʊˈɛdɪtər/| n.|共同编辑；合编者|A person who edits something jointly with another or others.|Example: She and her husband were coeditors of the magazine. 她和她丈夫是这本杂志的共同编辑。|Example: The coeditors worked well together despite their different approaches. 尽管方法不同，两位共同编辑合作得很好。|近义词：joint editor, associate editor|反义词:|词性:noun
