bulbo:/ˈbʌlboʊ/ (US), /ˈbʌlbəʊ/ (UK)| n.|球茎；鳞茎|A rounded underground storage organ present in some plants, consisting of a short stem surrounded by fleshy scale leaves or leaf bases, as in the onion or daffodil.|The gardener carefully planted the tulip bulbo in the fall. 园丁在秋天小心地种下了郁金香球茎。|The bulbo of the onion was starting to sprout. 洋葱的球茎开始发芽了。|近义词：bulb, corm|反义词: (No direct antonym)|Noun
