destocked:/diːˈstɒkt/
verb
减少库存，清仓
To reduce the level of stock or inventory, often by selling goods at a reduced price.
The company destocked its summer clothing line in preparation for fall.  这家公司清仓了夏季服装系列，为秋季做准备。
The warehouse was destocked after the holiday sales.  节假日促销结束后，仓库的库存减少了。
近义词: depleted, reduced, liquidated, cleared out
反义词: stocked, replenished, accumulated
词性: verb
