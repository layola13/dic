fourbe:/fuʁb/| n. |骗子，诡计多端的人|A deceitful and cunning person; a rogue.| He was a fourbe, always scheming and manipulating others. 他是个骗子，总是算计和操纵别人。|近义词：deceiver, cheat, trickster, rogue, scoundrel|反义词：honest person, saint|词性:noun
Etymology: From Old French *forbe*, from Frankish *forb*, related to Old High German *furb*, meaning "sly," "cunning." Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *furba-*.
