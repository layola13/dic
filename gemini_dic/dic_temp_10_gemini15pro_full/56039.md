dobbed:/dɒbd/| v. |告密，告发|To inform on someone, typically to an authority figure.|He dobbed his classmates in for cheating on the test. 他向老师告发他的同学考试作弊。|Synonyms: informed on, snitched, ratted out, betrayed|Antonyms: covered up, protected, shielded|Verb
