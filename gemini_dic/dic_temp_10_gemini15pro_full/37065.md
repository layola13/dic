combative:/kəmˈbætɪv/ (US), /ˈkɒmbətɪv/ (UK)| adj.|好斗的，好战的|Ready or eager to fight or argue;  having or showing a tendency to fight or argue.| He was in a combative mood after the meeting.  他开完会后心情很不好斗。

The lawyer adopted a combative stance in the courtroom.  律师在法庭上采取了强硬的姿态。|近义词：aggressive, belligerent, pugnacious, antagonistic, contentious|反义词：peaceful, conciliatory, cooperative, passive, submissive|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *combativus, from combātere (to fight), from com- (together) + bātere (to fight).
