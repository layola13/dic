inscribers:/ɪnˈskraɪbərz/
noun
题写者；铭刻者
A person or people who inscribe something, such as words or symbols, onto a surface.
The inscribers carved the king's name onto the monument.  题写者将国王的名字刻在了纪念碑上。
The ancient inscribers used a sharp tool to write on the clay tablets.  古代的铭刻者用锋利的工具在泥板上书写。
synonyms: engravers, carvers, writers, scribes
antonyms: erasers, obliterators
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English inscribe, from Latin inscribere (to write in or on), from in- (in, on) + scribere (to write).
