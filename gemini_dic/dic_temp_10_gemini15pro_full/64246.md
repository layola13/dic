equipotentiality:/ˌiːkwɪpəˌtɛnʃiˈælɪti/ | n. |均势; 等势; 同等潜能| The property of having equal potential or capability.  It suggests that any part of a system can perform any function. This concept has been applied in various fields, particularly in psychology and neurology.|  Example: The equipotentiality of the brain suggests that if one area is damaged, other areas can take over its function. 大脑的等势性表明，如果一个区域受损，其他区域可以接管其功能。

Example:  The concept of equipotentiality in learning suggests that different stimuli can be equally effective in producing a learned response.  学习中的等势性概念表明，不同的刺激在产生学习反应方面可能同样有效。 | 近义词: equivalence, parity | 反义词: specialization, differentiation | 词性: noun

Etymology: From equi- (equal) + potential + -ity (suffix forming nouns of quality or state).  词源：来自 equi-（相等的）+ potential（潜力）+ -ity（构成表示性质或状态的名词的后缀）。
