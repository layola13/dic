legroom:/ˈleɡruːm/ /ˈleɡˌruːm/| n.|腿部空间|The amount of space available for your legs, especially when seated in a car or airplane.|例句：There isn't much legroom in the back of this car. 这辆车的后座腿部空间不大。

例句：I requested an aisle seat for extra legroom. 我要求一个靠过道的座位，以便有更大的腿部空间。|近义词：space, room|反义词:|noun
