cavalero:/ˌkævəˈlɪəroʊ/ | n. | 骑士；绅士；护花使者 | A gallant or chivalrous man, especially one serving as escort to a woman. |  He acted like a true cavalero, opening doors and pulling out chairs for her. 他表现得像个真正的绅士，为她开门、拉椅子。 | Example: My grandfather was a true cavalero, always treating my grandmother with respect and courtesy. 我的祖父是一位真正的绅士，总是以尊重和礼貌对待我的祖母。 | Synonyms: gentleman, knight, escort, gallant | Antonyms: boor, churl, lout | noun
