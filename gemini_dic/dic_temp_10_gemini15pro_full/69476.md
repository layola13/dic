felafel:/fəˈlɑːfəl/ (US), /fəˈlaːfɛl/ (UK)| n.|炸豆丸，鹰嘴豆泥炸丸子|A Middle Eastern dish of spiced mashed chickpeas or other beans formed into balls or fritters and deep-fried, usually served in pita bread.|例句：I had a delicious falafel sandwich for lunch. 我午餐吃了一个美味的炸豆丸三明治。

例句：The street vendor sells the best falafel in town. 街边小贩卖着镇上最好吃的炸豆丸。

近义词：falafil|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
