cryer:/ˈkraɪər/| n.| 哭喊者；宣告员；传令员|A person who shouts out announcements in a public place.|The town crier announced the king's arrival.|近义词: announcer, herald, proclaimer|反义词: listener, audience|词性: noun

Etymology: From Middle English *criour*, from Anglo-Norman *criour*,  from Old French *crieor*, from *crier* ("to cry, shout"), from Latin *quīrītāre* ("to call for help, shriek").
