downlying:/ˈdaʊnˌlaɪɪŋ/| adj.|低洼的；底层的；基本的；潜在的|Situated at a lower level; underlying; fundamental; recessive (in genetics).|The downlying valleys were flooded after the heavy rain. 大雨过后，低洼的山谷被洪水淹没了。|Near-surface water can contaminate downlying aquifers. 地表水会污染下方的含水层。|近义词：low-lying, underlying, basal, fundamental, recessive|反义词：elevated, high-lying, superficial, dominant|词性:形容词

词源:  "down" (adverb meaning "towards a lower place") + "lying" (present participle of "lie," meaning "to be situated").
