decamp:/dɪˈkæmp/
verb
撤营；突然离开；逃走
to leave a place suddenly and secretly
The circus decamped in the middle of the night. 马戏团半夜偷偷离开了。

近义词: flee, abscond, depart, leave
反义词: arrive, stay, remain
词性: verb
