estramacon: /ɛstrəˈmeɪkən/ | n. | 旧式胃药; 治胃病的药 | An archaic remedy for stomach ailments; a stomachic. | He took a dose of estramacon to settle his upset stomach. 他服了一剂estramacon来缓解胃部不适。|  The apothecary recommended an estramacon for the traveler's indigestion. 药剂师推荐了一种estramacon来治疗旅行者的消化不良。 | 近义词: stomachic, digestive, antacid | 反义词: emetic | 词性: 名词

词源:  From French "estramaçon," a large two-handed sword, later applied figuratively to anything large or powerful, including strong medicines.  The connection likely arose from the idea of a medicine powerfully "cutting through" or resolving stomach problems.
