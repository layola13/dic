nondynastic:/ˌnɒn daɪˈnæstɪk/
adjective
非王朝的；非世袭的
Not belonging to, or characteristic of, a dynasty; not hereditary.
The president's power was nondynastic, based on popular support. 总统的权力并非世袭，而是基于民众的支持。
The organization's leadership is nondynastic, chosen through elections. 这个组织的领导层是非世袭的，通过选举产生。
近义词: nonelective, appointed, nonhereditary
反义词: dynastic, hereditary, inherited
词性: adjective
