minions:/ˈmɪnjənz/| n.| 小黄人；奴才；下属；爪牙 |A group of small, yellow, cylindrical creatures who serve villains.  They are known for their childlike behavior and unique language. |The minions are always up to mischief. 小黄人总是恶作剧。|synonyms: followers, subordinates, henchmen |antonyms: leaders, bosses |noun

minions:/ˈmɪnjənz/| n.|爪牙 |A person or group of people who are under the control of a powerful person or organization, especially one involved in illegal or immoral activities.|The dictator surrounded himself with minions who carried out his every whim. 独裁者身边都是唯命是从的爪牙。|synonyms:  henchmen, underlings, lackeys |antonyms:  leaders, superiors |noun
