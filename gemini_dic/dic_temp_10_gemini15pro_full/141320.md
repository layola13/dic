opencast:/ˈəʊpənˌkɑːst/ (UK) /ˈoʊpənˌkæst/ (US)| adj. n.|露天开采的，露天矿；|Opencast refers to a method of mining in which minerals are extracted from the earth by removing the surface layers of soil and rock, rather than by tunneling underground.  As a noun, it refers to a mine operated in this way. |The opencast coal mine scarred the landscape. 这个露天煤矿破坏了景观。|Synonym: open-pit, strip mining|Antonym: underground mining|adjective, noun

opencast:/ˈəʊpənˌkɑːst/ (UK) /ˈoʊpənˌkæst/ (US)| n.|露天矿|An opencast mine is a mine where minerals are extracted by removing the surface layers of soil and rock. |The company operates a large opencast mine in the region. 这家公司在该地区经营着一个大型露天矿。|Synonym: open-pit mine, strip mine|Antonym: underground mine|noun
