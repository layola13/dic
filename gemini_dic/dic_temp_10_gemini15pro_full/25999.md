bursty:/ˈbɜːrsti/| adj.|突发的，爆发性的，一阵阵的|Occurring in bursts or sudden increases of activity; characterized by short periods of intense activity followed by lulls.|例句：Bursty traffic can overload network servers.  突发性的流量会使网络服务器超载。

例句：The stock market experienced bursty trading activity. 股票市场经历了爆发性的交易活动。|近义词：intermittent, sporadic, irregular, fitful, spasmodic|反义词：continuous, steady, constant, uniform|词性:形容词
词源: From burst + -y.  来自动词 burst（爆发）加上形容词后缀 -y。
