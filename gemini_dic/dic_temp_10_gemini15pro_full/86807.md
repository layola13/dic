hematin:/ˈhiːmətɪn/| n. |血红素，正铁血红素|A brownish-black or bluish-black derivative of hemoglobin, containing iron in the ferric state, formed by oxidation.|Hematin crystals were observed under the microscope. 在显微镜下观察到了血红素晶体。|Synonyms: ferriheme, oxidized heme|Antonyms: heme (in the ferrous state)|noun
