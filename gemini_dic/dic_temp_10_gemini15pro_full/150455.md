panther:/ˈpænθər/ (US), /ˈpænθə(r)/ (UK)| n.|豹，黑豹|A large, wild cat with black fur or yellowish fur marked with dark spots.|例句：The panther stalked its prey through the jungle.  这只豹子在丛林中跟踪它的猎物。

例句：Black panthers are melanistic color variants of any of several species of larger cat. 黑豹是几种大型猫科动物的黑化变种。|近义词：leopard, cougar, jaguar|反义词: (无明确的反义词)|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *pantere*, from Old French *pantere*, from Latin *panthēra*, from Ancient Greek *πάνθηρ* (*pánthēr*). Ultimately from Sanskrit *पुण्डरीक* (*puṇḍarīka*), meaning "yellowish," or perhaps from a non-Indo-European language.
