capitalism: /ˈkæpɪtəlɪzəm/ | n. | 资本主义 | An economic and political system in which a country's trade and industry are controlled by private owners for profit, rather than by the state. |  The United States is often considered a prime example of capitalism. 美国通常被认为是资本主义的主要例子。

Capitalism has driven innovation in many industries. 资本主义推动了许多行业的创新。


近义词: free enterprise, private enterprise | 反义词: socialism, communism | 词性: noun
