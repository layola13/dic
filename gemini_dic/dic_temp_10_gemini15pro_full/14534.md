averpenny:/ˈeɪvərˌpɛni/
noun
吝啬鬼；守财奴
A miserly person; someone who hoards money.
He's such an averpenny, he reuses tea bags three times. 他真是个吝啬鬼，茶包都用三次。
Synonyms: miser, skinflint, tightwad, penny-pincher
Antonyms: spendthrift, prodigal


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *averpeni, from Old English *oferpænig (“spare penny, surplus money”), from ofer (“over, above, beyond”) + pænig (“penny”).  Compare German überpfennig.
