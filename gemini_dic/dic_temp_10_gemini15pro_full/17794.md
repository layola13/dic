bedrench:/bɪˈdrɛntʃ/
verb
使湿透，浸透
to wet thoroughly; soak
The sudden downpour bedrenched the hikers. 突如其来的倾盆大雨把徒步旅行者淋得浑身湿透。
近义词：drench, soak, saturate
反义词：dry, dehydrate
词性：v.
