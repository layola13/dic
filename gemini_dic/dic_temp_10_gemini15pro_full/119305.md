microanatomist: /ˌmaɪkroʊəˈnætəmɪst/ | n. | 显微解剖学家 | A specialist in microanatomy, the study of the microscopic structure of tissues and organs. | The microanatomist examined the tissue sample under the microscope. 显微解剖学家在显微镜下检查组织样本。 |  The microanatomist identified the cancerous cells. 显微解剖学家识别出癌细胞。 |Synonyms: histologist, pathologist | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps macrobiologist - studying large-scale life) | noun
