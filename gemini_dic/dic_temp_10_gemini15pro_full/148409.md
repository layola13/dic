overstay:/ˌoʊvərˈsteɪ/ /ˌəʊvəˈsteɪ/ | v. | 逾期居留，逗留过久 | to stay longer than the permitted time | He overstayed his visa by two weeks. 他签证逾期两周了。|近义词：outstay |反义词: leave promptly, depart on time | verb

overstay:/ˌoʊvərˈsteɪ/ /ˌəʊvəˈsteɪ/ | n. | 逾期居留 | the act of staying longer than permitted | His overstay resulted in a fine. 他的逾期居留导致了罚款。|近义词：overstaying |反义词: timely departure | noun
