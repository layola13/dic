doubtfulness:/ˈdaʊtfʊlnəs/
n.
可疑；疑惑；不确定
The doubtfulness of his story made it hard to believe.  他故事的可疑性让人难以相信。
The project was abandoned due to the doubtfulness of its success.  由于成功的不确定性，该项目被放弃了。
Near Synonyms: uncertainty, dubiety, suspicion, skepticism
Antonyms: certainty, assurance, confidence
