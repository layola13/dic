greaves: /ɡriːvz/
n.
护胫，腿甲
Armor for the shins, typically made of metal and worn by soldiers or warriors.
The knight polished his greaves until they gleamed.  骑士把他的护胫擦得闪闪发亮。
Near synonyms: leg guards, shin guards, jambeaus
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but could consider concepts like vulnerability, exposure)
