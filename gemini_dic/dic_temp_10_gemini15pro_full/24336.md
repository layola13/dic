brimfully:/ˈbrɪmfʊli/| adv.|满满地，盈溢地|Filled to the brim; completely full.|The cup was brimfully of tea.  茶杯里盛满了茶。|近义词：fully, completely, totally, entirely|反义词:partially,  incompletely|词性:副词
Etymology: From brim + full + -ly.  "Brim" refers to the top edge of a container, and "full" indicates fullness.  The suffix "-ly" turns the adjective "full" into an adverb.
