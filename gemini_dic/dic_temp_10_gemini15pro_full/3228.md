affect: /əˈfɛkt/ | v. | 影响；感染；假装；感动 | To have an influence on; make a difference to. |  The cold weather affected the crops.  寒冷的天气影响了农作物。| synonyms: influence, impact, alter | antonyms: leave unchanged, ignore | verb

affect: /ˈæfɛkt/ | n. | 情感；引起感情的因素 |  A person's feelings or emotions, especially as shown by facial expression or body language. |  He displayed a wide range of affects during the interview.  他在面试中展现了丰富的情感。| synonyms: emotion, feeling, sentiment | antonyms: apathy, indifference | noun
