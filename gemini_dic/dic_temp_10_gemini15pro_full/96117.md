indelibly:/ɪnˈdɛləbli/| adv.|不可磨灭地，永久地|in a way that is impossible to remove or forget.  Meaning it's permanent and lasting.|The experience was indelibly etched in her memory. 这段经历深深地铭刻在她的记忆中。|Synonyms: permanently, lastingly, enduringly, fixedly, ineradicably|Antonyms: temporarily, fleetingly, transiently|Adverb
