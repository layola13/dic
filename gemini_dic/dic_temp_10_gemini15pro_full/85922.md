headlight: /ˈhedˌlaɪt/ (US) /ˈhedlaɪt/ (UK)| n.|前照灯，车头灯|A light at the front of a vehicle, such as a car, motorcycle, or bicycle, that illuminates the road ahead.|The car's headlights cut through the dense fog. 汽车的前灯穿透了浓雾。|Synonyms: headlamp|Antonyms: taillight|Noun

headlight: /ˈhedˌlaɪt/ (US) /ˈhedlaɪt/ (UK)| n.| (比喻) 榜样，引导|Something that serves as a leading example or guiding influence.|The new electric car model is a headlight for the industry. 这款新型电动汽车是行业的榜样。|Synonyms: example, model|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
