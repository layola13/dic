chattiest:/ˈtʃætiɪst/ | adj. |最健谈的，最爱聊天的 | Superlative form of 'chatty,' meaning most inclined to talk. |例句：She was the chattiest person at the party, flitting from group to group. 她是聚会上最健谈的人，在人群中穿梭来往。

例句：He's always been the chattiest member of the family, always with a story to tell. 他一直是家里最健谈的人，总是有故事可讲。| 近义词：most talkative, most loquacious | 反义词：quietest, most reserved | 词性:形容词
