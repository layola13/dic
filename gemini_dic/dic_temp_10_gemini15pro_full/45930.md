cuttings: /ˈkʌtɪŋz/
noun
插枝，插条；剪报；剪纸
pieces of a plant cut off to grow new plants; pieces cut from newspapers, magazines, etc.; pieces cut from paper, cloth, etc.
Gardeners take cuttings from existing plants to grow new ones. 园丁从现有的植物上剪下插枝来培育新的植物。
She kept a scrapbook of newspaper cuttings about her favorite band. 她保存了一本剪贴簿，里面都是关于她最喜欢的乐队的剪报。
clippings, snippets, offcuts
--
n.
剪切，切割（cut的ing形式）; 切下的东西
The act of cutting something; pieces cut off.
The cuttings from the hedge were used as mulch.  从树篱上剪下来的枝条被用作覆盖物。
