livelink: /ˈlaɪvˌlɪŋk/ | n. | 实时链接，现场连接 | A system that allows the transmission of live video and audio feeds from a remote location. | We used a livelink to broadcast the conference to attendees around the world. 我们使用实时链接向世界各地的与会者广播会议。| synonyms: live feed, live stream, live broadcast | antonyms: recording, offline access | noun

livelink: /ˈlaɪvˌlɪŋk/ | v. | 进行实时连接，建立现场连接 | To connect two or more locations via a live video and audio feed. | They livelinked the studio in London with the newsroom in New York. 他们将伦敦的工作室与纽约的新闻编辑室进行了实时连接。 | synonyms: connect live, stream live, broadcast live | antonyms: record, disconnect | verb
