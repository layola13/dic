courtliness:/ˈkɔːrtlinəs/ | n. | 礼貌；殷勤；彬彬有礼 | Politeness and graciousness, especially as shown in manners and behavior. |  She accepted his apology with courtliness. 她很有礼貌地接受了他的道歉。| synonyms: politeness, civility, courtesy, graciousness, affability, gentility | antonyms: rudeness, impoliteness, discourtesy, incivility, boorishness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *curteisnes*, from Old French *corteisie*, from *corteis* ("courteous"). Ultimately from Latin *cohors* ("enclosure, retinue, cohort").
