hell-like:/ˈhelˌlaɪk/
adjective
地狱般的; 如地狱的
resembling or characteristic of hell; extremely unpleasant or difficult.
The conditions in the refugee camp were hell-like.  难民营里的状况如同地狱一般。
The heat in the factory was hell-like. 工厂里的热度简直像地狱。
similar: infernal, hellish, diabolical, fiendish
opposite: heavenly, paradisaic
词性: adjective
