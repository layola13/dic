cassocked:/ˈkæsəkt/| adj.|穿着法衣的，戴着袈裟的|Dressed in a cassock, a long, close-fitting garment worn by clergy.|The cassocked priest led the procession. 穿着法衣的牧师带领着队伍。|近义词：robed, vested|反义词: unrobed, secular|词性:形容词
词源: From the French "casaque" (meaning "long coat"), ultimately from Turkish "kazak".
