nonportrayable:/ˌnɒnpɔːˈtreɪəbl/ | adj. | 不可描绘的，难以表达的 |  Not capable of being portrayed or described, often due to its sensitive or disturbing nature.  | The effects of the trauma were so severe and nonportrayable that she couldn't speak about them.  这种创伤的影响如此严重，难以言表，以至于她无法谈论它们。 | synonyms: indescribable, ineffable, unutterable | antonyms: describable, expressible, depictable | adjective
