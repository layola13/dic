aeonian: /iˈoʊniən/
adjective
永恒的，永久的
lasting for an immeasurably or indefinitely long period of time; eternal.
例句：
The aeonian cycle of life and death continues. = 生与死的永恒循环仍在继续。

The stars seemed to hold an aeonian watch over the sleeping world. = 星辰似乎在永恒地守护着沉睡的世界。

近义词：eternal, everlasting, perpetual, timeless, enduring
反义词：temporary, transient, fleeting, ephemeral
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek αἰώνιος (aiṓnios, “eternal, everlasting”), from αἰών (aiṓn, “age, lifetime, eternity”).
