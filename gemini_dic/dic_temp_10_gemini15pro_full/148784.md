overutilizes:/ˌoʊvərˈjuːtəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
过度使用，滥用
to use something more than is necessary or desirable.
The hospital's emergency room is often overutilized.  这家医院的急诊室经常被过度使用。
He tends to overutilize technical jargon in his presentations. 他倾向于在演示中过度使用技术术语。
overuse, misuse, exploit
underuse, underemploy
