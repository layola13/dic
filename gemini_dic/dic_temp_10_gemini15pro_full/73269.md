foreappointment:/ˌfɔːrəˈpɔɪntmənt/

预先约定；预定

例句：The restaurant requires foreappointment, especially on weekends. 这家餐厅需要提前预订，尤其是在周末。

近义词：prearrangement, prior arrangement, booking, reservation
反义词：impromptu, unplanned
