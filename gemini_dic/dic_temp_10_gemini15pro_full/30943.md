chairperson: /ˈtʃɛərpɜːrsən/ (UK) /ˈtʃɛrpɜːrsn/ (US)| n. |主席，主持人|A person who presides over a meeting, committee, board, etc.|例句：The chairperson called the meeting to order. 主席宣布开会。|例句：The chairperson guided the discussion effectively. 主席有效地引导了讨论。|近义词：chairman, chairwoman, president, moderator|反义词: None|词性: noun

chairperson: /ˈtʃɛərpɜːrsən/ (UK) /ˈtʃɛrpɜːrsn/ (US)| n. |主席，主持人|From "chair" (referring to the presiding officer's seat) + "person" (making it gender-neutral). Originally, "chairman" was used for both men and women, but "chairperson" gained popularity in the late 20th century as a more inclusive term.|
