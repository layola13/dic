cottolene:/ˈkɑːtəˌlin/| n.|人造猪油，起酥油|A solid fat made from vegetable oil, used in cooking.|例句：She used Cottolene for frying the chicken. 她用人造猪油炸鸡。|例句：Cottolene was a popular alternative to lard in the early 20th century. 在20世纪初，人造猪油是猪油的流行替代品。|近义词：shortening, lard, vegetable shortening|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 butter 黄油,  oil 油  这类天然油脂)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
A blend of "cotton" and "olene" (suggesting oil).  It was originally made from cottonseed oil.
