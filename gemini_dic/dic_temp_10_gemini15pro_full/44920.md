cryptobranchiata: /ˌkrɪptoʊbræŋˈkiːətə/ | n. | 隐鳃鲵科 | A family of primitive salamanders characterized by having external gills throughout life. They are found in eastern Asia and eastern North America. |  Cryptobranchiata are considered living fossils due to their ancient lineage. 隐鳃鲵科由于其古老的血统被认为是活化石。|  The Japanese giant salamander is a member of the Cryptobranchiata.  日本大鲵是隐鳃鲵科的一员。 | synonyms: giant salamanders, hellbenders | antonyms: N/A | noun
