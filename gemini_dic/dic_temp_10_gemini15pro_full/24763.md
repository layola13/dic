brooklike: /ˈbrʊklaɪk/ | adj. | 小溪般的；潺潺的 | Resembling a brook, especially in its murmuring sound or gentle flow. |  The brooklike stream flowed gently through the meadow. 小溪般的溪流缓缓流过草地。| synonyms: murmuring, babbling, gurgling, rippling, purling | antonyms: roaring, turbulent, raging | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From brook (n.) + -like (suffix).  "Brook" comes from Old English "brōc," meaning "stream," of Germanic origin.  "-like" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling," "characteristic of," from Old English "-līc."
