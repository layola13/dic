acclimatable:/əˈklaɪmətəbl/
adjective
可适应的，可驯化的
Able to acclimate; adaptable.
Newcomers usually find it easy to become acclimatable to the hot weather.  新来的人通常很容易适应炎热的天气。

acclimatable:/əˈklaɪmətəbl/
adjective
可适应气候的
able to adjust to changes in climate or environment
These plants are acclimatable to a wide range of temperatures. 这些植物能适应各种温度。

acclimatable:/əˈklaɪmətəbl/
adjective
可适应环境的
able to adapt to new surroundings or conditions
The animals proved to be remarkably acclimatable to their new habitat. 这些动物被证明对它们的新栖息地适应性很强。

acclimatable:/əˈklaɪmətəbl/
adjective
易适应的
easily adaptable
She's an acclimatable person and will soon feel at home here. 她是一个适应能力很强的人，很快就会在这里感到宾至如归。


ETYMOLOGY->
From French acclimater, from à (to) + climat (climate), from Greek klima (slope, zone).  源自法语 acclimater，由 à（到）+ climat（气候）组成，源自希腊语 klima（斜坡，地带）。

USAGE->
The puppies are highly acclimatable to their new environment.  这些小狗对新环境的适应能力很强。
The species is acclimatable to a wide range of temperatures.  该物种能够适应很广的温度范围。


SYNONYMS->
adaptable, adjustable, flexible, malleable, pliant, compliant, tractable

ANTONYMS->
inadaptable, inflexible, rigid, unyielding

PHRASE->
highly acclimatable = 高度适应性强的
easily acclimatable = 易于适应的
readily acclimatable = 容易适应的
poorly acclimatable = 适应性差的
not acclimatable = 不能适应的
