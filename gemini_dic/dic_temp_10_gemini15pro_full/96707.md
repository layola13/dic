ineffectively:/ˌɪnɪˈfɛktɪvli/
adverb
无效地；无用地
in a way that does not produce the desired effect.
The meeting was run ineffectively and no decisions were made.  会议进行得效率低下，没有做出任何决定。
He tried to argue his case, but he did so ineffectively. 他试图为自己辩护，但做得无效。
近义词:uselessly, inefficiently, fruitlessly, pointlessly
反义词:effectively, efficiently, successfully
词性:adverb
