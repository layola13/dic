nondeafening: /nɒnˈdɛfənɪŋ/ | adj. | 不震耳欲聋的 | Not loud enough to cause deafness or be overwhelmingly loud. | The music was loud but nondeafening, allowing for conversation.  音乐很响亮，但不震耳欲聋，可以进行交谈。|  Near synonyms: quiet, moderate, soft, subdued | Antonyms: deafening, loud, earsplitting, thunderous | adjective
