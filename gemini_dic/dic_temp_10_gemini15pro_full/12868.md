assuage:/əˈsweɪdʒ/ /əˈswedʒ/| v. |减轻，缓和，平息|To make (an unpleasant feeling) less intense.|The government has tried to assuage public fears about the spread of the virus. 政府试图减轻公众对病毒传播的恐惧。|近义词：relieve, ease, alleviate, soothe, mitigate|反义词:aggravate, exacerbate, intensify|词性:verb

词源:
From Latin *assuaviare, from ad- (“to, towards”) + suavis (“sweet, agreeable”).
