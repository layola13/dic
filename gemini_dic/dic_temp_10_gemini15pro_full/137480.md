nontonal: /nɑːnˈtoʊnl/ | adj. | 非声调的 | Not based on variations in pitch to distinguish meaning between words; lacking tones. | Mandarin Chinese is a tonal language, while English is nontonal.  普通话是一种声调语言，而英语是非声调语言。| Example:  The nontonal nature of English makes it easier for some learners to pronounce words. 英语的非声调性质使得一些学习者更容易发音。| Synonyms: atonal, unpitched | Antonyms: tonal | word property: adjective
