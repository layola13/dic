chipotles: /tʃɪˈpoʊtleɪz/ | n. | 干辣椒，烟熏墨西哥辣椒 | Dried, smoked jalapeño peppers.  They are typically rehydrated before use in various dishes. |  I added chipotles to the chili for a smoky flavor. 我在辣椒里加了烟熏辣椒，以增加烟熏味。|  The chipotles in adobo sauce added a rich depth to the stew.  用阿斗酱腌制的烟熏辣椒为炖菜增添了浓郁的层次感。 | Synonyms: smoked jalapeños, chile morita (when ripe and dried) | Antonyms: N/A | Noun

Etymology: From Nahuatl *chīlpoctli* (composed of *chīlli* "chile" and *poctli* "smoke").
