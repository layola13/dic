glazy:/ˈɡleɪzi/| adj.|呆滞的，无神的，光滑的|Covered with or resembling glaze; smooth and shiny; lacking liveliness or expression.|Her eyes were glazy with exhaustion. 她累得双眼无神。|近义词：dull, glassy, unfocused, vacant, expressionless|反义词：bright, alert, lively, expressive|词性:形容词
Etymology: From glaze + -y.  Ultimately from the Vulgar Latin *glasium, related to glass.
