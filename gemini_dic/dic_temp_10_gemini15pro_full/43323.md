crammed:/kræmd/ | adj. | 挤满的，塞满的 | Filled to excess; packed tightly. | The room was crammed with people. 房间里挤满了人。| The bus was crammed full of passengers. 公交车挤满了乘客。 | crowded, packed, jammed | empty, spacious | adjective

Etymology: From the verb "cram," which comes from the Middle English "crammen," meaning "to stuff, press, or force in."  Ultimately of uncertain origin, possibly related to Old Norse "krama" (to squeeze, press).
