outleap:/ˌaʊtˈliːp/ | v. | 超越，胜过 | To surpass or exceed, especially in a sudden or dramatic way. | The new technology outleaped all expectations in its performance. 这项新技术的表现超出了所有预期。

The athlete outleaped his competitors to win the gold medal.  这位运动员超越了他的竞争对手，赢得了金牌。 | 近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo, outperform | 反义词：lag behind, fall short of | 词性：动词
