back-breaking:/ˈbækˌbreɪkɪŋ/
adjective
极其辛苦的，累断腰的
(of manual labor) requiring great physical exertion.
Example: Harvesting sugarcane is back-breaking work.  收割甘蔗是一项非常辛苦的工作。

Example:  She had a back-breaking day at the office, dealing with a mountain of paperwork. 她办公室里的一天累得要命，处理了一大堆文件。

近义词: arduous, strenuous, grueling, exhausting, laborious
反义词: easy, effortless, simple

词性: adjective
