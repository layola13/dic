overcourteousness:/ˌoʊvərˈkɜːrtiəsnəs/| n.|过分礼貌，过分客气|Excessive politeness; being overly courteous. |His overcourteousness made her feel uncomfortable. 他过分的礼貌让她感到不舒服。|The overcourteousness of the staff was almost suffocating.  员工过分的殷勤让人几乎窒息。|近义词：excessive politeness, obsequiousness, fawning|反义词：rudeness, impoliteness, discourtesy|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + courteousness.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much". "Courteousness" is derived from "courteous," ultimately from the Old French "corteis" meaning "courtly," relating to the manners of a royal court.
