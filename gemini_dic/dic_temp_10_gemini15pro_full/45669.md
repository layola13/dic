curtains:/ˈkɜːrtnʒ/ (US) /ˈkɜːtənz/ (UK)| n.|窗帘；帷幕；结束；终结|A piece of cloth intended to cover a window, stage, etc.  It can also figuratively mean the end of something.|例句：The curtains were drawn to block out the sunlight. 窗帘被拉上以遮挡阳光。

例句：It's curtains for his career if this scandal gets out. 如果这桩丑闻曝光，他的职业生涯就完蛋了。|近义词：drapes, blinds, shades (for window coverings); end, finish, conclusion (for figurative meaning)|反义词:beginning, start (for figurative meaning)|词性:noun
