hurd:/hɜːrd/ /hɝd/| n.| 栏; 障碍; 羊栏; 栅栏|A portable barrier used to enclose or divide an area, often for livestock. |The sheep were safely contained within the hurd. 羊被安全地关在羊栏里。|
近义词：fence, barrier, enclosure, pen|反义词:opening, gap|词性:名词

hurd:/hɜːrd/ /hɝd/| v.| (用栏)围; 把…赶入栏; 驱赶; 挤入|To drive or force (animals or people) into an enclosed area. |The farmer hurded the sheep into the pen. 农民把羊赶进羊圈。|近义词：herd, corral, round up, pen|反义词:release, free|词性:动词
