beautifications:/ˌbjuːtɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/
n.
美化；装饰；修饰
The act or process of making something more beautiful.
The city underwent extensive beautifications before the Olympics.  这个城市在奥运会前进行了大规模的美化。
The beautifications of the park made it a more pleasant place to visit. 公园的美化使它成为一个更令人愉快的地方。
embellishments, improvements, enhancements
uglifications, deteriorations
noun
