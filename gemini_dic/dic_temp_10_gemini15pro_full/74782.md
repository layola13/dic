freieslebenite:/ˈfraɪslɛbəˌnaɪt/ | n. |弗莱斯莱本矿|A rare lead-silver sulfosalt mineral, PbAgSbS3, with a monoclinic crystal system. It is named after the German mineralogist Johann Carl Freiesleben (1774-1846). |Freieslebenite is a valuable ore of silver. 弗莱斯莱本矿是一种珍贵的银矿石。|Synonyms: None (as it is a specific mineral name) | Antonyms:  None (as it is a specific mineral name)| noun
