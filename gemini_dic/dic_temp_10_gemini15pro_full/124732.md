mozzetta:/mɒˈtsɛtə/ | n. | 短披肩，莫采塔 | A short cape with a small hood, worn by Catholic clergy, especially bishops, cardinals, and the pope. | The bishop wore a scarlet mozzetta over his cassock. 主教在他的长袍外套了一件猩红色的短披肩。|  近义词:cape, mantle | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "robe" as a broader garment) | noun
