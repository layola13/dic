embillow:/ɪmˈbɪloʊ/
verb
使膨胀，使鼓起；像波浪般翻滚
(especially of fabric) fill with air and swell outward.
The sails embillowed in the wind.  风鼓起了船帆。
The curtains embillowed in the breeze. 微风吹拂着窗帘，使其鼓起。
synonyms: billow, swell, puff out, balloon, inflate, belly out
antonyms: deflate, collapse, shrink
词性:动词
