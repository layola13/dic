embryonal:/ˌɛmbrɪˈoʊnəl/| adj.|胚胎的，胚胎期的|Relating to or characteristic of an embryo.  |例句：The embryonal cells began to differentiate. 胚胎细胞开始分化。

例句：The study focused on embryonal development in mice. 这项研究的重点是小鼠的胚胎发育。|近义词：embryonic, rudimentary, fetal|反义词：adult, mature|词性：adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *embryonalis, from Latin embryo, from Ancient Greek ἔμβρυον (émbruon) "a young one in the womb, fetus," from ἐν (en) "in" + βρύειν (brúein) "to swell, be full."
