archaeology:/ˌɑːrkiˈɒlədʒi/ (US) /ˌɑːrkiˈɑːlədʒi/| n.|考古学|The study of human history and prehistory through the excavation of sites and the analysis of artifacts and other physical remains.|例句：She is studying archaeology at university.  她在大学学习考古学。

例句：Archaeology can reveal much about past civilizations. 考古学可以揭示很多关于过去文明的信息。


近义词：paleontology, anthropology|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "modern study")|词性:noun
