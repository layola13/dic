loveliest:/ˈlʌvliɪst/| adj.|最可爱的，最美丽的|Superlative form of lovely; most lovely.  Meaning the most beautiful, charming, or delightful.|She wore the loveliest dress to the ball. 她穿着最漂亮的礼服去参加舞会。|近义词：most beautiful, most charming, prettiest, most delightful|反义词：ugliest, most unpleasant, least attractive|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *loveli, from Old English luflic (“loving, affectionate, amiable; lovely, beautiful, pleasant”), equivalent to love +‎ -ly. Cognate with Scots luvely, West Frisian leaflik (“lovely”), Dutch lieflijk (“lovely”), German lieblich (“lovely”). More at love.
