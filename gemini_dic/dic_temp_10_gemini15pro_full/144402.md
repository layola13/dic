outstrive:/ˌaʊtˈstraɪv/
verb
超越，胜过
To strive harder or more successfully than; surpass in striving.
He outstrived all his competitors to win the race. 他超越了所有竞争对手赢得了比赛。
近义词：outdo, surpass, excel, outstrip
反义词：fail, lose, succumb
词性：v.

词源：
From out- + strive.  "Out-" prefix indicating exceeding or surpassing. "Strive" comes from Old French "estriver" meaning to quarrel, dispute, or exert oneself.
