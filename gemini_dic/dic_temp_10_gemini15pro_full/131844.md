nonconstituent:/ˌnɒnkənˈstɪtʃuənt/ | adj. n.| 非成分的，非组成部分的；非选民 | A linguistic unit that is not a constituent of a larger unit.  A person who is not a constituent of a particular representative. | The phrase "the big red" is nonconstituent in the sentence "The big red ball bounced."  “大红”这个短语在“大红球弹起来了”这个句子中是非成分的。 As a nonconstituent, he couldn't vote in the local election. 作为非选民，他不能在地方选举中投票。 | 近义词: nonmember, outsider | 反义词: constituent, member | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From non- + constituent.  "Constituent" comes from Latin "constituentem," present participle of "constituere" meaning "to set up, establish."
