mauger:/ˈmɔːɡər/| prep. |尽管，不顾|in spite of; despite.  Expressing opposition or resistance. |He went ahead with his plan, mauger all warnings. 他不顾所有警告，继续执行他的计划。|近义词：despite, notwithstanding, in spite of|反义词: because of, due to|词性:介词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *maugre, from Old French *malgre, from mal (badly) + gré (pleasure, liking). Ultimately from Latin male gratum (ill-pleasing).
