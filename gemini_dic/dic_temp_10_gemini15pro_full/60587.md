elatery:/ɪˈlætəri/ | n. | 弹性，弹力 | The property of a material that enables it to return to its original shape after being stretched or compressed. | The elatery of the rubber band allowed it to snap back into place.  橡皮筋的弹性使它能够迅速弹回原位。 | 近义词: elasticity, resilience, springiness | 反义词: inelasticity, rigidity | 词性: noun

elatery:/ɪˈlætəri/ | n. | 弹性，弹力 | The ability of something to recover its original shape after being deformed. |  The elatery of the spring ensured a smooth ride. 弹簧的弹性确保了平稳的行驶。 | 近义词: flexibility, suppleness | 反义词: stiffness, inflexibility | 词性: noun
