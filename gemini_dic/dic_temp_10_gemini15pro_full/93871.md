immodestly:/ɪˈmɑːdɪstli/ | adv. |不谦虚地，不客气地| In a way that lacks modesty; boastfully. | He immodestly boasted about his achievements. 他不谦虚地吹嘘自己的成就。|She spoke immodestly of her own talents. 她不谦虚地谈论自己的才能。| 近义词：boastfully, arrogantly, presumptuously | 反义词：modestly, humbly, unassumingly |词性: 副词

immodest:/ɪˈmɑːdɪst/ | adj. |不谦虚的，不客气的| Having or showing a lack of modesty. | It was immodest of him to brag about his wealth. 他吹嘘自己的财富是不谦虚的。|  It's considered immodest to talk about your accomplishments.  谈论自己的成就被认为是不谦虚的。| 近义词：boastful, arrogant, presumptuous | 反义词：modest, humble, unassuming |词性: 形容词
