non-aspirated:/ˌnɒnˈæspɪreɪtɪd/| adj.|非送气的|Not aspirated; pronounced without a puff of air following the initial sound, as the 'p' in 'spin'.|The 'p' in 'spin' is non-aspirated, unlike the 'p' in 'pin'.  'spin' 中的 'p' 是非送气的，不像 'pin' 中的 'p'。|The /b/ sound in "bin" is non-aspirated. "bin" 中的 /b/ 音是非送气的。|unaspirated|aspirated|adjective
