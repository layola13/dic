gadwall:/ˈɡædˌwɔːl/ (US), /ˈɡædˌwɔːl/ (UK)| n. |赤膀鸭|A common dabbling duck, *Anas strepera*, with a grey body, a white belly, and a black rear end.  It is found in much of North America, Europe, and Asia. |I saw a gadwall swimming in the pond. 我看到一只赤膀鸭在池塘里游泳。|近义词：duck, dabbling duck, |反义词: N/A|Noun

词源: From Old French *gadwal*, possibly from *gadel*, a variant of *cadel*, meaning "duckling".
