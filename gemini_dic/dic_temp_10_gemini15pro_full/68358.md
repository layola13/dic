fallbacks:/ˈfɔːlbæks/| n.|备用方案，后备计划，退路|a plan or course of action that may be used if another fails|The company has several fallbacks in case the primary strategy doesn't work. 公司有一些备用方案，以防主要策略不起作用。|synonyms: backup, contingency, alternative, reserve, safety net|antonyms: primary plan, main strategy|noun

fallbacks:/ˈfɔːlbæks/| n.|（计算机）回退，后备|a method or value used if the preferred one is unavailable|If the primary server is down, the system will use the fallback server. 如果主服务器宕机，系统将使用备用服务器。|synonyms: default, reserve, substitute, alternative|antonyms: primary, main|noun
