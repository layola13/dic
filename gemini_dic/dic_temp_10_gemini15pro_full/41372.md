ETYMOLOGY->
From "cork" + "screw."  The word "cork" comes from the Old Spanish "corcho," referring to the bark of the cork oak tree. "Screw" comes from the Old French "escroue."  The combined word "corkscrew" first appeared in the mid-17th century.
