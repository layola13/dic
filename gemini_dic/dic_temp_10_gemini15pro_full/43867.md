cresol:/ˈkriːsɒl/ (UK), /ˈkriːsɔːl/ (US)| n. |甲酚；煤酚；煤馏油酸|A thick, oily liquid obtained from coal tar and wood tar, consisting of a mixture of three isomeric phenols.  It is used as a disinfectant and antiseptic. | Cresol is effective against a wide range of bacteria. 甲酚对多种细菌有效。|  Cresol was commonly used as a disinfectant in hospitals. 甲酚曾普遍用作医院的消毒剂。| synonyms: methylphenol | antonyms: None | noun
