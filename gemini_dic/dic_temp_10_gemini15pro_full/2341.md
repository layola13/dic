adhesive:/ədˈhiːsɪv/ /ədˈhizɪv/
n.|胶粘剂|A substance used for sticking objects or materials together.|The carpenter used a strong adhesive to bond the two pieces of wood. 木匠用强力胶粘合两块木头。|glue, cement, paste|solvent
adj.|粘性的|Able to stick fast to a surface or substance; sticky.|The adhesive tape held the bandage in place.  胶带将绷带固定住。|sticky, clinging, tenacious|non-adhesive, slippery
词性: 名词, 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adhaesivus*, from adhaerere "to stick to," from ad- "to" + haerere "to stick."

USAGE->
名词用法:

I need a strong adhesive to fix this broken vase.
我需要一种强力胶来粘合这个破花瓶。

They use a special adhesive in the manufacture of these shoes.
他们在制造这些鞋时使用一种特殊的粘合剂。


形容词用法:

The adhesive properties of this new material are remarkable.
这种新材料的粘合性能非常出色。

The label was covered with an adhesive film.
标签上覆盖着一层粘性薄膜。



PHRASE->
adhesive tape = 胶带
adhesive bandage =  创可贴
adhesive properties = 粘合性能
adhesive strength = 粘合强度
adhesive force = 粘附力
