compurgatorial:/kəmˌpɜːrɡəˈtɔːriəl/
adj.
宣誓证实的，通过宣誓断案的
relating to, or involving compurgation (a practice of clearing an accused person of a charge by having a certain number of people swear to the truth of his or her innocence).
The defendant offered compurgatorial evidence to support his alibi.  被告提供了宣誓证据来支持他的不在场证明。
Synonym:  exculpatory, vindicatory
Antonym:  incriminatory, accusatory
词性: adjective
