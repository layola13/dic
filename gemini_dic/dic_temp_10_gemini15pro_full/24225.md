bridges:/ˈbrɪdʒɪz/| n.|桥梁；桥牌；纽带；桥接器|A structure carrying a road, path, railway, etc. across a river, road, or other obstacle.  Something that connects two things or groups of people.|例句：The bridges of Madison County are famous. 麦迪逊郡的桥梁很有名。

例句：They formed a bridge between the two communities. 他们在两个社群之间搭建了桥梁。|近义词：link, connection, overpass, viaduct|反义词：barrier, division|noun
