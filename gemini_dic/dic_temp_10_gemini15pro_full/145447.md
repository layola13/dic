overchill:/ˌoʊvərˈtʃɪl/| adj.| 过于冷淡；过分冷静；过分放松| excessively relaxed or calm, sometimes to the point of appearing uninterested or apathetic. |例句：His overchill attitude made it seem like he didn't care about the outcome of the game. 他过分冷静的态度让人觉得他并不在乎比赛结果。

例句：She tried to play it overchill, but I could tell she was nervous about the presentation. 她试图表现得非常放松，但我看得出来她对演讲很紧张。|近义词：nonchalant, aloof, laid-back, apathetic|反义词：eager, enthusiastic, anxious, excited|词性:形容词

词源:  A combination of "over" (meaning excessively) and "chill" (meaning relaxed or calm). This is a relatively recent slang term.
