jilted:/ˈdʒɪltɪd/
adjective
被抛弃的，被甩的
Suddenly abandoned or rejected (especially by a lover).
She felt jilted and betrayed when he broke off their engagement. 她被他解除婚约后感到被抛弃和背叛。
近义词：abandoned, deserted, rejected, dumped
反义词：accepted, chosen
