oxidizing:/ˈɒksɪdaɪzɪŋ/ | adj. | 氧化的，氧化性的 | Causing oxidation; acting as an oxidizing agent. |  Rust is formed by the oxidizing action of air and moisture on iron. 铁锈是由空气和水分对铁的氧化作用形成的。 | The oxidizing agent readily accepts electrons. 氧化剂很容易接受电子。 | Synonyms: corrosive, erosive | Antonyms: reducing | adjective

oxidizes:/ˈɒksɪdaɪzɪz/ | v. | 氧化，生锈 |  (especially of a metal) combine or become combined chemically with oxygen. | Iron oxidizes to form rust. 铁氧化形成铁锈。 |  Apples oxidize and turn brown when cut and exposed to air. 苹果切开暴露在空气中会氧化变褐。| Synonyms: rust, corrode, tarnish | Antonyms: reduce | verb
