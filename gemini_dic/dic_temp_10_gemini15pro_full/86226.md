heathlands:/ˈhiːθləndz/
n.
石南荒原，欧石南丛生的荒地
A tract of open, uncultivated land covered with low-growing shrubs, especially heather.
The heathlands are a haven for many species of birds.  这些石南荒原是许多鸟类的避难所。
近义词：moor, moorland, heath
反义词:  woodland, forest
