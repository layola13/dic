de-powered:/diːˈpaʊərd/
adjective
失去能力的，被剥夺权力的
Deprived of power or strength; made powerless or ineffective.
The superhero was de-powered by the villain's weapon.  超级英雄被恶棍的武器剥夺了力量。
The government de-powered the regulatory agency. 政府削弱了监管机构的权力。
近义词: weakened, disabled, incapacitated
反义词: empowered, strengthened, enabled
词性: adjective

de-power:/diːˈpaʊər/
verb
使失去能力，剥夺权力
To deprive of power or strength; to make powerless or ineffective.
The villain's weapon de-powered the superhero.  恶棍的武器使超级英雄失去了力量。
The new law de-powered the local government. 新法律削弱了地方政府的权力。
近义词: weaken, disable, incapacitate
反义词: empower, strengthen, enable
词性: verb


词源:
"De-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "down, away from, off, reversal."  "Power" comes from Old French "poer" meaning "to be able."  Therefore, "de-power" literally means to take away the ability to do something.
