flourishes:/ˈflʌrɪʃɪz/ (US), /ˈflʌrɪʃɪz/ (UK)| v. (third-person singular simple present flourishes, present participle flourishing, simple past and past participle flourished)| 繁荣，兴旺；茂盛；挥舞；炫耀 | to grow or develop successfully; to thrive; to wave something around in a showy way| The company flourished under her leadership.  这家公司在她的领导下蓬勃发展。
He flourished his sword dramatically. 他夸张地挥舞着他的剑。| synonyms: thrive, prosper, boom, brandish, wave | antonyms: decline, wither, fail, fade | verb
