painterlinesses:/ˈpeɪntərlinəsnɪsɪz/
noun
绘画性（复数）
The quality or state of being painterly, characterized by visible brushstrokes, textured surfaces, and an emphasis on the process of painting rather than precise representation.
Her later works emphasized painterlinesses, with thick impasto and vibrant colors. 她后期的作品强调绘画性，笔触厚重，色彩鲜艳。
Near Synonyms: brushwork, texture, impasto
Antonyms: precision, smoothness, detail
词性: 名词
