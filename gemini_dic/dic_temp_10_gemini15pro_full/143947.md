outquaffing: /ˌaʊtˈkwɑːfɪŋ/
verb
胜过豪饮; 比…喝得更多
Drinking more than someone else.  Surpassing someone in drinking.
He outquaffed everyone at the party, finishing three bottles of wine by himself. 他在派对上比任何人都喝得多，自己喝完了三瓶酒。
He boasted of outquaffing his companions. 他吹嘘自己比同伴喝得多。
outdrink, outdo
be outdrank, be outdone
