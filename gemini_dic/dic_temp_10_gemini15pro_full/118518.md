mesocoele:/ˈmɛsəˌsiːl/| n. |中腔，体腔中段|The mesocoele is the middle portion of the body cavity in a developing embryo, specifically between the procoele and metacoele.  它是发育中胚胎体腔的中间部分，特别是在前腔和后腔之间。|The mesocoele develops into the pericardial cavity surrounding the heart in vertebrates. 在脊椎动物中，中腔发育成围绕心脏的围心腔。|Synonym: mid-coelom, middle coelom|Antonym: N/A|noun
mesocoele:/ˈmɛsəˌsiːl/| n. |中腔，体腔中段|In some invertebrates, the mesocoele houses excretory organs. 在一些无脊椎动物中，中腔容纳排泄器官。|The development of the mesocoele is crucial for the proper formation of internal organs. 中腔的发育对内部器官的正常形成至关重要。|Synonym: mid-coelom, middle coelom|Antonym: N/A|noun
