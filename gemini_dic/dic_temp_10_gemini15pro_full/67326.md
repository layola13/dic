extempore:/ɪkˈstɛmpəri/| adj. adv.| 即席的，即兴的|Spoken or done without preparation.|He gave an extempore speech. 他发表了一场即兴演讲。|近义词：impromptu, off-the-cuff, improvised|反义词：prepared, rehearsed, planned|词性: 形容词/副词


词源:  From Latin *ex tempore* "out of the time," from *ex* "out of" + *tempore* "time."
