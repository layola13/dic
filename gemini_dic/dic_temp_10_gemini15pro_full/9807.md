anxious:/ˈæŋkʃəs/| adj.|焦虑的，担忧的，渴望的|Experiencing worry, nervousness, or unease, typically about an imminent event or something with an uncertain outcome. Also, strongly desiring something.|例句：She was anxious about her upcoming exam. 她对即将到来的考试感到焦虑。

例句：He was anxious to hear the results. 他急切地想听到结果。|近义词：worried, concerned, apprehensive, eager, keen|反义词：calm, relaxed, tranquil, indifferent|词性:adjective

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French ancios ‘solicitous, distressed’, from Latin anxius .
