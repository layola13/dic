cootfoot:/ˈkuːtfʊt/ | n. | 瓣蹼足 | A lobed foot, like that of a coot or grebe, having each toe edged with a separate membrane. | The coot used its cootfoot to propel itself through the water.  这只骨顶鸡用它的瓣蹼足在水中推进自己。 | synonyms: lobed foot, webbed foot | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps unwebbed foot or clawed foot) | noun


cootfoot:/ˈkuːtfʊt/ | adj. | 瓣蹼足的 | Having a lobed foot like a coot or grebe. | The cootfoot bird easily navigated the marshy waters.  这种瓣蹼足的鸟很容易在沼泽地水中航行。 | synonyms: lobed-footed, web-footed | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps unwebbed or clawed) | adjective
