nonfeloniously:/ˌnɒnˌfɛˈləʊniəsli/
adverb
非重罪地，合法地
Not constituting or involving a felony; legally.
He acquired the property nonfeloniously. 他合法地获得了这处房产。
近义词:legally, lawfully
反义词:feloniously, illegally
词性:adverb

词源:
From non- + felonious + -ly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Felonious" comes from the Old French "felon," meaning "villain" or "traitor," which ultimately derives from the Medieval Latin "felo," meaning "felon."  "-ly" is a suffix used to form adverbs.
