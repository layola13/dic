fuchi:/ˈfuːtʃiː/
noun
刀镡,刀的护手
A metal handguard of a Japanese sword or dagger, often ornately decorated.
The fuchi of his katana was intricately carved with a dragon motif. 他的武士刀的刀镡上雕刻着复杂的龙纹图案。
Synonyms: handguard, hilt
Antonyms: blade, edge
