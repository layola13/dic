insectivora:/ɪnˌsɛkˈtɪvərə/ | n. |食虫动物|An order of mammals that primarily eat insects.  This term is now considered outdated in modern taxonomy, having been replaced by Eulipotyphla and Afrosoricida. |例句：Shrews and moles were once classified under the order Insectivora.  鼩鼱和鼹鼠曾经被归类为食虫目。

例句：In older textbooks, you might find hedgehogs listed as insectivora. 在旧教科书中，你可能会发现刺猬被列为食虫动物。 |近义词：(No direct single-word synonym due to taxonomic changes, but related groups include) Eulipotyphla, Afrosoricida |反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性: noun
