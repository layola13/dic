crate:/kreɪt/| n. |板条箱；装货箱|A large wooden or plastic container used for storing or transporting goods.|The workers loaded the crates onto the truck. 工人们把板条箱装上了卡车。|近义词：box, case, container|反义词:|noun

crate:/kreɪt/| v. |把…装入板条箱|To pack in a crate.|They crated the vegetables for shipping. 他们把蔬菜装箱以便运输。|近义词：box, pack|反义词: unpack|verb
