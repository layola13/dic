confuse:/kənˈfjuːz/| vt.|使困惑，使糊涂|To make someone unable to think clearly or understand something.|The instructions confused me.  这些指示把我弄糊涂了。|近义词：bewilder, perplex, puzzle|反义词:clarify, enlighten|动词

confusing:/kənˈfjuːzɪŋ/| adj.|令人困惑的，难以理解的|Something that is difficult to understand.|The instructions were very confusing.  这些指示非常令人困惑。|近义词：bewildering, perplexing, puzzling|反义词:clear, straightforward|形容词

confused:/kənˈfjuːzd/| adj.|困惑的，糊涂的|Being unable to think clearly or understand something.|I'm confused about the instructions. 我对这些指示感到困惑。|近义词：bewildered, perplexed, puzzled|反义词:clear-headed, certain|形容词

词源: From Late Latin *confusus, past participle of confundere "to pour together, mix, confuse," from com- "together" + fundere "to pour."
