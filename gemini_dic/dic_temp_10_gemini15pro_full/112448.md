louder:/ˈlaʊdər (US) /ˈlaʊdə(r) (UK)| adj. |更大声地，更响亮地| comparative form of "loud," meaning producing or capable of producing much sound.| Speak louder, I can't hear you.  说大声点，我听不见你。|The music was louder than I expected.  音乐比我想象的要响亮。|近义词：more loudly, noisier|反义词: quieter, softer|词性:adjective
