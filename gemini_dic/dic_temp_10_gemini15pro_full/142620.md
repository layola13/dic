osteoporoses:/ˌɒstioʊpəˈroʊsiːz/ | n. | 骨质疏松症 | A medical condition in which the bones become brittle and fragile from loss of tissue, typically as a result of hormonal changes, or deficiency of calcium or vitamin D. |  Osteoporoses can increase the risk of fractures. 骨质疏松症会增加骨折的风险。 | osteoporosis (singular form), bone loss, osteopenia | bone density | noun
