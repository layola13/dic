nonperfections:/ˌnɒnpərˈfɛkʃənz/
n.
缺点，缺陷，不完美之处
flaws, imperfections, shortcomings; things that are not perfect
The artist embraced the nonperfections in the pottery, seeing them as adding character.  这位艺术家欣然接受了陶器中的不完美之处，认为它们增添了特色。
synonyms: flaws, imperfections, defects, shortcomings, blemishes
antonyms: perfections, flawlessness, impeccability
noun
