ametabolism:/əˌmɛˈtæbəlɪzəm/ | n. |无变态；直接发育 |Absence of metamorphosis, as in certain primitive insects that hatch from the egg resembling the adult. |Silverfish exhibit ametabolism, hatching from the egg as miniature versions of the adults.  银鱼表现出无变态的特征，从卵中孵化出来就是成年银鱼的微缩版。|  Ametabolism allows some insects to reproduce quickly. 无变态使得一些昆虫能够快速繁殖。 |  近义词: direct development | 反义词: metamorphosis | 词性: noun
