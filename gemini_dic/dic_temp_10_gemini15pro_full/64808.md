esoteric:/ˌɛsəˈtɛrɪk/ |adj.|深奥的；秘传的；限于圈内人的|Intended for or likely to be understood by only a small number of people with a specialized knowledge or interest.|例句：The esoteric knowledge of the ancient mystics. 古代神秘主义者深奥的知识。|例句：He was drawn to the esoteric world of alchemy. 他被炼金术的神秘世界所吸引。|近义词：abstruse, arcane, obscure, recondite|反义词：exoteric, common, simple|词性: adjective

esoterica:/ˌɛsəˈtɛrɪkə/ |n.|深奥的事物；秘传的事物|Things that are esoteric.|例句：He delved into esoterica of ancient religions. 他钻研古代宗教的深奥知识。|例句：The book was filled with esoterica that only a few scholars could understand. 这本书充满了只有少数学者才能理解的深奥内容。|近义词：arcana, mysteries|反义词：common knowledge|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *esotericus*, from Greek esōterikos "inner," from esō "within," comparative of en "in."
