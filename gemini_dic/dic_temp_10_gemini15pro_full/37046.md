comatosely:/ˌkɒməˈtəʊsli/ /ˌkɑːməˈtoʊsli/| adv.|昏迷地；不省人事地|In a comatose manner; as if in a coma.  Characterized by a state of coma; deeply unconscious.|He lay comatosely on the hospital bed after the accident. 他在事故后昏迷不醒地躺在医院病床上。|Synonyms: unconsciously, insensibly, inertly|Antonyms: consciously, alertly, responsively|词性:副词

comatose:/ˈkəʊmətəʊs/  /ˈkɑːmətoʊs/| adj.|昏迷的；不省人事的|In a state of deep unconsciousness for a prolonged or indefinite period, especially as a result of severe injury or illness.|The patient remained comatose for several days. 病人昏迷了几天。|Synonyms: unconscious, insensible, inert|Antonyms: conscious, alert, responsive|词性:形容词

coma:/ˈkəʊmə/ /ˈkoʊmə/| n.|昏迷|A state of deep unconsciousness for a prolonged or indefinite period, especially as a result of severe injury or illness.|He was in a coma for three weeks after the car crash. 车祸后他昏迷了三周。|Synonyms: unconsciousness, insensibility|Antonyms: consciousness, alertness|词性:名词
