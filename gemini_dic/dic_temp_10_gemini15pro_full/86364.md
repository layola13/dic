hectoring:/ˈhɛktərɪŋ/| adj.|威吓的，欺侮的，专横的|Characterized by bullying or nagging; harassing with persistent criticism or demands.|例句：His hectoring tone made everyone uncomfortable. 他那颐指气使的语气让每个人都不舒服。|例句：The hectoring manager made his employees dread coming to work.  那个专横跋扈的经理让他的员工害怕上班。|近义词：bullying, browbeating, intimidating, domineering, overbearing|反义词：gentle, kind, encouraging, supportive|词性:形容词
词源:From Hector, the Trojan warrior in Homer's Iliad, known for his bravery and bluster.  源自荷马史诗《伊利亚特》中的特洛伊勇士赫克托耳，他以勇敢和咆哮而闻名。
