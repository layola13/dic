outmaster:/ˌaʊtˈmæstər/
verb
胜过，超越
to surpass in skill or mastery; to outdo
He outmastered his opponent in the chess tournament. 他在国际象棋比赛中战胜了对手。
近义词:surpass, excel, outdo, outperform, outstrip
反义词:lose to, be defeated by
词性:v.
