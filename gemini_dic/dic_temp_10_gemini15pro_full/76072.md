furniture: /ˈfɜːrnɪtʃər/
n.
家具
Furniture refers to movable objects intended to support various human activities such as seating (e.g., chairs, stools, and sofas), eating (tables), and sleeping (beds). Furniture is also used for storage (e.g., cupboards, cabinets, and shelves).
例句：The furniture in their living room is modern and stylish.  他们客厅里的家具很现代也很时尚。

例句：We need to buy new furniture for the dining room. 我们需要为餐厅购买新家具。
近义词：furnishings, appointments
反义词：None (It's a category of objects, not a concept with an opposite.)
词性:Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French fourniture, from fournir "to furnish, provide," from Frankish *frumjan "to provide, perform," from Proto-Germanic *frumjanan "to perform, do, accomplish," from PIE *pro- "forth" + *eme- "to take."
