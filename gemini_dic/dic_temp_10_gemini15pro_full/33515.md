cicatrose:/sɪˈkætrəˌtʃoʊs/ /sɪˈkætrəˌtroʊs/
adjective
结疤的，疤痕状的；(尤指植物表面)有疤痕的
Marked with scars; (especially of a plant surface) having a scar-like marking.
The cicatrose surface of the fruit was evidence of its struggle to survive the harsh weather.  这种水果疤痕累累的表面证明了它在恶劣天气中挣扎求生的过程。
The bark of the ancient tree was thick and cicatrose. 古树的树皮又厚又满是疤痕。
近义词: scarred, scabbed, cicatrized
反义词: smooth, unblemished
词性: adjective

词源: From Late Latin *cicatrōsus, from Latin cicatrix (“scar”).  源自晚期拉丁语 *cicatrōsus，来自拉丁语 cicatrix（“疤痕”）。
