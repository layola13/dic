backdated:/ˌbækˈdeɪtɪd/
adjective
追溯的；有回溯效力的
Given an earlier date than the actual one.
例句：The payment was backdated to January 1st.  这笔款项的日期被追溯到1月1日。

例句：He backdated the check to avoid a late fee. 他把支票的日期提前了，以避免滞纳金。
synonym:retroactive, antedated
antonym:current, postdated
词性:adjective

backdate:/ˌbækˈdeɪt/
verb
回溯；追溯到
To give something an earlier date than the actual one.
例句：The invoice was backdated to last month. 这张发票被回溯到上个月。

例句：They backdated the agreement to cover the previous work. 他们将协议的日期回溯，以涵盖之前的工作。
synonym:antedate, retroact
antonym:postdate
词性:verb
