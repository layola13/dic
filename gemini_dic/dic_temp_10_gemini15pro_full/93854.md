immobilize: /ɪˈmoʊbəlaɪz/ | v. | 使不动；固定；使不能动用 | To prevent something from moving or from being used; to prevent someone from moving or operating normally. | The doctor immobilized his broken leg with a cast. 医生用石膏固定了他断掉的腿。|  The heavy snow immobilized traffic. 大雪使交通瘫痪。 | 近义词: restrict, disable, paralyze | 反义词: mobilize, activate, free | 词性: 动词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *immobilisāre, from in- (“not”) + mobilis (“movable”) + -āre (infinitive suffix).  Related to mobile.
