centesm:/sɛnˈtɛzəm/ | n. | 百分之一 |A hundredth part; specifically, a unit of land area equal to one hundredth of an are, or 1 square meter. | The area of the garden is 100 centesms.  花园的面积是100平方米。 | synonym: are (in some contexts), square meter | antonym: N/A (units of measurement typically don't have direct antonyms) | noun
