freshet:/ˈfrɛʃɪt/ | n. |春汛，融雪造成的河水上涨|A sudden overflow or flooding of a river or stream caused by heavy rain or melting snow. |The spring freshet brought much-needed water to the valley. 春汛给山谷带来了急需的水。| The freshet inundated the fields.  春汛淹没了田野。|近义词：flood, spate, deluge|反义词: drought, low water|noun
