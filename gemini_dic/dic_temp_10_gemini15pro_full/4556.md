alchemister:/ˈælkɪmɪstər/ | n. | 炼金术士 | An alchemist; one who practices alchemy.  A person who attempts to transmute base metals into gold, or who searches for a universal elixir. |  The alchemister toiled in his laboratory, hoping to discover the philosopher's stone.  炼金术士在他的实验室里辛勤工作，希望能发现点金石。|  Near synonyms: alchemist, chemist, sorcerer |  Antonyms: scientist (in the modern sense) | noun
