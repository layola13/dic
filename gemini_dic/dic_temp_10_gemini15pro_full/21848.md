bodice:/ˈbɒdɪs/ (US) /ˈbɔːdɪs/ (UK)| n. |紧身胸衣；女紧身衣|A woman's close-fitting garment for the upper part of the body, typically having a boned bodice.  A bodice is the part of a dress covering the body from the neck to the waist. |She wore a dress with a beautiful lace bodice. 她穿着一件带漂亮蕾丝胸衣的连衣裙。|Synonyms: corset, basque, camisole|Antonyms: N/A|noun
