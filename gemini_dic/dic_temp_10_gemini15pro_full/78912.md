gipsiest:/ˈdʒɪpsiɪst/| adj.|最像吉普赛人的；最具吉普赛风格的|Most characteristic of or resembling a Gypsy; most nomadic or free-spirited.|She had the gipsiest soul I'd ever encountered, always yearning for the open road.|近义词：nomadic, bohemian, free-spirited, unconventional|反义词：settled, conventional, conformist|词性:形容词
词源: From "gipsy" (now usually spelled "Gypsy") + "-est" (superlative suffix). "Gypsy" ultimately derives from the mistaken belief that the Romani people originated in Egypt.
