bindingnesses:/ˈbaɪndɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
约束力，契约义务
The state of being binding; the quality of being obligatory.
The bindingnesses of the contract are clear.  合同的约束力很明确。

近义词: obligations, commitments, liabilities
反义词: freedoms, liberties

词性: noun (plural)

词源: From binding + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality).  "Binding" itself comes from the verb "bind," from Old English "bindan," meaning "to tie, fasten."
