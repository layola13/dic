outreproduce:/ˌaʊtrɪprəˈduːs/
verb
过度繁殖；生育过多
to reproduce in excessive numbers or to an excessive extent.
The rabbits outreproduced their predators and overpopulated the island. 兔子过度繁殖，超过了捕食者的数量，导致岛屿人口过剩。
synonyms: overproduce, overpropagate
antonyms: underproduce
词性: 动词
