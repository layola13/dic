overnormality:/ˌoʊvərˌnɔːrˈmælɪti/
n.
过度正常；超常状态
A state of being excessively or abnormally normal, often to a point where it seems artificial or unsettling.  This can refer to behaviors, appearances, or social situations that adhere rigidly to norms, even when circumstances call for flexibility or deviation.
The overnormality of her behavior after the tragedy was disturbing.  她在这场悲剧后表现得过于正常，令人不安。
synonyms: hypernormality, unnatural normalcy, excessive conformity
antonyms: abnormality, deviance, eccentricity
noun

overnormal:/ˌoʊvərˈnɔːrml/
adj.
超常的；过分正常的
Exceeding or surpassing what is considered normal.
His overnormal politeness made her suspicious. 他过分的礼貌让她起了疑心。
synonyms:  excessive, exaggerated, extreme, inordinate
antonyms: subnormal, abnormal, deficient
adjective
