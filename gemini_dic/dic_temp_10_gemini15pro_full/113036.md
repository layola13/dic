luminescence:/ˌluːmɪˈnesəns/ | n. | 发光；冷光 | the emission of light by a substance that has not been heated, as distinguished from incandescence. | The luminescence of the fireflies danced in the night. 萤火虫的光芒在夜空中飞舞。| near synonyms: glow, radiance, shimmer, shine | antonyms: darkness, blackness | noun

luminescent:/ˌluːmɪˈnesənt/ | adj. | 发光的；发冷光的 | emitting light not caused by heat. | The luminescent paint glowed in the dark.  发光涂料在黑暗中闪闪发光。| near synonyms: glowing, radiant, shining | antonyms: dark, dull | adjective
