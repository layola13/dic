digestion:/daɪˈdʒɛstʃən/ /dɪˈdʒɛstʃən/ | n. |消化; 领悟; 理解|The process by which food is broken down into substances that can be absorbed and used by the body.  The process of understanding or assimilating information.|例句：Good digestion is essential for good health. 良好的消化对健康至关重要。

例句：He had trouble with the digestion of complex ideas. 他难以理解复杂的想法。|近义词：absorption, assimilation, breakdown|反义词：indigestion|词性:名词
