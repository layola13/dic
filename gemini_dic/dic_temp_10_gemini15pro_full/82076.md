grizzle:/ˈɡrɪzl/| vi. vt. n.| (因痛苦或烦恼)哭泣，抱怨；(天空)阴沉；细雨；灰白色 |To complain peevishly or whine; to fret.  (n.) A fine drizzle; a gray or roan color.|The baby grizzled when his toy was taken away.  婴儿的玩具被拿走时，他哭了起来。|近义词：whine, complain, grumble, fret, drizzle (v.); gray, roan (n.)|反义词：rejoice, celebrate (v.); bright, colorful (n.)|词性:verb, noun
词源:From Old French *grisler, frequentative of griser (“to turn gray”), from gris (“gray”).
