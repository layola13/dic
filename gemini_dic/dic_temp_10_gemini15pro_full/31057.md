chamfer:/ˈtʃæmfər/ (US), /ˈtʃæmfə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|倒角，削棱；|A beveled edge or corner.| The carpenter chamfered the edges of the table. 木匠将桌子的边缘倒了角。|近义词：bevel, cant|反义词：round, sharpen|n.
chamfer:/ˈtʃæmfər/ (US), /ˈtʃæmfə(r)/ (UK)| v.|倒角，削棱；|To cut away a corner or edge.| He chamfered the concrete block to make it fit. 他把混凝土块的棱角削掉以使其合适。|近义词：bevel, cant|反义词：round, sharpen|v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *chanfrein* (modern French *chanfrein*), meaning "muzzle, chamfer," from Late Latin *cantus* ("corner") + *frons* ("forehead, front").
