ghettoization:/ˌɡɛtəʊɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 隔离；少数民族聚居区化；贫民窟化 | The process of confining a group of people to a particular area, often with negative connotations of poverty, social exclusion, and limited opportunities. | The ghettoization of certain neighborhoods has led to a decline in property values and an increase in crime.  某些街区的贫民窟化导致了房产价值下跌和犯罪率上升。 | synonyms: segregation, isolation, marginalization | antonyms: integration, inclusion, assimilation | noun

ghettoize:/ˈɡɛtəʊˌaɪz/ | vt. | 使隔离；使集中居住于少数民族聚居区；使贫民窟化 | To confine or restrict a group of people to a particular area, often due to social, economic, or political factors. |  The government policies have effectively ghettoized immigrant communities. 政府的政策实际上使移民社区被隔离。 | synonyms: segregate, isolate, marginalize | antonyms: integrate, include, assimilate | verb
