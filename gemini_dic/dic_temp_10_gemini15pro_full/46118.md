cylindriform:/ˌsɪlɪnˈdrɪfɔːrm/
| adj. |圆柱形的，圆筒状的|Having the form of a cylinder; cylinder-shaped.|The bacteria are cylindriform and occur singly or in pairs. 这些细菌呈圆柱形，单独或成对出现。|The cactus has a cylindriform stem.  这棵仙人掌有一个圆柱形的茎。|近义词：cylindrical, columnar|反义词：spherical, globular|词性: 形容词
词源：cylindr(o)- (combining form meaning "cylinder") + -iform (suffix meaning "having the form of")
