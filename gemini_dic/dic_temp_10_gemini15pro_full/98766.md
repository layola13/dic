contaminated:/kənˈtæmɪneɪtɪd/
adj.
受污染的；被污染的
Made impure by contact or mixture with something unclean or harmful.
The river was contaminated with industrial waste. 这条河被工业废料污染了。
近义词：polluted, tainted, infected
反义词：pure, clean, unpolluted
词性:形容词

contaminate:/kənˈtæmɪneɪt/
vt.
污染；弄脏
To make something impure by exposure to or addition of a poisonous or polluting substance.
The oil spill contaminated the beach. 漏油污染了海滩。
近义词：pollute, taint, infect
反义词：purify, cleanse
词性:动词

contamination:/kənˌtæmɪˈneɪʃn/
n.
污染；污染物
The action or state of making or being made impure by polluting or poisoning.
The contamination of the water supply caused a health crisis.  水源的污染造成了健康危机。
近义词：pollution, taint, infection
反义词：purity, cleanliness
词性:名词


词源: From Latin contaminatus, past participle of contaminare "to pollute," from com- "together" + *tag-men-, from the root *tag- "to touch."
