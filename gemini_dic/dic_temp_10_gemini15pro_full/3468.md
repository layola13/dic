after-shave: /ˈæftərˌʃeɪv/ /ˈɑːftərˌʃeɪv/
noun
须后水；须后润肤露
A lotion, balm, or cream applied to the face after shaving.
He splashed some after-shave on his face. 他往脸上扑了些须后水。
Synonyms: after-shave lotion, after-shave balm, after-shave cream
Antonyms: pre-shave oil

词性: n.
