out-of-it:/ˌaʊt əv ˈɪt/ | adj. | 昏昏沉沉的；心不在焉的；不了解情况的 | Not fully alert or aware of one's surroundings; dazed, confused, or disoriented. | Example 1: After the surgery, I felt completely out of it for a few days.  手术后，我感觉昏昏沉沉了好几天。

Example 2: He seemed a bit out of it during the meeting, like he hadn't slept well.  他在会议期间看起来有点心不在焉，好像没睡好一样。| 近义词: dazed, confused, disoriented, spaced-out, foggy | 反义词: alert, aware, focused, attentive | 词性: 形容词
