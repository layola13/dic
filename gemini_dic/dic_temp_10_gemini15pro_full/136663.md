nonseasonally:/ˌnɒnˈsiːzənəli/ | /ˌnɑːnˈsiːzənəli/ | adv. | 非季节性地 | Not varying with the seasons; in a way that is not connected with or influenced by the time of year. |  The unemployment rate rose nonseasonally by 0.2%. 失业率非季节性地上升了0.2%。| Synonym: constantly, regularly | Antonym: seasonally | Part of Speech: adverb

PHRASE->

Nonseasonally adjusted (非季节性调整的):  This refers to data that has not been modified to remove the regular seasonal fluctuations.

Nonseasonally high/low (非季节性高/低): Used to describe values significantly above or below the expected range for a particular time of year.

Occurs nonseasonally (非季节性发生):  Describes events or phenomena that happen without a predictable seasonal pattern.

Remain nonseasonally consistent (保持非季节性一致): Indicates a stable trend not influenced by seasonal variations.

Fluctuate nonseasonally (非季节性波动): Describes variations that don't follow a predictable seasonal pattern.
