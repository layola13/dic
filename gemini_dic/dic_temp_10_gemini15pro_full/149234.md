oxyrrhodine:/ˌɒksɪˈrəʊdaɪn/ | n. |氧菱纹碱;氧菱纹花青素|A red anthocyanidin pigment found in certain plants.|Oxyrrhodine is a component of the red pigment in the flowers of the corn poppy. 氧菱纹碱是虞美人花红色素的组成部分。|Example 2:  Oxyrrhodine has been studied for its potential antioxidant properties. 氧菱纹碱的潜在抗氧化特性已得到研究。|Synonyms:  pelargonidin, cyanidin (related anthocyanidins)|Antonyms: None (no direct opposite for a specific pigment)|Noun
