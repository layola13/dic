ingraftment:/ɪnˈɡræftmənt/ | n. |移植, 植入, 接枝|The process of transplanting living tissue or an organ from one part of the body to another or from one individual to another, and its establishment in its new location.  The successful establishment of transplanted tissue or an organ.|The patient's prognosis depends on the successful ingraftment of the donated bone marrow.  病人的预后取决于捐赠骨髓的成功植入。|Synonyms: implantation, transplantation, grafting|Antonyms: rejection, expulsion|noun
