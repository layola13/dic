couchancy:/ˈkaʊtʃənsi/
n.
躺卧；伏卧；(尤指鹿)准备睡觉的姿势
The act of lying down, especially of a deer, in preparation for sleep.
The deer assumed a posture of couchancy in the tall grass.  鹿在高高的草丛中摆出伏卧的姿势。
近义词：recumbency, prostration
反义词: standing, uprightness
词性: noun

Etymology: From Old French *cocheant (“lying down”), present participle of cocher (“to lie down”), from Vulgar Latin *colcāre, from Latin col·lo·cā·re (“to place, arrange”), from com- (“together”) + locare (“to place”).
