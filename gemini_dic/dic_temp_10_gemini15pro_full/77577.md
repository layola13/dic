gecko:/ˈɡekoʊ/| n. |壁虎|A small lizard with soft skin, short legs, and suction pads on its feet, enabling it to climb walls and ceilings.|The gecko effortlessly scaled the smooth wall. 壁虎毫不费力地爬上了光滑的墙壁。|Synonyms: lizard, reptile|Antonyms: None|Noun
