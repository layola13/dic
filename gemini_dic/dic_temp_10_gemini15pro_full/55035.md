disquieter:/dɪsˈkwaɪətər/| n.|扰乱者，令人不安的人或事物|Someone or something that causes anxiety or unrest. |The constant noise was a disquieter to the whole neighborhood.  持续不断的噪音让整个社区的人都感到不安。|近义词：agitator, troublemaker, disturber|反义词:pacifier, soother|词性:noun
Etymology: From disquiet (v.) + -er (agent suffix). Disquiet comes from Old French *desquiete, from des- "away" + quiet "rest."
