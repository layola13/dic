clinographic:/ˌklaɪnəˈɡræfɪk/ |adj.|斜投影的，斜线图的|Relating to or denoting a method of graphical projection in which the object is represented by a drawing on a plane that is not perpendicular to any of the principal axes of the object.|The clinographic projection made it easier to visualize the three-dimensional structure of the molecule. 斜投影图使得更容易观察分子的三维结构。|Near synonyms: oblique, pictorial|Antonyms: orthographic, isometric|adjective
