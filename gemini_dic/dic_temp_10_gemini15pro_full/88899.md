holarctic:/hoʊˈlɑːrktɪk/ |adj. n.| 全北区的；全北区 |Relating to or denoting the biogeographic region comprising the northern parts of the Old and New Worlds.|The wolverine has a holarctic distribution.  狼獾的分布是全北区的。|The Holarctic realm includes North America and Eurasia. 全北界包括北美和欧亚大陆。|近义词:boreal, northern|反义词:austral, southern|词性:adjective, noun

Etymology:  From  *holo-* (Greek 'whole') + *Arctic*, referring to the Arctic region as a central part of this biogeographic zone.
