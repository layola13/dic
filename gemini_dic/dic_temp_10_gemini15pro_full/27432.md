camisole:/ˈkæmɪˌsoʊl/ (US) /ˈkæmɪsɒl/ (UK)
n.|吊带背心；camisole top|A camisole is a sleeveless undergarment, typically for women, similar to a tank top but often made of more delicate fabrics like silk or satin. It can also be worn as outerwear.|She wore a lace camisole under her sheer blouse.|top, tank top, undershirt|jacket, coat|noun
camisoles:/ˈkæmɪˌsoʊlz/ (US) /ˈkæmɪsɒlz/ (UK)
n.|吊带背心（复数）|The plural form of camisole, referring to more than one camisole.|She packed several camisoles for her vacation.|tops, tank tops, undershirts|jackets, coats|noun
