funkinesses:/ˈfʌŋkɪnɪsɪz/
|n.|古怪；独特；时髦|The plural of funkiness, referring to the quality or state of being funky.  It describes the distinctive characteristics, often unconventional or stylish, that contribute to a unique and appealing quality.  It can also refer to the state of being in a bad mood or experiencing a period of depression or anxiety.|Her vintage clothes and eclectic style contributed to her overall funkinesses. 她的复古服装和不拘一格的风格让她整体显得时髦古怪。|Synonyms: eccentricities, quirks, idiosyncrasies, peculiarities, styles, grooves|Antonyms: conventionalities, normalities, ordinariness|noun
