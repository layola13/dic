micromanaged:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈmænɪdʒd/
|v.|被微观管理的；被事无巨细地管理的|controlled or managed with excessive attention to minor details|例句：The employees felt micromanaged and demotivated. 员工感到被过度管理，失去了动力。|近义词：overmanaged, overcontrolled|反义词: empowered, trusted|词性: 动词(过去分词形式，用作形容词)
