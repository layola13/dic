orthotomy:/ɔːrˈθɒtəmi/
n.
正交,直角切割
A surgical incision made at right angles to the long axis of a part.  The act or process of cutting at right angles.
The surgeon performed an orthotomy to access the underlying tissue.  外科医生进行正交切开术以进入下面的组织。
orthotomy:/ɔːrˈθɑːtəmi/
n.

A surgical incision made at right angles to the long axis of a part. The act or process of cutting at right angles.
The orthotomy allowed for a clearer view of the damaged tendon. 正交切开术可以更清晰地看到受损的肌腱。
近义词:right-angle incision, perpendicular cut
反义词:oblique incision
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek orthos "straight" + -tomy  "cutting," from temnein "to cut."
