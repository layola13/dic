modernists:/ˈmɒdənɪsts/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdərˌnɪsts/ (US)| n.| 现代主义者；现代派艺术家|People who embrace modernism, especially in art, literature, or architecture.  Those who advocate for and practice new and experimental styles and ideas, breaking with traditional forms.|例句：The modernists rejected traditional artistic conventions. 现代主义者摒弃了传统的艺术惯例。

例句：Many modernists experimented with new forms of expression. 许多现代主义者尝试了新的表达形式。|近义词：innovators, reformers, avant-garde|反义词：traditionalists, conservatives|Noun
