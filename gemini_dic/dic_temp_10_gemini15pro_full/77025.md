gargoulette:/ɡɑːrɡuˈlet/| n.| 水壶，(尤指阿拉伯式)陶制水壶|A gargoulette is a type of earthenware water jug with a long neck and one or two handles, typically used in North Africa and the Middle East.|He filled the gargoulette with cool water from the well. 他用水井里的凉水装满了水壶。|Near synonyms: water jug, pitcher, ewer|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "cup" in terms of size)|noun
