blotch:/blɒtʃ/ (UK) /blɑːtʃ/ (US)| n. v.|污点，斑点；弄脏|A mark or stain, typically one that is irregular in shape.  To mark or stain with blotches.|The ink blotch on the paper ruined the drawing. 墨水在纸上的污渍毁了这幅画。 |He blotched the wall with paint. 他把墙上弄上了油漆污渍。|近义词：stain, spot, blemish, mark, patch|反义词：clearness, purity|词性: noun, verb

blotchy:/ˈblɒtʃi/ (UK) /ˈblɑːtʃi/ (US)| adj.|有污点的，有斑点的|Marked with blotches.|Her skin was blotchy after being in the sun too long.  在太阳下晒太久后，她的皮肤变得斑驳。|近义词：spotted, stained, blemished|反义词：clear, unblemished|词性: adjective
