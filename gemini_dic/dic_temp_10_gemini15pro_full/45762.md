cushionless:/ˈkʊʃən.ləs/
adjective
无缓冲的，无衬垫的
lacking a cushion; hard, uncomfortable
The cushionless chairs were hard on our backs.  这些没有垫子的椅子让我们背部很不舒服。
The old wooden bench was cushionless and uncomfortable.  这把旧的木凳子没有垫子，很不舒服。
近义词:unpadded, hard, uncomfortable
反义词:cushioned, padded, comfortable
词性:adjective

词源: cushion + -less.  "Cushion" comes from Old French "coissin," ultimately from Latin "coxinum" (cushion, pillow). "-less" is a suffix meaning "without" or "lacking."
