collages:/kəˈlɑːʒɪz/
n.
拼贴画，拼贴作品
A piece of art made by sticking various different materials such as photographs and pieces of paper or fabric onto a backing.
The artist's latest work was a collage of old photographs and newspaper clippings.  这位艺术家的最新作品是一幅由旧照片和剪报组成的拼贴画。
montage, patchwork
---
词性
noun
