mutualism:/ˈmjuːtʃuəlɪzəm/| n. |互利共生|A relationship between two species in which both species benefit.|例句：The relationship between the bee and the flower is a classic example of mutualism. 蜜蜂和花之间的关系是互利共生的典型例子。

例句：Mutualism is common in nature, with examples ranging from bacteria in the gut to ants protecting acacia trees. 互利共生在自然界中很常见，例子从肠道细菌到蚂蚁保护金合欢树都有。|近义词：symbiosis, cooperation|反义词:parasitism, competition|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *mūtuālis, from Latin mūtuus (“reciprocal, mutual”) + -ism.
