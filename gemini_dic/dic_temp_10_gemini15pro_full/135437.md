nonpersonal:/ˌnɒnˈpɜːsənəl/ /ˌnɑːnˈpɜːrsənəl/| adj.|非个人的，非人称的，物化的|Not relating to or involving particular people; not having or showing human qualities or feelings.|The CEO sent a nonpersonal memo to all employees regarding the new policy. 首席执行官就新政策向所有员工发送了一份非个人备忘录。|近义词：impersonal, objective, detached|反义词：personal, subjective, involved|词性:形容词

词源:
From non- + personal.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not." "Personal" comes from the Old French word "personnel" and ultimately from the Latin word "persona," meaning "mask" or "character."
