outbantered:/ˌaʊtˈbæntərd/
verb
胜过(某人)的玩笑;以玩笑胜过
To surpass someone in banter; to outdo someone in witty or playful conversation.
He outbantered his opponent, leaving them speechless with laughter. 他以玩笑胜过了对手，让他们笑得说不出话来。
近义词: outwit, outsmart, defeat
反义词: lose to, be defeated by
词性: verb

outbanter:/ˌaʊtˈbæntərd/
词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning surpassing or exceeding) + "banter" (verb meaning to engage in playful conversation).
中文翻译: 以玩笑胜过，在说笑方面胜过
