chairpeople:/ˈtʃɛːrˌpiːpəl/
noun
主席（复数，性别包容）
A gender-neutral plural form of chairperson, referring to people who preside over a meeting, committee, or organization.
The chairpeople of the various committees met to discuss the upcoming conference. 各个委员会的主席们开会讨论即将召开的会议。
synonyms: chairpersons, chairs, presidents, moderators, facilitators
antonyms: members, participants, attendees
