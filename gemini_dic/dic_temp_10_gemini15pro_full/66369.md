excusers:/ɪkˈskjuːzərz/ | n. | 找借口的人；辩解者 |  People who habitually make excuses for themselves or others.  Those who tend to justify or defend actions, often inappropriately. |  He's one of those excusers who always has a reason why he can't do something. 他是那种总能找到理由不做事情的找借口的人。|  The meeting was full of excusers trying to explain away the project's failure.  会议上满是找借口的人，试图为项目的失败辩解。 | synonyms: apologists, justifiers | antonyms:  critics, accusers | noun
