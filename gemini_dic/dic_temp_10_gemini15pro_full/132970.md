nonemulative:/ˌnɒnˈemjʊlətɪv/
adjective
非竞争性的；非模仿的
Not involving or based on emulation or rivalry.
She took a nonemulative approach to learning, focusing on self-improvement rather than comparing herself to others. 她采取了一种非竞争性的学习方法，专注于自我提升而不是与他人比较。
The artist developed a nonemulative style, distinct from any other contemporary. 这位艺术家形成了一种非模仿性的风格，与任何其他当代艺术家都不同。
近义词:noncompetitive, individualistic, independent
反义词:emulative, competitive, imitative
词性:adjective
