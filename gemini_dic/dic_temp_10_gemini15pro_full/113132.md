lungwort:/ˈlʌŋˌwɜːrt/| n.|肺草；肺衣|Pulmonaria, a genus of flowering plants in the borage family, traditionally used in herbal medicine for lung ailments. Also, Lobaria pulmonaria, a type of lichen resembling lung tissue, also with purported medicinal properties.|例句：She made a tea from dried lungwort. 她用干肺草泡了茶。
