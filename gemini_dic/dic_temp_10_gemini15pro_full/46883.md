daringly:/ˈdeərɪŋli/ | adv. | 大胆地；勇敢地 | In a bold and adventurous manner, taking risks. | She daringly climbed the highest mountain without any safety equipment. 她大胆地攀登了最高的山峰，没有任何安全设备。|  He daringly confronted the bully. 他勇敢地与恶霸对峙。 | 近义词: boldly, bravely, courageously, audaciously | 反义词: timidly, cautiously, fearfully | 词性: 副词

词源: From daring + -ly. Daring comes from the Old English *dearrian, meaning "to dare," which is related to the German "dürfen."
