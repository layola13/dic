mission-critical:/ˈmɪʃən ˌkrɪtɪkəl/| adj.|关键任务的，至关重要的|Essential for the success of a mission or project; vital, crucial.|例句：The server is mission-critical to our business operations.  这台服务器对我们的业务运营至关重要。|例句：Redundancy is built into mission-critical systems to prevent failures.  关键任务系统内置了冗余以防止故障。|近义词：essential, crucial, vital, indispensable|反义词：unimportant, nonessential, trivial|词性:形容词

词源:
"mission" (任务) + "critical" (关键的).  "Mission" comes from the Latin "missio," meaning "a sending." "Critical" comes from the Greek "kritikos," meaning "able to judge."
