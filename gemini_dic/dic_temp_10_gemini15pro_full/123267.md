monodramas:/ˈmɒnəʊˌdrɑːməz/ (US) /ˈmɑːnəˌdrɑːməz/
n.
独角戏，单人剧
A play or dramatic piece for a single performer.
Samuel Beckett's "Krapp's Last Tape" is a powerful example of a monodrama.
萨缪尔·贝克特的《克莱普最后的磁带》是一部极具感染力的独角戏。

The actor captivated the audience with his intense performance in the monodrama.
这位演员以其在独角戏中精彩的表演深深吸引了观众。


近义词:solo performance, one-man show, one-woman show
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider plays with multiple actors)
