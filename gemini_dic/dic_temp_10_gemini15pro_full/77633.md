gelatigenous:/dʒəˌlætˈɪdʒənəs/ |adj.|产胶的；胶质的|Producing or containing gelatin; gelatinous.|例句：Certain seaweeds are gelatigenous and are used in making agar.  某些海藻是产胶的，可用于制造琼脂。

例句：The gelatigenous substance coated the wound and helped it heal. 这种胶状物质覆盖了伤口并帮助其愈合。|近义词：gelatinous, colloidal|反义词：non-gelatinous, watery|词性：形容词

Etymology: From Latin *gelatin-, stem of gelatio ("freezing, congealing"), + -genous ("producing, generating").  Ultimately from gelare ("to freeze").
