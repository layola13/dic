discourse:/ˈdɪskɔːrs/ (US), /ˈdɪskɔːs/ (UK)| n. vi.| 讨论；演讲；论文；话语 | A formal discussion of a subject in speech or writing. | The academic discourse surrounding climate change is complex.  气候变化的学术讨论很复杂。 | synonyms: discussion, conversation, dialogue, talk, speech, lecture, address, sermon, treatise, essay, paper, thesis | antonyms: silence, quiet | noun
