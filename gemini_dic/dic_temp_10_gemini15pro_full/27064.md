calf's:/kæfs/
n.
小牛的; 牛犊的
The possessive form of "calf," referring to something belonging to or associated with a young cow or bull.
The farmer bottle-fed the calf's milk.  农民用奶瓶喂小牛牛奶。
The calf's leg was injured. 小牛的腿受伤了。
Synonyms:  young bovine's, baby cow's
Antonyms: cow's, bull's
POS: noun, possessive
