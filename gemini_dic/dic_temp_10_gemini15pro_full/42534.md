countermanifestoes: /ˌkaʊntərˌmænɪˈfɛstoʊz/
|n.| 反宣言；反对声明|A public declaration of policy and aims, especially one issued before an election by a political party or candidate, which opposes another party's manifesto.|The socialist party issued a series of countermanifestoes to challenge the capitalist agenda.|Synonyms: rebuttal, denunciation, riposte|Antonyms: manifesto, proclamation, declaration|noun
