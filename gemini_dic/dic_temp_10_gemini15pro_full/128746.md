niggardship:/ˈnɪɡərdʃɪp/
n.
吝啬，小气
Extreme unwillingness to spend money; stinginess.
His niggardship prevented him from donating to the charity. 他的吝啬使他不愿向慈善机构捐款。

Scrooge McDuck was famous for his niggardship.  史高治·麦克老鸭以其吝啬而闻名。
近义词: stinginess, parsimony, miserliness, meanness
反义词: generosity, liberality, munificence
词性: 名词
