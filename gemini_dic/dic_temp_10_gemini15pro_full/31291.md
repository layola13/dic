chaperon:/ˈʃæpərəʊn/ (UK), /ˈʃæpəroʊn/ (US)| n. vt.|陪伴人；监护人； chaperones (n. 复数)|A person, usually an older woman, who accompanies and supervises a young unmarried woman or a group of young people on social occasions.|例句：The school hired chaperones for the prom. 学校为毕业舞会聘请了陪伴人。

例句：Her aunt chaperoned her to the dance. 她阿姨陪她去跳舞。|近义词：escort, guardian, attendant|反义词: N/A|词性: noun, verb
