osho:/ˈoʊʃoʊ/| n.|奥修|Indian mystic and spiritual teacher, known for his controversial teachings and lifestyle.  His original name was Chandra Mohan Jain, later changed to Acharya Rajneesh and finally to Osho.|Osho's teachings emphasized meditation, mindfulness, and individual freedom.|近义词:guru, spiritual leader|反义词:disciple, follower|Noun

osho:/ˈoʊʃoʊ/| adj.|奥修的|Relating to or characteristic of the teachings or philosophy of Osho.|The Osho meditation techniques are quite popular.|近义词:Rajneeshee|反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "conventional" or "traditional" in a spiritual context)|Adjective
