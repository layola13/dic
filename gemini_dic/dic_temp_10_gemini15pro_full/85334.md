harpoon:/hɑːrˈpuːn/ | n. vt. | 鱼叉，捕鲸叉；用鱼叉刺 | A barbed spear or iron hook used for catching whales and other large fish; to strike, catch, or kill with a harpoon. | The whalers harpooned the massive blue whale. 捕鲸人用鱼叉刺中了那头巨大的蓝鲸。 |  spear, lance |  net | noun, verb

harpoons:/hɑːrˈpuːnz/ | n. | 鱼叉（复数） | Harpoons are used to hunt large marine animals. 鱼叉是用来捕猎大型海洋动物的。| spears, lances | nets | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French harpon, from harpe "harp, grapple," of Germanic origin.  The word originally referred to a grappling hook, and the resemblance to a harp may have influenced the name.
