cubilose: /ˈkjuːbɪˌloʊs/ | n. | 燕窝 |  A gelatinous substance secreted by certain swiftlets, notably Aerodramus fuciphagus, used in Chinese cuisine. It is made of the hardened saliva of the bird and is considered a delicacy. |  The restaurant's signature dish was bird's nest soup made with genuine cubilose. 这家餐厅的招牌菜是用正宗燕窝做的燕窝汤。 | Synonym: bird's nest, edible bird's nest | Antonym: (No direct antonym) | noun
