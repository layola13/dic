ascii:/ˈæski/ | n. | 美国信息交换标准码 | American Standard Code for Information Interchange, a character encoding standard for electronic communication. ASCII codes represent text in computers, telecommunications equipment, and other devices. |  The document was saved in ASCII format.  该文件以ASCII格式保存。 | synonym: character set, code | antonym: Unicode (in a broad sense, as a more comprehensive alternative) | noun
