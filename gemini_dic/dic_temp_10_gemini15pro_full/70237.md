fictile:/ˈfɪktaɪl/
adjective
可塑的；陶土制的
Capable of being molded; made of pottery or earthenware.
The artist enjoyed working with the fictile clay, shaping it into intricate forms.  艺术家喜欢用可塑的粘土工作，把它塑造成复杂的形状。
The museum boasts a large collection of fictile artifacts from ancient Greece.  这家博物馆收藏了大量来自古希腊的陶土文物。
近义词:malleable, pliable, plastic, earthen, ceramic
反义词:rigid, inflexible, unyielding
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *fictilis*, from *fingere* "to shape, mold, fashion."
