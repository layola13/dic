detrition:/dɪˈtrɪʃən/| n. |损耗，磨损，消耗|  The gradual wearing away or weakening of something, often as a result of friction or erosion.  Also, a reduction in size or strength, as by loss of members.|  The coastal cliffs suffer from constant detrition by the sea.  海岸悬崖不断受到海水的侵蚀。|  Years of detrition have weakened the power of the trade unions.  多年的损耗削弱了工会的力量。| synonyms: erosion, abrasion, wearing away, attenuation, weakening, decline | antonyms: accretion, growth, strengthening, augmentation | noun
