aforecited:/əˈfɔːrˌsaɪtɪd/
adjective
前述的，上述的
mentioned earlier in a text or speech.
The aforementioned article discusses this issue in detail.  上述文章详细讨论了这个问题。
The judge referred to the aforecited evidence. 法官提到了前述的证据。
synonym: aforementioned, previously mentioned, aforesaid
antonym: following, subsequent
词性: adjective
