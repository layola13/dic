decomp:/ˌdiːˈkɒmp/ (UK), /ˌdiːˈkɑːmp/ (US)| v. |分解，腐烂|(From decompose) To break down or rot. |The body began to decomp rapidly in the heat. 尸体在高温下迅速腐烂。|近义词：decompose, rot, decay, break down|反义词:synthesize, compose, grow|词性:动词 (abbreviation)


词源:  Shortening of "decompose".  "Decompose" comes from French "décomposer," from "dé-" (reversal, removal) + "composer" (to compose).  Ultimately from Latin "componere" (to put together), from "com-" (together) + "ponere" (to place).
