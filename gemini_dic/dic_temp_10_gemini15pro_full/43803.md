crenel:/ˈkrɛnəl/ (US), /ˈkrɛnl/ (UK)| n. vt.|城垛，垛口；使有城垛|A rectangular gap or indentation in a battlement.  A battlement is a low wall at the top of a fort or castle, with open spaces for shooting through.|The castle walls were topped with crenels. 城墙顶部有城垛。|Synonyms: battlement, merlon, embrasure|Antonyms: solid wall (no direct antonym exists for the specific opening)|noun

crenel:/ˈkrɛnəl/ (US), /ˈkrɛnl/ (UK)| vt.|使有城垛；锯齿状|To furnish with crenels or battlements.|They crenelled the top of the wall for defensive purposes.  他们为了防御的目的在墙顶上设置了城垛。|Synonyms: embattle, fortify|Antonyms: smooth, flatten|verb
