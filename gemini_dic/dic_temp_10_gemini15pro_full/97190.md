inflameable:/ɪnˈfleɪməbl/| adj.|易燃的，可燃的|Easily set on fire; flammable.  Note: Inflammable and flammable mean the same thing.  The in- prefix does *not* mean "not" in this case.|The curtains are made of highly inflameable material.|近义词：flammable, combustible|反义词:nonflammable, fire-resistant|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *inflammabilis*, from Latin *inflammāre* (to set on fire)
