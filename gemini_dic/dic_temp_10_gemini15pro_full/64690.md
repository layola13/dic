escaper's:/ɪˈskeɪpərz/| n.| 逃脱者(的) | possessive form of escaper, referring to something belonging to or associated with an escaper. | The escaper's backpack was left behind in the tunnel. 逃脱者的背包被落在隧道里了。| 近义词: fugitive's, runaway's | 反义词: captor's, jailer's | 词性: possessive noun


词源: From escape (v.) + -er (suffix forming agent nouns) + -'s (possessive suffix).  "Escape" ultimately comes from the Old French "eschaper," from the Vulgar Latin "excappare," meaning "to get out of one's cape," hence "to extricate oneself."
