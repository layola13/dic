antioxygenators:/ˌæntiˈɒksɪdʒəneɪtəz/
n.
抗氧化剂，除氧器
Substances that inhibit oxidation or remove oxygen from a system.  These are used in various industrial and scientific applications, particularly where the presence of oxygen is undesirable.
Antioxygenators are crucial for preserving food products. 抗氧化剂对于保存食品至关重要。
The boiler system uses antioxygenators to prevent corrosion. 锅炉系统使用除氧器来防止腐蚀。
近义词: antioxidants, deoxygenators
反义词: oxidants, oxygenators
词性: noun
