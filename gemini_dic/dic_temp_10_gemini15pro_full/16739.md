barterable:/ˈbɑːrtərəbl/
adjective
可交换的，可易货的
Able to be bartered or exchanged for other goods or services without using money.
She had some barterable skills, like sewing and gardening. 她有一些可以交换的技能，比如缝纫和园艺。
Synonyms: exchangeable, tradable, swappable
Antonyms: non-exchangeable, unsalable
词性: adjective
