avocation:/ˌævəˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 副业，嗜好，消遣 |  An activity pursued for pleasure; a hobby.  |  His avocation is collecting stamps. 他的副业是集邮。 | synonyms: hobby, pastime, pursuit, leisure activity | antonyms: vocation, profession, job | noun

avocation:/ˌævəˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |  （古）转移注意力的东西; 诱惑 | Something that calls one away from one's regular work or vocation; a distraction. | The noisy traffic was an avocation from his studies. 嘈杂的交通声使他无法专心学习。 | synonyms: distraction, diversion | antonyms: concentration, focus | noun
