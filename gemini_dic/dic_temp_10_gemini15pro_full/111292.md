littlest:/ˈlɪtlɪst/| adj.|最小的，最幼小的|Superlative form of little: smallest in size, amount, or degree.|She's the littlest girl in the class. 她是班里最小的女孩。|Synonyms: smallest, tiniest, youngest|Antonyms: biggest, largest, oldest|adjective
