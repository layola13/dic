nerviness:/ˈnɜːrvɪnɪs/| n.|紧张不安，神经质|The nerviness of the young actor was apparent before his audition.  这位年轻演员在试镜前的紧张显而易见。|例句：His nerviness before the big game was understandable. 他在大赛前的紧张是可以理解的。

例句：She tried to hide her nerviness, but her trembling hands gave her away. 她试图掩饰自己的紧张，但她颤抖的双手暴露了她。|近义词：anxiety, nervousness, apprehension, edginess|反义词：calmness, composure, tranquility|词性:noun

词源: From nerve + -iness.  源自 nerve（神经）+ -iness（名词后缀，表示状态或性质）。
