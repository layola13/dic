havocker:/ˈhævəkər/ | n. | 肆虐者；破坏者 | Someone who causes havoc or widespread destruction. | The havocker stormed through the town, leaving a trail of destruction in their wake.  肆虐者席卷了小镇，留下一片狼藉。|  The hacker wreaked havoc on the company's computer systems.  这个黑客对公司的计算机系统造成了严重破坏。 | Synonyms: vandal, destroyer, wrecker, saboteur | Antonyms: protector, guardian, preserver | noun

Etymology: From the noun "havoc" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Havoc" comes from the Anglo-Norman "havok," an exclamation of encouragement to pillage.
