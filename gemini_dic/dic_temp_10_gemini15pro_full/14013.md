aurochs:/ˈɔːrɒks/ | n. | 欧洲野牛（已灭绝） | A large wild ox (Bos primigenius) that inhabited Europe, Asia, and North Africa in prehistoric times and was domesticated into present-day cattle.  It is now extinct. | 例句：Cave paintings depict aurochs hunting scenes.  洞穴壁画描绘了欧洲野牛狩猎的场景。

例句：The aurochs is the ancestor of domestic cattle. 欧洲野牛是家牛的祖先。 | 近义词：wild ox, ur | 反义词:  (No direct antonym as it refers to a specific extinct species.  Perhaps "domestic cattle" could be considered a conceptual opposite.) | 词性: noun
