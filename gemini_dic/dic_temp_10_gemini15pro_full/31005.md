chalklike:/ˈtʃɔːklaɪk/
adjective
像粉笔的，白垩似的
Resembling chalk in appearance or texture; chalky.
The walls had a chalklike texture.  墙的质地像粉笔一样。
The pills left a chalklike residue on his tongue. 药片在他的舌头上留下了一种像粉笔一样的残留物。
近义词:chalky, calcareous
反义词:smooth, glossy
词性:adjective
