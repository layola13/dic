outwatch:/ˈaʊtwɒtʃ/ | v. | 监视；密切注视；熬夜；比…活得长 | To watch out for or observe something or someone closely, often for a long period.  Also, to live longer than someone else. | I outwatched the storm, staying up all night until it passed. 我熬夜看着暴风雨，直到它过去。 | Synonyms: observe, monitor, surveil, outlive | Antonyms: ignore, neglect | verb
