exult:/ɪɡˈzʌlt/ | v. | 欢欣鼓舞，欣喜若狂 | To feel or show great happiness or triumph. |  She exulted at having finally achieved her goal. 她为最终实现了目标而欣喜若狂。

The team exulted in their victory.  团队为他们的胜利而欢欣鼓舞。


近义词：rejoice, triumph, celebrate | 反义词: mourn, grieve | 词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin exsultare "to leap up, jump for joy,"  from ex- "out" + saltare "to leap, dance"
