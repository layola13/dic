fuyu:/ˈfuːjuː/ /ˈfjuːjuː/ | n. | 冬 | Winter (especially in a poetic or literary context, often referring to the hardships of winter). | The fuyu brought with it biting winds and heavy snow. 冬天带来了刺骨的寒风和鹅毛大雪。| Synonyms: winter, cold season | Antonyms: summer, natsu (Japanese) | noun

Etymology:  From Japanese 冬 (fuyu), meaning "winter."
