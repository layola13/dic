illuminative:/ɪˈluːmɪnətɪv/
adjective
启发的，启迪的；阐明的
Making things clearer or easier to understand.  Shedding light on a subject.
例句：The professor's lecture was illuminative, helping the students understand the complex topic. 教授的讲座很有启发性，帮助学生理解了这个复杂的主题。

例句：His illuminative comments clarified the confusing passage in the book. 他具有启发性的评论阐明了书中令人困惑的段落。

近义词：enlightening, informative, insightful, revealing
反义词：obscure, confusing, unclear, mystifying
词性：adjective

词源：From Late Latin illuminativus, from illuminare "to illuminate," from Latin in- "in" + luminare "to light up," from lumen "light."
