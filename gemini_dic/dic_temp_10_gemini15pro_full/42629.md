counterrioters:/ˌkaʊntərˈraɪətərz/ | n. | 反暴乱者，镇暴者 | People who oppose and actively resist rioters. |  The counterrioters formed a human barricade to protect the store. 反暴乱者组成人墙来保护商店。| Synonyms: anti-rioters, vigilantes | Antonyms: rioters, insurgents | Noun
counterrioter:/ˌkaʊntərˈraɪətər/ | n. | 反暴乱者，镇暴者 | A person who opposes and actively resists rioters. | The counterrioter was injured in the clash.  这名反暴乱者在冲突中受伤。 | Synonyms: anti-rioter, vigilante | Antonyms: rioter, insurgent | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- (prefix meaning "against, opposite") + rioter (noun meaning "a person who participates in a riot").  "Riot" ultimately derives from Old French "riote" meaning "dispute, quarrel".
