deerberry:/ˈdɪərˌbɛri/ | n. | 鹿莓；越橘的一种 | A type of shrub in the heath family, producing edible but rather insipid berries.  The plant is a food source for deer and other wildlife. |  The deerberry bushes were laden with fruit. 鹿莓灌木上果实累累。|  Deerberry jam is not very common. 鹿莓酱不是很常见。 | synonyms: huckleberry, whortleberry | antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a specific berry type, perhaps "artificial fruit") | noun
