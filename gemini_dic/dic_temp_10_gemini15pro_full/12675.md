assemblywomen: /əˈsɛmbliˌwɪmɪn/
n.
女议员（复数）
A group of women who are members of an assembly, especially a legislative one.
The assemblywomen voted in favor of the new bill. 女议员们投票赞成这项新法案。
近义词：female legislators, congresswomen
反义词：assemblymen
词性：名词


assemblywoman: /əˈsɛmbliˌwʊmən/
n.
女议员（单数）
A woman who is a member of an assembly, especially a legislative one.
The assemblywoman gave a powerful speech on the importance of education.  这位女议员就教育的重要性发表了强有力的讲话。
近义词：female legislator, congresswoman
反义词：assemblyman
词性：名词


词源:
From assembly + woman.  The plural form is irregular, using -women instead of -women.  The word "assembly" itself comes from Old French "assembler," meaning "to assemble," ultimately derived from the Latin "ad-" (to) + "simulare" (to bring together).
