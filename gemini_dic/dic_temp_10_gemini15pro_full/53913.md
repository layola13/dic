discouragement:/dɪˈskɜːrɪdʒmənt/ | n. | 灰心；沮丧；气馁；令人灰心或沮丧的事物 | A feeling of loss of confidence or enthusiasm; something that causes this feeling. |  His constant criticism led to her discouragement. 他不断的批评导致她灰心丧气。| synonyms: despair, despondency, hopelessness, dispiritedness | antonyms: encouragement, hope, optimism | noun

discourage:/dɪˈskɜːrɪdʒ/ | vt. | 使灰心；使沮丧；劝阻；阻拦 | To cause someone to lose confidence or enthusiasm; to try to prevent someone from doing something. |  The rain discouraged us from going out.  下雨让我们打消了外出的念头。| synonyms: deter, dissuade, hinder, demoralize | antonyms: encourage, motivate, inspire | verb
