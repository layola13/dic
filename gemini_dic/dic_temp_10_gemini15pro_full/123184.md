monob:/ˈmɒnɒb/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnɑːb/ (US)| n. |单眼望远镜|A small telescope designed for use with one eye.|He used a monob to get a closer look at the bird. 他用单筒望远镜仔细观察那只鸟。|近义词: monocular, telescope|反义词: binoculars|词性:名词

词源: "mono-" (one) + "-ob" (abbreviation of obsolete "op" from "optical").  From Greek "monos" (single, alone).  The "-ob" ending is also found in words like "stereob" (stereoscope).
