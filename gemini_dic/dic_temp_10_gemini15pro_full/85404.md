haruspication:/həˌrʌspɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
内脏占卜，观察动物内脏以预测未来
the practice of examining the entrails of sacrificed animals, especially the liver, in order to divine the future.
Haruspication was a common practice in ancient Rome.  古罗马时期，内脏占卜是一种常见的做法。
The haruspex examined the liver of the sacrificed ox.  占卜师检查了献祭的牛的肝脏。
近义词：augury, divination, prophecy
