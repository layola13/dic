moralisingly:/ˈmɒrəlaɪzɪŋli/ /ˈmɔːrəlaɪzɪŋli/
| adv. | 道德化地，教训人地 | In a manner intended to teach a moral lesson, often in a self-righteous or tedious way. | He spoke moralisingly about the dangers of drinking. 他教训人似地谈论酗酒的危害。| 近义词：preachily, sanctimoniously, sententiously | 反义词：nonjudgmentally, open-mindedly | 词性: 副词
