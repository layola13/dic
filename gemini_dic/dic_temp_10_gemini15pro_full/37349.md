commendator:/ˌkɒmənˈdeɪtər/ | n. | 推荐者；赞扬者；委托管理者 | A person who praises or recommends someone or something; someone entrusted with the administration of a benefice. |  The king appointed a commendator to oversee the abbey's lands. 国王任命了一位委托管理者来监督修道院的土地。

The commendator spoke highly of the young artist's talent.  推荐者高度赞扬了这位年轻艺术家的才华。 | synonyms:  praiser, advocate, supporter, patron,  administrator, trustee | antonyms: critic, detractor, opponent | noun
