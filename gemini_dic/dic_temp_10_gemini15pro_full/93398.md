illiquation:/ɪlɪˈkweɪʃən/
n.
渗滤; 淋溶; 溶滤; 浸滤
The process of downward movement of dissolved or suspended material from one soil horizon to another by percolating water.
Rainwater caused the illiquiation of minerals from the topsoil. 雨水导致表土矿物质的淋溶。
leaching, eluviation, percolation
accumulation, deposition
noun
