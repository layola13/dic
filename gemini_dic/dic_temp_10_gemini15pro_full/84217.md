half-rueful:/ˌhɑːfˈruːfəl/
adjective
半懊悔的，略带悔意的
feeling or expressing slight regret or sorrow, often with a touch of amusement or irony.
He gave a half-rueful smile. 他带着一丝懊悔的微笑。
near synonyms:  apologetic, contrite, penitent, remorseful, regretful
antonyms:  unrepentant, unapologetic, remorseless
词性: adjective
