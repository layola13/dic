antlerless:/ˈæntlərlɪs/
adjective
无角的；没有鹿角的
Lacking antlers.  Referring to female deer, or young male deer that haven't yet grown antlers.
The hunter was only permitted to take antlerless deer this season.  这个狩猎季猎人只允许猎捕无角的鹿。
The regulations specified an antlerless deer hunt to help manage the population.  该规定指定了一场无角鹿狩猎以帮助管理种群数量。
synonym: hornless, without antlers
antonym: antlered, horned
