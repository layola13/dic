garageable:/ɡəˈrɑːdʒəbl/
adjective
可停放于车库的; 可放入车库的
Suitable to be put or stored in a garage.
The car is small and garageable. 这辆车很小，可以停放在车库里。
近义词:storable, parkable
反义词:ungarageable, too large for a garage
词性:adjective
