localism:/ˈloʊkəlɪzəm/| n.|地方主义，地方观念；方言，土语|A preference for one's own area or region, especially when this results in limited outlook or hostility to outsiders.  The use of local words or pronunciations.|例句：The localism of the villagers made it difficult for the new teacher to integrate. 村民的地方观念使新来的老师难以融入。

例句：His speech was full of localisms that city dwellers couldn't understand. 他的讲话充满了城里人听不懂的方言。|近义词：provincialism, regionalism|反义词: cosmopolitanism, globalism|词性:noun
