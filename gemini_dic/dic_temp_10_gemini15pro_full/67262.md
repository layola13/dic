expurgatorious:/ˌekspərˈɡeɪtɔːriəs/
adjective
净化（的）；删除不当内容（的）
Tending to expurgate, or purify; expurgatory. Removing objectionable passages from a publication.
例句：The editor made several expurgatorious changes to the manuscript before publication.  编辑在出版前对稿件进行了几处净化修改。

例句：The expurgatorious version of the novel omitted several sexually explicit scenes. 小说净化版删除了几个露骨的性爱场景。

近义词：cleansing, purifying, bowdlerizing, censoring
反义词：contaminating, polluting, uncensored

词性：adjective

词源：From Latin *expurgatorius, from expurgat-, past participle stem of expurgare "to cleanse, purify," from ex- "out" + purgare "to cleanse."
