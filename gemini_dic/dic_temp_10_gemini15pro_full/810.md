abstainers: /əbˈsteɪnərz/
noun
节制者；戒绝者
People who abstain from something, typically alcohol or other potentially harmful substances.
The abstainers at the party stuck to soda and juice.  聚会上不喝酒的人坚持喝苏打水和果汁。
The group of abstainers met weekly to support each other. 这群戒酒者每周聚会互相支持。
近义词: teetotalers, non-drinkers
反义词: drinkers, addicts
词性: noun

abstain: /əbˈsteɪn/
verb
戒除；弃权
To restrain oneself from doing or enjoying something.
He abstained from voting on the new policy. 他对新政策投了弃权票。
She decided to abstain from sugar for a month. 她决定戒糖一个月。
近义词: refrain, forbear, avoid
反义词: indulge, partake
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English abstynen, from Old French abstenir, from Latin abstinēre (to hold back from, abstain from), from abs- (from, away from) + tenēre (to hold).

USAGE->
一般现在时:
Many people abstain from alcohol for health reasons. = 许多人出于健康原因戒酒。
Some religious groups abstain from certain foods. = 一些宗教团体戒除某些食物。

一般过去时:
He abstained from voting in the last election. = 他在上次选举中弃权了。
She abstained from dessert because she was on a diet. = 她因为节食而没有吃甜点。

现在完成时:
They have abstained from smoking for several years. = 他们已经戒烟好几年了。
She has abstained from caffeine since she was pregnant. = 自从怀孕以来，她一直没有摄入咖啡因。

过去完成时:
He had abstained from alcohol for five years before he started drinking again. = 他戒酒五年后又开始喝酒了。
She had abstained from red meat for years before she decided to try it again. = 她戒红肉多年后决定再试一次。

将来时:
I will abstain from alcohol during my pregnancy. = 我在怀孕期间将戒酒。
He will abstain from voting if he feels he doesn't have enough information. = 如果他觉得信息不足，他将弃权。

虚拟语气:
If I were to abstain from coffee, I would be very tired. = 如果我不喝咖啡，我会很累。
I wish I could abstain from sweets, but they are my weakness. = 我希望我能戒掉甜食，但它们是我的弱点。

被动语态:
Voting was abstained from by several members of the committee. = 委员会的几位成员弃权了。
(Note: The passive voice is less common with "abstain" and can sound awkward.)


疑问句:
Do you abstain from any foods or drinks? = 你戒除任何食物或饮料吗？
Why did you abstain from voting? = 你为什么弃权？

否定句:
I don't abstain from alcohol completely, but I drink in moderation. = 我没有完全戒酒，但我有节制地喝酒。
She didn't abstain from expressing her opinion. = 她没有放弃表达自己的意见。
