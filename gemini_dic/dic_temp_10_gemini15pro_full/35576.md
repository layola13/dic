coalsack: /ˈkoʊlsæk/ | n. |煤袋；暗星云|A dark nebula in the Southern Hemisphere, near the Southern Cross.  It appears as a dark patch against the Milky Way. |  The Coalsack Nebula is easily visible to the naked eye.  煤袋星云肉眼很容易看到。| The Coalsack is one of the most prominent dark nebulae.  煤袋星云是最突出的暗星云之一。 | synonyms: dark nebula, black nebula | antonyms: bright nebula | noun
