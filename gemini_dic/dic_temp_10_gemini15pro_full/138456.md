novator:/ˈnoʊveɪtər/| n.|革新者，创新者|A person who introduces new methods, ideas, or products.|例句：He was a true novator in the field of medicine. 他是医学领域的真正革新者。

例句：The company is looking for novators who can help them stay ahead of the competition.  这家公司正在寻找能够帮助他们保持竞争优势的创新者。|近义词：innovator, pioneer, reformer, groundbreaker|反义词:conservative, traditionalist|词性:noun
Etymology: From Latin *novator*, from novāre (“to renew, make new”), from novus (“new”).
