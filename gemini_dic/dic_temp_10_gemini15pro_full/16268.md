bap:/bæp/
noun
轻拍；轻打；小圆面包
a gentle tap or pat.  A small, soft round roll or bun.
He gave the dog a bap on the head. 他拍了拍狗的头。
She bought a bap filled with ham and cheese. 她买了一个夹着火腿和奶酪的小圆面包。
近义词:pat, tap, bun, roll
反义词:slap, punch
