metanephros:/ˈmɛtənɛfrɒs/ | n. | 后肾 | The definitive kidney of amniotes, developing from the posterior portion of the nephrogenic cord.  It replaces the mesonephros and forms the permanent kidney in reptiles, birds, and mammals. | The metanephros becomes functional late in fetal life. 后肾在胎儿后期开始发挥作用。 |  The metanephros filters waste products from the blood. 后肾过滤血液中的废物。 | permanent kidney, final kidney |  pronephros, mesonephros | noun
