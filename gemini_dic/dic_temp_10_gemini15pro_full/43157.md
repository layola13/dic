cozying:/ˈkoʊziɪŋ/| v. |使舒适；窝在…里；变得亲密|Making oneself or something else warm and comfortable, often in a close or intimate way.  Also, becoming friendly or intimate with someone, often for personal gain.|She was cozying up to the fire with a good book. 她拿着一本好书依偎在火炉旁。|Synonyms: snuggling, cuddling, nestling, ingratiating oneself with|Antonyms: distancing, separating|verb

cozying:/ˈkoʊziɪŋ/| v. |奉承；讨好|Becoming friendly or intimate with someone, often for personal gain.|He was cozying up to the boss, hoping for a promotion. 他在讨好老板，希望能升职。|Synonyms: flattering, fawning, buttering up|Antonyms: antagonizing, alienating|verb
