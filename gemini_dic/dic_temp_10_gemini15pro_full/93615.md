imbezzle:/ɪmˈbɛzl/| vt.| 盗用，挪用，贪污 | To secretly and illegally take money that is in your care or that belongs to an organization or business you work for. | He was found guilty of embezzling $10 million from the company. 他被判犯有挪用公司1000万美元的罪行。

The former CEO was charged with embezzling funds from the pension fund.  这位前首席执行官被指控挪用养老基金的资金。

| 近义词：misappropriate, defraud, peculate, steal | 反义词：donate, contribute, reimburse | 词性：动词

Etymology:
From Old French *embesillier "to make away with," from en- "in" + besillier "to destroy, damage" (source also of Old North French besiler "to plunder").
