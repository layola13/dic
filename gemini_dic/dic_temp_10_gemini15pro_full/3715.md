aggressivities:/əˌɡrɛsˈɪvɪtiz/| n. |侵略性，攻击性|The plural form of aggressiveness, referring to multiple instances or types of aggressive behavior.  It describes the tendency to behave in a hostile or forceful way. |His aggressions towards his classmates were cause for concern. 他对同学的攻击性令人担忧。|The rising aggressions in the region worried international observers. 该地区日益增长的侵略性令国际观察员感到担忧。|近义词：hostilities, belligerence, combativeness|反义词：passivity, docility, meekness|词性:Noun
