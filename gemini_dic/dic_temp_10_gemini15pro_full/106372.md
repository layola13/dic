Simple Present:
What do you do for a living? = 你靠什么谋生？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? = 他晚饭在做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned recently? = 你最近学到了什么？
What has she been up to lately? = 她最近在忙什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been reading lately? = 你最近一直在读什么？
What has he been working on all day? = 他一整天都在做什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么事？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? =  她在派对上穿了什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you left? = 你走之前做了什么？
What had they eaten before the movie? = 他们在看电影之前吃了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I called? = 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 她换专业之前一直在学什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们下次会议会讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have finished by the end of the day? = 你到今天结束会完成什么？
What will she have achieved by next year? = 到明年她会取得什么成就？
