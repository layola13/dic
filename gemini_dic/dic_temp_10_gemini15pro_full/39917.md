constrainers:/kənˈstreɪnərz/| n.|限制者，约束者|People or things that limit or restrict something. |Budget constraints are preventing us from hiring more staff. 预算限制使我们无法雇用更多员工。|近义词：restrictions, limitations, inhibitors|反义词:enablers, facilitators|Noun

constrain:/kənˈstreɪn/| v.|限制，约束|To limit or restrict someone or something.|Financial regulations constrain banks' lending ability. 金融法规限制了银行的贷款能力。|近义词：restrict, limit, confine|反义词:liberate, free|Verb

constraint:/kənˈstreɪnt/| n.|限制，约束|A limitation or restriction.|Time constraints forced us to cut the meeting short. 时间限制迫使我们缩短会议时间。|近义词：restriction, limitation, restraint|反义词:freedom, liberty|Noun
