USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
She has a lot of friends. 她有很多朋友。
He has a meeting every Monday. 他每个星期一都有会议。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
She is having a party tonight. 她今晚正在举办一个派对。（have 在此表示“举办”）
He is having a good time. 他玩得很开心。（have 在此表示“经历，拥有”）


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
She has lived in London for five years. 她在伦敦住了五年。
He has just finished his homework. 他刚刚完成了他的家庭作业。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
She has been having trouble sleeping lately. 她最近一直有睡眠问题。（have 在此表示“经历”）
He has been having a conversation with his boss. 他一直在和他的老板谈话。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
She had a headache yesterday. 她昨天头疼。
He had a car when he was younger. 他年轻的时候有一辆车。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
She was having dinner when the phone rang. 电话响的时候她正在吃晚饭。
He was having a dream when he woke up. 他醒来时正在做梦。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
She had already had breakfast before he arrived. 他到达之前她已经吃过早餐了。
He had had a long day at work. 他在工作中度过了漫长的一天。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
She had been having problems with her computer all day. 她一整天都在为她的电脑问题而苦恼。
He had been having an affair for years. 他有外遇已经好几年了。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
She will have a new car next year. 她明年将有一辆新车。
He will have a chance to explain himself. 他将有机会为自己解释。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
She will be having a meeting at 10 am tomorrow. 她明天上午10点将举行会议。（have 在此表示“进行”）
He will be having dinner with his family. 他将和他的家人一起吃晚饭。


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
She will have finished her degree by next summer. 到明年夏天，她将完成她的学位。
He will have traveled all over the world by the time he is 50. 到他50岁时，他将周游世界。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
She will have been having piano lessons for ten years by next year. 到明年，她将已经上钢琴课十年了。
He will have been having therapy for two years by the end of this year. 到今年年底，他将接受治疗两年了。



虚拟语气:
If she had more time, she would have a dog. 如果她有更多时间，她会养一只狗。
I wish I had a million dollars. 我希望我有一百万美元。


被动语态:（have 一般不用于被动语态，除非在某些特定语境下，例如 "A good time was had by all." 大家都玩得很开心。）


疑问句:
Does she have any siblings? 她有兄弟姐妹吗？
Did he have a good time at the party? 他在派对上玩得开心吗？


否定句:
She doesn't have any money. 她没有钱。
He didn't have a car when he was younger. 他年轻时没有车。
