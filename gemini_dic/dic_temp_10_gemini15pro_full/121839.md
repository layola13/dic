misremembrance:/ˌmɪsrɪˈmɛmbrəns/
n.
错误的记忆；记错；误记
An inaccurate or faulty memory of something.
He had a misremembrance of where he parked his car. 他记错了停车的地方。
近义词:false memory, paramnesia, distorted memory
反义词:accurate memory, correct recollection
