flavoured:/ˈfleɪvərd/ (US) /ˈfleɪvəd/ (UK)| adj.|加味的，有味道的|Given a particular taste, especially artificially.|例句：I bought some strawberry-flavoured yogurt. 我买了一些草莓味的酸奶。|近义词：flavored, seasoned, spiced|反义词:unflavored, plain|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *flavouren, from Anglo-Norman flaur, Old French flaur, feminine of *flaur ("fragrance, aroma, smell, perfume"), from Latin flōs (“flower”), from Proto-Italic *flōs, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰleh₃- (“to blossom, bloom”).
