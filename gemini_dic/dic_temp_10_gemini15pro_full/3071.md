aerology:/ˌɛəˈrɒlədʒi/ | n. | 气象学；高空气象学 | The study of the free atmosphere throughout its vertical extent, as distinguished from studies confined to the layer of the atmosphere adjacent to the Earth's surface. | Aerology plays a crucial role in understanding weather patterns.  高空气象学在理解天气模式方面起着至关重要的作用。

The aerology report predicted strong winds at high altitude.  高空气象报告预测高空会有强风。 | 近义词: meteorology, atmospheric science | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps oceanography as a study of a different domain) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek ἀήρ (aḗr, “air”) + -λογία (-logía, “study of”).
