miserlinesses:/ˈmaɪzərlɪnɪsɪz/
n.
吝啬，小气
extreme stinginess; the quality of being unwilling to spend money
His miserlinesses were legendary; he would reuse tea bags for days.  他的吝啬是出了名的；他会把茶包重复使用好几天。
The miserlinesses of the old man shocked his family. 老人的吝啬震惊了他的家人。

近义词: stinginess, parsimony, meanness, tightness
反义词: generosity, liberality, munificence

词性: noun (plural)
词源: From Middle English *miserlinesse, from Old French *miserlinesse, from miser (“wretched”) + -nesse.
