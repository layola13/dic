breastheight: /ˈbrɛstˌhaɪt/ | n. | 胸高 | The standard height at which the diameter of a tree is measured, typically 4.5 feet (1.37 meters) above the ground.  This ensures consistency in measurements across different individuals and locations. |  The DBH (diameter at breast height) of the oak was 24 inches. 这棵橡树的胸径为24英寸。| synonyms: DBH (Diameter at Breast Height) | antonyms: N/A | noun
