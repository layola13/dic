gaber:/ˈɡeɪbər/| n.|闲聊者，饶舌者|A person who talks idly, especially about other people's affairs; a gossip.|例句：He's known as the office gaber, always spreading rumors. 他是办公室里有名的饶舌者，总是散布谣言。

例句：The gabers were huddled in the corner, whispering about the latest scandal.  那些饶舌的人挤在角落里，窃窃私语着最新的丑闻。|近义词：gossip, chatterbox, blabbermouth|反义词:reticent person, quiet person|词性:noun
