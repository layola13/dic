distribution: /ˌdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃən/ (US) /ˌdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃn/ (UK)| n.| 分配，分发；分布，散布；销售；发行|The distribution of food and supplies is crucial after a natural disaster. 自然灾害后，食物和物资的分配至关重要。|例句：The unequal distribution of resources led to social unrest. 资源分配不均导致了社会动荡。|近义词：apportionment, scattering, spreading|反义词：gathering, accumulation|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *distributionem,  from Latin distributus, past participle of distribuere "to distribute," from dis- "apart, asunder" + tribuere "to allot, assign."
