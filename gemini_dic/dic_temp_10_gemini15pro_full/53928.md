discoursive:/dɪˈskɜːsɪv/| adj.|散论式的，推理的，论证的|Proceeding by argument or reasoning rather than intuition.  Characterized by reasoned argument.  Moving from topic to topic without order; rambling.|例句：The professor's lectures were always discursive, covering a wide range of topics. 教授的讲座总是散论式的，涵盖了广泛的主题。

例句：She wrote a discursive essay on the nature of consciousness. 她写了一篇关于意识本质的论证性文章。|近义词：argumentative, reasoned, rambling, digressive, analytical|反义词：concise, succinct, focused, intuitive|词性:adjective
