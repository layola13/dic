oblateness:/əˈbleɪtnəs/
n.
扁率; 扁平; 扁圆形
the quality or state of being oblate; flatness at the poles (as of a planet).
The oblateness of the Earth is caused by its rotation. 地球的扁率是由其自转引起的。
flatness, compression, squashedness
roundness, sphericity
noun
