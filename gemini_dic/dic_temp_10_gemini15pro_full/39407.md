conkers:/ˈkɒŋkəz/ (UK), /ˈkɑːŋkərz/ (US)| n.| 七叶树果实；斗七叶树游戏|A game in which two players try to break each other's horse chestnut fastened to a string by hitting it.|例句：We used to play conkers in the playground at school. 我们以前在学校操场上玩斗七叶树游戏。

例句：He won the conkers championship three years in a row. 他连续三年赢得了斗七叶树游戏冠军。
