consumability:/kənˌsuːməˈbɪləti/| n.|可消耗性；易耗性|The consumability of resources is a key factor in sustainable development. 资源的可消耗性是可持续发展的一个关键因素。|The report assessed the consumability of various food products. 这份报告评估了各种食品的可消耗性。|近义词：expendability, disposability|反义词：durability, permanence|词性:noun
词源:From consume (v.) + -ability.  Ultimately from Latin consumere "to use up, devour, waste."
