conquerless: /ˈkɒŋkələs/ (US) /ˈkɒŋkələs/ (UK)
adjective
不可征服的，未被征服的
Not conquered; unconquerable; unable to be defeated or subdued.
The conquerless spirit of the team led them to victory.  这支球队不可征服的精神带领他们走向了胜利。
The small village, nestled high in the mountains, remained conquerless for centuries.  这个坐落在高山深处的小村庄几个世纪以来一直未被征服。
近义词: invincible, unconquerable, indomitable, unyielding
反义词: conquerable, defeatable, vulnerable
词性: adjective

词源:
From "conquer" + "-less".  "Conquer" comes from Old French "conquerre," from Latin "conquirere"  (to seek together, procure, gain, win). The suffix "-less" is an Old English suffix meaning "without" or "lacking."
