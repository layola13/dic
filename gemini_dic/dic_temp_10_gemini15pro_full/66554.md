exhibiters:/ɪɡˈzɪbɪtərz/
n.
参展者，展出者
People or organizations that display their work or products at an exhibition.
The exhibiters at the trade show ranged from small startups to multinational corporations.
贸易展上的参展商从小型初创企业到跨国公司不等。

近义词: exhibitors, displayers, presenters
反义词: visitors, attendees, audience
词性: Noun


exhibitor:/ɪɡˈzɪbɪtər/
n.
参展者，展出者
A person or company that displays their work or products at an exhibition.
The exhibitor showcased their latest innovations at the consumer electronics show.
参展商在消费电子展上展示了他们的最新创新。

近义词: displayer, presenter
反义词: visitor, attendee
词性: Noun
