inflate:/ɪnˈfleɪt/ (US) /ɪnˈfleɪt/ (UK)| v. |膨胀；使充气；夸大；通货膨胀|To fill with air or gas so that it becomes larger; to increase the size or amount of something, often artificially or to an excessive degree.| The hot air balloon slowly inflated. 热气球慢慢膨胀起来。

The company inflated its profits by manipulating the accounts.  该公司通过操纵账目夸大了利润。|synonyms: expand, enlarge, swell, pump up, amplify, exaggerate |antonyms: deflate, shrink, contract, reduce, diminish|verb
