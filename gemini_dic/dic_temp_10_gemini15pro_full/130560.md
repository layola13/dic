nonacquittal: /ˌnɒnəˈkwɪtəl/
noun
未宣告无罪；不作无罪判决
The nonacquittal led to a retrial.  未宣告无罪导致了重审。
The jury's nonacquittal meant another trial for the defendant. 陪审团没有做出无罪判决，意味着被告将面临另一次审判。
Etymology: non- (not) + acquittal (a judgment that a person is not guilty of the crime with which the person has been charged.)
Synonyms: conviction, condemnation
Antonyms: acquittal, exoneration
词性: 名词
