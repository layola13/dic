achieves: /əˈtʃiːvz/
verb
实现，达到，完成
Successfully brings about or reaches a desired objective or result.
She worked hard and achieved her goal of becoming a doctor. 她努力工作，实现了成为医生的目标。

He finally achieved his dream of climbing Mount Everest. 他终于实现了攀登珠穆朗玛峰的梦想。

近义词: accomplish, attain, reach, fulfill, realize
反义词: fail, forfeit, lose

词性: verb

USAGE->

He achieves his goals through hard work. = 他通过努力工作实现了他的目标。
