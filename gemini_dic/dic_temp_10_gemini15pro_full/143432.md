outfloating:/ˈaʊtˌfloʊtɪŋ/
adjective
外浮的，漂浮在外的
The process of floating or being floated outward. Something that is outfloating is floating on the surface and drifting away.
The outfloating debris from the shipwreck posed a hazard to navigation.  沉船漂浮出来的残骸对航行构成威胁。
近义词:  afloat, drifting
反义词: submerged, sinking
词性: adjective


outfloating:/ˈaʊtˌfloʊtɪŋ/
noun
外浮，漂浮物
The act of floating outward, or the material that is outfloating.
The outfloating of oil from the tanker created a large slick. 油轮漂浮出来的石油形成了一大片浮油。
近义词: flotsam, jetsam
反义词:  sediment
词性: noun
