crocetin:/kroˈsetɪn/ |n.|藏红花素，藏红花酸|A carotenoid dicarboxylic acid, C20H24O4, that is the coloring principle of saffron and the garden crocus.|Saffron is a spice derived from the flower of Crocus sativus, containing crocetin. 藏红花是一种源自番红花花的香料，含有藏红花素。|Crocetin has shown promising results in preclinical studies for its potential health benefits. 藏红花素在临床前研究中显示出其潜在的健康益处。|Synonyms:  crocin (though technically a glycoside of crocetin),  saffron pigment|Antonyms: N/A (Coloring agents don't typically have direct antonyms)|noun
