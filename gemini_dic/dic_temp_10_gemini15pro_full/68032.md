factorising:/ˈfæktəˌraɪzɪŋ/ | n./v. | 因式分解，分解 | The process of finding the factors of a mathematical expression.  | Example: Factorising the expression x² + 5x + 6 gives (x+2)(x+3).  因式分解表达式 x² + 5x + 6 得到 (x+2)(x+3). | Synonyms: factoring, decomposition | Antonyms:  expansion, multiplication | Noun/Verb

factorise:/ˈfæktəˌraɪz/ | v. | 因式分解，分解 | To express a mathematical expression as a product of its factors. | Example: Factorise the quadratic equation.  将二次方程式因式分解。| Synonyms: factor, decompose | Antonyms: expand, multiply | Verb
