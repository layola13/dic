melanuric:/ˌmɛlənˈjʊərɪk/| adj.|黑色素尿的，黑尿的|Relating to or characterized by melanuria, the presence of melanin or dark pigment in the urine.|The patient presented with melanuric symptoms, prompting further investigation for underlying conditions.|Synonyms: dark-pigmented urine, black urine|Antonyms: non-melanuric, normal urine|adjective

melanuria:/ˌmɛlənˈjʊərɪə/| n.|黑色素尿|The presence of melanin or dark pigment in the urine, often a symptom of certain medical conditions.|Melanuria can be a sign of alkaptonuria, a rare genetic disorder.|Synonyms: dark urine, black urine|Antonyms: normal urine|noun
