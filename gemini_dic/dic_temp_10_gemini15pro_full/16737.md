bartends:/bɑːrˈtɛndz/
verb
做酒吧招待；调酒
works as a bartender
例句：He bartends at a local pub. 他在当地一家酒吧做酒保。

例句：She bartends part-time to earn extra money.  她兼职做酒保赚外快。

近义词：mixes drinks, serves drinks, pours drinks
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider perhaps: abstains, refuses service)
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From bar + tend (verb).  "Tend" in this context means to take care of or manage.
