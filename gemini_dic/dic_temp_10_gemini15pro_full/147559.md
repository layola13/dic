overpensivenesses: /ˌoʊvərˈpɛnsɪvnɪsɪz/
| n. |过度沉思，过度忧郁|The state of being excessively pensive or thoughtful, often to the point of melancholy.  A plural form of overpensiveness, referring to multiple instances or occurrences of this state.|Her constant overpensivenesses worried her family. 她持续不断的过度沉思让家人很担心。|Near synonyms: broodings, ruminations, contemplations|Antonyms: lightheartedness, cheerfulness|Noun
