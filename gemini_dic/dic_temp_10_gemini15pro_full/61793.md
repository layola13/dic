emphasize:/ˈɛmfəsaɪz/ (US), /ˈɛmfəsaɪz/ (UK)| vt.|强调，着重|To give special importance or prominence to something in speaking or writing.|The teacher emphasized the importance of doing homework. 老师强调了做作业的重要性。|stress, highlight, underscore|downplay, de-emphasize|verb
emphasize:/ˈɛmfəsaɪz/ (US), /ˈɛmfəsaɪz/ (UK)| n.|强调,重视 (较少用作名词)|The act of giving special importance or prominence to something.|The emphasis was on practical skills. 重点是实践技能。|stress, importance|neglect, disregard|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *emphasizare, from Greek emphasis 'significance, force', from emphainein 'to display, exhibit'.
