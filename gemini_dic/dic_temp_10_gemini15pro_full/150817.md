paraformaldehyde:/ˌpærəfɔːrˈmældɪhaɪd/ | n. |多聚甲醛|A white, powdery solid, (CH2O)n, formed by polymerization of formaldehyde, used as a disinfectant and in making resins.  It depolymerizes to formaldehyde in solution.|Paraformaldehyde is commonly used to fix tissues for microscopy. 多聚甲醛通常用于固定显微镜下的组织。|Paraformaldehyde is a source of formaldehyde for many industrial processes. 多聚甲醛是许多工业过程中甲醛的来源。|Synonyms: polyoxymethylene|Antonyms: None|Noun

paraform:/ˈpærəfɔːrm/ | n. |多聚甲醛 (paraformaldehyde的缩写)|A shortened form of paraformaldehyde.|Paraform is often used as a convenient source of formaldehyde. 多聚甲醛常被用作一种方便的甲醛来源。|Paraform tablets are used for fumigation. 多聚甲醛片剂用于熏蒸消毒。|Synonyms: paraformaldehyde, polyoxymethylene|Antonyms: None|Noun
