epiphytal:/ˌɛpɪˈfaɪtəl/ | adj. | 附生的 | (of a plant) growing on another plant but not parasitic, i.e. not obtaining food from it. |  Many orchids are epiphytal. 很多兰花都是附生的。| epiphytic | parasitic | adjective

epiphytal:/ˌɛpɪˈfaɪtəl/ | adj. | 附生的 | Relating to or characteristic of an epiphyte; growing on the surface of another plant for support but obtaining nourishment independently. |  The bromeliad is an epiphytal plant that gathers water in its leaves.  凤梨科植物是一种附生植物，它的叶子可以收集水分。| epiphytic | terrestrial/grounded | adjective
