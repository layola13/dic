overexerted:/ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzɜːrtɪd/
adjective
过度劳累的；用力过度的
having exerted oneself too much; strained or worn out.
She felt overexerted after the marathon. 她跑完马拉松后感到筋疲力尽。
The horse was overexerted and collapsed. 马过度劳累而倒下了。

近义词: exhausted, fatigued, worn out, strained, overtired, spent
反义词: rested, refreshed, energetic

词性: adjective
词源: From over- + exert. "Overexert" first appeared in the early 19th century.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively," and "exert" comes from the Latin "exertus," meaning "to thrust forth" or "put forth effort."  Therefore, "overexerted" literally means "having put forth too much effort."
