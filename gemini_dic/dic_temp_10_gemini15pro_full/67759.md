eyedrop:/ˈaɪˌdrɒp/ (US) /ˈaɪˌdrɒp/ (UK)| v.|滴眼药水|To apply eyedrops to the eye.|The nurse eyedropped the patient's eyes. 护士给病人的眼睛滴了眼药水。|Synonyms: apply eye drops, administer eye drops|Antonyms: N/A|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
