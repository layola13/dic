oryxes:/ˈɔːrɪksɪz/
n.
oryxes(羚羊的一种)
Plural form of oryx. Large, heavily built antelopes with long, straight or slightly curved horns.
例句：The oryx lowered its horns defensively as the lion approached.  羚羊在狮子靠近时防御性地 lowered its horns defensively as the lion approached. 

例句：Oryxes are well adapted to desert life, able to survive for long periods without water.  羚羊很适应沙漠生活，能够长时间不喝水。

近义词：antelope,gemsbok(南非大羚羊)
反义词:None(无明确的反义词)
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ὄρυξ (óryx, “gazelle, antelope”).
