censusses:/ˈsɛnsəsɪz/
n.
人口普查（复数）
official counts of the population of a place, including information about its people
The government conducts censuses every ten years. 政府每十年进行一次人口普查。
synonyms: counts, surveys, polls
antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "undercounting" or "misrepresentation" could be considered contextually antonymous)
noun


census:/ˈsɛnsəs/
n.
人口普查（单数）
an official count of the population of a place, including information about its people
The latest census shows a decline in the birth rate. 最新的人口普查显示出生率下降。
synonyms: count, survey, poll
antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps "undercounting" or "misrepresentation" could be considered contextually antonymous)
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "census" meaning "register of citizens and property," originally related to "censere" meaning "to assess, estimate, or tax."
