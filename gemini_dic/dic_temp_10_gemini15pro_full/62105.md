enchantment:/ɪnˈtʃæntmənt/ /ɛnˈtʃæntmənt/
n.
魔法; 魅力; 迷惑; 使人高兴的事物
A magical effect or power. The quality of being delightful and captivating. A feeling of great pleasure or delight.
例句：The enchantment of the forest captivated her. 森林的魅力深深地吸引了她。

例句：The wizard cast an enchantment on the sword.  巫师给剑施了魔法。

近义词：magic, spell, charm, fascination, allure, delight, joy, rapture
反义词：disenchantment, disillusionment, boredom, tedium, aversion
词性：名词
