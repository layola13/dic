breakbeat:/ˈbreɪkbiːt/| n.|碎拍；碎拍音乐|A rhythmic drum beat, often sampled from funk or soul recordings, used in hip hop, jungle, and other electronic music genres. It's characterized by its syncopation and heavy use of the snare and kick drums.|The DJ's use of breakbeats had everyone dancing.|Synonyms: drum break, beat, rhythm|Antonyms: melody, harmony|Noun
