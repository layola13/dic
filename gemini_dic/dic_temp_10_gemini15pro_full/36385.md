coinsurance:/ˌkoʊɪnˈʃʊrəns/ /ˌkəʊɪnˈʃʊərəns/| n. |共同保险|A type of insurance in which the insured pays a share of the payment made against a claim.| My health insurance plan has a 20% coinsurance, meaning I pay 20% of the medical bills. 我的健康保险计划有 20% 的共同保险，这意味着我需要支付 20% 的医疗费用。|Example: After reaching her deductible, she was responsible for the coinsurance payment for her surgery.  达到免赔额后，她需要支付手术的共同保险费用。|Synonym: co-pay, cost-sharing|Antonym: full coverage|noun
