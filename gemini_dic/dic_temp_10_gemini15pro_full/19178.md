bewilderingly:/bɪˈwɪldərɪŋli/
adverb
令人困惑地，令人迷惑地
in a bewildering way, so as to confuse someone
The city's streets were bewilderingly complex. 这座城市的街道复杂得令人困惑。
Synonyms: confusingly, perplexingly, bafflingly
Antonyms: clearly, understandably
词性: 副词

bewilder:/bɪˈwɪldər/
verb
使迷惑，使不知所措
cause (someone) to become perplexed and confused.
The sudden change in policy bewildered everyone. 政策的突然改变使每个人都感到困惑。
Synonyms: confuse, perplex, baffle
Antonyms: clarify, enlighten
词性: 动词

bewilderment:/bɪˈwɪldərmənt/
noun
困惑，迷惑
a feeling of being perplexed and confused.
She stared at him in bewilderment. 她困惑地盯着他。
Synonyms: confusion, perplexity, bafflement
Antonyms: clarity, understanding
词性: 名词
