incurrence:/ɪnˈkɜːrəns/ | n. | 招致，发生，承担 |The act of incurring something, such as a debt, a cost, or a penalty.  |  The company reported a significant incurrence of debt in the last quarter.  该公司报告称上季度负债大幅增加。|Synonyms: acquisition, assumption, encountering |Antonyms: avoidance, evasion| noun

incur:/ɪnˈkɜːr/ | v. |招致，引起，遭受| To become subject to something undesirable, such as a debt, a cost, or a penalty. | If you exceed your credit limit, you will incur additional charges. 如果你超过你的信用额度，你将招致额外的费用。 |Synonyms:  obtain, receive, acquire, contract, sustain | Antonyms: avoid, evade, prevent | verb
