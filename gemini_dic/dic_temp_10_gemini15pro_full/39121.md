Confucius: /kənˈfjuːʃəs/ | n. | 孔子 | A Chinese philosopher and politician of the Spring and Autumn period who is traditionally considered the paragon of Chinese sages. | Confucius's teachings emphasized personal and governmental morality, correctness of social relationships, justice, kindness, and sincerity. | 近义词: sage, philosopher | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a person, perhaps "fool" in a very loose sense, but not recommended) | 词性: noun
