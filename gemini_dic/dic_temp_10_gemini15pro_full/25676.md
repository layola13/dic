bunging:/ˈbʌŋɪŋ/
verb
塞住，堵住; (非正式)扔，丢
The act of closing or stopping up something, often casually or haphazardly. Also (informally) means throwing something.
He was bunging old clothes into a suitcase. 他正把旧衣服塞进手提箱里。
He bunged the ball to me. 他把球扔给我。
近义词:stuffing, cramming, throwing, tossing
反义词:removing, extracting, unpacking, catching
词性:v.


bunging:/ˈbʌŋɪŋ/
noun
塞，堵
The action of bunging something.
The bunging of the drain caused a flood. 下水道的堵塞导致了洪水。
近义词:blocking, obstruction
反义词:clearing, unblocking
词性:n.
