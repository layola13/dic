corpus:/ˈkɔːrpəs/ (US) /ˈkɔːpəs/ (UK)| n. |语料库，文集，全集，资料，汇编，尸体|A collection of written or spoken material stored on a computer and used to find out how language is used. A large collection of written or spoken texts, often used for linguistic research.  The body of a dead person or animal.|例句：The researchers compiled a vast corpus of spoken English. 研究人员汇编了大量的英语口语语料库。|例句：The police found the corpus in the woods. 警方在树林里发现了尸体。|近义词：collection, compilation, anthology, text, body|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
