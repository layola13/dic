falling-off:/ˈfɔːlɪŋ ˈɒf/ | n. |减少，下降，衰退|A decrease or decline in something. |The falling-off in attendance at the meetings was noticeable.  会议出席人数的下降很明显。|例句： There has been a falling-off in sales this quarter. 本季度销售额有所下降。|近义词：decline, decrease, downturn, slump|反义词:increase, rise, growth|词性:名词

falling-off:/ˈfɔːlɪŋ ˈɒf/ | n. |脱离，脱落|The act of becoming detached or separated from something. |The falling-off of the plaster revealed the brick underneath. 灰泥脱落露出了下面的砖块。|例句：The falling-off of leaves in autumn is a beautiful sight. 秋天落叶的景象很美。|近义词：detachment, separation|反义词:attachment, adhesion|词性:名词
