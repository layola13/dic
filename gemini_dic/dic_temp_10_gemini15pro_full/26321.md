buying:/ˈbaɪɪŋ/| n.|购买; 采购|The act of purchasing something.  The buying and selling of goods is the essence of trade. 商品的买卖是贸易的本质。|例句：The buying power of the dollar has decreased. 美元的购买力下降了。

例句：Online buying has become increasingly popular.  网上购物越来越流行了。|近义词：purchasing, acquiring, obtaining|反义词：selling, divesting|noun

buying:/ˈbaɪɪŋ/| adj.|用于修饰名词，表示“购买的”或“采购的”|Relating to the act of purchasing.  A buying agent acts on behalf of a purchaser. 采购代理代表买方行事。|例句：The buying public is becoming more discerning. 消费者越来越有鉴别力了。

例句：Buying habits have changed significantly in recent years. 近年来，购买习惯发生了显著变化。|近义词：purchasing|反义词：selling|adjective
