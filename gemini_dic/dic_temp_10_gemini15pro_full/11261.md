archwife:/ˈɑːrtʃwaɪf/
n.
元配；正妻；糟糠之妻
A woman's principal wife, especially when she has more than one.  (Especially used humorously or ironically.)
例句：He introduced the woman as his archwife, much to the amusement of his friends. 他介绍那个女人是他的元配，把他的朋友们逗乐了。

例句：She jokingly referred to herself as the archwife, having been married to him for over 50 years.  她开玩笑地称自己为元配，因为她已经和他结婚50多年了。
近义词：wife, spouse, consort
反义词: concubine, mistress
词性:名词
