akene:/əˈkiːn/| n. |瘦果，单瓣闭果|A small, dry, one-seeded fruit that does not open to release the seed.|例句：The sunflower produces an akene. 向日葵产生瘦果。|例句：The dandelion seed is technically an akene. 蒲公英种子严格来说是瘦果。|近义词：achene, cypsela|反义词: (没有明确的反义词，因为它是一种特定的果实类型)|词性:noun
