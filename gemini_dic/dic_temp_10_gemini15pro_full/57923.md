drily:/ˈdraɪli/| adv.|干燥地；冷淡地；枯燥无味地|In a dry manner; without emotion or interest.  She drily remarked that the weather was typical for November. |例句：He drily observed that the meeting was likely to be long and tedious.  他冷淡地评论说，这次会议可能会冗长乏味。

例句："That's interesting," she said drily, clearly not meaning it. “那很有趣，”她淡淡地说，显然不是真心的。|近义词：sharply, coldly, tersely, curtly, flatly|反义词：warmly, enthusiastically, emotionally, effusively|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From dry (adjective) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  The adjective "dry" ultimately comes from the Proto-Germanic *drauziz.
