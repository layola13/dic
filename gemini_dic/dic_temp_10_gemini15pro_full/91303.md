hydromellonic:/ˌhaɪdrəməˈlɒnɪk/ | adj. |蜂蜜酒的，与蜂蜜酒相关的 | Relating to or characteristic of hydromellon (an archaic term for mead, a fermented honey beverage). |  Ancient texts often mention the hydromellonic traditions of certain cultures. 古代文献经常提到某些文化的蜂蜜酒传统。|  The festival featured various hydromellonic beverages, each with its unique flavor profile.  这个节日以各种蜂蜜酒饮料为特色，每一种都有其独特的风味。 | Near synonyms: mead-like, honey-wine related | Antonyms: None (specific to mead) | adjective

Etymology:
From hydro- (water) + Greek meli, melitos (honey).  The term "hydromellon" is an archaic synonym for "mead," reflecting the beverage's composition of honey and water.
