calmly:/ˈkɑːmli/ (US) /ˈkɑːmli/ (UK)| adv.|平静地；镇定地；沉着地|In a calm and relaxed manner, without agitation or excitement.|She calmly explained the situation to the angry customer. 她平静地向愤怒的顾客解释情况。|He calmly walked towards the exit, ignoring the commotion. 他镇定地走向出口，无视骚乱。|近义词：peacefully, serenely, tranquilly, quietly, composedly|反义词：nervously, anxiously, frantically, hysterically|词性:副词
