misdirecting:/ˌmɪsdɪˈrɛktɪŋ/
verb
误导; 指错方向
leading someone in the wrong direction or giving them wrong information.
The misleading sign misdirected many tourists.  误导性的标志使许多游客迷路了。
misguiding, misleading
guiding, directing
v.
