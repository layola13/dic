beekeeping:/ˈbiːˌkiːpɪŋ/
n.
养蜂；养蜂业
The practice of raising honeybees, especially for honey and beeswax.
例句：Beekeeping is a fascinating hobby. 养蜂是一个迷人的爱好。

例句：He learned beekeeping from his grandfather. 他从他祖父那里学会了养蜂。

近义词：apiculture
反义词：(无直接反义词)
词性: 名词


beekeepings (This word is not standard English.  The correct form is "beekeeping" which is an uncountable noun and does not have a plural form.)


词源:  bee + keeping (from the verb "to keep")
