atonalists:/ˈeɪˈtəʊnəlɪsts/ /əˈtoʊnəlɪsts/
noun
无调性音乐作曲家；无调性音乐支持者
Composers or advocates of atonal music, music that avoids traditional tonality or key.
Arnold Schoenberg was one of the pioneers of atonal music, and his followers became known as the atonalists. 阿诺德·勋伯格是无调性音乐的先驱之一，他的追随者被称为无调性音乐作曲家。
The atonalists sought to break free from the constraints of traditional harmony. 无调性音乐作曲家试图摆脱传统和声的束缚。
Synonyms: twelve-tone composers, serialists
Antonyms: tonal composers, traditionalists
词性:n.
