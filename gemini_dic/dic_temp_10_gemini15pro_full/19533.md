biflorate: /baɪˈflɔːreɪt/ /baɪˈflɔːrət/
| adj. |双花的，有两朵花的|Having two flowers. |This rare orchid is biflorate, producing two blossoms on a single stem.  这种稀有的兰花是双花的，在单茎上开两朵花。|The plant is biflorate.  这种植物有两朵花。|Synonym: two-flowered|Antonym: uniflorate|adjective

biflorous: /baɪˈflɔːrəs/ /baɪˈflɔːrəs/
| adj. |双花的，有两朵花的|Having two flowers. |The biflorous stem produced a pair of delicate white blooms. 双花的茎上开出一对精致的白花。|I noticed a biflorous orchid in the greenhouse. 我在温室里注意到一株双花的兰花。|Synonym: two-flowered|Antonym: uniflorate|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bi- (two) + *floratus (flowered), past participle of *florare (to flower).
