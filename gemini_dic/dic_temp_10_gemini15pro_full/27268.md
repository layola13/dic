calquing:/ˈkɔːlkɪŋ/ | n. | 翻译借用;仿造;描摹 | The process of forming a new word or phrase by literally translating the parts of an existing word or phrase from another language. |  The English word "skyscraper" was calqued from the German "Wolkenkratzer".  英语单词“skyscraper”（摩天大楼）是从德语单词“Wolkenkratzer”仿造而来。

Calquing can sometimes lead to awkward or unnatural-sounding expressions.  翻译借用有时会导致表达笨拙或不自然。 | 近义词: loan translation, borrowing | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps neologism - creating a completely new word) | 词性: noun
