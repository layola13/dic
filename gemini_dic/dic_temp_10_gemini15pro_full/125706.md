mongoose:/ˈmʌŋɡuːs/ (US), /ˈmɒŋɡuːs/ (UK)| n. |猫鼬，獴|A small carnivorous mammal with a long body and tail, native to Africa and Asia.  It is noted for its ability to kill venomous snakes. |The mongoose quickly darted and killed the cobra. 猫鼬快速冲刺并杀死了眼镜蛇。|  A mongoose is known for its bravery in facing venomous snakes. 猫鼬以其面对毒蛇的勇气而闻名。|近义词:meerkat, ichneumon|反义词: (No direct antonym exists for an animal. Could consider "prey" in the context of its relationship with snakes)|noun
