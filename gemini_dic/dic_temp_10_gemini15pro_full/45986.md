cyanosis:/ˌsaɪəˈnəʊsɪs/ | n. |紫绀，发绀|A bluish discoloration of the skin and mucous membranes resulting from inadequate oxygenation of the blood.| The patient presented with cyanosis of the lips and fingertips. 病人的嘴唇和指尖出现紫绀。|Synonyms: blueness, discoloration|Antonyms: ruddiness, flush|noun

Etymology: From Ancient Greek  κυάνωσις (kuánōsis, “dark-blue coloring”), from κυανόω (kuanóō, “to make dark blue”), from κύανος (kúanos, “dark blue enamel”).
