non-regression:/ˌnɒn rɪˈɡrɛʃən/ | n. | 非回归 | The absence of regression; the state of not returning to a previous, usually worse, state.  |  The software team performed non-regression tests to ensure the new update didn't break existing functionalities.  软件团队执行了非回归测试，以确保新的更新不会破坏现有的功能。 | synonyms: stability, maintenance,  continuation | antonyms: regression, relapse, deterioration | noun

non-regression:/ˌnɒn rɪˈɡrɛʃən/ | adj. | 非回归的 |  Relating to or involving the prevention of regression.  |  They implemented a non-regression strategy to maintain the quality of their product. 他们实施了非回归策略来维护其产品的质量。 | synonyms: preventative, protective, stabilizing | antonyms: regressive, deteriorating, relapsing | adjective
