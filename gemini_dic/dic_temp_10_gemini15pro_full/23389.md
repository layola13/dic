brachycephalism: /ˌbrækiˈsɛfəlɪzəm/ | n. | 短头畸形 | A condition characterized by a disproportionately short head, broad face, and flattened nose.  It can occur in humans and other animals. |  Brachycephalism is common in certain dog breeds like pugs and bulldogs. 短头畸形在某些犬种中很常见，例如哈巴狗和斗牛犬。|  Brachycephaly | Dolichocephalism | noun
