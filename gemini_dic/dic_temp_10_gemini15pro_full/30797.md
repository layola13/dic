cetologist:/siːˈtɒlədʒɪst/ /ˌsiːtɑːˈlɑːdʒɪst/| n.|鲸类学家|A scientist who studies whales, dolphins, and porpoises.|例句：The cetologist spent years studying the communication patterns of humpback whales.  这位鲸类学家花了数年时间研究座头鲸的交流模式。

例句：The cetologist tagged the whale to track its migration patterns.  这位鲸类学家给鲸鱼做了标记以追踪它的迁徙模式。|近义词：marine biologist, whale scientist|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
