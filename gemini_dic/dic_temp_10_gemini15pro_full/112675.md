lowfat:/ˈloʊfæt/ (American) /ˈləʊfæt/ (British)| adj.|低脂的|Containing a reduced amount of fat.  Designed to help people reduce their fat intake.|例句：She switched to a lowfat diet to improve her health. 她为了改善健康，改用了低脂饮食。

例句：Lowfat milk is a good alternative for those watching their calorie intake.  低脂牛奶对于那些关注卡路里摄入量的人来说是一个不错的选择。|近义词：light, reduced-fat|反义词：full-fat, high-fat|词性:adjective

词源:  A compound word formed from "low" and "fat." "Low" comes from Old English "lah," meaning "not high" or "short." "Fat" comes from Old English "fætt," related to German "fett."
