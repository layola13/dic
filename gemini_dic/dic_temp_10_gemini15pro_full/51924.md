dia:/ˈdaɪə/| n. |直径; (解剖学)横膈膜|A straight line passing from side to side through the center of a body or figure, especially a circle or sphere.  (Anatomy) The muscle that separates the chest from the abdomen in mammals.|The dia of the circle is 10cm.  这个圆的直径是10厘米。 The diaphragm helps us breathe. 横膈膜帮助我们呼吸。|近义词：diameter (for diameter sense), midriff (informal for diaphragm)|反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性:noun
