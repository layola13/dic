outcreeping: /aʊtˈkriːpɪŋ/
Verb
缓慢蔓延; 逐渐扩展
Growing or extending outward, especially slowly and stealthily.
The ivy was outcreeping from the garden onto the wall. 常春藤从花园里蔓延到墙上。
近义词: spreading, extending, creeping, sprawling
反义词: receding, shrinking, contracting
词性: v.

Etymology:
"out-" (prefix indicating outward motion or extension) + "creep" (verb meaning to move slowly and quietly)
