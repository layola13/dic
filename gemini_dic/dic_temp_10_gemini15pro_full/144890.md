over-seed:/ˌoʊvərˈsiːd/
verb
覆播，补播
To sow seed over an existing lawn or pasture to thicken or improve it.
He decided to over-seed the lawn in the fall to improve its density. 他决定在秋天覆播草坪以提高其密度。
The farmer over-seeded the pasture with ryegrass to provide winter forage for his cattle.  农场主在牧场上补播黑麦草，为他的牛提供冬季草料。
Synonyms: reseed, oversow
Antonyms: N/A
词性: 动词
