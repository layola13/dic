fuzzing:/ˈfʌzɪŋ/| n.|模糊测试|The act of providing invalid, unexpected, or random data as input to a computer program to identify vulnerabilities.|Software developers use fuzzing to find bugs in their code. 软件开发者使用模糊测试来查找代码中的错误。|近义词：random testing, vulnerability scanning|反义词:directed testing|词性:名词
