paseng: /ˈpɑːsɛŋ/ | n. |通行证，护照，许可证| A document or token that allows someone to pass or travel through a place, especially one that is restricted or requires permission. |  Example:  He showed his paseng to the guard and entered the building. 他向警卫出示了通行证，然后进入了大楼。

Example: She needed a paseng to cross the border. 她需要通行证才能过境。| Synonym: permit, pass, license, authorization | Antonym: restriction, prohibition | Noun
