nonabandonments:/ˌnɑːnəˈbændənmənts/
n.
不放弃，不抛弃
The act or policy of not abandoning something or someone.
The government's policy of nonabandonment ensured that the elderly received the care they needed. 政府的不放弃政策确保了老年人得到所需的照顾。
persistence, continuance, maintenance
abandonment, desertion, relinquishment
noun
