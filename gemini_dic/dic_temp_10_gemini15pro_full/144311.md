outspread:/ˌaʊtˈspred/ /ˌaʊtˈsprɛd/| v. (outspreads, outspreading, outspread)|伸展，扩展；张开|To extend, expand, or spread open.|
He outspread his map on the table. = 他把地图摊在桌子上。
The city outspread before them. = 城市在他们面前展开。
|近义词：extend, expand, unfold, unfurl|反义词：fold, contract, close|词性:动词


词源: From out- + spread.  "Out-" intensifies the meaning of "spread", implying a full or complete spreading.
