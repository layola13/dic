fistulate:/ˈfɪstʃəˌleɪt/ /ˈfɪstjʊˌleɪt/| v. adj.|形成瘘管; 瘘管的|To make or become fistulous; to develop a fistula.  Referring to something that has become fistulous.|The surgeon had to fistulate the abscess to allow it to drain.  外科医生不得不将脓肿瘘管化以使其排出。|The fistulate tract allowed the infection to spread. 瘘管使感染扩散。|近义词：canalize,  perforate|反义词: heal, close|词性: 动词, 形容词


fistula: /ˈfɪstʃələ/ /ˈfɪstjʊlə/ | n. |瘘管|An abnormal passage or opening between two organs or between an organ and the surface of the body.|The patient developed a fistula between the bladder and the vagina. 病人膀胱和阴道之间形成了瘘管。|Doctors discovered a fistula in his intestines. 医生在他的肠道里发现了一个瘘管。|近义词： sinus, tract|反义词: None|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *fistulāre, from Latin fistula (“pipe, tube, reed, flute”).
