overgrievousnesses: /ˌoʊvərˈɡriːvəsnɪsɪz/ | n. | 过度悲伤，过度痛苦 |  Excessive or extreme states of grief or sorrow.  This word emphasizes an excessive or unnecessary level of grieving.  It suggests that the sorrow is disproportionate to the situation or prolonged beyond what is considered normal or healthy. |  The overgrievousnesses of the mourners seemed performative rather than genuine. 送葬者的过度悲伤看起来更像是表演而不是真的。 | synonyms: excessive sorrow, undue grief, immoderate sadness | antonyms: resilience, fortitude, acceptance | noun
