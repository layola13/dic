ambrein:/ˈæmbreɪn/ | n. |  龙涎香醇 | A triterpene alcohol that is the main constituent of ambergris and is thought to be derived from the bile of the sperm whale. It is used in perfumery as a fixative. | Ambergris, highly valued in perfumery, contains ambrein. 龙涎香在香水制造业中备受推崇，它含有龙涎香醇。|  Synonym: None (as it's a specific chemical compound) | Antonym: None | noun
