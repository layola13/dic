debarring:/dɪˈbɑːrɪŋ/
verb
禁止，阻止
The act of officially preventing someone from doing something, especially from entering a place or taking part in an activity.
The committee recommended debarring him from holding public office. 委员会建议禁止他担任公职。
synonyms:  prohibiting, preventing, excluding, barring, precluding
antonyms: permitting, allowing, admitting, including
verb
