canai:/ˈtʃɑːnaɪ/ /tʃəˈnaɪ/| n.|印度煎饼, 油炸面饼|A type of Indian flatbread, often served with curry. It is made from dough that is stretched and folded repeatedly before being pan-fried.|例句：I love to eat canai for breakfast. 我喜欢早餐吃印度煎饼。

例句：The canai was crispy and flaky. 这印度煎饼酥脆可口。|近义词：roti, paratha, flatbread|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "thick bread")|词性:noun
