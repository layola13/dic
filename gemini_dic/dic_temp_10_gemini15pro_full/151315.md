paripinnate:/ˌpærɪˈpɪneɪt/| adj.|偶数羽状的|Having leaflets arranged in pairs along the rachis (the central stalk of a compound leaf) and lacking a terminal leaflet.|例句：The leaves of the honey locust tree are paripinnate. 刺槐树的叶子是偶数羽状的。

例句：Many legumes have paripinnate leaves. 许多豆科植物有偶数羽状的叶子。|近义词：even-pinnate|反义词:imparipinnate|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *pari- (equal) + pinnatus (feathered, winged), from pinna (feather, wing).
