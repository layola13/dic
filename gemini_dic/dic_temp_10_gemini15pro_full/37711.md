comparablenesses:/kəmˈpærəb(ə)lnəsɪz/
noun
可比性；相似性
The quality or state of being comparable.  The degree to which things are similar or can be compared.
The comparablenesses between the two studies are striking. 这两项研究的可比性非常显著。
synonyms: similarities, resemblances, parallels, analogies
antonyms: differences, dissimilarities, disparities
