humanizer:/ˈhjuːmənaɪzər/| n.|人性化者；人道主义者；使更人道的东西|Something that humanizes.  It makes something more humane or relatable to humans.|The author used humor as a humanizer to make the complex scientific concepts more accessible to the general public.  作者使用幽默作为人性化的方式，使复杂的科学概念更容易被大众理解。|近义词：humanitarian, philanthropist|反义词:brute, monster|noun
