anteport:/ˈæntiˌpɔːrt/
n.
外港; 前港; 外门
An outer port or harbor; an outport. Also, an outer gate or door.
Ships were required to stop at the anteport for inspection before entering the main harbor. 船只被要求在进入主港前在外港停靠接受检查。
The anteport was heavily fortified to protect the city from naval attacks. 外港被重重设防，以保护城市免受海军攻击。
synonym: outport, outer harbor, outer gate
antonym: inner harbor, inner gate
noun
