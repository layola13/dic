divi:/ˈdɪviː/ |n.|股息; 红利; 股利|A sum of money paid regularly (typically annually) by a company to its shareholders out of its profits.|The company announced a divi of $2 per share. 公司宣布每股派息2美元。|Synonyms: dividend, payout|Antonyms: loss, debt|noun
