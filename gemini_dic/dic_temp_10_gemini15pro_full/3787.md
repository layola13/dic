aglow:/əˈɡloʊ/
adjective
发光的，通红的
lit up with a soft light; glowing.
The embers were still aglow in the fireplace. 壁炉里的余烬仍在发光。
近义词:glowing, luminous, radiant, shining
反义词:dark, dim, dull, unlit
词性:adjective

词源:a- (prefix meaning "on," "in," "at") + glow (noun meaning "steady light")
