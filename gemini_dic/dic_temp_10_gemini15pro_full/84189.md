half-reasonable:/ˌhæf ˈriːzənəbl/ | adj. | 尚可的，勉强说得过去的 | Partially or moderately reasonable; not entirely unreasonable, but also not fully satisfactory. |  The food at the restaurant was half-reasonable, but nothing special. 这家餐馆的食物还可以，但没什么特别的。

The price was half-reasonable, considering the location. 考虑到位置，这个价格还算合理。 | 近义词:tolerable, passable, adequate | 反义词:unreasonable, absurd, ridiculous | 词性:形容词

Etymology:
"half-" (Old English *healf, from Proto-Germanic *halbaz) + "reasonable" (from Old French raisonable, from Latin rationabilis, from ratio "reason")
