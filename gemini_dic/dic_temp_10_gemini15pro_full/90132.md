hotbed:/ˈhɑːtbed/ | n. |温床；滋生地；热烈讨论的场所 |A place or situation that favors rapid growth or development of something, especially something unwelcome. |Silicon Valley is a hotbed of innovation. 硅谷是创新的温床。

The Internet has become a hotbed of misinformation. 互联网已经成为错误信息的滋生地。| 近义词：breeding ground, seedbed, cradle, center, hub | 反义词：dead zone, wasteland | 词性: 名词
