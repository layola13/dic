noght:/nɔːxt/ | archaic form of "naught"| 无，零，没事 |  This word is an archaic form of "naught," meaning nothing or zero. It is rarely used in modern English. |  He gave me noght for my troubles. 他对我的麻烦不予理睬。 | 近义词: nothing, zero, nil | 反义词: something, anything | 词性: pronoun, noun, adverb

词源: From Middle English *noght, *naught, from Old English nāwiht, nōwiht (“nothing, not a whit, naught”), contraction of nāwiht, nōwiht (“no thing”). Equivalent to ne (“not”) + āwiht, ōwiht (“aught, anything”).
