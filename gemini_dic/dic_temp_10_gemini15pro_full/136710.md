nonselections:/ˌnɒnsɪˈlɛkʃənz/
noun
未选中的人或物；落选者；淘汰品
The nonselections were disappointed, but took it in stride.  落选者很失望，但还是泰然处之。
The nonselections from the art competition were sold at a discount.  艺术比赛落选的作品打折出售。
synonyms: rejects, discards, omissions, exclusions
antonyms: selections, choices, picks, inclusions
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + selection.  Non- is a prefix meaning "not," and selection refers to the act of choosing something.  Therefore, nonselection refers to the things or people that were *not* chosen.
