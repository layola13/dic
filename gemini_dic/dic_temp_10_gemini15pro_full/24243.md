bridling:/ˈbraɪdlɪŋ/ | n. | 控制；抑制；不服 | The act of restraining or controlling, often with a sense of resentment or indignation. |  Her bridling at his criticism was evident in her tightened jaw. 她对他的批评感到不满，从她紧绷的下巴就能看出来。| synonyms: restraining, curbing, checking, reining in | antonyms: indulging, releasing, unleashing | noun

bridling:/ˈbraɪdlɪŋ/ | adj. |  表示不满的；抑制的 | Showing resentment or indignation, especially when being controlled or restrained. |  She gave him a bridling look. 她给了他一个不满的眼神。 | synonyms: resentful, indignant, disapproving, offended | antonyms: accepting, submissive, compliant | adjective
