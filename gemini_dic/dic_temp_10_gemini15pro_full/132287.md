nondefensibleness:/ˌnɒndɪˈfɛnsɪbəlness/ | n. | 不可辩护性，无力辩护 |  The nondefensibleness of his actions led to his resignation. 他的行为无力辩护，导致他辞职。| Example: The nondefensibleness of his position was clear to everyone. 他的立场站不住脚，这一点大家都清楚。 | Synonyms: indefensibility, unjustifiability, inexcusability | Antonyms: defensibility, justifiability, excusability | noun

Etymology:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + defensible + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives)
From "defensible," which comes from Old French "defensible" and ultimately from Latin "defendere" (to defend).
