connoting:/kəˈnoʊtɪŋ/ | v. | 暗示，意味着 |  Suggesting or implying an association or secondary meaning in addition to the primary meaning. | The word "home" connotes warmth and comfort. “家”这个词意味着温暖和舒适。| Synonyms: implying, suggesting, intimating | Antonyms: denoting, stating explicitly | Verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *connotare, from Latin com- 'together' + notare 'to mark'.
