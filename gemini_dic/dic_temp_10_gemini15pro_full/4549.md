alcedo:/ælˈsiːdoʊ/ /ælˈsedoʊ/ | n. |翠鸟|A small, brightly coloured bird of the family Alcedinidae, found near water and known for its swift dives to catch fish. |例句：The alcedo flashed a brilliant blue as it dived into the stream.  翠鸟俯冲入溪流时，闪现出耀眼的蓝色。

例句：Alcedoes are renowned for their fishing prowess. 翠鸟以其捕鱼能力而闻名。 |近义词：kingfisher |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *alcedo, alcedinis*, ultimately of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Greek *ἀλκυών (alkyōn)* "kingfisher," perhaps from a pre-Greek substrate.
