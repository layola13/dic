encage:/ɪnˈkeɪdʒ/
verb
关入笼中；囚禁
To confine or imprison, especially in a cage.
The zookeepers encaged the lion after its performance. 动物园管理员在狮子表演结束后把它关进了笼子。
The feeling of being trapped encaged her spirit. 被困住的感觉禁锢了她的精神。
confine, imprison, trap, restrict
liberate, free, release
