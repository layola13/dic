cuckoldly: /ˈkʌkəldli/
adjective
戴绿帽子的，妻子有外遇的
(of a man) having a wife who commits adultery.
例句：He felt cuckoldly and humiliated after discovering his wife's affair.  他发现妻子有外遇后感到自己被戴了绿帽子，倍感羞辱。

例句：The play explored the cuckoldly husband's descent into madness.  这出戏探讨了被戴绿帽的丈夫如何陷入疯狂。

近义词：dishonored, cheated on, betrayed
反义词：faithful, loyal
词性：adjective
