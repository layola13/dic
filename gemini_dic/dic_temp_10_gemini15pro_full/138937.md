nutshell:/ˈnʌtʃɛl/ | n. |坚果壳；极小的空间；简而言之|a hard outer covering of a nut; a very small space; in a few words|The explanation was complex, but she managed to put it in a nutshell for us.  这个解释很复杂，但她设法将其简要地概括给我们。|synonyms: summary, gist, epitome, abstract | antonyms: elaboration, expansion, detail| noun

nutshells:/ˈnʌtʃɛlz/ | n. |坚果壳（复数）；极小的空间（复数）|plural form of nutshell|  He explained the complex theory in a few nutshells. 他用几句话简要地解释了这个复杂的理论。|synonyms: summaries, gists, epitomes, abstracts | antonyms: elaborations, expansions, details| noun
