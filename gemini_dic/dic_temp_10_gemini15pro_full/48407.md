dedicator:/ˈdedɪkeɪtər/| n. |奉献者，献词者|A person who dedicates something, especially a book or a building, to someone or something.|The dedicator of the new library was a local philanthropist.  这位新图书馆的捐赠者是一位当地的慈善家。|Synonyms: donor, contributor, patron|Antonyms: recipient, receiver|noun


dedicator:/ˈdedɪkeɪtər/| n. |题献者|Someone who inscribes a dedication in a book or other work.|He was the dedicator of the poem to his late wife. 他将这首诗献给了他已故的妻子。|Synonyms: inscriber|Antonyms: N/A|noun
