equi-signal:/ˈiːkwɪˌsɪɡnəl/
adjective
等信号的；等强度的
Characterized by equality of signal strength.  Relating to or denoting a condition in which two or more signals have the same strength or intensity.
The equi-signal zone is the area where the signals from two transmitters are of equal strength. 等信号区是两个发射机信号强度相等的区域。
The pilot followed the equi-signal beacon to locate the runway. 飞行员跟随等信号信标找到跑道。
近义词: isogonal, co-channel
反义词: differential-signal
词性: adjective
