encoffin:/ɪnˈkɒfɪn/ /ɛnˈkɔːfɪn/
verb
装入棺材，埋葬
To place (a corpse) in a coffin.
The undertaker encoffined the body. 殡葬人员将尸体装入棺材。
近义词: bury, inter
反义词: exhume, disinter
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From en- + coffin.  "Coffin" comes from Old French cofin, ultimately from Greek kophinos, meaning "basket."
