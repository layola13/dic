animes: /ˈænɪmeɪz/
n.
动画片（尤指日本动画）
Japanese animated productions, often characterized by stylized colorful graphics, fantastic themes, and dynamic action.
My favorite animes include Attack on Titan and My Hero Academia. 我最喜欢的动画片包括《进击的巨人》和《我的英雄学院》。
近义词: cartoons, animation
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "live-action")
