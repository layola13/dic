巨大的，庞大的
Exceptionally large; huge.
The museum houses a gigantean skeleton of a blue whale.  博物馆里收藏着一具巨大的蓝鲸骨架。

近义词: enormous, colossal, immense, massive, huge, gigantic
反义词: tiny, minuscule, small, diminutive

词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *giganteus, from gigas (“giant”).
