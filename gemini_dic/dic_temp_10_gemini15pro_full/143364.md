outfaming: /aʊtˈfeɪmɪŋ/
verb
超越…的名声; 使…的名声相形见绌
Surpassing someone in fame or reputation.
She outfamed her siblings with her groundbreaking research. 她凭借其突破性的研究超越了兄弟姐妹的名声。
The new star quickly outfamed her mentor.  这位新星迅速使她的导师黯然失色。
近义词: eclipsing, surpassing, outshining
反义词: overshadowed, eclipsed
词性: 动词 (verb)
