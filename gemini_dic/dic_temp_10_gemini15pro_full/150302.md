paneulogism:/ˌpænjuːˈlɒdʒɪzəm/
n.
泛赞, 泛美之辞
A panegyric; extravagant praise or eulogy.
He delivered a paneulogism at the funeral, praising the deceased's virtues. 他在葬礼上发表了泛赞之辞，赞扬了死者的美德。
近义词: eulogy, encomium, panegyric, tribute
反义词: criticism, condemnation, denunciation
词性: noun
