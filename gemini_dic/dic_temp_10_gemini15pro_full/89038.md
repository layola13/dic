holmos:/ˈhɒlmɒs/ (UK) /ˈhoʊlmoʊs/ (US)| n.|小岛；河中小岛|A small island, especially in a river or lake. |例句：The boat drifted towards a secluded holmos in the river.  船漂向河中一个僻静的小岛。

例句：Several species of birds nested on the holmos.  几种鸟类在小岛上筑巢。|近义词：islet, ait, eyot |反义词:mainland|词性:名词

词源: From Old Norse *holmr "small island," related to Old English holm.
