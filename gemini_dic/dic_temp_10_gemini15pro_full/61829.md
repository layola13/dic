empiristic:/ɪmˈpɪrɪsɪstɪk/ |adj.|经验主义的；经验论的|Based on, concerned with, or verifiable by observation or experience rather than theory or pure logic.| His approach to medicine is deeply empiristic, relying heavily on observed patient outcomes. 他对医学的方法是深入的经验主义，非常依赖观察到的病人结果。|empirical, observational, experiential, pragmatic|theoretical, speculative, abstract, a priori|adjective
