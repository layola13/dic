chati:/ˈtʃɑːtiː/
noun
聊天; 闲聊; 聊天工具
A casual conversation; an informal talk. Also refers to a platform or tool used for online conversation.
We had a long chati about our plans for the summer. 我们聊了很久关于暑假的计划。
synonyms: chat, conversation, talk, gossip
antonyms: silence, quiet
词性: 名词

chati:/ˈtʃɑːtiː/
verb
聊天; 闲聊
To engage in a casual conversation; to talk informally.
They chatied for hours on the phone. 他们在电话里聊了几个小时。
synonyms: chat, converse, talk, gossip
antonyms: be silent, be quiet
词性: 动词


词源:  Likely a modern coinage, potentially influenced by "chat" and possibly with a playful or informal suffix added.  No definitive etymology is readily available.
