imperforation:/ɪmˌpɜːrfəˈreɪʃn/
n.
未穿孔，闭锁
The absence of a normal opening, especially in a membrane or partition of the body.
The baby was born with an imperforation of the anus.  这个婴儿出生时肛门闭锁。
Synonyms: atresia, occlusion, blockage, closure
Antonyms: perforation, opening, patency
词性: 名词
