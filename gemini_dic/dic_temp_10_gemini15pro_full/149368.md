pacifical: /pəˈsɪfɪkəl/
adjective
爱好和平的; 和平主义的
Relating to or characteristic of pacifism or pacifists; peaceful in intent or effect.
The Dalai Lama made a pacifical speech, urging for non-violent solutions.  达赖喇嘛发表了和平主义的演讲，呼吁以非暴力方式解决问题。

The organization promotes a pacifical approach to international conflicts.  该组织提倡以和平方式解决国际冲突。
Synonym: peaceable, peaceful, dovish, nonviolent, anti-war
Antonym:  bellicose, belligerent, hawkish, warmongering, violent
词性: adjective
