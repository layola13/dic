bastes:/beɪsts/| v.| 浇油(烤肉时); 痛打; 粗缝; 严厉批评| to moisten (roasting meat) with melted fat, butter, etc.; to sew with long, loose stitches; to beat severely; to criticize harshly|  She bastes the turkey every half hour. 她每半小时给火鸡浇一次油。|Synonyms: moisten, douse, sew, tack, thrash, lambaste |Antonyms: dry,  praise, commend|verb

bastes:/beɪsts/| n.| 粗缝; 浇油; 痛打| act of basting; long, loose stitches used to hold fabric temporarily; the melted fat, etc. used for basting| The bastes held the seams together until she could sew them properly. 粗缝把接缝固定在一起，直到她能把它们缝好。|Synonyms: stitching, tacking, beating, thrashing|Antonyms: N/A|noun
