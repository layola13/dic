licencees:/ˌlaɪsənˈsiːz/
n.
被许可人，获得许可的人
A person or company that has been granted a licence.
The licensees of the pub were required to comply with strict regulations. 这家酒吧的被许可人必须遵守严格的规定。

licensees:/ˌlaɪsənˈsiːz/
n.
被授予许可证者
Individuals or entities granted a license for a specific activity.
All licensees are required to display their licenses prominently. 所有被授予许可证者都必须将他们的许可证放在显眼位置。

Synonyms: permit holders, franchisees
Antonyms: licensors, grantors
