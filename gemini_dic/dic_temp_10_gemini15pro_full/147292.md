overmodernizing:/ˌoʊvərˈmɒdərnˌaɪzɪŋ/ /ˌoʊvərˈmɑːdərˌnaɪzɪŋ/
Verb
过度现代化
Modernizing something excessively or unnecessarily, often to the detriment of traditional values or practices.
The architect was accused of overmodernizing the historic building, stripping it of its original charm.  这位建筑师被指责过度现代化这座历史建筑，剥夺了它原有的魅力。
He felt the town was overmodernizing, losing its unique character in the pursuit of progress. 他觉得这个小镇过度现代化了，在追求进步的过程中失去了它独特的个性。
Synonyms: overdeveloping, excessively modernizing
Antonyms: preserving, conserving
词性: 动词
