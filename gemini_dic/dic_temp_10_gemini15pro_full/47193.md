daydreamed:/ˈdeɪdriːmd/| v.|做白日梦，幻想|indulged in a daydream; fantasized.  The act of having pleasant, dreamy thoughts while awake, often about things that are unlikely to happen.|He daydreamed about winning the lottery. 他幻想着中了彩票。|Synonyms: fantasized, mused, imagined, envisioned|Antonyms: focused, concentrated|verb

daydream:/ˈdeɪdriːm/| n. v.|白日梦，幻想；做白日梦|a series of pleasant thoughts that distract one's attention from the present; to have such thoughts.|Her daydream of becoming a famous singer kept her motivated.  她成为著名歌手的白日梦一直激励着她。|Synonyms: fantasy, reverie, fancy|Antonyms: reality, actuality|noun, verb
