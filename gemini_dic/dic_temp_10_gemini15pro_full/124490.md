mouldily:/ˈməʊldɪli/ /ˈmoʊldɪli/| adv.|发霉地；陈腐地|Covered with or smelling of mould; musty.  Characterized by or suggestive of mould; stale and antiquated.|The books smelled mouldily after being stored in the damp basement. 这些书在地下室潮湿的环境中存放后，散发着霉味。|近义词：musty, fusty, mildewy|反义词:fresh, clean|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From mouldy + -ly.  Mouldy comes from Middle English *mowledy, from Old English *molig, from mould.
