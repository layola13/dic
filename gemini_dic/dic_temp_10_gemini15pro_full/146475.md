overfunctioning:/ˌoʊvərˈfʌŋkʃənɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度运作的；过度负责的 | Taking on too much responsibility and doing more than one's share, often to compensate for others' perceived inadequacies.  It describes someone who takes on excessive responsibilities, often in relationships or family dynamics, to the detriment of their own well-being. |  She's overfunctioning in this project, doing the work of three people.  她在这个项目中过度运作，承担了三个人的工作。 |  He was overfunctioning in the family, always picking up the slack for his less responsible siblings.  他在家庭中过度负责，总是弥补他不负责任的兄弟姐妹的不足。 | 近义词: hyper-responsible, controlling, overbearing | 反义词: underfunctioning, irresponsible, passive | 词性: adjective

overfunction:/ˌoʊvərˈfʌŋkʃən/ | v. | 过度运作；过度负责 | To take on too much responsibility and do more than one's share. | She tends to overfunction in relationships, trying to fix everyone's problems. 她倾向于在人际关系中过度负责，试图解决所有人的问题。 |  He overfunctioned at work, taking on extra tasks even when he was already overwhelmed. 他在工作中过度运作，即使他已经不堪重负，仍然承担额外的任务。| 近义词: overwork, overdo, overextend | 反义词: underperform, neglect, shirk | 词性: verb
