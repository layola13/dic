gouts:/ɡaʊts/| n. |痛风发作; 一阵突发(尤指愤怒或痛楚)|A sudden outburst or attack, especially of anger or pain.  Derived from the painful attacks associated with gout, the disease.|He suffered gouts of rage. 他一阵阵暴怒。|近义词：attacks, fits, outbursts, spasms|反义词:calm, peace|词性:名词

词源: From Old French *gote, from goutte (“drop”), from Late Latin gutta (“drop”), of uncertain origin, perhaps imitative.  The connection to gout (the disease) is due to the medieval humoral theory that saw the disease as caused by drops of morbid matter in the joints.
