foresails:/ˈfɔːrseɪlz/ | n. | 前帆；船首三角帆 | The foresail is the lowest sail set on the foremast of a square-rigged vessel.  前帆是设置在横帆船前桅杆上的最低的帆。| The strong wind filled the foresail, propelling the ship forward.  强风鼓满了前帆，推动着船前进。 | synonyms: jib, staysail | antonyms: mainsail, mizzen | noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *foresayl, from fore- + sail.  来自中古英语 *foresayl，由 fore- + sail 组成。
