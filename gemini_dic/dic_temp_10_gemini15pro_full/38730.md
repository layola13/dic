conditioned:/kənˈdɪʃənd/ | adj. |受制约的，受条件限制的；习惯于…的，适应…的；在特定条件下的 |Influenced or determined by a particular factor or set of factors; accustomed to something, especially a particular lifestyle or situation; existing or occurring under specified conditions.|  The athlete's performance was conditioned by years of training.  这位运动员的表现得益于多年的训练。| Synonyms: trained, adapted, accustomed, habituated, inured, acclimatized | Antonyms: unaccustomed, unadapted, unprepared | Adjective

conditioned:/kənˈdɪʃənd/ | v. |使习惯于，使适应；训练；决定，制约 | To accustom (someone or something) to something; to train or accustom (someone or an animal) to behave in a certain way or to accept certain circumstances; determine or restrict.| Years of living in poverty had conditioned them to hardship. 多年的贫困生活使他们习惯了艰苦。| Synonyms: train, adapt, accustom, habituate, inure, acclimatize | Antonyms: unaccustomed, unadapted, unprepared | Verb
