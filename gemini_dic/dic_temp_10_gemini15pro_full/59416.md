earthily:/ˈɜːrθɪli/ | adv. | 世俗地；粗俗地；现实地 | In a manner characteristic of or resembling earth; coarsely; materially; realistically. |  He spoke earthily about the realities of farm life. 他粗俗地谈论了农场生活的现实。| Examples:  She laughed earthily at the joke. 她对这个笑话放声大笑。  The novel portrays the characters earthily and without sentimentality. 这部小说对人物的描写很现实，没有感情色彩。 | 近义词: coarsely, crudely, grossly, materially, realistically, worldly | 反义词: spiritually, ideally, refinedly, ethereally | 词性: 副词
