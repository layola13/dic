chondropterygii: /ˌkɒndrɒptəˈrɪdʒiaɪ/ /ˌkɑːndrəptəˈrɪdʒiaɪ/
noun
软骨鱼纲
a class of fishes that includes sharks, rays, and chimaeras, characterized by a cartilaginous skeleton.
Sharks, rays, and skates belong to the class Chondrichthyes, also known as Chondropterygii.  鲨鱼、鳐鱼和魟鱼属于软骨鱼纲，也称为 Chondropterygii。
The Chondropterygii are ancient and fascinating creatures. 软骨鱼纲是古老而迷人的生物。
近义词: Chondrichthyes, Elasmobranchii (for sharks and rays specifically)
反义词:  Osteichthyes (bony fish)
词性: n.


Etymology:
chondro- (Greek: χόνδρος, chondros, "cartilage") + pterygii (Greek: πτέρυξ, pteryx, "fin" or "wing")
