Eskimos: /ˈeskɪmoʊz/ (American) /ˈeskɪməʊz/ (British) | n. |爱斯基摩人| A term formerly used to refer to the Inuit and Yupik peoples of the Arctic regions of North America and Greenland. This term is now considered outdated and often offensive, and the preferred terms are Inuit (for those in Canada and Greenland) or Yupik (for those in Alaska and Siberia). |  The documentary followed the lives of Eskimos in northern Canada.
