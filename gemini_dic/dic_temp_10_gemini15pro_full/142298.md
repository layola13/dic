orthoclase: /ˈɔːrθəˌkleɪs/ (US) /ˈɔːθəʊˌkleɪs/ (UK)| n.|正长石|A common feldspar mineral, KAlSi3O8, occurring in white, pink, or other pale-colored crystals and used in making porcelain and glass.|Orthoclase is a common constituent of granite. 正长石是花岗岩的常见成分。|The pottery glaze was made using finely ground orthoclase.  这种陶器釉是用精细研磨的正长石制成的。|Synonyms: feldspar, potassium feldspar|Antonyms: None|noun
