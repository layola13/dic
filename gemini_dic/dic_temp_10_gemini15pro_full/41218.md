coral:/ˈkɔːrəl/ (US) /ˈkɒrəl/ (UK)| n. adj.|珊瑚，珊瑚色|A hard stony substance secreted by certain marine coelenterates as an external skeleton, typically forming large reefs in warm seas.  Also, a reddish-pink color.|例句：The coral reefs are teeming with life. 珊瑚礁充满了生机。

The divers admired the vibrant coral. 潜水员欣赏着鲜艳的珊瑚。|近义词：reef, polyp|反义词:None|词性:noun, adjective

coral:/ˈkɔːrəl/ (US) /ˈkɒrəl/ (UK)| adj.|珊瑚色的|Of a reddish-pink color.|例句：She wore a coral dress to the party. 她穿了一件珊瑚色的连衣裙去参加聚会。

The sunset painted the sky in coral hues.  日落将天空染成了珊瑚色。|近义词：pink, salmon|反义词:None|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *coral, from Latin corallum, from Greek korallion.
