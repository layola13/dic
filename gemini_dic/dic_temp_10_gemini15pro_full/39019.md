confiteor:/ˌkɒnfiˈtiːɔːr/ /kənˌfɪtiˈɔːr/
| n. |忏悔; 忏悔祷文|A prayer in which one confesses sins, especially in the Catholic Mass.|例句：The priest began the Mass with the Confiteor. 神父以忏悔祷文开始了弥撒。|近义词：confession, penitence, mea culpa|反义词: absolution, forgiveness |词性:名词
