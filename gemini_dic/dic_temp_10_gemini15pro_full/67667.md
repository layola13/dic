extruded:/ɪkˈstruːdɪd/
adjective
挤出的，压出的
shaped or formed by being forced through an opening
The plastic is extruded into long strips. 塑料被挤压成长条。
近义词:  pressed, molded, shaped, formed
反义词:  cast, molded (sometimes, depending on the context)
词性: adjective

extrude:/ɪkˈstruːd/
verb
挤出，压出，推出
to shape (metal, plastic, etc.) by forcing it through a die
The machine extrudes the plastic into various shapes. 这台机器将塑料挤压成各种形状。
近义词:  force out, press out, squeeze out
反义词:  inject, insert, draw in
词性: verb
