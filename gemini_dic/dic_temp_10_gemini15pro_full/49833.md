一般现在时 (Simple Present):
This is my pen.  这是我的钢笔。
That is your phone. 那是你的手机。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
He is looking at this picture. 他正在看这张照片。
They are discussing those issues. 他们正在讨论那些问题。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have seen this movie before. 我以前看过这部电影。
She has read those books already. 她已经读过那些书了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been looking at this document all morning. 我整个上午都在看这份文件。
They have been discussing those proposals for weeks. 他们已经讨论那些提议好几个星期了。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I saw that bird yesterday. 我昨天看到了那只鸟。
She bought this dress last week. 她上周买了这条裙子。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was looking at this painting when you called. 你打电话时我正在看这幅画。
They were discussing those matters when the meeting was interrupted.  会议中断时，他们正在讨论那些事情。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had seen that movie before you recommended it. 在你推荐之前，我已经看过那部电影了。
She had read those books before she started college.  在她上大学之前，她已经读过那些书了。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been looking at this problem for hours before I found a solution. 在找到解决方案之前，我已经研究这个问题好几个小时了。
They had been discussing those ideas for months before they reached a decision.  在他们做出决定之前，他们已经讨论那些想法好几个月了。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will read this book tomorrow. 我明天会读这本书。
She will visit that museum next week. 她下周会参观那个博物馆。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
I will be reading this book tomorrow afternoon. 明天下午我会在读这本书。
She will be visiting that museum next Tuesday.  下周二她将会参观那个博物馆。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will have finished reading this book by next week. 我下周之前会读完这本书。
She will have visited that museum by the end of the month.  她会在月底前参观完那个博物馆。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
