commemoratively:/kəˈmɛmərətɪvli/ | adv. | 纪念地，庆祝地 | In a way that serves to commemorate someone or something. |  The museum was commemoratively opened on the anniversary of the event.  这家博物馆在事件周年纪念日隆重开幕。 | Synonyms: ceremonially,  in honor of,  in remembrance of | Antonyms:  disrespectfully,  ignominiously | 词性: 副词

commemorate:/kəˈmɛməreɪt/ | v. | 纪念，庆祝 | Recall and show respect for (someone or something). | The statue commemorates the soldiers who died in the war. 这座雕像纪念在战争中牺牲的士兵。 | Synonyms: celebrate, remember,  honor | Antonyms:  forget,  ignore, dishonor |词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin commemorativus, from commemorare "call to remembrance," from com- "together" + memorare "to remind, bring to remembrance," from memor "mindful."
