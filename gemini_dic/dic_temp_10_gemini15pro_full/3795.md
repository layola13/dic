agnail: /ˈæɡneɪl/ | n. | 忧虑，烦恼 | A cause of irritation or annoyance. (Obsolete) | His constant complaining was an agnail to her. 他不断的抱怨让她很烦。(古语用法) | Synonym: annoyance, irritation | Antonym: comfort, solace | noun
