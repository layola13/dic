newwavers:/ˈnjuːˌweɪvərz/| n.|新浪潮者；新浪潮音乐爱好者|People who are fans of or involved in the new wave music genre, which emerged in the late 1970s and early 1980s, characterized by its electronic, experimental, and often unconventional sound and style.|The newwavers packed the club to hear the band's debut album. 新浪潮乐迷挤满了俱乐部，就为听乐队的首张专辑。| They were newwavers, sporting skinny ties and asymmetrical haircuts. 他们是新浪潮一族，戴着细领带，留着不对称的发型。|近义词：new wave fans, post-punk fans|反义词:traditionalists, conservatives|词性:noun
