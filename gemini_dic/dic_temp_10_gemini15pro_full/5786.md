amanuensis:/əˌmænjuˈɛnsɪs/
n.
抄写员；秘书；笔记者
A person employed to write what another dictates or to copy what has been written.
Milton, blind in later life, employed an amanuensis.  弥尔顿晚年失明，雇用了一名抄写员。

She worked as an amanuensis for a renowned author. 她是一位著名作家的抄写员。

Synonym:
scribe, secretary, clerk, copyist

Antonym:
author, dictator, originator


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *amanu- (related to manus "hand") + -ensis (suffix forming nouns denoting place, origin, or agent).  源自拉丁语 *amanu-（与 manus “手”相关）+ -ensis（构成表示地点、起源或代理人的名词的后缀）。
