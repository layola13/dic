aureation:/ɔːˈreɪʃən/
n.
镀金, 涂金
the application of gold leaf or paint.
The aureation of the dome gave the building a majestic look.  穹顶的镀金使建筑物显得雄伟壮观。
The frame underwent a process of aureation to enhance its beauty.  这个框架经过了镀金处理以增强其美感。
近义词:gilding, gold plating
反义词:tarnishing, stripping

词性:noun

词源:From Latin *aurātiōn*, from aurātus, past participle of aurāre  ('to gild'). Ultimately from aurum ('gold').
