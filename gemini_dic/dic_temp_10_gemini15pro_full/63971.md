episodicity:/ˌɛpɪsəˈdɪsəti/ | n. | 情节性；片段性；偶发性 | The quality or state of being episodic; occurring in episodes or separate incidents rather than continuously or as a unified whole. |  The episodicity of the rain made it difficult to plan outdoor activities. 这场雨断断续续的，很难计划户外活动。|  intermittency, sporadic nature, discontinuity | continuity, persistence, regularity | noun
