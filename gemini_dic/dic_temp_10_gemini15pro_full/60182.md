efficacies: /ˈefɪkəsiz/
noun
功效，效力，效能
the ability of something to produce the desired result
The doctor questioned the efficacy of the new drug. 医生质疑这种新药的疗效。
Synonyms: effectiveness, potency, strength
Antonyms: ineffectiveness, weakness, impotence
词性: 名词 (plural of efficacy)
