forers:/ˈfɔːrərz/

先行者，先驱者
Those who go before, pioneers.
The forers of the new technology paved the way for future advancements.  新技术的先行者为未来的进步铺平了道路。
pioneers, predecessors, vanguard
stragglers, laggards
