minish:/ˈmɪnɪʃ/| vt.| 减少，缩小|To make smaller or less; diminish. |例句：The wizard could minish his size to fit through the tiny door.  巫师可以缩小自己的尺寸以穿过那扇小门。

例句：He tried to minish the importance of his mistake. 他试图降低自己错误的重要性。|近义词：diminish, reduce, decrease, lessen|反义词：increase, enlarge, augment, amplify|词性：动词
