pantheistic:/ˌpænθiˈɪstɪk/ | adj. | 泛神论的 | Relating to or characterized by pantheism, the belief that God is everything and everywhere, or that God is identical with the universe. |  Many ancient cultures held pantheistic beliefs, seeing the divine in all of nature. 许多古代文化持有泛神论的信仰，在所有自然事物中都看到了神性。

Pantheistic spirituality often emphasizes interconnectedness and reverence for the natural world. 泛神论的精神性通常强调相互联系和对自然世界的崇敬。 | 近义词：panentheistic, cosmic | 反义词：atheistic, polytheistic | 词性: adjective

词源:  From pan- "all" + theistic "relating to god," from Greek theos "god."  源自 pan- “所有” + theistic “与神有关的”，来自希腊语 theos “神”。
