nocturnalities: /ˌnɒktərˈnælɪtiz/ | n. |夜间活动; 夜行性 |Nocturnalities refers to the habits and behaviors of animals that are active primarily at night.  It can also refer to the characteristics of things related to the night.|  The naturalist documented the nocturnalities of the desert fox.  博物学家记录了沙漠狐的夜间活动。| Synonyms: night habits, nocturnal activities, nocturnal behavior | Antonyms: diurnal activities, diurnal habits | Noun

nocturnality: /ˌnɒktərˈnælɪti/ | n. |夜行性; 夜间活动| The state or quality of being nocturnal; activity or behavior during the night. | Owls are known for their nocturnality. 猫头鹰以其夜行性而闻名。| Synonyms: nocturnalism, night activity | Antonyms: diurnality, daytime activity | Noun
