curare:/kjʊˈrɑːriː/ /kjʊˈreriː/| n.|箭毒，库拉雷|A highly toxic extract from certain South American plants, traditionally used as a paralyzing agent on arrow tips.|The indigenous people used curare to hunt animals. 土著人用箭毒来猎杀动物。|Synonyms: poison, toxin|Antonyms: antidote, remedy|noun


curare:/kjʊˈrɑːriː/ /kjʊˈreriː/| n.|箭毒|Curare is a muscle relaxant that causes paralysis.  箭毒是一种导致瘫痪的肌肉松弛剂。|Synonyms: paralytic, neurotoxin|Antonyms: stimulant|noun
