etonians:/iːˈtəʊniənz/
noun
伊顿公学学生；伊顿人
Students or former students of Eton College, a prestigious British independent school for boys.
The Etonians were known for their distinctive uniforms and privileged backgrounds. 伊顿公学的学生们以其独特的制服和优越的背景而闻名。
synonyms: Eton College alumni, Old Etonians
antonyms: N/A
词性: noun
