bigwigs:/ˈbɪɡwɪɡz/
noun
重要人物；大人物；要人
Important people; influential individuals, often in positions of power.
The bigwigs at the company decided to merge with their competitor. 公司的要员们决定与竞争对手合并。
VIPs, dignitaries, top brass, leading figures
minnows, underlings, nobodies
n.
