hexacanth:/ˈheksəkænθ/| n.|六钩蚴，六钩胚|A larval stage of certain parasitic flatworms (e.g., tapeworms) characterized by six hooks.  It is the first larval stage and is typically the infective stage for the intermediate host.|The hexacanth larva bores through the intestinal wall and migrates to the muscles. 六钩蚴幼虫钻穿肠壁并迁移到肌肉中。|Synonym: oncosphere|Antonym: N/A|Noun


hexacanth:/ˈheksəkænθ/| adj.|六钩的|Having six hooks.  Relating to or characteristic of a hexacanth larva.|The hexacanth embryo develops into the next larval stage. 六钩胚胎发育成下一个幼虫阶段。|Synonym: six-hooked|Antonym: N/A|Adjective
