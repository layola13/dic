nonresistant:/ˌnɒnrɪˈzɪstənt/ | /ˌnɑːnrɪˈzɪstənt/ | adj. | 无抵抗力的，不抵抗的 | Not resistant; offering no resistance. |  The nonresistant population was easily conquered. 无抵抗力的人口很容易被征服。|  The bacteria proved nonresistant to the new antibiotic. 这种细菌被证明对这种新抗生素没有抵抗力。 | 近义词：susceptible, vulnerable, defenseless | 反义词：resistant, immune, impervious | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From non- + resistant.  "Non-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not". "Resistant" comes from the Latin "resistere" meaning "to stand back, withstand".
