oleometer:/ˌoʊliˈɒmɪtər/ | n. |油量计，油酸计|An instrument for measuring the oil content of a substance, particularly the percentage of oil in olives.|例句：The oleometer revealed a high oil content in the harvested olives. 油量计显示收获的橄榄油含量很高。

例句：The olive oil producer uses an oleometer to ensure quality control. 橄榄油生产商使用油量计来确保质量控制。|近义词：oil meter, oil gauge|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
