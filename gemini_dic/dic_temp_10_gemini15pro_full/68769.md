farro:/ˈfæroʊ/ (US), /ˈfærəʊ/ (UK)| n.|法罗小麦，一种古老的谷物|A type of hulled wheat, cultivated since ancient times, that is high in fiber and protein. It is often used in salads, soups, and other dishes.|例句：
I made a delicious salad with farro, roasted vegetables, and feta cheese. 我做了一份美味的沙拉，里面有法罗小麦、烤蔬菜和羊乳酪。

The farro risotto was creamy and flavorful. 法罗小麦烩饭口感柔滑，味道浓郁。
