hylism:/ˈhaɪlɪzəm/
n.
物质主义；唯物论
The philosophical doctrine that matter is the fundamental substance in nature, and that all things, including mental states and consciousness, are results of material interactions.
Hylism is a philosophical doctrine that emphasizes the importance of matter. 物质主义是一种强调物质重要性的哲学学说。
The philosopher argued for hylism, rejecting the idea of a separate realm of spirit. 这位哲学家支持物质主义，反对存在独立精神领域的想法。
近义词:materialism, physicalism
反义词:idealism, spiritualism
词性:noun
