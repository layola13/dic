nondetentions:/ˌnɒndɪˈtenʃənz/ |n.| 非拘留；免拘留|The policy of nondetentions for minor offenses has been controversial.  对于轻微犯罪不予拘留的政策一直存在争议。| This school operates a system of nondetentions for students with good behavior. 这所学校对行为良好的学生实行非拘留制度。| 近义词: releases, discharges| 反义词: detentions, imprisonments| 词性: 名词

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "detention" (from Latin "detent-," past participle of "detinēre" meaning "to hold back, detain").
