cheaply:/ˈtʃiːpli/| adv.|便宜地，廉价地|At a low cost; inexpensively. |I bought this dress cheaply at a sale. 我在 распродаже 便宜地买下了这条裙子。|Synonyms: inexpensively, affordably, economically|Antonyms: expensively, dearly, lavishly|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *cēaplice (attested only in compounds like cēapman "merchant"), from cēap "bargain, purchase, price" + -lice (adverbial suffix). The root is related to Old Frisian kāp "bargain," Old Saxon kōp "purchase, price," Dutch koop "bargain, purchase," Old High German kouf "purchase," German Kauf "purchase."
