euphoria's:/juːˈfɔːriəz/
noun
欣快感（的）
Possession form of "euphoria," meaning a feeling or state of intense excitement and happiness.
She reveled in the euphoria's embrace after winning the race. 她沉浸在赢得比赛后的欣快感中。
The euphoria's intensity was overwhelming. 欣快感强烈得令人不知所措。
Synonyms: elation's, joy's, bliss's, rapture's
Antonyms: despair's, misery's, depression's
