integrative: /ˈɪntɪɡreɪtɪv/ | adj. | 综合的，一体化的 | Combining or coordinating separate elements so as to provide a harmonious, interrelated whole. |例句：The hospital adopted an integrative approach to patient care. 这家医院采用了综合的病人护理方法。|例句：Integrative medicine combines conventional treatments with alternative therapies. 综合医学将传统疗法与替代疗法相结合。| 近义词：holistic, comprehensive, unified, combined | 反义词：divisive, fragmented, separate | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *integrativus, from Latin integrat-, past participle stem of integrare "to make whole," from integer "whole, complete."
