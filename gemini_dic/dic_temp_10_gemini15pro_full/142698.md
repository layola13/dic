otherguise:/ˈʌðərɡaɪz/
noun
伪装，幌子
a different appearance or form used to conceal someone's identity or true nature
He attended the meeting in otherguise to avoid being recognized. 他化了装参加会议以免被认出来。
Synonyms: disguise, camouflage, pretense
Antonyms:  true form, real identity
词性: 名词
