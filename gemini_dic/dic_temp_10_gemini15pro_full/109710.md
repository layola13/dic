leveeing:/ˈlɛviɪŋ/
n.
筑堤；堤防；堤坝
The act of building a levee or embankment.
The city invested millions in leveeing to protect against floods.  这座城市投资数百万美元修建堤坝以防洪水。
embankment, dike, floodwall
damming, removing
noun
