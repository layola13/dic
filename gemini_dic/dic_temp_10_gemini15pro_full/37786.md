comped:/kɑːmpt/ /kɒmpt/ | v. |免费赠送；(尤指餐馆或酒店)因服务不周而免单；作为补偿赠送|To give something complimentary, often a meal or a service, especially as compensation for poor service or to encourage future patronage. |例句：The restaurant comped our desserts because of the long wait.  餐厅因为我们等了很久而免了甜点的费用。

例句：The hotel comped us a night's stay due to the noisy construction next door. 由于隔壁施工噪音很大，酒店免费让我们住了一晚。|近义词：complimentary, free, gratis|反义词：charged, paid|词性：动词
