alloquy: /ˈæləkwɪ/
n.
正式讲话；演说；谈话
a formal speech, address, or conversation
The principal delivered a brief alloquy to the graduating class. 校长向毕业班做了简短的讲话。

近义词: address, speech, discourse, oration, sermon
反义词: silence, quiet, hush

词性: noun

词源: From Late Latin *alloquium, from Latin alloquī ("to address, speak to"), from ad- ("to") + loquī ("to speak"). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *lewk- ("to speak").
