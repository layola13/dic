cosine:/ˈkoʊsaɪn/| n.|余弦|A trigonometric function that is equal to the ratio of the side adjacent to an acute angle (in a right-angled triangle) to the hypotenuse.|The cosine of 30 degrees is 0.866. 30度的余弦是0.866。|近义词: (none - as it's a specific mathematical function)|反义词: (none - as it's a specific mathematical function)|词性:noun

The cosine of an angle is calculated by dividing the length of the adjacent side by the length of the hypotenuse.  一个角的余弦是通过将邻边的长度除以斜边的长度来计算的。
