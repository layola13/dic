inflammation:/ˌɪnfləˈmeɪʃn/ | n. | 炎症；发炎 |A localized physical condition in which part of the body becomes reddened, swollen, hot, and often painful, especially as a reaction to injury or infection. | The doctor diagnosed the redness and swelling as inflammation. 医生诊断红肿为炎症。 |  Inflammation can be a sign of infection. 炎症可能是感染的迹象。 | 近义词: swelling, redness, soreness | 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可用表示健康的词) health, wellness | 词性: noun
