deictical:/daɪˈɪktɪkəl/| adj.|指示的|Relating to or denoting a word or expression whose meaning is dependent on the context in which it is used, e.g., *here*, *you*, *now*.|“Here” is a deictical word, as its meaning depends on the speaker's location.|近义词：indexical, demonstrative|反义词:non-deictic|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek *deiktikós* (pointing, showing), from *deíknȳmi* (I show).
