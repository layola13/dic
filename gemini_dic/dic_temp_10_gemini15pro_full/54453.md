disinherits:/ˌdɪsɪnˈhɛrɪts/ |v. |剥夺继承权|To prevent someone from inheriting property, especially by making a will that excludes them. |Example 1: The wealthy businessman disinherited his son after a bitter feud.  这位富有的商人在一场激烈的争执后剥夺了他儿子的继承权。

Example 2:  She was disinherited for marrying against her father's wishes. 她因为违背父亲的意愿结婚而被剥夺了继承权。 |Synonym: cut off, exclude |Antonym: bequeath, inherit |verb
