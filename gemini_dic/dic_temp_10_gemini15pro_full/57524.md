dragooner:/drəˈɡuːnər/ | n. | 龙骑兵；恐吓者；勒索者 | A soldier originally trained to fight on horseback and later on foot.  A person who harasses or coerces someone into doing something. |  例句：The dragoons charged the enemy lines. 龙骑兵向敌人的防线冲锋。

例句：He acted like a dragoon, trying to force her into selling her property. 他像个勒索者，试图强迫她卖掉她的财产。 | 近义词：cavalryman, trooper, bully, intimidator | 反义词: protector, defender | 词性: noun
