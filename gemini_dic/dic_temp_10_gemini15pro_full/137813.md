nonversatility:/ˌnɒnvɜːsəˈtɪləti/
n.
lack of versatility; inability to adapt or be adapted to many different functions or activities
The nonversatility of the tool made it unsuitable for the task.  这个工具缺乏多功能性，使其不适合这项任务。
inflexibility, rigidity,  inadaptability
versatility, adaptability, flexibility
noun
