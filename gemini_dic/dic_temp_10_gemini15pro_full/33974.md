cissy:/ˈsɪsi/| n. |胆小鬼，懦夫；女人气的男人|A timid or cowardly person; an effeminate man.|He was called a cissy for not wanting to play football.  他因为不想踢足球而被称为胆小鬼。|近义词：coward, wimp, milksop|反义词：brave, courageous|noun
cissy:/ˈsɪsi/| adj. |女人气的，娇气的|Effeminate; characteristic of a cissy.|He had a cissy way of walking. 他走路的样子很女人气。|近义词：effeminate, unmanly|反义词：masculine, manly|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
