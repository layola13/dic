non-solution:/ˌnɒn səˈluːʃən/ | n. | 无效的解决方案；不能解决问题的方法 | Something presented as a solution but which fails to actually address the underlying problem. |  The committee proposed a non-solution to the budget crisis. 委员会提出了一个无法解决预算危机的方案。|  The software update was a non-solution; it didn't fix the bug and created new problems.  这个软件更新是一个无效的解决方案；它没有修复错误，反而产生了新的问题。 | near synonyms:  inadequate solution, ineffective measure, stopgap, placebo, band-aid solution | antonyms: solution, remedy, cure, fix, answer | noun
