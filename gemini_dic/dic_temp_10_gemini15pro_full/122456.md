mobilised:/ˈməʊbɪlaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈmoʊbɪlaɪzd/ (US)| adj. |动员的，调动的|Made ready for action or use, especially of military troops or equipment.|例句：The army was mobilised for war. 军队已动员起来准备作战。|近义词：deployed, activated, marshalled|反义词: demobilised, deactivated|词性:形容词

mobilise:/ˈməʊbɪlaɪz/ (UK) /ˈmoʊbɪlaɪz/ (US)| v. |动员，调动|Prepare and organize (troops) for active service.|例句：The government mobilised the army. 政府动员了军队。|近义词：deploy, activate, marshal|反义词: demobilise, deactivate|词性:动词

Etymology: From French *mobiliser*, from *mobile* (“movable”) + *-iser*, from Latin *mobilis* (“movable”) and *-izare*. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *meue- (“to move, to push away”).
