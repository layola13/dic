demotivates:/diːˈməʊtɪveɪts/ | v. | 使失去动力，使灰心 | To make someone lose their enthusiasm or motivation. |  The constant criticism demotivates the team. 不断的批评使团队失去动力。| synonyms: discourages, disheartens, demoralizes | antonyms: motivates, encourages, inspires | verb

demotivating:/diːˈməʊtɪveɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人沮丧的，使人失去动力的 | Causing someone to lose their enthusiasm or motivation. | The demotivating work environment made it hard to stay focused. 令人沮丧的工作环境让人难以集中注意力。| synonyms: discouraging, disheartening, demoralizing | antonyms: motivating, encouraging, inspiring | adjective

demotivation:/ˌdiːˌməʊtɪˈveɪʃn/ | n. | 失去动力，灰心 | The feeling of losing enthusiasm or motivation. |  The team's demotivation was evident in their poor performance. 团队的士气低落在他们糟糕的表现中显而易见。| synonyms: discouragement, despair, hopelessness | antonyms: motivation, encouragement, inspiration | noun
