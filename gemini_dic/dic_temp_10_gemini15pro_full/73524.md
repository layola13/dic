foreshorten:/fɔːrˈʃɔːrtn/ /fɔːrˈʃɔːrttn/ | v. | 缩短，(绘画中)用透视法缩短 | To represent the lines of (an object) shorter than they actually are in order to create the illusion of perspective. | The artist foreshortened the outstretched arm of the figure in the painting.  艺术家在画中用透视法缩短了人物伸出的手臂。| synonyms: abridge, condense, curtail, shorten, contract | antonyms: lengthen, extend, elongate | verb

foreshortening:/fɔːrˈʃɔːrtnɪŋ/ /fɔːrˈʃɔːrtənɪŋ/ | n. | 透视缩短法 | The technique of depicting an object or human figure in a picture so as to produce an illusion of projection or extension in space. | The foreshortening in the painting made the figures appear to be receding into the background.  画中的透视缩短法使人物看起来像是退到了背景中。| synonyms: perspective | antonyms: N/A | noun
