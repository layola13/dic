hansom:/ˈhænsəm/| n. |双轮马车；出租马车|A two-wheeled horse-drawn carriage, especially one available for hire.|例句：We took a hansom cab back to the hotel. 我们乘出租马车回了酒店。

例句：The hansom clip-clopped down the cobbled street.  马车在鹅卵石街道上发出得得的响声。

|近义词：cab, carriage, hackney |反义词: - |词性:noun


hansom:/ˈhænsəm/| adj. |英俊的(主要用于指男性) |Having a pleasing and usually impressive or dignified appearance (used chiefly of men).|例句：He was a tall, hansom man with a charming smile. 他是一位高大英俊的男人，面带迷人的微笑。

例句：The hansom stranger caught everyone's attention. 这位英俊的陌生人吸引了所有人的目光。


|近义词：handsome, attractive, good-looking |反义词:ugly, unattractive |词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the proper noun "Hanansom," the surname of a London coachbuilder who patented the design in 1834.
