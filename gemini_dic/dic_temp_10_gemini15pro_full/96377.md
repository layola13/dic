indiscoverable:/ˌɪndɪˈskʌvərəbl/
adjective
无法发现的；难以探测的
not able to be discovered or found
The lost city remained indiscoverable for centuries. 失落的城市几个世纪以来都未被发现。
The cause of the disease is indiscoverable. 这种疾病的病因难以探测。
Synonyms: undetectable, untraceable, unfindable
Antonyms: discoverable, detectable, findable
词性: 形容词
