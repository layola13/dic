deftly:/ˈdeftli/| adv.|灵巧地，熟练地|In a skillful, nimble, or clever way.  Showing skill and cleverness in handling things.|She deftly caught the falling vase. 她灵巧地接住了掉落的花瓶。|skillfully, adroitly, nimbly, expertly|clumsily, awkwardly|adverb
词源: From deft + -ly.  Deft comes from Middle English *deft, from Old English gedæfte "mild, meek," later "fitting, suitable," from Proto-Germanic *daft-.
