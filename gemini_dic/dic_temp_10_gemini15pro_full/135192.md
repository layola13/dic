nonorganizations:/ˌnɒnɔːrɡənaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ |n.| 非组织，非机构 | Entities that are not formally structured or recognized as organizations.  These can include informal groups, networks, or movements. |  Example:  Nonorganizations, such as online communities, can still exert significant influence.  在线社区等非组织仍然可以发挥重大影响。
