electroretinographic:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˌrɛtɪnəˈɡræfɪk/
adjective|视网膜电图的|Relating to or obtained by electroretinography, a technique for measuring the electrical activity of the retina in response to light stimulation.|Electroretinographic testing can detect early signs of retinal disease. 视网膜电图检查可以检测到视网膜疾病的早期迹象。|Synonyms: ERG, electroretinographical|Antonyms: None|词性:形容词

electroretinogram:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈrɛtɪnəɡræm/
noun|视网膜电图|A record or graph of the electrical activity of the retina, obtained by electroretinography.|The electroretinogram showed reduced amplitude, suggesting retinal dysfunction. 视网膜电图显示振幅降低，提示视网膜功能障碍。|Synonyms: ERG|Antonyms: None|词性:名词

词源: electro- (electric) + retin- (retina) + -graphic (related to writing or recording)
