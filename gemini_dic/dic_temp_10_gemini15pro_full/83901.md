half-awake:/ˌhɑːf əˈweɪk/ | adj. | 半睡半醒的 | Partially awake; not fully alert. |  I stumbled out of bed, still half-awake. 我跌跌撞撞地走下床，仍然半睡半醒。

She answered the phone, sounding half-awake. 她接了电话，听起来半睡半醒。 | Near synonyms: drowsy, sleepy, groggy | Antonyms: wide-awake, alert, fully awake | Adjective
