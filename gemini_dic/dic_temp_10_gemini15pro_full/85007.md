harangue:/həˈræŋ/ | n. vt.| 慷慨激昂的演说，长篇大论；滔滔不绝地劝说|A lengthy and aggressive speech.  A scolding or a long or intense verbal attack. |The coach delivered a long harangue to his team after their poor performance. 教练在球队表现糟糕后，对他们进行了一番长篇大论的训斥。|He harangued the crowd about the evils of pollution. 他向人群慷慨激昂地讲述了污染的危害。|近义词：tirade, rant, diatribe, lecture|反义词：discussion, chat, praise|词性：名词，动词

harangue:/həˈræŋ/ | v.| 慷慨激昂地演说，长篇大论；滔滔不绝地劝说|To deliver a lengthy and aggressive speech. To lecture someone at length in an aggressive and critical manner. |She harangued them about their lack of commitment. 她滔滔不绝地斥责他们缺乏奉献精神。|The street preacher harangued passersby about the wages of sin. 街头布道者向路人长篇大论地讲述罪恶的代价。|近义词：rant, lecture, scold, berate|反义词：discuss, chat, praise|词性：动词
