blunge:/blʌndʒ/
| v. | 投入；跳入；使（陶土）与水混合成泥浆 | To mix clay with water and agitate it until a creamy mixture is formed; to plunge or immerse oneself suddenly into something. |  The potter blunged the dry clay in a vat of water. 陶工把干粘土投入一桶水中搅拌。 |  immerse, plunge, submerge | emerge | verb
