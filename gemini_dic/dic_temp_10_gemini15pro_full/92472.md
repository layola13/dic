hyposulphite:/ˌhaɪpəˈsʌlfaɪt/
n.
连二亚硫酸盐;大苏打
a salt of hyposulphurous acid.
例句：Sodium hyposulphite is used in photography. 连二亚硫酸钠用于摄影。

例句：Hyposulphite solutions are unstable and decompose easily. 连二亚硫酸盐溶液不稳定，容易分解。

近义词：sodium thiosulfate

注意事项:
This term, while historically used, is now considered outdated and chemically inaccurate.  The correct term for the substance commonly referred to as "hyposulphite" (especially in photography) is **sodium thiosulfate (Na₂S₂O₃)**.  Hyposulfurous acid (H₂S₂O₄) does exist, but its salts are not commonly encountered.  Therefore, any modern scientific context should utilize "sodium thiosulfate" instead.
