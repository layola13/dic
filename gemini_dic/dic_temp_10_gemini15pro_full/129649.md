non-custodial:/ˌnɒn kʌˈstəʊdiəl/ (US) /ˌnɑːn kʌˈstoʊdiəl/| adj.|非保管的，非托管的|Not having custody or guardianship, especially of a child.  Relating to financial assets that are held by a third party, not the owner.|例句：She has non-custodial rights to her children. 她对她的孩子拥有非监护权。|近义词：independent, decentralized|反义词:custodial|词性:形容词

non-custodial:/ˌnɒn kʌˈstəʊdiəl/ (US) /ˌnɑːn kʌˈstoʊdiəl/| adj.|非保管的，非托管的|In finance, referring to wallets where the user holds their private keys, giving them full control of their assets.|例句：A non-custodial cryptocurrency wallet allows users to manage their own private keys.  一个非托管的加密货币钱包允许用户管理自己的私钥。|近义词：self-managed, user-controlled|反义词:custodial|词性:形容词
