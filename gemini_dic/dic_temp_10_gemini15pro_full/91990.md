hypernormally:/ˌhaɪpərˈnɔːrməli/ | adv. | 超常地，过度正常地 |  Exceeding what is normal; abnormally normal. Often used ironically or paradoxically to describe something that appears normal on the surface but is actually excessive or dysfunctional. | The family seemed hypernormally happy, which made her suspicious.  这个家庭看起来幸福得有些不正常，这让她起了疑心。 | The CEO, with hypernormal calm, announced the company's bankruptcy.  首席执行官异常平静地宣布了公司破产。 | 近义词: excessively, abnormally, unusually | 反义词: normally, typically | 词性: 副词

词源:  "Hyper-" (Greek: over, above) + "normal"
