hornbug:/ˈhɔːrnˌbʌɡ/| n. |角虫，鞘翅目昆虫|A beetle, especially a large ground beetle or stag beetle, characterized by prominent antennae or mandibles.  These insects are often found in wooded areas and are known for their hard exoskeletons.|Example 1: The children were fascinated by the hornbug they found under a log. 孩子们对他们在木头下发现的角虫很着迷。

Example 2:  The hornbug scurried across the forest floor. 角虫快速地穿过森林地面。|近义词：beetle, insect|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
