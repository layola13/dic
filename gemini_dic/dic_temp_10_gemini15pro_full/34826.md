clinger:/ˈklɪŋər/| n.|粘人的人，紧缠不放的人|Someone who is emotionally dependent and has difficulty letting go of relationships.  They tend to be overly attached and needy.|She's such a clinger; she never lets her boyfriend out of her sight.|近义词：stage-five clinger, leech, hanger-on|反义词:independent person, loner|noun
