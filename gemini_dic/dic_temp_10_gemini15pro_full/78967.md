girlies: /ˈɡɜːrliz/ (UK) /ˈɡɜːrliz/ (US)| n.|女孩们；闺蜜们|A slang term, often used affectionately, to refer to a group of girls or women, especially close friends. It emphasizes a sense of shared femininity, fun, and camaraderie.|例句：I'm going out with the girlies tonight.  今晚我要和闺蜜们出去玩。

例句：Let's have a girlies' night in and watch a movie. 我们今晚来个闺蜜之夜，看部电影吧。|近义词：friends, gals, ladies|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly boys/guys in certain contexts)|词性: Noun
