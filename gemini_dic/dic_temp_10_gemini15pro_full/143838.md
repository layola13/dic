outpitched:/ˌaʊtˈpɪtʃt/
verb
胜过投球，在投球上胜过
To surpass in pitching; to pitch better than.
The rookie outpitched the veteran in the crucial game.  这个新秀在关键比赛中胜过老将。
surpass, outdo, outperform
be outpitched by, be surpassed by
