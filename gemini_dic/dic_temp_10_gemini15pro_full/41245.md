corb:/kɔːrb/| n.| 突出物，（尤指）鸟巢，篮子；乌鸦|A corb is a projection, especially one that is shaped like a bird's nest or a basket. It can also refer to a raven or crow.|The castle walls featured decorative corbs.|城堡的墙壁上有装饰性的突出物。|近义词：projection, bracket, ledge, raven, crow|反义词: indentation, hollow|noun
