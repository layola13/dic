lipothymous:/ˌlɪpəˈθaɪməs/| adj.|快要昏厥的；感觉快要昏厥的|Characterized by a sensation of faintness or lightheadedness, often a precursor to syncope (fainting).|She felt lipothymous after standing in the hot sun for too long.  她站在烈日下太久，感到快要昏厥了。

The hiker, dehydrated and exhausted, became lipothymous near the summit.  脱水又疲惫的徒步旅行者在接近山顶时感到快要昏厥了。|近义词：faint, lightheaded, dizzy, weak, swooning|反义词：alert, vigorous, energetic|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Greek λειποθυμία (leipothymia), from λείπειν (leipein, “to leave”) + θυμός (thymos, "spirit, mind").  源自希腊语 λειποθυμία (leipothymia)，由 λείπειν (leipein，“离开”) + θυμός (thymos，“精神，心灵”) 组成。
