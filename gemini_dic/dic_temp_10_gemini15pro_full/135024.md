nonnervousness:/ˌnɒnˈnɜːvəsnəs/ /ˌnɑːnˈnɜːrvəsnəs/| n. |镇静，不紧张|The state of being not nervous; calmness or composure.|She displayed remarkable nonnervousness during the interview. 她在面试中表现出非凡的镇定。|近义词：calmness, composure, tranquility, serenity|反义词：nervousness, anxiety, apprehension|词性:名词

词源:
non- (prefix) + nervousness (noun)

non-:  from Latin *nōn* "not"
nervousness: from nerve + -ous + -ness
