bore-down:/bɔːr daʊn/
verb
压制；向下钻孔；承受向下压力
To press down forcefully; to exert downward pressure.  In some contexts, it can also refer to the act of drilling downwards.
The doctor bore down on her abdomen to check for tenderness. 医生用力按压她的腹部以检查是否有压痛。
The drill bore down through the rock. 钻头向下钻穿岩石。
近义词: press down, push down, bear down, drill down
反义词: lift, raise
词性: verb (动词)


bore-down (as a phrasal verb - bore down on/upon): /bɔːr daʊn ɑːn/ /əˈpɑːn/
verb
逼近；猛扑；对…施加压力
To approach something or someone in a menacing or overwhelming way; to put pressure on someone.
The deadline bore down on us, and we had to work overtime. 截止日期逼近，我们不得不加班。
The prosecutor bore down on the witness, demanding answers.  检察官向证人施加压力，要求他回答问题。
近义词:  press, pressure, loom, approach menacingly
反义词: retreat, withdraw
词性: verb (phrasal verb)
