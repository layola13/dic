navvying:/ˈnævɪɪŋ/
n.
挖土方；土方工程；(尤指开凿运河、铁路等的)挖土工作
The act or process of digging or excavating, especially for the construction of canals, railroads, etc.
The navvying for the new railway line took several years.  这条新铁路线的挖土工程耗时数年。
近义词:excavation, earthwork, digging
反义词:filling, backfilling
词性:名词

navvy:/ˈnævɪ/
n.
(尤指开凿运河、铁路等的)挖土工人；筑路工人
A laborer employed in excavating, especially for the construction of canals, railroads, etc.
Many navvies lost their lives during the construction of the Panama Canal.  许多挖土工人在巴拿马运河的修建过程中丧生。
近义词:laborer, worker, digger
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑supervisor, engineer等)
词性:名词

词源:
From "navigator," originally referring to laborers who dug navigation canals.  源自“navigator（航海家）”，最初指开凿运河的工人。
