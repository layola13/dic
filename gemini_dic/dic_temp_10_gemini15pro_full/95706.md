incongenial:/ˌɪnkənˈdʒiːniəl/ | adj. | 不合意的；不相宜的；志趣不相投的 | Not suitable to one's temperament or tastes; not agreeable or compatible. |  Living in the country is uncongenial to some people.  对某些人来说，住在乡下是不合意的。 | Example:  She found the atmosphere at the party quite incongenial and left early. 她在聚会上感觉格格不入，很早就离开了。| Synonyms:  unsuitable, incompatible, unconformable, unsympathetic, disagreeable,  | Antonyms:  congenial, agreeable, suitable, compatible,  | word property: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *incongēniālis, from in- "not" + congēniālis "of the same kind" (from Latin com- "with, together" + gēniālis "of birth, genial, relating to marriage").
