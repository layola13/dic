furthermost:/ˈfɜːrðəməʊst/ (UK) /ˈfɜːrðərmoʊst/ (US)| adj.|最远的；最遥远的| Situated at the greatest distance; most remote.|The furthermost reaches of the universe. 宇宙最遥远的区域。|近义词：farthest, remotest, utmost|反义词：nearest, closest|词性:形容词

词源:  From Old English *fyrðemest (unattested), superlative of *fyrðe "fourth," with intrusive -m- as in formest "first." The sense development is from "fourth" to "last" to "farthest."  The modern form is a refashioning after further.
