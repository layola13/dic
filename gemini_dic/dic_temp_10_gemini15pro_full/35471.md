coachful:/ˈkoʊtʃfəl/| adj.|充满指导的；富于指导精神的；善于指导的|Full of coaching; characterized by or providing a lot of coaching.  This word is not standard English and is rarely used. It's constructed by combining "coach" (as in a mentor or instructor) with the suffix "-ful," which typically means "full of."|Example 1:  He took a very coachful approach to managing the new team members. 他对新团队成员的管理采取了一种非常注重指导的方式。

Example 2:  She gave me some coachful advice on how to improve my presentation skills. 她就如何提高我的演讲技巧给了我一些有指导意义的建议。|近义词：mentoring, instructive, supportive, guiding|反义词:unsupportive, unhelpful, uncaring|词性:形容词
