bridleless:/ˈbraɪdl̩ləs/
adjective
无辔头的；不用缰绳的
Without a bridle.
The horse ran bridleless across the field.  那匹马没有辔头地穿过田野。
Near Synonyms: unrestrained, unbridled, free
Antonyms: bridled, restrained, controlled
词性: adjective


bridleless:/ˈbraɪdl̩ləs/
adverb
无辔头地；不用缰绳地
In a bridleless manner.
She rode bridleless through the forest. 她不用缰绳骑马穿过森林。
Near Synonyms: freely, unrestrainedly
Antonyms: restrainedly, controlledly
词性: adverb


词源:
From bridle + -less.  Bridle comes from Old English *brigdel, from Proto-Germanic *brigdila-.
