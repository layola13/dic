gildings:/ˈɡɪldɪŋz/
noun
镀金，涂金；虚饰，虚伪的外表
The act of covering something with a thin layer of gold, or something that appears valuable but is actually superficial.
The ornate gildings on the picture frame added to its grandeur.  画框上华丽的镀金增添了它的庄严。
The politician's promises were just gildings, hiding his true intentions. 这位政客的承诺只是虚饰，掩盖了他的真实意图。
synonyms: gilding, ornamentation, embellishment, decoration, veneer
antonyms: plainness, simplicity, austerity
