alcarraza:/ˌælkəˈrɑːsə/| n.| 阿尔卡拉萨(一种西班牙多孔陶土水罐) |A porous earthenware pitcher, originally from Spain, used for cooling water by evaporation.|The alcarraza kept the water cool even on the hottest days.  阿尔卡拉萨即使在最热的日子里也能保持水凉爽。|Synonyms: water jug, pitcher, cooler|Antonyms:  heater, boiler|noun
