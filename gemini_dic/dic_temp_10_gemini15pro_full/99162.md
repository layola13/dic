interchanging:/ˌɪntərˈtʃeɪndʒɪŋ/
interchanging| adj.| 交互的，互换的|The act of exchanging or swapping things; reciprocal giving and receiving.|The two groups were interchanging ideas and information. 这两个团体正在交换想法和信息。|近义词：exchanging, swapping, alternating, rotating|反义词:fixed, static|词性:形容词
interchange:/ˌɪntərˈtʃeɪndʒ/
interchange| n. v.| 交换，互换；立交桥|An act of giving and receiving something reciprocally; a junction of two or more highways by a system of separate levels.|We had a lively interchange of ideas. 我们进行了热烈的思想交流。|近义词：exchange, swap, trade|反义词:retention|词性:名词,动词

词源:
From inter- + change.  "Inter-" prefix meaning "between, among, mutually, reciprocally." "Change" from Old French "changier" meaning "to change, alter."
