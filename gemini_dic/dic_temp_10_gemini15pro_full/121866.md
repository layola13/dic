misresemblance:/ˌmɪsrɪˈzɛmbləns/
noun
不像，相异，差异
lack of resemblance; dissimilarity.
例句：The misresemblance between the twins was striking. 这对双胞胎之间的差异令人吃惊。

例句：There's a marked misresemblance between the two portraits.  这两幅肖像之间有明显的差异。

近义词：dissimilarity, difference, unlikeness, disparity, divergence
反义词：resemblance, similarity, likeness, similitude

Etymology:
From mis- + resemblance.  "Mis-" is a prefix of Old English and Old French origin meaning "wrong," "bad," "wrongly," or "badly." "Resemblance" comes from Old French "resemblance" and ultimately from Latin "similis" meaning "like."
