dreamish:/ˈdriːmɪʃ/| adj.|梦幻般的；爱幻想的；不切实际的|Given to daydreaming; not practical.  Characterized by a dreamy or fanciful quality.|She had a dreamish look in her eyes as she listened to the music. 她听着音乐，眼神里充满了梦幻般的神采。|Synonyms: dreamy, fanciful, imaginative, impractical, unrealistic, visionary|Antonyms: practical, realistic, down-to-earth|adjective
