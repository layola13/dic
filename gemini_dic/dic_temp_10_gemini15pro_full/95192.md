inaugural: /ɪˈnɔːɡjərəl/ (US), /ɪˈnɔːɡjʊrəl/ (UK) | adj. | 就职的，开幕的 | Marking the beginning of an institution, activity, or period of office; first |  The president's inaugural address was well received. 总统的就职演说很受欢迎。| synonyms: first, initial, opening, introductory | antonyms: final, closing, last | adjective

inaugural: /ɪˈnɔːɡjərəl/ (US), /ɪˈnɔːɡjʊrəl/ (UK) | n. | 就职演说，开幕词 | An inaugural speech or ceremony | The inaugural was held in the grand hall. 就职典礼在大会堂举行。| synonyms:  opening ceremony, initiation | antonyms: closing ceremony, farewell | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *inauguralis, from Latin inaugurare (“to take omens from the flight of birds, consecrate, install in office”), from in- (“in, on”) + augurare (“to foretell, predict”), from augur (“soothsayer, diviner”).
