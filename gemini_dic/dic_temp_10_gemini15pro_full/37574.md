communalist: /ˈkɒmjʊnəlɪst/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmjʊnəlɪst/ (US)| n.|宗派主义者；地方自治主义者；公社成员|A person who advocates or participates in communalism, often characterized by prioritizing the interests of one's own religious or ethnic community over others, sometimes leading to violence or discrimination.|The communalist stirred up hatred between the two groups.  宗派主义者煽动两组之间的仇恨。|The rise of communalist violence is a serious concern. 宗派主义暴力的兴起是一个严重的问题。|近义词：sectarian, partisan, bigot|反义词：universalist, humanist|词性: noun

communalist: /ˈkɒmjʊnəlɪst/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmjʊnəlɪst/ (US)| adj.|地方自治主义的；公社的|Relating to or characteristic of communalism or a commune.|Communalist tensions are high in the region. 该地区宗派紧张局势加剧。|The group established a communalist lifestyle.  这个团体建立了一种公社式的生活方式。|近义词：communal, collective|反义词：individualistic, private|词性: adjective
