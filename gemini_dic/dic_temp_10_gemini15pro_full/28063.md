capercaillie:/ˌkæpərˈkeɪli/ | n. |西方松鸡| A large, black grouse, Tetrao urogallus, found in coniferous forests of Eurasia. It is the largest member of the grouse family. |  The capercaillie strutted proudly through the forest. 西方松鸡骄傲地在森林中昂首阔步。 | Example 2:  The male capercaillie has a distinctive fan-shaped tail. 雄性西方松鸡有一条独特的扇形尾巴。 | Synonym: wood grouse, heather cock | Antonym: N/A | Noun

Etymology:
From the Scottish Gaelic *capall-coille*, meaning "horse of the wood".
