augur:/ˈɔːɡər/ (US) /ˈɔːɡə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|预言家，预兆；预言，预示|A person who predicts the future, especially by interpreting signs or omens; to be a sign of something that will happen in the future. |例句：The ancient Romans consulted augurs before making important decisions. 古罗马人在做重要决定之前会咨询预言家。
