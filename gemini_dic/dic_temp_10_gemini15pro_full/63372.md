entomophilous: /ˌɛntəˈmɒfɪləs/ | adj. | 虫媒的 | Pollinated by insects.  Relating to or describing plants that are pollinated by insects. | Example 1: Many brightly colored flowers are entomophilous, attracting bees and butterflies. 许多颜色鲜艳的花都是虫媒花，吸引蜜蜂和蝴蝶。

Example 2:  The orchid's intricate structure is a clear indication of its entomophilous nature.  兰花复杂的结构清楚地表明了它的虫媒性。| 近义词: insect-pollinated | 反义词: anemophilous (风媒的), hydrophilous (水媒的) | 词性: adjective
