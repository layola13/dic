also:/ˈɔːlsoʊ/ (US) /ˈɔːlsəʊ/ (UK)| adv.|也，此外，还|Used to add another fact or piece of information to what you have already said.|She's a teacher and also a writer. 她是一位老师，也是一位作家。|Synonyms: too, as well, in addition, besides, moreover|Antonyms: neither, nor|adverb
