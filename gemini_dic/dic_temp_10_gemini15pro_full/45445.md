curari:/kjʊˈrɑːriː/ /kʊˈrɑːriː/ | n. |箭毒，箭毒碱|A highly toxic substance extracted from certain South American plants, traditionally used as an arrow poison.|The surgeon used curari to paralyze the patient's muscles during the operation. 手术过程中，外科医生使用箭毒来麻痹病人的肌肉。|Synonyms: curare, arrow poison|Antonyms: antidote|noun


curarine:/kjʊˈrɑːriːn/ /kʊˈrɑːriːn/ | n. |箭毒碱，箭毒素|The main active alkaloid in curare, responsible for its paralytic effects.|Curarine blocks nerve impulses to the muscles. 箭毒碱阻断神经冲动到达肌肉。|Synonyms:  tubocurarine, d-tubocurarine|Antonyms:  neostigmine|noun
