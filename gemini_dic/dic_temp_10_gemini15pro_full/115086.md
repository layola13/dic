manfully:/ˈmænfəli/| adv.|勇敢地，坚决地|In a brave and determined manner, typically used of men.|He faced his accusers manfully. 他勇敢地面对了他的指控者。|近义词：bravely, courageously, resolutely, boldly, valiantly|反义词：cowardly, timidly, fearfully|词性:副词

词源:
From *man* (noun) + *-ful* (suffix) + *-ly* (suffix).  The *-ful* suffix creates adjectives meaning "full of" or "characterized by," and the *-ly* suffix turns adjectives into adverbs.
