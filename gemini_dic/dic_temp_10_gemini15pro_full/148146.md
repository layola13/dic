oversensitized: /ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsɪtaɪzd/ | adj. | 过于敏感的，过敏的 | Excessively sensitive; easily irritated or offended.  Made to react to things more strongly than is usual or desirable. |  She's become oversensitized to criticism after the incident.  她在那件事之后对批评变得过于敏感。| Near Synonyms: hypersensitive, touchy, irritable, thin-skinned | Antonyms: insensitive, thick-skinned, impassive | Word Form: adjective

oversensitized: /ˌoʊvərˈsɛnsɪtaɪzd/ | adj. | 过于敏感的，过敏的 |  Her skin was oversensitized by the sun.  她的皮肤被阳光晒得过敏了。| Near Synonyms: allergic, reactive | Antonyms: tolerant, resistant | Word Form: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + sensitized, the past participle of sensitize.  "Sensitize" comes from "sensitive," ultimately from Latin "sentire" (to feel).
