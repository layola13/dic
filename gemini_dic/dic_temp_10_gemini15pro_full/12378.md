ashram: /ˈɑːʃrəm/ (US), /ˈæʃrəm/ (UK) | n. | 静修处，道场 | A secluded place of religious retreat or instruction, typically associated with Hinduism. |  He spent several months meditating at an ashram in India. 他在印度的一个静修处冥想了几个月。

|  She found peace and tranquility at the ashram. 她在道场找到了平静和安宁。

| Synonym: monastery, hermitage, retreat, sanctuary | Antonym: city, metropolis | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Sanskrit आश्रम (āśrama), meaning "hermitage," "religious retreat,"  from श्रम् (śram) "to exert oneself," "to toil."
