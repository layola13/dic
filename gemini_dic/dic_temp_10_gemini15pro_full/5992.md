ambushes:/ˈæmbʊʃɪz/ (plural) /ˈæmbʊʃ/ (singular)
noun|埋伏，伏击；埋伏地点|A surprise attack by people lying in wait in a concealed position.|The soldiers lay in ambush in the woods, waiting for the enemy to pass. 士兵们埋伏在树林里，等待敌人经过。|
ambush:/ˈæmbʊʃ/ (singular)
verb|埋伏，伏击|To attack suddenly and unexpectedly from a concealed position.|The rebels ambushed a convoy of government troops. 叛军伏击了一支政府军的车队。|
ambush:/ˈæmbʊʃ/ (singular)
noun|埋伏，伏击；埋伏地点|A surprise attack by people lying in wait in a concealed position.|The troops were caught in an ambush. 部队中了埋伏。 |近义词：trap, snare, waylay,  ambuscade|反义词：open attack, frontal assault|
