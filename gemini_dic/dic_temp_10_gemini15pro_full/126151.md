mutism: /ˈmjuːtɪzəm/ | n. | 缄默症，沉默症 | Inability to speak, typically as a result of a psychological condition. |  The trauma left him with selective mutism.  这创伤使他患上了选择性缄默症。| synonyms: silence, speechlessness | antonyms: loquacity, talkativeness | noun
