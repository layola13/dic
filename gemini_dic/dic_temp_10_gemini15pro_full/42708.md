countertransports:/ˈkaʊntərˌtrænsˌpɔːrts/ | n. |反向转运，逆向运输| The simultaneous transport of two substances across a membrane in opposite directions.  This can be either an antiport or an exchange. |  Countertransports of sodium and calcium ions help regulate cellular calcium levels. 钠离子和钙离子的反向转运有助于调节细胞内的钙水平。| synonyms: antiport, exchange | antonyms: symport, cotransport | noun
