electoress:/ɪˈlɛktrɪs/ | n. | 女选帝侯；有选举权的妇女 | A woman who has the right to vote, especially for an emperor or other sovereign ruler.  Historically, this term specifically referred to a woman who was a member of the electoral college of the Holy Roman Empire. |  In the 18th century, the Electress of Saxony was a major patron of the arts.  在18世纪，萨克森的女选帝侯是艺术的主要赞助人。|  voter, constituent | elector | noun
