e-tickets:/ˈiːˌtɪkɪts/
n.
电子客票
Electronic tickets; tickets purchased and delivered electronically, eliminating the need for a physical ticket.
I purchased my e-tickets online and showed them on my phone at the gate. 我在网上购买了电子机票，并在登机口用手机出示了它们。

The airline offered a discount for booking e-tickets in advance.  这家航空公司对提前预订电子机票提供折扣。

Synonym: online tickets, digital tickets
Antonym: paper tickets, physical tickets
noun
