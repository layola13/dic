antipathize:/ænˈtɪpəθaɪz/ | v. | 反感；厌恶；| To feel or express strong dislike or aversion towards someone or something.  To share the feelings of dislike or aversion of another. |  He antipathized with the politician's views on immigration. 他很反感那位政治家对移民问题的看法。

She antipathized with her friend's sadness after the breakup.  她对朋友分手后的悲伤感同身受（此处指共同拥有负面情绪）。 | 近义词: dislike, detest, abhor, loathe | 反义词: sympathize, empathize, like, love | 词性: verb
