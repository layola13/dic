oversuspiciousnesses:/ˌoʊvər səˈspɪʃəsnɪsɪz/
|n.|过度怀疑，过分猜疑|The state of being excessively suspicious; undue distrust.|His oversuspiciousnesses made it difficult for him to form close relationships. 他过度的猜疑让他难以建立亲密关系。|近义词: paranoia, distrust, mistrust|反义词: trust, confidence|词性:名词

oversuspiciousness:/ˌoʊvər səˈspɪʃəsnɪs/
|n.|过度怀疑，过分猜疑|The state of being excessively suspicious; undue distrust.|Her oversuspiciousness led her to believe everyone was plotting against her. 她的过度怀疑让她觉得每个人都在密谋陷害她。|近义词: paranoia, distrust, mistrust|反义词: trust, confidence|词性:名词

oversuspicious:/ˌoʊvər səˈspɪʃəs/
|adj.|过度怀疑的，过分猜疑的|Inclined to suspect too readily; unduly distrustful.|He was oversuspicious of strangers. 他对陌生人过度怀疑。|近义词: paranoid, distrustful, mistrustful|反义词: trusting, confident|词性:形容词


词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "suspicious" (from Latin suspiciosus, from suspicere "to suspect") + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).
