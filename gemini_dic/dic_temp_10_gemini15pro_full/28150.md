capitule:/ˈkæpɪtʃuːl/| n.|小头状花序，小节|A capitulum is a type of inflorescence (flower cluster) in which many small, stalkless flowers are arranged compactly on a flattened, rounded, or elongated receptacle. |The dandelion has a capitulum. 蒲公英有一个头状花序。|Synonym: flower head|Antonym: N/A|noun
capitule:/ˈkæpɪtʃuːl/| v.|（使）形成头状花序|To form or arrange into a capitulum. |The flowers capitulate in the spring. 这些花在春天形成头状花序。|Synonym: cluster, head|Antonym: N/A|verb
