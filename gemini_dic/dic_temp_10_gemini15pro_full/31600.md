chasteningly:/ˈtʃeɪsənɪŋli/ | adv. | 惩戒地，磨炼地，使人反省地 | In a manner that humbles or subdues someone, often through discipline or misfortune.  It implies a sobering effect, often leading to reflection and improved behavior. | The defeat was a chasteningly experience for the team. 这场失败对团队来说是一次磨炼的经历。|  The teacher spoke chasteningly to the misbehaving student. 老师严厉地对行为不端的同学说话。 | Synonyms: humblingly, soberingly, subduingly | Antonyms: encouragingly, upliftingly |词性: 副词

chasten:/ˈtʃeɪsn/ | v. | 惩戒，磨炼，使反省 | To correct by punishment or suffering; to subdue or restrain.  |  The experience chastened him. 这段经历使他变得谦逊。|  He was chastened by the criticism.  他因批评而反省。| Synonyms: discipline, subdue, humble | Antonyms: encourage, praise, embolden |词性: 动词

词源: From Old French *chastier, from Latin castigāre "to punish, correct, chastise," frequentative of castīgāre "to purify, correct."
