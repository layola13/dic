disproportionableness:/ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːʃənəblnəs/
n.
不相称；不成比例；不均衡
The state or quality of being disproportionate; lack of proportion.
The disproportionableness of her reaction to the minor incident surprised everyone. 她对这件小事的反应如此过度，让每个人都感到惊讶。
Near Synonyms:  disproportion, imbalance, inequality, disparity, asymmetry
Antonyms:  proportion, balance, equality, symmetry, parity
词性: 名词
