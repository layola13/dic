anaka: /ɑːˈnɑːkə/ | n. | 长老，酋长 | A respected elder or chief, especially in Polynesian cultures.  |  例句：The anaka called a meeting of the tribe.  长老召集了部落会议。

例句：The villagers sought the anaka's wisdom. 村民们寻求长老的智慧。


近义词：elder, chief, leader | 反义词: follower, subordinate | 词性: noun
