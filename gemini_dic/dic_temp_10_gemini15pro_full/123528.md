monotower: /ˈmɒnəʊtaʊər/ (UK) /ˈmɑːnətaʊər/ (US)| n. |单塔,单柱塔|A tower supporting antennas for mobile phone or other wireless communication services, characterized by its single, slender structure.| The monotower blends into the surrounding landscape more effectively than a traditional lattice tower.  单塔比传统的格子塔更容易融入周围的景观。| The council approved the construction of a new monotower to improve mobile phone reception.  委员会批准建造一座新的单塔以改善手机信号。|Synonyms: cell tower, mobile phone mast, radio tower, telecommunications tower|Antonyms: N/A|noun
