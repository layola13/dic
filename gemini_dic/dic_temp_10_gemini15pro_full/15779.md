baler:/ˈbeɪlər/| n.|打包机，压捆机|A machine used to compress and bind materials such as hay, straw, paper, or cotton into compact bales for storage or transport.|The farmer used a baler to make hay bales for the winter.|近义词：bundler, compactor|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "shredder" in a loose sense)|noun
