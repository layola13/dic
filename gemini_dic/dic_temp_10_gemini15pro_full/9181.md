antinomy:/ænˈtɪnəmi/ | n. |矛盾，对立，二律背反 |A contradiction between two beliefs or conclusions that are in themselves reasonable; a paradox. |例句：There is an antinomy between determinism and free will. 决定论和自由意志之间存在着一种矛盾。

例句：The philosopher explored the antinomies of reason. 这位哲学家探讨了理性的二律背反。|近义词：contradiction, paradox, conflict, dichotomy, opposition |反义词：agreement, harmony, consistency, concord, unity |词性: 名词
