hansa:/ˈhɑːnsə/ | n. | 汉萨同盟；汉萨商船 | A medieval league of North German towns and cities formed for mutual aid and the protection of trade. |  The Hanseatic League, or Hansa, was a powerful trading alliance. 汉萨同盟，或称汉萨，是一个强大的贸易联盟。| The hansa traded extensively throughout Northern Europe. 汉萨商船在北欧各地进行广泛的贸易。 | synonyms: league, alliance, confederation | antonyms:  division, separation | noun
