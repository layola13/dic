coiffures:/kwɑːˈfjʊərz/
noun
发型，发式
A style of hair; especially a woman's hairstyle, often elaborate and fashionable.
Her elaborate coiffures always turned heads at parties. 她的精致发型在派对上总是引人注目。
synonyms:hairstyles, hairdos, dos
antonyms:unstyled hair, messy hair
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French coiffure, from coiffer "to dress (one's hair)," from Old French coife "head covering." Ultimately from Latin cofea "basket, helmet."
