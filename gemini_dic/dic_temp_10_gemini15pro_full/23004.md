boughless:/ˈbaʊləs/| adj.|无枝的，没有大树枝的|Lacking boughs or large branches; without boughs.  Referring to a tree or landscape that is devoid of branches, often creating a stark or barren appearance.|The boughless trees stood like sentinels against the winter sky. 冬天无枝的树木像哨兵一样矗立在天空中。|The boughless landscape appeared desolate and unforgiving.  没有枝干的景象显得荒凉而无情。|bare, branchless|leafy, branched|adjective
