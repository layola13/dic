isigi:/ɪˈsiːɡi/| n.| 伊西吉（一种非洲乐器）| A type of musical instrument from Africa, a notched friction idiophone played by rubbing a stick along its notched surface. |例句：The isigi player created a rhythmic sound. 伊西吉演奏者创造了一种有节奏的声音。|例句：The isigi is a traditional instrument in some African cultures.  伊西吉在一些非洲文化中是一种传统乐器。 |近义词：friction idiophone, notched instrument|反义词: (无直接反义词，可考虑electronic instrument 电子乐器) |词性:noun
