inpatients:/ˈɪnˌpeɪʃənts/ | n. | 住院病人 | Patients who stay in a hospital for medical treatment. | The hospital has a separate wing for inpatients.  这家医院有一个单独的住院病人病房。|  The number of inpatients increased dramatically during the flu season. 流感季节住院病人数量急剧增加。 | synonyms: hospitalized patients | antonyms: outpatients | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "in" + "patients". "In" meaning within or inside, and "patients" referring to people receiving medical care.
