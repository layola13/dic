overfondled: /ˌoʊvərˈfɒndəld/ (British) /ˌoʊvərˈfɑːndəld/ (American)| adj.|过度爱抚的，过分溺爱的|Having been fondled excessively, often with negative connotations implying inappropriate or unwelcome touching.|The child seemed overfondled and clingy.| 近义词：over-pampered, over-indulged, spoiled| 反义词：neglected, ignored| 词性:形容词

Etymology:

"Over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi, meaning "above, over, beyond") + "fondle" (from "fond," meaning "foolishly affectionate," from 16th century, perhaps related to "foolish").  The combination suggests an excessive degree of fondling.
