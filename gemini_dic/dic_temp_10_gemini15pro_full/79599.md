glorifications:/ˌɡlɔːrɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/| n. |荣耀化，颂扬，赞美|The act of glorifying something or someone; extolling the virtues or greatness of something or someone.  The glorifications of war are dangerous and misleading. |例句：The book is full of glorifications of violence.  这本书充满了对暴力的颂扬。

例句：His glorifications of the past annoyed everyone. 他对过去的赞美让每个人都很烦。|近义词：praises, exaltations, celebrations, encomiums |反义词: condemnations, criticisms, denunciations |词性:noun, plural
