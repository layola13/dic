midbrain:/ˈmɪdbreɪn/| n.|中脑|The central part of the brainstem, developing from the middle of the primitive or embryonic brain. It is responsible for eye movement, among other functions.|Damage to the midbrain can affect vision and hearing. 中脑损伤会影响视力和听力。|近义词:mesencephalon|反义词:None|词性:noun
Etymology: mid + brain.  "Mid" meaning middle and "brain" referring to the organ.  The term reflects its anatomical location within the brain.
