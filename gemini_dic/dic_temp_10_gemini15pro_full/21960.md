bogs:/bɑːɡz/ /bɒɡz/
n.|沼泽；泥塘；厕所|A piece of wet, spongy ground; a swamp or marsh. Also, informal British term for toilet. |The hikers got lost in the bogs.  徒步旅行者在沼泽中迷路了。| synonyms: marshes, swamps, mires, fens, quagmires | antonyms:  dry land, desert
n.|厕所 (英式非正式)|Informal British term for toilet. |I need to use the bogs. 我需要用一下厕所。| synonyms: loo, lavatory, toilet, restroom| antonyms: None

bog:/bɑːɡ/ /bɒɡ/
n.|沼泽；泥塘|A piece of wet, spongy ground; a swamp or marsh.|The car got stuck in the bog. 车陷在泥沼里了。| synonyms: marsh, swamp, mire, fen, quagmire | antonyms: dry land, desert
v.|陷入泥沼；使陷入困境|To become stuck in mud or mire; to cause to become stuck or impeded. |The heavy rain bogged down the truck.  大雨使卡车陷入了泥潭。| synonyms: mire, entangle, stall, impede | antonyms: free, extricate
