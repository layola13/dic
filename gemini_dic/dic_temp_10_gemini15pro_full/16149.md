banishments:/ˈbænɪʃmənts/
n.
放逐，驱逐，流放
the act or state of being banished; exile.
The king ordered the banishments of several of his rivals. 国王下令放逐他的几个竞争对手。

近义词:expulsions, exiles, deportations
反义词:admissions, welcomes, repatriations
词性:名词
