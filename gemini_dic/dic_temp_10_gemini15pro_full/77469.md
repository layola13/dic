gayne:/ɡeɪn/ | n. |  收益，利润；获得，赢得 | Something that is gained; profit or advantage. | He invested his money hoping for a substantial gayne. 他投资了他的钱，希望能获得丰厚的收益。 | 近义词: profit, earnings, winnings, gain | 反义词: loss, forfeiture | 词性: 名词

词源:  Middle English *gayne*, *geyn*, from Old North French *gain*, *gayn* "profit, advantage," from *gaignier* "to cultivate, till, work, earn," perhaps of Germanic origin. Related to Old French *gaaignier* (see gain (v.)).  The original spelling *gayne* is now archaic and usually replaced by *gain*.
