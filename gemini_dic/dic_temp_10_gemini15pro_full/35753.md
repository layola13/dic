cochleary:/ˈkɒkliəri/ (US) /kɒˈkliːəri/ (UK)| adj.|耳蜗的，蜗牛状的|Relating to the cochlea (a spiral-shaped cavity forming part of the inner ear) or shaped like a snail shell.|The cochlear implant significantly improved her hearing. 耳蜗植入物显著改善了她的听力。|The medicine is administered directly into the cochlear duct.  这种药物直接注入耳蜗管。|Synonyms: spiral, helical, turbinate|Antonyms: linear, straight|adjective
