dentated:/ˈdɛnˌteɪtɪd/
adjective
齿状的，有齿的
Having a toothed margin or toothlike projections.
The leaf was dentated, with small, sharp teeth along its edge.  这片叶子是齿状的，边缘有小而尖的齿。
The ancient coin had a dentated edge. 这枚古钱币的边缘呈锯齿状。
serrated, toothed, notched
smooth, even
