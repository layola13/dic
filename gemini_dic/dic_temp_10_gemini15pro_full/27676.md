candleberry:/ˈkænd(ə)lˌberi/ | n. | 月桂果；杨梅；蜡烛树 | A shrub or tree bearing waxy berries that can be used to make candles.  A fragrant North American shrub (Morella pensylvanica syn. Myrica pensylvanica) of the sweet gale family, bearing small grayish-white waxy berries that were formerly used in candlemaking. Also called bayberry, wax myrtle. | The early settlers used candleberry to make fragrant candles. 早期的定居者用月桂果制作香薰蜡烛。 | The scent of candleberry filled the air. 空气中弥漫着月桂果的香味。 | synonyms: bayberry, wax myrtle, tallow shrub | antonyms: N/A | noun
