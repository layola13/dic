complimenter:/ˈkɒmplɪˌmɛntər/ (US) /ˈkɒmplɪmentər/ (UK)| n. |恭维者；称赞者|A person who gives compliments; someone who expresses praise or admiration.|She's a skillful complimenter, always knowing the right thing to say. 她是一位善于恭维的人，总是知道该说什么。|近义词：praiser, flatterer|反义词：critic, detractor|词性:名词

Etymology:
From the verb "compliment," which comes from French "complimenter" (17th century), ultimately from Italian "complimentare," meaning "to comply with the formalities."  The "-er" suffix denotes the agent or one who performs the action.
