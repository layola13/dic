outvie:/ˌaʊtˈvaɪ/
verb
胜过，超越
surpass, exceed, or defeat in competition
She outvied all her rivals in the competition. 她在这个比赛中胜过了所有对手。
近义词: surpass, exceed, outdo, beat, triumph over, eclipse
反义词: lose to, be defeated by, fall behind
词性: verb
