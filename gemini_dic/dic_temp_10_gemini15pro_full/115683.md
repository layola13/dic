margay:/ˈmɑːrɡeɪ/ (US), /ˈmɑːɡaɪ/ (UK)| n. |虎猫|A small, spotted wild cat native to Central and South America, known for its arboreal lifestyle and long tail.|The margay is an excellent climber, able to rotate its ankles 180 degrees. 虎猫是很棒的攀爬者，它的脚踝可以旋转180度。| The margay stalked its prey silently through the dense rainforest canopy. 虎猫在茂密的雨林树冠中悄悄地跟踪猎物。|近义词:ocelot, tree ocelot|反义词:None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Portuguese *margai*, from Tupi *mbaraka'i* meaning "small wild cat."
