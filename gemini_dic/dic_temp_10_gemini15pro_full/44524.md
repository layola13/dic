crossposts:/ˈkrɒspəʊsts/ (British) /ˈkrɔːspoʊsts/ (American)| n. |交叉帖子，跨平台发布|The act of posting the same message or content to multiple online forums, social media platforms, or other websites.|She increased her reach by using crossposts on several social media sites. 她通过在几个社交媒体网站上交叉发布来扩大影响范围。|synonyms:  shared posts, syndicated content, multi-posting|antonyms: original post, unique content|noun
