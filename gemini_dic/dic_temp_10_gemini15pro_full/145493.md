overclog:/ˌoʊvərˈklɑːɡ/ | v. |阻塞，堵塞|to clog or fill to excess; to obstruct or overload something. | The pipes were overclogged with hair. 管道被头发堵塞了。| The excessive amount of data overclogged the network. 过量的数据堵塞了网络。 |近义词：block, choke, congest, obstruct |反义词: clear, unclog |词性:动词

overclog:/ˌoʊvərˈklɑːɡ/ | n. |堵塞，阻塞(较少用作名词) |The state of being overclogged; an excessive accumulation or blockage. | The overclog in the drain caused the sink to overflow.  排水管的堵塞导致水槽溢出。|  We experienced an overclog of the system due to the sudden surge in traffic. 由于流量突然激增，我们经历了系统堵塞。|近义词：blockage, obstruction |反义词: clearance |词性:名词
