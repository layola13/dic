baksheesh: /bækˈʃiːʃ/ | n. | 小费，赏钱，贿赂 | A tip or gratuity, especially in some Middle Eastern and Asian countries. It can also refer to a bribe. |  Example 1: He gave the porter a small baksheesh for carrying his luggage. 他给了搬运工一小笔小费来搬运行李。

Example 2:  The official hinted that a little baksheesh would expedite the process.  这位官员暗示，一点小费可以加快办理速度。 | synonym: tip, gratuity, bribe, kickback | antonym:  penalty, fine | noun
