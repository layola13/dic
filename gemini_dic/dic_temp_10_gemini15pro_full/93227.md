iia:/ˈaɪ iː ˈeɪ/ |abbr.| Independent Impact Assessment (独立影响评估)| An independent impact assessment (IIA) is an evaluation conducted by an external party to determine the likely effects of a proposed project or policy.| Example: The project requires an iia before it can proceed.  这个项目需要进行独立影响评估才能继续进行。| Example:  The iia showed that the project would have a positive impact on the local economy. 独立影响评估显示该项目将对当地经济产生积极影响。 |Synonym: independent evaluation, external review| Antonym: internal review, self-assessment| noun
