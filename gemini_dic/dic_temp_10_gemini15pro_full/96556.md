indorser:/ɪnˈdɔːrsər/ /ɪnˈdɔːrsɚ/ | n. |背书人；签注者|Someone who signs their name on the back of a check or other financial document to transfer ownership or guarantee payment.| John was the indorser of the check.  约翰是这张支票的背书人。| synonym: signer, guarantor | antonym:  N/A | noun

indorse:/ɪnˈdɔːrs/ /ɪnˈdɔːrs/ | vt. |背书；签注；赞同|To sign one's name on the back of a check or other financial document to transfer ownership or guarantee payment; to support or approve something.|  She indorsed the check over to her daughter. 她把支票背书给了她女儿。 He indorsed the candidate for mayor. 他支持这位市长候选人。 | synonym: sign, approve, support, endorse | antonym: oppose, reject | verb
