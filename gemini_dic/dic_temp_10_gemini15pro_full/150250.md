pander:/ˈpændər/ (US), /ˈpændə(r)/ (UK)| vi. vt. n.|迎合；怂恿；拉皮条| to gratify or exploit the weaknesses of others; to cater basely to the desires of others |例句：The tabloid press panders to the public's appetite for scandal. 小报迎合公众对丑闻的渴望。|近义词：indulge, cater to, gratify, exploit|反义词：challenge, confront, resist|verb

pander:/ˈpændər/ (US), /ˈpændə(r)/ (UK)| n.|迎合者；怂恿者；拉皮条的人|a person who panders|例句：He was a pander to the rich and powerful. 他是富人和权贵的迎合者。|近义词：flatterer, sycophant|反义词：critic, challenger|noun
