dms:/ˌdiː ɛmˈɛs/ |n.| 度分秒; 十进制度分秒 |Decimal Minute Second (DMS) is a way of expressing latitude and longitude coordinates as degrees, minutes, and seconds.  It's a traditional method, contrasted with decimal degrees. | Example: The coordinates of the location are 37°23'24"N, 122°02'54"W (latitude 37 degrees, 23 minutes, 24 seconds North; longitude 122 degrees, 2 minutes, 54 seconds West).  这个地点的坐标是北纬37度23分24秒，西经122度2分54秒。| Synonym: degrees, minutes, seconds coordinate | Antonym: decimal degrees | noun
