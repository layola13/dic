epigraph: /ˈepɪɡræf/ | n. |题词，碑文，引言|A short quotation or saying at the beginning of a book or chapter, intended to suggest its theme.|The epigraph to the novel was a line from Shakespeare. 这部小说的题词引自莎士比亚的一句话。|Synonyms: quotation, inscription, motto, citation|Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider "afterword," "postscript")|noun
