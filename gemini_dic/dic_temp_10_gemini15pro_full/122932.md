momental:/moʊˈmɛntəl/| adj.|瞬间的，片刻的|Relating to a moment; momentary.|The momental flicker of a candle before it died out. 蜡烛熄灭前的瞬间闪烁。|近义词：momentary, fleeting, transient, ephemeral|反义词：lasting, enduring, permanent|词性:形容词

词源: From Late Latin *momentālis, from Latin momentum (“movement, motion; instant, importance”) + -ālis.
