biosecurity:/ˈbaɪoʊsɪˌkjʊrɪti/ /ˈbaɪəʊsɪˌkjʊərɪti/
n.
生物安全;生物安保
Protection against harmful biological agents, including pathogens, invasive species, and genetically modified organisms.
例句：Biosecurity measures are crucial to prevent the spread of disease.  生物安全措施对于防止疾病传播至关重要。

例句：The farm has strict biosecurity protocols to protect its livestock.  该农场有严格的生物安全协议来保护其牲畜。

近义词：biosafety, bioprotection
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider concepts like vulnerability or negligence)
词性:noun
