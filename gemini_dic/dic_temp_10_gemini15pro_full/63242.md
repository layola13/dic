enterplead:/ˌɛntərˈpliːd/| v.|相互辩护，互相起诉；共同答辩|To plead against each other; to make a joint plea. |例句：The two companies enterpleaded, each claiming the other was responsible for the breach of contract.  两家公司互相起诉，各自声称对方违反了合同。

例句：The defendants enterpleaded, arguing that they should not both be held liable for the same damages.  被告共同答辩，认为他们不应同时为相同的损害承担责任。|近义词：cross-plead, counterplead|反义词: N/A |词性：动词
