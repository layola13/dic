fashion-forward:/ˈfæʃən ˈfɔːrwərd/ | adj. |  时尚前卫的；走在时尚前沿的 | Interested in and following the latest fashions and trends; ahead of the current fashion trends. |  She has a fashion-forward sense of style. 她有着时尚前卫的风格。 |  The fashion-forward designs were showcased at the event.  这些前卫的设计在活动中得到了展示。 | 近义词：trendy, stylish, chic, avant-garde | 反义词：old-fashioned, outdated, traditional | 词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
fashion (n.)  from Old French "façon" (manner, shape, form)  from Latin "factio" (a making, a doing) from "facere" (to make, to do).
forward (adj.) from Old English "foreweard" (directed towards the front) from "fore" (before) + "weard" (toward).
