galvanoscopy:/ˌɡælvəˈnɒskəpi/ | n. | 电流镜检查法 | The detection of the presence of an electric current by its effect on a sensitive galvanometer.  It's a method used to test the excitability of muscles and nerves by observing the muscle contraction or sensation produced by the application of an electric current. |  Galvanoscopy was used to determine the extent of nerve damage. 电流镜检查法被用来确定神经损伤的程度。 | electromyography, electroneurography | - | noun
