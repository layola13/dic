overlaboring:/ˌoʊvərˈleɪbərɪŋ/
verb
过度劳作；过分雕琢
working too hard or elaborately; belaboring
The artist was overlaboring the details, making the painting appear stiff and unnatural.  这位艺术家过度刻画细节，使这幅画显得僵硬而不自然。
overworking, toiling excessively, straining
underworking, relaxing
v.
