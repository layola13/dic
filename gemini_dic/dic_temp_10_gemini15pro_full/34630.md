clematis: /ˈklɛməˌtɪs/ (US), /ˈklɛmətɪs/ (UK)| n. |铁线莲，藤本植物|A climbing plant with brightly coloured flowers, often grown in gardens. |例句：The clematis climbed up the trellis, covering it in beautiful purple blossoms. 铁线莲爬上棚架，开满了美丽的紫色花朵。

例句：She planted a clematis near the fence, hoping it would provide some privacy. 她在篱笆附近种了一株铁线莲，希望它能提供一些隐私。|近义词：vine, climber|反义词: (No direct antonym for a plant name) |词性:noun
