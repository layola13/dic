beefwood:/ˈbiːfwʊd/| n. |牛肉木，澳洲红木|A type of Australian tree (Casuarina equisetifolia) with reddish wood resembling beef in color.  It's also known as Australian pine or sheoak.|The beefwood tree is highly resistant to salt spray, making it a common sight along coastal areas. 牛肉木对盐雾有很强的抵抗力，因此在沿海地区很常见。

The furniture was made from sturdy beefwood. 这件家具是用结实的牛肉木做的。|近义词：sheoak, Australian pine|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
