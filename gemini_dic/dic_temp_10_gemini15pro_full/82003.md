PHRASE->
come to grips with:  认真处理，正视（问题或局面） He needs to come to grips with his gambling addiction. 他需要正视他的赌瘾问题。
get a grip on:  控制；掌握 She needs to get a grip on her spending habits. 她需要控制自己的消费习惯。
lose one's grip:  失去控制；失去理智 He seems to have lost his grip on reality. 他似乎已经失去了对现实的把握。
in the grip of:  受…的控制；受…的折磨 The country is in the grip of a severe economic crisis.  这个国家正遭受严重的经济危机。
tight grip:  紧握；牢牢控制 He kept a tight grip on the reins of power. 他牢牢地控制着权力。
