adjective
嗜金的；贪财的
Excessively greedy for gold or wealth.
The dragon, with its aurivorous nature, hoarded a mountain of gold. 这条龙，天性贪婪，囤积了一座金山。
The aurivorous dictator plundered the nation's treasury.  贪婪的独裁者掠夺了国库。
Synonyms: avaricious, greedy, covetous, rapacious
Antonyms: generous, altruistic, philanthropic
adjective

Etymology:
From Latin aurum (gold) + -vorus (eating), from vorare (to devour).
