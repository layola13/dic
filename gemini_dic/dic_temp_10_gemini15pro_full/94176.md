imperant:/ɪmˈpɛrənt/
adjective
必要的，紧急的，极重要的
Of vital importance; crucial.
例句：It is imperant that we act now. 我们现在必须采取行动。

例句：Swift action is imperant in this situation. 在这种情况下，迅速行动至关重要。

近义词：crucial, vital, essential, critical, urgent, pressing
反义词：unimportant, trivial, insignificant, unnecessary
词性：adjective

词源：From Latin *imperant- (stem of imperans), present participle of imperare (“to command”), from in- (“in, on, upon”) + parare (“to prepare, procure, order, command”).
