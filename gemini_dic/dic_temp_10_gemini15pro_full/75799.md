fumblingly:/ˈfʌmblɪŋli/
adverb
笨拙地；慌乱地
In a manner characterized by awkwardness or clumsiness, often due to nervousness or haste.
He fumblingly searched for his keys in the dark. 他在黑暗中笨手笨脚地摸索着钥匙。
clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly
deftly, skillfully, adroitly
词性: 副词

fumble:/ˈfʌmbl/
verb
摸索；笨拙地做；漏接（球）
1. To handle or search for something clumsily or awkwardly.
2. To make mistakes or perform poorly, especially in a sport.
She fumbled in her purse for her phone. 她在钱包里摸索着找手机。
The quarterback fumbled the ball. 四分卫漏接了球。
grope, fumble around
manage, handle, grasp
词性: 动词

fumble:/ˈfʌmbl/
noun
摸索；失误；漏球
An act of handling something clumsily or awkwardly, or a mistake or failure, especially in a sport.
He made a fumble in the last minute of the game. 他在比赛的最后一分钟失误了。
Her fumble for the light switch left her in darkness. 她摸索电灯开关时让自己陷入了黑暗。
blunder, mistake, error
success, accomplishment, triumph
词性: 名词
