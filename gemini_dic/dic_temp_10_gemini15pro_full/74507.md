frappe:/fræp/ | n. |冰咖啡，奶昔 |A blended iced beverage, often made with coffee, milk, and sugar.  A thick milkshake. | I ordered a caramel frappe. 我点了一杯焦糖冰咖啡。|  synonyms: iced coffee, milkshake, smoothie | antonyms: hot coffee, hot chocolate | noun

frappe:/fræp/ | v. | (使)冷却，(使)变冷  | To chill, especially a drink, by shaking or stirring with ice. |  We frapped the wine before serving it. 我们在上酒之前把酒冰镇了一下。| synonyms: chill, cool, ice | antonyms: heat, warm | verb
