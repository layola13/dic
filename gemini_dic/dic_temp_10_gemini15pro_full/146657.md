overhastening: /ˌoʊvərˈheɪstənɪŋ/ | n. | 过于匆忙，操之过急 | The act of hastening something excessively; rushing or hurrying something too much.  It implies a negative consequence due to the undue speed. | His overhastening in selling the property led to a financial loss. 他过快地出售房产导致了经济损失。|  Overhastening of the project resulted in several critical errors.  项目的过度匆忙导致了几个严重错误。 | 近义词: rushing, hurrying, precipitating | 反义词: delaying, procrastinating,  taking one's time | 词性: 名词 (derived from the verb "overhasten")
