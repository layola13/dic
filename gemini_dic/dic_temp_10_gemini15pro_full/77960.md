genteel:/dʒɛnˈtiːl/
adjective
有教养的，文雅的，彬彬有礼的
Of or relating to people of high social class; aristocratic.  Polite, refined, or respectable, often in an affected or ostentatious way.
She comes from a genteel family of old money. 她来自一个有旧钱的世家。
He had a genteel manner, always opening doors for ladies. 他举止文雅，总是为女士开门。
近义词:refined, polite, courteous, aristocratic, well-bred
反义词:vulgar, coarse, rude, unrefined, common
词性:adjective
