harmonicas:/hɑːrˈmɒnɪkəz/ (US), /hɑːˈmɒnɪkəz/ (UK)| n. |口琴（复数）|A small rectangular wind instrument with a row of metal reeds along one long edge, played by blowing or sucking air through it.|He plays the blues on his harmonicas. 他用他的口琴演奏蓝调音乐。|Synonym: mouth organ|Antonym: None|Noun

harmonica:/hɑːrˈmɒnɪkə/ (US), /hɑːˈmɒnɪkə/ (UK)| n. |口琴（单数）|A small rectangular wind instrument with a row of metal reeds along one long edge, played by blowing or sucking air through it.|The harmonica is a portable and versatile instrument. 口琴是一种便携且多功能的乐器。|Synonym: mouth organ|Antonym: None|Noun
