climb:/klaɪm/| v. n.|攀登，爬；上升，增长|To go up something using your feet and hands, or to go up something that is steep or difficult; to increase in level, amount, or number.|He climbed the stairs slowly. 他慢慢地爬上楼梯。

The temperature climbed to 30 degrees. 气温升至30度。|近义词：ascend, mount, scale|反义词：descend, fall, decline|词性:动词,名词

climb:/klaɪm/| n.|攀登，爬|An act of climbing something.|It was a difficult climb to the top of the mountain. 爬到山顶很艰难。|近义词：ascent, scaling|反义词：descent|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *climban, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch klimmen and German klimmen.
