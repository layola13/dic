deservedness:/dɪˈzɜːrvɪdnəs/| n. |应得;  当之无愧|The deservedness of her punishment is not in question.  她受到的惩罚是罪有应得，毋庸置疑。|近义词：worthiness, merit, rightfulness|反义词:unworthiness, undeservedness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From deserve + -ed + -ness.  Deserve comes from Old French *deservir*, from Latin *deservire* "serve well, deserve," from *de-* "fully" + *servire* "to serve."
