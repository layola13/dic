dissemination:/dɪˌsemɪˈneɪʃn/ | n. |传播，散布，普及|The dissemination of information is crucial in a democratic society. 信息的传播在一个民主社会中至关重要。| The internet has greatly facilitated the dissemination of knowledge. 互联网极大地促进了知识的传播。|近义词：distribution, propagation, spreading, diffusion|反义词：suppression, concealment, withholding|词性: noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin disseminatio, from disseminare “to sow, spread,” from dis- “widely” + seminare “to sow,” from semen “seed.”
