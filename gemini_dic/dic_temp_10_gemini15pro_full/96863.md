inexhalable:/ˌɪnɛksˈheɪləbl/
adjective
不可呼出的；不能呼气的
Not capable of being exhaled.  Unable to be breathed out.
例句：The dense, noxious fumes were inexhalable, trapping the miners underground.  浓密的有毒烟雾无法呼出，将矿工困在地下。

例句：She felt a strange, inexhalable pressure in her chest. 她感到胸腔里有一种奇怪的、无法呼出的压力。
近义词：nonexhalable, unbreathable
反义词：exhalable, breathable
词性：adjective

词源:
From in- (not) + exhalable (capable of being exhaled), from exhale (breathe out) + -able (capable of being).  Ultimately from Latin ex- (out) + halare (to breathe).
