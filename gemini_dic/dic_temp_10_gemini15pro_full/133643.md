nonformalistic:/ˌnɒnfɔːməˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
非形式主义的
Not adhering to formalism; characterized by a rejection of rigid rules and conventions, especially in art, literature, or philosophy.
Her nonformalistic approach to painting allowed for greater freedom of expression. 她非形式主义的绘画方法允许更大的表达自由。
The critic praised the film's nonformalistic narrative structure.  评论家称赞这部电影非形式主义的叙事结构。
Synonyms: informal, unconventional, non-traditional, experimental
Antonyms: formalistic, conventional, traditional, rigid
