electrotypic:/ɪˌlɛktrəˈtɪpɪk/
adjective
电版的；电铸的
Relating to or produced by electrotyping.
The museum has a collection of electrotypic copies of ancient coins.  博物馆收藏了一些古代硬币的电版复制品。
The printing plates were created using an electrotypic process.  印刷版是使用电铸工艺制作的。
近义词:galvanoplastic, electroformed
反义词:(No direct antonym, possibly "hand-engraved" or "original" in certain contexts)
词性:adjective
