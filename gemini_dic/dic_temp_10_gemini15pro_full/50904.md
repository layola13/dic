desertrix: /dɪˈzɜːtrɪks/
n.
female deserter; a woman who deserts (especially her husband or family)
女性逃兵；遗弃者（尤指遗弃丈夫或家庭的女人）
She was branded a desertrix when she left her husband and children. 她离开丈夫和孩子后，被 клеймили 为逃兵。
近义词: runaway, absconder, renegade
反义词: loyalist, adherent


Etymology:
From Latin *desertrix*, feminine form of desertor ("deserter"), from deserere ("to desert").
词源：
源自拉丁语 *desertrix*，desertor（“逃兵”）的阴性形式，来自 deserere（“遗弃”）。
