paddlefish:/ˈpædlˌfɪʃ/| n. |匙吻鲟|A large freshwater fish (Polyodon spathula) of the Mississippi River system, having a long, paddle-shaped snout.|例句：The paddlefish uses its snout to detect plankton. 匙吻鲟用它的鼻子探测浮游生物。

例句：Paddlefish are considered a primitive fish species. 匙吻鲟被认为是一种原始鱼类。|近义词：spoonbill cat, |反义词: N/A |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
