exhaustivity:/ɪɡˈzɔːstɪvɪti/| n.|穷尽性；彻底性；详尽性|The quality of being exhaustive; thoroughness.  The state of including everything possible; completeness.|The lawyer's exhaustivity in preparing the case ensured his client's victory. 律师在准备案件时的详尽性确保了他的客户的胜利。|Near Synonyms: completeness, thoroughness, comprehensiveness|Near Antonyms: incompleteness, inadequacy, superficiality|noun
