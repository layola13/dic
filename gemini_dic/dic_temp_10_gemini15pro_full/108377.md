law-abiding:/ˈlɔː əˈbaɪdɪŋ/
adjective
守法的，遵纪守法的
Someone who is law-abiding obeys the law.
例句：She is a law-abiding citizen. 她是一个守法的公民。

例句：He has always been a law-abiding member of the community. 他一直是社区里一个遵纪守法的成员。

近义词：legitimate, lawful, legal, licit
反义词：lawless, criminal, illegal, illicit
词性: adjective
