gleamier:/ˈɡliːmiər/| adj.|更闪亮的，更光亮的|Comparative form of gleaming: shining brightly, especially with reflected light.|The polished floor was gleamier than ever. 磨光的地板比以往任何时候都更闪亮。|例句：Her eyes were gleamier with excitement. 她的眼睛因兴奋而更加闪亮。|近义词：shinier, brighter, more radiant|反义词：duller, dimmer, darker|词性:形容词
gleam:/ɡliːm/| v. n.|闪耀，闪烁；微光|To shine brightly, especially with reflected light; a faint or brief light.|The sun gleamed on the water.  阳光在水面上闪耀。|例句：A gleam of hope appeared in his eyes.  一丝希望出现在他的眼中。|近义词：shine, glitter, sparkle, glimmer|反义词：dull, fade|词性:动词/名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *glǣm, from Proto-Germanic *glaimaz. Cognate with Old Norse gleima (“to gleam”).  Related to gloom (originally meaning "twilight").
