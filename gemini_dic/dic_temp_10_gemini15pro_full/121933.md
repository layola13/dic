misshipments:/ˌmɪsˈʃɪpmənts/| n. |错运，误运|The act of shipping goods to the wrong destination, or shipping the wrong goods.|例句：The company apologized for the misshipments and promised to rectify the error. 公司为货物错运道歉，并承诺纠正错误。

例句：Due to a labeling error, several misshipments occurred last quarter. 由于标签错误，上季度发生了多次货物错运。|近义词：misdeliveries, erroneous shipments|反义词:correct shipments, accurate deliveries|词性:名词

词源:  "misshipment" comes from the prefix "mis-" (meaning "wrong" or "badly") and the verb "ship" (meaning "to send goods by any form of transport").  Therefore, it literally means "wrongly shipped".
