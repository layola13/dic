horatian:/həˈreɪʃən/| adj.|贺拉斯的；贺拉斯风格的；乐观的，快乐的|Of or relating to the Roman poet Horace or his style, characterized by geniality and urbanity; lighthearted; optimistic.|His poetry is Horatian in its wit and grace. 他的诗歌以其机智和优雅而具有贺拉斯风格。|Synonyms: cheerful, optimistic, lighthearted, genial, urbane|Antonyms: pessimistic, gloomy, melancholic|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Horace + -an.  Horace was the name of Quintus Horatius Flaccus (65–8 BC), a leading Roman lyric poet.
