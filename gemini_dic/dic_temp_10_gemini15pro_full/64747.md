escouade:/ɛskwad/| n.|小队，班，组|A small group of people organized for a specific purpose, typically military or police.  A squad is a small tactical unit, typically consisting of 8-14 soldiers.|The escouade was sent to secure the perimeter.  这个小队被派去保卫周边地区。|Synonyms: squad, team, unit, group, detachment|Antonyms: army, crowd, multitude|noun
