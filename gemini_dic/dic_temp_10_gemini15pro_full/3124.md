aesculin:/ˈɛskjʊlɪn/ | n. |七叶苷;七叶灵|A coumarin glycoside found in the bark of the horse chestnut tree (Aesculus hippocastanum), used as a fluorescent indicator.  It exhibits blue fluorescence under ultraviolet light.|The researchers used aesculin to study the permeability of capillaries. 研究人员使用七叶苷来研究毛细血管的通透性。|Synonym: esculin|Antonym: None|noun
