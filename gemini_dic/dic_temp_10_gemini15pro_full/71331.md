flabbier:/ˈflæbiər/ | adj. | 更松弛的，更肥胖的 |  The comparative form of "flabby," meaning more loose and soft; less firm.  It often refers to body tissue, but can also describe other things that lack firmness. |  Her arms were getting flabbier as she got older. 她的手臂随着年龄的增长变得越来越松弛。|  My old sofa cushions are much flabbier than the new ones.  我的旧沙发垫比新的要松弛得多。 |  softer, looser, less firm, more flaccid | firmer, tighter, more toned, more muscular | adjective


flabby:/ˈflæbi/ | adj. | 松弛的，肥胖的 | Loose and soft; not firm. Often used to describe body tissue that lacks muscle tone. |  He had a flabby stomach. 他有一个松弛的肚子。| The mattress was old and flabby.  床垫又旧又松弛。| soft, loose, flaccid | firm, toned, muscular | adjective
