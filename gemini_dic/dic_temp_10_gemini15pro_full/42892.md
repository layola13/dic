couscous: /ˈkuːskuːs/ (US), /ˈkʊskʊs/ (UK) | n. | 库斯库斯（一种北非食物） | A North African dish of semolina steamed over a stew of meat and vegetables. |  I had couscous for dinner last night. 我昨晚吃了库斯库斯。

Couscous is a staple food throughout North Africa. 库斯库斯是整个北非的主食。
