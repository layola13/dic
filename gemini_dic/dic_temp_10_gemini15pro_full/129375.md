nomadization:/noʊˌmædɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 游牧化，游牧生活 | The process of becoming nomadic or adopting a nomadic lifestyle.  The transformation of a settled population into a mobile, migratory one. |  The nomadization of the tribe was a gradual process driven by climate change.  该部落的游牧化是一个由气候变化驱动的渐进过程。|  nomadism, migration | settlement, sedentarization | noun
