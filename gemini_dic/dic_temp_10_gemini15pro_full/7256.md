anglomania:/ˌæŋɡləˈmeɪniə/
n.|媚英症，崇英狂|An excessive admiration or fondness for England, English customs, and English manners.|She had a severe case of anglomania, decorating her house with Union Jack flags and drinking only English tea. 她患有严重的媚英症，用英国国旗装饰她的房子，只喝英国茶。|近义词: Anglophile, Britishmania|反义词: Francophilia, Germanophilia|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From French angle "English" + mania "madness, obsession". Ultimately from Latin Anglus "English" and Greek mania "madness". 源自法语angle“英语”+ mania“疯狂，痴迷”。最终来自拉丁语Anglus“英语”和希腊语mania“疯狂”。
