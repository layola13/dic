overarrange:/ˌoʊvərəˈreɪndʒ/| v. |过度安排，安排过细|To arrange or plan something in excessive detail or to an unnecessary degree.|例句：She had overarranged the trip, leaving no room for spontaneity. 她把旅行安排得过于细致，一点儿空间都没留给即兴发挥。

例句：He overarranged the children's schedule, filling every hour with activities.  他把孩子们的日程安排得满满的，每个小时都排满了活动。|近义词：overorganize, overplan|反义词: underarrange, underplan|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + arrange.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much." "Arrange" comes from Old French "arengier" meaning "to put in a rank or row."
