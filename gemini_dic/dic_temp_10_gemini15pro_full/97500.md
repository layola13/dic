ingestions:/ɪnˈdʒɛstʃənz/
n.
摄取，吸收；吃，喝
the process of taking food, drink, or another substance into the body by swallowing or absorbing it.
The patient's recent ingestions included a large amount of caffeine.  这个病人最近摄入了大量的咖啡因。

Excessive alcohol ingestion can lead to liver damage. 过量摄入酒精会导致肝损伤。

近义词:consumption, intake, absorption
反义词:excretion, elimination
词性:名词
