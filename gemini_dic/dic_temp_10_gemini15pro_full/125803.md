murkiness:/ˈmɜːrkɪnɪs/ /ˈmɝːkinɪs/ | n. |阴暗，昏暗；模糊，含糊不清|The murkiness of the water made it impossible to see the bottom. 水的浑浊使人无法看到底部。|例句：The murkiness of the situation made it difficult to make a decision.  情况的不明朗使人难以做出决定。

The murkiness of his explanation left me with more questions than answers. 他含糊不清的解释让我产生了更多疑问，而不是答案。|近义词：darkness, obscurity, dimness, vagueness, ambiguity|反义词：clarity, brightness, transparency, lucidity|词性：名词
