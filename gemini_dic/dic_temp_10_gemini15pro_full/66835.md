expatiate:/ɪkˈspeɪʃieɪt/| v. |详述，细说|To speak or write at length or in detail. |例句：The professor expatiated on the history of the Roman Empire. 教授详细讲述了罗马帝国的历史。

例句：She expatiated upon her travels in Europe. 她详细地讲述了她在欧洲的旅行。

近义词：elaborate, expand, discuss, explain|反义词:summarize, condense|词性:动词
