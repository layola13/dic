nitroxyl: /naɪˈtrɒksɪl/ | n. | 一氧化氮 |  A compound of nitrogen and oxygen with the formula HNO.  It is the one-electron reduced form of nitric oxide (NO) and is considered a reactive nitrogen species.  | Nitroxyl donors are being investigated for their potential therapeutic benefits in treating heart failure. 一氧化氮供体正被研究用于治疗心力衰竭的潜在疗效。 | Nitroxyl can react with thiols to form sulfinamides. 一氧化氮可以与硫醇反应形成亚磺酰胺。| synonyms: HNO, azanone | antonyms:  (Nitroxyl doesn't have a direct antonym as a chemical compound.  One might consider oxidizing agents as conceptual opposites, as they increase oxidation state, while nitroxyl can be considered a reductant.)  | noun
