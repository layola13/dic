gratuity: /ɡrəˈtuːɪti/ /ɡrəˈtjuːɪti/ | n. | 小费，赏钱，赠款 | A tip given to someone for their services. | Example 1: He left a generous gratuity for the waiter. 他给服务员留了一笔丰厚的小费。

Example 2:  The hotel staff received a year-end gratuity. 酒店员工收到了一笔年终奖金。 | Synonym: tip, bonus, gift | Antonym: deduction, penalty | noun
