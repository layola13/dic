corollated: /ˈkɒrəleɪtɪd/ | adj. |相关的，相互关联的 | Connected, related, or showing a mutual relationship.  Existing in or demonstrating correlation. | The amount of rainfall is strongly correlated with the growth of crops. 降雨量与作物生长密切相关。|  The study showed a positive correlation between exercise and mental health.  这项研究表明，运动与心理健康之间存在正相关。 | synonyms: related, connected, linked, associated, interdependent,  | antonyms: unrelated, unconnected, independent, dissociated | adjective
