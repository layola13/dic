coarticulation:/ˌkəʊɑːˌtɪkjʊˈleɪʃən/ (UK) /ˌkoʊɑːrˌtɪkjuˈleɪʃən/ (US)| n.|协同发音，共调|The overlapping of articulatory movements for different phones, so that the production of one phone is influenced by the preceding and following phones.  The influence of one sound on a neighboring sound.|Example: The pronunciation of "can't" is affected by coarticulation, where the /t/ is often unreleased or nasalized due to the preceding /n/. “can't”的发音受到协同发音的影响，其中/t/由于前面的/n/经常不释放或鼻音化。

Example: Coarticulation makes speech faster and more fluent but can also make it harder to understand for language learners. 协同发音使讲话更快、更流利，但也可能使语言学习者更难理解。|近义词：assimilation|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
