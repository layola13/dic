debates:/dɪˈbeɪts/ /dəˈbeɪts/
noun
辩论，讨论
a formal discussion on a particular topic in a public meeting or legislative assembly, in which opposing arguments are put forward.
The presidential debates were televised.  总统辩论进行了电视转播。

verb
(第三人称单数 debates; 现在分词 debating; 过去式 debated; 过去分词 debated)
辩论，讨论
argue about (a subject), especially in a formal manner.
They debated the question for hours.  他们就这个问题辩论了几个小时。

近义词: discussions, arguments, disputes
反义词: agreements, harmony, consensus
词性: noun, verb
