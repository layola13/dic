maharishi:/ˌmɑːhəˈrɪʃi/| n. |大师，圣人|A Hindu spiritual leader or teacher, especially one who has attained a high state of enlightenment.|例句：The Maharishi Mahesh Yogi introduced Transcendental Meditation to the West.  玛赫西·玛赫什·优济大师将超脱冥想引入了西方。

例句：Many people sought guidance from the maharishi. 许多人向这位大师寻求指导。|近义词：guru, sage, master, swami|反义词: disciple, student|词性:noun
