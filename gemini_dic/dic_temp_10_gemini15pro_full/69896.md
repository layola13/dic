fertilities:/fərˈtɪlɪtiz/| n. |肥沃度；生育力；繁殖力|The fertility of the soil is crucial for a good harvest. 土壤的肥沃度对于丰收至关重要。  The declining fertility rate is a concern for many countries.  生育率下降是许多国家关注的问题。|近义词：fecundity, productivity, richness|反义词：infertility, barrenness, sterility|词性:名词

Etymology: From Late Middle English *fertilite*, from Old French *fertilité*, from Latin *fertilitās*, from *fertilis* ("fertile").
