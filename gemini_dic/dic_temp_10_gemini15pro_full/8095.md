anthropophagous:/ˌænθrəˈpɒfəɡəs/
adjective
食人的，吃人肉的
Consuming human flesh; cannibalistic.
The tribe was rumored to be anthropophagous, a terrifying prospect for the explorers.  这个部落被谣传是食人的，这对探险者来说是一个可怕的前景。
The anthropophagous practices of the cult horrified the public.  该邪教的食人行为使公众感到震惊。
近义词:cannibalistic, man-eating
反义词:herbivorous, vegetarian
词性:adjective
词源:From Ancient Greek ἀνθρωποφάγος (anthrōpophágos, “man-eating”), from ἄνθρωπος (ánthrōpos, “human being”) + φαγεῖν (phageîn, “to eat”).
