leman:/ˈlɛmən/| n.|情人，爱人（尤指女性）|A sweetheart or lover, especially a woman.|例句：He wrote a poem for his leman. 他为他的情人写了一首诗。

例句：The king's leman held considerable power. 国王的宠妃拥有相当大的权力。|近义词：lover, sweetheart, mistress, paramour|反义词:enemy, rival|词性:noun
