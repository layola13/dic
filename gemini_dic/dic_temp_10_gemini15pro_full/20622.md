blacklead: /ˈblækˌled/ | n. | 石墨; 黑铅笔 | a soft, dark gray form of carbon that is used in pencils and as a lubricant.  |  I need a piece of blacklead to finish this drawing. 我需要一块石墨来完成这幅画。 | synonyms: graphite, plumbago | antonyms: diamond (in terms of crystalline structure) | noun
