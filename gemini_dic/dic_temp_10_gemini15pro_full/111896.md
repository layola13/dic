logroller:/ˈlɔːɡˌroʊlər/| n.|圆木滚动工；互相吹捧者；政治交易者|A person who moves logs by rolling them, often with the aid of tools. Also, someone who engages in mutual praise or political exchange of favors.|The skilled logroller navigated the river with ease.  那熟练的圆木滚动工轻松地在河上航行。

He's known as a logroller in Washington, always trading favors to get his bills passed. 他在华盛顿以政治交易者闻名，总是为了通过他的法案而互相交换好处。|近义词：wheeler-dealer, back-scratcher, horse-trader|反义词: reformer, idealist|词性:noun
