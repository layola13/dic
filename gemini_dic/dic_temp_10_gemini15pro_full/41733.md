corrosibility:/kəˌroʊzəˈbɪləti/| n.|腐蚀性，可腐蚀性|The corrosibility of the metal is a major concern.  这种金属的腐蚀性是一个主要问题。|The high corrosibility of the acid makes it dangerous to handle.  这种酸的强腐蚀性使其处理起来很危险。|近义词：corrosiveness, erodibility|反义词: resistance to corrosion, inertness|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From *corrode* (from Latin *corrodere*, "to gnaw to pieces") + *-ible* (suffix meaning "capable of being") + *-ity* (suffix forming abstract nouns).
