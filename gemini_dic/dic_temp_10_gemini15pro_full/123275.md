monoecia: /məˈniːʃə/ /məˈniːsiə/ | n. | 雌雄同株 |  A condition in plants in which the male and female reproductive organs are in separate flowers on the same plant. | Example 1:  Corn exhibits monoecia, with the tassel being the male flower and the ear being the female flower. 玉米表现出雌雄同株，雄花是雄穗，雌花是雌穗。

Example 2: Cucumbers also display monoecia, having separate male and female flowers on the same vine. 黄瓜也表现出雌雄同株，在同一藤蔓上有单独的雄花和雌花。| 近义词: monoecism | 反义词: dioecia | 词性: noun
