infringe:/ɪnˈfrɪndʒ/
verb
侵犯，违反
act so as to limit or undermine (something); encroach on
例句：They infringed copyright by copying music. 他们在复制音乐时侵犯了版权。

例句：The government infringed on their right to protest. 政府侵犯了他们抗议的权利。

近义词：violate, breach, contravene
反义词：respect, uphold, comply with
词性：v.
