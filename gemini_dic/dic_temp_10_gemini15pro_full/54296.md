disgusting:/dɪsˈɡʌstɪŋ/
adjective
令人作呕的；令人厌恶的
extremely unpleasant or unacceptable
The smell was absolutely disgusting. 这气味实在令人作呕。
近义词:revolting, repulsive, sickening, nauseating, foul, vile, abhorrent, loathsome, offensive
反义词:pleasant, agreeable, delightful, appealing
词性:adjective

disgusting:/dɪsˈɡʌstɪŋ/
词源:From Middle French desgouster, from des- (expressing reversal) + gouster "to taste," from Latin gustare.
词性:adjective
