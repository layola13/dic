enthralled:/ɪnˈθrɔːld/ | adj. | 着迷的，被迷住的 | Filled with wonder and delight; captivated. |  The children were enthralled by the magician's tricks. 孩子们被魔术师的戏法迷住了。 |  Example: She was utterly enthralled by the beauty of the sunset. 她完全被日落的美丽所迷住。 | Synonyms: captivated, fascinated, charmed, mesmerized, spellbound | Antonyms: bored, uninterested, apathetic | word property: adjective

enthrall:/ɪnˈθrɔːl/ | verb |  使着迷，迷住 | To captivate or charm completely. | The storyteller enthralled the audience with his tales of adventure.  讲故事的人用他的冒险故事迷住了听众。 | Example: The performance enthralled the audience, holding them spellbound for two hours.  这场演出让观众着迷，让他们听得如痴如醉了两个小时。 | Synonyms: captivate, fascinate, charm, mesmerize, spellbound | Antonyms: bore, repel, disgust | word property: verb
