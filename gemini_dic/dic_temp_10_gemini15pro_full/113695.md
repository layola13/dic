macled:/ˈmækəld/ | adj. |  孪晶的；双晶的 | Composed of two or more crystals intergrown in a specific manner.  | The macled crystal shimmered in the sunlight.  孪晶在阳光下闪闪发光。| Synonym: twinned | Antonym: single (crystal) | adjective

macled:/ˈmækəld/ | adj. |  斑驳的；有斑点的 |  Marked with spots or blotches of different colors. (Less common usage) | The macled butterfly wings were a beautiful sight.  斑驳的蝴蝶翅膀非常美丽。| Synonym: mottled, spotted | Antonym: uniform, plain | adjective
