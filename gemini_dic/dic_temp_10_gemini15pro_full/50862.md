deselection:/ˌdiːsɪˈlɛkʃən/
noun
取消选择，撤销选择，落选
The act of removing someone from a position, especially a political one, by not choosing them in an election.
The deselection of several long-serving MPs caused a rift in the party.  几位长期任职的议员落选导致了党内分裂。
The committee's deselection of her proposal was a major setback. 委员会否决了她的提议，这是一个重大的挫折。
Synonyms: rejection, dismissal, removal, ousting
Antonyms: selection, election, appointment
