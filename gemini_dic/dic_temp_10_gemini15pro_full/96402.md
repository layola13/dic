indispensable:/ˌɪndɪˈspɛnsəbl/ | adj. n. | 不可缺少的；必需品 |Something that is indispensable is so important or useful that it is impossible to manage without it. |  A good dictionary is indispensable for learning a new language. 一本好词典对于学习一门新语言是必不可少的。| synonyms: essential, crucial, vital, necessary | antonyms: dispensable, unnecessary, nonessential | adjective

indispensables:/ˌɪndɪˈspɛnsəblz/ | n. | 必需品 |  Plural form of indispensable, referring to essential items or people. | The camping trip required a few indispensables: a tent, sleeping bag, and a first-aid kit.  露营旅行需要一些必需品：帐篷、睡袋和急救箱。| synonyms: essentials, necessities, requisites | antonyms: nonessentials, luxuries | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin indispensabilis, from in- "not" + dispensabilis "that can be dispensed with," from dispensare "to dispense, distribute," from dis- "apart" + pensare "to weigh."
