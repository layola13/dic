overparked:/ˌoʊvərˈpɑːrkt/
adjective
过度停车的；停放过多车辆的
Too many vehicles parked in an area, often causing congestion or inconvenience.
The street was overparked, making it difficult to find a space.  这条街停了太多的车，很难找到停车位。
The parking lot was overparked, with cars double-parked and blocking the aisles. 停车场停满了车，还有很多车并排停放，堵塞了通道。
overcrowded, congested
underparked
