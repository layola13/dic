airfreight:/ˈɛərfreɪt/| n. |空运；空运货物|The goods were shipped by airfreight. 这些货物是空运的。|例句：The airfreight costs were higher than expected. 空运费用比预期的要高。

例句：We chose airfreight to ensure speedy delivery. 我们选择空运以确保快速交付。

近义词：air cargo, air shipment, air transport|反义词: sea freight, land transport|词性:noun

airfreight:/ˈɛərfreɪt/| vt. |空运|They airfreighted the package to London. 他们把包裹空运到伦敦。|例句：We airfreighted the perishable goods to avoid spoilage.  我们空运了易腐烂的货物以避免变质。

例句：The company airfreights its products all over the world.  这家公司将其产品空运到世界各地。


近义词：airlift, ship by air|反义词: ship by sea, transport by land|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From air + freight.  "Freight" comes from the Middle Dutch word "vrecht" meaning "cargo, the hire of a ship."  "Air" in this context refers to transport by aircraft.
