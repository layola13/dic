histopathologists:/ˌhɪstoʊˌpæˈθɑːlədʒɪsts/
n.
组织病理学家
A specialist in the study of diseased tissues.
The histopathologists examined the biopsy samples under a microscope. 组织病理学家在显微镜下检查了活检样本。
The histopathologists' report confirmed the presence of cancer cells. 组织病理学家的报告证实了癌细胞的存在。

近义词：pathologists, anatomical pathologists
反义词: 无


词源:
histo- (tissue) + pathology (study of disease) + -ist (one who specializes in)

词性:
Noun (Plural)
