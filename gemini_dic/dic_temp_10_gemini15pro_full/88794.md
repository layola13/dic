hoedown:/ˈhoʊˌdaʊn/| n. |乡村舞会，欢快的聚会|A lively, informal social gathering, typically with folk music and dancing.  It originated in rural areas of the United States.|例句：We're having a hoedown at the barn next weekend.  下周末我们将在谷仓举办一场乡村舞会。

例句：The hoedown was a lot of fun, with fiddles playing and everyone dancing.  乡村舞会非常有趣，有小提琴演奏，每个人都在跳舞。|近义词：party, celebration, shindig, get-together|反义词:funeral, wake|词性:noun

词源：The word "hoedown" is of American origin, likely emerging in the 19th century.  It combines "hoe," referring to a farming tool, and "down," suggesting a lively, energetic gathering, perhaps initially associated with post-harvest celebrations.
