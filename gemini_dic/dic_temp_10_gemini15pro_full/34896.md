clique: /klik/ | n. | 小集团，派系，朋党 | A small, exclusive group of people, often with shared interests or status, who tend to socialize among themselves and exclude others. |  She belonged to a clique of artists who met regularly to discuss their work. 她属于一个艺术家小圈子，他们定期聚会讨论他们的作品。

The office was full of cliques, and it was hard to feel like you belonged. 办公室里到处都是小集团，很难有归属感。

| 近义词: circle, group, coterie, faction | 反义词:  general public, masses | 词性: noun
