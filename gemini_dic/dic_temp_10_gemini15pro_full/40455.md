contravener:/ˈkɒntrəˌviːnər/ (US) /ˈkɑːntrəˌviːnər/| n.|违反者，违规者|Someone who contravenes a law, rule, or agreement.|The company was fined for being a contravener of environmental regulations.  该公司因违反环境法规而被罚款。|Synonyms: violator, infringer, transgressor, offender|Antonyms: conformist, follower, adherent|noun

contravene:/ˌkɒntrəˈviːn/ (US) /ˌkɑːntrəˈviːn/| v.|违反，违背|To go against or break a law, rule, or agreement.|Their actions contravened the terms of the treaty. 他们的行为违反了条约的条款。|Synonyms: violate, infringe, breach, break|Antonyms: comply with, obey, uphold, observe|verb
