avarice: /ˈævərɪs/ | n. | 贪婪，贪财 | Extreme greed for wealth or material gain. |  He was consumed by avarice and would stop at nothing to acquire more money. 他被贪婪吞噬，为了获取更多金钱不择手段。

The company's avarice led them to exploit their workers. 该公司的贪婪导致他们剥削工人。

| 近义词：greed, cupidity, rapacity | 反义词：generosity, altruism | 词性: noun
