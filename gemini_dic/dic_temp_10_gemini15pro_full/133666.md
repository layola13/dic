nonfossil:/nɒnˈfɒsɪl/ /nɑːnˈfɑːsəl/
adjective

Not derived from fossilized organic matter.
The report advocates for increased investment in nonfossil fuel energy sources.  该报告主张增加对非化石燃料能源的投资。

synonym

antonym
