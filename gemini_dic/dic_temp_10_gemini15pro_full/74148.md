foundershaft: /ˈfaʊndərʃæft/
noun
创始股; 创始人股份
The equity or ownership stake held by the founders of a company.
Example: The foundershaft was divided equally among the three original partners.  三位最初的合伙人平分了创始人股份。

Example:  He negotiated a larger foundershaft percentage before the company went public. 在公司上市之前，他协商了更大的创始人股份比例。

synonym: founder's equity, founder's shares
antonym:  (No direct antonym, but consider) acquired shares, employee stock options
n.
