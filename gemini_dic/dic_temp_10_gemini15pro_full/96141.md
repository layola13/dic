indentation:/ˌɪnˌdɛnˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 缩进；凹陷；锯齿状 |The indentation of the coastline provides natural harbors.  海岸线的锯齿状提供了天然的港口。|  A formatting feature that indents lines of text from the margin.  一种将文本行从页边缩进的格式化功能。|  indent, notch, recess | protrusion, projection | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *indentatio, from Latin indentare “to notch, furnish with teeth,” from in- “in” + dens (genitive dentis) “tooth.”
