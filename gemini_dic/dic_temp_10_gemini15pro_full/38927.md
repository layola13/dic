confessorship: /kənˈfɛsəʃɪp/
noun
忏悔师的职位；告解神父的职位
The office or position of a confessor, especially in the Roman Catholic Church.
He was appointed to the confessorship at the local cathedral.  他被任命为当地大教堂的忏悔神父。
The confessorship has been vacant for several months. 这告解神父的职位已经空缺好几个月了。
近义词: priesthood, chaplaincy
反义词:  None (This word represents a specific religious office, and thus doesn't have a direct opposite.)
词性: n.
