inherit:/ɪnˈhɛrɪt/ /ɪnˈherɪt/| v. |继承; 遗传|To receive (property, a title, etc.) from one's ancestors or predecessors by legal succession or will.  To derive (a quality, characteristic, or predisposition) genetically from one's parents or ancestors.|She inherited a fortune from her grandfather. 她从她祖父那里继承了一笔财富。

He inherited his father's love of music. 他遗传了他父亲对音乐的热爱。|近义词：receive, acquire, derive, obtain|反义词:forfeit, lose|词性:动词
