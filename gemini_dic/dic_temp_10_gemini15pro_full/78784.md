gillie: /ˈɡɪli/ | n. | 吉利，钓鱼向导；苏格兰高地舞蹈演员 | A gillie is a person who attends someone on a hunting or fishing expedition, especially in the Scottish Highlands. |  例句：The gillie led us to the best fishing spot on the river.  钓鱼向导带我们去了河上最好的钓鱼点。

例句：He hired a gillie to help him navigate the Highlands while hunting deer. 他雇了一个向导，在猎鹿时帮他在高地地区引路。| 近义词：guide, attendant, escort | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
