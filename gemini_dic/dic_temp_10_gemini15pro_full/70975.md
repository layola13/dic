fireproofed:/ˈfaɪərpruːft/| adj.|防火的，耐火的|Treated or constructed with a material to make it resistant to fire.|例句：The building was fireproofed to comply with safety regulations. 这栋建筑经过防火处理以符合安全规定。

例句：They used fireproofed materials in the construction of the new theater.  他们在新剧院的建造中使用了防火材料。|近义词：fire-resistant, non-flammable, flameproof|反义词：flammable, combustible|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "fireproof" + "-ed" (past participle suffix).  "Fireproof" itself comes from "fire" + "proof." "Proof" in this context originally meant "tested and resistant to," related to the idea of testing the quality of something.
