armoric:/ɑːrˈmɒrɪk/ | adj. n.|布列塔尼的，布列塔尼语；布列塔尼人|Relating to Brittany, its people, or their language.  Sometimes used as a noun to refer to the Breton language.|例句：He spoke fluent Armorican. 他说一口流利的布列塔尼语。|例句：The Armorican peninsula is known for its rugged coastline. 布列塔尼半岛以其崎岖的海岸线而闻名。|近义词：Breton, Celtic|反义词: N/A |词性: adjective, noun
Etymology: From Late Latin *Armorica, from Gaulish *Aremorica (“coastal region”), from are- (“at, on, near”) + mori- (“sea”). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₂m̥mōr- (“sea”).
