overmuch:/ˌoʊvərˈmʌtʃ/| adj. adv.|过度的，过分的；过量地，过分地|Excessive; too much.  It emphasizes the negative consequences of excess.  While "too much" simply denotes excess, "overmuch" suggests a harmful or undesirable excess.|He worried overmuch about his health. 他过分担心自己的健康。|近义词：excessive, undue, immoderate, exorbitant|反义词:moderate, reasonable, insufficient|词性:形容词/副词

Etymology: From Old English *ofermycel, from ofer (over) + mycel (much).
