darkful:/ˈdɑːrkfʊl/
adj.
黑暗的，阴沉的
Characterized by darkness; gloomy.
The darkful forest was filled with mysterious sounds.  黑暗的森林里充满了神秘的声音。
近义词:dark, dim, gloomy, murky
反义词:bright, light, sunny
词性:形容词

词源:  "Darkful" is formed by combining "dark" (Old English "deorc") with the suffix "-ful" (Old English "-ful," meaning "full of").  While not considered standard English, it is an archaic or poetic term.
