ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *fortunat*, from Old French *fortunat*, from Latin *fortunātus* (“lucky, prosperous, happy”), perfect passive participle of *fortunō* (“I make prosperous, make happy”), from *fortūna* (“fortune, chance, luck”).  Doublet of *fortunate*.
