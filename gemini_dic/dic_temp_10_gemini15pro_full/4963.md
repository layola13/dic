alkalescent: /ˌæl.kəˈlɛs.ənt/ | adj. | 逐渐碱性的；微碱性的 | Becoming slightly alkaline; mildly alkaline. | The soil in this area is becoming increasingly alkalescent.  这个地区的土壤正变得越来越偏碱性。| The solution turned alkalescent after the addition of the base. 添加碱后，溶液变成了微碱性。 | 近义词: mildly alkaline, slightly basic | 反义词: acidic, acidulous | 词性: adjective | 词源: From alkali + -escent (a suffix forming adjectives meaning "becoming" or "beginning to be").
