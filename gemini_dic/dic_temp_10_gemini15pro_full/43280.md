craftsmanship: /ˈkræftsmənʃɪp/ (US) /ˈkrɑːftsmənʃɪp/ (UK) | n. | 技艺，工艺；精湛技艺 | Skill in a particular craft; the quality of design and work shown in something made by hand. |  The craftsmanship of the furniture was evident in its intricate carvings.  这件家具的精湛工艺体现在其复杂的雕刻上。| synonyms: artistry, workmanship, expertise, skill | antonyms: incompetence, clumsiness | noun
