capped:/kæpt/
|adj.|
|加盖的；戴帽子的；受限制的；被封顶的|
|Having a cap or cover.  Limited or restricted, especially by an upper limit.|
|The bottle was capped tightly. 瓶子盖得很紧。|
|covered, topped, limited, restricted|
|uncapped, unlimited, unrestricted|
|adjective|


capped:/kæpt/
|v.|
|加盖；戴帽子；设定上限|
|To put a cap on something. To set an upper limit.|
|He capped the bottle with a cork. 他用软木塞盖上瓶子。|
|cover, top, limit, restrict|
|uncap, unleash|
|verb|
