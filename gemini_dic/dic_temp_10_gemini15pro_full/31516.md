charred:/ˈtʃɑːrd/| adj.|烧焦的，炭化的|Reduced to charcoal by burning.  The steak was charred on the outside but still rare inside. 牛排外面烧焦了，但里面仍然很嫩。|近义词：burnt, scorched, singed|反义词:raw, uncooked|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *charken, from Old English *ċearcian (“to crackle”), from Proto-Germanic *karkōną (“to crack, creak”), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ker- (“to turn, bend”).  The past participle form "charred" became the dominant form.
