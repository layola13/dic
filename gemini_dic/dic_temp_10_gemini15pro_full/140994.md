oneon:/ˈwʌnˌɒn/| adj. |一对一的，单挑的|A one-on-one meeting is a meeting between two people, usually to discuss something private or important.  一对一会议是指两个人之间的会议，通常是为了讨论一些私密或重要的事情。|例句：I had a one-on-one with my boss about my performance review. 我和我的老板进行了一对一的绩效评估面谈。

|近义词：individual, private, personal|反义词: group, collective|词性:形容词


oneon: /ˈwʌnˌɒn/| n. |一对一比赛，单挑|A one-on-one is a contest or competition between two individuals. 一对一是两个人之间的比赛或竞争。|例句：The basketball player challenged his rival to a one-on-one. 这位篮球运动员向他的对手挑战一对一。|近义词：duel, match, contest|反义词: group competition, team game|词性:名词
