counterarguing:/ˌkaʊntərˈɑːrɡjuɪŋ/
verb
反驳，提出相反的论点
Presenting reasons or evidence in opposition to an argument.
He was skilled at counterarguing his opponent's points. 他善于反驳对手的观点。
近义词: rebutting, refuting, disputing
反义词: agreeing, conceding, accepting
词性: verb (动名词)
