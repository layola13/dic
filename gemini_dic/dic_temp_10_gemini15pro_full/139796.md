octene:/ˈɒktin/ /ˈɑːktiːn/| n.|辛烯|An alkene with eight carbon atoms.  A colorless flammable liquid hydrocarbon, C₈H₁₆, obtained from the cracking of petroleum and used in the manufacture of plastics and other chemicals.|Octene is used in the production of polyethylene. 辛烯用于生产聚乙烯。

Octene is a component of some fuels. 辛烯是某些燃料的成分。|近义词:1-octene, caprylene|反义词:None|词性:noun
