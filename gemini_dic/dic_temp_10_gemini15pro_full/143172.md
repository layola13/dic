outclerk:/ˌaʊtˈklɜːrk/
noun
外勤办事员；外勤职员
A clerk who works outside of a main office, often performing tasks like deliveries or field work.
The outclerk delivered the important documents to the courthouse. 外勤办事员把重要文件送到了法院。
field clerk, outside clerk
office clerk, inside clerk
