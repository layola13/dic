baubee: /ˈbɔːbiː/ | n. | 半便士，少量钱 | A Scottish coin of low value, originally equal to half a penny.  It now refers to a small amount of money, often used figuratively. |  He didn't have a baubee to his name. 他身无分文。 | synonyms: pittance, trifle, mite, farthing, penny | antonyms: fortune, wealth | noun
