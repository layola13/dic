dataframe:/ˈdeɪtə freɪm/ | n. |数据框，数据帧 | A two-dimensional labeled data structure with columns of potentially different types.  It is generally the most commonly used pandas object. Think of it like a spreadsheet or SQL table, or a dict of Series objects. |  The dataframe contains information about all the students.  这个数据框包含所有学生的信息。 | synonyms: table, grid, matrix | antonyms: N/A | noun
