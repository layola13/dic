inhibitors:/ɪnˈhɪbɪtərz/ | n. |抑制剂，抑制物 |A substance that slows down or stops a chemical reaction or other process, or that reduces the activity of an enzyme or other agent. | Enzyme inhibitors can be used to treat various diseases. 酶抑制剂可以用来治疗各种疾病。| synonyms: suppressants, blockers, repressors | antonyms: activators, catalysts, stimulants | noun

inhibitor:/ɪnˈhɪbɪtər/ | n. |抑制剂，抑制物 |Something that inhibits or restrains. | Fear can be a powerful inhibitor of action.  恐惧会强烈地抑制行动。| synonyms: deterrent, constraint, check | antonyms:  stimulus, impetus, incentive | noun
