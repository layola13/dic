bonobo:/bəˈnoʊboʊ/ /ˈbɒnəbəʊ/
n.
倭黑猩猩
A kind of great ape, closely related to the chimpanzee but slightly smaller and more slender, known for its relatively peaceful social behavior and frequent sexual interactions.
Bonobos are known for their peaceful nature. 倭黑猩猩以爱好和平的特性而闻名。
Bonobos use sexual activity to resolve conflicts. 倭黑猩猩用性行为来解决冲突。
近义词:ape, chimpanzee
反义词: (No direct antonym for an animal species, but could consider contrasting behavior) aggressive animal
词性: noun
