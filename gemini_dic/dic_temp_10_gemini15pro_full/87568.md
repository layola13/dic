heterophasia:/ˌhɛtərəˈfeɪʒə/ /ˌhɛtərəˈfeɪziə/ | n. |异语症，错语症|A speech disorder in which the patient uses wrong words, or uses words in a wrong or inappropriate way, although knowing their correct meaning.  It's a form of paraphasia. | Example 1:  The patient with heterophasia repeatedly called his wife "mother," although he knew her name.  病人患有异语症，尽管他知道妻子的名字，却反复地叫她“妈妈”。

Example 2: Her heterophasia made communication difficult, as she often substituted related but incorrect words in her sentences. 她的异语症使沟通变得困难，因为她经常在句子中用相关但不正确的词语代替。 | 近义词：paraphasia,  dysphasia | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider clear speech or articulate speech) | 词性: noun
