oxides:/ˈɒksaɪdz/ | n. | 氧化物 | A binary compound of oxygen with another element or group. | Iron oxides are common in nature. 铁氧化物在自然界中很常见。|  Rust is an oxide of iron. 铁锈是铁的一种氧化物。 | synonyms: compounds,  | antonyms: (No direct antonym for a chemical compound) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *oxide*, from *oxygène* (oxygen) + *-ide* (suffix forming names of binary compounds). Ultimately from Greek *oxys* (sharp, acid) and *-genes* (forming).
