mismeasurement:/ˌmɪsˈmeʒəmənt/
|n.|错误测量，测量不准|An act or instance of measuring something incorrectly.  The mismeasurement of the ingredients ruined the cake.|例句：The mismeasurement of the land led to a dispute between the neighbors. 土地的测量错误导致邻居之间发生争执。

例句：The mismeasurement in the experiment resulted in inaccurate conclusions. 实验中的测量误差导致结论不准确。
|近义词：inaccurate measurement, faulty measurement, incorrect measurement|反义词：accurate measurement, precise measurement|词性:名词
