indexer:/ˈɪndɛksər/| n.|索引器，标引员，指示器|A person or thing that indexes something; a program that indexes data.  |例句：The indexer is responsible for categorizing the library's books.  标引员负责图书馆书籍的分类。

例句：The search engine uses an indexer to crawl the web and categorize websites.  搜索引擎使用索引器抓取网页并对网站进行分类。|近义词：cataloguer, classifier, compiler|反义词: N/A|noun
