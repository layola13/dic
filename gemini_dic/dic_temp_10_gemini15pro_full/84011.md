half-divinely:/ˌhæf dɪˈvaɪnli/
adj.
一半神圣地，略带神性地
partly or somewhat divinely; possessing a quality that is suggestive of or reminiscent of divinity, but not fully or completely so.
Her voice resonated with a half-divinely inspired tone. 她的声音带着一种略带神圣的启示的语气。
The half-divinely lit landscape seemed ethereal.  这片略带神圣光照的风景显得空灵。
Near synonyms: semi-divine, partly divine, quasi-divine
Antonyms: mundane, earthly, profane
word class: Adjective
