anticlimax:/ˌæntiˈklaɪmæks/| n.|虎头蛇尾；突降法|A disappointing end to an exciting or impressive series of events.|The movie ended in an anticlimax.| 近义词:disappointment, letdown|反义词:climax, culmination|noun

anticlimactic:/ˌæntiˌklaɪˈmæk.tɪk/| adj.|虎头蛇尾的；突降法的|Causing disappointment at the end of an exciting or impressive series of events.|The ending of the play was anticlimactic.|近义词:disappointing, underwhelming|反义词:climactic, exciting|adjective

anticling:/ˌæntiˈklɪŋ/| adj.|防粘的；不粘的|Treated or designed to prevent clinging or sticking.|An anticling fabric softener.|近义词:nonstick, noncling|反义词:clingy, sticky|adjective

词源: "anti-" (against) + "cling" (to adhere closely).  "Cling" comes from the Old English "clingan." "Anti-" is derived from Greek.
