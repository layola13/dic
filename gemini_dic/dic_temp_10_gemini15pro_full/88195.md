Himmler:/ˈhɪmlər/| n.|希姆莱|Heinrich Himmler (1900-1945) was a leading member of the Nazi Party and one of the most powerful men in Nazi Germany. He was the head of the Schutzstaffel (SS), the Nazi Party's paramilitary organization.|Hitler entrusted Himmler with the task of implementing the "Final Solution."|近义词:Nazi leader, SS chief|反义词:None (due to the nature of the term)|Noun
