fishnets:/ˈfɪʃˌnɛts/| n. |渔网；网眼袜|A type of hosiery or stockings made of a net-like fabric, resembling a fishing net. They are often worn as a fashion statement.|She wore fishnets and a short skirt. 她穿着网眼袜和短裙。|Synonyms: stockings, hosiery, tights|Antonyms: pants, trousers|noun

fishnets:/ˈfɪʃˌnɛts/| n. |鱼网状物|Anything resembling the open, net-like structure of a fishing net.|The pattern on the fabric was like fishnets.  布料上的图案像渔网。|Synonyms: netting, mesh, lattice|Antonyms: solid, opaque|noun
