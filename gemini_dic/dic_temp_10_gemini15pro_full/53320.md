disabuse:/ˌdɪsəˈbjuːz/
verb
纠正，使醒悟，打消…的错误想法
To free someone from a misconception or deception.
例句：
I disabused her of the notion that she was somehow indispensable to the company.
我让她打消了她对公司来说不可或缺的想法。

The teacher disabused the students of the idea that memorization was the key to learning.
老师纠正了学生认为死记硬背是学习关键的错误想法。

近义词：undeceive, enlighten, correct, set straight
反义词：deceive, mislead, delude
词性：v.
