nonperpetration: /ˌnɒnpɜːr.pəˈtreɪ.ʃən/ | n. | 不犯罪，不实施（犯罪），不作恶 | The nonperpetration of violence is a core principle of pacifism. |  The treaty focuses on the nonperpetration of war crimes. | 近义词：abstention, avoidance, forbearance | 反义词：perpetration, commission, execution | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From non- + perpetration.  "Perpetration" comes from the Latin "perpetrat-" the past participle stem of "perpetrare" meaning "to perform, accomplish, achieve, complete," formed from "per-" (thoroughly) + "patrare" (to perform, execute).  "Non-" is a prefix of negation.
