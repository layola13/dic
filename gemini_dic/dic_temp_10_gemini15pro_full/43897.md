cretin:/ˈkriːtɪn/ (US), /ˈkriːtɪn/ (UK)| n.|呆小症患者；愚蠢的人|A person with cretinism, a condition characterized by physical deformity and mental retardation; a stupid person.|He called him a cretin for making such a stupid mistake. 他因为他犯了如此愚蠢的错误而称他为白痴。|近义词：idiot, fool, moron, imbecile|反义词：genius, intellectual|noun
