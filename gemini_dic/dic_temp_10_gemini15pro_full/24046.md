breezily:/ˈbriːzɪli/| adv.|轻快地，轻松地|In a manner characterized by lightness and ease, like a gentle breeze.  It suggests a carefree and unconcerned attitude.|She breezily dismissed his concerns. 她轻描淡写地打消了他的顾虑。|Synonyms: airily, casually, cheerfully, lightly, nonchalantly|Antonyms: seriously, solemnly, tensely|adverb
