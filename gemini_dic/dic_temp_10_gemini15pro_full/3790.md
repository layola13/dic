agm:/ˌeɪdʒiːˈem/ |n.| 年度股东大会(annual general meeting)| An annual general meeting (AGM) is a yearly gathering of a company's interested shareholders. At an AGM, the directors of the company present an annual report containing information for the past year. |The company's AGM is scheduled for next month. 该公司的年度股东大会定于下个月举行。| synonyms: annual meeting, general meeting | antonyms: N/A | noun
