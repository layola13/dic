bollworm:/ˈbɒlwɜːm/ (UK) /ˈboʊlwɜːrm/ (US)| n. |棉铃虫；棉铃实夜蛾|A moth larva that feeds on the boll or seed pod of cotton and other plants.|The bollworm infestation destroyed a significant portion of the cotton crop. 棉铃虫害毁坏了很大一部分棉花作物。|近义词：pest, insect|反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider beneficial insect)|noun
