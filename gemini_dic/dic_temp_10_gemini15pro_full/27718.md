canelike:/ˈkeɪnˌlaɪk/
adjective
像拐杖的; 类似于拐杖的
Resembling a cane, especially in slenderness, stiffness, or fragility.  A canelike stalk.
The plant had a tall, canelike stem.  这种植物有一个高高的、像拐杖一样的茎。
近义词: reedlike, stalklike, sticklike
反义词: flexible, supple
