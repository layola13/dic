nonconducive:/ˌnɑːnkənˈduːsɪv/
adjective
不利于的，不适宜的
Not favorable or helpful; tending to hinder or obstruct.
This noisy environment is nonconducive to studying.  这个嘈杂的环境不利于学习。
The current political climate is nonconducive to economic growth. 当前的政治气候不利于经济增长。
近义词:unfavorable, detrimental, adverse, inimical
反义词:conducive, favorable, beneficial, helpful
词性:adjective
词源:From non- + conducive, from Latin conducere "to lead or bring together," from com- "together" + ducere "to lead."  The prefix "non-" negates the meaning.
