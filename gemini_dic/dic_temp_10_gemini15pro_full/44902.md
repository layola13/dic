cryonicist:/kraɪˈɒnɪsɪst/ |n.|人体冷冻学家；冷冻法支持者|A person who advocates or practices cryonics, the freezing of the bodies or heads of the recently deceased in the hope that they can be revived in the future when advanced medical technology becomes available.|例句：The cryonicist believed that future technology could revive the deceased.  这位人体冷冻学家相信未来的科技可以使死者复生。

例句：Some cryonicists donate their bodies to research in cryonics. 一些人体冷冻学家把他们的遗体捐献给人体冷冻研究。|近义词：cryonics supporter, advocate of cryonics|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
