displeasures:/dɪsˈpleʒərz/
n.
不悦，不满，生气
a feeling of annoyance and disapproval
The king showed his displeasure at the news. 国王对这个消息表示不悦。

Synonyms: annoyance, disapproval, irritation, dissatisfaction, resentment, anger, vexation, umbrage
Antonyms: pleasure, satisfaction, delight, happiness

词性: 名词 (Noun)
