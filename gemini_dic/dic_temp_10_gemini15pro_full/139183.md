objectification:/əbˌdʒɛktɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 物化；客体化 | The act of treating a person, or sometimes an animal, as an object or a thing. |  Her objectification in the media made her feel dehumanized.  她在媒体上的物化让她感到被非人化了。 | Synonyms: dehumanization, depersonalization, reification | Antonyms: humanization, personalization, subjectification | noun

objectification:/əbˌdʒɛktɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 物化；客体化 |  The act of representing something abstract in concrete form. | The objectification of his fears in the form of monsters helped him understand them better.  他将恐惧物化为怪物的形式，这有助于他更好地理解它们。 | Synonyms: embodiment, externalization, manifestation | Antonyms: abstraction, internalization | noun
