licitness:/lɪˈsɪtnəs/
n.
合法性, 正当性
The state or quality of being licit; lawfulness.
The licitness of the transaction was questioned by the authorities.  当局质疑这笔交易的合法性。

近义词: legality, lawfulness, legitimacy
反义词: illegality, unlawfulness, illicitness
词性: noun

词源: From late Middle English *licite, from Latin licitus (“lawful”), from licet (“it is lawful”).
