inflexure:/ɪnˈflɛkʃər/| n.|弯曲，屈曲；转折点|a bend or curve; a point of change in direction|The bridge showed a slight inflexure due to the weight of the traffic. 这座桥由于交通负荷而略微弯曲。|synonyms: bend, curve, turn, bow, crook|antonyms: straightness, linearity|noun

inflexure:/ɪnˈflɛkʃər/| n.|（曲线）拐点；（道路）弯道|a point where a curve changes from being concave to convex or vice versa; a bend in a road or path|The graph showed an inflexure at the point where the two lines intersected. 图表显示两条线相交的点是一个拐点。|synonyms: turning point, bend, curve|antonyms: straight line|noun
