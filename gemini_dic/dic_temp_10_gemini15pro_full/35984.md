codes:/koʊdz/| n. |代码，密码，编码|A system of words, letters, figures, or symbols used to represent something, especially in computing or data communication. |Example 1: The programmer wrote efficient codes for the software. 程序员为软件编写了高效的代码。

Example 2:  She cracked the codes and accessed the secret files. 她破解了密码，访问了秘密文件。|Synonyms: ciphers, symbols, signals |Antonyms: plain text, clear text|noun
