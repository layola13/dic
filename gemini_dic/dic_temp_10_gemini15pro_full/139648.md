occultist:/ˈɒkʌltɪst/ (US) /ˈɒkʌltɪst/ (UK)| n.|神秘学家；玄学家|A person who studies or practices occult arts, such as magic, alchemy, or astrology.|例句：The occultist claimed to be able to communicate with spirits.  这位神秘学家声称能够与灵魂沟通。

例句：The occultist predicted the future using tarot cards.  这位神秘学家用塔罗牌预测未来。|近义词：mystic, esoteric, magician, sorcerer, wizard, witch|反义词：skeptic, rationalist|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin occultista, from Latin occultus (“hidden, secret”), past participle of occulere (“to hide, conceal”).
