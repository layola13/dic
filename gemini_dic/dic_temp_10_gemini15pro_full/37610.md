communicativenesses:/kəˌmjuːnɪkətɪvnəsɪz/
|n.| 交际能力，沟通能力（复数）|The quality of being communicative; willingness to communicate.  The plural form is less common but refers to multiple instances or types of communicativeness.|Her communicativenesses in different social settings varied greatly.  她在不同社交场合的沟通能力差异很大。|近义词：communication skills, expressiveness, openness, loquacity|反义词：reticence, taciturnity, uncommunicativeness|词性: noun

Etymology:
From communicative + -ness + -es (plural suffix).  Ultimately from Latin communicare "to share, impart,"  from communis "common."
