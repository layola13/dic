cornelians:/kɔːrˈniːliənz/
|n.|
|玛瑙|
|A translucent reddish-brown variety of chalcedony.|

|She wore a necklace of cornelians.|
|她戴着一条玛瑙项链。|


|Synonyms: agate, chalcedony|
|Antonyms: N/A|
|Noun|

Etymology:
From Old French *corneline*, from Medieval Latin *corneolus*, diminutive of Latin *cornum* "cornel cherry," so called for its resemblance in color to the fruit.
