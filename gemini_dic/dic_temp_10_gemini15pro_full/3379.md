affrontiveness:/əˈfrʌntɪvnəs/
n.
冒犯; 失礼; 傲慢无礼
The quality of being offensive or disrespectful.
His affrontiveness shocked the guests. 他的傲慢无礼震惊了客人。
近义词：rudeness, impoliteness, insolence, offensiveness
反义词：politeness, courtesy, respectfulness
词性：名词

Etymology:  From affront + -ive + -ness. Affront comes from Old French afronter "to strike on the forehead, insult," from a- "to, at" + front "forehead."  Ultimately from Latin frons "forehead, front."
