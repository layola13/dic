imploratory:/ɪmˈplɔːrətɔːri/
adjective
恳求的；哀求的
Making an earnest or desperate plea; begging.
She cast an imploratory glance at the judge, hoping for leniency. 她恳求地看了法官一眼，希望他能宽大处理。
The lost child gave his mother an imploratory look.  迷路的孩子恳求地看着他的母亲。
Synonyms: beseeching, pleading, supplicating, entreating
Antonyms: demanding, commanding, insistent
词性: adjective
