overcivilities:/ˌoʊvərˌsɪvɪˈlɪtiz/ | n. | 过分的客套；过度的礼貌 | Excessive politeness or courtesy, often insincere or overly formal. | He overwhelmed me with overcivilities, making me suspicious of his motives. 他过分的客套让我不知所措，也让我怀疑他的动机。| 近义词: excessive politeness, exaggerated courtesy | 反义词: rudeness, impoliteness | 词性: 名词

词源:  From "over-" (excessive) + "civilities" (plural of civility, from French "civilité," ultimately from Latin "civis" - citizen).
