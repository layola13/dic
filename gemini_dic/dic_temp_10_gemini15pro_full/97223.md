inflatingly:/ɪnˈfleɪtɪŋli/| adv.|膨胀地，夸张地| in a way that inflates or exaggerates something.  It describes an action or process that causes something to increase in size, value, or importance, often beyond what is reasonable or justified.|The prices were inflatingly high. 价格膨胀得离谱。|Synonyms: expansively, exaggeratedly, disproportionately|Antonyms: moderately, reasonably, realistically|词性:副词
