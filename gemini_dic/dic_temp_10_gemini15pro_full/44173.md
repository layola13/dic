criticling: /ˈkrɪtɪsɪzɪŋ/
criticling: v.|批评；评论；|The act of expressing disapproval of someone or something, typically after evaluating their merits or faults.  Giving feedback, often negative, on a piece of work.|He spent the afternoon criticling her essay. 他花了整个下午评论她的文章。|近义词: reviewing, evaluating, appraising, assessing, judging|反义词: praising, commending, lauding, approving|词性: 动名词 (gerund)
词源: From the verb "criticize," which comes from the French "critiquer," ultimately from the Greek "kritikos" meaning "able to judge."
