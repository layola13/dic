dispeopler:/dɪsˈpiːplər/
|n.|驱逐者；使人口减少的人（或事物）
|Someone or something that depopulates a place.  The act of reducing the population of a place. |
|The war was a brutal dispeopler of the region.|战争残酷地减少了该地区的人口。

|The plague acted as a dispeopler, decimating the population of entire villages.|瘟疫像一个驱逐者，摧毁了整个村庄的人口。
|近义词：depopulator|
|反义词：populator|
|词性：名词|


dispeople:/dɪsˈpiːpl/
|vt.|减少人口；驱逐居民
|To reduce the population of; to deprive of inhabitants.|
|The government's policies dispeopled the rural areas, forcing people to migrate to the cities.|政府的政策导致农村地区人口减少，迫使人们迁移到城市。

|The Black Death dispeopled vast swathes of Europe in the 14th century.|14世纪的黑死病使欧洲大片地区人口减少。
|近义词：depopulate, evacuate|
|反义词：populate, inhabit|
|词性：动词|
