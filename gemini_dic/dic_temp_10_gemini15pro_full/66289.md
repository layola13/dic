excoriable:/ɪkˈskɔːriəbl/
adjective
可剥落的，可擦伤的
Susceptible to excoriation; easily abraded or chafed.
The skin of newborns is delicate and excoriable.  新生儿的皮肤娇嫩，容易擦伤。
The paint on the old wall was excoriable and flaked off easily. 旧墙上的油漆很容易剥落。
近义词:abrasive, chafeable, tender, delicate
反义词:tough, resilient, durable
词性:adjective

excoriate:/ɪkˈskɔːriˌeɪt/
verb
擦破；剥落；严厉批评
To wear off the skin of; abrade. To censure scathingly.
The harsh soap excoriated his skin.  粗糙的肥皂擦伤了他的皮肤。
The critic excoriated the director for his poor handling of the film.  评论家严厉批评了导演对这部电影的拙劣处理。
近义词:abrade, chafe, denounce, condemn
反义词:praise, commend, heal, soothe
词性:verb
