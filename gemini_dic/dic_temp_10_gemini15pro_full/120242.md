minglements: /ˈmɪŋɡlmənts/
n.
混合物，交融，交往
The act or process of mingling; the state of being mingled.  A mixture of different things or people.
The minglements of different cultures created a vibrant and diverse community. 不同文化的交融创造了一个充满活力和多元化的社区。
Synonyms: mixture, blend, amalgamation, fusion, intermingling
Antonyms: separation, division, segregation
