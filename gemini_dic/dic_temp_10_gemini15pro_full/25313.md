bugging-out:/ˈbʌɡɪŋ aʊt/| phrasal verb | 匆忙撤离；逃走；（昆虫）突然飞出 | The act of leaving a place quickly, often due to an emergency or perceived threat.  It can also refer to insects suddenly flying away. |  The family was bugging out of their home as the wildfire approached.  家人在野火逼近时匆忙撤离家园。

The hikers bugged out when they saw a bear.  徒步旅行者看到熊后逃跑了。| 近义词：fleeing, escaping, evacuating | 反义词：staying, remaining | verb phrase
