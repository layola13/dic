actino:/ˌæktɪnoʊ/| prefix |放射性的，星形的|A combining form meaning “ray,” “radial,” “radiant,” used in the formation of compound words:  actinolite; actinometer. |The actinomorphic flowers are radially symmetrical. 这些放射状花是辐射对称的。| 近义词: radio-| 反义词: None| 词缀


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *aktīn- (stem of aktís ray, beam), akin to Latin actio act.

USAGE->
This is a prefix and doesn't have tense variations like verbs.

Example 1: Actinomycosis is a bacterial infection. 放线菌病是一种细菌感染。

Example 2:  Actinolite is a green mineral.  阳起石是一种绿色的矿物。


PHRASE->
actinomorphic flower = 放射状花
actinotherapy = 放射疗法
actinomycosis = 放线菌病
actinometer = 光能计
actinium = 锕
