canisters:/ˈkænɪstərz/| n. | 罐，筒，容器 |A metal container, typically cylindrical and airtight, used for storing dry goods, chemicals, or gases. |例句：The tea is kept in canisters to preserve its freshness. 茶叶保存在罐子里以保持新鲜。

例句：Firefighters wore breathing apparatus and used special canisters to neutralize the chemical spill. 消防员戴着呼吸器，并使用特殊的罐子来中和化学品泄漏。|近义词：containers, receptacles, tins, boxes |反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "open container") |词性:noun
