limit:/ˈlɪmɪt/| n. vt.|限制, 限度, 界限|A point or level beyond which something does not or may not extend or pass.|The speed limit is 30 mph. 限速为每小时30英里。|近义词：restriction, boundary, constraint|反义词:infinity, boundlessness|noun
limit:/ˈlɪmɪt/| vt.|限制,限定|To restrict or confine within bounds.|We must limit our spending. 我们必须限制我们的开支。|近义词：restrict, confine, restrain|反义词:expand, increase|verb
