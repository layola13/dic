befalling:/bɪˈfɔːlɪŋ/ | v. (present participle of befall) | 降临；发生 | Something unpleasant or unfortunate happening to someone. |  A great misfortune befell him.  巨大的不幸降临到他身上。 |  happening to, occurring, taking place | N/A | verb

被动语态 (Befalling is rarely used in the passive voice as it is an intransitive verb. However, a similar meaning can be conveyed with other verbs):

He was overtaken by misfortune. 他被不幸所压倒。
The city was struck by disaster. 这座城市遭受了灾难。
