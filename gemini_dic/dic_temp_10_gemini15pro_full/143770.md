outmeasures:/ˌaʊtˈmɛʒərz/
| v. | 超越，胜过，在…上超过 |  Surpasses in measurement or quantity; exceeds.  |  Our new product outmeasures the competition in terms of performance. 我们的新产品在性能方面超过了竞争对手。 |  outperforms, surpasses, exceeds, betters |  falls short of, loses to | Verb

outmeasures:/ˌaʊtˈmɛʒərz/
| n. | 超越的量度，胜过的方式 |  The extent to which something outmeasures another. (This usage is less common). | The outmeasures of our success are clear to everyone. 我们成功的超越程度对每个人都很明显。 | superiority, advantage, edge | inferiority, disadvantage | Noun
