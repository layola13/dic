expecto:/ɪkˈspɛktoʊ/ /ɛkˈspɛktoʊ/ | v. | 期望，期待，预料 | To think or believe that something will happen or that someone will arrive. |  I expect him to arrive soon. 我预计他很快就会到达。

I expect a lot from my students. 我对我的学生期望很高。

近义词: anticipate, await, hope, presume | 反义词: doubt, question | 词性: verb
