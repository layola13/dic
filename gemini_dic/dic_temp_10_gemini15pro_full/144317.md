outsprinted:/ˌaʊtˈsprɪntɪd/
verb
超越冲刺，比…跑得快
outpaced in a sprint.
例句：He outsprinted his rival in the final meters of the race. 他在比赛的最后几米冲刺中超过了他的对手。

例句：She was outsprinted just before the finish line. 她在终点线前被超越了。

近义词：outran, outpaced
反义词：lost to, was overtaken by
词性：动词
