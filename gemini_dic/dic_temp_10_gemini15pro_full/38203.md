compulsoriness:/kəmˈpʌlsərɪnɪs/
|n.|强制性，义务性|The state of being compulsory; the quality of being mandatory or obligatory.|The compulsoriness of wearing seatbelts has saved many lives.  安全带强制佩戴的规定挽救了许多生命。|近义词：obligation, necessity, requirement|反义词：optionality, voluntariness|词性:名词
compulsory:/kəmˈpʌlsəri/
|adj.|强制的，义务的|Required by law or a rule; obligatory.|Attendance at school is compulsory for children of certain ages.  特定年龄的儿童必须上学。|近义词：mandatory, obligatory, required|反义词：optional, voluntary|词性:形容词
compel:/kəmˈpɛl/
|v.|强迫，迫使|Force or oblige (someone) to do something.|The law compels employers to provide health insurance.  法律强制雇主提供健康保险。|近义词：force, oblige, coerce|反义词：persuade, encourage|词性:动词
