icicle:/ˈaɪsɪkl/ (US), /ˈaɪsɪkl/ (UK)| n. |冰柱，冰垂|A hanging piece of ice formed by the freezing of dripping water.|Example:  The icicles hung from the roof of the cabin. 小屋的屋顶上挂着冰柱。

Example:  The melting icicles dripped onto the ground. 融化的冰柱滴落在地上。


|近义词：ice formation, ice pendant|反义词:|词性:noun
