ethylene: /ˈɛθəlˌin/ (US), /ˈɛθɪliːn/ (UK) | n. | 乙烯 | A colorless flammable gas, C₂H₄, the simplest alkene, used as a starting material in making polyethylene and many other plastics. It is also a plant hormone involved in fruit ripening and other processes. | Ethylene is commonly used in the production of plastics. 乙烯通常用于塑料生产。|  Ethylene promotes the ripening of fruits. 乙烯促进水果成熟。 | Synonyms: ethene | Antonyms: None | Noun
