overrepresentativeness:/ˌoʊvərˌrɛprɪˌzɛntətɪvnəs/ | n. | 过度代表性；代表性过高 | The state of being overrepresented, meaning a group is represented in a greater proportion than its actual presence in the population. |  The overrepresentativeness of certain demographics in leadership positions can perpetuate inequality.  某些人口群体在领导职位中的过度代表性会使不平等现象持续存在。 | 近义词: disproportion, imbalance, overrepresentation | 反义词: underrepresentativeness, underrepresentation | 词性: noun

词源:
From over- + representativeness.  Over- signifies "excessive" or "too much". Representativeness is derived from represent, ultimately from Latin repraesentare "to present again, exhibit."
