linotype: /ˈlaɪnəˌtaɪp/ | n. | 排字机；莱诺字体 | A typesetting machine that casts an entire line of type as a single slug of metal. |  The newspaper used linotype machines to set type until the 1970s. 这家报纸直到20世纪70年代才使用排字机排版。 | 近义词: typesetter, composing machine | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From line + o + type.  "Line-o'-type" was originally a trademark name.
