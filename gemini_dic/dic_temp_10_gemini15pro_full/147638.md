overpoliced:/ˌoʊvərˈpliːst/ | adj. |过度警备的，警察过多的|  Subject to excessive policing, often targeting specific communities or demographics.  Experiencing a disproportionately high level of police presence and surveillance. |  Example 1:  The residents of the neighborhood felt overpoliced, with officers constantly patrolling the streets.  居民们感觉社区警力过剩，警察不断在街上巡逻。

Example 2:  Studies have shown that communities of color are often overpoliced, leading to increased tension and mistrust.  研究表明，有色人种社区经常被过度警备，导致紧张局势加剧和不信任感增强。 | Near Synonyms:  over-surveilled, heavily policed | Antonyms: underpoliced, neglected | Word Type: Adjective
