expectorator:/ɪkˈspɛktəˌreɪtər/
noun
祛痰剂; 促使咳出的人或物
a person or thing that expectorates (causes the ejection of sputum or phlegm from the throat or lungs).
The doctor prescribed an expectorator to help clear his lungs. 医生开了祛痰药来帮助清除他的肺部。
expectorant, mucolytic
- (No clear antonym)
n.


expectorate:/ɪkˈspɛktəˌreɪt/
verb
咳出(痰等); 吐出
cough up and spit out (phlegm, sputum, or saliva) from the throat or lungs.
He expectorated a large wad of phlegm. 他咳出一大口痰。
cough, spit
swallow
v.
