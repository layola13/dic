ecderon: /ˈɛkdərɒn/ | n. | 外胚层; 上皮 | The outermost of the three primary germ layers of an embryo, from which the epidermis, nervous system, and sensory organs develop. | The ecderon gives rise to the skin and nervous system. 外胚层会发育形成皮肤和神经系统。| synonyms: ectoderm, ectoblast | antonyms: endoderm, mesoderm | noun
