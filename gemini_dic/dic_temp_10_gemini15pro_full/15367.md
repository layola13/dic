backtack: /ˈbækˌtæk/ | n. v. |回针；倒缝；暂时固定 | A stitch made backward to secure the end of a seam or for reinforcement.  To sew with back stitches. | Example: She used a backtack to secure the button. 她用回针固定纽扣。
Example: Backtack the edges of the fabric before sewing the seam. 在缝合接缝之前，先将织物边缘回针固定。| Synonym: backstitch, oversew | Antonym: (No direct antonym for the sewing action. Perhaps "unpick," "remove stitching") | Noun, Verb
