brattishing: /ˈbrætɪʃɪŋ/
adjective
幼稚的，孩子气的，任性的
Behaving in a childish, spoilt way.

例句：Stop your brattishing whining and get on with your work!  别再像个孩子一样抱怨了，继续你的工作吧！

近义词：childish, immature, spoilt, petulant, fractious
反义词：mature, grown-up, well-behaved
词性: adjective
