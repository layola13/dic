formalize:/ˈfɔːrməlaɪz/ | v. |使正式化；正式制定；用形式表示|To give something a definite structure or shape, often making it official or written. | The agreement was formalized in a signed contract.  该协议以签署的合同正式化。

The two countries formalized their relationship with a new trade agreement.  两国通过一项新的贸易协定正式确立了双边关系。

|近义词：codify, systematize, regularize, institutionalize|反义词: informalize|词性:动词
