augustine:/ɔːˈɡʌstɪn/ | n. | 奥古斯丁, 八月瓜 |A male given name, equivalent to Augustus. Also, a type of pear. augustine:/ɔːˈɡʌstɪn/ | adj. | 八月的, 奥古斯丁式的 | Relating to or characteristic of Saint Augustine or his doctrines. | His theological views were distinctly Augustinian. 他的神学观点明显是奥古斯丁式的。 |  Augustinian thought has influenced Western philosophy for centuries. 奥古斯丁思想影响了西方哲学数个世纪。| synonyms: Augustinian | antonyms: N/A | adjective
