newspaper: /ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpər/ (US) /ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpə(r)/ (UK)| n. |报纸|A printed publication consisting of folded sheets of paper that contains news, feature articles, advertisements, and correspondence.| I read the newspaper every morning to stay informed. 我每天早上读报纸以保持消息灵通。| synonyms: paper, journal, gazette, periodical | antonyms: blog, website, newsfeed | noun

Etymology:  From "news" + "paper". "News" comes from Middle English "newes," plural of "newe," meaning "new thing," from Old French "noveles," plural of "novelle." "Paper" comes from Middle English "papir," from Old French "papier," from Latin "papyrus," from Greek "papyros."
