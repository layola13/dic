masoretic:/ˌmæsəˈrɛtɪk/ | adj. | 马索拉的，马索拉文本的 | Relating to the Masoretes, Jewish scholars who worked between the 7th and 11th centuries CE to standardize the Hebrew text of the Bible.  They added vowel points and other markings to ensure accurate pronunciation and interpretation. | The Masoretic Text is the authoritative Hebrew text of the Tanakh (Hebrew Bible). 马索拉文本是塔纳赫（希伯来圣经）的权威希伯来文本。

The scholar dedicated his life to studying the masoretic traditions. 这位学者毕生致力于研究马索拉传统。 | 近义词: textual, traditional | 反义词:  (No direct antonym; perhaps "unpointed" for the Hebrew text) | 词性: adjective
