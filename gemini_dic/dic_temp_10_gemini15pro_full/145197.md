overbaked:/ˌōvərˈbākt/| adj.|烤过头的，烤焦的|Baked for too long, resulting in dryness or burning.|The cookies were overbaked and slightly burnt.  饼干烤过头了，有点焦。|近义词：burnt, overdone, scorched|反义词:underbaked, doughy|adjective
