cotrustees:/ˌkoʊtrʌˈstiːz/
noun
共同受托人
Two or more people who are jointly responsible for managing property or assets on behalf of another person or entity.
My sister and I are cotrustees of our father's estate. 我和我的姐姐是我们父亲遗产的共同受托人。
The cotrustees were required to act in the best interests of the beneficiaries. 共同受托人被要求为受益人的最大利益行事。

近义词:joint trustees
反义词: (No direct antonym.  Perhaps "sole trustee" in contrast.)
词性:noun
