copaiva:/kəˈpeɪvə/ /koʊˈpaɪvə/
n.|柯巴脂，香脂树|A resin obtained from various tropical American trees of the genus Copaifera.  It is used in varnishes and perfumes, and formerly in medicine.|The artist used copaiva balsam in his oil paints.  这位艺术家在他的油画中使用了柯巴香脂。|Synonyms: balsam, resin, oleoresin|Antonyms: None|noun


copaiva:/kəˈpeɪvə/ /koʊˈpaɪvə/
adj.|柯巴树的，由柯巴树制成的|Relating to or derived from the copaiva tree.|Copaiva oil has a distinct aroma.  柯巴油有一种独特的香味。|Synonyms: resinous, balsamic|Antonyms: None|adjective
