denticle:/ˈdɛntɪk(ə)l/
n.
小齿，齿状突起
A small tooth or tooth-like projection.
The fossilized shark's skin was covered in tiny denticles. 这块鲨鱼皮化石上覆盖着微小的齿状突起。

近义词:toothlet, serration, projection
反义词:(No direct antonym, consider "smoothness" for the surface lacking denticles)
词性:noun

(建筑)齿饰，方齿
(architecture) A small, rectangular block used as a decorative element in a cornice.
