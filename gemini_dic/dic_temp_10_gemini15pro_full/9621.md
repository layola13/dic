antistate:/ˌæntiˈsteɪt/
adjective
反国家的；反对国家存在的
opposed to the existence of a state or government
The group holds antistate views and advocates for self-governance.  这个团体持有反国家观点，并倡导自治。
synonyms: anarchist, stateless, rebellious
antonyms: pro-government, statist, nationalist
adjective

antistate:/ˌæntiˈsteɪt/
noun
反国家主义者；反对国家存在的人
a person who is opposed to the existence of a state or government
He's a known antistate and has been involved in several protests. 他是一个知名的反国家主义者，并参与了多次抗议活动。
synonyms: anarchist, rebel, insurgent
antonyms: patriot, loyalist, conformist
noun
