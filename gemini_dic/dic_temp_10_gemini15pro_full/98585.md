institutive:/ˈɪnstɪtjuːtɪv/
adjective
设立的，制定的，创立的
Having the power to establish or institute something; enacting.  Relating to the act of instituting or establishing something.
例句：The institutive act of the legislature created the new agency. 立法机关的设立法案创建了新的机构。

例句：The document served as the institutive charter for the organization. 该文件作为该组织的设立章程。

近义词：founding, establishing, constitutive, formative
反义词：destructive, abolishing
词性: adjective
