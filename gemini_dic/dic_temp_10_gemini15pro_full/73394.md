forehook:/ˈfɔːrhʊk/| n.|前钩，前挂钩|A hook located at the front of something, often used for hanging or attaching items.  It can also refer to a type of grappling hook used in whaling.|The forehook on the crane was used to lift the heavy steel beam.  起重机上的前钩用来吊起沉重的钢梁。|The whalers used a forehook to secure the whale. 捕鲸人用前钩固定鲸鱼。|Synonym: hook, grapple|Antonym: N/A|noun
