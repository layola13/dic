mamillion:/mæˈmɪljən/ | n. | 百万; 巨款 | A mamillion is a humorous, informal, and exaggerated way of saying "a million," often implying a very large, unspecified amount of money or something of great value. It is not a standard English word. |  I've told you a mamillion times to clean your room! 我已经告诉你无数次要打扫你的房间了！| Synonyms: a million, a lot, tons, heaps | Antonyms: few, little | noun
