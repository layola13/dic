bruh's:/bruːz/| n.| 兄弟们的；老兄的（复数，非正式）|A colloquial, informal, and often pluralized form of "bruh," used to address a group of male friends or acquaintances.  It's a shortened version of "brother" and carries a sense of camaraderie and familiarity.|例句：Hey bruh's, what are you up to tonight? 嘿，兄弟们，今晚你们有什么安排？

例句：I'm chilling with my bruh's this weekend. 我这周末要和我的兄弟们一起放松一下。|近义词：dudes, guys, pals, mates|反义词: (No direct antonym, but could be considered opposite of close friends/allies)|词性:名词
