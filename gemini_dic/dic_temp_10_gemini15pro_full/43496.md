crating:/ˈkreɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 装箱;  板条箱 | The crating of the goods was done carefully to prevent damage during shipping. 货物装箱非常仔细，以防止运输过程中损坏。|  The crating protected the fragile items inside. 板条箱保护了里面的易碎物品。 | 近义词: boxing, packaging | 反义词: unpacking | 词性: 名词

crating:/ˈkreɪtɪŋ/ | v. | 把…装入板条箱 | They are crating the furniture for shipment. 他们正在把家具装入板条箱准备装运。 | We crated the sculptures before sending them to the museum.  我们将雕塑装箱后送往博物馆。 | 近义词: boxing, packaging | 反义词: unpacking | 词性: 动词 (动名词)

词源: From the verb "crate," which comes from the noun "crate" (meaning a wooden box), ultimately from Middle Dutch "crate" (basket, hurdle).
