binned:/bɪnd/ | v. | 放入箱子；丢弃；拒绝；解雇 |  past participle of *bin*, meaning to put something into a bin (trash can) or to discard something. It can also mean to reject an idea or plan, or to fire someone. | The rejected manuscripts were binned. 被拒绝的稿件被丢弃了。 | Synonyms: discarded, trashed, dumped, rejected, fired | Antonyms: kept, saved, accepted, hired | verb

binned:/bɪnd/ | adj. |  已放入垃圾箱的；已丢弃的；被拒绝的 |  Used as an adjective, usually in a passive sense, meaning something that has been put in a bin, discarded, or rejected. | The binned proposal gathered dust in the archives. 被否决的提案在档案室里积满了灰尘。 | Synonyms: discarded, rejected, trashed | Antonyms: kept, saved, accepted | adjective
