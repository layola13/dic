gastromyth: /ˈɡæstrəʊmɪθ/ | n. | 饮食谬误，关于食物的错误观念 |  A widespread but untrue story about food. It often involves the origins, preparation, or effects of food. | Example: The idea that spinach is a great source of iron is a common gastromyth.  菠菜是铁的丰富来源这一观点是一种常见的饮食谬误。

Example:  Many gastromyths persist despite scientific evidence to the contrary. 尽管有相反的科学证据，许多饮食谬误仍然存在。 | Synonym: food myth, culinary myth, food fallacy | Antonym: nutritional fact, culinary truth | noun
