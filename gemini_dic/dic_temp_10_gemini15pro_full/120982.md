mischristen:/ˌmɪsˈkrɪsən/
verb
错误地命名；给...取错名字
To give a wrong or inappropriate name to, especially at baptism.
He was mischristened "George" but his real name was "Gregory". 他被错误地命名为“乔治”，但他的真名是“格雷戈里”。
misname, miscall
name correctly
v.
