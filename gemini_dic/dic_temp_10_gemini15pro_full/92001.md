hyperobtrusively:/ˌhaɪpərəbˈtruːsɪvli/
| adv. | 过分强加于人地，过分唐突地 |  in a way that is excessively noticeable or intrusive, pushing oneself forward or one's opinions on others too forcefully. |  He hyperobtrusively inserted himself into the conversation, dominating it with his loud opinions. 他过分强加于人地插话，用他高亢的意见主导了谈话。| 近义词：pushily, aggressively, intrusively, officiously | 反义词：discreetly, subtly, unobtrusively | 词性:副词
