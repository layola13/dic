amenders:/əˈmɛndərz/
n.
修改者；修正者；改善者
People who make changes or improvements, especially to laws or documents.
The amenders of the constitution worked tirelessly to ensure fairness. 宪法的修改者孜孜不倦地工作以确保公平。
synonyms: revisers, modifiers, correctors, improvers
antonyms: damagers, destroyers

词性: noun (plural)
