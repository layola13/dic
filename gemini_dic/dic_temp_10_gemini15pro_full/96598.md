inductive:/ɪnˈdʌktɪv/| adj.|归纳的，感应的|Characterized by or based on the inference of general laws from particular instances; relating to or employing induction.|例句：Scientists use inductive reasoning to form hypotheses. 科学家使用归纳推理来形成假设。|例句：Inductive charging is becoming increasingly popular for smartphones.  感应充电在智能手机中越来越流行。|近义词：inferential, reasoned|反义词：deductive|词性:形容词

inductive:/ɪnˈdʌktɪv/| n.|感应器|A device that uses electromagnetic induction.|例句：The metal detector uses an inductive coil to sense metal objects. 金属探测器使用感应线圈来感应金属物体。|例句：The stovetop uses inductive heating to cook food. 炉灶使用感应加热来烹饪食物。|近义词：sensor, coil|反义词：N/A|词性:名词 (rarely used)
