bipolarized:/baɪˈpoʊləraɪzd/
adjective
双极化的，两极分化的
Having two poles or opposite extremes.  Divided into two sharply contrasting groups or sets of opinions or beliefs.
The political landscape has become increasingly bipolarized in recent years. 近年来，政治格局日益两极分化。
polarized, divided
unified, homogeneous
词性:形容词
