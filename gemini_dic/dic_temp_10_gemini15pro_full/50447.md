depreciate:/dɪˈpriːʃieɪt/ /dɪˈpriːsiˌeɪt/
verb
贬值；贬低；轻视
to decrease in value over a period of time; to regard or represent as of little worth.
My car has depreciated a lot since I bought it. 我的车自从我买来以后贬值了很多。
He tends to depreciate his own achievements. 他总是贬低自己的成就。
synonyms: devalue, decline, diminish, belittle, undervalue, underestimate
antonyms: appreciate, increase, enhance, value, overestimate
词性: 动词
