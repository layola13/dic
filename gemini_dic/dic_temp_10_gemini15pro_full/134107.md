nonimmigration:/ˌnɑːnɪmɪˈɡreɪʃən/ |n.|非移民；非移民身份|The process of entering a country legally without intending to become a permanent resident.  Entering a country temporarily for tourism, work, or study, without the intention of establishing permanent residency.|She entered the country on a nonimmigration visa. 她以非移民签证进入该国。|Synonyms: non-immigrant status, temporary residency|Antonyms: immigration, permanent residency|Noun
