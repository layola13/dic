merch:/mɜːrtʃ/ | n. | 商品；周边产品 | Merchandise, typically sold at concerts or events, or relating to a particular band or artist. |  例句：
The band's merch table was crowded with fans eager to buy t-shirts and posters.
乐队的周边产品摊位挤满了渴望购买T恤和海报的粉丝。

例句：
I spent way too much money on merch at the festival.
我在音乐节上花了太多钱买周边产品。


| 近义词：goods, merchandise, products | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: noun
