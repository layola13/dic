outwaving:/ˌaʊtˈweɪvɪŋ/
adjective
向外挥舞的，挥舞超过…的
extending or waving outward beyond something else; surpassing in waving.
The outwaving branches of the willow tree danced in the breeze.  垂柳向外挥舞的枝条在微风中舞动。
The flag, outwaving all others, signaled the start of the race.  那面旗帜，挥舞得超过所有其他旗帜，标志着比赛的开始。
近义词: outreaching, extending, surpassing
反义词: withdrawing, receding, shrinking
词性: adjective
