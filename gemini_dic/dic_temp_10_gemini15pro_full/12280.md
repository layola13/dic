asceticisms:/əˈsɛtɪˌsɪzəmz/| n. |禁欲主义，苦行主义|The practice of severe self-discipline and abstention from all forms of indulgence, typically for religious reasons.  The various practices of an ascetic.|He followed a life of asceticism, rejecting material possessions and physical comforts. 他过着苦行僧般的生活，拒绝物质财富和肉体享受。|近义词：austerity, self-denial, abstinence|反义词：hedonism, indulgence, luxury|noun
