Allston: /ˈɔːlstən/ (US) /ˈɔːlstən/ (UK)| n. |奥尔斯顿|A neighborhood in Boston, Massachusetts, known for its student population, live music venues, and independent businesses. It's considered a vibrant and artistic area.| I'm moving to Allston next month. 我下个月要搬到奥尔斯顿。|He lives in Allston, near Harvard University. 他住在奥尔斯顿，靠近哈佛大学。|近义词: Brighton (neighboring area),  |反义词: N/A |词性: Noun

Etymology:  Named after Washington Allston (1779-1843), a prominent American painter and poet. The area was part of the former town of Brighton, annexed by Boston in 1870.
