converging:/kənˈvɜːrdʒɪŋ/ | adj. | 会聚的，收敛的 | tending to come together from different directions; meeting at a point | The two roads are converging just ahead. 这两条路就在前面汇合。| synonyms:  meeting, joining, uniting, merging, intersecting, connecting | antonyms: diverging, separating, splitting | adjective

converge:/kənˈvɜːrdʒ/ | v. | 会聚，集中，趋同 | (of lines) tend to meet at a point.  (of people or vehicles) come together from different directions so as eventually to meet. |  The crowd converged on the speaker. 人群向演讲者聚集。| synonyms: meet, join, unite, merge, intersect, connect | antonyms: diverge, separate, split | verb
