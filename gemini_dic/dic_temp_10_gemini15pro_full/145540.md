overcommercialized: /ˌoʊvər kəˈmɜːrʃəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 过度商业化的 | Excessively focused on or exploited for commercial gain; having too much emphasis on commerce.  The focus is shifted from the inherent value or quality of something to its profit potential. | Times Square is often criticized for being overcommercialized.  时代广场经常因过度商业化而受到批评。

The Christmas holiday has become overcommercialized, losing some of its original meaning. 圣诞节已经变得过度商业化，失去了一些原本的意义。


|近义词: commercialized, over-commercialized, materialistic, mercenary |反义词: noncommercial, uncommercialized, authentic |词性: adjective
