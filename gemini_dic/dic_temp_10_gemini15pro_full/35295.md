clysmian: /klɪzˈmiən/ | adj. |  大洪水的，灾变性的 | Relating to a cataclysm, a sudden and violent change in the earth's surface; or a violent upheaval, especially social or political. | The clysmian event wiped out the dinosaurs.  大洪水事件灭绝了恐龙。The clysmian changes in the political landscape led to widespread unrest.  政治格局的灾变性变化导致了广泛的动荡。 | 近义词: catastrophic, cataclysmic, apocalyptic | 反义词: peaceful, tranquil | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Greek *kataklysmos* "deluge, flood," from *kataklyzein* "to inundate, deluge," from *kata-* "down" + *klyzein* "to wash, dash over."
