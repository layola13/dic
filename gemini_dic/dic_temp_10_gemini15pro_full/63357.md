entomological:/ˌɛntəməˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ | adj. | 昆虫学的，昆虫学上的 | Relating to the branch of zoology concerned with insects. |  The museum has a vast entomological collection. 博物馆收藏了大量的昆虫标本。|  The researcher published an entomological study on the behavior of ants.  这位研究人员发表了一篇关于蚂蚁行为的昆虫学研究。 |近义词: insect-related, insectological |反义词: N/A |词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From entomology + -ical. Entomology from French entomologie, from Modern Latin entomologia, from Ancient Greek ἔντομον (éntomon, “insect”) + -λογία (-logía, “-logy”).
