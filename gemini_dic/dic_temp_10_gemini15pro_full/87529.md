heterogeneously:/ˌhetərəˈdʒiːniəsli/
adjective
异质地，不均匀地
composed of parts of different kinds; diverse
例句：The population of the city is heterogeneously distributed, with pockets of affluence next to areas of poverty.  这个城市的人口分布不均匀，富裕地区紧挨着贫困地区。

例句：The data was collected heterogeneously from various sources. 这些数据是从不同来源异构收集的。

近义词：diversely, variously, dissimilarly
反义词：homogeneously, uniformly, similarly
词性: adverb

词源: From heterogeneous + -ly.  Heterogeneous comes from the Greek heterogenēs, from hetero- "different" + genos "kind, race."
