myopic:/maɪˈɑːpɪk/ | adj. | 近视的；目光短浅的 | Lacking foresight or intellectual insight.  |  He made a myopic decision to invest all his money in one stock. 他目光短浅地把所有钱都投资在一支股票上。 | nearsighted, shortsighted | farsighted, prescient | adjective

myopia:/maɪˈoʊpiə/ | n. | 近视；缺乏远见 | Nearsightedness; lack of foresight or long-range perspective. |  His political myopia prevented him from seeing the long-term consequences of his actions. 他的政治短视使他看不到自己行为的长期后果。| nearsightedness, shortsightedness | foresight, farsightedness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *myopicus, from Greek μυωπικός (muōpikos), from μύωψ (muōps) "one who blinks," from μύειν (muein) "to shut the eyes" + ὤψ (ōps) "eye, face."
