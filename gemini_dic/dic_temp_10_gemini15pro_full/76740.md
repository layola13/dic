game-plans:/ˈɡeɪm plænz/
noun
游戏计划；作战方案；行动计划
A detailed plan or strategy for achieving success in a particular game, competition, or situation.
The coach laid out the game-plan for the upcoming match. 教练为即将到来的比赛制定了作战计划。
The company needs a new game-plan to increase sales.  公司需要一个新的行动计划来提高销售额。
synonyms: strategy, plan, scheme, tactic, approach
antonyms: improvisation, spontaneity
n.
