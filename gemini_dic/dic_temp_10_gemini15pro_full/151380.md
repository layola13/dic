parleying: /ˈpɑːrliɪŋ/ | n. | 谈判，会谈 |  A discussion or conference, especially one between enemies over terms of a truce or other matters. |  The general sent a messenger, parleying with the enemy for their surrender. 将军派了一名信使，与敌人谈判投降事宜。| synonyms: negotiating, conferring, discussing | antonyms: fighting, battling | noun

parley: /ˈpɑːrli/ | v. | 谈判，会谈 | To have a discussion, especially with an enemy, typically to discuss terms of a truce or other matters. |  The two sides parleyed for hours, trying to reach a peaceful resolution. 双方谈判了几个小时，试图达成和平解决方案。 | synonyms: negotiate, confer, discuss | antonyms: fight, battle | verb
