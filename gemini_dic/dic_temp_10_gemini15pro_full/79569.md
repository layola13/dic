gloomed:/ɡluːmd/| adj.|忧郁的，沮丧的| darkened or depressed, as in mood or appearance.  Characterized by gloom; dismal; dreary. | The gloomed sky hinted at impending rain.  阴沉的天空预示着即将到来的雨。| The team was gloomed after their defeat. 球队输球后很沮丧。|近义词：depressed, dejected, despondent, glum, melancholy, somber|反义词：cheerful, happy, bright, joyful|词性:形容词
词源: From the verb "gloom" + "-ed".  "Gloom" comes from Middle English *gloumen, from Old Norse *glāma "to stare, glimmer darkly".
