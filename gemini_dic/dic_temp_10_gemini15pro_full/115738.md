mariculturists:/ˌmærɪˈkʌltʃərɪsts/
n.
海水养殖者，海产养殖者
People who cultivate marine life, such as fish or shellfish, in a controlled environment.
Mariculturists play a vital role in providing seafood for the growing global population.  海水养殖者在为日益增长的全球人口提供海产品方面发挥着至关重要的作用。

The mariculturists carefully monitor the water quality in their fish farms.  海水养殖者仔细监测他们渔场的/鱼塘的水质。

Synonym: aquaculturists, fish farmers
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "hunter-gatherer" in a broad sense)
词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "mariculture" + "-ist"
"mariculture" comes from "mari-" (sea) + "culture" (cultivation)
"mari-" is from Latin "mare" (sea)
