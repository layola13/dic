loess:/loʊs/ /ˈləʊs/ | n. | 黄土 | A loosely compacted yellowish-gray deposit of windblown sediment of which extensive deposits occur, e.g., in eastern China and the American Midwest. | The fertile loess plateau of China supports a large agricultural population.  中国的肥沃黄土高原养活了大量农业人口。 | Example: The loess soils are highly erodible. 黄土非常容易被侵蚀。 | Synonyms: silt, dust, sediment | Antonyms: bedrock, rock | noun

loes:/loʊs/ /ˈləʊs/ | n. | (Scottish, archaic) praise | Praise or commendation | He received loes for his bravery. 他因勇敢而受到赞扬。 | Example: The bard sang loes to the king.  吟游诗人向国王歌颂赞美。| Synonyms: acclaim, commendation, applause | Antonyms: criticism, condemnation, censure | noun
