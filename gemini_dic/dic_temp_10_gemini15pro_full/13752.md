auctioning:/ˈɔːkʃənɪŋ/
v.
拍卖；竞卖
The process of selling something at an auction.
They are auctioning off their furniture. 他们正在拍卖他们的家具。
Sotheby's is auctioning a rare Van Gogh painting next month. 苏富比拍卖行下个月将拍卖一幅罕见的梵高画作。
selling, bidding, vending
buying, purchasing
verb
