outbarters:/ˌaʊtˈbɑːrtərz/
| n. |以物易物胜过他人者；在交易中胜过他人者|Someone who outdoes others in bartering or trading; a person who gets the better of a bargain.| He was a shrewd outbarter, always coming away with the best trades. 他是一个精明的交易者，总是能获得最好的交易。|近义词：trader, bargainer, negotiator|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "dupe" - someone easily tricked in a bargain)|词性: Noun
