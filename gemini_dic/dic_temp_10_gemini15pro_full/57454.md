draff:/dræf/
noun
糟粕，废料，渣滓
worthless refuse; dregs; hogwash
The brewer discarded the spent grains, or draff, after the mashing process.  酿酒师在糖化过程后丢弃了用过的谷物，也就是糟粕。
The critic dismissed the movie as mere draff. 评论家认为这部电影纯粹是垃圾。
synonyms: dregs, refuse, waste, rubbish, garbage, trash, dross
antonyms: essence, valuable, treasure
