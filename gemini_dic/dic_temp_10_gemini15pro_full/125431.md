multiple: /ˈmʌltɪpl/ | adj. n. | 多个的，多样的；倍数 | Having or involving several parts, elements, or members.  A number that can be divided by another number without a remainder. |  例句：The company has multiple offices across the country.  这家公司在全国各地有多个办事处。| 近义词：numerous, various, manifold | 反义词：single, singular | 词性：形容词, 名词


multiple: /ˈmʌltɪpl/ | n. | 倍数 | A product of a quantity by an integer. |  例句：12 is a multiple of 3. 12是3的倍数。| 近义词：product | 反义词：factor, divisor | 词性：名词


Etymology:  From Late Latin *multiplus, from Latin multus (“much, many”) + -plus (“-fold”).
