dowel:/ˈdaʊəl/| n. |销钉，木钉，暗榫|A short wooden or metal rod used to reinforce a joint or to align parts.|例句：He used a dowel to join the two pieces of wood. 他用一个木钉连接两块木头。

例句：The shelves were held up by dowels and brackets.  这些架子由销钉和支架支撑。|近义词：peg, pin, rod|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
