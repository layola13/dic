disspirit:/dɪˈspɪrɪt/
v.
使沮丧，使气馁
To discourage or depress; to damp the spirits of.
The team was dispirited by their recent loss.  该队因最近的失利而士气低落。

The constant rain dispirited the hikers.  持续的降雨使徒步旅行者感到沮丧。

近义词:discourage, deject, demoralize, dispirit
反义词:encourage, hearten, cheer
词性:动词
