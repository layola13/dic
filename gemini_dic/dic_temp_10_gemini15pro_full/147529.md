overpassionate: /ˌoʊvərˈpæʃənɪt/ | adj. | 过于激动的，过度热情的 | Excessively passionate; having or showing too much emotion or enthusiasm.  | She delivered an overpassionate speech about the importance of animal rights. 她就动物权利的重要性发表了一场过于激情的演讲。|  He's overpassionate about football, which can sometimes be annoying. 他对足球过于热情，有时很烦人。 | 近义词:  fervent, impassioned, zealous, fanatical | 反义词: apathetic, indifferent, dispassionate | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From 'over-' (prefix indicating excess) + 'passionate' (from Late Latin 'passionatus', past participle of 'passionari' "to suffer," from Latin 'passio' "suffering, enduring," from past participle stem of 'pati' "to suffer, endure").
