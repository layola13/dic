coresident:/ˌkɔːˈrɛzɪdənt/| adj. n.|共同居住的，同住者|Living in the same place or residence; a person who lives in the same place as another.|Example: The coresident siblings shared a bedroom.  同住的兄弟姐妹共用一个卧室。|Synonyms: cohabitant, roommate, housemate|Antonyms: nonresident, outsider|adjective

coresident:/ˌkɔːˈrɛzɪdənt/| n.|同住者|A person who lives in the same place as another.|Example:  My coresident at the hostel was very friendly. 我在旅舍的同住者非常友好。|Synonyms: cohabitant, roommate, housemate|Antonyms: nonresident, outsider|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "co-" (together) + "resident" (dwelling, residing).  "Resident" comes from the Latin "residere" (to reside, sit back, remain).
