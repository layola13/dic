fatso:/ˈfætsəʊ/ (UK) /ˈfætsoʊ/ (US)| n.|胖子，肥仔|A very fat person (offensive).  This word is considered rude and should be avoided. |Example 1: "He called him a fatso, which was very unkind." 他叫他胖子，这很刻薄。

Example 2:  "Don't call people fatso, it's hurtful." 不要叫别人胖子，这很伤人。|Near synonyms:  overweight person, obese person (more formal and polite), chubby (less offensive, often used for children)|Antonyms:  thin person, skinny person, slim person|Noun
