foulest:/ˈfaʊləst/| adj.|最污秽的；最邪恶的；最糟糕的|Superlative form of foul: most foul.  It describes something extremely unpleasant, dirty, or morally bad.|The foulest crime imaginable.  可以想象到的最肮脏的罪行。|Synonyms: dirtiest, nastiest, vilest, wickedest, worst|Antonyms: cleanest, purest, best, finest|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *fūl, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch vuil and German faul ‘rotten, lazy’.
