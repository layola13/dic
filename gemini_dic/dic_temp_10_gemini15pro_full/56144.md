documentary:/ˌdɑːkjəˈmentəri/ |adj. n.| 纪录片的，文献的；纪录片 | Relating to, based on, or consisting of documents; a film or television or radio programme that provides a factual record or report. |例句：I watched a documentary about the history of the Roman Empire. 我看了一部关于罗马帝国历史的纪录片。| 近义词：factual, nonfictional, record, archive | 反义词：fictional, imaginary |词性: 形容词, 名词

documentational:/ˌdɑːkjəmenˈteɪʃənl/ | adj. | 文件的，纪录的 |  Relating to or based on documentation. | 例句：The lawyer requested all documentational evidence related to the case. 律师要求提供与案件相关的所有文件证据。 | 近义词：documentary, recorded, archival | 反义词：unrecorded, undocumented | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From document + -al (suffix forming adjectives). The word "document" itself ultimately derives from Latin "documentum," meaning "lesson" or "proof," from "docēre," meaning "to teach."
