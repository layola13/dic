dye:/daɪ/
n.|染料，染色|A substance used to color materials.|The artist used a natural dye made from plants. 艺术家使用了一种由植物制成的天然染料。
dye:/daɪ/
vt.|染色，染|To color something by soaking it in dye.|She dyed her hair blue. 她把头发染成了蓝色。


Etymology->
From Old English *dēag, from Proto-Germanic *daʒaz, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰeh₁- (“to put, place, do”). Cognate with Dutch deeg (“dough”), German Teig (“dough”), Old Norse deig (“dough”).  The original meaning was related to kneading and working a substance, which later evolved to include the process of coloring.


Synonyms->
color, stain, tint, pigment


Antonyms->
bleach, fade
