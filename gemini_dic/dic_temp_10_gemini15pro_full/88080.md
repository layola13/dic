highly:/ˈhaɪli/| adv.|高度地，非常，极，很|To a high degree; extremely.|例句：He is a highly skilled musician. 他是一位技艺高超的音乐家。|例句：She is highly regarded in her profession. 她在她的职业中备受尊敬。|近义词：extremely, very, exceptionally, remarkably|反义词：slightly, moderately, barely, hardly|词性: 副词
词源: From Middle English *highly, *hyly, from Old English *hīhlice (attested as hēalice), from hēah (“high”) + -līce (adverbial suffix). Cognate with Scots hiely, highly (“highly”), West Frisian heechlik (“highly”), Dutch hooglijk (“highly”), German höchlich (“highly”).
