nascency:/ˈneɪsənsi/| n.| 初生, 诞生, 起源|The nascency of the internet brought about a revolution in communication. 互联网的诞生引发了通讯领域的革命。|The project is still in its nascency.  这个项目仍处于起步阶段。|近义词：birth, origin, beginning, infancy, inception|反义词：end, demise, decline, maturity|词性:名词

Etymology: From Latin *nascentia (“a being born”), from nascens, present participle of nascor (“to be born”).
