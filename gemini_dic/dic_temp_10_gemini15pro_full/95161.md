inarticulatenesses:/ɪnɑːrˈtɪkjʊlətɪsnɪsɪz/
|n.|表达不清，不善言辞|The state of being unable to express oneself clearly or fluently.|His inarticulateness made it difficult to understand his point. 他不善言辞，难以理解他的意思。|Synonyms: incoherence, unintelligibility, inability to express oneself|Antonyms: eloquence, fluency, articulateness|Noun

inarticulate:/ˌɪnɑːrˈtɪkjʊlət/
|adj.|说不清楚的，表达不清的|Unable to express oneself clearly or fluently.|He became inarticulate with rage. 他愤怒得说不出话来。|Synonyms: speechless, tongue-tied, mumbled|Antonyms: articulate, eloquent, fluent|Adjective
