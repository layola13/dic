oatmeal:/ˈoʊtmil/ (US), /ˈəʊtmiːl/ (UK)| n. |燕麦片，燕麦粥|A meal made from ground oats, typically boiled in water or milk. |例句：I had a bowl of oatmeal for breakfast. 我早餐吃了一碗燕麦粥。|近义词：porridge, rolled oats|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider contrasting foods like "bacon and eggs")|Noun


oatmeals:/ˈoʊtmilz/ (US), /ˈəʊtmiːlz/ (UK)| n. |(较少用) 多种燕麦食品/多份燕麦粥|Refers to different types of oatmeal or multiple servings of oatmeal.  Less commonly used than the singular form.|例句：The restaurant offered several different oatmeals on the menu. 这家餐厅的菜单上提供了几种不同的燕麦食品。 |近义词：porridge, rolled oats|反义词: (No direct antonym)|Noun
