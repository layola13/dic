ecclesiast:/ɪˌkliːziˈæst/ | n. | 传道者；讲道者 | A preacher or a person who preaches. |  The ecclesiast delivered a powerful sermon on forgiveness. 传道者做了一场关于宽恕的强有力的布道。| synonym: preacher, minister, parson | antonym:  congregant, parishioner | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ecclesiastes, from Ancient Greek ἐκκλησιαστής (ekklēsiastḗs, “member of the assembly, speaker in the assembly”), from ἐκκλησιάζω (ekklēsiázō, “I belong to the assembly”), from ἐκκλησία (ekklēsía, “assembly”). Ultimately from ἐκ (ek, “out”) + καλέω (kaléō, “I call”).
