impenitency:/ɪmˈpɛnɪtənsi/
n.
不悔悟，顽固，执迷不悟
Lack of remorse or regret for wrongdoing; stubborn persistence in sin.
His impenitency made it difficult to forgive him. 他的顽固不化使得很难原谅他。
Near synonyms:  obduracy, hardness of heart, unrepentance, remorselessness, intransigence
Antonyms: remorse, penitence, repentance, contrition
noun

impenitent:/ɪmˈpɛnɪtənt/
adj.
不悔悟的，顽固的，执迷不悟的
Not feeling or showing regret or remorse for wrongdoing.
He remained impenitent despite the evidence against him. 尽管证据确凿，他仍然拒不悔改。
Near synonyms:  unrepentant, remorseless, unremorseful, hardened, obdurate
Antonyms: repentant, remorseful, contrite, penitent
adjective
