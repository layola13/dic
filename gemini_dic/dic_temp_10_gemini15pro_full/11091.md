archaized:/ˈɑːrkeɪaɪzd/ | adj. | 古体化的，仿古的 | Made to appear or sound old-fashioned or archaic. |  The author archaized the language in his novel to create a sense of historical authenticity.  作者在他的小说中使用了古体化的语言，以营造一种历史的真实感。 | Example: The building was archaized to match the historical period. 这座建筑被仿古以配合历史时期。 | Synonyms: antiquated, obsolete, old-fashioned, archaic | Antonyms: modern, contemporary, current | Word Type: Adjective

archaize:/ˈɑːrkeɪaɪz/ | v. |  (使)古体化，(使)仿古  | To make something appear or sound old-fashioned or archaic. | The playwright archaized the dialogue to give the play a classical feel. 剧作家将对白古体化，赋予戏剧一种古典的感觉。 | Example:  They archaized the spelling of some words in the document. 他们在文件中把一些词的拼写古体化了。 | Synonyms: antiquate,  obsolete  | Antonyms: modernize, update | Word Type: Verb
