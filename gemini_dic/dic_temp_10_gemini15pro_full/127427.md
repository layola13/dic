neatify:/ˈniːtɪfaɪ/| v. |使整洁，使整齐|To make neat and orderly; to tidy up.|She neatified her desk before leaving the office. 她离开办公室前整理了桌子。|近义词：tidy, organize, arrange, clean, straighten|反义词:mess up, clutter, disorganize|词性:动词
Etymology: From neat + -ify.  "Neat" comes from Old French "net" meaning "clean, pure, clear," ultimately from Latin "nitidus" meaning "shining, bright, elegant."
