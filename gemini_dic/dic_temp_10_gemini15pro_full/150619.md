papilionaceous:/pəˌpɪliəˈneɪʃəs/| adj.|蝶形的，蝶状的|Resembling a butterfly, especially in shape; specifically, having a corolla like that of the sweet pea, with a standard, wings, and keel.|Example: The garden was filled with papilionaceous flowers of vibrant hues.  花园里开满了色彩鲜艳的蝶形花。|Example:  Many members of the Fabaceae family have papilionaceous blossoms. 许多豆科植物都有蝶形花。|Synonym: butterfly-like,  fabaceous|Antonym: (No direct antonym, consider "irregular" for flower shape)|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *papilionāceus*, from papilio (“butterfly”) +‎ -āceus.
