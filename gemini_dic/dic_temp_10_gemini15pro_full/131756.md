noncongratulatory:/ˌnɒnkənˈɡrætʃʊlətɔːri/ /ˌnɑːnkənˈɡrætʃələtɔːri/
| adj. |非祝贺的，不表示祝贺的|Not expressing or characterized by congratulations.  Lacking in congratulatory remarks or expressions.|Her response to his promotion was surprisingly noncongratulatory. 她对他升职的反应令人惊讶地没有表示祝贺。|Near Synonyms:  uncongratulatory,  uncelebratory,  reserved,  subdued,  cool|Antonyms: congratulatory, celebratory, enthusiastic,  laudatory|adjective
