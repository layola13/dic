neoprenes:/ˈniːəˌpriːnz/| n. |氯丁橡胶；氯丁橡胶制品|A synthetic rubber resistant to oils, heat, and weathering, used especially in wetsuits and waders.|例句：The neoprenes kept the divers warm in the cold water. 氯丁橡胶让潜水员在冰冷的水里保持温暖。

例句：He bought a pair of neoprenes for his upcoming surfing trip. 他为即将到来的冲浪之旅买了一双氯丁橡胶鞋。|近义词：polychloroprene, synthetic rubber|反义词: None|词性:noun
