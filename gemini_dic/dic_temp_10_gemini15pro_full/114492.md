malacosteon: /ˌmæləkɒsˈtiːɒn/ | n. | 软骨; 骨软化症 |A condition characterized by softening of the bones.  It is often caused by a deficiency in vitamin D or calcium. |  Rickets in children can lead to malacosteon.  儿童佝偻病会导致骨软化症。|  The doctor diagnosed the patient with malacosteon after observing the x-rays.  医生观察X光片后诊断病人患有骨软化症。 | synonyms: osteomalacia, bone softening | antonyms: osteosclerosis, bone hardening | noun
