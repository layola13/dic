exploitation:/ˌɛksplɔɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. |开发, 利用, 剥削 |The act of using something or someone unfairly or selfishly for personal gain. |The exploitation of workers is a serious issue in many developing countries.  在许多发展中国家，对工人的剥削是一个严重的问题。| synonyms: abuse, misuse, manipulation, profiteering | antonyms: conservation, preservation, protection | noun

exploitation:/ˌɛksplɔɪˈteɪʃən/ | n. |开采, 开拓 |The act of making use of natural resources, especially for industrial or commercial purposes. |The exploitation of natural resources is essential for economic growth. 对自然资源的开采对经济增长至关重要。| synonyms: development, utilization, harnessing | antonyms: neglect, abandonment | noun
