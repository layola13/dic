myriadfold:/ˈmɪriədfoʊld/| adj. adv.|无数倍的，大量的|Increased by a factor of a myriad; in many thousands of aspects or ways.  Increased very greatly in number or degree.|The flowers bloomed myriadfold in the spring. 春天里鲜花盛开了无数倍。|The impact of the discovery was myriadfold, affecting everything from medicine to engineering. 这项发现的影响是多方面的，从医学到工程都受到了影响。|近义词：manifold, multifold, numerous|反义词：single, few|词性:形容词，副词|词源: From "myriad" (meaning a very large number, ten thousand) + "-fold" (indicating multiplication or repetition).
