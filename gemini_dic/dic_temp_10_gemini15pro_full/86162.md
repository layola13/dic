heartseed:/ˈhɑːrtˌsiːd/| n. |心之种, 心灵之种|A metaphorical or symbolic seed representing the core essence, emotions, or potential of the heart.  Often used in fiction, fantasy, or poetic contexts. |The heartseed planted in the young boy's heart grew into a powerful love for nature.|近义词: core, essence, kernel, germ, seed, root |反义词: (No direct antonym, consider antonyms for related concepts like "apathy," "indifference," or "emptiness")|noun
