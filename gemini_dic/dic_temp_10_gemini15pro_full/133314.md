nonexplorative:/ˌnɒnɪkˈsplɔːrətɪv/ | adj. | 非探索性的，非探究性的 | Not exploratory; not involving or intended for exploration or investigation. |  The study adopted a nonexplorative approach, focusing on confirming existing hypotheses. 这项研究采用了非探索性方法，重点是证实现有的假设。

The committee's nonexplorative review simply summarized previous findings. 委员会的非探索性审查只是总结了以前的调查结果。 | 近义词: noninvestigative, descriptive, confirmatory | 反义词: exploratory, investigative, inquiring | 词性: 形容词

词源:
Prefix "non-" (not) + "exploratory" (from "explore" - from Old French explorer "to search out," from Latin explorare "search out, examine, explore," from ex- "out" +plorare "to cry out, weep, wail, implore").
