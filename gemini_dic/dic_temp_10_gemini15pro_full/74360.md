frailer:/ˈfreɪlər/| adj.|更虚弱的，更脆弱的|comparative form of frail: weaker and more delicate than before|例句：After his illness, he looked much frailer.  他病后看起来虚弱多了。

例句：The frailer seedlings were removed to give the stronger ones more space.  较弱的幼苗被移除，以便给更强壮的幼苗更多空间。|近义词：weaker, feebler, more delicate, more fragile|反义词:stronger, healthier, more robust|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *freilere, comparative of *freil (“frail”), from Old French frele, fraile, feminine and oblique case forms of frels, from Late Latin fragilis (“fragile”), from Latin frango (“I break”).  Double r by analogy with similar adjectives.
