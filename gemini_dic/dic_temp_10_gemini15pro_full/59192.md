This refers to a group of symptoms affecting the digestive system, characterized by discomfort or pain in the upper abdomen. | He frequently suffers from dyspepsias after eating rich foods. 他吃完油腻食物后经常消化不良。 | 近义词: indigestion, upset stomach, heartburn, acid reflux | 反义词: (No direct antonym, consider using terms like "good digestion" or "healthy stomach") | 词性: noun
