flyfish:/ˈflaɪfɪʃ/ (US) /ˈflaɪfɪʃ/ (UK)| v. n.|飞蝇钓（动词）; 飞蝇钓鱼者（名词）|To fish using artificial flies as bait.  As a noun, it refers to a person who fly fishes. |He likes to flyfish in the mountain streams. 他喜欢在山涧溪流中飞蝇钓。|近义词：angle, fish|反义词: - |verb, noun
