hyperethically:/ˌhaɪpərˈeθɪkli/| adv.|超级伦理地，过度伦理地|Acting in a way that goes beyond typical ethical considerations, often to an excessive or impractical degree.|例句：He approached the situation hyperethically, refusing even the smallest gift that might be perceived as a conflict of interest. 他以超级伦理的方式处理这种情况，即使是最小的礼物也拒绝接受，因为他认为这可能被视为利益冲突。

例句：Her hyperethical stance on environmentalism led her to live completely off the grid, which proved to be quite challenging. 她对环保主义的超级伦理立场导致她完全脱离电网生活，这被证明是相当具有挑战性的。|近义词：over-scrupulous, excessively ethical|反义词：unethical, immoral|词性:副词

词源:
"hyper-" (Greek): over, beyond, excessive
"-ethical" (Greek): relating to ethics or morality
