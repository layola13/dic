nosiest:/ˈnɔɪziɪst/ (US), /ˈnɔɪzɪɪst/ (UK)| adj.|最爱管闲事的；最好奇的|Showing too much curiosity about other people's affairs.|例句：She's the nosiest person in the office, always trying to find out everyone's business. 她是办公室里最好奇的人，总是想打听每个人的事。

例句：The nosiest neighbor always peeked through her curtains. 最爱管闲事的邻居总是透过窗帘偷看。|近义词：inquisitive, curious, prying, meddlesome|反义词：uninterested, indifferent, discreet|词性：形容词
