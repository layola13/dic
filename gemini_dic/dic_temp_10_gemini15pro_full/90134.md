hotbedding:/ˈhɒtˌbɛdɪŋ/ | n. | 共享办公桌；轮用办公桌；办公桌轮用制 |The practice of having employees share desks or workspaces, typically on different shifts.  This allows companies to maximize office space utilization, particularly when some employees work remotely or have flexible schedules.| Many companies are adopting hotbedding to reduce overhead costs. 许多公司正在采用办公桌轮用制来降低管理费用。|Example: With the rise of remote work, hotbedding has become a popular strategy for companies looking to optimize office space. 随着远程办公的兴起，办公桌轮用制已成为希望优化办公空间的公司的流行策略。

hotbedding:/ˈhɑːtˌbɛdɪŋ/ | n. |温床;滋生地;温床;滋生地|A situation or place that favours the development or rapid growth of something, especially something unwelcome.| Extremism finds a hotbed in poverty and lack of education. 极端主义在贫困和缺乏教育的环境中找到了滋生的温床。 | Example: The overcrowded refugee camp became a hotbed of disease.  拥挤的难民营成了疾病滋生的温床。|Synonyms: breeding ground, seedbed, cradle,  nest|Antonyms: deterrent, preventative, impediment|noun
