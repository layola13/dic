comelier:/ˈkʌmliər/| adj.|更美丽的，更清秀的|Comparative form of comely: more comely.  Meaning more attractive or pleasing in appearance.|例句：She was even comelier than her sister. 她甚至比她姐姐更漂亮。

例句：The comelier of the two sisters caught the prince's eye.  两姐妹中较漂亮的那位吸引了王子的目光。|近义词：prettier, more attractive, more beautiful, fairer, handsomer|反义词：uglier, homelier, less attractive, plainer|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *comlyer, comparative of comly (“comely”), from Old English cymlic (“comely, lovely, splendid, fine, choice, elegant, noble, glorious”), equivalent to cyme (“suitable, fitting, proper”) +‎ -lic (“-ly”). More at become.
