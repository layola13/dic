crucifix:/ˈkruːsəfɪks/| n. |十字架；耶稣受难像|A representation of a cross with a figure of Jesus Christ crucified on it.|She wore a small silver crucifix around her neck. 她脖子上戴着一个小的银质十字架。|近义词:cross|反义词:|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *crucifixus*, past participle of crucifigere "to crucify," from Latin crux "cross" + figere "to fix, fasten."
