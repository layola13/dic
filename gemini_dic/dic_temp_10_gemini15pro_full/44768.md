crump:/krʌmp/ | n. | 脆响声；爆炸声；(尤指炮弹落地的)沉闷声 |A dull, heavy thud or explosive sound, especially of a shell exploding. | The crump of the mortar shook the ground. 迫击炮的爆炸声震动了地面。|  synonyms: thud, bang, boom, explosion | antonyms: whisper, murmur | noun

crump:/krʌmp/ | v. | (使)发出低沉的爆炸声；(使)嘎吱作响 |To make a dull, heavy thud or explosive sound; to crunch or crackle. | The snow crumped under our feet. 雪在我们脚下嘎吱作响。| synonyms: crunch, crackle, explode | antonyms: soften, melt | verb
