blinking:/ˈblɪŋkɪŋ/ | adj.  | 眨眼的，闪烁的 | The act of closing and opening the eyes rapidly. Can also refer to lights flashing on and off. | The blinking lights of the Christmas tree were mesmerizing. 闪烁的圣诞树灯光令人着迷。| synonyms: flashing, flickering, winking, twinkling | antonyms: steady, constant | adjective

blinking:/ˈblɪŋkɪŋ/ | n. | 眨眼，闪烁 | The act of blinking. | The rapid blinking of his eyes betrayed his nervousness.  他快速的眨眼暴露了他的紧张。| synonyms: flash, flicker, wink, twinkle | antonyms: steadiness, constancy | noun
