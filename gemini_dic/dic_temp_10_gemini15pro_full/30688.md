certes:/ˈsɜːrtiːz/ /ˈsɜːrteɪz/ | adv. | 的确，无疑 | Indeed; certainly.  Used for emphasis or to concede a point. | Certes, it is a difficult problem. 的确，这是一个难题。| 近义词: indeed, certainly, truly, assuredly | 反义词: perhaps, maybe, possibly | 词性: 副词

词源: From Old French *certes, from Latin *certus “settled, fixed, sure," ultimately from PIE *ḱerto-, past participle of *ḱer- “to turn, bend.”  Relates to words like "certain."
