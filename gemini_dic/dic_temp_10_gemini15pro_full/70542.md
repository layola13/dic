filiciform:/fɪˈlɪsɪfɔːrm/| adj.|蕨形的，像蕨类植物的|Resembling a fern or fern frond; fern-shaped.  Having the form or shape of a fern.|例句：The plant had delicate, filiciform leaves. 这种植物有纤细的蕨形叶子。

例句：The fossil showed a clear filiciform imprint.  这块化石显示出一个清晰的蕨类植物形状的印记。|近义词：fernlike, fern-shaped|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective

Etymology:  From Latin *filicis* (genitive of *filix*, "fern") + *-formis* ("-shaped," "having the form of").
