crash-land:/ˈkræʃ lænd/ (US) /ˈkræʃ lænd/ (UK)| v. | 紧急迫降；坠落 | To land roughly or violently, as of an aircraft, often resulting in damage. | The pilot had to crash-land the plane in a field. 飞行员不得不将飞机迫降在田野里。

The plane crash-landed after experiencing engine failure.  飞机在发动机故障后紧急迫降。

| 近义词：force-land, ditch | 反义词: land smoothly | 词性: 动词


crash-land:/ˈkræʃ lænd/ (US) /ˈkræʃ lænd/ (UK)| n. | 紧急迫降；坠落 | An instance of crash-landing. | The crash-land was surprisingly smooth considering the circumstances. 考虑到当时的情况，这次迫降出奇地平稳。

The investigators examined the wreckage from the crash-land.  调查人员检查了坠机现场的残骸。


| 近义词：forced landing, ditching | 反义词: smooth landing | 词性: 名词
