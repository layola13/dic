glyptography:/ɡlɪpˈtɒɡrəfi/ | n. |雕刻术，雕刻学|The art or science of engraving, especially inscriptions on gems or precious stones.  It also refers to the practice of carving figures or designs in relief on a hard surface.|  The museum's collection includes examples of ancient Egyptian glyptography.  博物馆的藏品包括古埃及雕刻术的例子。 | 近义词: engraving, carving, sculpture | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *gluptós* (carved) + *-graphía* (writing, representing).
