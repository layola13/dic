dislocate: /ˈdɪsləkeɪt/ (US), /dɪsˈləʊkeɪt/ (UK)
| vt. | 使脱位；使脱臼；扰乱；使混乱 | To displace (a bone) from its normal position in a joint. To disrupt the normal arrangement or connection of. | The fall dislocated his shoulder. 这次摔跤使他的肩膀脱臼了。|  The war dislocated the economy. 战争扰乱了经济。 | synonyms: displace, disturb, disrupt, unsettle | antonyms: locate, order, organize, connect | verb


dislocate: /ˈdɪsləkeɪt/ (US), /dɪsˈləʊkeɪt/ (UK)
| adj. | 脱节的；混乱的 |  Out of its proper or usual place, especially dislocated or disjointed.  | The refugees told stories of their dislocated lives. 难民讲述了他们颠沛流离的生活。 | synonyms: disordered, chaotic, disrupted | antonyms: ordered, organized, connected | adjective
