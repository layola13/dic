fricandeau:/ˌfriːkænˈdoʊ/
n.
红烧肉；焖肉
A dish of meat, typically veal, that is larded and braised.
She prepared a delicious fricandeau for the dinner party. 她为晚宴准备了一道美味的红烧肉。
近义词:pot roast, braised meat
反义词:(None - no direct opposite for a specific dish)
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *fricandeau*, from *fricande*, "slice of veal for frying," from Old French *friquandi*, "fried dish," of Germanic origin.
