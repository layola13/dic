echolocation:/ˌekəʊləʊˈkeɪʃən/ /ˌɛkoʊloʊˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 回声定位；回声测距 | the location of objects by reflected sound, in particular that used by animals such as dolphins and bats. | Bats use echolocation to navigate in the dark.  蝙蝠利用回声定位在黑暗中导航。| synonym: biosonar | antonym: None | noun
