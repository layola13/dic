allaying:/əˈleɪɪŋ/
verb
减轻，缓和
reducing or diminishing the intensity of (something unpleasant, such as pain, fear, or anger).
The government is trying to allay public fears about the virus. 政府正在努力减轻公众对病毒的恐惧。

近义词: mitigating, assuaging, relieving, easing, softening, moderating, lessening, diminishing, reducing, abating, tempering, alleviating, tranquilizing, calming, soothing, pacifying, mollifying, appeasing
反义词: aggravating, intensifying, exacerbating, increasing, heightening, worsening
词性: verb (present participle of allay)


词源:
From Middle English *aleien, *aleggen, from Old French *aleier, *alegier (“to alleviate, lighten”), from Latin allēvāre, from ad- (“to”) + lēvāre (“to lift, lighten”), from levis (“light”).
