non-traceable:/ˌnɒnˈtreɪsəbl/ | adj. | 不可追踪的，无法追溯的 | Not able to be traced or tracked back to its origin or source. | The money was transferred through a series of non-traceable accounts. 这笔钱是通过一系列无法追踪的账户转移的。 | The origin of the virus is non-traceable. 这种病毒的起源无法追溯。 | 近义词: untraceable, undetectable, unidentifiable | 反义词: traceable, identifiable, detectable | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non, meaning "not"
traceable (adjective): from trace (verb) + -able (suffix). Trace comes from Old French tracer "to draw, sketch," from Latin *tractiare, frequentative of trahere "to pull, draw."  The suffix -able derives from Latin -abilis, meaning "capable of being."
