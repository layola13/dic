out-valued:/ˌaʊtˈvæljuːd/ | v. | 胜过；价值超过 | To exceed in value; to be worth more than. |

例句：
The new house out-valued the old one by $50,000.
新房子的价值比旧房子高出5万美元。

His experience out-valued his youthful competitor's enthusiasm.
他的经验比他年轻对手的热情更有价值。

近义词：surpass, exceed, outstrip, outdo, better
反义词：undervalue,  be inferior to

词性：动词
