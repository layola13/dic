honorary:/ˈɑːnəˌreri/ /ˈɒnərəri/| adj.|荣誉的，名誉的|Conferred as an honor, without the usual requirements or duties.|She received an honorary degree from Harvard University. 她获得了哈佛大学的荣誉学位。|
honorary:/ˈɑːnəˌreri/ /ˈɒnərəri/| adj.|义务的|Performing duties or holding a position without pay.|He is an honorary member of the committee. 他是委员会的荣誉会员。|
honorary:/ˈɑːnəˌreri/ /ˈɒnərəri/| adj.|名义上的|Existing in name only.|He's the honorary chairman, but he doesn't actually do anything. 他是名誉主席，但实际上什么也不做。|


Etymology->
From Late Latin *honorarius*, from Latin honor (“honor”).

Synonyms->
titular, nominal, symbolic, ceremonial

Antonyms->
substantive, actual, real, paid
