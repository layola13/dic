ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + expulsion.  "Expulsion" comes from Late Middle English expulsion, from Old French expulsion, from Latin expulsiōn- (stem of expulsiō ), from the verb expellere "to drive out," from ex- "out" + pellere "to push, drive."
