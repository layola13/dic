cyclosis:/saɪˈkloʊsɪs/
n.
细胞质环流，胞质环流
Streaming movement of the cytoplasm within a living cell.
The cyclosis distributed nutrients throughout the cell. 细胞质环流将营养物质输送到整个细胞。
近义词：cytoplasmic streaming
反义词：（无常用反义词）
词性：名词

Etymology:  From Greek *kýklos* (circle) + *-osis* (process, condition).
