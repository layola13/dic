discomposing:/ˌdɪskəmˈpoʊzɪŋ/

discompose:/ˌdɪskəmˈpoʊz/
| vt. |使不安，使心烦意乱|To disturb the composure of; to upset or agitate.|The loud noise discomposed the speaker. 巨大的噪音使演讲者心烦意乱。|Synonyms: unsettle, disturb, perturb, ruffle|Antonyms: calm, soothe, comfort|verb
