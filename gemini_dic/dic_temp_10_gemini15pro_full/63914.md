epiphyllous:/ˌɛpɪˈfɪləs/| adj.|叶生的，叶上的|Growing on, or attached to, the surface of a leaf or leaves.  Referring to fungi, algae, lichens, or liverworts that grow on leaves.|Epiphyllous algae are common in humid environments.  叶生藻类在潮湿环境中很常见。|近义词:foliicolous|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider terms like terrestrial, saxicolous, or corticolous depending on the specific contrasting location)|词性:adjective
