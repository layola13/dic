manufacturability:/ˌmænjʊˌfæktʃərəˈbɪləti/
n.
可制造性
The ease with which a product can be manufactured.
The manufacturability of the new design is a key concern.  这个新设计的可制造性是一个关键问题。
producibility, feasibility
impossibility, impracticability
noun
