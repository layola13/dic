instamp:/ɪnˈstæmp/ | v. | 盖印；铭刻；使铭记 | To impress or imprint something firmly, as if with a stamp. |  He instamped his seal on the document.  他将他的印章盖在文件上。

The experience was instamped on his memory. 那次经历深深地铭刻在他的记忆中。 | 近义词：imprint, impress, engrave | 反义词：erase, efface | 词性: 动词
