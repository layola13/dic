marionis:/ˌmæriˈoʊnɪs/ | n. | 木偶，牵线木偶 |  While "marionis" isn't a standard English word, it appears to be a plural form derived from "marionette," meaning puppet. It likely originates from the French word "marionnette,"  a diminutive of "Marion," a popular name for the Virgin Mary.
