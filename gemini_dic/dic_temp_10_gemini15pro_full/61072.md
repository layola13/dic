ellipsis:/ɪˈlɪpsɪs/ /iˈlipsis/| n.|省略号，省略，|An ellipsis is a series of dots that indicates an intentional omission of a word, sentence, or section from a text without altering its original meaning. |She trailed off, her voice fading into an ellipsis. 她渐渐没了声音，最后只留下省略号。| The contract included a clause with an ellipsis, suggesting some terms were intentionally left out.  合同中有一条款包含省略号，表明有些条款被故意省略了。|近义词：omission, deletion, gap|反义词:inclusion, addition|noun
