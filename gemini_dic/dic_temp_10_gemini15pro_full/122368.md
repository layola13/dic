mizzen:/ˈmɪzən/| n. |后桅; 后桅帆|The mizzen mast is the third mast from forward on a ship having three or more masts. |后桅是具有三个或更多桅杆的船上从船头算起的第三个桅杆。|
mizzen:/ˈmɪzən/| adj. |后桅的; 后部的|The mizzen sail is smaller than the main sail. |后桅帆比主帆小。|
