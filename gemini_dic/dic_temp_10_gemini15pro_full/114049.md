magnetics:/mæɡˈnɛtɪks/ | n. | 磁学; 磁性材料; 磁力 | the study of magnetism or magnetic materials and phenomena.  It can also refer to magnetic components or devices. |  The magnetics of this new alloy are quite remarkable. 这种新合金的磁性非常显著。| synonyms: magnetism, magnetic properties, magnetic forces | antonyms: non-magnetic properties | noun

magnetics:/mæɡˈnɛtɪks/ | adj. | 磁的; 有磁性的 | related to or exhibiting magnetism.  This usage is less common than the noun form. | The magnetics field is strong here.  这里的磁场很强。（更常见的说法是 magnetic field）| synonyms: magnetic | antonyms: non-magnetic | adjective
