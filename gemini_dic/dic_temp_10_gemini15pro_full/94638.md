impressibility:/ɪmˌprɛsəˈbɪləti/
n.
易受影响；敏感性；可印性
the quality of being easily impressed or influenced.
Her impressibility makes her vulnerable to emotional manipulation. 她的易受影响性使她容易受到情绪操控。
近义词：sensitivity, receptiveness, responsiveness
反义词：insensitivity, impassivity, resistance

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: from Old French *impressibilité, from impressible (see impressible).
