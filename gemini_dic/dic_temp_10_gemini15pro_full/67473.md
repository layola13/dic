extorters:/ɪkˈstɔːrtərz/
noun
勒索者；敲诈者
People who obtain something, especially money, from someone by force, threats, or unlawful pressure.
The extortioners threatened to release damaging information unless they were paid a large sum of money. 勒索者威胁要公布破坏性的信息，除非他们得到一大笔钱。
synonyms: blackmailers, racketeers, con artists
antonyms: philanthropists, donors, benefactors
n.

extorter:/ɪkˈstɔːrtər/
noun
勒索者；敲诈者
A person who extorts money or other things.
The business owner was harassed by extortioners who demanded protection money.  这位企业主受到勒索者的骚扰，他们要求保护费。
synonyms: blackmailer, racketeer
antonyms: donor, benefactor
n.
