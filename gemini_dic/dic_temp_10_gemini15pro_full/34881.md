clip-ons:/ˈklɪp ɑːnz/ (US) /ˈklɪp ɒnz/ (UK)
n.
夹式; 夹扣式配件; 夹式耳环; 夹式太阳镜
Accessories that are attached with clips; specifically clip-on earrings or sunglasses.
She prefers clip-on earrings because her ears aren't pierced. 她更喜欢夹式耳环，因为她的耳朵没有穿孔。
My grandmother uses clip-on sunglasses over her regular glasses. 我的祖母在她的普通眼镜上戴夹式太阳镜。
近义词: attachments, accessories
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "pierced" for earrings)
词性: noun


clip-on:/ˈklɪp ɑːn/ (US) /ˈklɪp ɒn/ (UK)
adj.
夹式的; 可夹住的
Describes something that can be attached with a clip.
He bought a clip-on tie because he didn't know how to tie a regular one. 他买了一条夹式领带，因为他不知道如何系普通的领带。
The lamp has a clip-on attachment so you can secure it to your desk. 这盏灯有一个夹式附件，可以把它固定在你的桌子上。
近义词: attachable, clipable
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: adjective
