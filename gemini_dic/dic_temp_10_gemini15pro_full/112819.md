lucid:/ˈluːsɪd/| adj.|清晰的，明了的；清醒的；神志清楚的|Characterized by clarity and ease of understanding; rationally coherent, especially when experiencing a mental disorder; bright or luminous.|She gave a lucid explanation of the complex theory. 她对这个复杂的理论作出了清晰的解释。|The patient became lucid after the surgery. 病人在手术后恢复了清醒。|近义词：clear, transparent, coherent, rational, sane|反义词：obscure, unclear, confused, irrational, insane|词性:形容词
词源:From Latin *lūcidus, from lūx (“light”).
