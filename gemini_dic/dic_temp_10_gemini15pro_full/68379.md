fallon:/ˈfɔːlən/ (US) /ˈfɔːlɒn/ (UK)| adj.|堕落的，倒下的，死去的|Fallen can mean morally degraded, physically down, or dead. It's the past participle of the verb "to fall".|The fallen angel was cast out of heaven. 堕落的天使被逐出天堂。

The tree had fallen across the road.  树倒在路上。

Many soldiers have fallen in battle. 许多士兵在战斗中阵亡。|近义词：downed, prostrated, deceased, degraded|反义词：upright, standing, living, virtuous|词性:形容词
