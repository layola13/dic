defectious:/dɪˈfɛkʃəs/
adjective
有缺陷的；不完全的；背叛的；叛逃的
Failing in or neglecting a duty or obligation; disloyal; deserting a cause or party.
The defectious soldier abandoned his post.  这个叛逃的士兵放弃了他的岗位。
The bridge was defectious and needed repairs. 这座桥有缺陷，需要修理。
Synonyms: disloyal, faithless, treacherous, traitorous, rebellious, mutinous, deserter, renegade
Antonyms: loyal, faithful, steadfast, true, devoted, patriotic
词性: 形容词
