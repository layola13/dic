noncoinage:/ˌnɑːnˈkɔɪnɪdʒ/
n.
非创造词；未经创造的词语
A word or phrase that is not newly coined; an existing word.
The term "internet" was a noncoinage, having been used previously in a different context.  “互联网”一词并非新造词，它以前在不同的语境中使用过。
Synonyms: established word, existing term
Antonyms: neologism, coinage
noun
