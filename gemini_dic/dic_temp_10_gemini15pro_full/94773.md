imprudentness:/ɪmˈpruːdntnəs/| n. |轻率，鲁莽|The state of being imprudent; lack of caution or good judgment; rashness.|His imprudentness cost him dearly. 他的轻率让他付出了高昂的代价。|Synonyms: recklessness, foolishness, indiscretion, rashness, carelessness|Antonyms: prudence, caution, wisdom, foresight, discretion|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From imprudent + -ness.  Imprudent comes from the Latin *imprūdent-, from in- "not" + prūdent- "prudent," from prōvidēns "foreseeing, provident".
