nvm:/ˌɛn viː ˈɛm/ | abbreviation | 没关系；不要紧 | Short for "never mind". Used online and in text messages to indicate that something previously said or asked is no longer relevant or important. | Example: "I thought I lost my keys, but nvm, I found them." 我以为我钥匙丢了，不过没关系，我找到了。| Synonym:  it's okay, don't worry about it, forget it | Antonym:  it's important, pay attention | abbreviation

PHRASE->

N/A - nvm is generally used on its own and doesn't form many common phrases.  However, it can be combined with other internet slang. For example:

1. "lol nvm"  (laughing out loud, never mind) =  哈哈，没关系
2.  "omg nvm" (oh my god, never mind) =  我的天，没关系
3. "jk nvm" (just kidding, never mind) = 开玩笑的，没关系
4.  "nvm lol"  (never mind, laughing out loud) =  没关系，哈哈
5.  "nvm idk" (never mind, I don't know) =  算了，我不知道
