clobbering:/ˈklɒbərɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈklɑːbərɪŋ/ (US)| n. |痛打，猛击，严厉批评，彻底击败|A sound defeat; a thrashing.  The act of hitting or criticizing someone severely.|例句：Our team took a real clobbering in the final. 我们的队伍在决赛中惨败。 The critics gave the new movie a real clobbering.  评论家们对这部新电影进行了严厉的批评。|近义词：thrashing, beating, drubbing, defeat, hammering, walloping|反义词:victory, triumph|词性:noun

clobber:/ˈklɒbə(r)/ (UK) /ˈklɑːbər/ (US)| v. |痛打，猛击，严厉批评，彻底击败|To hit someone or something hard; to criticize someone severely; to defeat decisively.|例句：He clobbered his opponent in the second round. 他在第二轮比赛中击败了对手。  The new tax laws will clobber small businesses. 新的税法将重创小型企业。|近义词：thrash, beat, drubbing, defeat, hammer, wallop|反义词:lose, surrender|词性:verb
