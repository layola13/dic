crabstick:/ˈkræbstɪk/| n. |蟹肉棒|A food product made from minced white fish meat that is shaped and colored to resemble crab leg meat.|I like crabstick in my California rolls. 我喜欢加州卷里的蟹肉棒。|Synonym: imitation crab, surimi|Antonym: real crab meat|noun
