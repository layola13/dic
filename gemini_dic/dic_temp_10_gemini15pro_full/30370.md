centerfold: /ˈsentərfoʊld/ (US) /ˈsɛntəfəʊld/ (UK)| n.|插页, 中央折页, (通常指杂志中带有性感照片的)折页|A large picture printed across the central pages of a magazine, often of an attractive, scantily-clad person.|The magazine's centerfold this month is a model in a bikini. 这本杂志本月的插页是一位穿着比基尼的模特。|Synonyms: gatefold, spread|Antonyms: None|Noun

centerfold: /ˈsentərfoʊld/ (US) /ˈsɛntəfəʊld/ (UK)| adj.|插页的，中央折页的|Relating to or resembling a centerfold.|The centerfold image caused quite a stir. 这张插页图片引起了不小的轰动。|Synonyms: None|Antonyms: None|Adjective
