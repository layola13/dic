gutsiest:/ˈɡʌtsiɪst/| adj.|最有胆量的，最有勇气的|Showing the most courage, determination, and spirit, even in difficult or dangerous situations.  It is the superlative form of "gutsy."|例句：That was the gutsiest performance I've ever seen. 那是我见过的最勇敢的表演。

例句：Making that speech in front of all those people was the gutsiest thing she'd ever done. 在那么多人面前演讲是她做过的最勇敢的事情。|近义词：bravest, most courageous, most daring, boldest|反义词：cowardly, timid, fearful|词性:形容词
