agonized:/ˈæɡənaɪzd/| adj.|痛苦的，极度痛苦的|Experiencing or expressing great mental or physical suffering.|She let out an agonized cry when she heard the news. 她听到这个消息后发出一声痛苦的哭喊。|Synonyms: tortured, distressed, anguished|Antonyms: comfortable, relieved, calm|adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *agonizatus, past participle of agonizare “to struggle, contend, agonize,” from Greek agonizesthai “to contend for a prize, struggle, be in anguish,” from agon “contest, struggle.”
