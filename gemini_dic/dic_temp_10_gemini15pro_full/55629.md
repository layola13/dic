ditch:/dɪtʃ/| n. vt.|沟渠，壕沟；丢弃，抛弃|A long narrow channel dug in the ground, typically used for drainage or irrigation. To get rid of something or someone that is no longer wanted.|The car skidded and ended up in a ditch. 汽车打滑，最后掉进了沟里。 He ditched his old car for a new one. 他把旧车换成了新车。|近义词：trench, canal, discard, abandon|反义词：keep, retain|noun, verb
