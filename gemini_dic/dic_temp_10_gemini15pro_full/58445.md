duckmeat:/ˈdʌkˌmiːt/| n. |浮萍，水萍|Small, free-floating aquatic plants, especially of the genera *Lemna*, *Spirodela*, *Wolffia*, and *Wolffiella*, often eaten by ducks and other waterfowl.  They are also known as duckweed. |The pond was covered in duckmeat. 池塘里长满了浮萍。|Synonyms: duckweed, water lentil|Antonyms: N/A|noun

duckmeat:/ˈdʌkˌmiːt/| n. |鸭肉|The meat of a duck used as food.|We had roast duckmeat for dinner. 我们晚饭吃了烤鸭肉。|Synonyms: duck (as food)|Antonyms: N/A|noun
