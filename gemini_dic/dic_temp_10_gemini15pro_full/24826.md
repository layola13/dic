brown:/braʊn/
adj. n.|棕色的，褐色的；棕色，褐色|A dark tertiary color, like that of coffee or chocolate.|The leaves turned brown in the fall. 秋天树叶变成了褐色。|近义词：tan, auburn, chestnut|反义词:white, black|词性:形容词，名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English brūn, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch bruin and German braun.
