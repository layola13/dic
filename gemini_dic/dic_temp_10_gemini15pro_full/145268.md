overboldnesses:/ˌoʊvərˈboʊldnəsɪz/
n.
过度大胆；鲁莽；冒失
Excessive boldness; audacity; temerity.
His overboldnesses in challenging the established authorities ultimately led to his downfall. 他对权威的过度挑战最终导致了他的垮台。
Synonyms: audacity, temerity, presumption, effrontery, impudence
Antonyms: timidity, shyness, hesitancy, diffidence, meekness
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + boldness + -es (plural suffix).  Boldness itself comes from Middle English *boldnes*, from Old English *bealdnes*, from *beald* (bold).  The prefix *over-* intensifies the meaning, indicating an excess of boldness.
