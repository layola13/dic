hydrick:/ˈhaɪdrɪk/ | n. | 行骗者; 江湖骗子 | A person who claims to have supernatural powers, especially the ability to find water using a divining rod, but is actually a fraud. |  He claimed to be able to find water anywhere, but he was exposed as a hydrick. 他声称可以在任何地方找到水，但他被揭露是一个骗子。| synonym: charlatan, fraud, imposter, quack | antonym: expert, professional | noun
