murkily:/ˈmɜːkɪli/ | adv. | 朦胧地，阴暗地 | In a dark or dim way; obscurely. | The details of the incident remain murkily shrouded in secrecy.  该事件的细节仍然笼罩在朦胧的秘密之中。| 近义词：obscurely, dimly, vaguely | 反义词：clearly, brightly, distinctly | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From murky + -ly. Murky itself comes from the Middle English *mirke*, from Old Norse *myrkr* meaning "darkness".
