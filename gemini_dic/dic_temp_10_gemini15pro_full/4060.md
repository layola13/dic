aie:/eɪ/ | interj. | 哎哟，唉 | An exclamation expressing sudden pain or distress. | Example: "Aie! I stubbed my toe!" 哎哟！我的脚趾撞到了！| Synonym: ouch, ow | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for an exclamation of pain) | Interjection

aie:/eɪ/ | n. | 哎哟，唉(疼痛的叫声) |  A cry of pain or distress.  | Example:  "I heard an 'aie' from the other room." 我听到从另一个房间传来一声“哎哟”。 | Synonym: cry, moan | Antonym: (No direct antonym for a sound of pain) | Noun
