breedable:/ˈbriːdəbl/
adjective
可繁殖的; 适于繁殖的
Suitable for breeding; able to be bred.
例句：These dogs are specially breedable for their temperament. 这些狗因其性情而特别适合繁殖。

例句：The farmer selected only the most breedable animals for his stock.  这位农民只选择最适合繁殖的动物作为他的牲畜。

近义词：reproducible, fertile, procreative
反义词：infertile, sterile
词性：adjective

词源: From "breed" (v.) + "-able" (suffix). "Breed" comes from Old English "brēdan," meaning "to nourish, cherish, keep warm, hatch, produce." The suffix "-able" forms adjectives meaning "capable of being," "worthy of being," or "tending to."
