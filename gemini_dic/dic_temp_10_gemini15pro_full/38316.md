concealment:/kənˈsiːlmənt/
n.
隐藏，隐蔽; 掩盖; 隐匿处
the action of hiding something or preventing it from being known
例句：The concealment of the evidence led to a mistrial.  证据的隐瞒导致审判无效。

例句：The spy's concealment was so effective that no one suspected his true identity.  这个间谍的隐藏如此有效，以至于没有人怀疑他的真实身份。


近义词：hiding, secrecy, cover-up, disguise
反义词：disclosure, revelation, exposure
词性：名词
