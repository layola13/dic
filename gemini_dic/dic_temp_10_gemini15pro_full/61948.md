emulsifications: /ɪˌmʌlsɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 乳化作用；乳状液 | The process of making an emulsion, or the state of being emulsified.  The mixture of two or more liquids that are normally immiscible (unmixable or unblendable).| The emulsifications of fats in the digestive system is aided by bile. 消化系统中脂肪的乳化作用是由胆汁辅助的。 |  The chef carefully monitored the emulsifications of the oil and vinegar for the dressing.  厨师仔细观察着油和醋的乳化情况，以便制作沙拉酱。 | synonyms: blending, mixing, homogenization, suspension | antonyms: separation, demulsification | noun
