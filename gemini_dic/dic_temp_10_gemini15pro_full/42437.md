counterdemand:/ˈkaʊntər dɪˌmænd/ /ˈkaʊntər dɪˌmænd/ | n.| 反要求，反要价 |A demand made in response to another demand.  It's a reciprocal demand, often used in negotiations or legal disputes.|  The union made a counterdemand for higher wages after the company proposed longer working hours. 工会对公司提出的延长工作时间的建议提出了提高工资的反要求。| synonyms: counterproposal, counteroffer, counterclaim | antonyms: offer, proposal, demand | noun

counterdemand:/ˈkaʊntər dɪˌmænd/ /ˈkaʊntər dɪˌmænd/ | vt.| 提出反要求 |To make a counterdemand.|  The defendant counterdemanded compensation for emotional distress. 被告反要求赔偿精神损失。 | synonyms: counterpropose, counteroffer | antonyms: offer, propose, demand | verb
