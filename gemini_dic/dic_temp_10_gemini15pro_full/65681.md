evener's:/ˈiːvənərz/
n.
平衡器；使均衡的人或物的所有格；
Possessive form of "evener," a tool or person that makes things even or level.  It signifies ownership or belonging.
The evener's adjustment was crucial for plowing the field properly. 平衡器的调整对于正确耕地至关重要。
Synonym: leveler's, equalizer's
Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a possessive; could consider something that creates imbalance, e.g., "tilter's")
noun
