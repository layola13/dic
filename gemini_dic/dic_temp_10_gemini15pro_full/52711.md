digression:/daɪˈɡrɛʃən/ /dɪˈɡrɛʃən/| n. |离题，跑题，岔开话题|A temporary departure from the main subject in speech or writing.|The lecturer's frequent digressions made it difficult to follow his argument. 演讲者频繁的离题使人难以理解他的论点。|
例句：
He launched into a long digression about his childhood. 他开始长篇大论地讲述他的童年。

His speech was full of humorous digressions. 他的演讲充满了幽默的题外话。

|近义词：deviation, detour, tangent|反义词:conciseness, focus|词性:名词
