nonconfrontation:/ˌnɒnkɒnfrʌnˈteɪʃn/ | n. |不对抗，不冲突|The act of avoiding confrontation; a peaceful or cooperative approach to conflict resolution.  A state of avoiding disagreement or conflict.|例句：Her policy of nonconfrontation has kept the peace in the family. 她的不对抗政策维持了家庭的和睦。

例句：The two countries pursued a policy of nonconfrontation. 这两个国家奉行不对抗政策。|近义词：pacifism, appeasement, compromise, diplomacy, negotiation|反义词：confrontation, aggression, hostility, antagonism|词性:名词
