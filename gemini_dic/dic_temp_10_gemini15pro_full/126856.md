ETYMOLOGY->
From napless + -ness.  "Napless" itself comes from "nap" (referring to the fuzzy surface of a fabric) + "-less" (a suffix indicating absence).  The word "nap" ultimately derives from Old English *hnæppian, meaning "to pluck, bite off."
