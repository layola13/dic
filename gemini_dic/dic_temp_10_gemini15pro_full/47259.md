ddp:/ˈdiː diː ˈpiː/ |abbr.| Delivered Duty Paid (完税后交货)|Delivered Duty Paid is an incoterm that means the seller takes care of all costs, including import duties and taxes, until the goods are delivered to the buyer's specified location.|The shipment was sent DDP, so we didn't have to worry about any customs clearance or import duties. 这批货物是以完税后交货的方式发送的，因此我们不必担心任何清关或进口税。|Synonym: none (as it's a specific Incoterm)|Antonym: none (as it's a specific Incoterm)|Abbreviation
