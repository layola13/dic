monomaniacally:/ˌmɒnəʊməˈnaɪəkli/ /ˌmɑːnəʊməˈnaɪəkli/| adv. |偏执地，着魔地|In a way that shows excessive or obsessive enthusiasm for or preoccupation with one thing.|He pursued his research monomaniacally, neglecting all other aspects of his life. 他偏执地进行他的研究，忽略了他生活的其他所有方面。|Synonyms: obsessively, fanatically, fixedly|Antonyms: reasonably, moderately, rationally|词性:副词
