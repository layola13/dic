indogenide:/ˈɪndəʊdʒɪnaɪd/ | n. |吲哚染料|A class of intensely colored dyes derived from indoxyl.|Indogenide dyes are known for their vibrant hues, often used in textile dyeing. 吲哚染料以其鲜艳的色彩而闻名，常用于纺织品染色。|近义词: dye, pigment |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "bleach")| noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "indoxyl" + "-genide," denoting a substance that forms a dye.  Indoxyl itself is derived from "indigo" (the natural dye) + "-yl" (a chemical suffix). Ultimately, "indigo" comes from the Greek "indikon" meaning "Indian dye," reflecting its origin.
