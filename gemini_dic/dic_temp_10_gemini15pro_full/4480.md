albicant: /ˈælbɪkənt/
adjective
变白的，发白的
Becoming white; whitish.
The fungus had an albicant mycelium. 这种真菌的菌丝体发白。
近义词: whitish, pale, white
反义词: dark, black
词性: adjective

词源: From Latin *albicāns*, present participle of *albicāre* (“to become white”), from *albus* (“white”).
