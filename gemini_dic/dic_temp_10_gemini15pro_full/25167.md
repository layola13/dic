buckwheat:/ˈbʌkwiːt/ | n. |荞麦；荞麦粉；荞麦片|A plant cultivated for its triangular seeds, which are ground into flour. A type of grain that is not related to wheat. |例句：I made pancakes with buckwheat flour. 我用荞麦粉做了煎饼。

例句：Buckwheat is a popular crop in cooler climates. 荞麦是较冷气候下的一种受欢迎的作物。|近义词：kasha |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps other grains like wheat or rice could be considered contextually opposite) |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *boecweite, from Middle Low German Bōkwēte, from bok "beech" + wēte "wheat".  So called because the seeds resemble beechnuts and are used like wheat.
