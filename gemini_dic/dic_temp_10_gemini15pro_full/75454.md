fruitlessnesses:/ˈfruːtləsnɪsɪz/
n. |无果，徒劳，无效|The fruitlessnesses of their efforts discouraged them. 他们的努力毫无成果，让他们感到沮丧。|例句：The repeated fruitlessnesses of his job search led him to despair. 他找工作屡屡失败，陷入了绝望。

The fruitlessnesses of her attempts to reconcile with her family saddened her deeply. 她试图与家人和解的努力一次次失败，这让她深感悲痛。|近义词：futilities, barrenness, sterility, inefficacy|反义词：effectiveness, productivity, success, fertility
词性: 名词


词源:
From fruitlessness + -es (plural suffix).  Fruitlessness itself comes from fruitless (meaning "unproductive, lacking fruit") + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  Fruitless comes from fruit + -less (suffix meaning "without").
