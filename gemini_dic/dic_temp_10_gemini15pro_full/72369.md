flowy:/ˈfloʊi/| adj.|飘逸的，飘动的|hanging or draping loosely and gracefully, often suggestive of flowing movement|Her flowy dress danced in the breeze. 她的飘逸的裙子在微风中舞动。|The curtains were long and flowy, creating a romantic atmosphere. 窗帘又长又飘逸，营造出浪漫的氛围。|近义词：flowing, draping, loose, billowing|反义词:tight, stiff, rigid|词性:形容词
Etymology: From flow + -y, a suffix that forms adjectives meaning "full of" or "characterized by". The word "flow" comes from Old English "flōwan," meaning "to flow, stream, abound."
