narthex: /ˈnɑːrθɛks/ | n. | 门厅，前厅 | An enclosed porch or vestibule at the west end of a church, serving as an entryway and sometimes a place for certain ceremonies. | The bride and groom greeted guests in the narthex after the ceremony. 婚礼结束后，新郎新娘在前厅接待来宾。|  The narthex was filled with the scent of incense. 前厅里充满了熏香的味道。| synonym: vestibule, lobby, porch, antechamber | antonym: nave, sanctuary | noun
