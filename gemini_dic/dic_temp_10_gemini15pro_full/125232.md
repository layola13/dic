multiculture:/ˈmʌltiˌkʌltʃər/ | n. |多元文化|The presence of, or support for the presence of, several distinct cultural or ethnic groups within a society.|  The city prides itself on its vibrant multiculture.  这座城市以其充满活力的多元文化而自豪。|近义词：cultural diversity, pluralism |反义词: monoculture |词性:名词

multicultural:/ˌmʌltiˈkʌltʃərəl/ | adj. |多元文化的|Relating to or constituting several cultural or ethnic groups within a society.|  London is a multicultural city. 伦敦是一个多元文化的城市。|近义词：diverse, pluralistic |反义词: monocultural |词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From multi- + culture.  "Multi-" is a combining form meaning "many," "multiple," from Latin multus "much, many." "Culture" comes from Latin cultura "a cultivating, agriculture," figuratively "care, culture, an honouring," from cultus, past participle of colere "to till, cultivate, cherish, worship."
