begets:/bɪˈɡets/| v.|生育，生(子女)|To procreate; to father or mother (offspring).|He begot three sons and two daughters. 他生了三个儿子和两个女儿。|近义词：fathers, sires, procreates|反义词： (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 adopts) |verb
