commits:/kəˈmɪts/| n. v.|提交，承诺，犯（罪），托付|A commit is an act of committing, especially a crime or a mistake, or an instance of this.  It can also refer to the act of sending someone to prison or a mental hospital. In the context of version control systems, it refers to saving changes.|She committed herself to finishing the project on time. 她承诺按时完成项目。|近义词：pledges, perpetrates, consigns|反义词：abandons, reneges|词性: noun, verb
