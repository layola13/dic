outrode:/aʊtˈroʊd/
verb
胜过；超过；骑马超过
to ride faster or further than someone else
例句：He easily outrode his companions. 他轻松地超过了他的同伴。

例句：She outrode the storm and reached safety. 她骑马超过了暴风雨，到达了安全的地方。
近义词：outstrip, surpass, excel, exceed
反义词：lag behind, fall behind
词性：v.
