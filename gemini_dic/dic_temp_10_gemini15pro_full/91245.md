hydrodynamical:/ˌhaɪdrəʊdaɪˈnæmɪkəl/ |adj.|流体动力学的；水力的|Relating to the motion of fluids, especially liquids, and the forces acting on solid bodies immersed in fluids.|The hydrodynamical properties of the submarine were carefully calculated. 潜水艇的流体动力学特性经过仔细计算。|The ship's hydrodynamical design minimized drag. 这艘船的流体动力学设计最大限度地减少了阻力。|近义词: fluid dynamic, hydraulic|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From hydro- (water) + -dynamic (pertaining to force) + -al (adjectival suffix). Ultimately from Greek "hydōr" (water) and "dynamis" (power).
