furniture:/ˈfɜːrnɪtʃər/ (US) /ˈfɜːnɪtʃə/ (UK)| n. |家具|The movable articles that are used to make a room or building suitable for living or working.|例句：The furniture in the living room is new. 客厅里的家具是新的。|例句：They bought new furniture for their apartment. 他们为他们的公寓买了新家具。|近义词：furnishings, fittings|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle French *fourniture*, from fournir "to furnish, supply, provide," from Old French fornir, from Frankish *fornjan* "to provide," from Proto-Germanic *frumjaną* "to perform, accomplish," from PIE *per- "to lead, pass over."
