fastidiously:/fəˈstɪdiəsli/ | adv. |一丝不苟地，挑剔地|In a meticulous and demanding manner, showing great attention to detail and often excessively concerned with cleanliness or order.|She fastidiously arranged the flowers in the vase. 她一丝不苟地把花插在花瓶里。|Synonyms: meticulously, scrupulously, painstakingly|Antonyms: carelessly, negligently, sloppily|词性:副词

fastidious:/fəˈstɪdiəs/ | adj. |一丝不苟的，挑剔的|Very attentive to and concerned about accuracy and detail.|He is fastidious about his appearance. 他对自己的外表很挑剔。|Synonyms: meticulous, scrupulous, painstakingly|Antonyms: careless, negligent, sloppy|词性:形容词
