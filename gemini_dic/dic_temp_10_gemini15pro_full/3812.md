agnostically:/æɡˈnɑːstɪkli/ | adv. | 不考虑特定理论、信仰或观点地；与特定理论、信仰或观点无关地 | In a way that does not consider or depend on a particular system of beliefs, assumptions, or doctrines. |  We approached the problem agnostically, considering all possible solutions regardless of our personal biases. 我们不带任何偏见地处理这个问题，考虑所有可能的解决方案，无论我们的个人偏好如何。| neutrally, impartially, objectively | dogmatically, biasedly | adverb
