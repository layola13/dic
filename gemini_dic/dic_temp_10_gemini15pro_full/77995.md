gentlefolk:/ˈdʒɛntlfəʊk/ (UK) /ˈdʒɛntlfolk/ (US)| n. |人们；各位；先生们；女士们| Polite term for people, especially those considered well-mannered and of good social standing.  A more formal and traditional alternative to "people" or "folks."|例句：The gentlefolk of the town gathered for the annual summer fete.  小镇上的居民们聚集在一起参加一年一度的夏季游园会。

例句：I trust the gentlefolk will enjoy the entertainment provided. 我相信各位会喜欢提供的娱乐活动。

近义词：people, folks, individuals, ladies and gentlemen|反义词:rabble, riffraff|词性:noun
