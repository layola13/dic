hyperdelicacy:/ˌhaɪpərˈdɛlɪkəsi/
n.
极佳的美味；极致的美味佳肴
An extremely rare or luxurious food item considered a delicacy.
The chef prepared a hyperdelicacy of white truffles and foie gras.  厨师准备了一道白松露和鹅肝的极致美味。
近义词: delicacy, gourmet food, luxury food
反义词: staple food, basic food
词性: noun
