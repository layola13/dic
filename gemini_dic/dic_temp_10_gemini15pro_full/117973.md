memorabile:/memɔˈrabile/ (Italian) /ˌmɛməˈrɑːbɪleɪ/ (Anglicized pronunciation)| adj.| 难忘的；值得纪念的|Something memorable is worth remembering or easily remembered, especially because of being special or unusual.|例句：It was a truly memorable occasion. 那真是一个值得纪念的时刻。|例句：Her performance was memorable for all the wrong reasons. 她的表演令人难忘，但都是因为不好的原因。|近义词：unforgettable, noteworthy, remarkable|反义词：forgettable, unremarkable, insignificant|词性:形容词

词源: From Italian *memorabile*, from Late Latin *memorabilis*, from Latin *memorāre* "to remind, mention, relate," from *memor* "mindful."
