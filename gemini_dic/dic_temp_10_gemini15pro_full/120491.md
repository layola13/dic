minty:/ˈmɪnti/| adj.|薄荷味的；含有薄荷的；清新的|Having the flavor of mint; containing mint; refreshingly cool.|The air was minty and fresh. 空气清新，带有薄荷味。|Synonyms: peppermint, mentholated|Antonyms: bland, tasteless|adjective

minty:/ˈmɪnti/| n.|薄荷糖；薄荷味的糖果 (Informal)|A mint candy or sweet. (Informal usage)|He offered me a minty. 他给了我一颗薄荷糖。|Synonyms: mint, peppermint|Antonyms: N/A|noun
