affidavit:/ˌæfɪˈdeɪvɪt/
n.
宣誓书， affidavit
a written statement confirmed by oath or affirmation, for use as evidence in court.
例句：The witness signed an affidavit confirming what she had seen.  证人签署了一份宣誓书，确认了她所看到的事情。

例句：He submitted an affidavit to the court swearing to the truth of his statement. 他向法庭提交了一份宣誓书，发誓他的陈述是真实的。

近义词：declaration, oath, testimony, affirmation
反义词: denial, refutation
词性:noun
