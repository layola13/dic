acetosity:/ˌæsɪˈtɒsɪti/
noun
酮症; 乙酰化作用
A state of ketosis, the metabolic state characterized by raised levels of ketone bodies in the body, associated with abnormal fat metabolism and diabetes mellitus.  Also refers to the process of introducing an acetyl group into a compound.
The patient exhibited acetosity due to uncontrolled diabetes.  病人由于未控制的糖尿病而出现酮症。
The experiment explored the acetosity of different proteins.  该实验探讨了不同蛋白质的乙酰化作用。
近义词:ketosis, acetonemia (for the metabolic state) , acetylation (for the process)
反义词: (No direct antonym for the metabolic state. For the process, perhaps "deacetylation")
词性:noun
