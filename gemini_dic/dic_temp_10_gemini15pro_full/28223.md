capsicum:/ˈkæpsɪkəm/ | n. |辣椒，甜椒|A tropical plant whose fruit is a pod with many seeds.  The fruit can be mild or hot, and is commonly used as a vegetable or spice. |例句：I added some chopped capsicum to the stir-fry. 我在炒菜里加了一些切碎的辣椒。

例句：Bell peppers and jalapeños are both types of capsicum.  甜椒和墨西哥辣椒都是辣椒的一种。 |近义词：pepper, chili, chilli |反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "mild vegetable" in a culinary context) |词性:noun
