matura:/məˈtʊərə/ (UK), /məˈtʃʊrə/ (US)| n. | 高考；毕业考试；(尤指瑞士、奥地利、波兰等欧洲国家的)中学毕业会考 | A final examination in a secondary school, especially in some European countries, qualifying a student for higher education. |  In Austria, the Matura is the final exam students take to graduate from high school. 在奥地利，Matura是学生高中毕业的期末考试。

Matura certificates are highly valued in Switzerland. 在瑞士，Matura证书非常有价值。

近义词: exam, examination, finals, school-leaving exam | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
