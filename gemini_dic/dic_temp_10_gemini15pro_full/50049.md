denounce:/dɪˈnaʊns/ /dəˈnaʊns/| vt.|谴责，告发，公开指责|To publicly declare something or someone to be wrong or evil.|The government denounced the terrorist attack. 政府谴责了这次恐怖袭击。|Synonyms: condemn, criticize, censure, reprehend, castigate|Antonyms: praise, commend, laud, extol|Verb

denounce:/dɪˈnaʊns/ /dəˈnaʊns/| vt.|抨击，指责|To criticize something or someone strongly and publicly.|He denounced the new policy as a complete failure. 他抨击新政策彻底失败。|Synonyms: lambaste, attack, decry|Antonyms: support, endorse, champion|Verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *denoncier (Modern French dénoncer), from Latin dēnūntiāre ("to announce, denounce"), from dē- ("down, completely") + nūntiāre ("to announce, report").
