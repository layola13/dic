ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + lucidness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "lucidness" refers to the state of being clear and easily understood.  Therefore, "nonlucidness" literally means the state of *not* being clear or easily understood, especially in relation to mental clarity.
