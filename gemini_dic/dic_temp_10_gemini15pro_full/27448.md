camouflaged:/ˈkæməˌflɑːʒd/
adjective
伪装的，被掩饰的
disguised or concealed by some means that alters or obscures the appearance
The soldiers were camouflaged in green and brown uniforms. 士兵们穿着绿棕相间的制服进行伪装。
camouflaged:/ˈkæməˌflɑːʒd/
verb (past tense)
伪装，掩饰
Past tense of camouflage. To disguise or conceal by some means that alters or obscures the appearance.
The sniper camouflaged himself with leaves and branches. 狙击手用树叶和树枝伪装自己。


近义词: disguised, concealed, hidden, masked
反义词: exposed, visible, uncovered, obvious
词性: adjective, verb (past participle)
