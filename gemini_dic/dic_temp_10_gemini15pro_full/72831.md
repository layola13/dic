foliferous:/fəˈlɪfərəs/
adjective
多叶的；叶茂盛的
bearing or producing leaves; leafy
例句：The foliferous tree provided ample shade. 这棵枝繁叶茂的树提供了充足的阴凉。

例句：The plant is known for its foliferous nature, producing a dense canopy of leaves.  这种植物以其多叶的特性而闻名，能形成茂密的树冠。

近义词：leafy, frondose
反义词：leafless, bare
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From Latin *folifer*, from *folium* (leaf) + *-fer* (bearing).  *-fer* is related to the verb *ferre* (to bear, carry).
