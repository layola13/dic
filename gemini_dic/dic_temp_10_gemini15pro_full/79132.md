gladiole: /ˈɡlædiˌoʊl/ (US), /ˈɡlædiəʊl/ (UK)| n. | 唐菖蒲，剑兰 | A plant with sword-shaped leaves and tall spikes of brightly coloured flowers.  It is a popular garden flower and is also often used in floral arrangements. |  例句：The gladioli in her garden were a riot of colour. 她花园里的唐菖蒲色彩缤纷。

例句：He brought her a bouquet of pink gladioles. 他给她带来了一束粉红色的唐菖蒲。

| 近义词：sword lily, corn flag | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *gladiolus, diminutive of gladius (“sword”), because of the shape of its leaves.
