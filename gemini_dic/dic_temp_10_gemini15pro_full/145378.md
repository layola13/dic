overcareful: /ˌoʊvərˈkɛrfəl/ | adj. | 过分小心谨慎的，过于谨慎的 | Excessively careful; too cautious. Characterized by extreme or unnecessary caution. | She was overcareful in checking the locks before leaving the house. 她离开房子前过分小心地检查了所有的锁。 | He's so overcareful with his money that he never enjoys himself. 他对自己的钱太谨慎了，以至于从来不享受生活。 | 近义词: cautious, meticulous, scrupulous, fastidious | 反义词: careless, reckless, negligent | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "careful" (adjective from "care" + "-ful")
