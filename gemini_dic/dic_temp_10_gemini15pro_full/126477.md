mythologize:/ˌmɪθəˈlɑːdʒaɪz/| v. |使神话化；将…神化；赋予神话色彩|To interpret something as legendary or mythical, often exaggerating its significance or idealizing its qualities. |They mythologized their ancestors, portraying them as heroes and gods. 他们把他们的祖先神话了，把他们描绘成英雄和神。

The media often mythologizes successful entrepreneurs, glossing over their struggles and failures. 媒体经常把成功的企业家神话化，掩盖了他们的奋斗和失败。|近义词：romanticize, idealize, glorify, exalt|反义词：demythologize, debunk, demystify|词性:动词
