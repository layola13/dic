giddier:/ˈɡɪdiər/| adj.|更头晕的，更轻浮的，更眼花的|More dizzy, lightheaded, or frivolous than before; comparative form of "giddy".|例句：The higher we climbed on the Ferris wheel, the giddier I felt. 我们在摩天轮上爬得越高，我就感觉越头晕。

例句：She felt giddier with excitement as the concert approached. 随着演唱会的临近，她兴奋得更加晕头转向了。|近义词：dizzier, lightheaded, flighty, frivolous|反义词：steady, sober, serious|词性:adjective
词源:From Middle English *gidi*, from Old English *gydig* (“possessed by a god, insane, mad”), from *god* (“god”) + *-ig*. Cognate with Dutch *gijdig* (“giddy”). More at god.
