decrepitation:/dɪˌkrɛpɪˈteɪʃən/
noun
爆裂，劈啪作响
The sudden decrepitation of the rock startled the hikers.  岩石突然爆裂声吓到了徒步旅行者。
crackling, popping, splitting
-
noun
(尤指旧物)破裂，崩塌
The decrepitation of the old building was evident in its crumbling facade. 这栋老建筑的破败在其摇摇欲坠的正面显而易见。
deterioration, decay, disintegration
-

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *decrepitātiō, from Latin decrepitāre (“to crackle, rattle”), from decrepitus (“very old, infirm, broken down”), from de- + crepitus (“crackling, rattling”).
