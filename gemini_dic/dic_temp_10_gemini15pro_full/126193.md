muzzle: /ˈmʌzəl/ (US), /ˈmʌzl/ (UK)| n. v. | 口套；枪口；（动物的）鼻口部；压制（言论等） | A covering for an animal's nose and mouth to prevent it from biting or barking; the open end of a gun barrel; the projecting nose and mouth of an animal; to prevent (someone) from expressing their opinions freely. |

例句：
The dog's muzzle prevented it from biting.
这只狗的口套防止它咬人。

The muzzle of the gun was pointed at the target.
枪口指向目标。

The cat cleaned its muzzle after eating.
猫吃完东西后清理了它的鼻口部。

The government tried to muzzle the press.
政府试图压制新闻界。

近义词：snout (for animal's nose), gag (for suppressing speech), silence, restrain|
反义词：unmuzzle, free (speech) |
词性：noun, verb |


muzzles: /ˈmʌzəlz/ (US), /ˈmʌzlz/ (UK)| n. v. | 口套（复数）；枪口（复数）；（动物的）鼻口部（复数）；压制（言论等）（第三人称单数） | Plural of muzzle |

枪口被彻底清洁干净了。

近义词：snouts (for animal's noses), gags (for suppressing speech) |
反义词：unmuzzles, frees (speech) |

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *musel (modern French museau), diminutive of muse "snout," of uncertain origin.
