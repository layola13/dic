injectable:/ɪnˈdʒɛktəbl/| n.|注射剂；可注射的药物|A substance designed to be injected into the body.|The doctor prescribed a course of injectables for the patient's pain. 医生给病人开了疗程的注射剂来止痛。|近义词：injection, shot|反义词:oral medication|Noun

injectable:/ɪnˈdʒɛktəbl/| adj.|可注射的|Suitable or designed for injection.|Injectable medications are absorbed quickly into the bloodstream. 可注射药物能迅速吸收到血液中。|近义词：injectable|反义词:oral|Adjective
