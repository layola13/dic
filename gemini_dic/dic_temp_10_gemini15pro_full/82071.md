gritting:/ˈɡrɪtɪŋ/
n.
咬牙切齿；(道路等的)撒沙防滑；(牙齿的)紧咬
The act of clenching one's teeth, often due to pain or determination. Also, the act of spreading grit, such as sand or salt, on a surface, especially a road, to improve traction.
He was gritting his teeth in pain. 他疼得咬牙切齿。
The council is gritting the roads because of the ice. 由于结冰，市政会正在道路上撒沙。
近义词：grinding, clenching, sanding
反义词：relaxing
词性：名词

gritting:/ˈɡrɪtɪŋ/
adj.
坚毅的; 勇敢的;
Showing courage and resolve.
She showed a gritting determination to finish the race. 她表现出坚毅的决心要完成比赛。
His gritting performance inspired the whole team. 他勇敢的表现鼓舞了整个团队。
近义词：determined, resolute, courageous
反义词：cowardly, fearful
词性：形容词
