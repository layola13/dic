carpet-bombed:/ˈkɑːrpɪt bɒmd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːrpɪt bɑːmd/ (US)| v. |地毯式轰炸|To bomb (an area) heavily and indiscriminately.|The city was carpet-bombed during the war.  这座城市在战争期间遭到地毯式轰炸。|The enemy's air force carpet-bombed the region, causing widespread destruction. 敌人的空军对该地区进行了地毯式轰炸，造成了大范围的破坏。| 近义词: blitz, bombard, saturate, blanket bomb | 反义词: precision bombing, targeted bombing | 词性: 动词 (past participle)
