interglobular:/ˌɪntərˈɡlɒbjʊlə(r)/ (US) /ˌɪntərˈɡlɑːbjələr/| adj.|球间质的，小球间的|Located or occurring between globules, especially between the globules of dentin in a tooth.|Interglobular dentin is a defect in the mineralization of dentin. 球间牙本质是牙本质矿化的一种缺陷。|Interglobular spaces are a histological feature of dentin. 球间隙是牙本质的组织学特征。|intergranular|N/A|adjective
Etymology: inter- + globule + -ar.  "Inter-" means "between" and "globule" refers to a small spherical body. The suffix "-ar" forms adjectives meaning "relating to" or "characterized by".
