mutate:/ˈmjuːteɪt/ (US) /mjuːˈteɪt/ (UK)| v. | 突变，变化 | to change from one form or type to another, usually in a sudden and permanent way | The virus has mutated into a more contagious form. 病毒已经变异成一种更具传染性的形式。| evolve, transform, alter | remain, stay | verb
mutate:/ˈmjuːteɪt/ (US) /mjuːˈteɪt/ (UK)| n. | 突变体 | a mutated form of something |  The scientists studied the new mutate to understand its properties. 科学家们研究了新的突变体以了解其特性。 | mutant, variation | original, prototype | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *mūtātus, past participle of mūtāre (“to change”), from Latin mūtō (“I change”).
