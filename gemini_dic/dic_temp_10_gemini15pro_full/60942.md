elevators:/ˈɛləˌveɪtərz/ (US) /ˈɛlɪˌveɪtəz/ (UK)| n.|电梯|A platform or cage moved vertically in a shaft to transport people or goods to different floors or levels of a building.|We took the elevator to the tenth floor. 我们乘电梯到了十楼。|近义词：lifts (UK), escalators (for moving staircases)|反义词:stairs, escalators (in some contexts, as they move in a different way)|noun

elevators:/ˈɛləˌveɪtərz/ (US) /ˈɛlɪˌveɪtəz/ (UK)| n.|升降机|A device or machine used for lifting something.|The grain elevator transported the wheat to the top of the silo. 谷物升降机把小麦运到筒仓的顶部。|近义词：lifts, hoists|反义词:N/A|noun
