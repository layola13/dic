over-indexed:/ˌoʊvərˈɪndekst/ | adj. | 过度索引的，占比过高的 |  Having a higher representation in a particular category or group than would be expected based on the overall population or a comparison group. This term is often used in marketing and data analysis to describe groups that are more likely to engage with a product, service, or content. | Millennials are over-indexed in online shopping compared to older generations. 千禧一代在网上购物方面比老一辈占比更高。|  overrepresented, disproportionately high | underrepresented, under-indexed | adjective
