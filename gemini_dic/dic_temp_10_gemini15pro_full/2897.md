advertising: /ˈædvərˌtaɪzɪŋ/
noun
广告; 广告活动;
The activity or profession of producing advertisements for commercial products or services.
The company is spending a lot of money on advertising.  这家公司在广告上花费了很多钱。

We are considering a new television advertising campaign. 我们正在考虑一个新的电视广告活动。

近义词: promotion, publicity, marketing
反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑  concealment 隐藏)
词性: noun


USAGE->
一般现在时:
Advertising plays a crucial role in modern business. = 广告在现代商业中扮演着至关重要的角色。
Their advertising campaign targets young adults. = 他们的广告活动针对的是年轻人。


一般过去时:
The company advertised their new product extensively last year. = 这家公司去年大力宣传他们的新产品。
They advertised the job opening in the local newspaper. = 他们在当地报纸上刊登了招聘广告。


现在完成时:
We have been advertising this product for three months now. = 我们已经为这个产品做了三个月的广告了。
They have advertised the position on several websites. = 他们在几个网站上发布了这个职位。


过去完成时:
By the time the product launched, they had already advertised it heavily. = 产品上市时，他们已经大力宣传了。
She had advertised her services online before finding a steady client. = 在找到稳定的客户之前，她已经在网上宣传过她的服务了。


将来时:
We will be advertising our new services online next month. = 下个月我们将在网上宣传我们的新服务。
The company will advertise the job vacancy internationally. = 公司将在国际上发布招聘广告。


被动语态:
The product is being advertised on television and radio. = 该产品正在电视和广播上做广告。
The vacancy was advertised in the national newspaper. = 这个空缺在全国性报纸上刊登了广告。
