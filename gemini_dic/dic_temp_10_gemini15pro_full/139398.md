observational:/ˌɒbzəˈveɪʃənl/ | adj. | 观察的；基于观察的 | Relating to or based on observation rather than theory or pure logic. |  This study is based on observational data collected over a five-year period. 这项研究基于五年期间收集的观察数据。

Observational studies can provide valuable insights into human behavior. 观察性研究可以为了解人类行为提供宝贵的见解。


| 近义词: empirical, descriptive, experiential | 反义词: theoretical, hypothetical | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From observation + -al.  Observation comes from the Latin word "observatio," meaning "watching, observance."
