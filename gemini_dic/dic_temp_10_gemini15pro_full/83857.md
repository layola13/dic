hale:/heɪl/ | adj.|健康的，强壮的|Strong and healthy.|例句：My grandfather is still hale and hearty at 90. 我的祖父90岁了仍然健康硬朗。|近义词：healthy, robust, sound, well|反义词：frail, weak, sickly, feeble|词性:形容词
