词源： From Hindi मौसम (mausam), from Arabic موسم (mawsim, “season”). Ultimately from Semitic *mws- (“to designate time”).


注意事项：
* "Mosum" is more commonly used in South Asian contexts.
* While it can be used interchangeably with "season" in these contexts, "season" is the more widely understood term.
