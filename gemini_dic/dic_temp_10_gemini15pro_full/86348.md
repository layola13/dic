hectic:/ˈhɛktɪk/| adj.| 忙碌的，紧张的，狂热的|Full of activity, or very busy and fast.|例句：I had a hectic day at work. 我今天工作非常忙。|例句：The store was hectic on Black Friday. 黑色星期五那天，商店里熙熙攘攘。|近义词：busy, frantic, chaotic, turbulent|反义词：calm, peaceful, quiet, relaxing|词性:形容词
词源: From Greek *hektikos* "habitual," from *hexis* "state of health, constitution," from *ekhein* "to have, hold, be in a (certain) state." Originally used in medical contexts to describe a fever or other feverish conditions.
