defectionist:/dɪˈfɛkʃənɪst/
n.|叛逃者，变节者|A person who abandons a country, cause, or group to which they formerly belonged.|Many defectionists from the ruling party joined the opposition. 许多执政党的叛逃者加入了反对派。|synonyms: deserter, traitor, renegade, apostate|antonyms: loyalist, patriot|noun


defectionist:/dɪˈfɛkʃənɪst/
adj.|变节的，叛逃的|Relating to or characteristic of a defectionist.|The defectionist movement gained momentum after the scandal.  丑闻之后，变节运动势头增强。|synonyms: traitorous, disloyal, renegade|antonyms: loyal, faithful|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Latin word "defectus" meaning "a desertion, revolt," past participle of "deficere"  "to fail, desert, revolt," from "de-"  "down, away from" + "facere"  "to do, make."
