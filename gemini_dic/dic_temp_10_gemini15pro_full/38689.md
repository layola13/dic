condensed:/kənˈdenst/ | adj. | 浓缩的，压缩的，简略的 |  reduced to a denser form; made shorter; summarized | The condensed milk is very sweet.  炼乳很甜。| synonyms: compressed, concentrated, abridged, shortened | antonyms: expanded, diluted, lengthened, detailed | adjective

condense:/kənˈdens/ | v. |  浓缩，压缩，简略 | to make something denser or more concentrated; to reduce something in length or volume | The author condensed his long novel into a short story. 作者把他的长篇小说压缩成一个短篇故事。| synonyms: compress, concentrate, abridge, shorten | antonyms: expand, dilute, lengthen, detail | verb
