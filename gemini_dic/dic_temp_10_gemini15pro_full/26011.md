bustee's: /bʌˈstiːz/ | n. |贫民窟（居民）|  Refers to a slum area or its inhabitants, particularly in India.  Derived from the Hindi word "basti" meaning settlement. | Example: The bustee's narrow alleys were teeming with life. 贫民窟狭窄的小巷里充满了生机。| Synonym: slum, shantytown | Antonym: affluent neighborhood | Noun
