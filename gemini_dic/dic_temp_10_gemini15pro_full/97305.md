inform:/ɪnˈfɔːrm/(UK) /ɪnˈfɔːrm/(US)| vt.|通知，告知；了解，熟悉|to tell someone about something, especially in an official way|例句：I informed him that the meeting had been cancelled. 我通知他会议取消了。|例句：Please keep me informed of any developments. 请随时通知我任何进展。|近义词：notify, tell, apprise|反义词：conceal, withhold|verb

inform:/ɪnˈfɔːrm/(UK) /ɪnˈfɔːrm/(US)| adj. (formal)|提供信息的，见闻广博的|giving a lot of useful information|例句：an informed decision 知情的决定|例句：He is well-informed about current affairs. 他对时事非常了解。|近义词：knowledgeable, enlightened|反义词：ignorant, uninformed|adjective
