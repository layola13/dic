desmoid:/ˈdɛzmɔɪd/| adj. n.|腱样的，腱膜样的；腱样瘤|Resembling a tendon; a fibrous tumor arising from connective tissue, especially in the abdominal wall, often recurring after surgical removal. |Example: Desmoid tumors are rare but can be locally aggressive.  腱样瘤很罕见，但在局部可能具有侵袭性。

Example: The desmoid appearance of the tissue concerned the surgeon. 这块组织的腱样外观使外科医生感到担忧。|近义词：fibromatous, tendinous|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective, noun


Etymology: From Greek *desmoeidēs*  "like a tendon, ligament," from *desmos*  "band, ligament" + *-oeidēs* "like, resembling."
