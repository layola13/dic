osteomalacia: /ˌɒstiːəməˈleɪʃə/ /ˌɑːstiːoʊməˈleɪʃə/| n. | 骨软化症 | Softening of the bones, typically through a deficiency of vitamin D or calcium. |  Osteomalacia can cause bone pain and muscle weakness. 骨软化症会导致骨痛和肌肉无力。| Synonym: rickets (in children), bone softening | Antonym: bone hardening | noun
