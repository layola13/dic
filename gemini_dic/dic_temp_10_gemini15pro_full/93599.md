imbase:/ɪmˈbeɪs/ | vt. | 使…道德败坏；贬低；降低…的价值 | To lower the quality or moral character of something or someone. |  He accused the media of imbasing political debate. 他指责媒体降低了政治辩论的格调。| 近义词: degrade, debase, corrupt, demean | 反义词: elevate, ennoble, uplift | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From Late Latin *imbasare, from in- "in, into" + Medieval Latin basis "low."  Ultimately related to "base" (low, of inferior quality).
