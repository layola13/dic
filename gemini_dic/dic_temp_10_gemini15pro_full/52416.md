didgeridoo:/ˌdɪdʒəriˈduː/
n.|迪吉里杜管|A wind instrument developed by Indigenous Australians of northern Australia. It is a long wooden tube, traditionally made from a eucalyptus tree hollowed out by termites, that produces a low-pitched droning sound.|He played a haunting melody on the didgeridoo. 他用迪吉里杜管演奏了一曲令人难忘的旋律。|近义词: drone pipe (虽然不完全相同，但在音色上相似) |反义词: 无 (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑一些高音调的乐器作为对比，例如flute笛子)
词性: 名词
