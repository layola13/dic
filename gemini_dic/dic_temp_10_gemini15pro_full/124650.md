mouthlike:/ˈmaʊθlaɪk/
adjective
像嘴的；嘴状的
Resembling a mouth in shape or function.
The lamprey has a mouthlike sucker.  七鳃鳗有一个像嘴一样的吸盘。
The cave had a dark, mouthlike opening.  山洞有一个黑暗的、像嘴一样的开口。
近义词:oral, stomal
反义词:None
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From "mouth" + "-like". "Mouth" comes from Old English "mūþ", from Proto-Germanic "*munþaz".  "-like" is a suffix meaning "resembling," from Old English "-līc".
