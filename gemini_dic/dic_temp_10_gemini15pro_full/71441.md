flambeed:/flɑ̃ˈbeɪd/ /flɑːmˈbeɪd/
adjective
 flambéed（英式）/ flambéed（美式）
Covered with liquor, typically brandy or rum, and ignited.
The flambéed cherries made a dramatic finish to the dessert.   flambéed 的樱桃为甜点画上了戏剧性的句点。
Near Synonyms:  flamed, ignited, ablaze
Antonyms: extinguished, doused, unlit
Word origin: French, past participle of flamber 'to flame,' from Old French flamber.
詞性: adjective
