misstamp:/ˌmɪsˈstæmp/
|n. vt.|错误盖章，盖错邮戳;盖错戳|An act of stamping incorrectly, as with a postage stamp or a date stamp.  A postage stamp that has been incorrectly affixed or cancelled.|I accidentally misstamped the envelope and had to buy a new stamp. 我不小心把信封盖错了邮戳，不得不重新买一张邮票。|misprint, mislabel|correct stamp|verb, noun
