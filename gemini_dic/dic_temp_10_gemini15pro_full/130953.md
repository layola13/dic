nonassistants:/ˌnɒnəˈsɪstənts/ | n. | 非助手；不提供帮助的人；不协助者 | People who do not assist or provide help.  Those who are not assistants in a particular role or task. |  The nonassistants were asked to leave the operating room.  非助手被要求离开手术室。

The project suffered due to the lack of commitment from the nonassistants.  由于非助手缺乏投入，该项目受到了影响。 | 近义词: bystanders, onlookers, nonparticipants | 反义词: assistants, helpers, aides | 词性: 名词

词源: non- + assistants.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "without." "Assistants" comes from the Middle English "assistant," from the Old French "assistant," present participle of "assister," from the Latin "assistere" meaning "to stand by, to help."
