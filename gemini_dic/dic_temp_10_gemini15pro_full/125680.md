mundanely:/mʌnˈdeɪnli/ | adv. | 平凡地；普通地；世俗地 | In a mundane, ordinary, or commonplace manner; without excitement or interest. | She described the events of her day mundanely, focusing on the routine rather than the exceptional. 她平淡地描述了她一天的经历，着重于日常事务而不是特殊的事情。 |  He spoke mundanely about his job, revealing little passion for his work. 他平淡地谈论他的工作，对他的工作几乎没有表现出热情。 | 近义词: ordinarily, commonly, routinely, dully, plainly | 反义词: exceptionally, extraordinarily, excitingly, remarkably, unusually | 词性: 副词

词源: From mundane + -ly.  Mundane comes from Old French mondein "of this world," from Latin mundānus "belonging to the world," from mundus "world, universe."
