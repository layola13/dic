cognatic:/kɒɡˈnætɪk/
adjective
同族的；母系的
related by blood; descended from the same ancestor.
例句：Cognatic kinship is reckoned through both the father and the mother.  同族亲属关系是通过父亲和母亲来计算的。

例句：She traced her cognatic ancestry back to the 17th century. 她追溯了她的母系血统到17世纪。
近义词： kindred, related
反义词： unrelated, agnatic
