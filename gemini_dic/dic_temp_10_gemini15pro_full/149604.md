painfully:/ˈpeɪnfəli/| adv.|痛苦地，费力地|In a way that causes pain or distress; with difficulty.|She moved painfully slowly after the surgery. 她手术后行动得非常缓慢和痛苦。|例句：He coughed painfully. 他痛苦地咳嗽着。|近义词：agonizingly, excruciatingly, distressingly|反义词：easily, effortlessly, comfortably|词性: 副词
Etymology: From "painful" + "-ly." "Painful" comes from Old French "painful," from "paine" (pain) + "-ful." Ultimately from Latin "poena" (penalty, punishment).
