pariah:/pəˈraɪə/ /ˈpɛriə/| n. |贱民，被社会遗弃者，流浪者|A person who is rejected from society or home.|例句：He was treated as a pariah by his former friends after the scandal.  丑闻曝光后，他被以前的朋友们视为贱民。

例句：She felt like a pariah after her controversial opinions became public.  在她有争议的观点公开后，她感觉自己像个被社会遗弃的人。|近义词：outcast, exile, leper, undesirable|反义词：insider, celebrity, VIP|词性:名词
