minionize:/ˈmɪnjənaɪz/ | v. | 使成为奴才；使卑躬屈膝；使失去个性 | To cause someone to become subservient or unquestioningly obedient, often by suppressing their individuality. | The totalitarian regime sought to minionize the population through propaganda and fear. 极权主义政权试图通过宣传和恐惧使民众变成奴才。

The company's strict dress code and rigid hierarchy minionized its employees. 公司严格的着装规定和僵化的等级制度使其员工唯命是从。| 近义词: subjugate, enslave, domineer | 反义词: empower, liberate, free | 词性: 动词

词源:  From "minion" (meaning a subservient follower) + "-ize" (a suffix meaning "to make or become").  源自“minion”（意为卑躬屈膝的追随者）+“-ize”（后缀，意为“使成为”）。
