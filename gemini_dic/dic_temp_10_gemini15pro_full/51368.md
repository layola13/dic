deterioration:/dɪˌtɪəriəˈreɪʃn/ | n. |恶化，退化，变坏|The deterioration of the building was evident in the crumbling facade.  这栋建筑的恶化从破碎的正面看得出来。|例句：The patient's condition showed signs of deterioration. 病人的病情出现恶化迹象。

例句：The economic deterioration led to widespread unemployment. 经济恶化导致普遍失业。|近义词：decline, decay, worsening, degradation|反义词:improvement, amelioration, betterment|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dēteriorātiōn- (stem of dēteriorātiō), from Latin dēteriorātus (past participle of dēteriorāre ‘make worse’), from dēterior ‘worse’.
