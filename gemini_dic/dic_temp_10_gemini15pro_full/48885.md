deframed:/dɪˈfreɪmd/| adj.|去框的，脱离框架的|Removed from a frame, either literally (as a picture) or figuratively (as a concept or idea).  The act of removing limitations or constraints.|The painting was deframed for cleaning. 这幅画被取下画框进行清洁。|近义词：unframed, unbound, unrestricted, liberated|反义词:framed, bound, restricted, confined|词性:形容词

deframe:/ˌdiːˈfreɪm/| vt.|去框，脱离框架|To remove from a frame, either literally (as a picture) or figuratively (as a concept or idea). The act of removing limitations or constraints.|They decided to deframe the traditional curriculum and adopt a more modern approach. 他们决定摆脱传统课程的框架，采用更现代的方法。|近义词：unframe, liberate, free|反义词:frame, confine, restrict|词性:动词
