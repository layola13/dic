mince:/mɪns/| v.|切碎；剁碎；绞碎；(尤指) 装腔作势地走|To cut food, especially meat, into very small pieces, typically by using a machine.|He minced the garlic finely. 他把大蒜切得很碎。|近义词：chop, grind, dice|反义词: N/A|动词

mincing:/ˈmɪnsɪŋ/| adj.|矫揉造作的；装腔作势的|(of a person's way of walking) affectedly dainty or delicate.|He walked with a mincing gait. 他走路的姿势矫揉造作。|近义词：affected, dainty, pretentious|反义词: natural, unaffected|形容词


词源:  From Middle English *minsen, from Old French mincier (“to cut into small pieces”), from Vulgar Latin *minutiare, from Latin minutia (“smallness”).
