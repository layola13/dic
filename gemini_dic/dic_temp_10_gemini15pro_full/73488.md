forerun:/fɔːrˈrʌn/ | n. |先驱，预兆|something that comes before and leads to something else| The invention of the telegraph was a forerunner of the internet. 电报的发明是互联网的先驱。|近义词：precursor, harbinger, herald |反义词: successor, follower |词性:名词
forerun:/fɔːrˈrʌn/ | v. |预示，预兆|to act as a sign of something that will happen later| The dark clouds foreran a storm. 乌云预示着暴风雨的到来。|近义词：presage, portend, foreshadow |反义词: succeed, follow |词性:动词

注意事项:

词源：From fore- + run.  fore- 表示“在前面”，run 表示“跑”。

"forerun" 主要用作名词和动词。 虽然它也可以用作形容词，但这种情况非常罕见。
