hang-around:/ˈhæŋ əˈraʊnd/| n.|闲逛的人；常客；追随者|Someone who spends a lot of time in a particular place or with a particular group of people, often without having anything specific to do.|He's a hang-around at the local coffee shop. 他是当地咖啡店的常客。|近义词：loafer, idler, loiterer|反义词: hard worker, busybody|词性:noun


hang around:/ˌhæŋ əˈraʊnd/| v.|闲逛；逗留；徘徊|To spend time in a place or with someone, usually without a specific purpose.|The teenagers were hanging around the mall.  这些青少年在商场闲逛。|近义词：loiter, linger, mill around|反义词: leave, depart|词性:verb

词源: From the verb "hang" + "around," indicating aimless lingering.  来源于动词"hang" + "around"，表示漫无目的地逗留。
