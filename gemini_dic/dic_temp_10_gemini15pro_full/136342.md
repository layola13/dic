nonresilience:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈzɪ.li.əns/ | n. | 非韧性，缺乏韧性 | The nonresilience of the economy led to a prolonged recession.  这个经济体缺乏韧性导致了长期的经济衰退。 |  The bridge's nonresilience to earthquakes caused it to collapse. 这座桥梁对地震缺乏抵抗力导致其倒塌。| 近义词: fragility, vulnerability, weakness | 反义词: resilience, strength, toughness | 词性: 名词

词源:  non- (prefix meaning "not") + resilience (from Latin resilire "to leap back," from re- "back" + salire "to leap")
