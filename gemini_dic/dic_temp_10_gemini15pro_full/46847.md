daran:/daˈʁan/| adv. |thereon, on it, about it, of it, to it|  Relates to something previously mentioned; often used with verbs like "denken" (to think), "arbeiten" (to work), "erinnern" (to remember). |  Ich denke oft daran. (I often think about it.)| 近义词: darüber (about it), davon (of it, from it),  dazu (to it) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adverb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Ich arbeite daran. = I am working on it.
Er denkt oft daran. = He often thinks about it.

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Ich bin gerade daran am Arbeiten. = I am currently working on it.
Sie ist daran gewöhnt. = She is used to it.


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Ich habe lange daran gearbeitet. = I have worked on it for a long time.
Wir haben uns daran erinnert. = We remembered it.


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Ich bin schon seit Stunden daran am Arbeiten. = I have been working on it for hours.
Sie ist die ganze Zeit daran gewöhnt gewesen. = She has been used to it the whole time.


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Ich arbeitete daran. = I worked on it.
Er dachte daran. = He thought about it.


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Ich war daran am Arbeiten, als du anriefst. = I was working on it when you called.
Sie war daran gewöhnt. = She was used to it.


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Ich hatte lange daran gearbeitet, bevor ich es aufgab. = I had worked on it for a long time before I gave up.
Wir hatten uns daran erinnert. = We had remembered it.


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
Ich war schon seit Stunden daran am Arbeiten gewesen, als der Strom ausfiel. = I had been working on it for hours when the power went out.
Sie war die ganze Zeit daran gewöhnt gewesen. = She had been used to it the whole time.


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Ich werde morgen daran arbeiten. = I will work on it tomorrow.
Er wird daran denken. = He will think about it.


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
Ich werde morgen um diese Zeit daran arbeiten. = I will be working on it this time tomorrow.
Sie wird daran gewöhnt sein. = She will be used to it.


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
Ich werde bis morgen Abend daran gearbeitet haben. = I will have worked on it by tomorrow evening.
Wir werden uns daran erinnert haben. = We will have remembered it.


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
Ich werde bis dahin schon seit Tagen daran gearbeitet haben. = I will have been working on it for days by then.
Sie wird die ganze Zeit daran gewöhnt gewesen sein. = She will have been used to it the whole time.


PHRASE->
denken an + Akkusativ (daran) = to think of/about something (e.g., Ich denke oft an meine Familie. I often think about my family. / Ich denke oft daran. I often think about it.)
arbeiten an + Dativ (daran) = to work on something (e.g., Ich arbeite an einem neuen Projekt. I’m working on a new project. / Ich arbeite daran. I'm working on it.)
sich erinnern an + Akkusativ (daran) = to remember something (e.g., Ich erinnere mich an den Urlaub. I remember the holiday. / Ich erinnere mich daran. I remember it.)
gewöhnt sein an + Akkusativ (daran) = to be used to something (e.g., Ich bin an das kalte Wetter gewöhnt. I’m used to the cold weather. / Ich bin daran gewöhnt. I’m used to it.)
sich freuen auf + Akkusativ/über + Akkusativ (darauf/darüber) = to look forward to something (While not "daran", it shows the similar prepositional adverb usage. e.g., Ich freue mich auf das Wochenende. I’m looking forward to the weekend. / Ich freue mich darauf. I'm looking forward to it.)


注意事项:

"daran" doesn't have a direct English equivalent and its meaning changes depending on the verb it's used with. It always refers to something previously mentioned.  It combines "da" (there) and "an" (on/at/to).
