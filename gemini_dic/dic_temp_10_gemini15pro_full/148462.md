overstrain:/ˌoʊvərˈstreɪn/| n. v. |过度紧张，过度劳累；扭伤，拉伤| to strain or exert (oneself, a muscle, etc.) to an excessive degree |例句：He overstrained his back lifting heavy boxes. 他抬重箱子拉伤了背。|近义词：overexert, overwork, strain, tax |反义词: relax, rest |词性:名词,动词

overstrain:/ˌoʊvərˈstreɪn/| n. |过度紧张，过度劳累；扭伤，拉伤 | The result of overexertion; excessive strain. |例句：The overstrain caused by working too many hours made her ill.  长时间工作带来的过度劳累使她病倒了。|近义词：overexertion, fatigue, exhaustion |反义词: relaxation, rest |词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + strain.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much". "Strain" comes from the Old French "estraindre," meaning "to bind tightly, constrain."
