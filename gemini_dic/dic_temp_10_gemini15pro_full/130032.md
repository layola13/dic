non-parametric:/ˌnɒnˌpærəˈmetrɪk/ | adj. | 非参数的 |  Not relying on or involving any assumptions as to the form or parameters of a frequency distribution.  Relating to or involving statistical methods that do not make assumptions about the underlying distribution of the data. |  We used a non-parametric test to analyze the data because it wasn't normally distributed. 我们使用非参数检验来分析数据，因为它不是正态分布的。 | Nonparametric methods are often used when the data is ordinal or nominal. 当数据是序数或名词时，通常使用非参数方法。 | distribution-free, assumption-free | parametric | adjective
