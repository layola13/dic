cantabrians:/kænˈteɪbriənz/
noun
坎塔布里亚人；坎塔布连山脉居民
People from Cantabria, a region in northern Spain, or inhabitants of the Cantabrian Mountains.
The Cantabrians are known for their strong cultural identity.  坎塔布里亚人以其浓厚的文化认同而闻名。
Ancient Cantabrians resisted Roman conquest for centuries. 古代坎塔布里亚人抵抗了几个世纪的罗马征服。
