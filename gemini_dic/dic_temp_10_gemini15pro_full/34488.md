claymore:/ˈkleɪmɔːr/| n. |阔剑；定向雷 |A large two-edged sword formerly used by Scottish Highlanders. Also, a type of anti-personnel mine.|例句：The claymore was a formidable weapon on the battlefield. 阔剑在战场上是一种可怕的武器。

例句：The soldiers carefully placed the claymores around the perimeter. 士兵们小心翼翼地将定向雷放置在周边。|近义词：(sword) broadsword, zweihander; (mine) landmine, anti-personnel mine|反义词: (mine) none (mines don't have direct antonyms)|词性:noun
