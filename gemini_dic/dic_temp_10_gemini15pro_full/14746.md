awesomeness:/ˈɔːsəmnəs/
|n.|令人敬畏的事物；雄伟；壮观|The awesomeness of the Grand Canyon is breathtaking. 大峡谷的壮丽景色令人叹为观止。|近义词：splendor, magnificence, grandeur|反义词：ordinariness, mediocrity|词性:名词

awesomenesses:/ˈɔːsəmnəsɪz/
|n.|令人敬畏的事物（复数）|She described the awesomenesses of the natural world. 她描述了自然界的种种奇观。|近义词：splendors, magnificences, grandeurs|反义词：ordinarinesses, mediocrities|词性:名词

词源:  From awesome + -ness.  "Awesome" originally meant "inspiring awe" and comes from "awe" (from Old Norse *agi) + "-some" (meaning "characterized by").  The suffix "-ness" turns an adjective into a noun, indicating a state or quality.
