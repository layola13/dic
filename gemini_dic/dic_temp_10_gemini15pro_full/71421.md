flake:/fleɪk/| n. v. |薄片，碎片；古怪的人；不靠谱的人；下雪；（非正式）放弃，爽约|A small, thin piece of something, typically one that has broken away from a larger piece.  Also, an unreliable or eccentric person.  As a verb, it means to come off in flakes or to abandon or fail to attend something.|
The paint was flaking off the wall. 油漆正从墙上剥落。

He's such a flake, he always cancels plans at the last minute. 他真是个不靠谱的人，总是最后一刻取消计划。

It's starting to flake. 开始下雪了。

He flaked on our meeting. 他放了我们鸽子。|
近义词:chip, fragment, scale, eccentric, unreliable, flake out, bail|
反义词:stick, adhere, reliable, dependable|词性:名词, 动词
