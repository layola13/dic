effulgent:/ɪˈfʌldʒənt/| adj.|光芒四射的，灿烂的|Shining brightly; radiant.|The effulgent sunset painted the sky in vibrant hues. 灿烂的日落将天空染上了鲜艳的色彩。|近义词：radiant, luminous, brilliant, dazzling|反义词：dull, dim, dark|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin effulgens, present participle of effulgere "to shine forth," from ex- "out" + fulgere "to shine."
