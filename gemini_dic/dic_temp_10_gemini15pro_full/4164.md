airbursts:/ˈɛərbɜːrsts/
n.
空中爆炸
An explosion that occurs in the air, before the object causing it reaches the ground.
The meteor created a series of airbursts as it entered the atmosphere.
流星进入大气层时产生了一系列的空中爆炸。

The airbursts were visible for miles. 这些空中爆炸在几英里外都可见。

Near synonyms: aerial explosions, atmospheric explosions
Antonyms: ground bursts, surface explosions
