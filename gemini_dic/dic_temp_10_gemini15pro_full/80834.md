gouge:/ɡaʊdʒ/| n. vt.|凿子；凿痕；欺诈；敲竹杠|A gouge is a tool or act of scooping or digging out.|He used a gouge to carve the wood. 他用凿子雕刻木头。|近义词：chisel, scoop, extort, overcharge|反义词:fill, level|noun, verb

gouge:/ɡaʊdʒ/| vt.|凿；挖；欺骗；敲竹杠|To gouge is to make a hole or groove in something with a sharp tool, or to charge someone an excessive amount of money.|The artist gouged a deep line into the clay. 艺术家在泥土上凿出一道深沟。|近义词：chisel, scoop, extort, overcharge|反义词:fill, level|verb
