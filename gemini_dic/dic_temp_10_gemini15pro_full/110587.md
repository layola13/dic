limiter:/ˈlɪmɪtər/| n.|限制器；限幅器；限流器|A device that limits something, such as the flow of current, the level of sound, or the speed of a vehicle.|The car's speed limiter prevented it from going over 70 mph. 汽车的速度限制器使其无法超过每小时70英里。|近义词: restrictor, regulator, controller|反义词: accelerator, amplifier|noun
