nonsanguine:/ˌnɒnˈsæŋɡwɪn/| adj.|不乐观的，悲观的|Not optimistic or hopeful; pessimistic.  Lacking enthusiasm or confidence.|例句：He was nonsanguine about the team's chances of winning. 他对球队获胜的机会不抱乐观态度。

例句：Investors are nonsanguine about the company's future prospects. 投资者对该公司未来的前景不乐观。|近义词：pessimistic, gloomy, downbeat, despondent|反义词：optimistic, sanguine, hopeful, cheerful|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *nonsanguineus*, from non- "not" + sanguineus "optimistic," literally "bloody," from sanguis "blood."  The original meaning related to the medieval theory of the four humors, where blood was associated with optimism and cheerfulness.
