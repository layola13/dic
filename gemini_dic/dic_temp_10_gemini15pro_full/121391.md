mishit:/ˌmɪsˈhɪt/ | n. v. | 失误，打偏；错误搭配 | A shot or stroke that does not connect properly with the intended target.  To hit (a ball, etc.) badly or inaccurately. | He shanked his tee shot, resulting in a terrible mishit. 他把开球杆打歪了，导致严重失误。| Synonyms: miscue, misstroke, blunder, flub | Antonyms: perfect shot, accurate hit | noun
mishit:/ˌmɪsˈhɪt/ | v. | 失误，打偏；错误搭配 | To hit (a ball, etc.) badly or inaccurately. |  He mishit the ball and it went into the rough. 他把球打歪了，球落入了长草区。 | Synonyms: miscue, misstroke, bungle, fluff | Antonyms: hit accurately, connect perfectly | verb
