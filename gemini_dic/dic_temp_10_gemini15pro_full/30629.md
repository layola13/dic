ceremonially:/ˌserɪˈməʊniəli/ /ˌserɪˈmoʊniəli/
adverb
仪式上地；正式地
in a ceremonial way; formally
The treaty was ceremonially signed by the presidents of both countries.  两国总统正式签署了条约。
The new mayor was ceremonially sworn into office. 新市长正式宣誓就职。
近义词: formally, ritually, officially
反义词: informally, casually
词性: adverb
词源: From ceremony + -al + -ly. Ultimately from Latin caerimonia "religious rite".
