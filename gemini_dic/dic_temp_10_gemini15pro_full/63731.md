epiblast: /ˈɛpɪblæst/ | n. | 外胚层 | The outermost layer of cells of the inner cell mass of the blastocyst. It gives rise to the embryo proper and to the amnion. | The epiblast is crucial for the development of the embryo. 外胚层对胚胎发育至关重要。|  The cells of the epiblast differentiate into the three germ layers. 外胚层细胞分化成三个胚层。 | synonym: primitive ectoderm | antonym: hypoblast | noun

epiblast: /ˈɛpɪblæst/ | n. | 上胚层 |  The outer layer of the bilaminar blastoderm in the early stages of embryonic development.  | The epiblast differentiates to form the amniotic ectoderm and the embryonic epiblast. 上胚层分化形成羊膜外胚层和胚胎上胚层。| The epiblast is a crucial structure in gastrulation.  上胚层是原肠胚形成中的关键结构。 | synonym: primary ectoderm | antonym: hypoblast | noun
