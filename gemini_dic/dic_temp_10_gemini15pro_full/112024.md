long-sighted:/ˌlɒŋˈsaɪtɪd/ (UK) /ˌlɔːŋˈsaɪtɪd/ (US)| adj.|远视的；有远见的|Able to see things clearly that are far away but not things that are close. Also, able to understand what will likely happen in the future and make good plans. |例句：She's long-sighted and needs glasses to read. 她远视，需要戴眼镜才能阅读。

例句：He made a long-sighted decision to invest in renewable energy. 他做了一个有远见的决定，投资可再生能源。|近义词：far-sighted, prescient, visionary|反义词：short-sighted, myopic|词性:adjective
