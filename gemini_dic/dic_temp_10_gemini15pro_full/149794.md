paleechinoidea:/ˌpæliːɪkaɪˈnɔɪdiə/
| n. |古海胆纲|An extinct class of echinoderms, related to sea urchins, that existed from the Ordovician to the Permian periods.|These paleechinoidea fossils are incredibly well-preserved. 这些古海胆纲化石保存得异常完好。| The museum's collection includes several rare paleechinoidea specimens.  这家博物馆的收藏包括几个稀有的古海胆纲标本。|近义词:archaic echinoid (古海胆), extinct echinoderm (已灭绝的棘皮动物)|反义词: extant echinoid (现存的海胆), modern echinoderm (现代棘皮动物)|名词
