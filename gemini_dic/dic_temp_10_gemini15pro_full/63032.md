enrapt:/ɪnˈræpt/ | adj. |着迷的，狂喜的 | Filled with delight; captivated. |  例句：The audience was enrapt by the performance. 观众被表演深深吸引。

例句：She was enrapt in the beauty of the sunset. 她沉醉于日落的美丽之中。

近义词：captivated, fascinated, enthralled, spellbound, charmed | 反义词：bored, uninterested, apathetic | 词性：形容词
