ephemeral: /ɪˈfɛmərəl/ | adj. | 短暂的，朝生暮死的 | Lasting for a very short time. |  The beauty of the cherry blossoms is ephemeral, lasting only a few weeks. 樱花的美丽是短暂的，只持续几周。|Synonyms: transient, fleeting, short-lived | Antonyms: enduring, permanent, lasting | adjective

ephemera: /ɪˈfɛmərə/ | n. |  蜉蝣，短暂的事物 | Things that exist or are used or enjoyed for only a short time. | Old concert tickets and playbills are examples of ephemera. 旧的音乐会门票和节目单是 ephemera 的例子。| Synonyms: trivia, ephemeralities | Antonyms: memorabilia, keepsakes | noun
