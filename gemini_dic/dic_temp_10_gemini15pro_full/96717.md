ineffervescent:/ˌɪnɪfərˈvɛsənt/| adj.|无泡的；沉闷的；不兴奋的|Lacking effervescence; not bubbly or lively.  Figuratively, lacking enthusiasm or spirit.|The party was surprisingly ineffervescent; nobody seemed to be having much fun.  这个派对出奇地沉闷；似乎没有人玩得很开心。|Synonyms: flat, dull, lifeless, vapid, insipid|Antonyms: bubbly, lively, sparkling, effervescent, vibrant|adjective
