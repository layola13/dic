bibliopegist:/ˌbɪbliəˈpɛdʒɪst/
n.
书籍装订家；藏书装帧者
A person who binds books, especially skillfully or elaborately.
Example: The bibliopegist carefully sewed the pages together with linen thread.  这位书籍装订家小心翼翼地用亚麻线把书页缝合在一起。

Example:  The wealthy collector hired a renowned bibliopegist to create a custom binding for his first edition.  这位富有的收藏家聘请了一位著名的书籍装订家为他的一版书制作定制装订。
近义词: bookbinder
反义词: (No direct antonym)
