gharry:/ˈɡæri/| n.|轻马车，出租马车|A horse-drawn carriage for hire, especially in India and other parts of Asia.|例句：We took a gharry to the market. 我们乘出租马车去了市场。

例句：The gharry driver expertly navigated the crowded streets. 马车夫熟练地在拥挤的街道上行驶。|近义词：carriage, cab, taxi|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

词源: From Hindi/Urdu  गारी/گاری (gāṛī), meaning 'cart' or 'carriage', ultimately from Sanskrit गर्त (gárta), meaning 'pit', 'hollow', or 'cart'.
