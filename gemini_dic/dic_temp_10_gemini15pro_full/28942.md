carroting:/ˈkærətɪŋ/ | n. |胡萝卜疗法（一种未经证实的治疗方法）; 欺骗; |A fraudulent or deceptive practice, often involving a promise of reward that is never fulfilled; specifically, a historical practice of enticing someone with a carrot on a stick, only to keep it just out of reach.  It can also refer to the (unproven) practice of using carrots to treat medical conditions. |例句：They promised him a promotion, but it was just a carroting tactic to keep him working hard. 他们承诺给他升职，但这只是一种让他继续努力工作的欺骗策略。

例句：He tried carroting his horse to jump the fence, but it didn't work. 他试图用胡萝卜引诱他的马跳过栅栏，但没有成功。|近义词：deception, trickery, swindle, baiting |反义词: honesty, truthfulness |词性: noun
