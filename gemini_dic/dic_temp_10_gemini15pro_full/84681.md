handed-off: /ˈhændɪd ɒf/ | v. | 交付，移交 | to pass responsibility for something to someone else |  The project was handed off to a new team.  这个项目被移交给了新团队。| synonyms: transferred, delegated, passed on | antonyms: retained, kept, assumed | verb

handed-off: /ˈhændɪd ɒf/ | adj. |  已移交的 | describing something that has been transferred to another person or entity |  The handed-off project is now under new management.  已移交的项目现在由新的管理层负责。| synonyms: transferred, delegated, relinquished | antonyms: retained, kept, held | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "hand off," a phrasal verb meaning to transfer or pass something to someone else. "Hand" comes from Old English *handus*. "Off" comes from Old English *of*.
