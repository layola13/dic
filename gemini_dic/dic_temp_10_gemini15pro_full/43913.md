crevassing:/krəˈvæsɪŋ/
n.
裂隙形成；冰隙形成
The formation of crevasses, especially in glaciers.
Deep crevassing made the glacier extremely dangerous. 深深的裂隙使得冰川极其危险。
fissuring, cracking, splitting
healing, closing
noun
