agoraphobics:/ˌæɡərəˈfəʊbɪks/ | n. | 广场恐惧症患者 | People who suffer from agoraphobia, a fear of open or crowded spaces. |  Agoraphobics often find it difficult to leave their homes. 广场恐惧症患者经常发现很难离开自己的家。| 近义词: sufferers of agoraphobia | 反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider)  people comfortable in crowds | 词性: noun


agoraphobic:/ˌæɡərəˈfəʊbɪk/ | adj. | 患广场恐惧症的；广场恐惧症的 | Relating to or suffering from agoraphobia. | She became agoraphobic after a traumatic experience in a crowded market.  在拥挤的市场经历了一次创伤后，她患上了广场恐惧症。| 近义词: claustrophobic (related but distinct) | 反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider)  comfortable in open spaces | 词性: adjective
