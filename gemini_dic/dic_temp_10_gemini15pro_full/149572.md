pah:/pɑː/ | int. |呸; 表示厌恶或轻蔑| An exclamation expressing disgust or contempt. | Pah! That's disgusting! 呸！真恶心！|Synonym: ugh, yuck | Antonym: (No direct antonym for an interjection of disgust) | Interjection

pah:/pɑː/ | n. |微不足道的事物; 无价值的东西 (Rare usage) | Something insignificant or worthless. |  He dismissed their concerns as mere pah. 他认为他们的担忧微不足道。 | Synonym: trifle, nothing, nonsense | Antonym: something, important | Noun
