conjury:/ˈkʌndʒəri/ (US), /ˈkʌndʒəri/ (UK)| n.|魔法，戏法，魔术|The magician amazed the audience with his feats of conjury.  魔术师以其精湛的魔术使观众惊叹。|He seemed to produce the rabbit by conjury. 他像变戏法一样变出了一只兔子。|近义词：magic, sorcery, witchcraft, wizardry|反义词: reality|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *conjure "to invoke, conjure," from Latin conjurare "to swear together, conspire, conjure," from com- "together" + jurare "to swear."
