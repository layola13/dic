misdiagnose: /ˌmɪsdaɪəɡˈnoʊz/ (US) /ˌmɪsdaɪəɡˈnəʊz/ (UK)
| vt. | 误诊 | To diagnose incorrectly; to give a wrong diagnosis. | The doctor misdiagnosed her condition as the flu. 医生误诊她的病情为流感。 |  Doctors sometimes misdiagnose illnesses, which can lead to improper treatment. 医生有时会误诊疾病，这可能导致治疗不当。 | 近义词：misidentify, mistake | 反义词：diagnose correctly | 词性: 动词

misdiagnosis: /ˌmɪsdaɪəɡˈnoʊsɪs/ (US) /ˌmɪsdaɪəɡˈnəʊsɪs/ (UK)
| n. | 误诊 | An incorrect diagnosis. | The misdiagnosis led to months of unnecessary treatment. 误诊导致数月的无效治疗。 | A delayed or incorrect misdiagnosis can have serious consequences. 延误或不正确的误诊可能会造成严重后果。 | 近义词：mistake, error | 反义词：correct diagnosis | 词性: 名词
