deuterogenic:/ˌdjuːtərəˈdʒɛnɪk/ | adj. | 后生的，次生的 | Formed or derived from a pre-existing formation; secondary.  Relating to or denoting rocks, minerals, or landforms that have been altered by processes such as weathering, erosion, or metamorphism. | Example: The deuterogenic minerals were formed long after the original rock. 这些后生矿物是在原生岩石形成很久之后形成的。


Example:  Deuterogenic alteration of the basalt resulted in the formation of clay minerals. 玄武岩的后生蚀变导致了粘土矿物的形成。 | Near synonyms: secondary, epigenetic, supergene | Antonym: primary, hypogene | Word type: Adjective
