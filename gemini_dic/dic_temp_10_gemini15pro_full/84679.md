handed:/ˈhændɪd/| adj.|惯用的；用…手的；handed down相传的；handed in提交的；handed over交出的；|skilled with a particular hand; given, delivered, or transmitted.|例句：She's a left-handed tennis player. 她是一位左手惯用网球选手。

例句：The tradition was handed down through generations. 这个传统代代相传。|近义词：dexterous, adept, skilled|反义词:clumsy, awkward|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *handod, past participle of *handian "to put into someone's hand," from hand + -ed.
