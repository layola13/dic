logomachy:/loʊˈɡɒməki/| n. |争论，舌战；(尤指)关于文字的争论 |a dispute over or about words, especially one that is prolonged or tedious | The two scholars engaged in a fierce logomachy over the correct interpretation of the ancient text.  两位学者就古代文本的正确解释进行了激烈的争论。|  Synonyms: argument, dispute, debate, altercation, wrangle, squabble | Antonyms: agreement, harmony, concord, accord | noun
