inquire:/ɪnˈkwaɪər/ (US), /ɪnˈkwaɪə(r)/ (UK)| v. | 询问，打听 |To ask for information about someone or something.| I inquired about the price of the car. 我询问了汽车的价格。|
inquire:/ɪnˈkwaɪər/ (US), /ɪnˈkwaɪə(r)/ (UK)| v. | 调查，查究 |To investigate or look into something.| The police are inquiring into the cause of the accident. 警方正在调查事故原因。|
inquire:/ɪnˈkwaɪər/ (US), /ɪnˈkwaɪə(r)/ (UK)| v. |  |  |  We inquired after his health. 我们问候了他的健康。|

近义词: ask, question, investigate, query
反义词: answer, reply, respond
词性: verb (动词)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French enquerre, from Latin inquirere "to seek into, search for," from in- "into, in" + quaerere "to seek, ask."
