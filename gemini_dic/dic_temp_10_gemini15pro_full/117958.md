memento:/məˈmentoʊ/ /mɛˈmɛntoʊ/
n.|纪念品；引起回忆的东西|Something that serves as a reminder or souvenir of a person, place, or event. |She kept the ticket as a memento of her trip to Europe. 她把票留作她欧洲之行的纪念。|Synonyms: souvenir, keepsake, token, relic|Antonyms: None|Noun
