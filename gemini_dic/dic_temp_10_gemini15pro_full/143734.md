outlove:/ˌaʊtˈlʌv/
verb
胜过…的爱; 比…更爱
To love someone or something more or longer than someone or something else.
My grandmother outloved everyone. 我的祖母比任何人都爱得更深沉。
She outloved all her rivals. 她比她所有的竞争对手都更爱他。
近义词:surpass in love, love more
反义词:be outloved by
词性:v.
