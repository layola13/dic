frame-ups:/ˈfreɪmˌʌps/| n.|陷害，诬陷|The act of framing someone; a false accusation or setup designed to make someone appear guilty of a crime they did not commit.|He claimed the charges were a frame-up. 他声称这些指控是诬陷。|近义词：setup, conspiracy, fabrication|反义词:exoneration, vindication|noun
frame-up:/ˈfreɪmˌʌp/| n.|构架，框架|The basic structure or framework of something.|The frame-up of the new building is complete. 新建筑的框架已经完成。|近义词：framework, structure, skeleton|反义词:dismantling, demolition|noun
