noncounty:/ˌnɑːnˈkaʊnti/
adjective
非县的；不属于县的
Not belonging to or administered by a county.
The noncounty areas are governed by the state directly.  非县地区由州直接管辖。
The report focused on the specific needs of noncounty residents.  这份报告关注的是非县居民的具体需求。
近义词: unincorporated, extra-county
反义词: county
词性: adjective
