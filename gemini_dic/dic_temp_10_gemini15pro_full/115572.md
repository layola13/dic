词源: From Aramaic *māran (“our lord”), from mār (“lord”) +‎ -an (first person plural possessive suffix). Cognate with Hebrew מָרַן (marán, “our lord”).


注意事项:  Maran is primarily used in religious contexts by Christians.  While it shares the meaning of "Lord" with other terms, it carries a specific connotation of reverence and devotion to Jesus Christ.
