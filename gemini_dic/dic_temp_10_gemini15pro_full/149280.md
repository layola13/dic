ozones:/ˈoʊzoʊnz/| n. |臭氧|A pale blue gas (O3) that is an allotrope of oxygen, formed in the upper atmosphere by the action of ultraviolet radiation on oxygen and also produced in the lower atmosphere by electrical discharges.|The ozones layer protects us from harmful UV radiation.|近义词:trioxygen|反义词:|名词
