clear-outs:/ˈklɪərˌaʊts/
noun
清除；清理；清仓甩卖
The act of removing unwanted items, often in large quantities.  This can refer to physical objects, or abstract concepts like clearing out old files or bad habits.
The store had a big clear-out of summer clothes.  这家商店夏季服装大减价。
The CEO initiated a clear-out of underperforming employees. 首席执行官启动了对表现不佳员工的清除计划。
clearance, purge, removal
accumulation, hoarding
n.
