catchiest:/ˈkætʃiɪst/| adj.|最动听的；最引人注意的；最容易记住的|Superlative form of catchy: most catchy.  Having a quality that attracts attention or sticks in one's mind easily.|The song has the catchiest tune I've heard all year. 这首歌是我今年听过的最动听的歌曲。|Synonyms: memorable, infectious, appealing, unforgettable, haunting|Antonyms: forgettable, bland, uninteresting|adjective
