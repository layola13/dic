outtraded:/ˌaʊtˈtreɪdɪd/
verb
在交易中胜过
surpassed in trading; performed better in trading than another entity.
Example:  The small company outtraded its larger competitors in the emerging market. 这家小公司在新兴市场上的交易胜过了规模更大的竞争对手。

Example: Despite their size, they were outtraded by a more agile startup. 尽管他们的规模很大，但在交易方面却被一家更灵活的初创公司超越了。
synonym: outperformed, bested, surpassed
antonym: underperformed
