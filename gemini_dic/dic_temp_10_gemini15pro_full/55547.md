district:/ˈdɪstrɪkt/ | n. |区域，地区，行政区|A district is a particular area of a town, city, country, or other area, especially one that has official boundaries.|例句：The school is in the wealthiest district of the city. 这所学校位于该市最富裕的地区。

例句：They divided the city into four districts. 他们把这个城市划分成四个区。

近义词：region, area, zone, territory, sector|反义词：N/A|词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "districte"  (modern French "district"), from Latin "districtus" "territory, jurisdiction," past participle of "distringere" "draw apart, distract, divide," from "dis-" "apart, aside" + "stringere" "to draw tight, bind."
