hyperrationally:/ˌhaɪpərˈræʃənəli/ | adv. | 过于理性地，极端理性地 |  In an excessively rational manner; with extreme reliance on logic and reason, often to the exclusion of emotion or intuition. | He approached the problem hyperrationally, ignoring the emotional impact on those involved. 他过于理性地处理这个问题，忽略了对相关人员的情感影响。 |  He hyperrationally analyzed the situation, neglecting the human element. 他极端理性地分析了情况，忽略了人为因素。 | 近义词: excessively rationally, overly logically | 反义词: irrationally, emotionally | 词性: 副词
