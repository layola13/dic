antipiracies:/ˌæntiˈpaɪrəsiːz/
n.
反盗版措施，打击盗版行为
Actions taken to prevent or stop piracy, especially the unauthorized copying and distribution of copyrighted material.
The government has stepped up its antipiracies efforts. 政府加强了反盗版力度。
近义词：anti-piracy measures, copyright protection
反义词：piracy, copyright infringement
词性：名词(plural)

Etymology:
From anti- + piracy.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to," derived from Greek. "Piracy" comes from the Late Latin "piratia," from Greek "peirateia," meaning "piracy, robbery," from "peiratēs" ("pirate").
