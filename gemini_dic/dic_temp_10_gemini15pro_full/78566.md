ghq:/ˌdʒiːeɪtʃˈkjuː/
n.|总司令部；总指挥部|General Headquarters, the location from which a military commander exercises command.  It is often used to refer to the highest level of military command.|The GHQ issued orders for a full-scale invasion. 总司令部发布了全面入侵的命令。|headquarters, command center|branch office|noun
