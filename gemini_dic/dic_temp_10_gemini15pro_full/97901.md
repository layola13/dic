innervation:/ˌɪnərˈveɪʃn/
n.
神经支配；神经分布
The process by which nerves stimulate or activate muscles, glands, or other tissues.
The innervation of the heart allows it to beat regularly. 心脏的神经支配使它能够规律地跳动。

The loss of innervation to the muscles can cause paralysis. 肌肉失去神经支配会导致瘫痪。

近义词: nerve supply, stimulation
反义词: denervation

词源:  Late Middle English: from late Latin innervatio(n-), from innervat- ‘furnished with nerves’, from the verb innervare, from in- ‘into’ + nervus ‘nerve’.
