negation:/nɪˈɡeɪʃən/| n.|否定，否认，拒绝|The negation of his statement was unexpected. 他的声明被否定，出乎意料。|例句：His constant negations are starting to wear me down. 他不断的否定让我感到疲惫不堪。

例句：The evidence presented is a clear negation of his claims. 提出的证据清楚地否定了他的说法。

|近义词：denial, refutation, contradiction|反义词：affirmation, confirmation, assertion|词性:名词

negations:/nɪˈɡeɪʃənz/| n.|否定，否认，拒绝（复数）|Multiple negations can make a sentence difficult to understand. 多重否定会使句子难以理解。|例句：Her negations of any wrongdoing were unconvincing. 她否认有任何不当行为，但并不令人信服。

例句：The witness's repeated negations cast doubt on his testimony. 证人反复的否认使他的证词受到质疑。

|近义词：denials, refutations, contradictions|反义词：affirmations, confirmations, assertions|词性:名词


词源:  From Late Latin *negatio, from Latin negare "to deny," from nec "not" + aiō "I say."
