hyperemotionalities:/ˌhaɪpərɪˌmoʊʃəˈnælɪtiz/| n. |过度情绪化；过度情感化|A tendency to experience and express emotions with excessive intensity and frequency.  It can manifest as exaggerated displays of joy, sadness, anger, or fear.|Her hyperemotionalities made it difficult for her to maintain stable relationships. 她的过度情绪化使她难以维持稳定的关系。|Near Synonyms: hypersensitivity, emotional lability, excessive emotionality|Antonyms: emotional stability, composure, stoicism|noun
