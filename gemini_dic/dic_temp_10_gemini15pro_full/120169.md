mindblown:/ˈmaɪndbləʊn/| adj.| 震惊的，惊呆的，被震撼的 | Extremely impressed or astonished, as if one's mind has been blown away by something. | Example: I was mindblown by the special effects in the movie. 我被电影里的特效惊呆了。

Example: The news of his sudden resignation left everyone mindblown. 他突然辞职的消息让所有人都惊呆了。| 近义词: astonished, amazed, awestruck, stunned, shocked, flabbergasted | 反义词: unimpressed, indifferent, apathetic | 词性: adjective
