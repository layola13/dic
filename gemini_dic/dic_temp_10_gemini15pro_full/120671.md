misadapts:/ˌmɪsəˈdæpts/| n.| 适应不良，不适应|The act or process of misadapting.| The misadapts of the introduced species led to its decline.  外来物种的适应不良导致其数量下降。|Synonyms: maladaptation, poor adjustment|Antonyms: adaptation, adjustment|Noun
