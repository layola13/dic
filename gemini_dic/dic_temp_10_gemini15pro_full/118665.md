meta-language:/ˈmetəˌlæŋɡwɪdʒ/| n.|元语言|A language used to describe or analyze another language (the object language).  A metalanguage may be identical to the object language, but is often a distinct language or a modified subset of the object language.|Linguists use a metalanguage to describe the grammar of a language. 语言学家使用元语言来描述语言的语法。|Synonyms: metalanguage (no real synonyms as it's a specific term)|Antonyms: object language|noun
