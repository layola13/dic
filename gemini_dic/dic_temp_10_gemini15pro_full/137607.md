nontronite:/ˈnɒntrənˌaɪt/ | n. | 绿脱石 | A clay mineral, a hydrated iron-rich silicate of the smectite group. It is typically greenish-yellow in color. | Nontronite is often found in weathered basaltic rocks. 绿脱石常发现于风化的玄武岩中。|  Nontronite clays exhibit unique swelling properties. 绿脱石粘土表现出独特的膨胀特性。 | synonyms:  smectite, montmorillonite | antonyms: N/A | noun
