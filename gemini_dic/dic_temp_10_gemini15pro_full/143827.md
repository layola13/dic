outpatient: /ˈaʊtˌpeɪʃənt/
n.
门诊病人
A person who receives medical treatment at a hospital or clinic without being admitted as an inpatient.
Example: The outpatient clinic was busy with people seeking treatment for minor illnesses. 门诊诊所里挤满了寻求轻微疾病治疗的人。

Example:  She was seen as an outpatient for her sprained ankle. 她因扭伤脚踝而被当作门诊病人看待。
Synonyms:  clinic patient, day patient
Antonyms: inpatient, resident patient
词性: noun
