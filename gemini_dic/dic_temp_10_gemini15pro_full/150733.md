parachuter:/ˈpærəʃuːtər/ (US), /ˈpærəʃuːtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|跳伞者；伞兵|A person who jumps from an aircraft using a parachute.|The parachuter landed safely in the field. 跳伞者安全地降落在田野里。|Synonyms: skydiver, paratrooper|Antonyms: N/A|noun

parachute:/ˈpærəʃuːt/ (US), /ˈpærəʃuːt/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|降落伞；跳伞；空投|A cloth canopy which fills with air and allows a person or heavy object attached to it to descend slowly from an aircraft; to descend by parachute; to drop (supplies, troops, etc.) by parachute.|He parachuted out of the burning plane. 他跳伞逃离了燃烧的飞机.  They parachuted food and medical supplies into the disaster area. 他们将食品和医疗用品空投到灾区。|Synonyms: chute (informal)|Antonyms: N/A|noun, verb
