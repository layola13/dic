contested:/kənˈtestɪd/ /kənˈtestəd/| adj.|有争议的，争夺的|Disputed, argued about; challenged.|例句：The election results were contested. 选举结果受到了质疑。|例句：The ownership of the island is contested.  这个岛屿的所有权是有争议的。|近义词：disputed, challenged, debatable|反义词：uncontested, undisputed, agreed upon|词性:形容词

contested:/kənˈtestɪd/ /kənˈtestəd/| v. (past tense)|争夺，竞争，辩驳|To compete or fight for; to oppose or challenge.|例句：The two teams contested the championship. 两支队伍争夺冠军。|例句：She contested the charges against her. 她对控告她的罪名提出了抗辩。|近义词：challenge, dispute, oppose|反义词：accept, concede, surrender|词性:动词
