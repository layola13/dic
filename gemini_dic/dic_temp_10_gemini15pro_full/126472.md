例句：She has a deep interest in mythological studies. 她对神话学研究有着浓厚的兴趣。|近义词：legendary, mythical, fabulous, fabled|反义词：real, historical, factual|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *mythologicus, from Greek mythologikos, from mythologia (mythology) + -ikos (suffix forming adjectives).
