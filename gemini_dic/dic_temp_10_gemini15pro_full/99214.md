intercohesion:/ˌɪntərkəʊˈhiːʒən/ |n.|相互凝聚；内聚力|The intercohesion of the team was remarkable, leading to their project's success.|团队成员间的相互凝聚力非常显著，这使得他们的项目取得了成功。|近义词：unity, solidarity, cohesion|反义词：division, disunity, fragmentation|词性: 名词

词源： "inter-" (prefix) meaning "between" or "among" + "cohesion" (noun) meaning "the action or fact of forming a united whole".  "Cohesion" itself comes from the Latin "cohaerere" meaning "to stick together".
