downweed:/ˈdaʊnwiːd/
n.
绒毛草，棉毛草
A type of plant covered with soft, downy hairs.
例句：The downweed grew thick along the riverbank.  河岸边长满了绒毛草。

例句：The sheep grazed on the downweed in the pasture.  羊在牧场上吃着绒毛草。

近义词：cottonweed, downy plant
