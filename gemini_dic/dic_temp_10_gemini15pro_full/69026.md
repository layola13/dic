fatting:/ˈfætnɪŋ/ | adj. | 使人发胖的; 养肥的 |  Causing an increase in body fat; used for making animals fatter before slaughter. |  Example: This cake is so fattening!  这蛋糕太容易让人发胖了！| Synonyms: enriching, nourishing (for animals), unhealthy, calorie-rich | Antonyms: slimming, diet, low-calorie | Adjective

fatting:/ˈfætnɪŋ/ | n. | 养肥; 肥育 | The process of making animals fatter before slaughter. | Example: The farmer is focusing on the fattening of his pigs. 农民正专注于养肥他的猪。| Synonyms: feeding, rearing | Antonyms: slimming, dieting | Noun
