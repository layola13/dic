anti-radar:/ˌæntiˈreɪdɑːr/
adjective
反雷达的，抗雷达的
Designed to prevent detection by radar.  Designed or intended to avoid detection by radar.
The plane was coated with an anti-radar paint. 这架飞机涂上了反雷达涂料。
The stealth bomber uses anti-radar technology.  这架隐形轰炸机使用了反雷达技术。

近义词: radar-absorbent, radar-resistant, stealth
反义词: radar-detectable

词性: adjective
