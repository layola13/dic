anthocyanin: /ˈænθoʊˈsaɪənɪn/ | n. | 花青素 | A blue, violet, or red pigment found in plants. |  例句：Anthocyanins give blueberries their vibrant color. 花青素赋予蓝莓鲜艳的颜色。

例句：The anthocyanin content of grapes increases as they ripen. 葡萄成熟时，花青素含量会增加。

近义词：flavonoid | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a chemical compound) | 词性: noun
