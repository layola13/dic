hypoblastic:/ˌhaɪpəˈblæstɪk/
adj.
下胚层的，下胚叶的
Relating to or derived from the hypoblast.  Pertaining to the inner layer of the blastoderm.
The hypoblastic layer of the embryo will eventually form the endoderm.  胚胎的下胚层最终会形成内胚层。
hypoblastic layer 下胚层
inner layer 内层
endoderm 内胚层


近义词:endodermal, entodermal
反义词:epiblastic
词性:adjective
