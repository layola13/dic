neurologist: /nʊˈrɒlədʒɪst/ (British) /nʊˈrɑːlədʒɪst/ (American)| n. |神经科医生|A medical doctor who specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders of the nervous system.|例句：The neurologist diagnosed her with multiple sclerosis. 神经科医生诊断她患有多发性硬化症。|例句：My grandmother saw a neurologist after experiencing numbness in her arm. 我的祖母在手臂麻木后去看了一位神经科医生。|近义词：nerve specialist, brain doctor|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

neurologist: 词源来自 Greek  "neuron" (nerve) + "-logist" (one who studies).
