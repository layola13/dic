graal:/ɡrɑːl/ | n. | 圣杯;  渴望得到但难以得到的东西 | Something that is eagerly pursued or sought after but is difficult or impossible to attain; the object of a quest. |  The Holy Grail is a legendary chalice. 圣杯是一个传说中的圣爵。  He searched for the graal of perfect happiness. 他追寻着完美幸福的圣杯。 | 近义词: goal, ambition, aspiration, quest, ideal, dream | 反义词: reality, banality | 词性: noun

词源: From Old French *graal or *greal, probably from Medieval Latin gradalis, a platter or shallow dish, perhaps from Late Latin cratalis, from Greek kratēr, a mixing bowl.
