charismatically:/ˌkærɪzˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 魅力十足地；神采飞扬地 | In a way that shows charisma; with charm and persuasiveness. |  He spoke charismatically, captivating the entire audience. 他演讲得魅力非凡，吸引了全场观众。| 近义词: charmingly, magnetically, captivatingly | 反义词: dully, blandly, unimpressively | 词性: 副词

词源:
From charisma + -atically.  "Charisma" comes from the Greek "chárisma," meaning "favor" or "gift of grace," ultimately from "cháris," meaning "grace" or "favor."
