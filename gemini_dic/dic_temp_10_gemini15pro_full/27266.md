calque:/kælk/ | v. n. |描摹；仿造；直译词|A word or expression that is a direct translation of a word or expression in another language.|The English word "skyscraper" is a calque of the German word "Wolkenkratzer".|loanword, borrowing|neologism|verb, noun

calque:/kælk/ | n. |直译词；仿造词|A word or expression formed by closely imitating the structure of a word or expression in another language.| "Beer garden" is a calque from the German "Biergarten".|loanword, borrowing|coinage|noun
