exhausting:/ɪɡˈzɔːstɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人精疲力竭的；使人疲惫不堪的 | Making one feel very tired; very tiring. |例句：The hike up the mountain was exhausting.  爬山真是累人。|近义词：tiring, draining, wearying, fatiguing |反义词: invigorating, refreshing, energizing |词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From exhaust + -ing.  Exhaust comes from Late Latin exhaurire "to draw out completely," from ex- "out" + haurire "to draw, drain."
