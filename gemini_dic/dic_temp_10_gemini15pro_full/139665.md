occupiable:/ˈɒkjʊpaɪəbl/ /ˈɑːkjʊpaɪəbl/
adjective
可占用的；可居住的
Suitable or available for occupation.
The house is small but occupiable. 这房子很小，但是可以住人。
This apartment will be occupiable in July.  这套公寓将在七月可以入住。
synonyms: habitable, inhabitable, livable, tenantable
antonyms: uninhabitable, unlivable, unoccupied
词性: adjective
