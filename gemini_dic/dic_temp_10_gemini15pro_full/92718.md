ichigo:/iˈtʃiːɡoʊ/ | n. |草莓; 一期一会 (一期一会)| From Japanese 一期一会 (ichi-go ichi-e), literally "one time, one meeting". Refers to a strawberry, or the Japanese cultural concept of treasuring each encounter, as it will never recur in exactly the same way.|例句：I love eating fresh ichigo. 我喜欢吃新鲜的草莓。|例句：The tea ceremony embodies the spirit of ichigo ichi-e. 茶道体现了一期一会的精神。|近义词：(for strawberry) strawberry |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
