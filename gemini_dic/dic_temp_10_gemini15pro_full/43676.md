credent:/ˈkriːdənt/| adj.|可信的；易信的；轻信的|Believing readily; gullible.  Having credibility. |The credent audience accepted the speaker's claims without question.|近义词：credible, believable, trusting, gullible|反义词：incredulous, skeptical, unbelieving|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *credent- (stem of credēns), present participle of crēdere "to believe," from PIE root *kerd- "heart."
