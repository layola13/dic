narco:/ˈnɑːrkoʊ/ (US) /ˈnɑːkəʊ/ (UK)| adj. n.|麻醉的，麻醉药；毒品的，贩毒者|Relating to narcotics or drug trafficking.  A shortened form of "narcotic".|The police made a large narco bust last night. 警方昨晚进行了一次大规模的缉毒行动。|Synonyms: drug, dope, narcotic|Antonyms: sober, clean|adjective, noun

narcotic:/nɑːrˈkɑːtɪk/ (US) /nɑːˈkɒtɪk/ (UK)| n. adj.|麻醉剂，麻醉药；麻醉的，催眠的|A drug or other substance affecting mood or behaviour and sold for non-medical purposes, especially an illegal one.  Also used as an adjective to describe something that induces sleep or drowsiness.|The doctor prescribed a narcotic to relieve the patient's pain. 医生开了麻醉药来缓解病人的疼痛。|Synonyms: opiate, analgesic, soporific|Antonyms: stimulant, invigorant|noun, adjective
