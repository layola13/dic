noncorrelativeness:/ˌnɑːnkɔːrˈɛlətɪvnəs/ | n. | 非相关性，不相关性 | The state or quality of not being correlated; lack of correlation.  This means there's no statistical relationship between two or more variables. | The noncorrelativeness of the data surprised the researchers, as they had expected to find a strong link. 数据的非相关性让研究人员感到惊讶，因为他们原本预计会发现强烈的联系。 |  independence, unrelatedness | correlation, relatedness | noun
