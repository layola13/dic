highness:/ˈhaɪnəs/| n.|殿下；阁下；高贵；崇高|A title of respect used when addressing or referring to royalty or other high-ranking figures. |例句：Your Highness, may I have a word? 殿下，我可以说句话吗？

例句：His Highness, the Prince of Wales, visited the hospital. 威尔士亲王殿下访问了这家医院。

近义词：excellency, majesty, grace, loftiness |反义词：lowliness, baseness|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *highnesse*, from *high* + *-ness*. Equivalent to *high* + *-ness*. Cognate with Dutch *hoogheid* (“highness”), German *Hoheit* (“highness”).
