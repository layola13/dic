abusiveness:/əˈbjuːsɪvnəs/| n.|辱骂，虐待，恶语|The abusiveness of her language shocked everyone. 她的恶语震惊了所有人。|Example: The level of abusiveness in online comments is alarming.  网络评论中的辱骂程度令人担忧。

Example:  He couldn't tolerate the abusiveness of his boss any longer. 他再也无法忍受老板的辱骂了。|近义词：maltreatment, mistreatment, cruelty, violence, aggression|反义词:kindness, respect, gentleness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From abuse (n.) + -ive (suffix forming adjectives) + -ness (suffix forming nouns from adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin *abusus*, past participle of *abuti* "to misuse," from *ab* "away, from" + *uti* "to use."

USAGE->
There are no verb tenses or grammatical moods associated with "abusiveness" as it is a noun. However, it can be used in various sentence structures:

陈述句:
The abusiveness of his words left her in tears. =  他辱骂的话让她泪流满面。
Her constant abusiveness created a hostile work environment. = 她不断的恶语相向制造了充满敌意的工作环境。


疑问句:
Was the abusiveness of his behavior ever addressed? =  他的恶劣行为有被处理过吗？
Is there any evidence of abusiveness in their relationship? =  他们的关系中是否有任何虐待的证据？


否定句:
There was no abusiveness in their interactions. = 他们的互动中没有任何辱骂行为。
