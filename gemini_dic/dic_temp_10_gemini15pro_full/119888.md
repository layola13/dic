militarises:/ˈmɪlɪtəraɪzɪz/
verb
使军事化，军事化
to equip or organize (a place, activity, etc.) for military purposes or to adopt military practices and characteristics
例句：The government is militarising the police force. 政府正在使警察部队军事化。

例句：The escalating conflict has led to the militarisation of the border region.  不断升级的冲突导致了边境地区的军事化。

近义词：arm, fortify, equip
反义词：demilitarise
词性：v.
