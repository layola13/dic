extramurally:/ˌekstrəˈmjʊərəli/
| adv.|校外地，院外地|Outside the walls or boundaries of an institution, especially a college or university.  Refers to activities, research, or studies conducted outside of an institution's own facilities or programs.| The research was conducted extramurally in collaboration with several other institutions. 这项研究是在校外与其他几个机构合作进行的。|Synonyms: externally, outside, off-campus|Antonyms: intramurally, internally, on-campus|adverb
