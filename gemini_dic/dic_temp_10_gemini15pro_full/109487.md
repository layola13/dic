n.
麻风病, 麻风病的特征
The state or condition of being leprous; prevalence of leprosy.  A state of moral corruption or defilement.
The leprousnesses of the abandoned city were evident in its crumbling walls and deserted streets.  这座废弃城市的麻风病在其摇摇欲坠的墙壁和荒凉的街道中显而易见。

词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *leprwsnes, from Old French leprosite, from Late Latin leprositās, from Latin leprosus ("leprous").  Equivalent to leprous +‎ -ness.
