condemner:/kənˈdɛmər/| n.|谴责者，责难者|A person who condemns someone or something.  Someone who expresses strong disapproval of someone or something, often for moral reasons.|The condemner of the policy was booed by the crowd. 政策的谴责者遭到了人群的嘘声。|Synonyms: critic, detractor, censurer|Antonyms: supporter, advocate, defender|noun

condemn:/kənˈdɛm/| vt.|谴责，责备，宣告…不适用，判刑|To express complete disapproval of; censure.  To declare (something, especially a building) to be unfit for use.|The judge condemned the thief to five years in prison. 法官判处小偷五年徒刑。|Synonyms: denounce, criticize, censure|Antonyms: praise, commend, approve|verb
