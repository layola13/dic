incomer:/ˈɪnkʌmə(r)/
noun
新来者；迁入者
A person who has recently arrived in a place, especially as a resident.
The village has been transformed by the influx of incomers.  这个村庄因新来者的涌入而改变。
Newcomers, immigrant, settler
Native, resident
