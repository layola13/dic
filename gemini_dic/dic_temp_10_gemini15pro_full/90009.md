horus:/ˈhɔːrəs/| n.|荷鲁斯|Horus is one of the most significant ancient Egyptian deities. He was often depicted as a falcon or a falcon-headed man.|Horus was a powerful god in ancient Egypt. 荷鲁斯是古埃及强大的神。

Horus was worshipped as the god of the sky and kingship. 荷鲁斯被尊为天空和王权之神。|近义词:Ra, (partially) Osiris|反义词:Set|词性:noun
