lemme:/ˈlɛmi/| contraction |让我|Lemme is a contraction of "let me".  It's informal and used in spoken or casual written English.|Lemme see that. 让我看看那个。|近义词：let me|反义词: (No direct antonym for a contraction. The antonym of "let" depends on the context.  For example, "prevent" or "forbid")|contraction
