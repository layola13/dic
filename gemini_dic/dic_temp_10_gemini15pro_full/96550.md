indorsable:/ɪnˈdɔːrsəbl/ /ɪnˈdɔːrsəbəl/
adjective
可背书的；可认可的；可赞同的
Able to be indorsed; transferable by indorsement.  Specifically referring to something (like a document, especially a check or bill of exchange) that can be legally transferred to another party by signing it.  More broadly, it can refer to something worthy of support or approval.
The check is indorsable.  这张支票可以背书。
This proposal is indorsable, as it benefits all stakeholders. 这项提案值得赞同，因为它对所有利益相关者都有利。
近义词:transferable, negotiable, approvable, acceptable, supportable
反义词:non-transferable, non-negotiable, unapprovable, unacceptable, unsupportable
词性:adjective
词源:From "indorse" + "-able." "Indorse" comes from Old French "endoser" meaning "to write on the back of," from "en-" (on) + "dos" (back).
