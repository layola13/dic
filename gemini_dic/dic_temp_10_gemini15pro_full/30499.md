centumvirate:/ˌsɛntəmˈvɪrət/| n. |百人会议；百人委员会|A body of one hundred men acting as judges in ancient Rome.  A group of one hundred people.|The centumvirate was responsible for settling inheritance disputes. 百人会议负责解决继承纠纷。|The modern committee acted as a sort of centumvirate, making decisions for the entire organization.  这个现代委员会充当了一种百人委员会的角色，为整个组织做决定。|synonyms: council, tribunal, committee, board|antonyms: individual, monarchy|noun
