parakeet:/ˈpærəkiːt/| n.|鹦鹉（尤指长尾小鹦鹉）|A small, slender parrot with a long, graduated tail.|例句：The parakeet perched on his finger.  这只鹦鹉栖息在他的手指上。

例句：She bought a bright green parakeet at the pet store. 她在宠物店买了一只亮绿色的鹦鹉。|近义词：parrot, budgerigar (budgie)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
