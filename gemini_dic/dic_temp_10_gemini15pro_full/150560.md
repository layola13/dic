papalize:/ˈpeɪpəlaɪz/| v. |使像教皇一样；教皇化；使具有教皇的特性|To make or become papal; to imbue with papal characteristics or authority. |The king sought to papalize the church in his realm, giving himself authority over religious matters. 国王试图将自己王国的教会教皇化，让自己拥有宗教事务的权威。|The new archbishop's pronouncements seemed to papalize the local church. 新大主教的声明似乎使当地教会教皇化了。| 近义词: Romanize, Catholicize | 反义词: secularize |词性:动词

papalization:/ˌpeɪpəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |教皇化；像教皇一样|The act or process of making or becoming papal; the state of being papalized. |The papalization of the church led to increased tensions with the secular rulers. 教会的教皇化导致与世俗统治者的关系日益紧张。|Historians debate the extent of papalization in medieval Europe. 历史学家们对中世纪欧洲教皇化的程度进行了辩论。| 近义词: Romanization, Catholicization | 反义词: secularization |词性:名词
