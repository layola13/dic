immunizations:/ˌɪmjənɪˈzeɪʃənz/| n. |免疫接种|The act of making someone immune to a disease, typically by inoculation. |例句：Childhood immunizations protect against many serious diseases. 儿童免疫接种可以预防许多严重疾病。

例句：Ensure your immunizations are up-to-date before traveling abroad. 出国旅行前确保你的疫苗接种是最新的。|近义词：vaccinations, inoculations|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用“exposure to disease”疾病暴露表示未接种疫苗的风险) |词性: noun


Etymology: From immunize + -ation.  "Immunize" comes from Latin "immunis" meaning "exempt, free, not paying a share."
