alkalimetric:/ˌælkəlɪˈmɛtrɪk/
adjective
碱量法的，碱量滴定的
Relating to or determined by alkalimetry; used in alkalimetry.  Measuring the concentration of a base in a solution.
The alkalimetric titration was performed using a standard acid solution.  用标准酸溶液进行碱量滴定。
The lab conducted an alkalimetric analysis to determine the purity of the base.  实验室进行了碱量分析以确定碱的纯度。
Synonyms:  acidimetric (sometimes used interchangeably, but technically refers to acid measurement), titrimetric
Antonyms:  acidimetric (when contrasting acid vs. base measurement)
词性: adjective
