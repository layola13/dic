congratulations: /kənˌɡrætʃuˈleɪʃənz/ | n. | 祝贺，恭喜 | an expression of pleasure at someone's success or good fortune. | Congratulations on your graduation! 恭喜你毕业！|  Congratulations on your new job! 恭喜你找到了新工作！ | synonyms: felicitations, best wishes, compliments | antonyms: condolences, commiserations | noun

词源:

From Late Latin *congratulationem, accusative singular of *congratulation-, *congratulatio ("act of congratulating, congratulation"), from Latin congratulari ("to wish joy with"), from con- ("together") + gratulari ("to give thanks, congratulate").
