immunity:/ɪˈmjuːnəti/ | n. | 免疫力; 免除;豁免 |The state of being resistant to a particular disease, poison, or other harmful agent.  | 例句：He has developed an immunity to the disease. 他对这种疾病产生了免疫力。

例句：The vaccination provides immunity against measles.  疫苗接种提供了对麻疹的免疫力。| 近义词：resistance, protection | 反义词：susceptibility, vulnerability | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English immunite, from Old French immunité, from Latin immunitās (“freedom from service, exemption, immunity”), from immunis (“exempt, free, not paying a share”), from in- (“not”) + munus (“service, duty, gift”).
