overfrighten: /ˌoʊvərˈfraɪtn/ | vt. | 过度惊吓，吓坏 | To frighten someone too much. | The loud noise overfrightened the children. 巨大的噪音把孩子们吓坏了。|  The horror movie overfrightened her and she couldn't sleep.  这部恐怖电影把她吓坏了，她睡不着觉。| 近义词: terrify, scare, alarm | 反义词: calm, soothe, comfort | 词性: 动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "frighten" (verb meaning "to cause fear")
