annealing:/əˈniːlɪŋ/ |n.|退火，韧化|The process of heating a material to a specific temperature and then cooling it slowly, usually to remove internal stresses or improve its properties.|The annealing of the glass made it less brittle. 玻璃的退火使其不易碎。|synonyms: tempering, hardening|antonyms: quenching, chilling|noun
