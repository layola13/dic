fogey /ˈfoʊɡi/ (US) /ˈfəʊɡi/ (UK)| n.|老古董，守旧的人|A person with old-fashioned ideas who is resistant to change. |He's a bit of a fogey when it comes to technology. 他对科技有点守旧。|近义词: conservative, old-fashioned, stick-in-the-mud, fuddy-duddy |反义词: modern, progressive, innovator| noun

fogeyism /ˈfoʊɡiɪzəm/ (US) /ˈfəʊɡiɪzəm/ (UK)| n.|守旧，老古董思想|The state of being a fogey; adherence to old-fashioned ideas. |Her fogeyism prevented her from appreciating the new art exhibit. 她的守旧思想使她无法欣赏新的艺术展览。|近义词: conservatism, traditionalism |反义词: modernism, progressivism| noun
