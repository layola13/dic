elegit:/ɪˈlɛdʒɪt/ | n. |  执行令; 债权人占有债务人土地之令状 | A writ of execution allowing a creditor to take possession of a debtor's land. |  The creditor obtained an elegit against the debtor's property. 债权人获得了针对债务人财产的执行令。| Synonyms: writ, execution, seizure | Antonyms: release, discharge | noun


elegit:/ɪˈlɛdʒɪt/ | adj. (obsolete) |  被选出的 | Chosen; elected. | The elegit members of the council held a meeting.  被选出的理事会成员举行了一次会议。(古语用法，现在几乎不用) | Synonyms: elected, chosen, selected | Antonyms: unelected, unchosen | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Anglo-French *elegit, from Latin ēlēgit (“he has chosen”), third-person singular perfect active indicative of ēligō (“I choose, elect”).  So called because the creditor "chose" this particular writ.
