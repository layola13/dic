chamfer/ˈtʃæmfər/ (US), /ˈtʃɑːmfə(r)/ (UK)| n. v.|倒角，削边；|A small angled surface formed on an edge or corner, typically to remove its sharpness or to facilitate assembly.| The carpenter chamfered the edges of the table. 木匠把桌子的边缘倒了角。|Synonyms: bevel, slope, cant, angle|Antonyms: round, curve|noun, verb

chamfret/ˈtʃæmfret/ (US), /ˈtʃɑːmfərɪt/ (UK)| n. v.| 小槽；细长凹槽； (尤指柱上的)装饰凹槽|A decorative groove or furrow, especially in a column.| The architect added chamfrets to the columns of the building. 建筑师在建筑的柱子上加了装饰凹槽。|Synonyms: groove, furrow, channel, flute|Antonyms: ridge, projection|noun, verb
