defenser:/dɪˈfɛnsər/ | n. | 防御者，守卫者 | A person who defends someone or something. |  He was a staunch defenser of human rights. 他是人权的坚定捍卫者。

The castle's defenders repelled the attack. 城堡的守卫击退了攻击。


| 近义词: protector, guardian, advocate, champion | 反义词: attacker, aggressor, opponent | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *defensor*, from Latin *dēfēnsor* ("protector, defender"), from *dēfendere* ("to ward off, defend").  Ultimately from *de-* ("off, away") + *fendere* ("to strike, push").
