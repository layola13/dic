nonoppressive:/ˌnɒnəˈprɛsɪv/| adj.|非压迫性的，非压制性的|Characterized by the absence of oppression; not oppressive.  This describes something that doesn't restrict, control, or subdue others unjustly.  It allows for freedom of thought, expression, and action.|The organization fosters a nonoppressive environment where everyone feels safe to share their ideas.  这个组织营造了一个非压迫性的环境，每个人都能安全地分享他们的想法。|Synonyms: liberating, freeing, emancipatory, tolerant, permissive,  |Antonyms: oppressive, repressive, tyrannical, authoritarian, restrictive|adjective
