conformations:/ˌkɒnfɔːˈmeɪʃənz/ /ˌkɑːnfɔːrˈmeɪʃənz/| n. |构象；构型；构造|A conformation is a particular three-dimensional shape of a molecule. It arises from rotation around single bonds.  Different conformations of the same molecule are called conformers or rotamers.|The molecule can adopt a number of different conformations.  这个分子可以呈现多种不同的构象。|synonyms: shapes, structures, forms|antonyms: N/A|noun

conformation:/ˌkɒnfɔːˈmeɪʃən/ /ˌkɑːnfɔːrˈmeɪʃənz/| n. |一致；符合|Conformation is the act of conforming or the state of being conformed.|His behavior was in conformation with the rules. 他的行为符合规则。|synonyms: compliance, accordance, obedience|antonyms: defiance, disobedience|noun
