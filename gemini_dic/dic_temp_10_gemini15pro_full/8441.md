antialbumose:/ˌæntiˌælˈbjuːmoʊs/
n.
抗白蛋白胨
A substance formed during peptic digestion of proteins, not coagulable by heat, and precipitated by saturation with ammonium sulfate.
Antialbumose is a product of protein digestion. 抗白蛋白胨是蛋白质消化后的产物。
Antialbumose is an intermediate product in protein hydrolysis. 抗白蛋白胨是蛋白质水解的中间产物。
