norimon:/ˈnɔːrɪmɒn/| n. |轿子，肩舆 |A Japanese palanquin, a box-like vehicle carried on poles by two or more bearers, used historically for transporting passengers.|The daimyo traveled in a luxurious norimon.  大名乘坐豪华的轿子出行。|Synonym: palanquin, litter|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "vehicle" in a broader sense representing modern transport)|Noun
