clivity: /ˈklɪvɪti/
n.
斜坡; 倾斜; 上坡
An upward slope.
The car struggled up the steep clivity. 汽车艰难地爬上陡峭的斜坡。

近义词: incline, slope, gradient, ascent
反义词: declivity, descent

词性: noun

词源: From Latin *clīvitās, from clīvus (“slope”).
