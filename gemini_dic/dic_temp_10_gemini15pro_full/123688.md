mooli:/ˈmuːli/| n.|萝卜（尤其指白萝卜）,|A long white Asian radish.  A long, white root vegetable, similar to a daikon radish, commonly used in South Asian cuisine.|例句：I added some grated mooli to the salad. 我在沙拉里加了一些磨碎的白萝卜。

例句：Mooli is a staple ingredient in many Indian dishes.  白萝卜是许多印度菜肴中的主要食材。|近义词：daikon, white radish, winter radish|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
