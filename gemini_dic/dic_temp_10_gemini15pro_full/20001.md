biohermal: /ˌbaɪoʊˈhɜːrməl/
adjective
生物礁的; 由生物礁形成的
Relating to a bioherm, a mound-like, organically formed structure built by sedentary organisms such as corals, sponges, or algae.
The geologist studied the biohermal limestone formations in the ancient reef.  地质学家研究了古代珊瑚礁中生物礁灰岩的形成。
The biohermal structures provided a habitat for diverse marine life. 这些生物礁结构为多种海洋生物提供了栖息地。
近义词: reefal, organic
反义词: abiotic, inorganic
词性: adjective

bioherm: /ˈbaɪoʊhɜːrm/
noun
生物礁
A mound-like, organically formed structure built by sedentary organisms such as corals, sponges, or algae.
The ancient bioherm was now a fossilized record of a prehistoric ecosystem.  古老的生物礁现在是史前生态系统的化石记录。
Divers explored the vibrant coral bioherm. 潜水员探索了充满活力的珊瑚生物礁。
近义词: reef, mound
反义词: basin
词性: noun


词源:
From bio- + -herm (from Greek herma, meaning “support,” “prop,” or “reef”).
