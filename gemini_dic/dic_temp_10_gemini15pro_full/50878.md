desensitizing:/diːˈsɛnsɪtaɪzɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
脱敏;使迟钝
Reducing sensitivity to something, often by repeated exposure.
The therapist is desensitizing the patient to their fear of spiders.  治疗师正在使病人对蜘蛛的恐惧脱敏。
The constant violence in the media is desensitizing people to real-life suffering.  媒体上持续不断的暴力正在使人们对现实生活中的苦难变得麻木。
近义词:numbing, dulling, inuring
反义词:sensitizing, heightening
词性:v.

desensitize:/diːˈsɛnsɪtaɪz/
verb
脱敏;使迟钝
To make someone less sensitive to something, often by repeated exposure.
The program aims to desensitize children to violence.  该计划旨在使儿童对暴力脱敏。
Repeated exposure to trauma can desensitize a person to emotional pain. 反复遭受创伤会使人对情感痛苦麻木。
近义词:numb, dull, inure
反义词:sensitize, heighten
词性:v.

desensitization:/diːˌsɛnsɪtaɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
脱敏;迟钝
The process of making someone less sensitive to something.
Desensitization therapy can be helpful for people with phobias. 脱敏疗法对恐惧症患者有帮助。
The study explored the desensitization of medical students to the sight of blood.  这项研究探讨了医学生对血液的脱敏过程。
近义词:numbing, dulling, inuring
反义词:sensitization
词性:n.
