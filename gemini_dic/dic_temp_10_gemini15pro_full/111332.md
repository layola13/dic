live up to: /lɪv ˈʌp tuː/
Phrasal verb
不辜负；做到；符合
to fulfill expectations or reach a certain standard.  To behave in accordance with.
He failed to live up to his parents' expectations. 他辜负了父母的期望。
The hotel certainly lived up to its reputation. 这家酒店确实名副其实。
近义词: fulfill, meet, satisfy, reach
反义词: disappoint, fall short of
词性: phrasal verb
