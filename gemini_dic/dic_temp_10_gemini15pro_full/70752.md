veneer:/vəˈnɪər/| n. vt.|薄板，饰面；虚饰，掩饰|A thin decorative covering of fine wood applied to a coarser wood or other material.|The table was made of cheap wood with a mahogany veneer. 这张桌子是用廉价的木头做的，表面贴了一层桃花心木薄板。|Synonyms: facing, overlay, covering, laminate, facade|Antonyms: core, substance, reality|noun

veneer:/vəˈnɪər/| vt.|用薄板覆盖；虚饰，掩饰|To cover (something) with a veneer.|The builder veneered the plywood with oak. 建筑工人用橡木薄板覆盖胶合板。|Synonyms: face, overlay, cover, laminate|Antonyms: expose, reveal, uncover|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *furnieren*, from French fournir "to furnish, provide," from Old French fornir, from Frankish *fornjan*, from Proto-Germanic *furnijan* "to provide, furnish."
