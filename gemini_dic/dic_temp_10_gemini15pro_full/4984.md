alkaloids:/ˈælkəlɔɪdz/ | n. | 生物碱 | Any of a class of nitrogenous organic compounds of plant origin that have pronounced physiological actions on humans. |  Many alkaloids, such as morphine and strychnine, are extremely poisonous. 许多生物碱，例如吗啡和士的宁，都是剧毒的。| synonyms: nitrogenous base, organic base | antonyms: acid | noun

alkaloid:/ˈælkəlɔɪd/ | n. | 生物碱 | Any of a class of nitrogenous organic compounds of plant origin that have pronounced physiological actions on humans. |  Caffeine is a commonly consumed alkaloid. 咖啡因是一种常见的生物碱。| synonyms: nitrogenous base, organic base | antonyms: acid | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From German *`Alkaloide`*, from `alkali` + `-oid`.  Alkali refers to the basic (alkaline) properties of these compounds.
