metallurgist: /ˈmɛtəlɜːrdʒɪst/ (US), /ˈmɛtəlɜːdʒɪst/ (UK)| n.|冶金学家；冶金工程师|A metallurgist is a scientist or engineer who specializes in the science and technology of metals – extracting metals from their ores, purifying and alloying them, and creating useful objects from them.|The metallurgist analyzed the composition of the alloy. 冶金学家分析了合金的成分。|Synonyms: metal engineer, metallurgical engineer|Antonyms: N/A|noun
