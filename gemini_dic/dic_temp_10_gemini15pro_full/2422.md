adjourning:/əˈdʒɜːrnɪŋ/
v.
休会，延期
To suspend a meeting or session to a future time or indefinitely.
The judge adjourned the trial until the following week. = 法官将审判延期到下周。

The meeting was adjourned until further notice. = 会议延期，另行通知。
近义词:postponing, suspending, deferring, recessing
反义词:convening, commencing, initiating
词性:动词(present participle)


adjourning:/əˈdʒɜːrnɪŋ/
n.
休会，延期
The act of suspending a meeting or session to a future time or indefinitely.
The adjourning of the meeting allowed members to review the proposal. = 会议的休会让成员们有机会审查提案。

The unexpected adjourning of the conference left many attendees confused. =  会议的意外休会让许多与会者感到困惑。
近义词:postponement, suspension, deferment, recess
反义词:convening, commencement, initiation
词性:名词
