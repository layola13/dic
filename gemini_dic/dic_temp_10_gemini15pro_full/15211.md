backdating:/ˈbækˌdeɪtɪŋ/
n.|回溯日期，倒填日期|The act of putting a date on a document that is earlier than the current date.|The contract was backdated to January 1st.  这份合同的日期被回溯到1月1日。|anachronism, retroaction|predating|noun
backdate:/ˌbækˈdeɪt/
vt.|回溯日期，倒填日期|To put a date on a document that is earlier than the current date.|He backdated the check to avoid paying the late fee. 他把支票的日期倒填以避免支付滞纳金。|anachronize, retroact|predate|verb
