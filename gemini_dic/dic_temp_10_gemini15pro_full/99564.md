interview: /ˈɪntərvjuː/ (US) /ˈɪntəvjuː/ (UK)| vt.|面试，采访；面谈|To ask someone questions in a formal meeting in order to find out if they are suitable for a particular job, or for a course of study at a college, university, etc.|例句：They interviewed several candidates for the job. 他们面试了几个求职者。|例句：She was interviewed by the police. 她被警察问讯了。|近义词：question, examine, assess|反义词：N/A|词性：verb

Etymology:  From late Middle French *entreveue*, from Old French entreveue "interview; look, glimpse," noun use of past participle of s'entrevoir "to see each other," from entre- "between" + voir "to see."
