octets:/ɒkˈtɛts/ | n. | 八位字节，八重奏 | A group of eight bits treated as a unit.  It is commonly used in computing and telecommunications to describe a byte of data. |  The file size is measured in octets.  该文件大小以八位字节为单位进行计量。 | synonyms: byte, eight bits | antonyms: N/A | noun

octette:/ɒkˈtɛt/ | n. | 八位字节，八重奏 |  A group of eight, especially eight singers or musicians. Also, a group of eight bits treated as a unit (same as octet). | The octette performed beautifully at the concert.  八重奏在音乐会上表演得很精彩。| synonyms: byte, eight bits, octet | antonyms: N/A | noun
