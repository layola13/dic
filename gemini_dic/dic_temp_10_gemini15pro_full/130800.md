nonantagonistically:/ˌnɒnænˌtæɡəˈnɪstɪkli/
adv.
非对抗性地
in a way that is not antagonistic; without opposition or hostility.
The two groups worked together nonantagonistically to achieve a common goal.  这两个团体非对抗性地合作以实现共同目标。
Synonym: cooperatively, harmoniously, amicably
Antonym: antagonistically, hostilely, aggressively
词性: 副词

词源:
"Non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "antagonistic" (adjective meaning "showing or feeling active opposition or hostility toward someone or something") + "-ally" (suffix forming adverbs).  "Antagonistic" ultimately comes from the Greek "antagonistes" meaning "opponent, competitor."
