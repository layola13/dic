anti-creationism:/ˌænti kriˈeɪʃənɪzəm/
n.
反创造论
Opposition to creationism, the belief that the universe and living organisms originate from specific acts of divine creation, as in the biblical account, rather than by natural processes such as evolution.
Anti-creationism advocates for scientific explanations like evolution. 反创造论支持像进化论这样的科学解释。
Many scientists are proponents of anti-creationism. 许多科学家是反创造论的支持者。
近义词:evolutionism, Darwinism
反义词:creationism
词性:名词
