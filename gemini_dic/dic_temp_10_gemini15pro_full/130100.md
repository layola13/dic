non-prosecution:/ˌnɑːn prɑːsɪˈkjuːʃən/ | n. |不起诉，不予起诉|The decision of a prosecutor or other legal authority not to pursue criminal charges against someone, even though there may be sufficient evidence to do so.|The suspect was granted non-prosecution due to his cooperation with the investigation. 嫌疑人因配合调查而获得不起诉。|Synonyms: immunity, pardon, amnesty|Antonyms: prosecution, indictment|noun
