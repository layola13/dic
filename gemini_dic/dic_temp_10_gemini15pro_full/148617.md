ETYMOLOGY->
"over-" (prefix) + "theatrical" + "-ness" (suffix)

"over-" denotes excess or beyond a normal limit.
"theatrical" derives from "theatre," ultimately from Greek "theatron" (a place for viewing).
"-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
