demotivation:/ˌdiːˌmoʊtɪˈveɪʃn/ | n. |失去动力， desmotivación|  The lack of motivation or the process of reducing motivation.  It refers to the feeling of being discouraged and unwilling to take action or pursue goals.|  The constant criticism led to demotivation among the team members. 不断的批评导致团队成员失去动力。| synonyms: discouragement, disillusionment, apathy | antonyms: motivation, encouragement, inspiration | noun

demotivate:/ˌdiːˈmoʊtɪveɪt/ | v. |使失去动力，desmotivar | To discourage or make someone lose motivation or enthusiasm.|  His negative comments demotivated the entire class. 他的负面评论使整个班级都失去了动力。| synonyms: discourage, dishearten, dispirit | antonyms: motivate, encourage, inspire | verb
