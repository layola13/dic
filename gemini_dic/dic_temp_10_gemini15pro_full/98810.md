intellective:/ɪnˈtɛlɪktɪv/| adj.|智力的，理智的|Relating to the intellect or understanding.|例句：She possesses strong intellective abilities. 她拥有很强的智力。

例句：His intellective pursuits led him to a career in academia. 他的求知欲使他走上了学术生涯。|近义词：intellectual, cognitive, mental|反义词：emotional, instinctive|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Late Latin *intellectivus*, from Latin intellectus (“understanding”), past participle of intellegō (“I understand”).  Ultimately from inter- (“between”) + legō (“choose, gather, read”).
