floored:/flɔːrd/| adj.| 惊呆的，不知所措的；被打败的；地板铺设好的|Overwhelmed with surprise or emotion, astonished; defeated; furnished with a floor. |例句1：I was absolutely floored when I heard the news. 我听到这个消息时完全惊呆了。

例句2：The boxer was floored in the first round. 拳击手在第一回合就被击倒了。|近义词：astonished, amazed, stunned, dumbfounded, overwhelmed, defeated|反义词：calm, composed, unexcited, victorious|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Old English "flor," meaning "floor," "ground," "bottom."  The figurative sense of being "floored" (overwhelmed) developed later.
