out-bellowing:/ˌaʊtˈbɛləʊɪŋ/
verb
大声吼叫，胜过…的吼叫
to shout louder than someone or something else.  To surpass in bellowing.
The captain out-bellowed the storm, urging his crew to fight on. 船长的声音盖过了风暴的怒吼，激励着他的船员们继续战斗。
The rival fans out-bellowed each other throughout the match.  敌对球迷在整场比赛中互相叫喊。
Synonyms: outshouting, overpowering, drowning out
Antonyms: whispering, murmuring, mumbling
词性: 动词
