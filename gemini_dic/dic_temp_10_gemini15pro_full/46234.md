cystolithic:/ˌsɪstəˈlɪθɪk/| adj.|含膀胱结石的，膀胱结石的|Containing, bearing, or pertaining to cystoliths.|The leaves of the fig tree are covered with cystolithic hairs.|近义词: calculus-containing (though this refers more generally to stones, not specifically cystoliths)|反义词: (No direct antonym.  Consider "cystolith-free")
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From cystolith + -ic.  Cystolith from Greek *kýstis* (bladder, sac) + *líthos* (stone).
