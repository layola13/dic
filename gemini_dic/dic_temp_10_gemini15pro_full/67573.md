extrapolators:/ɪkˈstræpəleɪtərz/| n. |外推者，推断者|People who extrapolate, or make inferences about the future based on present trends or data.  They extend known information to unknown areas.|例句：Market extrapolators predict a rise in housing prices next year. 市场预测者预测明年房价会上涨。|近义词：forecasters, predictors, interpreters, analysts|反义词: N/A |noun
