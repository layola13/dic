overwind:/ˌoʊvərˈwaɪnd/| vt. |上发条过多；卷绕过多；（尤指旧式发条装置的）上弦过紧；过分紧张，过度兴奋|To wind (something) too tightly, especially a spring-driven mechanism. Also, to become excessively tense or excited.|He overwound the clock and it broke. 他把钟上得太紧了，结果它坏了。|近义词：overtighten, overstrain|反义词: unwind, loosen|verb

overwind:/ˌoʊvərˈwaɪnd/| n. |上发条过多|The act of overwinding something.|The overwind of the clock spring caused it to snap.  时钟发条的上弦过紧导致它断裂。|近义词：overtightening|反义词: unwinding|noun
