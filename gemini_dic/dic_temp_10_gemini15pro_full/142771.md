Simple Present:
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does he do for a living? 他做什么工作？


Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner? 她晚饭在做什么？



Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has he done to his hair? 他对他的头发做了什么？



Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on recently? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has she been reading lately? 她最近一直在读什么？



Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say to him? 她对他说了什么？



Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when you saw her? 你看到她时她在想什么？



Past Perfect:
What had you done before you went to bed? 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? 她生病前吃了什么？



Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I called? 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major?  她换专业之前一直在学什么？



Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气怎么样？



Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be studying next semester? 她下学期会学什么？



Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the age of 30? 到30岁时你将取得什么成就？
What will she have finished by the end of the day? 到今天结束时她将完成什么？



Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时她将已经学习什么多久了？



Subjunctive Mood:
If I knew what to say, I would say it. 如果我知道该说什么，我就会说。
I wish I knew what she was thinking. 我希望我知道她在想什么。


Passive Voice:
What was discussed at the meeting? 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done about the problem? 正在对这个问题采取什么措施？



Interrogative Sentences:
What is your favorite color? 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? 你周末的计划是什么？



Negative Sentences:
I don't know what to do. 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what you mean. 她不明白你的意思。
