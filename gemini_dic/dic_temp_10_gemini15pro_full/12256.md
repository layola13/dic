ascension:/əˈsenʃən/| n.|上升，登高，晋升|The ascension of Jesus into heaven is a core belief in Christianity. 耶稣升天是基督教的核心信仰。|例句：The hot air balloon's ascension was a breathtaking sight.  热气球的上升是一个令人叹为观止的景象。

例句：His ascension to the CEO position was a surprise to many. 他晋升为首席执行官的职位让很多人感到意外。|近义词：rise, ascent, climb, elevation, promotion|反义词：descent, decline, fall|词性:名词

Etymology: From Middle English ascensioun, from Old French ascension, from Latin ascensio (“a rising”), from ascendere (“to climb”), from ad- (“to”) + scandere (“to climb”).
