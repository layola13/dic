align:/əˈlaɪn/ | v. |排列，对齐；使一致，使结盟 | to arrange something in a straight line, or to arrange things in a position parallel to something else; to change your opinions or behaviour so that they are the same as those of another person or group |  We need to align the edges of the paper. 我们需要对齐纸的边缘。 | synonyms: adjust, line up, coordinate, straighten, ally | antonyms: skew, misalign, disarrange, separate | verb

align:/əˈlaɪn/ | n. | 校准线;准线 | a line that things are arranged along, or that is used to help arrange things in the correct position | The architect drew an align to show the position of the wall. 建筑师画了一条准线来表示墙的位置。 | synonyms: guideline, axis, benchmark | antonyms: N/A | noun
