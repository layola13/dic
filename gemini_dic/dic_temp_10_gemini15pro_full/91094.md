hwyl:/ˈhuːɪl/ /hwɪl/| n.|激情，热情，一股劲儿；告别语（尤用于威尔士语）|A feeling of emotional uplift and energy, often accompanied by a sense of longing or nostalgia; a Welsh word of farewell.|例句：There's a certain hwyl about Welsh singing. 威尔士的歌声中有一种独特的激情。

例句：He left with a cheery "Hwyl!" 他兴高采烈地说了声“Hwyl！”告别。|近义词：fervor, passion, enthusiasm, spirit, zest, goodbye, farewell|反义词：apathy, indifference|词性:名词


词源:
From Welsh hwyl (“sail”), from Proto-Celtic *sēwlos. Cognate with Cornish hwil (“sail”), Breton gwel (“sail”).
