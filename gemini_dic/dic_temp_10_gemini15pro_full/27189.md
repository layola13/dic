calliper: /ˈkælɪpər/ (US) /ˈkælɪpə(r)/ (UK)| v.|用卡尺测量|To measure something using a calliper.|He callipered the thickness of the steel plate. 他用卡尺测量了钢板的厚度。|Synonyms: gauge, measure|Antonyms: None|Verb
