boomerangs:/ˈbuːməræŋz/ /ˈbuːməræŋs/
|n.|回旋镖；自食其果的行为；|A curved wooden missile that can be thrown so as to return to the thrower.  一种弯曲的木制投掷物，可以投掷后返回到投掷者手中。|The new tax laws have acted like a boomerang, hurting the very people they were intended to help. 新的税法就像回旋镖一样，伤害了他们原本想要帮助的人。|synonyms: comeuppance, backlash, kickback|antonyms: gift, blessing|noun
