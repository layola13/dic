nonliberalism:/ˌnɑːnˈlɪbərəlɪzəm/
n.
非自由主义
A political philosophy or ideology that rejects or opposes the principles of liberalism.  It encompasses a wide range of doctrines and movements, including conservatism, socialism, fascism, and others.
Nonliberalism gained traction in several countries after the 2008 financial crisis. 2008年金融危机后，非自由主义在一些国家获得了关注。
The rise of nonliberalism poses a challenge to democratic institutions. 非自由主义的兴起对民主制度构成了挑战。
Synonyms: illiberalism, anti-liberalism
Antonyms: liberalism
