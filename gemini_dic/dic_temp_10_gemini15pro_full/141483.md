opinionist:/əˈpɪnjənɪst/
|n.|持己见者，固执己见者，专栏作家|A person who is unduly attached to their own opinions; a dogmatist. Also, a writer of signed articles expressing personal views on current events or other topics.|He was known as a stubborn opinionist, rarely swayed by the views of others. 他以顽固的持己见者而闻名，很少被别人的观点动摇。  She's a respected opinionist for the New York Times. 她是纽约时报一位受人尊敬的专栏作家。|近义词：dogmatist, pundit, commentator|反义词: conformist|词性: noun
