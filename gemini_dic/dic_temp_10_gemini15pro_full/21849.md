bodice-ripper:/ˈbɒdɪs ˈrɪpər/| n. |情色浪漫小说（尤指历史题材）|A genre of historical romance novels characterized by sensual, often explicit, depictions of a passionate love affair, frequently involving abduction, seduction, or other forms of coercion.  The term often carries a negative connotation, suggesting formulaic plots and exaggerated portrayals of sexual encounters.|例句：She devoured bodice-rippers all summer. 她整个夏天都在狂读情色浪漫小说。

例句：The bookstore had a whole section dedicated to bodice-rippers. 这家书店有一个专区摆放情色浪漫小说。|近义词：romance novel, historical romance, erotic novel|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) literary fiction, classic literature|词性:noun
