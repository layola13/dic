PHRASE->
ensure against: 预防，确保免于
He took out insurance to ensure against financial loss. 他投保以防经济损失。

ensure for: 确保…得到…
The government is trying to ensure affordable housing for everyone. 政府正在努力确保人人享有负担得起的住房。

ensure to:  确保…
Please ensure to lock the door when you leave. 请确保离开时锁好门。 (不太常用，更常见的是 "make sure to")

ensure that: 确保…
Please ensure that all documents are submitted on time. 请确保所有文件都按时提交。

ensure success: 确保成功
Hard work and dedication will ensure success. 勤奋和奉献将确保成功。
