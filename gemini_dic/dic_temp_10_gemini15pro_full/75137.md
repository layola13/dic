frisky:/ˈfrɪski/| adj.|活泼的，欢闹的|Playful and full of energy.|The frisky puppy chased its tail around the room. 活泼的小狗在房间里追着它的尾巴跑。|近义词：playful, lively, spirited, energetic|反义词：lethargic, sluggish, listless|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Middle English *frisken*, frequentative of *frisen* (“to dance, jump about, frolic”), from Old Norse *frísk* (“quick, lively, eager”), from Proto-Germanic *friskaz* (“fresh, lively”), from Proto-Indo-European *preysk-* (“to leap, jump”). Cognate with Danish *frisk* (“fresh”), Swedish *frisk* (“fresh, healthy”), Dutch *frisch* (“fresh”), German *frisch* (“fresh”), Old English *fersc* (“fresh”).
