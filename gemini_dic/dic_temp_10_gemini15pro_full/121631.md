misparsed:/ˌmɪsˈpɑːrst/ | adj. | 错误解析的；语法分析错误的 | Incorrectly analyzed or interpreted, especially by a computer program.  A sentence that is misparsed can lead to incorrect understanding. | The sentence was misparsed by the software, resulting in a nonsensical translation.  这个句子被软件错误地解析了，导致翻译结果荒谬。| The computer misparsed the command and shut down unexpectedly.  计算机错误地解析了命令，意外关机了。| synonyms: misinterpreted, misread, misconstrued, misunderstood | antonyms: correctly parsed, accurately interpreted, understood | adjective
