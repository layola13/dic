multivane:/ˈmʌltiˌveɪn/
adjective|多叶片的，多翼片的|Having multiple vanes or blades.  A multivane impeller is more efficient than a conventional impeller.|The multivane fan provides superior airflow.|近义词:multiblade|反义词:single-vane
noun|多叶片装置|A device with multiple vanes, especially a type of impeller.|The multivane in the pump increased its efficiency.|近义词:multiblade impeller|反义词:single-vane impeller
