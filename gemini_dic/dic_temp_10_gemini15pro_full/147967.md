overresponds:/ˌoʊvər rɪˈspɑːndz/ | v. | 反应过度 | To react to something with greater intensity or to a greater extent than is necessary or appropriate. | The stock market overresponded to the news, causing a sharp drop in prices. 股票市场对这一消息反应过度，导致价格暴跌。| The child overresponded to the scolding and burst into tears. 孩子对责骂反应过度，突然大哭起来。| overreact, exaggerate | underreact, downplay | verb
