over-digests:/ˌoʊvər daɪˈdʒɛsts/ | v. | 过度消化，消化过多 | To digest food more thoroughly or for a longer period than is usual or necessary.  The process can sometimes lead to the breakdown of nutrients beyond their usable form, potentially reducing their nutritional value. |  He over-digests his food, leading to frequent indigestion. 他过度消化食物，导致经常消化不良。

She believes she over-digests everything, which makes her feel tired. 她认为她过度消化一切，这让她感到疲倦。 | 近义词: none (This term is relatively uncommon and lacks direct synonyms.) | 反义词: under-digest (less common, meaning to insufficiently digest) | 词性: verb
