medials: /ˈmiːdiəlz/ | n. | 中间音，中央元音 | Sounds articulated with the tongue positioned near the middle of the mouth, neither high nor low, and neither front nor back.  | Example: The schwa sound /ə/ is a common medial vowel in English. 中央元音/ə/在英语中很常见。 | Example: The vowel sounds in "bed" and "cut" are considered medial vowels.  单词"bed"和"cut"中的元音被认为是中间元音。 | Synonym: central vowels | Antonym: peripheral vowels | noun

medials: /ˈmiːdiəlz/ | adj. | 中间的，中央的 |  Relating to or situated in the middle.  This usage is less common than the noun form related to phonetics. | Example: Medial structures in the brain play a crucial role in memory. 大脑中的中间结构在记忆中起着至关重要的作用。 | Example: The medial ligaments provide stability to the knee joint. 中间韧带为膝关节提供稳定性。 | Synonym: middle, central | Antonym: lateral, peripheral | adjective
