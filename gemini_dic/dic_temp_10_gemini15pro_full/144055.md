outrivals: /ˌaʊtˈraɪvəl/
词性: verb
单词属性: 动词
中文翻译: 超越，胜过
英文解析: To surpass or exceed in rivalry; to be more successful than.
例句:
She outrivaled all her competitors in the sales contest.
她在销售竞赛中胜过所有竞争对手。

He outrivaled his brother in both academics and sports.
他在学习和运动方面都超过了他的兄弟。

近义词: surpass, exceed, outdo, outperform, outstrip
反义词: lose to, be defeated by, fall behind
词源:  From out- + rival (v.), from Old French rivaler "to quarrel," from Latin rivalis "one using the same stream (as another)," hence "rival, competitor," from rivus "stream."
