fomenters:/ˈfoʊˌmɛntərz/ /ˈfəʊˌmɛntərz/
n.|煽动者，教唆者|People who incite or stir up trouble or rebellion. |The fomenters of the rebellion were quickly arrested. 煽动叛乱的人很快就被逮捕了。|instigators, agitators, provocateurs|pacifiers, mediators, peacemakers|noun

foments:/foʊˈmɛnts/ /fəʊˈmɛnts/
v.|煽动，激起|To instigate or stir up (an undesirable or violent sentiment or course of action).|They were accused of fomenting political unrest. 他们被指控煽动政治动乱。|instigate, incite, provoke|quell, suppress, calm|verb
