overkicks: /ˌoʊvərˈkɪks/ | n. | 过度踢腿；过度热情；过度反应 | An excessive or exaggerated kicking motion, often in sports or performance; also can refer to an over-enthusiastic or exaggerated reaction. | The dancer's overkicks were distracting and took away from the elegance of the piece.  舞者的过度踢腿很让人分心，也破坏了作品的优雅。 |  The team's celebration after the goal was a bit of an overkick, considering they were still losing the game.  考虑到他们仍然输掉了比赛，球队在进球后的庆祝有点过度热情了。 | 近义词: overreaction, exaggeration, overkill | 反义词: understatement, restraint | 词性: 名词

词源: "Over-" (prefix indicating excess) + "kick" (verb meaning to strike with the foot)
