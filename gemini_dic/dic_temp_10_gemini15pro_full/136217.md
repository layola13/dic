nonremedial:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈmiː.di.əl/
adjective
非补救的，无需补习的
Not requiring remedial education; of or relating to students who do not require remedial courses.
Students with high entrance exam scores usually qualify for nonremedial English classes.  入学考试分数高的学生通常有资格参加非补救英语课程。
The university offers a range of nonremedial courses for advanced students.  这所大学为优秀学生提供一系列非补救课程。
近义词: advanced, standard, regular
反义词: remedial, basic, elementary
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not" or "opposite of"  来源于拉丁语，意为“不”或“相反的”。
remedial (adjective): meaning "giving or intended as a remedy or cure"  来源于拉丁语 remedium (“remedy”)，意为“补救的”或“治疗的”。
