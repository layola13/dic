horoscopist: /həˈrɒskəpɪst/ (British) /həˈrɑːskəpɪst/ (American)
noun
占星家，星座学家
A person who casts or interprets horoscopes.
She visited a horoscopist to learn about her future. 她拜访了一位占星家，想了解自己的未来。

He's a well-known horoscopist and often appears on television. 他是一位著名的占星家，经常出现在电视上。

Synonym: astrologer, astrologist
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps skeptic, doubter)
词性: noun
