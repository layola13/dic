meaningless:/ˈmiːnɪŋləs/| adj.|无意义的，无价值的|Lacking meaning or significance; pointless.|Her life felt meaningless after her retirement. 退休后，她的生活感到毫无意义。|The gifts were meaningless trinkets. 这些礼物都是毫无意义的小玩意儿。|insignificant, pointless, futile, senseless, valueless|meaningful, significant, important, valuable|adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From mean (adj.) "low, common" + -less.  The adjective meaning "having no meaning" is from the late 14c.
