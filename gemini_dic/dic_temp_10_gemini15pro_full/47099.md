dauby:/ˈdɔːbi/ | adj. | 涂抹的；拙劣的；俗气的 | Smeared or coated with a sticky substance; crudely or unskillfully done; gaudy or tasteless. | The painting was dauby and amateurish. 这幅画涂抹得很拙劣，像是业余爱好者画的。| 近义词:smeared, messy, crude, tacky, gaudy | 反义词:neat, refined, elegant, tasteful | 词性:形容词
daub:/dɔːb/ | n. | 涂抹；涂料 | A smear or coating of a sticky substance; a crude or unskillfully done painting. | He wiped a daub of paint from his forehead. 他擦掉了额头上的油漆污渍。|近义词:smear, smudge, blotch | 反义词: - | 词性:名词
daub:/dɔːb/ | vt. | 涂抹；涂抹颜料 | To smear or coat with a sticky substance; to paint crudely or unskillfully. | She daubed some cream on her face. 她在脸上涂抹了一些面霜。| 近义词:smear, plaster, coat | 反义词: - | 词性:动词

词源: From Middle English *dauben, from Old French dauber, of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to Frankish *dubban "to strike, beat."
