acrodont: /ˈækrəˌdɒnt/ | adj. n. | 侧生齿的，侧生齿动物 | Having teeth attached to the outer edge of the jawbone, rather than in sockets. Also refers to animals with such teeth. | Acrodont teeth are characteristic of some reptiles, like lizards.  侧生齿是某些爬行动物（如蜥蜴）的特征。| The tuatara is an example of an acrodont reptile.  大蜥蜴是侧生齿爬行动物的一个例子。 | 近义词:  pleurodont (in some contexts) | 反义词: thecodont | 词性: adjective, noun

acrodont: /ˈækrəˌdɒnt/ | n. | 侧生齿动物 | An animal with acrodont teeth. | Lizards and snakes are examples of acrodonts. 蜥蜴和蛇是侧生齿动物的例子。 |  Acrodonts are common in arid regions. 侧生齿动物在干旱地区很常见。| 近义词:  pleurodont (in some contexts) | 反义词: thecodont | 词性: noun
