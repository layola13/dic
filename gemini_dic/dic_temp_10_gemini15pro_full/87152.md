herbist:/ˈɜːrbəlɪst/ |/ˈhɜːrbəlɪst/| n. |草药师；药草学家|A person who is an expert in the use of herbs, especially medicinal herbs. |例句：The herbist recommended a tea made from chamomile and lavender to soothe her nerves. 草药师推荐她喝甘菊和薰衣草茶来舒缓神经。

例句：My grandmother was a skilled herbist who could identify and use a wide variety of plants for healing. 我的祖母是一位经验丰富的草药师，可以识别和使用各种各样的植物来治疗疾病。|近义词：herbalist, botanist, naturopath |反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑) conventional doctor, physician|词性: noun
