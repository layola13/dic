herbalists:/ˈɜːrbəlɪsts/ | n. | 草药师；草药医生 | A person who practices herbal medicine. |  例句：The herbalists recommended a tea made from chamomile and lavender for relaxation. 草药师推荐了一种用洋甘菊和薰衣草制成的茶来放松身心。

例句：Many herbalists believe in the healing power of plants. 许多草药师相信植物的治愈能力。 | 近义词：herbal practitioner,  botanist (in some contexts) | 反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps) conventional doctor, physician | 词性: noun
