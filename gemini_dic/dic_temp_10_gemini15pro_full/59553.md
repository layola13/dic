eavesdropper:/ˈiːvzˌdrɑːpər/ (US) /ˈiːvzˌdrɒpə(r)/ (UK)| n. |窃听者；偷听者|Someone who secretly listens to a private conversation.|例句：The eavesdropper was caught listening in on the confidential meeting.  那个窃听者在偷听机密会议时被抓住了。

例句：She suspected her neighbor was an eavesdropper. 她怀疑她的邻居是个窃听者。

近义词：listener, snooper, spy|反义词: None (No direct antonym for a person performing this action)|词性: Noun
