lobular:/ˈlɒbjʊlə(r)/ (US) /ˈlɑːbjələr/| adj.|小叶的，分叶的|Relating to or resembling a lobule (a small lobe or subdivision of a lobe, as in a gland or the lung).|The doctor identified lobular carcinoma in situ during the biopsy. 医生在活检过程中发现了小叶原位癌。|The liver is composed of numerous lobular units. 肝脏由许多小叶单位组成。|近义词:lobed, segmented|反义词:unlobed, undivided|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *lobulus*, diminutive of *lobus* ("lobe").
