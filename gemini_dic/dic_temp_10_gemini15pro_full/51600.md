devastates:/ˈdevəsteɪts/
verb
摧毁，毁灭
completely destroys or ruins something.
The hurricane devastated the coastal town. 飓风摧毁了沿海城镇。
近义词: ruins, destroys, wrecks, ravages
反义词: builds, creates, restores, improves
词性: verb


devastating:/ˈdevəsteɪtɪŋ/
adjective
毁灭性的，极具破坏力的
causing a lot of damage or destruction.
The earthquake had a devastating effect on the region. 地震对该地区造成了毁灭性的影响。
近义词: destructive, catastrophic, disastrous, calamitous
反义词: beneficial, helpful, constructive, restorative
词性: adjective

devastation:/ˌdevəˈsteɪʃn/
noun
毁灭，破坏
great destruction or damage.
The devastation caused by the war was immense. 战争造成的破坏是巨大的。
近义词: destruction, ruin, wreckage, havoc
反义词: construction, restoration, improvement, repair
词性: noun

词源: From Latin *devastare, from de- + vastare "to lay waste," from vastus "empty, desolate."
