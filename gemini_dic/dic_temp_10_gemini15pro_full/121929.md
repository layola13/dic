missheathed:/ˌmɪsˈʃiːðd/
adjective
错误入鞘的；收回不当的
returned or placed incorrectly in a sheath (a close-fitting cover for the blade of a knife or sword).
The knight missheathed his sword, accidentally cutting himself. 骑士把剑错误地插入鞘中，不小心割伤了自己。
Near synonyms: wrongly sheathed, improperly sheathed, incorrectly sheathed
Antonyms: properly sheathed, correctly sheathed
word type: adjective
