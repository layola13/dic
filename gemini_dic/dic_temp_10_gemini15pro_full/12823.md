asslike:/ˈæslaɪk/| adj.|像驴子一样的；愚蠢的|Resembling a donkey, especially in being stubborn or stupid. |Her asslike stubbornness prevented her from seeing the other side of the argument. 她像驴子一样的固执让她无法看到问题的另一面。|近义词：donkeyish, obstinate, stubborn, foolish, stupid|反义词: intelligent, smart, wise, compliant, flexible|词性:形容词

词源:  Compound of "ass" (donkey) + "like" (similar to).  "Ass" comes from Old English "assa," ultimately from Latin "asinus."  "Like" comes from Old English "gelic."
