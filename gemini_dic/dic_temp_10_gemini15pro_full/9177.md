antinihilists:/ˌæntiˈnaɪəˌlɪsts/
n.
反虚无主义者
a person who opposes nihilism (the rejection of all religious and moral principles, often in the belief that life is meaningless).
Antinihilists believe that life has meaning and purpose.  反虚无主义者相信生活是有意义和目的的。
The group of antinihilists gathered to discuss the importance of values in society. 这群反虚无主义者聚集在一起讨论价值观在社会中的重要性。
Synonyms:  optimist,  life-affirming
Antonyms: nihilist, pessimist
Noun
