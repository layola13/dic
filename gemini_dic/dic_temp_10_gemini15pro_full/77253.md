gastronomically:/ˌɡæstrəˈnɒmɪkli/ | adv. | 美食地；烹飪地；讲究地 |  In a way that relates to the art and practice of cooking and eating good food. | The meal was gastronomically exquisite.  这顿饭从美食的角度来说堪称精美。| 近义词: culinarily, epicureanly | 反义词: plainly, simply | 词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *gastronomique* + *-ly*.  *Gastronomique* derives from Greek *gastēr* (belly) + *nomos* (law, custom).
