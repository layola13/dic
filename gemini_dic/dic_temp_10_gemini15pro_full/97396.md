infrastructures:/ˈɪnfrəˌstrʌktʃərz/
n.
基础设施
The basic physical and organizational structures and facilities (e.g., buildings, roads, and power supplies) needed for the operation of a society or enterprise.
The country needs to invest more in its infrastructures, such as roads, bridges, and communication networks.  这个国家需要对其基础设施，如道路、桥梁和通信网络进行更多投资。

近义词:facilities, amenities, utilities
反义词:(No direct antonym, but consider "lack of development," "underdevelopment," or "disrepair")
词性:noun
