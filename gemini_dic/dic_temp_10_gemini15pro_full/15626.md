bain-marie:/ˌbeɪn məˈriː/ |n.| 水浴锅，隔水加热锅 |A container holding hot water into which a pan is placed for slow cooking. |The sauce was kept warm in a bain-marie. 酱汁放在水浴锅里保温。|Synonyms: double boiler, water bath|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps direct heat cooking)|noun

bain-marie:/ˌbeɪn məˈriː/ |n.| 隔水加热法 |The method of cooking something by placing the container in a pan of hot water.|The chocolate was melted using a bain-marie. 巧克力是用隔水加热法融化的。|Synonyms: double boiling, water bath cooking|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps direct heat cooking)|noun
