media's:/ˈmiːdiəz/
noun
媒体的；媒介的
Possessive form of "media," referring to something belonging to or associated with the media.
The media's coverage of the election was extensive. 媒体对选举的报道非常广泛。
The media's influence on public opinion is undeniable. 媒体对公众舆论的影响是不可否认的。
synonyms: press's, journalism's
antonyms: None (possessive forms don't typically have antonyms)
