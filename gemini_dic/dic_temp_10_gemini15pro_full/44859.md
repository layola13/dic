crutch: /krʌtʃ/ | n. | 拐杖；支撑；依靠 | A support, typically one of a pair, used by a person with an injury or disability to assist in walking.  Something that provides support or help, especially something that is relied on too much. |  He walked with the aid of crutches. 他拄着拐杖行走。 She uses her job as a crutch to avoid dealing with her personal problems. 她把工作当作逃避个人问题的依靠。 | synonyms: support, prop, aid, staff | antonyms: hindrance, obstacle | noun

crutch: /krʌtʃ/ | v. | 用拐杖支撑；支撑；依靠 | To support with a crutch or crutches. To prop up. | He crutched his way across the room. 他拄着拐杖穿过房间。| synonyms: support, prop, brace, shore up | antonyms: undermine, weaken | verb
