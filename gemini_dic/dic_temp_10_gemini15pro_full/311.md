abet: /əˈbɛt/
verb
教唆，煽动，支持
encourage or assist (someone) to do something wrong, in particular to commit a crime or offense.
He was abetted in the deception by his wife. = 他受妻子唆使参与了这起欺骗。
The group was suspected of abetting terrorists. = 该组织涉嫌唆使恐怖分子。

近义词: encourage, support, assist, aid, help, promote, facilitate
反义词: hinder, obstruct, impede, discourage, prevent, deter
词性: verb

过去完成时:

虚拟语气:

疑问句:
