nutlike:/ˈnʌtˌlaɪk/
adjective
坚果似的；像坚果的
Resembling a nut in shape, flavor, or texture.
The cookies had a nutlike flavor, even though they contained no nuts. 这些饼干有坚果的味道，即使它们不含坚果。
The seeds have a nutlike texture. 这些种子有像坚果一样的质地。
近义词: nutty, nut-flavored
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider bland, flavorless if referring to taste)
