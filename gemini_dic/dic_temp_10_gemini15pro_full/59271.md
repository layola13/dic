eagerer:/ˈiːɡərər/ (US), /ˈiːɡə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更渴望的，更热切的|Comparative form of "eager," meaning showing a keen interest or intense desire.|She was eagerer than ever to start the new project. 她比以往任何时候都渴望开始这个新项目。|The children were eagerer for the holidays than the adults. 孩子们比大人们更渴望假期。|近义词：keener, more enthusiastic, more avid|反义词：less eager, apathetic, indifferent|词性：形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *eger*, from Old French *aigre* (sharp, keen), from Latin *ācer* (sharp, keen, pungent).
