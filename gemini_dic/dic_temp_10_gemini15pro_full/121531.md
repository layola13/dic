mismanager:/ˌmɪsˈmænɪdʒər/| n. |管理不善者，经营不善者|A person who manages something badly, inefficiently, or dishonestly.|The company's downfall was largely due to the mismanagement of the previous CEO.  这家公司的倒闭很大程度上是由于前任首席执行官管理不善造成的。|Synonyms: incompetent, bungler, |Antonyms: expert, professional|Noun
