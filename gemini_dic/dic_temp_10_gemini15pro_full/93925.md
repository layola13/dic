immunities:/ɪˈmjuːnɪtiz/
|n.|免疫力；豁免权|The state of being exempt from or resistant to a particular disease or the like.  The ability of an organism to resist a particular infection or toxin by the action of specific antibodies or sensitized white blood cells.|He has developed an immunity to the disease. 他对这种疾病产生了免疫力。|Synonyms: resistance, exemption, impunity|Antonyms: vulnerability, susceptibility|Noun
