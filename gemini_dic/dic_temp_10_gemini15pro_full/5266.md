allomorphic:/ˌæləˈmɔːrfɪk/ | adj. | 同质异形的 | Existing in different forms, especially relating to morphemes (the smallest meaningful units in a language) that have the same meaning but different sounds or spellings. | The words "incapable" (in-) and "impossible" (im-) demonstrate allomorphic variations of the same prefix. “incapable”（in-）和“impossible”（im-）展示了相同前缀的同质异形变体。| 近义词: variant, allomorphous | 反义词: invariant | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From allo- + -morphic. Allo- comes from Greek "allos" meaning "other".  -Morphic comes from Greek "morphē" meaning "form".
