chastiser:/ˈtʃæstaɪzər/| n.|惩罚者；责骂者|A person who chastises or reprimands someone.  |The teacher acted as a chastiser to the unruly students. 老师对不守规矩的学生充当了惩罚者的角色。|Synonyms: reprover, rebuker, disciplinarian, corrector|Antonyms: praiser, flatterer|noun
chastise:/tʃæˈstaɪz/| vt.|惩罚；责骂；严厉批评|To rebuke or reprimand severely. |The coach chastised the team for their poor performance. 教练严厉批评了球队的糟糕表现。|He was chastised for his dishonesty. 他因为不诚实而受到惩罚。|Synonyms: reprimand, rebuke, scold, reprove, admonish, censure|Antonyms: praise, commend, laud, compliment|verb
