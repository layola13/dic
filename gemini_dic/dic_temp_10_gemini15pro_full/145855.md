overdepressive:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈprɛsɪv/| adj.|过度抑郁的，极度沮丧的|Excessively or unduly depressed; suffering from severe or extreme depression. This word is not commonly used in standard English and might be considered informal or even non-standard.|She felt overdepressive after losing her job. 她失业后感到极度沮丧。|近义词：extremely depressed, severely depressed, profoundly sad, deeply melancholic|反义词：elated, euphoric, joyful|词性:形容词

词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "depressive" (adjective from "depress").  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "depressive."
