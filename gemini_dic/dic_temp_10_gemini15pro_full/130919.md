nonascertainableness:/ˌnɒnæsərˈteɪnəbəlnəs/
n.
不可确定性
The state or quality of being impossible to ascertain or determine with certainty.
The nonascertainableness of the damage made it difficult to settle the insurance claim.  由于损失难以确定，保险索赔难以解决。
近义词: indeterminacy, uncertainty, unknowability
反义词: certainty, determinacy, ascertainability
词性: noun

nonascertainable:/ˌnɒnæsərˈteɪnəbl/
adj.
不可确定的
Impossible to ascertain or determine with certainty.
The exact cause of the fire remained nonascertainable.  火灾的确切原因仍然无法确定。
近义词: indeterminable, uncertain, unknowable
反义词: certain, determinable, ascertainable
词性: adjective

词源:
From non- + ascertainable + -ness.  Ascertain comes from Old French acertener "to make certain," from a- "to" + certain "certain."
