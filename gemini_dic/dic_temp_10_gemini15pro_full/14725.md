awayday:/əˈweɪdeɪ/
n.
外出日；远足日；客场比赛日
A day trip, often for leisure or to attend a sporting event away from one's home town.
We're having an awayday to the beach next weekend. 我们下周末要去海滩一日游。
The fans travelled to London for the team's awayday at Wembley. 球迷们前往伦敦观看球队在温布利的客场比赛。
近义词: outing, excursion, trip, day trip
反义词: home game
