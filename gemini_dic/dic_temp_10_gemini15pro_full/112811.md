lucernarian:/ˌluːsərˈnɛəriən/
noun
灯水母；灯水母类的动物
An animal belonging to the class Scyphozoa, typically having a bell-shaped body with tentacles. These jellyfish are often bioluminescent.
Lucernarians are a type of jellyfish often found in deep ocean waters. 灯水母是一种常见于深海的水母。
The lucernarian anchored itself to the seabed.  灯水母把自己固定在了海床上。
Synonyms: jellyfish, scyphozoan
Antonyms: None.
