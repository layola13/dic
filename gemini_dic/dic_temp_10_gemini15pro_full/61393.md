emblematicalness:/ˌɛmbləˈmætɪkəlnəs/
n.|象征性，标志性|The quality of being emblematical; symbolic representation.|The emblematicalness of the dove as a symbol of peace is widely recognized.  鸽子作为和平象征的标志性是得到广泛认可的。|
synonym: symbolism, representativeness, typicality
antonym: literalness, concreteness
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From emblematical + -ness.
Emblematical from emblem + -atical.
Emblem from French emblème, from Late Latin emblema, from Greek emblēma "something inserted," from emballein "to throw in, insert," from en- "in" + ballein "to throw."
-ness:  Old English suffix forming nouns from adjectives, meaning "state or quality of being."
