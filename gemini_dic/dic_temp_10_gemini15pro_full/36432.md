colander:/ˈkʌləndər/ /ˈkɑːləndər/| n. |濾器；漏勺|A bowl-shaped kitchen utensil with holes in it, used for draining food such as pasta or vegetables.|例句：She rinsed the pasta in a colander. 她用濾器沖洗意大利麵。

例句：The colander is essential for draining boiled potatoes.  这个漏勺对于沥干煮熟的土豆是必不可少的。|近义词：strainer, sieve|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
