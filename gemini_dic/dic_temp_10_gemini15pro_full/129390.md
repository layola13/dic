nomenclatural:/ˌnoʊmənˈkleɪtʃərəl/
adjective
命名法的; nomenclature的形容词形式
Relating to the system of naming things, especially in science.
The nomenclatural changes caused much confusion among biologists.  这些命名法的变化在生物学家之间引起了很多混乱。
Synonyms: taxonomic, naming, classificatory
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider: descriptive, unclassified)
词性: adjective
