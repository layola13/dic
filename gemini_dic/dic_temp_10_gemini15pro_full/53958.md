discreditability:/dɪsˌkrɛdɪtəˈbɪlɪti/
n.
不可信，不名誉
The state or quality of being discreditable.  The fact or condition of not being believable or trustworthy.
His discreditability stemmed from a series of lies and broken promises. 他的不可信源于一系列的谎言和违背的承诺。
dishonor, disrepute, ignominy
credibility, honor, respectability
