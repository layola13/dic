mortician: /mɔːrˈtɪʃən/ | n. |殡仪员，丧葬承办人 | A person whose job is to prepare dead bodies for burial or cremation and to arrange and manage funerals. |  The mortician prepared the body for viewing. 殡仪员为遗体瞻仰做了准备。

The family met with the mortician to discuss funeral arrangements. 家属与殡仪员见面讨论葬礼安排。 | 近义词: undertaker, funeral director | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

mortician: 词源来自mort- (death) + -ician (a person skilled in).  Ultimately from Latin *mors (death).
