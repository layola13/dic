dispassionately:/dɪsˈpæʃənɪtli/
adverb
冷静地；不带感情地；客观地
in a way that is free from emotion or bias
She dispassionately assessed the situation. 她冷静地评估了局势。
近义词: calmly, objectively, impartially, coolly
反义词: emotionally, passionately, subjectively, biasedly
词性: adverb

词源:
From dis- (not) + passionate + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *pati-, past participle stem of patī "to suffer, endure."
