nonstainers:/nɒnˈsteɪnərz/
noun
不染色剂; 不沾污的物品/材料
Substances or materials that resist staining or discoloration.
These new nonstainers are perfect for messy eaters. 这些新型不染色剂非常适合吃东西容易弄脏的人。
non-staining materials, stain-resistant materials
stainable materials, staining materials
n.
