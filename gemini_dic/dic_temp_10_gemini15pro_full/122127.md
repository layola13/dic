mistranslate:/ˌmɪs.trænsˈleɪt/ | v. | 误译，翻译错误 | To translate incorrectly; to give a wrong or inaccurate translation of. |  He mistranslated the sentence, leading to a misunderstanding. 他把句子翻译错了，导致了误解。

The document was mistranslated from Spanish to English. 这份文件从西班牙语翻译成英语时出现了错误。| 近义词：misinterpret, misconstrue | 反义词：translate correctly, interpret accurately | 词性：动词
