dryness:/ˈdraɪnɪs/ | n. | 干燥，干旱 |The dryness of the air made my skin crack. 空气的干燥使我的皮肤皲裂。|  aridity, dehydration | humidity, wetness | noun
Etymology: From Middle English *drinesse*, equivalent to dry +‎ -ness.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *drauziz (“dry”).|
The dryness of the desert made it difficult to survive. 沙漠的干燥使得生存变得困难。|
The doctor prescribed lotion for the dryness of her hands. 医生为她手部的干燥开了些乳液。
