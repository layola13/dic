directors:/dəˈrɛktərz/ /daɪˈrɛktərz/
n.
董事；导演；负责人；管理者；理事
A body of persons who direct or control some enterprise or organization.
The directors of the company voted to expand into new markets. 公司董事投票决定开拓新市场。

近义词:managers, administrators, executives, supervisors, board
反义词:subordinates, employees

词性:名词(复数)
