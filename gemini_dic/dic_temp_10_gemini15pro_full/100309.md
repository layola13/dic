intersituated:/ˌɪntərˈsɪtʃueɪtɪd/ | adj. | 相互坐落的，互相置于的 | Placed among or between other things; reciprocally situated. | The two villages are intersituated on the plain. 这两个村庄互相坐落在平原上。| The houses were intersituated among the trees. 这些房子坐落在树林之间。| 近义词：intermingled, interspersed, interwoven | 反义词：separated, isolated | 词性: adjective
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin inter- (between) + situatus (situated), past participle of situare (to place).
