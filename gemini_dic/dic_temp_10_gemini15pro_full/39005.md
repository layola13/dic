confirmors:/kənˈfɜːrmərz/
n.
确认者；证实者
People who confirm something; those who provide confirmation.
The confirmors of the transaction will receive a notification. 交易的确认者将收到通知。
近义词: validators, verifiers, ratifiers
反义词: deniers, objectors
