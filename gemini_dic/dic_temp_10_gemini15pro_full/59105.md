dyings:/ˈdaɪɪŋz/
noun
垂死，临终；临终遗言；绝迹
the act of dying; things said or done just before death; things that are disappearing

The dyings of the old ways are inevitable with modernization.  旧的传统随着现代化不可避免地走向消亡。
