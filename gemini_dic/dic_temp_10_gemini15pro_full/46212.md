cyst:/sɪst/| n.|囊肿，胞囊|A cyst is a closed sac, having a distinct membrane and division compared to the nearby tissue. It may contain air, fluids, or semi-solid material.|The doctor discovered a cyst on her ovary. 医生在她的卵巢上发现了一个囊肿。|近义词:sac, vesicle, lump|反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps healthy tissue)|词性:noun
