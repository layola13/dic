ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *architectures (plural), from Latin architectūra, from architectus (“architect”), from Ancient Greek ἀρχιτέκτων (arkhitéktōn, “master builder, director of works”), from ἄρχι- (arkhi-, “chief, master”) + τέκτων (téktōn, “builder, carpenter”).
