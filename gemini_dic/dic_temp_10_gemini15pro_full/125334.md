multilateralist: /ˌmʌltiˈlætərəlɪst/ | n. | 多边主义者 | A person who supports multilateralism, the principle of cooperation between multiple countries. |  The new ambassador is a staunch multilateralist, believing in international cooperation to solve global issues.  这位新大使是一位坚定的多边主义者，相信通过国际合作来解决全球问题。 | synonyms: internationalist, globalist | antonyms: unilateralist, isolationist | noun

multilateralist: /ˌmʌltiˈlætərəlɪst/ | adj. | 多边的 | Relating to or involving more than two nations or parties. | The multilateralist approach to trade negotiations involved representatives from dozens of countries.  多边贸易谈判方式涉及数十个国家的代表。| synonyms: multinational, polycentric | antonyms: unilateral, bilateral | adjective
