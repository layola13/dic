antistudents:/ˌæntiˈstjuːdənts/ | n. | 反学生者；反对学生的人 |  People who are opposed to students or student activism, often expressing hostility towards student ideals, protests, or the student body as a whole.  | The antistudents protested against the student union's decision. 反学生者抗议学生会的决定。 |  The antistudents disrupted the student protest with loudspeakers and counter-arguments. 反学生者用扩音器和反驳论点扰乱了学生抗议活动。 |  opponents, adversaries | supporters, allies | noun
