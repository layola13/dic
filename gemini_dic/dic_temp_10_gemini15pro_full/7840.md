antarchism: /ˈæntɑːrkɪzəm/ | n. | 反等级主义 | Opposition to hierarchy, particularly in the context of anarchist thought. It emphasizes horizontal organization and self-management. |  Example: Antarchism promotes decentralized decision-making processes. 反等级主义提倡分散的决策过程。

Example:  Many collectives operate on principles of antarchism. 许多集体按照反等级主义原则运作。 | 近义词: anarchism, anti-authoritarianism | 反义词: authoritarianism, hierarchy | 词性: noun
