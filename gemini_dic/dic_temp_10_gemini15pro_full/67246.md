expunction:/ɪkˈspʌŋkʃən/ |n.|删除；抹消；消除|The expunction of his criminal record allowed him to apply for the job. 他的犯罪记录被删除，使他能够申请这份工作。|例句：The judge ordered the expunction of the evidence from the court records. 法官下令从法庭记录中删除证据。

例句：She requested the expunction of her juvenile record. 她要求删除她的少年犯罪记录。|近义词：deletion, removal, erasure, obliteration|反义词：inclusion, addition, insertion|词性:名词

Etymology->
From Late Latin *expunctio(n-), from Latin expungere (“to erase, blot out, expunge”), from ex- (“out”) + pungere (“to prick, sting”).
