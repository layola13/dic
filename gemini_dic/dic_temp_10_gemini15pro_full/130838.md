nonapportionable:/ˌnɒnəˈpɔːrʃənəbl/
adjective
不可分配的
Not capable of being apportioned; indivisible.  Something that cannot be divided or distributed proportionally.
The cost of the new security system is nonapportionable between the different departments.  这个新安全系统的成本无法在不同部门之间分配。
The judge ruled that the damages were nonapportionable. 法官裁定损害赔偿金不可分割。
Synonyms: indivisible, unallocable, nondistributable
Antonyms: apportionable, divisible, allocable
词性: adjective
