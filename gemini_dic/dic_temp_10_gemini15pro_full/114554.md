malas:/ˈmɑːləs/ | adj. | 懒惰的，不情愿的 | Unwilling to do something because it involves hard work or effort; lazy. | He was too malas to walk to the store. 他懒得走去商店。|  She felt malas about doing her homework. 她对做作业感到不情愿。 | lazy, indolent, slothful, sluggish | energetic, industrious, diligent | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Malay *malas*. Cognate with Tagalog *matamád*, Javanese and Sundanese *males*.
