acquit:/əˈkwɪt/| vt.|宣判无罪；表现；履行；清偿|To free someone from a criminal charge by a verdict of not guilty.  To conduct oneself or perform in a specified way.|The jury acquitted the defendant of all charges. 陪审团宣判被告所有罪名不成立。

He acquitted himself well in his first job. 他在他的第一份工作中表现出色。|近义词：absolve, exonerate, clear, vindicate|反义词: convict, condemn|词性:动词
