grams: /ɡræmz/ | n. | 克 | A metric unit of mass equal to one thousandth of a kilogram. |  The recipe calls for 200 grams of flour.  配方需要200克面粉。 | synonyms: milligrams(for smaller units), kilograms(for larger units) | antonyms:  kilograms (in a relative sense, as a larger unit) | noun

Etymology: From French *gramme*, from Late Latin *gramma*, from Greek *grámma*  meaning "small weight," a small unit of weight.
