outreaches:/ˈaʊtriːtʃɪz/
n.
外展服务；推广活动；联络
an effort to bring services or information to people, especially those who would not otherwise have access to them
The church has several outreaches to help the homeless. 教会开展了几项外展服务来帮助无家可归的人。
outreach programs, community outreach,  social outreach
limitations, restrictions
noun
