nonadmissions:/ˌnɒnədˈmɪʃənz/
noun
不录取；不承认；不接纳
The act of not admitting someone or something, or the state of not being admitted.
The university's nonadmission policy for students with low grades was controversial.  这所大学针对低分学生的拒收政策备受争议。
The nonadmission of guilt does not imply innocence. 不承认罪行并不意味着无罪。
近义词: rejection, refusal, exclusion
反义词: admission, acceptance, inclusion
词性: noun
