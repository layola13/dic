capitally:/ˈkæpɪtəli/| adv.|极好地；出色地；要命地；死刑地|In a capital manner; excellently; first-rate.  It also means "by death penalty."|The play was capitally performed. 这场戏演得真好。|近义词：excellently, superbly, wonderfully, brilliantly, splendidly|反义词：poorly, badly, terribly, awfully|词性:副词
Etymology: From Late Latin *capitaliter, from Latin capitalis (“of the head, chief, principal”), from caput (“head”).  The meaning related to death penalty comes from the sense of "affecting the head/life".
