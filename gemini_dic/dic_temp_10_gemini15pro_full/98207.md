insectivore:/ɪnˈsektɪvɔːr/ | n. |食虫动物；食虫植物|An animal or plant that feeds on insects. |例句：Bats are nocturnal insectivores.  蝙蝠是夜行性食虫动物。

例句：The Venus flytrap is a well-known insectivore.  捕蝇草是一种著名的食虫植物。|近义词：carnivore (general meat-eater) |反义词: herbivore |词性:noun

insectivorous:/ˌɪnsekˈtɪvərəs/ | adj. |食虫的|Feeding on insects. |例句： Insectivorous plants trap insects for nutrients. 食虫植物捕获昆虫以获取营养。

例句：Shrews are small, insectivorous mammals.  鼩鼱是小型食虫哺乳动物。|近义词：carnivorous (general meat-eater) |反义词: herbivorous |词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *īnsectīvorus*, from īnsectum (“insect”) + -vorus (“-eating”), from vorāre (“to devour”).
