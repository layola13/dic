incorporeally:/ˌɪnkɔːrˈpɔːriəli/
adverb
无实体地；非物质地；精神上地
Without material body, form, or substance.

Existing in spirit only; not bodily.


例句：
The ghost appeared incorporeally, a shimmering mist in the moonlight.
幽灵无实体地出现了，在月光下像一团闪闪发光的薄雾。

She felt his presence incorporeally, a comforting warmth in the cold room.
她非物质地感觉到了他的存在，在寒冷的房间里感受到了一种令人欣慰的温暖。


近义词：spiritually, intangibly, immaterially
反义词：physically, bodily, materially
词性：adverb
