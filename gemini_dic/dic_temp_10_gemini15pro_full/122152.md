mistress:/ˈmɪstrɪs/| n.| 女主人，情妇，女教师，(尤指旧时的)女能手，女艺术家|A woman who is in a position of authority or control, especially in a household or over servants.  A woman having an extramarital sexual relationship with a married man.| 例句：She is the mistress of the house. 她是这家的女主人。

例句：He left his wife for his mistress. 他为了他的情妇离开了妻子。|近义词：lover, paramour, concubine,  teacher, instructor (for the meaning of "female teacher"), expert, proficient (for the meaning of "female expert")|反义词：servant, maid, employee (for the meaning of "mistress of the house")|词性: 名词
