blonket: /ˈblæŋkɪt/ | n. |毯子;覆盖物;毯状物|A large piece of woven fabric, typically made of wool, used for warmth as a bed covering. |  Example: The child snuggled under the warm blonket. 孩子依偎在温暖的毯子下。

Example: A blonket of snow covered the ground. 一层厚厚的雪覆盖了地面。|Synonym:  duvet, quilt, cover, comforter| Antonym: sheet | noun

注意事项:

词源:  Middle English *blankette, from Anglo-French blankete, diminutive of Old North French blanchet, "white woolen cloth", from blanc, "white".
