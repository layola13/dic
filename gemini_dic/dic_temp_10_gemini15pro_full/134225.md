nonindustries:/nɒnˈɪndəstriːz/ |n. |非工业部门，非产业部门 |Industries are typically categorized as primary (extraction of raw materials), secondary (manufacturing), and tertiary (services). Nonindustries encompass activities and sectors that don't fall neatly into these categories, often involving non-tangible goods or services, or activities focused on research, development, or social impact.  | Many economists argue that the growth of nonindustries is crucial for a balanced economy. 许多经济学家认为非工业部门的增长对于平衡的经济至关重要。| Near synonyms: non-profit sector, public sector, quaternary sector | Antonyms: industries, manufacturing sector | noun
