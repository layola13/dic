curtailer:/kərˈteɪlər/
noun
限制者，缩减者
Someone or something that curtails something.
The government was a curtailer of civil liberties during the war.  政府在战争期间限制了公民自由。
Congress acted as a curtailer of presidential power. 国会限制了总统的权力。
Synonyms: restrictor, reducer, limiter
Antonyms: expander, increaser, amplifier
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "curtail," which comes from the Old French "curtailler" (to shorten, dock), from "curt" (short) + "-ailler" (frequentative suffix).
