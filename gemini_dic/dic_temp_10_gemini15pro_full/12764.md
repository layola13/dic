assignational:/əˌsaɪɡˈneɪʃənəl/
adjective
幽会的；私会的；为幽会而安排的
Relating to or arranged for a secret meeting, especially a romantic one.
The hotel had a reputation for assignational encounters. 这家酒店以幽会而闻名。
The assignational nature of their meeting was obvious. 他们会面的私密性质显而易见。
近义词：clandestine, secret, private
反义词：public, open
词性：adjective
词源：From "assignation" (n.) + "-al" (suffix forming adjectives). "Assignation" comes from French "assignation," ultimately from Latin "assignare" (to assign, allot).
