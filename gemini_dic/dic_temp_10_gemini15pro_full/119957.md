millenarianism:/ˌmɪləˈnɛəriənɪzəm/| n. |千禧年主义；千年至福说|The belief that a time of great peace, prosperity, and happiness will occur in the future, especially one linked to the return of Christ or the establishment of God's kingdom on earth.|例句：The cult's millenarianism predicted the end of the world in 2012.  这个邪教的千禧年主义预言2012年是世界末日。

例句：Millenarianism often emerges during periods of social upheaval. 千禧年主义经常出现在社会动荡时期。|近义词：chiliasm, apocalypticism|反义词: secularism|词性:noun
