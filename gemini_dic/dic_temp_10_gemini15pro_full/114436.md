make-work:/ˈmeɪkwɜːrk/
noun
虚假工作；为了使人有事可做而安排的工作
Work, especially of a trivial or unnecessary nature, created to keep someone occupied.
The government schemes were dismissed as make-work by the opposition.  政府的计划被反对派斥为虚假工作。
近义词:busywork,  boondoggle
反义词:meaningful work,  productive work
n.

make-work:/ˈmeɪkwɜːrk/
adjective
(仅用于名词前)虚假的；徒劳无功的
Describing work, especially of a trivial or unnecessary nature, created to keep someone occupied.
He felt the tasks assigned were merely make-work projects. 他觉得分配的任务只是些徒劳无功的项目。
近义词:unnecessary, pointless
反义词:essential,  necessary
adj.
