overrealistic:/ˌoʊvərˌriːəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
过于逼真的；过分现实的
representing something in a way that is more extreme than it is in reality
The special effects in the movie were overrealistic and quite disturbing. 这部电影的特效过于逼真，令人十分不安。

The painting was so overrealistic that it looked like a photograph. 这幅画过于逼真，看起来像一张照片。

Near Synonyms: hyperrealistic, overly realistic, excessively realistic
Antonyms: unrealistic, idealized, romanticized
词性: 形容词
