helmsman: /ˈhɛlmzmən/ (US), /ˈhɛlmzmən/ (UK)| n.|舵手，驾驶员|A person who steers a ship or boat.|The helmsman steered the ship through the treacherous waters. 舵手驾驶船只穿越了危险的水域。|Example:  The experienced helmsman skillfully navigated the ship through the storm.  经验丰富的舵手熟练地驾驶船只穿过风暴。

Example:  A steady hand on the helm is essential for a good helmsman.  对于一名优秀的舵手来说，稳住舵柄至关重要。|Synonym:  steersman, pilot, navigator|Antonym: passenger
