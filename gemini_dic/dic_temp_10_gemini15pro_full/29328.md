castigators:/ˈkæstɪɡeɪtərz/
noun
惩罚者；严厉批评者；苛责者
People who criticize or punish someone severely.
The castigators of the new policy were very vocal. 这些新政策的批评者们表达了强烈的不满。
Synonyms: critics, censurers, reprovers, rebukers, reprimanders
Antonyms: praisers, commenders, flatterers
词性: 名词(n.)
