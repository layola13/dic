gnus:/nuːz/ /njuːz/| n. | 角马; 牛羚 | A large dark African antelope with a long head, a beard, and a mane, and short curved horns. |  Example 1: A herd of gnus migrated across the savanna. 一群角马迁徙穿过大草原。

Example 2: The lion stalked the gnus, waiting for the opportune moment to attack. 狮子跟踪着角马群，等待合适的时机发动攻击。 | 近义词:wildebeest, antelope | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From Afrikaans *gnoe*, from Khoekhoe *ǂnu*.
