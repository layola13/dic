chiten:/ˈtʃaɪtən/| n.|智人；智力；精神；|A spiritual being; intellect; mind.|例句：Develop your chiten through meditation. 通过冥想发展你的精神力。

例句：His chiten was sharp and focused. 他的思维敏捷而专注。|近义词：intellect, mind, spirit, soul|反义词：ignorance, stupidity|词性：名词

词源: From Japanese 智 (chi, “wisdom, intellect”) + 天 (ten, “heaven, sky, nature”).  Therefore, it literally translates to "wisdom of heaven" or "heavenly intellect".
