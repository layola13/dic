fabella:/fəˈbɛlə/| n.| (figurative) small, unimportant thing|Something insignificant or trivial.|He dismissed their concerns as mere fabellas. |他认为他们的担忧只不过是些鸡毛蒜皮的小事。|Synonym: trifle, triviality|Antonym: significant matter|noun
