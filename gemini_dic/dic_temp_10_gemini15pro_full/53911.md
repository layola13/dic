discourageable:/dɪˈskʌrɪdʒəbl/
adj.
容易灰心的，易于沮丧的
Easily discouraged or disheartened; susceptible to losing confidence or enthusiasm.
The team was discourageable after their third consecutive loss.  这个队在连续三次失利后很容易灰心。
He's a discourageable young man; he gives up too easily. 他是一个容易灰心的年轻人；他太容易放弃了。
synonyms: easily disheartened, easily discouraged, demoralizable, daunted
antonyms: resolute, determined, persistent, tenacious
词性: 形容词
