nonhabitableness:/ˌnɑːnhæbɪtəblnəs/
noun
不适宜居住；不宜居住性
The nonhabitableness of the planet made it impossible for humans to colonize. 这个星球的不适宜居住性使得人类无法殖民。
non-habitability, uninhabitability
habitableness, suitability for living
