papalist: /ˈpāpəlɪst/ | n. | 教皇拥护者；教皇至上论者 | A supporter of the pope or papal authority, especially one who believes in the supremacy of the pope. |  He was a staunch papalist and opposed any reforms that would diminish the power of the pope. 他是一位坚定的教皇拥护者，反对任何削弱教皇权力的改革。|  Synonym: ultramontanist | Antonym: antipapist | noun
