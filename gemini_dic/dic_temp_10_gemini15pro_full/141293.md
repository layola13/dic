open-endedness:/ˌoʊpənˈɛndɪdnəs/ | n. |开放性；非限定性|The open-endedness of the question allowed for a variety of responses. 这个问题的开放性允许各种各样的回答。|The survey questions were designed with open-endedness to encourage detailed feedback.  调查问卷的问题设计具有开放性，以鼓励详细的反馈。| 近义词: flexibility, ambiguity, indeterminacy | 反义词: restrictiveness, definiteness, closed-endedness | 词性: noun

open-ended:/ˌoʊpənˈɛndɪd/ | adj. |开放式的；无固定结局的|The students were given an open-ended assignment to encourage creativity.  学生们被布置了一项开放式的作业以鼓励创造力。|This is an open-ended question, so there is no single correct answer. 这是一个开放式的问题，所以没有唯一的正确答案。| 近义词: unrestricted, flexible, exploratory | 反义词: restricted, closed, defined | 词性: adjective
