mossinesses:/ˈmɑːsɪnəsɪz/ /ˈmɒsɪnəsɪz/
n.
苔藓覆盖的状态; 生苔的状态
the state or quality of being covered with moss; the presence of moss
The old stone walls were covered in mossinesses, giving the garden a sense of ancient peace.  古老的石墙上覆盖着苔藓，给花园增添了一种古老的宁静感。
The mossinesses on the roof indicated that it hadn't been cleaned in years.  屋顶上的苔藓表明它已经多年没有清理过了。
近义词: moss, moss growth
反义词: bareness, smoothness
词性: 名词 (Plural Noun)

词源:
From mossy + -ness + -es (plural suffix)
Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *mosa-
