cypsela:/sɪpˈsiːlə/| n. |瘦果，菊科植物的果实|A dry, indehiscent, one-seeded fruit formed from a double ovary, of which only one develops a seed, as in the dandelion.|The dandelion's cypsela is dispersed by the wind. 蒲公英的瘦果靠风传播。|achene, seed|capsule, berry|noun
