fixations:/fɪkˈseɪʃənz/
n.
固定；注视；固恋；偏执
an obsessive interest in or feeling about someone or something.
Her fixation on cleanliness borders on obsessive-compulsive disorder. 她对清洁的执着近乎强迫症。

His fixation on getting into Harvard consumed him. 他一心想进哈佛，这件事让他寝食难安。


obsession, preoccupation, complex
neutrality, indifference
