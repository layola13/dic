intercreating:/ˌɪntərkriˈeɪtɪŋ/
verb
互相创造；共同创造；交互创造
The process by which two or more entities, systems, or ideas influence and shape each other, leading to the emergence of something new and shared.
Artists and scientists were intercreating, blurring the lines between disciplines. 艺术家和科学家们互相创造，模糊了学科之间的界限。
Collaborating musicians often find themselves intercreating new melodies.  合作的音乐家经常发现他们自己在共同创造新的旋律。
Synonyms: co-creating, collaborating, synergizing, interacting, interweaving
Antonyms: isolating, separating, dividing, fragmenting
词性: 动词
