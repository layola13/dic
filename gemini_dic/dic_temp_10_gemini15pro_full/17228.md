bawdy: /ˈbɔːdi/ (US) /ˈbɔːdi/ (UK)| adj.|下流的，猥亵的，淫秽的|Dealing with or referring to sex in a rude and humorous way.|The comedian told a bawdy joke that made the audience roar with laughter.  喜剧演员讲了一个下流的笑话，让观众哄堂大笑。|例句：The sailors sang bawdy songs in the tavern. 水手们在酒馆里唱着下流的歌。|近义词：lewd, vulgar, obscene, indecent, ribald|反义词：decent, proper, chaste, pure|词性: adjective
词源: From Middle English *baudy, of uncertain origin. Possibly from Old French *baude "bold, happy," or from Old French *bauder "to rejoice," or a blend of these.
