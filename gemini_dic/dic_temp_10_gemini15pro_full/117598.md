megameter:/ˈmɛɡəˌmiːtər/ | n. |百万米；兆米|A unit of length equal to one million meters. |例句：A megameter is a very large unit of measurement, rarely used in everyday life. 百万米是一个非常大的测量单位，在日常生活中很少使用。

例句：The distance between these two cities is roughly one megameter. 这两个城市之间的距离大约是一百万米。|近义词：million meters|反义词:micrometer, nanometer|名词
