injudicious:/ˌɪndʒuˈdɪʃəs/| adj.|不明智的，不审慎的|Not showing good judgment; unwise.|His injudicious remarks caused a lot of trouble. 他不明智的言论引起了很多麻烦。|例句：Investing all your money in one stock is injudicious. 把你所有的钱都投资在一支股票上是不明智的。

例句：Her injudicious choice of words offended many people. 她不明智的措辞冒犯了很多人。|近义词：unwise, imprudent, foolish, ill-advised, ill-considered|反义词：wise, prudent, judicious, sensible, sagacious|词性:形容词
