adhesiveness:/ədˈhiːsɪvnəs/| n.|粘性，粘附性|The adhesiveness of the glue made it perfect for the project.  这种胶水的粘性使它非常适合这个项目。|The adhesiveness of the tape ensured the package stayed sealed.  胶带的粘性确保了包裹保持密封。|近义词：stickiness, tackiness, glutinousness|反义词：slipperiness, smoothness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From adhesive + -ness.  来自形容词 adhesive 加名词后缀 -ness。
Adhesive comes from Late Latin *adhaesīvus, from Latin adhaerēre "to stick to," from ad- "to" + haerēre "to stick."  Adhesive 源自晚期拉丁语 *adhaesīvus，来自拉丁语 adhaerēre “粘附”，来自 ad- “到” + haerēre “粘住”。
