frenches:/ˈfrɛntʃɪz/
noun
沟渠; 壕沟;  (尤指战壕)
trenches usually refers to deep ditches dug in the ground, especially as a defense in warfare.

The soldiers spent weeks in the muddy trenches.
士兵们在泥泞的战壕里待了几个星期。


The trenches were cold, wet, and rat-infested.
壕沟里阴冷潮湿，到处是老鼠。

ditch, furrow, trough
hill, mound

verb
(用沟环绕)
to surround or fortify with a trench or trenches.

They trenched the camp to protect it from attack.
他们挖沟渠环绕营地以防袭击。

The area was trenched and fortified.
这个区域被挖了壕沟并加固了。

surround, enclose, fortify
expose, uncover


adjective (罕见)
与战壕有关的；战壕里的
related to trenches.

This is a book about the frenches of World War I.
这是一本关于第一次世界大战战壕的书。

战壕里的情况骇人听闻。

peaceful, civilian
