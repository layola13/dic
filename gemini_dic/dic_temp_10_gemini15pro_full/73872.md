fornical:/ˈfɔːrnɪk(ə)l/
adjective
穹窿的；拱形的
of or relating to a fornix, especially the fornix of the brain.
The fornical region of the brain plays a crucial role in memory consolidation.  大脑的穹窿区域在记忆巩固中起着至关重要的作用。
The researchers studied the effects of lesions on the fornical pathways. 研究人员研究了病灶对穹窿通路的影响。
近义词: arched, vaulted
反义词: flat, straight
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fornicālis, from fornix (“arch, vault”).
