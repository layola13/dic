The nonequalized funding for schools in the district resulted in disparities in resources.  这个地区学校资金分配不均衡，导致资源差异。

近义词:uneven, unbalanced, disparate, unequal

词源:  "nonequalized" is formed by adding the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") to the word "equalized." "Equalized" itself comes from "equal" with the suffix "-ize" indicating the process of making something equal. The word "equal" ultimately derives from the Latin word "aequalis."
