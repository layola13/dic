mutant:/ˈmjuːtənt/ (US) /ˈmjuːtənt/ (UK)| n. adj.| 突变体，突变的 |An organism, gene, or chromosome that has undergone mutation.  A mutant form or variant, as of a gene. |
The scientists studied the mutant strain of the virus. 科学家们研究了这种病毒的突变株。

The mutant gene caused the plant to grow abnormally large.  这个突变基因导致植物异常生长。|
近义词：variation, variant, mutation, sport |
反义词：wild-type, normal |
词性: noun, adjective

Etymology: From Latin *mūtāns, *mūtant- (present participle of mūtāre ‘to change’), from which also comes English mutable.
