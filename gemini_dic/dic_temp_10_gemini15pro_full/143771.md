outmeasuring:/ˌaʊtˈmɛʒərɪŋ/
verb
超越，胜过，在测量上超过
Surpassing in measurement; exceeding in size, extent, or degree.
The new skyscraper outmeasures all the others in the city. 这座新的摩天大楼比城市里所有其他建筑都高。
His generosity outmeasured hers. 他的慷慨胜过她。
近义词: exceeding, surpassing, outstripping, outdoing
反义词: falling short of, being smaller than
词性: verb
