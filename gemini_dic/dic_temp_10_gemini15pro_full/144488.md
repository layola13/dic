outthunder:/ˌaʊtˈθʌndər/
verb
胜过…的雷声；以更大的声音压倒
To surpass in loudness of thunder; to drown out with a louder sound.
The rock band outthundered the noisy crowd.  这支摇滚乐队的音量盖过了嘈杂的人群。
The speaker outthundered the protesters. 演讲者的声音盖过了抗议者。
synonym:out-roar, drown out
antonym: whisper, murmur
词性:动词
