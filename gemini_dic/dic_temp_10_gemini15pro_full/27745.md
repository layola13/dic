cankered:/ˈkæŋkərd/| adj.|腐烂的，溃疡的，恶毒的|Affected by canker; ulcerous.  Figuratively, corrupted, evil, or malevolent. |The cankered branches of the old tree needed to be pruned.  这棵老树腐烂的树枝需要修剪。|近义词：ulcerated, festering, malignant, corrupt, venomous|反义词:healthy, wholesome, benign|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cankered*, from *canker* + *-ed*.  *Canker* comes from Old North French *cancre*, ultimately from Latin *cancer* ("crab, cancer"), due to the perceived resemblance of a spreading ulcer to a crab.
