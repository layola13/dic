osphradium:/ɒˈsfreɪdiəm/
n.
嗅检器，水质检测器官
A chemoreceptive sense organ in aquatic mollusks, often located in the mantle cavity, believed to be used to test the quality of the water passing over the gills.
The osphradium of the snail helps it detect changes in water chemistry.  蜗牛的嗅检器帮助它检测水中化学成分的变化。

The osphradium is thought to detect pollutants in the water. 嗅检器被认为可以检测水中的污染物。

近义词: chemoreceptor (化学感受器)
反义词:  (No direct antonym, as it's a specific organ)
词性: noun (名词)
