bos'ns: /ˈboʊsənz/ | n. | 水手长；舵手 | A petty officer on a warship or merchant ship who is in charge of the anchors, ropes, rigging, and deck crew.  |  The bos'ns piped the crew to quarters. 水手长吹哨召集船员到岗位。 | 近义词: boatswain | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Middle English *bosun, *botsweyn, from Old English *bātswegen (“boatman, boatswain”), from bāt (“boat”) + swegen (“swain, servant”).
