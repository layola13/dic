coexist:/ˌkoʊɪɡˈzɪst/| v. |共存，共处|To exist at the same time or in the same place.|例句：Different cultures can coexist peacefully. 不同的文化可以和平共处。|例句：Predators and prey often coexist in a delicate balance. 捕食者和猎物通常在微妙的平衡中共存。|近义词：cohabit, live together|反义词：clash, conflict|词性:动词

coexistence:/ˌkoʊɪɡˈzɪstəns/| n. |共存，共处|The state of existing at the same time or in the same place.|例句：Peaceful coexistence is essential for global stability. 和平共存对全球稳定至关重要。|例句：The coexistence of different species in an ecosystem is a complex phenomenon. 生态系统中不同物种的共存是一个复杂的现象。|近义词：harmony, compatibility|反义词：conflict, discord|词性:名词

词源: co- (together) + exist (to have being)
