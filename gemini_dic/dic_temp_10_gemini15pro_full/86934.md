hemp:/hɛmp/| n.|大麻，麻类纤维|A tall plant grown for its tough fibrous stem, used to make rope and cloth.|The rope was made of hemp. 这根绳子是用大麻纤维做的。|Synonyms: cannabis, marijuana (for the drug-producing variety), fiber|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the plant itself; for the fiber, perhaps synthetics like nylon or polyester)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hænep (attested only in compounds), from Proto-Germanic *hanapiz, from Proto-Indo-European *ḱanánabis (whence also Latin cannabis, Greek κάνναβις (kánnabis)).
