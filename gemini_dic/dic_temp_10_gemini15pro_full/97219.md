inflater:/ɪnˈfleɪtər/ | n. | 充气机，膨胀器 | A device used to inflate something, such as a tire, balloon, or air mattress. | Example:  I used an inflater to pump up the tires on my bike. 我用充气机给自行车轮胎打气。

Example: The air mattress comes with a built-in inflater.  这个充气床垫配有内置充气机。 | Synonyms: pump, air pump, blower | Antonyms: deflator | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "inflate," which comes from the Latin word "inflare," meaning "to blow into."  "In-" means "in" and "flare" means "to blow."
