overdemand:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈmænd/ | n.| 过度需求 | A situation where the demand for something exceeds the available supply. | The overdemand for housing in the city has driven up prices. 城市住房的过度需求推高了价格。| Example:  Tickets for the concert were in overdemand, selling out in minutes.  演唱会的门票供不应求，几分钟内就售罄了。| synonyms: excess demand, oversupply (opposite of the *result* of overdemand), shortage (of supply) | antonyms: undersupply, oversupply (opposite of the *result* of overdemand), surplus | noun
