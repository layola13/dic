disconnect:/ˌdɪskəˈnekt/ | v. n. |断开，分离；断开连接|To break the connection between something.|She disconnected the phone. 她挂断了电话.|近义词：detach, separate, uncouple, sever|反义词: connect, join, link|verb

disconnect:/ˌdɪskəˈnekt/ | n. | 断开；脱节|A lack of connection or a break in connection.|There is a disconnect between the government and the people. 政府与人民之间存在脱节。|近义词：separation, gap, rift|反义词: connection, link|noun
