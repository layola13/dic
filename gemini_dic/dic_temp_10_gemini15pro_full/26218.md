butterfish:/ˈbʌtərˌfɪʃ/| n.|黄油鱼，鲳科鱼类|A fish with smooth, buttery flesh. Several unrelated species are called butterfish.  This name can refer to fish in the families Stromateidae, Pholidae, and Centriscidae.|例句：The butterfish was pan-fried to perfection. 黄油鱼被煎至完美。

例句：Butterfish is a popular dish in many coastal regions. 黄油鱼在许多沿海地区是一道受欢迎的菜肴。|近义词：pompano, harvestfish|反义词: None (No direct opposite for a type of fish)|词性:noun
