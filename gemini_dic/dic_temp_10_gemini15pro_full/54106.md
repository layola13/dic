disembowelment:/dɪsɪmˈbaʊəlment/
noun
取出内脏; 开膛破肚
The act of removing the internal organs of a person or animal, usually violently.
The police report described the disembowelment of the victim in gruesome detail. 警方报告以可怕的细节描述了受害者被掏空内脏的情况。

The ritual disembowelment of the sacrificial animal was carried out by the high priest.  祭祀动物的仪式性取出内脏是由大祭司执行的。
evisceration, gutting
