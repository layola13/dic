fentanyl:/ˈfɛntənɪl/ | n. |芬太尼|A synthetic opioid drug that is 50 to 100 times more potent than morphine. It is used medically to treat severe pain, but it is also abused recreationally. |The doctor prescribed fentanyl for the patient's post-surgical pain. 医生给病人开了芬太尼来治疗术后疼痛。|近义词: carfentanil|反义词: naloxone|词性:noun
