magistratical:/ˌmædʒɪˈstreɪtɪkəl/ | adj. | 行政长官的；地方官员的；有地方官员权力的 | Relating to a magistrate or the office of a magistrate; having the authority of a magistrate. |  The magistrate made a magistratical decision. 行政长官做出了一个地方官员的决定。 | The governor exercised his magistratical powers to pardon the prisoner. 州长行使其地方官员的权力赦免了囚犯。 | 近义词: judicial, official, authoritative | 反义词: unofficial, unauthorized | 词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *magistraticālis, from Latin magistrāticus (“of or belonging to a master or magistrate”), from magistrātus (“magistracy, public office, magistrate”), from magister (“master, chief, director, administrator, teacher”).
