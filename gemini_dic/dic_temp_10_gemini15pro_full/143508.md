outgnawn:/ˌaʊtˈnɔːn/
verb
咬穿；啃坏；超过 (比喻)
To gnaw out or away; to surpass or erode by gnawing (literal or figurative).
The mouse had outgnawn a hole in the wall.  老鼠在墙上啃出了一个洞。
His ambition had outgnawn his conscience. 他的野心已经腐蚀了他的良心。
Synonym: erode, corrode, surpass, outstrip
Antonym: build, construct, preserve
词性: 动词
