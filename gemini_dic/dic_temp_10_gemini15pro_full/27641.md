candent:/ˈkændənt/| adj.|白热的；炽热的；热烈的；激情洋溢的|Glowing with or as with heat; intensely hot.  Figuratively, passionate or enthusiastic.|例句：The candent iron glowed brightly in the forge.  炽热的铁在锻炉里闪着亮光。|例句：Her candent eyes betrayed her passionate feelings. 她炽热的眼神流露出了她热情奔放的感情。|近义词：glowing, incandescent, fiery, fervent, passionate|反义词：cold, dull, apathetic|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin "candēns, candent-" (present participle of "candēre" - to shine, glow white, be white-hot), related to "candeō" - I am white, gleam, glow.
