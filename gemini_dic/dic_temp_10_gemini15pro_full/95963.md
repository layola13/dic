inculpable:/ɪnˈkʌlpəbl/| adj.|无罪的；无辜的；无可指责的|Free from blame or guilt. Not deserving punishment.|例句：He was found to be inculpable in the accident. 他被发现在这起事故中是无辜的。

例句：Her actions were entirely inculpable. 她的行为完全无可指责。|近义词：blameless, innocent, guiltless, irreproachable, faultless|反义词：guilty, culpable, blameworthy|词性:形容词
