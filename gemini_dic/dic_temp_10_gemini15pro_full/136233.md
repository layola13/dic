nonremuneratively:/ˌnɒnrɪˈmjuːnərətɪvli/
adverb
无偿地，无报酬地
without payment or reward; for free.
He worked nonremuneratively for the charity. 他为慈善机构无偿工作。
Synonyms: gratuitously, voluntarily, freely, for free
Antonyms: remuneratively, profitably, for pay, for a fee
词性: 副词
