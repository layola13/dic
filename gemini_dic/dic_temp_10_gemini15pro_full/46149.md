cymric:/ˈkɪmrɪk/ | adj. n.|威尔士的；威尔士人；威尔士猫|Relating to Wales, or its people or language. Also, a breed of tailless or short-tailed domestic cat.|The cymric cat is known for its rounded rump.|近义词：Welsh|反义词:English|词性:形容词, 名词

Etymology:
From Welsh *Cymro* "fellow countryman," from *com-* "together with" + *bro-* "region, land."
