alternates:/ˈɔːltərneɪts/ (US) /ˈɒltəneɪts/ (UK)| v.  n. adj.| 交替，轮流；交替的事物；|

*Verb:* To occur in turn repeatedly.  To cause to occur or act in turn repeatedly.

*Noun:* A person acting in place of another; a substitute.  Something that alternates with something else.

*Adjective:* Occurring or succeeding by turns; every other; alternate leaves on a stem.


英文解析:  The word "alternates" can function as a verb, noun, or adjective. As a verb, it signifies something happening in a successive, back-and-forth manner.  As a noun, it refers to a substitute or something that takes turns with another. As an adjective, it describes things occurring in turns.


例句:

*Verb:* The traffic lights alternate between red and green.  交通信号灯在红色和绿色之间交替。

*Verb:*  He alternates between hope and despair. 他在希望和绝望之间徘徊。

*Noun:*  She served as an alternate on the committee. 她在委员会中担任替补委员。

*Noun:* There are several alternates to choose from. 有几种选择可以交替使用。

*Adjective:*  He visits on alternate days. 他每隔一天来访一次。

*Adjective:* The cake had alternate layers of chocolate and vanilla. 蛋糕有巧克力和香草交替的层。



近义词: rotate, interchange, switch, substitute, alternative (n./adj.)

反义词:  constant, continuous, permanent (for adj.); primary, principal (for n.)

词性: verb, noun, adjective
