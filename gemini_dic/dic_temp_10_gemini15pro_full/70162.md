fibbery:/ˈfɪbəri/
noun
谎言；虚构的故事；无稽之谈
The act of telling fibs; lies.
He was caught in a web of fibbery. 他被发现编造了一系列谎言。
synonyms: lies, falsehoods, fabrication, prevarication, untruths, storytelling
antonyms: truth, honesty, veracity
