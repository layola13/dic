heteronuclear:/ˌhɛtərəʊˈnjuːklɪər/ /ˌhɛt̬əroʊˈnuːkliər/
adjective
异核的
Containing different nuclei; specifically (of a molecule) containing atoms of different elements.
Heteronuclear diatomic molecules have a permanent dipole moment. 异核双原子分子具有永久偶极矩。

Heteronuclear NMR spectroscopy is a powerful tool for studying the structure and dynamics of molecules. 异核核磁共振波谱是研究分子结构和动力学的有力工具。

近义词:multinuclear (when referring to NMR)
反义词:homonuclear

词性:adjective
