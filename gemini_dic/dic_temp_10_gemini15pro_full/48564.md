The intense heat defatigated the marathon runners. 酷热使马拉松选手疲惫不堪。

| 近义词: tire, exhaust, weary, enervate | 反义词: refresh, invigorate, energize | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *defatigatus, past participle of defatigare "to tire out completely," from Latin de- "completely" + fatigatus, past participle of fatigare "to weary."
