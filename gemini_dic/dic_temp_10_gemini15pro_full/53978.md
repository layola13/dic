discreteness:/dɪˈskriːtnəs/| n.| 离散性，不连续性 |The state or quality of being discrete; separateness.|The discreteness of the data points allows for individual analysis. 数据点的离散性允许进行单独分析。|近义词：separateness, individuality, distinctness|反义词：continuity, connectedness|词性: 名词
discrete:/dɪˈskriːt/| adj.| 离散的，不连续的，分立的|Individually separate and distinct.|The process involves a series of discrete steps. 这个过程包括一系列独立的步骤。|近义词：separate, distinct, individual|反义词：continuous, connected|词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin discretus, past participle of discernere "to separate, distinguish," from dis- "apart" + cernere "to sift, separate, distinguish."
