fire-raiser:/ˈfaɪərˌreɪzər/ | n. |纵火犯；煽动者|A person who deliberately sets fire to property or incites others to violence or disorder.| The police arrested the fire-raiser responsible for the blaze. 警察逮捕了造成这场火灾的纵火犯。| synonym: arsonist, incendiary, agitator | antonym: firefighter, peacemaker | noun

fire-raiser:/ˈfaɪərˌreɪzər/ | n. |煽动者；教唆者|A person who stirs up trouble or incites others to rebel.| He was known as a political fire-raiser, always challenging the status quo.  他被称为政治煽动者，总是挑战现状。| synonym: instigator, provocateur, rabble-rouser | antonym: peacemaker, mediator, conciliator | noun
