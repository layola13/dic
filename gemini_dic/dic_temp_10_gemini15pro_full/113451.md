lynden:/ˈlɪndən/| n. |林登，椴树；（男子名）|A place name derived from Old English meaning "lime tree valley".  It refers to a tree of the genus Tilia, known for its fragrant flowers and heart-shaped leaves. Can also be used as a given name, primarily for males.|Lynden is a beautiful town in Washington state.  林登是华盛顿州的一个美丽的城镇。
