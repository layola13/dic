momma:/ˈmɑːmə/ (US), /ˈmʌmə/ (UK)| n.|妈妈|An informal or childish way of saying "mother." It is often used as a term of endearment.| Momma, can I have a cookie? 妈妈，我可以吃块饼干吗？|近义词：mom, mommy, mother, ma|反义词: (No direct antonym.  Could consider terms like "stepmother" in specific contexts, but not a general antonym)|noun

momma:/ˈmɑːmə/ (US), /ˈmʌmə/ (UK)| n.|妈妈|Used to address one's mother directly, especially in a familiar or affectionate way. | Momma, I love you. 妈妈，我爱你。|近义词：mom, mommy, mother, ma|反义词: (No direct antonym)|noun
