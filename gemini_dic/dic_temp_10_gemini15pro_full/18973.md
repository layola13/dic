bestiary:/ˈbɛstiəri/ /ˈbiːstiəri/ | n. |  动物寓言集，动物志，怪兽图鉴 | A medieval book describing various real and mythical animals, often with allegorical interpretations of their behavior. |  Example 1: The medieval bestiary depicted the unicorn as a symbol of purity.  中世纪的动物寓言集将独角兽描绘成纯洁的象征。

Example 2:  She was fascinated by the illustrations in the old bestiary. 她被那本古老动物寓言集中的插图深深吸引。 | 近义词:  encyclopedia of animals,  animal lore,  menagerie (in a figurative sense) | 反义词: None (No direct opposite exists) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *bestiaire* (modern French *bestiaire*), from Late Latin *bestiarium* "book about beasts," from Latin *bestia* "beast."
