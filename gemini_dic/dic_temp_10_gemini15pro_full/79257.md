glassiness:/ˈɡlæsɪnɪs/
n.
光滑；光泽；透明
smoothness and shininess like that of glass; the state of being glassy.
The glassiness of the lake's surface reflected the surrounding mountains.  湖面光滑如镜，倒映着周围的群山。
smoothness, shininess, luster, glaze
roughness, dullness
noun
