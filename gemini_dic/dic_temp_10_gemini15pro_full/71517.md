flannel:/ˈflænəl/ (US), /ˈflænəl/ (UK)| n. adj.|法兰绒，法兰绒制的|A soft, slightly napped fabric of wool or cotton or a mixture. Also, garments made from this fabric, especially shirts.|He wore a red flannel shirt. 他穿着一件红色的法兰绒衬衫。|近义词：woolen, fleece|反义词：silk, linen|词性: noun, adjective

flannels:/ˈflænəlz/ (US), /ˈflænəlz/ (UK)| n.|法兰绒衣服（尤指裤子）|Flannel trousers or pajamas, typically worn for warmth and comfort.|He changed into his flannels for the evening. 他晚上换上了法兰绒睡衣。|近义词：pajamas, loungewear|反义词：formals, dress clothes|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Welsh *gwlanen (made of wool), from gwlan (wool).
