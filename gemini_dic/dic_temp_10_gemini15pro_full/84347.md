half-serious:/ˌhæf ˈsɪəriəs/| adj.|半开玩笑的，半认真的|Being only partly serious; not entirely serious.  A blend of seriousness and playfulness.|He gave a half-serious smile. 他露出了一个半开玩笑的笑容。|Near Synonyms:  semi-serious, partly serious, not entirely serious, playful yet serious|Near Antonyms: completely serious, deadly serious, totally serious|adjective
Etymology:  "half" + "serious".  "Half" meaning partly or incompletely, and "serious" meaning grave or earnest.|
