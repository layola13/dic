future-proofing:/ˈfjuːtʃəˌpruːfɪŋ/ | n. | 未来防护，面向未来设计 | The act of designing or developing something in a way that makes it resistant to becoming obsolete or outdated in the foreseeable future.  |  Example:  The architect emphasized future-proofing the building's design by incorporating adaptable spaces and advanced technology infrastructure.  建筑师强调通过结合适应性强的空间和先进的技术基础设施来对建筑设计进行面向未来的设计。 | Example:  Investing in future-proofing your skills is essential in today's rapidly changing job market.  在当今快速变化的就业市场中，投资于使你的技能面向未来至关重要。 | Synonyms:  forward-looking design,  anticipation of future needs,  obsolescence prevention | Antonyms: short-sighted planning, outdated design | Noun
