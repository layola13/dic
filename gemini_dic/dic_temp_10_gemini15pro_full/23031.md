boulevard:/ˈbuːləvɑːrd/ (US) /ˈbuːlvɑːd/ (UK)| n. |林荫大道；大马路|A wide street, often lined with trees.|We strolled along the boulevard, enjoying the Parisian atmosphere. 我们沿着林荫大道漫步，享受着巴黎的氛围。|近义词：avenue, street, thoroughfare|反义词:alley, lane|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French boulevard, originally meaning "bulwark," a defensive rampart.  The word's meaning shifted to refer to the broad streets atop these ramparts, and later to any wide, tree-lined street.
