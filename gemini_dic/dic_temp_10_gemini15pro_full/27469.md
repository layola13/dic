campaigns:/kæmˈpeɪnz/ | n. | 运动，活动；战役 | A series of planned activities that are intended to achieve a particular social, commercial or political aim. |  The presidential campaigns are heating up. 总统竞选活动正在升温。

campaigns:/kæmˈpeɪnz/ | v. | 参加运动，领导运动 | To take part in or lead a campaign. |  They campaigned tirelessly for human rights. 他们不知疲倦地为争取人权而奔走。


Etymology: From Old French *campagne "open country," from Late Latin campania "level tract of land," from Latin campus "field."  The military sense developed from the idea of armies operating in the open field, and the extended sense of "organized operation" followed.


Synonym: crusade, drive, movement, push, effort

Antonym: inaction, passivity

Word type: noun/verb
