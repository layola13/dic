camaraderie:/ˌkæməˈrɑːdəri/
n.
同志情谊；友情
mutual trust and friendship among people who spend a lot of time together
The soldiers who fought together in the war developed a strong sense of camaraderie.  一起在战争中战斗的士兵们培养了强烈的同志情谊。

例句：
There was great camaraderie among the team members.
队员之间有着极佳的同志情谊。

There's a spirit of camaraderie between the actors.
演员之间有一种同志情谊。


近义词：companionship, fellowship, friendship, amity, esprit de corps
反义词：enmity, hostility, animosity
词性：noun
