marigold: /ˈmærɪɡoʊld/ (US) /ˈmærɪɡəʊld/ (UK)| n. |万寿菊，金盏花|A plant with bright yellow or orange flowers, often grown in gardens. |例句：The marigolds in her garden were a vibrant splash of color. 她花园里的万寿菊是一片鲜艳的色彩。

例句：Marigolds are known for their ability to repel certain insects. 万寿菊以其驱除某些昆虫的能力而闻名。|近义词：calendula (specifically pot marigold), flower|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
