copsed:/kɒpst/| adj.| 被砍伐的，矮林作业的 |  Managed by periodic cutting back to ground level, as coppiced woodland. | The copsed hazel trees were ready for harvesting. 被砍伐的榛树可以收割了。 | 近义词: cut, trimmed, pruned | 反义词: overgrown, untrimmed | 词性:形容词
词源: From coppice (n.) + -ed, from Old French copeiz "a thicket, grove," from coper "to cut."
