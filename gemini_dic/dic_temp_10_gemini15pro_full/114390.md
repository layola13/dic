make a spectacle of oneself:/meɪk ə ˈspɛktəkəl əv wʌnˈsɛlf/ | idiom | 出丑，丢人现眼 | To behave in a foolish or embarrassing way that attracts a lot of attention.  |  He made a spectacle of himself by dancing on the table at the wedding. 他在婚礼上在桌子上跳舞，出了洋相。 | synonyms: embarrass oneself, humiliate oneself, make a fool of oneself | antonyms: behave oneself, act with decorum | idiom
