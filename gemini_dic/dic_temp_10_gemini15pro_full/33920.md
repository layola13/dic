circle:/ˈsɜːrkəl/ (US) /ˈsɜːkl/ (UK)| n. v.|圆圈，循环，圈子，集团|A round plane figure whose boundary (the circumference) consists of points equidistant from a fixed point (the center).  A group of people with shared interests, activity, or profession.|The children sat in a circle. 孩子们围成一圈坐着。| synonyms: ring, loop, cycle, orbit, group, club, society | antonyms: line, segment, tangent| noun
