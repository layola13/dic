idle-away:/ˈaɪdl əˈweɪ/| phrasal verb | 虚度，浪费 | to spend time doing nothing or very little of value | He idled away the whole afternoon watching TV. 他浪费了整个下午看电视。|例句：She idled away her summer vacation doing nothing. 她无所事事地虚度了暑假。

例句：Don't idle away your youth; it's a precious time. 不要虚度你的青春；这是宝贵的时光。

近义词：waste, squander, fritter away|反义词: utilize, make good use of | 词性: 动词短语
