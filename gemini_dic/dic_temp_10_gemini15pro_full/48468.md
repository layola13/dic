deen:/diːn/
n.|喧嚣，嘈杂声；山谷|A loud, confused noise; a valley (poetic/archaic).|The deen of the city kept him awake all night. 城市的喧嚣让他彻夜难眠。|Synonym: din, clamor, racket|Antonym: quiet, silence|noun
