nonscandalous: /ˌnɒnˈskændələs/ | adj. | 无诽谤性的，不引起公愤的 | Not scandalous; not causing scandal or outrage. | The mayor's remarks were entirely nonscandalous and appropriate for the occasion. 市长的讲话完全没有诽谤性，而且场合也很合适。|  The book offered a nonscandalous account of the royal family. 这本书对皇室家族进行了没有诽谤性的描述。 | 近义词: inoffensive, harmless, unobjectionable, blameless | 反义词: scandalous, outrageous, shocking, offensive | 词性: 形容词

词源:
non- (prefix): not, opposite of  非，相反的
scandalous (adjective): causing or likely to cause general public outrage by a perceived offense against morality or law.  引起公愤的；诽谤性的
