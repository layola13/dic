luminary: /ˈluːmɪneri/ (US) /ˈluːmɪnəri/ (UK)| n.|杰出人物， luminary，发光体|A person who inspires or influences others, especially one prominent in a particular sphere.|例句：He was a luminary in the field of astrophysics. 他是天体物理学领域的杰出人物。

例句：She is a luminary of the stage and screen. 她是舞台和银幕上的明星。

近义词：star, leader, expert, authority|反义词：nobody, unknown|词性: noun
