codevelopers:/koʊdɪˈvɛləpərz/| n. |共同开发者；合作开发者|People or organizations who work together to develop something, such as software, a product, or a project.|Example: The codevelopers of the new app met weekly to discuss progress.  这款新应用的共同开发者每周开会讨论进度。|Synonym: collaborators, partners, joint developers|Antonym: competitors, rivals|noun
