hook:/hʊk/ | n. vt.|钩，钩状物；吸引人的东西，（歌曲的）副歌；拳击的勾拳| A curved piece of metal or other hard material used for catching, holding, or pulling something. Something that attracts attention or serves as an enticement. | The fish took the bait and swallowed the hook. 鱼吃了诱饵，吞下了钩。|synonyms: clasp, fastener, catch, snare, lure, enticement| antonyms: release, repulsion| noun

hook:/hʊk/ | vt.|钩住，挂住；吸引（某人）| To catch, fasten, or pull something with a hook. To attract or entice someone.| The butcher hooked the carcass up on a rail. 屠夫把屠体挂在铁轨上。|synonyms: catch, snag, secure, attract, captivate| antonyms: detach, repel| verb
