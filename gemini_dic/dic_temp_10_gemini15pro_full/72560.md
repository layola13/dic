flutteringly:/ˈflʌtərɪŋli/
adverb|飘动地，颤抖地|In a way that flutters; with a fluttering motion.  Characterized by quick, light movements like a bird's wings.|The flags fluttered flutteringly in the breeze. 旗帜在微风中飘扬。|Synonyms: lightly, airily, waveringly|Antonyms: steadily, firmly|词性: 副词

flutteringly:/ˈflʌtərɪŋli/
词源: From the verb "flutter" + the adverbial suffix "-ingly".  "Flutter" comes from Old Norse *flotra, meaning "to float, flap."|词源说明：源自动词 "flutter" 加副词后缀 "-ingly"。"Flutter" 来自古诺尔斯语 *flotra，意思是“漂浮，拍打”。
