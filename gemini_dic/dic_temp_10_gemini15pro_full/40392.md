contradistinctive:/ˌkɒntrədɪˈstɪŋktɪv/ /ˌkɑːntrədɪˈstɪŋktɪv/| adj.|对比鲜明的，形成对照的|Serving to distinguish by contrast; contrastive.  Marking a difference or distinction.|Her style of painting is contradistinctive, bold colors contrasting with muted backgrounds. 她的绘画风格对比鲜明，大胆的色彩与柔和的背景形成对比。|Synonyms: contrastive, distinctive, differentiating, characteristic, typical|Antonyms: similar, alike, uniform, homogeneous|adjective
