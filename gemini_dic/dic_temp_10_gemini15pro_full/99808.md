intermigrated:/ˌɪntərˈmaɪɡreɪtɪd/| v. |互相迁移，互相融合|The two communities have intermigrated over the years, sharing customs and traditions.|这两个社群多年来互相融合，共享习俗和传统。|近义词：integrated, mingled, blended|反义词：segregated, separated|词性:动词

Etymology:
From inter- (between) + migrated (moved from one place to another).  "Migrate" ultimately comes from Latin "migrare" (to change one's abode, depart).
