chiefdoms:/ˈtʃiːfdəmz/
noun
酋邦；首领的地位
A form of social organization more complex than a tribe or band, and less complex than a state, typically consisting of a number of villages or communities under the authority of a single paramount chief.
Many Pacific Island cultures were organized into chiefdoms. 许多太平洋岛屿文化都被组织成酋邦。
synonyms:territory, domain, leadership
antonyms:subordination, subjection
