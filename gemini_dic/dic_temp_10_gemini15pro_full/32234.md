chickadees:/ˈtʃɪkəˌdiz/ | n. | 山雀（复数） | Small, active North American songbirds with black caps and throats.  They are known for their distinctive "chick-a-dee" call, which gives them their name. | Example: The chickadees flitted from branch to branch, searching for insects. 山雀在树枝间飞来飞去，寻找昆虫。 | Example: We filled the bird feeder, and soon several chickadees arrived. 我们装满了喂鸟器，很快就有几只山雀飞来了。| Synonym: titmouse | Antonym: N/A | Noun
