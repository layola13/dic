-arily:/-ərɪli/
| adv.|
|中文翻译：以...方式; -arily 本身不是一个独立的单词，而是一个后缀，用于构成副词，表示“以...方式”、“以...状态”或“关于...”。|
|英文解析：-arily is a suffix used to form adverbs, meaning "in a ... manner," "in a ... state," or "with regard to ...". It is typically added to adjectives ending in "-ary."  The suffix "-arily" can often be replaced by "-ily" without a significant change in meaning, although sometimes "-arily" is considered more formal or traditional. |
|例句：
He acted arbitrarily. = 他行为武断。

The case was summarily dismissed. = 此案被立即驳回。
|近义词：-ly, in a ... way|
|反义词：无|
|词性：后缀|
