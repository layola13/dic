edams:/ˈiːdəmz/ | n. |埃丹奶酪| A semi-hard cheese from the Netherlands, traditionally made in spherical shapes, with a pale yellow interior and a protected rind coated in red wax. | I love a sandwich with edams and ham. 我喜欢埃丹奶酪和火腿三明治。|  Example: The edams cheese was perfectly aged.  这埃丹奶酪熟成得恰到好处。 |Synonym:  Gouda,  Cheese | Antonym: None (as it's a specific type of cheese) | Noun
