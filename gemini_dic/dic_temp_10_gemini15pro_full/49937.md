dendrochronological:/ˌdɛndroʊˌkrɒnəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
树木年代学的
Relating to dendrochronology, the science or technique of dating events, environmental change, and archaeological artifacts by using the characteristic patterns of annual growth rings in timber and tree trunks.
Dendrochronological analysis helped determine the age of the ancient ruins.  树木年代学分析帮助确定了古代遗址的年代。
Near Synonyms: tree-ring dating
Antonyms:  None.
Etymology:  From Greek *dendron* (tree) + *chronos* (time) + *-logia* (study of).
