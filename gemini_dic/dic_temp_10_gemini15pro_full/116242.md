mass-marketing:/ˈmæs ˈmɑːrkɪtɪŋ/ |n.| 大众营销 |The act or practice of promoting and selling a product or service to the widest possible audience.| Coca-Cola is a classic example of mass-marketing. 可口可乐是大规模营销的典型例子。| 近义词: mass-merchandising | 反义词: niche marketing, targeted marketing | 词性: 名词

mass-marketing:/ˈmæs ˈmɑːrkɪtɪŋ/ |adj.| 大众营销的 |Relating to or characterized by the act or practice of promoting and selling a product or service to the widest possible audience.| The company adopted a mass-marketing strategy to reach a broader consumer base.  这家公司采取了大众营销策略以触达更广泛的消费者群体。| 近义词: mass-merchandising | 反义词: niche marketing, targeted marketing | 词性: 形容词
