obligedly:/əˈblaɪdʒɪdli/
adverb
殷勤地，乐意地
In a manner expressing willingness to do a service or kindness.
"He obligedly held the door open for her." 他殷勤地为她扶着门。
Synonym: willingly, graciously, courteously, kindly
Antonym: unwillingly, reluctantly, grudgingly
词性: 副词
