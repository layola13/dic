correctest: /kəˈrɛktɪst/
adjective
最正确的
(nonstandard) Most correct.  This form is generally considered incorrect and overly pedantic.  Use "most correct" instead.
He claims his answer is the correctest, but that's not proper English. 他声称他的答案是最正确的，但这不符合英语语法。
Near Synonyms: most correct, rightest (also nonstandard)
Antonyms: most incorrect, wrongest (also nonstandard)
词性: adjective
