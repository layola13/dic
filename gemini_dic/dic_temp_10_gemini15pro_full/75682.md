fulgently:/ˈfʌldʒəntlɪ/
adjective
闪亮地，辉煌地
Shining brightly; dazzlingly.
The sun shone fulgently in the clear sky. 太阳在晴朗的天空中闪耀着光芒。
近义词:brightly, radiantly, dazzlingly, brilliantly
反义词:dimly, faintly, obscurely
词性:adjective
词源:From Latin *fulgent-, present participle stem of fulgēre "to shine, flash, gleam."
