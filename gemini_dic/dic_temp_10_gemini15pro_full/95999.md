incurrable:/ɪnˈkjʊrəbl/ | adj. | 不可治愈的，无法医治的 | Not able to be cured or remedied. |  The doctor delivered the grim news that his condition was incurrable. 医生带来了坏消息，他的病情无法治愈。

The incurrable disease caused him great suffering.  这种不治之症给他带来了巨大的痛苦。

| 近义词: incurable, irremediable, terminal, hopeless | 反义词: curable, treatable, remediable | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *incurabilis, from in- "not" + curabilis "curable," from curare "to take care of, cure."
