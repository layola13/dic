goddesslike: /ˈɡɒdəsˌlaɪk/ (US) /ˈɡɒdesˌlaɪk/ (UK)
adjective
女神般的；像女神一样的
Resembling or characteristic of a goddess, especially in beauty, grace, or power.
She possesses a goddesslike beauty that captivates everyone. 她拥有一种令人着迷的女神般的美貌。
The model moved with a goddesslike grace.  这个模特举止优雅，宛如女神。

近义词: divine, ethereal, heavenly, angelic, celestial
反义词: mortal, human, earthly, mundane, ordinary
词性: adjective
