objectlessnesses:/ˌɒbdʒɪktlɪsnəsɪz/
n.
无目标性；无对象性；缺乏目标的状态
The state of lacking a purpose or objective; absence of an object of attention or pursuit.
His objectlessnesses led to a deep sense of dissatisfaction. 他的缺乏目标导致了深深的不安感。
Near Synonyms: aimlessness, purposelessness, lack of direction
Antonyms: purposefulness, focus, direction
