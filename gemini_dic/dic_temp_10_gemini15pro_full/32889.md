chondrogenesis:/ˌkɒndrəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/ /ˌkɑːndrəˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
noun
软骨形成；软骨生成
The process by which cartilage is formed, especially during embryonic development.  It involves the differentiation of mesenchymal cells into chondrocytes, which then synthesize and secrete the extracellular matrix of cartilage.
Chondrogenesis is essential for the development of the skeletal system.  软骨形成对于骨骼系统的发育至关重要。

The researchers are studying the factors that regulate chondrogenesis. 研究人员正在研究调节软骨形成的因素。

近义词: cartilage formation, cartilage development
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but potentially) cartilage degradation, cartilage resorption
词性: noun
