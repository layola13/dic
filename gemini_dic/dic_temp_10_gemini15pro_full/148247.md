overskip:/ˌoʊvərˈskɪp/| v. |跳过，略过，忽略|To pass over or omit; to skip. |She overskipped the introduction and went straight to the main point. 她跳过了引言，直接进入主题。|He overskipped several steps in the process and ended up with a faulty product.  他跳过了流程中的几个步骤，最终得到了一个有缺陷的产品。|近义词：omit, bypass, ignore, neglect|反义词: include, address, acknowledge|词性:动词
