garage: /ɡəˈrɑːʒ/ (UK) /ɡəˈrɑːdʒ/ (US)| n.| 车库；汽车修理厂 |A building where a car is kept, or a business that repairs cars.| I park my car in the garage. 我把车停在车库里。|The car is at the garage being repaired.  这辆车正在修理厂修理。| synonyms: carpark, workshop, service station| antonyms: outdoors, outside | noun

garage: /ɡəˈrɑːʒ/ (UK) /ɡəˈrɑːdʒ/ (US)| vt.| 把…放入车库 |To put or store a vehicle in a garage.| I garaged the car for the winter. 我把车存放在车库里过冬。| He garaged his classic convertible to protect it from the elements. 他把他的经典敞篷车停放在车库里以保护它免受风雨侵蚀。 | synonyms: park, store, shelter| antonyms: remove, take out | verb
