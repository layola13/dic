epaulement:/eɪˈpɔːlmɑːnt/ /eɪˈpoʊlmɑːnt/
n.
斜肩，肩的姿势，（尤指芭蕾舞中的）肩部动作
The graceful epaulement of the ballerina added to the beauty of the performance.  芭蕾舞演员优雅的肩部动作增加了表演的美感。
The dancer's epaulement was perfect. 舞者的肩部姿势很完美。
A slight epaulement added a touch of elegance to her posture. 轻微的斜肩使她的姿势更添优雅。
近义词: posture, pose, positioning
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "slouch")
词性: noun

epaulement:/eɪˈpɔːlmɑːnt/ /eɪˈpoʊlmɑːnt/
Etymology: French, from épaule 'shoulder', from Latin spatula
词源: 来自法语 épaule “肩膀”，源自拉丁语 spatula。
