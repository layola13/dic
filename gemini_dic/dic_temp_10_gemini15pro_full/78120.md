geomancer:/ˈdʒiːoʊmænsər/ | n. | 地卜师；风水师 | A person who practices geomancy; a diviner who attempts to foretell the future by means of figures or lines or geographic features. |  The geomancer predicted a prosperous future for the new building. 地卜师预测新建筑的未来一片光明。 |  The geomancer advised the emperor on the best location for his new palace.  地卜师向皇帝建议新宫殿的最佳位置。 | Synonyms: diviner, soothsayer, augur, prophet | Antonyms: scientist, skeptic | noun
