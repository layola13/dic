disappointer:/ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪntər/ | n. | 令人失望的人或事物 | Something or someone that disappoints. | The movie was a real disappointer. 这部电影真让人失望。 | Synonyms: letdown, disappointment, flop | Antonyms: success, triumph | noun

disappoint:/ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪnt/ | v. | 使失望 | Fail to fulfill the hopes or expectations of. | I hate to disappoint you, but I can't come. 我很不想让你失望，但我不能来。 | Synonyms: frustrate, disenchant, disillusion | Antonyms: please, delight | verb

disappointing:/ˌdɪsəˈpɔɪntɪŋ/ | adj. | 令人失望的 | Causing disappointment. | The team's performance was disappointing. 团队的表现令人失望。 | Synonyms: unsatisfactory, frustrating, disheartening | Antonyms: pleasing, satisfying, encouraging | adjective
