embolden:/ɪmˈboʊldən/ |v. |使大胆；使有胆量；鼓励|To make someone feel braver or more confident.|例句：His success emboldened him to try again. 他的成功鼓励他再次尝试。|例句：Emboldened by the wine, he asked her to dance.  受到酒的壮胆，他邀请她跳舞。|近义词：encourage, hearten, inspire|反义词：discourage, intimidate, daunt|词性：动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From en- + bold + -en,  "bold" meaning brave and confident. The suffix "-en" turns an adjective into a verb, meaning "to make or become."
