paleotheroid: /ˌpeɪliəˈθɪːrɔɪd/ | n. | 古兽, 古兽类动物 | An extinct mammal of the Eocene and Oligocene epochs, resembling the tapir.  These hoofed mammals were related to horses and rhinoceroses. |  Paleotheroids were common in Europe and North America during the Eocene.  古兽在始新世的欧洲和北美很常见。 |  The museum displayed a fossilized skeleton of a paleotheroid.  博物馆展出了一具古兽的化石骨架。 | synonyms: prehistoric mammal, extinct ungulate | antonyms: extant mammal | noun
