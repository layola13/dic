antiessentialist:/ˌæntiɪˈsɛnʃəlɪst/
| adj. n.|反本质主义的；反本质主义者|Rejecting the idea that things have inherent, fixed properties or essences.  Denying that there is a single, universal definition or nature of something.  | Her antiessentialist approach to gender allowed her to explore the fluidity of identity. 她反本质主义的性别观让她能够探索身份的流动性。|  Antiessentialist theories challenge traditional notions of race. 反本质主义理论挑战了传统的种族观念。| 近义词:constructivist, deconstructionist| 反义词:essentialist| 词性:adjective, noun
