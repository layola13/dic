carted:/ˈkɑːrtɪd/
verb
用车运送；用手推车运送
past tense and past participle of "cart"  Carried by a cart or as if by a cart.
The farmer carted the hay to the barn. 农民用车把干草运到谷仓。
synonyms: conveyed, transported, hauled, carried
antonyms:  walked, carried (if by hand), left (in situ)
verb

carted:/ˈkɑːrtɪd/
adjective
用手推车运送的
Carried by a cart or as if by a cart. (Less common usage as an adjective)
The carted goods arrived safely.  用车运送的货物安全到达。（较少见的形容词用法）
synonyms: conveyed, transported, hauled, carried
antonyms:  walked, carried (if by hand), left (in situ)
adjective
