cliquiest:/ˈklɪkiɪst/| adj.|小集团的；排外的；拉帮结派的|Most exclusive; tending to form or associate in small exclusive groups.|例句：She goes to a very cliquiest private school. 她上的是一所非常排外私立学校。

例句：The office was known for being cliquiest, making it difficult for newcomers to integrate. 这间办公室以拉帮结派而闻名，使得新来的人很难融入。|近义词：exclusive, clannish, snobbish|反义词：inclusive, welcoming, open|词性:形容词

词源: clique + -iest.  From French *clique*, of imitative origin.
