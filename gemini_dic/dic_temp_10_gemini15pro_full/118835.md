metaphony:/ˈmɛtəˌfoʊni/ /mɛˈtɒfəni/| n.|元音变音，变音|A vowel change in which a vowel is influenced by a vowel in a following syllable.|The metaphony in this dialect changed the 'o' to an 'u'.  这个方言中的元音变音将'o'变成了'u'.|Umlaut, vowel mutation| |noun|
