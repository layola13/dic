conceptualist:/kənˈsɛptʃuəlɪst/| n.|概念主义者；观念艺术家|A person who emphasizes the importance of concepts or ideas, especially in art or philosophy.  One who creates or analyzes concepts.| Damien Hirst is often considered a conceptualist artist. 达米恩·赫斯特通常被认为是一位观念艺术家。|近义词：theorist, ideologue|反义词：pragmatist, realist|词性:noun

conceptualist:/kənˈsɛptʃuəlɪst/| adj.|概念的；观念的|Of or relating to conceptualism; characterized by the use of concepts or ideas as the basis of art or philosophy.|The conceptualist nature of the piece makes it challenging for some viewers. 这件作品的概念性让一些观众觉得难以理解。|近义词：abstract, theoretical|反义词：concrete, practical|词性:adjective
