nonidiomaticalnesses:/ˌnɒnɪdɪəˌmætɪkəlnəsɪz/ | n. | 非惯用性；不自然 | The awkwardness or unnaturalness of language that doesn't conform to established idioms or patterns of usage.  It refers to expressions that sound strange or incorrect to native speakers. | His writing was plagued by nonidiomaticalnesses, making it difficult to understand. 他的写作充满了不自然的表达，让人难以理解。 | synonyms: unidiomaticness, awkwardness, unnaturalness | antonyms: idiomaticity, naturalness, fluency | noun
