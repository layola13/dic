hogans:/ˈhoʊɡɑːnz/
n.
霍根式圆顶棚屋（尤指美国西南部纳瓦霍人传统的）
A traditional Navajo dwelling, usually made of logs and earth.
The hogan is considered sacred by the Navajo people.  纳瓦霍人认为霍根是神圣的。
