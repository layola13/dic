/ˈkæpɪtl/ | /ˈkæpɪtəl/ | n. adj.|首都，资本，大写字母|A city serving as the seat of government of a country or state; wealth in the form of money or other assets owned by a person or organization or available for investment; a letter of the size and form used to begin sentences and names.|
The capital of France is Paris. 法国的首都是巴黎。

London is the capital of England and a major financial capital. 伦敦是英国的首都和一个主要的金融中心。

Write your name in capitals.  用大写字母写你的名字。

|近义词：chief city (for capital city), funds, resources (for capital as wealth), uppercase (for capital letter)|反义词：province, lowercase|noun, adjective
