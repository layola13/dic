florilege:/ˈflɔːrɪˌlɛʒ/ /ˈflɔːrəˌleʒ/| n. |选集，文选，花束；精华|An anthology or collection of literary extracts; a bouquet or selection of flowers; the best of something. |The chef presented a florilege of his best dishes.  这位厨师展示了他最拿手菜肴的精选。|Synonyms: anthology, collection, compilation, selection, bouquet, compendium, miscellany |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "omission" or "exclusion" in certain contexts) |noun
