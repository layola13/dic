delectations:/ˌdiːlɛkˈteɪʃənz/
n.
乐趣；使人快乐的事物
great pleasure or enjoyment
The chef prepared a feast of epicurean delectations.  厨师准备了一场美食盛宴。
近义词：pleasures, delights, enjoyments
反义词：displeasures, miseries, sufferings
词性: 名词 (plural noun)

词源:
From Late Latin delectation-, delectatio, from delectare ‘to delight,’ from de- ‘thoroughly’ + lactare ‘to entice, allure.’
