cognoscenti:/ˌkɒnjəˈʃɛnti/ /ˌkɑːnjəˈʃɛnti/| n.|专家；行家；鉴赏家|People who have expert knowledge of a particular subject, especially in the arts, or who are especially good at judging the quality of something.|The restaurant is popular with the cognoscenti.|近义词：experts, connoisseurs, specialists, authorities|反义词：amateurs, novices, laypersons|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Italian *cognoscenti*, plural of *conoscente* "acquaintance, expert," from present participle of *conoscere* "to know," from Latin *cognoscere* "to get to know, learn," from *co-* "together" + *gnoscere* "to know."
