oilinesses:/ˈɔɪlɪnəsɪz/
n.
油腻; 油性; 油滑
the quality or state of being oily.
The oilinesses of the skin can be controlled with proper skincare.  皮肤的油性可以通过适当的护肤来控制。
The various oilinesses of the different cooking oils affected the taste of the fried food. 不同食用油的油性影响了油炸食品的味道。

近义词:greasiness, slickness
反义词:dryness, aridity
词性:noun
