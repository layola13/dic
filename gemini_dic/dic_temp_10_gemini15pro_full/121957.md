missionate:/ˈmɪʃəneɪt/
verb
赋予使命；派遣；传教
to send (someone) on a mission. To imbue with a sense of mission.
例句：The organization missionated him to Africa to help with the relief effort.  这个组织派遣他去非洲参与救援工作。

例句：He felt missionated to spread awareness about climate change.  他感到自己有使命去传播气候变化的意识。

近义词：dispatch, send, commission, charge
反义词：recall, withdraw
词性：v.
