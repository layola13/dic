meatotome:/ˌmiːətəˈtoʊm/
noun
肉瘤切除器
A surgical instrument used to excise a meatus (a natural body opening).
The surgeon used a meatotome to widen the narrowed urethral meatus.  外科医生使用肉瘤切除器来扩大狭窄的尿道口。
Synonyms: meatoscope, urethrotome
Antonyms: N/A
