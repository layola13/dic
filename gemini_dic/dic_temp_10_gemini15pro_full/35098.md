clothings:/ˈkloʊðɪŋz/ | n. | 衣服，衣物 |  While grammatically correct, "clothings" is considered archaic or non-standard. The standard and preferred term is "clothing," which is an uncountable noun. It refers to garments collectively.  |  He donated all his old clothings. (Non-standard usage. Better: He donated all his old clothing.) | 近义词：clothing, garments, apparel, attire | 反义词: nudity | 词性: 名词
