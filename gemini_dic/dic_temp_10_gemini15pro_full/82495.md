gruntling:/ˈɡrʌntliŋ/| n.|小猪; 咕哝的人; 爱抱怨的人|A young pig, or a person who grumbles or complains. |例句：The farmer fed the gruntlings in the sty. 农场主给猪圈里的小猪喂食。

例句：He's such a gruntling, always complaining about something. 他真是个爱抱怨的人，总是抱怨个不停。|近义词：grumbler, complainer, whiner, piglet (for young pig)|反义词: optimist, enthusiast |词性:noun
