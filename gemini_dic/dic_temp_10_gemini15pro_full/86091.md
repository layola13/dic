heart:/hɑːrt/ (US) /hɑːt/ (UK)| n. v.| 心脏；中心；要点；感情；勇气|The core of something, both literally (the organ that pumps blood) and figuratively (the central or most important part). Can also refer to emotions, particularly love, compassion, or courage.|

例句：
My heart is racing. 我的心跳得很快。

The heart of the city is always bustling. 市中心总是熙熙攘攘。

近义词：core, center, essence, feeling, spirit, courage
反义词：periphery, edge, apathy, cowardice

词性: noun, verb
