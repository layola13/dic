agriculturism:/ˈæɡrɪkʌltʃərɪzəm/
n.
农业主义;重农主义
The belief in the importance of agriculture and farming.  A social or political philosophy that emphasizes the importance of agriculture.
例句：Agriculturism was a dominant philosophy in 18th-century France. 18世纪法国的重农主义盛行。

例句：The government's policies reflected a strong strain of agriculturism. 政府的政策反映了浓厚的重农主义色彩。

近义词：agrarianism, physiocracy
反义词：industrialism
词性：noun
