geniuses:/ˈdʒiːniəsiz/
noun
天才; 天赋; 天资; 
Exceptionally intelligent or creative people; plural of genius.
Einstein was one of the greatest geniuses of the 20th century. 爱因斯坦是20世纪最伟大的天才之一。
The company is run by a team of marketing geniuses. 该公司由一群营销天才团队运营。
Synonyms:  prodigies,  masterminds,  virtuosos,  talents,  intellects
Antonyms:  idiots,  dunces,  fools,  imbeciles
