ginning:/ˈdʒɪnɪŋ/| n.|轧棉; 轧棉加工; 去籽; 棉籽分离|The process of separating cotton fibers from the seeds.|The ginning process is crucial for cotton production. 轧棉过程对棉花生产至关重要。|The ginning mill was noisy and dusty.  轧棉厂又吵又脏。|synonyms: processing, separating, cleaning|antonyms: combining, integrating|noun
