paneer:/pəˈnɪər/| n.| 印度奶酪，芝士 | A fresh, unsalted, white cheese common in South Asian cuisine.  It is made by curdling heated milk with lemon juice, vinegar, or other food acids. |  例句：I used paneer in my curry tonight. 今晚我的咖喱用了印度奶酪。

例句：Paneer tikka masala is a popular Indian dish. 印度奶酪咖喱是一道很受欢迎的印度菜。| 近义词：Indian cheese, chhena (in its unpressed form)| 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
