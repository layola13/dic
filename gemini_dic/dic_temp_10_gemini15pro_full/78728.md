gigglers: /ˈɡɪɡlərz/
n.
爱咯咯笑的人; 咯咯笑的人
People who giggle frequently.
The group of gigglers in the back row disrupted the class. 后排那群咯咯笑的人扰乱了课堂秩序。
近义词: laughers, chucklers
反义词: mourners, wailers
词性: 名词
