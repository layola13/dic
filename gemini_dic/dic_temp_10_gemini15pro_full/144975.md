overaccentuating: /ˌoʊvərəkˈsɛntʃueɪtɪŋ/
verb
过分强调
placing too much emphasis or stress on something.
She was overaccentuating her British accent. 她过分强调她的英国口音。
The media is overaccentuating the negative aspects of the story. 媒体过分强调了故事的负面。
overemphasizing, overstressing
understating, downplaying
v.
