informedness:/ɪnˈfɔːrmɪdnəs/ | n. | 知情度；消息灵通；信息量 | The state of being well-informed; having or showing awareness or knowledge of something. |  Her informedness about current events was impressive. 她对时事的了解程度令人印象深刻。 | Synonyms: awareness, knowledge, intelligence, wisdom | Antonyms: ignorance, unawareness, naivety | noun

Etymology: From "informed" + "-ness". "Informed" comes from the Old French "enformer" (to instruct, train), from "in-" (in) + "former" (to form).  "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns expressing state or condition.
