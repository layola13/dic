interdetermination:/ˌɪntərdiˌtɜːrmɪˈneɪʃn/ | n. |相互决定，互相决定，相互制约|The concept of interdetermination emphasizes the reciprocal relationship and mutual influence between different factors or elements.|The interdetermination of supply and demand affects market prices. 供求关系的相互作用影响市场价格。|Synonyms: interdependence, interaction, reciprocity|Antonyms: independence, isolation|noun
