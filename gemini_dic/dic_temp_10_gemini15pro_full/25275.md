buffoon:/bəˈfuːn/ | n. | 小丑；滑稽可笑的人 | A buffoon is a person who behaves in a silly or ridiculous way, often to make other people laugh. |  He acted like a complete buffoon at the party, making silly jokes and falling over. 他在派对上像个十足的小丑，讲着愚蠢的笑话，还摔倒了。

The king employed a buffoon to entertain the court. 国王雇了一个小丑来娱乐宫廷。| 近义词：clown, jester, fool, comedian | 反义词：sage, intellectual | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *buffone* "clown, jester," from *buffare* "to puff, blow, jest, play the fool," of uncertain origin.  Possibly related to Old French *bouffer* "to puff, swell." The sense evolution likely went from "one who puffs out his cheeks" (like a clown) to "jester."
