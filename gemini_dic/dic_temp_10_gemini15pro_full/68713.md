farfetch:/ˌfɑːrˈfɛtʃt/ | adj. | 牵强的；不着边际的；难以置信的 |  Unlikely and unconvincing; implausible.  Derived from "fetch" meaning "to go and bring back." Originally referred to something difficult to fetch or obtain, later evolving to describe ideas or explanations that seem improbable. | That's a farfetch explanation for his absence. 他缺席的那个解释很牵强。| The idea that he could fly unaided was farfetched.  他认为自己可以不借助任何工具飞行，这想法真是异想天开。| 近义词: improbable, unlikely, implausible, unbelievable | 反义词: credible, believable, plausible, likely | 词性: 形容词
