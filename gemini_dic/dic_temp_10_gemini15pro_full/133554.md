nonfinitenesses:/ˌnɒnfɪnɪtnɪsɪz/| n. |非限定性|The quality or state of being nonfinite (not having tense or person).  It refers to verb forms that do not function as the main verb of a clause, such as infinitives, participles, and gerunds.|His analysis focused on the nonfinitenesses in the sentence structure.|近义词:non-finiteness|反义词:finiteness|词性:noun
