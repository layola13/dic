parthenogenesis:/ˌpɑːrθɪnoʊˈdʒɛnɪsɪs/
|n.|单性生殖，孤雌生殖|Reproduction from an ovum without fertilization, especially as a normal process in some invertebrates and lower plants.|例句：Parthenogenesis is a common form of reproduction in aphids. 蚜虫的常见繁殖方式是单性生殖。

例句：Some lizards reproduce through parthenogenesis. 一些蜥蜴通过单性生殖进行繁殖。
|近义词：apomixis, asexual reproduction|反义词：sexual reproduction|词性：名词

parthenogenetic:/ˌpɑːrθɪnoʊdʒəˈnɛtɪk/
|adj.|单性生殖的，孤雌生殖的|Relating to or characterized by parthenogenesis.|例句：Scientists are studying parthenogenetic reproduction in certain species of fish. 科学家正在研究某些鱼类的单性生殖。

例句：The parthenogenetic offspring are all female. 单性生殖的后代都是雌性。
|近义词：apomictic, asexual|反义词：sexual|词性：形容词
