overaffirmatively:/ˌoʊvərəˈfɜːrmətɪvli/
| adv. | 过于肯定地，过分肯定地 | In an excessively or unnecessarily affirmative manner; too positively or assertively. | He overaffirmatively agreed to everything she said, making her suspicious. 他对她说的一切都过分肯定地表示同意，这让她起了疑心。 |  He overaffirmatively supported the proposal, raising eyebrows in the room. 他过分积极地支持这项提议，让房间里的人感到惊讶。 | 近义词: excessively affirmatively, too positively, overzealously | 反义词: hesitantly, uncertainly, tentatively | 词性: 副词
