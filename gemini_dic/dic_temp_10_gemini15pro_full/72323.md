flouting:/ˈflaʊtɪŋ/| v. |蔑视，嘲笑，公然违抗|openly disregard (a rule, law, or convention).|例句：They were flouting the rules. 他们公然违反规则。|例句：She flouted convention by wearing a pantsuit to the wedding. 她穿着裤套装参加婚礼，公然违反习俗。|近义词：disregarding, ignoring, defying, contravening, violating|反义词：obeying, respecting, adhering to, complying with|词性：动词

flout:/flaʊt/| n. |蔑视，嘲笑，公然违抗（名词形式较少用）|an act or instance of flouting.|例句：His flout of the rules angered the teacher. 他对规则的蔑视激怒了老师。|例句：Her flout of tradition caused a scandal. 她对传统的蔑视引起了轩然大波。|近义词：disregard, defiance, violation|反义词：obedience, respect, adherence|词性：名词
