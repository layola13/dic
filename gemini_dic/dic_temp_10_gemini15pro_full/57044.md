doubt:/daʊt/| n. v. |怀疑，疑问；不确定|a feeling of uncertainty or lack of conviction.|I have some doubts about his ability to do the job. 我对他的工作能力有些怀疑。|近义词：uncertainty, suspicion, misgiving|反义词：certainty, confidence, belief|词性:名词,动词

doubt:/daʊt/| v. |怀疑；不相信；不确定|to feel uncertain about; to distrust; to consider unlikely.|I doubt he'll come. 我怀疑他会不会来。|近义词：question, distrust, disbelieve|反义词：believe, trust, accept|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *douter*, from Latin *dubitare* "to hesitate, waver in opinion," frequentative of *dubare* "to be in doubt, waver," related to *dubius* "doubtful," literally "of two minds," from *duo* "two."
