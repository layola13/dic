dispensableness:/dɪˌspɛnsəbəlˈnɛs/
n.
可有可无；不必要；可分配性
The dispensableness of certain administrative procedures.
某些行政程序的可有可无。
The quality of being dispensable; dispensability.
可有可无的性质；可分配性。
He spoke of the dispensableness of the monarchy.
他谈到了君主制可有可无。
近义词: unimportance, nonessentiality, redundancy, expendability
反义词: indispensability, essentiality, necessity, importance
词性: 名词

dispensable:/dɪˈspɛnsəbəl/
adj.
可有可无的；不必要的；可分配的
Many of the old skills had become dispensable.
许多旧技能已经变得可有可无了。
Part-time workers are considered dispensable.
兼职工人被认为是可有可无的。
近义词: unnecessary, nonessential, redundant, expendable
反义词: indispensable, essential, necessary, crucial
词性: 形容词

词源: From Late Latin dispensabilis, from Latin dispensare "to disburse, distribute, administer," from dis- "apart" + pensare "to weigh, ponder."  The original meaning referred to something that could be distributed or administered piecemeal. The modern meaning of "nonessential" developed later.
