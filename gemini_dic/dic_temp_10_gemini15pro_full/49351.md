definition:/ˌdefɪˈnɪʃən/ | n. | 定义，释义 | A statement of the exact meaning of a word, especially in a dictionary. |  Example: The definition of "love" is complex and varied.  爱的定义是复杂和多样的。

Example:  Can you give me a precise definition of this term?  你能给我这个术语的精确定义吗？ | Synonyms: meaning, explanation, interpretation | Antonyms: ambiguity, vagueness | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: definicioun, via Old French from Latin definitio(n-), from definire ‘to limit, define’.
