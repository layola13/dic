infamous:/ˈɪnfəməs/| adj.|臭名昭著的，声名狼藉的|Well known for some bad quality or deed.|例句：He is an infamous criminal. 他是一个臭名昭著的罪犯。|例句：The city is infamous for its high crime rate.  这个城市因其高犯罪率而臭名昭著。|近义词：notorious, disreputable, ignominious|反义词：famous, renowned, celebrated|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *infamosus, from in- "not" + Latin famosus "famous," ultimately from fama "fame, rumor."  The sense shifted from simply "not famous" to "famous in a bad way."
