packhorse:/ˈpækhɔːrs/| n.|驮马，负重的人|A horse used for carrying packs or loads, especially in mountainous regions.  A person who carries a heavy burden or does a lot of tedious work.|The packhorse struggled up the steep mountain trail.|近义词：beast of burden, sumpter, draft animal|反义词:|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *pakhors*, from *pak* (pack) + *hors* (horse).  The word "pack" in this context refers to a bundle or load carried by a pack animal.
