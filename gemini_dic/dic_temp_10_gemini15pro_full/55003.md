disputable:/dɪˈspjuːtəbl/ /dɪˈspjutəbl/| adj.|有争议的，可争辩的|Open to argument or debate; not certain. |例句：The cause of the fire is still disputable. 火灾的原因仍然有争议。|例句：Whether this policy will be effective is highly disputable. 这项政策是否有效还存在很大争议。|近义词：debatable, arguable, questionable, contestable|反义词：indisputable, undeniable, unquestionable|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from Old French disputable, from disputer ‘dispute’, from Latin disputare, frequentative of dis- ‘apart’ + putare ‘think’.
