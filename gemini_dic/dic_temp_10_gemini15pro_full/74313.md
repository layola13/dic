fractured:/ˈfræktʃərd/ | adj. | 断裂的，破碎的；骨折的 | Broken, splintered, or cracked.  Also used figuratively to describe something damaged or impaired. | The earthquake left several fractured buildings. 地震导致几栋建筑物断裂。 | synonyms: broken, cracked, splintered, shattered, ruptured, fragmented, damaged, impaired | antonyms: whole, intact, undamaged, healed, repaired | adjective

fractured:/ˈfræktʃərd/ | v. | (使)断裂，(使)破碎；(使)骨折 | To cause something to break, splinter, or crack. |  The impact fractured his skull.  撞击使他的头骨骨折。 | synonyms: break, crack, splinter, shatter, rupture, fragment, damage, impair | antonyms: mend, repair, heal | verb
