fewer:/ˈfjuːər/| determiner, pronoun |更少的，较少的|Used with plural nouns to indicate a smaller number than another.  It is the comparative form of "few."|There are fewer students in the class today than yesterday. 今天班上的学生比昨天少。|近义词：less (often used incorrectly with plural nouns), smaller|反义词：more|词性:限定词，代词
