monotremes:/ˈmɒnətriːmz/ | n. | 单孔目动物 | Mammals that lay eggs, such as the echidna and the platypus. |  The echidna and the platypus are monotremes.  针鼹和鸭嘴兽都是单孔目动物。 | synonyms: prototheria | antonyms: (no direct antonym, could consider placental mammals or marsupials as contrasting groups) | noun


Etymology: From Greek *mónos* "single" + *trēma* "hole," referring to the single opening for both excretion and reproduction.
