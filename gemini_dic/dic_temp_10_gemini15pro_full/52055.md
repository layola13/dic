dialogging:/ˈdaɪəlɒɡɪŋ/ /ˈdaɪəˌlɔːɡɪŋ/
verb
对话，进行对话
engaging in conversation or discussion, typically to explore a particular issue or reach a shared understanding.
例句：They were dialogging about the future of the company. 他们在就公司的未来进行对话。

例句：The two sides are dialogging to find a peaceful solution. 双方正在对话以寻求和平解决方案。

近义词：conversing, discussing, communicating, negotiating
反义词：arguing, disputing, fighting, ignoring
词性：v.
