half-closed:/ˌhɑːf ˈkloʊzd/ (US) /ˌhɑːf ˈkləʊzd/ (UK)| adj.|半闭的，半合上的|Partially closed; not fully open or shut.  Describes something that is in a state between fully open and fully closed.|The curtains were half-closed, letting in a sliver of light. 窗帘半掩着，透进一丝光线。|The cat sat with half-closed eyes, enjoying the sun. 猫半闭着眼睛，享受着阳光。|nearly closed, partly open|wide open, fully closed|adjective
