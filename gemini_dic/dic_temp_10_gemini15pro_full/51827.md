dewire:/diˈwaɪər/
verb
拆除线路，去除导线；去除（炸弹等的）引信；使摆脱控制，使脱离束缚
To remove the wires from something, especially a bomb or explosive device; to free someone from the influence or control of something.
The bomb squad carefully dewired the explosive device.  拆弹小组小心翼翼地拆除了爆炸装置的引线。
The therapist helped him dewire the negative thought patterns he had developed.  治疗师帮助他摆脱了形成的消极思维模式。
近义词:disconnect, dismantle, disable, neutralize
反义词:wire, connect, arm
词性:v.
