hammily:/ˈhæmɪli/| adv.| 过分戏剧化地，做作地|Overly theatrical or dramatic in behavior or speech; affectedly.| He delivered his lines hammily, rolling his eyes and gesturing wildly. 他夸张地念着台词，翻着白眼，疯狂地做着手势。|近义词： theatrically, dramatically, affectedly, pretentiously|反义词： naturally, sincerely, genuinely|词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From *ham* (actor) + *-ily* (adverbial suffix).  "Ham," meaning a bad actor, originated in the late 19th century, possibly from a minstrel song called "The Ham-Fat Man."
