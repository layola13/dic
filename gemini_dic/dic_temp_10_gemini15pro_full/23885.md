break-the-back-of: /breɪk ðə bæk əv/
|idiomatic phrase|完成最困难的部分；克服主要障碍|To complete the most difficult or time-consuming part of a task; to overcome the main obstacle.|The team worked late into the night to break the back of the project.  团队工作到深夜，以完成项目最艰巨的部分。|
|Synonyms: make a major dent in, overcome the worst of, get past the hump, turn the corner|Antonyms: prolong, exacerbate, worsen, hinder|phrase|

break-the-back-of: /breɪk ðə bæk əv/
|idiomatic phrase|严重削弱；使元气大伤|To severely weaken or cripple something; to deal a major blow.|The recession broke the back of the local economy. 经济衰退使当地经济元气大伤。|
|Synonyms: cripple, devastate, undermine, enfeeble|Antonyms: strengthen, bolster, fortify, invigorate|phrase|
