niches:/ˈnɪtʃɪz/ /ˈniːʃɪz/| n. |壁龛；合适的位置；商机；生态位|A shallow recess, especially one in a wall to display a statue or other ornament. A comfortable or suitable position in life or employment. A specialized segment of the market for a particular kind of product or service. The role or position of an organism or population within its community or ecosystem.|
例句：She found her niche in the fashion industry. 她找到了自己在时尚界的位置。
例句：The company has carved out a niche for itself in the luxury goods market.  这家公司在奢侈品市场开拓出了一片天地。


近义词：place, position, slot, corner, market, segment|
反义词：misplacement|
词性: noun
