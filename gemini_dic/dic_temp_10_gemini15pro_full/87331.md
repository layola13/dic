hero-worshipping:/ˈhɪəroʊ ˌwɜːrʃɪpɪŋ/| adj.|英雄崇拜的，盲目崇拜的|Excessively admiring someone, often treating them as a perfect or infallible figure.  A feeling or attitude of excessive admiration towards someone.|例句：He has a hero-worshipping attitude towards his favorite athlete.  他对最喜欢的运动员有一种英雄崇拜的态度。|近义词：idolatrous, adulatory, worshipful|反义词：critical, skeptical, cynical|词性:形容词

hero-worship:/ˈhɪəroʊ ˌwɜːrʃɪp/| n.|英雄崇拜|The excessive admiration of someone.|例句：The fans' hero-worship of the rock star was intense.  歌迷对摇滚明星的崇拜非常狂热。|近义词：idolatry, adulation|反义词：disdain, contempt|词性:名词

词源：The word "hero-worship" combines "hero" (from the Greek "hērōs") and "worship" (from Old English "weorðscipe," meaning "worth" or "honor").
