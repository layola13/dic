leaser:/ˈliːsər/| n.|出租人；租赁者|A person or company that leases property, especially real estate, to another.|The leaser retains ownership of the property while the lessee pays for its use.  出租人保留财产的所有权，而承租人支付使用费。|Example: The leaser required a security deposit before handing over the keys. 出租人要求在交钥匙之前支付保证金。

Example:  The leaser and the lessee negotiated the terms of the lease agreement. 出租人和承租人协商了租赁协议的条款。|Synonym: lessor, landlord (for real estate)|Antonym: lessee, tenant (for real estate)|noun
