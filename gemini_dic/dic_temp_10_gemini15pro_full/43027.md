cower:/ˈkaʊər/ (US), /ˈkaʊə(r)/ (UK)| vi.| 畏缩，蜷缩，退缩|To crouch or shrink down in fear.|The dog cowered under the table during the thunderstorm.  狗在雷雨中畏缩在桌子底下。|
例句：
The child cowered in the corner, afraid of the dark. 孩子害怕黑暗，蜷缩在角落里。
The witness cowered before the aggressive lawyer.  证人在咄咄逼人的律师面前退缩了。
|近义词：cringe, shrink, recoil, flinch|反义词:  brave, defy, face, stand up to|词性: verb
