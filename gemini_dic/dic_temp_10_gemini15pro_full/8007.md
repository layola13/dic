anthozoan:/ˌænθəˈzoʊən/ | n. | 珊瑚虫纲动物，珊瑚虫 | An anthozoan is a member of the class Anthozoa, which includes corals and sea anemones. 珊瑚虫是珊瑚虫纲的成员，包括珊瑚和海葵。 |  Coral reefs are built by colonies of anthozoans. 珊瑚礁是由珊瑚虫群体建造的。 | The Great Barrier Reef is home to a diverse range of anthozoans. 大堡礁是各种珊瑚虫的家园。 | synonyms: coral, sea anemone | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a taxonomic classification) | noun
