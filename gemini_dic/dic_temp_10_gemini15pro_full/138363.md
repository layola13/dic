notify:/ˈnoʊtɪfaɪ/ (US) /ˈnəʊtɪfaɪ/ (UK)| vt.|通知，告知|to inform someone officially about something|例句：The school notified parents of the upcoming field trip. 学校通知了家长即将到来的实地考察。

例句：Please notify me of any changes to the schedule.  如有任何时间表变动，请通知我。

近义词：inform, advise, apprise, tell|反义词: withhold, conceal|词性:动词
