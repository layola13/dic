distally:/ˈdɪstəli/| adv.|末梢地，远端地| Situated away from the center of the body or from the point of attachment.|The rash extended distally down the arm. 皮疹沿着手臂向远端延伸。|近义词：peripherally, outwardly|反义词: proximally, centrally|词性:副词
Etymology: From Late Latin *distalis, from Latin distare (“to stand apart, be distant”), from dis- (“apart”) + stare (“to stand”).
