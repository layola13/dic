ballistite:/ˈbælɪstaɪt/ | n. |巴里斯太火药; 无烟火药 |A smokeless propellant, typically composed of nitrocellulose and nitroglycerin. | Ballistite was one of the first smokeless powders, offering a significant advantage over black powder. 巴里斯太火药是最早的无烟火药之一，比黑火药具有显著优势。|  Ballistite is still used in some applications today. 巴里斯太火药今天仍在某些应用中使用。 | synonyms: smokeless powder, propellant | antonyms: black powder | noun
