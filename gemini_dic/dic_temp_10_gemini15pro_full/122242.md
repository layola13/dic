miszone:/mɪsˈzoʊn/
verb
错误地划分区域;划分错误的区域
To zone incorrectly; to assign to an incorrect zone.
The city council miszoned the property as residential when it should have been commercial. 市议会将该房产错误地划分为住宅区，而它本应该是商业区。
misclassify, misallocate
correctly zone
