inalienability:/ɪnˌeɪliənəˈbɪləti/| n.|不可剥夺性；不可分割性|The quality of being unable to be taken away or given away by the possessor.|The inalienability of human rights is a cornerstone of modern democracy. 人权的不可剥夺性是现代民主的基石。|inalienable right, non-transferability|transferability, alienability|noun
