ill-bred:/ˌɪlˈbrɛd/
adjective
没有教养的，粗鲁的
badly brought up; impolite.
His ill-bred behavior embarrassed everyone at the dinner party. 他粗鲁的行为让宴会上每个人都感到尴尬。
近义词:rude, impolite, unmannerly, discourteous, disrespectful
反义词:well-bred, polite, courteous, respectful, well-mannered
词性:adjective

ill-bred:/ˌɪlˈbrɛd/
词源: From ill + bred (past participle of breed).  "Ill" here means "badly" or "poorly," and "bred" refers to upbringing and training.  Therefore, "ill-bred" literally means "badly raised."
词性:adjective
中文翻译:没有教养的，粗鲁的
英文解析:badly brought up; impolite; lacking in good manners.  Refers to someone who has not been taught proper social behavior.
例句:
The ill-bred child threw a tantrum in the restaurant.  这个没有教养的孩子在餐厅里大发脾气。

Her ill-bred comments shocked the guests. 她粗鲁的评论让客人们感到震惊。
