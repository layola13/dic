cabrilla:/kəˈbriːə/ | n. | 石斑鱼；加利福尼亚石斑鱼 | A type of rockfish found in the Pacific Ocean, particularly off the coast of California.  |  例句：The cabrilla is a popular game fish. 石斑鱼是一种很受欢迎的游钓鱼。

例句：We caught a large cabrilla while fishing off the coast. 我们在离岸捕鱼时捕获了一条大石斑鱼。 | 近义词：rockfish, sea bass | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific fish) | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *cabrilla*, diminutive of *cabra* "goat," referring to the fish's sometimes "goat-like" stubbornness when caught.
