aisleless:/ˈaɪl.ləs/
adj.
无通道的；没有走道的
Having no aisles.
The warehouse was aisleless, making it difficult to navigate.  这个仓库没有通道，使得通行很困难。
The open-plan office was aisleless, encouraging collaboration. 开放式办公室没有走道，鼓励合作。
近义词: unobstructed, open-plan
反义词: aisled


ETYMOLOGY->
From aisle + -less.  Aisle (from Old French ele "wing (of a church)") + -less (suffix meaning "without").
