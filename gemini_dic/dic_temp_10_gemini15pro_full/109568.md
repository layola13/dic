let oneself go: /lɛt wʌnˈsɛlf ɡoʊ/
verb phrase
放纵自己；尽情享受；不拘礼节
to relax completely and enjoy yourself, behaving in a free and natural way
After the exams, we all let ourselves go and had a huge party.  考试结束后，我们都放纵自己，开了个盛大的派对。
She really let herself go on the dance floor. 她在舞池里尽情舞动。
近义词: indulge oneself, unwind, loosen up, let loose
反义词: control oneself, restrain oneself, behave oneself
词性: verb phrase

let oneself go: /lɛt wʌnˈsɛlf ɡoʊ/
verb phrase
变得邋遢；不修边幅
to stop caring about your appearance and how you behave
He's really let himself go since his wife left him. 自从妻子离开后，他真的变得不修边幅了。
She used to be so glamorous, but she's really let herself go lately. 她以前很迷人，但最近真的变得邋遢了。
近义词: neglect oneself, become slovenly
反义词: take care of oneself, groom oneself
词性: verb phrase
