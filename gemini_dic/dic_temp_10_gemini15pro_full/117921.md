meltingness:/ˈmɛltɪŋnɪs/| n. |熔化性，融化度，熔化状态|The quality or state of being meltable; meltability.|The meltingness of the wax was crucial for the candle making process. 蜡的熔化性对于蜡烛制作过程至关重要。|近义词：meltability, fusibility|反义词：infusibility, solidity|词性：名词

Etymology: From "melt" + "-ing" + "-ness". "Melt" comes from Old English "meltan," meaning "to melt, dissolve, digest."  "-ing" is a suffix forming present participles and verbal nouns. "-ness" is a suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality.
