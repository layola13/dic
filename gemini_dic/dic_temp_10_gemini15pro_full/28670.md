carino:/kaˈriːno/| adj.|可爱的，亲爱的，讨人喜欢的|Charming, dear, sweet, cute. Used as a term of endearment, especially in Italian and Spanish.|例句：He gave her a carino smile. 他给了她一个可爱的微笑。|近义词：cute, lovely, sweet, adorable, charming|反义词：unpleasant, nasty, ugly|词性:形容词
词源: From Italian carino, diminutive of caro (“dear, expensive”). Ultimately from Latin cārus.
