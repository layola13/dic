meticulous:/məˈtɪkjələs/| adj. |一丝不苟的，极其细致的|Showing great attention to detail; very careful and precise.|He is meticulous in his work, checking every detail. 他工作一丝不苟，每个细节都检查。|近义词：precise, exact, scrupulous, careful|反义词：careless, negligent, sloppy|Adjective
