angelfishes:/ˈeɪndʒəlˌfɪʃɪz/| n.|神仙鱼|A group of brightly colored, perciform fish found in tropical reefs, known for their compressed bodies and elongated fins.  They belong to the family Pomacanthidae.|例句：The angelfishes swam gracefully through the coral.  神仙鱼优雅地在珊瑚中游动。

例句：We saw a school of angelfishes while snorkeling. 我们在浮潜时看到了一群神仙鱼。|近义词：butterflyfish, surgeonfish|反义词:None|词性:Noun
