canings:/ˈkeɪnɪŋz/
noun
鞭笞; 杖刑
The act of hitting someone with a cane as a punishment.
The headmaster gave the boy six canings for stealing. 校长因男孩偷窃而对他进行了六次鞭打。
flogging, whipping, beating
reprimand, scolding
