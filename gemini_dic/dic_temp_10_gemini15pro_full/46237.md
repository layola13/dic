cystotome:/ˈsɪstətəʊm/| n. |囊刀，晶状体囊膜切开刀|A surgical instrument used to cut a circular opening in the anterior capsule of the lens of the eye, especially in cataract surgery.  It is typically used to initiate the removal of a cataractous lens. |The surgeon used a cystotome to begin the cataract surgery.|Synonym:capsulotome|Antonym: N/A|noun
