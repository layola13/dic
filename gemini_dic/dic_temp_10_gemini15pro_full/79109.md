gladdening:/ˈɡlædnɪŋ/| adj.|令人高兴的，使人欢喜的|Causing or expressing joy and pleasure; cheering.|Her gladdening smile lit up the room. 她的笑容照亮了整个房间，令人愉悦。|Synonyms: cheering, heartening, exhilarating, uplifting|Antonyms: saddening, depressing, disheartening|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *glædnung, from glædnian "to make glad," from glæd "glad."
