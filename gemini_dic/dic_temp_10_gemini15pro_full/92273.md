hyperviscosity:/ˌhaɪpərˌvɪsˈkɒsɪti/ /ˌhaɪpərˌvɪsˈkɑːsɪti/| n. |高黏度，高黏滞性|The state of being highly viscous; excessive viscosity.  It refers to a condition where a fluid (like blood) is thicker than normal. |The patient's symptoms suggested hyperviscosity syndrome.  病人的症状表明是高黏滞综合征。|Synonyms:  increased viscosity, thickening|Antonyms: hypoviscosity, fluidity|Noun
