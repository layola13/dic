guideway:/ˈɡaɪdweɪ/
n.
导轨，导槽；轨道
a track or other structure along which something such as a train or other vehicle travels
The train derailed because a section of the guideway was damaged. 火车出轨是因为一段导轨受损。

The monorail glides smoothly along its guideway. 单轨列车沿着轨道平稳滑行。

track, rail, line, path
road, street
