nonaccompanying:/ˌnɒnəkʌmpəniɪŋ/
adjective
非陪伴的，不陪同的
Not accompanying someone or something; traveling or attending something alone.
The nonaccompanying baggage will be sent separately.  非随身行李将单独发送。
Children under 12 are not allowed to travel nonaccompanying. 12岁以下的儿童不允许单独旅行。
近义词: unaccompanied, solo
反义词: accompanying, escorted
词性: adjective
