mistiest:/ˈmɪstiɪst/| adj.|最雾蒙蒙的，雾气最重的|Covered with or obscured by mist, especially to a greater extent than something else.|The mistiest valley I've ever seen was in the Scottish Highlands. 我见过的雾气最浓的山谷是在苏格兰高地。|近义词：foggiest, haziest|反义词：clearest, brightest|词性:形容词
词源: From mist + -y + -est.  Ultimately from Old English *mist "mist."
