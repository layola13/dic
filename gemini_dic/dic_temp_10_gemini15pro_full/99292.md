intercorpuscular:/ˌɪntərˌkɔːrˈpʌskjʊlər/
adjective
微粒间的，粒子间的
Existing or occurring between corpuscles or small particles.
例句：Intercorpuscular forces hold the molecules together. 微粒间的力将分子结合在一起。

例句：The intercorpuscular spaces in some materials are very small.  一些材料中的微粒间隙非常小。

近义词：intermolecular, interatomic
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but could consider "intracorpuscular" meaning within a corpuscle)
词性: adjective
