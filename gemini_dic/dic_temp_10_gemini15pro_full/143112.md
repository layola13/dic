outbulges:/ˌaʊtˈbʌldʒɪz/
| n. |凸起部分，鼓起部分|Something that bulges outwards.|The outbulges of the rock face made it difficult to climb.|近义词：projections, protuberances, swellings|反义词：indentation, depression, concavity|词性:名词

outbulge:/ˌaʊtˈbʌldʒ/
| v. |向外凸出，膨胀|To bulge outwards.|The muscles in his arms outbulged as he lifted the heavy weight.|近义词：protrude, project, swell|反义词：recede, contract, shrink|词性:动词


词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "outward") + "bulge" (from Middle English "bulgen," of uncertain origin, perhaps related to Old Norse "bolginn," meaning "swollen").
