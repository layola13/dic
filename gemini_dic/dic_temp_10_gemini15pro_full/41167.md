copycat:/ˈkɒpiˌkæt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpiˌkæt/ (US)| n. adj.|模仿者，山寨货|A person who copies another person's actions or work, especially in a childish or annoying way.  Can also refer to a product that imitates another.|He called her a copycat when she bought the same shoes as him.|近义词：imitator, mimic, emulator|反义词：innovator, originator|noun

copycat:/ˈkɒpiˌkæt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːpiˌkæt/ (US)| adj.|模仿的，抄袭的|Made or done in imitation of something else.|That's a copycat version of a famous designer handbag.|近义词：imitative, unoriginal|反义词：original, innovative|adjective
