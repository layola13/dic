contesting:/kənˈtɛstɪŋ/ | v.| 争夺，质疑 | engaging in a competition or challenge; disputing something | They are contesting the validity of the election. 他们在质疑选举的有效性。 | Synonyms: challenging, disputing, opposing | Antonyms: accepting, conceding, agreeing | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Old French "contester" and Latin "contestari," meaning "to call to witness, to invoke as witness, to bring an action," from "com-" (together) + "testari" (to bear witness).
