broadish:/ˈbrɔːdɪʃ/| adj.|稍宽的，较宽的|Somewhat broad; moderately broad.|The table was broadish, enough to seat six people comfortably.  桌子稍宽，足以舒适地容纳六个人。| The path was broadish, allowing two people to walk side-by-side.  这条小路比较宽，可以让两个人并肩行走。|近义词：wide, ample, spacious|反义词：narrow, slender, thin|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From broad + -ish.  The suffix "-ish" is used to form adjectives meaning "somewhat" or "rather".  "Broad" itself comes from Old English "brād".
