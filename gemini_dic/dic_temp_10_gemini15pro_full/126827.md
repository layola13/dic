naphthalene:/ˈnæfθəliːn/ | n. |萘，萘烷|A white crystalline aromatic hydrocarbon produced by the distillation of coal tar, used in mothballs and in the manufacture of dyes, resins, and insecticides.|Mothballs are made of naphthalene. 樟脑丸是由萘制成的。|Synonyms:  mothballs, white tar, tar camphor|Antonyms: None|Noun
