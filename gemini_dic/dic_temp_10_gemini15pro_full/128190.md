neurapophysial:/ˌnʊərəpəˈfɪziəl/
adjective
神经垂体的
Relating to or originating in the posterior lobe of the pituitary gland (neurohypophysis).
The neurapophysial hormones are vasopressin and oxytocin. 神经垂体激素是血管加压素和催产素。
Synonyms: neurohypophysial
Antonyms: adenohypophysial
词性: adjective
