gnomelike:/ˈnoʊmˌlaɪk/
adjective
像侏儒的；矮小的；奇异古怪的
Resembling a gnome, especially in being small, dwarflike, or grotesque.
The old man had a gnomelike appearance with his long white beard and pointed hat.  这位老人留着长长的白胡子和尖尖的帽子，看起来像个侏儒。
He peered at me with a gnomelike intensity. 他用侏儒般专注的眼神盯着我。
近义词:dwarfish, elfin, pixieish
反义词:giantlike, enormous, huge
词性:adjective
