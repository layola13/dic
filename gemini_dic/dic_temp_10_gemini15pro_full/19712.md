bilingual:/baɪˈlɪŋɡwəl/ | adj. n. | 双语的；能说两种语言的人 |Speaking two languages fluently. Able to communicate effectively in two languages. | She is bilingual in English and Spanish. 她精通英语和西班牙语。| synonyms: multilingual, fluent in two languages | antonyms: monolingual | adjective, noun
bilingualism:/baɪˈlɪŋɡwəlɪzəm/ | n. | 双语现象 | The ability to use two languages. The practice of using two languages. | Bilingualism is common in many parts of the world. 双语现象在世界很多地方都很普遍。| synonyms: multilingualism | antonyms: monolingualism | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From bi- (two) + lingual (relating to language).  Ultimately from Latin *bi- (two) and lingua (tongue, language).
