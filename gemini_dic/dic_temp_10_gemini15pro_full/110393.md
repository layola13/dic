ligulate:/ˈlɪɡjʊleɪt/ /ˈlɪɡjəleɪt/
adjective
舌状的；带舌片的；舌形的
Having the shape of a strap or tongue.  Relating to or resembling a ligule, a small, strap-shaped outgrowth at the junction of the leaf and leafstalk in many grasses and other plants.

The ligulate leaves of the dandelion are easily recognizable.
蒲公英的舌状叶很容易辨认。

The petals of the daisy are ligulate.
雏菊的花瓣是舌状的。

近义词:strap-shaped, tongue-shaped
反义词: (No clear antonym, perhaps "ovate" or other leaf shape descriptors depending on context)
词性: adjective
