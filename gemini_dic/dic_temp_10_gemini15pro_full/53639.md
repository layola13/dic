discerpibility:/dɪˌsɜːrpɪˈbɪləti/
n.
可辨别性，可识别性
The quality of being discerpible; discerpibleness.  The ability to be distinguished or perceived as separate.
The discerpibility of the individual stars in the cluster is difficult due to the atmospheric conditions.  由于大气条件的原因，很难辨别星团中的单个恒星。
近义词:distinguishability, perceptibility, differentiability
反义词:indistinguishability, imperceptibility
词性:名词


discerpible:/dɪˈsɜːrpɪbl/
adj.
可辨别的，可识别的
Capable of being discerned; distinguishable; perceptible.
The slight difference in shade was barely discerpible.  色调的细微差别几乎难以辨认。
近义词:distinguishable, perceptible, noticeable
反义词:indistinguishable, imperceptible, unnoticeable
词性:形容词
