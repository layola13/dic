bicorn:/ˈbaɪkɔːrn/ | adj. n. | 两角的；两角帽；双角形 | Having two horns or horn-like projections.  Also, a hat with two points, often worn by bishops or academic dignitaries. |  The bicorn hat was a popular style in the 18th century.  十八世纪，双角帽是一种流行的款式。| The moon sometimes appears bicorn when partially obscured by clouds.  当被云部分遮蔽时，月亮有时看起来像有两个角。 | Synonyms: two-horned, crescent-shaped (for the moon), cocked hat | Antonyms: single-horned, round (for the moon) | adjective, noun
