clumsiness:/ˈklʌmzɪnəs/| n.|笨拙，笨手笨脚|The state of being clumsy; awkwardness in movement or handling things.|Her clumsiness led to the vase being knocked over. 她的笨拙导致花瓶被打翻了。|近义词：awkwardness, gaucheness, ineptitude|反义词:grace, dexterity, skill|词性:名词
词源:From clumsy + -ness.  Clumsy itself comes from Middle English *clumsen, from Old Norse *klumsa (“to be numb with cold”), ultimately from Proto-Germanic *klum- (“lump, clod”).
