nacre:/ˈneɪkər/ | n. |珍珠母，珍珠层| Iridescent inner layer of a mollusk shell, also known as mother-of-pearl. | The artisan carefully polished the nacre to reveal its shimmering beauty. 工匠小心翼翼地打磨珍珠母，展现其闪耀的光彩。| The inside of the abalone shell shimmered with nacre.  鲍鱼壳的内部闪耀着珍珠母的光泽。| synonyms: mother-of-pearl, pearl-shell | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps dull shell) | noun
