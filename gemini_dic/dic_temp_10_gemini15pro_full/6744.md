analytically:/ˌænəˈlɪtɪkli/ | adv. | 分析地；解析地 |  In a way that separates something into its parts in order to understand it; using analysis. |  She examined the data analytically, looking for patterns and trends. 她分析地检查了数据，寻找模式和趋势。|  The problem can be approached analytically. 这个问题可以分析地处理。 |Synonyms: logically, rationally, methodically, systematically | Antonyms: intuitively, instinctively, emotionally | 词性: 副词
