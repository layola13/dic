eminence:/ˈɛmɪnəns/| n.|显赫，卓越，高地|a position of high status or distinction|例句：His Eminence, the Cardinal, will preside over the mass.  枢机主教阁下将主持弥撒。|近义词：distinction, prominence, renown, fame|反义词：obscurity, insignificance|noun

eminence:/ˈɛmɪnəns/| n.|高处，高地|a high point of land that projects from its surroundings|例句：The castle was built on an eminence overlooking the valley. 这座城堡建在俯瞰山谷的高地上。|近义词：hill, elevation, summit|反义词：valley, depression|noun
