cantillation:/ˌkæntɪˈleɪʃən/ | n. |吟诵，吟唱;抑扬顿挫的语调|A rhythmic chanting of sacred texts, often in accordance with a system of musical notation.  It also refers to the system of such notation itself.|The cantor's cantillation of the prayers was mesmerizing.  领唱者的祈祷吟诵令人着迷。|近义词：chant, intonation, recitation|反义词: monotone|词性:noun
