auntre:/ˈɑːntri/ |n.| 洞穴，岩洞 |A natural underground chamber or series of chambers, typically of large or indefinite extent. | 例句：The explorers discovered a vast auntre filled with ancient artifacts. 探险家们发现了一个巨大的洞穴，里面充满了古代文物。

例句：Bats often make their homes in dark auntres.  蝙蝠经常在黑暗的洞穴里安家。

近义词：cave, cavern, grotto | 反义词：surface, exterior | 词性：名词
