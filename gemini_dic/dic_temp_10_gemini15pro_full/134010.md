nonhostile:/nɒnˈhɒstaɪl/ (British), /nɑːnˈhɑːstl/ (American)| adj.|非敌对的，不怀敌意的|Not hostile; friendly or neutral.  Characterized by the absence of hostility.|例句：The two countries maintained a nonhostile relationship despite their political differences. 这两个国家尽管存在政治分歧，但仍保持着非敌对关系。|例句：The ambassador emphasized the nonhostile nature of the visit. 大使强调了此次访问的非敌对性质。|近义词：friendly, amicable, peaceful, neutral|反义词：hostile, aggressive, belligerent|词性:形容词
词源:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "hostile" (from Latin "hostilis," meaning "of an enemy")
