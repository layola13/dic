half-willfully:/ˌhæfˈwɪlfəli/
| adv. |半故意地，有点故意地|Acting in a way that is partly intentional, but also partly not fully conscious or deliberate.  A blend of "half" and "willfully".|He half-willfully ignored the warning signs. 他有点故意地忽略了警告标志。|She half-willfully let the door slam shut. 她有点故意地让门砰地关上了。|Synonyms: partly deliberately, semi-intentionally, almost purposely|Antonyms: unintentionally, accidentally, involuntarily|adverb
