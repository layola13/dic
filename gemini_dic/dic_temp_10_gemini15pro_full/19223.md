bezoardic:/ˌbezɔːˈɑːrdɪk/
| adj.|毛粪石的；似毛粪石的|Relating to or resembling a bezoar (a mass of undigested material found in the stomach or intestines).|The goat produced a large bezoardic mass. 这只山羊产生了一大块毛粪石。|近义词: calculous|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: adjective


bezoar:/ˈbiːzɔːr/
| n.|毛粪石（胃或肠中未消化的物质团块）|A mass of undigested material found in the stomach or intestines, especially of a ruminant.|The veterinarian removed a bezoar from the cow's stomach. 兽医从牛的胃里取出了一个毛粪石。|近义词: calculus|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性: noun

词源:

From French *bézoard*, from Persian *pādzahr* "antidote, that which expels poison," from *pā* "protection" + * zahr* "poison."
