hoared:/hɔːrd/ /hɔrd/| adj.|白发的，灰白的，古老的|Covered with white or gray hair, especially due to age; ancient or venerable.|例句：The hoared old man sat by the fire, telling stories of his youth. 白发苍苍的老人坐在火炉旁，讲述着他年轻时的故事。

例句：The hoared walls of the castle stood testament to centuries of history.  城堡斑驳的墙壁见证了几个世纪的历史。|近义词：gray, white, ancient, venerable, old|反义词:young, new, youthful|词性:形容词

词源: From Old English *hāriġ, from Proto-Germanic *hairaz (“gray, hoary”). Cognate with Old High German her (“venerable, sublime, gray”), Old Norse hárr (“hoary”), Gothic 𐌷𐌰𐌹𐍂𐍃 (hairs, “hoary”). Related to hair.
