levelling-off:/ˈlɛvəlɪŋ ˈɒf/ (UK) /ˈlɛvəlɪŋ ˈɔːf/ (US)| n.|趋平；稳定；平稳状态|The levelling-off of sales figures suggests the market is saturated. 销售数字的稳定表明市场已经饱和。|例句：After a period of rapid growth, the company experienced a levelling-off of profits. 经历一段时期的快速增长后，公司的利润趋于稳定。

例句：The levelling-off in the number of new cases is a positive sign. 新增病例数量的平稳是一个积极的信号。|近义词：stabilization, plateau, flattening|反义词：increase, rise, surge|词性:noun
