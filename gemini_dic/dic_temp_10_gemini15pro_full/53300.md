disruption:/dɪsˈrʌpʃən/
n.
中断，扰乱
a disturbance or interruption of an event, activity, or process.
The construction caused a major disruption to traffic.  施工造成了严重的交通中断。
The pandemic caused widespread disruption to education.  疫情对教育造成了广泛的干扰。
interruption, disturbance, disorder, chaos
continuity, order, stability
noun
