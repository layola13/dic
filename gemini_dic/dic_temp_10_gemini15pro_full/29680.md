cathartic:/kəˈθɑːrtɪk/
adjective
宣泄的；净化心灵的
Providing psychological relief through the open expression of strong emotions; causing catharsis.
The movie was a cathartic experience for her, allowing her to release pent-up emotions. 这部电影对她来说是一次宣泄的经历，让她释放了压抑的情绪。
近义词: cleansing, purifying, therapeutic
反义词: repressive, suppressive
词性: adjective

catharsis:/kəˈθɑːrsɪs/
noun
宣泄；净化
The process of releasing, and thereby providing relief from, strong or repressed emotions.  
Writing poetry can be a form of catharsis for some people. 写诗对某些人来说是一种情感宣泄的方式。
近义词: release, purgation, venting
反义词: repression, suppression
词性: noun
