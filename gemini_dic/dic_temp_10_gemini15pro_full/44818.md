crushability:/ˌkrʌʃəˈbɪləti/
n.|可压碎性；易碎性|The quality of being easily crushed or broken.  The crushability of the material makes it unsuitable for packaging.|The crushability of the biscuit was perfect, not too hard and not too soft.  饼干的酥脆度恰到好处，既不太硬也不太软。|fragility, friability|durability, strength|noun
