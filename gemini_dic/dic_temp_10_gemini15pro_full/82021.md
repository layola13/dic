gripping:/ˈɡrɪpɪŋ/| adj.|扣人心弦的，引人入胜的，紧张的|Holding the attention firmly; exciting.|The gripping finale had the audience on the edge of their seats. 扣人心弦的结局让观众坐在座位的边缘。|Synonyms: enthralling, captivating, riveting, compelling, fascinating|Antonyms: boring, tedious, dull, uninteresting|adjective

gripping:/ˈɡrɪpɪŋ/| n.|紧握，抓牢|The act of holding something firmly.|His gripping of the steering wheel tightened as the car skidded. 汽车打滑时，他紧握方向盘的手更紧了。|Synonyms: grasp, clutch, hold|Antonyms: release, loosen|noun
