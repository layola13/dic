foundationally:/faʊnˈdeɪʃənəli/
adv.
从根本上，基础地
In a foundational way; fundamentally.  Referring to the foundation or basis of something.
This theory is foundationally flawed.  这个理论从根本上来说是有缺陷的。
Synonyms: fundamentally, basically, essentially, radically
Antonyms: superficially, outwardly
词性:副词
