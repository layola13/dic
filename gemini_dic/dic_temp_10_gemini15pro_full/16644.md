barque:/bɑːrk/| n.|三桅帆船，轻舟 |A sailing ship with three or more masts, the aftmost of which (the mizzenmast) is fore-and-aft rigged and the others are square-rigged.|The old barque sailed majestically across the ocean.  这艘古老的三桅帆船雄赳赳地航行在大洋上。|近义词：ship, vessel, sailing ship|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可考虑 motorboat 机动船)|名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *barque* (modern French *barque*), from Occitan *barca*, from Vulgar Latin *barca*, from Late Latin *barca*, from Greek *βάρις* (baris, “Egyptian boat”).
