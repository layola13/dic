interlaminated:/ˌɪntərˈlæmɪneɪtɪd/
adjective
相互层压的，交叠的
composed of layers of different materials bonded together.
The table top was made of interlaminated wood and plastic.  桌子面板是由木材和塑料交叠层压制成的。
The material is strong and lightweight because it's interlaminated. 这种材料又结实又轻便，因为它采用了交叠层压结构。
近义词: laminated, layered, stratified
反义词: homogeneous, uniform
词性: adjective
