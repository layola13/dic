broguery: /ˈbroʊɡəri/ (US), /ˈbrɒɡəri/ (UK)| n.| 炫耀, 卖弄, 夸夸其谈| an ostentatious display of something, especially one's knowledge or learning; bragging, boasting. | He displayed his extensive knowledge of wine with unnecessary broguery. 他不必要地炫耀了他渊博的葡萄酒知识。| The professor's broguery about his latest research bored the students. 教授炫耀他最新研究的行为让学生们感到无聊。|近义词: ostentation, pretension, affectation, bragging, boasting|反义词: modesty, humility, understatement|词性: noun
