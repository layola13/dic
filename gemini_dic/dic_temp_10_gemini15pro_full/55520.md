distressingly:/dɪˈstrɛsɪŋli/
adverb|令人苦恼地，令人担忧地|in a way that causes worry or anxiety|The poverty in the city is distressingly widespread.  这座城市的贫困状况令人担忧地普遍存在。|disturbingly, alarmingly, worryingly|comfortingly, reassuringly|词性: 副词

distressingly:/dɪˈstrɛsɪŋli/
Etymology: From distress + -ing + -ly.  Distress ultimately comes from the Latin "districtus," past participle of "distringere" meaning "to pull apart, distract."|词源: 来自distress + -ing + -ly。Distress最终来自拉丁语"districtus"，"distringere"的过去分词，意思是"拉开，分散"。
