geyseral:/ˈɡaɪzərəl/| adj.|间歇喷泉的；像间歇喷泉的|Relating to or resembling a geyser; eruptive. |The geyseral activity was spectacular, with water shooting high into the air. 间歇喷泉的活动非常壮观，水柱高高地射向空中。|The mixture created a geyseral effect, bubbling and overflowing. 这种混合物产生了间歇喷泉般的效果，不断冒泡和溢出。|近义词:eruptive, volcanic|反义词:dormant, inactive|词性:形容词


词源: From "geyser" + "-al" (suffix forming adjectives). "Geyser" itself comes from Icelandic "geysir," meaning "gusher," from "geysa" (to gush).
