anoura:/əˈnʊərə/
noun
无尾类动物（尤指蛙和蟾蜍）
Tailless amphibians, especially frogs and toads.
Anoura are found in a variety of habitats, from tropical rainforests to deserts.  无尾类动物栖息在各种各样的环境中，从热带雨林到沙漠。
Frogs and toads belong to the order Anura, also known as Anoura. 青蛙和蟾蜍属于无尾目，也称为Anoura。
近义词:Salientia, Anura
反义词:Urodela (tailed amphibians 有尾两栖动物)
词性:n.


Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ἀ(ν-) *a(n-)* (not, without) + οὐρά *ourá* (tail).
