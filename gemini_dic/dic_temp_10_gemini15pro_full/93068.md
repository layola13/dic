idolize:/ˈaɪdəlaɪz/ | vt. | 崇拜；把…当作偶像 | To admire or love someone or something very much, often to an excessive degree. |  She idolizes her older brother. 她崇拜她的哥哥。

He idolized the famous scientist. 他把这位著名的科学家当作偶像。

| 近义词: worship, adore, revere, venerate | 反义词: despise, loathe, scorn | 词性: verb
