bushfires:/ˈbʊʃfaɪərz/ (US) /ˈbʊʃfaɪəz/ (UK)
n.
丛林大火，山火
A fire that burns in scrub or a forest, especially one that spreads rapidly.
Australia is prone to bushfires, especially during the dry summer months. 澳大利亚容易发生丛林大火，尤其是在干燥的夏季。
The bushfires ravaged the landscape, leaving a trail of destruction.  丛林大火肆虐大地，留下满目疮痍。
近义词：wildfires, forest fires
反义词: controlled burn (可控燃烧)
词性: 名词 (plural noun)
