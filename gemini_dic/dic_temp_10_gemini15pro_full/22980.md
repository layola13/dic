botulism:/ˈbɒtʃʊlɪzəm/ (UK) /ˈbɑːtʃəlɪzəm/ (US)| n.|肉毒中毒|A rare but serious illness caused by a toxin produced by the bacterium Clostridium botulinum.|The doctor diagnosed him with botulism after he ate the contaminated food. 医生在他吃了受污染的食物后诊断他患有肉毒中毒。|Synonyms:  food poisoning, toxicoinfection|Antonyms: health, wellness|noun

botulinum toxin:/ˌbɒtʃʊˈlaɪnəm ˈtɒksɪn/ (UK) /ˌbɑːtʃʊˈlaɪnəm ˈtɑːksɪn/ (US)| n.|肉毒杆菌毒素|A neurotoxic protein produced by the bacterium Clostridium botulinum that causes botulism.|Botulinum toxin is one of the most potent toxins known. 肉毒杆菌毒素是已知最强的毒素之一。|Synonyms: Botox (trade name), neurotoxin|Antonyms: antitoxin|noun
