leglessness:/ˈleɡləsnəs/| n.|无腿；没有腿的状态|The leglessness of the snake allows it to move through tight spaces. 蛇没有腿，这使得它能够穿过狭窄的空间。|例句：The leglessness of the worm is an adaptation to its subterranean lifestyle.  蚯蚓的无腿状态是对其地下生活方式的一种适应。

例句：Leglessness in some lizards is a result of evolutionary adaptation. 一些蜥蜴的无腿状态是进化适应的结果。|近义词：apody, absence of legs|反义词：leggedness|词性: noun
