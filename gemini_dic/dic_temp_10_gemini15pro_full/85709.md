havoc:/ˈhævək/| n.|浩劫，大破坏，混乱| widespread destruction or devastation; chaos. |The storm wreaked havoc on the coastal towns.  暴风雨对沿海城镇造成了严重破坏。

The virus caused havoc in the computer system. 病毒使计算机系统瘫痪。|近义词：devastation, destruction, ruin, chaos, mayhem|反义词：order, peace, tranquility|词性:名词


词源: From Anglo-French *havok, from the exclamation *havok! "pillage!"
