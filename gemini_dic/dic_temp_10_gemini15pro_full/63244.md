enterprise: /ˈentərpraɪz/ (US) /ˈentəpraɪz/ (UK)| n.| 企业; 事业; 事业心; 进取心 |A project or undertaking, typically one that is difficult or requires effort.  A company or business. Initiative and resourcefulness.|
例句：
The new enterprise quickly became successful.  这个新企业很快就成功了。

She showed great enterprise in starting her own business. 她创办自己的企业展现了非凡的进取心。


近义词：business, company, venture, undertaking, initiative, resourcefulness|
反义词：inactivity, apathy, laziness|
词性：noun


enterprise: /ˈentərpraɪz/ (US) /ˈentəpraɪz/ (UK)| adj. |（组织）国营的；私营的| Relating to or denoting a privately owned business or company.|
例句：
It's an enterprise zone designed to encourage investment. 这是一个旨在鼓励投资的企业区。

The government supports enterprise initiatives. 政府支持企业倡议。


近义词：private, commercial, business|
反义词：public, government|
词性：adjective
