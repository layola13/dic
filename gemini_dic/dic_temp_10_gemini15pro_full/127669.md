nefandous:/nɪˈfændəs/| adj.|邪恶的；极坏的；可憎的|Extremely wicked or villainous; abhorrent.|例句：His nefarious deeds shocked the community. 他的恶行震惊了整个社区。|例句：The nefarious plot to overthrow the government was foiled. 推翻政府的邪恶阴谋被挫败了。|近义词：evil, wicked, heinous, atrocious, villainous|反义词：righteous, virtuous, good|词性:形容词
词源:From Latin *nefārius (“wicked, impious”), from nefās (“wrong, sin, crime”) + -ārius (adjective-forming suffix). Ultimately from ne (“not”) + fās (“divine law, right”).
