epicurean:/ˌɛpɪˈkjʊəriən/ | adj. n. |  享乐主义的；享乐主义者 | Devoted to the pursuit of sensual pleasure; a person with refined tastes in food and drink. | He led an epicurean lifestyle, indulging in fine wines and gourmet meals. 他过着享乐主义的生活方式，沉迷于美酒和美食。 | 近义词: hedonistic, sybaritic, gourmet, gourmand | 反义词: ascetic, abstemious | 词性: adjective, noun

epicurean:/ˌɛpɪˈkjʊəriən/ | n. | 享乐主义者 | A person devoted to refined sensuous enjoyment (especially good food and drink). | He is a true epicurean, always seeking out the finest restaurants. 他是一个真正的享乐主义者，总是寻找最好的餐厅。| 近义词: hedonist, sybaritic, gourmet, gourmand | 反义词: ascetic, abstemious | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *epicurēus, from Latin Epicūrus (“Epicurus”), from Ancient Greek Ἐπίκουρος (Epíkouros, “Epicurus”), the name of a Greek philosopher who advocated moderate pleasure as the highest good.  Doublet of epicure.
