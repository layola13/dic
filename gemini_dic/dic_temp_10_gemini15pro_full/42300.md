counter-mart:/ˈkaʊntərˌmɑːrt/ | n. | 对抗策略; 对抗手段;  | A countermove designed to offset or nullify another's strategy or action. |  They launched a counter-mart against the competitor's aggressive advertising campaign. 他们针对竞争对手的激进广告活动发起了一项对抗策略。 | Synonyms: countermeasure, countermove, counter-strategy | Antonyms: cooperation, collaboration | noun

counter-mart:/ˈkaʊntərˌmɑːrt/ | n. |  反击; 回击 |  A retaliatory action or measure. |  His counter-mart was swift and decisive. 他的反击迅速而果断。 | Synonyms: retaliation, reprisal, comeback | Antonyms: concession, surrender | noun
