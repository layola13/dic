heirships:/ˈɛərʃɪps/
n.
继承权；继承资格
The state or fact of being an heir; legal right of inheritance.
After his father's death, he assumed the heirships of the family estate.  在他父亲去世后，他继承了家族的遗产。
The complex laws governing heirships can be difficult to understand.  复杂的继承法很难理解。
synonym: inheritance, succession, legacy
antonym: disinheritance, forfeiture
noun
