castellated:/ˈkæstəleɪtɪd/
adjective
城垛状的；有城堡的
Having battlements and high walls like a castle.
The castellated walls of the old city are still standing.  古老城市的城垛状城墙依然矗立。
The mansion was castellated and imposing. 这座大厦有城垛，显得雄伟壮观。
近义词:crenellated, fortified, embattled
反义词:undefended, unprotected
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *castellatus, past participle of castellāre (“to fortify”), from Latin castellum (“castle”).
