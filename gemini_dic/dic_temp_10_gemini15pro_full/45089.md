cuboid:/ˈkjuːbɔɪd/| n. adj.|长方体，矩形的|A solid figure with six rectangular faces.|例句：The box was a perfect cuboid.  这个盒子是一个完美的长方体。|例句：The child was playing with wooden cuboid blocks. 孩子正在玩积木长方体。|近义词：rectangular prism, block|反义词:sphere, globe|词性:名词, 形容词
Etymology: From cubo- + -oid.  cubo- comes from Latin cubus "cube," from Greek kubos. -oid means "resembling," from Greek -oeidēs.
