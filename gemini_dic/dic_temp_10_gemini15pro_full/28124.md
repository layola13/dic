capitated:/ˈkæpɪteɪtɪd/
adjective
按人头收费的；定额补贴的
Paid a fixed amount per person.
Example: The doctor is paid on a capitated basis, receiving a set fee for each patient enrolled in the plan. 医生按人头收费，每位参加该计划的患者都收取固定费用。

Example: Capitated payments encourage healthcare providers to focus on preventative care. 按人头付费鼓励医疗保健提供者注重预防性护理。

近义词: per capita, fixed-fee
反义词: fee-for-service


词性: adjective
词源: From Latin "caput" (head) + "-ated" (forming adjectives), implying a fixed amount "per head."
