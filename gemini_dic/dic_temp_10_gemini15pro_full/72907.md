foment:/ˈfoʊment/ (US) /fəˈment/ (UK)| v. |煽动，助长，激起|To instigate or stir up (an undesirable or violent sentiment or course of action).|例句：The politician’s speeches fomented unrest in the crowd. 政治家的演讲煽动了人群的不安。

例句：The rebels fomented revolution against the government.  叛乱分子煽动了反对政府的革命。

|近义词：incite, instigate, provoke, stir up, agitate|反义词：quell, suppress, allay, calm, soothe|词性:动词
