heediness:/ˈhiːdinəs/| n. |注意，留心，谨慎|The state of being heedful; attentiveness; cautiousness.|His heediness prevented a serious accident. 他的谨慎避免了一场严重的事故。|近义词：carefulness, caution, vigilance, attention, mindfulness|反义词：carelessness, negligence, recklessness, inattention|词性:名词

Etymology: From Middle English *hedinesse*, equivalent to *heed* + *-ness*.  Ultimately from Old English *hēdan* "to heed, observe, protect".
