gie: /ɡiː/ /dʒiː/
词性: n.
中文翻译:  活力; 精力; 干劲 (Scottish)
英文解析: Energy; spirit; vigour. Primarily used in Scottish English.
例句: He's got plenty of gie. 他精力充沛。
例句:  She put some real gie into her dancing. 她跳舞很有活力。
近义词: energy, vigour, pep, zest, drive, spirit
反义词: lethargy, apathy, listlessness
词性: n.
