nailers:/ˈneɪlərz/| n.|钉子工，钉钉机|People who work with nails, especially those who use nail guns, are often called nailers.  Also refers to the tools themselves (nail guns).|The construction crew hired several experienced nailers to finish the roofing. 建筑队雇佣了几个经验丰富的钉子工来完成屋顶工程。|The nailers jammed, causing a delay in the project.  钉枪卡住了，导致项目延误。|Synonyms: nail gun operators, carpenters, builders (when referring to people), nail guns, staplers (when referring to tools)|Antonyms:  None (for people);  remover (for tools, referring to the opposite function)|noun
