ceilidh:/ˈkeɪli/ (UK) /ˈkeɪli/ (US)| n. |凯利舞会；（苏格兰或爱尔兰的）传统社交聚会|A ceilidh is a social gathering which involves traditional Gaelic folk music and dancing, particularly in Scotland and Ireland. |We went to a ceilidh last night and had a great time. 我们昨晚去了一个凯利舞会，玩得很开心。

The village hall hosts a ceilidh every month. 村公所每个月都会举办一次凯利舞会。|近义词：party, gathering, social|反义词:|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
