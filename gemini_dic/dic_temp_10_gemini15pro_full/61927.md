emulation:/ˌɛmjuˈleɪʃn/| n.|仿真；效仿；竞争|the effort to match or surpass a person or achievement, typically by imitation.|Many companies have been working on the emulation of human intelligence. 许多公司一直致力于模拟人类智能。|The two brothers are locked in constant emulation. 这两兄弟一直处于不断的竞争之中。|近义词：imitation, copying, mimicking, simulation, competition, rivalry|反义词：innovation, originality|noun
