devoted:/dɪˈvoʊtɪd/| adj.|挚爱的；忠诚的；专心的|Dedicated to a cause, activity, or person; characterized by loyalty and dedication.|例句：She is a devoted mother. 她是一位慈爱的母亲。

例句：He is devoted to his work. 他全心投入于他的工作。|近义词：dedicated, committed, loyal, faithful|反义词：unfaithful, disloyal, indifferent|词性:形容词
词源:Late Middle English: past participle of devote, from Latin devovere ‘vow solemnly,’ from de- ‘down, away’ + vovere ‘to vow.’
