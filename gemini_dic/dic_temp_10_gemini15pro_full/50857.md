deselect:/ˌdiːsəˈlekt/ | v. |取消选择，撤销选择 | To reverse the selection of; to choose not to select. |  I accidentally selected all the files, so I had to deselect them. 我不小心选择了所有文件，所以我不得不取消选择它们。| The user can deselect an option by clicking it again. 用户可以通过再次单击来取消选择一个选项。| 近义词: unselect, dechoose | 反义词: select, choose | 词性: 动词

Etymology:
de- (prefix): meaning "reverse" or "remove," from Latin.
select (verb): from Latin *selectus, past participle of seligere "to choose," from se- "apart" + legere "to gather, choose."
