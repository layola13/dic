cannoning:/ˈkænənɪŋ/
verb
峡谷漂流，溪降
the sport of travelling down fast-flowing mountain streams in canyons, usually involving jumping into pools and sliding down rocks
Example: We went cannoning in the Alps last summer.  去年夏天我们去阿尔卑斯山玩了溪降。
Example:  Canyoning is an exhilarating and challenging adventure sport. 溪降是一项令人兴奋和具有挑战性的冒险运动。
Synonyms: canyon descending, canyoneering, gorge walking
Antonyms: hiking, climbing (these are opposites in terms of the direction of travel in a mountainous environment)
词性: 动词
