clinopinacoid:/ˌklɪnoʊˈpɪnəkɔɪd/
n.
斜板
A crystal face parallel to the inclined axes and perpendicular to the vertical axis.
Clinopinacoids are common in monoclinic crystals.  斜板在单斜晶系中很常见。
Near synonyms:pinacoid
No commonly used antonyms.
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *klino* (sloping, inclined) + *pinax* (board, tablet) + *-oid* (resembling).
