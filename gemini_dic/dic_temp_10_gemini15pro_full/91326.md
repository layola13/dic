hydrophobia:/ˌhaɪdrəˈfoʊbiə/ | n. | 恐水症；狂犬病 | An abnormal and persistent dread of water.  A morbid fear of water. Also, rabies. |  Example 1:  The dog exhibited signs of hydrophobia, foaming at the mouth and avoiding water. 这只狗表现出恐水症的迹象，口吐白沫，避水。

Example 2: His hydrophobia prevented him from even taking a bath. 他的恐水症让他甚至无法洗澡。 | Synonym: aquaphobia | Antonym: aquaphilia | noun
