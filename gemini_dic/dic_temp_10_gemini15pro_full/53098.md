diphthongised:/dɪfˈθɒŋɡaɪzd/ /dɪfˈθɔŋɡaɪzd/| adj.|双元音化的|Having undergone diphthongisation, a phonetic process where a single vowel sound changes into a diphthong (a combination of two vowel sounds).|The vowel in "house" has been diphthongised in some dialects of English.|近义词: altered, changed, modified|反义词: monophthongized|词性: adjective
