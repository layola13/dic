blueprinting:/ˈbluːˌprɪntɪŋ/
n.|蓝图绘制，蓝图设计|The act or process of making a blueprint.  It refers to the detailed planning or design of something.|例句：The blueprinting phase of the project took several weeks.  该项目的蓝图设计阶段花了几个星期。|近义词：planning, designing, outlining, drafting|反义词:implementation, execution|词性:名词

blueprinting:/ˈbluːˌprɪntɪŋ/
n.|蓝图，设计图|A detailed plan or program of action.|例句：The architect presented the blueprinting for the new building. 建筑师展示了新建筑的蓝图。|近义词：plan, design, scheme, draft|反义词:improvisation|词性:名词
