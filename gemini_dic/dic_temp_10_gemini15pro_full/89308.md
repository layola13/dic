homoeroticisms: /ˌhɒməʊɪˈrɒtɪsɪzəmz/ (UK) /ˌhoʊmoʊɪˈrɑːtɪsɪzəmz/ (US)
n.
同性色情
The depiction or suggestion of sexual attraction between members of the same sex, often in a literary or artistic context.
His later poems are filled with homoeroticisms, exploring themes of male beauty and desire.  他后期的诗歌充满了同性恋的意味，探索了男性美和欲望的主题。
The film is subtle in its homoeroticisms, relying more on suggestive glances than explicit scenes. 这部电影的同性色情意味很微妙，更多地依靠暗示性的眼神而不是露骨的场景。
近义词: homoeroticism, same-sex desire, homosexual themes
反义词: heterosexuality, heteronormativity
词性: noun (plural)
