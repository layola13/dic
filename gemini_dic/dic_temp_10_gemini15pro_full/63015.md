enquires:/ɪnˈkwaɪəz/ /ɛnˈkwaɪrz/ | v. | 询问，打听 | This verb is the British English spelling of "inquires," meaning to ask for information.  She politely enquires about the train schedule. 她礼貌地询问火车时刻表。| 近义词：asks, questions, investigates | 反义词：answers, replies | 词性: verb

enquiry:/ɪnˈkwaɪəri/ /ˈɛnkwəri/ | n. | 询问，打听 | This noun is the British English spelling of "inquiry," referring to an act of asking for information.  I made an enquiry about the job vacancy. 我对这个职位空缺进行了询问。| 近义词：question, investigation, query | 反义词：answer, reply | 词性: noun
