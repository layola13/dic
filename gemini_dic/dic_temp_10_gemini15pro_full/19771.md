billionaire:/ˌbɪljəˈnɛər/ | n. | 亿万富翁，巨富 | A person who has assets worth at least one billion units of a given currency (e.g., dollars, euros, etc.). |  Example 1: The billionaire invested heavily in renewable energy.  这位亿万富翁大力投资可再生能源。

Example 2:  Many billionaires donate to charity. 许多亿万富翁向慈善机构捐款。 |  Synonyms: tycoon, magnate, plutocrat | Antonyms: pauper | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From billion + -aire. Billion from French billion, from bi- + million. -aire from French -aire, from Latin -arius.
