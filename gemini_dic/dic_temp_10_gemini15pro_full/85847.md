n.
High Dynamic Range Image (高动态范围图像)
A high dynamic range image (HDRI or HDR image) is a computer graphic image format that encodes more levels of color and brightness than are typically possible with standard digital images or photographic methods.
Example: The artist used an HDRI to create realistic lighting in the 3D scene.  艺术家使用HDRI在3D场景中创建逼真的照明。
Synonym: HDR image, HDR photograph
Antonym: LDRI (Low Dynamic Range Image)
词性: noun (名词)
