hasis: /ˈheɪsɪs/ | n. | 困惑，犹豫，不确定 | A state of uncertainty or indecision. |  He was in a hasis about whether to accept the job offer. 他对于是否接受这份工作邀请犹豫不决。| synonyms: uncertainty, indecision, hesitation, doubt | antonyms: certainty, decisiveness, resolution, conviction | noun

Etymology:  Likely a variant or misspelling of "hesitance," from "hesitant," from the Latin "haesitare" meaning "to stick fast, hesitate."
