pacs:/pæks/
n.
Political Action Committees (政治行动委员会)
A private group organized to raise and distribute money to elect or defeat political candidates.
Example: Many PACs contribute heavily to election campaigns. 许多政治行动委员会为竞选活动提供了大量资金。
Example:  The influence of PACs on political decisions is a subject of ongoing debate. 政治行动委员会对政治决策的影响是一个持续争论的话题。
Synonym:  pressure group, lobby
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "individual donor")
