nebbish:/ˈnɛbɪʃ/| n. |懦弱的人；胆小鬼；无用的人|A timid, meek, or ineffectual person; a nobody.|He was a nebbish, always afraid to speak up. 他是个懦夫，总是害怕大声说话。|Synonyms: weakling, wimp, milquetoast, pushover|Antonyms: leader, extrovert, go-getter|Noun
