meaners:/ˈmiːnərz/
noun
刻薄的人；吝啬鬼
People who are unkind, cruel, or ungenerous.
Those meaners never share their toys! 那些刻薄鬼从来不分享他们的玩具！
Synonyms: bullies, cheapskates, Scrooges, misers
Antonyms: kind people, generous people, philanthropists
n.
