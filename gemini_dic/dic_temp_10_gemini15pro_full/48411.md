dedimus: /ˈdɛdɪməs/| n.|委托书，授权令|A writ or warrant by which a private person is authorized to act as a judge or commissioner; a delegation of authority.|The court issued a dedimus potestatem to allow the witness to testify remotely.|Synonym: commission, warrant, authorization|Antonym: prohibition, restriction|noun
