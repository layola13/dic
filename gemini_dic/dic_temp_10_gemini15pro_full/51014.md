deskill:/diːˈskɪl/
verb
使…不再需要技能；降低…的技能
To reduce the level of skill required to do a job, typically by automating it.
Automation has deskilled many factory jobs.  自动化降低了许多工厂工作的技能要求。
Many traditional crafts have been deskilled through the introduction of machinery. 许多传统工艺因引进机器而降低了技能要求。
近义词: downgrade, deprofessionalize, automate
反义词: upskill, train, enhance
词性: verb

deskill:/diːˈskɪl/
noun
技能降低；去技能化
The process of reducing the level of skill required to do a job.
There are concerns about the deskilling of the workforce. 人们担心劳动力的技能下降。
The deskilling of certain professions can lead to job dissatisfaction.  某些职业的技能降低会导致工作不满。
近义词: degradation, reduction
反义词: upskilling, enhancement
词性: noun
