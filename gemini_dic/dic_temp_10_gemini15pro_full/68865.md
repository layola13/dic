fast lane:/ˈfæst leɪn/ (US) /ˈfɑːst leɪn/ (UK)| n.|快车道，成功之路，捷径|A route or method that leads to success or advancement more quickly than usual.|He's in the fast lane to promotion. 他正走在晋升的快车道上.|近义词：express lane, shortcut, quick route|反义词:slow lane, long route|词性:名词

Etymology:  "fast" (quick) + "lane" (a narrow road).  The term originally referred to the leftmost lane on a highway designed for faster-moving traffic.  Its figurative meaning developed to represent a quicker path to success.
