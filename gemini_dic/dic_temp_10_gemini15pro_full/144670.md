outwriting: /ˈaʊtraɪtɪŋ/
n.
超越写作；胜过写作
The act of writing that surpasses or excels in some way.
He achieved outwriting by incorporating diverse perspectives into his novel. 他通过在小说中融入不同的视角实现了超越写作。
Her outwriting captivated readers with its emotional depth and lyrical prose. 她的卓越写作以其情感深度和抒情散文吸引了读者。
近义词: surpassing, excelling, outdoing, transcending
反义词: underperforming, failing, lacking
