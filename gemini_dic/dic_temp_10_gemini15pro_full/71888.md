fleur-de-lis:/flɜːr də ˈliː/ /flɝːr də ˈliː/
noun
鸢尾花; 金百合花;  百合花饰
A stylized iris, used as a royal emblem of France.
The fleur-de-lis is a symbol of French royalty.  鸢尾花是法国皇室的象征。
synonyms: iris, lily symbol, royal emblem
antonyms: N/A (Symbol, no direct opposite)
词性: noun


fleur-de-lys:/flɜːr də ˈliː/ /flɝːr də ˈliː/
noun (plural fleurs-de-lis or fleurs-de-lys)
鸢尾花; 金百合花;  百合花饰
A stylized iris, often used as a decorative motif or heraldic symbol.
The king's robe was embroidered with fleurs-de-lys. 国王的袍子上绣着鸢尾花。
synonyms: iris, lily symbol, royal emblem
antonyms: N/A (Symbol, no direct opposite)
词性: noun
