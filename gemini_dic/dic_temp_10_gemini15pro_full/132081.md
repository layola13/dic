noncredibilities:/ˌnɑːnˌkrɛdəˈbɪlətiz/
n.
不可信性；不可信之处；令人怀疑之处
Lack of credibility; things that are not believable or trustworthy.
The report was riddled with noncredibilities, making it impossible to take seriously.  这份报告充满了不可信之处，让人无法认真对待。
The witness's numerous noncredibilities led the jury to doubt his testimony. 该证人的诸多不可信之处导致陪审团怀疑他的证词。
synonyms:  unbelievabilities, implausibilities, improbabilities, doubts
antonyms: credibilities, believabilities, plausibilities
