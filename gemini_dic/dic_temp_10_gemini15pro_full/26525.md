cabbler:/ˈkɑːblər/ (US), /ˈkɒblə(r)/ (UK)| n.|修鞋匠；补鞋匠；水果馅饼|A person who mends shoes.  Also, a type of fruit dessert.|例句：The cobbler repaired my worn-out boots. 补鞋匠修好了我破旧的靴子。

例句：We had apple cobbler for dessert. 我们甜点吃了苹果馅饼。|近义词：shoemaker, shoe repairer (for person); cobbler, crisp, crumble (for dessert)|反义词: None (for person); None (for dessert)|词性: Noun
