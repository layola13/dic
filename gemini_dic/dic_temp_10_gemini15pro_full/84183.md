half-quixotically:/ˌhæf kwɪkˈsɒtɪkli/
half-quixotically| adv.|半堂吉诃德式地，半浪漫主义地|In a way that is partly impractical, idealistic, or romantic, like Don Quixote.|He half-quixotically decided to build a windmill in his backyard. 他有点堂吉诃德式地决定在后院建一个风车。|Near synonyms: romantically, idealistically, impractically, unrealistically|Near antonyms: practically, realistically, pragmatically
