basutos:/bəˈsuːtoʊz/
noun
巴苏陀人
A member of a Bantu-speaking people inhabiting Lesotho and parts of South Africa.
The Basutos have a rich cultural heritage. 巴苏陀人拥有丰富的文化遗产。
synonyms:Sotho, Basotho
antonyms: N/A
