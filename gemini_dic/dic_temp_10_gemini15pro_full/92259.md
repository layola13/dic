hypervascularity:/ˌhaɪpərˌvæskjʊˈlærɪti/
noun
血管增生，血管过多
The presence of an excessive number of blood vessels in a tissue or organ.
Example: Hypervascularity is a common feature of many tumors. 血管增生是许多肿瘤的共同特征。

Example: The doctor noted the hypervascularity of the tissue during the ultrasound. 医生在超声波检查中注意到组织的血管增生。
近义词:angiogenesis, neovascularization
反义词:hypovascularity, avascularity
词性:n.
