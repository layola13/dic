overboiling:/ˌōvərˈbɔɪlɪŋ/ | n. | 过度沸腾，沸溢 | The act of boiling a liquid too vigorously, causing it to spill over. | The overboiling of the milk made a mess on the stove. 牛奶过度沸腾弄得炉子上到处都是。| 近义词: boil over | 反义词: simmer | 词性: 名词

overboiling:/ˌōvərˈbɔɪlɪŋ/ | adj. | 过度沸腾的 | Relating to or characterized by overboiling.  |  Be careful with the pot, it's prone to overboiling. 小心那个锅，它容易沸溢。 | 近义词: boiling over | 反义词: simmering | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "boiling" (present participle of the verb "boil")
