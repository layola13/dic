birdcatching:/ˈbɜːrdˌkætʃɪŋ/| n.|捕鸟; 捉鸟|The act or practice of catching birds.  The practice or sport of catching birds.|Birdcatching was once a common practice in many parts of the world. 捕鸟曾经是世界许多地方的常见做法。|Near synonyms: fowling, birding (in older usage).|Antonyms: bird releasing, bird conservation|noun

birdcatching:/ˈbɜːrdˌkætʃɪŋ/| adj.|捕鸟的|Relating to or involved in the catching of birds.|He used birdcatching nets. 他使用了捕鸟网。|Near synonyms: fowling|Antonyms: bird-releasing|adjective
