elected:/ɪˈlɛktɪd/| adj.|当选的，被选中的|Chosen by vote.|The newly elected mayor will address the city tomorrow. 新当选的市长明天将在全市发表讲话。|近义词：chosen, appointed, selected|反义词：defeated, rejected|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *ēlēctus*, past participle of *ēligere* ("to choose out, select"), from *ē-* ("out") + *legere* ("to choose, gather, read").
