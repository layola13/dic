fenianism:/ˈfiːniənɪzəm/ | n. | 费尼主义（19世纪支持爱尔兰从英国统治下独立的运动） | A movement in the latter half of the 19th century advocating Irish independence from British rule, often by violent means. |  Fenianism was a significant force in Irish politics during this period.  费尼主义是这一时期爱尔兰政治中的一股重要力量。

Fenianism sometimes resorted to violence to achieve its goals. 费尼主义有时会诉诸暴力来达到其目的。 | 近义词：Irish nationalism, republicanism | 反义词：Unionism, Loyalism | 词性: noun
