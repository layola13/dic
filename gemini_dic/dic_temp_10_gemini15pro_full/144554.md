outwaiting:/ˌaʊtˈweɪtɪŋ/
verb
比...等得更久; 等到...结束
waiting out someone or something means waiting longer than them or it.
He outwaited his rivals and eventually won the contract. 他比他的竞争对手等得更久，最终赢得了合同。
The protestors outwaited the police, who eventually left. 抗议者比警察等得更久，警察最终离开了。
outlast, outstay
