bovy: /ˈboʊvi/ | adj. | 牛的; 似牛的; 迟钝的; 笨拙的 |  Resembling an ox or cow, especially in being slow-moving and dull-witted.  | He had a bovy expression on his face. 他脸上带着迟钝的表情。 | synonyms: bovine, oxlike, stolid, dull | antonyms: sharp, bright, intelligent, quick-witted | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

From Middle English *bovy, from Old French *bovin, from Latin bovīnus (“of or pertaining to oxen or cows”), from bōs (“ox, cow”).
