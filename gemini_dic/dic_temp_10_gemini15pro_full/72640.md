flypast:/ˈflaɪˌpɑːst/ (US) /ˈflaɪˌpɑːst/ (UK)| n.|飞行表演，空中分列式|A ceremonial flight of aircraft, typically low over a crowd or landmark.|The Queen's birthday celebrations included a spectacular flypast by the Royal Air Force.|近义词：air show, aerial display|反义词: N/A|词性:名词

Etymology:  A compound of 'fly' and 'past', indicating the action of aircraft flying past a point.
