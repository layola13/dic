a combination:/əˌkɑːmbɪˈneɪʃn/ | n. | 组合，结合，联合；密码 | The combination of colors is very pleasing.  这些颜色的组合非常赏心悦目。 |  A combination lock requires a specific sequence of numbers to open.  打开密码锁需要特定的数字序列。 | mix, blend, union, mixture | separation, division | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *combinatio, from Latin combinare "to combine," from com- "together" + binus "two by two."
