effectuating:/ɪˈfɛktʃueɪtɪŋ/
verb
使生效;实现;实行
bringing about; causing to happen; putting into effect
例句：The government is effectuating new policies to address climate change. 政府正在实施新政策来应对气候变化。

例句：They are effectuating the plan they developed last year. 他们正在执行去年制定的计划。

近义词：implementing, achieving, realizing, executing, carrying out
反义词：hindering, obstructing, preventing, impeding
词性：v.
