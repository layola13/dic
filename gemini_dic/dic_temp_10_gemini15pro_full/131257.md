noncastigating:/ˌnɒnkæˈstɪɡeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
不加责备的，不惩罚的
Not criticizing or punishing someone for something they have done wrong.
She offered noncastigating advice, focusing on solutions instead of blame. 她没有责备，而是提出了建议，侧重于解决问题而不是指责。
He took a noncastigating approach with the student, understanding the reasons behind his misbehavior. 他对这个学生采取了不责骂的态度，理解他行为不端背后的原因。

近义词:
forgiving, lenient, tolerant, understanding, merciful, compassionate, nonjudgmental

反义词:
castigating, critical, judgmental, censorious, condemnatory, punitive, reproving

词性:
adjective
