margaritaceous:/ˌmɑːrɡərɪˈteɪʃəs/
adj.
珍珠似的，似珍珠的
Resembling a pearl; pearly.  Having the iridescent luster of pearl.
The archaeologist unearthed a margaritaceous bead from the ancient tomb. 考古学家从古墓中挖掘出一颗珍珠般的小珠子。
The inside of the shell had a margaritaceous sheen. 贝壳的内部有着珍珠般的光泽。
近义词: pearly, nacreous, iridescent
反义词: dull, matte
词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *margaritaceus, from margarita (“pearl”) + -aceus (suffix forming adjectives).
