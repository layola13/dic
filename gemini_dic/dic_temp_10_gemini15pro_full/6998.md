androgenic:/ˌæn.drəˈdʒɛn.ɪk/
adjective
雄激素的；产生雄性激素的
Relating to or tending to promote male characteristics.
This drug has androgenic effects. 这种药物有雄激素作用。

This steroid has powerful androgenic properties. 这种类固醇具有强大的雄激素特性。

masculine, masculinizing, virilizing
feminine, estrogenic
adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From andro- (male, man) + -genic (producing, forming).  Ultimately from Greek *andrós (man) and -genēs (born, produced).
