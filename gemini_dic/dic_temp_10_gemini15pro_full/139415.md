observingly:/əbˈzɜːrvɪŋli/
adverb
观察地；注意地
In a way that involves close observation.
She watched him observingly, noting every detail of his expression. 她观察着他，注意着他表情的每一个细节。
近义词: attentively, watchfully, closely, carefully
反义词: inattentively, carelessly, negligently
词性: adverb

词源: From observe + -ing + -ly.  "Observe" comes from Latin observare "to watch, heed, comply with," from ob- "over" + servare "to keep, watch."
