overmelodiously: /ˌoʊvərˌmɛləˈdoʊiəsli/
adverb
过于旋律优美地，甜得发腻地
in a way that is excessively or unpleasantly melodious
The song was sung overmelodiously, making it sound saccharine and insincere. 这首歌唱得过于甜腻，听起来做作而不真诚。
Near Antonyms: discordantly, harshly, jarringly
No exact antonyms due to its specific meaning of "excessively melodious."
