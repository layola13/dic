overvaliant:/ˌoʊvərˈvæliənt/| adj.|过于勇敢的，鲁莽的|Excessively valiant; rashly brave; foolhardy.  Characterized by excessive or misplaced valor.  Showing more bravery than is wise or necessary.|He made an overvaliant attempt to rescue the cat from the burning building. 他过于勇敢地试图从燃烧的建筑物中救出那只猫。|The knight's overvaliant charge into the enemy ranks proved to be a fatal mistake.  这位骑士过于勇敢地冲进敌阵，结果证明是一个致命的错误。|近义词：reckless, foolhardy, rash, impetuous|反义词：cautious, prudent, circumspect|词性:形容词

词源:
From over- + valiant.  "Over-" acts as an intensifier, meaning "excessively" or "too much". "Valiant" comes from Old French "vaillant", meaning "worth, value, strength".
