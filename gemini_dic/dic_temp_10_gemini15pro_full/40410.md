contramure:/ˈkɒntrəmjʊə(r)|/ˈkɑːntrəˌmjʊr/ |n.|外郭墙，外护墙|An outer wall or rampart built in front of and protecting another, especially the main wall of a fortress.|The castle's contramure provided an additional layer of defense. 城堡的外郭墙提供了额外的防御层。|Synonyms: outer wall, rampart, bulwark, parapet|Antonyms: inner wall|noun
