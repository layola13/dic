词源：navigation + -ally
navigation (n.)  来自拉丁语 navigationem (nominative navigatio) "a sailing, a voyage, navigation," 来自 navigare "to sail, sail over, navigate,"  来自 navis "ship" + agere "to drive, lead, act, do."
-ally (suffix)  形成副词，表示“以...方式”，“关于...”。
