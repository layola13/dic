overunionized: /ˌoʊvərˈjuːnjənaɪzd/
adjective
过度工会化的
Having excessive union representation or influence, often to the point of hindering productivity or flexibility.
The factory became overunionized, with multiple unions competing for members and making it difficult to implement new policies.  工厂变得过度工会化，多个工会争夺成员，使得实施新政策变得困难。
Some argue that the airline industry is overunionized, leading to higher labor costs and reduced competitiveness.  一些人认为航空业过度工会化，导致劳动力成本上升，竞争力下降。
近义词: excessively unionized
反义词: underunionized, non-unionized
词性: adjective
