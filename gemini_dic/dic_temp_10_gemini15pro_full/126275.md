Etymology: Myers is a patronymic surname, meaning "son of Myer."  Myer is a variant of Meier, a German and Yiddish given name derived from the Hebrew name Meir, meaning "light" or "shining." The surname may also be locational, derived from places named Meyer or Meierhof.
