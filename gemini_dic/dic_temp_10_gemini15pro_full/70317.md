fiefdoms:/ˈfiːfdəmz/
|n.|领地；势力范围；（比喻）个人领域；私人王国|An area or sphere of operation controlled by a particular person or group.|The company is divided into several fiefdoms, each ruled by a different vice president.  这家公司被划分成几个领域，每个领域都由一位不同的副总裁统治。|Synonyms: domains, territories, bailiwicks, provinces, spheres of influence|Antonyms: commonwealth, shared territory|noun


fiefdom:/ˈfiːfdəm/
|n.|领地；势力范围；（比喻）个人领域；私人王国|An area or sphere of operation controlled by a particular person or group.|He treats the department as his personal fiefdom. 他把这个部门当作自己的私人王国。|Synonyms: domain, territory, bailiwick, province, sphere of influence|Antonyms: commonwealth, shared territory|noun
