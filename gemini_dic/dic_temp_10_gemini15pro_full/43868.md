cresorcin: /krɛˈsɔːrsɪn/ | n. |间苯二酚|A white crystalline solid, C6H4(OH)2, used as an antiseptic and disinfectant, and in the manufacture of resins, dyes, and explosives. | Cresorcin is commonly used in topical acne treatments.  间苯二酚通常用于局部痤疮治疗。| Cresorcin is an important chemical intermediate in the production of certain dyes. 间苯二酚是生产某些染料的重要化学中间体。|Synonyms: resorcinol, m-dihydroxybenzene | Antonyms: None | Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *crésorcine*, from *crésol* (cresol) + *-orcine* (from *orcinol*), ultimately from *Orchis* (orchid), as orcinol was originally obtained from certain lichens growing on orchids.
