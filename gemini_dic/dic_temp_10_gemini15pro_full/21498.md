bludgeon:/ˈblʌdʒən/| n. vt.|棍棒；用棍棒打|A short, heavy club. To strike with a heavy club; to force or coerce.|例句：The robber bludgeoned the shopkeeper with a lead pipe. 强盗用铅管猛击店主。

|例句：He felt bludgeoned by their constant criticism. 他感到被他们不断的批评压得喘不过气。|近义词：club, cosh, cudgel, beat, batter, pummel|反义词: caress, soothe|词性:noun, verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *bloignon, from bloing "a clump, lump," of Germanic origin.
