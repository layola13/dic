adenographic: /ˌædɪnəˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
腺体描记的
Relating to or involving adenography, which is the radiographic visualization of glands, especially lymph glands.
Adenographic imaging helps to determine the extent of the disease in the lymph nodes. 腺体显像有助于确定淋巴结疾病的程度。
The adenographic study revealed an enlarged lymph node. 腺体造影研究显示淋巴结肿大。

近义词:  glandular
反义词:  None
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From adeno- (relating to glands) + -graphic (relating to writing or recording).  "Adeno-" comes from the Greek "adēn" (gland). "-graphic" comes from the Greek "graphikos" (of or for writing).
