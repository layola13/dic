nonphagocytic:/ˌnɒnˌfæɡəˈsɪtɪk/
adjective
非吞噬的
Not capable of phagocytosis, the process by which cells engulf and digest foreign particles or other cells.
Macrophages are phagocytic cells, while lymphocytes are generally nonphagocytic. 巨噬细胞是吞噬细胞，而淋巴细胞通常是非吞噬细胞的。
