outagamies:/ˌuːtəˈɡæmiːz/| n. |乌塔加米人|A Native American people formerly inhabiting an area around the Fox River in present-day Wisconsin and upper Michigan. They were part of the Algonquian language family.  |The Outagamies were allies of the Sauk tribe. 乌塔加米人是索克部落的盟友。|Synonym: Fox Indians|Antonym: N/A|noun
