flintiness:/ˈflɪntinəs/
n.
坚硬；冷酷无情；无动于衷
The flintiness in his eyes suggested he would not be swayed.
他眼神中的冷酷表明他不会动摇。
The flintiness of her character made her a formidable opponent.
她性格的坚强使她成为一个可怕的对手。
Near Synonyms: hardness, firmness, inflexibility, rigidity, unyieldingness, implacability, ruthlessness
Antonyms: softness, gentleness, compassion, empathy, yieldingness, flexibility
词性: 名词
