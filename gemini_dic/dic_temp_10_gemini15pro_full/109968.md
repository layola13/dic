liberetto:/lɪˈbrɛtoʊ/ (US), /lɪˈbretəʊ/ (UK)
n.
歌剧，音乐剧等的剧本；歌词
the text of an opera or other long vocal work
The libretto for Mozart's "The Magic Flute" is full of symbolism.  莫扎特的《魔笛》的剧本充满了象征意义。

The composer and librettist worked closely together. 作曲家和歌剧脚本作者密切合作。
synonym: script, text, book
antonym:  (No direct antonym, but one could consider "score" as a contrasting element)
noun
