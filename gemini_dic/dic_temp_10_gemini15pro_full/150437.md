panta:/ˈpæntə/| n. | all, every, whole, the whole |  From Ancient Greek πᾶν (pân, “all”) + -τα (-ta, neuter plural suffix).  Refers to the totality or whole of something. | The panta rhei doctrine suggests that everything is in constant flux.  万物流变学说认为一切都在不断变化。| synonyms: totality, entirety, whole | antonyms: part, fraction, piece | noun
