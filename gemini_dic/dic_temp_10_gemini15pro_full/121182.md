misdistinguish:/ˌmɪsdɪˈstɪŋɡwɪʃ/
verb
误认，区别错误
To perceive, understand, or classify wrongly; mistake something for another.
She misdistinguished the twins and called the younger one by the elder's name. 她误认了这对双胞胎，把弟弟的名字叫成了哥哥的。
He misdistinguished between irony and sarcasm. 他把讽刺和挖苦混淆了。
synonyms: misidentify, mistake, confuse, confound
antonyms: distinguish, differentiate, discern
