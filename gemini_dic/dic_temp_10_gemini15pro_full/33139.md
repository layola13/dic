christophany: /krɪˈstɒfəni/ (US) /krɪˈstɒfəni/ (UK)| n. |显现；耶稣显圣|An appearance or manifestation of Christ, especially after the Ascension.|例句：The feast of the Epiphany celebrates the christophany to the Magi. 主显节庆祝耶稣显现给东方三博士。

例句：Some theologians interpret the Transfiguration as a christophany. 一些神学家将耶稣变形解释为一次显现。

|近义词：epiphany, theophany, manifestation|反义词: occultation, hiding|词性: 名词
