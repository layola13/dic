indemnification:/ɪnˌdɛmnɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. |赔偿，保障|The contract includes an indemnification clause protecting the company from liability.  该合同包含一项赔偿条款，保护公司免于承担责任。| The company offered indemnification for any losses incurred. 公司对任何损失提供赔偿。| synonyms: compensation, reimbursement, restitution, redress | antonyms: penalty, forfeiture | noun

indemnify:/ɪnˈdɛmnɪfaɪ/ | vt. |赔偿，保障|The insurance company will indemnify you for the damage to your car. 保险公司将赔偿你的汽车损失。| We indemnified the client against any legal action. 我们保障客户免受任何法律诉讼。| synonyms: compensate, reimburse, recompense | antonyms: penalize, fine | verb
