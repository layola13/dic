benefiter:/ˈbɛnɪfɪtər/
n.
受益人，受惠者
One who receives a benefit; someone who profits or gains an advantage.
The charity aims to help children, and they are the primary benefiter of the fundraising efforts.  这个慈善机构旨在帮助儿童，他们是筹款活动的主要受益者。
The new tax law will make high-income earners the main benefiter. 新的税法将使高收入者成为主要受益者。
beneficiary, recipient
loser, victim
noun
