carer:/ˈkɛərər/ (UK), /ˈkɛrər/ (US)| n.|照顾者，护理员|A person who regularly looks after a sick, elderly, or disabled person.|My grandmother has a live-in carer. 我祖母有一个住家护理员。|例句：The carer helped the elderly man with his bath. 护理员帮助老人洗澡。|近义词：caregiver, attendant, nurse|反义词: N/A (No direct opposite)|词性:noun
