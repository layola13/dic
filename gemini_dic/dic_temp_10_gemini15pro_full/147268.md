overmilks:/ˌoʊvərˈmɪlks/| v. |过度挤奶；榨取过多| To extract too much milk from (a cow, goat, etc.), especially to the detriment of the animal's health or future milk production.| The farmer overmilked his cows, and their production dropped significantly.  农民过度挤奶，导致奶牛的产量显著下降。|  The company overmilked its employees, demanding excessive overtime without proper compensation. 这家公司过度压榨员工，要求他们加班过度，却没有适当的补偿。 |近义词: overwork, exploit, overuse |反义词: underuse, conserve |词性:动词
