hydropathical:/ˌhaɪdrəˈpæθɪkəl/
adjective
水疗法的；用水治疗的
relating to hydropathy (= the treatment of disease using water)
Example: Hydropathical treatments were popular in the 19th century. 19世纪，水疗法很受欢迎。
Example: The hotel offered a range of hydropathical facilities. 这家酒店提供一系列水疗设施。
Synonym: hydrotherapeutic
Antonym: allopathic (对抗疗法的)
word class: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From hydro- (water) + -pathical (relating to suffering or disease).  Ultimately from Greek *hudro- (water) + pathos (suffering).
