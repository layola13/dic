capless:/ˈkæpləs/
adjective
无帽的；敞篷的
without a cap or lid; convertible (referring to a car)
The capless gas tank is a convenient feature.  无盖油箱是一项便捷的功能。
The couple enjoyed a ride in their capless sports car. 这对夫妇享受着他们敞篷跑车的兜风之旅。
synonyms: uncovered, open, lidless, convertible, topless
antonyms: capped, covered, enclosed
词性: adjective
