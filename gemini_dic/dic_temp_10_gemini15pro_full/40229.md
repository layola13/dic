contextually:/kənˈtekstʃuəli/ | adv. | 根据上下文地 |  Considering the surrounding words, phrases, or circumstances; relating to the context. |  The meaning of the word "run" can only be understood contextually.  “run”这个词的意思只能通过上下文来理解。 |  In context, within the context of, situationally | N/A | adverb


ETYMOLOGY->
From "context" + "-ual" + "-ly".  "Context" comes from Latin "contextus", meaning "a joining together".
