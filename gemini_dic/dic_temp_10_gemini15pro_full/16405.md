barcoded:/ˈbɑːrkoʊdɪd/
adjective
条形码化的，带有条形码的
Marked with a barcode.
The barcoded products are easier to track in the warehouse.  仓库中带有条形码的产品更容易追踪。
Synonyms: labeled, tagged, marked
Antonyms: unlabeled, unmarked, untagged
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "barcode" + "-ed." "Barcode" is a portmanteau of "bar" (referring to the lines) and "code" (referring to the representation of data).
