paintership: /ˈpeɪntərʃɪp/ | n. | 绘画；油画；绘画艺术 | The practice or art of painting.  A painted picture. |  例句：The museum has a remarkable collection of 17th-century Dutch paintings.  这家博物馆收藏了大量17世纪的荷兰绘画作品。

例句：Her latest painting depicts a vibrant sunset over the ocean. 她最新的画描绘了海上充满活力的日落景象。 | 近义词：picture, artwork, canvas, art | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From Middle English *peyntyng, equivalent to paint +‎ -ing. Cognate with Dutch schildering (“painting”), German Malerei (“painting”).
