estrich: /ˈɛstrɪtʃ/ | n. | 水泥砂浆地面; (尤指)未铺地面的水泥地面 | A floor made of cement and sand, especially one that has not yet been covered with another material. |  The estrich was still damp, so we couldn't walk on it. 水泥地面还是湿的，所以我们不能在上面走。|  The builders laid the estrich before installing the tiles. 建筑工人在铺瓷砖之前铺设了水泥砂浆地面。 | synonyms: screed, underlayment, subfloor | antonyms: finished floor, flooring | noun
