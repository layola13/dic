abbreviate: /əˈbriːvieɪt/ | v. | 缩写，缩短 | To make something shorter, especially by using only the first letters of each word or by using fewer words. |  We abbreviated the name of the organization to WHO. 我们将该组织的名称缩写为WHO。

The word "information" is often abbreviated to "info".  单词“information”通常缩写为“info”。

近义词: shorten, condense, reduce | 反义词: lengthen, expand, extend | 词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *abbreviatus, past participle of abbreviare "to shorten," from Latin ad- "to" + brevis "short."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
We abbreviate long words to save time. = 我们缩写长单词以节省时间。
They abbreviate technical terms in the report. = 他们在报告中缩写了技术术语。

一般过去时:
They abbreviated the name of the company for convenience. = 为了方便起见，他们缩写了公司名称。
He abbreviated the title of the book in his essay. = 他在论文中缩写了书名。

现在完成时:
The author has abbreviated several lengthy quotations. = 作者缩写了几个冗长的引用。
We have abbreviated the process to make it more efficient. = 我们简化了流程以使其更高效。

过去完成时:
The editor had abbreviated the manuscript before sending it to the publisher. = 编辑在将稿件发送给出版商之前已经将其缩写了。
She had abbreviated the article so it would fit in the newsletter. = 她缩短了文章以便它能放入通讯中。

将来时:
We will abbreviate the meeting due to time constraints. = 由于时间限制，我们将缩短会议时间。
I will abbreviate my presentation so I don't go over time. = 我会缩短我的演讲时间，以免超时。

虚拟语气:
If I were to abbreviate this word, I would use its first three letters. = 如果我要缩写这个词，我会用它的前三个字母。
I wish I could abbreviate this process, but it requires every step. = 我希望我可以缩短这个过程，但它需要每个步骤。


被动语态:
The document was abbreviated for clarity. = 该文件为清晰起见被缩写了。
The term is often abbreviated in informal writing. = 该术语在非正式写作中经常被缩写。

疑问句:
Can you abbreviate this phrase? = 你能缩写这个短语吗？
How should this word be abbreviated? = 这个词应该如何缩写？

否定句:
I didn't abbreviate the title of the article. = 我没有缩写文章的标题。
We don't usually abbreviate names in formal documents. = 我们通常不在正式文件中缩写名称。


PHRASE->
