desertifying:/dɪˈzɜːtɪfaɪɪŋ/
verb
使沙漠化;使(土地)变得荒芜
The process of land becoming desert, typically losing its bodies of water as well as vegetation and wildlife.  This is usually caused by climate change or unsuitable agriculture.
Excessive farming is desertifying the land. 过度耕作正在使土地沙漠化。
desertize, aridity, barrenness
forestation, fertility
