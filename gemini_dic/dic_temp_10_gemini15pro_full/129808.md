non-gothic:/ˌnɑːnˈɡɑːθɪk/ | adj. | 非哥特式的 | Not pertaining to or characteristic of the Gothic style in architecture, art, or literature. |  The church's architecture was distinctly non-gothic, with its rounded arches and bright, airy interior.  教堂的建筑明显是非哥特式的，有着圆形的拱门和明亮通风的内部。| synonyms: non-Gothic, modern, contemporary, classical, Romanesque | antonyms: Gothic | adjective
