cyclotrons:/ˈsaɪklətrɒnz/ (UK), /ˈsaɪkloʊtrɑːnz/ (US)| n. |回旋加速器|A type of particle accelerator in which charged particles accelerate outwards from the centre along a spiral path. They are held to a spiral trajectory by a static magnetic field and accelerated by a rapidly varying (radio frequency) electric field.|The cyclotron is a crucial tool in nuclear physics research.  回旋加速器是核物理研究中的关键工具。

The university's new cyclotron will enable scientists to conduct cutting-edge experiments. 这所大学的新回旋加速器将使科学家能够进行前沿实验。|近义词：particle accelerator 粒子加速器|反义词: N/A |词性:noun
