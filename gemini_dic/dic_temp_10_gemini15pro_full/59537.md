eateries:/ˈiːtəriːz/
n.
餐馆，饮食店
places where people eat, usually restaurants
We explored the various eateries in the town. 我们探索了镇上的各种餐馆。
restaurants, cafes, dining establishments
---
近义词:restaurants, cafes, dining establishments
反义词:(None -  A direct antonym for a place to eat doesn't readily exist)
词性:noun (plural)
