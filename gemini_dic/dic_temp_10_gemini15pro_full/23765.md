bravoing:/ˈbrɑːvoʊɪŋ/
verb
喝彩；大声叫好
The act of shouting "Bravo!" to express approval or appreciation, especially after a performance.
The audience was bravoing enthusiastically after the opera singer's aria.  观众在歌剧演唱家唱完咏叹调后热情地叫好。
近义词: applauding, cheering, acclaiming
反义词: booing, jeering, hissing
词性:v.
