minor-leaguers:/ˈmaɪnər ˈliːɡərz/ | n. |  小联盟球员 | Players in a minor league, especially in baseball.  A minor league is a professional sports league that is below the major leagues in skill level. | The team is full of promising minor-leaguers hoping to make it to the majors.  这个队伍充满了有前途的小联盟球员，他们都希望能进入大联盟。

The minor-leaguers were called up to the majors due to injuries on the main team.  由于主力队队员受伤，小联盟球员被召入大联盟。 | 近义词: minor league players, farm team players | 反义词: major-leaguers | 词性: 名词 (plural)
