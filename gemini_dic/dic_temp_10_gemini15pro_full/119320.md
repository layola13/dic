microbic:/maɪˈkrɒʊbɪk/| adj.|微生物的；由微生物引起的|Relating to or caused by microbes; a less common variant of "microbial".|The microbic activity in the soil is essential for plant growth. 土壤中的微生物活动对植物生长至关重要。|The researchers studied the microbic composition of the sample. 研究人员研究了样品的微生物组成。|近义词:microbial, bacterial, germinal|反义词:sterile, abiotic|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From French *microbien*, from microbe + -ic.  Microbe itself comes from Greek *mīkros* (small) + *bios* (life).
