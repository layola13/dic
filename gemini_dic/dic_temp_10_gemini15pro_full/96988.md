infantilization:/ɪnˌfæn.tɪ.laɪˈzeɪ.ʃən/ /ɪnˌfæn.t̬əl.əˈzeɪ.ʃən/| n. |幼稚化；婴儿化；幼儿化|The act of treating someone as if they were a child, often by restricting their freedom or responsibility.|The infantilization of women in advertising is a common complaint.  广告中对女性的幼稚化是一个普遍的抱怨。|近义词：patronizing, condescending, demeaning|反义词：empowerment, maturation|词性: noun

infantilize:/ɪnˌfæn.tɪ.laɪz/ /ɪnˌfæn.t̬əl.aɪz/| vt. |使幼稚化；使幼儿化|To treat someone as if they were a child, often by restricting their freedom or responsibility.|Stop infantilizing your teenage children; they're capable of making their own decisions. 别再把你的青少年孩子幼稚化了；他们有能力自己做决定。|近义词：patronize, condescend, demean|反义词：empower, mature|词性: verb
