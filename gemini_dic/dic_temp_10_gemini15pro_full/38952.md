confiding:/kənˈfaɪdɪŋ/
adjective
信任的；易于相信他人的；表示信任的
showing trust and intimacy
例句：She had a confiding nature. 她天性信任别人。

例句：He gave her a confiding smile. 他给了她一个充满信任的微笑。

近义词：trusting, open, frank, intimate
反义词：secretive, guarded, suspicious
词性：adjective

词源：From the verb "confide," which comes from the Latin "confidere," meaning "to trust entirely."  The "-ing" suffix transforms the verb into a present participle, used here as an adjective.
