non-muscular:/ˌnɒnˈmʌskjʊlə(r)/ /ˌnɑːnˈmʌskjələr/
adjective
非肌肉的；无肌肉的
Not involving or depending on muscles; not having well-developed muscles.
Example: Non-muscular tissues like ligaments and tendons play a crucial role in joint stability.  非肌肉组织，如韧带和肌腱，在关节稳定性中起着至关重要的作用。

Example:  He had a non-muscular physique, more suited to intellectual pursuits than athletics.  他的体格不发达，更适合从事脑力活动而不是体育运动。
synonym:  flabby, weak, undeveloped, atrophied
antonym:  muscular, strong, well-developed, toned
词性: adjective
