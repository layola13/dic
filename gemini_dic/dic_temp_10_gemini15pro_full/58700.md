dumpish:/ˈdʌmpɪʃ/| adj.|闷闷不乐的；沮丧的；抑郁的|Feeling or showing low spirits; gloomy; depressed.|She felt dumpish after hearing the bad news. 她听到坏消息后感到闷闷不乐。|近义词：gloomy, dejected, downcast, sad, depressed|反义词：cheerful, happy, joyful|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From dump (n.) + -ish.  The noun "dump" originally referred to a state of melancholy or depression, and the suffix "-ish" adds the meaning "somewhat" or "having the quality of."
