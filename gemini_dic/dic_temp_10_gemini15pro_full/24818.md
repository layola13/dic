browbeat:/ˈbraʊbiːt/| v. |恐吓，威胁，威逼|To intimidate or bully someone into doing something, typically by persistent criticism or threats. |The boss browbeat his employees into working overtime. 老板威逼员工加班。

She was browbeaten into accepting the deal. 她被胁迫接受了这笔交易。|近义词：intimidate, bully, coerce, threaten, cow |反义词：encourage, support, persuade|词性: 动词

browbeater:/ˈbraʊˌbiːtər/| n. |恃强凌弱者，恐吓者|A person who intimidates or bullies someone into doing something. |He's a real browbeater, always trying to get his way. 他真是个恃强凌弱的人，总是想方设法达到目的。

The team was tired of dealing with the company's browbeater. 团队厌倦了与公司里那个恐吓者打交道。|近义词：bully, intimidator, tyrant |反义词：supporter, encourager|词性: 名词

词源：
From brow (eyebrow) + beat. Originally meant "to lower one's brows menacingly," hence "to intimidate."  来自 brow（眉毛）+ beat。最初的意思是“威胁性地皱起眉毛”，因此引申为“恐吓”。
