dispossession:/ˌdɪspəˈzɛʃən/
n.
剥夺，驱逐，丧失
The act of taking something away from someone, especially land or property.
The dispossession of the Native Americans from their ancestral lands was a tragic chapter in history.  美国原住民被剥夺祖先土地是历史上一个悲惨的篇章。
Eviction, expropriation, seizure, deprivation
Restoration, restitution, return
