convectively:/kənˈvɛktɪvli/| adv.|对流地，通过对流|In meteorology, it describes processes related to convection, the transfer of heat through the movement of fluids (liquids or gases).|The air was heated convectively, causing it to rise.  空气被对流加热，导致它上升。|The heat was distributed convectively throughout the liquid. 热量通过对流分布在整个液体中。|Synonyms:  by convection, through convection|Antonyms: conductively, radiatively|词性: 副词
