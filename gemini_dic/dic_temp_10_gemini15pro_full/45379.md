cunningly:/ˈkʌnɪŋli/| adv.|狡猾地，巧妙地，诡诈地|In a clever and deceitful way, often to achieve a hidden purpose. |The thief cunningly bypassed the security system. 小偷狡猾地绕过了安全系统。|

The fox cunningly lured the rabbit into a trap. 狐狸狡猾地引诱兔子进入陷阱。|

Synonyms: slyly, craftily, artfully, deceptively, shrewdly|Antonyms: honestly, openly, frankly, plainly, artlessly|词性:副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *cunningeli*, from Old English *cunnunglice* (“skillfully, cleverly, wisely”), equivalent to *cunnung* (“knowledge, skill”) + *-lice* (adverbial suffix). More at cunning, -ly.
