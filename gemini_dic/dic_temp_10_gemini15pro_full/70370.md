fiesta:/fiˈɛstə/ | n. | 节日，盛宴，庆祝活动 | A public celebration or festival, especially one originating in Spain or Latin America. |例句：The town held a fiesta to celebrate the harvest.  小镇举行了一场丰收庆典。|例句：We went to a vibrant fiesta with music, dancing, and delicious food. 我们参加了一个充满活力、有音乐、舞蹈和美味佳肴的节日。| 近义词：celebration, festival, holiday, feast | 反义词：wake, funeral | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *fiesta* "festival, feast," from Late Latin *festa* "feast, holiday" (source also of French *fête*, Italian *festa*), from Latin *festus* "festive, joyful, solemn; of a holiday," related to *feriae* "holidays."
