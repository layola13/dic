constricting:/kənˈstrɪktɪŋ/
adjective
收缩的；压缩的；限制的
making something narrower or tighter; limiting or restricting something

The constricting bandage was cutting off his circulation.
收缩的绷带阻碍了他的血液循环。

The tight collar was constricting.
紧的衣领令人窒息。

His fear was a constricting force, preventing him from taking action.
他的恐惧是一种约束力，阻止他采取行动。


Synonyms: tightening, squeezing, compressing, limiting, restricting
Antonyms: expanding, widening, loosening, relaxing, freeing
词性: 形容词
