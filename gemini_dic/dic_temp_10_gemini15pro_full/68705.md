farewell:/fɛərˈwɛl/ | n. int. adj.|告别，再见，告别的|An act of parting or of bidding someone goodbye.  A goodbye.|例句：We bid her a fond farewell. 我们和她深情告别。|近义词：goodbye, adieu, so long|反义词：greeting, hello, welcome|词性:名词,感叹词,形容词

farewell:/fɛərˈwɛl/| v.|告别，辞别|To say goodbye; to take leave of.|例句：He waved farewell to his family. 他向家人挥手告别。|近义词：depart, leave, quit|反义词：greet, welcome, meet|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *farewel*, from Old English *fǽrwel* (noun), *fǽr wēl* (interjection), from *faran* ("to go, travel, journey, depart") + *wēl* ("well").  Cognate with Dutch *vaarwel*, German *Fahrwohl*, West Frisian *fearwol*.
