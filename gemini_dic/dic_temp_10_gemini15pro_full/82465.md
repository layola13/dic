grumping: /ˈɡrʌmpɪŋ/ | n. | 抱怨，发牢骚 | The act of complaining or expressing discontent in a bad-tempered way. |  He couldn't stand her constant grumping about the weather. 他受不了她不停地抱怨天气。|  complaining, moaning, whining | contentment, happiness | noun

grumpy: /ˈɡrʌmpi/ | adj. | 脾气暴躁的，爱抱怨的 | Bad-tempered and irritable. |  He woke up grumpy this morning. 他今天早上醒来脾气很暴躁。|  irritable, bad-tempered, crabby | cheerful, happy | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "grump," of uncertain origin, possibly imitative.  First recorded in the mid-19th century.
