outscolds:/ˌaʊtˈskoʊldz/
verb
责骂，胜过…的责骂
rebukes or criticizes someone loudly and angrily, surpasses someone in scolding.
She outscolds her husband every time he forgets to take out the trash. 她每次丈夫忘记倒垃圾都会把他骂得狗血淋头。
近义词: berates, upbraids, rebukes, chastises
反义词: praises, commends, lauds
词性: verb


outscolds:/ˌaʊtˈskoʊldz/
词源:  'out-' (prefix indicating surpassing or exceeding) + 'scold' (verb meaning to rebuke angrily)
中文翻译: 胜过…的责骂，把…骂得更厉害
英文解析: To scold someone more intensely or for a longer duration than another person.  It implies a competition or comparison of scolding.
例句:
The two fishwives outscold each other at the market.  两个鱼贩在市场上互相责骂，比谁骂得更凶。

Her mother outscolds her father when it comes to the children's behavior.  说到孩子们的行为，她妈妈比她爸爸骂得更厉害。

近义词: outdo in scolding, surpass in reprimanding
反义词:  praise, commend
词性: verb
