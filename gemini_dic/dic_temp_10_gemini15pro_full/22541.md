boorish:/ˈbʊərɪʃ/| adj.|粗鲁的，粗野的，笨拙的|Resembling or characteristic of a boor; rude and insensitive; clumsy.|He exhibited boorish behavior at the dinner party, insulting the host and spilling wine. 他在晚宴上表现粗鲁，侮辱了主人，还洒了酒。|近义词：rude, uncivilized, uncouth, insensitive, loutish, churlish|反义词：refined, sophisticated, courteous, polite, civil|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *borissh, *boorish, from boor +‎ -ish.
