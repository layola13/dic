illicitly: /ɪˈlɪsɪtli/
adverb
非法地；不正当地
in a way that is not allowed by law or rules
He was accused of illicitly copying confidential documents. 他被指控非法复制机密文件。
Synonyms: illegally, unlawfully, wrongly
Antonyms: legally, lawfully, legitimately
词性: 副词
