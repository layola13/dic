autocratical:/ˌɔːtəˈkrætɪkl/ | adj. | 独裁的，专制的 | Of or relating to an autocrat; absolute in power or authority. |  The autocratical ruler refused to listen to his people's pleas.  独裁的统治者拒绝听取人民的请求。 | The CEO's autocratical management style stifled creativity.  首席执行官的独裁管理风格扼杀了创造力。 | 近义词: dictatorial, tyrannical, despotic, authoritarian | 反义词: democratic, egalitarian | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From autocrat + -ical. Autocrat from Greek autokratēs (self-ruling), from autos (self) + kratos (strength, power).
