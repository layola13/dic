loan-translations:/ˈloʊn trænsˈleɪʃənz/ | n. |  翻译借词；语义转借 | A word or phrase adopted from another language by literally translating its component parts. |  The English word "skyscraper" is a loan-translation of the German word "Wolkenkratzer".  英语单词“skyscraper”（摩天大楼）是德语单词“Wolkenkratzer”的翻译借词。 | synonyms: calque, loan translation | antonyms: N/A | noun
