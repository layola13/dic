disbarment:/dɪsˈbɑːrmənt/
n.|取消律师资格，逐出律师界|The act of officially excluding a lawyer from the practice of law.  |His disbarment followed a conviction for fraud. 他的律师资格被取消是因为他被判犯有欺诈罪。|synonyms: expulsion, removal, exclusion|antonyms: reinstatement, admission, readmission|noun
