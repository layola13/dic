algologist:/ælˈɡɒlədʒɪst/ | n. |藻类学家|A scientist who studies algae (phycology).|Example: The algologist collected samples from the lake to study the algal bloom.  这位藻类学家从湖中采集样本以研究藻华。|Example:  The algologist identified a new species of algae.  这位藻类学家发现了一种新的藻类。|Synonym: phycologist|Antonym: N/A|noun
