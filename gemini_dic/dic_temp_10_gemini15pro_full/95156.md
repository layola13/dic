inarguably:/ɪnˈɑːrɡjuəbli/
adv.
无可争辩地，毋庸置疑地
Undeniably; cannot be argued against; beyond dispute.
Her performance was inarguably the best of the night. 她当晚的表演无疑是最好的。
Synonyms: undeniably, indisputably, unquestionably, certainly, definitely
Antonyms: arguably, disputably, questionably, possibly, doubtfully
词性: 副词
