alkekengi:/ælˈkɛkɪndʒiː/
noun
酸浆,灯笼果
A plant with bright orange-red, papery husks enclosing edible berries.
The alkekengi lanterns glowed in the twilight. 酸浆的灯笼在暮色中闪闪发光。
synonyms: Chinese lantern plant, bladder cherry, winter cherry
antonyms: N/A
词性: 名词


alkekengi:/ælˈkɛkɪndʒiː/
词源: From Medieval Latin *alkekengi, from Arabic *alkaknaj, ultimately from Persian *kākanaj.
用法说明:  This word refers to the plant *Physalis alkekengi*, also known as the Chinese lantern plant or bladder cherry. It's primarily used in botanical or horticultural contexts.  While edible, the berries are not commonly consumed. The decorative husks are more often the focus.
