fawner:/ˈfɔːnər/ (US), /ˈfɔːnə(r)/ (UK)| n. |奉承者，拍马屁的人|A person who gives a servile display of exaggerated flattery or affection, typically in order to gain favor or advantage.|The fawner constantly praised the boss, hoping for a promotion. 这个奉承者不断地赞扬老板，希望能得到晋升。|Synonyms: sycophant, flatterer, yes-man, toady, lickspittle|Antonyms: critic, detractor|Noun
