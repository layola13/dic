crabgrasses:/ˈkræbˌɡræsɪz/
n.
crabgrasses
马唐属植物；蟋蟀草
Any of various weedy grasses of the genus Digitaria, especially D. sanguinalis (large crabgrass or hairy crabgrass) and D. ischaemum (smooth crabgrass), which are common lawn pests.
例句：Crabgrasses are a common problem in lawns during the summer.  夏天草坪里常见的杂草是马唐。

例句：The crabgrasses have overtaken the entire garden. 马唐已经侵占了整个花园。

近义词：weeds,  grass
反义词: desirable plants
