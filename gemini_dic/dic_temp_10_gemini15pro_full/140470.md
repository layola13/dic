old-maidism:/ˌoʊldˈmeɪdɪzəm/ | n. | 老处女主义，过分拘谨，古板 | The state of being an old maid, often characterized by excessive neatness, primness, and fussiness. It also refers to the stereotypical behaviors and attitudes attributed to unmarried women, particularly those considered to be past the typical age for marriage. |  She exhibited classic old-maidism, fussing over every detail and constantly rearranging the furniture. 她表现出典型的老处女作风，对每个细节都吹毛求疵，不断地重新摆放家具。

Her old-maidism prevented her from enjoying the spontaneous fun of the party. 她过分拘谨的性格让她无法享受派对上自发的乐趣。 |  primness, prudery, fussiness |  open-mindedness, flexibility,  easygoingness | noun
