hazarding:/ˈhæzərdɪŋ/ | v. | 冒险；大胆提出；使遭受危险 | venturing something, often with the risk of loss or failure; putting something at risk |  He was hazarding a guess. 他在冒险猜测。 | synonyms: venturing, risking, gambling, speculating | antonyms: securing, protecting, safeguarding | verb

hazarding:/ˈhæzərdɪŋ/ | v. | 冒险；大胆提出；使遭受危险 | exposing to danger or risk |  The company was hazarding its future by not investing in new technology.  这家公司因没有投资新技术而使其未来处于危险之中。| synonyms: endangering, jeopardizing, imperiling | antonyms: protecting, shielding, safeguarding | verb
