bid:/bɪd/ | n. vt. vi.| 投标，出价，努力，争取 | An offer of a particular amount of money for something that is being sold. |  I placed a bid of $100 for the antique vase. 我为那只古董花瓶出价100美元。 | synonyms: offer, tender, proposal | antonyms: withdraw, decline, reject | noun, verb

bid:/bɪd/ | n. vt. vi.| 命令，吩咐，邀请 | To command or order someone to do something. |  She bade him farewell. 她向他告别。 | synonyms: command, order, instruct | antonyms: request, ask, implore | verb

bid:/bɪd/ | n. vt. vi.| 尝试，企图 | An attempt to achieve or obtain something. |  His bid for the presidency failed. 他竞选总统失败了。 | synonyms: attempt, effort, try | antonyms:放弃，abstain | noun, verb
