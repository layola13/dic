myor:/ˈmaɪɔːr/ | n. | 市长 | A mayor is the elected leader of a city or town. | The mayor announced a new initiative to improve public transportation. 市长宣布了一项改善公共交通的新举措。| Example: The mayor vetoed the city council's proposal. 市长否决了市议会的提案。 | Synonyms: city manager, burgomaster, chief magistrate | Antonyms: citizen, resident | noun
