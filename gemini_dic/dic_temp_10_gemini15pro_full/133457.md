nonfealties: /nɒnˈfiːəltiz/
noun
不效忠; 不忠诚;  (尤指对封建领主的)不尽义务;  反叛
lack of fealty; disloyalty; especially refusal to perform feudal obligations
The king punished the nonfealties of his rebellious barons. 国王惩罚了他那些反叛的男爵的不忠。
近义词: disloyalty, treachery, infidelity, rebellion
反义词: loyalty, fidelity, allegiance
