autochthonous: /ɔːˈtɒkθənəs/ /ɑːˈtɑːkθənəs/ | adj. | 土生土长的，本地的 | Originating or occurring naturally in a particular place; indigenous. | The island has an autochthonous population.  这个岛屿有土生土长的人口。|  Researchers discovered an autochthonous species of frog. 研究人员发现了一种土生土长的青蛙。 | 近义词: indigenous, native | 反义词: foreign, exotic | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From Greek *autokhthōn* (meaning "sprung from the land itself"), from *autos* ("self") + *khthōn* ("earth").
