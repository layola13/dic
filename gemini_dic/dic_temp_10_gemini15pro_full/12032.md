articled:/ˈɑːrtɪk(ə)ld/
adjective
已签订协议的；已订婚的；已受条款约束的
bound by articles of apprenticeship; engaged.
例句：He's an articled clerk. 他是一名签约的职员。

例句：She is articled to a solicitor. 她已签约给一名律师做学徒。

近义词：bound, contracted, engaged
反义词：free, unengaged, unbound
词性：adjective
