mainly:/ˈmeɪnli/| adv.|主要地；大体上；基本上|Primarily; for the most part.  The group consists mainly of doctors.|例句：The problem is mainly due to a lack of funding. 这个问题主要是由于缺乏资金。|例句：I mainly eat vegetables. 我主要吃蔬菜。|近义词：primarily, chiefly, largely, mostly|反义词：rarely, seldom, slightly|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mainli, from main (“important, principal, chief, main”) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  Ultimately from Old French maine (“hand”) and Latin manus (“hand”). The semantic shift occurred because the right hand was considered the dominant hand.
