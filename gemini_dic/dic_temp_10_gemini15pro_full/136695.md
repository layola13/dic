nonsegmentally:/ˌnɒnˌsɛɡˈmɛntəli/ /ˌnɑːnˌsɛɡˈmɛntəli/| adv.|非分段地，不分段地|Without being divided into segments; as a whole, considering features that extend over more than one sound segment.|The tone is applied nonsegmentally to the entire utterance. 声调非分段地应用于整个话语。|non-segmentally, holistically|segmentally|adverb
Etymology->
From non- + segmentally.  Segmentally comes from segment + -ally. Segment comes from Late Middle English, from Latin segmentum "piece cut off," from secare "to cut."
