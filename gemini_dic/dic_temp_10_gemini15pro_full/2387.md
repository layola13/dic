adipose:/ˈædɪˌpoʊs/ (US) /ˈædɪpəʊs/ (UK)| adj.|脂肪的；肥胖的|Relating to animal fat; consisting of, resembling, or relating to fat.|Bears accumulate adipose tissue before winter. 熊在冬天来临之前积累脂肪组织。

The surgeon removed the excess adipose tissue.  外科医生切除了多余的脂肪组织。|近义词：fatty, obese, corpulent|反义词：lean, thin|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adipōsus*, from adipis (“fat”).

USAGE->
Adipose is typically used to describe tissues or cells related to fat.  It's less commonly used to describe a person as being fat.

Example 1:  Adipose tissue plays a crucial role in energy storage. 脂肪组织在能量储存中起着至关重要的作用。

Example 2: The researchers studied the effects of the drug on adipose cells. 研究人员研究了这种药物对脂肪细胞的影响。

Example 3:  People with excess adipose tissue around their middles are at higher risk for heart disease.  腹部脂肪组织过多的人患心脏病的风险更高。

Example 4: The biopsy revealed the presence of adipose tumors. 活检显示存在脂肪瘤。

PHRASE->
adipose tissue = 脂肪组织
adipose cells = 脂肪细胞
excess adipose tissue = 过多的脂肪组织
brown adipose tissue = 棕色脂肪组织 (A type of fat involved in thermogenesis)
white adipose tissue = 白色脂肪组织 (A type of fat primarily involved in energy storage)
