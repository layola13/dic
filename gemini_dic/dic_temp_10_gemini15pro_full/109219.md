leiger:/ˈliːdʒər/| n. |常驻大使，常驻代表|(especially in former times) a resident ambassador.|The king sent a leiger to the French court. 国王派了一位常驻大使到法国宫廷。|近义词:ambassador, envoy, resident, legate |反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 transient, temporary visitor)|词性:noun

词源: From Middle French *legier*, from Old French *legier* (“one who lies (stays) somewhere”), present participle of *legier*, from Latin *iacēre* (“to lie”). Doublet of *lair*.
