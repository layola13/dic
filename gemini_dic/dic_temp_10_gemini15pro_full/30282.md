cementless:/sɪˈmɛntləs/
adjective
无水泥的
Without cement.  Used especially of prosthetic implants that are fixed in place by other means, such as bone ingrowth.
The surgeon opted for a cementless hip replacement.  外科医生选择了无水泥髋关节置换术。
near synonyms: uncemented
antonym: cemented
词性: 形容词
