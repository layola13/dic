out-talk:/ˌaʊtˈtɔːk/
verb
胜过…的讲话；比…说得更多
to talk longer or more loudly than someone else so that they cannot be heard
He out-talked his opponents and won the debate. 他在辩论中说得比对手多，最终赢得了辩论。
outargue, outspeak
be out-talked by
verb
