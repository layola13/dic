overcome:/ˌoʊvərˈkʌm/ (US); /ˌəʊvəˈkʌm/ (UK)| v. |克服，战胜，征服|To successfully deal with a problem or difficulty; to defeat or conquer.|She overcame her shyness and gave a great presentation. 她克服了害羞，做了一个很棒的演讲。|近义词：conquer, defeat, surmount, vanquish|反义词：succumb, yield, surrender|词性:动词

overcome:/ˌoʊvərˈkʌm/ (US); /ˌəʊvəˈkʌm/ (UK)| adj. |被（某种情绪）压倒的，无法控制的|Overwhelmed by emotion or feeling.|He was overcome with grief when he heard the news.  他听到这个消息时悲痛欲绝。|近义词：overwhelmed, overpowered|反义词：calm, composed|词性:形容词
