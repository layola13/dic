nonconventional:/ˌnɒnkənˈvɛnʃənəl/ (US) /ˌnɒnkənˈvenʃənəl/ (UK)| adj.|非传统的，非惯例的|Not conforming to accepted, established, or traditional patterns, methods, or standards.|Example:  She has a nonconventional approach to teaching. 她有一种非传统的教学方法。

Example:  They chose a nonconventional wedding venue. 他们选择了一个非传统的婚礼场地。|Synonyms: unorthodox, unconventional, atypical, unusual, irregular, non-traditional, alternative, innovative, groundbreaking, pioneering, avant-garde, experimental, radical, bohemian, freethinking, independent, idiosyncratic, eccentric, offbeat, quirky|Antonyms: conventional, traditional, orthodox, usual, typical, standard, customary|adjective
