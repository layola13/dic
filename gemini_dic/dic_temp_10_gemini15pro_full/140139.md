off-whites:/ˌɔːfˈwaɪts/ | n. adj. | 灰白色，米白色 | A color that is a very pale tint of white, often with a slight yellowish or grayish tinge.  It's essentially white with a touch of another color, making it less stark than pure white. |  She wore an off-white dress to the wedding. 她穿了一件米白色的连衣裙去参加婚礼。 | Synonyms: cream, ivory, eggshell | Antonyms: black, pure white | noun, adjective
