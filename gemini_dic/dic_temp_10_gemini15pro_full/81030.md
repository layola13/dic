graduation: /ˌɡrædʒuˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 毕业；毕业典礼；刻度；分等级 | the successful completion of a course of study; a ceremony at which degrees or diplomas are conferred; a mark on a scale |  She attended her son's graduation. 她参加了儿子的毕业典礼。 | synonyms: commencement, completion, convocation | antonyms:  inception, beginning, commencement | noun
