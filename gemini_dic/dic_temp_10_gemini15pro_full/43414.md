crantara:/krænˈtɑːrə/ | n. | 喧闹的聚会；狂欢 | A lively, noisy celebration or party, often involving music, dancing, and drinking. | The annual crantara was a highlight of the village calendar, bringing together young and old for a night of revelry.  村里的年度狂欢是日历上的一个亮点，让老老少少聚在一起狂欢一夜。 | 近义词：revelry, festivity, celebration, carousal | 反义词：wake, funeral | 词性：名词
