palliative:/ˈpæliətɪv/ /ˈpælɪətɪv/| adj. n.|缓和的，治标的；缓和剂|Relieving pain or alleviating a problem without dealing with the underlying cause. |The doctor prescribed palliative care for the terminally ill patient. 医生为这位晚期病人开了姑息疗法。|Synonyms: soothing, alleviating, mitigating, relieving |Antonyms: curative, exacerbating, aggravating|adjective

palliative:/ˈpæliətɪv/ /ˈpælɪətɪv/| n.|缓和剂，治标的措施|Something that relieves pain or alleviates a problem without dealing with the underlying cause. |The morphine was a palliative for his excruciating pain. 吗啡是他剧痛的缓和剂。|Synonyms: remedy, relief, solace |Antonyms: cure, aggravation|noun
