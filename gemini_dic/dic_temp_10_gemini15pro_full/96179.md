indesert:/ɪnˈdɛzərt/ | vt. | 抛弃，遗弃 | To leave someone or something in a place with no intention of returning or providing further help. |  He indeserted his family and ran off with another woman. 他抛弃了家人，和另一个女人跑了。

She indeserted her post when the going got tough.  当情况变得艰难时，她放弃了自己的职位。 | 近义词: abandon, forsake, desert | 反义词: support, assist, help | 词性: 动词

词源:  From in- + desert (v.), from Latin *deserere, frequentative of deserere "leave, abandon," from de- "down, away" + serere "to join, link, bind."  The prefix "in-" in this case intensifies the meaning of "desert".
