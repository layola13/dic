disarrayed:/dɪsəˈreɪd/
adjective
混乱的，紊乱的
thrown into a state of disorganization or untidiness.
Her clothes were disarrayed, strewn across the floor. 她的衣服乱七八糟地散落在地板上。
The troops were disarrayed after the surprise attack.  突袭之后，部队被打乱了阵脚。
近义词: disordered, chaotic, messy, jumbled, confused, disorganized
反义词: ordered, organized, tidy, neat, arranged

词性: adjective
