antinordic:/ˌæntiˈnɔːrdɪk/ | adj. | 反北欧的，反对北欧的 | Opposed to or hostile towards Nordic countries, cultures, or peoples.  |  The antinordic sentiment was evident in his criticism of their social policies.  他对他们社会政策的批评中明显地体现了反北欧的情绪。 | Example: Some southern European countries expressed antinordic views during the economic crisis. 一些南欧国家在经济危机期间表达了反北欧的观点。 |  Near Synonyms: anti-Scandinavian | Antonyms: pro-Nordic, Scandinavianist | adjective
