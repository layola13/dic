naturelike:/ˈneɪtʃərlaɪk/
adjective
自然的，像自然的
Resembling nature; natural.
The garden had a naturelike quality, with winding paths and wild flowers.  这个花园有一种自然的气息，蜿蜒的小路和野花。
The artificial waterfall looked surprisingly naturelike. 人造瀑布看起来出奇的自然。
近义词:natural, naturalistic, lifelike
反义词:artificial, unnatural, synthetic
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From nature + like.  Nature comes from Old French nature, from Latin natura "nature," literally "birth, origin, character," from natus, past participle of nasci "to be born." Like comes from Old English gelic "similar," from Proto-Germanic *galikaz "having the same form."
