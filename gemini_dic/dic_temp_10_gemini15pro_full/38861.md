confederates:/kənˈfɛdərɪts/| n.|同盟者，同伙，共犯|People who have united for a common purpose, especially in a conspiracy or rebellion. |The bank robbers and their confederates were eventually caught and brought to justice. 银行劫匪及其同伙最终被抓获并绳之以法。|Synonyms: allies, accomplices, associates, partners in crime|Antonyms: adversaries, enemies, opponents|noun

confederates:/kənˈfɛdərɪts/| adj.| 结盟的，同盟的|United in a confederacy or league. |The confederate states seceded from the Union.  这些邦联州脱离了联邦。|Synonyms: allied, federated, united|Antonyms: independent, separate, disunited|adjective
