collidine:/ˈkɒlɪdiːn/ /ˈkɑːlɪdiːn/| n. |可力丁，甲基吡啶|A colorless, flammable, toxic liquid, C₈H₁₁N, any of several isomeric methyl derivatives of pyridine, obtained from coal tar and bone oil and used in organic synthesis.|Collidine is used as a solvent in organic synthesis. 可力丁在有机合成中用作溶剂。|Synonyms: methylpyridine |Antonyms: None|noun


collidine:/ˈkɒlɪdiːn/ /ˈkɑːlɪdiːn/| n. |碰撞|An instance of colliding. (Rare usage, primarily seen in older texts)|The collidine of the two particles resulted in a release of energy. 两个粒子的碰撞导致能量释放。|Synonyms: collision, impact|Antonyms: separation|noun
