nonsolution:/ˌnɒn səˈluːʃən/ |n.| 无解，无效的方案 |Something that does not solve a problem; an inadequate or failed attempt at a solution. |The proposed policy is a nonsolution; it doesn't address the root causes of the problem. 这项提议的政策是一个无效的方案，它没有解决问题的根本原因。| The committee dismissed the proposal as a nonsolution and opted for a more practical approach. 委员会认为该提议是无效的方案，选择了更实际的方法。 |Near synonyms:  failure, flop, washout, dead end,  ineffective measure |Antonyms: solution, answer, remedy, resolution| noun
