designable:/dɪˈzaɪnəbl/ | adj. | 可设计的，可规划的 | Capable of being designed; suitable for being designed. |  This chair is designable to fit any body type. 这把椅子可以设计成适合任何体型。

The software is highly designable, allowing users to customize the interface.  这款软件具有高度可设计性，允许用户自定义界面。 |  Near synonyms:  plannable, configurable, customizable, adaptable | Antonyms:  inflexible, rigid, fixed, unchangeable | Word type: adjective
