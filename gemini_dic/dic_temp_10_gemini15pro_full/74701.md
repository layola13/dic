freelance:/ˈfriːlæns/ | adj. n. v. | 自由职业的；自由职业者；做自由职业 | a person who works freelance | He's a freelance journalist. 他是一名自由撰稿人。| synonyms: independent contractor, freelancer | antonyms: employee, staff member | noun
