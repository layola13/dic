leadway:/ˈliːdweɪ/ | n. |领先地位；领先优势；带头|A position of advantage or leadership. |The company has a significant leadway in the development of new technologies.  这家公司在新技术开发方面拥有显著的领先优势。| Synonyms: lead, advantage, head start | Antonyms: disadvantage, deficit | noun


Etymology:  From 'lead' (meaning 'to guide or direct') and 'way' (meaning 'path or route').  Essentially, the way of the leader.
