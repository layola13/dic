fissilinguia:/ˌfɪsɪˈlɪŋɡwə/
|n.|双舌，口是心非|A forked tongue; double-dealing; deceitful speech.  The act of speaking one way and acting another.|He showed his fissilinguia by promising to help but then doing nothing. 他承诺帮忙却什么也没做，显露出了他的两面三刀。|近义词：duplicity, hypocrisy, mendacity|反义词：honesty, sincerity|词性：名词

Etymology:
From Latin *fissilinguis* (having a forked tongue), from *fissus* (split, divided) + *lingua* (tongue).
