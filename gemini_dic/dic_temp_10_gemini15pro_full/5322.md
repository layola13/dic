allowancing:/əˈlaʊənsɪŋ/
n.
定量供应; 配给; 允许; 津贴;
The act of giving an allowance or the system of allowances.  It refers to the practice of allocating resources, especially money, in a systematic way.
The company's strict allowancing of office supplies meant employees had to request even basic items. 这家公司对办公用品的严格配给意味着员工即使是基本用品也必须申请。
Budgeting and allowancing are essential skills for managing personal finances. 预算和配给是管理个人财务的必备技能。
rationing, allocation, allotment, apportionment
granting, permission
