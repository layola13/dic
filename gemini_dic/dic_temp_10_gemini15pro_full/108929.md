leechlike:/ˈliːtʃlaɪk/
adjective
水蛭似的，寄生虫似的
Resembling or characteristic of a leech, especially in being bloodsucking or clinging.  Exploiting others, often in a parasitic way.
He had a leechlike advisor who drained his funds. 他有一个像水蛭一样的顾问，耗尽了他的资金。
The company's leechlike hold on its employees prevented them from seeking better opportunities. 这家公司对员工的控制像水蛭一样，阻止他们寻找更好的机会。
parasitic, bloodsucking, clinging, tenacious, exploitative
independent, generous, altruistic
