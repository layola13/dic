gasteropodous:/ˌɡæstərˈɒpədəs/| adj.|腹足类的；有腹足的|Relating to or characteristic of gastropods; having a broad foot on the ventral side of the body, used for crawling.|Snails and slugs are gasteropodous mollusks.  蜗牛和蛞蝓是腹足类软体动物。|The gasteropodous creature slowly made its way across the leaf.  这只腹足动物缓慢地爬过树叶。|Synonyms: gastropod, univalve|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, potentially bivalve for contrasting shell structure)|adjective
