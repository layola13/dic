hypotenuse: /haɪˈpɒtənjuːs/ (UK) /haɪˈpɑːtənˌuːs/ (US)| n. |斜边|The longest side of a right-angled triangle, opposite the right angle.|The hypotenuse of this triangle measures 10 cm.  这个三角形的斜边长10厘米。|Synonyms: long side (informal)|Antonyms: leg, cathetus|noun
