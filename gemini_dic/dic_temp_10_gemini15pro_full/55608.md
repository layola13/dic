disvaluation:/ˌdɪsvæljʊˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 贬值，低估 |  The act of devaluing or undervaluing something; a reduction in value. | The disvaluation of the currency led to economic chaos. 货币贬值导致了经济混乱。 | Synonyms: devaluation, depreciation, undervaluation | Antonyms: appreciation, valuation, increase | noun

disvalue:/ˌdɪsˈvæljuː/ | vt. | 贬低，低估 | To consider or represent as having less value or importance than is generally recognized. |  He tends to disvalue the contributions of his colleagues. 他倾向于低估同事的贡献。 | Synonyms: undervalue, depreciate, belittle | Antonyms: value, appreciate, overvalue | verb
