例句：
He had the audacious idea of starting his own business with no experience.  他有一个大胆的想法，那就是在没有经验的情况下自己创业。

例句：
It was an audacious plan, but it might just work.  这是一个大胆的计划，但它可能会成功。

近义词：bold, daring, adventurous, fearless, intrepid, brazen, impudent
反义词：timid, cautious, hesitant, reserved, cowardly
词性：adjective
