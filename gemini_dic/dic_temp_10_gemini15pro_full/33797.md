circumesophageal:/ˌsɜːrkəmɪˌsɒfəˈdʒiːəl/ /ˌsɜːrkəmɛˌsɑːfəˈdʒiːəl/
adjective
环食道的，绕食道的
Encircling the esophagus.  The vagus nerve has a circumoesophageal distribution.
The circumoesophageal ganglion connects the brain to the ventral nerve cord in invertebrates.  迷走神经有环绕食道的分布。环食道神经节连接无脊椎动物的大脑和腹神经索。
Synonym: periesophageal
Antonym: None (lack of a direct anatomical opposite)
词性: adjective
