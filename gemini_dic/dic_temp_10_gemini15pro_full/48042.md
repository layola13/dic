deckest:/ˈdɛkɪst/
adjective
最华丽的，装饰最盛的 (古英语，最高级)
A superlative adjective, archaic form of "decked," meaning "adorned" or "decorated" to the greatest extent.  Derived from the Old English word "deccan."
例句：The hall was deckest with boughs of holly for the Yuletide feast.  大厅用冬青树枝装饰得最华丽，以迎接圣诞节盛宴。

例句：The knight wore the deckest armour, shining brightly in the sun.  骑士穿着最华丽的盔甲，在阳光下闪闪发光。

近义词：most ornate, most decorated, fanciest
反义词：plainest, simplest, most austere
词性: adjective
