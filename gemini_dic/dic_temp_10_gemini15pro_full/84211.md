half-rises:/ˈhɑːf raɪzɪz/
noun
半起身；半起立
A movement where you lift your upper body halfway up, often used in Pilates or other exercise routines.
He did ten half-rises, engaging his core muscles.  他做了十个半起身，锻炼核心肌肉。
The Pilates instructor guided them through a series of controlled half-rises. 普拉提教练指导他们完成一系列受控的半起身动作。
near synonyms:  partial sit-ups, mini crunches
opposite: lie-downs, full sit-ups
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
"half" (Old English healf) + "rise" (Middle English risen, from Old Norse rísa)
