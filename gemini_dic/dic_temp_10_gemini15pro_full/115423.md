mantises:/ˈmæntɪsiːz/
noun
螳螂(复数)
Plural form of mantis, referring to insects of the order Mantodea, characterized by their prominent front legs adapted for grasping prey.
I saw two mantises in my garden yesterday. 我昨天在我的花园里看到了两只螳螂。
The mantises were camouflaged against the green leaves. 这些螳螂伪装在绿叶中。
Synonyms:mantids
Antonyms:N/A
