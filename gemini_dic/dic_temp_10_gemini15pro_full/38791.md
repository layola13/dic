conduction:/kənˈdʌkʃən/| n.|传导，导电，导热|The conduction of heat through metal is very efficient. 金属的导热性非常高效。|例句：The conduction of electricity through copper wires is essential for powering our homes.  铜线对电的传导对于我们家庭供电至关重要。

例句：Heat conduction is a key factor in cooking. 热传导是烹饪的关键因素。

近义词：transmission, conveyance|反义词:insulation, resistance|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin conductio(n-), from conducere ‘bring together’ (see conduct).
