dragooning:/drəˈɡuːnɪŋ/
n.|强迫征兵，强迫，胁迫|The act of forcing someone into military service or other unwanted tasks. |He resented the dragooning of young men into the army. 他憎恨年轻人被强征入伍。|coercion, compulsion, press-ganging|voluntarism, persuasion|noun
dragoon:/drəˈɡuːn/
v.|强迫，胁迫|To force someone to do something against their will.|She was dragooned into helping with the preparations for the party. 她被迫帮忙准备派对。|force, coerce, compel, pressurize|persuade, encourage|verb
