forslouthe:/fɔːrˈslaʊð/ | v. | 懒惰，懈怠 | To waste in sloth; to idle away; to neglect. | He forslothed his duties and was subsequently fired. 他玩忽职守，后来被解雇了。 |  She forslothed the opportunity to study abroad. 她错过了出国留学的机会。 | 近义词: neglect, idle, shirk, dawdle | 反义词:  work, strive, persevere | 词性: 动词


词源:
From Middle English *forslouthen, from for- + slouthen (“to be slothful”), from slouth (“sloth”).  The prefix for- intensifying the meaning of sloth.  Essentially means "to be excessively slothful."
