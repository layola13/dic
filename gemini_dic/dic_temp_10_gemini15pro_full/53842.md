disconsolatory:/dɪskənˈsɒlətəri/ /dɪskənˈsɑːlətɔːri/
adjective
令人沮丧的；无法安慰的；使人灰心的
Causing or expressing dejection; incapable of being consoled.
The news of the defeat came as a disconsolatory blow.  这失败的消息传来，令人沮丧。
The disconsolatory weather matched her mood. 阴沉的天气和她沮丧的心情相符。
近义词: disheartening, discouraging, depressing, cheerless, gloomy
反义词: cheering, encouraging, comforting, uplifting
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *disconsolatorius, from Latin dis- ‘not’ + consolatorius ‘consolatory’.
