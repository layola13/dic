caput:/ˈkeɪpət/ (US), /ˈkæpʊt/ (UK)| adj. (informal, humorous)| 坏掉的，故障的|Broken, not working|My phone's gone completely caput. 我的手机彻底坏了。|Synonyms: broken, damaged, faulty, out of order, defunct|Antonyms: working, functional, operational, intact|adjective
