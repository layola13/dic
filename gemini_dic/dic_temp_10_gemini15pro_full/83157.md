gutterlike:/ˈɡʌtəlaɪk/
adjective
像排水沟的，像槽状的
Resembling or suggestive of a gutter, especially in being narrow, deep, or dirty.
The narrow, gutterlike alley was dark and damp.  狭窄的、像排水沟般的小巷又黑又潮湿。
The ravine was a deep, gutterlike chasm.  峡谷是一条深邃的、像排水沟一样的裂缝。
近义词:  channel-like, trench-like, ditch-like
反义词:  wide, open, spacious
词性: adjective
