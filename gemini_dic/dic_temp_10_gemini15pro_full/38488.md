conchometer:/kɒŋˈkɒmɪtər/ | n. | 贝壳测量器; 贝壳厚度测量计 | An instrument for measuring the thickness of shells, especially those of mollusks. | The archaeologist used a conchometer to determine the age of the shell. 考古学家用贝壳测量器来确定贝壳的年代。|  The museum curator carefully measured the shell with a conchometer.  博物馆馆长用贝壳测量器仔细测量了贝壳。 | 近义词: caliper (卡尺, more general) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Greek *konkhe* (shell) + *-meter* (measure).
