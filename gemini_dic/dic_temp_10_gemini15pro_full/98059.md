inquest:/ˈɪŋkwɛst/ | n. | 调查；审讯（尤指死因调查）| A judicial inquiry, especially one held to determine the cause of a person's death. | The coroner ordered an inquest into the cause of death.  验尸官下令调查死因。| Synonyms: inquiry, investigation, probe, examination | Antonyms: concealment, cover-up | noun

inquest:/ˈɪŋkwɛst/ | n. | (对某事的)探究 | A seeking or request for information. |  There was a public inquest into the government's handling of the crisis. 公众对政府处理危机的方式进行了探究。 | Synonyms: exploration, research, study | Antonyms: ignorance, apathy | noun
