castable:/ˈkɑːstəbl/ (US) /ˈkɑːstəbəl/ (UK)
adjective|可浇铸的，可铸造的|able to be cast (into a particular shape or form).|This type of concrete is highly castable and durable. 这种类型的混凝土易于浇铸且经久耐用。|The molten metal was castable into various shapes. 熔化的金属可以浇铸成各种形状。|moldable, pourable|non-castable
noun|可浇铸材料|a substance that can be cast (into a particular shape or form).|This new castable is ideal for creating intricate designs.  这种新的可浇铸材料非常适合制作复杂的图案。|The factory uses a special castable for its products. 这家工厂使用一种特殊的可铸造材料来生产其产品。|moldable material, pourable material|non-castable material


词源:
From "cast" (verb) + "-able" (suffix).  "Cast" comes from Old Norse "kasta" (to throw). "-able" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being," from Old French "-able" and Latin "-abilis."
