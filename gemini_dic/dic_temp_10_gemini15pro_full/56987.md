double-dated:/ˌdʌbl ˈdeɪtɪd/
adj.
有双重日期的，(支票等)写有两个日期的；(尤指年轻人)四人约会
Bearing two dates.  Used especially of checks or other documents. A date involving two couples.
The check was double-dated, raising suspicions of fraud. 这张支票写了两个日期，引起了人们对欺诈的怀疑。
They went on a double-dated to the movies. 他们四个一起去看电影。
近义词: dual-dated
反义词: (No direct antonym, could use "single-dated" in context of dating)
词性: adjective
