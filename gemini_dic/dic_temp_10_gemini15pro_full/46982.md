darwinistic: /ˌdɑːrˈwɪnɪstɪk/ | adj. | 达尔文主义的；适者生存的 |  Characterized by the belief that the strongest or most adaptable will survive and prosper, often at the expense of the weaker. | 例句：The business world can be a darwinistic environment. 商业世界可能是一个适者生存的环境。 | 近义词：competitive, ruthless, cutthroat | 反义词：cooperative, altruistic | 词性：形容词
