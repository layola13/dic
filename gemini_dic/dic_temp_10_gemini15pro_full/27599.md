ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English cancellacion, from Late Latin cancellātiō, from Latin cancellō (“to cross out, cancel”), from cancelli (“lattice”), from cancer (“crab, lattice”).  The sense development is from crossing out writing with lattice-like strokes.
