harmonized:/ˈhɑːrmənaɪzd/ | /ˈhɑːr.mə.naɪzd/ | adj. | 和谐的，协调的 | brought into agreement or accord; made compatible or consistent | The international trade laws have been harmonized. 国际贸易法已经协调统一。| The different parts of the machine were harmonized to work together smoothly.  机器的不同部件协调一致地工作。| 近义词：coordinated, unified, consistent, balanced, integrated | 反义词：discordant, conflicting, dissonant, incompatible | 词性: adjective

harmonize:/ˈhɑːrmənaɪz/ | /ˈhɑːr.mə.naɪz/ | v. | 使和谐，协调；使一致 | to bring into agreement or accord; to make compatible or consistent |  The government is trying to harmonize the different tax laws. 政府正在努力协调不同的税法。|  We need to harmonize our efforts to achieve our goal. 我们需要协调我们的努力来实现我们的目标。| 近义词：coordinate, unify, reconcile, integrate, synthesize | 反义词：clash, conflict, disagree,  discord | 词性: verb

词源: From Late Latin *harmonizāre, from Ancient Greek ἁρμονίζειν (harmonízein, “to fit together, join”), from ἁρμονία (harmonía, “harmony”).
