Ephesians: /ɪˈfiːʒənz/ /ɪˈfiːʒiənz/
| n. | 以弗所人; 以弗所书 | People from the ancient city of Ephesus; The Book of Ephesians in the New Testament. |  The Ephesians were known for their temple of Artemis. 以弗所人以他们的阿耳忒弥斯神庙而闻名。|  Paul wrote a letter to the Ephesians. 保罗写了一封信给以弗所人。 | synonyms: Ephesian Christians, recipients of Paul's letter | antonyms: N/A | noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *Ephesinus, from Greek *Éphesios, from Éphesos ‘Ephesus’.
