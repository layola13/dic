melinis:/məˈliːniːz/ | n. | 梅利尼斯（一种希腊开胃菜）| A Greek appetizer typically consisting of fried eggplant slices topped with a mixture of feta cheese, tomatoes, and herbs, often served with tzatziki. | Example: We started our meal with a plate of delicious melinis. 我们以一盘美味的梅利尼斯开始用餐。| Example: The melinis were perfectly crispy and the feta topping was tangy and fresh. 这些梅利尼斯炸得非常酥脆，菲达奶酪的配料又酸又新鲜。| Synonym:  fried eggplant appetizer, Greek eggplant dip | Antonym: N/A | Noun
