electrotonize:/ɪˌlɛktroʊtənaɪz/ | v. |电紧张, 电钝|To modify the excitability of a nerve or muscle by passing a continuous current through it. | Example 1: The researchers electrotonized the nerve to study its response to stimuli. 研究人员电紧张神经以研究其对刺激的反应。

Example 2: Electrotonizing the muscle fibers allowed them to observe changes in contractility. 电紧张肌肉纤维使他们能够观察到收缩性的变化。| Near synonyms: stimulate, activate, polarize | Antonyms: inhibit, deactivate, depolarize | verb
