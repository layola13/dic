molendinaceous:/ˌmɒlɪndɪˈneɪʃəs/
adjective
磨坊的；像磨坊的；与磨坊有关的
Pertaining to a mill, especially a windmill; having the appearance of a windmill.
The old, molendinaceous structure stood proudly atop the hill, its sails long gone.  古老的、像磨坊一样的建筑骄傲地矗立在山顶上，它的帆早已不见了。
The artist captured the molendinaceous quality of the landscape in his painting.  艺术家在他的画中捕捉到了这片土地像磨坊一样的特质。
近义词:mill-like, mill-related
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider using terms like "un-mill-like," "dissimilar to a mill")
