birded:/bɜːrdɪd/ | v. |  观察到；记录（鸟类）| To have observed or recorded a species of bird. | Example: I birded three new species on my trip to Costa Rica. 我在哥斯达黎加的旅行中观察到了三种新鸟类。

Example:  She has birded extensively in Europe. 她在欧洲进行了广泛的鸟类观察。 | 近义词: observed (birds), recorded (birds), spotted (birds) | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: 动词


词源:  From the noun "bird" + "-ed" (past participle suffix used to form verbs).  来源于名词“bird” + “-ed”（过去分词后缀，用于构成动词）。
