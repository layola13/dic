hypersusceptibility:/ˌhaɪpər səˌsɛptəˈbɪləti/ | n. |过敏性，超敏性|Increased sensitivity or responsiveness to an agent, substance, or stimulus, often resulting in an exaggerated or unusual reaction.  A state in which an individual is excessively prone to or acutely affected by a disease or other stimulus.| People with certain genetic mutations may exhibit hypersusceptibility to specific infections. 某些基因突变的人可能对特定感染表现出过敏性。| hypersensitivity, increased susceptibility, heightened sensitivity | hyposensitivity, resistance | noun
