hoistaway:/ˈhɔɪst əˈweɪ/ | phrasal verb | 吊走，升走；（尤指用起重机）吊起并移走 | To lift and move something, especially with a crane or hoist, to another location. |  The workers hoisted away the old machinery.  工人们把旧机器吊走了。 |  The crane hoisted the container away from the ship. 起重机将集装箱从船上吊走。 | 近义词: lift away, remove, crane up,  | 反义词: lower, bring down | 词性: 动词短语

hoist:/hɔɪst/ |  vt. n. |  升起，吊起；起重机 | To raise or lift something, especially something heavy, using ropes or a machine.  A device used for lifting or raising something heavy. | They hoisted the flag to the top of the pole. 他们把旗子升到旗杆顶端。 |  The sailors hoisted the sails. 水手们升起了帆。 | 近义词: raise, lift, elevate | 反义词: lower | 词性: 动词/名词

away:/əˈweɪ/ | adv. | 离开；在远处；持续地 |  From a particular place or position; at a distance in space or time; continuously. |  He walked away without saying a word. 他一句话也没说就走开了。 |  The beach is miles away. 海滩在几英里外。 | 近义词: off, distant, apart | 反义词: near, close, toward | 词性: 副词
