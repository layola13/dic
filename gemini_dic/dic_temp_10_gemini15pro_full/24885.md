brucine:/ˈbruːsiːn/ | n. | 番木鳖碱 |A very poisonous alkaloid, C₂₃H₂₆N₂O₄, similar to but less potent than strychnine, obtained from nux vomica and other strychnos plants. |Brucine is sometimes used as a pesticide. 番木鳖碱有时被用作杀虫剂。|  Brucine can be used to separate racemic mixtures of chiral compounds. 番木鳖碱可以用来分离手性化合物的消旋混合物。 | strychnine |  None | noun
