misvalues:/ˌmɪsˈvæljuːz/
verb|错误估价；低估；不重视|To underestimate or fail to appreciate the true worth or importance of something or someone.|She misvalues her own abilities and constantly undersells herself. 她低估了自己的能力，总是贬低自己。| undervalues, underestimates, underrates|overvalues, overestimates, appreciates|v.
misvalue:/ˌmɪsˈvæljuː/
noun|错误估价；低估|An underestimate or failure to appreciate the true worth or importance of something or someone.|The company's misvalue of its assets led to financial difficulties. 公司对其资产的错误估价导致了财务困难。| undervaluation, underestimation|overvaluation, overestimation|n.
