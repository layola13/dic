lay-it-on:/ˌleɪ ɪt ˈɑːn/ | phrasal verb |  坦白说;  直截了当地说;  责备;  批评;  | To tell someone something frankly, often something unpleasant or critical; to exaggerate or embellish a story. | "Lay it on me, doc, what's the diagnosis?"  医生，坦白告诉我，诊断结果是什么？| Synonyms: be frank, be honest, come clean, tell it like it is, give it to someone straight, exaggerate, embellish | Antonyms:  sugarcoat, beat around the bush, be evasive | verb phrase
