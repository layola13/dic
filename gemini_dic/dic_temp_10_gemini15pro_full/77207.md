gasteropod:/ˈɡæstrəpɒd/ (US) /ˈɡæstrəpɑːd/| n. |腹足纲动物；腹足类动物|A mollusk of the large class Gastropoda, such as a snail, slug, or whelk, having a single shell or no shell at all, a distinct head, and a broad, flat foot. |Snails and slugs are common examples of gasteropods.  蜗牛和蛞蝓是腹足纲动物的常见例子。| The garden snail is a terrestrial gastropod.  花园蜗牛是一种陆生腹足纲动物。| synonyms: univalve, snail, slug | antonyms:  (no direct antonym for a taxonomic classification) | noun
