mugs:/mʌɡz/| n.|马克杯; 大杯; 脸; 傻瓜|A large cup, typically cylindrical with a handle and used without a saucer.  A person's face. A foolish or gullible person.|例句：He drank a mug of hot chocolate. 他喝了一大杯热巧克力。

例句：She’s got a real poker mug. 她有一张真正的扑克脸。|近义词：cups, faces, fools, dupes|反义词: geniuses|noun
