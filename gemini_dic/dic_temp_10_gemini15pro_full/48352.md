decryptions:/diːˈkrɪpʃənz/| n. |解密，破译|The process of transforming encrypted information into its original, understandable form.  It involves converting ciphertext back into plaintext using a key or algorithm.|The decryptions of the enemy's coded messages provided valuable intelligence.  敌方密码信息的解密提供了宝贵的情报。|近义词：decoding, decipherment|反义词:encryption, encoding|名词
