alpinely:/ælˈpaɪnli/
adverb.
高山地，高地地；高尚地
In a manner characteristic of high mountains; nobly or loftily.
He gazed alpinely at the distant peaks. 他高尚地凝视着远处的山峰。
近义词：highly, loftily, nobly
反义词：basely, lowly, ignobly
词性: 副词

词源: From alpine + -ly.  "Alpine" ultimately derives from the Latin "Alpes," referring to the Alps mountains.
