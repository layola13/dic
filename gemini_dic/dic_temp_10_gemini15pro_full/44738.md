crumbed:/krʌmd/ | v. |弄碎，捏碎；裹上面包屑|To break or coat something into small fragments, often breadcrumbs.  Specifically refers to coating food in breadcrumbs before cooking.| The chicken was crumbed and fried to a golden brown.  炸鸡裹上面包屑炸至金黄色。 | 近义词：breaded, coated | 反义词: whole | 词性：动词

crumbed:/krʌmd/ | adj. | 裹上面包屑的 | Covered in breadcrumbs. | We ordered crumbed fish and chips. 我们点了裹面包屑的炸鱼薯条。 | 近义词：breaded, coated | 反义词: plain, uncoated | 词性：形容词
