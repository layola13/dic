fucivorous:/fuːˈsɪvərəs/
adjective
食藻的；以海藻为食的
Feeding on seaweed or algae.
Example:  Fucivorous fish play an important role in maintaining the health of coral reefs.  食藻鱼在维护珊瑚礁的健康方面发挥着重要作用。

Example:  Scientists are studying the fucivorous habits of certain marine snails. 科学家正在研究某些海螺的食藻习性。

Synonym: algivorous
Antonym: carnivorous, herbivorous (depending on the specific context)
词性: adjective
