acipenser: /æsɪˈpɛnsər/
noun
鲟鱼
A genus of fish that includes the sturgeons.  These fish are known for their bony plates and are a source of caviar.
The beluga sturgeon, an acipenser, is critically endangered.  白鲟，一种鲟鱼，极度濒危。
Acipenser transmontanus is a species of sturgeon native to North America.  太平洋鲟是一种原产于北美的鲟鱼。
近义词: sturgeon
反义词: (No direct antonym for a genus of fish)
词性: noun
