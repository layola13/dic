despited: /dɪˈspaɪtɪd/ | prep. | 尽管，虽然 | Although something happened or is true.  Used to introduce a fact that makes the rest of the sentence surprising. | He went to work despite being ill. 尽管生病了，他还是去上班了。| 近义词: in spite of, notwithstanding, regardless of | 反义词: because of, due to | 词性: 介词

词源: From the past participle of *despite*, which comes from Old French *despit* "contempt, scorn," from Latin *despectus*, past participle of *despicere* "look down on, despise".
