hispaniola:/ˌhɪspænˈjoʊlə/ | n. | 海地岛（伊斯帕尼奥拉岛） | A large island in the Caribbean Sea, divided into the countries of Haiti and the Dominican Republic. |  Christopher Columbus landed on Hispaniola in 1492.  克里斯托弗·哥伦布于 1492 年登陆海地岛。

Hispaniola is the second largest island in the Caribbean. 海地岛是加勒比海地区的第二大岛。 | 近义词: Haiti and the Dominican Republic (referring to the two countries that make up the island), Española (an older spelling) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
