bedouin:/ˈbɛdʊɪn/ (US) /ˈbɛdʊɪn/ (UK)| n.|贝都因人；游牧的阿拉伯人|A nomadic Arab of the desert.|例句：The bedouin led a nomadic life, traveling from oasis to oasis. 贝都因人过着游牧生活，从一个绿洲旅行到另一个绿洲。

例句：Bedouin tribes traditionally rely on camels for transportation.  贝都因部落传统上依靠骆驼作为交通工具。|近义词：nomad, wanderer|反义词：settler, resident|词性：名词

词源: From French bédouin, from Arabic badawī, from badw “desert”.
