hockle:/ˈhɑːkəl/ | v. | 欺骗，诈骗；（尤指）向…要高价 | to cheat someone, especially by charging them too much money |  He hockled me out of $20. 他骗了我20美元。 | synonyms: cheat, swindle, defraud | antonyms: be honest, deal fairly | verb

hockle:/ˈhɑːkəl/ | n. | 跗关节；飞节 | the joint bending backwards in the hind leg of a horse, cow, etc. |  The horse injured its hockle. 这匹马伤了飞节。 | synonyms: ankle, joint | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *hokene, possibly related to hoek ("corner, angle").
