overcontrolled:/ˌoʊvərˈkənˈtroʊld/ | adj. | 过度控制的，过度克制的 | Excessively controlled, often referring to emotions or behavior.  Characterized by an excessive degree of control, often to the point of suppression or inhibition. | He was so overcontrolled that he rarely showed any emotion. 他太过于克制自己的情绪，以至于很少表露出来。 | The child's overcontrolled behavior made him seem withdrawn.  这个孩子过度控制的行为让他看起来很孤僻。 | 近义词: repressed, inhibited, restrained | 反义词: uninhibited, expressive, impulsive | 词性: adjective

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "controlled" (past participle of "control")
