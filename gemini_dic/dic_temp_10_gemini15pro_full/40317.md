contorts:/kənˈtɔːrts/
verb
扭曲，扭歪
twists or bends out of the normal or natural shape.
The acrobat contorted his body into unbelievable positions.  杂技演员把他的身体扭成了令人难以置信的姿势。
The pain made him contort his face. 疼痛使他扭曲了脸。
synonyms: twist, writhe, deform, distort
antonyms: straighten, smooth, align
词性:v.


contortion:/kənˈtɔːrʃən/
noun
扭曲，扭歪
a twisted or strained shape or position.
The contortions of the dancer's body were mesmerizing. 舞蹈演员身体的扭动令人着迷。
The mountain road was full of hairpin contortions. 山路充满了急转弯。
synonyms: twist, curve, bend, deformation
antonyms: straightness, smoothness
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin contortus, past participle of contorquēre "to twist violently," from com- "together" + torquēre "to twist."
