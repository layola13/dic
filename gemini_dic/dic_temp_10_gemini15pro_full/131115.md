nonbiological:/ˌnɒnˌbaɪəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ /ˌnɑːnˌbaɪəˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
adjective
非生物的;非起源于生物体的
Not derived from living organisms.
例句：The scientists are studying the effects of nonbiological factors on the environment.  科学家们正在研究非生物因素对环境的影响。

例句：The soil contains both biological and nonbiological components. 土壤包含生物和非生物成分。

近义词：abiotic, inorganic, synthetic
反义词：biological, organic, living
词性 adjective
