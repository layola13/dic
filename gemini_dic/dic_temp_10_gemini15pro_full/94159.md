impenetrability:/ɪmˌpɛnɪtrəˈbɪlɪti/| n. |不可渗透性；难以理解|The impenetrability of the jungle made it difficult to explore.  这片丛林的不可渗透性使得它难以被探索。|The impenetrability of his reasoning frustrated me. 他的推理难以理解，让我很沮丧。|近义词：impermeability, opacity, incomprehensibility|反义词：permeability, transparency, clarity|词性：名词

impenetrable:/ɪmˈpɛnɪtrəbl/| adj. |不可渗透的；难以理解的|The dense forest was impenetrable.  茂密的森林不可渗透。|His motives were impenetrable. 他的动机难以理解。|近义词：impervious, opaque, incomprehensible|反义词：permeable, transparent, clear|词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *impenetrabilitas, from Latin impenetrabilis (“impenetrable”) + -itas (suffix forming abstract nouns of quality).  Impenetrabilis is from in- (not) + penetrabilis (penetrable), ultimately from penetrare (to penetrate).
