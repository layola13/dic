mezuzoth:/məˈzuːzɒθ/| n. |门柱圣卷盒（复数）|A parchment inscribed with religious texts and attached in a case to the doorpost of a Jewish house as a sign and reminder of God's presence.|The mezuzoth on their doorposts were a visible sign of their faith.|近义词:phylacteries (in a broader sense, as both are reminders of religious commandments)|反义词:None (lack of a mezuzoth doesn't have a single-word antonym)|noun

mezuzah:/məˈzuːzə/| n. |门柱圣卷盒（单数）|A parchment inscribed with religious texts and attached in a case to the doorpost of a Jewish house as a sign and reminder of God's presence.|When entering the house, he touched the mezuzah as a sign of respect.|近义词:phylactery (in a broader sense)|反义词:None|noun
