decommercialize:/ˌdiːkəˈmɜːʃəlaɪz/
verb
使非商业化；去除商业性质
To remove from the commercial realm; to make less commercially oriented.
The government decided to decommercialize the national broadcaster. 政府决定将国家广播公司非商业化。
The artist tried to decommercialize their music by releasing it for free online.  这位艺术家试图通过在线免费发布音乐来去除其商业性质。
