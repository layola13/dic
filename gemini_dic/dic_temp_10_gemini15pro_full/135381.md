Etymology:

* non- (prefix):  From Latin *non*, meaning "not".
* perilous: From Old French *perilleus*, from Latin *periculosus*, meaning "dangerous," from *periculum* "danger," of uncertain origin.
* -ly (suffix): Forms adverbs from adjectives.
