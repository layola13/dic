gobies:/ˈɡoʊbiz/| n.|虾虎鱼|Small, often brightly colored fish belonging to the family Gobiidae, typically found in shallow marine or brackish waters.|例句：Gobies are often found in tide pools. 虾虎鱼经常在潮汐池中被发现。

例句：Many species of gobies have symbiotic relationships with invertebrates. 许多种类的虾虎鱼与无脊椎动物具有共生关系。|近义词：fish, darter|反义词:N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *gobio, from Latin gobius, gobio, a small fish, perhaps from Greek κωβιός (kōbios).
