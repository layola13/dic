etherealization:/ɪˌθɪəriəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
n.
灵化；空灵化；虚无缥缈化
The process of making something ethereal; the state of being etherealized.  The act of making something spiritual or otherworldly.
Her etherealization through meditation allowed her to transcend earthly concerns. 她通过冥想获得的灵性让她超越了尘世的忧虑。
spiritualization, refinement, sublimation
materialization, concretization
noun


etherealize:/ɪˈθɪəriəlaɪz/
v.
使…空灵；使…超凡脱俗
To make ethereal; to give an ethereal quality to. To deprive of earthly substance.
The artist attempted to etherealize the human form in his paintings.  这位艺术家试图在他的画作中使人体形象变得空灵。
spiritualize, refine, sublimate
materialize, concretize
verb
