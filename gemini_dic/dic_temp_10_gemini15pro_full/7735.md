anopla:/əˈnoʊplə/ | n. | 无吻动物门 | A phylum of marine worms characterized by the absence of a proboscis and the presence of a retractable introvert with rows of spines.  They are commonly known as spiny-headed worms. |  Anopla are exclusively marine animals. 无吻动物门是完全海洋生物。| 近义词: None (specific to taxonomic rank) | 反义词: None (specific to taxonomic rank) | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ἀ- (a-, 'without') + νόπλον (nóplon, 'weapon').  This refers to the lack of a proboscis, which in related groups is used for capturing prey and is considered a "weapon".
