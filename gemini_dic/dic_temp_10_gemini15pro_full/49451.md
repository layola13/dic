deltaic:/dɛlˈteɪɪk/
adjective
三角洲的；三角洲性质的
Relating to or like a delta, a landform at the mouth of a river.
The deltaic deposits are rich in nutrients. 三角洲的沉积物营养丰富。
The Mississippi River has a large deltaic plain. 密西西比河有一个巨大的三角洲平原。
近义词:alluvial, estuarine
反义词:(No direct antonym, consider upland, mountainous)
词性:adjective

词源:From delta + -ic.  Delta comes from the Greek letter Δ (delta), which resembles the triangular shape of a river delta.
