mana:/ˈmɑːnə/ | n. |  魔力，法力，精力；(太平洋岛屿)超自然力量 | Supernatural power or spiritual force believed to reside in people, places, or things, especially in Polynesian cultures; also, vital energy or life force. | The shaman drew on the mana of the ancient stones.  萨满汲取了古代石头的魔力。| synonyms: power, force, energy, magic, charisma | antonyms: weakness, impotence, lethargy | noun

mana:/ˈmɑːnə/ | n. |  (非正式)威望，声望，影响力 | (informal) Prestige, authority, influence, or charisma. | The CEO lost mana after the company's stock plummeted. 首席执行官在公司股票暴跌后失去了威望。| synonyms: prestige, authority, influence, charisma, clout | antonyms: insignificance, weakness, impotence | noun
