duckies:/ˈdʌkiz/| n.|小鸭子们，可爱的小东西们|A diminutive and affectionate term for ducks, often used when referring to multiple small or cute ducks, or even other cute objects.|Look at all those cute duckies in the pond!  看看池塘里所有那些可爱的小鸭子！|The baby was playing with her rubber duckies in the bath.  宝宝在浴缸里玩她的橡皮小鸭子。|近义词：ducklings, little ducks|反义词:|noun
