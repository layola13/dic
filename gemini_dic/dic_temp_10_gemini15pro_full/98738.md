insurrectionally:/ˌɪnsəˈrɛkʃənəli/
adv.
暴乱地，叛乱地
in a way that relates to or involves insurrection; rebelliously.
The group planned to act insurrectionally and overthrow the government.  该组织计划发动叛乱，推翻政府。
The protesters behaved insurrectionally, defying the authorities.  抗议者以叛乱的方式行事，公然反抗当局。
Near Synonyms: rebelliously, seditiously, mutinously
Antonyms: obediently, submissively, peacefully
词性: 副词
