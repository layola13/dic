emptiers:/ˈɛmptiərz/
noun|清空者，使空的人或物|People or things that make something empty.|The emptiers cleared the tables quickly after the meal.  用餐后，清空者迅速清理了桌子。|synonyms: clearers, evacuators|antonyms: fillers, loaders
word origin: From the verb "empty," derived from Old English "æmettig" meaning "at leisure, unoccupied," later evolving to mean "empty" in Middle English. The suffix "-er" denotes an agent or one who performs the action.
