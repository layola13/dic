pastorality:/ˌpæstəˈrælɪti/ | n. | 田园主义，田园风光，乡村生活 | The quality or state of being pastoral; a pastoral style or feeling.  It evokes a sense of peace, simplicity, and harmony with nature, often idealized. | The painting captured the pastorality of the English countryside. 这幅画捕捉到了英国乡村的田园风光。| 近义词：bucolicism, rusticity, idyll | 反义词：urbanity, sophistication | 词性: noun
