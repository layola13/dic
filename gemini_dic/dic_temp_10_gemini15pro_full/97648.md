inheritrices:/ɪnˈhɛrɪtrɪsiz/
n.
女继承人（复数）
A female person who inherits property, a title, or a right.
The inheritrices of the vast fortune were all under the age of twenty-one.  这些巨额财富的女继承人都未满21岁。
近义词:heiresses
反义词: (No direct antonym for a person inheriting.  One could consider "disinherited" as an opposite state but not an antonym for the person.)
词性:noun
