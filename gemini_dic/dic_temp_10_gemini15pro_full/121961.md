missionist:/ˈmɪʃənɪst/| n. |传教士；倡导者|A person sent on a religious mission, especially one sent to promote Christianity in a foreign country.  A person who advocates a belief, cause, or policy.|例句：The missionist spent years living among the indigenous people.  这位传教士在土著居民中生活了很多年。

例句：She is a passionate missionist for educational reform. 她是一位充满激情的教育改革倡导者。|近义词：evangelist, missionary, advocate, proponent, champion|反义词：opponent, antagonist|Noun
