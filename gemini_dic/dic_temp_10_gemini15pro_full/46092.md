cyclostylar:/ˌsaɪkloʊˈstaɪlər/
adjective
环柱的，环状花柱的
Having a style that forms a complete ring around the top of the ovary.
The cyclostylar ovary is a key characteristic of the dogbane family. 环柱状子房是夹竹桃科植物的一个关键特征。
The botanist identified the plant as belonging to the Apocynaceae family due to its cyclostylar flowers.  植物学家根据其环状花柱的花朵，将该植物鉴定为夹竹桃科。
