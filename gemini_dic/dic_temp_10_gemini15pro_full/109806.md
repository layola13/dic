The play was banned for its lewdness.  这出戏因为其淫秽内容而被禁演。|
近义词：lust, lasciviousness, salaciousness, indecency, obscenity|反义词：chastity, decency, modesty, purity|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *lewdnes*, equivalent to *lewd* + *-ness*.  *Lewd* comes from Old English *læwede* "lay, not clerical; ignorant, unlearned," later "base, vulgar, low."
