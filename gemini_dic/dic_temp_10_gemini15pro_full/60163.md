effemination:/ɪˌfɛmɪˈneɪʃən/| n. |柔弱化，女性化，娘化|The process of making someone or something appear or behave like a woman or girl.  The loss of strength and masculinity.|His effemination was apparent in his delicate gestures and high-pitched voice. 他的女性化表现在他优雅的手势和尖细的嗓音上。|Synonyms: feminization, softening, weakening|Antonyms: masculinization, strengthening|Noun
