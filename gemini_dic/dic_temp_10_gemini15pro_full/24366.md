bring-to-bear: /ˌbrɪŋ tə ˈber/ | v. |施加；运用；发挥；瞄准| To apply or exert (influence, pressure, etc.) on something; to direct or focus (something) on a target.| The company brought all its resources to bear on the problem. 公司运用其所有资源来解决这个问题。|  The artillery brought their guns to bear on the enemy position. 炮兵将火炮瞄准了敌人的阵地。| synonyms: apply, exert, utilize, employ, leverage, wield, direct, focus | antonyms: withhold, neglect, ignore | verb

bring-to-bear: /ˌbrɪŋ tə ˈber/ | n. | (罕见) 施加；运用 | The act of applying or exerting influence, pressure, etc. |  The bring-to-bear of political pressure forced a change in policy. 政治压力的施加迫使政策发生改变。| synonyms: application, exertion, utilization, employment | antonyms: inaction, negligence | noun (rarely used)
