outtraveling:/ˌaʊtˈtrævəlɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
超过…旅行的距离；比…更常旅行
Surpassing someone in the extent or frequency of travel.
He's outtraveling all his colleagues this year, visiting clients in over 20 countries.  他今年的出差次数超过了所有同事，访问了20多个国家的客户。
She's been outtraveling me lately, exploring remote corners of the world I haven't even heard of. 她最近的旅行比我多，探索了我闻所未闻的世界偏远角落。
近义词: outdoing, surpassing
反义词: being surpassed, being outdone
词性: verb (present participle)
