circumflant: /ˌsɜːrkəmˈflænt/ | adj. | 包围的，环绕的 | Flowing around and enclosing something; surrounding. | The circumflant river encircled the ancient city. 包围的河流环绕着这座古城。 | The circumflant atmosphere was thick with fog. 周围的大气中弥漫着浓雾。 | 近义词：surrounding, enclosing, encompassing | 反义词:  exposed, open | 词性: adjective

词源: From Latin circumflans, circumflant- (present participle of circumfluere ‘flow around’), from circum ‘around’ + fluere ‘to flow’.
