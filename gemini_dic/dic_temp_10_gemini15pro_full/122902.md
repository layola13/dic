molossus:/məˈlɒsəs/ (US) /məˈlɒsʊs/ (UK)| n.|长短短格; 扬抑抑格; 大型猛犬|A metrical foot consisting of three long syllables or, in accentual verse, three stressed syllables.  A large and powerful breed of dog.|The poet employed molossus for dramatic effect. 诗人运用扬抑抑格来达到戏剧性的效果。|Synonyms: mastiff, bulldog |Antonyms: toy dog| noun
