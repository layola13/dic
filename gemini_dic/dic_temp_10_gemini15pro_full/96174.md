indescribabilities:/ˌɪndɪˈskraɪbəˈbɪlɪtiz/ | n. | 不可名状的事物；无法形容的事物 |  The plural form of "indescribability," referring to things or qualities that are impossible to describe adequately. | The artist attempted to capture the indescribabilities of the natural world.  艺术家试图捕捉自然界无法形容的美妙。 | 近义词: inexpressibilities, ineffabilities | 反义词: describables, expressibles | 词性: noun

词源:
From in- (not) + describable (able to be described) + -ity (noun suffix forming abstract nouns) + -ies (plural suffix). Ultimately from Latin *describere* (to write down).
