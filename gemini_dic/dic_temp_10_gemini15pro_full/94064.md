impassible:/ɪmˈpæsɪb(ə)l/
adjective
无感情的；无感觉的；冷漠的
Not feeling or showing emotion.

The judge listened to the defendant's tearful plea with an impassible expression.
法官面无表情地听着被告声泪俱下的请求。

He remained impassible throughout the ordeal.
在整个磨难过程中，他始终保持着冷漠。

Synonyms: emotionless, unfeeling, indifferent, stoic, apathetic, phlegmatic, imperturbable, inscrutable
Antonyms: emotional, expressive, passionate, sensitive, sympathetic, responsive

Etymology: Late Middle English: from Old French impassible, from late Latin impassibilis, from in- ‘not’ + passibilis ‘capable of suffering’, from pati ‘suffer’.
