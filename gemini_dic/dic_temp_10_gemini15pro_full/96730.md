inefficient:/ˌɪnɪˈfɪʃnt/ | adj. | 无效率的，效率低的 | Not achieving maximum productivity; wasting or failing to make the best use of time or resources. | The inefficient system wasted both time and money. 这个低效的系统浪费了时间和金钱。 | inefficient, ineffective, unproductive | efficient, effective, productive | adjective

inefficients:/ˌɪnɪˈfɪʃnts/ | n. | 低效的人或事物 |  Plural form of inefficient, referring to people or things that are not efficient. | The inefficients in the company were let go. 公司解雇了那些效率低下的人。| inefficiencies, inadequacies | efficiencies, assets | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + efficient (from Latin efficiēns, present participle of efficiō “I bring about, accomplish,” from ex- “out” + faciō “I do, make”).
