inquisitive:/ɪnˈkwɪzətɪv/| adj.|好奇的，爱打听的|Given to inquiry; eager for knowledge; curious.  Characterized by a desire to learn and understand.|She has always been an inquisitive child, constantly asking "why?". 她一直是个好奇的孩子，总是问“为什么？”|
Example: His inquisitive nature led him to a career in journalism. 他好奇的天性使他从事了新闻业。|
Example: The inquisitive child opened every drawer in the house.  这个好奇的孩子打开了房子里的每一个抽屉。|
Synonyms: curious, inquiring, probing, investigative|
Antonyms: apathetic, indifferent, uninterested|
词性: adjective
