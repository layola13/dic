boominess:/ˈbuːminɪs/| n.|低沉的轰鸣声，嗡嗡声；（声音）浑浊|A booming, resonant, and often prolonged quality of sound, especially in a space or through a system.|The boominess of the bass made the music unpleasant.  低音的嗡嗡声让音乐很不悦耳。|resonance, rumble, hollowness|clarity, crispness|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From boom + -iness.  来自 boom（轰鸣）+ -iness（名词后缀，表示状态或性质）。
