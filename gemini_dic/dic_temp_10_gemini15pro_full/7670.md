anodization:/ˌænədaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n.|阳极氧化，阳极化处理|The process of coating a metal, particularly aluminum, with a protective oxide layer by electrolytic means.  This process increases corrosion resistance and can provide a decorative finish.|The anodization of the aluminum frame improved its durability.  铝框架的阳极氧化处理提高了它的耐用性。|Near Synonyms:  anodizing, oxidation, surface treatment|Antonyms:  cathodization (although not a direct opposite in all contexts), reduction|noun


anodize:/ˈænədaɪz/| v.|阳极氧化，阳极化处理|To coat a metal, particularly aluminum, with a protective oxide layer by electrolytic means.|The company anodizes aluminum parts for various industries.  这家公司为各行各业的铝制零件进行阳极氧化处理。|Near Synonyms:  oxidize, plate, coat|Antonyms:  reduce|verb
