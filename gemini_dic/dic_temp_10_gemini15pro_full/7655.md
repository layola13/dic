annunciation:/əˌnʌnsiˈeɪʃn/| n.|天使报喜；宣布；通告|The Annunciation is the announcement by the angel Gabriel to Mary that she would conceive and become the mother of Jesus, the Son of God, according to Christian belief. |根据基督教的信仰，天使报喜是天使加百列向玛丽宣布她将怀孕并成为上帝之子耶稣的母亲。|近义词：announcement, proclamation, declaration|反义词:concealment, suppression|noun
