alibis:/ˈælɪbaɪz/| n.|不在场证明；托辞，借口|A claim or piece of evidence that one was elsewhere when an act, typically a criminal one, is alleged to have taken place. | She had a perfect alibi for the night of the murder. 她有不在场证明，证明她在谋杀案发生当晚在别处。| synonyms: excuse, pretext, defense, justification | antonyms: accusation, confession, admission | noun
