multi-circuited:/ˌmʌltiˈsɜːrkɪtɪd/
adjective
多电路的
Having multiple circuits; composed of several circuits.
This multi-circuited system provides redundancy in case of failure. 这个多电路系统在出现故障时提供冗余。
The multi-circuited design ensures a stable power supply.  多电路设计确保了稳定的电源供应。
近义词: complex, intricate
反义词: simple, single-circuited
词性: adjective
