conceiting:/kənˈsiːtɪŋ/
adjective
自负的，骄傲自满的
having an excessively high opinion of oneself; vain.
He was conceiting about his musical talent. 他对自己的音乐才能非常自负。
近义词:vain, arrogant, egotistical, narcissistic
反义词:humble, modest, unassuming
词性:adjective

词源:
From conceit + -ing. Conceit itself comes from the Old French "conceit" meaning concept, thought, or opinion, which in turn derived from the Latin "concipere" meaning to conceive or grasp.  The modern meaning of excessive pride developed later.
