partitively:/pɑːrˈtɪtɪvli/
adverb|部分地，分配地|In a partitive way; with distributive force.|She divided the cake partitively among the children. 她把蛋糕分给了孩子们。|近义词:distributively, proportionally|反义词: wholly, entirely|词性:副词
词源: From Late Latin *partītīvus, from Latin partītus (past participle of partīre ‘to divide’), from pars, part- ‘part’.
