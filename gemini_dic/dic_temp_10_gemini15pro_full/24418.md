brisket:/ˈbrɪskɪt/| n.|牛胸肉|The cut of meat from the breast or lower chest of beef or veal.|We smoked a brisket for the barbecue. 我们熏了一块牛胸肉用来烧烤。|Synonyms: breast, chest|Antonyms: N/A|noun

brisket:/ˈbrɪskɪt/| n.|胸板|The breast of an animal, especially a quadruped.|The dog's brisket was broad and muscular. 这只狗的胸板宽阔而肌肉发达。|Synonyms: chest, breastbone|Antonyms: N/A|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *brusket, from Old Norse *brjósk, from Proto-Germanic *brusts, meaning "breast."  The word is related to "breast" itself.
