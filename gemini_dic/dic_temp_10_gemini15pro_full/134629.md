nonmaintenance:/ˌnɒnˈmeɪntənəns/ |n.| 非维护，免维护 |Lack of maintenance; the state of not requiring maintenance. |This product boasts nonmaintenance operation for the first five years.  这款产品标榜前五年免维护操作。|The software is designed for nonmaintenance operation, reducing IT workload. 该软件旨在实现免维护操作，从而减少IT工作量。|近义词: no-maintenance, maintenance-free|反义词: maintenance, upkeep|词性: noun

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "maintenance" (from Old French "maintenir," meaning "to hold in hand," ultimately from Latin "manu tenere")
