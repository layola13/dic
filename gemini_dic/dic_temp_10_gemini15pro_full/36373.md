coiners:/ˈkɔɪnərz/| n.|造币者；创造新词的人|People who create new words or expressions are called coiners.  创造新词或表达方式的人被称为造币者。|Shakespeare is considered one of the greatest coiners of the English language.  莎士比亚被认为是英语中最伟大的造词者之一。|The coiners of this new technology are revolutionizing the industry. 这些新技术的创造者正在彻底改变这个行业。|Synonyms: inventors, creators, originators|Antonyms: imitators, copiers|noun
