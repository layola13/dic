虚拟语气:
If the project had begun earlier, we would be finished by now. 如果这个项目早点开始，我们现在就完成了。
I wish they had begun the repairs sooner. 我希望他们早点开始修理。


被动语态:
Work has been begun on the new bridge. 新桥的建设工作已经开始。
The project was begun last year.  该项目于去年启动。


疑问句:
Has the meeting begun yet? 会议开始了吗？
When did the construction begin?  施工是什么时候开始的？


否定句:
The game hasn't begun yet.  比赛还没有开始。
The project hadn't begun when I left. 我离开时，项目还没有开始。
