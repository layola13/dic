hypersceptically:/ˌhaɪpərˈskeptɪkli/ | adv. | 极度怀疑地 | In an extremely skeptical manner, expressing excessive doubt or disbelief. | He looked at the proposal hypersceptically, questioning every detail. 他极度怀疑地看着这份提案，对每一个细节都提出了质疑。 |  She listened hypersceptically to his explanation, unconvinced by his assurances. 她极度怀疑地听着他的解释，并不相信他的保证。 | 近义词: extremely skeptically, overly doubtingly, cynically | 反义词: trustingly, credulously, naively | 词性: 副词
词源: From "hyper-" (meaning "over, excessive") + "skeptical" (from Greek "skeptikos," meaning "inquiring, thoughtful").
