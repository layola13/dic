munificent:/mjuːˈnɪfɪsənt/
adjective
慷慨的，丰厚的
very generous and giving a lot of money
例句：A munificent benefactor donated a million dollars to the hospital. 一位慷慨的捐助者向医院捐赠了一百万美元。

例句：The king made a munificent grant to the artist. 国王慷慨地资助了这位艺术家。

近义词：generous, bountiful, lavish, liberal
反义词：stingy, miserly, parsimonious
词性：adjective

词源：From Latin munificus ("generous, bountiful"), from munus ("gift, service, duty") + -ficus ("making, doing").
