inspired: /ɪnˈspaɪərd/ | adj. | 受到鼓舞的；有灵感的 | Filled with the urge or ability to do or feel something, especially to do something creative; stimulated. |  Her inspired performance moved the audience to tears. 她充满灵感的表演让观众感动得流下了眼泪。 |  motivated, stimulated, enthusiastic | uninspired, unmotivated | adjective


Etymology:
From Middle English *inspiren, from Old French inspirer, from Latin īnspīrāre (“to breathe or blow into”), from in- (“in, into, on, upon, at, towards”) + spīrāre (“to breathe”).
