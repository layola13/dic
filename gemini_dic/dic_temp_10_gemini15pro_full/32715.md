chlorinator:/ˈklɔːrɪneɪtər/ | n. | 氯化器；加氯机 | A device used to add chlorine to something, typically water, for the purposes of disinfection or sanitation. | The chlorinator keeps the pool water clean and safe.  泳池的氯化器保持池水清洁安全。

The hotel installed a new chlorinator to ensure the safety of the drinking water.  酒店安装了一个新的氯化器以确保饮用水的安全。|Synonyms: chlorination system, chlorine dispenser | Antonyms: dechlorinator | noun
