booziest:/ˈbuːziɪst/| adj.|最醉的，最爱喝酒的|Superlative form of boozy: most boozy.  It means extremely fond of or involving alcoholic drink; very drunk.|例句：It was the booziest party I've ever been to. 这是我去过的最醉人的派对。|近义词：drunken, intoxicated, inebriated|反义词:sober, temperate|词性:adjective
Etymology:  From booze + -y + -est. Booze comes from Middle Dutch būsen "to drink heavily."
