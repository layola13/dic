baptism:/ˈbæptɪzəm/| n.|洗礼，浸礼|A Christian ceremony in which a person is sprinkled with or immersed in water as a sign of purification and admission into the Christian faith.  The ceremony marking initiation into a particular Christian church or denomination.|例句：His baptism was a very important moment in his life. 他的洗礼是他人生中非常重要的时刻。

例句：The baptism of fire for the new recruits was a grueling experience.  对新兵来说，这次初次上战场的严酷考验是一次痛苦的经历。|近义词：christening, initiation, rite|反义词:None|词性:noun
