antoeci: /ænˈtiːsaɪ/ /ænˈtiːsiː/ | n. | 对蹠人 | People who live on opposite sides of the earth but at the same latitude, so that they have opposite seasons but the same length of day and night. |  The antoeci of the ancient Greeks were a source of fascination, their lives mirroring yet contrasting with their own.  对跖人对古希腊人来说是一个迷人的话题，他们的生活与古希腊人自身的生活既相似却又相反。

The antoeci of a given location experience midday and midnight at the same time.  一个特定地点的对跖人同时经历正午和午夜。 | 近义词: antipodes | 反义词: - | 词性: noun
