boree:/ˈbɔːriː/
n.
暴风雪，东北风
A sudden violent wind, especially a cold northerly wind.
The boree swept across the plains, bringing snow and ice. 暴风雪席卷了平原，带来了冰雪。
近义词: blizzard, gale, squall
反义词: calm, stillness
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From French *borée*, from Latin *Boreas*, the north wind, from Greek *Βορέας* (Boréas).
