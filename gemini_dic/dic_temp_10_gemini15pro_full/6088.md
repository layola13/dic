americanising:/əˈmɛrɪkənaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使美国化
The process of making something or someone more American in character or style.
Many immigrants find themselves americanising their names to fit in better. 许多移民发现自己为了更好地融入而使自己的名字美国化。
Fast food chains are americanising the diets of many countries. 快餐连锁店正在使许多国家的饮食美国化。

localize, nationalize
