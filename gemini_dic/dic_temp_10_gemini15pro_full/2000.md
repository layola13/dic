actualism:/ˈæktʃuəlɪzəm/| n.|现实主义;均变论|The doctrine that present-day processes can explain past geological events; uniformitarianism.|Geologists use actualism to infer past events from present processes. 地质学家利用现实主义从目前的过程推断过去发生的事件。

Actualism suggests that the same natural laws and processes that operate in the universe now have always operated in the universe in the past and apply everywhere in the universe.  现实主义认为，现在在宇宙中运行的相同的自然规律和过程，过去一直在宇宙中运行，并且适用于宇宙中的任何地方。|近义词：uniformitarianism|反义词: catastrophism|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French actualisme, from actuel (“actual, present”) + -isme.  Ultimately from Latin actualis, from actus (“deed, act”).

USAGE->
The principle of actualism is fundamental to geological interpretation. 现实主义原则是地质解释的基础。

Scientists use actualism to understand how ancient rock formations were created. 科学家利用现实主义来理解古代岩层是如何形成的。
