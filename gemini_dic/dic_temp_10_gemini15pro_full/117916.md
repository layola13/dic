melter:/ˈmɛltər/| n.|熔化器，熔炉|A device or person that melts something.  A melter is used to melt materials like metal, glass, or plastic.|The glass melter was operating at full capacity. 玻璃熔炉正以满负荷运转。|Synonyms: furnace, smelter, liquefier|Antonyms: freezer, solidifier|noun

melter:/ˈmɛltər/| n.|熔化工人|A person who operates a melting furnace.|The melter carefully monitored the temperature of the molten metal. 熔化工人仔细监测熔融金属的温度。|Synonyms: foundry worker, smelterman|Antonyms: N/A|noun
