galenical:/ɡəˈlɛnɪk(ə)l/
adjective
盖伦式的; 盖伦派的;
Relating to or containing medicinal preparations, especially those made from herbs.  These preparations are typically based on the teachings of Galen, a prominent Greek physician of antiquity.
Galenical remedies were widely used before the advent of modern pharmacology. 盖伦派的药物在现代药理学出现之前被广泛使用。
Many herbalists still use galenical preparations. 许多草药医生仍然使用盖伦制剂。
近义词:herbal, botanical, natural
反义词:synthetic, chemical
词性:adjective

galenical:/ɡəˈlɛnɪk(ə)l/
noun
盖伦制剂
A medicine prepared according to the formulas of Galen.
The apothecary prepared a galenical for the patient's cough. 药剂师为病人的咳嗽准备了一种盖伦制剂。
Traditional galenicals are often made by maceration or infusion. 传统的盖伦制剂通常通过浸渍或浸泡制成。
近义词:herbal remedy, botanical medicine
反义词:synthetic drug, chemical medicine
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *galēnicālis, from Galēnus “Galen” (c. 130 – c. 210 AD), a prominent Greek physician, surgeon and philosopher in the Roman Empire + -ālis -al.
