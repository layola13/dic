palaverists:/pəˈlævərɪsts/
n.
空谈者，夸夸其谈者
People who engage in lengthy or idle talk, especially about unimportant matters.
The meeting was dominated by palaverists who offered no real solutions.  会议被那些没有提供任何真正解决方案的空谈者所主导。
The palaverists spent hours discussing the menu but accomplished nothing else. 夸夸其谈者花了几个小时讨论菜单，但除此之外什么也没做。
近义词: chatterboxes, gossips, windbags
反义词: doers, pragmatists
词性: 名词
