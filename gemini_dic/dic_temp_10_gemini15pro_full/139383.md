observable:/əbˈzɜːrvəbl/ /əbˈsɜːrvəbl/| adj. n.|可观察的；显著的；可观测量|Something that is observable can be noticed or seen.|The changes in his behavior were readily observable. 他的行为变化很容易观察到.|近义词：noticeable, perceptible, visible, apparent|反义词：imperceptible, unnoticeable, invisible|adjective

observable:/əbˈzɜːrvəbl/ /əbˈsɜːrvəbl/| n.|可观测量；可观察的事物|Something that can be observed.|Scientists use instruments to measure observables in the universe. 科学家使用仪器来测量宇宙中的可观测量。|近义词：phenomenon, occurrence|反义词: N/A|noun
