hyperscholastically:/ˌhaɪpərskəˈlæstɪkəli/ | adv. | 过分学究地，过分拘泥于学问地 |  in an excessively scholastic manner; with excessive attention to formal rules of scholarship. |  He approached the problem hyperscholastically, citing obscure texts and ignoring practical considerations. 他以过分学究的方式处理这个问题，引用了一些晦涩难懂的文本，而忽略了实际的考虑。| 近义词: pedantically, academically, bookishly | 反义词: practically, pragmatically | 词性: 副词

词源:
hyper- (prefix): over, excessive  (来自希腊语，意为“超过，过度”)
scholastic (adjective): relating to schools or scholarship (来自拉丁语 scholasticus, 意为“学校的”)
-ally (suffix): forming adverbs from adjectives  (来自拉丁语 -alis)
