cottonweed:/ˈkɑːtnˌwid/ | n. | 棉绒，棉絮；某些菊科植物（尤指香艾纳）| A soft, white, fibrous substance resembling cotton, typically found on plants like cudweeds (Gnaphalium) and other members of the Asteraceae family.  It's often used as stuffing or tinder.| The dried cottonweed was used as tinder to start the fire. 干燥的棉绒被用作引火物来生火。 | 近义词:cudweed,  down, fluff | 反义词: N/A | noun
