nitrobenzene:/ˌnaɪtroʊˈbɛnˌzin/ | n. | 硝基苯 | An organic compound with the formula C6H5NO2. It is a pale yellow oil with an almond-like odor. It is produced by nitrating benzene and is used in the production of aniline, as a solvent, and in the manufacture of dyes, perfumes, and pharmaceuticals. | Nitrobenzene is used in the production of aniline. 硝基苯用于苯胺的生产。

Nitrobenzene has a characteristic almond-like odor. 硝基苯有一种特有的杏仁味。| 近义词: oil of mirbane (obsolete) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun
