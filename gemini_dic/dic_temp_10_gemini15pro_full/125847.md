musafar:/mʊˈsɑːfər/ | n. | 旅行者；旅客 | A traveler, especially one who undertakes a long journey. | He was a seasoned musafar, having journeyed across many lands. 他是一位经验丰富的旅行者，游历过许多地方。|  Example: The musafar finally reached his destination after months of travel. 这位旅行者经过几个月的旅行终于到达了目的地。| Synonym: traveler, voyager, journeyer, pilgrim | Antonym: resident, local, native | Noun

Etymology: From Urdu مسافر (musāfir), from Arabic مُسَافِر (musāfir), active participle of سَافَرَ (sāfara, “to travel”).
