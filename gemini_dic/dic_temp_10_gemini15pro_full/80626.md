goosebumps:/ˈɡuːsbʌmps/ (US), /ˈɡuːsbʌmps/ (UK)| n.|鸡皮疙瘩；起鸡皮疙瘩|A prickly feeling on the skin, often caused by cold, fear, or excitement.  It refers to the small bumps that appear on a person's skin when they are cold or frightened.  The name comes from the resemblance of the skin to the skin of a plucked goose.|The scary movie gave me goosebumps.|近义词：chills, shivers, the creeps|反义词: (No direct antonym, but one might consider "warmth" or "comfort" in certain contexts)|词性:noun
