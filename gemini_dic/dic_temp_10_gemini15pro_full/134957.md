nonnationalism:/nɒnˈnæʃənəlɪzəm/ | n. | 非民族主义; 超国家主义 |  The rejection of nationalism, advocating for global or supranational structures and identities over national ones.  It emphasizes shared human values and cooperation beyond national borders. |  The rise of nonnationalism is evident in the increasing popularity of international organizations. 非民族主义的兴起在国际组织日益普及中显而易见。 |  Globalism, internationalism, cosmopolitanism | Nationalism, patriotism | noun
