actinophone: /ækˈtɪnəˌfoʊn/
noun
光电话，光线电话，光声电话
A device for producing sound by the action of light or radiant energy on a selenium cell.  An instrument for transmitting sounds (especially speech) by means of a beam of light.
The actinophone, invented by Alexander Graham Bell, used sunlight to transmit speech.  贝尔发明的放声器利用阳光来传输语音。
The museum displayed an early actinophone, a testament to Bell's innovative spirit.  博物馆展出了早期的放声器，证明了贝尔的创新精神。
Synonyms: photophone
