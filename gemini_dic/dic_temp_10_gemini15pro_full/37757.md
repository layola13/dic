compassionately:/kəmˈpæʃənɪtli/
adverb
同情地，怜悯地
In a way that shows compassion or sympathy for others.
She spoke compassionately to the grieving family. 她同情地对悲痛的家人讲话。
The nurse compassionately cared for the injured soldier.  护士同情地照顾受伤的士兵。
近义词:sympathetically, kindly, mercifully, tenderly
反义词:cruelly, heartlessly, callously
词性:副词

compassion:/kəmˈpæʃən/
noun
同情，怜悯
A strong feeling of sympathy and sadness for the suffering or bad luck of others and a wish to help them.
She felt deep compassion for the homeless man. 她对这个无家可归的人深感同情。
He was moved by compassion to help the victims of the earthquake.  他出于同情心帮助地震灾民。
近义词:sympathy, empathy, pity, mercy
反义词:cruelty, indifference, callousness
词性:名词

词源:
From Late Latin *compassionem (nominative compassio) "sympathy, fellow-feeling," from compati "to suffer with," from com- "with, together" (see com-) + pati "to suffer" (see passion).
