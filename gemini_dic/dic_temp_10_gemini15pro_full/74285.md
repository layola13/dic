fracid:/ˈfrækɪd/ | adj. | 有雀斑的；布满斑点的 | Covered with freckles; spotted. |  Her face was fracid with tiny brown freckles. 她的脸上布满了细小的棕色雀斑。|spotted, freckled| clear, unspotted | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fracticius, from fractus (“broken”), ultimately from PIE *bʰreg-, *bʰerǵ- (“to break, crack”).  Related to fraction. The sense development is likely from the appearance of freckles as small "broken" spots on the skin.
