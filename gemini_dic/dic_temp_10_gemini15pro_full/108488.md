layaway:/ˈleɪəˌweɪ/ | n. | 分期付款;预留商品服务 | A system of paying a deposit to reserve an item and then making regular payments until the full price is paid. The item is then given to the customer. | I put the TV on layaway and will pick it up when I've finished paying for it. 我把电视机放在分期付款上，付完款后就去取。 | She used layaway to buy her daughter a bicycle for Christmas. 她用分期付款的方式给女儿买了一辆圣诞自行车。 | 近义词: installment plan, payment plan | 反义词: outright purchase | 词性: 名词


layaways:/ˈleɪəˌweɪz/ | n. | 分期付款(复数) | Plural form of layaway, referring to multiple layaway plans or items. |  She had several layaways at different stores for Christmas gifts.  她在不同的商店有几件分期付款的圣诞礼物。 | They offered layaways on all major appliances during the holiday season.  他们在假期期间提供所有主要电器的分期付款服务。| 近义词: installment plans, payment plans | 反义词: outright purchases | 词性: 名词


词源: From "lay away," meaning to put something aside for later use.  源自“lay away”，意思是把东西放在一边供以后使用。
