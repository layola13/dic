disinteresting:/dɪsˈɪntrɪstɪŋ/ | adj. |  不感兴趣的；引不起兴趣的；枯燥乏味的 | Lacking interest or excitement; boring. | The lecture was so disinteresting that many students fell asleep.  讲座太枯燥乏味了，许多学生都睡着了。 | Synonyms: uninteresting, tedious, boring, monotonous, dull | Antonyms: interesting, fascinating, engaging, captivating | adjective


disinterested:/dɪsˈɪntrɪstɪd/ | adj. | 公正的；无私的；不偏不倚的 | Not influenced by considerations of personal advantage; impartial. | A disinterested judge is essential for a fair trial.  公正的法官对公平审判至关重要。 | Synonyms: impartial, unbiased, neutral, objective | Antonyms: biased, prejudiced, partial, interested | adjective
