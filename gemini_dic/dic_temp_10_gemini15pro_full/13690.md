attributer's:/əˈtrɪbjʊtərz/
noun
归因者(的)
belonging to or associated with the attributer;  someone who attributes something to a cause or source.
The attributer's analysis was flawed. 归因者的分析有缺陷。
近义词:assigner, imputer
反义词:None
词性:noun (所有格)

attributer:/əˈtrɪbjʊtər/
