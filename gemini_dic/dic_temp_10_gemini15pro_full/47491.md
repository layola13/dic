deaning:/ˈdiːnɪŋ/ | n. | 去除污垢，清洁；院长职位(罕用) | The act of removing dirt, grime, or other unwanted substances, particularly from a surface or object.  It can also rarely refer to the position or office of a dean. |  The deaning of the church took several days. 教堂的清洁工作花了几天时间。|Cleaning, purging, purification | Soiling, dirtying | noun
