compellatory:/kəmˈpɛləˌtɔːri/ /kəmˈpʌlsəri/
adjective
强制性的；义务的；必修的
obligatory; required; mandatory; compelling
例句：Attendance at these lectures is compellatory.  这些讲座必须参加。

例句：Compellatory education is essential for a well-informed citizenry.  义务教育对于一个消息灵通的公民群体至关重要。

近义词：obligatory, mandatory, compulsory, required
反义词：optional, voluntary, elective
词性：adjective
