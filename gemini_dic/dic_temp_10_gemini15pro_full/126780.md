nanometres:/ˈnænəʊmiːtəz/ /ˈnænəˌmitɚz/| n. |纳米|A unit of length equal to one billionth of a meter.|例句：The wavelength of visible light is measured in nanometres. 可见光的波长是用纳米来测量的。

例句：Nanometres are used to measure extremely small objects, such as viruses. 纳米用于测量极其微小的物体，例如病毒。|近义词：nm (abbreviation) |反义词: kilometres, meters |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From nano- (prefix denoting one billionth) + metre (unit of length).  "Nano-" comes from the Greek word "nanos" meaning "dwarf."
