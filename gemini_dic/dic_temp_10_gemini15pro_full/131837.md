nonconsorting:/ˌnɒnkənˈsɔːtɪŋ/
adjective
不交往的，不厮混的
The act of not associating or keeping company with someone or a particular group, especially those considered undesirable or disreputable.
Police issued a nonconsorting notice, barring him from associating with known criminals. 警方发出了一份禁止交往的通知，禁止他与已知的罪犯交往。
Synonyms: shunning, avoiding, ostracizing, boycotting
Antonyms: associating, fraternizing, socializing
