nonimpacts:/ˌnɒnˈɪmpækts/
n.|无影响；非冲击；无碰撞|The report analyzed the nonimpacts of the policy change. 报告分析了政策变化的无影响之处。|
noun|Absence of impacts or effects.|The study measured the nonimpacts of the new regulation on small businesses.  这项研究衡量了新规对小型企业的无影响。|
synonym:null effects, lack of influence, absence of consequences
antonym:impacts, effects, influences
词性:名词
