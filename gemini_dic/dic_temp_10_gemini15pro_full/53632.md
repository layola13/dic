discernibly:/dɪˈsɜːrnəbli/
adverb
可辨别地，明显地
in a way that is easy to see, hear, or notice
Her hand trembled discernibly. 她的手明显地颤抖。
Synonyms: noticeably, perceptibly, visibly, clearly, distinctly
Antonyms: imperceptibly, subtly, faintly
词性: 副词
