nymphly:/ˈnɪmfli/ | adv. | 像仙女般地；优雅地；轻盈地 | In a manner resembling or characteristic of a nymph; gracefully, delicately. | She danced nymphly across the stage. 她像仙女般轻盈地在舞台上舞动。 | 近义词：gracefully, elegantly, delicately, ethereally | 反义词:clumsily, awkwardly | 词性:副词

词源: From nymph + -ly.  "Nymph" comes from Latin "nympha,"  from Greek "nύmpha," referring to a mythological spirit of nature, often depicted as a beautiful maiden. The suffix "-ly" transforms the noun into an adverb.
