chrysology:/krɪˈsɒlədʒi/
n.
炼金术; 点金术
The study of gold and its properties, especially its production or purification.  Also, alchemy.
Chrysology was a popular pursuit in the Middle Ages.  炼金术在中世纪很流行。
近义词: alchemy
反义词: (无明确反义词)
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek χρυσός (khrusós, “gold”) + -λογία (-logía, “study of”).
