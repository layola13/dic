flashbulb:/ˈflæʃˌbʌlb/ | n. | 闪光灯泡；闪光灯 | A bulb that produces a brief, intense flash of light, typically used in photography. | The photographer used a flashbulb to capture the moment. 摄影师用闪光灯泡捕捉了这一刻。| synonym: flash, flash lamp | antonym: - | noun

flashbulb memory:/ˈflæʃˌbʌlb ˈmɛməri/ | n. | 闪光灯记忆 | A vivid and detailed memory of an emotionally significant event, often of a public nature. |  Many people have flashbulb memories of where they were when they heard about 9/11. 许多人对他们听到9/11事件时所在的地方都有着闪光灯般的记忆。 | synonym: vivid memory, photographic memory | antonym: - | noun
