out-mastered:/ˌaʊtˈmæstərd/
verb
胜过，技高一筹
Surpassed in skill or expertise, especially in a game or competition.
He out-mastered his opponent in the chess tournament. 他在国际象棋比赛中技高一筹，战胜了对手。
The young apprentice quickly out-mastered his teacher in the art of calligraphy.  年轻的学徒很快在书法技艺上超过了他的老师。
近义词: outmaneuvered, outwitted, surpassed, excelled, triumphed over
反义词: defeated, conquered, subdued
