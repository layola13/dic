antiseptic:/ˌæntiˈsɛptɪk/| adj. n.|防腐的，抗菌的；防腐剂，抗菌剂|Preventing the growth of disease-causing microorganisms.|The doctor cleaned the wound with an antiseptic solution. 医生用消毒液清洗了伤口。|近义词：disinfectant, germicidal, antimicrobial|反义词: septic, infected|adjective, noun

antiseptic:/ˌæntiˈsɛptɪk/| n.|防腐剂，抗菌剂|A substance that prevents the growth of disease-causing microorganisms.|The nurse applied an antiseptic to the cut. 护士在伤口上涂了防腐剂。|近义词：disinfectant, germicide|反义词: contaminant|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀντί (anti, “against”) + σηπτικός (sēptikos, “putrefactive”).
