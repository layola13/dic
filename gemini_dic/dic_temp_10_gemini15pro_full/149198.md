oxygenicities:/ˌɒksɪdʒəˈnɪsɪtiz/ | n. |含氧量，产氧量，需氧量| The plural form of oxygenicity, referring to the level of oxygen present or required in different environments or processes. |  The varying oxygenicities of different water bodies influence the type of aquatic life that can thrive there. 不同水体的含氧量差异会影响能够在那里繁衍生息的水生生物的种类。| Near synonyms: oxygen levels, oxygen concentrations | Antonyms: anoxia, hypoxia | noun
