genotypically: /ˌdʒɛnəˈtɪpɪkli/ | adv. | 基因型地，从基因型上看 |  Relating to or determined by an individual's genotype. |  The twins are genotypically identical, but their phenotypes differ due to environmental factors. 这对双胞胎基因型相同，但由于环境因素，它们的表型不同。 | Synonyms: genetically | Antonyms: phenotypically | part of speech: adverb
