ceduous: /ˈsiːdʒuəs/
adj.
勤勉的，坚持不懈的
diligent and persistent in application or effort.
例句：The ceduous student studied diligently for the exam.  这个勤奋的学生为考试刻苦学习。

例句：Her ceduous efforts finally paid off when she received the promotion. 她坚持不懈的努力最终在她获得晋升时得到了回报。

近义词：diligent, assiduous, persevering, industrious
反义词：lazy, indolent, remiss, negligent
词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *cēduus, from cēdō (“I yield, give way, go”).  源自拉丁语 *cēduus，来自 cēdō（“我屈服，让步，走”）。  The original meaning likely refers to the persistent yielding or giving way of obstacles through continuous effort.  最初的意思可能是指通过不断的努力，持续地屈服或让步于障碍。
