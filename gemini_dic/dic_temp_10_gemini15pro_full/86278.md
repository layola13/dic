The heavinesses in the air foretold a storm. 空气中的沉闷预示着一场暴风雨。|近义词：weights, burdens, gravity, gloom, depression|反义词：lightness, levity, cheerfulness|词性:名词

词源: From Middle English *hevynysse, from Old English *hefignes, from hefignes (“heaviness”). Equivalent to heavy + -ness.
