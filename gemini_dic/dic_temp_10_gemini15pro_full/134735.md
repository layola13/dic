nonmechanicalness:/ˌnɒn.məˈkæn.ɪ.kəl.nəs/ | n. | 非机械性；非机械化 |  The quality or state of not being mechanical; absence of mechanical properties or operation.  It refers to something that doesn't involve machines or mechanical processes.  | The nonmechanicalness of the artist's brushstrokes gave the painting a unique texture. 艺术家非机械化的笔触赋予了这幅画独特的质感。 |  The nonmechanicalness of human interaction is what makes it so special. 人类互动中的非机械性使其如此特别。 |  naturalness, organicity, simplicity | artificiality, mechanization | noun
