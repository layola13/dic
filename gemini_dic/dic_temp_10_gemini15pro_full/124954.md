muffler:/ˈmʌflər/ (US) /ˈmʌflə(r)/ (UK)| n.|消音器；围巾；头巾|A device used to reduce noise, especially on a car exhaust or gun.  Also, a scarf worn around the neck for warmth.|例句：The car's muffler needs to be replaced. 汽车的消音器需要更换。

例句：She wrapped a thick woolen muffler around her neck. 她用一条厚厚的羊毛围巾围住脖子。|近义词：silencer (for exhaust), scarf, neck warmer|反义词: amplifier (opposite of silencing function) |词性:noun

muffle:/ˈmʌfl/ (US) /ˈmʌfəl/ (UK)| vt.|使消音；使低沉；抑制；裹住|To make a sound quieter or less distinct; to wrap or cover for warmth or protection.|例句：The heavy curtains muffled the street noise. 厚重的窗帘降低了街道的噪音。

例句：She muffled her cough with her hand. 她用手捂住嘴咳嗽。|近义词：mute, deaden, soften, stifle, suppress|反义词: amplify, enhance |词性:verb
