counterstreams:/ˈkaʊntərˌstriːmz/ | n. | 逆流，反向潮流 | Currents flowing in the opposite direction to the main stream. | Salmon swim against the counterstreams to reach their spawning grounds. 鲑鱼逆流而上到达它们的产卵地。 | synonyms: countercurrents, reverse flows, upstreams | antonyms: downstreams, main streams | noun

counterstreams:/ˈkaʊntərˌstriːmz/ | n. | 对抗性趋势，相反的观点 | Trends or opinions that oppose the prevailing ones. | There are counterstreams of thought challenging the established norms. 有一些对抗性思潮挑战既定规范。| synonyms: opposing views, dissenting opinions, counter-movements | antonyms: mainstream views, prevailing opinions, dominant trends | noun
