deserted:/dɪˈzɜːrtɪd/ /dɪˈzɝːtɪd/
adjective
荒芜的，废弃的，无人居住的
Empty of people, abandoned
例句：The deserted town was eerie and silent.  这个荒芜的小镇阴森而寂静。
例句：They found a deserted cabin in the woods. 他们在树林里发现了一间废弃的小屋。
近义词：abandoned, desolate, empty, uninhabited
反义词：populated, inhabited, crowded, bustling
词性: adjective

deserted:/dɪˈzɜːrtɪd/ /dɪˈzɝːtɪd/
verb (past participle of desert)
遗弃，抛弃
Left or abandoned
例句：The soldier deserted his post.  这名士兵擅离职守。
例句：She deserted her family for a new life in the city. 她为了在城市开始新生活而抛弃了家人。
近义词：abandoned, forsaken, left
反义词：supported, stayed with, protected
词性: verb (past participle)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *desert, from Old French deserter, from Latin dēserere (“to abandon, forsake”), from dē- (“away from”) + serere (“to join, link, bind”).
