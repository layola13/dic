circummure:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈmjʊər/ /ˌsɜːrkəmˈmjʊr/| vt. | 用墙围住，禁闭 | To enclose within walls, especially for protection or confinement. | The king circummured the castle to protect it from invaders. 国王用墙围住城堡以保护它免受侵略者的侵袭。

The prisoners were circummured in their cells. 囚犯被关在牢房里。| 近义词：enclose, wall in, imprison, confine | 反义词：liberate, free, release | 词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin circummūrāre (to wall around), from circum- (around) + mūrāre (to wall).
