panic:/ˈpænɪk/ (US) /ˈpænɪk/ (UK)| n. v. adj.|恐慌，惊慌；使恐慌|A sudden overwhelming fear, with or without cause, that produces hysterical or irrational behavior; to affect with panic.|The crowd panicked when the fire broke out. 人群在火灾爆发时陷入恐慌。|近义词：fear, alarm, terror, fright|反义词：calm, composure, tranquility|noun
