deciduously: /dɪˈsɪdʒuəsli/ | adv. | 落叶地，定期脱落地 | Shedding leaves annually, typically in autumn. |  Many trees in temperate climates grow deciduously, losing their leaves in the fall. 许多温带气候的树木都会落叶，在秋天落叶。| synonym: seasonally, annually | antonym: evergeenly | word type: adverb

deciduous: /dɪˈsɪdʒuəs/ | adj. | 落叶的，脱落的 | (of a tree or shrub) shedding its leaves annually, typically in autumn.  |  Oak trees are deciduous. 橡树是落叶树。| synonym: shedding, falling | antonym: evergreen, persistent | word type: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *deciduus*  "falling off," from *decidere* "fall down, fall off," from *de-* "down" + *cadere* "to fall."
