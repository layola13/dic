deathlike:/ˈdeθlaɪk/| adj.|死一般的，像死了一样的|Resembling death; as pale and still as a corpse.|His face was deathlike after the accident. 他的脸在事故后像死了一样苍白。|
Her hand was deathlike in its coldness. 她的手冰冷得像死人一样。|
Synonyms: deathly, lifeless, pallid, ghostly|
Antonyms: lively, vibrant, animated|
