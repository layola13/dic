nontransmittible:/ˌnɑːntrænzˈmɪtəbl/
adjective
不可传播的
Not capable of being transmitted or passed on, especially of a disease.
Measles is a highly transmittible disease, while osteoarthritis is nontransmittible.
麻疹是一种高度传染性疾病，而骨关节炎则不传染。
This form of arthritis is nontransmittible.
这种关节炎不会传染。
近义词:noncommunicable, noncontagious
反义词:transmittible, communicable, contagious
词性:adjective

词源:
non- (prefix): not
transmit (verb): to send or pass something from one person, place, or thing to another
-ible (suffix): capable of; worthy of
