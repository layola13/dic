leptostraca:/ˌlɛptɒˈstreɪkə/ | n. | 薄甲纲 | An order of small, marine crustaceans characterized by a bivalved carapace enclosing the head and thorax, and a segmented abdomen ending in a caudal furca. They are considered the most primitive extant malacostracans. | Leptostraca are found in marine environments worldwide. 薄甲纲动物在世界各地的海洋环境中都有发现。|  The Nebalia bipes is a common example of Leptostraca.  纳波虫是薄甲纲的一个常见例子。 | synonyms: Nebaliacea | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a taxonomic group) | noun
