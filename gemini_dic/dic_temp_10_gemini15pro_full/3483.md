aftergame:/ˈæftərɡeɪm/| n.|赛后活动；(尤指)赛后评论|An informal social gathering after a game or sporting event, often involving analysis and discussion of the game.|例句：The team gathered for the aftergame at a local pub. 球队在当地一家酒吧举行赛后聚会。

例句：The aftergame analysis revealed several key mistakes that cost them the victory. 赛后分析揭示了几个导致他们输掉比赛的关键错误。|近义词：post-game, postmortem|反义词:pregame|词性:名词
