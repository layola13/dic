deadlily:/ˈdɛdlɪli/| adv.|致命地，极度地|In a manner likely to cause death; extremely effectively.|The silence hung deadlily in the air. 沉默在空气中致命地蔓延。|近义词：fatally, lethally, mortally, perniciously|反义词：harmlessly, safely|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *dedliche, from Old English dēadlic (“subject to death, mortal”), equivalent to dead +‎ -ly. Cognate with Scots deidly (“deadly”), West Frisian deadlik (“deadly”), Dutch dodelijk (“deadly, fatal”), German tödlich (“deadly”), Swedish dödlig (“deadly, mortal”), Icelandic dauðlegur (“mortal”). More at dead.
