nonvascular:/ˌnɒnˈvæskjʊlə(r)/ | adj. | 非维管的；无维管的 | Lacking vascular tissue; not containing vessels for transporting water and nutrients, as in some plants. | Mosses are nonvascular plants. 苔藓是非维管植物。| Examples of nonvascular plants include mosses, liverworts, and hornworts. 非维管植物的例子包括苔藓、地钱和角苔。| synonyms: avascular | antonyms: vascular | adjective
