inseam:/ˈɪnsiːm/| n.|内缝；内侧骨缝|The inseam of a pair of trousers is the seam on the inside of the leg, from the crotch to the bottom of the leg opening. 裤子的内缝是从胯部到裤腿底部的内侧缝。|My inseam is 32 inches. 我的内缝是32英寸。|近义词:inside leg|反义词:outseam|词性:noun
