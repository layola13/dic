armature:/ˈɑːrməˌtʃʊr/ /ˈɑːrməˌtʃər/ | n. | 电枢; 衔铁; 支架; (雕塑的)骨架; |The rotating part of an electric motor or generator.  A piece of iron or steel across which magnetism is created by an electric current. A framework or skeleton used to support something. | The armature of the generator was damaged. 发电机的电枢损坏了。|Synonyms: rotor, stator, framework, skeleton, support|Antonyms: core, center|noun
