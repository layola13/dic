compendious:/kəmˈpɛndiəs/| adj.|简明的，概要的，全面的|Containing or presenting much information in a concise or brief form; succinct. |This compendious guide covers all the essential information you need for the exam. 这本简明指南涵盖了你需要的所有考试要点。| The author managed to produce a compendious history of the world in a single volume. 作者设法将世界历史浓缩在一本书里。|近义词：concise, succinct, brief, summary, abridged|反义词：prolix, verbose, lengthy, diffuse, rambling|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin *compendiosus, from Latin compendium ‘saving, abridgement’ (see compendium).
