line-caught:/ˈlaɪnˌkɔːt/
adj.
用鱼线钓的；以延绳钓捕获的
Caught by a fishing line, typically a single line with a hook, as opposed to nets or traps.
This restaurant serves only line-caught tuna. 这家餐厅只供应用鱼线钓的吞拿鱼。
近义词:hook-caught, pole-caught
反义词:netted, trawled
词性:形容词


line-caught:/ˈlaɪnˌkɔːt/
n.
用鱼线钓的鱼
Fish caught by a fishing line.
Line-caught fish is often considered higher quality. 用鱼线钓的鱼通常被认为质量更高。
近义词:hook-caught fish, pole-caught fish
反义词:netted fish, trawled fish
词性:名词
