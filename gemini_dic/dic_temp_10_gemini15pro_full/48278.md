decoupling:/diːˈkʌplɪŋ/
n.|脱钩，分离，去耦|The decoupling of economic growth from environmental damage is a key challenge for the 21st century.  21 世纪的关键挑战是将经济增长与环境破坏脱钩。|
decoupling: The act of reducing or eliminating the connections between two things. For example, decoupling economic growth from resource consumption means finding ways to grow the economy without using more resources.  将经济增长与资源消耗脱钩意味着找到在不使用更多资源的情况下发展经济的方法。|
The government is encouraging the decoupling of executive pay from company performance. 政府正在鼓励将高管薪酬与公司业绩脱钩。|
Near synonyms: separation, disconnection, uncoupling, divorce
Antonyms: coupling, linking, connection
