dispatch:/dɪˈspætʃ/ (US), /dɪˈspætʃ/ (UK)| n. vt.|派遣，发送；急件；迅速处理；（杀死）|The general dispatched a messenger to headquarters. 将军派了一名信使去总部。|近义词：send, transmit, kill, execute|反义词：delay, withhold, receive|词性:名词,动词

Etymology: From Old French despescher "to expedite, hasten," from des- "dis-" + pescher "to fish out, pull out."  The original sense was "to get rid of (quickly)," which developed into the senses of "send off quickly" and "kill quickly."
