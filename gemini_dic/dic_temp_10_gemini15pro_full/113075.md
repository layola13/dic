luna:/ˈluːnə/| n.|月亮，月神|The moon, often personified as a goddess. From the Latin word for "moon".|例句：The luna shone brightly in the night sky. 月亮在夜空中闪耀。

|例句：In Roman mythology, Luna is the divine embodiment of the Moon. 在罗马神话中，露娜是月亮的神圣化身。|近义词：moon, selene|反义词:sol (sun)|noun
