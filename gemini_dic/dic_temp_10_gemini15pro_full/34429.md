claustral:/ˈklɔːstrəl/| adj.|隐遁的，修道院的，幽闭的|Relating to a cloister, or enclosed and secluded, like a monastery.  Suggesting confinement or seclusion.|The monks lived a claustral life, rarely leaving the monastery. 僧侣们过着隐居的生活，很少离开修道院。|近义词：secluded, cloistered, monastic, confined|反义词：open, public, worldly|词性:形容词
词源: From Late Latin *claustrālis*, from Latin *claustrum* "lock, bar, bolt; enclosed place, cloister," from *claudere* "to close."
