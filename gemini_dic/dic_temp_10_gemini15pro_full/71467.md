flamer:/ˈfleɪmər/| n.|喷火器；煽动者；发出火焰的人或物；(非正式)发表激烈言论的人|A person who expresses inflammatory opinions or behaves in an inflammatory manner, especially online.  Someone who makes offensive and provocative comments online. A device that produces a stream of burning fuel.|That guy is such a flamer, always starting arguments in the comments section. 那家伙真是个喷子，总是在评论区挑起争端。|The firefighters used a flamer to burn off the excess vegetation. 消防队员用喷火器烧掉多余的植被。|近义词：incendiary, agitator, provocateur, troll (online)|反义词:peacemaker, mediator|词性:noun

flamer:/ˈfleɪmər/| vi.|燃烧；爆发；激动|To burn brightly or intensely. To suddenly become angry or excited.|The campfire flamed merrily in the darkness. 篝火在黑暗中欢快地燃烧着。|He flamed with anger when he heard the news.  听到这个消息，他勃然大怒。|近义词：blaze, ignite, erupt|反义词:extinguish, die down|词性:verb
