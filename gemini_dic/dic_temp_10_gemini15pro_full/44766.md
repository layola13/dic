Etymology:
From crumminess + -es (plural suffix).  Crumminess is derived from crummy (meaning "lousy, shoddy, inferior") + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality).  The origin of "crummy" is uncertain, possibly related to "crumb," suggesting something broken down or worthless.
