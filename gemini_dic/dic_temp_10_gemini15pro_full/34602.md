cleave-to (1):/ˈkliːv tuː/| v. |坚持；忠于；紧贴；粘附|To adhere firmly and closely to something, either physically or figuratively. |The wet shirt cleaved to his back. 湿衬衫紧紧地贴在他的背上。|近义词：adhere to, stick to, cling to, hold fast to|反义词：detach from, separate from|词性: 动词

cleave-to (2):/ˈkliːv tuː/| v. |依附；依靠|To remain faithful to a person, belief, or cause. |She cleaved to her principles despite the pressure to conform. 尽管面临从众的压力，她仍然坚持自己的原则。|近义词：be loyal to, be faithful to, be devoted to|反义词：abandon, forsake|词性: 动词
