blizzard: /ˈblɪzərd/ | n. |暴风雪；大量繁乱的事物|A severe snowstorm with high winds and low visibility. | The blizzard made travel impossible. 暴风雪使得出行变得不可能。|  The office was bombarded with a blizzard of paperwork. 办公室被大量 paperwork 轰炸了。 | 近义词: snowstorm, whiteout | 反义词: sunshine, calm | 词性: 名词
Etymology: From the earlier meaning of "a violent blow," perhaps from German Blitz "lightning," though this is disputed. The current sense of a snowstorm emerged in the American Midwest in the 1870s.
