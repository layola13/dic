nimbyism:/ˈnɪmbiɪzəm/
n.
邻避症候群；邻避效应
The opposition of residents to development projects in their neighborhood.
The council faced strong nimbyism from residents when they proposed building a new waste facility. 当地方议会提议建造新的垃圾处理设施时，遭到了居民强烈的邻避抵制。
近义词: local opposition, community resistance
反义词:  welcoming attitude, community support
词性: noun

nimbyisms:/ˈnɪmbiɪzəmz/
n. (plural)
邻避症候群；邻避效应（复数形式）
Multiple instances of opposition by residents to development projects in their neighborhoods.
The mayor cited growing nimbyisms across the city as a major obstacle to affordable housing initiatives. 市长指出，全市日益增长的邻避主义是经济适用房计划的主要障碍。
近义词: local oppositions, community resistances
反义词: welcoming attitudes, community supports
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Acronym from "Not In My Back Yard".  It originated in the late 1970s or early 1980s, reflecting community opposition to locally undesirable projects.
