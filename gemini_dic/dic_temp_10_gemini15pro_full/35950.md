/wɒt/ (UK) /hwɒt/ (US) /wʌt/ (US) | pronoun, determiner, adverb | 什么，哪一个，多么 | used to ask for information about something; used to show surprise or disbelief |

例句：
What is your name?
你叫什么名字？

What did you say?
你说了什么？

近义词：which, how, why
反义词: (What doesn't have a direct antonym as it's a question word)
词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb
