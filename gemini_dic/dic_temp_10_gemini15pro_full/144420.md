outsuffered:/ˌaʊtˈsʌfərd/
verb
比...更长久地受苦，比...经受更多的苦难
Having endured or experienced suffering for a longer period or to a greater degree than another.
He outsuffered his rivals and eventually triumphed. 他比他的对手们承受了更多的苦难，最终取得了胜利。
outsuffer, outlast, endure
(No clear antonyms, potentially thrive, flourish)
词性: 动词
