chymiferous:/ˌkaɪmɪˈfɪrəs/| adj.| 产生乳糜的，含乳糜的 | Containing chyle; conveying chyle. | The lacteals are chymiferous vessels. 乳糜管是输送乳糜的血管。| The chymiferous ducts carried the digested fats. 这些输送乳糜的导管输送消化的脂肪。 | 近义词: chyle-bearing, chyliferous | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective

词源:
From chyme + -ferous.  "Chyme" comes from Greek *khymos* meaning "juice".  "-ferous" comes from Latin *ferre* meaning "to bear" or "to carry".
