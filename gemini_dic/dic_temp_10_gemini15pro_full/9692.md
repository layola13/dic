antithesis:/ænˈtɪθəsɪs/
n.
对照, 对立, 对偶
a person or thing that is the direct opposite of someone or something else.  A contrast or opposition between two things.
Example: Love is the antithesis of selfishness. 爱是自私的对立面。

Example: His carefree nature is the antithesis of his brother's seriousness. 他无忧无虑的性格与他兄弟的严肃形成了鲜明的对比。

近义词:opposite, contrast, converse
反义词:similarity, resemblance, likeness
词性:noun
