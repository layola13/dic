autoclave: /ˈɔːtəˌkleɪv/ | n. |高压灭菌器，高压釜|A strong, heated container used for chemical reactions and other processes using high pressures and temperatures, especially for sterilizing medical equipment.|The dentist's office uses an autoclave to sterilize their instruments. 牙医诊所使用高压灭菌器对器械进行消毒。|Synonyms: sterilizer, pressure cooker |Antonyms: None | Noun


autoclave: /ˈɔːtəˌkleɪv/ | v. |用高压灭菌器消毒|To sterilize something using an autoclave.|All surgical instruments must be autoclaved before use. 所有手术器械使用前必须进行高压灭菌。|Synonyms: sterilize, sanitize |Antonyms: contaminate | Verb


Etymology:  From French *autoclave*, from auto- + Latin clavis “key” (because it closes itself).
