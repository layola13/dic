advancing:/ədˈvænsɪŋ/
verb
前进，推进；促进；提出；预付
Making progress or moving forward. Also, promoting or furthering the progress of something.  Can also mean lending money.
The army is advancing on the enemy.  军队正在向敌人推进。

Our understanding of the universe is constantly advancing. 我们对宇宙的理解在不断进步。

近义词:progressing, developing, proceeding, promoting, furthering
反义词:retreating, withdrawing, hindering, receding
词性:Verb


advancing:/ədˈvænsɪŋ/
adjective
前进的；进展的；年迈的
Moving forward, progressing, or of increasing age.
The advancing army captured the city.  前进的军队占领了这座城市。

His advancing years made him more cautious.  他年事渐高，变得更加谨慎。

近义词:progressive, developing, increasing
反义词:retreating, declining, decreasing
词性:Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *avancer* (to put forward, promote), from *avant* (before, forward), from Latin *abante* (from before), from *ab* (from) + *ante* (before).

USAGE->
