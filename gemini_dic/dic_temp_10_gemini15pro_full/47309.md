de-intensifying:/diːɪnˈtɛnsɪfaɪɪŋ/
adjective
降低强度的，缓和的
reducing the intensity of something; making something less intense or severe.
The therapist suggested de-intensifying the exercise routine to avoid further injury.  治疗师建议降低锻炼强度，以避免进一步受伤。
The diplomat worked to de-intensify the conflict between the two nations.  外交官努力缓和两国之间的冲突。
synonyms: moderating, mitigating, lessening, weakening, softening, tempering, toning down
antonyms: intensifying, escalating, aggravating, exacerbating
词性: adjective
