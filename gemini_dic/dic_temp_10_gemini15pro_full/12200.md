PHRASE->
(as to is generally not used in set phrases. It functions more like a preposition connecting clauses and phrases.)



词源:  "as to" originated from Old English "æt" (at) and "tō" (to).  Over time, "æt" evolved into "as," and the combination "as to" became a prepositional phrase.
