anti-institutional:/ˌæntiˌɪnstɪˈtjuːʃənl/
|adj.|反体制的，反机构的|Opposed to or distrustful of established institutions, especially those of government, education, or religion.|Example:  Her art reflects an anti-institutional stance. 她的艺术反映了一种反体制的立场。

Example: The punk rock movement was known for its anti-institutional lyrics. 朋克摇滚运动以其反体制的歌词而闻名。
|近义词:nonconformist, rebellious, iconoclastic, dissident, radical|反义词:establishment, conventional, orthodox, conformist|词性:形容词
