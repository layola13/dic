outsiderly:/ˌaʊtˈsaɪdərli/
adjective
局外人的；旁观者的；与世隔绝的
From the perspective of someone outside a particular group, activity, or situation; in a detached or uninvolved way.  Characteristic of an outsider.
例句：He looked at the bustling city with an outsiderly gaze. 他以一个局外人的眼光看着繁忙的城市。

例句：She maintained an outsiderly perspective throughout the debate. 在整个辩论过程中，她保持了局外人的视角。
近义词：detached, aloof, external, objective
反义词：involved, engaged, insider, internal
词性: adjective
