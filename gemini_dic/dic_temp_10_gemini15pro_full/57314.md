downticks:/ˈdaʊntɪks/| n.|下跌，下降趋势|A series of decreases in the price of a security or index, often represented as a series of downward movements on a graph.  A period of general decline, especially in economic activity.|The stock market experienced several downticks today. 股市今天经历了几次下跌。|Synonyms: declines, drops, falls, dips, slumps|Antonyms: upticks, rises, increases|noun

downtick:/ˈdaʊntɪk/| n.|下跌，小幅下降|A small decrease in the price of a security or index.|The stock had a slight downtick after the news. 消息公布后，该股票略有下跌。|Synonyms: decline, drop, fall, dip|Antonyms: uptick, rise, increase|noun
