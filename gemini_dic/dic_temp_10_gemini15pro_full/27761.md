cannel:/ˈkænəl/| n.|烛煤; 沟渠; 水道|A hard, dull black coal that burns with a bright flame, formerly used for making candles.  一种质地坚硬、暗黑色的煤，燃烧时火焰明亮，以前用于制作蜡烛。|The cannel coal burned brightly in the fireplace. 烛煤在壁炉里燃烧得很旺。|近义词：coal, shale|反义词: -(none applicable)|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *canel, from Anglo-Norman canel, chanel (“canal, channel, pipe”), from Late Latin canalis, canāle (“pipe, groove, canal”), from Latin canna (“reed, cane”).  Doublet of canal and channel. 源自中古英语 *canel，来自盎格鲁-诺曼语 canel, chanel（“运河、渠道、管道”），来自晚期拉丁语 canalis, canāle（“管道、沟槽、运河”），来自拉丁语 canna（“芦苇、藤条”）。canal 和 channel 的同源异形词。
