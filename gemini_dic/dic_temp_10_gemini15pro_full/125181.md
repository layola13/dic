multicarbon:/ˌmʌltiˈkɑːrbən/
adjective
多碳的
Containing multiple carbon atoms, often referring to organic molecules or compounds with a carbon backbone or chain.
例句：Multicarbon compounds are the building blocks of life. 多碳化合物是生命的基石。

例句：Scientists are studying the formation of multicarbon molecules in interstellar space. 科学家正在研究星际空间中多碳分子的形成。
