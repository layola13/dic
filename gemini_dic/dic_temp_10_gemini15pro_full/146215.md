overexpenditure:/ˌoʊvərɪkˈspɛndɪtʃər/| n. |超支，过度支出|The act of spending more than one has budgeted or more than one can afford.  The government's overexpenditure led to a budget deficit.|例句：The overexpenditure on the project caused a financial crisis for the company.  这个项目的超支导致了公司的财务危机。

例句：The family's overexpenditure during the holidays put them in debt.  这个家庭假期期间的过度支出让他们负债累累。|近义词：overspending, extravagance|反义词:underspending, saving|词性:名词

overspend: /ˌoʊvərˈspɛnd/ | v. |超支|To spend more than one has budgeted or more than one can afford.|例句：They overspent on their vacation and had to borrow money. 他们在度假时超支了，不得不借钱。

例句：He tends to overspend when he goes shopping. 他购物时容易超支。|近义词：splurge|反义词:save, economize|词性:动词
