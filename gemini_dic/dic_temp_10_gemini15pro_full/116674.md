Etymology:
From Old French *materas (Modern French matelas), from Old Italian materasso, from Arabic مطرح (maṭraḥ, “something thrown down, mat, mattress”), from طرح (ṭaraḥa, “to throw down”).  The original meaning was a quilt or other thick covering placed on the floor to lie on.
