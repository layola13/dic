hotpressed:/ˈhɑːtˌprɛst/| adj.|热压的，热压成型的|Made or finished using heat and pressure, typically referring to paper, board, or other materials.|例句：The hotpressed paper has a smooth surface. 热压纸表面光滑。|例句：Hotpressed watercolor paper is ideal for detailed work.  热压水彩纸是精细作品的理想选择。|近义词：compressed, calendered|反义词：coldpressed, rough|词性:形容词
词源: "hot" + "pressed" -  From the process of applying heat and pressure.  "Hot" meaning high temperature, and "pressed" being the past participle of "press," meaning to apply force.
