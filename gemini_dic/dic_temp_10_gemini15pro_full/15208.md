backdate:/ˈbækdeɪt/ (US), /ˈbækdeɪt/ (UK)| v. n.|回溯日期，追溯既往|To put an earlier date on (a document, cheque, etc.) than the actual date of writing; antedate.  To cause something to have an earlier effective date.|The company backdated the contract to January 1st.  公司将合同的日期回溯到1月1日。|Synonyms: antedate, predate|Antonyms: postdate|verb/noun
