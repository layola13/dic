beak-like:/ˈbiːkˌlaɪk/
adjective
像喙的; 喙状的
Resembling a beak in shape or function.
The bird has a beak-like mouth.  这只鸟的嘴像喙。
The platypus has a beak-like snout.  鸭嘴兽有一个像喙一样的鼻子。
pointed, sharp, hooked
round, blunt
adjective
