dispatchers:/dɪˈspætʃərz/
n.
调度员，派遣员
People who send out vehicles or other resources to deal with situations.
The dispatchers sent an ambulance to the scene of the accident.  调度员派了一辆救护车到事故现场。

The 911 dispatchers are trained to remain calm in emergencies. 911调度员都接受过在紧急情况下保持冷静的训练。

近义词：controllers, coordinators, operators
反义词：receivers
