entireties:/ɪnˈtaɪrətiz/
noun
完整；全部；全体
The estate was sold in its entirety.  整个庄园被出售了。
The book is fascinating in its entirety. 这本书从整体来看很吸引人。
近义词: whole, totality, completeness
反义词: part, portion, fragment
词性: noun (plural)

Etymology: From Middle English *entirte, from Old French entirté, from entier (“whole”) + -té (“-ty”), from Latin integritās (“wholeness, soundness”). Equivalent to entire +‎ -ty.
