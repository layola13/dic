manbird:/ˈmænˌbɜːrd/| n. |人鸟，鸟人 (通常指虚构的生物，半人半鸟)|A mythical creature that is part human and part bird.  It is often depicted in fantasy literature and art.|例句：The manbird swooped down from the sky and snatched the fish from the water. 人鸟从天而降，从水中叼走了一条鱼。

例句：In her dream, she saw a manbird singing a beautiful song. 在她的梦里，她看到一只人鸟在唱一首优美的歌。|近义词：harpy, griffin|反义词: (No direct antonym, could be considered the opposite of a purely human or purely bird creature)|词性:noun
