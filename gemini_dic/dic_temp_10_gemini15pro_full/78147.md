geophysically:/ˌdʒiːoʊˈfɪzɪkli/| adv.|地球物理地|In a way relating to the physics of the earth.  It describes actions or processes that are related to the earth's physical properties, such as its magnetic field, gravity, or seismic activity.|例句：The area was geophysically surveyed before drilling commenced.  在开始钻探之前，对该地区进行了地球物理勘测。

例句：The data were geophysically interpreted to identify potential oil reservoirs.  对数据进行地球物理解释以识别潜在的石油储层。|近义词：geologically|反义词: N/A|词性: adverb
