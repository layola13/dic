mistlike:/ˈmɪstlaɪk/
adjective
像雾的; 模糊的
resembling or suggestive of mist; hazy.
The morning was mistlike, with a soft gray light diffusing everything. 早晨雾蒙蒙的，柔和的灰色光线弥漫着一切。
The memory was mistlike and indistinct. 这段记忆模糊不清。
近义词: misty, hazy, foggy, nebulous
反义词: clear, distinct, sharp
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From mist +‎ -like.
