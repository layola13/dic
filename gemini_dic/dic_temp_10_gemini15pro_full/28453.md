carbureted:/ˈkɑːrbəreɪtɪd/
adjective
汽化的；装有化油器的
equipped with a carburetor; (of an engine) using a carburetor to mix fuel and air.
His carbureted engine sputtered and died. 他的化油器引擎发出噼啪声然后熄火了。
近义词:carburettor,  carburetor
反义词:fuel-injected
词性:adjective
