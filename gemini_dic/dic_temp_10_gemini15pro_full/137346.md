nontaxably:/nɒnˈtæksəbli/
adverb
免税地
in a way that is not subject to taxation
例句：The prize money was paid nontaxably.  奖金是免税支付的。

例句：The inheritance was passed on nontaxably. 这笔遗产是免税继承的。
近义词：tax-free, duty-free
反义词：taxably
词性：adverb
