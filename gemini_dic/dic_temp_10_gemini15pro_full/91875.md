hyperintellectually:/ˌhaɪpərˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəli/
adverb
过度理智地，过度运用智力地
in a manner characterized by excessive intellectual activity or analysis.
She analyzed the situation hyperintellectually, missing the emotional nuances. 她过度理性地分析了情况，忽略了其中的情感细微之处。
He approached the problem hyperintellectually, getting lost in abstract theories. 他过度理性地处理这个问题，迷失在抽象的理论中。
近义词: excessively intellectually, overly cerebrally
反义词: intuitively, emotionally, instinctively
词性: adverb

Etymology:
hyper- (prefix): from Greek, meaning "over, beyond, excessive"
intellectually (adverb): from intellect (noun) + -ual (suffix) + -ly (suffix), ultimately from Latin intellectus "understanding, perception"
