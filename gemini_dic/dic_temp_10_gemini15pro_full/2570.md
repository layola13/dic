admitters:/ədˈmɪtərz/
noun
承认者; 准许进入者
People who admit something or someone. Those who grant entrance or access.
The admitters of their guilt received a reduced sentence. 承认自己罪行的人得到了减刑。

The university's admitters for the fall semester were notified by mail.  秋季学期的大学录取者已通过邮件收到通知。

近义词:confessors, acknowledgers, entrants
反义词:deniers, rejecters
词性:n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "admit," which comes from the Latin "admittere," meaning "to send to" or "to let in."  The suffix "-er" denotes a person who performs the action of the verb.
