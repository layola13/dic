auln:/ɔːln/ | n. | awl; stitching | An awl, a pointed tool for piercing small holes. | He used an auln to make holes in the leather for stitching. | 近义词: awl, bodkin, pricker | 反义词: None | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From Middle English *alene, *awelene, from Old English *ælene (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *alīną (“awl”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂el- (“awl, needle”). Cognate with Old High German āla, ālina (“awl”), Old Norse alr (“awl”). More at awl.
