mite:/maɪt/ | n. | 螨虫；极少量 | A tiny arachnid, often parasitic.  A very small amount of something. |  The dust mites in the carpet made him sneeze. 地毯里的尘螨让他打喷嚏。 |  She didn't contribute a mite to the project. 她对这个项目一点贡献都没有。 | synonyms: speck, iota, bit, crumb | antonyms: lot, abundance | noun

mite:/maɪt/ | v. (archaic) |  可能 | Might - past tense of may. (Expressing possibility).  | He mite have gone to the store, but I'm not sure. 他可能去了商店，但我不确定。(古英语用法，现代英语已不用) | synonyms: may, could, perhaps | antonyms: cannot, will not | verb (archaic)
