burgoo:/bərˈɡuː/
noun
浓稠的炖菜；（尤指）户外大锅炖菜
A thick stew, especially one cooked outdoors in a large pot.
The Kentucky Derby is famous for its mint juleps and burgoo.  肯塔基赛马会以薄荷朱利酒和浓稠炖菜而闻名。
Synonyms: stew, chowder, soup
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but perhaps) light meal, snack
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain. Possibly from a dialectal word meaning "oatmeal gruel," or from the French "bouillon" (broth).  词源不确定。可能来自意为“燕麦粥”的方言词，或来自法语“bouillon”（肉汤）。
