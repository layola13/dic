frondous:/ˈfrɑːndəs/ /ˈfrɒndəs/| adj.|多叶的；枝叶繁茂的|Characterized by abundance of leaves; leafy.  Having or resembling fronds.|The jungle was filled with frondous ferns and palms.  丛林里长满了枝繁叶茂的蕨类植物和棕榈树。|近义词：leafy, luxuriant, lush|反义词：bare, leafless|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *frondōsus*, from frōns (“leaf, foliage”) + -ōsus (“-ous”).
