delayable:/dɪˈleɪəb(ə)l/
adj.
可延期的，可推迟的
able to be delayed or postponed without causing problems
The meeting is delayable until next week. 会议可以推迟到下周。
The launch of the new product is delayable by a few weeks.  新产品的发布可以推迟几周。
近义词:postponable, deferrable
反义词:urgent, pressing
词性:形容词


词源:
From "delay" + "-able"
