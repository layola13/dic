deathlessnesses:/ˈdɛθləsnɪsɪz/
n.
不朽, 永生, 永恒
The state or quality of being deathless; immortality.
His poems speak of the deathlessnesses of love and beauty. 他的诗歌歌颂了爱情与美丽的不朽。
近义词: immortality, eternity, perpetuity
反义词: mortality, finiteness, transience
词性: 名词

词源:  From deathless + -ness.  "Deathless" comes from Old English *dēaðlēas, from dēað (death) + -lēas (without).  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns denoting a state, quality, or condition.
