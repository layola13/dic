leang: /liːŋ/ | adj. | 瘦的，消瘦的; 吝啬的，小气的 | Lean can describe a person or animal that is thin and healthy, or it can describe someone who is unwilling to spend money.  It can also refer to meat that has little fat. |  He has a lean and muscular physique. 他身材精瘦且肌肉发达。| synonyms: thin, slender, slim, spare, meager, stingy, mean | antonyms: fat, obese, plump, generous, lavish | adjective
