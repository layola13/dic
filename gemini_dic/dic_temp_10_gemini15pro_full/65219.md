etiologies: /ˌiːtiˈɑːlədʒiz/ | n. | 病因，病因学 |  The study of the etiologies of diseases is crucial for developing effective treatments. 对疾病病因的研究对于开发有效治疗至关重要。 |  Example:  Researchers are studying the etiologies of Alzheimer's disease. 研究人员正在研究阿尔茨海默病的病因。

etiology: /ˌiːtiˈɑːlədʒi/ | n. | 病因，病因学 | The etiology of the disease is still unknown. 这种疾病的病因仍然未知。 | Example: The doctor explained the etiology of my condition. 医生解释了我的病情的病因。|Synonyms: cause, origin, reason, root, source| Antonyms: effect, result, outcome | noun
