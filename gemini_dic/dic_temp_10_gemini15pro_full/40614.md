convenership:/kənˈviːnəʃɪp/
n.
召集人职位；召集人的身份
The position or role of a convener.
She took on the convenership of the committee. 她担任了委员会的召集人。
近义词：chairmanship, leadership, directorship
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps subordination or membership)
词性: 名词
