cheerio:/ˈtʃɪəriəʊ/ (UK), /ˈtʃɪrioʊ/ (US)| int. n.|再见；干杯；（英式）脆玉米圈|An informal British word used to say goodbye. Also a brand of breakfast cereal.|例句：Cheerio, then!  那么，再见！

例句：He had a bowl of Cheerios for breakfast. 他早餐吃了一碗脆玉米圈。|近义词：goodbye, bye, farewell (for goodbye),  Corn Flakes, Rice Krispies (for cereal)|反义词:hello (for goodbye) |词性:interjection, noun
