legislates:/ˈlɛdʒɪsleɪts/
verb
立法；制定法律
makes laws; creates legislation
The Congress legislates on behalf of the people. 国会代表人民立法。
The city council legislates on local matters. 市议会就地方事务立法。
Synonyms: enacts, decrees, ordains, establishes
Antonyms: repeals, abolishes, nullifies
词性: 动词(verb)
