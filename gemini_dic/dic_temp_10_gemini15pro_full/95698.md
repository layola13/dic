inconform:/ˌɪnkənˈfɔːrm/
adjective
不整合的；不一致的
Not conforming to established standards, customs, or ideas.
The unconformity between the two rock layers suggests a period of erosion. 这两个岩层之间的不整合表明存在一个侵蚀期。
Near synonyms: nonconforming, unorthodox, deviant, divergent, dissident, heretical, irregular, anomalous
Antonyms: conforming, orthodox, conventional, regular, standard
词性: 形容词
