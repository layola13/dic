gluts:/ɡlʌts/| n. |供过于求，过剩|A glut is a situation where there is more of something than is needed or wanted.  It often leads to lower prices.|The market was glutted with cheap imports. 市场充斥着廉价的进口商品。|synonyms: surplus, excess, oversupply, overabundance|antonyms: shortage, scarcity, dearth, deficiency|noun
gluts:/ɡlʌts/| v. (gluts, glutting, glutted) |充斥，使…过量|To glut a market means to supply it with far more of a product than is needed or wanted.|The oil glut caused prices to plummet. 石油过剩导致价格暴跌。|synonyms: flood, inundate, saturate, overload|antonyms: deprive, starve, deplete|verb
