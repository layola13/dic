nonsubsidiary:/ˌnɒnsʌbˈsɪdiəri/ /ˌnɑːnsʌbˈsɪdiəri/
adjective|非附属的，非子公司的|Not subsidiary; not controlled by a parent company.  Relating to or denoting something that is not a subsidiary or subordinate part of something else.| The company's nonsubsidiary operations showed strong growth this quarter.  该公司非附属业务在本季度表现出强劲增长。|independent, autonomous, separate|subsidiary, dependent, subordinate|adjective

nonsubsidiary:/ˌnɒnsʌbˈsɪdiəri/ /ˌnɑːnsʌbˈsɪdiəri/
noun|非附属机构/公司|An entity or company that is not a subsidiary of another.|The report focused on the performance of the nonsubsidiaries.  该报告重点关注非附属公司的业绩。|independent entity, separate company, autonomous organization|subsidiary, branch, division|noun
