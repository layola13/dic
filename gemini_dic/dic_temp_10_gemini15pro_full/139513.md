obstupefaction:/ˌɒbstjuːpɪˈfækʃən/ /ˌɑːbstuːpəˈfækʃən/| n.|茫然，昏迷，麻木|A state of mental numbness or bewilderment; a daze.|The news of her sudden death left everyone in a state of obstupefaction.|近义词：stupor, daze, bewilderment, numbness, shock|反义词: alertness, awareness, consciousness|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *obstupefactiōn- (stem of obstupēfactiō), from Latin obstupēfactus (past participle of obstupēfacere ‘stupefy’), from ob- ‘against, towards’ + stupēfacere (from stupēre ‘be amazed’ + facere ‘to make’).
