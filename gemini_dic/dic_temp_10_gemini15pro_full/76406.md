gage:/ɡeɪdʒ/| n. vt.| 测量仪器，规格，标准，抵押品，挑战，保证|An instrument for measuring dimensions, pressure, etc., or for testing accuracy. Also, a pledge or security.|例句：The mechanic used a feeler gage to check the gap between the spark plug and the cylinder head. 技工用塞尺检查火花塞和气缸盖之间的间隙。

例句：He offered his house as gage for the loan. 他把房子作为贷款的抵押。|近义词：gauge, measure, instrument, pledge, security, guarantee|反义词：uncertainty|词性：名词, 动词

gage:/ɡeɪdʒ/| vt.| 测量，估计，判断|To measure or estimate.|例句：He gaged the depth of the hole with a stick. 他用一根棍子测量了洞的深度。

例句：She gaged the mood of the room before speaking. 她发言前判断了一下房间里的气氛。|近义词：estimate, assess, judge, measure|反义词：misjudge|词性：动词
