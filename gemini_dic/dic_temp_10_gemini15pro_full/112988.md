lulz:/lʌlz/ | n. |  网络俚语，嘲笑，幸灾乐祸 |  An expression of laughter, often used online or in text messages, sometimes indicating schadenfreude or amusement at another's misfortune.  Derived from "LOL" (laughing out loud), with the "z" added for emphasis or to convey a more sarcastic or ironic tone. | Example:  The video of the cat failing to jump was full of lulz.  那只猫跳跃失败的视频充满了笑料。| Synonym:  LOL, ROFL, lmao | Antonym:  sympathy, empathy | noun
