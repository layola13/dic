fever:/ˈfiːvər/ (US) /ˈfiːvə(r)/ (UK)| n.|发烧，发热；狂热，热情|A body temperature that is higher than normal.  A state of excitement or intense emotion.|例句：He has a high fever. 他发高烧。

例句：She was in a fever of anticipation. 她处于极度期待的狂热之中。


|近义词：temperature, heat, excitement, passion|反义词:calmness, apathy|词性:noun
