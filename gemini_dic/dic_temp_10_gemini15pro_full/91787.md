hyperexcited:/ˌhaɪpərɪkˈsaɪtɪd/| adj.|过度兴奋的，极度兴奋的|Characterized by excessive excitement; extremely agitated and enthusiastic.|The children were hyperexcited about going to the amusement park. 孩子们对去游乐园感到非常兴奋。|The fans were hyperexcited after their team won the championship. 球迷们在他们的球队赢得冠军后欣喜若狂。|近义词：overexcited, thrilled, elated, ecstatic|反义词：calm, tranquil, serene, peaceful|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From hyper- + excited, where hyper- is a prefix meaning "over, beyond, excessively," from Greek hyper "over, above, beyond."
