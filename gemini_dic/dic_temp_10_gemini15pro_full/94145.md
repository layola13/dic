impeditive:/ɪmˈpɛdɪtɪv/
adjective
妨碍的，阻碍的
Having the effect of impeding; hindering.
The thick mud was impeditive to their progress.  厚厚的泥浆阻碍了他们的前进。
impeding, hindering, obstructive
facilitative, helpful
