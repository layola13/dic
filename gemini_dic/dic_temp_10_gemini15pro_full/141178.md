ooh-and-aah:/ˌuː ən ˈɑː/ | interjection | 赞叹声，惊叹声 | Expressing admiration or appreciation, typically for something visually impressive.  Derived from the sounds people make when impressed. | The fireworks display elicited oohs and aahs from the crowd. 烟火表演引来了人群的阵阵赞叹。 | synonyms: exclaim, gasp, marvel | antonyms: boo, criticize | interjection
