non-cooperation:/ˌnɒnˌkəʊˌɒpəˈreɪʃən/ | n. | 不合作 | The refusal to cooperate; the practice of not cooperating. |  India achieved independence through non-cooperation with the British government. 印度通过不与英国政府合作而获得了独立。

Gandhi's non-cooperation movement was a powerful tool against British rule. 甘地的不合作运动是对抗英国统治的有力工具。

| 近义词: resistance, defiance, obstruction | 反义词: cooperation, collaboration, assistance | 词性: 名词
