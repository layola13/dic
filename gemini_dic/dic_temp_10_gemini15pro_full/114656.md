Malian: /ˈmɑːliən/ | adj. n. | 马里的；马里人 |  Many Malians speak French. 很多马里人说法语。 | 近义词: (for person) West African | 反义词: None (nationality has no direct opposite) | 词性: adjective, noun


Etymology: From Mali + -an.  "Mali" itself derives from the name of the Mali Empire.
