magnetitic:/ˌmæɡnɪˈtɪtɪk/
adjective
磁铁的；含磁铁的
Relating to or containing magnetite.
The ore is highly magnetitic. 这种矿石富含磁铁矿。
Synonyms: magnetic, ferrous
Antonyms: non-magnetic, non-ferrous
词性: 形容词


magnetite:/ˈmæɡnɪtaɪt/
noun
磁铁矿
A black iron oxide mineral, Fe3O4, that is strongly magnetic.
Magnetite is a common iron ore. 磁铁矿是一种常见的铁矿石。
Synonyms: lodestone, iron oxide
Antonyms: None (as it's a specific mineral)
词性: 名词
