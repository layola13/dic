decemfid:/ˈdɛsəmfɪd/| adj.|十裂的，分成十份的|Divided into ten parts or segments.  It is primarily used in botanical contexts to describe leaves, petals, or other plant structures.|The decemfid leaves of the cannabis plant are easily recognizable.  大麻植物的十裂叶很容易辨认。|近义词：ten-cleft, ten-parted|反义词: entire, undivided|词性: adjective
词源: From Latin *decemfidus*, from *decem* ("ten") + *-fidus* (a suffix meaning "divided," related to *findere* "to split, cleave").
