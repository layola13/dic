parabolically:/ˌpærəˈbɒlɪkli/ | adv. |抛物线地，像抛物线一样|in the shape of a parabola; in a way that resembles the curve of a parabola|The ball flew parabolically through the air. 球在空中呈抛物线状飞行。|The rocket descended parabolically. 火箭呈抛物线状下降。|近义词: curvilinearly, arcwise|反义词: linearly, straightly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From parabola + -ical + -ly. Parabola comes from Greek parabolē, meaning "application," from para- "beside" + ballein "to throw."
