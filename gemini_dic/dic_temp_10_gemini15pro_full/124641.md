mouthguard:/ˈmaʊθɡɑːrd/| n.|护齿，牙套|A protective device worn over the teeth, typically by athletes, to prevent injury to the mouth.|Boxers wear mouthguards to protect their teeth. 拳击手戴护齿来保护他们的牙齿。|The dentist fitted him with a custom mouthguard. 牙医为他安装了一个定制的护齿。|Synonyms: gumshield, mouth protector|Antonyms: N/A|noun
