overderisivenesses: /ˌoʊvərˌdɛrɪˈsaɪvnəsɪz/ | n. | 过分嘲笑，过度嘲讽 | The state of being excessively derisive; repeated or extreme instances of derision. |  His overderisivenesses made him unpopular. 他的过度嘲讽让他不受欢迎。|  Her constant overderisivenesses alienated her friends. 她不断的过度嘲讽疏远了她的朋友。 | 近义词：mockery, scorn, contempt, ridicule | 反义词: respect, admiration, praise | 词性: 名词

词源:

From over- + derisiveness, from derisive + -ness.  "Derisive" ultimately comes from the Latin "dērīdēre," meaning "to laugh at" or "to mock."  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning, indicating an excessive or extreme degree of derisiveness.
