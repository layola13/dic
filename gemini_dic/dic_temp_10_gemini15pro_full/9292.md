antiphrasis:/ænˈtɪfrəsɪs/
n.
反语，用反话
The use of words in a sense opposite to their literal meaning, often ironically or humorously.
Calling a tall person "Shorty" is an example of antiphrasis.
把一个高个子叫“矮子”是反语的一个例子。
近义词:irony, sarcasm
反义词:literalism
