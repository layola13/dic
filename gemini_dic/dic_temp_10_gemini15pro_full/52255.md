dibstone:/ˈdɪbstəʊn/ (UK) /ˈdɪbstoʊn/ (US)| n.|羊拐骨；羊拐骨游戏；用羊拐骨铺成的路面|A small, naturally rounded stone, typically a sheep's knucklebone, used in a children's game. Also, a pavement made of such stones.|Children used to play games with dibstones. 孩子们过去常常玩羊拐骨游戏。|The path was paved with dibstones. 这条小路是用羊拐骨铺成的。|Synonyms: knucklebone, jackstone, chuckie-stone (Scotland)|Antonyms: None|noun
