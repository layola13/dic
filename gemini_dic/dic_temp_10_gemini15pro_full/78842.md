ginan:/ˈɡiːnæn/ | n. |银杏, 白果|A Japanese word referring to the ginkgo nut, often used in culinary contexts.  The ginkgo tree itself is referred to as "ichō" (イチョウ) in Japanese. |例句：Ginan are often served roasted as a snack in Japan.  白果在日本经常被烤着当零食吃。
