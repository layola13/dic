blottingly:/ˈblɒtɪŋli/
adverb
污秽地，邋遢地
in a blotting manner; so as to blot or stain.  Making something dirty or messy.
The child ate blottingly, smearing food all over his face.  这孩子吃得邋遢，把食物弄得满脸都是。
The ink spread blottingly across the page. 墨水污秽地蔓延到整个页面。
messily, sloppily, untidily, dirtily
neatly, cleanly, tidily


ETYMOLOGY->
From blot (verb) + -ing (present participle suffix) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  The word "blot" ultimately comes from the Old Norse "blottr," meaning "bare patch."
