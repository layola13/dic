What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What is his job? = 他的工作是什么？


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What is he doing now? = 他现在在做什么？
What is she cooking in the kitchen? = 她在厨房里做什么饭？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What has she eaten today? = 她今天吃了什么？
What has he done with the money? = 他用这笔钱做了什么？


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What has she been studying lately? = 她最近一直在学习什么？
What have they been working on for the past month? = 过去一个月他们一直在做什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What was her name? = 她叫什么名字？
What did you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃了什么？


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were they doing when the phone rang? = 电话铃响时他们在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had they discussed before the meeting? = 会议前他们讨论了什么？
What had she done before she left? = 她离开之前做了什么？


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had they been arguing about before I arrived? = 我到达之前他们一直在争论什么？
What had she been working on before she got sick? = 她生病前一直在做什么？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天要做什么？
What will the weather be like next week? = 下周天气会怎样？


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? = 明天这个时候你会在做什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):

What will they have finished by the end of the day? = 到今天结束时他们会完成什么？


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
