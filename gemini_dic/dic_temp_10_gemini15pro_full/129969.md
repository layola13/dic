non-militant:/ˌnɒnˈmɪlɪtənt/ | adj. n.| 非好战的；非战斗人员 | Not militant; opposed to war or to the use of force.  |  Example: The group adopted a non-militant approach to achieving its goals.  该团体采取非军事手段来实现其目标。| Synonyms: peaceful, pacifistic, dovish, non-violent, non-aggressive | Antonyms: militant, aggressive, belligerent, hawkish | Adjective

non-militant:/ˌnɒnˈmɪlɪtənt/ | n.| 非战斗人员 | A person who is not militant; a civilian.  |  Example: The non-militants were evacuated from the war zone. 非战斗人员从战区撤离。| Synonyms: civilian, pacifist, noncombatant | Antonyms: soldier, combatant, warrior | Noun
