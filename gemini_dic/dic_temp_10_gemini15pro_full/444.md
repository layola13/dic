ablegation: /ˌæblɪˈɡeɪʃən/
n.
派遣；委托；特使(团)
The act of sending someone with a specific mission or message.  The group of people sent on such a mission.
The president sent an ablegation to the peace talks. 总统派遣了一个代表团参加和平谈判。

The ablegation from the UN arrived yesterday.  联合国代表团昨天抵达。

近义词: delegation, mission, embassy, legation
反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider "recall" in a specific context)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *ablegatio, from Latin ablegare (“to send away, banish”), from ab- (“away from”) + legare (“to send as a legate, depute, bequeath”).
