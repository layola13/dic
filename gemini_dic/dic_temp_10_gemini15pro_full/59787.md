ecotour:/ˈiːkoʊtʊr/ | n. | 生态旅游 | A tour designed to have minimal impact on the environment and often to educate the traveler about the local ecology. | Example 1: We took an ecotour of the rainforest and learned about the diverse plant and animal life. 我们参加了一个热带雨林的生态旅游，了解了那里多样化的动植物。

Example 2:  The ecotour included hiking, kayaking, and birdwatching.  这个生态旅游包括徒步旅行、皮划艇和观鸟。 | 近义词: nature tour, green tour, sustainable tourism | 反义词: mass tourism | 词性: noun


ecotourism:/ˈiːkoʊˌtʊrɪzəm/ | n. | 生态旅游 | Tourism directed toward exotic, often threatened, natural environments, especially to support conservation efforts and observe wildlife. | Example 1: Ecotourism is becoming increasingly popular as people become more aware of the importance of environmental protection. 随着人们越来越意识到环境保护的重要性，生态旅游变得越来越受欢迎。


Example 2:  The country relies heavily on ecotourism for its economic development. 这个国家严重依赖生态旅游来发展经济。 | 近义词: sustainable tourism, green tourism, responsible travel | 反义词: mass tourism | 词性: noun
