cultirostres:/kʌltɪˈrɒstriːz/ | n. | 涉禽类 |  A taxonomic group of birds characterized by their long legs and adapted for wading in shallow water, typically with long bills for probing for food.  This group is now considered obsolete and its members are classified within different orders. |  Cultirostres, an outdated classification, once included herons and storks. 涉禽类，一个过时的分类，曾经包括鹭和鹳。| 近义词: wading birds | 反义词:  (No direct antonym as it's a taxonomic group, could consider terrestrial birds or birds of prey for contrast) | 词性: noun
