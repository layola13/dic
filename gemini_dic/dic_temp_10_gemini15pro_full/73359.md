foregathering:/ˈfɔːrɡæðərɪŋ/ /ˈfɔːrɡæðərɪŋ/
noun
集会，聚会
a gathering or meeting of people
例句：The annual foregathering of the clan was a grand affair.  家族一年一度的聚会是一件盛事。

例句：There was a foregathering of local artists at the gallery opening.  当地艺术家在画廊开幕式上举行了一次聚会。

近义词：gathering, meeting, assembly, convention
反义词：dispersal, separation, scattering
词性：n.
