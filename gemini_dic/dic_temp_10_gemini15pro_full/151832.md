PHRASE->
pass by:  经过，流逝  Time passes by so quickly. 时间过得真快。
pass on:  传递，去世  Please pass this message on to your manager. 请把这条信息传达给你的经理。
pass out:  昏倒，分发  He passed out from the heat. 他因高温而昏倒了。
pass through:  穿过，经历  The river passes through the mountains. 这条河流穿过群山。
pass up:  放弃，拒绝  I couldn't pass up the opportunity to travel to Europe. 我无法放弃去欧洲旅行的机会。
