noncoordination:/ˌnɒnkəʊˌɔːrdɪˈneɪʃn/ | n. |不协调，缺乏协调|Lack of coordination or harmony between different elements or actions. |The noncoordination between the departments led to a disastrous marketing campaign. 各部门之间缺乏协调导致了灾难性的营销活动。

The project failed due to noncoordination between the teams.  这个项目由于团队之间缺乏协调而失败了。|近义词：disharmony, discord, conflict, incoordination |反义词: coordination, harmony, cooperation, synchronization |词性:名词
