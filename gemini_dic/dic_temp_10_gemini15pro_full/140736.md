omnipresence:/ˌɒm.nɪˈprez.əns/ | n. |无所不在；遍在|The concept of God's omnipresence is central to many religions.  上帝无所不在的概念是许多宗教的核心。|例句：The omnipresence of surveillance cameras in the city makes some people feel uneasy. 城市里监控摄像头无处不在，这让一些人感到不安。|例句：Her omnipresence in the media made her a household name.  她在媒体上的无所不在使她家喻户晓。|近义词：ubiquity, universality|反义词：absence, localization|词性: 名词

omnipresent:/ˌɒm.nɪˈprez.ənt/ | adj. |无所不在的；遍在的|God is considered omnipresent by many believers. 许多信徒认为上帝无所不在。|例句：The omnipresent threat of war loomed over the country. 战争的威胁无处不在，笼罩着整个国家。|例句：The omnipresent smell of coffee filled the cafe. 咖啡馆里弥漫着咖啡的香味。|近义词：ubiquitous, pervasive|反义词：absent, localized|词性: 形容词
