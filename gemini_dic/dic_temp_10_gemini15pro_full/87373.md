heronsew:/ˈherənˌsuː/
n.
苍鹭的缝合线, 鹭鸶缝合法
A type of surgical stitch used to close wounds.  It's a continuous stitch that creates a tight, leak-proof closure.
The surgeon used a heronsew to close the incision.  外科医生用鹭鸶缝合法缝合了切口。
近义词: continuous stitch, running stitch
反义词: interrupted stitch
词性: 名词
