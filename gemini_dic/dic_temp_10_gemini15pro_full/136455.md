nonreversibly:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈvɜː.sə.bli/ /ˌnɑːn.rɪˈvɝː.sə.bli/
adverb|不可逆地|in a way that cannot be reversed or undone|The damage to the environment is nonreversibly changed. 对环境的损害是不可逆转地改变了。|irreversibly, permanently|reversibly|adverb
nonreversibly:/ˌnɒn.rɪˈvɜː.sə.bli/ /ˌnɑːn.rɪˈvɝː.sə.bli/
adjective|不可逆的|not able to be reversed or undone|The chemical reaction is nonreversible.  这个化学反应是不可逆的。|irreversible, permanent|reversible|adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin "non" meaning "not"
reversible (adjective/verb): from Late Middle English; from Old French "reversible" or Latin "reversabilis," from "revertere" meaning "to turn back"
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives
