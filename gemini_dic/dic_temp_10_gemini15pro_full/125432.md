multiplan:/ˈmʌltɪplæn/ | n. | 多方案；多平面；（尤指）电子表格程序 | A spreadsheet program that allows users to create and manipulate numerical data.  It was a popular software application in the early days of personal computing. |  In the 1980s, Multiplan was a popular alternative to Lotus 1-2-3.  在20世纪80年代，Multiplan是Lotus 1-2-3的流行替代品。 | synonyms: spreadsheet, worksheet | antonyms: N/A | noun

multiplan:/ˈmʌltɪplæn/ | v. | 制定多项计划；多方面计划 | To develop multiple plans or strategies.  This usage is less common than the noun form. | We need to multiplan for different scenarios. 我们需要为不同的情况制定多项计划。 | synonyms: strategize, plan, prepare | antonyms: improvise | verb
