luxuriates:/ˈlʌɡʒərieɪts/ | v. | 享受；沉溺于；繁茂生长 | to enjoy oneself in a luxurious way; to grow or thrive in abundance | She luxuriates in long, hot baths. 她喜欢享受长时间的热水澡。 The vegetation luxuriates in the humid climate.  植被在潮湿的气候中繁茂生长。 | 近义词: indulge, revel, bask, flourish | 反义词: abstain, deprive, wither | 词性: 动词

词源:  From Latin *luxuriatus, past participle of luxuriare "to be luxuriant, abound, riot," from luxuria "luxury, abundance," from luxus "dislocation, excess," related to lucrum "gain, profit."
