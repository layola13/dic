diagnosis:/ˌdaɪəɡˈnoʊsɪs/ (US) /ˌdaɪəɡˈnəʊsɪs/ (UK)| n.|诊断; 诊断结果|The doctor made a diagnosis of pneumonia. 医生诊断为肺炎。|The diagnosis was a relief; it wasn't cancer.  诊断结果令人宽慰；不是癌症。|synonyms: identification, determination, analysis|antonyms: misdiagnosis, uncertainty|noun

etymology->
From Late Latin *diagnōsis, from Ancient Greek διάγνωσις (diágnōsis, “distinguishing, discerning”), from διαγιγνώσκειν (diagignṓskein, “to distinguish, discern”), from διά (diá, “through”) + γιγνώσκειν (gignṓskein, “to learn”).
