might:/maɪt/
aux. v.
可能，也许；可以，或许
Used to express possibility, permission, or a polite request.
She might be at home. 她可能在家。
Might I borrow your pen? 我可以借你的笔吗？
近义词：may, could, perhaps, possibly
反义词：cannot, must not
词性：助动词

词源：From Middle English *mihte, *mizte, from Old English meahte, mihte (first and third person singular preterite of magan (“may, to be able”)), from Proto-Germanic *mahtiz (“might”), from Proto-Indo-European *mégh₂ti (“powerful”). Cognate with Scots micht (“might”), West Frisian mooglik (“possible”), Dutch macht (“power, might”), German Macht (“power, might”), Danish magt (“power, might”), Swedish makt (“power, might”), Icelandic máttur (“might, power”). Related to may.
