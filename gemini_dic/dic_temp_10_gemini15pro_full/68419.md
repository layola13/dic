falsify:/ˈfɔːlsɪfaɪ/ | v. | 伪造；篡改；歪曲 | He falsified the figures in the accounts. 他篡改了账目中的数字。 | The evidence was falsified to make him look guilty. 证据被伪造，使他看起来有罪。 | synonyms: forge, fake, counterfeit, doctor | antonyms: verify, validate, authenticate | verb
