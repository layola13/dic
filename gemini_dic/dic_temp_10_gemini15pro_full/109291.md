lemuridous:/ˌliːmjʊˈraɪdəs/ | adj. |狐猴似的，像狐猴的 | Relating to or resembling a lemur.  | The small, nocturnal primate had large eyes and a lemuridous appearance.  这只小型夜行灵长类动物长着大眼睛，外形像狐猴。| Near Synonyms: lemurlike, lemurine | Antonyms: (No common direct antonym; consider using antonyms for specific lemur traits, e.g., diurnal for nocturnal) | adjective
