noncryptic: /ˌnɒnˈkrɪptɪk/ /ˌnɑːnˈkrɪptɪk/ | adj. | 非隐秘的，清晰易懂的 | Not cryptic; clear and easy to understand.  | The instructions were noncryptic and easy to follow. 说明书写得清楚易懂，很容易理解。 | The message was thankfully noncryptic, so everyone understood immediately.  幸好这条信息清晰易懂，所以大家都立刻明白了。 | 近义词: clear, straightforward, explicit, unambiguous, plain, perspicuous | 反义词: cryptic, obscure, ambiguous, enigmatic, puzzling | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  "non-" (not) + "cryptic" (hidden, secret). "Cryptic" comes from the Greek word "kryptikos" meaning "fit for concealing."
