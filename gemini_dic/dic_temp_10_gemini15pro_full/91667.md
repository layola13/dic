hyperchlorhydric:/ˌhaɪpərˌklɔːrˈhaɪdrɪk/
adjective
胃酸过多的
Characterized by excessive secretion of hydrochloric acid in the stomach.
The patient's hyperchlorhydric condition caused him constant heartburn.  病人胃酸过多的情况导致他持续的烧心。
近义词: hyperacid
反义词: hypochlorhydric
