boraginaceous:/ˌbɔːrədʒɪˈneɪʃəs/
adjective
紫草科的
Relating to or characteristic of the borage family (Boraginaceae), typically having hairy leaves and small blue, purple, or white flowers.
Forget-me-nots are boraginaceous plants.  勿忘我是紫草科植物。

The borage plant has boraginaceous flowers.  琉璃苣有紫草科的花朵。
