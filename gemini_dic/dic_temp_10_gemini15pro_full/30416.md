centrale:/sɑ̃ˈtʁal/ (French), /senˈtrɑːleɪ/ (anglicized)| adj. n.|中心的，中央的；总站；中心局|Located in the center; the main office or station; a power plant.  Originates from the French word "central," ultimately from Latin "centralis," meaning "relating to the center."|The centrale téléphonique is busy. (The telephone exchange is busy.)|Synonyms: central, main, principal, chief, head|Antonyms: peripheral, outer, outlying|adjective, noun

centrale:/sɑ̃ˈtʁal/ (French), /senˈtrɑːleɪ/ (anglicized)| n.|发电厂|A power station, especially one generating electricity.  Originates from the French word "centrale," ultimately from Latin "centralis," meaning "relating to the center."|France relies heavily on nuclear centrales for its electricity. (France relies heavily on nuclear power plants for its electricity.)|Synonyms: power plant, power station, generating station|Antonyms: substation|noun
