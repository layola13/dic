incendiary:/ɪnˈsɛndiəri/ /ɪnˈsɛndiˌɛri/| adj. n.|煽动性的，纵火的；燃烧弹；煽动者| (adj.) designed to cause fires; tending to stir up strife or rebellion; (n.) a person who stirs up strife or rebellion; a substance or weapon used to start fires.|The protesters threw incendiary devices at the police. 抗议者向警察投掷燃烧装置。|近义词：inflammatory, provocative, combustible, arsonist|反义词：conciliatory, peaceful, calming|词性:adjective, noun

incendiary:/ɪnˈsɛndiəri/ /ɪnˈsɛndiˌɛri/| n.|燃烧弹；煽动者|A substance or weapon used to start fires; a person who stirs up strife or rebellion.|The police found several incendiaries in the suspect's car. 警方在嫌疑人的车里发现了几个燃烧弹。|近义词：firebomb, arsonist, agitator|反义词：peacemaker, mediator|词性:noun
