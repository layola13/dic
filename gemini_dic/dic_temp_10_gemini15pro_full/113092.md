lunch: /lʌntʃ/ | n. | 午餐 | A meal eaten in the middle of the day. |  I had a sandwich for lunch. 我午饭吃了个三明治。| luncheon, midday meal | breakfast, dinner | noun

lunch: /lʌntʃ/ | v. | 吃午餐 | To eat lunch. | I usually lunch with my colleagues. 我通常和我的同事一起吃午饭。| dine, eat |  | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From *nuncheon*, meaning "noon drink," from *none* (noon) and *schench* (drink). Over time, the meaning shifted from a drink to a light meal eaten at noon.
