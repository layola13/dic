abbrev:/əˈbriːv/| n. & vt.|缩写, 缩写词|Abbreviation; shortened form of a word or phrase.  |Example: "Prof." is the common abbrev. for "Professor". "教授"是"Professor"的常见缩写。

The company name is often abbreviated to three letters. 公司名称通常缩写为三个字母。|近义词：abbreviation, shortening, contraction|反义词:expansion, elaboration|词性:名词, 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *abbreviatus*, past participle of *abbreviare* "to shorten," from *ab-* "from, away" + *brevis* "short."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The article abbrev. the names of the authors. = 这篇文章缩写了作者的名字。
She always abbrev. "street" to "st." = 她总是把“street”缩写成“st.”。

一般过去时:
They abbreviated the name of the organization to make it easier to remember. = 他们缩写了组织的名称，以便于记忆。
He abbreviated the word in his notes. = 他在笔记中缩写了这个词。

现在完成时:
We have abbreviated the title to save space. = 我们为了节省空间缩写了标题。
She has abbreviated her name to its initials. = 她把她的名字缩写成了首字母。

过去完成时:
He had abbreviated the term before using it in the report. = 他在报告中使用该术语之前先将其缩写。
They had abbreviated the company name for years. = 他们多年来一直使用公司名称的缩写。

将来时:
I will abbrev. the word to make it fit on the form. = 我会缩写这个词，使其适合表格。
We will abbrev. the lengthy title for clarity. = 为了清晰起见，我们将缩写冗长的标题。

虚拟语气:
If I were to abbrev. the word, I would use the standard form. = 如果我要缩写这个词，我会使用标准形式。
I wish I had abbreviated the phrase, it would have saved space. = 我希望我缩写了这个短语，这样可以节省空间。


被动语态:
The word was abbreviated in the text. = 这个词在文本中被缩写了。
The names of the countries are being abbreviated for the table. = 表格中各国的名称正在被缩写。


疑问句:
How do you abbrev. "Mister"? = 你如何缩写“先生”？
Is this the correct way to abbrev. this word? = 这是缩写这个词的正确方法吗？


否定句:
I didn't abbrev. the word, I wrote it out in full. = 我没有缩写这个词，我完整地写出来了。
He doesn't abbrev. words unless it's absolutely necessary. = 除非绝对必要，否则他不缩写单词。
