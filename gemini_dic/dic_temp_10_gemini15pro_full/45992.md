cyanide:/ˈsaɪənaɪd/ | n. | 氰化物 | A highly poisonous compound of carbon and nitrogen, especially the anion CN− or any of its salts, such as potassium cyanide KCN.  | Potassium cyanide is a potent poison. 氰化钾是一种剧毒的毒物。| synonyms: prussic acid, cyanide of potassium | antonyms: antidote | noun
