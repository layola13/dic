biacuminate:/ˌbaɪəˈkjuːmɪneɪt/
adjective
双尖的，渐尖的
Having two tapering points.
The biacuminate leaf of the willow tree is a distinguishing feature. 柳树的双尖叶子是一个显著特征。
The rare orchid boasted biacuminate petals, a unique characteristic that set it apart from other species.  这种稀有兰花以其双尖的花瓣而自豪，这是它区别于其他物种的独特特征。
近义词:acuminate, pointed, sharp
反义词:obtuse, rounded
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *bi- "two" + acuminatus "sharpened," past participle of acuminare "to sharpen," from acus "needle."
