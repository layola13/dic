gabs:/ɡæbz/ | n. |闲聊，饶舌，空谈|Informal conversations; idle talk. | She spends hours on the phone, just gabs with her friends. 她成小时地在电话里和朋友闲聊。 | 近义词: chatter, prattle, gossip | 反义词: silence, reticence | 词性: noun

gabs:/ɡæbz/ | v. | 闲聊，饶舌 |Third-person singular simple present indicative form of gab. | He gabs incessantly about nothing important.  他不停地闲聊些无关紧要的事情。| 近义词: chatters, prattles, gossips | 反义词: is silent, refrains from speaking | 词性: verb
