adventurous:/ədˈventʃərəs/
adjective
爱冒险的；大胆的；充满冒险精神的
Willing to take risks or try new methods, ideas, or experiences.
She's an adventurous traveler who loves exploring new places. 她是一位喜欢探索新地方的冒险旅行家。

He's always been adventurous and willing to try new things. 他一直都很有冒险精神，愿意尝试新事物。

synonym: daring, bold, audacious, intrepid, venturesome
antonym: cautious, timid, fearful, apprehensive
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *aventurous*, from Old French *aventureus*, from *aventure* (“adventure”) + *-ous* (suffix forming adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin *adventura* (“that which happens”).
