aneroid:/ˈænərɔɪd/| adj. n.|无液的，非液体的；无液气压计|Containing no liquid; specifically designating a barometer that measures air pressure without using liquid. |An aneroid barometer is more portable than a mercury barometer. 无液气压计比水银气压计更便携。|Synonyms: dry, anhydrous |Antonyms: liquid, fluid|adjective, noun

aneroid:/ˈænərɔɪd/| n.|无液气压计|A barometer that measures air pressure without using liquid. |The pilot checked the aneroid barometer before takeoff. 飞行员在起飞前检查了无液气压计。|Synonyms: barometer |Antonyms: mercury barometer|noun
