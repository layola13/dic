massively:/ˈmæsɪvli/| adv.|大量地，大规模地，非常地|In a massive or extensive way; to a great extent or degree.|The game has massively increased in popularity. 这款游戏的人气大增。|近义词：hugely, enormously, extensively, greatly|反义词: slightly, minimally, insignificantly|词性:副词
Etymology: From "massive" + "-ly."  "Massive" comes from French "massif" (solid, bulky), ultimately from Late Latin "massa" (lump, kneaded dough).
