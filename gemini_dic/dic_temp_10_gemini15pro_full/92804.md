iconicism:/aɪˈkɒnɪsɪzəm/ | n. |图像性；肖像性；图符象征性|The resemblance between a sign and its referent, as in the case of a photograph or a diagram.| The iconicism of the painting, with its realistic depiction of the scene, was striking. 这幅画的图像性引人注目，它对场景的描绘非常逼真。| resemblance, similarity, likeness | abstraction, symbolism | noun
