crescent:/ˈkresənt/| n. adj.|新月，月牙形，新月形的|A curved shape that is wider in the middle and pointed at the ends, like the moon when it is less than half full.  Also can be used to describe something shaped like a crescent. |例句：The crescent moon shone brightly in the night sky.  新月在夜空中闪耀。

例句：The baker made crescent rolls for breakfast.  面包师为早餐做了新月形面包卷。|近义词：curve, arc, half-moon|反义词：full moon, gibbous|词性:名词，形容词
crescent:/ˈkresənt/| n. adj.|新月，月牙形，新月形的|A curved shape that is wider in the middle and pointed at the ends, like the moon when it is less than half full.  Also can be used to describe something shaped like a crescent. |Etymology: From Old French "cressant" and Latin "crescens," present participle of "crescere" meaning "to grow," referring to the waxing moon.|
