araucarian:/ˌærɔːˈkɛəriən/ | adj. |南洋杉的，属于南洋杉科的|Relating to or belonging to the Araucariaceae family of coniferous trees, characterized by their stiff, pointed, scale-like leaves and often symmetrical branching.  |Example: The araucarian forests of South America are home to many unique species. 南美洲的南洋杉森林是许多独特物种的家园。

Example:  The monkey puzzle tree is a well-known araucarian species. 智利南洋杉是一种著名的南洋杉科树种。|近义词:coniferous, evergreen|反义词:deciduous|词性:形容词
