consent-to:/kənˈsɛnt tuː/
verb phrase
同意，准许
to agree to something; to give permission for something
She consented to the medical treatment. 她同意接受治疗。
The landlord consented to our request to keep a pet. 房东同意了我们养宠物的请求。
近义词:agree to, accede to, acquiesce in
反义词:refuse, object to, decline
词性:动词短语

词源:
From Latin consentire, from com- 'together' + sentire 'feel'.
