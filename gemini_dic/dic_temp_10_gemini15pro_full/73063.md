footpath:/ˈfʊtpɑːθ/ (UK) /ˈfʊtˌpæθ/ (US)| n. |人行道；小路|A path for people to walk on, especially alongside a road or across a field. |例句：We strolled along the footpath beside the river. 我们沿着河边的人行道漫步。

例句：The footpath led us through a beautiful meadow. 这条小路带我们穿过一片美丽的草地。

近义词：sidewalk (US), pavement (UK), trail, path, walkway|反义词:roadway, carriageway|词性:noun
