multitool:/ˈmʌltiˌtuːl/| n. |多功能工具|A hand tool that combines several individual functions, such as pliers, a screwdriver, a knife, and a saw, in a single compact unit.|I always keep a multitool in my car for emergencies. 我总是在车里放一个多功能工具以备不时之需。|Synonyms: multi-purpose tool, Swiss Army knife|Antonyms: single-purpose tool|noun
