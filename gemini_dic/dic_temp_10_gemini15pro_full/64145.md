equalist:/ˈiːkwəlɪst/ | n. | 平等主义者；主张人人平等者 | A person who believes in or advocates for the principle of equality, especially social equality. |  Example 1: The equalist argued that everyone should have the same opportunities.  平等主义者认为每个人都应该有相同的机会。

 Example 2:  She considered herself an equalist and fought for equal rights for all. 她自认为是一个平等主义者，并为所有人的平等权利而奋斗。| 近义词: egalitarian | 反义词: elitist, supremacist | 词性: 名词
