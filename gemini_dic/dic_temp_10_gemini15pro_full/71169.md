fissipation:/fɪsɪˈpeɪʃən/
n.
分裂，分散，浪费
the act of dissipating or dispersing something.  The fissipation of energy.
The fissipation of the crowd after the concert. 音乐会结束后人群的散开。
dispersion, scattering, dissipation, diffusion
concentration, gathering, accumulation
noun
