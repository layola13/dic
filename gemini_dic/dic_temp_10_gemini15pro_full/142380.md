orthoscope: /ˈɔːθəˌskoʊp/ | n. | 正像镜；正交镜 | An optical instrument designed to produce an image free of distortion, especially in telescopes or microscopes. It allows the observer to see objects in their true proportions and relationships. |  I used an orthoscope to observe the lunar surface. 我使用正像镜观测月球表面。 | The orthoscope provided a clear, undistorted view of the cell structure.  正像镜提供了清晰、无失真的细胞结构视图。 |  synonym: rectiscope | antonym: N/A | noun
