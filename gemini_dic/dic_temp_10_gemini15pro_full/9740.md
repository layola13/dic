antiviruses:/ˈæntivaɪrəsɪz/
noun
杀毒软件（复数）
Software designed to detect and remove computer viruses.
I have several antiviruses installed on my computer to protect it from malware. 我的电脑上安装了几个杀毒软件来保护它免受恶意软件的侵害。
synonyms: virus scanners, malware protection, security software
antonyms: viruses, malware, spyware
n.
