cointension:/ˌkəʊɪnˈtɛnʃən/ | n. |共同内涵，共同外延|The cointension of two terms is the set of attributes they share. 两个术语的共同内涵是它们共有的属性集合。|The cointension of "mammal" and "dog" includes being warm-blooded and having fur.  “哺乳动物”和“狗”的共同内涵包括温血和有毛。|近义词: connotation, implication |反义词: denotation, extension |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From co- (together) + intension (the set of attributes that define a concept). 源自 co-（一起）+ intension（定义一个概念的属性集合）。
