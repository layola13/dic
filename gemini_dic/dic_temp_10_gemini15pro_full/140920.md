oncoming:/ˈɑːnkʌmɪŋ/ /ˈɔːnkʌmɪŋ/| adj.|迎面而来的，即将发生的|Approaching; advancing.  Something that is oncoming is moving towards you or is about to happen.|例句：The oncoming car swerved to avoid a collision. 迎面而来的汽车突然转向避免了碰撞。|近义词：approaching, advancing, forthcoming, imminent|反义词： receding, retreating, outgoing|词性:形容词

oncoming:/ˈɑːnkʌmɪŋ/ /ˈɔːnkʌmɪŋ/| n.|来临，接近|The approach or arrival of something.|例句：The oncoming of winter brought freezing temperatures. 冬天的来临带来了冰冻的温度。|近义词：approach, arrival, advent|反义词：departure, retreat|词性:名词
