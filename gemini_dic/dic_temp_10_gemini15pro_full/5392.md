almacantar: /ˈælməkæntər/ | n. | 地平纬圈, 等高圈, 赤纬圈 | A circle parallel to the horizon, especially one used as a basis for astronomical calculations.  An almucantar is a circle on the celestial sphere parallel to the horizon. |  The astronomer used an almacantar to measure the star's altitude. 天文学家用地平纬圈来测量恒星的高度。 |  The ancient observatory used a series of almacanters to track celestial bodies.  古代天文台使用一系列地平纬圈来追踪天体。 | synonym: circle of equal altitude, parallel of altitude | antonym: vertical circle | noun
