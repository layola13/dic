floody:/ˈflʌdi/| adj.|泛滥的，洪水般的|Covered or saturated with water; resembling or characteristic of a flood.  Characterized by an abundance or excess of something.|The river was floody after the heavy rain.  河水在大雨过后泛滥成灾。|The market was floody with cheap imports.  市场上充斥着廉价的进口商品。|Synonym: inundated, overflowing, awash|Antonym: dry, arid|Etymology: From flood + -y|
