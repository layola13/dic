cut-off:/ˈkʌtɒf/ (UK) /ˈkʌtɔːf/ (US)| n. adj.|截止;界限;中断;截止点|A point or level which is a limit of something.|The cut-off for applications is May 1st.  申请截止日期是5月1日。|deadline, limit, termination|continuation, inclusion|noun
cut-off:/ˈkʌtɒf/ (UK) /ˈkʌtɔːf/ (US)| adj.|截断的;切断的|Stopped or ended abruptly.|He was wearing cut-off jeans. 他穿着截断的牛仔裤。|truncated, shortened, cropped|extended, lengthened|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb phrase "cut off," meaning to stop or interrupt something.  The noun and adjective forms developed from this verb phrase.
