margining:/ˈmɑːrdʒɪnɪŋ/
n.
追加保证金，保证金追缴
The act of depositing additional funds into a margin account to cover potential losses.
The broker issued a margin call, requiring margining of the account. 经纪人发出了追加保证金通知，要求为账户追加保证金。
Near Synonyms:  margin call, collateralization
Antonyms:  withdrawal, liquidation
词性: 名词
