objectivation: /əbˌdʒɛktɪvɪˈkeɪʃən/ /əbˌdʒɛktɪvəˈzeɪʃən/
n.
客观化；物化；实体化
The act of treating a person as an object, especially sexually.  Turning something abstract into something concrete or objective.
例句：The objectivation of women in advertising is a serious issue.  广告中女性的物化是一个严重的问题。

例句：The artist's objectivation of his emotions in his paintings is remarkable.  艺术家在画作中将其情感客观化的能力令人惊叹。

近义词：reification, depersonalization, thingification
反义词：subjectification, personalization, humanization
词性：noun
