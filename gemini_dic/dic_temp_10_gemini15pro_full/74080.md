fossillike:/ˈfɒsɪlˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 像化石的；类似化石的 | Resembling a fossil; similar to a fossil in appearance or nature. | The pottery shards were fossillike in their hardness and texture.  这些陶器碎片的硬度和质地像化石一样。 | The imprint in the rock was fossillike. 岩石上的印记像化石一样。| 近义词: fossilized, petrified | 反义词: modern, contemporary | 词性: 形容词


词源:

fossil (n.) + -like (suffix)

"fossil" comes from Late Latin "fossilis" meaning "dug up,"  from "fossus," past participle of "fodere" meaning "to dig."

"-like" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling, characteristic of."
