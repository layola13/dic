hindsight:/ˈhaɪndˌsaɪt/| n.|后见之明，事后诸葛亮|The understanding of a situation or event only after it has happened or developed.|With hindsight, I should have taken the job offer. 事后看来，我应该接受那份工作.|近义词：retrospect, afterthought|反义词：foresight, anticipation|词性:名词

词源: From hind (behind) + sight.  Literally "seeing behind," or seeing after the event.
