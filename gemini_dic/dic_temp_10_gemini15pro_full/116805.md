mawmish:/ˈmɔːmɪʃ/| adj.|感伤的，多愁善感的|Sentimental in a silly or tearful way, often exaggerated or affected.  Overly sentimental to the point of being mawkish.|The film was so mawmish I almost walked out.|近义词：sentimental, cloying, saccharine, oversweet|反义词：unsentimental, cynical, dry|词性:形容词

词源: From *mawkish*, from obsolete *mawk* (maggot) + *-ish*.  Originally referring to a nauseating sweetness, like the taste of maggots, later extended to excessive sentimentality.
