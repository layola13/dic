lune:/luːn/| n.|月牙，新月;  月牙形物|Anything shaped like a crescent moon.  The visible part of the moon when less than half full. |The pale gold lune of the young moon hung low in the sky.  淡金色的新月低垂在天空中。|crescent, sickle |full moon|noun
