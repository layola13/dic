pantascope: /pænˈtəˌskoʊp/ (US) /pænˈtæskəʊp/ (UK)
|n.|全景望远镜，潜望镜|A periscope-like device, often large and elaborate, used for viewing distant objects or scenes, particularly panoramic views. It historically referred to devices used in theatres and for viewing processions, but can also refer to modern panoramic viewing instruments.|From a high vantage point, using the pantascope, the general surveyed the battlefield. 将军站在高处，用全景望远镜观察战场。|Synonyms: periscope, panorama, wide-angle lens|Antonyms: microscope|noun
