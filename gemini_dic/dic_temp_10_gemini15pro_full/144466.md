outteased:/ˌaʊtˈtiːzd/
verb
戏弄胜过; 比...更会戏弄
to tease or mock someone more effectively than they tease or mock you.
He outteased his brother, leaving him speechless. 他把弟弟戏弄得说不出话来。
She outteased her classmates with her witty remarks. 她用机智的言辞胜过同学们的戏弄。
Synonyms: outwit, outsmart, surpass in teasing
Antonyms: be outteased, lose at teasing
词性: 动词
