freeform:/ˈfriːfɔːrm/| adj. n.|自由形式的，自由格式；自由格式|Having or allowing much freedom or flexibility in form, style, or manner.  Characterized by a lack of a predetermined structure or plan.|The artist prefers freeform jazz improvisation. 这位艺术家更喜欢自由形式的爵士即兴演奏。|近义词：unstructured, improvisational, flexible, open-ended|反义词：structured, formal, rigid, fixed|adjective
freeform:/ˈfriːfɔːrm/| n.|自由格式|Something that is freeform or unstructured.|Her pottery is mostly freeform. 她的陶艺大多是自由形式的。|近义词： improvisation, ad-lib|反义词： structure, framework|noun
