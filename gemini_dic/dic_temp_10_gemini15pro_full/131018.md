nonaugmentatives:/ˌnɒnɔːɡˈmɛntətɪvz/ | n. |非增义词，非增强词 | Words that do not indicate increased size, intensity, or force. These are the standard forms of words without any suffixes that augment their meaning. |  Many common nouns like "house," "car," and "book" are nonaugmentatives. 许多常见的名词，如“房子”、“汽车”和“书”，都是非增义词。 | 近义词: base words, root words | 反义词: augmentatives | 词性: noun
