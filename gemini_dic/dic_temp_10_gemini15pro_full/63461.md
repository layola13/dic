entrepreneurisms:/ˌɑːntrəprəˈnɜːrɪzəmz/
noun
创业精神（复数）
The collective activities and mindsets associated with entrepreneurship, often used to describe the prevalence or various forms of entrepreneurial behavior within a specific context.  This word is not commonly used; "entrepreneurship" (singular) is much more frequent.
The professor praised the student's entrepreneurisms, citing their innovative project and proactive approach. 教授称赞了学生的创业精神，列举了他们的创新项目和积极主动的态度。
The government is trying to foster entrepreneurisms in the tech sector. 政府正在努力促进科技领域的创业精神。
Near Synonyms: entrepreneurial spirit, entrepreneurial activities, ventures, initiatives
Antonyms:  risk aversion, complacency, inertia
