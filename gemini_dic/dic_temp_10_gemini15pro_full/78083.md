geoengineering:/ˌdʒiːoʊˌɛndʒɪˈnɪrɪŋ/
n.|地球工程|The deliberate large-scale manipulation of an environmental process that affects the earth's climate, in an attempt to counteract the effects of global warming.|Scientists are exploring various geoengineering techniques to combat climate change. 科学家们正在探索各种地球工程技术来应对气候变化。|近义词:climate engineering, climate intervention|反义词:(No direct antonym, potentially "natural climate variability")
词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
geo- (earth) + engineering (the application of science and mathematics to develop useful structures and machines)
