nondiminishing:/ˌnɒndɪˈmɪnɪʃɪŋ/
adjective
不减少的，不衰减的
Not becoming smaller or weaker; remaining constant in strength, intensity, or amount.
The nondiminishing enthusiasm of the fans kept the team going. 球迷们不减的热情激励着球队前进。
近义词: constant, unwavering, persistent, sustained, unrelenting
反义词: diminishing, decreasing, declining, waning, fading
词性: adjective

non- + diminishing (diminish 的现在分词)
non-:  prefix meaning "not"
diminish: from Old French diminuir, from Latin diminuere "to lessen," from dis- "apart" + minuere "to make small"
