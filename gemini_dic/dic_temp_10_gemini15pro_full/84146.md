half-nylon:/ˌhɑːfˈnaɪlɒn/ (US) /ˌhɑːfˈnaɪlən/ (UK)| adj.|半尼龙的|Made of a blend of nylon and another fabric, typically cotton or polyester.  A garment or fabric that is composed partly of nylon and partly of another material.|例句：She wore a half-nylon, half-cotton jacket. 她穿了一件半尼龙半棉的夹克。|近义词：part-nylon, nylon blend|反义词:all-cotton, pure cotton|词性:形容词

half-nylon:/ˌhɑːfˈnaɪlɒn/ (US) /ˌhɑːfˈnaɪlən/ (UK)| n.|半尼龙织物|A fabric that is half nylon and half another material.|例句：The tent is made of a durable half-nylon.  这个帐篷是用耐用的半尼龙材料制成的。|近义词：nylon blend fabric, part-nylon material|反义词:pure cotton fabric, all-cotton material|词性:名词
