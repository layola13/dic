clip-clopped:/klɪpˈklɒpt/ | v. |发出“哒哒”的马蹄声|The sound of a horse's hooves striking a hard surface.|The horse clip-clopped down the cobbled street. 马儿发出哒哒的马蹄声走过鹅卵石铺成的街道。|近义词：clattered, trotted|反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "glided" for a contrasting movement)|词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
Onomatopoeic, imitating the sound of hooves.  From "clip" + "clop," both imitative of the sound.
