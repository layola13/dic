antifeudalists:/ˌæntiˈfjuːdəlɪsts/| n. |反封建主义者|People who opposed feudalism, a social system in which land ownership and power were based on a hierarchy of lords and vassals.| The antifeudalists advocated for land reform and greater equality. 反封建主义者倡导土地改革和更大的平等。|
antifeudalists:/ˌæntiˈfjuːdəlɪsts/| n. |反封建主义者|Those who actively worked to dismantle or resist the feudal system.| The French Revolution saw the rise of powerful antifeudalists who sought to overthrow the monarchy and aristocracy. 法国大革命见证了旨在推翻君主制和贵族的强大的反封建主义者的兴起。|Synonyms: reformers, revolutionaries, egalitarians|Antonyms: feudalists, aristocrats, conservatives| Noun
