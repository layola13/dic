biome: /ˈbaɪoʊm/ | n. | 生物群系 | A large naturally occurring community of flora and fauna occupying a major habitat, e.g., forest or tundra. | The Amazon rainforest is a vibrant biome teeming with biodiversity. 亚马逊雨林是一个充满生物多样性的充满活力的生物群系。| Example:  Scientists are studying the effects of climate change on different biomes. 科学家正在研究气候变化对不同生物群系的影响。| synonym: ecosystem, habitat | antonym: N/A | noun
