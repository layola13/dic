driftingly:/ˈdrɪftɪŋli/| adv.|漂流地，飘忽不定地|In a drifting manner; aimlessly; without purpose.|The boat drifted driftingly down the river. 小船漫无目的地漂流而下。|近义词：aimlessly, lazily, idly, dreamily|反义词: purposefully, deliberately|词性:副词

Etymology:
From drift + -ing + -ly.  Drift ultimately comes from the Old Norse *drifa, meaning "to drive."
