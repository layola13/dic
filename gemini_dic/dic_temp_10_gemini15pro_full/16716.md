barrow:/ˈbæroʊ/ (US) /ˈbærəʊ/ (UK)| n.|独轮手推车；（医院里的）运送病人、尸体的手推车；古坟，坟冢|A cart with one wheel and two handles that you use to carry things, especially soil or plants; A covered frame on wheels, used in hospitals for transporting patients, equipment, or bodies; A mound of earth or stones placed over a burial site|He wheeled the barrow full of stones down the path. 他把装满石头的手推车沿着小路推了下去。|近义词：cart, wheelbarrow|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

barrow:/ˈbæroʊ/ (US) /ˈbærəʊ/ (UK)| n.|公猪（尤指阉割过的）|A male pig, especially a castrated one|The farmer brought the barrow to market. 农夫把公猪带到市场上去卖。|近义词：boar, hog|反义词:sow|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *bearwe (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *berwaz ("carried"). Cognate with Old High German bërawa ("bier"). The "barrow" meaning "mound of earth over a grave" comes from Old English beorg ("hill, mound").
