olivine: /ˈɒlɪviːn/ (UK) /ˈɑːləviːn/ (US) | n. |橄榄石|A magnesium iron silicate (Mg, Fe)2SiO4, occurring widely in basalt, peridotite, and other basic igneous rocks, typically as olive-green crystals.|Olivine is a common mineral in Earth's mantle. 橄榄石是地幔中常见的矿物。|
Olivine is the birthstone for August. 橄榄石是八月的生辰石。|
Synonyms: chrysolite, peridot|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
