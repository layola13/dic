aoe:/eɪ oʊ iː/ |abbr.| area of effect (游戏术语：范围效果)|A term used in gaming to describe abilities or spells that affect a designated area.  It stands for "area of effect".|The mage cast a powerful aoe spell, damaging all the enemies in the circle.  法师施放了一个强大的范围效果法术，对圆圈内的所有敌人造成伤害。|synonyms: area effect, splash damage|antonyms: single target, targeted|abbreviation
