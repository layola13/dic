harier:/ˈhɛriər/ (US), /ˈhæriər/ (UK)| adj.|更毛茸茸的；更粗糙的；更凶猛的；更粗暴的|more hairy, rough, fierce, or violent than another or than usual|The harier dog was more intimidating. 更毛茸茸的狗看起来更吓人。| The terrain became harier as they climbed higher.  随着他们爬得越高，地形变得越崎岖。|近义词：shaggier, rougher, fiercer, more violent|反义词: smoother, gentler, calmer, less violent|词性:形容词

词源:
From Middle English *harier, comparative of *hari (“hairy”), from Old English hǣrig (“hairy”), from Proto-Germanic *hairaz (“hair”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱer- (“hair, bristle”). Cognate with Scots hairy (“hairy”), West Frisian hierrich (“hairy”), Dutch harig (“hairy”), German haarig (“hairy”), Danish håret (“hairy”), Swedish hårig (“hairy”).  The comparative suffix -er is from Old English -ra.
