nonartistical:/ˌnɒnɑːˈtɪstɪkəl/
adjective
非艺术的；不艺术的
Not related to or characteristic of art or artists.
His nonartistical approach to design resulted in a functional but uninspired product.  他非艺术的设计方法导致产品实用但不具启发性。
The report was a nonartistical document, focusing solely on the facts and figures. 这份报告是一份非艺术性文件，只关注事实和数据。
Near Synonyms: unartistic, inartistic, nonaesthetic
Antonyms: artistic, aesthetic, creative
词性: 形容词
