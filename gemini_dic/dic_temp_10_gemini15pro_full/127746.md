negotiatory:/nɪˈɡoʊʃiətɔːri/
adjective
谈判的；协商的
Relating to or involving negotiation; of the nature of negotiation.
The two parties began negotiatory talks to resolve the trade dispute. 双方开始谈判以解决贸易争端。
Negotiatory skills are essential in diplomacy.  谈判技巧在外交中至关重要。
近义词: negotiating, bargaining, deliberative
反义词: inflexible, uncompromising, rigid
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *negotiatorius, from Latin negotiari "to do business," from negotium "business," from nec otium "not leisure."
