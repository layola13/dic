biddableness:/ˈbɪdəbəlnəs/
n.
顺从;温顺;驯服
The quality of being biddable; obedience or tractability.
Her biddableness made her an easy target for manipulation. 她温顺的性格使她很容易被操纵。
Synonyms: obedience, tractability, compliance, docility, submissiveness
Antonyms: disobedience, defiance, rebelliousness, intractability, obstinacy
