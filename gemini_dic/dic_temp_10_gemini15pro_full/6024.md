ameliorating:/əˈmiːliəreɪtɪŋ/
Verb
改善，改进
making (something bad or unsatisfactory) better.
The new policy is aimed at ameliorating the housing crisis. 这项新政策旨在改善住房危机。
Synonyms: improving, bettering, enhancing, upgrading
Antonyms: worsening, exacerbating, deteriorating
