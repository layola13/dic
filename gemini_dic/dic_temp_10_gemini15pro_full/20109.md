biotechnologist:/ˌbaɪoʊtekˈnɑːlədʒɪst/ | n. | 生物技术专家，生物工艺学家 | A scientist or engineer who works in the field of biotechnology, using living organisms or their products to develop or make useful products. |  Biotechnologists are working to develop new treatments for cancer. 生物技术专家正在努力开发新的癌症治疗方法。|  Biotechnologists play a crucial role in developing sustainable agricultural practices. 生物技术专家在开发可持续农业实践中发挥着至关重要的作用。 | Synonyms: genetic engineer, bioscientist | Antonyms: N/A | noun
