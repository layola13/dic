gamified:/ˈɡeɪmɪfaɪd/
adjective
游戏化的
Made into a game or presented as a game; given game-like qualities.
The company gamified its training program to make it more engaging.  公司将培训项目游戏化，使其更具吸引力。
The app uses gamified challenges to encourage users to exercise more.  这个应用程序使用游戏化挑战来鼓励用户多锻炼。
近义词:  interactive, engaging, playful, stimulating
反义词:  boring, tedious, monotonous, dull
词性: adjective
