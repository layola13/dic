para-church:/ˌpærəˈtʃɜːrtʃ/
adjective
准教会的; 教会附属的
Relating to or denoting organizations or activities that are Christian in character but not formally affiliated with or controlled by a church.
Para-church organizations often focus on specific ministries or social issues.  准教会组织通常专注于特定的部门或社会问题。
Youth With A Mission (YWAM) is a well-known para-church organization.  青年使命团是一个著名的准教会组织。
Near Synonyms:  interdenominational, non-denominational, independent Christian
Antonyms: denominational, ecclesiastical
word type: adjective
