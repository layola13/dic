glassiest:/ˈɡlæsiɪst/| adj.|最光滑的，最像玻璃的；最冷漠的，最无表情的|Superlative form of glassy: most glassy.  Having a smooth, shiny surface like glass; (of a person's eyes or expression) showing no emotion or understanding.|The lake was glassiest in the early morning, reflecting the sky perfectly. 清晨的湖面最光滑，完美地映照着天空。|His glassiest stare made her uncomfortable. 他那毫无表情的凝视让她感到不舒服。|近义词：smooth, shiny, polished, glazed, expressionless, blank, vacant|反义词：rough, dull, matte, expressive, animated|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From glass + -y + -est.  "Glass" comes from Proto-Germanic *glasam, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ghel- (to shine).
