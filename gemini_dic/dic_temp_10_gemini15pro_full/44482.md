crossbred:/ˈkrɒsbrɛd/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsbrɛd/ (US)| adj. n.|杂交的，杂种的|Bred from parents of different breeds or varieties.|例句：The farmer raises crossbred cattle for their hardiness.  这位农民饲养杂交牛，因为它们很耐寒。|近义词：hybrid, mongrel, mixed-breed|反义词：purebred, pedigree|词性：形容词、名词

crossbred:/ˈkrɒsbrɛd/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsbrɛd/ (US)| n.|杂种动物/植物|An animal or plant produced by crossbreeding.|例句：The crossbred puppies were a mix of Labrador and Collie.  这些杂交的小狗是拉布拉多犬和柯利牧羊犬的混种。|近义词：hybrid, mongrel, mixed-breed|反义词：purebred, pedigree|词性：名词
