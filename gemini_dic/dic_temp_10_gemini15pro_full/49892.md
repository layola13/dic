demurrable:/dɪˈmɜːrəbl/ | adj. | 可异议的，可驳回的 | Open to objection; that may be demurred to.  Relating to a demurrer, which is a legal pleading that objects to the legal sufficiency of an opponent's pleading. |  The lawyer argued that the plaintiff's claim was demurrable due to lack of sufficient evidence. 律师辩称，原告的主张由于缺乏足够的证据而可被驳回。 | Example: The defendant filed a demurrable plea, arguing the charges were insufficient.  被告提出了一个可驳回的抗辩，辩称指控不充分。| Synonyms: objectionable, challengeable, disputable, contestable | Antonyms: incontestable, irrefutable, unchallengeable | adjective
