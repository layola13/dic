parodic: /pəˈrɒdɪk/ (British) /pəˈrɑːdɪk/ (American)
|adj.|滑稽模仿的；戏仿的|Imitating something in a humorous way; relating to or being a parody.|The show was a parodic take on current events.  这个节目是对时事的滑稽模仿。|例句：The film is a parodic tribute to the classic horror movies. 这部电影是对经典恐怖片的滑稽致敬。|近义词：satirical, mocking, burlesque, ironic|反义词：serious, respectful, reverent|词性: 形容词
