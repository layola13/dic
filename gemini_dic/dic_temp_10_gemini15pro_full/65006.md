estrepe:/esˈtrepe/ | n. |喧闹，吵闹；喧嚣；骚动|A loud, harsh, or disagreeable noise; a din; clamor. | The estrepe of the construction work made it impossible to concentrate. 建筑工程的喧嚣声让人无法集中注意力。|  The market was filled with the estrepe of hawkers selling their wares.  市场上充满了小贩叫卖的喧嚣声。| 近义词: noise, din, clamor, racket, uproar | 反义词: quiet, silence, peace, tranquility | 词性: noun

词源: From Spanish *estrepito*, ultimately from Latin *strepitus* (“noise”).
