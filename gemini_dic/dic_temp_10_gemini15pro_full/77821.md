generational:/ˌdʒɛnəˈreɪʃənl/ | adj. | 世代的，代际的 | Relating to or characteristic of a particular generation. |  Example: Generational differences in attitudes towards technology are significant.  科技态度上的代际差异很显著。

Example:  There's a generational shift happening in the workplace.  职场正在发生代际转变。| 近义词: intergenerational | 反义词: (No direct antonym, consider using terms like "individual" or "universal") | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From generation + -al.  "Generation" comes from the Latin "generātiō," meaning "a begetting, production, propagation, race, kind."
