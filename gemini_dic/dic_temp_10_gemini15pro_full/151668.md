partisanry:/ˈpɑːrtɪzənri/ | n. | 党派性，党派偏见 |  Prejudice in favor of a particular cause; bias. | The fierce partisanry of the election made reasoned debate impossible. 选举中激烈的党派之争使得理性辩论成为不可能。 | 近义词：bias, partisanship, sectarianism | 反义词：impartiality, neutrality | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From French partisanerie, from partisan. Ultimately from Italian partigiano.
