ga-ga:/ˈɡɑːɡɑː/| adj.|痴呆的，糊涂的；过分热情的；着迷的|Infatuated or foolishly enthusiastic; mentally senile or impaired.|例句：She's ga-ga over the new boy band. 她对这个新成立的男子乐队非常着迷。|例句：My grandfather is getting a bit ga-ga in his old age. 我的祖父年纪大了，有点糊涂了。|近义词：infatuated, obsessed, senile, dotty|反义词：lucid, sane, rational|词性:形容词

ga-ga:/ˈɡɑːɡɑː/| n.|婴儿语；胡言乱语|Baby talk; nonsensical chatter.|例句：The baby was gurgling and making ga-ga noises. 婴儿咯咯地叫着，发出咿呀学语的声音。|例句：He was talking ga-ga after hitting his head. 他撞到头后开始胡言乱语。|近义词：babble, gibberish|反义词：sense, coherence|词性:名词
