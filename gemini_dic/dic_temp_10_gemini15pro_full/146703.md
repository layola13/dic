overhelpful:/ˌoʊvərˈhɛlpfʊl/| adj.|过分热心的，过分帮忙的|Excessively eager to help, often to the point of being intrusive or interfering.|例句：She was so overhelpful that it became annoying. 她太热心了，以至于让人觉得烦。|近义词：intrusive, interfering, meddlesome|反义词：unhelpful, inconsiderate|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From 'over-' (meaning "excessive") + 'helpful' (meaning "providing assistance").  The prefix "over-" intensifies the meaning of "helpful" to suggest an excess of helpfulness.
