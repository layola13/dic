epidemiography:/ˌɛpɪˌdiːmiˈɒɡrəfi/ | n. | 流行病志; 流行病描述; |The study of the geographic distribution of disease and its change over time. | Epidemiography helps us understand how diseases spread and affect different populations. 流行病志帮助我们了解疾病是如何传播和影响不同人群的。| 近义词: descriptive epidemiology, disease mapping | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, potentially disease eradication/control) | Noun
