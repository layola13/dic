arrears:/əˈrɪərz/| n.|欠款，拖欠，积压|Money that is owed and should have been paid earlier.  The state of being behind in the fulfilling of an obligation. |例句：The company is in arrears with its rent.  这家公司拖欠租金。

例句：He is several months in arrears with his mortgage payments.  他的抵押贷款已拖欠数月。|近义词：debts, dues, unpaid balance, overdue payments|反义词：prepayments, advance payments|词性:名词

词源: From Old French arere "behind, backward," from Latin ad retro "to the rear." Originally used in the phrase in arrears, meaning "behind in payment."
