menaccanite:/mɛˈnækəˌnaɪt/ | n. |钛铁矿，钛铁氧化物|A black, opaque mineral consisting of iron and titanium oxides; ilmenite.  It is a common accessory mineral in igneous and metamorphic rocks.|例句：Menaccanite is a source of titanium. 钛铁矿是钛的来源。

例句：The beach sands were rich in menaccanite. 海滩沙子中富含钛铁矿。|近义词：ilmenite|反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific mineral) |词性:noun
