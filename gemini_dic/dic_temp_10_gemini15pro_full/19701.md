bilimbi:/bɪˈlɪmbi/ | n. | 比林比，三敛 | A small, sour fruit of a tree native to Southeast Asia, used in curries and pickles.  It's also known as cucumber tree or tree sorrel. |  The bilimbi is too sour to eat raw.  比林比太酸了，不能生吃。

|  Bilimbi juice makes a refreshing drink. 比林比汁是一种提神的饮料。 | 近义词: Averrhoa bilimbi (scientific name), cucumber tree, tree sorrel | 反义词:  None (as it's a specific fruit) | noun


bilimbi:/bɪˈlɪmbi/ | adj. (less common) | 比林比的 | Made from or relating to the bilimbi fruit. | The bilimbi chutney was surprisingly tasty. 比林比酸辣酱出奇地好吃。 | Bilimbi flavour is quite unique. 比林比的味道很独特。 | 近义词: None | 反义词: None | adjective
