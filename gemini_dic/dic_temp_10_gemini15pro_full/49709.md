demobs:/ˌdiːˈmɒbz/ /ˌdiːˈmɑːbz/| n. |复员，复员军人|The process of demobilizing members of the armed forces.  A person who has been demobilized from the armed forces.|
The demobs were given train tickets home. 复员军人拿到了回家的火车票。|
The government announced a plan for the demob of thousands of soldiers. 政府宣布了一项数千名士兵复员的计划。|
近义词：discharge, muster out|
反义词：enlistment, recruitment|
词性：名词

demob:/ˌdiːˈmɒb/ /ˌdiːˈmɑːbz/| v. |复员，使…复员|To demobilize someone from the armed forces.|
Thousands of soldiers were demobbed after the war.  战争结束后，成千上万的士兵复员了。|
He was demobbed in 1945. 他于1945年复员。|
近义词：discharge, muster out|
反义词：enlist, recruit|
词性：动词
