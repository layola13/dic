lighterman:/ˈlaɪtərmən/| n. |驳船夫，运河船夫|A person who operates or assists in operating a lighter, a type of flat-bottomed barge used to transfer goods to and from larger ships in harbors or rivers.|The lighterman skillfully maneuvered his barge alongside the cargo ship.  驳船夫熟练地将驳船停靠在货船旁边。|Synonyms: bargeman, boatman, waterman|Antonyms: landsman|noun
