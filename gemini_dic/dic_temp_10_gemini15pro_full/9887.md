apastron:/əˈpæstrɒn/ | n. |远星点; 拱点 | The apastron is the point in the orbit of a star or planet at which it is furthest from the star around which it revolves. | The comet reached its apastron and began its long journey back towards the sun. 这颗彗星到达了它的远星点，并开始了它返回太阳的漫长旅程。|  The satellite reached apastron last week. 该卫星上周到达了远星点。 | synonym: apoapsis, apocenter | antonym: periastron, pericenter | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀπό (apó, “from, away from”) + ἄστρον (ástron, “star”).
