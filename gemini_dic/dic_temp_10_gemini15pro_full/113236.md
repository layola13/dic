lustering:/ˈlʌstərɪŋ/ | adj. |  有光泽的，闪亮的 | Having a sheen or gloss; gleaming. | The car had a lustrous paint job. 汽车漆面很有光泽。| 近义词: shiny, glossy, gleaming, shimmering | 反义词: dull, matte | 词性:形容词

lustering:/ˈlʌstərɪŋ/ | n. | 光泽，闪光 | The state or quality of being lustrous. | The lustering of the silk was beautiful. 丝绸的光泽很美。| 近义词: sheen, gloss, gleam, shimmer | 反义词: dullness, matteness | 词性:名词


词源: From luster + -ing.  Luster itself comes from Old French *lustre "shine, brilliance," from Latin *lustrare "to brighten, illuminate."  Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European root *leuk- "light, brightness."
