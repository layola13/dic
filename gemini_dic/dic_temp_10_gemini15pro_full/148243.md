overskeptically: /ˌoʊvərˈskɛptɪkli/
| adv. | 过于怀疑地 | In a way that shows too much skepticism; excessively doubtfully. | He examined the evidence overskeptically, unwilling to accept any conclusion. 他过于怀疑地检查了证据，不愿接受任何结论。|  Example 2:  She overskeptically questioned every detail of his story, making him feel defensive. 她过于怀疑地质疑他故事的每一个细节，让他感到很防备。 | 近义词：cynically, doubtfully, suspiciously | 反义词：credulously, naively, trustingly | 词性: 副词

词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessively") + skeptically (adverb form of skeptical, from Greek skeptikos, meaning "inquiring, reflective")
