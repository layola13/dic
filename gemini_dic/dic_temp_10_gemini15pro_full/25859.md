burka:/ˈbɜːrkə/ (US), /ˈbɜːkə/ (UK)| n. | 长袍,罩袍 |A long, loose garment covering the whole body from head to feet, worn in public by many Muslim women.|She wore a burka that concealed her entire face. 她穿着一件遮住整个脸的长袍。| The woman in the burka walked quickly through the market.  穿着罩袍的女人快速穿过市场。|近义词：niqab, chador, hijab |反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly Western clothing) |词性:noun
