despairingly:/dɪˈspeərɪŋli/
adverb
绝望地，失望地
in a way that shows you have lost all hope
He shook his head despairingly. 他绝望地摇了摇头。
Synonyms: hopelessly, despondently, dejectedly
Antonyms: hopefully, optimistically
词性: 副词
