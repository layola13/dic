oestruses:/ˈɛstrəsɪz/ | n. | 发情期 | A recurring period of sexual receptivity and fertility in many female mammals; heat. | The veterinarian said the dog's oestruses occur approximately every six months. 兽医说这只狗的发情期大约每六个月一次。 |  The farmer carefully monitored the oestruses of his cows to optimize breeding. 农场主仔细监测了他奶牛的发情期，以优化繁殖。 | 近义词：heat, estrus | 反义词：anoestrus | 词性: 名词

词源: From Greek _oîstros_ "gadfly, frenzy," referring to the agitated behavior of some animals during this period.
