concordancer:/kənˈkɔːrdənsər/| n.|索引器，词汇索引汇编程序|A concordancer is a computer program that searches a large body of text to find all occurrences of a particular word or phrase and displays them in context.  索引器是一个计算机程序，它搜索大量的文本以查找特定单词或短语的所有出现位置，并在上下文中显示它们。|This online concordancer allows you to search millions of words. 这个在线索引器允许你搜索数百万个单词。|synonyms: index, lexicon, thesaurus|antonyms: N/A|noun
