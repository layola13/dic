anesthetized:/əˈnɛsθəˌtaɪzd/ |adj.|麻醉的，失去知觉的|Deprived of feeling or sensation, especially by a drug.|The patient was anesthetized before the surgery. 病人在手术前被麻醉了。|Synonyms: numb, insensible, unconscious|Antonyms: conscious, aware, sensitive|adjective

anesthesia:/ˌænɪsˈθiːʒə/ |n.|麻醉，麻醉状态|A loss of feeling or sensation, especially of pain, induced by a drug.|The patient was given general anesthesia. 病人进行了全身麻醉。|Synonyms: numbness, insensibility|Antonyms: consciousness, awareness|noun

anesthetize:/əˈnɛsθəˌtaɪz/ |v.|麻醉，使失去知觉|To deprive of feeling or sensation, especially by a drug.|The vet anesthetized the cat before examining it. 兽医在检查前给猫麻醉了。|Synonyms: numb, desensitize|Antonyms: sensitize, stimulate|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *anesthésier*, from Ancient Greek *ἀναισθησία* (anaisthesia, “lack of sensation”), from *ἀν-* (an-, “without”) + *αἴσθησις* (aisthēsis, “sensation, feeling”).
