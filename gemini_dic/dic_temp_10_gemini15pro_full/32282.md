chiefest:/ˈtʃiːfɪst/
adjective
最重要的；最主要的
Most important; leading.
He was her chiefest confidant. 他是她最主要的知己。
synonyms: principal, foremost, leading, main, primary, paramount, predominant, supreme, highest, greatest, top, prime, first, major, key, central, cardinal, pre-eminent, outstanding, prominent, eminent, superior, excellent, best
antonyms: least, minor, insignificant
词性: adjective
