abruption: /əˈbrʌpʃən/
n.
突然中断，中断；突然分离；胎盘早剥
a sudden breaking off or cessation
The abruption of the negotiations put the whole project in jeopardy.  谈判的突然中断使整个项目处于危险之中。

The doctor diagnosed placental abruption. 医生诊断为胎盘早剥。

近义词: separation, detachment, disruption, rupture, break, severance
反义词: continuation, connection, junction
词性: 名词
