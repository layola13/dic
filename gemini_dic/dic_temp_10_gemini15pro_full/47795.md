decapitators:/dɪˈkæpɪˌteɪtərz/
n.
斩首者，行刑者
People who decapitate; executioners.
The decapitators carried out the sentence swiftly and silently. 行刑者快速而无声地执行了判决。
The movie depicted brutal decapitators in the French Revolution.  这部电影描绘了法国大革命中残暴的刽子手。
synonyms: executioners, headsmen, killers, murderers
antonyms: saviors, protectors, rescuers
noun
