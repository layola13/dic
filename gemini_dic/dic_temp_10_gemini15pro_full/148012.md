overripe:/ˌoʊvərˈraɪp/ | adj. | 过熟的；熟透的 | Excessively ripe; past its prime. | The overripe bananas were starting to turn brown.  过熟的香蕉开始变成褐色了。 | The peaches were overripe and mushy. 桃子熟过头了，都软烂了。 | 近义词: rotten, decayed, putrid | 反义词: unripe, green | 词性: 形容词

词源: Old English *oferrip*, from *ofer* (over) + *ripe* (ripe).
