homogenise:/həˈmɒdʒənaɪz/ /həˈmɑːdʒənaɪz/| vt.|使均匀，使同质，使类同|To make something uniform or consistent throughout.|The company homogenised its product line to appeal to a broader market.  该公司使其产品线同质化，以吸引更广泛的市场。|standardize, unify, blend|vary, diversify|verb
homogenize:/həˈmɒdʒənaɪz/ /həˈmɑːdʒənaɪz/| vt.|使均匀，使同质，使类同|To make something uniform or consistent throughout.|The milk is homogenized to prevent the cream from separating. 牛奶经过均质化处理，以防止奶油分离。|standardize, unify, blend|vary, diversify|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *homogénéiser*, from *homogène* (homogeneous) + *-iser* (-ize). Ultimately from Greek *homogenēs* (homogeneous).
