hypervigilance:/ˌhaɪpərˈvɪdʒɪləns/| n.|过度警觉，高度警惕|A state of abnormally increased arousal and attentiveness to potential threats, often accompanied by anxiety and difficulty relaxing.|例句：After the trauma, she experienced constant hypervigilance, startled by the slightest noise. 创伤之后，她一直处于高度警惕的状态，一点轻微的响动都会吓到她。

例句：Hypervigilance can be a symptom of PTSD. 过度警觉可能是创伤后应激障碍的一种症状。|近义词：watchfulness, alertness, vigilance, cautiousness|反义词：inattentiveness, carelessness, obliviousness|词性:名词

词源:  From *hyper- "over, excessive" + *vigilant "watchful, alert".  The word "vigilant" ultimately comes from the Latin *vigilāns*, present participle of *vigilāre* "to keep watch."
