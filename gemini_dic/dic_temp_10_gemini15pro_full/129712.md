non-egocentric:/ˌnɒn iːɡəʊˈsɛntrɪk/ | adj. | 非自我中心的，不以自我为中心的 | Not focused primarily on oneself; considering the perspectives and needs of others. |  She displayed a non-egocentric attitude by always putting her team's needs before her own. 她总是把团队的需求放在自己的需求之前，表现出一种非自我中心的态度。

She took a non-egocentric approach to the problem, considering all stakeholders' viewpoints. 她采取了一种非自我中心的方法来解决这个问题，考虑了所有利益相关者的观点。

| 近义词: altruistic, selfless, other-regarding, considerate, empathetic | 反义词: egocentric, self-centered, selfish, narcissistic | 词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

From non- + egocentric.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of".  "Egocentric" comes from "ego" (Latin for "I") + "centric" (from Latin "centrum" meaning "center").  Therefore, "non-egocentric" literally means "not I-centered."
