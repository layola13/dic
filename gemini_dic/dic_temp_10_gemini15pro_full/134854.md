nonmodernnesses:/ˌnɒnˌmɒdərnɪsnɪsɪz/ /ˌnɑːnˌmɑːdərnɪsnɪsɪz/
noun
非现代性（复数）
The state or quality of not being modern; lack of modern characteristics or features.
The exhibition explored the nonmodernnesses of traditional craft practices.  这个展览探讨了传统工艺实践的非现代性。
The architect intentionally incorporated certain nonmodernnesses into the building's design.  建筑师有意在建筑设计中融入了一些非现代元素。
Near Synonyms:  traditionalism, anachronism, antiquity, archaism
Antonyms:  modernity, modernism, contemporaneity
