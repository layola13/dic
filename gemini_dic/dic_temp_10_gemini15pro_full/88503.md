historionomer: /hɪˌstɔːriˈɑːnəmər/
n.
历史学家；史学研究者
A scholar who studies history, especially with a focus on methodology and the philosophical foundations of historical research.
He is a renowned historionomer, specializing in the intellectual history of the Enlightenment. 他是一位著名的史学研究者，专攻启蒙运动的思想史。
近义词: historian, history scholar, historiographer
反义词:  (No direct antonym, possibly fabricator of history, liar)
