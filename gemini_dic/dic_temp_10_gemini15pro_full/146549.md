overgluts:/ˌoʊvərˈɡlʌts/
n.|过剩，过饱和|A supply that greatly exceeds demand. |The market is suffering from an overglut of cheap imports. 市场正遭受廉价进口商品过剩的困扰。| surplus, excess, overabundance | shortage, dearth, scarcity | noun

overgluts:/ˌoʊvərˈɡlʌts/
vt.|使…过饱和，过量供应|To supply to excess. |The market was overglutted with low-quality goods.  市场上充斥着劣质商品。| saturate, flood, inundate | deprive, starve, deplete | verb
