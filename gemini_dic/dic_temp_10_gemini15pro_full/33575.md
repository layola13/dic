ciliated:/ˈsɪlieɪtɪd/
adjective
有纤毛的
covered with cilia (short, hairlike structures)
The ciliated cells lining the trachea sweep mucus and debris out of the lungs.  气管内壁的纤毛细胞将粘液和碎屑清除出肺部。

The paramecium is a ciliated protozoan. 草履虫是一种有纤毛的原生动物。

近义词: hairy, fringed
反义词: smooth, glabrous
词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *ciliatus*, past participle of *ciliare* "to furnish with eyelashes," from *cilium* "eyelid, eyelash."
