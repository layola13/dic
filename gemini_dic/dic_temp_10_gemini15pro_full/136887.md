nonsociablenesses:/ˌnɒnsəʊʃəbəlˈnɛsɪz/
noun
不善交际；孤僻；不合群
The state or quality of being nonsociable; unsociability.  The plural form emphasizes multiple instances or degrees of this trait.
His nonsociablenesses made it difficult for him to make friends. 他的不善交际使他难以交友。
Near Synonyms: unsociabilities, introversion, aloofness, standoffishness
Antonyms: sociabilities, gregariousness, extroversion
