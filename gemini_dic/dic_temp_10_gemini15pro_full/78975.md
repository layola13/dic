girly:/ˈɡɜːrli/ /ˈɡɝːli/| adj.|少女的；女孩子气的；娇气的|Characteristic of or suitable for a girl or young woman; feminine; dainty.  Often used with a slightly negative connotation, suggesting excessive femininity or immaturity. |She had a very girly bedroom, decorated in pink and white. 她有一个非常少女的卧室，装饰着粉红色和白色。|Synonyms: feminine, maidenly, ladylike, girlish, dainty, sweet|Antonyms: boyish, masculine, tomboyish|adjective


girly:/ˈɡɜːrli/ /ˈɡɝːli/| n.|少女；女孩子气的女人；爱打扮的女孩|A girl or young woman who is considered girly.  This usage is less common than the adjective form.|She's such a girly, always fussing over her hair and makeup. 她真是个爱打扮的女孩，总是忙着弄头发和化妆。|Synonyms: girl, young woman, lass|Antonyms: tomboy|noun
