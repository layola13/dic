hollo:/ˈhɒləʊ/ (UK) /ˈhɑːloʊ/ (US)| interjection, verb |喂；（大声喊叫以引起注意）|A loud shout or cry to attract attention. |例句：Hollo there! Can you hear me? 喂！你能听见我吗？

例句：He holloed across the field to his friend. 他隔着田野对他的朋友大声喊叫。

近义词：shout, yell, call |反义词: whisper, murmur| interjection

hollo:/ˈhɒləʊ/ (UK) /ˈhɑːloʊ/ (US)| verb |喊叫；呼喊|To shout or cry loudly. |例句：She holloed for help when she saw the smoke. 她看到烟雾时大声呼救。

例句：He holloed at the dog to come back.  他大声喊叫让狗回来。
