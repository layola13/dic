chaparreras:/ˌtʃɑːpəˈrɛrəz/ | n. | 皮裤；护腿；| Leather leggings worn over trousers by cowboys as protection from thorns and brush. |  Example 1: The cowboy wore chaparreras to protect his legs while riding through the dense brush. 牛仔穿着皮裤保护他的腿，以便在茂密的灌木丛中骑行。

Example 2:  Authentic chaparreras are often made from hairy cowhide. 真正的皮裤通常由多毛的牛皮制成。 | 近义词: chaps, leggings | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *chaparreras*, from *chaparro* ("evergreen oak; thicket of evergreen oaks"). Ultimately from Basque *txapar* (“bramble; thornbush”).
