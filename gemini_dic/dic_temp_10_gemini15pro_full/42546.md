countermoved:/ˌkaʊntərˈmuːvd/ | v. | 反击，对抗 | To make an opposing move in response to another move, especially in a game, debate, or conflict. |  The company countermoved by lowering their prices.  公司通过降低价格来反击。| Synonyms: retaliated, responded, reacted | Antonyms: yielded, surrendered | verb

countermove:/ˈkaʊntərˌmuːv/ | n. | 反击，对抗行动 | An opposing move made in response to another move. | His countermove was unexpected and effective. 他的反击出人意料且有效。| Synonyms: retaliation, response, reaction | Antonyms: surrender, concession | noun
