nonviscidly:/nɒnˈvɪskɪdli/ /nɑːnˈvɪskɪdli/
adverb
非粘性地；无粘滞地
In a nonviscous or frictionless manner.  Moving smoothly without resistance.
The fluid flowed nonviscidly through the pipe.  流体在管道中非粘性地流动。
The particles moved nonviscidly through the air.  粒子在空气中无粘滞地运动。
Synonyms: smoothly, effortlessly, frictionlessly
Antonyms: viscously, stickily, resistantly
词性: adverb
