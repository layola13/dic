cynorexia:/ˌsaɪnəˈrɛksiə/ | n. | 食欲过盛；贪食；暴食 | An excessive appetite or a morbid craving for food. It is often associated with emotional or psychological issues. |  Example 1: Her cynorexia led to significant weight gain. 她的食欲过盛导致体重显著增加。

Example 2: The doctor suspected cynorexia might be contributing to the patient's health problems. 医生怀疑食欲过盛可能是导致病人健康问题的原因之一。 | 近义词: bulimia, gluttony, polyphagia, hyperphagia | 反义词: anorexia | 词性: 名词
