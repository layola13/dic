hornito:/ɔːrˈniːtoʊ/ (US), /hɔːrˈniːtəʊ/ (UK)| n.|熔岩丘，熔岩塔|A small, cone-shaped mound of solidified lava formed by the ejection of molten lava through a vent in the crust of a solidified lava flow.|Lava often erupts from hornitos on the surface of a lava flow.  熔岩经常从熔岩流表面的熔岩丘喷发出来。

The scientists observed the formation of a hornito during the volcanic eruption.  科学家们在火山爆发期间观察到了熔岩丘的形成。|近义词：spatter cone, rootless cone|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

hornito:/ɔːrˈniːtoʊ/ (US), /hɔːrˈniːtəʊ/ (UK)| n.|小烤箱，小炉子 (especially in Spanish-speaking regions) |A small oven or stove, especially a portable one.|They used a hornito to bake bread in the outdoor kitchen. 他们在户外厨房用一个小炉子烤面包。

The aroma of roasting chilies filled the air as she cooked them in the hornito. 当她在小烤箱里烤辣椒时，烤辣椒的香味弥漫在空气中。|近义词：oven, stove, kiln|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
