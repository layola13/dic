hyperflexion:/ˌhaɪpərˈflɛkʃən/
noun
过度屈曲;超屈
Excessive bending of a limb or joint.
The gymnast suffered a hyperflexion injury to her knee.  这个体操运动员的膝盖过度屈曲受伤。

近义词:overflexion
反义词:hyperextension

词性:n.

词源:  From hyper- + flexion, from Latin flexio, from flectere "to bend."
