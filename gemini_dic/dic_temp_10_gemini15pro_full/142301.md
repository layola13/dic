orthoconic:/ˌɔːrθəˈkɒnɪk/
adjective
正圆锥的; 直锥形的
Having a circular base with a straight axis perpendicular to it.
The orthoconic projection is a type of map projection. 正圆锥投影是一种地图投影。
These crystals have an orthoconic structure. 这些晶体具有正圆锥结构。
近义词:conical
反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "oblique conical" for contrasting shapes)
词性:adjective
