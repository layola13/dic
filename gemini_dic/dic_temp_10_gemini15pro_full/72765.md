fogie:/ˈfoʊɡi/ | n. | 老古董，守旧的人 | An old-fashioned or conservative person, often seen as resistant to change. |  Example: He's a bit of a fogie and doesn't approve of modern music. 他有点守旧，不赞成现代音乐。

Example: My grandfather's a real fogie when it comes to technology. 我祖父在科技方面真是个老古董。
| 近义词: conservative, traditionalist, old-timer, stick-in-the-mud | 反义词: modern, progressive, radical | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Origin uncertain. Possibly related to "fusty" meaning stale or musty.  There's also a suggestion it might be connected to "fogyish," describing military officers considered too old for active service.
