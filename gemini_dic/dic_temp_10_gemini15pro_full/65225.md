etiquette: /ˈetɪkət/ (US), /ˈetɪket/ (UK) | n. | 礼仪，礼节 | The customary code of polite behavior in society or among members of a particular profession or group. |  It's important to observe proper etiquette at a formal dinner. 在正式晚宴上遵守适当的礼仪是很重要的。| synonym: decorum, protocol, manners | antonym: rudeness, impoliteness | noun

etiquettes: /ˈetɪkəts/ (US), /ˈetɪkets/ (UK) | n. | 礼仪，礼节 (通常用单数形式) |  While less common, "etiquettes" can sometimes refer to different sets of etiquette depending on the context.  | Different cultures have different dining etiquettes. 不同的文化有不同的用餐礼仪。 (更常见的说法是 *Different cultures have different dining etiquette.*) | synonym: decorums, protocols, manners | antonym: rudenesses, impolitenesses | noun
