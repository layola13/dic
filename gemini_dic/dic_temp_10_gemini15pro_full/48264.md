decorousness:/ˈdekərəsnəs/ /dɪˈkɔːrəsnəs/
noun
端庄，合宜，礼貌
The quality of being decorous; propriety.  A display of proper behavior and good taste.
Her decorousness and polite manners impressed everyone at the dinner party. 她的端庄和礼貌的举止给晚宴上的每个人都留下了深刻的印象。
Synonyms: propriety, decency, seemliness, modesty, dignity
Antonyms: impropriety, indecency, unseemliness, immodesty, indignity
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin decorosus (“becoming, proper”), from Latin decus (“ornament, grace, honor”) + -osus (suffix forming adjectives indicating fullness or abundance).
