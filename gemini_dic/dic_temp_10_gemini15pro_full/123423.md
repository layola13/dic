monopodium:/ˌmɒnəˈpəʊdiəm/ /ˌmɑːnəˈpoʊdiəm/ | n. |单轴分枝; 单轴 |A main axis or stem continuing to grow at the apex and producing lateral branches. |The coconut palm exhibits monopodial growth. 椰子树呈现单轴分枝生长。|The spruce tree's monopodial branching gives it a classic conical shape. 云杉的单轴分枝赋予了它经典的圆锥形。| 近义词: main stem, central axis | 反义词: sympodium | 词性: noun
