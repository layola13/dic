pasterned:/ˈpeɪstərnd/| adj.| 模仿的，贴图的，拼贴风格的 | Having the appearance of something made by pasting different pieces together, often in a collage-like manner. | The artwork had a pasterned look, with images and textures layered on top of one another. 这幅艺术作品看起来像拼贴画，图像和纹理层层叠加。 |  collaged, patched, mosaic | original, seamless | adjective
Etymology:  Likely a neologism (newly coined word) derived from "paste" + "-erned" (a suffix suggesting a state or condition). This suggests the act of creating something by pasting. |
