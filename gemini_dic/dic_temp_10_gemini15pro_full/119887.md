militarised:/ˈmɪlɪtəraɪzd/
adjective
军事化的
Equipped for or adapted to warfare; given a military character.
The government militarised the border region after the attack. 政府在袭击后将边境地区军事化。
The island was heavily militarised during the Cold War.  冷战期间，该岛屿被高度军事化。
