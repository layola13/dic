nunatak: /ˈnuːnəˌtæk/ (US), /ˈnʊnətæk/ (UK)| n. |冰原岛峰；冰原岩峰|A peak of rock projecting above a surface of inland ice or snow, especially in Greenland.  An isolated rocky peak or ridge that projects above the surface of a glacier or ice sheet.|例句：The climbers reached the nunatak and set up camp. 登山者到达了冰原岛峰并搭建了营地。|例句：Nunataks offer unique habitats for specialized plants and animals. 冰原岛峰为特殊的动植物提供了独特的栖息地。|近义词：rock outcrop, inselberg|反义词: glacier, ice sheet|词性:noun
