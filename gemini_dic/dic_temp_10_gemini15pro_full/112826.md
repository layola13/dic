lucifer:/ˈluːsɪfər/ | n. | 恶魔，撒旦，启明星 |  The name "Lucifer" comes from the Latin word meaning "light-bringer" and originally referred to the planet Venus when seen in the morning sky. In Christian tradition, it became associated with Satan, the fallen angel. | Lucifer is often depicted as the embodiment of evil.  路西法通常被描绘成邪恶的化身。|  He blamed Lucifer for all the world's evils. 他把世界上所有的罪恶都归咎于路西法。 | synonyms: Satan, the Devil, Beelzebub | antonyms: God, angel | noun
