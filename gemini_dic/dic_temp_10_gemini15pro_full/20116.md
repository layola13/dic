biotypes:/ˈbaɪəʊtaɪps/ | n. | 生物型，生物类型 | A group of organisms sharing a specific genetic constitution and phenotype.  A population of organisms that are genetically identical or very similar, and that share a particular set of characteristics. |  Different biotypes of the same bacteria can have varying levels of antibiotic resistance.  同一细菌的不同生物型可以具有不同水平的抗生素抗性。| synonyms:  phenotype, genotype, strain, variety | antonyms: N/A | noun
