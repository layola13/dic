nonrescue:/nɒnˈrɛskjuː/
adjective
非救援的;不涉及救援的
Not of or relating to rescue; not involving rescue.
例句：The nonrescue team focused on providing medical aid. 非救援队专注于提供医疗援助。

例句：They opted for a nonrescue approach, prioritizing containment. 他们选择了非救援方案，优先考虑控制。

近义词：non-emergency, non-saving
