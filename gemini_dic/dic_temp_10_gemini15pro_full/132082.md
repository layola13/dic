noncredibility: /ˌnɑːnˌkrɛdəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可信性；缺乏可信度 | The noncredibility of the witness's testimony led to the acquittal of the defendant.  证人证词的不可信导致被告被判无罪。| Synonyms: unreliability, implausibility, doubtfulness | Antonyms: credibility, believability, plausibility | noun

Etymology:  From non- + credibility.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "credibility" comes from the Late Latin "credibilitas," meaning "worthiness of belief," from "credibilis" ("worthy of belief").
