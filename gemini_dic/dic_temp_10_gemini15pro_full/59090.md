dyeabilities:/ˌdaɪəˈbɪlətiz/| n. |染色性，可染性|The dyeabilities of different fabrics vary greatly. 不同织物的染色性差异很大。|例句：The dyeabilities of natural fibers are generally better than synthetic ones. 天然纤维的染色性通常比合成纤维好。

例句：The researchers are studying the dyeabilities of this new material. 研究人员正在研究这种新材料的染色性。|近义词：dye uptake, colorfastness|反义词: resistance to dyeing|词性:名词
