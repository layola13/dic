neurofibrillar:/ˌnʊəroʊfɪˈbrɪlər/ /ˌnjʊərəʊfɪˈbrɪlər/
adjective
神经纤维的
Relating to or composed of neurofibrils (threadlike structures found in nerve cells).
Neurofibrillar tangles are a hallmark of Alzheimer's disease.  神经纤维缠结是阿尔茨海默病的一个标志。
The researchers studied the neurofibrillar structure of the brain. 研究人员研究了大脑的神经纤维结构。
Synonyms: nerve fiber, axon
Antonyms: N/A
词性: adjective
