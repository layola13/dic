词源：Gamp comes from the character Mrs. Gamp in Charles Dickens' novel *Martin Chuzzlewit*. Mrs. Gamp was a large, clumsy nurse who always carried a large umbrella.  This led to "gamp" becoming a slang term for an umbrella, and sometimes for a clumsy woman.
