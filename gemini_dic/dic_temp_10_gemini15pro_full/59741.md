ecocide: /ˈiːkəʊsaɪd/ | n. | 生态灭绝，生态灾难 | The destruction of the natural environment, especially when deliberate.  The deliberate destruction of large areas of the natural environment, especially by pollutants. |  The oil spill was an act of ecocide. 石油泄漏是一场生态灾难。|  Ecocide threatens the delicate balance of the rainforest ecosystem. 生态灭绝威胁着雨林生态系统的微妙平衡。 | 近义词: environmental destruction, biocide,  mass destruction | 反义词: conservation, preservation | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From eco- (from Greek oikos 'house, dwelling place', hence 'environment') + -cide (from Latin -cidium, from caedere 'to kill, cut down').
