conglutinant:/kənˈɡluːtɪnənt/
adjective
粘合的；胶凝的
Having the quality of uniting or sticking together.
The conglutinant properties of the glue made it ideal for repairing broken pottery.  这种胶水的粘合特性使其成为修复破碎陶器的理想选择。
Synonyms: adhesive, binding, cementing, cohesive, glutinous, sticky
Antonyms: non-adhesive, separating

conglutinant:/kənˈɡluːtɪnənt/
noun
粘合剂
A substance that unites or sticks things together.
The carpenter used a powerful conglutinant to join the pieces of wood.  木匠用一种强力的粘合剂将木块连接起来。
Synonyms: adhesive, binder, cement, glue
Antonyms: solvent, separator
