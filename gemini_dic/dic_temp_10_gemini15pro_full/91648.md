hyperbrutal:/ˌhaɪpərˈbruːtəl/
adjective
极端残暴的;超级野蛮的
excessively brutal; extremely savage
The video game depicted hyperbrutal violence.  这个电子游戏描绘了极端残暴的暴力。
The dictator's regime was known for its hyperbrutal suppression of dissent.  这个独裁者的政权以其极端残暴地压制异见而闻名。
近义词: savage, barbaric, ferocious, ruthless, vicious
反义词: humane, compassionate, merciful, gentle
词性: adjective

Etymology:
hyper- (prefix): from Greek hyper "over, beyond"
brutal: from Late Latin brutalis "of beasts," from Latin brutus "dull, stupid, irrational, beastly"
