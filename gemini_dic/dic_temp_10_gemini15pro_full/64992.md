estrade:/ɛˈstrɑːd/ | n. | 舞台；讲台；高台 | A small raised platform, especially one used for speeches or performances. | The conductor took his place on the estrade and raised his baton. 指挥走上指挥台，举起了指挥棒。 | The president delivered his speech from the estrade. 总统在讲台上发表了讲话。 | 近义词：platform, stage, podium, dais | 反义词：floor, ground | 词性: noun

词源: From French estrade, from Italian estrada "paved road," from Latin strata "paved way."
