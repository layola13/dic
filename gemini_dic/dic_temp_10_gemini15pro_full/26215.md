butterfat:/ˈbʌtərˌfæt/| n.|乳脂，奶油脂肪|The butterfat content of milk varies from cow to cow. 牛奶的乳脂含量因奶牛而异。|例句：Cream is rich in butterfat. 奶油富含乳脂。

The ice cream was made with high-quality butterfat.  这种冰淇淋是用优质乳脂制成的。

|近义词：milkfat, cream fat|反义词:skim milk (脱脂牛奶 - 指的是成分, 不是真正意义上的反义词)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From butter + fat.  Butter itself comes from Old English butere, from Latin butyrum, from Greek boutyron. Fat comes from Old English fætt.
