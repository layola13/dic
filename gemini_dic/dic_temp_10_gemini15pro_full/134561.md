nonlitigious:/ˌnɒnlɪˈtɪdʒəs/ | adj. | 非诉讼的，避免诉讼的 | Not inclined to engage in lawsuits; avoiding litigation. |  The company prefers nonlitigious methods of resolving disputes.  这家公司更喜欢非诉讼方式解决争端。|  Example: They sought a nonlitigious solution to the contract dispute. 他们寻求一种非诉讼的合同纠纷解决方案。| Synonyms: amicable, conciliatory, peaceable | Antonyms: litigious, contentious, quarrelsome | adjective
