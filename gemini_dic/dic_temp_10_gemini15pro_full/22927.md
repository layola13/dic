botswanan:/ˌbɒtsˈwɑːnən/ | adj. n.|博茨瓦纳的；博茨瓦纳人|Relating to Botswana, its people, or their language.  A person from Botswana.|例句：She is a Botswanan citizen. 她是一位博茨瓦纳公民。|例句：The Botswanan landscape is stunning. 博茨瓦纳的风景令人惊叹。|近义词：Motswana (for a person)|反义词:N/A|词性:adjective, noun

Etymology: Derived from "Botswana," the name of the country.  "Botswana" itself comes from "Batswana," the name of the dominant ethnic group, the Tswana people.
