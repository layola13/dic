anopheles:/əˈnɒfəliːz/ | n. |按蚊|A mosquito of the genus Anopheles, certain species of which transmit malaria to humans.|例句：Anopheles mosquitoes are the primary vectors of malaria. 按蚊是疟疾的主要传播媒介。

例句：Controlling the Anopheles population is crucial in preventing malaria outbreaks. 控制按蚊的数量对于预防疟疾爆发至关重要。|近义词：mosquito(泛指蚊子)|反义词: None (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用 predator 捕食者)|词性: noun
