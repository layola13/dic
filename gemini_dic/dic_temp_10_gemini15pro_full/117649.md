megaweber:/ˈmeɡəˌvebər/| n. |兆韦伯|A unit of magnetic flux equal to one million webers.  The magnetic flux through a surface is the surface integral of the normal component of the magnetic field density B over that surface.|The generator produced a magnetic flux of several megawebers.|近义词:  (none - it's a specific unit)|反义词: (none - units of measurement don't typically have opposites)
词性:noun
