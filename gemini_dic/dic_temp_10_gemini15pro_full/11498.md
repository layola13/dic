arid:/ˈærɪd/| adj.|干旱的，贫瘠的|Having little or no rain; too dry or barren to support vegetation.|The desert landscape was arid and desolate.  沙漠的景象荒凉而贫瘠。|例句：The arid climate made it difficult to grow crops. 干旱的气候使得种植庄稼很困难。|近义词：dry, barren, desert, parched|反义词：humid, moist, fertile, lush|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *aridus*, from *arēre* "to be dry, parched, burn."
