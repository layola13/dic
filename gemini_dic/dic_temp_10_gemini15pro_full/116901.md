mayhaps:/meɪˈhæps/| adv.|或许，可能|An archaic or humorous adverb meaning perhaps.|Mayhaps we'll meet again someday. 也许我们有一天会再见。|近义词：perhaps, maybe, perchance|反义词:certainly, definitely|副词
