asphyxiated: /əsˈfɪksieɪtɪd/ | adj. | 窒息的，闷死的 |  Deprived of oxygen, causing unconsciousness or death.  Suffocated. | The fire victims were asphyxiated by the smoke. 火灾受害者被烟熏窒息而死。 | 近义词: suffocated, choked, smothered | 反义词: resuscitated, revived | 词性: 形容词

asphyxiate: /əsˈfɪksieɪt/ | vt. | 使窒息，使闷死 | To cause someone to become unconscious or die by depriving them of oxygen. | The fumes from the car exhaust asphyxiated the driver. 汽车尾气使司机窒息。| 近义词: suffocate, choke, smother | 反义词: resuscitate, revive | 词性: 动词


词源: From French asphyxié, past participle of asphyxier, from Greek asphuktos "pulseless," from a- "without" + sphuktos "pulse."
