ctrl:/kənˈtrəʊl/ (UK) /kənˈtroʊl/ (US)| n.  |控制; (键盘上的)控制键|A key on a computer keyboard that you press together with another key to perform a particular function.  |Press Ctrl+Alt+Delete to restart the computer.|近义词：command, key|反义词: release, lose control |noun


ctrl:/kənˈtrəʊl/ (UK) /kənˈtroʊl/ (US)| vt.  |控制; 支配; 管理|Determine the behavior or supervise the running of.|She struggled to control her temper.|近义词：manage, regulate, govern|反义词: release, lose control |verb
