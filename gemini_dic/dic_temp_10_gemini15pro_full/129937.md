non-lucid:/nɒnˈluːsɪd/ /nɑːnˈluːsɪd/| adj.|不清醒的，神志不清的|Not clear or lucid; lacking clarity and coherence of thought or expression.|The patient's speech was non-lucid, making it difficult to understand what he was trying to communicate. 病人的言语含糊不清，很难理解他想表达的意思。|The elderly man experienced non-lucid intervals during his illness.  这位老人在生病期间经历了神志不清的间歇。|Synonyms: unclear, confused, incoherent, unintelligible, disoriented, delirious|Antonyms: lucid, clear, coherent, intelligible, rational|adjective
