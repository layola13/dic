elocutionally:/ˌɛləˈkjuːʃənəli/
adverb
雄辩地；演说技巧娴熟地
In a manner characterized by fluent and effective public speaking.
He spoke elocutionally, captivating the audience with his clear and persuasive delivery. 他雄辩地演讲，清晰而有说服力的表达深深吸引了观众。
She delivered her lines elocutionally, demonstrating excellent stage presence. 她以娴熟的演讲技巧完成了台词，展现了出色的舞台表现力。
近义词: articulately, eloquently, fluently
反义词: inarticulately, haltingly, poorly
