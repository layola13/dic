depopulative:/diːˌpɒpjʊˈleɪtɪv/
adjective
减少人口的
Tending or designed to reduce population.  Relating to depopulation.
The Black Death had a depopulative effect on Europe. 黑死病对欧洲的人口造成了减少的影响。
The war had a depopulative impact on the region. 战争对该地区的人口造成了减少的影响。
近义词:depleting, diminishing
反义词:populative, increasing
词性:adjective

depopulation:/diːˌpɒpjʊˈleɪʃən/
noun
人口减少
A reduction in population.
The depopulation of rural areas is a serious problem. 农村地区人口减少是一个严重的问题。
The island suffered from depopulation after the volcano erupted.  火山爆发后，该岛遭受了人口减少的困境。
近义词:decline, decrease
反义词:population growth, increase
词性:noun


Etymology:
From Late Latin *dēpopulātīvus, from Latin dēpopulātus (past participle of dēpopulārī ‘to depopulate’), from dē- ‘completely’ + populārī ‘to ravage, lay waste’, from populus ‘people’.
