nondisingenuously:/ˌnɒndɪsɪnˈdʒɛnjuəsli/ | adv. | 不虚伪地，真诚地 |  In a way that is not disingenuous; frankly, sincerely, honestly. | He nondisingenuously admitted his mistakes. 他真诚地承认了自己的错误。| 近义词: frankly, sincerely, honestly, candidly | 反义词: disingenuously, deceptively, falsely | 词性: 副词

词源:
nondisingenuously 来自于 disingenuous，在其前面加上否定前缀 non- 构成。
disingenuous 本身来自 dis- (not) + ingenuous (innocent, frank).
ingenuous 最终来自拉丁语 ingenuus (native, freeborn, noble, frank).
