distinct-from:/dɪˈstɪŋkt frəm/| adj.|截然不同的，有区别的|Clearly separate and different from something else.  Indicates a noticeable difference or separateness.|例句：His voice is distinct from his brother's. 他的声音和他哥哥的截然不同。|例句：The two species are genetically distinct from each other. 这两个物种在基因上彼此截然不同。|近义词：different, separate, discrete, dissimilar|反义词：similar, alike, same|词性:形容词
