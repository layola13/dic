holstered:/ˈhɒlstəd/ (UK) /ˈhoʊlstərd/ (US)| v. |放入枪套；收起|To have put (a pistol, etc.) into a holster; to have sheathed or encased.|The cowboy holstered his gun after the duel. 牛仔在决斗后把枪放回枪套。|Synonyms: sheathed, encased, put away|Antonyms: drawn, unsheathed|Verb (past participle)

holster:/ˈhɒlstə(r)/ (UK) /ˈhoʊlstər/ (US)| n. |枪套|A leather case for a pistol, attached to a belt or worn under the arm.|He reached for the holster at his hip. 他伸手去拿腰间的枪套。|Synonyms: sheath, case|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
