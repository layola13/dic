nonmaterialistic:/ˌnɒnməˌtɪəriəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非物质主义的；不注重物质的 | Not focused on material possessions or wealth; valuing spiritual or intellectual pursuits over material things. |  She lives a nonmaterialistic lifestyle, finding joy in experiences rather than possessions. 她过着非物质主义的生活方式，从体验而不是拥有中寻找快乐。

| Example: He's a nonmaterialistic person, more interested in experiences than possessions. 他是一个不注重物质的人，对体验比拥有更感兴趣。| Synonym: spiritual, ascetic, simple, unworldly | Antonym: materialistic, consumerist, greedy, acquisitive | 词性: 形容词
