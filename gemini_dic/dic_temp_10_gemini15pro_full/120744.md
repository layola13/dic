misanthropes:/ˌmɪsənˈθrəʊps/ | n. | 厌世者；遁世者 | A person who dislikes humankind and avoids human society. |  He was a bitter misanthrope who lived alone in the woods. 他是一个愤世嫉俗的厌世者，独自住在树林里。
