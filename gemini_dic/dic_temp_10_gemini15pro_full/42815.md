courageousness:/kəˈreɪdʒəsnəs/| n.|勇敢, 大胆, 勇气|The quality of being courageous; bravery.  It describes a state of possessing and demonstrating courage.|She showed great courageousness in facing her fears. 她面对恐惧表现出了极大的勇气。|Synonyms: bravery, valor, boldness, daring, intrepidity|Antonyms: cowardice, fearfulness, timidity|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From courageous + -ness.  Courageous comes from Middle English corageous, from Old French corageus, from corage "heart, spirit, courage," from Vulgar Latin *coraticum, from Latin cor "heart."
