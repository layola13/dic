epistolet: /ɪˈpɪstəl/ | n. | 书信，信函 | A letter, especially a formal or literary one. | Example 1: He penned a lengthy epistlet to his friend overseas. 他给远在海外的朋友写了一封长信。

Example 2: The apostle Paul's epistlets are important texts in Christian theology. 使徒保罗的书信是基督教神学中的重要文献。 | Synonyms: letter, missive, communication, correspondence | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider: conversation, speech, phone call) | noun
