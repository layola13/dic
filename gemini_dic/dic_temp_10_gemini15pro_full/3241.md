affidavit:/ˌæfɪˈdeɪvɪt/| n.|宣誓书， affidavit |A written statement confirmed by oath or affirmation, for use as evidence in court. |例句：The witness signed an affidavit confirming what she had seen.  证人签署了一份宣誓书，确认她所看到的情况。

例句：The lawyer presented several affidavits to the court. 律师向法庭提交了几份宣誓书。


近义词：declaration, oath, testimony, affirmation|反义词: denial, refutation |词性:noun
