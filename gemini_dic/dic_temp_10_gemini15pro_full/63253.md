entertainable:/ˌentəˈteɪnəbl/ | adj. | 有趣的，娱乐的 | Providing amusement or enjoyment; capable of holding one's attention agreeably. | The movie was highly entertaining. 这部电影非常有趣。|近义词：amusing, enjoyable, diverting, engaging, interesting |反义词：boring, tedious, dull, uninteresting |词性:形容词

Etymology:
From late Middle English *enterteynable*, from Anglo-French *entertenable*, from *entretenir* "to hold between, maintain," from *entre-* "between" + *tenir* "to hold." The sense development in English followed that of "entertain."
