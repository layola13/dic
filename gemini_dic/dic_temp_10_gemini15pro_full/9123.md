antimusical:/ˌæntiˈmjuːzɪkl/
adjective
反对音乐的；不懂音乐的；缺乏音乐感的
opposed to or lacking appreciation of music; unmusical.
His brother is quite antimusial, he prefers the sound of machinery.  他的兄弟对音乐很反感，他更喜欢机器的声音。
Her father is so antimusial, he can't even tell the difference between a violin and a viola. 她的父亲对音乐一窍不通，他甚至分不清小提琴和中提琴。
近义词:unmusical, tone-deaf, unmelodious
反义词:musical, melodic, harmonious
词性:adjective

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to  (from Greek)
musical (adjective): relating to music (from Latin "musicus")
