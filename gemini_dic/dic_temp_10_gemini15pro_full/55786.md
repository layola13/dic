divertedly:/daɪˈvɜːrtɪdli/
| adv. |心不在焉地，转而注意其他事情地|in a way that shows that your attention is not fully on what you are doing|She listened divertedly to the conversation, her mind elsewhere. 她心不在焉地听着谈话，心思飘到了别处。|Example: He tapped his pen divertedly on the desk, thinking about the upcoming holiday.  他漫不经心地用笔敲着桌子，想着即将到来的假期。|Synonyms: absentmindedly, distractedly, inattentively|Antonyms: attentively, carefully, intently|词性:副词

diverted:/daɪˈvɜːrtɪd/
| adj. |转移的，被分散注意力的|having had one's attention diverted|The diverted river now flowed into a reservoir. 改道的河流现在流入了一个水库。|Example: Her diverted attention caused her to miss the crucial moment in the film. 她分散的注意力使她错过了电影中的关键时刻。|Synonyms: distracted, redirected|Antonyms: focused, concentrated|词性:形容词

词源: From the verb "divert," from Late Latin *divertere, from Latin divertere "to turn aside," from dis- "aside" + vertere "to turn."
