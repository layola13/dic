electrogenic:/ɪˌlɛktroʊˈdʒɛnɪk/
adjective
产电的; 生电的
Producing a change in the electrical potential of a cell.  Relating to the generation of an electric field or current.
The sodium-potassium pump is electrogenic, contributing to the negative membrane potential.  钠钾泵是产电的，有助于形成负膜电位。
synonyms: electromotive
antonyms: None specifically.  Perhaps "electroneutral" could be considered a contextual antonym.
词性: 形容词
