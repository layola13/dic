jetting:/ˈdʒɛtɪŋ/
n.|喷射，喷出；|The jetting of water from the fountain was mesmerizing. 喷泉喷出的水流令人着迷。|近义词：spouting, spraying, gushing|反义词：dripping, trickling
v.|喷射，喷出；快速移动；|The firefighters were jetting water onto the burning building. 消防员正用水喷射燃烧的建筑物。|近义词：spouting, spraying, gushing|反义词：dripping, trickling

词源: From the verb "jet," which comes from the Old French "jetter" (to throw).
