antisacerdotal:/ˌæntiˌsæsərˈdoʊtl/
adjective
反僧权主义的；反对僧侣统治的
Opposed to the power or influence of priests or the priesthood.
The writer's antisacerdotal views were evident in his satirical portrayal of the clergy.  这位作家的反僧权主义观点在他对神职人员的讽刺性描写中显露无疑。
The politician's antisacerdotal stance alienated many religious voters. 这位政治家的反僧侣立场疏远了许多宗教选民。

近义词: anticlerical, secular
反义词: pro-clerical, sacerdotal
词性: adjective
