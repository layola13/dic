adapting:/əˈdæptɪŋ/
v.|适应; 改编|The process of changing to suit different conditions or uses.  It can refer to both physical and behavioral changes.|例句：They are adapting to the new environment. 他们在适应新环境。
The play was adapted from a novel. 这部戏剧是由一部小说改编的。|近义词：adjusting, accommodating, modifying, converting, altering|反义词:resisting, opposing|词性:Verb (present participle)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adaptare* "to fit to," from *ad-* "to" + *aptare* "to fit."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He is adapting well to his new job. = 他很好地适应了他的新工作。
She adapts quickly to new situations. = 她能很快适应新环境。

一般过去时:
They adapted the novel into a screenplay. = 他们把这部小说改编成了电影剧本。
The company adapted its marketing strategy to the changing market. = 公司根据不断变化的市场调整了营销策略。

现在完成时:
The animals have adapted to the harsh conditions of the desert. = 动物们已经适应了沙漠的恶劣环境。
We have adapted the software to meet the needs of our clients. = 我们已经调整了软件以满足客户的需求。


过去完成时:
By the time they arrived, the refugees had adapted to their new lives. = 到他们到达时，难民们已经适应了他们的新生活。
He had adapted the script several times before the director was satisfied. = 在导演满意之前，他已经改编了剧本好几次。


将来时:
We will adapt our plans as needed. = 我们将根据需要调整我们的计划。
They will adapt the building for use as a school. = 他们将把这栋建筑改建成学校。


虚拟语气:
If I were you, I would adapt to the new rules quickly. = 如果我是你，我会很快适应新规则。
I wish they would adapt their behavior to the situation. = 我希望他们能根据情况调整自己的行为。


被动语态:
The novel is being adapted for the screen. = 这部小说正在被改编成电影。
The house was adapted to accommodate a growing family. = 这栋房子经过改造以容纳一个日益增长的家庭。


疑问句:
Are you adapting well to the new climate? = 你适应新气候了吗？
How did they adapt the play for a modern audience? = 他们是如何将这部戏剧改编给现代观众的？


否定句:
They did not adapt the book accurately. = 他们没有准确地改编这本书。
He is not adapting well to city life. = 他不适应城市生活。


PHRASE->
adapt to = 适应
adapt from = 从...改编
well adapted = 很好地适应
adapt for = 为...改编
adapt oneself to = 使自己适应
