bugle: /ˈbjʊɡəl/ (US) /ˈbjuːɡəl/ (UK)| n. v.| 军号，吹号|A brass instrument like a small trumpet, typically without valves or keys, used for military signaling.|The bugle sounded, signaling the start of the day. 军号响起，标志着一天的开始。|He bugled the troops to advance. 他吹号命令部队前进。|近义词：trumpet, horn|反义词: (无)|词性: 名词, 动词
