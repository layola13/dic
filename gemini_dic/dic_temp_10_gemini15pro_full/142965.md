outawe:/ɔːˈtɔː/ | vt. | 使敬畏；使惊叹 | to inspire awe in; to fill with profound respect mixed with fear or wonder | The vastness of the Grand Canyon outawed us.  大峡谷的广阔让我们敬畏。| Synonyms: amaze, astound, awe, impress | Antonyms: bore, disappoint, tire | Verb

outawed:/ɔːˈtɔːd/ | adj. | 充满敬畏的；感到惊叹的 | filled with awe; deeply impressed | We were outawed by the beauty of the Northern Lights. 我们被北极光的美景深深震撼。| Synonyms: amazed, astonished, awed, impressed | Antonyms: bored, unimpressed | Adjective
