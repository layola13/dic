bandlet:/ˈbændlɪt/| n. |小带；细带；窄带|A small band or strip, often of fabric or metal.  A small, narrow band or ribbon. |The dress was adorned with delicate silk bandlets. 这件连衣裙装饰着精致的丝绸细带。|Synonyms: ribbon, strip, strap, band, fillet|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but consider: expanse, sheet, bulk)|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *bandelette*, diminutive of *bandelle*, diminutive of *bande* ("band"), from Frankish *binda* ("band, tie"). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bindō* ("band, tie").
