indiscreetly:/ˌɪndɪˈskriːtli/
adverb
不慎地，轻率地
in a way that is not careful about what you say or do, and that is likely to offend people or reveal something secret
He talked indiscreetly about his colleagues. 他轻率地谈论了他的同事。
近义词: carelessly, thoughtlessly, unwisely, imprudently
反义词: discreetly, cautiously, prudently
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From in- (not) + discreet (careful and prudent in one's speech or actions, especially in order to keep something confidential or to avoid embarrassment) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  "Discreet" ultimately comes from the Latin "discernere" (to separate, distinguish).
