disfaith:/dɪsˈfeɪθ/
n.
lack of faith; distrust
Lack of faith or trust; unfaithfulness.  Disfaith can refer to religious doubt or skepticism, or more generally to a breach of trust or loyalty.
His disfaith in the project was apparent. 他对这个项目的缺乏信心显而易见。
近义词: distrust, mistrust, skepticism, unbelief, infidelity
反义词: faith, trust, belief, loyalty, fidelity
词性: 名词

词源:  From dis- "expressing negation" + faith.  The prefix "dis-" dates back to Old French and ultimately Latin, while "faith" comes from Old French "fei," ultimately from Latin "fides."
