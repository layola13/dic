gnarliest:/ˈnɑːrliɪst/| adj.|最扭曲的，最粗糙的，最棘手的，最棒的（俚语）|Most gnarled, rough, difficult, or awesome (slang).  It's used to describe something extreme, impressive, or challenging, especially in the context of surfing, skateboarding, or other extreme sports.  Originally referred to the knotted and twisted appearance of tree branches.|例句：That wave was the gnarliest I've ever seen. 那个浪是我见过最猛的。

例句：He landed the gnarliest trick on his skateboard. 他在滑板上完成了最牛的技巧。


近义词：awesome, amazing, incredible, radical, extreme, challenging, difficult, tough|反义词:easy, simple, straightforward|词性:adjective
