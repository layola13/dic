nemertean:/nɪˈmɜːrtiən/ | adj. n. |  纽形动物的；纽形动物 | Relating to or denoting the nemerteans, a phylum of invertebrate worms, also called ribbon worms or proboscis worms. |  Nemertean worms are found in marine, freshwater, and terrestrial environments. 纽形虫存在于海洋、淡水和陆地环境中。 |  The nemertean stretched its proboscis to capture its prey.  纽形虫伸出吻管捕捉猎物。 | 近义词:ribbon worm, proboscis worm | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From New Latin *Nemertea, from Greek Νημερτής (Nēmērtēs), a sea nymph.
