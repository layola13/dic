choice:/tʃɔɪs/| n. adj.|选择，挑选；选择权；供选择的品种；入选者；精华|An act of selecting or making a decision when faced with two or more possibilities.  Something that one has chosen or may choose. Of very fine quality.|例句：She had to make a difficult choice between her career and her family. 她不得不在事业和家庭之间做出艰难的选择。|近义词：option, alternative, selection, preference|反义词:obligation, necessity|词性:noun, adjective
