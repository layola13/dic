overprominences:/ˌoʊvərˈprɒmɪnənsɪz/ | n. | 过分突出；过度强调；（复数）|  A state of being too noticeable or emphasized, often referring to physical features or aspects of a person's character or behavior.  | The overprominences of his cheekbones made him look gaunt. 他的颧骨过于突出，使他看起来很憔悴。| synonyms:  projections, protuberances, bulges, swellings | antonyms:  recessions, depressions, hollows, concavities | noun

Etymology: "over" (Old English *ofer) + "prominence" (from Latin prominentia "a jutting out," from prominere "to jut out")
