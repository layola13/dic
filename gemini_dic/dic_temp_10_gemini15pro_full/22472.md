bookmark: /ˈbʊkmɑːrk/ (US) /ˈbʊkmɑːk/ (UK) | n. | 书签；网页书签 | A marker placed between the pages of a book to mark a particular place.  A saved link to a website. | I use a bookmark to keep my place in the book. 我用书签来标记我在书中的位置。 |  I saved the website as a bookmark for future reference. 我把这个网站保存为书签，以便将来参考。 | synonym: placeholder, favorite, marker | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "unmarked page") | noun

bookmark: /ˈbʊkmɑːrk/ (US) /ˈbʊkmɑːk/ (UK) | vt. |  把…加为书签；标记 | To mark something for later reference, especially a website or a location in a document. | I bookmarked several articles on the topic. 我把几篇关于这个主题的文章加了书签。| I bookmarked that page so I can find it again easily. 我给那个页面加了书签，这样我就能很容易地再次找到它。 | synonym:  save, favorite, flag | antonym: (No direct antonym) | verb
