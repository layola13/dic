conterminate:/kənˈtɜːrmɪneɪt/ /kɑːnˈtɜːrmɪneɪt/
verb
使有共同边界；使同时结束
To have a common boundary; to end at the same time.
The new fence conterminates the two properties. 新的栅栏使两处房产有了共同的边界。
The treaty conterminated the war.  该条约结束了战争。
近义词: delimit, bound, define
反义词: separate, divide
词性: verb
