countryes:/ˈkʌntriz/ | n. | countries (拼写错误) | An archaic plural of "country," meaning a nation or area of land.  Modern spelling is "countries." |  In olden times, men spoke of faraway countryes. (古时候，人们谈论遥远的国家。) | 近义词: lands, nations, territories, states | 反义词: N/A | noun

Etymology: From Old French *cuntrée*, from Vulgar Latin *(terra) contrata* ("(land) lying opposite"), from Latin *contra* ("against, opposite"). The archaic plural form "-es" was common in Middle English but has since been standardized to "-ies."
