lugubriousness:/luːˈɡuːbriəsnəs/
|n.|悲哀；忧郁；阴沉|The lugubriousness of the funeral was palpable. 葬礼上的悲哀是显而易见的。|Etymology: From lugubrious + -ness. Lugubrious comes from Latin lūgubris "mournful,"  from lūgēre "to mourn."|例句：The lugubriousness of the music matched her mood.  这音乐的忧郁和她此刻的心情很相配。

The pervading lugubriousness of the abandoned house was unsettling. 这栋废弃房屋弥漫的阴森气氛令人不安。|近义词：gloom, melancholy, sadness, mournfulness, somberness|反义词：cheerfulness, happiness, joy, lightheartedness|词性: noun
