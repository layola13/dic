nonfact:/nɑːnˈfækt/
n.|非事实; 虚构的事物|Something that is not a fact; a falsehood.|The story he told was a complete nonfact.|近义词：falsehood, fiction, fabrication, untruth, lie|反义词:fact, truth, reality|词性:名词

词源:  "non-" (not) + "fact".  A relatively modern word, formed by adding the prefix "non-" to "fact".
