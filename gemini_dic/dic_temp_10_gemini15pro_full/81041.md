graffer:/ˈɡræfər/ | n. | 嫁接者; 贪污受贿者; | A person who grafts plants or, informally, one who takes bribes or engages in corrupt practices. | 例句：The old graffer skillfully joined the two branches.  老练的嫁接者熟练地将两根树枝嫁接在一起。

例句：The newspaper exposed the graffer and his illicit dealings.  报纸曝光了那个贪污受贿者及其非法交易。 | 近义词：bribe-taker, corrupt official, grafter | 反义词：honest official | 词性：名词
