conspicuity:/kənˌspɪkˈjuːɪti/ | n. | 显眼；显著；引人注目 | The state or quality of being easily seen or noticed; prominence. |  The brightly colored flowers ensured their conspicuity in the garden.  这些颜色鲜艳的花朵确保它们在花园里很显眼。 |  prominence, noticeability, visibility, salience | obscurity, inconspicuousness | noun

conspicuous:/kənˈspɪkjuəs/ | adj. | 显眼的；明显的；引人注目的 | Standing out so as to be clearly visible; attracting notice or attention. | He was conspicuous by his absence. 他的缺席很引人注目。 |  prominent, noticeable, obvious, striking | inconspicuous, unnoticeable, subtle | adjective
