nonplasticities:/ˌnɑːnplæˈstɪsətiz/ | n. | 非塑性，非可塑性 |  Lack of nonplasticities can lead to structural failure. 缺乏非塑性会导致结构失效。| 例句：The nonplasticities of the alloy contribute to its durability.  这种合金的非塑性有助于其耐久性。| 近义词：rigidity, stiffness, inflexibility | 反义词：plasticity, malleability, flexibility | 词性: 名词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "plasticity" (from Greek "plastikos" meaning "moldable").  "non-"（前缀，意思是“不”）+ "plasticity"（来自希腊语“plastikos”，意思是“可塑的”）。
