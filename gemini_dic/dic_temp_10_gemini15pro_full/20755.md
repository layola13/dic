blandest:/ˈblændɪst/ | adj. | 最温和的；最淡而无味的；最乏味的 |  Superlative form of bland: most bland. Lacking strong features or characteristics and therefore uninteresting. | The blandest soup I've ever tasted had absolutely no flavor. 我尝过的最淡而无味的汤一点味道都没有。|  The hospital food was the blandest I’d ever encountered.  医院的食物是我吃过最清淡的。 | insipid, tasteless, mild | flavorful, spicy, interesting | adjective
