bookman:/ˈbʊkmən/| n.|书商；书呆子；爱书人|A person who sells or deals in books; a scholarly or bookish person; a lover of books.|He was a true bookman, with shelves overflowing with volumes on every subject. 他是一位真正的爱书人，书架上摆满了各种主题的书籍。|近义词：bibliophile, scholar, book lover, bookseller|反义词：illiterate, philistine|noun
