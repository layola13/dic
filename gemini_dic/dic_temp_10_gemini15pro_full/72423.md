fluffy:/ˈflʌfi/| adj.|毛茸茸的，蓬松的|Covered with soft fur or feathers; light and airy.|The kitten was small and fluffy. 小猫又小又毛茸茸的。|例句：The cake was light and fluffy. 蛋糕松软可口。|近义词：downy, soft, feathery|反义词:smooth, flat, hard|词性:形容词
词源:From fluff + -y.  Fluff itself comes from Middle English *fluffen, of imitative origin.
