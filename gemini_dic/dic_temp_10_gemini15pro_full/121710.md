misprincipled:/ˌmɪsˈprɪnsəpəld/
adjective
不道德的，无原则的
lacking moral principles; based on wrong or dishonest principles
例句：His misprincipled actions led to his downfall. 他的不道德行为导致了他的垮台。

例句：The misprincipled CEO embezzled millions from the company.  这位不道德的CEO从公司盗用了数百万美元。


近义词：unscrupulous, unethical, immoral, dishonest, corrupt
反义词：principled, ethical, moral, honest, upright
词性：adjective
