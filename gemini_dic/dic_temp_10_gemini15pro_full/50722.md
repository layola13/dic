derogant: /ˈdɛrəɡənt/ | adj. | 贬低的，贬损的 | Showing a critical or disrespectful attitude; belittling. |  His derogant remarks about her work made her feel discouraged. 他对她工作的贬低性言论让她感到沮丧。|  He made some derogant comments about his colleagues. 他对他的同事发表了一些贬损的评论。| 近义词：disparaging, belittling, demeaning, derogatory | 反义词：complimentary, flattering, praising | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Late Latin *derogantem, present participle of derogare “to repeal (a law), detract from,” from de- “down, away” + rogare “to ask, propose a law."
