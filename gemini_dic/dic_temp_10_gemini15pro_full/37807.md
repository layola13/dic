compendiously:/kəmˈpɛndiəsli/| adv.|简要地，简明地|In a brief and comprehensive manner; concisely.|He compendiously summarized the main points of the meeting. 他简要地总结了会议的要点。|Synonyms: concisely, briefly, succinctly, summarily|Antonyms: verbosely, lengthily, prolixly|adverb

Etymology->
From Late Latin *compendiōsus*, from Latin compendium (“saving, abridgment”) + -ōsus.
