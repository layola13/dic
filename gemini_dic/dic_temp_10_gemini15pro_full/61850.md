employer:/ɪmˈplɔɪər/ | n. |雇主；老板|A person or organization that employs people. |例句：My employer offers excellent benefits. 我的雇主提供优厚的福利。

例句：She was looking for a new employer closer to home. 她正在寻找一个离家更近的新雇主。


近义词：boss, manager, hirer |反义词：employee, worker |词性：noun
