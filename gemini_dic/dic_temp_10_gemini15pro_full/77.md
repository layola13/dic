ETYMOLOGY->
From Hawaiian ʻaʻā, meaning "stony rough lava".

USAGE->
This word is primarily used in geological contexts to describe a specific type of lava flow.

Example 1: The aaraa flow advanced slowly but relentlessly. =  熔岩缓缓地却无情地向前推进。

Example 2:  Geologists studied the aaraa formation to understand the volcano's eruption history. = 地质学家研究了熔岩的形成过程，以了解这座火山的喷发历史。

Not commonly used in phrases.  It is generally used as a standalone noun.
