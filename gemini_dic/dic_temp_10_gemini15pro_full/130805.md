nonanticipation:/nɒnˌæntɪsɪˈpeɪʃn/
|n.|非预期，不预料|The principle of nonanticipation means decisions should be based on current information, not future predictions.|非预期原则意味着决策应该基于当前信息，而不是未来的预测。|
|n.|非预见|Nonanticipation of market changes led to significant losses.|对市场变化的非预见导致了重大损失。|
|近义词:|foresight, prediction, expectation|
|反义词:|surprise, unexpectedness, unforeseen|
|词性:|noun|


词源:
non- (prefix):  meaning "not"
anticipation (noun): from Latin anticipatio, from anticipare "to take beforehand, occupy in advance," from ante "before" + capere "to take."
