collision:/kəˈlɪʒən/| n.|碰撞，冲突，抵触|an instance of one moving object or person striking violently against another.|The car was involved in a collision with a truck. 这辆汽车与一辆卡车相撞。|synonyms: crash, impact, smash|antonyms: avoidance, agreement, harmony|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *collisionem, accusative of collisio (“a striking together”), from Latin collidere (“to strike together”), from com- (“together”) + lædere (“to hurt, strike”).
