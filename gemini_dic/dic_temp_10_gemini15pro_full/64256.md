equipollent:/ˌiːkwɪˈpɒlənt/ /ˌiːkwɪˈpoʊlənt/
adjective
相等的，等价的，等效的
Having equal power, force, or validity.  Equivalent in effect or significance.
例句：The two arguments are equipollent; each has equal force. 这两个论点是等效的；每一个都具有同等效力。

例句：The opposing forces were equipollent, resulting in a stalemate. 对抗的力量相等，导致了僵局。

近义词：equivalent, equal, commensurate, tantamount
反义词：unequal, disparate, dissimilar
词性：adjective

词源：From Latin *aequipollēns*, present participle of *aequipollēre* (“to have equal power”), from aequi- (“equal”) + pollēre (“to be strong”).
