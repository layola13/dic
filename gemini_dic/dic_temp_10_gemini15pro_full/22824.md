bossage:/ˈbɒsɪdʒ/ (US) /ˈbɒsɪdʒ/ (UK)| n.|凸饰；粗面石工；壁柱|A projecting part of a building, especially masonry, such as a rusticated surface or the part of a stone that is left rough and projecting.|The bossage on the castle walls gave it a formidable appearance. 城堡墙壁上的粗面石工使其外观令人生畏。|Synonyms: projection, protrusion, prominence, embossment|Antonyms: indentation, depression, recess|noun
