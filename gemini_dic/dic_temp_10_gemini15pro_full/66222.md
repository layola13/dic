excitement:/ɪkˈsaɪtmənt/| n.|兴奋，激动|A feeling of great enthusiasm and eagerness.|The children were filled with excitement as they opened their presents. 孩子们打开礼物时充满了兴奋。|近义词：thrill, exhilaration, enthusiasm, eagerness|反义词：boredom, apathy, indifference|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from excite + -ment.
