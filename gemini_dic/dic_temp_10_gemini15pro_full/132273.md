nondefaulting:/nɒndɪˈfɔːltɪŋ/ | adj. | 未违约的，未拖欠的 | Not failing to fulfill an obligation, especially the repayment of a loan. |  Example: The nondefaulting borrowers maintained good credit scores. 未违约的借款人保持了良好的信用评分。| Example:  The bank offered lower interest rates to nondefaulting customers. 银行向未违约的客户提供较低的利率。 | 近义词: solvent, creditworthy | 反义词: defaulting, delinquent | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "defaulting" (present participle of "default," from Old French "defaillir" meaning "to fail, be lacking").
