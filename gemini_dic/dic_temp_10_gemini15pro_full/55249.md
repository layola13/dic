dissimulates:/dɪˈsɪmjʊleɪts/
verb
掩饰，假装，装作
To conceal or disguise one's true thoughts, feelings, or intentions.
He dissimulated his anger with a forced smile. 他强颜欢笑，掩饰内心的愤怒。
synonyms: disguise, conceal, mask, feign, pretend
antonyms: reveal, expose, disclose
词性: 动词
