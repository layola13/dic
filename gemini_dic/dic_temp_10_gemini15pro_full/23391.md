brachycephaly: /ˌbrækɪˈsɛfəli/ | n. | 短头畸形 | A condition in which the head is unusually short in the anteroposterior (front to back) diameter. |  Brachycephaly can be a normal variation, but it can also be associated with certain genetic syndromes. 短头畸形可以是一种正常的变异，但也可能与某些遗传综合征有关。 | 近义词: short-headedness | 反义词: dolichocephaly (长头畸形) | 词性: noun

Etymology: From Greek *brakhys* "short" + *kephalē* "head."
