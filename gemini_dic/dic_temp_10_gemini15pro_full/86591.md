heliographer:/hɪˈliːɒɡrəfər/ /hiːˈliːɑːɡrəfər/
n.
日光通信员；日光摄影师；太阳摄影师
A person who operates a heliograph, a device used for sending signals using reflected sunlight.  Also, a person who photographs the sun.
The heliographer signaled the approaching ships. 日光通信员向驶近的船只发出信号。
The heliographer captured stunning images of the solar flares. 太阳摄影师捕捉到了令人惊叹的太阳耀斑图像。
Synonym:signaler, photographer
Antonym:None
noun
