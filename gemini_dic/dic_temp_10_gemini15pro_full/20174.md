biquintile:/baɪˈkwɪntaɪl/| n. |双五分位数，每组占总体20%|A biquintile is one of four values (other than the median) which divide a frequency distribution into five groups, each containing one fifth of the total population.|The income of the second biquintile increased by 10% last year. 去年第二组双五分位数的收入增长了10%。|近义词:quintile|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

词源:
From bi- + quintile, ultimately from Latin *quinti- , ordinal of quinque five.
