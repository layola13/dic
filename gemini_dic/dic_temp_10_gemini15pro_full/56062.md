docile:/ˈdɑːsəl/ /ˈdoʊsaɪl/ | adj. | 温顺的，易驯服的，容易教的 | Easily managed or handled; readily trained or taught; submissive to instruction. | The farmer preferred docile animals that were easy to manage.  这位农民更喜欢容易管理的温顺动物。

The teacher praised the docile student for his willingness to learn. 老师表扬了这个温顺的学生的学习意愿。| 近义词: obedient, compliant, submissive, tame, manageable | 反义词: unruly, unmanageable, defiant, obstinate, rebellious | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Latin *docilis, from docēre "to teach."
