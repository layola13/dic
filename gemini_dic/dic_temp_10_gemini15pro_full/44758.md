crumbs:/krʌmz/
n.
碎屑，面包屑
small fragments of something, especially bread, cake, or crackers.
The child dropped crumbs all over the floor. 孩子把碎屑掉了一地。
synonyms: fragments, bits, pieces, particles
antonyms: whole, complete
noun


crumbs:/krʌmz/
int.
（表示轻微恼怒或惊讶）哎呀，糟糕
used to express mild annoyance or surprise.
"Crumbs! I've forgotten my keys." “哎呀！我忘了带钥匙了。”
synonyms:  drat, blast, shoot
antonyms:  (No direct antonym for this interjection)
interjection


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *cruma (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *krumō (compare West Frisian kromme, Dutch kruim, German Krume), from Proto-Indo-European *(s)ker- (to cut).
