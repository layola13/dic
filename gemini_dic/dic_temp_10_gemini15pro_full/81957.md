grimmer:/ˈɡrɪmər/| adj.|更冷酷的，更严峻的，更令人沮丧的|Comparative form of grim: more grim.  It describes something that has become more serious, severe, or depressing than it was before.|The news from the front lines grew grimmer each day. 前线的消息一天比一天严峻。|The economic forecast is grimmer than it was last month.  经济预测比上个月更悲观了。|synonyms: sterner, harsher, more severe, more serious, more somber, bleaker, darker|antonyms: brighter, more cheerful, more optimistic, rosier, less severe|adjective
