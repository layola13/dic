haematitic:/ˌhiːməˈtɪtɪk/ | adj. | 血红色的，赤铁矿的 | Relating to, resembling, or containing hematite (iron ore). | The rocks were haematitic, stained with the deep red of iron oxide. 岩石呈赤铁矿状，被铁氧化物的深红色所染。| The archaeologist discovered a haematitic axe head. 考古学家发现了一个赤铁矿斧头。 | 近义词：rusty, reddish, iron-rich | 反义词:  non-ferrous | 词性: 形容词

haematic:/hɪˈmætɪk/ | adj. | 血的，与血有关的 | Relating to the blood. |  He suffered from a haematic disorder. 他患有血液疾病。 |  The doctor ordered a haematic test. 医生要求做血液测试。 | 近义词：sanguine, bloody | 反义词: bloodless | 词性: 形容词
