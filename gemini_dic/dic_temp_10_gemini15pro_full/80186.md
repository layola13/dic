godhood:/ˈɡɒdhʊd/ (US) /ˈɡɒdhʊd/ (UK)| n. |神性；神格；神位|The state of being a god; divinity. |He achieved godhood after completing a series of arduous trials. 他在完成了一系列艰巨的考验后获得了神格。|

The ancient Egyptians believed their pharaohs attained godhood upon death. 古埃及人相信他们的法老死后会获得神性。|
近义词：divinity, deity, godship|反义词：mortality, humanity|词性: 名词
