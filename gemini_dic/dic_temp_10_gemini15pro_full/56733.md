doorless:/ˈdɔːrlɪs/
adjective
无门的
lacking a door; without a door.
The old barn was doorless and exposed to the elements.  旧谷仓没有门，暴露在风雨中。
近义词: open, ungated, unobstructed
反义词: doored, gated, enclosed
词性: adjective
