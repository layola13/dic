changeably:/ˈtʃeɪndʒəbli/
adverb
易变地，变化无常地
in a manner that is subject to change or variation
The weather here is changeably, one moment it's sunny, the next it's raining.  这里的天气变化无常，一会儿晴天，一会儿下雨。
Her moods were changeably, shifting from happy to sad in an instant. 她的情绪变化无常，瞬间从高兴转为悲伤。
Synonyms: variably, unpredictably, erratically, inconstantly
Antonyms: consistently, steadily, uniformly, constantly
词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "changeable" + "-ly"
"Changeable" comes from the verb "change" (from Old French "changier") + the suffix "-able" (meaning "capable of being").
