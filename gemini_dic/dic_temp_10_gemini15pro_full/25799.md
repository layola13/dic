bergamot:/ˈbɜːrɡəmɒt/ (UK), /ˈbɜːrɡəˌmɑːt/ (US)| n.|香柠檬，佛手柑|A small citrus tree with fragrant leaves and pear-shaped fruit, or the fruit itself.
