long-haul:/ˈlɔːŋ hɔːl/ (UK) /ˈlɔːŋ hɔːl/ (US)| adj. n.|长途的，长途运输；长途旅程|Traveling a long distance; involving a long distance.|例句：Long-haul flights can be tiring. 长途飞行可能会很累人。|近义词：long-distance, extended|反义词：short-haul, short-distance|词性:形容词, 名词

long-haul:/ˈlɔːŋ hɔːl/ (UK) /ˈlɔːŋ hɔːl/ (US)| n.|长途运输；长途旅程|A long distance journey or the business of transporting goods over long distances.|例句：The company specializes in long-haul trucking. 这家公司专门从事长途货运。|近义词：long-distance travel, long-distance transportation|反义词：short-haul travel, short-distance transportation|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "long" + "haul". "Long" meaning extended in space or time, from Old English "lang," ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*langaz." "Haul" meaning to pull or drag with effort, from Old Norse "hala" to drag, pull.  The combined term signifies a journey or transport requiring a long pull or extended effort.
