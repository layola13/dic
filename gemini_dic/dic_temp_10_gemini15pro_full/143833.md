outperformance:/ˌaʊtpərˈfɔːrməns/| n. |超越；优于；胜过；优异表现|The fund's outperformance has been attributed to its focus on small-cap stocks. 该基金的出色表现归功于其对小型股的关注。|例句：The company's outperformance of its competitors was remarkable.  这家公司胜过其竞争对手的表现非常出色。
