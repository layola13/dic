overemoting:/ˌoʊvərɪˈmoʊtɪŋ/
verb
过度表演；表现得过分夸张
Expressing emotions in an excessive or exaggerated way.
She was overemoting in the scene, and it felt unnatural.  她在那一幕中表演得过分夸张，感觉很不自然。
The actor was criticized for overemoting.  这个演员因表演过火而受到批评。
近义词: overacting, exaggerating
反义词: underexpressing, underplaying
词性: verb (动词)
