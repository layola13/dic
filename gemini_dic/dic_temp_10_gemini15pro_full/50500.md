depressurizing:/diːˈprɛʃəˌraɪzɪŋ/
verb
减压;卸压
The act of reducing pressure, typically in a sealed environment like an aircraft cabin or a pressure cooker.
The pilot announced they were depressurizing the cabin in preparation for landing.  飞行员宣布他们正在降低机舱压力，准备着陆。
The pressure cooker started depressurizing automatically after the timer went off.  定时器响后，压力锅开始自动排气减压。
depressurize, venting, releasing pressure
pressurizing, compressing
verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- + pressurize, from pressure + -ize.  Ultimately from Latin pressura (“pressure”).
