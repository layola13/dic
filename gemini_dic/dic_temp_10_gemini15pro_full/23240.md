bowline:/ˈboʊlɪn/ (US), /ˈbaʊlɪn/ (UK)| n.| 系绳结，单套结 |A knot used to make a fixed loop at the end of a rope, especially in sailing.  It is considered a very secure knot. |Sailors use a bowline to secure the mooring line to the dock. 水手用单套结把系泊绳固定在码头上。| 近义词: loop knot, running bowline (specific variations) | 反义词: N/A | noun

bowlines:/ˈboʊlɪnz/ (US), /ˈbaʊlɪnz/ (UK)| n.| 系绳结（复数） |Plural form of "bowline," referring to multiple instances of the knot. |He tied two bowlines to secure the sails. 他打了两个单套结来固定帆。|近义词:  loop knots| 反义词: N/A | noun
