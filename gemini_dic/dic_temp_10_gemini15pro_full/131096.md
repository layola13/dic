nonbiblical:/ˌnɒnˈbɪblɪkəl/
adj.
非圣经的; 世俗的
Not found in or not derived from the Bible.  Relating to subjects that are not religious.
The professor specialized in nonbiblical literature.  这位教授专门研究非圣经文学。
The museum's collection includes nonbiblical artifacts from the region.  博物馆的藏品包括来自该地区的非圣经文物。
Synonyms: secular, worldly, profane, temporal
Antonyms: biblical, scriptural, sacred, religious
词性: adjective
