inculcatory:/ɪnˈkʌlkətɔːri/
adjective
 inculpatory（责备的，归罪的）
Tending or serving to inculcate; instilling.  Also, incriminating; accusatory.
The prosecutor presented inculcatory evidence against the defendant.  检察官出示了针对被告的罪证。
近义词:incriminating, accusatory, implicating
反义词:exculpatory, absolving, vindicating
词性: adjective

inculcate:/ɪnˈkʌlkeɪt/
verb
谆谆教诲；反复灌输
To impress (something) upon the mind of another by frequent instruction or repetition; instill.
The teacher inculcated in her students the importance of honesty.  老师向学生反复灌输诚实的重要性。
近义词:instill, implant, ingrain
反义词:eradicate, efface, extirpate
词性: verb
