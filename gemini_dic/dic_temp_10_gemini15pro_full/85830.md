hazmat:/ˈhæzmæt/| n.|危险物品；危险材料|A contraction of "hazardous materials," referring to substances that pose a risk to health, safety, property, or the environment.|The truck was carrying hazmat, so the road was closed. 这辆卡车运载着危险物品，所以道路被封闭了。|Synonyms: dangerous goods, hazardous waste, toxic materials|Antonyms: safe materials, non-hazardous materials|noun


hazmat:/ˈhæzmæt/| adj.|危险物品的；危险材料的|Used attributively to describe something related to or containing hazardous materials.|Hazmat crews were called to clean up the spill. 危险物品处理小组被召来清理泄漏物。|Synonyms: dangerous, hazardous, toxic|Antonyms: safe, non-hazardous|adjective
