limpet:/ˈlɪmpɪt/| n.|帽贝；顽固的人；坚持不懈的人|A marine mollusk with a low conical shell, which clings tightly to rocks.  A person who persistently clings to something, such as a job or position.|The limpets clung tightly to the rocks despite the crashing waves. 这些帽贝紧紧地附着在岩石上，尽管海浪拍打着。|近义词：barnacle, clingfish, stick-in-the-mud (figurative)|反义词: rolling stone (figurative)|词性:noun

limpet:/ˈlɪmpɪt/| adj.|坚持不懈的；固执的|Clinging tightly; stubbornly persistent.|He's a real limpet when it comes to his beliefs.  当谈到他的信仰时，他是一个真正顽固的人。|近义词：tenacious, persistent, obstinate|反义词: flexible, adaptable|词性:adjective
