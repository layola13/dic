distil:/dɪˈstɪl/ (US) /dɪˈstɪl/ (UK)| v. n.|蒸馏；提取；提炼|To purify a liquid by heating it until it becomes a gas and then cooling the gas back into a liquid; to extract the essential elements of something.|They distil whisky from malted barley. 他们用麦芽大麦酿造威士忌。|近义词：extract, refine, purify, concentrate|反义词: mix, combine, dilute|词性:动词, 名词

distil:/dɪˈstɪl/ (US) /dɪˈstɪl/ (UK)| n.|蒸馏液；精华|The product of distillation; the essential meaning or message of something.|The distil of his research was published in a scientific journal.  他研究的精华发表在一份科学期刊上。|近义词：essence, concentrate, extract|反义词: mixture, whole|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *distiller*, from Latin *distillare* "to drip down," from *de-* "down" + *stillare* "to drip," from *stilla* "a drop."
