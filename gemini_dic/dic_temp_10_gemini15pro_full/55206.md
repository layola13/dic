dissertations:/ˌdɪsəˈteɪʃənz/
n.
论文；专题论文
A long research paper written for a degree or diploma.
She is writing her dissertation on the history of feminist literature. 她正在写关于女权主义文学史的论文。
thesis, treatise, study, paper, essay
article, blog post
