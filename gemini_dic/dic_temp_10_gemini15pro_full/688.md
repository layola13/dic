abscond:/əbˈskɒnd/ (UK) /æbˈskɑːnd/ (US)| vi.|潜逃，逃遁，(尤指携款) 潜逃|To leave hurriedly and secretly, typically to avoid detection of or arrest for an unlawful action such as theft.|The cashier absconded with the day's takings. 出纳员携当日收入潜逃了。|近义词：flee, escape, decamp, bolt, vanish|反义词: remain, stay, surrender|动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin abscondere, from abs- “away” + condere “to put together, store, hide.”  The original meaning was "to hide oneself," later evolving to imply "to depart secretly and hide, especially to avoid arrest or prosecution."
