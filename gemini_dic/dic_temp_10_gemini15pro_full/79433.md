glisters:/ˈɡlɪstərz/ (US), /ˈɡlɪstəz/ (UK)| v.| 闪耀，闪烁 |To shine with a soft reflected light; sparkle. |The dew glisters on the grass. 露水在草地上闪闪发光。|Synonyms: sparkles, shimmers, glitters|Antonyms: dulls, fades|Verb


glistening:/ˈɡlɪsənɪŋ/ (US), /ˈɡlɪsənɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.| 闪闪发光的，闪亮的 |Shining with a soft reflected light. |The glistening snow covered the mountains. 闪闪发光的雪覆盖了群山。|Synonyms: sparkling, shimmering, glittering|Antonyms: dull, matte|Adjective


词源:
From Middle English *glisteren, *glisnen, from Old English *glisian (attested only in Northumbrian glisnaþ "shines"), from Proto-Germanic *glīsaną (“to shine, glisten”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵleys- (“to shine”). Cognate with Scots glister (“to glitter, glisten”), West Frisian glipse (“to shine, glitter”), Dutch glinsteren (“to glitter, sparkle”), German glimmen (“to glimmer, glow”), Swedish glimma (“to glitter, gleam”).
