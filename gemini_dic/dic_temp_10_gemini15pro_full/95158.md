inarticulated:/ˌɪnɑːrˈtɪkjʊleɪtɪd/
inarticulated:|adj.|表达不清的，不善言辞的|Not expressed clearly or easily understood; unable to express oneself clearly.|His inarticulated rage boiled inside him. 他无法言喻的愤怒在他内心翻腾。|Synonyms: unclear, mumbled, incoherent, inexpressible, unspoken|Antonyms: articulate, clear, eloquent, fluent, expressive
inarticulated:|adj.|未连接的，未接合的|Not jointed or articulated; not having joints.|The skeleton was inarticulated, the bones scattered across the floor.  骸骨未连接，骨头散落在地板上。|Synonyms: unconnected, disjointed, separate|Antonyms: connected, joined, articulated
