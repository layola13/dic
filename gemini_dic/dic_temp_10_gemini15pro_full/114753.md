malpighia: /mælˈpɪɡiə/ | n. |金虎尾属（植物）| A genus of tropical American shrubs or small trees (family Malpighiaceae) having usually showy pink, purplish, or white flowers succeeded by cherrylike red, orange, or yellow drupes.  | Example: The malpighia in the garden is bearing fruit.  花园里的金虎尾属植物正在结果。| Example:  Many species of Malpighia are rich in Vitamin C. 许多金虎尾属物种富含维生素C.| Synonym: acerola (specifically for *Malpighia glabra*, also known as Barbados cherry) | Antonym: N/A (Antonyms not applicable for genus names) | Noun
