nondeviations:/ˌnɒndiːviˈeɪʃənz/
|n.| 不偏离，无偏差 |The report emphasized the nondeviations from the established protocol.  报告强调了没有偏离既定协议。| The system's nondeviations ensured consistent performance. 该系统的无偏差确保了一致的性能。|近义词：conformity, adherence, compliance|反义词：deviations, variations, discrepancies|词性: 名词

词源:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
deviation (noun): from Late Middle English: from late Latin deviatio(n-), from the verb deviare, from de- ‘away’ + via ‘way’.
