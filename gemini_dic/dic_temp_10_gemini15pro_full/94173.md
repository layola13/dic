impennous:/ɪmˈpɛnəs/
adjective
贫困的；身无分文的
Having little or no money.
He was so impecunious that he couldn't even afford a bus ticket. 他如此贫困，甚至连一张公交车票都买不起。
Synonyms:  penniless, indigent, destitute, impoverished
Antonyms: wealthy, affluent, rich, prosperous
词性：形容词
