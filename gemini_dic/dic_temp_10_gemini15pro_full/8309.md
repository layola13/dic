antioxidant:/ˌæntiˈɑːksɪdənt/ |n. adj.|抗氧化剂; 抗氧化的|A substance that inhibits oxidation, especially one used to counteract the deterioration of stored food products.| Example:  This cream contains antioxidants to help protect your skin from sun damage. 这款面霜含有抗氧化剂，有助于保护你的皮肤免受阳光伤害。

Example:  Berries are rich in antioxidants.  浆果富含抗氧化剂。|Synonyms: free radical scavenger, preservative|Antonyms: oxidant, pro-oxidant| Noun, Adjective
