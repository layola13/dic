aeolian's:/iˈoʊliənz/| adj.|风成的，风蚀的|Relating to or arising from the action of the wind; wind-blown.  Formed by the wind.  Aeolian processes involve erosion, transportation, and deposition of sediment by the wind.|The aeolian's harp produced a haunting melody. 风弦琴发出令人难忘的旋律。|The geologist studied the aeolian's landforms of the desert. 地质学家研究了沙漠的风蚀地貌。|近义词:wind-blown, wind-formed|反义词:water-formed, fluvial|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *Aeolius*, from Aeolus, the Greek god of the winds.
