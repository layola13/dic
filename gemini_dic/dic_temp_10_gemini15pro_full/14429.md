autumny: /ˈɔːtəmni/ (US), /ɔːˈtʌmni/ (UK)| adj.|秋天的，似秋的|Characteristic of autumn; resembling autumn.|例句：The autumny leaves fell gently to the ground. 秋天的叶子轻轻地落在地上。

例句：The air had an autumny chill. 空气中弥漫着秋天的寒意。

近义词：autumnal, fall-like (US)|反义词：springlike, summery|词性: adjective
