eel:/iːl/| n.|鳗鱼|A snake-like fish with a smooth, slippery skin.|例句：The eel slithered through the mud. 鳗鱼在泥里滑行。

例句：We ate grilled eel for dinner. 我们晚饭吃了烤鳗鱼。|近义词：moray, conger|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑栖息地或食物链上的对立生物，例如某些捕食鳗鱼的鱼类，但并非严格意义上的反义词)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *æl, from Proto-Germanic *ēlaz, from Proto-Indo-European *el- "brown, yellow."  Related to Old High German āl, German Aal, Dutch aal.
