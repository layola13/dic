extenuate: /ɪkˈstɛnjuˌeɪt/ /ɪkˈstɛn.ju.eɪt/
verb
减轻；为…辩解
to make (a fault, an offense, etc.) seem less serious or more forgivable
Her youth extenuates her crime to some extent. 她的年轻在某种程度上减轻了她的罪行。

His good record extenuates the seriousness of this offense. 他良好的记录减轻了这次违规的严重性。

近义词: mitigate, lessen, diminish, excuse, palliate
反义词: aggravate, worsen, exacerbate


词性: v.

词源:  From Latin extenuatus, past participle of extenuare "to make thin, small, or weak," from ex- "out" + tenuis "thin."
