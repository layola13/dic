majuscule: /məˈdʒʌskjuːl/ /ˈmædʒəskˌjuːl/
adjective
大写的；大写字母的
of or in capital letters.
The title of the book was printed in majuscule letters. 这本书的标题是用大写字母印刷的。
Majuscule script is clear and easy to read. 大写字母清晰易读。
近义词: uppercase, capital
反义词: minuscule, lowercase
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *majusculus “somewhat larger,” diminutive of Latin major “larger, greater” (from PIE root *meg- “great”).
