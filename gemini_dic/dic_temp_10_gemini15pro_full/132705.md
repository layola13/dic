nondistorting:/ˌnɒndɪˈstɔːtɪŋ/ | adj. | 非扭曲的，不失真的 | Not causing distortion; preserving the original form or meaning. |  The audiophile sought a nondistorting amplifier for his high-end speakers. 音响发烧友为他的高端扬声器寻找一款不失真的放大器。| Synonyms: undistorted, unwarped, accurate, faithful | Antonyms: distorting, warping, inaccurate, unfaithful | adjective
