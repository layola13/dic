demi-hag:/ˈdɛmi hæɡ/| n. | 半妖婆，小妖婆 |A lesser hag, often the offspring of a hag and another creature.  A demi-hag possesses some, but not all, of the powers of a full hag. | The adventurers encountered a demi-hag guarding the bridge, its power less formidable than a full hag's.  探险者们遇到一个守桥的小妖婆，它的力量不如一个真正的妖婆强大。| 近义词: hag, witch, sorceress | 反义词: (No direct opposite, perhaps consider "fairy" or "nymph" for a being with generally benevolent magic) | 词性: noun
