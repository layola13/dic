detainee:/ˌdiːteɪˈniː/
n.
被拘留者；扣留者
a person who is being kept in prison, especially for political reasons
例句：The detainee was held without charge for several weeks.  这名被拘留者未经指控就被关押了数周。

例句：The rights of detainees must be respected. 被拘留者的权利必须得到尊重。

近义词：prisoner, captive
反义词：freeman, parolee
词性：noun
