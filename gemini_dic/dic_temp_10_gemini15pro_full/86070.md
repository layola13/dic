hearing:/ˈhɪərɪŋ/ (US), /ˈhɪərɪŋ/ (UK)| n.| 听力；听觉；审讯；听证会 |A hearing is the ability to perceive sounds, or the act of listening to something. It can also refer to a formal session in a court or other official setting. |  The old man's hearing was failing. 老人的听力正在衰退。
The committee held a hearing on the proposed new law. 委员会就拟议的新法律举行了听证会。 | 近义词: audition, listening, trial,  | 反义词: deafness | noun
