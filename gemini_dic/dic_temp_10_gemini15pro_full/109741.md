levering:/ˈliːvərɪŋ/ /ˈlevərɪŋ/
n.
杠杆作用；杠杆比率；举债经营
The act of using leverage. Using debt to finance investments.
The company's high levering made it vulnerable to economic downturns. 该公司的高杠杆率使其容易受到经济衰退的影响。
leverage, gearing
deleveraging
noun
