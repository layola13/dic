magistrate:/ˈmædʒɪstreɪt/| n. |地方法官，治安官|A civil officer or lay judge who administers the law, especially one who conducts a court dealing with minor offenses and holds preliminary hearings for more serious ones. |The magistrate sentenced him to 30 days in jail. 地方法官判处他30天监禁。|近义词：judge, justice of the peace|反义词: defendant, accused|noun

magistrate:/ˈmædʒɪstreɪt/| n. |地方行政官|A local government official. (Less common usage) |The magistrate oversaw the distribution of aid to the flood victims. 地方行政官监督了对洪灾受害者的援助分配。|近义词：official, administrator|反义词: citizen, resident|noun
