outdeliver:/ˌaʊt.dɪˈlɪ.vər/
verb
超越交付；在交付方面胜过
To deliver more or better than a competitor or expectation.
Our company consistently outdelivers our competitors in terms of customer service. 我们公司在客户服务方面一直优于竞争对手。
They outdelivered their sales target by 20%. 他们的销售额超出目标20%。
近义词: outperform, surpass, exceed
反义词: underperform, fall short of
词性: verb
