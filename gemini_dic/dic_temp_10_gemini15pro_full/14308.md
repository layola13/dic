automate:/ˈɔːtəmeɪt/ (US) /ˈɔːtəmæt/ (UK)| v. |使自动化；自动操作|To make a process or system operate automatically, without human intervention.|例句：The factory automated its production line. 工厂自动化了它的生产线。

例句：We're looking at ways to automate the payment system. 我们正在寻找使支付系统自动化的方法。|近义词：mechanize, computerize, robotize|反义词: manualize, de-automate|词性:动词


automate:/ˈɔːtəmeɪt/ (US) /ˈɔːtəmæt/ (UK)| adj. |自动化的|Operated automatically, without human intervention.|例句：An automate factory. 一个自动化工厂。

例句：The automate system saves a lot of time.  自动化系统节省了很多时间。|近义词：automatic, mechanized, robotic|反义词: manual, non-automatic|词性:形容词
