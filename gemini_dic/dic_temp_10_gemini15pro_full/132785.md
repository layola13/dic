nonductility:/ˌnɒndʌkˈtɪləti/
noun
非延展性；不延性
The lack of ability of a material to deform permanently before fracturing.  It describes a material's inability to be drawn into wires or hammered into thin sheets without breaking.
The nonductility of the material made it unsuitable for the intended application.  这种材料的非延展性使其不适合预期的应用。
The bridge's collapse was attributed to the nonductility of the steel used in its construction.  这座桥的倒塌归因于其建造中使用的钢材的非延展性。
Synonym: brittleness, inflexibility, rigidity
Antonym: ductility, malleability, flexibility
