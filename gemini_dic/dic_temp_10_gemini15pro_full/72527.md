flushingly:/ˈflʌʃɪŋli/
adv.
脸红地，因羞愧或激动而
In a manner characterized by blushing; with a sudden reddening of the face, typically as a result of shame or embarrassment.
She accepted the compliment flushingly. 她脸红地接受了赞美。
She flushingly admitted her mistake. 她羞愧地承认了自己的错误。
近义词: blushingly, shyly, self-consciously
反义词: calmly, coolly, confidently
词性: 副词

词源:  Derived from the verb "flush" + "-ing" (present participle) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Flush" comes from Old French "flux" meaning "flow."
