minihomes:/ˈmɪniˌhoʊmz/| n. |小型住宅，微型住宅|A small, compact dwelling, often designed for affordability, mobility, or minimal environmental impact.  It can be a prefabricated structure, a converted vehicle, or a small, standalone house.|They decided to live in minihomes to reduce their living expenses. 他们决定住在小型住宅里以减少生活开支。|Synonyms: tiny houses, small homes, compact houses|Antonyms: mansions, large houses|Noun
