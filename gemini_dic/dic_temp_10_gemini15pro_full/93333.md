ill-used:/ˌɪlˈjuːzd/
adjective
受虐待的，被苛待的
Treated badly or unfairly.
She felt ill-used by her employer, who never gave her a raise. 她觉得被雇主苛待了，因为他从未给她加薪。
The ill-used furniture showed signs of years of neglect.  这件受损的家具显示出多年来被忽视的迹象。
近义词: mistreated, abused, maltreated
反义词: cherished, well-treated, protected
词性: adjective
词源: From "ill" (badly) + "used" (treated).
