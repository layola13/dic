mitosis: /maɪˈtoʊsɪs/ /maɪˈtəʊsɪs/
n.
有丝分裂
The process of cell division in which the nucleus divides into two nuclei containing the same number of chromosomes.
Mitosis is essential for growth and repair in multicellular organisms. 有丝分裂对于多细胞生物的生长和修复至关重要。

Mitosis ensures that each new cell receives a complete set of chromosomes. 有丝分裂确保每个新细胞都获得一套完整的染色体。

近义词: cell division, karyokinesis
反义词: meiosis
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek  μίτος (mitos, “thread”) +‎ -osis.
