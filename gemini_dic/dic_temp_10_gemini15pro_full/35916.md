cocreator:/ˌkoʊkriˈeɪtər/| n. |共同创造者，合著者|A person who creates something jointly with another or others. |例句：She is listed as a cocreator of the television series. 她被列为这部电视剧的共同创作者。

例句：He and I were cocreators of the project. 他和我是这个项目的共同创造者。|近义词：collaborator, coauthor, joint author|反义词:|noun
