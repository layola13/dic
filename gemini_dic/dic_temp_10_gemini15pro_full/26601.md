cacheable:/ˈkæʃəbl/| adj.|可缓存的|Able to be stored in a cache (a temporary storage area) to speed up retrieval later.|The website's images are cacheable, so they load quickly on subsequent visits.  该网站的图片是可缓存的，所以在后续访问中加载速度很快。|近义词:storable, reusable|反义词:uncacheable|词性:形容词
