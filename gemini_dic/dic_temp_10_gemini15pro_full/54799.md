dispenser:/dɪˈspɛnsər/ | n. |分配器，售货机，药剂师|A machine or container that is designed to release a specific amount of something, such as a liquid, powder, or pills.  |例句：The soap dispenser is empty.  肥皂机空了。

例句：He bought a candy dispenser for his daughter. 他给他女儿买了一个糖果机。|近义词：distributor, vendor, machine, apparatus|反义词:collector, accumulator|词性:noun
