hyetography:/ˌhaɪɪˈtɒɡrəfi/ /ˌhaɪəˈtɑːɡrəfi/
noun
雨量记录；雨量分布图
The scientific description and recording of rainfall.
Hyetrography plays a vital role in understanding regional climate patterns. 雨量记录在理解区域气候模式中起着至关重要的作用。
Hyetrography charts show the distribution of rainfall over a specific area. 雨量分布图显示了特定区域的降雨分布。
近义词:pluviography, ombrography
反义词:N/A
词性:n.

Etymology: From Greek *hyeto-* (rain) + *-graphy* (writing, description)
