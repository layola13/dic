femicides:/ˈfɛmɪsaɪdz/ | n. |杀害女性(尤指出于性别歧视或厌女症的杀害女性行为) | The systematic killing of women, especially because they are women. |  The rising femicides rate in the country is alarming. 这个国家不断上升的杀害女性比率令人担忧。

The government has implemented new policies to address the issue of femicides. 政府已经实施了新的政策来解决杀害女性的问题。 | 近义词: gendercide, feminicide | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

Etymology: From the combination of "femin-" (from Latin "femina," meaning "woman") and "-cide" (from Latin "-cida," meaning "killer" or "killing").
