outrushes:/ˈaʊtrʌʃɪz/
noun
涌出；冲出；流露
A sudden rapid flow or bursting forth.
例句：The outrushes of lava from the volcano were spectacular. 火山喷出的熔岩蔚为壮观。

例句：There was an outrush of emotion when the team won the championship. 当球队赢得冠军时， emotions surged forth.

近义词：surges, bursts, eruptions, effusions
反义词：inflows, trickles
