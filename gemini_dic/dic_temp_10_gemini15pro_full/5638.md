altiloquent:/ælˈtɪləkwənt/| adj.|高谈阔论的；辞藻华丽的|Characterized by lofty, inflated, or pompous language.  Speaking in a grand or elaborate way, often to impress.|Her altiloquent speech impressed the audience, but some found it insincere. 她那辞藻华丽的演讲给观众留下了深刻的印象，但也有人觉得它不真诚。|近义词：bombastic, grandiloquent, pompous|反义词：simple, plain, unpretentious|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *altus* (high) + *loqui* (to speak).
