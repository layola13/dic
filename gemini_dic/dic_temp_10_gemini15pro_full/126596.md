naggy:/ˈnæɡi/| adj.|唠叨的，爱抱怨的|Complaining or criticizing frequently, especially about trivial matters.|She has a naggy husband who always complains about the housework. 她有一个爱抱怨的丈夫，总是抱怨家务事。|例句：My mother gets naggy when I don't call her regularly.  我妈如果我不经常打电话给她，她就会唠叨。

例句：He's got a really naggy cough that won't go away. 他一直咳嗽，总不好。|近义词：carping, complaining, fault-finding, critical|反义词：tolerant, patient, understanding|词性:adjective
