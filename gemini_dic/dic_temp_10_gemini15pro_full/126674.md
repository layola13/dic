namaskar:/nʌməˈskɑːr/ |n. int.|合十礼，问候|A respectful greeting or salutation used in India and Nepal, typically accompanied by bringing the palms together before the chest or face.|Example:  She greeted us with a namaskar. 她用合十礼向我们问候。

Example:  Namaskar, welcome to our home. 合十礼，欢迎来到我们的家。|近义词: greeting, salutation, hello|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "disrespectful gesture")|词性: noun, interjection
