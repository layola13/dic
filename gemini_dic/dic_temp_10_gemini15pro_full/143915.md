outprice:/ˌaʊtˈpraɪs/
verb
索价过高; 出价高于
to price (goods or services) so high that (competitors or customers) cannot afford to buy or compete
例句：Smaller firms were outpriced by the larger companies. 小公司因大公司的索价过高而无法竞争。

例句：They were outpriced by a rival bidder. 他们的出价被竞争对手超过了。

近义词：overprice, price out of the market
反义词：underprice
词性：v.
