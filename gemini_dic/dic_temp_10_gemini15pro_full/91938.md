hypermetaphorical:/ˌhaɪpərˌmetəˈfɔːrɪkl/ | adj. | 超级隐喻的 |  Excessively or elaborately metaphorical; using metaphors layered upon other metaphors, often to a convoluted or exaggerated degree. | Her poetry is so dense and hypermetaphorical that it's almost impossible to decipher. 她的诗歌密集且充满超级隐喻，几乎无法解读。 |  The novel's hypermetaphorical language created a dreamlike atmosphere. 这部小说的超级隐喻语言营造了一种梦幻般的氛围。 | 近义词:  overly metaphorical, excessively figurative, elaborately metaphorical | 反义词: literal, straightforward, plain | 词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
hyper- (prefix): from Greek, meaning "over, beyond, excessive"
metaphorical (adjective): from metaphor, from Greek metaphora, meaning "transfer," from metapherein, meaning "to carry over, transfer"
