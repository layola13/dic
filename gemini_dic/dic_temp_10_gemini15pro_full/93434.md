illuminati:/ɪˌluːmɪˈnɑːti/ | n. | 光明会；先觉者 |  persons claiming special intellectual or spiritual enlightenment.  A name that has been given to various groups, historic and modern, and both real and fictitious. Historically, the name refers to the Bavarian Illuminati, an Enlightenment-era secret society founded on May 1, 1776.  |  Many conspiracy theories center around the supposed influence of the Illuminati. 许多阴谋论都围绕着光明会所谓的势力展开。 | synonyms: enlightened,  cognoscenti | antonyms: ignorant, unenlightened | noun
