costard:/ˈkɒstərd/ (UK), /ˈkɑːstərd/ (US)| n.|苹果; 衣衫褴褛的人; 粗人|A large ribbed kind of apple. / A person, especially a man, wearing shabby or showy clothes. / A rude, ignorant, or uncouth person.|例句：He bit into the juicy costard with relish. 他津津有味地咬了一口多汁的苹果。

例句：The costard stumbled down the street, muttering to himself. 那衣衫褴褛的人在街上跌跌撞撞，自言自语。|近义词：apple, lout, boor|反义词:gentleman, refined person|词性:noun

costard:/ˈkɒstərd/ (UK), /ˈkɑːstərd/ (US)| n.|粗鲁的人，乡巴佬|A head; pate (often used contemptuously).|例句：I'll give the costard a knock! 我要给他脑袋一击！
