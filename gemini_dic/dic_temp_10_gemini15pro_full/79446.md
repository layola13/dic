glitterer:/ˈɡlɪtərər/| n.|闪光体，闪光的人或物|Something that glitters; a person or thing that displays superficial brilliance.|The diamond was a true glitterer, catching the light from every angle. 这颗钻石真是闪闪发光，从各个角度都能捕捉到光线。|近义词：sparkler, shiner|反义词:dull thing, matte object|词性:名词

词源: From the verb "glitter" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Glitter" comes from Middle English *glitren, frequentative of *gliteren "to shine, sparkle," probably of Scandinavian origin, related to Old Norse glitra "to glitter, sparkle."
