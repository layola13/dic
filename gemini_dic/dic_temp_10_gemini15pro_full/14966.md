baa's:/bɑːz/| n.|羊的叫声; (尤指)咩咩声|The baa's of the lambs filled the air. 羊羔的咩咩声充满了空气。|近义词：bleat, bleating|反义词: (无明确的反义词)|noun


Etymology->
From Middle English *ba, from Old English *bē, from Proto-Germanic *bēaną (“to bleat”). Cognate with Dutch baa (“baa”), German bäh (“baa”).  Ultimately imitative of the sound.
