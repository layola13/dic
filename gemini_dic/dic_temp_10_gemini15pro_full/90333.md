housings:/ˈhaʊzɪŋz/
noun
房屋; 住宅; 外壳; 外罩
protective casings or structures for equipment.

The housings for the electronics were made of durable plastic.
这些电子设备的外壳由耐用的塑料制成。

New housings are being built to accommodate the growing population.
正在建造新的房屋以容纳不断增长的人口。

近义词:accommodations, dwellings, shelters, residences, homes
反义词:None (lack of housing could be considered an antonym in a contextual sense)
词性:noun (plural)

词源:From "house" + "-ing" (suffix forming nouns from verbs, often with the sense of "action or process," or "result of an action").  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *husan.
