overdose:/ˈoʊvərˌdɒʊs/ (US) /ˈəʊvədɒʊs/ (UK)| n. vi. vt.|过量；过量服用；（使）过量服用|An overdose of this drug can be fatal. 这种药物过量服用可能是致命的。|例句：He died of a drug overdose. 他死于药物过量。

例句：She took an overdose of sleeping pills. 她服用了过量的安眠药。


|近义词：excess, surfeit|反义词: underdose|noun


overdose:/ˈoʊvərˌdɒʊs/ (US) /ˈəʊvədɒʊs/ (UK)| vi. vt.|过量服用；（使）过量服用|Don't overdose on vitamin supplements. 不要过量服用维生素补充剂。|例句：He overdosed on heroin and died. 他过量服用海洛因而死。

例句：She overdosed him with sleeping pills. 她让他服用了过量的安眠药。


|近义词：overindulge|反义词: underdose|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + dose.  "Over-" signifies "excessive" or "too much." "Dose" comes from the Old French word "dose" meaning a portion of medicine, ultimately from Greek "dosis" meaning "a giving" or "gift."
