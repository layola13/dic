cretin:/ˈkriːtɪn/ | n. | 呆子；白痴；愚人 | A stupid, obtuse, or mentally deficient person. |  He called the politician a cretin for proposing such a ridiculous policy. 他称那位提出如此荒谬政策的政客是个白痴。| synonyms: idiot, fool, imbecile, moron, dolt | antonyms: genius, intellectual, savant | noun

cretin:/ˈkriːtɪn/ | adj. (informal, offensive) | 愚蠢的；痴呆的 | Exceptionally stupid.  | That cretinous driver nearly caused an accident. 那个愚蠢的司机差点酿成事故。| synonyms: stupid, idiotic, foolish, imbecilic, moronic | antonyms: intelligent, smart, bright, clever, wise | adjective
