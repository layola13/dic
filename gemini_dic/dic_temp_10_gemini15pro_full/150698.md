parablast:/ˈpærəblæst/| n.|副胚层，外胚层下层|The parablast is a layer of cells in the early embryo of some animals, lying beneath the epiblast.  它是某些动物早期胚胎中位于外胚层下方的一层细胞。|The parablast contributes to the formation of extraembryonic membranes. 副胚层有助于胚外膜的形成。|Synonym: hypoblast|Antonym: epiblast|noun
