hemisect:/ˈhɛmɪˌsɛkt/| v. |二等分，对半切|To divide (something) into two equal parts.|The surgeon hemisected the brain to study its structure.  外科医生将大脑对半切开以研究其结构。|Example:  The line hemisects the angle.  这条线将角二等分。|Synonyms: bisect, halve|Antonyms: combine, join|verb
