influxively: /ɪnˈflʌksɪvli/ | adv. | 流入地，涌入地 |  In a manner characterized by influx or flowing in.  This word is not commonly used and may be considered non-standard. |  Resources flowed influxively into the developing nation. 资源涌入发展中国家。| 近义词: abundantly, copiously, profusely | 反义词: sparsely, meagerly | 词性: 副词

词源:  Derived from "influx" (n.), which comes from Latin "influxus," past participle of "influere" ("to flow in"), from "in-" ("in, into") + "fluere" ("to flow").
