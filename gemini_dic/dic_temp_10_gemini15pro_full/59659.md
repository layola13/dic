echinid:/ˈɛkɪnɪd/ | n. |海胆|A sea urchin; a small, round sea animal with a hard shell covered in spines.|例句：We saw many echinids while snorkeling in the coral reef. 我们在珊瑚礁浮潜时看到了许多海胆。

例句：The echinid's spines protect it from predators. 海胆的刺保护它免受捕食者的侵害。|近义词：sea urchin|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


Etymology: From Modern Latin *Echinida*, from Greek *ekhinos* "hedgehog, sea urchin."
