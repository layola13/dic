misplacing: /mɪsˈpleɪsɪŋ/ | v. (present participle) | 放错地方；乱放 | Putting something in the wrong place and then being unable to find it. |

例句：
I'm always misplacing my keys.
我总是把钥匙放错地方。

He misplaced his glasses and couldn't read the menu.
他把眼镜放错了地方，没法看菜单。

近义词： losing, mislaying | 反义词： finding, locating | 词性： verb (present participle of misplace)
