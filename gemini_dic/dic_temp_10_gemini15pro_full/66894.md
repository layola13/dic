expediment:/ɪkˈspɛdɪmənt/| n.|障碍，阻碍；累赘，拖累|something that makes it difficult to do or achieve something|例句：The lack of funding was a major impediment to the project. 资金短缺是该项目的主要障碍。

例句：Her fear of flying was an impediment to her career as a travel agent. 她对飞行的恐惧是她旅行社事业的阻碍。|近义词：obstacle, hindrance, obstruction, barrier|反义词：aid, help, assistance|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin impedimentum "hindrance," from impedire "to shackle the feet of," from in- "in, on" + pes (genitive pedis) "foot".
