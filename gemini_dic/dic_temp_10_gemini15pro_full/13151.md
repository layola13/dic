asymptote: /ˈæsɪmptəʊt/ (UK) /ˈæsɪmptoʊt/ (US) | n. | 渐近线 | A line that a curve approaches, as it heads towards infinity, but never reaches. | The x-axis is an asymptote to the curve. x轴是这条曲线的渐近线。| limit | intersection | noun
