idem:/ˈaɪdɛm/ /ˈɪdɛm/ | adj. pron.| 同上，同前|The same; the same as previously mentioned or indicated.  Referring to something that has just been mentioned or described.| The author of this book is listed as Anonymous, idem the editor.| 近义词:same, aforesaid, aforementioned, ditto| 反义词: different, other| 词性:adjective, pronoun

Etymology:
From Latin *idem* "the same," from *is* "he, that" + *-dem* (enclitic demonstrative particle).
