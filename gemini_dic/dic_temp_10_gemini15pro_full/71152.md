fissigemmation:/ˌfɪsɪdʒɛˈmeɪʃən/ | n. |裂殖生殖| Reproduction by fission or budding, where a new organism develops from an outgrowth or bud due to cell division at one particular site. | The fissigemmation of the coral polyp resulted in a new, genetically identical individual.  珊瑚虫的裂殖生殖产生了一个新的、基因相同个体。| 近义词: budding, fission | 反义词: sexual reproduction | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Latin *fissus* (past participle of *findere*, "to split") + *gemmatio* ("budding"), from *gemma* ("bud").
