geriatricians: /ˌdʒɛriəˈtrɪʃənz/ | n. |老年病学家，老年医学专家|A physician who specializes in the care of older adults, focusing on age-related diseases and conditions.|The hospital has a team of skilled geriatricians. 这家医院有一支技术娴熟的老年病专家团队。|Geriatricians often work with other specialists to provide comprehensive care for their patients. 老年病专家经常与其他专家合作，为病人提供全面的护理。|Synonyms: gerontologists,老年病医生|Antonyms: pediatrician, 儿科医生|noun
