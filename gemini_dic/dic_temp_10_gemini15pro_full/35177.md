cloyingly:/ˈklɔɪɪŋli/
adverb
甜得发腻地；膩得令人作嘔地
Excessively sweet, sentimental, or flattering to the point of being unpleasant.
Her cloyingly sweet voice grated on my nerves. 她甜得发腻的声音让我神经紧张。
The romance novel was cloyingly sentimental. 这部爱情小说感伤得令人作呕。
近义词: saccharine, sugary, syrupy, oversweet
反义词: austere, dry, understated
词性: adverb

cloy:/klɔɪ/
verb
使膩煩；使生膩
To cause disgust or aversion by sweetness or excess.
The overly sweet dessert began to cloy. 过于甜的甜点开始让人腻烦。
Too much praise can cloy. 过多的赞美会让人反感。
近义词: satiate, surfeit, glut
反义词: stimulate, excite, tantalize
词性: verb
