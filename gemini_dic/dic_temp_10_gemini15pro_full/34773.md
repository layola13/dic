climactic:/klaɪˈmæk.tɪk/
adjective
高潮的，顶点的
Forming or relating to a climax; constituting a climax.
The movie's climactic scene was breathtaking.  这部电影的高潮部分令人叹为观止。
The climactic battle decided the outcome of the war. 这场决定性的战役决定了战争的结果。

近义词:culminating, decisive, pivotal, peak
反义词:anticlimactic, introductory, preliminary
词性:adjective

词源: From Late Latin climacticus, from Greek klimaktērios “critical, climacteric,” from klimax “ladder, staircase, critical point in sickness, climax.”
