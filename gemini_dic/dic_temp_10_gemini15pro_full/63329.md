entitled:/ɪnˈtaɪt(ə)ld/ | adj. | 有资格的；有权利的；名为…的 |  Having a right to something; believing oneself to be inherently deserving of privileges or special treatment; given a title. | The book is entitled "The Art of War." 这本书名为《孙子兵法》。| Synonyms: authorized, qualified, eligible, permitted | Antonyms: ineligible, unqualified, unauthorized, forbidden | adjective

entitled:/ɪnˈtaɪt(ə)ld/ | adj. | 自以为是的 | Feeling that you have the right to do or have what you want without working for it or deserving it, even if other people do not have the same rights.  | He acts like he's entitled to everything. 他表现得好像他什么都有资格得到一样。| Synonyms: arrogant, presumptuous, self-important | Antonyms: humble, modest, unassuming | adjective
