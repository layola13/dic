lingua-francas:/ˌlɪŋɡwə ˈfræŋkəz/| n.|通用语，共通语|A language that is adopted as a common language between speakers whose native languages are different.|English is often used as a lingua franca in international business.  英语在国际商务中经常被用作通用语。|Example:  Swahili is a lingua franca across much of East Africa. 斯瓦希里语是东非大部分地区的通用语言。|Synonyms: common language, shared language, bridge language|Antonyms: vernacular, dialect|noun
