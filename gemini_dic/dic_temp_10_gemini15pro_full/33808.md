circumflex:/ˈsɜːrkəmfleks/ (US) /ˈsɜːkəmfleks/ (UK)| adj. n.|抑扬符，弯曲的|A mark (^) placed over a vowel, used in some languages to indicate contraction, length, or a particular vowel quality; curved or arched.|The word "hôpital" in French uses a circumflex over the "o".|近义词:caret, arc, bend, curve|反义词:straight|词性:adjective, noun
