extrusible:/ɪkˈstruːzəbl/
adjective
可挤压的，可压出的
Capable of being extruded; able to be forced or pressed out of a small opening.
The toothpaste is extrusible from the tube.  牙膏可以从管中挤出来。
The soft metal was extrusible into various shapes. 这种软金属可以挤压成各种形状。
Synonyms: squeezable, pushable, extrudable
Antonyms: non-extrusible, rigid, inflexible
词性: adjective
