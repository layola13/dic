|n.|快乐，欢乐，高兴|The possessive form of "merriness," indicating belonging to or associated with merriness.  It refers to the quality or state of being merry.|The merriness's infectious nature spread throughout the room.  房间里充满了欢乐的气氛。|近义词：joy, gaiety, cheerfulness, mirth|反义词：sadness, gloom, melancholy|词性:名词

词源:
From Middle English *merinesse, from Old English *myrignes, from myrge (“pleasant, merry”) + -nes. Cognate with Scots mirrines (“merriment”), Dutch *meerigheid.
