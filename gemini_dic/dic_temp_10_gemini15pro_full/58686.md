dumped:/dʌmpt/ | v. |倾倒, 抛弃, 丢弃| to drop or throw something carelessly or in a large amount; to end a romantic relationship with someone | He dumped the trash in the bin. 他把垃圾倒进了垃圾桶。 She dumped her boyfriend last week. 她上周甩了她的男朋友。 | 近义词: discarded, abandoned, jettisoned, ditched | 反义词: kept, saved, cherished | 词性: 动词

词源:  From dump (n.), from Middle Low German or Middle Dutch *dumpen "to strike heavily, thump."  Originally referring to the thudding sound of something being dropped.
