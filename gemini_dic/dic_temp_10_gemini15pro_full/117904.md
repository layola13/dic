melpomene:/məlˈpɒməniː/ /məlˈpɑːməniː/
noun
墨尔波墨涅（希腊神话中悲剧的缪斯女神）
Melpomene is the Muse of Tragedy in Greek mythology, often depicted holding a tragic mask and a club or sword.
Melpomene is often invoked by playwrights seeking inspiration for their tragic works.  墨尔波墨涅经常被剧作家们祈求，以获得创作悲剧作品的灵感。
The statue of Melpomene stood majestically in the theatre.  墨尔波墨涅的雕像雄伟地矗立在剧院里。
近义词: Muse (general sense),  Clio (Muse of history - for a sense of recording tragic events)
反义词: Thalia (Muse of comedy), Euterpe (Muse of music - for a joyful contrast)
