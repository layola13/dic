olde:/oʊld/| adj.|古老的，旧的|Archaic form of "old."  Meaning "ancient, of long standing." Derived from Old English "eald."|The olde mill stood by the stream.  古老的磨坊矗立在溪流旁。|Synonyms: ancient, antique, venerable|Antonyms: new, modern, recent|adjective
