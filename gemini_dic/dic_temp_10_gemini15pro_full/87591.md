heterosporous:/ˌhɛtərəˈspɔːrəs/
|adj.|异孢子的|Producing two different kinds of spores, megaspores and microspores. |Ferns are an example of a heterosporous plant. 蕨类植物是异孢子植物的一个例子。|heterosporic|homosporous|adjective
heterospory:/ˌhɛtərəˈspɔːri/
|n.|异孢子现象|The production of two types of spores differing in size and sex potential.|Heterospory is a key adaptation in the evolution of seed plants.异孢子现象是种子植物进化中的一个关键适应。|heterosporism|homospory|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From hetero- (different) + -sporous (spore-bearing).  Ultimately from Greek *héteros* (other, different) and *sporá* (seed, sowing).
