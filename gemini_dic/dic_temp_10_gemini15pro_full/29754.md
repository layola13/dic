cataract:/ˈkætərækt/ (US) /ˈkætərækt/ (UK)| n.| 白内障；瀑布；洪水；奔流|A medical condition in which the lens of the eye becomes progressively opaque, resulting in blurred vision.|Example 1:  His eyesight was failing because of cataracts. 他的视力因白内障而衰退。

Example 2: The waterfall was a spectacular cataract. 瀑布是壮观的急流。|近义词：waterfall, cascade, deluge, flood (for the meaning of "flood" or "rushing water") opacity, cloudiness (for the medical condition)|反义词: clarity, transparency (for the medical condition)|词性: noun
