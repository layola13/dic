dervish:/ˈdɜːrvɪʃ/| n.|托钵僧，苦行僧；狂热者|A member of a Muslim (especially Sufi) religious order who has taken vows of poverty and austerity. Often characterized by ecstatic dancing or whirling.|The whirling dervishes of Istanbul are a popular tourist attraction. 伊斯坦布尔的旋转托钵僧是一个很受欢迎的旅游景点。|synonyms: ascetic, mystic, fakir|antonyms: materialist, hedonist|noun
