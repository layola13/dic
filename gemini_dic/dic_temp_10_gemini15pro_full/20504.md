biventral:/baɪˈventrəl/
adjective
双腹的；有两条腹部的
Having two bellies or protuberances.  Specifically referring to muscles with two fleshy portions separated by a tendon.
The digastric muscle is a biventral muscle.  下颌二腹肌是一块双腹肌。
biventral:/baɪˈventrəl/
noun
双腹肌
A muscle with two bellies or fleshy portions separated by an intermediate tendon.
The omohyoid is an example of a biventral muscle. 肩胛舌骨肌是双腹肌的一个例子。

近义词: digastric
反义词: (No direct antonym.  Consider "single-bellied" or "unifascicular" in specific contexts)
词性: adjective, noun
