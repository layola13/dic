foolproof:/ˈfuːlˌpruːf/| adj.|防呆的；万无一失的|Designed so that it cannot fail or be misused, even by someone who is foolish or inexperienced.|This recipe is foolproof - even I can't mess it up.  这个食谱万无一失——即使是我也做不错。|The instructions are foolproof, so anyone can assemble it. 说明书非常简单易懂，任何人都可以组装。|近义词：infallible, fail-safe, certain, reliable|反义词：faulty, flawed, unreliable|词性:形容词
