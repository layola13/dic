legitimist:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmɪst/
n./adj.|正统主义者；拥护正统者|A person who supports a legitimate ruler, especially a monarch, or the principle of hereditary rule, often in opposition to a usurper or revolutionary government.|The aging legitimist refused to acknowledge the new republic.  这位年迈的正统主义者拒绝承认新共和国。|Synonyms: royalist, monarchist|Antonyms: republican, revolutionary|noun
legitimist:/ləˈdʒɪtɪmɪst/
adj.|正统的；拥护正统的|Supporting or relating to a legitimate ruler, especially a monarch, or the principle of hereditary rule.|He held a legitimist view of the monarchy. 他持有拥护君主正统的观点。|Synonyms: lawful, rightful|Antonyms: illegitimate, unlawful|adjective
