hallooing:/həˈluːɪŋ/
| n. |叫喊，大声招呼|A loud shout or call to attract attention.|The hunters were hallooing to each other across the valley. 猎人隔着山谷互相大声呼喊。|
近义词: shouting, yelling, calling, whooping
反义词: whispering, murmuring
词性: noun (也可作为动词 halloo 的现在分词形式)

halloo:/həˈluː/
| v. |叫喊，大声招呼|To shout or call loudly, especially to attract attention.|He hallooed to his friend across the street. 他隔着街道大声招呼他的朋友。|
近义词: shout, yell, call, whoop
反义词: whisper, murmur
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *haloler, of Germanic origin. Related to "hola".
