asbestosis:/ˌæsbesˈtoʊsɪs/| n. |石棉肺，石棉沉着病|A chronic lung disease caused by inhaling asbestos fibers.|例句：Asbestosis is a serious and progressive disease. 石棉肺是一种严重且进展性的疾病。

例句：He developed asbestosis after years of working in a shipyard.  他在造船厂工作多年后患上了石棉肺。|近义词：pneumoconiosis, lung disease|反义词: health, wellness|词性:noun
