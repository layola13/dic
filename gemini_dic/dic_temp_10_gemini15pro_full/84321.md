half-yearly:/ˌhɑːfˈjɪə(r)li/ /ˌhæfˈjɪrli/
adjective
半年一次的；每半年的
Occurring or payable twice a year.
The company publishes half-yearly financial reports.  这家公司每半年发布一次财务报告。
The school has half-yearly exams. 学校每半年举行一次考试。
synonym:biannual, semiannual
antonym:annual, quarterly
词性:adjective


half-yearly:/ˌhɑːfˈjɪə(r)li/ /ˌhæfˈjɪrli/
adverb
每半年地
Twice a year; every six months.
Dividends are paid half-yearly. 股息每半年支付一次。
The magazine is published half-yearly. 这本杂志每半年出版一次。
synonym:biannually, semiannually
antonym:annually, quarterly
词性:adverb


half-yearly:/ˌhɑːfˈjɪə(r)li/ /ˌhæfˈjɪrli/
noun
半年刊；半年一次的付款
A publication issued twice a year; a payment made twice a year.
The company's half-yearly is now available. 公司的半年刊现已发行。
She receives a half-yearly payment of interest. 她每半年收到一次利息。
synonym:biannual, semiannual publication/payment
antonym:annual publication/payment, quarterly publication/payment
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "half" + "yearly". "Half" comes from Old English "healf," meaning "one of two equal parts." "Yearly" comes from Old English "ġēarlic," meaning "occurring every year," from "ġēar" (year) + "-lic" (suffix forming adjectives).
