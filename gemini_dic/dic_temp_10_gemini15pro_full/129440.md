non-ability:/ˌnɒn əˈbɪləti/ /ˌnɑːn əˈbɪləti/| n. |无能力，不能|Lack of ability; inability to do something.|He admitted his non-ability to solve the problem. 他承认自己没有能力解决这个问题。|The non-ability to speak the language hindered her progress. 她不会说这种语言，这阻碍了她的进步。|近义词：inability, incapacity, incompetence|反义词: ability, capability, competence|词性:名词

词源:
non- (prefix):  From Latin *non, from ne (“not”) + oinom (“one”).  Meaning "not".
ability (noun): From Middle English abilite, from Old French habilité, from Latin habilitās (“aptitude, skill”), from habilis (“apt, fit”), from habeō (“to have, hold”).
