moratoria:/ˌmɔːrəˈtɔːriə/ | n. | 暂停，延期，缓期 | A temporary suspension of an activity or law.  A legally authorized period of delay in the performance of a legal obligation or the payment of a debt. | The government announced a moratorium on new building in the area. 政府宣布暂停在该地区的新建工程。| synonyms: suspension, postponement, deferment, delay, reprieve | antonyms: continuation, resumption, commencement | noun

moratorium:/ˌmɔːrəˈtɔːriəm/ | n. | 暂停，延期，缓期 | A temporary suspension of an activity or law.  A legally authorized period of delay in the performance of a legal obligation or the payment of a debt. |  The bank agreed to a moratorium on loan repayments. 银行同意暂停偿还贷款。| synonyms: suspension, postponement, deferment, delay, reprieve | antonyms: continuation, resumption, commencement | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *morātōrium*, neuter of *morātōrius* (“delaying, causing delay”), from *morārī* (“to delay”), from *mora* (“delay”).
