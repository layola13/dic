deitate:/ˈdiːɪteɪt/
verb
使神化; 把…奉若神明
To make a god of; to deify.
The Romans deitated their emperors after their deaths. 罗马人在皇帝死后将他们神化。
近义词:deify, apotheosize, exalt
反义词:humanize, degrade
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *deitāre, frequentative of deō (“to make a god of”), from deus (“god”).
