cofavorites:/ˈkoʊˈfeɪvərɪts/
noun
共同的最爱，共同喜爱的事物
People or things that are equally favored or preferred by a group or individual.
My cofavorites are pizza and tacos. 我最喜欢的是披萨和玉米卷。
synonyms: joint favorites, equal favorites
antonyms: least favorites, dislikes
词性:n.
