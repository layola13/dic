no-balls:/ˌnəʊ ˈbɔːlz/ | n. | 无效投球（板球术语） |  In cricket, a no-ball is an illegal delivery by the bowler.  It adds one run to the batting team's score and the batsman cannot be dismissed (except for certain other infringements). | The bowler bowled a no-ball because his foot was over the line. 投球手投了一个无效球，因为他的脚越线了。

The umpire called a no-ball, giving the batting team an extra run. 裁判员判罚无效球，给击球队加了一分。| 近义词: illegal delivery | 反义词: legal delivery | 词性: noun

no-balls: /ˌnəʊ ˈbɔːlz/ | n. (figurative, informal) |  胆量，勇气（通常用在否定句中）|  Used figuratively to refer to a lack of courage or audacity.  | He's got no balls to stand up to his boss. 他没有胆量反抗他的老板。

She showed no balls when she quit her job without notice. 她不辞而别，一点勇气都没有。 | 近义词: guts, nerve, courage (in negative sentences, lack of) | 反义词: courage, bravery | 词性: noun


词源:  Compound of "no" and "balls" (testicles).  The figurative meaning derives from the association of testicles with masculinity and courage.
