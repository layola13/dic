nonnecessity:/ˌnɒn.nəˈses.ə.ti/ | n. | 非必需品；不必要；不必要的事物 |Something that is not necessary or essential. |  The report highlighted the nonnecessity of the proposed new building.  这份报告强调了拟建新大楼的不必要性。

The removal of the extra features demonstrated the nonnecessity of their inclusion in the first place.  移除额外的功能证明了最初将它们包含在内的不必要性。 | synonyms: nonessential, inessential, superfluous, dispensable | antonyms: necessity, essential, requirement, requisite | noun
