componentry:/kəmˈpəʊnəntri/ /kəmˈpoʊnəntri/| n.|组件；部件；元件|The bicycle's componentry was top-of-the-line, ensuring a smooth and efficient ride.  这辆自行车的部件都是顶级的，确保了平稳高效的骑行。|The company specializes in the manufacture of high-quality electronic componentry. 这家公司专门生产高质量的电子元件。|近义词：components, parts, elements|反义词: whole, entirety|词性:noun
Etymology:  From component + -ry.  Component ultimately derives from the Latin verb *compōnere*, meaning "to put together."  The suffix *-ry* forms nouns denoting a collection or group of things.
