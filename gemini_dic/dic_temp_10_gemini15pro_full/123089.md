monger:/ˈmʌŋɡər/| n.| 商人，贩子；散播…的人|A person who promotes a specified activity, cause, or feeling, especially one that is undesirable or discreditable. |He's a war-monger who wants to see the world burn. 他是一个战争贩子，想看到世界毁灭。|  The scandal exposed him as a fear-monger and a liar. 这桩丑闻揭露了他是一个散布恐惧的人和一个骗子。| synonyms: dealer, trader, trafficker, peddler, vendor, merchant, purveyor, promoter, propagator, disseminator | antonyms: pacifist, peacemaker | noun
