excusably:/ɪkˈskjuːzəbli/
adverb
可原谅地，情有可原地
in a way that can be excused or forgiven.
He was excusably late for the meeting due to the heavy traffic. 由于交通拥堵，他开会迟到是可以原谅的。
She was excusably upset after hearing the bad news.  听到坏消息后，她感到难过是可以理解的。
近义词:understandably, justifiably, forgivably
反义词:unjustifiably, inexcusably
词性:adverb

词源:
From excuse (v.) + -able + -ly.  The root word "excuse" comes from the Old French "excuser," which in turn comes from the Latin "excusare," meaning "to free from blame."
