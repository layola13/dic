contravene:/ˌkɒntrəˈviːn/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈviːn/| v. |违反，违背，抵触|to go against a law, rule, or agreement|例句：The company contravened building regulations.  这家公司违反了建筑法规。

例句：Their actions contravened the terms of the treaty. 他们的行为违反了条约的条款。

近义词：violate, breach, infringe, disobey, defy|反义词：obey, comply with, uphold, observe|词性：动词
