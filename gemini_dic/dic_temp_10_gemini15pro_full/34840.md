clinical:/ˈklɪnɪkl/| adj.|临床的；诊所的；冷静的；客观的| Relating to the observation and treatment of actual patients rather than theoretical or laboratory studies.|例句：The clinical trial showed promising results. 临床试验显示出有希望的结果。|近义词：medical, practical, objective, analytical, detached|反义词：theoretical, subjective, emotional|adjective

clinical:/ˈklɪnɪkl/| n.|临床试验；临床教学|A session of supervised practical instruction in medicine, typically in a hospital setting.|例句：Clinicals are an essential part of medical training. 临床实践是医学培训的重要组成部分。|近义词：practicum, internship, residency|反义词：theory, lecture|noun
