harborous:/ˈhɑːrbərəs/ | adj. | 提供庇护的；藏匿的；包庇的 | Providing shelter or refuge; concealing or hiding something, especially something illegal or undesirable. | The harborous cove provided a safe haven for the small fishing boats during the storm.  暴风雨期间，这个港湾为小渔船提供了一个安全的避风港。| 近义词: sheltering, protecting, concealing, harboring | 反义词: exposing, revealing | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From "harbor" (meaning "a place of shelter") + "-ous" (a suffix forming adjectives meaning "full of" or "possessing the qualities of").  源自“harbor”（意为“庇护所”）+“-ous”（构成形容词的后缀，意为“充满”或“拥有……的品质”）。
