circumscriptly:/sərkəmˈskrɪptli/| adv.|谨慎地，限定地|Within clearly defined limits; concisely and precisely.  Restricted in scope or extent.|He answered circumspectly, careful not to reveal too much information. 他谨慎地回答，小心翼翼地不透露太多信息。|Synonyms: cautiously, carefully, guardedly, prudently, discreetly|Antonyms: carelessly, recklessly, indiscreetly, rashly|adverb
