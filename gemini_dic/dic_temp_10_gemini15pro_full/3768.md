agitatingly:/ˈædʒɪteɪtɪŋli/
adverb
令人不安地，令人焦躁地
In a manner that causes anxiety, excitement, or disturbance.
Her hands fluttered agitatingly as she waited for the news. 她焦急地等待着消息，双手不停地颤抖。
The wind howled agitatingly through the trees. 风不安地呼啸着穿过树林。
近义词:nervously, anxiously, restlessly, disturbingly
反义词:calmly, peacefully, soothingly
词性:adverb

词源:
From agitate (verb) + -ing (present participle suffix) + -ly (adverb suffix).  Agitate comes from Latin *agitāre, frequentative of agere "to drive, lead, act."
