machinists:/məˈʃiːnɪsts/| n. |机械师，机械工|A person who operates or repairs machines, especially metal-working machines. |The machinists carefully calibrated the equipment before starting production. 机械师在开始生产前仔细校准了设备。

The machinists' union negotiated a new contract. 机械师工会谈判了一份新合同。|近义词：mechanic, operator, technician|反义词: N/A |noun
