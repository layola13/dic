gummiferous:/ˌɡʌˈmɪfərəs/
adjective
产胶的；树胶质的
Producing or exuding gum.
The gummiferous tree oozed a sticky resin. 这棵产胶的树渗出一种粘稠的树脂。
resinous, gummy
non-resinous

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *gummifer, from gummi (“gum”) + -fer (“bearing”).
