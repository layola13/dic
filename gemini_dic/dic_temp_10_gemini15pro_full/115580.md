marathons:/ˈmærəθɒnz/ (UK), /ˈmærəθɑːnz/ (US)| n. |马拉松，耐力赛|A long-distance running race of 26 miles 385 yards (42.195 kilometers).  It can also refer to any long and arduous undertaking. |例句：He ran his first marathon last year.  他去年跑了他的第一个马拉松。

例句：The negotiations were a real marathon.  这次谈判是一场真正的马拉松。|近义词：endurance race, long-distance race, ordeal|反义词:sprint, dash|词性:noun

marathon:/ˈmærəθɒn/ (UK), /ˈmærəθɑːn/ (US)| adj. |马拉松式的，漫长的|Characterized by great length or duration. |例句：We had a marathon meeting that lasted eight hours.  我们开了一个长达八小时的马拉松式会议。

例句：She embarked on a marathon study session before her exams. 她开始了一场马拉松式的学习，为考试做准备。|近义词：extended, prolonged, lengthy|反义词:short, brief|词性:adjective
