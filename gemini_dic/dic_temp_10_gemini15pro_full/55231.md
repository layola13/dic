dissight:/dɪsˈsaɪt/
n.
缺乏洞察力；缺乏远见；失察
lack of insight or foresight; a failure to perceive or understand something
例句：His dissight in not foreseeing the market crash cost him dearly. 他没能预见到市场崩盘，这种缺乏远见使他付出了高昂的代价。

例句：The committee's dissight led to a poorly planned project. 委员会的失察导致了项目计划不周。
近义词：shortsightedness, myopia, oversight, blindness
反义词：foresight, insight, vision, perspicacity
词性: 名词
