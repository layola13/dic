interarboration:/ˌɪntərˌɑːrbəˈreɪʃən/
n.
相互交错的树木;树木的交错种植
The interweaving of branches from different trees, forming a canopy.  树枝相互交错，形成树冠。
The park is characterized by its beautiful interarboration, creating a shaded and peaceful atmosphere.  这个公园的特点是美丽的树木交错，营造出阴凉而宁静的氛围。
intertwining, interlacing, interwoven canopy
--- (no clear antonym)
