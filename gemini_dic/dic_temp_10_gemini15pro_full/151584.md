parthenic:/pɑːrθəˈnɪk/
adjective
处女生殖的；单性生殖的；未受精的
Relating to or characterized by parthenogenesis, the development of an egg without fertilization.
例句：Parthenic reproduction is common in some insect species.  某些昆虫物种中，单性生殖很常见。

例句：Scientists are studying the possibility of parthenic human reproduction. 科学家正在研究人类单性生殖的可能性。
近义词：asexual, virginal
反义词：sexual
词性：adjective
