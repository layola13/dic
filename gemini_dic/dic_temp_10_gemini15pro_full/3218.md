affectionately:/əˈfekʃənətli/
adverb
深情地，亲切地
In a loving or tender way; with affection.
She hugged her son affectionately. 她深情地拥抱了她的儿子。
affectionately:/əˈfekʃənətli/

Showing love and tenderness.
