exterritoriality:/ˌɛkstɛrɪˌtɔːriˈælɪti/ | n. |治外法权|The state of being exempted from the jurisdiction of local law, usually as the result of a treaty.|  The embassy enjoys extraterritoriality. 大使馆享有治外法权。| synonyms: immunity, privilege | antonyms: jurisdiction, subjection | noun

exterritorial:/ˌɛkstɛrɪˈtɔːriəl/ | adj. | 治外法权的 |  Beyond the jurisdiction of the local courts.| The ambassador's residence is considered extraterritorial. 大使的住所被认为是治外法权的。| synonyms: exempt, immune | antonyms: subject, jurisdictional | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *exterritorialis*, from ex- (out of) + territorialis (territorial).
