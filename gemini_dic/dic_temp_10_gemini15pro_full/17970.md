befoul:/bɪˈfaʊl/
verb
弄脏，污损
to make something dirty or unpleasant
The oil spill befouled the beaches.  石油泄漏污染了海滩。
近义词: contaminate, pollute, taint, soil
反义词: cleanse, purify, clean
词性:v.

Etymology:
From be- + foul.  Be- is a prefix meaning "thoroughly" or "completely." Foul means "offensive to the senses," especially through having a disgusting smell or taste.
