ideogrammatic:/ˌaɪdiəɡrəˈmætɪk/
| adj.|表意文字的，表意的|Relating to or expressed by ideograms; symbolic.|Chinese characters are ideogrammatic, representing ideas or concepts rather than sounds.|近义词:symbolic, representative, emblematic|反义词:phonetic, alphabetic|词性:adjective
Etymology: From "ideogram" + "-atic". "Ideogram" comes from French "idéogramme," from  Greek "idea" (idea) + "-gramma" (something written).
