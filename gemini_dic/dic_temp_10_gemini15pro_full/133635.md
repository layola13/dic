nonforfeiting:/ˌnɒnˈfɔːrfɪtɪŋ/
| adj. | 不可丧失的，(指保险)不失效的 |  Guaranteeing the continuation of benefits or rights, especially in an insurance policy, even if premiums are not paid or certain conditions are not met.  |  Example: He chose a nonforfeiting life insurance policy to ensure his family's financial security even if he missed some payments. 他选择了一份不可丧失的人寿保险，以确保即使他错过了一些付款，他的家人的经济安全也能得到保障。 |  Synonyms:  incontestable, guaranteed, secure, vested | Antonyms: forfeitable, revocable, conditional | adjective

nonforfeiture:/ˌnɒnˈfɔːrfɪtʃər/
| n. | (保险)不失效，不可丧失 | The quality or state of being nonforfeiting; the right to retain certain benefits even if premiums are not paid. | Example: The nonforfeiture clause in the insurance policy protected his investment.  保险单中的不可丧失条款保护了他的投资。 | Synonyms:  incontestability, guarantee, security, vesting | Antonyms: forfeiture, revocation | noun
