graphitoidal:/ˌɡræfɪˈtɔɪdəl/ | adj. | 石墨状的，类似石墨的 |  Having a layered structure similar to graphite, often referring to minerals that are soft, dark, and have a greasy feel. | The rock sample contained graphitoidal inclusions.  岩石样品中含有石墨状夹杂物。 | Similar: plumbaginous, graphitic | Opposite: crystalline, amorphous | adjective
