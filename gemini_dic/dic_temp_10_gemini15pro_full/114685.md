malignly:/məˈlaɪnli/| adv.|恶毒地，恶意地|In a manner intended to cause harm or damage, especially by saying false or unfair things about someone or something.  Characterized by malice; malevolent.|He was malignly accused of stealing. 他被恶意指控偷窃。|Synonyms: malevolently, viciously, spitefully, maliciously|Antonyms: kindly, benevolently, charitably|词性:副词
