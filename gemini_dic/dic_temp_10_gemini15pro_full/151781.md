pass-fail:/ˈpæs feɪl/| adj.|合格/不合格; 及格/不及格|A system of grading where students receive either a passing or failing grade, without letter grades or percentages.  This system emphasizes mastery of material over specific scores.|The university adopted a pass-fail system for some introductory courses.|Synonyms: credit/no credit, satisfactory/unsatisfactory|Antonyms: graded, lettered|adjective
