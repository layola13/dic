goosish:/ˈɡuːsɪʃ/| adj.|愚蠢的，傻的|Resembling or suggesting a goose, especially in being foolish or silly.|He gave a goosish grin. 他傻傻地咧嘴一笑。|近义词：silly, foolish, stupid, inane|反义词：wise, intelligent, clever|词性:形容词
词源：From goose + -ish.  Relating to or characteristic of a goose. The sense development from “goose-like” to “silly” likely stems from the perceived stupidity of geese.
