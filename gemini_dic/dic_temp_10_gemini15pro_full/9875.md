aparejo:/ɑːpəˈreɪhoʊ/ | n. |  tackle, gear, rigging (especially for horses) |  A set of ropes and pulleys used for hoisting or lowering weights; the harness and other equipment used for a horse or other draft animal. |  The sailor checked the aparejo before hoisting the sail.  水手在升帆之前检查了索具。 | Synonyms: equipment, gear, harness, rigging, tackle | Antonyms: N/A | Noun

aparejo:/ɑːpəˈreɪhoʊ/ | n. |  pack saddle |  A type of saddle used on pack animals, especially in Latin America.  | The mule carried heavy loads on its aparejo. 骡子用驮鞍驮着重物。 | Synonyms: packsaddle | Antonyms: N/A | Noun
