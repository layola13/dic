asyndeton:/əˈsɪndɪtɒn/ (US) /əˈsɪndɪtən/ (UK)| n.|省略连词|A stylistic device in which conjunctions are omitted, producing a fast-paced and rapid prose.|I came, I saw, I conquered. 我来了，我看见了，我征服了。|近义词：brachylogia|反义词：polysyndeton|noun

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He runs, jumps, swims. 他跑步，跳跃，游泳。
She sings, dances, acts. 她唱歌，跳舞，表演。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
He is running, jumping, swimming. 他正在跑步，跳跃，游泳。
She is singing, dancing, acting. 她正在唱歌，跳舞，表演。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He has run, jumped, swum. 他跑过，跳过，游过。
She has sung, danced, acted. 她唱过，跳过，表演过。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
He has been running, jumping, swimming. 他一直跑步，跳跃，游泳。
She has been singing, dancing, acting. 她一直唱歌，跳舞，表演。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He ran, jumped, swam. 他跑，跳，游。
She sang, danced, acted. 她唱，跳，表演。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
He was running, jumping, swimming. 他当时正在跑步，跳跃，游泳。
She was singing, dancing, acting. 她当时正在唱歌，跳舞，表演。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

PHRASE->
(Asyndeton is a stylistic device, and doesn't typically appear in set phrases.  It's a way of constructing sentences rather than a phrase itself.  However, here are some examples of its *use*):

Venit, vidit, vicit.  He came, he saw, he conquered.
Friends, Romans, countrymen, lend me your ears. 朋友们，罗马人，同胞们，请听我说。
The car crashed, the glass shattered, the driver screamed. 汽车撞毁了，玻璃碎了，司机尖叫起来。
Reduce, reuse, recycle. 减少使用，重复使用，循环利用。
Live, laugh, love. 活着，欢笑，爱。
