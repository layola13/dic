encounterer:/ɪnˈkaʊntərər/| n. |遭遇者，遇见者|A person who encounters or meets someone or something.|例句：The encounterer was surprised by the sudden appearance of a deer in the forest.  这位遭遇者对森林里突然出现的一只鹿感到惊讶。

例句：The encounterer described the strange object they saw in the sky.  这位目击者描述了他们在天空中看到的奇怪物体。|近义词：observer, witness, experiencer|反义词:avoider|词性:noun
