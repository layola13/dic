moose:/muːs/| n.|驼鹿|A large deer with palmate antlers, found in the forests of North America, Europe, and Asia.|例句：We saw a moose while hiking in the Canadian Rockies. 我们在加拿大落基山脉徒步旅行时看到了一只驼鹿。

例句：The moose crossed the road in front of our car. 驼鹿在我们车前穿过马路。


近义词：elk (especially in North America)|反义词:|词性:noun
