carbon:/ˈkɑːrbən/ (US) /ˈkɑːbən/ (UK)|n.|碳|A nonmetallic chemical element, atomic number 6, that readily forms compounds with many other elements and is a constituent of organic compounds.|Diamonds are made of carbon. 钻石是由碳构成的。|Synonyms:  C (chemical symbol), charcoal, graphite, soot|Antonyms:  None (as it is an element)|noun


carbon:/ˈkɑːrbən/ (US) /ˈkɑːbən/ (UK)|adj.|碳的|Pertaining to, or composed of, carbon.|Carbon emissions are a major contributor to climate change.  碳排放是气候变化的主要原因。|Synonyms:  carbonaceous|Antonyms:  None|adjective
