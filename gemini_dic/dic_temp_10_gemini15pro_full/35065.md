closet: /ˈklɒzɪt/ (UK) /ˈklɑːzɪt/ (US)| n. adj.|衣橱，壁橱；密室，私下的；隐藏的|A cupboard or wardrobe, typically one tall enough to walk into. Also, a small room used for storage, or (figuratively) a state of secrecy.|例句：She hung her clothes in the closet. 她把衣服挂在衣橱里。|近义词：cupboard, wardrobe, cabinet|反义词: open, public|词性: noun, adjective

closet: /ˈklɒzɪt/ (UK) /ˈklɑːzɪt/ (US)| adj.|秘密的，隐蔽的| Kept secret or done secretively, especially because of fear of disapproval.|例句：He is a closet smoker. 他是一个偷偷抽烟的人。|近义词：secret, hidden, private|反义词: open, public|词性: adjective
