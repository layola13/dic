illusion:/ɪˈluːʒn/ | n. | 幻想，错觉 | something that deceives by producing a false or misleading impression of reality. |例句：The magician created the illusion of a disappearing rabbit. 魔术师制造了兔子消失的幻觉。

例句：He had no illusions about his chances of winning. 他对自己的获胜机会不抱幻想。|近义词：delusion, fantasy, mirage |反义词：reality, truth |词性: noun
