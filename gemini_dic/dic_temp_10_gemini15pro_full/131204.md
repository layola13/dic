nonbursting:/ˌnɒnˈbɜːrstɪŋ/ | adj. | 非突发的，非脉冲式的 | Not bursting; continuous or steady.  Relating to a type of network traffic that sends data at a constant rate, rather than in bursts. | This network connection offers a nonbursting 10 Mbps speed. 这个网络连接提供稳定的 10 Mbps 的速度。|  continuous, steady, constant | bursting, pulsating | adjective
