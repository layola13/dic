minitour:/ˈmɪniˌtʊr/| n. |小型巡回演出，短期旅行|A short tour, often involving a limited number of performances or visits to places. |例句：The band went on a minitour of the Midwest.  乐队在中西部进行了小型巡回演出。

例句：We took a minitour of the wineries in the region. 我们对该地区的酿酒厂进行了短暂的参观。|近义词：short tour, brief tour, whistle-stop tour|反义词:long tour, extended tour|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From mini- (meaning "small") + tour.  The prefix "mini-" comes from Latin "minimus" meaning "smallest". "Tour" comes from Old French "tour" meaning "a going round," from Latin "tornus" "a lathe," from Greek "tornos" "a tool for making circular objects."
