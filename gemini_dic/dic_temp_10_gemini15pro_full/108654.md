leafinesses:/ˈliːfinɪsɪz/
n.
叶茂; 多叶; 枝繁叶茂
The state or quality of being leafy; abundance of leaves.
The leafinesses of the trees provided ample shade.  树木的茂密叶子提供了充足的阴凉。
The park was notable for the leafinesses of its ancient oaks.  这个公园以其古老橡树的枝繁叶茂而闻名。
近义词: lushness, verdure, foliage
反义词: barrenness, sparseness
