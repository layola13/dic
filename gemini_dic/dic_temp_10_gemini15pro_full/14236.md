autocrat: /ˈɔːtəkræt/ (US) /ˈɔːtəkrat/ (UK)| n. |独裁者，专制君主|A ruler who has absolute power.|例句：The autocrat held absolute power over the small nation.  这个独裁者对这个小国拥有绝对权力。

例句：He ruled as an autocrat, making all decisions without consulting anyone. 他作为独裁者进行统治，所有决定都不与任何人商量。

近义词：dictator, tyrant, despot|反义词：democrat|词性：noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek "autokratēs" (self-ruler, absolute ruler), from "autos" (self) + "kratos" (power, strength).
