deglutination:/ˌdiːɡluːtɪˈneɪʃn/ | n. |吞咽；吞咽困难|The act of swallowing.  It is a complex process involving the coordinated action of several muscles in the mouth, pharynx, and esophagus.|  Dysphagia, or difficulty with deglutination, can be a serious medical problem. 吞咽困难，或吞咽困难，可能是一个严重的医学问题。| synonyms: swallowing, ingesting | antonyms: regurgitation, vomiting | noun
