cubism:/ˈkjuːbɪzəm/| n.|立体主义|An early-20th-century avant-garde art movement that revolutionized European painting and sculpture, and inspired related movements in music, literature and architecture.  It is characterized by the use of geometric shapes, especially cubes, to represent objects and figures.|Picasso's painting "Les Demoiselles d'Avignon" is considered a proto-cubist work.  毕加索的画作《亚威农少女》被认为是原始立体主义作品。

Cubism was a highly influential movement in art. 立体主义是艺术史上一个非常有影响力的运动。|近义词:constructivism, futurism|反义词:realism, impressionism|词性:noun
