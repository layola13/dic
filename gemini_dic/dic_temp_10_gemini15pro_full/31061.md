chamfering:/ˈtʃæmfərɪŋ/| n.|倒角，切斜边|The process of beveling an edge or corner.  |例句：The chamfering on the edge of the table prevents splintering. 桌子边缘的倒角可以防止裂片。|近义词：beveling, rounding, easing|反义词：sharpening|noun
chamfer:/ˈtʃæmfər/| n. vt.|倒角，切斜边；斜面，斜边|A beveled edge or corner; to cut a chamfer. |例句：The carpenter added a chamfer to the shelf. 木匠在架子上加了一个倒角。|近义词：bevel, slope, cant|反义词：edge, corner|noun, verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *chamfrein (Modern French chanfrein), from Vulgar Latin *cantus franus "broken corner," from Late Latin cantus "corner" + Latin frangere "to break".
