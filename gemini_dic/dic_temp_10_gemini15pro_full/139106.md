obduratenesses: /ˌɒbdjʊrətˈnɛsɪz/ /ˌɑːbdʊrətˈnɛsɪz/
n.
顽固，执拗，冷酷无情
The state of being obdurate; stubbornness; impenitence.  
His obdurateness in the face of pleas for mercy was shocking.  他面对求饶仍冷酷无情，令人震惊。

近义词: stubbornness, intransigence, inflexibility, rigidity, obstinacy
反义词: flexibility, compliance, yielding, acquiescence, submissiveness
词性: noun (plural)
