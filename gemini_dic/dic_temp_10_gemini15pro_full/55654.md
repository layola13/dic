dithyrambic:/ˌdɪθɪˈræmbɪk/ | adj. | 狂热的，热情洋溢的，酒神赞歌式的 | Wildly enthusiastic; extravagant; relating to a dithyramb (a passionate, irregular poem or song of ancient Greece). |  The poet's dithyrambic verses celebrated the beauty of nature. 这位诗人热情洋溢的诗句歌颂了大自然的美。| Synonyms: ecstatic, rhapsodic, passionate, fervent, effusive | Antonyms: restrained, subdued, calm, unemotional | adjective

dithyramb:/ˈdɪθɪræm/ | n. | 酒神赞歌，狂热赞颂 | A wildly enthusiastic speech or piece of writing; a passionate, irregular poem or song of ancient Greece. | His latest novel is a dithyramb to the joys of rural life. 他的最新小说是对乡村生活乐趣的热情赞颂。| Synonyms: eulogy, panegyric, accolade, tribute | Antonyms: criticism, condemnation, censure | noun
