dropsical: /ˈdrɑːpsɪkəl/ | adj. | 水肿的，患水肿病的 | Affected with, or characteristic of, dropsy; edematous; hydropic.  Swollen with an excessive accumulation of serous fluid. |  His dropsical legs made it difficult for him to walk.  他水肿的双腿使他行走困难。| The patient's dropsical condition worsened overnight. 病人的水肿状况一夜之间恶化了。|  synonyms: edematous, hydropic, swollen | antonyms:  normal, healthy | adjective
