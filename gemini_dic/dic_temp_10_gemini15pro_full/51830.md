dewiring:/diːˈwaɪərɪŋ/
v.
拆除线路，去除线路
The process of removing wires or cables from a structure or device.
The electrician is dewiring the old house before the renovation.  电工在翻修前正在拆除老房子的线路。
The bomb squad carefully dewired the explosive device.  拆弹小组小心翼翼地拆除了爆炸装置的线路。
近义词:  disconnecting, unwiring
反义词: wiring, connecting
词性: 动词
