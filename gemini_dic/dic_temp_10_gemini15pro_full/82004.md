gripe:/ɡraɪp/| n. v. |抱怨，牢骚；肠绞痛|A complaint, especially a minor or persistent one.  To complain naggingly or constantly.|例句：My only gripe about the hotel was the lack of a pool. 我对这家酒店唯一的抱怨就是没有游泳池。|近义词：complaint, grumble, moan, whine|反义词：praise, compliment|词性:名词,动词
gripe:/ɡraɪp/| v. |使痛苦，夹痛；惹恼|To cause pain or discomfort to; to irritate or annoy.|例句：The new shoes griped my feet. 新鞋子夹痛了我的脚。|近义词：irritate, annoy, bother|反义词：soothe, please|词性:动词

Cognate with Dutch grijpen (“to grab, seize, grip”), German greifen (“to grab, grip, seize, grasp”), Swedish gripa (“to seize, arrest, grip”), Icelandic grípa (“to seize, grab”).
