interannually:/ˌɪntərˈænjuəli/
adverb
逐年地；每年之间地
occurring between years; comparing one year to another.
The interannual variability of rainfall in this region is quite high.  这个地区的降雨量年际变化很大。
近义词: yearly, annually
反义词: N/A
词性: adverb

词源:
From inter- + annually.  "Inter-" means "between" and "annually" means "yearly."
