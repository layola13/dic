garland:/ˈɡɑːrlənd/ /ˈɡɑːrland/| n.|花环，花冠，桂冠；荣誉|A circle of flowers and leaves worn around the head or neck or used for decoration.|The bride wore a garland of white roses. 新娘戴着白色的玫瑰花环。|Synonyms: wreath, chaplet, crown, festoon|Antonyms: none|noun

garland:/ˈɡɑːrlənd/ /ˈɡɑːrland/| vt.|用花环装饰，授予…荣誉|To decorate with a garland.|They garlanded the winning horse. 他们给获胜的马戴上花环。|Synonyms: adorn, decorate, festoon, wreathe|Antonyms: strip, denude|verb
