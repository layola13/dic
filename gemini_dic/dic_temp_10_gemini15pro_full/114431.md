make use of:/meɪk ˈjuːs əv/ | phrasal verb | 利用，使用 | To utilize or employ something for a purpose. |  We should make use of every opportunity to learn. 我们应该利用每一个学习的机会。

| 近义词：utilize, employ, exploit, apply | 反义词：neglect, ignore, waste | 词性: 动词短语


make:/meɪk/ | v. | 制作，使，成为 | To create, cause, or become something. | He made a cake for her birthday. 他为她的生日做了一个蛋糕。

| 近义词：create, produce, form | 反义词：destroy, break | 词性: 动词

use:/juːz/ | n. v. | 使用，用途 | The act or practice of employing something; the purpose for which something is used. | The use of mobile phones is prohibited in the exam. 考场内禁止使用手机。

| 近义词：utilization, application, employment | 反义词：disuse | 词性: 名词/动词

of:/əv/ | prep. | …的，属于…的 | Expressing relationship, possession, or belonging. | The history of China is fascinating. 中国的历史引人入胜。

| 近义词：belonging to, relating to | 反义词： (无直接反义词) | 词性: 介词
