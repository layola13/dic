accriminate: /əˈkrɪmɪneɪt/
verb
控告；指控；使负罪
to make someone appear guilty of a crime or wrongdoing
The evidence seemed to acriminate the defendant. 证据似乎是指控被告。
He refused to answer questions for fear he might acriminate himself. 他拒绝回答问题，因为他害怕这可能会使自己负罪。
近义词: incriminate, indict, charge, accuse
反义词: exonerate, acquit, absolve
词性: verb
