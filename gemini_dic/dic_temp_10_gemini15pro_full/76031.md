furious:/ˈfjʊəriəs/| adj.|狂怒的，暴怒的|Extremely angry.|He was furious when he found out his car had been stolen. 他发现他的车被偷后勃然大怒。|近义词：angry, enraged, indignant, livid|反义词：calm, peaceful, tranquil|词性:形容词

词源:  From Latin *fūrōsus* "raging, mad," from *fūror* "rage, madness," related to *furere* "to rage, be mad."
