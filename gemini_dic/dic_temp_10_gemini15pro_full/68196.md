fair-mindedly:/ˌfeərˈmaɪndɪdli/| adv.|公平地；不偏不倚地|In a fair and impartial manner, without bias or prejudice.  Showing a willingness to consider all sides of an issue.|The judge listened to both sides of the argument fair-mindedly before making a decision. 法官在做出决定之前公平地听取了双方的论点。|Synonyms: impartially, objectively, justly, equitably|Antonyms: unfairly, biasedly, unjustly, partially|adverb
