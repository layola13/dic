demi-gorge:/ˈdɛmiˌɡɔːrdʒ/
demi-gorge|n.|半月堡，胸墙|A defensive work consisting of a low, detached wall, usually semicircular, in front of the main wall of a fortification.|The demi-gorge protected the gate from direct fire.|Synonym: barbican, outwork|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider terms like inner wall, keep)|Noun
