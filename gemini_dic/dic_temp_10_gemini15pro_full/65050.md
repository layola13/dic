eternity: /ɪˈtɜːrnɪti/ (US) /ɪˈtɜːnəti/ (UK) | n. | 永恒，无限期 | A state of endless or infinite time; time without beginning or end. |  He promised to love her for all eternity. 他承诺永远爱她。

The universe may have existed for eternity. 宇宙可能已经存在了永恒。

近义词: infinity, perpetuity, timelessness
反义词: brevity, transience, temporality
词性: noun

词源:  From Old French *eternité and directly from Latin aeternitas, from aeternus (“eternal”), from aevum (“age, lifetime”) + -tas.
