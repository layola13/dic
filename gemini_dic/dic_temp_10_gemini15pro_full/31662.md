chatters:/ˈtʃætərz/ (US), /ˈtʃætəz/ (UK)| n.| 聊天，鸟鸣声，打颤声|The sound of the birds' chatters filled the air. 鸟儿的鸣叫声充满了空气。|近义词：chattering, jabbering, prattling|反义词: silence, quiet|词性:名词 (noun)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *chateren, frequentative of chatten (“to chat”), of imitative origin.
