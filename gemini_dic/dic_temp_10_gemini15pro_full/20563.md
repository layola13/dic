blackband:/ˈblækˌbænd/| n.|黑带，黑条纹，黑色饰带,一种黑色页岩|A blackband is a dark layer of rock, especially a type of shale containing ironstone.  黑带是一种深色的岩层，尤指一种含铁矿石的页岩。|The miners were working the blackband ironstone. 矿工们正在开采黑带铁矿石。|近义词：dark band, stripe, layer|反义词:light band, white band|词性:名词

blackband:/ˈblækˌbænd/| adj.|黑带的，有黑条纹的|This is a blackband ironstone formation. 这是一个黑带铁矿石地层。|近义词：dark-banded, striped|反义词:light-banded, unstriped|词性:形容词
