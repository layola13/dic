fossils:/ˈfɒsəlz/ (British), /ˈfɑːsəlz/ (American)| n.|化石|The remains or impression of a prehistoric plant or animal embedded in rock and preserved in petrified form.|例句：The museum has a fascinating collection of dinosaur fossils.  博物馆收藏了令人惊叹的恐龙化石。

例句：She found some interesting fossils on the beach.  她在海滩上发现了一些有趣的化石。|近义词：remains, relics|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "living organisms")|词性:Noun
