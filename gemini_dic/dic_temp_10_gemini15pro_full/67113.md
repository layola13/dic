explosivenesses:/ɪkˈsploʊsɪvnəsɪz/
n.
爆炸性，爆发力，易爆炸性
The quality of being explosive; tendency to explode.
The explosivenesses of the chemicals made them dangerous to transport. 这些化学品的爆炸性使得它们的运输很危险。
explosivity, volatility, instability
stability, inertness
