deluxe:/dəˈlʌks/ | adj. n. | 豪华的；高级的；豪华型；奢侈品 | Of a superior kind; luxurious.  A luxury item or service. |  例句：We stayed in a deluxe hotel suite. 我们住在一个豪华的酒店套房里。

例句：They offer a deluxe version of the software with more features. 他们提供了一个功能更丰富的豪华版软件。| 近义词：luxurious, lavish, opulent, premium, high-end | 反义词：basic, standard, economy, budget | 词性: 形容词，名词

词源: From French *de luxe* "of luxury," from Italian *di lusso*, from Latin *de luxu* "from luxury."
