|例句：The company keeps a detailed record of the nonexpirations of its software licenses.  该公司详细记录了其软件许可证的未到期情况。|
|例句：Checking the nonexpirations of our contracts is a crucial part of our administrative process. 检查我们合同的未到期情况是我们管理流程的关键部分。|

|反义词：expirations, expired terms|
|词性：noun|
