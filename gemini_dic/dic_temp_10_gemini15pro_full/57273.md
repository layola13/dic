downrange:/ˌdaʊnˈreɪndʒ/ | adv. adj.|下游的，靶场方向的|Located or operating in the direction of a firing range or missile launch site; far away, especially in a remote or sparsely populated area.|The missile landed downrange. 导弹落在靶场方向。|近义词：distant, remote, faraway, outback|反义词：uprange|词性:副词/形容词


downrange:/ˌdaʊnˈreɪndʒ/ | n.|靶场|An area where missiles or other projectiles are tested.|The downrange was heavily guarded. 靶场戒备森严。|近义词：firing range, test range|反义词：N/A|词性:名词

词源： "Down" indicates direction, and "range" refers to a distance or area, specifically a firing range.  So, "downrange" literally means "down the range."  The term originated in the military context of missile and artillery testing.
