dependent-clause:/dɪˈpɛndənt klɔz/ | n. |从句|A group of words that contains a subject and a verb but does not express a complete thought. It cannot stand alone as a sentence and needs an independent clause to complete its meaning.|Because I was tired, I went to bed early. 因为我累了，所以早早睡觉了。|近义词:subordinate clause|反义词:independent clause|词性:noun
