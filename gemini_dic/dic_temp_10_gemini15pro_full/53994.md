discriminate:/dɪˈskrɪmɪneɪt/| v. |歧视，区别，辨别 |To recognize a distinction; differentiate.  To make a distinction in favor of or against a person or thing on a basis other than individual merit.|She was discriminated against because of her age. 她因为年龄而受到歧视。|近义词：differentiate, distinguish, separate|反义词：confuse, mix,  treat equally|verb

discriminate:/dɪˈskrɪmɪnətɪŋ/| adj. |有识别力的，有鉴赏力的，歧视的|Having or showing refined taste or good judgment.|He has a discriminating palate for wine. 他对葡萄酒有很高的鉴赏力。|近义词： discerning, selective, judicious|反义词： undiscriminating, indiscriminate|adjective
