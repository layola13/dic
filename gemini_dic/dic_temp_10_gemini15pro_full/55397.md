distends:/dɪˈstɛndz/
verb
膨胀，扩张
to swell, expand, or become inflated
The balloon distends as you blow air into it.  气球在你吹气进去的时候膨胀。
His stomach distended after the large meal.  他吃完大餐后肚子胀起来了。
swell, inflate, expand, bloat
contract, shrink, deflate, compress
