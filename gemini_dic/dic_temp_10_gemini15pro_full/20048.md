biophore:/ˈbaɪəfɔːr/ | n. | 生物基团，生物载体，生物活性基团 | A group of atoms in a molecule thought to be responsible for its biological activity. |  Example 1: The biophore of the drug is responsible for its pain-relieving properties.  这药物的生物基团具有止痛的特性。

Example 2: Researchers are trying to identify the biophore responsible for the antibiotic activity of the compound. 研究人员正试图确定该化合物抗生素活性的生物基团。 | 近义词: pharmacophore, auxophore | 反义词:  None | 词性: noun
