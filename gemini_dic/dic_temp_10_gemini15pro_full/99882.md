internalises:/ɪnˈtɜːnəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使内化；吸收（尤指观念、态度、行为等）
to make (attitudes or behaviour) part of one’s nature by learning or unconscious assimilation.
He internalized the values of his parents. 他内化了他父母的价值观。
internalize, absorb, assimilate, incorporate
externalize, reject
v.
