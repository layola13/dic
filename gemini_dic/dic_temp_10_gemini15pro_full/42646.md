countersink:/ˈkaʊntərsɪŋk/ (US), /ˈkaʊntəsɪŋk/ (UK)| n. v.|埋头孔，锥形孔; 沉头钻，|A countersink is a conical hole cut into a manufactured object, or the tool used to cut such a hole. It allows the head of a countersunk bolt or screw to sit flush with or below the surface of the surrounding material.|The carpenter used a countersink to create a recess for the screw head.|近义词:recess, chamfer|反义词:protrusion|n. v.

countersink:/ˈkaʊntərsɪŋk/ (US), /ˈkaʊntəsɪŋk/ (UK)| v.|钻埋头孔，|To make a countersink (hole) in something.|He countersank the holes for the screws.|近义词:recess, chamfer|反义词:protrude|v.
