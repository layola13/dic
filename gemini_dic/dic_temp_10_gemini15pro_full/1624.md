acidophilous: /əˈsɪdəfɪləs/
adjective
嗜酸的
Thriving in or tolerating highly acidic conditions.  Growing or occurring in acidic environments.
Acidophilous plants are those that thrive in acidic soils. 嗜酸性植物是指在酸性土壤中茁壮成长的植物。

Heathers are acidophilous plants and prefer soils with a low pH. 石楠花是嗜酸性植物，喜欢低pH值的土壤。

近义词: acid-loving, acid-tolerant
反义词: basophilous, calciphile
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From acido- (acid) + -philous (loving), from Greek philos (loving).


USAGE->
These plants are acidophilous and thrive in acidic soil. 这些植物嗜酸，在酸性土壤中生长茂盛。
The acidophilous nature of these bacteria allows them to survive in the stomach. 这些细菌的嗜酸性使它们能够在胃中存活。

acidophilous organisms = 嗜酸生物
