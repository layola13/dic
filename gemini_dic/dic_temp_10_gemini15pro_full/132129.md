nonculminating:/ˌnɒnˈkʌlmɪneɪtɪŋ/
adjective
非高潮的; 非顶点的; 不达顶峰的
Not culminating; not reaching a climax or peak.
The symphony's quiet ending was surprisingly nonculminating.  这首交响乐安静的结尾出人意料地没有达到高潮。
The nonculminating nature of the hike made it suitable for families. 这次徒步旅行的非顶峰性质使其适合家庭参与。
near synonyms: anticlimactic, inconclusive, undeveloped
antonyms: culminating, climactic, peak
词性: 形容词
