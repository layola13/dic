impulsing:/ɪmˈpʌlsɪŋ/ | v. | 推动，激励；促使 | driving or urging forward; motivating | The coach's speech was impulsing the team to victory. 教练的讲话激励着团队走向胜利。| The new economic policy is impulsing growth in the manufacturing sector. 新的经济政策正在推动制造业的增长。 | 近义词：propelling, driving, urging, motivating, stimulating | 反义词：hindering, obstructing, deterring | 词性: 动词

impulsing:/ɪmˈpʌlsɪŋ/ | adj. | 冲动的，受冲动驱使的 | driven by impulse; acting on a sudden desire |  His impulsive decision to quit his job left him with regrets. 他一时冲动辞掉了工作，让他后悔不已。 |  Her impulsive nature often led her into trouble. 她冲动的性格经常让她陷入困境。 | 近义词：spontaneous, impetuous, rash | 反义词：considered, deliberate, cautious | 词性: 形容词 (较少用)


词源: From the Latin "impulsus," past participle of "impellere" (to push, drive).
