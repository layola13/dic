imbibe:/ɪmˈbaɪb/| v. |吸收，吸取（液体），接受（信息、思想等）|To absorb or assimilate something, especially information or ideas, or to drink (something, especially alcohol).|He imbibed the culture of the country during his year abroad. 他在国外的那一年里吸收了这个国家的文化。

She imbibed vast quantities of coffee. 她喝了大量的咖啡。|近义词：absorb, assimilate, drink, consume, ingest, take in|反义词：excrete, expel, discharge|词性:动词
