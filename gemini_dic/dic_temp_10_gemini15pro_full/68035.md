factorize:/ˈfæk.tə.raɪz/ | v. | 因式分解，分解 | To express (a number, algebraic expression, matrix, etc.) as a product of two or more factors. |  The teacher asked the students to factorize the equation. 老师要求学生们对这个方程式进行因式分解。|  The number 12 can be factorized as 2 x 2 x 3. 数字12可以被分解为2 x 2 x 3。 | synonyms: decompose, resolve, analyze, break down | antonyms: multiply, combine, synthesize | verb
