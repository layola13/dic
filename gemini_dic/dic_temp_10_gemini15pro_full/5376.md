alluviate:/əˈluːvieɪt/
verb
冲积，淤积
to deposit or form by deposition of sediment (as from a river)
The river alluviated the valley floor over thousands of years. 这条河在几千年的时间里淤积了谷底。
近义词: deposit, silt up, accumulate
反义词: erode, abrade
词性:v.

alluviation:/əˌluːviˈeɪʃn/
noun
冲积，淤积；冲积层，冲积物
the process of alluviating; the alluvial material deposited
The alluviation of the Nile delta has created fertile farmland. 尼罗河三角洲的冲积作用创造了肥沃的农田。
近义词: deposition, sedimentation, accretion
反义词: erosion, abrasion
词性:n.

alluvial:/əˈluːviəl/
adjective
冲积的
relating to or consisting of alluvium
The alluvial soil is rich in nutrients. 冲积土富含营养物质。
近义词: sedimentary, deposited
反义词: igneous, metamorphic
词性:adj.
