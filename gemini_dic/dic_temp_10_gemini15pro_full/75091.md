frilliness:/ˈfrɪlɪnɪs/
noun
花哨；褶边；虚饰
The quality of being frilly; excessive ornamentation.
Her dress was criticized for its excessive frilliness. 她的连衣裙因过于花哨而受到批评。

frilliness:/ˈfrɪlɪnɪs/
noun
轻浮；无足轻重
A lack of seriousness or substance.
The frilliness of the movie's plot disappointed many viewers.  这部电影情节的轻浮让许多观众失望。

近义词: ornamentation, embellishment, fussiness, superfluity, triviality, flippancy
反义词: simplicity, plainness, seriousness, substance
