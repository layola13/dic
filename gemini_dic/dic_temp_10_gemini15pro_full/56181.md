dodecaphonists:/ˌdoʊˌdekəˈfoʊnɪsts/
noun
十二音作曲家；十二音列主义者
Composers who write twelve-tone music, a compositional technique based on a series of all twelve notes of the chromatic scale.
Schoenberg was one of the most influential dodecaphonists.  勋伯格是最有影响力的十二音作曲家之一。
The dodecaphonists sought to create a new musical language. 十二音作曲家试图创造一种新的音乐语言。
Synonyms: serialists, twelve-tone composers
Antonyms: tonal composers
