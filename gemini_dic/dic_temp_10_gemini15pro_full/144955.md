overabundance:/ˌoʊvərəˈbʌndəns/| n. | 过多，过剩，过量 | An excessive amount of something; a surplus. |  The farmer had an overabundance of tomatoes this year and had to sell them at a discount. 今年农民的西红柿产量过剩，不得不打折出售。

The overabundance of information online can make it difficult to find what you're looking for. 网上信息过多，可能很难找到你要找的东西。

| 近义词：surplus, excess, plethora, profusion | 反义词: scarcity, lack, dearth, deficiency | 词性: noun
