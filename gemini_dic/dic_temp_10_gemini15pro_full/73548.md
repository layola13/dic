forespent:/fɔːrˈspent/| adj.|筋疲力尽的，疲惫不堪的|Exhausted, worn out, especially from exertion.|The runners collapsed, forespent, at the finish line. 赛跑者们精疲力竭地倒在终点线上。|The forespent travelers finally reached their destination.  疲惫不堪的旅行者终于到达了目的地。|近义词：exhausted, worn out, spent, fatigued, weary|反义词：energetic, refreshed, invigorated|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *forspenten, from Old English forspendan (“to spend, consume, use up”), equivalent to for- +‎ spend. Cognate with Dutch verspenden (“to squander”), German verspenden (“to donate”), Swedish förspilla (“to waste”).  来自中古英语 *forspenten，源自古英语 forspendan（“花费、消耗、用尽”），相当于 for- + spend。同源词有荷兰语 verspenden（“挥霍”）、德语 verspenden（“捐赠”）、瑞典语 förspilla（“浪费”）。
