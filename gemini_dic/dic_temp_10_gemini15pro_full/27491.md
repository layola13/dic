campbellite: /ˈkæmbəlaɪt/
noun
坎贝尔派信徒；基督教会的成员
A follower of the religious teachings of Alexander Campbell, specifically a member of the Churches of Christ.
Campbellites believe in the Bible as the sole source of religious authority. 坎贝尔派信徒相信圣经是唯一的宗教权威来源。
Early Campbellites faced persecution for their beliefs. 早期的坎贝尔派信徒因其信仰而面临迫害。
Synonyms:  Disciples of Christ, Church of Christ member
Antonyms: None (It's a specific religious group, not a concept with an opposite.)
