nonlitigation:/ˌnɑːnˌlɪtɪˈɡeɪʃən/
| n. |非诉讼| The process of resolving disputes outside of court. | Nonlitigation is often a faster and less expensive way to resolve disputes. 非诉讼通常是解决争议更快、更便宜的方式。|近义词：alternative dispute resolution, negotiation, mediation, arbitration|反义词：litigation, lawsuit, court case|词性: Noun, singular

词源: "Non-" (Latin, meaning "not") + "litigation" (from Latin "litigare," meaning "to go to law").
