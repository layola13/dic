etamine:/ˈetəmiːn/
n.
étamine (fabric)
A fine, strong, plain-woven cotton, linen, or worsted fabric, used for curtains, needlework, etc.
A sheer, lightweight fabric typically made of silk or rayon.
薄纱；细平布（一种细而结实的平纹棉布、亚麻布或精纺毛纱织物，用于窗帘、针线活等）
例句：She embroidered the delicate design onto a piece of etamine. 她在一块细平布上绣出了精致的图案。

例句：The curtains were made of a beautiful ivory etamine. 窗帘是用漂亮的象牙色薄纱制成的。

近义词：batiste, voile, muslin, organdy
