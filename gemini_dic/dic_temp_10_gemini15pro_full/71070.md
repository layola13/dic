firstness:/ˈfɜːrstnɪs/ /ˈfɝːst.nəs/ | n. | 初始性；第一性；原始性 | The quality of being first; originality; immediacy.  A philosophical concept developed by Charles Sanders Peirce referring to the immediate, unmediated character of experience. |  The painting captured the firstness of the experience, the raw sensation of seeing the ocean for the first time. 这幅画捕捉到了体验的初始性，第一次看到大海的原始感觉。| 近义词：originality, novelty, immediacy, freshness | 反义词：derivativeness, secondariness, thirdness | 词性: noun
