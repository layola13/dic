call-names:/ˈkɔːl neɪmz/ | n. | 辱骂，绰号 |  Offensive names or insults used to address someone. | Example: He hurled call-names at the referee after receiving a red card.  他被罚红牌后对着裁判破口大骂。

Example: Children can be cruel, and sometimes resort to using call-names. 孩子们有时会很残忍，会用绰号来骂人。 | Synonym: insults, slurs, epithets, abuse | Antonym: compliments, praise | Noun
