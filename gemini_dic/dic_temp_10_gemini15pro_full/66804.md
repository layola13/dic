expands:/ɪkˈspændz/| v.| 扩张，扩展，展开；扩大，增加；详述，详细阐明 |The company expands its business into new markets.  这家公司将其业务扩展到新的市场。|近义词：increase, enlarge, grow, develop, widen, broaden, stretch, spread, augment |反义词:contract, shrink, reduce, condense, compress |词性:动词
Etymology: From Middle English *expanden, from Old French espandre, espendre, from Latin expandere (“to spread out, unfold, expand”), from ex- (“out, forth”) + pandere (“to spread, stretch”).
