elflike:/ˈelfˌlaɪk/
adjective
像精灵的；仙女似的
Resembling an elf; delicate and graceful.
She had an elflike quality, with her pointed ears and delicate features. 她有一种精灵般的气质，尖尖的耳朵和精致的五官。
The child, with her wide, innocent eyes, had an elflike appearance.  这个孩子，有着天真无邪的大眼睛，看起来像个小精灵。
近义词:fairylike, elfin, ethereal, delicate, graceful
反义词:clumsy, awkward, coarse, rough
