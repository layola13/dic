meawl: /mjɑʊl/ | vi. | (猫)咪咪叫；哀叫；(人)哭诉 |  To make a long, high-pitched cry like a cat. Also, to complain in a whining way. | The kitten meawled pitifully when separated from its mother. 小猫与母猫分开时可怜地咪咪叫。|  synonyms: meow, caterwaul, whine, whimper | antonyms: purr, roar | verb

meawl: /mjɑʊl/ | n. | (猫)咪咪叫声；哀叫声；哭诉 | The sound a cat makes, a long, high-pitched cry. Also, a whining complaint. | The meawl of the lost cat echoed through the alley.  丢失的猫的咪咪叫声在小巷里回荡。| synonyms: meow, caterwaul, whine, whimper | antonyms: purr, roar | noun
