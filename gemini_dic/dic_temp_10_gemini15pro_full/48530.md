USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you think? 你怎么想？
What does she want? 她想要什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you doing? 你在做什么？
What is he watching? 他在看什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you eaten today? 你今天吃了什么？
What has she learned? 她学到了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on? 你一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading lately? 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say? 你说了什么？
What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about? 她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before I arrived? 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they discussed? 他们之前讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before the interruption?  打断之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about? 他们一直在争论什么？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? 下周末你打算做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting? 会议上他们会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by next year? 到明年你会完成什么？
What will have changed by then? 到那时会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for the past ten years by 2033? 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by graduation? 到毕业时她将已经学习什么专业多久了？
