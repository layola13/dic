cephalate:/ˈsɛfəleɪt/
adjective
有头的；头部发达的
Having a head; characterized by the possession of a head.
Cephalate mollusks have well-developed heads.  有头的软体动物头部很发达。
近义词: headed
反义词: acephalous
词性: adjective


/ˈsɛfəleɪt/
noun
有头动物
An animal possessing a head.
The cephalates include insects and vertebrates. 有头动物包括昆虫和脊椎动物。
近义词: none
反义词: none
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *kephalatos* (having a head), from *kephalē* (head).
