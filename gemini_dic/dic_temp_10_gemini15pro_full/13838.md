audits:/ˈɔːdɪts/| n. |审计，审核|An official inspection of an individual's or organization's accounts, typically by an independent body.|The company undergoes annual audits.  这家公司每年都进行审计。|synonyms: inspections, reviews, examinations|antonyms: none|noun
audits:/ˈɔːdɪts/| v. |审计，审核|Make an official inspection of (an individual's or organization's accounts).|They audit the company's finances every year. 他们每年都会审核公司的财务状况。|synonyms: inspect, review, examine|antonyms: none|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *audītus*, past participle of *audīre* "to hear." Originally, audits were conducted orally.
