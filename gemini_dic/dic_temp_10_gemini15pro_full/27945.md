canto:/ˈkæntoʊ/ (US) /ˈkæntəʊ/ (UK)| n. |(长诗的)篇; (史诗的)章; 歌曲; 圣歌|A section of a long poem, especially one telling a story, such as an epic poem. Also, a song or a hymn.|Example:  The first canto of Dante's Inferno describes the pilgrim's entry into Hell.  但丁《地狱篇》的第一章描述了朝圣者进入地狱的情景。

Example: The choir sang a beautiful canto.  唱诗班唱了一首优美的圣歌。|近义词：section, chapter, song, hymn|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
