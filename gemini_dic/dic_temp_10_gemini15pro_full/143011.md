outbid:/ˌaʊtˈbɪd/| v.|出价高于（某人）；胜过|To offer a higher price than (someone) at an auction or other bidding process.|例句：She outbid all her rivals for the antique vase. 她出价比所有竞争对手都高，买下了那只古董花瓶。|例句：He was outbid by a Japanese collector. 他被一位日本收藏家出价超过了。|近义词：outdo, surpass, overbid|反义词:underbid|词性:动词

词源: out- + bid  "out-" 表示超过，"bid" 表示出价
