overleaven:/ˌōvərˈlɛvən/ | vt.| 使过度发酵；使过度膨胀；夸张；过分渲染 | To cause (dough) to rise too much due to excessive leavening. To overdo or exaggerate something. | The baker overleavened the dough, resulting in a loaf of bread that was too airy.  面包师使面团过度发酵，导致烤出的面包太蓬松了。| 近义词：exaggerate, overstate, overdo | 反义词: understate, downplay | 词性: 动词

overleaven:/ˌōvərˈlɛvən/ | adj.| 过度发酵的；过度膨胀的 | (Of dough) risen too much due to excessive leavening. Exaggerated or overdone. | The overleavened dough spilled out of the bowl.  过度发酵的面团从碗里溢了出来。| 近义词：exaggerated, inflated | 反义词: understated | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From Old English *oferlēfnian, from ofer- 'over' + lēfnian 'to leaven'.
