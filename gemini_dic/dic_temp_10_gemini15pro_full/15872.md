A ballistic missile follows a parabolic trajectory during free flight. | North Korea has been testing ballistic missiles. 朝鲜一直在试验弹道导弹。 |Synonyms: guided missile, rocket | Antonyms: N/A | noun


ballistic:/bəˈlɪstɪk/ | adj. | 弹道的 | Relating to the flight of projectiles, especially bullets or missiles, after power has ceased to be applied.  Of or relating to ballistics. | The ballistic properties of the bullet were carefully analyzed. 子弹的弹道特性经过仔细分析。 | Synonyms: projectile, trajectory | Antonyms: N/A | adjective
