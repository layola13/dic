creakier: /ˈkriːkiər/ (US), /ˈkriːkɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|更嘎吱作响的|Comparative form of creaky: making a harsh, grating sound, typically when weight is applied or pressure exerted.|The stairs were much creakier than I remembered. 楼梯比我记忆中的嘎吱声更大了。|The old rocking chair became creakier with each passing year. 这把旧摇椅一年比一年嘎吱作响。|近义词：squeakier, rickety, wobbly|反义词：sturdy, stable, solid|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *creken, of imitative origin.
