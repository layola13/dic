cirrocumulus:/ˌsɪrəʊˈkjuːmjʊləs/ | adj. n.|卷积云|A type of cloud forming a thin white layer of small ripples. It is a high-altitude cloud formed of ice crystals.  It's often called "mackerel sky" because of its resemblance to fish scales.|例句：The cirrocumulus clouds drifted lazily across the sky. 卷积云懒洋洋地飘过天空。

例句：The appearance of cirrocumulus clouds can sometimes indicate an approaching weather change. 卷积云的出现有时预示着天气即将变化。|近义词：high cloud, mackerel sky|反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific cloud type) |词性: adjective, noun
