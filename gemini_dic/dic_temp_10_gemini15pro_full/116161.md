mascot:/ˈmæskɑːt/ (US), /ˈmæskɒt/ (UK)| n. |吉祥物|A person, animal, or object adopted by a group as a symbolic figure, especially to bring them good luck.|The team's mascot is a ferocious-looking lion.  这个队的吉祥物是一头看起来很凶猛的狮子。|Synonym: symbol, emblem, icon|Antonym: jinx, curse|noun
