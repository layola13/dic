dual:/ˈdjuːəl/ (US), /ˈdʒuːəl/ (UK)| adj.|双的；两重的；双重的|Having two parts, aspects, or functions.|This car has dual controls for both the driver and instructor. 这辆车有驾驶员和教练的双重控制装置。|例句：The room has dual aspect windows, offering views to both the east and west. 房间有双面窗户，可以看到东西两边的景色。

例句：She has dual citizenship in both France and the UK. 她拥有法国和英国的双重国籍。|近义词：double, twofold, twin|反义词：single, singular|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *dualis, from duo (“two”).
