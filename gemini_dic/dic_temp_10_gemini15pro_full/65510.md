eurostar:/ˈjʊərəʊstɑːr/ (UK) /ˈjʊroʊstɑːr/ (US)| n.|欧洲之星（高速列车）|A high-speed passenger rail service that links the United Kingdom with mainland Europe (France, Belgium, and the Netherlands) via the Channel Tunnel.|例句：I took the Eurostar from London to Paris. 我乘坐欧洲之星从伦敦到巴黎。

例句：The Eurostar is a convenient way to travel between London and Brussels. 欧洲之星是往返于伦敦和布鲁塞尔之间的一种便捷的旅行方式。

近义词：train, high-speed rail|反义词:|词性:noun
