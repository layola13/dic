flan:/flæn/
n.
烤布丁，果馅饼
An open pastry or sponge case containing a sweet or savory filling.
She baked a delicious apple flan. 她烤了一个美味的苹果馅饼。
近义词：tart, pie, quiche
反义词: (没有明确的反义词)
词性: 名词


flan:/flæn/
词源：From Old French *flaon (unattested), from Frankish *flado "flat cake," from Proto-Germanic *flaþō "flat object."  Cognate with Old High German flado "flat cake," German Fladen "flat cake," Dutch vlade "custard tart."
英文解析: A flan is an open pastry or sponge case containing a sweet or savory filling. It is typically baked in a shallow dish and can be filled with a variety of ingredients, including fruit, custard, cheese, or vegetables.
例句：
The restaurant's signature dessert was a caramel flan. 这家餐厅的招牌甜点是焦糖布丁。

My grandmother makes the best flan I've ever tasted. 我祖母做的烤布丁是我吃过最好吃的。


近义词：tart, pie, quiche

词性: 名词
