hurrahing:/həˈrɑːɪŋ/
| n.|欢呼，喝彩|The hurrahing of the crowd was deafening. 人群的欢呼声震耳欲聋。|
| v.|欢呼，喝彩|They were hurrahing for their team. 他们在为他们的队伍欢呼。|
|近义词：cheering, applauding, acclaiming|
|反义词：booing, jeering, hissing|
|词性: 动名词,现在分词|
|词源：From hurrah (interjection), imitative of a shout of joy or excitement.|
