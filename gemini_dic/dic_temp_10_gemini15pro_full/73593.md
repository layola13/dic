foretelling:/fɔːrˈtɛlɪŋ/
foretelling| n. |预言，预测|The act of predicting or prophesying future events.|Her foretelling of the disaster proved accurate. 她对灾难的预言被证明是准确的。|
foretelling| adj. |预示的，预兆的|Indicating or suggesting something that will happen in the future.|The dark clouds were foretelling of a storm. 乌云预示着暴风雨即将来临。|
Synonyms: predicting, prophesying, forecasting, auguring, portending
Antonyms:  hindcasting (technical term for projecting past conditions)
Etymology: From Old English *foretellan, from fore- "before" + tellan "to count, relate."
Word Type:  Noun, Adjective (present participle of the verb "foretell")
