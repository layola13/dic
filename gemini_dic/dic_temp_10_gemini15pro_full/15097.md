bacchante:/bəˈkæntiː/ /bəˈkɑːntiː/
noun
狂女，酒神的女祭司，(尤指艺术作品中的)女 Bacchus信徒
A female follower of Bacchus; a woman who participates in orgiastic rites.
The painting depicted a bacchante dancing with abandon. 这幅画描绘了一个狂女放肆地跳舞。
Synonyms: maenad, reveler, devotee
Antonyms: prude, ascetic
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *bacchante*, from Latin *bacchans*, present participle of *bacchārī* (“to celebrate the rites of Bacchus”), from *Bacchus* (“Bacchus, the Roman god of wine”).
