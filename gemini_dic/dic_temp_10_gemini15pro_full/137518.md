nontraditionalistic:/ˌnɑːnˌtrəˈdɪʃənəˈlɪstɪk/
adjective
非传统的
Not adhering to or in accordance with tradition; unconventional.  Breaking with established customs, beliefs, or practices.
Her nontraditionalistic approach to education emphasized hands-on learning and real-world experience. 她非传统的教育方法强调实践学习和实际经验。
Synonyms: unconventional, unorthodox, innovative, modern, progressive, avant-garde, groundbreaking
Antonyms: traditional, conventional, orthodox, conservative
词性: adjective
