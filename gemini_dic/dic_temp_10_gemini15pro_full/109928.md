libellulid:/ˌlɪbəˈljuːlɪd/| n. |蜻蜓科昆虫；蜻蜓|A dragonfly of the Libellulidae family, characterized by a robust body and broad, often brightly colored wings. |例句：The libellulid perched on a reed, its wings shimmering in the sunlight.  蜻蜓栖息在芦苇上，翅膀在阳光下闪闪发光。

例句：Several different species of libellulid were observed near the pond.  在池塘附近观察到了几种不同的蜻蜓。|近义词：dragonfly, skimmer |反义词: N/A|词性:noun


Etymology: From Modern Latin *Libellulidae, from Libellula (genus name), diminutive of Latin libella "level," from libra "balance, scales." The wings of dragonflies, when held outstretched, resemble a level or balance.
