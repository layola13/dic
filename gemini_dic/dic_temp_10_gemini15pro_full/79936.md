gnomonical:/nəˈmɒnɪk(ə)l/ | adj. | 日晷的；晷针的；与日晷有关的 | Relating to or resembling a gnomon (the part of a sundial that casts the shadow); also, related to the science of dialling (the art of constructing sundials). | The gnomonical projection is used in creating sundials.  日晷投影用于制造日晷。 |  The museum had a fascinating exhibit on gnomonical instruments.  博物馆里有一个关于日晷仪器的引人入胜的展览。| 近义词: sundial,  | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | 词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *gnōmon* "one that knows, discerner" (because the gnomon of a sundial indicates the time), from *gignōskein* "to know."
