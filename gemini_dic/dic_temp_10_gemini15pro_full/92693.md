icebreakers:/ˈaɪsˌbreɪkərz/| n. |破冰游戏，破冰活动；破冰船|A game or activity designed to relieve the tension or awkwardness between strangers.|We played some icebreakers to get everyone relaxed and chatting. 我们玩了一些破冰游戏，让大家放松下来聊天。|The cruise ship had a reinforced hull, designed for use as an icebreaker. 这艘游轮的船体经过加固，设计用作破冰船。|synonyms: ice-breaking activities, warm-up exercises, introductory games|antonyms: awkward silence, tension, formality|noun
