biogeny:/baɪˈɒdʒɪni/ | n. | 生物起源; 生源论 | The study of the origin of life. |  The theory of biogeny states that all living things come from other living things. 生物起源学说指出，所有生物都来自其他生物。|  Biogenesis is the principle underlying biogeny. 生物发生是生物起源的基本原理。| 近义词: abiogenesis | 反义词: abiogenesis | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Greek *bios* "life" + *-geny* "producing," from *gignesthai* "to be born, become."
