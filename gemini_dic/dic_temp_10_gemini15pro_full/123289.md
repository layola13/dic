monogamously: /məˈnɒɡəməsli/ /məˈnɑːɡəməsli/ | adv. | 一夫一妻地，单配偶地 | In a way that involves having only one spouse or sexual partner at a time. |  They lived monogamously for fifty years. 他们一夫一妻地生活了五十年。|  She chose to live monogamously. 她选择过一夫一妻的生活。 |  faithfully, exclusively | polygamously, promiscuously | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From mono- (one) + -gam- (marriage) + -ously (adverbial suffix).  "Mono-" comes from Greek "monos" (single, alone). "-gamy" comes from Greek "gamos" (marriage).
