diphenyl:/daɪˈfɛnəl/ | n. | 联苯，二苯基 | An organic compound with the formula (C6H5)2. Two phenyl rings connected by a single C-C bond. |  Diphenyl is used as a heat transfer agent. 联苯用作传热剂。

Diphenyl crystals are colorless. 联苯晶体是无色的。 | 近义词: biphenyl | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From *di-* (two) + *phenyl* (a univalent radical derived from benzene)
