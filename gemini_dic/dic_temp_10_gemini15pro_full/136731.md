nonsensicalnesses:/ˌnɒnˈsɛnsɪkəlnɪsɪz/
n.
胡说八道，荒谬
The state or quality of being nonsensical; absurdity.
His speech was filled with nonsensicalnesses, making it difficult to understand his point. 他的演讲充满了胡说八道，让人难以理解他的观点。
近义词：absurdities, silliness, foolishness
反义词：sense, logic, rationality
词性: noun

nonsensical:/nɒnˈsɛnsɪkəl/
adj.
荒谬的，无意义的
Having no meaning; making no sense.
The plot of the movie was utterly nonsensical. 这部电影的情节完全荒谬。
近义词：absurd, illogical, meaningless
反义词：sensible, logical, meaningful
词性: adjective

nonsensicality:/ˌnɒnˈsɛnsɪˈkælɪti/
n.
荒谬性，无意义
The quality or state of being nonsensical.
The nonsensicality of his argument was apparent to everyone. 他的论点的荒谬性对每个人来说都是显而易见的。
近义词：absurdity, meaninglessness
反义词：sense, logic
词性: noun


词源:
From nonsensical + -ness. Ultimately from Latin non + sensus (sense).
