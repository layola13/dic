feminines:/ˈfɛmənɪnz/| n.|女性，阴性词|A group of nouns or adjectives in a language that are associated with the female gender.|The feminines of actor, waiter, and host are actress, waitress, and hostess.|Synonyms: female(s), women, girls|Antonyms: masculines|Noun
