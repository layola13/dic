baiters:/ˈbeɪtərz/| n.|煽动者，引诱者，用饵钓鱼的人|People who deliberately provoke others, especially online, are called baiters.  故意挑衅他人，尤其是在网上挑衅他人的人，被称为煽动者。|Online baiters often create inflammatory posts to stir up controversy. 网上煽动者经常发表煽动性言论来引发争议。|The experienced fisherman skillfully used baiters to attract fish. 经验丰富的渔夫巧妙地使用鱼饵来吸引鱼。|synonyms: instigators, provokers, trolls, teasers|antonyms: peacemakers, mediators, conciliators|noun
