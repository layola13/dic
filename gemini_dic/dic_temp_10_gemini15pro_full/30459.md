centrifuge: /ˈsɛntrɪfjuːdʒ/ (US) /ˈsɛntrɪfjuːdʒ/ (UK)| n. v. |离心机，离心分离机；用离心机分离|A machine with a rapidly rotating container that applies centrifugal force to its contents, typically to separate fluids of different densities or liquids from solids.|The biologist used a centrifuge to separate the blood components. 生物学家使用离心机分离血液成分。|近义词：separator, extractor|反义词: N/A|词性:名词, 动词

centrifuge: /ˈsɛntrɪfjuːdʒ/ (US) /ˈsɛntrɪfjuːdʒ/ (UK)| v. |用离心机分离|To rotate something at high speed in a centrifuge to separate its components.|The chemist centrifuged the solution to isolate the precipitate. 化学家用离心机分离溶液以分离沉淀物。|近义词：spin, whirl, rotate|反义词: N/A|词性:动词
