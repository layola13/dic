all-important:/ɔːlɪmˈpɔːrtənt/
adjective
至关重要的，极其重要的
of the greatest importance; crucial.
例句：Securing financial backing is all-important for the success of this project.  为这个项目的成功，确保资金支持至关重要。

例句：Time management is all-important when you're a student.  作为学生，时间管理至关重要。


近义词：crucial, essential, vital, critical, paramount
反义词：unimportant, insignificant, trivial
词性: adjective
