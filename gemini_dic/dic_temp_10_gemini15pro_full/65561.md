eutrophy:/ˈjuːtrəfi/ /ˈjuːtroʊfi/| n. |富营养化|A condition in a body of water characterized by excessive nutrient enrichment, often due to runoff from land, leading to algal blooms, oxygen depletion, and the death of aquatic life.|The excessive use of fertilizers has contributed to the eutrophy of the lake. 过度使用化肥导致了湖泊的富营养化。|近义词：hypertrophication|反义词:oligotrophy|词性:noun

eutrophic:/juːˈtrɒfɪk/ /juːˈtroʊfɪk/| adj. |富营养的|Characterized by excessive nutrient enrichment.|The eutrophic lake was covered in green algae. 富营养化的湖泊覆盖着绿藻。|近义词：hypertrophic|反义词:oligotrophic|词性:adjective
