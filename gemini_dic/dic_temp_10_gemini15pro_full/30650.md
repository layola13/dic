cerises:/səˈriːz/
n.
cherries
Sweet, fleshy red fruit of the Prunus cerasus tree.
例句：The cerises were ripe and juicy. 樱桃熟透多汁。

例句：She baked a pie with fresh cerises. 她用新鲜樱桃烤了一个派。

近义词：cherries
反义词: N/A
词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *cerise*, from Vulgar Latin *ceresia*, alteration of Late Latin *cerasum*, from Latin *cerasus* "cherry tree," from Ancient Greek *κερασός (kerasós)*.
