feather-brained:/ˈfeðərˌbreɪnd/| adj.|轻浮的；愚蠢的；缺心眼的|Silly or frivolous; not very intelligent.  Origin: late 16th century: from feather + brain + -ed, suggesting that the brain is as light and insubstantial as a feather.|She made a feather-brained decision to quit her job without another one lined up.|近义词：silly, frivolous, foolish, giddy, flighty, scatterbrained, dizzy, ditzy|反义词：sensible, intelligent, wise, prudent|词性:形容词
