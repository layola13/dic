morling: /ˈmɔːrlɪŋ/ | n. | 羊毛碎屑，羊毛废料 | Wool fibers and bits of fleece that accumulate as waste in the process of shearing or working with wool. | The floor of the shearing shed was covered in morling. 剪羊毛棚的地板上满是羊毛碎屑。 | synonyms: wool waste, fleece scraps, shearing waste | antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, but consider: finished wool product, clean fleece) | noun
