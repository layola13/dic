bushwhacks:/ˈbʊʃˌwæks/| v.| 丛林开路；伏击；（非正式）艰苦地前进 |To cut one's way through dense woods or bushes. To ambush someone from a concealed position in the woods. To proceed with difficulty or clumsily, especially in unfamiliar territory. |例句：The explorers bushwhacked through the dense jungle. 探险家们在茂密的丛林中开路。

例句：The soldiers were bushwhacked by the enemy in the forest. 士兵们在森林里被敌人伏击了。|近义词：ambush, waylay, hack, clear, struggle, stumble |反义词: stroll, sail, glide |词性: verb

A difficult or clumsy progression, especially in unfamiliar territory. |例句：The team faced a difficult bushwhack through the undergrowth. 团队面临着穿越灌木丛的艰难跋涉。

例句：The bushwhack was successful, and the enemy was captured. 伏击成功，敌人被俘虏了。|近义词：ambush, attack, struggle |反义词: walk, stroll |词性: noun
