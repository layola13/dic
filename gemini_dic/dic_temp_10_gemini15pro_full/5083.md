allantoic:/ˌælənˈtɔɪɪk/
adjective
尿囊的，羊膜的
Relating to the allantois, a sac-like structure that develops from the hindgut of the embryo in reptiles, birds, and mammals.
The allantoic membrane plays a crucial role in gas exchange and waste removal for the developing embryo.  尿囊膜在发育中胚胎的气体交换和废物清除中起着至关重要的作用。

The allantoic cavity stores metabolic waste produced by the embryo. 尿囊腔储存胚胎产生的代谢废物。

Synonym:  allantoidian
Antonym:  None
词性: adjective
