exhaustingly:/ɪɡˈzɔːstɪŋli/| adv.|令人疲惫地，筋疲力尽地|in a way that makes you feel very tired|The journey was exhaustingly long. 这趟旅程长得令人筋疲力尽。|近义词: tiringly, arduously, laboriously|反义词: refreshingly, invigoratingly|词性:副词

词源: From exhaust + -ing + -ly.  "Exhaust" comes from the Latin "exhaurire" meaning "to draw out completely, drain, empty."
