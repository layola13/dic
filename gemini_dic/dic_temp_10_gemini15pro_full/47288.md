de-employed:/ˌdiːɪmˈplɔɪd/
adjective
失业的，下岗的
Deprived of employment, typically as a result of company downsizing or economic downturn.  No longer employed, often through no fault of one's own.
Many skilled workers were de-employed during the recession. 许多熟练工人在经济衰退期间失业了。
The factory closed, leaving hundreds of workers de-employed. 工厂关闭了，数百名工人失业了。
近义词: unemployed, jobless, laid-off, redundant
反义词: employed, working
