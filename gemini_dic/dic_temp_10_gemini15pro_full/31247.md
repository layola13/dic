chanty:/ˈʃænti/ (US), /ˈtʃænti/ (UK)| n.| 船夫曲；劳动号子|A rhythmical song sung by sailors in time with their work, especially while hoisting sails or weighing anchor.|The sailors sang a chanty as they hauled the ropes.|近义词：work song, sea shanty|反义词: (没有直接的反义词)|noun
