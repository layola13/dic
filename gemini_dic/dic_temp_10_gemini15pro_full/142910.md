out-roar:/ˌaʊtˈrɔːr/
|v. |胜过…的吼叫；大声盖过|To shout louder than someone or something else.|The crowd out-roared the speaker. |人群的喊叫声盖过了演讲者。|
out-roar:/ˌaʊtˈrɔːr/
|n. |更大的吼叫声|A louder roar than another.|The out-roar of the crowd was deafening. |人群的吼叫声震耳欲聋。|
out-roar: /ˌaʊtˈrɔːr/
|词源| From "out-" (meaning exceeding or surpassing) + "roar" (a loud, deep cry).|
out-roar: /ˌaʊtˈrɔːr/
|近义词|outcry, yell, shout, bellow, overpower|
out-roar: /ˌaʊtˈrɔːr/
|反义词|whisper, murmur, mutter|
