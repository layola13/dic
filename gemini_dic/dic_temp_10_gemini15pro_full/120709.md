misalignment:/ˌmɪsəˈlaɪnmənt/| n. |不对准，错位，偏差|A state of being out of line or not properly adjusted.  The condition of not matching or agreeing with something else.|The misalignment of the wheels caused the car to pull to one side. 车轮错位导致汽车向一侧跑偏。|synonyms: discordance, disagreement, disparity, disharmony, imbalance, incompatibility, incongruity, inconsistency, mismatch, variance|antonyms: alignment, agreement, concordance, harmony, balance, compatibility, congruity, consistency, match|noun
