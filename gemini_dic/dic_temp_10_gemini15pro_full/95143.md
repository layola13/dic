inappropriacy:/ˌɪnəˈproʊpriəsi/ | n. |不恰当，不得体|The inappropriacy of his remarks shocked everyone. 他的言论不得体，震惊了所有人。|例句：His inappropriacy of dress for the funeral was noted by several people.  他穿的衣服不适合参加葬礼，被几个人注意到了。

例句：The teacher pointed out the inappropriacy of using slang in a formal essay. 老师指出了在正式论文中使用俚语的不恰当之处。|近义词：unsuitability, impropriety, unfitness |反义词: suitability, appropriateness, propriety |词性:noun

inappropriate:/ˌɪnəˈproʊpriət/ | adj. |不恰当的，不得体的|It would be inappropriate to discuss such matters in public. 在公共场合讨论这种事情是不合适的。|例句：Wearing jeans to a wedding is considered inappropriate. 穿牛仔裤参加婚礼被认为是不合适的。

例句：His inappropriate joke caused a lot of embarrassment. 他不合时宜的笑话引起了很多尴尬。|近义词：unsuitable, improper, unfit |反义词: suitable, appropriate, proper, fitting |词性:adjective
