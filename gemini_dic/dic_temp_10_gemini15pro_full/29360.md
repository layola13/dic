castrates:/ˈkæˌstreɪts/
verb
阉割；删除（令人不快的部分）
removes the testicles of a male animal or human.  It can also mean to remove parts of a book, film, etc. considered offensive or unsuitable.
例句：The farmer castrates the young bulls to make them less aggressive. 农民阉割小公牛以减少它们的攻击性。

例句：The censors castrated the film by removing all the violent scenes.  审查员通过删除所有暴力场景来阉割这部电影。
近义词：geld, neuter, expurgate, censor
反义词：preserve
