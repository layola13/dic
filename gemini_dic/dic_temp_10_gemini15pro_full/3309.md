afflict:/əˈflɪkt/| v. |使痛苦，折磨|to cause pain or suffering to; trouble greatly|例句：The disease afflicts millions of people worldwide. 这种疾病折磨着全世界数百万人。|近义词：trouble, distress, torment, plague|反义词：comfort, soothe, relieve|词性：动词
afflicts:/əˈflɪkts/| v. |使痛苦，折磨（第三人称单数形式）|Third-person singular present tense form of "afflict"|例句：Drought afflicts the region every summer.  干旱每年夏天都困扰着这个地区。|近义词：troubles, distresses, torments, plagues|反义词：comforts, soothes, relieves|词性：动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English afflicten, from Old French aflicter, from Latin afflictus, past participle of affligere ("to strike down, damage, weaken, overthrow"), from ad- ("to, toward") + fligere ("to strike").
