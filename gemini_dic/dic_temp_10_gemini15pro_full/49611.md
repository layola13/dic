demi-lion:/ˈdɛmiˌlaɪən/| n. |半狮; 狮身人面像的一种; 半狮半鹫的怪兽|A mythical creature with the head and forequarters of a lion and the hindquarters of another animal, typically an eagle or a horse.  It is a common figure in heraldry and architectural ornamentation. |The demi-lion ramped on the shield. 盾牌上饰有跃立的半狮。|近义词：griffin, wyvern|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

demi-lion:/ˈdɛmiˌlaɪən/| adj. |半狮的|Resembling or characteristic of a demi-lion. |The demi-lion figure guarded the entrance. 半狮塑像守护着入口。|近义词：leonine|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective
