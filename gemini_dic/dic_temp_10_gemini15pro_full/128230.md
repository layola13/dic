neurodivergence:/ˌnʊərəʊdaɪˈvɜːdʒəns/ /ˌnʊroʊdaɪˈvɜːrdʒəns/| n. |神经差异|The state of having a brain that functions in ways that are different from what society considers “typical.”  It encompasses conditions like autism, ADHD, dyslexia, and Tourette's Syndrome.|例句：Neurodivergence is gaining more recognition and acceptance in society.  神经差异正在社会中得到越来越多的认可和接纳。

例句：Many companies are now actively seeking to employ neurodivergent individuals. 许多公司现在正积极寻求雇佣神经分歧的个人。|近义词：neurodiversity, difference, variation|反义词：neurotypicality, normalcy|词性:noun
