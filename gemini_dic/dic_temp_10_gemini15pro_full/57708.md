draying:/ˈdreɪɪŋ/
n.|搬运;用货车运输|The act of transporting goods, typically by dray.  Specifically, the business of hauling goods short distances by dray or truck.|The draying of the beer to the pub was delayed by the snowstorm.  啤酒用货车运往酒吧的行程因暴风雪而延误。|haulage, cartage, transportation|loading, unloading|noun
draying:/ˈdreɪɪŋ/
v.|用货车运输;搬运|(Present Participle of 'dray')  Transporting something by dray or truck. |They were draying the furniture to the new house. 他们正用货车把家具搬到新家。|hauling, carting, transporting|loading, unloading|verb
