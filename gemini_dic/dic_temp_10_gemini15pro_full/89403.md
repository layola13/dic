homostyled:/ˌhəʊməˈstaɪld/| adj.|同型花柱的|Having styles of the same length as the stamens, facilitating self-pollination.|Many apple varieties are homostyled, which means they can pollinate themselves.|近义词:self-pollinating, self-fertile|反义词:heterostyled|词性:形容词
