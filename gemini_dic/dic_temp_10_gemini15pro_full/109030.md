leg-pullers:/ˈleɡˌpʊlərz/
n.
开玩笑的人; 恶作剧者; 哄骗者
People who tease or try to fool others by telling them something untrue, often as a joke.
"Don't believe him, he's just a leg-puller."  别相信他，他只是在开玩笑。
jokers, teasers, pranksters
serious people, honest people
noun
