lingulate:/ˈlɪŋɡjʊlət/ /ˈlɪŋɡjʊˌleɪt/
adjective
舌形的；舌状的
shaped like a tongue or strap.
The lingulate bracts of the plant are a distinctive feature.  这种植物舌状的苞片是一个显著特征。
The surgeon pointed out the lingulate process of the bone.  外科医生指出了骨头的舌状突起。
Synonyms: tongue-shaped, strap-shaped, ligulate
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, consider describing the opposite shape, e.g., round, ovate, etc.)
词性: 形容词
