matting:/ˈmætɪŋ/| n.|垫子；衬垫；无光泽的表面；（图片的）卡纸|A piece of coarse material used as a floor covering, for packing fragile objects, or for other protective purposes.  A dull or non-glossy surface, especially on a photograph or painting. A cardboard border around a picture.|The photographer used matting to frame the print.|近义词：padding, cushioning, liner, backing, mount|反义词:gloss, shine|noun

matting:/ˈmætɪŋ/| v.|(使)缠结；(使)交叠；(使)无光泽|Become tangled together in a thick mass; cover (a photograph or picture) with a mat or mount.|The cat's fur was matted with mud.  He matted the photograph before framing it.|近义词：tangle, snarl, matte|反义词: untangle, smooth, gloss|verb
