blood-relative:/ˈblʌd ˈrɛlətɪv/
n.
血亲，近亲
A person who is related to another by birth rather than by marriage.
My closest blood relative is my brother. 我最近的血亲是我的兄弟。

He visited his blood relatives during the holidays. 他在假期期间拜访了他的血亲。

近义词:kin, kinsman, family member
反义词:non-relative, unrelated person
词性:noun
