diplomate:/ˈdɪpləmæt/ (US), /ˈdɪpləˌmæt/ (UK)| n.|外交官，外交家|A person appointed by a state to conduct diplomacy with another state or international organization.|例句：The diplomat negotiated a peace treaty.  这位外交官谈判了一项和平条约。

例句：She is a skilled diplomat with years of experience. 她是一位经验丰富、技术娴熟的外交官。

近义词：envoy, ambassador, negotiator|反义词: warmonger|词性:noun
