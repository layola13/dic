nonimputatively:/ˌnɒnɪmˈpjuːtətɪvli/
| adv. |非归罪地，不归咎地| In a way that does not attribute blame or fault.  It describes actions or situations where responsibility or blame is not assigned to a particular individual or group. |The issue was resolved nonimputatively, with both parties agreeing to move forward without assigning blame.| 近义词:blamelessly, irreproachably, faultlessly | 反义词:culpably, blamefully |词性:副词
