monoamine:/ˈmɒnəʊəmiːn/ (UK), /ˈmɑːnoʊəmiːn/ (US)| n. |单胺|A class of neurotransmitters and neuromodulators that contain a single amine group connected to an aromatic ring by a two-carbon chain.|Dopamine is a monoamine that plays a role in reward and motivation. 多巴胺是一种在奖励和动机方面起作用的单胺。|Synonyms: biogenic amine|Antonyms: None|Noun
