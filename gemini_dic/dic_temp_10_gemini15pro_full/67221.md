expressways:/ɪkˈsprɛsweɪz/
|n.|高速公路|A road, typically limited-access, designed for high-speed vehicular traffic.|We took the expressway to get to the city faster. 我们走高速公路更快到达了城市。|近义词：freeway, highway, motorway, thruway|反义词：local roads, byways|noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From express + way.  "Express" in this context means "direct" or "fast," originating from the idea of an "express train."
