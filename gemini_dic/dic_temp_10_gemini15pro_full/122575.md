moderato:/ˌmɒdəˈrɑːtəʊ/ (UK), /ˌmɑːdəˈrɑːtoʊ/ (US)| adj. adv.| 中等的；适度的；(音乐) 中板的 |moderate in tempo, quality, or intensity|Play the piece at a moderato tempo.  用中板速度演奏这首曲子。|Synonyms: moderate, medium, temperate|Antonyms: fast, slow, extreme|adjective, adverb

moderato:/ˌmɒdəˈrɑːtəʊ/ (UK), /ˌmɑːdəˈrɑːtoʊ/ (US)| n.| (音乐) 中板 |A tempo marking indicating a moderate speed, usually between andante and allegro.|The symphony's second movement is a moderato.|Synonyms: medium tempo|Antonyms: allegro, presto|noun
