debunkery:/diːˈbʌŋkəri/| n.|揭穿；拆穿；驳斥|The act of debunking; exposing false claims and popular misconceptions.|His book is a work of thorough debunkery, exposing the myths surrounding the legend.|近义词：exposure, refutation, debunking|反义词：propaganda, mythmaking|词性:名词

Etymology:  From the verb "debunk" + "-ery" (a suffix forming nouns denoting a place or practice). "Debunk" itself comes from "de-" (meaning "remove" or "reverse") + "bunk" (meaning "nonsense").
