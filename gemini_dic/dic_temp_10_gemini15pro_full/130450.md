nonabsorbabilities:/ˌnɒnəbˌsɔːrbəˈbɪlətiz/
n.
不可吸收性；非吸收性
The nonabsorbabilities of certain materials make them suitable for specific applications.  某些材料的不可吸收性使它们适用于特定的应用。
This study investigates the nonabsorbabilities of various synthetic fabrics. 这项研究调查了各种合成纤维的不可吸收性。
近义词: impermeabilities, imperviousities
反义词: absorbencies, permeabilities
词性: noun (plural)

Etymology:
non- (prefix) + absorb (verb) + -ability (suffix) + -ities (plural suffix)

"non-" negates the meaning of the root word.  "Absorb" comes from the Latin "absorbēre," meaning "to swallow up." "-ability" transforms the verb into a noun denoting capacity or capability. "-ities" indicates the plural form.
