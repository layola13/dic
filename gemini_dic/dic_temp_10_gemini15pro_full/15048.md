baby-like:/ˈbeɪbiˌlaɪk/| adj.|婴儿般的，幼稚的|Resembling or characteristic of a baby, especially in behavior; infantile.|She spoke in a baby-like voice. 她用婴儿般的声音说话。|近义词：childish, infantile, immature|反义词：mature, adult, grown-up|词性:形容词
Etymology: baby + like.  "Baby" comes from Middle English *babi*, likely imitative of baby sounds. "Like" comes from Old English *gelic* meaning "similar, resembling."
