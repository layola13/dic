miscaptioned:/ˌmɪsˈkæpʃənd/
adjective
配有错误字幕的；贴错标签的
incorrectly captioned; having a wrong caption
The photo was miscaptioned as "Sunset over the Pacific" when it was actually a sunrise over the Atlantic. 这张照片被错误地标注为“太平洋上的日落”，而实际上是大西洋上的日出。

miscaptioned:/ˌmɪsˈkæpʃənd/
verb (past participle)
被错误地加上标题或字幕
to provide (a picture, photograph, etc.) with a wrong caption
Several photos in the album were miscaptioned. 相册中的几张照片标题错了。


Synonyms: mislabeled, wrongly captioned, incorrectly described
Antonyms: correctly captioned, accurately described
词性: adjective, past participle of the verb "miscaption"
