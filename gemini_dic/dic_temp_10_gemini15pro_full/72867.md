folksy:/ˈfəʊksi/ (UK) /ˈfoʊksi/ (US)| adj.| 平易近人的；亲切的；有民间风味的；通俗的|Having a friendly, informal, and unpretentious manner; characteristic of traditional folk culture.|He had a folksy way of talking that made everyone feel comfortable. 他说话的方式很平易近人，让每个人都感到很舒服。|例句：

The restaurant had a folksy atmosphere.
这家餐厅有一种亲切的氛围。

She gave a folksy speech about her childhood in the countryside.
她做了一个关于她乡村童年的通俗演讲。

|近义词：friendly, informal, down-to-earth, unpretentious, amiable, affable, genial, approachable, common, simple, homespun, rustic|反义词：formal, sophisticated, pretentious, aloof, standoffish|词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From folk + -y (suffix forming adjectives).  "Folk" comes from Old English "folc," meaning "people."
