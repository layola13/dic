disconsolacy:/dɪskənˈsoʊləsi/
n.
inconsolable grief; extreme sadness
A state of deep sadness and unhappiness, often caused by the death of someone you love.
Her inconsolacy after her husband's death was profound. 她丈夫去世后，她悲痛欲绝。
Near synonyms: grief, sorrow, sadness, heartbreak, bereavement, desolation, despair
Antonyms: joy, happiness, elation, comfort, solace
noun


disconsolate:/dɪsˈkɒnsəlɪt/
adj.
without consolation or comfort; unhappy
Extremely sad and unable to be comforted.
The disconsolate widow sat alone in her darkened room. 伤心欲绝的寡妇独自坐在昏暗的房间里。
Near synonyms: heartbroken, dejected, despondent, miserable, wretched, inconsolable
Antonyms: cheerful, happy, joyful, elated, comforted
