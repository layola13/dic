hereditariness:/həˌredɪˈtɛrɪnəs/
n.
遗传性，可遗传性
The quality of being heritable; the extent to which a characteristic is heritable.
The hereditariness of eye color is well established. 眼睛颜色的遗传性已得到充分证实。
Synonyms: inheritability, transmissibility
Antonyms: acquired characteristic
