enal:/ˈiːnæl/ | adj. | 烯基的，烯属的 | Relating to or containing an alkene, a hydrocarbon with a carbon-carbon double bond. |  The enal group is responsible for the characteristic odor of many aldehydes. 烯基使许多醛类具有独特的香气。| Example:  The synthesis of this enal was challenging.  合成这种烯醛极具挑战性。| Synonym: alkenyl | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps saturated) | word type: adjective

词源: From en- (double bond) + -al (aldehyde).  来自en-（双键）+ -al（醛）。
