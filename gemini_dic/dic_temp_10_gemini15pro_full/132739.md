nondoctrinaire:/ˌnɒnˌdɒktrɪˈnɛː(r)/ | adj. | 非教条的，不拘泥于教条的 | Not rigidly adhering to a set of doctrines or theories; pragmatic.  |  The senator took a nondoctrinaire approach to the issue, considering all sides before making a decision.  这位参议员对这个问题采取了非教条主义的态度，在做出决定之前考虑了各方面的意见。 |  Example: He has a nondoctrinaire approach to politics. 他对政治的态度不拘泥于教条。| Synonyms: pragmatic, practical, flexible, open-minded, adaptable | Antonyms: doctrinaire, dogmatic, rigid, inflexible, unyielding | 词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + doctrinaire (from French, from doctrine 'doctrine').  最终来源于拉丁语doctrina，意思是“教学，教义”。
