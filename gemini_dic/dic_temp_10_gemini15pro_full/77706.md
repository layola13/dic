gemless:/ˈdʒɛmlɪs/
adjective
无宝石的；无装饰的
lacking gems; unadorned
The gemless ring was simple but elegant.  这枚无宝石的戒指朴素却优雅。
近义词:plain, undecorated, unadorned
反义词: jeweled, ornate, decorated
词性:adjective

词源: gem (宝石) + -less (无...的)
