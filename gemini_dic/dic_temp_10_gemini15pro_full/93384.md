illiberalnesses:/ɪˈlɪbərəlnəsɪz/
|n.|偏狭；缺乏雅量；不宽容|The illiberalnesses of the regime were evident in its suppression of free speech.|该政权对言论自由的压制暴露了其偏狭性。|
|Synonyms:|intolerance, narrow-mindedness, bigotry, prejudice, dogmatism|
|Antonyms:|tolerance, open-mindedness, liberality, broad-mindedness, magnanimity|
|Etymology:|From illiberal + -ness.  "illiberal" comes from the Latin "in-" (not) + "liberalis" (liberal), meaning "not befitting a free person."|
|Word Type:|Noun|
