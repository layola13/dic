conveyance:/kənˈveɪəns/
noun
运输, 运输工具, 财产转让
The act of conveying; transmission; communication.  A means of conveying, especially a vehicle; transportation. The legal transfer of property from one owner to another.
The bus is a common conveyance in this city.  这辆公共汽车是这个城市常见的交通工具。
Conveyance of goods by sea is cheaper than by air.  海运货物比空运便宜。
The conveyance of the property was completed last week.  房产的转让上周完成了。
synonyms: transport, transportation, vehicle, transfer, transmission
antonyms: retention, withholding
