hypermoral:/ˌhaɪpərˈmɔːrəl/ |adj.| 过于道德的，道德洁癖的 | Excessively or rigidly moral; exhibiting an exaggerated concern with morality.  This word suggests an excessive focus on moral principles, often to the point of being judgmental or self-righteous. |  He had a hypermoral attitude towards everything, constantly criticizing others' behavior. 他对每件事都抱有过于道德的态度，不断批评别人的行为。

The book's hypermoral protagonist was so concerned with doing the right thing that he alienated everyone around him. 这本书中道德洁癖的主人公一心想做正确的事，以至于疏远了周围所有人。 | 近义词: puritanical, moralistic, self-righteous, priggish | 反义词: immoral, amoral, unethical | 词性: adjective
