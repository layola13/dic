fungivorous:/fʌŋˈdʒɪvərəs/
adjective
食真菌的
Feeding on fungi; mushroom-eating.
The fungivorous beetle plays a vital role in the forest ecosystem.  这种食真菌的甲虫在森林生态系统中起着至关重要的作用。
Fungivorous insects help break down decaying organic matter. 食真菌的昆虫有助于分解腐烂的有机物。
近义词:mycophagous
反义词:N/A
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *fungus* (fungi) + *-vorus* (eating), from *vorare* (to devour).
