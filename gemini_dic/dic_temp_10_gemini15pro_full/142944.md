out-win:/ˌaʊtˈwɪn/
verb
胜过，以更好的表现击败
To defeat or surpass someone by performing better.
Team A out-winned Team B in the championship.  A队在锦标赛中胜过了B队。
The company out-winned its competitors by offering superior customer service. 该公司通过提供优质的客户服务胜过竞争对手。
近义词: outdo, outperform, surpass, beat, defeat
反义词: lose to, be defeated by
词性: verb
