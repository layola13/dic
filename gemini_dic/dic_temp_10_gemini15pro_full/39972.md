consubstantiality:/ˌkɒnsəbˌstænʃiˈælɪti/ /ˌkɑːnsəbˌstænʃiˈælɪti/| n.|同体，同质，同一实体|The doctrine of consubstantiality is central to Christian theology.  同质说是基督教神学的核心。|例句：The consubstantiality of the Father and the Son is a core belief in Christianity. 圣父和圣子同体是基督教的核心信仰。

例句：The concept of consubstantiality is difficult to grasp for those outside the faith. 对于信仰之外的人来说，同质的概念很难理解。|近义词：unity, oneness, identity, sameness|反义词: difference, separateness, otherness|词性:noun

Etymology->
From Late Latin *consubstantialitas*, from consubstantialis ("of the same substance") + -itas.  Equivalent to con- + substantial + -ity.
