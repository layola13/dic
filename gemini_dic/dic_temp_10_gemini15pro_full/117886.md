melody:/ˈmɛlədi/| n.|旋律，曲调|A sequence of single notes that is musically satisfying. |The melody of the song was haunting and beautiful. 这首歌的旋律优美动听。|近义词：tune, air, theme|反义词: dissonance, noise|词性:noun

melodies:/ˈmɛlədiz/| n.|旋律，曲调（melody的复数形式）|Plural form of melody, referring to multiple sequences of single notes that are musically satisfying.|She played several beautiful melodies on the piano. 她在钢琴上演奏了几首优美的旋律。|近义词：tunes, airs, themes|反义词: dissonances, noises|词性:noun
