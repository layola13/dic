orthocenter: /ˈɔːrθəˌsɛntər/ | n. | 垂心 | The point where the three altitudes of a triangle intersect. | The orthocenter of an acute triangle lies inside the triangle.  这个锐角三角形的垂心位于三角形内部。

The orthocenter of this obtuse triangle lies outside the triangle.  这个钝角三角形的垂心位于三角形外部。 | synonym:  none (highly specific term) | antonym: none | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From ortho- + center. Ortho- comes from Greek orthos meaning "straight," "right," or "correct."  Center comes from Latin centrum meaning "center."
