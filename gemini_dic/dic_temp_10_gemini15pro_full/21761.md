boatman:/ˈboʊtmən/ | n. | 船夫；摆渡人 | A person who operates or works on a boat, especially one who transports passengers or goods across a river or lake.  | The boatman ferried us across the river. 船夫把我们渡过了河。 |  The boatman skillfully navigated the narrow canal.  船夫熟练地驾驶着小船穿过狭窄的运河。|  Synonyms:  ferryman, oarsman, waterman | Antonyms: passenger | noun
