dispatcher:/dɪˈspætʃər/ | n. | 派遣员，调度员 | A person whose job is to receive messages and organize the movement of people or vehicles, especially emergency vehicles. |  The dispatcher sent an ambulance to the scene of the accident.  派遣员派了一辆救护车到事故现场。| synonyms: controller, coordinator, director | antonyms: receiver, recipient | noun

dispatcher:/dɪˈspætʃər/ | n. | (消息、信件等的)发送者，发送器 | A device that sends out electronic signals. | The dispatcher sent a signal to the train.  调度员向火车发送了信号。| synonyms: transmitter, sender | antonyms: receiver | noun
