distillers:/dɪˈstɪlərz/
n.|蒸馏酒制造商；酿酒厂|People who manufacture distilled alcoholic beverages, often working in a distillery.  The term can also refer to the distillery itself.|The distillers in Scotland are famous for their whisky. 苏格兰的酿酒厂以其威士忌闻名。|synonyms: brewers, vintners, liquor manufacturers|antonyms: consumers, drinkers|noun

distiller:/dɪˈstɪlər/
n.|蒸馏器；蒸馏酒制造商；酿酒厂|A person or company that produces distilled alcoholic beverages, or an apparatus used for distilling.|The distiller uses copper stills to make their gin.  这家酿酒厂使用铜制蒸馏器来酿造杜松子酒。|synonyms: brewer, vintner, liquor manufacturer|antonyms: consumer, drinker|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *distillour*, from Anglo-French *distillour*, from Old French *destillere*, from Medieval Latin *distillator*, agent noun from Latin *distillare* (“to distill”), from *de-* (“down, away”) + *stillare* (“to drip, trickle”), from *stilla* (“a drop”).
