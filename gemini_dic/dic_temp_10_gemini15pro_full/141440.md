opianic: /oʊpiˈænɪk/ | adj. |鸦片的；含有鸦片的 | Relating to or containing opium. | Opianic acid is a derivative of opium. 鸦片酸是鸦片的衍生物。 | The opianic effects of the drug were immediately apparent. 这种药物的鸦片作用立刻显现出来。 | 近义词: narcotic, opiate | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, consider "sobering") | 词性: adjective

词源: From French *opianique*, from *opium* + *-ique*. Ultimately from Latin *opium*, from Greek *ópion*.
