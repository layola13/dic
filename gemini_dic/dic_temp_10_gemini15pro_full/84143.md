half-normal:/ˌhɑːfˈnɔːrməl/ | adj. | 半正态的 |  Relating to or characterized by a half-normal distribution, a probability distribution obtained by taking the absolute value of a normally distributed variable with mean zero.  | The residuals of the model followed a half-normal distribution.  模型的残差服从半正态分布。 | The half-normal plot indicated no outliers. 半正态图显示没有异常值。 |  Near-normal | N/A | adjective
