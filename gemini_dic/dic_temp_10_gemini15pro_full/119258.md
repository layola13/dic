michelangelo:/ˌmaɪkəlˈændʒəloʊ/ /ˌmɪkəlˈændʒəloʊ/| n.|米开朗基罗|An Italian sculptor, painter, architect, and poet of the High Renaissance born in the Republic of Florence, who exerted an unparalleled influence on the development of Western art.|Michelangelo's David is one of the most famous sculptures in the world. 米开朗基罗的大卫是世界上最著名的雕塑之一。

|近义词:artist, sculptor, painter, architect, genius|反义词: (No direct antonym for a person. Could consider "amateur" in terms of skill level.)|词性:noun

michelangelesque:/ˌmaɪkəlˌændʒəˈlesk/ /ˌmɪkəlˌændʒəˈlesk/| adj.|米开朗基罗式的；像米开朗基罗作品的|Having a grand or heroic quality reminiscent of the works of Michelangelo.|The ceiling of the Sistine Chapel is a michelangelesque masterpiece.  西斯廷教堂的天花板是米开朗基罗式的杰作。

|近义词:grand, heroic, majestic, sublime|反义词: unimpressive, modest, simple|词性:adjective
