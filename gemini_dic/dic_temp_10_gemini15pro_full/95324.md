incendiary:/ɪnˈsɛndiəri/ | adj. n. |煽动性的；纵火的；燃烧弹；煽动者 | designed to cause fires; tending to stir up conflict | The incendiary device caused significant damage.  燃烧装置造成了重大损失。 | synonyms: inflammatory, provocative, combustible | antonyms: calming, soothing, peaceful | adjective

incendiary:/ɪnˈsɛndiəri/ | n. |燃烧弹；煽动者 | a substance or weapon (such as a bomb) used to start fires; a person who stirs up trouble or violence | The police arrested the incendiaries responsible for the recent fires.  警察逮捕了对最近几起火灾负责的纵火犯。 | synonyms: arsonist, agitator, firebrand | antonyms: peacemaker, mediator, conciliator | noun
