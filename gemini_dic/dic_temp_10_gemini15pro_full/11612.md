arm-twists:/ˈɑːrmˌtwɪsts/| n.|强行施压，逼迫，扭胳膊|The contract was signed after a lot of arm-twisting.  这份合同是在多番施压后签署的。|例句：He didn't want to go, but he agreed after some arm-twisting. 他不想去，但在一些压力下同意了。

|例句：She got her way through a combination of charm and arm-twisting. 她通过魅力和施压结合的方式达到了目的。|近义词：pressure, coercion, intimidation|反义词:persuasion, encouragement|词性:noun
