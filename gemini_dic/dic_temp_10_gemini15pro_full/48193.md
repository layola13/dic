decompresser:/ˌdɪːkəmˈprɛsər/ | n. |解压器，减压器|A device or program that decompresses data, such as a file or a stream. |Example: I used a decompresser to extract the files from the zipped folder. 我使用解压器从压缩文件夹中提取文件。

|Example: The decompresser quickly restored the image to its original size.  解压器迅速将图像恢复到原始大小。|Synonym: extractor, unzipper, unpacker |Antonym: compressor, zipper, packer |noun


decompress:/ˌdiːkəmˈprɛs/ | v. |解压，减压，放松|To reduce the pressure on something; to release compressed data.  |Example: I need to decompress this file before I can use it. 我需要先解压这个文件才能使用它。

|Example:  After a long week, I like to decompress by reading a book.  经过漫长的一周后，我喜欢通过读书来放松。|Synonym: extract, unzip, unpack, relax, unwind |Antonym: compress, zip, pack, stress |verb
