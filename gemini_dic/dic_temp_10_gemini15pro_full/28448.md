carbuncular:/kɑːrˈbʌŋkjʊlər/ | adj. |痈的；似痈的；患痈的；红似红玉的| Relating to, resembling, or affected with a carbuncle (a severe boil or multiple boils).  | Example 1: He had a carbuncular infection on his back. 他背上有一个痈样感染。

Example 2: The ruby had a deep, carbuncular red hue. 这颗红宝石有着深邃的、红玉般的红色调。 | 近义词:inflamed, infected, purulent, fiery (for color) | 反义词:healthy, clear (for skin), pale (for color) | 词性:adjective
