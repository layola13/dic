misinformants:/ˌmɪsɪnˈfɔːrmənts/ | n. | 误报者，提供错误信息的人 | People who give incorrect information, either intentionally or unintentionally. | The journalists were misled by misinformants who claimed to have witnessed the event. 记者们被声称目睹了该事件的误报者误导了。 |  The article was based on information from misinformants, leading to a retraction. 这篇文章是基于来自误报者的信息，导致了撤回。 | synonyms: liars, deceivers, fabricators | antonyms: informants, truth-tellers | noun
