overcharitableness:/ˌoʊvərˌtʃærɪtəbəlnəs/
|n.|过度慈善；过分宽容|The overcharitableness of the judge led to a lenient sentence.  法官的过分宽容导致了轻判。|
|英文解析: Excessive charitableness; undue leniency or tolerance.|
|例句：His overcharitableness towards his employees was sometimes taken advantage of. 他对员工的过分宽容有时会被利用。|
|例句：Her overcharitableness blinded her to his obvious flaws. 她过分的仁慈使她对他的明显缺点视而不见。|
|近义词：leniency, tolerance, indulgence, magnanimity|
|反义词：severity, harshness, strictness, intolerance|
|词性: noun|
