astonishes:/əˈstɒnɪʃɪz/
verb
使惊讶，使震惊
To surprise or impress someone very much.
Her knowledge of computers astonishes me. 她对计算机的了解令我惊讶。
synonyms: amazes, surprises, stuns, shocks
antonyms: bores, disappoints
词性: 动词(verb)
