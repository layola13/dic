annotation:/ˌænəˈteɪʃən/ | n. | 注解，注释，批注 | A note added to a text or diagram, giving explanation or comment. |  Example 1: The professor's annotations in the margins of the book were very helpful. 教授在书页空白处的注释很有帮助。

Example 2:  The online document allows for collaborative annotation.  在线文档允许进行协作注释。 | synonyms: note, comment, remark, gloss, explanation | antonyms:  omission, deletion | noun
