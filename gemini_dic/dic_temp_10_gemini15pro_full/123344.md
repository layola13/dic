monolinguals:/ˌmɒnəˈlɪŋɡwəlz/ | n. | 单语者；只使用一种语言的人 | People who can only speak one language are called monolinguals. 只会说一种语言的人被称为单语者。|  Monolingual dictionaries are designed for native speakers. 单语词典是为母语人士设计的。 | synonyms: unilinguals | antonyms: bilinguals, multilinguals | noun
