epideictic:/ˌɛpɪˈdaɪktɪk/ | adj. |  展示的，夸耀的；仪式性的 |  Of or relating to a formal speech delivered on a special occasion, designed primarily to display the speaker's eloquence.  |  The president's inaugural address was a powerful piece of epideictic oratory. 总统的就职演说是篇雄辩的展示性演讲。 | synonyms: ceremonial, demonstrative, rhetorical | antonyms: informal, casual,  improvised | adjective
