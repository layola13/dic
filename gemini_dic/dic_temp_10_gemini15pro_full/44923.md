cryptocrystalline:/ˌkrɪptoʊˈkrɪstəlɪn/| adj.|隐晶质的|Composed of crystals too small to be seen with an ordinary light microscope.|Chert is a cryptocrystalline form of quartz. 燧石是石英的一种隐晶质形式。|The rock has a cryptocrystalline texture. 这块岩石具有隐晶质结构。|近义词:microcrystalline, fine-grained|反义词:macrocrystalline, coarse-grained|词性:形容词

Etymology:
crypto- (from Greek kryptos "hidden") + crystalline
