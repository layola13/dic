ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bolwerk*, from Middle Low German *bolwerk*, from *bole* (“tree trunk, plank”) + *werk* (“work”).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *balkaz* (“beam, log”) and *werkan* (“to do, to work”).
