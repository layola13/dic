disauthorize:/dɪsˈɔːθəraɪz/
verb
取消授权，剥夺权力
To withdraw authorization or permission from; to deprive of authority.
The CEO disauthorized the manager's purchase order. 首席执行官取消了经理的采购订单。
The board disauthorized the use of company funds for personal expenses. 董事会取消了将公司资金用于个人开支的授权。
synonyms: revoke, cancel, withdraw, rescind, countermand, repeal
antonyms: authorize, empower, commission, enable, sanction
词性: 动词
