forego:/fɔːrˈɡoʊ (US) / fɔːˈɡəʊ (UK)| v. |放弃，摒弃|To choose not to have or do something, especially something enjoyable or pleasant.|She decided to forego dessert in order to lose weight. 她为了减肥决定不吃甜点。|

He had to forego his vacation to finish the project. 他为了完成项目不得不放弃休假。|近义词：forgo, abstain from, give up, relinquish, sacrifice|反义词：indulge in, partake in|词性：动词
