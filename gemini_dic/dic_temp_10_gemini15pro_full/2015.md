actuarial: /ˌæktʃuˈɛəriəl/
adjective|精算的；保险精算师的|Relating to the calculation of risks and premiums by insurance companies.|例句：He's an actuarial student. 他是一名精算专业的学生。|近义词：statistical, insurance-related|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective

actuarial: /ˌæktʃuˈɛəriəl/
noun|精算师|An actuary is a business professional who analyzes the financial consequences of risk.|例句：Actuaries use statistical models to assess risk. 精算师使用统计模型来评估风险。|近义词：statistician, risk analyst|反义词: N/A|词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *actuarius (“registrar, clerk”), from Latin actus (“act, official document, record”), from agō (“I do, drive, lead, act”).
