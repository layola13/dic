dobbing:/ˈdɒbɪŋ/
dobbing| n.|告密，举报（尤指非法活动）|The act of informing on someone, especially to the police, about illegal activities. |He was caught dobbing on his classmates for cheating.|Synonyms: informing, snitching, ratting, grassing|Antonyms: concealing, protecting, shielding|noun

dobbing:/ˈdɒbɪŋ/
dobbing| v.|告密，举报（尤指非法活动）|To inform on someone, especially to the police, about illegal activities. |She dobbed him in to the teacher for stealing.|Synonyms: inform, snitch, rat, grass|Antonyms: conceal, protect, shield|verb
