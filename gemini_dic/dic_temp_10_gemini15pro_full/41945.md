cosmopolite:/ˈkɒzməpəlʌɪt/ (UK) /ˈkɑːzməpəlʌɪt/ (US)| adj. |世界性的；世界主义的|Familiar with and at ease in many different countries and cultures.|London is a cosmopolitan city, attracting people from all over the globe.| 近义词:international, global, multicultural| 反义词:provincial, local, parochial|词性:adjective
