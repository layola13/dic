enpatron:/ɛnˈpeɪtrən/ | v. | 赞助，资助；以恩人自居 | To act as a patron to; to support or sponsor. | He enpatroned the young artist, providing him with a studio and materials. 他赞助了这位年轻的艺术家，为他提供工作室和材料。 |  She enpatroned several local charities. 她资助了几家当地慈善机构。 | 近义词: sponsor, support, back, fund, finance | 反义词: hinder, obstruct, oppose | 词性: 动词


词源: From en- + patron. "Patron" comes from the Latin "patronus," meaning protector or defender.  The prefix "en-" adds the sense of putting into the state or condition of being a patron.
