cowpox:/ˈkaʊpɒks/ (UK) /ˈkaʊpɑːks/ (US)| n.|牛痘|A mild viral disease of cows, closely related to smallpox, that when contracted by humans through contact, produces immunity to smallpox.  It was the basis of the first smallpox vaccines.|The discovery of cowpox's protective effect against smallpox was a major medical breakthrough.|Synonym: vaccinia|Antonym: N/A|noun
