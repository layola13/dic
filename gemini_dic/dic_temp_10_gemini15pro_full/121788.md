misrecollection:/ˌmɪsrɛkəˈlɛkʃən/
|n.|错误的记忆，记错|An inaccurate or faulty memory of something.|例句：He had a misrecollection of the events of that night. 他对那天晚上发生的事情记忆有误。

例句：Her misrecollection of the conversation caused a misunderstanding. 她对谈话的错误记忆导致了误解。
|近义词：false memory, paramnesia, distorted memory|反义词：accurate memory, correct recollection|词性：名词
