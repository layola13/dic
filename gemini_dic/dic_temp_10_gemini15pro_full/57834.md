dresser: /ˈdrɛsər/ (US), /ˈdrɛsə(r)/ (UK)| n.|梳妆台；碗柜；穿着者|A piece of furniture with drawers, often with a mirror, used for storing clothes and other items. Also, a person who dresses in a specific way.|She kept her jewelry in her dresser. 她把她的珠宝放在梳妆台里。|
The dresser was antique and very valuable.  这个碗柜是古董，非常值钱。|
He is a stylish dresser. 他是一个穿着讲究的人。|Synonyms: bureau, chest of drawers, wardrobe (for furniture);  well-dressed person, fashionable person (for person)|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but for furniture, maybe an open shelf? For person, perhaps slob, unkempt person)|noun
