artlessly:/ˈɑːrtləsli/ | adv. | 天真烂漫地，自然地，不做作地 | In a natural, simple, and unpretentious way, without any artificiality or cunning. | She artlessly confessed her love for him. 她天真烂漫地向他表白了爱意。|例句：The child artlessly asked the stranger for candy.  孩子天真地向陌生人要糖果。| 近义词: naturally, innocently, ingenuously, candidly | 反义词: artfully, cunningly, deceptively, artificially | 词性: 副词

artlessly:/ˈɑːrtləsli/ | adv. |  无技巧地，拙劣地 |  Lacking skill or finesse; crudely.  | He artlessly attempted to repair the broken vase. 他笨拙地试图修理破损的花瓶。 | 例句：The painting was artlessly executed, lacking any sense of composition. 这幅画画得很拙劣，没有任何构图感。 | 近义词: clumsily, awkwardly, crudely | 反义词: skillfully, expertly, deftly | 词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From artless + -ly.  Artless from art + -less. Art in this sense means "skill" or "cunning," from Old French art, ultimately from Latin artem (nominative ars) "art, skill, craft."
