nonhabitably:/ˌnɒnˈhæbɪtəbli/
adjective
不适宜居住地
Not suitable for habitation; uninhabitable.
The planet is nonhabitably hot due to its proximity to the sun.  这个星球由于靠近太阳而不适宜居住，非常炎热。
The moon's surface is currently nonhabitably barren. 月球表面目前荒芜，不适合居住。
近义词: uninhabitably, unlivably
反义词: habitably, livably
词性: adjective

词源:
From non- + habitably.  "Habitable" comes from the Old French "habitable," from Late Latin "habitabilis," from Latin "habitare" (to dwell, inhabit).  The prefix "non-" indicates negation.
