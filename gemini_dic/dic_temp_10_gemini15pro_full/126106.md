mutationist:/mjuːˈteɪʃənɪst/ | n. | 突变论者 | A biologist who believes that mutation is the primary driver of evolution. | Hugo de Vries was a prominent mutationist in the early 20th century. 雨果·德弗里斯是20世纪早期著名的突变论者。 |  proponent of mutation theory, saltationist |  selectionist, gradualist | noun

mutationist:/mjuːˈteɪʃənɪst/ | adj. | 突变论的 | Relating to the theory of mutationism. |  He proposed a mutationist explanation for the sudden appearance of new traits. 他对新性状的突然出现提出了突变论的解释。|  saltationist | gradualist | adjective
