billabong:/ˈbɪləbɒŋ/ | n. | 死水潭，洼地，水坑 | A billabong is a stagnant pool of water, typically formed when a river changes course and leaves an isolated body of water behind. | The kangaroos gathered around the billabong to drink.  袋鼠聚集在死水潭边喝水。 |  The platypus swam in the billabong, searching for food.  鸭嘴兽在死水潭里游泳，寻找食物。 | synonym: oxbow lake, lagoon, backwater | antonym: river, stream | noun
