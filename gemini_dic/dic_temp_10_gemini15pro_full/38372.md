concepted:/kənˈsɛptɪd/
v. |构思的，被设想出的|  Formed or devised as a concept. |The project was well concepted, but poorly executed.  这个项目构思很好，但执行很差。|近义词：imagined, conceived, devised|反义词: unformed, undeveloped|动词(过去分词)


concepted:/kənˈsɛptɪd/
adj. |构思的，被设想出的| Formed or devised as a concept.  |The film had a very tightly concepted plot. 这部电影的情节构思非常紧凑。|近义词：developed, planned, designed|反义词: unplanned, undeveloped|形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From concept + -ed.  "Concept" comes from the Latin "conceptus," meaning "something conceived."
