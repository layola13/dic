cringingly:/ˈkrɪndʒɪŋli/ | adv. | 令人尴尬地，难堪地 | In a way that makes you feel embarrassed or uncomfortable because it is not sincere or natural. | She laughed cringingly at her boss's bad jokes. 她尴尬地对她老板的烂笑话笑了笑。 |  Synonyms: awkwardly, embarrassingly, uncomfortably | Antonyms: comfortably, confidently, gracefully | adverb

cringe:/krɪndʒ/ | v. | 畏缩，感到尴尬 | To feel very embarrassed and uncomfortable. | I cringed when I realized what I had said. 当我意识到我说了什么的时候，我感到很尴尬。| Synonyms: recoil, flinch, wince | Antonyms:  relax, embrace | verb
