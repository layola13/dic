advisement:/ədˈvaɪzmənt/| n.|建议, 劝告, 忠告, 通知|The matter is now under advisement. 这件事现在正在审议中。|例句：The committee took the matter under advisement. 委员会把这件事提交审议。

The lawyer gave her client some advisement. 律师给了他的客户一些建议。|近义词：advice, counsel, guidance, recommendation|反义词：discouragement, dissuasion|词性:名词

词源:
From Middle English *avisement, *avysement, from Anglo-Norman avisement, from aviser (“to advise”) + -ment.

USAGE->
一般现在时:
The board keeps the proposal under advisement. = 董事会正在考虑这项提议。
She seeks advisement from her mentor. = 她向她的导师寻求建议。

一般过去时:
He took her advisement to heart. = 他认真地考虑了她的建议。
They requested further advisement from the expert. = 他们向专家请求进一步的建议。

现在完成时:
The council has taken the matter under advisement. = 理事会已将此事提交审议。
She has received advisement from several sources. = 她已从多个渠道获得了建议。

过去完成时:
After he had received advisement, he made his decision. = 在得到建议后，他做出了决定。
They had sought advisement before implementing the plan. = 他们在实施计划之前寻求了建议。

将来时:
The committee will take the proposal under advisement next week. = 委员会下周将审议该提案。
He will seek advisement from his lawyer before signing the contract. = 在签署合同之前，他将征求律师的意见。


被动语态:
The issue was taken under advisement by the committee. = 委员会已将这个问题提交审议。
Further advisement was sought from the consultant. = 已向顾问寻求进一步的建议。


PHRASE->
take under advisement = 认真考虑
hold under advisement = 保留考虑
seek advisement = 寻求建议
offer advisement = 提供建议
upon/on advisement = 经过考虑后
