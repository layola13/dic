carport:/ˈkɑːrpɔːrt/ |n.|车棚，车库|A covered structure, often made of wood or metal, used to shelter a car or other vehicle from the elements. |We parked the car under the carport. 我们把车停在了车棚下。

|The carport protected our car from the hail. 车棚保护了我们的车免受冰雹的袭击。|近义词:garage, shelter, canopy|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑露天 parking lot)|词性:名词
