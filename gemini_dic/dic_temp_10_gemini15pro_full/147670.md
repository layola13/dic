overpost:/ˌoʊvərˈpoʊst/| v.|过度发布；发布过多帖子|To post excessively, especially on social media platforms.|She overposted on Instagram during her vacation, sharing dozens of photos each day. 她假期时在Instagram上过度发布，每天分享几十张照片。|例句：He tends to overpost on Facebook, sometimes sharing multiple updates per hour. 他倾向于在Facebook上过度发布，有时每小时分享多个更新。

例句：Worried about overposting, she limited her tweets to just a few per day. 担心过度发布，她将自己的推文限制在每天几条。|近义词：overshare, flood (with posts)|反义词: underpost|词性：动词
