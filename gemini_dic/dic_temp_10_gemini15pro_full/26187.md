butlerlike:/ˈbʌtləˌlaɪk/| adj.|像管家一样的，仆人似的|Resembling or characteristic of a butler; impeccably dressed, deferential, and discreet. |He had a butlerlike demeanor, always anticipating our needs. 他举止像个管家，总是能预料到我们的需求。|近义词：servile, obsequious, deferential|反义词：impolite, disrespectful, casual|词性:形容词

butlerlike (Etymology): From "butler" + "-like". "Butler" comes from Old French "bouteillier" (bottle bearer), ultimately from Latin "buticula" (small bottle). "-like" is a common suffix meaning "resembling" or "characteristic of".
