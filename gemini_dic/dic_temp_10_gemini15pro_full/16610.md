barometric: /ˌbærəˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. | 气压的; 气压计的 | Relating to or determined by barometry; of or relating to a barometer. | Barometric pressure readings are crucial for weather forecasting. 气压读数对于天气预报至关重要。| synonyms:  pressure-related, atmospheric | antonyms: N/A | adjective
