eye-rolling:/ˈaɪˌroʊlɪŋ/ | n. | 翻白眼 | The act of rolling one's eyes, often to express disbelief, annoyance, or contempt. |  She responded to his suggestion with an exaggerated eye-roll. 她对他提出的建议翻了个大白眼。| Synonym: eye roll, ocular gyration | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, consider attentive gaze, respectful look) | noun


eye-rolling:/ˈaɪˌroʊlɪŋ/ | adj. | 翻白眼的 | Characterized by rolling one's eyes. |  Her eye-rolling response showed her disdain. 她翻着白眼的回应表明了她的轻蔑。| Synonym: disdainful, contemptuous | Antonym:  respectful, attentive | adjective


Etymology:  Compound of "eye" and "rolling,"  describing the physical action.  The figurative meaning of expressing disdain through eye-rolling developed later.
