misalign:/ˌmɪsəˈlaɪn/| v. |未对准，错位|To put something in a position that is not correct or appropriate, especially so that it is not in line with something else. |The wheels were misaligned after the accident. 车轮在事故后错位了。

The printer is misaligned and the text is blurry. 打印机未对准，文本模糊。|近义词：malalign, disalign, offset|反义词: align, adjust, straighten|词性:动词

misalignment:/ˌmɪsəˈlaɪnmənt/| n. |未对准，错位|The state of being misaligned; an instance of being misaligned. |The misalignment of the gears caused the machine to malfunction. 齿轮未对准导致机器故障。

There's a slight misalignment of the door frame. 门框有点错位。|近义词：malalignment, disalignment, offset|反义词: alignment, adjustment|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (expressing wrongness or error) + align (to bring into line or alignment).  The prefix "mis-" comes from Old English "mis-", meaning "wrongly, badly, astray."  "Align" comes from Old French "alignier," meaning "to arrange in a line," ultimately from Latin "linea" (line).
