anti-patriotism: /ˌæntiˈpeɪtriətɪzəm/ | n. | 反爱国主义；不爱国 | Lack of patriotism; opposition to patriotism. |  His anti-patriotism was evident in his refusal to salute the flag. 他的反爱国主义体现在他拒绝向国旗敬礼。

| Example: Some people consider his refusal to join the army as a sign of anti-patriotism.  有些人认为他拒绝参军是不爱国的表现。| Synonym: disloyalty, sedition, treason | Antonym: patriotism, nationalism, loyalty | noun
