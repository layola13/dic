installment:/ɪnˈstɔːlmənt/ (US), /ɪnˈstɔːlmənt/ (UK)| n. |安装；安置|The act of installing something.|例句：The installment of the new software went smoothly. 新软件的安装很顺利。|近义词：installation, setting up|反义词: removal, uninstallation|noun
