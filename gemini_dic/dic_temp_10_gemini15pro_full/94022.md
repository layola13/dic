impanelments:/ɪmˈpænəlmənts/
|n.| 选任陪审团；陪审团名单；陪审团的组成|The act of enrolling or constituting a jury. The list of jurors enrolled for a particular case. The jury itself.|The lawyer requested a review of the impanelments due to concerns about bias. 律师因担心存在偏见，要求审查陪审团的组成。|Synonyms: jury selection, jury list, panel, juries|Antonyms: dismissal of jury, discharge of jury|noun
