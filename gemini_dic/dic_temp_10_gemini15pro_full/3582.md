agate:/ˈæɡət/| n.|玛瑙|A hard, translucent variety of chalcedony, typically banded in shades of color.|She wore an agate pendant. 她戴着一个玛瑙吊坠。|近义词：chalcedony, onyx|反义词:|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *agate*, from Latin *achates*, from Greek *Ἀχάτης* (*Akhátēs*), the name of a river in Sicily where agates were first found.
