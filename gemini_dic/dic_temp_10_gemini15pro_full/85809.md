hazardry:/ˈhæzərdri/
n.
冒险; 危险行为; 冒险的举动;  危险的职业或活动
The act or practice of taking risks; a dangerous occupation or activity.
His youthful hazardry had cost him dearly. 他年轻时的冒险行为让他付出了高昂的代价。
近义词: risk-taking, daredevilry, recklessness
反义词: caution, prudence, safety
词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From hazard + -ry.  Hazard comes from Old French hasard, hasart "game of dice," of uncertain origin.
