interhaemal:/ˌɪntərˈhiːməl/
adjective
血腔间的，血窦间的
Located or occurring between blood sinuses or blood cavities.
Interhaemal spaces are found in some invertebrates.  血腔间隙存在于一些无脊椎动物中。
The interhaemal system plays a role in nutrient transport. 血窦系统在营养运输中发挥作用。
近义词: intersinusal
反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "intrahaemal" if such a word existed, meaning "within a blood sinus")
词性: adjective
