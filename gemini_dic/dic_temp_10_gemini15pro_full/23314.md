boycotters:/ˈbɔɪkɒtəz/ (UK) /ˈbɔɪkɑːtərz/ (US)| n. |抵制者，联合抵制者|People who participate in a boycott, refusing to buy, use, or deal with a person, organization, or country as a protest.|Many boycotters refused to buy products from the company due to its unethical labor practices. 许多抵制者因为该公司不道德的劳动行为而拒绝购买其产品。|近义词：protesters, objectors|反义词：supporters, patrons|词性:Noun

boycotters:/ˈbɔɪkɒtəz/ (UK) /ˈbɔɪkɑːtərz/ (US)| n. |抵制者，联合抵制者|Those who abstain from using, buying, or dealing with a person, organization, or country as a form of protest.|The boycotters picketed outside the store, urging others to join their cause.  抵制者在商店外设置纠察线，敦促其他人加入他们的行列。|近义词：campaigners, activists|反义词：customers, clients|词性:Noun
