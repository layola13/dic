exilers:/ˈeksaɪlərz/
n.
流亡者，放逐者
People who are forced to live in a foreign country.
The exilers longed to return to their homeland. 这些流亡者渴望回到自己的祖国。
synonyms: refugees, exiles, displaced persons
antonyms: residents, citizens, natives
noun
