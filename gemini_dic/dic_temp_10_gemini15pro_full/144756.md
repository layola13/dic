over-colors:/ˈoʊvərˌkʌlərz/ | n. | 套印色, 叠印色 |  The process or result of printing one color over another, often used in printing to create a wider range of colors or effects. | The printer used over-colors to achieve the rich, deep tones in the brochure. 印刷商使用套印色来实现手册中丰富、深沉的色调。| synonyms: overlay colors, superimposed colors | antonyms: base colors, underlying colors | noun

over-colors:/ˈoʊvərˌkʌlərz/ | v. | 套印, 叠印 | To print one color over another. | He over-colored the image to give it a vintage feel. 他在图像上套印颜色，使其具有复古感。 | synonyms: overlay, superimpose | antonyms: underlay | verb
