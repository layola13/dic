modifies:/ˈmɒdɪfaɪz/ |v. | 修改，更改；修饰 | To make small changes to something in order to improve it or make it more suitable; to limit or reduce something. |  He modified the recipe by adding more sugar. 他通过添加更多糖来修改食谱。 | alter, change, adjust, amend | maintain, preserve | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *modificāre, from Latin modus (“measure, manner, mode”) + -ficāre, combining form of facere (“to make, do”).
