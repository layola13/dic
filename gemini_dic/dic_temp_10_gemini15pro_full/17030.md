batement:/əˈbeɪtmənt/| n.|减少，减轻，降低;【建】(突出部分的)截去，削平;【律】(地产等的)废除，取消|A lessening in amount, intensity, or degree.  The abatement of the noise allowed me to concentrate. 噪音的减弱让我能够集中注意力。|近义词：reduction, decrease, diminution, mitigation|反义词：increase, rise, growth|词性:noun

Etymology->
From Old French *abattement, from abatre "to beat down," from a- "down" + batre "to beat".
