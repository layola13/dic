lentiginose:/ˌlɛntɪˈdʒɪnoʊs/| adj.|雀斑的；多雀斑的|Covered with lentigines; freckled.  Characterized by numerous lentigines.|She has lentiginose skin, especially on her face and arms. 她脸上和手臂上尤其多雀斑。|Synonyms: freckled, speckled, spotted|Antonyms: clear, unblemished|adjective

lentiginous:/ˌlɛnˈtɪdʒɪnəs/| adj.|雀斑的；多雀斑的|Synonym of lentiginose.|His lentiginous complexion made him easily recognizable. 他满脸雀斑,很容易被人认出来。|Synonyms: freckled, speckled, spotted|Antonyms: clear, unblemished|adjective
