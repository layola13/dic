近义词：supple, flexible, agile, nimble, graceful|反义词:stiff, rigid, inflexible, clumsy|词性:形容词

词源： From Middle English *lithi, from Old English līðe (“gentle, mild, meek; agreeable, pleasant; soft, smooth, supple, flexible; calm, quiet”), from Proto-Germanic *linþjaz (“soft, yielding”). Cognate with Old Frisian līthe (“mild, gentle”), Old Saxon līthi (“soft, mild”), Old High German lind (“soft, gentle, weak”) (German lind (“soft, gentle, mild; soothing, lenient”)), Old Norse linnr (“soft, gentle”). Related also to Old English lenian (“to soften, mitigate”).
