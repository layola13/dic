bose:/boʊz/| adj. n.|一流的，高品质的；玻色（姓氏；公司名）|Referring to high-quality audio equipment, often associated with the Bose Corporation.  Also, a surname, and the name of a corporation.|He has a Bose sound system in his car. 他在他的车里安装了一套Bose音响系统。|近义词：high-quality, premium, top-of-the-line|反义词:low-quality, cheap, inferior|词性:adjective, noun

bose:/boʊz/| n.|玻色子|A type of fundamental particle in physics.|Photons and gluons are examples of bosons. 光子和胶子是玻色子的例子。|近义词: (In some contexts) fermion (as a contrasting type of particle)|反义词:fermion|词性:noun
