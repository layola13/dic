electroneutrality:/ɪˌlɛktrəʊnjuːˈtrælɪti/ /ɪˌlɛktroʊnjuːˈtrælɪti/
n.
电中性
The state of having equal amounts of positive and negative charges.
Ionic compounds exhibit electroneutrality because the positive and negative charges of the ions cancel each other out. 离子化合物呈现电中性，因为离子的正负电荷相互抵消。
The principle of electroneutrality dictates that the overall charge of a solution must be zero. 电中性原理规定溶液的总电荷必须为零。
neutrality, electrical neutrality
charged
