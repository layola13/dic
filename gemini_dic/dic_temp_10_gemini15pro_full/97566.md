ingross:/ɪnˈɡroʊs/ | vt. |  大量购买; 独占;  | To purchase in large quantities; to monopolize or absorb completely. | The company ingrossed the entire supply of raw materials. 这家公司独占了所有原材料的供应。 |  近义词: monopolize, corner, buy up, stockpile, amass | 反义词: distribute, disperse, sell off | 词性: 动词

ingross:/ɪnˈɡroʊs/ | vt. | 全神贯注于；使埋头于；使沉迷于 | To completely occupy the attention or interest of. |  He was completely engrossed in his work. 他完全沉浸在他的工作中。| 近义词: absorb, engage, immerse, preoccupy | 反义词: distract, bore, disinterest | 词性: 动词
