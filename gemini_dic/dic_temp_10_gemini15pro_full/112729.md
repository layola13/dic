lozenged:/ˈlɒzɪndʒd/ /ˈlɑːzɪndʒd/| adj.|菱形的，锭形的|Shaped like a lozenge; rhombic.|The window panes were lozenged.|近义词：diamond-shaped, rhombic, rhomboid|反义词:square, circular|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From lozenge + -ed. Lozenge comes from Old French *losenge, from Vulgar Latin *lausa, from Latin laus "praise, commendation."  Originally referred to a small tablet of flavored medicine held in the mouth to dissolve slowly.  The shape of these tablets gave rise to the geometric meaning.
