brainwash: /ˈbreɪnwɒʃ/ (UK) /ˈbreɪnwɑːʃ/ (US) | n. vt. | 洗脑 | to force someone to believe something by repeatedly telling them that it is true and preventing them from hearing other points of view | The government tried to brainwash the citizens into believing they were living in a perfect society. 政府试图给公民洗脑，让他们相信自己生活在一个完美的社会里。|  The cult leader brainwashed his followers into giving him all their money.  邪教头目给他的追随者洗脑，让他们把所有的钱都给他。 | synonyms: indoctrinate, propagandize, condition | antonyms: deprogram, enlighten, educate | noun, verb
