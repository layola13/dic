hypoderm: /ˈhaɪpəʊdɜːm/ (UK) /ˈhaɪpəʊdɜːrm/ (US) | n. |皮下组织；皮下注射|The hypoderm is a layer of tissue beneath the dermis that contains fat cells. 皮下组织是真皮下方的一层组织，包含脂肪细胞。|The needle penetrated the hypoderm. 针头穿透了皮下组织。|Synonyms: subcutaneous tissue, subcutis|Antonyms: epidermis|noun
