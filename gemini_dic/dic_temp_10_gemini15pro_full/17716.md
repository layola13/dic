bedcord:/ˈbedkɔːrd/| n. |床绳|A cord or rope used to support a mattress or bed frame, especially in older beds.|The old bedcord snapped, sending the mattress crashing to the floor. 床绳断了，床垫摔到了地上。| The bedstead creaked alarmingly as he leaned against the bedcord. 他靠在床绳上时，床架发出令人担忧的吱吱声。| synonym: rope, cord | antonym: N/A | noun
