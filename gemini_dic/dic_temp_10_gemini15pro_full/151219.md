pare:/pɛr/ | v. | 削皮; 削減; 修剪; 减少 | to remove the thin outer layer of something (such as a fruit or vegetable) or to reduce something gradually in size or amount |  I pared the apple before eating it. 我吃苹果前削了皮。| synonyms: peel, trim, reduce, cut back | antonyms: increase, augment, expand | verb

pare down:/pɛr daʊn/ | v. | 削减; 减少 | to reduce the size or amount of something | The company pared down its workforce to cut costs.  公司为了削减成本裁员了。| synonyms: reduce, decrease, diminish, curtail | antonyms: increase, expand, enlarge | verb
