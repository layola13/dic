hypersensuousness:/ˌhaɪpərˈsɛnʃuːəsnəs/
n.
过度感性，极度感官享受
Excessive sensuality; extreme responsiveness to or awareness of sensory stimulation.
Her poetry is filled with a rich hypersensuousness, evoking the scent of jasmine and the taste of ripe mangoes. 她的诗歌充满了丰富的过度感性，唤起了茉莉花的香味和成熟芒果的味道。

The artist's work explored the themes of hypersensuousness and the pursuit of pleasure. 这位艺术家的作品探索了过度感性和追求享乐的主题。

近义词:sensuality, voluptuousness
反义词:insensitivity, numbness
词性:noun
