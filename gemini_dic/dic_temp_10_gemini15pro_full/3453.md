afrobeat:/ˈæfroʊbiːt/ | n. | 非节拍;非洲节拍 | A style of West African music featuring complex interlocking rhythms, chanted vocals, and horns. It combines elements of traditional Yoruba music, Ghanaian highlife, jazz, and funk. |  Fela Kuti is considered the pioneer of afrobeat.  费拉·库提被认为是非洲节拍的先驱。

|  Afrobeat has become increasingly popular around the world. 非洲节拍在世界范围内越来越受欢迎。| synonyms: Afro-beat,  West African music | antonyms: N/A | noun
