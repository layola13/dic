adumbrate: /ædˈʌmbreɪt/
verb
预示;  概述;  遮蔽;
to give a slight indication of something to come; to outline or sketch broadly; to overshadow or obscure.
The architect's sketches adumbrate the final design of the building.  建筑师的草图预示了建筑物的最终设计。

The clouds adumbrated the sun. 云遮蔽了太阳。

近义词: foreshadow, prefigure, intimate, suggest, outline, sketch
反义词: reveal, clarify, illuminate
词性: verb
