flow-diagram:/ˈfloʊ ˌdaɪəɡræm/ | n. | 流程图，流程表 | A diagram that represents a workflow or process, showing the steps as boxes of various kinds, and their order by connecting them with arrows.  | The flow-diagram clearly illustrates the steps involved in manufacturing the product.  流程图清楚地说明了产品制造的步骤。| synonyms: flowchart, flow chart, process chart | antonyms: N/A | noun

flow-diagram:/ˈfloʊ ˌdaɪəɡræm/ | n. | 流程图，流程表 | A visual representation of a sequence of operations or steps in a process. |  We used a flow-diagram to map out the customer journey. 我们用流程图来绘制客户旅程。 | synonyms: flowchart, flow chart | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From flow + diagram.  "Flow" meaning the movement or passage of something (like a liquid) and "diagram" meaning a simplified drawing showing the components of something.
