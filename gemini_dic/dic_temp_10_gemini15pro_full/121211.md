miseducate:/ˌmɪsˈɛdʒʊkeɪt/
verb
误导，错误地教育
To educate inadequately or improperly, especially to teach (someone) inaccurate or misleading information.
Many children were miseducated about the true history of their country. 许多孩子都被错误地教育了关于他们国家的真实历史。
The textbook miseducates students about the causes of the war. 这本教科书误导学生关于战争的起因。
misinform, misguide, misteach
educate, inform correctly
词性: 动词
