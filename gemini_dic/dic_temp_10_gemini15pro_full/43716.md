creds:/krɛdz/| n. |信用; 资格; 证书; 学分| Street creds are important for anyone who wants to make it big in the music business. 在音乐界想要出人头地，街头信誉很重要。|近义词：credentials, qualifications, merits|反义词: discredit|词性:名词

词源: Shortening of "credentials".  From the Latin "credentia" meaning "belief, trust."
