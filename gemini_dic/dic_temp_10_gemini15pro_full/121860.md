misrepresents:/ˌmɪsrɛprɪˈzɛnts/
verb
歪曲；不如实地叙述；误传
gives a false or misleading account of
The advertisement misrepresents the qualities of the product.  这则广告歪曲了产品质量。
synonyms: distorts, falsifies, misstates
antonyms: represents accurately, portrays truthfully
词性: 动词
