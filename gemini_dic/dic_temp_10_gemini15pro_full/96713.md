ineffectually:/ˌɪnɪˈfɛktʃuəli/
| adv. | 无效地，徒劳地 | Without producing any desired effect; unsuccessfully. |  He tried ineffectually to open the locked door.  他试图打开锁着的门，但徒劳无功。 | synonyms: fruitlessly, vainly, uselessly, pointlessly | antonyms: effectively, successfully, efficiently | adverb

ineffective:/ˌɪnɪˈfɛktɪv/
| adj. | 无效的，不起作用的 | Not producing any significant or desired effect. | The medicine was ineffective against the virus.  这种药对病毒无效。| synonyms: useless, futile, unproductive | antonyms: effective, successful, productive | adjective

effect:/ɪˈfɛkt/
| n. | 效果，影响 | A change which is a result or consequence of an action or other cause. | The effect of the medication was immediate.  药物的效果是立竿见影的。| synonyms: impact, influence, outcome | antonyms: cause, origin | noun
