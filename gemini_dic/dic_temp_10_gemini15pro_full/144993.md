overactive:/ˌōvərˈæktɪv/| adj.|过度活跃的；活动过度的|Characterized by excessive activity, restlessness, or fidgeting.  More active than is usual or desirable.|The child was diagnosed as overactive.  这个孩子被诊断为活动过度。|近义词：hyperactive, restless, energetic, fidgety|反义词：calm, quiet, inactive, lethargic|词性: adjective
词源: From 'over-' (excessively) + 'active'.
