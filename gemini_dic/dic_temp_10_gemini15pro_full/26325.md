buz:/bʌz/| n.|嗡嗡声, 兴奋, 骚动|A low, continuous humming or vibrating sound, like that of a bee; a feeling of excitement or activity.|The room was filled with the buzz of conversation. 房间里充满了嗡嗡的谈话声。|近义词：hum, drone, murmur, excitement, activity|反义词:silence, quiet|Noun
