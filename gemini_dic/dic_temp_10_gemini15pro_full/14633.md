avoiders:/əˈvɔɪdərz/| n.|逃避者；回避者|People who tend to avoid certain situations, activities, or people.  Those who actively try to stay away from something or someone.|例句：Avoiders often struggle with social anxiety. 逃避者经常与社交焦虑作斗争。

例句：She is an avoider of conflict. 她是一个避免冲突的人。|近义词：shirkers, evaders, dodgers|反义词：confrontationalists, seekers|词性:Noun (plural)

avoid:/əˈvɔɪd/| v.|避免；回避|To stay away from someone or something; to prevent something from happening.|例句：He avoids eye contact. 他避免眼神接触。

例句：She tries to avoid fatty foods. 她尽量避免油腻的食物。|近义词：evade, dodge, shun, elude|反义词：confront, face, seek|词性:Verb
