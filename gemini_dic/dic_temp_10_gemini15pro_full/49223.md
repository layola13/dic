delegatee:/ˌdɛlɪˈɡiː/
n.
被委托人，代表
a person to whom authority or power is delegated
The delegatee was tasked with presenting the proposal at the conference.  被委托人负责在会议上提出提案。
近义词：representative, agent, deputy, proxy
反义词：delegator
词性：名词
