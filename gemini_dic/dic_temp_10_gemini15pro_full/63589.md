envision:/ɪnˈvɪʒən/ | v. | 想象，展望 | To form a mental picture of something, especially something that you think might happen or exist in the future. |  I envisioned a future where everyone had access to clean water.  我展望了一个人人都能获得清洁水的未来。

| 近义词：imagine, visualize, foresee, contemplate, anticipate | 反义词: disregard, ignore | 词性: 动词
