metapectic:/ˌmetəˈpektɪk/ | adj. | 后期果胶的，过度成熟的 |  Relating to or denoting pectin that has undergone extensive degradation, typically found in overripe fruit.  | The metapectic texture of the overripe bananas made them unsuitable for baking. 过熟香蕉的后果胶质地使它们不适合烘焙。| Near synonyms: overripe, decomposed, degraded | Antonym: unripe | Adjective

metapectic:/ˌmetəˈpektɪk/ | n. | 后期果胶 | A highly degraded form of pectin. | The metapectic content of the fruit increased as it overripened. 水果的后果胶含量随着过度成熟而增加。| Near synonyms: pectinic acid | Antonym: protopectin | Noun
