easement:/ˈiːzmənt/| n.|地役权，通行权，缓和，减轻|A right to cross or otherwise use someone else's land for a specified purpose.|Example 1: The farmer granted an easement to the power company so they could run lines across his property. 农场主授予电力公司地役权，以便他们可以在他的土地上架设线路。

Example 2:  They negotiated an easement for access to the beach through their neighbor's yard. 他们通过邻居的院子协商了通往海滩的地役权。|近义词：right-of-way, access, relief, mitigation|反义词: restriction, prohibition|词性:noun
