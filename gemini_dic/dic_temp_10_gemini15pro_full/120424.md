minivan:/ˈmɪniˌvæn/| n. |小型货车，面包车|A small van that is designed to carry passengers.|例句：We rented a minivan for our family road trip. 我们租了一辆小型货车进行家庭公路旅行。

例句：The minivan is perfect for transporting a large group of people.  这辆小型货车非常适合运送一大群人。


近义词：van, MPV (multi-purpose vehicle), people carrier|反义词:truck, lorry|词性:noun
