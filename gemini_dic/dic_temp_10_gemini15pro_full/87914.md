hierogram:/ˈhaɪərəɡræm/ /ˈhaɪroʊɡræm/| n.|圣书文字，圣像文字|A written character that represents a sacred concept, object, or idea, especially in ancient Egyptian and other writing systems.  It is a type of ideogram that conveys religious or sacred meaning.|The Rosetta Stone contains hieroglyphs, demotic script, and ancient Greek, allowing scholars to decipher the Egyptian writing system.| 例句：The walls of the temple were covered in intricate hieroglyphs.  寺庙的墙壁上覆盖着复杂的圣书文字。

例句：Researchers are still deciphering the meaning of some Mayan hieroglyphs. 研究人员仍在破译一些玛雅圣书文字的含义。|近义词：ideogram, symbol, pictogram|反义词:|noun
