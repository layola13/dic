dubitation:/ˌduːbɪˈteɪʃən/| n.| 怀疑，疑惑，迟疑 |The project was delayed due to the CEO's dubitation about its feasibility.  由于首席执行官对其可行性表示怀疑，该项目被推迟了。|  He expressed dubitation about the accuracy of the report. 他对报告的准确性表示怀疑。| 近义词：doubt, hesitation, uncertainty, skepticism | 反义词：certainty, conviction, confidence | 词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *dubitationem, accusative singular of dubitatio (“doubt, hesitation”), from Latin dubito (“I doubt, hesitate”), frequentative of dubius (“doubtful, uncertain”).
