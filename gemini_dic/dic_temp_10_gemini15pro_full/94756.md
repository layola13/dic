improvisatrice:/ɪmˈprɒvɪzətriːs/ /ɪmˈprɑːvɪzətriːs/
n.
女即兴表演者，女即兴诗人
A female performer who improvises, especially in music, poetry, or drama.
She was a talented improvisatrice, captivating the audience with her spontaneous verses. 她是一位才华横溢的女即兴诗人，用她自发的诗句吸引了观众。
Near Synonyms:  improviser, ad-libber, extemporizer
Antonyms: planner,  preparer
Word Family: improvise (v.), improvisation (n.)
