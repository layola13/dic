forsythia: /fɔːrˈsɪθiə/ | n. |连翘; 黄寿丹| A shrub with bright yellow flowers that bloom in early spring.  A popular ornamental plant. | The forsythia bushes were a vibrant splash of yellow against the gray sky. 连翘灌木丛在灰色的天空下是一片鲜艳的黄色。|Forsythia brightened up the garden with its cheerful yellow flowers. 连翘以其欢快的黄色花朵照亮了花园。|Synonyms: yellow bell, golden bells | Antonyms: N/A | noun
