modishly:/ˈmɒdɪʃli/ (US) /ˈmɑːdɪʃli/| adv.|时髦地；流行地|In a fashionable or stylish manner. |She was modishly dressed in the latest designer clothes. 她穿着最新款的设计师服装，非常时髦。|例句：He modishly wore a scarf draped around his neck. 他时髦地围着一条围巾。|近义词：fashionably, stylishly, trendily|反义词：unfashionably, dowdily|词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "modish" + "-ly".  "Modish" comes from "mode" (fashion) + "-ish" (suffix indicating resembling or characteristic of). Ultimately from French "mode" and Latin "modus" (manner, measure).
