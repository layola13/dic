meliorism:/ˈmiːliərɪzəm/
noun
改良主义;乐观主义
The belief that the world can be made better by human effort.
Example: Her meliorism inspired her to start a charity. 她因改良主义而受到启发，创办了一个慈善机构。

Example: He approached the problem with a sense of meliorism, believing a solution could be found. 他以改良主义的态度来处理这个问题，相信可以找到解决方案。

近义词:optimism, progressivism, improvement
反义词:pessimism, cynicism, fatalism
词性:n.

Etymology:  From Latin *meliorismus*, from melior "better".
