disestablishing:/dɪsɪˈstæblɪʃɪŋ/ | v. | 废除；取消…的建制；解除…的正式地位 | To deprive (a church, for example) of its status as an officially established institution. |  The act of disestablishing the Church of England was a controversial topic.  废除英格兰教会的建立是一个有争议的话题。 | synonyms: abolishing, dissolving, terminating | antonyms: establishing, founding, instituting | verb

disestablishment:/dɪsɪˈstæblɪʃmənt/ | n. |  废除；取消建制；解除正式地位 | The act of depriving (something, typically a church) of its established status. | The disestablishment of the church led to a period of social upheaval. 教会的废除导致了一段时期的社会动荡。| synonyms: abolition, dissolution, termination | antonyms: establishment, foundation, institution | noun
