parturious:/pɑːrˈtʃʊəriəs/ /pɑːrˈtʊriːəs/| adj.|分娩的；临产的；多产的| Inclined to give birth; about to bring forth young; prolific.|例句：The parturient cat was meowing loudly.  临产的猫大声地喵喵叫。

例句：The farmer was pleased with his parturient cows. 农民对他的多产奶牛感到满意。|近义词：pregnant, fertile, fecund, prolific|反义词：barren, infertile, sterile|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *parturient- (stem of parturiēns), present participle of parturire "to be in labor, desire to bring forth," from parturiō "I am in labor," from parere "to bring forth, produce, bear."
