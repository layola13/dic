expired:/ɪkˈspaɪərd/ /ɛkˈspaɪərd/| adj.|过期的；失效的；终止的|Having come to the end of a period of validity; no longer in effect.| My passport has expired. 我的护照过期了.|Synonyms: lapsed, terminated, out of date, void, invalid|Antonyms: valid, current, unexpired|adjective

expired:/ɪkˈspaɪərd/ /ɛkˈspaɪərd/| v.|期满；终止；失效 (past participle of expire)|To come to an end; to cease to be valid.|His driving licence expired last week. 他的驾照上周到期了.|Synonyms: run out, lapse, terminate, cease, end|Antonyms: renew, extend, validate|verb
