nonviviparously:/ˌnɒnˌvɪvɪˈpærəsli/
adv.
非胎生地，非胎生
in a manner that is not viviparous; by producing eggs that hatch outside the body.
例句：Some snakes reproduce nonviviparously, laying eggs that hatch outside the mother's body.  一些蛇以非胎生的方式繁殖，产下的卵在母体外孵化。

例句：Unlike mammals, most reptiles reproduce nonviviparously. 与哺乳动物不同，大多数爬行动物以非胎生的方式繁殖。
近义词：oviparously
反义词：viviparously
词性：副词
