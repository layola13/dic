conceivably:/kənˈsiːvəbli/
adverb
可以想象地，可能地
It is conceivable that she will refuse to go. 她有可能拒绝去。
It's conceivable the hospital made a mistake.  可以想象，医院弄错了。
Synonyms: possibly, imaginably, perhaps, maybe
Antonyms: inconceivably, impossibly
词性: 副词
