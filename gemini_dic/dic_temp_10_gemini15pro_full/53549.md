disbanding:/dɪsˈbændɪŋ/
n.|解散，遣散|The disbanding of the army caused widespread unemployment.  军队的解散造成了广泛的失业。|
disband:/dɪsˈbænd/
v.|解散，遣散|The government decided to disband the committee. 政府决定解散委员会。|
Etymology:  From dis- + band (n.), influenced by French *desbander. Ultimately from Frankish *band "sign, banner," hence "group of men under a banner."
Synonyms: dissolving, breaking up, dismissing
Antonyms: assembling, gathering, uniting
