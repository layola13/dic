gallican:/ɡəˈlɪkən/
adjective
高卢的；法国的；法国教会的
Of or relating to Gaul or France, especially to the Roman Catholic Church in France or its doctrines asserting the liberties of the national church against papal claims.
The Gallican church maintained a certain independence from Rome. 法国教会保持着一定程度的独立于罗马教廷。
Near Synonyms: French, Gaulish
Antonyms: Roman (in the context of church authority)
词性: adjective


gallican:/ɡəˈlɪkən/
noun
高卢人；法国人；法国教会主义者
A person who adheres to the doctrines of Gallicanism.
He was a staunch Gallican, believing in the autonomy of the French Church. 他是一位坚定的法国教会主义者，相信法国教会的自治权。
Near Synonyms: Frenchman
Antonyms: Ultramontanist
