dreadly:/ˈdrɛdli/ | adv. |可怕地，非常地，极其地 | Extremely; terribly.  It emphasizes the negative quality of something. | The movie was dreadly boring. 这部电影非常无聊。| Synonyms: terribly, awfully, extremely, horribly | Antonyms: wonderfully, delightfully, pleasantly | adverb

dreadly:/ˈdrɛdli/ | adj. (archaic, rare) |可怕的，令人恐惧的 | Causing dread; inspiring great fear. | The dreadly silence filled the room.  可怕的寂静充满了房间。| Synonyms: dreadful, fearsome, terrifying | Antonyms: pleasant, delightful, comforting | adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From *dread* + *-ly*.  *Dread* comes from Old English *drǣdan* "to fear greatly."
