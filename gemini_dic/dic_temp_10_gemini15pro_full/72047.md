flirtatious:/flɜːrˈteɪʃəs/ | adj. | 爱调情的；轻佻的 | Behaving in such a way as to suggest a playful sexual attraction to someone. |  She gave him a flirtatious glance. 她轻佻地瞥了他一眼。|  She has a flirtatious manner. 她举止轻浮。| 近义词: coquettish, playful, teasing | 反义词: serious, reserved, modest | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From flirt + -atious.  "Flirt" itself possibly comes from the Old French "fleureter" meaning "to flick or flutter," suggesting a light and playful manner.
