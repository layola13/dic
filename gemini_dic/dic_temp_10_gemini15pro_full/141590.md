opprobrium:/əˈproʊbriəm/| n.|耻辱，责难，咒骂|the public disgrace arising from someone's shameful conduct.|The politician resigned in opprobrium after the scandal.  这位政客在丑闻曝光后耻辱地辞职了。|Synonyms: disgrace, shame, ignominy, dishonor, obloquy|Antonyms: honor, glory, acclaim, praise|noun
opprobrious:/əˈproʊbriəs/| adj.|表达责难的，骂人的，可耻的|expressing scorn or criticism.|His opprobrious remarks about his colleagues caused a great deal of tension in the office. 他对同事的辱骂性言论在办公室造成了很大的紧张气氛。|Synonyms: abusive, scurrilous, insulting, offensive, vituperative|Antonyms: complimentary, flattering, praising, laudatory|adjective
