|近义词：foolishness, silliness, tomfoolery, absurdity, idiocy|

词源：
From nincompoop (a fool) + -ery (a suffix forming nouns denoting a place where a specific activity is carried on, a state or condition, or a collection or group).  Ultimately from a fanciful or arbitrary formation.
