geniously:/ˈdʒiːnjəsli/ | adv. | 天才地；巧妙地；灵巧地 | In a way that shows great intelligence, skill, or cleverness. | He solved the problem geniously. 他天才地解决了这个问题。| 近义词：brilliantly, cleverly, ingeniously, skillfully | 反义词：stupidly, clumsily, awkwardly | 词性: 副词

词源: From genius + -ly.  Ultimately from Latin genius "guardian deity or spirit which watches over each person from birth; innate ability or inclination," later "high mental capacity."
