elytriform: /ɪˈlɪtrɪfɔːrm/
adjective
鞘翅状的， elytra-shaped
Resembling an elytron or elytra; having the form of an elytron or elytra.  The insect's elytriform forewings protected its delicate hindwings.  这种昆虫鞘翅状的前翅保护着它脆弱的后翅。
elytra-shaped, wing-cased
