bushiness:/ˈbʊʃɪnɪs/
noun
浓密，茂密
The quality of being bushy; thickness of foliage or hair.
The bushiness of his eyebrows added to his stern expression.  他浓密的眉毛让他看起来更严厉了。
thickness, denseness, fullness
thinness, sparseness
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From bushy + -ness.  Bushy comes from the Old English word "busc" meaning a shrub or thicket.
