drier:/ˈdraɪər/ (US), /ˈdraɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj. n.|更干燥的, 干燥剂|Comparative form of dry.  Used to describe something with less moisture or humidity than something else. Also refers to a substance or appliance used for drying.|The drier air of the desert made my skin feel tight. 沙漠中干燥的空气让我的皮肤感到紧绷。|The clothes drier is broken.  烘干机坏了。|近义词：more arid, less humid|反义词: wetter, more humid|词性:形容词, 名词|词源：From Middle English *drier, *dryere, comparative of drie (“dry”), from Old English drīgra, comparative of drīg (“dry”).

drier:/ˈdraɪər/ (US), /ˈdraɪə(r)/ (UK)| n.|干燥剂, 烘干机|A substance or appliance used for drying.|This paint needs a drier to speed up the process.  这种油漆需要干燥剂来加快干燥过程。|I put the laundry in the drier. 我把洗好的衣服放进了烘干机。|近义词：desiccant, dehumidifier (for substance); dryer, tumble dryer (for appliance)|反义词: humidifier|词性:名词|词源：From Middle English *drier, *dryere, comparative of drie (“dry”), from Old English drīgra, comparative of drīg (“dry”).
