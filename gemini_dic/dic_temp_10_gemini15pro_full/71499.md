flangeless:/ˈfleɪndʒləs/| adj.|无凸缘的，无边的|Lacking a flange or rim.  A flangeless wheel, for example, would have a smooth, continuous surface where the tire mounts, without the usual raised lip (flange).|The flangeless design of the wheel made it difficult to mount the tire.  无凸缘的车轮设计使得安装轮胎很困难。|The flangeless design allows for a smoother transition between surfaces. 无凸缘设计使表面之间过渡更平滑。|近义词：rimless, edgeless|反义词：flanged|词性: 形容词
