bad:/bæd/| adj.|坏的，不好的，严重的；不舒服的；腐败的；低劣的；不利的|Not good or pleasant; unpleasant or harmful; severe or serious; rotten or decayed; of poor quality; unfavorable.|The weather was bad yesterday. 昨天天气很糟。|The milk has gone bad. 牛奶变质了。|近义词:awful, terrible, horrible, poor, rotten|反义词:good, excellent, wonderful, great|词性:形容词

worse:/wɜːrs/| adj. adv.|更坏的，更糟的；更严重的|Comparative form of "bad"; more unpleasant, severe, or serious.|The traffic is worse today than yesterday. 今天的交通比昨天更糟。|His condition has gotten worse. 他的病情恶化了。|近义词:poorer, inferior|反义词:better|词性:形容词，副词
