flake:/fleɪk/| n. |薄片，碎片，雪花，古怪的人|A small, thin piece of something, typically one that has broken away from a larger piece.  Also, a person considered eccentric or unreliable.|The paint was coming off the wall in flakes.  油漆正一片片地从墙上脱落。 He's a bit of a flake, but he's harmless. 他有点古怪，但无害。|Synonyms: chip, shaving, fragment, eccentric, oddball|Antonyms: lump, block, normal, conventional|noun


flake:/fleɪk/| v. |剥落，变成碎片|To come off in flakes.|The old paint flaked off the wall. 旧油漆从墙上剥落了。|Synonyms: peel, chip, crumble|Antonyms: stick, adhere|verb
