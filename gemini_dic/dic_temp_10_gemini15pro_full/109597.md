lethean:/ˈliːθiən/| adj.|忘却的，令人遗忘的|Inducing forgetfulness or oblivion; of or relating to Lethe, the river of forgetfulness in Greek mythology.|The lethean waters of the river washed away all memory of her past life.  忘川之水洗去了她过去生活的所有记忆。|Synonyms: oblivious, amnesiac|Antonyms: mindful, remembering|adjective

lethean:/ˈliːθiən/| n.|忘却之物，麻醉剂|Something that causes forgetfulness, especially a drug.|He sought a lethean potion to escape the pain of his memories. 他寻找一种忘却的药水来逃避记忆的痛苦。|Synonyms: opiate, narcotic|Antonyms: stimulant, reminder|noun
