middle-of-the-roader:/ˈmɪdl əv ðə ˈroʊdər/| n.|中间派；溫和派；不走極端的人|Someone who holds moderate views, especially in politics, and avoids extreme positions.  A person who is not extreme in their political or social views. |例句：He's a middle-of-the-roader, not really liberal or conservative. 他是一个中间派，既不自由也不保守。

例句：The party attracts middle-of-the-roaders who are turned off by extremism. 该党吸引了那些厌恶极端主义的中间派。|近义词：moderate, centrist|反义词：extremist, radical|词性:noun
