anticommunisms:/ˌæntiˈkɒmjunɪzəmz/
|n.|反共产主义（复数）
|Opposition to communism, often in the form of organized movements or government policies.|
|During the Cold War, anticommunisms flourished in many Western countries.|冷战时期，许多西方国家盛行反共产主义。
|The various anticommunisms across the globe employed different strategies.|世界各地的各种反共产主义运动采取了不同的策略。
|近义词：anti-Marxism, anti-socialism|
|反义词：communism, Marxism, socialism|
|词性：noun|


Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek anti-)
communism (noun): a political theory derived from Karl Marx, advocating class war and leading to a society in which all property is publicly owned and each person works and is paid according to their abilities and needs.
