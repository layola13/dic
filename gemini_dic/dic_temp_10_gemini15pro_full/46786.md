danglers:/ˈdæŋɡlərz/ (US), /ˈdæŋɡləz/ (UK)| n.|悬挂物；悬垂物；未完成句；垂悬修饰语|Something that hangs or dangles loosely. In grammar, a dangling modifier is a word or phrase that modifies a word not clearly stated in the sentence. |The crystal danglers on the chandelier shimmered in the light. 枝形吊灯上的水晶垂饰在灯光下闪闪发光。|Synonyms: pendants, ornaments, decorations, dangling modifiers|Antonyms: supports, attachments|noun

dangling:/ˈdæŋɡlɪŋ/ (US), /ˈdæŋɡlɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|悬垂的；悬挂的；未完成的；垂悬修饰的|Hanging loosely or swinging freely.  In grammar, describes a modifier that does not logically modify any word in the sentence.|The dangling wires posed a safety hazard.  悬空的电线构成了安全隐患。|Synonyms: hanging, suspended, pendent, loose|Antonyms: attached, fixed, secure|adjective
