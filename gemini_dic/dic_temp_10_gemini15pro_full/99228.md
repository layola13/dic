intercom:/ˈɪntərkɒm/ (UK) /ˈɪntərˌkɑːm/ (US)| v.|通过内部通话系统联系|To communicate with someone using an intercom.|例句：He intercommed the security desk to report the suspicious activity. 他通过对讲机联系保安处报告可疑活动。|例句：She intercommed her assistant to bring her a coffee. 她用对讲机通知她的助手给她带杯咖啡。|近义词：call, contact|反义词: N/A|词性:verb
