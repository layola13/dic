falsifiable:/ˈfɔːlsɪfaɪəbl/ /ˈfɑːlsəˌfaɪəbəl/
adjective
可证伪的
Able to be proven false.  A statement, hypothesis, or theory is falsifiable if it can be logically contradicted by an empirical test.
A good scientific theory must be falsifiable.  一个好的科学理论必须是可证伪的。
The claim that "all swans are white" is falsifiable because observing a black swan would prove it false. “所有天鹅都是白色的”这一说法是可以证伪的，因为观察到一只黑天鹅就能证明它是错误的。
testable, refutable, disprovable
verifiable, provable, demonstrable
