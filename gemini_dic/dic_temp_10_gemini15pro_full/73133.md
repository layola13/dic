foragers:/ˈfɔːrɪdʒərz/ (US), /ˈfɒrɪdʒərz/ (UK)| n.|觅食者，搜寻者|People or animals who search for food or provisions.|例句：The foragers returned to the camp with berries and nuts. 觅食者带着浆果和坚果回到了营地。

例句：The squirrels are diligent foragers, always searching for nuts. 松鼠是勤奋的觅食者，总是在寻找坚果。

近义词：hunter-gatherers, scavengers, searchers|反义词:farmers, cultivators|词性:名词
