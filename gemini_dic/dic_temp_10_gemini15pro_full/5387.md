allying:/əˈlaɪɪŋ/
verb
结盟，联合
The two countries are allying against their common enemy.  这两个国家联合起来对抗共同的敌人。
present participle of ally
She is allying herself with the environmentalists. 她正与环保人士结盟。
近义词: uniting, joining forces, collaborating, cooperating
反义词: separating, dividing, opposing
