divet:/ˈdaɪvɪt/ | n. |跳水; (尤指从跳板上)跳入水中;  (从飞机上)跳伞; | A sudden plunge headfirst into water, especially from a springboard or diving board. Also a plunge from an aircraft. |  He did a perfect divet into the pool. 他完美地跳入泳池。|  The pilot had to divet the plane into the sea. 飞行员不得不将飞机迫降到海里。 | synonyms: plunge, dive, leap | antonyms: ascend, rise | noun
