embankment: /ɪmˈbæŋkmənt/ | n. | 堤岸，路堤 | A wall or bank of earth or stone built to prevent a river flooding an area, or to support a road or railway. |  The river overflowed its embankments. 河水漫过了堤岸。| synonyms: levee, dike, bank, rampart | antonyms: ditch, channel | noun

embankment: /ɪmˈbæŋkmənt/ | n. | (铁路或公路的)路基 | A raised bank or wall that carries a road or railway over an area of low ground. | The train sped along the embankment. 火车沿着路基飞驰。| synonyms: roadbed, causeway | antonyms: cutting, excavation | noun
