coheiress:/ˌkoʊˈɛərɪs/ | n. |共同女继承人|A woman who inherits property jointly with another or others.| Jane and Mary are coheiresses to their grandfather's fortune. 简和玛丽是她们祖父财产的共同继承人。|例句：The sisters were coheiresses to a vast estate.  姐妹俩是庞大遗产的共同继承人。

例句：As coheiresses, they had to decide how to divide the property. 作为共同继承人，她们必须决定如何分割财产。|近义词：joint heiress, co-inheritor (female)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *coerede, from Latin cohērēs (“joint heir”), from com- (“together”) + hērēs (“heir”).
