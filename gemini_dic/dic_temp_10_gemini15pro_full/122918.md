molybdena:/məˈlɪbdənə/ | n. | 钼矿; 二硫化钼 | A soft black mineral, molybdenum disulfide (MoS₂), the principal ore of molybdenum. It is used as a solid lubricant. |  Molybdena is an important source of molybdenum metal. 钼矿是钼金属的重要来源。|  The molybdena deposits were discovered in the 18th century.  这些钼矿床是在18世纪被发现的。 | synonyms: molybdenum disulfide, molybdenite | antonyms: None | noun
