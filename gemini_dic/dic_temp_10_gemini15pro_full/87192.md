here-and-there:/ˌhɪər ənd ˈðeər/ | adv. | 到处；零散地；偶然| In a scattered or haphazard manner; in various places. |  The books were scattered here and there on the floor. 书散落在地板上各处。| 近义词: everywhere, around, about | 反义词: nowhere, in one place | 词性: 副词

here:/hɪər/
there:/ðeər/

词源:  "Here" comes from Old English "hēr," and "there" comes from Old English "þær."  The combined phrase "here and there" simply denotes location in multiple unspecified places.
