outstrain:/ˌaʊtˈstreɪn/
verb
过度拉紧；过度劳累
to strain or exert to the utmost; surpass in straining
The athlete outstrained a muscle in his leg during the marathon.  这位运动员在马拉松比赛中拉伤了腿部肌肉。
近义词: overexert, overstrain, tax
反义词: relax, rest
词性: v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + strain.  Out- 表示“超过，过度”，strain 表示“拉紧，劳累”。
