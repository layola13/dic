oftener:/ˈɔːfənər (UK), ˈɔːftənər (US)| adv.|更经常地，更频繁地|Comparative adverb of "often," meaning "more frequently."|She visits her grandmother oftener now that she lives closer. 她现在住得更近了，所以更常去看望她的祖母。|I should exercise oftener to improve my health. 我应该更经常锻炼以改善我的健康。|近义词：more frequently, more often|反义词：less often, less frequently|词性:副词
