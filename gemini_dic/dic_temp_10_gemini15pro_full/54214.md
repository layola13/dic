disfigured:/dɪsˈfɪɡərd/
adjective
毁容的，破相的
marred or spoiled in appearance.
Her face was disfigured by a burn. 她的脸被烧伤毁容了。
近义词:deformed, mutilated, scarred
反义词:unblemished, flawless, perfect
词性:adjective

disfigure:/dɪsˈfɪɡər/
verb
损毁…的外形，使…变丑
to spoil the appearance of something or someone.
The accident disfigured him for life.  这起事故使他终生毁容。
近义词:mar, spoil, deform
反义词:beautify, enhance, improve
词性:verb
