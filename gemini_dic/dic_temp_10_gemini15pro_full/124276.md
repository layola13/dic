motettist:/moʊˈtɛtɪst/
n.
作曲家, especially one who composes motets
A composer of motets, particularly during the Renaissance or Baroque periods.
The motettist skillfully blended different voices to create a harmonious and expressive piece.  这位作曲家巧妙地融合了不同的声部，创作出一部和谐而富有表现力的作品。
composer, musician
--- (No clear antonym for a person who composes a specific type of music)
