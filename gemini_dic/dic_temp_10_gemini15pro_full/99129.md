interceptable:/ˌɪntərˈseptəbl/
adjective
可拦截的，可截取的
Capable of being intercepted.
例句：The missile was interceptable by our defense systems.  这枚导弹可以被我们的防御系统拦截。

例句：The enemy's communication was interceptable, giving us valuable intelligence. 敌人的通讯可以被截取，这为我们提供了宝贵的情报。

近义词：blockable, stoppable
反义词：unstoppable, unimpeded
词性：adjective
词源：From intercept + -able.  Intercept comes from the Latin intercipere, meaning "to take between," from inter- "between" + capere "to take."
