nominalise:/ˈnɒmɪnəlaɪz/ /ˈnɑːmɪnəlaɪz/| v.|名词化，将…变成名词|To convert (a word or phrase, such as an adjective or verb) into a noun, or to derive a noun from (another part of speech).|They nominalised the adjective "difficult" into the noun "difficulty." 他们把形容词“困难的”名词化成了名词“困难”。|The verb "fail" can be nominalised to form the noun "failure." 动词“失败”可以名词化形成名词“失败”。|synonyms: nounify, substantivize|antonyms: verbalize|verb
