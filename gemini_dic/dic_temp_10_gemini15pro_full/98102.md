incredible: /ɪnˈkrɛdəbəl/ (US), /ɪnˈkredɪbəl/ (UK)| adj.| 难以置信的，惊人的| not credible; impossible to believe; extraordinary.|  The view from the mountaintop was incredible. 山顶的景色令人难以置信。|  She has an incredible talent for music. 她有惊人的音乐天赋。|近义词：unbelievable, astonishing, amazing, remarkable, extraordinary|反义词：believable, credible, ordinary, unimpressive|词性: 形容词

词源:
From Latin *incredibilis*, from in- (“not”) + credibilis (“believable”), from credere (“to believe”).
