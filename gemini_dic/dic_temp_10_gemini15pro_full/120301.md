minicameras:/ˈmɪniˌkæmərəz/| n. |微型摄像机|A very small camera, often used for surveillance or covert recording. |例句：He used minicameras to monitor his store. 他用微型摄像机监控他的商店。

例句：Minicameras are becoming increasingly popular for personal security. 微型摄像机正变得越来越流行，用于个人安全。|近义词：spy cameras, tiny cameras, miniature cameras|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps large cameras) |词性:Noun
