click:/klɪk/| n. v.|点击，咔嗒声，发出咔嗒声|A short, sharp sound as of a switch being operated or of two hard objects coming quickly into contact.|She heard the click of the door lock. 她听到了门锁咔嗒一声。|近义词：snap, tick|反义词:(For the sound) thud, clunk|词性:名词，动词

click:/klɪk/| v.|点击，(使)发出咔嗒声，突然明白,一见如故|To make a short, sharp sound; to become suddenly clear or understood; to get along well with someone immediately.|He clicked the mouse button. 他点击了鼠标按钮。|近义词：press, tap (for mouse/button);  connect with, hit it off (for getting along)|反义词:(For sound) scrape, grind|词性:动词
