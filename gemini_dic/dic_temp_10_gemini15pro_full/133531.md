nonfiduciary:/ˌnɑːnfɪˈduːʃiəri/ /ˌnɒnfɪˈdjuːʃəri/
nonfiduciary: |adj. n.|非信托的；非受信的；非受托人的；非信用的|Not holding, involving, or requiring trust, especially not in a fiduciary capacity.| The investment advisor provided nonfiduciary services, meaning they weren't legally obligated to act in the client's best interest.| 近义词: nontrust, nontrustee |反义词: fiduciary|词性:形容词, 名词

nonfiduciary: |n.| 非受托人|A person who is not a fiduciary.| The nonfiduciary was not held to the same legal standards as the trustee.| 近义词: nontrustee |反义词: fiduciary, trustee|词性:名词
