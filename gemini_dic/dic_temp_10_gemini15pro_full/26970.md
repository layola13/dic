calcimine:/ˈkælsɪmaɪn/ | n. | 煅烧涂料；粉白| A white or tinted wash for walls, ceilings, etc., consisting of whiting or zinc white, glue, and water. |  The walls were coated with a cheap calcimine. 墙壁上涂了一层廉价的煅烧涂料。| synonyms: whitewash, distemper | antonyms: paint, varnish | noun


calcimine:/ˈkælsɪmaɪn/ | v. | 用煅烧涂料粉刷 | To coat or wash with calcimine. | They calcimined the ceiling of the living room.  他们用煅烧涂料粉刷了客厅的天花板。| synonyms: whitewash, coat | antonyms: strip, uncover | verb


ETYMOLOGY->
