evildoers:/ˈiːvɪlˌduːərz/| n. |作恶者；坏人|A person who does evil or commits wicked acts. |The evildoers were punished for their crimes. 这些作恶者因他们的罪行受到了惩罚。|Evildoers rarely consider the consequences of their actions. 作恶者很少考虑他们行为的后果。|Synonyms: criminals, villains, malefactors, wrongdoers, offenders|Antonyms: heroes, saints, benefactors, do-gooders|Noun
