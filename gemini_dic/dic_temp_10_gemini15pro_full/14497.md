avenage:/ˈævɪnɪdʒ/ | n. | 燕麦；野燕麦；（总称）谷类作物 |  Oats or wild oats; grain crops generally. | The farmer planted avenage in the field. 农民在地里种了燕麦。 | Synonyms: oats, grain, corn | Antonyms: none | noun

Etymology:
From Old French *avenage, from avene ("oat"), from Latin avena ("oat").
