hilltops:/ˈhɪlˌtɑːps/ | n. | 山顶，山峰 | The highest hilltops offered stunning views of the valley below.  最高的几个山顶可以俯瞰山谷，景色令人叹为观止。| summits, peaks, crests | valleys, bottoms | noun
Etymology: From Middle English *hiltoppes*, from Old English *hylltoppas*, plural of *hylltopp* (“hilltop”), from *hyll* (“hill”) + *topp* (“top”).
