nonexcusablenesses:/ˌnɒnɪkˈskjuːzəbəlnəsɪz/
n.
不可原谅的行为；不可原谅之处
The state or quality of being inexcusable; actions or behaviors that cannot be justified or forgiven.
His nonexcusablenesses led to his dismissal from the company. 他的不可原谅的行为导致他被公司解雇。
Synonyms: unjustifiability, unforgivability, indefensibility
Antonyms: justifiability, excusability, forgivability
