delaying:/dɪˈleɪɪŋ/
verb
推迟，延迟； 延期
putting something off to a later time; postponing
The heavy snow is delaying the train. 大雪延误了火车。
近义词: postponing, deferring, stalling
反义词: expediting, hastening, advancing
词性: present participle of "delay"

delay:/dɪˈleɪ/
noun
延误，延迟； 耽搁
the act of delaying or postponing something
There was a delay in the delivery of the mail. 邮件递送延误了。
近义词: postponement, deferment, holdup
反义词: expedition, haste, advance
词性: noun

delay:/dɪˈleɪ/
verb
推迟，延迟； 延期； 耽搁
to put something off to a later time; to postpone
We decided to delay our vacation until next year. 我们决定把假期推迟到明年。
近义词: postpone, defer, stall
反义词: expedite, hasten, advance
词性: verb

词源: From Old French *delaier (Modern French délai), from Latin dilatare "to spread out, extend, enlarge, postpone," from di- "apart, aside" + latus "wide, broad."
