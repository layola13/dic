counterinfluenced:/ˌkaʊntərˈɪnfluənst/
verb
对抗影响，抵消影响
To exert influence against; to neutralize or offset the influence of something or someone.
The government's new policy counterinfluenced the negative effects of the recession. 政府的新政策抵消了经济衰退的负面影响。
The positive message in the film counterinfluenced the negative stereotypes often portrayed in media.  电影中的积极信息抵消了媒体中经常描绘的负面刻板印象。
near synonyms: counteract, neutralize, offset
antonyms: reinforce, amplify, strengthen
