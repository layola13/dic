nonceremoniousnesses:/ˌnɒnsɛrɪˈməʊniəsnɪsɪz/
noun
缺乏仪式感；随便；唐突
lack of ceremony; informality; abruptness
The nonceremoniousness of his departure surprised everyone. 他不辞而别的唐突举动让每个人都感到惊讶。
Near Synonyms: abruptness, bluntness, brusqueness, informality, casualness
Antonyms: formality, politeness, courtesy, ceremony
n.

nonceremonious:/ˌnɒnsɛrɪˈməʊniəs/
adjective
缺乏仪式感的；随便的；唐突的
lacking ceremony; informal; abrupt
His nonceremonious dismissal angered the staff.  他不讲情面的解雇激怒了员工。
Near Synonyms: abrupt, blunt, brusque, informal, casual
Antonyms: formal, polite, courteous, ceremonial
adj.

ceremonious:/ˌsɛrɪˈməʊniəs/
adjective
讲究仪式的；隆重的；正式的
marked by ceremony; formal; stately
The opening ceremony was very ceremonious. 开幕式非常隆重。
Near Synonyms: formal, ritualistic, stately, conventional, traditional
Antonyms: informal, casual, unceremonious
adj.

ceremony:/ˈsɛrɪməni/
noun
仪式；典礼；礼节
a formal act or series of acts performed on a special occasion
The wedding ceremony was beautiful. 婚礼很漂亮。
Near Synonyms: rite, ritual, observance, formality, protocol
Antonyms: informality, casualness
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
"nonceremoniousnesses" comes from "nonceremonious" + "-ness".
"nonceremonious" comes from "non-" (not) + "ceremonious".
"ceremonious" comes from "ceremony" + "-ous".
"ceremony" comes from Old French "ceremonie" and ultimately from Latin "caerimonia" (sacred rite).
