antiswedish:/ˌæntiˈswiːdɪʃ/
adjective
反瑞典的；敌视瑞典的
Opposed to or hostile towards Sweden, its people, or its culture.
He held strong antiswedish sentiments due to historical conflicts. 他由于历史冲突而抱有强烈的反瑞典情绪。

The antiswedish propaganda spread quickly through the region. 反瑞典的宣传在该地区迅速传播。

近义词: anti-Scandinavian
反义词: pro-Swedish, Swedophile

词性: adjective
