barranca:/bəˈræŋkə/ | n. |峡谷，深谷|A deep, steep-sided ravine or gorge, especially one in Spain and Spanish America.  |例句：The mule train slowly descended into the barranca. 骡队缓缓下到峡谷中。

|例句：The barranca was carved by centuries of erosion.  这条峡谷是几个世纪的侵蚀雕刻而成的。|近义词：canyon, ravine, gorge, gully |反义词:mesa, plateau|noun
