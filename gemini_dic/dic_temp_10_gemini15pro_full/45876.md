cutely:/ˈkjutli/ | adv. | 可爱地，俏皮地 | In a cute manner; charmingly or endearingly. | She smiled cutely at the camera. 她对着镜头可爱地笑了笑。|  The kitten played cutely with the ball of yarn. 小猫俏皮地玩着毛线球。 | 近义词：adorably, charmingly, sweetly, prettily | 反义词：awkwardly, clumsily, uglily | 词性: 副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From cute + -ly. "Cute"  originates from the 18th century, a shortened form of "acute," meaning sharp or clever. Over time, its meaning shifted to charming and attractive, particularly in a childlike or playful way.  The suffix "-ly" is a common adverbial ending, transforming adjectives into adverbs.
