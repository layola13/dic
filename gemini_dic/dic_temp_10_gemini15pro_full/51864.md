dextrorotatory:/ˌdɛkstroʊˈroʊtətɔːri/
adjective
右旋的
Rotating the plane of polarized light to the right, clockwise.
The solution was dextrorotatory, indicating the presence of a specific sugar.  该溶液是右旋的，表明存在一种特定的糖。
Dextrose is dextrorotatory. 葡萄糖是右旋的。
近义词: clockwise
反义词: levorotatory
词性: adjective
