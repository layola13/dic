hawfinch:/ˈhɔːfɪntʃ/ | n. | 厚嘴鸟，星雀 | A finch (Coccothraustes coccothraustes) with a large, powerful beak for cracking seeds and nuts.  |  例句：The hawfinch is a rare sight in my garden.  厚嘴鸟在我的花园里很少见。

例句：We were lucky enough to spot a hawfinch cracking open a cherry stone. 我们很幸运地看到一只厚嘴鸟啄开了一个樱桃核。 | 近义词：grosbeak | 反义词: N/A | 词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *hagafinc, from haga "hedge, enclosure" + finc "finch."  So literally, a "hedge finch."
