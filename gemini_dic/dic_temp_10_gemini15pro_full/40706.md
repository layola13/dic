converter:/kənˈvɜːrtər/ (US), /kənˈvɜːtə(r)/ (UK)| n.|转换器，转化器|A device or machine that converts something from one form to another.|The catalytic converter reduces harmful emissions from the car. 催化转化器减少汽车的有害排放。|synonyms: transformer, adapter, transducer|antonyms:  (No direct antonym, depends on the specific type of conversion)|noun
