devising:/dɪˈvaɪzɪŋ/
verb
设计;发明;想出;策划
present participle of devise
例句：
They are devising a new marketing strategy.
他们正在制定新的营销策略。

He devised a plan to escape.
他想出了一个逃跑计划。

近义词：
creating, inventing, planning, formulating, scheming, contriving, engineering

反义词：
copying, imitating


词性: v.
