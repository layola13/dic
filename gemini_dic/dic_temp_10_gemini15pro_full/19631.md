bijugate:/baɪˈdʒuːɡət/| adj.|双轭的，两对的|Having two pairs of leaflets; paired.  Referring to a pinnate leaf with two pairs of leaflets.|The bijugate leaves of the plant were a distinctive characteristic. 这种植物的双叶复叶是一个显著特征。|Near synonyms: bigeminate, paired|Antonym: single, unpaired|adjective

bijugate:/baɪˈdʒuːɡət/| n.|双轭体|A bijugate structure or organism.|The botanist studied the bijugate structure under the microscope. 植物学家在显微镜下研究了这种双轭结构。|Near synonyms: double pair, twin pair|Antonym: single unit, monomer|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *bijugus* (“yoked two together”), from *bi-* (“two”) + *jugum* (“yoke”).
