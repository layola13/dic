overturned:/ˌōvərˈtərnd/| adj. v. (past participle)| 翻倒的，推翻的|Reversed, upset, or overthrown.  Derived from the verb "overturn," which means to cause something to turn over or capsize.|The overturned car blocked the road. 翻倒的汽车挡住了道路.|The court overturned the previous ruling. 法院推翻了之前的裁决.|Synonyms: capsized, inverted, upset, reversed, overthrown|Antonyms: upright, righted, restored, upheld|verb (past participle), adjective

overturn:/ˌōvərˈtɜːrn/| v.| 翻倒，推翻|To cause something to turn over or capsize.
