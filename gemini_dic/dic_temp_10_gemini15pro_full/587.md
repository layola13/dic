about-turning:/əˈbaʊtˌtɜːrnɪŋ/ | adj. |  转向的，掉头的，转变的 |  Relating to the act of turning around or changing direction, often suddenly.  | The car made an about-turning maneuver to avoid the obstacle. 汽车做了一个掉头动作以避开障碍物。

The about-turning point in his life was when he decided to pursue his passion.  他人生的转折点是他决定追求自己的热情的时候。| 近义词: turning, reversing, U-turning | 反义词:  straightforward, continuing | 词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From "about" (around) + "turning" (the act of changing direction).  "About" comes from Old English "abūtan," meaning "on the outside of, around."  "Turning" comes from Old English "turnian," meaning "to rotate, revolve."

USAGE->

The bus is about-turning at the end of the road. = 公交车在路的尽头掉头。
