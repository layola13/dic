nineteen:/ˌnaɪnˈtiːn/ /ˌnaɪnˈtin/| num. adj.|十九；十九个；十九的|A cardinal number, 10 + 9.  It's the number after eighteen and before twenty.|There are nineteen students in the class.  班上有十九个学生。|She is nineteen years old. 她十九岁了。|近义词: none (as nineteen is a specific number, true synonyms are difficult.  "A couple of dozen" could be a loose synonym in some contexts)|反义词: none|词性:数词,形容词
