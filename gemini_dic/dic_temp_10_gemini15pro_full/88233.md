hindmost:/ˈhaɪndməʊst/ (UK) /ˈhaɪndˌmoʊst/ (US)| adj.|最后面的，最后方的|Furthest back or last in order or position.  Relates to the back part of something, especially a moving line or procession.|The hindmost car in the race crashed.  赛跑中最后一辆车撞毁了。|The hindmost sheep in the flock were always vulnerable to predators. 羊群中最后面的羊总是容易受到捕食者的攻击。|近义词：rearmost, last, backmost|反义词：foremost, first, leading|词性:形容词
