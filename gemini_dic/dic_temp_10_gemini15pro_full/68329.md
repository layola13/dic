fall-from-grace:/ˈfɔːl frəm ɡreɪs/| n.|失宠，堕落，名誉扫地|A sudden loss of status, respect, or prestige.  A decline from a state of favor or innocence.|例句：The politician's fall from grace was swift after the scandal broke.  这位政客在丑闻曝光后迅速失宠。

例句：His fall from grace was a cautionary tale for everyone in the industry. 他的失宠对业内所有人都是一个警示。|近义词：downfall, disgrace, decline, ruin|反义词:rise, ascent, elevation|词性:noun
