outthinking:/ˌaʊtˈθɪŋkɪŋ/
verb
智胜；在思考上胜过
outsmarting someone by thinking more clearly and effectively
She outthought her opponents and won the negotiation. 她智胜了对手，赢得了谈判。
近义词: outsmarting, outmaneuvering, outslicking
反义词: being outsmarted, being outwitted


ETYMOLOGY->
From out- + thinking, from think.  The prefix "out-" suggests exceeding or surpassing in the action of the verb.
