mazed:/meɪzd/ | adj. | 困惑的，迷茫的 | Feeling confused and bewildered, like being lost in a maze. | She looked mazed when I told her the news. 她听到这个消息时看起来很困惑。|  I was completely mazed by the complex instructions.  我完全被复杂的指示弄糊涂了。 | 近义词: confused, bewildered, perplexed, lost | 反义词: clear, certain, understanding | 词性: 形容词

词源: From maze (n.) + -ed (suffix forming adjectives). Ultimately from amaze (v.), from Old French *amaaser, of uncertain origin.
