overcompensatingly: /ˌoʊvərˈkɒmpənˌseɪtɪŋli/ | adv. | 过度补偿地，矫枉过正地 |  In an excessive manner to make up for a perceived deficiency or weakness.  It implies an effort that goes beyond what is necessary or appropriate to correct a problem. | He smiled overcompensatingly, trying too hard to appear friendly after his earlier outburst. 他笑得太过分了，在他先前爆发之后，过于努力地想要显得友好。|  excessively, overly, too much | insufficiently, inadequately | adverb
