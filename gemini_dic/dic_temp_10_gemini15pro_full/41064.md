copoulos:/ˈkɒpʊləs/ | n. | 配偶，伴侣 | A spouse or partner, especially in a marriage or long-term relationship. |  例句：He introduced his copoulos to his family.  他把他的伴侣介绍给了他的家人。

例句：They've been copoulos for over a decade. 他们已经在一起超过十年了。| 近义词：spouse, partner, consort, companion | 反义词：ex, former spouse | 词性: 名词

词源: From Greek _κόπουλος_ (kópoulos), meaning "young man, stripling".  While the original Greek word referred specifically to a young man, the modern English usage has broadened to encompass partners of any gender.
