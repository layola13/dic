fortuned:/ˈfɔːrtʃənd/ /ˈfɔːrtʃʊnd/| adj.|幸运的；带来好运的；偶然发生的|Having had good fortune; lucky.  Experiencing good or bad luck by chance.|He was fortuned to escape the fire. 他很幸运地逃脱了火灾。|近义词：lucky, fortunate, blessed|反义词:unlucky, unfortunate, cursed|词性:形容词

词源：From Middle English *fortunen, from Old French fortuner, from Latin fortunare “to prosper, make happy,” from fortuna “fortune, chance.”

注意事项:

fortuned虽然是正确的英文单词，但现代英语中更常用fortunate。fortuned 带有古英语的色彩，现代英语中较少使用，尤其是在进行时态中。 建议使用fortunate及其各种形式。
