dioecious:/daɪˈiːʃəs/
adjective
雌雄异株的
Having male and female reproductive organs in separate individuals.
Holly trees are dioecious, meaning that a male tree must be planted near female trees in order for the latter to bear fruit. 冬青树是雌雄异株的，这意味着必须在雌树附近种植雄树才能让雌树结果。
Ginkgo trees are also dioecious. 银杏树也是雌雄异株的。
synonym: unisexual
antonym: monoecious
词性: adjective
