blers:/ˈblɜːrz/| n.|胡说八道，废话|Nonsense; meaningless or nonsensical talk.  Often used dismissively.|He was talking a load of blers, I didn't understand a word.|近义词：nonsense, gibberish, balderdash, claptrap|反义词:sense, truth|名词


词源:
Likely a variant of "blabbers," from "blab" (to talk foolishly or indiscreetly), which has origins in imitative words for chattering or babbling.
