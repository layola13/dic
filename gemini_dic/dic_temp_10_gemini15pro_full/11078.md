archaic:/ɑːrˈkeɪɪk/ | adj. | 古老的；过时的；古代的 | Very old or old-fashioned. Belonging to an earlier period and no longer in general use. |  例句：The company's computers are archaic.  这家公司的电脑很老旧。 |  例句：'Thou art' is an archaic form of 'you are'. “Thou art”是“you are”的古体。 | 近义词：ancient, obsolete, outdated, antiquated | 反义词：modern, contemporary, current | 词性: 形容词
