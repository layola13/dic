banderas:/bænˈdɛrəs/| n. |旗帜; 旗; |Flags, especially those of a country, organization, or group. banderas:/bænˈdɛrəs/| n. |旗帜; 旗; (象征)|Something that represents a particular idea, belief, or cause.
