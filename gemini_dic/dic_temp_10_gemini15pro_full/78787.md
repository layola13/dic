gillis:/ˈɡɪlɪs/| n.|吉利斯（姓氏；男子名）|A surname and a male given name, primarily of Scottish origin. It is an Anglicized form of the Gaelic personal name *Gille Iosa*, meaning "servant of Jesus."|例句：I met a man named Gillis yesterday. 昨天我遇到一个名叫吉利斯的男人。

例句：Gillis is a common surname in Nova Scotia. 吉利斯是新斯科舍省的一个常见姓氏。|近义词: (For given name) Gil, Giles |反义词: N/A|词性:noun
