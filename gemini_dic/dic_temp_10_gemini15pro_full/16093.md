例句：The carpenter bandsawn the wood to create the desired shape. 木匠用带锯切割木材以创造所需的形状。|近义词：sawed, cut|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "uncut", "whole")|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "band saw" + "-n" (adjectival suffix).  "Band saw" refers to a saw with a continuous toothed blade running over wheels.
