drop-ins:/ˈdrɒpɪnz/ (British) /ˈdrɑːpɪnz/ (American)| n.|即兴来访者；顺便拜访者；临时参加者|People who come to a class, event, or place without having arranged it in advance. |例句：The yoga class welcomes drop-ins.  瑜伽课欢迎随时参加。

例句：We had a few drop-ins at the party last night. 昨晚的聚会上有一些不速之客。|近义词：visitors, guests, callers|反义词: regulars, members|词性:noun
