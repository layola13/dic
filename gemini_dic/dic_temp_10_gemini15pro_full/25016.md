brutify:/ˈbruːtɪfaɪ/| vt.|使成畜生；使野蛮；使残酷|To make brutal; to deprive of human qualities; to make like a brute.|The constant exposure to violence had brutified the soldiers.  持续接触暴力使士兵们变得残暴。| The prison system can brutify even the most gentle souls. 监狱制度甚至可以让最温柔的灵魂变得野蛮。| 近义词:debase, degrade, brutalize|反义词:civilize, humanize, refine|词性:动词

Etymology:
From French *brutifier*, from *brute* (brute) + *-ifier* (suffix forming verbs from adjectives). Ultimately from Latin *brutus* (brute, irrational).
