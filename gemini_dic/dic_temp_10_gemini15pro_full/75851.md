funambulo:/fuːnæmˈbjuːloʊ/ /fʊˌnæmˈbjuːloʊ/
n.
tightrope walker; rope dancer
A person who performs acrobatics on a tightrope or slack rope.
The funambulo walked across the high wire with grace and precision.  走钢丝者以优雅和精准的姿态走过高空钢丝。
The crowd gasped as the funambulo performed a daring backflip.  当走钢丝者表演一个大胆的后空翻时，人群倒吸一口气。
Synonyms: acrobat, aerialist, tightrope walker, rope walker, wire walker
Antonyms: None (lack of a direct antonym for a specific profession)
Noun


Etymology->
From Italian *funambolo*, from Late Latin *funambulus*, from Latin *funis* ("rope") + *ambulare* ("to walk").
