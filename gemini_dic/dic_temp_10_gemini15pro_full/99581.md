interinhibitions:/ˌɪntərɪnhɪˈbɪʃənz/ | n. |相互抑制，交互抑制|The interplay of interinhibitions within the neural network allows for precise control of movement.  神经网络内相互抑制的相互作用允许对运动进行精确控制。|例句：His interinhibitions prevented him from speaking up. 他的自我抑制让他没法开口说话。

The dog's natural interinhibitions prevented it from attacking its owner, even when provoked.  即使被激怒，这只狗的自然抑制也阻止了它攻击主人。|近义词：inhibitions, restraints, suppression|反义词: impulsiveness, disinhibition |noun
