overoptimist:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːptɪmɪst/ | n. | 过度乐观者；盲目乐观的人 | Someone who is excessively optimistic; a person who is unrealistically hopeful. | He was known as an overoptimist, always believing everything would work out perfectly.  他被认为是一个过度乐观者，总是相信一切都会完美解决。| Near Synonyms: Pollyanna, utopian, idealist | Near Antonyms: pessimist, cynic, realist | Noun

overoptimistic:/ˌoʊvərˌɑːptɪˈmɪstɪk/ | adj. | 过度乐观的；盲目乐观的 | Excessively or unrealistically optimistic. | His overoptimistic projections for the company's growth proved to be inaccurate. 他对公司发展的过度乐观预测被证明是不准确的。| Near Synonyms:  excessively hopeful, unduly optimistic, unrealistic | Near Antonyms: pessimistic, cynical, realistic | Adjective
