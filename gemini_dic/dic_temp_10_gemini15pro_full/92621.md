-iation:/iˈeɪʃən/
noun
-ation, 名词后缀
Suffix forming nouns of action or process, result of an action or process, or state or condition.
The -iation suffix is used to form nouns from verbs ending in -iate.  For example, the verb "differentiate" becomes the noun "differentiation".
Differentiation is a key process in biology. 差异化是生物学中的一个关键过程。
