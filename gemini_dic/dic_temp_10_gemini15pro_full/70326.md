field-worker:/ˈfiːld ˌwɜːrkər/ (US) /ˈfiːld ˌwɜːkə(r)/ (UK)| n.|野外工作人员；现场工作人员|A person who works in the fields, especially in agriculture or research.  A person who gathers information or provides a service in the field, rather than in an office.|The field-workers were out harvesting the wheat. 野外工作人员正在收割小麦。|Synonyms: field hand, farmworker, researcher, surveyor|Antonyms: office worker, desk jockey|noun
