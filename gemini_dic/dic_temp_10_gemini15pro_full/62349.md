endermic:/ɛnˈdɜːrmɪk/
adjective
经皮的；皮内的
Administered or absorbed through the skin.
例句：The endermic application of the medicine provided quick relief.  这种药物的经皮给药提供了快速缓解。

例句：Endermic patches are a common way to deliver certain medications. 经皮贴剂是递送某些药物的常用方法。

近义词：transdermal, percutaneous
反义词：oral, intravenous
词性：adjective
