generification:/ˌdʒɛnərɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 泛化，通用化 | The process of making something more general or applicable to a wider range of situations or things.  It often involves abstracting common features or principles from specific instances. | The generification of the software allowed it to be used on multiple platforms.  该软件的泛化使其能够在多个平台上使用。 | generalization, abstraction | specialization, concretization | noun
