gamekeepers:/ˈɡeɪmˌkiːpəz/
n.
猎场看守人，野生动物管理员
A person employed to breed and protect game, typically on a large estate.
The gamekeeper patrolled the woods, looking for poachers.  猎场看守人在树林里巡逻，寻找偷猎者。
The gamekeeper ensures the pheasants are well-fed before the shooting season.  猎场看守人确保在狩猎季节开始前，野鸡都得到充足的喂养。
近义词：warden, ranger
反义词：poacher
词性：名词
