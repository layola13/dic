exanthema:/ˌɛksænˈθiːmə/ | n. |疹，发疹|An eruption or rash on the skin, typically occurring as a symptom of a disease.|Measles is an exanthema. 麻疹是一种发疹性疾病。|The child developed an exanthema after being exposed to poison ivy. 这孩子接触到毒葛后，身上起了疹子。|Synonyms: rash, eruption, efflorescence|Antonyms: None|Noun
