deleteriousnesses:/ˌdɛlɪˈtɪəriəsnɪsɪz/ | n. | 有害性，毒性 | The state or quality of being deleterious; harmfulness. | The deleteriousnesses of smoking are well documented. 吸烟的危害性已被充分证明。 | 近义词: harmfulness, noxiousness, perniciousness | 反义词: benefit, harmlessness | 词性: 名词

deleterious:/ˌdɛlɪˈtɪəriəs/ | adj. | 有害的，有毒的 | Causing harm or damage. |  Smoking is deleterious to your health. 吸烟有害健康。 | 近义词: harmful, damaging, injurious | 反义词: beneficial, helpful, advantageous | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Latin *dēlēteriōsus* "injurious," from *dēlēre* "to destroy, wipe out" (from *dē-* "down, away from" + *lēre* "to smear, blot out").
