crosshatching:/ˈkrɒsˌhætʃɪŋ/ (US) /ˈkrɒsˌhætʃɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|交叉影线，交叉排线|The technique of using intersecting sets of parallel lines to shade a drawing or engraving, creating different tones and textures. |Example 1: The artist used crosshatching to give the drawing a sense of depth. 艺术家运用交叉影线使画面更有深度感。

Example 2: Crosshatching is a common technique in pen and ink drawings. 交叉影线是钢笔水墨画中常用的技巧。|近义词：hatching, shading, rendering|反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider:  smoothness, flat color)|noun
