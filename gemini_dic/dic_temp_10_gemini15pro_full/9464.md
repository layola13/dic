antireligious:/ˌænti-rɪˈlɪdʒəs/
adjective
反对宗教的；非宗教的
opposed to religion; secular
例句：He has antireligious views. 他有反宗教的观点。

例句：The government implemented antireligious policies. 政府实施了反宗教政策。

近义词：irreligious, secular, profane, godless
反义词：religious, pious, devout
词性: adjective
