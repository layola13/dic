acetify: /əˈsɛtɪfaɪ/
verb
使醋化；变成醋；使变酸
To turn into vinegar; to make or become sour like vinegar.
The cider left in the barrel began to acetify.  留在桶里的苹果酒开始变酸。

The warm weather acetified the wine. 天气暖和使葡萄酒变酸了。

近义词: sour, acidify
反义词: sweeten, alkalize
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *acétifier*, from acéte (“vinegar”) + -fier (suffix forming verbs meaning “to make” or “to become”).  From Latin *acētum* (“vinegar”) + *-ficāre* (from *facere* “to make, to do”).
