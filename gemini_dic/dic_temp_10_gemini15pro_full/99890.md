internalizes:/ɪnˈtɜːrnəlaɪzɪz/
v.
使内化；吸收
to make (attitudes or behavior) part of one's nature by learning or unconscious assimilation.
He internalized the values of his parents. 他将父母的价值观内化于心。
assimilate, incorporate, absorb
