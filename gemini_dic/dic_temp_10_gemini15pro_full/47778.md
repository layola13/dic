decantation:/diːkænˈteɪʃn/
n.
倾析；滗析；倾注
The process of pouring a liquid gently so as not to disturb the sediment or other matter.
The wine was decanted before serving.  这葡萄酒在上桌前先被滗析了。

sedimentation:/ˌsedɪmenˈteɪʃn/
n.
沉淀；沉降
The process of settling or being deposited as a sediment.
Sedimentation is a natural process that helps to purify water. 沉淀是一个有助于净化水的自然过程。

pouring:/ˈpɔːrɪŋ/
n.
倾倒；倾泻
The action of pouring a liquid.
The pouring of the concrete foundation took several hours.  浇筑混凝土基础花了几个小时。

filtration:/fɪlˈtreɪʃn/
n.
过滤；滤清
The process of filtering a liquid or gas.
Filtration is an essential step in water purification. 过滤是水净化中的一个重要步骤。

centrifugation:/ˌsɛntrɪfjuˈɡeɪʃn/
n.
离心分离
The process of separating substances by the use of a centrifuge.
Centrifugation is used to separate blood cells from plasma. 离心分离用于从血浆中分离血细胞。
