epistolatory: /ɪˌpɪstəˈlɔːri/ /ɪˌpɪstəˈleɪtɔːri/
adjective
书信体的；用书信表达的
Relating to or suitable for letters; written in the form of a series of letters.
Her epistolatory style is reminiscent of 18th-century novelists. 她的书信体风格让人想起18世纪的小说家。
The novel unfolds through an epistolatory exchange between the two main characters. 这部小说通过两位主角之间的书信往来展开。
Synonyms:  letter-writing, correspondence-based
Antonyms: conversational, spoken
词性: 形容词
