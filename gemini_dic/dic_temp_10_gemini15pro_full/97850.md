inlayer:/ˈɪnˌleɪər/
n.
嵌入层;内层;中间层
A layer embedded within another material.
The artisan carefully added an inlayer of gold leaf to the wooden box.  工匠小心翼翼地在木盒上加了一层金箔内层。
The inlayer of silk provided extra warmth to the winter coat.  丝绸内衬为冬衣增添了额外的保暖性。
lining, layer, lamination
outer layer, exterior
noun
