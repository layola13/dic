old-timer:/ˈoʊldˌtaɪmər/| n. |老前辈；老手；老居民|A person who has been involved in a particular place or activity for a long time.|例句：He's an old-timer in the fire department. 他是消防队里的老前辈。|近义词：veteran, expert, old hand|反义词：newcomer, rookie, beginner|词性:名词

old-timer:/ˈoʊldˌtaɪmər/| n. |老式计时器；老式钟表|(Less common) An old-fashioned timer or clock.|例句：My grandfather still uses his old-timer pocket watch. 我祖父还在用他的老式怀表。|近义词：antique clock, vintage timer|反义词：modern clock, digital timer|词性:名词
