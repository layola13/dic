low-rises: /ˈloʊˌraɪzɪz/ | n. | 低层建筑（通常指公寓楼）| Buildings, especially apartment buildings, having few stories. |  Many people prefer living in low-rises because they feel more connected to the community. 许多人更喜欢住在低层建筑里，因为他们觉得与社区的联系更紧密。

| Example: The city council approved the construction of several new low-rises in the downtown area. 市议会批准在市中心区域建造几座新的低层建筑。| Synonym: low-rise buildings, low-story buildings | Antonym: high-rises, skyscrapers | noun
