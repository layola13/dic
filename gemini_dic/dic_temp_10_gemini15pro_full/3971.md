agrotourist:/ˈæɡroʊˌtʊrɪst/ | n. | 农业旅游者；乡村旅游者 | A person who takes a vacation in a rural location, and often participates in activities related to agriculture. | Example: The agrotourists enjoyed picking apples in the orchard. 这些农业游客喜欢在果园里摘苹果。

Example: Many agrotourists are interested in learning about sustainable farming practices. 许多农业游客有兴趣了解可持续农业的实践。 | Near synonyms: rural tourist, farm tourist, agricultural tourist | Antonym: urban tourist | Noun
