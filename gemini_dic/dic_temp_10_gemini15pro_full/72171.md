Etymology:
From Middle English *flor, *flore, from Old French flor, from Latin flōr, from Proto-Italic *flōs, from Proto-Indo-European *bʰleh₃- (“to blossom, bloom”). Cognate with Italian fiore, Spanish flor, Portuguese flor. More at flower.
