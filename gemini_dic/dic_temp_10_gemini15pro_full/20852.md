blat:/blæt/ | v. | 公开地、大声地表达；吹牛；炫耀 | To express something loudly and publicly, often in a boastful or self-satisfied way.  | He blatted his opinions to anyone who would listen. 他向任何愿意听的人大声宣扬他的观点。| Synonyms: boast, brag, trumpet | Antonyms: whisper, murmur | verb

blat:/blæt/ | n. | 喧闹的声音；喇叭声 | A loud, harsh sound, especially of a horn. | The blat of the car horn startled the pedestrians. 汽车喇叭的刺耳声吓到了行人。 | Synonyms: blare, blast, honk | Antonyms: whisper, murmur | noun
