hack-up:/ˈhæk ʌp/ (US) /ˈhæk ʌp/ (UK)| n. |咳出之物；(编程)粗制滥造的程序|Something coughed up from the lungs; (informal) A piece of software or code that is poorly designed or implemented. |The smoker had a terrible hack-up in the morning.  那个吸烟者早上咳得很厉害。

He showed me the website he built – it was a real hack-up.  他给我看了他建的网站——真是个粗制滥造的东西。|近义词: (for cough) phlegm, sputum; (for code) kludge, botch|反义词: (for code) elegant code, well-designed software|词性:名词

hack-up:/ˈhæk ʌp/ (US) /ˈhæk ʌp/ (UK)| v. |咳出；(编程)草草拼凑|To cough something up; (informal) To quickly and poorly produce a piece of software or code. |He hacked up a lungful of phlegm. 他咳出了一大口痰。

She hacked up a basic website in a few hours.  她几个小时就草草拼凑了一个基本的网站。|近义词: (for cough) expectorate; (for code) cobble together, throw together|反义词: (for code) carefully develop, meticulously craft|词性:动词
