inerrancy:/ɪˈnɛrənsi/| n. |无误性，绝对正确|The quality or state of being without error; infallibility.|The doctrine of biblical inerrancy is central to some Christian denominations. 圣经无误论是某些基督教派的核心教义。|近义词：infallibility, accuracy, correctness|反义词：fallibility, error, inaccuracy|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *inerrantia*, from in- (“not”) + errantia (“wandering, error”), from errans, present participle of errare (“to wander, to err”).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ers- (“to move, to wander”).
