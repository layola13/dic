hardly:/ˈhɑːrdli/| adv.|几乎不，简直不，刚刚|Used to say that something is almost not true or almost does not happen at all.  It emphasizes the smallness or insignificance of something.|He hardly ate anything. 他几乎什么都没吃。|近义词：barely, scarcely|反义词：easily, definitely|词性:副词
词源: From Middle English *hardily, from hard + -ly.
