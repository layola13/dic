antistyles:/ˈæntiˌstaɪlz/ | n. |对立的风格，相反的风格|A style that is the opposite of another, or that reacts against another. |  The minimalist architecture of the building was a deliberate antistyle to the ornate Victorian houses surrounding it.  这栋建筑的极简主义风格是特意与周围华丽的维多利亚式房屋形成对比的。|  Her punk rock fashion was a clear antistyle to her mother's classic elegance.  她的朋克摇滚时尚和她母亲的古典优雅风格截然相反。| synonyms: counter-styles, opposing styles | antonyms: similar styles, matching styles | noun
