nonsensorial:/ˌnɒnsɛnˈsɔːriəl/
adjective
非感官的，非感觉的
Not pertaining to or perceived by the senses; not involving or derived from sensation.
The nonsensorial aspects of meditation can be difficult to grasp.  冥想的非感官方面很难理解。
Nonsensorial experiences, like intuition, are often discounted by science.  像直觉这样的非感官体验经常被科学所忽视。

近义词: extrasensory, immaterial, intangible, abstract
反义词: sensory, physical, material, tangible

词性: adjective
