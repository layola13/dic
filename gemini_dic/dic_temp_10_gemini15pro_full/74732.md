freesias:/ˈfriːʒəz/ |ˈfriːziəz/| n. |小苍兰, 香雪兰|A fragrant South African plant with narrow, sword-shaped leaves and spikes of brightly colored, funnel-shaped flowers.  They are often grown for cut flowers and their sweet scent. | The freesias filled the room with a delightful fragrance. 小苍兰让房间充满了宜人的香味。|  The bride carried a bouquet of white freesias.  新娘拿着一束白色小苍兰。|Synonyms:  None (specific flower) - similar scents might be compared to other flowers like lily of the valley, hyacinth| Antonyms:  N/A (it's a noun representing a plant) | Noun
