immixture:/ɪmˈmɪkstʃər/
n.
混合, 混合物
the action of mixing or being mixed; a mixture.
The cake was an immixture of flour, sugar, eggs, and butter.  这个蛋糕是面粉、糖、鸡蛋和黄油的混合物。
The immixture of different cultures created a unique atmosphere in the city. 不同文化的融合在这个城市创造了独特的氛围。
Synonyms: mixture, blend, combination, compound, admixture
Antonyms: separation, division, segregation, purity
noun
