libelers:/ˈlaɪbələrz/
n.
诽谤者
People who publish or spread false and malicious statements that damage someone's reputation.
The libelers were sued for defamation. 诽谤者被控告诽谤。
近义词：defamers, slanderers, calumniators
反义词：N/A
词性：名词
