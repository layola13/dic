non-voter:/ˌnɒnˈvəʊtər/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈvoʊtər/| n. |非投票者，不投票的人|A person who is eligible to vote but chooses not to or is unable to.|例句：Non-voters often feel disenfranchised from the political process. 非投票者常常感到自己被剥夺了政治权利。

例句：The number of non-voters increased in the last election. 上次选举中不投票的人数增加了。

|近义词：abstainer|反义词:voter|词性:noun
