disprover:/dɪsˈpruːvər/| n. |反驳者，证伪者|Someone who disproves something, especially a theory or belief. |例句：The scientist acted as a disprover to the long-held theory.  这位科学家反驳了长期以来持有的理论。

例句：He became a disprover of the popular misconception. 他成为了这一普遍误解的反驳者。

|近义词：refuter, contradictor, opponent|反义词:proponent, supporter, advocate|词性:noun
