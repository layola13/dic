nonacquiescence: /ˌnɒnˌækwiˈɛsəns/ | n. | 不默从，不服从，不顺从 |  The state of not agreeing or consenting; refusal to accept a decision or policy.  It implies a passive resistance rather than active opposition. | The government's nonacquiescence to the court ruling led to further legal battles. 政府对法院裁决的不默从导致了进一步的法律斗争。|  The union showed their nonacquiescence by refusing to sign the new contract. 工会通过拒绝签署新合同来表示他们的不顺从。 | Synonyms: dissent, disagreement, refusal, objection, defiance | Antonyms: acquiescence, agreement, compliance, acceptance, obedience | noun
