gravitating:/ˈɡrævɪteɪtɪŋ/
v.
被吸引；受吸引；倾向；移动
Moving towards something; being attracted to something.
Young people are gravitating towards city life.  年轻人正被城市生活所吸引。
Synonyms: moving towards, tending towards, inclining towards, drifting towards
Antonyms: repelling, pushing away
