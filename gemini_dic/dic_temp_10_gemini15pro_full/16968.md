bastardised:/ˈbæstərdaɪzd/ | adj. | 杂交的，窜改的，劣化的 | Changed from its original form in a way that makes it impure or inferior.  Degraded or debased. |  The architect complained that the builder had bastardised his design.  建筑师抱怨说建筑商篡改了他的设计。 | Synonyms: corrupted, debased, adulterated, distorted, mangled, perverted | Antonyms: purified, refined, improved, enhanced | 词性: 形容词

bastardise:/ˈbæstərdaɪz/ | vt. |  使杂交，窜改，劣化 | To change something from its original form in a way that makes it impure or inferior. To degrade or debase. | The script was bastardised by the director, who added unnecessary scenes.  剧本被导演窜改了，他添加了不必要的场景。| Synonyms: corrupt, debase, adulterate, distort, mangle, pervert | Antonyms: purify, refine, improve, enhance | 词性: 动词
