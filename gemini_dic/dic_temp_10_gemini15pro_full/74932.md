fretum:/ˈfriːtəm/| n.|海峡，狭窄的海域|A narrow passage of sea connecting two larger bodies of water.|The Strait of Gibraltar is a fretum separating Europe and Africa. 直布罗陀海峡是一条分隔欧洲和非洲的海峡。|近义词：strait, channel, sound|反义词：isthmus, peninsula|词性:名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fretum* "strait, narrow sea," related to *ferre* "to carry."  The notion is likely of a "carried" or "flowing" body of water.
