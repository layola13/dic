fantasized:/ˈfæntəsaɪzd/
verb
幻想，想像
indulged in fantasies; imagined something unreal or improbable.
She fantasized about winning the lottery. 她幻想中了彩票。
He fantasized about traveling the world. 他幻想环游世界。
Synonyms: dreamed, imagined, envisioned, daydreamed
Antonyms:  faced reality,  acknowledged the truth
