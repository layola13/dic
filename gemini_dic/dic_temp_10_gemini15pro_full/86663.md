hellenistic:/ˌhɛlɪˈnɪstɪk/| adj.|希腊化的，希腊主义的|Of or relating to Greek history, language, and culture from the death of Alexander the Great to the defeat of Cleopatra and Mark Antony by the Romans (323-30 BC). This period was marked by the spread of Greek culture and language throughout the Mediterranean and Near East.|The museum has a wonderful collection of Hellenistic sculptures.| 近义词: Hellenic, Greek| 反义词: Roman, Non-Greek|词性:形容词

hellenistic:/ˌhɛlɪˈnɪstɪk/| n.|希腊化时期|The period of Greek history, language, and culture from the death of Alexander the Great to the defeat of Cleopatra and Mark Antony by the Romans (323-30 BC).|The Hellenistic period saw the fusion of Greek and Eastern cultures.| 近义词: Hellenistic Age| 反义词: Classical Greek period|词性:名词
