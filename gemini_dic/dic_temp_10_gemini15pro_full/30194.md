celestinian:/səˈlɛstiən/ /səˈlestʃən/ | n. adj. | 天青石，天蓝色的 | A mineral, strontium sulfate (SrSO4), occurring in orthorhombic crystals, typically pale blue or white. Also used as an adjective to describe the pale blue color of the mineral. |  The museum displayed a beautiful celestinian crystal.  博物馆展出了一块漂亮的天青石晶体。| 近义词: sky-blue, azure, cerulean | 反义词:  (As a color) dark, black | 词性: noun, adjective
