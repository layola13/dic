encrusting:/ɪnˈkrʌstɪŋ/
adjective
使覆盖硬壳的；结壳的；镶嵌的
Covering with a hard crust; forming a crust.
The encrusting algae covered the rocks near the shore.  这些结壳藻覆盖了海岸附近的岩石。
Barnacles were encrusting the hull of the ship. 藤壶包住了船体。
近义词:coating, covering, overlaying
反义词:bare, exposed, uncovered
词性:adjective

encrust:/ɪnˈkrʌst/
verb
使覆盖硬壳；结壳；镶嵌
To cover with a hard crust; to inlay.
Salt crystals encrusted the edges of the pan. 盐晶体结壳覆盖在锅的边缘。
The jeweler encrusted the crown with diamonds. 珠宝商在王冠上镶嵌了钻石。
近义词:coat, cover, inlay
反义词:strip, uncover, expose
词性:verb
