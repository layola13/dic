dopplerite:/ˈdɒpləraɪt/ | n. | 多普勒泥炭 | A dark brown to black, elastic, jelly-like organic mineral found in peat bogs, consisting primarily of humic acids and having a high water content. It shrinks considerably upon drying. | Dopplerite was discovered in a peat bog in Austria. 多普勒泥炭是在奥地利的泥炭沼泽中发现的。|Synonyms: humic gel, doppelerite | Antonyms: None | Noun
