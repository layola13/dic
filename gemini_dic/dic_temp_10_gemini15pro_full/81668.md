green-thumbed:/ˌɡriːnˈθʌmd/| adj.|有园艺天赋的；擅长种植的|Having or showing a natural talent for growing plants successfully.|例句：My grandmother is green-thumbed; everything she plants flourishes. 我的祖母很擅长园艺；她种的每样东西都很茂盛。

例句：He's not very green-thumbed; his houseplants are always dying. 他不太擅长种植物；他的室内植物总是枯死。|近义词：skilled gardener, expert grower, good with plants|反义词：having a black thumb, unable to grow plants|词性:形容词
