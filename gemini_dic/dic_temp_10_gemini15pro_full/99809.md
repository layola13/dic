intermigrates:/ˌɪntərˈmaɪɡreɪts/ | v. |迁徙，移居（尤指在同一国家或地区内）| To migrate within a country or region. |  Example: The population often intermigrates between rural and urban areas for work. 人口经常在农村和城市地区之间迁移工作。

Example:  Birds intermigrate between different parts of the country depending on the season. 鸟类根据季节在国内不同地区之间迁徙。 | 近义词: migrate, relocate, resettle | 反义词: emigrate, immigrate | 词性: verb

词源:  From inter- + migrate.  "Inter-" prefix meaning "between" or "among" combined with "migrate" meaning "to move from one place to another."
