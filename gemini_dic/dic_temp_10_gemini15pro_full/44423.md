cross-pollinated:/ˌkrɒs ˈpɒləneɪtɪd/ | adj. |异花授粉的| Fertilized by pollen from a different plant of the same species. | The apple tree was cross-pollinated with a variety of crabapple. 这棵苹果树与一种海棠杂交授粉。|  cross-fertilized, hybridized | self-pollinated | adjective
cross-pollinate:/ˌkrɒs ˈpɒləneɪt/ | v. |异花授粉| To fertilize a plant with pollen from another plant of the same species. | Bees cross-pollinate flowers as they collect nectar. 蜜蜂在采集花蜜时会使花朵异花授粉。| cross-fertilize, hybridize | self-pollinate | verb
