oedematously: /ɪˌdiːməˈtəsli/ /ɪˌdeməˈtəsli/
adjective
水肿地，肿胀地
Characterized by or exhibiting edema; swollen.  Marked by an excessive accumulation of fluid in the tissues.
His ankles were oedematously swollen after the long flight.  长时间飞行后，他的脚踝肿胀了。
Synonyms: swollen, puffy, bloated, distended
Antonyms: deflated, shrunken, contracted
词性: adjective
