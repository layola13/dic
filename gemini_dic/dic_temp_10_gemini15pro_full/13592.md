attenuator:/əˈtenjʊeɪtər/ | n. | 衰减器; 减弱器; 缓冲器 | A device that reduces the strength of a signal, sound, light, or other form of energy. | The engineer used an attenuator to reduce the signal strength to an acceptable level. 工程师使用衰减器将信号强度降低到可接受的水平。 |  The guitarist used an attenuator to lower the volume of his amplifier without affecting the tone. 吉他手使用衰减器来降低放大器的音量而不影响音调。 | synonym: damper, reducer, suppressor | antonym: amplifier, booster | noun
