mossed:/mɒst/ /mɔːst/| adj.| 生满苔藓的；覆盖着苔藓的 |Covered with moss; mossy.| The old stone wall was mossed and crumbling.  古老的石墙长满了苔藓，正在 crumbling。|Near Synonyms: mossy, overgrown, verdant|Antonyms: bare, barren|adjective

moss:/mɒs/ /mɔːs/| n.| 苔藓；泥沼 |A small flowerless green plant that lacks true roots, growing in low carpets or rounded cushions in damp habitats and on rocks, walls, and trees.| The rocks were covered in thick green moss. 岩石上覆盖着厚厚的绿色苔藓。|Near Synonyms: lichen, algae|Antonyms: N/A|noun
