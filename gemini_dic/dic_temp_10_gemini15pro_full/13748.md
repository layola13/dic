auctioned:/ˈɔːkʃənd/ | v. |拍卖的|Past participle of "auction," meaning something was sold at an auction.|The painting was auctioned off for a million dollars. 这幅画以一百万美元的价格拍卖出去了。|Synonyms: sold, traded, bartered|Antonyms: bought, purchased|verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From the Latin "auctio" meaning "increase, enhancement, auction."  It entered English in the 16th century.
