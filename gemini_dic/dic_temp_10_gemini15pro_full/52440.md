die-down:/daɪ daʊn/
verb
逐渐减弱，平息
to become less strong, loud, or noticeable
The excitement eventually died down.  兴奋最终平息了。
The applause died down and the speaker began. 掌声逐渐平息，演讲者开始了。
subside, lessen, decrease, fade, wane
increase, intensify, escalate, flare up
verb
