geocells:/ˈdʒiːoʊsɛlz/
n.
蜂窝约束系统；土工格室
Three-dimensional cellular confinement systems made of geosynthetics like geotextiles, geogrids, or geomembranes.  They are used to confine and stabilize soil or other aggregate materials, often in civil engineering applications.
The geocells were installed to prevent erosion on the steep slope. 土工格室被安装在陡坡上以防止水土流失。

The project utilized geocells filled with gravel to create a stable foundation. 该项目利用装满砾石的土工格室来创建一个稳定的地基。

Synonyms: cellular confinement systems, geocell confinement system
Antonyms: N/A
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"geo-" (earth) + "cell" (small compartment).  "Geo" comes from Greek "gē" (earth), and "cell" comes from Latin "cella" (small room, compartment).
