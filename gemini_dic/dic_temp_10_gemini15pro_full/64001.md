epistolical: /ɪˌpɪˈstɒlɪkəl/ /ɪˌpɪˈstɑːlɪkəl/
adjective
书信体的；与书信有关的
Of or relating to letters; contained in or suitable to a letter.
Example:  The novel is written in an epistolary style. 这部小说是以书信体写成的。

Example:  His epistolary exchanges with his friend revealed much about his life. 他与朋友的书信往来揭示了他生活的很多方面。

近义词:lettered, literary
反义词:None
词性:adjective
