overpassing:/ˌōvərˈpæːsɪŋ/ | n. | 超越，胜过，高架桥 | An act of surpassing or exceeding. overpassing:/ˌōvərˈpæːsɪŋ/ | adj. | 超越的，胜过的 |  Surpassing or exceeding something. | His overpassing ambition led to his downfall. 他过度的野心导致了他的垮台。  | synonyms: surpassing, exceeding, superior | antonyms: inferior, lesser | adjective
