parodied:/ˈpærədid/ | adj. | 被模仿的 |  having been imitated in an amusing or satirical way | His parodied walk was instantly recognizable. 他被模仿的走路姿势立刻就被认出来了。| synonyms: mimicked, satirized, lampooned, mocked | antonyms: original, genuine | adjective
