insufficientness:/ˌɪnsəˈfɪʃəntnəs/
n.
不足，不够
The state or quality of being insufficient; inadequacy.
His insufficientness as a leader led to the project's failure. 他作为领导者的不足导致了项目的失败。

近义词: inadequacy, deficiency, shortage, lack, scarcity
反义词: sufficiency, adequacy, abundance, plenty
