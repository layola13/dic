edginess:/ˈɛdʒɪnɪs/| n.|紧张不安；急躁；易怒|A state of being nervous, irritable, or easily agitated.  The quality of being exciting and slightly dangerous.|例句：There's an edginess in the city these days due to the recent protests. 最近由于抗议活动，城市里弥漫着一种紧张不安的气氛。

例句：The film's edginess kept me on the edge of my seat. 这部电影的紧张刺激让我一直坐立不安。|近义词：nervousness, anxiety, irritability, tension, unease|反义词:calmness, tranquility, composure|词性:noun
