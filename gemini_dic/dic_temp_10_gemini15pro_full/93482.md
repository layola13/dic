illustrative:/ɪˈlʌstrətɪv/| adj.|（用图片）说明的，（用插图）装饰的|Containing illustrations. (Less common usage) | The book is illustrative with colorful diagrams. 这本书配有彩色图表进行说明。| synonyms: illustrated, pictorial, visualized | antonyms: unillustrated, plain, text-only | adjective
