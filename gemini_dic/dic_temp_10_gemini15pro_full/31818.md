checkup:/ˈtʃɛkʌp/ (US), /ˈtʃekʌp/ (UK)| n.|检查；体检|A thorough examination, especially a medical one to detect any problems.|I went to the doctor for my annual checkup. 我去看了医生做了年度体检。|Synonyms: examination, inspection, review|Antonyms: neglect, disregard|noun

checkup:/ˈtʃɛkʌp/ (US), /ˈtʃekʌp/ (UK)| n.|核实，核对|A check or verification of something.|The police conducted a checkup on the suspect's alibi. 警方对嫌疑人的不在场证明进行了核实。|Synonyms: verification, confirmation, audit|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps "overlooking" or "disregarding")|noun
