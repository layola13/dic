chondro:/ˈkɒndrəʊ/ /ˈkɑːndrəʊ/| combining form |软骨，软骨的|Relating to cartilage.  From Greek *chondros* "cartilage, gristle."|chondrocyte (软骨细胞):  A chondrocyte is a cell found in cartilage. 软骨细胞是一种存在于软骨中的细胞。|chondroma (软骨瘤):  A chondroma is a benign tumor of cartilage. 软骨瘤是一种良性的软骨肿瘤。|近义词:cartilaginous|反义词: (None -  it's a combining form describing a tissue type, not a quality)|combining form
