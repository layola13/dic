grappler:/ˈɡræplər/| n. |格斗者；摔跤选手；抓住…的人（或物）|A person who grapples, especially a wrestler.|The grappler quickly subdued his opponent.  这位格斗者迅速制服了他的对手。|Synonyms: wrestler, fighter, combatant|Antonyms: pacifist|noun

grapple:/ˈɡræpəl/| n. vi. vt. |抓住；格斗；搏斗；努力克服（困难）|1.(n.) An act of grappling; a close fight or struggle. 2.(v.) Engage in a close fight or struggle without weapons; wrestle.|1.(n.) He released his grapple on the rope. 他松开了抓住绳子的手。 2.(v.)  The two men grappled with each other.  两个人扭打在一起。|Synonyms: wrestle, contend, struggle|Antonyms: surrender, yield|noun, verb
