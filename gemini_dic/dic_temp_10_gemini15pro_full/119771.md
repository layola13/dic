mightiest:/ˈmaɪtiɪst/| adj.|最强大的，最有力的|Superlative form of mighty: possessing great and impressive power or strength.|例句：The mightiest warrior challenged the king.  最强壮的战士向国王挑战。

例句：They faced the mightiest army in the world. 他们面对的是世界上最强大的军队。|近义词：strongest, most powerful, supreme, dominant|反义词：weakest, feeblest, least powerful|词性:形容词

詞源: From Middle English *myghtiest, a superlative of myghty, from Old English mihtig (“mighty, powerful, strong, great, important, possible”), from Proto-Germanic *mahtīgaz (“mighty”), from *maht- (“might, power”). Cognate with West Frisian machtich (“mighty, powerful”), Dutch machtig (“powerful, mighty, potent”), German mächtig (“mighty, powerful, potent”), Danish mægtig (“mighty, powerful”).
