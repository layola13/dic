doryphora:/ˌdɔːrɪˈfɔːrə/ | n. | 马铃薯甲虫，科罗拉多马铃薯甲虫 | A beetle (Leptinotarsa decemlineata) that is a serious pest of potatoes. | 例句：The doryphora is a destructive pest in potato fields.  马铃薯甲虫是马铃薯田里的有害害虫。

例句：Farmers use pesticides to control the doryphora population. 农民使用杀虫剂来控制马铃薯甲虫的数量。 | 近义词：Colorado potato beetle, potato bug | 反义词:  (无直接反义词，可以考虑其天敌，例如瓢虫ladybug) | 词性: noun

词源:  From Greek *doryphoros* "spear-bearer," from *dory* "spear" + *phoros* "bearing."  The beetle's striped back resembles a soldier's uniform.
