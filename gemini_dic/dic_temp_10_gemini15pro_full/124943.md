muffin-top:/ˈmʌfɪnˌtɒp/ /ˈmʌfɪnˌtɑːp/| n.|腰间赘肉，腰部脂肪圈|A roll of fat that spills over the top of tight-fitting low-rise jeans or pants, similar to the top of a muffin.  |例句：Her muffin top was spilling over her jeans. 她的腰间赘肉从牛仔裤上方溢了出来。

例句：I need to hit the gym; I've developed a bit of a muffin top. 我得去健身房了；我的腰上长了一圈赘肉。|近义词：spare tire, love handles, belly fat|反义词: flat stomach, toned abs|词性:noun
