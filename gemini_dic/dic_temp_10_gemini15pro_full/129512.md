non-assignment:/ˌnɒn əˈsaɪnmənt/ | n. |不转让，不分配|The contract included a clause of non-assignment of copyright. 合同中包含一项关于版权不转让的条款。|例句：The non-assignment clause prevented the lessee from transferring the lease to another party. 不转让条款阻止了承租人将租约转让给另一方。

例句：There is a non-assignment clause in the agreement, so you cannot transfer your rights to someone else.  协议中有不转让条款，所以你不能把你的权利转让给其他人。


近义词：non-transfer, non-cession |反义词: assignment, transfer |词性:名词
