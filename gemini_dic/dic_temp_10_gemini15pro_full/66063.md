exauthorate: /ɛksˈɔːθəreɪt/
verb
剥夺权力；罢免
To deprive of authority or office; to depose.
The king was exauthorated by the revolutionary forces. 国王被革命军罢免了。
The corrupt official was exauthorated and imprisoned.  腐败的官员被罢免并监禁。
近义词: depose, oust, remove, dismiss
反义词: appoint, install, inaugurate
