far-sighted:/ˈfɑːrˈsaɪtɪd/ /ˈfɑːrˈsaɪtəd/| adj.|有远见的；目光长远的|able to see things clearly that are far away, but not things that are close; able to think clearly about what will happen in the future|例句：She made a far-sighted decision to invest in renewable energy. 她做了一个有远见的决定，投资可再生能源。

例句：He's a far-sighted leader who always plans for the future. 他是一位有远见的领导者，总是为未来做计划。|近义词：prescient, visionary, forward-thinking|反义词：short-sighted, myopic|词性：形容词
