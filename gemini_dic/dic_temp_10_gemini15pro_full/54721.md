disoxygenation:/dɪˌsɒksɪdʒəˈneɪʃən/ | n. |脱氧，去氧|The process of removing oxygen from a substance, such as water or blood. |The disoxygenation of the lake led to a massive fish kill.  湖泊的脱氧导致了大量鱼类死亡。|近义词：deoxygenation, hypoxia (referring to low oxygen levels in tissues) |反义词: oxygenation |词性: noun
