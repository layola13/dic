hyperconformist:/ˌhaɪpərˈkənˈfɔːrmɪst/ | n. | 超级墨守成规者；极端遵奉者 | Someone who conforms to an excessive degree, often due to social pressure or fear of deviating from the norm.  A person who adheres rigidly and uncritically to the prevailing beliefs, attitudes, and practices of a group or society. |  Example: He was such a hyperconformist that he wouldn't even wear a different colored shirt than everyone else. 他是一个超级墨守成规的人，甚至连一件与别人不同颜色的衬衫都不愿意穿。

Example: The company's culture bred hyperconformists, stifling creativity and independent thought.  这家公司的文化孕育了超级墨守成规者，扼杀了创造力和独立思考。 |  Near synonyms: conformist, sheep, follower | Antonyms: nonconformist, individualist, rebel, maverick | Noun
