graf:/ɡræf/ /ɡrɑːf/| n.|嫁接枝; 接穗; 插条; (组织或皮肤的)移植片|A graf is a small piece of healthy skin or bone that is taken from one part of a person's body and put onto or into another part that has been damaged.|The surgeon performed a skin graf.|近义词：graft, scion, slip|反义词:|noun
