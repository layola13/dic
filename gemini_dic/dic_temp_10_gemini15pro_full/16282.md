baptistical:/bæpˈtɪstɪkl/| adj.|浸礼的，施洗礼的|Of or relating to baptism; characterized by or adhering to the practices of the Baptist church.|例句：They held a baptistical service in the river. 他们在河里举行了洗礼仪式。|例句：His beliefs are quite baptistical. 他的信仰完全是浸信会的。|近义词：baptism-related, Baptist|反义词:None|词性:adjective

词源: From Late Latin *baptisticalis*, from Latin baptista (“baptist”) + -ical (“-ical”).
