inclinatory:/ɪnˈklaɪnətɔːri/ /ɪnˈklaɪnətəri/| adj.|倾向的，有…倾向的|Having a tendency or inclination towards something; disposed. |She had an inclinatory feeling towards helping the homeless. 她有帮助无家可归者的倾向。|He was inclinatory to agree with her proposal. 他倾向于同意她的提议。|近义词：disposed, prone, inclined, tending|反义词：averse, disinclined, opposed|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *inclinatorius*, from Latin inclinatus (past participle of inclinare “to bend, incline”), from in- “toward” + clinare “to lean.”
