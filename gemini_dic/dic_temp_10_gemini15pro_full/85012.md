harangue:/həˈræŋ/ /həˈræŋɡ/ | n. vt.|长篇大论，高谈阔论；训斥|A long, pompous speech; a tirade; to deliver a harangue.|例句：The coach delivered a lengthy harangue to his team after their poor performance. 教练在球队表现糟糕后对他们进行了长篇大论的训斥。

例句：His harangue about the evils of modern society went on for hours. 他关于现代社会弊病的高谈阔论持续了几个小时。|近义词：tirade, rant, diatribe, lecture, speech|反义词:discussion, chat, dialogue|词性:noun, verb
