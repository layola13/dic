mistrustingly:/mɪsˈtrʌstɪŋli/
adverb
不信任地，怀疑地
In a manner that shows a lack of trust or confidence.
He looked at her mistrustingly, unsure of her motives.  他怀疑地看着她，不确定她的动机。
She mistrustingly handed him the money. 她不信任地把钱递给他。
近义词:suspiciously, doubtfully, skeptically
反义词:trustingly, confidently, believingly
词性:adverb
