jibba:/ˈdʒɪbə/
n.
胡说，废话，谎言
meaningless talk; nonsense.
He was talking a load of jibba. 他在胡说八道。
近义词：nonsense, rubbish, balderdash, gibberish
反义词：truth, fact
词性：名词
