imagineer:/ɪˈmædʒɪˈnɪr/ | n. |幻想工程师；创意工程师|A person who is an expert at devising and producing creative and imaginative ideas, especially in the fields of entertainment and theme park design.|Walt Disney Imagineering is responsible for creating all of the Disney theme parks.  华特迪士尼幻想工程负责创建所有迪士尼主题公园。|Synonyms: designer, creator, innovator, inventor, visionary|Antonyms:  copyist, imitator|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
Blend of *imagine* and *engineer*.  Coined by the Walt Disney Company. 由*imagine*和*engineer*混合而成。由华特迪士尼公司创造。
