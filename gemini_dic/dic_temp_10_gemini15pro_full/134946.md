nonnarcissism: /nɒnˈnɑːsɪsɪzəm/ | n. | 非自恋 | The lack of narcissism; the state of not being narcissistic.  A modest and empathetic approach to life, characterized by a lack of self-absorption and an interest in others. |  Her nonnarcissism was refreshing in a world obsessed with selfies and self-promotion.  在充斥着自拍和自我推销的世界里，她的非自恋令人耳目一新。 |  humility, modesty, selflessness | narcissism, egotism, self-centeredness | noun
