confraternal: /ˌkɒnfrəˈtɜːrnl/ /ˌkɑːnfrəˈtɜːrnl/ | adj. | 兄弟般的；友爱的；同会的 |  Relating to or characteristic of a confraternity or association of people with shared interests or beliefs. |  The confraternal bonds between the members of the club were strong.  俱乐部成员之间的兄弟情谊很深厚。 |  brotherly, fraternal,  |  unrelated, hostile | adjective

confraternal: /ˌkɒnfrəˈtɜːrnl/ /ˌkɑːnfrəˈtɜːrnl/ | n. | 同会会员 | A member of a confraternity. | He is a confraternal of the Knights of Columbus. 他是哥伦布骑士会的会员。 | brother, comrade |  stranger, outsider | noun


Etymology:
From Late Latin *confraternālis, from Latin con- + fraternālis (“brotherly”), from frāter (“brother”).
