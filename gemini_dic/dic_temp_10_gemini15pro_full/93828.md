imminent:/ˈɪmɪnənt/| adj.|即将发生的，临近的|likely to happen very soon; about to occur|The storm seemed imminent, and everyone rushed indoors.  暴风雨似乎即将来临，大家都冲进屋里。

The country is in imminent danger of invasion.  这个国家面临着迫在眉睫的入侵危险。|近义词：impending, approaching, forthcoming, near, close at hand|反义词：distant, remote, far off|词性:形容词

词源:  From Latin *imminens*, present participle of *imminēre* (“to overhang, lean towards, impend, threaten”), from *in-* (“on, upon”) + *minēre* (“to project, jut out, threaten”).
