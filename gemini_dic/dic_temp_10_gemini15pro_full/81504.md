gravestones: /ˈɡreɪvˌstoʊnz/ (US) /ˈɡreɪvˌstəʊnz/ (UK) | n. | 墓碑 |A stone that is placed at the head of a grave, typically inscribed with the name of the deceased. |  The graveyard was filled with rows of weathered gravestones. 墓地里排列着成排的风化的墓碑。 |  tombstones, headstones, markers | monuments, cenotaphs (empty tombs) | noun
