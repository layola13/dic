imported:/ɪmˈpɔːrtɪd/ | adj. | 进口的，输入的 | Brought in from a foreign country. |  The store sells imported cheeses. 这家商店出售进口奶酪。 |  The car is imported from Germany. 这辆车是从德国进口的。 | 近义词: foreign, introduced, overseas | 反义词: domestic, native, indigenous | 词性: adjective

词源: Late Middle English: past participle of import.
