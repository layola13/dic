bequeathal:/bɪˈkwiːðəl/
n.
遗产；遗赠；遗赠物
The act of bequeathing something (especially personal property) by will.
His father's bequeathal included the family estate and a sizable collection of art. 他父亲的遗产包括家族房产和大量的艺术收藏品。
The bequeathal of his grandfather's watch was a treasured memory.  他祖父的手表是珍贵的遗物。
synonyms: legacy, inheritance, bequest
antonyms: forfeiture, deprivation
noun
