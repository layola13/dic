overtroubling: /ˌoʊvərˈtrʌbəlɪŋ/
adjective
过度令人不安的；过分麻烦的
Causing excessive trouble or worry; excessively disturbing.
The news from the hospital was overtroubling for the family.  医院传来的消息令家人过度不安。
The constant noise from the construction site was overtroubling.  建筑工地持续不断的噪音令人过分烦恼。
近义词: distressing, upsetting, worrisome, disturbing, bothersome
反义词: reassuring, comforting, calming, soothing
词性: adjective
词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "troubling" (adjective from "trouble")
