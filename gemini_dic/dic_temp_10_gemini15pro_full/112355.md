lorises:/ˈlɔːrɪsiz/ (US), /ˈlɒrɪsiz/ (UK)| n. |懒猴，蜂猴|A small, nocturnal primate with large eyes, found in tropical Asia and Africa. They are known for their slow movements.|Lorises are arboreal animals, meaning they live in trees. 懒猴是树栖动物，意味着它们生活在树上。

Lorises are threatened by habitat loss and the illegal pet trade. 懒猴受到栖息地丧失和非法宠物贸易的威胁。|近义词：bushbabies, pottos|反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider diurnal primates like monkeys) |词性:noun
