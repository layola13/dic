brook's:/brʊks/
noun
小溪的；布鲁克斯的
Possessive form of "brook," meaning belonging to or associated with a brook.  It can also be a surname.
The brook's gentle murmur lulled me to sleep. 小溪的潺潺流水声使我昏昏欲睡。
Mr. Brook's car is parked outside. 布鲁克斯先生的车停在外面。
Synonyms: stream's, creek's (for the geographical feature)
Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a possessive.  One could consider antonyms for "brook" itself, such as "mountain" or "desert")
