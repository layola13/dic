graystone:/ˈɡreɪstoʊn/| n.|灰石，灰色石头|A type of building stone that is gray in color.  It's often used in architecture for its durability and aesthetic appeal.|The old mansion was built of graystone.|近义词:bluestone, limestone, granite|反义词:(No direct antonym, could consider "white stone" or a specific brightly colored stone)|noun
