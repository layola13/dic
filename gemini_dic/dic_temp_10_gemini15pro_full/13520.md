attaining:/əˈteɪnɪŋ/
v.
获得，达到
succeeding in achieving something or reaching a particular level or standard
She is attaining a high level of proficiency in English. 她正在达到很高的英语水平。
近义词：achieving, accomplishing, reaching, gaining, obtaining
反义词：failing, missing, losing, forfeiting
词性：动词（present participle of attain）


attain:/əˈteɪn/
v.
获得，达到
to succeed in achieving something or reaching a particular level or standard
He finally attained his goal of becoming a doctor. 他最终实现了成为一名医生的目标。
近义词：achieve, accomplish, reach, gain, obtain
反义词：fail, miss, lose, forfeit
词性：动词

词源：From Old French *ateindre, from Latin attingere (“to touch, reach, arrive at”), from ad- (“to”) + tangere (“to touch”).
