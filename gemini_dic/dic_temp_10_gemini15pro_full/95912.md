increment:/ˈɪŋkrəmənt/ (US), /ˈɪŋkrɪmənt/ (UK)| n. |增加；增量；增值|an amount by which something increases|The salary increases in annual increments of $2,000.  工资每年增加2000美元。|synonyms: increase, rise, gain, addition|antonyms: decrease, reduction, loss|noun

increment:/ˈɪŋkrəmənt/ (US), /ˈɪŋkrɪmənt/ (UK)| v. |增加；增大|to increase something by a particular amount|The program increments the counter by one each time the loop is executed.  该程序每次执行循环时，计数器都会加一。|synonyms: increase, raise, augment, boost|antonyms: decrease, reduce, diminish, lower|verb
