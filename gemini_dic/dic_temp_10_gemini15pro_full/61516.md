embryo:/ˈɛmbriːoʊ/ (US), /ˈɛmbriəʊ/ (UK)| n. |胚胎|An organism in the early stages of development, especially before it has reached a distinctively recognizable form.  A rudimentary stage (something not yet fully developed).|例句：The embryo develops inside the mother's womb. 胚胎在母体的子宫内发育。

例句：The project is still in embryo.  这个项目仍处于萌芽阶段。|近义词：fetus, germ, seed, bud|反义词: adult, mature|noun
