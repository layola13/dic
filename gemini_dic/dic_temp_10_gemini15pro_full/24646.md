brokest:/ˈbroʊkɪst/| adj.|最破产的，最穷的|Superlative form of broke: having no money.|例句：I'm the brokest I've ever been. 我是我这辈子最穷的时候了。|例句：He was the brokest of the bunch. 他是那群人中最穷的。|近义词：poorest, penniless, destitute|反义词：richest, wealthiest|词性:形容词
词源: From the word "broke," which originated as slang in the late 19th century, possibly from the past participle of "break."  The "-est" suffix is a standard English superlative ending.
