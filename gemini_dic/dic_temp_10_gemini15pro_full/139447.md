obsoleted: /ˈɒbsəliːtɪd/ (US) /ˈɒbsəliːtɪd/ (UK)
verb
使过时，淘汰
made something no longer used or needed because something newer and better exists
例句：New technology has obsoleted many traditional skills. 新技术淘汰了许多传统技艺。
例句：The invention of the automobile quickly obsoleted horse-drawn carriages. 汽车的发明很快淘汰了马车。
近义词：outdated, superseded, deprecated, archaic
反义词：current, modern, updated
词性：v.
