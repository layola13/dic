nonincident:/nɒnˈɪnsɪdənt/ | adj. | 非附带的；非伴随发生的；非同时发生的 | Not occurring as a consequence of something else; not concomitant.  | This loss was nonincident to the closure of the factory.  这个损失与工厂关闭无关。 | 近义词: unrelated, unconnected, independent | 反义词:  incident, related, connected | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "incident" (from Latin "incidens," present participle of "incidere" - to fall upon, happen).
