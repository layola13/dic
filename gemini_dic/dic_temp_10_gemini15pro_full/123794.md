mope:/moʊp/ | v. n. | 闷闷不乐，无精打采；闲荡，游荡 | To be sad and without energy or enthusiasm, often staying quiet and not doing anything. | He's been moping around the house ever since he lost his job. 他自从失业后就一直在家闷闷不乐。| 近义词：sulk, brood, languish | 反义词：rejoice, celebrate | 词性：动词, 名词

mope:/moʊp/ | n. | 闷闷不乐的人 | A person who is sad and without energy or enthusiasm. | He was a real mope after his girlfriend left him.  他女朋友离开他以后，他整个人都萎靡不振。 | 近义词：sad sack, killjoy | 反义词：optimist, enthusiast | 词性：名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle Dutch *mōpen "to sulk, be silent," probably of imitative origin.
