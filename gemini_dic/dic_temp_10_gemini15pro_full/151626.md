participialize: /pɑːrˌtɪsɪˈpeɪʃəlaɪz/ | v. | 使…分词化；使…具有分词的性质 | To make or become a participle; to give something the function or form of a participle. |  The writer participialized the verb "run" to create the adjective "running".  这位作家将动词“run”分词化，创造了形容词“running”。 |  He participialized the clause to make it more concise. 他将从句分词化，使之更简洁。 | 近义词:  convert, transform | 反义词:  None | 词性: 动词

Etymology: From participle + -ize.  Participles themselves come from Latin participium, meaning "sharing," as they share characteristics of both verbs and adjectives.
