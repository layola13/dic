amarantus:/ˌæməˈrænθəs/| n.|苋菜，苋属植物|A genus of herbaceous plants, typically having dense plumes of tiny greenish or reddish flowers.  Many species are cultivated as ornamentals, and some are grown for their edible seeds or leaves.|The ancient Greeks used amaranthus in their funeral rites.|近义词：pigweed (especially for weedy species)|反义词: N/A|noun
