cashier:/kæˈʃɪr/ | n. |收银员|A person who handles cash transactions in a store or other business. |例句：The cashier scanned my groceries. 收银员扫描了我的杂货。

例句：He worked as a cashier at the supermarket. 他在超市当收银员。|近义词：teller, clerk|反义词:customer|noun


cash:/kæʃ/ | n. vt. |现金,兑现|Money in the form of bills or coins; to exchange a check or other negotiable instrument for cash. |例句：I need to get some cash from the ATM. 我需要从自动取款机取些现金。

例句：She cashed her paycheck at the bank. 她在银行兑现了她的薪水支票。|近义词：currency, money; redeem|反义词:credit|noun, verb
