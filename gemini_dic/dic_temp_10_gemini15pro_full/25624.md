bumpy:/ˈbʌmpi/| adj.|颠簸的，崎岖不平的|Covered with or full of bumps; uneven.|The road was bumpy and full of potholes. 这条路坑坑洼洼，颠簸不平。|近义词：rough, uneven, irregular|反义词:smooth, flat|形容词

bumpy:/ˈbʌmpi/| n.|颠簸|A bump or unevenness on a surface.|The car ride was full of bumpies.  车程一路颠簸。|近义词：bump, jolt|反义词:smoothness, flatness|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From bump + -y.  Ultimately from Middle Low German *bumpe.
