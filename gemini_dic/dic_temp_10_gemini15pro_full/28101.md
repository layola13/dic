capitalising:/ˈkæpɪtəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 大写化；利用；使资本化 |The author was capitalising the first letter of every sentence. 作者将每个句子的首字母大写。| Synonyms: uppercase, writing in capitals | Antonyms: lowercasing, writing in lowercase | verb

Etymology: From capitalize + -ing. Capitalize comes from capital (meaning "chief, principal") + -ize (a suffix forming verbs).  The word "capital" ultimately derives from the Latin "caput" meaning "head."
