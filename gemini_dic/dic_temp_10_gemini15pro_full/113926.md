maestro:/ˈmaɪstroʊ/ (US), /ˈmaɪstrəʊ/ (UK)| n.|大师，名家；（尤指）著名指挥家|A highly skilled and respected artist, especially a musician, conductor, or composer.|The maestro led the orchestra with passion and precision.  这位大师以热情和精确的指挥带领着乐队。|Synonyms: expert, virtuoso, master, genius, prodigy|Antonyms: amateur, beginner, novice|noun
