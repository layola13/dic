horde:/hɔːrd/ | n. | 群; 大量; 游牧部落 | A large group or crowd, especially one that is moving or difficult to control.  A nomadic tribe. | The tourists descended on the island in hordes.  游客成群结队地涌入岛上。|  The invading horde swept across the plains. 入侵的游牧部落席卷了平原。 | synonyms: crowd, mob, swarm, throng, mass, multitude | antonyms: few, handful, scattering | noun
