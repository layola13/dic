non-tabular:/ˌnɒnˈtæbjʊlə(r)/ | adj. | 非表格的，非表格式的 | Not organized in a table or tabular format; having a structure other than rows and columns. |  This information is presented in a non-tabular format.  这些信息以非表格格式呈现。

The data is stored in a non-tabular database.  数据存储在一个非表格数据库中。 | 近义词: unstructured, non-grid | 反义词: tabular, gridded | 词性: 形容词
