chubbedness:/ˈtʃʌbɪdnəs/| n. |圆胖，丰满|The state of being chubby; plumpness.  A childlike fullness. |例句：The baby's chubbedness was adorable.  这个婴儿的胖乎乎的样子很可爱。

例句：She missed the chubbedness of her cheeks when she lost weight.  当她减肥后，她想念她脸颊上婴儿肥的样子。|近义词：plumpness, rotundity, chubbiness|反义词: thinness, slenderness, leanness|词性:noun

chubby:/ˈtʃʌbɪ/| adj. |圆胖的，丰满的|Slightly fat in a pleasant way, especially of babies and children.|例句：He had chubby cheeks and a cheerful smile. 他有着胖乎乎的脸蛋和开朗的笑容。

例句：The chubby toddler wobbled as he walked.  胖乎乎的小孩走路摇摇晃晃的。|近义词：plump, portly, stout|反义词: thin, skinny, slender|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From chubby + -ness.  Chubby itself likely comes from a variant of "chop," referring to a thick cut of meat, implying fullness.
