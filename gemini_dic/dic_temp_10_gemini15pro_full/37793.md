compellation:/ˌkɒm.pəˈleɪ.ʃən/ /ˌkɑːm.pəˈleɪ.ʃən/
n.
称呼，指名
The act of addressing someone by a particular name or title.
He addressed her by the compellation of "Madam President." 他以“总统夫人”的称呼来称呼她。
address, title, appellation, designation
nickname
