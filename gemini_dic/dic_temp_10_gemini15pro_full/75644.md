fuhrer:/ˈfʊərər/ (UK), /ˈfjʊərər/ (US)| n.|元首，领袖 (尤指纳粹德国的)|A German word meaning "leader" or "guide,"  infamously used as the title of Adolf Hitler, head of the Nazi Party and dictator of Germany from 1934 to 1945.  Its usage is now strongly associated with Nazism and is generally avoided, except in historical contexts.|Hitler was known as the Fuhrer. 希特勒被称为元首。|Synonym: leader, dictator, chief|Antonym: follower, subordinate|noun
