nowadays:/ˈnaʊədeɪz/| adv.|如今，现在|At present, in this day and age.  It is used to talk about the present time, especially when comparing it to the past.|Nowadays, people are more aware of environmental issues. 如今，人们更加关注环境问题。|Synonyms: currently, presently, these days|Antonyms: formerly, previously|adverb

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
People nowadays rely heavily on technology.  现在人们非常依赖科技。
Children nowadays spend more time indoors. 现在的孩子们更多时间待在室内。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Many companies are nowadays investing in renewable energy. 许多公司如今正在投资可再生能源。
Students are nowadays facing increasing pressure to succeed.  现在的学生面临着越来越大的成功压力。


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Technology has nowadays advanced at an incredible pace.  如今科技进步的速度令人难以置信。
The cost of living has nowadays risen dramatically.  如今生活成本急剧上升。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Scientists have nowadays been researching new ways to combat climate change.  如今，科学家们一直在研究应对气候变化的新方法。
Consumers have nowadays been demanding more sustainable products.  如今，消费者一直在要求更可持续的产品。

Even ten years ago, people were nowadays becoming more reliant on mobile phones. (Incorrect usage) 即使在十年前，人们也越来越依赖手机。(错误用法)


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
By the 21st century, technology had nowadays advanced significantly. (Incorrect usage) 到21世纪，技术已经取得了显著进步。(错误用法)


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
Prior to the internet, people had nowadays been communicating through traditional means. (Incorrect usage)  在互联网出现之前，人们一直通过传统方式进行交流。(错误用法)
