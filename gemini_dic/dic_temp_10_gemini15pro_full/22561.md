boot-up:/ˈbuːt ʌp/ (US), /ˈbuːt ʌp/ (UK)| n. |启动，开机|The process of starting a computer or other electronic device.|例句：The boot-up process took several minutes. 开机过程花了数分钟。

例句：A slow boot-up can be frustrating.  启动缓慢可能会令人沮丧。


近义词：start-up, launch, initialization|反义词:shutdown, power-down|词性:noun


boot-up:/ˈbuːt ʌp/ (US), /ˈbuːt ʌp/ (UK)| v. |启动，开机|To start a computer or other electronic device.|例句：I booted up the computer and checked my email. 我启动了电脑并查看了我的电子邮件。

例句：He booted up the system to begin work. 他启动了系统开始工作。


近义词：start, launch, power on|反义词:shut down, power off|词性:verb
