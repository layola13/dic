biopic:/ˈbaɪoʊpɪk/ | n. |传记片|A biographical movie; a film dramatizing the life of a particular person, typically a public figure.|例句：The biopic about the famous scientist was a box office hit.  这部关于著名科学家的传记片票房大卖。

例句：She starred in a biopic of the renowned author. 她主演了一部关于这位著名作家的传记片。
