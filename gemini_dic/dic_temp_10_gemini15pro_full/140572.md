oligistic:/ˌɒlɪˈɡɪstɪk/
adjective
寡头垄断的
Relating to or characterized by oligopoly; a state of limited competition, in which a market is shared by a small number of producers or sellers.
The oligistic nature of the mobile phone market makes it difficult for new companies to enter.  手机市场的寡头垄断性质使得新公司难以进入。
The report criticized the oligistic control of the media by a handful of corporations. 该报告批评了少数几家公司对媒体的寡头垄断控制。
Near Synonyms: monopolistic, concentrated, cartelized
Antonyms: competitive, fragmented, decentralized
词性: adjective
