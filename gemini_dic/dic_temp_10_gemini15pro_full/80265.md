goggliest:/ˈɡɒɡliɪst/
adjective
戴着护目镜的；瞪大眼睛的；眼球突出的
Having or characterized by bulging or wide-open eyes, often suggesting surprise, fear, or excitement.
She gave him the goggliest look when he told her he'd won the lottery. 她听到他中了彩票后，瞪大了眼睛看着他。
近义词:wide-eyed, bug-eyed, pop-eyed
反义词:narrow-eyed, squinty-eyed
词性:adjective
词源:From "goggle" (to stare with wide eyes) + "-est" (superlative suffix).  "Goggle" likely originates from a frequentative form of Middle English "gogelen," of uncertain origin.
