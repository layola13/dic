non-linear:/ˌnɒnˈlɪniər/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈlɪnɪə(r)/ (UK)| adj.|非线性的|Not linear; not denoting, involving, or arranged in a straight line.  Relating to or denoting a system whose output is not directly proportional to its input.|The graph shows a non-linear relationship between the two variables. 这张图表显示了两个变量之间的非线性关系。|nonlinear, curvilinear|linear, straight|adjective

non-linearity:/ˌnɒnˌlɪniˈærəti/ (US) /ˌnɒnˌlɪnɪˈærəti/ (UK)| n.|非线性|The quality or state of being non-linear.|The non-linearity of the system makes it difficult to predict its behavior.  系统的非线性使其难以预测其行为。|nonlinearness|linearity|noun
