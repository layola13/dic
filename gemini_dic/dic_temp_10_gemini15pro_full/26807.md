cag:/kæɡ/ | n. | CAG重复序列; 细胞毒性相关基因 | A sequence of three DNA or RNA nucleotides (cytosine-adenine-guanine) that is repeated multiple times in a row. Expansion of this trinucleotide repeat is associated with several genetic disorders, including Huntington's disease. |  The CAG repeat expansion in the huntingtin gene is the cause of Huntington's disease.  亨廷顿基因中的CAG重复扩增是亨廷顿病的病因。| synonym: trinucleotide repeat | antonym: N/A | noun
