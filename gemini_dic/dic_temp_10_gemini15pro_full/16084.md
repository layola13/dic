bandolier:/ˌbændəˈlɪr/ |n.| 子弹带，弹药带 |A belt worn over the shoulder and across the chest, with loops or pockets for holding cartridges. | The soldier wore a bandolier across his chest. 士兵胸前挎着一条子弹带。|Synonyms: cartridge belt, ammunition belt| Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "holster" for a single pistol) | Noun

bandolier:/ˌbændəˈlɪr/ |n.| (旧) 绶带 |A shoulder sash worn as part of a ceremonial dress or uniform. |  The mayor wore a bandolier for the city's anniversary celebration. 市长在城市周年庆典上佩戴了绶带。|Synonyms: sash, baldric| Antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | Noun
