drosses:/ˈdrɑːsɪz/
n.
渣滓，浮渣；废物
Waste matter; refuse.
The drosses from the smelting process were dumped into the landfill. 熔炼过程中产生的废渣被倾倒到垃圾填埋场。
synonyms:  scum, refuse, waste, debris, dregs
antonyms:  essence, valuable, treasure
