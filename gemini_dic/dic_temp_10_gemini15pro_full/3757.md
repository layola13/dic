agister:/ˈædʒɪstər/| n.|放牧人，牧场主|An agister is someone who takes in livestock for feeding, usually for a fee. 放牧人是指收养牲畜进行喂养的人，通常收取费用。|The agister charged a reasonable fee for grazing the cattle.  放牧人对放牧牛群收取合理的费用。|The agister carefully monitored the health of the sheep. 放牧人仔细监测羊群的健康状况。|近义词：grazier, herdsman,pastoralist|反义词:|noun
