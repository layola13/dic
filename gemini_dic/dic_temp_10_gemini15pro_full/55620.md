diswitted:/dɪsˈwɪtɪd/
adjective
愚蠢的，糊涂的
Lacking good judgment or sense; foolish.
The diswitted burglar locked himself in the garage.  那个愚蠢的窃贼把自己锁在了车库里。
近义词: foolish, silly, stupid, witless, unwise, unintelligent, doltish, daft, dumb
反义词: intelligent, clever, smart, wise, astute, shrewd
词性: adjective
