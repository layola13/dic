incapsulate:/ɪnˈkæpsjʊleɪt/ /ɪnˈkæpsəleɪt/ | vt. |  封装；概括；浓缩 | To enclose something in a capsule or other protective covering; to summarize or condense something. | The company incapsulates the medicine in small, easy-to-swallow pills.  该公司将药物封装在易于吞咽的小药丸中。

The report incapsulates the main findings of the research. 这份报告概括了研究的主要发现。| synonyms: enclose, encase, encapsule, summarize, condense, epitomize | antonyms: release, unpack, expand, elaborate | verb

incapsulate:/ɪnˈkæpsjʊleɪt/ /ɪnˈkæpsəleɪt/ | adj. | 被封装的 | Enclosed in a capsule. |  The incapsulated vitamins are designed for slow release.  这些被封装的维生素被设计成缓慢释放。| synonyms: enclosed, encased, encapsulated | antonyms: released, unpacked | adjective
