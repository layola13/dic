gyle:/ɡaɪl/| n.|麦芽汁，酿造批次|A batch of wort or beer brewed at one time. A brewing term referring to a specific batch or run of beer made with the same ingredients and process.  It can also refer to the different runnings of wort extracted from the same mash.|The first gyle produced the strongest beer. 第一批麦芽汁酿造出了最浓烈的啤酒。|Synonym: batch, brew, brewing|Antonym: N/A|noun

gyle:/ɡaɪl/| n.|诡计，狡猾|Cunning or sly behaviour; trickery. (This meaning is archaic and rarely used.)|He used all his gyle to win the competition. 他用尽了他的诡计赢得了比赛。|Synonym: cunning, guile, trickery|Antonym: honesty, forthrightness|noun
