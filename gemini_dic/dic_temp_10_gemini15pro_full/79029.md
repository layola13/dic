give-a-hand:/ɡɪv ə hænd/| phrasal verb | 帮助；帮忙；支援 | To help someone with a task, especially a physical one. | Can you give me a hand with these boxes? 你能帮我搬一下这些箱子吗？

| Could you give me a hand moving this furniture? 你能帮我搬一下这家具吗？ | synonyms: assist, help, aid | antonyms: hinder, obstruct, impede | verb phrase
