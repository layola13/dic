natachi: /nɑːˈtɑːtʃi/ | pron. | 我们（非正式，主要用于女性或女孩）|  A Japanese pronoun meaning "we" used primarily by female speakers.  It carries a casual and informal tone. | 例句：Natatchi wa eiga ni ikimasu.  我们（女生们）要去看电影。| 近义词：watashitachi (more formal), bokutachi (used by males) | 反义词: anata/anatatachi (you)| 词性: pronoun
