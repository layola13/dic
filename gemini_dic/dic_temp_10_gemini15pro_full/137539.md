nontranscribing:/ˌnɒntrɑːnˈskraɪbɪŋ/ /ˌnɑːntrænˈskraɪbɪŋ/| adj. |非转录的|Not transcribing; specifically (of a region of DNA) not transcribed into RNA.|Certain sections of DNA are nontranscribing and play a regulatory role.|近义词：noncoding, silent|反义词:transcribing, coding|词性:形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "transcribing" (present participle of "transcribe," from Latin "transcribere" - "to copy across," from "trans" - "across" + "scribere" - "to write").
