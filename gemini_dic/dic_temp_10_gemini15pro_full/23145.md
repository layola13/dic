boustrophedon:/ˌbuːstrəˈfiːdən/ | adj. n.|  往复书写的，牛耕式书写的 |  Written in alternating lines from right to left and left to right, as in the manner of an ox plowing a field. | The ancient inscription was written in boustrophedon. 这段古代铭文是以牛耕式书写而成。| synonyms: alternating, bidirectional | antonyms: unidirectional | adjective/noun

boustrophedonic:/ˌbuːstrəˈfiːdɒnɪk/ | adj.| 往复书写的，牛耕式书写的 |  Relating to or written in boustrophedon. | The boustrophedonic script was difficult to decipher.  这种往复书写的文字难以破译。| synonyms: alternating, bidirectional | antonyms: unidirectional | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *boustrophēdón* "ox-turning," from *bous* "ox" + *strophḗ* "a turning" + *-dōn* adverbial suffix.
