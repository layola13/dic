equipaged:/ɪˈkwɪpɪdʒd/
adjective
配备齐全的；装备好的
equipped or furnished for a particular purpose.
The explorers were well equipaged for their journey into the jungle. 探险家们为进入丛林的旅程配备了齐全的装备。
近义词:equipped, outfitted, furnished, prepared
反义词:unequipped, unprepared, ill-equipped
词性:adjective
词源:From French équiper, from Old French esquiper "to fit out a ship," from Dutch schippen "to ship, embark," literally "to put into a ship."  The suffix -ed creates the past participle form, used adjectivally.
