biplicity:/baɪˈplɪsɪti/
noun
双重性，二重性
The state or quality of being twofold or dual; duality.  The simultaneous occurrence of two different states or qualities.
His personality exhibited a strange biplicity, shifting between jovial extroversion and brooding introspection.  他的人格展现出一种奇特的双重性，在开朗外向和沉思内向之间转换。
