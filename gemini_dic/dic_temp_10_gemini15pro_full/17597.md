beautifulest:/ˈbjuːtɪfələst/ | adj. |  最美丽的 | This word is a non-standard and grammatically incorrect form of "most beautiful".  "Beautiful" is already a superlative in meaning, so adding "-est" is redundant and considered incorrect. |  Incorrect: She is the beautifulest girl I've ever seen. (Avoid this usage) |  Correct: She is the most beautiful girl I've ever seen.| most beautiful, gorgeous, stunning, prettiest | ugly, plain, unattractive | adjective
