mythologists:/mɪˈθɒlədʒɪsts/ | n. |神话学家；神话研究者|A person who studies myths or mythology.  Experts in mythology, mythologists research and interpret the stories, symbols, and beliefs of different cultures.|例句：Mythologists have offered various interpretations of this ancient myth.  神话学家对这个古老的神话提出了各种各样的解释。

例句：The mythologists studied the symbolism in the hero's journey.  神话学家研究了英雄旅程中的象征意义。|近义词：folklorists, mythographers|反义词: N/A |词性: noun
