dunned:/dʌnd/
dunned| vt.|催促；索要|To make repeated and insistent demands upon, especially for the payment of a debt.|The landlord dunned me for the rent every week. 房东每周都催促我付房租。|Synonyms: pester, harass, badger, hound, press, importune|Antonyms: ignore, neglect|verb

dunned:/dʌnd/
dunned| adj.(rare) |颜色暗淡的; 褐色的|Of a dull grayish-brown color.|He wore a dunned coat. 他穿着一件暗褐色的大衣。|Synonyms: dusky, drab, muted|Antonyms: bright, vibrant|adjective
