multilateralness:/ˌmʌltɪˈlætərəlnəs/ | n. | 多边主义，多边性 | The quality or state of being multilateral; involving many different countries or parties. |  The agreement emphasized the importance of multilateralness in addressing global challenges. 该协议强调了多边主义在应对全球挑战中的重要性。| synonyms:  multilateralism, plurilateralism | antonyms: unilateralism, bilateralism | noun

multilateral:/ˌmʌltɪˈlætərəl/ | adj. | 多边的，多国参与的 | Involving more than two nations or parties. | The multilateral trade agreement boosted economic growth in the region.  多边贸易协定促进了该地区的经济增长。| synonyms:  multinational, polycentric | antonyms: unilateral, bilateral | adjective
