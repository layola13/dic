effet:/ɛˈfɛ/ | n. | 效果，影响，结果 | A change which is a result or consequence of an action or other cause. | The effet of the medicine was immediate.  这药的效果立竿见影。| synonyms: result, consequence, outcome, impact, influence | antonyms: cause, origin, source | noun

Etymology: From Old French <i>effet</i>, from Latin <i>effectus</i> ("accomplishment, execution"), past participle of <i>efficere</i> ("to work out, accomplish"), from <i>ex-</i> ("out") + <i>facere</i> ("to make, to do").
