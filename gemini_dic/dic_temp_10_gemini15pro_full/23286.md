boxhaul:/ˈbɑːkshɔːl/| n.|盒子运输，箱式运输；（尤指卡车运输的）货物；（尤指空载）往返行程|The movement of cargo in boxes, especially by truck. Also refers to the round trip a truck makes, especially when empty.|例句：The boxhaul from the warehouse to the store was quick and efficient. 从仓库到商店的箱式运输既快捷又高效。

例句：The trucker made a long boxhaul across the country. 卡车司机进行了漫长的跨国空载往返行程。|近义词：backhaul, cargo, freight, shipment, transport, haulage|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
