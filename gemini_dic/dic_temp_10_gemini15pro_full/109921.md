libeling: /ˈlaɪbəlɪŋ/ | v. | 诽谤，中伤 | The act of making a false and malicious statement that damages someone's reputation. |  He is libeling me by saying I stole the money. 他诽谤我，说我偷了钱。|  slandering, defaming, maligning, vilifying |  praising, commending | verb

libel: /ˈlaɪbəl/ | n. | 诽谤，中伤 | A published false statement that is damaging to a person's reputation; a written defamation. | The newspaper article was a libel against the politician. 这篇报纸文章是对这位政治家的诽谤。| slander, defamation | praise, commendation | noun
