lelang:/ləˈlɑːŋ/ /ˈleɪlɑːŋ/ | n. |拍卖; 荷兰语拍卖| A public sale of goods or property, where people make bids and the item is sold to the highest bidder.  Specifically, a Dutch auction, where the price starts high and is gradually lowered until a buyer accepts. |The painting was sold at a lelang for $1 million. 这幅画在拍卖会上以100万美元的价格售出。| synonym: auction | antonym: purchase (as a direct buyer) | noun


lelang:/ləˈlɑːŋ/ /ˈleɪlɑːŋ/ | v. |拍卖; 进行荷兰式拍卖| To sell something at a public sale, where people make bids and the item is sold to the highest bidder. Specifically, to sell using a Dutch auction method. |They decided to lelang their old car to raise money for charity. 他们决定拍卖他们的旧车为慈善事业筹款。| synonym: auction off | antonym: buy | verb
