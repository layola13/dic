governably:/ˈɡʌvərnəbli/| adv. | 可统治地，可管理地 | In a way that can be governed or controlled; manageably. | The country is now governably democratic. 这个国家现在是可统治的民主国家。|Synonyms: manageably, controllably, tractably | Antonyms: ungovernably, unmanageably, uncontrollably |词性: 副词

Etymology:
From "governable" + "-ly".  "Governable" comes from "govern" + "-able". "Govern" comes from Old French "governer," from Latin "gubernare" "to steer, to pilot, direct, rule," from "gubernator" "pilot, governor," from Greek "kybernan" "to steer, to guide."
