fats:/fæts/
n.
脂肪，肥肉
A natural oily or greasy substance occurring in animal bodies, especially when deposited as a layer under the skin or around certain organs.
Eating too many fats can lead to health problems. 吃太多脂肪会导致健康问题。

lipids, oils
carbohydrates, proteins
noun

Etymology:
From Old English *fǣtt (attested only in the plural fǣttu), from Proto-Germanic *faito- (source also of Old Frisian fett "fat, grease," Old Saxon fat "fat, vessel," Dutch vet "fat, grease," Old High German feizit "fat," German Fett "fat, grease," Old Norse feitr "fat"), from Proto-Indo-European *peie- "to be fat, swell."
