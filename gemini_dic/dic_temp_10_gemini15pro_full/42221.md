counselling: /ˈkaʊnsəlɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈkaʊnsəlɪŋ/ (US)| n.|咨询，辅导|The process of giving advice and guidance to someone, especially on personal problems.|She received counselling after the death of her husband. 她丈夫去世后，她接受了心理咨询。|近义词: guidance, advice, therapy, support|反义词: condemnation, criticism|noun


counsel: /ˈkaʊnsəl/ (UK) /ˈkaʊnsəl/ (US)| n. v.|建议，忠告；律师|Advice and guidance given to someone, especially on social or personal problems; a lawyer or group of lawyers representing someone in court.|He gave me some good counsel. 他给了我一些好建议。|近义词: advice, guidance, recommendation, lawyer, attorney|反义词: dissuasion, discouragement|noun, verb
