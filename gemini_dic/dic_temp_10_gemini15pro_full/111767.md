lodgers:/ˈlɒdʒəz/ (UK), /ˈlɑːdʒərz/ (US)| n. |房客，寄宿者|A person who rents a room in someone else's house.|例句：The landlady had three lodgers staying in her house.  房东太太家里住着三个房客。

例句：The lodgers shared a bathroom and kitchen. 寄宿者共用浴室和厨房。

近义词：tenants, roomers, boarders|反义词:landlord, landlady|词性:名词
