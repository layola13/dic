claustrophobia:/ˌklɔːstrəˈfoʊbiə/ (US) /ˌklɔːstrəˈfəʊbiə/ (UK)| n. |幽闭恐惧症|An anxiety disorder characterized by an extreme or irrational fear of confined places.|She suffers from claustrophobia and avoids elevators.|近义词：fear of enclosed spaces, confinement anxiety|反义词: agoraphobia (广场恐惧症)|词性: noun

Etymology: From Latin *claustrum* "enclosed place, bolt, bar" + -phobia.
