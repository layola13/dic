horseflies:/ˈhɔːrsflaɪz/ (US), /ˈhɔːsflaɪz/ (UK)| n. |马蝇|A large fly that bites horses, cattle, and sometimes people. Its bite is painful.|The horseflies were swarming around the horses in the field.  马蝇在田野里成群地围着马转。|
The farmer used a special spray to keep the horseflies away from his cows.  农场主用一种特殊的喷雾剂来防止马蝇靠近他的奶牛。|近义词：gadflies, cleggs (UK)|反义词: N/A |noun
