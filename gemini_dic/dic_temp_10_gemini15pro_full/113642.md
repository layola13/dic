machine-gunned:/məˈʃiːn ɡʌnd/ |v. (past tense)|用机关枪扫射|To shoot with a machine gun, often repeatedly and indiscriminately.|The retreating soldiers were machine-gunned by the enemy. 撤退的士兵被敌人用机关枪扫射。|近义词:strafed, riddled (with bullets)|反义词:(No direct antonym, consider "protected," "defended")|词性:动词

machine-gunned:/məˈʃiːn ɡʌnd/ |adj.|被机关枪扫射的|Having been shot with a machine gun.|The machine-gunned car was riddled with bullet holes.  被机关枪扫射的汽车布满了弹孔。|近义词:bullet-ridden, strafed|反义词:(No direct antonym, consider "intact," "undamaged")|词性:形容词


词源:  "machine gun" (n.) + "-ed" (suffix forming past participle and adjectives)

"machine gun" itself comes from "machine" (referring to the mechanical nature of the weapon) + "gun."
