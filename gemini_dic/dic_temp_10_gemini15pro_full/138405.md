noty:/ˈnoʊti/| n.| 便条，短笺；(尤指正式的) 通知，通告 |A brief written record or communication, often a formal announcement.| Example: He left a noty on the door saying he would be back later. 他在门上留了一张便条，说他晚点回来。| Synonyms: memo, note, message, announcement, notification | Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but consider) silence, suppression, withholding | Noun

Etymology:  From Middle English *note*, from Old French *note* "mark, sign, note; musical note; letter, missive," from Latin *nota* "mark, character, letter; brand," from past participle stem of *noscere* "come to know."
