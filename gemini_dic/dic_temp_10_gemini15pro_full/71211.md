fitche:/fɪtʃ/
n.
狹長三角旗;  尖頭小盾章;
A pointed or forked device, especially on a coat of arms.
The knight's shield bore a gold fitche on a field of blue. 骑士的盾牌上蓝底绘有一枚金色尖头小盾章。

近义词:pale, chevron, ordinaries (纹章学用语)
反义词:N/A (由于是纹章学专业词汇，难以找到确切的反义词)
词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *fiche, from Vulgar Latin *ficta, feminine past participle of *figere, from Latin figere (“to fix, fasten”).  Related to fix.
