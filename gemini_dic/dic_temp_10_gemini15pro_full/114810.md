mamaluke:/ˈmæməˌluːk/| n. |马木留克，奴隶兵|A member of a former military caste originally composed of slave soldiers from various ethnic backgrounds, notably Turkic, Circassian, and Georgian, who rose to prominence in Egypt and other parts of the Islamic world during the Middle Ages.|The Mamalukes ruled Egypt for centuries. 马木留克统治了埃及几个世纪。|近义词：slave soldier, warrior|反义词:citizen, civilian|词性:noun

mamaluke:/ˈmæməˌluːk/| adj. |马木留克的，奴隶兵的|Relating to or characteristic of the Mamluks.|The Mamaluke era was a significant period in Egyptian history.  马木留克时代是埃及历史上的重要时期。|近义词：slave soldier, warrior|反义词:citizen, civilian|词性:adjective
