hyponatraemia:/ˌhaɪpəʊnəˈtriːmiə/ (US) /ˌhaɪpoʊnəˈtriːmiə/| n. |低钠血症|A condition in which the concentration of sodium in the blood is abnormally low.  It is primarily a problem of water balance, rather than a problem of sodium balance.|The marathon runner suffered from hyponatraemia due to excessive water intake. 马拉松运动员由于摄入过多的水而患上了低钠血症。|近义词:hyponatremia (American spelling), low blood sodium|反义词:hypernatraemia (high blood sodium)|词性:noun

Etymology:
hypo- (Greek): under, below
natr- (Latin, natrium): sodium
-emia (Greek): blood condition
