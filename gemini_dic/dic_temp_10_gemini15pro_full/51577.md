deutoplastic:/ˌdjuːtəˈplæstɪk/
adjective
继发性的，后生的
Relating to or influenced by environmental factors rather than heredity.  Formed after birth.  Pertaining to acquired characteristics or changes in an organism brought about by the environment or other external factors, rather than by heredity.
The researchers studied the deutoplastic changes in the finches' beaks due to their changing food sources. 研究人员研究了由于食物来源的变化而导致的雀类喙的继发性变化。
synonym: acquired, adaptive, environmental
antonym:  congenital, hereditary, innate
词性: adjective
