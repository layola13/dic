cadenza:/kəˈdɛntsə/ /kəˈdɛnzə/
n.
华彩乐段；炫技段落
A brilliant solo passage, often improvised, inserted near the end of a movement in a concerto or other musical work, typically allowing the performer to display virtuosity.
The pianist dazzled the audience with a breathtaking cadenza.  钢琴家用一段令人叹为观止的华彩乐段征服了观众。
solo, flourish, embellishment
-
noun
cadenzas (plural)
