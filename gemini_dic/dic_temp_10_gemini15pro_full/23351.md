what:/wɒt/(UK) /wɑːt/(US) |pron. det. adv.| 什么,多么 | Used to ask for information about people or things. | What do you want for dinner? 你晚饭想吃什么？ | 近义词: which | 反义词:  (What doesn't have a direct antonym) | pronoun, determiner, adverb

USAGE->
Simple Present:
What is your favorite color? = 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What does he do for a living? = 他做什么工作？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is she cooking for dinner tonight? = 她今晚在做什么晚餐？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned from this experience? = 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? = 她用这笔钱做了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on lately? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading this week? = 他这周一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do last night? = 你昨晚做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the wedding? = 她去婚礼穿了什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you went to bed? = 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had they decided before the meeting? = 会议前他们决定了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 在她换专业之前她一直在学什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你会完成什么？
What will they have built by next summer? = 到明年夏天他们会建成什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for the past ten years by 2033? = 到2033年你将已经做了十年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么多久了？

Subjunctive Mood:
If I knew what to say, I would tell you. = 如果我知道该说什么，我会告诉你。
I wish I knew what she was thinking. = 我希望我知道她在想什么。

Passive Voice:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？

Interrogative Sentence:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？

Negative Sentence:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该做什么。
She doesn't understand what he's saying. = 她不明白他在说什么。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What the hell! = 搞什么鬼！
What if we go to the beach? = 如果我们去海滩怎么样？
What for? = 为了什么？
No matter what happens, I'll be there for you. = 无论发生什么，我都会在你身边。
What's more, it's free! = 而且，它是免费的！
What about going to the movies? = 去看电影怎么样？
So what? = 那又怎样？
