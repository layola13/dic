iteration:/ˌɪtəˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 迭代，重复，反复 | the repetition of a process or utterance. |  The software is on its third iteration. 该软件是第三版。 | Example: After several iterations, the design was finalized. 经过多次迭代，设计最终定稿。 | Synonyms: repetition, recurrence, reiteration | Antonyms: singularity, uniqueness | noun

iteration:/ˌɪtəˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 版本，（同一事物的）不同形式 | a new version of a piece of computer hardware or software. |  This is the latest iteration of the iPhone. 这是最新版本的iPhone。 | Example: Each iteration of the game has improved graphics.  游戏的每个版本都改进了图形。 | Synonyms: version, edition, release | Antonyms: original, prototype | noun
