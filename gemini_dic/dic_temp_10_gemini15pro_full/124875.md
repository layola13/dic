mudbaths:/ˈmʌdbɑːðz/
n.
泥浴
A bath in mud, often believed to have therapeutic properties.
She took a mudbath at the spa. 她在这个水疗中心洗了泥浴。

近义词:mudpacks, mud treatments
反义词:(No direct antonym, perhaps "shower," "bath" in regular water)
词性:Noun (Plural)
