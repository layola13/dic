cylindrical:/səˈlɪndrɪkəl/
adjective
圆柱形的；圆筒状的
Having the shape of a cylinder; relating to or resembling a cylinder.
The cylindrical container held a variety of tools.  这个圆柱形的容器里装着各种各样的工具。
The cake was baked in a cylindrical tin.  蛋糕是在一个圆柱形的罐子里烤的。
近义词:columnar, tubular
反义词:spherical, cubical
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Late Latin *cylindricus, from Latin cylindrus (“cylinder”), from Ancient Greek κύλινδρος (kúlindros, “roller, cylinder”).
