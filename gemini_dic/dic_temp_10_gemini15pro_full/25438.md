bull-like:/ˈbʊlˌlaɪk/
adjective
像公牛的；似牛的；粗暴的
Resembling a bull in appearance, behavior, or temperament; strong and aggressive.
He had a bull-like neck and shoulders. 他有着像公牛一样的脖子和肩膀。
The linebacker charged with bull-like ferocity.  这位线卫以公牛般的凶猛冲锋。
近义词:strong, powerful, aggressive, ferocious, bullish
反义词:weak, timid, gentle, docile
词性:adjective

bull-like: /ˈbʊlˌlaɪk/
词源:  Combination of "bull" (from Old English "bula") and "-like" (from Old English "-lic").
词性: adjective
