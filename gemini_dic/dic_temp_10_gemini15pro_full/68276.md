faking:/ˈfeɪkɪŋ/| v.|假装，伪造|Pretending to have a particular feeling or to be a particular kind of person; making something so that it seems to be something else.|He was faking a headache to avoid going to school. 他假装头痛以逃避上学。|近义词：pretending, feigning, simulating, counterfeiting|反义词：genuine, authentic, real|词性: 动名词 (gerund) or present participle


词源:  From the verb "fake," which originated in the 19th century, possibly from German "fegen" (to sweep) or "faken" (to cheat at cards).  Another possibility is from the Romany language.
