constituently:/kənˈstɪtʃuəntli/| adv.| 组成地；作为选民地| in a way that relates to the parts that make up something; as a member of a political constituency |The university is constituently represented on the board. 这所大学在董事会中有代表。|近义词：componently, elementally|反义词:independently, separately|词性:副词

constituent:/kənˈstɪtʃuənt/| n. adj.| 成分；选民；委托人；要素；组成的| a person who is represented in a legislature by an elected official; a component part of something; serving to form something |The MP worked hard for his constituents.  这位议员为他的选民们辛勤工作。|近义词：component, element, voter, elector|反义词: whole, entirety|词性:名词，形容词
