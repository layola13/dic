hobblers:/ˈhɒblərz/ (UK) /ˈhɑːblərz/ (US)| n.|蹒跚而行者；跛行者；从事粗活的人|People who hobble; those who walk with a limp or who do rough work. |The hobblers struggled through the deep snow. 跛行者在深雪中艰难前行。|The hobblers were tasked with clearing the field of rocks. 粗工们被派去清理田里的石头。|近义词:cripples, limpers, laborers|反义词:sprinters, runners|noun
