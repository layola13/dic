documented:/ˈdɒkjʊmentɪd/ /ˈdɑːkjəmentɪd/| adj.|记录在案的，有文件证明的|Supported with documents or other proof.|例句：The incident was well documented by the police.  这起事件被警方详细记录在案。|近义词：recorded, proven, substantiated, verified|反义词：undocumented, unproven|词性:形容词

documented:/ˈdɒkjʊmentɪd/ /ˈdɑːkjəmentɪd/| v.|记录，证明（document的过去分词）|(past participle of document) To record something in written, photographic, or other form.|例句：The historian documented the events of the war.  历史学家记录了战争的事件。|近义词：recorded, chronicled, logged|反义词：omitted, ignored|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *documentātus, past participle of documentāre (“to furnish with documents, to prove”), from documentum (“document, proof, lesson”).
