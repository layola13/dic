fatiguing:/fəˈtiːɡɪŋ/| adj.|令人疲倦的，使人疲劳的|Causing weariness or exhaustion.|The hike up the mountain was fatiguing.  爬山很累。|近义词：tiring, exhausting, draining|反义词：refreshing, invigorating, energizing|词性:形容词

词源: From French fatiguer (to tire), from Late Latin fatigare (to tire out), from Latin fatisci (to crack, break, weaken)
