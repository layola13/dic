edentulous:/iːˈdɛntʃələs/
adjective
无齿的；缺齿的
lacking teeth; toothless
The edentulous old man gummed his bread.  这位没牙的老人用牙龈咀嚼面包。
Synonyms: toothless, gummy
Antonyms: dentate, toothed
词性: 形容词
