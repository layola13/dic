analyze: /ˈænəlaɪz/ (US), /ˈænəlaɪz/ (UK) | v. | 分析，解析 | To examine something methodically and in detail, typically in order to explain and interpret it. |  We need to analyze the data before drawing any conclusions. 我们需要在得出任何结论之前分析数据。

The lab will analyze the blood sample. 实验室将分析血液样本。


近义词: examine, investigate, study, scrutinize, assess, evaluate
反义词: synthesize, combine
词性: verb
