autarchist:/ˈɔːtɑːrkɪst/
n.|独裁者；主张自给自足的人|A person who believes in or practices autarchy; an advocate of national economic self-sufficiency.|The autarchist refused to trade with any other nation, believing his country could produce everything it needed.|近义词：dictator, autocrat, despot|反义词: democrat|词性: noun

autarchy:/ˈɔːtɑːrki/
n.|独裁；专制；自给自足|A system of government in which supreme power is concentrated in the hands of one person; national economic self-sufficiency.|The small island nation practiced autarchy, relying entirely on its own resources.|近义词：dictatorship, despotism, self-sufficiency|反义词: democracy, interdependence|词性: noun

autarchic:/ɔːˈtɑːrkɪk/
adj.|独裁的；专制的；自给自足的|Of or relating to autarchy; practicing or advocating autarchy.|The autarchic regime controlled every aspect of its citizens' lives.|近义词：dictatorial, despotic, self-sufficient|反义词: democratic, interdependent|词性: adjective
