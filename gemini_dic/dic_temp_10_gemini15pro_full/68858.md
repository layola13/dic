fashions:/ˈfæʃənz/ /ˈfæʃnz/| n.|时尚，潮流；时装；方式，方法|The way something is done or happens.  A currently popular style or trend, especially in clothing, footwear, or accessories.|The latest fashions from Paris. 巴黎最新的时装。|Synonyms: trends, styles, modes, customs, practices|Antonyms: old-fashioned, outdated, traditional|noun
