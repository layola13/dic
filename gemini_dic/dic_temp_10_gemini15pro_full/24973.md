brusquely:/ˈbrʌskli/ /ˈbrʊskli/| adv.|唐突地，粗鲁地，|acting or speaking in a blunt, abrupt, and often rude manner|He answered her question brusquely and turned away. 他粗鲁地回答了她的问题，然后转身离开了。|She brusquely told him to leave. 她粗暴地叫他离开。|近义词：abruptly, curtly, bluntly, sharply, roughly|反义词：politely, gently, kindly, courteously|词性:副词
