greediness:/ˈɡriːdinəs/| n.|贪婪，贪心|An excessive desire to acquire or possess more than one needs or deserves, especially with respect to material wealth.|His greediness led him to cheat his business partners.|近义词: avarice, cupidity, rapacity|反义词: generosity, altruism, contentment|词性: noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *gredienes, equivalent to greedy +‎ -ness. More at greedy.
