adrenaline: /əˈdrɛnəlɪn/ (US) /əˈdrɛnəlɪn/ (UK)| n.| 肾上腺素 | A hormone secreted by the adrenal glands, especially in conditions of stress, increasing rates of blood circulation, breathing, and carbohydrate metabolism and preparing muscles for exertion. |  The sudden fright caused an adrenaline rush.  突然的惊吓导致肾上腺素激增。

A surge of adrenaline helped him finish the race.  一股肾上腺素的涌动帮助他完成了比赛。 | 近义词: epinephrine | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider "tranquilizer" as a functional opposite) | 词性: noun
