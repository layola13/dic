grants:/ɡrɑːnts/ /ɡrænts/| n. v.|拨款，资助；授予，准予|A sum of money given by a government or other organization for a particular purpose.  (n.) To agree to give or allow (something requested) to someone. (v.)|The government grants subsidies to farmers. 政府向农民提供补贴。 (n.)  He granted her request. 他同意了她的请求。(v.)|近义词：(n.) subsidies, endowments, appropriations, awards; (v.) allow, concede, permit, bestow|反义词：(v.) refuse, deny, reject|词性:名词, 动词
