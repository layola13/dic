deterring:/dɪˈtɜːrɪŋ/
adjective
威慑的，制止的
Discouraging someone from doing something, typically by instilling doubt or fear of the consequences.
The high security fence acts as a deterring factor to thieves.  高高的安全栅栏对小偷起到了威慑作用。
近义词: dissuading, discouraging, preventing, inhibiting
反义词: encouraging, promoting, inciting, abetting
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From the verb "deter" + "-ing" (present participle suffix).
"Deter" comes from the Late Latin "dēterrēre,"  meaning "to frighten from," from "dē-" (from, away) + "terrēre" (to frighten).  "Terrēre" is related to the Latin word "terror" (fear, dread).
