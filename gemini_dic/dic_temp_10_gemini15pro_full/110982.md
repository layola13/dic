liquidises:/ˈlɪkwɪˌdaɪzɪz/ (UK), /ˈlɪkwəˌdaɪzɪz/ (US)| v.| 清算；变卖；（使）成为液体| To convert assets into cash; to kill. The company was liquidated after filing for bankruptcy.  公司在申请破产后被清算。|Synonyms: dissolve, sell off, kill, eliminate|Antonyms: establish, solidify, create|verb

liquidize:/ˈlɪkwɪˌdaɪz/ (UK), /ˈlɪkwəˌdaɪz/ (US)| v.| 清算；变卖；（使）成为液体| To convert assets into cash; to kill. He had to liquidize some of his assets to pay off his debts. 他不得不变卖一些资产来偿还债务。|Synonyms: dissolve, sell off, kill, eliminate|Antonyms: establish, solidify, create|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin liquidus (“liquid”), from Latin liqueo (“I melt, become liquid”).
