noninsistence:/ˌnɑːnɪnˈsɪstəns/
n.
非坚持；不坚持；不强求
lack of insistence; absence of demanding behavior
Her noninsistence on the matter made the negotiation smoother. 她对这件事不坚持，使得谈判更加顺利。

近义词: acquiescence, compliance, acceptance, resignation
反义词: insistence, persistence, demand, requirement
词性: 名词
