antisensuously:/ˌæntiˈsensjuəsli/| adv.| 反感官地，非感官地，禁欲地|Appealing to the intellect rather than the senses; not sensuously or voluptuously.  Rejecting or opposing sensual indulgence. |He lived antisensuously, rejecting the pleasures of the flesh. 他过着禁欲的生活，拒绝肉体的享乐。|Near synonyms: ascetically, abstinently, austerely, puritanically|Antonyms: sensuously, voluptuously, hedonistically|Adverb
