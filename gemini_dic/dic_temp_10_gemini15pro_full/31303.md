chaplain: /ˈtʃæplɪn/ (US), /ˈtʃæplɪn/ (UK)
noun
牧师；神职人员
A member of the clergy attached to a private chapel, institution, non-religious organization, or the armed forces.
The chaplain visited the sick soldiers in the hospital.  这位牧师探望了医院里生病的士兵。

近义词: minister, priest, clergy, pastor
反义词: N/A
词性: noun
