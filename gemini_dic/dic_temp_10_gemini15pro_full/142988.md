outbarter:/ˌaʊtˈbɑːrtər/
|v.|胜过(在交易或讨价还价中)|To surpass or defeat someone in bartering or negotiating a trade.|He outbartered the rug merchant and got a great deal. 他在讨价还价中胜过了地毯商，得到了一个很好的交易。|Synonyms: outtrade, outsmart, outperform|Antonyms: be outdone, be outsmarted|verb

outbarter:/ˌaʊtˈbɑːrtər/
|n.|胜过(在交易或讨价还价中的人)|One who surpasses or defeats another in bartering or negotiating a trade. (Rare usage)|He was known as a shrewd outbarter in the marketplace. 他在市场上被称为精明的交易高手。|Synonyms: shrewd trader, expert negotiator|Antonyms: poor negotiator, easy mark|noun
