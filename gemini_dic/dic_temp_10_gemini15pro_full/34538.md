cleanses:/ˈklɛnzɪz/| v.| 清洁，净化，涤荡| To rid of impurities, foreign matter, or undesirable elements.|  This soap cleanses the skin thoroughly.  这种肥皂能彻底清洁皮肤。|近义词：purifies, purges, cleans|反义词: pollutes, contaminates, dirties|词性:动词

cleansing:/ˈklɛnzɪŋ/| n. adj.| 清洁，净化，涤荡|The act of cleaning or purifying something, or having the ability to clean or purify.|  The facial cleansing left her skin feeling refreshed.  面部清洁让她感觉皮肤神清气爽。  She used a cleansing lotion. 她用了一种清洁乳液。|近义词：purifying, purging, cleaning|反义词: polluting, contaminating, dirtying|词性:名词, 形容词


词源: From Middle English clensen, from Old French clenser, from Latin clēnsāre, frequentative of clēnsō (“I clean, cleanse, purify, make bright”), from clēns (“clean, pure, bright”).
