mesoblast: /ˈmɛzəˌblæst/ /ˈmiːzəˌblæst/ | n. | 中胚层 | The middle germ layer of a metazoan embryo, between the ectoderm and endoderm, that develops into connective tissue, muscle, bone, urogenital and circulatory systems. |  Mesenchymal stem cells are derived from mesoblast.  间 mesenchymal 干细胞来源于中胚层。| synonyms: mesoderm | antonyms: ectoderm, endoderm | noun
