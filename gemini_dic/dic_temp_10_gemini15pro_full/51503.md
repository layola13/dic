detrain:/diːˈtreɪn/ | v. |下火车；（尤指部队）下运输工具|To leave a train, or (especially of troops) a transport vehicle. |The soldiers detrained at the station. 士兵们在车站下火车。|  The passengers detrained and headed for the terminal.  乘客们下了火车，前往航站楼。 |近义词：disembark, alight |反义词：entrain, board |词性:动词

detrained:/diːˈtreɪn/ | adj. |下过火车的|Having left a train. | The detrained passengers were met by a welcoming committee. 下了火车的乘客受到欢迎委员会的迎接。 | The detrained soldiers marched to their barracks. 下了火车的士兵们向他们的营房走去。| 近义词：disembarked | 反义词：entrained | 词性:形容词
