equability:/ˌiːkwəˈbɪləti/
n.
平静；均匀；稳定
the quality of being steady and predictable; the quality of not changing much
The equability of his temperament made him a good mediator. 他的性情平和使他成为一名优秀的调解员。

近义词:stability, steadiness, evenness, uniformity, consistency
反义词:variability, instability, unevenness, inconsistency
词性:名词


equabilities:/ˌiːkwəˈbɪlətiz/
n.
平静；均匀；稳定（复数）
plural form of equability, referring to multiple instances of steadiness, uniformity or consistency.
The equabilities of the climate made it ideal for growing certain crops.  这种气候的稳定性使其非常适合种植某些作物。

近义词:stabilities, steadinesses, evennesses, uniformities, consistencies
反义词:variabilities, instabilities, unevennesses, inconsistencies
词性:名词

词源:
From Late Latin aequabilitatem, from Latin aequabilis "equal, consistent," from aequus "equal, level, fair, calm."
