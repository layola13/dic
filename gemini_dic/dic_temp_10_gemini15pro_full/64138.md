equal: /ˈiːkwəl/ (US) /ˈiːkwəl/ (UK)| adj. n. vt.|相等的，平等的；同等的人或物；比得上|being the same in quantity, size, degree, or value.|例句：Two plus two equals four. 2加2等于4。|近义词：equivalent, identical, uniform, alike|反义词：unequal, different, disparate|词性:形容词

equal: /ˈiːkwəl/ (US) /ˈiːkwəl/ (UK)| n.|同等的人或物|a person or thing that is equal to another.|例句：He is my equal in strength. 他和我的力气一样大。|近义词：peer, counterpart|反义词：opposite, inferior, superior|词性:名词

equal: /ˈiːkwəl/ (US) /ˈiːkwəl/ (UK)| vt.|等于；比得上|be equal to; amount to.|例句：Four times five equals twenty. 四乘五等于二十。|近义词：match, rival|反义词：differ from, fall short of|词性:动词
