hope:/hoʊp/ (US) /həʊp/ (UK)| n. v.|希望，期望；抱有希望的人或事物|A feeling of expectation and desire for a certain thing to happen.|例句：I hope to see you soon. 我希望很快能见到你。

例句：She is the hope of her family. 她是家人的希望。


近义词：expectation, wish, desire, aspiration|反义词:despair, hopelessness|词性:名词,动词
