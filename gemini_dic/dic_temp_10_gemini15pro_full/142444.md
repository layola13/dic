oscillative:/ˈɒsɪleɪtɪv/ (US) /ˈɒsɪlətɪv/ (UK)| adj.|振荡的，振动的，摆动的|Characterized by oscillation; swinging back and forth.|The pendulum's oscillative motion kept time. 钟摆的振荡运动保持着时间。|近义词：vibratory, fluctuating, swinging, pulsating|反义词：stable, steady, fixed, motionless|词性:形容词
oscillate:/ˈɒsɪleɪt/ (US) /ˈɒsɪlət/ (UK)| v.|振荡，振动，摆动|To swing back and forth regularly.|The fan oscillates to cool the entire room. 风扇摆动以冷却整个房间。|近义词：vibrate, fluctuate, swing, pulsate|反义词：stabilize, steady, fix|词性:动词
oscillation:/ˌɒsɪˈleɪʃn/ (US) /ˌɒsɪˈleɪʃən/ (UK)| n.|振荡，振动，摆动|The act or state of oscillating.|The oscillation of the stock market makes investors nervous. 股市的波动让投资者感到紧张。|近义词：vibration, fluctuation, swing, pulsation|反义词：stability, steadiness, fixity|词性:名词

词源: From Latin *oscillatus*, past participle of *oscillare* "to swing," from *oscillum* "swing."
