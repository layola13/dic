anacrotic:/ˌænəˈkrɒtɪk/| adj.|上升支的，逆向搏动的|Relating to or characterized by anacrotism (a pulse with a small additional beat or notch on the ascending part of the main beat).  Characterized by a notch in the upstroke of a pulse tracing.|The doctor noted an anacrotic pulse in the patient, suggesting a possible heart condition.|近义词：bisferious (rare), dicrotic (somewhat related but opposite in the notch location)|反义词：catacrotic|词性: adjective
