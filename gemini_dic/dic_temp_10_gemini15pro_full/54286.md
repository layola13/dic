disguising:/dɪsˈɡaɪzɪŋ/
verb
伪装，掩饰
the act of changing someone's appearance so that people cannot recognize them
He was disguising his real intentions. 他在掩饰他的真实意图。
近义词: camouflaging, concealing, masking
反义词: revealing, exposing
词性: Verb (present participle of disguise)

disguise:/dɪsˈɡaɪz/
noun
伪装，掩饰
something that is worn to hide someone's true appearance
He wore a disguise to avoid being recognized. 他为了避免被认出来而乔装打扮。
近义词: camouflage, concealment, mask
反义词: revelation, exposure
词性: Noun

disguise:/dɪsˈɡaɪz/
verb
伪装，掩饰
to change your appearance so that people cannot recognize you
She disguised herself as a man. 她把自己伪装成一个男人。
近义词: camouflage, conceal, mask
反义词: reveal, expose
词性: Verb

词源: From Old French *desguisier, from des- "opposite of" + guise "manner, fashion."
