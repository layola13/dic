nonliterariness:/ˌnɒnlɪtəˈrɛrɪnɪs/
n.
非文学性，非文字性
The nonliterariness of the text made it difficult to analyze using traditional literary methods.  这篇文本的非文学性使得很难用传统的文学方法来分析。
The nonliterariness of the oral tradition presents challenges for scholars. 口头传统的非文学性给学者们带来了挑战。
Synonym:  informality, unliterary quality
Antonym: literariness, literary quality
noun
