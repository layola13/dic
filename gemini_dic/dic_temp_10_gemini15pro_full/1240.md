accomplish:/əˈkɒmplɪʃ/ (UK) /əˈkɑːmplɪʃ/ (US)| vt.|完成，实现，达到|To successfully complete something or achieve a goal.|She finally accomplished her dream of becoming a doctor. 她终于实现了成为医生的梦想。

The team accomplished the mission in record time.  团队以创纪录的时间完成了任务。

近义词:achieve, attain, complete, fulfill, realize|反义词:fail, abandon|词性:动词

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He accomplishes his tasks efficiently. = 他高效地完成他的任务。
She accomplishes her goals through hard work. = 她通过努力工作实现了自己的目标。

一般过去时:
They accomplished the project ahead of schedule. = 他们提前完成了项目。
We accomplished a lot during our meeting. = 我们在会议期间完成了许多工作。

现在完成时:
I have accomplished everything on my to-do list. = 我已经完成了待办事项清单上的所有内容。
She has accomplished more than she ever thought possible. = 她完成的事情比她想象的还要多。

过去完成时:
He had accomplished a great deal before he retired. = 他在退休前取得了很大的成就。
They had accomplished their mission before the deadline. = 他们在截止日期前完成了任务。

将来时:
We will accomplish our objectives by the end of the year. = 我们将在年底前完成我们的目标。
She will accomplish great things in her career. = 她将在事业上取得伟大的成就。

被动语态:
The task was accomplished successfully. = 任务成功完成。
The project was accomplished ahead of schedule. = 项目提前完成。
