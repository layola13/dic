incoercible:/ˌɪnkəʊˈɜːsɪbl/ /ˌɪnkoʊˈɜːrsəbl/
adjective
不能强制的；无法控制的；无法压制的
Not able to be forced or controlled.
His incoercible optimism was infectious. 他那不可抑制的乐观情绪很有感染力。
The laughter was incoercible. 笑声无法抑制。
synonyms: uncontrollable, irrepressible, unconstrained, unstoppable,  unsuppressible
antonyms: controllable, repressible, restrainable, suppressible
词性: adjective
