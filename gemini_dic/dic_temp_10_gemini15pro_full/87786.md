hibernating:/ˈhaɪbərneɪtɪŋ/| adj.|冬眠的，蛰伏的|spending the winter in a dormant state|例句：The bear is hibernating in its den. 熊在它的洞穴里冬眠。|例句：Many insects are hibernating during the cold winter months. 许多昆虫在寒冷的冬季月份冬眠。|近义词：dormant, inactive, resting|反义词:active, awake, lively|词性:adjective
hibernating:/ˈhaɪbərneɪtɪŋ/| n.|冬眠|the state of being dormant during the winter|例句：Hibernating is a survival strategy for some animals. 冬眠是一些动物的生存策略。|例句：The squirrel's hibernating lasted for several months. 松鼠的冬眠持续了几个月。|近义词：dormancy, inactivity|反义词:activity, wakefulness|词性:noun
