cattily:/ˈkætɪli/| adv.|刻薄地，恶意地|In a spiteful or mean-spirited way, often with the intention to hurt or offend.  Characterized by malicious remarks or behavior.|She cattily remarked on her rival's dress. 她刻薄地评论对手的着装。|近义词：spitefully, maliciously, meanly, nastily, venomously|反义词：kindly, generously, nicely, pleasantly|词性:副词

cattily:/ˈkætɪli/| adv.|恶意地，尖酸刻薄地|Done in a way that is intended to be unkind and to upset someone.  Marked by a deliberate and often subtle desire to inflict emotional pain.|He cattily whispered to his friend about her new hairstyle. 他恶意地跟朋友窃窃私语她的新发型。|近义词：bitingly, sarcastically, snidely|反义词：graciously, politely, respectfully|词性:副词
