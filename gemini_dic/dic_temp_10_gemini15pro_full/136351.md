nonresistibility:/ˌnɒnrɪˌzɪstəˈbɪləti/ /ˌnɑːnrɪˌzɪstəˈbɪləti/
noun
不可抗拒性；无法抗拒
The quality of being impossible to resist; irresistibility.
Her beauty had a certain nonresistibility. 她的美貌有一种不可抗拒的魅力。
Synonyms: irresistibility, allure, magnetism, fascination, charm
Antonyms: resistibility, unattractiveness, repulsion
n.
