nadir:/ˈneɪdɪr/ /ˈneɪdər/| n.|最低点；最糟的时刻；天底|The nadir of her career came when she was fired from her dream job. 她事业的最低谷是她被梦想的工作解雇的时候。|例句：The relationship reached its nadir last year. 这段关系在去年跌到了谷底。|例句：The economy reached its nadir during the recession. 经济在衰退期间跌至谷底。|近义词：bottom, lowest point, rock bottom|反义词：zenith, peak, apex|词性:名词

Etymology: From Arabic *naẓīr* "opposite, corresponding to," meaning "opposite" the zenith.
