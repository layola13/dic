interactor:/ˌɪntərˈæktər/ | n. | 互动者；交互者；交谈者 | A person who interacts or communicates with another.  | Example: The interactor asked the audience questions throughout the presentation. 演示过程中，互动者向观众提问。

| Example: The online platform allows interactors to connect with experts in various fields.  这个在线平台允许互动者与各领域的专家联系。 | Synonyms: communicator, participant, interlocutor | Antonyms: observer, bystander | Noun
