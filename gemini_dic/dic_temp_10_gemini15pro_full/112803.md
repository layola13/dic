lucchese:/luːˈkeɪzeɪ/ /luːˈkeɪsi/
n.
Lucchese boots (plural)
鲁凯斯(牛仔靴品牌); 鲁凯斯靴子
High-quality, handcrafted cowboy boots made by the Lucchese Boot Company, a Texas-based bootmaker known for its meticulous craftsmanship and premium materials.
These Lucchese boots are my most prized possession.  这些鲁凯斯靴子是我最珍贵的财产。
近义词: cowboy boots, western boots, riding boots
反义词: sneakers, sandals
词性: noun
