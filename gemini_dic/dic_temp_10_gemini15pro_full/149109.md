oxfordish:/ˈɒksfədɪʃ/
adjective
牛津式的；带有牛津大学风格的
Characteristic of or resembling the University of Oxford, its inhabitants, or its way of life.
His accent was distinctly Oxfordish. 他的口音明显是牛津式的。
近义词:Oxonian, academic, intellectual, scholarly
反义词:uneducated, ignorant, common, vulgar
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Oxford + -ish.  Oxford refers to the city of Oxford, England, home to the University of Oxford. The suffix "-ish" is used to form adjectives meaning "of or belonging to," "characteristic of," or "somewhat like."
