lectureships:/ˈlɛktʃərʃɪps/
n.
讲师职位；讲师的职务
positions or posts as lecturers
Several lectureships in the English department are currently open.  英语系目前有几个讲师职位空缺。
synonyms:  lecturer positions, teaching posts, academic appointments
antonyms:  studentships, fellowships (could be considered a contrasting position, not a direct opposite)
noun
