nonprotestations:/ˌnɒnprəʊtɛˈsteɪʃənz/
n.
lack of protests; absence of objections
The nonprotestations of the citizens about the new law was surprising. 市民对新法律的缺乏抗议令人惊讶。
Her nonprotestations about the extra work implied she was happy to do it. 她对额外工作的默许暗示她乐意去做。
近义词: acquiescence, silence, acceptance
反义词: objections, protests, dissent
词性: noun
