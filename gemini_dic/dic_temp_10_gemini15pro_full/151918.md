pasteurising:/ˈpæstʃəˌraɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 巴氏消毒，巴氏灭菌 | The process of heating a liquid, such as milk or juice, to a specific temperature for a short period of time in order to kill harmful bacteria without changing the flavor or nutritional value. |  The milk is pasteurised to ensure it's safe to drink.  牛奶经过巴氏消毒，确保可以安全饮用。 | sterilizing, sanitizing | contaminating | verb
