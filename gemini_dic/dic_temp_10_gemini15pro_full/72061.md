flisk: /flɪsk/
n.|轻弹； flick, flip|A flisk of the wrist sent the coin spinning.  手腕一抖，硬币就旋转起来。|例句：She flisked the dust off her jacket. 她轻弹了一下夹克上的灰尘。

例句：He flisked a pebble into the pond. 他把一块小石头弹进池塘里。

近义词：flick, flip, whisk
反义词：(No direct antonym, but consider "press firmly," "hold," or "grasp")
词性: noun (primarily used as a verb in informal contexts)


flisk: /flɪsk/
v.|轻弹；轻拂|To quickly move or flick something. |例句：She flisked her hair out of her eyes. 她把头发从眼前拂开。

例句：He flisked the fly away. 他轻弹一下把苍蝇赶走了。

近义词：flick, flip, whisk
反义词：(No direct antonym, but consider "press firmly," "hold," or "grasp")
词性: verb (informal)


词源:  Likely a blend of "flick" and "whisk," both words describing quick, light movements.  It's considered informal or dialectal.  No definitive etymological source exists for "flisk" as it's not a widely recognized word.
