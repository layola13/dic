ducts:/dʌkts/
n.|管道，导管|A duct is a tube or passageway in a building or machine for air, liquid, cables, etc. |The air conditioning ducts need cleaning.  空调管道需要清洁。|tubes, pipes, channels, conduits| blockages, obstructions
词性: 名词

ducts:/dʌkts/
v.|输送，引导 (第三人称单数)|Third-person singular simple present indicative form of duct. |The pump ducts water into the reservoir.  泵把水输送到水库。|convey, transport, channel, guide|N/A
词性: 动词


词源:
From Latin *ductus*, past participle of *ducere* "to lead."
