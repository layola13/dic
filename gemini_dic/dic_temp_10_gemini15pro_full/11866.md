被动语态 (Passive Voice):  Although less common, "arrive at" can be used in a passive sense with a different meaning.  It suggests a result or outcome is achieved:
A compromise was finally arrived at. 最终达成了妥协。
A verdict will be arrived at after all the evidence is presented. 在所有证据提交后，将作出判决。
