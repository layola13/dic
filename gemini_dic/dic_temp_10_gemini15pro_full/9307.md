antipleasure:/ˌæntiˈpleʒər/ | n. | 反快感，厌恶快乐 |The concept of antipleasure explores the rejection or suppression of pleasure.  反快感这一概念探讨的是对快乐的排斥或抑制。|  He seemed to find a perverse antipleasure in his own suffering. 他似乎在自己遭受的痛苦中找到了一种反常的反快感。 | 近义词: aversion to pleasure, displeasure, pain, suffering | 反义词: pleasure, enjoyment, delight, joy | 词性: 名词

词源:
anti- (prefix) : against, opposite of (源自希腊语 anti-)
pleasure (noun): a feeling of happy satisfaction and enjoyment (源自古法语 plaisir)
