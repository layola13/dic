endowment:/ɪnˈdaʊmənt/| n.| 捐赠，天赋，才能| Something that is endowed; a gift, ability, or quality bestowed on someone or something.  It can also refer to the act of giving such a gift, or to funds or property donated to an institution. |The university received a large endowment for the new library. 这所大学收到了为新图书馆建造的大笔捐款。|Synonyms: gift, bequest, grant, talent, aptitude, attribute|Antonyms: deprivation, forfeiture|noun

endowment:/ɪnˈdaʊmənt/| n.|捐赠物，基金|  Money or property donated to an institution, individual, or group as a source of income.|The hospital's endowment allows it to provide free care to low-income patients. 医院的捐赠基金使其能够为低收入病人提供免费护理。|Synonyms: donation, contribution, benefaction|Antonyms: debt, withdrawal|noun
