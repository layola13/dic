binging:/ˈbɪndʒɪŋ/| adj. |  狂欢的，放纵的 | Characterized by excessive indulgence. | He's been on a binging spree all week, eating junk food and playing video games. 他整个星期都沉迷于吃垃圾食品和玩电子游戏。 | excessive, immoderate, uncontrolled | moderate, restrained, controlled | adjective
