oversocializes: /ˌoʊvərˈsoʊʃəlaɪzɪz/
verb
过度社交
Engages in social interaction to an excessive degree, often to the point of exhaustion or neglecting other responsibilities.
He tends to oversocialize on weekends, leaving him too tired for work on Monday.  他周末往往过度社交，导致周一上班很累。
She oversocialized at the conference, missing several important workshops.  她在会议上过度社交，错过了几个重要的研讨会。
Synonyms: overdoes it socially, socializes excessively
Antonyms: isolates, withdraws, keeps to oneself
word type: verb

oversocializing: /ˌoʊvərˈsoʊʃəlaɪzɪŋ/
noun
过度社交
The act of engaging in social interaction to an excessive degree.
Oversocializing can lead to burnout and neglecting personal needs. 过度社交会导致倦怠和忽视个人需求。
His oversocializing made it difficult for him to focus on his studies. 他的过度社交使他难以专注于学习。
Synonyms: excessive socializing
Antonyms: isolation, withdrawal
word type: noun
