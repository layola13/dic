misvalued:/ˌmɪsˈvæljuːd/
adjective
估值过低的，价值被低估的
incorrectly valued; underestimated.
The company's assets were misvalued by the accountants.  这家公司的资产被会计师低估了。
The painting was misvalued and sold for much less than it was worth. 这幅画被低估了，售价远低于其实际价值。

近义词:undervalued, underestimated
反义词:overvalued, overestimated
词性:adjective

词源:
mis- (prefix): wrongly, badly.  来自古英语，意为“错误地”，“坏地”。
value (verb): to estimate the monetary worth of. 来自古法语，意为“价值”，“价格”。
-ed (suffix): forms the past participle of regular verbs.  过去分词后缀。
