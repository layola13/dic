controllerships:/kənˈtroʊlərʃɪps/
n.
主计长职位；主计长职权
The position and responsibilities of a financial controller.  A controllership encompasses a wide range of tasks, including financial reporting, budgeting, and internal controls.
The company is looking to fill the controllership after the previous controller retired.  这家公司正在寻找人选来填补前任财务主管退休后空缺的主计长职位。
He accepted the controllership of a major corporation. 他接受了一家大公司的主计长职位。
Synonym: comptrollership
Antonym: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "lack of financial control" or "financial mismanagement")
