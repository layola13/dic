fungia:/ˈfʌndʒiə/
n.
蕈珊瑚, 菌珊瑚, 碟形珊瑚
A genus of mushroom corals.  These corals are solitary and disc-shaped.
Divers often spot large fungia on sandy bottoms.  潜水员经常在沙质底部发现大型蕈珊瑚。

近义词:mushroom coral, disc coral
反义词:(No direct antonym, but could consider colonial corals which are not solitary)
词性:noun
