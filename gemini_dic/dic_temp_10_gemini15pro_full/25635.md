bunchiness:/ˈbʌntʃɪnɪs/
noun
成束性，簇生，丛生，聚集成群
The quality or state of being bunchy or occurring in bunches.  The bunchiness of the grapes made them difficult to store.  葡萄的簇生使得它们难以储存。
The fabric had an undesirable bunchiness where it was gathered.  这种织物在褶皱的地方有一种不理想的簇生感。
clusteredness, clumpiness,  lumpiness
evenness, smoothness, dispersion
n.
