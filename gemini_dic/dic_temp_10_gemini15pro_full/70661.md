filum:/ˈfaɪləm/| n. |丝；线；细丝；（生物分类学的）门|A threadlike structure or filament. In biology, it refers to a principal taxonomic category that ranks above class and below kingdom.|例句：The spider spins a silken filum. 蜘蛛吐出丝线。

例句：The Arthropoda is a large filum including insects, spiders, and crustaceans. 节肢动物门是一个很大的门，包括昆虫、蜘蛛和甲壳类动物。|近义词：thread, filament, fiber|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From Latin *fīlum* "thread," related to *fībere* "to spin."
