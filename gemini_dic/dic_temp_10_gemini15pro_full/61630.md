emigrative:/ˈemɪɡreɪtɪv/
adjective
迁徙的，移民的
Relating to or tending to emigrate; migrating from one country to another.
The family's emigrative journey was fraught with challenges, but they eventually settled in their new home.  这个家庭的移民之旅充满了挑战，但他们最终在新家安顿下来。
She felt an emigrative urge, a strong desire to leave her homeland and start anew elsewhere. 她有一种移民的冲动，强烈地渴望离开家乡，到别处重新开始。
近义词: migratory, expatriate
反义词: immigrative, repatriative
词性: adjective

词源:
From emigrate + -ive.  Emigrate itself comes from the Latin ex- (out of) + migrare (to migrate).
