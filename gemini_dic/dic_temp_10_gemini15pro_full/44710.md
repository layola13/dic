cruelest: /ˈkruːɪlɪst/ (UK) /ˈkruːəlɪst/ (US) | adj. | 最残酷的，最残忍的 |  Superlative form of cruel: most cruel.  It describes something or someone that inflicts the greatest amount of pain, suffering, or distress. | The king's cruelest punishment was reserved for traitors. 国王最残酷的惩罚是留给叛徒的。

The cruelest month is April, breeding lilacs out of the dead land. 四月是最残忍的一个月, 荒地上丁香花怒放。 | Synonyms: harshest, fiercest, most brutal, most ruthless, most savage | Antonyms: kindest, gentlest, most compassionate, most merciful | Word type: Adjective
