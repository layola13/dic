crustiest: /ˈkrʌstiɪst/
adjective
最硬的，最粗糙的，最暴躁的
Superlative form of crusty: having a hard or brittle surface or coating.  Also can refer to a bad-tempered person.
The crustiest bread in the bakery was surprisingly delicious.  面包店里最硬的面包出奇地好吃。
He was the crustiest old sailor I'd ever met. 他是我见过的脾气最暴躁的老水手。
近义词: hardest, toughest, grumpiest, surliest
反义词: softest, smoothest, kindest, friendliest
词性: adjective
词源: From "crust" (Old French "crouste," from Latin "crusta") + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives) + "-est" (superlative suffix).
