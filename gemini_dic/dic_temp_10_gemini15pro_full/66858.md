expectationists:/ɛkspɛkˈteɪʃənɪsts/
n.
期望者；期待者；預期者
Those who have expectations; people who anticipate specific outcomes or believe that certain things will happen.
The expectationists were disappointed when the predicted boom did not materialize.  期望者们对预测的繁荣没有实现感到失望。
The expectationists eagerly awaited the announcement of the lottery results. 期望者们热切地等待着彩票结果的公布。
近义词: anticipators, hopefuls
反义词: pessimists, skeptics
词性: noun
