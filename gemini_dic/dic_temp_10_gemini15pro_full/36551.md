collagist:/ˈkɒləʒɪst/ (US) /ˈkɒlɑːʒɪst/ (UK)| n. |拼贴艺术家|An artist who creates collages, artworks composed of various materials such as paper, photographs, and fabric glued to a surface.|Hannah Höch was a prominent German collagist in the Dada movement. 汉娜·霍克是达达运动中一位杰出的德国拼贴艺术家。|近义词：collage artist, mixed-media artist|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *collagiste*, from *collage* (collage), from *coller* (to glue, stick), from Late Latin *collare* (to glue), from Latin *collum* (neck).  Originally referred to the act of gluing, and later extended to the artistic technique.
