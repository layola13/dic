billethead:/ˈbɪlɪtˌhɛd/| n.|账单抬头，信笺抬头，便条抬头|A printed heading on a bill, letterhead, or notepaper, typically containing the name and address of the sender.|The company's billethead featured a stylized logo and their contact information.  公司信笺抬头印有程式化的标志和联系方式。|Synonyms: letterhead, heading, masthead|Antonyms: N/A|noun

billethead:/ˈbɪlɪtˌhɛd/| n.|（旧）招兵广告|A poster or notice used to advertise for recruits, especially in the military.|Old-fashioned billetheads called for young men to join the army.  老式的招兵广告号召年轻人参军。|Synonyms: recruitment poster, notice, advertisement|Antonyms: N/A|noun
