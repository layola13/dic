bonded:/ˈbɒndɪd/ (UK) /ˈbɑːndɪd/ (US)| adj.|粘合的；结合的；保税的；有担保的；团结的|Joined securely; having formed a close relationship; stored in a bonded warehouse; secured by a bond. |The bricks are bonded together with cement. 这些砖是用水泥粘合在一起的。|Synonyms: attached, connected, united, fused, linked, secured, guaranteed|Antonyms: separated, detached, unconnected, unbonded|adjective

bonded:/ˈbɒndɪd/ (UK) /ˈbɑːndɪd/ (US)| v.|粘合；结合；(使)建立密切关系|(past tense and past participle of bond) To join securely; to develop a close relationship. |The experience bonded them together for life.  这段经历使他们一生都紧密联系在一起。|Synonyms: connect, unite, link |Antonyms: separate, detach|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "bond," which comes from the Old English "bindan" meaning "to tie, bind."
