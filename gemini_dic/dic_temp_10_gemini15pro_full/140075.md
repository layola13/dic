of-age:/əvˈeɪdʒ/
adj.
成年的，达到法定年龄的
Having reached the legal age; adult.
例句：He is of-age to vote. 他已达到投票年龄。
例句：She's of-age now and can make her own decisions. 她现在成年了，可以自己做决定。
近义词：adult, mature, grown-up
反义词：underage, minor
词性: 形容词

Etymology: "of age," from Old French "en age" (13c.), from Latin "in aetate" ("in age").
