floodgate:/ˈflʌdɡeɪt/| n.|防洪闸门; 涌出; 泄洪道; (感情等的)闸门|A gate used to control the flow of water, especially one in a flood barrier.  Figuratively, something that releases a large amount of repressed emotion or something undesirable.|The heavy rains threatened to breach the floodgates.|Synonyms: barrier, dam, sluice, gate, valve|Antonyms: opening, inlet|noun
