candlefish:/ˈkænd(ə)lfɪʃ/| n.|烛鱼|A small, oily fish (Thaleichthys pacificus) found in the North Pacific, so rich in oil that it can be burned like a candle.|The indigenous peoples of the Pacific Northwest have traditionally used candlefish as a source of food and light. 太平洋西北部的土著居民传统上将烛鱼用作食物和光源。|近义词：eulachon, oolichan|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider lean fish)|noun
