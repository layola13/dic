durably:/ˈdjʊərəbli/ /ˈdʊrəbli/| adv.|持久地，耐用地|In a way that is able to resist wear, decay, or damage for a long time.|This sofa is durably constructed and will last for years. 这款沙发经久耐用，可以使用很多年。|近义词：lastingly, enduringly, permanently|反义词：temporarily, briefly, transiently|词性:副词

词源:
From Middle English *durablely, derived from durable + -ly. Ultimately from Latin dūrābilis (“lasting, durable”), from dūrō (“harden, endure, last”).
