pahlevi: /ˈpɑːləviː/ | n. adj. | 巴列维语; 巴列维王朝的 | An official language of Persia from the 3rd to the 10th century, descended from Old Persian and written in a script derived from Aramaic. Also used as an adjective to describe things related to the Pahlavi dynasty (1925-1979). |  The inscription was written in Pahlavi script. 这段铭文是用巴列维文字书写的。|  The Pahlavi era saw significant modernization in Iran. 巴列维时代见证了伊朗的重大现代化。| synonyms: Middle Persian (language), Imperial (dynasty) | antonyms: Modern Persian (language), Republican (dynasty relating to government type) | noun, adjective
