norroy:/ˈnɔːrɔɪ/
n.
北面纹章官; 北方纹章之王
The chief herald of the north of England.
例句：The Norroy King of Arms granted the town a new coat of arms. 北方纹章之王授予该镇新的盾形纹章。

例句：Historically, Norroy had jurisdiction over all coats of arms north of the River Trent. 从历史上看，北方纹章官对特伦特河以北的所有盾形纹章拥有管辖权。
近义词：herald, king of arms
反义词: (No direct antonym exists.  Consider "Clarenceux King of Arms" for the southern counterpart.)
词性: noun
