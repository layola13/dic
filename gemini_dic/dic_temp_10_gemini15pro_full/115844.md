markis:/mɑːrˈkiːs/ /mɑːrˈkis/| n.|侯爵；女侯爵；遮阳篷|A nobleman ranking below a duke and above an earl/count; a large awning over a window or door, often supported by a frame attached to the wall.|The marquis lived in a grand estate. 侯爵住在一个宏伟的庄园里。|The markis over the shop entrance provided shade from the sun. 商店入口上方的遮阳篷遮挡了阳光。|Synonyms: awning, canopy, marquee (for awning); nobleman, peer (for title)|Antonyms: commoner (for title)|noun
