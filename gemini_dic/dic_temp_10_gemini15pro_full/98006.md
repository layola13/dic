inoperably:/ɪnˈɒpərəbli/
adjective
不能手术地，无法操作地
Not able to be operated on or corrected by surgery; incurable.
The tumor was declared inoperably advanced.  肿瘤被宣布为晚期，无法手术。
The equipment was inoperably damaged.  设备损坏严重，无法操作。
Synonyms: incurable, unrecoverable, terminal, hopeless
Antonyms: operable, curable, treatable
词性: adjective
