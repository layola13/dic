Simple Present:
What do you think? = 你觉得怎么样？
What does she want for her birthday? = 她生日想要什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing over there? = 他在那里做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she been up to lately? = 她最近在忙些什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on? = 你一直在忙什么？
What has he been reading all this time? = 他一直都在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加派对穿了什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had she eaten for dinner? = 她晚饭吃了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before the phone rang? = 电话响之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about? = 他们一直在争论什么？


Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will happen next? = 接下来会发生什么？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will she be studying next semester? = 她下学期会学习什么？


Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将会取得什么成就？
What will they have finished by tomorrow? = 到明天他们将完成什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业的时候，她将已经学习了多久什么专业？


Subjunctive Mood:
If I knew what to say, I would tell you. = 如果我知道该说什么，我会告诉你的。
I wish I knew what he was thinking. = 我希望我知道他在想什么。


Passive Voice:
What was said at the meeting? = 会议上说了什么？
What is being done about the problem? = 正在对这个问题采取什么措施？


Interrogative Sentence:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？


Negative Sentence:
I don't know what to do. = 我不知道该怎么办。
She doesn't understand what I'm saying. = 她不明白我在说什么。
