crappy:/ˈkræpi/| adj.|糟糕的，劣质的|Something that is crappy is of poor quality or very bad.  It is an informal word and can be considered mildly vulgar.|The movie was really crappy.  这部电影真的很烂。|Synonyms: awful, terrible, lousy, rubbish (UK), trashy, poor, shoddy|Antonyms: excellent, great, wonderful, fantastic|adjective

crappy:/ˈkræpi/| adj.|令人不愉快的，烦人的|Something that is crappy can also refer to a situation or experience that is unpleasant or annoying.|I had a really crappy day at work. 我今天工作过得很糟糕。|Synonyms: unpleasant, annoying, frustrating, irritating|Antonyms: pleasant, enjoyable, delightful|adjective
