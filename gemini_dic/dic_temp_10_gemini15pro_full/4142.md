air-out:/ˈer aʊt/| phrasal verb | 晾干；透气；公开 |To expose something to fresh air in order to freshen it or remove unpleasant smells; to make something public, especially a problem or disagreement, so that it can be discussed and resolved. |例句：I aired-out the sleeping bag after camping. 我在露营后把睡袋晾干了。

例句：It's time to air-out these old clothes. 是时候把这些旧衣服晾出去了。

例句：They decided to air-out their differences in a public forum. 他们决定在一个公共论坛上公开他们的分歧。

例句：Let's air-out this issue and come to a resolution. 让我们公开讨论这个问题，并找到解决方案。|近义词：ventilate, air, freshen, publicize, expose |反义词：conceal, suppress |词性: 动词短语
