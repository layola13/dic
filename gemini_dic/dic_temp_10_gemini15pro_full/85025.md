harassingly:/həˈræsɪŋli/
| adv. | 扰人地；不断骚扰地 | In a way that annoys or bothers someone repeatedly or persistently. |  The phone rang harassingly throughout the night. 电话整夜响个不停，令人烦扰。| 近义词: persistently, annoyingly, vexatiously, troublingly | 反义词: pleasantly, agreeably, soothingly | 词性: 副词

harassment:/ˈhærəsmənt/
| n. | 骚扰；烦扰 | Aggressive pressure or intimidation. |  He faced harassment from the police. 他面临警方的骚扰。| 近义词: persecution, intimidation, bullying, torment | 反义词: support, encouragement | 词性: 名词

harass:/ˈhærəs/
| v. | 骚扰；烦扰 | Subject to aggressive pressure or intimidation. | The company was accused of harassing its employees.  该公司被指控骚扰员工。 | 近义词: pester, bother, torment, persecute | 反义词: assist, support | 词性: 动词
