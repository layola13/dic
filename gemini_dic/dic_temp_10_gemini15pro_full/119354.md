microclimate: /ˈmaɪkroʊˌklaɪmɪt/ | n. | 微气候；小气候 |  The climate of a small, specific place within a larger area, which differs from the climate of the surrounding area. |  The valley has its own unique microclimate, much warmer and drier than the surrounding mountains.  山谷有其独特的微气候，比周围的山脉温暖干燥得多。 | synonyms: local climate, microhabitat | antonyms: macroclimate | noun
