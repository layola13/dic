antichrists:/ˈæntɪkraɪsts/| n. |敌基督者，假基督|Someone who denies or opposes Christ; a great antagonist expected to fill the world with wickedness but be conquered by Christ at his second coming.|The book describes the emergence of the Antichrist in the last days. 这本书描述了末世敌基督的出现。|Antichrist is often portrayed as a charismatic leader who deceives many. 敌基督通常被描绘成一个欺骗许多人的有魅力的领袖。|Synonyms: false prophet, adversary, opponent|Antonyms: Christ, Messiah|Noun
