cymbling:/ˈsɪmblɪŋ/
n.
小铃铛,铙钹,小镲
A small cymbal.  A cymbling is a small cymbal, often used in pairs, creating a delicate, tinkling sound.
The dancer’s movements were accented by the delicate chime of the cymblings. 舞者的动作被小镲的精致铃声所强调。
The cymblings clashed together, creating a bright, shimmering sound. 两个小镲撞在一起，发出明亮闪烁的声音。
近义词:small cymbal, tiny cymbal, finger cymbals
反义词:gong, large cymbal
