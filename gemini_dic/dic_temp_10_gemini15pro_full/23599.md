brambles:/ˈbræmbəlz/| n.|荆棘，灌木丛|A prickly shrub or vine, especially a blackberry bush.  A tangled mass of prickly shrubs or vines.|例句：The path was overgrown with brambles.  这条小路被荆棘覆盖了。

例句：We had to fight our way through the brambles. 我们不得不奋力穿过荆棘丛。|近义词：thorns, thickets, bushes, undergrowth|反义词: clearing, open space|词性:noun

brambles:/ˈbræmbəlz/| n.| 难题，困境(比喻)|A difficult or complicated situation.|例句：The project was beset by brambles from the start. 这个项目从一开始就困难重重。

例句：Navigating the legal brambles was a nightmare.  在法律的荆棘中摸索简直就是一场噩梦。|近义词：difficulties, obstacles, problems, complications|反义词:ease, simplicity|词性:noun

词源：From Middle English *brembel, from Old English bræmbel (“bramble”), from Proto-Germanic *brēmlaz (“thorn bush, bramble”).

注意事项：虽然brambles本身是复数形式，但在表示“困境”的比喻义时，通常不加s。 例如：The project was beset by bramble from the start.  这个项目从一开始就困难重重。  这两种用法都需要根据上下文来判断。
