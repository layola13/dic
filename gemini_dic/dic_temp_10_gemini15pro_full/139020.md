oaf:/oʊf/ | n. | 笨蛋，傻瓜 | A clumsy, stupid person. | He's such an oaf, he tripped over his own feet. 他真是个笨蛋，居然被自己的脚绊倒了。 |  He spilled his drink all over himself, the clumsy oaf.  他把饮料洒得满身都是，真是个笨手笨脚的傻瓜。 | 近义词:clumsy, awkward, dolt, idiot, lout | 反义词: genius, mastermind | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old Norse *álfr, meaning "elf." Originally, "oaf" referred to a changeling, a deformed or simple-minded child left by fairies in place of a stolen human child. The meaning shifted over time to refer to any clumsy or stupid person.
