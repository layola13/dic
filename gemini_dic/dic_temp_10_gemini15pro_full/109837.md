lexicologies:/ˌlɛksɪˈkɒlədʒiz/ | n. |词汇学|The study of the form, meaning, and use of words.|例句：Lexicologies is a branch of linguistics. 词汇学是语言学的一个分支。

例句：He is studying lexicologies at university. 他在大学学习词汇学。|近义词：lexicography, semantics|反义词: None|词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French lexicologie, from lexico- +‎ -logie.  Ultimately from Ancient Greek λεξικόν (lexikón, “dictionary”) + -λογία (-logía, “study of”).
