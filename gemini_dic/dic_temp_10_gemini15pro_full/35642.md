coauthors:/ˈkoʊˌɔːθərz/
noun
合著者，共同作者
People who write something together are coauthors.
Mary and John are coauthors of the new textbook.
玛丽和约翰是这本新教科书的合著者。

The scientists coauthored a groundbreaking paper on climate change.
科学家们合著了一篇关于气候变化的突破性论文。

近义词:collaborators, joint authors
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "sole author")
词性:n.
