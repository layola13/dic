nonoccupations:/ˌnɒnɒkjuˈpeɪʃənz/ |n.| 非职业；非工作；闲暇活动 |Activities or pursuits that are not considered occupations or jobs.  These could include hobbies, leisure activities, or volunteer work. |Many people find fulfillment in nonoccupations such as gardening, painting, or playing music. 很多人在园艺、绘画或演奏音乐等非职业活动中找到了满足感。| Synonyms: hobbies, pastimes, leisure activities, avocations | Antonyms: occupations, professions, jobs, careers | noun
