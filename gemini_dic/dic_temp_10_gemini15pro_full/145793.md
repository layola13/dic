overdebilitating: /ˌoʊvər dəˈbɪlɪˌteɪtɪŋ/
adjective
过度衰弱的；极度虚弱的
Excessively weakening; causing extreme debility.
The overdebilitating effects of the chemotherapy left her unable to walk. 化疗带来的过度衰弱的影响使她无法行走。

The illness had an overdebilitating effect on him, making even simple tasks impossible.  这种疾病对他产生了极度虚弱的影响，即使是简单的任务也无法完成。

近义词: crippling, incapacitating, devastating
反义词: invigorating, strengthening, restorative
词性: adjective

词源:  "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "debilitating" (from Latin "debilitare," meaning "to weaken").
