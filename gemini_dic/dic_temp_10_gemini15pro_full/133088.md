nonequivocating:/ˌnɒnɪˈkwɪvəkeɪtɪŋ/
adjective
明确的，毫不含糊的
Clearly and firmly stated; leaving no doubt about its meaning.
The president issued a nonequivocating statement condemning the violence. 总统发表了一份明确谴责暴力的声明。
Her answer was a nonequivocating "yes". 她的回答是毫不含糊的“是”。
Synonyms: unambiguous, unequivocal, clear, direct, explicit, forthright, plain, straightforward
Antonyms: ambiguous, equivocal, vague, unclear, evasive, indirect
词性: adjective
