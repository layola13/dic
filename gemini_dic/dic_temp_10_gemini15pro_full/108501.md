laying-out:/ˈleɪɪŋ aʊt/
noun
规划；布置；安排
the act of arranging something, especially a plan or design
The laying-out of the new garden took several weeks.  新花园的规划花了几个星期。
arrangement, design, plan
disorganization, mess

laying-out:/ˈleɪɪŋ aʊt/
verb
规划；布置；安排
to arrange something, especially a plan or design
They laid out the city in a grid pattern.  他们将城市规划成网格状。
arrange, design, plan
disorganize, mess
