antipope:/ˈæntiˌpoʊp/| n.|伪教皇，对立教皇|A person claiming or considered to be claiming papacy in opposition to the one canonically elected.|During the Great Schism, there were periods with two, and even three, competing antipopes. 在大分裂期间，曾有时期存在两个甚至三个相互竞争的伪教皇。|The antipope set up a rival court in Avignon.  这位伪教皇在阿维尼翁建立了一个敌对的教廷。|近义词：rival pope, pretender|反义词：pope|词性:noun
