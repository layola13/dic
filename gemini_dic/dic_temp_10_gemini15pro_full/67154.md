explosion:/ɪkˈsploʊʒən/ | n. | 爆炸，爆发，激增 | A sudden, violent release of energy, often accompanied by a loud noise and the shattering or destruction of something.  | The explosion destroyed the building. 爆炸摧毁了这座建筑。|  The explosion of the bomb caused widespread damage. 炸弹爆炸造成了广泛的破坏。 | synonyms: detonation, eruption, blast, outburst | antonyms: implosion, collapse | noun

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin explosio(n-), from the verb explodere (see explode).
