lefts:/lɛfts/| n.|剩余物，残羹剩饭；（尤指食物的）残渣，渣滓|Something that remains or is left over, especially food. |The lefts from dinner were given to the dog. 晚饭的剩菜给了狗。|近义词：remains, leftovers, scraps, remnants|反义词: entirety, whole|词性:名词

lefts:/lɛfts/| n.|（政治上）左派，左翼|Those who support left-wing policies. |The lefts are pushing for social reform.  左派正在推动社会改革。|近义词：liberals, progressives, socialists |反义词: right-wing, conservatives|词性:名词

词源: 'lefts' is the plural of 'left', which originates from Old English *lyft, meaning "weak" or "useless," as the left hand was traditionally considered less dominant.  The political sense developed later, referring to the side of the French National Assembly where the more radical members sat.
