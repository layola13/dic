catelectrotonus:/kætˌɪlɛkˈtrɒtənəs/ /kætˌilɛkˈtroʊtənəs/
noun
阴极电紧张
Increased excitability of a nerve or muscle in the region of the cathode during the passage of an electric current.
Example: Catelectrotonus can be observed in nerve fibers when a cathode is applied.  在神经纤维上施加阴极时可以观察到阴极电紧张。
Example: The experiment demonstrated the effects of catelectrotonus on muscle contraction.  该实验演示了阴极电紧张对肌肉收缩的影响。
Synonym: cathodal depression (although not a perfect synonym, it relates to the same phenomenon)
Antonym: anelectrotonus
