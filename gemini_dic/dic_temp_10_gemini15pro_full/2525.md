admirability:/ˌædmərəˈbɪləti/ | n. |可钦佩性，令人赞赏的特性|The quality of being admirable; the capacity to inspire admiration.|Her courage and determination in the face of adversity were of great admirability.  她在逆境中的勇气和决心令人十分钦佩。

The admirability of her dedication to helping others is truly inspiring. 她致力于帮助他人的奉献精神令人敬佩， truly inspiring。|近义词：excellence, merit, worthiness, virtue|反义词：unworthiness, undesirability, contemptibility|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From admirable + -ity.  Admirable comes from Late Middle English, from Old French admirable, from Latin admirabilis "worthy of admiration," from admirari "to wonder at," from ad- "to" + mirari "to wonder."

USAGE->
The admirability of her work ethic is clear. 她的职业道德令人钦佩，显而易见。
The project's admirability lies in its innovative approach. 这个项目的可取之处在于其创新方法。
