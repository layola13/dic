grows:/ɡroʊz/ (American) /ɡrəʊz/ (British)| v. |生长，成长，发展，种植，变成|Third-person singular simple present indicative form of grow|The tree grows taller every year. 这棵树每年都长得更高。|The business grows steadily. 这家公司稳步发展。|近义词：develops, expands, increases, cultivates|反义词:shrinks, declines, diminishes|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Middle English *growen*, from Old English *grōwan* (intransitive, “to grow, flourish, become green, spring up”), from Proto-Germanic *grōaną* (“to grow, become green”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵreh₁- (“to grow, become green”). Cognate with West Frisian *groeie* (“to grow”), Dutch *groeien* (“to grow”), German *grünen* (“to become green”), Danish *gro* (“to sprout, grow”), Swedish *gro* (“to sprout, grow”), Icelandic *gróa* (“to grow, heal”).
