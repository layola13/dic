paramedics:/ˌpærəˈmedɪks/ | n. |护理人员；急救医士|People trained to give emergency medical treatment before or instead of a doctor. |例句：The paramedics arrived quickly and stabilized the patient. 急救人员迅速赶到并稳定了病人的病情。

例句：The paramedics rushed the injured man to the hospital. 急救人员将受伤的男子紧急送往医院。|近义词：EMTs, emergency medical technicians, first responders|反义词: None (No direct opposite exists) |词性: Noun


词源:  From para- (meaning "beside," "auxiliary," or "similar to," but ultimately from Greek para "beside") + medical. Originally short for "paramedic personnel."
