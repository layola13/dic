intercloud:/ˈɪntərklaʊd/| n. |云间，云际; 云间连接; |A network connecting two or more distinct cloud infrastructures.|Example: The intercloud facilitates data transfer between private and public clouds. 云间连接促进了私有云和公共云之间的数据传输。

Example:  Intercloud security is a major concern for businesses. 云间安全是企业的主要关注点。|近义词：cloud federation, multi-cloud|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "single cloud" or "isolated cloud")|词性:noun
