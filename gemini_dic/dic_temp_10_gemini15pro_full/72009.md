flintlock: /ˈflɪntˌlɒk/ (UK) /ˈflɪntˌlɑːk/ (US)| n.|燧发枪|A type of gun fired by a spark from a flint striking steel.|The hunter used a flintlock to shoot the deer.|近义词:firelock, musket|反义词:modern firearm|词性:名词

Etymology:
From flint + lock (as in the mechanism that fastens something).  The "lock" refers to the firing mechanism of the gun.
