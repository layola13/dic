hysteron:/ˈhɪstərɒn/ (UK), /ˈhɪstəˌrɑːn/ (US)| n. |后置，后叙|A rhetorical device in which the natural or logical order of events, ideas, or phrases is reversed.  A later event or idea is presented as if it occurred earlier.|The hysteron in the sentence "He died and went to the hospital" creates a humorous effect.|近义词：inversion, antimetabole|反义词:  N/A (No direct antonym for a rhetorical device)|noun
