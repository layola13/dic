ephemeras: /ɪˈfɛmərəz/ | n. |蜉蝣; 短暂的事物;|Something short-lived or transient.|例句：The beauty of the cherry blossoms is an ephemera.  樱花的美丽是短暂的。

例句：These ephemeras of popular culture are quickly forgotten.  这些流行文化的短暂事物很快就被遗忘了。|近义词：transient, fleeting, temporary, short-lived|反义词：permanent, lasting, enduring|词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἐφήμερος (ephḗmeros, “lasting only a day”), from ἐπί (epí, “on, upon”) + ἡμέρα (hēméra, “day”).
