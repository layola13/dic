filing:/ˈfaɪlɪŋ/| n.|提交; 备案; 存档; 文件; 锉屑|The act of submitting a document to a court or other official body, the documents submitted, or the act of smoothing or shaping something with a file.|例句：The filing deadline for tax returns is April 15.  纳税申报的截止日期是4月15日。

例句：The lawyer presented the filing to the court.  律师向法院提交了文件。|近义词：submission, application, document, record|反义词:withdrawal, retrieval|词性:noun
