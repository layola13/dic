competitively:/kəmˈpɛtɪtɪvli/
adverb
竞争地；好竞争地；有竞争力地
In a competitive manner; in a way that strives to win or be superior.
They priced their products competitively to gain market share.  他们以具有竞争力的价格为其产品定价以获得市场份额。
The team played competitively throughout the tournament. 该队在整个锦标赛中都表现得很有竞争力。
Synonyms: aggressively, ambitiously, vyingly, emulously
Antonyms: cooperatively, collaboratively, passively
词性: 副词
