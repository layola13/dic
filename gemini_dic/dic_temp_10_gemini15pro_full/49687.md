demiurgeous:/ˌdɛmiˈɜːrdʒəs/
adjective
造物主般的; 创世神般的; 有创造力的
Having the power to create; resembling a demiurge.
例句：Her demiurgeous powers brought forth a vibrant and complex world within the novel.  她在小说中以其造物主般的能力创造出一个充满活力和复杂的世界。

例句：He approached the blank canvas with a demiurgeous spirit, ready to unleash his creativity. 他带着创世神般的精神走向空白的画布，准备释放他的创造力。

近义词：creative, inventive, innovative, generative
反义词：destructive, uncreative, barren

词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *dēmiourgos*  "craftsman, artisan," from *dēmios* "of the people" + *ergon* "work."
