meteorous:/ˌmiːtiˈɔːrəs/ /ˌmiːtiˈɔːrəs/ | adj. |  流星般的；昙花一现的；迅疾的 | Resembling a meteor in speed or brilliance; transiently brilliant or spectacular, like a meteor's brief but dazzling passage across the sky.  Characterized by rapid rise and fall, like a meteor. | His meteorous rise to fame was followed by an equally rapid fall from grace. 他像流星般迅速崛起，随后又以同样快的速度衰落。| Synonyms: fleeting, ephemeral, transient, evanescent, short-lived | Antonyms: enduring, lasting, permanent, perpetual | word property: adjective
