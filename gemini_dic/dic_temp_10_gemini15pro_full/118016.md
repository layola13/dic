These individuals often employ various techniques and strategies to improve their memory and retention abilities.|These memorizers can recite the entire poem flawlessly. 这些记忆者可以完美地背诵整首诗。|The team of memorizers astounded the audience with their ability to recall long strings of numbers.  这支记忆团队以其回忆长串数字的能力使观众震惊。|近义词：learners, students, rote learners|反义词:forgetters|词性:noun
