excommunicators:/ˌɛkskəˈmjuːnɪkeɪtərz/
|n.|逐出教会者；开除教籍者
|A person who excommunicates someone from a church or religious community.|
|The excommunicators declared the heretic no longer a member of the church.|那些逐出教会者宣布这个异教徒不再是教会成员了。

|The excommunicators were feared for their power to ostracize individuals.|这些开除教籍者因为他们排斥个人的权力而被人畏惧。
|Synonyms:  anathematizers, denouncers,  expellers|
|Antonyms: includers, welcomers, accepters|
|Noun|
