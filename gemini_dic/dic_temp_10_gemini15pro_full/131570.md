noncommunities:/ˌnɑːnkəˈmjuːnɪtiz/
|n.|非社区；缺乏共同感的人群|A group of people who lack interaction, shared values, or a sense of belonging, often characterized by transience, anonymity, and a lack of social cohesion.|The rise of online interactions has led to the formation of numerous noncommunities, where individuals connect superficially but lack genuine relationships.|Synonyms:  disconnected groups, fragmented populations|Antonyms: communities, societies
|noun|
