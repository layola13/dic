outvoicing:/ˌaʊtˈvɔɪsɪŋ/
|n.| 外部配音；外包配音 |The process of hiring an external voice actor or company to provide voiceovers for a project, such as a video, animation, or commercial. |Example: The company decided to use outvoicing for their new commercial to get a professional voice. 这家公司决定为他们的新广告使用外部配音以获得专业的配音效果。 |Example:  Outvoicing can be a cost-effective way to achieve high-quality audio. 外包配音可以是一种经济高效地获得高质量音频的方式。|Synonyms: outsourcing voiceover, external voiceover, third-party voiceover |Antonyms: in-house voiceover, internal voiceover|noun
