nonverbal:/ˌnɒnˈvɜːbəl/ /ˌnɑːnˈvɜːrbəl/| adj.|非语言的；不用语言的|Not involving or using words; relating to communication that does not involve words.|Her nonverbal communication skills are excellent; she can convey so much with just a look. 她非语言的沟通技巧非常出色；她只需一个眼神就能表达很多。|She gave me a nonverbal cue to be quiet. 她给了我一个不要说话的非语言暗示。|近义词：unspoken, wordless, silent|反义词：verbal, spoken|词性:形容词
nonverbal:/ˌnɒnˈvɜːbəl/ /ˌnɑːnˈvɜːrbəl/| n.|非语言交际；非语言表达|The process of communication through sending and receiving wordless cues between people.|Body language is a common form of nonverbal.  肢体语言是一种常见的非语言表达形式。|The psychologist studied the patient's nonverbals for clues about his emotional state. 心理学家研究了病人的非语言行为，以寻找有关他情绪状态的线索。|近义词：body language, gestures|反义词：verbal communication|词性:名词
