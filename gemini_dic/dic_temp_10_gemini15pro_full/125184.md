multicasting:/ˈmʌltɪˌkɑːstɪŋ/ | n. | 多播 | The transmission of a message to a group of recipients.  It's like sending a single email to multiple subscribers on a mailing list. |  The company uses multicasting to distribute software updates to its employees. 公司使用多播向其员工分发软件更新。

Multicasting is more efficient than sending individual messages to each recipient.  多播比向每个接收者单独发送消息更有效。 | 近义词: group communication, broadcasting (in some contexts) | 反义词: unicasting | 词性: 名词
