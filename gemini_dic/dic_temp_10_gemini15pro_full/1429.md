ace-one-sleeve:/eɪs-wʌn-sliːv/
adjective
仅用一只手就能轻松完成的; 轻而易举的
Something that is ace-one-sleeve can be done very easily, using only one hand.
He aced the exam one-sleeve. 他轻而易举地通过了考试。
The experienced chef chopped the vegetables ace-one-sleeve. 经验丰富的厨师单手轻松地切菜。
近义词:easy, simple, effortless, child's play, a piece of cake
反义词:difficult, challenging, arduous
词性:adjective
