fleetest:/ˈfliːtɪst/| adj.| 最快的，最迅速的 | Superlative form of "fleet," meaning fastest or most rapid. |  The fleetest horse won the race. 最快的马赢得了比赛。| synonyms: quickest, fastest, swiftest, rapidest | antonyms: slowest, tardiest | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *flēot* "swift, shallow," related to *fleotan* "to float, swim," from Proto-Germanic *fleutanan* "to float," from PIE root *pleu-* "to flow, to swim, to sail."  The meaning shifted from "swiftly flowing" (like water) to simply "swift."
