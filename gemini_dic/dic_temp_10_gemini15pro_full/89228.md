homicidal:/ˌhɒmɪˈsaɪd(ə)l/ (US) /ˌhɑːmɪˈsaɪdl/| adj.|杀人的；有杀人倾向的|Characterized by or having a tendency toward homicide; murderous.|例句：He had homicidal thoughts. 他有杀人的念头。|例句：The movie portrayed a homicidal maniac. 这部电影描绘了一个杀人狂。|近义词：murderous, killer, deadly|反义词：peaceful, harmless|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Late Latin homicida "manslayer," from Latin homo "man" + -cida "killer".
