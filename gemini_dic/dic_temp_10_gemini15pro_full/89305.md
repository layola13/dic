homoeothermal:/ˌhəʊmiːəˈθɜːməl/ |adj.|恒温的，温血的|Maintaining a stable internal body temperature regardless of the external environment.  Able to regulate body temperature internally.|例句：Birds and mammals are homoeothermal animals. 鸟类和哺乳动物是恒温动物。

例句：Homoeothermal animals can survive in a wider range of climates. 恒温动物可以在更广泛的气候范围内生存。|近义词：endothermic, warm-blooded|反义词：poikilothermic, cold-blooded|词性:形容词
homoiothermal:/ˌhəʊmiːəˈθɜːməl/ |adj.|恒温的，温血的|Maintaining a stable internal body temperature regardless of the external environment.  Able to regulate body temperature internally.|例句：Humans are homoiothermal and can maintain a core body temperature of around 37°C. 人类是恒温动物，可以保持核心体温在37摄氏度左右。

例句：The homoiothermal nature of mammals allows them to remain active in cold environments. 哺乳动物的恒温特性使它们能够在寒冷的环境中保持活跃。|近义词：endothermic, warm-blooded|反义词：poikilothermic, cold-blooded|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *homoio* (similar) + *thermē* (heat).  Relates to maintaining a similar, consistent body temperature.
