decliners:/dɪˈklaɪnərz/
n.
下降者；衰退者；拒绝者
People or things that decline or decrease in number, value, or strength.
The number of churchgoers has been among the steepest decliners in recent years. 近年来，去教堂的人数下降得最快。
Synonyms: losers, fallers, drops
Antonyms: gainers, risers, increasers
noun

decline:/dɪˈklaɪn/
v.
下降；衰退；拒绝
(1) To decrease in number, value, or strength.  (2) To politely refuse an invitation or offer.
(1) The company's profits have declined steadily over the past year.  这家公司的利润在过去一年里稳步下降。
(2) I had to decline his invitation to dinner because I had a prior engagement. 我不得不拒绝他共进晚餐的邀请，因为我之前有约了。
Synonyms: decrease, diminish, refuse
Antonyms: increase, rise, accept
