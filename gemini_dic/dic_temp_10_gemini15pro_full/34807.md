USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He climbs mountains every year. = 他每年都爬山。
She climbs the stairs quickly. = 她快速地爬楼梯。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
He is climbing the mountain now. = 他现在正在爬山。
She is climbing the ladder carefully. = 她正在小心地爬梯子。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
He has climbed Mount Everest. = 他已经攀登过珠穆朗玛峰。
She has climbed many trees in her life. = 她一生中爬过很多树。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
He has been climbing for hours. = 他已经爬了好几个小时了。
She has been climbing that wall all afternoon. = 她整个下午都在爬那堵墙。

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He climbed the tree yesterday. = 他昨天爬了树。
She climbed the mountain last year. = 她去年爬了那座山。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
He was climbing the mountain when it started to rain. = 开始下雨时，他正在爬山。
She was climbing the stairs when she heard a noise. = 她正在爬楼梯时听到一个声音。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
He had climbed the mountain before I arrived. = 在我到达之前，他已经爬上了山。
She had climbed the tree before her mother called her. = 在她妈妈叫她之前，她已经爬上了树。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

She had been climbing that rock face all day. = 她一整天都在爬那块岩壁。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):

词源: From Middle English *climben, *climen, from Old English climban (“to climb, mount, ascend”), from Proto-Germanic *climbaną (“to climb”), from Proto-Indo-European *ḱley- (“to lean, incline”). Cognate with West Frisian klimme (“to climb”), Dutch klimmen (“to climb”), German klimmen (“to climb”), Danish klatre (“to climb”), Swedish klättra (“to climb”).
