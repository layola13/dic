nonassault:/ˌnɒnəˈsɔːlt/ | adj. | 非攻击性的 | Not involving assault; not intended to cause physical harm. |  The police used nonassault weapons to subdue the suspect. 警察使用非攻击性武器制服了嫌疑人。|  The protesters engaged in nonassault resistance. 抗议者进行了非攻击性抵抗。 | 近义词: nonviolent, peaceful, harmless | 反义词:  aggressive, violent, offensive | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "assault" (noun meaning "a physical attack").  The word "assault" comes from Old French "assaut" from "assaillir" meaning "to leap upon, attack," from Latin "assilire" meaning "to leap at, jump upon," from "ad-" (to) + "salire" (to leap).
