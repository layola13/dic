massiveness:/ˈmæsɪvnəs/| n.|巨大；庞大；雄伟|The quality of being massive; enormous size, weight, or extent.|例句：The massiveness of the pyramids is awe-inspiring. 金字塔的雄伟令人敬畏。

例句：The sheer massiveness of the project intimidated the team.  项目的庞大规模吓到了团队。

近义词：immensity, vastness, hugeness, enormity|反义词:smallness, tininess, insignificance|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From massive + -ness.  Massive comes from Old French massif, from Vulgar Latin *massivus, from Latin massa "lump, kneaded dough, mass".
