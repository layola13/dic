hatchable:/ˈhætʃəbl/
adjective
可孵化的
Able to be hatched.
例句：The eggs are hatchable in about three weeks. 这些蛋大约三周后就能孵化。

例句：Only a small percentage of the eggs were hatchable. 只有少量蛋可以孵化。

近义词：incubatable
反义词：unhatchable
词性: adjective
