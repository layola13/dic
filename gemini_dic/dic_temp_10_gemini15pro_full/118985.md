methane:/ˈmɛθeɪn/ /ˈmiːθeɪn/| n. |甲烷|A colorless, odorless flammable gas which is the main constituent of natural gas. It is the simplest alkane.|例句：Methane is a potent greenhouse gas. 甲烷是一种强效的温室气体。

例句：The decomposition of organic matter produces methane. 有机物的分解会产生甲烷。|近义词：natural gas, marsh gas|反义词: None|词性:noun
