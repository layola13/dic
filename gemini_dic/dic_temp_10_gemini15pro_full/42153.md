couchant:/ˈkaʊtʃənt/| adj.|躺着的，伏卧的|Lying down, especially with the head raised, as a lion in heraldry.|The crest featured a lion couchant.  徽章上有一只躺着的狮子。|近义词：recumbent, prostrate, lying|反义词：rampant, standing, erect|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Old French *cochant (Modern French couchant), present participle of coucher "to lie down," from Vulgar Latin *colcare, from Latin col·lo·ca·re "to place, set, arrange," from com- "together" + locare "to place."
