overnourished: /ˌoʊvərˈnɜːrɪʃt/ | adj. | 营养过剩的；营养过度的 |  Supplied with too many nutrients or too much food; excessively nourished.  |  The child was clearly overnourished, with rolls of fat around his middle.  这个孩子明显营养过剩，肚子上有一圈圈的肥肉。

The overnourished cat had difficulty jumping. 这只营养过剩的猫跳起来很费劲。


|Synonyms: overfed, well-fed, plump, obese (in extreme cases)|Antonyms: undernourished, malnourished, starved| adjective
