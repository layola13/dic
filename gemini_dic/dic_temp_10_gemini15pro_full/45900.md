cutlases:/ˈkʌtləsɪz/
noun
弯刀（cutlass的复数）
plural of cutlass, a short, heavy, curved sword.
The pirates brandished their cutlases, ready to board the ship.  海盗们挥舞着弯刀，准备登上船。
The museum displayed a collection of antique cutlases. 博物馆展出了一系列古董弯刀。
synonyms:sabers, swords
antonyms:shields
