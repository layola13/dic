outsnatched:/aʊtˈsnætʃt/
verb
抢夺；突然夺走
To snatch something away from someone else, especially in a quick or unexpected way.
He outsnatched the ball from his opponent just before the goal line. 他在球门线前从对手手中抢走了球。
近义词: grab, seize, wrest, snatch
反义词: give, relinquish, surrender
词性: verb
