cliquism's:/ˈkliːkɪzəmz/ | n. | 小集团主义，派系主义 | The practice of forming small, exclusive groups, often with shared interests or goals, and showing favoritism towards members of the group while excluding or discriminating against those outside the group. |  Cliquism's negative effects were felt throughout the office, hindering collaboration and creating a hostile work environment. 小集团主义的负面影响遍及整个办公室，阻碍了合作，并创造了敌对的工作环境。 |  近义词: factionalism, sectarianism, exclusivity | 反义词: inclusivity, integration | 词性: 名词

Etymology:
From French *cliqueisme*, from clique + -isme. Ultimately from *klicken* "to click, latch."
