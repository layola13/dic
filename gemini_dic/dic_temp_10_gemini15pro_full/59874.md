ecumenism: /ɪˈkjuːmənɪzəm/ /ɛˈkjuːmənɪzəm/
| n. |基督教合一运动；普世教会主义|The principle or aim of promoting unity among the world's Christian churches. |  The conference focused on ecumenism and interfaith dialogue.  这次会议的重点是基督教合一运动和不同信仰间的对话。|  The Pope's visit was seen as a significant step towards ecumenism. 教皇的访问被视为朝着基督教合一迈出的重要一步。 | 近义词:  interfaith dialogue, religious unity, Christian unity | 反义词: sectarianism, denominationalism | 词性: 名词


词源: From Late Latin oecumenismus, from Ancient Greek οἰκουμένη (oikouméne, “the inhabited world”), from οἰκέω (oikéō, “I inhabit”).
