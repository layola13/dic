misdistributions:/ˌmɪsdɪstrɪˈbjuːʃənz/
n.
错误分配，分配不当
an act or instance of distributing wrongly or improperly.
The misdistribution of resources led to widespread poverty. 资源分配不均导致普遍贫困。
maldistribution, unequal distribution, disproportionate allocation
proper distribution, equitable distribution
noun
