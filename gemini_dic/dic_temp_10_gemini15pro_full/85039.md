例句：The harborless island offered little protection from the harsh weather. 这座没有港口的岛屿几乎无法抵御恶劣天气。|近义词：unsheltered, exposed, unprotected|反义词：sheltered, protected, harboring|词性:形容词

词源: harbor (港口) + -less (无，没有)
