editorializations:/ˌɛdɪˌtɔːriəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/
|n.|社论式评论；主观评论|The news report was filled with editorializations, making it hard to discern the facts. 这篇新闻报道充满了主观评论，难以分辨事实。|The frequent editorializations in his speeches detract from his credibility. 他演讲中频繁的主观评论降低了他的可信度。|近义词：commentaries, opinions, interpretations|反义词：facts, objectivity|词性:名词

editorialization:/ˌɛdɪˌtɔːriəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
|n.|社论化；发表社论|The editorialization of the news is a concerning trend.  新闻的社论化是一个令人担忧的趋势。|His editorialization on the topic sparked a heated debate. 他就该话题发表的社论引发了激烈的辩论。|近义词：commentary, opinion|反义词：neutrality|词性:名词

词源:  From editorialize + -ation.  Editorialize itself comes from editorial + -ize.  Ultimately derived from editor, from Latin ēditor "publisher," from ēdō "I give out, publish."
