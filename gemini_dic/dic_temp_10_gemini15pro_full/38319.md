concede:/kənˈsiːd/ (US), /kənˈsiːd/ (UK)| v. |承认；让步；认输|To admit that something is true or valid, often reluctantly; to surrender or yield (something).|例句：He conceded defeat after the election results were announced. 他在选举结果公布后承认失败。

例句：She conceded that she had made a mistake. 她承认自己犯了一个错误。

近义词：admit, acknowledge, grant, yield, surrender|反义词：deny, dispute, refuse, resist|词性:动词

concede:/kənˈsiːd/ (US), /kənˈsiːd/ (UK)| n. |让步；承认|An act of conceding or yielding.|例句：The concession he made surprised everyone. 他做出的让步让每个人都感到惊讶。

例句：Her concession of defeat was unexpected. 她承认失败出乎意料。

近义词：admission, acknowledgment, compromise|反义词：denial, refusal|词性:名词
