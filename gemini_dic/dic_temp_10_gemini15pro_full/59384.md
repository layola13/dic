earringed:/ˈɪə.rɪŋd/ | adj. | 戴着耳环的 | Adorned with earrings; having earrings. |  She wore an earringed and elegant look. 她戴着耳环，看起来很优雅。|  The earringed girl smiled shyly. 戴着耳环的女孩害羞地笑了。| 近义词:  bejeweled, adorned | 反义词: unembellished, unadorned | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From earring + -ed.  Earring itself comes from Old English *ēarhring, meaning "ear-ring," a compound of ēare "ear" and hring "ring."
