lite:/laɪt/| adj.|轻的，清淡的|Containing less of some ingredient (especially fat or alcohol) than a standard product.|例句：I ordered a lite beer. 我点了一杯淡啤酒。|近义词：light, reduced, low-fat, diet|反义词:regular, full, strong|词性:形容词

lite:/laɪt/| n.|清淡型产品|A product that contains less of some ingredient (especially fat or alcohol) than a standard product.|例句：This lite beer has fewer calories. 这种淡啤酒的卡路里更少。|近义词：light version, diet version|反义词:regular version, full version|词性:名词
