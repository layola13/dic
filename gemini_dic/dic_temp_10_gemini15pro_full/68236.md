fairways:/ˈfeə(r)weɪz/
n.
球道（高尔夫球）
The closely mown area between the tee and the green on a golf course.
He hit his drive straight down the fairway. 他一杆开球，球径直落在了球道上。

近义词:course, fairway
反义词:rough, hazard
词性:名词(plural)
