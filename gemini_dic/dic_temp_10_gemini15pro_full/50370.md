depopulations:/diːˌpɒpjuˈleɪʃənz/
n.
人口减少，人口稀少
The act or process of reducing the population of a place, especially by a large amount.
The depopulations caused by the war created ghost towns. 战争造成的人口减少造成了鬼城。

The Black Death caused widespread depopulations across Europe. 黑死病导致了欧洲各地人口的大量减少。

近义词:decline, decrease, reduction, emptying
反义词:population growth, increase, expansion

词性:noun


词源：
From de- + population,  ultimately from Latin populus "people".
