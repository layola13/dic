moonish:/ˈmuːnɪʃ/ | adj. | 多情的，梦幻的，愚蠢的 | Like the moon; dreamy, whimsical, or foolish. moonish:/ˈmuːnɪʃ/ | adj. | 月光下的 | Relating to or occurring in the moonlight. |  They took a moonish walk along the beach. 他们在月光下沿着海滩散步。 | synonyms: lunar | antonyms: solar | Word Type: Adjective
