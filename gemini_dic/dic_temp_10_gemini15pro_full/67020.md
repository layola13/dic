expiring:/ɪkˈspaɪərɪŋ/ | adj. | 到期的，即将期满的 |  Ceasing to be valid after a fixed period.  |  My driver's license is expiring next month. 我的驾照下个月到期。 | Synonyms: lapsing, terminating, ending, ceasing  | Antonyms:  renewing, extending, continuing, valid, permanent | adjective


expiring:/ɪkˈspaɪərɪŋ/ | n. | 期满，到期 | The ending of a fixed period of time. | The expiring of the lease forced them to move. 租约到期迫使他们搬家。 | Synonyms: termination, expiry, conclusion | Antonyms: renewal, extension, commencement | noun

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
The contract expires next week.  合同下周到期。
His passport expires in two years. 他的护照两年后到期。


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
My membership is expiring soon, so I need to renew it. 我的会员资格即将到期，所以我需要续订。
The coupon is expiring at midnight tonight. 优惠券今晚午夜到期。


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
The warranty has expired, so we can't get a free repair. 保修期已过，所以我们无法获得免费维修。
The offer has expired, unfortunately.  优惠期已经结束了，真遗憾。


现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
The milk has been expiring slowly in the fridge. 牛奶在冰箱里慢慢过期。 (This usage is less common.  It would be more natural to say "The milk has been slowly going bad/souring in the fridge.")


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
The lease expired last month. 租约上个月到期。
The coupon expired yesterday. 优惠券昨天到期。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
The contract was expiring when they finally reached an agreement.  当他们最终达成协议时，合同即将到期。
The offer was expiring while I was considering it. 当我还在考虑的时候，优惠就结束了。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
The visa had expired before he could apply for a new one.  他的签证在他可以申请新签证之前就过期了。
The offer had expired by the time I got to the store.  到我到商店的时候，优惠已经结束了。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
The food had been expiring in the pantry for months. 食品已经在储藏室里过期好几个月了。(This usage is less common.  It would be more natural to say "The food had been going bad/spoiling in the pantry for months.")


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
The contract will expire next year.  合同明年到期。
The license will expire soon. 执照很快就会到期。


将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
The agreement will be expiring next month. 协议下个月到期。 (This usage is less common. It's more natural to say "The agreement will expire next month.")


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next year, the contract will have expired. 到明年，合同将已到期。
By the end of the month, the offer will have expired. 到月底，优惠将已结束。


将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next July, the lease will have been expiring for six months.  到明年七月，租约将已经到期六个月了。(This usage is awkward and unnatural.  It's better to say "By next July, the lease will have been expired for six months," or  "By next July, it will have been six months since the lease expired.")
