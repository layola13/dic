durable:/ˈdʊrəbl/ /ˈdjʊrəbl/| adj.|持久的，耐用的|Able to resist wear, decay, or damage; lasting.|This furniture is made of durable materials.  这件家具是用耐用材料制成的。|例句：These shoes are very durable and have lasted me for years.  这双鞋很耐穿，我已经穿了好几年了。|近义词：long-lasting, lasting, enduring, strong, sturdy, tough|反义词：fragile, weak, flimsy, perishable, short-lived|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *durable*, from Old French *durable*, from Latin *dūrābilis* (“lasting, enduring”), from *dūrō* (“to harden, last, endure”).
