gild the lily:/ɡɪld ðə ˈlɪli/ | v. | 画蛇添足；多此一举 | To add unnecessary ornamentation or embellishment, making something already beautiful or perfect seem excessive or gaudy.  It implies trying to improve something that doesn't need improvement and often making it worse in the process. | The dress was already stunning, adding more sequins just gilded the lily. 这件衣服已经很漂亮了，再加亮片就画蛇添足了。 |  overdo, over-embellish, overdecorate | understate, simplify | verb
