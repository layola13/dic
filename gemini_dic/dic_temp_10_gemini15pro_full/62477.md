endosmosis:/ˌɛndoʊzˈmoʊsɪs/ /ˌɛndɒzˈməʊsɪs/
noun
内渗
The inward flow of a fluid through a semipermeable membrane toward a fluid of lower concentration, especially the inward passage of water into a cell or plant.
Water enters the roots of plants by endosmosis. 水通过内渗作用进入植物的根部。
近义词: osmosis, inward diffusion
反义词: exosmosis
