brattish:/ˈbrætɪʃ/| adj.|幼稚的，孩子气的，讨厌的|Behaving in a spoiled, unpleasant way, like a badly behaved child.|例句：He's always been a bit brattish. 他总是有点孩子气。|例句：She displayed brattish behavior by stomping her feet and demanding candy. 她跺着脚，要糖吃，表现得很孩子气。|近义词：childish, immature, spoilt, petulant|反义词：mature, well-behaved|词性:形容词
