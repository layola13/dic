ligate:/ˈlaɪɡeɪt/ /ˈlɪɡeɪt/| vt.|连接，捆绑，结扎|The surgeon will ligate the artery during the procedure. 外科医生将在手术过程中结扎动脉。|They ligated the ends of the severed rope. 他们把断绳的两端连接起来。|近义词：bind, join, fasten, connect|反义词：separate, disconnect|词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ligātiōn- (stem of ligātiō ), from ligātus (past participle of ligāre ‘to tie, bind’), from ligāmen ‘band, tie’ (from the same root as ligament ).
