bowed:/baʊd/
adjective
弯曲的，鞠躬的
curved or bent over
The old man walked with a bowed head.  这位老人低着头走路。
The branches of the tree were bowed down with snow. 树枝被雪压弯了。
synonym:bent, curved, stooped
antonym:straight, erect
词性:形容词

bowed:/baʊd/
verb
鞠躬，弯腰
to bend the head or body forward as a sign of respect or greeting
He bowed deeply to the queen. 他向女王深深地鞠躬。
The actors bowed after the performance.  演员们在演出结束后鞠躬致谢。
synonym:stoop, incline, curtsy
antonym:straighten
词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *būgan "to bend, bow, turn, dwell," from Proto-Germanic *buganą (source also of Old Norse bjúga "to bend, curve," Old High German biogan "to bend, curve," Gothic biugan "to bend").
