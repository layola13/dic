dressier:/ˈdresɪər/ | adj. |更衣着的，更正式的|More elegant or formal in style of dress than usual. |She wore a dressier outfit for the wedding. 她为婚礼穿了一套更正式的服装。|Example: It's a dressier affair, so you might want to wear a tie.  这是一个更正式的场合，所以你可能需要戴领带。|Synonyms: smarter, more formal, fancier|Antonyms: casual, informal, everyday|adjective

dressy:/ˈdresi/ | adj. |衣着的，正式的|Suitable for formal occasions; elegant or stylish. |She always looks very dressy. 她看起来总是很衣着光鲜。|Example: He wore a dressy suit to the opera. 他穿了一套正式的西装去歌剧院。|Synonyms: smart, stylish, chic|Antonyms: casual, informal, scruffy|adjective
