brassed:/bræst/
adjective
厚脸皮的，无耻的；醉醺醺的
Impudent, shameless; intoxicated.
He gave her a brassed stare. 他厚颜无耻地盯着她。
She stumbled in, brassed and boisterous. 她跌跌撞撞地走了进来，醉醺醺的，吵吵嚷嚷的。
近义词：brazen, impudent, shameless, drunk, intoxicated
反义词：shy, timid, modest, sober
词性：adjective

brassed:/bræst/
verb (past participle of *brass*)
用黄铜覆盖；给…装上黄铜配件；(非正式)给…钱；(非正式)厚着脸皮做…
Covered with brass; fitted with brass fittings; (informal) to give money to; (informal) to do something impudently.
The ornate box was brassed and polished.  这个华丽的盒子是用黄铜覆盖并抛光的。
He brassed his way into the exclusive club. 他厚着脸皮混进了那个高级俱乐部。
近义词：bronze, gild, finance, bribe
反义词：N/A
词性：verb (past participle)


词源：From the noun "brass" + "-ed" suffix, indicating the past participle or adjectival form.  The meaning of "impudent" or "shameless" likely derives from the perceived boldness or loudness associated with brass instruments.
