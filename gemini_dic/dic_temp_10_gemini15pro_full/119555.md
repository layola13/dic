microsthenic: /ˌmaɪkrəˈsθɛnɪk/
| adj. |体格瘦弱的，体型纤细的|Having a slender or thin body build; characterized by a small, delicate frame.|She has a microsthenic body type, which makes her look very graceful. 她体型纤细，看起来很优雅。|He's always been microsthenic, even as a child. 他从小就体格瘦弱。|近义词：asthenic, ectomorphic, slender, thin, slight|反义词：hypersthenic, pyknic, robust, stocky|词性: 形容词

Etymology:
From micro- (small) + -sthenic (relating to strength).  The -sthenic element comes from Greek *sthenos* "strength".
