osteopterygious: /ˌɒstiːɒpˈtɛrɪdʒəs/ /ˌɑːstiːoʊpˈtɛrɪdʒəs/
adjective
硬骨鱼类的
Relating to or characteristic of the Osteichthyes, a class of fish having bony skeletons.
The osteopterygious fish are the dominant group of vertebrates in most aquatic ecosystems.  硬骨鱼类是大多数水生生态系统中的主要脊椎动物群。

The scientist studied the osteopterygious features of the newly discovered species.  科学家研究了新发现物种的硬骨鱼类特征。

近义词: bony, teleost
反义词: cartilaginous (软骨的)
词性: adjective


Etymology:
From osteo- + Ancient Greek πτέρυξ (ptérux, “wing, fin”) + -ious.
