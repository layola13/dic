cue-up:/kjuː ˈʌp/ |v. |准备播放; 将（磁带、唱片等）调到播放位置; 安排; 组织|To prepare something for playing, especially a record or tape on a turntable or other device, or to arrange or organize something.|The DJ cued up the next song. DJ准备播放下一首歌。|He cued up the video on his laptop. 他在笔记本电脑上调好了视频。|近义词:prepare, set up, arrange, organize|反义词:disorganize|verb

cue-up:/kjuː ʌp/ |n. |(准备播放音乐或视频的)提示; (台球)开球|The act of preparing something for playing, or a signal to begin. |The band waited for their cue-up before starting to play. 乐队等待他们的提示才开始演奏。|He missed the cue-up and started playing too early. 他错过了提示，开始演奏得太早了。|近义词:signal, prompt, cue|反义词:N/A|noun
