dutiable:/ˈdjuːtiəbl/
adjective
应纳税的；应缴税的
Subject to import duty; taxable.
例句：Dutiable goods are subject to customs inspection. 应纳税的货物需要接受海关检查。

例句：He declared all his dutiable items at customs. 他在海关申报了所有应纳税的物品。

近义词：taxable, chargeable
反义词：duty-free, tax-exempt
词性：adjective
词源：From duty + -able.
