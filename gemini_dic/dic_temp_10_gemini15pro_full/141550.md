oppose:/əˈpoʊz/ (US) /əˈpəʊz/ (UK)| vt.|反对，抵抗，对抗|to disagree with and try to prevent something|例句：Many people oppose the construction of the new highway. 很多人反对修建这条新高速公路。|近义词：resist, object to, counter, fight, combat|反义词:support, advocate, endorse|verb

oppose:/əˈpoʊz/ (US) /əˈpəʊz/ (UK)| vt.|使对立，使对抗|to set someone or something in opposition to another|例句：The coach opposed his best player against their strongest competitor. 教练让他的最佳球员对抗他们最强的竞争对手。|近义词：pit against, match against|反义词:unite, ally|verb
