meding:/ˈmiːdɪŋ/ | v. | 修补，缝补 |  Mending refers to the act of repairing something, typically fabric or clothing, by sewing. |  She was mending a tear in her coat. 她正在缝补她外套上的一个破洞。 | Synonyms: repairing, patching, darning | Antonyms: tearing, ripping, breaking | Verb
