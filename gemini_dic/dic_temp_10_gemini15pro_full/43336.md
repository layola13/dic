crampet:/ˈkræmpɪt/| n.|烤饼; 薄煎饼|A thin, flat, savory cake with a soft, porous texture, cooked on a griddle and typically eaten toasted.|I enjoy a crampet with butter and jam for breakfast. 我喜欢早餐吃涂黄油和果酱的烤饼。|Synonyms: crumpet, English muffin, pikelet|Antonyms: N/A|noun
