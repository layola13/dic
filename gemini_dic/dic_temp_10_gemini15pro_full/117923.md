meltingnesses: /ˈmɛltɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
熔化性；熔化度（罕用）
The degree or quality of being melted or meltable.  This word is rarely used.
Due to the extreme heat, the meltingnesses of the various alloys were significantly different. 由于极端高温，各种合金的熔化度差异很大。
The meltingnesses of the ice cream in the hot sun was evident.  在烈日下，冰淇淋的熔化性很明显。
近义词：fusibility, meltability
反义词：infusibility, solidity
词性: 名词

词源:  From "melt" (Old English *meltan) + "-ing" (present participle suffix) + "-ness" (noun-forming suffix indicating a state or quality) + "-es" (plural suffix).
