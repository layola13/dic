fozy: /ˈfoʊzi/ | adj. | 模糊的，不清楚的 | resembling fuzz; downy or fluffy; indistinct or vague | The picture was fozy and out of focus.  照片模糊不清，焦点没对准。| 近义词：fuzzy, blurry, hazy, unclear, vague | 反义词：clear, distinct, sharp, precise | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Likely a variant spelling of "fuzzy," which originated in the late 19th century, possibly as an alteration of "fuzz" (fine down, light downy fibers), which in turn may be related to Low German "fusseln" (to fray).
