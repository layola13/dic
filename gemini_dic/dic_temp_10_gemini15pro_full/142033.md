organogram: /ˈɔːɡənəɡræm/ | n. | 组织结构图 | A diagram that shows the structure of an organization, especially a business, and the relationships between the different departments and jobs within it. | The company's organogram clearly shows who reports to whom.  这家公司的组织结构图清楚地显示了谁向谁汇报。 | The new CEO reorganized the company, and the new organogram reflects these changes. 新任首席执行官重组了公司，新的组织结构图反映了这些变化。 | synonyms: organizational chart, organization chart | antonyms: N/A | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *organo-* + *-gram*. *Organo-*  relates to organization and comes from Greek *όργανον* (organon), meaning "tool" or "instrument." *-gram* is a suffix meaning "something written or drawn," from Greek *γράμμα* (gramma), meaning "letter" or "writing."
