durables:/ˈdjʊrəblz/
n.
耐用品
consumer goods that are not destroyed by use, such as automobiles, furniture, or refrigerators
Consumers are reluctant to spend money on big-ticket durables like cars and appliances. 消费者不愿花钱购买汽车和家电等高价耐用品。
近义词:long-lasting goods, hard goods
反义词:consumables, perishables, non-durables
词性:noun (plural)


durable:/ˈdjʊrəbl/
adj.
耐用的，持久的
able to resist wear, decay, or other deterioration over a long period of time
These boots are made of a very durable material. 这些靴子是用非常耐用的材料制成的。
近义词:lasting, long-lasting, enduring, strong, sturdy, tough, resilient, resistant
反义词:fragile, perishable, short-lived, flimsy
词性:adjective


durability:/ˌdjʊrəˈbɪləti/
n.
耐久性，耐用性
the ability to resist wear, decay, or other deterioration over a long period of time
The durability of the new material is being tested. 新材料的耐久性正在接受测试。
近义词:strength, sturdiness, toughness, resilience, resistance
反义词:fragility, perishability
词性:noun
