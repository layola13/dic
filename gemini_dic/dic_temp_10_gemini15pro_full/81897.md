griffinish:/ˈɡrɪfɪnɪʃ/
adjective
狮鹫似的；似狮鹫的
Resembling or characteristic of a griffin; like a griffin.  A griffin is a mythical creature with the body of a lion and the head and wings of an eagle.
The statue had a griffinish appearance, with its eagle head and lion body.  这座雕像有着狮鹫般的外观，鹰头狮身。
The creature's griffinish claws tore at the rock.  这个生物狮鹫般的爪子撕扯着岩石。
近义词:  mythical, fantastical, legendary
反义词:  real, mundane, ordinary
词性:  adjective
