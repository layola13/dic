nucamentaceous:/ˌnjuːkəmənˈteɪʃəs/
adj.
坚果状的；像坚果的
Having the characteristics of a nucament; relating to, resembling, or consisting of a nucament.  A nucament is a dry fruit, such as a hazelnut or acorn, consisting of a hard shell enclosing a single seed.
The tree bears small, nucamentaceous fruits that are dispersed by the wind. 这棵树结着小的、坚果状的果实，它们靠风传播。
近义词:nut-like, nutty
