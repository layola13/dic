mosaism:/ˈmoʊzeɪˌɪzəm/| n. |镶嵌现象，嵌合体|The presence of two or more populations of cells with different genotypes in one individual, derived from a single fertilized egg.|This individual displays mosaicism, with some cells having a different chromosome count than others.| 近义词: chimerism|反义词: (None - it's a biological phenomenon)|词性:noun
