nanodiamonds:/ˈnænəʊˌdaɪməndz/| n. |纳米金刚石|Extremely small diamonds, typically with dimensions on the nanometer scale. They are formed through various processes, including detonation synthesis and high-pressure/high-temperature treatment.|Nanodiamonds are used in various applications, including drug delivery and bioimaging. 纳米金刚石用于各种应用，包括药物输送和生物成像。|近义词：nanocrystals, nanoparticles|反义词: (No direct antonym, but perhaps "bulk diamonds")|词性:noun
