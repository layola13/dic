curb:/kɜːrb/ (US) /kɜːb/ (UK)| n. vt.|路缘，抑制，控制|A curb is the raised edge of a sidewalk or street.  As a verb, it means to restrain or control something.|例句：The car parked close to the curb. 汽车停在靠近路缘的地方。|近义词：restrain, control, check, limit|反义词:release, encourage, promote|词性:noun, verb

curb:/kɜːrb/ (US) /kɜːb/ (UK)| n. vt.|路缘，抑制，控制|To curb something is to keep it within limits.  |例句：The government is trying to curb inflation. 政府正在努力控制通货膨胀。|近义词：restrain, control, check, limit|反义词:release, encourage, promote|词性:noun, verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *curbe, from Latin curvus (“curved, bent”).
