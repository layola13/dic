newspaperism:/ˈnjuːzˌpeɪpərɪzəm/ | n. | 报纸上的新闻报道风格; 报纸上的新闻报道工作; 报章杂志 |Newspaperism refers to the style and practices of writing and reporting news in newspapers. It often involves a focus on objectivity, factual accuracy, and concise writing.  It can also refer to the profession or business of newspaper publishing. |The prevalence of sensationalism in modern newspaperism is often criticized. 现代报纸新闻报道中耸人听闻的现象经常受到批评。| synonyms: journalism, reporting, press | antonyms:  None (It's difficult to have a direct antonym for a style of writing.) | noun
