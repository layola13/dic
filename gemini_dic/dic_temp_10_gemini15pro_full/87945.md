higgler:/ˈhɪɡlər/| n.|小贩；流动商贩；（尤指）在市场上或挨家挨户兜售农产品的小贩|A higgler is a person who travels around selling small items, especially food, door to door or at markets.|例句：The higgler sold fresh vegetables from his cart. 小贩从他的手推车上出售新鲜蔬菜。

例句：She bought some mangoes from a higgler at the market. 她在市场上从一个小贩那里买了一些芒果。|近义词：vendor, peddler, hawker, trader|反义词: customer, buyer|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->

From the obsolete verb *higgle*, meaning "to haggle" or "to bargain," which comes from the Middle English *higgen*, of uncertain origin.
