adult:/əˈdʌlt/ (US), /ˈædʌlt/ (UK)| n. adj.|成年人，成年的|A person who is fully grown or developed.  A mature animal or plant.|例句：Only adults can purchase alcohol. 只有成年人才能购买酒。  The film is suitable for adults only. 这部电影只适合成年人观看。|近义词：grown-up, mature|反义词:child, juvenile, minor|词性:名词, 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *adultus* (past participle of *adolescere* "to grow up"), from *ad- "to"* + *olescere "to grow."*

USAGE->
作为名词:
This movie is for adults only. = 这部电影仅限成人观看。
Adults are responsible for their own actions. = 成年人要对自己的行为负责。

作为形容词:
Adult education is important for lifelong learning. = 成人教育对终身学习很重要。
He has an adult son who lives overseas. = 他有一个住在海外的成年儿子。

PHRASE->
adult education = 成人教育
adult life = 成年生活
adult content = 成人内容
adult beverages = 含酒精饮料
young adult = 青年人
