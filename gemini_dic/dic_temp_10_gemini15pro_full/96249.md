indicatively:/ɪnˈdɪkətɪvli/| adv.|指示性地，象征性地，大约|  Meaning "in a way that serves to indicate" from indicate + -ive + -ly. It suggests approximation or representation rather than precise measurement or fact.|The price is £500, indicatively. 价格大约是500英镑。|Synonyms: approximately, roughly, around, about|Antonyms: precisely, exactly, accurately|Word Type: Adverb
