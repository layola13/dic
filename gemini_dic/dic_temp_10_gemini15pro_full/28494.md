card-carrying:/ˈkɑːrd ˌkæriɪŋ/| adj.|持有会员卡的，正式的（成员），坚定的（支持者）|A card-carrying member is someone who is officially registered as a member of a group, especially a political party, and usually carries a membership card. It can also be used figuratively to describe someone who strongly supports a particular cause or ideology.|例句：He's a card-carrying member of the Labour Party. 他是工党的正式党员。

例句：She's a card-carrying feminist. 她是一位坚定的女权主义者。|近义词：committed, dedicated, devout, staunch|反义词：uncommitted, lukewarm, apathetic|词性:形容词
