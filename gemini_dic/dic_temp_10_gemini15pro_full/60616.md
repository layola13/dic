elderlies:/ˈɛldərlɪz/
noun
老年人
A term referring to older people, often considered non-standard or informal.  It is generally better to use "elderly people" or "older adults."
The elderlies at the nursing home enjoyed the afternoon tea. (This usage is considered less acceptable.)  It is better to say: The elderly people at the nursing home enjoyed the afternoon tea.
near synonyms: seniors, older adults, the aged
opposite: youngsters, children, youth
