noway:/ˈnoʊˌweɪ/| interj. adv.|没门，不可能，不行|Expressing strong disagreement or refusal; absolutely not.  It's often used informally.|Example:  "Noway! I'm not going to jump out of a plane!" 没门！我不会从飞机上跳下去！|近义词：absolutely not, no way, by no means, not at all|反义词:definitely, certainly, absolutely, by all means|词性:interjection, adverb
