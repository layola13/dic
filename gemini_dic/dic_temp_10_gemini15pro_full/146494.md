overgarnished: /ˌoʊvərˈɡɑːrnɪʃt/ | adj. | 过度装饰的；装饰过多的 | Excessively decorated or adorned, especially with food garnishes.  Having too many garnishes. | The steak was perfectly cooked, but slightly overgarnished with parsley and fried onions.  牛排做得很好，但是欧芹和炸洋葱装饰得有点太多了。| The cake was overgarnished and looked cluttered.  蛋糕装饰过度，看起来很杂乱。 | 近义词: overdecorated, overdone | 反义词: undergarnished, plain | 词性: 形容词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "garnish" (verb meaning "to decorate or embellish").  "Garnish" comes from Old French "garnir" (to furnish, equip).
