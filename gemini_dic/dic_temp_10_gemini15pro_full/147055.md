overlearnedly:/ˌoʊvərˈlɜːrnɪdli/ | adv. | 过度学习地；学得过熟地 |  In a manner characterized by excessive or rote learning, often to the point of hindering true understanding or flexibility.  It implies learning something so thoroughly that it becomes automatic, but potentially at the expense of deeper comprehension or adaptability. | He recited the poem overlearnedly, without any real feeling for the words. 他机械地背诵了这首诗，对诗句没有任何真实的感受。| Synonyms: mechanically, rotely, rigidly, by rote | Antonyms: creatively, flexibly, adaptively |词性: 副词
