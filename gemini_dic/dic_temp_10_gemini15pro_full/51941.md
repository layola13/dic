ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *diabolicus, from Ancient Greek διαβολικός (diabolikós, “slanderous”), from διάβολος (diábolos, “slanderer, accuser, devil”).  Ultimately from διαβάλλω (diabállō, “I slander, accuse”).
