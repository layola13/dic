imposter:/ɪmˈpɑːstər/ (US) /ɪmˈpɒstə(r)/ (UK)| n.|冒名顶替者；骗子|A person who pretends to be someone else in order to deceive others.|He was exposed as an imposter, claiming to be a doctor when he had no medical training. 他被揭露是一个冒名顶替者，声称自己是医生，而实际上他根本没有受过医学训练。|
例句：
The imposter was eventually caught and sent to prison.  这个骗子最终被抓获并送进了监狱。

The imposter tricked everyone into believing he was a wealthy businessman.  这个冒名顶替者欺骗了所有人，让他们相信他是一个富有的商人。

近义词：fraud, charlatan, fake, pretender|
反义词：genuine person, authentic individual|
词性：noun

词源:
From Old French *impostor (modern French imposteur), from Latin impostorem (nominative impostor) "deceiver, cheat," from imponere "to impose upon, deceive," literally "to place upon," from in- "upon" + ponere "to put, place".
