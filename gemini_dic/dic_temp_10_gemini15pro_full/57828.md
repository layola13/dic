dress-uniforms: /ˈdrɛs ˌjuːnɪfɔːrmz/
noun
制服；礼服
Military personnel often wear dress-uniforms for formal occasions.  军人经常在正式场合穿礼服。
The band members looked sharp in their dress-uniforms.  乐队成员穿着制服看起来很精神。
Synonyms: full dress, formal uniform, ceremonial uniform
Antonyms: casual clothes, everyday clothes
词性: 名词

dress: /drɛs/
noun, verb
服装；连衣裙；打扮；穿着
She wore a beautiful dress to the party. 她穿了一件漂亮的连衣裙去参加聚会。
He helped her dress the wound. 他帮她包扎伤口。
Synonyms: (n.) gown, garment, attire; (v.) clothe, attire
Antonyms: (v.) undress
词性: 名词, 动词

uniform: /ˈjuːnɪfɔːrm/
noun, adjective
制服；统一的；一致的；始终如一的
The students all wear school uniforms. 学生们都穿着校服。
The quality of the products is uniform. 产品质量始终如一。
Synonyms: (n.) livery, costume; (adj.) consistent, regular, homogenous
Antonyms: (adj.) varied, diverse, irregular
词性: 名词, 形容词
