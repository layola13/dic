outflies:/ˌaʊtˈflaɪz/
verb
胜过；飞得比…远；飞得比…快
To fly faster or farther than.
The new plane outflies all others.  这架新飞机比所有其他飞机飞得都快。
The eagle outflies the sparrow.  鹰飞得比麻雀快。
Synonyms: surpass, exceed, outstrip, outdo
Antonyms: lag behind, trail
词性: 动词
