naphthalidine:/ˈnæfθəlɪˌdiːn/ | n. |萘啶|An aromatic amine derived from naphthalene, specifically 1-naphthylamine or 2-naphthylamine.  It is used in the production of dyes and other organic compounds.| Naphthalidine is an important intermediate in the synthesis of certain azo dyes. 萘啶是合成某些偶氮染料的重要中间体。

Naphthalidine derivatives exhibit interesting fluorescence properties. 萘啶衍生物表现出有趣的荧光特性。|Synonym: naphthylamine|Antonym: None (lack of a direct chemical antonym)|noun
