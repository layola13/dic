fossilizes:/ˈfɑːsəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
使…变成化石；使…僵化
to become a fossil; to make something become a fossil.
Over millions of years, the remains of the dinosaur fossilized. 数百万年来，恐龙的遗骸变成了化石。
The pressure of societal expectations can fossilize traditional gender roles. 社会期望的压力会使传统的性别角色僵化。
Synonyms: petrify, harden, solidify
Antonyms: liquefy, melt, dissolve
词性: 动词
