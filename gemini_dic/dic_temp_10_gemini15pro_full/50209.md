department:/dɪˈpɑːrtmənt/ /dəˈpɑːrtmənt/ | n. | 部门，系，科 | A specialized division of a large organization. |  The sales department is having a meeting. 销售部门正在开会。| division, section, branch | whole, entirety | noun

department:/dɪˈpɑːrtmənt/ /dəˈpɑːrtmənt/ | n. | (尤指法国的)省 | A major administrative district in France and some other countries.  |  The department of Loire-Atlantique is in western France.  卢瓦尔-大西洋省位于法国西部。 | province, region, district |  country | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *departement, from *departer "to divide," from Latin dis- "apart" + partire "to part, divide".
