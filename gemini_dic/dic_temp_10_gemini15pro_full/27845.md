canonicity:/ˌkænəˈnɪsəti/
n.
正典性；规范性；经典性
The quality of being canonical; the fact of being included in a list of sacred or important writings.
The canonicity of the Bible is a complex issue debated for centuries.  圣经的正典性是一个几个世纪以来争论不休的复杂问题。
The canonicity of Shakespeare's works is rarely questioned.  莎士比亚作品的经典地位很少受到质疑。
authenticity, validity, legitimacy, authority
spuriousness, illegitimacy, apocryphality
noun
