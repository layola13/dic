disproportionality: /ˌdɪsprəˌpɔːrʃəˈnæləti/ | n. | 不成比例，不相称 | A state in which things are not in proportion to one another, often referring to an imbalance or inequality. |  There is a disproportionality in the number of men and women in engineering. 工程师中男女数量不成比例。| disproportion, imbalance, inequality | proportionality, balance, equality | noun

disproportionate: /ˌdɪsprəˈpɔːrʃənət/ | adj. | 不成比例的，不相称的 | Too large or too small in comparison with something else. | The punishment was disproportionate to the crime. 惩罚与罪行不相称。| excessive, unequal, unbalanced | proportionate, balanced, equal | adjective
