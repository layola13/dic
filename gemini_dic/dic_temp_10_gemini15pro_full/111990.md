lonelier: /ˈloʊnliər/ (US) /ˈləʊnliər/ (UK) | adj. | 更孤独的，更寂寞的 | Comparative form of "lonely," meaning more lonely than someone or something else.  It describes a greater degree of loneliness. |  She felt lonelier than ever after her best friend moved away. 她最好的朋友搬走后，她感到比以往任何时候都更加孤独。

 He became lonelier as he grew older.  随着年龄的增长，他变得更加孤独了。| 近义词: more desolate, more isolated, more solitary | 反义词: less lonely, more sociable, more gregarious | 词性: 形容词


词源: From Old English `anlēp`  (alone, solitary) evolving through Middle English to "lonely" and then to the comparative form "lonelier."
