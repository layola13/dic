luncheon-meats:/ˈlʌntʃən miːts/| n.|午餐肉|Pre-cooked processed meats, typically made from pork, beef, or a combination, often served cold in sandwiches or as part of a cold buffet.  These meats are often cured, seasoned, and packaged for convenience.|例句：I made a sandwich with luncheon meats and cheese. 我用午餐肉和奶酪做了个三明治。

例句：Luncheon meats are a popular choice for quick lunches. 午餐肉是快捷午餐的热门选择。|近义词：cold cuts, deli meats, processed meats|反义词:fresh meat|词性:noun
