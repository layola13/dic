outspokenly:/ˌaʊtˈspoʊkənli/
adverb
坦率地，直言不讳地
In a frank and unreserved manner; candidly.
She outspokenly criticized the government's policies. 她直言不讳地批评了政府的政策。
synonyms: frankly, candidly, bluntly, directly, plainly
antonyms: reservedly, cautiously, discreetly, subtly, indirectly
词性: 副词
