foreshorten:/fɔːrˈʃɔːrtn/ /fɔːrˈʃɔːr.tən/| v. |缩短, 在透视画法中通过缩短线条来表现长度或距离|To portray or depict (an object or view) as closer than it is or as having less depth or distance, as by converging lines or dimishing size toward the background. |The artist foreshortened the outstretched arm in the painting. 艺术家在画中缩短了伸出的手臂。|近义词：abbreviate, condense, contract, curtail, reduce, shorten, truncate|反义词: lengthen, extend, elongate|词性: 动词

foreshortening:/fɔːrˈʃɔːrtnɪŋ/ /fɔːrˈʃɔːr.tən.ɪŋ/| n. |透视缩短法|The technique of depicting an object or human figure in a picture as shorter than its actual length, in order to create an illusion of receding into the distance or background.|The foreshortening in the painting made the figures look dynamic. 画中的透视缩短法使人物看起来充满活力。|近义词：perspective|反义词: 无|词性: 名词
