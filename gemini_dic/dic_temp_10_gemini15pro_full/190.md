abbas:/ˈæbəs/ /ˈæbɑːs/| n.|阿巴斯，阿拉伯男名|A male given name of Arabic origin, meaning "lion". It is derived from the Arabic word "أسد" (ʾasad), which means "lion".|Abbas Kiarostami was an acclaimed Iranian filmmaker. 阿巴斯·基亚罗斯塔米是一位著名的伊朗电影制作人。

The Abbasid Caliphate was a major Islamic empire. 阿巴斯王朝是一个主要的伊斯兰帝国。|近义词: (As a name, no direct synonyms, but other Arabic male names like: Ali, Omar, Hassan) |反义词: None|词性:noun
