fearfulest:/ˈfɪərfəlɪst/| adj.|最害怕的，极度恐惧的|Superlative form of fearful: most fearful.  This form is now considered archaic and "most fearful" is generally preferred.|例句：He was the fearfulest child I'd ever met. 他是我见过最胆小的孩子。|近义词：most fearful, terrified, petrified|反义词:fearless, brave, courageous|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *ferfulest, from Old English *fǣrfullost, superlative of fǣrfull (“terrible, dreadful, fraught with danger”). Equivalent to fear +‎ -ful +‎ -est.
