fowlerite:/ˈfaʊləˌraɪt/| n. |锰硅酸钙石|A pink to red mineral, a variety of rhodonite containing zinc.  It is a manganese silicate mineral, often used as a gemstone. |This fowlerite cabochon displays a beautiful swirl of pink and red. 这颗锰硅酸钙凸圆面宝石展现出美丽的粉红色和红色漩涡。|
Fowlerite is a relatively rare gemstone. 锰硅酸钙石是一种相对稀有的宝石。|近义词：rhodonite(蔷薇辉石)|反义词: 无|名词
