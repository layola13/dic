mutterings:/ˈmʌtərɪŋz/ (US), /ˈmʌtərɪŋz/ (UK)| n.| 喃喃自语；低声抱怨|The act of speaking softly and indistinctly, often complaining. | I could hear the mutterings of the crowd as the losing team left the field. 我能听到失败的队伍离场时人群的抱怨声。| synonyms: grumbling, murmuring, complaining, whispering | antonyms: shouting, yelling, proclaiming | noun
