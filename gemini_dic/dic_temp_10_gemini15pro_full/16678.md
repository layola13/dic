barrels:/ˈbærəlz/| n.|桶；枪管；炮管；大量；许多|A cylindrical container bulging out in the middle, traditionally made of wooden staves bound by hoops and having a flat top and bottom.|The winery stored the wine in oak barrels. 酒庄将葡萄酒储存在橡木桶中。|Near synonyms: casks, drums, kegs|Near antonyms:  (No direct antonym for the physical object, perhaps "containerless")|noun

barrels:/ˈbærəlz/| n.|大量；许多 (Informal)|A large quantity or number of something.|He has barrels of money. 他有很多钱。|Near synonyms: lots, tons, heaps|Near antonyms: little, few|noun
