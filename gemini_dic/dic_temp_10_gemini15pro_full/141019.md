ong:/ɒŋ/ /ɔŋ/ | suffix | 翁(用于姓氏)；(表示持续时间或重复动作的)长时间，不停地 |  A suffix used in surnames, often of Cantonese origin, or to indicate a prolonged duration or repeated action. |  Example: Mr. Wong is a renowned chef. 王先生是一位著名的厨师。| 近义词: (as a duration marker) long, continuously | 反义词: (as a duration marker) shortly, briefly | 词性: suffix


ong:/ɒŋ/ /ɔŋ/ |  onomatopoeia | 嗡嗡声；轰鸣声 | The sound of a continuous low humming or buzzing noise. | Example: The bees made an ong sound as they flew around the hive. 蜜蜂在蜂巢周围飞舞，发出嗡嗡的声音。 Example: The ong of the machinery filled the factory.  机器的轰鸣声充满了工厂。 | 近义词: buzz, hum, drone | 反义词: silence, quiet | 词性: onomatopoeia
