paralian:/pəˈreɪliən/ | adj. n.| 海岸的；海岸居民 |Relating to or inhabiting the shore of a sea or lake.  A person who lives near the sea. | The paralial communities are particularly vulnerable to rising sea levels.  海岸社区特别容易受到海平面上升的影响。| The paralial enjoyed the fresh sea air every morning.  这位海岸居民每天早晨都享受着新鲜的海风。 |近义词：coastal, littoral, seaside, shore |反义词：inland |词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Greek *parálios* (παράλιος) "by the sea," from *pará* (παρά) "beside" + *háls* (ἅλς) "sea, salt."
