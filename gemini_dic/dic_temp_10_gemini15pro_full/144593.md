outwear: /ˈaʊtwɛːr/ /ˈaʊtwɛr/
noun
外衣，外套
Clothing designed to be worn outdoors, over other garments, especially for protection from the elements.
She packed a variety of outwear for her hiking trip, including a waterproof jacket and a fleece vest. 她为她的徒步旅行打包了各种外套，包括一件防水夹克和一件羊毛背心。
近义词: outerwear, coat, jacket
反义词: underwear, innerwear
词性: n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From out + wear (v.).  The noun form dates back to the late 19th century.  "Out-" indicates intended for outside or outdoor use, and "wear" refers to clothing.
