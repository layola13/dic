birder:/ˈbɜːrdər/ (US), /ˈbɜːdə/ (UK)| n.|观鸟者；爱鸟人|A person who engages in the activity of observing and identifying birds, often as a hobby.|例句：The birder spotted a rare warbler in the bushes.  观鸟者在灌木丛中发现了一只稀有的莺。

例句：He's a keen birder and travels extensively to see different species. 他是一位热衷的观鸟者，经常旅行去看不同的鸟类。|近义词：birdwatcher, ornithologist (more formal/scientific)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
