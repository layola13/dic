ovi:/ˈoʊviː/ /ˈɒviː/
adjective|卵生的|Relating to or characterized by the production of eggs that hatch outside the body.|The platypus, an oviparous mammal, lays eggs.|近义词:ovigerous|反义词:viviparous|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ōvi-* (combining form of *ōvum* "egg"), from Proto-Italic *ōwom*, from Proto-Indo-European *ōwyóm* "egg".
