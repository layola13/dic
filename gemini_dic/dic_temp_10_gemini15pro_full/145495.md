overclogging:/ˌoʊvərˈklɒɡɪŋ/ | n. | 过度堵塞，过度阻塞 | The act of clogging something excessively, usually a drain or filter.  This implies a negative consequence due to the excessive blockage. | The overclogging of the kitchen sink caused the water to back up. 厨房水槽过度堵塞导致水倒流。 |The overclogging of the air filter reduced the engine's efficiency. 空气滤清器过度堵塞降低了发动机的效率。|近义词：blocking, obstructing, choking |反义词: clearing, unblocking | 词性:名词

词源: "Over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "clog" (verb meaning "to block or become blocked")

Over- 来自古英语 ofer-，意为“在…之上，超越”。
Clog 来自中古英语 clogge，可能源自一个表示“块状物”的词根。
