mischaracterization:/ˌmɪskærɪktərəˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 误传；歪曲；错误描述 | An act of misrepresenting the character of someone or something.  |  The politician claimed the report was a gross mischaracterization of his views.  这位政治家声称该报告严重歪曲了他的观点。 | synonym: misrepresentation, distortion, falsification | antonym: accurate portrayal, faithful representation | noun

mischaracterize:/ˌmɪskærɪktəˈraɪz/ | v. | 歪曲；误传；错误描述 | To represent inaccurately or unfairly. | The media mischaracterized her as a radical. 媒体把她错误地描述成一个激进分子。| synonym: misrepresent, distort, falsify | antonym: portray accurately, represent faithfully | verb
