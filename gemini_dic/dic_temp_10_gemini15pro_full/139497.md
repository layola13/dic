obstructionism:/əbˈstrʌkʃənɪzəm/
n.
阻挠主义；故意阻挠
The deliberate action of hindering or delaying progress, especially in legislation.
The senator was accused of obstructionism for filibustering the bill.  这位参议员因阻挠该法案而被指控为阻挠主义。
His obstructionism prevented the project from moving forward. 他的阻挠使得项目无法推进。
near synonyms: delaying tactics, stonewalling, foot-dragging, filibustering
antonyms: cooperation, facilitation, support, assistance
noun
