gelded:/ˈɡeldɪd/| adj.|阉割的；失去活力的|Deprived of vitality, strength, or vigor; castrated.  Originally referring to the castration of male horses, but now can be used metaphorically.|The king's gelded power left him vulnerable to his enemies. 国王的权力被阉割，使他容易受到敌人的攻击。|近义词：castrated, emasculated, weakened, impotent|反义词：potent, virile, strong|词性:形容词

gelded:/ˈɡeldɪd/| v. (past participle)|阉割；使无力|Past participle of "geld."  To castrate (a male animal).  Also used figuratively to describe weakening or depriving of strength or vigor.|The farmer gelded the young stallion. 农民阉割了这匹年轻的种马。|近义词：castrated, emasculated|反义词：empower, strengthen|词性:动词
