hackneyed:/ˈhæk.nid/ | adj. | 陈腐的；老套的；缺乏创新的 |  (of a phrase, idea, etc.) used so often that it has become boring and has no meaning | The plot of the movie was hackneyed and predictable.  这部电影的情节老套且可预测。 | synonyms: banal, clichéd, commonplace, overused, stale, tired, trite, unoriginal | antonyms: fresh, innovative, new, original, novel | adjective
