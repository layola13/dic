disenroll:/ˌdɪsɪnˈroʊl/| v. |取消注册，登记；退出|To officially remove someone's name from the list of members of a course, college, or group.|He disenrolled from the online course because it was too difficult.|近义词：unregister, withdraw, opt out|反义词:enroll, register, sign up|词性:动词
词源:dis- (not) + enroll (to register)
