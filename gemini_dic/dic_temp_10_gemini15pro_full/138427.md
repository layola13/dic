nourishable:/ˈnʌrɪʃəbl/| adj.|营养的，滋养的|Providing nourishment; able to sustain life and promote growth.|例句：A nourishing diet is essential for good health.  营养丰富的饮食对健康至关重要。|例句：The soil is rich and nourishable, perfect for growing vegetables. 这片土壤肥沃且营养丰富，非常适合种植蔬菜。|近义词：nutritious, wholesome, healthful|反义词：unhealthy, unwholesome, innutritious|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *norisshable*, from Old French *norrissable*, from *norrir* (“to nourish”), from Latin *nutrire* (“to nourish, suckle”), from *nutrix* (“nurse”).
