circumnavigation:/ˌsɜːrkəmˌnævɪˈɡeɪʃn/ |n.|环球航行，环游世界|The act of sailing completely around the world.|Magellan's circumnavigation of the globe proved that the Earth was round. 麦哲伦的环球航行证明了地球是圆的。|近义词：round-the-world voyage, global voyage|反义词:|名词

circumnavigate:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈnævɪɡeɪt/ |v.|环航，环游|To sail or travel completely around.|They circumnavigated the globe in a small yacht. 他们驾驶一艘小型游艇环游世界。|近义词：sail around, travel around|反义词:|动词
