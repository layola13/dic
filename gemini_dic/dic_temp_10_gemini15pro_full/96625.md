indulge:/ɪnˈdʌldʒ/| v.|放纵；沉溺；满足；纵容|to allow oneself or another person to have something enjoyable, especially more than is good for them|例句：I indulged myself with a long hot bath. 我让自己享受了一个长时间的热水澡。|近义词：pamper, gratify, spoil, treat|反义词：abstain, refrain, restrict|verb

indulge:/ɪnˈdʌldʒ/| n.|沉溺；放纵|an act of indulging; something indulged in|例句：Chocolate is my only indulgence.  巧克力是我唯一的放纵。|近义词：treat, luxury, pleasure|反义词：abstention, restraint|noun
