barm:/bɑːm/| n.|酵母，泡沫，(尤指啤酒上的) 泡沫|The barm on the beer looked creamy and inviting.  啤酒上的泡沫看起来很creamy，很诱人。|Synonym: yeast, froth, foam, head (on beer)|Antonym: flat (beer)|noun


barm:/bɑːm/| n.|（方言）温暖，舒适|The fire gave off a pleasant barm. 火散发着令人愉悦的温暖。|Synonym: warmth, coziness|Antonym: cold, chill|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *beorma (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *bermō. Cognate with Dutch barm ("yeast"), German Bärme ("yeast, dregs").
