diphthongization:/dɪfθɒŋɡaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /dɪfθɔŋɡaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n.|双元音化；元音音变|The diphthongization of vowels is a common phenomenon in many languages. 元音的双元音化在许多语言中是一种常见现象。|The Great Vowel Shift involved significant diphthongization. 大元音音变包含了重要的双元音化。|近义词: vowel change, umlaut, monophthongization (somewhat)|反义词: monophthongization|noun

diphthongize:/dɪfˈθɒŋɡaɪz/ /dɪfˈθɔŋɡaɪz/| v.|使成双元音；双元音化|The vowel "i" diphthongizes to "ai" in certain dialects. 在某些方言中，元音“i”双元音化为“ai”。|The pronunciation of "house" has diphthongized over time.  "house" 的发音随着时间的推移已经双元音化了。|近义词: change, alter, modify (in relation to vowels)|反义词: monophthongize|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From diphthong + -ization.  Diphthong comes from Greek *diphthongos* meaning "having two sounds," from *di- "two" + *phthongos* "sound."
