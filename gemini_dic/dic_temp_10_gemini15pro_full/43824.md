creolizations:/ˌkriːəlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. |克里奥尔化；混合语化|The process by which a pidgin becomes the native language of a speech community. Also, the language that develops from this process.  It involves the blending of two or more languages, often resulting in a new language with its own unique grammar and vocabulary.|例句：The creolizations of English in the Caribbean have resulted in a fascinating diversity of languages. 加勒比地区的英语克里奥尔化导致了语言的迷人多样性。

例句：Researchers are studying the creolizations of various African languages in the Americas. 研究人员正在研究美洲各种非洲语言的克里奥尔化。|近义词：hybridization, amalgamation, blending, fusion|反义词：purification, separation|词性: noun
