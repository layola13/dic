friended:/ˈfrɛndɪd/
verb
加为好友；与…交朋友
past tense and past participle of "friend," meaning to add someone to a list of friends on a social networking site.
She friended me on Facebook last week. 她上周在脸书上加了我为好友。
He has friended several of his colleagues on LinkedIn. 他在领英上加了几个同事为好友。
Synonyms: added, connected with, befriended
Antonyms: unfriended, blocked, defriended
词性: 动词
