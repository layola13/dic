chrysophanic:/krɪˈsɒfənɪk/ | adj. | 大黄酸的；含大黄酸的 | Relating to or containing chrysophanic acid, a yellow crystalline substance found in rhubarb and other plants. |  Chrysophanic acid is used in some skin treatments. 大黄酸用于一些皮肤治疗。|  Rhubarb contains chrysophanic acid, which can stain fabrics yellow. 大黄含有大黄酸，会使织物染成黄色。 | Synonyms:  aperient, cathartic, laxative | Antonyms:  constipating | adjective
