overabused: /ˌoʊvərəˈbyuːzd/ | adj. | 过度使用的，滥用的 | Excessively used or misused, often to the point of losing its original meaning or effectiveness. | The word "awesome" is so overabused these days that it hardly means anything anymore.  “棒极了”这个词现在被滥用得太多了，几乎没有任何意义了。 | The term "literally" is often overabused in casual conversation.  “字面意思”这个词在日常对话中经常被滥用。 | 近义词: overused, misused, exploited | 反义词: underused, rare | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
over- (prefix): from Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *ubar "over, above, beyond"
abuse (verb): from Middle French abuser, from Latin abuti "to misuse, abuse," from ab- "away" + uti "to use"
-ed (suffix): forms the past participle of regular verbs
