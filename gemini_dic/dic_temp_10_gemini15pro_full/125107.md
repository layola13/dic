muls:/mʌlz/ | n. | 骡子 | Hybrid offspring of a male donkey and a female horse, typically sterile. | Example: The farmer used muls to carry heavy loads across the mountainous terrain.  农民用骡子驮重物穿过山区。| Synonym: hinny (when the father is a horse and the mother a donkey) | Antonym: horse, donkey | noun
