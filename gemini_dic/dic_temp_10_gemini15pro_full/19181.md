bewilder:/bɪˈwɪldər/| vt.|使迷惑，使不知所措|To confuse or puzzle someone greatly; to cause someone to lose their bearings.|The complex instructions bewildered her. 复杂的指令把她搞糊涂了。|近义词：confuse, puzzle, perplex, disorient|反义词：clarify, enlighten, orient|词性:动词
Etymology: From be- + wilder (to lead astray, from wild + -er).  The prefix "be-" intensifies the meaning of "wilder".
