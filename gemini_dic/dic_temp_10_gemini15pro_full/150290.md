panelled:/ˈpænəld/ (US) /ˈpænəld/ (UK)| adj.|镶板的；方格的|Having or decorated with panels (flat, usually rectangular pieces forming a distinct section of a wall, ceiling, door, etc.).|The room was panelled in dark oak. 房间镶嵌着深色橡木板。|The library had beautifully panelled walls. 图书馆的墙面装饰着漂亮的嵌板。|近义词:paneled, wainscoted, boarded|反义词:unpanelled|词性:形容词
