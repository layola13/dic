clearheaded:/ˌklɪrˈhɛdɪd/| adj.|头脑清醒的，冷静的|Having a clear mind; able to think clearly and rationally, especially in a difficult situation.|例句：She remained remarkably clearheaded throughout the crisis.  她在整个危机期间都保持着清醒的头脑。

例句：He made a clearheaded decision to sell the company. 他做出了一个清醒的决定，要卖掉公司。|近义词：lucid, rational, level-headed, composed, calm|反义词：confused, muddled, bewildered, disoriented, addled|词性:形容词
