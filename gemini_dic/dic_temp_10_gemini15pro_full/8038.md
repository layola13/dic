anthropocentricities:/ˌænθrəpəʊˈsɛntrɪsɪtiz/
n.
以人类为中心；人类中心主义
the belief that human beings are the most important entity in the universe.  The excessive focus on human values, needs, and perspectives, often at the expense of other species or the environment.
例句：The anthropocentricities of our culture are increasingly being challenged.  我们文化的以人类为中心的特点正日益受到挑战。

例句：The project's anthropocentricities blinded the team to the ecological consequences. 该项目的人类中心主义使团队对生态后果视而不见。


近义词：anthropocentrism, human-centeredness
反义词：biocentrism, ecocentrism
词性：noun (plural)
