margaritiferous:/ˌmɑːrɡərɪˈtɪfərəs/| adj.|产珍珠的；似珍珠的|Producing pearls; pearly.  Resembling a pearl or pearls.|例句：The margaritiferous oyster is highly valued for its pearls. 产珍珠的牡蛎因其珍珠而备受珍视。|例句：The inside of the shell had a margaritiferous sheen. 贝壳的内部有一种珍珠般的光泽。|近义词：pearly, nacreous|反义词:dull, matte|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Latin *margarita* (pearl) + *-fer* (bearing, producing)
