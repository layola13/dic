overruns:/ˌoʊvərˈrʌnz/ | n. | 超支，超出限度 | An overrun is an amount by which something exceeds a limit, especially a limit on the cost of a project or the amount of time it is supposed to take. | The project overran its budget by 20%.  这个项目超出了预算20%。

overruns:/ˌoʊvərˈrʌnz/ | v. | 超过，泛滥，蔓延 |  If something overruns a place, it spreads over it quickly and in large numbers. |  The garden was overrun with weeds. 花园里杂草丛生。


overrun:/ˌoʊvərˈrʌn/ | n. | 超支，超出限度 | The overrun on the project was significant. 项目的超支很严重。| 近义词: excess, surplus, overflow | 反义词: shortfall, deficit | 词性: 名词

overrun:/ˌoʊvərˈrʌn/ | v. | 超过，泛滥，蔓延 |  The river overran its banks. 河水泛滥成灾。| 近义词:  infest, overwhelm, exceed | 反义词:  underperform, fall short of | 词性: 动词
