deafening:/ˈdefnɪŋ/ (US), /ˈdef(ə)nɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|震耳欲聋的；极喧闹的|Making a loud and overwhelming noise that is difficult or impossible to hear over.  It can also be used figuratively to describe something overwhelming or impactful.|The deafening roar of the jet engine made conversation impossible. 喷气发动机的巨大轰鸣声使交谈成为不可能。|Synonyms: loud, earsplitting, thunderous, overpowering, overwhelming|Antonyms: quiet, silent, hushed, faint, subtle|adjective


deafenings:/ˈdefnɪŋz/ (US), /ˈdef(ə)nɪŋz/ (UK)| n.|震耳欲聋的声音（deafening 的名词复数形式，较少使用）|The plural form of "deafening," referring to multiple instances of extremely loud noises.|The constant deafenings from the construction site made it hard to concentrate.  建筑工地不断传来的巨大噪音使人难以集中注意力。|Synonyms: roars, blasts, clamors, din|Antonyms: silences, hushes, whispers|noun
