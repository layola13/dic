obligingness:/əˈblaɪdʒɪŋnɪs/| n. |乐于助人；殷勤；亲切|The state or quality of being obliging; willingness to do favors or help others. |She showed great obligingness in helping me move. 她非常乐于助人，帮我搬家。|Synonyms: kindness, helpfulness, courtesy, amiability, generosity |Antonyms: unkindness, unhelpfulness, rudeness, hostility, selfishness|noun

obliging:/əˈblaɪdʒɪŋ/| adj. |乐于助人的；殷勤的；亲切的|Willing to do favors or help others. |He was very obliging and offered to carry my bags. 他非常乐于助人，主动提出帮我拿行李。|Synonyms: helpful, kind, courteous, accommodating, amiable|Antonyms: unhelpful, unkind, rude, unaccommodating, disagreeable|adjective
