credulousness:/krɪˈdʒuːləsnəs/| n. |轻信，易受骗|A tendency to believe things too readily; gullibility.|His credulousness made him an easy target for con artists. 他太容易轻信别人，这让他很容易成为骗子的目标。|Synonyms: gullibility, naivety, ingenuousness|Antonyms: skepticism, cynicism, distrust|noun

credulous:/ˈkrɛdʒələs/| adj. |轻信的，易受骗的|Having or showing too great a readiness to believe things.|She was so credulous that she believed his every word. 她太容易轻信别人，以至于她相信他说的每一句话。|Synonyms: gullible, naive, trusting|Antonyms: skeptical, cynical, suspicious|adjective
