gravimetric:/ˌɡrævɪˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. | 重量的；重量分析的 | Relating to or determined by weight or mass.  It often refers to a method of quantitative chemical analysis by weighing a precipitate. |  The gravimetric analysis showed a high concentration of iron in the sample. 重量分析表明样品中铁的浓度很高。|  The chemist used a gravimetric method to determine the amount of sulfate.  化学家使用重量分析法测定硫酸盐的含量。 | synonyms:  ponderal | antonyms: volumetric | word type: adjective
