noncongregative:/ˌnɒnkɒŋˈɡrɛɡətɪv/
adjective
非聚集的，不群居的
Not congregating; not coming together in a group or crowd.
The hermit led a noncongregative life, preferring solitude to society.  这个隐士过着非群居的生活，喜欢独处胜过社交。
Noncongregative species of birds tend to forage alone. 非群居的鸟类倾向于单独觅食。
Synonyms: solitary, isolated, dispersed, scattered
Antonyms: gregarious, social, congregative, clustered
adjective
