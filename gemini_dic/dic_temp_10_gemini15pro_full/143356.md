outfaced: /ˌaʊtˈfeɪst/
verb
战胜；使丢脸；使无言以对；厚着脸皮对待
to stare down or intimidate someone until they give in or look away
He outfaced his accusers and maintained his innocence. 他直面原告，坚称自己无罪。
近义词: intimidate, defy, browbeat
反义词: submit, yield
词性: v.

outfaced: /ˌaʊtˈfeɪst/
adjective
厚颜无耻的 (rare)
Having a bold and shameless face; impudent. (This usage is less common.)
He had an outfaced manner that was off-putting.  他厚颜无耻的态度令人反感。
近义词: brazen, impudent
反义词: timid, bashful
词性: adj.
