ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *abisten, from Anglo-Norman abister, from Old French abester, from a- (intensive prefix) + beter (to bait, to harass), from Vulgar Latin *battuere, frequentative of Latin battuō (I beat).  The meaning shifted from "to harass" to "to urge on" and then to "to help."
