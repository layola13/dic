infographics:/ˌɪnfəˈɡræfɪks/
n.
信息图表；信息图
a visual representation of information or data, e.g. as a chart or diagram
The infographics clearly showed the growth in sales over the last year. 信息图清楚地显示了去年销售额的增长。

infographics:/ˌɪnfəˈɡræfɪks/
noun
信息图表
a visual image such as a chart or diagram used to represent information or data
The website uses infographics to explain complex data in an easy-to-understand way.  该网站使用信息图表以易于理解的方式解释复杂的数据。

Etymology->
From information + graphics.  由 information（信息）和 graphics（图形）组合而成。
