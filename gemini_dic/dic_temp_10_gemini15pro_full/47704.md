debuted:/deɪˈbjuːtd/ /dɪˈbjuːtɪd/| v. |首次亮相，初次登场|The band debuted their new album last night.  这支乐队昨晚首次推出了他们的新专辑。|例句：The actress debuted on Broadway in 1998.  这位女演员于1998年在百老汇首次亮相。|近义词：premiered, launched, introduced|反义词:retired, withdrew|词性:动词

词源: From French débuter, from début 'beginning,' from de- 'from' + but 'end, aim.'
