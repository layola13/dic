in-chorus:/ɪn ˈkɔːrəs/ | adv., adj. |齐声地，合唱地；齐声的，合唱的 |Singing or speaking all together at the same time.  Derived from "in" meaning "within" and "chorus" meaning a group of singers or speakers. |The audience sang "Happy Birthday" in chorus.  观众齐声唱了“生日快乐”。 |The birds sang in chorus at dawn. 鸟儿们在黎明时分齐声歌唱。 | synonym:  unison, simultaneously, together | antonym: separately, individually | adverb, adjective
