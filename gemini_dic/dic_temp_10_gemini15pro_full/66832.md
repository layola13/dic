expatiating:/ɪkˈspeɪʃieɪtɪŋ/
v.
详述；细说；详细解释
speaking or writing at length or in detail
The professor was expatiating on the history of the Roman Empire. 教授正在详细阐述罗马帝国的历史。
expounding, elaborating, enlarging
concise, succinct
verb
