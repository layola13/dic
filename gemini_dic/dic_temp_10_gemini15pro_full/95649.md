incompliance:/ˌɪnkəmˈplaɪəns/
noun
不顺从，违规，不合规
The state of not being in compliance with rules, regulations, or standards.
The company was found to be in incompliance with environmental regulations.  该公司被发现不符合环境法规。
incompliance with building codes.  不符合建筑规范。
noncompliance, violation, infringement, breach
compliance, conformity, obedience
n.
