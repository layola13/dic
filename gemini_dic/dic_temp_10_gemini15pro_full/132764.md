nondoubtingly:/nɒnˈdaʊtɪŋli/ | adv. | 毋庸置疑地，毫无疑问地 | Without any doubt; certainly. | He nondoubtingly accepted the challenge. 他毫不犹豫地接受了挑战。 |  She nondoubtingly believed his story. 她毫无疑问地相信了他的故事。 | 近义词: undoubtedly, certainly, definitely, unquestionably | 反义词: doubtfully, questionably | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From non- (not) + doubt + -ing + -ly.  "Doubt" comes from Old French "douter" (to doubt), ultimately from Latin "dubitare" (to hesitate, waver).
