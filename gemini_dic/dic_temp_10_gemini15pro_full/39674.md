conserver:/kənˈsɜːrvər/ | n. | 保护者，保存者；节约者 | A person who conserves something, especially natural resources. |  Example: She is a dedicated conserver of the rainforest. 她是一位致力于保护雨林的人。


Example:  He is a conserver of antique books. 他是一位古籍收藏者。 | 近义词: preserver, protector, guardian, conservator | 反义词: waster, squanderer, destroyer | 词性: noun

conserve: /kənˈsɜːrv/ | v. | 保存；保护；节省 | To protect something from harm or destruction; to avoid wasteful use of something. | Example: We must conserve our natural resources. 我们必须保护我们的自然资源。


Example:  Try to conserve your energy for the climb. 尽量保存体力以备攀登。 | 近义词: preserve, protect, save, safeguard | 反义词: waste, squander, deplete | 词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *conservare, from Latin conservare "to keep, preserve, maintain," from com- "together" (see com-) + servare "to keep, guard" (see observe).
