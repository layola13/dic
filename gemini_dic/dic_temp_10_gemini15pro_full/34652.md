cleptomania: /ˌklɛptəˈmeɪniə/| n. |盗窃癖，偷窃狂|An impulse control disorder characterized by a recurrent failure to resist urges to steal items that are not needed for personal use or for their monetary value.|She was diagnosed with kleptomania after being caught stealing a candy bar, even though she had plenty of money to buy it. 她被诊断出患有盗窃癖，因为她被抓到偷了一块糖果，即使她有很多钱可以买。|近义词:shoplifting addiction, compulsive stealing|反义词:honesty|词性:noun

词源: From Greek kleptēs "thief" + -mania "madness."
