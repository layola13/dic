eaglestone:/ˈiːɡəlˌstoʊn/| n.|鹰石|A nodule of clay ironstone, formerly believed to be found in eagles' nests and to have magical properties.|Sailors used to carry eaglestones as good luck charms, believing they could prevent shipwreck.|Synonyms: aetites, eagle-stone|Antonyms: None|noun
