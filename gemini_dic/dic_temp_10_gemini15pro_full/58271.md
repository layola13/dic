drunkenhead:/ˈdrʌŋkənˌhed/| n. |醉汉，酒鬼|A person who is habitually drunk or who behaves foolishly when drunk.  A drunkenhead is someone who drinks too much alcohol and often acts foolishly or irresponsibly as a result.|He was a drunkenhead who often spent his nights in the local pub.| 近义词:drunkard, sot, inebriate |反义词:teetotaler|词性:名词
