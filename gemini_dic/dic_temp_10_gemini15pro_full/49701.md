demobilises:/diːˈmoʊbɪˌlaɪzɪz/ |v. |复员，遣散（尤指战后遣散军人）| To release (someone) from military service. | The army demobilised thousands of soldiers after the war. 战后军队遣散了数千名士兵。| The government demobilised the troops after the ceasefire. 停火后政府遣散了军队。| synonyms: discharge, release, muster out | antonyms: enlist, recruit, conscript | verb
