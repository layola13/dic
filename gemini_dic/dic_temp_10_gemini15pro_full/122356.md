mixtilinear:/ˌmɪkstɪˈlɪniər/| adj.|混合线的，曲线与直线混合的|Relating to or consisting of both straight and curved lines.|The architect's design incorporated mixtilinear elements, blending curves and straight lines seamlessly.  这位建筑师的设计融合了混合线元素，将曲线和直线无缝地融合在一起。|Example 2: Mixtilinear curves are commonly used in garden design to create visually appealing pathways. 混合线曲线常用于花园设计中，以创造视觉上吸引人的路径。|近义词: curvilinear, nonlinear|反义词: rectilinear|词性: adjective
