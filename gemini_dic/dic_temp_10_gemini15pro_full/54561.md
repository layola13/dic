dislodge:/dɪsˈlɒdʒ/ (US) /dɪsˈlɑːdʒ/| vt.|驱逐，取出，移动|To remove something or someone, often with difficulty, from a fixed position.|The earthquake dislodged several boulders from the mountainside. 地震使几块巨石从山坡上滚落下来。|The rebels were dislodged from their stronghold. 叛军被从他们的要塞中驱逐出去。|近义词：remove, displace, extricate, oust|反义词: lodge, embed, fix|verb

dislodge:/dɪsˈlɒdʒ/ (US) /dɪsˈlɑːdʒ/| n.|（少见）移位，脱离|The act of dislodging something.|The dislodge of the rock caused a landslide. 岩石的移位造成了山体滑坡。 (This usage is less common.)|近义词：removal, displacement|反义词: placement, fixing|noun
