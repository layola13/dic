cranny:/ˈkræni/| n.|裂缝，缝隙|A small, narrow opening or space, especially in a rock or wall.|例句：A tiny wildflower grew in a cranny of the rock wall. 一朵小小的野花生长在岩壁的裂缝里。

例句：The squirrel hid the nuts in a cranny in the tree. 松鼠把坚果藏在树上的一个缝隙里。|近义词：crevice, fissure, crack, gap, chink, nook|反义词: expanse, closure, seal|词性:名词
