inflect:/ɪnˈflɛkt/ | v. | 使屈折; 使彎曲; 改变; 影响; | To change the form of a word to show a grammatical function such as tense, mood, person, or number. |  He inflected his voice to express his anger. 他变换声调以表达他的愤怒。 |  conjugate, decline, modify | maintain, stabilize | verb

inflection:/ɪnˈflɛkʃ(ə)n/ | n. | 屈折变化; 音调变化; 弯曲; | A change in the form of a word (typically the ending) to express a grammatical function or attribute such as tense, mood, person, number, case, and gender. | The inflection of his voice gave away his true feelings.  他语调的变化暴露了他的真实感受。|  modulation, variation | monotone, uniformity | noun
