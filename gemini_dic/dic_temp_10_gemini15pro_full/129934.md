non-localized:/nɒnˈləʊkəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 非局部的，非本地化的 | Not confined or restricted to a particular place or area; spread out or diffused. | The pain was non-localized, making it difficult to diagnose. 疼痛是非局部的，因此难以诊断。| The infection was non-localized and had spread throughout his body. 感染是非局部的，已经扩散到他的全身。 | widespread, diffuse, general, global | localized, local, specific, confined | adjective

Etymology:
From non- + localized.  "Localized" comes from the French "localiser," from "local" meaning "place."  "Non-" is a prefix of Latin origin meaning "not."
