flowingness:/ˈfloʊɪŋnɪs/| n.|流畅，流畅性|The flowingness of her movements was mesmerizing. 她的动作流畅得令人着迷。|The flowingness of the river was calming.  这条河的流畅让人平静。|近义词：fluidity, smoothness, grace, elegance|反义词：stiffness, rigidity, awkwardness|词性:名词

Etymology: From "flowing" + "-ness". "Flowing" comes from the Old English "flowan," meaning "to flow."  The suffix "-ness" is used to form nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality.
