headspring:/ˈhɛdsprɪŋ/| n.|（尤指突然的）开端，发端|(especially of something unwelcome) a sudden start or outburst|The incident was the headspring of a long and bitter feud.  这起事件是一场漫长而激烈的世仇的开端。|Synonyms: beginning, start, outset, commencement, inception|Antonyms: end, conclusion, finish, termination|noun
