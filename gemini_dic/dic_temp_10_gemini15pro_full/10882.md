arachnoidea: /əˌræknɔɪˈdiːə/ | n. | 蛛网膜 | The delicate membrane covering the brain and spinal cord, located between the dura mater and the pia mater. |  The arachnoidea is one of the three meninges that protect the central nervous system. 蛛网膜是保护中枢神经系统的三个脑膜之一。|  Bleeding into the space between the arachnoidea and the dura mater is called a subdural hematoma.  蛛网膜和硬脑膜之间的出血称为硬膜下血肿。 | Synonym:  meningeal layer | Antonym: None | Noun
