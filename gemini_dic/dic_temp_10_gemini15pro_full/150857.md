paragrandine:/ˌpærəˈɡrændɪn/ | n. | 防冰雹网，防雹网 | A net or covering used to protect crops from hail. | The farmer installed paragrandine over his vineyard to protect the grapes from hailstorms. 农民在他的葡萄园上安装了防雹网，以保护葡萄免受冰雹的袭击。 | 近义词: hail net, hail protection netting | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "unprotected") | 词性: noun
