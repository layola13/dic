birdbrain:/ˈbɜːrdbreɪn/ (US) /ˈbɜːdbreɪn/ (UK)| n.|笨蛋，傻瓜|A person who is not very intelligent or who makes silly mistakes.  A derogatory term.|例句：Don't be such a birdbrain!  别这么傻！|近义词：idiot, fool, moron, nitwit, airhead, ditz|反义词：genius, intellectual, mastermind|词性: 名词

birdbrained:/ˈbɜːrdbreɪnd/ (US) /ˈbɜːdbreɪnd/ (UK)| adj.|愚蠢的，傻的|Having or showing a lack of intelligence.  A derogatory term.|例句：That was a birdbrained thing to do. 这事做得真蠢。|近义词：stupid, foolish, silly, unintelligent, unwise|反义词：intelligent, smart, bright, clever, wise|词性: 形容词
