dismember:/dɪsˈmɛmbər/
verb
肢解，分割，拆散
To cut or tear (a body) into pieces; to take apart.
The serial killer dismembered his victims and buried them in the woods.  连环杀手肢解了他的受害者并将他们埋在了树林里。
dissect, mutilate, tear apart
assemble, unite, join
