loanwords:/ˈloʊnˌwɜːrdz/ | n. | 外来词；借词 | Words adopted from one language and incorporated into another language without translation. | Many English words are loanwords from French. 许多英语单词源自法语。 |  Loanwords enrich the English language. 外来词丰富了英语语言。 | synonyms: borrowing, adopted word | antonyms: native word, inherited word | noun
