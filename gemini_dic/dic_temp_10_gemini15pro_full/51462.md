detonative:/ˈdetəneɪtɪv/
adjective
爆炸的；起爆的；爆震的
Relating to or causing detonation; explosive.
The detonative power of the bomb was immense. 这颗炸弹的爆炸力非常巨大。

The compound is highly detonative.  这种化合物具有很高的爆炸性。

synonym: explosive, blasting
antonym: non-explosive, inert
词性: adjective
