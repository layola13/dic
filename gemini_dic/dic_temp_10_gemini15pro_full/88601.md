hitting-up:/ˌhɪtɪŋˈʌp/ | phrasal verb |  联系；拜访；索要 | To contact someone, often casually or informally, especially to ask for something. | Example: I'm hitting up my friends for some money. 我正在向我的朋友们要钱。 | Example: I'm hitting up the mall after work. 我下班后要去逛商场。 | Synonyms: contacting, visiting, asking, requesting | Antonyms: ignoring, avoiding | phrasal verb

hitting-up:/ˌhɪtɪŋˈʌp/ | phrasal verb |  开始猛烈抨击；开始猛烈批评| To start criticizing or attacking someone or something forcefully. | Example: The media is hitting up the politician for his controversial remarks. 媒体正在猛烈抨击这位政治家发表的有争议言论。 | Example: The coach started hitting up the team for their poor performance. 教练开始严厉批评球队的糟糕表现。 | Synonyms: criticizing, attacking, assailing, lambasting | Antonyms: praising, commending | phrasal verb
