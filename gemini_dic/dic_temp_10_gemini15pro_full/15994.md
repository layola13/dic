bamboozle:/bæmˈbuːzl/
verb
欺骗，迷惑，哄骗
To trick or deceive someone, often in a playful or mischievous way.
The salesman bamboozled him into buying a used car at a ridiculously high price.  推销员把他骗了，让他以高得离谱的价格买了一辆二手车。

The magician bamboozled the audience with his clever tricks. 魔术师用他巧妙的戏法迷惑了观众。

近义词:deceive, trick, hoodwink, mislead, confound, bewilder
反义词:enlighten, clarify, explain
词性:verb

bamboozled:/bæmˈbuːzld/
adjective
被欺骗的，被迷惑的
Feeling confused and tricked.
I felt completely bamboozled by the complicated instructions.  我被这些复杂的指示完全搞糊涂了。

He was bamboozled by the con artist's smooth talk. 他被骗子的花言巧语给骗了。
近义词:deceived, tricked, hoodwinked, misled, confused, bewildered
反义词:clear-headed, informed
词性:adjective
