orpin:/ˈɔːrpɪn/| n. |景天属植物，佛甲草|A succulent plant of the genus *Sedum*, typically having fleshy leaves and clusters of small, star-shaped flowers. |The rockery was covered in yellow orpin.  那片假山布满了黄色的景天。|Synonym: stonecrop, sedum|Antonym: N/A|Noun
