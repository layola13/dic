misappraisal:/ˌmɪsəˈpreɪzəl/| n. |误判，错误的评估|An act of misjudging or assessing something incorrectly.  The company's misappraisal of the market led to significant losses.|The report highlighted the serious misappraisal of the risks involved.|Synonyms: misjudgment, misestimation, miscalculation, undervaluation, overvaluation|Antonyms: accurate assessment, correct evaluation, proper valuation|noun
