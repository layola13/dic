noule:/ˈnuːl/
n.
空值；零值；零位
A value of zero.  Specifically in computing, a value that represents the absence of a value.
The program returned a noule result, indicating no data was found.  程序返回了一个空值结果，表明未找到数据。
近义词:null, zero, nil
反义词:value, non-zero, non-null
词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *nulle*, feminine of *nul*, from Latin *nullus* “not any”.
