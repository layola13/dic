cochair:/ˈkoʊtʃer/ (US) /ˈkəʊtʃɛə/ (UK)| n. v. |共同主持；共同主席；共同担任主席|A person who shares the responsibility of presiding over a meeting or committee with another person.  |例句：She will cochair the conference with her colleague. 她将和她的同事共同主持这次会议。|近义词：co-presenter, joint chair, co-leader|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps subordinate)|词性: noun, verb
