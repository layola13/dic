congeneracy:/ˌkɒndʒɪˈnerəsi/ /ˌkɑːndʒəˈnerəsi/ | n. |同源性，同类性，同属性 |  The state of belonging to the same genus or kind.  Sharing a common origin or ancestry. |  The congeneracy of the two species was evident in their similar skeletal structures. 这两个物种的同源性体现在它们相似的骨骼结构上。 | synonyms: kinship, relatedness, affinity, similarity | antonyms:  dissimilarity, difference, unrelatedness | noun

congener:/ˈkɒndʒɪnə(r)/ /ˈkɑːndʒənər/ | n. |同属物，同类物 | A thing of the same kind or nature.  A member of the same genus. |  Ethanol and methanol are congeners, both being types of alcohol. 乙醇和甲醇是同类物，都是醇类。 | synonyms:  analogue, counterpart, parallel | antonyms: opposite, dissimilar | noun
