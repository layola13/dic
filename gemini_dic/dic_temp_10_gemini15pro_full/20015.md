biolysis:/baɪˈɒlɪsɪs/
n.
生物分解; 生物溶解
The decomposition of organic matter by living organisms, especially bacteria.
Bacteria play an essential role in biolysis of organic pollutants.  细菌在有机污染物的生物分解中起着至关重要的作用。

Biolysis of the pollutant reduced its toxicity. 这种污染物的生物分解降低了其毒性。

近义词: biodegradation, decomposition
反义词: biosynthesis
