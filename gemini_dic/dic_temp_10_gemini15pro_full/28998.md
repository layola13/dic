cartesian:/kɑːrˈtiːʒən/ /kɑːrˈtiːʒn/| adj.|笛卡尔的；直角坐标系的|Relating to the philosophy of René Descartes, or to his system of coordinates.|The Cartesian coordinate system is fundamental to much of modern mathematics. 笛卡尔坐标系是现代数学的基础。|近义词:rectangular (referring to coordinates)|反义词:polar (referring to coordinates)|词性:adjective

cartesian:/kɑːrˈtiːʒən/ /kɑːrˈtiːʒn/| n.|笛卡尔信徒；坚持心物二元论者|A follower of the philosophy of René Descartes.|He considered himself a Cartesian, believing in the separation of mind and body. 他自认为是笛卡尔信徒，相信身心二元论。|近义词:dualist|反义词:monist|词性:noun
