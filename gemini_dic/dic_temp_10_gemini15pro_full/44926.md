cryptogam:/ˈkrɪptəˌɡæm/ | n. | 隐花植物 | A plant that reproduces by spores, without flowers or seeds. |  Example: Ferns and mosses are examples of cryptogams.  蕨类植物和苔藓是隐花植物的例子。 | Example:  Many cryptogams thrive in damp, shady environments. 许多隐花植物在潮湿阴暗的环境中茁壮成长。| Synonym: spore-bearing plant, flowerless plant | Antonym:  phanerogam (flowering plant), spermatophyte (seed plant) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek κρυπτός (kruptós, “hidden, secret”) + γάμος (gámos, “marriage, union”).  The name refers to the hidden reproductive structures of these plants.
