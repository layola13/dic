bleeper:/ˈbliːpər/| n.|哔哔声；呼叫器；（电视或广播节目中消去的）粗话；错误|A small electronic device that makes a beeping sound, used for signaling or paging someone; a word or phrase considered offensive that is removed from a broadcast.|例句：The doctor was called away by his bleeper. 医生被他的呼叫器叫走了。

例句：They bleeped out the swear words. 他们把脏话消音了。|近义词：pager, beeper, alarm, censor (v.)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
