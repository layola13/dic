nondeductibles:/ˌnɑːndɪˈdʌktɪblz/
|n.|不可扣除额；非扣除项目|Amounts that cannot be subtracted from taxable income or gross income when calculating taxes or insurance reimbursements.  These expenses must be paid out-of-pocket by the individual or business.|The insurance policy covered most expenses, but the nondeductibles were significant, putting a strain on our finances. 保险单涵盖了大部分费用，但不可扣除的部分数额巨大，给我们的财务带来了压力。|My medical nondeductibles this year were surprisingly high. 今年我的医疗不可扣除额高得惊人。|deductibles, out-of-pocket expenses|tax-deductible expenses, allowable deductions|noun
