angled:/ˈæŋɡəld/| adj.|有角的，成角的|Having an angle or angles; not straight or rounded.  The picture frame was angled slightly.|例句：The roof was angled steeply. 屋顶很陡。

例句：The angled mirror reflected the light in an interesting way.  成角度的镜子以一种有趣的方式反射光线。|近义词：sloping, slanted, inclined, oblique|反义词:straight, level, flat|词性:形容词
词源:From angle + -ed.  Ultimately from Latin angulus "corner."
