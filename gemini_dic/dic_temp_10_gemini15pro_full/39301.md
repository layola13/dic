conicoid:/ˈkɒnɪkɔɪd/ | n. |二次曲面；锥面 |A surface generated by a straight line moving so that it always intersects a given fixed curve and passes through a given fixed point not in the plane of the curve. |The equation x²/a² + y²/b² + z²/c² = 1 represents an ellipsoid, a type of conicoid.  这个方程式 x²/a² + y²/b² + z²/c² = 1 表示一个椭球体，一种二次曲面。| conic, quadric surface | - | noun
