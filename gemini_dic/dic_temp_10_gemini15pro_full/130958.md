nonassociational:/ˌnɒnəˌsəʊʃɪˈeɪʃənəl/ | adj. | 非联想的，非关联的 | Not connected by or involving association, especially between ideas or sensations.  | The artist's work was praised for its nonassociational use of color and form, creating a sense of pure abstraction. 艺术家的作品因其对色彩和形式的非联想运用而受到赞扬，创造了一种纯粹的抽象感。

The psychologist studied nonassociational learning, focusing on how individuals acquire knowledge without relying on previously learned connections.  这位心理学家研究了非联想学习，重点关注个体如何在不依赖先前学习的联系的情况下获取知识。 | 近义词: unrelated, independent, discrete | 反义词: associative, connected, related | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "associational" (derived from "associate," from Latin "associare," meaning "to join with").
