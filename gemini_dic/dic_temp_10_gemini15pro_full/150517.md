pantomimist:/ˈpæntəmaɪmɪst/| n. |哑剧演员；默剧演员|A performer who acts without speaking, using only gestures and facial expressions.|The pantomimist conveyed a range of emotions with just a tilt of his head.  这位哑剧演员只用一个头部倾斜就表达了一系列的情感。|
|近义词：mime, mime artist|反义词:|noun
