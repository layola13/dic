infidel: /ˈɪnfɪdəl/ (US), /ˈɪnfɪdiːl/ (UK)| n. |异教徒；不信教的人|A person who does not believe in a particular religion, especially Christianity or Islam. |例句：The crusaders fought against the infidels. 十字军与异教徒作战。

例句：In the past, those who didn't follow the state religion were often labeled as infidels. 过去，不信仰国教的人常被贴上异教徒的标签。|近义词：nonbeliever, unbeliever, heretic, pagan|反义词：believer, faithful|词性：名词


infidel: /ˈɪnfɪdəl/ (US), /ˈɪnfɪdiːl/ (UK)| adj. |不信教的；异教的|Relating to or characteristic of infidels. |例句：Infidel armies threatened the kingdom. 异教徒的军队威胁着王国。

例句：The king declared war on the infidel nations. 国王向异教国家宣战。 |近义词：unbelieving, heathen, pagan|反义词：believing, faithful|词性：形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *infidele*, from Latin infidelis "unfaithful," from in- "not" + fidelis "faithful," from fides "faith."
