outvie:/ˌaʊtˈvaɪ/
verb
胜过，超越
To surpass or excel someone in achievement or skill.
He outvied his rivals in the competition. 他在比赛中胜过对手。
The two companies constantly outvie each other in developing new products. 这两家公司在新产品开发方面不断超越彼此。
Synonyms: surpass, excel, outdo, outstrip, eclipse
Antonyms: lose to, be inferior to, fall behind
词性: 动词
