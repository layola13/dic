diphthongising:/dɪfˈθɒŋɡaɪzɪŋ/ /daɪˈfθɔŋɡaɪzɪŋ/
verb
双元音化
The process of making a single vowel sound into a diphthong, or of becoming a diphthong.
The Great Vowel Shift involved the diphthongising of several Middle English long vowels. 中古英语的长元音在元音大推移中经历了双元音化。
Near synonyms:  diphthongization
Antonyms: monophthongization
词性: 动词
