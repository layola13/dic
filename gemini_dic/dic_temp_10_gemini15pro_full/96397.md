indispensabilities:/ˌɪndɪspɛnsəˈbɪlɪtiz/| n. | 必需品；不可缺少之物 |  Things that are absolutely necessary or essential.  These are usually plural, referring to multiple essential items or qualities. |  Access to clean water and food are among the basic indispensabilities of life.  获得干净的水和食物是生命的基本必需品。| synonyms: essentials, necessities, requisites | antonyms: nonessentials, superfluities | noun
