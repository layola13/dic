blitz:/blɪts/| n. v.|闪电战，突击，速战速决|A sudden, overwhelming military attack.|The German blitz of London in 1940 caused widespread destruction. 1940年德国对伦敦的闪电战造成了大范围的破坏。|The advertising company launched a media blitz to promote the new product. 这家广告公司发起了一场媒体闪电战来推广新产品。|近义词：attack, assault, onslaught, barrage|反义词:defense, retreat|词性:名词,动词
词源: From German Blitz, meaning "lightning."  Borrowed into English during World War II.
