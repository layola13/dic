nonpsychoanalytic:/ˌnɒnˌsaɪkoʊˌænəˈlɪtɪk/
adjective
非精神分析的
Not pertaining to or involving psychoanalysis; not based on the theories of psychoanalysis.
This therapist uses a nonpsychoanalytic approach, focusing on cognitive behavioral therapy.  这位治疗师采用非精神分析的方法，专注于认知行为疗法。
Her nonpsychoanalytic methods proved effective in treating the patient's anxiety. 她的非精神分析方法被证明对治疗病人的焦虑有效。
synonyms: non-Freudian, non-analytic
antonyms: psychoanalytic, Freudian
词性: adjective
