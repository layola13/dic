counter-potent:/ˌkaʊntərˈpoʊtnt/ | n. | 对手，对抗者 | A person or thing that has equal power or force; an opponent or adversary. | In the chess match, he faced a counter-potent adversary. 在国际象棋比赛中，他面对的是一个势均力敌的对手。 | Example: The king viewed the neighboring kingdom as a counter-potent and a threat to his own rule. 国王将邻国视为一个势均力敌的对手，并对其统治构成威胁。 | Synonyms: rival, competitor, antagonist, foe | Antonyms: ally, supporter, friend, partner | noun
