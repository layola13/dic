exhortatory:/ɪɡˈzɔːrtətɔːri/
adjective
劝告的；勉励的
serving or intended to exhort; urging earnestly by advice, encouragement, etc.
The coach gave an exhortatory speech to his team before the final game. 教练在决赛前给他的队员们做了一次激励人心的演讲。
近义词: encouraging, urging, inspiring, motivational
反义词: discouraging, dissuading, deterrent
词性: adjective

词源:  From Late Latin *exhortatorius, from Latin exhortārī (“to exhort, urge strongly”), from ex- (“out, thoroughly”) + hortārī (“to encourage, urge”).
