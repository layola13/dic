mayonnaise:/ˌmeɪəˈneɪz/ | n. |蛋黄酱; mayonnaise(尤用于美式英语) |A thick, creamy dressing made from oil, egg yolks, and vinegar or lemon juice.  |例句：I like to put mayonnaise on my sandwiches. 我喜欢在我的三明治里放蛋黄酱。

|例句：The chef whisked the mayonnaise until it was light and fluffy.  厨师搅拌蛋黄酱直到它变得轻盈蓬松。|近义词：aioli, dressing |反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑食材上的区别，例如) vinaigrette |词性:noun


词源: From French *moyeunaise*, apparently from *moyeu* "yolk" (see also *moyen*), but perhaps influenced by *Bayonne*, a city in France.
