calamitously:/kəˈlæmɪtəsli/
adverb
灾难性地；悲惨地
in a disastrous or calamitous way.
The project ended calamitously, with the company losing millions of dollars. 该项目以灾难性的结局告终，公司损失了数百万美元。
The bridge collapsed calamitously during the storm. 在暴风雨中，桥梁轰然倒塌。
近义词: disastrously, catastrophically, ruinously
反义词: fortunately, luckily, successfully
词性: adverb
词源: From Late Latin *calamitosus, from Latin calamitas (“disaster, damage, loss”), from calamus (“reed, stalk of grain”).  Originally meaning "blighted by mildew," as reeds or grain.
