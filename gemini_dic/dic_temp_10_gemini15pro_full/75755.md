fulminate:/ˈfʊlmɪneɪt/ (US), /ˈfʌlmɪneɪt/ (UK)| v. | 抨击；斥责；爆炸；引爆 | to criticize harshly and vehemently; to explode suddenly and violently | The senator fulminated against the proposed tax increases.  参议员猛烈抨击了拟议中的增税方案。|近义词：denounce, condemn, explode, detonate |反义词: praise, commend, implode |词性: 动词

fulminate:/ˈfʊlmɪnət/ (US), /ˈfʌlmɪnət/ (UK)| n. | 雷酸盐；雷汞 | a salt of fulminic acid, a highly explosive substance | Mercury fulminate is a primary explosive used in detonators. 雷汞是一种用于雷管的起爆药。|近义词：explosive |反义词: none |词性: 名词
