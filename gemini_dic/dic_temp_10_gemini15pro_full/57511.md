dragonflies:/ˈdræɡənflaɪz/
n.
蜻蜓
Fast-flying long-bodied predatory insect with two pairs of large transparent wings which are spread out sideways at rest.
Dragonflies hovered over the pond. 蜻蜓在池塘上盘旋。
近义词:Odonata, darning needle
反义词:None (There is no direct antonym for an animal)
词性:Noun
