parchednesses:/ˈpɑːrtʃɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
极度干燥，焦渴
the state of being extremely dry, especially because of hot weather and lack of rain.
The parchednesses of the desert landscape was breathtaking.  沙漠景观的极度干燥令人叹为观止。
The prolonged drought resulted in the parchednesses of the farmlands. 长期干旱导致农田极度干旱。
近义词: aridity, dryness, desiccation
反义词: wetness, humidity, moisture
词性: 名词

词源:  From "parched" + "-ness". "Parched" comes from the Middle English "parchen," from Old North French "parchir," meaning "to scorch," of uncertain origin.
