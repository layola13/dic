barometric:/ˌbærəˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. | 气压的；晴雨表的；由气压计测出的 | Relating to or measured by a barometer; indicating atmospheric pressure. | The barometric pressure dropped significantly before the storm.  暴风雨来临前，气压显著下降。| Synonyms: atmospheric,  pressure-related | Antonyms: N/A | adjective

barometric:/ˌbærəˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. | （气氛、情绪）变化莫测的 | (figuratively) Subject to or indicative of variations (as in mood or pressure). | The barometric mood of the stock market made investors nervous. 股市变化莫测的情绪让投资者感到紧张。| Synonyms: changeable, volatile, fluctuating, mercurial | Antonyms: stable, steady, constant | adjective
