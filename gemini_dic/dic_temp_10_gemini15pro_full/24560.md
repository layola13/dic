broadwayites: /ˈbrɔːdweɪˌaɪts/
noun
百老汇人；百老汇工作人员；百老汇的常客
People associated with Broadway, especially actors, producers, and other theatre professionals, or those who frequent Broadway theatres.
Broadwayites often gather at Sardi's after a show.  百老汇的人们经常在一场演出结束后聚集在萨迪餐厅。
The Tony Awards are a big night for Broadwayites. 托尼奖对百老汇的人们来说是一个重要的夜晚。
Synonyms: theater people, Broadway enthusiasts, theatre professionals
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "non-theatregoers")
词性: noun
