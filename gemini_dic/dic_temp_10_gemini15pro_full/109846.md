lexington:/ˈleksɪŋtən/| n.|莱克星顿|A city in central Kentucky, site of the first battle of the American Revolution (April 19, 1775). Also, a town in Massachusetts, site of another early Revolutionary War battle.|例句：I visited Lexington, Kentucky last year. 去年我参观了肯塔基州的莱克星顿。|近义词：city, town|反义词:countryside|词性:noun

lexington:/ˈleksɪŋtən/| n.|莱克星顿|A surname; a habitational name from Lexington in Nottinghamshire or from Laxton in Northamptonshire and Yorkshire.|例句：He is a Lexington. 他姓莱克星顿。|近义词：(None - as it's a proper noun)|反义词:(None - as it's a proper noun)|词性:noun
