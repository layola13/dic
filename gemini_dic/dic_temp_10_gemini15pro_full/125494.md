multiradial:/ˌmʌltiˈreɪdiəl/
adjective
多辐射的；多射线的
Having many radii or rays.
例句：The multiradial symmetry of the starfish is a defining characteristic. 海星的多辐射对称性是其一个 определяющая 特征。

例句：The artist created a multiradial pattern using vibrant colors. 艺术家运用鲜艳的色彩创作了一个多辐射图案。

近义词：radiate, stellate
反义词：bilateral, unilateral
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From multi- (many) + radial (relating to a radius or ray).  Ultimately from Latin *radius* (ray, spoke of a wheel).
