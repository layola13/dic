Often used in a medical context to describe a specific strain of bacteria (Clostridium difficile).|例句：The hike up the mountain was difficile.  爬山很困难。

例句：He's a very difficile person to work with. 他是一个很难共事的人。|近义词：challenging, arduous, demanding, tricky, awkward, fastidious|反义词：easy, simple, straightforward, accommodating|词性:形容词
