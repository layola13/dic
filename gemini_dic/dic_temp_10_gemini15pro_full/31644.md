chatspeak:/ˈtʃætˌspiːk/ | n. |网络语言；聊天语言|Informal language used in online chat and instant messaging, often characterized by abbreviations, acronyms, and emoticons. | Example:  BRB, gotta go.  一会儿回来，得走了。 | Synonym:  netspeak, cyberspeak, online slang, textese | Antonym: formal language | noun
