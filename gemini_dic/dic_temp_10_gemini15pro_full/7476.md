ankh: /æŋk/ | n. | 安卡 | An ankh is an ancient Egyptian hieroglyphic symbol that represents the concept of eternal life.  It is often depicted as a cross with a loop at the top. | The pharaoh held an ankh, symbolizing his divine power. 法老手持安卡，象征着他的神圣力量。|  The museum displayed several artifacts featuring the ankh symbol.  博物馆展出了几件带有安卡符号的文物。 | synonyms: crux ansata, key of life | antonyms: death, mortality | noun
