misadministration:/ˌmɪs.ədˌmɪn.ɪˈstreɪ.ʃən/
n.
管理不善，治理不当
bad or dishonest management of something, especially public money
The report criticizes the misadministration of the company's funds.  这份报告批评了公司资金管理不善。

近义词：mismanagement, maladministration, incompetence, bungling
反义词：good governance, competent administration, efficient management
词性：名词
