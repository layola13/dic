ETYMOLOGY->
From "runner," likely a spelling variation based on pronunciation. "Runner" originates from the Middle English word "renner," derived from the verb "rennen," meaning "to run." This ultimately traces back to the Old English "rinnan" and Proto-Germanic "*rennaną."
