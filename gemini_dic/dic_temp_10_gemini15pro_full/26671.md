cactuslike: /ˈkæktəsˌlaɪk/
adjective
像仙人掌的；仙人掌状的
Resembling a cactus in appearance or characteristics.
The plant had cactuslike spines covering its stems.  这种植物的茎上覆盖着像仙人掌一样的刺。
The terrain was barren and cactuslike.  这片土地贫瘠，像仙人掌生长的地方一样。
近义词:cactoid, prickly, spiny, thorny
反义词:smooth, soft
词性:adjective
