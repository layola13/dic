birch:/bɜːrtʃ/ (US) /bɜːtʃ/ (UK)| n. |桦树；桦木|A slender tree with thin, papery bark, typically peeling in horizontal strips, and typically found in northern temperate regions. Birch wood is fine-grained and pale in color.|例句：The birch trees in the forest shimmered in the sunlight. 森林里的桦树在阳光下闪闪发光。

例句：He carved a small figure from a piece of birch. 他用一块桦木雕刻了一个小雕像。

|近义词：alder (赤杨), aspen (白杨)|反义词: N/A (没有直接的反义词，可以根据具体语境选择对比性强的树木，如oak 橡树)|词性: 名词
