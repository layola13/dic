complaintive:/kəmˈpleɪntɪv/ | adj. | 抱怨的；哀怨的；诉苦的 | Expressing or suggesting a complaint; whining. |  Her complaintive tone irritated me. 她抱怨的语气让我很恼火。 |  She cast a complaintive glance at her boss. 她埋怨地瞥了老板一眼。 | 近义词: whining, plaintive, querulous, doleful | 反义词: cheerful, optimistic, content | 词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *complantivus, from Latin complantare “to lament, bewail,” from com- “together” + planctus “beating of the breast in grief, lamentation” (from plangere “to beat, strike, lament”).
