circuit-breaker:/ˈsɜːr.kɪt ˌbreɪ.kər/| n.|断路器;  (股市) 熔断机制|A safety device that automatically stops the flow of electricity in a circuit if there is a fault.|The circuit breaker tripped and the lights went out. 断路器跳闸了，灯灭了。|Synonyms: fuse, safety switch, cutout|Antonyms: conductor, connector|noun

circuit-breaker:/ˈsɜːr.kɪt ˌbreɪ.kər/| n.|熔断机制 (金融)|A regulatory measure that temporarily halts trading in a financial market to prevent a crash.|The stock market triggered the circuit breaker after a sharp decline. 股市暴跌触发了熔断机制。|Synonyms: trading halt, market curb|Antonyms: market boom, bull market|noun
