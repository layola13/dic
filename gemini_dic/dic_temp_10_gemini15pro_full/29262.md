cassiterite:/kəˈsɪtəraɪt/
n.
锡石
A dark brown or black mineral, SnO2, the chief ore of tin.
Cassiterite is the most important source of tin. 锡石是锡的最重要来源。
Cassiterite is found in alluvial deposits. 锡石产于冲积矿床中。

近义词:tin ore, tinstone
反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific mineral)
词性:noun
