chinaldine: /ˈkɪnəlˌdaɪn/ | n. |喹哪啶|A synthetic antimalarial drug, C₉H₆NCH₃, a colorless liquid, formerly used as a substitute for quinine.|Chinaldine was once a popular treatment for malaria, but has been largely replaced by more effective medications. 喹哪啶曾是治疗疟疾的常用药物，但现在已被更有效的药物所取代。

|近义词: quinine, mefloquine|反义词: None|词性: noun
