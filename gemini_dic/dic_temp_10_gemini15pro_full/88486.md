historicism:/hɪˈstɔːrɪsɪzəm/
n.
历史主义
The theory that social and cultural phenomena are determined by history.
The painting reflected the artist's historicism, drawing heavily on Renaissance styles.  这幅画反映了艺术家的历史主义，大量借鉴了文艺复兴时期的风格。
近义词:  historical relativism
反义词:  universalism, presentism
词性: noun


historicism:/hɪˈstɔːrɪsɪzəm/
n.
历史相对主义; 历史决定论
The belief that all phenomena are historically conditioned.
Historicism suggests that we cannot judge past actions by today's standards. 历史相对主义表明，我们不能用今天的标准来评判过去的行为。
近义词:  contextualism
反义词:  absolutism
词性: noun



ETYMOLOGY->
From German *Historismus*, coined in the early 19th century, from *Historie* (history) + *-ismus* (-ism). Ultimately from Latin *historia* (history).
