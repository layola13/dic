deler:/ˈdiːlər/ | n. | 商人，经销商 | A person or company that buys and sells goods. |  He's a car dealer. 他是一个汽车经销商。| vendor, trader, merchant | customer, buyer | noun

Etymology:
From Middle English *delere*, from Anglo-Norman *deler*, from Old French *deleor*, from Late Latin *dēlātōrem* (nominative *dēlātor*), agent noun from past participle stem of Latin *dēferre* ("to carry down, bring, convey, deliver"), from *dē* ("down, away") + *ferre* ("to carry, bear").
