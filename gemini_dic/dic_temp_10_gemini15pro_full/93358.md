illegible:/ɪˈlɛdʒəbl/ | adj. | 难以辨认的，无法阅读的 |  Not clear enough to be read.  Derived from the Latin word "legibilis" (readable) with the prefix "il-" (not). |  Her handwriting was so messy it was almost illegible. 她的字迹太潦草了，几乎难以辨认。| The contract was illegible because of the water damage. 合同因为水渍而无法辨认。|  unreadable, indecipherable | readable, clear, legible | adjective
