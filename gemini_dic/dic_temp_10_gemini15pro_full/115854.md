marksmanship: /ˈmɑːrksmənʃɪp/ | n. | 射击术，枪法 | The skill of shooting accurately.  | He demonstrated impressive marksmanship at the shooting range. 他在射击场展示了令人印象深刻的射击术。|  His marksmanship was legendary. 他的枪法堪称传奇。 | 近义词: accuracy, precision, proficiency | 反义词: inaccuracy, incompetence | 词性: 名词

词源: From marksman + -ship.  Marksman comes from mark (target) + man.
