eliquation:/ɪˌlɪˈkweɪʃən/ | n. | 液化;熔化; 淘析 |The process of separating a solid from a liquid by heating the mixture until the solid melts and separates.  It also refers to the separation of one substance from another by washing. |The eliquation of bismuth from lead is a common refining process. 从铅中淘析铋是一种常见的精炼工艺。| Synonyms: liquefaction, melting, separation, refining | Antonyms: solidification, freezing | noun

eliquate:/ˈɛlɪkweɪt/ | v. | 液化;熔化; 淘析 |To separate a solid from a liquid by heating the mixture until the solid melts and separates. It can also mean to separate by washing. |The metallurgists eliquated the silver from the ore. 冶金学家从矿石中淘析出银。| Synonyms: liquefy, melt, separate, refine | Antonyms: solidify, freeze | verb
