counteragents:/ˈkaʊntərˌeɪdʒənts/| n.|对抗剂，抵消剂|Substances that act against something else, neutralizing or minimizing its effect.  They counteract something harmful or undesirable.|The doctor prescribed a counteragent to the poison.|近义词：antidote, remedy, neutralizer|反义词:  agonist, catalyst|noun
