depending:/dɪˈpɛndɪŋ/
preposition|取决于，视…而定|depending on something/somebody means that something will only happen or be true under certain circumstances related to that thing or person.|The trip depends on the weather.  这次旅行取决于天气。|Synonyms: conditional on, contingent on, subject to, reliant on|Antonyms: independent, regardless|词性: 介词
