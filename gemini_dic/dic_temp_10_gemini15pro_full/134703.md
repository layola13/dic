nonmaterialistically:/ˌnɒnməˌtɪəriəˈlɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 非物质地；不注重物质地 |  In a way that is not focused on material possessions or wealth.  It emphasizes spiritual, intellectual, or emotional values over material ones. |  She lives nonmaterialistically, finding joy in experiences rather than possessions. 她生活不注重物质，从体验而非拥有中寻找快乐。|  spiritually, simply, ethically | materially, greedily | adverb
