mysticity:/ˈmɪstɪsɪzəm/| n. |神秘性，神秘主义，神秘感|A quality of being mysterious or obscure; a belief in the spiritual apprehension of truths that are beyond the intellect.|例句：The mysticism surrounding the ancient ruins drew many tourists. 古老废墟周围的神秘感吸引了许多游客。

例句：She was drawn to the mysticism of Eastern religions. 她被东方宗教的神秘主义所吸引。|近义词：enigma, mystery, secretiveness, occultism, spiritualism|反义词：clarity, obviousness, transparency|词性:noun
