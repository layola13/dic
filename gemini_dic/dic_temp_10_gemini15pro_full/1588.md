achroodextrin:/ˌeɪkroʊˈdɛkstrɪn/
n.
消色糊精
A type of dextrin that is not colored by iodine, produced by the incomplete hydrolysis of starch.
Achroodextrin is formed during the breakdown of starch by amylase. 消色糊精是淀粉被淀粉酶分解过程中形成的。
The presence of achroodextrin indicates a more advanced stage of starch hydrolysis. 消色糊精的存在表明淀粉水解到了更高级的阶段。

近义词:dextrin, limit dextrin
反义词:starch
词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From a- (not) + chrōma (color) + dextrin.  "A-" negates the color reaction of dextrin with iodine.

USAGE->
The achroodextrin test is used to monitor the progress of starch hydrolysis. 消色糊精测试用于监测淀粉水解的进程。
Achroodextrin is less viscous than starch and is used in various industrial applications. 消色糊精的粘性比淀粉低，用于各种工业应用。
