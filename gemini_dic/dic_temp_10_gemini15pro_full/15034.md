babu:/ˈbɑːbuː/| n.|先生，官员，职员；（印度对欧洲人的称呼）|A term of respect, often used in India, formerly for a Hindu gentleman or a clerk, and by Indians to address or refer to a European man.|Example: He was addressed as "babu" by the locals.  当地人称他为“先生”。|Example: The babu was busy with his paperwork.  这位职员正忙着他的文书工作。|Synonyms: sir, mister, gentleman, clerk, official|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
