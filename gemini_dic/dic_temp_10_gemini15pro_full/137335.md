nontarnishing:/ˌnɑːnˈtɑːrnɪʃɪŋ/
adjective
不变色的；不失去光泽的
Resistant to tarnishing; not becoming dull or discolored, especially as a result of exposure to air or moisture.
This nontarnishing silverware will look beautiful for years to come.  这种不变色的银器在未来几年都会保持漂亮。
Sterling silver is known for tarnishing easily, while some stainless steel alloys are nontarnishing. 纯银容易失去光泽是众所周知的，而一些不锈钢合金则不会失去光泽。
近义词:corrosion-resistant, rustproof, stainless
反义词:tarnishable, oxidizing
词性:adjective
