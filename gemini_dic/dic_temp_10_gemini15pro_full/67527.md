extraditable:/ɛkˈstrædɪtəbl/
adjective
可引渡的
Subject to extradition; able to be extradited.
The suspect is extraditable to the United States.  该嫌疑人可引渡到美国。
He is not extraditable under the current treaty.  根据现行条约，他不可引渡。
synonyms: deportable, returnable
antonyms: non-extraditable, protected

Etymology:
From extradite + -able.  Extradite comes from the Latin ex- "out" + traditus, past participle of tradere "to hand over."
