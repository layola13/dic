freestanding:/ˌfriːˈstændɪŋ/ | adj. | 独立式的，独立存在的 | existing or functioning independently; not attached to or supported by anything else |  The freestanding sculpture was the centerpiece of the garden.  独立式雕塑是花园的中心装饰品。 | independent, separate, detached, self-contained, self-supporting | attached, connected, dependent | adjective

freestanding:/ˌfriːˈstændɪŋ/ | n. | 独立式的东西 | something that stands alone, such as a piece of furniture or a building | The new refrigerator is a freestanding model, not built-in.  这台新冰箱是独立式的，不是嵌入式的。 | independent unit, separate structure | attached unit, built-in structure | noun
