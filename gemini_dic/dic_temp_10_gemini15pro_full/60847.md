electrotonus:/ɪˌlɛktrəˈtoʊnəs/| n. |电紧张|A change in the electrical potential of a nerve or muscle fiber upon the application of a steady electric current.|Electrotonus can be used to study the properties of nerve and muscle membranes.|近义词：electrotonic potential|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly depolarization if considering its effect on membrane potential)|词性:noun
