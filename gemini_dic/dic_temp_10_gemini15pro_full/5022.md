all-hands:/ˌɔːlˈhændz/| adj.|全体人员的，全体动员的|An all-hands meeting means a meeting that involves all staff members of a company or organization. It signifies the importance and inclusivity of the gathering.  It can also be used as a noun phrase, meaning a meeting involving all staff.|例句：We had an all-hands meeting to discuss the new strategy. 我们召开了一次全体员工会议来讨论新的战略。|近义词：company-wide, organization-wide, general, involving everyone|反义词: select, exclusive|词性: adjective (primarily), noun phrase (occasionally)
