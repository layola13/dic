dysphagia:/dɪsˈfeɪdʒə/ (US), /dɪsˈfeɪʒɪə/ (UK)| n. |吞咽困难|Difficulty or discomfort in swallowing, as a symptom of disease.|The stroke patient experienced dysphagia and required a feeding tube. 中风病人出现吞咽困难，需要插胃管。|Near synonyms: deglutition disorder, swallowing difficulty|Antonym: None (the absence of dysphagia is simply normal swallowing)|Noun
