parrock:/ˈpærək/| n.|小围场，畜栏|A small enclosed field or paddock, especially one near a house or farm. |The sheep grazed peacefully in the parrock. 羊群在小围场里平静地吃草。|近义词：paddock, enclosure, close, field|反义词:open field|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *parrok, from Old English pearruc, pearroc (“park, enclosure”), from Proto-Germanic *parrukaz (“enclosure”). Cognate with West Frisian parreg (“small enclosed field”), Dutch park (“park”), German Park (“park”). Ultimately from the Proto-Indo-European *perḱ- (“enclosure”).
