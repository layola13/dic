affrontive:/əˈfrʌntɪv/
adjective
冒犯的，无礼的
Causing offense; insulting.
His affrontive behavior caused a scene at the party. 他的无礼行为在派对上引起了骚动。
insulting, offensive, disrespectful, rude
polite, respectful, courteous, civil
词性: 形容词
