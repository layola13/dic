cestoidea:/sɛsˈtɔɪdiə/
n.
绦虫纲
a class of parasitic flatworms, the tapeworms
Tapeworms, which belong to the class Cestoidea, can infect humans through contaminated food. 绦虫属于绦虫纲，可通过受污染的食物感染人类。

Cestoidea are a class of parasitic flatworms. 绦虫纲是一类寄生扁形虫。
