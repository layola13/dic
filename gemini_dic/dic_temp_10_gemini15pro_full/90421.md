hq:/eɪtʃ ˈkjuː/| n.|总部，司令部|Headquarters.  The central location for an organization's operations. |Example: The company's hq is located in New York City.  这家公司的总部位于纽约市。|Example:  All commands will be issued from hq. 所有指令都将从总部发出。|Synonym: headquarters, head office, main office|Antonym: branch, satellite office|noun
