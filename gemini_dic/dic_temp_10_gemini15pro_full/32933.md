chopboat:/ˈtʃɒpˌboʊt/ |n.|舢板，小艇|A small boat, often flat-bottomed, used for short trips or harbor work.  Historically, some were used for carrying chopped straw. |The fishermen used a chopboat to reach their nets. 渔民们用一艘小艇去取渔网。|Synonyms: dinghy, skiff, rowboat|Antonyms: yacht, ship|noun

chopboat:/ˈtʃɒpˌboʊt/ |n.|碎料船（主要指历史上用来运输切碎稻草等农业碎料的平底船）|A small, flat-bottomed boat historically used for transporting chopped agricultural materials, such as straw. |Farmers used chopboats to transport chopped straw for animal bedding. 农民们用碎料船运送切碎的稻草作为动物的寝具。|Synonyms: barge, lighter (for cargo)|Antonyms: yacht, ship|noun
