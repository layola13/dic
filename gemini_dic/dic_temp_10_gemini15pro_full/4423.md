alarum:/əˈlɑːrəm/ |n.|警报，惊恐，喧嚣|A sudden loud noise or outcry caused by fear, danger, or excitement.  A warning signal, especially a loud bell or siren.|The alarum bell rang, signaling the start of the fire drill. 警报铃响了，标志着消防演习的开始。|Synonyms: alarm, alert, warning, signal|Antonyms: silence, quiet, peace|noun

alarum:/əˈlɑːrəm/ |v.|使惊恐，发出警报|To give a warning signal, especially by means of a loud bell or siren. To cause alarm or fear.|The sudden movement alarumed the birds. 突然的移动惊吓了鸟儿。|Synonyms: alarm, alert, warn, frighten|Antonyms: reassure, calm, soothe|verb
