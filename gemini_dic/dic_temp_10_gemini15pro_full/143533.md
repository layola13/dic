outgrowths:/ˈaʊtˌɡroʊθs/
n.
自然结果，产物；发展，副产品
Something that grows out of something else; a consequence or result.
The tumors were identified as outgrowths of the surrounding tissue. 这些肿瘤被确定为周围组织的生长物。
n.
分支机构；旁系机构
A branch or offshoot of something larger.
The research center is an outgrowth of the university's physics department. 该研究中心是大学物理系的一个分支机构。
近义词: consequences, results, products, offshoots, branches, developments
反义词: origins, sources, roots, causes
