barometrical:/ˌbærəˈmɛtrɪk(ə)l/
adjective
气压的；晴雨表似的
relating to the barometer or atmospheric pressure.
The barometrical pressure dropped significantly before the storm.  暴风雨前气压显著下降。
The barometrical readings indicated an approaching cold front. 晴雨表的读数表明冷锋正在靠近。
近义词: atmospheric, pressure-related
反义词: N/A
词性: adjective
词源: From barometer + -ical.  Barometer comes from Greek báros "weight" + métron "measure".
