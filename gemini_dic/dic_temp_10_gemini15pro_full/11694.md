armours:/ˈɑːməz/| n.|盔甲（复数）|Protective covering, typically made of metal, worn in battle.|The knights wore shining armours into battle. 骑士们穿着闪亮的盔甲上战场。|近义词：armor, mail, protection|反义词: vulnerability, weakness|名词

armour:/ˈɑːmə(r)/| n. vt.|盔甲，装甲；为…穿上盔甲|Protective covering, typically made of metal, worn in battle; to equip with armour.|The tank was armoured with thick steel plates.  坦克被厚厚的钢板装甲着。|近义词：protection, shield, safeguard|反义词: expose, disarm|名词,动词

词源: From Old French *armure*, from Latin *armatura* ("arms, equipment"), from *arma* ("arms, tools, weapons").
