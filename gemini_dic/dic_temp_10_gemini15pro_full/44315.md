crop out:/ˈkrɒp aʊt (UK), ˈkrɑːp aʊt (US)| v.|露出，显露|When something crops out, it appears or becomes visible unexpectedly.|Several problems cropped out during the project.|近义词：appear, emerge, surface|反义词:disappear, vanish|v.
