clunkyly:/ˈklʌŋkɪli/| adv.|笨拙地，粗鲁地|Moving or operating in a slow, awkward, and noisy way.  Characterized by clumsiness; ungraceful.|The robot moved clunkily across the room. 机器人笨拙地穿过房间。|clumsily, awkwardly, stiffly|gracefully, smoothly, elegantly|adverb

Etymology->
From clunky + -ly.  Clunky itself is likely imitative of a heavy, dull sound.
