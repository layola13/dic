averageness:/ˈævərɪdʒnəs/| n.| 平均水平；普通；平常|The averageness of the data surprised the researchers.  数据的平均水平令研究人员感到惊讶。| The study highlighted the averageness of the responses. 这项研究强调了回复的普遍性。|近义词：normality, mediocrity, typicality|反义词：exceptionality, uniqueness, distinctiveness|词性:名词

Etymology: From average + -ness.  "Average" comes from Old French avarie "damage to ship or cargo," later "equal sharing of such damage," from Italian avaria, from Arabic 'awariya "damaged goods."  The "-ness" suffix transforms an adjective into a noun indicating a state or quality.
