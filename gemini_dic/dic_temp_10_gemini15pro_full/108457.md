lay-aside:/ˈleɪ əˈsaɪd/ | n.| 储蓄; 搁置; | Something laid aside, especially money saved for a specific purpose.  |  She had a small lay-aside for emergencies. 她有一小笔应急储蓄。| synonym: savings, reserve, nest egg | antonym: expenditure, spending | noun

lay-aside:/ˈleɪ əˈsaɪd/ | v. | 放弃; 搁置; 储存; | To put something to one side, either temporarily or permanently.  | He laid aside his book and went to answer the door. 他把书放在一边，去开门。 | synonym: set aside, put away, save, shelve, postpone | antonym:  resume, continue,  take up | verb
