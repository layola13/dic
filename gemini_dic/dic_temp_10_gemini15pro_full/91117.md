hyalotype:/ˈhaɪələˌtaɪp/| n.|玻璃体标本，玻璃体模型|A transparent preparation of the vitreous humor of the eye.  A mold of the vitreous humor.|The museum displayed a hyalotype of a cow's eye, showing the intricate structure of the vitreous body.  博物馆展出了一个牛眼的玻璃体标本，展示了玻璃体复杂的结构。|Near Synonyms: vitreous cast, vitreous mold|Antonyms: None|noun
