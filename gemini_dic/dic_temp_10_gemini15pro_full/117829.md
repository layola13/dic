mellifluently:/ˌmɛlɪˈfluːəntli/ | adv. | 流利地，流畅地，甜美地 | Speaking smoothly and sweetly, like honey.  Characterized by a smooth and persuasive flow of words. |  She spoke mellifluently, captivating the audience with her soothing voice. 她说话流畅，用她甜美的声音吸引了观众。

|  The diplomat mellifluently navigated the complex negotiations.  这位外交官流畅地引导着复杂的谈判。 | 近义词: eloquently, smoothly, sweetly, fluently | 反义词: haltingly, awkwardly, roughly | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Latin *mellifluus* (“flowing with honey”), from *melli-* (“of honey”) (from *mel*, *mellis* (“honey”)) + *fluus* (“flowing”) (from *fluō* (“I flow”)).
