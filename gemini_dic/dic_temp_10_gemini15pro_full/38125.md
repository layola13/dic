comprehensible:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhensɪbl/ (UK) /ˌkɑːmprɪˈhensɪbl/ (US)| adj.|可理解的，易懂的|Able to be understood; intelligible.|The instructions were clear and comprehensible. 说明书清晰易懂。|近义词：understandable, clear, lucid, intelligible|反义词：incomprehensible, unclear, confusing, obscure|词性:形容词
comprehend:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhend/ (UK) /ˌkɑːmprɪˈhend/ (US)| v.|理解，领会|To understand the meaning of something.|I couldn't comprehend what she was saying. 我无法理解她在说什么。|近义词：understand, grasp, perceive|反义词：misunderstand|词性:动词
comprehension:/ˌkɒmprɪˈhenʃn/ (UK) /ˌkɑːmprɪˈhenʃn/ (US)| n.|理解力，领悟力|The ability to understand something.|Reading comprehension is an important skill. 阅读理解是一项重要的技能。|近义词：understanding, grasp, perception|反义词：incomprehension, misunderstanding|词性:名词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin comprehensibilis, from comprehendere (see comprehend).
