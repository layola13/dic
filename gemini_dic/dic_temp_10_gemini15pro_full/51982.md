diaereses: /daɪˈɪərɪsiːz/ | n. | 分音符 | Two dots placed over a vowel, especially a second vowel in a pair, to indicate that it is pronounced separately, as in naïve or Brontë. | The diaereses in "naïve" distinguish it from "nave."  分音符将 "naïve" 与 "nave" 区分开来。 |  The diaeresis in "Brontë" indicates that the "e" is pronounced. 分音符表明 "Brontë" 中的 "e" 要发音。 |  diaeresis (singular), dieresis | N/A | noun
