badgers:/ˈbædʒərz/ (US), /ˈbædʒəz/ (UK)| n. |獾; 困扰; 纠缠不休的人; 徽章(尤指运动队的);  |A badger is a short-legged omnivore in the weasel family. |Badgers are nocturnal animals. 獾是夜行动物。|Near synonyms: brock, |Antonyms: N/A |noun
badgers:/ˈbædʒərz/ (US), /ˈbædʒəz/ (UK)| vt. |反复劝说; 纠缠; 烦扰; |To badger someone is to pester or annoy them persistently. |She badgered her parents until they agreed to let her go to the concert. 她缠着父母，直到他们同意让她去听音乐会。|Near synonyms: harass, pester, bother, annoy, hound, plague|Antonyms: soothe, comfort, appease |verb
