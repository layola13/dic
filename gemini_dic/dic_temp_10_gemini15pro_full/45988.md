cyanotic:/ˌsaɪəˈnɒtɪk/ /ˌsaɪəˈnɑːtɪk/| adj.|发绀的，青紫的|Marked by or causing a bluish or purplish discoloration (as of skin and mucous membranes) due to deficient oxygenation of the blood.|The baby's skin was cyanotic, indicating a serious lack of oxygen.|Synonyms: blue, purplish, livid|Antonyms: rosy, pink|adjective

cyanosis:/ˌsaɪəˈnoʊsɪs/| n.|发绀，青紫|A bluish or purplish discoloration (as of skin and mucous membranes) due to deficient oxygenation of the blood.|The doctor observed cyanosis in the patient's lips.|Synonyms: blueness, lividity|Antonyms: rosiness, pinkness|noun
