illude:/ɪˈlud/| v. | 欺骗，迷惑，哄骗 | to deceive or trick someone | The magician illuded the audience with his clever tricks. 魔术师用他巧妙的戏法迷惑了观众。

The shimmering heat illuded us into thinking there was water on the road ahead. 闪烁的热浪让我们误以为前面的路上有水。| 近义词: deceive, mislead, delude, trick | 反义词: enlighten, inform, undeceive | 词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *illudere*, from in- "in, on" + ludere "to play."  Literally, "to play with," hence "to mock, deceive."
