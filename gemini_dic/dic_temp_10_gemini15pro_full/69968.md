festivities:/fɛˈstɪvɪtiz/| n. |庆祝活动，节庆|Celebrations or activities organized for a festival or special occasion.|例句：The Christmas festivities included carols, presents, and a feast. 圣诞节的庆祝活动包括唱颂歌、送礼物和举行盛宴。

例句：The town held week-long festivities to mark its bicentennial.  该镇举行了为期一周的庆祝活动以纪念其建镇二百周年。|近义词：celebrations, merrymaking, rejoicing|反义词:mourning, grieving|词性:noun

Etymology:  From Late Middle English *festivite*, from Late Latin *festivitatem*,  from Latin *festivus* ("festive, joyous"), from *festum* ("festival, holiday"). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\dʰes-*  ("to do, perform").
