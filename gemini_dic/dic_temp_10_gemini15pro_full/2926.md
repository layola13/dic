advisorily /ədˈvaɪzərɪli/
adverb
咨询地，劝告地
in a manner of giving advice; by way of advice.  Acting or speaking in the capacity of an advisor.
He advisorily suggested she consider other options. 他以顾问的身份建议她考虑其他选择。
The board advisorily approved the new budget. 董事会以咨询的方式批准了新的预算。
近义词: consultatively, advisingly
反义词:  imperatively, dictatorially
词性: adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From advise + -ory + -ly.  Advise ultimately comes from Latin *advidēre "to see, look at," later "consider, examine."
