green-lighting: /ˌɡriːnˈlaɪtɪŋ/
verb
批准，准许；放行
To give permission or approval for something to proceed, often a project.
The studio has finally green-lighted the sequel.  电影制片厂终于批准了续集的拍摄。
The council green-lighted the construction project.  委员会批准了这个建筑项目。
近义词: approve, authorize, sanction, permit
反义词: reject, refuse, deny, veto
词性: verb

green-lighting: /ˌɡriːnˈlaɪtɪŋ/
noun
批准，准许；放行
The act of giving permission or approval for something to proceed.
The project is awaiting green-lighting from the board.  该项目正在等待董事会的批准。
The film received green-lighting after a long delay.  这部电影在长时间的延迟后得到了批准。
近义词: approval, authorization, sanction, permission
反义词: rejection, refusal, denial, veto
词性: noun
