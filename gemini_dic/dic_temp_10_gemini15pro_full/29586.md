catechiser:/ˈkætɪˌkaɪzər/ (US), /ˈkætɪˌkaɪzə/ (UK)| n.|教导者，问答式教学者|A person who catechizes, especially one who instructs by means of questions and answers.|The catechiser asked the children about the Ten Commandments.|近义词：instructor, teacher, tutor, examiner|反义词:student, pupil|noun

catechise:/ˈkætɪˌkaɪz/ (US), /ˈkætɪˌkaɪz/ (UK)| v.|（用问答法）教导，盘问|To instruct by means of questions and answers, often concerning religious doctrine; to question closely or interrogate.|The priest catechised the children before their first communion.  |近义词：instruct, teach, quiz, interrogate|反义词:learn, answer|verb
