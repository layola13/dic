bountifully:/ˈbaʊntɪfəli/
adverb
丰富地，慷慨地
in a bountiful way; generously.
The land produced bountifully. 这片土地物产丰富。
近义词:abundantly, copiously, generously, lavishly, profusely
反义词:meagerly, sparsely, sparingly
词性:adverb

词源:
From Middle English *bountifullī, equivalent to bountiful +‎ -ly.  Bountiful itself comes from bounty + -ful.  Ultimately from Old French *bonté (“goodness, kindness”).
