decillionth:/dɪˈsɪljənθ/
| adj. n.|百万的十次方的|representing one part in a decillion (1 followed by 33 zeros)|A decillionth of a gram is an unimaginably small amount. 百万的十次方分之一克是一个小到难以想象的量。|Synonym:infinitesimal, minuscule|Antonym: enormous, immense|Adjective, Noun

decillionth:/dɪˈsɪljənθ/
| n.|百万的十次方分之一|one part of a whole divided into a decillion parts (1 followed by 33 zeros).|Only a decillionth of the sun's energy reaches Earth. 只有百万的十次方分之一的太阳能量到达地球。|Synonym:  a tiny fraction|Antonym: a large portion|Noun
