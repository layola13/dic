monotherapy: /ˌmɒnəˈθɛrəpi/ (UK) /ˌmɑːnəˈθɛrəpi/ (US) | n. | 单一疗法 | Treatment of a disease with a single drug or therapeutic method. | The doctor decided on monotherapy with an antibiotic. 医生决定采用单一抗生素疗法。|  Monotherapy proved effective in this case. 在这种情况下，单一疗法证明是有效的。 | synonyms: single-drug therapy, single-agent therapy | antonyms: combination therapy, polytherapy | noun
