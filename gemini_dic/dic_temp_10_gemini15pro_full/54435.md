disinflation:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfleɪʃən/
n.
通货膨胀率下降, 通货减缓
A reduction in the rate of inflation.
Example 1: The central bank's policies are aimed at achieving disinflation without causing a recession.  中央银行的政策旨在实现通货膨胀率下降而不导致经济衰退。

Example 2:  Disinflation is a positive sign, but it doesn't mean prices are falling. 通货膨胀率下降是一个积极的信号，但这并不意味着价格在下跌。
近义词: deceleration of inflation
反义词: inflation, reflation
