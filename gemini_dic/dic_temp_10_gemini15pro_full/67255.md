expurgate: /ˈekspərɡeɪt/ | v. | 净化；删节；修订 | To remove objectionable material from (a book, for example). | The editor expurgated the novel before it was published. 编辑在小说出版前删去了令人反感的内容。 |  近义词: censor, bowdlerize, cleanse, purge | 反义词: include, insert, retain | 词性: 动词

expurgated: /ˈekspərɡeɪtɪd/ | adj. | 已删节的；已净化的 |  Having been expurgated; cleansed. |  They released an expurgated version of the film for television. 他们发布了经过删节的电影电视版。 | 近义词: censored, bowdlerized, cleansed | 反义词: uncensored, unexpurgated, complete | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From Latin *expurgatus*, past participle of expurgare "to cleanse, purify," from ex- "out" + purgare "to cleanse."
