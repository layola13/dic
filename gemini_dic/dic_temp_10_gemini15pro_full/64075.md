epizooen:/ˌɛpɪˈzoʊən/ | n. |动物流行病|An epizootic disease; an outbreak of disease affecting many animals of one kind at the same time, similar to an epidemic in humans.| The epizooen of avian flu devastated the poultry industry.  禽流感疫情摧毁了家禽业。| 近义词：epizootic, outbreak, epidemic (when referring to humans) | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

epizooen:/ˌɛpɪˈzoʊən/ | adj. |动物流行病的|Relating to or characteristic of an epizooen.|  The epizooen outbreak caused widespread panic. 动物流行病的爆发引起了广泛的恐慌。 | 近义词：epizootic | 反义词: None | 词性: adjective
