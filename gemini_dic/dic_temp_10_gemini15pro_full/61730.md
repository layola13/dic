emotiveness:/ɪˈmoʊtɪvnəs/ /ɪˈməʊtɪvnəs/| n. |感情丰富，情绪激动，感性|The quality of expressing emotions readily or being easily moved emotionally.  The emotiveness of the music brought tears to her eyes.|例句：The emotiveness of his speech resonated with the audience. 他的演讲充满感情，引起了听众的共鸣。

例句：Her emotiveness made her a compelling actress. 她的情感丰富使她成为一名引人注目的女演员。|近义词：sentimentality, passion, expressiveness|反义词：apathy, coldness, impassivity|词性:noun
