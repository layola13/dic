hyperdelicateness:/ˌhaɪpərˈdɛlɪkətnəs/
|n.|过度精致; 过分讲究; 过于敏感|An excessive or exaggerated sense of delicacy, often to the point of fastidiousness or prudishness.|Her hyperdelicateness made it difficult to have a frank conversation.|近义词: fastidiousness, prudery, squeamishness, oversensitivity|反义词: robustness, coarseness, insensitivity|词性:名词

Etymology:
* hyper- (prefix): excessive, above, beyond (from Greek "hyper")
* delicate (adjective): fine in texture, fragile, easily damaged, refined, subtle (from Latin "delicatus")
* -ness (suffix): forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state or quality
