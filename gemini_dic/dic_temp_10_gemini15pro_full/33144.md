chroma:/ˈkroʊmə/| n.|色度，饱和度|The chroma of this painting is vibrant and intense. 这幅画的色彩鲜艳浓烈。|The intensity of a color, its saturation or purity.  It distinguishes a strong color from a weak one.  It is one of the three characteristics of color, along with hue and value.|The photograph lacked chroma and appeared washed out. 照片缺乏色度，显得褪色。|saturation, intensity, vividness|dullness, paleness|noun
