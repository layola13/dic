briniest:/ˈbraɪnɪɪst/| adj.|最咸的|Having the most salt; most saline.|The Dead Sea is known for having the briniest water in the world. 死海以拥有世界上最咸的水而闻名。|近义词：saltiest, most saline|反义词:freshest, least saline|词性:形容词
Etymology: From brine + -iest (superlative suffix).  Brine comes from Old English "bryne," meaning "salt water," which is related to Old High German "brina," also meaning "salt water."  The "-iest" suffix is a common superlative ending in English, used to form the superlative degree of adjectives.
