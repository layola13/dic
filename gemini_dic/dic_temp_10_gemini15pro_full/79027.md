give-a-damn:/ɡɪv ə dæm/
idiom
不在乎；毫不在意
to not care at all about something or someone.
I don't give a damn what he thinks. 我一点也不在乎他的想法。
近义词: not care, not give a hoot, couldn't care less
反义词: care, be concerned
词性: idiom (习语)


give-a-damn:/ɡɪv ə dæm/
verb phrase
不在乎；毫不在意
to not care at all about something or someone.

近义词: not care, not give a hoot
反义词: care
词性: verb phrase (动词短语)

词源:
"Give a damn"  起源于19世纪晚期，"damn" 在这里指代诅咒或谴责。该短语的字面意思是“不在乎被诅咒”，引申为“根本不在乎”。
