gustatory:/ˈɡʌstətɔːri/ (US), /ˈɡʌstətri/ (UK)| adj.|味觉的；品尝的|Relating to the sense of taste or tasting.|The restaurant offers a gustatory experience you won't soon forget. 这家餐厅提供了一种你不会很快忘记的味觉体验。|Synonyms: taste-related, palatable, flavorful|Antonyms: tasteless, bland|adjective

gustation:/ɡʌˈsteɪʃən/|n.| 味觉；品尝|The act or faculty of tasting; the sense of taste.|Gustation is one of the five traditional senses. 味觉是五种传统感官之一。|Synonyms: tasting, savoring, palate|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could use "lack of taste" or "insensitivity to taste")|noun
