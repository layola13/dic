nonslippery:/ˌnɒnˈslɪpəri/ /ˌnɑːnˈslɪpəri/
adjective
防滑的
Not slippery; providing a good grip.
The nonslippery surface of the yoga mat prevented me from falling during my practice.  瑜伽垫的防滑表面使我在练习时不会摔倒。
The new tires provide a nonslippery grip on wet roads. 新轮胎在潮湿的路面上提供防滑抓地力。
近义词:anti-skid, grip, traction, adhesive
反义词:slippery, slick, smooth

词性
adjective

词源
From non- + slippery.  来自non- + slippery。
