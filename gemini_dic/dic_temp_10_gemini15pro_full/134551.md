nonliteralness:/ˌnɒnlɪtərəlnəs/ | n. | 非字面性；比喻性 | The quality of not being literal; figurative or metaphorical meaning. |  Her poetry is full of nonliteralness, using metaphors and symbolism to convey deeper meaning. 她的诗歌充满了比喻性，用隐喻和象征来表达更深层的含义。

| Example: The comedian's jokes relied heavily on nonliteralness and wordplay.  这个喜剧演员的笑话很大程度上依赖于非字面性和文字游戏。| Synonyms: figurativeness, metaphor, symbolism, allegory | Antonyms: literalness, directness | noun
