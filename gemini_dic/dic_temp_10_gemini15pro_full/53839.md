disconsolatenesses:/dɪsˈkɒnsələtnəsɪz/
noun
极度悲伤，无法安慰的状态
a state of extreme sadness and inability to be consoled

The disconsolatenesses of the mourners was palpable.
哀悼者极度悲伤，令人感同身受。

After the loss of her child, she fell into a deep disconsolatenesses.
失去孩子后，她陷入了深深的悲伤之中。


近义词: sadness, grief, sorrow, despair, despondency, melancholy
反义词: happiness, joy, cheerfulness, elation


词源:  From disconsolate + -ness.  Disconsolate from dis- "not" + consolatus, past participle of consolari "to comfort, console".
