masterer:/ˈmæstərər/| n. |母带工程师；母带制作人|A person who specializes in the process of mastering audio recordings, preparing them for final distribution.|The masterer enhanced the sound quality of the album. 母带工程师增强了专辑的音质。|近义词:audio engineer, sound engineer|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

Etymology:  Derived from the verb "to master" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Master," in the audio context, evolved from its general sense of "having control or proficiency" to specifically refer to the final stage of audio production.
