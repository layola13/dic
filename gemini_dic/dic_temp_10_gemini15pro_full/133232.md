nonexclusions:/ˌnɒnɪkˈskluːʒənz/
n.
非排除；不排除；未排除事项
The absence of exclusions; the state of not being excluded.
The contract includes a clause on nonexclusions of pre-existing conditions.  该合同包含一项关于不排除既往病史的条款。
Near Synonyms: inclusions, allowances
Antonyms: exclusions, prohibitions
noun
