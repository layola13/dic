nonpromotion:/ˌnɒnprəˈməʊʃn/ /ˌnɑːnprəˈmoʊʃn/| n. |不晋升，未晋级|Lack of promotion to a higher rank, position, or grade.  The state of not being promoted.| He was disappointed by his nonpromotion. 他对未能晋升感到失望.|Synonyms: stagnation, standstill, no advancement|Antonyms: promotion, advancement, elevation|noun
