directors-cuts: /dɪˈrɛktərz kʌts/ | n. | 导演剪辑版 | A version of a film, as prepared by the director, that may differ considerably from the studio's release version, often containing additional footage. | Example 1: The director's cut of the movie was two hours longer and included several deleted scenes.  这部电影的导演剪辑版多了两个小时，包含了几个被删减的场景。

Example 2:  Many fans prefer the director's cut, believing it to be a truer representation of the director's vision. 许多影迷更喜欢导演剪辑版，认为它更真实地体现了导演的意图。 | 近义词: director's version, extended cut | 反义词: theatrical cut, studio cut | 词性: 名词
