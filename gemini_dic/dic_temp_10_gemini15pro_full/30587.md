cercus:/ˈsɜːrkəs/ /ˈsɝːkəs/| n. |尾须；尾毛|A small, paired appendage at the posterior end of the abdomen of many insects and other arthropods.|The cerci of the earwig are modified into pincers.  蠼螋的尾须特化成钳子。|

The mayfly nymph uses its cerci to detect predators.  蜉蝣若虫用它的尾须来探测捕食者。|近义词：appendage, filament|反义词:|noun
