donship:/ˈdɒnʃɪp/| n. |大学教师职位；大学教师身份|The status or position of a don (a university teacher, especially a senior member of a college at Oxford or Cambridge).|He was awarded a donship at Trinity College. 他被授予三一学院的教师职位。|近义词：fellowship, professorship|反义词: N/A|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "don" (university teacher) + "-ship" (suffix denoting status or office).  "Don" ultimately derives from Latin "dominus" (lord, master).
