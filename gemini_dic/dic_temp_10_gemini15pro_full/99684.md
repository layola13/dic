interlobate:/ˌɪntərˈloʊbeɪt/
adjective
叶间的；小叶间的
Situated between lobes.
The interlobate ducts drain bile from the liver.  叶间胆管排出肝脏的胆汁。
The radiologist pointed to the interlobate effusion on the X-ray. 放射科医生指着X光片上的叶间积液。
近义词:interlobar
反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps intralobar - within a lobe)
词性:adjective
