incisely:/ɪnˈsaɪsli/| adv.|简明扼要地，清晰地|In a precise and concise manner, clearly and briefly expressing something.|She spoke incisively, cutting to the heart of the matter. 她说话简明扼要，直指问题的核心。|The surgeon made an incisive cut.  外科医生做了一个精确的切口。|近义词：precisely, concisely, succinctly, sharply, acutely|反义词：vaguely, ramblingly, diffusely|词性:副词
Etymology: From Latin *incīsus*, past participle of *incīdere*  "to cut into," from *in- "into" + *caedere* "to cut."
