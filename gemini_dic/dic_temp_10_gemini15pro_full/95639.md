incompatible:/ˌɪnkəmˈpætɪbl/
adj.
不兼容的；不相容的；矛盾的；合不来的
not able to exist or work with another person or thing because of basic differences
例句：This software is incompatible with my operating system. 这个软件与我的操作系统不兼容。

例句：Their personalities are incompatible, which leads to frequent arguments. 他们的性格不合，导致经常争吵。

近义词：conflicting, clashing, discordant, antagonistic, irreconcilable
反义词：compatible, consistent, harmonious
词性: 形容词


词源:
Late 16th century: from French incompatible or late Latin incompatibilis, from in- ‘not’ + compatibilis (see compatible).
