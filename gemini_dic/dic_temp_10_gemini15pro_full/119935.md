milks:/mɪlks/
|v.|挤奶；榨取|To extract milk from a mammal, typically a cow or goat.  It can also be used metaphorically to mean to extract something for personal gain.|The farmer milks the cows every morning. 农民每天早上挤牛奶。|近义词:extract, draw, drain|反义词:inject, infuse|词性:动词

milks:/mɪlks/
|n.| (非标准用法) 牛奶|A non-standard plural form of milk, sometimes used informally.|The cafe offers different milks, like almond and soy.  这家咖啡馆提供不同的牛奶，比如杏仁奶和豆奶。|近义词:milk (standard form)|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
