deiformity:/ˌdiːɪˈfɔːrmɪti/ | n. |神性，神形 |The state or quality of having a godlike form or nature; resemblance to a god. |The artist attempted to capture the deiformity of the goddess in his sculpture. 艺术家试图在他的雕塑中捕捉女神的神性。| Near synonyms: divinity, godliness, godhead | Near antonyms: mortality, humanness | noun


deiform:/ˈdiːɪfɔːrm/ | adj. |神形的，神似的 | Having the form or nature of a god; godlike. |The deiform statue radiated an aura of power and serenity.  神形的雕像散发着力量和宁静的光环。| Near synonyms: divine, godlike, celestial | Near antonyms: mortal, human, earthly | adjective
