overfervently: /ˌoʊvərˈfɜːrvəntli/
adverb
过度热情地，过分热烈地
With excessive fervor; too intensely or passionately.
He praised her overfervently, making her uncomfortable. 他过分热情地赞扬她，让她感到不舒服。
overzealously, excessively, immoderately, too enthusiastically
apathetically, indifferently, halfheartedly
