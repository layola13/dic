noddingly:/ˈnɒdɪŋli/ (UK) /ˈnɑːdɪŋli/ (US)| adv.|点头地，首肯地|In a manner that suggests agreement or understanding through nodding.|She listened noddingly as he explained the situation. 她一边听着他解释情况，一边点头表示同意。|例句：He noddedly agreed to her request. 他点头同意了她的请求。|近义词：agreeably, approvingly|反义词：disapprovingly, dissentingly|词性: 副词
词源: From "nodding" (present participle of the verb "nod") + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Nod" comes from Old English *hnoddian, of imitative origin.
