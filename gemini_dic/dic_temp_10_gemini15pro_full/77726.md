gemmulation:/ˌdʒɛmjuˈleɪʃən/| n.|芽生，出芽繁殖；芽殖|The process of forming new animal or plant individuals by budding or gemmation.  This is a form of asexual reproduction.|Coral reefs grow through gemmation, with new polyps budding off from existing ones. 珊瑚礁通过芽生生长，新的珊瑚虫从现有的珊瑚虫中萌发出来。|synonyms: budding, sprouting, propagation|antonyms: fission, fragmentation|noun
