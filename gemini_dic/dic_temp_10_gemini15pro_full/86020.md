heald:/hild/ | n. |综丝, healds (复数)|A heald is a part of a loom that separates and guides the warp threads, forming the shed for the weft to pass through.|The weaver adjusted the heald to create a complex pattern.  织布工调整综丝以创造复杂的图案。|Synonyms: heddle |Antonyms: N/A |noun
