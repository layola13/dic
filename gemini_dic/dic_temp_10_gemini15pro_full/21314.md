bloodthirstiest:/ˈblʌdˌθɜːrstɪɪst/ | adj. | 极度嗜血的，最残忍的 | Having or showing the greatest desire to kill or shed blood. |  The bloodthirstiest warrior led the charge.  最嗜血的战士领导了冲锋。|  The dictator was known for his bloodthirstiest policies.  这位独裁者以其最残忍的政策而闻名。 | 近义词：cruel, ruthless, savage, brutal | 反义词：merciful, compassionate, humane | 词性: 形容词
Etymology: From blood + thirsty + -est (superlative suffix).  "Bloodthirsty" itself combines "blood" with "thirsty," meaning having a craving for blood.
