documentaries:/ˌdɒkjuˈment(ə)riz/ (US) /ˌdɑːkjuˈment(ə)riz/| n.|纪录片|Films or television or radio programmes that provide factual records or reports on various subjects.|I enjoy watching documentaries about nature. 我喜欢看关于自然的纪录片。|synonyms:  non-fiction films, factual films|antonyms: fiction films|noun

documentary:/ˌdɒkjuˈment(ə)ri/ (US) /ˌdɑːkjuˈment(ə)ri/| adj.|纪录的，纪实的|Consisting of or based on official documents or providing factual records.|The film is a documentary account of the war. 这部电影是对战争的纪实描述。|synonyms: factual, non-fictional|antonyms: fictional, fabricated|adjective
