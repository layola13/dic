gallimatia:/ˌɡælɪˈmeɪʃə/ | n. |胡言乱语；乱七八糟的话；杂乱无章|A confused mixture of words and ideas; unintelligible jargon.|He spoke a gallimatia of French, Spanish, and English. 他说法语、西班牙语和英语混杂在一起，语无伦次。|近义词：gibberish, nonsense, babble|反义词：sense, clarity|词性:名词

词源: From Late Latin *gallimathias*, from Greek *kalleia mathēmata* ‘fine accomplishments’.  Originally referred to a rooster's crow, later broadened to mean unintelligible chatter.
