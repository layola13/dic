eventuate:/ɪˈvɛntʃueɪt/
verb
最终发生；结果是
result in; lead to
His early interest in airplanes eventually eventuated in a career as a pilot. 他早期对飞机的兴趣最终使他成为了一名飞行员。

The negotiations eventually eventuated in a compromise.  谈判最终以妥协告终。


synonym:  result, culminate, end, terminate, conclude
antonym:  begin, commence, start, initiate, originate
词性: 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin eventuatus, past participle of eventuare (“to come out, happen”), from Latin eventus (“outcome, event”), from evenire (“to come out, happen”), from ex- (“out of, from”) + venire (“to come”).
