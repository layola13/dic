nonpracticalities:/ˌnɒnpræktɪˈkælɪtiz/
noun
不切实际之处；不实用性；不可行性
The report highlighted the nonpracticalities of the proposed scheme.  这份报告强调了拟议方案的不切实际之处。
The nonpracticalities of the design made it impossible to build.  设计的不可行性使得它无法建造。
Synonyms: impracticalities, infeasibilities, difficulties, drawbacks, disadvantages
Antonyms: practicalities, feasibilities, advantages, benefits
n.
