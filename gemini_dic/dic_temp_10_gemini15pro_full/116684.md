|n.| 成熟（指发展到完善的状态）| The cheese has reached its full maturation.  奶酪已经完全成熟。|

|例句：He studied the maturation of the human brain. 他研究了人脑的成熟过程。|

maturation:/ˌmætʃəˈreɪʃən/
|n.|成熟，成熟期|The maturation of the fruit takes several weeks. 水果的成熟需要几周时间。|
|n.| 成熟（指发展到完善的状态）|  She displayed a surprising level of political maturation for her age.  就她的年龄而言，她展现出了惊人的政治成熟度。|
|例句：The wine undergoes a slow maturation process in oak barrels.  葡萄酒在橡木桶中经历缓慢的成熟过程。|
|例句：The psychologist observed the child's social maturation. 心理学家观察了孩子的社会成熟度。|
|近义词：ripening, development, growth, evolution|
|反义词：immaturity, deterioration, regression|
|词性：名词|


词源:  From Late Latin *maturationem, from Latin maturus ("ripe").  源自晚期拉丁语 *maturationem，来自拉丁语 maturus（“成熟的”）。
