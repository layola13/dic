misattributions:/ˌmɪsˌætrɪˈbjuːʃənz/
|n.|错误归因，误判|The act of attributing something to the wrong source or cause.  Incorrectly assigning authorship or origin.|
例句：Misattributions of famous quotes are common on the internet.  网络上名言的错误归因很常见。
例句：The historical misattributions led to considerable confusion. 这些历史上的错误归因导致了相当大的混乱。
|近义词：misinterpretations, errors, mistakes|
|反义词：correct attributions, accurate interpretations|
|词性：noun (plural)|
