fluctuating:/ˈflʌktʃueɪtɪŋ/
adjective
波动的，起伏的
rising and falling irregularly in number or amount.
The fluctuating price of gold makes it a risky investment. 黄金价格的波动使其成为一项风险投资。
fluctuating temperatures  波动的气温
synonyms: varying, changing, unstable, shifting, erratic, irregular, volatile, inconstant
antonyms: stable, constant, steady, fixed, unchanging
词性: adjective


fluctuate:/ˈflʌktʃueɪt/
verb
波动，起伏
rise and fall irregularly in number or amount.
Oil prices have fluctuated wildly in recent months.  近几个月来，石油价格大幅波动。
The temperature fluctuated throughout the day.  温度一整天都在波动。
synonyms: vary, change, shift, alter, oscillate, swing, waver, seesaw
antonyms: stabilize, remain, stay
词性: verb

Etymology: From Latin fluctuatus, past participle of fluctuare "to wave, undulate, fluctuate," from fluctus "wave, billow."
