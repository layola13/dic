inamovable:/ˌɪnəˈmuːvəbl/
adjective
不可移动的；固定的；不动产的
not able to be moved
The inamovable rock stood firm against the crashing waves. 巨大的岩石在海浪的冲击下屹立不动。
近义词:immobile, fixed, steadfast, unmovable
反义词:movable, mobile, portable
词性:adjective

词源:
From in- (not) + movable.  The prefix "in-" negates the meaning of "movable," which comes from the Old French "movable" and ultimately from the Latin "movēre" (to move).
