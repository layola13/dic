musculophrenic:/ˌmʌskjʊloʊˈfrɛnɪk/| adj.|肌膈的|Relating to both the muscles and the diaphragm.  Formed from the words "musculo" (relating to muscle) and "phrenic" (relating to the diaphragm).|The musculophrenic artery supplies blood to the diaphragm and some intercostal muscles.|近义词:diaphragmatic, phrenic|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective
