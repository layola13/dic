liege:/liːdʒ/| n. |君主；领主；臣下；封臣|A feudal superior or sovereign.  A vassal or subject.|The knight swore fealty to his liege.  这位骑士向他的君主宣誓效忠。|近义词：lord, sovereign, overlord, vassal (as a reciprocal relationship)|反义词:rebel, subject (when emphasizing subordination)|noun

liege:/liːdʒ/| adj. |（封建时代用语）君主的；领主的；臣下的；封臣的；受某人统治的|Loyal and obligated by oath to a feudal lord.|He was a liege man to the king. 他是国王的封臣。|近义词：loyal, faithful, subject|反义词:rebellious, disloyal|adjective
