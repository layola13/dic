becomes:/bɪˈkʌmz/| v.|变得，成为|A linking verb indicating a change of state or condition.  It connects the subject to a predicate adjective or noun that describes the new state.|She becomes nervous before exams. 她考试前会变得紧张。|synonyms: turns into, grows into, develops into|antonyms: remains, stays|verb

becomes:/bɪˈkʌmz/| v.|适合，相称|Indicates that something suits or looks good on someone.|That dress becomes you. 那件衣服很适合你。|synonyms: suits, flatters|antonyms: clashes with, ill-suits|verb
