fringeless:/ˈfrɪndʒləs/
adjective
无边的，无穗的，没有边缘的
lacking a fringe; without a fringe.
The fringeless curtains looked modern and sleek.  无边的窗帘看起来现代而时尚。
The artist preferred fringeless scarves for their simplicity.  这位艺术家喜欢简洁的无边围巾。
近义词:unfringed, borderless, edgeless
反义词:fringed, bordered, edged
词性:adjective
