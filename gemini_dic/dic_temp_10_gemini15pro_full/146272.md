overfaithfully:/ˌoʊvərˈfeɪθfəli/
adv. | 过分忠实地，过分虔诚地 |  Performing a duty or task with excessive zeal and adherence to instructions, often to the point of being unnecessary or counterproductive.  | He followed the instructions overfaithfully, even when they didn't make sense. 他过分忠实地执行了指示，即使这些指示毫无意义。| 近义词: excessively, scrupulously, rigidly | 反义词: carelessly, negligently | 词性: 副词
