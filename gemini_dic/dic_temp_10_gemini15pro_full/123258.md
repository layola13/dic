monodelphous:/ˌmɒnəˈdelfəs/ | adj. | 单体雄蕊的，单雄蕊的 | Having stamens united into a single bundle or tube by their filaments. | Example: Malvaceae family plants are known for their monodelphous stamens. 锦葵科植物以其单体雄蕊而闻名。 Example:  The flower exhibited a monodelphous androecium. 这朵花展现了单体雄蕊的雄蕊群。 | Near Synonyms: synandrous, monadelphic | Antonym:  diadelphous, polydelphous | word type: adjective
