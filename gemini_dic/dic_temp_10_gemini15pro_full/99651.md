interleave: /ˌɪntərˈliːv/ | v. | 交叉，交错，插入 | To arrange by alternating or inserting something between parts of something else. |  She interleaved the letters with blank sheets of paper. 她把空白的纸张插入信件之间。| 近义词: intersperse, interpose, insert | 反义词: separate, divide | 词性: 动词

interleave: /ˈɪntərliːv/ | n. | 交叉，交错，插入物 | The act of interleaving or something that is interleaved. |  The interleave of tissue paper protected the delicate china. 薄纸的插入保护了精致的瓷器。| 近义词: insertion, interposition | 反义词: separation, division | 词性: 名词

词源:  From Latin *interfoliare, from inter- "between" + folium "leaf."  Originally referred to inserting blank leaves between the pages of a book.
