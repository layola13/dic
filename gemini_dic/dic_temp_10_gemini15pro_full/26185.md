butlerage:/ˈbʌtlərɪdʒ/
noun
酒窖管理费；酒窖服务费；酒水服务费
The fee charged by a hotel or restaurant for storing and serving a customer's own wine.  This often covers the service of opening, decanting, and serving the wine.
The restaurant charged a hefty butlerage for bringing our own wine. 这家餐馆对自带酒水收取了高昂的服务费。
Synonyms: corkage, service charge
Antonyms: gratuity (although sometimes a butlerage might be considered a type of gratuity by the establishment)
