objected:/əbˈdʒɛktɪd/
verb
反对；不赞成
expressed or felt opposition to or dislike of something or someone.
The lawyer objected to the question. 律师反对这个问题。
近义词: opposed, protested, dissented
反义词: agreed, consented, approved
词性: verb (past tense of "object")

objection:/əbˈdʒɛkʃən/
noun
反对；异议
an expression or feeling of disapproval or opposition; a reason for disagreeing.
I have no objection to your proposal. 我对你的提议没有异议。
近义词: protest, opposition, dissent
反义词: agreement, consent, approval
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *objecten*, from Latin *obiectāre* (“to throw against, oppose, present”), from *ob-* (“against, towards”) + *iactāre* (“to throw frequently”), frequentative of *iacere* (“to throw”).
