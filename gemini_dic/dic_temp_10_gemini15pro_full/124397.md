motoneuron: /ˈməʊtəʊˌnjʊərɒn/ (UK) /ˈmoʊtərˌnʊrɑːn/ (US)| n. |运动神经元|A nerve cell forming part of a pathway along which impulses pass from the brain or spinal cord to a muscle or gland.|The motoneuron transmits signals from the spinal cord to the muscle, causing it to contract.|例句：The motoneuron fires, causing the muscle to twitch. 运动神经元放电，导致肌肉抽搐。

例句：Damage to motoneurons can lead to muscle weakness and paralysis.  运动神经元的损伤会导致肌肉无力和瘫痪。|近义词：motor neuron, efferent neuron|反义词: sensory neuron (传入神经元), afferent neuron|词性: noun
