anglophiles:/ˈæŋɡləfaɪlz/
n.|亲英者，喜爱英国文化的人|A person who greatly admires or favors England and its customs.
|Many anglophiles in the US collect British memorabilia.|美国的许多亲英者收集英国纪念品。
|近义词: Anglophile, admirer of England|反义词: Anglophobe|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
