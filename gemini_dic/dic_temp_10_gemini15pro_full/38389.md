conceptualisms:/kənˈsɛptʃuəˌlɪzəmz/
n.|概念主义，观念艺术|A style or movement in art emphasizing the idea or concept over the physical form or execution. It often involves the use of text, language, or found objects to convey the artist's intention.|The exhibition showcased a range of conceptualisms, from performance art to text-based installations.|近义词：conceptual art, idea art, conceptualist art|反义词:formalism, representational art|词性:noun


conceptualism:/kənˈsɛptʃuəˌlɪzəm/
n.|概念化，观念化|The act or process of forming a concept or idea.|His conceptualism of the project was innovative and thought-provoking.|近义词：conceptualization, ideation|反义词: concretization, materialization|词性:noun
