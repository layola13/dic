demandant:/dɪˈmændənt/| n.|原告，请求者|A plaintiff; one who demands something, especially in a legal context.  A person who brings a legal action; a claimant.|例句：The demandant filed a lawsuit against the defendant. 原告对被告提起了诉讼。

例句：The demandant sought restitution for the damages. 原告寻求赔偿损失。|近义词：plaintiff, claimant, petitioner|反义词：defendant, respondent|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *demandant*, present participle of demander (“to ask, demand”), from Latin demandāre (“to entrust, commit, enjoin, order, demand”), from de- (“down, completely”) + mandāre (“to entrust, commission, order”).
