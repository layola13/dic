corruptness:/kəˈrʌptnəs/
n.
腐败；堕落；贪污
The state of being dishonest or immoral, especially in positions of power.
The corruptness of the officials led to the downfall of the government. 官员们的腐败导致了政府的垮台。

Synonyms: corruption, dishonesty, depravity, venality, turpitude
Antonyms: honesty, integrity, probity, rectitude
