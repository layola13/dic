half-wild:/ˌhæf ˈwaɪld/ | adj. | 半野生的；未完全驯化的 | Partly wild; not completely tamed or domesticated. | The zoo has a program for half-wild animals to help them transition to the wild.  这个动物园有一个帮助半野生动物过渡到野外的项目。 | Example: The farmer kept a flock of half-wild geese. 这位农民养了一群半野生的鹅。 | Synonyms: semi-wild, feral, untamed | Antonyms: domesticated, tame, captive | adjective

half-wild:/ˌhæf ˈwaɪld/ | n. | 半野生动物 | A partly wild animal. |  The half-wild clung to the edge of the forest, wary of humans. 这只半野生动物紧贴着森林边缘，对人类保持警惕。 | Example: The half-wild paced nervously in its enclosure. 这只半野生动物在围栏里紧张地踱步。| Synonyms: semi-wild animal, feral creature | Antonyms: domesticated animal, pet | noun
