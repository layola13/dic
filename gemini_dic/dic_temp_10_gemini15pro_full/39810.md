consortship:/kənˈsɔːrtʃɪp/ /kɑːnˈsɔːrtʃɪp/
n.
陪伴；结交；配偶关系
the state of being a consort; a consort's position or term of office.  The close association of a person or group with another.
例句：Her consortship with the king lasted for many years. 她与国王的陪伴持续了很多年。

例句：The artist enjoyed a long consortship with his muse.  这位艺术家与其灵感女神有着长久的结交。

近义词：companionship, association, partnership, marriage, union
反义词：separation, isolation, divorce

词性: noun
