exemptive:/ɪɡˈzɛmptɪv/
adjective
免除的；豁免的
Free from an obligation or liability imposed on others.
例句：Exemptive regulations allow small businesses to avoid certain reporting requirements. 小型企业可以根据豁免条例免除某些报告要求。

例句：This income is exemptive from federal taxes. 这笔收入免缴联邦税。

近义词：exempt, excused, released
反义词：liable, subject (to), responsible
词性: adjective
