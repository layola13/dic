christology:/ˌkrɪstˈɒlədʒi/
n.
基督论
The study of the nature of Christ, including his person, divinity, and earthly life.
Christology is a complex and debated topic in theology. 基督论是神学中一个复杂且有争议的话题。
The book offers a new perspective on christology. 这本书提供了一个关于基督论的新视角。
近义词:theology of Jesus, study of Christ
反义词:N/A
词性:noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek Χριστολογία (Khristología), from Χριστός (Khristós, “Christ”) + -λογία (-logía, '-logy').
