microfelsitic:/ˌmaɪkroʊfɛlˈsɪtɪk/
adjective
微霏细岩的；微晶质的
Composed of extremely fine-grained crystals, too small to be distinguished with an ordinary microscope.  Referring to the texture of volcanic rocks.
The rock sample exhibited a microfelsitic texture, indicating rapid cooling.  这块岩石样本呈现出微霏细岩结构，表明其快速冷却。
microcrystalline, aphanitic
phaneritic (coarse-grained)
