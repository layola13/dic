humbuggers:/ˈhʌmbʌɡərz/
n.
骗子，欺骗者；胡说，废话
A person who deceives or cheats others; nonsense, deceptive talk.
"He promised me a raise, but it was all a bunch of humbuggers." 他答应给我加薪，但全是胡说八道。
近义词：frauds, impostors, deceivers, charlatans, liars, fakers, phonies, con artists, tricksters, swindlers, cheats, hoaxers, humbug, nonsense, baloney, bunk, claptrap, hogwash, hokum, malarkey, rubbish, tommyrot, piffle, twaddle
反义词：truth-tellers, honest people
词性：名词 (plural)
