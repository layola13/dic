masterpieces:/ˈmæstərpiːsɪz/
noun
杰作；名著
a work of outstanding artistry, skill, or workmanship.
例句：The museum houses several masterpieces of Renaissance art.  这家博物馆收藏了几件文艺复兴时期的杰作。

例句：Shakespeare's Hamlet is considered one of the greatest masterpieces in English literature.  莎士比亚的《哈姆雷特》被认为是英国文学中最伟大的杰作之一。

近义词：masterwork, classic, chef-d'œuvre, tour de force
反义词：failure, flop, disaster
