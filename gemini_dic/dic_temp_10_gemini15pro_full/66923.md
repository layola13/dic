expendables:/ɪkˈspɛndəbəlz/| n.|消耗品；可牺牲的人或物|Items or people considered not worth saving or preserving, often in a military or wartime context.  These could be ammunition, fuel, or even low-ranking soldiers. |The army considered the foot soldiers expendable in the face of the enemy's superior forces.  军队认为面对敌人优势兵力时，步兵是可以牺牲的。

The film "The Expendables" features a team of veteran mercenaries.  电影《敢死队》讲述了一群老牌雇佣兵的故事。|近义词：consumables, disposables, sacrifices|反义词：invaluables, essentials|noun
