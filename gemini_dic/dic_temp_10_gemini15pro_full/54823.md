dispersibilities:/dɪˌspɜːsɪˈbɪlɪtiz/
n.
分散性，分散能力
The ability of a substance to be dispersed or spread out, typically in a liquid.
The dispersibilities of the pigments in the paint affect its overall quality.  颜料在油漆中的分散性会影响其整体质量。

The new formulation improved the carbon black's dispersibilities in the polymer matrix. 新配方提高了炭黑在聚合物基质中的分散性。
近义词:dispersability, spreadability, distribution
反义词:cohesion, aggregation
词性:noun


disperse:/dɪˈspɜːs/
v.
分散，散开
To distribute or spread over a wide area.
The crowd dispersed after the concert.  演唱会结束后人群散开了。

The wind dispersed the seeds across the field. 风把种子吹散到田野各处。

近义词:scatter, disseminate, distribute
反义词:gather, collect, concentrate
词性:verb


dispersible:/dɪˈspɜːsəbl/
adj.
可分散的
Able to be dispersed or spread out.
The powder is dispersible in water.  这种粉末可分散在水中。

This new type of ink is easily dispersible, making it ideal for inkjet printers. 这种新型墨水易于分散，非常适合喷墨打印机。

近义词:spreadable, distributable, diffusible
反义词:cohesive, agglomerated
词性:adjective
