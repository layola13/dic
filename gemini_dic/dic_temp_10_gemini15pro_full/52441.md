die-for:/ˈdaɪ fɔːr/ | adj. | 令人垂涎的，非常想要的 | Extremely desirable, attractive, or appealing, so much so that one would do almost anything to obtain it. |  This cake is absolutely die-for! 这蛋糕真是太好吃了，让人欲罢不能！|  The view from the top of the mountain was die-for. 从山顶看到的景色美不胜收。| 近义词: desirable, coveted, irresistible, gorgeous | 反义词: undesirable, unattractive, repulsive | 词性:形容词

词源:  This expression likely originated from the exaggerated expression "to die for something," meaning to desire it so intensely that one would be willing to die to obtain it. Over time, "die-for" became an adjective describing something extremely desirable.
