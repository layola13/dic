conchie:/ˈkɒntʃi/ (UK), /ˈkɑːntʃi/ (US)| n.| 拒服兵役者，尤其指因宗教或道德原因拒服兵役的人 |A person who refuses to perform military service, especially on religious or moral grounds.| He was branded a conchie during the war for his pacifist beliefs.  他因为和平主义信仰在战争期间被贴上拒服兵役者的标签。|Synonyms: pacifist, conscientious objector, draft dodger|Antonyms: soldier, warmonger|noun
