interwreathed:/ˌɪntərˈriːðd/
adjective
相互缠绕的，交织的
closely intertwined or entwined together.
The roses and lilies were interwreathed around the columns. 玫瑰和百合花缠绕在柱子上。
intertwined, interwoven, interlaced
separate, disentangled
词性: 形容词
