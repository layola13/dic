anesthesia:/ˌænɪsˈθiːʒə/ (US), /ˌænəsˈθiːziə/ (UK)| n.|麻醉|Loss of sensation, especially pain, induced by drugs.|The patient was given general anesthesia before the operation. 病人在手术前进行了全身麻醉。|Synonyms: numbness, insensibility|Antonyms: sensation, feeling|noun
