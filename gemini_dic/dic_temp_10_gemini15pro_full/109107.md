legged:/ˈleɡɪd/ | adj. | 有…条腿的；腿形的 | Having a specified number or kind of legs. |  A four-legged animal. 一只四条腿的动物。 | long-legged, multi-legged | legless | adjective

Etymology->
From Middle English *legged, *leggede, from Old English -lēged (“having legs”), from Proto-Germanic *-lagidaz, from Proto-Indo-European *-logʰ-edos.  Cognate with Dutch -potig, German -beinig.
