mustily:/ˈmʌstɪli/ | adv. | 发霉地，霉臭地 | Having a stale, moldy, or damp smell.  Characterized by a stale, moldy, or damp smell. | The old books smelled mustily, like a forgotten attic.  这些旧书闻起来有股霉味，就像被人遗忘的阁楼。| The basement room was dark and mustily. 地下室房间又黑又霉。 | synonym: fustily, moldily, dankly | antonym: freshly, airily, sweetly | adverb
