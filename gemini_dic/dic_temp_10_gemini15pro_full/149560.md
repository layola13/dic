paginates:/ˈpædʒɪneɪts/
v. |分页，标注页码| To number the pages of (a book, manuscript, etc.).  |The editor paginates the manuscript before sending it to the printer. 编辑在将稿件送到印刷厂之前先标注页码。|近义词: number, foliate |反义词:  N/A
词性: 动词

paginated:/ˈpædʒɪneɪtɪd/
adj. |分页的，标注页码的|Having pages numbered. |The paginated document was easier to reference.  分页后的文件更容易参考。|近义词: numbered |反义词: unnumbered, unpaginated
词性: 形形词

pagination:/ˌpædʒɪˈneɪʃn/
n. |分页，页码|The act or process of paginating something, or the system of numbers used. |The pagination of this book is inconsistent. 这本书的页码不一致。|近义词: numbering, foliation |反义词: N/A
词性: 名词


词源: From Late Latin *paginatus, past participle of *paginare “to arrange in pages,” from Latin pagina “page.”
