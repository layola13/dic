availing:/əˈveɪlɪŋ/
v.
利用，生效，有益于
Taking advantage of something; being of use or advantage.
She wasn't availing herself of the services offered by the library. 她没有利用图书馆提供的服务。
近义词:using, utilizing, benefiting from
反义词:neglecting, ignoring, hindering

availing:/əˈveɪlɪŋ/
adj.
有用的，有效的
Helpful, effective, or of use.
His efforts, though considerable, were not availing. 他的努力虽然很大，但没有效果。
近义词:useful, helpful, effective
反义词:useless, futile, ineffective
