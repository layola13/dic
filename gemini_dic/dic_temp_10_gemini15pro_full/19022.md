beta:/ˈbeɪtə/ (US), /ˈbiːtə/ (UK)| n. adj.|测试版，贝塔系数|The second letter of the Greek alphabet (Β, β), used to denote the second in a series, a test version of a product, or a measure of a stock's volatility relative to the market.|The company released a beta version of their new software to a select group of users.  这家公司向一组选定的用户发布了新软件的测试版。|Synonyms: trial, test, experimental|Antonyms: final, released|Noun, Adjective

beta:/ˈbeɪtə/ (US), /ˈbiːtə/ (UK)| n. |贝塔系数|A measure of a stock's volatility relative to the market.|A beta of 1 indicates that the stock's price will move with the market.|Synonyms: volatility measure, market risk|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
