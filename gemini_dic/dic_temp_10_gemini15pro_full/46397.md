daemon:/ˈdiːmən/ /ˈdeɪmən/| n.|守护进程，后台程序|A computer program that runs as a background process, rather than being under the direct control of an interactive user.|The email server runs as a daemon, constantly checking for new messages.|Synonyms: background process, service, agent|Antonyms: foreground process|Noun

daemon:/ˈdiːmən/ /ˈdeɪmən/| n.|精灵，守护神（常指不好的）|A spirit, often one with malevolent intentions, in ancient Greek mythology.|In some cultures, daemons are believed to be mischievous spirits.|Synonyms: spirit, demon, evil spirit|Antonyms: angel, guardian angel|Noun
