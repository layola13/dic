amputating: /ˈæmpjʊˌteɪtɪŋ/
verb
截肢; 切断
The act of surgically removing a limb or other body part.
The surgeon is amputating the patient's leg.  外科医生正在截除病人的腿。
They had to amputate his arm because of the infection. 由于感染，他们不得不截肢他的手臂。

近义词: severing, excising, removing
反义词: attaching, grafting, joining
词性: verb (present participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "amputare" (to cut around, prune), from "ambi-" (around) + "putare" (to prune, cleanse).
