overpolemically:/ˌoʊvərˌpɒˈlɛmɪkli/ | adv. | 过于论战地，过度争论地 |  Engaging in or characterized by excessive argumentation, often in a heated or aggressive manner.  It implies going beyond reasonable debate and into the realm of unnecessary or unproductive contention. |  He argued his point overpolemically, alienating even his supporters. 他过于论战地争论自己的观点，甚至疏远了他的支持者。|  The blogger's overpolemically charged response only inflamed the situation.  这位博主的过度论战的回应只会加剧局势。 | 近义词: contentiously, aggressively, belligerently, pugnaciously | 反义词: diplomatically, conciliatorily, amicably | 词性: 副词

词源:
"over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "polemic" (noun meaning "a strong verbal or written attack on someone or something") + "-al" (suffix forming adjectives) + "-ly" (suffix forming adverbs)
