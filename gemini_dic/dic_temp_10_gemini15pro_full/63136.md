ensnares:/ɪnˈsneərz/
|v.| 诱捕，使陷入圈套 |To catch or trap something, often in a metaphorical sense.|The spider ensnares its prey in a sticky web. 蜘蛛用粘性的网诱捕猎物。|Synonyms: traps, catches, entangles, |Antonyms: frees, releases, liberates|Verb


ensnare:/ɪnˈsneər/
|v.| 诱捕，使陷入圈套 |To catch or trap something, often in a metaphorical sense.|His charming words ensnared her heart. 他迷人的言语俘获了她的芳心。|Synonyms: traps, catches, entangles, |Antonyms: frees, releases, liberates|Verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From en- (cause to be) + snare (trap).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *snarhō (noose, snare).
