circumnutate:/ˌsɜːrkəmˈnjuːteɪt/ | v. | 盘旋生长，回旋转动 | To move in a circular or spiral course, especially of the growing tip of a stem or tendril. | The vine tendrils circumnutated, searching for something to grasp.  藤蔓卷须盘旋生长，寻找可以抓住的东西。| The sunflower heads circumnutated throughout the day, following the sun's path. 向日葵花盘整天都在回旋转动，跟随太阳的路径。| 近义词: twine, spiral, revolve | 反义词: straighten | 词性: 动词
