parsniplike:/ˈpɑːrsnɪpˌlaɪk/
adjective
像欧洲防风草的；类似欧洲防风草的
Resembling a parsnip in appearance, taste, or some other characteristic.  Having the long, tapering, pale yellowish-white root of a parsnip.
The plant has a parsniplike root.  这种植物的根类似欧洲防风草。

The root vegetable tasted surprisingly parsniplike.  这种根茎蔬菜尝起来竟然像欧洲防风草。

Near synonyms: parsnip-shaped, carroty (in color), tapered
Antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but could use antonyms for "tapered" such as "wide," "broad," "thick")
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "parsnip" + "-like."  "Parsnip" comes from Middle English *passenep,* from Anglo-Norman *pasnaie* (compare Old French *pastenade*),  ultimately from Latin *pastīnāca* (parsnip, carrot). The suffix "-like" is a common English suffix used to form adjectives indicating resemblance.
