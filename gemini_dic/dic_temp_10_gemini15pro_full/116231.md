masquerade:/ˌmæskəˈreɪd/ /ˌmɑːskəˈreɪd/| n. v. | 化装舞会；伪装；掩饰 | A party where people wear masks and costumes.  An attempt to hide something's true nature. |  例句：They attended a grand masquerade ball. 他们参加了一个盛大的化装舞会。| 近义词：disguise, pretense, charade | 反义词：reality, truth, honesty | 词性：名词

masquerade:/ˌmæskəˈreɪd/ /ˌmɑːskəˈreɪd/| v. | 伪装；掩饰 | To pretend to be someone or something that you are not. | 例句：He masqueraded as a police officer. 他伪装成一名警官。| 近义词：pose, impersonate, feign | 反义词：reveal, expose | 词性：动词

词源: From French *mascarade*, from Italian *mascherata*, from *maschera* "mask". Ultimately from Arabic *masḫara* "buffoonery, mockery".
