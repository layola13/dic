molotov:/ˈmɒlətɒv/ (UK) /ˈmɑːləˌtɔːf/ (US)| n. | 莫洛托夫鸡尾酒；燃烧瓶 |A crude incendiary device typically consisting of a bottle filled with flammable liquid and with a means of ignition such as a soaked, burning rag.  It is named after Vyacheslav Molotov, the Soviet foreign minister during World War II. |  The protesters hurled Molotov cocktails at the police. 抗议者向警察投掷燃烧瓶。|  The rioters armed themselves with Molotov cocktails and rocks. 暴徒用燃烧瓶和石块武装自己。 | synonyms: petrol bomb, firebomb, incendiary device | antonyms: fire extinguisher, fire suppressant | noun
