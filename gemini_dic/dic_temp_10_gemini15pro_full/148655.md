overthwart:/ˌoʊvərˈθwɔːrt/ | v. | 阻挠，阻碍，横阻 | To oppose and defeat the plans or efforts of someone or something. | The government's attempts to overthwart the protests only fueled public anger. 政府试图阻挠抗议活动，但这反而激起了公众的愤怒。

The heavy rain overthwarted our plans for a picnic.  大雨阻碍了我们的野餐计划。

| 近义词: frustrate, foil, thwart, obstruct, hinder | 反义词: aid, assist, help, support, facilitate | 词性: 动词
