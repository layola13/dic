idiomuscular:/ˌɪdioʊˈmʌskjʊlər/
adjective
肌内的; 局部肌肉的
Relating to or involving a muscle itself, rather than its nerve supply.  A localized muscular contraction that is directly stimulated mechanically.
The doctor tested for idiomuscular contraction by tapping the patient's muscle. 医生通过轻敲病人的肌肉来测试局部肌肉收缩。
Idiomuscular response can be elicited by percussion of the belly of a muscle. 通过敲击肌肉腹部可以引出局部肌肉反应。
synonyms: intramuscular
antonyms: (No direct antonyms, but consider terms like "neuromuscular" or "systemic")
词性: adjective
