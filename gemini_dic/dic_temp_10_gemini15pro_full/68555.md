fang:/fæŋ/| n.| 犬齿；尖牙；毒牙 |A long, pointed tooth, especially a canine tooth of a dog or wolf, or a similar tooth of a snake or other animal. |The wolf bared its fangs. 狼露出了它的獠牙。|Synonyms: tooth, canine, tusk|Antonyms: none|noun

fang:/fæŋ/| v. (fanged, fanging) | (用牙)咬；给…装尖牙| To bite or seize with fangs. |The snake fanged its prey. 蛇用毒牙咬住了猎物。 |Synonyms: bite, seize, grip|Antonyms: release|verb
