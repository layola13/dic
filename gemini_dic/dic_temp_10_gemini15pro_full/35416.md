co-ordinateness:/koʊˈɔːrdɪnɪtnɪs/| n.|协调性，同等性|The co-ordinateness of their movements was remarkable. 他们的动作协调性很出色。|例句：The dancers displayed impressive co-ordinateness. 舞者们展现了令人印象深刻的协调性。

The co-ordinateness of the different departments is essential for the success of the project.  不同部门之间的协调对于项目的成功至关重要。|近义词：harmony, synchronization, coherence, unity|反义词：discord, disharmony, incongruity|词性: 名词

Etymology:
From co-ordinate + -ness.  Co-ordinate itself comes from Late Latin coordinatus, past participle of coordinare "to arrange together," from com- "together" + ordinare "to arrange."
