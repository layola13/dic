integropallial:/ɪnˌtɛɡroʊˈpæliəl/| adj.|完整外套膜的；具有完整外套膜的|Having a continuous, unbroken pallial line, referring to the scar left by the attachment of the mantle to the shell in bivalve mollusks.|The integropallial condition of the shell indicates that the clam did not have siphons.|近义词:entire, complete (referring to the pallial line)|反义词:sinupallial|词性:形容词

词源:

* **integro-**:  from Latin *integer*, meaning "whole" or "complete."
* **pallial**: from Latin *pallium*, meaning "mantle" or "cloak," referring to the mantle of a mollusk.
