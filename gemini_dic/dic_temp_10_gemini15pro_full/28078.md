capillaceous:/ˌkæpɪˈleɪʃəs/
adjective
毛细管的; 毛状的; 细如毛发的
Resembling a hair; fine and slender.  Characterized by or consisting of threadlike structures.
The plant has capillaceous leaves. 这种植物有毛细管状的叶子。
Synonyms: hairlike, filamentous, threadlike, capillary
Antonyms: thick, coarse
词性: 形容词
