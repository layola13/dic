antidysenteric:/ˌæntiˌdɪsənˈtɛrɪk/
adjective
抗痢的，治疗痢疾的
Preventing or curing dysentery.
例句：The doctor prescribed antidysenteric medication. 医生开了抗痢疾的药。

例句：Traditional remedies often include antidysenteric herbs.  传统的治疗方法通常包括抗痢疾的草药。

近义词：antidiarrheal
反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider "dysentery-causing" or "dysentery-promoting")
词性 adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- (against) + dysentery (diarrhea, especially with blood)
