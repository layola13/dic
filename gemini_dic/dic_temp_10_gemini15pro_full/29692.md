catho:/ˈkæθoʊ/| n.| cathode |阴极|The cathode is the negatively charged electrode.|阴极是带负电的电极。|Example: Electrons flow from the anode to the cathode in a circuit. 电路中电子从阳极流向阴极。

Example:  A heated cathode emits electrons.  加热的阴极会发射电子。|近义词: negative electrode|反义词: anode (阳极)|词性: noun

词源: From Greek *kathodos* "a going down, descent," from *kata* "down" + *hodos* "way."  This referred originally to the electrode at which the sun appears to set (downward movement), since positive charge conventionally flows in that direction.
