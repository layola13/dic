anticonservationist:/ˌæntiˌkɑːnsərˈveɪʃənɪst/
n.
反环保主义者，反对保护环境的人
Someone who opposes environmental conservation efforts.
The anticonservationist lobby argued against the new national park proposal.  反环保主义游说团体反对新的国家公园提案。
The developer, known for his anticonservationist views, continued to build on protected wetlands.  这位以反环保主义观点闻名的开发商继续在受保护的湿地上进行建设。
近义词: environmental opponent, anti-environmentalist
反义词: conservationist, environmentalist
词性: noun
