parathecium:/ˌpærəˈθiːsiəm/
noun
子囊壳周壁，囊层周壁
The outer wall of a perithecium, enclosing the asci and paraphyses.
The parathecium of this species is particularly thick and robust.  这个物种的子囊壳周壁特别厚实而坚固。
Synonyms: perithecial wall, outer perithecial wall
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, potentially inner perithecial wall if discussing layers)
