mispointing:/ˌmɪsˈpɔɪntɪŋ/
n.|误指，指向错误|The mispointing of the sign caused confusion among tourists. 路标指错方向导致游客混乱。
|例句：The mispointing of the compass led them astray. 指南针的指向错误使他们迷路了。|近义词：misdirection, misguidance|反义词: accurate pointing, correct guidance|词性:名词
mispointing:/ˌmɪsˈpɔɪntɪŋ/
v.|错误地指向，指错方向|The surveyor mispointed the boundary marker. 测量员错误地标出了边界标记。|例句：He mispointed the telescope, and we missed the comet. 他把望远镜指向了错误的方向，我们错过了彗星。|近义词：misdirect, misguide|反义词: point accurately, guide correctly|词性:动词
