biosecurity:/ˌbaɪoʊsɪˈkjʊrɪti/
n.|生物安全|Measures taken to protect against the spread of harmful biological agents, such as diseases, invasive species, and bioweapons.|例句：Biosecurity measures are crucial to prevent the spread of avian flu. 生物安全措施对于防止禽流感的传播至关重要。

例句：The farm has strict biosecurity protocols in place.  这个农场制定了严格的生物安全协议。
|近义词：biosafety, biodefense|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider vulnerability)|词性:noun
