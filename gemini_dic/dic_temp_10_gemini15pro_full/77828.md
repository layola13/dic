generativenesses:/ˌdʒɛnərəˈtɪvnɪsɪz/ | n. | 生成性，创造性（复数） | The quality or state of being generative; the capacity to generate, produce, or create, especially new ideas or forms. |  The artist's generativenesses were evident in the diverse range of her work. 这位艺术家的创造性在她各种各样的作品中显而易见。| Synonyms: creativity, productivity, fertility, fecundity | Antonyms: barrenness, sterility, unproductiveness | noun


Etymology:  From generative + -ness + -es (plural suffix). Ultimately from Latin *generativus*, from generare "to beget, produce," from genus "birth, race, stock, kind."
