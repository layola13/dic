frondose:/ˈfrɒndoʊs/ | adj. | 多叶的，枝叶繁茂的 | Having many fronds; leafy; luxuriant. |  The rainforest was dense and frondose, with sunlight struggling to reach the forest floor. 雨林茂密而枝繁叶茂，阳光难以照射到森林地面。

The fern grew frondose and vibrant in the humid greenhouse.  这株蕨类植物在潮湿的温室里生长得枝繁叶茂，充满生机。| 近义词: leafy, luxuriant, verdant | 反义词: sparse, bare, leafless | 词性: 形容词

Etymology: From Latin *frondōsus, from frōns (“leaf, foliage”) + -ōsus (suffix forming adjectives indicating fullness or abundance).
