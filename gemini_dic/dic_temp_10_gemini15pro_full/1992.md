acts: /ækts/
verb
行为，举动；(戏剧的)幕；法令，条例
The play has five acts.  这部戏剧有五幕。
Congress passed a series of acts to protect the environment. 国会通过了一系列保护环境的法案。
例句：
His acts of kindness were appreciated by everyone. = 他的善举受到了大家的赞赏。

She always acts without thinking. = 她总是做事不经思考。

近义词: actions, deeds, laws, statutes, performs, behaves
反义词: inaction, refrains
词性: verb (plural form of "act")

acts: /ækts/
noun
行为，举动；(戏剧的)幕；法令，条例
The charity's acts of generosity helped many people.  慈善机构的慷慨行为帮助了很多人。
The first act of the play was rather slow.  这出戏的第一幕节奏相当慢。
例句：
Her heroic acts saved many lives. = 她的英雄行为拯救了许多生命。

The government passed new acts to control pollution. = 政府通过了新的法令来控制污染。

近义词: actions, deeds, laws, statutes
反义词: omissions
词性: noun (plural form of "act")
