confoundedly:/kənˈfaʊndɪdli/
adverb
极其地，非常地，讨厌地
extremely, very, annoyingly
The train was confoundedly slow.  这火车慢得要命。
近义词: awfully, terribly, extremely, excessively
反义词: slightly, mildly
词性: adverb

词源: From confound + -ed + -ly.  Confound comes from the Old French confondre, from Latin confundere "to pour together, mix, mingle, confuse," from com- "together" + fundere "to pour."
