overbrood:/ˌoʊvərˈbruːd/ | v. | 过度担忧；过分沉思；(鸟)伏巢孵卵 | To think anxiously about something; to worry excessively, especially about something in the past that cannot be changed; (of a bird) to sit on (eggs) to hatch them. | She overbrooded her failure in the exam. 她过度担忧自己考试不及格。 | The hen overbrooded her eggs. 母鸡伏在蛋上孵蛋。 | synonyms: worry, fret, stew, agonize, ruminate | antonyms: disregard, ignore, neglect | verb
