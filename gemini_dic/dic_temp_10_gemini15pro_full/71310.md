fizzer:/ˈfɪzər/| n.|嘶嘶声，失败，汽水|A fizzer is something that starts out with a lot of excitement or promise but ends up being a disappointment. It can also refer to a carbonated drink.|The new product launch was a total fizzer.  新产品的发布完全失败了。|The soda was a refreshing fizzer on a hot day.  这汽水在炎热的天气里真是清爽。|近义词：flop, failure, dud, carbonated drink|反义词:success, triumph|noun
