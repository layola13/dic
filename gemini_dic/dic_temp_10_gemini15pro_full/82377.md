groyne:/ɡrɔɪn/ | n. | 防波堤，突堤 | A short wall or other structure built out from the shore to protect the land from erosion, or to trap sand and shingle and prevent the movement of beach material by longshore drift. |例句：The groynes helped to stabilize the beach and prevent erosion. 这些突堤有助于稳定海滩并防止侵蚀。

例句：Several wooden groynes have been constructed along the coastline.  沿着海岸线建造了几个木质防波堤。 | 近义词：breakwater, jetty, seawall, mole | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 erosion) | 词性: 名词

groynes:/ɡrɔɪnz/ | n. | 防波堤，突堤（复数） | Plural form of groyne. |例句：The groynes are effective in reducing coastal erosion. 这些防波堤能有效减少海岸侵蚀。

例句：New groynes were built after the severe storms.  在强风暴之后，建造了新的防波堤。| 近义词：breakwaters, jetties, seawalls, moles | 反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 erosion) | 词性: 名词
