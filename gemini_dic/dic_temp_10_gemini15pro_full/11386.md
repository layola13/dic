argal:/ˈɑːrɡəl/| conj.|因此，所以|Archaic or humorous form of ergo.  Often used humorously to indicate a falsely logical or far-fetched conclusion. |"The door was locked, the windows were shut, and the key was inside; argal, it was impossible to get into the room." “门锁着，窗户关着，钥匙在里面；因此，不可能进入房间。”|近义词：therefore, consequently, thus, hence, ergo|反义词：however, nevertheless, yet, but|连词
