disingenuously:/ˌdɪsɪnˈdʒɛnjuəsli/
| adv. | 不真诚地，虚伪地 |  In a manner that lacks sincerity or is deliberately misleading. | He disingenuously claimed to know nothing about the incident. 他假惺惺地声称对这件事一无所知。 | Example:  She disingenuously praised her rival's work, hoping to gain her trust. 她虚情假意地赞扬对手的作品，希望获得她的信任。 | Synonyms:  dishonestly, falsely, hypocritically, insincerely | Antonyms: genuinely, honestly, sincerely | 词性: 副词

disingenuous:/ˌdɪsɪnˈdʒɛnjuəs/
| adj. |  不真诚的，虚伪的 | Not candid or sincere, typically by pretending that one knows less about something than one really does. |  His disingenuous apology fooled no one. 他不真诚的道歉骗不了任何人。 | Example: It was disingenuous of him to pretend he hadn't seen the report. 他假装没看过报告，真是虚伪。 | Synonyms: deceitful, dishonest, insincere, untruthful | Antonyms:  frank, genuine, honest, sincere | 词性: 形容词
