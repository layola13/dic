citibank: /ˈsɪtiˌbæŋk/ | n. | 花旗银行 | A major international bank, formed by the merger of Citicorp and Travelers Group in 1998. | I have a savings account at Citibank. 我在花旗银行有一个储蓄账户. | 近义词: bank, financial institution | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

词源: "Citibank" is a shortened form of "City Bank of New York," reflecting its origins in New York City.  The "Citi" prefix became widely used in the company's branding and eventually became the official name.
