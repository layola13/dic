engage:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒ/ (US), /ɛnˈɡeɪdʒ/ (UK)| v.  n.| 参与，从事；雇佣；吸引；订婚|To involve oneself or become occupied; to hire; to attract and hold the attention of; to promise to marry.|I engaged in a conversation with my neighbor. 我和我的邻居聊了起来。|近义词：involve, participate, employ, hire, captivate, attract|反义词:disengage, detach, ignore, release|verb

engage:/ɪnˈɡeɪdʒ/ (US), /ɛnˈɡeɪdʒ/ (UK)| n.| 约定，契约；交战|An agreement or contract; a battle or conflict.|The two armies met in a brief engage. 两军短暂交锋。|近义词：agreement, commitment, battle, encounter|反义词:disengagement, retreat|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *engager (“to pledge, pawn”), from en- (“in, into”) + gage (“pledge, security”).
