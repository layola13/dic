ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + talkative + -ness + -es (plural).  "Over-" indicates excess. "Talkative" comes from "talk" (from Middle English *talken*, of uncertain origin) + "-ative" (from Latin -ativus). "-ness" forms nouns indicating a state or quality.  "-es" creates the plural form of the noun.
