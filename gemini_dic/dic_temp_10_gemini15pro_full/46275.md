czarism: /ˈzɑːrɪzəm/ | n. | 沙皇统治；专制统治 | A system of government by a czar; autocratic rule. |  The Romanov dynasty's czarism lasted for over three centuries.  罗曼诺夫王朝的沙皇统治持续了三个多世纪。

| Example:  The downfall of czarism in Russia paved the way for the rise of communism.  俄国沙皇统治的垮台为共产主义的兴起铺平了道路。| Synonym:  autocracy, despotism, absolutism | Antonym: democracy, republic | Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Russian царь (carĭ, “czar”), from Old Church Slavonic царь (carĭ), from Latin Caesar, from the Roman cognomen Caesar.
