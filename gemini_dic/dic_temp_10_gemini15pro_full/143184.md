outcompass:/ˌaʊtkəmˈpəs/
verb
超过，胜过
to surpass in compass or scope; exceed
例句：His knowledge outcompassed mine. 他的知识超过了我。
例句：The company's influence outcompassed its size. 这家公司的影响力超过了它的规模。
近义词：surpass, exceed, outstrip, outdo, transcend
反义词：trail, lag behind, fall short of
词性：v.
