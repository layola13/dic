cesspit:/ˈsespɪt/| n.|污水池，粪坑；藏污纳垢的地方|A cesspit is an underground container for the temporary storage of liquid waste and sewage.| The smell from the cesspit was overwhelming.  污水池散发出的气味令人难以忍受。  The investigation revealed the town was a cesspit of corruption. 调查显示，该镇是一个腐败的污水池。|近义词：cesspool, sump|反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider "clean environment," "moral place") |词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *sespitte, from Old French *cespit, from Latin *cesspittus, from cessare (“to cease, stop, come to rest”) + pit (“pit, hole”).  The notion is of a place where waste "rests."
