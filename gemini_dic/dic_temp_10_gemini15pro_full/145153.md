overassessing:/ˌoʊvərəˈsesɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
过度评估；过高估计
Evaluating something more thoroughly than necessary, often leading to an inflated or inaccurate perception of its value or importance.
He's overassessing the risk; it's not as dangerous as he thinks. 他高估了风险；没有他想的那么危险。
The company was overassessing the value of its assets.  这家公司过高估计了其资产的价值。

近义词: overestimating, overvaluing
反义词: underestimating, undervaluing
词性: verb (present participle)

词源:
From over- + assessing, the present participle of assess (from Old French assesser "to sit beside, to fix the amount of (a tax)," from Latin assidere "to sit by," from ad- "to" + sedere "to sit").
