bowdlerize: /ˈboʊdləˌraɪz/ (US), /ˈbaʊdləˌraɪz/ (UK) | v. | 删改，净化，（尤指）删除（书籍或剧本中）被认为不道德的内容 | To remove material that is considered improper or offensive from (a text or account), especially with the result that the text becomes weaker or less effective. |  Example: The publisher bowdlerized the novel before releasing it to a younger audience. 出版社在将小说发行给年轻读者之前删改了其中的内容。

Example:  They bowdlerized the play so much that it lost its original meaning.  他们对这部戏剧进行了大量的删改，以至于它失去了原有的意义。


|近义词: censor, expurgate, sanitize |反义词:  unexpurged |词性: verb
