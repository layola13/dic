devanagari: /ˌdeɪvəˈnɑːɡəri/ | n. | 天城文; देवनागरी | Devanagari is an abugida alphabet of India and Nepal.  It is used to write Sanskrit, Hindi, Marathi, Nepali and other languages. | The Devanagari script is written from left to right. 天城文是从左往右书写的。|  Sanskrit, Hindi, Marathi, Nepali and other languages. | None | proper noun
Etymology: From Sanskrit  देवनागरी (devanāgarī, literally "city of the gods' writing").
