hereditament:/ˌhɛrɪˈdɪtəm(ə)nt/
n.
可继承财产；遗产
Property that can be inherited.
例句：The old manor house was a valuable hereditament. 这座古老的庄园是一笔宝贵的遗产。

例句：The farm, including all buildings and livestock, was considered a hereditament.  这个农场，包括所有的建筑物和牲畜，都被认为是遗产。


近义词：inheritance, legacy, patrimony
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider) debt, liability
词性: noun
