merenchyma:/ˈmɛrənˌkaɪmə/ | n. | 薄壁组织 | A tissue of plants composed of thin-walled living cells that function in photosynthesis, storage, secretion, and other processes. |  Merenchyma cells make up the soft parts of plants, such as the cortex and pith. 薄壁组织细胞构成了植物的柔软部分，例如皮层和髓。 | parenchyma | sclerenchyma, collenchyma | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek  μέρος (méros, “part”) + ἔγχυμα (énchyma, “infusion”).
