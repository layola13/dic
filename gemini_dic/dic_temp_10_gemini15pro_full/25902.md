burmite:/ˈbɜːrmaɪt/
n.
缅甸琥珀
a fossilized resin from Myanmar, often containing inclusions of insects, plants, and other organisms.
Burmite often contains well-preserved insects. 缅甸琥珀通常包含保存完好的昆虫。
The museum's collection includes a rare piece of burmite with a lizard inclusion.  博物馆的藏品中有一块罕见的带有蜥蜴内含物的缅甸琥珀。
近义词: amber
反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific type of amber)
词性: noun
