exsuccous:/ˌɛksˈsʌkəs/
| adj. | 无汁的，干的 | Lacking sap or juice; dry. | The exsuccous leaves crumbled in my hand.  这些干枯的叶子在我手中碎裂了。| The desert plants had exsuccous leaves, an adaptation to conserve water. 这些沙漠植物的叶子是干枯的，这是为了保存水分的一种适应。 | 近义词：dry, arid, desiccated, juiceless | 反义词：succulent, juicy, moist | 词性: 形容词
词源:  From Latin *exsuccus, from ex- 'out of' + succus 'juice, sap'.
