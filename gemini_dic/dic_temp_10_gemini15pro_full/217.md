abbreviators:/əˈbriːvieɪtərz/| n.|缩写者，使用缩写的人|People who use abbreviations are abbreviators. 使用缩写的人被称为缩写者。|The abbreviators saved space on the form by using initials.  缩写者通过使用首字母缩写在表格上节省了空间。|synonyms: shorteners, condensers|antonyms: expanders, elaborators|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From abbreviate (v.) + -or (suffix forming nouns denoting an agent).  Abbreviate comes from Late Latin abbreviatus, past participle of abbreviare "to shorten," from Latin ad- "to" + brevis "short."
