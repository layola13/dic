blagged:/blæɡd/| v. |骗取，哄骗，（未经允许）使用；（英国非正式）成功完成（困难的事情）|To obtain something by using clever talk and persuasion, often dishonestly.  (British informal) To succeed in doing something difficult.|I blagged a couple of free tickets to the concert. 我骗到了几张免费的音乐会门票。|Synonyms: wheedle, coax, charm, finagle, wangle (British informal), swing (informal)|Antonyms: earn, deserve, pay for|verb
