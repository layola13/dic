bairam: /ˈbaɪrəm/ (US), /ˈbaɪræm/ (UK) | n. | 节日，(尤指穆斯林的)开斋节或宰牲节 | A Muslim festival, especially Eid al-Fitr or Eid al-Adha, marking the end of Ramadan or the culmination of the annual pilgrimage to Mecca. |  We celebrated Bairam with a feast and family gatherings. 我们和家人聚在一起庆祝开斋节。 |  Eid, festival | holiday | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Turkish *bayram*, from Ottoman Turkish *بايرام* (*bayrām*), from Persian *بایرام* (*bayrām*). Ultimately from Turkic.
