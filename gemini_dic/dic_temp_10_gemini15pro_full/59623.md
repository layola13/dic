ecclesia: /ɪˈkliːziə/ (US), /ɪˈkliːʒə/ (UK)| n.|教会；集会；集合体|A gathering of citizens called out from their homes into some public place, an assembly.  Specifically, the Church, the assembly of the faithful.  Derived from Greek, meaning "called out" or "called forth".|The ecclesia met to discuss important matters. 教会开会讨论重要事项。|Synonyms: church, congregation, assembly, gathering|Antonyms: dispersal, scattering|noun
