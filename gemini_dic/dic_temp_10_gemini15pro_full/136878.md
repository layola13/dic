nonsoberest:/ˌnɒnˈsəʊbərɪst/ | adj. | 最不清醒的 |  Superlative form of 'nonsober', meaning least sober or most intoxicated.  Derived from the prefix "non-" (not) and the adjective "sober".  | Example: He was the nonsoberest person at the party, stumbling around and slurring his words. 他是聚会上最不清醒的人，踉踉跄跄，说话含糊不清。| Example: Of all the guests, she was by far the nonsoberest. 在所有客人中，她显然是最不清醒的。 | Synonym: drunkest, most intoxicated | Antonym: soberest, most clear-headed | Word Type: Adjective
