earthward:/ˈɜːθwəd/ /ˈɝːθwərd/| adj. adv.|朝向地球的，向地球的|Moving or directed toward the earth.|The satellite began its earthward descent. 卫星开始向地球下降。|近义词：downward, groundward|反义词: upward, skyward|词性:形容词/副词

Etymology:
From "earth" + "-ward" (a suffix indicating direction).  "Earth" comes from Old English "eorþe," meaning "ground, soil, dry land," ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*erþō," related to Dutch "aarde" and German "Erde."  The suffix "-ward" comes from Old English "-weard," meaning "toward, in the direction of."
