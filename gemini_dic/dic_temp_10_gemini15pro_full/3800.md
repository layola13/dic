agnition:/æɡˈnɪʃən/ | n. | 认知; 承认; 认识 | The process of knowing, or being aware of something.  Acknowledgement, recognition. | He showed no agnition of his mistakes. 他对自己的错误没有丝毫认识。 | 近义词: recognition, acknowledgement, awareness, perception | 反义词: ignorance, denial | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From Late Latin *agnitionem, accusative singular of *agnitio (“recognition, acknowledgment”), from Latin agnitus, past participle of agnoscere (“to recognize, acknowledge”), from ad- (“to”) + gnoscere (“to know”). Related to cognition.
