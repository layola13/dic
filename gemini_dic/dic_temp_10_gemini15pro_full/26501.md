caballeros:/ˌkæbəˈljɛroʊz/ (US), /ˌkæbəˈljɛərəʊz/ (UK)| n.|先生们，绅士们|A Spanish word meaning "gentlemen" or "knights," often used as a polite form of address to a group of men.|Caballeros, welcome to our fiesta! 先生们，欢迎来到我们的节日！|近义词：gentlemen, sirs|反义词: ladies, women|noun
