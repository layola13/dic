overcomplacent:/ˌoʊvər.kəmˈpleɪ.sənt/
adjective
过于自满的，沾沾自喜的
Excessively self-satisfied; smug and unaware of potential dangers or deficiencies.
The team became overcomplacent after their initial victories and ended up losing the championship.
这个团队在最初的胜利后变得过于自满，最终输掉了冠军。

They were overcomplacent about security measures. 他们对安全措施过于自满。

近义词:smug, complacent, self-satisfied
反义词:concerned, vigilant, apprehensive
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "over-" (excessive) + "complacent" (self-satisfied). "Complacent" comes from Latin "complacere" (to please greatly), from "com-" (with, together) + "placere" (to please).
