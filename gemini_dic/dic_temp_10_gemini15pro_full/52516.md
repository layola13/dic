differentiation:/ˌdɪfəˌrenʃiˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 分化，区别，变异 | the process of becoming different or of making something different | Cell differentiation is essential for the development of complex organisms. 细胞分化对于复杂生物体的发育至关重要。| synonyms: distinction, discrimination, separation | antonyms: similarity, uniformity | noun

differentiate:/ˌdɪfəˈrenʃiˌeɪt/ | v. | 区分，区别，使不同，求微分 | to recognize or show that two things are not the same; to make or become different | It's difficult to differentiate between the two species.  很难区分这两个物种。| synonyms: distinguish, discriminate, separate | antonyms: confuse, assimilate | verb
