defervescence:/ˌdiːfərˈvɛsəns/| n. |退烧，热度下降|The abatement of a fever.  The period when a fever subsides.|After taking the medicine, the patient experienced a welcome defervescence.|Synonyms: abatement, decline, remission, subsiding|Antonyms: fever, increase, rise|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *defervescentia, from Latin defervescere "to cool down, cease boiling," from de- "down, away" + fervescere "to begin to boil, grow hot," from fervere "to boil, glow."
