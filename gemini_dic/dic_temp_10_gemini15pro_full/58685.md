dumpage:/ˈdʌmpɪdʒ/| n.|倾倒物，垃圾堆，倾倒的行为|The illegal dumpage of toxic waste is a serious environmental crime. 非法倾倒有毒废物是一项严重的环保犯罪。|近义词：dump, waste, refuse, rubbish, trash|反义词: collection, recycling|noun

Etymology:  From "dump" (verb meaning "to deposit or unload abruptly and carelessly") + "-age" (suffix forming nouns denoting an action, process, or result). "Dump" ultimately originates from a Scandinavian source, possibly Old Norse *dumpa.
