come-to-your-senses:/kʌm tu jɔːr ˈsɛnsɪz/
verb phrase
恢复理智；醒悟过来
To begin to think and behave reasonably after a period of folly or irrationality.
He finally came to his senses and realized he was making a huge mistake. 他终于醒悟过来，意识到自己犯了一个巨大的错误。

After weeks of denial, she came to her senses and accepted the truth.  几周的否认之后，她终于恢复了理智，接受了事实。

近义词: regain your composure, see reason, think clearly, wise up, sober up
反义词: lose your mind, go crazy, act irrationally
词性: verb phrase
