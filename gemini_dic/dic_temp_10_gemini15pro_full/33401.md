churchier:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃiər/
adj.
更像教堂的；更虔诚的；更具宗教色彩的
More resembling a church; more devout or religious.
The new building was churchier than the old one, with stained glass windows and a tall spire.  新建筑比旧建筑更像教堂，有彩色玻璃窗和高高的尖顶。
He's become much churchier in recent years, attending services every Sunday.  近年来，他变得更加虔诚，每个星期天都去教堂做礼拜。
近义词:more religious, more devout, pious, saintly
反义词:secular, worldly, irreligious, impious
词性:adjective

词源:
From church + -ier (comparative adjective suffix).  源自 church + -ier（比较级形容词后缀）。
