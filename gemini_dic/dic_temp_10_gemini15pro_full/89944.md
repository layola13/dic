horsemanship:/ˈhɔːrsmənʃɪp/ | n. |骑术；马术|The art or skill of riding and handling horses.  The ability to ride and control a horse well. |She demonstrated impressive horsemanship in the competition. 她在比赛中展现了令人印象深刻的骑术。|His horsemanship is legendary. 他的骑术堪称传奇。|Synonyms: equitation, riding skill |Antonyms: incompetence (in riding) |Noun
