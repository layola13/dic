bourrée:/bʊˈreɪ/ (US), /ˈbʊreɪ/ (UK)| n.|布雷舞|A lively French folk dance and accompanying music, typically in duple meter with an upbeat.  It is also used as a ballet step.|The band played a lively bourrée.  乐队演奏了一曲轻快的布雷舞曲。|Synonyms: jig, gavotte, reel|Antonyms: waltz, slow dance|noun

bourrée:/bʊˈreɪ/ (US), /ˈbʊreɪ/ (UK)| n.| (芭蕾舞步)|A ballet step consisting of quick, even movements, often performed in a series. |The ballerina executed a series of perfect bourrées. 芭蕾舞女演员表演了一系列完美的布雷舞步。|Synonyms: pas de bourrée|Antonyms: N/A|noun
