pallid:/ˈpælɪd/| adj.|无生气的，平淡乏味的|Lacking vigor or intensity.|He gave a pallid performance. 他的表演毫无生气。|Near Synonyms: dull, lifeless, insipid, vapid|Antonyms: lively, vibrant, exciting, stimulating|adjective
