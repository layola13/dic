effranchise:/ɪnˈfræntʃaɪz/ /ɛnˈfræntʃaɪz/
|vt.|给予选举权；解放；赋予特权|To grant the right to vote to; to free, as from slavery or bondage; to endow with a franchise or privilege.|The Nineteenth Amendment enfranchised women in the United States.  美国宪法第十九修正案赋予了美国妇女选举权。

The slaves were enfranchised after the Civil War. 内战后奴隶们获得了自由。|近义词：liberate, empower, free|反义词：disenfranchise, enslave, restrict|词性: verb
