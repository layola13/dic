ambulator:/ˈæmbjʊleɪtər/| n.|步行者，能够行走的人；流动诊所|A person who is able to walk; a place where medical treatment is provided to people who are not admitted to a hospital. |The ambulator was able to walk around the park easily. 这个步行者能够轻松地在公园里走动。|The hospital has a busy ambulator for patients who need minor procedures. 这家医院有一个繁忙的流动诊所，为需要做小手术的病人提供服务。|Synonyms: walker, outpatient clinic|Antonyms: bedridden, inpatient|noun
