delightfully:/dɪˈlaɪtfəli/ | adv. | 令人愉快地，怡人地 | In a manner that gives great pleasure or enjoyment. | The food was delightfully spicy.  这食物辣得令人愉快。 |  enjoyably, agreeably, appealingly |  disagreeably, unpleasantly, awfully | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From delight + -fully. Delight comes from the Old French deleiter, from the Latin delectare "to allure, delight," frequentative of delicere "to entice."
