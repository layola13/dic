misgrades:/mɪsˈɡreɪdz/
| vt. n.|错误评分，打错分数; 错误等级; | To assign an incorrect grade, especially a lower one than deserved.  Giving a wrong classification or ranking. The act of misgrading or the result of being misgraded. | The professor misgraded my exam, giving me a C instead of an A. 教授把我的考试成绩评错了，给了我C而不是A。|近义词：undergrade, mark down incorrectly|反义词: overgrade, grade correctly|词性: 动词, 名词

misgrade:/mɪsˈɡreɪd/
| n.|错误的等级; 错误的分数;|An incorrect grade or classification. |The misgrade on my transcript affected my GPA. 我成绩单上的错误分数影响了我的GPA。|近义词：error, mistake, inaccuracy|反义词: accurate grade, correct mark|词性: 名词
