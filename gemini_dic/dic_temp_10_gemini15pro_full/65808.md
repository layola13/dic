evidentiary:/ˌevɪˈdɛnʃəri/ /ˌevɪˈdɛnʃiəri/
adjective
证据的；关于证据的
Serving as or based on evidence.
例句：The evidentiary hearing will determine if there is enough evidence for a trial.  证据听证会将决定是否有足够的证据进行审判。

例句：The prosecutor presented evidentiary photographs to the jury.  检察官向陪审团出示了证据照片。

近义词：probative, demonstrative, substantiating
反义词：inconclusive, unsubstantiated
词性：adjective

词源：From Late Latin *ēvidentiārius, from Latin ēvidēns (“evident”).
