overcredited:/ˌoʊvərˈkrɛdɪtɪd/
adjective
过誉的，给予过多信誉的
Having received more credit or recognition than is deserved.
例句：The overcredited actor struggled to live up to the hype.  这位被过誉的演员难以达到人们的期望。

例句：The company was overcredited for its role in the project's success.  这家公司在项目成功中所起的作用被过分夸大了。
synonym:overrated, overpraised, overvalued
antonym:underrated, undervalued, underappreciated
word type:adjective
