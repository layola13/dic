frisson:/ˈfriːsɔːn/ (US), /ˈfrɪsɒn/ (UK)| n. |寒颤；激动；震颤|A sudden, brief sensation of excitement, fear, or thrill; a shiver or shudder.|例句：A frisson of excitement ran through the crowd as the music began. 音乐响起时，人群中掠过一阵激动。

例句：She felt a frisson of fear as she walked through the dark alley. 当她穿过黑暗的小巷时，感到一阵恐惧。|近义词：shiver, thrill, shudder, tingle|反义词: apathy, indifference |词性:noun
