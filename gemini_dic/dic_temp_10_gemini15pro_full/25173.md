bucolic:/bjuːˈkɒlɪk/ /buːˈkɑːlɪk/| adj.|田园的，乡村的，牧歌式的|Relating to the pleasant aspects of the countryside and country life.|The painting evoked a bucolic scene of farmhands harvesting wheat in a sun-drenched field. 这幅画描绘了一幅阳光普照的田野里农民收割小麦的田园景象。|Synonyms: rustic, pastoral, idyllic, rural|Antonyms: urban, metropolitan, industrial|adjective
