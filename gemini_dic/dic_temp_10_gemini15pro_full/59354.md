earmarkings:/ˈɪərmɑːrkɪŋz/
n.
专款；指定用途的款项；标记
funds or resources that are set aside or designated for a specific purpose or project; the act of setting aside funds for a specific purpose.
The government has announced earmarkings for education and healthcare. 政府已经宣布了教育和医疗保健的专款。
The earmarkings for the new hospital were approved by the council. 新医院的拨款得到了委员会的批准。
近义词:allocations, appropriations, designations
反义词:general funds
