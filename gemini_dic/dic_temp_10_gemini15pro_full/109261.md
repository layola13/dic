lemmata:/ˈlɛmətə/| n. |词目，词元|A headword in a dictionary; a base form of a word, especially one with different inflected forms.|The lemma for "ran," "runs," and "running" is "run."  词元“ran”、“runs”和“running”的词目是“run”。|synonyms: headword, keyword, entry, base form|antonyms: inflection, derivative|noun
