overdraws:/ˌoʊvərˈdrɔːz/ /ˌəʊvəˈdrɔːz/| v. (overdraws, overdrawing, overdrew, overdrawn)|透支；超出信用额度|To draw money from (one's bank account) in excess of the credit balance.|He overdrew his account by $200. 他透支了200美元。|Synonyms: overspend, exhaust|Antonyms: deposit, save|verb

overdraft:/ˈoʊvərˌdræft/ /ˈəʊvəˌdrɑːft/| n.|透支；透支额|An act of overdrawing a bank account.|I have an overdraft of $500. 我有500美元的透支额。|Synonyms: overspending, deficit|Antonyms: deposit, credit|noun

overdrawn:/ˌoʊvərˈdrɔːn/ /ˌəʊvəˈdrɔːn/| adj.|透支的|Having drawn more money from a bank account than is available.|My account is overdrawn. 我的账户透支了。|Synonyms: overspent, bankrupt|Antonyms: solvent, in credit|adjective
