mellowed:/ˈmeloʊd/| v. adj.|变得柔和，成熟，醇厚|Having become softer and more mature in taste, character, or feeling.  The cheese has mellowed nicely.  The years have mellowed him considerably.  The harsh sunlight mellowed into a warm glow.|例句：The wine has mellowed with age.  这酒随着时间的推移变得更加醇厚了。

例句：His personality has mellowed over the years.  这些年来，他的性格变得温和了。|近义词：softened, matured, ripened, aged|反义词：harsh, immature, raw, green|词性:动词(过去分词形式)，形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *mellowen*, from Old English *mearu*, meaning "soft," "tender," "ripe."  Related to Old High German *muro* and Latin *maturus* ("ripe").
