nonvolubleness:/ˌnɒnvəˈluːb(ə)lnəs/ /ˌnɑːnvəˈluːb(ə)lnəs/
n.
沉默寡言，不健谈
Lack of fluency or readiness of speech; reticence.
His nonvolubleness made him seem aloof and mysterious. 他的沉默寡言使他看起来冷漠而神秘。

近义词: reticence, taciturnity, reserve, silence
反义词: loquacity, volubility, talkativeness, garrulity
词性: noun
