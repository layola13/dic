chimed:/tʃaɪmd/| v.|鸣响，插话|The clock chimed three. 钟敲了三下。|近义词：rang, pealed, resonated|反义词:silenced|verb

chimed:/tʃaɪmd/| v.|附和，同意|Her opinion chimed with mine. 她的意见与我的意见一致。|近义词：agreed, harmonized, coincided|反义词:disagreed, clashed|verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *chimen*, from Old French *chimer*, from Vulgar Latin *cimare*, from Late Latin *cymbalum*, from Ancient Greek *κύμβαλον* (kúmbalon, “cymbal”). Ultimately from an imitative root.
