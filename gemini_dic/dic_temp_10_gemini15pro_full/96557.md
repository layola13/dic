indorsor:/ɪnˈdɔːrsər/ | n. | 背书人；批注者 | Someone who endorses a check or other negotiable instrument.  The indorsor signs the back of the instrument to transfer ownership or guarantee payment. |  The indorsor of the check was the original payee.  支票的背书人是最初的收款人。| synonyms: endorser, signer, guarantor | antonyms: N/A | noun


indorse:/ɪnˈdɔːrs/ | vt. | 背书；批注；认可 | To sign one's name on the back of a check or other negotiable instrument to transfer ownership or guarantee payment. Also, to support or approve something. |  She indorsed the check and deposited it. 她背书了支票并存入银行。 He indorsed the candidate for mayor. 他认可了这位市长候选人。| synonyms: endorse, sign, approve, support, back, ratify | antonyms: oppose, reject, disapprove | verb
