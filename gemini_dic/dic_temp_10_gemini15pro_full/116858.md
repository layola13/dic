maximizes:/ˈmæksɪmaɪzɪz/
verb
最大化
makes as large or great as possible.
The company maximizes profits by minimizing costs.  公司通过最大限度地降低成本使利润最大化。
The athlete trained hard to maximize her performance. 运动员刻苦训练以最大限度地提高她的成绩。

synonyms: optimize, boost, amplify, enhance, increase, magnify, augment
antonyms: minimize, reduce, decrease, diminish, lessen
