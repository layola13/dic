hyperconservatives:/ˌhaɪpər.kənˈsɜːr.və.tɪvz/ | n. |极端保守主义者；超级保守派| People who hold extremely conservative political views, often characterized by a strong adherence to traditional values and a resistance to social change.  They are generally considered to be further to the right on the political spectrum than traditional conservatives. |  The hyperconservatives blocked any attempts at modernizing the party platform. 极端保守主义者阻挠了所有试图使党纲现代化的努力。

The hyperconservatives in the Senate voted against the bill. 参议院中的极端保守派投票反对该法案。 | 近义词：reactionaries, ultraconservatives | 反义词: liberals, progressives | 词性: 名词 (plural)
