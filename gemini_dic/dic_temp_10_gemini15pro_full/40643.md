conventionalized:/kənˈvɛnʃənəlaɪzd/
adjective
程式化的，约定俗成的
made to conform to generally accepted standards; made conventional.
The play's plot was highly conventionalized, following a predictable pattern.  这出戏的情节非常程式化，遵循着可预测的模式。
The artist's style is conventionalized, lacking originality.  这位艺术家的风格很程式化，缺乏 originality.
近义词:stereotyped, formalized, standardized, formulaic
反义词:original, unconventional, innovative, unique
词性:adjective

Etymology:
From convention + -al + -ize + -ed.  Ultimately from Latin *conventiōn- (stem of conventiō), from convenīre "to come together, assemble," from con- "together" + venīre "to come."
