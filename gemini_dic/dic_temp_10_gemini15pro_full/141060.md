/iːˈnɒlədʒi/ (British) /iːˈnɑːlədʒi/ (American)| n. | 葡萄酒学；酿酒学 |The scientific study of all aspects of wine and winemaking, except vine-growing and grape harvesting, which are part of viticulture. |Example: He studied enology at university and now works as a winemaker. 他在大学学习葡萄酒学，现在是一名酿酒师。

|Example: Her knowledge of enology is impressive; she can identify the grape varietal and region of almost any wine. 她对葡萄酒学的知识令人印象深刻；她几乎可以识别任何葡萄酒的葡萄品种和产区。|近义词：viniculture, winemaking |反义词: N/A |词性:noun
