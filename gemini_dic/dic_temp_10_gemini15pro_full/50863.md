deselects:/ˌdiːsəˈlekts/
verb
取消选择，撤销选择
To reverse the selection of something, typically on a computer or other digital device.
She deselected the file by clicking it again. 她再次点击文件取消了选择。
The program automatically deselects all other options when you choose this one. 当你选择这个选项时，程序会自动取消选择所有其他选项。
synonyms: unselect, clear, deactivate, undo
antonyms: select, choose, activate, check
词性: 动词
