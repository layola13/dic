fidejussor:/ˌfɪdeɪˈdʒʌsər/ /ˌfaɪdeɪˈdʒʊər/ | n. | 保证人，担保人 | A person who acts as surety for another, guaranteeing to pay a debt or perform a duty if the principal fails to do so. | Example 1: The fidejussor agreed to cover the loan if the borrower defaulted. 保证人同意如果借款人违约则偿还贷款。

Example 2:  The court held the fidejussor liable for the debt. 法院判决保证人对债务负有责任。 | 近义词: surety, guarantor | 反义词: debtor, obligee | noun
