druidry: /ˈdruːɪdri/ | n. | 德鲁伊教 | A modern spiritual or religious movement that generally promotes harmony and reverence for the natural world.  It draws inspiration from the ancient Celtic priests known as druids, but often incorporates elements from other pagan traditions. |  Modern druidry emphasizes connection with nature. 现代德鲁伊教强调与自然的联系。|  Many people find solace and meaning in the practices of druidry. 许多人在德鲁伊教的实践中找到了慰藉和意义。 |  paganism, neopaganism, nature worship | N/A | noun
