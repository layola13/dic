amberlike:/ˈæmbərlaɪk/
adjective
琥珀色的; 像琥珀的
Resembling amber in color or translucence.  Having the yellowish-orange color or semitransparent quality of amber.
The fossilized resin had an amberlike quality. 这块化石树脂具有琥珀般的特质。
The setting sun cast an amberlike glow over the landscape. 落日为大地披上了一层琥珀色的光辉。
近义词: amber-colored, amber-hued, golden, yellowish-brown
