ecumenical: /ˌɛkjuˈmɛnɪk(ə)l/ | adj. | 普世的；促进基督教各派团结的 | Promoting or relating to unity among the world's Christian churches. |  Example 1: The Pope called for an ecumenical council to address the issues dividing the church.  教皇呼吁召开普世会议来解决分裂教会的问题。

Example 2:  The ecumenical movement seeks to foster greater understanding and cooperation between different Christian denominations.  普世运动旨在促进不同基督教派别之间更大的理解和合作。 |  Synonyms: universal, general, worldwide, global, catholic | Antonyms: sectarian, parochial, denominational | word property: adjective
