aureole:/ˈɔːriˌoʊl (US), ˈɔːriˌəʊl (UK)| n.|光环，光轮，晕|A radiant light around the head or body of a sacred personage, often depicted in art.  A circle of light or color; a halo.|The saint was depicted with a golden aureole around his head.  圣徒被描绘成头部周围有一圈金色的光环。|The setting sun cast an aureole of light around the mountains. 落日给群山披上了一层光晕。|Synonyms: halo, nimbus, corona, radiance, glow|Antonyms: darkness, shadow|noun
