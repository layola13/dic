floppy's:/ˈflɒpiz/ | n. | 软盘（的） |  Possessive form of "floppy," referring to something belonging to or associated with a floppy disk.  Derived from the flexible nature of the disk. From "flop" (meaning to move loosely) + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives). | This is floppy's case. 这是软盘的盒子。| Synonym: diskette's | Antonym: hard drive's | noun

floppy:/ˈflɒpi/ | adj. | 松软的；下垂的 |  Describes something soft and flexible that hangs loosely.  From "flop" (meaning to move loosely) + "-y" (suffix forming adjectives). | The dog has floppy ears. 这只狗的耳朵很松软。| Synonym: limp, droopy | Antonym: stiff, rigid | adjective
