misassessments:/ˌmɪsəˈsɛsmənts/| n. |错误评估，误判|An act of assessing something wrongly.  It refers to instances where judgments or evaluations are inaccurate or incorrect.|The company's financial misassessments led to significant losses.  该公司的财务误判导致了重大损失。|The misassessments of the market trends resulted in a failed product launch. 对市场趋势的错误评估导致了产品发布的失败。|近义词：misjudgments, miscalculations, misestimations|反义词: accurate assessments, correct evaluations|词性:noun
