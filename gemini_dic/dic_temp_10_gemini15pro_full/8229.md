anti-hero:/ˈæntiˌhɪəroʊ/ (US) /ˈæntiˌhɪərəʊ/ (UK)| n.|反英雄|A central character in a story, movie, or drama who lacks conventional heroic attributes.|Deadpool is a popular anti-hero. 死侍是一个受欢迎的反英雄。|Synonyms: protagonist, villain, rogue, misfit|Antonyms: hero, protagonist|Noun

Etymology->
From anti- + hero.  "Anti-" comes from Greek "anti" meaning "against" or "opposite." "Hero" comes from Greek "hērōs" denoting a demigod or a person of superhuman qualities.
