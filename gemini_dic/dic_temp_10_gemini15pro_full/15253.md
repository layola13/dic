backhanded:/ˌbækˈhændɪd/ | adj. | 间接的，反讽的，虚情假意的；(网球等)反手的 | Something that is backhanded appears complimentary but is actually insulting.  A backhand is a stroke played with the back of the hand facing the direction of the stroke. Figuratively, it implies something done indirectly or deviously. |  "He gave her a backhanded compliment about her dress, implying it was too youthful for her." 他对她着装的赞美很虚伪，暗示她的裙子太年轻了。|  Synonyms: indirect, sarcastic, insincere, left-handed (for tennis), ambiguous, double-edged | Antonyms: direct, sincere, genuine, forthright, right-handed (for tennis) | adjective


backhand: /ˈbækˌhænd/ | n. | 反手击球；反手的 | A stroke played with the back of the hand facing the direction of the stroke.  Can also refer to something done in a devious or indirect way. | "He hit a powerful backhand down the line." 他打了一个强有力的反手下线球。 | Synonyms: backhand stroke, backhand shot | Antonyms: forehand | noun


backhand: /bækˈhænd/ | vt. | 反手击球 | To hit (a ball) with a backhand. | "He backhanded the ball across the court." 他反手将球击过球场。 | Synonyms: hit with a backhand | Antonyms: forehand | verb
