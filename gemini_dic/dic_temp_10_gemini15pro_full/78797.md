gilse:/ɡɪls/ | n. | 母鲑鱼(产卵后返回大海)|A female salmon that has spawned and returned to the sea.|The gilse were plentiful in the river this year. 今年河里的母鲑鱼很多。|Synonym:  salmon (generally), female salmon | Antonym: male salmon, grilse (male equivalent) | noun
