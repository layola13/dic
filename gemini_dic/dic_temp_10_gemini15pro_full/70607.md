filmier:/ˈfɪlmiər/ | adj. | 更薄的，更像薄膜的 | Covered with or resembling a thin film; more filmy. | The old windowpane was filmier than the new one. 旧的窗玻璃比新的更薄。 | The sauce had become filmier after sitting on the stove for too long. 酱汁在炉子上放太久后变得更稀薄了。 | 近义词: thinner, gauzier, flimsier | 反义词: thicker, denser, more substantial | 词性:形容词

词源:  From filmy + -er (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from film (n.) + -y (adjective-forming suffix).
