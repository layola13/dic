doublehearted:/ˌdʌbəlˈhɑːrtɪd/| adj.| двуличный, двуличный, неискренний, коварный (Russian);  两面三刀的, 虚伪的, 口是心非的 (Chinese)| deceitful, insincere, treacherous; having conflicting feelings or intentions | He played a doublehearted game, pretending to be her friend while secretly plotting against her. 他玩两面三刀，假装是她的朋友，却暗中密谋陷害她。| 近义词：two-faced, hypocritical, duplicitous, deceitful, treacherous | 反义词：sincere, honest, genuine, loyal, faithful |词性: adjective

doublehearted:/ˌdʌbəlˈhɑːrtɪd/| adj.| 口是心非的,  表里不一的 (Chinese); having divided loyalties or affections | She felt doublehearted about leaving her family to pursue her career.  对于离开家人去追求事业，她感到很矛盾。| 近义词：ambivalent, uncertain, wavering, torn | 反义词：resolute, determined, committed, single-minded |词性: adjective
