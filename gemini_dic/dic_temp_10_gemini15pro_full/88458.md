historiographical:/ˌhɪstɔːriəˈɡræfɪkəl/ | adj. | 史学的，史学编纂学的 |  Relating to the writing of history; concerning the writing of history. |  Example:  A historiographical approach to the subject reveals a bias towards the victors. 对该主题的史学研究方法揭示了对胜利者的偏见。

Example:  The book offered a fresh historiographical perspective on the events leading up to the war. 这本书对导致战争的事件提供了一个新的史学视角。 |  Synonyms: historical, historic | Antonyms: ahistorical |  词性: 形容词
