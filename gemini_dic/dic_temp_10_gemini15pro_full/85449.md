hashimoto:/ˌhɑːʃiˈmoʊtoʊ/ /ˌhæʃɪˈmɒtəʊ/ | n. | 桥本氏甲状腺炎 |  Named after Hakaru Hashimoto, the Japanese physician who first described it. An autoimmune disorder that can cause hypothyroidism.  It's characterized by the immune system attacking the thyroid gland. | Hashimoto's disease is the most common cause of hypothyroidism in the United States. 桥本氏甲状腺炎是美国甲状腺功能减退症最常见的原因。

Hashimoto's thyroiditis can sometimes lead to goiter. 桥本氏甲状腺炎有时会导致甲状腺肿。 | 近义词: Hashimoto's thyroiditis, autoimmune thyroiditis | 反义词:  None (It's a specific disease, not a concept with an opposite) | 词性: noun
