autosuggestive:/ˌɔːtəsəˈdʒɛstɪv/| adj.|自我暗示的，心理暗示的|Producing ideas or feelings in oneself; relating to or characterized by autosuggestion.  Influencing one's own attitudes or behavior by mental suggestion.|Her illness was thought to be autosuggestive, brought on by her own anxieties.|近义词：self-suggestive, autogenic|反义词: N/A|词性: adjective

Etymology:  From auto- (self) + suggestive (tending to suggest).  Ultimately from Latin *suggerere* (to carry or bring under).
