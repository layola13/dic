例句：The park was filled with frondent trees, providing ample shade on a hot summer day. 公园里满是枝繁叶茂的树木，在炎热的夏日提供了充足的阴凉。|近义词：leafy, lush, luxuriant|反义词：bare, barren, leafless|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *frondent-, present participle stem of frondēre "to put forth leaves," from frōns (genitive frondis) "leaf, foliage, branch."
