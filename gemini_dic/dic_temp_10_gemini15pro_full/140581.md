oligosepalous:/ˌɒlɪɡəˈsɛpələs/ | adj. | 少萼片的 | Having few sepals (the outer parts of a flower that enclose the petals before it blooms). |  The oligosepalous flower had only three sepals.  这朵少萼片的花只有三片萼片。|  microsepalous | polysepalous | adjective
Etymology->
From *oligo- "few" + sepalous, from sepal + -ous. Ultimately from Greek *oligos*  "few, little" and  *skepe*, covering.
