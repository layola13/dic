nonstriated: /ˌnɒnˈstraɪeɪtɪd/ /ˌnɑːnˈstraɪeɪtɪd/
adjective
无条纹的；非横纹肌的
Not having transverse striations or stripes.  Relating to smooth muscle tissue, which lacks the striped appearance of skeletal or cardiac muscle.
The walls of the intestines are composed of nonstriated muscle.  肠壁由无纹肌组成。
The nonstriated muscles in the digestive system contract rhythmically to move food along. 消化系统中的无纹肌有节奏地收缩以推动食物前进。

近义词: smooth, involuntary
反义词: striated, voluntary
词性: adjective

词源:
non- (prefix meaning "not") + striated (from Latin *striatus, past participle of striare "to groove, furrow")
