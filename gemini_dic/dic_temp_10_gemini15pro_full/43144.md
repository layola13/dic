cozied:/ˈkoʊ.zid/| adj.|舒适的；温暖的|Feeling warm and comfortable.|The room felt cozy with the fire burning. 房间里生着火，感觉很舒适。|comfortable, snug, warm|uncomfortable, cold, exposed|adjective
