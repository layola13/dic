aerological:/ˌɛrəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/ /ˌɛroʊˈlɑːdʒɪkəl/
adjective
高空气象学的
Relating to the study of the atmosphere, especially its upper layers.
The aerological data showed a significant temperature inversion.  高空气象数据显示存在明显的逆温现象。
Aerological balloons are used to gather atmospheric data.  高空气象气球被用来收集大气数据。
近义词:atmospheric, meteorological
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From aero- (relating to air or the atmosphere) + -logical (relating to the study of).  The prefix "aero-" comes from the Greek "aer" meaning "air."  The suffix "-logical" comes from the Greek "logos" meaning "study" or "word."
