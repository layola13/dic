debunk:/diːˈbʌŋk/| vt.|揭穿；拆穿；驳斥|To expose the falseness or hollowness of (a myth, idea, or belief).|例句：The article debunks the myth that money buys happiness. 这篇文章揭穿了金钱能买到幸福的谬论。

例句：Scientists have debunked the theory that eating late at night causes weight gain.  科学家已经驳斥了深夜进食会导致体重增加的理论。

近义词：expose, disprove, refute, discredit|反义词：confirm, validate, substantiate|词性：动词

词源：de- (prefix meaning "remove, reverse") + bunk ("nonsense")  源自美国俚语"bunkum" (nonsense), 后缩短为"bunk"。 "Debunk" 最早出现于20世纪初。
