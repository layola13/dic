misworships:/ˌmɪsˈwɜːrʃɪps/
|v.|错误地崇拜，不当地崇拜|To worship someone or something incorrectly or inappropriately.|He misworships material possessions, believing they will bring him happiness. 他错误地崇拜物质财富，认为它们会给他带来幸福。|例句：
Some people misworship celebrities, treating them like gods.
一些人错误地崇拜名人，把他们当作神一样对待。


The ancient tribe misworshipped a volcano god.
这个古代部落错误地崇拜一个火山神。|近义词:idolize wrongly, venerate falsely|反义词:worship correctly, revere properly|词性:动词
