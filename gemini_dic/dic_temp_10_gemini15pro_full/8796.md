antiemetics:/ˌæntiɪˈmetɪks/ | n. | 止吐药 | Medications used to prevent or treat nausea and vomiting. |  The doctor prescribed antiemetics to help with the patient's nausea after surgery.  医生给病人开了止吐药，以帮助缓解术后恶心。 | Synonyms: antinauseants | Antonyms: emetics | noun


antiemetic:/ˌæntiɪˈmetɪk/ | adj. | 止吐的 | Having the effect of preventing or relieving nausea and vomiting. | The antiemetic properties of ginger are well known.  生姜的止吐特性是众所周知的。 | Synonyms: antinauseant | Antonyms: emetic | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From anti- + emetic.  "Anti-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposite of," and "emetic" refers to something that induces vomiting.
