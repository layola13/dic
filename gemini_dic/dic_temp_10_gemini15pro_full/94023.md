impanels:/ɪmˈpænəlz/| v.| 列入陪审员名单；组成(陪审团) | To enroll or enlist on a jury panel; to constitute or form (a jury). | The judge impaneled a jury of 12 citizens. 法官组成一个由12名公民组成的陪审团。|  The court impaneled a jury to hear the case. 法院组成陪审团审理此案。 | synonyms: enroll, enlist, constitute, form | antonyms: disband, dismiss | verb
