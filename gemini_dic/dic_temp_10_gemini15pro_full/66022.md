/ɪɡˈzæmpəlz/ | n. | 例子，实例 | Examples are used to illustrate a point. 例子是用来阐明观点的。 | Examples illustrate a concept.  例子阐述了一个概念。 | synonyms: instances, illustrations, samples | antonyms: counter-examples, exceptions | noun

/ɪɡˈzæmpəlz/ | v. |  (古用法，罕见) 举例说明 |  He exampless his argument with several anecdotes. (古用法) 他用几个轶事举例说明他的论点。 | He exampless the concept with a case study. (古用法) 他用一个案例研究来举例说明这个概念。 | synonyms: illustrates, exemplifies, demonstrates | antonyms: obscures, confuses | verb (rare)


Etymology: From Late Middle English *exaumple, from Old French example, essaumple, from Latin exemplum (“sample, example, copy; image, portrait, likeness”), from eximere (“to take out, remove; pick out, select, choose”), from ex- (“out, out of, from”) + emere (“to take, buy, procure, obtain”).
