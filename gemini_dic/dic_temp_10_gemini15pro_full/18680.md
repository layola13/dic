bequeathes:/bɪˈkwiːðz/
verb
遗赠，把…传下去
leaves (property) to a person or other beneficiary by a will.
He bequeathed his art collection to the museum. 他把他的艺术收藏品遗赠给了博物馆。
近义词:leave, will, bestow, grant
反义词:inherit, receive
词性:v.

bequeath:/bɪˈkwiːθ/
verb
遗赠，把…传下去
leave (a personal estate or one's body) to a person or other beneficiary by a will.
She bequeathed her fortune to her niece. 她把她的财产遗赠给了她的侄女。
近义词:leave, will, bestow, grant
反义词:inherit, receive
词性:v.
