no-good:/ˌnoʊˈɡʊd/| adj.| 无用的，不中用的，坏的 |A no-good person or thing is useless, worthless, or bad. | He's a no-good liar. 他是个没用的骗子。| 近义词: worthless, useless, bad, no good | 反义词: good, useful, valuable | 词性: 形容词

no-good:/ˌnoʊˈɡʊd/| n.| 废物，无用的人 | A person who is no good. | He's a no-good. 他是个废物。| 近义词: bum, loser, good-for-nothing | 反义词: achiever, winner, asset | 词性: 名词

词源:  From the phrase "no good," meaning "not good."  It began as an adjective in the early 20th century and later also became used as a noun.
