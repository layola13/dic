mobcap:/ˈmɒbkæp/ (US) /ˈmɒbkæp/ (UK)| n. |怪物上限;生物数量上限;群体上限|A limit on the number of mobs (creatures or characters) that can exist in a particular area or at a particular time in a video game.  It's used to manage performance and prevent overcrowding. |Example: The server's mobcap was too low, so there weren't many animals spawning.  服务器的生物数量上限太低了，所以没有很多动物生成。

Example: Increasing the mobcap can lead to lag if your computer isn't powerful enough.  提高生物数量上限可能会导致延迟，如果你的电脑不够强大。|近义词：mob limit, spawn limit|反义词: None|词性: noun
