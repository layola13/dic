hardock:/ˈhɑːrdɒk/ (UK) /ˈhɑːrdɑːk/ (US)| n.| 硬毛坞，牛蒡 |A biennial plant (Arctium lappa) of the daisy family, with large leaves and prickly heads of purplish flowers, common in waste places.
