germane:/dʒɜːrˈmeɪn/ | adj. |相关的，贴切的|Relevant or appropriate to a subject under consideration.|Her remarks were not germane to the discussion. 她的言论与讨论无关.|近义词:relevant, pertinent, applicable, apposite|反义词:irrelevant, inappropriate, extraneous|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *germanus* "of the same stock, genuine, true," literally "having the same parents."
