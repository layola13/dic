fusspots:/ˈfʌsˌspɒts/ (UK), /ˈfʌsˌspɑːts/ (US)| n. |爱挑剔的人；大惊小怪的人|A person who is easily upset or annoyed about small, unimportant things.  A person who worries excessively about minor details.|例句：My aunt is such a fusspot about her house being tidy. 我的阿姨对房子的整洁非常挑剔。

例句：He's a terrible fusspot about his food; everything has to be just so. 他对食物挑剔得要命，每样东西都得恰到好处。|近义词：nitpicker, quibbler, complainer, worrier|反义词：easygoing person, relaxed person|词性:名词
