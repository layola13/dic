counter-tide: /ˈkaʊntərˌtaɪd/ | n. |逆潮，对立的潮流，反潮流 | A tide that runs in the opposite direction to the main tide or current; something that opposes a prevailing tendency. | The small boat struggled against the counter-tide. 小船在逆潮中艰难行进。|  The new political movement represented a counter-tide to the established order. 这场新的政治运动代表了对现有秩序的反抗。 | synonyms: backlash, reaction, resistance, opposition | antonyms: trend, current, mainstream | noun
