nontangible:/ˌnɒnˈtændʒəbl/ | adj. |无形的，非物质的 |Not able to be touched; not having physical presence.  |  The company's most valuable assets are nontangible, such as its brand reputation and customer loyalty.  这家公司最有价值的资产是无形的，例如品牌声誉和客户忠诚度。| intangible, incorporeal, abstract | tangible, concrete, material | adjective
Etymology-> From non- + tangible. "Tangible" comes from Late Latin "tangibilis", from Latin "tangere" (to touch).
