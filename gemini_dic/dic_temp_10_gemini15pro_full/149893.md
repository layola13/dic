palisade:/ˌpælɪˈseɪd/| n.| 栅栏，围栏；悬崖峭壁 |A fence made of pointed wooden stakes fixed upright in the ground, typically used for enclosure or defense. | The fort was surrounded by a sturdy palisade.  这个堡垒被坚固的栅栏包围着。| synonyms: fence, barrier, stockade | antonyms: opening, gap | noun

palisade:/ˌpælɪˈseɪd/| adj.| 栅栏状的，围栏状的; 崖壁似的| Resembling a palisade in form or arrangement. | The palisade cells of a leaf are closely packed together.  叶子的栅栏状细胞紧密地排列在一起。| synonyms: fenced, enclosed, barricaded | antonyms: open, exposed | adjective
