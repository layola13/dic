hotspot:/ˈhɒtspɒt/ (UK) /ˈhɑːtspɑːt/ (US)| n.|热点，热门地点；危险地区；无线网络连接点|A place of significant activity, danger, or controversy; a location offering public wireless internet access.|例句：This cafe is a popular hotspot for students. 这家咖啡馆是学生们常去的热门地点。

例句：The border region has become a hotspot for armed conflict. 边境地区已成为武装冲突的热点地区。

近义词：center, hub, focal point, trouble spot, access point|反义词:backwater, quiet place|词性:noun
