nondexterities:/ˌnɒndɛkˈstɛrɪtiːz/
n.
笨拙，不灵巧
lack of dexterity; clumsiness.
His nondexterities made it difficult for him to play the piano. 他的笨拙使他很难弹钢琴。
近义词：clumsiness, awkwardness, ineptness
反义词：dexterity, skill, adroitness
词性：名词


Etymology:
From non- (not) + dexterity (skill, especially with the hands). Dexterity comes from Latin dexter (right, skillful).
