disprince:/dɪsˈprɪns/
n.
废黜王子; 剥夺王子身份的人
The act of depriving someone of their princely status.  The person who has been deprived of their princely status.
He was disprinced for marrying a commoner. 他因为娶了一个平民而被废除了王子身份。
近义词:depose, dethrone
反义词:enthrone, crown
词性:名词

disprince:/dɪsˈprɪns/
vt.
废黜，剥夺王子身份
To deprive someone of their princely status.
The king disprinced his son for treason. 国王以叛国罪废除了他儿子的王子身份。
近义词:depose, dethrone
反义词:enthrone, crown
词性:动词


词源: dis- (prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of") + prince (noun).  The prefix "dis-" negates or reverses the meaning of the word "prince."
