doctorate: /ˈdɒktərət/ (UK) /ˈdɑːktərət/ (US)
| n. |博士学位|A doctorate is the highest degree awarded by a university.  |She earned her doctorate in physics. 她获得了物理学博士学位。|近义词: PhD, doctoral degree |反义词: N/A |词性: 名词
Etymology: From Late Latin doctoratus, past participle of doctorare "to teach," from Latin doctor "teacher."
