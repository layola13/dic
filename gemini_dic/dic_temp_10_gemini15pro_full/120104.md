mimetic:/mɪˈmɛtɪk/ |adj.|模仿的；拟态的|Characterized by, exhibiting, or of the nature of mimesis or mimicry; imitative.  Relating to, or marked by mimicry.|The chameleon's mimetic skin allows it to blend in with its surroundings. 变色龙的拟态皮肤让它可以与周围环境融为一体。|Synonyms: imitative, mimic, aping, emulative|Antonyms: original, genuine, authentic|adjective

mimetic:/mɪˈmɛtɪk/ |n.|模仿者；拟态物|Something that is mimetic or exhibits mimicry.|The insect's mimetic coloration protected it from predators.  这种昆虫的拟态颜色保护它免受捕食者的侵害。|Synonyms: imitation, copy, replica|Antonyms: original, prototype|noun
