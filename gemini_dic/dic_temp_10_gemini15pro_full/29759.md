catskill: /ˈkætskɪl/ | n. (usually plural)| 卡茨基尔山脉; 卡茨基尔地区 | The Catskills are a mountain range in southeastern New York.  卡茨基尔山脉是位于纽约州东南部的一座山脉。| The Catskill region is known for its scenic beauty. 卡茨基尔地区以其优美的风景而闻名。 |近义词: Catskill Mountains, Catskill region |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

Etymology: From Dutch * Kats Kill, literally "cat creek," perhaps referring to mountain lions or bobcats once found in the area. 源于荷兰语 * Kats Kill，字面意思是“猫溪”，可能指的是曾经在该地区发现的美洲狮或山猫。
