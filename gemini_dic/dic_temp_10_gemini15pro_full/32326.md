child's:/tʃaɪldz/| adj. |孩子的，儿童的|Belonging to or associated with a child.|This is my child's toy. 这是我孩子的玩具。|近义词：children's, kid's|反义词:adult's|词性:形容词

词源: From Old English 'cild' (child) with the possessive suffix '-s'.  The '-s' ending indicates singular possession.
