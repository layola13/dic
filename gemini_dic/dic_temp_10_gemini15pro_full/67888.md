fabulosity:/ˌfæbjəˈlɒsəti/ /ˌfæbjəˈlɑːsəti/

难以置信；惊人；极棒的状态
The quality of being fabulous; fabulousness.  惊人的品质；难以置信。
Her outfits were a monument to fabulosity. 她的服装是华丽的象征。
近义词：fabulousness, splendor, magnificence, grandeur, marvelousness, wonderfulness, amazingness
反义词：drabness, dullness, ordinariness
