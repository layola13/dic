non-Semites:/ˌnɒnˈsɛmɑɪts/ | n. |非閃米特人|People who are not Semites, referring to those who do not speak a Semitic language or belong to cultures traditionally considered Semitic (e.g., Arabic, Hebrew, Aramaic). The term is often used in discussions of ethnicity, religion, and history, sometimes with negative connotations.|Many early civilizations in the region were non-Semites. 該地區的許多早期文明都是非閃米特人。|Near synonyms: Gentiles, non-Semitic peoples|Antonym: Semites|Noun

non-Semitic:/ˌnɒn səˈmɪtɪk/ | adj. |非閃米特的|Not Semitic; relating to people or languages not belonging to the Semitic family.|The archaeologist discovered non-Semitic inscriptions on the ancient tablets. 考古學家在古代石板上發現了非閃米特的銘文。|Near synonyms: Gentile, non-Shemitic|Antonym: Semitic|Adjective
