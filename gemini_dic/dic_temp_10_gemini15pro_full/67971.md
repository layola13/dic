facingly:/ˈfeɪsɪŋli/
adv.
面对面地，公开地
directly; openly; in a way that confronts something
He facingly addressed the criticisms leveled against him. 他公开地回应了对他的批评。
近义词: openly, directly, frankly
反义词: indirectly, covertly, secretly
词性: 副词

词源：From "face" + "-ing" + "-ly".  "Face" comes from Old French "face" and ultimately from Latin "facies" meaning "appearance, form, face."  The "-ing" suffix forms a present participle, and the "-ly" suffix transforms it into an adverb.
