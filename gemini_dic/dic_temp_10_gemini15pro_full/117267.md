mechanising:/ˈmekənaɪzɪŋ/
verb
机械化; 用机械装备
equipping with machinery, especially to replace manual labour.
The farm has been completely mechanised.  这个农场已经完全机械化了。
mechanizing:/ˈmekəˌnaɪzɪŋ/
verb
机械化; 用机械装备
equipping with machinery, especially to replace manual labour.
They are mechanizing the process of sorting the mail. 他们正在使邮件分拣过程机械化。
近义词: automating, modernizing
反义词: manualizing
