legumin:/ˈlɛɡjʊmɪn/| n.|豆类蛋白质；豆球蛋白|A globulin which is the principal protein in the seeds of most leguminous plants.|Legumin is a major storage protein found in peas and other legumes. 豆球蛋白是豌豆和其他豆类中主要的储存蛋白。| Legumes like beans and lentils are rich in legumin, which is an important source of protein. 像豆类和小扁豆这样的豆类富含豆球蛋白，它是重要的蛋白质来源。|Synonyms: globulin, protein|Antonyms: None|Noun
