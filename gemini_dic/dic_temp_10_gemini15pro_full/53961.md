discredited:/dɪˈskredɪtɪd/
adj.
名誉扫地的，不可信的
Having lost credibility or respect; disgraced.
The discredited politician resigned from office.  名誉扫地的政治家辞去了职务。
近义词：disgraced, shamed, dishonored, debunked
反义词：respected, honored, credible, trusted
词性：adjective

discredit:/dɪˈskredɪt/
vt.
使失去信誉，使丢脸
To harm the reputation of; to cause to be doubted or disbelieved.
The scandal discredited the entire organization. 这桩丑闻使整个组织名誉扫地。
近义词：disgrace, shame, dishonor, debunk
反义词：honor, praise, credit, endorse
词性：verb

词源：
From Late Latin *discreditāre, from Latin dis- "not, opposite of" + creditāre "to trust, believe" (from creditum, past participle of credere "to believe").
