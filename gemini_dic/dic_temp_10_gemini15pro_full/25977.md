bursal:/ˈbɜːrsəl/
adjective
囊的；滑囊的
Relating to or resembling a bursa (a small fluid-filled sac that reduces friction between moving parts in the body).
The bursal cavity is inflamed. 滑囊腔发炎了。
Synonyms: saclike, pouch-like
Antonyms: N/A
词性: adjective


bursa:/ˈbɜːrsə/
noun
囊；滑囊
A small fluid-filled sac or saclike cavity, especially one countering friction at a joint.
The bursa in his knee was swollen. 他膝盖里的滑囊肿了。
Synonyms: sac, pouch
Antonyms: N/A
词性: noun
