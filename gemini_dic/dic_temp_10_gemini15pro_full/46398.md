daer:/dɛər/ /deɪr/
adjective
dear
precious; beloved; costly
例句：My dear friend, how are you? 我亲爱的朋友，你好吗？

例句：This necklace is very dear to me. 这条项链对我来说非常珍贵。
近义词：precious, beloved, expensive, costly
反义词：cheap, inexpensive, worthless
词性: adjective
