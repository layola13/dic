look-ins:/ˈlʊkɪnz/ | n. | 短暂的访问；露面；(尤指比赛中)短暂的参与 | brief visits or appearances, especially a short period of participation in a game or competition |  He had a couple of look-ins during the game, but didn't manage to score. 他在比赛中有几次短暂的出场，但没有得分。 | synonyms: brief appearances, cameos, short visits | antonyms: extended stays, long visits | noun

look-ins:/ˈlʊkɪnz/ | n. | (广播或电视节目的)插播短片; 片段 | short segments inserted into a broadcast or television program | There were a few look-ins from reporters live at the scene.  有一些来自现场记者的插播短片。 | synonyms: inserts, short segments, clips | antonyms: full programs, complete broadcasts | noun
