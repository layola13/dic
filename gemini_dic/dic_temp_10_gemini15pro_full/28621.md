caretakers:/ˈkɛrteɪkərz/
noun
管理员，看护人，守护者
A person who takes care of a person, a place, or an organization.
The caretakers of the national park work hard to protect the environment. 国家公园的管理员努力保护环境。
custodians, guardians, managers
neglecters, destroyers
