commensally: /kəˈmɛnsəli/ | adv. | 共餐地；共生地 |  In a commensal relationship, one organism benefits while the other is neither harmed nor helped. |  The two species lived commensally, sharing the same food source without affecting each other. 这两个物种共生，共享相同的食物来源，互不影响。 | 近义词: symbiotically | 反义词: parasitically | 词性: 副词

commensally: /kəˈmɛnsəli/ | n. | 共生者；食客 | An organism that benefits from another without affecting it. | The bacteria acted as commensals in the gut, deriving nutrients without harming the host. 这种细菌在肠道中充当共生体，获取营养而不伤害宿主。 | 近义词: symbiont | 反义词: parasite | 词性: 名词

commensally: /kəˈmɛnsəli/ | adj. | 共生的；共餐的 | Relating to or exhibiting commensalism. | The commensal relationship between the two species allowed them to coexist peacefully. 这两个物种之间的共生关系使它们能够和平共处。| 近义词: symbiotic | 反义词: parasitic | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From Late Latin *commensalis*, from Latin com- 'together' + mensa 'table'.
