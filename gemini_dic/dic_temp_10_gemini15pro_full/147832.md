overpunish: /ˌōvərˈpʌnɪʃ/ | v. | 过度惩罚；处罚过重 | To punish someone too severely or excessively. | The school overpunished the student for a minor infraction. 学校对学生的小过错处罚过重。|  The judge was accused of overpunishing the defendant. 法官被指控对被告处罚过重。 | 近义词: overdiscipline, penalize excessively | 反义词: underpunish, be lenient | 词性: 动词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "punish" (from Old French "punir," from Latin "punire" meaning "to punish")
