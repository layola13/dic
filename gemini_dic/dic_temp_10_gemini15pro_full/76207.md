n.

词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
Likely a blend of "fusty" (stale-smelling, old-fashioned) and "lugs" (heavy, clumsy objects or a clumsy person).  "Fusty" comes from Old French "fust" (tasting of the cask).  "Lugs" origin is uncertain, possibly related to Swedish "lugga" (to pull by the hair).
