ingest:/ɪnˈdʒest/| v. |摄取，吸收|To take food, drink, or another substance into the body by swallowing or absorbing it.|例句：The snake ingested the mouse whole. 蛇把老鼠整个吞了下去。|例句：Humans ingest a variety of nutrients through food. 人类通过食物摄取各种营养物质。|近义词：consume, devour, absorb, take in|反义词:excrete, expel, discharge|词性:动词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin ingestus, past participle of ingerere (“to carry in”), from in- (“in, into”) + gerere (“to carry, bear”).
