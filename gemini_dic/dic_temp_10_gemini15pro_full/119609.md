mid-Atlantic:/ˌmɪdətˈlæntɪk/ | adj. n.|中大西洋的；中大西洋地区|Refers to the region of the United States located between New England and the South, generally including Delaware, Maryland, New Jersey, New York, Pennsylvania, Virginia, and West Virginia. Can also refer to the middle of the Atlantic Ocean.|例句：The mid-Atlantic states experienced heavy rainfall this summer.  今年夏天，中大西洋各州经历了强降雨。

|例句：The ship was sailing in the mid-Atlantic. 这艘船正在大西洋中部航行。|近义词：Mid-Atlantic region|反义词:|adjective, noun
