lethally:/ˈliːθəli/| adv.|致命地，足以致命地|In a manner likely to cause or capable of causing death.|The spider's venom is lethally potent. 这种蜘蛛的毒液足以致命。|例句：The virus can lethally infect humans. 这种病毒可以致命地感染人类。

例句：He was lethally wounded in the battle. 他在战斗中受了致命伤。|近义词：fatally, mortally, deadly|反义词：harmlessly, safely|词性:副词

词源: From lethal + -ly.  "Lethal" comes from Late Latin *lēthālis, from Latin lēthum "death," from Greek lēthē "forgetfulness, oblivion; death."
