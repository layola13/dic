angelicize:/ænˈdʒɛlɪsaɪz/
v.
使英国化;使像英国人;使更像天使;净化；理想化
To make English in form or character; to give an angelic appearance or character to.
They angelized their foreign-sounding surname. 他们把听起来像外国人的姓氏英国化了。
The artist angelized the features of the Madonna in his painting.  艺术家把他画中的圣母玛利亚的容貌理想化了。

近义词: anglify, purify, idealize, elevate, refine
反义词: demonize, corrupt, degrade
词性: verb
