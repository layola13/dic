lushing:/ˈlʌʃɪŋ/| adj.| 茂盛的，葱郁的；丰富的，大量的；奢华的|Characterized by abundant growth, rich and plentiful, or luxurious and extravagant.|The lush gardens were filled with colorful flowers. 茂盛的花园里开满了五颜六色的花朵。|近义词：luxuriant, abundant, lavish, opulent|反义词：sparse, barren, meager|词性:形容词
词源: From the Middle English word *lusshe* meaning "loose, slack, soft," likely related to the Old English *lysan* meaning "to loosen, release."  The sense of "abundant, plentiful" developed later.
