metaphysical:/ˌmɛtəˈfɪzɪkəl/
adjective
形而上学的；抽象的；深奥的
Concerned with abstract thought or subjects, as existence, causality, or truth.  Relating to metaphysics.
例句：
He is interested in metaphysical questions about the nature of reality.
他对关于现实本质的形而上学问题感兴趣。

例句：
The play explores metaphysical themes of life and death.
这部戏剧探讨了生死存亡的形而上学主题。

近义词：abstract, philosophical, speculative, esoteric
反义词：physical, concrete, empirical, mundane
词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin metaphysica, neuter plural of metaphysicus (“metaphysical”), from Greek τὰ μετὰ τὰ φυσικά (tà metà tà physiká, “the things after the physical things”), referring to the arrangement of Aristotle's works by Andronicus of Rhodes, who placed the books on metaphysics after the books on physics.
