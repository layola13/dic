gooeys: /ˈɡuːiz/ | adj. | 粘糊糊的；黏性的 |  Likely a misspelling of "gooey" which means soft and sticky, often referring to sweet or melted foods. | The melted marshmallow was gooey and delicious.  融化的棉花糖又黏又好吃。 | 近义词：sticky, gummy, viscous | 反义词：dry, firm, solid | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "Gooey" is derived from "goo," which likely imitates the sound of something thick and sticky.
