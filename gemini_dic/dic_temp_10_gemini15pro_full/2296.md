adapter: /əˈdæptər/
noun|适配器，转接器|A device used to connect two incompatible pieces of equipment or to allow something to be used in a different way.|I needed an adapter to plug my American hairdryer into the British socket. 我需要一个适配器才能将我的美式吹风机插入英式插座。|synonyms: connector, converter, coupler|antonyms: N/A|n.

This adapter allows me to use European plugs in the US. = 这个适配器允许我在美国使用欧洲插头。
