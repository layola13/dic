gerfalcon:/ˈdʒɜːrfɔːlkən/ |ˈdʒɜːrˌfɔːlkən/| n. |矛隼，白隼|A large, powerful falcon (Falco rusticolus) found in arctic regions. It is the largest falcon species.|例句：The gerfalcon soared through the icy air. 矛隼在冰冷的空气中翱翔。|例句：The hunter trained a gerfalcon for hunting grouse.猎人训练了一只矛隼来捕猎松鸡。|近义词：gyrfalcon, falcon|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑prey猎物)|词性:noun
