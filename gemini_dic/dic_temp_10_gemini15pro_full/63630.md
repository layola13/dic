eolian:/iˈoʊliən/
adjective
风的；风成的；风蚀的
pertaining to or caused by the wind; wind-blown
例句：The eolian harp produces musical sounds when the wind blows through it. 风弦琴在风吹过时会发出音乐般的声音。

例句：The eolian deposits formed sand dunes in the desert. 风积物在沙漠中形成了沙丘。
近义词：windy, airy
反义词:calm, still
词性:adjective

Etymology: From Latin Aeolus, the god of the winds, from Greek Aiolos.
