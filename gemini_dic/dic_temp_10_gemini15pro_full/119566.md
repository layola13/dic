microtomical: /ˌmaɪkrəˈtɒmɪkəl/ (US) /ˌmaɪkrəˈtɒmɪkl/ (UK)
adjective
显微切片的；切片术的
Relating to or involving the use of a microtome, an instrument used to cut extremely thin sections of material for microscopic examination.
The microtomical sections revealed the intricate structure of the tissue. 显微切片揭示了组织的复杂结构。
The laboratory utilizes specialized microtomical techniques for preparing tissue samples.  该实验室采用专门的切片技术制备组织样本。

近义词: histological (组织学的)
反义词:  None (没有明确的反义词)
词性: adjective

词源:
From microtome + -ical.  Microtome itself comes from Greek mikros "small" + temnein "to cut."
