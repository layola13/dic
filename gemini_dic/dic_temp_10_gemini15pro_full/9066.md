antimethodical:/ˌæntɪməˈθɒdɪkəl/
adjective
无条理的，缺乏方法的
Lacking method or system; unsystematic.
Her antimethodical approach to research often led to wasted time. 她无条理的研究方法经常导致时间浪费。
The detective's antimethodical investigation frustrated his colleagues.  这位侦探毫无章法地展开调查，让他的同事们感到沮丧。
synonyms: disorganized, unsystematic, haphazard, chaotic, disorderly
antonyms: methodical, systematic, organized, orderly, structured
词性: 形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix meaning "against, opposite of") + methodical (from method + -ical, meaning "characterized by method")
