motts:/mɑts/ | n. | 杂斑；斑点；污点；(尤指水果或蔬菜的)疵点 | Spots, blemishes, or imperfections, especially on fruits or vegetables. | The apples had a few motts on them, but were still good to eat. 这些苹果上有一些斑点，但还是可以吃的。 |  近义词: specks, blemishes, flaws, imperfections | 反义词: perfection, flawlessness | 词性: 名词

motts (plural): /mɑts/ | n. |  (非正式)苹果酒 | (informal) Cider, especially apple cider.  | We drank a jug of motts at the harvest festival.  我们在丰收节上喝了一壶苹果酒。| 近义词: cider, apple juice | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词


词源:  "Motts" as meaning cider likely originates from Samuel Mott, who founded Mott's, a well-known American brand of apple products, including apple sauce and apple juice. The brand name became associated with apple cider in some regions.  "Motts" meaning spots or blemishes is of uncertain origin, possibly related to words indicating small marks or spots.
