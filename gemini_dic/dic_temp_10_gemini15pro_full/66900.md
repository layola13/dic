expedites:/ˈɛkspɪˌdaɪts/| v. |加快；促进；迅速完成|to make something happen more quickly|例句：The company expedited the shipping process to ensure timely delivery. 公司加快了运输流程，以确保及时交货。

例句：The new software expedites the data analysis process.  这个新软件加快了数据分析的过程。


近义词：accelerates, hastens, speeds up, facilitates, promotes|反义词:delays, hinders, obstructs|词性:动词

expedite:/ˈɛkspɪˌdaɪt/| n. |快捷方式；快速通道|a way of making something happen more quickly | This special lane is an expedite for diplomats. 这条专用车道是外交人员的快速通道。

例句：The online application offers an expedite for renewing your license. 在线申请提供了一个更新许可证的快速通道。


近义词：shortcut, express route|反义词:detour, long way|词性:名词
