equiradial: /ˌiːkwɪˈreɪdiəl/
adjective
等径的；辐射对称的
Having radii of equal length.  Extending outward equally from a common center.
例句：The starfish exhibits equiradial symmetry.  海星呈现出等径对称。

例句：The artist created an equiradial pattern with vibrant colors.  艺术家用鲜艳的色彩创作了一个等径图案。

近义词：isoradial, symmetrical
反义词: asymmetrical
词性: adjective
