muskrat:/ˈmʌskræt/ (US), /ˈmʌskræt/ (UK)| n.|麝鼠|A large, semi-aquatic rodent with a long, scaly tail, native to North America and introduced to Europe and Asia. It is known for its musky odor and valued for its fur.|The muskrat built its lodge in the marsh.|近义词: water rat, vole|反义词: None|Noun
