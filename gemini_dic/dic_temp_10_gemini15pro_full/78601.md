gibberish:/ˈɡɪbərɪʃ/ /ˈdʒɪbərɪʃ/| n. |胡言乱语，乱语，呓语|Unintelligible or nonsensical talk or writing.|例句：He was talking complete gibberish. 他说的完全是胡言乱语。|近义词：nonsense, babble, drivel, balderdash|反义词: sense, meaning|词性:名词


gibberish:/ˈɡɪbərɪʃ/ /ˈdʒɪbərɪʃ/| vi. |说胡话，说呓语|To speak unintelligibly or nonsensically.|例句：The patient started gibbering incoherently. 病人开始语无伦次地说胡话。|近义词：babble, ramble, drivel|反义词: articulate, enunciate|词性:动词
