moldboard:/ˈmoʊldbɔːrd/ (US) /ˈməʊldbɔːd/ (UK)| n. |犁壁; 翻土板|A curved metal plate on a plow that turns over the soil.|The farmer adjusted the moldboard to plow deeper.  农民调整了犁壁，以便犁得更深。|The moldboard sliced through the earth, turning the soil. 犁壁划破土地，翻转土壤。|近义词：ploughshare, share |反义词: None| noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *moldbrede*, from Old English *molde* (“earth, soil, dust”) + *brede* (“board”).
