diplodocus:/dɪˈplɒdəkəs/| n. |梁龙|A large herbivorous dinosaur of the Late Jurassic period, with a long neck and tail.|例句：The Diplodocus is one of the longest dinosaurs ever discovered. 梁龙是迄今为止发现的最长的恐龙之一。

例句：The museum's centerpiece is a complete Diplodocus skeleton.  博物馆的中心展品是一具完整的梁龙骨架。|近义词：sauropod|反义词:N/A|词性:noun
Etymology: From Greek diplóos "double" + dokos "beam," referring to the double-beamed chevron bones located in the underside of the tail.
