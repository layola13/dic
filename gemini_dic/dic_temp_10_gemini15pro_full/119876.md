militancy:/ˈmɪlɪtənsi/
noun
好战性，战斗性，交战状态
The state or quality of being militant.
The union's militancy has decreased in recent years.  工会的好战性近年来有所下降。

The growing militancy of the workers worried the management. 工人们日益增长的战斗性让管理层担忧。

近义词: belligerence, aggressiveness, combativeness
反义词: pacifism, passiveness, docility
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From militant + -cy.  From Latin mīlitāns, present participle of mīlitō (“serve as a soldier”), from mīles (“soldier”).
