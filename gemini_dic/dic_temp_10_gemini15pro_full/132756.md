nondominant:/nɒnˈdɒmɪnənt/ /ˌnɑːnˈdɑːmənənt/| adj.|非优势的，非支配的|Not dominant; referring to a side of the body (hand, foot, eye, etc.) that is used less often and with less skill than the dominant side.|She writes with her right hand; her left hand is nondominant. 她用右手写字；她的左手是非惯用手。|近义词：subordinate, secondary, weaker|反义词：dominant, primary, stronger|词性:形容词

nondominant:/nɒnˈdɒmɪnənt/ /ˌnɑːnˈdɑːmənənt/| n.|非优势手/眼/肢体|The nondominant hand, foot, eye, etc.|He injured his nondominant hand. 他伤了他的非惯用手。|近义词：subordinate hand/foot/eye|反义词：dominant hand/foot/eye|词性:名词
