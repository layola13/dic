hypermania:/ˌhaɪpərˈmeɪniə/ | n. | 躁狂症，极度狂躁 | A severe form of mania characterized by extreme excitement, euphoria, delusions, and overactivity.  |  She experienced a period of hypermania after winning the lottery. 她中彩票后经历了一段躁狂症时期。 |  Near synonyms:  mania, frenzy, delirium  |  Near antonyms: depression, lethargy, apathy | noun

hypermania:/ˌhaɪpərˈmeɪniə/ | n. |过度兴奋，极度活跃 | A state of extreme excitement and overactivity. | His hypermania made it difficult for him to focus on anything. 他的过度兴奋使他难以集中注意力。 |Near synonyms:  exuberance, elation, exhilaration | Near antonyms: calmness, tranquility, serenity | noun
