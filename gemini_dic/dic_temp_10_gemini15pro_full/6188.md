amma:/ˈæmə/ | n. | 妈妈，母亲 (South Indian, chiefly Tamil & Malayalam) | A term of endearment for mother, primarily used in South Indian languages like Tamil and Malayalam. | Example: Amma, I'm hungry. 妈妈，我饿了。 | Example:  Amma made a delicious meal. 妈妈做了一顿美味的饭菜。 | Synonym: mother, mom, mum | Antonym: (Generally no direct antonym for a familial term like "mother") | noun
