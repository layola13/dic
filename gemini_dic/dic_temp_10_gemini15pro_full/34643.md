clenched:/klɛntʃt/| adj.|紧握的，咬紧的，攥紧的|Firmly closed or squeezed together, often due to tension, anger, or determination.|He stood there, fists clenched, his face red with anger. 他站在那里，双拳紧握，脸涨得通红。|近义词：gripped, grasped, tightened|反义词: relaxed, open, unclenched|词性:形容词

clenched:/klɛntʃt/| v.| 握紧，咬紧，攥紧|(past tense and past participle of *clench*) To close or squeeze something tightly, especially your hand or teeth.|She clenched her fists in frustration. 她沮丧地握紧了拳头。|近义词：grip, grasp, tighten|反义词: relax, open, unclench|词性:动词
