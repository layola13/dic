husbanders:/ˈhʌzbəndərz/
n.|节俭的人，精打细算的人|A person who manages resources carefully and economically; a thrifty person.|The husbanders of the community carefully preserved their resources for the winter.|近义词：economizers, savers, conservers|反义词: spendthrifts, wasters|词性:名词

词源: From the verb "husband," meaning "to manage carefully," from Old Norse *hūs-bóandi, meaning "house dweller."
