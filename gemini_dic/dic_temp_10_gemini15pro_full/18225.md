belfry: /ˈbelfri/ (US), /ˈbelfri/ (UK)| n. |钟楼，钟塔|A bell tower, especially one attached to a church or other building; a room or framework in which bells are hung.|The bells in the belfry rang out across the town. 钟楼里的钟声响彻全城。|Synonyms: bell tower, campanile, steeple|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps ground floor?)|noun
