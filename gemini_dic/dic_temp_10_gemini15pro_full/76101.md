furry:/ˈfɜːri/ (US) /ˈfəːri/ (UK)| adj. n.|毛茸茸的；毛皮的；兽迷|Covered with fur; resembling fur; a person interested in anthropomorphic animals, often as a sexual fetish.|

例句：
The kitten was small and furry.
这只小猫又小又毛茸茸的。

The furry lining of the coat kept her warm.
外套的毛皮衬里让她很暖和。

He's a furry and enjoys attending conventions.
他是一个兽迷，喜欢参加相关展会。

近义词：hairy, fluffy, downy (adj.);  fursona (n. - specific to the fandom)|
反义词：bald, smooth, hairless|
词性: adjective, noun|
