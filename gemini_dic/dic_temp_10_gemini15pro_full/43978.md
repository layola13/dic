crikey:/ˈkraɪki/| interjection |哎呀；乖乖；我的天哪 |An exclamation of surprise or alarm. |例句：Crikey! That was a close call!  哎呀！那真是千钧一发！

例句：Crikey, it's cold out here! 乖乖，外面真冷！|近义词：gosh, jeepers, blimey |反义词: (No direct antonym for an interjection of surprise) |词性: interjection

PHRASE->

(Crikey doesn't typically form set phrases.  It's primarily a stand-alone interjection.  However, it can be used in context like the following):

"Crikey, what a mess!" = 哎呀，真是一团糟！
"Well, crikey!" =  哎呀！/ 真是的！
"Crikey, I didn't expect that!" = 哎呀，我没想到会这样！
"Crikey O'Reilly!" (an older, more emphatic variation) = 我的老天爷！
"Crikey, is that the time?" = 哎呀，都这个时间了？
