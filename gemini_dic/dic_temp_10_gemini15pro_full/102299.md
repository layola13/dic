Simple Present:
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? = 他在花园里做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? = 她用这笔钱做了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been studying at university? = 他在大学里一直在学什么？

Simple Past:
What did you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃了什么？
What did she say to him? = 她对他说了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before you went to bed? = 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had she seen before she screamed? = 她尖叫之前看到了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before I called? = 我打电话之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been planning for her birthday? = 她一直在计划她的生日什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this weekend? = 这个周末你会做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have achieved by the age of 30? = 到30岁时你将取得什么成就？
What will she have finished by the end of the day? = 到今天结束时她将完成什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been doing for the past five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业时她将已经学习什么多久了？
