brighter:/ˈbraɪtər/| adj.|更明亮的，更鲜艳的，更聪明的，更有希望的|Comparative form of "bright".  Meaning more intense in light or color, more intelligent, or more promising. |The sun is brighter today than yesterday. 今天太阳比昨天更明亮。|The future looks brighter now.  现在未来看起来更有希望了。|近义词：shinier, lighter, more intelligent, more promising|反义词：dimmer, darker, duller|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *brighter, *brightre, comparative of bright, from Old English *beorhtra, *bryhtra, comparative of beorht (“bright”).
