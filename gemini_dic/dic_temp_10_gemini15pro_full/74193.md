fourfooted:/ˈfɔːrˌfʊtɪd/| adj.|四足的；四脚的|Having four feet.  Relating to an animal that has four feet.|例句：A fourfooted beast emerged from the forest. 一只四足的野兽从森林里出现。

例句：Fourfooted animals are common in this area.  这个地区有很多四足动物。|近义词：quadruped, tetrapod|反义词:bipedal, two-footed|词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From four + footed.  "Footed" comes from "foot" + "-ed" (suffix meaning "having").  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *fot (foot).
