hitcher:/ˈhɪtʃər/| n.| 搭便车的人；请求搭便车的人 |A person who travels by getting free rides in other people's vehicles.  He picked up a hitcher on the way to Los Angeles. 他在去洛杉矶的路上载了一个搭便车的人。|Synonyms: hitchhiker, free rider|Antonyms: driver, passenger|noun
