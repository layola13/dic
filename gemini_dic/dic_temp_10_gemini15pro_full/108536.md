lazarwort:/ˈleɪzərwɜːrt/| n. |拉撒草，麻风草|A European plant (Laserpitium latifolium) of the carrot family, formerly believed to cure leprosy. Also, any of several other plants, such as sulfurwort (Peucedanum officinale).|Lazarwort was once a highly prized medicinal herb. 拉撒草曾经是一种非常珍贵的药草。|Lazarwort is said to have healing properties. 据说拉撒草具有愈合特性。|Synonyms: laserwort, masterwort|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
