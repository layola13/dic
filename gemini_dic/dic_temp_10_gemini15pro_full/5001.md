all-action:/ˌɔːlˈækʃən/| adj.|充满动作的；惊险刺激的；动作频频的|Characterized by much exciting action and often violence.|He starred in an all-action thriller. 他主演了一部充满动作的惊悚片。|近义词：action-packed, exciting, thrilling|反义词：boring, dull, uneventful|词性:形容词

词源:  Compound of "all" and "action". "All" emphasizes the pervasive nature of the action, meaning full of or involving constant action.
