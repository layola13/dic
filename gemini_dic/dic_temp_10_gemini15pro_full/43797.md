creams:/kriːmz/| v. (cream something) | 打成糊状；把…打成糊状；(尤指在烹饪中)将(黄油和糖)打成松软的奶油状 |To cream butter and sugar together means to beat them together until the mixture is soft, smooth, and creamy.|Cream the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.|近义词：blend, puree, whip|反义词: - |动词
