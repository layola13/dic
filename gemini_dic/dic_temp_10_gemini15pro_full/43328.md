cramoisie:/kræmˈwɑːzi/| adj.|深红色，鲜红色|A vivid crimson or purplish-red color.|The cramoisie curtains added a touch of drama to the room. 深红色的窗帘给房间增添了一丝戏剧性。|Synonyms: crimson, scarlet, ruby, vermilion|Antonyms: pale, light, dull|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *cramoisi*, from Medieval Latin *cremesinus* (referring to kermes, a red dye derived from insects), ultimately from Arabic *qirmiz* (crimson).
