overconcerned:/ˌoʊvər.kənˈsɜrnd/| adj.|过分关心的，过虑的|Excessively worried or anxious about someone or something.|She was overconcerned about her son's safety. 她过分担心她儿子的安全。|例句：He was overconcerned about his health after reading an article about a rare disease.  读了一篇关于罕见疾病的文章后，他过分担心自己的健康。|近义词：anxious, apprehensive, solicitous, worried|反义词：indifferent, unconcerned, apathetic|词性:形容词
词源: over- (prefix meaning "excessively") + concerned (adjective meaning "worried or anxious")
