agroforesters:/ˌæɡroʊˈfɔːrɪstərz/| n.|农林复合经营者，农林复合学家|People who practice or study agroforestry, a land use management system that integrates trees and shrubs into crop and/or livestock farming systems.  These individuals aim to create more diverse, productive, profitable, healthy, and sustainable land-use systems.|Agroforesters are working to develop sustainable farming practices in the region.  农林复合经营者正在该地区努力发展可持续农业实践。

The community hired agroforesters to help them integrate trees into their agricultural practices. 社区聘请了农林复合经营专家来帮助他们将树木融入农业实践中。|Synonyms: agroforestry practitioners, agroforestry specialists|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, but possibly) conventional farmers, monoculture farmers|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From *agroforestry* + *-er* (agent suffix).  *Agroforestry* is a blend of *agro-* (from Latin *ager*, "field") and *forestry*.
