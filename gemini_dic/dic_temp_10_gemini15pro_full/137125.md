nonsubscriptions:/ˌnɑːnsəbˈskrɪpʃənz/
|n.|非订阅，未订阅|The report analyzed the reasons for nonsubscriptions to the service. 该报告分析了未订阅该服务的原因。|The number of nonsubscriptions has decreased significantly. 未订阅数量已显著减少。|近义词:non-registrations, opt-outs|反义词:subscriptions, registrations|词性:名词

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  from Latin *non "not"
subscription (noun): from Latin subscriptionem (nominative subscriptio) "a writing underneath," noun of action from past-participle stem of subscribere "write underneath," from sub "under" + scribere "to write."
