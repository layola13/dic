asterolepis:/ˈæstərəʊˌlɛpɪs/ | n. |星鳞鱼（属）| An extinct genus of placoderm fish from the Devonian period, characterized by large bony plates covering its head and body.  These fish were among the largest vertebrates of their time. | Example: Asterolepis fossils have been found in Scotland and Russia. 星鳞鱼化石在苏格兰和俄罗斯被发现。| Example:  Asterolepis was a top predator in the Devonian ecosystems. 星鳞鱼是泥盆纪生态系统中的顶级掠食者。| Synonym:  (No direct synonym, but can be referred to as a "placoderm" more generally) | Antonym:  N/A | Noun
