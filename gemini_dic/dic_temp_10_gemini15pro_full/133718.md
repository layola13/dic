nonfrugally:/ˌnɒnˈfruːɡəli/
adverb
不节俭地，奢侈地
In a way that is not economical or thrifty.
She spent her inheritance nonfrugally, buying designer clothes and luxury cars. 她不节俭地花掉了遗产，购买名牌服装和豪车。
He furnished his apartment nonfrugally, opting for antique furniture and expensive artwork. 他不节俭地装修了他的公寓，选择了古董家具和昂贵的艺术品。
近义词: extravagantly, lavishly, profusely, wastefully
反义词: frugally, economically, thriftily, sparingly
词性: adverb
词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "frugal" (from Latin "frugalis" meaning "economical") + "-ly" (adverb suffix)
