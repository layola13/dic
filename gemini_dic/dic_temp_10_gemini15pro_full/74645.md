free-floater:/ˈfriː ˈfloʊtər/ | n. | 自由浮动者；自由职业者；无固定职业者 | A person or thing that is not tied down or attached to anything, often in a literal or metaphorical sense.  | The company has a number of free-floaters who work on different projects as needed.  这家公司有许多自由职业者，根据需要从事不同的项目。|Synonyms: independent contractor, freelancer, consultant | Antonyms: employee, staff member | noun


free-floater:/ˈfriː ˈfloʊtər/ | n. | 流动投票者；无党派选民 | A voter who is not affiliated with any particular political party and is therefore free to vote for any candidate. | Free-floaters can be a decisive factor in close elections. 流动投票者在势均力敌的选举中可以起到决定性作用。| Synonyms: independent voter, swing voter | Antonyms: party loyalist | noun


free-floater:/ˈfriː ˈfloʊtər/ | n. | (金融)自由流通股 | The portion of a company's shares that are readily available for trading on the open market. | The free float of the company's stock increased after the IPO. 公司股票的自由流通股在首次公开募股后增加了。| Synonyms: floating stock | Antonyms: restricted stock, locked-up shares | noun
