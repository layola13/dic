outbranched:/ˌaʊtˈbrɑːntʃt/
verb
多分支的;胜过
having produced more branches than another plant or animal; surpassed
The oak tree outbranched all the other trees in the forest.  这棵橡树的枝条比森林里所有其他树的枝条都多。
The company's sales outbranched its competitors'. 这家公司的销售额超过了竞争对手。
surpassed, exceeded, outstripped
--
verb
词源:  "out-" (超过) + "branch" (分支)
用法:  主要用于描述植物或动物的分支生长，引申义为超过，胜过。
