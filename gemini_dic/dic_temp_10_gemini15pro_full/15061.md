babyish:/ˈbeɪbiɪʃ/| adj.|幼稚的，孩子气的|Resembling or characteristic of a baby, especially in behavior; childish.|He spoke in a babyish voice. 他用幼稚的语气说话。|近义词：childish, infantile, immature|反义词：mature, adult, grown-up|词性:形容词
Etymology: baby + -ish (suffix forming adjectives meaning "like," "of the nature of," "somewhat," "rather")
