overpackages:/ˌoʊvərˈpækɪdʒɪz/
| v. |过度包装|To wrap or box (something) excessively, using more material than necessary.|The company overpackages its products, leading to a lot of waste.|近义词：overwrap|反义词: underpackage
|词性:动词


overpackage:/ˌoʊvərˈpækɪdʒ/
| n. |过度包装|Something that has been wrapped or boxed excessively.|The gift was a beautiful overpackage, but I felt bad about all the wasted paper.|近义词：excess packaging|反义词: minimal packaging
|词性:名词


词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "package" (noun and verb referring to wrapping or boxing).
