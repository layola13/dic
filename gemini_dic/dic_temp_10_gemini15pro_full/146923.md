overintellectually: /ˌoʊvərˌɪntəˈlɛktʃuəli/
| adv. | 过于理性地，过于智力化地 |  In a manner that is excessively intellectual; relying too much on intellect and not enough on intuition, emotion, or practical experience. |  He analyzed the situation overintellectually, missing the obvious emotional cues. 他过于理性地分析了情况，错过了明显的情绪暗示。| synonym: cerebrally, excessively intellectually | antonym: intuitively, emotionally, practically | 词性: 副词
