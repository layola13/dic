mysticities:/mɪˈstɪsɪtiz/
n.
神秘事物；奥秘；神秘性
Things that are mystic or mysterious; qualities of being mystic.
The speaker alluded to the mysticity of the ancient rituals. 演讲者提到了古代仪式的奥秘。
近义词:enigmas, mysteries, secrets, arcana
反义词:certainties, clarities, realities
词性:名词(plural)
