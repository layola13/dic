bylining: /ˈbaɪˌlaɪnɪŋ/ | n. |署名；冠名；标题下署名 | The act of crediting a journalist or author by name, usually at the beginning of an article or story. | Example 1: The bylining of the article clearly stated it was written by a guest contributor. 这篇文章的署名清楚地表明它是由一位特邀撰稿人撰写的。

Example 2:  Bylining her work gave the young journalist a sense of pride. 在她的作品上署名让这位年轻的记者感到自豪。 | Synonyms: attribution, crediting, acknowledgment | Antonyms: anonymity, uncredited | noun
