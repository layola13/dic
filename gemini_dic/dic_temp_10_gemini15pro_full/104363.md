如果您是想了解疑问词 "what" 的用法，我可以提供如下信息：

what: /wɒt/ (UK) /wʌt/ (US) | pron. det. adv. | 什么，多么 | used to ask for information about something | Example: What is your name? 你叫什么名字？ | Synonym: which | Antonym: N/A | pronoun, determiner, adverb

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you do for a living? = 你做什么工作？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing over there? = 他在那里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned so far? = 你到目前为止学到了什么？
What has she been working on? = 她一直在做什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been doing since I last saw you? = 自从我上次见到你以来，你一直在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened at the party? = 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she wearing to the party? = 她参加派对时穿了什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? = 你离开之前做了什么？
What had happened before the police arrived? = 警察到达之前发生了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? = 在她换专业之前，她一直在学习什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the meeting tomorrow? = 他们明天会在会议上讨论什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到今年年底你将取得什么成就？
What will have changed by the time we get back? = 等我们回来的时候会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? = 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? = 到她毕业的时候，她将已经学习什么专业多久了？
