page:/peɪdʒ/| n. v.|页，页面；（给…）标页码|A page is one side of a sheet of paper in a book, magazine, newspaper, or similar document.  As a verb, it means to number the pages of a document.|She tore a page out of her notebook. 她从笔记本上撕下一页。|He paged through the document quickly. 他快速翻阅了文件。|近义词：leaf, sheet, folio (n.); number (v.)|反义词: (n.) None. (v.) None|词性: noun, verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French "page" meaning "page of a book," derived from Latin "pagina" meaning "page, leaf, sheet of papyrus." Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European "*pag-" meaning "to fasten."
