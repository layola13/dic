flat-panel:/ˈflæt ˈpænəl/ | adj. n. |平板的；平板显示器|A flat-panel display is a type of display that uses a thin, flat panel to create an image. |  She bought a flat-panel TV for her living room. 她为她的客厅买了一台平板电视。 |  The new computer lab is equipped with flat-panel monitors. 新的电脑实验室配备了平板显示器。 | 近义词: flat-screen, thin-film | 反义词: CRT (cathode ray tube) | 词性: 形容词，名词

flat:/flæt/ | adj. n. adv. |平的；公寓；平坦地|Having a level surface, not sloping or curved. | The table surface is perfectly flat. 桌子的表面非常平坦。 | He lives in a flat in London. 他住在伦敦的一套公寓里。 | 近义词: level, even, smooth, apartment (n.) | 反义词: curved, bumpy, uneven, house (n.)| 词性: 形容词，名词，副词

panel:/ˈpænəl/ | n. |面板；专家组 | A distinct section of a wall, door, or other surface, typically rectangular. A group of specialists who give advice or make decisions. | The car door panel was damaged in the accident. 车门面板在事故中受损了。 | A panel of experts will review the proposal. 一个专家小组将审查该提案。 | 近义词: board, section, group, committee | 反义词: whole | 词性: 名词
