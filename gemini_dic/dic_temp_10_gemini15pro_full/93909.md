The immovability of the mountain is awe-inspiring.  这座山的巍然不动令人敬畏。|近义词：fixedness, firmness, steadfastness, stability|反义词：mobility, movability, flexibility, instability|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *immovabilitas, from Latin immovabilis (“immovable”) + -itas.  Equivalent to immov(able) + -ability.
