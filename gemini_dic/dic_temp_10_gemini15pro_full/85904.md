headhunting:/ˈhedˌhʌntɪŋ/
n.|猎头; 物色人才|The practice of approaching employed individuals and offering them another job.|例句：Headhunting has become a common practice in many industries. 猎头在很多行业已经成为了一种普遍的做法。|例句：The company hired a headhunting firm to find a new CEO.  这家公司聘请了一家猎头公司来寻找新的首席执行官。|近义词：recruitment, executive search|反义词: N/A|词性:名词
