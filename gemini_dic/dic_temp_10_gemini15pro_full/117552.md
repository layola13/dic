megacephalous:/ˌmɛɡəˈsɛfələs/| adj.|巨头的，头大的|Having an abnormally large head.  Characterized by excessive size of the head.|The child was diagnosed as megacephalous shortly after birth.|近义词：macrocephalic|反义词: microcephalous|词性: adjective

Etymology:
From mega- + -cephalous (from Greek kephalē “head”).
