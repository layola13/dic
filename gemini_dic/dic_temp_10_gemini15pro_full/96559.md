endowment:/ɪnˈdaʊmənt/ | n. | 捐赠，捐款；天赋，才能 |  Something that is bestowed; a gift.  The act of providing a permanent fund for support. Natural abilities or qualities. | The university received a large endowment for the new library. 大学收到了为新图书馆建设的大笔捐款。| Synonyms: gift, bequest, donation, grant, talent, aptitude, faculty, attribute | Antonyms: deprivation, divestment | noun

endowment:/ɪnˈdaʊmənt/ | n. | 捐赠，捐款；天赋，才能 | The act of giving or bequeathing something, especially property or money, to an institution, individual, or group.  A natural gift, ability, or quality. |  She has a natural endowment for music. 她有音乐天赋。 | Synonyms:  contribution, benefaction, provision, aptitude, flair, gift | Antonyms: debt, liability | noun
