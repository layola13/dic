cirrhosis:/sɪˈroʊsɪs/| n. |肝硬化|A chronic disease of the liver marked by degeneration of cells, inflammation, and fibrous thickening of tissue.  It is typically caused by alcoholism or hepatitis.| His heavy drinking led to cirrhosis of the liver.| 近义词: liver disease, hepatic fibrosis| 反义词:  (没有直接的反义词，可用healthy liver)|词性: 名词
