deckline: /ˈdɛklaɪn/ | n. | 甲板线，干舷 | The line formed by the intersection of the top of the deck and the side of the vessel.  | The deckline of the ship was barely above the water. 这艘船的甲板线几乎没露出水面。 | 近义词:  gunwale (specifically the upper edge of the side) | 反义词: keel (the bottom of the hull) | 词性: noun

Etymology:  "deck" + "line."  "Deck" comes from Middle Dutch *dec  and "line" from Old French ligne, ultimately from Latin linea.
