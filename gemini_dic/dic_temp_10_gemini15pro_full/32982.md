choreographed: /ˈkɔːriəˌɡræft/ (US) /ˈkɒriəˌɡrɑːft/ (UK)| adj.| 编排好的，精心设计的| arranged or planned in a skillful and elaborate way, especially in relation to dance or other performance | The dancers performed a beautifully choreographed routine.  舞者们表演了一段精心编排的舞蹈。| 近义词: staged, planned, rehearsed, organized, arranged | 反义词: improvised, impromptu, spontaneous | 词性: adjective

choreograph: /ˈkɔːriəˌɡræf/ (US) /ˈkɒriəˌɡrɑːft/ (UK)| vt.| 编舞，设计，安排| to design the steps and movements for a dance or other performance | She choreographed the opening ceremony of the Olympic Games. 她为奥运会开幕式编排了舞蹈。| 近义词: stage, plan, rehearse, organize, arrange | 反义词: improvise | 词性: verb

词源: From Greek *khoreographia*, from *khoreia* "dance in chorus" + *graphein* "to write."
