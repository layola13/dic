bookmarking:/ˈbʊkmɑːrkɪŋ/ | adj. |  书签的；与书签相关的 (较少用作形容词) | Relating to the act of bookmarking. |  Bookmarking features are essential in a good web browser.  好的网络浏览器中书签功能是必不可少的。| synonyms: saving, storing, tagging | antonyms: deleting, removing | adjective
