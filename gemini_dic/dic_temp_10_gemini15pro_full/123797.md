mopeful:/ˈmoʊpfəl/| adj.|闷闷不乐的；沮丧的|Characterized by moping; dejected.  Feeling gloomy and dispirited.|He sat in the corner, mopeful and silent after hearing the bad news.  他听到坏消息后，闷闷不乐地坐在角落里一言不发。|近义词：dejected, gloomy, downcast, glum, despondent|反义词：cheerful, happy, joyful, optimistic|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "mope" (to be dull and spiritless) + "-ful" (suffix indicating abundance).  The word "mope" likely comes from Dutch "moppen" meaning "to sulk."
