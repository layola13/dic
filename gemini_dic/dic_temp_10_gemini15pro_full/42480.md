counterflory:/ˈkaʊntərˌflɔːri/ /ˈkaʊntərˌflɔːri/ | n. | 反花饰，反瓣花饰 | An ornament or architectural detail, especially in Gothic architecture, resembling a stylized flower, used in a series and typically projecting upward from a molding or cornice. It's the opposite of a pendentive, which projects downward. | The elaborate counterflory along the chapel's roofline was a testament to the skill of the stonemasons. 教堂屋顶线上的精致反花饰证明了石匠的技艺。

The architect incorporated a delicate counterflory into the design of the archway.  建筑师将精致的反花饰融入到拱门的设中。| 近义词: crocket, finial, ornament | 反义词: pendentive | 词性: noun
