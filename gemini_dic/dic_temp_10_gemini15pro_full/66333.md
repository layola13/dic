excruciation:/ɪkˌskruːʃiˈeɪʃən/
n.
极度的痛苦；折磨
The process of inflicting severe pain or agony, both physical or mental.
The soldier endured months of excruciation as a prisoner of war.  这名士兵作为战俘忍受了数月的折磨。
agony, torment, torture
comfort, solace
noun
