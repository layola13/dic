confessorial:/kənˈfɛʃənəl/ | adj. |忏悔的；坦白的；告解的 | Characteristic of a confession; in the manner of a confession; revealing something private or embarrassing. | 例句：The novel has a confessorial tone, revealing the author's deepest fears. 这部小说带有忏悔的语气，揭示了作者内心深处的恐惧。

例句：He wrote a confessorial blog post about his struggles with addiction. 他写了一篇坦白自己与毒瘾斗争的博客文章。 | 近义词：frank, candid, intimate, revealing, open, honest | 反义词：secretive, reserved, guarded, evasive |词性: 形容词

confessor:/kənˈfɛsər/ | n. | 忏悔者；告解者；神父；听忏悔的神父| A person who confesses, especially their sins to a priest.  A priest who hears confessions and gives absolution. | 例句：The confessor listened patiently to the penitent's story. 忏悔神父耐心地听着忏悔者讲述自己的故事。

例句：In the Catholic Church, a priest acts as a confessor. 在天主教堂，神父充当忏悔神父的角色。| 近义词：penitent, priest | 反义词：None (This word doesn't have a direct antonym) |词性: 名词
