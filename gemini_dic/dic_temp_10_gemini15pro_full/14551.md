avert:/əˈvɜːrt/ (US) /əˈvɜːt/ (UK)| v. |避免，防止，转移（目光、注意力等）|To prevent something bad from happening; to turn away (one's eyes, gaze, etc.).|The company took steps to avert a financial crisis. 公司采取措施避免了一场金融危机。|He averted his eyes from the gruesome scene. 他把目光从可怕的景象中移开。|近义词：prevent, avoid, forestall, preclude, obviate, stave off|反义词：cause, incite, provoke, allow, permit|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *avertir "to warn, inform," from Latin avertere "to turn away, turn aside," from ab- "away" + vertere "to turn."
