auriferous:/ɔːˈrɪfərəs/
adjective
含金的；产金的
Containing gold; gold-bearing.
The auriferous rocks of the region attracted many prospectors.  该地区的含金岩石吸引了许多探矿者。
The mine is located in an auriferous zone.  该矿位于一个产金区。
Synonyms: gold-bearing, gold-producing, golden
Antonyms: barren, infertile
Etymology: From Latin *aurifer* (gold-bearing), from *aurum* (gold) + *-fer* (bearing).
