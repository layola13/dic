amphidromical:/ˌæmfɪˈdrɒmɪkəl/| adj.| 双潮的，无潮的| Relating to a point in a tidal system where the tide is negligible and the direction of the tidal current revolves through all points of the compass during each tidal period. | The amphidromic point in the North Sea experiences minimal tidal range. 北海的无潮点潮差极小。| The amphidromic system governs the tidal patterns in this region. 双潮系统控制着该地区的潮汐模式。| 近义词: tideless| 反义词: tidal| 词性:形容词

amphidromic:/ˌæmfɪˈdrɒmɪkəl/| n.| 无潮点| A point of zero amplitude of one harmonic constituent of the tide. | The amphidromic point is located near the center of the basin. 无潮点位于盆地中心附近。|  Scientists study amphidromic points to understand tidal behavior. 科学家研究无潮点以了解潮汐行为。| 近义词: nodal point| 反义词: antinode| 词性:名词
