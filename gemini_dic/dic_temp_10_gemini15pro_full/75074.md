frigidity:/frɪˈdʒɪdɪti/ /frɪˈdʒɪdəti/ | n. | 冷淡，冷漠；(尤指女性的)性冷淡 |Lack of emotional warmth or responsiveness; coldness. frigidity:/frɪˈdʒɪdɪti/ /frɪˈdʒɪdəti/ | n. | (气候的)严寒，酷寒 |Extreme coldness of climate or temperature. |The frigidity of the arctic air was biting. 北极空气的酷寒刺骨。|近义词：coldness, iciness|反义词：warmth, heat|词性:名词
