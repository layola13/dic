child-proof:/ˈtʃaɪldˌpruːf/
adj.
防儿童的，防止儿童误操作的
Designed to prevent children from opening, operating, or being harmed by something.
This medicine bottle is child-proof.  这个药瓶是防儿童开启的。
The cabinet locks are child-proof.  橱柜的锁是防儿童开启的。
近义词:child-resistant, tamper-proof
反义词:accessible to children
词性:形容词


childproof:/ˈtʃaɪldˌpruːf/
v.
使…防儿童开启（或误操作）
To make something safe for children by preventing them from opening, operating, or being harmed by it.
We need to childproof the house before the baby starts crawling. 我们需要在婴儿开始爬行之前做好房屋的儿童安全防护。
They childproofed the electrical outlets with plastic covers. 他们用塑料盖子保护电源插座，防止儿童触电。
近义词:child-resistant, secure
反义词: leave accessible to children
词性:动词


词源:
child (n.) + proof (adj. suffix)

"child" 源自古英语 "cild"，意为“小孩”。
"proof" 作为后缀源自古法语 "prove," 最终源自拉丁语 "probare"，意为“测试”或“证明”。"-proof" 作为后缀表示“防…的”或“抗…的”。
