calibrated:/ˈkælɪbreɪtɪd/ | adj. | 校准的，标定的 | Adjusted or standardized against a known scale or standard.  | The instrument was calibrated before the experiment. 仪器在实验前进行了校准。| 近义词: adjusted, standardized, measured, gauged | 反义词: uncalibrated, inaccurate, imprecise | 词性: adjective | 词源: From the verb "calibrate," which comes from Italian "calibro" (caliber, diameter of a bullet or gun barrel) and ultimately from Arabic "qālib" (mold).

calibrate:/ˈkælɪbreɪt/ | v. | 校准，标定 | To check or adjust (something, especially a measuring instrument) by comparison with a standard. |  We need to calibrate the equipment regularly to ensure accuracy. 我们需要定期校准设备以确保准确性。 | 近义词: adjust, standardize, measure, gauge | 反义词: miscalibrate | 词性: verb | 词源: From the verb "calibrate," which comes from Italian "calibro" (caliber, diameter of a gun barrel) and ultimately from Arabic "qālib" (mold).
