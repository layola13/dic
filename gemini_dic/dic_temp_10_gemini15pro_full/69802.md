ferns:/fɜːrnz/
n.
蕨类植物
Flowerless plants that have feathery or leafy fronds and reproduce by spores.
The forest floor was covered in ferns. 森林的地面上覆盖着蕨类植物。
近义词：bracken, pteridophyte
反义词：(none - ferns are a specific plant category)
词性:名词
