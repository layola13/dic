chivalry:/ˈʃɪvəlrɪ/ | n. |骑士精神；骑士制度；彬彬有礼|The system, spirit, or customs of medieval knighthood.  A courteous behavior, especially that of a man toward women.|He behaved with the utmost chivalry towards her. 他对她表现得极其彬彬有礼。|Synonyms:  courtliness, gallantry, courtesy, politeness|Antonyms: rudeness, discourtesy, boorishness|noun
