conversableness:/ˌkɒnvəˈseɪb(ə)lnəs/ /ˌkɑːnvərˈseɪb(ə)lnəs/
n.
健谈，爱说话，善于交谈
The quality of being conversable; inclination to converse.
Her conversableness made her a popular party guest. 她健谈的性格使她成为受欢迎的派对嘉宾。
近义词：talkativeness, sociability, affability, gregariousness
反义词：taciturnity, reticence, uncommunicativeness
词性: noun

Etymology:
From conversable + -ness.  Conversable from  converse (v.) + -able. Converse (v.) from Old French converser "to live with, keep company with," from Latin conversari "to associate with, live with," frequentative of convertere "to turn about, turn back, be transformed," from com- "together" (see com-) + vertere "to turn" (from PIE root *wer- (2) "to turn, bend").
