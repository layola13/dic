burbling:/ˈbɜːblɪŋ/ (US), /ˈbɜːblɪŋ/ (UK)| v.  n.|发出潺潺声；汩汩地流动；喋喋不休|making a gentle, bubbling sound; talking continuously and cheerfully about unimportant things|The brook burbled down the mountainside. 小溪沿着山坡汩汩流下。|近义词：gurgling, babbling, chattering, prattling|反义词：silent, quiet|词性: 动词/名词

burbling:/ˈbɜːblɪŋ/ (US), /ˈbɜːblɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|潺潺声；汩汩声；喋喋不休|the sound of water bubbling; continuous and cheerful talk about unimportant things|The burbling of the baby was music to her ears. 婴儿的咿呀声对她来说就像音乐一样。|近义词：gurgling, babbling, chattering, prattling|反义词：silence, quiet|词性: 名词

词源:  From *burble* + *-ing*.  *Burble* is likely imitative of the sound.
