contravene:/ˌkɒntrəˈviːn/ /ˌkɑːntrəˈviːn/
verb
违反，违背，抵触
To go against or break a law, rule, or agreement.
The company's actions contravened the agreement.  这家公司的行为违反了协议。
The new policy contravenes international law.  这项新政策违背了国际法。

近义词: violate, breach, infringe, disobey, defy
反义词: comply with, obey, observe, uphold, conform to
词性: verb
