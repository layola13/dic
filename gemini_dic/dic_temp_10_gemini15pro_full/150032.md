palpation:/pælˈpeɪʃən/ | n. |触诊|The act of feeling with the hand or fingers for the purpose of examining some part of the body.  |The doctor used palpation to check the patient's abdomen for swelling. 医生用触诊检查病人的腹部是否有肿胀。|近义词:examination, exploration |反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider: observation,  non-invasive examination) | noun
