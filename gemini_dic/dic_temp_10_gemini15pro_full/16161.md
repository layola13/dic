bankability:/ˌbæŋkəˈbɪləti/
n. |融資能力，銀行融資價值|The quality of being likely to attract investment or funding from banks.| The project's bankability was enhanced by government guarantees. 这个项目的银行融资能力因政府担保而增强。|  investability, financeability | unviability, unfundability | noun

bankabilities:/ˌbæŋkəˈbɪlətiz/
n. |(复数) 融资能力，可融资性| The plural form of bankability, referring to the various factors that make something attractive to banks for financing.|  The bankabilities of the different renewable energy projects were assessed.  对不同可再生能源项目的融资能力进行了评估。|investabilities, financeabilities| unviabilities, unfundabilities | noun
