eponym:/ˈɛpənɪm/ | n. | 名字的来源；同名的人或物 | A word derived from a person's name, or a person whose name is, or is thought to be, the source of the name of something.  |  The sandwich is an eponym, named after the Earl of Sandwich.  三明治是一个名字的来源，以三明治伯爵的名字命名。 | synonyms: namesake, namegiver | antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | noun

eponymous:/ɪˈpɒnɪməs/ | adj. | 同名的；名字来源于…的 | Giving one's name to something. | The eponymous hero of the novel is a young woman seeking her fortune. 小说的同名主人公是一位寻求财富的年轻女子。 | synonyms: namesake, titled | antonyms:  (No direct antonym) | adjective
