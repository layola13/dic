fuelled:/ˈfjuːəld/ (UK) /ˈfjuːld/ (US)| v. | 加燃料；激起；刺激 | past tense and past participle of fuel  |

例句：
The growth in sales was fuelled by strong demand in Asia.
亚洲的强劲需求推动了销售增长。

The rumours fuelled speculation about her future.
这些谣言激起了对她未来的猜测。

近义词：powered, driven, propelled, stimulated, encouraged
反义词：extinguished, dampened, suppressed
词性：动词 (past participle)


ETYMOLOGY->
From fuel + -ed. Ultimately from Old French feuaille "firewood."
