macrolinguistics:/ˌmækroʊlɪŋˈɡwɪstɪks/ | adj. n.| 宏观语言学 |The study of language in relation to social, cultural, and historical factors, rather than the internal structure of language itself.  It encompasses areas like sociolinguistics, historical linguistics, language typology, and dialectology. | Macrolinguistics examines how language is used in different social contexts. 宏观语言学研究语言在不同社会环境中的使用。 | Macrolinguistics helps us understand the evolution of languages. 宏观语言学帮助我们理解语言的演变。 | 近义词: sociolinguistics, anthropological linguistics | 反义词: microlinguistics | 词性: 名词, 形容词
