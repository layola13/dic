delta:/ˈdeltə/ (US), /ˈdɛltə/ (UK)| n.|三角洲; (字母表中) 第四个字母; 变化, 差额|A triangular tract of land formed at the mouth of a river, typically where it diverges into several outlets.|The Nile delta is a fertile region. 尼罗河三角洲是一片肥沃的区域.|Synonyms: estuary, mouth, confluence|Antonyms: (No direct antonyms for a geographical feature)|noun

delta:/ˈdeltə/ (US), /ˈdɛltə/ (UK)| n.|（数学符号）Δ，δ|The fourth letter of the Greek alphabet (Δ, δ), transliterated as ‘d’.|The delta symbol represents change.  δ符号代表变化。|Synonyms: Δ, δ (symbols)|Antonyms: N/A|noun

delta:/ˈdeltə/ (US), /ˈdɛltə/ (UK)| n.|差值，差额;变化量|A difference or change.|There was a significant delta between the projected and actual sales figures. 预计销售额和实际销售额之间存在显著差异。|Synonyms: difference, change, variance, deviation|Antonyms: similarity, constancy|noun
