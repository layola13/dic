outjourneys:/ˈaʊtˌdʒɜːniz/
n.
外出旅行；远行
journeys away from a place, especially ones that are long or difficult
例句：Their outjourneys to remote villages were arduous but rewarding. 他们前往偏远村庄的旅程 arduous but rewarding.
例句：The outjourneys of the early explorers were fraught with danger. 早期探险家的远行充满了危险。
近义词：expeditions, travels, voyages
反义词：homecomings, returns
词性：名词
