nightclubbing:/ˈnaɪtˌklʌbɪŋ/
n.
夜总会活动；上夜总会
The activity of visiting nightclubs.
例句：She enjoys nightclubbing with her friends. 她喜欢和朋友们一起去夜总会。

例句：Nightclubbing is popular among young people.  上夜总会很受年轻人欢迎。

近义词：clubbing, partying, going out
反义词：staying in


词性: noun

词源:  A combination of "nightclub" (itself a compound of "night" and "club") and the suffix "-ing", which turns a noun into a gerund, indicating an activity or process.
