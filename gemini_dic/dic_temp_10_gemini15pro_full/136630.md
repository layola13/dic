nonscarcity:/nɒnˈskeəsɪti/ | n. | 非稀缺性 | The state or quality of not being scarce; abundance.  A resource is nonscarce if it is available in unlimited quantities. |  Digital information exhibits nonscarcity, as it can be easily copied and shared. 数字信息展现出非稀缺性，因为它可以轻松复制和共享。| synonyms: abundance, plentifulness, profusion | antonyms: scarcity, lack, dearth | noun
