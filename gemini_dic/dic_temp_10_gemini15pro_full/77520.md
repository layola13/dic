gazump:/ɡəˈzʌmp/ | vt. | 抬高要价（尤指在最后时刻）; 抢购（尤指以更高的价格）| To raise the price of something (especially a house) that has already been agreed upon, often at the last minute, in order to profit from another buyer's offer.  Also, to outbid someone (especially by offering a higher price). |  The seller gazumped us by accepting a higher offer from someone else. 卖家抬高了要价，接受了别人的更高报价。| synonyms: outbid, overbid, up the ante | antonyms: underbid, undercut | verb
