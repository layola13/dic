outpace:/ˌaʊtˈpeɪs/| v. |超越，胜过|To go faster, perform better, or develop more quickly than someone or something else.|Electric cars are starting to outpace gasoline-powered cars in sales. 电动汽车的销量开始超过汽油动力汽车。|The runner outpaced his rivals in the final lap. 这位赛跑者在最后一圈超过了他的对手。|近义词：surpass, exceed, outstrip, outrun|反义词：lag behind, trail|词性:动词

词源: out- (prefix meaning "outside, beyond") + pace (verb meaning "to walk or run at a steady speed")
