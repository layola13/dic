non-reason:/ˌnɒnˈriːzn/ /ˌnɑːnˈriːzn/ | n. | 非理由，不合理的原因 |An explanation or justification that is not based on logic or evidence.  A pretext or excuse that lacks validity. |  He gave some non-reason for being late, something about a traffic jam that didn't exist. 他给迟到找了个不合理的理由，说什么交通堵塞，其实根本没有。 | Synonyms: pretext, excuse, fabrication, sham, fallacy | Antonyms: reason, justification, logic, rationale | noun

non-reason:/ˌnɒnˈriːzn/ /ˌnɑːnˈriːzn/ | adj. | 非理性的，无根据的 |Not based on reason or logic. | His non-reason argument made no sense. 他无根据的论点毫无意义。 | Synonyms: illogical, irrational, unreasonable, groundless, unfounded | Antonyms: reasonable, logical, rational, valid, sound | adjective
