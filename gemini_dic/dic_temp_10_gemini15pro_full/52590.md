digamous:/ˈdɪɡəməs/
adj.
两次结婚的；再婚的
Having been married twice.
He was a digamous man, having lost his first wife to illness. 他是一个再婚的男人，他的第一任妻子因病去世。
The digamous celebrity kept details of his first marriage private.  这位再婚的名人对他第一次婚姻的细节保密。
近义词: remarried, twice-married
反义词: single, unmarried
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *digamus (unattested), from Greek δίγαμος (dígamos, “married twice”), from δίς (dís, “twice”) + γάμος (gámos, “marriage”).
