inspographic: /ˌɪnspəˈɡræfɪk/
n.
灵感图表; 激励图片
A visual representation, often a combination of image and text, designed to inspire or motivate.
例句：She created an inspographic about achieving your dreams. 她创作了一张关于实现梦想的灵感图表。

例句：The inspographic on healthy eating motivated him to change his diet.  这张关于健康饮食的激励图片促使他改变了饮食习惯。

近义词：mood board, vision board, motivational poster
反义词: demotivational poster
词性: noun
