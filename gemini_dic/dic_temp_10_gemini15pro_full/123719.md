moonlights:/ˈmuːnlaɪts/ | v. (third-person singular simple present moonlights, present participle moonlighting, simple past and past participle moonlighted) |  兼职；从事第二职业 | To work at a second job, typically secretly and at night, in addition to one's regular employment. | He moonlights as a bartender to earn extra cash. 他兼职做酒保赚外快。| synonyms: works a second job, has a side hustle, freelances | antonyms: unemployed, jobless | verb
