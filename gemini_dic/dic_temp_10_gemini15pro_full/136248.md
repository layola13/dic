nonrepayable:/ˌnɒnriˈpeɪəbl/ | adj. | 不可偿还的，无须偿还的 | Not required to be repaid.  | Example: The grant is nonrepayable, so you don't have to worry about paying it back. 这笔赠款是无须偿还的，所以你不用担心要还钱。 | Example:  He received a nonrepayable loan to start his business. 他获得了一笔无须偿还的贷款来创业。 | Synonyms: non-refundable, grant, unforgivable | Antonyms: repayable, refundable, recoverable | word property: adjective
