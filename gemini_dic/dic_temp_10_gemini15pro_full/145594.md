overcondense:/ˌoʊvərˈkənˈdɛns/ | v. | 过度压缩；浓缩过度 | To condense excessively; to make too dense or compact. | The author overcondensed the information, making it difficult to understand.  作者过度压缩了信息，使其难以理解。| The chef overcondensed the sauce, making it too thick.  厨师把酱汁浓缩过度了，太稠了。| 近义词: overconcentrate, thicken, compress | 反义词: dilute, expand, thin | 词性: 动词

词源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "condense" (from Latin "condensare," meaning "to make dense")
