breviary:/ˈbriːviəri/ (US), /ˈbreɪviəri/ (UK)| n.|祈祷书；摘要；概要|A book containing the daily prayers, hymns, and readings prescribed for use in the Divine Office, especially in the Roman Catholic Church. Also, a short summary or abridgment of a larger work.|The priest read from the breviary each morning.  神父每天早上读祈祷书。|He carried a breviary with him on his travels. 他旅行时随身带着一本祈祷书。|Synonyms: prayer book, missal, liturgical book, compendium, abstract, summary, epitome|Antonyms: expansion, elaboration|noun
