Example: She gave a curter explanation this time.  这次她给出了一个更简洁的解释。

近义词: shorter, briefer, more concise, abrupt, terse|反义词: longer, more verbose, prolix|词性:形容词


词源:  From Middle English *curt*, from Old French *curt*, from Latin *curtus* ("short"), originally the past participle of *cortare*, from Proto-Italic *kortāō, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *kert- (“short”).
