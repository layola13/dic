egocentric:/ˌiːɡoʊˈsɛntrɪk/ | adj. n. |自我中心的；利己主义者|Thinking only about yourself and your own needs; self-centered. |例句：He's so egocentric he can't see things from anyone else's perspective. 他太以自我为中心了，无法从别人的角度看待问题。

例句：She's an egocentric who only cares about her own success. 她是一个自私自利的人，只关心自己的成功。 |近义词：self-centered, selfish, narcissistic |反义词：altruistic, selfless |词性: adjective, noun

egocentrics:/ˌiːɡoʊˈsɛntrɪks/ | n. |自我中心者; 利己主义者(复数)| People who are egocentric. |例句：Egocentrics often have difficulty forming lasting relationships.  自我中心者通常难以建立持久的关系。

例句：The egocentrics in the group were constantly vying for attention.  小组中的自我中心者不断争夺注意力。|近义词：egoists, narcissists |反义词：altruists |词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Greek ego (I) + Latin centrum (center).  "Ego" refers to the self, and "centric" means centered. Thus, egocentric literally means "self-centered."
