acing-it:/ˈeɪsɪŋ ɪt/| informal adj. |做得非常好，轻松应对|Succeeding or performing exceptionally well, often with ease.  Derived from "acing" a test, meaning to get a perfect score.  "It" refers to the task or challenge being undertaken.|例句：He's acing it in his new job. 他在新工作中表现出色。

She's acing it on the guitar. 她的吉他弹得非常好。|近义词：excelling, thriving, mastering, nailing it, killing it|反义词:failing, struggling, bombing|词性:形容词
