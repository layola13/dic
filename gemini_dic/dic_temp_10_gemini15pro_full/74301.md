fractionation:/ˌfrækʃəˈneɪʃən/
n.
分馏，分级，分步提取
the process of separating a mixture into its components by making use of their different boiling points.
Crude oil is refined by fractional distillation. 原油通过分馏提炼。
separation, division, segregation
combination, unification
noun
