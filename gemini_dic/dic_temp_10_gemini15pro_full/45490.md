curdier:/ˈkɜːrdiər/| adj.|更凝乳的；更像凝乳的|More like curd; resembling curd more closely.  Containing or producing more curd.|This cheese is curdier than the other one.  这种奶酪比另一种更凝乳。|近义词：coagulated, thickened|反义词:runny, thin|词性:形容词
Etymology: From curd + -ier (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from Middle English crud, from Old English crude.
