hydrodynamics:/ˌhaɪdrəʊdaɪˈnæmɪks/ | n. | 流体力学 | The branch of physics concerned with the motion of fluids (liquids or gases) and the forces acting on solid bodies immersed in fluids. | Example 1: The hydrodynamics of the ship's hull were carefully considered in its design.  这艘船船体的流体力学特性在设计时被仔细考虑。

Example 2:  Understanding hydrodynamics is essential for designing efficient pipelines. 理解流体力学对于设计高效的管道至关重要。 | Near synonyms: fluid mechanics, fluid dynamics | Antonym: N/A | Noun
