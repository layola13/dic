half-rayon:/hæfˈreɪɒn/ | adj. n. | 半人造丝的，半人造丝织物 | Made of a blend of rayon and another fiber, typically cotton. | This shirt is made of half-rayon, half-cotton. 这件衬衫是由半人造丝半棉制成的。 |  The half-rayon drapes beautifully. 这块半人造丝垂坠感很好。 | 近义词: rayon blend, semi-synthetic | 反义词: pure cotton, all-natural | 词性: 形容词，名词

Etymology:
half (Old English healf) + rayon (from French rayonne, meaning "rayon," coined in the early 20th century)
