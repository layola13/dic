brilliantness:/ˈbrɪljəntnəs/
n.
光辉;才华;卓越;杰出
great intelligence or skill; the quality of being brilliant
Her writing shines with wit and brilliance. 她的作品闪耀着智慧和才华。
Synonyms: radiance, luminosity, splendor, genius, intelligence, brilliance
Antonyms: dullness, dimness, mediocrity
noun
