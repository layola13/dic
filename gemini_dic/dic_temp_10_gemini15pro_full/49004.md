degradation:/ˌdeɡrəˈdeɪʃn/ | n. | 退化；降解；堕落 |The degradation of the environment is a serious problem. 环境恶化是一个严重的问题。|  The moral degradation of society is a worrying trend. 社会道德沦丧是一个令人担忧的趋势。 | deterioration, decline, decay |  improvement, enhancement | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *degradationem (nominative degradatio), noun of action from past-participle stem of degradare “to reduce in rank,” from de- “down” + gradus “step, rank.”
