gestural:/ˈdʒɛstʃərəl/| adj.|姿态的；手势的|Relating to, or expressed by, gestures.|Her teaching style was very gestural, using hand movements to emphasize points.|近义词：nonverbal, expressive, communicative|反义词:verbal, spoken|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From gesture + -al.  Ultimately from Latin *gestūra "bearing, carriage, posture," from gestus, past participle of gerere "to carry, bear, manage, administer, perform," from PIE root *ǵhes- "to reach, extend, project, stick out."
