craterous:/ˈkreɪtərəs/| adj.|布满陨石坑的；像火山口的|Resembling a crater; having many craters.  Marked by or resembling craters; pitted.|The moon's surface is craterous. 月球表面布满了陨石坑。|The asteroid had a craterous landscape. 这颗小行星的地貌布满了陨石坑。|近义词：pitted, pocked,  cratered|反义词:smooth, flat|词性:形容词
