dogwatch:/ˈdɒɡwɒtʃ/ (UK) /ˈdɔːɡwɑːtʃ/ (US)| n. |更次,夜班|A period of watch duty on a ship, generally one of two short watches (4-6 pm or 6-8 pm) between the afternoon and first night watches.|The sailors on the dogwatch were responsible for keeping an eye out for other ships.  船上的水手在更次期间负责注意其他船只。| The crew dreaded the dogwatch because it disrupted their sleep.  船员们害怕更次,因为它扰乱了他们的睡眠。|近义词:shift, watch|反义词: (No direct antonym, but could be contrasted with) daywork|词性:名词
