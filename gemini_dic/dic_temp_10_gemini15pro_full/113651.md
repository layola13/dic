machineability:/məˌʃiːnəˈbɪləti/| n.|可加工性，机械加工性|The ease with which a material can be machined. This depends on several factors, including hardness, ductility, and the type of material.|The machineability of aluminum is high, making it a popular choice for manufacturing.  铝的可加工性很高，因此是制造业的热门选择。|Synonyms: workability, manufacturability|Antonyms: intractability, unworkability|noun
