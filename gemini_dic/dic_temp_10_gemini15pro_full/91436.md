hygienically:/ˌhaɪdʒɪˈɛnɪkli/ | adv. | 卫生地，干净地 | In a way that maintains health and prevents disease, by being clean. | The food was prepared hygienically. 食物是卫生准备的。| The surfaces were hygienically cleaned. 表面经过卫生清洁。 | 近义词: cleanly, sanitarily | 反义词: dirtily, unhygienically | 词性: 副词

词源: From hygiene + -ically.  Hygiene comes from Greek *hygieinos*, from *Hygieia*, the goddess of health.
