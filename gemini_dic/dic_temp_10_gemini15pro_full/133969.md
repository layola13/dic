nonheroical:/ˌnɒnhɪˈrəʊɪkəl/
adjective
非英雄的；不英勇的
Not heroic; lacking the qualities of a hero; undistinguished.
His actions were distinctly nonheroical, choosing to hide rather than confront the danger. 他选择躲避而不是面对危险，他的行为明显缺乏英雄气概。
