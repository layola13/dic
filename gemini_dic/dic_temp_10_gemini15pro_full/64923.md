establishment: /ɪˈstæblɪʃmənt/ | n. | 建立；机构；企业；当权派 | the act of establishing something or the state of being established; a business or other organization, or the place where an organization operates; the ruling class or inner circle of a society |  The establishment of the new company took several months. 新公司的建立花了几个月的时间。| synonyms: foundation, institution, organization, corporation, business, firm, the powers that be, the ruling class | antonyms: abolition, closure, destruction | noun

establishment: /ɪˈstæblɪʃmənt/ | n. | 体制；权势集团 | the existing power structure in society; the dominant group or elite that controls a nation or organization |  The anti-establishment movement gained momentum. 反建制运动势头强劲。| synonyms: the system, the powers that be, the ruling class, the elite | antonyms: the counterculture, the underground | noun
