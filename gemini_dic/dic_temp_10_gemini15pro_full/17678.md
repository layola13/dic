becomings:/bɪˈkʌmɪŋz/
noun
变成；适合；相称；合适的行为；得体
the process of coming into being or of becoming something specified.  Appropriate behavior or dress.
Her becomings as a queen were impeccable. 她作为女王的举止无可挑剔。
Synonyms:  development, evolution, maturation, growth, progress, transformation, metamorphosis,  suitability, appropriateness, decorum, propriety
Antonyms:  decline, regression, deterioration, decay, atrophy,  unsuitability, inappropriateness, impropriety
词性: 名词
