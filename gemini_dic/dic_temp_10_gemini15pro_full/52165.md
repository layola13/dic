diaphragmatically:/ˌdaɪəfræɡˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 用横膈膜地；隔膜地 |  In a manner relating to or involving the diaphragm, the muscle separating the chest from the abdomen. | She breathed diaphragmatically, deeply filling her lungs. 她用横膈膜呼吸，深深地充满了她的肺。 | He spoke diaphragmatically, projecting his voice powerfully. 他用横膈膜发声，有力地投射出他的声音。 |  deeply, abdominally | shallowly, chestly | adverb
