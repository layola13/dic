medley:/ˈmedli/ | n. |混合物，杂烩，混杂物 | A mixture of different things, especially a musical piece made up of parts of other pieces. | The concert ended with a medley of popular songs. 音乐会以一首流行歌曲串烧结束。|  synonyms: mixture, assortment, blend, potpourri, miscellany, anthology, compilation | antonyms: uniformity, homogeneity | noun

medley:/ˈmedli/ | n. |混合泳 | A swimming race in which the swimmers use a different stroke for each part. | She won the gold medal in the 400-meter individual medley. 她在400米个人混合泳中赢得了金牌。 | synonyms: mixed race (in swimming) | antonyms: single-stroke race | noun
