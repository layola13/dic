lineolate:/ˈlaɪniəˌleɪt/| v. |用线标出；画线于|To mark with lines.|He lineolated the map to show the route. 他在地图上画线标出了路线。|近义词：mark, delineate, outline|反义词: erase, obliterate|词性:动词

词源: From Latin *lineolatus*, past participle of *lineolare* "to mark with little lines," from *lineola*, diminutive of *linea* "line."
