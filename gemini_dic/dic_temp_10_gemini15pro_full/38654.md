concurringly:/kənˈkɜːrɪŋli/
adv.
同时地，一致地
happening at the same time; in agreement
The two events occurred concurringly. 这两件事同时发生。
Synonym: simultaneously, concurrently, at the same time
Antonym: separately, independently, asynchronously
词性: 副词
