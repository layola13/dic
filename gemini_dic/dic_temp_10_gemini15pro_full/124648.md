mouthingly:/ˈmaʊðɪŋli/ | adv. | 默默地，无声地，用唇语 | In a manner that involves moving the lips as if speaking, but without producing sound.  Expressing something with the lips without uttering audible words. | She thanked him mouthingly across the crowded room. 她隔着拥挤的房间无声地向他道谢。| He repeated the instructions mouthingly, trying not to disturb the sleeping baby. 他默默地重复着指示，尽量不吵醒熟睡的婴儿。 | silently, noiselessly, wordlessly | audibly, aloud | adverb
