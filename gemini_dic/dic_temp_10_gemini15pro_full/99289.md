intercoordinates:/ˌɪntərkoʊˈɔːrdɪnɪts/ | n. |相互坐标，互相协调 |A system or framework that establishes relationships or connections between different coordinates or systems, enabling interaction or interoperability. |Example: The intercoordinates of the two systems allowed for seamless data transfer. 两个系统的相互坐标允许无缝的数据传输。 |Example:  Understanding the intercoordinates of the various departments is crucial for effective project management. 了解各部门间的相互协调对于有效的项目管理至关重要。 | 近义词: interconnection, interrelation, integration | 反义词: isolation, separation | 词性: 名词

词源: "inter-" (prefix meaning "between, among, mutually") + "coordinates" (plural noun referring to a set of values that determine the position of a point in space).  The term is a relatively modern formation, likely arising with the increased need for systems to interact and exchange data.
