barned:/bɑːrnd/ | adj. | 有谷仓的；储存（或收藏）在谷仓中的 |  Having a barn or barns; stored or kept in a barn. | The farmer's barned hay provided food for his livestock through the winter. 农民储存的干草在整个冬天为他的牲畜提供食物。| Example: The barned owl is a common species in North America. 谷仓猫头鹰是北美的一种常见物种. | Synonyms: stored, housed | Antonyms: exposed, unsheltered | word type: adjective

barned:/bɑːrnd/ |  v. (past participle of barn) | 把…贮存入仓 | To store (something) in a barn. |  The farmer barned the hay for the winter. 农民把干草储存起来过冬。| Example: The classic cars were carefully barned to protect them from the elements. 古董车被小心地存放在谷仓里，以保护它们免受风雨侵蚀。 | Synonyms: store, house | Antonyms: remove, expose | word type: verb
