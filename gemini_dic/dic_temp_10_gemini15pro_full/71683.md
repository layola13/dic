flauntier:/ˈflɔːntɪər/ | adj. | 更炫耀的，更招摆的 | More flaunting; showing off more. | She wore a flauntier dress than usual to the party, hoping to attract attention. 她穿着比平时更招摇的裙子去参加派对，希望能引起注意。|  She gave a flauntier wave than she intended, accidentally knocking over a vase. 她挥了挥手，比预想的更夸张，不小心碰倒了一个花瓶。 | 近义词:showier, ostentatious, flamboyant | 反义词:modest, understated, discreet | 词性:形容词
词源: From flaunt + -ier (comparative suffix).  Ultimately from Old Norse *flanta "to hasten, hurry."
