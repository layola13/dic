dog-house: /ˈdɒɡˌhaʊs/ (British) /ˈdɑːɡˌhaʊs/ (American) | n. | 狗窝；困境；失宠 | A small shelter for a dog to sleep in. Also, informally, a state of disfavor. | The dog spent most of the day in his dog-house. 狗狗一天的大部分时间都在它的狗窝里度过。

He was in the dog-house after forgetting his wife's birthday.  他忘记了妻子的生日，所以现在很失宠。 | 近义词：kennel, doghouse (informal), in bad books (informal) | 反义词: favor | 词性: 名词
