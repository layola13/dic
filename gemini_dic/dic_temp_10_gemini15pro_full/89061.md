holophanerous:/ˌhɒləˈfænərəs/
adjective
完全透明的；透亮的
Entirely transparent or translucent; able to transmit light throughout.
The holophanerous wings of the dragonfly shimmered in the sunlight.  蜻蜓透明的翅膀在阳光下闪闪发光。
Synonyms:  diaphanous, transparent, translucent, pellucid
Antonyms:  opaque, cloudy, turbid
Etymology:  From Greek *holophanēs* "wholly shining," from *holos* "whole" + *phainein* "to show, appear."
word type: adjective
