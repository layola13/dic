genes:/dʒiːnz/ | n. |基因| The basic physical and functional unit of heredity made up of DNA. |  Scientists have identified the gene responsible for the disease. 科学家已经确定了导致这种疾病的基因。| synonyms: DNA, chromosome, genotype | antonyms:  (Genes don't have a direct opposite. Perhaps a relevant antonym in certain contexts could be "phenotype," which is the observable expression of genes.) | noun
