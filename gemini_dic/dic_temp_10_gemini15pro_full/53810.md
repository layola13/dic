disconformable:/dɪskənˈfɔːrməbl/
adj.
不整合的
Characterized by a break in the stratigraphic sequence, indicating a period of erosion or non-deposition.  A disconformity is an unconformity between parallel layers of sedimentary rocks which represents a period of erosion or non-deposition.
The geologist identified a disconformable contact between the two rock layers.  地质学家发现了两个岩层之间的不整合接触面。
Near synonyms: nonconformable, unconformable
Antonyms: conformable, concordant
词性: 形容词
