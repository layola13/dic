hedra:/ˈhɛdrə/| n. |(plural of hedron) 面; (尤指多面体的)面|A polyhedron is a solid figure with many plane faces, typically more than six. |Example:  A cube has six hedra. 一个立方体有六个面。

Example: The icosahedron has twenty hedra. 二十面体有二十个面。|近义词：faces, surfaces|反义词: N/A|词性:Noun
