immix:/ɪmˈɪks/ | v. | 混合，掺杂 | To mix; to blend. |  She immixed the different types of flour for the bread. 她把不同种类的面粉混合起来做面包。 | 近义词: mix, blend, combine, mingle | 反义词: separate, divide, unmix | 词性: 动词


词源:

From Latin *immixtus, past participle of *immiscēre, from in- 'in' + miscēre 'to mix'.
