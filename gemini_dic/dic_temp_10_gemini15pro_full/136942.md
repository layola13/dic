nonspeaking: /ˈnɒnˈspiːkɪŋ/ (British) /ˈnɑːnˈspiːkɪŋ/ (American)
adjective
不会说话的；不说话的
Not able to speak, especially because of a disability.
The play included several nonspeaking roles for children.  这出戏里有几个为孩子们安排的不用说话的角色。
The film sensitively portrays the challenges faced by nonspeaking autistic individuals. 这部电影敏锐地描绘了不会说话的自闭症患者所面临的挑战。

近义词: mute, silent, voiceless
反义词: speaking, vocal
词性: adjective
