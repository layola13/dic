feared:/fɪrd/ | adj. | 害怕的，恐惧的 |  Feeling or showing fear; causing fear.  | The villagers feared the dragon that lived in the cave. 村民们害怕住在山洞里的龙。 | 近义词: dreaded, apprehensive, afraid | 反义词: unafraid, fearless, brave | 词性:形容词

词源:  From Middle English *feren, from Old English *fǣran, from Proto-Germanic *fērōną (“to frighten”), from Proto-Indo-European *per- (“to strike, hit, frighten”). Cognate with West Frisian fierre (“to frighten”), Dutch veren (“to frighten”), German fürchten (“to fear”).
