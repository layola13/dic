depredator:/ˈdɛprɪdeɪtər/ (US), /ˈdɛprədeɪtər/ (UK)| n. |掠夺者，捕食者|An animal that hunts and kills other animals for food; someone who robs or exploits others.|例句：The lion is a major depredator in the African savanna. 狮子是非洲大草原上的主要捕食者。

例句：The company was accused of being a depredator of the environment. 这家公司被指控破坏环境。|近义词：predator, raider, plunderer, looter, spoiler|反义词：prey, victim|词性：noun
