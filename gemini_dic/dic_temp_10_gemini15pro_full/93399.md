illish:/ˈɪlɪʃ/| adj.|略有不适的，有点儿不舒服的|Somewhat ill or unwell; slightly indisposed.  This word is often used to describe a mild or vague feeling of sickness. |例句：I'm feeling a bit illish today. 我今天感觉有点不舒服。

例句：She looked illish and went home early. 她看起来有点不舒服，就早早回家了。|近义词：unwell, poorly, peaked, off-color|反义词:well, healthy|词性:形容词
