fomo:/ˈfoʊmoʊ/| n.|害怕错过 (Fear Of Missing Out)|The fear of missing out (FOMO) is a social anxiety stemming from the belief that others are having rewarding experiences that one is missing.  It is characterized by a compulsive concern that one might miss out on something, typically a social event, a new experience, or a profitable investment.|She didn't want to go to the party, but FOMO got the better of her.|近义词：anxiety, unease|反义词：contentment, satisfaction|词性:noun
