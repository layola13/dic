half-theatrical:/ˌhæf θiˈætrɪkəl/
adjective
半戏剧化的；略带戏剧性的
Characterized by exaggerated or dramatic behavior, but not fully or genuinely so.  A mix of genuine emotion and performance.
His response was half-theatrical, a mix of genuine anger and a desire to make a scene. 他的反应半戏剧化，既有真正的愤怒，也有想制造一场戏的愿望。

The singer's farewell tour felt half-theatrical, as if he might change his mind.  这位歌手的告别巡演感觉有点做作，好像他可能会改变主意。

近义词:histrionic, melodramatic, affected, stagy
反义词:genuine, sincere, unaffected, natural
词性:adjective
