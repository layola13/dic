feign:/feɪn/| v. |假装，装作|To pretend to feel or be affected by (something).|She feigned sleep when her mother checked on her. 她妈妈来看她的时候，她假装睡着了。

He feigned ignorance, even though he knew exactly what had happened. 即使他知道发生了什么，他还是假装不知情。

|近义词：pretend, simulate, sham, affect|反义词：be genuine, be sincere|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *feindre*, from Latin *fingere* "to shape, form, invent, devise, feign," related to *figura* "a shape, form, figure."
