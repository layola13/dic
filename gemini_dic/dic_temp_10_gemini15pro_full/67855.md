fabber:/ˈfæbər/| v.|制造，装配，（尤指）使用3D打印机制作|To make or assemble something, especially using a 3D printer.|They fabbed a new phone case using the 3D printer.  他们用3D打印机制作了一个新的手机壳。|Near synonyms:  make, manufacture, produce, build, construct|Antonyms: dismantle, deconstruct, destroy|Verb
