discussers:/dɪˈskʌsərz/
n.
讨论者；商讨者；议论者
People who discuss something.  Those who engage in a discussion or debate.
The discussers reached a consensus after hours of debate. 讨论者经过数小时的辩论后达成了一致。
The online forum attracted many discussers from around the world.  这个在线论坛吸引了来自世界各地的许多讨论者。
Synonyms: debaters, conferees, participants, interlocutors
Antonyms: listeners, audience, observers
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "discuss" + "-er" (agent suffix).  "Discuss" comes from the Latin "discutere," meaning "to shake apart," from "dis-" (apart) + "quatere" (to shake).
