monomaniac: /ˌmɒnəˈmeɪniæk/ (UK) /ˌmɑːnəˈmeɪniæk/ (US)| n. adj.|偏执狂，独断专行者|A person who is obsessively fixated on a single idea or subject; exhibiting excessive zeal for one thing.| He was a monomaniac about cleanliness, constantly washing his hands. 他有洁癖，不停地洗手。|近义词: fanatic, zealot, obsessive|反义词: moderate, balanced|词性: 名词，形容词

monomaniacal: /ˌmɒnəməˈnaɪəkl/ (UK) /ˌmɑːnəməˈnaɪəkl/ (US)| adj.|偏执的，独断专行的|Excessively preoccupied with one idea or subject.| His monomaniacal pursuit of wealth led him to neglect his family. 他对财富的偏执追求导致他忽略了家人。|近义词: obsessive, fanatical, single-minded|反义词: balanced, moderate, reasonable|词性: 形容词

词源: From Greek *monos* "single, alone" + *mania* "madness, frenzy."
