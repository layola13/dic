lithoglypher: /lɪˈθɒɡlɪfər/ /lɪˈθɑːɡlɪfər/
noun
宝石雕刻师，宝石雕刻工
A person who engraves on gemstones.
The lithoglypher skillfully carved the image of a lion onto the ruby. 宝石雕刻师巧妙地在红宝石上雕刻了一头狮子的图像。

The museum exhibited several ancient cameos created by a master lithoglypher. 博物馆展出了几位大师级宝石雕刻师创作的几件古代浮雕。

Synonym: gem engraver, lapidary
Antonym: N/A (lack of a direct opposite)
词性: noun
