allyship:/ˈælaɪʃɪp/
n.
团结, 联盟, 盟友关系
The active support of a cause or group, especially by those who are not directly affected by the cause or are not members of the group.
Many companies are now vocal in their allyship with the LGBTQ+ community. 许多公司现在公开表示支持 LGBTQ+ 社区。
She expressed her allyship with the striking workers. 她表达了她对罢工工人的支持。
support, solidarity, advocacy
opposition, antagonism
noun
