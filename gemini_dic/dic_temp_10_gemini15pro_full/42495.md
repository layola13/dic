counterincentive:/ˌkaʊntərɪnˈsɛntɪv/
noun
反激励；抑制因素
Something that discourages a particular action or behaviour.
High taxes on cigarettes are a counterincentive to smoking.  香烟的高税收是对吸烟的一种反激励。
The threat of jail time is a counterincentive to committing crimes. 牢狱之灾的威胁是对犯罪的一种抑制因素。
Synonyms: deterrent, disincentive, obstacle, impediment
Antonyms: incentive, encouragement, motivation
n.
