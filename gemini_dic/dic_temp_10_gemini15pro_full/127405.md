nearliest:/ˈnɪrɪst/| adj.|最近的|The nearest exit is on your right. 最近的出口在你的右边。|例句：The nearest star to Earth is the Sun. 离地球最近的恒星是太阳。|近义词：closest, proximate|反义词：farthest, furthest|词性:形容词

词源:  From Middle English *nerrest, *nerest, from Old English nēahst, nīehst (“nearest, next; last, final”), superlative of nēah (“near”). Cognate with West Frisian neist (“nearest, next”), Dutch naast (“next to, beside”), German nächst (“next”), Old Norse næstr (“next”).  Related to nigh.  The -est suffix is a superlative marker.
