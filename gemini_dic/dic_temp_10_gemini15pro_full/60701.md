electrizer:/ɪˈlɛktrɪˌzaɪzər/ | n. | 电疗器，带电体 |A device used to administer electric shocks, especially for medical purposes.  Something that electrifies or charges with electricity. | This old electrizer was used to treat rheumatism.  这个老式的电疗器过去是用来治疗风湿病的。|  The electrizer charged the capacitor quickly.  这个带电体很快给电容器充了电。 | synonyms: galvanizer, stimulator | antonyms: insulator, dielectric | noun

electrify:/ɪˈlɛktrɪfaɪ/ | v. | 通电；使兴奋；使震惊 |To charge with electricity; to thrill or excite; to shock or amaze. | The news electrified the nation. 这消息震惊了全国。| The performance electrified the audience. 表演使观众激动不已。| synonyms: excite, thrill, stimulate, energize, shock, astound | antonyms: bore, calm, soothe, depress | verb
