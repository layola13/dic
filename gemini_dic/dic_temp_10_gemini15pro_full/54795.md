dispensatorily:/dɪspɛnˈsəˈtɔrɪli/
adverb
分配地；免除地；特赦地
In a dispensatory manner; by dispensation.  Meaning doing something by exemption from a rule or requirement, or by distributing something.
The pope dispensatorily granted him a divorce. 教皇特赦了他的离婚。
The funds were dispensatorily allocated to the various departments. 资金被分配给了各个部门。
近义词: exceptionally, specially
反义词: obligatorily, mandatorily
词性: adverb

Etymology:
From Late Latin *dispensatorius, from Latin dispensator (“steward, manager”), from dispensare (“to weigh out, distribute, manage”), from dis- (“apart, away”) + pensare (“to weigh, pay”).
