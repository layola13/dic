jerky:/ˈdʒɜːrki/ (US), /ˈdʒɜːki/ (UK)| adj. n.|抽搐的，牛肉干|Sudden and uneven in movement; dried strips of meat, usually beef.|

例句：
His movements were jerky and uncoordinated.
他的动作抽搐且不协调。

例句：
She loves to snack on beef jerky.
她喜欢吃牛肉干作为零食。
