handpicked:/ˈhændˌpɪkt/| adj.|精心挑选的，手工采摘的|Carefully selected by hand; chosen with great care.|例句：These are handpicked strawberries, the best of the crop. 这些是精心挑选的草莓，是这一季最好的。|例句：The team was handpicked by the coach.  这个团队是由教练亲自挑选的。|近义词：selected, chosen, prime, choice|反义词：random, unselected|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From hand + picked, the past participle of pick.  "Pick" comes from Old English *pician "to prick, pierce," of uncertain origin.
