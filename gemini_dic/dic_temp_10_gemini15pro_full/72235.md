florid:/ˈflɔːrɪd/ (US) /ˈflɒrɪd/ (UK)| adj.|华丽的，绚丽的；（脸色）红润的；过分装饰的|Having a red or flushed complexion; elaborately or excessively decorated or embellished. Also, having a healthy, rosy complexion, often suggesting excessive vitality.  Derived from Latin "floridus," meaning "flowering, blooming."|例句：
He gave a florid speech, full of elaborate metaphors.  他发表了一篇华丽的演讲，充满了精美的比喻。

She has a florid complexion, a sign of her robust health. 她脸色红润，表明她身体健康。

|近义词：ornate, flamboyant, flowery, ruddy, flushed|反义词：plain, simple, pale, austere|词性: adjective
