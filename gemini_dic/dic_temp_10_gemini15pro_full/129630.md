non-contradiction:/ˌnɒn kɒntrəˈdɪkʃən/ |n.| 无矛盾；不矛盾；一致性 |The principle of non-contradiction states that a statement cannot be both true and false at the same time and in the same respect. 无矛盾律指出，一个陈述在同一时间、同一方面不可能既真又假。|The lawyer pointed out the non-contradiction in the witness's testimony. 律师指出了证人证词中的自相矛盾之处。| 近义词：consistency, agreement, harmony| 反义词：contradiction, inconsistency, conflict | 词性: 名词
