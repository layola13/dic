examiner:/ɪɡˈzæmɪnər/ (UK) /ɪɡˈzæmɪnər/ (US)| n.|审查员；主考人；检查员|A person who tests someone's knowledge or ability by asking them questions or by giving them tasks to perform.|The examiner asked a series of difficult questions. 主考人问了一系列难题。|近义词：inspector, assessor, tester|反义词: examinee, candidate|noun
