indisputed:/ˌɪndɪˈspjuːtɪd/
adjective
无可争议的；无异议的
not disputed or called in question; accepted.
The evidence presents an undisputed fact.  证据呈现了一个无可争议的事实。
Her talent is undisputed. 她的才华是无可争议的。
Synonyms: unquestionable, undeniable, unchallenged, irrefutable, incontestable, acknowledged, accepted
Antonyms: disputed, questionable, debatable, controversial
词性: adjective
