intercirculate:/ˌɪntərˈsɜːrkjəleɪt/
verb
相互循环，互相流通
To circulate or cause to circulate among each other or one another.
The blood intercirculates between the heart and the lungs. 血液在心脏和肺之间循环。
The rumors intercirculated quickly through the small town. 谣言很快就在小镇上传开了。
近义词: circulate, distribute, disseminate
反义词: stagnate, localize
词性: verb
