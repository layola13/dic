exinanition:/ɪɡˌzɪnˈeɪʃən/| n. |精疲力竭，极度衰弱|The state of being emptied or exhausted; extreme weakness or fatigue.  The marathon runner collapsed in a state of utter exinanition.|例句：The climbers reached the summit in a state of complete exinanition. 登山者们到达山顶时已精疲力竭。

例句：He was in a state of exinanition after working for three days straight.  他连续工作了三天，已经精疲力竭了。|近义词：exhaustion, fatigue, prostration, debilitation, enervation|反义词: vigor, energy, strength|词性:noun
