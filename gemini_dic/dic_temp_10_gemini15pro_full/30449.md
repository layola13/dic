centred:/ˈsentəd/ (British English), /ˈsɛntərd/ (American English)| adj.|中心的，居中的，集中于…的|Positioned in or around the centre; having its centre at a specified point; concentrated on or around a particular person or thing.|The text is centred on the page.  文本在页面上居中。|The discussion centred around the new proposal.  讨论集中在新提案上。|近义词：central, middle, focused, concentrated|反义词：peripheral, off-centre, dispersed|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "centre" + "-ed" (past participle suffix).  The verb "centre" comes from the noun "centre," which ultimately derives from the Latin "centrum" meaning "center point."
