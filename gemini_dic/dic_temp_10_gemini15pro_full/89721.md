hopeite:/ˈhoʊpaɪt/| n.|磷锌矿|A hydrous zinc phosphate mineral, Zn₃(PO₄)₂·4H₂O, occurring as colorless-to-white orthorhombic crystals.|Hopeite is a relatively rare mineral found in oxidized zinc deposits. 磷锌矿是一种相对稀有的矿物，发现于氧化锌矿床中。|近义词: parahopeite (similar mineral)|反义词:  None (It's a specific mineral, doesn't have a direct opposite)|词性: noun
