miterers:/ˈmaɪtərərz/ | n. | 斜接匠，斜角切割者 | People who cut (usually wood) at an angle, typically 45 degrees, to form a corner joint. | The miterers carefully joined the pieces of molding for the picture frame. 斜接匠仔细地将画框的造型部件连接起来。|  近义词: joiners, carpenters | 反义词: N/A | noun
