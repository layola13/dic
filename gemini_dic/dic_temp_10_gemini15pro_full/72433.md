fluidity:/fluːˈɪdəti/| n.|流畅性，流动性|The fluidity of the situation made it difficult to make plans.  情况的变化莫测使制定计划变得困难。|例句：The fluidity of his movements was mesmerizing. 他的动作流畅得令人着迷。|近义词：smoothness, flexibility, adaptability|反义词: rigidity, stiffness, inflexibility|词性:名词
Etymology:  Late Middle English: from late Latin fluiditas, from fluidus (see fluid).
