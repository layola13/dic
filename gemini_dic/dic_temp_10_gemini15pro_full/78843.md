ginata:/ɡɪˈnɑːtɑː/ | n. |椰奶，椰浆|Coconut milk, extracted from grated mature coconuts. It is a thick, creamy liquid used in Southeast Asian and Pacific Island cuisines.|Example 1:  The chicken adobo was cooked in ginata, giving it a rich, creamy flavor. 这道鸡肉阿斗波是用椰奶烹制的，味道浓郁香醇。

Example 2:  She added ginata to the rice pudding for a tropical twist.  她在米布丁里加了椰奶，增添了一丝热带风味。| 近义词: coconut milk | 反义词: (No direct antonym) |词性: noun
