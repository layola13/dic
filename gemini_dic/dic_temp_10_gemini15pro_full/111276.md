litten:/ˈlɪtn/
adjective
照亮的；被闪电击中的
lighted by lightning; struck by lightning
The tree was litten by a flash of lightning.  这棵树被闪电照亮了。
The barn was litten and caught fire. 谷仓被闪电击中并着火了。
近义词:struck, illuminated
反义词:dark, unlit
词性:adjective


词源:
From Middle English *liten, past participle of liten (“to light”), from Old English līhtan (“to light, shine, kindle, set fire to”), from Proto-Germanic *līhtjaną (“to light”), from Proto-Indo-European *lewk- (“light, brightness”). Cognate with Scots liten (“lighted”), West Frisian ljochtsje (“to light”), Dutch lichten (“to light”), German leuchten (“to shine, gleam”), Danish lyse (“to shine, light”). More at light.
