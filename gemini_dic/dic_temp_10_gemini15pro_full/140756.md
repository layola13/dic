omnivalent:/ˌɒmnɪˈveɪlənt/ | adj. | 全价的；万能的；有效对抗所有株系的 | Having unlimited or universal power, validity, or force; effective against all strains (as of a virus or bacterium). |  This new omnivalent vaccine protects against all known strains of the flu.  这种新的全价疫苗可以预防所有已知的流感病毒株。 | The omnivalent serum was developed to combat the rapidly mutating virus.  这种万能血清是为了对抗快速变异的病毒而研发的。 | Synonyms: universal, all-embracing, all-inclusive, panvalent | Antonyms: limited, specific, restricted | adjective
