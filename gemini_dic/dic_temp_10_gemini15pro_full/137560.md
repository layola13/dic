nontransitiveness:/ˌnɑːntrænˈzɪtɪvnəs/ | n. | 非及物性 | The quality or state of being nontransitive.  A nontransitive relation is one where if a is related to b, and b is related to c, it does not necessarily follow that a is related to c. |  The nontransitiveness of "is a friend of" is evident: If Alice is a friend of Bob, and Bob is a friend of Carol, it doesn't mean Alice is a friend of Carol. |  intransitivity | transitivity | noun
