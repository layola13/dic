hartfordite:/ˈhɑːrtfərdˌaɪt/| n. |哈特福石|A rare hydrated arsenate mineral, typically occurring as small, colorless crystals.  Chemically, it is a hydrated lead-zinc arsenate. |Hartfordite crystals were found in the mine. 在矿井中发现了哈特福石晶体。|近义词:mimetite, adamite (mineralogically similar)|反义词: N/A (Minerals don't typically have antonyms)|词性:noun
