magazining:/ˈmæɡəˌziːnɪŋ/
n.|杂志化；杂志编辑工作|The act of creating or working on magazines.  The process of making something like a magazine, or the business of magazine publishing.|  The rise of digital magazining has changed the publishing industry. 数字杂志化的兴起改变了出版业。|journalism, publishing|blogging|noun
magazining:/ˈmæɡəˌziːnɪŋ/
v.|将…编成杂志；像杂志一样编辑|To compile or edit something in the style of a magazine. To present something in a magazine format.|They are magazining their travel photos and stories. 他们正在把他们的旅行照片和故事编成杂志。|publishing, compiling|discarding|verb
