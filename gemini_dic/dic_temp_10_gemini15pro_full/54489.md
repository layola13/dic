disinter:/ˌdɪsɪnˈtɜːr/
disinter:v.
disinter:掘出；挖出
disinter:To dig up (something buried, especially a corpse) from the earth.
disinter:The archaeologists disinterred a Viking longship. 考古学家挖出了一艘维京长船。
disinter:exhume, unearth
disinter:bury, inter
disinter:verb
