galactica:/ɡəˈlæktɪkə/ | adj. | 银河的，星系的 | Relating to a galaxy or galaxies; incredibly vast. |  例句：The galactica empire spanned across numerous star systems.  银河帝国横跨了无数的星系。

例句：Scientists are constantly searching for galactica civilizations. 科学家们一直在寻找星系文明。| 近义词：cosmic, interstellar, astronomical | 反义词：terrestrial, earthly | 词性: adjective

Etymology: From Greek "galaktikos" (γαλακτικός), meaning "milky," referring to the Milky Way.  Ultimately from "gala" (γάλα), meaning "milk."
