garter:/ˈɡɑːrtər/ (US), /ˈɡɑːtə/ (UK)| n.|吊袜带；袜带|A band worn around the leg, typically above the knee, to hold up a stocking or sock.|She adjusted her garter before going on stage. 她上台前整理了一下吊袜带。|近义词：sock suspender|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "bare legs" as a contrasting concept)|noun

garter:/ˈɡɑːrtər/ (US), /ˈɡɑːtə/ (UK)| vt.|用袜带系住|To fasten or secure with a garter.|He gartered his socks before putting on his boots. 他穿靴子前用袜带系住了袜子。|近义词：fasten, secure|反义词: unfasten, loosen|verb
