antitropous:/ænˈtɪtrəpəs/| adj.|倒生的，反向的| (of an ovule) inverted; completely turned back on itself so that the micropyle lies close to the funicle.  (胚珠)倒生的；完全反转，以使珠孔靠近珠柄。| The antitropous ovule is common in flowering plants.  倒生胚珠在开花植物中很常见。|  synonym: inverted, resupinate | antonym: orthotropous, atropous | adjective
