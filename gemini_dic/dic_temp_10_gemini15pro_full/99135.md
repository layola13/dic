interception:/ˌɪntərˈsepʃn/ | n. |拦截，截取，截获|The interception of the enemy's communication was crucial to the victory.  截获敌人的通讯对胜利至关重要。| The safety made an interception in the end zone.  安全卫在端区完成了一次拦截。|近义词：interruption, blockage, seizure|反义词: continuation, allowance, progression|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin interceptionem, accusative of interceptio (“a seizing between, interception”), from intercipere (“to intercept”), from inter- (“between”) + capere (“to take”).
