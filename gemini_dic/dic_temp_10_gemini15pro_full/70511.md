filemot:/ˈfiːləmɒt/ /ˈfaɪləmɑːt/ | adj. |浅黄色的；枯叶色的；黄褐色的 |Of a dull yellowish brown or light olive-brown color, like a faded leaf. | The walls were painted a filemot color, reminiscent of autumn leaves. 墙壁被漆成了枯叶色，让人想起秋天的落叶。| The old book's cover was a faded, filemot hue. 这本旧书的封面是褪色的黄褐色。 | 近义词: buff, beige, tawny, drab, yellowish-brown | 反义词: vibrant, bright, colorful | 词性:形容词

词源:
From Old French *filemot, *felemot "dead leaf," from filer "to spin" (see file (n.1)) + mot "dead," from Latin mortuus (see mortal (adj.)). Originally "color of a dead leaf."
