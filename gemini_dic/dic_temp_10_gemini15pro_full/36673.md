colliding:/kəˈlaɪdɪŋ/ | v. | 碰撞，冲突 | To strike or dash against each other; to be in opposition or disagreement. | Their views on the matter are colliding. 他们在这个问题上的观点相冲突。| synonyms: crashing, clashing, impacting, conflicting | antonyms: separating, diverging, agreeing, harmonizing | word type: verb
