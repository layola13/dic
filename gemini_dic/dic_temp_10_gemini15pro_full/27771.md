cannibalic:/ˌkænɪˈbælɪk/| adj.|食人的；同类相食的；残忍的|Relating to or characterized by cannibalism; involving the eating of one's own kind.|The tribe was known for its cannibalic rituals.  这个部落以其食人仪式而闻名。|The company's cannibalic marketing strategy was hurting its own product lines. 该公司自相残杀的营销策略正在损害其自身的产品线。|Synonyms: anthropophagous, man-eating|Antonyms: compassionate, humane|Adjective

cannibalistically:/ˌkænɪbəˈlɪstɪkli/| adv.|食人地；同类相食地|In a cannibalic manner.|The tribe cannibalistically consumed their enemies.  该部落以食人的方式吃掉了他们的敌人。|They cannibalistically devoured their competitors' market share. 他们以自相残杀的方式吞噬了竞争对手的市场份额。|Synonyms: anthropophagously|Antonyms: humanely|Adverb
