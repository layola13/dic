miscensuring:/ˌmɪsˈsɛnʃərɪŋ/ | adj. | 错误地责备的，不公正地批评的 | Expressing or characterized by wrongful or unjust censure; blaming or criticizing unfairly. |  He felt miscensured for a mistake he didn't make. 他为自己没有犯的错误感到被不公正地责备了。 |  She offered a miscensuring glance at the messy room. 她对凌乱的房间投以责备的目光。 | 近义词: wrongly criticizing, unjustly blaming, unfairly condemning | 反义词: praising, commending, approving | 词性: adjective

词源: "mis-" (prefix meaning wrong or bad) + "censure" (from Old French censurer, from Latin censēre "to assess, judge")
