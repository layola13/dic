decategorize:/diːˈkætəɡəˌraɪz/ | v. | 去分类，取消分类 | To remove from a category or classification; to make something no longer fit into a particular category. |  The library decided to decategorize its fiction section, allowing books to be shelved alphabetically regardless of genre. 图书馆决定取消其小说部分的分类，允许书籍按字母顺序排列，而不管其类型如何。

The museum decategorized its collection of artifacts, opting for a more thematic display.  博物馆取消了其人工制品的分类，选择了一种更有主题性的展示方式。| synonyms: reclassify, regroup, reorganize, desegregate | antonyms: categorize, classify, label, pigeonhole | verb
