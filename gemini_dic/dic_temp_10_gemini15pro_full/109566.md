let-it-all-hang-out:/ˌlɛt ɪt ɔːl hæŋ ˈaʊt/| idiom | 放松，尽情享受，不拘礼节 | To relax and enjoy oneself without worrying about behaving in a socially acceptable way; to be uninhibited and spontaneous. |例句：At the party, everyone was encouraged to let it all hang out and have a good time.  在派对上，每个人都被鼓励尽情放松，玩得开心。

例句：After a long week at work, I just want to go home and let it all hang out.  在漫长的一周工作之后，我只想回家放松一下。|近义词：unwind, loosen up, let loose, be oneself |反义词：be formal, be reserved, hold back| idiom
