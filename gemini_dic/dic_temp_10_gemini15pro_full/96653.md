industrialisms: /ɪnˈdʌstriəlɪzəmz/
n.
工业主义，工业化（现象）
The social and economic systems characteristic of industrialized societies, often considered in negative contexts due to their potential negative impacts on society and the environment.
The rapid growth of industrialisms in the 19th century led to significant social and environmental changes.
19世纪工业主义的迅速发展导致了重大的社会和环境变化。

The book critiques the negative effects of industrialisms on traditional communities.
这本书批判了工业主义对传统社区的负面影响。

Synonym: industrialization, industrial society
Antonym: agrarianism, pre-industrial society
