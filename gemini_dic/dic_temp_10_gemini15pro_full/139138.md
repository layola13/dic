obesely:/oʊˈbisli/ | adv. | 肥胖地，过度肥胖地 | in a way that shows someone is very fat |  He was obesely overweight and struggled to walk. 他过度肥胖，走路都很困难。|  She sat obesely in the chair, barely able to move. 她肥胖地坐在椅子上，几乎动弹不得。 | 近义词: extremely fat, morbidly obese | 反义词: thinly, slimly | 词性: 副词

词源: From the adjective "obese" + "-ly" (adverbial suffix).  "Obese" comes from Latin "obesus" (fat, stout), past participle of "obesere" (to eat away, devour, grow fat), from "ob-" (towards, against) + "edere" (to eat).
