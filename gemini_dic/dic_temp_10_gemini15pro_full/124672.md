moved-in: /ˌmuːvd ˈɪn/
adjective|搬进的；入住的|Having settled into a new residence.|例句：They are moved-in and unpacked. 他们已经搬进去了，并且已经打开行李了。

例句：The apartment felt moved-in and cozy. 这间公寓感觉已经住过一段时间了，很舒适。
|近义词：settled, installed|反义词: moved-out|词性:形容词


moved-in: /ˌmuːvd ˈɪn/
verb|搬进；迁入|The act of establishing residence in a new place.|例句：We moved-in last week. 我们上周搬进来了。

例句：They moved-in with their parents. 他们搬去和父母一起住了。
|近义词：relocate, settle in|反义词: move out|词性:动词
