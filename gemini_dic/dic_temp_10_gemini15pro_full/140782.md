omphalomesaraic:/ˌɒmfəloʊˌmɛsəˈreɪɪk/ /ˌɑːmfəloʊˌmɛsəˈreɪɪk/
adjective
卵黄肠系的；脐肠系的
Relating to the omphalomesenteric duct, a duct connecting the yolk sac to the midgut embryo in early development.  This duct normally disappears during fetal development.
The omphalomesaraic duct normally closes off by the seventh week of gestation. 卵黄肠管通常在妊娠第七周闭合。
Persistence of the omphalomesenteric duct can lead to various anomalies. 卵黄肠管的持续存在会导致各种异常。
近义词:vitelline,  yolk stalk
反义词: None
词性: adjective


omphalomesaraic:/ˌɒmfəloʊˌmɛsəˈreɪɪk/ /ˌɑːmfəloʊˌmɛsəˈreɪɪk/
noun
卵黄肠系
The anatomical structures derived from the omphalomesenteric duct.
The surgeon identified an omphalomesaraic cyst during the laparotomy.  外科医生在剖腹手术中发现了一个卵黄肠系囊肿。
Omphalomesaraic remnants can sometimes be found in the adult abdomen. 卵黄肠系残余物有时可以在成人腹部找到。
近义词:vitelline duct remnants
反义词: None
词性: noun
