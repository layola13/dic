cadaster:/kəˈdæstər/ /ˈkædæstər/
cadaster|n.|地籍；地籍册；土地登记簿|A comprehensive register of the real property of a country, often including details such as ownership, location, and value.  It's a public record that provides information about land parcels within a specific jurisdiction.|The cadaster is essential for property taxation and urban planning.|近义词:register, survey, record|反义词: None|词性: Noun

cadastral:/kəˈdæstrəl/ /ˈkædæstrəl/
cadastral|adj.|地籍的|Relating to a cadaster or the system of land registration.|Cadastral maps are used to identify and define property boundaries.|近义词: surveying, mapping|反义词: None|词性: Adjective
