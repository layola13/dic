gulden:/ˈɡʊldən/ | n. | 荷兰盾; 古尔登 | A former monetary unit of the Netherlands, equal to 100 cents. Also, a former gold coin of the Netherlands and several other European countries. | Example 1:  He paid 100 guilders for the antique clock.  他花了100荷兰盾买了那座古董钟。

Example 2: The old painting was valued at several thousand guilders. 这幅古老的画作价值几千荷兰盾。 | Near synonyms: florin, guilder | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a unit of currency) | noun
