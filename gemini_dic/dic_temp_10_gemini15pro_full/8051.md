anthropolatry:/ˌænθrəˈpɒlətri/ | n. | 人类崇拜；人神崇拜 | The worship of a human being as a god. |  Early civilizations sometimes practiced anthropolatry, elevating their rulers to divine status.  早期文明有时会实行人类崇拜，将他们的统治者提升到神的地位。 | 近义词: deification, apotheosis | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:
From Ancient Greek ἄνθρωπος (ánthrōpos, “human being”) + -λατρεία (-latreía, “worship”).
