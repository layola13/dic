frenchness:/ˈfrɛntʃnɪs/
noun
法国气质；法国特色
The quality of being French or having characteristics considered typical of France.
Her paintings capture the frenchness of the countryside. 她的画作捕捉到了法国乡村的特色。
Synonyms: Gallicism, French character, French spirit
Antonyms: un-Frenchness (not a commonly used word)
词性: 名词
