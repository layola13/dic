herschel:/ˈhɜːrʃəl/ | n. |赫歇尔|  Primarily a surname, referring to the prominent German-born British astronomer Sir William Herschel (1738-1822) and his sister Caroline Herschel (1750-1848), both of whom made significant contributions to astronomy. It can also refer to the Herschel Space Observatory, a space telescope named after William Herschel. |  William Herschel discovered Uranus. 威廉·赫歇尔发现了天王星。| synonym: astronomer (in some contexts, when referring to William Herschel himself) | antonym: N/A | noun
