nonreligion:/ˌnɒnrɪˈlɪdʒən/ | n. |非宗教; 世俗主义| The growth of nonreligion is a global phenomenon.  非宗教的增长是一个全球现象。|  Nonreligion encompasses a wide range of beliefs and practices. 非宗教包含了广泛的信仰和实践。 | 近义词:secularism, atheism, agnosticism | 反义词: religion, faith | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From non- + religion. "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not" or "opposite of." "Religion" comes from the Latin "religio," meaning "respect for what is sacred, reverence for the gods, obligation, the bond between man and gods."
