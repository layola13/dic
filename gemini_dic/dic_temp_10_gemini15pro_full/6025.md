amelioration:/əˌmiːliəˈreɪʃn/ |n.|改善，改进，改良|The amelioration of working conditions has improved morale. 工作条件的改善提高了士气。|例句：The government implemented policies for the amelioration of poverty. 政府实施了改善贫困的政策。|近义词：improvement, betterment, enhancement|反义词：deterioration, worsening, decline|词性:名词
Etymology: From Late Latin *amelioratio*, from Latin ameliorare "to make better," from ad- "to" + melior "better."
