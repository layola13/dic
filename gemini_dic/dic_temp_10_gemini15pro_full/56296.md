doggo:/ˈdɒɡəʊ/ (UK), /ˈdɑːɡoʊ/ (US)| n. |狗狗|A slang term for a dog, often used in a humorous or affectionate way, implying a sense of cuteness or goofiness.  It is often used online and in social media.| Look at that cute doggo playing in the park! 看看那只在公园里玩耍的可爱狗狗！|近义词：dog, puppy, pooch|反义词:cat (not a direct opposite, but a contrasting animal)|noun
