nonimplication:/ˌnɒnɪmplɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 非蕴涵，不蕴含 |The statement "A does not imply B" expresses nonimplication. | This logic textbook explains nonimplication clearly. 这本逻辑教科书清晰地解释了非蕴涵。|  Nonimplication asserts that one statement doesn't necessitate another.  非蕴涵断言一个陈述并不必然导致另一个陈述。| synonyms: non-entailment, lack of implication | antonyms: implication, entailment | noun
