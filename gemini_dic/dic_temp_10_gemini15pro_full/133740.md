nonfundamentally:/ˌnɒnˌfʌndəˈmentəli/ | adv. | 非根本地；非本质地 | Not in a fundamental or essential way; superficially. | Example: The issue was nonfundamentally addressed, leading to further problems down the line.  这个问题没有得到根本上的解决，导致了后续的更多问题。| Example:  They differed nonfundamentally on the approach to the problem. 他们在解决问题的方法上并没有本质上的分歧。 | Synonym: superficially, peripherally | Antonym: fundamentally, essentially | 词性: 副词
