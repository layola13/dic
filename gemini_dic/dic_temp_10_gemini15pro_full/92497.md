hypothermia:/ˌhaɪpəˈθɜːrmiə/ (US) /ˌhaɪpəˈθɜːmiə/ (UK)| n. |体温过低；低体温症|A medical condition in which the body loses heat faster than it can produce it, resulting in a dangerously low body temperature.|The hiker suffered from hypothermia after being lost in the snow.  这名徒步旅行者在雪中迷路后患上了低体温症。|近义词:coldness, chill|反义词:hyperthermia, fever|名词
