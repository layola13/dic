milker:/ˈmɪlkər/| n.|挤奶器；挤奶工人|A person or machine that milks cows or other animals.  A device used for milking.|例句：The farmer used a mechanical milker.  农民使用了一个机械挤奶器。|例句：He's a skilled milker and can milk a cow in under five minutes. 他是一个熟练的挤奶工，可以在五分钟内挤完一头奶牛。|近义词：milkmaid (female), dairy farmer|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

milker:/ˈmɪlkər/| n.|产奶动物（尤指奶牛）|A cow or other animal kept for milk production.|例句：She has a herd of fifty milkers. 她有一群五十头奶牛。|例句：The prize milker produced over 10,000 gallons of milk this year. 这头获奖的奶牛今年产奶超过10,000加仑。|近义词：dairy cow|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *milkoure*, equivalent to milk + -er.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *melkaną* (to milk).
