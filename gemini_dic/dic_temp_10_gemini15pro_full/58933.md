duskish:/ˈdʌskɪʃ/| adj.|微暗的，昏暗的|Somewhat dark; shadowy.|例句：The duskish light of the late afternoon made it difficult to see.  傍晚昏暗的光线使人难以看清。|近义词：dim, shadowy, gloomy, twilight|反义词:bright, light, luminous|词性:形容词
Etymology: From dusk + -ish.  Dusk comes from the Proto-Germanic *duskaz.
