acidimeter: /ˌæsɪˈdɪmɪtər/
noun
酸度计
An instrument for measuring the strength of acids.
The chemist used an acidimeter to determine the concentration of the acid.  化学家使用酸度计来测定酸的浓度。
The acidimeter showed that the solution was highly acidic. 酸度计显示该溶液酸性很强。
synonyms: acidometer
antonyms: alkalimeter
词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French acidimètre, from acide (“acid”) + -mètre (“-meter”).

USAGE->
The acidimeter was used to test the pH of the soil. = 酸度计被用来测试土壤的pH值。

The lab technician calibrated the acidimeter before each use. = 实验室技术员在每次使用前校准酸度计。

PHRASE->
use an acidimeter = 使用酸度计

a digital acidimeter = 数字酸度计
