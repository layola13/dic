headline:/ˈhedlaɪn/ | n. | 标题；头条新闻 | A heading at the top of an article or page, especially in a newspaper or magazine. | Example: The headline read "Local Man Wins Lottery." 本地男子赢得彩票。

Example:  The headline grabbed my attention.  这条标题吸引了我的注意。


|近义词：title, caption, heading |反义词:  (Headline doesn't have a direct antonym. Consider "small print" or "details" for contrasting concepts.) | 词性: noun
