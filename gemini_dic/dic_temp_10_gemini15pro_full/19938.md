biodegradability:/ˌbaɪoʊˌdiːˌɡreɪdəˈbɪləti/
n.|生物降解性|The capability of a substance to be broken down into simpler substances by the action of microorganisms, such as bacteria or fungi.|This bag is made of a material with high biodegradability.  这个袋子是由具有高生物降解性的材料制成的。|Synonym: decomposability,  disintegrability|Antonym: non-biodegradability,  persistence|noun

biodegradable:/ˌbaɪoʊdɪˈɡreɪdəbl/
adj.|可生物降解的|Capable of being decomposed by bacteria or other living organisms.|Biodegradable packaging helps reduce environmental pollution. 可生物降解的包装有助于减少环境污染。|Synonym: compostable, decomposable|Antonym: non-biodegradable|adjective
