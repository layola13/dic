palolo:/pəˈloʊloʊ/
n.
帕罗洛虫，海生蠕虫
A marine annelid worm (Eunice viridis) found in the Pacific Ocean, whose posterior segments, filled with reproductive products, break off and swarm at the surface at certain times of the year, especially during the last quarter of the moon in October and November.
The islanders gathered the palolo worms as a delicacy. 岛民们采集帕罗洛虫作为美味佳肴。
近义词:annelid, worm
反义词:(None -  No direct opposite for a specific species of worm)
词性:noun
