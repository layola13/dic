outdazzling: /ˌaʊtˈdæzəlɪŋ/
verb
使…相形见绌；胜过；光芒盖过
To surpass in dazzling or brilliance; to outshine.
Her performance outdazzled all the others. 她的表演使所有其他人都黯然失色。
近义词: outshining, eclipsing, surpassing
反义词: paling, dimming
词性: 动词
