minicity:/ˈmɪnɪsɪti/ |n.|微型城市，小型城市|A small, self-contained city, often planned and built with specific features, like being eco-friendly or focused on a particular industry.  It can also refer to a scaled-down model of a city. |They are building a minicity designed to be entirely self-sufficient in energy.| 近义词: small city, miniature city, model city |反义词: metropolis, megacity| noun
