battlefront:/ˈbætlfʌnt/ | n. | 战线；斗争前沿 |The main battlefront is in the mountains.  主要战线在山区。|  The political battlefront is heating up. 政治斗争前沿正在升温。| synonyms: frontline, front line, firing line | antonyms: rear, home front | noun

battlefront:/ˈbætlfʌnt/ | n. | 战线；斗争前沿| A place where a struggle or conflict is being actively fought, either literally (as in war) or figuratively (as in politics or social issues). |  The union rep was a veteran of many a shop-floor battlefront.  这位工会代表是很多车间斗争前沿的老将。 | The internet has become a key battlefront in the fight against misinformation.  互联网已成为打击虚假信息斗争的关键前沿。 | synonyms: frontline, front, vanguard, theater (of war/operations) | antonyms: rear, base, headquarters | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From "battle" + "front."  "Battle" comes from Old French "bataille" (battle, combat). "Front" comes from Old French "front" (forehead, face, front), ultimately from Latin "frons" (forehead, brow, front).
