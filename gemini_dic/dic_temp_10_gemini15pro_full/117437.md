mediostapedial:/ˌmiːdioʊsteɪˈpiːdiəl/
adjective
中镫骨的
Relating to the middle ear and the stapes (one of the three small bones in the middle ear).
The mediostapedial muscle helps to regulate sound transmission.  中镫骨肌有助于调节声音的传递。
The surgeon carefully examined the mediostapedial structures.  外科医生仔细检查了中镫骨的结构。
Near Synonyms:  middle ear, stapedial, ossicular
Antonyms: N/A (Anatomical term, lacks a direct opposite)
Word Origin:  medio- (Latin, middle) + staped- (Latin, stirrup, referring to the stapes bone) + -ial (suffix forming adjectives)
