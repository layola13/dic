ETYMOLOGY->
From *accelerate* + *-meter*. *Accelerate* comes from Latin *accelerare*, meaning "to hasten," from *ad-* ("to, toward") + *celer* ("swift"). *-meter* comes from Greek *metron*, meaning "measure."
