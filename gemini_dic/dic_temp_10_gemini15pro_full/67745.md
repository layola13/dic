eyeballing:/ˈaɪˌbɔːlɪŋ/| v. | 目测；粗略估计；盯着看 | To look or stare at something, typically to assess it visually or informally; to estimate something by sight. | I eyeballed the distance between the two buildings. 我目测了两栋楼之间的距离。

The cook eyeballed the ingredients instead of measuring them.  厨师目测了配料而不是测量它们。

| 近义词：estimating, gauging, appraising, staring, gazing | 反义词: measuring precisely, calculating | 词性: 动词
