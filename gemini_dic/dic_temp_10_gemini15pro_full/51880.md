如果您是想了解单词 "what" 的用法，我可以提供以下信息：

what: /wɒt/ (British) /wɑːt/ (American) | pronoun, determiner, adverb | 什么 | used to ask for information about something | 

例句：
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？
What do you want for dinner? = 你晚饭想吃什么？

What color is your car? = 你的车是什么颜色？
What time is the meeting? = 会议是什么时间？

近义词: which, who, how
反义词:  无明确的反义词


词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb
