fertilizing:/ˈfɜːrtəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
施肥，使肥沃
the act of adding fertilizer to soil to make plants grow better
例句：Fertilizing the lawn in the spring helps it grow thick and green.  春天给草坪施肥有助于它长得茂密翠绿。

例句：They are fertilizing the fields with manure. 他们在用肥料给田地施肥。

近义词：enriching, nourishing, manuring, composting
反义词：depleting, impoverishing
词性：v.
