bearish:/ˈbɛərɪʃ/| adj.|看跌的，悲观的|Resembling or likened to a bear, especially in being surly, gruff, or clumsy.  Also, (of stock markets, etc.) tending to fall in value; characterized by or reflecting such a tendency. |The stock market has been bearish for the past few months.  股市在过去几个月里一直看跌。|Synonyms: pessimistic, gloomy, negative, declining, downward |Antonyms: bullish, optimistic, positive, rising, upward|adjective

bearish:/ˈbɛrɪʃ/| adj.|笨拙的，粗鲁的|Resembling a bear in being surly, gruff, or clumsy. |He gave me a bearish glare. 他恶狠狠地瞪了我一眼。|Synonyms: surly, gruff, unsociable, clumsy, awkward |Antonyms: graceful, agile, sociable, friendly|adjective
