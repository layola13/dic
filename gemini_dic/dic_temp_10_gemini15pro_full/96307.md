indigentness:/ˈɪndɪdʒəntnəs/| n. |贫困，穷困|The state of being indigent; poverty.  The indigentness of the family was apparent in their threadbare clothing.|例句：The family's indigentness forced them to rely on charity.  这个家庭的贫困迫使他们依靠慈善。

The report highlighted the growing indigentness in the region. 这份报告强调了该地区日益严重的贫困状况。|近义词：poverty, destitution, penury, impoverishment|反义词：wealth, affluence, prosperity|词性:noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *indigentĭa, from Latin indigēns (“needy”) + -ness.
