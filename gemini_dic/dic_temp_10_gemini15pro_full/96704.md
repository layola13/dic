ineffaceably:/ˌɪnɪˈfeɪsəbli/
adjective
不可磨灭地，永久地
Not able to be erased or forgotten; permanent.
The experience was ineffaceably etched in her memory. 这段经历深深地铭刻在她的记忆中。
Synonyms: indelibly, permanently, enduringly
Antonyms: temporarily, fleetingly, transiently
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From in- (not) + efface (to erase) + -able (capable of being) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  The root "efface" comes from the Old French "effacer," from "ex-" (out) + "face" (face).
