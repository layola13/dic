heavenly:/ˈhɛvənli/| adj.|天堂般的，神圣的，极好的|Of or relating to heaven; divine, celestial; exceptionally good or beautiful.|例句：The music was heavenly. 音乐优美极了。|近义词：divine, celestial, blissful, ethereal, sublime|反义词：hellish, infernal, earthly, mundane|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *hevenly*, from Old English *heofonlic* (“heavenly, celestial”), from *heofon* (“heaven”) + *-līc* (suffix forming adjectives). Cognate with Scots *hevinlie* (“heavenly”), West Frisian *himmelsk* (“heavenly”), Dutch *hemels* (“heavenly”), German *himmlisch* (“heavenly”), Danish *himmelsk* (“heavenly”), Swedish *himmelsk* (“heavenly”).
