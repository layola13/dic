gigantic:/dʒaɪˈɡæntɪk/
adjective
巨大的，庞大的
extremely large; of immense size.
例句：The museum boasts a gigantic collection of dinosaur fossils.  博物馆拥有大量的恐龙化石。

例句：They faced a gigantic task in rebuilding the city after the earthquake.  地震后重建城市，他们面临着艰巨的任务。

近义词：huge, enormous, colossal, immense, massive
反义词：tiny, small, miniature, minuscule
词性：adjective

Etymology:
From Latin *giganticus, from gigas (“giant”), from Ancient Greek γίγας (gígas).
