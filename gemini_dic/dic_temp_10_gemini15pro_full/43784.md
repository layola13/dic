cremationist: /krɪˈmeɪʃənɪst/
n.
火葬承办人；火葬场操作员
A person whose job is to cremate bodies.
The cremationist prepared the body for the cremation ceremony. 火葬承办人为火葬仪式准备遗体。
The family met with the cremationist to discuss the arrangements. 家属与火葬承办人会面讨论安排。
Synonyms:  cremator operator, funeral director (in some contexts)
Antonyms:  embalmer (someone who prepares bodies for burial)
noun
