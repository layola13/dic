conducivenesses:/kənˌduːsɪvnɪsɪz/ | n. | 有利性，促进作用 | The quality or state of being conducive; conduciveness.  A combination of factors created the conducivenesses for economic growth. | 例句：The conducivenesses of the environment to learning are numerous, including quiet spaces and ample resources.  环境对学习的诸多益处，包括安静的空间和充足的资源。

例句：The report examined the conducivenesses of different policies to economic development. 报告考察了不同政策对经济发展的促进作用。| 近义词：advantageousness, favorableness, auspiciousness | 反义词：unfavorableness, disadvantageousness, inauspiciousness | 词性: 名词 (plural noun)
