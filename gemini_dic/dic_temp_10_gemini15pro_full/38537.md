conclavist:/kɒnˈkleɪvɪst/ | n. | 教皇选举秘密会议参与者; 隐居者 | A cardinal or other authorized person who attends a papal conclave.  A person who lives in seclusion. |  Example 1: The conclavists were sworn to secrecy during the papal election.  教皇选举期间，与会人员宣誓保密。

Example 2: He lived as a conclavist, dedicating his life to prayer and study. 他像隐居者一样生活，将一生奉献给祈祷和学习。 | Synonym:  cardinal (in the context of papal elections), recluse, hermit | Antonym: extrovert, socialite | Noun
