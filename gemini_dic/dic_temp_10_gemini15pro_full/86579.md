heliocentrical:/ˌhiːliəˈsɛntrɪkəl/ | adj. |日心的；以太阳为中心的| Relating to the heliocentric theory, which places the sun at the center of the solar system. | Copernicus proposed a heliocentrical model of the universe. 哥白尼提出了一个以太阳为中心的宇宙模型。| The heliocentric theory revolutionized our understanding of the cosmos. 日心说彻底改变了我们对宇宙的理解。| 近义词:Sun-centered | 反义词:Geocentric | 词性:形容词

词源:
From Ancient Greek  ἥλιος (hḗlios, “sun”) +‎ κέντρον (kéntron, “center”)
