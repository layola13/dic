climatology:/ˌklaɪməˈtɑːlədʒi/| n. |气候学|The scientific study of climate, including its variability and change and the factors that affect it.|例句：Climatology helps us understand long-term weather patterns. 气候学帮助我们理解长期的天气模式。

例句：He is studying climatology at university. 他在大学学习气候学。|近义词：meteorology, atmospheric science|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

climatic:/klaɪˈmætɪk/ | adj. |气候的，与气候有关的|Relating to climate.|例句：Climatic changes are affecting the planet. 气候变化正在影响地球。

例句：The climatic conditions in this region are harsh.  这个地区的气候条件很恶劣。|近义词：meteorological, atmospheric|反义词: N/A|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *climatologie*, from Ancient Greek *klima* (“region, zone, slope, inclination”) + *-logie* (“study of”).
