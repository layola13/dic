alfalfa:/ælˈfælfə/
n.
紫苜蓿；苜蓿
A leguminous plant (Medicago sativa) widely grown as forage.
Alfalfa is a nutritious feed for livestock. 紫苜蓿是营养丰富的牲畜饲料。
近义词:lucerne
反义词:None
词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish alfalfa, from Arabic al-fisfisa "the best fodder."
