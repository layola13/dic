aerates:/ˈeəreɪts/
verb
充气，透气
introduces air into (a material).
The machine aerates the lawn by punching small holes in it. = 这台机器通过在草坪上打小孔来给它充气。

The addition of organic matter to the soil helps to aerate it. = 在土壤中添加有机物有助于使其透气。

近义词:oxygenate, ventilate, air
反义词:stifle, suffocate
词性:v.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *aeratus, past participle of *aerare "to put air into," from Latin aer "air"
