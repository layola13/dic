paedobaptist:/ˌpiːdoʊˈbæptɪst/ | n. | 婴儿洗礼论者 | A person who believes in the practice of infant baptism. |  Example: The paedobaptist argued that infant baptism was a tradition dating back to the early church. 婴儿洗礼论者认为婴儿洗礼是可追溯到早期教会的传统。

Example:  Many denominations are paedobaptist, practicing infant baptism as a sign of God's grace. 许多教派都是婴儿洗礼派，实行婴儿洗礼作为上帝恩典的标志。| 近义词:  infant baptizer | 反义词: credobaptist, anabaptist | 词性: noun
