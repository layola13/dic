menopause:/ˈmɛnəˌpɔz/ | n. |更年期 |The time in a woman's life when menstruation ceases permanently, usually between the ages of 45 and 55, marked by various physical and emotional changes due to declining ovarian function. |  She began experiencing symptoms of menopause in her late 40s. 她40多岁后期开始出现更年期症状。|  climacteric, change of life |  menarche (月经初潮) | noun
