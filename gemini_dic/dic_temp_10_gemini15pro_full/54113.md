disemploy:/ˌdɪsɪmˈplɔɪ/ | v. |使失业，解雇|To deprive of employment; to put out of work. |例句：The factory disemployed hundreds of workers due to the economic downturn. 由于经济低迷，这家工厂解雇了数百名工人。

例句：The company was forced to disemploy a significant portion of its workforce. 公司被迫解雇了很大一部分员工。

近义词：dismiss, fire, lay off, make redundant, discharge|反义词：employ, hire, recruit|词性：动词
