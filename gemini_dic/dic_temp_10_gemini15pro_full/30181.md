celesta:/səˈlɛstə/ | n. |钢片琴；天琴 |A keyboard instrument resembling a small upright piano but with hammers that strike steel plates instead of strings, producing a clear, bell-like tone. | The composer used a celesta to create a magical, ethereal sound. 作曲家使用钢片琴营造出一种神奇空灵的声音。

The celesta's delicate sound added a touch of whimsy to the piece. 钢片琴细腻的声音为这首曲子增添了一丝奇思妙想。 | synonyms: glockenspiel, bell-lyra | antonyms: N/A | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
