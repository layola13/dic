mutably:/ˈmjuːtəbli/| adv.|可变地，易变地|In a mutable manner; capable of or susceptible to change or alteration.|The weather is mutably unpredictable. 天气变幻莫测。|近义词：changeably, variably, alterably|反义词: immutably, unchangeably|词性:副词
词源: From Late Latin *mutabilis, from Latin mutare (“to change”) +‎ -abilis.
