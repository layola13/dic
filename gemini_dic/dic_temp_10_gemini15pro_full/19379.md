bibulousness:/bɪˈbjuːləsnəs/
n.
嗜酒;酗酒;
The state of being bibulous; fondness for or excessive indulgence in alcoholic beverages.
His bibulousness eventually cost him his job. 他的酗酒最终让他丢了工作。
近义词: alcoholism, drunkenness, inebriety
反义词: sobriety, abstinence, temperance
词性: 名词

bibulous:/ˈbɪbjʊləs/
adj.
嗜酒的;爱喝酒的;
Given to or marked by the consumption of alcoholic beverages. Fond of drinking. Absorbent; spongy.
He has bibulous habits. 他有酗酒的习惯。
The soil is bibulous and retains water well. 这种土壤吸水性强，保水性好。
近义词: alcoholic, drunken, inebriated, absorbent, spongy
反义词: sober, abstemious, temperate
词性: 形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin bibulus "drinking, fond of drink," from bibere "to drink."
