ETYMOLOGY->
From French *galiote*, Italian *galeotta*, diminutive of *galea* (galley), from Medieval Greek *galea*, from Late Latin *galea*.  Ultimately of uncertain origin, possibly from Greek *galeos* “a kind of shark”.
