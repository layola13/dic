ledger:/ˈlɛdʒər/| n.|分类账，账簿|A book or other collection of financial accounts of a particular type.  A record of business transactions.|The company's ledger showed a significant loss this quarter.  这家公司的账簿显示本季度亏损严重。|近义词：account book, record, register|反义词: None|noun
