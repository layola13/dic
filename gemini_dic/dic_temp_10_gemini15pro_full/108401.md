lawlessness:/ˈlɔːləsnəs/
n.
无法无天；违法乱纪；不法行为
absence of government and of law and order; state of disorder due to a disregard of laws
The city descended into lawlessness after the earthquake. 地震过后，这座城市陷入了无法无天的状态。
近义词：anarchy, chaos, disorder, mayhem
反义词：order, lawfulness, peace
词性: 名词

Etymology:
From law + -less + -ness.  "Lawless" meaning "not controlled by law" dates from the late 14th century. The suffix "-ness" forms nouns meaning "state or quality of being".
