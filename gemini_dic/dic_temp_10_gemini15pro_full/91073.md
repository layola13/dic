hutchinsonian:/ˌhʌtʃɪnˈsoʊniən/
adjective
哈钦森的；哈钦森学派的
Relating to John Hutchinson (1674-1737), an English theological writer and natural philosopher, or his doctrines.
Hutchinsonianism rejected Newtonian physics and advocated a literal interpretation of the Bible.  哈钦森主义拒绝牛顿物理学，提倡对圣经的字面解释。
His hutchinsonian views were controversial among his contemporaries. 他的哈钦森学派的观点在他同时代的人中很有争议。
近义词:  fundamentalist, literalist
反义词:  modernist, liberal
词性: adjective
