intermigrate:/ˌɪntərˈmaɪɡreɪt/
|v.|互相移居，不同群体之间互相迁移|To migrate between different groups or areas within a larger region or country.|例句：The two tribes intermigrated, sharing resources and customs. 这两个部落互相迁移，共享资源和习俗。

例句：Intermigration between rural and urban areas increased during the industrial revolution. 工业革命期间，城乡之间的人口迁移加剧。
|近义词：migrate, relocate, resettle|反义词: stay, remain|词性: verb
Etymology: From Latin "inter-" (between) + "migrare" (to migrate)
