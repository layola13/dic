guardian:/ˈɡɑːrdiən/ (US) /ˈɡɑːdiən/ (UK)| n.|监护人，守护者，保护者|A person who is legally responsible for the care of another person, especially a minor or someone incapable of managing their own affairs.  A defender or protector of something.|The court appointed a guardian for the orphaned child. 法院为这个孤儿指定了一名监护人。| synonym: protector, custodian, keeper, warden | antonym: ward, dependent| noun

guardian:/ˈɡɑːrdiən/ (US) /ˈɡɑːdiən/ (UK)| adj. (less common)|守护的，保护的|Serving as a protector or guardian.|The guardian angel watched over the sleeping child.  守护天使守护着熟睡的孩子。|synonym: protective, safeguarding | antonym:  threatening, endangering| adjective
