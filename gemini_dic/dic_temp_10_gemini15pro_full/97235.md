inflection:/ɪnˈflɛkʃən/ | n. | 变格; 屈折变化; 音调变化; 拐点 |A change in the form of a word (typically the ending) to express a grammatical function or attribute such as tense, mood, person, number, case, and gender.  The modulation of the voice; change in pitch or tone. A bend or curve; the point at which such a change of direction occurs.| The inflection of the verb "to be" changes depending on the subject.  动词“to be”的变位取决于主语。 The speaker's inflection conveyed a sense of sarcasm. 演讲者的语调表达了一种讽刺的意味。 There is an inflection point on the graph where the rate of increase begins to slow. 图上有一个拐点，增速开始放缓。|近义词：modulation, variation, conjugation, declension |反义词: uniformity, consistency |词性: noun
