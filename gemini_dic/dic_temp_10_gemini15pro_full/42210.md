councils:/ˈkaʊnslz/
n.
委员会；理事会；地方议会
A body of people elected or appointed to manage the affairs of a city, town, etc.
The city council voted to approve the new budget.  市议会投票通过了新的预算。
boards, committees, assemblies

noun

councils:/ˈkaʊnslz/
n.
(尤指政治上的) 商议，会议；讨论
An act of deliberation or consultation.
After hours of councils, they finally reached a decision.  经过数小时的商议，他们终于做出了决定。
