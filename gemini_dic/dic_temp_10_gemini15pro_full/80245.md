goey:/ˈɡuːi/| adj.|粘糊糊的，胶状的；感伤的，多愁善感的|Something that is goey is sticky and soft.  It can also refer to something excessively sentimental.|The caramel sauce was deliciously goey.  焦糖酱美味极了，粘稠稠的。

The movie was a bit too goey for my taste. 这部电影对我来说有点太煽情了。|近义词：sticky, gooey, sentimental, mushy|反义词：dry, unsentimental|词性:形容词
