formality:/fɔːrˈmælɪti/ /fɔːrˈmæləti/
n.
礼节；拘谨；正式手续；例行公事
The strict observance of social rules and customs; an established procedure or custom, often unnecessary and tedious.
Please excuse the formality of this letter.  请原谅这封信的正式语气。
近义词：ceremony, ritual, protocol, convention
反义词：informality, casualness
词性: 名词

formality:/fɔːrˈmælɪti/ /fɔːrˈmæləti/
n.
遵守礼节；拘泥形式
The state or quality of being formal.
He greeted them with a degree of formality. 他略带拘谨地向他们打招呼。
近义词：stiffness, rigidity
反义词：naturalness, spontaneity
词性: 名词


词源: Late Middle English: from Old French formalite or late Latin formalitas, from Latin formalis ‘relating to form’ (see formal).
