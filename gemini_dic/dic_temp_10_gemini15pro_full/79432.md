glisteringly:/ˈɡlɪstərɪŋli/| adv.|闪闪发光地，闪烁地|Shining with a soft reflected light; sparkling.|The diamonds glistened glisteringly under the spotlights. 钻石在聚光灯下闪闪发光。|近义词：sparklingly, glitteringly, brightly|反义词:dully, dimly|词性:副词
词源: From 'glistering' + '-ly'.  'Glister' is a variant of 'glitter', which comes from Old Norse *glitra "glitter, sparkle".
