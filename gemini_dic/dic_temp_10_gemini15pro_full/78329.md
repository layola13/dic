gerrymander: /ˈdʒɛriˌmændər/ (US) /ˈɡɛriˌmændər/ (UK)| n. vt.|不公正地划分选区；操纵选区|To divide a state, county, etc., into election districts so as to give one political party a majority in many districts while concentrating the voting strength of the other party into as few districts as possible. |The state legislature gerrymandered the districts to ensure their party's reelection. 州议会不公正地划分选区以确保他们党派连任。|Synonyms: manipulate, redistrict, contrive|Antonyms:  fair apportionment, equitable distribution|noun, verb

gerrymandering: /ˈdʒɛriˌmændərɪŋ/ (US) /ˈɡɛriˌmændərɪŋ/ (UK)| n.|不公正地划分选区；操纵选区|The practice of gerrymandering. |Gerrymandering is a controversial tactic used to gain political advantage. 不公正地划分选区是一种用来获取政治优势的有争议的策略。|Synonyms: manipulation, redistricting|Antonyms: fair apportionment, equitable distribution|noun
