momentary:/ˈmoʊmənˌteri/ (US) /ˈməʊməntəri/ (UK)| adj.|短暂的，瞬间的|Lasting for a very short time; brief.|例句：The pain was momentary, but intense. 疼痛短暂却剧烈。|例句：She felt a momentary pang of guilt. 她感到一阵短暂的内疚。|近义词：brief, fleeting, transient, ephemeral|反义词：lasting, enduring, permanent|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *momentaneus, from Latin momentaneum (“of a moment, momentary”), neuter of momentaneus (“of a moment”), from momentum (“movement, motion; moving power, force; instant, moment”), from movēre (“to move”).
