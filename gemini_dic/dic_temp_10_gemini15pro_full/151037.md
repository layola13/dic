parapetalous:/ˌpærəˈpɛtələs/| adj.|分离花瓣的|Having petals that are separate or distinct from each other, not united or joined.|The flower is parapetalous, with five distinct petals.  这朵花是分离花瓣的，有五个不同的花瓣。|Example: The buttercup is a common example of a parapetalous flower. 毛茛是分离花瓣花的常见例子。|Synonyms: choripetalous, polypetalous|Antonyms: gamopetalous, sympetalous|Adjective
