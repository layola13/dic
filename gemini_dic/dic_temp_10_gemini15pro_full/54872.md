displeasingnesses:/dɪsˈpliːzɪŋnɪsɪz/
n.
令人不快的事物；讨厌的事物
The state or quality of being displeasing; things that are displeasing.
His constant complaining and displeasingnesses made him unpopular. 他不断的抱怨和令人讨厌的行为让他不受欢迎。
近义词:unpleasantries, offensiveness, disagreeableness
反义词:pleasantries, agreeableness
词性:名词

displeasingness:/dɪsˈpliːzɪŋnɪs/
n.
令人不快；讨厌
The state or quality of being displeasing.
The displeasingness of the smell made her leave the room.  难闻的气味让她离开了房间。
近义词:unpleasantness, offensiveness, disagreeableness
反义词:pleasantness, agreeableness
词性:名词

displeasing:/dɪsˈpliːzɪŋ/
adj.
令人不快的；讨厌的；不令人满意的
Causing displeasure, annoying, unsatisfactory.
He found her behavior displeasing and told her so. 他觉得她的行为令人不快，并告诉了她。
近义词:unpleasant, offensive, disagreeable
反义词:pleasant, agreeable
词性:形容词

displease:/dɪsˈpliːz/
v.
使不高兴；使生气；得罪
To cause displeasure or annoyance to; offend.
His actions displeased his parents greatly. 他的行为让他的父母非常生气。
近义词:annoy, irritate, offend
反义词:please, delight
词性:动词
