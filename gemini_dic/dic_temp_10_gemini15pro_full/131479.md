noncoincident:/ˌnɒnkəʊˈɪnsɪdənt/ | adj. |不重合的，不一致的 | Not occurring at the same time or in the same place; not matching or agreeing. | The two events were noncoincident, happening on different days. 这两件事发生在不同的日子，并不重合。| The noncoincident schedules made it difficult to arrange a meeting.  不一致的时间表使得安排会议变得困难。| synonyms: disparate, different, dissimilar, incongruous, divergent | antonyms: coincident, concurrent, simultaneous, synchronous | adjective
