panicky:/ˈpænɪki/| adj.|恐慌的，惊慌失措的|Experiencing or characterized by panic; extremely anxious and frightened.|She felt panicky when she realized she was lost. 她意识到自己迷路了，感到惊慌失措。|Example: The investors became panicky when the stock market crashed.  投资者在股市崩盘时变得恐慌起来。|Synonyms: frantic, hysterical, agitated, alarmed|Antonyms: calm, composed, tranquil|word type: adjective
