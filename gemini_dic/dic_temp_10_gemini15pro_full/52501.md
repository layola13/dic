difference:/ˈdɪfrəns/ (US), /ˈdɪfrənts/ (UK)| n.| 差异，不同，争执| a point or way in which people or things are not the same| There's a big difference between saying you'll do something and actually doing it.  说到做到和只说不做有很大的区别。| synonyms: distinction, dissimilarity, contrast, variation | antonyms: similarity, likeness, resemblance | noun

difference:/ˈdɪfrəns/ (US), /ˈdɪfrənts/ (UK)| v.| 导致差异，使…不同，有影响，起作用| to make someone or something different or distinct|  Her volunteer work has made a real difference in the community. 她的志愿工作对社区产生了真正的影响。| synonyms: distinguish, vary, alter, modify | antonyms:  harmonize, equalize | verb
