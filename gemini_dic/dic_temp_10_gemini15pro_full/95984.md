incunabulum:/ɪnˌkjuːˈnæbjʊləm/ | n. | 摇篮；初期作品；古版书 | A book printed before 1501.  A product of the infancy of printing. | 例句：This incunabulum is a valuable piece of printing history. 这部古版书是印刷史上珍贵的作品。

例句：The museum's collection includes several incunabula from the 15th century.  这家博物馆的藏品包括几件15世纪的古版书。| 近义词：early printed book, cradle | 反义词: modern book | 词性: noun
