allegiance:/əˈliːdʒəns/
n.
忠诚，忠贞；效忠
loyalty or commitment of a subordinate to a superior or of an individual to a group or cause.
例句：
She pledged allegiance to the flag. 她对国旗宣誓效忠。

He swore allegiance to the king. 他向国王宣誓效忠。


近义词：loyalty, fidelity, devotion, commitment
反义词：treachery, betrayal, disloyalty
词性：名词
