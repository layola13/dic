antemosaic:/ˌæntiˈmoʊzeɪɪk/
adjective
前摩西时代的; 摩西律法之前的
Relating to the period before Moses, especially before the giving of the Mosaic Law.
The antemosaic patriarchs lived by a simpler code of conduct. 前摩西时代的族长们遵循更简单的行为准则。
Near Synonyms: pre-Mosaic, pre-Sinaitic
Antonyms: post-Mosaic, Mosaic
词性: adjective
