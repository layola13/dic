cafeteria:/ˌkæfəˈtɪəriə/| n.|自助餐厅，食堂|A restaurant or dining room in a school, factory, hospital, etc., where people serve themselves or are served from a counter and pay before eating.|例句：The school cafeteria serves lunch every day. 学校食堂每天都供应午餐。

例句：The company cafeteria is always crowded at lunchtime. 公司食堂午餐时间总是很拥挤。|近义词：canteen, dining hall, lunchroom|反义词:restaurant (more formal, table service)|noun
