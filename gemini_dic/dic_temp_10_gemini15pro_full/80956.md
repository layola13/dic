gracefully:/ˈɡreɪsfəli/ | adv. |优雅地；优美地|In a graceful manner; with elegance and poise.  Moving, behaving, or done in a smooth and refined way. |She gracefully accepted the award. 她优雅地接受了奖项。|  The dancer moved gracefully across the stage. 舞蹈演员优雅地在舞台上移动。| 近义词: elegantly, smoothly, fluidly, effortlessly | 反义词: clumsily, awkwardly, stiffly | 词性: 副词
