ornithon:/ˈɔːrnɪθɒn/ (UK) /ˈɔːrnɪθɑːn/ (US)| n. |鸟舍；大型鸟笼|A large cage or aviary for birds, especially one for exotic birds.|The ornithon housed a variety of colorful parrots and macaws.  这个鸟舍里养着各种各样色彩艳丽的鹦鹉和金刚鹦鹉。|Synonym: aviary, birdhouse|Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "open field")|Noun
