foldings:/ˈfoʊldɪŋz/
n.
折叠；褶皱；折叠物
the act of bending something, especially paper or cloth, so that one part of it lies on top of another part; something that has been folded
The artist used intricate foldings in her paper sculptures. 艺术家在她的纸雕作品中使用了复杂的折叠。
creases, pleats, wrinkles
unfoldings, straightenings
noun
