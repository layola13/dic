moblogged:/moʊˈblɒɡd/ /ˈmoʊblɑːɡd/
verb
用手机博客; 手机博客发布
Created a blog post using a mobile device.
例句：She moblogged her experience at the concert. 她用手机博客记录了她在音乐会的经历。

例句：He moblogged a photo of the sunset from his phone. 他用手机上传了一张日落的照片到博客。

近义词：blogged, posted, updated
反义词：(No direct antonym, perhaps "kept private")
词性：verb (past tense and past participle of "moblog")
