freeze:/friːz/| v. n.| 结冰，冻结，(使)停止，冷冻 | To change from a liquid to a solid because of low temperature; to stop moving suddenly. | The lake froze over in winter. 湖在冬天结冰了。| The company froze all salaries due to the recession. 由于经济衰退，公司冻结了所有工资。 | synonyms: chill, congeal, solidify, halt | antonyms: melt, thaw, liquefy | verb, noun

freeze:/friːz/| n. | 冻结；冰冻期 | A period of very cold weather. |  We had a freeze last night and the plants are all dead. 昨晚我们这里结冰了，植物都死了。| The economic freeze led to many job losses. 经济冻结导致许多人失业。 | synonyms: frost, cold snap | antonyms: thaw | noun
