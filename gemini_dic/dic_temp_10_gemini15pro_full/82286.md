groundwards:/ˈɡraʊndwədz/ | adv. | 向地面；朝下 | Towards the ground; downwards. | The apple fell groundwards from the tree. 苹果从树上掉落到地面。 |  The kite plummeted groundwards after the string broke.  风筝线断了之后，风筝直落地面。 | 近义词: downwards, earthwards | 反义词: upwards, skywards | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
ground (from Old English grund, meaning "ground, bottom, foundation") + -wards (Old English -weardes, indicating direction)
