overroasting:/ˌoʊvərˈroʊstɪŋ/ | n./v.| 烤过头，过度烘烤 | The act of roasting something for too long, resulting in dryness or burning. |  I ruined the chicken by overroasting it. 我把鸡烤过头了，烤坏了。| synonyms: burning, scorching | antonyms: undercooking, parboiling | verb/noun

overroast:/ˌoʊvərˈroʊst/ | v. | 烤过头，过度烘烤 | To roast something for too long, resulting in dryness or burning. | Be careful not to overroast the vegetables. 小心别把蔬菜烤过头了。 | synonyms: burn, scorch | antonyms: undercook, parboil | verb


Etymology:
"over-" (Old English *ofer, from Proto-Germanic *uberi) meaning "above, beyond, excessively" + "roast" (Middle English rosten, from Old French rostir, ultimately from Vulgar Latin *arrustīre, from Latin ad- + rūstum "roasted meat").
