griddling:/ˈɡrɪdlɪŋ/| n. v.|煎; 在烤盘上烘烤|The act of cooking something on a griddle.  It can also refer to the food being cooked in this way.|例句：The griddling of the pancakes created a delicious aroma. 煎薄饼散发出诱人的香味。

例句：I love the sound of bacon griddling on a Sunday morning. 我喜欢周日早上培根在烤盘上煎的声音。|近义词：pan-frying, frying, grilling|反义词:N/A|词性:noun, verb (present participle)
Etymology: From griddle + -ing.  Griddle comes from Old North French *gredil, diminutive of greille "gridiron," from Latin craticula, diminutive of crates "hurdle, wickerwork."
