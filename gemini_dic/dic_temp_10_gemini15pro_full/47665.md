debriefers:/ˌdiːˈbriːfərz/
n.
汇报人；询问情况的人；事后调查员
People who conduct debriefings.  Those who question individuals after an event or mission to gather information.
The debriefers questioned the pilots after their mission.  汇报员在飞行员执行任务后询问了他们。
The debriefers gathered valuable intelligence from the returning agents.  汇报员从返回的特工那里收集了宝贵的情报。
synonyms: interviewers, questioners, investigators
antonyms: briefers, informants
