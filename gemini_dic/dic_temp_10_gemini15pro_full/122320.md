mitzvah:/ˈmɪtsvə/ (US), /ˈmɪtsva/ (UK)| n.|戒律，善行，功德|A commandment of the Jewish law; a meritorious or charitable act.|例句：It is a mitzvah to help those in need. 帮助有需要的人是一种善行。

例句：Celebrating a Bar Mitzvah is an important Jewish tradition. 庆祝成年礼是犹太人的一项重要传统。|近义词：good deed, precept, commandment|反义词：sin, transgression|词性: noun

词源: From Hebrew מִצְוָה (miṣwāh, “commandment”).
