ontologies:/ɒnˈtɒlədʒiz/ /ɑːnˈtɑːlədʒiz/| n.|本体；本体论|A set of concepts and categories in a subject area or domain that shows their properties and the relations between them.  A formal representation of knowledge as a set of concepts within a domain, and the relationships between those concepts.|The development of ontologies is crucial for knowledge representation and reasoning in artificial intelligence.|近义词：frameworks, schemas, taxonomies|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consider "disorder," "chaos," or "randomness" in a philosophical context)|noun
