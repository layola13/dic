benighted:/bɪˈnaɪtɪd/
adjective
愚昧的，落后的
Intellectually or morally ignorant; unenlightened.
例句：The benighted villagers clung to their superstitious beliefs. 愚昧的村民固守着他们的迷信观念。

例句：Missionaries sought to convert the benighted tribes of the Amazon. 传教士试图改变亚马逊地区落后部落的信仰。

近义词：ignorant, unenlightened, backward, primitive, uncivilized
反义词：enlightened, educated, informed, civilized, sophisticated
词性: adjective
