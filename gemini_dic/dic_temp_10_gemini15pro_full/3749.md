agings:/ˈeɪdʒɪŋz/
n.
老化，陈化；老化的情况；陈酿
the process of growing old or maturing, or the state of being old or mature.
The agings of the wines in the cellar vary significantly. 酒窖里葡萄酒的陈酿时间差别很大。
aging, maturation, ripening
rejuvenation, renewal
noun

agings:/ˈeɪdʒɪŋz/
n.
（账龄分析中的）账龄，逾期
periods of time debts have been outstanding.
The report showed the agings of all outstanding invoices.  报告显示了所有未付发票的账龄。
maturity, due date
current, paid
noun
