co-obligor:/ˌkoʊˈɒblɪɡər/ /ˌkəʊˈɒblɪɡər/| n.|共同债务人，连带债务人|A person or entity who is jointly obligated with another party to repay a loan or debt.|例句：My brother is my co-obligor on the mortgage. 我哥哥是这笔抵押贷款的共同债务人。

例句：The bank required a co-obligor on the loan because of her limited credit history. 由于她的信用记录有限，银行要求贷款需要一个共同债务人。

近义词：joint debtor, co-signer, guarantor|反义词:|词性:noun
