chambers: /ˈtʃeɪmbərz/ (US) /ˈtʃeɪmbəz/ (UK)| n.| 律师，大律师 | (usually plural) a lawyer or a group of lawyers who give legal advice and represent people in court|  She instructed her chambers to begin proceedings. 她指示她的律师事务所开始诉讼程序。 | synonyms: lawyer, barrister, solicitor, attorney, counsel | antonyms: N/A | noun
