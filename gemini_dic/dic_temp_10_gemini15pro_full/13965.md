aureate:/ˈɔːriət/| adj.|金色的，华丽的，（文辞）浮夸的|Characterized by a golden color or brilliance; elaborately or excessively ornamented in style or language.  Marked by ornate or pompous language.|例句：The king wore an aureate crown. 国王戴着金色的王冠。

例句：His aureate prose style is not to everyone's taste. 他华丽的散文风格并非每个人都喜欢。|近义词：golden, gilded, ornate, flamboyant, flowery, bombastic|反义词：plain, simple, unadorned, austere|词性:adjective
词源:From Latin *aureātus, past participle of aureāre (“to gild”), from aureus (“golden”).
