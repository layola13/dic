adhocery: /ædˈhɒkəri/
adjective
特别的；临时的；权宜之计的
Created or done for a particular purpose as necessary.
The investigation was conducted under the auspices of an ad hoc committee.  这个调查是在一个特别委员会的主持下进行的。

The group was formed ad hoc to address the specific issue.  该小组是临时组建的，以处理具体问题。

近义词:makeshift, improvised, impromptu, temporary, provisional
反义词:planned, permanent, long-term
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *ad hoc* "for this," from *ad* "to, toward" + *hoc* "this."  源自拉丁语 *ad hoc* “为此”，由 *ad* “到，朝向” + *hoc* “这” 组成。


USAGE->
Attributive usage (before a noun):

The ad hoc committee met only twice. 特别委员会只开了两次会。
An ad hoc solution was implemented to fix the immediate problem.  一个临时解决方案被实施以解决眼前的问题。

Predicative usage (after a verb):
The arrangements were strictly ad hoc. 这些安排完全是临时的。
The meeting was rather ad hoc, with no clear agenda. 会议相当随意，没有明确的议程。

PHRASE->
ad hoc committee = 特别委员会
ad hoc basis = 临时基础
ad hoc arrangement = 临时安排
ad hoc solution = 临时解决方案
ad hoc network = 自组织网络
