flusteration:/ˌflʌstəˈreɪʃn/ | n. | 慌乱，不安，紧张 | A state of agitated confusion or nervous excitement. |  The unexpected news threw him into a state of flusteration.  这突如其来的消息让他感到慌乱。|  Near synonyms: agitation, confusion, discombobulation, perturbation | Near antonyms: calmness, composure, serenity | noun

fluster:/ˈflʌstər/ | vt.  | 使慌乱，使不安 | To make someone agitated or confused. | The loud noise flustered the speaker. 巨大的噪音使演讲者感到慌乱。 | Near synonyms: agitate, confuse, disconcert, rattle | Near antonyms: calm, compose, reassure | verb
