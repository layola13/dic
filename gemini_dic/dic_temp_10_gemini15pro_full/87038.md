hepatogenic:/ˌhepətəˈdʒɛnɪk/| adj.|肝源性的|Originating in the liver; produced by the liver.|例句：Hepatogenic jaundice is caused by liver disease. 肝源性黄疸是由肝病引起的。

例句：The doctor suspected a hepatogenic cause for the patient's elevated liver enzymes. 医生怀疑病人肝酶升高是由肝脏引起的。|近义词：hepatic|反义词: (No direct antonym, but could consider terms related to other organ origins, e.g., nephrogenic (kidney), cardiogenic (heart))|词性:adjective
