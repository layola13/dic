exempts:/ɪɡˈzɛmpts/| v.  n.|免除，豁免; 被免除者|This certificate exempts you from paying the tax. 这张证明免除你缴税。|近义词：excuses, releases, frees|反义词: includes, obligates|verb, noun


exempt:/ɪɡˈzɛmpt/| adj. v.|免除的，豁免的; 免除，豁免|He is exempt from military service. 他免服兵役。|近义词： excused, free, released|反义词: liable, subject|adjective, verb

词源:  From Old French *exempt, past participle of exempter "to remove, take out," from Latin exemptus, past participle of eximere "to take out, remove; free from; deliver," from ex- "out of" + emere "to take, buy, obtain."
