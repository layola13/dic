condominium:/ˌkɒndəˈmɪniəm/ (US), /ˌkɒndəˈmɪniəm/ (UK)| n.|公寓；一套公寓；共同统治；共管|A building or complex of buildings containing a number of individually owned apartments or houses.|She lives in a condominium overlooking the ocean. 她住在一套可以俯瞰大海的公寓里。|Synonyms: apartment, flat, unit|Antonyms: house, detached house|noun

condominiums:/ˌkɒndəˈmɪniəmz/ (US), /ˌkɒndəˈmɪniəmz/ (UK)| n.|公寓（复数）；一套公寓（复数）|Plural form of condominium.|The condominiums on the beach are very expensive. 海滩上的公寓非常昂贵。|Synonyms: apartments, flats, units|Antonyms: houses, detached houses|noun
