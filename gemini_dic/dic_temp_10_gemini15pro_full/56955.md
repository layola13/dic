what:/wɒt/(UK) /wɑːt/(US) |pron. det. adv.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information specifying something. 用于询问具体信息的疑问词。|What is your name? 你叫什么名字？

What color is the sky? 天空是什么颜色？

近义词:which(在有限选项中选择)|反义词: N/A |pronoun, determiner, adverb

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she do for a living? 她做什么工作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned so far? 你到目前为止学到了什么？
What has she bought at the mall? 她在商场买了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading all afternoon? 他整个下午都在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last weekend? 你上周末做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? 她对这部电影说了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 o'clock last night? 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you went to bed? 你睡觉前做了什么？
What had she eaten before she got sick? 她生病前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been studying before you changed your major? 你换专业前一直在学什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? 他们下次会议会讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 你到年底会完成什么？
What will have changed by the time we get there? 等我们到那里的时候会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by 2028? 到2028年你将已经做了五年什么？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时，她将已经学习什么专业多久了？
