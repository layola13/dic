georama:/ˌdʒɪəˈrɑːmə/|/ˌdʒɪəˈræmə/| n.|西洋景；立体透视画；世界全图；地理模型|A model or other visual representation of the earth's surface or part of it, especially one in which the natural features are represented in three dimensions.|The museum boasts a fascinating georama of the Grand Canyon.|近义词：diorama, panorama, model, globe|反义词: None|词性:noun

Etymology: From geo- (earth) + -orama (view).  The suffix -orama comes from Greek horama "a view, spectacle".
