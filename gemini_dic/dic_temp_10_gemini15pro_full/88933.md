holding:/ˈhoʊldɪŋ/ (US) /ˈhəʊldɪŋ/ (UK)| n. |持有物，股份；财产；(尤指农业)土地|Something that is held or owned, especially land or shares in a company. |例句：He has substantial holdings in real estate. 他拥有大量的房地产。|近义词：possession, asset, property|反义词:debt, liability|Noun

holding:/ˈhoʊldɪŋ/ (US) /ˈhəʊldɪŋ/ (UK)| adj. |握着的；抓着的；支撑的|Used to describe something that holds or supports something else.  |例句：A holding company is a company that controls other companies. 控股公司是一家控制其他公司的公司。|近义词：supporting, gripping, grasping|反义词:releasing, dropping|Adjective
