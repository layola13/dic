antischizophrenic: /ˌæntiˌskɪtsəˈfrɛnɪk/ | adj. n. | 抗精神分裂症的；抗精神分裂症药物 |  Relating to or denoting drugs or other treatments that alleviate the symptoms of schizophrenia.  |  The doctor prescribed an antischizophrenic medication. 医生开了抗精神分裂症的药物。 |  The new antischizophrenic has shown promising results in clinical trials.  这种新的抗精神分裂药物在临床试验中显示出 promising 的结果。 | Synonyms: neuroleptic, antipsychotic | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, but potentially  "schizophrenia-inducing" which isn't a standard medical term) | adjective, noun


词源:  anti- (Greek: against) + schizophrenia (Greek: skhizein - to split + phrēn - mind) + -ic (suffix forming adjectives)
