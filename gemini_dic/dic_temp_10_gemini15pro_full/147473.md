overopinionatedness:/ˌoʊvərəˈpɪnjəneɪtɪdnəs/
|n.|过度自信，固执己见|The state of being excessively opinionated; having or expressing too many opinions, especially in a dogmatic or arrogant way.|His overopinionatedness made it difficult to have a productive conversation. 他过于固执己见，难以进行有效的沟通。|Synonym: dogmatism, arrogance, self-righteousness|Antonym: open-mindedness, humility, receptiveness|noun
