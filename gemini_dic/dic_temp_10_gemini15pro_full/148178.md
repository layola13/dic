oversettlement:/ˌoʊvərˈsɛtlmənt/
n.
过度殖民; 过度定居; 人口过剩
An excessive number of settlers in an area, leading to overcrowding and depletion of resources.
The oversettlement of the small island led to a shortage of fresh water.  这个小岛的人口过剩导致了淡水短缺。

近义词: overcrowding, overpopulation
反义词: underpopulation
