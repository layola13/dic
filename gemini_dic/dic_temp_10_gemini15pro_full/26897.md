calabash:/ˈkæləbæʃ/| n. |葫芦，瓢|A large, round fruit with a hard shell, used for making bowls, containers, etc.  The dried, hollowed shell of this fruit used as a container.|The traveler carried water in a calabash. 旅行者用葫芦装着水。|Synonyms: gourd, bottle gourd|Antonyms: None|Noun

calabashes:/ˈkæləbæʃɪz/| n. |葫芦，瓢（复数）|Plural form of calabash.|She decorated her home with colorful calabashes. 她用色彩缤纷的葫芦装饰她的家。|Synonyms: gourds, bottle gourds|Antonyms: None|Noun
