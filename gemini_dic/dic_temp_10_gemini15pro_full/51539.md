detour:/ˈdiːtʊr/ /dɪˈtʊr/| n. vi. vt.|绕道；迂回；绕行；使绕道而行|To detour means to take a longer route in order to avoid a problem on the usual route. 绕道意味着走更长的路线以避开通常路线上的问题。|The road was closed, so we had to detour. 道路封闭了，所以我们不得不绕道而行。|synonyms: bypass, divert, circumnavigate|antonyms: go direct, proceed directly|noun, verb
