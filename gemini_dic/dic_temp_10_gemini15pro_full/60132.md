effectivenesses:/ɪˈfɛktɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
有效性（复数）
the quality of being effective; the degree to which something is successful in producing a desired result
The effectivenesses of the different treatments were compared.  比较了不同疗法的有效性。

近义词:efficacies, potencies, strengths
反义词:ineffectiveness, inefficiencies, weaknesses
词性:名词
