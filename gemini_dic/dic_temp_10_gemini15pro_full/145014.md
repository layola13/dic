overadvance:/ˌoʊvərədˈvæns/ | n. vt.| 过度垫款，预付过多 | An overadvance is a payment made in excess of what is due or appropriate. | The company issued an overadvance to the supplier, which they later regretted. 公司向供应商支付了过多的预付款，后来他们后悔了。| The bank accidentally made an overadvance on the loan. 银行不小心多付了贷款。 | 近义词：overpayment, prepayment | 反义词：underpayment, arrears | n.
overadvance:/ˌoʊvərədˈvæns/ | vt.| 过度垫款，预付过多 | To overadvance is to pay more than is due or necessary. | They overadvanced him on his salary. 他们预支了他过多的薪水。 | The publisher overadvanced the author on royalties. 出版商向作者预支了过多的版税。 | 近义词：overpay, prepay | 反义词：underpay | vt.
