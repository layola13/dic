crossfit: /ˈkrɒsfɪt/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsfɪt/ (US) | n. | 交叉健身 | A high-intensity fitness program incorporating elements from several sports and types of exercise. |  I started CrossFit a few months ago and I'm already seeing results. 我几个月前开始做CrossFit，现在已经看到了效果。|  synonyms:  high-intensity training, functional fitness, boot camp | antonyms:  low-impact exercise, yoga (depending on the style) | noun

crossfit: /ˈkrɒsfɪt/ (UK) /ˈkrɔːsfɪt/ (US) | adj. | 交叉健身的 | Relating to or characteristic of CrossFit. | She follows a CrossFit lifestyle. 她遵循CrossFit的生活方式。 | synonyms:  functional fitness, WOD-based | antonyms: traditional weightlifting, bodybuilding | adjective
