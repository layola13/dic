assails:/əˈseɪlz/| v.|攻击，袭击，困扰|to attack someone violently or criticize someone strongly|例句：The enemy assailed our defenses. 敌人攻击了我们的防御工事。|近义词：attack, assault, beset, bombard, criticize|反义词:defend, protect, support|词性:动词

词源: From Old French *assalir, from Latin ad + salire "to leap, jump."
