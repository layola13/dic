delusively:/dɪˈluːsɪvli/ | adv. | 妄想地，错觉地 | In a way that is based on or produces a delusion. | He was delusively optimistic about the outcome. 他对结果抱有不切实际的乐观态度。| She delusively believed she could fly. 她妄想自己能飞。 | 近义词: misleadingly, deceptively, falsely | 反义词: realistically, truthfully, accurately | 词性: 副词

词源: From delusive + -ly.  "Delusive" comes from the Latin word "deludere" meaning "to mock, deceive, play false."
