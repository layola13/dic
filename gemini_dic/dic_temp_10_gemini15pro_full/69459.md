feign:/feɪn/
verb
假装，装作
to pretend to feel or be affected by (something)
She feigned illness to avoid going to school. 她装病不去上学。
Synonyms: pretend, simulate, sham, affect
Antonyms: be honest, be genuine
词性: 动词

feign:/feɪn/
verb
佯作；装出
to give a false appearance of : SIMULATE
He feigned indifference, but I knew he was really upset. 他假装不在乎，但我知道他真的很沮丧。
Synonyms:  dissemble, fake, counterfeit
Antonyms:  be real, be sincere, be true
词性: 动词
