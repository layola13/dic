impregnability:/ɪmˌprɛɡnəˈbɪləti/
n.
不可攻陷；坚不可摧
The state or quality of being impregnable; incapable of being taken by force, broken into, or overthrown.
The castle's impregnability made it a safe haven during the war.  这座城堡的坚不可摧使它在战争期间成为一个安全的避难所。
近义词:invincibility, invulnerability, unassailability
反义词:vulnerability, weakness, penetrability
