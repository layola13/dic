darkliest:/ˈdɑːrkɪst/| adj.|最黑暗的|Superlative adjective of dark. Meaning the most lacking in light or without any light. Can also mean gloomy, pessimistic, or evil.|例句：It was the darkest hour before the dawn. 黎明前的时刻是最黑暗的。

例句：He committed his darkest deeds under the cover of night. 他在夜幕的掩护下犯下了最黑暗的罪行。|近义词：gloomiest, bleakest, blackest|反义词：brightest, lightest|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *derkest, from Old English *diercost, superlative of *dierc (“dark”). Cognate with West Frisian tsjusterst (“darkest”), Dutch donkerst (“darkest”), German dunkelst (“darkest”). The -est suffix is a common superlative ending in English, derived from Proto-Germanic *-istaz.
