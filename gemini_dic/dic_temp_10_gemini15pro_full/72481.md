fluocerine:/ˌfluːəˈsɪərɪn/ /ˌfluːəˈsɪriːn/ | n. |氟铈矿|A mineral, essentially a hydrated cerium fluoride, CeF₃·½H₂O. It occurs as yellowish to reddish-brown hexagonal crystals.|Fluocerine is a rare earth mineral found in hydrothermal veins.|近义词: None (as it's a specific mineral) |反义词: None |词性: noun
