entrepreneurially:/ˌɑːntrəprəˈnɜːriəli/
adverb
企业家式地；创业精神地
In a way that shows the qualities of an entrepreneur, such as initiative, innovation, and risk-taking.
She approached the project entrepreneurially, identifying a gap in the market and developing a unique solution. 她以企业家的方式处理这个项目，发现了市场上的一个缺口，并开发了一个独特的解决方案。
He entrepreneurially started a new business selling handmade goods online. 他以企业家的精神在网上创办了一家销售手工制品的新公司。
近义词: innovatively, proactively, enterprisingly
反义词: cautiously, conservatively, passively
词性: adverb

词源:
From entrepreneur + -ial + -ly.  "Entrepreneur" comes from French, ultimately from the verb entreprendre "to undertake," from entre- "between" + prendre "to take."
