hyperintelligent:/ˌhaɪpərɪnˈtɛlɪdʒənt/
adjective
超级智能的
Possessing extremely high intelligence; far surpassing normal human intelligence.
例句：The hyperintelligent computer quickly solved the complex equation. 这台超级智能的电脑迅速解开了复杂的方程式。

例句：Scientists are exploring the potential dangers of hyperintelligent AI. 科学家们正在探索超级智能人工智能的潜在危险。

近义词：superintelligent, exceptionally intelligent, highly intelligent
反义词：unintelligent, stupid, dull-witted
词性: adjective
