certainly:/ˈsɜːrtnli/ (US) /ˈsɜːtənli/ (UK)| adv.| 当然；肯定地；一定；没问题 |Used to express complete agreement or to emphasize something.| "Will you help me?" "Certainly!"  “你会帮我吗？” “当然！”|近义词:definitely, absolutely, surely, of course|反义词:not at all, definitely not, by no means|词性:副词

词源: From Middle English *certeinli, *certenly, from Old French *certainement, from certain (“certain”) + -ment.
