blithest:/ˈblaɪðɪst/ /ˈblaɪðəst/| adj.|最快乐的，最无忧无虑的|Happiest, most carefree, showing a cheerful indifference considered to be improper or foolish.|She danced with blithest unconcern, ignoring the worried glances of her parents. 她跳舞时无忧无虑，全然不顾父母担忧的目光。|近义词：cheeriest, merriest, happiest, most joyful|反义词：saddest, gloomiest, most miserable|词性:形容词
词源:From Middle English *blithest, *blythest, from Old English blīþost (“most blithe, most merry, most kind, most gentle, most pleasant”), superlative of blīþe (“blithe”).
