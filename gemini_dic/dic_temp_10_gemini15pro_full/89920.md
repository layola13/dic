horse-trader:/ˈhɔːrsˌtreɪdər/| n. |马贩子，精明的交易者|A person who buys and sells horses, often with a reputation for shrewd bargaining or sometimes questionable dealings.  A skilled negotiator who drives a hard bargain, sometimes implying a willingness to use manipulative tactics.|He was a shrewd horse-trader, always getting the best deal. 他是个精明的马贩子，总是能得到最好的交易。|近义词：dealer, trader, bargainer, negotiator|反义词: customer, buyer|noun
