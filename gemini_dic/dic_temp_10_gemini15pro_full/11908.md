arrowroot:/ˈærəʊruːt/ (UK) /ˈærˌoʊˌruːt/ (US)| n.| 竹芋粉，竹芋淀粉|A starch obtained from the rhizomes of certain tropical American plants.|例句：She used arrowroot to thicken the sauce. 她用竹芋粉使酱汁变稠。

例句：Arrowroot is a good substitute for cornstarch. 竹芋粉是玉米淀粉的良好替代品。

近义词：tapioca starch, cornstarch|反义词:|n.
