/wɒt/ (British) /hwɑːt/ (American) |pron. det. int. adv.|什么，多么|Something that is not known or specified.|

What is your name?|你叫什么名字？

What did you say?|你说了什么？

What a beautiful day!|多么美好的一天！

What time is it?|现在几点了？

近义词: which, how|
反义词: (No direct antonym for "what")|
