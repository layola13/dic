aconital:/ˌækəˈnaɪtəl/| adj.|乌头的，附子的|Relating to or containing aconite, a poisonous plant extract.|The aconital poison caused severe heart problems.  乌头毒素引起了严重的心脏问题。|Synonyms: aconitic|Antonyms: None|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *aconitalis, derived from Latin aconitum ("aconite"), ultimately from Ancient Greek ἀκόνιτον (akóniton, “wolfsbane”).

USAGE->
Attributive usage:
The aconital extract is highly toxic. = 乌头提取物有剧毒。
He studied the effects of aconital poisoning. = 他研究了乌头中毒的影响。

Predicative usage (less common):
The symptoms were aconital in nature. = 这些症状本质上是乌头中毒的症状。（这种用法比较少见，通常用 "caused by aconite" 或 "characteristic of aconite poisoning" 等表达方式）


PHRASE->
aconital poisoning = 乌头中毒
aconital alkaloids = 乌头生物碱
aconital extract = 乌头提取物
aconital symptoms = 乌头中毒症状
aconital effects = 乌头的影响
