faintish:/ˈfeɪntɪʃ/| adj.|有点晕的，略感虚弱的|Slightly faint; feeling weak or dizzy.|例句：I felt a bit faintish after standing up too quickly.  我站起来太快，感觉有点晕。

例句：She had a faintish smile on her face. 她脸上带着一丝淡淡的微笑。|近义词：dizzy, lightheaded, weak, queasy|反义词：strong, healthy, robust|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From faint + -ish.  Faint comes from Old French *feint, past participle of feindre "to feign, pretend," from Latin fingere "to shape, form, invent, feign." The suffix -ish means "somewhat," "rather," or "having the quality of."
