dependant:/dɪˈpɛndənt/ (UK) /dɪˈpɛndənt/ (US)| n. adj.|受抚养者，依赖的|A person who relies on another, especially financially.  As an adjective, it means relying on someone or something else for support.|例句：She has three dependants: her two children and her elderly mother. 她有三个受抚养者：她的两个孩子和她年迈的母亲。|近义词：dependent, reliant, ward (n.), subject (adj.)|反义词:independent, self-reliant|词性:noun, adjective

dependant:/dɪˈpɛndənt/ (UK) /dɪˈpɛndənt/ (US)| adj.|依赖的|Relying on someone or something else for support.|例句：The project is dependant on funding from the government.  这个项目依赖于政府的资金。|近义词：dependent, reliant, subject|反义词:independent, self-reliant|词性:adjective

词源：Late Middle English: from Old French *dependant, present participle of dependre, from Latin dependere ‘hang down from’.
