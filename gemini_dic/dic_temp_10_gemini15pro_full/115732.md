marich:/ˈmɑːrɪtʃ/ | n. | 辣椒; 辣椒粉 | A pungent spice made from dried and ground chili peppers. |  The marich added a fiery kick to the curry.  这辣椒粉给咖喱增添了火辣的味道。| synonyms: chili powder, cayenne pepper, paprika | antonyms: mild spice, bland seasoning | noun


marich:/ˈmɑːrɪtʃ/ | n. | (有时大写)玛丽奇（印度教神话中的恶魔）|  In Hindu mythology, Marich is a demon who was killed by Rama. |  The story of Rama and Marich is a popular tale in Hindu mythology.  罗摩和玛丽奇的故事是印度神话中一个流行的故事。| synonyms: demon, asura | antonyms: deva, god | noun
