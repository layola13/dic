materiarian:/məˌtɪəriˈɛəriən/| n.|唯物主义者；唯物论者|A person who believes that nothing exists except matter and its movements and modifications.|例句：He is a staunch materiarian who believes only in what he can see and touch. 他是一个坚定的唯物主义者，只相信他所能看到和触摸到的东西。

例句：The materiarian philosophy rejects the notion of a spiritual realm. 唯物主义哲学否认精神领域的概念。|近义词：physicalist, materialist|反义词：idealist, spiritualist|词性:noun
