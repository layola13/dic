foregleam:/ˈfɔːrɡliːm/| n.|预兆，先兆；微光|A faint light or indication of something to come. |A foregleam of hope flickered in his eyes.  他眼中闪现出一丝希望的曙光。|近义词：premonition, presentiment, foreshadowing, glimmer, inkling|反义词:aftermath, consequence|词性:noun

foregleam:/ˈfɔːrɡliːm/| v.|预示，预兆|To give a faint indication of something to come.|The rising sun foregleamed a beautiful day.  冉冉升起的太阳预示着美好的一天。|近义词：foreshadow, presage, portend, augur|反义词: belie|词性:verb
