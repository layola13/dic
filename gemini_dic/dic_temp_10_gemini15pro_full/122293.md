mitral:/ˈmaɪtrəl/| adj.|二尖瓣的，僧帽形的|Relating to the mitral valve of the heart, which lies between the left atrium and the left ventricle.|The patient had mitral valve prolapse.  病人患有二尖瓣脱垂。|Synonyms: bicuspid|Antonyms: tricuspid|adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *mitralis, from mitra (“mitre, turban”), from Ancient Greek μίτρα (mítra, “headband, turban”).  The mitral valve's resemblance to a bishop's mitre (a ceremonial headdress) gave rise to the name.
