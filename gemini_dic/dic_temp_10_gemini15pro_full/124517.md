mountable:/ˈmaʊntəbl/| adj.|可安装的，可裱装的，可乘骑的|Capable of being mounted, attached, or put into position for use.  Something that can be mounted onto something else. |The TV is mountable on the wall.  这台电视可以挂在墙上。|The diamond is mountable on a ring. 这颗钻石可以镶嵌在戒指上。| attachable, installable, fixable |unmountable, non-mountable|adjective
