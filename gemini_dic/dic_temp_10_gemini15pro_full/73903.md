forslack:/fɔːrˈslæk/| adj.| 松弛的，不紧的；懒散的，懈怠的 |  Lacking in firmness, tightness, or resilience; negligent, careless. | The rope was forslack, allowing the boat to drift. 绳子松了，让船漂走了。|  loose, lax, slack, negligent, remiss | tight, taut, diligent, careful | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From for- + slack.  "For-" in this context acts as an intensifier, similar to "thoroughly" or "completely." "Slack" comes from the Old Norse "slakki," meaning "loose" or "sloppy."
