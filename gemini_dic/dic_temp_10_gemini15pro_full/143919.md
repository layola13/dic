outprize: /aʊtˈpraɪz/
verb
胜过，超过（在价值或质量上）
to surpass in value or excellence; to exceed in worth or quality.
He outprized all competitors with his innovative design. 他以其创新设计胜过所有竞争对手。
The new model outprizes the old one in terms of fuel efficiency.  新款车型在燃油效率方面超过了旧款。
近义词: surpass, exceed, outdo, outperform
反义词: lose to, be inferior to
词性: verb (动词)

Etymology:  "out-" (prefix indicating surpassing) + "prize" (from Old French "pris," past participle of "prendre" - to take).  The concept is of "taking a greater prize" than another.
