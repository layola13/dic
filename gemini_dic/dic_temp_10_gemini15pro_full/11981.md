artesian:/ɑːrˈtɪːʒən/ | adj. | 自流的，喷泉式的 |  Relating to or denoting a well bored perpendicularly into a water-bearing stratum lying between impermeable rocks so that natural pressure produces a constant supply of water at the surface. |  The town's water supply comes from an artesian well.  小镇的供水来自一口自流井。 | synonyms: flowing, spouting, gushing | antonyms: stagnant, still | adjective

artesian well:/ɑːrˈtɪːʒən wɛl/ | n. | 自流井 | A well bored perpendicularly into a water-bearing stratum lying between impermeable rocks so that natural pressure produces a constant supply of water at the surface. | The artesian well provided a reliable source of fresh water. 这口自流井提供了可靠的淡水来源。 | synonyms: flowing well, spouting well | antonyms: dry well | noun
