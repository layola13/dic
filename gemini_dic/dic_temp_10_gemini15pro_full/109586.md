lethality:/liːˈθælɪti/| n.|致命性，杀伤力|The lethality of the virus is a major concern. 这种病毒的致命性是一个主要问题。|例句：The lethality of the new weapon was terrifying.  这种新武器的杀伤力令人恐惧。

例句：The study examined the lethality of different snake venoms.  这项研究调查了不同蛇毒的致命性。

近义词：deadliness, fatality, mortality|反义词:harmlessness, safety|词性:noun

词源: From Late Latin *lēthālitās, from Latin lēthālis (“of death, deadly”), from lēthum (“death”) + -ālis.
