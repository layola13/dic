no-fault:/noʊˈfɔːlt/| adj.|无过错的，(尤指离婚)双方均无过失的|  Pertaining to a situation where blame or fault is not assigned to any particular party, especially in legal contexts like divorce or insurance. |The couple had a no-fault divorce. 这对夫妇进行了无过错离婚。|  The no-fault insurance policy covered the damages regardless of who was at fault.  这份无过错保险单涵盖了损害赔偿，无论是谁的过错。|Synonyms: blameless, unblameable | Antonyms: culpable, at-fault | Adjective
