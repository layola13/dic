impercipient:/ˌɪmpərˈsɪpiənt/ | adj. | 无感觉的；无知觉的；未察觉的；不了解的 | Not perceiving; unobservant; unaware; lacking in perception or insight. |

例句：
He was impercipient to the subtle changes in her mood.
他未察觉到她情绪的微妙变化。

例句：
The impercipient driver didn't notice the pedestrian crossing the street.
粗心大意的司机没有注意到正在过马路的行人。

近义词：unaware, oblivious, unobservant, insensitive, inattentive |
反义词：perceptive, aware, observant, sensitive, attentive |
词性：adjective |

词源：Late 16th century: from Latin *impercipiens, impercipient- ‘not perceiving’, from in- ‘not’ + percipiens, percipient- (present participle of percipere ‘take in, perceive’). |
