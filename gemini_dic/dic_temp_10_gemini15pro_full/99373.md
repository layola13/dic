interdeterminations:/ɪntɜːrdɪˌtɜːrmɪˈneɪʃənz/ | n. |相互决定; 互相决定; 互为决定|The concept of interdeterminations suggests that various factors influence and shape each other reciprocally.|The complex interdeterminations of social, economic, and political forces make it difficult to predict future trends. 社会、经济和政治力量之间复杂的相互决定关系使得预测未来趋势变得困难。|Synonyms: interconnections, interrelations, interdependence|Antonyms: independence, isolation|noun
