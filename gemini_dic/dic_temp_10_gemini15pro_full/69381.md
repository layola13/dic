feebler:/ˈfiːblər/| adj.|更虚弱的，更微弱的|Comparative form of feeble: more feeble.  Meaning weaker in physical strength or health. Also, less intense or effective.|Her voice was feebler than yesterday. 她的声音比昨天更虚弱了。|The light from the candle grew feebler. 蜡烛的光越来越微弱了。|近义词：weaker, frailer, fainter|反义词：stronger, healthier, more intense|词性:形容词|词源:From Middle English *febleer, comparative of feble, from Old French feble, from Late Latin flebilis (“lamentable”), from Latin flēbilis, from flēō (“I weep”).
