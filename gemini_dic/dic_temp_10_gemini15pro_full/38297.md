concaving:/kɒnˈkeɪvɪŋ/ /kɑːnˈkeɪvɪŋ/
adjective
凹陷的，内凹的
Curving inwards.
The lens was concaving, distorting the image.  镜头是凹的，使图像变形。
The artist skillfully rendered the concaving surface of the bowl. 艺术家巧妙地描绘了碗的凹面。
近义词: concave, hollow, indented
反义词: convex, bulging, protruding
词性: adjective
