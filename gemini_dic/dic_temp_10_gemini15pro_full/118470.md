meshing:/ˈmɛʃɪŋ/ | n. |啮合，咬合；协调，融合|The meshing of gears is essential for the smooth operation of the machine. 齿轮的啮合对于机器的平稳运行至关重要。|例句：The meshing of the two companies' cultures was a challenge. 两家公司文化的融合是一个挑战。|近义词：interlocking, engaging, integrating, harmonizing|反义词：disengaging, clashing, conflicting|词性:名词

meshing:/ˈmɛʃɪŋ/ | adj. |啮合的|The meshing gears turned smoothly. 啮合的齿轮平稳地转动。|例句：The project required a meshing of different skill sets. 这个项目需要不同技能的融合。|近义词：interlocking, engaging, integrating, harmonizing|反义词：disengaging, clashing, conflicting|词性:形容词

词源: From mesh + -ing.  Ultimately from Old English *masc "mesh" (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *maskwōn.
