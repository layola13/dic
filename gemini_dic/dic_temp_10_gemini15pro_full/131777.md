nonconnectives:/ˌnɒnkəˈnɛktɪvz/ | n. | 非连接词 | Words or phrases that do not function as connectives, meaning they don't link clauses or sentences.  They stand alone grammatically. |  Example: "Dog," "run," and "quickly" are nonconnectives.  | 近义词: isolated words, discrete terms | 反义词: connectives, conjunctions, linking words | 词性: noun
