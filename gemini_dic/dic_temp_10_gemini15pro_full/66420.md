executory:/ɪɡˈzɛkjʊtɔːri/
adjective
待执行的；履行的；生效的
A contract or agreement that is executory is one that has not yet been fully performed or completed.
例句：The executory contract stipulated the terms of the land sale.  这项待执行的合同规定了土地出售的条款。

例句：The lease agreement remained executory until both parties signed the final document.  直到双方签署最终文件，租赁协议才开始生效。

近义词：pending, unfulfilled, incomplete
反义词：executed, completed, fulfilled
词性:adjective
