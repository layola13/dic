advantaging: /ədˈvæntɪdʒɪŋ/
verb
使…处于有利地位；有利于
Giving an advantage to; benefiting.
The new trade agreement is advantaging domestic producers.  新的贸易协定有利于国内生产商。
Their wealth advantaged them greatly in the pursuit of power. 他们的财富在追求权力方面给了他们很大的优势。
近义词: benefiting, favoring, promoting, boosting
反义词: disadvantaging, hindering, harming, obstructing
词性: verb (动词)

USAGE->
现在进行时:
The current policy is advantaging large corporations. = 当前的政策有利于大公司。
He is advantaging his friend by giving him insider information. = 他通过向朋友提供内幕消息使其获利。

现在完成进行时:
They have been advantaging their own family members for years. = 他们多年来一直优待自己的家人。
She has been advantaging her career by taking on extra responsibilities. = 她一直通过承担额外的责任来提升自己的事业。

一般过去时:
The tax cuts advantaged the wealthy. = 减税政策使富人受益。
The early rain advantaged the farmers. = 早期的降雨对农民有利。

过去进行时:
The company was advantaging its shareholders at the expense of its employees. = 该公司以牺牲员工为代价，使其股东受益。
He was advantaging himself by using company resources for personal gain. = 他利用公司资源谋取私利。

过去完成时:
The changes had advantaged some groups while disadvantaging others. = 这些变化使一些群体受益，同时也使其他群体处于不利地位。
The new law had advantaged big businesses. = 新法律使大企业受益。

将来时:
The new technology will advantage smaller companies. = 新技术将有利于小型公司。
This strategy will advantage us in the long run. = 从长远来看，这个策略对我们有利。

条件句:
If the weather advantaged us, we would win the race. = 如果天气对我们有利，我们就会赢得比赛。
If the new policy advantaged small businesses, the economy would improve. = 如果新政策对小企业有利，经济将会改善。

被动语态:
