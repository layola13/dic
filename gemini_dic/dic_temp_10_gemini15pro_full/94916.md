in-house:/ˌɪnˈhaʊs/ | adj. adv.|内部的，公司内部的；在内部，在公司内部|Done or existing within an organization. | Example: We have an in-house design team. 我们有一个内部设计团队。| Example: All our printing is done in-house. 我们所有的印刷都在公司内部完成。| 近义词: internal, domestic|反义词: external, outsourced|词性:形容词/副词

in-house:/ˌɪnˈhaʊs/ | n.|内部，公司内部|The resources or expertise that a company has within its own organization. | Example: We need to develop more in-house expertise in software development. 我们需要在软件开发方面培养更多的内部专业知识。| Example: The company relies heavily on its in-house for marketing. 这家公司非常依赖其内部营销资源。| 近义词: internal department, internal resources |反义词: external agency, external resources|词性:名词
