overadvancing: /ˌoʊvərədˈvænsɪŋ/
Verb
过度推进，前进过快
advancing too far or too quickly
The army was overadvancing and risked being cut off from its supply lines.  军队推进过快，有被切断补给线的危险。
Overadvancing into enemy territory can be a fatal mistake.  过度深入敌方领土可能是一个致命的错误。
Near synonyms: overextending, overreaching
Antonyms: retreating, withdrawing
