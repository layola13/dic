cerolite:/ˈsɪərəˌlaɪt/| n. |硅镁石|A hydrous silicate of magnesium, 2MgO·3SiO2·2H2O, occurring in soft, waxlike masses, usually white or light green.|Cerolite is a secondary mineral found in serpentine rocks. 硅镁石是一种在蛇纹岩中发现的次生矿物。|The museum displayed a rare sample of cerolite. 博物馆展出了一块稀有的硅镁石样本。|Synonyms: retinalite, deweylite|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
