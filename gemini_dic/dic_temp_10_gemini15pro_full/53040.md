diogenes:/ˌdaɪˈɒdʒɪˌniːz/
n.|第欧根尼|A Greek philosopher and one of the founders of Cynic philosophy. He is known for his rejection of social conventions and his pursuit of a simple, natural life.  He famously lived in a barrel.|Diogenes believed that true happiness could be found through self-sufficiency and independence from material possessions. 第欧根尼相信真正的幸福可以通过自给自足和摆脱物质占有来获得。|
近义词:cynic, ascetic|反义词:conformist, materialist|词性:noun
