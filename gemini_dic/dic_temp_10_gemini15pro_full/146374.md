overflooded:/ˌoʊvərˈflʌdɪd/| adj.|被淹没的，泛滥的|Overwhelmed or inundated with an excessive amount; flooded to excess.  The word emphasizes the extent of the flooding, suggesting it is more than just full.|The river overflooded its banks after the heavy rain.  河水在大雨后漫过了河岸。|The market is overflooded with cheap imports. 市场充斥着廉价的进口商品。|近义词：flooded, inundated, overwhelmed, saturated|反义词:dry, arid, parched|词性:形容词

词源:
From "over-" (meaning "excessively") + "flood" (meaning "to cover or submerge with water").  The word "flood" comes from Old English "flōd," which in turn comes from Proto-Germanic "*floduz."
