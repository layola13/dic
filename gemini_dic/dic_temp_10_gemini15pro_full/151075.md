parapsychical:/ˌpærəˈsaɪkɪkl/ | adj. | 超心理学的，超感官的 | Relating to parapsychology; pertaining to alleged psychic phenomena outside the normal range of sensory experience. |  Parapsychical research often involves studying telepathy and precognition. 超心理学研究通常包括研究心灵感应和预知能力。|  She had a parapsychical experience where she saw her grandmother shortly after she passed away. 她有过一次超心理的经历，在她祖母去世后不久就看到了她。| synonyms: psychic, extrasensory, supernatural | antonyms: normal, ordinary, conventional | adjective
