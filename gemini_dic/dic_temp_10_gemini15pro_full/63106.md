enshrouding:/ɪnˈʃraʊdɪŋ/| adj. | 掩盖的，笼罩的 |  Surrounding and covering something completely, so that it cannot be seen clearly.  |  A thick fog was enshrouding the city.  浓雾笼罩着城市。| 近义词: covering, enveloping, obscuring, concealing, veiling | 反义词: revealing, exposing, uncovering | 词性:  Adjective

enshroud:/ɪnˈʃraʊd/| vt. | 掩盖，笼罩 | To cover or enclose something completely. | Mystery enshrouds the disappearance of the artifacts.  文物的消失笼罩着神秘色彩。| 近义词: cover, envelop, obscure, conceal, veil | 反义词: reveal, expose, uncover | 词性: Verb
