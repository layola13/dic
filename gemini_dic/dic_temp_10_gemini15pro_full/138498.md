novilunar:/ˌnoʊvɪˈluːnər/| adj.|新月形的；朔望月的|Relating to the new moon, or the time of the new moon; occurring at the time of the new moon.|The novilunar tide is the lowest tide of the month.  朔望月潮汐是每月中最低的潮汐。|The novilunar cycle influences the breeding patterns of certain marine animals. 新月周期影响某些海洋动物的繁殖模式。|近义词：crescent, lunar|反义词：full moon (not a direct antonym, but represents the opposite phase)|词性: adjective
Etymology: From Latin *novilunaris*, from *novus* (new) + *luna* (moon).
