daguerreotyper:/dəˈɡɛrətaɪpər/
n.
达盖尔摄影师；银版照相师
A person who takes daguerreotypes, an early type of photograph.
Louis Daguerre was not only the inventor of the daguerreotype, but also a skilled daguerreotyper himself. 路易·达盖尔不仅是达盖尔摄影法的发明者，也是一位技艺娴熟的达盖尔摄影师。
