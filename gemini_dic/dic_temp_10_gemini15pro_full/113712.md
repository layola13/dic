macro-scale:/ˈmækroʊ skeɪl/ | adj. |宏观的，大规模的 | Relating to or involving large units or elements; large-scale.  |  The macro-scale effects of climate change are being felt worldwide. 全球都感受到了气候变化的宏观影响。

Macro-scale economic policies can influence individual businesses. 宏观经济政策会影响个体企业。 | 近义词: large-scale, global, overall, broad | 反义词: micro-scale, small-scale, local | 词性: adjective

词源:  "macro-" comes from Greek "makros" meaning "long" or "large". "Scale" comes from Old Norse "skal" meaning "bowl" or "scale of a balance".  The combined meaning relates to a large or overall measure.
