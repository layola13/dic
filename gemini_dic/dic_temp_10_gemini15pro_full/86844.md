hemerocallis:/ˌhɛmərəˈkælɪs/| n. |萱草属植物，黄花菜|A genus of flowering plants in the family Asphodelaceae, commonly known as daylilies.  These plants are not true lilies (Lilium) but are popular garden perennials.|The hemerocallis bloomed brightly in the summer sun. 萱草在夏日的阳光下绽放得格外鲜艳。|synonyms: daylily|antonyms: N/A|noun
