deadlined:/ˈdɛdˌlaɪnd/| adj.|截止日期已到的，逾期的|Having a deadline that has passed; overdue. |The project is deadlined for Friday.  这个项目截止日期是星期五。| The deadlined assignment caused him stress.  逾期的作业让他很有压力。|近义词：overdue, late, expired|反义词:on-time, early|adjective

词源:  From "deadline" (a date or time by which something must be completed) + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives).  The word "deadline" originally referred to a line around a prison beyond which prisoners could be shot.
