damic:/ˈdeɪmɪk/ | adj. |  有害的，诅咒的 |  Relating to or characteristic of a dam; causing harm or destruction.  Often used figuratively to describe something as cursed or destructive.  This word is not commonly used and may be considered archaic or rare. | The damic flood destroyed the village.  这有害的洪水摧毁了村庄。 | The damic influence corrupted the land.  这诅咒的影响败坏了这片土地。 | 近义词: destructive, harmful, cursed, ruinous | 反义词: beneficial, helpful, constructive | 词性: adjective

词源: From "dam" (n.) + "-ic" (suffix forming adjectives). "Dam" ultimately comes from Proto-Germanic *dammaz.
