heroisms: /ˈhɛrəʊɪzəmz/ | n. | 英雄主义；英勇行为 | Great acts of bravery or courage, often in difficult or dangerous situations.  |  The soldier's heroisms on the battlefield earned him a medal.  这名士兵在战场上的英勇行为为他赢得了一枚勋章。 | synonyms: bravery, courage, valor, gallantry | antonyms: cowardice, fear, timidity | noun

Etymology:  From "heroism,"  from "hero" (ultimately from Greek "hērōs") + "-ism" (a suffix forming nouns of action, state, or quality).
