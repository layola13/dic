moneyist:/ˈmʌniɪst/| n.|金钱主义者；理财专家|A person who is obsessed with or places excessive importance on money, or an expert in personal finance. |例句：The moneyist advised her to diversify her investments. 理财专家建议她分散投资。

例句：He's such a moneyist, always talking about the stock market. 他真是个金钱主义者，总是谈论股市。|近义词：financier, financial advisor, accountant, economist|反义词：spendthrift, philanthropist|词性:noun
