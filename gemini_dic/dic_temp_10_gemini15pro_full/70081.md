feuding:/ˈfjuːdɪŋ/| n. adj.|不和，长期争斗，世仇| prolonged and bitter quarrel or dispute, often between families or groups, typically involving violence or other forms of aggression.|The two families have been feuding for generations. 这两个家族几代人都在争斗。|近义词：quarreling, fighting, clashing, bickering|反义词:reconciling, harmonizing|词性:noun (primarily), adjective (less common)
feuding:/ˈfjuːdɪŋ/| adj.|不和的，长期争斗的| engaged in a feud.|The feuding families finally agreed to a truce.  这两个长期争斗的家族最终同意休战。|近义词：quarrelsome, hostile, antagonistic|反义词:friendly, amicable|词性:adjective
