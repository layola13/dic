inertnesses:/ɪˈnɜːrtnəsɪz/
n.
惰性；迟钝；不活动
lack of activity or interest
The inertnesses of the noble gases make them useful in certain applications. 惰性气体的惰性使它们在某些应用中很有用。

近义词:inactivity, passivity, sluggishness, apathy, torpor, inertia
反义词:activity, liveliness, energy, vigor, dynamism

词性:名词 (plural of inertness)

词源: Late Middle English: from Old French inerte (now inertie ) or Latin iners, inert- ‘idle, unskilled’, from in- ‘not’ + ars, art- ‘skill, art’.  The ending -ness forms nouns from adjectives.
