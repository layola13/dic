crusading:/kruːˈseɪdɪŋ/
adjective
参与改革的；热衷改革的；进行斗争的
engaged in a crusade; characterized by or involved in a reform movement or campaign.
例句：Her crusading spirit led her to fight for social justice. 她那颗斗争的精神引领她为社会正义而战。

例句：The crusading journalist exposed corruption in the government.  这位充满斗志的记者揭露了政府的腐败。

近义词：campaigning, fighting, militant, reforming
反义词：apathetic, indifferent, passive

词性: adjective

词源: From crusade + -ing.  Ultimately from Latin crux, crucis "cross."  The original crusades were religious wars sanctioned by the Pope to recover the Holy Land from the Muslims.
