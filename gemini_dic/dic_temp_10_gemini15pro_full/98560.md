instinctual: /ɪnˈstɪŋktʃuəl/ (US), /ɪnˈstɪŋktʃʊəl/ (UK)| adj.|本能的；直觉的|Relating to or of the nature of instinct; prompted by instinct; arising spontaneously.|例句：Cats have an instinctual desire to hunt. 猫天生就有狩猎的欲望。|例句：Her reaction was purely instinctual. 她的反应完全是本能的。|近义词：instinctive, innate, inherent, visceral, intuitive|反义词：learned, acquired, reasoned, conscious|词性: adjective

词源: Late 19th century: from instinct + -ual.
