beachcomber:/ˈbiːtʃˌkoʊmər/ (US), /ˈbiːtʃˌkəʊmə(r)/ (UK)| n. |海滩拾荒者；海滩流浪汉|A person who walks along beaches looking for things that have been washed ashore, especially valuable or interesting objects. |例句：The beachcomber found a beautiful seashell on the shore. 海滩拾荒者在岸边发现了一个美丽的贝壳。

例句：He lived a solitary life as a beachcomber, surviving on what he could find. 他过着海滩流浪汉的孤独生活，靠捡到的东西生存。|近义词：scavenger, forager, driftwood hunter|反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "landlubber" in a loose sense)|词性: noun
