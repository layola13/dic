cerebralist:/ˈsɛrɪbrəlɪst/| n.| 理智主义者；注重理性的人|A person who emphasizes or is characterized by the use of intellect rather than intuition or instinct.|He's a cerebralist, more interested in ideas than emotions. 他是一个理智主义者，对思想比对情感更感兴趣。|近义词：intellectual, rationalist|反义词：emotionalist, sentimentalist|词性:noun
cerebral:/ˈsɛrɪbrəl/| adj.| 大脑的；理性的|Relating to the cerebrum or brain; intellectual rather than emotional or physical.|Chess is a cerebral game. 国际象棋是一种智力游戏。|近义词：intellectual, mental|反义词：emotional, instinctive|词性:adjective
