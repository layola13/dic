naturalises:/ˈnætʃrəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使归化；使入籍；移植；引进
To grant full citizenship to (a foreigner). To introduce and establish (a plant or animal) in a new region or environment so that it flourishes as if native.
The government naturalises immigrants who meet certain requirements. 政府将符合特定要求的移民归化。
They have successfully naturalised several species of trees in the park.  他们在公园里成功地移植了几种树木。
synonyms:acclimatize, accommodate, adapt, adjust, assimilate, domesticate, incorporate, integrate
antonyms:exclude, expatriate
词性:动词
