disinsure:/ˌdɪsɪnˈʃʊr/ |v.| 取消保险 | To deprive of insurance; to cause to be no longer insured. | The company decided to disinsure the building after repeated flooding.  这家公司在多次洪水后决定取消该建筑物的保险。| 近义词: cancel (insurance) | 反义词: insure | 词性: 动词

disinsure:/ˌdɪsɪnˈʃʊr/ |v.|  使不再投保 | To terminate insurance coverage on something or someone. | After assessing the risk, they opted to disinsure the high-value cargo. 经过风险评估后，他们选择取消高价值货物的保险。 |近义词: revoke (insurance), terminate (insurance) | 反义词: insure | 词性: 动词
