devon:/ˈdɛvən/| n. |德文郡；德文纪；泥盆纪|A county in southwestern England, known for its beautiful coastline and moorland. Also refers to the Devonian period in geology, a geologic period and system of the Paleozoic, spanning 60 million years from the end of the Silurian, 419.2 million years ago (Mya), to the beginning of the Carboniferous, 358.9 Mya.|例句：We spent our holiday in Devon. 我们在德文郡度假。

例句：Devon is famous for its cream teas. 德文郡以它的奶油茶而闻名。

近义词：Devonshire (a less common name for Devon)|反义词: N/A|词性: noun
