aminol:/ˈæmɪnɒl/ | n. |氨基醇|An organic compound containing both an amine group (-NH2) and an alcohol group (-OH).  An aminol is both an amine and an alcohol. | Aminols are important intermediates in the synthesis of various pharmaceuticals. 氨基醇是各种药物合成中的重要中间体。|  Ethanolamine is a simple aminol. 乙醇胺是一种简单的氨基醇。 | synonyms: aminoalcohol | antonyms: N/A | noun
