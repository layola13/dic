calmucks:/kɑːlˈmʌks/ | n. | 卡尔梅克人 |  A member of a Western Mongol people, now living chiefly in Kalmykia, a republic of Russia.  They migrated westwards from Dzungaria in the 17th century. |  The Calmucks are known for their nomadic traditions. 卡尔梅克人以其游牧传统而闻名。|  Synonyms: Kalmyks | Antonyms: N/A | noun

calmuck:/kɑːlˈmʌk/ | n. adj. | 卡尔梅克人/卡尔梅克人的 |  (n.) A member of the Kalmyk people. (adj.) Relating to the Kalmyk people or their language. |  The calmuck language is closely related to Mongolian.  卡尔梅克语与蒙古语密切相关。| Synonyms: Kalmyk | Antonyms: N/A | noun, adjective
