overanalyzed: /ˌoʊvərˈænəˌlaɪzd/ | adj. | 过度分析的 |  Having been analyzed too much, often to the point of losing clarity or meaning.  Excessively scrutinized. | She overanalyzed the situation and ended up more confused than before. 她过度分析了情况，结果比以前更困惑了。 |  overthought, dissected, scrutinized, examined excessively | underanalyzed,  ignored | adjective

overanalyse: /ˌoʊvərˈænəˌlaɪz/ | v. | 过度分析 | To analyze something excessively, often to the point of losing clarity or meaning. | He tends to overanalyse every little thing she says. 他倾向于过度分析她说的每一件小事。 | overthink, dissect, scrutinize, examine excessively | underanalyse, ignore, neglect | verb

詞源: "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "analyze" (from Greek "analyein" meaning "to loosen up, resolve")
