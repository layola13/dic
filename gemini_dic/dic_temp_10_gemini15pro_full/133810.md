nonglacially:/nɒnˈɡleɪʃəli/ | adv. | 非冰川地；非冰川作用地 |  Not related to, caused by, or occurring during a glacial period or by glaciers.  Relating to processes, formations, or deposits that are not of glacial origin. |  The valleys were formed nonglacially, primarily by river erosion. 这些山谷是非冰川作用形成的，主要是河流侵蚀造成的。| Synonyms: fluvially, interglacially | Antonyms: glacially | 词性: 副词
