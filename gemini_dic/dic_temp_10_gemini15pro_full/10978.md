arbitrationists:/ˌɑːrbɪˈtreɪʃənɪsts/
n.
仲裁员，仲裁人
A person who settles a dispute or disagreement between two parties.
The arbitrationists listened carefully to both sides of the argument before making their decision. 仲裁员在做出决定之前仔细听取了双方的论点。
The two arbitrationists were unable to reach a unanimous decision. 两位仲裁员未能达成一致意见。

近义词: arbitrator, mediator, referee, judge, umpire
反义词: litigant, disputant
词性: noun (plural)

词源:
From Late Middle English: from Old French arbitratour, arbitre, from Latin arbiter ‘judge, witness’, from ad- ‘to’ + baetere ‘to come, go’.
