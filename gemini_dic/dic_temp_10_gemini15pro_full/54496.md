disinvesting:/dɪsɪnˈvɛstɪŋ/
|v.|撤资，减少投资|The act of reducing investment, especially in an area or activity.|Many companies are disinvesting from fossil fuels. 许多公司正在从化石燃料行业撤资。|
|v.|出售，脱售|The act of selling off assets, such as stocks or property.|The government is disinvesting in some state-owned enterprises. 政府正在出售一些国有企业的资产。|
|近义词:|divesting, withdrawing, liquidating|
|反义词:|investing, funding, financing|
|词性:|Verb (Gerund or present participle of disinvest)|

disinvesting from (从...撤资):  Many organizations are disinvesting from fossil fuels. 许多组织正在从化石燃料中撤资。
disinvesting in (撤资于):  The government is disinvesting in certain sectors. 政府正在对某些部门撤资。
actively disinvesting (积极撤资):  Several firms are actively disinvesting from the region. 几家公司正积极从该地区撤资。
completely disinvesting (完全撤资):  They are considering completely disinvesting from the project. 他们正在考虑完全撤出该项目。
strategically disinvesting (战略性撤资):  The company is strategically disinvesting to focus on core businesses.  该公司正在进行战略性撤资，以专注于核心业务。
