andabatism:/ænˈdæbətɪzəm/
n.
盲目行走; 蒙眼行走
The ability to walk about in darkness without striking against objects.
He demonstrated remarkable andabatism, navigating the darkened maze with ease. 他展现了非凡的蒙眼行走能力，轻松地走过了黑暗的迷宫。
近义词: blind walking, navigating in darkness
反义词: sighted walking
