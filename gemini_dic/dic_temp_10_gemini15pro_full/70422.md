fighting:/ˈfaɪtɪŋ/| n. adj.|战斗，斗争；打架；奋斗；战斗的；好斗的|The act of physical struggle between two or more people or groups; the act of striving to achieve something; actively opposing something.|
例句：The fighting continued for days. 战斗持续了数日。
例句：They showed a fighting spirit throughout the competition.  他们在整个比赛中都表现出顽强的拼搏精神。
近义词：struggle, combat, battle, striving, contending|反义词：peace, surrender, yielding|词性:名词，形容词
