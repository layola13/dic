词源：From Middle English *craft, *craeft, from Old English cræft (“strength, skill, power, ability, art, science, talent, craft, occupation, business, trade, property, resources, riches, cunning, wisdom”), from Proto-Germanic *kraftō (“strength, power”), from Proto-Indo-European *ger- (“to gather, assemble”). Cognate with Scots craft (“skill, trade, occupation”), West Frisian kreft (“strength, power”), Dutch kracht (“strength, power, force”), German Kraft (“strength, power, force”), Danish kraft (“strength, power”), Swedish kraft (“strength, power”), Icelandic kraftur (“strength, power, force”). Related also to Old English cræftiga (“crafty, cunning”).
