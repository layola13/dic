nonpharmaceutically: /ˌnɑːnfɑːrməˈsuːtɪkli/ /ˌnɒnfɑːməˈsuːtɪkli/
adverb
非药物地
Without the use of pharmaceuticals; using methods other than drugs.
例句：
The virus can be controlled nonpharmaceutically through measures like social distancing and handwashing.  病毒可以通过社会隔离和洗手等非药物措施来控制。

The pain was managed nonpharmaceutically with acupuncture and massage.  疼痛通过针灸和按摩等非药物方式来控制。

近义词：naturally, holistically
反义词：pharmaceutically, medicinally
词性：adverb
