integrity:/ɪnˈtɛɡrəti/ | n. | 诚实正直；完整；完善 |  The quality of being honest and having strong moral principles; moral uprightness.  The state of being whole and undivided.|  She is a woman of great integrity. 她是一位非常正直的女人。|  The integrity of the building was compromised by the earthquake.  地震损害了建筑物的完整性。 | 近义词: honesty, probity, rectitude, completeness, wholeness | 反义词: dishonesty, corruption, incompleteness | 词性: 名词

词源: From Late Middle English: from Old French integrité or late Latin integritas, from integer ‘whole’ (see integer).
