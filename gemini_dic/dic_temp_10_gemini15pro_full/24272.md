brigaded:/brɪˈɡeɪdɪd/
verb|被编成旅；被集体分配|formed into a brigade; assigned to a group for a specific task|The journalists were brigaded with the peace activists for the duration of the protest. 记者们在抗议期间与和平活动家们一起被编成一队。|
例句：The engineers were brigaded to work on the urgent project.  工程师们被集体分配到紧急项目工作。

近义词：grouped, organized, assigned
反义词：separated, dispersed
词性：v.
