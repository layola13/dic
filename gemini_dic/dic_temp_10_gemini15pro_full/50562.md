deputizes:/ˈdɛpjʊˌtaɪzɪz/
verb
代理；委任…为副手；授权
acts as a deputy for another person; appoints someone as a deputy
The manager deputized his assistant to run the meeting while he was away.  经理不在的时候，授权他的助手主持会议。
Synonyms:  represent, substitute, stand in for, act for
Antonyms:  resign, abdicate
词性: 动词
