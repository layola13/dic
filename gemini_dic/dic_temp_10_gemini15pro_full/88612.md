hives:/haɪvz/| n.|荨麻疹；（非正式）焦虑，烦躁|an outbreak of swollen, pale red bumps or plaques (wheals) on the skin that appear suddenly as a result of the body's reaction to certain allergens, infections, or other causes.  Informally, it can also refer to a feeling of anxiety or nervousness.|例句：I broke out in hives after eating shellfish. 我吃了贝类后起了荨麻疹。

|例句：Waiting for the results gave me the hives. 等待结果让我焦虑不安。|近义词：rash, urticaria,  (informal) jitters, nerves|反义词:calmness, tranquility|noun
