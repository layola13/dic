artilize: /ˈɑːrtɪlaɪz/
verb
使艺术化;使艺术性增强;赋予艺术性
To give something artistic qualities or to make it more artistic.
They artilized the design of the building by adding intricate carvings. 他们在建筑设计中加入了复杂的雕刻，使其更加艺术化。
The photographer artilized the portrait with soft lighting and a blurred background. 摄影师用柔和的光线和模糊的背景使这幅肖像更加艺术化。
Synonyms: stylize, aestheticize, embellish, refine
Antonyms: simplify, coarsen, debase
