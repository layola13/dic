aftereffects:/ˈæftərɪˌfekts/| n.| 后遗症；副作用；后果；余波|The aftereffects of the earthquake were devastating. 地震的后遗症是毁灭性的。|例句：He suffered from the aftereffects of the illness for months. 他遭受了几个月的病后后遗症。

例句：The aftereffects of the storm included flooding and power outages.  暴风雨的后果包括洪水和停电。


近义词：consequences, repercussions, results, side effects|反义词:causes, origins|词性:名词
