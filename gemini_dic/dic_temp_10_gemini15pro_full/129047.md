nitride:/ˈnaɪtraɪd/| n. |氮化物|A compound of nitrogen with another more electropositive element.|Titanium nitride is often used as a coating on cutting tools. 氮化钛常被用作切削工具的涂层。|近义词: compound |反义词:  (No direct antonym for a chemical compound) |词性:noun

Etymology:
From nitr- + -ide.  "nitr-" comes from Latin "nitrum" meaning "native soda; saltpeter," from Greek "nitron," also meaning "native soda."  The suffix "-ide" indicates a binary compound.
