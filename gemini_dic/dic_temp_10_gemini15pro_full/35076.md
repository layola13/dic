closure:/ˈkloʊʒər/ (US), /ˈkləʊʒə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 关闭，结束；了结；封锁；（情感上的）解脱|An act of closing something; the condition of being closed.  The feeling that an emotional or traumatic experience has been resolved.|例句：The closure of the factory resulted in hundreds of job losses. 工厂的关闭导致数百人失业。

例句：She needed closure after the death of her mother.  在她母亲去世后，她需要情感上的解脱。|近义词：ending, completion, finish, resolution, conclusion|反义词: beginning, start, commencement, opening|词性:noun
