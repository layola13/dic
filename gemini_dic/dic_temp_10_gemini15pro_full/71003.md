fireworm:/ˈfaɪərwɜːrm/| n. |火蠕虫，火刺虫|A marine polychaete worm (Amphinomidae) with bristles that cause a burning sensation when touched.  These worms are often brightly colored.|例句：The fireworm's bristles can cause a painful sting. 火蠕虫的刚毛会引起疼痛的刺痛。

例句：Divers should be cautious of fireworms in coral reefs. 潜水员在珊瑚礁中应该小心火蠕虫。|近义词：bristle worm, fire worm |反义词: N/A |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From fire + worm.  The name refers to the burning sensation caused by contact with the worm's bristles.
