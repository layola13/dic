epinastic:/ɛpɪˈnæstɪk/
adjective
下弯的；偏上生长的
Relating to or characterized by epinasty, the downward bending of a plant part, typically a leaf or petiole, caused by more rapid growth of the upper surface.
The epinastic leaves of the plant suggested it was suffering from too much water.  这种植物向下弯曲的叶子表明它水分过多。
The herbicide caused epinastic symptoms in the treated plants. 除草剂导致处理过的植物出现下弯症状。
近义词:hyponastic (in some contexts, as both describe curvature, though in opposite directions)
反义词:hyponastic
词性:adjective

epinasty:/ɛpɪˈnæsti/
noun
下弯；偏上生长
Downward bending of a plant part, typically a leaf or petiole, caused by more rapid growth of the upper surface.
Epinasty can be a symptom of various plant diseases. 下弯可能是各种植物病害的症状。
The epinasty of the leaves was a clear sign of the plant's distress. 叶子的下弯清楚地表明了植物的困境。
近义词: (No direct synonym, but related terms include geotropism, phototropism)
反义词:hyponasty
词性:noun
