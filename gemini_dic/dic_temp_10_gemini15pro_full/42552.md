countermyth:/ˈkaʊntərˌmɪθ/ | n. | 反驳神话，反驳虚构的故事 | An argument or story intended to disprove a commonly held myth. |  Example: The book presented a compelling countermyth to the idea that all Vikings were violent barbarians. 这本书提出了一个令人信服的反驳神话，反驳了所有维京人都是暴力野蛮人的观点。 | Example:  Her research provided a powerful countermyth to the prevailing narrative. 她的研究为流行的叙事提供了一个强有力的反驳神话。 | Synonyms: debunking, refutation, rebuttal | Antonyms: myth, fabrication, legend | noun
