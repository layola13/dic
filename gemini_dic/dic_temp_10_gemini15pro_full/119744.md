midwifely: /ˈmɪdˌwaɪfli/ | adj. | 助产的；像助产士的 | Relating to or characteristic of a midwife; resembling the care or concern of a midwife. |  The midwife provided midwifely care during the birth.  助产士在分娩期间提供了助产护理。| 近义词: obstetric, maternal  | 反义词:  (No clear antonym) | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
From Middle English *midwifli, from Old English *midwiflic (“of or pertaining to a midwife”), equivalent to midwife +‎ -ly.  Cognate with Dutch mijdelijk.
