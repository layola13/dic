halfliberally:/ˌhæfˈlɪbərəli/
adjective
半自由地；半开明地
Partially or incompletely liberal; exhibiting liberal tendencies in some areas but not others.  Characterized by a mixture of liberal and conservative principles or viewpoints.
He governed halfliberally, supporting some social reforms but maintaining a fiscally conservative stance. 他执政比较半自由化，支持一些社会改革，但在财政上保持保守立场。
Near Antonyms: illiberally, undemocratically
Near Synonyms: moderately liberal, partially liberal, selectively liberal
