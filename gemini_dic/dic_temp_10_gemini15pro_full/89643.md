hookier:/ˈhʊkiər/| adj.|更吸引人的，更上瘾的，更易记住的|More appealing, more addictive, more catchy.  Relating to something that is more likely to catch someone's attention or interest, often in a way that is hard to resist.|That new song is so hookier than their last one. 那首新歌比他们上一首更吸引人。|Synonyms: catchier, more appealing, more addictive, more compelling|Antonyms: less appealing, less interesting, less catchy, dull|adjective
