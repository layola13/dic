dut:/dʌt/ | n. |  灰尘，尘土; (尤指)锯屑，煤屑；税; 关税 |  Fine dry powder consisting of tiny particles of earth or waste matter lying on the ground or on surfaces or carried in the air. Also, a tax or duty. |  The floor was covered in sawdust and dut.  地板上满是锯末和灰尘。 | synonyms: dust, dirt, grime, soot | antonyms: cleanliness, purity | noun

dut:/dʌt/ | n. |  (南非荷兰语)衣服; 服装 | Clothing; apparel. (South African Dutch) | He bought new duts for the occasion. 他为这个场合买了新衣服。(南非用法) | synonyms: clothes, clothing, garments, attire | antonyms: nudity | noun
