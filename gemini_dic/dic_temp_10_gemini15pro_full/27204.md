callous:/ˈkæləs/| adj.|冷酷无情的；麻木不仁的；硬结的，起老茧的|Showing or having an insensitive and cruel disregard for others.  Thickened and hardened.|His callous indifference to her suffering shocked everyone. 他对她痛苦的漠不关心震惊了所有人。  The labourer's hands were callous from years of hard work.  劳动者的双手因多年的辛苦工作而变得粗糙。|近义词：insensitive, unfeeling, hard, indifferent, apathetic|反义词：compassionate, sympathetic, kind, tender, soft|词性:形容词
词源: From Latin *callōsus*, from *callus* "hard skin".
