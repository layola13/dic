enterprisingly:/ˈentərˌpraɪzɪŋli/
adverb|有进取心地，积极地，大胆地|In an enterprising way; showing initiative and a willingness to undertake new projects.|She enterprisingly started her own business at the age of 22. 她22岁就积极地创办了自己的公司。|
近义词:actively, ambitiously, assertively, dynamically, energetically, industriously, proactively, resourcefully|
反义词:apathetically, idly, indifferently, passively, lazily|
词性:adverb

enterprising:/ˈentərˌpraɪzɪŋ/
adjective|有进取心的；有事业心的；有魄力的|Having or showing initiative and resourcefulness.|He was an enterprising young man, always looking for new opportunities. 他是一个有进取心的年轻人，总是寻找新的机会。|
近义词:active, ambitious, assertive, dynamic, energetic, industrious, proactive, resourceful|
反义词:apathetic, idle, indifferent, passive, lazy|
词性:adjective


词源:
From enterprise + -ing.  "Enterprise" comes from Old French "entreprise," meaning "undertaking."
