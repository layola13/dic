malty:/ˈmɔːlti/ (US) /ˈmɔːlti/ (UK)| adj.|麦芽的；像麦芽的|Resembling malt in appearance or texture.|The bread had a slightly malty aroma.  这面包有淡淡的麦芽香味。|Synonyms: grainy,cereal|Antonyms: smooth,refined|adjective
