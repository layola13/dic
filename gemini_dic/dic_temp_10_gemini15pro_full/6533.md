amygdalin: /əˈmɪɡdəlɪn/ | n. | 苦杏仁苷; 维生素B17 (非正式名称，科学界不认可) | A cyanogenic glycoside found in the kernels of certain fruits, such as apricots and bitter almonds.  It releases cyanide when metabolized. |  Amygdalin is found in apple seeds. 苦杏仁苷存在于苹果籽中。 | Amygdalin was once promoted as a cancer cure, but scientific studies have disproven this claim. 苦杏仁苷曾被宣传为一种癌症治疗方法，但科学研究已经驳斥了这一说法。 | 近义词: laetrile (非正式名称，两者化学结构相同) | 反义词: 无 | 词性: noun
