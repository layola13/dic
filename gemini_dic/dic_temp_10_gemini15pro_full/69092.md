fauteuil: /fōˈtœj/ (US), /ˈfəʊtɜɪ/ (UK)| n.|扶手椅；单人沙发|An upholstered armchair.  A comfortable chair with arms.|例句：She sank into the soft fauteuil and sighed contentedly. 她舒服地坐进柔软的扶手椅里，心满意足地叹了口气。

例句：The antique fauteuil was covered in a rich tapestry.  这张古董扶手椅覆盖着华丽的织锦。|近义词：armchair, chair, seat|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps stool or bench as less comfortable seating)|词性: Noun
