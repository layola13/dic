hyperaggressively:/ˌhaɪpərəˈɡrɛsɪvli/
| adv. |极具侵略性地，过度攻击性地|
In an excessively aggressive manner, marked by extreme or exaggerated hostility or assertiveness.|
He hyperaggressively pursued the business deal, alienating potential partners. 他极具侵略性地进行这笔商业交易，疏远了潜在的合作伙伴。

The child hyperaggressively grabbed the toy from his classmate.  这个孩子非常攻击性地从同学手里抢走了玩具。


|近义词：overly aggressively, excessively aggressively, extremely aggressively|
|反义词：passively, submissively, meekly|
|词性：副词|

Etymology:
From hyper- (prefix meaning "over, excessive") + aggressive + -ly (adverbial suffix). The root of "aggressive" comes from Latin *aggredi* "to attack," from *ad* "to" + *gradi* "to step, walk."
