hatchers:/ˈhætʃərz/| n.|孵化器; 孵化的人或物|People or devices that hatch eggs or young.  Those who work in a hatchery are also called hatchers. |The hatchers carefully monitored the temperature and humidity in the incubation room.  孵化器的工作人员仔细监测孵化室的温度和湿度。

The salmon hatchers released thousands of fry into the river. 鲑鱼孵化人员向河里放生了数千条鱼苗。|近义词：incubators, breeders|反义词: N/A|词性: noun (plural)

Etymology: From the verb "to hatch," which comes from Middle English "hatchen," from Old English "hæccan" (to hatch, produce young), of Germanic origin.
