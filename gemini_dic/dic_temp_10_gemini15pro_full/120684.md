misadjustments:/ˌmɪsəˈdʒʌstmənts/| n. |失调，错位，误调|A failure to adjust correctly or a state of being poorly adjusted.  A situation in which something is not adjusted correctly or is not working properly.|The economic misadjustments caused by the war led to widespread poverty. 战后经济失调导致普遍贫困。|近义词：maladjustments, imbalances, discrepancies|反义词: adjustments, balances, harmonies|词性:noun
