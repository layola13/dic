hafniums:/ˈhæfniəmz/| n.|铪(元素)|A chemical element with the symbol Hf and atomic number 72. It is a lustrous, silvery gray, tetravalent transition metal, chemically similar to zirconium and found in zirconium minerals.|Hafniums are used in nuclear reactors due to their high neutron capture cross-section.|Synonym: none (as it's a specific element)|Antonym: none (as it's a specific element)|noun
