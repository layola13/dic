coholder:/ˈkoʊˌhoʊldər/| n.|共同持有者，共同所有人|A coholder is a joint holder, especially of a property title or financial instrument.|John and Mary are coholders of the mortgage on their house. 约翰和玛丽是他们房子抵押贷款的共同持有者。|近义词：joint owner, joint tenant|反义词:sole owner|noun
