filarial:/fɪˈleəriəl/| adj.|丝虫的；由丝虫引起的|Relating to or caused by filariae (parasitic nematodes).|例句：Filarial infections are common in tropical regions. 丝虫感染在热带地区很常见。

例句：He suffered from a filarial disease. 他患了一种丝虫病。|近义词：nematode, parasitic|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "non-parasitic")|词性: adjective

Etymology: From Latin *fīlāria* ("thread") + *-ālis* (adjectival suffix).  Ultimately from Latin *fīlum* ("thread").
