fuze:/fjuːz/| n. v. |引信，导火索；熔断器；融合；装引信|A device used to ignite an explosive charge, such as a bomb or firework; to equip with a fuse; to melt or blend together.|例句：The bomb's fuze malfunctioned. 炸弹的引信失灵了。|近义词：fuse, detonator, igniter|反义词： (v.) separate, divide |n. |

fuze:/fjuːz/| n. v. |保险丝，熔丝；熔合；装保险丝|A safety device consisting of a strip of wire that melts and breaks an electric circuit if the current exceeds a safe level; to melt or blend together.|例句：The overloaded circuit blew a fuze. 电路过载烧断了保险丝。|近义词：fuse, circuit breaker|反义词： (v.) separate, divide |n. |
