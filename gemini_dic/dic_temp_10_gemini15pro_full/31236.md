chanteuse:/ʃɑːnˈtɜːz/ /ʃɑːnˈtuːz/
n.
女歌手
A female singer, especially one who sings popular music.
She was a famous chanteuse in the 1920s. 她是一位1920年代的著名女歌手。
The Parisian chanteuse captivated the audience with her sultry voice.  这位巴黎女歌手用她撩人的嗓音迷住了观众。

近义词:singer, vocalist, songstress
反义词:(无直接反义词，可考虑non-singer, listener)
词性:noun
