funkyest:/ˈfʌŋkiɪst/
adjective
最时髦的，最别致的，最节奏强烈的
superlative adjective of funky; having a strong rhythm and feeling, usually referring to music; stylish and unconventional.
Her apartment had the funkyest decor I'd ever seen, with vintage furniture and colorful artwork. 她公寓的装饰是我见过的最别致的，有复古家具和色彩鲜艳的艺术品。
The DJ played the funkyest tunes all night, keeping the dance floor packed.  DJ整晚都在播放最节奏强烈的曲子，让舞池挤满了人。
近义词:coolest, hippest, trendiest, grooviest
反义词:square, conventional, unfashionable, boring
词性:adjective

词源: From "funky" + "-est" (superlative suffix). "Funky" originated in the mid-20th century, likely from African American Vernacular English, related to "funk" (a strong unpleasant smell), perhaps suggesting the earthy, visceral quality of the music.
