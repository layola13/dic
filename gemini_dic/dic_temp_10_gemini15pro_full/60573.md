elastins: /ɪˈlæstɪnz/
noun
弹性蛋白
A protein forming the main constituent of elastic connective tissue, found especially in the dermis of the skin, the lungs, tendons, and blood vessel walls.
Elastins allow the skin to stretch and recoil. 弹性蛋白使皮肤能够伸展和回弹。
The loss of elastins leads to wrinkles. 弹性蛋白的流失会导致皱纹。
Synonyms: elastic fibers, elastic tissue
Antonyms: collagen (provides structure and strength, while elastin provides elasticity)
