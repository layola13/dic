half-heathen:/ˌhæf ˈhiːðən/ | adj. | 半异教的，半开化的 | Partially or imperfectly Christianized; not fully converted to Christianity.  Describing someone who blends Christian beliefs with pagan or non-Christian practices, or someone superficially Christian. | The missionaries described the remote villagers as half-heathen due to their continued adherence to traditional rituals.  传教士将偏远村庄的村民描述为半异教徒，因为他们继续坚持传统的仪式。

|  The settlers viewed the native population as half-heathen and sought to impose their own religious beliefs.  殖民者将当地居民视为半异教徒，并试图强加他们自己的宗教信仰。 | Near synonyms: semi-pagan, partially converted, nominally Christian | Antonyms: devout, pious, orthodox | adjective
