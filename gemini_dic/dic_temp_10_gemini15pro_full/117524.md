meet:/miːt/ /mit/
v. |遇见，碰见；满足；迎接；对付|To come into the presence of (someone) by chance or arrangement.  To satisfy (a demand or requirement).|We met at a conference. 我们在一个会议上相遇。|encounter, see, gather, satisfy, fulfill|miss, avoid|verb

meet:/miːt/ /mit/
n. |集会，运动会；猎狐大会|A gathering of people for a particular purpose, especially a sporting competition.|We went to the track meet. 我们去参加了田径运动会。|gathering, assembly, meeting|separation|noun

meet:/miːt/ /mit/
adj. |合适的，适宜的 (古语，罕用)|Suitable; proper.|It seemed a meet punishment for his crime. (archaic) 这似乎是对他的罪行合适的惩罚。|fitting, suitable, appropriate|inappropriate, unsuitable|adjective
