aslantwise:/əˈslæntwaɪz/
| adv. adj.|斜地，倾斜地|In an oblique or slanting direction or position. |The picture hung aslantwise on the wall.  这幅画斜挂在墙上。|近义词：slanting, sloping, oblique, diagonally|反义词:straight, level|词性:副词，形容词
Etymology: a- + slant + -wise.  "A-" is a prefix meaning "on" or "in," "slant" refers to an oblique direction, and "-wise" is a suffix indicating manner or direction. Thus, "aslantwise" literally means "in a slanting manner."
