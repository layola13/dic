bakshish: /ˈbɑːkʃiːʃ/ (UK) /ˈbɑːkʃiːʃ/ (US)| n.|小费，赏钱，贿赂|A tip, gratuity, or bribe, especially one given in countries of the Middle East and South Asia.|例句：He gave the porter a small bakshish. 他给了搬运工一小笔小费。|例句：She offered a bakshish to the official to speed up the process. 她向官员提供贿赂以加快办理速度。|近义词：tip, gratuity, bribe, reward|反义词: penalty, fine|词性: 名词

词源:
From Persian بخشش (bakhshesh), from بخشیدن (bakhshidan, “to give, to forgive”).
