outsidedness:/ˌaʊtˈsaɪdɪdnəs/| n. |外在性，局外性，外部性|The state or quality of being outside or external; external existence. The feeling or sense of being an outsider.|She felt a profound sense of outsidedness, like she didn't belong.|Near synonyms: exteriority, externality, otherness, alienation|Antonyms: belonging, inclusion, insiderness|noun
