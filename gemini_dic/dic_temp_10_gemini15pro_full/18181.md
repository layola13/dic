belamour: /ˈbɛləˌmʊr/ | n. | 爱人，情人，甜蜜的人| A beloved person; a sweetheart. | She was his belamour, his greatest love. 她曾是他的爱人，他最爱的人。| He called her his belamour, a term of endearment from a bygone era. 他称她为他的爱人，一个来自过去时代的亲昵的称呼。 | 近义词：sweetheart, lover, darling, beloved | 反义词: enemy, foe | 词性: noun

词源: From Middle French *bel amour*, meaning "beautiful love."  The word is ultimately derived from the Latin words *bellus* ("beautiful") and *amor* ("love").
