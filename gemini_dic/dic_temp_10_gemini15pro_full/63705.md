ephemerally: /ɪˈfɛmərəli/ | adv. | 短暂地，转瞬即逝地 | In a way that lasts for a very short time; transiently. | The snowflakes fell ephemerally, melting as soon as they touched the ground. 雪花飘落得很短暂，一碰到地面就融化了。|  The beauty of the fireworks display was ephemeral, lasting only a few minutes.  烟花的美丽转瞬即逝，只持续了几分钟。 | 近义词: briefly, fleetingly, momentarily, transiently | 反义词: permanently, enduringly, lastingly | 词性: 副词

ephemeral: /ɪˈfɛmərəl/ | adj. | 短暂的，转瞬即逝的 | Lasting for a very short time. |  The joy of childhood is often ephemeral. 童年的快乐往往是短暂的。 |  Ephemeral art, like sandcastles, is beautiful but short-lived.  像沙堡一样的短暂艺术，美丽却短暂。| 近义词: fleeting, transient, momentary, short-lived | 反义词: permanent, enduring, lasting, perpetual | 词性: 形容词

词源: From Greek *ephēmeros* "lasting only a day," from *epi* "on, for" + *hēmera* "day."
