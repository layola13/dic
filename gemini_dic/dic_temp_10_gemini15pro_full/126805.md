naon:/ˈnaʊn/| pronoun, determiner|什么，哪一个（用于疑问句）|Used to ask questions about the identity or nature of something.|What is your name? 你叫什么名字？| synonym: which| antonym: none |pronoun, determiner

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she like to do in her free time? 她空闲时间喜欢做什么？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？


现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned from this experience? 你从这次经历中学到了什么？
What has she done to help the situation? 她做了什么来帮助解决这种情况？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on recently? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? 他最近一直在读什么？


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you say to him? 你对他说了什么？
What happened last night? 昨晚发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? 会议期间她在想什么？


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? 你离开之前做了什么？
What had they discussed before making the decision? 他们在做决定之前讨论了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I arrived? 我到达之前你一直在做什么？
What had she been studying before she changed her major? 她转专业之前一直在学什么？


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? 你毕业后要做什么？
What will happen next? 接下来会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? 他们在会议上会讨论什么？


将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by the time we get back? 等我们回来的时候会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by then? 到那时你将已经做这件事五年了？
What will she have been studying by the time she graduates? 到她毕业时她将已经学习什么多久了？



虚拟语气:
What would you do if you won the lottery? 如果你中了彩票你会做什么？
If I knew what the answer was, I would tell you. 如果我知道答案是什么，我会告诉你的。


被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being done to address the problem? 正在采取什么措施来解决这个问题？



疑问句:
What is your favorite color? 你最喜欢的颜色是什么？
What are you planning to do for your birthday? 你打算怎么过生日？



否定句:
I don't know what to expect. 我不知道该期待什么。
She didn't tell me what she was thinking. 她没有告诉我她在想什么。
