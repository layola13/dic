ghostiest:/ˈɡoʊstiɪst/| adj.|最像鬼的；最阴森的|Most resembling a ghost; most eerie and spectral.|例句：The ghostiest room in the house was the attic. 房子里最像鬼屋的地方是阁楼。

例句：It was the ghostiest silence I had ever experienced. 这是我经历过的最阴森的寂静。

近义词：spectral, eerie, spooky, haunted, unearthly|反义词：cheerful, bright, lively, earthly|词性：形容词

词源：ghost (n.) + -y (adj. suffix) + -est (superlative suffix)  From Old English "gast" (spirit, ghost).  The "-y" suffix creates the adjective "ghostly," and the "-est" suffix indicates the superlative form, meaning "most ghostly."
