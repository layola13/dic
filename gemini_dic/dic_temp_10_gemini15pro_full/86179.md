heartwood:/ˈhɑːrtˌwʊd/| n.|心材|The dense inner part of a tree trunk, yielding the hardest timber.|The craftsman preferred using heartwood for his intricate carvings because of its durability.  工匠喜欢用心材进行复杂的雕刻，因为它很耐用。|Synonyms: duramen, corewood|Antonyms: sapwood|noun

heartwood:/ˈhɑːrtˌwʊd/| n.|心材|The central, non-living, and usually darker-colored wood of a tree; it is stronger and more resistant to decay than sapwood.|The heartwood of the redwood tree is highly valued for its resistance to rot. 红杉树的心材因其抗腐性而备受重视。|Synonyms: duramen|Antonyms: sapwood|noun
