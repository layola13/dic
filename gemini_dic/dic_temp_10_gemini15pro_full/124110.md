mortality:/mɔːrˈtælɪti/ /mɔrˈtælɪti/| n.|死亡率，必死性|the state of being subject to death; the number of deaths in a given time or place|The mortality rate from lung cancer is increasing. 肺癌的死亡率正在上升。|Synonyms: death rate, fatality rate|Antonyms: immortality, survival|noun

mortality:/mɔːrˈtælɪti/ /mɔrˈtælɪti/| n.|死亡人数，（尤指战乱、灾难、疾病造成的）死亡人数|the number of deaths resulting from a particular cause, such as a disease or a natural disaster|The mortality from the earthquake was staggering. 地震造成的死亡人数令人震惊。|Synonyms: death toll, fatalities|Antonyms: survivors, living|noun
