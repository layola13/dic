coffee-table:/ˈkɒfi teɪbl/ (UK), /ˈkɔːfi ˌteɪbl/ (US)| n. |咖啡桌|A low table, typically placed in front of a sofa, used to place drinks, books, magazines, etc.|We put the vase of flowers on the coffee-table. 我们把花瓶放在咖啡桌上。|Synonyms: cocktail table, occasional table|Antonyms: dining table|noun


coffee-table:/ˈkɒfi teɪbl/ (UK), /ˈkɔːfi ˌteɪbl/ (US)| n. |咖啡桌式的;适合茶余饭后阅读的|Relating to or suitable for casual reading or conversation, often implying superficiality or lack of depth.|He wrote a coffee-table book about his travels. 他写了一本关于他旅行的咖啡桌式画册。|Synonyms: lightweight, superficial|Antonyms: serious, in-depth|adjective
