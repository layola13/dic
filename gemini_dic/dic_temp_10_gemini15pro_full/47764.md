decamped:/dɪˈkæmpt/
verb
突然离开；撤离；逃走
departed suddenly or secretly, especially to relocate one's business or household
The circus decamped in the night. 马戏团连夜离开了。
synonyms:  abscond, flee, depart, leave, retreat, withdraw, make off, run away, bolt, clear out, take flight
antonyms: arrive, stay, remain
词性: 动词
