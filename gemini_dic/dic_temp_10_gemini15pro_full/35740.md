cochairs:/ˈkoʊtʃɛrz/ | n. | 共同主席，联合主席 | Two individuals sharing the responsibility and authority of a chairperson.  |  Example: The cochairs of the committee worked effectively together. 委员会的两位共同主席有效地合作。 | Example:  The cochairs will lead the discussion at the next meeting. 两位共同主席将在下次会议上领导讨论。 | Synonyms: co-chairs, joint chairs, co-leaders | Antonyms: subordinate, follower | noun

cochair:/ˈkoʊtʃɛr/ | n. | 共同主席，联合主席 | One of two or more individuals sharing the responsibility and authority of a chairperson. | Example:  Each cochair brought a unique perspective to the project. 每位共同主席都为这个项目带来了独特的视角。 | Example: The cochair announced the start of the meeting. 共同主席宣布会议开始。 | Synonyms: co-leader, joint chair | Antonyms: subordinate, follower | noun

cochair: /ˈkoʊtʃɛr/ | v. | 共同主持，联合主持 | To preside over a meeting or committee jointly with another person. | Example: They will cochair the fundraising event.  他们将共同主持这次筹款活动。| Example: We cochaired the conference last year.  去年我们共同主持了这次会议。 | Synonyms: co-lead, jointly preside | Antonyms: follow, be led | verb
