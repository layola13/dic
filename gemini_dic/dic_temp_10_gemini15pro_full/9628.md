antistimulants:/ˌæntiˈstɪmjʊlənts/| n.|抗兴奋剂，抑制剂|Substances that reduce stimulation of the central nervous system.|Doctors sometimes prescribe antistimulants to children with ADHD to help them focus. 医生有时会给患有多动症的儿童开抗兴奋剂，以帮助他们集中注意力。|The patient was given antistimulants to counteract the effects of the previous medication.  病人服用了抗兴奋剂来抵消之前药物的作用。|近义词：depressants, sedatives|反义词: stimulants, excitants|词性:noun


Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposing  (from Greek anti-)
stimulant (noun): something that stimulates or excites (from Latin stimulans, present participle of stimulare "to prick, urge, incite")
