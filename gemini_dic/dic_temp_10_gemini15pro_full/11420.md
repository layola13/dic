argillaceous:/ˌɑːrdʒɪˈleɪʃəs/
adjective
泥质的，含黏土的
Containing clay; consisting of or resembling clay.
The cliff face was composed of argillaceous rocks.  悬崖表面由泥质岩石构成。
The soil in this area is heavy and argillaceous.  这个地区的土壤很重，而且是粘土质的。
clayey, loamy
sandy, gravelly
