loup:/luː/| n.|狼 (尤指雄狼); 骗子, 恶棍|A wolf, especially a male wolf; a swindler, a scoundrel.|The old loup stalked its prey. 老狼跟踪着它的猎物。|近义词:wolf, cheat, rogue|反义词:hero|词性:名词
