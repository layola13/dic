dishlike:/ˈdɪʃˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 不喜欢的；讨厌的 | Resembling a dish, especially in being shallow and concave.  Also, used figuratively to describe something unpleasant or undesirable. | The stagnant water in the bird bath had become dishlike and murky.  鸟盆里停滞的水变得像盘子一样，又浑浊又难看。| synonyms:  unpleasant, disagreeable, undesirable, distasteful, unappetizing, repellent | antonyms: pleasant, agreeable, desirable, appealing, appetizing, attractive | adjective
