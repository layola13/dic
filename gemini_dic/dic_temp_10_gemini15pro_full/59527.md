eat high off the hog:/iːt haɪ ɔːf ðə hɒɡ/ /iːt haɪ ɔːf ðə hɑːɡ/ | idiom | 奢侈地生活；挥霍无度；过着养尊处优的生活 | To live luxuriously or extravagantly, enjoying the best of everything.  This idiom refers to eating the best cuts of pork, which are higher up on the hog. | They've been eating high off the hog ever since they won the lottery.  他们自从中了彩票就一直过着奢侈的生活。| synonyms: live it up, live in the lap of luxury,  be in clover | antonyms: live frugally, scrimp and save, tighten one's belt | idiom
