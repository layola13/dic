friction:/ˈfrɪkʃən/| n.|摩擦，摩擦力；冲突，不和|The friction between the two surfaces creates heat. 两个表面之间的摩擦产生热量。|例句：There is a lot of friction between the two departments. 这两个部门之间有很多摩擦。

例句：Friction between moving parts caused the machine to overheat.  运动部件之间的摩擦导致机器过热。

近义词：abrasion, attrition, conflict, disagreement, discord, resistance|反义词：harmony, agreement, accord|词性:名词
