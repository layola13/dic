fasteners:/ˈfæsnərz/| n.|紧固件，扣件|A fastener is a hardware device that mechanically joins or affixes two or more objects together.|The toolbox contained a variety of fasteners, including screws, nuts, and bolts. 工具箱里装有各种各样的紧固件，包括螺丝、螺母和螺栓。|synonyms: fixings, attachments, connectors|antonyms: looseners, detachers|noun
