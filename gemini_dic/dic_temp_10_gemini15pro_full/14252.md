autodidacts: /ˌɔːtəˈdaɪdækts/ | n. | 自学者 | A person who teaches themselves a subject or skill without formal instruction. |  He was a brilliant autodidact, mastering several languages on his own. 他是一位才华横溢的自学者，自学了多门外语。

Lincoln was largely an autodidact. 林肯很大程度上是一位自学者。


近义词: self-learner, self-taught person | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

词源: From Greek autos 'self' + didaktos 'taught'.
