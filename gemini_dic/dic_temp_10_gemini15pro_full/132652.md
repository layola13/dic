nondiscursiveness:/ˌnɒndɪsˈkɜːsɪvnəs/
|n.|非散漫性；简洁性；直接性
|The quality of being concise and not rambling or digressive.|
|Her nondiscursiveness was appreciated in the meeting, as everyone was short on time. 她的简洁性在会议上受到赞赏，因为每个人都时间紧迫。|
|The report was notable for its nondiscursiveness and clarity.  这份报告以其简洁性和清晰性而著称。|
|conciseness, brevity, directness|
|discursiveness, prolixity, rambling|
|noun|
