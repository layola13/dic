mean-well:/ˌmiːn ˈwɛl/ | adj. | 善意的，好心的 | Having good intentions, but often ineffectual or clumsy. |  例句：He's a mean-well guy, but he always seems to mess things up. 他是个好心人，但他似乎总是把事情搞砸。

例句：She meant well by offering advice, but it wasn't what I needed to hear. 她提供建议是好意，但这并不是我需要听的。


近义词：well-meaning, well-intentioned, benevolent, kind-hearted | 反义词：malicious, malevolent, ill-intentioned | 词性：adjective
