close-down:/ˈkloʊzdaʊn/ (US) /ˈkləʊzdaʊn/ (UK)| n.| 关闭，倒闭|The factory faced a permanent close-down due to lack of funds.  这家工厂由于缺乏资金面临永久关闭。|近义词：closure, shutdown|反义词: opening, start-up|词性:名词

close down:/kloʊz daʊn/ (US) /kləʊz daʊn/ (UK)| v.| 关闭，停止营业|The restaurant had to close down after the fire.  这家餐馆在火灾后不得不关门。|近义词：shut down, cease trading|反义词: open, start up|词性:动词


词源: From the phrasal verb "close down," combining "close" (from Old French "clos," past participle of "clore," from Latin "claudere" - to shut) and "down" (Old English "dūn" - downwards, from hill).
