double-booked: /ˌdʌbəlˈbʊkt/ | adj. | 双重预订的；重复预订的 |  Reserved twice for the same period; overbooked.  Making two appointments for the same person or resource at the same time. | The restaurant was double-booked and there wasn't a table for us.  这家餐厅被重复预订了，没有我们的位子。|  overbooked, reserved twice | available, unbooked | adjective

double-booked: /ˌdʌbəlˈbʊkt/ | v. | 双重预定；重复预定 | The act of booking something twice for the same time slot. | I accidentally double-booked myself for two meetings this afternoon. 我不小心把今天下午的两个会议重复预定了。 | overbook, schedule twice | cancel, free up | verb
