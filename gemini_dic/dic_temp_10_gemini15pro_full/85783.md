hayrack:/ˈheɪræk/| n. |干草架，草料棚|A frame for holding hay or straw, typically used for feeding livestock.|The farmer loaded the hayrack with fresh hay. 农民把新鲜的干草装到干草架上。|例句：The old hayrack leaned precariously against the barn.  旧的干草架摇摇欲坠地靠在谷仓上。

例句：The children played hide-and-seek in the hayrack. 孩子们在干草堆里玩捉迷藏。|近义词：hayloft, manger|反义词: (No direct antonym)|词性:noun
