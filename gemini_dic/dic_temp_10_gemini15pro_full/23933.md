breakwaters:/ˈbreɪkwɔːtərz/ (US), /ˈbreɪkwɔːtəz/ (UK)| n. |防波堤；防浪堤|A structure built in the sea to protect a harbor or beach from the force of waves.|例句：The breakwaters protected the harbor from the storm. 防波堤保护了港口免受风暴的袭击。

例句：The breakwaters were constructed to reduce coastal erosion.  这些防波堤是为了减少海岸侵蚀而建造的。|近义词：sea wall, jetty, mole, groin|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "open sea")|词性:noun
