overabuses:/ˌoʊvərəˈbjuːzɪz/| n.|过度滥用；滥用行为（通常用复数形式）|The report documented the overabuses of power by the government. 这份报告记录了政府滥用权力的行为。|近义词：misuses, exploitations|反义词: proper uses, responsible applications|词性:名词

词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessive") + "abuse" (from Old French "abuser," meaning "to misuse").
