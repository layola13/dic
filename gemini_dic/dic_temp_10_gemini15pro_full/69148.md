favourite:/ˈfeɪvərɪt/ (US) /ˈfeɪvərɪt/ (UK)| adj. n.|最喜欢的；特别喜爱的人（或事物）|Something or someone that is preferred above all others.|My favourite colour is blue. 我最喜欢的颜色是蓝色.|She's my favourite teacher. 她是我最喜欢的老师.|近义词：preferred, beloved, dearest|反义词：least favourite, disliked|词性:形容词/名词


Etymology->
From Old French *favorit, past participle of favoriser (“to favour”), from Latin favor (“favour”).  The spelling with -ou- is a later respelling influenced by the noun favour.
