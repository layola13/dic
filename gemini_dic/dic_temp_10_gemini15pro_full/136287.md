nonreprehensibly:/ˌnɒnˌrɛprɪˈhɛnsɪbli/
adverb
无可非议地，无懈可击地
in a way that is not reprehensible; blamelessly.
His conduct throughout the entire ordeal was nonreprehensibly noble. 他在整个考验中的行为无可非议地高尚。
近义词:blamelessly, impeccably, irreproachably, unimpeachably
反义词:reprehensibly, culpably, blameworthily
词性:adverb

Etymology:
From non- + reprehensible + -ly.  Reprehensible comes from Late Latin reprehensībilis, from reprehendere "to blame, censure," from re- "back" + prehendere "to grasp, seize."
