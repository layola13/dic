gangwayed:/ˈɡæŋweɪd/
| adj. | 装有舷梯的；用舷梯连接的 | Having a gangway or gangways; connected by a gangway. | The gangwayed ships were moored side by side.  这些装有舷梯的船并排停泊着。 | The two vessels were gangwayed together.  这两艘船用舷梯连接在一起。 |  connected, linked | separate, detached | adjective

词源: From "gangway" (n.) + "-ed" (suffix forming adjectives). "Gangway" itself comes from Old Norse "gangvegr" (walking way).
