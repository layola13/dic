confutable:/kənˈfjuːtəbl/
adjective
可驳倒的，可反驳的
Able to be proven wrong or argued against successfully.
The lawyer presented a confutable argument, easily dismantled by the opposition. 律师提出了一个可反驳的论点，很容易被对方推翻。
Synonyms: disputable, refutable, debatable, challengeable
Antonyms: irrefutable, indisputable, undeniable, incontestable
词性: 形容词
