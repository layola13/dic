billionths:/ˈbɪljənθs/
noun
十亿分之一
One part in a billion; 10⁻⁹.
The concentration of the pollutant was measured in billionths. 这种污染物的浓度以十亿分之一来衡量。
近义词: parts per billion (ppb)
反义词: N/A
词性: n. (noun)


billionths:/ˈbɪljənθs/
adjective
十亿分之一的
Representing one part in a billion.
Billionths of a second are crucial in high-speed computing. 在高速计算中，十亿分之一秒至关重要。

ETYMOLOGY->
From billion + -th (ordinal suffix) + -s (plural suffix). Billion itself comes from French billion, ultimately from bi- (two) + million + -on (augmentative suffix).
