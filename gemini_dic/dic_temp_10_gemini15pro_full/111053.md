list-prices:/ˈlɪst praɪsɪz/| n.|标价，目录价格|The list price of the car is $30,000, but you can probably negotiate a lower price. 这辆车的标价是3万美元，但你或许可以商量一个更低的价格。|List prices are often higher than the actual selling price. 标价通常高于实际售价。|近义词：sticker price, retail price, asking price|反义词：discounted price, sale price|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
"list" comes from Old French "liste" meaning "border, band, strip of cloth," later "catalog, inventory."
"price" comes from Old French "pris" meaning "price, value, reward."
