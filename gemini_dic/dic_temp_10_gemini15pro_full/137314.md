nontactility:/ˌnɒntækˈtɪləti/
n.
非触觉性；无触觉
lack of the sense of touch; inability to perceive tactile stimuli.
The nontactility of virtual reality can be a limiting factor. 虚拟现实的非触觉性可能是一个限制因素。
近义词:intangibility, impalpability
反义词:tactility, tangibility
词性:名词

nontactility:/ˌnɒntækˈtɪləti/
adj.
非触觉的
not perceptible by touch.
The ghost was described as a nontactile presence. 这个鬼魂被描述为一种非触觉的存在。
近义词:intangible, impalpable
反义词:tactile, tangible
词性:形容词
