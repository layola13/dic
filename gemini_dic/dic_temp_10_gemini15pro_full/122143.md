mistress:/ˈmɪstrɪs/ (US), /ˈmɪstrəs/ (UK)| n.|女主人，情妇，(尤指已婚男子的)情人，女教师，女艺术家，女能手|A woman who is in a position of authority or control, a woman having an extramarital sexual relationship with a man (especially a married man), a female teacher or artist, or a woman skilled in something.|例句：She is the mistress of the house. 她是这栋房子的女主人。

例句：He left his wife for his mistress. 他为了他的情妇离开了妻子。

近义词：lover, paramour, concubine, girlfriend (for mistress in the sense of 'lover'), owner, lady, madam (for other senses)|反义词：servant, employee, master (in some contexts)|词性:名词
