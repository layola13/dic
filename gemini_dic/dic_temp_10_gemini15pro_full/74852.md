frequented:/ˈfriːkwentɪd/| adj.|常去的，时常光顾的|Past participle of "frequent" used as an adjective, meaning visited or inhabited often.|The bar was frequented by locals and tourists alike. 这家酒吧是当地人和游客常去的地方。|The park is a frequented spot for picnics. 这个公园是人们常去野餐的地方。|近义词：popular, common, habitual, visited, patronized|反义词：unpopular, deserted, unvisited|词性:adjective

Etymology:
From Middle French *frequenter*, from Latin frequentāre (“to visit often, resort to, haunt, crowd, throng”), from frequēns (“crowded, numerous, often”).
