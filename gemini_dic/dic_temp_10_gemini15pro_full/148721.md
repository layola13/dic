overtrained:/ˌoʊvərˈtreɪnd/ | adj. | 过度训练的 | Having trained too much, resulting in reduced performance and other negative effects.  | Example: The athlete was overtrained and had to take a break from competition. 运动员过度训练，不得不暂停比赛。 | Example:  She felt overtrained and exhausted after months of intense workouts. 几个月的高强度训练后，她感到过度训练和疲惫。 | Near synonyms: overworked, exhausted, burnt out | Antonyms: undertrained, rested, fresh | word property: adjective

overtrain:/ˌoʊvərˈtreɪn/ | v. | 过度训练 | To train too much, resulting in reduced performance and other negative effects. | Example: It's easy to overtrain if you don't listen to your body. 如果你不听从你的身体，很容易过度训练。 | Example:  He overtrained for the marathon and injured himself. 他为马拉松过度训练，结果受伤了。 | Near synonyms: overwork, exhaust | Antonyms: undertrain, rest | word property: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + trained, past participle of train.  The prefix "over-" indicates an excess or too much.
