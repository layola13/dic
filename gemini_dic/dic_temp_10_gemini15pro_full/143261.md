outdevil:/ˌaʊtˈdɛvəl/
verb
胜过魔鬼; 超越魔鬼般的邪恶
to surpass in devilry; to be more devilish than
例句：His cruelty outdevilled anything I could have imagined. 他的残忍超出了我的想象。
例句：The dictator's actions outdevilled even the most wicked tyrants in history.  这位独裁者的行为甚至比历史上最邪恶的暴君还要残暴。
近义词：outdo, surpass, exceed
反义词：(No direct antonym, consider "be angelic," "act virtuously")
词性:v.

outdevil:/ˌaʊtˈdɛvəl/
noun (rare usage)
极其邪恶的人或事
something or someone extremely wicked
例句: The outdevil of war brought unimaginable suffering. 战争带来的极度邪恶造成了难以想象的苦难。
例句：He was considered an outdevil by his community due to his cruel acts. 由于他的残忍行径，他被社区视为恶魔。
近义词：monster, fiend, devil
反义词：(No direct antonym, consider "angel," "saint")
词性:n.
