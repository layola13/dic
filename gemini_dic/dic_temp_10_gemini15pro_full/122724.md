mohr:/mɔːr/ | n. | 莫尔(硬度单位) | A unit of hardness, especially of minerals, equal to 7 on the Mohs scale. |  Mohs hardness scale is used to determine the relative hardness of minerals. 莫氏硬度标用于确定矿物的相对硬度。| synonym: hardness unit | antonym: softness unit | noun
