disclaimant:/dɪsˈkleɪmənt/ | n. |放弃者；拒绝承认者；否认者| A person who disclaims or renounces a legal claim, right, or title. | The disclaimant relinquished all rights to the property. 放弃者放弃了所有财产的权利。| The disclaimant stated that he had no involvement in the crime. 否认者声称他没有参与犯罪。 | Near Synonyms: renouncer, repudiator, abjurer | Antonyms: claimant, assertor | Noun
