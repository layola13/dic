curatorial:/kjʊərəˈtɔːriəl/
adjective
策展的；博物馆馆长的
Relating to the selection and organization of exhibits in a museum or gallery.
The museum's curatorial staff is highly respected.  博物馆的策展人员备受尊敬。
The curatorial decisions were controversial.  策展决定备受争议。
Synonyms:  museum, gallery, exhibition, archival
Antonyms:  unorganized, chaotic
词性: adjective
