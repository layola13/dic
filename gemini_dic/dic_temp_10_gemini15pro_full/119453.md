micromanagement:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈmænɪdʒmənt/ | n. | 微观管理；事无巨细的管理 | The act of managing or controlling with excessive attention to minor details.  It often implies a lack of trust and can demotivate employees. |  Micromanagement stifled creativity in the department.  部门里的微观管理扼杀了创造力。|  Micromanagement can lead to employee burnout. 微观管理会导致员工倦怠。 | close supervision, excessive control |  delegation, empowerment | noun

micromanagements:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈmænɪdʒmənts/ | n. |  微观管理（复数形式，不常用）|  Multiple instances of micromanagement.  It refers to several occasions or situations where micromanagement is practiced. |  The report cited numerous micromanagements as a key factor in the project's failure.  报告指出，该项目失败的一个关键因素是大量的微观管理。 | The various micromanagements throughout the company created a culture of distrust.  整个公司的各种微观管理造成了不信任的文化。 | close supervisions, excessive controls | delegations, empowerments | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
"micro-" + "management"
"micro-" : from Greek "mikrós" meaning "small"
"management": from Old French "ménagement" meaning "the act of managing"
