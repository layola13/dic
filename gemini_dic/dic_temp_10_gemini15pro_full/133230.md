nonexclamatory:/ˌnɒnɪksˈklæmətəri/ | adj. | 非感叹的；平静的；不带感叹号的 | Not expressing strong emotion or excitement; not using exclamation marks. |  The chairman delivered a nonexclamatory statement about the company's earnings. 主席就公司的收益发表了一份平静的声明。| The email was nonexclamatory and professional. 这封电子邮件语气平静，很专业。 | 近义词: unemotional, calm, impassive, restrained | 反义词: exclamatory, excited, passionate | 词性: adjective

词源:  Formed from the prefix "non-" (meaning "not") + "exclamatory" (derived from "exclamation," ultimately from Latin "exclamare" – "to cry out").
