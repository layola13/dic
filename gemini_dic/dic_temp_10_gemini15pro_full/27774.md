cannibalising:/ˈkænɪbəlaɪzɪŋ/ | v. | 蚕食，内部竞争 |  The act of reducing sales of one product by introducing a new product that competes with it within the same company. | The company's new phone is cannibalising sales of its older model.  这家公司的新手机正在蚕食其旧型号的销售。 | synonyms: undermining, eroding, eating into | antonyms: boosting, supporting, enhancing | verb

cannibalise:/ˈkænɪbəlaɪz/ | v. | 蚕食，内部竞争 |  To take parts from one machine or product to repair or build another. Also, to reduce sales of one product by introducing a new product that competes with it within the same company. |  He cannibalised parts from an old bicycle to build a new one. 他从一辆旧自行车上拆下零件来组装一辆新自行车。 | synonyms:  strip, salvage, reuse, undermine, eroding, eating into | antonyms:  boosting, supporting, enhancing | verb

cannibalisation:/ˌkænɪbəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ | n. | 蚕食，内部竞争| The act or process of cannibalising something. | The cannibalisation of the company's existing products by the new range is a serious concern. 新产品系列对公司现有产品的蚕食是一个严重的问题。| synonyms:  undermining, erosion, encroachment | antonyms:  boostment, support, enhancement | noun
