cackle: /ˈkækəl/ (US) /ˈkækəl/ (UK)| n. v. |咯咯笑；咯咯叫声|A sharp, broken sound like that of a hen, often associated with gleeful or malicious laughter. |The witch cackled gleefully as she stirred her potion. 女巫一边搅拌着她的药水，一边高兴地咯咯笑着。|Synonyms: chuckle, chortle, giggle, guffaw|Antonyms: whimper, sob, moan|词性:名词/动词

cackle: /ˈkækəl/ (US) /ˈkækəl/ (UK)| n. v. |咯咯笑；咯咯叫声|The act of emitting a cackle. |She let out a cackle when she heard the joke. 她听到笑话后发出了一阵咯咯的笑声。|Synonyms: chuckle, chortle, giggle, guffaw|Antonyms: whimper, sob, moan|词性:名词/动词
