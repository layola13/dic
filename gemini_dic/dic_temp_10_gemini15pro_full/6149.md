amicably:/ˈæmɪkəbli/
adverb
友好地；和睦地
In a friendly and peaceful manner; without disagreement or rancor.
The two parties settled their differences amicably.  双方友好地解决了分歧。
They divorced amicably. 他们友好地离婚了。
近义词:peacefully, harmoniously, agreeably, cordially
反义词:hostilely, unpleasantly, disagreeably
词性:adverb

词源:
From Late Latin amicabilis (“friendly”), from Latin amicus (“friend”) + -abilis (suffix forming adjectives meaning "capable of being," "worthy of being," "tending to," "liable to," "apt to").
