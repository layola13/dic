overpresumptuousness:/ˌoʊvərprɪˈzʌmpʧuəsnəs/| n.| 过于自信；傲慢；放肆|The state of being excessively presumptuous; characterized by an unwarranted assumption of importance or privilege.  It suggests an overstepping of appropriate boundaries and a lack of humility.| His overpresumptuousness alienated his colleagues, who found his constant boasting unbearable. 他的傲慢疏远了他的同事，他们觉得他不断的吹嘘令人难以忍受。| 近义词：arrogance, audacity, conceit, presumption, hubris | 反义词：humility, modesty, diffidence| noun
