giving:/ˈɡɪvɪŋ/| n. adj.|给予，赠送；捐赠；有弹性的|The act of bestowing something.  The quality of being yielding or flexible.|例句：Giving is better than receiving.  给予胜于接受。|近义词：donating, bestowing, contributing, yielding, flexible|反义词:receiving, taking, withholding, rigid, inflexible|词性:名词/形容词


Etymology->
From Old English *giefan (attested only in the past participle giefen), from Proto-Germanic *gebaną (source also of Old Frisian jeva, Old Saxon geban, Old High German geban, Old Norse gefa, Gothic ), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵʰebʰ- (“to give, take, hold”).
