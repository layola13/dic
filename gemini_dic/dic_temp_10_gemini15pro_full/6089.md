americanism:/əˈmɛrɪkənɪzəm/
n.|美国主义，美国英语，美国特色|A word or phrase that is characteristic of American English or American culture. | The use of "elevator" instead of "lift" is an americanism.  用“elevator”而不是“lift”是美国英语的用法。 |Example: "Fall" for "autumn" is a common Americanism.| synonyms: American way, American characteristic | antonyms: Britishism | noun


americanism:/əˈmɛrɪkənɪzəm/
n.|美国精神，美国国民性|The spirit, traits, or character regarded as typical of the United States or its citizens.| Patriotism and a strong work ethic are often seen as Americanisms.| Example:  Her belief in individual liberty is a core Americanism. 她对个人自由的信仰是美国精神的核心。 | synonyms: American spirit, American ideal | antonyms: un-American | noun
