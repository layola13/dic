afrikanerization:/æˌfrɪkɑːnərɪˈzeɪʃən/ |n.| 南非荷兰人化；布尔人化|The process of imposing Afrikaner language, culture, and political dominance, especially during the apartheid era in South Africa.  指在南非，尤其是在种族隔离时期，强制推行南非荷兰语、文化和政治统治的过程。|The afrikanerization of the civil service led to the exclusion of many black South Africans. 公务员队伍的南非荷兰人化导致许多南非黑人被排除在外。| 近义词:Boerization | 反义词: Africanization, decolonization |词性: 名词
