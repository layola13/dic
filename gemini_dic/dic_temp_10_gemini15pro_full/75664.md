fulcrum:/ˈfʌlkrəm/ /ˈfʊlkrəm/ | n. | 支点；杠杆的支点；起支撑作用的人或物 | A thing that plays a crucial role in supporting something abstract, such as an idea or theory, or in achieving something.  | The fulcrum of the seesaw shifted, causing one side to drop suddenly. 跷跷板的支点移动了，导致一侧突然下降。

|  The government was seen as the fulcrum of the national recovery effort. 政府被视为国家复苏工作的支柱。 | synonyms: pivot, lever, support, prop, cornerstone, linchpin | antonyms: obstacle, hindrance, impediment | noun
