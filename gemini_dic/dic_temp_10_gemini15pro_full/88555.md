hitchel:/ˈhɪtʃəl/ | n. | 麻梳, 梳理麻的工具 | A tool with long teeth, used for combing flax or hemp. | The farmer used a hitchel to prepare the flax for spinning. 农民用麻梳准备亚麻用于纺纱。|  Synonyms: hackle, comb | Antonyms: N/A | noun

hitchel:/ˈhɪtʃəl/ | vt. | 梳理(麻), 苛刻地批评 | To comb flax or hemp; to criticize harshly. |  She hitcheled the flax until it was smooth and ready to spin. 她梳理亚麻直到它光滑并准备好纺纱。 He hitcheled his opponent's arguments. 他严厉批评了对手的论点。 | Synonyms: hackle, comb, criticize, lambaste | Antonyms: praise, commend | verb
