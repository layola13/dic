geometrician:/ˌdʒiːəməˈtrɪʃn/
n.
几何学家
A person skilled in geometry; a specialist in geometry.
Euclid is considered the father of geometry, and thus a great geometrician.  欧几里得被认为是几何学之父，因此是一位伟大的几何学家。
The geometrician calculated the volume of the complex shape.  这位几何学家计算出了这个复杂形状的体积。
Synonyms: geometer, mathematician
Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps one could consider "non-mathematician")
词性: noun
