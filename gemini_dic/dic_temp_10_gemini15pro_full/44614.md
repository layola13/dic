crowdfunding:/ˈkraʊdfʌndɪŋ/| n.|众筹|The practice of funding a project or venture by raising many small amounts of money from a large number of people, typically via the Internet.|The band used crowdfunding to finance their new album.  这支乐队用众筹的方式为他们的新专辑筹集资金。|近义词：collaborative funding, crowdsourcing|反义词:traditional financing|词性:noun
