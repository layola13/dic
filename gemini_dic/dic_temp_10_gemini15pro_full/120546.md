miraculous:/mɪˈrækjʊləs/ | adj. | 奇迹般的，不可思议的 | Performed by or involving a miracle; supernatural.  |  例句：It was a miraculous recovery from her illness. 她从疾病中奇迹般地康复了。 | 近义词：amazing, astonishing, extraordinary, phenomenal, remarkable, unbelievable, wonderful | 反义词：ordinary, common, usual, natural | 词性: 形容词

词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin miraculosus, from Latin miraculum ‘miracle’.
