Example 2:  We had a delicious meal of rice and cowpeas. 我们吃了一顿美味的米饭和豇豆。|Synonym: black-eyed pea, southern pea, crowder pea|Antonym: N/A|Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the English word "cow" and "pea".  Originally "cow pea" (two words).  The plant was so named because it was commonly used as fodder for cows.
