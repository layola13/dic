empyema:/ˌɛmpaɪˈiːmə/ | n. | 积脓；脓胸 | A collection of pus in a body cavity, especially the pleural cavity. |  The patient was diagnosed with empyema after experiencing persistent chest pain and fever.  病人因持续胸痛和发烧被诊断为脓胸。| 近义词: suppuration, abscess | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider "healthy tissue" or "clear fluid") | 词性: noun
