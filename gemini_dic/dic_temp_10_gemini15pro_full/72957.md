foodful:/ˈfuːdfʊl/| adj.|食物丰富的；充满食物的| Characterized by an abundance of food; abounding in food.|The Thanksgiving table was foodful, laden with turkey, stuffing, and all the fixings. 感恩节的餐桌上摆满了食物，有火鸡、馅料和各种配菜。|例句：The market was foodful, with stalls overflowing with fresh produce.  市场里食物丰富，摊位上摆满了新鲜的农产品。|近义词：abundant, plentiful, copious|反义词:scarce, meager, barren|词性:形容词

Etymology: "food" + "-ful" (suffix meaning "full of").  "Food" comes from Old English "fōda," meaning "food, fodder."  "-ful" comes from Old English "-ful," a suffix meaning "full of."
