diaphanously:/ˌdaɪəˈfænəsli/| adv.|透明地；薄如轻纱地|Characterized by such fineness of texture as to permit seeing through; sheer and translucent; delicately hazy.|Her diaphanously veiled figure moved like a phantom in the moonlight. 她那薄如蝉翼的面纱身影在月光下像个幽灵一样移动。|近义词：transparently, sheerly, gauzily|反义词：opaquely, densely|词性:副词
diaphanous:/daɪˈæfənəs/| adj.|透明的；精致的；飘渺的| (especially of fabric) light, delicate, and translucent.|She wore a diaphanous white dress. 她穿着一件透明的白色连衣裙。|近义词：transparent, sheer, translucent, gossamer|反义词：opaque, thick, dense|词性:形容词

词源:
From Greek diaphanēs "transparent," from dia- "through" + phainein "to show."
