conspicuous:/kənˈspɪkjuəs/ | adj. | 显眼的，引人注目的 | Standing out so as to be clearly visible; attracting notice or attention. |  Her bright pink hair made her conspicuous in the crowd. 她亮粉色的头发让她在人群中很显眼。

The error was conspicuous to everyone in the room.  这个错误对房间里的每个人来说都很明显。


近义词: noticeable, prominent, obvious, striking, outstanding | 反义词: inconspicuous, unnoticeable, subtle, hidden, obscure | 词性: adjective
