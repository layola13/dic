overstretch:/ˌoʊvərˈstrɛtʃ/| v. n.|过度伸展，承担过多，力所不及|To extend or strain beyond a safe or reasonable limit; to take on more than one can handle.|例句：The company overstretched itself by expanding too quickly.  这家公司扩张太快，力不从心了。

例句：Don't overstretch yourself by taking on too many projects. 不要承担太多项目，以免力不从心。|近义词：overextend, strain, overburden|反义词:undertake moderately,  limit, restrain|词性:动词

overstretch:/ˈoʊvərstrɛtʃ/| n.|过度伸展，承担过多|The act of overstretching or the state of being overstretched.|例句：The overstretch of resources led to the project's failure. 资源的过度使用导致了项目的失败。

例句：The company's financial overstretch caused a crisis. 公司的财务过度紧张引发了危机。|近义词：overextension, strain, excess|反义词: moderation, restraint, limit |词性:名词
