parochialnesses:/pəˈroʊkiəlˌnɛsɪz/| n. |狭隘，地方观念|The state of being parochial; limited outlook or scope, especially focused on local or narrow interests.|His parochialnesses prevented him from appreciating other cultures. 他的狭隘使他无法欣赏其他文化。|Example 1: The small town's parochialnesses was evident in their resistance to new ideas.  小镇的狭隘体现在他们对新思想的抵制上。

Example 2:  Her parochialnesses made it difficult for her to adapt to life in the big city. 她的狭隘使她难以适应大城市的生活。|近义词：narrow-mindedness, insularity, provincialism|反义词: cosmopolitanism, open-mindedness, worldliness |词性: 名词
