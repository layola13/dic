debasing:/dɪˈbeɪsɪŋ/
adjective
贬低的；堕落的；降低身份的
lowering the quality or character of something; degrading
例句：
The debasing effects of poverty can be seen in many parts of the world.
贫穷的堕落影响在世界许多地方都可以看到。

He engaged in debasing activities that tarnished his reputation.
他从事有损名誉的堕落活动。

近义词：degrading, corrupting, demoralizing, lowering, cheapening
反义词：ennobling, uplifting, elevating, improving
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From the verb "debase," from the Old French "debaser" (to lower in rank, value, or quality), from "de-" (down) + "bas" (low).  Ultimately from Late Latin "bassus" (low).
