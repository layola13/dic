pamphleted:/ˈpæmflɪtɪd/
v.|散发传单；用传单宣传|distributed or publicized with pamphlets.  They pamphleted the neighborhood about the upcoming election. 他们在附近散发传单宣传即将到来的选举。|leaflet, circulate, distribute|suppress, withhold|verb

pamphlet:/ˈpæmflɪt/
n.|小册子；传单|a small booklet or leaflet containing information or arguments, often political.  She picked up a pamphlet about healthy eating. 她拿起了一本关于健康饮食的小册子。|brochure, leaflet, booklet|book, volume|noun
