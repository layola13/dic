brachiopods:/ˈbrækioʊˌpɑːdz/
noun
腕足动物
Marine animals with two shells, typically attached to the seabed by a stalk.
Brachiopods are often mistaken for clams, but their internal structure is quite different.
腕足动物经常被误认为是蛤蜊，但它们的内部结构完全不同。
近义词:lamp shells
反义词:None
n.


brachiopod:/ˈbrækioʊˌpɑːd/
noun
腕足动物
A single organism of the brachiopod group.
This brachiopod fossil dates back to the Devonian period.
这块腕足动物化石可以追溯到泥盆纪时期。

反义词:None
