miscutting:/ˌmɪsˈkʌtɪŋ/
n.
错误切割，裁剪错误
The miscutting of the fabric resulted in a ruined garment.
布料的错误裁剪导致一件衣服被毁了。
The miscutting of the diamond reduced its value significantly.
钻石的错误切割大大降低了它的价值。
近义词: mis-cutting, incorrect cutting, faulty cutting
反义词: accurate cutting, precise cutting
词性: noun

词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrong" or "badly") + "cutting" (from the verb "to cut")
