clear-sightedly: /ˌklɪərˈsaɪtɪdli/ | adv. | 清晰地；明智地；有远见地 | In a way that shows a clear understanding of a situation and good judgment | She clear-sightedly assessed the situation and made the right decision. 她清晰地评估了情况并做出了正确的决定。|  He clear-sightedly predicted the economic downturn. 他有远见地预测了经济衰退。| 近义词: perspicaciously, astutely, wisely, judiciously | 反义词: shortsightedly, blindly, foolishly | 词性: 副词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "clear-sighted" + "-ly".  "Clear" comes from Old French "cler" (bright, shining), ultimately from Latin "clarus". "Sighted" comes from Old English "gesīhþ" (having sight).  "-ly" is a common suffix forming adverbs.
