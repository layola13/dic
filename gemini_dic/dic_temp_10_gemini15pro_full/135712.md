nonpreventively:/ˌnɑːnprɪˈvɛntɪvli/
adverb
非预防性地
in a way that does not prevent something from happening
The disease was treated nonpreventively, meaning no measures were taken to stop its spread.  这种疾病的治疗是非预防性的，意味着没有采取任何措施来阻止其传播。
The issue was addressed nonpreventively, only dealing with the consequences rather than the root cause.  这个问题的处理是非预防性的，只处理了后果，而不是根本原因。
近义词: reactively, remedially
反义词: preventively, prophylactically
词性: adverb
