hallucination:/həˌluːsɪˈneɪʃn/ | n. | 幻觉，幻想 |  A sensory experience of something that does not exist outside the mind, caused by various factors such as mental illness, drugs, or sleep deprivation. |  He was having hallucinations due to his high fever.  他因为高烧而出现幻觉。

He experienced hallucinations after taking the drug. 他服药后出现了幻觉。

|  delusion, illusion, mirage | reality, actuality | noun
