agitator:/ˈædʒɪteɪtər/| n.|煽动者，搅拌器|A person who stirs up political or social unrest; a device for stirring liquids.|The political agitator was arrested for inciting violence.  这个政治煽动者因煽动暴力而被捕。|Synonyms: rabble-rouser, instigator, provocateur, firebrand|Antonyms: peacemaker, conciliator, mediator|noun

agitator:/ˈædʒɪteɪtər/| n.|搅拌器|A device for stirring liquids, often used in washing machines or industrial processes.|The washing machine's agitator helps to clean the clothes thoroughly. 洗衣机的搅拌器有助于彻底清洁衣物。|Synonyms: stirrer, mixer|Antonyms: N/A|noun
