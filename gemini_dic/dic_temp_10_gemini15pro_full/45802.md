custodian:/kʌˈstoʊdiən/ | n. | 保管人，监护人，管理人 | A person who has responsibility for or looks after something. | The museum custodian carefully dusted the ancient artifacts. 博物馆管理员小心翼翼地擦拭着古代文物。| synonyms: guardian, keeper, caretaker, conservator | antonyms: destroyer, vandal | noun

custodian:/kʌˈstoʊdiən/ | n. | 托管人 | A person who is legally responsible for the care of someone who is unable to manage their own affairs, such as a child or someone with a mental disability. | He was appointed custodian of his younger brother after their parents died. 父母去世后，他被任命为弟弟的监护人。 | synonyms: guardian, trustee | antonyms: ward | noun
