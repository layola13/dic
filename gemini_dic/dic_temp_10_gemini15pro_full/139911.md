odeling:/ˈjoʊdlɪŋ/ |n.|约德尔唱法|A form of singing characterized by rapid and repeated changes of pitch between the chest register and head register (falsetto). This often creates a yodeling effect. It is a traditional form of singing in the Alps and other mountainous regions.|He impressed the crowd with his impressive odeling skills. 他以令人印象深刻的约德尔唱法技艺打动了观众。|Synonyms: yodeling|Antonyms: (No direct antonym, perhaps chanting or monotone singing)|noun
