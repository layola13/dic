comforting:/ˈkʌmfərtɪŋ/| adj.|安慰的，令人欣慰的|Providing comfort or solace; making someone feel better emotionally.|例句：A comforting hug can make all the difference.  一个安慰的拥抱可以带来很大的不同。|近义词：soothing, reassuring, consoling|反义词：distressing, upsetting|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *comforten*, from Old French *conforter*, from Late Latin *confortāre*, from Latin *com-* (“together”) + *fortāre*, from *fortis* ("strong").  The "-ing" suffix transforms the verb "comfort" into a present participle adjective.
