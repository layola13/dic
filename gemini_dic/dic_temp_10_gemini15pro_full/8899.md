antigenic:/ˌæn.tɪˈdʒɛn.ɪk/ | adj. |抗原的；产生抗体的|Capable of stimulating the production of antibodies.|The virus is highly antigenic and can trigger a strong immune response. 这种病毒具有高度抗原性，可以引发强烈的免疫反应。|Synonyms: immunogenic |Antonyms: non-antigenic |adjective

antigen:/ˈæn.tɪ.dʒən/ | n. |抗原|A toxin or other foreign substance which induces an immune response in the body, especially the production of antibodies.|The immune system recognizes antigens and produces antibodies to fight them. 免疫系统识别抗原并产生抗体来对抗它们。|Synonyms: immunogen |Antonyms: antibody |noun
