fettle:/ˈfɛtəl/| n. v. |状态，状况；整理，修整|A state of fitness or order; to make or restore to a proper state.|The horse was in fine fettle before the race. 这匹马在比赛前状态极佳。|近义词：condition, shape, order, repair, mend|反义词：disrepair, disorder|词性:名词,动词

fettle:/ˈfɛtəl/| n. v. |健康状况，精神状态|State of health or well-being, often physical.|He's been in fine fettle ever since he quit smoking. 自从戒烟以来，他的身体一直很好。|近义词：health, spirits, mood|反义词：illness, depression|词性:名词

fettle:/ˈfɛtəl/| n. v. |修整，修理|The act of putting something in order or repairing it.|He spent the afternoon fettling his bicycle. 他用了一下午的时间修理他的自行车。|近义词：repair, mend, fix, adjust|反义词：break, damage|词性:动词
