morguelike:/ˈmɔːrɡlaɪk/
adjective
像停尸房一样的
Resembling a morgue, especially in being cold, stark, or depressing.
The hospital waiting room was morguelike, with its harsh fluorescent lighting and uncomfortable plastic chairs.  医院的候诊室像停尸房一样，有着刺眼的荧光灯和不舒服的塑料椅子。
The abandoned factory had a morguelike atmosphere.  废弃的工厂有一种像停尸房一样的气氛。
近义词:grim, bleak, sterile, cold, depressing
反义词:cheerful, lively, warm, inviting
词性:adjective
词源:morgue + -like.  "Morgue" comes from French, ultimately from Arabic "marqad" meaning "resting place." "-like" is a suffix forming adjectives meaning "resembling," "characteristic of."
