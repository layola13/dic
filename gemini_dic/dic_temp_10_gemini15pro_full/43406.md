crankshaft: /ˈkræŋkʃæft/ (US), /ˈkræŋkʃɑːft/ (UK)| n. | 曲轴 |A shaft with one or more cranks, especially that which transforms the reciprocating motion of the pistons of an internal combustion engine into rotary motion.|The crankshaft converts the up-and-down motion of the pistons into rotational motion that powers the wheels. 曲轴将活塞的上下运动转换为驱动车轮的旋转运动。| The crankshaft broke, causing the engine to seize. 曲轴断裂，导致发动机卡死。| synonym: main shaft | antonym: N/A | noun
