distraction:/dɪˈstrækʃn/| n.|分心，注意力分散，使人分心的事物|Something that prevents someone from giving their attention to something else.|The noise of the traffic was a constant distraction. 交通噪音一直让人分心。|synonyms: interruption, disturbance, diversion|antonyms: concentration, focus|noun

distract:/dɪˈstrækt/| v.|使分心，转移(注意力)，分散(思想)|Prevent (someone) from giving full attention to something.|Don't distract her while she's driving. 她开车时别打扰她。|synonyms: divert, sidetrack|antonyms: focus, concentrate|verb
