multigauge:/ˈmʌltiɡeɪdʒ/
adjective
多规格的，多量程的
designed to measure multiple ranges or values.
例句：The multigauge instrument can measure voltage, current, and resistance.  多量程仪器可以测量电压、电流和电阻。

例句：A multigauge wire has several conductors of different gauges. 多规格导线有几根不同规格的导体。
近义词：multi-range, multiple-scale
反义词：single-gauge, single-range
词性：adjective
