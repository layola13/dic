christianize: /ˈkrɪstʃənaɪz/ /ˈkrɪstiənaɪz/
verb
使基督教化；使皈依基督教
To convert to Christianity; to make Christian.
Early missionaries tried to Christianize the native populations. 早期的传教士试图使当地居民皈依基督教。
The Roman Empire was Christianized in the 4th century. 罗马帝国在4世纪皈依了基督教。
近义词: evangelize, proselytize
反义词: secularize, paganize
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *christianisen*, from Old French *cristianiser*, from *cristian* (Christian) + *-iser* (suffix forming verbs from nouns or adjectives).  Ultimately from Latin *Christianus*.
