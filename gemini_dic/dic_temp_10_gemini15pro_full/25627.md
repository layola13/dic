bun's:/bʌnz/
|n.|小圆面包（复数），通常指带甜味或果干的小面包|A bun is a small, sweet, bread roll, often containing dried fruit.|例句：The bakery sells delicious currant buns. 这家面包店卖好吃的葡萄干小圆面包。|例句：She enjoyed a hot cross bun on Good Friday.  她在耶稣受难日吃了一个热十字面包。|近义词：roll, scone|反义词:(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑loaf - 一整条面包)|词性:名词

Etymology:
From Middle English *bunne*, perhaps from Old North French *buigne*, *bigne* (“swelling, bump”), from Latin *būfo* (“toad”).  The word originally referred to a swelling or rounded shape.
