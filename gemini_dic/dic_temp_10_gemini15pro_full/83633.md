haemodynamics:/ˌhiːməʊdaɪˈnæmɪks/ /ˌheməʊdaɪˈnæmɪks/| n. | 血流动力学 | The study of the physical principles governing blood flow.  It encompasses the interrelationships between blood pressure, blood flow, and vascular resistance. | The haemodynamics of the circulatory system are complex. 循环系统的血流动力学很复杂。 | synonyms: circulatory dynamics, blood flow dynamics | antonyms: N/A | noun

haemodynamic:/ˌhiːməʊdaɪˈnæmɪk/ /ˌheməʊdaɪˈnæmɪk/| adj. | 血流动力学的 | Relating to haemodynamics. | Haemodynamic monitoring is crucial during surgery. 手术期间血流动力学监测至关重要。 | synonyms: circulatory, vascular | antonyms: N/A | adjective
