interlaid:/ˌɪntərˈleɪd/
verb (past tense and past participle of interlay)
交错放置,嵌入,插入
placed or inserted between layers of something else
The handwritten pages were interlaid with tissue paper. 手写的页面中间夹有薄纸。
近义词: interspersed, inserted, interleaved
反义词: removed, extracted
词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From inter- + lay.  "Inter-" is a prefix meaning "between" or "among."  "Lay" means to put or place something down.
