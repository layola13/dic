nonelicited: /ˌnɒnɪˈlɪsɪtɪd/
adjective
非诱发的，非引出的
Not elicited or drawn out; spontaneous.
The child's nonelicited laughter filled the room.  孩子的自发的笑声充满了整个房间。
His nonelicited confession surprised everyone. 他主动的坦白让每个人都感到惊讶。
Synonyms: spontaneous, unprompted, voluntary, unforced
Antonyms: elicited, evoked, prompted, induced
词性: 形容词
