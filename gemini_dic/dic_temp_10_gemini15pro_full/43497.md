craton: /ˈkreɪtɒn/ (UK) /ˈkreɪtɑːn/ (US) | n. |克拉通；稳定地块|A large, stable block of the earth's crust, generally of Precambrian rock, that has remained relatively undisturbed by mountain-forming events. |例句：The Canadian Shield is a large craton in North America. 加拿大地盾是北美的一个大型克拉通。

例句：Cratons are the ancient cores of continents. 克拉通是大陆的古老核心。|近义词：shield, platform|反义词: orogen|词性:noun
