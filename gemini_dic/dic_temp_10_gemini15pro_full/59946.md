edi:/ˈiːdiː/| n. |Electronic Data Interchange (电子数据交换)|The use of computer systems to exchange business documents, such as orders and invoices, between organizations.|Example:  EDI has greatly improved the efficiency of our supply chain.  电子数据交换极大地提高了我们供应链的效率。|Example: We use EDI to communicate with our suppliers. 我们使用电子数据交换与我们的供应商进行沟通。|Synonym: electronic commerce, e-commerce|Antonym: paper-based transaction|noun
