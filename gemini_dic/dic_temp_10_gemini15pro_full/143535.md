outgun:/ˌaʊtˈɡʌn/
verb
胜过，以火力压制
to defeat someone by having more or better weapons
The rebels were heavily outgunned by the government's forces. 叛军在火力上被政府军严重压制。

近义词:defeat, overpower, surpass, outmatch
反义词:lose to, be defeated by
词性:verb
