interested: /ˈɪntrəstɪd/ | adj. | 感兴趣的；关心的 | Showing curiosity or concern about something or someone. |  She was very interested in the history of the town. 她对这个小镇的历史很感兴趣。 |  I'm interested in learning more about this opportunity. 我有兴趣了解更多关于这个机会的信息。| 近义词: fascinated, intrigued, keen, curious | 反义词: uninterested, apathetic, indifferent, bored | 词性: 形容词

词源: From the past participle of *interest*, from Old French *interest*, ultimately from Latin *interesse* "to be of importance, concern, make a difference."
