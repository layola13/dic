overinforms:/ˌoʊvərɪnˈfɔːrmz/ | v. | 过度告知，提供过多信息 | To provide someone with more information than they need or want, often to the point of being overwhelming or tedious. |  Example: The doctor overinformed me about the potential side effects of the medication, making me anxious. 医生过度告知我药物的潜在副作用，让我很焦虑。

Example: He tends to overinform his clients, burying the important details in a flood of unnecessary information. 他倾向于过度告知他的客户，将重要的细节淹没在大量不必要的信息中。 | 近义词: overshare, inundate, bombard | 反义词: underinform, withhold information | 词性:动词
