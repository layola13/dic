astrophyton:/ˌæstroʊˈfaɪtɒn/ | n. | 筐蛇尾 | A genus of brittle stars characterized by a large disk and branched arms. |  例句：The astrophyton gracefully moved its arms across the ocean floor. 筐蛇尾优雅地在海底移动着它的触手。

例句：Divers were amazed by the vibrant colors of the astrophyton. 潜水员对筐蛇尾鲜艳的色彩感到惊叹。 | 近义词：brittle star, serpent star | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun

Etymology:  From Greek *astron* (star) + *phyton* (plant).  Referring to its star-like appearance.
