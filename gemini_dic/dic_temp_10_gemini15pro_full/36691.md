collimator:/ˈkɒlɪmeɪtə(r)/ (US: /ˈkɑːləˌmeɪtər/)| n. | 准直器，平行光管 |A device that narrows a beam of particles or waves. It may use lenses, mirrors, or other devices to create a parallel or nearly parallel beam. |The scientist adjusted the collimator to focus the X-ray beam. 科学家调整了准直器以聚焦X射线束。| The collimator ensured a narrow beam of light for the experiment. 准直器确保了实验的光束狭窄。 | synonyms: focuser | antonyms: diffuser | noun
