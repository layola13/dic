disinhabited:/ˌdɪsɪnˈhæbɪtɪd/
adjective
无人居住的; 无人定居的
Not inhabited; having no inhabitants.
The island was disinhabited after the volcanic eruption.  火山爆发后，岛上无人居住了。
The old mining town appeared completely disinhabited.  这座古老的矿镇似乎完全无人居住。
近义词: deserted, uninhabited, vacant, empty
反义词: inhabited, populated, occupied
词性: adjective

词源:
From dis- (expressing negation) + inhabited (from inhabit, from Latin inhabitare "to dwell in," from in- "in" + habitare "to dwell").
