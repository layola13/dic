anathema: /əˈnæθəmə/ | n. | 诅咒，咒逐，令人厌恶的人或事物 | something that is strongly disliked or disapproved of |  His political views were anathema to her. 他的政治观点让她深恶痛绝。 | synonyms: curse, ban, denunciation | antonyms: blessing, benediction | noun

anathema: /əˈnæθəmə/ | n. | 宗教诅咒，革出教门 | a formal curse by a pope or a council of the Church, excommunicating a person or denouncing a doctrine |  In the Middle Ages, being declared anathema by the Church was a serious punishment.  在中世纪，被教会宣布为异端是一种严重的惩罚。 | synonyms: excommunication, condemnation | antonyms: absolution, acceptance | noun
