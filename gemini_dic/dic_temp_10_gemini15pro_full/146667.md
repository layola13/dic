overhaul:/ˌoʊvərˈhɔːl/ (US) /ˌəʊvəˈhɔːl/ (UK)| n. vt.|彻底检修，大修；改造；赶上|A thorough examination and repair of a machine or system; to renovate, repair, or revise extensively.|例句：The car is due for an overhaul. 这辆车该大修了。|近义词：renovate, repair, revamp, restore, service|反义词:damage, neglect|noun, verb
