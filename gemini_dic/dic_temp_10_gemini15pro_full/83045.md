gurdwaras:/ˈɡʊərdwɑːrəz/
n.
锡克教寺庙
A Sikh temple, the central place of worship for Sikhs.
Many Sikhs visit gurdwaras daily to pray and participate in community activities. 许多锡克教徒每天都会去谒师所祈祷并参与社区活动。
The gurdwara provides free meals to anyone, regardless of their religion. 谒师所向所有人提供免费餐食，无论他们的宗教信仰是什么。
Synonyms: Sikh temple,  temple
Antonyms: N/A
