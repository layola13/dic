chipboard:/ˈtʃɪpbɔːrd/| n. |硬纸板，刨花板|A type of board made from compressed wood chips, often used in furniture making and packaging.  It is a cheaper alternative to solid wood or plywood.|The back of the notepad was made of chipboard.|近义词:particleboard, fiberboard, cardboard|反义词:solid wood, plywood|词性:noun

chipboards:/ˈtʃɪpbɔːrds/| n. |硬纸板（复数），刨花板（复数）|Plural form of chipboard, referring to multiple pieces or sheets of this material.|We used several chipboards to build the bookshelf.|近义词:particleboards, fiberboards, cardboards|反义词:solid woods, plywoods|词性:noun
