aqueousness:/ˈeɪkwiəsnəs/
n.
含水性; 水分
The state or quality of being aqueous, or containing water.
The aqueousness of the solution was apparent from its clarity.  这种溶液的含水性从它的清澈度就可以看出。
humidity, moisture, wateriness
dryness, aridity
noun
