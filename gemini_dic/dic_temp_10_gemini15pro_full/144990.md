overactivated:/ˌoʊvərˈæktɪveɪtɪd/
adjective
过度激活的
Excessively or abnormally active; stimulated beyond normal levels.  This often refers to biological systems, like an overactivated immune system.
Her immune system was overactivated, leading to inflammation. 她的免疫系统过度激活，导致了炎症。
The stress response in her body was overactivated due to the constant pressure at work.  由于工作上的持续压力，她体内的应激反应过度活跃。
近义词:overstimulated, hyperactive
反义词:underactive, suppressed
词性:adjective

overactivate:/ˌoʊvərˈæktɪveɪt/
verb
过度激活
To stimulate or activate something beyond normal levels, often leading to negative consequences.
The constant notifications overactivated my stress response.  不断的通知过度激活了我的应激反应。
Certain foods can overactivate the immune system, triggering inflammation.  某些食物会过度激活免疫系统，引发炎症。
近义词:overstimulate
反义词:suppress, inhibit
词性:verb

词源:
over- + activate
