backfires:/ˌbækˈfaɪərz/
| v. |适得其反，事与愿违| (of a plan or action) have an unintended and unwelcome result that is the opposite of what was intended.|  His plan to get her attention backfired spectacularly. 他想引起她注意的计划彻底失败了。|
backfires:/ˌbækˈfaɪərz/
| n. |逆火| an explosion of unburnt gases in the exhaust of an internal combustion engine, especially one caused by a fault in the timing of the ignition.| The old car backfired loudly as he started it. 那辆旧车启动时发出很大的逆火声。|
backfire:/ˌbækˈfaɪər/
| v. |(of a gun or other explosion) make a loud, sharp bang.| The shotgun backfired, startling the birds. 猎枪发出逆火声，惊吓了鸟儿。|
