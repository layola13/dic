biolytic:/ˌbaɪəˈlɪtɪk/
adjective
生物分解的，生物溶解的
Relating to or causing the breakdown of organic matter by biological means, especially by microorganisms.
Biolytic enzymes are used in some detergents to break down stains.  生物溶解酶被用于一些洗涤剂中以分解污渍。
Biolytic processes are crucial for composting. 生物分解过程对于堆肥至关重要。
近义词: biodegradable, biodegradative
反义词: non-biodegradable
词性: adjective
