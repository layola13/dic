batardeau:/ˌbætərˈdoʊ/| n.|拦水坝，堤堰|A temporary dam built to exclude water from an area so it can be worked on dry.|例句：The engineers constructed a batardeau to repair the bridge pier. 工程师们建造了一个拦水坝来修复桥墩。

例句：The batardeau allowed the workers to access the damaged section of the pipeline. 拦水坝使工人们能够进入管道受损的部分。|近义词：cofferdam, temporary dam|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "open channel")|词性:noun
