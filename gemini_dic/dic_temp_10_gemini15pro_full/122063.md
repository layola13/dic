mister:/ˈmɪstər/| n.|先生；Mister|A polite or respectful form of address for a man, used without his surname. |Example: Excuse me, mister, do you have the time?  对不起，先生，请问现在几点了？|Example: Mister Smith will see you now. 史密斯先生现在要见你。|近义词:sir, Mr.|反义词:miss, Mrs., Ms.|noun
