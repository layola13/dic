claylike:/ˈkleɪlaɪk/
adjective
像黏土的；似黏土的
Resembling clay in texture, consistency, or appearance.
The soil was claylike and difficult to dig. 土壤像黏土一样，很难挖。
The sculptor molded the claylike substance into a beautiful figure.  雕塑家将这种类似黏土的物质塑造成一个美丽的雕像。

近义词:clayey, argillaceous
反义词:sandy, granular
