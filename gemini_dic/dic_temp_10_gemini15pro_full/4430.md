alases:/ˈeɪliəsɪz/ | n. | 化名，别名（复数） | A name that is not someone's real or official name, especially one used by a criminal, writer, or performer. | Sherlock Holmes, whose real name is William Sherlock Scott Holmes, sometimes uses alases when working undercover. 夏洛克·福尔摩斯，真名是威廉·夏洛克·斯科特·福尔摩斯，有时在卧底工作时使用化名。 | synonyms: pseudonyms, aliases, assumed names | antonyms: real name, legal name | noun

Etymology: From Middle English *alias, from Anglo-Norman and Old French *alis "otherwise," from Latin *alias "at another time, elsewhere," from alius "other."
