epanastrophe:/ɪˌpænəˈstrɒfi/ /ɪˌpænəˈstrɑːfi/
| n. |首语重复法; 回环|
|Repetition of a word or phrase at the end of successive clauses.  It is the opposite of anaphora.|
|“The government of the people, by the people, for the people, shall not perish from the earth.” - Abraham Lincoln (This is also an example of tricolon.)|
|近义词: epiphora, anadiplosis|
|反义词: anaphora|
|词性: noun|
