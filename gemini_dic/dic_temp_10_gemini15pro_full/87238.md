heretically:/həˈretɪkli/| adv.|异端地，邪说地|In a way that goes against accepted religious beliefs or established customs or rules.|He was accused of speaking heretically about the church's teachings. 他被指控异端邪说地谈论教会的教义。|Synonyms: unorthodoxly, dissidently, iconoclastically|Antonyms: orthodoxly, traditionally, conventionally|词性:副词
