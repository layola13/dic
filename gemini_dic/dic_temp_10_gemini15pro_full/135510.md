nonplastic:/ˌnɒnˈplæstɪk/ /ˌnɑːnˈplæstɪk/| adj.|非塑料的；非塑性的|Not made of plastic; not capable of being molded.|例句：She uses nonplastic containers for her lunch. 她用非塑料容器装午餐。|近义词：non-plastic |反义词: plastic|词性:形容词

nonplastic:/ˌnɒnˈplæstɪk/ /ˌnɑːnˈplæstɪk/| n.|非塑料制品|A substance or material that is not plastic.|例句：The store sells a variety of nonplastics, including glass and metal. 这家商店出售各种非塑料制品，包括玻璃和金属制品。|近义词：non-plastic material|反义词: plastic|词性:名词
