nonabsorbable:/ˌnɒnəbˈzɔːbəbl/ /ˌnɑːnəbˈsɔːrbəbl/| adj.|不可吸收的|Not capable of being absorbed.  This term often refers to materials used in medicine, such as sutures, that the body cannot break down and absorb.|The surgeon used nonabsorbable sutures to close the wound.  外科医生使用不可吸收的缝线来缝合伤口。

The doctor recommended a nonabsorbable dressing for the deep cut. 医生建议对深度伤口使用不可吸收的敷料。|近义词：indigestible,impermeable|反义词:absorbable, digestible|词性:adjective
