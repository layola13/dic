doom-and-glooms: /ˌduːm ən ˈɡluːmz/ | n. | 悲观主义者；悲观情绪；阴郁气氛 | Pessimists or a general atmosphere of pessimism and negativity.  It often refers to people who constantly predict or focus on negative outcomes. | The doom-and-glooms are predicting a recession. 悲观主义者预言经济衰退。|  The air was thick with doom and gloom after the team lost the championship.  球队输掉冠军后，气氛一片阴郁。 |  pessimists, naysayers, prophets of doom | optimists, positive thinkers | noun
