arapakis:/ˌærəˈpɑːkis/| n. |阿拉帕基斯（一种希腊甜点）|A Greek dessert made with semolina, sugar, and spices, often flavored with orange or lemon. It's typically baked and served warm, sometimes with syrup or ice cream.|She made a delicious arapakis for dessert. 她做了一份美味的阿拉帕基斯作为甜点。|近义词：halva, semolina cake|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
