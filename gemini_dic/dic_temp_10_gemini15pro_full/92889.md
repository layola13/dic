idealized:/aɪˈdiːəlaɪzd/ | adj. |理想化的；完美的|Regarded or presented as perfect or better than in reality.|例句：She had an idealized view of country life. 她对乡村生活抱有理想化的看法。|例句：He presented an idealized version of his childhood. 他描述了一个理想化的童年版本。|近义词：romanticized, glorified, exalted, utopian|反义词：realistic, pragmatic, cynical|词性:形容词
idealize:/aɪˈdiːəlaɪz/ | v. |理想化；将…理想化|To regard or represent as perfect or better than in reality.|例句：He tends to idealize his childhood. 他倾向于将自己的童年理想化。|例句：Don't idealize him; he's just a normal person. 不要把他理想化；他只是一个普通人。|近义词：romanticize, glorify, exalt|反义词：vilify, disparage, denigrate|词性:动词
