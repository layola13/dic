now-and-again:/ˌnaʊ ən əˈɡen/ | adv. |偶尔，有时|Happening occasionally; from time to time.| I see her now and again at the library. 我偶尔会在图书馆见到她。| 近义词: occasionally, sometimes, every so often, once in a while | 反义词: frequently, often, always | 词性: 副词

词源:  This phrase is a simple combination of the adverbs "now" and "again," with "and" connecting them.  It emphasizes the intermittent nature of the action or occurrence.  "Now" implies a present or immediate moment, while "again" suggests repetition after a period of time.
