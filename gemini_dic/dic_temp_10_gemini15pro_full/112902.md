ludification:/ˌluːdɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/ | n. | 游戏化 | The process of making something more game-like, often by adding game mechanics or game thinking to non-game contexts.  It aims to increase engagement and motivation. | Example 1: Ludification of learning can make education more enjoyable for students.  游戏化学习可以使学生更享受教育。

Example 2: The company used ludification to improve employee productivity.  该公司使用游戏化来提高员工生产力。 | 近义词: gamification | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "ludus" (game, play) + "-ification" (process of making).
