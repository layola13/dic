mishitting:/ˌmɪsˈhɪtɪŋ/
|v.|击错；打偏|The golfer was mishitting the ball all day.  这位高尔夫球手一整天都打偏了球。|The tennis player mishit the ball and it went out of bounds.  网球运动员打偏了球，球出界了。|mistiming, miscalculating|hitting accurately, connecting properly|verb
ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- + hitting, present participle of hit.
