massacrers:/ˈmæsəkərərz/| n.|大屠杀者；屠夫|People who commit massacres. Those responsible for the systematic killing of a large number of people.|The *massacrers* were never brought to justice. 屠杀者从未被绳之以法。|近义词：killers, murderers, slaughterers|反义词:saviors, rescuers|Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle French *massacre*, from Old French *maçacre*, of uncertain origin. Possibly from Vulgar Latin *macecre*, from *mazacre*, alteration of *mattiacre* "slaughterhouse, butcher."
