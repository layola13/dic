hence:/hens/| adv.|因此，所以；从此，今后|From this time; from now.  From this source or origin; from this reason or cause.|The evidence is weak, hence our skepticism.  证据不足，因此我们持怀疑态度。|therefore, consequently, thus, accordingly| 反义词:  (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑用 but, however, yet 等表示转折的词)|副词
