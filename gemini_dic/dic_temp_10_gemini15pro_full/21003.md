blessedest:/ˈblesɪdɪst/ | adj. | 最有福的，最神圣的 |  Superlative form of "blessed," meaning most blessed or most holy.  It is archaic and rarely used in modern English. |  "The blessedest of women," he declared, though she looked quite ordinary.  他宣称她是“最有福的女人”，尽管她看起来很普通。| Synonym: most blessed, holiest | Antonym: most cursed, most unholy | Adjective
