bring-to-light:/ˌbrɪŋ tə ˈlaɪt/ | phrasal verb | 揭露；发现；公开 | To reveal something that was previously hidden or unknown. | The investigation brought to light a series of financial irregularities. 调查揭露了一系列财务违规行为。| The documents brought to light the truth about the government's involvement. 这些文件揭露了政府参与的真相。 |  reveal, uncover, expose, unearth | conceal, hide, cover up | verb phrase
