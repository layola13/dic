displeasedly:/dɪsˈpliːzɪdli/
adverb
不高兴地，不满意地
in a way that shows displeasure or dissatisfaction.
She frowned displeasedly at the mess. 她不高兴地皱着眉头看着这一团糟。
He shook his head displeasedly. 他不高兴地摇了摇头。
近义词:unhappily, sullenly, resentfully, grumpily, angrily
反义词:happily, gladly, cheerfully, contentedly, pleasantly
词性:adverb

词源:
From displeased + -ly. Displeased itself comes from the prefix dis- (expressing negation) + pleased (from the verb please, ultimately from Latin placere "to please").
