neorama:/ˌniːəˈrɑːmə/ /ˌniːəˈræmə/
noun
新全景画；新全景电影；新奇的景象
A panoramic depiction of a scene, often on a large scale, using innovative techniques and sometimes including three-dimensional elements.
The neorama transported viewers to the heart of the Amazon rainforest.  这幅新全景画将观众带到了亚马逊雨林的中心。
The artist created a stunning neorama of the city using projections and sound.  这位艺术家用投影和声音创作了一幅令人惊叹的城市新全景画。
Synonyms: panorama, diorama, cyclorama
Antonyms:  (No direct antonyms, could consider "limited view", "close-up")
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *νεός* (neos, "new") + *ὅραμα* (horama, "sight, spectacle"). Coined in the late 19th century.
