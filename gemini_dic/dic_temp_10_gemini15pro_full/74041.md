fos:/fɒs/ /fɑːs/ | n. |窝，巢穴；凹陷| A den or hollow place, especially one occupied by an animal. | The fox retreated to its fos. 狐狸退回到它的窝里。|  近义词：den, lair, burrow, nest | 反义词: mound, hill | noun

fos:/fɒs/ /fɑːs/ | n. |(解剖学)窝，凹| A pit or depression, especially in a bone. | The fos of the femur articulates with the tibia.  股骨的窝与胫骨相连。| 近义词：cavity, depression, hollow | 反义词: protuberance, bulge | noun

词源: From Old French *fosse (attested in Old Norman), from Latin fossa (“ditch, trench, moat”), related to fodere (“to dig”).
