creepier: /ˈkriːpiər/ | adj. | 更令人毛骨悚然的，更令人害怕的 | Comparative form of "creepy," meaning more unsettling, strange, or frightening.  Evoking a sense of unease or fear. | The abandoned house felt even creepier at night.  那栋废弃的房子在晚上感觉更令人毛骨悚然了。| The woods were creepier than I remembered.  这片树林比我记忆中的更令人害怕了。 | 近义词: spookier, eerier, more unsettling | 反义词: more comforting, more reassuring | 词性: 形容词

词源:
From "creepy," which likely originated from a combination of "creep" (meaning to move slowly and stealthily) and the suffix "-y" (forming adjectives).
