geographies:/dʒiˈɒɡrəfiz/ | n. |地理学，地理环境，地域|The study of the physical features of the earth and its atmosphere, and of human activity as it affects and is affected by these, including the distribution of populations and resources, land use, and industries.  Also: the geographical features of a particular region or area; the topography of a specific place or environment.|例句：The geographies of these two countries are quite different. 这两个国家的地理环境差异很大。| 近义词: topography, terrain, landscapes | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a field of study or physical characteristics) | 词性: noun


geographies:/dʒiˈɒɡrəfiz/ | n. |地域，地区|The features and arrangement of a place or area, especially in terms of its physical features or its inhabitants.|例句：She studies the geographies of poverty and crime. 她研究贫困和犯罪的地域分布。| 近义词: regions, territories, areas, zones | 反义词: (No direct antonym for a concept of areas) | 词性: noun
