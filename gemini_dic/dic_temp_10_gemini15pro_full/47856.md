例句：The politician's deceitfulness was eventually exposed by investigative journalists.  这位政客的欺骗行径最终被调查记者曝光。

近义词：dishonesty, duplicity, fraudulence, trickery, cunning|反义词：honesty, sincerity, truthfulness|词性: 名词

词源：From Middle English *deceitfulnes*, from deceit + -ful + -ness.  Ultimately from Latin *decēptio*.
