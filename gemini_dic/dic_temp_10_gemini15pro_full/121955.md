missionary:/ˈmɪʃəneri/ (US), /ˈmɪʃənəri/ (UK)| n. adj.|传教士，传教的|A person sent on a religious mission, especially one sent to promote Christianity in a foreign country. Also, relating to or characteristic of missionaries or their work.|例句：She worked as a missionary in Africa for many years. 她多年来在非洲担任传教士。

The missionary society funded the building of a new school.  这个传教士协会资助建造了一所新学校。|近义词：apostle, evangelist, preacher|反义词: None (A direct antonym doesn't exist, perhaps "non-believer" in a specific context)|词性: Noun, Adjective
