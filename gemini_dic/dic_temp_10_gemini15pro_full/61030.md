elites:/ɪˈliːts/ | n. | 精英；精英人士 | A select group of people considered to be superior in intellect, talent, power, or privilege. |  The social elites often control the majority of the resources.  社会精英通常控制着大部分资源。| synonyms: best, aristocracy, cream of the crop | antonyms: masses, commoners, rabble | noun

elites:/ɪˈliːts/ | adj. | 精英的；优等的  |  Relating to or consisting of the best or most skilled people in a group.  |  The elite athletes competed in the Olympics. 精英运动员参加了奥运会。 | synonyms: top, choice, select | antonyms: ordinary, average, mediocre | adjective
