continents:/ˈkɒntɪnənts/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntɪnənts/ (US)| n. |大陆，洲|Large landmasses on Earth, conventionally considered as seven in number: Africa, Antarctica, Asia, Australia, Europe, North America, and South America.|例句：The continents drifted apart over millions of years.  各大洲经过数百万年漂移开来。|近义词：landmasses|反义词:N/A|词性:noun

continents:/ˈkɒntɪnənts/ (UK) /ˈkɑːntɪnənts/ (US)| n. |大陆，洲|Relating to or characteristic of a continent.|There is a distinctly continental climate here, with hot summers and cold winters. 这里的气候明显是大陆性的，夏季炎热，冬季寒冷。|近义词：land-based|反义词:island, insular|词性:adjective
