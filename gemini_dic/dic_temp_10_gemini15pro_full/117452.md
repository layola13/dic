meditative:/ˈmedɪteɪtɪv/| adj.|沉思的；冥想的|Given to, characterized by, or inducing meditation; contemplative; thoughtful.|She sat in a meditative pose, contemplating the meaning of life. 她以冥想的姿势坐着，思考着生命的意义。|例句：The quiet garden provided a meditative atmosphere.  安静的花园提供了一个冥想的氛围。

例句：He listened to the meditative music and felt his stress melt away. 他听着冥想的音乐，感到压力消散了。|近义词：contemplative, reflective, pensive, thoughtful|反义词：unthinking, impulsive, rash, inattentive|词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *meditativus, from Latin meditatus (past participle of meditari “to meditate”), from *meditat-, frequentative of mederi "to heal, remedy," from PIE root *med- "to take appropriate measures."  The sense evolution is from "ponder (a remedy)" to "consider thoughtfully."
