boydekin:/ˈbɔɪdɪkɪn/ | n. | 小男孩，男童 | A young boy, often used affectionately or playfully.  This word is archaic or dialectal and not commonly used in modern English. It is likely a diminutive of "boy." |  He was just a wee boydekin then.  他那时还是个小不点儿。| 近义词: lad, youngster, little boy, urchin | 反义词: adult, man, girl | 词性: noun

词源:  From Middle English *boydekyn, a diminutive of boy + -kin.  Ultimately from Middle Low German *boidekin.
