deforce:/dɪˈfɔːrs/| vt. n.|非法剥夺，强占；扣留(土地或财产)|To wrongfully withhold possession (of land or property) from the rightful owner.|He was accused of deforcing the widow of her property. 他被指控非法剥夺了寡妇的财产。|The landlord deforced the tenant from the apartment. 房东强行把房客赶出了公寓。|近义词: usurp, dispossess, wrest|反义词: relinquish, surrender, restore|词性:动词, 名词

词源:
From Old French *deforcer*, from *de-* (from Latin *dis-*) + *forcer* "to force" (from Latin *fortis* "strong").
