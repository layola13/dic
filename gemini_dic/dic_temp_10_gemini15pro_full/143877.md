outpolitick: /ˌaʊtˈpɑːlətɪk/ /ˌaʊtˈpɒlɪtɪk/
verb
以智取胜，玩弄权术胜过
to defeat someone by being more clever and skillful in political matters
The young senator outpoliticked his rivals and won the election.  年轻的参议员以智取胜，赢得了选举。
Synonyms: outmaneuver, outsmart, outwit
Antonyms: be outmaneuvered, be outsmarted, be outwitted
词性: v.
