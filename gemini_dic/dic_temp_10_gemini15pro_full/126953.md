narratory:/ˈnærətɪv/ /ˈnærətɔːri/ | adj. n.|叙事的，叙述的；故事；叙述|Relating to narration; a story or account of events.|The film had a compelling narratory structure. 这部电影有一个引人入胜的叙事结构.|近义词：narrative, storytelling, descriptive|反义词：lyrical, dramatic|词性: adjective, noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *narrātōrius*, from Latin narrāt-, past participle stem of narrāre ‘to relate’, from nārus ‘knowing’.
