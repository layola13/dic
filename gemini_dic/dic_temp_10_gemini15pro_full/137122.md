nonsubscribers:/ˌnɒnsəbˈskraɪbərz/ /ˌnɑːnsəbˈskraɪbərz/
n.
非订阅者；未订阅者
People who have not subscribed to a particular service or publication.
Newspapers often offer discounts to new subscribers, while nonsubscribers pay the full price.  报纸通常会给新订阅用户提供折扣，而非订阅用户则需支付全价。
The website offers limited access to nonsubscribers.  该网站对非订阅用户提供有限的访问权限。
