acetize: /əˈsiːtaɪz/
verb
乙酰化; 使乙酰化
To convert into acetate, or to introduce an acetyl group into.
The process acetizes cellulose into cellulose acetate.  这个过程将纤维素乙酰化成醋酸纤维素。
The chemist acetized the compound. 化学家将该化合物乙酰化。
近义词: acetylate
反义词: deacetylate
词性: v.
