chartered:/ˈtʃɑːrtərd/ | adj. | 特许的，包租的 |  Renting or hiring something for exclusive temporary use. Having been granted a charter, which is a formal document outlining rights and privileges. |  We took a chartered flight to the island. 我们乘坐包机去了岛上。 | synonyms: hired, rented, leased, licensed, authorized | antonyms: public, unlicensed, unauthorized | word type: adjective

chartered:/ˈtʃɑːrtərd/ | n. |  特许状; 执照 | A formal document issued by a government or other authority, granting specific rights and privileges to a person, group, or organization. | The company received a chartered to operate a ferry service. 这家公司获得了经营渡轮服务的特许状。 | synonyms: license, permit, authorization, warrant | antonyms: prohibition, ban | word type: noun
