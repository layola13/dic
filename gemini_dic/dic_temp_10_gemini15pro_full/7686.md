anoint:/əˈnɔɪnt/| vt.|涂油，施以圣油，选定，指定|To apply oil or ointment to, especially as part of a religious ceremony; to choose someone for a special purpose. |The priest anointed the baby's forehead.  牧师在婴儿的额头上涂了油。

The king anointed his eldest son as his successor. 国王指定他的长子为继承人。|近义词：consecrate, ordain, bless, designate, appoint|反义词: desecrate, condemn|词性: verb

Etymology: From Old French enoint, past participle of enoindre, from Latin inunguere, from in- + unguere "to anoint."
