dumfounded:/dʌmˈfaʊndɪd/
adjective
惊呆的，目瞪口呆的
So amazed and confused that you are unable to speak.
She was dumbfounded by the news. 她被这个消息惊呆了。

The sudden appearance of her ex-husband left her dumbfounded. 她前夫的突然出现让她目瞪口呆。

Near Synonyms: astonished, speechless, stunned, bewildered, flabbergasted
Antonyms: unsurprised, unfazed, composed, calm

ETYMOLOGY->
From dumb (unable to speak) + confound (to confuse and surprise).  The original sense was "to make speechless with amazement."
