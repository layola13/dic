omniscient: /ɒmˈnɪsɪənt/ /ɑːmˈnɪʃənt/ | adj. | 无所不知的；全知的 | Knowing everything; having unlimited awareness or understanding. |  The narrator in the story is omniscient, revealing the thoughts and feelings of all the characters.  故事中的叙述者是无所不知的，揭示了所有角色的思想和感受。

God is often depicted as an omniscient being. 上帝通常被描述为无所不知的存在。 | 近义词: all-knowing, all-seeing | 反义词: limited, ignorant | 词性: adjective
