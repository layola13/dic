calcographic:/ˌkælkəˈɡræfɪk/
adjective
雕刻的，铜版雕刻的
Relating to or produced by calcography, a printmaking technique in which an image is incised into a metal plate.
Calcographic prints are known for their fine detail and rich textures. 铜版画以其精细的细节和丰富的纹理而闻名。
The museum boasts a vast collection of calcographic works by Rembrandt. 该博物馆收藏了大量伦勃朗的铜版画作品。
近义词:engraved, etched
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "painted," "lithographic," or "digital")
词性: adjective
