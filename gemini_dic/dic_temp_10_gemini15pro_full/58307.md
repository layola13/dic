dry-roast:/ˈdraɪ roʊst/ | v. | 干烤，烘烤 | To roast (nuts, coffee beans, etc.) without oil or fat. |  I dry-roasted the peanuts until they were fragrant. 我干烤花生直到它们散发出香味。 |  The coffee beans are dry-roasted to bring out their natural flavors.  咖啡豆经过干烤以带出其天然风味。 | roast, bake | fry, boil | verb
