lupines:/ˈluːpɪnz/ /ˈluːpaɪnz/| n.|羽扇豆，鲁冰花|A plant of the pea family, with deeply divided leaves and tall, colorful, pea-like flowers.|例句：The lupines were in full bloom, painting the field with shades of purple and pink. 羽扇豆盛开着，给田野涂上了紫色和粉红色的色调。

例句：She scattered lupine seeds in the garden, hoping for a vibrant display next spring.  她在花园里撒了羽扇豆种子，希望明年春天能看到一片鲜艳的花朵。|近义词：bluebonnets (specifically for blue lupines)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun
