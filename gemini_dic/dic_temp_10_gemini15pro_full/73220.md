forcers:/ˈfɔːrsərz/| n.|强迫者，强制因素，推动者|Something that forces or compels a particular course of action or outcome.  Those factors that contribute to change or development.|Greenhouse gases are major climate forcers. 温室气体是主要的气候强迫因子。|Synonyms: drivers, agents, catalysts, compelling factors|Antonyms: inhibitors, deterrents|Noun
