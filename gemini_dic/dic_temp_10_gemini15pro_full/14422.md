autre:/oʊtr(ə)/ |adj.| other, different |  Meaning "other," "different," "second." Used only before a noun. | Une autre voiture. Another car. | Synonyms: different, other, another | Antonyms: same, identical | Adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *altre, auter, from Latin alter "the other (of two), second," from PIE root *al- (1) "beyond."
