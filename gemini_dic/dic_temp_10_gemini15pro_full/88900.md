holaspidean:/ˌhɒləˈspɪdiən/
adjective
全盾形的；全盾海胆类的
Relating to or belonging to the Holaspidea, a group of sea slugs characterized by a shell that completely covers the body.
The  holaspidean sea slugs are known for their vibrant colors. 全盾海胆类海蛞蝓以其鲜艳的色彩而闻名。
近义词: tectibranch (more general, includes other groups)
反义词: (No direct antonym, could use terms like "shell-less" for contrasting slugs)
词性: adjective
