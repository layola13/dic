odorousnesses:/ˈoʊdərəsnɪsɪz/
n.
恶臭，臭味
The state or quality of being odorous; the degree to which something is odorous.
The odorousnesses emanating from the landfill were unbearable.  来自垃圾填埋场的恶臭令人难以忍受。
近义词:smells, stenches, aromas (if unpleasant), fetors
反义词:fragrances, perfumes, sweet smells
词性:noun

odorous:/ˈoʊdərəs/
adj.
有气味的，有香味的（通常指难闻的气味）
Having a distinct smell, often unpleasant.
The odorous fumes from the factory caused complaints from nearby residents. 工厂散发出的刺鼻烟雾引起了附近居民的投诉。
近义词:smelly, stinky, fragrant (if pleasant), aromatic
反义词:odorless, scentless, fragrance-free
词性:adjective
