lengths:/leŋθs/ | n. |长度，距离，期限，一段，一节| The measurement of something from end to end; the duration of something in time. |The length of the table is six feet. 桌子的长度是六英尺。| synonyms:  distance, extent, duration, span | antonyms: shortness, brevity | noun

lengths:/leŋθs/ | n. |（时间、空间的）长，长度；一段，一节|A piece or portion of something, typically of a specified length. |He bought a length of rope. 他买了一段绳子。| synonyms: piece, section, segment | antonyms: whole, entirety | noun
