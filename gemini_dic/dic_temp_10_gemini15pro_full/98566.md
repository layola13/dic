instituters:/ˈɪnstɪˌtjuːtərz/
n.
创立者；设立者；发起人
People who establish or initiate something, especially an organization or system.
The instituters of the new law were praised for their foresight.  新法律的制定者因其远见而受到赞扬。
The instituters of the charity worked tirelessly to help those in need.  慈善机构的发起人不知疲倦地工作，以帮助那些需要帮助的人。
synonyms: founders, originators, establishers, creators
antonyms: destroyers, dismantlers
