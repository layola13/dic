molasses:/məˈlæsɪz/| n.| 糖蜜 |A thick, dark brown syrup obtained from raw sugar during the refining process, a by-product of sugar production.| The gingerbread recipe calls for molasses.  姜饼食谱需要用到糖蜜。| synonyms: treacle, syrup | antonyms: (No direct antonym, possibly refined sugar) | noun

molasses:/məˈlæsɪz/| adj. (informal) | 慢吞吞的; 粘稠的 |Extremely slow-moving; viscous.| The traffic moved at a molasses pace. 交通慢得像糖蜜一样。| synonyms: slow, sluggish, viscous | antonyms: fast, quick, rapid | adjective
