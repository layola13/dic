hyperflexibly:/ˌhaɪpərˈflɛksɪbli/| adv.| 超灵活地，极灵活地 |  Meaning "in a hyperflexible manner," formed from "hyperflexible" + "-ly." This describes actions or processes performed with extreme flexibility, adaptability, or responsiveness. | The company adapted hyperflexibly to the changing market conditions.  这家公司对不断变化的市场环境做出了极其灵活的调整。|  The gymnast moved hyperflexibly, contorting her body into incredible positions.  体操运动员的动作极其灵活，将她的身体扭曲成令人难以置信的姿势。 | 近义词: extremely flexibly, highly adaptably, very responsively | 反义词: inflexibly, rigidly, unresponsively | 词性: 副词
