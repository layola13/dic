episcopalianism:/ɪˌpɪskəˈpeɪliənɪzəm/
n.
主教制；圣公会教义
The system of church government by bishops, and especially the doctrines and practices of Episcopal churches.
Episcopalianism is the dominant religion in parts of Virginia.  弗吉尼亚州的部分地区以圣公会为主流宗教。
The church follows the tenets of Episcopalianism.  该教堂遵循圣公会的教义。
近义词: Anglicanism
反义词: (No direct opposite, could consider other Christian denominations like Catholicism, Presbyterianism, etc.)
词性: noun
