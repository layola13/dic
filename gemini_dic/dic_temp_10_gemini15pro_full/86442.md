heenan: /ˈhiːnən/ | n. | 打手，暴徒 | A hired thug or bully. | He acted as the boxer's heenan, intimidating his opponents. 他充当拳击手的打手，恐吓他的对手。|  近义词：thug, bully, goon, hoodlum | 反义词: protector, guardian | 词性: 名词

词源:  The word "heenan" likely originates from the name of John C. Heenan, an American bare-knuckle prizefighter in the mid-19th century. His aggressive fighting style and reputation led to his name being associated with hired thugs and bullies.
