denude:/dɪˈnjuːd/ /dɪˈnuːd/| vt.|剥夺，使裸露，脱去…的覆盖物|To strip (something) of its covering, possessions, or assets; to make bare or naked. |The hillside was denuded of trees by the fire. 山坡上的树木被大火烧光了。|The constant winds had denuded the mountaintop of vegetation.  持续的大风使得山顶的植被消失殆尽。|Synonyms: strip, bare, expose, divest|Antonyms: cover, clothe, protect|verb
