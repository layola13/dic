overbriefs: /ˈoʊvərˌbriːfs/
verb
过度指导；提供过多信息
To provide someone with excessive or unnecessarily detailed instructions or information, often to the point of confusion or inefficiency.
The lawyer overbriefed his client, causing him to become overwhelmed and confused before the trial.  律师过度指导了他的客户，导致他在审判前不知所措。

The marketing team overbriefed the advertising agency, giving them so much information that they struggled to create a focused campaign.  营销团队向广告公司提供了过多的信息，以至于他们难以创建一个重点突出的活动。

Synonym: overinstruct, overload
Antonym: underbrief, underinstruct
词性: 动词
