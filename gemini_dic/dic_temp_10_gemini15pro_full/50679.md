dermapteran: /dɜːrˈmæptərən/ (US), /ˌdɜːˈmɒptərən/ (UK)
n.
蠼螋；革翅目昆虫
An insect of the order Dermaptera, characterized by cerci modified into forceps-like structures at the end of the abdomen.  These insects are commonly known as earwigs.
Earwigs, or dermapterans, are often found in gardens. 蠼螋，或者说革翅目昆虫，经常在花园里被发现。
Dermapterans use their cerci for defense and capturing prey. 革翅目昆虫用它们的尾铗进行防御和捕捉猎物。
近义词: earwig
反义词: None (No direct opposite for a biological classification)
词性: Noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek derma "skin" + pteron "wing"
