bordered:/ˈbɔːrdərd/ | adj. |有边界的，接壤的|Having a border or boundary; edged or enclosed.|The garden is bordered by a tall hedge. 花园被高高的树篱包围。|近义词：bounded, edged, fringed, neighboring, adjacent|反义词:unbounded, limitless, open|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From border (n.) + -ed.  The noun "border" comes from Old French "border" (to border, edge), from a Germanic source related to board (n.).
