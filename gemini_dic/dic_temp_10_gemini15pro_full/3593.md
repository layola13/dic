agaves:/əˈɡɑːveɪz/
noun
龙舌兰（复数）
A succulent plant, native to hot, dry regions of the Americas, with thick, fleshy leaves and a tall flower stalk.  Many species are used to make agave nectar, tequila, and other products.
The agaves in my garden are starting to bloom. 我花园里的龙舌兰开始开花了。
近义词:succulents, cacti
反义词: (No direct antonym for a plant type)
词性:noun
