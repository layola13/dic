assume: /əˈsuːm/ (US), /əˈsjuːm/ (UK) | v. | 假定，假设；承担；呈现 | To suppose to be the case, without proof; to take or begin to have (power or responsibility); to begin to have a particular quality or appearance |  She assumed he was coming, so she was surprised when he didn't show up. 她假设他会来，所以当他没有出现时，她很惊讶。| synonyms: presume, suppose, take on, adopt | antonyms: doubt, question, relinquish | verb
