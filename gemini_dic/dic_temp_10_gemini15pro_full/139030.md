oaklet:/ˈoʊklɪt/| n. |小橡树，橡树苗|A small or young oak tree. |例句：The oaklet swayed gently in the breeze. 小橡树在微风中轻轻摇曳。

例句：He planted an oaklet in his backyard. 他在后院种了一棵小橡树。|近义词：sapling, seedling |反义词:mature oak, ancient oak|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From oak + -let (diminutive suffix).  The suffix "-let" comes from Old French "-et" or "-ette," which in turn derives from the Latin diminutive suffix "-ellus," "-ella," "-ellum."
