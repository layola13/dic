as a matter of fact:/æz ə ˈmætər əv fækt/
adverb|事实上，其实，实际上|Used to introduce a statement that contradicts something previously said or assumed, or adds to a previous statement, often providing surprising information.|I thought he was from California, but as a matter of fact, he's from New York. 我以为他来自加利福尼亚，但实际上他来自纽约。|In fact, actually, indeed|Not applicable|adverb
