ding-dong:/ˈdɪŋ dɒŋ/ (UK), /ˈdɪŋ dɑːŋ/ (US)| n., int., adv. |叮咚声，门铃声|The sound of a doorbell or a similar chime.  Also used as an interjection to represent this sound, or adverbially to describe something happening repeatedly or rhythmically.|例句：The doorbell went ding-dong. 门铃响了叮咚一声。|近义词：chime, ring, jingle|反义词:silence, quiet|noun
