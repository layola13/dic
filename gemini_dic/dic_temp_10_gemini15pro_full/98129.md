insanely:/ɪnˈseɪnli/| adv.|疯狂地；极其地；非常地|Extremely; to an excessive degree.  It describes something done to a wild, unreasonable, or excessive degree. |The music was insanely loud.  音乐吵得要命。|
The heat was insanely oppressive.  酷热令人难以忍受。|近义词：madly, crazily, extremely, ridiculously|反义词：sanely, reasonably, moderately|词性:副词

词源:
From insane + -ly.  "Insane" comes from the Latin "insanus," meaning "not healthy," composed of "in-" (not) and "sanus" (healthy).
