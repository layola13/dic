monothetic:/ˌmɒnəˈθɛtɪk/ | adj. |单系的，单性状的 | Formed or defined by having a single, specified property or characteristic.  Relating to a classification in which group membership is based on possession of a single specified trait. | Example: A monothetic group of bacteria might be defined by their ability to ferment lactose.  单系的细菌群可以根据它们发酵乳糖的能力来定义。

Example:  The traditional approach to classifying organisms has been monothetic.  传统的生物分类方法是单系的。 | 近义词: homogeneous, uniform | 反义词: polythetic, heterogeneous | 词性: adjective
