adj.

extremely lonely and unhappy; desolate or dreary

例句：The forelonely landscape seemed to mirror her inner sadness.  荒凉的景色似乎反映了她内心的悲伤。

近义词：desolate, forlorn, lonesome, isolated
反义词：cheerful, happy, sociable, accompanied
词性: 形容词
