abound-with:/əˈbaʊnd wɪð/| v.|富于，充满|To exist in large numbers or amounts; to be plentiful; to be filled with something.|例句：The forests abound with wildlife. 森林里充满了野生动物。

The lake abounds with fish.  这个湖里有很多鱼。|近义词：teem with, be filled with, overflow with, be rife with, be replete with|反义词:lack, be deficient in|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *abunder, from Latin abundare "to overflow, abound," from ab- "from, away from" + undare "to rise in waves," from unda "wave."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
This area abounds with natural resources. = 这个地区自然资源丰富。
The garden abounds with colorful flowers. = 花园里开满了五颜六色的花。

一般过去时:
The river abounded with salmon in the past. = 过去这条河里有很多鲑鱼。
The market abounded with fresh produce during the summer. = 夏天市场上有很多新鲜农产品。

现在完成时:

过去完成时:
