overhumble:/ˈoʊvərˈhʌmbəl/| adj.|过分谦逊的，过谦的|Excessively humble; too modest.|He gave an overhumble acceptance speech, downplaying his significant contributions to the project. 他发表了过分谦逊的获奖感言，淡化了他在项目中的重要贡献。|近义词：excessively modest, unduly humble, self-deprecating|反义词：arrogant, conceited, boastful|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From over- + humble.  "Over-" is an Old English prefix meaning "above, excessively." "Humble" comes from the Old French "huble," ultimately from the Latin "humilis" meaning "low, lowly, humble."
