ETYMOLOGY->
From heat + -ed + -ness.  Heat (from Old English *hætu) refers to high temperature. The suffix "-ed" forms adjectives from nouns, indicating a state or quality.  The suffix "-ness" forms nouns from adjectives, indicating a state, quality, or condition.
