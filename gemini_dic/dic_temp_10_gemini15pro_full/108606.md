leadden:/ˈlɛdn/| adj.|铅制的；沉重的；阴沉的|Made of lead; heavy, dull, or gloomy. |The sky was leaden and threatened rain. 天空阴沉，预兆着要下雨。|近义词：gray, heavy, dull, gloomy, depressing|反义词：bright, cheerful, light|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *leden, from Old English lēaden (“made of lead; leaden”), from lēad (“lead”).
