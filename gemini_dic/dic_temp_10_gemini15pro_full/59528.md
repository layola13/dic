eat-in:/ˈiːtɪn/| adj. n.|在家吃的，堂食的；可供堂食的（餐厅）；在家吃的饭菜|A meal eaten at home, or a restaurant offering the option to eat on the premises.|We decided to have an eat-in tonight instead of going out. 我们今晚决定在家吃，而不是出去吃。|近义词：takeaway, takeout, carryout|反义词: eat-out, dine out|adjective, noun
