honourless:/ˈɒnələs/ (UK) /ˈɑːnərləs/ (US)| adj.|不名誉的，无耻的，卑鄙的|Without honour; shameful; despicable.|He committed an honourless act of betrayal. 他犯下了一个不名誉的背叛行为。|The honourless thief stole from the poor.  这个无耻的贼偷穷人的东西。|近义词：shameful, disgraceful, dishonorable, ignoble, base|反义词：honourable, noble, reputable|词性:形容词

词源: From "honour" (from Old French "onor," ultimately from Latin "honor") + "-less" (a native English suffix meaning "without").
