bowbell:/ˈbəʊˌbɛl/| n.|伦敦佬，伦敦东区居民|A Bow Bell cockney, referring to someone born within earshot of the Bow Bells (the bells of St. Mary-le-Bow church in Cheapside, London).|例句：He's a true Bow Bell, born and bred in the East End. 他是个地地道道的伦敦佬，在东区出生长大。

例句：She may sound like a Bow Bell, but she actually grew up in Essex. 她可能听起来像个伦敦东区的人，但她实际上是在埃塞克斯郡长大的。|近义词：cockney, East Ender|反义词:None|词性:noun
