hypochlorhydric:/ˌhaɪpoʊˌklɔːrˈhaɪdrɪk/
adjective
次氯酸过低的；胃酸过低的
deficient in hydrochloric acid (especially in the gastric juice)
Example: Hypochlorhydric conditions can impair digestion. 胃酸过低的情况会损害消化。
Example:  The doctor diagnosed him with a hypochlorhydric stomach. 医生诊断他患有胃酸过低的症状。
Synonym: achlorhydric, anacid
Antonym: hyperchlorhydric
词性: adjective
