bristly:/ˈbrɪsli/ (US), /ˈbrɪs.li/ (UK)| adj.|毛茸茸的；具刚毛的；易怒的|Covered with short, stiff hairs; easily annoyed or offended.|The cactus was covered in bristly spines.  仙人掌上覆盖着粗硬的刺。

The old man had a bristly beard. 老人的胡子很粗硬。

He gave me a bristly reception. 他对我的接待很不友好。|近义词：prickly, spiky, hairy, irritable, touchy|反义词:smooth, soft, even-tempered, affable|词性:adjective

词源:
From Old English *byrstlic, from byrst "bristle" (related to burst), + -lic "-ly."
