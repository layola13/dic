courbaril:/ˈkʊər.bə.rɪl/ | n. | 库巴利尔，西印度苦树，南美树脂 | A large tropical American tree (Hymenaea courbaril) of the legume family, yielding a hard resinous copal and edible pods.  It is also known as West Indian locust, jatoba, or Brazilian copal. | The courbaril tree provides valuable timber.  库巴利尔树提供珍贵的木材。| Synonym: jatoba, West Indian locust, Brazilian copal | Antonym: N/A | Noun

courbaril:/ˈkʊər.bə.rɪl/ | n. | 库巴利尔树脂 | The hard, resinous copal obtained from the courbaril tree. | Courbaril is used in varnishes and other finishes. 库巴利尔树脂用于清漆和其他饰面。| Synonym:  copal, South American copal | Antonym: N/A | Noun
