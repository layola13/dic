disengage:/dɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒ/
verb
脱离，分开，解脱
to separate or release (something or someone) from something that it or they are attached to
The soldiers disengaged from the enemy. 士兵们脱离了敌人。
detach, disconnect, extricate
attach, connect, engage
