cadent:/ˈkeɪd(ə)nt/| adj. n.|下降的；抑扬顿挫的；终止式；华彩乐段|Falling rhythmically; having a measured and falling rhythm; a concluding flourish or embellishment.  A cadent phrase in poetry. |Her voice had a beautiful, cadent quality. 她的声音优美而抑扬顿挫。|近义词：falling, descending, declining, rhythmic, melodious|反义词：rising, ascending, monotone|词性: adjective, noun
Etymology: From Latin cadent- (stem of cadēns), present participle of cadere  "to fall."
