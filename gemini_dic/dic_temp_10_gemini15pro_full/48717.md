defied:/dɪˈfaɪd/| v.| 违抗，反抗，蔑视|resisted or refused to obey.  The tiny country defied the superpower's threats.|例句：He defied his parents and dropped out of school. 他违抗父母，辍学了。

例句：The mountain climbers defied the blizzard and reached the summit. 登山者不顾暴风雪，最终到达了顶峰。

近义词：resisted, disobeyed, challenged, opposed|反义词:obeyed, submitted, yielded|词性:动词
