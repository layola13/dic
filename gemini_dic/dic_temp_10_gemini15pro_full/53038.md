dioeciousness:/daɪˈiːʃəsnəs/
|n.|雌雄异株性|The condition of having separate male and female individuals.|Holly trees exhibit dioeciousness, meaning you need a male and female plant to produce berries. 冬青树表现出雌雄异株性，这意味着你需要雄性和雌性植物才能产生浆果。|近义词: gonochorism|反义词: monoeciousness, hermaphroditism|词性: 名词

dioecious:/daɪˈiːʃəs/
|adj.|雌雄异株的|Having male and female reproductive organs in separate individuals.|The ginkgo tree is dioecious. 银杏树是雌雄异株的。|近义词: gonochoric|反义词: monoecious, hermaphroditic|词性: 形容词
