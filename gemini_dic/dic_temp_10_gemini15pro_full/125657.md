mummychog:/ˈmʌmiˌtʃɒɡ/ | n. | 木乃伊狗; (尤指儿童用语)包得很严实的人 |A child's term for a person wrapped up warmly, like a mummy, often used playfully or affectionately.  It combines "mummy" (referring to the Egyptian preserved body wrapped in bandages) and "chog," which doesn't have an independent meaning and likely serves to create a rhyming, child-friendly sound. | "Look at you, all wrapped up like a little mummychog!" 看看你，把自己裹得像个小木乃伊狗！| 近义词: bundle, snugglebug | 反义词: (No direct opposite, perhaps) exposed, uncovered | 词性: noun
