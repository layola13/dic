derange:/dɪˈreɪndʒ/ | v. | 使精神错乱；扰乱；打乱 | to make someone mentally ill; to disturb the order or arrangement of something | The fever seemed to have deranged his mind. 发烧似乎使他精神错乱了。|  The furniture had been deranged by the removal men. 家具被搬运工弄乱了。 | synonyms: unbalance, unsettle, disturb, disarrange, disorder | antonyms: organize, arrange, order, calm, soothe | verb

derangement:/dɪˈreɪndʒmənt/ | n. | 精神错乱；扰乱；混乱 | the state of being mentally ill; a disordered or confused state | He suffered from mental derangement. 他患有精神错乱。| The derangement of her plans caused her much stress.  她的计划被打乱了，这给她带来了很大的压力。| synonyms: insanity, madness, disorder, chaos, confusion | antonyms: order, organization, sanity, stability, normalcy | noun
