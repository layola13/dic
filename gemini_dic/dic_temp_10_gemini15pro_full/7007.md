android:/ˈændrɔɪd/| n.|安卓，机器人|A mobile operating system developed by Google, based on a modified version of the Linux kernel and designed primarily for touchscreen mobile devices such as smartphones and tablets.|例句：He bought a new Android phone. 他买了一部新的安卓手机。|例句：Most apps are available on both iOS and Android. 大多数应用程序在 iOS 和 Android 系统上都可用。|近义词：robot, automaton|反义词:human, organic|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Ancient Greek ἀνδρός (andrós, “of a man, masculine”) +‎ -oid (“like, resembling”). Coined by French writer Auguste Villiers de l'Isle-Adam in his 1886 novel L'Ève future ("The Future Eve").
