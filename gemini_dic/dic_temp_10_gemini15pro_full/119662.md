middleware:/ˈmɪdəlweər/| n.|中间件|Software that acts as a bridge between an operating system or database and applications, especially on a network.|Example 1: The middleware allows different software applications to communicate and share data.  中间件允许不同的软件应用程序进行通信和共享数据。

Example 2:  We use middleware to connect our legacy systems with newer cloud-based applications. 我们使用中间件来连接我们的遗留系统和新的基于云的应用程序。|近义词：interface, connector, bridge|反义词: (No direct antonym exists, potentially "standalone application")|词性:noun
