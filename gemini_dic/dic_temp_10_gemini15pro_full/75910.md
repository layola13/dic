funebrious:/fjuːˈnɪːbriəs/| adj.|悲伤的，凄凉的，丧葬的|Gloomy, mournful, or suggestive of funerals.  Relating to or suggestive of funerals or mourning.|The old house had a funebrious atmosphere, with its dark curtains and dusty furniture.  这栋老房子弥漫着悲伤的气氛，到处是深色的窗帘和满是灰尘的家具。|Synonyms: mournful, somber, doleful, lugubrious, gloomy|Antonyms: cheerful, joyous, happy, festive|Etymology: From Latin *fūnebris* "of a funeral," from *fūnus* "funeral."
