annoyed:/əˈnɔɪd/ | adj. | 恼火的，生气的 | Feeling slightly angry or impatient. |  I was annoyed by the constant interruptions. 我被不断的打扰惹恼了。

She was annoyed with him for being late. 她因为他迟到而生他的气。

| 近义词: irritated, vexed, irked, bothered, displeased | 反义词: pleased, delighted, happy, content |词性:形容词

PHRASE->
annoyed with someone: 生某人的气  ( I'm annoyed with him for forgetting my birthday.)
annoyed at/about something: 对某事生气 (She was annoyed about the delay.)
annoyed by something: 被某事惹恼 (He was annoyed by the constant noise.)
get annoyed: 生气 (I get annoyed easily.)
slightly annoyed:  有点生气 (I was slightly annoyed by his comment.)


注意事项:  annoyed 通常用来描述轻微的恼怒或不耐烦，而不是强烈的愤怒。
