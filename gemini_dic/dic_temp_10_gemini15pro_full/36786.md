colocynthin:/ˌkɒləˈsɪnθɪn/ | n. | 药葫芦素，药葫芦苦素 | A purgative glucoside obtained from the pulp of the colocynth.  It is a powerful cathartic. | Colocynthin is used in some traditional medicines as a laxative. 药葫芦素在一些传统药物中用作泻药。|  Colocynthin can cause severe abdominal cramping. 药葫芦素会导致严重的腹部绞痛。 | Synonyms:  purgative, cathartic | Antonyms:  antidiarrheal, constipating | noun
