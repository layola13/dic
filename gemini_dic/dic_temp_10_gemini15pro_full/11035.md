arcadic:/ɑːrˈkeɪdɪk/ | adj. | 田园诗般的；世外桃源的；快乐无忧的 | Relating to or resembling Arcadia, a region in ancient Greece, traditionally portrayed as a rural paradise; simple and pleasant, especially in a way that suggests a return to a past golden age. |  The painting depicted an arcadic scene of shepherds and nymphs. 这幅画描绘了一个牧羊人和仙女的田园景象。| synonyms: pastoral, idyllic, bucolic, rustic, utopian | antonyms: urban, modern, sophisticated, complex, stressful | 词性: 形容词
