mini-condominiums:/ˈmɪniˌkɒndəˈmɪniəmz/ (US) /ˈmɪniˌkɒndəˈmɪniəmz/ (UK)| n. |小型公寓；迷你公寓|A smaller than average condominium unit, often found in densely populated urban areas.  These units often prioritize affordability and efficient use of space.|Example:  Mini-condominiums are becoming increasingly popular in city centers due to their lower price point. 小型公寓因为价格较低，在市中心越来越受欢迎。

Example: She decided to invest in a mini-condominium near her workplace. 她决定在她工作地点附近投资一套小型公寓。|近义词：studio apartment, small apartment, micro-apartment|反义词: large apartment, mansion, house|词性:noun
