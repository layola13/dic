inrushings:/ˈɪnrʌʃɪŋz/| n. |涌入，涌进；突然涌入的事物|A sudden influx or invasion of something, often a fluid or a large number of people or things.  It describes the act of rushing in. |The inrushings of cold air chilled the room. 冷空气涌入房间，使房间变冷了。|The inrushings of refugees strained the resources of the small town.  涌入的难民使小镇的资源紧张。| influx, surge, flood, inflow | outflow, ebb, departure | noun
