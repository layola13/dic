backspace:/ˈbækspeɪs/| n. v.|退格键，删除键|A key on a computer keyboard that moves the cursor one position backward and deletes the preceding character.  It returns the typing cursor one position backwards, deleting the character in that position. |She hit the backspace key to correct her typo. 她按了退格键来纠正她的打字错误。|Synonyms: delete key, erase key|Antonyms:  N/A|noun/verb
