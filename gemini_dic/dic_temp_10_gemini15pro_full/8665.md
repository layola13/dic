anticonstitutionally:/ˌæntikɑːnstɪˈtuːʃənəli/
adv. |违宪地；违反宪法地|In a manner that violates or contradicts the principles or provisions of a constitution. | The law was deemed to have been applied anticonstitutionally.  该法律被认为是违宪适用的。|
近义词: illegally, unlawfully, illegitimately
反义词: constitutionally, legally, lawfully
词性: adverb

词源:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to  (来自希腊语 anti-)
constitution:  the system of fundamental principles according to which a nation, state, corporation, or the like, is governed. (来自拉丁语 constitutio)
-al (suffix): relating to, characteristic of
-ly (suffix): forms adverbs from adjectives
