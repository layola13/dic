non-volatile:/ˌnɒnˈvɒlətaɪl/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈvɑːlətəl/| adj.|非易失性的，非挥发性的|Not easily evaporated or vaporized; retaining data even when power is removed. |This memory chip is non-volatile, so it retains data even after the computer is turned off.  这个内存芯片是非易失性的，即使电脑关机后也能保存数据。| The flash drive uses non-volatile memory.  闪存驱动器使用非易失性存储器。|近义词：permanent, persistent, stable|反义词: volatile, temporary|词性: adjective

non-volatile:/ˌnɒnˈvɒlətaɪl/ (US) /ˌnɑːnˈvɑːlətəl/| n.|非挥发物|A substance that does not readily evaporate. |The non-volatiles in the perfume give it a lasting scent.  香水中不挥发的成分赋予了它持久的香味。|  Many non-volatiles are left behind after the distillation process. 许多非挥发物在蒸馏过程后被留下。|近义词：residue, solid|反义词: volatile|词性: noun
