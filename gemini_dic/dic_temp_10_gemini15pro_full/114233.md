mailability:/ˈmeɪləˈbɪləti/
n.
可邮寄性，适合邮寄
The quality or condition of being mailable; suitability for sending by mail.
The mailability of the package depends on its size and weight.  这个包裹的可邮寄性取决于它的大小和重量。
suitability for mailing, postage
non-mailability
noun
