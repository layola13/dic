nonclass:/nɑːnˈklæs/ | adj. | 非阶级的，不分阶级的 | Not divided into social classes; without class distinctions. |  The speaker advocated for a nonclass society. 演讲者提倡一个无阶级社会。 |  The event aimed to create a nonclass atmosphere.  这次活动旨在营造一种不分阶级的氛围。| 近义词: classless, egalitarian | 反义词: class-based, stratified | 词性: adjective

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "class" (noun).  The prefix "non-" comes from Latin "non". "Class" comes from Old French "classe" and ultimately from Latin "classis" (a division of the Roman people).
