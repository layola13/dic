demandingly:/dɪˈmændɪŋli/ | adv. | 苛求地，要求高地 | in a demanding way; requiring a lot of effort, skill, or attention | The audience listened demandingly, eager for every detail. 观众聚精会神地听着，渴望了解每一个细节。| exacting, challenging | accommodating, undemanding | adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From demanding + -ly.  Demanding from demand + -ing. Demand from Old French demander (to ask, request), from Latin demandare (to entrust, commit, enjoin), from de- (down, completely) + mandare (to entrust, commit).
