algaroba:/ˌælɡəˈroʊbə/ | n. | 角豆树，刺槐豆 | A thorny tree (Prosopis juliflora) of tropical America, having fragrant yellow flowers and long pods containing a sweet pulp. | The algaroba tree is highly adaptable and can grow in arid conditions. 角豆树适应性很强，可以在干旱条件下生长。

The algaroba pods are a valuable source of food for livestock. 角豆树的豆荚是牲畜的宝贵食物来源。| 近义词: mesquite, kiawe | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific tree) | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Arabic "al-kharūbah" (الخروب) meaning "carob tree". The name was later applied to the similar-looking Prosopis species in the Americas.
