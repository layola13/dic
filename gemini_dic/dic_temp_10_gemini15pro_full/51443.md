dethronement:/diːˈθroʊnmənt/
n.
废黜；罢免；推翻
The act of removing a king or queen from power.  The dethronement of the king led to a period of instability.
The dethronement of the former CEO shocked the company.
removal, deposition, ousting
enthronement, coronation
noun
