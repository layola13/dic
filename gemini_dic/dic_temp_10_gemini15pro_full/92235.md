hypertragical: /ˌhaɪpərˈtrædʒɪkəl/
adjective
极其悲惨的，过于悲剧的
Exaggeratedly or excessively tragical.  Overly dramatic or sorrowful.
She launched into a hypertragical monologue about her lost puppy. 她开始了一段关于她丢失的小狗的极其悲惨的独白。
The play was so hypertragical it bordered on parody.  这出戏太悲惨了，近乎滑稽剧。
近义词: melodramatic, overwrought, histrionic
反义词: comedic, lighthearted, joyful
词性: adjective
词源:  From "hyper-" (Greek, meaning "over, excessive") + "tragical" (from Greek "tragōidia" meaning "tragedy").
