heterocercy:/ˌhɛtərəˈsɜːrsi/
n.
异尾性
The condition of having a tail that is asymmetrical, especially in certain fish, where the vertebral column extends into the upper lobe of the caudal fin.
Certain fish exhibit heterocercy, with their spine extending into the upper lobe of the tail fin.  某些鱼类表现出异尾性，它们的脊椎延伸到尾鳍的上叶。
近义词: asymmetry (in tail structure)
反义词: homocercy
词性: noun
