infinitesimally:/ˌɪnfɪnɪˈtɛsɪməli/| adv.| 极微地；无限小地| In a manner that is so small as to be almost immeasurable; approaching zero. | The dose was increased infinitesimally. 剂量被极微地增加了。| The car moved infinitesimally forward. 汽车极微地向前移动了一下。| 近义词: microscopically, minutely, negligibly | 反义词: hugely, enormously, vastly | 词性: 副词

词源: From Late Latin *infinitesimalis, from infinit(us) ("infinite") + -esimus (ordinal number suffix, "hundredth," extended to other fractions) + -alis (adjective suffix).
