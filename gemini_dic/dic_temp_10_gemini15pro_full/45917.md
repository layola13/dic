cutscene: /ˈkʌtˌsiːn/ | n. | 过场动画；剧情动画 | A non-interactive sequence in a video game or movie that advances the plot or provides background information. | Example: The cutscene explained the protagonist's backstory.  过场动画解释了主角的背景故事。

Example: I skipped the cutscene because I wanted to get back to playing. 我跳过了过场动画，因为我想回去玩游戏。| 近义词: cinematic, interlude, intermission | 反义词: gameplay, interactive sequence | 词性: noun
