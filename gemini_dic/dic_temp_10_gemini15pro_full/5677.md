altruists: /ˈæltruːɪsts/ | n. | 利他主义者；无私的人 | People who practice altruism, prioritizing the well-being of others over their own. | Mother Teresa is often cited as an example of an altruist.  特蕾莎修女经常被引用为利他主义者的例子。|  philanthropists, humanitarians | egoists, narcissists | noun

Etymology: From French *altruiste*, from Italian *altrui* "of others," from Latin *alteri huic* "to this other."
