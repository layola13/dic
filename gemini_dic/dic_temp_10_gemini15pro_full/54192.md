disestablishmentarianism: /dɪsɪˌstæblɪʃmənˈtɛəriənɪzəm/ | n. | 解除国教主义；政教分离主义 | The belief that a state should not have an established religion; advocating the separation of church and state. |  The prime minister faced opposition from those who supported disestablishmentarianism. 首相面临着来自支持政教分离主义者的反对。

Disestablishmentarianism was a major political issue in 19th-century England. 政教分离主义是19世纪英国的一个主要政治问题。 | 近义词: secularism, separationism | 反义词: establishmentarianism | 词性: 名词
