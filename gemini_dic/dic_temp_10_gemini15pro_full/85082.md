hard-won:/ˌhɑːrdˈwʌn/
adjective
来之不易的；辛苦赢得的
achieved or obtained only after a lot of effort and difficulty.
The team celebrated their hard-won victory.  这个团队庆祝他们来之不易的胜利。
After years of struggle, she finally achieved her hard-won goal. 经过多年的奋斗，她终于实现了来之不易的目标。
近义词:  laboriously earned, well-earned, hardly won, dearly bought
反义词: easily gained, easily won

词性: adjective
