grousey:/ˈɡraʊsi/| adj.|抱怨的，牢骚满腹的|Inclined to complain; irritable.|He's been grousey all day because he didn't get enough sleep.|近义词：grumpy, irritable, crabby, cantankerous|反义词：cheerful, happy, pleasant|词性:形容词

词源: From grouse (n.) + -y.  The noun "grouse" originally referred to the bird's characteristic low murmuring sound, which was perceived as a complaint.  This sense extended to human complaining, and the adjective "grousey" followed.
