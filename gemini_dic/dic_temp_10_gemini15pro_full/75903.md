fundraise:/ˈfʌndreɪz/| v.|筹款；募捐|To collect money for a cause or organization. |例句：The school is fundraising for a new library. 学校正在为新图书馆筹款。

例句：They held a bake sale to fundraise for the charity. 他们举办了烘焙义卖来为慈善机构筹款。|近义词：solicit, collect, canvass|反义词:spend, donate (if considered from the opposite perspective of giving rather than receiving funds)|词性:动词

fundraising:/ˈfʌndreɪzɪŋ/| n.|筹款；募捐活动|The act of collecting money for a cause or organization. |例句：Fundraising is essential for non-profit organizations. 筹款对非营利组织至关重要。

例句：The fundraising event was a huge success. 筹款活动取得了巨大成功。|近义词：solicitation, collection|反义词:spending, disbursement (if considered as the opposite action of distributing funds)|词性:名词
