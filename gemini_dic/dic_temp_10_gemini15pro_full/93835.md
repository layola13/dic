immiscibility:/ˌɪmɪsɪˈbɪləti/
n.
不混溶性，不相溶性
The immiscibility of oil and water is a common observation.
油和水的不混溶性是一个常见的现象。
immiscibility refers to the inability of two liquids to mix together to form a homogeneous solution.
不混溶性是指两种液体不能混合在一起形成均匀溶液的性质。
例句：The immiscibility of oil and water causes them to separate into distinct layers. 油和水的不混溶性使它们分离成不同的层。
The experiment demonstrated the immiscibility of the two solvents.  这个实验证明了两种溶剂的不混溶性。

近义词：incompatibility, non-miscibility
反义词：miscibility, compatibility
词性：noun
