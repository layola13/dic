desiccator:/ˈdɛsɪkeɪtər/
n.
干燥器，保干器
A sealable container used for preserving moisture-sensitive items or for drying out substances.
The chemist stored the hygroscopic chemical in a desiccator.  化学家将吸湿性化学物质储存在干燥器中。

近义词:drying chamber, dehydrator
反义词:humidifier
