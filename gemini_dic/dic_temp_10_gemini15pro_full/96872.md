inexistent:/ˌɪnɪɡˈzɪstənt/| adj.|不存在的，虚构的|Not existing; having no existence.|例句：The problem is virtually nonexistent. 这个问题实际上不存在。|例句：Unicorns are inexistent creatures. 独角兽是不存在的生物。|近义词：nonexistent, missing, absent, lacking|反义词：existent, existing, present, real|词性:形容词
Etymology: From in- "not" + existent, from Latin existentem (nominative existens), present participle of existere "stand forth, emerge; appear, be visible; exist, be," from ex- "out" + sistere "cause to stand," frequentative of stare "to stand," from PIE root *sta- "to stand, make or be firm."
