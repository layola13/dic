acquainting:/əˈkweɪntɪŋ/
v.
使熟悉；使认识
The purpose of the meeting is acquainting new employees with company policies.  这次会议的目的是让新员工熟悉公司政策。
He spent the first few days acquainting himself with the new city. 他花了最初几天时间熟悉这个新城市。

近义词:familiarizing, informing, introducing
反义词:estranging, alienating
词性:动词(present participle of acquaint)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *acquainten*, from Old French *acointier*, from Vulgar Latin *accognitare*, from Latin *cognitus* (known), past participle of *cognoscere* (to get to know).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ǵneh₃-(t)-* (to know).
