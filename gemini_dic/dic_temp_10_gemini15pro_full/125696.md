munerary:/ˈmjuːnərɛri/ /ˈmjuːnəreri/
adjective
丧葬的，墓葬的，陪葬的
Relating to gifts or services provided for the dead, especially those placed in tombs.
The archaeologist discovered several munerary objects in the tomb, including pottery and jewelry.  考古学家在墓中发现了几件陪葬品，包括陶器和珠宝。
The family left munerary offerings of food and drink at the grave. 家人在坟前留下了食物和饮料作为陪葬品。
Synonyms:  funerary, sepulchral, tomb
Antonyms:  living, vital

Etymology:
From Latin *munerarius*, from *munus* ("gift, office, duty") + *-arius* (suffix forming adjectives).
