dependence:/dɪˈpɛndəns/| n. |依赖，依靠|His dependence on drugs ruined his life. 他对毒品的依赖毁了他的人生。|近义词: reliance, addiction (for negative dependence)|反义词: independence | noun

Etymology: From Old French *dependre*, from Latin *dēpendere* "to hang down, hang from," from *dē* "down" + *pendēre* "to hang."
