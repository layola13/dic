diffusibility:/ˌdɪfjuːzəˈbɪləti/ | n. |扩散性，弥散性|The property of being able to spread or be spread easily. | The diffusibility of gases is much greater than that of liquids. 气体的扩散性比液体的扩散性大得多。| The diffusibility of the new technology is a key factor in its success. 新技术的扩散性是其成功的关键因素。| synonyms: dispersibility, spreadability, permeability | antonyms: impermeability, impenetrability | noun
