Also: a bay of the Atlantic, in Rhode Island.|例句1：The Narragansett tribe has a rich history. 纳拉甘西特部落有着悠久的历史。

例句2：He studies the Narragansett language. 他研究纳拉甘西特语。|近义词：Rhode Islander (for the people), Algonquian (for the language)|反义词: N/A|词性:noun, adjective
