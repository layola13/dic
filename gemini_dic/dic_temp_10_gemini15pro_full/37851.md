competitivenesses:/kəmˈpɛtɪtɪvnɪsɪz/
n.
竞争力（复数）
The state or quality of being competitive; the ability to compete successfully.  This word is less commonly used in the plural form.  It is generally used in the singular (competitiveness).
The company is investing heavily in research and development to improve its competitivenesses in the global market. 这家公司正在大力投资研发以提高其在全球市场的竞争力。（这个例句虽然语法正确，但听起来不太自然，单数形式更常见）
The various competitivenesses of the different teams made for an exciting tournament. 各个队伍的不同竞争力使得锦标赛激动人心。（这个例句虽然语法正确，但听起来不太自然，单数形式更常见）
Near Synonyms:  capabilities, competencies, strengths, advantages, proficiencies
Antonyms:  weaknesses, disadvantages, deficiencies, inadequacies
