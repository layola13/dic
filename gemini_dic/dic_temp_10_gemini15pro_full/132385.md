nondemobilizations: /ˌnɒndiːˌmoʊbɪlaɪˈzeɪʃənz/ | n. | 非复员，不遣散 | The act of not demobilizing military personnel or equipment. This implies a continued state of military readiness or preparation, often in anticipation of further conflict or prolonged tension. |  The nondemobilizations after the ceasefire led to continued economic strain. 停火后的非复员导致经济持续紧张。

The government's nondemobilizations signaled a potential escalation of the conflict. 政府的非复员预示着冲突可能升级。 | Near synonyms:  retention,  maintenance of forces, continued deployment | Antonyms: demobilization, discharge,  stand-down | noun
