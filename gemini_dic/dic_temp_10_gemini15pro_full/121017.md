miscolor:/ˌmɪsˈkʌlər/ |v. |使变色；染色不当；给…涂上不合适的颜色|To give something the wrong color or to color something badly.  |The artist miscolored the sky, making it green instead of blue. 艺术家把天空涂错了颜色，把它涂成了绿色而不是蓝色。|mispaint, discolor, stain|color correctly, paint correctly|verb
miscolor:/ˌmɪsˈkʌlər/ |n.|染色不当；错误的颜色|An instance of giving something the wrong color. |The miscolor of the fabric ruined the entire dress.  布料的染色不当毁了整条裙子。|discoloration, stain, blemish|correct color, proper color|noun
