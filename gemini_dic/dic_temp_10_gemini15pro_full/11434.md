argonauta:/ˌɑːrɡəˈnɔːtə/ | n. | 船蛸；纸鹦鹉螺 | A cephalopod mollusk of the genus Argonauta, related to the octopus, the female of which secretes a thin, papery shell. |  The argonaut, unlike other octopuses, lives in the open ocean. 船蛸不像其他章鱼，生活在广阔的海洋中。 |  The argonaut uses its shell as a brood chamber and for buoyancy. 船蛸用它的壳作为育儿室和浮力工具。 |  近义词: paper nautilus, | 反义词: (No direct antonym, as it's a specific species) | 词性: noun
