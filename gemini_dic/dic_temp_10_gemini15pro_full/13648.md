attorney-general:/əˈtɜːrni ˈdʒɛn(ə)rəl/
n.
首席检察官；司法部长
The principal legal officer who represents a country or a state in legal matters.
The attorney-general filed charges against the corporation. 首席检察官对该公司提起诉讼。

attorneys-general:/əˈtɜːrni ˈdʒɛn(ə)rəlz/
n.
首席检察官们；司法部长们
Plural form of attorney-general.
