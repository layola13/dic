antithyroids:/ˌæntiˈθaɪrɔɪdz/ | n. | 抗甲状腺药 | Substances that inhibit the production of thyroid hormones. |  Patients with hyperthyroidism are often prescribed antithyroids.  甲状腺功能亢进症患者通常服用抗甲状腺药物。| Synonyms: thyroid inhibitors, goitrogens | Antonyms: thyroid hormones, thyroxine | noun


antithyroid:/ˌæntiˈθaɪrɔɪd/ | adj. | 抗甲状腺的 | Relating to or denoting substances that inhibit the production of thyroid hormones. |  Antithyroid medication helps to regulate thyroid hormone levels. 抗甲状腺药物有助于调节甲状腺激素水平。| Synonyms: goitrogenic, thyroid-suppressing | Antonyms: thyroid-stimulating | adjective
