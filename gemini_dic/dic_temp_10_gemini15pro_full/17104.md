batswana:/bɑːˈtswɑːnə/
n.
博茨瓦纳人; 博茨瓦纳国民
People from Botswana, a country in Southern Africa.
The Batswana are known for their rich cultural heritage. 博茨瓦纳人以其丰富的文化遗产而闻名。
Batswana music is often characterized by strong vocals and rhythmic drumming. 博茨瓦纳音乐的特点通常是强劲的歌声和有节奏的鼓声。
synonyms: citizens of Botswana, people of Botswana
antonyms: N/A (This is a nationality, there's no direct opposite)
