mimetical:/mɪˈmɛtɪkəl/| adj.|模仿的，拟态的|Relating to, characterized by, or exhibiting mimicry; imitative.|The mimetical insect blended perfectly with the bark of the tree. 这种善于模仿的昆虫与树皮完美地融合在一起。|近义词：imitative, mimic, aping|反义词：original, genuine|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Greek *mimētikos* "imitative," from *mimeisthai* "to imitate."
