assurable:/əˈʃʊərəbl/
adjective
可担保的；可保证的
Capable of being assured; that can be promised with confidence.
The company offers assurable quality on all its products.  这家公司对其所有产品提供质量保证。
This is an assurable outcome with proper planning.  通过适当的规划，这是一个可以保证的结果。
近义词:guaranteed, certain, reliable, dependable
反义词:uncertain, unreliable, doubtful, risky
词性:adjective
词源:From assure + -able, from Old French assurer "to assure, make certain," from a- "to" (see ad-) + sur "sure."
