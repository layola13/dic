methanol:/ˈmɛθənɒl/ (UK) /ˈmɛθənɔl/ (US)| n. |甲醇|A colorless, volatile, flammable liquid alcohol, CH3OH, formed by the destructive distillation of wood or synthesized from carbon monoxide and hydrogen, used as a solvent, antifreeze, fuel, and denaturant for ethyl alcohol.| Methanol is commonly used as a solvent in industrial processes. 甲醇通常在工业过程中用作溶剂。| Methanol is a highly toxic substance. 甲醇是一种剧毒物质。|synonyms: methyl alcohol, wood alcohol, wood spirit| antonyms: N/A | noun
