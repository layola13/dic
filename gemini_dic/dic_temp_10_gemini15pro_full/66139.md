|The exceptionalness of her talent was evident from a young age. 她非凡的才华从小就显而易见。|
|Her exceptionalness in academics earned her a full scholarship. 她在学术上的卓越成就为她赢得了全额奖学金。|

|词性: 名词|
|词源: From exceptional + -ness.  Exceptional comes from the Late Latin exceptionālis, from Latin exceptiō, meaning "exception."|
