dunk:/dʌŋk/| v. n.|扣篮，浸泡|To submerge something briefly in liquid.  To score by slamming a basketball through the hoop from above.|He dunked his donut in his coffee. 他把甜甜圈浸在了咖啡里。| He dunked the ball with authority. 他霸气地扣篮。 |近义词：dip, submerge, immerse, slam (basketball)|反义词:remove, withdraw|词性:动词, 名词

dunks:/dʌŋks/| n. v.|扣篮（复数），浸泡（复数）|Plural form of "dunk". Refers to multiple instances of dunking.|He made three dunks in the final quarter. 他在最后一节扣了三个篮。| She dunks the cookies in milk. 她把饼干浸在牛奶里。 |近义词：dips, submersions, immersions, slams (basketball)|反义词:removals, withdrawals|词性:名词, 动词

词源:
From Middle Dutch *dunken, frequentative of *dūken (“to dive, stoop, duck”).
