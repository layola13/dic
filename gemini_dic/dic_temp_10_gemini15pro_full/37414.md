commercializer:/kəˈmɜːʃəlaɪzər/
n.|商业化者，商品化者|A person or company that commercializes something, typically an invention or discovery, by developing and marketing it.|The university partnered with a commercializer to bring their groundbreaking research to market.|Synonyms: marketer, promoter, developer, entrepreneur|Antonyms: researcher, inventor, academic|Noun

commercialize:/kəˈmɜːʃəlaɪz/
v.|商业化，商品化|To make something commercial; to exploit or develop something for profit.|The company commercialized the new technology quickly.|Synonyms: market, promote, exploit, monetize|Antonyms: preserve, protect, conserve|Verb
