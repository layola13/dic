harmonica:/hɑːrˈmɒnɪkə/ (UK), /hɑːrˈmɑːnɪkə/ (US)| n.|口琴|A small rectangular wind instrument with a row of metal reeds fixed at one end, played by blowing or sucking air through it.|He played a melancholic tune on his harmonica. 他用口琴演奏了一曲忧郁的调子。|Synonyms: mouth organ, French harp, gob iron|Antonyms: None|Noun
