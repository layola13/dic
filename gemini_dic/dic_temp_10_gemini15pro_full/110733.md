lingam:/ˈlɪŋɡəm/| n. |林伽; 男根;|An object that is worshipped by Hindus and represents the god Shiva.  It is usually a tall, rounded stone.|The lingam is a sacred symbol in Hinduism. 林伽是印度教中的一个神圣象征。|The temple housed a large, black lingam. 这座寺庙里供奉着一尊巨大的黑色林伽。|Synonym: phallus|Antonym: yoni (although they are often considered complementary rather than opposite)|noun
