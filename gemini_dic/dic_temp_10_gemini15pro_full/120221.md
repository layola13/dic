minestrone:/ˌmɪnɪˈstrəʊni/ (UK), /ˌmɪnɪˈstroʊni/ (US)| n.|蔬菜通心粉汤|A thick Italian soup containing vegetables, pasta, and often beans and meat.|例句：We had minestrone soup for lunch. 我们午餐吃了蔬菜通心粉汤。

例句：My grandmother makes a delicious minestrone. 我祖母做美味的蔬菜通心粉汤。|近义词：vegetable soup, Italian soup|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "clear broth")|词性:noun
