half-ashamedly:/ˌhɑːf əˈʃeɪmɪdli/
half-ashamedly|adv.|略带羞愧地，半羞愧地|Feeling or showing slight shame or embarrassment.|He half-ashamedly admitted he'd been crying. 他略带羞愧地承认他一直在哭。|例句：She half-ashamedly peeked at the love letter. 她半羞愧地偷看了那封情书。|近义词：sheepishly, apologetically, guiltily|反义词：proudly, boldly, confidently|词性: 副词

half-ashamedly|词源|From "half" + "ashamed" + "-ly".  "Half" indicates a partial degree, "ashamed" refers to the feeling of shame, and "-ly" is an adverbial suffix.|
half-ashamedly|例句：He half-ashamedly confessed to eating the last cookie.  他半羞愧地承认自己吃了最后一块饼干。|
