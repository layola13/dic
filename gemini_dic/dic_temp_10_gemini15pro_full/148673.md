overtimidity:/ˌoʊvərˈtɪmɪdɪti/
n.
过度羞怯，过度胆怯
Excessive timidity or shyness.
Her overtimidity prevented her from speaking up in class.  她过度害羞，不敢在课堂上发言。
He missed many opportunities due to his overtimidity. 由于过度胆怯，他错过了很多机会。

近义词: shyness, bashfulness, diffidence, timorousness
反义词: boldness, confidence, audacity
