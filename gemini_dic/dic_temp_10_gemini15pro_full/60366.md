egret:/ˈiːɡrət, ˈɛɡrət/| n.|白鹭|A type of heron with long legs and a long neck, typically white in color.  It is known for its elegant appearance and is often found in wetlands. |例句：The egret stood motionless in the shallow water, waiting for a fish. 白鹭一动不动地站在浅水中，等待着鱼儿。

例句：We saw a beautiful egret wading in the marsh. 我们看到一只美丽的白鹭在沼泽地里涉水。|近义词：heron, crane|反义词:|noun
