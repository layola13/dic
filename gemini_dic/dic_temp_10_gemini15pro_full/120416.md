minisummits:/ˈmɪniˌsʌmɪts/
noun
小型峰会，小型首脑会议
A series of small summits or meetings, often focusing on a specific topic or involving a smaller group of participants than a full summit.
Several minisummits were held to address the regional crisis.  几个小型峰会召开，以解决区域危机。
The organization hosted a series of minisummits on climate change. 该组织举办了一系列关于气候变化的小型峰会。
synonyms: small conferences, meetings, gatherings
antonyms: large conferences, plenary sessions
n.
