nonmovable:/ˌnɒnˈmuːvəbl/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈmuːvəbəl/ (UK)
adjective
不可移动的；固定的
Not able to be moved.
The antique desk is nonmovable due to its size and weight. 这张古董桌子由于其尺寸和重量而无法移动。
The nonmovable property includes the land and the building on it.  不可移动的财产包括土地及土地上的建筑物。
近义词: fixed, immovable, stationary, permanent
反义词: movable, mobile, portable
词性: adjective
