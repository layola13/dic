diphtheria:/dɪfˈθɪəriə/ /dɪpˈθɪriə/| n.|白喉|An acute infectious disease of the throat and nose caused by the diphtheria bacterium and marked by formation of a false membrane lining the throat that can block respiration.|Diphtheria is a serious disease that can be fatal if left untreated. 白喉是一种严重的疾病，如果不治疗可能会致命。| Diphtheria used to be a common childhood illness. 白喉曾经是一种常见的儿童疾病。|Synonyms:  croup, angina|Antonyms: health, wellness|Noun
