nonphilosophic:/ˌnɒnˌfɪləˈsɒfɪk/
adjective
非哲学的
Not philosophical; not dealing with or related to philosophy.
His approach to the problem was entirely nonphilosophic, focusing on practical solutions rather than abstract theories. 他处理问题的方式完全是非哲学的，专注于实际的解决方案而不是抽象的理论。

The committee's nonphilosophic concerns were limited to budget and logistics.  委员会的非哲学关切仅限于预算和后勤。

近义词:non-philosophical, unphilosophical, pragmatic, practical
反义词:philosophical, theoretical, abstract
词性:adjective
