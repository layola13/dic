expectant:/ɪkˈspektənt/| adj.|期待的，期望的|Having or showing an excited feeling that something is about to happen, especially something pleasant and interesting.|The expectant mother felt a kick. 怀孕的母亲感到胎动。|近义词:anticipating, hopeful, waiting|反义词:uninterested, apathetic|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin *expectant- (stem of *expectāns), present participle of *expectāre “to await, look out for,” frequentative of exspectāre “to look out for, await, expect,” from ex- “out” + spectāre “to look at.”
