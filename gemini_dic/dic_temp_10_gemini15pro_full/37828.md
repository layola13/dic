compesce:/kəmˈpɛs/| vt.|压制，克制，抑制|To restrain or control, especially strong or undesirable impulses or feelings.|He struggled to compesce his anger. 他努力抑制自己的愤怒。|近义词：restrain, curb, suppress, repress, subdue, quell, check, control|反义词：indulge, unleash, release|verb

Etymology:
From Latin compēscō ("I restrain, curb, check, repress"), from com- ("together, completely") +‎ *pēscō, related to pāscō ("I feed, nourish, cherish").  The original sense may have been "to confine by feeding," hence "to tame, restrain."
