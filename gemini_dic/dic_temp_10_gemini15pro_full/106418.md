The imam read a passage from the Koran.  伊玛目朗读了古兰经中的一段。|近义词：Quran, Islamic scripture|反义词:None|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Arabic  قرآن (qurʼān, “recitation”), verbal noun of  قرأ (qaraʼa, “he recited, read”).
