oxidised:/ˈɒksɪdaɪzd/ (UK) /ˈɑːksɪdaɪzd/ (US)| adj.|氧化的，被氧化的|Combined with or having undergone a chemical reaction with oxygen.|The metal surface was oxidised, giving it a rusty appearance. 金属表面被氧化了，呈现出锈迹斑斑的样子。|Example: The oxidised apple turned brown. 被氧化的苹果变成了棕色。|Synonyms: rusted, corroded, tarnished|Antonyms: reduced, deoxidized|Adjective

oxidize:/ˈɒksɪdaɪz/ (UK) /ˈɑːksɪdaɪz/ (US)| v.|氧化，使氧化|To combine with oxygen; to make something combine with oxygen.|Iron oxidizes in the presence of air and moisture. 铁在空气和水分的存在下会氧化。|Example: The iron railing quickly oxidized in the damp air. 铁栏杆在潮湿的空气中迅速氧化。|Synonyms: rust, corrode, tarnish|Antonyms: reduce, deoxidize|Verb
