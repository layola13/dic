nightmarish:/ˈnaɪtˌmɛərɪʃ/ (US), /ˈnaɪtˌmeərɪʃ/ (UK)| adj.|噩梦般的，可怕的|Resembling a nightmare; extremely unpleasant, horrifying, or distressing.|The traffic was nightmarish. 交通状况糟透了。|The whole experience was nightmarish. 整个经历都像噩梦一样。|近义词：horrible, terrible, awful, dreadful, horrific|反义词：pleasant, delightful, wonderful|词性:形容词

词源: From nightmare + -ish.  "Nightmare" itself comes from Middle English "nihtmare," where "niht" means "night" and "mare" refers to a mythological demon or goblin believed to sit on people's chests and cause bad dreams.
