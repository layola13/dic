nonusuriousness:/ˌnɒnjuːˈʒʊəriəsnəs/
|n.|不放高利贷；合理利息|The nonusuriousness of the loan was confirmed by the bank. 银行确认了这笔贷款的合理利息。|English Explanation: The state or quality of not being usurious; the practice of lending money at a fair and reasonable rate of interest.|例句：The nonusuriousness of the loan terms attracted many borrowers.  这笔贷款的合理利息吸引了许多借款人。

例句：He emphasized the nonusuriousness of his lending practices. 他强调他的贷款行为不放高利贷。|近义词：fairness, reasonableness, legality|反义词：usuriousness, exploitation|词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + usurious + -ness.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not". "Usurious" comes from the Latin "usura" meaning "interest" and later "excessive interest".  "-ness" is a suffix used to form nouns expressing a state or quality.
