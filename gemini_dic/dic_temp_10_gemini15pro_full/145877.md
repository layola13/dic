overdesires:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈzaɪərz/
verb
过度渴望；欲望过强
To have an excessive or inordinate longing or craving for something.
He overdesires power and wealth. 他过度渴望权力和财富。
近义词: crave, covet, yearn for
反义词:  abstain from, forgo
词性: verb
