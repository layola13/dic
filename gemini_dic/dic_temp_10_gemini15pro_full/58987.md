duty: /ˈdjuːti/ (US) /ˈdʒuːti/ (UK) | n. | 责任，义务，职责；税；值班 | A moral or legal obligation; a responsibility.  Something that one is required or expected to do. A tax levied by a government on imported or exported goods. A period of work or service. | It is my duty to inform you of the risks.  我有责任告知你这些风险。 | responsibility, obligation, function, task | negligence, dereliction | noun
