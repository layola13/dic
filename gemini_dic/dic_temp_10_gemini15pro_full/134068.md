nonignorant:/ˌnɒnˈɪɡnərənt/| adj.|非无知的，有知识的，见多识广的|Not ignorant; possessing knowledge or awareness.  Characterized by or showing knowledge; not ignorant. |She made a nonignorant decision to invest in the company after thorough research. 她经过彻底的研究，做出了一个明智的决定，投资该公司。| The nonignorant public demanded answers from the government.  见多识广的公众要求政府给出答案。| 近义词: knowledgeable, informed, aware, erudite, learned | 反义词: ignorant, unaware, uninformed, uneducated | 词性: adjective

词源:
From non- + ignorant.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "ignorant" comes from the Latin "ignorantem," meaning "not knowing."
