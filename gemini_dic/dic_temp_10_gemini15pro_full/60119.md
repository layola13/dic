effacing:/ɪˈfeɪsɪŋ/
adjective
谦逊的，不引人注目的，使不明显的
Making oneself appear insignificant or inconspicuous; modest.
She has an effacing personality, preferring to listen rather than dominate the conversation. 她性格谦逊，喜欢倾听而不是主导谈话。
synonyms: modest, humble, unassuming, self-effacing, retiring, unobtrusive
antonyms: assertive, bold, confident, conspicuous, ostentatious
词性: 形容词

efface:/ɪˈfeɪs/
verb
擦掉，抹去，使…不明显
Erase (a mark) from a surface.
Time had effaced almost all signs of the struggle. 时间几乎抹去了所有斗争的痕迹。
synonyms: erase, obliterate, expunge, wipe out, delete, remove, rub out
antonyms: preserve, enhance
词性: 动词
