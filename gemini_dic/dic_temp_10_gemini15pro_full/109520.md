lese-majesty:/ˌlɛz ˈmædʒɪsti/ | n. | 冒犯君主罪，大不敬 | An offense against the dignity of a reigning sovereign; treason. |  The journalist was accused of lese-majesty after publishing an article critical of the king.  这位记者因发表批评国王的文章而被指控犯有大不敬罪。 | synonyms: treason, sedition, high treason | antonyms: loyalty, allegiance, fidelity | noun

lese-majesty:/ˌlɛz ˈmædʒɪsti/ | n. |  不敬，亵渎  |  Disrespectful or irreverent behavior towards something considered sacred or important. | Criticizing the company's founder was considered lese-majesty by the employees.  批评公司创始人被员工视为大不敬。 | synonyms: sacrilege, blasphemy, irreverence, disrespect | antonyms: reverence, respect, deference | noun
