ailuroidea:/ˌaɪljʊˈrɔɪdiə/
noun
猫超科
A superfamily of carnivoran mammals that includes cats, both large and small.
The Ailuroidea includes the Felidae family (true cats) and other related species. 猫超科包括猫科动物（真正的猫）和其他相关物种。
The extinct family Stenoplesictidae is also part of the Ailuroidea superfamily. 已灭绝的Stenoplesictidae科也是猫超科的一部分。
近义词:Feliformia (sometimes used interchangeably)
反义词:Canoidea (dog-like carnivorans)
词性:n.
