outworded:/ˌaʊtˈwɜːrdɪd/ | adj. | 被说得哑口无言的，词穷的 |Defeated in argument; at a loss for words.  Unable to express oneself adequately because someone else has expressed themselves better or more extensively.| He was completely outworded by her eloquent response and could only stammer in agreement. 他被她雄辩的回答说得哑口无言，只能结结巴巴地表示同意。| Synonyms: silenced, speechless, dumbfounded, tongue-tied | Antonyms: articulate, eloquent, fluent, voluble | adjective
