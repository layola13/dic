emotivity:/ɪˌmoʊˈtɪvɪti/ | n. | 情感性，情绪化 | The quality or state of being emotional or easily moved by emotions.  It refers to the tendency to experience and express emotions readily. | Her emotivity made her a compelling actress. 她的情感性使她成为一名引人注目的女演员。|  sensitivity, responsiveness, emotionalism | apathy, coldness, unemotionality | noun

Etymology: From French *émotivité*, from *émotif* (“emotional”), from *émotion* (“emotion”) + *-if* (adjective suffix) + *-ité* (noun suffix). Ultimately from Latin *ēmovēre* (“to move out, remove, stir up”).
