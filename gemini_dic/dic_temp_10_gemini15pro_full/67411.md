externalizing: /ɪkˈstɜːrnəlaɪzɪŋ/ /ɛkˈstɜːrnəˌlaɪzɪŋ/
| adj. | 外化的 | Relating to or characterized by externalization. |  Externalizing behaviors can manifest as aggression or impulsivity. 外化行为可以表现为攻击性或冲动性。| outward, expressive | internalizing | adjective
