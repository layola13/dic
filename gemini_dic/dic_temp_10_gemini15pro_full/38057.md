composers:/kəmˈpoʊzərz/| n.|作曲家|People who write music, especially classical music.|Many famous composers lived in Vienna. 许多著名的作曲家都住在维也纳。|The composers of the Romantic era were highly influential. 浪漫主义时期的作曲家影响深远。|synonyms: musicians, songwriters, creators|antonyms: listeners, audience|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *composeor* (modern French *compositeur*), from Latin *compositor* "one who arranges," from *componere* "to put together," from *com-* "together" + *ponere* "to place."
