inclining:/ɪnˈklaɪnɪŋ/
adjective
倾斜的，倾向的
Having a tendency or disposition towards something; leaning.
The tree is inclining towards the house.  这棵树正朝着房子倾斜。
The public is inclining towards the new policy. 公众倾向于支持新政策。
近义词:leaning, sloping, tilting, tending, predisposed
反义词:straight, upright, vertical, averse
词性:adjective


inclining:/ɪnˈklaɪnɪŋ/
verb (present participle of incline)
使倾斜，使倾向
Causing to have a tendency or disposition towards something.
He was inclining his head towards her. 他正把头倾向她。
The evidence is inclining me to believe her story.  证据使我倾向于相信她的故事。
近义词:leaning, sloping, tilting, tending, predisposing
反义词:straightening, uprighting, verticalizing, deterring
词性:verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *enclinen*, from Old French *encliner*, from Latin *inclināre* (“to bend, lean, incline”), from *in-* + *clināre* (“to bend, lean”).
