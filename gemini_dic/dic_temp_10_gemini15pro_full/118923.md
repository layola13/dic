metempirical:/ˌmɛtəmˈpɪrɪkəl/
adjective
形而上学经验的; 超验的
Going beyond the empirical; transcendental.  Relating to knowledge gained by means other than sensory experience, often by intuition or pure reason.
His metempirical beliefs led him to believe in reincarnation.  他超验的信仰使他相信轮回转世。
Synonyms: transcendental, metaphysical, supersensible, spiritual, otherworldly
Antonyms: empirical, experiential, sensory, physical, mundane
词性: adjective
