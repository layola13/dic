covens:/ˈkʌvənz/
n.
女巫集会，巫术集会
A group or assembly of witches, typically thirteen in number.
The covens met secretly in the woods at night.  女巫集会在夜里秘密地在树林里集会。
Near synonyms: covenstead, circle, group
Antonyms: N/A (lacks a direct opposite)
