aggressions: /əˈɡrɛʃənz/
| n. |侵略，攻击；侵犯；挑衅|Hostile or violent behavior or attitudes toward another; readiness to attack or confront.|The aggressions of the neighboring country led to war. 邻国的侵略导致了战争。|近义词：attacks, assaults, offenses, hostilities|反义词：peace, diplomacy, kindness, reconciliation|词性:名词

Etymology: From Latin *aggressio*, from *aggredi* "to attack," from *ad-* "to" + *gradi* "to step, walk."
