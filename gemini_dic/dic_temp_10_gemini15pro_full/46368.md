dadaistically:/ˌdɑːdəˈɪstɪkli/ | adv. | 达达主义地 | In a manner characterized by the principles of Dadaism, an avant-garde art movement that rejected logic and reason in favor of nonsense, irrationality, and protest. | The artist dadaistically glued random objects to the canvas, creating a nonsensical yet thought-provoking piece. 艺术家达达主义地将随机物品粘在画布上，创造出一件荒谬却又引人深思的作品。| synonym: absurdly, irrationally, nonsensically | antonym: rationally, logically, conventionally | word type: adverb
