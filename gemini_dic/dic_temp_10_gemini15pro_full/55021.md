disqualifiable:/dɪsˈkwɒlɪfaɪəbl/ | adj. | 可取消资格的，不合格的 |  Able to be disqualified or rendered ineligible.  This describes something or someone that can be removed from a competition, position, or privilege due to not meeting the required standards or rules. | The athlete was disqualifiable for using performance-enhancing drugs.  这个运动员因使用兴奋剂而被取消资格。

He submitted a disqualifiable application because it was incomplete. 他提交的申请不完整，因此不合格。| synonyms: ineligible, unfit, unsuitable | antonyms: eligible, qualified, suitable | word type: adjective
