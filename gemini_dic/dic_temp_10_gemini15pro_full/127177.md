naturalized:/ˈnætʃrəlaɪzd/| adj.|已入籍的，归化的；驯化的；引进的|Admitted to citizenship; made familiar; adapted.|例句：He became a naturalized citizen of the United States. 他成为了美国公民。

例句：The naturalized plant thrived in its new environment.  这种引进的植物在新环境中茁壮成长。|近义词：acclimatized, adapted, assimilated|反义词:alien, foreign, indigenous|词性:形容词
naturalize:/ˈnætʃrəlaɪz/| vt. |使入籍；移植；归化；吸收|To grant full citizenship to someone not born in the country; to introduce a plant or animal to a new region.|例句：The government naturalized many immigrants. 政府使许多移民入籍。

例句：They successfully naturalized the exotic flower in their garden. 他们在花园里成功地移植了这种奇异的花。|近义词：acclimatize, adapt, assimilate|反义词:repatriate, expel|词性:动词
