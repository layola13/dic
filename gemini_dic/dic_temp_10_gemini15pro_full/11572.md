aristotelical: /ˌærɪstəˈtɛlɪkəl/ | adj. | 亚里士多德式的；亚里士多德学派的；迂腐的；形式主义的 | Of or relating to Aristotle or his philosophy; resembling or characteristic of the philosophy of Aristotle, especially in being formal, logical, or systematic; pedantic. |例句：His arguments were very Aristotelical. 他的论证非常具有亚里士多德风格。|例句：The professor's Aristotelical approach to literature focused on analyzing the structure and logic of texts.  教授对文学的亚里士多德式研究方法侧重于分析文本的结构和逻辑。|近义词：philosophical, logical, systematic, pedantic |反义词：unphilosophical, illogical, unsystematic, pragmatic |词性: adjective
词源: From Aristotle + -ical.  Ultimately from the Greek name Ἀριστοτέλης (Aristotelēs).
