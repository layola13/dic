nonenigmatical:/ˌnɒnˌɛnɪɡˈmætɪkəl/
adjective
非谜的；清晰易懂的
Not enigmatic; straightforward and easily understood.
The instructions were clear and nonenigmatical, leaving no room for misinterpretation.  指令清晰易懂，没有误解的余地。
The speaker's nonenigmatical language made it easy for everyone to understand the complex topic. 演讲者简洁明了的语言使每个人都能轻松理解这个复杂的话题。
Synonyms: clear, straightforward, plain, simple, unambiguous, perspicuous
Antonyms: enigmatic, puzzling, mysterious, obscure, ambiguous, cryptic
词性: 形容词
