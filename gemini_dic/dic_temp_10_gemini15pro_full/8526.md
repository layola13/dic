anticensorious:/ˌæntiˌsɛnˈsɔːriəs/
adj.
不爱批评的，宽容的
Not censorious; tolerant and forgiving.
He took an anticensorious view of her behavior, understanding the pressures she was under. 他对她的行为采取了宽容的态度，理解她所承受的压力。
近义词：tolerant, forgiving, lenient, indulgent, understanding
反义词：censorious, critical, judgmental, fault-finding
词性: adjective
