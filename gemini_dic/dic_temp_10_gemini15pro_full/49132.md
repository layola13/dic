deistical:/ˌdiːˈɪstɪkəl/
adjective
自然神论的
Pertaining to or characteristic of deism; believing in a God who created the universe but does not intervene in its affairs.
Thomas Paine's "The Age of Reason" is a deistical work that argues for a creator God but rejects organized religion. 托马斯·潘恩的《理性时代》是一部自然神论著作，它主张造物主上帝，但反对有组织的宗教。

John Locke's philosophical views could be considered deistical, as he believed in a God who established natural laws but did not actively interfere in human affairs.  约翰·洛克的哲学观点可以被认为是自然神论的，因为他相信一个建立了自然法则但不积极干预人类事务的上帝。


近义词:theistic, rationalistic
反义词:atheistic, agnostic
词性:adjective
