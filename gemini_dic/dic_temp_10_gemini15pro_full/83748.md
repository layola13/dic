hailstones:/ˈheɪlˌstoʊnz/| n. |冰雹|Small balls of ice that fall like rain from the sky during a thunderstorm. |The hailstones were so large they damaged several cars. 冰雹很大，损坏了数辆汽车。|近义词：ice pellets|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly rain or sunshine)|noun
