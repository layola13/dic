countersubversive: /ˌkaʊntər səbˈvɜːsɪv/ /ˌkaʊntər səbˈvɜːrsɪv/
| adj. n.|反颠覆的，反颠覆者|Intended to counteract or prevent subversion.|The government implemented countersubversive measures to protect national security. 政府实施了反颠覆措施以保护国家安全。|The CIA agent specialized in countersubversive tactics.  这位中央情报局特工专门研究反颠覆策略。|近义词：anti-subversive, counterrevolutionary|反义词: subversive, revolutionary|词性:形容词, 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From counter- + subversive.  "Counter-" is a prefix meaning "against" or "opposed to." "Subversive" comes from the Late Latin "subversivus," meaning "overthrowing," from the Latin "subvertere," meaning "to overturn."
