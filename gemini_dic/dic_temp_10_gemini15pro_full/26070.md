bushment:/ˈbʊʃmənt/| n.|埋伏，伏击；埋伏地|An ambush; a place of ambush.  A body of troops lying in ambush.|例句：The soldiers lay in bushment, waiting for the enemy to pass. 士兵们埋伏起来，等待敌人经过。

例句：The bushment was well concealed in the dense foliage. 埋伏地点隐藏在茂密的树叶中。|近义词：ambush, ambuscade, trap|反义词:openness, exposure|词性:noun
