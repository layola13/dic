overarm:/ˌoʊvərˈɑːrm/ /ˌəʊvərˈɑːm/| n.|上手投球|A method of throwing a ball with the hand and arm above shoulder level.|The overarm is a common technique in cricket and baseball. 上手投球是板球和棒球中常见的技巧。|近义词: overhead throw|反义词: underarm throw|词性:名词

词源: "over" + "arm"，字面意思为“在手臂之上”。
