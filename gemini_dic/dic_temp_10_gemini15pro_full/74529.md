fraternities:/frəˈtɜːrnətiz/
n.
兄弟会；大学生联谊会
A group of male students at a college or university who have formed a club with its own social activities, or the building used by such a club.
Example: He joined one of the college's fraternities. 他加入了大学里的一个兄弟会。

Example: Fraternities are known for their social events and community service. 兄弟会以其社交活动和社区服务而闻名。

近义词: brotherhoods, societies, associations
反义词: sororities (specifically for women)
词性: 名词 (plural)


fraternity:/frəˈtɜːrnəti/
n.
兄弟情谊；友爱
A feeling of friendship and support between people who share an interest or are members of the same group.
Example: There is a strong sense of fraternity among the members of the team. 团队成员之间有强烈的兄弟情谊。

Example: The fraternity of firefighters is well-known. 消防员之间的兄弟情谊是众所周知的。

近义词: brotherhood, fellowship, camaraderie
反义词: hostility, animosity
词性: 名词 (singular)


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English: fraternite, from Old French fraternité, from Latin fraternitas, from frater ‘brother’.
