gamesmanship: /ˈɡeɪmz.mən.ʃɪp/
noun
策略；手腕；比赛技巧
The use of dubious methods, often including psychological tactics, to gain an advantage in a game or contest.
He was accused of gamesmanship after he questioned the umpire's eyesight. 他因质疑裁判的视力而被指责玩弄策略。
gamesmanship: /ˈɡeɪmz.mən.ʃɪp/
noun
中文翻译：策略；手腕；比赛技巧
英文解析：The use of tactics, often close to the limit of what is fair or sportsmanlike, to gain an advantage over an opponent.
例句：He won the election by sheer gamesmanship. 他纯粹靠策略赢得了选举。
例句：Her gamesmanship is unparalleled in the world of professional tennis. 她在职业网球界的策略无人能及。
近义词：trickery, cunning, strategy, tactics
反义词：sportsmanship, fair play
