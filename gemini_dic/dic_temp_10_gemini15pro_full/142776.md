oubliette:/ˌuːbliˈet/ /ˌoʊbliˈet/ | n. | 地牢；囚室； oubliette |A dungeon with an opening only at the top, originally built as a dungeon, and typically serving as a place of confinement from which it was impossible to escape. It could also be used for the disposal of corpses. |The prisoner was thrown into the oubliette and forgotten.  囚犯被扔进了地牢，被人遗忘了。

The castle had a dark, dank oubliette beneath the main hall. 城堡的大厅下面有一个阴暗潮湿的地牢。| 近义词：dungeon, cell, prison | 反义词: freedom, liberty | 词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *oublier (“to forget”), from Latin oblīvīscor (“I forget”).  The name suggests a place where prisoners were put and then forgotten.
