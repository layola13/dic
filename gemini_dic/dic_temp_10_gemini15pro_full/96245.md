indicational:/ˌɪndɪˈkeɪʃənl/
adjective
指示的，表明的
serving to indicate something.
例句：The high fever is indicational of a serious infection. 高烧表明感染严重。

例句：His facial expression was indicational of his displeasure. 他的面部表情表明他不高兴。

近义词：suggestive, indicative, symptomatic
反义词：concealing, hiding
词性：adjective
