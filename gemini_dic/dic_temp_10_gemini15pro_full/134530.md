nonlicentiously:/ˌnɒnlaɪˈsɛnʃəsli/
adverb
不放荡地；严肃地；规矩地
in a way that is not licentious (sexually immoral or offensive).  Characterized by seriousness and restraint, especially in sexual matters.
She behaved nonlicentiously throughout the evening, engaging in polite conversation and refraining from any inappropriate behavior. 她整晚都表现得端庄得体，参与礼貌的谈话，避免任何不恰当的行为。
The book was written nonlicentiously, focusing on the historical context rather than sensationalizing the events. 这本书写得严肃认真，侧重于历史背景，而不是夸大事件。
近义词: chastely, purely, virtuously, decently, modestly
反义词: licentiously, lewdly, lasciviously, wantonly, dissolutely
词性: adverb
