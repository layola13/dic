例句：The nonsupporters of the new policy voiced their concerns at the town hall meeting. 新政策的非支持者在市政厅会议上表达了他们的担忧。|近义词：opponent, adversary, antagonist, detractor|反义词：supporter, advocate, proponent, backer|词性: 名词
