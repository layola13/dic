bulldog:/ˈbʊldɒɡ/ (UK), /ˈbʊldɔːɡ/ (US)| n.|斗牛犬，顽固的人|A breed of dog with a short, wrinkled face and a strong, muscular body.  Figuratively, it can refer to a person who is tenacious and determined.|The bulldog refused to let go of the toy. 这只斗牛犬不肯松开玩具。|近义词：pug, mastiff (for dog breed); tenacious, resolute (for figurative meaning)|反义词:cowardly, timid|noun

bulldog:/ˈbʊldɒɡ/ (UK), /ˈbʊldɔːɡ/ (US)| vt.|顽强地克服，坚持不懈地对付|To persist stubbornly in overcoming something.|He bulldoed his way through the crowd. 他顽强地挤过人群。|近义词：persevere, persist|反义词:give up, surrender|verb
