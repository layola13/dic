glyphs:/ɡlɪfs/| n.| 字形，符号 |A glyph is an elemental symbol within an agreed set of symbols, intended to represent a readable character for the purposes of writing. |The ancient Egyptian hieroglyphs are a complex system of glyphs. 古埃及象形文字是一个复杂的字形系统。|synonyms: symbol, character, sign, mark |antonyms: N/A |noun


glyphs:/ɡlɪfs/| n.| (字体的)字形，(软件的)图形符号 |In typography, a glyph is an individual form of a character. In software, it can refer to a graphical symbol. |The designer carefully adjusted the glyphs of the new font. 设计师仔细调整了新字体的字形。|synonyms: figure, shape, form |antonyms: N/A |noun
