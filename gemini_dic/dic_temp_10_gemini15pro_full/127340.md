nazified:/ˈnɑːtsɪfaɪd/
|adj.|纳粹化的，被纳粹控制的|Made to conform to Nazi principles or practices; imbued with Nazi ideology.|The historian argued that the education system had been thoroughly nazified in the 1930s.| 近义词:fascistized, totalitarianized|反义词:democratized, liberalized|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Nazi + -fy + -ed.  "Nazi" is a shortened form of Nationalsozialist (National Socialist), the name of the German political party led by Adolf Hitler.  "-fy" is a suffix meaning "to make or cause to be," and "-ed" is the past participle suffix.
