outsearches:/ˌaʊtˈsɜːrtʃɪz/
verb
超过…的搜索量
Surpasses in the number of searches performed.
Google outsearched Bing last quarter.  谷歌上个季度的搜索量超过了必应。
Synonyms: outranks, surpasses, beats, exceeds
Antonyms: trails, lags behind
词性: 动词
