cruddle:/ˈkrʌd(ə)l/| v. |凝结，凝固；擁抱，依偎|To curdle or coagulate; to huddle together for warmth or comfort.|The milk cruddled when I added the lemon juice.  牛奶加了柠檬汁后凝结了。|The children cruddled together for warmth in the cold room. 孩子们在寒冷的房间里挤在一起取暖。|近义词：curdle, clot, coagulate, huddle, snuggle|反义词:separate, disperse|词性:动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *crudlen, frequentative of crudden (“to curdle”), from Old English *crudian, from Proto-Germanic *krudōną (“to press, crowd, condense”), from Proto-Indo-European *kreut- (“to bend, curve”). Cognate with Middle Dutch crodelen (“to curl”), German kräuseln (“to curl, crimp”).
