onto:/ˈɑːntuː/ /ˈɔːntuː/| prep. adv.|到…之上，在…之上；意识到，明白|A cat jumped onto the table. 一只猫跳到桌子上。|近义词：on, upon|反义词:off, off of|词性:介词, 副词

USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
He puts the book onto the shelf. 他把书放在书架上。
She climbs onto the roof. 她爬上屋顶。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
The cat is jumping onto the chair. 猫正跳到椅子上。
He is loading boxes onto the truck. 他正在把箱子装到卡车上。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
The bird has flown onto the branch. 鸟儿已经飞到树枝上了。
She has stepped onto the stage. 她已经走上舞台了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
They have been loading cargo onto the ship all day. 他们整天都在往船上装货物。
He has been pasting stickers onto his laptop. 他一直在往他的笔记本电脑上贴纸。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
He threw the ball onto the roof. 他把球扔到屋顶上。
She climbed onto the horse. 她爬上了马。


过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was walking onto the field when I saw him. 当我看到他时，我正走向田野。
She was climbing onto the bus when it started moving.  当公共汽车启动时，她正在上车。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
The dog had jumped onto the bed before I could stop it. 在我阻止它之前，狗已经跳到床上了。
He had loaded the luggage onto the plane before it took off. 他在飞机起飞前把行李装上了飞机。

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
They had been putting boxes onto the shelves for hours. 他们已经往架子上放箱子好几个小时了。
She had been dripping paint onto the floor. 她一直在往地板上滴油漆。

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
I will put the vase onto the table. 我会把花瓶放在桌子上。
She will climb onto the rock. 她会爬到岩石上。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
This time tomorrow, I will be moving onto the next chapter. 明天这个时候，我将进入下一章。
He will be loading the furniture onto the van. 他将会把家具装到货车上。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
By next week, I will have moved onto a new apartment. 到下周，我将搬到一个新的公寓。
She will have loaded all the files onto the server. 她将把所有文件都加载到服务器上。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, I will have been living onto this street for ten years. 到明年，我将在这条街上住了十年了。 (此例句onto用法略有不妥，更合适的介词是on)
By the time you arrive, he will have been loading boxes onto the truck for hours. 当你到达时，他已经装了好几个小时的箱子到卡车上。


虚拟语气:
If I were to climb onto the roof, I would be able to see the ocean. 如果我爬到屋顶上，我就能看到大海。
I wish I had held onto the railing. 我希望我抓住了栏杆。

被动语态: (onto较少用于被动语态)
The files were loaded onto the server. 文件被加载到服务器上。
The painting was hung onto the wall. 这幅画被挂在墙上。

疑问句:
Did he jump onto the stage? 他跳上舞台了吗？
What are you putting onto the bread? 你在面包上放什么？

否定句:
The cat didn't jump onto the counter. 猫没有跳到柜台上。
He hasn't put the book onto the shelf yet. 他还没有把书放到书架上。


PHRASE->
catch onto = 明白，理解
latch onto = 抓住，缠住
hold onto = 抓住，坚持
move onto =  继续前进到
get onto =  开始谈论，联系


注意事项: onto 强调从一个地方到另一个地方的移动，并且通常带有向上或向前的含义。单纯的“在...之上”更常用on。
