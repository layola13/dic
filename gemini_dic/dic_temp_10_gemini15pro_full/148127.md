overseeds:/ˌoʊvərˈsiːdz/ | v. | 播种过多; 超量播种;在已有的草坪上再次播种 | To sow (seed) too densely or thickly; to sow (a lawn or other area) with additional seed, especially of a different variety, to improve the quality or density of the existing vegetation. | The gardener overseeds the lawn every spring to ensure a lush, green carpet. 园丁每年春天都会在草坪上再次播种，以确保草坪郁郁葱葱。|  He overseeded the bare patches in the lawn with ryegrass. 他在草坪光秃秃的地方播种了黑麦草。| 近义词: resow, replant | 反义词: N/A | 词性: verb

overseeds:/ˌoʊvərˈsiːdz/ | n. | 过量播种的种子 | Seeds sown in excess or on top of existing vegetation. | The overseeds quickly germinated and filled in the bare spots. 播下的种子很快发芽，填补了光秃的地方。|  The birds ate most of the overseeds scattered on the ground. 鸟儿吃掉了散落在地上的大部分种子。| 近义词: excess seed | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


词源:  From over- + seed.  "Over-"  prefix meaning "excessively" or "in addition to".
