nonhabitually: /ˌnɒnhəˈbɪtʃuəli/ (US) /ˌnɒnhəˈbɪtʃʊəli/ (UK)| adv.| 非习惯性地，不经常地 | Not done, performed, or occurring as a habit; infrequently. | She nonhabitually attends social gatherings, preferring quiet evenings at home. 她不经常参加社交聚会，更喜欢在家安静地度过夜晚。 |  He drinks coffee nonhabitually, only when he needs an extra boost. 他不经常喝咖啡，只有当他需要提神的时候才喝。 | 近义词: infrequently, rarely, occasionally, sporadically | 反义词: habitually, regularly, usually, customarily | 词性: 副词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "habitual" (adjective meaning "done or acquired by habit") + "-ly" (adverb suffix)
