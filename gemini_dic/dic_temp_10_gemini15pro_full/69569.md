邪恶; 罪恶; 凶恶
The quality or state of being felonious; wickedness.
His feloniousness was evident in his cold, calculating eyes. 他的邪恶从他冰冷、精明的眼神中显露无疑。

近义词: wickedness, evil, criminality, depravity, villainy
反义词: goodness, righteousness, virtue, morality

词性: noun

词源: From felon + -ness.  Felon comes from Anglo-Norman felon, from Old French felon, from Medieval Latin fellō, fellōnis “villain, traitor, cruel person.”
