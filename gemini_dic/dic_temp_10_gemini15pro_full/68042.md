factorylike:/ˈfæktəriˌlaɪk/ | adj. | 像工厂一样的 | Resembling a factory, especially in being large, impersonal, or highly organized. | The office had a factorylike atmosphere, with rows of identical desks. 办公室有一种像工厂一样的氛围，一排排相同的桌子。| The warehouse was a vast, factorylike space.  仓库是一个巨大的，像工厂一样的空间。 | synonyms: industrial, mechanized, impersonal | antonyms: homely, personal, cozy | adjective
