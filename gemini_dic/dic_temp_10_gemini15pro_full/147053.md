overleap:/ˌoʊvərˈliːp/ | v. |  超越，跳过，忽略 | To pass over without notice or mention; to jump or leap over. |  He overleaped the small stream. 他跳过了小溪。 |  The report overleaped an important detail. 报告忽略了一个重要的细节。 | 近义词:bypass, ignore, jump, omit, surpass | 反义词: acknowledge, address, include, notice, observe | 词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *overlepen*, from Old English *oferhlēapan* (“to leap over, jump over, overleap”), from *ofer* (“over”) + *hlēapan* (“to leap, run, jump”). Cognate with German *überlaufen*, Dutch *overlopen*.
