outperforms:/ˌaʊtpərˈfɔːrmz/
verb
胜过，优于
performs better than; exceeds the performance of.
Our new model outperforms all competitors in fuel efficiency. 我们的新型号在燃油效率方面胜过所有竞争对手。
synonyms: surpasses, exceeds, betters, trumps, eclipses
antonyms: underperforms, lags behind
词性: 动词
