falernian:/fɔːlˈɜːrniən/
adjective|法莱尔尼安酒的；（古罗马）坎帕尼亚地区产的|Of or relating to Falernus, an ancient city in Campania, Italy, famed for its wine.  Relating to the strong, dry, white wine produced there.|The poet Horace praised the virtues of Falernian wine. 荷拉斯诗人赞美了法莱尔尼安酒的美德。|近义词:Campanian, Italian (referring to origin)|反义词:None|词性:adjective
falernian:/fɔːlˈɜːrniən/
noun|法莱尔尼安酒|A strong, dry, white wine originally produced in ancient Falernus, Campania, Italy.|He brought out a bottle of vintage Falernian. 他拿出一瓶年份久远的弗莱尔尼安酒。|近义词:wine|反义词:None|词性:noun
