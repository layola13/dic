echon:/ˈekən/ | n. |回声，共鸣，重复|a sound that is caused by the reflection of sound waves from a surface back to the listener |The canyon echoed with the sound of their laughter. 峡谷里回荡着他们的笑声。| synonyms: reverberation, resonance, repetition | antonyms: silence, hush | noun

echon:/ˈekən/ | v. |发出回声，重复，模仿| (of a sound) be repeated or reverberate after the original sound has stopped; repeat (someone's words or opinions), typically to express agreement |His voice echoed around the empty hall. 他的声音在空荡荡的大厅里回荡。| synonyms: resound, reverberate, repeat, mirror, imitate | antonyms: originate, muffle, stifle | verb
