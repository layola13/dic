coffined:/ˈkɒfɪnd/ /ˈkɔːfɪnd/| adj.|被装入棺材的；像棺材一样的；幽禁的|Confined within a coffin; resembling a coffin; imprisoned or confined as if in a coffin.|The soldier's body was coffined and sent home.  这名士兵的遗体被装入棺材送回家。|
|The small, dark room felt coffined and claustrophobic.  这间狭小昏暗的房间让人感觉像被关在棺材里一样幽闭恐惧。|Synonyms: entombed, encased, confined, imprisoned|Antonyms: liberated, freed, released|adjective
Etymology->
From coffin + -ed.  Ultimately from the Greek kophinos (basket).
