diopter:/daɪˈɑːptər/ (US), /daɪˈɒptər/ (UK)| n. |屈光度；焦度|A unit of refractive power, equal to the reciprocal of the focal length in meters. It is used to express the power of a lens or curved mirror.|The optician prescribed lenses of two diopters for her glasses. 眼科医生给她开了两屈光度的眼镜。|近义词:refractive power|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

dioptre:/daɪˈɒptər/ (UK)| n. |屈光度；焦度| Same as diopter, primarily used in British English. |The lens has a power of three dioptres. 这个镜片的屈光度是三度。|近义词:refractive power|反义词: N/A|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *dioptre*, from Greek *dioptron* "instrument for measuring," from *dia- + optos* "seen."
