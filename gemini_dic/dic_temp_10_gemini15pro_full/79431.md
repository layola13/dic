glistering:/ˈɡlɪstərɪŋ/| adj.|闪闪发光的，闪烁的|Covered with or reflecting light; shining brightly.|The lake was glistening in the morning sun. 早晨的阳光下，湖面波光粼粼。|近义词：sparkling, shimmering, glittering, gleaming|反义词：dull, matte|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *glisteren*, from Old English *glit(e)rian*, frequentative of *glītan* (“to glitter, shine, glisten”).  Cognate with German *glittern* (“to glitter”).
