gung-ho:/ˌɡʌŋˈhoʊ/ | adj. | 热心的，充满热情的 | extremely enthusiastic and eager to do something | Example: The team was gung-ho about the new project.  团队对新项目充满热情。 Example:  She's gung-ho about going to the concert. 她对去听音乐会非常热心。 | Synonyms: enthusiastic, eager, zealous, keen, avid | Antonyms: apathetic, indifferent, unenthusiastic | Adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Chinese 工合 (gōnghé), meaning "work together," which was the motto of a Chinese industrial cooperative society active during World War II.  The term was adopted by US Marines during the war to mean "eager."
