big-hearted:/ˌbɪɡˈhɑːrtɪd/
adjective
慷慨的；宽大的
kind, generous, and sympathetic.
She's a big-hearted woman who always puts others first. 她是一位心地善良的女人，总是把别人放在第一位。
He made a big-hearted donation to the charity. 他向慈善机构慷慨捐赠。

近义词:generous, kind, compassionate, charitable, benevolent, altruistic, philanthropic
反义词:mean, selfish, stingy, uncharitable, hard-hearted, cold-hearted

词性:adjective
