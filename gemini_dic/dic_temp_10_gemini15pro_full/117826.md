mellification:/ˌmɛlɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
n.
甜蜜化；酿蜜；使甜蜜
The process of making or becoming sweet like honey.
The mellification of the fruit made it perfect for dessert.  这种水果的甜蜜化使它成为完美的甜点。
sweetening, dulcification
bitterness, souring
noun
