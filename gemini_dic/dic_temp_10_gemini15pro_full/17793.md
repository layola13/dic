bedraggled:/bɪˈdræɡəld/
adjective
邋遢的，凌乱不堪的
Limp and soiled, as with rain or dirt.
The bedraggled survivors of the shipwreck were finally rescued.  那些在船难中幸存下来的衣衫褴褛的人终于获救了。

The bedraggled kitten looked pathetic in the pouring rain.  那只浑身湿透的小猫在倾盆大雨中看起来很可怜。

近义词:dishevelled, unkempt, messy, rumpled, draggled
反义词:neat, tidy, groomed, pristine
词性:adjective
