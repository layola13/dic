bewrought:/bɪˈrɔːt/
adjective
精工细作的；（感情上）激动的，兴奋的
deeply affected or wrought up emotionally; elaborately or intricately fashioned.
例句：Her face was bewrought with worry. 她的脸上充满了担忧。

例句：The bewrought iron gates guarded the entrance to the estate.  精工细作的铁门守护着庄园的入口。

近义词：elaborate, ornate, wrought, agitated, distressed
反义词：simple, plain, calm, composed
词性: adjective


词源: From Middle English *biwroght, from Old English bewroht, past participle of bewyrcan (“to work, labor, produce”). Equivalent to be- +‎ wrought.
