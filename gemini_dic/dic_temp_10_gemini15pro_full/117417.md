medicornu:/ˌmɛdɪˈkɔːrnjuː/ | n. | 额角 |  The medicornu is the middle portion of the frontal bone between the superciliary arches. It corresponds roughly to the location of the third eye in mythological creatures. | The paleontologist pointed to the medicornu on the dinosaur skull.  古生物学家指着恐龙头骨上的额角。| synonyms: glabella, intercilium | antonyms: N/A | noun
