diffident:/ˈdɪfɪdənt/| adj.|缺乏自信的；胆怯的；羞怯的|Lacking self-confidence; shy; timid.|He was diffident about speaking in public. 他对在公共场合讲话感到胆怯。|Synonyms: shy, timid, hesitant, insecure, self-doubting|Antonyms: confident, bold, assertive|adjective
