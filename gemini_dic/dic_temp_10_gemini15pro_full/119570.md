microtrabecular: /ˌmaɪkrəʊtrəˈbekjʊlər/ | adj. | 微梁的 |  Relating to or consisting of microtrabeculae, which are fine, rod-like structures found within the cytoplasm of cells. | The microtrabecular network plays a role in cell shape and intracellular transport.  微梁网络在细胞形态和细胞内运输中发挥作用。 | Synonym:  microfibrillar | Antonym: N/A | adjective

microtrabecula: /ˌmaɪkrəʊtrəˈbekjʊlə/ | n. | 微梁 | A fine, rod-like structure found within the cytoplasm of cells, forming part of the microtrabecular network. | Microtrabeculae are believed to provide structural support and aid in intracellular transport. 微梁被认为可以提供结构支撑并帮助细胞内运输。| Synonym: microfibril | Antonym: N/A | noun
