chinook:/tʃɪˈnʊk/ /ʃɪˈnʊk/| n.|奇努克风; 暖风|A warm, dry wind that blows down the eastern slopes of the Rocky Mountains.|The chinook melted the snow quickly. 奇努克风很快融化了雪。|近义词:foehn, warm wind|反义词:cold wind|noun

chinook:/tʃɪˈnʊk/ /ʃɪˈnʊk/| n.|奇努克鲑; 春鲑|A large species of Pacific salmon.|Chinook salmon are prized for their rich flavor. 奇努克鲑鱼因其浓郁的风味而备受珍视。|近义词:king salmon, tyee salmon|反义词:N/A|noun

chinook:/tʃɪˈnʊk/ /ʃɪˈnʊk/| n.|奇努克语|A language spoken by several Native American groups in the Pacific Northwest.|The Chinook language is now endangered. 奇努克语现在濒临灭绝。|近义词:Chinook jargon (a related trade language)|反义词:N/A|noun
