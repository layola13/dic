panoramic:/ˌpænəˈræmɪk/ | adj. | 全景的，全貌的 | encompassing a wide view; presenting a comprehensive perspective |  From a mountain top, you can get a panoramic view of the valley below. 从山顶上，你可以看到山谷的全景。 | synonyms: wide-ranging, sweeping, comprehensive, all-encompassing | antonyms: limited, narrow, restricted | adjective

panoramic:/ˌpænəˈræmɪk/ | n. | 全景，全景照片 | a wide and comprehensive view or picture | He took a panoramic photo of the city skyline. 他拍了一张城市天际线的全景照片。 | synonyms: vista, panorama | antonyms: close-up, detail | noun
