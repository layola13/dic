emotivities: /ɪˌmoʊˈtɪvɪtiz/
noun
情感，情绪（尤指容易激动的情绪）
The study of emotivities is crucial for understanding human behavior.  研究情感对于理解人类行为至关重要。
Her emotivities often got the better of her in stressful situations. 在压力大的情况下，她的情绪经常战胜她。
近义词: emotions, feelings, passions, sentiments
反义词: apathy, indifference
词性: n.


Etymology:  From "emotive" + "-ities," a suffix used to form plural nouns denoting qualities or properties. "Emotive" comes from Latin "emovere" (to move out, excite) through French "émouvoir."
