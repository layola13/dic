gamechanger:/ˈɡeɪmˌtʃeɪndʒər/| n. |变革者；颠覆者；游戏规则改变者|Something that significantly alters the course or rules of something, especially in a competitive situation; a disruptive innovation. |The development of the smartphone was a real gamechanger in the mobile phone industry.  智能手机的发展是手机行业真正的变革。

The new law could be a gamechanger for small businesses. 这项新法律可能会给小型企业带来翻天覆地的变化。|近义词：disruptor, innovator, revolutionary, groundbreaker|反义词: status quo, stagnation|词性:noun
