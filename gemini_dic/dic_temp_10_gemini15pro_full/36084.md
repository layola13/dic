coelenterate:/sɪˈlentəreɪt/ /siːˈlentəreɪt/ | n. |腔肠动物|An aquatic invertebrate animal of the phylum Cnidaria, which comprises the corals, sea anemones, jellyfish, hydroids, and Portuguese man-of-war.|例句：Corals are coelenterates that build reefs. 珊瑚是建造珊瑚礁的腔肠动物。

|例句：Jellyfish are free-swimming coelenterates. 水母是自由游动的腔肠动物。|近义词：cnidarian|反义词:  (No direct antonym, but could consider bilaterian as a contrasting group)|词性: noun
