fluorescence:/flʊəˈres(ə)ns/ /flɔːˈres(ə)ns/
n.|荧光|The emission of light by a substance that has absorbed light or other electromagnetic radiation.  A material absorbs light at one wavelength and then emits light at a longer wavelength.|The mineral showed a bright green fluorescence under UV light. 这种矿物在紫外线下显示出明亮的绿色荧光。|luminescence, glow|absorption|noun

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Fluorescence occurs when a substance absorbs light and then re-emits it. 当物质吸收光线后再发射光线时，就会发生荧光。
