excoction:/ɛkˈskɒkʃən/
n.
煎煮液；熬出物；精华
The process of extracting a substance by boiling.  A substance extracted by boiling.
The excoction of herbs created a potent medicine.  草药的煎煮液制成了一种有效的药物。
extract, decoction, infusion
solution, dilution
noun
