monther: /ˈmʌðər/ (This is likely a misspelling; the correct word is "mother") | n. | 母亲；妈妈 | A female parent. | My mother always makes me breakfast. 我的母亲总是为我做早餐。|  She baked a cake for her mother's birthday. 她为她母亲的生日烤了一个蛋糕。 | 近义词: mom, mum | 反义词: father | 词性: noun


Etymology:
From Old English "mōdor," from Proto-Germanic "*mōdēr," from Proto-Indo-European "*méh₂tēr."  Cognates include Dutch "moeder," German "Mutter," Latin "māter," Greek "mḗtēr," Sanskrit "mātṛ," and Persian "mādar."
