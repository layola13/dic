overlaxness: /ˌoʊvərˈlæksnɪs/
noun
过度松懈，过度宽松
The state of being excessively lax or negligent.
His overlaxness in enforcing the rules led to chaos. 他在执行规则上的过度松懈导致了混乱。
The overlaxness of security measures allowed the thief to enter.  安全措施的过度宽松让小偷得以进入。
近义词: negligence, laxity, carelessness, remissness
反义词: strictness, diligence, carefulness, rigor
词性: n.

Etymology: "over" + "laxness"
"Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessive" or "too much."
"Laxness" comes from the adjective "lax," derived from the Latin word "laxus," meaning "loose" or "slack."
