bathymetrical:/ˌbæθɪˈmɛtrɪkəl/
adjective
水深的；测深的
relating to bathymetry (the measurement of the depths of oceans, seas, or other large bodies of water).
The bathymetrical survey revealed a previously unknown trench.  测深调查揭示了一条以前不为人知的沟渠。
近义词: bathymetric
反义词: (None -  lacks a direct opposite)
词性: adjective

bathymetry:/bæˈθɪmətri/
noun
测深学；水深测量
the measurement of depth of water in oceans, seas, or lakes.
Bathymetry is essential for navigation and understanding underwater terrain. 测深对于航行和了解水下地形至关重要。
近义词: depth sounding, hydrography
反义词: (None - lacks a direct opposite)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *bathus* (deep) + *metron* (measure).
