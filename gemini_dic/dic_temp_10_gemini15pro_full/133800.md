nongeometrically:/ˌnɒnˌdʒiːəˈmɛtrɪkli/
adjective
非几何地
not geometrically; in a way that does not involve geometry or geometric shapes.
The problem was solved nongeometrically, using algebra.  这个问题是用代数而非几何方法解决的。
The artist arranged the objects nongeometrically, creating a sense of organic flow.  艺术家以非几何的方式排列物体，营造出一种有机流动的感觉。
近义词:non-geometrically
反义词:geometrically
词性:adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix): from Latin *non "not"
geometrically (adverb): from geometry + -ally. Geometry comes from Greek geometria, from geō- "earth" + -metria "measuring."
