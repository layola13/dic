palter:/ˈpɔːltər/ (US) /ˈpɔːltə(r)/ (UK)| v. | 含糊其辞；敷衍；推诿；讨价还价 | to speak or act insincerely or deceitfully; to equivocate |  He paltered with the truth to avoid getting into trouble. 他含糊其辞以避免惹上麻烦。|
|Synonyms: equivocate, prevaricate, hedge, evade, fudge, waffle|
|Antonyms: be honest, be forthright, be direct, be candid |
