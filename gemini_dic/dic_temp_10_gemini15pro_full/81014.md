gradualism: /ˈɡrædʒuəlɪzəm/ | n. | 渐进主义；渐变论 | The theory that change occurs slowly and by degrees, rather than suddenly or dramatically. |  The government's approach to economic reform was marked by gradualism. 政府的经济改革方法以渐进主义为特点。| synonyms:  incrementalism, piecemeal change,  evolution, progression | antonyms:  revolution, radicalism, sudden change, abruptness | noun

gradual: /ˈɡrædʒuəl/ | adj. | 逐渐的；逐步的 | Happening or developing slowly or by degrees. | The recovery from his illness was gradual. 他从病中逐渐恢复。| synonyms:  slow, steady, progressive,  incremental | antonyms:  sudden, abrupt, rapid, immediate | adjective
