oleoptene:/ˌoʊliˈɒptiːn/ | n. |油树烯; 植物蜡的液态部分 |The oleoptene is the liquid portion of an essential oil, separated from the stearoptene (the solid portion) by cooling and filtration. |The oleoptene of rose oil is rich in fragrant compounds. 玫瑰油的油树烯富含芳香化合物。| The oleoptene was carefully separated from the solid stearoptene. 油树烯被小心地从固态硬脂烯中分离出来。|  Synonyms:  liquid fraction, essential oil fraction | Antonyms: stearoptene | noun
