flowery:/ˈflaʊəri/ (US) /ˈflaʊəri/ (UK)| adj.|华丽的，花哨的；多花的|Full of flowers; excessively ornate or elaborate in style or language.|The writer's flowery prose was a bit much for the simple story.  这位作家华丽的散文对于这个简单的故事来说有点过了。|The garden was flowery and fragrant. 花园里花团锦簇，香气扑鼻。|近义词：ornate, elaborate, embellished, flamboyant|反义词：plain, simple, unadorned|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *flourye, *florie, from Old French florie (“flowery, blooming”), from flor (“flower”), from Latin flōs (“flower”).
