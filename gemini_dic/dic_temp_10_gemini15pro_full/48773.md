deflaters:/dɪˈfleɪtərz/| n.| 降压器，放气装置，泄压器 |A device or substance that deflates something, especially a tire or balloon.|The deflaters quickly emptied the air mattresses. 放气装置很快地排空了气垫。|近义词：valves, vents, releases|反义词:inflators, pumps|词性:名词

Etymology:  From the verb "deflate," which comes from the prefix "de-" (meaning "down" or "away") + "flate" (from Latin *flātus*, past participle of *flāre* "to blow"). The "-er" suffix denotes an agent or instrument that performs the action.
