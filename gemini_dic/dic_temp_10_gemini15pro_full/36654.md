collegiate:/kəˈliːdʒiət/ /kəˈliːdʒɪət/
adjective
大学的；学院的
Relating to a college or its students.
Collegiate sports attract large crowds.  大学体育运动吸引了大批观众。
近义词: academic, university
反义词: non-collegiate, professional
词性: adjective


collegiate:/kəˈliːdʒiət/ /kəˈliːdʒɪət/
noun
大学生
A college student. (Often used humorously or archaically)
The collegiates gathered at the pub after class.  大学生们下课后聚集在酒吧。
近义词: undergraduate, student
反义词: professor, faculty
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *collegiatus, from Latin collegium (“association, society, guild”).  源自后期拉丁语 *collegiatus，来自拉丁语 collegium（“协会，社团，公会”）。
