nonrequirements:/ˌnɑːnrɪˈkwaɪərmənts/
n.
非必要条件；非强制性要求
Things that are not required or mandatory.
例句：The project manager identified several nonrequirements to streamline the process. 项目经理确定了几个非必要条件以简化流程。

例句：Listing nonrequirements helps to clarify the scope of the project. 列出非必要条件有助于明确项目范围。

近义词：nonessentials, optional features, unnecessary items
反义词：requirements, necessities, essentials
词性：名词
