nonnatives:/nɒnˈneɪtɪvz/
noun
非本地人；非母语者
People who are not native to a particular place or language.
Many nonnatives struggle with the pronunciation of certain sounds. 许多非母语人士难以发出某些音。
synonyms:foreigners, immigrants, aliens
antonyms:natives, locals, indigenous people
