deejay:/ˈdiːdʒeɪ/| n. |唱片骑师，音乐节目主持人|A person who plays recorded music, especially popular music, for an audience, typically on radio or at a club or party.  The term comes from "disc jockey."|例句：The deejay played a great mix of old and new music. 唱片骑师播放了新旧音乐的精彩组合。

例句：She's a popular deejay on the local radio station. 她是当地广播电台的热门唱片骑师。|近义词：DJ, disc jockey|反义词:|n.
