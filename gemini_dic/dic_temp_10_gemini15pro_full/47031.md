dat:/dæt/ | pron. | that（口语化的缩写） |  A colloquial, shortened form of "that". |  Dat's a good idea. = 那是个好主意。 |  That | None | Pronoun


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Dat's my car. = 那(口语)是我的车。
Dat's how it works. = (口语)事情就是这样运作的。

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Dat's happening right now. = (口语)那现在正在发生。
Dat's changing quickly. = (口语)那正在迅速变化。

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
Dat's already been done. = (口语)那已经做过了。
Dat's been a long time coming. = (口语)这已经酝酿很久了。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
Dat's been happening all day. = (口语)那已经一整天都在发生了。
Dat's what I've been saying. = (口语)那就是我一直以来所说的。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
Dat was my fault. = (口语)那曾经是我的错。
Dat happened yesterday. = (口语)那发生在昨天。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
Dat was happening when I arrived. = (口语)当我到达时，那正在发生。
Dat's what I was thinking. = (口语)那就是我当时的想法。


过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
Dat had already happened. = (口语)那已经发生了。
Dat had been decided before the meeting. = (口语)那在会议前就已经决定了。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
Dat had been happening for weeks. = (口语)那已经持续了好几个星期了。
Dat's what we had been discussing. = (口语)那就是我们一直在讨论的。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
Dat'll be fine. = (口语)那会没事的。
Dat'll happen soon. = (口语)那很快就会发生。
