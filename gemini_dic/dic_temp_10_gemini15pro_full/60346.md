egotheism:/ˈiːɡoʊθiːɪzəm/
|n.|自我神论；极端利己主义|The belief that oneself is God or the supreme being; extreme self-centeredness or egotism.|He exhibited a disturbing level of egotheism, believing himself to be divinely inspired.|近义词：egotism, narcissism, self-worship|反义词：altruism, humility|词性: 名词

Etymology:  From ego + theism.  "Ego" comes from the Latin word for "I," and "theism" refers to belief in God or gods.
