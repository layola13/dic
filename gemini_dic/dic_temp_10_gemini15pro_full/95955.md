inculcation:/ɪnˌkʌlˈkeɪʃən/
n.|灌输,谆谆教诲|The inculcation of moral values is the duty of parents and teachers.|道德价值观的灌输是父母和老师的责任。|instillation, indoctrination, implantation|removal, eradication|noun
Etymology->
From Late Latin inculcātiō, from Latin inculcātus, past participle of inculcāre (“to tread or stamp in; to impress”), from in- (“in, into, upon, towards, at, near, against”) + calcāre (“to tread, press”), from calc- (“heel”).
