celestially:/səˈlɛstiəli/ /səˈlɛʃtʃəli/| adv.|天体地，神圣地，超凡地|In a way that relates to the sky or heaven; divinely; supremely well.|例句：The music was celestially beautiful.  这音乐美妙极了。|例句：She felt celestially inspired to paint. 她感觉受到了上天的启示去画画。|近义词：divinely, heavenly, ethereally, supernally|反义词:earthly, worldly, mundane|词性:副词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *caelestialis, from Latin caelestis (“heavenly”), from caelum (“sky, heaven”).
