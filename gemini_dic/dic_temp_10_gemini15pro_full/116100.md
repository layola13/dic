martyrologic: /ˌmɑːrtərəˈlɒdʒɪk/ /ˌmɑːrtərəˈlɑːdʒɪk/ | adj.|殉道者的，殉道录的| Relating to a martyrology (a catalogue or list of martyrs).  It can also describe something that resembles or evokes the style or spirit of a martyrology, often emphasizing suffering or persecution. |  His speech had a martyrologic tone, emphasizing the sacrifices made by the pioneers. 他的演讲带有殉道者的口吻，强调了先驱者们做出的牺牲。

The museum's exhibit included martyrologic accounts of the early Christians.  博物馆的展览包括早期基督徒殉道的记载。 |近义词: hagiographic, saintly |反义词: impious, irreverent|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->

From Late Latin *martyrologicus*, from Greek *martyrologikos*, from *martyrologos* "writer of martyrologies,"  from *martyr* "martyr" + *-logos* "one who deals with (a specific subject)," related to *legein* "to speak, tell."
