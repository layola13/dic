deuteropathic:/ˌdjuːtərəˈpæθɪk/| adj.|继发性病的，继发性病变的|Relating to or arising from a secondary disease or pathological condition.  Caused by a pre-existing disease or condition.|Example: The rash was deuteropathic, a result of his underlying liver disease.  皮疹是继发性的，是他潜在肝病的结果。|Example:  Deuteropathic conditions can often be more challenging to treat than the primary ailment. 继发性疾病通常比原发疾病更难治疗。|近义词:secondary, consequential|反义词:primary, idiopathic|词性:adjective

deuteropathy:/ˌdjuːtərəˈpɒθi/| n.|继发性疾病|A secondary disease or pathological condition resulting from another disease.|Example: The deuteropathy developed as a complication of the initial infection.  这种继发性疾病是最初感染的并发症。|Example:  Understanding the underlying cause is crucial in managing the deuteropathy effectively. 了解根本原因对于有效管理继发性疾病至关重要。|近义词:secondary disease, sequela|反义词:primary disease|词性:noun
