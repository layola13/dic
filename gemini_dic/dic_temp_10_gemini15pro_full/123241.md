monoculturally:/ˌmɒnəʊˈkʌltʃərəli/ | adv. | 单一文化地 |  In a way that relates to or is characterized by a single culture, often excluding or marginalizing other cultures.  |  The community was criticized for functioning too monoculturally, neglecting the contributions of immigrant groups.  这个社区因其运作过于单一文化而受到批评，忽视了移民群体的贡献。 |  homogeneously, uniformly | diversely, multiculturally | adverb
