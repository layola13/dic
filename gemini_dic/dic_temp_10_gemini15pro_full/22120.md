bolt-action:/ˈboʊltˌækʃən/| adj. n.|栓动式的，栓动步枪|A type of firearm action in which the user manually manipulates a bolt to chamber a round, lock the breech, fire the cartridge, and eject the spent casing.|The hunter used a bolt-action rifle. 猎人使用了一把栓动步枪。|He prefers the precision of a bolt-action. 他更喜欢栓动式步枪的精度。|近义词:lever-action, pump-action|反义词:semi-automatic, automatic|词性:adjective, noun|词源:bolt (n.) + action (n.)
