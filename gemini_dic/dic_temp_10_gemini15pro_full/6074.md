amerasians:/əˌmɛrˈeɪʒənz/ | n. | 亚裔美国人（特指美亚混血儿）| People of mixed Asian and American descent, specifically those whose fathers served in the U.S. military and whose mothers were Asian.  Often used in the context of the Vietnam War, but can refer to other Asian countries as well.|  Many Amerasians faced discrimination in both Asia and America. 许多亚裔美国人在亚洲和美国都面临歧视。|Synonyms: multiracial, mixed-race, biracial | Antonyms: uniracial | noun
