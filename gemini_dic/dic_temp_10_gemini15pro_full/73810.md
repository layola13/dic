formatting:/ˈfɔːrˌmætɪŋ/ |n.|格式化，格式编排|The formatting of this document is inconsistent.  这份文件的格式不一致。|例句：The formatting of the website makes it easy to navigate.  这个网站的格式编排使其易于浏览。

|例句：Please check the formatting of your essay before submitting it.  请在提交论文前检查一下格式。|近义词：arrangement, layout, structure|反义词：disorganization|词性:noun

Etymology: From format + -ing.  "Format" comes from the French "format" (size, shape), ultimately from the Latin "forma" (form, shape).
