一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? 你早餐吃什么？
What does she do for a living? 她做什么工作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching right now? 你现在在看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? 他在花园里做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? 你今天学到了什么？
What has she done with the money? 她用这笔钱做了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on lately? 你最近一直在做什么？
What has she been reading this week? 她这周一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do last night? 你昨晚做了什么？
What happened at the party? 派对上发生了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about when I saw her? 我看到她的时候她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you done before you left? 你离开之前做了什么？
What had they eaten before the movie? 他们在看电影之前吃了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you?  在我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about before they calmed down? 在他们平静下来之前他们一直在争论什么？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? 你下周末要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? 明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
