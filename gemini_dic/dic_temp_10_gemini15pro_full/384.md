abiogenist: /ˌeɪbaɪˈɒdʒɪnɪst/ /ˌeɪbaɪˈɑːdʒɪnɪst/
noun
非生物成因论者；自然发生论者
A scientist who believes that life arose from inorganic matter.  One who studies or believes in abiogenesis.
The abiogenist presented his theory on the origin of life.  这位非生物成因论者提出了他关于生命起源的理论。
An abiogenist might study the conditions of early Earth. 一位非生物成因论者可能会研究早期地球的状况。

synonyms:  proponent of abiogenesis, supporter of spontaneous generation (historical)
antonyms:  creationist, biogenist (rarely used)
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From a- (not) + bio- (life) + genesis (origin).  Coined in the 19th century.
