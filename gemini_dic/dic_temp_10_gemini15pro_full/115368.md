manqueller:/ˈmænˌkwɛlər/
noun
杀人犯；凶手
A person who kills another human being.
例句：In the medieval ballad, the manqueller was brought to justice.  在中世纪的民谣中，杀人凶手被绳之以法。

例句：The manqueller showed no remorse for his heinous crime.  凶手对自己的滔天罪行毫无悔意。

近义词：murderer, killer, homicide, assassin, slayer
反义词：savior, protector
词性：n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *manquellere, equivalent to man + queller (“killer”).  From Old English *manncwellere, equivalent to mann (“man”) + cwellere (“killer”). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *manną (“man”) + *kwaljaną (“to kill, torment”).
