closetfuls:/ˈklɒzɪtfʊlz/ (UK) /ˈklɑːzɪtfʊlz/ (US)| n.|满满几柜;  大量的|A large quantity of something, enough to fill multiple closets.  It emphasizes abundance.|She had closetfuls of clothes she never wore. 她有满满几柜的衣服从未穿过。|Synonyms: abundance, plethora, heaps, loads|Antonyms: scarcity, lack, dearth|noun
