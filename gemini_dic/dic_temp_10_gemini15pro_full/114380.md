makaron:/ˌmækəˈrɒn/ /ˌmækəˈroʊn/ | n. | 杏仁蛋白饼; 马卡龙 (一种用杏仁粉、糖和蛋白制成的甜点) | A small round cake made of ground almonds, sugar, and egg white. | Example:  She bought a box of colorful makarons from the bakery. 她从面包店买了一盒五颜六色的马卡龙。

Example: The makarons were delicate and flavorful. 这些马卡龙精致美味。 | 近义词: macaroon | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a type of food) | 词性: noun
