cultrivorous:/kʌlˈtrɪvərəs/
adjective
食刀的；用刀切割的
Feeding on or habitually using knives.  The cultrivorous mantis uses its sharp forelegs like knives to capture and devour its prey.
The cultrivorous ritual involved cutting symbolic patterns into the earth.
近义词:knife-wielding, sharp-edged
反义词:blunt, dull
词性:adjective

cultrivorous:/kʌlˈtrɪvərəs/
noun
食刀者；用刀切割者
A creature or person that feeds on or habitually uses knives. In the fantasy novel, the cultrivorous beast terrorized the village with its blade-like appendages.
The archaeologist unearthed evidence suggesting a cultrivorous society that worshipped sharp objects.
近义词:knife-user, blade-wielder
反义词:pacifist
词性:noun
