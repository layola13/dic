ecteron: /ˈektərɒn/ | n. | 外胚层, 外层 | The outermost of the three primary germ layers of an embryo, from which the epidermis, nervous system, and sensory organs develop. | The ecteron gives rise to the skin and nervous system. 外胚层会形成皮肤和神经系统。| The ecteron folds inward to form the neural tube. 外胚层向内折叠形成神经管。| synonyms: ectoderm, epiblast | antonyms: endoderm, mesoderm | noun
