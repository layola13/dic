lobe:/loʊb/ (US), /ləʊb/ (UK)| n. |叶，瓣，耳垂|A rounded projection or division, especially of an organ such as the lung, brain, or liver.|The frontal lobe of the brain is responsible for higher cognitive functions.|Synonyms: segment, section, division|Antonyms: whole, entire|Noun

lobe:/loʊb/ (US), /ləʊb/ (UK)| n. |（植物的）裂片，圆形突出物|A rounded projection or division, especially of a leaf or petal.|The leaves of the oak tree have distinctive lobes.|Synonyms: segment, section, division|Antonyms: whole, entire|Noun
