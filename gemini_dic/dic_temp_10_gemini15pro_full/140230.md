officious: /əˈfɪʃəs/| adj.|爱管闲事的，多事的|Assertive of authority in an annoyingly domineering way, especially with regard to petty or trivial matters.|例句：The officious waiter kept interrupting our conversation. 多管闲事的服务员不断打断我们的谈话。|例句：He found the security guard officious and unnecessarily strict. 他觉得保安人员爱管闲事，不必要地严格。|近义词：meddlesome, interfering, intrusive, pushy|反义词：helpful, deferential, respectful|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *officiosus*, from *officium* (“duty, service”).
