nonconjunction:/ˌnɒnkənˈdʒʌŋkʃən/ | n. | 非合取；非连接 | The absence of conjunction, or the state of not being conjoined.  A logical operation that produces a true result only if both operands are false. | The nonconjunction of these two events led to unforeseen consequences. 这两件事没有同时发生导致了无法预见的后果。 |  non-occurrence, disjunction | conjunction, union | noun
