explicitly: /ɪkˈsplɪsɪtli/ /ɛkˈsplɪsɪtli/ | adv. | 明确地，清楚地 | stated clearly and in detail, leaving no room for confusion or doubt |  The instructions explicitly stated that the machine should not be used near water.  说明书明确指出，该机器不得在靠近水的地方使用。

She explicitly forbade him from attending the party. 她明确禁止他参加聚会。

| 近义词: clearly, definitely, specifically, directly, unequivocally | 反义词: implicitly, vaguely, obscurely | 词性: 副词
