fringing:/ˈfrɪndʒɪŋ/| n. |边缘，穗边，毛边；次要部分；附加效应|An ornamental border of threads left loose or formed into tassels or twists.  A secondary or peripheral activity or effect.|The rug had a bright red fringing. 这块地毯有亮红色的穗边。|Synonyms: border, edge, fringe, rim, margin|Antonyms: center, core, middle|noun

fringing:/ˈfrɪndʒɪŋ/| adj. |边缘的，外围的|Located on the edge or periphery.|The fringing reefs of the island are teeming with marine life. 岛屿边缘的珊瑚礁充满了海洋生物。|Synonyms: peripheral, marginal, outer, outlying|Antonyms: central, inner, core|adjective
