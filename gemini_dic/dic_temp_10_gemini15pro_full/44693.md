cruddy:/ˈkrʌdi/| adj.|糟糕的，差劲的，肮脏的|Something that is cruddy is dirty, unpleasant, or of poor quality.  It can also refer to feeling unwell.|The apartment was small and cruddy. 这公寓又小又脏。|近义词：lousy, awful, terrible, disgusting, grimy, dirty|反义词:excellent, great, wonderful, clean|词性:形容词

词源:
From crud + -y.  Crud itself likely originated as a shortening of curdled.
