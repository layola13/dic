chelifer: /ˈkɛlɪfər/ | n. | 螯肢动物; 伪蝎 | An arachnid of the order Chelonethida, having pedipalps resembling the pincers of a scorpion. | Example:  The chelifer, though small, is a fierce predator in the leaf litter.  这种螯肢动物虽然很小，但在落叶层中是一个凶猛的捕食者。

Example: He discovered a tiny chelifer hiding under a rock. 他在一块石头下发现了一只微小的螯肢动物。 | 近义词: pseudoscorpion | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

Etymology: From New Latin *Chelifer, from Ancient Greek χηλή (khēlḗ, “claw, pincer”) + -fer (from Latin -fer, “-bearing”).
