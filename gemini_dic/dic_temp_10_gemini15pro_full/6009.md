ameba:/əˈmiːbə/ (US), /əˈmiːbə/ (UK)| n. | 阿米巴，变形虫 | A single-celled animal that catches food and moves about by extending fingerlike projections of protoplasm.  A microscopic organism belonging to the kingdom Protista, characterized by its ability to change shape and move by extending pseudopods.|  The ameba is a simple organism. 变形虫是一种简单的生物。| synonyms: amoeba, protozoan | antonyms: multicellular organism | noun

amebas:/əˈmiːbəz/ (US), /əˈmiːbəz/ (UK)| n. | 阿米巴，变形虫（复数） | Plural form of ameba.  Multiple single-celled animals that catch food and move about by extending fingerlike projections of protoplasm.| Amebas can be found in freshwater.  变形虫可以在淡水中找到。| synonyms: amoebas, protozoa | antonyms: multicellular organisms | noun
