ongoingly:/ˌɒnˈɡəʊɪŋli/ /ˌɑːnˈɡoʊɪŋli/
adverb|持续地，不断地|Continuing; still in progress.|The investigation is ongoingly pursued.  调查正在持续进行中。|例句：The project is ongoingly being developed.  这个项目正在持续开发中。|近义词：continuously, constantly, persistently, perpetually|反义词：intermittently, sporadically|词性:副词
词源: 由"ongoing"（进行中的）加上副词后缀"-ly"构成。 "ongoing" 来自 "on" + "going"，表示持续进行的动作或状态。
