half-worsted:/ˌhɑːf ˈwɜːrstɪd/ |adj.|半精纺的|Made from yarn that is less tightly twisted than worsted yarn, resulting in a slightly softer and fuzzier fabric.|This half-worsted yarn is perfect for knitting a cozy sweater. 这款半精纺纱线非常适合织舒适的毛衣。|Example 2: The half-worsted fabric drapes beautifully. 半精纺面料悬垂性很好。|Near synonyms: semi-worsted, light worsted|Opposite: worsted|adjective
