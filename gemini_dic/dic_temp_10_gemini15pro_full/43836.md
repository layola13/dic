crepane:/kreɪˈpeɪn/ /krɪˈpæn/ | n. |薄饼，可丽饼 |A thin pancake.  A type of very thin pancake, often served rolled up with a sweet or savoury filling.|例句：I had a delicious crepe filled with Nutella and bananas for breakfast. 我早餐吃了一个美味的可丽饼，里面装满了巧克力榛子酱和香蕉。

例句：Street vendors sell crepes with various fillings. 街头小贩出售各种馅料的可丽饼。|近义词：pancake, blini |反义词: N/A |词性:noun

Etymology: From French *crêpe*, from Old French *crespe*, meaning "curled," from Latin *crispus*, meaning "curled, wrinkled."
