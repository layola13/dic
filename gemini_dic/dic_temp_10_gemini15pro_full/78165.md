geordies:/ˈdʒɔːdiːz/
noun
乔迪人（纽卡斯尔及泰恩赛德地区的人）
People from Newcastle upon Tyne and the surrounding area, famous for their distinctive accent and dialect.
例句：The Geordies are known for their friendly nature. 乔迪人以其友好的天性而闻名。

例句：Many Geordies are passionate supporters of Newcastle United Football Club. 许多乔迪人都是纽卡斯尔联足球俱乐部的热情支持者。

近义词：Newcastle people, northeasterners (more general)
反义词: (No direct antonym, as it refers to a specific group of people)
词性:n.
