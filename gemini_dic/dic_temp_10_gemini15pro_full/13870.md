augmentors:/ɔːɡˈmɛntərz/
n.
增强者；增幅器；扩张器
People or things that augment something; something that increases or strengthens.
The new software includes several augmentors designed to improve productivity.  这款新软件包含几个旨在提高生产力的增强器。
Researchers are exploring augmentors for human intelligence.  研究人员正在探索人类智力的增强器。
Synonyms: enhancer, booster, amplifier, intensifier
Antonyms: reducer, diminisher, weakener
