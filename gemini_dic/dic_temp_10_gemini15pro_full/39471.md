connivence:/kəˈnaɪvəns/
n.
共谋；纵容；默许
Secret or tacit consent to wrongdoing.
The crime was committed with the connivance of the security guard.  这起犯罪是在保安人员的纵容下进行的。
conniving, collusion, complicity
opposition, resistance
noun
