outfeed:/ˈaʊtfiːd/
noun
输出;出料;输出物
The processed material emerges from the machine's outfeed. 加工后的材料从机器的输出口出来。
The outfeed of the sawmill was piled high with lumber. 锯木厂的输出物堆满了木材。
近义词: output, discharge, effluent
反义词: intake, input
词性: 名词


outfeed:/ˈaʊtfiːd/
verb
输出;送出
The machine outfeeds the finished product onto a conveyor belt. 机器将成品输出到传送带上。
The system outfeeds data to a central server. 系统将数据输出到中央服务器。
近义词: output, deliver, eject
反义词: input, receive
词性: 动词
