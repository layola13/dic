customly:/ˈkʌstəmli/| adv.|习惯地，通常地|Usually; habitually.  Derived from "custom" (habitual practice) with the suffix "-ly" (forming adverbs).|He customly arrives early for meetings. 他通常早到开会。|近义词：usually, habitually, typically, ordinarily, normally|反义词:unusually, rarely, seldom|词性:副词

词源: From "custom" + "-ly".  "Custom" comes from Old French "costume"  (habit, usage), ultimately from Latin "consuescere," frequentative of "suescere" (to become accustomed).
