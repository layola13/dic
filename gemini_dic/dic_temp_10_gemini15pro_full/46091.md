cyclostomous:/ˌsaɪkləˈstoʊməs/
adjective
圆口的，环口的
Having a circular mouth; belonging to or resembling the Cyclostomata (a group of jawless fish).
The lamprey is a cyclostomous fish. 七鳃鳗是一种圆口鱼。
近义词: jawless
反义词: jawed
词性: adjective

Etymology:
From cyclo- (circular) + -stomous (mouthed), from Greek *kyklos* (circle) + *stoma* (mouth).
