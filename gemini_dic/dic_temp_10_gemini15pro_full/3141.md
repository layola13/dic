aesthetics:/ɛsˈθɛtɪks/ /iːsˈθɛtɪks/ | n. |美学；审美观|A set of principles concerned with the nature and appreciation of beauty, especially in art.|The aesthetics of the building are very pleasing. 这座建筑的美学令人非常愉悦。|近义词：beauty, taste, artistry|反义词：ugliness, vulgarity|noun


aesthetics:/ɛsˈθɛtɪks/ /iːsˈθɛtɪks/ | adj. |审美的；美学的|Relating to the philosophy of aesthetics; concerned with notions such as the beautiful and the ugly.|The new gallery is showcasing aesthetics design.  新的画廊正在展示审美设计。|近义词：artistic, beautiful|反义词：ugly, displeasing|adjective
