affecter's:/əˈfɛktərz/
n.|影响者，感染者|Someone or something that affects or influences something else.  The word itself is uncommon and usually arises in specific contexts like discussing diseases or psychological influence.|The affecter's actions had a profound impact on the group.  影响者的行为对这个群体产生了深远的影响。|近义词:influencer, agent, cause|反义词:N/A|词性:名词

Etymology: From the verb "affect" + "-er" (agent suffix) + "-'s" (possessive suffix). "Affect" comes from the Latin "afficere," meaning "to act on, influence."
