enfranchisable:/ɪnˈfræntʃaɪzəbl/
adjective
可赋予公民权的; 可给予选举权的
Capable of being enfranchised, or admitted to political privileges; capable of receiving the franchise.
Formerly enslaved people in the United States were enfranchisable after the Civil War.  美国内战后，以前的奴隶被赋予了公民权。
The company's employees became enfranchisable in the company's decision-making process. 该公司的员工在公司的决策过程中获得了投票权。
近义词:qualifiable, eligible
反义词:disenfranchisable, ineligible
词性:adjective
