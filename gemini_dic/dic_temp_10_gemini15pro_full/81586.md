greasepaint:/ˈɡriːspeɪnt/ /ˈɡriːsˌpeɪnt/
noun
油彩; 演员化妆油彩
A type of makeup used by actors, especially in the theatre.
The actor applied the greasepaint thickly to his face.  演员往脸上涂了厚厚的油彩。

greasepaint:/ˈɡriːspeɪnt/ /ˈɡriːsˌpeɪnt/
noun
演员生涯; 舞台生活 (figurative)
Theatrical activities or the world of the theatre.
He spent his life in greasepaint, touring from town to town.  他一生都在舞台上度过，从一个城市巡演到另一个城市。

近义词:makeup, stage makeup, theatrical makeup
反义词: (No direct antonym, but one could consider "natural look" or "bare face")
