habitably:/ˈhæbɪtəbli/
adjective
可居住地；适宜居住地
Suitable for habitation; offering the conditions necessary for people to live.
The planet is not currently habitably, but scientists are exploring ways to make it so.  这个星球目前不适宜居住，但科学家们正在探索使其适宜居住的方法。
Mars may one day be made habitably.  火星有一天或许可以变得适宜居住。
近义词:inhabitable, livable
反义词:uninhabitable, unlivable
词性:adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *habitabilis, from Latin habitare (“to inhabit”) + -bilis (“-able”).
