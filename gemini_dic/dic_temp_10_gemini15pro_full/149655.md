pair:/per/ (US) /peə/ (UK)| n. v.|一对，一双；配对|A set of two things used together or regarded as a unit. |例句：I need a new pair of shoes. 我需要一双新鞋。|近义词：couple, duo, set|反义词:single, individual|noun
pair:/per/ (US) /peə/ (UK)| v.|使成对；配对|To join or match (two people or things) together. |例句：The teacher paired us up for the project. 老师把我们配对做这个项目。|近义词：match, couple, link|反义词:separate, divide|verb
