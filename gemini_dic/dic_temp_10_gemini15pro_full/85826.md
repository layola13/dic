haziness:/ˈheɪzinəs/| n.|模糊，朦胧，不清楚|The haziness of the photograph made it difficult to identify the people in it. 照片的模糊使得很难辨认出里面的人。|近义词：mistiness, fogginess, obscurity, vagueness|反义词: clarity, sharpness, distinctness|词性:名词

Etymology->
From hazy + -ness. Hazy itself comes from haze, which has uncertain origins, possibly related to Old Norse *höss "greyish white."
