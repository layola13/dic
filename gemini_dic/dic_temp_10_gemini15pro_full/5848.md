amazements:/əˈmeɪzmənts/
n.
惊奇，惊叹
a feeling of great surprise or wonder
The child stared in amazement at the magician. 孩子惊奇地盯着魔术师。
Synonyms: wonder, astonishment, awe
Antonyms: apathy, indifference
