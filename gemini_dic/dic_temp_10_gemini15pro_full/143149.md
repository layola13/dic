outcharges:/ˈaʊtˌtʃɑːrdʒɪz/
noun
额外费用；附加费用
The cost of shipping is included, but there may be outcharges for special handling.  运费已包含在内，但特殊处理可能会有额外费用。
The invoice listed several outcharges for packing and insurance. 发票上列出了几项包装和保险的额外费用。
Synonym: surcharges, extra charges, additional fees
Antonym: discounts, rebates
