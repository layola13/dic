词源:

From Italian *gioia*, from Late Latin *gaudia*, plural of *gaudium* (“joy”), from Latin *gaudeo* (“I rejoice”). Cognate with Old French *joie*, French *joie*, Spanish *gozo*. Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *\*gā̆w-*, *\*gōu̯h₂-* (“to rejoice”).
