domesticant: /dəˈmɛstɪkənt/
noun
佣人，家仆
A person employed to perform household tasks.
The family employed a domesticant to help with the cleaning and cooking.  这个家庭雇佣了一个佣人来帮忙做清洁和烹饪。
近义词: servant, maid, housekeeper
反义词: employer, master
词性: n.
