cabbing:/ˈkæbɪŋ/| n. vi.| 乘出租车；开出租车 |The act of traveling or working as a taxi driver.  | Example:  I was cabbing home from the airport. 我从机场打车回家。

| Example: He makes a living by cabbing in the city. 他靠在城里开出租车谋生。 | 近义词: taxiing, taking a taxi, driving a cab | 反义词: walking, cycling, driving (one's own car) | 词性: noun, verb (gerund or present participle)
