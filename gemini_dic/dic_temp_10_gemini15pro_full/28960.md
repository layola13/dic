carry the day:/ˈkæri ðə deɪ/
verb phrase
获胜，占上风
to be successful against opposition; to win
例句：Despite strong opposition, their innovative proposal carried the day. 尽管反对声强烈，他们创新的提案最终胜出。

例句：The superior argument carried the day, and the proposal was adopted.  更胜一筹的论点占了上风，提案被采纳了。

近义词：win, triumph, prevail, succeed
反义词：lose, fail, be defeated

词性: verb phrase
词源: This phrase originates from the 16th century and likely refers to carrying off the prize or honors at the end of a contest or battle.
