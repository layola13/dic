herbose:/hɜːrˈboʊs/ /ɜːrˈboʊs/| adj.|草本的；草味浓的|Characterized by a strong herbaceous odor or flavor, resembling grass or herbs.|The tea had a herbose, slightly minty flavor.  这茶有一种草本的、略带薄荷的味道。|近义词：grassy, leafy, vegetal|反义词:fruity, floral|词性:形容词
词源: From French *herbeux*, from herbe (“herb”) + -eux (suffix forming adjectives). Ultimately from Latin *herba* (“grass, herb”).
