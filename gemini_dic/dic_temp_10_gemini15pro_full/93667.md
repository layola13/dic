imbuing:/ɪmˈbjuːɪŋ/ | v. | 灌输，使充满 | to fill or inspire someone or something with a particular feeling, quality, or idea |  The writer imbued his novel with a sense of hope.  作家给他的小说灌输了一种希望感。 |  instilling, infusing, permeating, saturating | draining, emptying | verb

imbued:/ɪmˈbjuːd/ | adj. | 充满...的 | filled or inspired with |  He was imbued with a sense of patriotism.  他充满了爱国主义精神。| infused, saturated, steeped | devoid, lacking | adjective
