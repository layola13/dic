goofily:/ˈɡuːfɪli/| adv.|傻傻地，笨拙地|In a silly or foolish manner, often in a way that is endearing or amusing.|He grinned goofily at the camera. 他对着镜头傻傻地咧嘴一笑。|近义词：foolishly, clumsily, awkwardly, absurdly, ridiculously|反义词：seriously, sensibly, gracefully|词性: 副词

词源:  From goofy (adjective) + -ly (adverb suffix).  "Goofy" itself likely originated in the early 20th century, possibly as a variant of "goop" or "gawky," both referring to awkwardness or silliness.
