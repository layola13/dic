insuperable:/ɪnˈsuːpərəbl/ /ɪnˈsʊpərəbəl/
adjective
不可克服的；无法超越的
incapable of being overcome or defeated.
例句：The challenge seemed insuperable at first, but they persevered.  起初，挑战似乎不可克服，但他们坚持了下来。

例句：She faced what seemed like insuperable odds. 她面对着看似不可逾越的困难。

近义词：insurmountable, unconquerable, invincible
反义词：surmountable, conquerable, vincible
词性：adjective
