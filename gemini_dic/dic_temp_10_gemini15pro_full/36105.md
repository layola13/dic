coequally:/koʊˈiːkwəli/
| adv. |同等地，平等地 |In a truly democratic society, all citizens should be treated coequally. 在一个真正的民主社会中，所有公民都应该被平等对待。|  Coequally important is the need for education.  同样重要的是教育的需求。 | 近义词: equally, evenly, uniformly | 反义词: unequally, disproportionately | 词性: 副词

Etymology:
From Latin *coaeque* (unattested) + *-alis*, from *co-* (together, jointly) + *aequus* (equal, level, fair).
