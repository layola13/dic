inconsequentiality:/ɪnˌkɒnsɪˈkwɛnʃiˈælɪti/ /ɪnˌkɑːnsɪˈkwɛnʃiˈælɪti/| n. |无关紧要，不重要|The state or quality of being inconsequential; lack of importance or significance.|The inconsequentiality of the matter was apparent to everyone. 这件事的无关紧要对每个人来说都是显而易见的。|Synonyms: insignificance, triviality, unimportance|Antonyms: importance, significance, consequence|Noun

inconsequential:/ˌɪnkɒnsɪˈkwɛnʃəl/ /ˌɪnkɑːnsɪˈkwɛnʃəl/| adj. |无关紧要的，不重要的|Not important or significant; trivial.|He dismissed the issue as inconsequential. 他认为这个问题无关紧要而不予理会。|Synonyms: insignificant, trivial, unimportant, negligible, petty|Antonyms: important, significant, consequential, crucial, vital|Adjective
