frisette:/frɪˈzɛt/| n. |卷发，波浪形假发|A style of hair created by tightly curling or crimping it, often using a heated iron.  A small, tight curl or frizzed piece of hair, especially one in a wig or hairpiece.|She wore a frisette to give her hair more volume. 她戴了一个波浪形假发让头发看起来更蓬松。|近义词：curl, ringlet, crimp|反义词:straight hair|词性:noun
frisette:/frɪˈzɛt/| n. |波形褶边|A ruffled, crimped, or wavy edging, as on fabric or lace.|The dress was adorned with a delicate frisette along the neckline.  这件连衣裙的领口装饰着精致的波浪形褶边。|近义词：ruffle, flounce, frill|反义词:plain edge|词性:noun
