classicalism:/ˈklæsɪsɪzəm/| n.|古典主义|A style in art, architecture, literature, and music that is based on the styles of ancient Greece and Rome.|The museum's collection showcases the beauty of European classicalism.  这家博物馆的藏品展示了欧洲古典主义之美。

The architect's designs reflect a strong influence of classicalism.  这位建筑师的设计体现了古典主义的强烈影响。|近义词：classicism, traditionalism|反义词：modernism, romanticism|词性:noun
ETYMOLOGY->
From French *classicisme*, from *classique* (“classical”), from Latin *classicus* (“belonging to the highest class of citizens”), from *classis* ("class"). Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱleys- (“to call, shout”).
