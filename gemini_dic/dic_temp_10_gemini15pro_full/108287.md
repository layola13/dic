laurasias:/lɔːˈreɪʒəz/ | n. | 劳亚大陆 |  A large continental mass in the Northern Hemisphere, comprising North America, Greenland, Europe, and Asia (except peninsular India). It existed during the Mesozoic Era before splitting into the continents we know today. | Laurasia and Gondwana were two supercontinents. 劳亚大陆和冈瓦纳大陆是两个超级大陆。| The geological formations of Laurasia show evidence of similar flora and fauna across its landmasses. 劳亚大陆的地质构造显示其陆地上的动植物群相似。 | synonym: supercontinent | antonym: N/A | noun
