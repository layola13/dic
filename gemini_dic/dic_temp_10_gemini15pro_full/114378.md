makabayan:/ˌmɑːkəˈbɑːjən/ | adj. |爱国的，民族主义的 | Showing love and loyalty to one's country, patriotic, nationalistic.  Derived from the Tagalog words "maka-" (for, pro-) and "bayan" (country).  |例句：Siya ay isang makabayang Pilipino. = He/She is a patriotic Filipino.


例句：Ang pagboto ay isang makabayang tungkulin. = Voting is a patriotic duty.


近义词：patriotic, nationalistic | 反义词：unpatriotic, traitorous | 词性: adjective
