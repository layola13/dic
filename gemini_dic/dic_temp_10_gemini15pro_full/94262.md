impermissibilities:/ˌɪmpərˌmɪsəˈbɪlətiz/
|n.|不可允许的事物；不允许的行为|The impermissibilities of certain actions in formal settings. 在正式场合某些行为的不可允许性。|
|例句：The company's code of conduct outlines the impermissibilities of accepting bribes. 公司的行为准则概述了受贿的不可允许性。|

|例句：The teacher stressed the impermissibilities of plagiarism in academic work. 老师强调了学术工作中剽窃的不可允许性。|
|近义词：illegalities, improprieties, offenses, transgressions|
|反义词：permissibilities, allowances|
|词性：名词（plural noun）|
