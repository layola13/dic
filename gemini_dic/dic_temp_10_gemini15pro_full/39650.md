conservationists:/ˌkɑːnsərˈveɪʃənɪsts/
n.
自然资源保护者，环保主义者
A person who advocates or acts for the protection and preservation of the natural environment and its resources.
Conservationists are working to protect the rainforest.  环保主义者正在努力保护雨林。
The conservationists protested the new dam project. 这些环保人士抗议新的水坝项目。

近义词:environmentalists, preservationists, ecologists
反义词:developers, polluters, destroyers

词性:noun


词源：Late 19th century: from conservation + -ist.
