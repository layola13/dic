fidgets:/ˈfɪdʒɪts/| n. |坐立不安，烦躁不安|A state of restlessness and nervous activity, typically manifested by small movements of the body, especially the hands and feet.|She had the fidgets and couldn't sit still. 她坐立不安，无法安静地坐着。|Synonyms: restlessness, unease, nervousness|Antonyms: calmness, composure|Noun

fidget:/ˈfɪdʒɪt/| vi. & vt.|烦躁不安，坐立不安|To make small movements, especially of the hands and feet, through nervousness or impatience.|He fidgeted with his pen during the meeting.  会议期间，他不停地摆弄他的钢笔。|Synonyms: twitch, squirm, wriggle|Antonyms: relax, be still|Verb
