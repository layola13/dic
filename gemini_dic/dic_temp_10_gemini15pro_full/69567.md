felonious:/fəˈloʊniəs/
adjective
重罪的；极恶的
pertaining to or constituting a felony; wicked; villainous.
The felonious act of stealing the car resulted in a lengthy prison sentence.  偷车这种重罪行为导致了漫长的牢狱之灾。
Synonyms: criminal, illegal, unlawful, illicit, villainous, nefarious
Antonyms: lawful, legal, innocent, virtuous, righteous
词性: adjective
