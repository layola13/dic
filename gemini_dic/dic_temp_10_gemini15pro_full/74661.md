freebase:/ˈfriːbeɪs/| n.| 自由基,  免费数据库 (专有名词)|A collaborative knowledge base that aims to structure general human knowledge.  It was developed by Google and shut down in 2014. |Google's Freebase project was an ambitious attempt to categorize all the world's information. 谷歌的Freebase项目是一次雄心勃勃的尝试，旨在对世界上的所有信息进行分类。|The researchers used Freebase to train their AI model. 研究人员使用Freebase来训练他们的AI模型。|近义词：knowledge base, database, Wikidata|反义词: None (lacks a direct opposite)|noun


free:/friː/| adj.| 自由的, 免费的, 空闲的|Not under the control or in the power of another; costing nothing.|The birds were free to fly wherever they wanted. 鸟儿可以自由地飞到任何想去的地方。|The software is free to download.  这款软件可以免费下载。|近义词：liberated, independent, gratis, complimentary|反义词: restricted, captive, paid, costly|adjective
