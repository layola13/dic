day:/deɪ/
n.|一天，白天|A period of 24 hours, especially from twelve o'clock midnight to the next midnight. |例句：It took three days to finish the project. 完成这个项目花了三天时间。|例句：We spent the day at the beach. 我们在海滩上度过了一天。|近义词：daylight, daytime|反义词:night|词性:名词

day:/deɪ/
n.|白昼，日间|The time between sunrise and sunset. |例句：The days are getting longer. 白天越来越长了。|例句：Birds sing during the day. 鸟儿在白天唱歌。|近义词：daylight, daytime|反义词:night|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *dæg, from Proto-Germanic *dagaz, from Proto-Indo-European *dʰegʰ-. Cognates include Dutch dag, German Tag, and Latin dies.
