burglary: /ˈbɜːrɡləri/ (US) /ˈbɜːɡləri/ (UK)| n. |盗窃，入室盗窃|The act of illegally entering a building and stealing things.  It involves entering a structure with the intent to commit a crime, typically theft.|  例句：The burglary occurred in the middle of the night.  入室盗窃发生在午夜。

例句：There has been a spate of burglaries in the area recently. 最近该地区发生了一连串的入室盗窃案。|近义词：robbery, theft, housebreaking, larceny|反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可用lawful entry 合法进入)|词性:noun
