annoying:/əˈnɔɪɪŋ/ | adj. | 讨厌的，恼人的 | Causing irritation or annoyance. |  The constant dripping tap was very annoying. 不断滴水的水龙头很烦人。

| 近义词: irritating, bothersome, vexing, troublesome | 反义词: pleasant, agreeable, delightful | 词性: 形容词


annoyous:  This is not a standard English word.  The correct word is "annoying".
