argument: /ˈɑːrɡjumənt/ (US) /ˈɑːɡjʊmənt/ (UK) | n. | 争论，辩论；论据，理由 | A reason or set of reasons given with the aim of persuading others that an action or idea is right or wrong. |  She had a strong argument for leaving early. 她有充分的理由早点离开。


| Example: The argument for and against the death penalty continue. 关于死刑的正反两方争论仍在继续。| Synonym: dispute, debate, contention, disagreement, quarrel, row, fight, squabble | Antonym: agreement, harmony, concord, accord | Noun
