micro-enterprise:/ˌmaɪkroʊˈɛntərpraɪz/ | n. | 微型企业 |A small business that employs a very small number of people, often operated by the owner or family members. | Example: Many micro-enterprises operate in the informal economy. 许多微型企业在非正规经济中运营。

Example:  The government is providing funding to support micro-enterprises. 政府正在提供资金以支持微型企业。 | Synonyms: small business, microbusiness, cottage industry | Antonyms: large corporation, multinational company | noun
