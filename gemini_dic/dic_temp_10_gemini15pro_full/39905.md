constitutive:/ˈkɒnstɪtjuːtɪv/ /ˈkɑːnstɪtuːtɪv/| adj.|组成的，构成的；基本的，本质的；制定性的，创立的|Having the power to establish or give organized existence to something; forming a part or element of something; inherent.|例句：Language is constitutive of thought. 语言构成思想。|近义词：essential, basic, fundamental, inherent, integral|反义词：nonessential, incidental|词性:形容词

constitutive:/ˈkɒnstɪtjuːtɪv/ /ˈkɑːnstɪtuːtɪv/| n.|组成要素，构成部分|A constituent part or element.|例句：The constitutive elements of water are hydrogen and oxygen.  水的组成元素是氢和氧。|近义词：component, element, ingredient|反义词：None|词性:名词
