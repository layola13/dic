loss-making:/ˈlɒsˌmeɪkɪŋ/ (US) /ˈlɔːsˌmeɪkɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|亏损的，赔钱的|Producing a financial loss; unprofitable.|The company closed its loss-making division.  这家公司关闭了亏损的部门。|Example: The loss-making factory was finally shut down. 这家亏损的工厂最终倒闭了。|Synonyms: unprofitable, uneconomic, in the red|Antonyms: profitable, lucrative, in the black|adjective
