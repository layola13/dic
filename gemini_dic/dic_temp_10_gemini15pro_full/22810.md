bosomier:/ˈbuːzəmɪər/ | adj. | 更亲密的，更贴心的 | More intimate or affectionate.  |  She felt bosomier with her sister after they shared their secrets.  她和她姐姐分享了秘密之后，感觉更亲密了。 |  Example 2: They became bosomier friends over the years. 多年来，他们成为了更亲密的朋友。|  Near Synonyms: closer, dearer, intimate |  Antonyms: distant, estranged |  Word type: Adjective

Etymology:  From "bosom" (meaning breast, or close relationship) + "-ier" (comparative suffix).  The word "bosom" comes from Old English "bōsm," meaning "breast," and later developed the figurative sense of a close or intimate relationship.
