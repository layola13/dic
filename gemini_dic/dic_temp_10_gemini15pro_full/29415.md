catadioptric:/ˌkætədaɪˈɒptrɪk/ | adj. | 反射折射的，折反射的 | Relating to or denoting optical systems that use both reflection and refraction of light. | Catadioptric telescopes are often used in astronomy because they can be made more compact than purely refractive telescopes. 反射折射望远镜常用于天文学，因为它们可以比纯折射望远镜做得更紧凑。 |  The catadioptric system in the headlight provides a brighter beam.  车前灯中的反射折射系统提供了更明亮的光束。|  reflective-refractive, catoptric | refractive, dioptric | adjective
