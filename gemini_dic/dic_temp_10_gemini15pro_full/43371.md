craniologist:/ˌkreɪniˈɒlədʒɪst/ | n. |颅骨学家；头盖学家|A person who studies craniology, the scientific study of the skull, especially its shape and size. |The craniologist examined the ancient skull, hoping to learn more about the individual's ancestry.  颅骨学家检查了这具古代头骨，希望能了解更多关于这个人祖先的信息。

The museum hired a craniologist to analyze the newly discovered skulls.  博物馆聘请了一位颅骨学家来分析新发现的头骨。|近义词:  phrenologist |反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
