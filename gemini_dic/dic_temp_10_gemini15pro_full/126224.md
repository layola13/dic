What do you think? = 你怎么想？
What does she usually eat for breakfast? = 她早餐通常吃什么？

What are you looking at? = 你在看什么？
What is he doing right now? = 他现在正在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned so far? = 到目前为止你学到了什么？
What has she been up to lately? = 她最近在忙些什么？

What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What happened last night? = 昨晚发生了什么事？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):

What had they decided before the announcement? = 宣布之前他们决定了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will happen if we don't act now? = 如果我们现在不行动会发生什么？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the next meeting? = 他们会在下次会议上讨论什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will have changed by 2030? = 到2030年会有什么变化？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been doing for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经做了五年什么？
