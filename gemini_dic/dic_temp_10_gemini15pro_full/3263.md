affirmance: /əˈfɜːrməns/ | n. | 确认，批准，肯定 | The court's affirmance of the lower court's decision meant the defendant lost the case. 法院确认下级法院的判决意味着被告败诉。 | The judge's affirmance of the verdict brought a sense of closure to the victims' families.  法官对判决的确认给受害者家属带来了一种解脱感。 | 近义词: confirmation, ratification, validation, approval | 反义词: denial, rejection, overruling, reversal | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English *affermaunce*, from Anglo-Norman *afermaunce*, from Old French *afermer* (to strengthen, confirm), from Latin *affirmare* (to strengthen, confirm), from *ad-* (to) + *firmare* (to strengthen, make firm), from *firmus* (firm, strong).
