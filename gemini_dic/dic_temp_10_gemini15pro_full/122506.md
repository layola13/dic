mocker:/ˈmɑːkər/ (US), /ˈmɒkə(r)/ (UK)| n. |嘲笑者，嘲弄者|Someone who mocks or makes fun of someone or something.|He's a cruel mocker who delights in other people's misfortunes. 他是一个残酷的嘲笑者，以他人的不幸为乐。|
The children were mockers, teasing the new boy relentlessly. 孩子们都是嘲笑者，无情地戏弄新来的男孩。|
Synonyms: scoffer, jeerer, teaser, taunter|
Antonyms: admirer, praiser, respecter|
