nonaccidentals:/ˌnɒnˌæksɪˈdɛntlz/
noun
非意外事件; 必然事件
events or outcomes that are considered inevitable or predictable, not due to chance or accident
The rise in sea levels is one of the nonaccidentals of climate change. 海平面上升是气候变化的必然结果之一。
synonyms: inevitabilities, certainties, predictable outcomes
antonyms: accidents, coincidences, chance occurrences
