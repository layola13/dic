multisite:/ˈmʌltiˌsaɪt/| adj. n.|多站点，多位置的；多站点系统|A multisite setup allows you to manage multiple websites from a single installation.|Our company uses a multisite platform to manage all of its blogs.|近义词:networked, distributed, multi-location|反义词:single-site, localized|词性:形容词, 名词

Etymology:
"multi-" (from Latin "multus" meaning "many, much") + "site" (from Latin "situs" meaning "place, position")
