corset:/ˈkɔːrsɪt/ (US), /ˈkɔːsɪt/ (UK)| n.|紧身胸衣|A close-fitting undergarment worn to support and shape the waist and torso, typically having a boned bodice and fastened by laces.|She wore a corset under her dress. 她穿着裙子里面穿了一件紧身胸衣。|Synonyms: bodice, girdle, stays|Antonyms: loose clothing|noun

corset:/ˈkɔːrsɪt/ (US), /ˈkɔːsɪt/ (UK)| vt.|用紧身胸衣束紧|To dress in a corset; to constrict with or as if with a corset.|She corseted herself tightly. 她用紧身胸衣把自己紧紧地束缚住。|Synonyms: constrict, confine, cinch|Antonyms: loosen, free|verb
