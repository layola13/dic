fests:/fɛsts/| n.|节日，庆祝活动|A plural form of "fest," meaning a festival or celebration.  It refers to multiple festivals or a general atmosphere of celebration.|The town held three fests in the summer.  这个小镇在夏天举办了三个节日。|近义词：festivals, celebrations, carnivals|反义词:mourning, funerals|词性:名词
Etymology:  From German *Fest*, from Middle High German *fest*, from Old High German *fest* "firm, strong; feast", from Proto-Germanic *fastuz* "firm, fixed, fast". Cognate with Dutch *feest*,  Swedish *fest*. The sense evolved from "a religious holiday" to "a joyful celebration."
