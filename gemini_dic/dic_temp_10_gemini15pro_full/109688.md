leukaemia: /luːˈkiːmiə/ (UK) /luːˈkimiə/ (US)| n. |白血病|A malignant progressive disease in which the bone marrow and other blood-forming organs produce increased numbers of immature or abnormal leucocytes. These suppress the production of normal blood cells, leading to anemia and other symptoms.| My uncle was diagnosed with leukaemia last year.  去年我叔叔被诊断出白血病。|Synonym: cancer of the blood, blood cancer|Antonym: health, wellness|Noun


leukeumia: /luːˈkiːmiə/ (US)| n. |白血病|(US spelling of leukaemia) A malignant progressive disease in which the bone marrow and other blood-forming organs produce increased numbers of immature or abnormal leukocytes. These suppress the production of normal blood cells, leading to anemia and other symptoms.| The doctor suspects leukeumia based on the blood test results. 医生根据验血结果怀疑是白血病。|Synonym: cancer of the blood, blood cancer|Antonym: health, wellness|Noun
