fill:/fɪl/|v. n.|填满，填充；担任(职位等)；满足；充满|to put something into a container or space until it is full; to perform a duty or job; to satisfy a need or desire; to be or become full of something|例句：Please fill the glass with water. 请把杯子装满水。|近义词：pack, stuff, occupy, fulfill, satisfy|反义词：empty, drain, vacate|词性：动词/名词

fill:/fɪl/|n.|填满的量；足够的东西|the amount that is needed to make something full; enough of something|例句：I ate my fill of cake. 我吃了足够的蛋糕。|近义词：abundance, sufficiency|反义词：lack, shortage|词性：名词
