espouse:/ɪˈspaʊz/ /ɪˈspaʊs/| vt.|支持；拥护；信奉（主义、学说等）；娶；嫁|To adopt or support (a cause, belief, or way of life).|例句：She espoused the cause of animal rights. 她拥护动物权利事业。

例句：He espoused a socialist ideology. 他信奉社会主义意识形态。|近义词：adopt, support, embrace, champion, advocate, defend, uphold, marry|反义词：reject, oppose, renounce, abandon, divorce|词性:动词

词源: From Old French *espouser "to marry," from Latin sponsare "to promise, betroth," from sponsus "bridegroom, betrothed man," past participle of spondere "to pledge, promise solemnly."  The meaning shifted from "marry" to "take up, adopt" in the 16th century.
