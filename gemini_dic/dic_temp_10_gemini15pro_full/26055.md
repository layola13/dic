bushiest:/ˈbʊʃiɪst/
adj.
灌木丛生的；浓密的
Most densely covered with bushes; having the most bushes.
The bushiest eyebrows I've ever seen belonged to my grandfather. 我见过的最浓密的眉毛是我祖父的。
Synonyms: thickest, densest, fullest, most overgrown
Antonyms: sparsest, thinnest, barest, least overgrown
Word type: Adjective
