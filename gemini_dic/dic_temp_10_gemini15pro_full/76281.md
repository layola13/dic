g'day:/ɡəˈdeɪ/
interjection
你好； 喂； 日安 (Australian informal)
A shortened form of "good day," used as an informal greeting.
G'day, mate! How are you doing?  你好，伙计！ 你好吗？
G'day, everyone! Welcome to the barbie. 大家好！欢迎来烧烤。
近义词: hello, hi, hey, howdy (especially in US)
反义词: (No direct antonym for a greeting)
