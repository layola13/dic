gravitates:/ˈɡrævɪteɪts/| v. |被吸引；倾向；沉向|moves towards something it is attracted to|例句：Dust gravitates towards the Earth. 灰尘被地球引力吸引。

例句：Young people gravitate towards the cities in search of work. 年轻人被吸引到城市寻找工作。|近义词：tend, incline, be drawn to, be attracted to|反义词：repel, avoid, shun|词性：动词
