contribute:/kənˈtrɪbjuːt/ (US) /kənˈtrɪbjuːt/ (UK)| v. | 贡献，捐献；促成，导致；投稿 | To give something, especially money or goods, to help achieve or provide something; to help to cause something to happen; to write articles for a newspaper, magazine, or book. |  She contributed $50 to the charity. 她向慈善机构捐款50美元。

He contributed significantly to the team's success. 他为团队的成功做出了重大贡献。

The study contributes to our understanding of the disease. 这项研究有助于我们了解这种疾病。

She regularly contributes to several magazines. 她定期为几家杂志撰稿。


近义词: donate, give, provide, add, support, assist,  | 反义词: take, withhold, detract | 词性: 动词
