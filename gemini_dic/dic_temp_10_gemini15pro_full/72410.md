flues:/fluːz/
n.
烟道，管道
A duct for smoke and waste gases produced by a fire, a gas heater, etc., especially one leading from a fireplace to the top of a chimney.
The chimney has four separate flues.  烟囱有四个独立的烟道。
近义词: chimneys, vents, ducts
反义词: None (No direct opposite for a physical object like a flue)
词性: Noun


flues:/fluːz/
n.
(动物的)绒毛，软毛
The downy, fluffy feathers of a bird.
The baby bird is covered in soft flues.  雏鸟身上覆盖着柔软的绒毛。
近义词: down, fluff
反义词: None (No direct opposite for a type of feather)
词性: Noun

词源: From Middle English *flewe*, from Old French *flue*, probably of Germanic origin, related to flow.
