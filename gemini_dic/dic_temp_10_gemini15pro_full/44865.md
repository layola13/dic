crux:/krʌks/| n.|关键，症结，核心|The crux of the matter is that we don't have enough money. 问题的症结在于我们没有足够的资金。|例句：The crux of the argument is whether the government should regulate the internet.  这争论的焦点是政府是否应该监管互联网。

例句：Identifying the crux of the problem is the first step towards solving it.  找出问题的症结是解决问题的第一步。|近义词：core, heart, essence, gist, nub, kernel|反义词:periphery, exterior, surface|词性:名词
