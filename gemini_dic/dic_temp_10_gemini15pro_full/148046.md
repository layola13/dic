overruling:/ˌoʊvərˈruːlɪŋ/
adjective
压倒一切的；支配的；最重要的
having superior power and influence
The overruling factor in their decision was the cost. 他们决定的最重要因素是成本。

overruling:/ˌoʊvərˈruːlɪŋ/
verb
(overrule) 否决；驳回；宣布…无效；(overrule oneself)  改变主意
reject or disallow by exercising one's superior authority.
The judge overruled the objection. 法官驳回了异议。


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + rule.  "Over-" signifies "above" or "in excess," and "rule" refers to exercising authority or control.  Thus, "overrule" literally means to exercise authority above or against another ruling or decision.

近义词: overriding, dominant, prevailing, governing, controlling
反义词: subordinate, secondary, minor, insignificant, upholding
