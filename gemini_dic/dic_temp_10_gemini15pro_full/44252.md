crofters:/ˈkrɒftəz/ (UK), /ˈkrɑːftərz/ (US)| n.|佃农；小农场主|A crofter is a person who farms a small landholding known as a croft, especially in Scotland.|The crofters worked hard to cultivate their small plots of land.|Synonyms: tenant farmer, smallholder, homesteader|Antonyms: landlord, landowner|Noun
