deritualizes:/diːˈrɪtʃuːəlaɪzɪz/
verb
使非宗教仪式化；使世俗化；去除仪式
To divest of ritual or ceremonial character; to make secular.
The modern wedding ceremony has been deritualized, with many couples opting for personalized vows and unconventional settings.  现代婚礼仪式已经世俗化，许多夫妇选择个性化的誓词和非传统的场所。
Many traditional festivals have been deritualized over time, becoming more focused on entertainment than religious observance. 许多传统的节日随着时间的推移已经世俗化，更多地关注娱乐而不是宗教仪式。
Synonyms: secularize, demystify, profane
Antonyms: ritualize, sanctify, consecrate
词性: 动词
