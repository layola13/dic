bitterly:/ˈbɪtərli/| adv.|痛苦地，怨恨地，极其|In a manner characterized by intense and unpleasant feelings, especially resentment, disappointment, or grief.|例句：She wept bitterly at the news of his death. 她听到他去世的消息后痛哭流涕。|近义词：resentfully, angrily, sharply, severely|反义词：sweetly, happily, gladly|词性：副词
Etymology: From Middle English *biterli, derived from bitter + -ly.  The word "bitter" ultimately comes from Proto-Germanic *bitraz, meaning "to bite," reflecting the sharp, unpleasant taste.
