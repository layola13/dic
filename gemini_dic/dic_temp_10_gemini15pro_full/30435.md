centralizes:/ˈsɛntrəlaɪzɪz/
verb
集中；中央集权化
to gather or bring something together at a single point or place; to concentrate control of an activity or organization under a single authority
The new CEO centralizes all the decision-making power.  新任首席执行官将所有决策权集中了起来。
The government is trying to centralize the education system. 政府正试图将教育系统集中起来。
centralise (British English)
