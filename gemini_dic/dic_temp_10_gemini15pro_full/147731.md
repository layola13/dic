overprized: /ˌōvərˈpraɪzd/ | adj. | 价格过高的，估价过高的 |  Charged more than it is worth.  Something is overprized when its price is excessively high, exceeding its true value or quality. | That restaurant is so overprized; the food is mediocre at best. 那家餐厅价格太高了；食物充其量也就是一般般。| The handbags in that boutique are ridiculously overprized.  那家精品店的包包贵得离谱。|近义词：overvalued, overpriced, exorbitant, expensive |反义词：undervalued, cheap, affordable, reasonable | 词性: 形容词
