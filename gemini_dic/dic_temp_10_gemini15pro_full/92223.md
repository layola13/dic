hyperthyrion:/ˌhaɪpərˈθɪriɒn/ | n. |海波西里翁; (希腊神话)泰坦神许珀里翁|A Titan in Greek mythology, the father of Helios (the sun), Selene (the moon), and Eos (the dawn).  He is often associated with light and celestial observation.|Hyperthyrion was one of the twelve original Titans. 海波西里翁是十二位原始泰坦神之一。

Hyperthyrion, a powerful Titan, ruled the heavens before Zeus.  强大的泰坦神海波西里翁在宙斯之前统治着天空。|近义词:Titan, giant |反义词:god, Olympian|词性:noun
