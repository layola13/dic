noncontagiously:/ˌnɒnkənˈteɪdʒəsli/ | adv. | 非接触性地，非传染性地 | In a manner that does not spread by contact or infection. |  The disease spreads noncontagiously, primarily through contaminated water.  这种疾病以非接触方式传播，主要是通过受污染的水。 | Synonym: noninfectiously | Antonym: contagiously | Adverb
