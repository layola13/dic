guesstimating:/ˈɡɛstɪmeɪtɪŋ/ | n. | 估测；估计 | The act of making an approximate calculation or judgment based on limited information. | We were guesstimating the number of people who would attend the event. 我们正在估计有多少人会参加这个活动。 |  | estimate, approximation, rough calculation | precise calculation | noun

guesstimate:/ˈɡɛstɪmeɪt/ | v. | 估测；估计 | To form an estimate or rough calculation based on incomplete information. |  I guesstimated that the project would take about three months to complete. 我估计这个项目大约需要三个月才能完成。| estimate, approximate, gauge, reckon | calculate precisely | verb
