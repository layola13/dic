bio-gases:/ˈbaɪoʊˌɡæsɪz/
n.
沼气，生物气体
A mixture of gases, primarily methane and carbon dioxide, produced by the breakdown of organic matter in the absence of oxygen.
Bio-gases can be used as a source of renewable energy. 沼气可以用作可再生能源。
Bio-gases are produced from anaerobic digestion. 沼气是由厌氧消化产生的。
Synonym: biogas, methane
Antonym:  (No direct antonym, but perhaps "fossil fuels" as a contrasting energy source)
