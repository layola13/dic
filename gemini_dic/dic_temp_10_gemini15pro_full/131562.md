noncommunications:/ˌnɒnkəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃənz/
n.
缺乏沟通，沟通不畅
Lack of communication; failure to communicate effectively.
The project's failure was largely due to noncommunications between departments.  这个项目的失败很大程度上是由于部门之间缺乏沟通。
The noncommunications between the two countries led to increased tensions. 两国之间缺乏沟通导致紧张局势加剧。
miscommunication, lack of communication
communication, interaction
noun
