agreeing:/əˈɡriːɪŋ/
adjective|同意的，一致的|present participle of "agree," meaning showing or expressing agreement. |例句：They were agreeing on the terms of the contract. 他们在合同条款上达成了一致。|近义词：concurring, consenting, approving|反义词：disagreeing, dissenting, opposing|词性: adjective (形容词)

agreeing:/əˈɡriːɪŋ/
verb|同意，赞成|present participle of "agree," meaning to have the same opinion, feeling, or purpose. |例句：We are agreeing to meet next week. 我们同意下周见面。|近义词：concurring, consenting, approving|反义词：disagreeing, dissenting, opposing|词性: verb (动词)
