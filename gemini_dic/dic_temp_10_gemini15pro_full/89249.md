hominesses:/ˈhoʊminɪsnɪsɪz/
n.
舒适感；亲切感；温馨感
The plural form of hominess, referring to multiple instances or qualities of being cozy, comfortable, and inviting.
Her apartment, filled with soft blankets and warm lighting, exuded many hominesses. 她的公寓里摆满了柔软的毯子和温暖的灯光，处处散发着温馨舒适的感觉。
The various hominesses of the small town made her feel welcome.  小镇的各种温馨之处让她感到宾至如归。
近义词: coziness, comforts, warmth, conviviality
反义词: coldness, unfriendliness, formality
词性: 名词 (plural)
