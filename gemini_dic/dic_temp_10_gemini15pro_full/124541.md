mountant:/ˈmaʊntənt/| n.|封片剂，装片剂|A substance used for mounting specimens on microscope slides or other surfaces.|The botanist carefully applied the mountant to the slide before placing the cover slip. 植物学家在放置盖玻片之前小心地将封片剂涂在载玻片上。|近义词:adhesive, fixative|反义词:(None -  lack of a direct opposite, potentially "solvent" if the context implies removal)|n.
