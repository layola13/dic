adsignify: /ˌædˈsɪɡnɪfaɪ/
verb
表明; 表示
To signify or assign a meaning to something; to indicate or connote.
The raised eyebrow adsignified his disapproval.  扬起的眉毛表明了他的不赞成。
Her silence adsignified her agreement. 她的沉默表示她同意。
近义词: signify, indicate, denote, connote
反义词: obscure, conceal
词性: verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin ad- (“to, towards”) + significare (“to signify”).
