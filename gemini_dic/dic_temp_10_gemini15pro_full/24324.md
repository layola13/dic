brilliantly:/ˈbrɪljəntli/| adv.|灿烂地，辉煌地，出色地|In a brilliant manner; with great skill or intelligence.|She played the piano brilliantly. 她钢琴弹得非常出色。|近义词：splendidly, superbly, excellently, magnificently|反义词：poorly, badly, awfully, terribly|词性:副词
Etymology: From brilliant + -ly.  "Brilliant" comes from the French "brillant," present participle of "briller" (to shine), ultimately from a Vulgar Latin word "beryllare" (to shine like a beryl).
