downscales:/ˈdaʊnˌskeɪlz/ | v. |缩减规模，降低规模|Reduces the size or scale of something. |The company downscaled its operations after the recession.  公司在经济衰退后缩减了业务规模。| The factory downscaled its production due to falling demand. 由于需求下降，工厂缩减了产量。|近义词: reduce, decrease, shrink, downsize |反义词: upscale, expand, increase |词性:动词

downscales:/ˈdaʊnˌskeɪlz/ | n. |缩减规模|The act of reducing the size or scale of something. |The downscale of the project was necessary due to budget cuts.  由于预算削减，该项目的缩减规模是必要的。|The downscale in manufacturing led to job losses. 制造业的缩减规模导致了失业。|近义词: reduction, decrease, shrinking, downsizing |反义词: upscaling, expansion, increase |词性:名词
