individualised:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlaɪzd/ | adj. |个性化的，个体化的| Designed or tailored to meet the specific needs of a particular person. |  例句：The school offers individualised learning programs. 这所学校提供个性化的学习方案。|例句：She received individualised attention from her tutor.  她得到了导师的个性化关注。| 近义词：personalized, customized, tailored | 反义词：standardized, generic | 词性: adjective

individualised:/ˌɪndɪˈvɪdʒuəlaɪzd/ | v. |个体化，个性化| To make something suitable for the needs of a particular person or thing. | 例句：The training program was individualised to suit each employee's skills and experience.  培训计划个性化，以适应每个员工的技能和经验。| 例句：The company individualises its marketing campaigns to target specific demographics.  这家公司将其营销活动个性化，以针对特定的人口统计数据。| 近义词：personalize, customize | 反义词：standardize, generalize | 词性: verb (past participle)
