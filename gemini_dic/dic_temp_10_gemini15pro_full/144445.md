outswimming:/ˌaʊtˈswɪmɪŋ/
verb
超过…游; 比…游得更好
The act of swimming farther or faster than someone or something else.
He easily outswam his competitors in the final race. 他在决赛中轻松地超过了他的对手。
outswim, surpass, beat
be outswam by
