overboastful: /ˌoʊvərˈboʊstfəl/ | adj. | 过于自夸的，夸大其词的 | Excessively boastful; bragging too much.  Characterized by excessive boasting or bragging. | He was so overboastful about his achievements that people started to avoid him. 他对自己的成就过于夸耀，以至于人们开始躲避他。|  He gave an overboastful account of his trip, exaggerating every detail. 他对他的旅行进行了夸大的描述，每个细节都夸大了。 | Synonyms: arrogant, conceited, egotistical, vainglorious | Antonyms: humble, modest, unassuming | adjective
