bruits:/bruːts/ /bruːɪts/ | n. |杂音；传闻；谣言 |An abnormal sound heard on auscultation.  A rumor or report.|
The doctor heard bruits in the patient's carotid artery. 医生在病人的颈动脉听到了杂音。
There were bruits about a possible merger. 有关于可能合并的传闻。|
近义词：murmurs, sounds, rumors, reports, gossip |
反义词：silence, quiet |
词性: noun


词源: From French *bruit* "noise, rumor," from Old French *bruire* "to roar, make noise."
