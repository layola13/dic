doubtable:/ˈdaʊtəbl/| adj.|可疑的，值得怀疑的|Open to doubt or suspicion; questionable.|Her claims about the incident were highly doubtable.|近义词：questionable, dubious, uncertain, debatable|反义词：undoubtable, certain, unquestionable|词性:形容词
Etymology: From doubt + -able.  Doubt ultimately comes from Latin *dubitāre, frequentative of dubāre ‘to hesitate.’
