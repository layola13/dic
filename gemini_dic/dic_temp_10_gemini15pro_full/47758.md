decameric:/ˌdekəˈmɛrɪk/
adjective
十聚体的，由十个亚基组成的
Composed of ten subunits, especially of protein monomers.
A decameric protein complex plays a crucial role in DNA replication.  一个十聚体蛋白复合物在DNA复制中起着至关重要的作用。
The enzyme forms a decameric ring structure. 这种酶形成一个十聚体环状结构。
近义词:decavalent
反义词:(无明确的反义词)
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *deka- (ten) + -mer (part) + -ic (adjectival suffix).
