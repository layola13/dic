noticing:/ˈnoʊtɪsɪŋ/ | n. | 注意，注意到 | The act of observing or paying attention to something. |  I'm noticing a change in her behavior. 我注意到她的行为有所改变。

| Synonyms: observing, perceiving, recognizing, detecting | Antonyms: ignoring, overlooking, neglecting | gerund

noticing:/ˈnoʊtɪsɪŋ/ | adj. | 注意到的，引人注目的 |  Worthy of attention; noticeable. | He made a noticing improvement in his grades.  他的成绩有了显著的提高。

| Synonyms:  remarkable, significant, noteworthy, conspicuous  | Antonyms:  inconspicuous, unremarkable, insignificant | present participle
