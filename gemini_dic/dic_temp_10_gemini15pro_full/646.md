abridger:/əˈbrɪdʒər/ | n. |  缩写者，删节者 |  A person who abridges or shortens something, typically a text. | The abridger skillfully condensed the lengthy novel into a manageable volume.  这位删节者巧妙地将冗长的小说压缩成了一本易读的册子。 | synonyms: condenser, summarizer, epitomizer | antonyms: expander, elaborator | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *abregger*, from Anglo-Norman *abreger*, from Old French *abreger*, from Late Latin *abbreviāre*, from Latin *brevis* (“short”).

USAGE->
The abridger carefully selected the key passages. = 缩写者仔细挑选了关键段落。

The abridger of the encyclopedia did a commendable job. = 百科全书的缩写者做得值得称赞。


PHRASE->
the abridger of the text = 文本的缩写者
an experienced abridger =  一位经验丰富的缩写者
the abridger's task = 缩写者的任务
the abridger's skill = 缩写者的技巧
the work of the abridger = 缩写者的工作
