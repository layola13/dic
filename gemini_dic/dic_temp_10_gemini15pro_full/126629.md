nailing:/ˈneɪlɪŋ/
n.|钉钉；确定；成功完成|The act of fastening something with nails.  The act of succeeding in something. |例句：The nailing of the boards was quick. 木板的钉钉很快。|近义词：fastening, securing, pinning, achieving, accomplishing|反义词:failing, missing, loosening|词性:名词

nailing:/ˈneɪlɪŋ/
v.|钉住；抓住；揭露；确定；成功完成 (nail 的现在分词)|Present participle of "nail". To fasten with nails. To catch someone doing something wrong. To identify something precisely. To succeed in something. |例句：He was nailing the sign to the wall. 他正在把标志钉在墙上。|近义词：fastening, securing, pinning, catching, exposing, identifying, achieving, accomplishing|反义词:missing, failing, releasing, freeing|词性:动词
