book-up:/bʊk ˈʌp/
| v. |预订满，预约满|to reserve something, such as a hotel room or a ticket, in advance, so that it is not available for anyone else| The hotel is booked up for the weekend.  这家酒店周末已经被订满了。|
|例句：The restaurant is fully booked up for tonight.  这家餐馆今晚已经全部订满了。|
|例句：We tried to book up a table for dinner, but the restaurant was full. 我们试图预订一张餐桌吃晚餐，但餐馆已经客满了。|
|近义词：reserve, sell out|
|反义词：cancel, be available|
|词性：动词短语|
