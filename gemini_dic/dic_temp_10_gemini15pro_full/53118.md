diploblastic:/ˌdɪpləˈblæstɪk/ | adj. | 双胚层的 | Having two germ layers, the ectoderm and endoderm, as in cnidarians and ctenophores. |  Cnidarians are diploblastic animals. 刺胞动物是双胚层动物。 |  Diploblastic organisms have a simpler body plan compared to triploblastic organisms. 与三胚层生物相比，双胚层生物的身体结构更简单。 | synonyms: two-layered | antonyms: triploblastic | adjective
