expeditious:/ˌɛkspɪˈdɪʃəs/| adj.|迅速的，敏捷的，效率高的|Characterized by promptness and efficiency; quick.|The company prides itself on its expeditious handling of customer orders. 该公司以其快速处理客户订单而自豪。|近义词：swift, rapid, efficient, prompt, quick|反义词：slow, sluggish, inefficient, dilatory|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Latin expedītus "unimpeded, free," past participle of expedīre "to extricate, disengage, set free," from ex- "out" + pes "foot."
