constabulary:/kənˈstæbjʊlərɪ/ | n. |警察部门，警力| a police force. | The local constabulary is investigating the crime. 当地警方正在调查这起犯罪。| 近义词: police force, police, law enforcement | 反义词: N/A | 词性: 名词

词源: From Late Latin *constabularia, from Latin comes stabuli (“count of the stable,” originally a high-ranking officer in charge of the imperial stables, later a chief of police).
