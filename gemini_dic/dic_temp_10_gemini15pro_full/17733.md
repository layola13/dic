bedegar:/ˈbɛdɪɡɑːr/| n.|蔷薇 Bedeguar|A mossy gall formed on roses by the cynipid wasp Diplolepis rosae. It is sometimes called a robin's pincushion. |The bedegar, despite its strange appearance, is harmless to the rose bush. 蔷薇 Bedeguar 虽然外观奇特，但对玫瑰丛无害。|近义词:rose gall, robin's pincushion|反义词:None|Noun
