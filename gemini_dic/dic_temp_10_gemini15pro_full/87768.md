hi-fis:/ˈhaɪ faɪz/
noun
高保真音响设备
High-fidelity sound equipment, often used in the plural to refer to a stereo system or components of a stereo system.
He invested a lot of money in his hi-fis. 他在高保真音响设备上投入了很多钱。
Synonyms: stereo, sound system, audio equipment
Antonyms: low-fi
词性: 名词


hi-fi:/ˈhaɪ faɪ/
adjective
高保真的
Producing high-quality sound reproduction.
He has a hi-fi stereo system. 他有一套高保真立体声音响系统。
Synonyms: high-fidelity, high-quality
Antonyms: low-fi
词性: 形容词


词源:  Shortening of "high fidelity," referring to the accurate reproduction of sound.
