affiliations:/əˌfɪliˈeɪʃənz/
|n.|隶属关系，联系，联盟|A connection or association with a group or organization. The state of being affiliated.|例句：He has affiliations with several charities. 他与几家慈善机构有联系。|例句：The university has affiliations with hospitals in the area. 这所大学与该地区的医院有合作关系。|近义词：associations, connections, memberships, alliances|反义词：dissociation, separation, independence|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *affiliationem, from Latin affiliare "to adopt as a son," from ad- "to" + filius "son."
