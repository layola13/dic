disrepairs:/ˌdɪsrɪˈpɛərz/
|n.| 失修状态; 破损状态 |A state of disrepair; broken or damaged condition.|The building was in a state of disrepair. 这栋建筑处于失修状态。| 近义词: dilapidation, deterioration, ruin | 反义词: repair, good condition | 词性: 名词

词源:  "dis-" (expressing negation) + "repair" (from Old French "reparer," meaning "to mend, restore").
