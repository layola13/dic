misbrew:/mɪsˈbruː/
verb
酿造失败; 冲泡失败
to brew (beer, tea, coffee, etc.) badly
The coffee was misbrewed and tasted bitter. 这咖啡冲泡得不好，味道很苦。
近义词: spoil, ruin, botch
反义词: perfect, brew well
词性: verb
