fasttracker:/ˈfæstˌtrækər/ | n. | 快速学习者；快速发展者；快速通道 | Someone who learns or progresses quickly, or something that develops rapidly.  |  She's a real fasttracker in her career, already managing a team after just two years. 她事业上真是个快速发展者，仅仅两年后就已经在管理一个团队了。 | The new product is a fasttracker in the market, gaining popularity very quickly. 这款新产品在市场上发展迅速，很快就获得了人气。 |  近义词: high-flyer, go-getter, achiever | 反义词: slowpoke, laggard | 词性: noun

Etymology:  A compound of "fast" (meaning quick) and "tracker" (one who tracks or follows a path). The term likely emerged in the late 20th century with the increasing pace of technological and social change.
