maximizer:/ˈmæksɪˌmaɪzər/| n.|最大化者，力求完美者|A person who strives to make the best possible decision, even in seemingly trivial matters, often leading to analysis paralysis and dissatisfaction.  A maximizer tends to compare options extensively and worry about making the wrong choice.|例句：She's a maximizer and spends hours researching even the smallest purchases. 她是个力求完美的人，即使是小件商品也要花几个小时研究。

例句：He's such a maximizer that he can't even decide what to have for lunch without agonizing over it. 他太追求完美了，连午饭吃什么都要纠结半天。|近义词：perfectionist, optimizer|反义词：satisficer|词性：noun
