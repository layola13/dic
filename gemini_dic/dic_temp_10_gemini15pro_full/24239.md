bridlepath:/ˈbraɪdlˌpæθ/ | n. |马道；骑马小径|A path or track specifically designed for riding horses.| We took a scenic stroll along the bridlepath. 我们沿着马道风景优美地漫步。|近义词：trail, path, track|反义词:road, highway|词性:名词

Etymology:
From "bridle" (Old English "bridel") + "path" (Old English "pæth").
