goitrogenous:/ˌɡɔɪtrəˈdʒɛnəs/| adj.|致甲状腺肿的；产生甲状腺肿的| Containing or producing goitrogens, substances that interfere with thyroid function and can cause goiter.|  Examples:  Goitrogenous foods can affect thyroid hormone production.  食用致甲状腺肿的食物会影响甲状腺激素的产生。

Cruciferous vegetables like cabbage are considered goitrogenous.  十字花科蔬菜如卷心菜被认为是致甲状腺肿的。|近义词: goiter-producing, thyroid-inhibiting|反义词:  (No direct antonym, but consider) thyroid-stimulating |词性: adjective
