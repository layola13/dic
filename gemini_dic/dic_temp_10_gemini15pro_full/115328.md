mannishness:/ˈmænɪʃnəs/
noun
男子气概，男性化
The quality of being like a man; masculinity.
Her mannishness was off-putting to some. 她身上的男子气概让一些人反感。
synonyms: masculinity, maleness, virility
antonyms: femininity, womanliness
