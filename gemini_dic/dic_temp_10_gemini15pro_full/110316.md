lighte:/ˈlaɪtə/| archaic adj.|更轻的，点燃的 (古英语)|A lighter load is easier to carry. 更轻的负载更容易携带。|例句：The feather is lighter than the stone. 羽毛比石头轻。|近义词：less heavy, airier|反义词:heavier, denser|词性:形容词


词源: From Middle English *light, from Old English lēoht (“light in weight”), from Proto-Germanic *linhtą (“light”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₁léngʰwtos, from *h₁lengʰ- (“light”). Cognate with Dutch licht (“light”), German leicht (“light, easy”), Danish let (“light, easy”). Related to light (adj.).  The verbal form "lighte" comes from Old English līhtan, meaning "to kindle, ignite."
