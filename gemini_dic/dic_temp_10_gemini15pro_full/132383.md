nondemises:/ˌnɒndɪˈmaɪzɪz/
n.
非转租部分；非租赁部分
Parts of a property not included in a lease.
The nondemises areas include the common hallways and the lobby. 非转租区域包括公共走廊和大厅。
non-leased areas, common areas, shared spaces
leased premises, demised premises
