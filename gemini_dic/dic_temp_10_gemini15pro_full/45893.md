cuticular:/kjuːˈtɪkjʊlə(r)/
adjective
表皮的
relating to the cuticle of an organism
The beetle has a hard, cuticular shell.  这只甲虫有一个坚硬的表皮壳。
近义词: epidermal, cutaneous
反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly "internal")
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "cuticula" (little skin), diminutive of "cutis" (skin).
