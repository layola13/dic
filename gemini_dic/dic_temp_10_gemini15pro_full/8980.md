antileveling:/ˌæntiˈlɛvəlɪŋ/
adjective
反均衡的，反对平均主义的
Opposing or resisting leveling, especially social leveling; promoting inequality.
Anti-leveling policies often exacerbate existing disparities in wealth and opportunity. 反均衡政策往往会加剧现有的财富和机会差距。
The think tank promoted antileveling measures, arguing that they incentivized innovation.  这个智库推行反平均主义措施，认为它们能激励创新。
近义词：elitist, hierarchical, inegalitarian
反义词：egalitarian, leveling
词性：adjective
