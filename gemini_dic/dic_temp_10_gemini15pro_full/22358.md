boo-boo:/ˈbuːbuː/| n.| 小错误；小伤；小麻烦 | a minor injury or mistake |  例句：It's just a little boo-boo, don't cry.  只是一点小伤，别哭。| 近义词：mistake, error, mishap, ouch, scrape | 反义词: miracle, perfection | 词性: 名词


boo-boo:/ˈbuːbuː/| n.| 笨蛋；傻瓜 (Informal, often used with children) | a foolish or silly person (informal, often used with children) | 例句：Don't be such a boo-boo!  别这么傻！| 近义词：silly, fool, goose | 反义词: genius, smart | 词性: 名词

ETYMOLOGY->
Probably imitative of a child's cry of pain.  Possibly influenced by "boo," expressing disapproval or contempt. 大概是模仿小孩哭喊疼痛的声音。可能受到表示反对或轻蔑的“boo”的影响。
