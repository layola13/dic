allusively:/əˈluːsɪvli/
adverb
暗示地，引喻地
In a manner that makes indirect references or allusions.
She spoke allusively about her past, never revealing specific details. 她暗示性地谈到了她的过去，从未透露具体细节。
He allusively referred to their shared history, causing her to blush. 他暗示地提到了他们共同的经历，使她脸红了。
Synonyms: indirectly, suggestively, obliquely, tacitly
Antonyms: directly, explicitly, plainly, overtly
词性: 副词
