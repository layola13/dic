inherited:/ɪnˈhɛrɪtɪd/ | adj. | 遗传的，继承的 | Received from one's parents or ancestors by genetic transmission; derived from previous generations. |  She has inherited her mother's beautiful eyes. 她遗传了她母亲美丽的双眼。 | Hereditary, genetic, ancestral | Acquired, learned | adjective
