diag:/ˈdaɪæɡ/ | n. | 诊断; (尤指) 医学诊断; 诊断结果 | A test performed to identify the nature or cause of something, especially a medical condition. |  The doctor ordered several diags to determine the cause of her illness. 医生为了确定她患病的原因，下令进行了几项诊断。| synonyms: diagnosis, assessment, examination | antonyms:  (No direct antonym, could consider "misdiagnosis" as an opposing concept) | noun

/ˈdaɪæɡ/ | abbr. | diagonal; diagram | Shortening of the words "diagonal" and "diagram". |  Draw a diag across the square. 在正方形上画一条对角线。 | synonyms:  (For diagonal) oblique, slanted; (For diagram) chart, graph, illustration | antonyms: (For diagonal) horizontal, vertical | abbreviation
