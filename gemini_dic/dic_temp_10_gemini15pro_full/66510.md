exfoliative:/ɛksˈfoʊliˌeɪtɪv/
adjective
脱落的，剥落的
Relating to or causing exfoliation; shedding or peeling in scales or layers.
The doctor diagnosed the patient with exfoliative dermatitis. 医生诊断该病人患有剥脱性皮炎。
The paint had an exfoliative effect, peeling off in large flakes. 油漆具有剥落效果，会大片脱落。
synonyms: desquamative, peeling, scaling, shedding
antonyms: adherent, sticking, clinging
