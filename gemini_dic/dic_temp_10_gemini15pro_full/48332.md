decriminalised: /diːˈkrɪmɪnəlaɪzd/ | adj. | 非罪化的 | No longer considered a crime. | The possession of small amounts of marijuana is decriminalised in some countries.  在一些国家，持有少量大麻是非罪化的。 | legalized, legitimate | criminalized, prohibited | Adjective
