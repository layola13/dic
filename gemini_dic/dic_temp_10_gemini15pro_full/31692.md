chautauqua: /ʃəˈtɔːkwə/ | n. | 读书会；讲习会；巡回演讲会 | An adult education movement in the United States, highly popular in the late 19th and early 20th centuries. Chautauquas brought entertainment and culture for the whole community, with speakers, teachers, musicians, entertainers, preachers and specialists of the day. | The town hosted a week-long chautauqua featuring lectures and musical performances.  小镇举办了为期一周的读书会，以讲座和音乐表演为特色。

The Chautauqua movement provided educational opportunities for rural communities. 读书会运动为农村社区提供了教育机会。
