dittying:/ˈdɪtiɪŋ/
verb
哼唱小曲;  创作小曲
the action of singing or composing short, simple songs.
He spent the afternoon dittying a silly tune about a squirrel. 他一下午都在哼唱一首关于松鼠的愚蠢小调。
近义词:  singing,  humming, composing, rhyming
反义词:  being silent,  not composing
词性: Verb (动名词)
