homelier: /ˈhoʊmliər/ (US) /ˈhəʊmliər/ (UK)| adj.|更朴素的，更家常的，更不好看的|More plain and simple, suggesting home life; less attractive or sophisticated.  It is the comparative form of "homely."|例句：The cottage was small and homelier than the grand mansion, but it had a certain charm. 这小屋比那座大宅子小而简朴，但它有一种独特的魅力。

例句：Her homelier style of dress made her seem more approachable. 她朴素的穿着风格使她看起来更平易近人。|近义词：plain, simple, unpretentious, less attractive|反义词：grand, sophisticated, elegant, beautiful|词性:形容词
