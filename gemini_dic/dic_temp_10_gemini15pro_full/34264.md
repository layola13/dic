clapt:/klæpt/ | v. | clapped的过去式和过去分词 | past tense and past participle of "clap" |  He clapt his hands in delight. 他高兴地拍了拍手。| 近义词: applauded, commended | 反义词: criticized, booed | 词性: 动词

词源:
From Middle English *clappen, from Old English clappan (“to clap, beat, strike, palpitate, throb”), from Proto-Germanic *klappōną (“to clap, flap”), from Proto-Indo-European *kloh₁p- (“to beat, strike”). Cognate with West Frisian klappe (“to clap, smack, flap”), Dutch klappen (“to clap, chat, bang”), German klappen (“to clap, work, succeed”), Danish klappe (“to pat, clap”), Swedish klappa (“to pat, stroke”).
