fored:/fɔːrd/|v. (Archaic)|禁止，阻止|To prevent; to obstruct; to hinder.|He fored them from entering the castle.| 近义词: forbade, prohibited, prevented|反义词: allowed, permitted|动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *foren*, from Old English *forbēodan* ("to forbid"), from *for-* (prefix indicating prohibition or destruction) + *bēodan* ("to command, offer, announce"). Cognate with Dutch *verbieden*, German *verbieten*, Gothic *𐍆𐌰𐌿𐍂𐌱𐌹𐌿𐌳𐌰𐌽* (faurbiudan).  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *furbidaną*, from *fur-* + *bidaną* ("to ask, command").
