nondebtors:/ˌnɑnˈdɛtərz/ | n. | 非债务人 | People or entities who do not owe money.  Those who are not in debt. |  Example: The nondebtors were unaffected by the new bankruptcy laws. 非债务人没有受到新的破产法的影响。| Example:  The bank offered special incentives to nondebtors to open new accounts.  银行向非债务人提供特别优惠以开设新账户。|Synonyms: creditors (opposite meaning in terms of debt relationship), savers, investors | Antonyms: debtors, borrowers | Noun

Etymology:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "debtors" (from Old French "detoire," meaning "duty" or "something owed," ultimately from Latin "dēbēre" "to owe").
