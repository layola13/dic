exponents:/ɪkˈspoʊnənts/ (US), /ɪkˈspəʊnənts/ (UK)| n. |指数，幂|A symbol or number placed above and after another symbol or number to denote the power to which the latter is to be raised.|2³ is read as "two to the power of three" and means 2 x 2 x 2.  2的3次方读作“2的3次幂”，意思是2乘以2乘以2。|近义词：powers, indices|反义词: roots, base|词性: Noun

Etymology:  Late Middle English: from Latin exponent- ‘putting forth, setting out’, from the verb exponere .
