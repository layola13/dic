eternalize: /ɪˈtɜːnəlaɪz/ /ɪˈtɝːnəlaɪz/
verb
使永恒，使不朽
To make something last forever; to immortalize.
The poet’s words eternalized the beauty of the sunset.  这位诗人的文字使日落的美景永恒。
They eternalized their love by getting married in a beautiful cathedral. 他们在美丽的教堂结婚，使他们的爱情永恒。
synonyms: immortalize, perpetuate, enshrine
antonyms: destroy, obliterate
词性: 动词
