bawson:/ˈbɔːsən/ | n. |  boatswain 的缩写，水手长 | A bawson is a shortened, informal term for a boatswain, a ship's officer in charge of equipment and the crew. | The bawson yelled orders to the deckhands. 水手长向甲板水手大声喊叫命令。| synonym: boatswain | antonym: (no direct antonym, perhaps subordinate crew member) | noun
