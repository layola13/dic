intermaxilla: /ɪntərˈmæksɪlə/ | n. |  前颌骨，上颌间骨 |  A pair of small cranial bones between the maxillae of most vertebrates, forming part of the palate, especially in amphibians, reptiles, and birds. In mammals they become incorporated into the maxillae. | The intermaxilla bone plays a crucial role in the development of the beak in birds.  前颌骨在鸟类喙的发育中起着至关重要的作用。| The paleontologist identified the fossil as the intermaxilla of a dinosaur.  古生物学家将这块化石鉴定为恐龙的前颌骨。| synonyms: premaxilla, incisive bone | antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a specific bone) | noun
