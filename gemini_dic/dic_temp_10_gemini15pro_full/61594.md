emeritus:/ɪˈmɛrɪtəs/| adj.|退休的，荣誉退休的|Having retired but retaining an honorary title corresponding to that held immediately before retirement.|Professor Smith is now emeritus professor of physics. 史密斯教授现在是物理学荣誉退休教授。|
Example 1: Dr. Jones is an emeritus professor at the university. 琼斯博士是这所大学的荣誉退休教授。

Example 2:  The emeritus chairman still attends board meetings. 荣誉退休的主席仍然参加董事会会议。|
Synonyms: retired, honorary|Antonyms: active, current|adjective
