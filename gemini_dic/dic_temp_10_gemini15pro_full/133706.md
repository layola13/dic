nonfrighteningly:/nɒnˈfraɪtənɪŋli/ | adv. | 不吓人地，不令人害怕地 |  In a way that does not cause fear or alarm.  It's formed by adding the negative prefix "non-" to "frightening" (which comes from "frighten," meaning to scare), and then adding the adverbial suffix "-ly." | The spider crawled across the floor nonfrighteningly.  蜘蛛在地板上爬行，一点也不吓人。|  calmly, gently, peacefully | terrifyingly, alarmingly | adverb
