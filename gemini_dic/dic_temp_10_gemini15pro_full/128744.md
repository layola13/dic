niggardness:/ˈnɪɡərdnɪs/| n. |吝啬，小气|Extreme unwillingness to spend money; stinginess.|His niggardness prevented him from enjoying life's simple pleasures. 他的吝啬让他无法享受生活中简单的乐趣。|Example: The niggardness of the company's offer angered the employees. 公司报价的吝啬激怒了员工。|Synonyms: stinginess, miserliness, parsimony, meanness|Antonyms: generosity, liberality, munificence|noun

词源:
From Middle English *nigardnesse*, equivalent to *nigard* (“niggard”) + *-nesse*.  Ultimately from Old Norse *hnǫggr* (“stingy”).
