ferromagnetism:/ˌfɛroʊˈmæɡnɪtɪzəm/| n. |铁磁性|The property of certain materials, such as iron, to exhibit strong magnetic effects.|Iron displays ferromagnetism. 铁显示出铁磁性。|Synonym: ferrimagnetism (similar, but not identical)|Antonym: diamagnetism|noun
