flaw:/flɔː/| n.|瑕疵，缺点，缺陷|a mark, fault, or other imperfection that mars a substance or object|The flaw in his argument was obvious. 他的论点中的缺陷很明显。|synonyms: defect, imperfection, blemish, fault, weakness|antonyms: strength, perfection, merit|noun

flaw:/flɔː/| v.|使有瑕疵，使无效|to cause a fault or weakness in; to become flawed|The scandal flawed his reputation.  这桩丑闻损害了他的名誉。|synonyms: impair, mar, damage, spoil, blemish|antonyms: enhance, improve, perfect|verb
