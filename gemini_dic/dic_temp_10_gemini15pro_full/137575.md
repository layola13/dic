nontransportability:/nɒntrænspɔːrtəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可移植性，不可转移性 | The inability of a research finding, statistical model, or other concept to be accurately or effectively applied to a different context or population.  It highlights the limitations of generalizing conclusions drawn from a specific study or situation. | The researchers acknowledged the nontransportability of their findings to other cultural contexts. 研究人员承认他们的发现无法推广到其他文化背景。

The nontransportability of the animal study results to humans limits their clinical relevance. 动物研究结果对人类的不可移植性限制了其临床相关性。 | near synonyms: inapplicability, non-generalizability, context-specificity,  | antonyms: generalizability, universality, transportability | noun
