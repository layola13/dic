coffin:/ˈkɒfɪn/ (UK) /ˈkɔːfɪn/ (US)|n.|棺材|A box in which a dead body is buried or cremated.|The coffin was lowered into the grave. 棺材被放入了墓穴。|
|近义词:casket, sarcophagus|反义词:(None -  There isn't a direct opposite of a coffin.)|noun|

coffin:/ˈkɒfɪn/ (UK) /ˈkɔːfɪn/ (US)|v.|（用棺材）收殓|To place (a corpse) in a coffin.|They coffined the body before the funeral service. 他们在葬礼前将尸体放入棺材。|近义词: entomb, inter |反义词: exhume, disinter|verb|
