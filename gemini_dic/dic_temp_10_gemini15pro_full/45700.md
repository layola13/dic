curvaceousnesses:/kɜːrˈveɪʃəsnəsɪz/
noun
曲線優美, 婀娜多姿 (plural form, rarely used)
The state or quality of having many curves, especially in an attractive way, referring to a woman's figure.  It emphasizes an abundance of curves.
Her curvaceousnesses were highlighted by the tight dress. 她那玲珑的曲线被紧身连衣裙衬托得淋漓尽致。
The sculptor captured the curvaceousnesses of the female form. 雕塑家捕捉到了女性形体的曲线美。
synonyms: curves, shapeliness, voluptuousness
antonyms: straightness, angularity
