coinquinate: /kɔɪnˈkwɪneɪt/ | v. | 污染，弄脏 | To make impure by contact or mixture; to contaminate or pollute. | The industrial waste coinquinated the river. 工业废料污染了河流。| The oil spill coinquinated the beach.  石油泄漏污染了海滩。 | 近义词: contaminate, pollute, defile, taint | 反义词: purify, cleanse, clean | 词性: 动词
Etymology: From Latin *coinquināre, from com- (together) + *inquināre (to stain, pollute), from in- (in) + *cunīre (wedge, dirt).  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *ḱwen- (dirt).
