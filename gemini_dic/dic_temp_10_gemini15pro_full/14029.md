auscultatory:/ɔːˈskʌltətɔːri/| adj.|听诊的|Relating to auscultation; characterized by listening for sounds within the body, typically with a stethoscope.|The doctor made an auscultatory examination of the patient's chest. 医生对病人的胸部进行了听诊。|近义词:auditory|反义词:None|词性:adjective
Etymology: From Latin *auscultātōrius*, from auscultāre ‘to listen attentively’.
