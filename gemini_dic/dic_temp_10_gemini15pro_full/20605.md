blackguardly: /ˈblæɡɑːrdli/
adjective
卑鄙的；恶棍似的；无赖的
Resembling or characteristic of a blackguard; villainous; scoundrelly.
He muttered blackguardly insults under his breath. 他低声咕哝着卑鄙的辱骂。
近义词: villainous, scoundrelly, dastardly, despicable, contemptible
反义词: honorable, noble, virtuous, respectable, decent
词性: adjective

blackguard: /ˈblæɡɑːrd/
noun
恶棍；无赖
A person, particularly a man, who behaves in a dishonorable or contemptible way.
He was a notorious blackguard, known for his cruelty and deceit. 他是一个臭名昭著的恶棍，以其残忍和欺骗而闻名。
近义词: villain, scoundrel, rogue, rascal
反义词: gentleman, hero
词性: noun

Etymology:
From "black guard," originally referring to the lowest menials in a large household, such as kitchen help, who were often dirty from their work.  Over time, the term became associated with unscrupulous and disreputable behavior.
