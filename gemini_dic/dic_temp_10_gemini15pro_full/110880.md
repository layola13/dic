lionizes:/ˈlaɪənaɪzɪz/
verb
给予…极高的评价；把…视为名人；吹捧
To treat (someone) as a celebrity.
The media lionizes athletes. 媒体把运动员捧为名人。
Synonyms: celebrate, exalt, glorify, extol
Antonyms: condemn, criticize, disparage, denigrate
词性: 动词
