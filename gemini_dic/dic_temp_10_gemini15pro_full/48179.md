decomposable:/ˌdiːkəmˈpoʊzəbl/ | adj. | 可分解的，可被分解的 | Able to be broken down into simpler parts or substances, either physically or conceptually. |  The waste is biodegradable and decomposable.  这种废料是可生物降解和可分解的。|  The problem is decomposable into smaller, more manageable parts.  这个问题可以分解成更小、更容易处理的部分。 |  breakable, separable, divisible, analyzable | indivisible, inseparable, unbreakable | adjective

decompose:/ˌdiːkəmˈpoʊz/ | v. | 分解，腐烂 |  To break down or be broken down into simpler parts or substances, either physically or conceptually. | The body had started to decompose. 尸体已经开始腐烂。 | The complex problem can be decomposed into several simpler ones. 这个复杂的问题可以分解成几个简单的问题。 |  decay, rot, break down, disintegrate | synthesize, combine, integrate | verb
