barkbound:/ˈbɑːrkbaʊnd/
adjective
被树皮束缚的；被树皮包裹的
Enclosed or constricted by bark; covered with bark.  Relating to a tree whose bark cannot expand to accommodate growth, leading to girdling and potentially death.
The barkbound tree eventually died because its trunk couldn't expand. 这棵被树皮束缚的树最终死了，因为它的树干无法扩张。
The old oak was barkbound and showed signs of decline.  这棵老橡树被树皮裹住，并显现出衰败的迹象。
近义词:  girdled, constricted
反义词:  free, unrestrained
词性: adjective
词源:  From "bark" (the outer covering of a tree) + "bound" (tied, restricted).
