ctr:/ˈsiː tiː ɑːr/ /ˈkənˈtroʊlər/ | n. | 点击率; 控制器 | Click-through rate (the ratio of users who click on a specific link to the number of total users who view a page, email, or advertisement).  Also short for "controller". | Example 1: The CTR on our latest banner ad was very impressive. 我们最新的横幅广告的点击率非常令人印象深刻。

Example 2: He's using a wireless ctr for his gaming console. 他正在使用无线控制器玩游戏机。 | Synonym: click-through rate (for CTR as click-through rate),  gamepad, joystick (for CTR as controller) | Antonym: N/A (no direct antonym for CTR) | abbreviation
