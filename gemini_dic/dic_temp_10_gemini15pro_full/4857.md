aliene: /ˈeɪliən/ | adj. | 外来的；异国的；陌生的；格格不入的 |  Coming from a different country, race, or group; unfamiliar and disturbing or distasteful. |  The customs of that country seemed aliene to him. 那个国家的风俗习惯对他来说很陌生。 | 近义词：foreign, strange, unfamiliar, exotic | 反义词：familiar, native, indigenous | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Old French *alien (modern French *alién*), from Latin *alienus* "of or belonging to another, foreign," from *alius* "other, another."
