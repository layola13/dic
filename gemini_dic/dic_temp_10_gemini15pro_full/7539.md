annexes:/ˈæneksɪz/
n. |附件，附属物；增建部分|Something added or attached, especially a supplement to a document.|The treaty included annexes listing the territories ceded to France.|近义词：appendix, supplement, attachment, addition|反义词:detachment, separation
词性:名词复数

annex:/əˈneks/
vt.|兼并，吞并；附加，添加|To attach, append, or add, especially to something larger or more important. To incorporate (territory) into the domain of a city, country, or state.|Germany annexed Austria in 1938.|近义词：append, attach, add, incorporate, seize, conquer|反义词:detach, separate, relinquish
词性:动词

词源: From Latin *annexus*, past participle of *annectere*, "to tie or bind to".
