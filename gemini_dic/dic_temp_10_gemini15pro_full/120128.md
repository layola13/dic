mina:/ˈmiːnə/ | n. | 米纳, 港口; 矿井;  | A mina is a unit of weight and currency used in ancient times, particularly in the Middle East. It can also refer to a harbor or a mine. |  The ancient city had a bustling mina, filled with ships from all over the world. 这座古城有一个繁忙的港口，来自世界各地的船只都停泊在那里。| synonym: harbor, port, mine | antonym: open sea, surface | noun
