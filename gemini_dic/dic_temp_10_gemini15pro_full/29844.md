causally:/ˈkɔːzəli/| adv.|因果地，原因上|In a way that relates to cause and effect. |例句：The two events are not causally related. 这两件事没有因果关系。|近义词：consequently, therefore, thus|反义词:accidentally, coincidentally|词性:副词
Etymology: From Late Latin *causalis, from Latin causa ("cause").
