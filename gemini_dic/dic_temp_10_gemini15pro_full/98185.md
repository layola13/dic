insculption:/ɪnˈskʌlpʃən/| n.|铭刻；雕刻；刻印|An inscription is words carved into a surface, such as stone or metal.|The inscription on the tombstone was barely legible. 墓碑上的铭文几乎难以辨认。|近义词：engraving, carving, etching|反义词: erasure, deletion|词性:noun
Etymology: From Latin "insculpere" (to carve in), from "in" (in) + "sculpere" (to carve).
