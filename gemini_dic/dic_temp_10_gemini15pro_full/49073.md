dehydrating:/diːˈhaɪdreɪtɪŋ/
| adj. |脱水的；使脱水的|removing water from; causing to lose water|例句：Dehydrating food is a good way to preserve it.  脱水食物是保存食物的好方法。|近义词：drying, parching|反义词：hydrating, moisturizing|词性:形容词
dehydrating:/diːˈhaɪdreɪtɪŋ/
| v. |脱水；使脱水|the process of removing water from something|例句：The hot sun was dehydrating the runners. 炎热的太阳使跑步者脱水。|近义词：drying, parching|反义词：hydrating, moisturizing|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From de- (prefix meaning "remove") + hydrate (to combine with water) + -ing (present participle suffix).  Ultimately from Greek *hudōr* (water).  由表示“去除”的前缀 de- + hydrate（与水结合）+ -ing（现在分词后缀）组成。最终源自希腊语 *hudōr*（水）。
