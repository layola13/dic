gradually:/ˈɡrædʒuəli/ (US), /ˈɡrædʒʊəli/ (UK)| adv.|逐渐地，逐步地|in a gradual way; slowly over a period of time|The pain gradually subsided. 疼痛逐渐消退了。|The weather gradually improved. 天气逐渐好转了。|近义词：slowly, progressively, steadily, bit by bit|反义词：suddenly, abruptly, rapidly, quickly|词性:副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *gradualis, from Latin gradus (“step, degree”) + -alis.
