inconspicuousnesses:/ˌɪnkənˈspɪkjuəsnəsɪz/
n.
不显眼，不引人注目
the state or quality of being inconspicuous; lack of prominence or visibility.
Her inconspicuousnesses in the crowd made it easy for her to observe unnoticed. 她在一群人中毫不起眼，让她得以不被注意地进行观察。
近义词:invisibility, unobtrusiveness, unnoticeableness
反义词:conspicuousness, prominence, noticeability
词性:名词

inconspicuous:/ˌɪnkənˈspɪkjuəs/
adj.
不显眼的，不引人注目的
not clearly visible or attracting attention.
He tried to remain inconspicuous in the corner of the room. 他试图待在房间的角落里，不引人注目。
近义词:unobtrusive, unnoticeable, subtle
反义词:conspicuous, prominent, noticeable
词性:形容词

词源:
From Latin *inconspicuus*, from in- "not" + conspicuus "clearly visible, striking," from conspicere "to look at attentively," from com- "together" + specere "to look."
