malvaceous: /mælˈveɪʃəs/ | adj. | 锦葵科的; 类似锦葵的 | Relating to or characteristic of plants of the mallow family (Malvaceae), typically having five petals and numerous stamens. |  The okra plant is malvaceous. 秋葵是锦葵科植物。| synonym: mallow-like | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps non-malvaceous) | adjective
