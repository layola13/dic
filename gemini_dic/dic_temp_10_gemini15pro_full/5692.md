alumina:/əˈluːmɪnə/ | n. |氧化铝|A white crystalline oxide of aluminum, Al₂O₃, occurring naturally as corundum, and used in various forms in making cement, refractories, ceramics, and abrasives, and as an adsorbent and catalyst. |例句：Alumina is a key ingredient in many industrial processes. 氧化铝是许多工业流程中的关键成分。

例句：The hardness of alumina makes it suitable for abrasives. 氧化铝的硬度使其适用于磨料。 |近义词：aluminum oxide, corundum |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

alumine: /əˈluːmiːn/ | n. |氧化铝 (旧称，现常用alumina) |An older term for alumina, the white crystalline oxide of aluminum.  This term is less common now. |例句：Alumine was once widely used in the production of ceramics. 氧化铝曾广泛用于陶瓷生产。（旧用法）

例句：The term alumine has largely been replaced by alumina.  "氧化铝"一词现在很大程度上已被"alumina"取代。|近义词：alumina, aluminum oxide |反义词: N/A |词性: noun
