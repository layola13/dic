adjective

badly shaped; deformed.
The tree had a misshapen trunk after being struck by lightning.  这棵树被闪电击中后，树干变得畸形了。

近义词:deformed, distorted, contorted, grotesque

词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From mis- (meaning "badly" or "wrongly") + shapen (past participle of the verb "shape").  The word "shape" comes from Old English "gesceap" meaning "creation, form, appearance."
