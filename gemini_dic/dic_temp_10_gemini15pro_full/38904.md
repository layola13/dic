confessed:/kənˈfest/| adj. |公开承认的，坦白的|openly admitted or acknowledged. |a confessed murderer 一个公开承认的凶手|近义词：admitted, avowed, acknowledged|反义词：unacknowledged, undisclosed, denied|adjective
