convertibleness:/kənˌvɜːrtɪblənəs/ | n. |可兑换性，可变换性| The quality or state of being convertible; convertibility.  The ease with which something can be changed into a different form or function. | The convertibleness of currency is crucial for international trade. 货币的可兑换性对国际贸易至关重要。|  The convertibleness of the sofa into a bed is a useful feature for small apartments. 沙发可转换成床的功能对于小公寓来说非常实用。 | synonyms: convertibility, interchangeability, transformability | antonyms: inconvertibility, fixity, immutability | noun
