millers:/ˈmɪlərz/| n.|磨坊主；磨工；经营磨坊的人；蛾类|People who own or operate mills, especially flour mills, or a type of moth.  The word is the plural form of "miller."|The millers ground the wheat into flour. 磨坊主把小麦磨成了面粉。|The millers predicted a good harvest this year. 磨坊主们预测今年会有好收成。|近义词：grinders, mill operators, millwrights|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps consumers)|词性:Noun
