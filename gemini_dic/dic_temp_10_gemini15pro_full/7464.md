anisotropic:/ˌænɪsəˈtrɒpɪk/ /ˌænɪsəˈtrɑːpɪk/| adj.|各向异性的|Exhibiting properties with different values when measured along axes in different directions.  Having a physical property that has a different value when measured in different directions.|例句：The anisotropic properties of the crystal caused the light to refract differently.  这种晶体的各向异性特性导致光线以不同方式折射。

例句：Wood is an anisotropic material, exhibiting different strength and stiffness properties along and across the grain. 木头是一种各向异性的材料，沿纹理和横纹理方向表现出不同的强度和刚度特性。|近义词：non-uniform, inhomogeneous|反义词：isotropic, uniform|词性: adjective
