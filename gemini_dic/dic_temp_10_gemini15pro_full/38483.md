conchoid:/ˈkɒŋkɔɪd/ (US) /ˈkɑːŋkɔɪd/ (UK)| n. |  蚌线；螺线 | A curve, resembling a shell, generated by a point moving so that its distance from a fixed line, measured along the line joining it to a fixed point, is constant. | The conchoid of Nicomedes is a specific type of conchoid. 尼科梅德斯蚌线是一种特殊的蚌线。| synonyms: shell-like curve, limaçon (in some cases) | antonyms: N/A | noun

conchoid:/ˈkɒŋkɔɪd/ (US) /ˈkɑːŋkɔɪd/ (UK)| adj. |  蚌线的；螺线的| Resembling or relating to a conchoid curve. | The artist used a conchoid shape in his design.  这位艺术家在他的设计中使用了蚌线形状。 | synonyms: shell-shaped, limaçon-like (in some cases) | antonyms: N/A | adjective
