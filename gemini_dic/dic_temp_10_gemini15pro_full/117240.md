mecate:/məˈkɑːteɪ/ /məˈkeɪt/
n.
measuring rope; lasso; rope
A long rope, typically made of horsehair or other strong fiber, used for various purposes such as measuring land, tethering animals, or as a lasso.
The surveyor used a mecate to measure the boundaries of the property.  勘测员用一根测量绳来测量房产的边界。
The vaquero expertly swung his mecate to capture the stray calf. 牛仔熟练地挥动套索捕捉走失的小牛。
近义词:rope, lasso, cord, line
反义词:(No direct antonym, possibly "chain" if contrasting flexibility)
词性:noun
