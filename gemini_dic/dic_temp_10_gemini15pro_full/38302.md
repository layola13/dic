concave:/ˈkɒnkeɪv/ (British), /ˈkɑːnkeɪv/ (American)| adj.|凹的，凹面的|Curved inward, like the inside of a bowl.|The mirror was concave, making the image appear larger.|近义词:hollow, recessed, sunken|反义词:convex, protuberant|adjective

concavous:/kənˈkeɪvəs/ (less common variant of concave)| adj.|凹的，凹面的|An older, less common form of "concave," meaning curved inward.|The concavous lens scattered the light.|近义词:concave, hollow, recessed|反义词:convex, protuberant|adjective
