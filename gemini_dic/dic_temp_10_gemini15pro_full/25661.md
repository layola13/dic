bung:/bʌŋ/
noun|塞子，盖子，软木塞|A stopper for closing a hole, especially in a barrel.|He lost the bung from his wine barrel. 他把酒桶的塞子弄丢了。|stopper, cork, plug|opening|n.

verb|塞住，堵住|To close with a bung or stopper.|He bunged the hole in the barrel with a rag. 他用破布堵住了桶上的洞。|stop, plug, seal|open|v.
