clear-headed:|adj.|头脑清醒的；冷静的；思路清晰的|Able to think clearly and rationally, especially in a difficult situation.|She remained clear-headed throughout the crisis.  在整个危机期间，她保持头脑清醒。|近义词：lucid, rational, level-headed, composed, calm|反义词：confused, muddled, bewildered, disoriented, addled|词性:adjective
