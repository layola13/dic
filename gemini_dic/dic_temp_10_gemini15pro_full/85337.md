harpsichord: /ˈhɑːrpsɪkɔːrd/ (US), /ˈhɑːpsɪkɔːd/ (UK)| n. |大键琴|A keyboard instrument that looks like a small grand piano but produces sounds by plucking the strings with plectra rather than hammering them as in a piano. |例句：The harpsichord's delicate sound filled the concert hall.  大键琴精致的声音充满了音乐厅。

|例句：Bach wrote many pieces for the harpsichord. 巴赫为大键琴创作了许多作品。|近义词：clavichord, virginal|反义词: piano (in terms of mechanism, not necessarily sound)|词性:noun
