ecce:/ˈɛtʃeɪ/| interj.|看哪！瞧！|Used to call attention to something.  From Latin, meaning "behold" or "look."|例句：Ecce homo! Behold the man!  看哪！这人！

例句：Ecce signum! Behold the sign!  看哪！标记！|近义词：behold, lo, look|反义词:|interjection
