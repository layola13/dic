fourthest:/ˈfɜːrðɪst/| adj.|最远的|This word is considered nonstandard and is a double superlative. It combines the already superlative "furthest" with another superlative ending "-est". Use "furthest" instead.|*Incorrect:* This is the fourthest I've ever walked. *Correct:* This is the furthest I've ever walked.  这是我走过的最远的路程。|近义词：furthest, farthest|反义词：nearest, closest|词性:adjective
