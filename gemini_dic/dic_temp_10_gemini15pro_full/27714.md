cane:/keɪn/ | n. | 拐杖；手杖；（某些植物的）茎；藤条 | A stick used to help someone walk; the stem of particular plants, especially long thin ones like bamboo; thin, flexible strips of rattan, used to make furniture | The old man leaned heavily on his cane.  老人沉重地倚在他的拐杖上。| synonyms: stick, staff, walking stick, rod | antonyms: crutch | noun

cane:/keɪn/ | vt. | 用藤条鞭打 | To beat someone with a cane |  He was caned for stealing. 他因为偷窃而被藤条鞭打。 | synonyms: flog, whip, lash, beat | antonyms:  comfort, soothe | verb
