dhoti: /ˈdəʊti/ (UK), /ˈdoʊti/ (US)| n. |缠腰布，兜提|A single piece of unstitched cloth, usually white or cream, wrapped around the waist and legs, traditionally worn by men in India. |Example:  He wore a crisp white dhoti. 他穿着一件挺括的白缠腰布。

Example:  Dhotis are a common sight in rural India.  在印度农村，缠腰布很常见。

|Synonyms:  lungi, mundu|Antonyms:  trousers, pants|Noun
