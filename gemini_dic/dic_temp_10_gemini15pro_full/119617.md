middens:/ˈmɪdnz/| n. |垃圾堆；废物堆；贝冢|A refuse heap; a dunghill; specifically, a kitchen midden, a mound or deposit containing shells, bones, and other refuse marking the site of a prehistoric settlement.|Archaeologists excavated the middens and found ancient pottery shards. 考古学家挖掘了这些垃圾堆，发现了古代陶器的碎片。|近义词：dumps, heaps, refuse piles|反义词: N/A|名词
