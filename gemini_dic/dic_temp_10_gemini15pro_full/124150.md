mortise:/ˈmɔːrtɪs/ (US) /ˈmɔːtɪs/ (UK)| v. n. adj.| 榫眼；榫接；固定；|A hole or recess cut into a piece of timber or other material into which another part (a tenon) is inserted to form a joint.|The carpenter carefully mortised the two pieces of wood together. 木匠小心地将两块木头榫接在一起。|近义词：join, connect, fasten, secure|反义词：separate, disconnect, loosen|词性:动词、名词、形容词

mortised:/ˈmɔːrtɪst/ (US) /ˈmɔːtɪst/ (UK)| adj.| 榫接的；固定的；|Joined by a mortise and tenon.|The table legs were mortised into the frame. 桌腿被榫接入框架中。|近义词：joined, connected, fastened, secured|反义词：separated, disconnected, loosened|词性:形容词
