outhunting:/ˈaʊtˌhʌntɪŋ/
verb
胜过狩猎，在狩猎中超过
Surpassing someone in hunting; hunting more successfully than someone else.
The young hunter was outhunting his father, bringing home more game each time.  年轻的猎人在狩猎方面胜过他的父亲，每次都带回更多的猎物。
近义词: outdoing, excelling, surpassing
反义词: underperforming, failing
词性: verb
