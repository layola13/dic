eugenin:/juːˈdʒiːnɪn/ | n. |优香精，丁香酚 | A crystalline phenolic compound C₁₀H₁₂O₃ extracted from clove oil and used as a local anesthetic and antiseptic.  It is also found in other essential oils, like pimento and bay leaf. | Eugenin is commonly used in dentistry as a local anesthetic. |  近义词: clove oil, caryophyllene | 反义词: N/A | noun
