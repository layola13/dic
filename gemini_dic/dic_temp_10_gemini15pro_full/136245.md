nonrepatriable:/ˌnɒnriːˈpeɪtriəbl/ /ˌnɑːnriːˈpeɪtriəbl/
adjective
不可汇回的，不能遣返的
Not allowed or able to be sent or brought back to one's own country.
Profits from the investment are nonrepatriable. 这项投资的利润不可汇回。
Near Synonyms: non-returnable, unrepatriable, unreturnable
Antonyms: repatriable, returnable
词性: adjective
