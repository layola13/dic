homonymic:/ˌhɒməˈnɪmɪk/ /ˌhɑːməˈnɪmɪk/| adj.|同音异义的|Having the same pronunciation but different meanings, origins, or spelling.|The words "right" and "write" are homonymic.|近义词：homonymous, homophonous|反义词：heteronymic|词性: adjective
