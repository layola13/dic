etude:/ˈeɪtuːd/ (US), /ˈeɪtjuːd/ (UK)| n. |练习曲；习作|A short musical composition, typically for one instrument, designed as an exercise to improve the technique or demonstrate the skill of the player.| Chopin's etudes are notoriously difficult. 肖邦的练习曲是出了名的难。|Synonyms: exercise, study, piece, composition|Antonyms: performance, improvisation|noun
