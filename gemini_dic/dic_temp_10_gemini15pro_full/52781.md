dilemmatical:/ˌdɪləˈmætɪkl/ | adj. | 困境的，进退两难的 | Presenting or involving a difficult problem or choice; puzzling. |  She faced a dilemmatical situation, whether to stay in her current job or accept a new offer. 她面临着一个进退两难的境地，是留在现在的工作还是接受新的工作邀请。 | difficult, perplexing, problematic | easy, straightforward | adjective

dilemma: /dɪˈlemə/ | n. | 困境，进退两难的局面 | A situation in which a difficult choice has to be made between two or more alternatives, especially equally undesirable ones. | He was in a dilemma about whether to tell the truth or lie. 他进退两难，不知道是该说实话还是撒谎。| predicament, quandary, problem | solution, advantage | noun
