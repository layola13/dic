almadie:/ˈælmədi/ | n. | 独木舟，小船 | A small canoe-like boat, usually made from a single tree trunk, used especially in West Africa. |  Example: The fisherman paddled his almadie down the river. 渔夫划着他的独木舟顺流而下。

Example: Almadies were a common sight on the Senegal River. 在塞内加尔河上，独木舟很常见。 | 近义词: canoe, dugout, pirogue | 反义词: ship, yacht | 词性: noun
