cerulean: /səˈruːliən/ /səˈruljən/
| adj. n.|天蓝色的，蔚蓝的；天蓝色|A deep blue color, like that of a clear sky. |The cerulean waters of the Mediterranean beckoned. 地中海蔚蓝的海水令人神往。|近义词：azure, sky-blue, sapphire|反义词：dark, black|词性:形容词, 名词


ceruleans: /səˈruːliənz/ /səˈruljənz/
| n.|天蓝色；蔚蓝色（复数）；天蓝色颜料|The plural form of cerulean, referring to instances or shades of the color, or pigments producing it. |The artist used various ceruleans in his landscape painting.  这位艺术家在他的风景画中使用了各种各样的天蓝色。|近义词：azures, sky-blues|反义词：darks, blacks|词性:名词
