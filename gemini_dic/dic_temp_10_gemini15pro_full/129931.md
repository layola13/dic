non-literary:/ˌnɒnˈlɪtərəri/ /ˌnɑːnˈlɪtəreri/
adjective
非文学的；不具文学性的
Not related to literature; not having the characteristics of literature.
This research focuses on non-literary texts from the 18th century. 这项研究的重点是18世纪的非文学文本。
