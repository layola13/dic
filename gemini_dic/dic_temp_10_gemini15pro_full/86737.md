What did he say? = 他说了什么？
I went to the park yesterday. = 我昨天去了公园。


Past Continuous:
What were you doing when I called? = 我打电话时你在做什么？
She was sleeping when the phone rang. = 电话响的时候她正在睡觉。


Past Perfect:
What had happened before I arrived? = 在我到达之前发生了什么事？
They had already eaten dinner before I got there. = 在我到那儿之前他们已经吃过晚饭了。


Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before the accident? = 事故发生前你一直在做什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do tomorrow? = 你明天要做什么？
I will go to the beach tomorrow. = 我明天要去海滩。


Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time tomorrow? = 明天这个时候你会在做什么？

Future Perfect:

Future Perfect Continuous:

I wish I had more time. = 我希望我有更多的时间。


Passive Voice:

The problem is being solved. = 问题正在被解决。
