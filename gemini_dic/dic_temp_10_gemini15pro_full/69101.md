faux:/foʊ/ (US), /fəʊ/ (UK)| adj.|人造的，假的|Made in imitation; artificial; not genuine.  Not real; fake.|例句：She wore a faux fur coat. 她穿了一件人造毛皮大衣。|近义词：artificial, fake, imitation, synthetic, sham, mock|反义词：real, genuine, authentic|词性: adjective

faux:/foʊ/ (US), /fəʊ/ (UK)| n.|仿制品|An imitation, typically of a fabric or material.|例句：The handbag was a cheap faux.  这个手袋是便宜的仿制品。|近义词：fake, imitation, copy, replica|反义词：original|词性: noun
