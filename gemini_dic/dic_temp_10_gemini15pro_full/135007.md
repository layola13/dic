nonnegligence:/ˌnɒnˈnɛɡlɪdʒəns/
noun
无过失；非疏忽
The state of not being negligent; absence of negligence.
The court found nonnegligence on the part of the defendant. 法院认定被告没有过失。
Synonyms: due care, diligence, carefulness
Antonyms: negligence, carelessness, neglect
词性: 名词
