ambled:/ˈæmbəld/ (US), /ˈæmbld/ (UK)| v. | 漫步，缓行 | walk or move at a slow, relaxed pace. |
He ambled down the street, enjoying the sunshine. 他漫步在大街上，享受着阳光。

She ambled through the park, stopping to admire the flowers.  她在公园里漫步，停下来欣赏鲜花。

近义词：strolled, sauntered, meandered | 反义词：rushed, hurried, sprinted | 词性: 动词

词源: From the Middle English *amblen*, perhaps from a Scandinavian source, ultimately of uncertain origin.
