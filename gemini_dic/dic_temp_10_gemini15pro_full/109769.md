levirostres:/ˌlɛvaɪˈrɒstriːz/
| n. |细喙鸟类；|A group of birds characterized by their slender beaks, including hummingbirds, swifts, and nightjars.|Hummingbirds are part of the levirostres group.  蜂鸟是细喙鸟类的一部分。|近义词:  Tenuirostres (sometimes used synonymously)|反义词:  (No direct antonym; could contrast with "thick-billed birds")|词性: noun


Etymology:
From Latin *levis*  (light, slender) + *rostrum* (beak, snout)
