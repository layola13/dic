broke away:/broʊk əˈweɪ/
verb
脱离，逃脱，摆脱
The prisoner broke away from his guards.  囚犯从他的警卫手中逃脱了。
A small group broke away from the main party and formed a new political party. 一小群人脱离了主要政党，组成了一个新的政党。
近义词: escape, flee, secede, separate
反义词: join, unite, merge
词性: verb

broke away:/broʊk əˈweɪ/
adjective
脱离的，分裂的
a broke-away group  一个脱离的团体
The broke-away republic declared its independence.  脱离的共和国宣布独立。
近义词: separatist, independent, rebellious
反义词: unified, integrated, affiliated
词性: adjective


词源:  "Break away" is a phrasal verb formed from the verb "break" and the adverb "away."  "Break" comes from Old English *brecan, related to Old High German brehhan.  "Away" comes from Old English aweg.
