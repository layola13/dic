hyperdelicacies:/ˌhaɪpərˈdɛlɪkəsiz/| n. |超级美味佳肴；极品美食|Extremely rare and expensive delicacies; food items considered to be the ultimate in luxury and refinement.|例句：The billionaire's birthday feast featured a table laden with hyperdelicacies from around the world.  这位亿万富翁的生日宴会上摆满了来自世界各地的极品美食。

例句： Only the most discerning palates could appreciate the subtle nuances of these hyperdelicacies. 只有最挑剔的味蕾才能欣赏到这些极品美食的微妙之处。|近义词：delicacies, gourmet food, luxury food|反义词:staple food, basic food|词性:noun
