affirmative action:/əˈfɜːrmətɪv ˈækʃ(ə)n/| n.|平权行动|A set of policies and practices intended to address past and present discrimination by providing opportunities to individuals and groups who have been historically excluded or underrepresented, especially in areas like employment and education.  These policies often focus on race, ethnicity, gender, disability, and other protected characteristics.|The university has a strong affirmative action policy to increase the number of minority faculty members.| 大学有一个强有力的平权行动政策，以增加少数族裔教师的人数。|positive discrimination, equal opportunity measures|reverse discrimination, discrimination|noun
