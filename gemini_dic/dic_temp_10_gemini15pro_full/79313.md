gleamiest:/ˈɡliːmiɪst/| adj.|最闪亮的，最光辉的|superlative of gleaming; shining brightly, reflecting light|The gleamiest car at the show was a vintage Rolls Royce. 展览会上最闪亮的车是一辆老式劳斯莱斯。|Synonyms: brightest, shiniest, most radiant, most sparkling|Antonyms: dullest, dimmest, darkest|adjective

gleaming:/ˈɡliːmɪŋ/| adj.|闪亮的，光辉的|shining brightly, reflecting light|The gleaming swords of the knights caught the sunlight. 骑士们闪亮的剑反射着阳光。|Synonyms: bright, shiny, radiant, sparkling|Antonyms: dull, dim, dark|adjective

gleam:/ɡliːm/| n. vi.|微光，闪光；闪烁|a faint or brief light; to shine faintly or briefly|A gleam of hope appeared in his eyes. 他眼中闪现出一丝希望。|Synonyms: glimmer, shimmer, flicker, sparkle|Antonyms: darkness, dullness|noun, verb
