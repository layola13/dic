boneache:/ˈboʊneɪk/| n. |骨痛；骨节疼痛|A dull, persistent pain in the bones or joints. |例句：I've had a terrible boneache in my leg all day. 我腿疼了一整天。

例句：The damp weather always gives me boneaches. 潮湿的天气总是让我骨头疼。|近义词：bone pain, joint pain, rheumatic pain, aches and pains|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider "comfort," "well-being")|词性:noun
