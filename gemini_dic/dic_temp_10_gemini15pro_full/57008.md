double-parkers:/ˈdʌbəlˈpɑːrkərz/ (US) /ˈdʌbəlˈpɑːkəz/ (UK)| n. |双排停车者|People who park their vehicles beside another already parked vehicle, usually blocking it in and obstructing traffic.|The double-parkers on Main Street caused a huge traffic jam. 主街上的双排停车造成了严重的交通堵塞。|Double-parkers are a nuisance to other drivers. 双排停车者给其他司机带来麻烦。|近义词：illegal parkers, inconsiderate parkers|反义词: considerate parkers|Noun
