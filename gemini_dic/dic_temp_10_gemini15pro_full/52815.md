diluent:/ˈdɪljuːənt/| n.|稀释剂; 冲淡剂|A diluent is a substance used to dilute something, typically a solvent used to reduce the concentration of a solution.|The painter added a diluent to the paint to make it easier to spread. 画家在油漆中加入稀释剂，使其更容易涂抹。|synonyms: thinner, solvent, weakening agent|antonyms:  concentrate, thickener|noun

diluent:/daɪˈluːənt/| adj.|稀释的|Diluting or tending to dilute.|The diluent effect of the water weakened the coffee. 水的稀释作用使咖啡变淡了。|synonyms: thinning, weakening|antonyms:  concentrating, thickening|adjective
