overforwardnesses: /ˌoʊvərˈfɔːrwərdnɪsɪz/
noun
过度热情，过分积极
Excessive eagerness or boldness; being too forward.
His overforwardnesses made his colleagues uncomfortable. 他的过度热情让他的同事感到不舒服。
Near Synonyms:  presumptuousness, audacity, effrontery, impertinence, impudence
Antonyms:  shyness, timidity, reticence, reserve
n.

Etymology:
From "over" (excessive) + "forward" (bold, presumptuous) + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns denoting a state or quality).  The word "forward" itself comes from Old English "foreweard" (toward the front).
