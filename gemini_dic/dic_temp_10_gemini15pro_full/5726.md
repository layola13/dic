alveolar: /ælˈviːələr/ | adj. | 肺泡的，齿槽的 | Relating to or involving the alveoli (tiny air sacs in the lungs) or the alveolar ridge (the part of the jaw where the teeth are set). | The alveolar consonant /t/ is pronounced by placing the tongue against the alveolar ridge. 齿槽辅音/t/是通过将舌头抵住齿槽嵴来发音的。 | 近义词: (depending on context) dental, pulmonary | 反义词: (no direct antonym) | 词性: 形容词


词源:  From Modern Latin *alveolāris, from Latin alveolus (“small cavity, pit, socket”), diminutive of alveus (“hollow, cavity, trough, tub”).
