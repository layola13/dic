impriming:/ɪmˈprɪntɪŋ/ | n. | 印记，铭刻；(尤指幼鸟)印随学习 | the process by which a young animal comes to recognize (another animal, person, or thing) as a parent or other object of habitual trust | The ducklings followed their mother everywhere, a classic example of imprinting. 小鸭子们到处跟着它们的妈妈，这是印随学习的经典例子。 |  imprinting, inscription, impress |  forgetting, erasure | noun
