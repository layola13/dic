frequents:/ˈfriːkwənts/| v.|常去，经常光顾|He frequents the local pub. 他经常光顾当地酒吧。|近义词：visits often, patronizes, haunts|反义词: avoids, shuns|词性:动词
Etymology: From Late Middle English *frequentyn*, from Latin *frequentāre* (“to visit often, resort to, crowd, throng”), from *frequēns* (“crowded, numerous, often”).
