commemorative: /kəˈmɛmərətɪv/ | adj. | 纪念的，庆祝的 | Serving to commemorate; intended as a memorial. |  This commemorative stamp was issued to mark the centenary of her birth. 这枚纪念邮票是为了纪念她百年诞辰而发行的。 | commemorative plaque 纪念牌匾 |  This building serves as a commemorative monument to the fallen soldiers. 这座建筑是为纪念阵亡将士而建的纪念碑。 | synonyms: memorial, celebratory, remembrance, anniversary | antonyms: unmemorable, forgotten | adjective


commemorative: /kəˈmɛmərətɪv/ | n. | 纪念物，纪念品 | An object, such as a coin or postage stamp, intended as a memorial. |  The family created a commemorative to honor their late grandfather.  家人制作了一个纪念物来纪念他们已故的祖父。|  She bought a commemorative T-shirt from the concert. 她从演唱会上买了一件纪念T恤。 | synonyms: souvenir, keepsake, memento, tribute | antonyms: none | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin commemorativus, from commemorare "to call to mind, remember," from com- "together" + memorare "to bring to mind," from memor "mindful."
