hitchcock:/ˈhɪtʃkɒk/ (UK) /ˈhɪtʃkɑːk/ (US)| n. |希区柯克|Alfred Hitchcock, a highly influential British film director and producer known for his suspense and thriller films.|His films are reminiscent of Hitchcock. 他的电影让人想起希区柯克。|近义词: filmmaker, director|反义词: N/A|noun
hitchcockian:/ˌhɪtʃˈkɒkiən/ (UK) /ˌhɪtʃˈkɑːkiən/ (US)| adj. |希区柯克式的|Characteristic of or resembling the style of Alfred Hitchcock, especially in creating suspense and psychological thrillers.|The film had a distinctly Hitchcockian atmosphere. 这部电影有一种明显的希区柯克式氛围。|近义词: suspenseful, thrilling|反义词: calming, peaceful|adjective
