old-norses:/ˌoʊld ˈnɔːrsɪz/| adj. n.| 古诺尔斯语（的）| Relating to the language of medieval Scandinavia, also known as Old Norse.  It refers to the North Germanic language that was spoken by the inhabitants of Scandinavia and their overseas settlements during the Viking Age.|The sagas are written in Old Norse.  这些萨迦是用古诺尔斯语写成的.|近义词：Old Scandinavian|反义词: Modern Scandinavian|词性:形容词, 名词


Etymology:
From Old English norðerne + -isc. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *nurþrą (“north”). Cognate with Old Frisian northarna, Old Saxon nordroni, Old High German nordarōni.
