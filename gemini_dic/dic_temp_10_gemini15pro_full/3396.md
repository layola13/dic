afk:/eɪ ef keɪ/ | adj. | 离开键盘，暂时离开 | Away From Keyboard. Used in online chat and gaming to indicate that the user is temporarily not at their keyboard. | Example: "I'll be afk for a few minutes, grabbing a drink."  我离开几分钟，去拿杯饮料。 | Example: "He went afk in the middle of the raid, causing us to lose." 他在突袭中离开键盘，导致我们输了。 | 近义词: away, unavailable, offline | 反义词: present, online, available | 词性: adjective (primarily used as an initialism)
