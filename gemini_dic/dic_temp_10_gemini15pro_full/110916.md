lipocephala:/ˌlɪpoʊˈsɛfələ/ | n. |亲脂性；脂溶性 | The tendency of a substance to dissolve in fats, oils, lipids, and non-polar solvents.  |  The lipocephala of a drug determines how easily it can cross the blood-brain barrier.  这种药物的亲脂性决定了它穿过血脑屏障的容易程度。 | 近义词: lipophilicity, fat-soluble | 反义词: lipophobia, hydrophilicity | 词性: noun

lipocephalic:/ˌlɪpoʊˈsɛfəlɪk/ | adj. |亲脂的；脂溶性的 | Relating to or having an affinity for lipids. | Lipocephalic substances are absorbed more readily by fatty tissues. 亲脂性物质更容易被脂肪组织吸收。 | 近义词: lipophilic, fat-soluble | 反义词: lipophobic, hydrophilic | 词性: adjective


词源:  From Greek *lipos* (fat) + *kephalē* (head), implying an affinity for lipids.
