nonbulbaceous:/ˌnɒnbʌlˈbeɪʃəs/
adjective
非球茎的
Not having a bulb; lacking a bulbous swelling at the base.
Many popular perennials are nonbulbaceous. 许多受欢迎的多年生植物是非球茎的。
Nonbulbaceous plants often spread through rhizomes or stolons. 非球茎植物通常通过根茎或匍匐茎蔓延。
近义词: acaulescent, nonbulbous
反义词: bulbous, bulbaceous
词性: adjective
