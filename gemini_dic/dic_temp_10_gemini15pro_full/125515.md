multisegmented:/ˌmʌltiˈsɛɡmɛntɪd/
adjective
多节的，多段的
composed of multiple segments or parts.
The multisegmented body of the millipede allows it to navigate tight spaces.  千足虫多节的身体使它能够在狭小的空间里穿行。
A multisegmented worm crawled across the damp soil. 一条多节的蠕虫爬过潮湿的土壤。

近义词: segmented, jointed, articulated
反义词: unsegmented, whole, entire
词性: adjective
