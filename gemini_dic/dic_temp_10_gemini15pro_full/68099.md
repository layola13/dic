fads:/fædz/
noun
一时流行的风尚；时尚；一时的狂热
A fashion that is followed enthusiastically for a short time; a craze.
例句：Bell bottoms were a fad in the 1970s.  喇叭裤是20世纪70年代的一种时尚。

例句：He's always chasing the latest fads. 他总是追逐最新的时尚。

synonym:crazes, trends, fashions, vogues
antonym:traditions, classics, standards
