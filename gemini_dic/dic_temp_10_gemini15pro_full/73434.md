foremanship: /ˈfɔːrmənʃɪp/ /ˈfɔːrmənˌʃɪp/
n.
工头职位；工头技艺；领导才能
The skill or position of a foreman.  The art of leading and supervising a group of workers.
He demonstrated excellent foremanship in managing the construction project.  他在管理这个建筑项目中展现了出色的领导才能。
leadership, supervision, management
subordination, neglect
noun
