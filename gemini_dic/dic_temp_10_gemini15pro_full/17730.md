bedecked:/bɪˈdɛkt/
adjective
装饰的，装点的
Decorated or adorned, typically richly or elaborately.
The Christmas tree was bedecked with twinkling lights and colorful ornaments.  圣诞树上装饰着闪烁的灯光和五颜六色的饰品。
The bride was bedecked in jewels. 新娘佩戴着珠宝。
近义词：adorned, decorated, ornamented, embellished, arrayed
反义词：bare, plain, unadorned, undecorated
词性: adjective

词源:
From be- + deck, from Middle English decken, from Old English decan "to cover," of Germanic origin; related to Dutch dekken and German decken.
