dooring:/ˈdɔːrɪŋ/
n.
开门杀；被车门撞击
The act of opening a car door into the path of a cyclist or other traffic, causing a collision.
A cyclist was injured in a dooring incident on Main Street.  一位骑自行车的人在主街上被车门撞伤。
近义词:car door collision, door strike
