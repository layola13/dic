The slime the kids were playing with was incredibly globbier after they added more glue. 孩子们玩的粘液在他们加了更多胶水后变得非常粘稠。|近义词：stickier, gooier, thicker, viscid, glutinous|反义词：thinner, runnier, watery|词性:形容词
