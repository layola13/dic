overliberalization: /ˌoʊvərˌlɪbərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/
| n. |过度自由化|The act of liberalizing something, such as an economy or a society, to an excessive degree.  This often leads to negative consequences such as instability or vulnerability.|The overliberalization of the financial markets contributed to the economic crisis. 金融市场的过度自由化导致了经济危机。| deregulation, liberalization |regulation, restriction|noun
