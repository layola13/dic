mouthpieces:/ˈmaʊθˌpiːsɪz/| n.|代言人，喉舌；（乐器）吹口；（电话）送话器|A mouthpiece is a part of a musical instrument that you put in or near your mouth to produce sound. It can also refer to a person or group that speaks on behalf of another person or organization, often expressing their views or opinions.  A mouthpiece can also be the part of a telephone, etc. that you speak into.|He's the president's mouthpiece on environmental issues. 他是总统在环境问题上的代言人。|Synonyms: spokesperson, advocate, representative, agent, mouthpiece (for a musical instrument)|Antonyms: listener, audience (These are not direct opposites, but represent contrasting roles).
词性:noun
