lops:/lɒps/ | n. |下垂物，垂耳，修剪下来的部分| something that lops or hangs down; ears that droop; parts cut off | The rabbit's lops framed its face.  兔子的垂耳环绕着它的脸。| synonyms: droop, hang, dangle | antonyms: stick up, protrude | noun


lops:/lɒps/ | v. |下垂，耷拉，修剪| to hang down limply; to cut off branches or twigs | The branches of the willow tree loped over the water. 柳树的枝条垂在水面上。| synonyms: droop, sag, trim | antonyms: rise, ascend | verb


Etymology:
From Middle English *loppen*, from Old Norse *loptr* (“a rag, something hanging loose”). Cognate with Icelandic *loptur*, Norwegian *lopt*.
