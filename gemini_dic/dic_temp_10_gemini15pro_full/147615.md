overplenteously:/ˌoʊvərˈplɛntɪəsli/
| adv.|过度丰盛地，过多地|Excessively, abundantly, more than enough.|He furnished his house overplenteously, with luxurious items in every room. 他把自己的房子布置得过于奢华，每个房间都摆满了豪华的物品。|The fields were blessed with an overplenteously supply of rain this year. 今年的田地雨水过多。|近义词：excessively, abundantly, lavishly, profusely|反义词: sparsely, meagerly, insufficiently|词性:副词
词源: over (过度) + plenteous (丰富的) + -ly (副词后缀)
