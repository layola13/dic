award-winning:/əˈwɔːrdˌwɪnɪŋ/| adj.|获奖的；成功的|Having received an award or awards, especially prestigious ones.  Recognized for excellence and achievement.|例句：The award-winning film captivated audiences worldwide. 这部获奖影片吸引了全世界的观众。

例句：She is an award-winning author with several bestsellers. 她是一位屡获殊荣的作家，拥有多部畅销书。|近义词：acclaimed, honored, prize-winning, successful, triumphant|反义词：unsuccessful, losing, failing|词性:形容词

Etymology->
"award" (from Old North French *awarder, from a- "to" + warder "to guard, protect, keep," from Frankish *wardon "to watch") + "-winning" (present participle of "win," from Old English winnan "to struggle, contend; suffer; gain by working, fighting, or enduring")
