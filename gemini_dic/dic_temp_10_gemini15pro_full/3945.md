agrochemical:/ˈæɡrəʊˈkɛmɪkəl/| n. |农药，农业化学品|A chemical product used in agriculture.  These products include fertilizers, pesticides, and soil conditioners.|Farmers use various agrochemicals to improve crop yields.|Synonyms: pesticide, fertilizer, herbicide, insecticide|Antonyms: organic fertilizer, natural pesticide|noun
