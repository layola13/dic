incidentally:/ˌɪnsɪˈdɛnt(ə)li/| adv.|顺便提一下，偶然地|Used to introduce a new topic, or some extra information, that is not strictly necessary to the main subject.|I found this book incidentally while looking for another one. 我在找另一本书时偶然发现了这本书。|Synonyms: by the way, by the bye, in passing, parenthetically|Antonyms: purposely, intentionally, deliberately|词性:副词

incidentally:/ˌɪnsɪˈdɛnt(ə)li/| adv.|附带地，伴随地|Happening as a natural result of something else.|The disease is incidentally fatal. 这种疾病会附带致命的后果。|Synonyms: consequently, accordingly, therefore|Antonyms: primarily, mainly, chiefly|词性:副词
