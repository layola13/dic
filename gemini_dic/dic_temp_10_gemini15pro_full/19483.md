biddies:/ˈbɪdiz/| n.|老妇人；小鸡（尤指母鸡）；女人（略带贬义）|A group of women, often older, sometimes used dismissively or condescendingly.  Originally referred to young chickens, especially hens.  Can also be used affectionately among close friends.|The biddies were gossiping by the fence. 这些老妇人正在篱笆边闲聊。|Synonyms: women, ladies, gossips (often derogatory), hens|Antonyms: men, gentlemen|noun
