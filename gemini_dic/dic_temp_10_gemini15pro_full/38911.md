confessingly:/kənˈfɛsɪŋli/
adverb
坦白地；承认地
in a way that involves confessing something
Confessingly, I had never heard of the band before.  坦白说，我以前从未听说过这个乐队。
He confessingly admitted his mistake. 他坦率地承认了自己的错误。
近义词:admittedly, frankly, openly
反义词:secretively, deceptively
词性:adverb
