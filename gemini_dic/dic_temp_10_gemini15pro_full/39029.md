conflates:/kənˈfleɪts/ | v. | 合并，混淆 | combines two or more things into one, often in a way that blurs the distinctions between them | The report conflates several distinct issues.  这份报告混淆了几个截然不同的问题。| Synonyms: merge, blend, fuse, combine | Antonyms: separate, distinguish, differentiate | verb

conflate:/kənˈfleɪt/ | v. | 合并，混淆 | combines two or more things into one, often in a way that blurs the distinctions between them | She conflated the two events in her memory.  她在记忆中把两件事混淆了。 | Synonyms:  amalgamate, integrate,  mix | Antonyms: divide, separate, analyze | verb

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin conflāre ("to blow together, melt together"), from con- ("together") + flāre ("to blow").
