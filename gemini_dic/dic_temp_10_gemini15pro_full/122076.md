misthreaded:/ˌmɪsˈθrɛdɪd/
adjective
错误地穿线的；穿线错误的
incorrectly threaded; having the thread passed through the wrong places
The sewing machine jammed because the bobbin was misthreaded. 缝纫机卡住了，因为梭芯穿线错误。
The needle was misthreaded, causing the stitches to be uneven. 针穿线错误，导致针脚不均匀。
misaligned, wrongly threaded
correctly threaded
adjective
