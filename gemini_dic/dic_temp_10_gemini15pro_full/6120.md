amethystine:/ˌæməˈθɪstaɪn/
adj.
紫水晶色的；紫色的
Of or like the colour of an amethyst.
The ring had an amethystine hue. 这枚戒指呈现紫水晶色。
近义词:violet, purple, lavender
反义词:colorless, white
词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *amethystīnus, from Latin amethystus (“amethyst”), from Ancient Greek ἀμέθυστος (améthustos, “not drunken”), from ἀ- (a-, “not”) + μεθύσκω (methúskō, “to intoxicate”).  The stone was believed to prevent intoxication.
