hoplites:/ˈhɒplaɪts/ (US) /ˈhɒplaɪts/ (UK)| n. |重装步兵|A heavily armed foot soldier of ancient Greece.|The hoplites formed a phalanx, a tight formation that was difficult to break.  希腊重装步兵组成了方阵，这是一种难以突破的紧密阵型。|例句：The hoplites were the backbone of the ancient Greek army.  重装步兵是古希腊军队的支柱。|近义词：soldier, infantryman|反义词:civilian|Noun
