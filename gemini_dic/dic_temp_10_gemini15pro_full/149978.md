palmer:/ˈpɑːmər/ (US), /ˈpɑːmə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 朝圣者；棕树；毛虫|A person, especially in the Middle Ages, who had returned from a pilgrimage to the Holy Land, often carrying a palm branch as a symbol of their journey. Also, a hairy caterpillar, or a device for measuring force.|例句：The palmer returned from Jerusalem, carrying a palm branch.|近义词：pilgrim, crusader (for the historical meaning), woolly bear (for the caterpillar meaning)|反义词: None (for the pilgrim meaning),  None (for the caterpillar meaning)|词性:noun
