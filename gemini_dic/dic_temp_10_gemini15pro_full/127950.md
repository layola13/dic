neoplasia:/ˌniːoʊˈpleɪʒə/ /ˌniːəˈpleɪʒə/| n. |肿瘤；赘生物|The formation of new, abnormal tissue, as a tumor or growth.  The process may be benign or malignant.|Doctors diagnosed a neoplasia in her lung. 医生诊断出她肺部有一个肿瘤。|Synonym: tumor, growth, lesion, malignancy (if cancerous)|Antonym: regression, remission|noun

neoplasm:/ˈniːoʊˌplæzəm/ /ˈniːəˌplæzəm/| n. |肿瘤；赘生物|A new and abnormal growth of tissue in some part of the body, especially as a characteristic of cancer.|The biopsy revealed the neoplasm to be benign. 活检显示肿瘤是良性的。|Synonym: tumor, growth, lesion, malignancy (if cancerous)|Antonym: healthy tissue|noun
