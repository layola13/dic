ETYMOLOGY->
From Old English *æpse (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *aspis, from Latin aspis, from Ancient Greek ἀσπίς (aspís, “asp, shield”).  The sense of "shield" is only present in the Greek word, which was borrowed into Proto-Germanic with its meaning of "asp".
