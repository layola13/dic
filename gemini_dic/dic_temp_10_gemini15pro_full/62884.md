enlay:/ɪnˈleɪ/ /ɛnˈleɪ/| n. vt.|镶嵌；镶嵌物；镶嵌细工|An inlay is a decorative design made by setting pieces of one material into the surface of another.|The cabinet was inlaid with mother-of-pearl.  这个橱柜镶嵌着珍珠母。|近义词：insert, embed, set|反义词:remove, extract|n.
enlay:/ɪnˈleɪ/ /ɛnˈleɪ/| vt.|镶嵌；嵌入|To enlay means to decorate something by setting pieces of one material into its surface.|He inlaid the wooden box with silver wire. 他用银丝镶嵌了木盒。|近义词：insert, embed, set|反义词:remove, extract|v.
