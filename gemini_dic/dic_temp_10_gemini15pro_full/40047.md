consummative:/kənˈsʌmətɪv/
adjective
完成的；最终的；完善的
of or relating to the final stage of something, especially the consumption or use of resources.
例句：The consummative act of the merger was the signing of the final agreement.  合并的最终行动是签署最终协议。

例句：The artist considered the exhibition the consummative moment of his career.  艺术家认为这次展览是他职业生涯的巅峰时刻。
近义词：final, ultimate, conclusive, definitive
反义词：initial, beginning, preliminary
词性: adjective
