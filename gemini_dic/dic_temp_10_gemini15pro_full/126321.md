myope:/ˈmaɪoʊp/ (US), /ˈmaɪɒp/ (UK)| n.|近视患者；目光短浅的人|A person who is nearsighted; a person who lacks foresight or intellectual insight.|例句：He's such a myope, he can't see the bigger picture. 他真是个目光短浅的人，看不到大局。

例句：The myope squinted at the distant sign.  这个近视眼眯着眼睛看远处的标志牌。|近义词：nearsighted person, shortsighted person|反义词：hypermetrope, farsighted person|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *myops, from Ancient Greek μύωψ (múōps, “short-sighted person”), from μύειν (múein, “to close, shut”) + ὤψ (ṓps, “eye, face”).  The eyes of nearsighted people often appear closed or squinting.
