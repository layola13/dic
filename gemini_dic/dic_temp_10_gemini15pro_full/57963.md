drippingly:/ˈdrɪpɪŋli/| adv.|滴落地，湿淋淋地|In a manner characterized by dripping; excessively or sentimentally.  Characterized by or resembling dripping.|The rain was drippingly falling from the eaves. 雨水湿淋淋地从屋檐滴落下来。|近义词：oozingly, sobbingly|反义词:dryly|词性:副词
词源: From drip + -ing + -ly.  Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *drupaną (“to drip”).
