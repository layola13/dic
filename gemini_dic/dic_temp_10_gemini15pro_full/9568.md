antiskidding:/ˌæntiˈskɪdɪŋ/
adjective
防滑的
Designed to prevent skidding.
例句：
The car has antiskidding brakes.
这辆车有防滑刹车。

The antiskidding surface of the tires provides good traction.
轮胎的防滑表面提供了良好的抓地力。

近义词：
non-skid, anti-slip, traction, grip
反义词：
slippery, slick

词性：
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
anti- (prefix): against, opposing. From Greek anti-.
skidding (verb/noun): sliding or slipping, especially on a wet or icy surface.  From Old Norse skidh, meaning "piece of wood."
