bordel: /bɔʁˈdɛl/ (French pronunciation)| n. |妓院; 乱糟糟的地方|A brothel; a place of disorder or chaos.|例句：The police raided the bordel and arrested several people. 警察突袭了妓院，逮捕了几个人。

例句：His apartment was a complete bordel; clothes were strewn everywhere. 他的公寓乱七八糟，衣服到处都是。|近义词：brothel, whorehouse, mess, shambles|反义词:order, tidiness|词性: noun

词源: From Old French *bordel, from Vulgar Latin *bordellum, diminutive of borda "hut, shed."  Originally referring to a small house, it later came to mean a brothel.
