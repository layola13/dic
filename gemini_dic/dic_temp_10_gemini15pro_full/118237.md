mercenary:/ˈmɜːrsənəri/ /ˈmɝːsənɛri/| n. adj.|雇佣兵，唯利是图的|A soldier who is paid to fight for a foreign country.  Motivated solely by financial gain; greedy.|例句：He was a mercenary, caring nothing for the cause for which he fought. 他是个雇佣兵，对他参战的目的漠不关心。|近义词：soldier of fortune, hired gun|反义词：volunteer, patriot|词性: 名词, 形容词

mercenary:/ˈmɜːrsənəri/ /ˈmɝːsənɛri/|adj.|唯利是图的|Primarily concerned with making money at the expense of ethics.|例句：Her mercenary motives were obvious to everyone. 她唯利是图的动机对每个人来说都很明显。|近义词：greedy, avaricious, grasping, venal|反义词：altruistic, charitable, philanthropic|词性: 形容词
