kels: /kɛlz/
noun|海带；巨藻；海藻|Large brown seaweeds (especially kelps) that live in cold coastal waters|The coast is rich in kels. 海岸线盛产海带。|近义词:seaweed, algae|反义词: (无明确的反义词，可以考虑陆生植物 terrestrial plant)|词性:n.

Etymology:
From Middle English *kelp, from uncertain origin, probably of Scandinavian origin; compare Norwegian dialectal kelp (“rockweed”).
