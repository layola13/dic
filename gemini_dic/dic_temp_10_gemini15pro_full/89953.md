horsepower:/ˈhɔːrsˌpaʊər/ (US), /ˈhɔːsˌpaʊə(r)/ (UK)| n. |马力|A unit of power equal to 550 foot-pounds per second (745.7 watts).|The engine has 200 horsepower. 这台发动机有200马力。|Synonyms: power, capacity, strength|Antonyms: weakness, impotence|noun
