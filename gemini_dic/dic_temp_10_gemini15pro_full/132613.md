nondirigible:/ˌnɒndɪˈrɪdʒəbəl/ /ˌnɑːndɪˈrɪdʒəbəl/
adjective
不可操纵的；（尤指飞艇）非硬式结构的
not able to be steered or controlled, especially of an airship
The nondirigible balloon drifted aimlessly across the sky.  这个不可操纵的气球漫无目的地飘过天空。
Synonyms: uncontrollable, unsteerable
Antonyms: dirigible, controllable
词性: adjective

nondirigible:/ˌnɒndɪˈrɪdʒəbəl/ /ˌnɑːndɪˈrɪdʒəbəl/
noun
非硬式飞艇
a non-rigid airship
The nondirigible was at the mercy of the wind.  这艘非硬式飞艇只能任由风吹。
Synonyms: blimp
Antonyms: dirigible
词性: noun
