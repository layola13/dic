coffered:/ˈkɒfəd/ (UK) /ˈkɔːfərd/ (US)| adj.| 镶嵌的，格状的，方格状的| Having a series of sunken panels in a ceiling, vault, or dome.  Decorated with recessed panels, usually square or rectangular. | The ceiling of the cathedral was beautifully coffered.  大教堂的天花板是美丽的方格镶嵌式样。| The coffered chocolate box contained a variety of delicious treats.  方格状的巧克力盒子里装着各种各样的美味佳肴。| 近义词: paneled, recessed, indented | 反义词: smooth, flat |词性:形容词
