despicable:/ˈdɛspɪkəbəl/| adj.|卑鄙的；可鄙的；令人厌恶的|Deserving hatred and contempt.|His behavior was despicable.|近义词：contemptible, vile, base, loathsome, abhorrent|反义词：admirable, commendable, praiseworthy|词性:形容词
词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin *despicabilis, from Latin despicari ‘look down on’, from de- ‘down’ + specere ‘to look’.
