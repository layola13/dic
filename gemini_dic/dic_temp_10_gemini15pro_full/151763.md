pashtos:/ˈpɑːʃtoʊ/, /ˈpæʃtoʊ/| n. |普什图语|Pashto, also known as Pakhto or Pashtu, is an Eastern Iranian language of the Indo-Iranian branch of the Indo-European language family. It is spoken as a native language by the Pashtun people, primarily in Afghanistan and Pakistan.|Many Pashto speakers are bilingual and also speak Dari or Urdu.|Synonym: Pakhto, Pashtu|Antonym: N/A|noun
