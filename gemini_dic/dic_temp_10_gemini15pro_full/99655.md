interlend:/ˌɪntərˈlend/| v.|相互借出;馆际互借|To lend and borrow materials between libraries or other institutions.|The two libraries interlend books and other resources. 这两家图书馆相互借阅书籍和其他资源。|近义词：share, exchange, loan|反义词: hoard, keep|动词

interlend:/ˌɪntərˈlend/| n.|相互借出;馆际互借|The practice of lending and borrowing materials between libraries or other institutions.|Interlend is a crucial service for academic research. 馆际互借是学术研究的一项重要服务。|近义词：resource sharing, interlibrary loan|反义词: N/A|名词
