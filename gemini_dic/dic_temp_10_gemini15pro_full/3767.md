agitating:/ˈædʒɪˌteɪtɪŋ/
adjective
使人不安的，焦虑的
Causing emotional disturbance; exciting.
The agitating news of the impending war caused widespread panic.  令人不安的战争迫近的消息引起了广泛的恐慌。
synonyms:  disturbing, unsettling, upsetting, distressing, troubling, worrisome, nerve-racking, anxiety-provoking,  disquieting
antonyms: calming, soothing, relaxing, peaceful, tranquil


agitating:/ˈædʒɪˌteɪtɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
煽动，激怒，搅拌
Stirring up; disturbing; exciting.
He was agitating the crowd with his fiery rhetoric. 他用他激烈的言辞煽动人群。
synonyms: stirring, whipping up, fomenting, inciting, provoking, instigating
antonyms: calming, soothing, pacifying, appeasing, allaying

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *agitatus, past participle of agitāre (“to agitate, drive, discuss”), frequentative of agere (“to do, act, drive”).
