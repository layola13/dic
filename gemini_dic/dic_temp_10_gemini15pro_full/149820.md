paleolith:/ˈpeɪlɪəlɪθ/ | n. | 旧石器时代; 旧石器时代的遗迹 |  The Paleolithic period, also known as the Old Stone Age, marked a significant stage in human development, characterized by the use of rudimentary chipped stone tools.  |  The museum's collection includes numerous paleoliths from various sites across Africa.  博物馆的藏品包括来自非洲各地遗址的许多旧石器。 | synonyms: Old Stone Age | antonyms: Neolithic | noun


paleolithic:/ˌpeɪlɪəˈlɪθɪk/ | adj. | 旧石器时代的 | Paleolithic humans were hunter-gatherers. |  Paleolithic art provides valuable insights into the minds of our ancestors. 旧石器时代的艺术为了解我们祖先的思想提供了宝贵的见解。 | synonyms: Old Stone Age | antonyms: Neolithic | adjective
