dehydrator:/diːˈhaɪdreɪtər/ | n. | 脱水器，干燥器 | A machine that removes water from food to preserve it.  It works by circulating warm air around the food, causing the water to evaporate. | I use a food dehydrator to make jerky. 我用食物脱水机做肉干。| Synonyms: dryer, desiccator | Antonyms: humidifier | noun


dehydrate:/diːˈhaɪdreɪt/ | v. | 脱水，使干燥 | To remove water from something. | The sun can dehydrate your body quickly.  太阳会很快使你的身体脱水。| Synonyms: dry, desiccate, parch | Antonyms: hydrate, moisten | verb
