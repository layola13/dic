PHRASE->
be overshadowed by: 被…所掩盖  Her accomplishments were overshadowed by her brother's fame. 她的成就被她哥哥的名气所掩盖。
overshadow something:  使…失色  The bad news overshadowed the joy of the occasion. 坏消息使这个场合的喜悦蒙上阴影。
overshadow someone: 使…相形见绌  His talent overshadowed all the other performers. 他的才华使所有其他表演者相形见绌。
completely overshadowed: 完全被…掩盖  The scandal completely overshadowed the positive news. 丑闻完全掩盖了正面新闻。
be largely overshadowed by:  很大程度上被…掩盖 The success of the movie was largely overshadowed by the controversy surrounding it. 这部电影的成功在很大程度上被围绕它的争议所掩盖。
