cross-my-heart:/ˌkrɔːs maɪ ˈhɑːrt/| idiom | 我发誓; 保证; 千真万确 | To swear that something is true, often while making a gesture of drawing an X over one's heart. | Example: I didn't break the vase, cross my heart! 我没有打碎花瓶，我发誓！ | Example:  Cross my heart, I'll be there on time. 我保证我会准时到那里。 | 近义词: I swear, I promise, truly, honestly | 反义词: I lie | 词性: idiom
