fonts:/fɑːnts/ (US) /fɒnts/ (UK)| n.|字体|A set of type of one particular face and size.  The typeface used for printed text.|Example: The website uses a variety of fonts to make it visually appealing.  该网站使用各种字体以使其更具视觉吸引力。|Example:  I prefer serif fonts for body text. 我更喜欢衬线字体作为正文。|Synonyms: typefaces, lettering, characters|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
