demographer:/dɪˈmɒɡrəfər/ (US) /dɪˈmɒɡrəfə(r)/ (UK)| n. |人口统计学家；人口学家|A specialist who studies the characteristics of human populations, such as size, growth, density, distribution, and vital statistics.|Demographers predict a population explosion in the next decade. 人口统计学家预测未来十年人口将爆炸式增长。|Synonyms: population scientist, population analyst|Antonyms: N/A|noun

demographist:/dɪˈmɒɡrəfɪst/ (US) /dɪˈmɒɡrəfɪst/ (UK)| n. |人口统计学家；人口学家|A specialist who studies the characteristics of human populations, such as size, growth, density, distribution, and vital statistics.
