binomial:/baɪˈnəʊmiəl/| adj. n.|二项式的，二项式|A mathematical expression consisting of two terms connected by a plus or minus sign.  An expression that is a sum or difference of two terms.|The binomial theorem is a fundamental concept in algebra. 二项式定理是代数中的一个基本概念。| The binomial (x + 2) can be expanded.  二项式(x + 2)可以展开。|synonyms: two-term, |antonyms: monomial, polynomial |adjective, noun
