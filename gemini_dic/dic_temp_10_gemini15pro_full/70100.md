feverishnesses:/ˈfiːvərɪʃnɪsɪz/
n.
发热；狂热；兴奋
The state of being feverish; a tendency to be feverish.  A display of excessive and often restless excitement, activity, or emotion.
She displayed a feverishnesses in her preparations for the trip. 她为旅行做准备表现出一种狂热。
The feverishnesses of the stock market worried investors.  股市的狂热令投资者担忧。
Synonym: fevers, excitement, agitation, restlessness, frenzy
Antonym: calmness, composure, tranquility, serenity
词性: 名词
