luge:/luːʒ/
verb
滑冰橇,乘坐滑冰橇滑行
to ride on a luge.
They luged down the snowy hill. 他们坐着滑冰橇滑下山坡。
近义词:sled, toboggan
反义词:None
词性:v.

luge:/luːʒ/
noun
滑冰橇
a small sled for one or two people, used for sliding down an ice track.
The luge went flying down the track. 滑冰橇沿着轨道飞驰而下。
近义词:sled, toboggan
反义词:None
词性:n.
