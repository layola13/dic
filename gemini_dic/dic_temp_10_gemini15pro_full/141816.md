orbiter:/ˈɔːrbɪtər/ (US) /ˈɔːbɪtə/ (UK)| n.|轨道飞行器；人造卫星；航天飞机|A spacecraft designed to orbit a celestial body, such as a planet or moon, without landing on it.  A person or thing that orbits something else.|例句：The orbiter sent back stunning images of Mars.  轨道飞行器发回了令人惊叹的火星图像。

例句：He was a lonely orbiter in the social scene, never quite fitting in. 他在社交圈里像一个孤独的卫星，从未真正融入其中。|近义词：satellite, spacecraft|反义词: lander|词性: noun
