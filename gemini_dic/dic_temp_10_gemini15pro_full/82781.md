guiltinesses:/ˈɡɪltinəsɪz/
noun
内疚，罪恶感
The state of being guilty; culpability.  A feeling of remorse or regret for having committed a wrong.
He was overwhelmed by guiltinesses after confessing his lie.  他坦白谎言后，被罪恶感淹没。
