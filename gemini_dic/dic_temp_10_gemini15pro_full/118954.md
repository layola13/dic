meteorologists:/ˌmiːtiəˈrɒlədʒɪsts/ /ˌmiːtiəˈrɑːlədʒɪsts/| n. |气象学家|A scientist who studies meteorology.  A person who studies the atmosphere and its phenomena, including weather and climate.|
The meteorologists predicted heavy rain. 气象学家预测会有大雨。

The meteorologists are tracking the hurricane's path. 气象学家正在追踪飓风的路径。


|近义词：weather forecaster, climatologist|反义词:|词性:noun
