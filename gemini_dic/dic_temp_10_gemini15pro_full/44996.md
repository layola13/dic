crystallographer: /ˌkrɪstəˈlɒɡrəfər/ (UK) /ˌkrɪstəˈlɑːɡrəfər/ (US)| n. |晶体学家|A scientist who studies the structure and properties of crystals.|例句：The crystallographer analyzed the diffraction pattern to determine the crystal's structure. 晶体学家分析了衍射图样以确定晶体的结构。

例句：Crystallographers play a vital role in developing new materials with specific properties. 晶体学家在开发具有特定性质的新材料方面发挥着至关重要的作用。|近义词：crystal scientist|反义词:  (No direct antonym)|词性: Noun
