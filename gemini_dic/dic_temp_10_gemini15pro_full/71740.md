flaxier:/ˈflæksiər/ | adj. | 更像亚麻的；更柔软的 |  Comparative adjective of "flaxy".  It means more resembling flax or having more of the qualities of flax, such as being pale yellow, or more generally, being more flexible or supple. |  The new fabric is flaxier than the old one.  这种新布料比旧的更像亚麻。

The stems of this plant are flaxier and easier to bend.  这种植物的茎更柔软，更容易弯曲。 | 近义词:suppler, more flexible, more pliant | 反义词:stiffer, less flexible, less pliant | 词性:形容词

词源: From flax + -y + -er (comparative suffix).  Flax is from Old English *fleax, from Proto-Germanic *flahsaz.
