nutmeggy:/ˈnʌtˌmeɡi/| adj.|肉豆蔻味的；有肉豆蔻香味的|Having the flavor or fragrance of nutmeg.  This spice adds a warm, nutmeggy flavor to the dish.  这种香料为菜肴增添了温暖的肉豆蔻香味。|近义词:aromatic, spicy, fragrant|反义词:bland, tasteless|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From nutmeg + -y.  Nutmeg itself comes from Middle English notemuge, from Old French noix muguette ("musk nut"), from noix ("nut") + muguette ("musk").
