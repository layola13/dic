babyface:/ˈbeɪbiˌfeɪs/| n. |娃娃脸，童颜|A person with a youthful-looking face, often appearing younger than their actual age.  A face that looks like that of a baby.|She has such a babyface, it's hard to believe she's already 30. 她长着一张娃娃脸，很难相信她已经30岁了。|近义词：youthful appearance, cherubic face|反义词:wizened face, aged face|名词
