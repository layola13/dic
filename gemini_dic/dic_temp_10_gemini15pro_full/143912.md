outpreached:/ˌaʊtˈpriːtʃt/
verb
胜过布道；讲道超过
to surpass in preaching; to preach more effectively or convincingly than.
The young minister outpreached the older, more experienced one.  这位年轻的牧师在讲道方面胜过了那位年纪更大、经验更丰富的牧师。
surpass, excel, outdo
be outdone by, be surpassed by
v.
