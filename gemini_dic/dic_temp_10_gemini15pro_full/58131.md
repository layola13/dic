dropwise:/ˈdrɒpwaɪz/ (US) /ˈdrɒpwaɪz/ (UK)| adv. adj.|逐滴地，滴加地|Falling or delivered in drops; one drop at a time.|Add the solution dropwise to the mixture. 将溶液逐滴加入混合物中。|Synonyms: gradually, slowly, bit by bit|Antonyms: suddenly, all at once|adverb

dropwise:/ˈdrɒpwaɪz/ (US) /ˈdrɒpwaɪz/ (UK)| adj.|逐滴的|Delivered or occurring drop by drop.|The dropwise addition prevented a violent reaction. 逐滴添加防止了剧烈反应。|Synonyms: dripping, trickling|Antonyms: pouring, gushing|adjective

Etymology:  From "drop" + "-wise" (suffix meaning "in the manner of").  "Drop" comes from Old English *dropa, of Germanic origin.  "-wise" comes from Old English -wīs.
