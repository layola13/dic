fluidly:/ˈfluːɪdli/ | adv. | 流畅地，流利地 | smoothly and continuously, like a liquid | The dancer moved fluidly across the stage. 舞者在舞台上流畅地移动。|  The transition between scenes was fluidly executed. 场景之间的过渡非常流畅。 | 近义词：smoothly, gracefully, effortlessly | 反义词：jerkily, stiffly, awkwardly | 词性: 副词

词源:  From fluid + -ly.  "Fluid" comes from the Latin "fluidus," meaning "flowing."
