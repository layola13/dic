fibrillated:/ˈfɪbrɪleɪtɪd/| adj.|颤动的，纤维颤动的|Characterized by or experiencing fibrillation, a rapid and irregular heartbeat or muscle contraction.|His heart fibrillated dangerously after the accident. 事故发生后，他的心脏危险地颤动着。|近义词：quivering, trembling, twitching|反义词:steady, stable|词性:形容词
fibrillate:/ˈfɪbrɪleɪt/| v. (intr., tr.)|（使）颤动，（使）纤维颤动|To have or cause to have fibrillation, a rapid and irregular heartbeat or muscle contraction.|The patient's muscles began to fibrillate. 病人的肌肉开始颤动。|The doctor used a defibrillator to fibrillate the heart. 医生用除颤器使心脏颤动（恢复正常心跳）。|近义词：quiver, tremble, twitch|反义词:stabilize|词性:动词
fibrillation:/ˌfɪbrɪˈleɪʃn/| n.|颤动，纤维性颤动|A rapid and irregular heartbeat or muscle contraction.|Atrial fibrillation is a common heart rhythm disorder. 心房颤动是一种常见的心律失调。|近义词：tremor, quivering|反义词:stability|词性:名词
