immeasurably:/ɪˈmɛʒərəbli/
adverb
不可估量地，无限地
To a degree that is impossible to measure; extremely.
His contribution to the project was immeasurably valuable. 他对这个项目的贡献价值不可估量。
近义词: vastly, immensely, infinitely, boundlessly
反义词: measurably, slightly
词性: 副词
