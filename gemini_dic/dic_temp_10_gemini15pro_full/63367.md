entomologize:/ˌɛntəˈmɑləˌdʒaɪz/ | v. |研究昆虫；采集昆虫|To study or collect insects. |例句：He spent his summer entomologizing in the rainforest. 他夏天在雨林里研究昆虫。

例句：She enjoyed entomologizing as a hobby. 她把研究昆虫作为一种爱好。|近义词：collect insects, study insects |反义词: N/A |词性: verb

Etymology: From entomology + -ize. Entomology comes from the Greek "entomon" (insect) and "logia" (study).
