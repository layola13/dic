drowners:/ˈdraʊnərz/
n.
淹死的人；溺死鬼；水鬼
Creatures that lure people to watery graves.
The drowners dragged the unsuspecting traveler into the murky depths.  水鬼把毫无戒心的旅行者拖进了黑暗的深处。
The villagers feared the lake, believing it was infested with drowners. 村民们害怕这个湖，认为里面到处都是水鬼。
synonyms: water demons, water spirits, kelpies, nixies
antonyms: rescuers, lifeguards
