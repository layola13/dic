lesseeships:/ˌlesiːʃɪps/
n.
租赁权; 租约
The legal right held by a lessee to use or occupy a property under the terms of a lease.
The company holds several lesseeships on properties in the city center.  该公司在市中心拥有几处房产的租赁权。
Synonyms: leasehold, tenancy
Antonyms: ownership, freehold
noun
