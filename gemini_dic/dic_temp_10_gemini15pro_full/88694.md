hobbism:/ˈhɒbɪɪzəm/ (US) /ˈhɒbizəm/ (UK)| n. |嗜好，业余爱好|An activity or interest pursued for pleasure or relaxation, rather than for financial reward; the practice of pursuing such activities.|例句：His hobbism includes collecting stamps and playing the guitar. 他的爱好包括集邮和弹吉他。

例句：Photography is more than just a hobbism for her; it's a passion. 摄影对她来说不仅仅是一种爱好，而是一种激情。|近义词：hobby, pastime, avocation, pursuit|反义词：profession, vocation, occupation, job|词性: noun

词源: From hobby + -ism.  "Hobby" itself has uncertain origins, possibly related to "hobbyhorse."
