IMF:/ˌaɪ ɛm ˈɛf/ | n. | 国际货币基金组织 (International Monetary Fund) | An international organization headquartered in Washington, D.C., consisting of 190 countries working to foster global monetary cooperation, secure financial stability, facilitate international trade, promote high employment and sustainable economic growth, and reduce poverty around the world. |  The IMF provided financial assistance to the country during its economic crisis. 国际货币基金组织在该国经济危机期间提供了财政援助。

The IMF's lending policies are often criticized for their impact on developing countries. 国际货币基金组织的贷款政策因其对发展中国家的影响而经常受到批评。

| 近义词: International monetary institution, global financial institution | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, but contrasting concepts could include organizations promoting financial deregulation or individual national banks) | 词性: noun
