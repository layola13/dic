bosky: /ˈbɒski/ (UK) /ˈbɔːski/ (US)
adjective
树木繁茂的；多树的；荫蔽的
covered with bushes and trees; wooded.
The children played in the bosky glade. 孩子们在树木繁茂的林间空地玩耍。
The path led through a bosky dell. 小路穿过一个树木繁茂的山谷。

近义词: wooded, forested, sylvan, leafy, verdant
反义词: barren, treeless, bare

词性: adjective

词源:  From Middle English *bosky, from Old French bosqué (“woody, leafy”), past participle of bosquer (“to cover with trees, plant trees”), from Old French bosc, from Late Latin boscus (“wood, forest”), of uncertain origin.
