loincloths:/ˈlɔɪnˌklɔːðz/ /ˈlɔɪnˌklɑːðz/
n.
缠腰布，遮羞布
A piece of cloth worn around the loins, typically by men in some cultures.
The tribesmen wore loincloths made of animal skins.  部落男子穿着兽皮制成的缠腰布。
loincloth,  skirt, kilt
trousers, pants
noun
