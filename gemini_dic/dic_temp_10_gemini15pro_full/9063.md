antimeter:/ˌæntiˈmiːtər/ | n. | 反计; 反量程表;  | An instrument that measures the opposite of what is intended or expected.  It's a more theoretical or literary term, often used to describe something that measures the reverse of a normal metric. | The antimeter showed a decrease in happiness as wealth increased.  财富增加，幸福反减，反计显示如此。| Near synonyms: counter, reverse indicator | Antonyms: meter, gauge | noun
