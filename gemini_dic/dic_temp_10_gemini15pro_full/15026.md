baboo:/bəˈbuː/
n.|奉承者，拍马屁的人|A person who uses flattery and servile behaviour, especially toward a superior, to gain favour; a sycophant.|He acted like a baboo to the visiting dignitaries, hoping to gain their favor. 他对来访的要员阿谀奉承，希望得到他们的青睐。|近义词:sycophant, flatterer, toady, yes-man|反义词:critic, detractor|词性:名词

baboo:/bəˈbuː/
n.|（旧时，尤指印度的）先生，官员，职员|(dated, chiefly India) A Hindu clerk or official.|The baboo was responsible for managing the office's paperwork.  这位先生负责管理办公室的文件。|近义词:clerk, official, officer|反义词:N/A|词性:名词
