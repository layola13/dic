bids:/bɪdz/| n. v.| 出价，投标；命令，吩咐 |Offers made in a competitive situation, such as an auction or tender, to buy something at a stated price. Can also mean to command or urge.|  The company submitted several bids for the construction project.  这家公司提交了几个建设项目的投标。| 近义词: offers, tenders, proposals | 反义词: asks, demands (as in asking price) |词性: Noun (plural), Verb (third-person singular present)
