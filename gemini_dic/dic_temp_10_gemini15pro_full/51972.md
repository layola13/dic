diactinic:/ˌdaɪækˈtɪnɪk/
adjective
透光性的; 能透过化学射线的
Allowing the passage of actinic rays, especially those of short wavelengths, such as ultraviolet rays.  Permeable to actinic rays of light; transmitting chemically active rays.
Diactinic glass is used in photography because it allows ultraviolet light to pass through.  透光玻璃用于摄影，因为它允许紫外线通过。
Synonyms: transmissive, transparent, permeable
Antonyms: opaque, non-transmissive, impermeable
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *diaktinos* "passing through rays," from *dia-* "through" + *aktis* "ray."
