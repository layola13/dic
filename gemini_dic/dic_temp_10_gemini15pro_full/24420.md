brisking:/ˈbrɪskɪŋ/| v.|使活跃，使 brisk，变得 brisk|The cold wind was brisking the walkers.|例句：The cool air was brisking up the walkers. 凉爽的空气使步行者们精神抖擞。

例句：The market is brisking up after the holiday. 假期过后市场活跃起来了。|近义词：enlivening, invigorating, stimulating|反义词：dulling, weakening|词性:动词

词源: From brisk + -ing.  Ultimately from the French word *brusque* meaning "lively, quick".
