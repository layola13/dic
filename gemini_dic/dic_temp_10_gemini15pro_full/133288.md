nonexpediently:/ˌnɒnɪkˈspiːdiəntli/
adverb
不明智地，不适宜地
Not in a way that is useful or convenient for achieving a particular purpose.
He acted nonexpediently by ignoring the advice of his lawyer. 他无视律师的建议，行事不明智。
Synonym:inadvisably, unwisely, imprudently, foolishly
Antonym: wisely, prudently, judiciously, expediently
词性: 副词
