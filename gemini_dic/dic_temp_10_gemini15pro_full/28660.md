carillon:/ˈkærɪlɒn/ (US) /kəˈrɪljən/ (UK)| n.|编钟（组），钟琴|A set of bells in a tower, played from a keyboard, or the music played on these bells.|The town square was filled with the beautiful music of the carillon.  广场上充满了美丽的钟琴音乐。|The carillon played a joyful melody. 钟琴演奏了一段欢快的旋律。|Synonyms: chimes, bells|Antonyms: silence|noun
