excising:/ɪkˈsaɪzɪŋ/
verb
切除；删去
the act of removing something, especially surgically
The surgeon is excising the tumor.  外科医生正在切除肿瘤。

excising:/ɪkˈsaɪzɪŋ/
adjective
切除的
relating to the act of removing something
The excising procedure was successful. 切除手术很成功。


近义词:removing, cutting out, deleting, extracting
反义词:inserting, adding, implanting
词性: verb, adjective (present participle of excise)
