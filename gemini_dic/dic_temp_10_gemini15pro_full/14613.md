avocado:/ˌævəˈkɑːdoʊ/ (US) /ˌævəˈkɑːdəʊ/ (UK)| adj. |牛油果色的，鳄梨绿的|Having a pale green color, like the flesh of an avocado.|She wore an avocado-colored dress. 她穿了一件牛油果绿的连衣裙。|Synonyms:  pale green, chartreuse |Antonyms:  (No direct antonym for a color) |adjective
