calorifically:/ˌkæləˈrɪfɪkli/ | adv. | 热量地；卡路里地 |  In a way that relates to the amount of heat or energy something produces. |  This food is calorifically dense, meaning it contains a lot of calories per serving.  这种食物热量密度很高，意味着每份食物含有大量的卡路里。| Synonyms: energetically, powerfully | Antonyms: weakly, feebly | Adverb
