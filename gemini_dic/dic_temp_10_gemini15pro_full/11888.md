arrogating: /ˈærəɡeɪtɪŋ/
verb
僭越；擅取；霸占
Taking or claiming something without justification.
The rebel group is arrogating power to itself.  叛乱组织正在僭取权力。
He arrogated to himself the right to decide what everyone should do. 他擅自决定每个人应该做什么。
近义词: usurping, seizing, appropriating, assuming
反义词: relinquishing, surrendering, abdicating
词性: verb (present participle)

Etymology:
From Late Latin *arrogatus, past participle of arrogare (“to claim for oneself”), from Latin ad (“to”) + rogare (“to ask”).
