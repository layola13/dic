heeder: /ˈhiːdər/
n.
注意者，倾听者，服从者
One who heeds; one who listens to and obeys.
The heeder of wise counsel will prosper. 注意聆听明智建议的人会成功。
listener, follower, obeyer
disregarder, ignorer
noun
