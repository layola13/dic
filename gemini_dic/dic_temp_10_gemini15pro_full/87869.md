hidey-hole:/ˈhaɪdi həʊl/ (US), /ˈhaɪdi həʊl/ (UK)| n. |藏身处，隐匿处|A place of concealment or refuge; a hiding place.|例句：The squirrel found a hidey-hole in the tree. 松鼠在树上找到了一个藏身处。

例句：The children built a hidey-hole in the backyard. 孩子们在后院建了一个秘密基地。

近义词：hiding place, hideout, retreat, sanctuary|反义词:exposure, open area|词性:名词
