hyoscine:/ˈhaɪəˌsiːn/ | n. |莨菪碱; 东莨菪碱|A drug made from a plant of the nightshade family. It is used to treat nausea, vomiting, and motion sickness.  It can also be used to dilate the pupils of the eyes.|She was given hyoscine to ease her travel sickness.|Synonyms: scopolamine|Antonyms: N/A|Noun
