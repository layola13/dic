high-fidelity:/ˌhaɪ fɪˈdeləti/ | adj. | 高保真的 | Reproducing sound with minimal distortion, thus matching the original sound as closely as possible.  |  This new stereo system boasts high-fidelity sound reproduction.  这个新的立体声系统拥有高保真音质。 | synonyms: high-quality, accurate, precise | antonyms: low-fidelity, distorted, inaccurate | adjective
high-fidelity:/ˌhaɪ fɪˈdeləti/ | n. | 高保真度 | The quality of being high-fidelity. | The high-fidelity of the recording was impressive. 这录音的高保真度令人印象深刻。| synonyms: accuracy, precision | antonyms: distortion, inaccuracy | noun
