chlorite: /ˈklɔːraɪt/ (US), /ˈklɔːrʌɪt/ (UK) | n. |绿泥石; 亚氯酸盐 |A dark green mineral of the mica group, or a salt of chlorous acid. | Chlorite is a common mineral found in metamorphic rocks. 绿泥石是一种常见的变质岩矿物。| The chlorite in the soil gives it a greenish tinge. 土壤中的绿泥石使其略带绿色。| Synonyms:  mica, vermiculite (for the mineral); chlorous salt (for the salt) | Antonyms: N/A | Noun

chlorite: /ˈklɔːraɪt/ (US), /ˈklɔːrʌɪt/ (UK) | adj. |绿泥石的; 亚氯酸盐的 | Containing or relating to chlorite. | Chlorite schists are common in mountainous regions. 绿泥石片岩在山区很常见。 |  Chlorite ions can act as oxidizing agents. 亚氯酸根离子可以作为氧化剂。| Synonyms: chloritic | Antonyms: N/A | Adjective
