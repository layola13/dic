decontrolling:/ˌdiːkənˈtrəʊlɪŋ/
verb
解除管制
The act of removing government controls from something, typically prices or wages.
The government is decontrolling the price of gasoline. 政府正在解除汽油价格管制。
deregulating, liberalizing
controlling, regulating
