nonaseptic:/ˌnɒn.eɪˈsɛptɪk/| adj.|非无菌的，不防腐的|Not aseptic; not sterile or free from microorganisms that cause disease or decay.|The wound was treated with a nonaseptic solution, leading to an infection.|近义词：contaminated, unsterile, septic|反义词: aseptic, sterile|词性:形容词
Etymology:  "non-" (Latin prefix meaning "not") + "aseptic" (from Greek "a-" (without) + "sēptikos" (putrefying)).
