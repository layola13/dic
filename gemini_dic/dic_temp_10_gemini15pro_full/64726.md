ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *escheuen*, from Old French *eschiver* "to shun, avoid," from Frankish *skiuhan* "to shy away from, avoid," from Proto-Germanic *skeukhaną* "to shy away from, fear," from Proto-Indo-European *skeu-* "to cover, conceal."  The word is related to "shy."
