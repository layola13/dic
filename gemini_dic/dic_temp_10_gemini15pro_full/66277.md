excommunicable:/ˌɛks.kəˈmjuː.nɪ.kə.bəl/
adjective
可逐出教会的;可被开除教籍的
Suitable to be excommunicated; deserving of excommunication.
The heretic was declared excommunicable for his unorthodox views.  这个异教徒因其非正统的观点被宣布可以被逐出教会。
近义词: ostracizable, banishable
反义词: acceptable, admissible
词性: adjective

excommunicate:/ˌɛks.kəˈmjuː.nɪ.keɪt/
verb
逐出教会
Officially exclude (someone) from participation in the sacraments and services of the Christian Church.
The bishop excommunicated the monk for his rebellious behavior. 主教因僧侣的叛逆行为将其逐出教会。
近义词: ostracize, banish
反义词: admit, accept
词性: verb

excommunication:/ˌɛks.kəˌmjuː.nɪˈkeɪ.ʃən/
noun
逐出教会;开除教籍
The action of officially excluding someone from participation in the sacraments and services of the Christian Church.
The excommunication of the king was a significant political event. 国王的被逐出教会是一项重要的政治事件。
近义词: ostracism, banishment
反义词: admission, acceptance
词性: noun


词源:
Late Middle English: from late Latin excommunicabilis, from excommunicatus (past participle of excommunicare ‘excommunicate’) + -abilis.
