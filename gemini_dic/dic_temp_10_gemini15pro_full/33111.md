例句：The spread of Christianity throughout the Roman Empire was a gradual process.  基督教在罗马帝国的传播是一个渐进的过程。|近义词：Christian faith, Christian religion|反义词: atheism, nonbelief |词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *Christianitas*, from Latin Christianus (see Christian) + -itas.
