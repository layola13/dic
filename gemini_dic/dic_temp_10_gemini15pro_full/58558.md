dukee: /ˈduːkiː/
n.
duke (especially in children's stories)
公爵（尤用于儿童故事中）
The dukee lived in a grand castle. 公爵住在一座宏伟的城堡里。
近义词: duke, nobleman, lord
反义词: commoner, peasant
词性: noun

词源:
From duke, with the -ee suffix often used in children's language to denote smallness or endearment.  源自duke，带有-ee后缀，常用于儿童语言中，表示小或亲昵。
