concretion:/kɒnˈkriːʃn/ /kɑːnˈkriːʃn/
n.
凝结物，结石
a hard solid mass formed by the local accumulation of matter, especially within the body or within a mass of loose material such as rock or soil.
Kidney stones are a common type of concretion. 肾结石是一种常见的凝结物。
mass, lump, deposit, accumulation, formation
dispersion, dissolution
noun
