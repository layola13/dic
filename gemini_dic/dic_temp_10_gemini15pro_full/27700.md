candor:/ˈkændər/| n.|坦率，直率，诚恳|The quality of being open and honest in expression; frankness.|She spoke with surprising candor about her past mistakes. 她令人惊讶地坦率地谈论了她过去的错误。|Synonyms: frankness, honesty, openness, sincerity, straightforwardness|Antonyms: dishonesty, deception, evasiveness, insincerity|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin candor "whiteness, brilliance, purity," from candēre "to shine, glow." The sense evolution likely progressed from "purity" to "sincerity" and then to "frankness."
