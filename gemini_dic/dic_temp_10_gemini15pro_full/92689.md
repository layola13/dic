icebound:/ˈaɪsbaʊnd/| adj.|冰封的，被冰困住的|Covered and obstructed by ice, unable to move.|The ship was icebound in the Arctic Ocean. 这艘船被困在北冰洋的冰层里。|近义词：frozen, ice-locked, glacier-bound|反义词:thawed, free|词性:形容词
Etymology: From 'ice' + 'bound' (past participle of 'bind'), meaning confined or restrained by ice.  'Ice' comes from Old English 'īs',  and 'bind' comes from Old English 'bindan'.
