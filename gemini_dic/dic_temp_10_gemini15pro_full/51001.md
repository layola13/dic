desistance:/dɪˈzɪstəns/
n.
停止，中止
the act of desisting; cessation
The government called for a desistance of hostilities. 政府呼吁停止敌对行动。

近义词: cessation, discontinuation, termination
反义词: continuance, persistence, perseverance
词性: 名词


词源: from Old French desistance, from desister "to desist," from des- "away" + sister "to stand," from Latin sistere "to cause to stand," frequentative of stare "to stand"
