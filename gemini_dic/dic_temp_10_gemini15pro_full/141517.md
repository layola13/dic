Example: He was opossuming when his boss asked about the missing files. 当老板问起丢失的文件时，他装傻充愣。| 近义词: playing dead, feigning death, faking sleep, playing possum, malingering, pretending ignorance | 反义词: confronting, engaging, acknowledging | 词性: 动词
