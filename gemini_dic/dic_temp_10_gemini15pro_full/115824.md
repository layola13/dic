marketable:/ˈmɑːrkɪtəbl/ /ˈmɑːrketəbl/| adj.|适销对路的，有销路的|able to be sold because people want to buy it|例句：This new phone is very marketable due to its innovative features.  这款新手机由于其创新功能非常畅销。

例句：He developed a marketable skill through years of training. 他通过多年的训练掌握了一项适销对路的技能。|近义词：salable, merchantable, commercial, marketable|反义词：unsalable, unmarketable|词性:adjective
Etymology: From market + -able.  Ultimately from Latin *mercatus* "trade, market."
