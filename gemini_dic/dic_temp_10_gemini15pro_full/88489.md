historicists:/hɪˈstɔːrɪsɪsts/ | n. | 历史主义者 | A person who believes that history is governed by inexorable laws and follows a predictable course; an adherent of historicism. |  Historians debated whether the historicists' interpretation of the past was accurate. 历史学家们争论历史主义者对过去的解释是否准确。| synonyms: determinists, historicism proponents | antonyms: presentists, ahistoricists | noun

historicist:/hɪˈstɔːrɪsɪst/ | adj. | 历史主义的 | Of or relating to historicism. |  A historicist approach to understanding culture emphasizes its context within a specific time period.  理解文化的历史主义方法强调其在特定时间段内的背景。 | synonyms: historical, contextual | antonyms: ahistorical, universal | adjective
