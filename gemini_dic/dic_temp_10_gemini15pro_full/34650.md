clepsine:/ˈklɛpsaɪn/| n. |医蛭，水蛭|A freshwater leech, specifically a member of the genus *Glossiphonia*. These leeches are often found in ponds and streams, and they feed on the blood of invertebrates like snails and worms. |Clepsine are common in freshwater habitats. 医蛭在淡水栖息地很常见。|The clepsine attached itself to the snail. 这只医蛭吸附在了蜗牛身上。|近义词：leech, bloodsucker|反义词: (No direct antonym, potentially predator of leeches like fish or turtles) |noun
