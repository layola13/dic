legating:/ˈlɛɡeɪtɪŋ/
v.
委托；遗赠；授权
The act of entrusting something to someone or assigning a task or responsibility.  It also refers to bequeathing something in a will.
He is legating his vast fortune to his children. 他正在把他的巨额财产遗赠给他的孩子们。
The king is legating the task of forming a new government to his most trusted advisor. 国王将组建新政府的任务委托给他最信任的顾问。
近义词：entrusting, assigning, bequeathing, delegating
反义词：retaining, withholding
词性：动词 (verb)
