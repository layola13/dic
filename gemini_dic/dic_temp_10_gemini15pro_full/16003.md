bamming:/ˈbæmɪŋ/
n.|猛烈的撞击，欺骗|A forceful impact or the act of cheating or defrauding someone.|The bamming of the waves against the rocks was deafening. 海浪猛烈地撞击着岩石，震耳欲聋。  They were accused of bamming investors out of their savings. 他们被指控欺骗投资者，骗取他们的积蓄。|近义词：slamming, bashing, defrauding, scamming|反义词:gentle touch, honesty|词性:名词

bamming:/ˈbæmɪŋ/
v.|猛烈撞击，欺骗|To hit or strike with great force, or to cheat or defraud someone.|The car bammed into the wall. 汽车猛烈地撞到了墙上。 He bammed me out of $50. 他骗了我50美元。|近义词：slamming, bashing, defrauding, scamming|反义词:caressing, being honest|词性:动词


词源:  Likely a variant of "banging," which imitates the sound of a loud impact.  The slang meaning of "cheating" likely developed from the idea of hitting someone hard, metaphorically speaking, in a financial or other exploitative way.
