bean-counter:/ˈbiːnˌkaʊntər/| n.| 会计；（尤指）锱铢必较的人|A person, typically an accountant, perceived as placing excessive emphasis on controlling expenditure.  |例句：The bean-counters wouldn't approve this expense. 这些会计不会批准这笔费用的。

例句：He's such a bean-counter, he always argues about the smallest amounts of money. 他真是个斤斤计较的人，总是为了一点小钱争论不休。|近义词：accountant, bookkeeper, penny-pincher, skinflint|反义词:spendthrift, big spender|词性:noun
