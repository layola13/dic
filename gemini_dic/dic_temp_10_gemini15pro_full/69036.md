fatuousness:/ˌfætʃuːəsnəs/
n.|愚蠢；愚昧；空洞|The quality of being silly and pointless.|His speech was marked by its fatuousness. 他的演讲以愚蠢空洞为特点。|
近义词：silliness, foolishness, stupidity, inanity, vacuity
反义词：wisdom, intelligence, sagacity, sense, profundity
词性:noun

fatuous:/ˈfætʃuəs/
adj.|愚蠢的；愚昧的；空洞的|Silly and pointless.|She made a fatuous remark about the weather. 她对天气做了一个愚蠢的评论。|
近义词：silly, foolish, stupid, inane, vacuous
反义词：wise, intelligent, sagacious, sensible, profound
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *fatuus* "foolish, silly, inspired by a god," from *fari* "to speak" (compare *infant*, literally "not speaking"). The sense evolution seems to have been "inspired by a deity" > "oracular" > "silly, foolish."
