befriends:/bɪˈfrɛndz/
verb
与…交朋友；友好对待
To act as a friend to; to offer support and companionship to.
She befriended the new student and helped her adjust to the new school. 她和新来的学生交了朋友，并帮助她适应新学校。
He befriended a stray dog and gave it a home. 他收养了一只流浪狗，给了它一个家。
synonyms: supports, assists, champions, advocates for
antonyms: alienates, ostracizes, shuns, isolates
词性:v.
