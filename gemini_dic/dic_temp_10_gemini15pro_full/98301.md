insidiously:/ɪnˈsɪdiəsli/| adv.|阴险地；潜伏地；不知不觉地|In a gradual, subtle way, but with harmful effects.|The disease spread insidiously throughout the population. 这种疾病不知不觉地在人群中蔓延开来。|近义词：stealthily, subtly, craftily, treacherously|反义词：openly, honestly, directly|词性:副词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *insidiosus, from Latin insidiae (“ambush, treachery”), from insidere (“to sit in, occupy”), from in (“in, on”) + sedēre (“to sit”).
