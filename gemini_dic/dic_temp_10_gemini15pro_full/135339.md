The scientist's jargon was nonpenetrable to the layman.  这位科学家的专业术语对门外汉来说难以理解。 | Synonyms: impervious, impenetrable, impermeable, opaque, incomprehensible | Antonyms: penetrable, permeable, transparent, comprehensible | word property: adjective
