coldfinch:/ˈkoʊldfɪntʃ/| n.|冷雀，冷漠的人|A fictional or hypothetical person who is emotionally detached or unresponsive.  This word is not a standard English word and likely a neologism. It combines "cold" (meaning unemotional) and "finch" (a type of bird).  The image evoked might be of a small, quiet bird, seemingly indifferent to its surroundings.|"She remained a coldfinch throughout the ordeal, never shedding a tear." 她自始至终都像个冷雀，从未流过一滴泪。|近义词：stoic, impassive, aloof, unfeeling, apathetic|反义词：warm, emotional, passionate, compassionate|词性:noun
