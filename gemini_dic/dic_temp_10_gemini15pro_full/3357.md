affricative:/ˈæfrɪkətɪv/| adj. n.|塞擦音；塞擦的|A sound that begins as a stop and releases as a fricative, such as the sounds represented by "ch" and "j" in English.|The sounds /tʃ/ and /dʒ/ are affricatives.|近义词:none (as a specific phonetic term)|反义词:none (as a specific phonetic term)|词性:adjective, noun

affricative:/əˈfrɪkətɪv/| n.|塞擦音|A consonant sound that begins as a stop and releases as a fricative.|The "ch" sound in "church" is an affricative.|近义词:none (as a specific phonetic term)|反义词:none (as a specific phonetic term)|词性:noun
