borscht:/ˈbɔːrʃt/ (US), /ˈbɔːʃt/ (UK)| n. |罗宋汤|A beet soup of Ukrainian origin, commonly made with beef stock and other vegetables.|例句：I had a delicious bowl of borscht for lunch. 我午餐喝了一碗美味的罗宋汤。|例句：Borscht is a staple in many Eastern European cuisines. 罗宋汤是许多东欧菜肴中的主食。|近义词：beet soup |反义词: (No direct antonym for a specific food) |词性:noun
Etymology: From Ukrainian борщ (boršč), from Proto-Slavic *bъrščь, from *bъrsti ("prickly"), referring to the hogweed originally used in the soup.
