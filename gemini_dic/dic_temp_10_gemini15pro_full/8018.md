anthracitic:/ˌænθrəˈsɪtɪk/
adjective
无烟煤的；似无烟煤的
Relating to, containing, or resembling anthracite.
The mine produces high-quality anthracitic coal. 该矿生产高质量的无烟煤。
The walls were painted a dark, anthracitic grey.  墙壁被漆成了深灰色，像无烟煤的颜色。
Near synonyms: coal-black, jet-black, ebony
Antonyms:  light, bright
