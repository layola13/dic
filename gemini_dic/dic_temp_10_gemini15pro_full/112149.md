look-back:/ˈlʊkˌbæk/ | n. | 回顾，回顾展，追溯 | An act of thinking about something that happened in the past. |  Example: The company held a look-back on its 50th anniversary. 公司在其50周年纪念日举行了一次回顾展。

| Example:  Taking a look-back at her career, she realized how much she had achieved. 回顾她的职业生涯，她意识到自己取得了多么大的成就。 | 近义词: review, retrospective, reflection | 反义词: preview, forecast | 词性: noun
