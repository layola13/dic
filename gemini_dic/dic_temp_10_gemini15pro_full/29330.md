castile: /kæˈstiːl/ /kæˈstaɪl/ | n. | 卡斯蒂利亚肥皂；卡斯蒂利亚 (西班牙中部地区) | A hard, white soap made from olive oil and sodium hydroxide, originally from Castile in Spain.  Also refers to the historical region of Castile in Spain. |  I use castile soap for everything from washing dishes to showering. 我用卡斯蒂利亚肥皂做所有的事情，从洗碗到洗澡。|  The landscape of Castile is breathtaking. 卡斯蒂利亚的风景令人叹为观止。 | synonym:  (for soap) olive oil soap, vegetable-based soap | antonym:  (no direct antonym for the soap, for the region there's no applicable antonym) | noun
