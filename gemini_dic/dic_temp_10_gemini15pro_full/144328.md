outspurting:/aʊtˈspɜːrtɪŋ/
|v. |喷出，涌出，迸发|To gush or burst forth suddenly and forcefully.|The volcano was outspurting lava and ash. 火山喷出熔岩和火山灰。|Synonyms: gushing, erupting, spurting, spewing|Antonyms: trickling, dripping|Verb

outspurting:/aʊtˈspɜːrtɪŋ/
|n.|喷出，涌出|The act of gushing or bursting forth.|The outspurting of lava created a spectacular display.  熔岩的喷涌景象蔚为壮观。|Synonyms: eruption, gush, spurt|Antonyms: trickle, drip|Noun
