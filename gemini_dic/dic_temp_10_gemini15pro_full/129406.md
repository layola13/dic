nominalize:/ˈnɒmɪnəlaɪz/ | v. |名词化；使名词化|To convert (a word or phrase, such as an adjective or verb) into a noun. |They nominalized the adjective "beautiful" into the noun "beauty." 他们把形容词“美丽的”名词化成了名词“美”。| Example: The verb "fail" can be nominalized into the noun "failure." 动词“失败”可以名词化成名词“失败”。| Synonyms: nounify, substantivize | Antonyms: verbalize | verb
