achromic:/əˈkrəʊmɪk/ /eɪˈkroʊmɪk/| adj.|无色的，消色的|Transmitting light without color separation; colorless.|The achromatic lens corrected the color distortion. 消色差透镜校正了颜色失真。

The scientist used an achromatic prism in the experiment.  科学家在实验中使用了消色棱镜。|近义词:colorless, uncolored, neutral|反义词:chromatic, colored|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From French achromatique, from Ancient Greek ἀ- (a-, “not, without”) + χρῶμα (khrôma, “color”).

USAGE->
The achromatic lens is essential for high-quality photography. =  消色差透镜对于高质量摄影至关重要。

Achromatic telescopes provide clearer images. = 消色差望远镜提供更清晰的图像。


PHRASE->
achromatic lens = 消色差透镜
achromatic objective = 消色差物镜
achromatic prism = 消色棱镜
achromatic doublet = 消色差双合透镜
achromatic telescope = 消色差望远镜
