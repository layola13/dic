chaoses:/ˈkeɪɒsɪz/ (US) /ˈkeɪɒsiz/ (UK)| n. |混乱，混沌|A state of utter confusion or disorder; a total lack of organization or order.|The city was in chaos after the earthquake. 地震过后，这座城市陷入一片混乱。|
The political situation descended into chaos. 政治局势陷入了混乱。|近义词：disorder, turmoil, anarchy, pandemonium|反义词：order, harmony, stability|词性: 名词 (plural of chaos)
Etymology: From Old French and Late Latin chaos, from Ancient Greek χάος (kháos, “vast chasm, void”).
