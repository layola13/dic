ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *daintie "delight, pleasure, desire; something pleasing," noun use of feminine of daintié "pleasant, delightful," past participle of dantier "to treat with honor, esteem," from Latin dignitari "to consider worthy," from dignus "worthy."
