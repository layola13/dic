entomolite:/ˈɛntəməˌlaɪt/| n. |昆虫化石，节肢动物化石|A fossil of an arthropod, especially an insect or crustacean.|Example: The entomolite found in the shale was identified as a trilobite.  这块页岩中发现的昆虫化石被鉴定为三叶虫。

Example:  The museum's collection includes a wide variety of entomolite specimens from different geological periods.  博物馆的藏品包括来自不同地质时期的各种昆虫化石标本。
|近义词：arthropod fossil, invertebrate fossil|反义词: None|词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Greek *éntomon* (insect) + *-lite* (stone, fossil)
