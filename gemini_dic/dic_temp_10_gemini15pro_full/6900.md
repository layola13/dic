anchorlike:/ˈæŋkərlaɪk/
adj.
像锚一样的，坚定的
Resembling an anchor; firm, steadfast.
The captain's anchorlike presence calmed the frightened passengers. 船长像锚一样坚定的存在使受惊的乘客平静下来。
近义词：stable, firm, steadfast, secure
反义词：unstable, wavering, insecure
词性:形容词

词源: anchor (n.) + like (suffix)
"anchor" 源自古英语 ancor, 来自拉丁语 ancora, 最终来自希腊语 ankura "anchor."
"like" 作后缀表示“像...一样的”，源自古英语 lic.
