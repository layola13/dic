cervelat:/sɜːrˈvɛlət/ /sərˈvɛlɑːt/| n. |香肠，萨拉米香肠|A type of highly seasoned, smoked sausage, typically made of beef and pork, often containing garlic and spices.  It's a popular sausage in Switzerland and France.| I had a cervelat sandwich for lunch.  我午餐吃了一个香肠三明治。|近义词：sausage, salami, pepperoni|反义词: None|词性:noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From French *cervelas*, from Italian *cervellato*,  ultimately from *cervello* "brain," because it originally contained brains.  Over time, the ingredients changed, but the name remained.
