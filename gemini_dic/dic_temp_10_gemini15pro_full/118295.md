mercuriality:/ˌmɜːkjʊəriˈælɪti/ | n. | 易变性，反复无常，水银般特性 | The actor is known for his mercuriality; one moment he's jovial, the next he's brooding.  这位演员以其反复无常而闻名；一会儿他很开心，一会儿他又在沉思。 | Example: The mercuriality of the stock market makes it a risky investment. 股市的波动性使其成为一项高风险投资。| Synonym: volatility, fickleness, capriciousness, instability | Antonym: stability, constancy, steadiness | noun
