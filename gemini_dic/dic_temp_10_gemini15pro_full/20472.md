bittor: /ˈbɪtər/ | adj. | 苦的，痛苦的，辛酸的；严寒的；怀恨的 | Something that is bitter has a sharp, unpleasant taste, not sweet.  It can also describe feelings of sadness, resentment, or anger, and even extreme cold. | The coffee was too bitter for me to drink. 这咖啡太苦了，我喝不下。 | Synonyms: acrid, sharp, resentful, hostile | Antonyms: sweet, pleasant, kind, forgiving | adjective

bittor: /ˈbɪtər/ | n. | 苦味；苦的东西；苦啤酒 | A substance or a type of beer that has a bitter taste.  This usage is less common than the adjective form. |  He added a bittor to the cocktail. 他在鸡尾酒里加了苦味剂。 | Synonyms:  (for beer) ale, stout | Antonyms: sweet, sweetener | noun
