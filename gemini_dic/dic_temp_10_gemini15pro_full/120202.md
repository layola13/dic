mineralist:/ˈmɪnərəlɪst/ | n. | 极简主义者；矿物学家 | A person who practices minimalism, a lifestyle characterized by owning only essential items. / A person who studies minerals. | 例句：She's become a minimalist, getting rid of almost everything she owned. 她已经成为一个极简主义者，几乎把她所有的东西都处理掉了。

例句：The minimalist decorated her apartment with only a few pieces of furniture.  这位极简主义者只用了几件家具装饰她的公寓。

近义词：simplifier, essentialist | 反义词：maximalist, consumerist | 词性：noun


mineralist:/ˈmɪnərəlɪst/ | adj. | 极简主义的 | Characterized by minimalism, a lifestyle that emphasizes simplicity and the reduction of possessions to only essential items. | 例句：She prefers a minimalist aesthetic in her home. 她喜欢家里是极简主义的美学风格。

例句：The minimalist design of the website is very appealing.  这个网站的极简主义设计非常吸引人。


近义词：simple, uncluttered, spare | 反义词：ornate, elaborate, cluttered | 词性：adjective
