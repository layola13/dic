henceforth:/ˌhɛnsˈfɔːrθ/| adv.|从此以后，今后|From this time on; from now on.|例句：Henceforth, you will be known as Sir Reginald. 从此以后，你将被称为雷吉纳德爵士。|近义词：hereafter, subsequently, from now on|反义词：heretofore, previously|词性:副词

henceforth:/ˌhɛnsˈfɔːrθ/| adv.|从此以后，今后|Starting from this time; from now on.|例句：The company will henceforth be known as "Acme Corp."  该公司今后将被称为“Acme Corp”。|近义词：thenceforth, from this day forward|反义词：hitherto|词性:副词
