noncriticalnesses:/ˌnɒnˈkrɪtɪkəlnəsɪz/
noun
非关键性；不重要性
The state or quality of not being critical; lack of importance or seriousness.
The noncriticalnesses of the issues raised were apparent to everyone. 这些提出的问题的非关键性对每个人来说都是显而易见的。
Synonyms: insignificance, unimportance, triviality
Antonyms: criticality, importance, significance
词性: 名词
