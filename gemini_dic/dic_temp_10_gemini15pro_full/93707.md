imitsu: /ɪˈmiːtsuː/
n.
secret; confidential information
A secret or confidential matter, especially one that is deliberately concealed.  This word is borrowed from Japanese.
例句：They swore to keep the imitsu to themselves. 他们发誓要保守这个秘密。

例句：The imitsu surrounding the project was finally revealed.  围绕该项目的秘密终于被揭开了。

近义词：secret, mystery, confidence
反义词：public knowledge, open secret
词性：noun
