Etymology->
non-: prefix meaning "not"
vigilant: from Latin *vigilantem (nominative vigilans), present participle of vigilare "to keep awake, watch," from vigil "awake, watchful," related to vigere "be lively, flourish"
-ness: suffix forming nouns expressing state or quality
