nonexpiry:/ˌnɒnɪkˈspaɪəri/ | adj. |  不失效的，永久有效的 | Not expiring; not subject to expiry or expiration. | This is a nonexpiry license.  这是一个永久有效的许可证。 |  This contract is nonexpiry. 这份合同是永久有效的。 | 近义词: permanent, perpetual, everlasting | 反义词:  temporary, expiring, short-term | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "expiry" (noun meaning "the end of a period of validity").  "Expiry" itself comes from the verb "expire," ultimately from Latin "expirare" (to breathe out, die).
