freeganism:/ˈfriːɡənɪzəm/
n.
拾荒主义；免费主义
The practice of reclaiming and eating discarded food.
Freeganism is a lifestyle that seeks to minimize participation in the conventional economy and reduce waste.  拾荒主义是一种生活方式，旨在最大限度地减少参与传统经济和减少浪费。
Freegans often find discarded food that is still perfectly edible.  拾荒者经常找到仍然完全可以食用的丢弃食物。
Synonym:anti-consumerism, dumpster diving
Antonym:consumerism
noun
