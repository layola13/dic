acknowledgedly:/əkˈnɒlɪdʒɪdli/
adv.
公认地，无可否认地
It is acknowledgedly a difficult problem. = 这无可否认是一个难题。
Acknowledgedly, the system is far from perfect. = 公认地，这个系统远非完美。
英文解析:Adverb form of acknowledged; in an acknowledged manner; admittedly.  It implies that something is widely accepted as true.
例句：
Acknowledgedly, he is the best player on the team. = 公认地，他是队里最好的球员。

He is acknowledgedly one of the most talented musicians of our time. = 他是公认的当代最有才华的音乐家之一。

近义词:admittedly, undeniably, confessedly, indisputably, certainly
反义词:denyingly, disputably, questionably
词性:adverb

USAGE->
用于修饰动词或形容词，表示“公认地”、“无可否认地”。

例句：
The task is acknowledgedly difficult, but we must persevere. = 这项任务公认地很难，但我们必须坚持下去。

He acknowledgedly made a mistake in his calculations. = 他承认自己在计算中犯了一个错误。


PHRASE->
没有常用的固定搭配。 通常直接用作状语。
