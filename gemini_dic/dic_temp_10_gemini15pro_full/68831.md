fascism:/ˈfæʃɪzəm/ | n. | 法西斯主义 | A political philosophy, movement, or regime that exalts nation and often race above the individual and that stands for a centralized autocratic government headed by a dictatorial leader, severe economic and social regimentation, and forcible suppression of opposition. |  Mussolini's Italy was a classic example of fascism.  墨索里尼统治下的意大利是法西斯主义的典型例子。 |  totalitarianism, authoritarianism, dictatorship | democracy, liberalism | noun
fascist:/ˈfæʃɪst/ | n. adj. | 法西斯主义者；法西斯的 |  A person who believes in or practices fascism. / Relating to or characteristic of fascism. | He was branded a fascist for his extreme views.  由于他的极端观点，他被贴上了法西斯主义者的标签。  | totalitarian, authoritarian, dictatorial | democratic, liberal | noun, adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Italian *fascismo*, from *fascio* ("bundle, group, union"), ultimately from Latin *fascis* ("bundle").  The term was first used by Mussolini to describe his political movement.
