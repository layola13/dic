negotiant:/nɪˈɡoʊʃiənt/ /nɪˈɡəʊʃiənt/
n.
谈判者；磋商者；洽谈者；商人
A person who negotiates, especially in business; a merchant.
例句：The negotiant finalized the deal for the import of coffee beans.  这位商人最终敲定了咖啡豆的进口协议。

例句：He was a shrewd negotiant, always getting the best possible price. 他是一位精明的谈判者，总是能拿到最好的价格。

近义词：merchant, trader, dealer, broker, negotiator
反义词：customer, buyer, client
词性：名词
