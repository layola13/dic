libertarianisms:/ˌlɪbərˈtɛəriənɪzəmz/
n.
自由意志主义（的各种形式）
Various forms or specific instances of the political philosophy of libertarianism.
Libertarianisms encompass a wide range of beliefs, from minarchism to anarcho-capitalism.  自由意志主义包含了广泛的信念，从最小国家主义到无政府资本主义。
Different libertarianisms prioritize different values, such as individual liberty, property rights, and free markets. 不同的自由意志主义优先考虑不同的价值观，例如个人自由、财产权和自由市场。
Synonyms: libertarian doctrines, libertarian philosophies
Antonyms: authoritarianisms, collectivisms
noun
