clueless:/ˈkluːləs/| adj.|无能的，笨拙的，没有头绪的|Having no knowledge, understanding, or ability; totally bewildered.|例句：He was completely clueless about how to fix the computer. 他完全不知道如何修理电脑。|例句：She felt clueless about what to do next. 她对接下来该做什么毫无头绪。|近义词：ignorant, unaware, bewildered, lost, confused|反义词：knowledgeable, aware, astute, savvy|词性:形容词
