nonoriginals:/ˌnɒnəˈrɪdʒɪnəlz/ | n. | 非原创作品；复制品；仿制品 |  Works that are copied or imitated from existing sources, lacking originality.  | The museum displayed a collection of nonoriginals, including replicas of famous sculptures.  博物馆展出了一系列非原创作品，包括著名雕塑的复制品。| synonyms: copies, imitations, reproductions, duplicates | antonyms: originals, authentic works | noun

nonoriginal:/ˌnɒnəˈrɪdʒɪnəl/ | adj. | 非原创的；缺乏独创性的；复制的 | Not original; derived or copied from something else. | His essay was criticized for being nonoriginal and full of plagiarism. 他的论文因非原创且充满抄袭而受到批评。 | synonyms: unoriginal, derivative, copied, imitative, uninspired | antonyms: original, innovative, creative, unique, authentic | adjective
