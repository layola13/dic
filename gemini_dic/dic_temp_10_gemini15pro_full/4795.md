algin: /ˈældʒɪn/ | n. | 海藻酸, 藻朊酸 | A gummy substance extracted from brown seaweed, used as a thickener and stabilizer in food and other products. |  Algin is commonly used in ice cream to prevent ice crystals from forming. 海藻酸通常用于冰淇淋中以防止冰晶形成。

Algin is also used in dental impressions. 海藻酸也用于牙科印模。| synonyms: alginate, sodium alginate | antonyms: None | noun


alginate: /ˈældʒɪneɪt/ | n. | 海藻酸盐 | A salt of alginic acid. | Calcium alginate is used in wound dressings. 海藻酸钙用于伤口敷料。

Sodium alginate is a common food additive. 海藻酸钠是一种常见的食品添加剂。| synonyms: algin | antonyms: None | noun
