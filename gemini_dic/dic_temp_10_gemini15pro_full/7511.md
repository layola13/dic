annats:/ˈænəts/| n. | 首年俸；第一年收入；年金 | First year's income of a spiritual living, formerly paid to the pope.  A payment to the Crown, usually the first year's income, made upon presentation to a benefice. | The annats were a significant source of papal revenue.  首年俸是教皇收入的重要来源。

The king abolished the payment of annats to Rome. 国王废除了向罗马支付首年俸的制度。 | 近义词: first fruits, income, revenue | 反义词: expenses, expenditure | 词性: noun

词源: From Old French *annates* (plural), from Late Latin *annata* (“things that return yearly”), from Latin *annus* (“year”).
