leave-out:/ˈliːvˌaʊt/| n.|遗漏，省略；被忽视的人（或物）|An act of omitting someone or something; a person or thing that has been omitted.|The annual picnic was a fun event, but there were a few unfortunate leave-outs from the invitation list.|近义词：omission, exclusion, oversight|反义词：inclusion|词性：名词

leave out:/ˌliːv ˈaʊt/| vt.|遗漏，省略；忽视，不理会|To omit or exclude someone or something; to not include or consider.|She left out an important detail in her report.  她在报告中遗漏了一个重要的细节。|近义词：omit, exclude, neglect|反义词：include|词性：动词
