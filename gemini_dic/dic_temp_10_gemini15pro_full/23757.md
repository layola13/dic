braves:/breɪvz/| n.|勇士（复数）;勇敢的人（复数）|Plural form of "brave" (noun), referring to courageous people.  It can also be the third person singular present tense form of the verb "brave." |The braves fought valiantly against the enemy. 勇士们英勇地与敌人作战。|近义词：heroes, warriors, champions|反义词: cowards|词性:noun

brave:/breɪv/| n. adj. vt.|勇士;勇敢的;勇敢地面对|A person who is courageous.  As an adjective, it describes someone courageous or an action showing courage. As a verb, it means to face something difficult or dangerous with courage. |He was a brave soldier. 他是一位勇敢的士兵。|近义词：courageous, valiant, bold|反义词: cowardly, timid|词性:noun, adjective, verb
