nargile:/ˈnɑːrɡɪleɪ/ (US), /ˈnɑːɡɪleɪ/ (UK)| n.|水烟袋，水烟壶|A Middle Eastern tobacco pipe with a long flexible tube connected to a container where the smoke is cooled by passing through water.|She relaxed on the cushions, puffing on her nargile. 她斜倚在垫子上，吸着水烟。|Synonyms: hookah, shisha, water pipe|Antonyms: None.|noun

narghile:/ˈnɑːrɡɪleɪ/ (US), /ˈnɑːɡɪleɪ/ (UK)| n.|水烟|The act of smoking a narghile. |We enjoyed an evening of narghile and conversation. 我们享受了一个吸水烟聊天的夜晚。|Synonyms: hookah smoking, shisha smoking|Antonyms: None.|noun
