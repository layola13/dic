palmitic:/pælˈmɪtɪk/| adj.|棕榈的，软脂酸的|Relating to, or derived from palm oil.  A saturated fatty acid occurring in animal and vegetable fats.|Palm oil is rich in palmitic acid. 棕榈油富含棕榈酸。|Synonyms: hexadecanoic, cetylic |Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific fatty acid, could consider unsaturated fatty acids as a broader contrasting group like oleic or linoleic) | Word Type: Adjective

palmitate:/ˈpæl.mɪ.teɪt/ | n. | 棕榈酸酯，棕榈酸盐 | A salt or ester of palmitic acid. | Sodium palmitate is a common ingredient in soap. 棕榈酸钠是肥皂中的常见成分。| Synonyms: hexadecanoate | Antonyms: (No direct antonym for a specific fatty acid salt) | Word Type: Noun
