Simple Present:
What do you do for a living? = 你靠什么谋生？
What does she like to eat? = 她喜欢吃什么？

Present Continuous:
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he doing in the garden? = 他在花园里做什么？

Present Perfect:
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she cooked for dinner? = 她晚饭做了什么？

Present Perfect Continuous:
What have you been working on recently? = 你最近一直在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

Simple Past:
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say to you? = 她对你说了什么？

Past Continuous:
What were you doing at 8 pm last night? = 昨晚八点你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 会议期间她在想什么？

Past Perfect:
What had you done before I arrived? = 在我到达之前你做了什么？
What had they eaten before the movie? = 看电影前他们吃了什么？

Past Perfect Continuous:
What had you been doing before the phone rang? = 电话响之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been discussing for hours? = 他们已经讨论了几个小时什么？

Simple Future:
What will you do next weekend? = 你下周末要做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气怎么样？

Future Continuous:
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be discussing at the conference? = 他们在会议上会讨论什么？

Future Perfect:
What will you have finished by Friday? = 到周五你将完成什么？
What will she have accomplished by the end of the year? = 到年底她将完成什么？

Future Perfect Continuous:
What will you have been studying for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经学习五年什么了？
What will they have been working on for so long? = 他们将一直在做什么这么长时间？
