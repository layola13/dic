incommensurate:/ˌɪnkəˈmɛnʃərət/ /ˌɪnkəˈmɛnʃʊrət/ | adj. |不相称的，不相应的，不能相比的 | Not proportionate; not adequate or sufficient; not of equal measure or extent.  | Her earnings are incommensurate with her skills. 她的收入与她的技能不相称。 |  The punishment was incommensurate with the crime.  惩罚与罪行不相称。 |  disproportionate, inadequate, insufficient |  commensurate, proportionate | adjective
