corrodiate:/kəˈrəʊdɪeɪt/ /kəˈroʊdieɪt/ |v. |腐蚀，侵蚀|to destroy or damage something slowly, especially by chemical action |Acid rain corrodiates stonework. 酸雨腐蚀石制品。|Synonyms: erode, wear away, eat away, decay, disintegrate|Antonyms: preserve, protect, strengthen|verb

corrodiate:/kəˈrəʊdɪeɪt/ /kəˈroʊdieɪt/ |v. |使腐蚀，使恶化|to make something weaker; to gradually destroy the strength or effectiveness of something|The scandal corroded public trust in the government. 这起丑闻损害了公众对政府的信任。|Synonyms: undermine, weaken, impair, damage, destroy|Antonyms: strengthen, reinforce, bolster|verb
