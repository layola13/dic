gibbering:/ˈdʒɪbərɪŋ/| adj.|语无伦次的，喋喋不休的|Speaking or uttering rapidly, incoherently, and unintelligibly.|He was found gibbering in the corner after the shock. 他受到惊吓后，被人发现缩在角落里语无伦次地说着话。|Synonyms: babbling, chattering, rambling|Antonyms: articulate, coherent, lucid|adjective

gibber:/ˈdʒɪbər/| v. n.|快而含糊不清地说，胡言乱语|To speak rapidly, incoherently, and unintelligibly.|He gibbered something about a monster in the closet. 他含糊不清地说着衣柜里有怪物。|Synonyms: babble, chatter, ramble|Antonyms: articulate, enunciate, pronounce|verb, noun
