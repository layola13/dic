monecious: /məˈniːʃəs/ | adj. | 雌雄同株的 | Having both male and female reproductive organs in the same individual; hermaphroditic. |  Corn is a monecious plant, meaning each plant has both male and female flowers. 玉米是雌雄同株的植物，这意味着每株植物都有雄花和雌花。

The cucumber plant is monecious. 黄瓜是雌雄同株的植物。


|近义词: hermaphroditic, bisexual |反义词: dioecious |词性: adjective
