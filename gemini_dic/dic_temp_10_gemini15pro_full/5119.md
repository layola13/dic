allegheny:/ˌæləˈɡeɪni/ | n. adj.| 阿勒格尼，阿勒格尼河的 |  A river flowing from northern Pennsylvania to Pittsburgh, where it joins the Monongahela to form the Ohio River. Also refers to the Allegheny Mountains, a range in the eastern US.  Can also be used as an adjective relating to these geographical features. | The Allegheny River is a major waterway. 阿勒格尼河是一条主要的水道。 | Synonyms:  (for the river) Ohio tributary, (for the mountains) Appalachian range | Antonyms:  None | noun, adjective
