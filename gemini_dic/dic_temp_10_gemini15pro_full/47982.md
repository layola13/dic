decimalising:/ˈdɛsɪməlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
十进制化
The act of converting a system of measurement or currency to a decimal system.
Many countries decimalized their currency in the 20th century.  许多国家在20世纪将货币十进制化。
The government is planning to decimalise the weights and measures system. 政府正计划将度量衡系统十进制化。
近义词: metricate
反义词: (No direct antonym, but consider "using a non-decimal system")
