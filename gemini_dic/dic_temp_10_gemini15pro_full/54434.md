disinflame:/ˌdɪsɪnˈfleɪm/| v. |消除炎症，减轻炎症|To reduce inflammation or swelling; to soothe or calm an inflamed area or condition.|The doctor prescribed medication to disinflame the swollen joint. 医生开了药来消肿发炎的关节。| The cool compress helped to disinflame the burn. 冷敷有助于减轻烧伤炎症。| 近义词: soothe, alleviate, reduce, assuage | 反义词: inflame, irritate, aggravate |词性:动词
