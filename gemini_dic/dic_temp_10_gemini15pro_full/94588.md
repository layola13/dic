impractical:/ɪmˈpræktɪk(ə)l/
adjective
不切实际的，不实用的
Not sensible or realistic; not likely to be effective or successful.
例句：
It's impractical to expect everyone to agree.
期望每个人都同意是不切实际的。

It would be impractical to take the car, as parking is difficult in the city center.
开车去是不切实际的，因为市中心的停车位很难找。

近义词：unrealistic, unworkable, infeasible, nonviable
反义词：practical, realistic, feasible, workable
词性: adjective

词源:
From in- (not) + practical.
