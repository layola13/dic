conceptionalist:/kənˌsɛpʃənəlɪst/
n.
概念艺术家，观念艺术家
An artist who creates conceptual art, where the idea or concept behind the work is more important than its aesthetic or material form.
Example: The conceptionalist's work challenged traditional notions of art.  这位概念艺术家的作品挑战了传统的艺术观念。

Example:  She was a renowned conceptionalist, known for her provocative installations. 她是一位著名的概念艺术家，以其具有煽动性的装置艺术而闻名。

近义词: conceptual artist
反义词: (No direct antonym.  Could consider "traditional artist" as a contrasting term)
词性: noun
