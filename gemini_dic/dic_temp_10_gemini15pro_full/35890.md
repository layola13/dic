coconspirators: /ˌkoʊkənˈspɪrətərz/
noun
同谋者；共谋者；阴谋同伙
A person who conspires with another or others; a fellow conspirator.
The coconspirators were arrested and charged with treason.  同谋者被逮捕并被指控叛国。
He and his coconspirators planned the robbery for months. 他和他的同伙策划了几个月的抢劫。
synonyms: accomplice, collaborator, partner in crime, confederate
antonyms: opponent, adversary, enemy
