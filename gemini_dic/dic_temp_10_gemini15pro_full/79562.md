glonoin:/ˈɡlaɪnɔɪn/| n.|硝酸甘油, 甘油三硝酸酯|A colorless, oily, explosive liquid used in medicine as a vasodilator, especially in the treatment of angina pectoris. It is also a key ingredient in dynamite.|He took some glonoin for his angina. 他服用了一些硝酸甘油治疗心绞痛。|近义词：nitroglycerin|反义词: None|词性:noun
