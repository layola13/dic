intermembral:/ˌɪntərˈmɛmbrəl/| adj.|肢间的，前肢与后肢之间的|Relating to or situated between the limbs, especially between the forelimbs and hindlimbs.|The intermembral index is a ratio used in comparative anatomy.  肢间指数是比较解剖学中使用的比率。|The researchers measured the intermembral index of several primate species. 研究人员测量了几种灵长类动物的肢间指数。|近义词: between limbs|反义词:  (No direct antonym, could consider terms like "within limb" or "intramembral" although not commonly used)|词性: adjective
