discontinuation:/ˌdɪskənˌtɪnjuˈeɪʃən/
n.
中止，停止，中断
The act of stopping something; cessation.
The discontinuation of the treatment led to a relapse.  治疗的中断导致了复发。
Synonyms: cessation, termination, interruption, suspension
Antonyms: continuation, commencement, resumption
