bay:/beɪ/
n.|海湾；狗吠声；隔间|A body of water partially enclosed by land.  A deep, prolonged bark of a dog. A compartment or recessed area.|The boats sailed into the bay. 船只驶入海湾。|近义词:gulf, cove, inlet|反义词:cape, headland|词性:名词

bay:/beɪ/
vi.|（狗）吠叫|To bark in a deep, prolonged way.|The hounds bayed at the moon. 猎犬对着月亮吠叫。|近义词:bark, howl|反义词:whimper, whine|词性:动词


bay:/beɪ/
vt.|（猎犬）对着（猎物）吠叫|To bark at (prey).|The dogs bayed the escaped convict.  猎犬对着逃犯吠叫。|近义词:bark, howl|反义词:whimper, whine|词性:动词


bay:/beɪ/
n.|月桂树；月桂叶|A laurel tree or its leaves.|A wreath of bay. 月桂花环|近义词:laurel|反义词: (无)|词性:名词

词源: From Old French *baee "opening, gap," from Latin *baia.  Related to Late Latin badare "to gape."
