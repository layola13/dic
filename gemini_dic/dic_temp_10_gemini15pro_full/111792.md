lofting: /ˈlɒftɪŋ/ (UK) /ˈlɔːftɪŋ/ (US) | n. |  阁楼搭建; 高尔夫球高吊球; 放样; | The act or process of building a loft in a building, or the loft itself. Also, in golf, hitting a shot high into the air. In engineering, it refers to creating a full-sized model from a smaller design. |  例句：They are lofting the bedroom to create more space. 他们正在搭建卧室阁楼以创造更多空间。

例句：His lofting shot landed softly on the green. 他的高吊球轻轻地落在了果岭上。

近义词：attic conversion, raising (in building context); chip shot, pitch shot (in golf context); prototyping, modeling (in engineering context) | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
