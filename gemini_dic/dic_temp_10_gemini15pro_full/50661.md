derivatively:/dɪˈrɪvətɪvli/| adv. | 衍生地；派生地 |In a derivative manner; not original or primary. |例句：The artist's work was criticized for being too derivative, lacking any originality.  这位艺术家的作品因过于衍生，缺乏原创性而受到批评。

例句：He derivatively composed the music based on a famous classical piece. 他根据一首著名的古典作品衍生创作了这首曲子。|近义词：unoriginally, secondarily, indirectly|反义词：originally, primarily, creatively|词性:副词
