conational:/kəˈnæʃənəl/ /koʊˈnæʃənəl/
adjective
同国的；同胞的
Of or relating to the same nation; a fellow national.
Example:  Conational companies often collaborate on international projects.  同国公司经常在国际项目上合作。

Example: She felt a sense of camaraderie with her conational athletes at the Olympics.  在奥运会上，她对她的同胞运动员感到一种友情。
Synonym: compatriot, fellow countryman/countrywoman
Antonym: foreign, alien
词性: adjective
