exhalant:/ˈɛkshələnt/ /ɛkˈseɪlənt/
| adj. n.|呼气的，呼出的；呼气器官|Something that exhales; specifically, a part through which air or other vapor is discharged, as a spiracle or a stoma.|The blowhole of a whale is an exhalant.|近义词：expiratory, efferent|反义词：inhalant, inspiratory
|词性:形容词, 名词


exhalant:/ˈɛkshələnt/ /ɛkˈseɪlənt/

|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *exhalant- (stem of exhalans), present participle of exhalare "to breathe out," from ex- "out" + halare "to breathe."
