dramatizing:/ˈdræməˌtaɪzɪŋ/
verb
戏剧化; 夸大; 把…改编成戏剧
present participle of dramatize
The media is dramatizing the situation. 媒体正在夸大局势。
exaggerating, overstating, sensationalizing
understating, downplaying
v.

ETYMOLOGY->
From dramatize + -ing.  Dramatize from drama + -tize. Drama from Ancient Greek δρᾶμα (drâma, “deed, drama”), from δράω (dráō, “I do, act”).
