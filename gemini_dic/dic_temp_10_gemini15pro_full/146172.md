overexacting:/ˌoʊvərɪɡˈzæktɪŋ/
adjective
过分苛求的；要求过高的
Making excessive demands; too rigorous or demanding.
He was an overexacting teacher, who expected perfection from his students. 他是一位过分苛求的老师，期望他的学生做到完美。
The overexacting client was never satisfied with the designs. 这位要求过高的客户对设计永远不满意。

近义词: demanding, exacting, stringent, rigorous, fastidious
反义词: lenient, tolerant, undemanding, easygoing
词性: adjective
词源:  "over-" (excessively) + "exacting" (demanding). "Exacting" comes from the Latin "exigere" (to drive out, demand).
