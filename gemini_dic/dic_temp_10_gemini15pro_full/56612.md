一般现在时 (Simple Present):
Rarely used as a simple present tense verb except in dialects.  
He done told me already. (dialectal) 他已经告诉我了。
She done finished her work. (dialectal) 她已经完成了她的工作。


现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
Not used in present continuous.

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
I have done all the necessary preparations. 我已经完成了所有必要的准备工作。
They have done a great job. 他们做得很好。

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
I have been doing my homework for two hours. 我已经做了两个小时的作业了。
She has been doing yoga every morning. 她每天早上一直在做瑜伽。


一般过去时 (Simple Past):
I did my homework yesterday. 我昨天做了我的作业。
He did a great job on the project. 他在这个项目上做得很好。

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
I was doing my homework when you called. 你打电话的时候我正在做作业。
She was doing yoga in the park. 她在公园里做瑜伽。

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
I had done my homework before dinner. 晚饭前我已经做完了作业。
They had done everything they could. 他们已经尽力了。


过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
I had been doing my homework for two hours before my friend arrived. 在我朋友来之前，我一直在做作业两个小时了。
She had been doing yoga for years before she became a teacher. 在她成为老师之前，她已经做了多年的瑜伽。


一般将来时 (Simple Future):
(Will is rarely used with done.  Use "will do" or "will have done.")
I will do my homework tomorrow. 我明天会做我的作业.
I will have done my homework by tomorrow evening. 明天晚上之前我会完成我的作业。

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
(Will be doing, not will be done.)
I will be doing my homework this evening. 今晚我将会做作业。
He will be doing the dishes after dinner. 晚饭后他将会洗碗。

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
I will have done the project by Friday. 我将在周五前完成这个项目。
They will have done all the preparations by next week. 他们将在下周前完成所有准备工作。

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
By next year, I will have been doing yoga for five years. 到明年，我将已经做瑜伽五年了。
By the time he finishes, he will have been working on this project for months. 到他完成时，他将已经在这个项目上工作数月了。

虚拟语气:
If I had done my homework, I would have gotten a better grade. 如果我做了作业，我就能得到更好的成绩。
I wish I had done things differently. 我希望我当时能以不同的方式做事。

被动语态:
The work was done quickly. 这项工作很快就完成了。
The cake was done to perfection. 蛋糕烤得非常完美。


疑问句:
Is the work done yet? 工作完成了吗？
Was the homework done on time? 作业按时完成了吗？


否定句:
The work isn't done yet. 工作还没完成。
The homework wasn't done on time. 作业没有按时完成。
