outglows:/ˌaʊtˈɡloʊz/
verb
发出比...更强的光；胜过
shines more brightly than; surpasses in brightness
The city outglows the stars at night.  这座城市在夜晚的光芒胜过星星。
Her kindness outglows everyone else's. 她的善良胜过所有人。
近义词: outshines, eclipses, surpasses
反义词: dims, pales
