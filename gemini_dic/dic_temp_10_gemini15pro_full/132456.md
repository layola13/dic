nondepreciating:/ˌnɒndɪˈpriːʃieɪtɪŋ/ | adj. | 不贬值的 | Not decreasing in value over time.  Maintaining its worth or even increasing in value. |  Gold is considered a nondepreciating asset. 黄金被认为是不贬值的资产。

This land is a nondepreciating investment. 这块土地是一项不贬值的投资。| 近义词: appreciating, inflation-proof | 反义词: depreciating, declining | 词性: adjective
