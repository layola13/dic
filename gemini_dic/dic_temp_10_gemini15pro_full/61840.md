employ:/ɪmˈplɔɪ/ /ɛmˈplɔɪ/ | vt. n.|雇用；使用；利用|To give work to someone and pay them for it.  To make use of something.|
The company employs over 500 people.  这家公司雇佣了500多人。

The report employs a variety of research methods.  这份报告采用了多种研究方法。|近义词：hire, use, utilize|反义词：dismiss, fire, lay off|词性：动词/名词


employ:/ɪmˈplɔɪ/ /ɛmˈplɔɪ/ |n.|雇用；使用|The act of employing.|He was in the employ of the government. 他受雇于政府。|近义词：use, utilization|反义词：unemployment, idleness|词性：名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *emploier*, from Latin implicare "to involve, enfold, entangle," from in- "in" + plicare "to fold."  The sense shifted from "to involve oneself in" to "to make use of" and then to "to give work to."
