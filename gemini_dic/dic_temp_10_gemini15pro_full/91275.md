hydrogenous:/haɪˈdrɒdʒɪnəs/
adjective
含氢的; 由氢产生的
produced by or containing hydrogen.
Hydrogenous deposits are formed by precipitation from water. 含氢沉积物是由水中沉淀形成的。
Synonyms: hydrogen-containing, hydrogen-rich
Antonyms: non-hydrogenous (though less common)
词性: adjective
