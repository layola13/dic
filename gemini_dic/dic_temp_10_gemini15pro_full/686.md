abscissa: /æbˈsɪsə/ (US), /æbˈsɪsːə/ (UK)| n. |横坐标|The horizontal (x) coordinate in a two-dimensional Cartesian coordinate system.|The abscissa of the point (3,2) is 3.  该点(3,2)的横坐标是3。

The graph shows the relationship between time (abscissa) and distance (ordinate).  该图显示了时间（横坐标）和距离（纵坐标）之间的关系。|近义词: x-coordinate, horizontal coordinate|反义词: ordinate|词性: noun
