outcomplimenting: /ˌaʊtkɑːmplɪˈmentɪŋ/
Verb
过分恭维，胜过…的恭维
Praising someone excessively, often to the point of seeming insincere, or praising someone more than another.
He outcomplimented her with flattery so excessive it was almost embarrassing. 他过分恭维她，奉承得令人尴尬。
Synonyms: overpraising, flattering excessively, overdoing the compliments
Antonyms: criticizing, disparaging, insulting
词性: 动词
