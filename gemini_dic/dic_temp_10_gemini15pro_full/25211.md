budge:/bʌdʒ/
verb
微微移动; 改变立场; 让步
to move slightly; to change an opinion or decision; to make a concession

例句：
I pushed the rock, but it wouldn't budge.
我推了推那块石头，但它一动也不动。

The government has refused to budge on the issue of tax increases.
政府拒绝在增税问题上让步。


近义词：
shift, move, stir, yield, give way

反义词：
resist, stay put, hold fast

词性
v.
