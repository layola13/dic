lovebirds:/ˈlʌvbɜːrdz/
n.|爱情鸟，鹦鹉的一种，恩爱夫妻|A small parrot, often brightly coloured, that is popular as a cage bird and is noted for the affection that pairs show for each other. Also used to refer to two people who are obviously very much in love. |The lovebirds were cuddling in their cage.  爱情鸟在笼子里依偎着。|Synonyms: love duo, sweethearts, inseparable couple|Antonyms: estranged couple, rivals, enemies|noun
