cabesse:/kəˈbɛs/| n.| 头部，领袖，首脑|A cabesse is a head or leader, particularly in a herd of animals.  It's derived from Portuguese and is rarely used in modern English. |The old stallion was the cabesse of the wild horses.|近义词：leader, chief, head, boss|反义词:follower, subordinate|noun
