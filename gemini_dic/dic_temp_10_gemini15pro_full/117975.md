memorability:/ˌmɛmərəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 难忘,值得纪念,记忆性 | The memorability of the event was enhanced by the beautiful weather.  这场活动的难忘程度因天气宜人而增强。| English explanation: The quality of being worth remembering or easily remembered. |例句：The song has great memorability. 这首歌非常令人难忘。

例句：The trip's memorability was heightened by the unexpected encounter with a pod of dolphins. 与一群海豚的不期而遇增加了旅行的难忘性。

近义词：unforgettable, remarkable, impressive | 反义词：forgettable, unremarkable, insignificant | 词性：noun
