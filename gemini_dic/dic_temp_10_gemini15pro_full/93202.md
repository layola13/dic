ignorantnesses:/ˈɪɡnərəntnəsɪz/| n. |无知，愚昧（复数）|The state of being ignorant; lack of knowledge or awareness.  This word is rarely used; ignorance is much more common.|His ignorantnesses about basic geography were astounding. 他对基本地理的无知令人震惊。|Near synonyms: ignorances, benightedness, nescience|Antonyms: knowledge, wisdom, awareness
詞性: noun
