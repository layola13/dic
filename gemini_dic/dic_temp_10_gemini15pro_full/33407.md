churchinesses:/ˈtʃɜːrtʃɪnɪsɪz/
noun
过度虔诚；宗教仪式繁琐
Excessive piety or emphasis on religious rituals and observances.
Her churchinesses made her seem out of touch with the modern world. 她过分的虔诚让她看起来与现代世界格格不入。
Near Synonyms: sanctimoniousness, pietism, religiosity
Antonyms: irreverence, impiety, secularism
