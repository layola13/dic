argentate:/ˈɑːrdʒənˌteɪt/
adjective
镀银的；银色的
coated with silver; silvery.
The argentate surface of the mirror reflected the candlelight. 镜子镀银的表面反射着烛光。
近义词:silver, silvery, silvered
反义词:golden, gilded
词性:adjective
词源:From Latin argentātus, past participle of argentāre (“to plate with silver”), from argentum (“silver”).

/ˈɑːrdʒənˌteɪt/
verb
镀银
to coat or plate with silver.
They argentated the copper bowl to give it a more elegant appearance. 他们把铜碗镀银，使其看起来更优雅。
近义词:silver, silver-plate
反义词:gild
词性:verb
词源:From Latin argentātus, past participle of argentāre (“to plate with silver”), from argentum (“silver”).
