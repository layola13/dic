mesdames:/meɪˈdɑːm/ /meɪˈdam/ | n. | 女士们；太太们 | A plural form of *madame*, used as a polite form of address to a group of women.  It is the feminine equivalent of *messieurs*. | Ladies and gentlemen, welcome to the show! 女士们，先生们，欢迎来到这场演出！| Mesdames, please take your seats. 女士们，请就座。 | Near Synonyms: ladies, women | Antonym: messieurs | Noun
