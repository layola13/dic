misarticulations:/ˌmɪsɑːrˌtɪkjʊˈleɪʃənz/
|n.|发音错误，吐字不清|Errors or difficulties in producing speech sounds accurately.  The child's misarticulations made it difficult to understand him.|例句：The speech therapist is working with the child on his misarticulations.  言语治疗师正在帮助这个孩子纠正他的发音错误。

例句：Frequent misarticulations can be a sign of a speech disorder.  频繁的发音错误可能是言语障碍的征兆。|近义词：mispronunciations, slurs, impediments|反义词:clear articulation, proper pronunciation|词性:noun
