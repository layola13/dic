maximalist:/ˈmæksɪməlɪst/| n. adj.| 极繁主义者，极繁主义的| A person who holds extreme views and is not prepared to compromise; a style that incorporates a variety of periods and cultures, usually involving a lot of decoration.|  The designer is known for his maximalist aesthetic, incorporating bold colors, rich textures, and eclectic furniture.  这位设计师以其极繁主义美学而闻名，融合了大胆的色彩、丰富的纹理和不拘一格的家具。| synonyms:immoderate, extremist, uncompromising| antonyms:minimalist, moderate| noun, adjective

maximalist:/ˈmæksɪməlɪst/| n. adj.| 极端主义者，极端主义的| A person who holds extreme views and is not prepared to compromise; relating to or denoting an extreme opinion or policy, especially in politics or art. |  His maximalist approach to politics alienated many moderate voters.  他极端的政治手段疏远了许多温和的选民。| synonyms:immoderate, extremist, uncompromising| antonyms:minimalist, moderate| noun, adjective
