crank-down:/ˈkræŋk daʊn/
crank-down|n.|强制降低，压制，取缔|A forceful reduction or suppression; a crackdown.|The government imposed a crank-down on illegal immigration. 政府对非法移民进行了压制。|近义词：crackdown, suppression, clampdown|反义词:liberalization, easing|词性:名词
crank-down:/ˈkræŋk daʊn/
crank-down|v.|强制降低，压制，取缔|To forcefully reduce or suppress something; to clamp down.|The police cranked down on drug trafficking in the area. 警方对该地区的毒品贩运进行了压制。|近义词：crack down on, suppress, clamp down on|反义词:ease, liberalize|词性:动词

crank-down:|词源:  "crank" (旋转曲柄) + "down" (向下).  最初指通过转动曲柄来降低或收紧某物，后引申为强制压制或取缔。|
