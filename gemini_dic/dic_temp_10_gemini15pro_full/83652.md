haemorrhage:/ˈhemərɪdʒ/ (US) /ˈhɛmərɪdʒ/ (UK)| n. v.|出血；失血|a large flow of blood from a damaged blood vessel.  He suffered a massive haemorrhage after the accident. 他在事故后大出血。|近义词：bleeding, loss of blood|反义词：coagulation, clotting|词性:名词; 动词


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *hémorragie*, from Late Latin *haemorrhagia*, from Greek *haimorrhagia*, from *haima* "blood" + *rhēgnusthai* "to burst forth."
