gaiterless:/ˈɡeɪtərləs/| adj.|无绑腿的，没有绑腿的|Without gaiters.  Gaiters are garments worn over the ankle and lower leg, often for protection in outdoor activities. Gaiterless describes something or someone lacking these.|例句：He went hiking gaiterless and ended up with scratched ankles. 他没穿绑腿就去远足，结果脚踝被刮伤了。

例句：She preferred a gaiterless look for her riding boots. 她更喜欢不穿绑腿的骑马靴造型。|近义词：ungaitered|反义词: gaitered|词性: 形容词
