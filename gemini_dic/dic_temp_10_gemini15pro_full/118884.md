metasternal: /ˌmɛtəˈstɜːnəl/ | adj. | 后胸骨的，中胸腹板的 | Relating to the metasternum, the ventral plate of the middle segment of the thorax in insects. | The metasternal plates of these beetles are quite distinctive. 这些甲虫的后胸腹板非常独特。 | The metasternal region shows a distinct groove.  后胸骨区域显示出一个明显的凹槽。 | Near Synonyms: mesothoracic, sternal | Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, possibly dorsal as it refers to the opposite side) | Word type: Adjective

Etymology:
From meta- (Greek: "after," "behind," "changed," "higher") + sternum (Latin: "breastbone").
