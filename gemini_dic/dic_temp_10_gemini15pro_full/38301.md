concavo:/kɒnˈkeɪvəʊ/ (UK), /kɑːnˈkeɪvoʊ/ (US)| adj.|凹的，凹面的|Curving inwards, like the inside of a bowl. |The telescope uses a concave mirror to gather light. 这架望远镜使用凹面镜来收集光线。|近义词：hollow, recessed|反义词：convex, protuberant|形容词

concavity:/kɒnˈkævɪti/ (UK), /kɑːnˈkævəti/ (US)| n.|凹度，凹面|The state or quality of being concave. |The concavity of the lens helps to focus the light. 렌즈의 오목함이 빛을 모으는 데 도움이 된다.|近义词：hollowness, depression|反义词：convexity, protuberance|名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *concavus, from Latin concavus (“hollow”), from com- (“together”) + cavus (“hollow”).
