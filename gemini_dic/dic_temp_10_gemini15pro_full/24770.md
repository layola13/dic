brookweed:/ˈbrʊkwid/ | n. |溪畔草，沼泽星宿菜|A small plant (Samolus valerandi) found in wet places, especially near streams. It has small white flowers and fleshy leaves. |The brookweed thrived in the damp soil along the riverbank. 溪畔草在河岸潮湿的土壤中茁壮生长。| Brookweed covered the marshy area. 溪畔草覆盖了沼泽地区。 | 近义词: water pimpernel, brooklime | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun
