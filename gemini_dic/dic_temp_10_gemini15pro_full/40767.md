convincedness:/kənˈvɪnstnɪs/
n.
确信，坚信；信服
The state of being convinced; firm belief.
His convincedness of her innocence was unshakeable. 他对她清白的坚信是不可动摇的。
近义词：conviction, certainty, assurance, confidence
反义词：doubt, uncertainty, skepticism
