nonscholarly:/ˌnɒnˈskɒləli/ (US) /ˌnɒnˈskɑːlərli/ (UK)| adj.|非学术的，不学究气的|Not scholarly; not characteristic of a scholar; not appropriate to serious academic study.|His writing style is engaging and nonscholarly, making it accessible to a wider audience. 他的写作风格引人入胜，不学究气，更容易被广大读者接受。

The book presents the information in a nonscholarly manner, focusing on practical application rather than theoretical discussion.  这本书以非学术的方式呈现信息，侧重于实际应用而不是理论讨论。

近义词：informal, popular, accessible, simple, unacademic|反义词：scholarly, academic, erudite, learned|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From non- + scholarly.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "scholarly" is an adjective meaning "characteristic of a scholar."
