diversifiable:/daɪˈvɜːrsɪfaɪəbl/
adjective
可分散的，可多样化的
able to be diversified or made more diverse.
例句：A diversifiable portfolio is less risky. 一个多元化的投资组合风险较低。
例句：The company's income streams are highly diversifiable. 这家公司的收入来源高度多元化。
近义词：variable, adaptable, flexible
反义词：non-diversifiable, concentrated, specialized
词性: adjective
