frithstool:/ˈfrɪθstuːl/
noun
庇护所，圣座，教堂中的寻求庇护的座位
a seat or chair in a church, offering sanctuary or protection to those fleeing from justice.
King Athelstan granted certain churches the right of frithstool.  埃塞尔斯坦国王授予某些教堂圣座权。
The fugitive sought sanctuary at the frithstool.  逃犯在圣座寻求庇护。
synonyms: sanctuary, asylum, refuge
antonyms:  danger, peril, exposure
