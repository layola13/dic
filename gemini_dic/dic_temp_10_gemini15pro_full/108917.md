leeboard:/ˈliːbɔːrd/| n.|活动中心板， leeboard(船的活动中心板)|A leeboard is a flat piece of wood, metal, or plastic attached to the side of a sailboat to prevent it from drifting sideways and to improve its ability to sail upwind.|The small sailboat used a leeboard instead of a fixed keel.|近义词：centerboard, daggerboard|反义词: keel (fixed)|词性:noun
