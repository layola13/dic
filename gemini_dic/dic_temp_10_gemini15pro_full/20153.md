biplane:/ˈbaɪˌpleɪn/| n. |双翼飞机|An airplane with two pairs of wings, one above the other.|The Red Baron flew a Fokker Dr.I, a famous triplane, not a biplane.  红男爵驾驶的是著名的三翼飞机福克Dr.I，而不是双翼飞机。

Biplanes were common in the early days of aviation. 早期航空时期，双翼飞机很常见。|近义词：aircraft, airplane|反义词: monoplane|词性:noun
