activated: /ˈæktɪveɪtɪd/
adjective
已激活的，启动的
Made active or operative; stimulated.
The alarm system is activated by movement.  该警报系统由运动激活。

The button activated the ejection seat.  这个按钮启动了弹射座椅。

近义词: triggered, stimulated, started, initiated, switched on
反义词: deactivated, dormant, inactive, inert
词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *activatus, past participle of activare “to make active,” from Latin activus “active” (from actus, past participle of agere “to do, drive, act”) + -atus, suffix forming participles.


USAGE->
The activated charcoal filter removes impurities from the water. =  活性炭过滤器去除水中的杂质。

The newly activated system experienced some initial glitches. =  新启动的系统最初出现了一些小故障。

The president activated the National Guard to respond to the disaster. = 总统启动了国民警卫队来应对这场灾难。

Once activated, the device will begin transmitting data. = 一旦激活，该设备将开始传输数据。

PHRASE->
newly activated = 新启动的
fully activated = 完全激活的
recently activated = 最近激活的
activated by = 由...激活
activated carbon = 活性炭
