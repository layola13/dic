chatroom: /ˈtʃætˌruːm/ (US), /ˈtʃætˌrʊm/ (UK)
n.
聊天室
A place, especially on the internet, where people can exchange messages about a particular topic.
People gathered in the chatroom to discuss the latest news.  人们聚集在聊天室讨论最新的新闻。
The online chatroom was buzzing with activity. 在线聊天室里热闹非凡。
