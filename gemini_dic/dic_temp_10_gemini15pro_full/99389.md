interdictor:/ˈɪntərˌdɪktər/| n.|禁止者，阻止者|A person or thing that interdicts or prohibits something.|The court appointed an interdictor to prevent the sale of the property. 法院指定了一名禁止者来阻止该房产的出售。|Synonyms: prohibitor, inhibitor, obstructor|Antonyms: facilitator, enabler|noun

interdict:/ˌɪntərˈdɪkt/| n. vt.|禁止，阻止，禁令|An authoritative prohibition; to officially forbid something.|The church interdicted the king from receiving the sacraments. 教会禁止国王领圣餐。|The interdict against smoking in public places is strictly enforced.  公共场所禁止吸烟的禁令得到了严格执行。|Synonyms: prohibit, forbid, ban, proscribe|Antonyms: permit, allow, authorize|noun, verb
