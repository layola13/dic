osculum:/ˈɒskjʊləm/ | n. |小孔; (海绵的) 出水孔 |A small mouth-like opening, especially one in a sponge or other invertebrate, through which water is expelled.| The sponge filters water through pores and expels it through the osculum.| 近义词: opening, pore, aperture | 反义词:  (没有明确的反义词，可以考虑 closed pore/opening) | 词性: noun
Etymology: From Latin *osculum*, diminutive of *os*, meaning "mouth".
