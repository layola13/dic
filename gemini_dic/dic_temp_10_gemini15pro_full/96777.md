inequitably:/ɪnˈɛkwɪtəbli/
adverb
不公平地，不公正地
in an unfair and unjust manner.
The resources were distributed inequitably among the community members.  这些资源在社区成员之间分配不公平。
The law was applied inequitably, favoring the wealthy. 这项法律适用不公平，偏袒富人。
近义词:unfairly, unjustly, disproportionately
反义词:equitably, fairly, justly
词性:adverb

词源:
From in- (not) + equitable (fair, just) + -ly (adverb suffix).  The root "equitable" comes from Latin "aequus" (equal, level, fair).
