ophicleide:/ˈɒfɪklaɪd/ | n. | 低音号，奥菲克莱德号 | A keyed brass instrument similar to a tuba, popular in the 19th century. | The ophicleide had a powerful sound and was often used in military bands.  奥菲克莱德号声音洪亮，经常用于军乐队。|  The ophicleide's sound filled the concert hall. 奥菲克莱德号的声音充满了音乐厅。 | synonym: tuba, bass horn | antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps piccolo or flute for contrasting high pitch) | Noun
