chamsin:/ˈkɑːmsɪn/ /ˈxɑːmsiːn/| n. |卡辛风，五旬节风|A hot, dry, and dusty southerly wind blowing in North Africa and the Middle East, especially in spring.|The chamsin blew relentlessly for three days, covering everything in a fine layer of dust. 卡辛风无情地刮了三天，万物都被一层细细的灰尘覆盖。|例句：The travelers took shelter from the oppressive chamsin. 旅行者们躲避着令人窒息的卡辛风。

例句：The chamsin made it difficult to breathe and see clearly. 卡辛风让人呼吸困难，视线模糊。

近义词：sirocco, khamsin|反义词:breeze|词性:noun
