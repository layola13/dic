celibatist:/ˈselɪbətɪst/ | n. | 独身主义者，禁欲者 | A person who abstains from marriage and sexual relations, especially for religious reasons. |  He lived as a celibatist for his entire adult life. 他成年后一直过着独身生活。| synonym:  single person, unmarried person, bachelor, spinster | antonym: married person | noun

celibate:/ˈselɪbət/ | adj. n. | 独身的，禁欲的；独身者，禁欲者 | Abstaining from marriage and sexual relations. | The monks took a vow of celibacy. 僧侣们立誓禁欲。|  synonym: abstinent, unmarried, chaste | antonym: married, promiscuous | adjective, noun
