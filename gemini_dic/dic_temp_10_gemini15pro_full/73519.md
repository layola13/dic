foreshore:/ˈfɔːrʃɔːr/ (US) /ˈfɔːʃɔː(r)/ (UK)
n.
前滩，潮间带
The part of a shore between high-water and low-water marks, or between the water and developed land.
The children were playing on the foreshore, searching for shells. 孩子们在前滩上玩耍，寻找贝壳。

The hotel has its own private foreshore. 这家酒店有自己的私人前滩。


近义词:beach, seashore, waterfront, tideland
反义词:hinterland, inland
词性:noun
