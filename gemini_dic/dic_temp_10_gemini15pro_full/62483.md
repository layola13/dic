endospore:/ˈɛndoʊspɔːr/ (US), /ˈɛndəspɔː(r)/ (UK)| n. | 内生孢子 | A resistant asexual spore that develops inside some bacterial cells.  It is formed in response to harsh environmental conditions and can survive extreme heat, cold, radiation, and lack of nutrients. | The bacterium *Bacillus subtilis* forms endospores under stressful conditions.  枯草芽孢杆菌在压力条件下形成内生孢子。

The discovery of endospores revolutionized food preservation techniques. 内生孢子的发现彻底改变了食品保存技术。| 近义词: spore | 反义词: vegetative cell | 词性: noun
