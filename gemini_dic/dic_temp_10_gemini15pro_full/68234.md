fairtown:/ˈfeərˌtaʊn/| n.|展览城镇；集市城镇|A town known for hosting fairs or exhibitions.  It can also be used figuratively to describe a place that seems ideal or utopian.|例句：The annual fair transformed the sleepy town into a bustling fairtown.  一年一度的集市把这个沉睡的小镇变成了一个熙熙攘攘的展览城镇。

例句：They described their community as a fairtown, where everyone was happy and prosperous. 他们把他们的社区描述成一个展览城镇，每个人都幸福而繁荣。|近义词：fairground, exhibition town, market town|反义词:ghost town|词性:noun
