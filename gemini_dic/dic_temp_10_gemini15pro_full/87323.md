hernon:/ˈhɜːrnən/ | n. | 苍鹭 | A large wading bird with long legs, a long S-shaped neck, and a long sharp bill. |例句：The hernon stood motionless in the shallows, waiting for a fish to swim by. 苍鹭一动不动地站在浅滩里，等待鱼儿游过。

例句：We saw a hernon take flight from the reeds. 我们看到一只苍鹭从芦苇丛中飞起。|近义词：heron |反义词: N/A |词性: noun


词源: From Middle English *hern, herne, from Old English *hearn, from Proto-Germanic *cranjō (“crane, heron”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵerh₂- (“to cry hoarsely”). Cognate with Scots hern (“heron”), West Frisian earn (“eagle”), Dutch reiger (“heron”), German Reiher (“heron”), Danish hejre (“heron”), Swedish häger (“heron”).
