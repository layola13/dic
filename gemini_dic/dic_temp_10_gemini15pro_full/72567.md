fluviatic:/fluːviˈætɪk/| adj.|河流的；生长于河流中的；由河流形成的|Of, relating to, or living in rivers.|例句：Fluviatic deposits are sediments laid down by rivers. 河流沉积物是由河流沉积下来的。

例句：The fluviatic ecosystem is rich in biodiversity. 河流生态系统生物多样性丰富。|近义词：riverine, riparian|反义词:marine, oceanic|词性:adjective

Etymology: From Latin *fluviaticus*, from fluvius (“river”).
