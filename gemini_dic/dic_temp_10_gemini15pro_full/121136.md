misdeliveries:/ˌmɪsdɪˈlɪvəriz/
n.
错误投递；投递错误
The act of delivering something to the wrong place or person.
Several customers complained about misdeliveries of their packages.  几位顾客抱怨他们的包裹投递错误。
近义词:  wrong deliveries, incorrect deliveries, delivery errors
反义词: correct deliveries, accurate deliveries
词性: 名词

词源:
mis- (prefix): wrongly; badly.  错误地；糟糕地。
deliver (verb): to take goods, letters, etc. to the person or people they have been sent to. 投递；递送。
-y (suffix): forming nouns.  构成名词。
