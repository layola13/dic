coercion:/koʊˈɜːrʃən/ | n. | 强迫，胁迫 | The confession was obtained under coercion.  供词是在胁迫下获得的。 | Example: He claimed the police had used coercion to obtain a confession. 他声称警方用胁迫手段获得了供认。

Example:  The government achieved its goals through coercion and intimidation. 政府通过强迫和恐吓达到了目的。 | Synonyms: compulsion, force, duress, intimidation, pressure | Antonyms: persuasion, freedom, liberty | noun
