axeman:/ˈæksmən/| n.|樵夫，斧头手，刽子手|A person who uses an axe, especially a lumberjack or a murderer. |The axeman chopped down the tall tree. 樵夫砍倒了那棵大树。

The police were searching for the axeman who had committed the gruesome murders. 警察正在搜捕犯下可怕谋杀案的刽子手。|近义词：lumberjack, woodcutter, logger, executioner|反义词: N/A|noun
