cold-stores:/ˈkəʊld stɔːz/ (UK) /ˈkoʊld stɔːrz/ (US)
n.|冷藏库；冷库|A building or room for storing perishable goods at low temperatures.|The company invested in new cold-stores to keep their produce fresh. 这家公司投资了新的冷库来保持产品的鲜度。|Synonyms: refrigerators, freezers, chillers|Antonyms: ovens, furnaces|noun

cold-store:/ˈkəʊld stɔː(r)/ (UK) /ˈkoʊld stɔːr/ (US)
v.|冷藏，冷冻|To store (something) at a low temperature.|They cold-store the meat to preserve it. 他们冷藏肉类以保存它。|Synonyms: refrigerate, freeze, chill|Antonyms: heat, warm|verb
