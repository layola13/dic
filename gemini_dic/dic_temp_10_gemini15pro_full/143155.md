outchatters:/ˌaʊtˈtʃætərz/
| v. |胜过…的谈话; 比…更健谈|
| talks more or chatters longer than someone else. |
| She outchattered everyone at the party, even the notorious gossip. 她在那场派对上比任何人都健谈，甚至超过了那个出了名的爱说闲话的人。|
| He outchattered his opponent in the debate, though not necessarily outsmarted him. 在辩论中，他说的比对手多，但这并不一定意味着他比对手聪明。|
| outtalk, outspeak |
