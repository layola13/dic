busier: /ˈbɪziər/ (US), /ˈbɪziə(r)/ (UK)| adj.| 更忙的 |Comparative form of "busy," meaning occupied with many activities or tasks.  It indicates a greater degree of activity than "busy."| She's much busier now that she has a second job. 她现在有了第二份工作，更忙了。

He's busier than usual this week. 他这周比平时更忙。|近义词：more occupied, more engaged |反义词：less busy, freer, idler |词性: adjective
词源: From Middle English *busier, *bisiere, from Old English *bysigere, comparative of bysig (“busy”).
