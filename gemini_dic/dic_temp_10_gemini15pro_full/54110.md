disembrace:/ˌdɪsɪmˈbreɪs/
verb
放弃，抛弃
To reject or abandon (a belief, cause, or way of life).
She disembraced her former religious beliefs. 她放弃了她以前的宗教信仰。
He disembraced the corporate world and started his own business. 他离开了企业界，开始自己创业。
近义词:reject, abandon, renounce, repudiate, forsake
反义词:embrace, accept, adopt, espouse
词性:verb

disembrace:/ˌdɪsɪmˈbreɪs/
noun
放弃，抛弃（的行为）
The act of rejecting or abandoning something.
His disembrace of materialism surprised his family. 他对物质主义的放弃令他的家人感到惊讶。
The artist's disembrace of traditional techniques led to a new style.  这位艺术家对传统技法的摒弃导致了一种新风格的诞生。
近义词:rejection, abandonment, renunciation
反义词:embrace, acceptance, adoption
词性:noun
