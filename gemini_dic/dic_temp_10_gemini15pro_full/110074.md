lichenivorous:/laɪˈkɛnɪvərəs/
adjective
食地衣的
Feeding on lichens.
Reindeer are lichenivorous animals, relying heavily on lichens for sustenance during winter months. 驯鹿是食地衣的动物，在冬季主要依靠地衣维持生命。
The snail is a lichenivorous creature, grazing on the lichens that grow on rocks and trees. 这只蜗牛是一种食地衣的生物，它以生长在岩石和树木上的地衣为食。
herbivorous, fungivorous
carnivorous
