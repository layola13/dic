enterlace: /ˌɛntərˈleɪs/ | v. |交织，缠绕，编结|To twist or twine together; interweave.|The branches of the trees enterlaced, forming a canopy overhead. 树枝交错，在头顶形成树冠。|近义词：interweave, intertwine, interlace, braid, plait|反义词: disentangle, separate|词性:动词

Etymology:
From Middle English *enterlasen, from Anglo-Norman *entrelacer, from Old French entrelacer (“to interlace”), from entre- (“between, among”) + lacer (“to lace, tie”).  The word ultimately derives from Latin laqueus (“noose, snare”).
