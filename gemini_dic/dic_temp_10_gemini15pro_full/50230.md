dependable:/dɪˈpɛndəbl/| adj.|可靠的，可信赖的|Someone or something that is dependable can be trusted to do what you need or expect.|例句：She is a dependable friend. 她是一个可靠的朋友。|例句：This car has proven to be very dependable. 这辆车已被证明非常可靠。|近义词：reliable, trustworthy, steadfast|反义词：unreliable, untrustworthy, fickle|词性:形容词
词源:From depend + -able.
