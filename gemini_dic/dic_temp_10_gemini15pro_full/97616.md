inharmonical: /ˌɪnhɑːrˈmɒnɪkəl/ (US) /ˌɪnhɑːˈmɒnɪkəl/ (UK)
adjective
不和谐的，不协调的
not harmonious; discordant
The inharmonical sounds of the construction site made it difficult to concentrate.  建筑工地不和谐的声音让人难以集中注意力。
The clash of colours in the painting created an inharmonical effect.  画作中颜色的冲突营造出一种不和谐的效果。
近义词: discordant, dissonant, jarring, unmelodious
反义词: harmonious, melodious, euphonious
词性: adjective

词源:
From in- + harmonical. Ultimately from Greek  ἁρμονικός (harmonikos).
