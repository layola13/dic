cabalism:/ˈkæbəlɪzəm/ /ˈkeɪbəlɪzəm/
n.
秘传主义；神秘主义；阴谋；秘密结社
Secret or hidden knowledge, especially of a mystical, esoteric, or occult nature; a secret plot or scheme.
The group's cabalism made it difficult for outsiders to understand their motives.  这个团体的秘传主义使外人很难理解他们的动机。
synonyms:  secrecy, intrigue, conspiracy, occultism, esotericism
antonyms:  openness, transparency, clarity
