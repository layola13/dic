damoclesian:/ˌdæməˈkliːʒən/
adj.
达摩克利斯之剑的；危险的；岌岌可危的
Impending or threatening like the sword of Damocles.
The company is facing a Damoclesian threat of bankruptcy.  这家公司面临着破产的达摩克利斯之剑般的威胁。
Synonyms: perilous, precarious, menacing, impending, looming
Antonyms: safe, secure, stable
词性: 形容词
