counted:/ˈkaʊntɪd/| v. |数过，计算过；重要，有价值|Past tense and past participle of "count".  It means something has been enumerated or considered significant. |例句：We counted the apples in the basket. 我们数了篮子里的苹果。|例句：Every vote counted in the election.  每一次投票在选举中都很重要。|近义词：tallied, enumerated, valued, mattered|反义词:ignored, disregarded|词性:动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *counten*, from Old French *conter*, from Vulgar Latin *computare* "to compute," from Latin *com* ("together") + *putare* ("to think, to reckon, to prune").
