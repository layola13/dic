alehouses:/ˈeɪlˌhaʊzɪz/
n.
啤酒屋；酒馆
A place where ale and other alcoholic beverages are sold and consumed.
The travelers stopped at an alehouse for a refreshing drink and a bite to eat. 旅行者们在一家啤酒屋停下来喝点饮料，吃点东西。
pubs, taverns, inns
bars, restaurants
