nyctitropism: /nɪkˈtɪtrəˌpɪzəm/ | n. | 向夜性，夜间运动 | The tendency of certain plants to turn or bend their leaves or flowers toward a source of darkness or away from a source of light during the night. |  The nyctitropism of the prayer plant is fascinating to watch as its leaves fold up at night.  祈祷植物的夜间运动令人着迷，因为它的叶子在夜间会折叠起来。 | 近义词: sleep movement | 反义词: phototropism | 词性: noun

nyctitropic: /ˌnɪktɪˈtrɒpɪk/ | adj. | 向夜性的 | Exhibiting or relating to nyctitropism. |  The nyctitropic movement of the leaves helps the plant conserve water during the night. 叶子的向夜性运动有助于植物在夜间保存水分。| 近义词: sleep movement related | 反义词: phototropic | 词性: adjective

词源: From Greek *nukti-* (night) + *tropos* (a turning).
