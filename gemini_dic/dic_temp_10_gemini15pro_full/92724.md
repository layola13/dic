ichnographic:/ˌɪknəˈɡræfɪk/ | adj. |平面图的，以平面图表示的|Representing a ground plan; of or relating to ichnography.  A drawing or representation of a horizontal section, as of a building.| The architect presented an ichnographic drawing of the new library. 建筑师展示了新图书馆的平面图。|Synonyms: planimetric, ground plan, floor plan|Antonyms: elevation, perspective|adjective
