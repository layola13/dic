iatromathematician:/aɪˌætroʊˌmæθəməˈtɪʃən/
noun
医数学家，医疗数学家
A person skilled in both medicine and mathematics, especially one who applies mathematical principles to medical problems.
Iatromathematicians are developing new models to predict the spread of infectious diseases.  医数学家正在开发新的模型来预测传染病的传播。
The iatromathematician used statistical analysis to identify risk factors for heart disease.  这位医数学家使用统计分析来确定心脏病的危险因素。
近义词: biostatistician, computational biologist
反义词:  (No direct antonym exists)
词性: n.

Etymology:
From Greek "iatros" (physician) + "mathematikos" (mathematical).
