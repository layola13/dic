heifer:/ˈhɛfər/| n.|小母牛|A young female cow that has not yet had a calf.|例句：The farmer bought a new heifer for his herd. 农民为他的牛群买了一头新的小母牛。

例句：The prize-winning heifer was sold for a high price.  那头获奖的小母牛以高价售出。|近义词：cow, bovine|反义词:bull, steer|noun
