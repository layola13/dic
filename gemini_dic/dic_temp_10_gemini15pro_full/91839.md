hypergrammaticalness:/ˌhaɪpərɡrəˈmætɪkəlnəs/ | n. |过度语法化；超语法化| The state of adhering too rigidly to grammatical rules, sometimes producing unnatural or awkward-sounding language.|  He exhibited hypergrammaticalness, correcting everyone's minor grammar slips. 他表现出过度语法化，纠正每个人细微的语法错误。|  His hypergrammaticalness made him sound like a robot. 他的过度语法化让他听起来像个机器人。|  pedantry, formalism | informality, colloquialism | noun
