overproficiently:/ˌōvərprəˈfɪʃəntli/
| adv. | 过于熟练地，能力过剩地 |  In a manner exceeding the requirements of proficiency; with excessive skill or expertise. | He played the piano piece overproficiently, making it sound almost mechanical. 他弹钢琴曲过于熟练，听起来几乎像机械的。| 近义词: expertly, masterfully, adeptly, consummately | 反义词: clumsily, awkwardly, ineptly, incompetently | 词性: 副词

overproficiently:/ˌōvərprəˈfɪʃəntli/
|词源| From "over" (excessive) + "proficient" (skilled) + "-ly" (adverbial suffix). "Proficient" comes from Latin "proficiens," present participle of "proficere" (to make progress). |
overproficiently:/ˌōvərprəˈfɪʃəntli/
|用法说明|  "Overproficiently" describes performing a task with a level of skill that surpasses what is necessary or expected.  It can sometimes carry a slightly negative connotation, suggesting that the excessive skill makes the performance seem unnatural or lacking in feeling. |
