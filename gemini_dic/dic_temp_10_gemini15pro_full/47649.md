debonairly:/ˌdɛbəˈnɛəli/
adverb
潇洒地；温文尔雅地
In a confident, stylish, and charming manner.
He debonairly strode into the room, a smile playing on his lips. 他潇洒地大步走进房间，嘴角挂着微笑。
He tipped his hat debonairly. 他潇洒地脱帽致意。

Synonyms: suavely, elegantly, gracefully, charmingly, urbane, sophisticatedly
Antonyms: awkwardly, clumsily, gracelessly, rudely, boorishly

Etymology: From French *débonnaire* "of good disposition," from *de* "of" + *bon aire* "good nature."
