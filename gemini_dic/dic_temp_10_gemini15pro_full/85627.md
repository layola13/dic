haulse:/hɔːls/ | v. | 拖，拉，拽 | To pull or drag something heavy with effort. |  He haulsed the heavy box across the floor. 他吃力地把沉重的箱子拖过地板。

The fishermen haulsed their nets onto the boat. 渔民们把他们的网拖到船上。 | 近义词: drag, pull, tug, heave | 反义词: push, shove | 词性: 动词


词源:  From Middle English *halsen*, *hailsen*, from Old Norse *halsa* (“to haul, pull, drag”). Cognate with Danish *halser* (“hauls”), Swedish *halsa* (“to hail, salute, call to”).
