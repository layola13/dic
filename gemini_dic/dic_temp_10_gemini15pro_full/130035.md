non-participant:/ˌnɒn pɑːˈtɪsɪpənt/ | n. | 非参与者；旁观者 |  A person who does not take part or participate in something. |  Example: He remained a non-participant in the discussion, preferring to observe. 他在讨论中仍然是一个旁观者，宁愿观察。

Example:  Many employees felt like non-participants in the decision-making process. 许多员工感觉自己在决策过程中没有参与感。| 近义词: observer, onlooker, bystander | 反义词: participant, member | 词性: 名词

non-participant:/ˌnɒn pɑːrˈtɪsɪpənt/ | adj. | 非参与的；旁观的 | Not participating or taking part in something. | Example:  She took a non-participant role in the project, offering only advice.  她在项目中扮演了不参与的角色，只提供建议。


Example: The study focused on the non-participant observation of children's play.  这项研究的重点是对儿童游戏的非参与式观察。 | 近义词: uninvolved, passive | 反义词: participating, active, involved | 词性: 形容词
