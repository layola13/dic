ETYMOLOGY->

From Middle English *glisteren, *glistren, from Old English *glisian (unattested), from Proto-Germanic *glīstraną (“to shine, glitter”), from Proto-Indo-European *ǵleys- (“to shine”). Cognate with Dutch glinsteren (“to glitter”), German glitzern (“to glitter”), Icelandic glísa (“to grin”).
