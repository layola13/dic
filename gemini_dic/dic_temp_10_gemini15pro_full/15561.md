bagreef: /ˈbæɡˌriːf/
noun
暗礁，礁脉
A submerged ridge of rock, sand, or coral near the surface of the water.
Sailors need to be aware of bagreefs to avoid running aground.  水手需要注意暗礁以避免搁浅。

The ship struck a bagreef and sank. 船撞上了暗礁沉没了。


近义词:reef, shoal
反义词:deep


词性:n.
