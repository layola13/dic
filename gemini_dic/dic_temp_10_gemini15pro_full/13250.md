ate-up:/eɪt ˈʌp/ | adj. | 着迷的；沉溺的；充满…的 |  Consumed by, obsessed with, or full of (a particular emotion or quality).  Derived from the verb "eat up" which means to consume entirely. The adjective form implies a complete absorption or immersion in something. |  He was ate-up with jealousy. 他充满了嫉妒。|  consumed, obsessed, engrossed | indifferent, apathetic | adjective

ate-up:/eɪt ˈʌp/ | adj. | 焦虑不安的; 紧张的 |  Worried, nervous, or anxious, often to an excessive degree.
