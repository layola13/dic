contractibility:/kənˌtrækˈtɪbɪləti/| n.|收缩性；挛缩性|The contractibility of muscle tissue allows for movement.|肌肉组织的收缩性使运动成为可能。|例句：The heart's contractibility is essential for pumping blood. 心脏的收缩性对于泵血至关重要。

例句：Loss of contractibility in the muscles can lead to serious health problems. 肌肉收缩性的丧失会导致严重的健康问题。

近义词：contractility, ability to contract|反义词: inextensibility, inability to contract|词性:noun

Etymology: From Late Latin *contractibilitas*, from Latin contract- (past participle stem of contrahere ‘draw together’) + -ibilitas (from -ibilis ‘-ible’ + -tas -ty).
