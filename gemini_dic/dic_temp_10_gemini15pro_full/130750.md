nonamendable:/ˌnɒnəˈmɛndəbl/
adjective
不可修改的，不能修正的
Not capable of being amended or altered.
The constitution is nonamendable in this respect.  在这方面，宪法是不可修改的。
The contract is nonamendable after it is signed. 合同一旦签署就不可修改。
近义词: unalterable, fixed, immutable, inalterable
反义词: amendable, alterable, modifiable
词性: adjective

词源:
From non- + amendable.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "amendable" comes from the Old French "amender," meaning "to correct or improve."
