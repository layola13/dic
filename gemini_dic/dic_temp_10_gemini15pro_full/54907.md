disposableness:/dɪˌspəʊzəbəlˈnɛs/
n.
可处置性，一次性，用完即弃
The quality of being designed to be thrown away after use.
Consumers are increasingly aware of the environmental impact of disposableness. 消费者越来越意识到一次性用品对环境的影响。

The disposableness of plastic bags contributes to pollution. 塑料袋的用完即弃性导致了污染。

Synonyms: expendability, transience, throwaway nature
Antonyms: durability, permanence, reusability
