divaricator:/ˈdaɪvəreɪkətər/| n. |分叉器；展肌；扩张器|A muscle that spreads apart or separates two parts, or a tool or instrument used to spread something open.|The surgeon used a divaricator to hold the incision open.  外科医生用扩张器撑开切口。|The divaricator muscle helps to open the vocal cords. 展肌有助于打开声带。|synonyms: spreader, retractor, separator|antonyms: constrictor, compressor|noun
