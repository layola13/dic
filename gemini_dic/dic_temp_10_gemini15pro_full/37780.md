compatriotic: /kəmˌpeɪtriˈɒtɪk/ /kəmˌpeɪtriˈɑːtɪk/
adjective
同胞的；爱国的
Sharing the same country of origin; patriotic.
例句：Compatriotic feelings united the exiles.  同胞情谊使流亡者团结起来。

例句：She felt a surge of compatriotic pride as the national anthem played.  国歌奏响时，她感到一股爱国自豪感涌上心头。

近义词：fellow, countryman, national
反义词：foreign, alien
词性：adjective

词源：
From com- (together) + patriotic.  由com-（共同）+ patriotic（爱国的）组成。
