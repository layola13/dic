awesomer:/ˈɔːsəmər/ | adj. | 更令人敬畏的，更棒的 |Comparative form of "awesome." Used to describe something that inspires greater awe or admiration than something else. | That concert was awesomer than the last one I went to. 那场音乐会比我上次去的更棒。|  This new phone is awesomer than my old one; it has so many cool features!  这款新手机比我的旧手机更棒；它有这么多酷炫的功能！|Synonyms: more awesome, more impressive, more amazing | Antonyms: less awesome, less impressive, less amazing | adjective
