impropriate:/ˌɪnəˈproʊpriət/ | adj. | 不合适的，不恰当的 | Not suitable or proper in the circumstances. | Her short skirt was inappropriate for a funeral.  她的短裙不适合参加葬礼。 | inappropriate attire is not allowed in the office.  办公室不允许穿着不合适的服装。 | 近义词：unsuitable, unfit, improper, indecent, unbecoming | 反义词：appropriate, suitable, proper, fitting, apt | 词性: adjective

词源:  Late Middle English (in the sense ‘not peculiar to, not restricted to a particular person or thing’): from in- ‘not’ + appropriate. The current sense dates from the mid 19th century.
