enhearten:/ɪnˈhɑːrtn/ /ɛnˈhɑːrtn/
verb
鼓励，鼓舞
To give courage or confidence to; cheer.
The coach's speech enheartened the team before the big game. 教练的讲话在大赛前鼓舞了球队。
The news of the successful surgery enheartened her family. 手术成功的消息鼓舞了她的家人。

近义词: encourage, inspire, cheer, uplift, embolden
反义词: discourage, dishearten, depress, deject
词性: verb
