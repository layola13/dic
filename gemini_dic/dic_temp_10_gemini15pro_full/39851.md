constabulary:/kənˈstæbjʊləri/ /ˈkɑːnstæbjʊˌleri/| n.|警察机关，警员(总称)|A body of constables or police officers.  A police force.|例句：The local constabulary was investigating the robbery. 当地警察正在调查这起抢劫案。|例句：The constabulary were out in force during the festival. 节日期间警察倾巢出动。|近义词：police, police force, law enforcement|反义词:criminals, lawbreakers|词性:noun

Etymology:
From Late Latin *constabularia, from Latin constabulus ("constable").
