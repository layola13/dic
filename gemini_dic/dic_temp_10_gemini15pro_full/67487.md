extra-illustration:/ˌekstrə ɪləˈstreɪʃən/
|n.|额外插图，补充说明|An extra illustration was added to the book to clarify the complex concept.|这本书添加了一幅额外的插图，以阐明这个复杂的概念。|Synonyms: supplementary illustration, additional illustration, further illustration|Antonyms: main illustration, primary illustration|noun

Etymology:
extra- (prefix): From Latin extra, meaning "outside," "beyond."
illustration (noun): From Late Latin illustratio, from Latin illustrare "light up, make bright, illuminate," figuratively "make clear, explain, elucidate."
