co-specific:/ˌkoʊ spɪˈsɪfɪk/| adj.|同种的，同物种的|Belonging to the same species.|例句：Co-specific competition is often more intense than competition between different species. 同种竞争通常比不同物种之间的竞争更激烈。

例句：Researchers studied the co-specific interactions of a group of monkeys. 研究人员研究了一群猴子的同种互动。|近义词：congeneric, conspecific|反义词:heterospecific|词性:形容词
