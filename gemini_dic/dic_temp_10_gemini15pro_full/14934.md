azoturia:/ˌeɪzoʊˈtjʊəriə/ /ˌæzəˈtjʊəriə/| n. |氮尿症，肌红蛋白尿症|A condition characterized by the presence of excessive amounts of nitrogenous substances, especially urea, in the urine. It is often associated with muscle damage and the release of myoglobin.|The horse developed azoturia after a period of intense exercise. 这匹马在剧烈运动后患上了氮尿症。|Synonyms: myoglobinuria, Monday morning disease, tying-up|Antonyms: None|Noun
