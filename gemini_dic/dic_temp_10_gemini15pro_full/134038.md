nonhysterical:/ˌnɒnɪˈstɛrɪkəl/
|adj.|
|非歇斯底里的；镇定的；冷静的|
|Not hysterical; calm and composed.|

例句：
She remained nonhysterical throughout the ordeal.
她在整个考验中保持镇定。

His nonhysterical response to the crisis was admirable.
他对危机的冷静反应令人钦佩。


近义词：calm, composed, cool, collected, level-headed, serene, tranquil
反义词：hysterical, frantic, agitated, distraught


词性:  adjective


词源：
non- (prefix meaning "not") + hysterical (from Greek hysterikos "of the womb," later associated with emotional outbursts)
