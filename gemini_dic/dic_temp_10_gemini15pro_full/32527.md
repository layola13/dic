chinwagging:/ˈtʃɪnwæɡɪŋ/
noun
闲聊，聊天
the act of chatting or gossiping, especially for a long time.
They spent the afternoon chinwagging about old times. 他们一下午都在聊过去的事情。
近义词：chatting, gossiping, talking, conversing
反义词：being silent, not talking
