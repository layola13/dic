conduciveness:/kənˈduːsɪvnəs/
n.
有利性，有助性
The quality or state of being conducive; the tendency to promote or assist.
The quiet atmosphere of the library is conducive to studying. 图書館安静的氛围有利于学习。
近义词: helpfulness, favorableness, advantageousness
反义词: unfavorableness, disadvantageousness, hindrance
词性: noun

conducive:/kənˈduːsɪv/
adj.
有利的，有益的，有助的
Tending to promote or assist; helpful.
Regular exercise is conducive to good health. 规律的运动有益于健康。
近义词: helpful, beneficial, advantageous
反义词: unhelpful, detrimental, disadvantageous
词性: adjective


词源:
From Latin *conducivus*, from conducere "to lead or bring together," from com- "together" + ducere "to lead."  The noun form "conduciveness" is derived from the adjective "conducive."
