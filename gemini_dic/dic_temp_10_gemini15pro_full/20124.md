bipartisan:/ˌbaɪˈpɑːrtɪzən/|/ˌbaɪˈpɑːrtəzn/| adj.|两党合作的；跨党派的|Involving the agreement or cooperation of two political parties that usually oppose each other's policies.|例句：The bill received bipartisan support in Congress. 这项法案在国会获得了 bipartisan support in Congress. 这项法案在国会获得了两党的支持。

例句：A bipartisan committee was formed to investigate the scandal.  一个两党合作的委员会成立来调查这个丑闻。|近义词：nonpartisan, unified, joint, collaborative|反义词：partisan, one-sided|词性:形容词
