outrush:/ˈaʊtrʌʃ/| n.|涌出，冲出，流出|A sudden rapid flow out, especially of a gas or liquid.|There was a sudden outrush of air from the punctured tire. 轮胎被刺破后，突然有一股气体冲了出来。|近义词：surge, outburst, eruption, gush|反义词：influx, inflow|词性:名词

Etymology: out- + rush.  "out-" denotes outward movement, while "rush" implies a swift, powerful movement.
