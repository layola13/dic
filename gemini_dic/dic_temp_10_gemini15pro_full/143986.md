outranks:/ˌaʊtˈræŋks/| v. | 比…等级高，比…地位高，胜过 | To have a higher rank or position than someone or something else. |  A colonel outranks a major. 上校的级别高于少校。

The new law outranks any previous legislation on this matter. 新法律优先于之前关于此事的任何立法。 | 近义词：surpasses, exceeds, transcends, outstrips, betters | 反义词：is outranked by, is subordinate to, is inferior to | 词性：动词
