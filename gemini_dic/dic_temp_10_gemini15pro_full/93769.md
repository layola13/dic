immedicable:/ɪmˈmedɪkəbl/
adj.
不可治愈的，无法治疗的
Not capable of being cured or healed.
The disease was deemed immedicable, leaving the patient with little hope.  这种疾病被认为是无法治愈的，病人几乎没有希望了。
Her grief was immedicable after the loss of her child.  失去孩子后，她的悲痛无法治愈。
近义词: incurable, irremediable, untreatable
反义词: curable, treatable, remediable
词性: adjective

词源:
From Late Latin *immedicabilis, from Latin in- "not" + medicabilis "curable," from medicari "to heal," from medicus "physician."
