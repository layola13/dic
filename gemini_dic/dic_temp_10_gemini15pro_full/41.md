a-team: /ˈeɪ tiːm/
n.
A-Team, 特种部队
A group of highly skilled and effective individuals.  指一群技艺高超、效率极高的人。
The company assembled an a-team of engineers to develop the new product. 公司组建了一支由工程师组成的特种部队来开发新产品。
近义词: crack team, elite team, task force
反义词: incompetent team, weak team
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the 1980s television series "The A-Team," about a group of former Special Forces soldiers working as mercenaries.  源自20世纪80年代的电视剧《A特工队》，讲述了一群前特种部队士兵作为雇佣兵的故事。
