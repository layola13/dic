myeloidin:/ˈmaɪəlɔɪdɪn/
noun
类髓素，髓鞘质
A nitrogenous substance obtained from the protoplasm of certain fungi.  A horny or chitinous substance forming the outer layer of the cell-wall of certain fungi.
Myeloidin makes up the cell walls of some fungi.  某些真菌的细胞壁由类髓素构成。
chitin, sclerotin
None

词性: n.
