evading:/ɪˈveɪdɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
逃避;躲避
Avoiding or escaping from someone or something.
He was charged with evading taxes. 他被指控逃税。

evading:/ɪˈveɪdɪŋ/
verb (present participle)
规避;回避
Avoiding dealing with or accepting something.
She's been evading my questions all day. 她一整天都在回避我的问题。


近义词: dodging, eluding, escaping, avoiding, shirking
反义词: confronting, facing, meeting, encountering
词性: Verb (present participle of evade)
