ornithichnology:/ˌɔːrnɪθɪkˈnɒlədʒi/
n.
鸟迹学
The study of bird tracks and other trace fossils left by birds.
Ornithichnology can provide valuable insights into the behavior and evolution of extinct bird species. 鸟迹学可以为了解灭绝鸟类的行为和进化提供宝贵的见解。
