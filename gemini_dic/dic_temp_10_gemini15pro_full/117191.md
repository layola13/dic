measured:/ˈmeʒərd/| adj.|慎重的，仔细斟酌的；精确测量的；有分寸的| carefully considered; deliberate and restrained; accurately measured|例句：He spoke in a measured tone. 他说话的语气很谨慎。|近义词：calculated, deliberate, careful, precise|反义词：rash, impulsive, careless, inaccurate|词性:形容词

Etymology: From Middle English *mesuren*, from Old French *mesurer*, from Latin *mēnsūrāre* ("to measure"), from *mēnsūra* ("measure").
