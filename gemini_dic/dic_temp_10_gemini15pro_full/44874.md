cry-baby:/ˈkraɪˌbeɪbi/| n.|爱哭鬼，爱抱怨的人|A person, especially a child, who cries easily or complains frequently, often for trivial reasons.|例句：Stop being such a cry-baby and deal with it! 别像个爱哭鬼一样，去处理它！

例句：He's a real cry-baby when he doesn't get his way.  当他得不到自己想要的东西时，他就是一个真正的爱哭鬼。

近义词：whiner, complainer|反义词: stoic|词性: noun
