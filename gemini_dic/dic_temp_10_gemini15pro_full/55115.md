disrupt:/dɪsˈrʌpt/ | v. | 打断，扰乱，使中断 | To interrupt the normal progress or activity of something. | The loud noise disrupted my concentration. 吵闹的声音打断了我的注意力。 | 近义词: interrupt, disturb, interfere with | 反义词: organize, order, facilitate | verb

disrupted:/dɪsˈrʌptɪd/ | adj. | 中断的，混乱的，瓦解的 | Having been interrupted or disturbed; thrown into disorder. | The disrupted train schedule caused widespread delays.  混乱的列车时刻表导致了大范围的延误。 | 近义词: interrupted, disturbed, disordered | 反义词: organized, ordered, stable | adjective
