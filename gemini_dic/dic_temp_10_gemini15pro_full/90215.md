houndstooths:/ˈhaʊndzˌtuːθs/
noun
犬牙纹，千鸟格
a woven twill fabric with a small broken or jagged check pattern.
The jacket was a classic houndstooth. 这件夹克是经典的千鸟格。
Synonyms: dogtooth, check, pied-de-poule
Antonyms: solid, plain
n.
