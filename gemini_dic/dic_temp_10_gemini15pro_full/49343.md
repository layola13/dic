delineates:/dɪˈlɪnieɪts/
verb
描述；勾勒；阐明
to describe, portray, or set forth the limits of something accurately
The report clearly delineates the responsibilities of each team member. 这份报告清晰地描述了每个团队成员的职责。
The artist delineated the features of the model with meticulous care.  这位艺术家一丝不苟地勾勒了模特的面部特征。

synonym: describes, outlines, defines, portrays, sketches
antonym: obscures, confuses, distorts

词性: verb
