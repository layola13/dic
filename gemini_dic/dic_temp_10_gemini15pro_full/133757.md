nongarrulousness:/ˌnɒnɡəˈruːləsnəs/ | n. | 寡言少语；沉默寡言 | The state of being not talkative or given to excessive chatter.  | Her nongarrulousness made her seem mysterious. 她沉默寡言，让人觉得她很神秘。 | Example: The monk's nongarrulousness was interpreted as wisdom.  这位僧侣的沉默寡言被理解为智慧。|Synonyms: reticence, taciturnity, silence, reserve | Antonyms: garrulousness, loquacity, talkativeness | noun
