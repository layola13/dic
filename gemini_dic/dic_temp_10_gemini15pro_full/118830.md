metapeptone:/ˈmɛtəˌpɛptəʊn/| n. |蛋白胨|A partially hydrolyzed protein product formed during digestion or by the action of acids or enzymes on proteins. It is soluble in water and is not coagulated by heat.|The scientist added metapeptone to the growth medium for the bacteria. 科学家在细菌生长培养基中添加了蛋白胨。|Metapeptone is used in various laboratory settings as a nutrient source for microorganisms. 蛋白胨在各种实验室环境中用作微生物的营养来源。|Synonyms: peptone, proteose|Antonyms: protein|Noun
