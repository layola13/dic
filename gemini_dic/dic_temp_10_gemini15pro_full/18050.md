begrudge:/bɪˈɡrʌdʒ/| vt.|吝惜，嫉妒，勉强给|to give reluctantly or resentfully; to envy someone for possessing or enjoying something|例句：I don't begrudge her success. 我不嫉妒她的成功。

例句：He begrudges every penny he spends. 他花的每一分钱都舍不得。

近义词：envy, resent, grudge|反义词：grant, give freely,  be happy for|词性：动词
