bondslave:/ˈbɒndsleɪv/ | n. |奴隶，被束缚的人|A person entirely enslaved to another, or to a passion or influence.  A person who is completely controlled by something, such as a bad habit or an emotion.| He was a bondslave to his ambition, working day and night to achieve his goals. 他是雄心壮志的奴隶，日夜工作以实现他的目标。| synonym: slave, captive, thrall | antonym: freeman, master | noun
