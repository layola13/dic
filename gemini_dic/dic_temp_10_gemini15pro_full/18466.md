bemused:/bɪˈmjuːzd/| adj.|困惑的，茫然的，发呆的|Characterized by a state of being confused, bewildered, or lost in thought; preoccupied.|She sat bemused, staring out the window. 她茫然地坐着，凝视着窗外。|例句：He was bemused by the sudden change in plans.  他被计划的突然改变弄得茫然了。

例句：The complicated instructions left her feeling utterly bemused. 复杂的说明让她完全不知所措。

|近义词：confused, bewildered, puzzled, perplexed, lost in thought|反义词：clear-headed, alert, focused|词性:形容词
