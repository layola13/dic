equalising:/ˈiːkwəlaɪzɪŋ/
verb
使均等；使相等
The government is trying to equalise educational opportunities. 政府正在努力使教育机会均等。
Present participle of equalise.  是动词equalise的现在分词形式。
They are equalising the workload between the two departments. 他们正在使两个部门的工作量均等。
近义词: levelling, balancing, evening out
反义词: differentiating,  uneven
词性: Verb (present participle)


equalise:/ˈiːkwəlaɪz/
verb
使均等；使相等
The new regulations will equalise the playing field for smaller businesses. 新规将为小型企业创造公平的竞争环境。
To make equal. 使...相等。
They equalised the scores in the last minute of the game. 他们在比赛的最后一分钟扳平了比分。
近义词: level, balance, even out
反义词: differentiate
词性: Verb
