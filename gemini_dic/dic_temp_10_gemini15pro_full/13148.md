asymptomatically:/ˌeɪsɪmptəˈmætɪkli/ | adv. | 无症状地 | Without showing or producing symptoms. |  Many people carry the virus asymptomatically and unknowingly spread it to others. 许多人无症状地携带病毒，并在不知不觉中传播给他人。|  He was infected with COVID-19 but remained asymptomatically for several weeks. 他感染了新冠病毒，但几周来一直没有症状。| Near synonyms: silently, subclinically, inapparently | Antonyms: symptomatically, obviously, visibly | 词性: 副词
