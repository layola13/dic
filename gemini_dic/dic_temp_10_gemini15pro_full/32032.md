cherished:/ˈtʃɛrɪʃt/| adj.|珍爱的，珍视的|Deeply loved and valued.|例句：The old woman cherished the memory of her late husband. 老妇人珍视着她已故丈夫的记忆。

例句：These photographs are my most cherished possessions. 这些照片是我最珍爱的财产。|近义词：treasured, beloved, prized, valued, adored|反义词：despised, scorned, neglected, unappreciated|词性:形容词

词源: From Middle English *cherissen, from Old French cheriss-, present participle stem of cherir "to hold dear," from Late Latin *carīre "to hold dear," from Latin cārus "dear, precious."
