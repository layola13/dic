cruive:/kruːv/
n.|鱼梁，捕鱼围栏|A cruive is a fixed trap for catching fish, typically built of stones and stakes across a stream.|The salmon were caught in the cruive. 鲑鱼在鱼梁中被捕获。|Synonyms: fish weir, fish trap, kiddle|Antonyms: None|noun

cruive:/kruːv/
v.|用鱼梁捕鱼|To catch fish using a cruive.|They cruived salmon in the river. 他们在河里用鱼梁捕鲑鱼。|Synonyms: trap, snare|Antonyms: release, free|verb
