conversely:/ˈkɒnvɜːsli/ /kənˈvɜːrsli/| adv.|相反地，反过来|On the one hand, they'd love to have kids; conversely, they don't want to give up their freedom. 一方面，他们想要孩子；另一方面，他们又不想放弃自由。|例句：He was poor but happy; conversely, his brother was rich but miserable. 他很穷但很快乐；相反，他的兄弟很富有却很痛苦。

例句：The hotel was expensive, and conversely, the food was quite average. 这家酒店很贵，相反地，食物却很一般。|近义词：on the other hand, in contrast, on the contrary|反义词：similarly, likewise|词性:副词
