fabricated:/ˈfæbrɪkeɪtɪd/
adjective
捏造的，伪造的，虚构的
Constructed, invented, or made up, often with deceitful intent.
The evidence he presented was completely fabricated. 他提供的证据完全是伪造的。

fabricated:/ˈfæbrɪkeɪtɪd/
verb (past participle)
制造，装配
To construct, manufacture, or build something.
The company fabricates steel components for bridges. 这家公司制造桥梁的钢构件。


近义词:forged, falsified, invented, manufactured, constructed, built
反义词:genuine, authentic, real, true
词性: adjective, verb (past participle)
