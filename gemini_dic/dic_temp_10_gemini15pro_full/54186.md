disestablished:/ˌdɪsɪˈstæblɪʃt/
adjective
被剥夺既定地位的；被取消建制的；不再受官方支持的
Deprived of its established status, especially as the official church of a state.
The Church of England was disestablished in Wales in 1920.  威尔士教会于1920年被取消了国教地位。
The government disestablished the national airline. 政府取消了国家航空公司的官方地位。
近义词: abolished, revoked, withdrawn
反义词: established, instituted
词性: adjective
