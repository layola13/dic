McGonagall: /məˈɡɒnəɡəl/ (British) /məˈɡɑːnəɡæl/ (American) | n. | 麦格教授 |  A fictional character, Minerva McGonagall, is a professor at Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry in the Harry Potter series. She is known for her strictness, intelligence, and transfiguration skills. | Example: Professor McGonagall transformed herself into a cat. 麦格教授把自己变成了一只猫。| Synonym: teacher, professor, headmistress (sometimes) | Antonym: student, pupil | Noun
