assessorial: /əˈsɛsɔːriəl/ /əˈsɛsəriəl/
| adj. | 估价的；辅助的；附属的 | Relating to an assessment, especially of property value; supplementary; auxiliary. | The assessorial charges for the shipment included insurance and handling fees. 这批货物的估价费用包括保险费和手续费。 |  近义词: supplementary, auxiliary, additional, ancillary | 反义词: primary, main, principal | 词性: 形容词

assessorial: /əˈsɛsɔːriəl/ /əˈsɛsəriəl/
| n. | 附加物；附件 | Something added, especially to a main item; an accessory. |  The dress came with several assessorials, including a belt and a scarf. 这件连衣裙配有几件附件，包括一条腰带和一条围巾。 | 近义词: accessory, adjunct, appendage, supplement | 反义词:  base, core, foundation| 词性: 名词


词源: Late Middle English: from medieval Latin *assessorius, from Latin assessor ‘assistant judge,’ from assess- ‘sat beside.’
