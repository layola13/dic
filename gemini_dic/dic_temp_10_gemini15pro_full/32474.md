chinch:/tʃɪntʃ/ | n. |臭虫，床虱|A small, wingless, bloodsucking insect (Cimex lectularius) that infests houses and especially beds, feeding on human blood. |例句：The hotel room was infested with chinches.  酒店房间里到处都是臭虫。

例句：She woke up covered in chinch bites. 她醒来时身上满是臭虫叮咬的痕迹。|近义词：bedbug |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

chinch:/tʃɪntʃ/ | v. |(非正式)敲诈，勒索|To cheat or swindle someone out of money.  |例句：He chinched me out of ten dollars. 他骗了我十块钱。

例句：They were chinched by the street vendor. 他们被街头小贩敲诈了。 |近义词：swindle, cheat, defraud |反义词: N/A |词性: verb
