defying:/dɪˈfaɪɪŋ/| adj.|违抗的，蔑视的，挑战的|Resisting or refusing to obey. |例句：The mountaineers were defying the blizzard to reach the summit. 登山者不顾暴风雪，力图登上峰顶。

例句：Her defiant attitude towards authority caused her many problems. 她对权威的蔑视态度给她带来了许多麻烦。|近义词：resisting, opposing, disobeying, challenging|反义词：obeying, complying, submitting|词性:形容词

词源: From Old French *desfier, from des- "dis-" + fier "to trust, confide in" (from Latin fidere). The original sense was "to renounce faith in, distrust, challenge".
