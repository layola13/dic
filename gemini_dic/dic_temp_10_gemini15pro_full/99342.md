interdeceive: /ˌɪntərdiˈsiːv/
verb
互相欺骗
To deceive one another; to practice mutual deception.
The two spies interdeceived each other, each believing they were getting the upper hand. 两个间谍互相欺骗，每个人都认为自己占了上风。
The con artists interdeceived their victims and each other in a complex web of lies. 骗子们在一个复杂的谎言网络中互相欺骗他们的受害者和彼此。
近义词: mislead, delude, trick, double-cross
反义词: be honest, tell the truth
