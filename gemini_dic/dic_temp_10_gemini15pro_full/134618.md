nonlyric:/nɒnˈlɪrɪk/ | adj. | 非抒情的；无歌词的 |  Not lyrical; lacking the qualities of a lyric; not adapted to song.  It describes something, usually a piece of music or poetry, that doesn't express personal emotions or feelings in a song-like way. |  The composer is best known for his nonlyric piano pieces. 作曲家最出名的是他的非抒情钢琴曲。| synonyms: non-melodic, instrumental, unsentimental | antonyms: lyrical, melodic, sentimental | adjective
