enquiry: /ɪnˈkwaɪəri/ (UK), /ˈɪnkwəri/ (US) | n. | 询问，查询 | An act of asking for information. |  I made an enquiry about train times. 我询问了火车时刻表。

 I've had a number of enquiries about the job. 我收到了很多关于这份工作的询问。

近义词: inquiry, question, query | 反义词: answer, reply, response | 词性: 名词

inquiry: /ɪnˈkwaɪəri/ (UK), /ˈɪnkwəri/ (US) | n. | 询问，查询 | An act of asking for information. | I made an inquiry about the price of the car. 我询问了这辆车的价格。

 The police are conducting an inquiry into the robbery. 警方正在对这起抢劫案进行调查。

近义词: enquiry, question, query | 反义词: answer, reply, response | 词性: 名词

PHRASE->
make an enquiry = 提出询问
enquiry about =  关于...的询问
enquiry into = 对...的调查
further enquiry = 进一步的询问
receive an enquiry = 收到询问


词源:  From Old French enquerre, from Latin inquirere "to seek into, search for," from in- "into" + quaerere "to seek, ask."


注意事项:
英式英语中更常用enquiry，美式英语中更常用inquiry，但两者意思相同，可以互换使用。
