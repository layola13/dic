bimensal:/baɪˈmɛnsəl/
adjective
双月的；一月两次的
Occurring every two months or twice a month.
The bimensal magazine is published every two months. 这本双月刊每两个月出版一次。
The company sends out bimensal newsletters to its employees. 公司每两个月向员工发送一次简报。
近义词: semimonthly, twice-monthly
反义词: annual, yearly
词性: adjective

bimensal:/baɪˈmɛnsəl/
noun
双月刊
A publication issued every two months.
She subscribes to several bimensals. 她订阅了几份双月刊。
The bimensal is a valuable resource for industry professionals. 这本双月刊是业内人士的宝贵资源。
近义词: semimonthly publication
反义词: annual publication
词性: noun
