lepismoid:/ˈlɛpɪsmɔɪd/| adj.|鳞状的，像鳞片的|Resembling or having the form of a scale; scaly.  Relating to or characteristic of the order Lepismoidea, which includes silverfish and firebrats.|The plant's leaves have a lepismoid texture.  这种植物的叶子具有鳞状结构。|scaly, squamate|smooth, glabrous|adjective
