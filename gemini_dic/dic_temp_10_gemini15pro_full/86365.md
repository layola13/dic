hectoringly:/ˈhɛktərɪŋli/ | adv. | 霸道地，威吓地 | In a bullying or intimidating manner, characterized by bluster and domineering behavior. | "He spoke to the staff hectoringly, demanding immediate results." 他霸道地对员工说话，要求立即出结果。 | Example: The coach hectoringly berated the players after their loss. 教练在球队输球后严厉斥责了球员们。| Synonyms: bullyingly, domineeringly, overbearingly, imperiously | Antonyms: gently, kindly, mildly, politely | 词性: 副词

hector:/ˈhɛktər/ | v. | 霸道地对待，威吓 | To intimidate or bully someone. | "She hectored her younger brother into doing her chores." 她威吓她弟弟替她做家务。| Example:  He was known to hector his employees into working overtime. 他以威吓员工加班而闻名。 | Synonyms: bully, intimidate, browbeat, badger | Antonyms: encourage, support, soothe, comfort | 词性: 动词
