faery:/ˈfeɪəri/ (US), /ˈfɛːri/ (UK)| n. adj.|仙女，仙子；仙境的|A mythical being of folk tales, typically having magical powers, often in the form of a small person with wings.  Sometimes used as an adjective to describe something related to fairies.|例句：The faery queen ruled over the enchanted forest. 仙女女王统治着魔法森林。

例句：She had a faery-like quality about her. 她有一种仙女般的气质。|近义词：fairy, sprite, pixie, elf|反义词:demon, monster|词性:noun, adjective

词源:
Middle English: from Old French faerie (noun), fae (adjective), from Latin fata (plural) ‘the Fates’, from fatum ‘fate’.
