astern:/əˈstɜːrn/| adv., adj.|在船尾，向船尾|At, toward, or in the stern (rear) of a ship or boat.  Situated behind a ship. |The sailor threw the rope astern. 水手把绳子扔向船尾。|The tugboat followed astern. 拖船在后面跟着。|近义词：abaft, behind|反义词：ahead, forward|词性:副词, 形容词
Etymology: From Old English *æftan stearn "after the steering oar," composed of æftan (from Proto-Germanic *after "after") + stearn (from Proto-Germanic *sturnan "to steer").
