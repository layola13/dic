Lombards: /ˈlɒmbɑːdz/ (UK), /ˈlɑːmbɑːrdz/ (US)| n.|伦巴第人；伦巴第族|A member of a Germanic people who conquered most of northern Italy in the 6th century.|The Lombards established a kingdom in Italy. 伦巴第人在意大利建立了一个王国。|Near synonyms:  Germanic tribes, barbarians |Antonym: Italians (in the context of the 6th-century conquest) | Noun

Lombard: /ˈlɒmbɑːd/ (UK), /ˈlɑːmbɑːrd/ (US)| adj.|伦巴第的；伦巴第人的|Relating to the Lombards or their language. |The Lombard language is now extinct. 伦巴第语现在已经灭绝了。|Near synonyms: Langobardic |Antonym:  N/A | Adjective


Etymology: From Old High German *Langobardi, meaning "long beards."
