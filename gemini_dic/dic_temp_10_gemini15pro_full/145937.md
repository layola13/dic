overdiscourage:/ˌoʊvər dɪˈskʌrɪdʒ/| v.|过度劝退，使极度灰心|To discourage excessively or to an extreme degree.  The constant criticism from his boss overdiscouraged him and he eventually quit.|例句：The repeated failures overdiscouraged the team.  反复的失败让团队极度灰心。

例句：He was overdiscouraged by the lack of support from his family. 家人缺乏支持让他非常沮丧。|近义词：demoralize, dishearten, dispirit|反义词: encourage, hearten, inspire|词性:动词
