loadstone:/ˈloʊdˌstoʊn/| n. |磁石，天然磁铁|A piece of magnetite or other naturally magnetized mineral, able to attract iron.  A thing that attracts strongly.|例句：The loadstone drew the iron filings towards it. 磁石把铁屑吸了过来。

例句：Her charisma was a loadstone, drawing people to her. 她的魅力像磁石一样，吸引着人们靠近她。|近义词：magnet, lodestone|反义词: None (no direct opposite for a naturally occurring magnetic material)|词性:noun
