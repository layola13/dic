incivilly:/ɪnˈsɪvɪlli/
adverb
不文明地；粗鲁地
in a rude or uncivil manner.
He behaved incivilly towards the waiter. 他对服务员的态度很粗鲁。
近义词:rudely, impolitely, disrespectfully, discourteously
反义词:politely, respectfully, courteously, civilly
词性:副词

词源:
From in- (not) + civilly (in a civil manner).  Ultimately from Latin *civis* (citizen).
