depressingly:/dɪˈprɛsɪŋli/
adverb
令人沮丧地
in a way that causes someone to feel depressed or makes something seem depressing.
The rain continued depressingly all day. 雨令人沮丧地下了一整天。
近义词：dismally, gloomily, sadly
反义词：cheerfully, happily, joyfully
词性：副词

词源: From *depress* (verb) + *-ing* (present participle suffix) + *-ly* (adverb suffix).  *Depress* comes from Latin *depressus*, past participle of *deprimere* "to press down," from *de-* "down" + *premere* "to press."
