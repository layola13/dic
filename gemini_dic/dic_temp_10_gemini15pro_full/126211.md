PHRASE->
1. mix up: 弄混; 混合  Example: I mixed up the sugar and the salt. 我把糖和盐弄混了。
2. mix with: 与...交往;  与...混合 Example: Oil and water don't mix. 油和水不相容。
3. mix in:  掺入; 加入 Example: Mix in the flour gradually. 逐渐加入面粉。
4. mix it up:  打架; 争吵 Example:  They were about to mix it up when the police arrived. 他们正要打架，警察就到了。
5.  mix and match:  混合搭配 Example:  You can mix and match these clothes to create different outfits. 你可以混合搭配这些衣服来创造不同的造型。


词源:
From Old English *mixan (inferred from miscian "to mix"), from Proto-Germanic *miskjanan, from the root *meik- "to mix."  The noun sense "mixture, blend" is from the 18th century.
