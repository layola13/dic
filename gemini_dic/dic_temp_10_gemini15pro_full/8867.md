antifeudalistic:/ˌæntiˌfjuːdəlˈɪstɪk/
| adj. | 反封建的 | Opposed to or seeking to abolish feudalism. | The antifeudalistic movement gained momentum in the 18th century.  18世纪反封建运动势头强劲。|  The government implemented antifeudalistic policies. 政府实施了反封建政策。 | 近义词: anti-feudal, progressive, revolutionary | 反义词: feudal, reactionary, conservative | 词性: 形容词

Etymology:
anti- (prefix): against, opposed to (from Greek *anti)
feudal (root): relating to feudalism (from Medieval Latin feudalis)
-istic (suffix): forming adjectives from nouns, often indicating a characteristic or doctrine
