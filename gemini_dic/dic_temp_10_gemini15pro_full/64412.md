ergo:/ˈɜːrɡoʊ/ /ˈɜːɡəʊ/| adv.|因此，所以|Therefore, consequently.  It indicates a logical conclusion. |The evidence is clear, ergo, the defendant is guilty. 证据确凿，因此，被告有罪。|近义词：therefore, hence, thus, consequently, accordingly|反义词:  (No direct antonym, but could consider words implying contrary conclusions like "however," "nevertheless," "yet," "but")|词性:副词
詞源: From Latin *ergō*, ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *h₁ergʰ- (“to work, to move”). Cognate with Greek ἔργον (*érgon*, “work”).
