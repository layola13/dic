melolonthidian:/ˌmɛləˈlɒnθɪdiən/ | adj. n.|金龟子科的；金龟子科的昆虫|Relating to or belonging to the family Melolonthidae, a family of beetles commonly known as chafers.|例句：The melolonthidian grubs are destructive to plant roots. 金龟子科幼虫对植物根部有破坏性。

例句：The farmer was concerned about the melolonthidian infestation in his field. 农民担心他的田地里金龟子科昆虫的侵扰。|近义词：chafer, scarab|反义词: N/A |词性: adjective, noun


Etymology:  From Modern Latin *Melolonthidae, from Melolontha (type genus), from Greek melolonthe 'cockchafer'.
