noses:/ˈnoʊzɪz/ | n. |鼻子（复数）|The plural form of "nose," referring to the olfactory organ on the face. |例句：Dogs have excellent noses. 狗的鼻子很灵敏。

例句：My noses are running because of the cold. 我因为感冒流鼻涕了。|近义词：schnozzes (informal), nostrils (specifically the openings), beaks (informal, often for birds) |反义词: N/A |词性: noun

Etymology: From Old English nosu, of Germanic origin; related to Dutch neus and German Nase.
