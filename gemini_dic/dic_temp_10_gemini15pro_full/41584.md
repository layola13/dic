corporealization:/kɔːrˈpɔːriəlaɪˈzeɪʃn/
n.
实体化，具体化
The act of giving a physical form to something that is not material; embodiment.
The corporealization of an idea into a physical product requires careful planning and execution. 将一个想法实体化为一个实体产品需要仔细的计划和执行。

corporealization:/kɔːrˈpɔːriəlaɪˈzeɪʃn/
n.

The act of becoming material or bodily; assuming a physical form.
The ghost's corporealization frightened the children. 鬼魂的物质化吓坏了孩子们。

corporealization:/kɔːrˈpɔːriəlaɪˈzeɪʃn/
n.
实现，具体表现
A concrete or tangible manifestation of something abstract.
The artist's painting was a corporealization of her emotions. 艺术家的画是她情感的具体表现。


近义词:embodiment, materialization, realization, manifestation, incarnation
