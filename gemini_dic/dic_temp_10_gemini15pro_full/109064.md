legalizer:/ˈliːɡəlaɪzər/
n.
合法化者；使合法的人（或事物）
A person or thing that legalizes something.
The governor signed the bill, becoming a legalizer of marijuana in the state. 州长签署了该法案，使大麻在该州合法化。
Synonyms: legitimizer, authorizer
Antonyms: criminalizer, outlaw
noun

legalize:/ˈliːɡəlaɪz/
vt.
使合法化；使合法
To make something legal.
Many countries have legalized same-sex marriage. 许多国家已经将同性婚姻合法化。
Synonyms: legitimize, authorize, decriminalize
Antonyms: criminalize, prohibit, outlaw
verb
