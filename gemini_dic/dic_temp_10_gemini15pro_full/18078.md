behalf:/bɪˈhæf/ /bɪˈhɑːf/| n.|利益，代表|A person's behalf refers to their interest or representation.|She spoke on my behalf at the meeting. 她代表我在会议上发言。|synonyms: interest, benefit, support|antonyms: detriment, disservice|noun
behalves:/bɪˈhævz/ /bɪˈhɑːvz/| n.|利益，代表（behalf的复数形式，现在很少使用）|Behalves is the plural form of behalf and is rarely used in modern English.  It refers to the interests or representation of multiple people. |In days of old, knights often fought on the behalves of their lords.  (Archaic usage)  在过去，骑士经常为他们的领主的利益而战。|synonyms: interests, benefits|antonyms: detriments, disservices|noun
