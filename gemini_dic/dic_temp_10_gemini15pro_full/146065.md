overearly:/ˌoʊvərˈɜːrli/ | adv. | 过早地，过早成熟地 | Too early; prematurely. | The fruit ripened overearly due to the unusually warm weather. 由于异常温暖的天气，水果过早成熟。| 近义词: prematurely, too early | 反义词: late, tardily | 词性: 副词

overearly: /ˌoʊvərˈɜːrli/ | adj. | 过早的，早熟的 | Happening or ripening too early. |  The overearly harvest left many of the crops underdeveloped. 过早的收获导致许多作物发育不良。 | 近义词: precocious, premature | 反义词: late, delayed | 词性: 形容词
