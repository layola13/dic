normativist:/ˈnɔːrmətɪvɪst/ | n. | 规范主义者；规范论者 |  Someone who believes that there are objective norms or standards of behavior, thought, or language, and that deviations from these norms are incorrect or undesirable.  |  The normativist argued that the new slang was a corruption of the language.  规范主义者认为新的俚语是对语言的败坏。| 近义词: prescriptivist, traditionalist | 反义词: descriptivist | 词性: noun

normative:/ˈnɔːrmətɪv/ | adj. | 规范的；标准的 | Relating to or prescribing norms or standards. |  Normative ethics deals with questions of right and wrong. 规范伦理学处理对与错的问题。 | 近义词: standard, prescriptive | 反义词: descriptive | 词性: adjective
