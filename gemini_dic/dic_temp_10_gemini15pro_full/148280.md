oversoaks:/ˌōvərˈsōks/
verb
浸透；过度浸泡
to soak something excessively; to saturate something beyond what is necessary or desirable.
The gardener oversoaked the plants, and they began to wilt. 园丁把植物浇得太多了，它们开始枯萎了。
oversaturates, waterlogs, douses
underwaters
v.
