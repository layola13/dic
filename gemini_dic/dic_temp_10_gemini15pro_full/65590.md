evaginate:/iˈvædʒɪneɪt/ | v. | 外翻，翻出 | To turn or be turned inside out; to cause (an organ or part) to be turned inside out. | The biologist observed the sea anemone evaginate its tentacles to capture prey. 生物学家观察到海葵翻出触手来捕捉猎物。 | The surgeon had to evaginate the patient's stomach to examine the lining.  外科医生不得不将病人的胃翻出来检查内壁。 |  Synonyms: protrude, extrude, evert | Antonyms: invaginate, introvert | verb

evagination:/iˌvædʒɪˈneɪʃən/ | n. | 外翻，翻出 | The act or process of evaginating; the state of being evaginated. | The evagination of the intestine was a serious complication. 肠道外翻是一个严重的并发症。| The evagination of the balloon allowed it to be easily packed away. 气球的放气让它很容易被收起来。| Synonyms: protrusion, extrusion, eversion | Antonyms: invagination, introversion | noun
