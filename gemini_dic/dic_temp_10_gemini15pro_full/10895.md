araeosystyle:/ˌɛəriˈɒsɪstaɪl/| adj.|疏柱式的，柱间距宽的|Characterized by wide spacing between columns, typically four or more times the diameter of the columns.  Relating to a classical architectural style where the columns are spaced widely apart.|The temple was built in the araeosystyle, giving it a sense of spaciousness.|近义词:widely spaced, open, airy|反义词:pycnostyle, closely spaced|词性:adjective

词源: From Greek *araióstylοs*  < *araiós* (rare, thin) + *stŷlos* (column).
