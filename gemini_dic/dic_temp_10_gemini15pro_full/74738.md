freestylers:/ˈfriːstaɪlərz/
noun
自由式选手；即兴表演者
People who improvise, especially in music, dance, or sports.
The freestylers wowed the crowd with their impressive skills. 自由式选手们以其令人印象深刻的技巧让观众惊叹。
Freestylers often create unique and exciting performances. 自由式选手经常创作独特而精彩的表演。
Synonyms: improvisers, ad-libbers
Antonyms: planners, traditionalists
