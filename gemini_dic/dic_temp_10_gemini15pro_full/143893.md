outpouring:/ˈaʊtpɔːrɪŋ/
n.
流露；倾泻；迸发
A sudden release of strong feelings or a large amount of something.
There has been an outpouring of grief since his death. 自他去世以来，人们悲痛不已。
近义词：effusion, outburst, surge, flood, torrent
反义词：suppression, withholding, restraint
词性：名词
