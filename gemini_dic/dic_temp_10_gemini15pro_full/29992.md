cavish:/ˈkeɪvɪʃ/| adj.|吹毛求疵的，挑剔的，难以取悦的|Inclined to make petty or unnecessary objections; fault-finding.|He was so cavish about the food that he sent it back three times. 他对食物太挑剔了，把菜退了三次。|近义词：critical, fastidious, picky, hypercritical, carping, faultfinding, censorious|反义词：uncritical, accepting, tolerant, easygoing|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Middle English *cavechen*, probably frequentative of *caven* (“to puff out, gasp”), from Old Norse *kafa* (“to dive, sink”). The original sense was probably “to raise trivial objections, carp.”
