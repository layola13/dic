exhaustions:/ɪɡˈzɔːsʃənz/ /ɪɡˈzɑːsʃənz/| n. |精疲力竭，疲惫不堪|The state of being completely tired out or worn down, either physically or mentally.|She collapsed from sheer exhaustion after the marathon. 她跑完马拉松后累得虚脱了。| The team was suffering from exhaustion after working for three days straight.  团队连续工作三天后疲惫不堪。 |Near synonyms: fatigue, tiredness, weariness, burnout |Near antonyms: energy, vigor, vitality| noun
