heartbreak: /ˈhɑːrtbreɪk/ (US) /ˈhɑːtbreɪk/ (UK)| n.| 心碎，极度悲伤|Overwhelming sadness, extreme sorrow, typically caused by romantic loss or other emotional trauma.| She suffered terrible heartbreak after the death of her husband. 她丈夫去世后，她遭受了极大的悲痛。| synonyms: grief, sorrow, anguish, devastation, despair| antonyms: joy, happiness, elation| noun

heartbreak: /ˈhɑːrtbreɪk/ (US) /ˈhɑːtbreɪk/ (UK)| adj.| 令人心碎的|Causing overwhelming sadness or sorrow.| The film's ending was truly heartbreaking.  这部电影的结局真是令人心碎。| synonyms: devastating, tragic, sorrowful, distressing| antonyms: joyful, happy, uplifting| adjective
