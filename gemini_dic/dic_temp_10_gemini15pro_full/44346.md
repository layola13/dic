cross-action:/ˈkrɒs ˌækʃən/ (US) /ˈkrɔːs ˌækʃən/ (UK)| n.|交叉诉讼; 交叉行为; 交叉作用|A lawsuit brought by one defendant against another defendant in the same suit, or a lawsuit brought by one plaintiff against another plaintiff in the same suit.  It also refers to an interaction or interference between different elements or processes.|例句：The defendant filed a cross-action against the co-defendant, claiming joint negligence.  被告对共同被告提起了交叉诉讼，声称共同过失。

例句：The cross-action complicated the already complex legal proceedings. 交叉诉讼使本来就很复杂的法律程序更加复杂了。|近义词：countersuit, counterclaim|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


cross-action:/ˈkrɒs ˌækʃən/ (US) /ˈkrɔːs ˌækʃən/ (UK)| adj.|交叉的; 交互的|Describing something that involves interaction or interference between different elements or processes.|例句：The report analyzed the cross-action of various economic factors. 该报告分析了各种经济因素的交叉作用。


例句：The scientists studied the cross-action of the two chemicals. 科学家们研究了这两种化学物质的交叉作用。|近义词：interacting, interconnected, interdependent|反义词: independent, separate|词性:adjective
