impetuosity:/ˌɪmpɛtʃuˈɑːsəti/ /ˌɪmpɛtʃuˈɒsəti/| n.|冲动，鲁莽，轻率|The quality of being impetuous; rash or impulsive action.  A tendency to act quickly and without careful thought.|Her impetuous nature led her to make hasty decisions. 她的冲动性格使她做出了仓促的决定。|Synonyms: impulsiveness, rashness, recklessness, hastiness, precipitateness|Antonyms: caution, prudence, deliberation, circumspection|noun
Etymology: Late Middle English: from late Latin *impetuositas, from Latin impetuosus ‘impetuous’.
