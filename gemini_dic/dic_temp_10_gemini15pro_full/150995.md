paramounts:/ˈpærəmaʊnts/| n.|首要，至高无上，最重要的东西|Something of paramount importance is more important than anything else.  至关重要的事情比其他任何事情都重要。|例句：The interests of the child are paramount.  孩子的利益至上。

例句：National security is paramount. 国家安全至关重要。

近义词：supreme, chief, principal, dominant, overriding, primary

反义词：minor, secondary, insignificant, trivial

词性:名词 (通常用复数形式，但意义不变)


词源:

From Old French *paramont (“above”), from par (“by, through”) + amont (“upward, uphill”). Cognate with Italian paramount (“paramount”).
