couturiers:/kuːˈtʊrieɪz/
n.
时装设计师；女装设计师
Fashion designers, especially of expensive, fashionable clothes.
The couturiers of Paris are renowned for their exquisite designs. 巴黎的时装设计师以其精美的设计而闻名。
Synonyms: designers, dressmakers, stylists
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "mass producer" in a contextual sense)
