chalks:/tʃɔːks/ | n. |粉笔；白垩| Pieces of chalk used for writing or drawing, typically on a blackboard.  A soft, white, powdery limestone consisting mainly of calcium carbonate, formed from the skeletal remains of marine organisms. |  The teacher used colored chalks to draw a diagram on the blackboard. 老师用彩色粉笔在黑板上画了一个图表。 | synonyms:crayons, pastels | antonyms: markers, pens | noun

chalk:/tʃɔːk/ | vt. |用粉笔写或画；记录；归咎于 | To write or draw with chalk; to record something, as with chalk on a slate; to attribute something to a particular cause. | She chalked the scores on the board. 她在黑板上用粉笔写下了分数。 He chalked his success up to hard work. 他把他的成功归功于努力工作。 | synonyms: write, draw, record, ascribe, attribute | antonyms: erase, delete | verb
