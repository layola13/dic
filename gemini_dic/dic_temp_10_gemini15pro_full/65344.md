eulogic: /juːˈlɒdʒɪk/ /juːˈlɑːdʒɪk/
adjective
赞美的；颂扬的；歌功颂德的
Praising someone or something very highly.
He delivered a eulogic speech about the retiring CEO. 他发表了歌颂这位退休首席执行官的讲话。
The film received eulogic reviews. 这部电影获得了赞誉如潮的评价。

近义词: laudatory, commendatory, complimentary, praising
反义词: critical, derogatory, defamatory
词性: adjective


词源:
From Greek eulogia "praise, blessing,"  from eu- "well" + -logia  "speaking," related to legein "to speak."
