histrionicism:/ˌhɪstriˈɑːnɪsɪzəm/
n.
做作；装腔作势；夸张
Exaggerated dramatic behavior designed to attract attention.
Her histrionicism was evident in her loud sobs and dramatic pronouncements. 她的装腔作势表现在她大声的哭泣和夸张的言辞中。
synonyms: theatricality, dramatics, melodrama, affectation, show, pretension
antonyms: sincerity, genuineness, understatement, reserve, modesty
noun


histrionic:/ˌhɪstriˈɑːnɪk/
adj.
装腔作势的；矫揉造作的
Overly theatrical or melodramatic in character or style.
He tends towards histrionic displays of emotion when he doesn't get his own way. 当他得不到自己想要的东西时，他往往会夸张地表现出情绪。
synonyms: theatrical, dramatic, melodramatic, affected, stagy, showy
antonyms: sincere, genuine, understated, reserved, modest
adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *histrionicus, from Latin histrio (“actor”), from Etruscan.
