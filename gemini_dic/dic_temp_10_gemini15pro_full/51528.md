detrude:/dɪˈtruːd/
verb
推下；挤出；强迫退位
To thrust or push down or outward; to displace.
The invading army detruded the rightful king from his throne. 入侵的军队将合法国王从王位上赶了下来。
近义词:expel, oust, displace, eject
反义词:insert, introduce, instate
词性:v.
