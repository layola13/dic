overpensively: /ˌoʊvərˈpɛnsɪvli/ | adv. | 沉思地，忧郁地 | in a way that shows you are thinking deeply and seriously, especially about something that worries you | She stared overpensively out the window. 她忧郁地望着窗外。|  近义词: thoughtfully, meditatively, contemplatively | 反义词: carelessly, thoughtlessly | 词性: 副词

overpensive: /ˌoʊvərˈpɛnsɪv/ | adj. | 沉思的，忧郁的 | thinking deeply and seriously, especially about something that worries you | He had an overpensive look on his face. 他脸上带着沉思的表情。| 近义词: thoughtful, meditative, contemplative | 反义词: carefree, lighthearted | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From over + pensive.  "Over-" intensifies the meaning of "pensive," suggesting excessive or deep thoughtfulness, often tinged with sadness or worry.  "Pensive" itself comes from Old French "pensif," ultimately from Latin "pensare" (to weigh, consider).
