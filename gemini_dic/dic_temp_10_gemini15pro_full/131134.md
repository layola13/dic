nonblended:/ˌnɒnˈblɛndɪd/ | adj. | 未混合的，未掺杂的 | Not blended; pure, unmixed.  Referring to something that has not been combined with other substances or elements. | The chef insisted on using nonblended spices for the dish. 厨师坚持用未混合的香料做这道菜。| Example:  She prefers nonblended coffee, just pure Arabica beans. 她更喜欢不混合的咖啡，只用纯阿拉比卡咖啡豆。 | Near Synonyms: pure, unmixed, single-origin, straight | Antonyms: blended, mixed, combined | Adjective
