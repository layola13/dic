furness:/ˈfɜːrnɪs/| n.|熔炉，火炉，壁炉|a metal container in which metals or other materials are melted at a very high temperature; a closed fireplace, especially a large one in a house or castle|The blacksmith worked diligently at his furness. 铁匠在他的熔炉旁辛勤地工作着。|Synonyms: furnace, forge, fireplace|Antonyms: cooler, refrigerator|noun


furness:/ˈfɜːrnɪs/| n.|福纳斯（英国地名）|a peninsula and former district of Lancashire, now part of Cumbria in north-west England|We visited Furness Abbey on our trip. 我们在旅行中参观了福纳斯修道院。|Synonyms: (as a place name, no direct synonyms)|Antonyms: (as a place name, no direct synonyms)|noun
