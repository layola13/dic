licheniform:/laɪˈkɛnɪfɔːrm/
adjective
地衣状的，类似地衣的
Resembling a lichen in appearance or growth form.
The crustose growth on the rock was licheniform. 岩石上硬壳状的生长物呈地衣状。
近义词: lichenous, crustose
反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider terms like "smooth," "non-crusty" depending on the specific context)
词性: adjective
