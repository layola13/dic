lungie:/ˈlʌŋi/| n.| 男子沙滩裤，大裤衩 (主要指澳大利亚和新西兰) |A pair of loose shorts or trousers worn by men, especially in Australia and New Zealand. |例句：He threw on his lungies and headed to the beach. 他穿上他的沙滩裤去了海滩。|例句：It's too hot for jeans; I'm going to wear my lungies. 天气太热了，不适合穿牛仔裤；我要穿我的大裤衩。|近义词：boardshorts, shorts, swim trunks|反义词:trousers, pants|词性:noun
