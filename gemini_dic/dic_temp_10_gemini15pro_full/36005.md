codical:/ˈkɒdɪk(ə)l/
adjective
法典的；属于法典的
Relating to or contained in a codex (a book, especially an ancient manuscript text).
The codical evidence for the early development of the legal system is fragmentary.  早期法律体系发展的法典证据是零碎的。
近义词:codex, manuscript, textual
反义词:non-codicial, unwritten
