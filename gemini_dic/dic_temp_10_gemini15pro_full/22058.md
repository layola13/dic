boleti:/bəˈleti/ /boʊˈleti/ | n. |牛肝菌|A large group of fleshy fungi of the family Boletaceae, many of which are edible.|例句：We found a cluster of boleti growing under the oak tree. 我们在橡树下发现了一丛牛肝菌。

例句：Some species of boleti are poisonous, so it's important to identify them correctly before eating. 有些种类的牛肝菌有毒，所以在食用前正确识别它们很重要。|近义词：mushrooms, fungi, cep, porcini |反义词: N/A |词性: Noun
