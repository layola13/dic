embastardize:/ɪmˈbæstərdaɪz/
verb
使…变质，使…堕落；掺杂，弄杂
To corrupt or debase; to contaminate or adulterate.
The pure teachings were embastardized over time by incorporating local superstitions.  随着时间的推移，纯正的教义因融入了当地的迷信而变质。
They embastardized the language with slang and jargon. 他们用俚语和行话使语言变得粗俗。
近义词:debase, corrupt, degrade, contaminate, adulterate
反义词:purify, refine, elevate, enhance, improve
词性:v.

embastardization:/ɪmˌbæstərdaɪˈzeɪʃən/
noun
堕落；变质；掺杂
The act or process of embastardizing.
The embastardization of the original recipe resulted in a less flavorful dish. 原食谱的变质导致菜肴味道变差。
He lamented the embastardization of traditional music. 他对传统音乐的堕落表示惋惜。
近义词:corruption, degradation, contamination, adulteration
反义词:purification, refinement, elevation, enhancement, improvement
词性:n.
