myrmotherine:/ˌmɜːrməˈθɜːraɪn/| adj.|蚁鸟的；与蚁鸟有关的|Relating to or resembling antbirds, a family of passerine birds found in tropical Central and South America, known for following army ant swarms.|The myrmotherine species followed the ants, picking off fleeing insects.  这种蚁鸟跟随着蚂蚁，捕食逃窜的昆虫。|近义词:formicarian, antbird-like|反义词:None|词性:形容词
