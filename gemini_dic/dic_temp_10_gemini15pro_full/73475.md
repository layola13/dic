forepurpose:/ˈfɔːrpɜːrpəs/| n.|预定目的，预先设定的目标|A forepurpose is a purpose or intention formed beforehand; a premeditated plan.  It suggests a deliberate and conscious decision made in advance.|He entered the competition with the forepurpose of winning. 他参加比赛的预定目的是获胜。|近义词：intention, aim, objective, goal, plan|反义词: accident, chance, spontaneity|词性:noun

Etymology:
fore- (prefix): before, beforehand (from Old English *fore)
purpose (noun): the reason for which something is done or created or for which something exists. (from Old French porpos)
