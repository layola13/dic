nonspiritual:/ˌnɒnˈspɪrɪtʃuəl/
adjective
非精神的; 世俗的
Not spiritual; worldly.  Relating to material things rather than to the needs of the human spirit.
She led a very nonspiritual life, focused on business and material possessions. 她过着非常世俗的生活，专注于生意和物质财富。
He preferred nonspiritual pursuits like hiking and photography. 他更喜欢徒步旅行和摄影等非精神追求。
近义词:worldly, secular, material, earthly, mundane
反义词:spiritual, religious, divine, sacred, holy
词性:adjective
