installs:/ɪnˈstɔːlz/
verb
安装;设置
sets up or fixes equipment or furniture so that it is ready to be used
The technician installs the new software on my computer.  技术人员在我的电脑上安装了新软件。
He installs the new air conditioner in the bedroom. 他在卧室里安装了新的空调。
Synonyms: sets up, fits, mounts, positions
Antonyms: uninstalls, removes, dismantles
词性:v.
