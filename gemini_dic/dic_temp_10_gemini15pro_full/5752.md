amacratic: /ˌæməˈkrætɪk/ | adj. | 无统治的；无政府主义的；不受任何政府管辖的 | Relating to or advocating amacracy, a state of society without government or law. |  例句：They envisioned an amacratic society where individuals lived in harmony without external control.  他们设想了一个没有外部控制、个人和谐共处的无政府社会。

例句：The amacratic commune operated on principles of shared responsibility and mutual respect. 这个无政府主义的公社以共同责任和互相尊重的原则运作。 | 近义词：anarchic, lawless, ungoverned | 反义词：governed, ruled, controlled | 词性: adjective

词源:  From Greek *amakratos* "weak, powerless," from *a-* "without" + *kratos* "power, strength."
