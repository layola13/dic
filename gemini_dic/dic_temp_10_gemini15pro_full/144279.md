outspan:/ˈaʊtspæn/| v.|超越，胜过，超过|To exceed in scope or range. |例句：His influence outspans that of his predecessors. 他的影响力超过了他的前任们。

例句：The company's operations outspan several continents.  这家公司的业务遍及多个大洲。|近义词：exceed, surpass, outstrip, transcend|反义词: fall short of, lag behind|词性: 动词
