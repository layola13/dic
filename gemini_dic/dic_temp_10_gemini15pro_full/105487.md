kills:/kɪlz/
verb
杀死，弄死
causes the death of; puts to death
The virus kills thousands of people every year. 这种病毒每年导致数千人死亡。
synonyms: slays, murders, assassinates, executes, exterminates
antonyms: saves, rescues, revives, protects
