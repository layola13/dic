deoperculate: /diːoʊˈpɜːrkjʊleɪt/
verb
除去盖子；开盖
to remove the operculum or covering from something, especially the gill cover of a fish or the lid of a spore case in mosses.
The biologists carefully deoperculated the fish to examine its gills. 生物学家小心地打开鱼鳃盖检查它的鳃。
The moss's spore capsules deoperculate when the spores are mature. 当孢子成熟时，苔藓的孢子囊就会打开。
近义词: uncover, open, expose
反义词: cover, close, conceal
词性:v.

Etymology:
From Latin *deoperculatus*, past participle of *deoperculare* "to uncover," from *de-* "from, away from" + *operculum* "lid, cover" (from *operire* "to cover").
