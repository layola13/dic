cocksureness:/ˌkɒkˈʃʊənəs/ (UK), /ˌkɑːkˈʃʊrnəs/ (US)| n.| 过分自信；自以为是 |The state of being excessively confident, often to the point of arrogance.  It implies a certainty that is not justified by the circumstances.|He spoke with a cocksureness that irritated everyone in the room. 他说话的语气非常自以为是，激怒了房间里的每一个人。|近义词：arrogance, overconfidence, presumptuousness, self-assurance|反义词：humility, modesty, diffidence, insecurity|词性: noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From "cock" (meaning a rooster, known for its boldness) + "sure" + "-ness" (suffix forming nouns of quality or state).  The image is of a rooster's strutting confidence.
