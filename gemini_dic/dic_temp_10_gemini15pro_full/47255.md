/wɒt/ (UK) /wʌt/ (US)| pron. det. int. adv.|什么，多么|Used to ask for information specifying something.|例句：What is your name? 你叫什么名字？|近义词：which|反义词: (No single antonym)|pronoun, determiner, interjection, adverb


USAGE->

一般现在时 (Simple Present):
What do you eat for breakfast? = 你早餐吃什么？
What does she do for a living? = 她做什么工作？

现在进行时 (Present Continuous):
What are you watching on TV? = 你在电视上看什么？
What is he cooking for dinner? = 他晚饭在做什么？

现在完成时 (Present Perfect):
What have you learned today? = 你今天学到了什么？
What has she bought at the store? = 她在商店买了什么？

现在完成进行时 (Present Perfect Continuous):
What have you been working on all week? = 你整个星期都在做什么？
What has he been reading lately? = 他最近一直在读什么？

一般过去时 (Simple Past):
What did you do yesterday? = 你昨天做了什么？
What did she say about the movie? = 她对这部电影说了什么？

过去进行时 (Past Continuous):
What were you doing when the phone rang? = 电话响的时候你在做什么？
What was she thinking about during the meeting? = 开会时她在想什么？

过去完成时 (Past Perfect):
What had you eaten before you came here? = 你来这里之前吃了什么？
What had she done before she left? = 她离开之前做了什么？

过去完成进行时 (Past Perfect Continuous):
What had you been doing before I interrupted you? = 我打断你之前你一直在做什么？
What had they been arguing about for so long? = 他们一直在争论什么这么久？

一般将来时 (Simple Future):
What will you do after graduation? = 毕业后你会做什么？
What will the weather be like tomorrow? = 明天天气会怎么样？

将来进行时 (Future Continuous):
What will you be doing this time next week? = 下周这个时候你会在做什么？
What will they be presenting at the conference? = 他们在会议上将要展示什么？

将来完成时 (Future Perfect):
What will you have achieved by the end of the year? = 到年底你将完成什么？
What will they have finished by tomorrow? = 到明天他们将完成什么？

将来完成进行时 (Future Perfect Continuous):
What will you have been studying for five years by then? = 到那时你将已经学习什么五年了？
What will she have been working on by the deadline? = 到截止日期前她将一直在做什么？


虚拟语气:
If I knew what to say, I would tell you. = 如果我知道该说什么，我会告诉你。
I wish I knew what she was thinking. = 我希望我知道她在想什么。

被动语态:
What was discussed at the meeting? = 会议上讨论了什么？
What is being built next to the library? = 图书馆旁边正在建什么？

疑问句:
What is the capital of France? = 法国的首都是什么？
What are your plans for the weekend? = 你周末有什么计划？

否定句:
I don't know what he wants. = 我不知道他想要什么。
She doesn't understand what I'm saying. = 她不明白我在说什么。


PHRASE->
What's up? = 怎么了？/ 你好吗？
What the hell! = 搞什么鬼！
What if we go to the beach? = 如果我们去海滩怎么样？
What for? = 为了什么？
No matter what happens, I'll be there. = 无论发生什么，我都会在那里。
