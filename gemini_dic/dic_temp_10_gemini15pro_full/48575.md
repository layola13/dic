defeasance:/dɪˈfiːzəns/ | n. |  废止；失效；解除；偿还 | The act of rendering something null and void; annulment.  Specifically, the discharge of a debt or obligation. |  The defeasance of the mortgage allowed them to sell the property.  抵押的解除允许他们出售房产。 | synonyms: annulment, cancellation, revocation, invalidation, discharge | antonyms: validation, enactment, establishment | noun

defeasance:/dɪˈfiːzəns/ | adj. | 可废止的 | Capable of being annulled or rendered void. |  The contract contained a defeasance clause. 合同包含一个废止条款。| synonyms: voidable, revocable, annullable | antonyms: irrevocable, permanent, binding | adjective
