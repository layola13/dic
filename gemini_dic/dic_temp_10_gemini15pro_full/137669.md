nonunisons:/nɒnˈjuːnɪsənz/
noun
非齐奏；不一致的声音
Instances or occurrences of sounds or musical notes not being in unison.
The piece featured a complex interplay of nonunisons, creating a rich and textured soundscape. 这首曲子以复杂的非齐奏交织为特色，创造出丰富而有质感的音景。
The nonunisons in the choir created a dissonant effect. 合唱团的非齐奏产生了不和谐的效果。
Synonyms: disharmony, discordance, cacophony
Antonyms: unison, harmony, concord
n.

ETYMOLOGY->
From "non-" (not) + "unison" (sounding together). "Unison" comes from Latin "unus" (one) + "sonus" (sound).
