mariolatry:/ˌmɛəriˈɒlətri/ | n. | 圣母崇拜；对圣母玛利亚的过度崇敬 | Excessive veneration of the Virgin Mary. |  The priest condemned mariolatry as a form of idolatry.  神父谴责圣母崇拜是一种偶像崇拜的形式。

Mariolatry is not accepted by all Christian denominations. 不是所有的基督教派都接受圣母崇拜。 | 近义词:  Mary-worship, hyperdulia (specifically in Catholic context) | 反义词: iconoclasm (反对偶像崇拜),  disregard for Marian doctrines | 词性: noun
