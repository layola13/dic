coacervates:/koʊˈæsərveɪts/| n. |凝聚层;团聚体|A collection of colloidal droplets held together by electrostatic attractive forces.|Coacervates may have played a significant role in the origin of life. 凝聚层可能在生命的起源中扮演了重要角色。|Synonyms: aggregates, clusters|Antonyms: dispersions, solutions|noun

coacervate:/koʊˈæsərveɪt/| v. |凝聚;聚集成团|To form into coacervates.|Certain organic molecules can coacervate under specific conditions.  某些有机分子在特定条件下可以凝聚。|Synonyms: aggregate, coalesce|Antonyms: disperse, dissolve|verb
