minimart:/ˈmɪnimɑːrt/| n. |小型超市，便利店|A small supermarket or convenience store, usually open long hours and selling a limited range of groceries, snacks, and other everyday items.|I stopped at the minimart to grab a bottle of water. 我在便利店停下来买了一瓶水。|近义词：convenience store, corner store, bodega (US), newsagent's (UK)|反义词: supermarket, hypermarket|noun
