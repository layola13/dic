|近义词：always, forever, ever|

aye:/aɪ/ (chiefly nautical)
| n.|
赞成票；投赞成票的人
|An affirmative vote or voter.|
|The ayes have it.|赞成票占多数。
|近义词：yes vote, affirmative vote|
|反义词：no vote, negative vote|
|词性: noun|


Etymology: From Middle English *ai, *ei, from Old Norse *ei (“ever”), from Proto-Germanic *aiwīną (“always, ever”).
