corruptive:/kəˈrʌptɪv/
adjective
腐败的；堕落的；贪污的
Tending to corrupt; having the power to corrupt or the quality of being corrupt.
The official was removed from his post due to his corruptive influence on the organization.  这个官员因其对组织的腐败影响而被免职。
The report detailed the company's corruptive practices. 报告详细说明了该公司的腐败行为。
Synonyms:  debauching, demoralizing, perverting, vitiating
Antonyms:  purifying, cleansing, edifying, uplifting
词性: adjective
