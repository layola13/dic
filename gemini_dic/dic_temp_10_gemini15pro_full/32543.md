chipotle: /tʃɪˈpoʊtleɪ/ (US) /tʃɪˈpɒtleɪ/ (UK)| n. |烟熏干辣椒，墨西哥辣椒的一种；奇波雷餐厅|A smoked, dried jalapeño chili pepper.  Also, often refers to the popular restaurant chain "Chipotle Mexican Grill".|I love the chipotle sauce on my burrito. 我喜欢墨西哥卷饼上的烟熏辣椒酱。|Chipotle peppers add a smoky flavor to dishes.  烟熏辣椒给菜肴增添了烟熏味。|Synonyms: jalapeño (fresh), smoked chili, chipotle pepper|Antonyms: sweet pepper, bell pepper|noun
