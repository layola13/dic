hibernator:/ˈhaɪbərˌneɪtər/| n.|冬眠动物|An animal that hibernates, spending the winter in a dormant state.|例句：The bear is a hibernator, sleeping through the cold winter months.  熊是冬眠动物，在寒冷的冬季睡上几个月。

例句：Groundhogs are well-known hibernators. 土拨鼠是著名的冬眠动物。|近义词：dormouse, groundhog, marmot|反义词:non-hibernator|词性:noun
