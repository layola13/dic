overbook: /ˌoʊvərˈbʊk/ | v. | 超额预订 | To sell tickets for (a flight, concert, etc.) in excess of the available seats or capacity. | The airline overbooked the flight, and several passengers were left stranded. 航空公司超额预订了航班，导致几名乘客滞留。|  overreserve, double-book | underbook | verb
