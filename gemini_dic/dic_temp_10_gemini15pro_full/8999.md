antiliterately:/ˌæntiˈlɪtərətli/
adv.
以缺乏文化的方式，不识字地
in an antiliterate manner; showing or marked by illiteracy.
He antiliterately scrawled his name on the document. 他没文化地潦草地在文件上签了名。
近义词:illiterately, unliterately
反义词:literately
词性:副词
