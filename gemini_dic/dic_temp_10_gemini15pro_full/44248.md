croesus:/ˈkriːsəs/| n.|克里索斯（古代吕底亚国王，以富有闻名）,巨富,财主|A person of great wealth.|例句：He's a regular Croesus, owning several mansions and a private jet. 他是个名副其实的巨富，拥有几处豪宅和一架私人飞机。

例句：Bill Gates is considered a modern-day Croesus. 比尔·盖茨被认为是现代的克里索斯。|近义词：tycoon, magnate, millionaire, billionaire|反义词:pauper|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin Croesus, from Greek Κροῖσος (Kroîsos).
