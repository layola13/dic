biotapestry:/ˌbaɪoʊˈtæpɪstri/| n.|生物挂毯; 生物图谱|A representation or overview of biological processes, relationships, or systems, often visualized as a complex interwoven pattern, similar to a tapestry.  It can refer to a literal visual representation, like a diagram, or a more abstract conceptualization of interconnected biological elements.|Researchers unveiled a biotapestry of the human microbiome, revealing intricate connections between gut bacteria and overall health.  研究人员揭示了人类微生物组的生物图谱，揭示了肠道细菌与整体健康之间错综复杂的联系。|synonyms:  biological network, biological map, biological framework,  interactome|antonyms: N/A|noun
