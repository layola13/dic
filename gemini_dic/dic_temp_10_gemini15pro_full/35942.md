codding:/ˈkɒdɪŋ/ | n. | 鳕鱼捕捞; 欺骗; 愚弄;  |The act of catching cod.  Also, an instance of deception or fooling. |They went codding off the coast of Newfoundland. 他们在纽芬兰海岸捕鳕鱼。| 近义词: fishing, angling, deceiving, tricking | 反义词: honesty, truthfulness | 词性: noun


codding:/ˈkɒdɪŋ/ | adj. | (罕见) 类似鳕鱼的 | Resembling a cod. (Rare Usage) | The fish had a codding appearance. 这条鱼长得像鳕鱼。(罕见用法) | 近义词: cod-like | 反义词: (无常用反义词) | 词性: adjective


Etymology: From the noun "cod" (referring to the fish) + "-ing" (forming a present participle or gerund). Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *kabuzą.
