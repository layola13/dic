ill-temperedly:/ɪlˈtɛmpərdli/
adverb
暴躁地；脾气坏地
in a bad-tempered or irritable way
He slammed the door ill-temperedly. 他暴躁地摔门。
angrily, irritably, crossly
calmly, pleasantly, patiently
词性: 副词
