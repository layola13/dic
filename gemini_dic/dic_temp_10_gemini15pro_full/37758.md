compassionateness:/kəmˈpæʃənətnəs/| n.|同情心，怜悯心|The quality of being compassionate; a feeling of deep sympathy and sorrow for another who is stricken by misfortune, accompanied by a strong desire to alleviate the suffering.|Her compassionateness led her to volunteer at the homeless shelter. 她的同情心驱使她到流浪汉收容所做志愿者。|近义词：compassion, empathy, kindness, sympathy|反义词：cruelty, indifference, heartlessness|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From compassion + -ateness.  Compassion from Late Middle English: from Old French compassion, from Latin compassio(n-), from compati ‘suffer with.’  -ateness: suffix forming nouns of quality or state from adjectives in -ate.
