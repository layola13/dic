breakaway:/ˈbreɪkəweɪ/| n. adj.| 突破；分裂；逃脱者；脱离的；突围的|A sudden escape or departure from a group or situation.  A breakaway group, product, or region is one that has separated from a larger group or area.|The cyclist made a breakaway and won the race.  这位自行车手突围而出，赢得了比赛。|近义词：escape, secession, rebellion, independent|反义词: union, adherence, conformity|词性: noun, adjective

break away:/breɪk əˈweɪ/| v.| 逃脱；脱离；放弃；突然离开|To escape or become separated from someone or something. To stop doing or having something.|The prisoner broke away from his guards.  囚犯从他的警卫手中逃脱了。|近义词：escape, secede, depart, leave|反义词: join, unite, stay|词性: verb
