Example 2:  Researchers are studying the effects of motorpathy on drivers' reaction times. 研究人员正在研究汽车病对驾驶员反应时间的影响。 | Near Synonyms: driver fatigue, highway hypnosis, road weariness | Antonyms:  alertness, vigor, energy | noun
