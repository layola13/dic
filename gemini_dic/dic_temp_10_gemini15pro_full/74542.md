fratricelli:/ˌfrɑːtrɪˈtʃɛli/ | n. | 小兄弟会成员；精神上极端激进的教派成员 | Members of a radical religious sect, especially a branch of the Franciscan order who adhered to the strict rule of poverty advocated by St. Francis. | The fratricelli were persecuted by the Church for their heretical beliefs. 小兄弟会成员因其异端信仰而受到教会的迫害。 | synonyms: Spiritual Franciscans, heretics | antonyms: conformists, orthodox | noun
