misprogramming:/ˌmɪsprəʊˈɡræmɪŋ/ | n. | 错误编程，程序编写错误 | The system crash was caused by misprogramming. 系统崩溃是由程序编写错误引起的。|  The misprogramming led to a significant loss of data. 错误的编程导致了大量的数据丢失。 | 近义词：coding error, program error, software bug, glitch | 反义词：correct programming, accurate coding | 词性: 名词

词源:  "mis-" (prefix meaning "wrong" or "badly") + "programming" (the act of creating computer programs).  From "mis-" (Old English "mis-") + "program" (from Greek "programma" -  a public notice, from "prographein" - to write publicly).
