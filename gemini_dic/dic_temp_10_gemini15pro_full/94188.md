imperator:/ɪmˈpərɑːtər/ | n. |皇帝，指挥官|A commander, especially a Roman emperor.  Derived from Latin "imperare" (to command). Originally a military title, later adopted by Roman emperors. |Caesar became imperator of Rome. 凯撒成为了罗马的皇帝。| The imperator led his legions to victory. 指挥官带领他的军团取得了胜利。 | synonym: emperor, ruler, commander, sovereign | antonym: subject, follower | noun
