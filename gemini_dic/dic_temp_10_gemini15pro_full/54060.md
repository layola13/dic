disdeify:/dɪsˈdeɪɪfaɪ/
verb
去除神性; 降低…的神圣性;  不再尊崇
To deprive of deity; to reduce from the state of a god; to treat as no longer divine.
例句：The movement sought to disdeify the emperor and establish a republic.  这场运动试图去除皇帝的神性并建立共和国。

例句：Modern science has disdeified natural phenomena, explaining them through natural laws rather than divine intervention.  现代科学已经不再将自然现象神化，而是通过自然规律而非神圣干预来解释它们。
近义词：demythologize, secularize, humanize
反义词：deify, apotheosize, idolize
词性：v.
