alure:/əˈlʊr/ /əˈljʊr/| vt. n.|诱惑；魅力|To tempt by flattery or an attractive offer; the power to attract or charm.|The siren song of the island alured many sailors to their doom.  岛屿的歌声诱惑许多水手走向毁灭。|近义词：entice, tempt, attract, charm, seduce|反义词：repel, deter, disgust|词性:动词,名词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *alurer "to attract, entice," from a- "to" (from Latin ad-) + lurer "to lure" (of uncertain origin).
