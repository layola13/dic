exchangeable:/ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒəbl/| adj.|可交换的，可兑换的|Able to be exchanged, especially for something of equal value.  Something that can be given and something of similar value received in return.|These tokens are exchangeable for merchandise at the store. 这些代币可以在商店兑换商品。|Synonyms: tradable, swappable, replaceable, interchangeable|Antonyms: nonexchangeable, irreplaceable, unique|adjective

exchangeable:/ɪksˈtʃeɪndʒəbl/| adj.|可交换的，可互换的| (of two or more things) able to be exchanged with each other without any significant loss of function or value.|The two parts are exchangeable, so it doesn't matter which way round you put them. 这两个部分可以互换，所以你把它们放在哪一边都无所谓。|Synonyms: interchangeable, substitutable, equivalent|Antonyms: non-interchangeable, unique, specific|adjective
