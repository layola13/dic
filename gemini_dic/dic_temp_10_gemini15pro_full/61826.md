empiricisms:/ɪmˈpɪrɪsɪzəmz/
| n. |经验主义，经验主义理论|Theories based on observation and experience, rather than logic or theory.  Based on, concerned with, or verifiable by observation or experience rather than theory or pure logic.|His philosophy is grounded in empiricisms, valuing observation over abstract thought. 他的哲学基于经验主义，重视观察胜过抽象思维。|Synonyms:  experimentalism, pragmatism, positivism|Antonyms: rationalism, idealism, apriorism|noun
