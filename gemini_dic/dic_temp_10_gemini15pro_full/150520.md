pantophagous:/pænˈtɒfəɡəs/ | adj. | 杂食性的 | Eating a variety of foods of both plant and animal origin. |  Pigs are pantophagous animals, consuming both plants and small animals. 猪是杂食性动物，既吃植物也吃小动物。| Bears are pantophagous and will eat berries, fish, and insects. 熊是杂食动物，会吃浆果、鱼和昆虫。 | omnivorous | herbivorous, carnivorous | adjective
