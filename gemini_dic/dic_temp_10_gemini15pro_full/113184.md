lurer:/ˈlʊərər/ (US), /ˈljʊərə(r)/ (UK)| n.| 诱惑者，骗子 |Someone who tempts or tricks people into doing something bad or illegal. | He posed as a talent scout, but he was really just a lurer preying on young hopefuls. 他伪装成星探，但实际上只是一个捕猎年轻有为者的骗子。| synonyms: deceiver, trickster, con artist, swindler | antonyms: protector, guardian | noun

lure:/lʊr/ (US), /ljʊə(r)/ (UK)| n. vt.| 诱惑，诱饵；引诱 |Something that tempts or is used to tempt someone to do something.  (n.) The lure of easy money led him into a life of crime.  (vt.) They lured him into a trap with promises of riches. 轻松赚钱的诱惑使他走上了犯罪的道路。(名词) 他们用财富的承诺引诱他落入陷阱。(动词) | synonyms: bait, attraction, enticement, temptation (n.); entice, tempt, seduce (vt.) | antonyms: deterrent, repellent (n.); repel, deter (vt.) | noun, verb
