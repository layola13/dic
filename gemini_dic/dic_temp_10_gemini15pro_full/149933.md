palliation:/ˌpæliˈeɪʃən/| n. | 减轻，缓和；姑息疗法 | the act of reducing the severity of something, especially pain or a disease, without curing it |  The doctor focused on palliation of his symptoms, as a cure was unlikely.  医生专注于减轻他的症状，因为治愈的可能性不大。 |  relief, alleviation, mitigation, easement |  aggravation, exacerbation | noun

palliate:/ˈpæliˌeɪt/| v. | 减轻，缓和；掩饰 | to make a disease or its symptoms less severe or unpleasant without removing the cause |  The drug palliated the pain, but it didn't address the underlying infection.  这种药物减轻了疼痛，但没有解决根本的感染问题。 | alleviate, mitigate, ease, relieve, soothe | aggravate, worsen, exacerbate | verb
