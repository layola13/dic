neutrophil: /ˈnjuːtrəfɪl/ (US), /ˈnjuːtrəfɪl/ (UK)| n. |中性粒细胞|A type of white blood cell that engulfs and destroys bacteria. It is the most abundant type of granulocyte and plays a crucial role in the innate immune system.|  A high neutrophil count often indicates an infection. 中性粒细胞计数高通常表明感染。

Neutrophils are essential for fighting bacterial infections. 中性粒细胞对抵抗细菌感染至关重要。|近义词: polymorphonuclear leukocyte (PMN)|反义词:  None (no direct opposite for a specific cell type)|词性: noun
