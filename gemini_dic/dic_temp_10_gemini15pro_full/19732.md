billable:/ˈbɪləbl/| adj.|可计费的，可开账单的|Something that is billable can be charged to a client or customer.|例句：Billable hours are the hours worked that can be charged to a client.  可计费工时是指可以向客户收费的工作时间。

例句：The lawyer tracked her billable hours meticulously.  这位律师一丝不苟地记录了她的可计费工时。|近义词：chargeable, invoiceable|反义词：non-billable, unbillable|词性:形容词

Etymology: From "bill" (n.) + "-able" (suffix). "Bill" comes from Medieval Latin "billa," meaning a sealed document.  "-able" is a suffix that forms adjectives meaning "capable of being," "fit to be," or "worthy of being."
