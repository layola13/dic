mouldies:/ˈməʊldiz/ | n. | 发霉的东西；霉菌 |Things that are mouldy; moulds.  | The bread had gone bad and was covered in mouldies.  面包坏了，上面长满了霉菌。 | 近义词: moulds, mildew, fungus | 反义词: freshness, cleanness | 词性: 名词

词源: "mouldies" is the plural form of "mouldy," which comes from the Old English word "moldig," meaning "mouldy."  Ultimately, it's related to the word "mold," referring to the fungal growth itself.
