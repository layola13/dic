exercisible:/ˈɛksərsaɪzəbl/
adj.
可行使的，可执行的
able to be exercised; capable of being put into effect or practiced.
例句：The warrants are exercisible at $50 per share. 认股权证可以每股50美元的价格行使。

例句：She has an exercisable right to buy the property. 她拥有购买该房产的可行使权利。

近义词：enforceable, applicable, usable, valid
反义词：non-exercisible, invalid, unusable

词性: adjective
