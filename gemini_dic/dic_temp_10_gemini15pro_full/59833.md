ectothermic:/ˈek.tə.θɝː.mɪk/
adjective
变温的，冷血的
Relating to an animal whose body temperature is primarily determined by the temperature of its surroundings.  Dependent on external sources of body heat.
Lizards are ectothermic animals and regulate their body temperature by basking in the sun. 蜥蜴是变温动物，通过晒太阳来调节体温。
Ectothermic animals, like snakes, are more active in warmer temperatures. 像蛇这样的变温动物在温暖的温度下更活跃。
近义词:poikilothermic, cold-blooded
反义词:endothermic, warm-blooded, homeothermic
词性:adjective
