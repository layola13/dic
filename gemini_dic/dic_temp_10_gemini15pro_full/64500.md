eroding:/ɪˈroʊdɪŋ/
verb
侵蚀，腐蚀；削弱，损害
gradually wearing away or destroying something
The coastline is slowly being eroded by the sea. 海岸线正慢慢地被海水侵蚀。
近义词: wearing away, dissolving, deteriorating, weakening
反义词: building up, strengthening, restoring
