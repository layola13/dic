charming:/ˈtʃɑːrmɪŋ/ (US), /ˈtʃɑːmɪŋ/ (UK)| adj.|迷人的，有魅力的|Pleasing or attracting because of a gracious or attractive quality.|She has a charming personality. 她性格迷人。|近义词：attractive, appealing, delightful, pleasant, lovely|反义词：unpleasant, repulsive, disagreeable|词性:形容词

词源: From Old French *charmer "to enchant, cast a spell," from Latin carmen "song, verse, incantation."
