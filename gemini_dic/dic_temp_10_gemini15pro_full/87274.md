hermaphroditic:/hɜːrˌmæfrəˈdɪtɪk/ | adj. | 雌雄同体的；阴阳体的 | Having both male and female reproductive organs. |  Some species of fish are hermaphroditic.  有些鱼类是雌雄同体的。 | synonyms: bisexual, androgynous, monoecious | antonyms: unisexual, dioecious | adjective

hermaphrodite: /hɜːrˈmæfrədaɪt/ | n. | 雌雄同体的人或生物 | An organism or person having both male and female reproductive organs. | The earthworm is a hermaphrodite. 蚯蚓是雌雄同体的。 | synonyms: bisexual, androgynous, monoecious | antonyms: unisexual, dioecious | noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *hermaphroditus, from Greek hermaphroditos, from Hermaphroditos, son of Hermes and Aphrodite, who became united in one body with the nymph Salmacis.
