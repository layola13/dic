close-knit:/ˌkloʊsˈnɪt/| adj.|紧密的，关系密切的|United in a close relationship; having strong bonds of friendship, loyalty, or affection.|例句：They are a close-knit family who support each other through thick and thin. 他们是关系密切的一家人，同甘共苦，互相扶持。

例句：The team is close-knit and works well together. 这个团队关系紧密，合作良好。|近义词：close, intimate, tight-knit, united, bonded|反义词：distant, estranged, alienated|词性：形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From close + knit.  "Knit" in this sense refers to the act of knitting fabric together, creating a strong and interconnected structure. This imagery is then applied metaphorically to relationships.
