bramblier:/bræmbliər/
noun
荆棘丛，黑莓丛
A thorny shrub, especially a blackberry bush.
The bramblier was thick with ripe berries. 荆棘丛上长满了成熟的浆果。

The children got scratched trying to pick berries from the bramblier. 孩子们想从荆棘丛中摘浆果，结果被划伤了。
synonym: thicket, bramble patch
antonym: meadow, clearing
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *bremblier, from bramble + -ier. Ultimately from Proto-Germanic *bramǭ.
