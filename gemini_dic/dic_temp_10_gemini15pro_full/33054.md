chow:/tʃaʊ/ | n. | 食物，伙食 | Food, especially hearty, simple food. |  例句：The hungry hikers devoured the chow with gusto. 饥饿的徒步旅行者津津有味地吃着食物。

例句：The chow in the mess hall wasn't bad tonight. 今晚食堂的饭菜还不错。

近义词：food, grub, eats, victuals | 反义词: None | 词性: noun

chow:/tʃaʊ/ | v. | 吃 | To eat. | 例句：Let's chow down before we hit the road. 我们上路前先吃点东西吧。

例句：We chowed on pizza after the game.  比赛结束后我们吃了披萨。


近义词：eat, dine, munch | 反义词: None | 词性: verb


词源:  From Chinese Pidgin English *[t͡ʃau²], from Cantonese 食 *[sɪk˨] (“to eat”).
