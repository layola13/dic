acervuline: /əˈsɜːrvjʊlaɪn/
adjective
聚集成堆的，簇生的
growing in dense clusters or clumps; heaped up.
The acervuline growth of the fungus made it difficult to identify individual fruiting bodies.  这种真菌簇生的生长方式使得很难识别单个子实体。

The lichen formed an acervuline crust on the rock.  地衣在岩石上形成了簇生的外壳。

近义词: clustered, aggregated, heaped, cespitose
反义词: scattered, dispersed
词性: adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *acervulinus, from acervus (“heap, pile”) + -inus (suffix forming adjectives).

USAGE->
The acervuline nature of the coral reef provided shelter for a variety of marine life.  珊瑚礁簇生的特性为各种海洋生物提供了庇护所。

Acervuline colonies of bacteria can be resistant to antibiotics.  成堆的细菌菌落可能对抗生素产生抗药性。
