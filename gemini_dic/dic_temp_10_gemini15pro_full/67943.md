fascia board:/ˈfeɪʃə bɔːrd/| n. |封檐板，屋檐板|A board covering the ends of rafters, usually under the eaves of a roof.|The fascia board needs repainting.  封檐板需要重新粉刷。|Synonym: eaves board, soffit board|Antonym: (No direct antonym, perhaps "rafter")|noun
fascia:/ˈfeɪʃə/ |n.| 筋膜；饰带；招牌；仪表板 |A thin sheath of fibrous tissue enclosing a muscle or other organ.|The surgeon carefully repaired the fascia.  外科医生仔细地修复了筋膜。|Synonym: membrane, covering|Antonym: (No direct antonym)|noun
board:/bɔːrd/ |n. v. |板; 董事会;  登上(交通工具) |A flat piece of wood or other stiff material used for various purposes; a group of people constituted as a governing body; get on or into (a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle).|The board of directors approved the merger. 董事会批准了合并。 We boarded the plane at gate 3. 我们在3号登机口登机。|Synonym: plank, panel (n.); council, committee (n.); embark, enter (v.)|Antonym: disembark, exit (v.)|noun, verb
