cheekily:/ˈtʃiːkɪli/
adverb
厚脸皮地，放肆地
In a way that is slightly rude but also amusing and confident.
He cheekily grinned at her after making the joke.  他开了个玩笑后，厚脸皮地对她咧嘴一笑。
She cheekily asked for a second helping of dessert. 她厚颜无耻地要求再来一份甜点。
近义词:impudently, saucily, boldly
反义词:respectfully, politely, timidly
词性:adverb

词源:From cheek + -ily.  源自 cheek（脸颊） + -ily（副词后缀）。最初指的是“脸颊丰满的”，后来引申为“大胆的，无礼的”。
