cohen:/ˈkoʊən/ (US), /ˈkəʊən/ (UK)| n. |祭司，科恩|A Cohen is a Jewish priest, a descendant of Aaron, the brother of Moses.  The Cohanim (plural) had specific duties in the Temple in Jerusalem. |例句：He is a Cohen, descended from a long line of priests. 他是一位科恩，出身于一个祭司世家。

例句：The Cohen led the prayers during the High Holy Days.  在犹太教重要节日里，这位科恩带领大家祈祷。|近义词：priest|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps layperson/congregant)|noun
