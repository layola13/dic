ectoparasite:/ˈɛktəʊˌpærəsaɪt/| n. |外寄生虫|An organism that lives on the outside of its host.|例句：Fleas and ticks are common ectoparasites of dogs. 跳蚤和蜱虫是狗的常见外寄生虫。

例句：The ectoparasite was identified as a type of mite.  这种外寄生虫被鉴定为一种螨虫。|近义词：external parasite|反义词:endoparasite|词性:noun
