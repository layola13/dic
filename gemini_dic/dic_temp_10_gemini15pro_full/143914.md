outpreaching:/ˌaʊtˈpriːtʃɪŋ/
verb
胜过布道；在布道方面超越；比...更会讲道
Surpassing someone in preaching; preaching more effectively or persuasively than another.
The young minister was outpreaching his older colleagues with his dynamic sermons.  这位年轻的牧师用他充满活力的布道胜过了他的老同事们。
He was known for outpreaching anyone in the district. 他以在该地区布道方面胜过任何人而闻名。
verb
近义词: outdo, surpass, excel, exceed
反义词:  underperform, lag behind
