gibbet:/ˈdʒɪbɪt/| n. v. |绞刑架; 绞死|A structure for hanging criminals after execution, typically consisting of a wooden post with a projecting arm or beam.|The gibbet stood silhouetted against the stormy sky. 绞刑架的轮廓映衬在暴风雨的天空中。|He was condemned to be hanged on the gibbet. 他被判处绞刑。|近义词：gallows, scaffold|反义词: None|词性: 名词, 动词

gibbet:/ˈdʒɪbɪt/| v. |绞死; 悬挂示众; 抨击; 谴责|To hang (a person) on a gibbet; to expose to public scorn or ridicule.|His body was gibbeted after execution as a warning to others.  他被处决后，尸体被悬挂示众，以儆效尤。|The press gibbeted him for his dishonesty.  新闻界抨击他的不诚实行为。|近义词：hang, execute, pillory, denounce|反义词: praise, commend|词性: 动词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *gibet*, of uncertain Germanic origin.  Possibly related to Old North French *wi-bet* “swing-beam”.
