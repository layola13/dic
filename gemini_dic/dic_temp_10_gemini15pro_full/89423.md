homozygotes:/ˌhəʊməˈzaɪɡəʊts/ | n. |纯合子|An individual having two identical alleles of a particular gene or genes and so breeding true for the corresponding characteristic.|The study focused on homozygotes for the sickle cell gene. 这项研究的重点是镰状细胞基因的纯合子。|近义词:homozygous individual|反义词:heterozygotes|词性:noun

homozygote:/ˌhəʊməˈzaɪɡəʊt/ | n. |纯合子|An individual having two identical alleles of a particular gene or genes and so breeding true for the corresponding characteristic.|The homozygote showed no signs of the disease.  这个纯合子没有表现出任何疾病迹象。|近义词:homozygous individual|反义词:heterozygote|词性:noun

homozygous:/ˌhəʊməˈzaɪɡəs/ | adj. |纯合的|Having two identical alleles of a particular gene or genes and so breeding true for the corresponding characteristic.|The plant is homozygous for the dominant allele.  这种植物对显性等位基因是纯合的。|近义词:purebred|反义词:heterozygous|词性:adjective
