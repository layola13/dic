contrastive:/kənˈtræstɪv/ /kənˈtrɑːstɪv/| adj.|对比的，对照的|Showing differences when compared; involving or based on linguistic contrast.|例句：A contrastive analysis of English and Chinese grammar can be very helpful for language learners. 对比分析英语和汉语语法对语言学习者很有帮助。|近义词：comparative, differential|反义词：similar, uniform|词性:adjective

contrastive:/kənˈtræstɪv/ /kənˈtrɑːstɪv/| n.|对比分析|A contrastive analysis, especially of languages.|例句：The contrastive highlighted the key differences between the two dialects.  这个对比分析突出了两种方言之间的主要区别。|近义词：comparison, differentiation|反义词：similarity, uniformity|词性:noun
