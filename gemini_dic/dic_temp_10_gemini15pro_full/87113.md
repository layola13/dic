herbaceous:/hɜːrˈbeɪʃəs/ | adj. | 草本的；叶状的 | Relating to, or characteristic of, an herb; having the characteristics of green and leafy plants, as opposed to woody plants.  | The garden is filled with herbaceous plants, providing a vibrant display of color.  花园里种满了草本植物，呈现出一片生机勃勃的景象。| synonyms: leafy, grassy | antonyms: woody, ligneous | adjective

herbaceous:/ɜːrˈbeɪʃəs/ | adj. | （味道或气味）像草的 | Having a flavor or aroma reminiscent of herbs. | The tea had a delicate, herbaceous scent.  这茶有一种淡淡的草香味。 | synonyms: herbal, grassy | antonyms: woody, spicy | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *herbaceus, from Latin herba (“herb”) + -aceus (“-aceous”).
