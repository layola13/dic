orogenic:/ˌɔːrəˈdʒɛnɪk/
adjective
造山的
Relating to orogeny; mountain-building.
The orogenic belt contains folded and faulted rocks. 这条造山带包含褶皱和断层的岩石。
The Himalayas are an orogenic belt. 喜马拉雅山脉是一个造山带。
Synonyms: tectonic, mountain-building
Antonyms: erosional
词性: adjective
