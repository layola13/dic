generous:/ˈdʒenərəs/| adj.|慷慨的；大量的；丰富的|Showing a readiness to give more of something, as money or time, than is strictly necessary or expected; plentiful.|She made a generous donation to the charity. 她向慈善机构慷慨捐赠。|近义词：liberal, bountiful, kind, charitable|反义词：stingy, mean, selfish|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old French *generos (Modern French généreux), from Latin generōsus “of noble birth, magnanimous,” from genus “birth, race, stock, kind.”
