agnostic:/æɡˈnɑːstɪk/ | adj. n. | 不可知论的，不可知论者 |  A person who believes that nothing is known or can be known of the existence or nature of God or of anything beyond material phenomena; a person who claims neither faith nor disbelief in God. |  He's an agnostic who doesn't believe in any specific religion. 他是一个不可知论者，不信仰任何特定的宗教。 | Synonyms: doubter, skeptic, unbeliever | Antonyms: believer, devotee, faithful | adjective

agnostic:/æɡˈnɑːstɪk/ | n. | 不可知论者 | A person who believes that nothing is known or can be known of the existence or nature of God or of anything beyond material phenomena; a person who claims neither faith nor disbelief in God. | Many agnostics find comfort in the uncertainty of the universe. 许多不可知论者在宇宙的不确定性中找到了慰藉。| Synonyms: doubter, skeptic, unbeliever | Antonyms: believer, devotee, faithful | noun
