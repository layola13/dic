etiolated: /ˈiːtiəˌleɪtɪd/ /ɪˈtiːəˌleɪtɪd/
adjective
(植物) 变得又细又长又白，因缺乏光照; 苍白的，虚弱的
Pale and drawn out due to lack of light; lacking in vigor or substance due to deprivation.
The etiolated seedlings were pale and spindly.  这些黄化的幼苗苍白而细长。
The long illness had left him etiolated and weak.  长期的疾病使他变得虚弱无力。
近义词: bleached, blanched, pale, wan, weakened, feeble
反义词: robust, healthy, vigorous
词性: adjective

etiolate: /ˈiːtiəˌleɪt/ /ɪˈtiːəˌleɪt/
verb
(使)变得又细又长又白，(使)变苍白虚弱
To cause (a plant) to become pale and drawn out due to lack of light; to make weak or feeble.
Lack of sunlight etiolated the plants. 缺乏阳光使植物变得细长而苍白。
Years of imprisonment had etiolated him.  多年的监禁使他变得虚弱。
近义词: bleach, blanch, weaken, enfeeble
反义词: strengthen, invigorate
词性: verb

词源: From French *étiolé*, past participle of *étioler* "to become pale and drawn out", from *étiau*, variant of *éteule* "stubble", from Latin *stipula*.
