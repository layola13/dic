duckboards:/ˈdʌkbɔːrdz/
noun
鸭板，跳板，铺板
A boardwalk or path made of slats or planks, typically used in wet or muddy areas.
The soldiers crossed the swamp on duckboards. 士兵们在鸭板上穿过沼泽。
Synonyms: boardwalk, walkway, path, platform
Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, possibly solid ground, pavement)
词性: noun
