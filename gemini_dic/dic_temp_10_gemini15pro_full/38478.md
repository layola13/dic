conchiform:/ˈkɒŋkɪfɔːm/ | adj. |贝壳状的，螺壳形的 | Shaped like a conch shell.  Resembling the spiral shell of a conch. | The conchiform structure of the building was striking against the skyline. 这座建筑的贝壳状结构在天际线上格外引人注目。|  The fossil had a distinct conchiform shape. 这块化石有着明显的贝壳形状。 |Synonyms: shell-shaped, spiral, turbinate | Antonyms: shapeless, amorphous | adjective
