committing:/kəˈmɪtɪŋ/ |v.|犯（罪）；做（错事）；承诺；投入|The act of perpetrating a crime, wrongdoing, or error; pledging or devoting oneself to a particular course of action or purpose.| He was charged with committing murder. 他被指控犯有谋杀罪。|Synonyms: perpetrating, performing, undertaking, pledging, devoting|Antonyms: abstaining, refraining, withdrawing|Verb (present participle)

committing:/kəˈmɪtɪŋ/ |adj.|尽心尽力的；坚定的|Dedicated, loyal, and unwavering in support or allegiance.|  She is a committing member of the team. 她是一位尽心尽力的团队成员。|Synonyms: dedicated, devoted, committed, loyal, steadfast|Antonyms: uncommitted, halfhearted, unenthusiastic|Adjective (present participle)
