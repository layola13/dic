meteorometer:/ˌmiːtiəˈrɒmɪtər/ | n. | 流星计，大气探测器 | An instrument for recording the state of the atmosphere; an athmospheric probe. |  A meteorometer was used to gather data about the upper atmosphere. 流星计被用来收集高层大气的数据。

The scientists launched a meteorometer attached to a weather balloon. 科学家们发射了一个系在气象气球上的大气探测器。 | 近义词: radiosonde, weather balloon, atmospheric probe | 反义词: N/A | 词性: noun


Etymology: From Greek *meteōros* (μετέωρος, “high in the air”) + *-meter* (-μετρον, “measure”)
