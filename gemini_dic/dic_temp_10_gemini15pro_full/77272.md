gastrostomy: /ɡæˈstrɒstəmi/ (US) /ɡæˈstrɒstəmi/ (UK)| n. |胃造口术|The surgical creation of an artificial opening into the stomach, often for feeding purposes.  A gastrostomy tube is inserted through this opening.|The patient required a gastrostomy due to difficulty swallowing.  病人因吞咽困难需要进行胃造口术。|Synonyms: G-tube placement, stomach tube insertion|Antonyms: N/A|noun
