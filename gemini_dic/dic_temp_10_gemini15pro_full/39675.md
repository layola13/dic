conserve:/kənˈsɜːrv/
v.
保存, 保护
To protect something from harm or destruction; to avoid wasteful or extravagant use of something.
We must conserve our natural resources. 我们必须保护我们的自然资源。
Try to conserve your energy for the climb.  尽量保存体力以备攀登。
近义词:preserve, protect, save
反义词:waste, squander, deplete
词性:动词


conserver:/kənˈsɜːrvər/
n.
保护者, 保存者
Someone who actively works to protect and preserve something, typically natural resources or historical artifacts.
She is a dedicated conserver of endangered species. 她是一位致力于保护濒危物种的人。
The museum employed several conservers to restore the ancient artifacts. 博物馆雇佣了几名文物保护者来修复古代文物。
近义词:protector, preservationist, guardian
反义词:waster, destroyer, squanderer
词性:名词
