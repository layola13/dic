epidemicities:/ˌɛpɪdɛˈmɪsɪtiz/| n. | 流行病，疫情爆发（复数）|The occurrence of epidemics, especially multiple outbreaks of disease across different locations or times. It refers to the frequency or pattern of epidemic events.| The researcher studied the epidemicities of cholera in the 19th century.  这位研究人员研究了19世纪霍乱的流行情况。|近义词：outbreaks, epidemics, plagues |反义词: remissions, recoveries |词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From epidemic + -ity (suffix forming nouns of quality or state).  Ultimately from Greek *epidēmios* "prevalent among the people."
