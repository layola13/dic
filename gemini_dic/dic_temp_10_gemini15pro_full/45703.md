curvaciousnesses:/kɜːrˈveɪʃəsnəsɪz/
noun
曲線優美, 婀娜多姿 (plural)
The quality of having many curves; the state of being curvacious.  This word emphasizes an abundance of curves.
Her curvaciousnesses were accentuated by the tight dress. 她紧身的连衣裙突显了她婀娜多姿的身材。
The painting celebrated the curvaciousnesses of the female form. 这幅画赞美了女性的曲线美。
synonyms: curves, shapeliness, voluptuousness
antonyms: straightness, angularity
n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From curvacious + -ness (suffix indicating a state or quality).  "Curvacious" itself comes from "curve" + "-acious" (suffix meaning "full of" or "having the quality of").  Ultimately derived from the Latin word "curvus" meaning "bent" or "curved".
