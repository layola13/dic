overrationalizes: /ˌoʊvərˈræʃənəˌlaɪzɪz/
verb
过度合理化
To explain or justify (one's own or another's behavior or attitude) excessively or illogically with plausible reasons.  To invent seemingly reasonable explanations to convince oneself or others that a decision, action, or feeling is justified, even if it’s not.
He overrationalizes his mistakes, making it hard for him to learn from them. 他过度合理化自己的错误，这让他很难从中吸取教训。
She overrationalized her spending habits by claiming it was all for "self-care." 她过度合理化自己的消费习惯，声称这都是为了“自我照顾”。
Synonyms: justify excessively, rationalize too much, explain away, make excuses for
Antonyms: accept, admit, acknowledge, face, confront
词性: 动词
