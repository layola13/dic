frankfurts:/ˈfræŋkfɜːrts/ (US), /ˈfræŋkfɜːts/ (UK)| n.|法兰克福香肠，热狗|A small smoked sausage (= a type of food made of meat, herbs, and spices, shaped into a long thin tube and often eaten cold) of minced beef and pork, often served in a long roll.|例句：I'd like two frankfurts with mustard, please. 我想要两根加芥末的法兰克福香肠。

例句：They sell frankfurts at the baseball game. 他们在棒球比赛上卖法兰克福香肠。


近义词：hot dogs, wieners, sausages|反义词:|词性:noun
