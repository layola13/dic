delegators:/ˈdɛlɪˌɡeɪtərz/ | n. |代表；授权者；委托人|A person or group of people who authorize someone to represent them, typically at a conference or meeting.|The delegators from each state voted on the proposal.  每个州的代表就这项提议进行了投票。|Representatives, commissioners, envoys|  -|noun
delegators:/ˈdɛlɪˌɡeɪtərz/ | n. |授权者；委托人|In blockchain technology, a person who delegates their cryptocurrency holdings to a validator to participate in consensus mechanisms (like Proof-of-Stake) and earn rewards.|The delegators received a portion of the staking rewards. 授权者获得了部分质押奖励。|Stakers, nominators| - |noun
