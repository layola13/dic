hentai:/hɛnˈtaɪ/
adjective|变态的，性变态的|Perverted; sexually deviant; relating to or depicting sexually explicit or fetishized themes, often involving anime or manga.|This store sells hentai manga. 这家店卖色情漫画。|近义词:perverted, lewd, obscene|反义词:wholesome, decent|词性:adjective
词源: From Japanese 変態 (hentai), literally "perversion," "transformation," or "metamorphosis."  The word combines 変 (hen, “strange, weird”) and 態 (tai, “form, appearance”).
