computable:/kəmˈpjuːtəbl/| adj.|可计算的，可计算出的|Able to be calculated or computed, especially by a computer.|The function is computable within a polynomial time bound.  这个函数可以在多项式时间内计算出来。|Example: The trajectory of the rocket is computable. 火箭的轨迹是可计算的.|Synonyms: calculable, solvable, determinable|Antonyms: incalculable, unsolvable, indeterminable|adjective
