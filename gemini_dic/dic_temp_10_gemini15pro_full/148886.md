overwetted:/ˌōvərˈwɛtɪd/
adjective
过度湿润的，浸透的
Excessively wet; saturated beyond the normal or desirable degree.
The overwetted soil became muddy and difficult to walk on. 过度湿润的土壤变得泥泞，难以行走。
The overwetted sponge dripped water everywhere. 过度湿润的海绵到处滴水。
synonyms: saturated, soaked, drenched, waterlogged
antonyms: dry, arid, parched
