disordained: /dɪsɔːrˈdeɪnd/
adj.
未被任命的；非圣职的;无序的，混乱的
Not ordained as a priest or minister; not in holy orders; lacking organization or order.
The disordained priest sought a new career.  这位被解除圣职的神父正在寻找新的职业。
His life became disordained after the loss of his job.  失去工作后，他的生活变得混乱不堪。
Synonyms: unordained, secular, lay, disorganized, chaotic
Antonyms: ordained, clerical, organized, orderly
词性: 形容词
