infectible:/ɪnˈfɛktɪbl/
adjective
易感染的；易受影响的
Susceptible to infection or contagion.  Able to be influenced or contaminated.
The wound was infectible and required careful cleaning.  伤口容易感染，需要仔细清洗。
His youthful idealism made him infectible to extremist ideologies. 他年轻的理想主义使他容易受到极端主义思想的影响。
Synonyms: susceptible, vulnerable, prone, liable
Antonyms: resistant, immune, impervious
词性: 形容词
