carry-overs:/ˈkæriˌoʊvərz/ (US), /ˈkæriˌəʊvəz/ (UK)| n. |结转，延滞，遗留物|Something (such as a cost, effect, or advantage) remaining from an earlier period or situation.| The tax carry-over will reduce next year's payments. 这项税收结转将减少明年的付款。|The experience gained from the project was a carry-over to his new job. 从这个项目中获得的经验被延续到了他的新工作中。 |Synonyms: surplus, remainder, residue, holdover| Antonyms: deficit, shortage| noun
