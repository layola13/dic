amplest:/ˈæmplɪst/| adj.|最充足的，最广阔的|Having the largest or most extensive area, scope, or capacity.|She had the amplest opportunity to succeed. 她有最充分的机会获得成功。|近义词:largest, most extensive, widest, most spacious, roomiest|反义词:smallest, narrowest, most limited, tiniest|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Middle English *amplest, from Old French ample (“large, spacious, plentiful”) + -est (superlative suffix). Ultimately from Latin amplus (“large, spacious, of great extent”).
