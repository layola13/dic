hexahedron:/ˌhɛksəˈhiːdrən/| n. |六面体，立方体|A solid figure with six plane faces.|A cube is a regular hexahedron.  一个立方体是一个正六面体。|The die is shaped like a hexahedron.  这颗骰子形状像一个六面体。|Synonym: cube, cuboid|Antonym: sphere, tetrahedron|noun
