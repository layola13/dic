lionesses:/ˈlaɪənɪsɪz/| n.|母狮，雌狮|A group of lionesses were hunting zebras. |例句：The lionesses stalked their prey through the tall grass.  母狮们在高高的草丛中跟踪猎物。

例句：A pride of lionesses protected their cubs. 一群母狮保护着它们的幼崽。|近义词：female lions|反义词:lions (male lions)|词性:noun
词源: From Middle English *lioness, *lionesse, from Anglo-Norman lionesse, Old French lionnesse, feminine of lion (see lion).
