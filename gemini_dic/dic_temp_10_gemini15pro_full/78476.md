g-force:/ˈdʒiː fɔːrs/| n.|重力加速度；G力|A force of acceleration, especially the force exerted on a body by gravity, or the force produced by a change in velocity, measured as a multiple of the standard acceleration of gravity.|The pilot experienced a g-force of 6 during the maneuver. 飞行员在机动过程中承受了6个G的重力。|Synonyms: gravitational force, acceleration force|Antonyms: weightlessness|noun
