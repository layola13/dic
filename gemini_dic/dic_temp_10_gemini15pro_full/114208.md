maidishness:/ˈmeɪdɪʃnɪs/
n.
少女气质；少女气；幼稚
The quality of being like a young, unmarried woman; girlishness and innocence.
She retained a certain maidishness despite being in her twenties.  她虽然二十多岁了，但仍然保留着某种少女气质。
innocence, girlishness, youthfulness, naiveté, simplicity
sophistication, worldliness, maturity, experience
