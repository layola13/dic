adenalgia: /ˌædɪˈnældʒə/
n.
腺痛
Pain in a gland.
The patient complained of adenalgia and fatigue. 病人抱怨腺体疼痛和疲劳。
He experienced adenalgia after the infection. 感染后，他感到腺体疼痛。
Near Synonyms: gland pain, glandular pain
Antonyms: analgesia, pain relief
noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From ad- + Greek aden- (gland) + -algia (pain).

USAGE->
He suffers from adenalgia. = 他患有腺痛。
Adenalgia can be a symptom of various conditions. = 腺痛可能是各种疾病的症状。

PHRASE->
adenalgia in the neck = 颈部腺痛
chronic adenalgia = 慢性腺痛
sudden adenalgia = 突然的腺痛
severe adenalgia = 严重的腺痛
treating adenalgia = 治疗腺痛
