nondelegate:/nɒnˈdɛlɪɡət/ | adj. | 不可委托的，不能委派的 | Not able to be delegated; not transferable. |  The president's authority to pardon is a nondelegate power.  总统的赦免权是不可委托的权力。|  The duty of a judge to be impartial is nondelegate. 法官保持公正的职责是不可转让的。| 近义词: nontransferable, inalienable | 反义词: delegable, transferable | 词性: adjective

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
delegate (verb): From Latin delegare "to send, dispatch, assign," from de- "down, away" + legare "to send with a commission, depute."
