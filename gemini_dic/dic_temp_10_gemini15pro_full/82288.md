groundwork:/ˈɡraʊndwɜːrk/ | n. | 基础工作，准备工作 | Preliminary or basic work that is done in preparation for something else. |  例句：
The team laid the groundwork for the new project. =  团队为新项目奠定了基础。

He did the groundwork for the peace talks. = 他为和平谈判做了准备工作。


近义词：foundation, basis, preparation, footing | 反义词:  superstructure, conclusion | 词性: 名词
