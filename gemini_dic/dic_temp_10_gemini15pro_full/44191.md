croakiness:/ˈkroʊkinɪs/| n.|嘶哑，声音沙哑|The croakiness in his voice suggested he had a cold. 他的声音嘶哑，表明他感冒了。|例句：The singer's croakiness added a certain gravitas to his performance. 歌手的嘶哑为他表演增添了一丝庄严。

例句：He woke up with a terrible croakiness and could barely speak. 他醒来时嗓子非常嘶哑，几乎说不出话来。|近义词：hoarseness, raspiness|反义词: clarity, smoothness|词性:名词

词源: From croaky + -ness.  Croaky itself comes from croak, imitating the sound a frog makes.
