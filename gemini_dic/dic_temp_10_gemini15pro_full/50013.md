denoising:/diːˈnɔɪzɪŋ/
n.|去噪，降噪|The process of removing noise from a signal, such as audio or image data.|例句：Denoising algorithms are essential for enhancing image quality.  图像质量增强算法对于提高图像质量至关重要。|近义词：noise reduction, noise suppression|反义词：noise addition, noise amplification|词性:名词
denoising:/diːˈnɔɪzɪŋ/
adj.|去噪的，降噪的|Relating to or involving the removal of noise from a signal.|例句：Denoising software can help to improve the clarity of audio recordings. 降噪软件可以帮助提高录音的清晰度。|近义词：noise-reducing, noise-suppressing|反义词：noise-adding, noise-amplifying|词性:形容词
