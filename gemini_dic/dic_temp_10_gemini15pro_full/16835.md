bash:/bæʃ/| n. vt.|盛会，痛击；猛击|A lively, often noisy party or celebration; a hard, resounding blow.|We're having a bash for his birthday. 我们要为他举办一个生日派对。|近义词：party, celebration, hit, strike|反义词:miss, failure|词性:名词, 动词

bash:/bæʃ/| n. vt.|尝试，努力；猛烈抨击|An attempt to do or achieve something; to criticize someone or something severely.|I had a bash at fixing the car. 我试着修了修车。|近义词：attempt, try, criticize, slam|反义词:avoid, praise|词性:名词, 动词
