concentrated:/ˈkɒnsəntreɪtɪd/ (UK) /ˈkɑːnsəntreɪtɪd/ (US)| adj.|集中的，浓缩的，专心的|Existing in a high proportion relative to other substances; having had water or other diluting agent removed or reduced.|The company is selling a concentrated form of orange juice.|近义词：intense, strong, potent, rich, thick, undiluted|反义词:dilute, weak, thin|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin concentratus, past participle of concentrare “to center, concentrate,” from com- “together” + centrum “center.”
