hanging-about:/ˈhæŋɪŋ əˈbaʊt/
n.|闲逛，无所事事|The act of spending time idly or loitering. |例句：There was a lot of hanging-about while we waited for the bus. 我们等公共汽车的时候，闲逛了很久。|近义词：loitering, idling, dawdling|反义词：working, hurrying, being busy|词性:名词
hanging about:/ˈhæŋɪŋ əˈbaʊt/
n.|闲逛，拖延|The act of delaying or wasting time. |例句：Stop all that hanging about and get to work! 别再闲逛了，快去工作！|近义词：delaying, procrastinating, tarrying|反义词：expediting, hastening, rushing|词性:名词
