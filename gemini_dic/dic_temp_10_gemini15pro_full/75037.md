friezing:/ˈfriːzɪŋ/| n. |壁带, 横条, 饰带|A decorative band, typically sculpted or painted, especially one extending along the upper part of a wall.|The friezing above the doorway depicted scenes from Greek mythology. 门口上方的饰带描绘了希腊神话中的场景。|synonyms: border, band, trim, molding|antonyms: plainness, simplicity|noun


friezing:/ˈfriːzɪŋ/| adj. |极冷的|Extremely cold.|It's frizing outside! 外面冷极了！|synonyms: freezing, icy, frigid, arctic|antonyms: hot, warm, balmy|adjective
