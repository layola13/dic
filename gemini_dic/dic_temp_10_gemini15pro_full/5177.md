alleviators:/əˈliːvieɪtərz/| n. |缓和者，减轻者，缓解物|Something that alleviates pain, suffering, or burden.|例句：The new pain medication proved to be an effective alleviator for her chronic back pain.  这种新的止痛药证明能有效缓解她的慢性背痛。

例句：The government introduced several alleviators to ease the financial burden on small businesses. 政府引入了几项措施来减轻小企业的财政负担。|近义词：remedies, mitigators, assuagers, palliatives |反义词: aggravators, exacerbators |词性: noun (plural)

词源:  From the verb "alleviate," which comes from Late Latin *alleviatus, past participle of alleviāre "to lighten," from Latin ad- "to" + levis "light."
