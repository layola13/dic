heterosporous:/ˌhetərəˈspɔːrəs/| adj.|异孢子的|Producing two different kinds of spores, one large (megaspore) and one small (microspore), which develop into female and male gametophytes respectively.|Ferns are not heterosporous, but seed plants are. |近义词:anisosporous|反义词:homosporous|形容词
