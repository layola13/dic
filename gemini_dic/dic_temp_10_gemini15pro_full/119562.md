microsystems:/ˈmaɪkroʊˌsɪstəmz/
noun
微系统；微型系统
Miniaturized systems, typically fabricated using integrated circuit technology, that combine electronic, mechanical, optical, and other components on a single chip or substrate.
例句：Researchers in the field of microsystems are developing tiny sensors for medical implants.  微系统领域的研究人员正在开发用于医疗植入物的微型传感器。

例句：Microsystems technology has revolutionized many industries, including healthcare and aerospace. 微系统技术彻底改变了许多行业，包括医疗保健和航空航天。
近义词：MEMS (Microelectromechanical systems), miniature systems
反义词：macrosystems
词性：n.


ETYMOLOGY->
From micro- + system.  "Micro-" comes from the Greek "mikros" meaning "small," and "system" comes from the Greek "systēma" meaning "organized whole."
