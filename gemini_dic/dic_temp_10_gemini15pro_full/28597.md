careless:/ˈkerləs/| adj.|粗心的，漫不经心的|Not paying enough attention to what you are doing, so that you make mistakes or cause accidents.|He made a careless mistake and lost the game. 他犯了一个粗心的错误，输掉了比赛。|近义词：negligent, thoughtless, inattentive, heedless|反义词：careful, cautious, attentive, meticulous|词性:形容词
词源: From Middle English *carles, from Old English *carlēas (“free from care, without anxiety”), equivalent to care +‎ -less. Cognate with Scots carless (“careless”), West Frisian soarchleas (“careless”), Dutch zorgeloos (“careless”), German sorglos (“careless”).
