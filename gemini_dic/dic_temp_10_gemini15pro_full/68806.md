fasciation:/ˌfæʃiˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 簇生，带化 |The abnormal flattening or coalescence of plant parts, such as stems, fruits, or flower heads, into a single broadened or crested structure.  植物部位（例如茎、果实或花头）异常扁平化或合并成单个加宽或嵴状结构。 | The cactus exhibited fasciation, with its stem flattened into a fan shape.  这棵仙人掌呈现出簇生现象，茎部扁平成扇形。| synonyms: cresting, banding | antonyms: normal growth | noun

fasciation:/ˌfæʃiˈeɪʃən/ | n. | 迷恋，着迷 |  Intense or obsessive interest or preoccupation. 强烈或过度的兴趣或关注 | He had a fasciation with trains and could name every model. 他对火车很着迷，能说出每一种型号。 | synonyms: obsession, infatuation, captivation | antonyms: disinterest, apathy | noun
