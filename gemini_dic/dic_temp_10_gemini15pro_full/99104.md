intercardinal:/ˌɪntərˈkɑːrdɪnl/ | adj. |居间的，介于主方位点之间的| Situated between the cardinal points (north, south, east, and west).|  The intercardinal directions are northeast, southeast, southwest, and northwest.  介于主方位点之间的方向是东北，东南，西南和西北。|  The wind blew from an intercardinal direction. 风从一个介于主方位点之间的方向吹来。 |Synonyms: intermediate, mid, median| Antonyms: cardinal| adjective
