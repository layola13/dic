biodiverse:/ˌbaɪoʊdaɪˈvɜːrs/
adjective
生物多样性的
Having a high level of biodiversity; rich in variety of plant and animal life.  Characterized by a great diversity of life forms.
例句：The Amazon rainforest is a biodiverse region. 亚马逊雨林是一个生物多样性丰富的地区。

例句：Coral reefs are known for their biodiverse ecosystems. 珊瑚礁以其生物多样性的生态系统而闻名。

近义词：species-rich, varied, multifarious
反义词：homogeneous, uniform, monotonous
词性：adjective

Etymology:
bio- (life) + diverse (different)
