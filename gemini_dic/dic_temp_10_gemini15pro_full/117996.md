memorializers:/məˈmɔːriəlaɪzərz/
n.
纪念者，追念者
People who create or establish memorials to commemorate someone or something.
The memorializers dedicated a plaque to the fallen soldiers. 纪念者们为阵亡的士兵献上了一块牌匾。
commemorators, celebrants
forgetters, ignorers
noun
