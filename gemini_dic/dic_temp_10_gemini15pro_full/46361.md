dactylotheca:/ˌdæktɪləˈθiːkə/
noun
指鞘；趾鞘
A tubular sheath enclosing a finger or toe, especially in some invertebrates.
The dactylotheca of the crab protects its delicate claws. 螃蟹的指鞘保护着它脆弱的爪子。
近义词: sheath, covering
反义词: (No direct antonym)
词性: n.


Etymology:  From Greek *daktylos* (finger) + *theke* (case, sheath).
