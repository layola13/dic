earthed:/ɜːrθt/ /ɝːθt/
adjective
接地的；接地的
connected to the ground, allowing electricity to flow into the earth
The electrician earthed the appliance. 电工将电器接地。
grounded, connected
unearthed, ungrounded
adjective

earthed:/ɜːrθt/ /ɝːθt/
verb
接地；埋葬（earth的过去式和过去分词）
to connect (an electrical device) to the earth
He earthed the wire to prevent electric shocks. 他将电线接地以防止电击。
grounded, buried
unearthed, exhumed
verb
