Judeans:/ˈdʒuːdiənz/
noun
犹太人；犹太居民
People of Judea, an ancient region in Palestine.
The Judeans revolted against Roman rule. 犹太人反抗罗马统治。
近义词:Jews, Israelites
反义词:Gentiles
词性:n.
