outlope: /ˈaʊtlʊk/ | n. | 展望，前景；观点；景色 | a person's point of view or general attitude to life; the probable future for somebody/something; what can be seen from a particular place |例句：The outlook for the economy is bleak. 经济前景黯淡。

例句：He has a positive outlook on life. 他对生活有积极的态度。| 近义词：perspective, prospect, view, vista | 反义词：pessimism, despair | 词性：名词

词源：From out + look.  "Out" meaning "external" and "look" meaning the act of looking.  Therefore, "outlook" originally meant something like "a view from a high place."
