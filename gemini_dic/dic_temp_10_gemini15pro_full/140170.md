offensively:/əˈfɛnsɪvli/| adv.|冒犯地，无礼地，攻击性地|In a way that causes someone to feel resentful, upset, or annoyed.  Causing offense; unpleasant.|He spoke offensively about his ex-wife. 他冒犯地谈论了他的前妻。|Synonym: insultingly, rudely, abusively, unpleasantly, disagreeably |Antonym: politely, respectfully, pleasantly, agreeably|adverb

offensively:/əˈfɛnsɪvli/| adv.|令人不快地，讨厌地|In a way that is unpleasant or causes disgust.|The room smelled offensively of stale cigarette smoke. 房间里散发着令人作呕的陈旧香烟味。|Synonym: displeasingly, disgustingly, nauseatingly, repulsively|Antonym: pleasantly, appealingly, attractively|adverb

ETYMOLOGY->
From offensive + -ly.  Offensive comes from Old French offensif, from Late Latin offensivus, from Latin offendere "to strike against, stumble, displease."
