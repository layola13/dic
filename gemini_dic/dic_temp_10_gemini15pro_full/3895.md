agreeingly:/əˈɡriːɪŋli/
adverb
同意地，一致地
In a manner that shows agreement or consent.
She nodded agreeably. 她同意地点点头。
He smiled agreeably at her suggestion. 他对她的建议报以赞同的微笑。
近义词:concordantly, harmoniously, approvingly
反义词:disagreeably, discordantly
词性:adverb

词源:
From agree + -ing + -ly.  "Agree" comes from Old French agreer, from a- (to) + gre (pleasure, satisfaction), from Latin gratus (pleasing).
