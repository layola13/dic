advocate: /ˈædvəkət/ (US) /ˈædvəkeɪt/ (UK)| n. vt.| 提倡者，拥护者；辩护律师；倡导，拥护，主张|A person who publicly supports or recommends a particular cause or policy.  He is a strong advocate for free speech. 他是言论自由的坚定拥护者。|例句：She advocates for women's rights. 她倡导妇女权利。

He's a well-known advocate of animal rights. 他是一位著名的动物权利倡导者。|近义词：supporter, proponent, champion, backer|反义词：opponent, critic, adversary|词性: noun, verb

advocate: /ˈædvəkeɪt/ (UK) /ˈædvəˌkeɪt/ (US)| vt.| 倡导，拥护，主张|To publicly support or recommend a particular cause or policy.  The report advocates that all children be taught to swim. 该报告主张所有儿童都应该学习游泳。|例句：He advocated for stricter gun control laws. 他主张更严格的枪支管制法律。

Many experts advocate breastfeeding for the first six months of a baby's life. 许多专家提倡在婴儿出生后的头六个月进行母乳喂养。|近义词：support, promote, champion, espouse|反义词：oppose, reject, condemn|词性: verb


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *advocatus*, past participle of *advocare* "to summon, to call to one's aid," from *ad-* "to" + *vocare* "to call."

USAGE->
一般现在时:
He advocates for environmental protection. = 他倡导环境保护。
She advocates a healthy lifestyle. = 她倡导健康的生活方式。

一般过去时:
They advocated for peace during the war. = 战争期间，他们倡导和平。
He advocated the use of renewable energy. = 他主张使用可再生能源。

现在完成时:
She has advocated for this cause for many years. = 多年来，她一直倡导这项事业。
The organization has advocated for the rights of the disabled. = 该组织一直倡导残疾人的权利。

过去完成时:
He had advocated for this policy before it became law. = 在这项政策成为法律之前，他就一直倡导它。
They had advocated the use of this technology for years. = 他们多年来一直倡导使用这项技术。

将来时:
We will advocate for changes to the current system. = 我们将倡导改变现有制度。
She will advocate for the rights of refugees. = 她将倡导难民的权利。

虚拟语气:
If I were in charge, I would advocate for a different approach. = 如果我负责，我会主张采取不同的方法。
I wish they would advocate more strongly for this issue. = 我希望他们能更强烈地倡导这个问题。

被动语态:
A new approach was advocated by the experts. = 专家们倡导了一种新方法。
Further research is being advocated by the scientists. = 科学家们正在倡导进一步的研究。

疑问句:
Do you advocate this policy? = 你支持这项政策吗？
What changes do you advocate? = 你主张哪些改变？

否定句:
I do not advocate violence. = 我不提倡暴力。
She does not advocate for this particular solution. = 她不支持这个特定的解决方案。


PHRASE->
advocate for (something/someone) = 倡导（某事/某人）
a strong advocate = 坚定的拥护者
an outspoken advocate = 直言不讳的拥护者
a leading advocate = 主要倡导者
advocate change/reform = 倡导变革/改革
