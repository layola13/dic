hipster: /ˈhɪpstər/ (US) /ˈhɪpstə/ (UK)| n.| 时髦人士；赶时髦的人；嬉皮士|A person who follows the latest trends and fashions, especially those regarded as being outside the cultural mainstream.|He's a hipster who only listens to vinyl records. 他是个只听黑胶唱片的时髦人士。|Synonyms: trendy, fashionable, stylish, cool, bohemian|Antonyms: mainstream, conventional, traditional|Noun
