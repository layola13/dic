homologize:/həˈmɒlədʒaɪz/ (US) /həˈmɑːlədʒaɪz/ (UK)| vt.|使同源；使同系；使符合标准；比准|To show or prove to be homologous; to make homologous; to make something conform to a standard|The government is trying to homologize building regulations across the country. 政府正试图使全国的建筑法规统一。|synonyms: standardize, normalize, conform, equate|antonyms: differentiate, distinguish, diverge|verb

homologization:/həˌmɒlədʒaɪˈzeɪʃn/ (US) /həˌmɑːlədʒaɪˈzeɪʃn/ (UK)| n.|同源化；同系化；标准化；比准|The act or process of homologizing|The homologization of car safety standards across Europe has improved road safety. 欧洲汽车安全标准的统一提高了道路安全。|synonyms: standardization, normalization, conformity|antonyms: differentiation, distinction, divergence|noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From French *homologiser*, from Greek *homologos* (agreeing, corresponding), from *homos* (same) + *logos* (word, reason).
