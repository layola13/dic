disengagednesses:/dɪsɪnˈɡeɪdʒɪdnɪsɪz/
n.
脱离; 冷漠;  不感兴趣
The state of being disengaged; a lack of connection or involvement.
Her disengagedness from the project worried her colleagues. 她对这个项目的漠不关心让她的同事们很担心。
Synonyms: detachment, aloofness, indifference, apathy
Antonyms: engagement, involvement, interest, enthusiasm
词性: 名词
