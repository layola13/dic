cestraciont:/ˌsɛstrəˈʃɒnt/ | n. | 异齿鲨 |A shark of the order Cestraciontiformes, characterized by a heterodont dentition adapted for crushing shellfish. |  Example: The cestraciont is an ancient group of sharks. 异齿鲨是一个古老的鲨鱼种群。
Example:  Fossil evidence suggests cestracionts were once much more diverse. 化石证据表明异齿鲨曾经更加多样化。 | 近义词:bullhead shark, Port Jackson shark | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a specific type of shark) | 词性:noun

Etymology: From Modern Latin *Cestraciontēs*, from Ancient Greek κέστρα (késtra, “weapon, tool for piercing or weaving”) + -ont (suffix forming nouns denoting agents or instruments).
