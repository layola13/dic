outreckons: /ˌaʊtˈrɛkənz/
verb
胜过，超过，计算得比…多
surpasses in calculating; calculates a sum to be greater than another
The accountant outreckoned the profits by a considerable margin. 会计师大大高估了利润。
近义词: outcalculate, surpass, exceed, outstrip
反义词: underestimate, fall short of
词性: v.

词源:  "out-" (prefix meaning "exceeding," "surpassing") + "reckon" (verb meaning "to calculate," "to count")
