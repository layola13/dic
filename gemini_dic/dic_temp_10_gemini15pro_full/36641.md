collectorate:/kəˈlɛktərɪt/| n.| 收税员办公室；收税区；收税员的职位|The collectorate is responsible for collecting taxes in this district.  这个地区的收税员办公室负责征税。|The documents were submitted to the collectorate for processing. 这些文件已提交给收税员办公室进行处理。|近义词：tax office, revenue office|反义词: None|词性:noun

Etymology: From collector + -ate.  "Collector" comes from the Latin word "collector," meaning "gatherer," from the verb "colligere," meaning "to gather together."  The suffix "-ate" forms nouns denoting offices, functions, or groups of people associated with a particular activity.
