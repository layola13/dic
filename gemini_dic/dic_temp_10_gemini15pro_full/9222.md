antipapacy:/æntiˈpeɪpəsi/
n.
反对教皇制; 反对教皇权威
Opposition to the authority of the pope, especially the temporal power of the papacy.
The rise of antipapacy sentiment led to several reform movements. 反对教皇情绪的兴起导致了几次改革运动。
Martin Luther's writings fueled the flames of antipapacy. 马丁·路德的著作加剧了反对教皇的情绪。
synonyms: anti-papalism
antonyms: papism, pro-papalism
noun
