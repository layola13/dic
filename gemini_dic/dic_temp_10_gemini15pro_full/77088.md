garrisoned:/ˈɡærɪsənd/
Verb|被驻守的，驻扎的|Provided with soldiers for defense; stationed in a place for its defense. |The city was garrisoned by troops.  这座城市驻扎着军队。|例句：The fort was garrisoned by a small detachment of soldiers.  这个堡垒由一小队士兵驻守。|近义词：occupied, defended, fortified, manned, stationed|反义词：ungarrisoned, undefended|v.

garrisoned:/ˈɡærɪsənd/
