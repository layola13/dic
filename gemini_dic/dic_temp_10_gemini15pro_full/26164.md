PHRASE->

button up:  扣好扣子  Button up your coat, it's cold outside.  扣好你的外套，外面很冷。
button down: 扣上（衬衫的）所有扣子 He buttoned down his shirt for the formal occasion. 他为正式场合扣上了衬衫的所有扣子。
button through:  用纽扣固定住  This dress buttons through the front. 这件连衣裙从前面用纽扣固定。
be buttoned up:  保守的，不苟言笑的 He's very buttoned up and doesn't show his emotions easily. 他很保守，不轻易表露自己的情感。
on the button:  准时，正点 The train arrived on the button. 火车准时到达。
