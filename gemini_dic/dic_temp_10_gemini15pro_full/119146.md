meve: /miːv/
n.
梦寐以求之物; 渴望得到的东西;
Something ardently desired or longed for.
例句：A trip to Paris is my meve.  去巴黎旅行是我梦寐以求的事。

例句：His meve is to own a sports car. 他渴望拥有一辆跑车。

近义词：desire, dream, aspiration, longing, yearning, wish, ambition
反义词：aversion, dislike, hatred, indifference

词性: 名词
