delitescence:/ˌdɛlɪˈtɛsəns/
n.
潮解; 溶解
the process by which a substance absorbs moisture from the atmosphere until it dissolves in the absorbed water and forms a solution
The delitescence of the chemical compound surprised the researchers.  这种化合物的潮解让研究人员感到惊讶。

近义词: deliquescence, dissolving, liquefaction
反义词: desiccation, drying, dehydration
词性: noun


Etymology:
From Latin dēlītēscere (“to hide, conceal oneself, vanish”), from dē- (“down, away from”) + latēscere (“to become hidden, lie concealed”), from lateō (“I lie hidden”).  Surface analysis suggests "to become liquid and disappear".
