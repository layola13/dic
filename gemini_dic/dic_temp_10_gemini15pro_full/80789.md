gotcha:/ˈɡɒtʃə/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːtʃə/ (US)| n. |陷阱，圈套；意外的困难；明白，了解|An unexpected and usually unpleasant problem or difficulty; I understand (informal).|

例句：
The contract is full of gotchas, so read it carefully.
合同里充满了陷阱，所以要仔细阅读。

例句：
"I see," he said. "Gotcha."
“我明白了，”他说。“知道了。”


近义词：trap, pitfall, snag, catch, quirk, "I understand", "I get it"|
反义词:advantage, boon|
词性:noun


gotcha:/ˈɡɒtʃə/ (UK) /ˈɡɑːtʃə/ (US)| interj. |（表示理解）知道了，明白了|Used to express understanding or agreement.|

例句：
"Meet me at the usual place at eight." "Gotcha."
“八点老地方见。”“明白了。”


例句：
"So you'll pick up the dry cleaning on your way home?" "Gotcha."
“那你回家路上顺便取一下干洗的衣服？” “知道了。”

近义词：OK, alright, understood, I see|
反义词: I don't understand|
词性:interjection
