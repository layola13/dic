nonmeditative:/ˌnɒnˌmedɪˈteɪtɪv/
adjective
非冥想的，不沉思的
Not meditative; not given to or involving meditation.
He took a nonmeditative walk, simply enjoying the fresh air. 他散步时没有沉思，只是单纯地享受新鲜空气。
The artist's nonmeditative approach resulted in a vibrant and spontaneous painting. 这位艺术家不加思索的创作方式，成就了一幅充满活力和自然奔放的画作。
Synonyms: unreflective, unthinking, mindless
Antonyms: meditative, contemplative, reflective
词性: 形容词
