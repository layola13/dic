enroller:/ɪnˈroʊlər/
noun
登记人；注册人；招收者
A person who enrolls or registers someone or something.
The school enroller was busy processing applications. 学校的登记员正忙着处理申请。
近义词: registrar, recorder
反义词: N/A
词性: n.

enroll:/ɪnˈroʊl/
verb
登记；注册；招生
To officially register as a member of an institution, course, or program.
He enrolled in the online course. 他注册了在线课程。
近义词: register, sign up
反义词: withdraw, unregister
词性: v.
