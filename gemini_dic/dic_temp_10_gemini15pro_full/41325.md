coresearcher:/ˌkoʊˈriːsɜːrtʃər/ | n. | 共同研究者；合作研究员 | A person who conducts research with another person or group. |  Example: Dr. Smith and his coresearcher published their findings in a scientific journal. 史密斯博士和他的共同研究者在一份科学期刊上发表了他们的研究结果。

Example: The coresearchers worked tirelessly to collect and analyze the data.  合作研究人员不知疲倦地收集和分析数据。 | 近义词: collaborator, research partner | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "competitor" in a research context) | 词性: noun
