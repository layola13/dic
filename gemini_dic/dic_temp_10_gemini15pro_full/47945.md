decidability:/dɪˌsaɪdəˈbɪləti/| n.|可判定性|The quality of being decidable; the property of a problem or question for which an effective method exists to determine a definite solution.|The decidability of the halting problem is a fundamental concept in computer science.  可停机问题的可判定性是计算机科学中的一个基本概念。|synonyms: solvability, computability|antonyms: undecidability, insolvability|noun


decidability:/dɪˌsaɪdəˈbɪləti/| n.|可决定性|The quality of a question or issue that can be definitively resolved or settled.|The decidability of the case rests on the judge's interpretation of the law.  案件的可决定性取决于法官对法律的解释。|synonyms: resolvability, determinability|antonyms: indeterminacy, undecidability|noun
