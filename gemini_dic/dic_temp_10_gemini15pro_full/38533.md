concise:/kənˈsaɪs/| adj.|简明的，简洁的|Expressing much in few words; brief and to the point.|Her writing style is concise and effective. 她的写作风格简洁有效。|近义词：brief, succinct, terse, compact|反义词：verbose, prolix, lengthy, wordy|词性:形容词
concite:/kənˈsaɪt/| v.|以简洁的文字表达；使简洁|To express something with concise language; to make concise.|He concites his arguments into a single paragraph. 他把他的论点精简成一段。|近义词：abbreviate, condense, compress, summarize|反义词：expand, elaborate, lengthen|词性:动词
