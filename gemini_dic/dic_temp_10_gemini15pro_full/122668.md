modulate:/ˈmɒdjʊleɪt/ (UK), /ˈmɑːdʒəleɪt/ (US) | v. | 调节；调制；变调 | to change the sound of your voice in order to make it more interesting or to express an emotion; to change the frequency, amplitude, phase, or other characteristic of a carrier wave |  He carefully modulated his voice to sound reassuring. 他小心翼翼地调节自己的声音，使其听起来令人安心。| synonyms: adjust, regulate, vary, modify | antonyms: fix, stabilize, maintain | verb

modulate:/ˈmɒdjʊleɪt/ (UK), /ˈmɑːdʒəleɪt/ (US) | n. | 调制；调节 | the act of modulating something | The modulation of the signal allowed for clear communication. 信号的调制实现了清晰的通信。| synonyms: adjustment, regulation, variation, modification | antonyms: fixation, stabilization, maintenance | noun
