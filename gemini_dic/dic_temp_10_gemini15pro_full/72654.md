fmri:/ˈɛf ɛm ɑːr ˈaɪ/ | n. |功能性磁共振成像|Functional magnetic resonance imaging (fMRI) is a neuroimaging procedure using MRI technology that measures brain activity by detecting changes associated with blood flow.|Researchers used fMRI to study brain activity during problem-solving tasks. 研究人员使用功能性磁共振成像来研究解决问题任务期间的大脑活动。|Synonyms: neuroimaging, brain scan|Antonyms: None|Noun
