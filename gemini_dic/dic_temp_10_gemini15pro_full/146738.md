overhoused:/ˌoʊvərˈhaʊzd/| adj.|住房过剩的，居住空间过大的|Having a dwelling that is larger than one needs or can reasonably afford.|They felt overhoused in their five-bedroom mansion after the children moved out. 孩子们搬出去后，他们觉得住在这栋五居室的豪宅里空间过剩。|近义词：underoccupied, underused|反义词：underhoused, overcrowded|词性:形容词
词源:  "over-" (prefix meaning "excessively") + "housed" (past participle of "house," meaning "to provide with lodging")
