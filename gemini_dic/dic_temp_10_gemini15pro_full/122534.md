modder:/ˈmɒdə(r)/ (UK) /ˈmɑːdər/ (US)| n. |修改者，模组制作者|A person who modifies something, typically software or hardware, to alter its functionality or appearance, especially in video games.|例句：The modder created a new level for the game.  这个模组制作者为游戏创建了一个新关卡。

例句：Modders are often responsible for adding new content to older games. 模组制作者通常负责为老游戏添加新内容。|近义词：tweaker, hacker, customizer|反义词:developer (sometimes), user (in a general sense)|词性:noun
