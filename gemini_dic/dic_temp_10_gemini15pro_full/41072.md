词源：From the verb "to copper" + "-ed" suffix, meaning "covered with copper." The verb "to copper" comes from the noun "copper."  Copper itself comes from Old English *coper,  ultimately from Latin *Cyprium (aes), meaning "(ore) of Cyprus," as Cyprus was a major source of copper in antiquity.
