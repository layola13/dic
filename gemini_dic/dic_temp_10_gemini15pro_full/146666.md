overhaughty: /ˌoʊvərˈhɔːti/ | adj. | 过于傲慢的，极其自负的 | Excessively proud and arrogant; disdainful. | He gave an overhaughty laugh, clearly dismissing my suggestion. 他傲慢地大笑了一声，显然对我的建议不屑一顾。|  The overhaughty CEO alienated his employees with his condescending remarks. 傲慢的CEO用居高临下的言论疏远了他的员工。 | 近义词: arrogant, supercilious, disdainful, haughty, conceited | 反义词: humble, modest, unassuming | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From "over-" (excessively) + "haughty" (proud and disdainful).  "Haughty" comes from Old French "haut" (high), ultimately from Latin "altus".
