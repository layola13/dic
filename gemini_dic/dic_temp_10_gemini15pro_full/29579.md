catechetic:/ˌkætɪˈkɛtɪk/| adj.|教义问答式的，教导的|Relating to or resembling religious instruction by question and answer.|The priest's catechetic method engaged the children and helped them learn the tenets of their faith. 神父的问答式教学法吸引了孩子们，并帮助他们学习了信仰的原则。|近义词：didactic, instructional, pedagogical|反义词：uninformative, unenlightening|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *catecheticus*, from Greek *katēkhētikos* "instructive," from *katēkhein* "to teach orally, instruct," from *kata-* "down" + *ēkhein* "to sound, resound; to hold."
