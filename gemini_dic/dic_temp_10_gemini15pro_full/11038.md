arcane:/ɑːrˈkeɪn/| adj.|神秘的，晦涩难懂的|Known or understood by only a few; mysterious.|例句：The professor spoke in arcane language that only a few students could understand. 教授用只有少数学生才能理解的晦涩难懂的语言讲话。

例句：The group's arcane rituals were a mystery to outsiders.  这个团体神秘的仪式对于外人来说是个谜。|近义词：esoteric, obscure, cryptic, mysterious|反义词：common, known, familiar, clear|词性:形容词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *arcanus (“secret, hidden”), from Latin arcānus, from arca (“chest, box”).  The original sense was of something locked away in a chest.
