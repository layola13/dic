bongos:/ˈbɒŋɡəʊz/ (UK) /ˈbɑːŋɡoʊz/ (US)| n. |邦戈鼓|A pair of small drums played with the fingers, typically held between the knees.|例句：He played a lively rhythm on the bongos. 他用邦戈鼓演奏了一段欢快的节奏。

例句：She bought a new set of bongos for her son's birthday. 她为儿子生日买了一套新的邦戈鼓。|近义词：drums, percussion|反义词: (No direct antonym for a musical instrument)|词性:noun
