eeny:/ˈiːni/ | determiner, pronoun | 一种用于从一组事物中随机选择一个的词语，通常用于儿童游戏或押韵 | Used to select someone or something at random, typically in children's games or rhymes. Part of the "eeny, meeny, miny, moe" rhyme. | Eeny, meeny, miny, moe, catch a tiger by the toe. If he hollers, let him go, eeny, meeny, miny, moe.  一二三，山羊抓，抓到谁，谁倒霉。| 近义词: none (in this context - the word itself doesn't have direct synonyms but the function is random selection) | 反义词: none | 词性: determiner, pronoun
