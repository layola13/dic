ordinariness:/ˌɔːrdɪˈnɛrɪnɪs/
n.
平凡；普通；平常
The state or quality of being ordinary.
The ordinariness of her life surprised him.  她的平凡生活让他感到惊讶。
Near Synonyms: normality, commonness, usualness, mediocrity, mundanity
Antonyms: extraordinariness, uniqueness, remarkableness, singularity
词性: 名词
