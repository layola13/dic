hookah:/ˈhʊkə/ (US), /ˈhʊkɑː/ (UK)| n. |水烟袋，水烟壶|A water pipe with a long flexible tube attached to a container where tobacco is burned and the smoke is drawn through water. |例句：
We relaxed in the cafe, smoking a hookah. 我们在咖啡馆里放松，抽着水烟。

She bought a new hookah with an intricate design. 她买了一个设计复杂的新水烟袋。


|近义词：water pipe, narghile, shisha|反义词: None (no direct antonym)|词性: Noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Arabic "huqqah," from "huqqa," meaning "jar, bottle, casket," ultimately from Aramaic.
