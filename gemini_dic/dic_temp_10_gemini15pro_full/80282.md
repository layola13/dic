"goinging" is not a standard English word.  The correct word is "going," which is the present participle of the verb "go."  Therefore, the following analysis pertains to "going":

going: /ˈɡoʊɪŋ/ (US) /ˈɡəʊɪŋ/ (UK)| v. (present participle)|进行，离去，发生| The car is going fast. 汽车跑得很快。|近义词：leaving, departing, proceeding, happening, occurring|反义词：coming, arriving, staying|verb
