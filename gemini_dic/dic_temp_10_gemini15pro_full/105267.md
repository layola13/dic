what: /wɒt/ (UK) /wʌt/ (US) | pron. det. adv. | 什么，多么 | used to ask for information about something |

英文解析:  "What" is a versatile word used to ask questions about things, actions, or qualities. It can function as a pronoun, determiner, or adverb.
