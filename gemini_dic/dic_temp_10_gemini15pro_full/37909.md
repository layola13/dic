complementary:/ˌkɒmplɪˈmentri/ /ˌkɑːmplɪˈmentri/| adj.|补充的，互补的| Enhancing or emphasizing the qualities of something else, especially by being different but in a way that combines well.|例句：The complementary colors red and green look great together. 红色和绿色这两种互补色搭配起来很好看。|例句：His skills are complementary to hers, making them a great team. 他的技能与她的技能互补，使他们成为一个很棒的团队。|近义词：supplemental, completing, matching|反义词：contrasting, clashing|词性:形容词
ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *complementārius, from Latin complementum (“that which fills up or completes”) + -ārius.
