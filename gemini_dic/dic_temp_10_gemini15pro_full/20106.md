biotechnological:/ˌbaɪoʊˌtɛknəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/
adjective
生物技术的
Relating to or using biotechnology; produced or developed using biotechnology.
Biotechnological advances have revolutionized medicine. 生物技术的进步彻底改变了医学。
Biotechnological research is crucial for developing new treatments. 生物技术研究对于开发新疗法至关重要。
Synonyms: genetic engineering, bioengineering, recombinant DNA technology
Antonyms: traditional, conventional
词性: 形容词
