exhauster:/ɪɡˈzɔːstər/ | n. | 排气装置，抽风机 | A device that removes air or other gases from a space, creating a partial vacuum. | The exhauster in the kitchen removed the smoke and cooking smells. 厨房里的排气装置排除了油烟和烹饪气味。 | The mine is fitted with powerful exhausters. 该矿配备了强大的抽风机。| synonyms: extractor, fan, ventilator, blower | antonyms:  compressor, intake | noun
