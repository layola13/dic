nondisastrously:/ˌnɒndɪˈzɑːstrəsli/ | adv. | 无灾无难地；平安地；顺利地 |  In a way that is not disastrous; without disaster.  It describes an action or event that occurred without any major negative consequences. | The mission, though challenging, concluded nondisastrously.  虽然任务具有挑战性，但最终平安無事地完成了。| synonym: safely, successfully, uneventfully | antonym: disastrously, catastrophically | Adverb

Etymology:
non- (prefix):  Meaning "not," from Latin non "not."
disastrous: From disaster + -ous. Disaster comes from Old Italian disastro "ill-starred," from dis- "bad" + astro "star."
-ly (suffix): Forms adverbs from adjectives.
