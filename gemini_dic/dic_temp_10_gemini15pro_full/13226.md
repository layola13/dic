atania:/əˈteɪniə/ | n. |阿塔尼亚（一种植物的属名）| A genus of flowering plants in the family Apiaceae, native to the Canary Islands.  It is sometimes included in the genus *Bupleurum*.|The atania plant is endemic to the Canary Islands. 阿塔尼亚植物是加那利群岛的特有物种。| The atania thrived in the volcanic soil. 阿塔尼亚在火山土壤中茁壮成长。|Synonym: Canary Samphire (when referring to *Atania canariensis*)|Antonym: N/A|noun
