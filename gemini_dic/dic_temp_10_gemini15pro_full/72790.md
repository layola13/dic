foist:/fɔɪst/ | vt. | 偷偷插入；强加；欺骗；把...硬塞给 | to introduce or insert surreptitiously or unwarrantably; to force upon or impose by fraud; to pass off as genuine or worthy | The salesman tried to foist a damaged car on me.  推销员试图把一辆破损的汽车硬塞给我。| synonyms: impose, inflict, palm off, pass off | antonyms:  take, accept, withdraw | verb
