convincing:/kənˈvɪnsɪŋ/| adj.|令人信服的；有说服力的|Making someone believe that something is true or real.  Able to persuade somebody to believe that something is true or to act.|Her argument was very convincing. 她的论点非常令人信服。|persuasive, compelling, credible|unconvincing, doubtful, dubious|adjective

convince:/kənˈvɪns/| vt.|使确信；使信服|To persuade someone to believe something or to do something.|I couldn't convince him that I was telling the truth. 我无法让他相信我说的是实话。|persuade, assure, satisfy|dissuade, deter, discourage|verb
