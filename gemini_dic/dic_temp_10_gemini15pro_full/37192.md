comedy: /ˈkɒmədi/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmədi/ (US)| n.| 喜剧 |A play, film, or other entertainment of light and humorous character with a happy or cheerful ending; a dramatic work in which the motive is primarily that of laughter.| We went to see a comedy last night.  我们昨晚去看了一场喜剧。|Synonyms: farce, humor, satire |Antonyms: tragedy, drama |noun

comedy: /ˈkɒmədi/ (UK) /ˈkɑːmədi/ (US)| n.| 喜剧性 |Professional entertainment consisting of jokes and sketches, intended to make an audience laugh.| His life is a comedy. 他的人生就是一出喜剧。 |Synonyms: funniness, hilarity |Antonyms: seriousness, tragedy |noun
