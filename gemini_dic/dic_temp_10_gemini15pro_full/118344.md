meridionally:/ˌmɛrɪˈdɪənəli/ | adv. |南北地，经线方向地 | In a north-south direction, along a meridian. |例句：The mountain range runs meridionally. 这座山脉呈南北走向。|例句：The wind blew meridionally across the plains. 风沿着经线方向吹过平原。|近义词：north-south, longitudinally |反义词: latitudinally, east-west |词性:副词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *merīdiōnālis, from Latin merīdiānus (“of or belonging to midday, southern”) + -ly (adverbial suffix).  The Latin word is derived from merīdiēs (“midday”), from medius (“middle”) + diēs (“day”).
