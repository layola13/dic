limbic:/ˈlɪmbɪk/| adj.|边缘的，大脑边缘系统的|Relating to the limbic system, a complex system of nerves and networks in the brain, involving several areas near the edge of the cortex concerned with instinct and mood.  它与本能和情绪有关。|The limbic system plays a crucial role in regulating emotions. 边缘系统在调节情绪方面起着至关重要的作用。|The patient's limbic responses were heightened due to the trauma. 由于创伤，病人的边缘系统反应增强了。|近义词:emotional, instinctive|反义词:cognitive, rational|词性:adjective
