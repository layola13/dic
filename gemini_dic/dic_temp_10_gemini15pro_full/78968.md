girlish:/ˈɡɜːrlɪʃ/ (US) /ˈɡɜːlɪʃ/ (UK)| adj.|少女般的；女孩子气的|Having qualities or characteristics traditionally associated with young girls.|She had a girlish giggle that was infectious. 她咯咯地笑着，像个小女孩，很有感染力。|例句：

Her girlish charm captivated everyone in the room.
她少女般的魅力迷住了房间里的每一个人。

He retained a certain girlish shyness even in adulthood.
即使成年后，他仍然保留着某种少女般的羞涩。

|近义词：feminine, maidenly, youthful|反义词：boyish, manly, mature|词性:adjective
