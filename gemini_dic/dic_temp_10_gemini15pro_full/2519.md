ETYMOLOGY->
From administrator + -ship.  "Administrator" comes from the Late Latin "administrator," agent noun from "administrare" meaning "to manage, direct," from "ad-" (to) + "ministrare" (to serve, minister).  "-ship" is an Old English suffix denoting state, condition, or office.
