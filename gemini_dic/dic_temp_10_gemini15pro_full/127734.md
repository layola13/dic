negotiability:/nɪˌɡoʊʃiəˈbɪləti/
n.|可转让性，可协商性|The quality of being transferable by endorsement or delivery, or of being capable of being negotiated.  This often applies to financial instruments like checks or promissory notes.|The negotiability of the bond made it an attractive investment.  该债券的可转让性使其成为一项有吸引力的投资。|transferability, tradability|non-negotiability
词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From negotiate + -ability.  "Negotiate" comes from the Late Latin "negotiari" meaning "to carry on business,"  from "negotium" meaning "business," from "nec otium" meaning "not leisure."
