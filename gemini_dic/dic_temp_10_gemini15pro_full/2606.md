adolescence: /ˌædəˈlesəns/ | n. | 青春期 | The period of life when a child develops into an adult. |  She had a troubled adolescence. 她有一个麻烦的青春期。

My brother is going through adolescence right now. 我的弟弟现在正在经历青春期。

近义词: puberty, youth, teens | 反义词: childhood, adulthood, infancy | 词性: 名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *adolescentia, from Latin adolescēns (“growing up, young”), present active participle of adolescō (“I grow up”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + ōlēs (“to grow”), from Proto-Indo-European *h₂el- (“to grow, nourish”).
