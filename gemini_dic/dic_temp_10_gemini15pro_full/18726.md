bergander:/bɜːrˈɡændər/ | n. |秋沙鸭（雄鸟）| A male shelduck, characterized by its brightly colored plumage. |  The bergander stood out amongst the other ducks with its striking white, chestnut, and black feathers. 秋沙鸭（雄鸟）以其鲜艳的白色、栗色和黑色羽毛在其他鸭子中脱颖而出。| 近义词: shelduck (雄性和雌性皆可指) | 反义词:  N/A (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑用female shelduck指雌性秋沙鸭) | 词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Middle English *bergandere, probably from Old French bergand, of uncertain origin. Possibly related to Old Norse brandgás (“brant goose”).
