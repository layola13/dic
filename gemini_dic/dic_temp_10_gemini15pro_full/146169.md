overestimations: /ˌōvərˌestɪˈmeɪʃənz/ | n. | 高估，过高估计 | An act of estimating something to be larger, better, or more important than it really is. | The company's overestimations of future sales led to significant losses. 公司对未来销售额的高估导致了重大损失。|  The project suffered from overestimations of available resources.  这个项目由于对可用资源的高估而受阻。 | synonyms: overvaluations, exaggerations, inflations | antonyms: underestimations, undervaluations | noun
