fins:/fɪnz/ | n. | 鱼鳍，鳍状物 | Thin, flat parts of a fish, other aquatic animal, or a vehicle that stick out and are used for swimming or steering. | The fish uses its fins to propel itself through the water. 这条鱼用它的鳍在水中推进。| synonyms: flippers, paddles | antonyms:  (no direct antonym, perhaps body/hull) | noun

fins:/fɪnz/ | n. | (汽车) 尾翼 | A fin-shaped part on a vehicle, especially a car, that improves its stability at high speeds. | The car's rear fins give it a sporty look. 汽车的尾翼让它看起来很运动。| synonyms: spoilers, aerofoils | antonyms:  (no direct antonym) | noun
