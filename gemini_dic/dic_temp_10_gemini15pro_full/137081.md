nonstructures:/nɒnˈstrʌktʃəz/| n.|非结构体，非结构物|Things that are not structures, typically referring to elements in a building or other construction that are not part of the main supporting framework.  This can include finishes, furniture, equipment, and other non-load-bearing components.|The earthquake damaged the nonstructures of the building, such as the windows and interior walls. 地震损坏了建筑物的非结构部分，例如窗户和内墙。|The cost of replacing the nonstructures after the fire was significant. 火灾后更换非结构部件的成本很高。|Synonyms: fittings, furnishings, equipment|Antonyms: structures, framework|noun
