crustalogy: /krʌˈstɒlədʒi/
n.
甲壳动物学
The scientific study of crustaceans.
Crustalogy is a fascinating field, encompassing a vast array of species. 甲壳动物学是一个引人入胜的领域，涵盖了大量的物种。
He dedicated his life to the study of crustalogy. 他毕生致力于甲壳动物学的研究。
近义词: carcinology
反义词:  (No direct antonym)
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Modern Latin *crustalogia, from Latin crusta ("shell, rind") + -logia ("study of").  Ultimately from Proto-Indo-European *kreus- ("hard; raw").
