blowgun:/ˈbloʊɡʌn/ | n. | 吹箭筒；吹管 | A tube through which darts or pellets are blown by the mouth. | He hunted small game with a blowgun. 他用吹箭筒猎捕小动物。| The tribesmen used blowguns tipped with poison darts. 部落成员使用带有毒镖的吹箭筒。 | 近义词: pea shooter, dart gun | 反义词:  (No direct antonym, perhaps rifle, bow and arrow as contrasting hunting weapons) | 词性: noun
