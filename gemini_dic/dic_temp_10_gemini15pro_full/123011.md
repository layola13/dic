monas: /ˈmoʊnæs/ (US), /ˈmɒnæs/ (UK)| n. |单子，单体，单细胞生物|A simple, single-celled organism; a flagellate protozoan.|例句：The monas is a tiny organism found in pond water.  单子是一种在池塘水中发现的微小生物。

|例句：Some species of monas are capable of photosynthesis. 一些单子物种能够进行光合作用。|近义词：protist, protozoan, unicellular organism |反义词: multicellular organism |词性:noun
monad: /ˈmoʊnæd/ (US), /ˈmɒnæd/ (UK)| n. |单子，单元，单体|A single unit; an indivisible entity.|例句：Leibniz believed that the universe is composed of monads.  莱布尼茨认为宇宙是由单子组成的。

|例句：In computer science, a monad is a design pattern that represents computations defined as sequences of steps. 在计算机科学中，单子是一种表示计算的设计模式，定义为一系列步骤。|近义词：unit, entity, individual |反义词: aggregate, composite |词性:noun
