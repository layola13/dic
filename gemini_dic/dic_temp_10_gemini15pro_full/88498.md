historiographership:/hɪˌstɔːriˈɒɡrəfərʃɪp/
noun
史学界；史学著作; the study of how history is written
The book offers a valuable contribution to the historiographership of the Cold War. 这本书为冷战史学研究做出了宝贵的贡献。
synonyms: historical writing, historical scholarship, historiography
antonyms:  (No direct antonyms exist. One might consider "fiction" or "mythmaking" as contrasting concepts, but not true antonyms.)
