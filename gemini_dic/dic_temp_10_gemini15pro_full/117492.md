medullary:/ˈmɛdəˌlɛri/ (US), /ˈmɛdjʊləri/ (UK)| adj.|髓质的，骨髓的，延髓的|Of or relating to the medulla, or any medulla-like structure; resembling marrow.|例句：The medullary cavity of the bone contains marrow.  骨的髓腔包含骨髓。

例句：Medullary thyroid cancer is a rare type of thyroid cancer. 髓质甲状腺癌是一种罕见的甲状腺癌。|近义词：central, inner, core|反义词：cortical, peripheral|词性: adjective

词源: Late 16th century: from late Latin medullaris, from Latin medulla ‘marrow’.
