analogist:/əˈnælədʒɪst/
n.
类比推理者，类推者
A person who uses or argues by analogy.
He is a skilled analogist, drawing parallels between seemingly disparate concepts. 他是一位熟练的类比推理者，能够在看似不同的概念之间找到相似之处。
Synonyms: comparer, correlator, assimilator
Antonyms: differentiator, opposer

Etymology: From analogy + -ist.  Analogy comes from Late Latin analogia, from Greek analogia "proportion," from analogos "proportionate," from ana- "according to" + logos "ratio."
