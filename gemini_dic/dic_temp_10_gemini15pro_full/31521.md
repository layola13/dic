chart's:/tʃɑːrts/ | n. | 图表；海图 |  The possessive form of "chart," meaning belonging to or associated with a chart.  It indicates ownership or relationship. | This chart's data is inaccurate.  这张图表的数据不准确。| synonyms: graph's, diagram's | antonyms: N/A |  noun (possessive)

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Middle English charte, from Old North French charte, from Latin charta (“paper, papyrus; map”), from Ancient Greek χάρτης (khártēs, “papyrus leaf, sheet of paper”).
