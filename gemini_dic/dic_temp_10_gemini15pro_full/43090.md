cowshed:/ˈkaʊʃɛd/| n. |牛棚，牛舍|A shelter for cows.|The farmer milked the cows in the cowshed.  农民在牛棚里挤牛奶。|Synonyms: barn, byre, stable|Antonyms:  (No direct antonym, perhaps "open field")|Noun


Etymology:
From "cow" + "shed". "Cow" comes from Old English *cū (female bovine animal). "Shed" comes from Old English *sceadan (to separate, divide).
