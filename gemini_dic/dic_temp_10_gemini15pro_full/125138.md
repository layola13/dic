multi-positive:/ˌmʌltiˈpɒzətɪv/ | adj. | 多阳性的，多重阳性的 | Having multiple positive qualities or aspects; exhibiting positivity in multiple ways.  Characterized by or displaying more than one positive feature, result, or effect. | This blood test came back multi-positive, indicating several infections. 这项血液测试结果多阳性，表明存在多种感染。

The new policy has multi-positive effects on the economy. 这项新政策对经济有多方面的积极影响。 | Near synonyms:  multifaceted, advantageous, beneficial, favorable  | Near antonyms:  negative, disadvantageous, detrimental, unfavorable | Adjective
