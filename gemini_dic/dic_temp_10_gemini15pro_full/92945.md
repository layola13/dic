ideogram:/ˈaɪdiəɡræm/ | n. |表意文字，表意符号 |A written symbol that represents an idea or object directly rather than a particular word or speech sound. |例句：Chinese characters are ideograms, representing whole words or concepts. 汉字是表意文字，代表整个单词或概念。

例句：Ancient Egyptian hieroglyphs are a complex system of ideograms and phonetic symbols. 古埃及象形文字是一个由表意文字和语音符号组成的复杂系统。|近义词：symbol, character, glyph |反义词:phoneme (represents a sound unit) |词性:noun
