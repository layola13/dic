narrow-minded: /ˌnæroʊˈmaɪndɪd/ | adj. | 心胸狭窄的，思想保守的 | Having or showing a limited outlook or understanding; unwilling to consider different ideas or opinions. |  She was too narrow-minded to appreciate other cultures. 她心胸太狭窄，无法欣赏其他文化。|  He has a very narrow-minded view of the world. 他对世界的看法非常狭隘。 |  illiberal, intolerant, bigoted, prejudiced  | open-minded, tolerant, broad-minded, liberal | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From narrow + minded.  "minded" as a suffix means "having a (specified kind of) mind". So, "narrow-minded" literally means having a narrow mind.
