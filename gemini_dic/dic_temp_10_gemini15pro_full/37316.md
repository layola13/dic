commemorate:/kəˈmɛməˌreɪt/| v. |纪念，庆祝|To remember and show respect for (someone or something), especially with a ceremony or by making a special effort|例句：A statue was built to commemorate the war hero.  一座雕像被建造来纪念这位战争英雄。

例句：We commemorate the founding of our nation every July 4th. 我们每年七月四日庆祝国庆。|近义词：celebrate, memorialize, honor, observe|反义词:forget, ignore, disregard|词性:动词
commemoration:/kəˌmɛməˈreɪʃən/| n. |纪念，庆祝|The act of commemorating someone or something|例句：The commemoration of the victory was held in the town square. 胜利的庆祝活动在城镇广场举行。

例句：The 50th anniversary commemoration was a grand affair. 50周年庆典是一场盛大的活动。|近义词：celebration, observance, remembrance|反义词:disregard|词性:名词


ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin commemoratus, past participle of commemorare "to call to mind, recount, relate," from com- "together" + memorare "to bring to remembrance," from memor "mindful."
