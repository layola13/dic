endorsement:/ɪnˈdɔːrsmənt/ /ɛnˈdɔːrsmənt/| n.|认可，支持，背书，签注|An act of giving one's public approval or support to someone or something.|The product received endorsements from several celebrities.  这个产品得到了几位名人的代言。|Synonyms: approval, support, backing, sanction, ratification|Antonyms: disapproval, opposition, rejection|noun

endorsement:/ɪnˈdɔːrsmənt/ /ɛnˈdɔːrsmənt/| n.|背书，签字|A signature on a document, especially a check, that makes it payable to someone else.|She wrote me a check and asked for my endorsement on the back. 她给我写了一张支票，要求我在背面签字。|Synonyms: signature, countersignature|Antonyms: N/A|noun
