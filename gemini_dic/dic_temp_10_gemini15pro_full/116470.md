material:/məˈtɪəriəl/ (US), /məˈtɪəriəl/ (UK)| n. adj.| 材料，物质；素材；布料 |A physical substance that things can be made from; information or ideas used in books, films, etc.|
The dress is made of a soft material. 这件衣服是用柔软的材料制成的。

She's collecting material for her new novel. 她正在为她的新小说收集素材。

近义词：substance, fabric, data, information|反义词: immaterial, spiritual|词性:noun, adjective


material:/məˈtɪəriəl/ (US), /məˈtɪəriəl/ (UK)| adj.| 物质的；重要的 |Relating to worldly possessions rather than spiritual or intellectual things; important; relevant.|
Material possessions don't bring happiness. 物质财富并不能带来幸福。

The police found material evidence at the scene of the crime. 警方在犯罪现场发现了重要的物证。

近义词：physical, tangible, significant, relevant|反义词:immaterial, spiritual, insignificant, irrelevant|词性:adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From late Middle English: from late Latin *materialis, from Latin materia ‘matter’.
