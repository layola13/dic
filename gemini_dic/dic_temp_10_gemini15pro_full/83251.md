gymnoblastea:/ˌdʒɪmnəˈblæstiə/
| n. |裸芽螅目|An order of hydrozoan coelenterates having hydroid colonies in which the polyps are not enclosed in a horny or chitinous sheath (hydrotheca). They are characterized by having naked polyps (i.e., polyps not protected by a hydrotheca).|The gymnoblastea, also known as Anthomedusae, are a diverse group of hydrozoans.|近义词:Anthomedusae|反义词:Calyptoblastea|词性:noun
