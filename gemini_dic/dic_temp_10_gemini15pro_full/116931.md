mayweed:/ˈmeɪwiːd/ | n. |五月菊，臭甘菊| A common name for several plants of the genus Anthemis, especially Anthemis cotula, which has a strong unpleasant smell.  It is considered a weed in many agricultural settings. |  Mayweed infests the field and reduces crop yield. 五月菊侵扰了田地，降低了作物产量。 |  Synonym: dog fennel, stinking chamomile | Antonym:  (No direct antonym for a plant, but perhaps "cultivated flower" in a gardening context) | noun
