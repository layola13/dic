nose-out:/ˌnoʊz ˈaʊt/| v. |以微弱优势胜过；险胜|To defeat (someone) by a narrow margin. | Our team nosed out the competition in the final seconds of the game. 我们的队伍在比赛的最后几秒险胜对手。

The champion was nosed out by a newcomer.  冠军被一个新人险胜。|近义词：edge out, narrowly defeat, outstrip, beat by a whisker|反义词: lose to, be defeated by|词性:动词
