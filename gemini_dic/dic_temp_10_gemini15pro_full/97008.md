infatigable:/ɪnˈfætɪɡəbl/| adj.|不知疲倦的，不屈不挠的|Showing or having a seemingly limitless capacity for working or other energetic activity; tireless.|例句：She was an indefatigable campaigner for human rights. 她是一位不知疲倦的人权活动家。|例句：The infatigable volunteers worked tirelessly to clean up the park. 不知疲倦的志愿者们不知疲倦地清理公园。|近义词：tireless, unflagging, relentless, persistent, persevering|反义词：tired, weary, exhausted, fatigued|词性:形容词

Etymology:
From Late Latin *infatigabilis, from in- "not" + fatigabilis "capable of being tired," from Latin fatigare "to tire out."
