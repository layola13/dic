extreat:/ɪkˈstriːt/ | vt. | 勒索；强求；强取 | to obtain by force, threats, or other undue means; to wring (money, a promise, etc.) from a person by violence, threats, or importunity. | He extorted money from the old lady by threatening to reveal her secrets. 他以揭露她的秘密为威胁，向老太太勒索钱财。

extreat:/ɪkˈstriːt/ | n. | 强求；勒索物 | Something extorted;  (archaic) a forced or unfair exaction. | The extreat was paid under duress. 这笔勒索款是在胁迫下支付的。


近义词: extract, wring, force, coerce, extort | 反义词: give, offer, bestow | 词性: verb, noun (archaic)
