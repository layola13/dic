breakfasts:/ˈbrekfəsts/ (US), /ˈbrekfəsts/ (UK)| n. |早餐（复数）|The meal eaten in the morning.  It is the plural form of "breakfast".|例句：She always cooks big breakfasts on Sundays. 她总是在星期天做丰盛的早餐。

|例句：The hotel offers continental breakfasts. 这家酒店提供欧陆式早餐。
|近义词：morning meals|反义词: dinners, suppers|词性:noun


breakfast:/ˈbrekfəst/ (US), /ˈbrekfəst/ (UK)| n. vi.|早餐，吃早餐|The first meal of the day, usually eaten in the morning.|例句：I had a quick breakfast before leaving for work.  我在上班前吃了一顿快餐。

|例句：He breakfasts at 7 am every day. 他每天早上7点吃早餐。
|近义词：morning meal|反义词: dinner, supper|词性:noun, verb
