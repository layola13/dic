nothingarian:/ˈnʌθɪŋˌɛəriən/| n. |虚无主义者；无信仰者|A person who believes in nothing; a person who has no religious beliefs. |例句：He declared himself a nothingarian, rejecting all forms of established belief.  他宣称自己是一个虚无主义者，拒绝所有形式的既定信仰。

例句：The nothingarian argued that life was meaningless and without purpose.  这个无信仰者认为人生毫无意义，没有目的。|近义词：nihilist, atheist, agnostic, unbeliever, skeptic |反义词: believer, theist, devotee |词性:noun
