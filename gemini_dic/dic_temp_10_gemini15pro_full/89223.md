homey:/ˈhoʊmi/ (American) /ˈhəʊmi/ (British)| adj.|舒适的，温馨的，像家的|Suggesting the pleasant and comfortable qualities of home; cozy and inviting.|例句：The cabin had a very homey atmosphere.  这个小屋有一种非常温馨的氛围。

例句：Her apartment is small but homey. 她的公寓虽小但很温馨。


近义词：cozy, comfortable, inviting, welcoming, homelike, snug|反义词：uncomfortable, uninviting, cold, impersonal|词性:形容词
