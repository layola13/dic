chimerical:/kɪˈmɛrɪkəl/ /kaɪˈmɛrɪkəl/ | adj. |空想的，幻想的，虚幻的 |Existing only as an idea, and unlikely to become real. |例句：His plans for starting his own business were entirely chimerical. 他自己创业的计划完全是异想天开。

例句：She chased a chimerical dream of becoming a famous actress. 她追逐着成为著名演员的虚幻梦想。

近义词：illusory, fanciful, imaginary, unreal, visionary, quixotic |反义词：realistic, practical, down-to-earth, achievable, attainable |词性：形容词
