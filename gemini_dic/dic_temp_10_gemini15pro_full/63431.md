entreatable:/ɪnˈtriːtəbl/
adjective
可恳求的；易求的
Able to be influenced by entreaty; easily persuaded or moved.
He was an entreatable man, always willing to listen to others' pleas. 他是一个容易被说服的人，总是愿意倾听别人的请求。
近义词:persuadable, susceptible, amenable
反义词:inapproachable, unyielding, obstinate
词性:adjective
