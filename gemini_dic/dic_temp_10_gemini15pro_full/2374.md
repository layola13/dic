adiola: /ˌædiˈoʊlə/ /ˌædiˈəʊlə/
n.
红景天
A plant, Rhodiola rosea, of the stonecrop family, whose root is used in herbal medicine as an adaptogen.
She takes adiola supplements to help manage stress. 她服用红景天补充剂来帮助控制压力。

He uses adiola root to improve his physical endurance. 他使用红景天根来提高他的身体耐力。

近义词:  golden root, roseroot
反义词: N/A
词性: noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From the Greek 'rhodon', meaning 'rose', referring to the rose-like fragrance of the freshly cut root.
