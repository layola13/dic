burnable:/ˈbɜːrnəbl/| adj.|可燃烧的，可燃的|Something that is burnable can be burned. |例句：The waste is burnable. 这些废物是可燃烧的。|例句：Only burnable materials should be put in the incinerator. 只有可燃材料才能放入焚化炉。|近义词：combustible, flammable, ignitable|反义词：non-burnable, non-flammable, fireproof|词性:形容词
Etymology: From "burn" (verb) + "-able" (suffix).  "Burn" comes from Old English "bærnan," of Germanic origin, related to Dutch "branden" and German "brennen." "-able" comes from Old French "-able" and Latin "-abilis," meaning "capable of being."
