bailors:/ˈbeɪlərz/
n.
委托人，寄托人
People who entrust goods to someone else for safekeeping or some other purpose.
The bailors entrusted their valuable paintings to the museum. 委托人将他们珍贵的画作委托给博物馆保管。

The bailors expected the warehouse to keep their goods safe. 委托人希望仓库保证他们货物的安全。

Synonym:  consignors, depositors
Antonym: bailees

词性:noun
