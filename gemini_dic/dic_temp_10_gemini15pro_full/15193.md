backcasting:/ˈbækˌkæstɪŋ/| n.|反向预测，回溯预测|The process of working backward from a desired future state to determine the steps needed to achieve that state.|Backcasting from a zero-carbon future helps us understand the necessary policy changes today. 从零碳排放的未来进行反向预测，有助于我们理解今天必要的政策变化。|近义词：retrospective analysis, hindsight analysis|反义词：forecasting, prediction|noun
