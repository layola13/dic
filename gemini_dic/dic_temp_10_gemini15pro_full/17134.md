battily:/ˈbætɪli/ | adv. | 疯狂地，古怪地 | In a crazy or eccentric manner.  Characterized by erratic or irrational behavior. | He dressed battily, with mismatched socks and a brightly colored tie. 他穿着古怪，袜子不配套，还打着一条颜色鲜艳的领带。 | The old woman wandered the streets battily, muttering to herself.  老妇人古怪地在街上游荡，自言自语。| synonym: crazily, eccentrically, oddly, peculiarly, strangely | antonym: sanely, rationally, normally | 词性: 副词
