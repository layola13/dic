freestyling: /ˈfriːstaɪlɪŋ/
freestyling| n. |自由式，即兴表演|The act of improvising, especially in music, rap, or other performance arts.  It's about creating something spontaneously, without a pre-planned structure or script.|He wowed the crowd with his amazing freestyling skills. 他以惊人的即兴表演技巧惊艳了全场。|improvisation, ad-libbing, improvising|rehearsal, planning|noun
