mindfully:/ˈmaɪndfəli/| adv.|留心地，谨慎地；深思熟虑地|Being present in the moment and aware of one's thoughts, feelings, and surroundings.|She ate her meal mindfully, savoring each bite. 她留心品尝每一口，细细地吃着饭。|近义词：attentively, consciously, deliberately|反义词：absentmindedly, carelessly, thoughtlessly|词性:副词

词源: From "mindful" + "-ly." "Mindful" comes from Old English "gemyndig" meaning "mindful, remembering."
