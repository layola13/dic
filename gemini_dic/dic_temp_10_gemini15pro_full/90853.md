hungered:/ˈhʌŋɡərd/| adj.|渴望的，饥饿的|Feeling a strong desire or craving for something, especially food.|The children, hungered for affection, clung to their mother. 这些渴望爱的孩子们紧紧依偎着他们的母亲。|近义词：craving, yearning, starving, ravenous|反义词:satiated, full|词性:形容词
Etymology: From Old English "hungrian," from Proto-Germanic "*hungrōną," from "*hungraz" (hungry). Related to Old Norse "hungra" (to hunger), Gothic "huggrjan" (to hunger).  The "-ed" suffix transforms the verb into a past participle, often used adjectivally.
