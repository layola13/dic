baptizements:/bæpˈtɪzmənts/
n.
洗礼，浸礼
The act of baptizing; baptism.  The administering of baptism. Instances of baptism.
The baptizements were conducted in the river. 这些洗礼是在河里进行的。
近义词: baptisms
反义词: None.
词性: Noun (plural)

baptizements:/bæpˈtɪzmənts/
词源: From Late Middle English baptysement, from Old French baptesme, baptisme, from Late Latin baptismus, baptisma, from Ecclesiastical Greek βάπτισμα (báptisma), from βαπτίζειν (baptízein, “to baptize”), from βάπτειν (báptein, “to dip, to immerse”).
用法:  A less common plural form of "baptism." Refers to multiple instances of the act of baptizing.  While "baptisms" is generally preferred, "baptizements" can be used for emphasis or in specific literary contexts.
