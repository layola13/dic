defiant: /dɪˈfaɪənt/ | adj. | 挑衅的，蔑视的，不屈的 | Showing resistance or disobedience; boldly challenging or opposing. |  He gave her a defiant look. 他挑衅地看了她一眼。

|  The protesters stood defiant against the police. 抗议者们不屈地对抗警察。

| 近义词: rebellious, resistant, disobedient, challenging | 反义词: submissive, obedient, compliant, yielding | 词性: 形容词

词源:  From Old French *defiant, present participle of defier "to challenge, defy," from Latin diffidare "to distrust, lack confidence in," from dis- "not, away" + fidare "to trust," from fidus "faithful, trusty."
