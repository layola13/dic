coacervation:/ˌkoʊæsərˈveɪʃən/ |n.|凝聚，凝聚层，团聚|The process of forming coacervates, droplets of organic molecules held together by hydrophobic forces.|Coacervation is thought to be a key step in the origin of life. 凝聚被认为是生命起源的关键步骤。|Example 1: Scientists are studying coacervation to understand how life may have originated. 科学家们正在研究凝聚，以了解生命是如何起源的。

Example 2: The coacervation of proteins can lead to the formation of membraneless organelles. 蛋白质的凝聚会导致无膜细胞器的形成。|Synonyms: aggregation, clumping, coalescence|Antonyms: dispersion, dissolution|noun
