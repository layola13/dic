eulogizing: /ˈjuːləˌdʒaɪzɪŋ/
verb
赞扬；歌颂；称赞
praising someone or something very highly, typically in a formal speech
He was eulogizing the virtues of the latest technology. 他正在赞美最新技术的优点。
The speaker eulogized the deceased as a great humanitarian. 演讲者称赞死者是一位伟大的人道主义者。
synonyms: praising, extolling, lauding, acclaiming
antonyms: criticizing, condemning, denouncing
verb

etymology: From Greek eulogia "praise, eulogy," from eu- "well" + -logia "-logy, speaking."
