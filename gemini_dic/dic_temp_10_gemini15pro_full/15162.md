back-slash:/ˈbækˌslæʃ/ | n. |反斜杠|A punctuation mark ( \ ) used in computing.  It is also called a "reverse solidus".|The file path is C:\Users\Documents.  文件路径是 C:\Users\Documents。|Synonym: reverse solidus, backslash character | Antonym: forward slash, slash | noun


back-slashes:/ˈbækˌslæʃɪz/ | n. |反斜杠（复数）|Plural form of "back-slash", referring to multiple instances of the punctuation mark ( \ ).|Use two back-slashes in the file path. 在文件路径中使用两个反斜杠。|Synonym: reverse solidi, backslash characters | Antonym: forward slashes, slashes | noun
