hamlets:/ˈhæmlɪts/| n. |小村庄（复数）; （罕见）小而简陋的住所（复数）|Small villages; (rare) small and mean dwellings. | Many hamlets dot the English countryside. 许多小村庄点缀着英国的乡村。|The abandoned hamlets stood as ghostly reminders of a bygone era. 这些废弃的小村庄如同幽灵般提醒着人们过去的时代。|近义词：villages, settlements|反义词: cities, metropolises|词性:名词

词源: From Old English *hām-lēact*  "home-leek," later *hām-lett*, meaning "small village," literally "little home."  The "-let" is a diminutive suffix.
