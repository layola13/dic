get-by:/ɡɛtˈbaɪ/ /ɡetˈbaɪ/ | v. |勉强过活，勉强应付 | to manage to survive or live with limited resources or skills. | I can get by with a little help from my friends. 我在朋友的一些帮助下勉强过活。

get-by:/ɡɛtˈbaɪ/ /ɡetˈbaɪ/ | v. |凑合，将就 | to manage with something that is not ideal. | We'll have to get by with this old computer for now. 我们现在只能凑合着用这台旧电脑了。


近义词: manage, cope, survive, subsist | 反义词: thrive, flourish, prosper | 词性: 动词

词源:  "Get" (from Old Norse "geta," meaning "to obtain") + "by" (from Old English "bi," meaning "near," "past," or "aside").  The combination suggests the idea of "getting past" difficulties or "managing with what's available."
