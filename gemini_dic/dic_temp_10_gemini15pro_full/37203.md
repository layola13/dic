comes:/kʌmz/ | v. | 来；来临；到达；出现；发生；变成；是；来自；起源于 | The train comes into the station at 2 pm. 火车下午2点进站。| arrive, appear, become | go, leave | verb

Etymology:
From Old English *cymst, third-person singular present indicative of cuman "to come," from Proto-Germanic *kweman, from Proto-Indo-European *gʷem- "to come, go."
