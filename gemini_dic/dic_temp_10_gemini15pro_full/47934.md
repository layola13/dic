decertification's:/ˌdiːˌsɜːrtɪfɪˈkeɪʃənz/ | n. | 去认证，取消认证，撤销证书 | The withdrawal or revocation of a certification, especially a professional or official one.  This often involves proving that the individual or organization no longer meets the required standards. | The decertification's impact on the company's reputation was significant.  该公司被取消认证对其声誉造成了重大影响。| The union faced decertification's threat after the failed strike.  罢工失败后，工会面临着被取消认证的威胁。 | synonyms: revocation, withdrawal, cancellation, annulment | antonyms: certification, accreditation, endorsement | noun
