compound:/kəmˈpaʊnd/ |/kəmˈpaʊnd/| adj. n. vt.|复合的，混合物，化合物；使加重，使恶化| This cream is a compound of oil and water.  这种乳霜是油和水的混合物。|近义词：complex, composite, mixture, blend, aggravate, worsen |反义词：simple, single, element, alleviate, improve |词性：形容词，名词，动词


词源: Late Middle English: from late Latin compositus, past participle of componere (see compose). The verb senses ‘mix’ and ‘settle’ date from the mid 16th century.
