assiege: /əˈsiːdʒ/
verb
围攻，包围
to surround a place with armed forces in order to capture it or force its surrender.
The army assieged the city for months.  军队围攻了这座城市数月。
Enemy forces are besieging the city. 敌军正在围攻这座城市。

近义词: besiege, blockade, surround, invest
反义词: liberate, free, relieve
词性: verb
