imbiber:/ɪmˈbaɪbər/ | n. | 饮者；吸收者 |  A person who drinks (especially alcoholic beverages); someone who absorbs or assimilates something. | He was a notorious imbiber of fine wines. 他是出了名的嗜好名酒的人。|  She was an avid imbiber of knowledge, always reading and learning. 她是一个求知若渴的人，总是阅读和学习。 |近义词：drinker, consumer, absorber | 反义词：abstainer, teetotaler | 词性: 名词

Etymology:  From the Late Middle English *imbiber*, from the Middle French *imbiber*, from the Latin *imbībō*  (I drink in, absorb), from *in-* (in, into) + *bibō* (I drink).
