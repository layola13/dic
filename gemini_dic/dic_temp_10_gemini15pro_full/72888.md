follow-ups:/ˈfɑːloʊ ʌps/ (US) /ˈfɒləʊ ʌps/ (UK)| n.|后续行动，后续跟进|Actions taken after an initial action, often to check on progress or effectiveness. |例句：The sales team conducted several follow-ups with potential clients. 销售团队对潜在客户进行了多次跟进。

例句：We need to schedule regular follow-ups to ensure the project stays on track. 我们需要安排定期的后续跟进，以确保项目按计划进行。

近义词：follow-through, check-in, review|反义词:preliminary, initial|词性:名词
