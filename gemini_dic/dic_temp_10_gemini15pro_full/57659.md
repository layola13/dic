drawboy:/ˈdrɔːbɔɪ/| n. |牵花工，拉花工|A boy or man employed to operate the drawloom, a handloom for weaving figured fabrics, by controlling the heddles (cords or wires) that raise and lower the warp threads. |The intricate patterns on the tapestry were created by a skilled drawboy. 这幅挂毯上复杂的图案是由一位熟练的牵花工制作的。|近义词：loom operator, weaver|反义词: None|Noun
