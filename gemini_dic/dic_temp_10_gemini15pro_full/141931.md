ordinate:/ˈɔːrdɪnət/| n. |纵坐标；纵线|The ordinate is the vertical axis on a graph. |例句：The point is located at (3, 5), where 3 is the abscissa and 5 is the ordinate.  这个点位于(3, 5)，其中3是横坐标，5是纵坐标。

例句：Plot the points on the graph, using the x-value as the abscissa and the y-value as the ordinate.  在图上绘制这些点，使用x值作为横坐标，y值作为纵坐标。|近义词：vertical coordinate, y-coordinate|反义词:abscissa, x-coordinate|词性:noun
