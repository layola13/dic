non-initiate:/ˌnɒn ɪˈnɪʃɪət/ /ˌnɑːn ɪˈnɪʃiət/| n. adj.|外行；未入会者；新手|Someone who is not a member of a particular group or organization, or who does not have special knowledge or experience of something.|例句：The technical jargon was confusing to the non-initiate.  这些专业术语让外行人感到困惑。
