oriented: /ˈɔːriˌɛntɪd/ | adj. | 以...为方向的，以...为导向的 | adjusted or aligned in relation to surroundings or circumstances; directed toward a particular interest or goal |  例句：The company is market-oriented.  这家公司以市场为导向。| 近义词：directed, focused, geared, inclined | 反义词：undirected, aimless | 词性: 形容词

oriented: /ˈɔːriˌɛntɪd/ | adj. | 熟悉情况的，适应环境的 | familiarized with or adjusted to a particular situation or set of facts or circumstances | 例句：The new employees are given an orientation program to get them oriented. 新员工会参加一个入职培训项目以帮助他们熟悉情况。 | 近义词：accustomed, adjusted, familiarized | 反义词：disoriented, lost | 词性: 形容词


词源: From the French word *orienter*, meaning "to turn towards the east," ultimately from Latin *oriens*, meaning "rising" (as in the rising sun).
