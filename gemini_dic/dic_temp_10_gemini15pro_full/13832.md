auditoriums:/ˌɔːdɪˈtɔːriəmz/
|n.|礼堂，观众席|A large room or hall used for meetings, lectures, performances, etc.  A building, part of a building, or outdoor area used for dramatic presentations or stage entertainments, or for concerts, lectures, meetings, etc., and having seats for an audience.|The university has two large auditoriums. 这所大学有两个大礼堂。|Synonyms: hall, theatre, lecture hall, concert hall|Antonyms: backstage, behind the scenes|noun
