incontrovertibility:/ˌɪnkɒntrəvɜːrtəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 不可争议性；不容置疑 | The quality of being impossible to dispute or disprove. | The incontrovertibility of the evidence led to a swift conviction.  证据的不可争议性导致了迅速定罪。 |  indisputability, irrefutability, unquestionability | disputability, contestability | noun

incontrovertible:/ˌɪnkɒntrəˈvɜːrtəbl/ | adj. |  不可争议的；不容置疑的 | Not able to be denied or disputed. |  His guilt was based on incontrovertible evidence. 他的罪行是基于无可争议的证据。 | indisputable, irrefutable, unquestionable | disputable, contestable | adjective
