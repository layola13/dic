dolly-out:/ˈdɑːli aʊt/ | v. |  摄影机拉远, 后退拍摄 | To move a camera away from the subject being filmed, making the subject appear smaller and revealing more of the surrounding scene.  This is often used to create a sense of scale or to show the subject's context. | The camera dollied out to reveal the vastness of the desert. 摄影机拉远，展现了沙漠的广阔。

The director dollied out slowly, gradually revealing the other actors in the scene. 导演慢慢地把摄影机拉远，逐渐展现了场景中的其他演员。 | 近义词：zoom out, pull back, back away | 反义词：dolly in, zoom in, push in | 词性: 动词

dolly-out:/ˈdɑːli aʊt/ | n. | 摄影机拉远镜头 |  A shot in which the camera moves away from the subject. | The dolly-out at the end of the scene was breathtaking. 场景结尾的拉远镜头令人叹为观止。

The dolly-out showed the entire city skyline. 拉远镜头展现了整个城市的天际线。 | 近义词：zoom out, pull back | 反义词：dolly in, zoom in | 词性: 名词
