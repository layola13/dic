carbolize:/ˈkɑːrbəlaɪz/ | v. | 碳化，使成炭 | To heat a substance in the absence of air so that it decomposes and leaves a residue of carbon. | The wood was carbolized in the fire, leaving only charcoal.  木头在火中碳化，只留下木炭。| The intense heat carbolized the organic material.  高温使有机物碳化。 | synonyms: char, carbonize | antonyms: oxidize | verb
