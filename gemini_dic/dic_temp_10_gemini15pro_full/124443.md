motormouth:/ˈmoʊtərˌmaʊθ/ (US), /ˈməʊtəˌmaʊθ/ (UK)| n.|喋喋不休的人，话匣子|A person who talks incessantly.|He's a real motormouth; he can't stop talking. 他真是个话匣子，说个不停。|
例句：She's such a motormouth, she never stops talking. 她真是个话匣子，从来没停过嘴。
例句：He earned a reputation as a motormouth after that three-hour interview. 在那次三个小时的采访后，他赢得了话匣子的名声。

|近义词：chatterbox, windbag, blabbermouth|反义词:reticent person, quiet person|词性:noun
