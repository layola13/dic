bookend: /ˈbʊkˌɛnd/ (US) /ˈbʊkˌend/ (UK)| n. | 书挡；支撑物；（常复数）首尾呼应的事物 |A support placed at the end of a row of books to keep them upright. Also, something that occurs at the beginning and end of a period of time, framing it, like bookends frame books. | The signed first edition served as a bookend to his successful writing career.  这本签名的第一版作品为他成功的写作生涯画上了完美的句号。|Synonyms: support, bracket, prop, border, frame | Antonyms: none (for physical object); middle, center (for figurative meaning) | noun

bookend: /ˈbʊkˌɛnd/ (US) /ˈbʊkˌend/ (UK) | vt. | 用书挡支撑；构成…的首尾 | To support or enclose with bookends; to mark the beginning and end of something. | The conference was bookended by two keynote speakers.  两位主讲人的演讲为这次会议拉开了序幕，也画上了圆满的句号。 | Synonyms: enclose, flank, border, frame | Antonyms: none | verb
