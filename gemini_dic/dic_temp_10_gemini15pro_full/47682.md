debuggability:/ˌdiːbʌɡəˈbɪləti/ | n. | 可调试性 | The ease with which a program can be debugged.  It encompasses aspects like code clarity, logging, and the availability of debugging tools. | The debuggability of the new software is much improved thanks to its modular design. 新软件的可调试性由于其模块化设计而大大提高。|  testability, maintainability | undebuggability, complexity | noun
