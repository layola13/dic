misleaders:/ˌmɪsˈliːdərz/
| n. |误导者；欺骗者|People who deliberately give false or misleading information are misleaders.  故意提供虚假或误导性信息的人是误导者。|The misleaders spread rumors to create chaos. 这些误导者散布谣言制造混乱。|近义词：deceivers, liars, manipulators|反义词:guides, mentors, truth-tellers|Noun
