forewaste:/ˈfɔːrweɪst/ | n. | 前面荒废的土地；前沿的荒地 | Land lying before a place, especially if waste or uncultivated.  A now chiefly archaic or literary term. |  The castle stood overlooking the forewaste, a desolate stretch of land. 城堡俯瞰着前方的荒地，一片荒凉的土地。 |  The travelers crossed the bleak forewaste before reaching the city walls.  旅行者们穿过荒凉的荒地才到达城墙。 | synonyms: wasteland, wilderness, desert, barren land | antonyms: cultivated land, fertile land | noun
