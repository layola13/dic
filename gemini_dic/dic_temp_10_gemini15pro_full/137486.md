nontortuous:/ˌnɒnˈtɔːrtʃʊəs/
adjective
非曲折的；笔直的
Not tortuous; straight or direct.
The surgeon made a nontortuous incision.  外科医生做了一个笔直的切口。
The river followed a nontortuous course to the sea. 这条河沿着一条笔直的路线流向大海。
近义词:straight, direct, straightforward
反义词:tortuous, winding, convoluted
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- + tortuous.  "Non-" is a prefix meaning "not," and "tortuous" means "full of twists and turns."
