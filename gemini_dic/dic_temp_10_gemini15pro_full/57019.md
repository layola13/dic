doublefold:/ˈdʌblˌfoʊld/| adj. adv.|双重的，双倍的；两倍地|Made of two layers folded together.  由折叠在一起的两层制成。|This tablecloth is doublefold for extra durability. 这块桌布是双层的，更加耐用。|近义词：double, twofold, duplex|反义词: single, simple|词性:形容词/副词
doublefold:/ˈdʌblˌfoʊld/| v.|加倍，双倍增加|To increase twofold.  增加两倍。|The company's profits doublefolded in the last quarter. 该公司上季度的利润翻了一番。|近义词：double, redouble|反义词: halve, reduce|词性:动词
