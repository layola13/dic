demoralised: /dɪˈmɒrəlaɪzd/ (UK) /dɪˈmɔːrəˌlaɪzd/ (US) | adj. | 失去信心的，泄气的 | Having lost confidence or hope; disheartened. |

例句：
The team was demoralised after losing three games in a row.
球队连续输了三场比赛后士气低落。

The constant criticism left her feeling demoralised.
不断的批评让她感到灰心丧气。


近义词: disheartened, discouraged, dispirited, dejected |
反义词: encouraged, heartened, optimistic, confident |
词性: adjective


词源:
From the French *démoraliser*, from dé- (expressing reversal) + moraliser ("to moralize").  Ultimately from Latin morālis (“moral”).
