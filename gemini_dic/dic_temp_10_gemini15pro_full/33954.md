cirrus:/ˈsɪrəs/ /ˈsɪrəs/| n. |卷云|A type of cloud, wispy and feathery in appearance, formed at high altitudes (above 20,000 feet) and composed of ice crystals.|The cirrus clouds drifted lazily across the sky. 卷云懒洋洋地飘过天空。|近义词：mare's tails|反义词: (No direct antonym for a cloud type.  Perhaps cumulus could be considered a contrasting cloud type.)|词性:noun

cirri:/ˈsɪraɪ/ /ˈsɪriː/| n. |卷云(复数)|Plural form of cirrus, referring to multiple wispy, feathery clouds formed at high altitudes.|The cirri indicated a change in weather was approaching.  这些卷云表明天气即将发生变化。|近义词：mare's tails (plural)|反义词: (Same as cirrus)|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin "cirrus" meaning "curl" or "tuft of hair."
