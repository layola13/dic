misprescribing:/ˌmɪsprɪˈskraɪbɪŋ/
n.|错误处方，不当处方|The act of prescribing medication incorrectly, either by giving the wrong drug, the wrong dosage, or to the wrong patient.|Misprescribing of antibiotics is a serious problem.  滥用抗生素是一个严重的问题。|overprescribing, incorrect prescribing|correct prescribing|noun
misprescribe:/ˌmɪsprɪˈskraɪb/
v.|错误地开处方，不当地开处方|To prescribe medication incorrectly, either by giving the wrong drug, the wrong dosage, or to the wrong patient.|The doctor misprescribed the medication, leading to serious side effects. 医生错误地开了药，导致了严重的副作用。|overprescribe, incorrectly prescribe|correctly prescribe|verb
