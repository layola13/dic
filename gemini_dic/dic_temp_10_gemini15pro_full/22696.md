boreal:/ˈbɔːriəl/ (US), /ˈbɒriəl/ (UK)| adj.| 北方的，北风的 |Relating to or characteristic of the north or the north wind.  Of or relating to northern regions, especially the subarctic zone.|The boreal forests of Canada are vast and largely untouched. 加拿大的北方森林广阔无垠，大部分未受破坏。|Synonyms: northern, arctic, northerly, polar|Antonyms: southern, tropical, equatorial|adjective

boreal:/ˈbɔːriəl/ (US), /ˈbɒriəl/ (UK)| n.| 北方，北风 |The north wind or a northern region. (This usage as a noun is less common.)|The boreal brought with it a chilling wind. 北风带来了一股寒风。|Synonyms: north, north wind|Antonyms: south, south wind|noun
