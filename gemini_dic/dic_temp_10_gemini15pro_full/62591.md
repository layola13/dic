enfeeblements: /ɪnˈfiːblmənts/ | n. | 虚弱，衰弱；弱化 | The process of weakening or debilitating something or someone.  The plural form of enfeeblement. |  Years of poor nutrition led to a gradual enfeeblement of his body. 多年营养不良导致他的身体逐渐衰弱。|  The enfeeblements caused by the disease made it difficult for her to walk. 疾病引起的虚弱使她行走困难。 | synonyms: weaknesses, debilities, impairments, failings | antonyms: strengths, empowerments, fortifications | noun
