mushiest:/ˈmʌʃiɪst/| adj.|最感伤的，最伤感的|Excessively sentimental; mawkish.  |The movie had the mushiest ending I've ever seen. 这部电影的结局是我见过的最煽情的。|近义词：sentimental, mawkish, soppy, slushy|反义词：unsentimental, hard-hearted|词性:形容词
Etymology: From mushy + -est (superlative suffix).  Ultimately from mash.  The original meaning of "mush" referred to boiled grain, which is soft and pulpy.  This tactile quality was extended metaphorically to describe overly sentimental or emotional expressions.
