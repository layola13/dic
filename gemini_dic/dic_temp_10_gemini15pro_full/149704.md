palatial:/pəˈleɪʃl/ | adj. | 宫殿般的；宏伟的；富丽堂皇的 | Resembling a palace in splendor or magnificence; spacious and luxurious. |  The celebrity lives in a palatial mansion overlooking the ocean.  这位名人住在一座可以俯瞰大海的富丽堂皇的豪宅里。|  The hotel lobby was palatial, with soaring ceilings and marble floors.  酒店大堂富丽堂皇，有着高耸的天花板和大理石地板。 | 近义词: luxurious, opulent, grand, magnificent, stately | 反义词: humble, modest, simple, plain, austere | 词性: 形容词

词源:  Late Middle English: from Old French palaicial, from palais ‘palace’ (see palace).
