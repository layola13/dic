encamping:/ɪnˈkæmpɪŋ/
verb
扎营；露营
The act of setting up camp in a temporary location, typically outdoors.
They were encamping near the river. 他们在河边露营。
The soldiers were encamping in the field. 士兵们在田野里扎营。
近义词: camping, pitching tents, bivouacking
反义词: decamping, breaking camp, packing up

encampment:/ɪnˈkæmpmənt/
noun
营地；露营地
A place where people set up camp, often temporarily.
The refugees set up an encampment on the outskirts of the city. 难民们在城市郊区建立了一个营地。
The annual music festival transformed the field into a vibrant encampment.  一年一度的音乐节把这片田地变成了一个充满活力的营地。
近义词: camp, campsite, bivouac
反义词:  departure, evacuation


词性:
encamping: verb (动词)
encampment: noun (名词)

词源:
From Old French "encamper" and ultimately from Latin "campus" (field).
