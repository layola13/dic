impaneling:/ɪmˈpænəlɪŋ/
n.
登记陪审员名单；选定陪审团
The act of selecting and swearing in a jury.
The impaneling of the jury took several hours.  登记陪审员名单花了几个小时。
The judge oversaw the impaneling of the jury for the high-profile case.  法官监督了这起备受瞩目的案件的陪审团遴选工作。
Synonyms:  enrollment, selection, listing, constitution
Antonyms: dismissal, discharge
