insects:/ˈɪnsekt/ (US), /ˈɪnsekt/ (UK)| n. |昆虫|Small invertebrate animals with six legs, a body divided into three parts (head, thorax, and abdomen), and often wings.  They belong to the class Insecta.|例句：Insects play a vital role in the ecosystem. 昆虫在生态系统中起着至关重要的作用。

例句：Many insects, such as bees, are important pollinators. 许多昆虫，例如蜜蜂，都是重要的传粉者。|近义词：bugs, creepy-crawlies|反义词: N/A (Insects is a broad biological classification, it doesn't have a direct opposite.)|词性: noun
