anthropophagi: /ˌænθrəˈpɒfədʒaɪ/
n.
食人者，食人族
People who eat human flesh.
Herodotus wrote about the anthropophagi of Scythia. 希罗多德写过关于斯基泰的食人族。
Synonyms: cannibals, man-eaters
Antonyms:  herbivores, vegetarians
noun
