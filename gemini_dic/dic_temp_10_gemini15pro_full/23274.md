boxcar:/ˈbɑːkskɑːr/ (US) /ˈbɒkskɑː(r)/ (UK)| n.|棚车，闷子车|A railroad freight car with a roof and sliding doors on the sides, used for carrying general merchandise.|The boxcar was filled with crates of oranges.  这节棚车装满了成箱的橘子。|例句：The boxcar derailed, causing a major delay. 棚车脱轨，导致严重延误。|近义词：freight car, goods wagon|反义词: (No direct antonym, possibly passenger car)|noun
