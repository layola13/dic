mother-figure:/ˈmʌðə(r) ˌfɪɡjə(r)/ | n. | 母性形象；母亲般的人物 | A woman who provides a child with emotional and psychological support, similar to that of a mother. |  She became a mother-figure to the children in the orphanage. 她成了孤儿院孩子们的母亲般的人物。

| 近义词: maternal figure, mother substitute | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | noun


mother-figure:/ˈmʌðə(r) ˌfɪɡjə(r)/ | n. | 母亲的形象；母亲的身影 | The visual representation or impression of a mother. | The painting captured the mother-figure with gentle grace. 这幅画以优雅的姿态描绘了母亲的形象。


| 近义词: maternal image | 反义词: (No direct antonym) | noun
