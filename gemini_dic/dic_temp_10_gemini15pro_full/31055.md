chameleonlike:/kəˈmiːliənˌlaɪk/
adjective
变色龙似的，善变的
Resembling a chameleon, especially in its ability to adapt or change; readily changing; adaptable.
He has a chameleonlike ability to blend in with any social group. 他有一种像变色龙一样融入任何社会群体的能力。
The chameleonlike nature of the virus makes it difficult to develop a vaccine. 病毒的多变性使得开发疫苗变得困难。
synonyms: adaptable, versatile, flexible, variable, mutable
antonyms: inflexible, rigid, unyielding, unchanging, constant
词性: 形容词
