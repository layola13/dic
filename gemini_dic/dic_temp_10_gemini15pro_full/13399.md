atomised:/ˈætəmaɪzd/ | adj. | 原子化的，雾化的，粉碎的 |  Reduced to atoms or minute particles; sprayed as a fine mist; utterly destroyed or fragmented. | The paint was atomised for even coverage.  油漆被雾化以获得均匀的覆盖。 | The bomb atomised the city center. 炸弹将市中心夷为平地。 | synonyms: vaporized, sprayed, pulverized, fragmented, shattered | antonyms: consolidated, unified, whole, intact | adjective

atomise:/ˈætəmaɪz/ | v. | 原子化，雾化，粉碎 | To reduce to atoms or minute particles; to spray as a fine mist; to utterly destroy or fragment. | The perfume atomises into a fine mist. 香水雾化成细小的薄雾。 | The enemy's defenses were atomised by the air strike. 敌人的防御工事被空袭摧毁。 | synonyms: vaporize, spray, pulverize, fragment, shatter | antonyms: consolidate, unify, integrate | verb


ETYMOLOGY->

From the late 19th century, from atom + -ize.  Ultimately from the Greek atomos "indivisible."
