overdiluted: /ˌoʊvərdaɪˈluːtɪd/
adjective
过度稀释的
weakened or reduced in concentration, value, or effectiveness by adding too much solvent or other substance.
The overdiluted solution was ineffective. 这个过度稀释的溶液失效了。
The company's stock was overdiluted after the merger.  公司股票在合并后被过度稀释了。
synonyms: weakened, thin, watery, diluted
antonyms: concentrated, strong, potent
词性: 形容词
