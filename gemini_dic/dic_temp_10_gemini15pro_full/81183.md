grandfatherly:/ˈɡrændˌfɑːðərli/| adj.|祖父般的；慈祥的|Kind and gentle, like a grandfather.  Characteristic of a kind and wise old man.|例句：He gave me a grandfatherly smile. 他给了我一个慈祥的微笑。

例句：He offered grandfatherly advice. 他给出了祖父般的建议。


近义词：kind, gentle, benevolent, paternal, avuncular|反义词：unkind, harsh, severe|词性: adjective

词源: From grandfather + -ly.
