outroar:/ˈaʊtrɔːr/| n.| 喧嚣，骚动，强烈抗议|A loud and prolonged expression of disapproval, typically by a large group of people. |The government's decision caused a public outroar. 政府的决定引起了公众的强烈抗议。|
outroar:/ˈaʊtrɔːr/| v.|发出喧嚣，大声抗议|To express strong disapproval, typically loudly and publicly.|The community outroared against the proposed development. 社区对拟议的开发项目大声抗议。|
outroar:|/ˈaʊtrɔːr/| 近义词: clamor, uproar, outcry, commotion, tumult | 反义词: silence, quiet, calm, peace, acceptance | 词性: 名词, 动词
