hyperviscous:/ˌhaɪpərˈvɪskəs/| adj.|高粘度的，极粘稠的|Having extremely high viscosity; excessively viscous.|The bacterial strain produced a hyperviscous exopolysaccharide, making the culture thick and difficult to handle.  这种细菌菌株产生了一种高粘度的胞外多糖，使得培养物浓稠且难以处理。|例句：The honey was so hyperviscous that it took a long time to pour.  这蜂蜜太粘稠了，倒出来要花很长时间。

例句：The hyperviscous lava flowed slowly down the volcano's side.  高粘度的熔岩缓慢地沿着火山侧面流下。|近义词：thick, viscous, gummy, sticky|反义词：thin, runny, watery|词性: adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
hyper- (prefix): from Greek, meaning "over, above, beyond"
viscous (adjective): from Latin "viscosus," meaning "sticky, glutinous," from "viscum," meaning "birdlime, mistletoe"
