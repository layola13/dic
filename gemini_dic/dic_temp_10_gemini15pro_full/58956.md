dustlike:/ˈdʌstˌlaɪk/| adj.|像灰尘的，粉末状的|Resembling dust in fineness or consistency.|The surface of the moon was dustlike.|近义词：powdery, dusty, fine|反义词:coarse, granular|词性:形容词
Etymology: dust + like.  "dust" comes from Old English *dust, from Proto-Germanic *dunsta-. "like" comes from Old English *līc, from Proto-Germanic *līkaz.
