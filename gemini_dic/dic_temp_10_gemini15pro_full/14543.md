avers:/əˈvɜːrz/| v.|断言，坚称|To state positively; to declare with confidence; to assert. |He avers that he is innocent of the crime. 他坚称自己没有犯罪。|Synonyms: assert, declare, affirm, maintain, contend|Antonyms: deny, refute, contradict|verb
avers:/əˈvɜːrz/| v.|保证，担保|To vouch for; to guarantee. (This meaning is less common.) |She averred the authenticity of the document. 她保证了文件的真实性。|Synonyms: guarantee, vouch for, attest to|Antonyms: question, challenge, dispute|verb
