convalesces:/ˌkɒnvəˈlɛsɪz/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈlɛsɪz/| v. |恢复健康，康复|To recover health and strength gradually after sickness or weakness.|例句：She is convalescing from a serious illness. 她正在从一场重病中康复。

例句：He spent a month convalescing at home after his surgery.  他手术后在家休养了一个月。

近义词：recover, recuperate, improve, heal|反义词: deteriorate, weaken, worsen|词性:动词
