cordeling: /ˈkɔːrdəlɪŋ/ | v. | 用绳子捆绑；用绳子测量 | To measure or mark off with a cord; to bind or tie with cord. | They were cordeling the wood for transport. 他们正在用绳子捆绑木材以便运输。 | synonyms: binding, tying, roping | antonyms: untying, loosening | verb
