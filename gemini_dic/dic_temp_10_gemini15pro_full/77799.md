generalize:/ˈdʒɛnərəˌlaɪz/ /ˈdʒɛnərəlaɪz/| v. |概括，归纳；推广；普及|To infer or formulate a general principle or law from particular instances; to make something more widespread or common.|Scientists are careful not to generalize from limited data. 科学家们很谨慎，不会从有限的数据中进行概括。|synonyms: summarize, abstract, extrapolate, universalize, broaden, expand|antonyms: specify, particularize|verb

generalization:/ˌdʒɛnərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/ /ˌdʒɛnərəlaɪˈzeɪʃən/| n. |概括，归纳；普遍化；推广|The act or process of generalizing; a general statement, principle, or law.|His generalization about women was offensive. 他对女性的概括性说法具有冒犯性。|synonyms: summary, abstraction, extrapolation, universalization|antonyms: specification, particularization|noun
