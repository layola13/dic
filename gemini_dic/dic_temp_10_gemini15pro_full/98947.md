inter-clasping: /ˌɪntərˈklɑːspɪŋ/
adjective
相互扣紧的，互相握住的
Clasping or holding each other tightly; intertwined.  The image showed two hands inter-clasping in a gesture of unity.
The lovers sat on the bench, their hands inter-clasping.  恋人坐在长椅上，双手紧握。
intertwined, interlocked, clasped
unlinked, separated
