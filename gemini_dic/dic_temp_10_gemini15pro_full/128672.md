nicotinate: /ˈnɪkətɪneɪt/ | n. | 烟酸盐；尼古丁酸盐 | A salt or ester of nicotinic acid.  It is a form of vitamin B3 and is sometimes used as a cholesterol-lowering medication. |  He was prescribed nicotinates to help manage his high cholesterol. 他被开了烟酸盐来帮助控制高胆固醇。| Synonym: niacin salt | Antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps a cholesterol-raising substance could be considered) | noun

nicotinic acid: /ˌnɪkəˈtɪnɪk ˈæsɪd/ | n. | 烟酸；尼古丁酸 | A form of vitamin B3, important for the body's metabolism.  Also known as niacin. | A deficiency in nicotinic acid can lead to pellagra. 烟酸缺乏会导致糙皮病。 | Synonym: niacin, vitamin B3 | Antonym: (No direct antonym) | noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From nicotinic + -ate.  "Nicotinic" refers to nicotine,  although nicotinic acid itself does not contain nicotine.  The "-ate" suffix is used to form nouns indicating salts or esters of acids.
