housemistress:/ˈhaʊsmɪstrɪs/| n. |舍监；女管家；女校长|A woman who is in charge of a house in a boarding school or other institution, or a woman who manages a large household. |例句：The housemistress was very strict about the girls' curfew. 舍监对女孩们的宵禁非常严格。

例句：She worked as a housemistress for a wealthy family. 她在一个富裕家庭做女管家。|近义词：matron, housekeeper, headmistress|反义词: (No direct antonym, perhaps "resident/student" depending on context) |词性: noun
