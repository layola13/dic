epi:/ˈɛpɪ/| prefix |在...之上，在...表面，在...后面| upon, on, over, at, after, in addition to |
The epidermis is the outer layer of skin. 表皮是皮肤的外层。|
An epicenter is the point on the earth's surface directly above the focus of an earthquake. 震中是地球表面正对着地震震源的点。|
近义词: hyper-, super- | 反义词: hypo-, sub- | 词性: prefix
