clumber:/ˈklʌmbər/| n.|克伦伯猎犬|A breed of spaniel, large and heavily built, with a white coat with lemon or orange markings.  It is known for its calm temperament and scenting ability. |例句：The clumber spaniel is a gentle giant among sporting dogs. 克伦伯猎犬是运动犬中的温柔巨人。

例句：Clumbers are known for their distinctive rolling gait. 克伦伯猎犬以其独特的滚动步态而闻名。|近义词：spaniel, gundog|反义词: N/A|词性:noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From Clumber Park, Nottinghamshire, England, where the breed was developed.
