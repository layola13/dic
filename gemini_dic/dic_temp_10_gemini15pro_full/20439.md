bitter:/ˈbɪtər/| adj.|苦的，痛苦的，辛酸的，严酷的，怀恨的|Having a sharp, pungent taste or smell; not sweet.  Causing pain or unhappiness. Feeling or showing resentment.|例句：The coffee was too bitter for my taste. 这咖啡太苦了，不合我的口味。

例句：The divorce left him feeling bitter. 离婚让他感到痛苦。|近义词：acrimonious, resentful, harsh, sharp, acrid|反义词：sweet, pleasant, happy|词性:adjective

bitter:/ˈbɪtər/| n.|苦味，苦的东西|Something that is bitter; bitterness.|例句：The bitter of the medicine lingered in her mouth. 药的苦味在她嘴里挥之不去。

例句：There's a lot of bitter in his heart. 他心里有很多苦楚。|近义词：bitterness, acrimony|反义词：sweetness|词性:noun
