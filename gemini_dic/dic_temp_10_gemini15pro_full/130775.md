nonanarchistic:/ˌnɒnˌænəˈkɪstɪk/ | adj. | 非无政府主义的 | Not anarchistic; opposed to or not characteristic of anarchy. |  The government implemented nonanarchistic policies to maintain order and stability. 政府实施了非无政府主义的政策以维护秩序和稳定。|  The community, while valuing individual freedom, maintained a nonanarchistic structure with elected leaders. 这个社区虽然重视个人自由，但仍保持着由民选领导人组成的非无政府主义结构。 | Near Antonyms: anarchist, rebellious, revolutionary | Antonyms: anarchistic | adjective
