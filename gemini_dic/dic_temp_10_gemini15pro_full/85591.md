hatstands:/ˈhætˌstændz/
n.
帽架；衣帽架
A piece of furniture with pegs or hooks for hanging hats, coats, and other clothing.
The hallway was furnished with a sturdy oak hatstand.  走廊里摆放着一个结实的橡木帽架。
Synonyms: coat stand, coat rack, hat rack, clothes stand
Antonyms: N/A (It's a physical object; no direct opposite)
noun


ETYMOLOGY->
From hat + stand.  "Stand" in this context means a support or frame.
