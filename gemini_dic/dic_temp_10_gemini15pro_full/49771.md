demoniac:/dɪˈmoʊniæk/ (US) /dɪˈmɒniæk/ (UK)| adj. n.|恶魔的，恶魔般的；被恶魔附身的；恶魔，疯子|Possessed or influenced by a demon; characteristic of a demon; a person possessed by a demon.|The painter’s demoniac energy infused every canvas with a breathtaking intensity. 画家的恶魔般能量为每一幅画布注入了惊人的强度。|He acted like a demoniac, screaming and thrashing about. 他表现得像个疯子，尖叫着又蹦又跳。|近义词：fiendish, hellish, satanic, possessed, frenzied|反义词：angelic, divine, saintly|词性:形容词,名词
Etymology: Late Middle English: from Old French demoniaque, via late Latin from Greek daimoniakos ‘of a demon’, from daimōn (see demon).
