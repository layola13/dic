clincher:/ˈklɪntʃər/| n.|定论；决定性因素；敲弯钉|Something that settles an argument or debate conclusively.|Her testimony was the clincher in the trial. 她的证词是审判中的决定性因素。|Synonyms: decisive factor, deciding factor, conclusive evidence|Antonyms: inconclusive evidence, ambiguity|noun

clincher:/ˈklɪntʃər/| n.|敲弯钉|A nail or bolt bent over at the end to secure it.|The clincher held the two pieces of wood together firmly.  敲弯钉将两块木头牢牢地固定在一起。|Synonyms: rivet, fastener|Antonyms: N/A|noun
