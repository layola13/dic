conventionalization:/kənˌvɛnʃənələˈzeɪʃən/ |n.| 习俗化；惯例化；程式化 |The conventionalization of artistic expression can lead to a lack of originality. 艺术表达的程式化会导致缺乏 originality.| Example 1: The conventionalization of greetings in different cultures can be fascinating to study.  不同文化中问候语的习俗化研究起来很有趣。

Example 2: The play suffered from the conventionalization of its plot and characters.  这出戏的情节和人物过于程式化，因此受到了影响。 |Synonyms: standardization, formalization, routinization| Antonyms: individualization, innovation, originality| noun
