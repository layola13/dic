bad-mouth:/ˌbædˈmaʊθ/ | v. | 诋毁，说坏话 | To criticize someone or something harshly, often unfairly. | He's always bad-mouthing his competitors. 他总是诋毁他的竞争对手。

He was bad-mouthing the new restaurant to everyone he knew. 他逢人就说那家新餐馆的坏话。

| 近义词: criticize, denigrate, slander, malign, disparage | 反义词: praise, commend, compliment | 词性: 动词
