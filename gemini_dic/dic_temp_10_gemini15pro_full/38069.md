compositing:/kəmˈpɑːzɪtɪŋ/ | n. |合成，影像合成|The process of combining visual elements from separate sources into single images, often used in film, video, and photography.|Example: The compositing of the live action footage with the CGI dinosaurs was seamless. 实景拍摄素材与电脑生成的恐龙影像的合成天衣无缝。|Example:  Compositing is a crucial part of modern filmmaking. 合成是现代电影制作的关键部分。|近义词: blending, merging, combining |反义词: separating, dividing |词性: noun

compositing:/kəmˈpɑːzɪtɪŋ/ | adj. |合成的|Made up of various parts or elements.|Example: The final image was a compositing masterpiece. 最终的图像是合成的杰作。|Example: He used compositing techniques to create the illusion of depth. 他使用合成技术来创造深度的错觉。|近义词: combined, compound, complex |反义词: simple, basic |词性: adjective
