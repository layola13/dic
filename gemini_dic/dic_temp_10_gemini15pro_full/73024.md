footfall:/ˈfʊtfɔːl/| n.|脚步声，客流量|The sound of a person's foot hitting the ground as they walk; the number of people entering a shop or business over a particular period.|The shopkeeper was encouraged by the increased footfall in his store.|Synonyms: footsteps, tread, traffic, patronage|Antonyms: desertion, emptiness|noun

footfall:/ˈfʊtfɔːl/| n.|足迹，到达|An act or instance of stepping or walking; the act of reaching or arriving at a place. |His footfall on the creaky stairs woke the baby. |Synonyms: arrival, advent, appearance |Antonyms: departure, exit|noun
