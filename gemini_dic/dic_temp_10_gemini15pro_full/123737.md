The company is undertaking a moonshot project to develop a cure for cancer. 该公司正在进行一项极具挑战性的项目，以开发一种治疗癌症的方法。|近义词：long shot, ambitious project, gamble, venture|反义词：safe bet, sure thing|词性:noun
