antiproton:/ˈæntiˌproʊtɒn/ | n. |反质子|A subatomic particle with the same mass as a proton but opposite charge (negative).  It is the antiparticle of the proton.|Physicists first observed antiprotons in 1955. 物理学家于1955年首次观测到反质子。

|近义词: (No direct synonym, but related terms include) antimatter particle, negative proton |反义词: proton |词性: noun
