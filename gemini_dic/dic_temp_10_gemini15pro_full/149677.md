Paiute: /ˈpaɪuːt/ (US), /ˈpaɪuːt/ (UK)| n. | 帕尤特人；帕尤特语 |  A member of a group of North American Indian peoples of the Great Basin area of the western US.  The Uto-Aztecan language of the Paiute peoples. |  The Paiute tribe traditionally lived in the areas around the Great Salt Lake. 帕尤特部落传统上居住在大盐湖周围的地区。

The Paiute language is closely related to Shoshone. 帕尤特语与肖肖尼语密切相关。| 近义词:  Numic (referring to the language family) | 反义词:  N/A | 词性: Noun
