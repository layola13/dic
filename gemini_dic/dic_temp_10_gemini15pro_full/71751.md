flays:/fleɪz/
v.|剥皮，严厉批评|The hunter flayed the deer. 猎人剥了鹿的皮。|
v.|严厉批评|The critic flayed the actor's performance. 评论家严厉批评了演员的表演。|
近义词：skin, excoriate, criticize, condemn|
反义词：praise, commend|
