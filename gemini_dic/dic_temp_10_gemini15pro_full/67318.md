extemporaneous: /ɪkˌstɛmpəˈreɪniəs/ /ɛkˌstɛmpəˈreɪniəs/
adjective
即兴的；无准备的；临时的
Spoken or done without preparation.
例句：He gave an extemporaneous speech. 他作了一场即兴演讲。

例句：Her extemporaneous remarks were surprisingly insightful. 她即兴的评论出奇地有见地。

近义词：impromptu, off-the-cuff, ad-lib, improvised
反义词：prepared, rehearsed, planned
词性: adjective

近义词：improvise, ad-lib
反义词：prepare, rehearse
词性: verb
