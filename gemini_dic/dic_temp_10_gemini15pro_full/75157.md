fritterers:/ˈfrɪtərərz/
n.
挥霍者，浪费者
People who fritter away their time or money.
He was one of those fritterers who never accomplished anything. 他是那种一事无成的浪费者。
近义词: spendthrifts, wasters, squanderers
反义词: savers, economizers
词性: 名词(plural)

fritter:/ˈfrɪtər/
v.
浪费，挥霍
To waste time, money, or energy on trifling matters.
She frittered away her inheritance on frivolous purchases. 她把遗产浪费在轻浮的购物上。
近义词: waste, squander, dissipate
反义词: save, conserve, preserve
词性: 动词
