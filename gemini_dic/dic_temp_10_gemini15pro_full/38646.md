concurrence:/kənˈkɜːrəns/ /kənˈkʌrəns/| n.|同意，一致；同时发生；并发|Agreement or harmony; the fact of two or more events or circumstances happening or existing at the same time.|The concurrence of several unfortunate events led to the company's bankruptcy.  几个不幸事件的同时发生导致了这家公司的破产。|Synonyms: agreement, accord, coincidence, simultaneity|Antonyms: disagreement, discord, difference, opposition|noun

concurrence:/kənˈkɜːrəns/ /kənˈkʌrəns/| n.|共存, 共同作用|The state of existing or operating together.|The concurrence of low interest rates and government incentives spurred a housing boom. 低利率和政府激励措施的共同作用刺激了房地产市场的繁荣。|Synonyms: coexistence, conjunction, convergence|Antonyms: separation, divergence, isolation|noun
