gendarmery:/ʒɑːnˈdɑːrməri/ | n. |宪兵队， жандармерия|A body of soldiers with police duties, especially in France and other European countries. |The gendarmery was called in to control the riot. 宪兵队被召来控制骚乱。|Synonym: police force, constabulary |Antonym: civilian population |noun
