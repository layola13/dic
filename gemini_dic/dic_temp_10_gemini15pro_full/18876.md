beshroud:/bɪˈʃraʊd/| vt.|掩盖，遮蔽|to cover or envelop so as to conceal from view|The mountains were beshrouded in mist. 群山笼罩在薄雾中。|近义词：conceal, veil, cloak, obscure, shroud|反义词：reveal, uncover, expose|词性:动词

Etymology:
From be- + shroud.  "Shroud" originally referred to a garment used to wrap a corpse, and later came to mean any covering.  The prefix "be-" adds intensity or completeness to the verb.
