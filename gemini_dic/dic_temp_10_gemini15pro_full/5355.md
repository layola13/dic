allurements:/əˈlʊərmənts/| n.|诱惑；魅力；诱惑物|Something that tempts or attracts someone.|The allurements of city life drew him away from his small town.  城市的诱惑吸引他离开了小镇。|Synonyms: temptations, attractions, enticements|Antonyms: deterrents, repulsions|Noun

allure:/əˈlʊər/| n. vt.|诱惑；魅力；吸引力；诱惑；吸引|The quality of being powerfully and mysteriously attractive or fascinating.|The allure of the unknown is strong. 未知的诱惑力很强。|Synonyms: attraction, charm, fascination|Antonyms: repulsion, aversion|Noun, Verb
