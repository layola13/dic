de-integrate:/diːˈɪntɪɡreɪt/
verb
取消整合，分解，使瓦解
To reverse the process of integration; to separate things that were previously combined.
The government's new policy seems to be de-integrating the public school system along racial lines. 政府的新政策似乎正在按种族路线取消公立学校系统的整合。
The company decided to de-integrate its software and sell each component separately. 该公司决定分解其软件并单独出售每个组件。
近义词:segregate, disintegrate, separate, dismantle
反义词:integrate, unify, combine
词性:v.
