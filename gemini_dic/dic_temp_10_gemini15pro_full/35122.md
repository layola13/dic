cloudiest:/ˈklaʊdiɪst/| adj.|最阴天的，云最多的|Superlative adjective of cloudy; most cloudy.  It describes the day, place, or time with the most cloud cover.|例句：Today is the cloudiest day of the week. 今天是这周云最多的一天。

例句：Seattle is known for its cloudiest months in winter. 西雅图以冬季阴天最多而闻名。|近义词：overcast, gray, gloomy|反义词:sunny, clear, bright|词性:形容词
