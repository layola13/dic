gondwanas:/ɡɒndˈwɑːnəz/
n.
冈瓦纳大陆（复数）
A vast continental area comprising present-day South America, Africa, Australia, India, and Antarctica, that existed from the Cambrian to the Jurassic.
Gondwanas began breaking apart about 180 million years ago. 冈瓦纳大陆大约在1.8亿年前开始分裂。
Gondwanaland, Gondwana
Pangaea
noun
