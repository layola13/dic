comedo:/ˈkɒmɪdəʊ/ (UK), /ˈkɑːmɪdoʊ/ (US)| n. |粉刺，黑头|A comedo is a clogged hair follicle (pore) in the skin. Keratin (skin debris) combines with oil to block the follicle. A comedo can be open (blackhead) or closed by skin (whitehead) and occur with or without acne.|
She had a few comedos on her nose. 她的鼻子上有一些粉刺。
He went to the dermatologist to get his comedos treated. 他去看皮肤科医生治疗他的粉刺。|近义词：blackhead, whitehead, zit, pimple|反义词: clear skin|词性:noun
