battlecruisers:/ˈbætlˌkruːzərz/ | n. | 战巡舰 | A large, fast warship, less heavily armored than a battleship but more heavily armed than a cruiser.  It was designed to outgun enemy cruisers and outrun enemy battleships. | The battlecruisers were deployed to intercept the enemy fleet. 战巡舰被部署以拦截敌方舰队。 |  The battlecruisers engaged the enemy at long range. 战巡舰与敌人在远距离交战。 | synonyms: capital ship, warship | antonyms: civilian ship, transport ship | noun
