dynamometric:/ˌdaɪnəməˈmɛtrɪk/ | adj. | 测力计的；测力的 | Relating to or measured by a dynamometer; an instrument for measuring force, power, or torque. | This dynamometric test measures the force of your grip. 这个测力测试测量你的握力。 | The dynamometric device accurately recorded the engine's output.  这台测力装置精确地记录了发动机的输出功率。 |  近义词: force-measuring, power-measuring | 反义词: N/A | 词性: adjective

Etymology:  From French *dynamométrique*, from *dynamo-* (from Greek *dýnamis* "power") + *-métrique* (from Greek *-metrikós* "of measuring").
