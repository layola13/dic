glisteningly:/ˈɡlɪsənɪŋli/
adverb
闪闪发光地; 闪耀地
in a glistening manner; with a sparkling or shimmering appearance.
The dew drops glisteningly clung to the spider's web. 露珠闪闪发光地附着在蜘蛛网上。
Synonyms:sparklingly, shimmeringly, brightly, radiantly
Antonyms:dully, dimly, faintly
词性: 副词
