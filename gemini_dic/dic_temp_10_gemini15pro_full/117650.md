megayacht:/ˈmɛɡəˌjɑːt/ | n. | 巨型游艇 | A very large, luxurious yacht, typically over 200 feet (61 meters) long.  It often includes features like a swimming pool, helipad, and multiple guest suites. | He owns a megayacht that is bigger than some cruise ships. 他拥有一艘比一些游轮还大的巨型游艇。 | Synonyms: superyacht, luxury yacht | Antonyms: dinghy, small boat | noun
