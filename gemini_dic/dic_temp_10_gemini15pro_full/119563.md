microtasimeter:/ˌmaɪkroʊtæˈsɪmɪtər/| n. |微量计，测微计|An instrument for detecting and measuring minute pressure changes, especially those caused by heat.  It was invented by Thomas Edison.| Edison used a microtasimeter in his experiments with sound. 爱迪生在他的声音实验中使用了微量计。|Synonyms:  micrometer, gauge |Antonyms:  N/A (measuring instruments typically lack direct antonyms)|noun
