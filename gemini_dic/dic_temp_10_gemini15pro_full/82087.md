groaner:/ˈɡroʊnər/| n.|令人 groan 的事物；（尤指）冷笑话|A joke so bad it makes you groan.  Something, especially a joke, that is so bad or obvious that it makes you groan.|例句：He told a real groaner about a talking dog. 他讲了一个关于会说话的狗的冷笑话。

例句：That movie was full of groaners. 那部电影充满了冷笑话。|近义词：corny joke, bad joke, dad joke, lame joke|反义词: witty remark, good joke, side-splitter|词性:noun
