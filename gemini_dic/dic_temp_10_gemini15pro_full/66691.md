exogyra:/ˌɛksˈɒdʒɪrə/| n. |外旋牡蛎，弓形牡蛎|An extinct oyster of the genus Exogyra, having a spirally twisted shell.  A type of fossil oyster with a characteristically curved or twisted shell. |例句：The exogyra fossil was embedded in the limestone. 这块外旋牡蛎化石嵌在石灰岩中。

例句：Exogyras are common fossils in Cretaceous rocks. 外旋牡蛎是白垩纪岩石中常见的化石。|近义词：oyster, fossil|反义词: None|词性:noun
