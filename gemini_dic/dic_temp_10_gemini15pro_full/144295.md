outspinning:/ˌaʊtˈspɪnɪŋ/
verb
超过旋转，胜过
surpassing in spinning or rotation; exceeding in rotational speed or effectiveness.  This often implies a competitive context, such as one object spinning faster or longer than another.
The new top was outspinning all the others.  这个新的陀螺比所有其他的陀螺旋转得都快。
The basketball player was outspinning his opponents with his fancy dribbling.  这位篮球运动员用他华丽的运球技巧胜过了他的对手。
近义词: outrotating, exceeding, surpassing
反义词: lagging, trailing
