leadership: /ˈliːdərʃɪp/ (US) /ˈliːdəʃɪp/ (UK)| n.|领导力，领导才能；领导阶层|The quality of being a leader; the ability to guide, direct, or influence others.  The group of people who lead an organization, country, etc.|例句：The company's success is a testament to her strong leadership.  这家公司的成功证明了她强大的领导能力。|近义词：guidance, direction, management, authority|反义词：subservience, followership|词性:名词

Etymology: From leader + -ship.  "Leader" comes from the Old English "lædere," meaning "one who leads."
