insistent:/ɪnˈsɪstənt/| adj.|坚持的，持续的，紧急的|Demanding something repeatedly and urgently.|例句：She was insistent that we should go. 她坚持我们应该去。|例句：The insistent ringing of the phone woke me up.  电话铃声不断地响，把我吵醒了。|近义词：persistent, persevering, tenacious|反义词：yielding, submissive|词性:形容词
insistence:/ɪnˈsɪstəns/| n.|坚持，坚决主张|The act of demanding something repeatedly and urgently.|例句：His insistence on punctuality was well known. 他对守时的坚持是众所周知的。|例句：At her insistence, I finally agreed to go. 在她的坚持下，我最终同意去了。|近义词：persistence, perseverance|反义词：acquiescence|词性:名词
