arabian:/əˈreɪbiən/| adj. n.|阿拉伯的；阿拉伯人|Relating to Arabia, its people, or their language.  A native or inhabitant of Arabia.|例句：She owns an Arabian horse. 她拥有一匹阿拉伯马。|近义词：Arabic, Middle Eastern|反义词:Western, European|词性:形容词

arabian:/əˈreɪbiən/| n.|阿拉伯人|A native or inhabitant of Arabia.|例句：Many Arabians are Muslims. 许多阿拉伯人是穆斯林。|近义词：Arab|反义词:None|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Old French *arabien*, from Late Latin *Arabianus*, from Latin *Arabia* (Arabia), from Ancient Greek *Ἀραβία (Arabía)*. Ultimately from a Semitic source, possibly related to Arabic *عَرَبٌ ('arabun, “nomads, bedouins”)*.
