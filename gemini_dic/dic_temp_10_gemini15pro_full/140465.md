old-maidenism: /ˌoʊld ˈmeɪdnɪzəm/ | n. | 老处女行为，老处女作风 | The state or quality of being an old maid; primness, fussiness, excessive concern with trivialities, and other characteristics stereotypically attributed to unmarried women. |  She exhibited classic old-maidenism, fretting over the placement of doilies and scolding children for laughing too loudly. 她表现出典型的老处女作风，为桌布的摆放而烦恼，并斥责孩子们笑得太大声。

| Example:  His aunt's old-maidenism manifested in her obsession with cleanliness.  他阿姨的老处女作风体现在她对清洁的痴迷上。| Synonym:  spinsterhood, prudery, primness | Antonym:  worldliness, sophistication | Noun
