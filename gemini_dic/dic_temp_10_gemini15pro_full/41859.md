cos's:/ˈkɒzɪz/ |n.|东西； belongings; possessions|Something belonging to or associated with a person or thing, especially articles of clothing.  Cos's is a colloquial contraction of "cos things," itself a contraction of "because of things." It often refers to personal belongings.| I packed all my cos's for the trip. 我打包了所有旅行用品。 |Near synonyms: stuff, things, belongings, possessions |Antonyms: N/A | noun
