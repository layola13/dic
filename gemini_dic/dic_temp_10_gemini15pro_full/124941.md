muffiest:/ˈmʌfiɪst/| adj.|最闷的，最沉闷的|superlative adjective of stuffy; most lacking fresh air or ventilation.  Characterized by a lack of freshness or vibrancy, often due to poor ventilation or confinement. Can also describe a sound that is indistinct or muffled.|The muffiest room in the house was the attic.  房子里最闷的房间是阁楼。|近义词：stuffiest, closest, airless, stifling|反义词：airiest, freshest, ventilated|词性:形容词
