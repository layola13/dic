hysterogenic:/ˌhɪstərəˈdʒɛnɪk/| adj.|癔症性的，引起癔症的|Causing or tending to cause hysteria; of or relating to hysteria.  Relating to or characterized by hysteria.|Her hysterogenic behavior disrupted the entire meeting. 她的癔症性行为扰乱了整个会议。|Synonyms: hysterical, hysteric|Antonyms: calming, soothing|adjective

hysterogenic:/ˌhɪstərəˈdʒɛnɪk/| n.|致癔症原|An agent or factor that causes or tends to cause hysteria.|The doctor suspected a hysterogenic toxin was the cause of the outbreak. 医生怀疑一种致癔症毒素是疫情爆发的原因。|Synonyms: hysterogen|Antonyms: tranquilizer|noun
