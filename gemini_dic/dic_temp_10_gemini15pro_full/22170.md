bombastical: /ˌbɒmbæstɪkəl/ /ˌbɑːmbæstɪkəl/
adjective
夸大的，浮夸的，言过其实的
Characterized by high-sounding but empty language; pompous or overblown.
His bombastical speech was met with eye-rolls from the audience.  他的夸夸其谈的演讲遭到了观众的翻白眼。
The critic ridiculed the movie's bombastical dialogue.  评论家嘲笑了电影中夸张的对话。

近义词: pompous, overblown, inflated, grandiloquent, pretentious
反义词: understated, simple, modest, plain, unpretentious
词性: adjective
