misnavigates:/ˌmɪsnˈævɪɡeɪts/
verb
错误导航，导航错误
To navigate incorrectly, leading to a wrong destination or course.
The ship's captain misnavigated, causing the vessel to run aground. 船长导航错误，导致船搁浅。
The GPS misnavigated us through the city. GPS带我们穿过城市时导航错误。
近义词:misdirects, misguides, loses one's way
反义词:navigates correctly, guides accurately
词性:verb

词源:
