maunderer:/ˈmɔːndərər/ (US), /ˈmɔːndərə/ (UK)| n. | 闲扯的人；絮絮叨叨的人；漫步者 | someone who talks in a rambling, unclear way for a long time; someone who wanders aimlessly | He was a known maunderer, often losing his audience within minutes. 他是一个出了名的絮叨鬼，经常几分钟内就失去听众。|Synonyms: rambler, driveler, chatterbox, wanderer| Antonyms:  concise speaker, listener | noun

maunder:/ˈmɔːndər/ (US), /ˈmɔːndə(r)/ (UK) | v. | 闲聊；漫步；絮叨 | to talk in a rambling, unclear way for a long time; to wander aimlessly | She maundered on about her childhood for hours. 她絮絮叨叨地讲了她几个小时的童年故事。|Synonyms: ramble, drivel, chatter, wander| Antonyms:  articulate, focus, stay put | verb
