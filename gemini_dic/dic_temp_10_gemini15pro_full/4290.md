airworthinesses:/ˈɛərˌwɜːrðiːnɪsɪz/
n.
适航性（复数）
The state of an aircraft being fit and safe to fly.
例句：The airworthinesses of the aging fleet were a major concern for the airline.  这些老旧机队的适航性是航空公司的一大担忧。

例句：Regular inspections are essential to maintain the airworthinesses of all aircraft.  定期检查对于维持所有飞机的适航性至关重要。

近义词：fitness, suitability, safety
反义词：unairworthiness, unsuitability
词性: 名词
