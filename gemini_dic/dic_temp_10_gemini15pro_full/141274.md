opaque:/oʊˈpeɪk/| adj.|不透明的；难理解的；晦涩的|Not able to be seen through; not transparent. Also, difficult to understand; obscure.|例句：The windows were opaque with steam. 窗户上满是蒸汽，变得不透明了。

例句：His explanation was opaque and confusing. 他的解释晦涩难懂，令人困惑。|近义词：cloudy, murky, obscure, impenetrable, incomprehensible|反义词：transparent, clear, lucid, understandable|词性:形容词

opaquest:/oʊˈpeɪkɪst/| adj.|最不透明的；最难理解的|Superlative form of opaque, meaning most opaque or most difficult to understand.|例句：The opaquest jargon is often used to exclude outsiders. 最晦涩的行话常常用来排除外行人。

例句：Of all the explanations, his was the opaquest. 在所有的解释中，他的最难理解。|近义词：most opaque, most obscure, most impenetrable, most incomprehensible|反义词：most transparent, clearest, most lucid, most understandable|词性:形容词

词源: From Latin *opacus*, meaning "shaded, dark."
