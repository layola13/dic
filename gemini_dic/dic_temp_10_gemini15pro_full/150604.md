paperlike:/ˈpeɪpərlaɪk/| adj.|像纸一样的|Resembling paper in texture, appearance, or thinness.|例句：The tablet has a paperlike screen protector that makes writing with a stylus feel natural. 平板电脑有一个像纸一样的屏幕保护膜，使使用手写笔书写感觉很自然。

例句：The material is paperlike and tears easily.  这种材料像纸一样，很容易撕破。|近义词：papery, parchment-like|反义词: thick, rigid|词性:形容词


ETYMOLOGY->
From "paper" + "-like" (suffix meaning "resembling").  "Paper" ultimately derives from the Latin "papyrus," referring to the ancient Egyptian writing material. "-like" is a native English suffix.
