ennoblingly:/ɪˈnoʊblɪŋli/
adv.
高尚地，崇高地
in a way that makes something or someone seem noble or of a high moral standard.
Her selfless act of charity was ennoblingly inspiring. 她无私的慈善行为令人崇敬和鼓舞。
The artist portrayed the working class ennoblingly, emphasizing their dignity and strength.  这位艺术家高尚地描绘了工人阶级，强调了他们的尊严和力量。
近义词: nobly, loftily, exaltedly
反义词: basely, ignobly, degradingly
词性: adverb

词源:
From ennoble + -ing + -ly. Ennoble comes from the Old French ennoblir,  from en- "make" + noble "noble".
