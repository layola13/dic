ornamentalness:/ˌɔːrnəˈmentəlnəs/
n.
装饰性，观赏性
the quality of being ornamental; decorativeness.
The ornamentalness of the vase added to the beauty of the room.  这个花瓶的装饰性增加了房间的美感。
近义词：decorativeness, embellishment, ornamentation
反义词：plainness, simplicity, austerity
