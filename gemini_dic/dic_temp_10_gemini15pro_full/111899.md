logs:/lɔːgz/ /lɑːgz/| n.|原木，日志，记录|A log is a thick piece of tree trunk or branch, especially one cut for burning on a fire.  A log can also refer to an official record of events during the voyage of a ship or aircraft, or a detailed record of computer or telephone activity.|We burned logs in the fireplace. 我们在壁炉里烧原木。|近义词：timber, record, journal|反义词: None|noun
