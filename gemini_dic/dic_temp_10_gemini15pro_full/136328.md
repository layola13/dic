nonrescues:/ˈnɒnˈreskjuːz/ /nɑːnˈreskjuːz/
noun
非救援，未获救的人/物
Those classified as "nonrescues" were left behind. 那些被归类为“未获救”的人被留下了。
The report detailed the number of rescues and nonrescues. 报告详细列出了救援和未获救的人数。
non-rescues (hyphenated form is also possible, though less common)
unrescued individuals/items
The nonrescues faced a grim future. 未获救的人面临着严峻的未来。
Emergency services are still searching for nonrescues. 紧急救援部门仍在搜寻未获救人员。
near antonyms:  rescues, saved, survivors
antonyms: fatalities, casualties
