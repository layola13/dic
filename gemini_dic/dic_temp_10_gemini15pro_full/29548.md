catcher:/ˈkætʃər/| n.|接球手；捕捉器；捕手|A baseball player who stands behind home plate and catches pitches that the batter does not hit.  一个站在本垒板后面的棒球运动员，负责接住击球手没有击中的投球。|The catcher signaled for a curveball. 接球手示意投一个曲线球。|近义词：receiver, fielder|反义词:pitcher|noun
