fleshless:/ˈflɛʃləs/| adj.|无肉的，瘦骨嶙峋的，没有果肉的|Without flesh; lacking flesh; thin and bony.  Can also refer to fruit without pulp.|The fleshless bones of the famine victims were a grim reminder of the disaster. 饥荒受害者瘦骨嶙峋的尸骨，惨痛地提醒着人们这场灾难。|近义词：lean, gaunt, skeletal, thin, emaciated|反义词：fleshy, plump, stout, obese|词性:形容词
词源:  From Old English *flæsclēas, from flæsc (flesh) + -lēas (-less).  Essentially meaning "without flesh."
