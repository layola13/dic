endopleurite:/ˈɛndoʊplʊˌraɪt/| n. |内胸骨片；内侧骨片|A sclerite of the pleural wall of an arthropod segment, lying between the episternum and the epimeron.|The insect's endopleurite provided attachment for its leg muscles.|Synonym: apodeme|Antonym: (No direct antonym, potentially exoskeleton component)|Noun
