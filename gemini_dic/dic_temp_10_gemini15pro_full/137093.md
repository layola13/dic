nonstyptic:/ˌnɒnˈstɪptɪk/
adjective
非止血的，不收敛的
Not having the ability to stop bleeding or constrict tissues.
This shaving cream is advertised as nonstyptic, so it shouldn't sting if you have any small cuts.  这种剃须膏被宣传为非止血的，所以即使你有一些小伤口，它也不应该有刺痛感。
After shaving, he used a nonstyptic aftershave balm. 刮完胡子后，他使用了不收敛的须后膏。
近义词:nonastringent
反义词:styptic, astringent
词性:adjective

词源:
From non- + styptic.  "Non-"  is a prefix meaning "not," and "styptic" comes from the Greek "styptikos," meaning "astringent" or "having the power to contract organic tissues."
