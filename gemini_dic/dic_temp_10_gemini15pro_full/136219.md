nonremedies:/ˌnɒnˈrɛmədiz/ | n. | 无救济措施；无法补救的事情 |  Something for which there is no legal remedy or redress. | The court ruled that the contract violations were nonremedies and offered no compensation. 法院裁定，违反合同的行为是无法补救的，不提供任何赔偿。| Near Synonyms: irremediable losses, irreparable harm, uncorrectable situations | Antonyms: remedies, solutions, redress | noun

nonremedies:/ˌnɒnˈrɛmədiz/ | n. | 无效的补救措施 |  A remedy that is ineffective or doesn't work. | The company attempted several nonremedies before finally finding a solution to the software glitch.  这家公司尝试了几种无效的补救措施，最终才找到解决软件故障的办法。 | Near Synonyms: ineffective remedies, failed solutions, futile attempts | Antonyms: effective remedies, successful solutions, viable options | noun
