noncrimes:/ˈnɑːnkraɪmz/
n.
非罪行；不被认为是犯罪的行为
Actions or behaviors that are not considered criminal offenses.
Many behaviors once considered deviant are now seen as noncrimes. 许多曾经被认为是 deviant 的行为现在被视为非罪行。
Synonyms: legal acts, permissible behaviors
Antonyms: crimes, offenses
noun
