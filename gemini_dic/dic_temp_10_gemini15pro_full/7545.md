annicut: /ˈænɪkʌt/ | n. |  灌溉水坝，拦河坝 | A low dam built across a stream to divert water for irrigation. | The farmers built an annicut to irrigate their fields. 农民们建造了一个拦河坝来灌溉他们的田地。| synonym: weir, dam, barrage | antonym:  (No direct antonym, perhaps something like 'breach' if referring to a broken annicut) | noun
