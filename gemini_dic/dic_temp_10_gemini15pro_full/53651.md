discharger:/dɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒər/
n.|排放者，卸货者，放电器|A person or thing that discharges something.
The factory was identified as the main discharger of pollutants into the river.|这家工厂被确定为河流污染物的主要排放者。
The discharger of the cargo was responsible for any damage.|货物卸货员对任何损坏负责。
The battery discharger helps to extend battery life.|电池放电器有助于延长电池寿命。
近义词:emitter, releaser, unloader
反义词:charger, loader
词性:名词

discharger:/dɪsˈtʃɑːrdʒər/
n.|退役军人|A person who has been discharged from the military.
The hospital provides specialized care for dischargers from the armed forces.|这家医院为退伍军人提供专门护理。
Many dischargers struggle to find employment after leaving the military.|许多退伍军人退伍后难以找到工作。
近义词:veteran, ex-serviceman/woman
反义词:recruit
词性:名词


词源: From the verb "discharge," which comes from Old French "deschargier," meaning "to unload."
