anti-popery:/ˌæntiˈpəʊpəri/
noun
反教皇制；反天主教；反对罗马天主教的运动
Opposition to the Catholic Church, especially the Pope's authority.
The strong anti-popery sentiment in 17th-century England led to many discriminatory laws.  17世纪英格兰强烈的反天主教情绪导致了许多歧视性法律。
During the Reformation, anti-popery pamphlets were widely circulated. 在宗教改革时期，反天主教的小册子被广泛传播。
Synonyms: anti-Catholicism, anti-Papism
Antonyms: pro-Catholicism, pro-Papism
