counterargued:/ˌkaʊntərˈɑːrɡjud/ | v. | 反驳，提出相反的论点 | To argue against something that has been argued, often by presenting opposing facts or reasoning. | He counterargued that the new policy would actually harm the economy. 他反驳说，这项新政策实际上会损害经济。|  The defense lawyer skillfully counterargued the prosecutor's claims. 辩护律师巧妙地反驳了检察官的主张。 | Synonyms: rebut, refute, dispute, challenge | Antonyms: agree, concede, accept, support | verb
