casemated:/ˈkeɪsmeɪtɪd/
adjective
设有炮台的，有掩体的
Protected by a casemate.  A structure having embrasures for artillery.
The casemated gun position offered excellent protection from enemy fire. 设有炮台的炮位提供了极佳的防敌火力保护。
The soldiers sheltered in the casemated bunker. 士兵们躲在有掩体的掩体内。
近义词: fortified, protected, shielded, bunkered
反义词: exposed, unprotected, vulnerable
词性: adjective

casemate: /ˈkeɪsmeɪt/
noun
炮台，掩体
A fortified gun emplacement or armored structure.  A vault, especially one beneath a rampart.
The fort's casemates housed heavy artillery.  堡垒的炮台内有重型火炮。
The troops retreated to the safety of the casemate. 部队撤退到安全的掩体内。
近义词: bunker, pillbox, emplacement, strongpoint
反义词: (没有直接的反义词，可以考虑 open ground)
词性: noun

词源: From French *casemate*, from Italian *casamatta*, literally "slaughterhouse," of uncertain origin.
