|例句：The novel's plot was bogged down by unnecessary overelaborations. 这部小说的情节因不必要的过度阐述而变得拖沓。|
|近义词：overkill, redundancy, verbosity, prolixity|

overelaborate: /ˌoʊvərɪˈlæbəreɪt/
|v.| 过度阐述；过于详细地说明|Don't overelaborate on minor details; focus on the main points. 不要过度阐述细枝末节；要专注于要点。|
|英文解析：To elaborate something excessively, often to the point of obscurity or redundancy.|
|例句：He tended to overelaborate his points, losing the audience's attention. 他倾向于过度阐述自己的观点，从而失去了听众的注意力。|

|例句：She overelaborated the instructions, making them more confusing than helpful. 她把说明书写得太详细了，反而让人更糊涂而不是更有帮助。|
|近义词：overdo, belabor, overstate|
|反义词：simplify, condense, summarize|
|词性：动词|


ETYMOLOGY->
From over- + elaborate.  "Over-" is a prefix meaning "excessively" or "too much". "Elaborate" comes from the Latin elaboratus, past participle of elaborare "to work out, produce by labor," from e- "out" + laborare "to labor."
