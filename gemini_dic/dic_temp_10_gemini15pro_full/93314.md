ill-gotten:/ˌɪlˈɡɒtn/ /ˌɪlˈɡɑːtn/
adjective
不正当获得的；非法获取的
obtained dishonestly or unjustly.
He deposited his ill-gotten gains in a Swiss bank account. 他把他非法获得的钱存入了一个瑞士银行账户。
Near Synonyms: illegally obtained, dishonestly acquired, ill-gained, stolen, purloined
Antonyms: honestly earned, legitimately acquired, rightfully owned
Word Origin: From ill + gotten, past participle of get.
