abaca:/əˈbɑːkə/| n.|麻蕉，马尼拉麻|A strong fiber obtained from the leaves of a Philippine plant, used in making rope and various fabrics.|The abaca rope is known for its strength and durability.  麻蕉绳以其强度和耐用性而闻名。

The dress was made from fine abaca cloth. 这件衣服是用精细的麻蕉布制成的。|近义词：Manila hemp, Musa textilis|反义词:|noun

ETYMOLOGY->
From Spanish *abacá*, from Tagalog *abaká*.
