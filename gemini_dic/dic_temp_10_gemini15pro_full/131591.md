noncompetition:/ˌnɑːnkɑːmpəˈtɪʃən/ | n. | 不竞争，竞业禁止 |  The absence of competition; an agreement not to compete. |  He signed a noncompetition agreement with his former employer. 他和他的前任雇主签署了一份竞业禁止协议。| synonym: non-compete, exclusivity | antonym: competition, rivalry | noun

non-compete:/ˌnɑːnkəmˈpiːt/ | n. | 竞业禁止协议 |  A clause in an employment contract that prevents an employee from working for a competitor after leaving the company. | She had to sign a non-compete before starting her new job. 她开始新工作之前必须签署一份竞业禁止协议。| synonym: noncompetition agreement, restrictive covenant | antonym: open competition | noun

noncompetitive:/ˌnɑːnkəmˈpɛtɪtɪv/ | adj. | 非竞争性的 | Not involving or characterized by competition. | The atmosphere at the retreat was noncompetitive and supportive. 研修班的氛围是非竞争性和支持性的。| synonym: cooperative, collaborative | antonym: competitive, cutthroat | adjective
