nonpuerilely:/ˌnɒnpjuːˈɪraɪlli/

词源:
* non- (prefix):  Latin, meaning "not"
* puerile (adjective):  From Latin *puerilis* "childish," from *puer* "child."
* -ly (suffix): Forms adverbs from adjectives.
