deflexure:/dɪˈflɛkʃər/| n.|弯曲，挠曲，偏斜|A deflexure is a bending or turning aside, often used in scientific contexts to describe the bending of a beam or ray.|The deflexure of the light beam was measured with a precision instrument. 光束的偏斜是用精密仪器测量的。|The bridge exhibited a slight deflexure under the weight of the truck. 在卡车的重压下，桥梁略微向下弯曲。|近义词：bending, deflection, deviation|反义词:straightening|noun
