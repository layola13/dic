bicycling: /ˈbaɪsɪklɪŋ/ | n. | 骑自行车，自行车运动 | The act of riding a bicycle. |  I enjoy bicycling on weekends. 我喜欢在周末骑自行车。

Bicycling is a great way to exercise. 骑自行车是锻炼身体的好方法。

| 近义词：cycling, biking | 反义词：driving, walking | 词性: 名词


Etymology:  From bicycle + -ing.  "Bicycle" itself comes from "bi-" (two) + "cycle" (wheel, circle).  Ultimately from Latin "bi-" (two) and Greek "kyklos" (circle).
