humpback:/ˈhʌmpˌbæk/| n. adj.|座头鲸，驼背|A whale with a humped back and long flippers.  A person with a humped back.|例句：We saw a humpback whale breaching in the distance. 我们看到远处有一头座头鲸跃出水面。

例句：The old man was a humpback.  这位老人是个驼背。|近义词：(n.) rorqual, whale, (adj.) hunchbacked, kyphotic |反义词: (adj.) straight-backed|词性: 名词, 形容词


hunchbacked:/ˈhʌntʃˌbækt/| adj.|驼背的|Having a humped back.|例句：The hunchbacked man walked slowly down the street. 驼背的老人慢慢地走在街上。

例句：She played a hunchbacked witch in the play. 她在剧中扮演一个驼背的女巫。|近义词：kyphotic, humpbacked|反义词:straight-backed|词性: 形容词
