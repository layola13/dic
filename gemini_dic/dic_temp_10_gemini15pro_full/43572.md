creak:/kriːk/| n. v.|吱吱作响的声音；发出吱吱声|A harsh, grating sound, typically made by something wooden or rusty moving.|The stairs creaked under his weight. 楼梯在他重压下发出吱吱声。|近义词：squeak, groan, grate|反义词：(没有直接的反义词，可以考虑silence, quiet)|词性：名词，动词
Etymology: From Middle English *creken, *criken, of imitative origin.  Related to Dutch kraken ("to crack, creak").
