floriferous:/ˌflɔːrˈɪfərəs/ /ˌflɔːrˈɪfərəs/
adjective
多花的；开花繁茂的
Bearing or producing many flowers; flowering profusely.
The floriferous cherry tree was a sight to behold.  开花繁茂的樱桃树是一道美丽的风景。
The garden was floriferous in spring. 春天花园里百花盛开。
近义词:blooming, blossoming, flowering
反义词:barren, sterile, flowerless
词性:adjective

ETYMOLOGY->
From Latin *florifer* (flower-bearing), from *flor-* (flower) + *-fer* (bearing).  The *-fer* element is related to the verb *ferre* (to bear, carry).
