chameleon:/kəˈmiːliən/ | n. | 变色龙；善变的人 | A small lizard that can change its skin color. Also used to describe a person who changes their behavior or opinions to fit in with others. | The chameleon blended in perfectly with the leaves. 变色龙完美地融入了树叶中。| synonyms: lizard, adapter, conformist | antonyms: nonconformist, maverick | noun

chameleon:/kəˈmiːliən/ | adj. | 善变的；反复无常的 | Adaptable or readily changing; like a chameleon. |  He has a chameleon-like ability to adapt to any social situation. 他有一种变色龙般的适应任何社会环境的能力。 | synonyms: adaptable, versatile, changeable | antonyms: inflexible, rigid, unyielding | adjective
