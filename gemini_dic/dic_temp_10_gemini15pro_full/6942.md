and-so-on:/ænd ˈsoʊ ɒn/ /ænd ˈsoʊ ɑːn/ | adv. | 等等；诸如此类 | Used to indicate that a list of items is not exhaustive. | We need to buy milk, bread, eggs, and so on. 我们需要买牛奶、面包、鸡蛋等等。 | She talked about her job, her family, her hobbies, and so on. 她谈论了她的工作、家庭、爱好等等。| synonyms: etcetera, and the like, and so forth | antonyms: specifically, particularly | adverb
