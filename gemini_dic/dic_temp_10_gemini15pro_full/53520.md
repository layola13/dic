disasters:/dɪˈzæstərz/
n.|灾难，灾祸|A sudden event, such as an accident or a natural catastrophe, that causes great damage or loss of life.|例句：The earthquake was a terrible disaster. 地震是一场可怕的灾难。|近义词：catastrophes, calamities, tragedies|反义词：blessings, fortunes|词性:名词
Etymology: From Old Italian *disastro, from dis- "badly" + astro "star". Originally referred to an unfavorable alignment of planets, later broadened to mean any catastrophic event.
