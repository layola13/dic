hinter:/ˈhɪntər/| n.| 内地; 腹地; 边远地区; (尤指山区的)偏僻处|A remote or less developed area, especially in the mountains or countryside.|The farm was located in the hinter, miles from the nearest town.  农场位于腹地，距离最近的城镇有数英里。|近义词：backcountry, outback, hinterland|反义词:foreland,  urban area|noun
