antelope:/ˈæntɪloʊp/ (US), /ˈæntɪləʊp/ (UK)| n. |羚羊|A swift-running deerlike ruminant with smooth hair and upward-pointing horns, native to Africa and Asia. |例句：The antelope leaped gracefully across the savanna. 羚羊优雅地跳跃过热带草原。

例句：A herd of antelope grazed peacefully near the watering hole. 一群羚羊在水坑附近平静地吃草。|近义词：gazelle, hartebeest, impala|反义词: N/A (No direct opposite for an animal)|词性:noun
