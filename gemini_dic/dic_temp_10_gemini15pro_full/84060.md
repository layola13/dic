half-furnished:/ˌhɑːf ˈfɜːrnɪʃt/
adjective
半配家具的
Partly furnished; having some but not all of the essential furniture.
The apartment is half-furnished, it comes with a sofa and a bed, but no dining table.  这套公寓是半配家具的，配有沙发和床，但没有餐桌。
The half-furnished house was perfect for us, as we had some of our own furniture. 这套半配家具的房子对我们来说很完美，因为我们自己也有一些家具。
Near Synonyms:  partially furnished, semi-furnished
Antonyms: fully furnished, unfurnished
词性: 形容词
