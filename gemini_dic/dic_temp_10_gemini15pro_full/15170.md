backwater:/ˈbækˌwɔːtər/ (US), /ˈbækˌwɔːtə(r)/ (UK)| n. adj.|死水，回水；闭塞的地方；落后地区|A place or condition in which no development or progress is taking place.|The town felt like a backwater, untouched by the changes sweeping the nation.  这个小镇感觉像一片死水，没有受到席卷全国的变化的影响。|Synonyms: stagnant, isolated, backward, provincial|Antonyms: progressive, modern, advanced|noun
