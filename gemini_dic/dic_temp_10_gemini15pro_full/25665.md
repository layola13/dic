ETYMOLOGY->
From Hindi बंगला (baṅglā), literally "Bengali", used elliptically to refer to a "house in the Bengali style".  Ultimately from the region of Bengal in the Indian subcontinent. The word originally referred to houses built in the Bengal style, which were typically single-story and had wide verandas.  The word then entered English in the 17th century.
