bubblegum's:/ˈbʌbəlɡʌmz/ | n. |泡泡糖(的) |  The possessive form of "bubblegum," referring to something belonging to or associated with bubblegum. | That's my bubblegum's wrapper. 那是我的泡泡糖包装纸。| My fingers are sticky from the bubblegum's residue. 我的手指因为泡泡糖的残留物而发粘。| 近义词: chewing gum's | 反义词:  (No direct antonym for a possessive) | 词性:  Possessive Noun

Etymology:  "Bubble" + "gum". "Bubble" from Middle Dutch "bobbel," referring to a swelling. "Gum" from Old English "gōma," referring to the palate or jaw, later extended to substances chewed.
