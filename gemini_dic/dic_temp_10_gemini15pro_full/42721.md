countervote:/ˈkaʊntərˌvoʊt/| n. v.|反对票，反投票；|A vote cast in opposition to another.|The council member cast the deciding countervote, defeating the proposal.  市议员投出了决定性的反对票，否决了这项提案。|例句：The countervote defeated the motion. 反对票否决了这项动议。

例句：He countervoted against the proposed changes. 他对提议的修改投了反对票。|近义词：opposing vote, dissenting vote, negative vote|反义词:supporting vote, affirmative vote|词性:名词, 动词
