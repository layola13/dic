out-county:/ˌaʊtˈkaʊnti/
adjective
外县的；县外的
Located outside the boundaries of a particular county.
例句：She lives in an out-county area. 她住在县外地区。

例句：Out-county residents often commute to the city for work. 县外居民经常通勤到城市工作。

近义词：non-local, extra-county
反义词：in-county, local
词性: adjective
