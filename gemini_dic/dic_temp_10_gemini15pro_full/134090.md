nonimaginary:/ˌnɒnɪˈmædʒɪnəri/ /ˌnɑːnɪˈmædʒɪneri/

非虚构的；真实的；非想象的
Not imaginary; real or actual.

反义词：imaginary, fictional, fictitious, unreal, fabricated

词性
