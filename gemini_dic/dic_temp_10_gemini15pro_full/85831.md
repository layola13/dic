hazy:/ˈheɪzi/ | adj. | 模糊的，朦胧的，不清楚的 | Covered by or filled with haze; vague or ill-defined | The mountains were hazy in the distance. 远处群山笼罩在薄雾中。|  The details of the agreement remain hazy.  协议的细节仍然模糊不清。 | 近义词: misty, foggy, vague, unclear, indistinct | 反义词: clear, distinct, sharp | 词性:形容词

hazy:/ˈheɪzi/ | adj. | 头脑不清醒的，迷糊的 | mentally confused; (of a person) unable to think clearly |  I felt hazy after the long flight. 长途飞行后我感到头脑昏沉。| I have only hazy memories of my childhood. 我对童年的记忆很模糊。 | 近义词: confused, muddled, bewildered | 反义词: lucid, clear-headed | 词性:形容词
