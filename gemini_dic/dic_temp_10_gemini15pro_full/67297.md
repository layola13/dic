exsolution:/ˌɛksəˈluːʃən/ | n. |析出；固溶体分解|The process by which a solid solution separates into two or more different solid phases.  A mineral previously dissolved in another mineral becomes solid and separates out, forming distinct crystals or grains.|Example 1: Exsolution lamellae of albite are common in orthoclase feldspar.  正长石中常见的钠长石析出纹层。

Example 2: The exsolution of ilmenite from magnetite is a common geological process.  钛铁矿从磁铁矿中析出是一个常见的地址过程。|Near synonyms: precipitation, separation, unmixing|Antonym: dissolution|Noun
