exhilarate: /ɪɡˈzɪləreɪt/ /ɛɡˈzɪləˌreɪt/
verb
使兴奋，使高兴
To make someone feel very happy, animated, or elated.
The news of their victory exhilarated the entire team.  他们的胜利消息让整个团队兴奋不已。
The roller coaster ride exhilarated the children.  过山车让孩子们兴奋不已。

近义词: thrill, excite, stimulate, invigorate, enliven
反义词: depress, sadden, bore, discourage
词性: verb
