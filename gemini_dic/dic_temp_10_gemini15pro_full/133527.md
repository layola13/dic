nonfictitiousnesses:/ˌnɒnfɪkˈtɪʃəsnɪsɪz/
noun
真实性；非虚构性
The quality or state of being nonfictitious; based on facts; reality.
The historian emphasized the nonfictitiousnesses of the events described in his book.  这位历史学家强调了他书中所描述事件的真实性。
近义词:truthfulness, factuality, authenticity, veracity
反义词:fictionality, fabrication, falsehood, spuriousness
词性:noun

Etymology:
From nonfictitious + -ness.  Non- (not) + fictitious (feigned, imaginary) + -ness (state or quality of being).
