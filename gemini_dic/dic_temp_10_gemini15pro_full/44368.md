cross-corner:/ˌkrɒsˈkɔːnər/ /ˌkrɔːsˈkɔːrnər/| adj.| 对角线的，斜对角的|Extending or situated from one corner to the opposite corner. |He hit a cross-corner shot in the billiards game. 他在台球比赛中击中了一个对角球。|

cross-corner:/ˌkrɒsˈkɔːnər/ /ˌkrɔːsˈkɔːrnər/| adv.| 斜对角地|In a diagonal direction across. |The ball rolled cross-corner across the table. 球斜对角地滚过桌子。|


cross-corner:/ˌkrɒsˈkɔːnər/ /ˌkrɔːsˈkɔːrnər/| n.| 对角线，斜对角|A diagonal line or direction. |The architect drew a cross-corner on the blueprint. 建筑师在蓝图上画了一条对角线。|


Etymology:  "cross-" (meaning "across" or "transverse") + "corner" (meaning "the point where two converging lines meet").  This compound word literally describes something that goes across from one corner to another.
