hobber's:/ˈhɒbəz/ (UK), /ˈhɑːbərz/ (US)| n.|滚齿工的（东西）; 爱好者的（东西）|Something that belongs to or is associated with a hobber (a person who operates a hobbing machine, or someone with a particular hobby).  It is the possessive form of "hobber".|This is the hobber's toolbox. 这是滚齿工的工具箱。| The painter's easel was in the corner, along with the hobber's collection of gears. 画家的画架在角落里，旁边是滚齿工收集的齿轮。 |Synonyms: hobbyist's (when referring to hobbies), machinist's (when referring to a hobbing machine operator)|Antonyms: N/A| Possessive noun
