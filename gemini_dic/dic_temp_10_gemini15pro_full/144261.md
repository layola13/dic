outsoaring:/ˌaʊtˈsɔːrɪŋ/ | v. | 超越，胜过 | Surpassing or exceeding in flight or altitude; exceeding in general. |  The new plane outsoared all previous models in terms of speed and fuel efficiency. 这架新飞机在速度和燃油效率方面超过了所有以前的型号。| Synonyms: surpassing, exceeding, transcending, outstripping, eclipsing | Antonyms: falling short of, lagging behind, being inferior to | Verb

outsoaring:/ˌaʊtˈsɔːrɪŋ/ | adj. | 超越的，胜过的 (less common usage) | Surpassing or exceeding in some quality. |  His outsoaring ambition drove him to great success. 他超越的雄心壮志使他取得了巨大的成功。| Synonyms: surpassing, exceeding, transcendent, superior | Antonyms: inferior, lesser, subordinate | Adjective
