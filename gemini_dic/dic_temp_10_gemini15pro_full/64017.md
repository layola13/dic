epitaphic:/ˌɛpɪˈtæfɪk/
adjective
墓志铭的; 适于作墓志铭的;  与死亡相关的
of, relating to, or suitable for an epitaph; suggestive of death
Her epitaphic poem was both mournful and beautiful. 她的墓志铭诗既悲伤又美丽。

The music had an epitaphic quality, solemn and reflective. 这首音乐有一种墓志铭的特质，庄严而反思。

近义词:funereal, sepulchral, mournful
反义词:joyful, celebratory, lively
词性:adjective
词源:From Greek *epitaphios*, from epi- 'upon' + taphos 'tomb'.
