diomedea:/ˌdaɪəˈmiːdiə/ | n. | 信天翁 | A large seabird with long narrow wings, found chiefly in the southern oceans. | 例句：The Diomedea exulans is the wandering albatross, known for its impressive wingspan.  漂泊信天翁是体型最大的信天翁，以其惊人的翼展而闻名。
