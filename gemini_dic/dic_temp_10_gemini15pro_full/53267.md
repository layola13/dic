dirges:/ˈdɜːrdʒɪz/
n.
挽歌；哀歌；悲歌
A song or hymn of mourning, especially one intended to be sung at a funeral.
The choir sang mournful dirges at the funeral.  唱诗班在葬礼上唱起了悲伤的挽歌。
Synonyms: lament, elegy, requiem, threnody
Antonyms: celebration, paean, anthem
noun
