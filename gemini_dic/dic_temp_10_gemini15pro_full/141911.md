ordin:/ˈɔːrdɪn/| adj.|普通的，平常的；规定的，惯例的|Ordinary describes something common, usual, or not special.  It can also refer to something established by custom or rule. |The restaurant served ordinary food. 这家餐厅供应普通的食物。|Synonyms: common, usual, typical, normal, regular, conventional, customary|Antonyms: extraordinary, unusual, special, exceptional, remarkable|adjective
