monosepalous:/ˌmɒnəˈsɛpələs/| adj.|单萼片的|Having sepals united into a tube or cup, at least at the base.|The flower is monosepalous, with the sepals fused together. 这朵花是单萼片的，萼片融合在一起。|近义词：gamosepalous, sympetalous (when referring to petals)|反义词：polysepalous, chorisepalous|词性:形容词
