PHRASE->
in disguise 伪装地  He traveled in disguise. 他乔装出行。
a disguise for something  …的伪装  His jovial manner was merely a disguise for his nervousness. 他那愉快的举止只不过是为了掩饰他的紧张。
disguise oneself as something/somebody 把自己伪装成… He disguised himself as a waiter. 他把自己伪装成服务员。
disguise something (from somebody)  (向某人)掩饰某事  It was impossible to disguise his disappointment. 无法掩饰他的失望。
make no disguise of something 不掩饰某事   He made no disguise of his ambition. 他毫不掩饰自己的野心。
