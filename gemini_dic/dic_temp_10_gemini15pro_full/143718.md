outliers:/ˈaʊtˌlaɪərz/
n.|离群值；异常值；局外人|A value far from most others in a set of data.  A person or thing situated away or detached from the main body or system. |The outliers in the data set skewed the average.  这些数据集中的离群值使平均值发生了偏差。|synonyms: anomaly, aberration, exception, rarity, outsider, maverick|antonyms: norm, average, standard|noun
