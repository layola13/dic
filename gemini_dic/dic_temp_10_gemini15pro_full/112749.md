what: /wɒt/(英式) /wʌt/(美式) |pron. det. adv. int.| 什么，多么 |

英文解析:  "What" can function as a pronoun (asking for information specifying something), a determiner (identifying or specifying a thing or things), an adverb (modifying an adjective or adverb, especially to express a high degree), or an interjection (expressing surprise or disbelief).

例句：
What is your name? = 你叫什么名字？

What do you want for dinner? = 你晚餐想吃什么？

近义词：which, who

反义词:  (What doesn't have a direct antonym.  Its meaning depends heavily on context.)

词性: pronoun, determiner, adverb, interjection
