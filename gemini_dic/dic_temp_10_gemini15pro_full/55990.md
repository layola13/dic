döner:/ˈdɜːnər/ (UK), /ˈdɜːrnər/ (US)| n.|土耳其烤肉|A Turkish dish consisting of thin slices of lamb, beef, or chicken roasted on a rotating spit.  It's often served in pita bread with various toppings.|I had a delicious döner kebab for lunch. 我午饭吃了一个美味的土耳其烤肉卷。|Synonyms: gyro, shawarma|Antonyms: N/A|noun
