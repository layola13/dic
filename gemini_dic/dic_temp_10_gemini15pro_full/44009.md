criminate: /ˈkrɪmɪneɪt/
verb
控告；归罪于；使负罪
to accuse of or show evidence of a crime or fault
The evidence criminates the defendant.  证据显示被告有罪。
近义词: incriminate, accuse, indict, charge
反义词: exonerate, acquit, absolve
词性: verb

词源:
From Latin criminatus, past participle of criminari (“to accuse”), from crimen (“accusation, crime”).
