expounders:/ɪkˈspaʊndərz/
|n.|阐释者，讲解者|People who explain or interpret something in detail; commentators.|The expounders of the new law clarified its complexities for the public. 新法律的阐释者向公众阐明了其复杂性。|synonyms: interpreters, commentators, explainers, elucidators|antonyms: obfuscators, misinterpreters|noun
