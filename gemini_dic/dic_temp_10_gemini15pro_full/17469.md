beardies:/ˈbɪədiz/
n.|大胡子的人，留胡子的人；（尤指）蓄山羊胡的人；（某些蜥蜴的）胡须|People with beards, especially those with goatees; the beard-like appendages on some lizards.|He joined a club for beardies. 他加入了一个留胡子的人的俱乐部。|The beardie puffed out its beard when threatened.  当受到威胁时，蜥蜴鼓起了它的胡须。|synonyms: bearded men, goatees|antonyms: clean-shaven men|noun
