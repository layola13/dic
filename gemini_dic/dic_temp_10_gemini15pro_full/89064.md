holophrasis:/ˌhɒləˈfreɪsɪs/ /ˌhɑːləˈfreɪsɪs/| n. |单词句，一言句|A single word or phrase that expresses a complex idea, often used by young children. |Example: A child saying "milk" could mean "I want milk."  孩子说“牛奶”可能意思是“我想要牛奶”。|Example: "Up" can mean "Pick me up." “抱抱”可以指“抱我起来”。|Synonym: one-word sentence, one-word utterance|Antonym:  multi-word utterance, sentence|noun
