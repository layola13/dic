monodrama:/ˈmɑːnəˌdrɑːmə/ /ˈmɒnəˌdrɑːmə/| n. |独角戏|A dramatic piece performed by only one actor.|
She captivated the audience with her powerful monodrama about a woman's journey through grief. 她以她关于一个女人悲伤旅程的独角戏表演深深吸引了观众。

The monodrama explored the complexities of human emotion with just a single performer.  这出独角戏仅用一名表演者就探索了人类情感的复杂性。


近义词:solo performance, one-man show, one-woman show|
反义词:|
词性:noun
