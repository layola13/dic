homebodies:/ˈhoʊmbɑːdiz/ (US) /ˈhəʊmbɒdiz/ (UK)| n. |宅男宅女；喜欢待在家里的人|People who prefer to stay at home and rarely go out for social activities.  They find enjoyment in activities that can be done at home, like reading, watching movies, or pursuing hobbies.|例句：They are real homebodies and rarely go out on weekends. 他们是真正的宅男宅女，周末很少出门。

例句：My parents are homebodies; they prefer quiet evenings at home. 我父母喜欢待在家里，他们更喜欢在家享受安静的夜晚。|近义词：homebirds, stay-at-homes|反义词：social butterflies, party animals|词性：名词(plural)
