luteocobaltic:/ˌluːtiːoʊkoʊˈbɔːltɪk/
adjective
黄钴的，六氨合钴(III)的
Relating to or containing the luteocobaltic cation, [Co(NH3)6]3+.  Specifically, referring to the yellow-colored coordination complex formed by cobalt(III) with six ammonia ligands.
Luteocobaltic chloride is a yellow crystalline solid.  黄钴氯化物是一种黄色晶体固体。

The luteocobaltic ion has a distinctive yellow color. 六氨合钴(III)离子具有独特的黄色。

Synonym:  hexaamminecobalt(III) (specifically for the cation)
