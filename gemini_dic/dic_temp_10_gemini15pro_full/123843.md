moralist:/ˈmɔːrəlɪst/ (US) /ˈmɒrəlɪst/ (UK)| n.|道德家；卫道士|A person who teaches or advocates morality, sometimes in a self-righteous or preachy way.|例句：He's such a moralist, always lecturing everyone on their behavior. 他真是个道德家，总是教训每个人的行为。

例句：The moralist condemned the behavior of the younger generation.  这位道德家谴责了年轻一代的行为。|近义词：preacher, puritan, moralizer|反义词：libertine, hedonist|词性: noun
