mispackaged:/ˌmɪsˈpækɪdʒd/
adjective
包装错误的，包装不当的
incorrectly packaged; packaged in a way that is unsuitable or misleading.
The mispackaged product had to be recalled. 这个包装错误的产品不得不被召回。
The mispackaged toys posed a choking hazard for infants. 包装不当的玩具对婴儿有窒息的危险。
近义词: incorrectly packaged, wrongly packaged, improperly packaged
反义词: correctly packaged, properly packaged
词性: adjective

Etymology:
mis- + packaged
mis-: prefix meaning "wrongly," "badly," or "incorrectly." From Old English mis-, from Proto-Germanic *missa- "wrongly," cognate with Old Norse mis-, Old High German missi-, Gothic missō "mutually, reciprocally."
packaged: past participle of the verb package, from Old North French pachage "packing," from Medieval Latin paccare "to pack."
