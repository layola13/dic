marcidity:/mɑːrˈsɪdɪti/
n.
枯萎，凋谢
The state of being withered, shrunken, and decayed, especially from lack of moisture.
The marcidity of the flowers was a sad reminder of the passing summer.  这些花的枯萎是夏天逝去的悲伤提醒。
Near synonyms: witheredness, dryness, desiccation, dehydration
Antonyms: freshness, succulence, turgidity
noun
