leapfrogger:/ˈliːpˌfrɒɡər/ (US) /ˈliːpˌfrɒɡə(r)/ (UK)| n.|跳蛙者；超越者；跳棋游戏者|Someone who leapfrogs, or advances by bypassing others; one who plays leapfrog.|The small company leapfrogged its competitors by investing in new technology. 这家小公司通过投资新技术超越了竞争对手。|近义词：overtaker, outstripper|反义词: laggard, straggler|noun

leapfrog:/ˈliːpfrɒɡ/ (US) /ˈliːpfrɒɡ/ (UK)| n. v.|跳背游戏；超越；跳棋|1. (n.) A game in which one player vaults over another who is bending over. 2. (v.) To surpass or overtake, especially by a sudden large advance.|The company leapfrogged its rivals to become the market leader. 这家公司跃居市场领导地位，超越了竞争对手。|近义词：(v.) overtake, bypass, surpass|反义词: lag behind, fall behind|noun, verb
