accuracy: /ˈækjərəsi/ | n. | 准确性，精确度 | The accuracy of the information is crucial.  信息的准确性至关重要。|例句：The archer was known for his accuracy.  这位弓箭手以精准闻名。|近义词：precision, exactness, correctness |反义词: inaccuracy, imprecision, error |词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From Late Latin *accuratia, from Latin accūrātus (“prepared with care, exact, accurate”), past participle of accūrō (“I take care of, attend to diligently”), from ad- (“to, towards”) + cūra (“care, attention, diligence”).
