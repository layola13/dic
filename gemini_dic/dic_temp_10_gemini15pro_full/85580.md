hatlessness:/ˈhæt.ləs.nəs/
n.
无帽; 不戴帽子的状态
the state of being without a hat
His hatlessness was unusual in such formal circumstances. 在如此正式的场合下，他不戴帽子很不寻常。
Synonyms: bareheadedness, uncovered head
Antonyms: hatted, wearing a hat
noun
