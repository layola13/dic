harken:/ˈhɑːrkən/ (US), /ˈhɑːkən/ (UK)| vi.|倾听，留意|To listen attentively; to give heed.|例句：Harken to the words of the wise. 留心听智者的话。

例句：He harkened back to the days of his youth. 他回想起他的青年时代。|近义词：listen, heed, attend|反义词：ignore, disregard|词性：动词
