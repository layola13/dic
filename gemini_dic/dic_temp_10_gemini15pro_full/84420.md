halloysite:/ˈhæloʊsaɪt/| n. |埃洛石；多水高岭石|A hydrated aluminium silicate clay mineral, Al₂Si₂O₅(OH)₄·nH₂O, occurring in a variety of forms.  It is used in ceramics, as a filler in plastics, and in various other industrial applications.|Halloysite nanotubes have attracted significant interest for controlled drug delivery. 埃洛石纳米管在药物控释方面引起了极大的兴趣。|Synonyms: kaolinite, dickite, nacrite|Antonyms: N/A (Minerals typically don't have direct antonyms)|noun
