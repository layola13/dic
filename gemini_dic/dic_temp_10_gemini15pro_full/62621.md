enforceabilities:/ɪnˌfɔːrsəˈbɪlətiz/
n.
可执行性；可强制性
The quality or state of being enforceable.  The degree to which something, such as a law, contract, or agreement, can be enforced.
The enforceabilities of the new regulations are still being debated.  新法规的可执行性仍在辩论中。
近义词:validity, legality
反义词:invalidity, illegality
词性:noun (plural)
