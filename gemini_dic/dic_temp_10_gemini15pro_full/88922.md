近义词: stand firm, stand one's ground, hold fast,  stay put, maintain one's position
反义词: retreat, withdraw, back down, give way, yield


词源:  The phrase is a metaphorical extension of the literal military meaning of holding a position on the battlefield.  "Hold" meaning to grasp or keep and "ground" referring to the land or position occupied.
