errant:/ˈerənt/| adj.|错误的；漫游的；不定的|straying from the proper course or standards; wandering in search of adventure|The errant knight soon found himself lost in the forest. 漫游的骑士很快发现自己在森林里迷路了。|The teacher reprimanded the errant student. 老师斥责了犯错的学生。|近义词：wandering, roving, straying, nomadic, wayward, deviant, aberrant|反义词：obedient, compliant, well-behaved, settled, stationary|词性:形容词
词源: From Old French errant, present participle of errer "to err, wander," from Latin errare "to wander, go astray, err."
