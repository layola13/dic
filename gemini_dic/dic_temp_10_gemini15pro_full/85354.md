harrington: /ˈhærɪŋtən/ | n. | 哈灵顿夹克，轻便防风夹克 | A lightweight, waist-length jacket with a distinctive tartan lining, typically made of cotton and featuring a Fraser tartan lining, a zip fastening, and two slanted pockets.  Originally designed as golfing attire, it gained popularity as a casual outerwear choice. | He wore a classic harrington jacket. 他穿着一件经典的哈灵顿夹克。 |  jacket, blouson | coat, overcoat | noun
