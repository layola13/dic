ferricyanide:/ˌfɛriˈsaɪənaɪd/ | n. |铁氰化物 |A salt containing the ferricyanide anion, [Fe(CN)6]3−.  It is used as an oxidizing agent in organic chemistry and in photography. |Potassium ferricyanide is commonly used in blueprints. 铁氰化钾常用于蓝图制作。| synonym: hexacyanoferrate(III) | antonym: ferrocyanide | noun

ferricyanate:/ˌfɛriˈsaɪəneɪt/ | n. |铁氰酸盐 |A salt containing the ferricyanide anion, [Fe(CN)6]3−. It refers to the same chemical species as ferricyanide but emphasizes the salt nature. |Sodium ferricyanate can be used as an oxidizing agent. 铁氰酸钠可以用作氧化剂。| synonym: hexacyanoferrate(III) | antonym: ferrocyanate | noun
