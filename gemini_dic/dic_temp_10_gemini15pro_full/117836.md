melliloquent:/məˈlɪləkwənt/
adj.
流利甜美的；口齿伶俐的；雄辩的
Having a smooth, sweet flow of language; fluent and eloquent.
Her melliloquent voice captivated the audience. 她流畅甜美的嗓音迷住了观众。
Synonyms: eloquent, articulate, fluent, silver-tongued, persuasive
Antonyms: inarticulate, tongue-tied, halting, stammering
词性: 形容词
