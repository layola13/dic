convalesce:/ˌkɒnvəˈles/ /ˌkɑːnvəˈles/ | vi. | 恢复健康，康复 | To recover health and strength gradually after sickness or weakness. |

例句：
She is convalescing at home after the operation.  她术后在家康复。

He spent a month convalescing in the countryside. 他在乡下疗养了一个月。

近义词：recover, recuperate, improve, heal |
反义词： deteriorate, weaken, decline |
词性：动词 (verb)
