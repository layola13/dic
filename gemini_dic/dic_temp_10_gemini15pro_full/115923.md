marquee:/mɑːrˈkiː/| n. |大帐篷，华盖；（剧院等的）遮檐；（显示文本的）选框|A large tent used for social or commercial functions. A rooflike projection over the entrance to a theater, hotel, etc. A rectangular area on a computer screen that displays a moving stream of text or images.|The wedding reception was held under a grand marquee. 婚礼招待会在一个大帐篷下举行。|近义词：tent, canopy, awning|反义词:|noun
