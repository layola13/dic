cheiropterous:/ˌkaɪrɒpˈtɛrəs/| adj.|手翼目的，蝙蝠似的|Relating to or resembling bats; having membranous wings like a bat.  Derived from Greek *kheir* (hand) and *pteron* (wing).|Bats are cheiropterous mammals. 蝙蝠是手翼目的哺乳动物。|The creature had a distinctly cheiropterous appearance.  这个生物有着明显的蝙蝠似的外观。|近义词:bat-like|反义词: (No direct antonym, consider antonyms for bat characteristics, e.g., wingless, diurnal)|词性:adjective
