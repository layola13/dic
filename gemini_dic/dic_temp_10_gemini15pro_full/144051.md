outrivaled:/ˌaʊtˈraɪvəld/
verb
胜过，超越
surpassed in competition or excellence
例句：She outrivaled all her competitors and won the championship. 她胜过所有竞争对手，赢得了冠军。

例句：The new company quickly outrivaled its older, established rivals. 这家新公司迅速超越了老牌竞争对手。

近义词：surpass, exceed, outdo, outstrip, beat, defeat, conquer, triumph over
反义词：lose to, be defeated by, fall behind
词性：verb (past tense)

outrival:/ˌaʊtˈraɪvəl/
