devore:/dɪˈvɔːr/| n. |(罕见) 吞噬，吞没；暴食|The act of devouring; a large amount of food consumed quickly.|His devore of the entire cake surprised everyone. 他狼吞虎咽地吃掉整个蛋糕让每个人都感到惊讶。|Synonyms: consumption, gluttony, ingestion|Antonyms: abstinence, fasting|noun
