high-fidelity:/ˌhaɪ fɪˈdelɪti/ | n. | 高保真 |  High fidelity refers to the accurate reproduction of sound or image, minimizing distortion and noise.  It aims to recreate the original signal as faithfully as possible. |  The audiophile invested in a high-fidelity sound system.  这个音响发烧友投资了一套高保真音响系统。| synonym: high-quality, accurate, precise | antonym: low-fidelity, distorted, inaccurate | noun

high-fidelities:/ˌhaɪ fɪˈdelɪtiz/ | n. | 高保真度（复数）|  The plural form of "high-fidelity," referring to multiple instances or types of high-fidelity reproduction. |  He compared the high-fidelities of different audio formats. 他比较了不同音频格式的高保真度。| synonym: high qualities, accuracies, precisions | antonym: low-fidelities, distortions, inaccuracies | noun
