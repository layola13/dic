hydrophobic:/ˌhaɪdrəˈfəʊbɪk/ (US) /ˌhaɪdrəˈfoʊbɪk/| adj.|疏水的，憎水的|Tending to repel or fail to mix with water.|Oil is hydrophobic and does not mix with water. 油是疏水的，不会与水混合。|The lotus leaf's hydrophobic surface causes water to bead up. 荷叶的疏水表面使水珠凝结。|近义词：water-repellent, nonpolar|反义词：hydrophilic, polar|词性:形容词
词源: From Greek *hydro-* (water) + *-phobos* (fear)
