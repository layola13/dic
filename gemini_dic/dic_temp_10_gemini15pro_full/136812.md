nonshopper:/ˈnɑːnˌʃɑːpər/| n.|非购物者，不购物的人|A person who does not enjoy shopping or does it infrequently.|She's a nonshopper; she buys only what she absolutely needs. 她是一个不购物的人；她只买她绝对需要的东西。|Near synonyms: non-buyer,  uninterested consumer|Antonyms: shopper, consumer|noun
