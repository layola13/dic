non-engineering:/ˌnɒnˌɛndʒɪˈnɪərɪŋ/ | adj. | 非工程的 | Not related to or involved in engineering. |  Many students in non-engineering majors choose to take some basic science courses. 许多非工科专业的学生选择学习一些基础科学课程。

| Example:  The university offers a wide range of non-engineering programs. 这所大学提供范围广泛的非工程专业。| Synonyms: nontechnical, nonscientific | Antonyms: engineering, technical, scientific | adjective


ETYMOLOGY->
From "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "engineering". "Engineering" comes from the Old French "engigneor" (meaning "one who constructs military engines"), ultimately from the Latin "ingenium" (meaning "natural talent," "invention").
