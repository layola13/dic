emcees:/ˌɛmˈsiːz/
|n.|主持人（复数）
|A master of ceremonies, especially at a hip-hop show or a rap battle.  Someone who introduces performers and keeps the event running smoothly.|
|The emcees kept the energy high throughout the entire concert.|音乐会期间，主持人始终保持着高涨的热情。
|The emcees introduced each act with a clever rhyme.|主持人用巧妙的押韵介绍了每个节目。
|hosts, presenters, moderators|
|audience, guests|
