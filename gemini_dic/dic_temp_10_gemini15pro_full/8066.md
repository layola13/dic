anthropomorphic:/ˌænθrəpəˈmɔːrfɪk/| adj.|拟人的，赋予人性的|Attributing human characteristics or behavior to a god, animal, or object.|例句：Children's cartoons often feature anthropomorphic animals.  儿童卡通片通常以拟人化的动物为主角。

例句：The ancient Greeks had many anthropomorphic gods. 古希腊人有很多被赋予人形的神。|近义词：humanlike, personified|反义词：zoomorphic|词性: adjective
