cloakless:/ˈkloʊklɪs/
adjective
无斗篷的；无遮蔽的；无伪装的
without a cloak; unprotected; undisguised
The cloakless figure stood exposed in the harsh sunlight.  无斗篷的身影暴露在刺眼的阳光下。
The spy, cloakless and vulnerable, was quickly apprehended.  那个间谍，没有伪装，很容易被抓获。

近义词:exposed, uncovered, unprotected, vulnerable, undefended
反义词:cloaked, covered, protected, shielded, disguised
词性:adjective
