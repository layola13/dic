egocentrically:/ˌiːɡoʊˈsɛntrɪkli/ /ˌiːɡəʊˈsɛntrɪkli/| adv.|以自我为中心地，自私地|In a manner focused primarily on oneself and one's own needs and interests, often disregarding the perspectives and feelings of others.|He egocentrically assumed everyone was interested in his story. 他自私地认为每个人都对他讲的故事感兴趣。|Synonyms: selfishly, narcissistically, self-centeredly|Antonyms: altruistically, selflessly|adverb
