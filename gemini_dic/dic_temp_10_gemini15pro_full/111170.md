lithodomous:/ˌlɪθəˈdɒməs/| adj.|凿石的，在岩石中穴居的|Living in or boring into rock.  A lithodomous animal creates its own habitat by excavating rock. |例句：Lithodomous mussels bore into rocks along the coast. 凿石贻贝在沿海岩石中钻洞。

例句：Several species of lithodomous clams are found in this region. 在这个区域发现了几个种类的凿石蛤。|近义词：rock-boring, rock-dwelling|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider "free-swimming" or "terrestrial")|词性: adjective
