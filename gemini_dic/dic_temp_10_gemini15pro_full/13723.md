atypicalities:/ˌeɪˌtɪpɪˈkælɪtiz/| n. |非典型性，异常情况|The report highlighted several atypicalities in the patient's blood work. 该报告强调了患者血液检查中的几个异常情况。|例句：The study revealed some atypicalities in the behavior of the animals.  这项研究揭示了这些动物行为的一些异常之处。

例句：There were a number of atypicalities in the data that warranted further investigation. 数据中存在一些异常情况，需要进一步调查。|近义词：irregularities, abnormalities, anomalies, deviations|反义词：normalities, typicalities|词性:名词

词源:  From a- (not) + typical + -ity (noun suffix) + -es (plural suffix).  来自a-（不）+ typical（典型的）+ -ity（名词后缀）+ -es（复数后缀）。
