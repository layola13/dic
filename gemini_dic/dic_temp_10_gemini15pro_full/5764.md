amalgam:/əˈmælɡəm/| n.|汞合金，混合物|A mixture or blend; a combination of different elements.|The band's music is an amalgam of jazz, rock, and blues.  这个乐队的音乐是爵士乐、摇滚乐和蓝调的混合体。|Example:  The new company is an amalgam of several smaller firms. 这家新公司是由几家小公司合并而成的。|Synonyms: mixture, blend, combination, fusion, composite|Antonyms: separation, division, component, element|noun

amalgamate:/əˈmælɡəˌmeɪt/| v.|合并，混合|To combine or unite to form one organization or structure.|Several small businesses amalgamated to form a larger corporation. 几家小企业合并成了一家大公司。|Example: The two schools will amalgamate next year. 这两所学校明年将合并。|Synonyms: merge, combine, unite, consolidate, integrate|Antonyms: separate, divide, split, disintegrate|verb

Etymology:
From Late Latin *amalgama, from Medieval Latin amalgama, from Arabic al-malgham, meaning "a soft mass."  Ultimately from Greek malagma "emollient, poultice," from malassein "to soften."
