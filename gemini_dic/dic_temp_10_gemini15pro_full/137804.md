nonveritableness:/ˌnɒnˌvɛrɪˈteɪblənəs/
n.
lack of truthfulness; falsity
The nonveritableness of his claims was quickly exposed. 他的说法不真实，很快就被揭穿了。
The report highlighted the nonveritableness of the information provided. 报告强调了所提供信息的虚假性。
Synonyms: falseness, untruthfulness, mendacity, dishonesty, spuriousness, fabrication, falsehood
Antonyms: truthfulness, verity, veracity, honesty, authenticity, genuineness
noun
