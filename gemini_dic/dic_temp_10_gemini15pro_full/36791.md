colognes:/kəˈloʊnz/| n. |古龙水|A perfumed liquid composed of alcohol and fragrant oils, lighter than perfume and originally made in Cologne, Germany.|She wears a variety of colognes, each suited to a different occasion. 她使用各种各样的古龙水，每一种都适合不同的场合。|Synonyms: scents, fragrances, perfumes, aftershaves|Antonyms: odor, stench|Noun
