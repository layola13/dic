madreporite:/ˌmæd.rɪˈpɔː.raɪt/
|n.|篩板|A calcareous plate, perforated with pores, through which water enters the water vascular system of echinoderms.|Sea stars use their madreporite to regulate the amount of water in their water vascular system.|Synonym:sieve plate|Antonym:N/A|noun

madreporite:/ˌmæd.rɪˈpɔː.raɪt/
|adj.|篩板的|Relating to or resembling a madreporite.|The madreporitic plate is easily visible on the aboral surface of a starfish.|Synonym:sieve-like|Antonym:N/A|adjective
