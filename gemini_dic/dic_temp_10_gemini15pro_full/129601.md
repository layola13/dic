non-compoundable:/ˌnɒn kəmˈpaʊndəbəl/ | adj. | 不可复合的，不能和解的 | Not able to be settled by compromise or agreement, especially of an offense or debt.  |  The judge ruled that the offense was non-compoundable and the defendant must serve time. 法官裁定该罪行不可和解，被告必须服刑。 | The debt was deemed non-compoundable, meaning it had to be paid in full.  这笔债务被认为是不可和解的，这意味着它必须全额支付。 | 近义词: uncompromisable, irreconcilable, inexpiable | 反义词: compoundable, compromisable, reconcilable | 词性: 形容词

词源:  "non-" (prefix meaning "not") + "compound" (from Latin "componere," meaning "to put together") + "-able" (suffix meaning "capable of being")
