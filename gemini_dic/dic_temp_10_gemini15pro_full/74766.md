freeze-dries:/ˈfriːz draɪz/ | v. | 冷冻干燥 |  To preserve (something) by freezing it rapidly and then subjecting it to a high vacuum that removes ice by sublimation. | The company freeze-dries the coffee to preserve its flavor.  这家公司将咖啡冷冻干燥以保存其风味。| 近义词: lyophilize | 反义词: hydrate | 词性: 动词
