northbound:/ˈnɔːrθbaʊnd/| adj.|向北的，朝北行驶的|Traveling or directed toward the north. |The northbound train is delayed. 向北行驶的火车晚点了。|Northbound traffic is heavy this morning. 今早北上的交通很拥堵。|近义词：northward, northerly|反义词: southbound|词性:形容词
词源:  From "north" + "bound" (meaning "going in a specified direction"). "North" comes from Old English "norþ", ultimately from Proto-Germanic "*norþrą". "Bound" comes from Old Norse "búinn" (past participle of "búa", meaning "to prepare, dwell").
