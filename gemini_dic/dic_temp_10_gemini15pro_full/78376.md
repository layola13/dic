gesture:/ˈdʒestʃər/ (US), /ˈdʒestʃə(r)/ (UK)| n. v. |姿势；手势；姿态；表示|A movement of part of the body, especially a hand or the head, to express an idea or meaning.|She made a gesture of impatience. 她做了一个不耐烦的手势。|近义词：sign, signal, motion, indication|反义词：stillness, inaction|noun

gesture:/ˈdʒestʃər/ (US), /ˈdʒestʃə(r)/ (UK)| v. |做手势；用动作示意|To make a movement with your hand, head, or body to show a particular meaning.|He gestured towards the door. 他朝门口做了个手势。|近义词：signal, motion, beckon, indicate|反义词：freeze, remain still|verb
