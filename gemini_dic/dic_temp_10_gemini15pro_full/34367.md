classicisms:/ˈklæsɪˌsɪzəmz/ | n. | 古典主义；古典风格；古典语汇 | The use of traditional and classical styles or forms, especially in art, literature, or architecture. classicism:/ˈklæsɪˌsɪzəm/ | n. | 古典主义；古典风格；(尤指艺术、文学或建筑方面的)古典传统 |  A movement or tendency in art, literature, and music reflecting the principles manifested in the art of ancient Greece and Rome. Adherence to or imitation of classic principles and style in art or literature. | The art gallery's new exhibit focuses on classicism in 18th-century painting.  这家美术馆的新展览聚焦于18世纪绘画中的古典主义。  He studied classicism and romanticism in literature. 他研究了文学中的古典主义和浪漫主义。 | 近义词: traditionalism, formalism | 反义词: modernism, avant-garde | 词性: noun
