boathook:/ˈboʊthʊk/| n. |船钩|A pole with a hook and spike on the end, used for maneuvering a boat or retrieving objects from the water. |The sailor used a boathook to fend off the dock. 水手用船钩把船撑离码头。| The boathook snagged a floating log. 船钩钩住了一根漂浮的原木。 | 近义词: gaff, hook | 反义词:  (No direct antonym) | noun
