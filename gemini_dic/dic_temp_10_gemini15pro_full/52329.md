dicotyledonous: /ˌdaɪˌkɒtɪˈliːdənəs/ (US) /ˌdaɪˌkɒtɪˈliːdənəs/ (UK)| adj.|双子叶的|Having two cotyledons (embryonic leaves) in the seed.|Example: Roses are dicotyledonous plants. 玫瑰是双子叶植物。|Example:  Dicotyledonous trees often have broad leaves. 双子叶树木通常有阔叶。|Synonym: dicot|Antonym: monocotyledonous|adjective


dicotyledon: /ˌdaɪˌkɒtɪˈliːdən/ (US) /ˌdaɪˌkɒtɪˈliːdən/ (UK)| n.|双子叶植物|A plant that has two seed leaves or cotyledons.|Example:  The garden is full of dicotyledons. 花园里满是双子叶植物。|Example: Sunflowers are a common example of a dicotyledon. 向日葵是双子叶植物的一个常见例子。|Synonym: dicot|Antonym: monocotyledon|noun
