nonsocieties:/ˌnɒn səˈsaɪətiz/
noun
非社会；不构成社会之群体
Groups of people who do not form or qualify as societies, often lacking shared norms, institutions, or social structures.
Anthropologists debate whether isolated groups without agriculture are nonsocieties. 人类学家争论没有农业的孤立群体是否是非社会。
Isolated communities lacking complex social structures could be considered nonsocieties. 缺乏复杂社会结构的孤立社区可以被认为是非社会。
Synonyms: non-societies, unorganized groups
Antonyms: societies, communities
