battiness:/ˈbætɪnɪs/| n.|古怪；疯狂；精神错乱|The degree of being batty; craziness or eccentricity.|His recent behavior suggests a certain degree of battiness.|近义词：craziness, insanity, madness, eccentricity, lunacy|反义词：sanity, lucidity, rationality|词性:名词

ETYMOLOGY->
From batty (adjective) + -ness (suffix forming nouns expressing a state or quality).  Batty itself is likely a shortened form of "bats in the belfry," a figurative expression for craziness. The image of bats fluttering around in a bell tower suggests a disordered and chaotic mind.
