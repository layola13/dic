lunette:/luːˈnɛt/ | n. |  月牙形物；(尤指旧时军帽上用作帽徽的)新月形饰物；(早期飞机、汽车或船上的)圆形舷窗；护目镜；小望远镜；【建】拱腹 |A crescent-shaped object or space; specifically, a crescent-shaped ornament, especially one worn as a badge on a military headdress. A small round window, especially in an early aircraft, car, or ship.  A pair of protective spectacles. A small telescope.  The surface between the intrados and the extrados of an arch. | The pilot peered through the lunette of his aircraft. 飞行员透过飞机的圆形舷窗向外张望。|Synonyms: crescent, half-moon, meniscus, lens, spectacles, spyglass| Antonyms: full moon| Noun
