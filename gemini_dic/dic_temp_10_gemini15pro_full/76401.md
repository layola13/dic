gag-up:/ˈɡæɡ ʌp/
verb
压制言论; 阻止…说话; 使…噤声
To suppress or silence someone, especially by preventing them from speaking freely.
The dictator gagged up the press and imprisoned his opponents. 独裁者压制了新闻界并监禁了他的反对者。

The company tried to gag up the whistleblower. 公司试图让举报人闭嘴。

近义词:silence, suppress, muzzle, censor
反义词:encourage, promote, liberate

词性:verb
