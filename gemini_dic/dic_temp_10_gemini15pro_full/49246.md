deleteriousness:/ˌdelɪˈtɪəriəsnəs/ | n. |有害；毒性；危害性 | The state or quality of being deleterious; harmfulness. | The deleteriousness of smoking is well documented. 吸烟的危害性已被充分证明。 |  近义词：harmfulness, noxiousness, perniciousness | 反义词：benefit, helpfulness | 词性: 名词

deleterious: /ˌdelɪˈtɪəriəs/ | adj. |有害的；有毒的 | causing harm or damage. | Smoking is deleterious to your health. 吸烟有害健康. | 近义词：harmful, damaging, detrimental | 反义词：beneficial, advantageous | 词性: 形容词
