iMessage:/ˈaɪˌmɛsɪdʒ/| n.|苹果公司即时通信软件|An instant messaging service developed by Apple Inc. that allows users to send text messages, photos, videos, and other media over Wi-Fi or cellular data networks to other Apple devices.|例句：I sent him an iMessage, but he hasn't replied yet. 我给他发了一条 iMessage，但他还没有回复。|近义词：text message, instant message, DM|反义词: (No direct antonym, could consider traditional mail or phone call)|noun
